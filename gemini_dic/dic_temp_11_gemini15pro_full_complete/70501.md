filch:/fɪltʃ/
verb
偷窃，窃取
to steal something, especially something small and not very valuable
He filched a cookie from the jar. 他从罐子里偷了一块饼干。
近义词:steal, pilfer, swipe, pinch
反义词:give, return, offer
词性:verb


Etymology:
From Middle English *filchen*, perhaps frequentative of *felen* (“to feel, perceive, grope”) (from Old English *fēlan* (“to feel, perceive”)), possibly influenced by Old French *felcher*, *felchier* (“to search”), or from a blend of these. More at feel.
