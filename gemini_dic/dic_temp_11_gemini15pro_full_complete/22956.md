bottom-feeder:/ˈbɑːtəmˌfiːdər/| n. |底层动物; 食腐动物; 卑鄙小人; |A bottom-feeder is an aquatic animal that feeds on or near the bottom of a body of water.  Figuratively, it refers to a person or organization perceived as unscrupulous or exploiting others' misfortune. |例句：The catfish is a bottom-feeder, scavenging for food in the mud. 鲶鱼是一种底层动物，在泥里觅食。

例句：He was known as a bottom-feeder in the business world, preying on failing companies. 他在商界被称为卑鄙小人，专门掠夺那些濒临倒闭的公司。|近义词：scavenger, opportunist, parasite|反义词: philanthropist, benefactor|词性: noun
