outgoings: /ˈaʊtɡəʊɪŋz/ | n. | 支出，开销 | Amounts of money spent regularly, such as on rent, bills, etc. |  Example: Our monthly outgoings are quite high due to the rising cost of living. 我们的月支出由于生活成本上升而相当高。

Example:  The company's outgoings exceeded its income last quarter.  该公司上季度的支出超过了收入。 | 近义词: expenses, expenditure, costs | 反义词: income, revenue | 词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "outgo" (to go out, to be spent), which is formed from "out" + "go".
