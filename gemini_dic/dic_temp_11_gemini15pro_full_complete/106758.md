laagered:/ˈlɑːɡərd/ /ˈleɪɡərd/| adj.|扎营的；围成营地的；(比喻)处于防御状态的；保守的|Encamped, especially in a laager; (figuratively) in a defensive position; conservative.  Comes from Afrikaans "laager," meaning an encampment formed by wagons in a circle.|The company, laagered against competition, refused to innovate. 这家公司为了抵御竞争，拒绝创新。|近义词：entrenched, fortified, defensive, conservative|反义词：progressive, liberal, open|词性:形容词
