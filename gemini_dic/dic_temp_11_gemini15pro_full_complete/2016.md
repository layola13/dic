actuary:/ˈæktʃʊˌɛri/ | n. | 精算师 | A person who compiles and analyzes statistics and uses them to calculate insurance risks and premiums. |  An actuary calculates how much an insurance company should charge its customers.  精算师计算保险公司应该向客户收取多少费用。 | statistician, analyst |  - | noun

actuarial: /ˌæktʃʊˈɛriəl/ | adj. | 精算的 | Relating to the work or calculations of an actuary. | Actuarial science involves complex mathematical models. 精算学涉及复杂的数学模型。 | statistical, mathematical | - | adjective
