cisser:/ˈsɪzər/| n.|剪刀；剪切机；剪切工人|A tool used for cutting cloth, paper, and other materials, consisting of two blades laid one on top of the other and fastened in the middle so that the sharp edges can be opened and closed.|例句：The tailor used his cisser to cut the fabric. 裁缝用剪刀剪布料。

例句：The cisser malfunctioned and tore the paper. 剪刀坏了，把纸撕破了。|近义词：scissors, shears, clippers|反义词:|noun
