benightedly:/bɪˈnaɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 愚昧地，无知地 | In a manner that shows a lack of awareness or understanding of the world and modern ideas. |  He benightedly clung to outdated beliefs. 他愚昧地 clinging to outdated beliefs. |  She benightedly dismissed the scientific evidence. 她愚昧地忽略了科学证据。 | 近义词: ignorantly, narrowly, unimaginatively | 反义词: enlightenedly, knowingly, wisely | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "benighted," past participle of "benight," from be- + night.  Meaning originally "overtaken by night," it later evolved to mean "intellectually or morally dark."
