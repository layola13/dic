goodship:/ˈɡʊdʃɪp/ | n. | 友谊；和谐；美好的事物 | A state of friendship and harmony; something pleasant or desirable. |  例句：There's a lot of goodship between the two families. 这两个家庭之间有很多友谊。

例句：The goodship of the community is important to us all. 社区的和谐对我们大家都很重要。 | 近义词：friendship, amity, camaraderie, goodwill, rapport | 反义词：enmity, hostility, animosity | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "good" + "ship" (suffix denoting state or condition).  The word is relatively modern, becoming more common in the 20th century.
