misintentions:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnʃənz/| n. |恶意；不良意图|Bad or dishonest intentions. |She had no misintentions; she simply made a mistake. 她没有恶意；她只是犯了一个错误。| He apologized for his misintentions, explaining he hadn't meant to cause any harm. 他为自己的不良意图道歉，解释说他并非有意造成任何伤害。 |近义词：bad intentions, malice, ill will|反义词：good intentions, goodwill|词性:名词
