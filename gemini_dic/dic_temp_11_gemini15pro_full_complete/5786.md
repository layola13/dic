amanuensis:/əˌmænjuˈɛnsɪs/
n.
抄写员，书记员
A person employed to write what another dictates or to copy what has been written.
Milton employed several amanuenses after he lost his sight.  弥尔顿失明后雇佣了几名抄写员。

amanuensis:/əˌmænjuˈɛnsɪs/
n.
Literary assistant; secretary.
文学助手；秘书
The author thanked his amanuensis for her tireless work on the manuscript. 作者感谢他的秘书为这份手稿所做的不懈努力。


Synonyms:
scribe, copyist, secretary, transcriber

Antonyms:
author, originator, speaker
