hermaphrodeity:/hərˈmæfrədəɪti/ |n.|雌雄同体；两性畸形|The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs.|Hermaphrodeity is rare in humans. 人类的雌雄同体是罕见的。|Synonyms: hermaphroditism, intersexuality, bisexuality (in older usage)|Antonyms: unisexuality, gonochorism|Noun

hermaphroditism:/hərˈmæfrədəˌtɪzəm/ |n.|雌雄同体；两性畸形|The state or condition of being a hermaphrodite; having both male and female reproductive organs.|Hermaphroditism is more common in some plant species than in animals.  在某些植物物种中，雌雄同体比在动物中更常见。|Synonyms: hermaphrodeity, intersexuality, bisexuality (in older usage)|Antonyms: unisexuality, gonochorism|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hermaphroditus, from Greek hermaphroditos, from Hermaphroditos, the son of Hermes and Aphrodite who became united in one body with the nymph Salmacis.
