conversionism:/kənˈvɜːrʒənɪzəm/
n.
皈依主义; 宗教信仰改变;
The act or process of converting or of being converted.  A policy of conversion, especially by missionaries.
He underwent a period of religious conversionism in his twenties.  他在二十多岁时经历了一段宗教皈依时期。
近义词:proselytism, evangelism
反义词:apostasy, secularism
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From conversion + -ism.  Conversion from Latin conversiō, conversiōn- turning, alteration, change. -ism suffix forming abstract nouns from other nouns or adjectives, denoting action, state, condition, doctrine, system, etc.
