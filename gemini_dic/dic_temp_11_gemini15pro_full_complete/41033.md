copelessness:/ˈkoʊpləsnəs/
n.
无能为力，绝望
The state of lacking the ability to cope or handle a situation, often accompanied by feelings of helplessness and despair.
He felt a deep sense of copelessness in the face of his mounting debts. 他面对日益增长的债务感到深切的无力感。
helplessness, despair, powerlessness, hopelessness, inability to cope
resourcefulness, resilience, capability, competence, self-efficacy
noun
