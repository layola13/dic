detaching:/dɪˈtætʃɪŋ/
verb
分离，拆卸
removing or separating something from something else it is attached to
The campers were detaching their tents from the ground.  露营者们正在把帐篷从地上拆下来。
The engine detached from the rocket after launch. 发射后，引擎与火箭分离。
synonyms: separating, disconnecting, uncoupling, removing
antonyms: attaching, connecting, joining
