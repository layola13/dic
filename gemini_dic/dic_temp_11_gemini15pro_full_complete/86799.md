hemastatical: /ˌhiːməˈstætɪkl/ /ˌheməˈstætɪkl/
adjective
止血的，止血药的
Relating to the stopping of bleeding; hemostatic.  Having the property of stopping bleeding.
He applied a hemastatical agent to the wound. 他在伤口上涂了一种止血药。
Synonyms: hemostatic, styptic, antihemorrhagic
Antonyms: hemorrhagic
