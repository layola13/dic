beardednesses:/ˈbɪərdɪdnəsɪz/
n.
胡须茂盛的状态；留胡须的习俗
The state or quality of having a beard, especially a long or profuse one.  The prevalence of beards in a group or society.
His beardedness gave him a wise and distinguished air.  他的胡须使他显得睿智而尊贵。
The Victorian era was a time of great beardedness. 维多利亚时代是一个胡须盛行的时代。
Synonyms: hirsuteness, hairiness
Antonyms: hairlessness, smoothness
