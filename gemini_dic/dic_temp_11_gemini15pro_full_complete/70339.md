fieldsman:/ˈfiːldzmən/| n. |外野手，守备员|A player in a cricket or baseball team who fields the ball.  A person who works in the fields, especially in agriculture. |例句：The fieldsman caught the ball near the boundary. 外野手在边界附近接住了球。

例句：The experienced fieldsman expertly harvested the wheat.  经验丰富的守备员熟练地收割了小麦。|近义词：fielder, outfielder, farmworker, agricultural worker|反义词:batsman (cricket), batter (baseball)|noun
