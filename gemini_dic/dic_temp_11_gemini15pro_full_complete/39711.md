consignee:/kənˌsaɪˈniː/
n.
收货人；受托人
a person or company to whom goods are sent or delivered
The consignee will be responsible for all import duties. 收货人将负责所有进口税。

近义词:recipient, receiver
反义词:consignor, shipper
词性:noun
