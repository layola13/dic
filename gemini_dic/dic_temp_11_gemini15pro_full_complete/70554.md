filing:/ˈfaɪlɪŋ/| n.|提交，归档，锉屑，锉|The act of submitting a document to an official record. Also, material removed by filing.|例句：The filing of the tax return is due next week.  纳税申报表下周到期提交。

例句：Metal filings covered the floor of the workshop.  金属锉屑覆盖了车间的地板。|近义词：submission, lodgement, application, shavings, sawdust|反义词:withdrawal, retrieval|词性:noun
