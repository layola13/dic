leprosity:/ˈlɛprəsi/ | n. | 麻风病，麻风病的症状；堕落，腐败 | A chronic infectious disease that mainly affects the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes.  It can cause skin lesions, nerve damage, and deformities.  Figuratively, it can refer to moral corruption or decay. |  Leprosy was once a widespread and feared disease. 麻风病曾经是一种广泛传播且令人恐惧的疾病。 The leprosity of the political system is evident in the widespread corruption. 政治体制的腐败在普遍存在的腐败现象中显而易见。 | 近义词: leprosy, Hansen's disease | 反义词: health, purity | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Old French *leprosité, from Late Latin leprositās, from Latin leprōsus (“leprous”).
