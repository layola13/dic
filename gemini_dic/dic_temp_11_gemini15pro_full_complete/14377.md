autopsying:/ˈɔːtɑːpsiɪŋ/ |v.| 尸检，进行尸体解剖 | The medical examiner was autopsying the body to determine the cause of death. 法医正在对尸体进行尸检以确定死因。|  performing an autopsy, conducting a postmortem examination | N/A | verb |
Etymology: From autopsy + -ing. Autopsy comes from the Greek autopsia, "seeing with one's own eyes," from autos "self" + opsis "sight."
