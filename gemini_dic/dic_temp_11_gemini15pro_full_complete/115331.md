mannitol:/ˈmænɪtɒl/ (UK) /ˈmænɪtɔːl/ (US)| n. |甘露醇|A white crystalline alcohol C6H14O6 found in many plants and used as a diuretic and a sweetening agent.|Mannitol is used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇用于减轻脑肿胀。|Synonyms: hexahydroxyhexane, manna sugar|Antonyms: None|Noun
