omnivory:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːri/ (US) /ˈɒmnɪvəri/ (UK)| n.|杂食性|The practice of consuming both animal and plant matter as food.|Bears are a classic example of omnivory in the wild.  熊是野生动物中杂食性的典型例子。|Synonyms: omnivorousness|Antonyms: herbivory, carnivory|noun

omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ (US) /ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ (UK)| adj.|杂食的|Feeding on both animal and plant matter.|Pigs are omnivorous animals, eating almost anything. 猪是杂食性动物，几乎什么都吃。|Synonyms: all-devouring, polyphagous|Antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous|adjective

omnivore:/ˈɒmnɪvɔː(r)/ (US) /ˈɒmnɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|杂食动物|An animal that feeds on both animal and plant matter.|Humans are omnivores, with a diet that includes both meat and vegetables. 人类是杂食动物，饮食中包括肉类和蔬菜。|Synonyms: generalist feeder|Antonyms: herbivore, carnivore|noun
