bahamas:/bəˈhɑːməz/
n.
巴哈马
a North American country consisting of an archipelago of about 700 islands and cays southeast of Florida; population 301,790 (est. 2009); official language, English; capital, Nassau. Formerly a British colony, the Bahamas became independent in 1973.
The Bahamas is a popular tourist destination.  巴哈马是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。
