formica:/fɔːrˈmaɪkə/ /fɔːrˈmiːkə/
n.
Formica (trademark), a hard, durable plastic laminate used for countertops, furniture, etc.
福米卡（商标），一种坚硬耐用的塑料层压板，用于台面、家具等。
The kitchen counters were made of formica. 厨房的台面是用福米卡做的。

formica:/fɔːrˈmaɪkə/ /fɔːrˈmiːkə/
n.
(also formic acid) A colorless, pungent, corrosive carboxylic acid, HCOOH, found especially in ants and used in dyeing and finishing textiles and paper and in the manufacture of fumigants, insecticides, and refrigerants.
（也称甲酸）一种无色、刺激性、腐蚀性的羧酸，HCOOH，尤其存在于蚂蚁体内，用于染色和整理纺织品和纸张，以及制造熏蒸剂、杀虫剂和制冷剂。
Formic acid is the simplest carboxylic acid. 甲酸是最简单的羧酸。


Etymology: From Formica (Modern Latin genus name for ants), from Latin formīca (ant).  The acid was so named because it was originally obtained by the distillation of ants.
词源：来自Formica（现代拉丁语中蚂蚁的属名），源自拉丁语formīca（蚂蚁）。这种酸之所以如此命名，是因为它最初是通过蒸馏蚂蚁获得的。

Synonyms: (for the laminate) laminate, countertop, surfacing
近义词：（指层压板）层压板，台面，表面材料
Synonyms: (for the acid) methanoic acid
近义词：（指甲酸）甲酸

Antonyms: (for the laminate)  No direct antonym, perhaps natural materials like wood or stone.
反义词：（指层压板）没有直接的反义词，也许是像木头或石头这样的天然材料。
Antonyms: (for the acid)  No direct antonym.
反义词：（指甲酸）没有直接的反义词。
