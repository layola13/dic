monomania: /ˌmɒnəˈmeɪnɪə/ (US) /ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniə/ (UK)| n. |偏执狂，狂热|An exaggerated or obsessive enthusiasm for or preoccupation with one subject or idea.|He had a monomania about cleanliness. 他有洁癖。|近义词: obsession, fixation, craze|反义词: indifference, apathy|词性:noun

Etymology: From French monomanie, from Greek monos "single" + mania "madness."
