outstartling:/ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtəlɪŋ/| adj.|令人吃惊的，惊人的|Surpassing others in causing surprise or astonishment.  It implies a higher degree of surprise than simply startling.|Her outstartling beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她惊人的美貌迷住了房间里的每一个人。|Synonyms: astonishing, amazing, surprising, astounding, shocking|Antonyms:  unremarkable, ordinary, mundane|adjective
