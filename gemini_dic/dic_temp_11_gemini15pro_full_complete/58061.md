dromond: /ˈdrɒmənd/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːmənd/ (US)| n. |大型桨帆船，快速战船| A large, fast, seagoing medieval ship propelled by both oars and sails, used for war and trade, especially in the Mediterranean. |例句：The dromond, a powerful vessel of its time, could carry hundreds of soldiers and their equipment.  大型桨帆船是那个时代的强大船只，可以运载数百名士兵及其装备。

例句：Dromonds played a crucial role in naval warfare during the Crusades. 大型桨帆船在十字军东征期间的海战中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：galley, bireme, warship|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "merchant ship" for a contrast in purpose) |词性: noun
