incompact:/ˌɪnkəmˈpækt/| adj.|不紧密的，疏松的，不坚实的|Not compact; not closely or densely packed; loose; not solid.|The soil was incompact and easily eroded. 土壤疏松，容易被侵蚀。|近义词：loose, porous, friable|反义词:compact, dense, solid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- (not) + compact (closely packed). The word "compact" itself comes from the Latin "compactus," past participle of "compingere," meaning "to fasten together."
