forwards:/ˈfɔːwədz/ (British) /ˈfɔːrwərdz/ (American)| adv. adj. n.|向前，前进；将来，今后；(足球、篮球等)前锋|Moving or directed towards the front or future.  Can also refer to a player position in some sports.|The ship sailed forwards.  船向前航行。|近义词：ahead, onward, frontward|反义词:backward, backwards|词性:副词

forwards:/ˈfɔːwədz/ (British) /ˈfɔːrwərdz/ (American)| adj.|向前的，前进的|Located at or near the front; moving or directed towards the front.|The forward cabins are more expensive. 前面的船舱更贵。|近义词：front, anterior|反义词:rear, back|词性:形容词

forwards:/ˈfɔːwədz/ (British) /ˈfɔːrwərdz/ (American)| n.|（足球、篮球等）前锋|An attacking player in sports like soccer, basketball, etc.|He plays as a forward for the local team. 他是当地球队的前锋。|近义词：striker, attacker|反义词:defender, goalie|词性:名词
