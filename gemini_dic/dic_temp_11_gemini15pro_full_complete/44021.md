crimp:/krɪmp/| n. vt.|卷曲，褶皱；压制；限制；妨碍|A curl or wave, typically one made artificially in hair. To press or pinch (something) into small, regular folds or ridges.|She used a curling iron to put a crimp in her hair. 她用卷发棒把头发弄卷了。|近义词：curl, wave, crease, fold, restrict, hinder|反义词:straighten, smooth, aid, assist|词性:名词,动词
crimp:/krɪmp/| n. vt.|压制；限制；妨碍|To have a limiting or adverse effect on (something).|The lack of funding has crimped our research efforts. 缺乏资金限制了我们的研究工作。|近义词：restrict, hinder, limit, constrain|反义词:aid, assist, promote, encourage|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *krimpen, frequentative of *krimmen (“to bend, curve, shrink”), from Proto-Germanic *krempaną (“to bend, curve”). Cognate with German krampfen (“to cramp”), Swedish krympa (“to shrink”).  The noun is from the verb.
