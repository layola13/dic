disassenter:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛntər/ | n. |持不同意见者，反对者|A person who dissents, especially from an established church or political party. |  Example: The disassenter voiced his opposition to the new policy.  持不同意见者表达了他对新政策的反对。

Example:  She was a known disassenter during the war.  在战争期间，她是一位知名的异议人士。|Synonym: dissident, objector, nonconformist, protester, rebel |Antonym: conformist, supporter, follower | noun
