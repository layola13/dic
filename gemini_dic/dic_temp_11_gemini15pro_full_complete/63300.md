enthusiasts:/ɪnˈθuːziæsts/ | n. | 爱好者，热衷者 | A person who is highly interested in a particular activity or subject. |  例句：Car enthusiasts will love the new model. 汽车爱好者会喜欢这款新车型。

例句：She is an avid enthusiast of classical music. 她是一位古典音乐的狂热爱好者。 | 近义词：fan, aficionado, devotee, admirer | 反义词：critic, detractor, opponent | 词性: Noun
