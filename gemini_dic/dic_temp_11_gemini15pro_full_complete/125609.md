multiverses:/ˈmʌltɪˌvɜːrsɪz/ | n. |多重宇宙，多元宇宙|A hypothetical collection of multiple universes, each of which comprises everything that is physically accessible or reachable by an observer.  It suggests that our universe may be just one of many, each with its own physical laws and constants.|Scientists are exploring the concept of multiverses. 科学家正在探索多重宇宙的概念。|synonyms: megaverse, omniverse, metaverse (sometimes)| antonyms: universe (singular)|noun
