nonius:/ˈnoʊniəs/| n.|游标卡尺，游标尺|A scale, graduated in fractions of a main scale division, that slides along the main scale to indicate measurements more precisely than the main scale allows.  A vernier.|例句：The nonius on the caliper allowed for precise measurements to the hundredth of an inch.  游标卡尺上的游标可以精确测量到百分之一英寸。

例句：He used the nonius to accurately measure the width of the metal plate. 他用游标尺精确地测量了金属板的宽度。|近义词：vernier, vernier scale|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
