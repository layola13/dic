felsic:/ˈfɛlsɪk/| adj.|长英质的，富含长石和石英的|Relating to or denoting a group of light-colored minerals including feldspar, feldspathoids, quartz, and muscovite.|The felsic rocks are lighter in color than mafic rocks. 长英质岩石比镁铁质岩石颜色更浅。|The volcano erupted felsic lava. 这座火山喷发了长英质熔岩。|近义词：sialic|反义词：mafic|词性：形容词

词源:  felsic is a portmanteau word derived from **fel**dspar and **si**lica (quartz).
