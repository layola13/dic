amiableness:/ˈeɪmiəblnəs/ | n. | 和蔼可亲；亲切友好 | The quality of being amiable; pleasantness; affability.  | Her amiableness made her a popular figure in the community. 她的和蔼可亲使她在社区很受欢迎. |  kindness, friendliness, affability, geniality, cordiality |  unfriendliness, hostility, coldness, aloofness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From amiable + -ness.
Amiable (adj.) comes from Old French amable "friendly, lovable," from Late Latin amabilis "lovable, worthy of love," from amare "to love."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
