lupinin:/ˈluːpɪnɪn/| n. |羽扇豆碱|A bitter, crystalline alkaloid, C10H19ON, obtained from the seeds of various lupines and having toxic properties.|Lupinin can cause poisoning in livestock that graze on lupines.  羽扇豆碱会导致放牧在羽扇豆上的牲畜中毒。|The researchers isolated lupinin from the lupine seeds. 研究人员从羽扇豆种子中分离出羽扇豆碱。|Synonyms: lupin alkaloid|Antonyms: None|noun
