hesper:/ˈhɛspər/| n. |西方，西方之星，晚星|A term referring to the evening star (usually Venus), or the west, or something pertaining to the west.|例句：The hesper shone brightly in the twilight sky.  暮色苍穹中，那颗昏星闪耀夺目。|近义词：vesper, evening star, Venus (when referring to the evening star)|反义词：phosphor (morning star)|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek ἕσπερος (hesperos, “evening, western”).
