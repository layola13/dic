ailment:/ˈeɪlmənt/| n.|疾病，病痛|An illness, typically a minor one.|She was suffering from a variety of ailments, including headaches and fatigue. 她患有各种疾病，包括头痛和疲劳。|近义词：illness, disease, sickness, malady|反义词:health, wellness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *aillemant "affliction," from ailler "to afflict," from Latin *aglare, frequentative of agere "to drive, lead, do, act," from PIE root *ag- "to drive, draw out or forth, move."
