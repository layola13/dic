nonenthusiastically: /ˌnɒnɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/ (US) | adv. | 不热情地，冷淡地 |  In a way that shows a lack of enthusiasm or interest. | She nonenthusiastically agreed to attend the meeting. 她不热情地同意参加会议。| He nonenthusiastically clapped his hands after the performance.  他演出结束后不热情地拍了拍手。| 近义词: apathetically, indifferently, listlessly | 反义词: enthusiastically, eagerly, keenly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + enthusiastically (with enthusiasm).  "Enthusiastic" comes from the Greek "enthousiasmos" meaning "divine inspiration or possession."
