cybersquatting: /ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtɪŋ/
| n. |域名抢注|The act of registering a domain name that is the same as, or confusingly similar to, the trademark of another person or company with the bad-faith intent to profit from it. |  Many companies have been victims of cybersquatting. 许多公司都是域名抢注的受害者。| 近义词: domain hijacking, domain name speculation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps legitimate domain registration) | 词性: noun

cybersquattings:  /ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtɪŋz/
| n. |域名抢注（复数）|Plural form of cybersquatting, referring to multiple instances of this practice. |  Cybersquattings are a growing problem for businesses. 域名抢注对企业来说是一个日益严重的问题。| 近义词: domain hijackings, domain name speculations | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
