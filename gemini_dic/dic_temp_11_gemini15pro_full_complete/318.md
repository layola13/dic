abetted:/əˈbɛtɪd/
verb
教唆；煽动；怂恿；支持；助长
encouraged or assisted someone to do something wrong
The accountant abetted the CEO in committing fraud.  会计师教唆首席执行官犯了欺诈罪。
He was abetted in the deception by his wife.  他的妻子怂恿他进行欺骗。
synonyms: aided, assisted, supported, encouraged, instigated, incited
antonyms: hindered, obstructed, prevented, discouraged, dissuaded
