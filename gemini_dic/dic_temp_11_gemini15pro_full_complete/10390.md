appendicle:/əˈpɛndɪk(ə)l/
n.| 附属物，附肢|A small, usually projecting part of an organism, especially one that is rudimentary or nonfunctional. |The sea squirt has an appendicle that helps it attach to rocks. |海鞘有一个帮助它附着在岩石上的附属物。|
近义词:appendix, adjunct, attachment, supplement
反义词:main body, core
词性:noun

appendicle:/əˈpɛndɪk(ə)l/
adj.| 附属的，附加的 | Relating to or resembling an appendicle; appendicular. | The appendicle skeleton includes the limbs and the bones that support them. | 附肢骨骼包括四肢和支撑它们的骨头。
近义词:appended, attached, supplementary
反义词:main, central, core
词性:adjective
