dandified:/ˈdændɪfaɪd/| adj.|衣着讲究的，纨绔子弟的，过分注重衣着的|A man who is dandified is very concerned with his clothes and appearance and always dresses in a stylish way.  A dandified person, especially a man, is excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance.|例句：He was a dandified young man, always impeccably dressed. 他是一个衣着讲究的年轻人，总是穿着无可挑剔。|近义词：foppish, dapper, stylish, fashionable|反义词：slovenly, unkempt, scruffy|词性:形容词

dandified:/ˈdændɪfaɪd/| v.|打扮得花哨，过分修饰|To make someone or something dandified is to make them excessively concerned with their clothes and appearance.|例句：He dandified himself before going out. 他出门前把自己打扮得花哨。|近义词：preen, primp, groom|反义词：neglect|词性:动词
