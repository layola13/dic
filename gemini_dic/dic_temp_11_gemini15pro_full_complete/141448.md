opiferous:/əˈpɪfərəs/
adjective
多产的，丰富的
producing or yielding much; abounding
The opiferous land produced a bountiful harvest.  这片肥沃的土地带来了丰收。
近义词:productive, fertile, fruitful, abundant, copious
反义词:barren, infertile, unproductive, scarce, meager
词性:adjective
词源:Latin *opifer "bringing help," from ops "power, resources, wealth" + -fer "bearing."
