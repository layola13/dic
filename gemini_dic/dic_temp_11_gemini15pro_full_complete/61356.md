emberizidae:/ˌɛmbərɪˈzaɪdiː/ | n. | 燕雀科 | A large family of passerine birds, the Emberizidae, that includes buntings, sparrows, and American sparrows.  This family was formerly considered a subfamily (Emberizinae) of the Fringillidae (finches). | The Dark-eyed Junco is a common bird belonging to the Emberizidae family.  暗眼灯芯草雀是燕雀科的一种常见鸟类。| synonyms: bunting family, sparrow family | antonyms: N/A | noun
