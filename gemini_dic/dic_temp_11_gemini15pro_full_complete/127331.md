nazariteship:/ˈnæzəˌraɪtˌʃɪp/
n.
纳粹尔人的身份；禁欲生活
the state or period of being a Nazirite, a person who took a vow to abstain from alcohol and certain other activities as a sign of devotion to God.
Samson followed the rules of nazariteship from birth.  参孙从出生就遵守纳粹尔人的戒律。
Nazirite vow,  vow of abstinence
indulgence, hedonism
noun
