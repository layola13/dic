brutalises:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
残酷对待，野蛮对待
To treat someone or something with extreme cruelty and violence.
The dictator brutalises his people. 独裁者残酷对待他的人民。
Prison can brutalise people. 监狱会使人变得残暴。
synonyms: abuses, mistreats, ill-treats, maltreats, manhandles, batters, assaults, outrages
antonyms: cherishes, protects, nurtures
v.

brutalises:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使丧失人性，使麻木不仁
To make someone insensitive to violence and suffering.
War brutalises people. 战争使人丧失人性。
Years of abuse had brutalised him. 多年的虐待使他麻木不仁。
synonyms: desensitises, dehumanises, hardens
antonyms: humanises, sensitises, softens
v.
