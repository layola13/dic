monocyte:/ˈmɒnəsaɪt/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnəsaɪt/ (US)| n. |单核细胞|A type of white blood cell that is part of the immune system and can differentiate into macrophages or dendritic cells.|Monocytes play a crucial role in defending the body against infection. 单核细胞在保护身体免受感染方面起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms:  leukocyte, white blood cell|Antonyms:  None (specific antonyms for cell types are uncommon)|noun
