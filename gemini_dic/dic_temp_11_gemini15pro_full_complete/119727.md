midsummery: /ˌmɪdˈsʌməri/ | adj. |仲夏的，盛夏的，像仲夏的|Characteristic of midsummer; suggestive of midsummer. | The weather was wonderfully midsummery. 天气好得就像盛夏一样。|  The air had a midsummery feel. 空气中弥漫着盛夏的气息。| 近义词: summery, estival | 反义词: wintry, hibernal | 词性: 形容词
词源:  From midsummer + -y.  Midsummer comes from Middle English *mydsomer, from Old English midsumer.
