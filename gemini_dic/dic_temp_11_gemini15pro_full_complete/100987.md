invalidation:/ɪnˌvælɪˈdeɪʃən/| n.|否定，驳斥|The act of making something seem unimportant or wrong.|She felt an invalidation of her feelings when her concerns were dismissed. 当她的担忧被忽视时，她感到自己的感受被否定了。|近义词: rejection, denial, repudiation, dismissal|反义词: acceptance, acknowledgment, recognition|noun
