outbuying:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪɪŋ/
verb
超过…的购买力; 比…买得多
The act of buying more than someone else, especially of a particular product or type of product.
Big companies are outbuying smaller companies for market share.  大公司正在超过小公司购买市场份额。
Our competitors are outbuying us in the raw materials market. 我们的竞争对手在原材料市场上的购买力超过了我们。
Synonyms: outspending, surpassing
Antonyms: underspending, losing (in a buying competition)
