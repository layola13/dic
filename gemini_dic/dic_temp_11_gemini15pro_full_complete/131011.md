nonaudibility:/ˌnɒnɔːdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可闻性；听不见 |The nonaudibility of the signal made communication difficult. 信号的不可闻性使通讯变得困难。|The experiment demonstrated the nonaudibility of dog whistles to humans.  这个实验证明了狗哨对人类来说是听不见的。| 近义词：inaudibility, silence | 反义词：audibility, loudness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + audibility (from Latin audibilis, "able to be heard," from audire, "to hear")
