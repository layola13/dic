aciurgy: /ˈæsɪɜːrdʒi/
noun
缝合术，缝合外科
The surgical closing of a wound.
例句：Aciurgy played a crucial role in the development of modern surgery. 缝合术在现代外科的发展中起到了至关重要的作用。

例句：The doctor's skilled aciurgy ensured a minimal scar. 医生熟练的缝合技术确保了疤痕最小化。
近义词：suture, stitching
反义词：incision, wound opening
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀκή (akḗ, “point, edge”) + ἔργον (érgon, “work”).
