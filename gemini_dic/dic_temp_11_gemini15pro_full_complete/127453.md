nebulize:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪz/ | v. |雾化；喷雾|To convert (a liquid) into a fine spray. To administer (a drug) by nebulization.|The doctor nebulized the medication for the patient to inhale. 医生将药物雾化，以便病人吸入。|Synonyms: atomize, vaporize, spray |Antonyms: condense, solidify|verb

nebulizer:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪzər/ | n. |喷雾器；雾化器|A device that converts a liquid into a fine spray, especially for medicinal purposes.|The patient used a nebulizer to inhale the prescribed medication. 病人使用喷雾器吸入处方药。|Synonyms: atomizer, vaporizer, sprayer |Antonyms: N/A|noun
