arteritis:/ɑːrtəˈraɪtɪs/
n.
动脉炎
Inflammation of the arteries.
Giant cell arteritis can cause headaches, jaw pain, and vision loss. 巨细胞动脉炎会导致头痛、下巴疼痛和视力下降。
Arteritis can lead to serious complications if left untreated. 如果不进行治疗，动脉炎会导致严重的并发症。
近义词:vasculitis
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a disease condition)
词性:noun
