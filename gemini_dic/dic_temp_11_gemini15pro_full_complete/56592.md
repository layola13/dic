/ˈdoʊnəˌteri/ | /ˈdɒnətri/
n.
受赠者；受赠机构
A person or organization to whom something is given or donated.
The donatary of the estate will be the local art museum.  这笔遗产的受赠者将是当地的艺术博物馆。
beneficiary, recipient, grantee
donor, grantor
noun
