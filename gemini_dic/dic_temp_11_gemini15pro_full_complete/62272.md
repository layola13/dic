encyclopedic:/ˌɪnsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ /ˌɛnsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/
adjective
百科全书式的；知识渊博的
covering a wide range of knowledge, like an encyclopedia
例句：He has an encyclopedic knowledge of wine. 他对葡萄酒的知识非常渊博。

例句：She gave an encyclopedic account of the history of the region. 她对该地区的历史做了百科全书式的描述。


近义词：comprehensive, exhaustive, thorough, wide-ranging
反义词：limited, narrow, superficial
词性：adjective
