goer:/ˈɡəʊər (UK) / ˈɡoʊər (US)| n. |常客；爱好者；参加者|A person who regularly attends a particular place or event; someone who enjoys a particular activity.|例句：He's a regular goer to the opera. 他是歌剧院的常客。|例句：She's a keen theatre-goer. 她是一位热衷于戏剧的观众。|近义词：attender, frequenter, participant, enthusiast, fan |反义词:stay-at-home, homebody |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "go" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).
