overbakes:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪks/ /ˌəʊvəˈbeɪks/| v. |烤过头，烘过头|To bake (something) for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.|She overbaked the cake, and it was dry. 她把蛋糕烤过头了，烤干了。|The cookies were slightly overbaked.  饼干烤得有点过了。|近义词：burn, scorch|反义词：underbake|词性:动词
