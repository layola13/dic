coned:/koʊnd/
| adj.|锥形的；圆锥状的；做成锥形的；【俚】醉醺醺的|Shaped like a cone; intoxicated, especially from smoking marijuana.|The mountain had a perfectly coned peak. 那座山有一个完美的锥形山峰。|近义词：conical, cone-shaped, tapered, inebriated, high|反义词:flat, sober|词性:形容词
coned:/koʊnd/
| v. (coned, coning)|使成锥形；用锥形物围住；【俚】（因交通违规）在（车辆）周围放置交通锥|To make something into the shape of a cone; to place traffic cones around a vehicle.|Police coned off the accident area. 警察用锥形物围住了事故区域。|近义词：taper, enclose, barricade|反义词:expand, open|词性:动词
