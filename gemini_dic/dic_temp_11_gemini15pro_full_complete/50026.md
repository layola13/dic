denominative:/dɪˈnɒmɪnətɪv/
denominative:| adj.|由名词派生的，从名词而来的|Derived from a noun; formed from a noun.|例句：A denominative verb is a verb formed from a noun. 由名词派生的动词就是从名词构成的动词。|近义词：nominal, nounal|反义词:|adjective
denominative:/dɪˈnɒmɪnətɪv/
denominative:| n.|由名词派生的词|A word derived from a noun.|例句：The word "governmental" is a denominative from the noun "government". “governmental”一词是从名词“government”派生出来的。|近义词：nominal, nounal|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēnōminātīvus*, from Latin dēnōmināre (“to name, designate”), from dē (“from, down from”) + nōmināre (“to name”), from nōmen (“name”).
