nonsynchronical: /ˌnɒnˈsɪŋkrənɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪŋkrənɪkəl/
adjective
非同步的，异步的
Not occurring at the same time; not synchronized.
The nonsynchronical flashing of the lights created a disorienting effect.  灯光不同步的闪烁造成了令人迷惑的效果。
Data transfer between the two devices was nonsynchronical, leading to errors. 两个设备之间的数据传输是异步的，导致了错误。
近义词: asynchronous, nonsimultaneous
反义词: synchronous, simultaneous
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + synchronical (adjective meaning "occurring at the same time")
