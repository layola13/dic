childishnesses:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
幼稚的行为；幼稚的言行
The plural of childishness, referring to multiple instances of behaving in an immature or silly way.
His childishnesses are becoming increasingly irritating. 他的幼稚行为越来越令人恼火。
Her constant childishnesses were embarrassing to her family. 她不断的幼稚行为让她的家人感到尴尬。
近义词：immaturities, puerilities, sillinesses
反义词：maturities, sophistication
词性：名词


childishness:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃnəs/
n.
幼稚；孩子气
The quality of being childish; behaving in a silly or immature way.
He exhibited a surprising childishness for a man of his age.  对于他这个年纪的人来说，他表现出惊人的幼稚。
She couldn't believe the childishness of his argument. 她简直无法相信他的论点如此幼稚。
近义词：immaturity, puerility, silliness
反义词：maturity, sophistication
词性：名词
