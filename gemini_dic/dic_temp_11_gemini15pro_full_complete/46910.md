darkling:/ˈdɑːklɪŋ/ | adj. adv.|昏暗的，秘密的；在黑暗中|Growing or occurring in the dark; secret, obscure.|The owl flew through the darkling woods. 猫头鹰飞过昏暗的树林。|近义词：dark, shadowy, dim, obscure, clandestine|反义词：light, bright, open, public|词性：形容词/副词

darkling:/ˈdɑːklɪŋ/ | n.|夜行性昆虫|A nocturnal insect, especially a beetle.|The entomologist discovered a new species of darkling beetle. 昆虫学家发现了一种新的夜行甲虫。|近义词：insect, beetle|反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑 diurnal insect - 昼行性昆虫)|词性：名词

词源: From Old English *deorcung, from *deorc "dark".  The adverbial use predates the adjectival use.
