fascia:/ˈfæʃ(i)ə/ (US), /ˈfæʃə/ (UK)| n.|筋膜；饰带|A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.|The surgeon pointed to the fascia surrounding the heart. 外科医生指着心脏周围的筋膜。|Synonyms: covering, membrane, sheath|Antonyms: core, interior|Noun

fascia:/ˈfæʃiː/ (US), /ˈfæʃiː/ (UK)| n.| (尤指商店、房屋的)面板，饰带|The front fascia of the shop was painted bright red.  商店的正面面板被漆成了鲜红色。|Synonyms: facade, face, front|Antonyms: back, rear|Noun
