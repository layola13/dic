cephalotripsy:/ˌsefəloʊˈtrɪpsi/ | n. | 碎颅术；碎头术 | The crushing of the fetal head in obstructed labor to facilitate delivery. |  Cephalotripsy was a last resort in difficult childbirth before the development of Cesarean sections.  在剖腹产出现之前，碎颅术是难产时的最后手段。 | 近义词: craniotomy, cranioclasis | 反义词: Cesarean section | noun
