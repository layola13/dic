lamingtons:/ˈlæmɪŋtənz/
noun
拉明顿蛋糕; 一种澳大利亚蛋糕
A lamington is a square of sponge cake coated in chocolate icing and rolled in desiccated coconut.
例句：Lamingtons are a popular treat in Australia.  拉明顿蛋糕在澳大利亚很受欢迎。

例句：We had lamingtons for afternoon tea. 我们下午茶吃了拉明顿蛋糕。

近义词：sponge cake, dessert
反义词: None (Lamingtons don't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Lord Lamington (Charles Cochrane-Baillie, 2nd Baron Lamington), Governor of Queensland, Australia, from 1896 to 1901. The exact origin is debated, but it's likely that the cake was created by his chef either accidentally or to use up leftover sponge cake.
