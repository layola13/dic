fortissimi:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪmiː/
adjective
极强音的；最强音的
Extremely loud; as loud as possible.
The fortissimi chords at the end of the symphony were truly breathtaking.  交响曲结尾的极强音和弦真是令人叹为观止。
近义词:  loudest, very loud
反义词: pianissimo, quietest
词性: adjective

fortissimi:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪmiː/
adverb
极强地；最强地
In an extremely loud manner; as loudly as possible.
The conductor signaled the orchestra to play the final passage fortissimi. 指挥示意乐队以最强音演奏最后一段。
近义词: very loudly, extremely loudly
反义词: pianissimo, very quietly
词性: adverb


词源:
From Italian, superlative of *forte* ("loud").  来自意大利语，*forte*（响亮）的最高级。
