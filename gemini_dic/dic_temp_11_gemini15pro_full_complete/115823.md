marketability:/ˌmɑːrkɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. |适销性；市场性 |The ease with which something can be sold. This depends on factors such as consumer demand, quality of the product, and competition.| This new product has high marketability due to its innovative features.  这款新产品由于其创新特性而具有很高的市场性。|Synonyms: salability, vendability| Antonyms: unmarketability, unsalability| noun
