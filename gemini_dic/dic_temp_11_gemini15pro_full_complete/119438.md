microlevel:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌlɛvəl/| n. |微观层面；微观水平|Relating to or involving analysis or observation at a small or detailed scale.  Focusing on individual or small-group interactions rather than large-scale patterns.|The sociologist conducted a microlevel study of family dynamics in a single community.|Synonyms: small-scale, detailed, localized, individual|Antonyms: macrolevel, large-scale, global, societal|noun
