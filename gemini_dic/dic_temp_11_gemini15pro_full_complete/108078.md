laterite:/ˈleɪtəraɪt/| n.|红土，砖红壤|A reddish clayey soil, rich in iron and aluminum oxides and formed in tropical and subtropical regions by the weathering of underlying rocks.|The ancient temple was built on a foundation of laterite. 这座古老的寺庙建在红土基岩上。|Laterite is a common building material in some parts of the world. 在世界某些地方，红土是常见的建筑材料。|近义词:clay, soil|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider bedrock if referring to underlying material)|Noun
