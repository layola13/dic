geochemistry:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪstri/ | n. |地球化学|The study of the chemical composition of the Earth and other planets, including the distribution and cycles of chemical elements and their isotopes.|例句：Geochemistry plays a crucial role in understanding the Earth's formation. 地球化学在理解地球的形成过程中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：He specialized in the geochemistry of volcanic rocks. 他专门研究火山岩的地球化学。|近义词：chemical geology|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- "earth" + chemistry.  "Geo-" comes from the Greek word "gē" (γῆ) meaning "earth".
