child-proofed:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːft/| adj.|防儿童的，对儿童安全的|Designed to prevent children from opening or using something potentially dangerous.  This ensures the safety of young children in the home.|例句：We child-proofed the house before our baby started crawling.  我们在宝宝开始爬行之前就对房子进行了儿童安全防护。

例句：The medicine bottle is child-proofed.  药瓶是防儿童开启的。|近义词：child-resistant, child-safe, tamper-proof|反义词:unsafe, accessible|词性:形容词
