heliocentric:/ˌhiːliəˈsɛntrɪk/ /ˌhiːlioʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. |日心的，以太阳为中心的|Relating to the heliocentric theory, which places the sun at the center of the solar system.|例句：The heliocentric model of the solar system eventually replaced the geocentric model.  日心说太阳系模型最终取代了地心说模型。

例句：Copernicus proposed a heliocentric theory of the cosmos. 哥白尼提出了宇宙的日心说。|近义词：solar-centric|反义词：geocentric|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek hēlios "sun" + Latin centrum "center."  来自希腊语 hēlios “太阳” + 拉丁语 centrum “中心”。
