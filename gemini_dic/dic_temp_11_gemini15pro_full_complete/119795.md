migrational:/maɪˈɡreɪʃənəl/
|adj.|迁徙的，迁移的|Relating to migration, the movement from one region to another.|The scientists tracked the migrational patterns of the monarch butterflies. 科学家们追踪了帝王蝶的迁徙模式。|例句：The researchers are studying the migrational behavior of birds. 研究人员正在研究鸟类的迁徙行为。|近义词：migratory, mobile, nomadic|反义词：settled, stationary, resident|词性:形容词

migrational:/maɪˈɡreɪʃənəl/
|n.|迁移|The act of migrating.|The annual migrational of the wildebeest is a spectacular sight. 角马的年度迁徙是一道壮观的景象。|例句：The salmon's migrational journey is incredibly arduous.  鲑鱼的迁徙旅程异常艰辛。|近义词：migration, movement, relocation|反义词：settling, staying|词性:名词 (rarely used as a noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From migration + -al.  Migration comes from the Latin "migratus," the past participle of "migrare," meaning "to move from one place to another."  The suffix "-al" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characterized by."
