attendees:/əˌtenˈdiːz/
n.
参加者，出席者
People who are present at a meeting, event, etc.
例句：The attendees at the conference came from all over the world.  与会者来自世界各地。

例句：The attendees were asked to sign in upon arrival.  出席者被要求在到达时签到。

近义词：participants, audience, guests
反义词：absentees
词性：名词
