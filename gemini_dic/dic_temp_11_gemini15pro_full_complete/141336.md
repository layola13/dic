openwork:/ˈoʊpənˌwɜːrk/| n. adj.|镂空细工；透雕细工|Ornamental work, especially of metal or wood, consisting of a pattern of interlaced lines or bands with open spaces.|The silver locket featured delicate openwork.  这个银质小盒子上有着精美的镂空图案。|The railing was made of openwork iron.  栏杆是用镂空锻铁制成的。|近义词：filigree, tracery, latticework|反义词：solid|词性:名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From open + work.  Open referring to the spaces within the pattern, and work referring to the crafted nature of the piece.
