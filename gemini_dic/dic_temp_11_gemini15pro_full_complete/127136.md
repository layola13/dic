natrolite:/ˈneɪtrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 钠沸石 | A fibrous zeolite mineral, hydrated sodium aluminum silicate.  A tectosilicate mineral species belonging to the zeolite group. It is a hydrated sodium and aluminium silicate with the formula Na2Al2Si3O10·2H2O. | Natrolite crystals were found in the volcanic rock.  在火山岩中发现了钠沸石晶体。| 近义词: zeolite, mesolite, scolecite | 反义词: None | noun
