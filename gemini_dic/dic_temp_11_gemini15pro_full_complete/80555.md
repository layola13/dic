goodwife:/ˈɡʊdˌwaɪf/| n.|主婦，女主人，妻子 (古语，现已少用)|A married woman, especially a respectful term of address.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |例句：The goodwife bustled about the kitchen, preparing the evening meal.  女主人在厨房里忙着准备晚餐。

例句：In the old tale, the goodwife spun wool into yarn. 在古老的故事中，这位妻子把羊毛纺成纱线。|近义词：wife, spouse, matron (in some contexts)|反义词: husband (opposite gender), spinster (unmarried woman, archaic)|词性:noun
