mistakers:/mɪˈsteɪkərz/
n.
犯错者，出错者
People who make mistakes.
The team learned from the mistakers of the previous game and ultimately won the championship.  团队从前一场比赛的失误者身上吸取了教训，最终赢得了冠军。
Synonyms: blunderers, offenders, culprits
Antonyms: experts, professionals
