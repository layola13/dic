depositum:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəm/ | n. | 存款，押金，寄存物 |Something entrusted to the care of another; something given as security for the performance of a contract or the payment of a debt; something deposited, especially for safekeeping. |  The landlord required a depositum of one month's rent. 房东要求一个月的租金作为押金。| synonyms: deposit, security, pledge, bailment | antonyms: withdrawal, retrieval | noun

depositum:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəm/ | n. | 沉淀物 | A sediment or precipitate; matter that settles out of a liquid. | The depositum at the bottom of the beaker contained valuable minerals. 烧杯底部的沉淀物含有珍贵的矿物质。| synonyms: sediment, precipitate, residue | antonyms: solution, supernatant | noun
