depopulative:/ˌdiːpɒpjʊˈleɪtɪv/
adjective
减少人口的
Tending to reduce the population.
The Black Death was a depopulative force in medieval Europe. 黑死病是中世纪欧洲人口减少的一个因素。
depopulating, reducing
populative, increasing

depopulate:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪt/
verb
减少人口
To reduce the population of (a place).
War and famine can depopulate a region. 战争和饥荒会使一个地区人口减少。
