nonsuccessfully:/ˌnɑːnsəkˈsɛsfəli/ | adv. | 不成功地，失败地 | Not successfully; without achieving the desired outcome. | The project was nonsuccessfully launched due to technical issues.  由于技术问题，该项目启动失败。| Synonyms: unsuccessfully, fruitlessly, vainly, abortively | Antonyms: successfully, effectively, fruitfully | adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix): Latin origin, meaning "not"
successful (adjective): From Latin successus "advance, a coming up, succession," past participle stem of succedere "come close after, follow after," from sub "up to" + cedere "to go, yield."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
