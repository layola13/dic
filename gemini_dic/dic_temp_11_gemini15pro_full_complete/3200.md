affectationist:/əˌfɛkˈteɪʃənɪst/
n.|做作的人；装腔作势的人|A person who behaves in an artificial way to impress people.|Her constant use of overly formal language made her seem like an affectationist.|近义词：poser, phony, show-off|反义词：natural, genuine|词性: noun
Etymology: Late 16th century: from French affectation + -ist.
