infestation:/ˌɪnfeˈsteɪʃn/ | n. | 侵扰，感染；蔓延 |  The presence of an unusually large number of insects or animals in a place, typically so as to cause damage or disease. | The house was condemned because of a rat infestation. 这房子因为鼠患严重被宣布不适宜居住。| 近义词：swarm, plague, invasion | 反义词：absence, eradication | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin infestatio(n-), from infestare ‘attack, annoy’.
