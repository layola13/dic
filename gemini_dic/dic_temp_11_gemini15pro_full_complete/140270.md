offstage:/ˌɒfˈsteɪdʒ/ /ˌɔːfˈsteɪdʒ/| adj. adv.| 幕后的，台下的；在后台|  Away from the stage or area of performance; behind the scenes. | The actors were chatting offstage.  演员们在后台聊天。 |  She went offstage to change her costume. 她到后台去换服装。|近义词：backstage, behind the scenes|反义词：onstage|词性：形容词，副词

offstage:/ˌɒfˈsteɪdʒ/ /ˌɔːfˈsteɪdʒ/| adv.| 幕后地，台下地|  In a manner that is away from the stage or area of performance. | He spoke offstage to the director. 他在后台跟导演说话。 | The musicians tuned their instruments offstage. 乐手们在后台调音。|近义词：backstage|反义词：onstage|词性：副词
