diazepam:/daɪˈæzɪpæm/ /daɪˈæzəpæm/| n.|安定; 地西泮|A type of benzodiazepine, a tranquilizing muscle-relaxant drug used chiefly to relieve anxiety, muscle spasms, and convulsions and to control agitation caused by alcohol withdrawal. It is also used as a sedative before surgery or other medical procedures.|例句：The doctor prescribed diazepam to help with her anxiety. 医生开了地西泮来缓解她的焦虑。

例句：Diazepam is often used to treat insomnia. 地西泮常用于治疗失眠。|近义词：Valium, lorazepam, alprazolam|反义词: stimulant|词性:noun
