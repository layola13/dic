megacity:/ˈmɛɡəˌsɪti/ | n. | 大城市；特大城市 | A very large city, typically one with a population of over ten million people. |  Tokyo is a bustling megacity with a rich cultural heritage. 东京是一个繁华的大都市，拥有丰富的文化遗产。

Megacities face unique challenges in terms of infrastructure and resource management.  特大城市在基础设施和资源管理方面面临着独特的挑战。 | 近义词: metropolis, megalopolis | 反义词: village, hamlet | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mega- "large" + city.  Mega- comes from Greek megas "great."
