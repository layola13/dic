overdecorate: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪt/
verb
过度装饰
To decorate excessively; to adorn with too many ornaments.
The living room was overdecorated with knick-knacks and tinsel. 客厅里摆满了小摆设和金箔，装饰得太过火了。
She overdecorated the Christmas tree, and it looked cluttered. 她把圣诞树装饰得太过头了，看起来很杂乱。
