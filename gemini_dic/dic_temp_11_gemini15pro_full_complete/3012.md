aerenchyma:/ˌɛərˈɛŋkɪmə/ | n. | 通气组织 | A spongy tissue with large intercellular air spaces, found especially in many aquatic plants.  It allows for the exchange of gases between the shoot and the root. |  The aerenchyma in the stems of water lilies helps them float.  睡莲茎中的通气组织帮助它们漂浮。

The extensive aerenchyma in mangrove roots allows them to survive in anoxic mud. 红树林根系中广泛的通气组织使它们能够在缺氧的泥浆中生存。 | 近义词:  aeriferous tissue | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + ἔγχυμα (énchyma, "infusion," from ἐγχέω "pour in").
