holoblastic:/ˌhɒləˈblæstɪk/ | adj. |全裂的，完全卵裂的 |  Of, relating to, or undergoing holoblastic cleavage, in which the entire egg is divided into smaller cells.  This is typical of eggs with little yolk, such as those of mammals and many invertebrates. |  The sea urchin embryo undergoes holoblastic cleavage. 海胆胚胎进行全裂卵裂。 |  complete cleavage |  meroblastic | adjective
