muddler:/ˈmʌdlər/| n.| 攪拌棒，調酒棒，糊塗蟲 |A tool with a blunt, rounded end used for crushing ingredients, especially fruit, for cocktails. Also, a person who muddles things or makes them confused. | He used a muddler to crush the mint leaves for the mojito. 他用攪拌棒壓碎了做莫吉托的薄荷葉。|Synonyms: pestle, masher, stirrer|Antonyms: clarifier, organizer|noun


muddler:/ˈmʌdlər/| n.| 糊塗蟲，笨手笨腳的人 |A person who makes a mess of things or is confused and disorganized.| He's such a muddler, he always forgets where he puts his keys. 他真是個糊塗蟲，總是忘記把鑰匙放在哪裡。|Synonyms: bumbler, klutz, scatterbrain|Antonyms: expert, professional|noun
