nongutturally:/nɒnˈɡʌtərəli/
| adv. |非喉音地；非咽喉地|Not produced in the throat; not guttural.  Relating to speech sounds not articulated in the back of the mouth. |She spoke the words nongutturally, almost whispering. 她说话的声音不是从喉咙发出的，几乎是在低语。|近义词：smoothly, clearly, softly|反义词：gutturally, hoarsely|词性:副词

Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "guttural" (from Latin "guttur," meaning "throat") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
