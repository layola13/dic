affirmative:/əˈfɜːmətɪv/ | n.|肯定；赞成；肯定语|An affirmative statement or vote. |例句：The vote was overwhelmingly in the affirmative. 投票结果压倒性地赞成。|近义词：agreement, assent, approval|反义词:negative, dissent|noun
