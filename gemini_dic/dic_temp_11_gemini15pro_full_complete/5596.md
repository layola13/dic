alterative:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/ | adj. n. |替代的，供选择的；替代物，可供选择的事物 |A medicine or therapy that is meant to improve health but is not considered standard or conventional. As a noun, it refers to another option or choice.|Example:  He suggested acupuncture as an alterative therapy for pain relief. 他建议针灸作为缓解疼痛的替代疗法。

Example:  There are several alterative routes to the city center. 有几条通往市中心的替代路线。|Synonyms: substitute, replacement, option, choice |Antonyms: primary, main, principal, conventional, orthodox |adjective, noun
