ammonitoidea:/ˌæmənaɪˈtɔɪdiə/ | n. | 菊石亚纲 | An extinct subclass of cephalopod mollusks with a coiled, chambered shell, related to the modern nautilus.  | Example: Ammonitoidea fossils are common in Mesozoic rocks. 菊石亚纲化石在中生代岩石中很常见。 | Example: The study of Ammonitoidea helps us understand ancient marine ecosystems. 对菊石亚纲的研究有助于我们了解古代海洋生态系统。 | Synonym: ammonites, Ammonoidea | Antonym: N/A | noun
