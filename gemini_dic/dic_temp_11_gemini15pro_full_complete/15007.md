babels:/ˈbeɪblz/| n.|巴别塔，混乱的景象，语言的混杂|A scene of noise and confusion resembling the biblical Babel; a confused mixture of sounds or voices.|The construction site was a babels of shouting workers and heavy machinery.  工地上一片混乱，工人们大声喊叫，机器轰鸣。|近义词：confusion, chaos, disorder, pandemonium|反义词：order, harmony, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

Etymology: From Babel, the name of a city in the Book of Genesis where the building of a tower to heaven was interrupted by God, who caused the builders to speak different languages, thus creating confusion and preventing the completion of the tower.  Ultimately from Akkadian *bāb-ilim*, meaning "gate of God".
