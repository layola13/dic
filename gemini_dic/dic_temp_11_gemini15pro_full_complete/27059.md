calendulin: /kəˈlɛndjʊlɪn/
n.
钙调蛋白; 金盏花蛋白
A calcium-binding protein involved in regulating a variety of cellular activities.  It is found in all eukaryotic organisms and plays a crucial role in signal transduction pathways.
Calendulin plays a vital role in muscle contraction. 钙调蛋白在肌肉收缩中起着至关重要的作用。
Synonyms: calmodulin (This is essentially the same word, often used interchangeably)
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific protein)
