consolidated:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 加固的，合并的，统一的 |  Combined into a single, stronger, or more efficient whole. consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ | v. | 加固，合并，统一 | To combine into a single, stronger, or more efficient whole.
