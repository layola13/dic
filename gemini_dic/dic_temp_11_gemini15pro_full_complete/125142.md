multi-tasking:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskɪŋ/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæsking/ | n. | 多任务处理 | The simultaneous execution of more than one program or task by a single computer or operating system.  The ability of a person to do several things at the same time. |  She's excellent at multi-tasking and can handle multiple projects simultaneously. 她擅长多任务处理，可以同时处理多个项目。

He found multi-tasking during meetings to be incredibly distracting. 他发现在会议期间进行多任务处理会让人分心。 | 近义词：multiprocessing, concurrent processing | 反义词: single-tasking, serial processing | 词性: 名词

multi-task:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæsk/ | v. | 多任务处理 | To perform more than one task at the same time. | I tried to multi-task, but ended up doing neither job well. 我试着同时做多项任务，但结果两件事都没做好。

She multi-tasks effortlessly, switching between phone calls and emails without missing a beat. 她毫不费力地进行多任务处理，在电话和电子邮件之间切换自如。 | 近义词: juggle, manage multiple tasks | 反义词: focus, concentrate | 词性: 动词
