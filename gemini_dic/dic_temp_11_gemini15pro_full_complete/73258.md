foregoneness:/fɔːrˈɡɔːnnɪs/

A state of being foregone; inevitability.
The foregoneness of the outcome made the competition feel pointless.  比赛结果已成定局，使得比赛毫无意义。

反义词: uncertainty, hope, possibility
