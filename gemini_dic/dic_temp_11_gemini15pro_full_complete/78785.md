gillies:/ˈɡɪliz/| n.|矿工助手；伙伴；诡计；(苏格兰)峡谷|A gillie is a person who acts as an attendant to a sportsman, especially one who fishes or hunts. gillie:/ˈɡɪli/| n.|伪装；伪装物；吉利服|Camouflage clothing, often used in military or hunting contexts.|The sniper wore a gillie suit to blend in with the surroundings. 狙击手穿了一套吉利服，与周围环境融为一体。|近义词：camouflage, disguise|反义词: exposure|词性:noun
