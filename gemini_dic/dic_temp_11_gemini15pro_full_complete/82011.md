gripingly:/ˈɡraɪpɪŋli/
| adv. |吝啬地，抱怨地|In a complaining or miserly manner; stingily. | He gripingly handed over the money. 他抱怨着交出了钱。|Synonyms: stingily, meanly, parsimoniously, grudgingly | Antonyms: generously, liberally, freely| adverb

gripingly:/ˈɡraɪpɪŋli/
| adv. |抓紧地，牢固地|In a firm and tenacious manner.  (Less common usage) |He held onto the rope gripingly, refusing to let go. 他紧紧地抓住绳子，不肯放手。|Synonyms: firmly, tightly, tenaciously | Antonyms: loosely, weakly | adverb
