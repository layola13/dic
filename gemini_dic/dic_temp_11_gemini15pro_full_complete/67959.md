facilitate:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/
v.
使容易；促进；帮助
to make an action or process possible or easier
The new ramp will facilitate the entry of wheelchairs.  这个新坡道将方便轮椅进入。
近义词：aid, assist, ease, expedite, further, help, promote, simplify, speed up
反义词：hamper, hinder, impede, obstruct
词性：动词
