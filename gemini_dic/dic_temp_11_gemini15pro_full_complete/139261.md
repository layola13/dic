oblige: /əˈblaɪdʒ/
verb
迫使；施恩惠于；答应请求
to require someone to do something for legal, social, or moral reasons; to do something as a favor for someone
例句：The law obliges companies to pay decent wages to their employees. 法律迫使公司向其员工支付体面的工资。

例句：I'm much obliged to you for your help. 非常感谢你的帮助。

近义词：compel, force, require, bind, favor, accommodate
反义词：disoblige
词性：v.
