mufflered:/ˈmʌfl̩d/ (US), /ˈmʌfəld/ (UK)| adj.| 闷住的，低沉的| Suppressed or muffled, as a sound.  Reduced in volume or clarity. | The muffled sound of the drums came from the next room.  从隔壁房间传来低沉的鼓声。| The singer's voice was muffled by the thick scarf.  歌手的声音被厚厚的围巾闷住了。|近义词：subdued, hushed, muted, dampened|反义词：loud, clear, distinct|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "muffle" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Muffle" comes from the Old French "mofler" meaning "to wrap up, muffle, conceal".
