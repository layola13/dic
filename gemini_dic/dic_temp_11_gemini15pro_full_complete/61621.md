emigrants: /ˈemɪɡrənts/ | n. | 移民（移出者）| People who leave their own country to live permanently in another. | Many emigrants left Ireland during the potato famine. 许多爱尔兰人在马铃薯饥荒期间离开了爱尔兰。|  synonyms: migrants, expats | antonyms: immigrants, natives | noun

emigrant: /ˈemɪɡrənt/ | adj. | 移居的，移民的 | Relating to emigration. | The emigrant ship was overcrowded and dangerous.  这艘移民船拥挤不堪，十分危险。 | synonyms: migrating, outbound | antonyms: immigrant, inbound | adjective
