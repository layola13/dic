creamer:/ˈkriːmər/
n.
奶精，奶油壶
A small container or pitcher for holding and serving cream or milk, often used for coffee or tea.  Also refers to the powdered or liquid non-dairy creamer used as a substitute for cream or milk.
I prefer powdered creamer in my coffee. 我喜欢在咖啡里加奶精粉。
近义词:cream pitcher, milk jug
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "sugar bowl" if contrasting additions to coffee)
词性:noun
