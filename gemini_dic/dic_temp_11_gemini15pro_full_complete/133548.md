nonfindings:/ˌnɒnˈfaɪndɪŋz/
noun
未发现的情况；无发现；未发现的结果
The report detailed the team's nonfindings.  报告详细说明了团队的未发现情况。
The nonfindings of the study were just as important as the findings. 这项研究的未发现结果与发现结果同样重要。
Synonyms: lack of findings, null result, negative result
Antonyms: findings, discoveries, results
词性: 名词
