backtack: /ˈbækˌtæk/ | n. v. | 回针；倒缝；临时固定 | A stitch sewn backward to secure the end of a seam or for reinforcement; to sew such a stitch. | She backtacked the seam to prevent it from unraveling. 她回针缝合接缝以防止其散开。|  The tailor backtacked at the beginning and end of the seam. 裁缝在接缝的开头和结尾处回针。 | synonyms: backstitch, lock stitch | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "continue stitching" in a general sense) | noun, verb
