overrigidness:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪdnəs/
n.
过度僵硬；过度刻板
Excessive rigidity or stiffness, often in behavior or thinking.
Her overrigidness made it difficult for her to adapt to new situations. 她过度刻板，难以适应新环境。
近义词:inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness
反义词:flexibility, adaptability, malleability
词性:名词

overrigid:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪd/
adj.
过度僵硬的；过度刻板的
Excessively rigid or stiff, often in behavior or thinking.
His overrigid parenting style stifled his children's creativity. 他过度刻板的教育方式扼杀了孩子们的创造力。
近义词:inflexible, rigid, strict
反义词:flexible, adaptable, lenient
词性:形容词
