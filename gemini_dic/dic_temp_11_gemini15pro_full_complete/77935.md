genocides:/ˈdʒɛnəˌsaɪdz/
|n.|种族灭绝（的事件）,大屠杀|The systematic and widespread extermination or attempted extermination of a national, racial, religious, or ethnic group.|The Rwandan genocide was a horrific event in human history. 卢旺达种族大屠杀是人类历史上骇人听闻的事件。|Synonyms: massacre, extermination, mass murder, ethnic cleansing|Antonyms: preservation, protection, salvation|Noun
