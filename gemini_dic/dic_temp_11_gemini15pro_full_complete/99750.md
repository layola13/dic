intermeasuring:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʒərɪŋ/ | adj. | 互相衡量的，互相测量的 |  The act of measuring each other or things against each other.  This word implies a reciprocal relationship in measurement. |  The two companies were intermeasuring their market share to determine their relative positions. 这两家公司正在互相衡量它们的市场份额，以确定它们的相对地位。 |  comparing, correlating, cross-referencing | N/A | adjective
