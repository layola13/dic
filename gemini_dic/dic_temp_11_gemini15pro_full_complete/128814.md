nightlife: /ˈnaɪtlaɪf/ | n. | 夜生活 | Entertainment and social activities that take place at night, such as going to bars, clubs, or restaurants. |  Example: The city is famous for its vibrant nightlife.  这个城市以其充满活力的夜生活而闻名。

Example:  She enjoys the exciting nightlife of New York City. 她喜欢纽约市令人兴奋的夜生活。 | 近义词: after-dark entertainment, evening entertainment | 反义词: daytime activities | 词性: noun
