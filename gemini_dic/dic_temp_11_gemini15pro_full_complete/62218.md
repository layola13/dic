encrinal:/ɛnˈkraɪnəl/
adjective
海百合的；与海百合有关的
relating to or resembling a crinoid (sea lily).
The museum boasts an impressive collection of encrinal fossils.  博物馆拥有令人印象深刻的海百合化石收藏。
The delicate, branching structure is characteristic of encrinal remains.  这种精致的、分支的结构是海百合遗骸的特征。
近义词:crinoid, sea lily
反义词: None.
词性: adjective
词源: From French *encrinal*, from *encrine* (“crinoid”) + *-al*.
