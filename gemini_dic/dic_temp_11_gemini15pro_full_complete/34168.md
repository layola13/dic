clammed:/klæmd/
verb
闭嘴，沉默不语，(尤指因紧张或恐惧而) 蚌壳式地紧闭双唇, 拒绝说话
become silent suddenly, usually because of nervousness or fear
He clammed up when the police started asking questions. 当警察开始问问题时，他闭口不言了。
近义词: shut up, quieten down, fall silent
反义词: speak up, open up


clammed:/klæmd/
adjective
(非正式)紧张的，害怕的
(informal) nervous and afraid
He felt clammed up before the exam. 他在考试前感到很紧张。
近义词: nervous, anxious, apprehensive
反义词: relaxed, calm, composed
