overripen:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpən/| v. adj.|过熟的，熟过头的|Ripened beyond the desired or optimal point.|The overripened fruit was mushy and unpleasant to eat. 熟过头的水果很软烂，不好吃。|近义词：overmature, decayed, rotten|反义词:underripe, green|adjective
