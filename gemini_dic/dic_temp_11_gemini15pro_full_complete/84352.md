halftone:/ˈhæfˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈhɑːfˌtəʊn/ (UK)| n. adj.|半色调|A reproduction of a photograph or other image in which the various tones of gray or color are produced by variously sized dots of ink.|The newspaper photo was printed in halftone.|Synonyms: screen, mesh|Antonyms: continuous tone|Noun|

halftone:/ˈhæfˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈhɑːfˌtəʊn/ (UK)| adj.|半色调的|Relating to or using a halftone process.|The halftone image was less detailed than the original.|Synonyms: screened|Antonyms: continuous tone|Adjective|
