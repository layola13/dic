foumart:/ˈfuːmɑːrt/| n.|臭鼬，欧洲臭鼬，白鼬|A polecat, especially the European polecat (Mustela putorius).  A strong smelling, weasel-like mammal.|The foumart sprayed a foul-smelling liquid when threatened.|近义词：polecat, weasel, skunk|反义词:|n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fulmart, from Old French fulmartre (“polecat”), possibly from ful (“foul”) + martre (“marten”).
