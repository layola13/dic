illustrated:/ˈɪləstreɪtɪd/ | adj. |插图的，有插图的|Containing pictures, photographs, diagrams, etc.|The book is beautifully illustrated with color photographs.|Synonyms: pictured, visual, graphic|Antonyms: unillustrated, plain, text-only|adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *illustrat*, from Latin *illustrātus*, past participle of *illustrāre* (“to illuminate, brighten, make illustrious”), from *in-* + *lūstrāre* (“to illuminate”), from *lūster* (“light”).
