clicker:/ˈklɪkər/| n.|点击器；计数器；遥控器|A device that makes a clicking sound, often used for counting or signaling.  A hand-held device used to transmit a radio signal, as for remotely controlling a slide projector or changing television channels.|The teacher used a clicker to advance the slides during the presentation. 老师在演示过程中使用点击器来推进幻灯片。|synonyms: counter, remote, control|antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "manual control")|noun
