foreboder:/fɔːrˈboʊdər/ | n. |预兆者；预言者|Someone or something that forebodes or presages something, typically something unpleasant.|The raven is a classic foreboder of doom in literature.  乌鸦在文学作品中是厄运的经典预兆。|近义词：harbinger, omen, portent, presage|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

forebode:/fɔːrˈboʊd/ | v. |预示；预兆|To act as a warning or sign of a future event (usually unpleasant).|The dark clouds forebode a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。|近义词：presage, portend, augur, foreshadow|反义词: N/A |词性: verb
