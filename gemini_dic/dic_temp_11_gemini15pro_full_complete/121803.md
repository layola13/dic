misreferring:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/
verb
错误地提及；错误引用
incorrectly referring to something or someone; citing incorrectly
He was misreferring to the author in his essay. 他在他的论文中错误地引用了作者。
misquoting, misattributing, misnaming
correctly referring, correctly citing
