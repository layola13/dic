bejade:/bɪˈdʒeɪd/| vt.|使疲惫，使厌倦，使失去光泽|To tire or weary, especially with work or effort; to make dull or worn, as by constant use. |The long journey bejaded the travelers. 长途旅行使旅行者疲惫不堪。|Synonyms: weary, tire, exhaust, fatigue, enervate|Antonyms: invigorate, refresh, energize|verb
