browbeaters:/ˈbraʊˌbiːtərz/
noun
恐吓者，威逼者
People who intimidate or bully others into doing something through persistent criticism, threats, or other forms of coercion.
The browbeaters forced the shopkeeper to hand over the money. 那些恐吓者强迫店主交出钱。
synonyms: bullies, intimidators, coercers, oppressors
antonyms: protectors, defenders, supporters, encouragers
n.

browbeater:/ˈbraʊˌbiːtər/
noun
恐吓者，威逼者
A person who intimidates or bullies others into doing something through persistent criticism, threats, or other forms of coercion.
He's a real browbeater; he makes life miserable for his employees. 他真是个恶霸，把他的员工们的生活搞得苦不堪言。
synonyms: bully, intimidator, coercer, oppressor
antonyms: protector, defender, supporter, encourager
n.

browbeat:/ˈbraʊˌbiːt/
verb
威吓，恐吓
To intimidate or bully someone into doing something through persistent criticism, threats, or other forms of coercion.
Don't let them browbeat you into accepting a deal you're not comfortable with. 不要让他们威逼你接受你不舒服的交易。
synonyms: intimidate, bully, coerce, pressure, badger
antonyms: encourage, support, persuade gently
v.
