interdisciplinarity:/ˌɪntərdɪsəplɪˈnærɪti/ | n. |跨学科性，交叉学科性 |The quality or fact of involving or drawing on two or more branches of knowledge. |例句：Interdisciplinarity is becoming increasingly important in modern research.  跨学科性在现代研究中变得越来越重要。

例句：The project fostered interdisciplinarity by involving experts from various fields.  这个项目通过让来自不同领域的专家参与进来，促进了跨学科性。|近义词：cross-disciplinarity, multidisciplinarity |反义词: disciplinarity, specialization |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + disciplinary (relating to a particular field of study) + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state).  The word "disciplinary" ultimately derives from the Latin word "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge".
