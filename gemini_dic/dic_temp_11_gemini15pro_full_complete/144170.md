outshowing:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊɪŋ/
verb
胜过，优于，比...更出色
surpassing or exceeding in performance or appearance.
Our team is outshowing all the others in the league this year. 今年，我们队在联赛中胜过所有其他队伍。
He completely outshowed his rival in the debate. 在辩论中，他完全胜过了他的对手。

synonyms: outperforming, excelling, surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, outstripping, eclipsing, overshadowing
antonyms: underperforming, failing, lagging

词性: verb (动词)
