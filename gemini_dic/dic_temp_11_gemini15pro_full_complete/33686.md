cinnamons:/ˈsɪnəmənz/| n. |肉桂（尤指作为香料的肉桂粉或肉桂棒）|A spice obtained from the inner bark of several trees from the genus *Cinnamomum*, notably *Cinnamomum verum* (Ceylon cinnamon), *Cinnamomum cassia* (Chinese cinnamon), and *Cinnamomum loureiroi* (Saigon cinnamon). It is used in both sweet and savoury foods. |例句：I love the smell of cinnamons in apple pie. 我喜欢苹果派里的肉桂味。|近义词：cassia, spice |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a spice) |词性:noun

cinnamons:/ˈsɪnəmənz/| n. |肉桂色|A reddish-brown colour, like that of ground cinnamon. |例句：She wore a cinnamons sweater. 她穿着一件肉桂色的毛衣。|近义词：russet, auburn |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a color) |词性:noun
