neuromuscular:/ˌnjʊərəˈmʌskjələr/| adj.|神经肌肉的|Relating to both nerves and muscles.  Pertaining to the relationship between a nerve and a muscle.|例句：Neuromuscular disorders can cause weakness and fatigue. 神经肌肉疾病会导致虚弱和疲劳。|例句：The athlete suffered a neuromuscular injury.  这位运动员遭受了神经肌肉损伤。|近义词：myoneural|反义词:(No direct antonym)|词性:adjective

词源:
neuro- (from Greek *neuron* nerve) + muscular (from Latin *musculus* muscle)
