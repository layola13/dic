half-starving:/ˌhæf ˈstɑːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 半饥饿的，饿得慌的 | Extremely hungry; almost starving. |  The half-starving children begged for food.  半饥饿的孩子们乞讨食物。|  The refugees arrived at the camp half-starving and exhausted.  难民们到达营地时又饿又累。 | Near synonyms: ravenous, famished, peckish | Near antonyms: full, satiated | adjective

half-starving:/ˌhæf ˈstɑːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 非常缺乏的，急需的 | Severely lacking something; desperately in need of. | The library is half-starving for funds.  图书馆急需资金。| The company was half-starving for new talent. 公司非常缺乏新的人才。| Near synonyms: deprived, lacking, deficient | Near antonyms: abundant, replete | adjective
