interdependabilities:/ˌɪntərˌdɪpɛndəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |相互依存性，互相依赖性|The state or quality of being interdependent; a system of interdependencies.|The complex interdependabilities of the global economy make it vulnerable to shocks. 全球经济复杂的相互依存性使其易受冲击。|近义词：interdependencies, interrelationships, interconnectedness|反义词：independence, autonomy|词性:名词

词源:  From interdependent + -ability + -ies.  Interdependent comes from inter- (meaning "between, among") + dependent (meaning "relying on").
