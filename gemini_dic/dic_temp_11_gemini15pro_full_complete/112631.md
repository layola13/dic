low-paid:/ˌloʊˈpeɪd/ | adj. |低收入的，低工资的 |  Receiving a low salary; poorly paid. | Many low-paid workers struggle to make ends meet. 许多低收入的工人难以维持生计。 |  Low-income, underpaid | Highly-paid, well-paid | adjective

Etymology:
"low" (Old English "lah," meaning "not high, humble") + "paid" (past participle of "pay," from Old French "paier," meaning "to pacify, satisfy a debt")
