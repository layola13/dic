circumference: /səˈkʌmfərəns/ | n. | 圆周，周长 | The outer boundary of a circle or other round shape. | The circumference of the Earth is about 40,075 kilometers. 地球的周长大约是40075公里。

The circumference of the wheel is crucial for calculating its speed.  轮子的周长对于计算它的速度至关重要。

| 近义词：perimeter, periphery | 反义词: diameter, radius | 词性: noun


circumferences: /səˈkʌmfərənsɪz/ | n. | 圆周，周长（复数） | The outer boundaries of circles or other round shapes. |  We calculated the circumferences of several different sized balls. 我们计算了几个不同大小球的周长。

The circumferences of the tree trunks were measured to track their growth. 树干的周长被测量以跟踪它们的生长。


| 近义词：perimeters, peripheries | 反义词: diameters, radii | 词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *circumferentia, from Latin circumferens (present participle of circumferre “to carry around”), from circum (“around”) + ferre (“to carry”).
