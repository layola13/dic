hiker:/ˈhaɪkər/| n.|徒步旅行者；远足者|A person who goes for long walks in the country, especially for pleasure or exercise. |例句：The hikers enjoyed the scenic views from the mountaintop. 徒步旅行者们从山顶欣赏风景。

例句：Experienced hikers know the importance of carrying plenty of water. 有经验的徒步旅行者知道携带足够的水的重要性。|近义词：walker, rambler, trekker|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hike + -er. Hike (v.) meaning "to walk vigorously," possibly from dialectal English "hich" (to hop, limp).
