meet-and-greet:/ˈmiːt ən ɡriːt/
noun
见面会，见面招呼
an informal occasion for people to meet, typically a celebrity and their fans.
例句：The band held a meet-and-greet after the concert. 乐队在演唱会后举办了一场见面会。

例句：The author will be at a meet-and-greet signing copies of her new book.  这位作者将在见面会上签售她的新书。

近义词：reception, gathering, social
反义词:farewell
