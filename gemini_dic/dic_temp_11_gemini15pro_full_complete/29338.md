casting-out:/ˈkæstɪŋ aʊt/| n. |剔除，舍弃，驱逐|The act of discarding or rejecting something, or driving someone away. |例句：The casting-out of demons is a common theme in religious texts.  驱魔是宗教文本中的一个常见主题。

例句：The casting-out of the rebels from the city was swift and brutal. 将叛乱分子从城市中驱逐出去是迅速而残酷的。|近义词：expulsion, rejection, dismissal, banishment|反义词: acceptance, inclusion, welcoming|noun
