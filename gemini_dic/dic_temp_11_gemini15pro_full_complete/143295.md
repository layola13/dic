outdressed:/ˌaʊtˈdrest/
adjective
穿着胜过；打扮比…漂亮
dressed more elaborately or stylishly than.
She was completely outdressed by the other guests at the gala. 她被晚会上其他客人彻底比下去了。
近义词:outshone, eclipsed
反义词:outdone, overshadowed
词性:adjective
