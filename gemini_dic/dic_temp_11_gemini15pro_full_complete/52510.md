differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj. n.|差别的，微分的；差速器；微分|A differential is a small change or difference in amount, value, or size. In mathematics, a differential is an infinitesimal change in a variable.|The differential between the two bids was only $5.  两个投标价格之间的差额只有5美元。|synonyms: difference, distinction, disparity, variance|antonyms: similarity, resemblance|adjective

differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/| n.|差速器；微分；差别|A differential is a gear assembly that allows the wheels on one axle of a motor vehicle to rotate at different speeds, as when turning a corner.  In mathematics, a differential is an infinitesimal change in a variable. |The car's differential needs to be repaired.  这辆车的差速器需要修理。|synonyms: difference, distinction, disparity, variance|antonym: similarity, resemblance|noun
