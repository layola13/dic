misfocus:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəs/ | v. n. | 失焦，聚焦错误 | To focus incorrectly; to fail to focus correctly. | The photographer misfocused the camera, resulting in a blurry picture. 摄影师对焦不准，导致照片模糊不清。 | The camera misfocused, and the subject was out of focus. 相机失焦了，主体不在焦点上。 | 近义词: blur, defocus | 反义词: focus | 词性: 动词，名词
