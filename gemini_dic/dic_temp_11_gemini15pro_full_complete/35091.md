clotheslessness:/ˈkloʊðzləsnəs/ /ˈkləʊðzləsnəs/
n.
无衣; 赤身裸体; 衣不蔽体
The state of being without clothes; nakedness.
The refugees were suffering from hunger and clotheslessness. 难民们正遭受饥饿和衣不蔽体的痛苦。
