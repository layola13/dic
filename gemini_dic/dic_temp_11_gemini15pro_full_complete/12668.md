assembly: /əˈsɛmbli/ /əˈsɛmbli/ | n. | 集会，会议；装配；配件；立法机构 | A group of people gathered together in one place for a common purpose. The process of putting parts together to make a whole. A collection of parts that make up a machine or structure. A legislative body. |  The school assembly is held every Monday morning. 学校集会每周一早上举行。 The assembly of the new furniture took several hours.  新家具的组装花了几个小时。| synonyms: gathering, meeting, congregation, convention, assemblage, construction, fitting | antonyms: dispersal, separation, dismantling | noun

assembly: /əˈsɛmbli/ /əˈsɛmbli/ | v. | 集合，聚集；装配 | To gather or bring together. To put parts together to make a whole. | The students assembled in the auditorium. 学生们在礼堂集合。  We assembled the bookcase following the instructions. 我们按照说明书组装了书柜。 | synonyms: gather, congregate, convene, collect, construct, build | antonyms: disperse, scatter, dismantle, disassemble | verb
