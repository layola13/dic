cofavorites:/ˈkoʊˈfeɪvərɪts/
noun
共同喜爱的事物/共同最喜欢的人
people or things that are equally liked by a group or individual.
My cofavorites for the award are both incredibly talented. 我最喜欢的两位获奖者都才华横溢。
synonyms: joint favorites, equal favorites
antonyms: least favorites, dislikes
词性:n.
