cornemuse:/kɔːr.nəˈmjuːz/ |n.|风笛|A bagpipe, especially a French or Breton bagpipe.  It is an instrument of the woodwind family, characterized by the continuous sound produced by air stored in a bag. |He played a lively tune on the cornemuse. 他用风笛演奏了一曲轻快的曲子。|Synonyms: bagpipe, pipes |Antonyms: None (It's a specific instrument, not a concept with an opposite.)|Noun
