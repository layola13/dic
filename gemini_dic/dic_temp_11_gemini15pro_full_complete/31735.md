cheaply:/ˈtʃiːpli/| adv.|便宜地，廉价地|At a low cost; inexpensively.|I bought this dress cheaply in the sales. 我在大减价时便宜地买了这条裙子。|近义词：inexpensively, affordably, economically|反义词:expensively, dearly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cēaplice (unattested), from cēap (cheap) +‎ -lice (-ly). Cognate with Dutch goedkoop (literally “good cheap”, “bargain”), German wohlfeil (“cheap”).
