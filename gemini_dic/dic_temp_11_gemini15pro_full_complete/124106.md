mortal:/ˈmɔːrtl/ (US) /ˈmɔːtl/ (UK)| adj. n.|终有一死的；凡人|Subject to death; a human being.|例句：All men are mortal. 所有的人都是凡人。|近义词：human, earthly, finite, perishable|反义词：immortal, eternal, divine|词性:形容词

mortal:/ˈmɔːrtl/ (US) /ˈmɔːtl/ (UK)| n.|凡人|A human being.|例句：He was a mere mortal facing a god. 他只是一个凡人，面对着一个神。|近义词：human, individual, person|反义词：god, deity|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mortel*, from Latin mortālis (“subject to death”), from mors (“death”).
