appeasingly:/əˈpiːzɪŋli/
adv.
抚慰地，劝慰地
in a way that is intended to make someone less angry or upset
He smiled appeasingly at his boss. 他向老板露出了讨好的笑容。

近义词:soothingly, placatingly, conciliatorily
反义词:provocatively, antagonistically, aggressively
词性:adverb

词源:From appease + -ing + -ly.  Appease comes from Old French *apaisier, from a- (from Latin ad 'to') + pais 'peace', from Latin pax.
