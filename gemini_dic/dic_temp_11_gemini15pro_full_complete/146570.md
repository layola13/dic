overgrateful: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtfəl/
adjective
过于感激的，感激过度的
excessively grateful; more grateful than is necessary or warranted.
She was overgrateful for his help, sending him thank you notes every day for a week. 她对他的帮助感激过分了，一周以来每天都给他寄感谢信。
I felt overgrateful for their understanding and support during a difficult time. 在困难时期，我对他们的理解和支持感到非常感激。
近义词: excessively grateful, too thankful, unduly appreciative
反义词: ungrateful, unappreciative
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "grateful" (adjective meaning "feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful")
