feyer:/ˈfeɪər/| n.|集市，庙会，义卖会|A feyer is a gathering of stalls and amusements, often held annually in a town or village.|例句：The annual village feyer was a great success.  一年一度的乡村庙会非常成功。

例句：We had a lot of fun at the school feyer.  我们在学校义卖会上玩得很开心。|近义词：fair, carnival, festival, bazaar|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle English *feire, from Old French feire, from Late Latin feria (“holiday, rest day”), from Latin feriae (“holidays”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (“to carry, bear”). Related to the word "fair".
