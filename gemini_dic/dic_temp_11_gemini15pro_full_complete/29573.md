catchweight:/ˈkætʃˌweɪt/| n. |约定重量，中间重量|A weight agreed upon for a boxing or other sporting contest, especially one between the established weight classes.  A non-standard weight for a product.|例句：The fight was set at a catchweight of 165 pounds. 这场比赛的约定重量是165磅。

例句：They sell flour in catchweight bags, ranging from 2 to 5 kg. 他们出售2至5公斤不等的散装面粉。|近义词：agreed weight, stipulated weight|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "standard weight")|词性:noun
