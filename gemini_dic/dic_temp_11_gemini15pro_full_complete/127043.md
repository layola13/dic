nastiest:/ˈnæstiɪst/ | adj. | 最讨厌的；最污秽的；最恶劣的 | superlative adjective of nasty: most unpleasant, unkind, or offensive.  It describes something extremely unpleasant or disagreeable in a severe or intense way. | That was the nastiest thing I've ever heard anyone say. 那是我听过的最恶毒的话。 |  Example: The nastiest storm in decades hit the coastal region.  几十年来最严重的暴风雨袭击了沿海地区。| Synonyms: vilest, meanest, worst, most unpleasant, most disagreeable | Antonyms: nicest, kindest, best, most pleasant, most agreeable | adjective
