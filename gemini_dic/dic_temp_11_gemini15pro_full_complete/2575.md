admixture:/ˈædˌmɪkstʃər/ |n.|混合物；掺杂物| something that is added to another substance, usually in small amounts, to improve it or give it particular qualities. |The concrete is an admixture of cement, sand, and gravel.|近义词: blend, mixture, compound, combination |反义词:  pure substance, element |词性: noun

admixture:/ˈædˌmɪkstʃər/ |n.|混合；掺杂（的行为）| the act of adding something to another substance. |The admixture of foreign substances can alter the properties of the material.|近义词: blending, mixing, compounding, combining |反义词: separation, purification |词性: noun


词源: From Late Middle English *admixtour, from Latin admixtūra (“a mixing in, admixture”), from admixtus, perfect passive participle of admiscēre (“to mix in, mingle”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + miscēre (“to mix”).
