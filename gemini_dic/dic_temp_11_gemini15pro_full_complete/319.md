abetter:/əˈbɛtər/ | n.|  煽动者，教唆犯 | someone who encourages or helps another person to do something wrong or illegal |  He was accused of being an abetter to the crime.  他被指控为这起犯罪的教唆犯。|  instigator, accomplice | opponent, adversary | noun

abetter:/əˈbɛtər/ | v.|  教唆，煽动 | to encourage or assist (someone) to do something wrong or unlawful | He abetted his friend in stealing the car. 他教唆他的朋友偷车。 |  instigate, incite | dissuade, deter | verb
