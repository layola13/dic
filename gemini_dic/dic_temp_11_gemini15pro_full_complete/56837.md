dorothy:/ˈdɔːrəθi/ | n. |多萝西|A female given name, derived from the Greek Δωροθέα (Dōrothéa), meaning "gift of God".  It gained popularity after the publication of L. Frank Baum's "The Wonderful Wizard of Oz".|Dorothy was a kind and brave girl. 多萝西是一个善良勇敢的女孩。|Example: My grandmother's name is Dorothy. 我的祖母的名字是多萝西。|Synonyms: Dora, Thea, Dolly|Antonyms: N/A|noun
