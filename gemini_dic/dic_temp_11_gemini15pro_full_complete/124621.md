mousseline:/ˌmuːsəˈliːn/ /ˌmuːslɪn/
n.
薄纱,平纹细布;奶油冻;果酱
A fine, sheer cotton fabric. A light, airy dessert or sauce, often made with whipped cream or egg whites.
例句：She wore a dress of white mousseline. 她穿了一件白色薄纱连衣裙。

例句：The chef prepared a delicate salmon mousseline.  厨师准备了一份精致的三文鱼奶油冻。

近义词：chiffon, voile, gauze (for fabric); cream, custard, purée (for food)
反义词：(No direct antonym, depends on the context.  For fabric, perhaps "coarse cloth".  For food, perhaps "heavy", "dense")
词性:名词

mousseline:/ˌmuːsəˈliːn/ /ˌmuːslɪn/
adj.
薄纱的;奶油冻的
Made of mousseline fabric; having the texture of a mousseline (food).
例句：The mousseline curtains billowed in the breeze. 薄纱窗帘在微风中飘动。

例句：The mousseline sauce was light and flavorful. 奶油冻酱汁清淡可口。

近义词：sheer, gauzy (for fabric); creamy, fluffy (for food)
反义词：(No direct antonym, depends on the context.  For fabric, perhaps "thick".  For food, perhaps "lumpy")
词性:形容词
