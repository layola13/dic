combats:/ˈkɒmbæts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbæts/ (US)| n. v.|战斗，搏斗；抗击|Struggles or fights, especially between armed forces;  actively opposes or struggles against.|The two knights engaged in fierce combats. 两位骑士进行了激烈的战斗。|近义词：battles, fights, struggles, opposes, resists|反义词: peace, harmony, surrender, yields|词性: noun (plural), verb (third person singular present)

combats:/ˈkɒmbæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbæt/ (US)| v.|战斗，搏斗；抗击|To struggle or fight against; to actively oppose.|The firefighters combated the blaze for hours. 消防员奋战数小时扑灭大火。|近义词：battles, fights, struggles, opposes, resists|反义词: peace, harmony, surrender, yields|词性: verb

词源: From Old French *combattre*, from *com-* (together) + *battre* (to beat). Ultimately from Latin *battuere*.
