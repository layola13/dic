niobium:/naɪˈoʊbiəm/ | n. |铌|A chemical element, a soft, grey, ductile transition metal, which is used in alloys, high-strength low-alloy steels, and superconducting magnets. |Niobium is used in the production of certain types of stainless steel. 铌用于生产某些类型的不锈钢。

Niobium alloys are used in aerospace applications. 铌合金用于航空航天应用。|近义词: columbium (older name)|反义词:  None (as it's a specific element)|词性: noun
