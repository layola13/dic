crucialities:/kruːʃiˈælɪtiz/
|n.|关键之处；重要性；至关重要的事情|The crucialities of the situation demand immediate action.  形势的关键之处需要立即采取行动。|English explanation: The plural form of "cruciality," referring to the essential or decisive aspects of something.  It highlights the elements that are of utmost importance and have a significant impact on the outcome.|例句：Identifying the crucialities of the project is the first step to success. 确定项目的关键是成功的第一步。

例句：The report outlined the crucialities of the upcoming negotiations.  报告概述了即将举行的谈判的关键之处。

|近义词：essentials, key factors, vital points, core issues|反义词:insignificances, trivialities|词性:名词
