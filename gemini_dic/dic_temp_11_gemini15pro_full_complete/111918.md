loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 闲逛，徘徊，游荡 | To stand or wait around idly or without apparent purpose. |  A group of teenagers were loitering outside the shop. 一群青少年在商店外面闲逛。

He loitered in the doorway, hoping to catch a glimpse of her. 他在门口徘徊，希望能瞥见她一眼。


|近义词： linger, dawdle, hang around, mill about |反义词: hurry, rush |词性: verb
