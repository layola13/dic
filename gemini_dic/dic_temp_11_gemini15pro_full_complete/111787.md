loftier:/ˈlɒftiə(r) (UK), ˈlɔːftiər (US)| adj.|更高贵的；更高傲的；更崇高的|Comparative form of lofty: more lofty. Having or displaying great dignity or nobility; of imposing height; extending high in the air; of high moral or intellectual value; elevated in thought or style. |例句：He had loftier ambitions than to become a mere shopkeeper. 他有比仅仅成为一个店主更高的抱负。

例句：She spoke of loftier ideals than mere material possessions. 她谈论的是比单纯的物质财富更高尚的理想。|近义词：noble, exalted, elevated, grand, majestic, superior, haughty, arrogant|反义词：humble, lowly, base, mean, ignoble, modest|词性: adjective
