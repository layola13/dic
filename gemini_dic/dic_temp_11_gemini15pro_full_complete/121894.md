missampled:/ˌmɪsˈsæmpəld/
adjective|错误采样的，采样不当的|gathered or selected incorrectly, usually in statistical sampling, leading to an unrepresentative sample.|The missampled data skewed the results of the survey. 错误采样的数据使调查结果出现了偏差。|The study's conclusions were flawed due to a missampled population.  由于人口抽样不当，该研究的结论存在缺陷。|inaccurately sampled, biased sample|representative sample|adjective
