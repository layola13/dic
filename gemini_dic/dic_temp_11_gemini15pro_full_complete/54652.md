disobediency:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiəns/ /ˌdɪsoʊˈbiːdiəns/| n. |不服从，违抗|The child's disobedience led to punishment.  这个孩子的抗命导致了惩罚。|Disobedience of the law can result in serious consequences. 不遵守法律可能会导致严重的后果。|近义词：noncompliance, defiance, insubordination|反义词: obedience, compliance, submission|词性:名词
disobey:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/ /ˌdɪsoʊˈbeɪ/| v. |不服从，违抗|The soldier disobeyed a direct order.  这个士兵违抗了直接命令。|He chose to disobey his parents and go to the party. 他选择违背父母的意愿去参加派对。|近义词：defy, resist, ignore|反义词: obey, comply, submit|词性:动词
