enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/| vt.|启发，启迪，开导|To give someone greater knowledge or understanding of a subject or situation.|The teacher enlightened the students about the history of the war. 老师向学生们讲解了战争的历史。|近义词：educate, inform, illuminate|反义词：confuse, mislead, obscure|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + light, meaning "to shed light on," figuratively meaning "to give intellectual or spiritual light to."  The "en-" prefix is a variant of "in-" used before "l."
