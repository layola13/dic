outdistanced:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstənst/
verb
超越，把…远远抛在后面
to leave someone or something far behind, especially by going faster
The Kenyan runner easily outdistanced his rivals in the marathon.  肯尼亚选手在马拉松比赛中轻松超越了他的对手。
近义词: outrun, surpass, exceed, outstrip
反义词: lag behind, trail
