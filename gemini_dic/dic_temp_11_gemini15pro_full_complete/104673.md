karoth: /ˈkærəθ/ | n. | carat; karat | A unit of weight for precious stones, equal to 200 milligrams (about 3 grains). Also, a measure of the purity of gold, with 24 karats being pure gold. |  Example: The diamond weighed three karots. 这颗钻石重三克拉。| Example:  My ring is 18-karat gold. 我的戒指是18K金的。 | Synonym: carat | Antonym: N/A | noun
