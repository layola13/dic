lindenbaum:/ˈlɪndənˌbaʊm/ | n. |椴树，菩提树|A deciduous tree of the genus Tilia, native to the Northern Hemisphere and having fragrant yellowish-white flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The linden tree is often planted as an ornamental and is known for its shade and the sweet scent of its blossoms.|  The lindenbaum in the town square was a popular gathering place. 城镇广场上的菩提树是一个受欢迎的聚会场所。|Synonym: lime tree, basswood | Antonym: N/A | noun

lindenbaum:/ˈlɪndənˌbaʊm/ | n. |椴树，菩提树|In German, "Lindenbaum" refers specifically to the linden or lime tree, which holds cultural significance in German folklore and literature, often symbolizing love, community, and nostalgia.  It's notably featured in Schubert's "Winterreise" song cycle. |  The song "Am Brunnen vor dem Tore" mentions a lindenbaum. 歌曲“城门前的喷泉”提到了菩提树。|Synonym: lime tree, basswood | Antonym: N/A | noun
