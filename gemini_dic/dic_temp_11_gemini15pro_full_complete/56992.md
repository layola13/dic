double-deckers:/ˈdʌbəlˌdɛkəz/| n.|双层公共汽车|A bus with two levels or decks.|例句：London is famous for its red double-deckers. 伦敦以其红色的双层巴士而闻名。

例句：We took a double-decker bus tour of the city. 我们乘坐双层巴士游览了这座城市。

近义词：double-decker bus|反义词:single-decker bus|词性:名词


double-decker:/ˈdʌbəlˌdɛkər/| adj.|双层的|Having two decks or levels.|例句：A double-decker sandwich is a filling meal.  双层三明治是一顿丰盛的餐点。

例句：He ordered a double-decker pizza with extra cheese. 他点了一个双层披萨，加了额外的奶酪。

近义词：two-tiered, two-story|反义词:single-decker, single-story|词性:形容词
