analyticity:/ˌænəˌlɪˈtɪsɪti/
n.|分析性|The property of being analytic.  A statement possessing analyticity is true by virtue of its meaning alone.|The analyticity of the statement "All bachelors are unmarried men" is evident.|近义词: demonstrability, provability|反义词: syntheticity|词性:noun

Etymology: From analytic + -ity.  Ultimately from Greek *analutikós* (analytic).
