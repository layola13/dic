anti-british:/ˌæntiˈbrɪtɪʃ/
adjective
反英的；仇视英国的
Opposed to or hostile towards Britain, its people, or its policies.
The anti-British sentiment grew stronger after the war.  战争结束后，反英情绪更加强烈。
The newspaper published several anti-British articles. 这家报纸发表了几篇反英文章。
Near Synonyms: anti-English, Anglophobic
Antonyms: pro-British, Anglophile
词性: adjective
