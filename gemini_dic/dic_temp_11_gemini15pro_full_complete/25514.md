bullishly:/ˈbʊlɪʃli/| adv.|看涨地，乐观地|In a confident and optimistic way, especially about financial markets; expecting prices to rise.  It suggests a strong belief in future success or positive outcomes. |The investor bullishly predicted a strong market rebound.  投资者乐观地预测市场将强劲反弹。|近义词：optimistically, confidently, hopefully|反义词:bearishly, pessimistically|词性:副词
bullish:/ˈbʊlɪʃ/| adj.|看涨的，乐观的|Feeling confident and optimistic about the future, especially in the financial markets; expecting prices to rise. |He's bullish about the company's prospects. 他对公司的前景很乐观。|近义词：optimistic, positive, confident|反义词:bearish, pessimistic|词性:形容词
