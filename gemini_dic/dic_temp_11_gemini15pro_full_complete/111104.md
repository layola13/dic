literacies:/ˈlɪtərəsiz/| n.| 读写能力，素养（复数）| The ability to read, write, and understand information in a particular context or contexts. Often used in the plural to acknowledge the multiple forms literacy can take, depending on cultural and societal contexts.|  The program aims to improve digital literacies among young people. 该项目旨在提高年轻人的数字素养。| Synonyms: competencies, skills, capabilities | Antonyms: illiteracy, ignorance| noun
