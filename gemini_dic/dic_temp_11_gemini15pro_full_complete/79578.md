gloomless:/ˈɡluːmləs/| adj.|无忧无虑的，快乐的|Free from gloom; cheerful; bright.|例句：The children played in the gloomless garden. 孩子们在阳光明媚的花园里玩耍。|近义词：cheerful, bright, sunny, lighthearted, merry, joyful, happy, carefree|反义词：gloomy, dark, sad, depressing, somber, melancholy|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From gloom + -less.  Gloom ultimately comes from the Proto-Germanic *glūmaz, meaning "twilight, dusk."
