address-to:/əˈdrɛs tuː/ | prep. | 致，寄给，指向 | To direct something (like a letter, speech, or remark) to a particular person or group. |  例句：The letter was addressed to the CEO. 这封信是寄给首席执行官的。

例句：She addressed her remarks to the graduating class. 她将讲话致辞献给毕业班。

近义词：direct to, send to,  | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "from") | 词性: 介词 (preposition)
