agrarianism:/əˈɡrɛəriənɪzəm/
|n.|农业主义；重农主义|A social or political philosophy which values rural society as superior to urban society, the independent farmer as superior to the paid worker, and sees farming as a way of life that can shape ideal social values.|The politician's speech evoked a strong sense of agrarianism, emphasizing the importance of supporting local farmers. 这位政治家的演讲唤起了人们强烈的农业主义意识，强调了支持当地农民的重要性。|Example:  The back-to-the-land movement is a modern manifestation of agrarianism.  回归土地运动是现代农业主义的一种表现形式。

|Example:  Thomas Jefferson was a strong proponent of agrarianism, believing that farmers were the backbone of the nation. 托马斯·杰斐逊是农业主义的坚定拥护者，他相信农民是国家的支柱。|近义词:ruralism, physiocracy|反义词:industrialism, urbanism|词性:名词
