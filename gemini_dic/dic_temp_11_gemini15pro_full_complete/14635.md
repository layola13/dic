avoidless:/əˈvɔɪdləs/
adj.
不可避免的
Unavoidable; inevitable.  Something that cannot be avoided.
例句：His fate seemed avoidless. 他的命运似乎不可避免。
例句：The avoidless consequence of her actions was dismissal. 她行为的不可避免的后果是被解雇。
近义词：inevitable, inescapable, unavoidable
反义词：avoidable, preventable
词性：形容词

词源：
From a- (not) + void (empty, null, useless) + -less (without).  Essentially meaning "without the ability to be voided or made null."
