overmoralizes: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过分道德化，过分说教
To moralize excessively; to impose or advocate moral standards too rigidly or frequently.
He overmoralizes everything, making even simple pleasures seem like sins.  他把每件事都过分道德化，即使是简单的快乐也像是罪过。
She tends to overmoralize about other people's choices. 她倾向于对别人的选择进行过分的说教。
Synonyms: preaches, sermonizes, pontificates
Antonyms: condones, overlooks, ignores
词性: verb (动词)
