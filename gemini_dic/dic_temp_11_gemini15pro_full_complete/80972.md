gracing: /ˈɡreɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 使优美；增添光彩；使增色；惠临；降临 | present participle of grace | Her presence graced the occasion. 她的出现使这个场合增色不少。 | 近义词: adorning, embellishing, honoring, attending | 反义词: disgracing, marring, blemishing | 词性: Verb

Etymology: From Middle French *gracier*, from grace + -ier. Ultimately from Latin gratia (“grace, favor, thanks”).
