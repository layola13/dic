guelphic:/ˈɡwɛlfɪk/ | adj. | 圭尔夫派的，支持教皇的 | Of or relating to the Guelphs, a medieval political faction supporting the papacy in a struggle against the Ghibellines, who supported the Holy Roman Emperor. |  The Guelphic party gained power in Florence during the 13th century. 圭尔夫党在13世纪获得了佛罗伦萨的权力。

The Guelphic and Ghibelline conflict shaped Italian politics for centuries. 圭尔夫派和吉伯林派的冲突塑造了几个世纪的意大利政治。| 近义词: papal, pro-papal | 反义词: Ghibelline, imperial | 词性: 形容词
