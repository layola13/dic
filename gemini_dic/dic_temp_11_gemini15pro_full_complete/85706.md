havildar:/ˈhɑːvɪldɑːr/ (UK), /ˈhɑːvəldɑːr/ (US)| n.|下士；准尉|A non-commissioned officer in the Indian army, equivalent in rank to a sergeant or staff sergeant.|The havildar barked orders at his men. 下士对着他的士兵大声吼叫。|近义词：sergeant, staff sergeant|反义词:private, recruit|词性:noun

Etymology: From Urdu हविलदार (havaldār), from Persian حوالدار (havāldār, “one in charge”), from حواله (havāle, “charge, custody”) + دار (-dār, “holder”).
