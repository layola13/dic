misrelated:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
错误相关的；不相关的；不协调的
incorrectly or inappropriately related; not connected in a logical or suitable way
The study misrelated cause and effect. 这项研究错误地关联了因果关系。
mismatched, unconnected, inappropriate
related, connected, relevant
adjective
