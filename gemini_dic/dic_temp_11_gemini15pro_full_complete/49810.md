demonstrative:/dɪˈmɒnstrətɪv/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːnstrətɪv/ (US) | adj. |指示的；公开表露感情的 |  serving to demonstrate or prove; openly expressive of emotions |  She is not a very demonstrative person. 她不是一个很外露的人。| expressive, affectionate, open | reserved, unemotional | adjective

demonstratively:/dɪˈmɒnstrətɪvli/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːnstrətɪvli/ (US) | adv. |指示地；证明地；公开表露感情地 | in a way that demonstrates or proves; in a way that openly expresses emotions | He demonstratively kissed his wife in front of everyone. 他当着所有人的面热情地吻了他的妻子。| openly, expressively, affectionately | reservedly, unemotionally | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *demonstrare*, from de- "fully" + monstrare "to show".
