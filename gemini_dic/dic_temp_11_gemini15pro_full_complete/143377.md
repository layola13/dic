outfence:/ˌaʊtˈfɛns/
|v.|胜过；以更好的围栏围住|To surpass or defeat someone in fencing or by building a better fence.  It can also more generally mean to outdo or surpass someone in a competitive situation, especially one involving strategy or cunning.|The neighboring farmer tried to outfence him by building a taller, more elaborate fence.  邻居农民试图通过建造一个更高、更精致的围栏来胜过他。|outdo, surpass, outmaneuver|be outdone by, lose to|verb

注意：outfence是一个相对不常用的词语，主要用于比喻意义上的竞争。在实际应用中，更常见的词语是outdo, surpass, outmaneuver等。它的词源来自"out-" (超过) 和 "fence" (围栏)，最初可能指通过建造更好的围栏来保护自己的牲畜或土地，后来引申为在竞争中胜过对手。
