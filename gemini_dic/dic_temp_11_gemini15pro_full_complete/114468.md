making-with:/ˈmeɪkɪŋ wɪð/ | n. | 共同创造，共同制作 | The philosophy of "making-with" emphasizes collaboration and shared ownership in the creative process.  "共同创造"的理念强调合作和在创作过程中共享所有权。 |  "Making-with" involves a reciprocal relationship between the maker and the materials. "共同创造"涉及创造者和材料之间的相互关系。 | collaboration, co-creation | individual creation | noun
