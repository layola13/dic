communalistic:/kəˌmjuːnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
集体主义的；公社主义的；地方主义的
Relating to communalism; emphasizing communal living.  Characterized by the principle of communal ownership.  Exhibiting strong attachment to one's own community, sometimes at the expense of other communities.
The communalistic lifestyle appealed to some, but others found it too restrictive.  这种集体主义的生活方式吸引了一些人，但也有人觉得它限制性太强。

The communalistic nature of the kibbutz provided a strong sense of community.  基布兹的集体主义性质提供了强烈的社区意识。

synonym: collectivist, communist, sectarian, parochial
antonym: individualistic, capitalist, cosmopolitan
