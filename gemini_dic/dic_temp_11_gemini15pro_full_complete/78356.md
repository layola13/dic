gestate:/ˈdʒɛsteɪt/ | v. | 孕育，酝酿，(想法、计划等)逐渐形成 | to carry a fetus in the uterus during pregnancy; to conceive and develop slowly | The company gestated the new product for over a year before releasing it.  这家公司在发布新产品之前酝酿了一年多。 | synonyms: develop, mature, incubate, brew | antonyms:  rush, hasten | verb

gestation:/dʒɛˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 妊娠，孕育，(想法、计划等)形成的过程 | the process of carrying or being carried in the womb between conception and birth; the development of something over a period of time | The gestation period for elephants is about 22 months. 大象的妊娠期约为22个月。|  synonyms: development, maturation, incubation | antonyms:  abortion, termination, cessation| noun
