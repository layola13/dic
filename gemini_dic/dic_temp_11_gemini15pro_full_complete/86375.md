heddling:/ˈhɛdlɪŋ/ | n. |综线; 穿综; 综框 | The act of threading yarn through the heddles of a loom.  The arrangement of threads in a loom. |  The weaver spent hours heddling the loom before beginning the intricate pattern.  织工在开始复杂的图案之前花了几个小时穿综。 | synonyms: warping, looming | antonyms: unweaving, dismantling | noun
