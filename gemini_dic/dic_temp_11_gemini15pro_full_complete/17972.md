befriended: /bɪˈfrɛndɪd/
verb
交朋友；与…成为朋友
To act as a friend to (someone), especially when they are in need of help or support.
She befriended the lonely old woman next door. 她和隔壁孤独的老妇人交了朋友。
近义词: make friends with, befriend
反义词: alienate, shun
词性: Verb (past tense and past participle of befriend)
