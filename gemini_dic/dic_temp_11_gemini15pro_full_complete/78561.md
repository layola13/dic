The movie featured ghoulish special effects that made the audience squirm. 这部电影以令人毛骨悚然的特效让观众感到不安。| 近义词：macabre, gruesome, morbid, ghastly, eerie | 反义词：pleasant, cheerful, comforting, wholesome | 词性: 形容词
