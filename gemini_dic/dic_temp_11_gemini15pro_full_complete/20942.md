bleed-white:/bliːd waɪt/| adj.|耗尽资源的，榨干的，精疲力竭的|Drained of resources, utterly exhausted, often financially.|The company was bled white by the endless lawsuits.|近义词：exhausted, drained, depleted, bankrupt|反义词:rich, affluent, prosperous|词性:形容词

词源:  "bleed" (流血) + "white" (白色).  The image evoked is that of something being drained of all its blood until it turns white, hence completely depleted.
