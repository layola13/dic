indemnifiers:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪərz/| n. |赔偿者，保障者|People or entities who agree to protect another party from financial loss or liability.|The contract clearly identifies the indemnitors and their responsibilities.|Synonyms: guarantors, surety, sponsors|Antonyms:  liable parties, defendants|noun

indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/| vt. |赔偿，保障|To compensate for loss or damage; to protect against future loss or damage.|The insurance company will indemnify you for the damage to your car.|Synonyms: compensate, reimburse, repay|Antonyms: penalize, fine, charge|verb
