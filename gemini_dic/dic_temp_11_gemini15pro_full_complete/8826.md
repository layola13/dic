antievolutionisms:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəmz/
n.
反对进化论
Doctrines or beliefs opposed to the theory of biological evolution.
The speaker addressed the various antievolutionisms prevalent in society.  演讲者谈到了社会上普遍存在的各种反进化论思想。
Antievolution sentiments have been around for centuries.  反进化论的情绪已经存在了几个世纪了。
近义词:creationism, intelligent design
反义词:evolutionism, Darwinism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to (from Greek)
evolution (n.): the gradual development of something, especially from a simple to a more complex form (from Latin evolvere "to unroll")
-ism (suffix) : a doctrine, belief, or theory (from Greek -ismos)
