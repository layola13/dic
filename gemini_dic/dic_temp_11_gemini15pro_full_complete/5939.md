amblingly:/ˈæmblɪŋli/| adv.|悠闲地，漫步地|In a leisurely, strolling manner.  Derived from "amble" which means to walk or move at a slow, relaxed pace.|He walked amblingly along the beach, enjoying the sunset. 他悠闲地在沙滩上漫步，欣赏着日落。|Near Synonyms: leisurely, slowly, casually, unhurriedly|Near Antonyms: quickly, rapidly, hurriedly, briskly|adverb
