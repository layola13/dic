overintensities:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪtiz/
|n.| 过度强度，过度增强|Areas of overintensities were observed on the MRI scan.|在MRI扫描中观察到过度增强区域。|
|英文解析:  Plural form of overintensity. Refers to areas of abnormally high signal intensity in medical imaging, particularly MRI.  Indicates regions of increased brightness on the image, which can be caused by various factors like inflammation, bleeding, or calcification.|
|例句：The overintensities on the MRI suggest areas of inflammation. MRI上的过度增强表明存在炎症区域。|
|例句：The radiologist noted several overintensities in the white matter. 放射科医生注意到白质中有几个过度增强区域。|
|近义词：hyperintensities|
|反义词：hypointensities|
|词性：noun (plural)|
