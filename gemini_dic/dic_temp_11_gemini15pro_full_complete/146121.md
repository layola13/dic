overemphasis:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəsɪs/| n.|过分强调，过分重视|An excessive amount of emphasis placed on something.|The media's overemphasis on the negative aspects of the story created unnecessary panic. 媒体对故事负面方面的过分强调造成了不必要的恐慌。|近义词：overstress, exaggeration|反义词:underemphasis, understatement|noun
