chinned:/tʃɪnd/| adj.| 有下巴的；坚毅的；| having a chin of a specified kind |He was a square-chinned, ruggedly handsome man. 他是一个方下巴的粗犷英俊的男人。|近义词：strong-jawed, determined |反义词: weak-chinned, irresolute |词性:形容词

词源: From chin + -ed.  Derived from the noun "chin," meaning the lower part of the face below the mouth. The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives, in this case indicating possession of a particular kind of chin.
