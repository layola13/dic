by-the-same-token:/baɪ ðə seɪm ˈtoʊkən/| adv.|同样地，出于同样的原因|Meaning "for the same reason; in the same way," this idiom emphasizes that a similar point or argument applies.|She’s very intelligent, and by the same token, she’s also very creative. 她很聪明，同样地，她也非常有创造力。

He’s a talented musician, and, by the same token, a gifted painter. 他是一位才华横溢的音乐家，同样也是一位天才画家。|近义词：similarly, likewise, in the same way, equally|反义词: conversely, on the other hand, in contrast|词性:副词
