frustule:/ˈfrʌstjuːl/
noun
硅藻细胞壁；硅藻壳
the hard and porous cell wall or external layer of a diatom.
Diatoms are single-celled algae that create intricate frustules from silica. 硅藻是单细胞藻类，它们用二氧化硅创造复杂的硅藻壳。
The delicate frustules of diatoms are often beautifully patterned.  硅藻的精致壳通常具有美丽的图案。
synonyms:  shell, casing, test (specifically for diatoms)
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific biological term.)
