indo-gangetic:/ˌɪndoʊ ɡænˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
印度河-恒河的
Relating to the plains of the Indus and Ganges rivers.
The Indo-Gangetic Plain is one of the most densely populated regions in the world. 印度河-恒河平原是世界上人口最稠密的地区之一。
The Indo-Gangetic region has a rich agricultural history. 印度河-恒河地区有着丰富的农业历史。
近义词: Gangetic, Indus Valley
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like non-Indo-Gangetic, or names of other regions)
