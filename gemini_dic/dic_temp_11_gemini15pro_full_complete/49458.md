deltoid: /ˈdɛltɔɪd/ | n. adj. | 三角肌；三角形的 |  A large triangular muscle covering the shoulder joint and serving to abduct and flex and extend and rotate the arm.  Also, shaped like a delta; triangular. |  He injured his deltoid lifting weights.  他举重时伤到了三角肌。 |  The building has a deltoid roof. 这座建筑有一个三角形的屋顶。 | Synonyms:  (n.) shoulder muscle, (adj.) triangular, delta-shaped | Antonyms: (adj.)  circular, square | noun, adjective

deltoids: /ˈdɛltɔɪdz/ | n. | 三角肌（复数） | The plural form of deltoid, referring to the muscles of both shoulders. | He worked his deltoids at the gym. 他在健身房锻炼了他的三角肌。| Synonyms: shoulder muscles | Antonyms: N/A | noun
