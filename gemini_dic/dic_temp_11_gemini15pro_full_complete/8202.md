anti-egoism:/ˌæntiˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/ | n. | 反利己主义，反自我主义 |  The philosophy that opposes egoism, holding that individuals should not act solely in their own self-interest.  It emphasizes the importance of considering the interests of others. |  Her actions demonstrated a strong sense of anti-egoism. 她的行为展现了强烈的反利己主义精神。 |  altruism, selflessness | egoism, selfishness | noun

anti-egoistic:/ˌæntiˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 反利己的，反自我中心的 |  Characterized by a lack of egoism; concerned with the welfare of others. | His anti-egoistic approach to leadership earned him the respect of his colleagues.  他反利己的领导方式赢得了同事们的尊重。 | altruistic, selfless | egoistic, selfish | adjective
