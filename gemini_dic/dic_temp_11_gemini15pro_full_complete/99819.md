词源:
From Latin *interminatus, past participle of *interminare (“to bound, limit, set bounds to, terminate, put an end to”), from in- (“in, within”) + terminus (“boundary, limit, end”).  来自拉丁语 *interminatus，*interminare（“界定、限制、设定界限、终止、结束”）的过去分词，源自 in-（“在...内”）+ terminus（“边界、限制、终点”）。
