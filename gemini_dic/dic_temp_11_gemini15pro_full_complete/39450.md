connectivity:/ˌkɒnɛkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌkɑːnɛkˈtɪvɪti/| n. |连接性，连通性|The state of being connected or interconnected.|例句：The internet connectivity in this area is poor.  这个地区的网络连接很差。

例句：The new bridge improved the connectivity between the two islands. 新建的桥梁改善了两个岛屿之间的连接。|近义词：connection, linkage, network|反义词:isolation, disconnection|词性:noun

Etymology: From connect + -ivity.  "connect" comes from Latin "connectere" (con- 'together' + nectere 'to bind'). "-ivity" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "state or quality of".
