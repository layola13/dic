amala:/əˈmɑːlə/ | n. | 阿玛拉，一种西非食物 | A dish of dark brown gravy, popular in Western Africa, especially among the Yoruba people. It's made from yam flour (èlùbó) or unripe plantain flour or cassava flour, and is traditionally served with ewédú (jute) soup, gbegiri (bean soup), and/or ilá (okra soup). | Example:  She prepared a delicious amala and ewedu soup for dinner. 她晚餐准备了美味的阿玛拉和蔬菜汤。

Example: Amala is a staple food in many Yoruba households.  阿玛拉是许多约鲁巴家庭的主食。 | 近义词: fufu (in some contexts, though not exactly the same) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
