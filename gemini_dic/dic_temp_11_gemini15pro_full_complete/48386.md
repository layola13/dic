dedans:/dəˈdɑ̃/ (French pronunciation)| adv. prep.|在里面，内部|Inside, within; into.  It signifies being located in an enclosed space or within the confines of something. It's primarily used in French. |例句：Le chat est dedans la maison.  The cat is inside the house.

例句：Il est entré dedans. He went inside. |近义词：à l'intérieur (French), inside (English), within (English)|反义词：dehors (French), outside (English)|词性: Adverb, Preposition


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French dedans, from de (“of, from”) + dens (“within”, from Latin dēnsus).
