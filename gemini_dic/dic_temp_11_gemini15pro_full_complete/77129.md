gasbag:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡ/ | n. | 空话连篇的人；吹牛大王；<非正式> 夸夸其谈者 | A person who talks at length but says little of substance; a braggart; a pompous, verbose person. | He's such a gasbag, always going on about nothing. 他真是个空话大王，总是喋喋不休地说着废话。 | synonyms: windbag, blowhard, braggart, loudmouth | antonyms: reticent person, quiet person | noun

gasbag:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡ/ | n. | （飞艇、气球等的）气囊 | A bag filled with gas, especially one used in an airship or balloon.  | The gasbag of the zeppelin was punctured by a stray bullet. 飞艇的气囊被一颗流弹刺穿了。 | synonyms: airbag, balloon | antonyms: N/A | noun
