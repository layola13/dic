angioplasties: /ˈændʒiəˌplæstiz/ | n. | 血管成形术 | A medical procedure in which a balloon-tipped catheter is inserted into a narrowed or blocked blood vessel and inflated to widen the vessel, restoring blood flow. |  The doctor recommended angioplasties to treat the blockage in his coronary artery. 医生建议进行血管成形术来治疗他冠状动脉的阻塞。 | Synonyms: balloon angioplasty, percutaneous transluminal angioplasty (PTA) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
