inventorying:/ˈɪnvəntɔːriɪŋ/
v.
清点，盘存
The act of making a complete list of items, typically of goods in stock.
The team is inventorying the warehouse to check stock levels.  团队正在清点仓库以检查库存水平。
inventorying supplies for the next quarter.  清点下个季度的物资。
cataloging, listing, registering
n/a
verb
