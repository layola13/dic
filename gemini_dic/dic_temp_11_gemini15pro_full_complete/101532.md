ironbark: /ˈaɪərnˌbɑːrk/ (US) /ˈaɪənˌbɑːk/ (UK)| n. adj.|铁皮树，铁皮树木材|A type of eucalyptus tree with hard, dark bark, or the wood from this tree.  The bark is so infused with resin that it resists fire.|例句：The ironbark tree is native to Australia. 铁皮树原产于澳大利亚。|近义词：eucalyptus, hardwood|反义词:softwood|词性:名词

ironbark: /ˈaɪərnˌbɑːrk/ (US) /ˈaɪənˌbɑːk/ (UK)| adj.|坚硬的，像铁皮一样的|Made of or resembling ironbark wood; strong and durable.|例句：The settlers built their houses from ironbark timber.  殖民者用铁皮树木材建造房屋。|近义词：resilient, tough|反义词:fragile|词性:形容词
