misdrew:/ˌmɪsˈdruː/
verb
错误地取出; 错画；开错(支票)
To draw wrongly or incorrectly, especially a check or other financial document.
She misdrew a check for $500 instead of $50. 她错开了500美元的支票，而不是50美元。
misdraw, overdraw, underdraw
correctly draw
