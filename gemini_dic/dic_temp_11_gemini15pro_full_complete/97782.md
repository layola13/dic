injudicial:/ˌɪndʒuˈdɪʃəl/
adjective
不明智的，缺乏判断力的
Not showing good judgment; unwise.
His injudicial remarks caused a lot of controversy. 他不明智的言论引发了很多争议。
The CEO made an injudicial decision that cost the company millions. 首席执行官做出了一个不明智的决定，使公司损失了数百万美元。
近义词: unwise, imprudent, impolitic, indiscreet
反义词: wise, prudent, judicious, discreet
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- "not" + judicial "pertaining to judgment."  Ultimately from Latin *judicare "to judge," from jus "law, right."
