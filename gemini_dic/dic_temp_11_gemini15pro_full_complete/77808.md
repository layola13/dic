generalnesses:/ˈdʒɛnərəlnəsɪz/
n.
泛性；普遍性；一般性
The generalness of the statement made it difficult to apply to specific situations. 该声明的普遍性使其难以应用于具体情况。
The generalness of his skills made him a valuable asset to the team.  他技能的全面性使他成为团队的宝贵资产。
Synonym: generality, universality, commonality
Antonym: specificity, particularity, uniqueness
词性: 名词
