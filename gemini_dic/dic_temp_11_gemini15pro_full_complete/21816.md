bocardo:/boʊˈkɑːrdoʊ/
noun
博卡多谬误，博卡多式三段论
A syllogistic mood in which the first two premises are particular negative propositions and the conclusion is a particular negative proposition.  A logical fallacy.
Example: Some mammals are not cats. Some cats are not pets. Therefore, some mammals are not pets.
Synonyms: fallacy, error in reasoning, invalid argument
Antonyms: valid argument, sound reasoning
词性: noun
