integrity: /ɪnˈtɛɡrəti/ | n. | 正直，诚实，完整性 | the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; moral uprightness; the state of being whole and undivided |  He is a man of great integrity. 他是一个非常正直的人。| synonyms: honesty, probity, rectitude, virtue, morality | antonyms: dishonesty, corruption, deceit, hypocrisy | noun

integrity: /ɪnˈtɛɡrəti/ | n. | 完整，完好 | the state of being whole and undivided | The integrity of the building was compromised after the earthquake.  地震后，建筑物的完整性受到了损害。 | synonyms: completeness, wholeness, soundness, unity | antonyms: incompleteness, fragmentation, damage | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *integritatem* (nominative *integritas*), from *integer* "whole, untouched," literally "not touched," from *in-* "not" (see in- (1)) + *tangere* "to touch" (see tangent).
