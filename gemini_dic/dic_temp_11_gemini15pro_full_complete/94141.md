impeding:/ɪmˈpiːdɪŋ/
adjective
阻碍的，妨碍的
delaying or preventing (someone or something) by obstructing them; hindering.
The heavy traffic was impeding our progress.  交通拥堵阻碍了我们的前进。
近义词: hindering, obstructing, blocking, hampering
反义词: facilitating, aiding, assisting, expediting
词性: adjective
