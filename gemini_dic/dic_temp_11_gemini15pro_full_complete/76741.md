gameable:/ˈɡeɪməbəl/| adj.|可游戏化的，可博弈的|Something that is gameable can be adapted or designed to be played as a game, often with the aim of manipulating or exploiting the rules or system for personal gain.  It can also refer to something that is suitable for or conducive to gambling.|例句：The new loyalty program is highly gameable, allowing savvy customers to earn excessive rewards.  这个新的忠诚度计划很容易被利用，精明的顾客可以获得过多的奖励。

例句：The loopholes in the tax code made the system gameable for corporations. 税法中的漏洞使得该系统容易被企业利用。|近义词：exploitable, manipulable,  vulnerable|反义词：secure, robust, tamper-proof|词性:形容词
