aridness:/ˈærɪdnəs/ | n. | 干旱，干燥 | The aridness of the desert made it difficult for plants to grow. | The prolonged drought contributed to the aridness of the land, making it unsuitable for agriculture. | 近义词: dryness, barrenness, drought | 反义词: humidity, wetness, moisture | 词性: noun | 词源: From arid + -ness. Arid comes from the Latin word "aridus" meaning "dry" or "parched".
