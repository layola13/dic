lattermath:/ˈlætərˌmæθ/ /ˈlɑːtərˌmæθ/| n. |后果，余波|the period of time after a bad and usually destructive event; the unpleasant results that follow an event, especially a war, disaster, or accident|例句：The country is still struggling with the lattermath of the war. 这个国家仍在与战争的后果作斗争。

例句：In the lattermath of the hurricane, thousands were left homeless.  飓风过后，成千上万的人无家可归。

近义词：aftermath, consequences, repercussions, fallout|反义词:cause, origin|词性:名词
