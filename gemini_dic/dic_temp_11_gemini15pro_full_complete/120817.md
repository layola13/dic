misassumptions:/ˌmɪsəˈsʌmpʃənz/
|n.|错误的假设，误解|An incorrect assumption or understanding of something.|例句：His misassumptions about the market led to significant financial losses. 他对市场的误判导致了巨大的经济损失。

例句：The project failed due to a series of misassumptions about the client's needs.  由于对客户需求的一系列误判，该项目失败了。|近义词：misconceptions, misapprehensions, misunderstandings, fallacies|反义词：correct assumptions, accurate understandings, truths|noun
