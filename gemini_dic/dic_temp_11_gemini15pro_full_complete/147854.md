overrapid:/ˌoʊvərˈræpɪd/| adj.|过快的，过速的|Excessively rapid; too fast.|The overrapid growth of the city led to infrastructure problems. 城市过快的发展导致了基础设施问题。|近义词：excessive, hasty, precipitate, accelerated, too rapid, unduly rapid|反义词:slow, gradual, measured|词性:形容词
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "rapid" (from French rapide, from Latin rapidus "swift, rapid," from rapere "to seize, snatch, carry off").
