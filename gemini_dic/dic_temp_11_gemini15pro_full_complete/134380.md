noninterpretatively:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈtɜːrprɪtətɪvli/ | adv. | 非解释性地，不带解释地 |  Without providing interpretation or explanation; literally or directly.  | The data was presented noninterpretatively, allowing the audience to draw their own conclusions. 数据以非解释性的方式呈现，让观众得出自己的结论。|  literally, directly, factually | interpretatively, subjectively | adverb

Etymology:
* non- (prefix):  from Latin *non*, meaning "not".
* interpretative: from Late Latin *interpretativus*, from *interpretari* "to explain, expound", from *interpres* "interpreter, translator, explainer".
* -ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives.
