biorhythmics: /ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðmɪks/ | n. | 生物节律；生物周期；生命节律|  The study of periodic cycles in physiological and psychological functioning. It hypothesizes that our physical, emotional, and intellectual states are influenced by predictable biological cycles. |  He's studying biorhythmics to see if it can help improve his athletic performance. 他正在研究生物节律，看看它是否能帮助提高他的运动成绩。 |  biorhythm, biological rhythm, biological clock | N/A | noun
