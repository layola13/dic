chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ /ˈʃæmfər/| n. vt.|倒角，削边；|A small angled surface formed on an edge or corner, typically to remove its sharpness or to decorate it.  |The carpenter chamfered the edges of the table. 木匠把桌子的边缘倒了角。|近义词：bevel, slope, cant|反义词：sharpen, point|noun
chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ /ˈʃæmfər/| vt.|给…倒角，削边|To cut a chamfer on (an edge or corner).|He chamfered the wood to make it safer. 他把木头倒角以使其更安全。|近义词：bevel, slope, cant|反义词：sharpen, point|verb
