daunt:/dɔːnt/ /dɑːnt/| vt.| 使气馁；使畏缩；使胆怯|to make someone feel slightly frightened or worried about their ability to achieve something|The sheer size of the task didn't daunt her.  这项任务的艰巨性并没有让她感到气馁。

The steep climb up the mountain daunts many hikers. 陡峭的山路让许多徒步旅行者望而生畏。|近义词：intimidate, discourage, dishearten|反义词：encourage, embolden, hearten|词性:动词
