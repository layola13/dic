esperantism:/ˌɛspəˈrænˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 世界语；世界语运动 |  A constructed international auxiliary language based on words common to the major European languages, with a simplified grammar and a vocabulary formed largely by adding prefixes and suffixes to root words. |  He learned Esperanto in hopes of communicating with people from different countries. 他学习世界语是为了能与来自不同国家的人交流。| synonym: planned language, artificial language | antonym: natural language | noun

esperantist:/ˌɛspəˈrænˌtɪst/ | n. | 世界语者 | A person who speaks or supports Esperanto. | She's an active Esperantist and attends international Esperanto congresses. 她是一位活跃的世界语者，经常参加国际世界语大会。 | synonym: Esperanto speaker | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
