hand-picked:/ˌhændˈpɪkt/| adj.|精心挑选的，手工采摘的|Carefully selected by hand.  Chosen with great care and attention to detail.  Implies a higher quality or exclusivity.|例句：These are hand-picked strawberries, the sweetest of the season. 这些是手工采摘的草莓，是这个季节最甜的。|例句：The company boasts a hand-picked team of experts. 这家公司拥有一支精心挑选的专家团队。|近义词：selected, chosen, prime, choice, best, elite|反义词：random, arbitrary, ordinary, common|词性:形容词

词源:
From "hand" + "picked" (past participle of "pick").  "Hand" refers to the use of one's hand in the selection process. "Pick" means to choose or select.
