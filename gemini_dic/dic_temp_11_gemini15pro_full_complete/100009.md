interpenetrate:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪt/| v. |互相渗透，穿透，交错|To penetrate mutually; permeate each other.|The two cultures interpenetrate each other. 这两种文化互相渗透。|近义词：permeate, intermingle, fuse, blend|反义词：separate, divide|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Latin *interpenetrare*, from inter- (“between, among, mutually”) + penetrare (“to penetrate”).
