over-trust:/ˌōvərˈtrəst/| v. |过分信任，过度信任|To place excessive trust or confidence in someone or something; to trust beyond what is warranted or prudent.|He over-trusted his business partner and lost a lot of money. 他过分信任他的生意伙伴，损失了很多钱。|近义词：overestimate, over-rely, over-depend|反义词:distrust, doubt, suspect|词性:动词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar, meaning "above, over") + "trust" (from Old Norse traust, meaning "confidence, support")
