carting:/ˈkɑːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 货运，运送；(尤指用卡车)运送 | The act of transporting goods, especially by cart or truck. |  The company specializes in the carting of heavy machinery.  这家公司专门从事重型机械的运输。| 近义词：hauling, trucking, transport, conveyance | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

carting:/ˈkɑːrtɪŋ/ | v. | (用卡车)运送；搬运 | Transporting something, typically by cart or truck. | They were carting away the debris from the demolition site. 他们在清理拆除现场的碎片。| 近义词：haul, truck, transport, convey | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb (present participle of 'cart')

Etymology: From the verb "cart," which comes from the Old North French "caret" and ultimately from the Late Latin "carrus" (wheeled vehicle).
