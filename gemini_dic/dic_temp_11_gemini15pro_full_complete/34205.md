clancy:/ˈklænsi/ | adj. |克兰西式的；(尤指惊悚小说)情节复杂的；充满高科技元素的 |  Characteristic of or reminiscent of the style of Tom Clancy, particularly his thrillers, often involving complex plots, high-tech weaponry, and geopolitical intrigue.  | His latest novel is very Clancyesque, with lots of military jargon and detailed descriptions of weapons systems. 他最新的小说非常克兰西式，充满了军事术语和武器系统的详细描述。 | 近义词: thrilling, suspenseful, complex, intricate | 反义词: simple, straightforward | 词性: 形容词
