bullying:/ˈbʊlɪɪŋ/| n.|欺凌，霸凌|The act of intimidating or harming someone weaker or less powerful, often repeatedly and over time.  It can involve physical violence, verbal abuse, social exclusion, or other forms of harassment.|He was a victim of bullying at school. 他在学校遭受了欺凌。|近义词：harassment, intimidation, persecution, victimization|反义词：protection, support, kindness|词性:noun

bullying:/ˈbʊlɪɪŋ/| adj.|恃强凌弱的，霸道的|Characterized by or exhibiting bullying behavior.|He displayed bullying tactics in the workplace. 他在工作场所表现出恃强凌弱的策略。|近义词：aggressive, intimidating, domineering, oppressive|反义词：supportive, kind, compassionate|词性:adjective
