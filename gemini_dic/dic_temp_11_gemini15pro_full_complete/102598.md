jailbreak:/ˈdʒeɪlbreɪk/ | n. v. |越狱，破解（电子设备的限制）| The act of removing software restrictions imposed by a manufacturer or carrier on a device such as a smartphone, tablet computer or video game console, in order to install software and applications not officially approved by the device's manufacturer or carrier. | He jailbroke his iPhone to install unauthorized apps. 他越狱了他的iPhone以安装未经授权的应用程序。| Synonyms: hack, crack, unlock, bypass | Antonyms: restrict, lock, secure | noun
