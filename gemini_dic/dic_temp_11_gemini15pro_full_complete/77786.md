generalissimo:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/
n.
最高统帅，大元帅
the supreme commander of a combined military force, especially one who has been given dictatorial powers.
Generalissimo Francisco Franco ruled Spain for over 30 years.  佛朗哥大元帅统治西班牙超过30年。
The generalissimo ordered a full-scale attack. 最高统帅下令全面进攻。

近义词: commander-in-chief, supreme commander
反义词: subordinate, follower
词性: noun
