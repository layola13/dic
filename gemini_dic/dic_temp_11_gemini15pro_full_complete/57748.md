dreamfulness:/ˈdriːmfʊlnəs/| n. |梦幻状态；沉思冥想；爱幻想|The state of being dreamy or given to daydreaming; a pensive or contemplative mood.|She drifted off into a state of dreamfulness, lost in her own thoughts. 她陷入了梦幻状态，沉浸在自己的思绪中。|例句：His dreamfulness often caused him to miss important details.  他爱幻想的性格常常让他错过重要的细节。

例句：Her dreamfulness made her seem distant and aloof. 她梦幻般的状态让她显得疏远而冷漠。|近义词：reverie, daydreaming, pensiveness, abstraction|反义词：alertness, attentiveness, practicality|词性:noun
