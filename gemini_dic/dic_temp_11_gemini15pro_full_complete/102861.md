jaundice:/ˈdʒɔːndɪs/| n.|黄疸|A medical condition in which the skin and the whites of the eyes turn yellow because of increased levels of bilirubin in the blood.|The baby was born with jaundice.  这个婴儿出生时患有黄疸。|

The doctor diagnosed him with jaundice. 医生诊断他患有黄疸。
