know:/noʊ/ (US) /nəʊ/ (UK)|v.|知道，了解，认识|To have information in your mind, to be familiar with someone or something.|I know the answer. 我知道答案。|Synonyms: understand, recognize, be acquainted with, be aware of|Antonyms: ignore, be unaware of|verb

know:/noʊ/ (US) /nəʊ/ (UK)|adj.(古英语用法，现在很少使用)|认识的，熟悉的 (archaic)|Known to someone; familiar.|He was a know thief. 他是个臭名昭著的贼。  (This usage is archaic.)|Synonyms: familiar, recognized|Antonyms: unknown, unfamiliar|adjective
