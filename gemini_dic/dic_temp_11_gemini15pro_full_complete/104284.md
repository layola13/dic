justiciary:/dʒuːˈstɪʃəri/ /dʒuːˈstɪʃiˌɛri/| n. |法官；司法官；司法权；司法系统|A justiciary is a judge or magistrate, especially one who administers justice in a court of law. It can also refer to the judicial system or the administration of justice in general.|The justiciary handed down a harsh sentence. 法官宣布了严厉的判决。|Synonyms: judge, magistrate, justice, jurist|Antonyms: defendant, accused, plaintiff|noun

justiciaries:/dʒuːˈstɪʃəriz/ /dʒuːˈstɪʃiˌɛriz/| n. |法官们；司法官们；司法系统|Plural form of "justiciary", referring to multiple judges, magistrates, or judicial systems.|The justiciaries of the Supreme Court deliberated for days. 最高法院的法官们商议了数天。|Synonyms: judges, magistrates, justices|Antonyms: defendants, accused, plaintiffs|noun
