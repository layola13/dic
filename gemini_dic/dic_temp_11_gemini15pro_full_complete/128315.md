neurotome:/ˈnʊərəˌtoʊm/ | n. |神经节段；肌节|A segment of the neural tube and its associated mesoderm that gives rise to a spinal nerve and the muscles it innervates. | The neurotome gives rise to the skeletal muscle of the back.  神经节段会形成背部的骨骼肌。| The development of the neurotome is crucial for the proper formation of the peripheral nervous system. 神经节段的发育对于周围神经系统的正常形成至关重要。 | synonym: myotome, somite | antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific anatomical structure.) | noun
