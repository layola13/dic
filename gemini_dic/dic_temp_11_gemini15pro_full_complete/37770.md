例句：Checking the software compatibility's is crucial before installation.  安装前检查软件的兼容性至关重要。


|近义词：interoperability, consistency, agreement, harmony|反义词：incompatibility, conflict, discord, disharmony|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compatibilitas, from compatibilis  "sympathetic, suffering with," from com- "with, together" + pati "to suffer, endure."  The modern sense of "able to exist together harmoniously" developed in the 17th century.
