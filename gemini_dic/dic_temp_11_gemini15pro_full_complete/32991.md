choreoid: /ˈkɔːrɔɪd/ | n. | 脉络膜 | The choroid provides oxygen and nutrients to the outer layers of the retina. 脉络膜为视网膜的外层提供氧气和营养。 | Damage to the choroid can lead to vision problems.  脉络膜的损伤会导致视力问题。 | 近义词: None | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *choroīdēs, from Ancient Greek  χορειοειδής (khorioeidēs, “like a membrane, skin-like”), from χόριον (khórion, “membrane enclosing the fetus, afterbirth”) + -ειδής (-eidēs, “-like, -shaped”).  The Greek χόριον is related to Latin corium (“skin, hide”).
