exhaustless: /ɪɡˈzɔːstlɪs/ /ɪɡˈzɑːstlɪs/
adjective
无穷无尽的; 用不完的
inexhaustible; that cannot be exhausted; never-ending
Her energy seemed exhaustless. 她似乎有用不完的精力。
His exhaustless patience made him a good teacher. 他无穷的耐心使他成为了一名好老师。
近义词: inexhaustible, boundless, limitless, infinite
反义词: limited, finite, exhaustible, meager
词性: 形容词
