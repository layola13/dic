outpity: /aʊtˈpɪti/
adjective
比…更可怜；胜过…的可怜
surpass in pity; exceed in showing pity for
She outpitied everyone else at the funeral, weeping uncontrollably for hours.  她在葬礼上比任何人都更悲伤，无法控制地哭了好几个小时。
近义词: outdo in sympathy, surpass in compassion
反义词: be less compassionate than, be less sympathetic than
词性: adjective
