gabioned:/ˈɡeɪbiənd/ | adj. | 用石笼加固的 | Enclosed or fortified with gabions (wire mesh cages filled with rocks). | The riverbank was gabioned to prevent erosion. 河岸用石笼加固以防止侵蚀。 | The gabioned wall protected the fort from attack. 石笼墙保护了堡垒免受攻击。 | fortified, reinforced |  | adjective

Etymology->
From French *gabionnade*, from Italian *gabionata*, from gabbione (gabion). Ultimately from Italian *gabbione* "large cage", augmentative of *gabbia* "cage", from Latin *cavea* "cage, enclosure".
