inviolableness:/ɪnˌvaɪəˈləb(ə)lnəs/| n. |不可侵犯性；神圣性|The quality of being inviolable; immunity from violation or profanation.  The inviolableness of human rights should be upheld by all nations.  所有国家都应该维护人权的神圣不可侵犯性。|近义词：sanctity, sacredness, inviolability|反义词：vulnerability, weakness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From inviolable + -ness. Inviolable comes from Late Latin inviolabilis, from in- "not" + violabilis "able to be violated," from violare "to violate," from Latin vis "force, violence."
