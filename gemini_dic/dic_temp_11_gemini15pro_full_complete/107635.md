What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Gothic 𐍈𐍀 (“what”). Related to who.
