microcosmical:/ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzmɪkəl/ |adj.|微观的，缩影的|Relating to or characteristic of a microcosm; representing something on a smaller scale.| This intricate ant colony serves as a microcosmical representation of a complex human society.  这个复杂的蚁群是复杂人类社会的缩影。| The model railway is a microcosmical version of the real rail network. 这个铁路模型是真实铁路网络的微观版本。 | miniature, epitomized | macroscopic, expansive | adjective

microcosm:/ˈmaɪkrəʊkɒzəm/ |n.|微观世界，缩影|A community, place, or thing regarded as encapsulating in miniature the characteristics of something much larger.| The school is a microcosm of society. 学校是社会的缩影。| The family is often seen as a microcosm of the larger world. 家庭通常被视为更广阔世界的缩影。 | miniature, epitome | macrocosm | noun
