foxlike:/ˈfɒkslaɪk/ | adj. | 像狐狸的; 狡猾的 | Resembling a fox in appearance, behavior, or cunning. |  The thief had a foxlike cunning. 那小偷有着像狐狸一样的狡猾。|  sly, cunning, vulpine |  honest, ingenuous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fox + -like.  "Fox" comes from Old English "fox," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fuhsaz.  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
