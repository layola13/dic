foregrounds:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊndz/| n. v.|前景，突出的位置；使突出，强调|The artist placed the figure in the foreground of the painting.  艺术家将人物置于画面的前景。|近义词：front, forefront, prominence|反义词:background, backdrop|词性:名词 复数; 动词 第三称单数

foreground:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊnd/| n. |前景，突出的位置|The flowers in the foreground of the photograph are beautiful. 照片前景的花很漂亮。|近义词：front, forefront, prominence|反义词:background, backdrop|词性:名词

foreground:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊnd/| v. |使突出，强调|The report foregrounds the need for better education.  报告强调了改进教育的必要性。|近义词：emphasize, highlight, stress|反义词:downplay, minimize|词性:动词

词源: From fore- + ground.  "Fore-" means "in front" or "before," and "ground" refers to the surface or area.
