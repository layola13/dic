nonerrant:/ˌnɑːnˈɛrənt/| adj.|无误的，不出错的|Free from errors; infallible.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "errant" (wandering, straying, or making errors).  Ultimately from Latin *iterare* "to repeat, go over," from *iter* "journey, way."|The Bible is considered by some to be nonerrant in its original manuscripts.  圣经在其原始手稿中被一些人认为是无误的。

The Pope's pronouncements on matters of faith are considered nonerrant by Catholics.  天主教徒认为教皇在信仰问题上的声明是无误的。|近义词：infallible, unerring, faultless, impeccable|反义词：errant, fallible, flawed, imperfect|词性:形容词
