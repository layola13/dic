nonincorporative:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkɔːrpərətɪv/
adjective
非公司的；不合并的；不结社的
Not incorporating or incorporated; not forming or formed into a corporation or legal body.
The group remained a nonincorporative association for many years before finally deciding to seek legal status.  这个团体多年来一直保持着非公司社团的状态，最终才决定寻求法律地位。
The artists formed a nonincorporative collective to showcase their work.  艺术家们组成了一个非公司性质的集体来展示他们的作品。
近义词:unincorporated, unassociated
反义词:incorporated, corporate
词性:adjective
