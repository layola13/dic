dorm:/dɔːrm/ | n. | 宿舍 | A large bedroom for a number of people in a school or institution. |  Example 1:  I lived in a dorm during my first year of college. 我大学第一年住在宿舍里。

Example 2: The dorm rooms are quite small, but they're comfortable. 宿舍房间很小，但很舒适。| Synonym: dormitory, residence hall, hostel | Antonym: apartment, house | noun
