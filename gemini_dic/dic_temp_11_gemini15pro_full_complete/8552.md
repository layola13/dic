antichrists:/ˈæntɪˌkraɪsts/| n. |敌基督者；基督的敌人|One who denies or opposes Christ; a false Christ.  A great antagonist expected to fill the world with wickedness but to be conquered by Christ at his second coming. |例句：Many cults believe in the coming of the antichrist. 许多邪教相信敌基督的到来。

例句：The book described the antichrist as a charismatic leader who would deceive many. 这本书将敌基督描述为一个会欺骗许多人的有魅力的领袖。|近义词：false prophet, adversary, opponent|反义词:Christ, Messiah|词性:noun
