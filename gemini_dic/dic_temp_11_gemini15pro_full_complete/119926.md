milkiness:/ˈmɪlkɪnɪs/
noun
乳白色; 乳状; 似牛奶
The quality or state of being milky.  The milkiness of the liquid suggested it was contaminated.
The milkiness of the opal made it seem to glow.
opacity, whiteness
clarity, transparency
