aquacultural:/ˌækwəˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|水产养殖的；水培的|Relating to aquaculture; the cultivation of aquatic animals or plants, especially for food.|例句：Aquacultural practices are becoming increasingly sophisticated. 水产养殖方法日益先进。|例句：The growth of the aquacultural industry has been remarkable. 水产养殖业的发展引人注目。|近义词：fish-farming, mariculture, hydroponic (for plants)|反义词: wild-caught, naturally occurring|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From aqua (Latin for 'water') + culture (Latin cultura, from colere 'to cultivate').
