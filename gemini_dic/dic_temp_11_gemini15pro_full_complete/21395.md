blouselike: /ˈblaʊslʌɪk/
adjective
类似女衬衫的；像女衬衫一样的
Resembling or characteristic of a blouse.  A loose-fitting, typically gathered garment worn by women.
The top she wore was blouselike, flowing and comfortable. 她穿的上衣像女衬衫一样，飘逸舒适。
Her new tunic had a blouselike fit. 她的新束腰外衣很像衬衫。
近义词: shirtlike, tunic-like, flowy, loose-fitting
反义词: tight-fitting, form-fitting, tailored


词性: adjective
