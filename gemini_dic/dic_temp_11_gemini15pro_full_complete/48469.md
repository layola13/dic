deens: /diːnz/
n.
dens (plural)
兽穴，窝点
A wild animal's den, lair, or shelter.
The fox retreated to its deen after being chased by the hounds.  狐狸被猎犬追赶后躲进了它的窝。
近义词: lair, den, burrow
反义词: exposure
