hectoring:/ˈhɛktərɪŋ/| adj.|霸道的；专横的；威吓的|Characterized by persistent nagging, bullying, or scolding.|例句：The teacher's hectoring tone made the students uncomfortable. 老师咄咄逼人的语气让学生们感到不舒服。|例句：He delivered a hectoring lecture about the importance of punctuality. 他就守时的重要性发表了一通教训人的长篇大论。|近义词：bullying, domineering, overbearing, intimidating|反义词：gentle, kind, encouraging, supportive|词性:形容词

hector:/ˈhɛktər/| v.|威吓；欺负|To intimidate or harass by bluster or personal pressure.|例句：Stop hectoring me about my homework! 别再为我的作业教训我了！|例句：He was known for hectoring the younger employees. 他以欺负年轻员工而闻名。|近义词：bully, intimidate, harass, badger|反义词：encourage, support, soothe, comfort|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hector, the Trojan hero in Homer's Iliad, known for his strength and courage, but later interpreted as a bully due to his challenges to the Greeks. The verb "hector" emerged in the 16th century, and the adjective "hectoring" followed in the 19th century.
