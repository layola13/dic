ensued:/ɪnˈsuːd/
verb
接着发生；随之而来
followed as a consequence.
例句：After the heavy rain, flooding ensued. 大雨过后，发生了洪水。

例句：A long and bitter feud ensued between the two families.  两个家族之间爆发了漫长而激烈的世仇。

近义词：followed, resulted, occurred, happened, developed
反义词：preceded, anteceded
词性：verb
