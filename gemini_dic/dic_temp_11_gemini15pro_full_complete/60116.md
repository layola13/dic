effacer:/e.fa.se/ (French pronunciation)| v. |擦掉，抹去，消除|To erase, rub out, or obliterate something, often a mark or writing, but also figuratively used for memories or feelings.|例句：Il a effacé le dessin au crayon. 他擦掉了铅笔画。

例句：Le temps efface les souvenirs. 时间会冲淡记忆。|近义词：erase, delete, obliterate, expunge|反义词：write, inscribe, record, preserve|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esfacier, *effacier “to deface, destroy,” from Vulgar Latin *exfaciare, from Latin ex- “out” + facies “face, appearance.”
