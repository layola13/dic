lovelornness:/ˈlʌvlɔːrnɪs/| n. |失恋的痛苦，单相思的苦恼 |The state of being lovelorn; sadness or despair caused by unrequited love or the loss of a loved one.|  She suffered from acute lovelornness after her boyfriend broke up with her. 她和男朋友分手后陷入了极度的失恋痛苦中。| Synonyms: heartache, lovesickness, pining, desolation | Antonyms: happiness, joy, contentment | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lovelorn + -ness.  Lovelorn itself comes from love + lorn (meaning "abandoned, forlorn").
