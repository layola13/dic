left-hander: /ˌleftˈhændər/ (US), /ˌleftˈhændə(r)/ (UK)| n.|左撇子|A person who uses their left hand more naturally and skillfully than their right hand for writing and other tasks.|My brother is a left-hander and he's a great baseball pitcher. 我的弟弟是个左撇子，他是个很棒的棒球投手。|Synonyms: southpaw|Antonyms: right-hander|Noun

left-handed: /ˌleftˈhændɪd/ (US), /ˌleftˈhændɪd/ (UK)| adj.|左手的；左撇子的|Using the left hand more naturally and skillfully than the right hand; designed for or done with the left hand.|She's left-handed and writes beautifully. 她是个左撇子，字写得很漂亮。|Synonyms: southpaw|Antonyms: right-handed|Adjective
