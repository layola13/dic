minihomes:/ˈmɪniˌhoʊmz/| n.|迷你住宅，小型房屋|A minihome is a small, usually prefabricated house, often used as a vacation home or for affordable housing.  It is typically smaller than a traditional house but larger than a mobile home. |Many people choose minihomes for their affordability and ease of maintenance. 许多人选择迷你住宅是因为它们价格实惠且易于维护。| synonyms: tiny homes, small houses, compact houses, prefabricated homes, modular homes| antonyms: mansions, large houses, estates | noun
