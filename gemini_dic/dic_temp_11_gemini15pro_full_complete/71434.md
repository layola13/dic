flakinesses:/ˈfleɪkinɪsɪz/
n.
薄片状；易剥落；古怪；反复无常
The state or quality of being flaky.  This can refer to a physical property, like the flakiness of pastry, or a behavioral trait, like the flakiness of a person who often cancels plans.
The pastry's flakiness made it incredibly delicious.  这块糕点的酥脆让它非常美味。
Her flakiness is starting to annoy me; she's cancelled our plans three times this week. 她的反复无常开始让我恼火了；这周她已经三次取消了我们的计划。
近义词: capriciousness, unreliability, eccentricity, fragility, crumbliness
反义词: reliability, consistency, stability, robustness
词性: 名词 (plural of flakiness)
