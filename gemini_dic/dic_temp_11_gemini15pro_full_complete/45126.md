cucurbitaceous: /ˌkjuːkərˈbɪteɪʃəs/ /ˌkʊkərˈbɪʃəs/
adjective
葫芦科的；属于葫芦科的
Relating to or denoting plants of the gourd family, Cucurbitaceae.  These plants typically have large, fleshy fruits and include cucumbers, pumpkins, squashes, and melons.
Pumpkins are cucurbitaceous plants.  南瓜是葫芦科植物。
Watermelons are cucurbitaceous and thrive in warm climates. 西瓜是葫芦科植物，在温暖的气候下生长茂盛。
近义词: gourd-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "non-cucurbitaceous")
词性: adjective
