etherification:/ɪˌθɜːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 醚化(作用) | The process of forming an ether, typically by reacting an alcohol with another alcohol or a halide. |  Etherification is an important reaction in organic synthesis. 醚化是有机合成中的一个重要反应。|  The Williamson ether synthesis is a common method of etherification. 威廉姆逊醚合成是醚化的一种常用方法。 |近义词: alkylation  | 反义词: hydrolysis | 词性: noun
