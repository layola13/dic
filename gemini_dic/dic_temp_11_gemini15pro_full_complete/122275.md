mitigators:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtərz/
|n.| 缓解者，减轻者 |People or things that mitigate something (make it less severe, serious, or painful).|  The company is investing in mitigators to reduce their carbon footprint.  该公司正在投资减缓措施以减少其碳足迹。|synonyms: alleviators, reducers, moderators |antonyms: aggravators, intensifiers| noun


mitigate:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/
|v.| 减轻，缓和 |Make something less severe, serious, or painful.| The government is trying to mitigate the effects of the recession. 政府正在努力减轻经济衰退的影响。|synonyms: alleviate, reduce, lessen, diminish|antonyms: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate|verb


mitigating:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
|adj.| 减轻的，缓和的|Making something less severe, serious, or painful.|  There are some mitigating circumstances in this case.  这个案件中有一些减轻处罚的情节。|synonyms: alleviating, reducing, lessening|antonyms: aggravating, worsening, exacerbating|adjective
