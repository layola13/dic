discharger:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒər/ | n. | 排放者，卸货者，放电器 |  A person or thing that discharges something. | The factory was identified as the main discharger of pollutants into the river.  这家工厂被认定为河流污染物的主要排放者。| synonyms: emitter, releaser,  outlet | antonyms: absorber, retainer, collector | noun

discharger:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒər/ | n. | 退伍军人， discharged person的简称 | A person who has been discharged from the military or a hospital. |  The hospital provides support services for dischargers.  医院为退伍军人提供支持服务。| synonyms: veteran, ex-serviceman, patient  (in hospital context)| antonyms: recruit,  enlistee | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "discharge," which comes from Old French "deschargier," meaning "to unload."  Ultimately from Latin "dis-," meaning "apart," and "carricare," meaning "to load."
