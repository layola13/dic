overillustrate:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləstreɪt/
verb
过度图解；插图过多
To illustrate excessively; to provide too many illustrations.
The textbook was overillustrated, making it distracting for the students. 这本教科书插图过多，让学生分心。
The author overillustrated the children's book, making it cluttered.  作者给这本儿童读物配了太多的插图，显得画面很杂乱。
近义词: overdecorate, overdo
反义词: underillustrate
