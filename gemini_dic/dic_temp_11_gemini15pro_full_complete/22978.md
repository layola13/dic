botulinal:/ˌbɒtʃʊˈlaɪnəl/ | adj. | 肉毒杆菌的；肉毒中毒的 | Relating to or caused by botulinum toxin (a neurotoxin produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum). | Botulinal toxin is one of the most potent toxins known. 肉毒杆菌毒素是已知最强的毒素之一。| Botulinal antitoxin can be used to treat botulism. 肉毒杆菌抗毒素可以用来治疗肉毒中毒。 |  近义词:  neurotoxic | 反义词: harmless | 词性: adjective


botulinum:/ˌbɒtʃʊˈlɪnəm/ | n. | 肉毒杆菌 | The bacterium Clostridium botulinum, which produces botulinum toxin. |  Botulinum can be found in improperly canned foods. 肉毒杆菌可以在不当罐装食品中找到。 |  Botulinum toxin is used in small doses for cosmetic purposes. 少量的肉毒杆菌毒素用于美容目的。| 近义词: Clostridium botulinum | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bacterium) | 词性: noun

词源:  From Latin *botulus "sausage," because early cases of botulism were associated with sausage poisoning.  最终来源于拉丁语“botulus”，意思是“香肠”，因为早期的肉毒中毒病例与香肠中毒有关。
