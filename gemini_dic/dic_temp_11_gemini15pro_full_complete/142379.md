orthorrhaphous:/ˌɔːrθəˈræfəs/
adjective
直缝翅的；正翅类的
Having straight, longitudinal wing veins, as in certain insects.  Specifically referring to insect wings where the main veins run parallel to the length of the wing.
例句：Orthorrhaphous insects are a large group including grasshoppers and cockroaches. 直翅目昆虫是一个很大的群体，包括蚱蜢和蟑螂。

例句：The orthorrhaphous wing structure allows for powerful flight. 直翅目的翅膀结构有利于强有力的飞行。
近义词：straight-winged
反义词：N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *orthos* (straight) + *rhaphe* (seam).
