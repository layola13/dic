excavator:/ˈɛkskəveɪtər/ (US), /ˈɛkskəveɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |挖掘机; 开凿者|A large, powerful machine with a bucket and an arm, used for digging and moving earth and rocks. |The excavators were busy clearing the site for the new building. 挖掘机正忙着清理新建筑工地的场地。|synonyms: digger, backhoe, shovel, steam shovel|antonyms: filler, backfiller|noun

excavator:/ˈɛkskəveɪtər/ (US), /ˈɛkskəveɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |发掘者;考古学家|A person who digs or excavates, especially an archaeologist. |The excavators uncovered ancient ruins at the site. 考古学家们在遗址发现了古代遗迹。|synonyms: archaeologist, digger, unearther|antonyms: burier, concealer|noun
