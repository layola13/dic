globosity:/ɡloʊˈbɑːsəti/
n.
球状；球形；圆形
The quality or state of being globe-shaped; sphericity.
The astronomer commented on the perfect globosity of the planet.  这位天文学家评论了这颗行星完美的球形。
sphericity, roundness, rotundity
flatness
noun
