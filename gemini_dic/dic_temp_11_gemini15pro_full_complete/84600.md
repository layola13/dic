hamshackle:/ˈhæmˌʃækəl/| v. |束缚，限制|To restrict the freedom of someone or something, usually by tying or confining them.|The company felt hamshackled by outdated regulations.  这家公司感到被过时的规章制度所束缚。|Synonyms: restrain, restrict, confine, fetter, hinder, impede|Antonyms: liberate, free, release, unfetter|verb
hamshackle:/ˈhæmˌʃækəl/| n. |束缚，限制|Something that restricts freedom or progress.|The lack of funding was a hamshackle on their research. 缺乏资金是他们研究的束缚。|Synonyms: restraint, restriction, constraint, impediment, obstacle|Antonyms: liberation, freedom, release|noun
