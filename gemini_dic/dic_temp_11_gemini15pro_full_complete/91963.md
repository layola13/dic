hypermoral:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɔːrəl/| adj.|过分道德的，道德洁癖的|Excessively or rigidly moral; exhibiting an exaggerated sense of morality, often to the point of being self-righteous or judgmental.|例句：His hypermoral attitude alienated many of his friends. 他过分道德的态度疏远了他的许多朋友。|近义词：priggish, sanctimonious, moralistic, puritanical|反义词：amoral, immoral, unethical|词性:形容词
