adinon:/ˈædɪnɒn/ | n. | 脂肪酮; 脂酮|A ketone body produced by the liver from fatty acids. It is used as an energy source by the brain and other tissues when glucose is not available. |Example: Adinon levels increase during periods of fasting or low-carbohydrate diets.  脂肪酮水平在禁食或低碳水化合物饮食期间会升高。|Example:  Measuring adinon levels can help assess the body's metabolic state. 测量脂肪酮水平可以帮助评估身体的代谢状态。|Synonym: ketone body, beta-hydroxybutyrate |Antonym: glucose|noun
