agouti:/ˈæɡuːti/ | n. |  刺豚鼠; (毛色)灰褐色的 | A rodent of South and Central America, similar to a large guinea pig, with coarse, speckled fur. |  The agouti scurried through the underbrush. 刺豚鼠窜过灌木丛。 | Synonyms:  paca, acouchi | Antonyms: N/A | noun

agouti:/əˈɡuːti/ | adj. | (毛色)灰褐色的 |  Having a grizzled greyish-brown colour, typically speckled with yellow or black. | The horse had an agouti coat. 这匹马的毛是灰褐色的。 | Synonyms: grizzled, speckled | Antonyms: solid-colored | adjective
