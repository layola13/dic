mustest:/ˈmʌstɪst/ |v. | archaic second person singular of must | You mustest go.  你必须走。 (archaic) | 近义词: had to, should, ought to | 反义词: needn't, shouldn't | 词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *mostest, mosten, from Old English *mōstest, *mōston (“you must, you had to”), from Proto-West Germanic *mōstī, from Proto-Germanic *mōstī (“you must, you had to”).

用法说明:  "Mustest" is an archaic form of "must," specifically the second-person singular form used in the past tense. It is no longer used in modern English.  It carries the same sense of obligation or necessity as "must."
