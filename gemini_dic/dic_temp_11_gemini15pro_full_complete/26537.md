cabezon:/ˈkæbəˌzɒn/ (US), /ˈkæbəzɒn/ (UK)| n.|大头鱼；桶眼鱼；圆鳍鱼|A large-headed sculpin, especially of the Pacific coast of North America.  It's known for its large head and firm, flavorful flesh.|We caught a cabezon while fishing off the coast of California. 我们在加利福尼亚海岸捕到了一条大头鱼。|近义词：sculpin, bullhead|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
