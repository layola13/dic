flighty:/ˈflaɪti/| adj.|轻浮的，反复无常的，易激动的|Given to sudden whims or changes of mind; capricious.  Easily excited or frightened and therefore difficult to control.|She's a bit flighty and tends to change her mind a lot. 她有点轻浮，想法经常变来变去。|The flighty horse was startled by the sudden noise. 那匹胆小的马被突如其来的噪音吓了一跳。|近义词：fickle, capricious, erratic, frivolous, skittish|反义词：stable, steady, reliable, constant|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *flighti, from flight + -y. Related to Old English flīht (“flight”).
