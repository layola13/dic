jabberingly:/ˈdʒæbərɪŋli/
adv.
喋喋不休地，絮絮叨叨地
in a jabbering manner; rapidly and indistinctly.
She talked jabberingly about her day, barely pausing for breath. 她喋喋不休地说着她的一天，几乎没有停下来喘口气。
He jabberingly recounted the whole incident, making it difficult to follow. 他絮絮叨叨地讲述了整个事件，让人很难理解。
近义词: volubly, loquaciously, garrulously
反义词: taciturnly, silently
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From jabber (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Jabber itself likely comes from imitative origins, echoing the sound of rapid, indistinct speech.
