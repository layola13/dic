licitation: /ˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/
n.
招标；投标；征求；恳求
The process of soliciting or inviting bids, proposals, or offers.
The city put out a licitation for the construction of a new bridge.  该市就新桥的建设进行招标。
The company received several bids in response to its licitation.  该公司收到了几份针对其招标的投标。
solicitation, invitation, bidding, tender
coercion, dissuasion
noun
