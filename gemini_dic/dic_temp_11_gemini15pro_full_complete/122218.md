miswear:/ˌmɪsˈswɛər/
verb
滥用；妄用；作伪证；诅咒
To swear falsely; to perjure oneself.  To use (language, oaths, etc.) wrongly or improperly; misuse. To wear (clothing) inappropriately or unsuitably.
He miswore his uniform by wearing the wrong insignia. 他制服上的徽章佩戴错误，滥用了制服。
The witness miswore himself during the trial. 证人在审判期间作了伪证。
misuse, abuse, perjure
use correctly, uphold
