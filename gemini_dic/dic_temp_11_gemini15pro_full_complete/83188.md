guv'nor: /ˈɡʌvənə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɡʌvərnər/ (US)| n.|老板，上司，长官，（尤指儿童对成年男子的称呼）先生|A colloquial and often respectful term of address for a man, especially one in a position of authority, or simply a friendly or familiar term.|例句：Alright, guv'nor, what can I do for you? 好吧，先生，我能为您效劳吗？

例句：The guv'nor told us to finish the job by Friday. 老板让我们周五前完成工作。

|近义词：boss, chief, manager, sir, mister|反义词: employee, subordinate|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened from "governor," influenced by Cockney pronunciation.  Originally referred to the governor of a prison, later extended to other authority figures and then as a general term of respect.
