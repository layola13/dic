mayorlet: /ˈmeɪərˌlɛt/
n.
小市长；年轻的市长
A young or minor mayor.  A diminutive of mayor, suggesting a mayor of a small town or a very young mayor.
The mayorlet of the tiny village welcomed the visiting dignitaries.  这个小村庄的小市长欢迎来访的贵宾。
近义词: young mayor, junior mayor
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) experienced mayor, senior mayor
词性: noun
