all-purpose:/ˌɔːlˈpɜːrpəs/| adj.|多用途的，万能的|Suitable for many different uses or purposes.|This is an all-purpose cleaner that can be used on any surface. 这是一种多用途清洁剂，可以在任何表面上使用。|Example:  She bought an all-purpose flour for baking. 她买了一种通用面粉来烘焙。|Synonyms: versatile, multipurpose, general-purpose|Antonyms: specialized, single-purpose|adjective
