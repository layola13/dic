dolled:/dɑːld/ /dɒld/ | adj. |打扮好的，盛装的|dressed up in fancy or stylish clothes, especially to look attractive|例句：She was all dolled up for the party. 她为派对盛装打扮。

例句：The children were dolled up in their Sunday best. 孩子们穿着他们最好的周日服装打扮得漂漂亮亮。


近义词：dressed up, decked out, spruced up, glammed up |反义词:casual, dressed down|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "doll," meaning to dress up elaborately, as one might a doll. The verb "doll" itself is likely a shortened form of "bedoll," meaning "to adorn," which dates back to the 16th century.
