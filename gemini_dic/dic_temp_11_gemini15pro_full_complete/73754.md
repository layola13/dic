forlornly:/fɔːˈlɔːrnli/ /fərˈlɔːrnli/
adverb
孤零零地，凄凉地
In a sad and lonely way; appearing abandoned and unhappy.
The dog sat forlornly by the empty food bowl.  这只狗孤零零地坐在空食盆旁。
She wandered forlornly through the deserted streets. 她凄凉地走在空荡荡的街道上。
近义词: sadly, miserably, desolately, wretchedly
反义词: happily, cheerfully, joyfully
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From forlorn + -ly.  Forlorn itself comes from Middle English forloren, past participle of forlesen "to lose completely," from Old English forlēosan "to lose, destroy, abandon," from for- "away, completely" + lēosan "to lose."
