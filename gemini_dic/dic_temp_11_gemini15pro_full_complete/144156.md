outshining:/ˌaʊtˈʃaɪnɪŋ/
verb
胜过，优于
surpassing in splendor, achievement, or excellence
例句：
She is outshining all her classmates in mathematics. 她数学成绩胜过所有同学。

He is outshining everyone in the company with his innovative ideas.  他以其创新的想法在公司里胜过所有人。


近义词：
excelling, surpassing, outclassing, outperforming, eclipsing
反义词：
underperforming, lagging behind, paling in comparison
词性
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + shining, present participle of shine.
