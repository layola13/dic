inergetical:/ˌɪnərˈdʒɛtɪkəl/
adjective
惰性的，不活动的
lacking energy or activity; inert.
The heavy meal left him feeling inergetical and sleepy.  这顿丰盛的晚餐让他感到懒洋洋的，昏昏欲睡。
近义词:inert, inactive, passive, sluggish, lethargic
反义词:energetic, active, dynamic, vigorous
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *inergeticus, from iners (“idle, inactive”) +‎ -eticus.  来自拉丁语 *inergeticus，由 iners（“空闲的，不活动的”）+ -eticus 组成。
