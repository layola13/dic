babas:/ˈbɑːbɑːz/| n. |巴巴, 甜点, 甜酒|A type of small yeast cake, sometimes dipped in rum syrup. Also, a type of alcoholic beverage.|例句：The babas were soaked in rum. 巴巴浸泡在朗姆酒里。

例句：She enjoyed a glass of babas after dinner. 晚饭后她享用了一杯巴巴甜酒。|近义词：rum cake, savarin, yeast cake|反义词:None|词性:noun


babas:/ˈbɑːbɑːz/| n. |(波兰语)老妇人，祖母|An informal term for an old woman or grandmother, primarily used in Polish.|例句：My babas always makes the best pierogi. 我的祖母总是做最好吃的饺子。(波兰语语境)

例句：The babas told stories of the old country. 老妇人讲述了故乡的故事。(波兰语语境)|近义词：grandmother, grandma, nana (English equivalents, not Polish)|反义词:None|词性:noun


词源:

From French *baba*, ultimately from Polish *baba* (“old woman, grandmother, cake”).  The cake is named so because of its resemblance to a woman’s skirt.
