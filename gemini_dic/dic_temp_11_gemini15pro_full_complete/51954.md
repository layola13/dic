diachronic:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈkrɑːnɪk/
adjective
历时的，贯时的
Relating to or studying the development of something, such as a language, over a period of time.
A diachronic study of the language traced its evolution from Latin roots. 对该语言的历时研究追溯了它从拉丁语根源的演变过程。
historical, evolutionary
