lelang:/ləˈlɑːŋ/ |n.|拍卖; 荷兰语的拍卖|A public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder.  This word is derived from Dutch.| The lelang for the antique car attracted many collectors. 古董车的拍卖吸引了许多收藏家。| auction, sale | purchase, buy | noun
