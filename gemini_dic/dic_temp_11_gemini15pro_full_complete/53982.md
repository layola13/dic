discretional:/dɪˈskreʃənəl/
adjective
自由决定的，酌情处理的
Left to individual choice or judgment; discretionary.
例句：Managers have discretional powers to award bonuses.  经理有权酌情发放奖金。

例句：The lawyer has discretional power over this case. 律师对这个案件有酌情处理权。

近义词：discretionary, optional, elective
反义词：obligatory, mandatory, compulsory
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *discretionalis, from Latin discretio (“separation, distinction, discernment”), from discernere (“to separate, distinguish”).
