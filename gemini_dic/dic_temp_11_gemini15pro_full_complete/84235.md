half-shared:/ˌhæf ˈʃeərd/ | adj. | 半共享的，部分共享的 |  Shared to some extent but not completely.  Relates to something that is used or experienced in common by two or more parties, but not entirely. |  The couple had a half-shared office space, each with their own designated area but sharing a common printer and meeting table. 这对夫妇有一个半共享的办公空间，每个人都有自己的指定区域，但共享一台打印机和会议桌。

The two siblings had a half-shared childhood, growing up in different households but spending summers together.  这两个兄弟姐妹有一个半共享的童年，在不同的家庭长大，但夏天在一起度过。 | Near synonyms: partially shared, jointly used (in part), co-owned (partially) | Antonyms: fully shared, exclusively owned, private, separate | Adjective
