allocative:/ˈæləkeɪtɪv/| adj.|分配的；配置的|Relating to the distribution and allocation of resources.|例句：Allocative efficiency is achieved when resources are used to produce the goods and services that consumers value most highly. 当资源被用于生产消费者最重视的商品和服务时，就实现了配置效率。|近义词：distributive, apportioning|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology: From allocate + -ive. Allocate comes from Late Latin allocatus, past participle of allocare "to place, allot," from ad- "to" + locare "to place."
