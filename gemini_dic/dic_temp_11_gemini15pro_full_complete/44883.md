crying:/ˈkraɪɪŋ/| adj. n.|哭泣的，令人悲伤的；哭泣|The crying baby needed to be fed. 哭闹的婴儿需要喂奶。|例句：The movie was so sad, it left me crying. 这部电影太悲伤了，让我哭了。

The crying of the child disturbed everyone.  孩子的哭声打扰了所有人。|近义词：weeping, sobbing, wailing|反义词:laughing, rejoicing|adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cryinge*, present participle of *cryen* (“to cry, weep, shout, call out”), from Old French *crier* (“to cry, shout, proclaim”), from Latin *quīrītāre* (“to call for help, shriek”), frequentative of *querī* (“to complain, lament”), related to *queror* (“to complain, lament”).
