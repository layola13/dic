incubational:/ˌɪŋkjuˈbeɪʃənəl/ |adj.| 潜伏期的，孵化期的 |Relating to the period of incubation, during which a disease develops without showing symptoms, or during which eggs are incubated. |The virus has a long incubational period, so symptoms may not appear for weeks. 病毒的潜伏期很长，因此症状可能在几周内都不会出现。| The study focused on the incubational stage of the disease. 这项研究的重点是疾病的潜伏期。| synonyms: latent, dormant, quiescent | antonyms: active, manifest, apparent | adjective
