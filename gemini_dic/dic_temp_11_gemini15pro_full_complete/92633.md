ibans:/ˈaɪbænz/
n.
International Bank Account Numbers
国际银行帐号
IBANs are used to identify bank accounts internationally.  国际银行帐号用于识别国际银行账户。
The IBAN is crucial for making international transfers.  IBAN对于进行国际转账至关重要。
近义词: bank account numbers, account details
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
