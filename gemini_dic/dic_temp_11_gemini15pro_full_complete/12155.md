arval:/ˈɑːrvəl/| adj.|耕地的；有关耕作的|Relating to plowing or arable farming; pertaining to crops.|The arval brotherhood in ancient Rome was associated with rites for ensuring good harvests. 古罗马的阿尔瓦兄弟会与祈求丰收的仪式有关。|近义词：agricultural, agrarian|反义词：non-agricultural, industrial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arv*ā*lis*, from *arvum* (“plowed field”).
