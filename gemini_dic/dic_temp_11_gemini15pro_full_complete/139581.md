obviate:/ˈɒbvieɪt/ (British), /ˈɑːbvieɪt/ (American)| v. |排除；避免；使不必要|to remove a difficulty, especially so that action to deal with it becomes unnecessary| Installing a new software obviates the need for constant updates. 安装新软件就不需要不断更新了。

The new medicine obviates the need for surgery. 这种新药避免了手术的必要性。

|近义词：prevent, preclude, avert, avoid, eliminate|反义词：necessitate, require, cause|词性：动词
