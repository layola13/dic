nominalization:/ˌnɒmɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |名词化；名词化作用|The process of turning a verb or adjective into a noun.  The nominalization of "fail" is "failure."|The frequent nominalization in his writing makes it sound very formal.| 近义词:noun formation, verbal noun formation |反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "verbification")| noun
