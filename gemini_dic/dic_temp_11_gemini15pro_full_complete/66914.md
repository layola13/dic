expelled:/ɪkˈspɛld/| adj. |被驱逐的，被开除的|Having been forced to leave a school, organization, or country.|The expelled student had to find a new school. 被开除的学生不得不另找一所学校。|近义词：ejected, ousted, dismissed, banished|反义词:admitted, accepted|adjective
