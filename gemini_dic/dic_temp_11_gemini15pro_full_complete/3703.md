aggregators:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtərz/
n.
聚合器；集成商；汇集者
A website or program that collects related items of content and displays them or links to them.
Price comparison websites are aggregators.  价格比较网站就是聚合器。
News aggregators pull together news from multiple sources.  新闻聚合器从多个来源汇集新闻。
synonyms: collector, compiler, assembler, consolidator
antonyms: distributor, disperser
noun
