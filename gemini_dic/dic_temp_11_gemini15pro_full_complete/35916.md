cocreator:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtər/| n. |共同创造者; 联合创始人|A person who creates something jointly with another or others. |例句：She is listed as a cocreator of the popular television series. 她被列为这部热门电视剧的共同创作者。

例句：The cocreators of the app met in college.  这个应用程序的联合创始人在大学相识。

近义词：collaborator, coauthor, partner|反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性: noun
