hyrcanian:/hɜːrˈkeɪniən/
| adj.|
希尔卡尼亚的；里海东南部的；指里海东南部的森林|
Relating to or characteristic of Hyrcania, an ancient region south of the Caspian Sea, or its inhabitants.  Also refers to the distinctive forest region of that area.|
The Hyrcanian forests are a unique ecosystem. 希尔卡尼亚森林是一个独特的生态系统。

The Hyrcanian tiger is a critically endangered subspecies.  里海虎是一个极度濒危的亚种。
|
近义词:Caspian (when referring to the region)|
反义词: N/A|
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *Hyrcanius*, from Greek *Ὑρκάνιος* (*Hyrkanios*), from Old Persian *Varkāna-* (meaning "wolf-land").
