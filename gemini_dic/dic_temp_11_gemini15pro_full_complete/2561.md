词源:

Middle English: from Old French *admettre, from Latin admittere ‘send to, let in’, from ad- ‘to’ + mittere ‘send’.  来自中古英语，源于古法语 *admettre，再往前追溯到拉丁语 admittere “发送到，让...进入”，由 ad- “到” + mittere “发送” 组成。
