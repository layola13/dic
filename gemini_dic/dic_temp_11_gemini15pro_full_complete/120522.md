minxish: /ˈmɪŋkʃɪʃ/ | adj. | 荡妇似的，轻佻的，妖媚的 | Suggestive of a minx; flirtatious and playfully mischievous.  | She gave him a minxish look and giggled. 她妖媚地看了他一眼，咯咯地笑了。 | 近义词: flirtatious, coquettish, playful, mischievous | 反义词: demure, modest, reserved | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from "minx," meaning a pert, flirtatious young woman.  "Minx" likely originates from the Low German "minske," meaning "nun," ironically used to refer to a woman of loose morals.  The suffix "-ish" indicates a resemblance or characteristic of the root word.
