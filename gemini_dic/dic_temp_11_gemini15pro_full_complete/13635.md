attitudinal:/ˌætɪˈtjuːdɪnl/
adjective
态度的，由态度引起的
Relating to attitudes or influenced by them.
Her attitudinal problems are affecting her performance at work.  她的态度问题正在影响她的工作表现。
The survey measured attitudinal changes towards environmental issues.  这项调查衡量了人们对环境问题态度的变化。
Synonyms:  mental, psychological, emotional, behavioral
Antonyms:  physiological, physical
词性: adjective
