autosuggested:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. |自动建议的|Offered as a suggestion automatically, typically by a software program or search engine.|例句：The autosuggested search terms helped me find what I was looking for quickly. 自动建议的搜索词条帮助我快速找到了我想要的东西。

例句：As I started typing, autosuggested options appeared in the search bar. 当我开始打字时，搜索栏中出现了自动建议的选项。|近义词：recommended, proposed, prompted|反义词：unprompted, unsolicited|词性:adjective

Etymology: auto- (self) + suggest (to propose) + -ed (past participle suffix)  词源: auto- (自身) + suggest (建议) + -ed (过去分词后缀)
