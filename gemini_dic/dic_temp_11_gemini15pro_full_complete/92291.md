hyphened:/ˈhaɪfənd/| adj.|带连字符的，加连字符的|Joined by a hyphen.  A compound word or name created by joining two or more words with a hyphen. |例句：Her hyphened last name caused some confusion at the hotel check-in. 她在酒店登记入住时，她带连字符的姓氏引起了一些混乱。

例句：He used a hyphened phrase like "state-of-the-art" to describe the new technology. 他用像“state-of-the-art”这样的带连字符的短语来描述这项新技术。|近义词：hyphenated|反义词:unhyphenated|词性:形容词
