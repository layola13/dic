considers:/kənˈsɪdərz/| v.| 考虑，认为，体谅 | thinks carefully about something; believes something to be true; takes into account | He considers her to be his best friend. 他认为她是他最好的朋友。|  consider, contemplate, deem, regard | disregard, ignore | verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *consideren*, from Old French *considerer*, from Latin *considerare* (“to contemplate, observe attentively”), from *com-* (“together”) + *sidus*, *sideris* (“star, constellation”).  Originally, it meant "to inspect the stars," implying careful observation and reflection.
