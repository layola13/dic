incompossible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpɒsəbl/ | adj. | 不相容的，不可能共存的 | Not able to exist together; mutually exclusive. |  The two theories are incompossible; they contradict each other. 这两个理论不相容，它们互相矛盾。| Example: His beliefs about free will and determinism are incompossible. 他关于自由意志和决定论的信仰是不相容的。 | Synonyms: incompatible, irreconcilable, conflicting, contradictory, antithetical | Antonyms: compatible, consistent, harmonious, reconcilable | word type: adjective
