bantered:/ˈbæntərd/ | v. | 开玩笑,打趣,戏谑 | to exchange playful remarks; to tease or mock lightly |  The friends bantered back and forth, their laughter echoing through the room. 朋友们互相打趣，他们的笑声在房间里回荡。| Synonyms: joked, teased, kidded | Antonyms:  insulted, criticized | verb

banter:/ˈbæntər/ | n. |  玩笑,打趣,戏谑 |  playful teasing or mocking remarks | The friendly banter between the siblings showed their close bond. 兄弟姐妹之间友好的玩笑表明了他们亲密的关系。| Synonyms: teasing, joking, raillery | Antonyms: seriousness, criticism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the  early 18th century. Possibly from French *badiner* "to jest," or perhaps of Scandinavian origin.
