overgratify: /ˌoʊvərˈgrætɪfaɪ/
verb
过度满足；过分纵容
to gratify to an excessive degree; to indulge (someone) to excess
He overgratified his children with toys and sweets. 他用玩具和糖果过度满足了他的孩子们。
近义词: overindulge, spoil, pamper
反义词: deprive, restrict
词性: verb
