discolorment:/dɪsˈkʌlər mənt/ | n. | 变色，褪色 |The act or process of changing color, often to a less desirable shade.  A state or instance of being discolored. | The sun caused discoloration of the curtains.  太阳导致窗帘褪色。 | synonyms: fading, bleaching, staining, discoloration, tarnish | antonyms: brightening, coloring, dyeing | noun

discolor:/dɪsˈkʌlər/ | v. | 使变色，使褪色 | To alter or change the color of something, typically to a less attractive shade. | Exposure to sunlight can discolor fabrics. 暴露在阳光下会使织物褪色。 | synonyms: fade, bleach, stain, tarnish | antonyms: brighten, color, dye | verb
