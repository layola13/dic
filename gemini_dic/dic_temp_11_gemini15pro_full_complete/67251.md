expungers:/ɪkˈspʌndʒərz/
n.
消除者，删除者
People or things that expunge something.  Those who remove or erase something, typically records or information.
The expungers worked tirelessly to remove all traces of the scandal from the internet.  消除者不知疲倦地工作，以清除互联网上所有关于丑闻的痕迹。
近义词:erasers, removers, deleters
反义词: preservers, keepers
