circadian:/sɜːrˈkeɪdiən/
adjective
昼夜节律的，生理节奏的
Relating to or exhibiting approximately 24-hour periodicity.
例句：Circadian rhythms control our sleep-wake cycle.  昼夜节律控制着我们的睡眠-觉醒周期。

例句：Jet lag is a disruption of your circadian rhythm.  时差会扰乱你的昼夜节律。

近义词：diurnal, daily
反义词：None

Etymology:
From Latin *circa* (about, approximately) + *dies* (day).  源自拉丁语 *circa*（大约）+ *dies*（天）。
