overnormalized:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度标准化的 |  Excessively normalized; normalized beyond a reasonable or desirable point, often leading to unintended negative consequences or distortions.  This implies that something has been adjusted or standardized to such an extent that it no longer functions effectively or accurately reflects reality. | The data was so overnormalized that it lost its predictive power. 数据被过度标准化，以至于失去了预测能力。| The overnormalized economic model failed to account for unpredictable human behavior.  过度标准化的经济模型未能考虑到不可预测的人类行为。 | synonyms: overstandardized, excessively adjusted | antonyms: undernormalized, unstandardized | adjective
