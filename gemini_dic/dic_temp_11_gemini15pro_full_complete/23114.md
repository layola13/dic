bourd:/bɔːrd/
|n.| jest, joke, game, trick |玩笑，笑话，游戏，恶作剧|He played a bourd on his friend. 他对他的朋友开了个玩笑。|近义词: prank, joke, jest, trick, gag|反义词: seriousness|词性: noun
词源:  From Middle English *bourden, from Old French bourder (“to jest, joke, mock, play, deceive”), of uncertain origin, possibly related to Frankish *bordan (“to boast”) (whence Old French border, “to border, edge, hem”), from Proto-Germanic *burdijaną (“to board”), from *burdą (“board”).
