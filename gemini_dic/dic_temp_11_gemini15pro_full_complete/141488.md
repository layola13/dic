adj.
丰盛的，奢华的，丰富的
Characterized by or exhibiting great abundance; sumptuous.
例句：The opiparous feast was a delight to all the guests. 丰盛的宴会让所有客人都很高兴。

例句：The king threw an opiparous banquet, sparing no expense. 国王举办了一场奢华的宴会，不惜一切代价。

近义词：sumptuous, lavish, luxurious, abundant, copious, profuse
反义词：meager, scanty, sparse, frugal
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin *opiparus*, from *ops* (wealth, abundance) + *-parus* (producing).
