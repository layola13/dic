orthodontist:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɑːntɪst/ | n. | 正畸医生；正牙医生 | A specialist who corrects irregularities of the teeth. |  I need to make an appointment with my orthodontist. 我需要和我的正畸医生预约一下。

I got braces from my orthodontist. 我从我的正畸医生那里戴上了牙套。 | synonyms: dental specialist, braces specialist | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ortho- (straight, correct) + odont- (tooth) + -ist (one who practices).  The "ortho-" prefix comes from Greek orthos meaning "straight" or "correct." "Odont-" comes from Greek odous meaning "tooth."  "-ist" is a suffix indicating a person who practices a particular profession or skill.
