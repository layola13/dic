oversmoke:/ˌoʊvərˈsmoʊk/| vt.|过度熏制; (使)吸烟过度|To smoke excessively or to expose something to too much smoke.|The salmon was oversmoked and tasted bitter.  这鲑鱼熏制过度，尝起来很苦。|oversmoke (v.) The chef oversmoked the meat, giving it a harsh flavor.  厨师把肉熏得太过火了，味道很刺鼻。|近义词: overcook, burn|反义词: undersmoke|词性: verb
