bellum:/ˈbɛləm/| n.|战争，战争状态|War, warfare.  A state of armed conflict between different nations or states or different groups within a nation or state.|The history books described the Punic bellum between Rome and Carthage.|近义词：war, conflict, strife, hostility|反义词：peace, harmony, tranquility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin bellum ("war").  Related to duellum (which became bellum by rhotacism), from duo ("two").  Originally referring to a formal declaration of war by the fetial priests.
