anthracosis:/ˌænθrəˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |炭末沉着症，煤肺|A black lung disease caused by prolonged inhalation of coal dust.  It is characterized by the accumulation of coal dust in the lungs and surrounding tissues.|例句：The miner was diagnosed with anthracosis after years of working underground.  这位矿工在地下工作多年后被诊断出患有炭末沉着症。

例句：Anthracosis is a common occupational hazard for coal miners. 炭末沉着症是煤矿工人常见的职业病。|近义词：black lung, coal worker's pneumoconiosis|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄνθραξ (ánthrax, “coal, carbuncle”) +‎ -ωσις (-ōsis, medical suffix denoting a disease or morbid condition).
