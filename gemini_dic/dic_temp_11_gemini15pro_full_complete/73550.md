forest:/ˈfɔːrɪst/ (US) /ˈfɒrɪst/ (UK)| n.|森林，林区|A large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth.|We went for a walk in the forest. 我们去森林里散步了。|近义词：woods, woodland|反义词：plain, grassland|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *forest (Modern French forêt), from Late Latin forestis (silva) "(outside) forest", from Latin foris "outside".  Originally referring to land outside the king's deer park, later to any wooded area.
