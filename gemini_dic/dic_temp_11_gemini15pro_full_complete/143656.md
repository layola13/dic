outkitchens:/ˈaʊtˌkɪtʃɪnz/| n. |户外厨房|An outdoor kitchen, typically located in a patio or garden, equipped with appliances and features for cooking and entertaining outdoors.|We built a beautiful outkitchen last summer, and now we eat outside almost every night. 我们去年夏天建了一个漂亮的户外厨房，现在我们几乎每天晚上都在外面吃饭。|例句：The outkitchen featured a built-in grill, sink, and refrigerator. 这个户外厨房配有内置烤架、水槽和冰箱。|近义词：outdoor kitchen, summer kitchen|反义词:indoor kitchen|词性:名词
