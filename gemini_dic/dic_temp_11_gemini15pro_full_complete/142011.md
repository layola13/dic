organizability:/ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪbɪləti/
noun
可组织性；组织能力
The quality or state of being organizable; the capacity to be organized.
The project's success depends on the team's organizability and efficiency.  这个项目的成功取决于团队的组织能力和效率。
Synonyms: orderliness, manageability,  systematizability
Antonyms: disorganization, chaos, disorder
词性: 名词
