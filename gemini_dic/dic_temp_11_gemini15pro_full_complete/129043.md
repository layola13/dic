nitratine:/ˈnaɪtreɪtiːn/ /ˈnaɪtrəˌtiːn/
nitratine: n.|硝酸钠，智利硝石|A mineral form of sodium nitrate, NaNO₃, occurring naturally as crystals or granular masses. It is used in fertilizers, explosives, and other chemical products.|Nitratine is an important source of nitrogen for fertilizers.|例句：Large deposits of nitratine are found in Chile.  智利发现了大量的硝酸钠矿床。

例句：Nitratine is readily soluble in water. 硝酸钠易溶于水。|近义词：soda niter, Chile saltpeter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nitratine*, from *nitrate* (nitrate) + *-ine* (suffix forming nouns of chemical substances). Ultimately from Latin *nitrum* (niter).
