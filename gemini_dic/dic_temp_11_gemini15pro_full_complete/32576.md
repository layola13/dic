chirographer:/kaɪˈrɒɡrəfər/
n.
书法家；抄写员；笔迹学家
A person skilled in handwriting, especially calligraphy; a copyist; a handwriting expert.
The chirographer meticulously penned the invitation using elegant, flowing script.  书法家一丝不苟地用优美流畅的字体书写了请柬。
He hired a chirographer to create a beautiful handwritten version of his poem. 他雇了一位书法家为他创作了一份精美的手写诗歌版本。
synonym: calligrapher, scribe, penman
antonym: typist (perhaps in a modern context)
词性: noun
