cooer:/ˈkuːər/| n.|咕咕叫的人(或动物); 柔声细语的人|A person or animal that coos, especially a dove or pigeon.  A person who speaks softly and lovingly.|The cooer of the dove echoed through the trees. 鸽子的咕咕声在树林中回荡。|近义词：dove, pigeon, murmurer, whisperer|反义词:|noun
