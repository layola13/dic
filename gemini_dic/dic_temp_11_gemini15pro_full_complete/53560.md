disbase:/dɪsˈbeɪs/
verb
剥夺…的基座; 从…取下基座; 降低…的地位
To remove from a base or pedestal; to lower in position or importance.
The statue was disbased after the revolution.  革命后雕像被从基座上移了下来。
The new evidence disbased the mayor's reputation. 新的证据降低了市长的声誉。
近义词: dethrone, depose, undermine
反义词: elevate, exalt, raise
词性: verb


disbase:/dɪsˈbeɪs/
verb
(数学)改变…的基数; 将…转换为不同的基数
(mathematics) To change the base of a number; to convert to a different base.
The program can disbase numbers from hexadecimal to binary. 该程序可以将十六进制数转换为二进制数。
We need to disbase the decimal number to octal. 我们需要将十进制数转换为八进制数。
近义词: convert
反义词: N/A
词性: verb
