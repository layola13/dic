affectioned:/əˈfɛkʃənd/ | adj. | 充满深情的，做作的 | Showing affection, especially in a demonstrative way; sometimes suggesting insincerity or artificiality. |  She wrote him long, affectioned letters. 她给他写了长长的充满深情的信。| synonyms: loving, fond, tender, warm, devoted, attached, doting, caring;  affected, pretentious, artificial, put-on, insincere, feigned, pretended, hollow, false | antonyms: cold, uncaring, insincere | adjective

affectioned:/əˈfɛkʃənd/ | adj. | 喜爱的，钟爱的 | Having a liking or fondness for. (archaic) | He was much affectioned to his sister. 他非常喜爱他的妹妹。| synonyms: fond of, partial to, keen on, enamored of, attached to | antonyms: averse to, indifferent to | adjective
