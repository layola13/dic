cicisbeism:/ˌtʃiːtʃɪsˈbeɪɪzəm/ /ˌsiːsɪsˈbeɪɪzəm/
n.
男伴，情夫；(尤指已婚妇女的)情夫；陪伴已婚妇女的男子
The practice of a married woman having a male companion or lover, especially with the knowledge and consent of her husband.
In 18th-century Italy, cicisbeism was a common practice among the aristocracy. 18世纪的意大利，陪伴已婚妇女的风气在上流社会很普遍。

The novel portrays the complexities and hypocrisies surrounding cicisbeism in high society. 这部小说描绘了上流社会围绕男伴关系的复杂性和虚伪性。
