keepership:/ˈkiːpəʃɪp/
n.
保管；守护；管理；职位
The position or duty of a keeper.
He was rewarded with the keepership of the royal forests. 他被授予皇家森林的守护之职。
custody, guardianship, care, charge, management
abandonment, neglect
