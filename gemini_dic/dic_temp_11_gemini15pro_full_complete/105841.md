The hotel room had a kitchenette so we could make our own breakfast. 酒店房间里有一个小厨房，所以我们可以自己做早餐。 | 近义词: mini-kitchen, galley kitchen | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) full kitchen, large kitchen | 词性: noun
