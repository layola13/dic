lactational:/lækˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
哺乳期的，泌乳的
Relating to the production and secretion of milk in mammals.
The lactational consultant helped the new mother with breastfeeding techniques. 哺乳顾问帮助新妈妈学习母乳喂养技巧。
She experienced some lactational difficulties after giving birth. 她产后经历了一些哺乳困难。
synonyms: breastfeeding, nursing, milk-producing
antonyms: non-lactating, post-lactational
