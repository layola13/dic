in-point:/ɪnˈpɔɪnt/| adj.|切题的，恰当的；相关的|Relevant; pertinent; appropriate to the matter at hand.  Apposite; germane.  |This example is very in-point.  这个例子很切题。|近义词：relevant, pertinent, applicable, apposite, germane, appropriate|反义词:irrelevant, inappropriate, inapplicable|词性:形容词
词源: From in + point.  Literally, "at the point," meaning directly addressing the issue.
