mtv: /ˌɛm tiː ˈviː/
n.
Music Television 音乐电视
MTV is a cable channel known for playing music videos.  MTV是一个以播放音乐录影带而闻名的有线电视频道。
MTV revolutionized the music industry. MTV彻底改变了音乐产业。
近义词: VH1, music channel
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider traditional radio)
词性: noun
