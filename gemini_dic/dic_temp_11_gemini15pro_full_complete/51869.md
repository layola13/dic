dextrous:/ˈdekstrəs/| adj.|灵巧的，熟练的|Skillful and quick, especially with the hands.|She is a dextrous knitter, creating intricate patterns with ease. 她是一位灵巧的编织者，能轻松地创造出复杂的图案。|例句：The surgeon's dextrous hands performed the complex operation flawlessly.  外科医生灵巧的双手完美地完成了复杂的手术。|近义词：adept, skillful, nimble, agile|反义词:clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *dexter* "right, favorable," also "skillful," from Proto-Italic *deks-tero-, perhaps from PIE root *deks- "right; south" (compare with "right hand"). The connection between "right" and "skillful" arose because the right hand is typically dominant and therefore more adept.
