isomorph:/ˈaɪsəmɔːrf/| n. |同形体，同构体|Something that has the same form or shape as something else, especially in crystals or organisms.  A structure that is similar or identical to another in shape or organization. |Crystals of salt and ice are isomorphs.|近义词：analogue, equivalent, homologue|反义词:heteromorph|noun
