munged:/mʌndʒd/ | adj. | 混乱的，损坏的 | damaged or spoiled, especially because of being badly treated |  The data was completely munged after the system crash.  系统崩溃后，数据完全损坏了。 | synonyms: garbled, corrupted, distorted, mangled | antonyms: intact, pristine, undamaged | adjective


/mʌndʒd/ | v. | (非正式) 弄乱，损坏（数据） | (informal) to make a mess of (data); to damage or spoil | He munged the code so badly that it took hours to fix. 他把代码弄得一团糟，花了几个小时才修复。 | synonyms: mess up, botch, screw up, ruin | antonyms: fix, repair, improve | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
"Mung" likely originated as jargon in the MIT or Stanford hacker culture in the 1960s. Its precise origin is uncertain, but it's thought to be related to "munge," meaning "to chew or grind up," possibly referencing the way data can be altered or corrupted.  “Mung”很可能起源于 20 世纪 60 年代麻省理工学院或斯坦福大学黑客文化的行话。它的确切起源尚不确定，但它被认为与“munge”有关，意思是“咀嚼或磨碎”，可能指的是数据被更改或损坏的方式。
