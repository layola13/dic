nonmammalian:/ˌnɒnˈmæməliən/ | /ˌnɑːnˈmæməliən/ | adj. | 非哺乳动物的 | Not belonging to the class Mammalia; not mammalian.  |  例句：Nonmammalian vertebrates include fish, amphibians, reptiles, and birds.  非哺乳动物的脊椎动物包括鱼类、两栖动物、爬行动物和鸟类。 | 近义词：anamniote (specifically referring to vertebrates that don't develop an amnion during embryonic development) | 反义词：mammalian | 词性：adjective

nonmammalian:/ˌnɒnˈmæməliən/ | /ˌnɑːnˈmæməliən/ | n. | 非哺乳动物 | An animal that is not a mammal. | 例句：The zoo has a large exhibit of nonmammalians, including reptiles and birds.  这个动物园有一个大型的非哺乳动物展览，包括爬行动物和鸟类。 | 近义词：invertebrate (although this includes many animals that aren't vertebrates at all) | 反义词：mammal | 词性：noun
