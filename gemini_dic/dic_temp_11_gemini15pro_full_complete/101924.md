islanded:/ˈaɪləndɪd/
adjective
孤立的，岛状的
Separated from others; isolated, like an island.
The small town felt islanded after the flood washed away the only bridge.  洪水冲走了唯一的桥梁后，小镇感觉与世隔绝。
The power outage left several neighborhoods islanded from the main grid.  停电导致几个街区与主电网隔绝。
isolated, detached, secluded
connected, integrated
adjective
