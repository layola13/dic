anacanthini:/ˌænəˈkænθɪnaɪ/
n.
棘鳍鱼亚目
A suborder of bony fishes characterized by the absence of spiny rays in their fins, including cods, hakes, and haddock.
The Anacanthini are a commercially important group of fish. 棘鳍鱼亚目是一类具有重要商业价值的鱼类。
