charizard: /ˈtʃɑːrɪzɑːrd/ | n. |喷火龙|A fire and flying type Pokémon introduced in Generation I.  It is the final evolution of Charmander.  Known for its powerful fire attacks and ability to fly.| Ash's Charizard is one of the most iconic Pokémon.  小智的喷火龙是最具代表性的宝可梦之一。| Charizard can mega evolve into two different forms: Mega Charizard X and Mega Charizard Y. 喷火龙可以超级进化成两种不同的形态：超级喷火龙X和超级喷火龙Y。| synonyms: none (proper noun)| antonyms: none (proper noun)| noun
