laggers:/ˈlæɡərz/| n.|落后者，迟缓者|People who lag behind others, either literally in movement or figuratively in progress or development.  Those who are slow to adopt new technologies or ideas are also often called laggers.|The laggers in the marathon finally crossed the finish line hours after the winner. 马拉松比赛中的落后者在获胜者之后几个小时才最终越过终点线。|Synonyms: stragglers, dawdlers, slowpokes|Antonyms: leaders, pioneers, innovators|noun
