growse:/ɡraʊs/ | vi. | 发抖；起鸡皮疙瘩；感到厌恶 | To feel a strong sense of disgust or revulsion. | He growsed at the sight of the rotten food. 他看到腐烂的食物感到恶心。| 近义词：shudder, recoil, flinch | 反义词：delight, relish | verb

growse:/ɡraʊs/ | n. | 厌恶；恐惧；毛骨悚然 | A strong feeling of disgust or revulsion.  | A growse of disgust came over him. 他一阵恶心涌上心头。| 近义词：revulsion, disgust, abhorrence | 反义词：delight, pleasure | noun


词源:
From Middle English *grousen, *grusen, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Old Norse *grøssa (“to shudder, shiver”), or to Middle Dutch gruwelen (“to shudder, shiver”).
