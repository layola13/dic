juno:/ˈdʒuːnoʊ/| n.|朱诺 (罗马神话中掌管婚姻和生育的女神);  六月;  朱诺号(美国宇航局探测木星的航天器)|Juno is the Roman equivalent of Hera, the Greek goddess of marriage and childbirth. 朱诺是希腊婚姻和生育女神赫拉的罗马对应神。|Juno is also the name of the sixth month of the year. 六月也叫朱诺。|NASA's Juno spacecraft is currently orbiting Jupiter. 美国宇航局的朱诺号宇宙飞船目前正在绕木星运行。|Synonyms: Hera (Greek equivalent), June (month)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
