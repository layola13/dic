forego:/fɔːrˈɡoʊ (US) / /fɔːˈɡəʊ (UK)| v.|放弃，摒弃；在…之前|To go without something, especially something pleasant; to refrain from. |She decided to forego dessert in order to lose weight. 她为了减肥决定不吃甜点。|近义词：forgo, abstain from, give up, relinquish, sacrifice|反义词：indulge in, partake of|词性：动词
forego:/fɔːrˈɡoʊ (US) / /fɔːˈɡəʊ (UK)| v.|先于，在…之前|To precede in place, time, or order. (This meaning is less common now.)|The signing of the treaty will forego a period of intense negotiation.  条约的签署之前会有一段激烈的谈判。(这个用法现在比较少见)|近义词：precede, antecede|反义词：follow, succeed|词性：动词
