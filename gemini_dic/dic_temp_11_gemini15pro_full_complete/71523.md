flapjack:/ˈflæpˌdʒæk/ (US), /ˈflæpˌdʒæk/ (UK)| n. |煎饼；燕麦饼|A flat cake made from oats, syrup, and butter, usually eaten for breakfast. |例句：I had a flapjack and a cup of coffee for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一块燕麦饼和一杯咖啡。

flapjack:/ˈflæpˌdʒæk/ (US), /ˈflæpˌdʒæk/ (UK)| n. | (非正式) 妓女| (Slang, derogatory) A prostitute.|例句：He picked up a flapjack on the street corner. 他在街角搭上了一名妓女。(This usage is considered offensive and should be avoided.)

近义词：pancake, oatcake, griddlecake (for the food meaning)|
