moderance:/ˈmɒdərəns/ (US) /ˈmɑːdərəns/|n.|适度；节制；温和|The practice of avoiding extremes, especially in one's behavior or political views. |He preached moderation in all things. 他提倡凡事都要适度。|synonyms: temperance, restraint, self-control|antonyms: excess, immoderation, extremism|noun
