adonist:/ˈædənɪst/| n.|美男子；崇尚美男者|A man who is excessively interested in his own physical appearance and spends a lot of time making himself look attractive.  A man who is devoted to Adonis.|例句：He preened himself like an adonist, checking his reflection in every shiny surface. 他像个美男子一样精心打扮自己，在每一个光亮的表面检查自己的倒影。

例句：The adonist spent hours in the gym sculpting his physique.  这位美男子在健身房花了几个小时雕琢自己的体格。|近义词：dandy, fop, coxcomb, narcissist|反义词：sloven, slob|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Adonis, the beautiful youth loved by Aphrodite in Greek mythology.  The name Adonis itself is of Semitic origin, probably meaning "lord".
