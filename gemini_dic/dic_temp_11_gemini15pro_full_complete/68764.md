farreation:/ˌfærɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 古罗马结婚仪式，谷物婚姻 | An ancient Roman form of marriage, the highest and most solemn of the three forms, involving an offering of spelt cake (farreus panis) to Jupiter. | The farreation ceremony was complex and reserved for patricians.  古罗马的谷物婚姻仪式复杂，仅限于贵族。|  Few couples chose farreation due to its strict requirements.  由于其严格的要求，很少有夫妇选择谷物婚姻。 |  matrimony, wedding, nuptials | divorce, separation | noun
