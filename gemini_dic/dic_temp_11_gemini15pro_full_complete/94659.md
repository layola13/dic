例句：The impressiveness of his knowledge on the subject astonished everyone. 他对这个主题知识的渊博程度令每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：impact, grandeur, magnificence, splendor|反义词：insignificance, mediocrity, ordinariness|词性:noun

Etymology: From impressive + -ness.  "Impressive" comes from the Latin "impressus," past participle of "imprimere," meaning "to press into,"  figuratively "to imprint, impress."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
