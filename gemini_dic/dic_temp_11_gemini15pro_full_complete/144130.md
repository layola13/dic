outsearch:/ˈaʊtsɜːrtʃ/| v.| 比…搜索得更彻底;  胜过…的搜索 | They outsearched their rivals and found the rare manuscript first. 他们比对手搜索得更彻底，先找到了那份珍稀的手稿。 | 近义词: surpass, excel, outdo, outperform | 反义词:  be inferior to, be worse than, lose to |词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing, exceeding") + "search" (verb meaning "to look for something")
