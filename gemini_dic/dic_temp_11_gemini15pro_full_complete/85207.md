hark:/hɑːrk/ (UK) /hɑːrk/ (US)| v. int.|倾听，留心听；注意|To listen attentively; pay heed.|例句：Hark! I hear the bells. 听！我听到钟声了。

例句：He harked back to his childhood. 他回忆起了他的童年。|近义词：listen, heed, pay attention, attend|反义词:ignore, disregard|verb
