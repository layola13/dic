misdemean:/ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːn/| vt.| 贬低；使降低身份；使行为不端|To behave in a way that is considered to be wrong or not acceptable.|He misdemeaned himself by accepting bribes. 他因收受贿赂而自贬身份。|Synonyms: debase, degrade, discredit|Antonyms: exalt, elevate, honor|Verb

misdemeanor:/ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːnər/| n.| 轻罪；不端行为|A minor wrongdoing.|Stealing a candy bar is a misdemeanor. 偷一块糖果棒是轻罪。|Synonyms: offense, infraction, transgression|Antonyms: felony, serious crime|Noun
