latching onto (something) =  抓住（某个东西），理解（某个概念） He latched onto the idea immediately. 他立刻理解了这个想法。
latching mechanism = 锁闩机构  The latching mechanism of the car door is faulty.  车门的锁闩机构有问题。
a latching relay =  自锁继电器  A latching relay maintains its state even after the control signal is removed. 自锁继电器即使在控制信号移除后也能保持其状态。
latching current = 锁存电流  The latching current is the minimum current required to maintain the latch in the closed position. 锁存电流是保持闩锁处于关闭位置所需的最小电流。
magnetic latching = 磁性锁存  Magnetic latching is a reliable way to secure cabinets and drawers. 磁性锁存是固定橱柜和抽屉的可靠方法。
