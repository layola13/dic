nondescriptness:/ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪptnəs/ | n. | 平凡；毫无特色；难以形容 | The nondescriptness of the building made it easy to overlook. 这栋楼平平无奇，很容易被忽略。|  Lack of distinguishing features or characteristics; the quality of being ordinary and unremarkable.  The nondescriptness of the hotel room made it blend in with every other hotel room he'd ever stayed in.  酒店房间毫无特色，让他觉得和以往住过的其他酒店房间没什么两样。 | synonyms: ordinariness, anonymity, unremarkableness, featurelessness, characterlessness | antonyms: distinctiveness, uniqueness, individuality, remarkableness | noun
