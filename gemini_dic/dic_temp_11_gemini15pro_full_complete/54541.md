dislikeness:/dɪsˈlaɪknɪs/
n.
不喜欢，厌恶
The child's dislikeness for vegetables was obvious.
这个孩子不喜欢蔬菜是很明显的。
He expressed his dislikeness for the new policy.
他对新政策表示了不满。
aversion, antipathy, distaste, hostility
liking, fondness, preference
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dislike + -ness.  Dislike itself comes from dis- (not) + like.
