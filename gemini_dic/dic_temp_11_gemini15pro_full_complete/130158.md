non-resistance:/ˌnɒn rɪˈzɪstəns/ | n. | 不抵抗，不反抗，顺从 | The policy of non-resistance is often associated with certain religious or philosophical beliefs. 不抵抗政策通常与某些宗教或哲学信仰有关。|  He chose non-resistance when faced with the aggressive stranger. 面对咄咄逼人的陌生人，他选择了不抵抗。 | 近义词：passivity, submission, acquiescence | 反义词：resistance, defiance, opposition | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + resistance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "resistance" comes from the Latin "resistentia," meaning "opposition."
