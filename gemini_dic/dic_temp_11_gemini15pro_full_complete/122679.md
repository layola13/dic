modules:/ˈmɒdʒuːlz/ (British), /ˈmɑːdʒuːlz/ (American)| n.| 模块，组件|A module is a self-contained unit of software or hardware that can be combined with other modules to form a larger system.  It's often designed with a specific function or purpose.|The software is made up of several different modules.  这个软件由几个不同的模块组成。|synonyms: components, units, sections, parts|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

modules:/ˈmɒdʒuːlz/ (British), /ˈmɑːdʒuːlz/ (American)| n.|  课程单元，学习模块|A module is a self-contained unit of a course of study.|Students can choose from a range of optional modules. 学生可以从一系列可选模块中进行选择。|synonyms: units, courses, subjects, components|antonyms: whole curriculum, entire program|noun
