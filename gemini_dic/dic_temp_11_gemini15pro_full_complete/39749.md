consolator:/ˈkɒnsəleɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəleɪtər/ (US)| n. |安慰者，慰藉者| someone who comforts and cheers a person experiencing grief or misfortune | He was a true consolator to his grieving friend. 他是他悲痛的朋友的真正慰藉者。| synonyms: comforter, soother, sympathizer | antonyms: tormentor, afflicter | noun

consolation:/ˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnsəˈleɪʃn/ (US)| n. |安慰，慰藉| comfort received by a person after a loss or disappointment | The book was a great consolation to her during her illness. 这本书在她生病期间给了她很大的安慰。| synonyms: comfort, solace, relief | antonyms: distress, anguish, torment | noun
