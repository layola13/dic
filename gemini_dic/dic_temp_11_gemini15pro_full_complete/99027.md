interagreement:/ˌɪntərəˈɡriːmənt/ | n. |相互协议，互相一致|An agreement between two or more parties.  It emphasizes the mutual nature of the agreement. |The interagreement between the two companies ensured a smooth merger. 两家公司之间的相互协议确保了合并的顺利进行。| The interagreement on the project's goals was crucial for its success.  项目目标上的相互一致对其成功至关重要。 | synonym: accord, concord, consensus, harmony | antonym: disagreement, discord, conflict | noun
