afoam:/əˈfoʊm/| adj.| 满是泡沫的，起泡沫的| Covered with foam; foaming.| The beer was afoam in the mug.  啤酒杯里满是泡沫的啤酒。|例句：The waves crashed against the rocks, afoam with sea spray. 海浪拍打着岩石，激起了白色的浪花。|近义词：foaming, frothy, bubbly|反义词:flat, still|词性:形容词

Etymology: a- + foam
From Old English *afām, from *afāmian "to foam," from *fām "foam."  The prefix a- is an intensive prefix.
