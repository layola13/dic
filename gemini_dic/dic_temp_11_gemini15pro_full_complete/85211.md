harkened:/ˈhɑːrkənd/ (US) /ˈhɑːkənd/ (UK)| v.|倾听，留心，注意|to listen attentively; to pay heed|例句：The children harkened to their mother's story. 孩子们倾听着他们母亲的故事。|近义词：listened, heeded, attended, paid attention|反义词：ignored, disregarded|词性:动词

词源: Middle English *herknen*, from Old English *heorcnian* “to listen, hearken,” from *heorcnung* "hearing, listening."  Related to Dutch *horchen*, German *horchen* "to listen."
