haemastatics:/ˌhɛməˈstætɪks/ | n. |止血药，止血法| The study or practice of stopping bleeding. | The surgeon is an expert in haemastatics.  这位外科医生是止血专家。| Synonyms: haemostatics, hemostatics | Antonyms:  coagulants (although often used interchangeably, coagulants specifically refer to substances that promote clotting, while haemostatics encompass a broader range of methods to stop bleeding) | noun


haemostatic:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ | adj. |止血的| Having the property of stopping bleeding. | The nurse applied a haemostatic dressing to the wound. 护士在伤口上敷了止血敷料。| Synonyms: styptic, antihemorrhagic | Antonyms:  hemorrhagic | adjective
