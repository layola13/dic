explorement:/ɪkˈsplɔːrmənt/
n.
探索，探究
The act of exploring or examining something.
His explorement of the Amazon rainforest led to the discovery of a new species.  他对亚马逊雨林的探索导致了一个新物种的发现。

The explorement of new ideas is essential for progress. 对新思想的探索对进步至关重要。

近义词:exploration, investigation, inquiry
反义词:neglect, ignorance
词性:名词
