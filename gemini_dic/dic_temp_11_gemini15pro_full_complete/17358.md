beaconless:/ˈbiːkənlɪs/| adj.|无灯塔的，无指引的，无信号的|Without a beacon; lacking guidance or signal.|The ship drifted helplessly in the beaconless night.  这艘船在没有灯塔的夜晚无助地漂流。|近义词：dark, guideless, unmarked, signal-less|反义词:guided, marked, lit|词性:形容词

Etymology:
beacon (n.) + -less (suffix)
beacon: from Old French *beacon, variant of *balquin "beacon, signal fire," from a Germanic source (compare Old High German balcho "beam, log").
-less: Old English -lēas "free from, without," from Proto-Germanic *-lausaz, from PIE root *leik- "to leave, forsake."
