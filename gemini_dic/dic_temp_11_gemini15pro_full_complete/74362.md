frailly:/ˈfreɪlli/| adv.|脆弱地，虚弱地|In a frail or delicate manner; weakly.|Her frailly hand reached out for the glass. 她那虚弱的手伸向玻璃杯。|近义词：weakly, feebly, delicately|反义词：strongly, robustly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *frailliche, from Old French frailement, from fraile (“frail”) + -ment.
