acts out:/ækts aʊt/| phrasal verb | 付诸行动；表现出来；把…表演出来 | to express (an emotion, fantasy, etc) by acting it out, as in a play | The child acted out his anger by hitting his brother. 孩子通过打弟弟来发泄他的愤怒。| express, demonstrate, perform | suppress, internalize | verb phrase

acts out:/ækts aʊt/| phrasal verb | 将…付诸行动；演出；表演 | to perform a role or a scene from a play | The students acted out the story of Romeo and Juliet. 学生们表演了罗密欧与朱丽叶的故事。| perform, enact, represent |  conceal, hide | verb phrase
