double-cross:/ˈdʌbəl krɔs/ (US), /ˈdʌbəl krɒs/ (UK)| v. n.|欺骗，背叛；出卖；双重欺骗|To deceive or betray someone, especially after feigning friendship or alliance.|He double-crossed his partner and stole all the money. 他欺骗了他的伙伴，偷走了所有的钱。|Synonyms: betray, deceive, cheat, backstab|Antonyms: support, help, assist|verb
