leaderlessness: /ˈliːdələsnəs/
n.
缺乏领导；群龙无首
the state of lacking a leader or leadership
The leaderlessness of the group resulted in chaos. 这个小组缺乏领导导致了混乱。
The team's leaderlessness hampered their progress.  团队缺乏领导力阻碍了他们的进步。

近义词: lack of leadership, absence of leadership, rudderlessness
反义词: leadership, direction, guidance
词性: noun
