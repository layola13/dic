encroachers:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃərz/| n. |侵入者，侵占者|People who intrude on the rights or property of others.  Those who gradually advance beyond proper or usual limits.| The encroachers were warned to leave the property immediately.  侵入者被警告立即离开该房产。| synonyms: intruders, trespassers, invaders, infringers | antonyms: defenders, protectors, guardians | noun
