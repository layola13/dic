apricate:/ˈæprɪkeɪt/ | adj. | 晒太阳的，向阳的 | exposed to sunshine; basking in the sun | The lizard was apricate on a warm rock.  蜥蜴在一块温暖的岩石上晒太阳。 | 近义词: sunny, sunlit, sun-drenched | 反义词: shaded, shadowy | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *apricātus, past participle of *apricāre (“to bask in the sun”), from apricus (“sunny”).
