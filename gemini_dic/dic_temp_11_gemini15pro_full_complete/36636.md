collectivize:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪz/| v. | 集体化，公有化 | To organize (something) on the basis of collective ownership or control.  To bring under collective control, especially by the state. | The government collectivized agriculture in the 1950s. 政府在20世纪50年代将农业集体化了。

The farming industry was collectivized to increase production. 农业被集体化以提高产量。

| 近义词：nationalize, communalize, socialize | 反义词：privatize, individualize | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *collectiviser*, from *collectif* (collective) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs from adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *colligere* (to collect).
