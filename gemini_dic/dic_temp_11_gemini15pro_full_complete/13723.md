atypicalities:/ˌeɪtɪpɪˈkælɪtiz/| n. |非典型性，异常情况|The report highlighted several atypicalities in the patient's blood work. 报告强调了病人血液化验中的几项异常情况。|例句：The study revealed some atypicalities in the behavior of the test subjects.  这项研究揭示了测试对象行为中的一些异常情况。

例句：The atypicalities in the data suggest a need for further investigation.  数据中的异常情况表明需要进一步调查。|近义词：irregularities, abnormalities, anomalies, deviations|反义词：normalities, typicalities|词性:名词

Etymology:
From a- (not) + typical + -ity (state or quality) + -ies (plural)
