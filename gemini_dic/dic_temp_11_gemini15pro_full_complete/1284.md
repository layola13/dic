account for:/əˈkaʊnt fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 解释；说明；占（比例）；（数量上）构成 | to explain the reason for something or the cause of something |  The poor weather may account for the small crowd.  糟糕的天气可能是人少的原因。| synonyms: explain, justify, answer for, make up, comprise | antonyms:  obfuscate, confuse | verb phrase

account for:/əˈkaʊnt fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 对…负有责任 | to be responsible for what happens to something or someone |  The government has to account for its spending. 政府必须对它的开支负责。 | synonyms: be responsible for, be liable for | antonyms: evade responsibility for | verb phrase
