misway:/mɪsˈweɪ/ | v. | 使迷路，使误入歧途 | To cause someone to become lost or take a wrong path. | The confusing signs miswayed the travelers.  令人困惑的标志使旅行者迷路了。| 近义词: mislead, misdirect | 反义词: guide, direct | 词性: 动词
