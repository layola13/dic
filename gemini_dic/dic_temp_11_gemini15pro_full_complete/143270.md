outdistrict:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstrɪkt/
adjective
外区的；郊区的
Located outside the main district or central area, typically referring to a less populated or more rural area.
The outdistrict schools often have smaller class sizes.  郊区的学校通常班级规模较小。
They live in an outdistrict suburb of the city. 他们住在城市的远郊。
近义词: suburban, outlying, peripheral, exurban
反义词: central, urban, inner-city
词性: adjective

outdistrict:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstrɪkt/
noun
郊区；外区
A region or area located outside the main district or central area.
The outdistricts saw a surge in population growth last year. 去年郊区的人口增长激增。
Many people prefer the peace and quiet of the outdistricts. 许多人更喜欢郊区的宁静。
近义词: outskirts, suburbs, periphery
反义词: center, downtown, city center
词性: noun


词源:  "out-" (meaning outside) + "district"
