nibblingly:/ˈnɪblɪŋli/
adverb|少量地，一点一点地|in a nibbling manner; by taking small bites or amounts.|She nibblingly ate the cookie, savoring each tiny piece. 她一小口一小口地吃着饼干，细细品味着每一小块。|例句：He nibblingly tasted the soup, unsure if it was too salty. 他小心翼翼地尝了尝汤，不确定是不是太咸了。|近义词：gingerly, cautiously, tentatively|反义词：voraciously, greedily|词性: 副词

词源:  From nibble (verb) + -ing + -ly.  "Nibble" comes from the Middle English *knibblen*, frequentative of *nibben*, "to nip, bite gently," of uncertain origin.

PHRASE->
Not commonly used in phrases.  "Nibblingly" is an adverb describing the *manner* of an action, and thus doesn't readily form set phrases.  It modifies verbs related to eating and trying small amounts.
