overindulgent: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒənt/ | adj. | 过分放纵的，溺爱的 | Excessively indulgent; allowing oneself or another person to have or do too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink. | He gave the children an overindulgent look. 他用溺爱的眼神看着孩子们。|  She was a bit overindulgent with her son. 她有点溺爱她的儿子。| 近义词: pampering, spoiling, excessive, immoderate | 反义词: moderate, temperate, abstemious, disciplined | 词性: 形容词

词源: From over- + indulgent.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much," and "indulgent" comes from the Latin "indulgentem" (nominative indulgens), present participle of "indulgere" meaning "to be kind or tender to, yield to, gratify."
