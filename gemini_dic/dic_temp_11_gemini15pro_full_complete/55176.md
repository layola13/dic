disseminators:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtərz/| n. |传播者，散布者|A disseminator is a person or thing that spreads something, especially information or ideas.|The media are important disseminators of news and information. 媒体是新闻和信息的重要传播者。|
The internet has become a powerful disseminator of knowledge. 互联网已成为强大的知识传播者。|
Synonyms: spreader, distributor, propagator|Antonyms: suppressor, withholder|noun

disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/| v. |传播，散布|To disseminate information or ideas means to spread them widely.|The organization disseminates information about health and safety. 该组织传播有关健康和安全的信息。|
They disseminated the findings of the study through a press release. 他们通过新闻稿发布了研究结果。|
Synonyms: spread, distribute, circulate, propagate|Antonyms: suppress, withhold|verb
