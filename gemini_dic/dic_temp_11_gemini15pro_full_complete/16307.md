barahir:/ˈbɑːrɑːhɪr/
proper noun|巴拉希尔|A Mannish chieftain who lived near the Gladden Fields during the First Age of Middle-earth in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium. He is the father of Beren and ancestor of Elrond and Elros.|Barahir was a renowned warrior and leader who fought against Morgoth's forces.  His son, Beren, later became famous for stealing a Silmaril from Morgoth.|Near synonyms: chieftain, leader, warrior|Antonyms: follower, coward|word type: proper noun
