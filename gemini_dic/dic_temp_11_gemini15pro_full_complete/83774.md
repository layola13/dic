hairdo:/ˈheɪrdʊː/ (US), /ˈheəduː/ (UK)| n. |发型，发式|A way of styling the hair, especially a woman's.  A particular arrangement of the hair.|例句：She had a new hairdo for the party. 她为派对做了个新发型。

例句：I love your hairdo!  我喜欢你的发型！|近义词：hairstyle, coiffure, coif|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "messy hair" or "unstyled hair")|词性: noun
