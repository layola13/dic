acquisitional:/ˌækwɪˈzɪʃənəl/
adjective
获取的; 收购的;  习得的
Relating to acquisition, the process of obtaining something.
The acquisitional cost of the property was higher than expected. 该房产的购置成本高于预期。
His acquisitional skills in language learning are impressive. 他的语言学习习得能力令人印象深刻。
近义词: obtained, acquired, procured
反义词:  lost, sold, disposed of
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *acquisition(em) (nominative acquisitio), from Latin acquīsītiō (“an obtaining”), from acquīsītus (“obtained”), past participle of acquīrō (“obtain, get, acquire, earn”).
