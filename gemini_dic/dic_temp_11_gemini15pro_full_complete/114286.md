mainly:/ˈmeɪnli/| adv.|主要地，大体上|Primarily, for the most part.  It indicates that something is the most important or largest part of something else.|例句：The audience was mainly composed of young people.  观众主要由年轻人组成。|例句：I mainly eat vegetables and fruits. 我主要吃蔬菜和水果。|近义词：primarily, chiefly, largely, mostly|反义词：slightly, marginally, minimally|词性：副词

词源: From Middle English *mainly, derived from main + -ly. "Main" traces back to Old French "magne," from Latin "magnus" meaning "great."
