hyetography:/ˌhaɪɪˈtɒɡrəfi/ /ˌhaɪɪˈtɑːɡrəfi/
noun
雨量记录；雨量分布图
The scientific description and recording of rainfall.
Hyetography plays a crucial role in understanding regional climate patterns. 雨量记录在理解区域气候模式中起着至关重要的作用。

The hyetography of the region shows a distinct increase in rainfall during the monsoon season.  该地区的雨量记录显示，季风季节降雨量明显增加。
近义词: pluviography, rainfall recording
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *hyetós* (rain) + *-graphía* (writing).
