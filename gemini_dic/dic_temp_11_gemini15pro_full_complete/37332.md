commenceable:/kəˈmɛnsəbl/| adj.|可以开始的，可实施的|Something that is commenceable is able to be begun or started.  It implies that the necessary conditions or preparations are in place for something to start.|The project is commenceable as soon as we receive the funding.  该项目一旦我们收到资金就可以开始。|The construction is commenceable next week.  施工下周可以开始。|近义词：startable, beginnable, initiatable|反义词：unstartable, impossible, impractical|词性:形容词
commence:/kəˈmɛns/| v.|开始，着手|To begin or start something.|The ceremony will commence at noon. 典礼将于中午开始。|We will commence construction on the new building next month. 我们将于下个月开始建造新大楼。|近义词：begin, start, initiate|反义词：end, finish, conclude|词性:动词
