interambulacral:/ˌɪntəræmbjʊˈlækrəl/
adjective
步带间的
Located between the ambulacra (the radial areas bearing tube feet) of an echinoderm.
The interambulacral plates of the sea urchin are covered in spines. 海胆的步带间骨板覆盖着棘刺。
近义词: interradial
反义词: ambulacral
词性: adjective
