nontropical:/ˌnɑːnˈtrɑːpɪkəl/| adj.|非热带的|Not located in or characteristic of the tropics; not tropical. |The nontropical plants thrived in the cooler climate. 这些非热带植物在较凉爽的气候中茁壮成长。|Example:  Nontropical cyclones can form over colder waters. 非热带气旋可以在较冷的水域形成。|Synonyms:  extratropical, temperate, moderate|Antonyms:  tropical|adjective
