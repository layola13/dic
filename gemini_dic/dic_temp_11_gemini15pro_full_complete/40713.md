convertibly:/kənˈvɜːrtəbli/ | adv. | 可兑换地，可变换地 | In a way that can be converted or exchanged, especially into another form, substance, or currency. |  The bonds are convertibly into common stock. 这些债券可以转换成普通股。| Synonyms: exchangeably, interchangeably, tradably | Antonyms: non-convertibly, irreversibly | Adverb

convert:/kənˈvɜːrt/ | v. | 转换，改变 | To change the form, character, or function of something. | He converted his garage into an office. 他把车库改成了办公室。| Synonyms: transform, change, modify | Antonyms: maintain, preserve, retain | Verb

convert:/ˈkɒnvɜːt/ | n. | 皈依者，改变信仰者 | A person who has been persuaded to change their religious or political belief system. | He was a convert to Buddhism. 他是佛教皈依者。| Synonyms: proselyte, neophyte | Antonyms: apostate | Noun
