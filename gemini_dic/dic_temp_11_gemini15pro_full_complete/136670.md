nonsecludedness:/ˌnɒnsɪˈkluːdɪdnəs/
n.
非隐蔽性；不隔离性
The state of not being secluded or isolated.
The nonsecludedness of the park made it feel unsafe at night.  公园不封闭，晚上感觉不安全。
Nearness, openness, accessibility
