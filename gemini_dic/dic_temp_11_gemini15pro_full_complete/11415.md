argentite:/ˈɑːrdʒənˌtaɪt/| n.|辉银矿|A mineral form of silver sulfide (Ag₂S), an important silver ore.  It is lead-gray, soft, and has a metallic luster.|Argentite is a valuable source of silver. 辉银矿是重要的银来源.|Synonyms: acanthite (a low-temperature polymorph of Ag₂S), silver glance|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|Noun
