caducous:/kəˈdjuːkəs/
adjective
脱落的；早落的
Falling off or shed at an early stage of development, as leaves, fruit, or flower parts.
The petals of the flower are caducous, falling off soon after the flower opens.  这朵花的花瓣是早落的，花一开就掉下来了。
The plant's caducous stipules protect the developing bud but fall off once the leaf unfolds. 这种植物的托叶保护着发育中的芽，但一旦叶子展开就会脱落。
近义词: deciduous, fugacious, ephemeral
反义词: persistent, permanent
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *cadūcus, from cadō (“I fall”).
