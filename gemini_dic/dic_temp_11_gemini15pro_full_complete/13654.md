attorneyship: /əˈtɜːrniʃɪp/
n.
律师的职位；律师身份
the position or office of an attorney
He began his attorneyship in a small law firm. 他在一个小型律师事务所开始了他的律师生涯。

近义词: legal practice, law practice
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider non-legal professions)


词性: noun

词源: From attorney + -ship.  "Attorney" comes from Old French *atorné "appointed (person)," past participle of atorner "to appoint, assign, equip." "-ship" is a suffix forming abstract nouns denoting state, condition, office, dignity, skill, etc.
