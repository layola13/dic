luteolin: /ˈluːtiːəlɪn/ | n. | 木犀草素 | A flavonoid, a yellow crystalline plant pigment found in many plants, including parsley, celery, thyme, and chamomile. It has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties and is being studied for its potential health benefits. | Luteolin is found in high concentrations in celery.  木犀草素在芹菜中的浓度很高。|  Luteolin supplements are available in health food stores. 木犀草素补充剂可以在保健食品商店买到。 | synonyms: flavonoid, bioflavonoid | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound.  Could consider substances with opposing effects, but that's context-dependent.) | noun
