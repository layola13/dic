evolving:/ɪˈvɒlvɪŋ/ (US) /iˈvɒlvɪŋ/ (UK)
adjective
发展的，演变的，进化的
Developing gradually, changing over time, especially from a simple to a more complex form.
例句：The company's evolving marketing strategy reflects changes in consumer behavior.  公司不断发展的营销策略反映了消费者行为的变化。

例句：The evolving situation requires us to be flexible and adaptable. 不断变化的局势要求我们灵活应变。


近义词：developing, progressing, changing, advancing, growing, maturing
反义词：static, unchanging, fixed, stagnant, regressing
词性: adjective
