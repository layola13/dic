nonperversely:/ˌnɒnpəˈvɜːsli/ | adv. | 不反常地，不歪曲地 | In a way that is not perverse; straightforwardly. | He interpreted the rules nonperversely, sticking to their literal meaning. 他不歪曲地解释了规则，坚持其字面意思。|  She answered the question nonperversely and honestly. 她直截了当地、诚实地回答了这个问题。 | 近义词: straightforwardly, directly, plainly, simply | 反义词: perversely, distortedly, wrongly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + perversely (in a perverse manner).  Perverse comes from Old French *pervers, from Latin perversus, past participle of pervertere "to overturn, corrupt," from per- "thoroughly" + vertere "to turn."
