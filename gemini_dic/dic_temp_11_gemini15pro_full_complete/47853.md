deceit:/dɪˈsiːt/| n. |欺骗，欺诈；诡计|an act or device intended to deceive; trickery|He was accused of obtaining money by deceit. 他被指控以欺诈手段获取钱财。|Synonyms: deception, fraud, trickery, chicanery, guile, subterfuge|Antonyms: honesty, truthfulness, sincerity, candor|noun


deceit:/dɪˈsiːt/| vt. |欺骗，蒙骗|to cause (someone) to believe something that is not true, typically in order to gain some personal advantage|The company deceived customers by selling them faulty products.  这家公司通过销售有缺陷的产品来欺骗顾客。|Synonyms: mislead, delude, beguile, trick, dupe, defraud, cheat|Antonyms: enlighten, inform, be honest with|verb
