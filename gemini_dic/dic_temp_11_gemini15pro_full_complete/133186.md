noneviction:/ˌnɒnɪˈvɪkʃən/ | n. | 不驱逐，禁止驱逐 | The noneviction policy protected tenants from being unfairly removed from their homes.  禁止驱逐政策保护租户不被不公平地赶出家门。| Example: The city council passed a noneviction ordinance. 市议会通过了一项禁止驱逐的法令。 | Synonym: non-removal, non-expulsion | Antonym: eviction | noun

词源:
"noneviction" is a compound word formed from "non-" (meaning "not") and "eviction". "Eviction" comes from the Old French word "esvincir" meaning "to conquer, to evict," ultimately from Latin "evincere," meaning "to overcome, prevail."
