chastisable:/ˈtʃæstaɪzəbl/
adjective
可责备的，应受惩罚的
Deserving of or liable to criticism or punishment.
His behavior was chastisable, but not criminal. 他的行为应该受到批评，但不构成犯罪。

近义词:reproachable, censurable, blameworthy, culpable
反义词:blameless, innocent, commendable, laudable
词性:adjective
