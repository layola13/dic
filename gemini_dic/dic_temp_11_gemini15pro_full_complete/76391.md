gaffe:/ɡæf/| n.|失礼, 失态, 出丑|An unintentional act or remark causing embarrassment to its originator; a blunder.|例句：He made a social gaffe by arriving late. 他因迟到而失礼了。

例句：Her gaffe cost her the election. 她的失误让她输掉了选举。|近义词：blunder, mistake, error, faux pas|反义词:tact, diplomacy|noun
