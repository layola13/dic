deductibility:/dɪˌdʌktɪˈbɪləti/| n.|可扣除性|The quality of being deductible, especially from taxable income.  The ability of an expense to be subtracted from income when calculating taxes.|例句：The deductibility of charitable donations is a major incentive for giving.  慈善捐款的可扣除性是捐赠的主要动机。

例句：He questioned the deductibility of certain business expenses. 他质疑某些业务支出的可扣除性。|近义词：allowability, claimability|反义词：non-deductibility
