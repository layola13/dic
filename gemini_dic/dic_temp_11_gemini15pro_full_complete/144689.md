ouzo:/ˈuːzoʊ/ (US), /ˈuːzɒ/ (UK)| n.|茴香酒，乌佐酒|A strong, dry anise-flavored aperitif that is the national alcoholic drink of Greece, typically served with water which turns it milky white.|例句：He ordered a glass of ouzo. 他点了一杯茴香酒。

例句：The ouzo tasted strongly of licorice. 这茴香酒有很浓的甘草味。

近义词：aperitif, pastis, sambuca|反义词:None|词性:noun
