nostalgia:/nɒˈstældʒə/ /nɑːˈstældʒə/| n. |怀旧，念旧|A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations.|例句：A wave of nostalgia swept over her as she looked at the old photographs. 当她看着旧照片时，一阵怀旧之情涌上心头。

例句：He felt a pang of nostalgia for his childhood. 他对自己的童年感到一阵怀旧之情。|近义词：reminiscence, wistfulness, homesickness, sentimentality|反义词:none|词性:noun
