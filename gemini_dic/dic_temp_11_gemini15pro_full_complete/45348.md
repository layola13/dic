cumulonimbus:/ˌkjuːmjʊləˈnɪmbəs/| n. |积雨云|A dense, towering vertical cloud forming from water vapor carried by powerful upward air currents. These clouds are associated with thunderstorms and atmospheric instability.|The pilot skillfully navigated the plane around the massive cumulonimbus. 飞行员巧妙地驾驶飞机绕过了巨大的积雨云。|

The dark cumulonimbus signaled the approaching storm.  乌黑的积雨云预示着暴风雨即将来临。
