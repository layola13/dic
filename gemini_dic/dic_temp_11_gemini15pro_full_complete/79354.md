glen:/ɡlɛn/| n. |峡谷，溪谷|A narrow valley, especially one with a stream running through it.|We hiked through the glen, enjoying the shade of the trees and the sound of the rushing water. 我们徒步穿过峡谷，享受着树荫和流水的声音。|The glen was filled with wildflowers in the spring. 春天，峡谷里开满了野花。|近义词：valley, ravine, canyon|反义词:mountain, peak|词性:名词

词源: From Scottish Gaelic *gleana*, from Old Irish *glinn* (“valley, glen”), from Proto-Celtic *glennā*. Cognate with Welsh *glyn*, Breton *gleann*, Cornish *glyn*.
