bottle-feeds:/ˈbɒtlˌfiːdz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtlˌfiːdz/ (US)| v. |用奶瓶喂养|To feed a baby or young animal with milk or formula from a bottle, rather than from the breast or udder.|The vet advised us to bottle-feed the orphaned kittens.  兽医建议我们用奶瓶喂养这些失去母亲的小猫。|Synonyms: formula-feed|Antonyms: breastfeed, nurse|Verb

bottle-feeds:/ˈbɒtlˌfiːdz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtlˌfiːdz/ (US)| n. |奶瓶喂养; 一次奶瓶的量|The act of feeding with a bottle, or the amount of milk or formula given in a single bottle-feeding.|The baby takes three bottle-feeds a day.  这个婴儿每天喝三次奶瓶。|Synonyms: bottle-feeding|Antonyms: breastfeeding, nursing|Noun
