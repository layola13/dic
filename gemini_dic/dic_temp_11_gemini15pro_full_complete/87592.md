heterosporous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒspərəs/ | adj. | 异孢子的 | Producing two different kinds of spores, namely, microspores and megaspores. | Example:  Ferns are mostly homosporous, while seed plants are heterosporous.  蕨类植物大多是同孢子的，而种子植物是异孢子的。| Example: Heterosporous plants have an evolutionary advantage in diverse environments. 异孢子植物在多样化的环境中具有进化优势。 | Synonym:anisosporous | Antonym:homosporous | adjective
