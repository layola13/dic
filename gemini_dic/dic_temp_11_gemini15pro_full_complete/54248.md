disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/ | v. | 吐出，呕出；被迫交出，放弃 | to vomit or discharge contents; to relinquish something unwillingly | The volcano disgorged molten lava. 火山喷出了熔岩。| The company was forced to disgorge its ill-gotten gains. 公司被迫交出非法所得。 | synonyms: vomit, spew, expel, relinquish, surrender | antonyms: retain, keep, swallow, absorb | verb

disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/ | n. | 吐出，呕出物 | the act of disgorging; something disgorged |  The disgorge of toxic waste polluted the river.  有毒废物的排放污染了河流。 | synonyms: vomit, discharge, output | antonyms: intake, absorption | noun (rarely used)
