branchiostegous: /ˌbræŋkiɒˈstiːɡəs/| adj.|鳃盖的，与鳃盖有关的|Having a gill cover; relating to or resembling a gill cover.  Specifically referring to fishes where the gills are covered by a bony operculum.|The branchiostegous membrane plays a crucial role in respiration for many fish species.|近义词: operculate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'exposed gills' in a descriptive sense)|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *bránkhia* (gills) + *stégō* (to cover)
