aft:/æft/
| adv. adj.|船尾, 向船尾|Near, toward, or in the stern (rear) of a ship or aircraft.|The aft cabin was more spacious. 船尾的客舱更宽敞。|近义词：stern, rear, back|反义词:fore, forward|词性:副词，形容词
Etymology: From Old English *æftan (behind, after), from Proto-Germanic *aftan.  Cognate with Dutch achter (behind).
