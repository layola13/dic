hallucination:/həˌluːsɪˈneɪʃn/ /həˌlusɪˈneɪʃn/| n.|幻觉，幻想|An experience involving the apparent perception of something not present.|She has been having hallucinations due to her illness. 她因病出现了幻觉。|Synonyms: delusion, illusion, mirage, vision|Antonyms: reality, perception|noun

hallucinate:/həˈluːsɪneɪt/ /həˈlusɪˌneɪt/| v.|产生幻觉|To experience a seemingly real perception of something not actually present, typically as a result of a mental disorder or of taking drugs.|He began to hallucinate after being lost in the desert for days.  他在沙漠里迷路了几天后开始出现幻觉。|Synonyms: fantasize, daydream,  trip|Antonyms: perceive, comprehend|verb
