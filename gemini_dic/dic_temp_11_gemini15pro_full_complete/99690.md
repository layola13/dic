interlocating:/ˌɪntərˈləʊkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˌɪntərˈloʊkeɪtɪŋ/
adj. |连锁的，互相扣住的| connected so that one part fits into another |The toy bricks are interlocking, allowing children to build complex structures.  这些积木是连锁的，可以让孩子们搭建复杂的结构。|Synonyms: interlaced, intertwined, interwoven|Antonyms: separate, detached|
interlocking:/ˌɪntərˈləʊkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˌɪntərˈloʊkeɪtɪŋ/
v. |(使)连锁，(使)互相扣住|(cause something to) become linked or locked together |The two pieces of the puzzle interlock perfectly.  这两块拼图完美地扣在一起。|Synonyms: join, connect, link|Antonyms: separate, disconnect, disengage|
