inquisition: /ˌɪnkwiˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 审讯，调查，(尤指宗教裁判所的)拷问 | A period of prolonged and intensive questioning or investigation, especially one that is seen as harsh or unfair.  Historically, it refers to a tribunal established by the Catholic Church to suppress heresy. |  The Spanish Inquisition was a dark period in history. 西班牙宗教裁判所是历史上黑暗的时期。

The government launched an inquisition into the company's finances. 政府对这家公司的财务状况展开了调查。 | synonyms: investigation, inquiry, examination, probe, scrutiny | antonyms: disregard, neglect, ignorance | noun
