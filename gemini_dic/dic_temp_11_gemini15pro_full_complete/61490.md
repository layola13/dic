embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/ (US), /ɪmˈbrɔɪdə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 刺绣，修饰 | To decorate cloth by sewing patterns on it with thread. | She embroidered a beautiful design on the tablecloth. 她在一块桌布上绣了一个美丽的图案。 | The history of the town was embroidered with legends. 这个小镇的历史被传说修饰得更加传奇。| 近义词：stitch, adorn, embellish | 反义词: simplify, strip | verb

embroiderer:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərər/ (US), /ɪmˈbrɔɪdərə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 刺绣者，绣工 | A person who embroiders. |  My grandmother was a skilled embroiderer. 我的祖母是一位技艺精湛的刺绣者. | The embroiderer carefully chose the colors for her next project.  这位绣工仔细地为她的下一个项目挑选颜色. | 近义词：sewer, needleworker | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered as someone who removes embroidery) | noun

PHRASE->
embroider on/upon something =  润色，渲染 (to add details to a story to make it more interesting): He embroidered on the tale until it was quite unrecognizable from the original. 他对这个故事添油加醋，直到它与原来的故事大相径庭。
embroider with something =  用…刺绣:  The dress was embroidered with silk thread. 这件衣服是用丝线绣的。
embroider a story/tale/account = 渲染故事/传说/描述: He embroidered his account of the journey to impress his listeners. 他添油加醋地讲述了他的旅程，以给听众留下深刻印象。
intricately embroidered =  精细地刺绣: The tapestry was intricately embroidered with gold and silver threads. 这幅挂毯用金银线精细地绣制而成。
hand-embroidered = 手工刺绣: The tablecloth was hand-embroidered with a delicate floral pattern. 这块桌布是用精致的花卉图案手工刺绣的。
