gangrened:/ˈɡæŋɡriːnd/ | adj. | 腐烂的，坏疽的 | Affected with gangrene; decaying. | The doctor had to amputate the gangrened toe. 医生不得不截掉腐烂的脚趾。|  The soldier's gangrened wound needed immediate attention.  士兵腐烂的伤口需要立即处理。 | 近义词: decayed, rotten, necrotic, mortified | 反义词: healthy, sound | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From French *gangrené*, past participle of *gangrener* "to gangrene," from Late Latin *gangraena*, from Greek *gangraina* "an eating sore," related to *grainein* "to gnaw."
