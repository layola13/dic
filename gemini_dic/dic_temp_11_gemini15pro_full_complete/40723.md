convexedly:/kənˈvɛksɪdli/ | adv. | 凸状地，凸起地 | In a convex manner; so as to be convex. | The lens curved convexedly outwards.  镜片向外凸起。| 近义词: outwardly, roundedly | 反义词: concavely, inwardly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From convex + -ed + -ly.  Convex comes from Late Latin convexus, from Latin convehere "to bring together," from com- "together" + vehere "to carry."
