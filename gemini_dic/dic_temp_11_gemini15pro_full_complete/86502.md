heirdom:/ˈɛərdəm/
n.
继承权；遗产
The state or fact of being an heir; inheritance.
Prince Charles is the heir to the British heirdom. 查尔斯王子是英国王位的继承人。
synonyms: inheritance, legacy, patrimony, heritage
antonyms: disinheritance
