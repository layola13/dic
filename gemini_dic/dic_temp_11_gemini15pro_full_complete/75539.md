fubs:/fʌbz/
noun
笨拙的错误；失误；失败
A clumsy mistake; a blunder; a failure.
He made a real fub of the presentation, forgetting his notes and stumbling over his words. 他在演讲中犯了一个大错，忘了笔记，还结结巴巴。
近义词: blunder, mistake, error, gaffe, slip-up
反义词: success, triumph, achievement
词性:n.


fubs:/fʌbz/
verb (fubs, fubbing, fubbed)
笨拙地做；搞砸
To do something clumsily; to bungle.
I completely fubbed my attempt to bake a cake - it came out burnt and flat. 我彻底搞砸了烤蛋糕的尝试——烤出来又糊又扁。
近义词: bungle, botch, mess up, screw up
反义词: succeed, achieve, accomplish
词性:v.
