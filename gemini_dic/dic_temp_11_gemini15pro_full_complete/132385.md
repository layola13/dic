词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < Old Latin *noenum, from ne "not" + oinom/unum "one."
demobilization (noun): From de- "reverse" + mobilization.  Mobilization comes from mobile, ultimately from Latin mobilis "movable," from movere "to move."
-s (suffix):  Plural marker.
