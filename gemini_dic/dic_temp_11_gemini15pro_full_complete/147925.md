overreliance:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪəns/| n. |过度依赖，过分依赖|Excessive dependence on something or someone.|The company's overreliance on a single supplier led to problems when the supplier went bankrupt.  这家公司过度依赖单一供应商，导致该供应商破产时出现问题。|Example:  Overreliance on technology can hinder the development of practical skills. 过度依赖技术会阻碍实践技能的发展。|Synonyms: overdependence, excessive dependence|Antonyms: independence, self-reliance|noun
