anteport:/ˈæntiˌpɔːrt/
noun
外港，前港
An outer harbor or port; an outport.
Ships anchored in the anteport before entering the main harbor. 船只在进入主港前停泊在外港。
近义词: outer harbor, outport
反义词: inner harbor
