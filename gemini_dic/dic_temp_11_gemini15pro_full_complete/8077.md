anthropomorphizing: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 赋予人性，人格化 | Attributing human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object. | 例句：He was anthropomorphizing the dog by talking to it as if it understood him. 他把狗人格化了，像跟人说话一样跟它说话。

例句：Anthropomorphizing animals in children's stories can help kids connect with nature.  在儿童故事中将动物人格化可以帮助孩子们与自然建立联系。

近义词：personifying, humanizing | 反义词: objectifying | 词性: 动词 (gerund or present participle)
