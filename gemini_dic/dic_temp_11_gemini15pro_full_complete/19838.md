binational:/baɪˈnæʃənəl/
adjective
双国的；两国的；涉及两国的
Relating to or involving two nations.
The binational commission worked on resolving the border dispute.  两国委员会致力于解决边境争端。

The binational agreement fostered trade between the two countries.  双边协议促进了两国之间的贸易。

近义词:international, bilateral
反义词:unilateral, domestic
词性:adjective
