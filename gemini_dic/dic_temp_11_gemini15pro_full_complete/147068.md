overlengthen:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛŋθən/
verb
过度延长；拉得太长
to make something too long or longer than necessary
The dressmaker overlengthened the sleeves, making the dress look awkward.  裁缝把袖子做得太长了，让裙子看起来很别扭。

The meeting was overlengthened by unnecessary discussions.  会议因为不必要的讨论而拖得太长了。

近义词: prolong, extend, stretch
反义词: shorten, curtail, truncate
词性: verb

词源: over- + lengthen
"over-" prefix meaning "excessively"
"lengthen" verb meaning "to make or become longer"
