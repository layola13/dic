dotted:/ˈdɒtɪd/ (UK), /ˈdɑːtɪd/ (US)| adj.| 点缀的，有斑点的 | Covered with dots or spots; marked with dots. | The artist used a dotted line to represent the horizon. 艺术家用一条点线来表示地平线。| synonyms: speckled, spotted, flecked, stippled | antonyms: plain, solid, clear | adjective

Etymology:  From the verb "dot" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives from verbs). "Dot" comes from Middle English "dotte," of uncertain origin, possibly related to Dutch "dot" (tuft, clump).
