goes-around:/ɡoʊz əˈraʊnd/ | n. | 传播，流传；四处走动；(尤指坏事)发生 | Something that goes around is passed from person to person or spreads through a group of people.  It can also mean to move from place to place or refer to events happening, especially negative ones. | Gossip goes around quickly in small towns. 小镇里流言蜚语传播得很快。 | synonyms: circulate, spread, travel, get around | antonyms: stay put, remain, localize | noun

goes-around:/ɡoʊz əˈraʊnd/ | v. | 四处走动，四处活动；(尤指坏事)发生，流行 |  To move from place to place or to be prevalent, especially negative things.  This is the verb form of the noun "goes-around". | A nasty flu is going around.  一种恶性的流感正在流行。 | synonyms: circulate, spread, travel, get around | antonyms: stay put, remain, localize | verb
