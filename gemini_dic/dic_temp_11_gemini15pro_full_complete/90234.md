house-hunting:/ˈhaʊsˌhʌntɪŋ/ | n. | 找房子；寻觅住房 | The act of searching for a house to buy or rent. |  We've been house-hunting for months, but haven't found anything we like yet. 我们已经找了好几个月的房子了，但还没有找到喜欢的。

| Synonyms: house-searching, property-hunting, looking for a house | Antonyms: settling down, staying put | noun
