overindustrialize:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪz/| v. | 过度工业化 | To develop too much industry in (a country, region, etc.), exceeding the point where it is useful or beneficial. |  Many economists believe that the region has been overindustrialized, leading to environmental damage and economic instability. 许多经济学家认为该地区过度工业化，导致环境破坏和经济不稳定。

The rapid overindustrialization of the city resulted in severe air pollution. 城市的快速过度工业化导致严重的空气污染。| 近义词: industrialize excessively | 反义词: deindustrialize | 词性: 动词
