overobjectifying: /ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过度物化 |  Treating someone as an object, focusing excessively on their physical attributes and ignoring their personality, feelings, or intellect.  This often involves reducing a person to their sexual appeal. |  The media is often criticized for overobjectifying women.  媒体经常因过度物化女性而受到批评。 |  The advertising campaign was accused of overobjectifying men and women.  该广告宣传活动被指责过度物化男性和女性。 |  objectifying, sexualizing, dehumanizing |  subjectifying, humanizing | Verb
