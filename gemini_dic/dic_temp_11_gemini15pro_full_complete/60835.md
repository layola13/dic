electrostatically:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪkli/
| adv. | 静电地 |  Relating to static electricity; by means of static electricity.  | The particles are electrostatically charged. 这些粒子带静电。 | 近义词: statically | 反义词: dynamically | 词性: 副词
electrostatic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk/
| adj. | 静电的 | Concerning static electricity; produced by static electricity. |  Electrostatic forces hold the molecules together. 静电力使分子结合在一起。 | 近义词: static | 反义词: dynamic | 词性: 形容词

词源: electro- (electric) + -static (stationary)  "electro-" comes from Greek "elektron" meaning "amber," which was known for its static properties. "-static" comes from Greek "statikos" meaning "causing to stand."
