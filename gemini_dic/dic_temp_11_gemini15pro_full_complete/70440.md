figural:/ˈfɪɡjərəl/
| adj. | 形象的；比喻的；图形的 | Relating to, represented by, or consisting of a figure or figures, especially in art. | The tapestry had a complex figural design. 这块挂毯有一个复杂的图形设计。 |  近义词: figurative, representational, symbolic | 反义词: abstract, nonrepresentational | 词性: 形容词

figural:/ˈfɪɡjərəl/
| adj. |  与数字有关的；数字的 | Relating to or expressed in figures or numbers. (This meaning is less common) |  The report presented figural data on population growth.  这份报告提供了关于人口增长的数字数据。 | 近义词: numerical, quantitative | 反义词: qualitative | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *figuralis, from Latin figura ("figure, shape, form").
