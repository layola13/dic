ladderless:/ˈlædərlɪs/
adjective
无梯的; 不用梯子的
Without a ladder; not requiring a ladder.
The workers performed a ladderless window cleaning using specialized equipment.  工人们使用专门的设备进行了无梯清洗窗户。
The cat made a ladderless ascent of the tree.  猫不用梯子就爬上了树。
near synonyms:  without a ladder,  ladder-free
antonyms:  requiring a ladder,  with a ladder
word origin: ladder + -less
