lovehate:/ˈlʌvheɪt/| n. adj.|爱恨交加，又爱又恨|A state of having mixed feelings of love and hate towards something or someone.|例句：She had a lovehate relationship with her hometown. 她对家乡有着一种爱恨交加的感情。|例句：I have a lovehate relationship with running – it's exhausting but I feel great afterwards. 我对跑步又爱又恨——它很累人，但之后我感觉很好。|近义词：ambivalence, conflicting emotions|反义词: indifference, apathy|词性:名词，形容词
