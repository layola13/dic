grouser:/ˈɡraʊzər/| n.|履带板，防滑条，抱怨者|A heavy cleated chain or metal plate which is driven by the sprockets of a continuous track and grips the ground to provide traction.|The bulldozer's grousers dug deep into the mud. 推土机的履带板深深地陷进了泥里。|近义词：cleat, tread, grip, grumbler|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

grouser:/ˈɡraʊzər/| n.|抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人|A person who complains frequently.|He's a grouser, always finding something to moan about. 他是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner|反义词: optimist|词性:noun
