chrysophane: /krɪˈsɒfeɪn/ | n. | 金黄色素，柯桠素 | A yellow crystalline substance found in various plants, especially rhubarb, and used as a fungicide and a purgative.  It is also known as chrysophanic acid. | Chrysophane is present in rhubarb root and contributes to its laxative properties.  柯桠素存在于大黄根中，并导致其具有通便的特性。|  Chrysophane is a naturally occurring anthraquinone. 柯桠素是一种天然存在的蒽醌。| 近义词: chrysophanic acid | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | 词性: noun
