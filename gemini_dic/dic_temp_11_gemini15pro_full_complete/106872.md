laburnic: /ləˈbɜːrnɪk/ | adj. | 金链花的；金链花似的 | Of or relating to the laburnum tree or its flowers; resembling the bright yellow of laburnum flowers. | The laburnic blossoms hung heavy on the branches. 金链花的花朵沉甸甸地挂在树枝上。 |  The dress was a vibrant laburnic yellow. 这件连衣裙是鲜艳的金链花黄色。 | 近义词: golden, yellow | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *laburnum, from Latin *lapurnum, alteration of *alburnum "sapwood," from albus "white."  The connection likely arose from the pale color of the laburnum's wood.
