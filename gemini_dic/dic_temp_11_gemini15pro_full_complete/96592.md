inducting:/ɪnˈdʌktɪŋ/
verb
正式就职；使正式就任；接纳…为会员；使了解；使入门
admitting (someone) formally to a position or organization. Introducing someone to a new skill, subject, or activity.
The new president was inducted into office. 新总统正式就职。
The club inducted three new members last night. 俱乐部昨晚接纳了三名新会员。
He was inducted into the mysteries of computer programming. 他被引入了计算机编程的奥秘。
synonyms:  inaugurating, installing, initiating, onboarding, familiarizing
antonyms:  discharging, dismissing, removing, expelling
