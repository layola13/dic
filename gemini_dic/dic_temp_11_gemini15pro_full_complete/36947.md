colourful:/ˈkʌləfəl/ (US) /ˈkʌləfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|五彩缤纷的；艳丽的|Having many different and bright colours.|例句：The birds have colourful feathers. 这些鸟有着五彩缤纷的羽毛。|例句：She wore a colourful dress to the party. 她穿了一件色彩艳丽的裙子去参加派对。|近义词：bright, vibrant, vivid, rich, multicolored|反义词：dull, drab, monochrome, colourless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From 'color' (or 'colour') + '-ful'.  'Color' comes from Old French 'couleur', ultimately from Latin 'color'. '-ful' is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by".
