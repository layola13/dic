homestay:/ˈhoʊmsteɪ/ (US), /ˈhɒmsteɪ/ (UK)| n.|寄宿家庭; 在寄宿家庭的住宿|A period of time during which a visitor, especially a foreign student, lives with a family in their home. |例句：I arranged a homestay with a local family. 我安排了在当地一个家庭寄宿。

例句：My homestay experience was incredibly enriching. 我的寄宿家庭经历非常丰富。

近义词：lodging, accommodation, billet|反义词: hotel|词性: noun
