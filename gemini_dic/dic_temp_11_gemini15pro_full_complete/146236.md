overexpress:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛs/| v. |过度表达；过量表达|To express (a gene or its product) at a higher level than normal.|Researchers found that the gene was overexpressed in cancer cells. 研究人员发现该基因在癌细胞中过度表达。|synonyms: upregulate, overproduce|antonyms: underexpress, downregulate|verb

overexpression:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspreʃən/| n. |过度表达；过量表达|The excessive expression of a gene or its product.|Overexpression of this protein can lead to disease. 这种蛋白质的过度表达会导致疾病。|synonyms: upregulation, overproduction|antonyms: underexpression, downregulation|noun
