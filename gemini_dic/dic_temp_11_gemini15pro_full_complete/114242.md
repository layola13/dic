mailer:/ˈmeɪlər/| n.|邮寄者，邮递员，邮轮，邮件程序|A person or thing that sends something by mail.  A program or system used to send emails, especially bulk emails.|The mailer delivered the package on time. 邮递员准时送来了包裹。|Synonyms: postal worker, mail carrier, sender, email program|Antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun
