audiobooks:/ˈɔːdiəʊbʊks/ (UK) /ˈɑːdioʊbʊks/ (US)| n.|有声读物|A recording of a text read aloud, typically a book.  It can be listened to on a computer, smartphone, or other audio device.|I enjoy listening to audiobooks while I commute. 我喜欢在通勤时听有声读物。|Synonyms: talking books, audio recordings, narrated books|Antonyms: printed books, ebooks, hardcovers|noun
