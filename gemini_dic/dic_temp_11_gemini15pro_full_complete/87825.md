hicks:/hɪks/| n.| 乡巴佬；土包子；粗俗的人|A person who lives in the country, regarded as unsophisticated or unintelligent. |例句：Those hicks from the backwoods don't know how to behave in a fancy restaurant. 那些来自穷乡僻壤的乡巴佬不知道在高级餐厅里该如何举止。|近义词：yokel, rustic, hillbilly|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan| Noun

hicks:/hɪks/| n.| 呃逆；打嗝|A spasm of the diaphragm causing a characteristic gulping sound.|例句：He had a bad case of the hicks after drinking too much soda. 他喝了太多汽水后不停地打嗝。|近义词：hiccup|反义词:| Noun
