circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌfleks/ | n. adj. | 抑扬符;  弯曲的 | A diacritical mark (^) placed over a vowel. Curved or bending around. | The circumflex is used in some languages. 抑扬符用在某些语言中。  A circumflex path through the woods.  一条蜿蜒穿过树林的小路。 | 近义词: arc, bend, curve (for adj.); caret (for n.) | 反义词: straight (for adj.) | 词性: 名词, 形容词
