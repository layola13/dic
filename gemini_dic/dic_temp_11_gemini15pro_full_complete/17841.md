beebread:/ˈbiːbrɛd/| n. |蜂粮|A mixture of pollen and honey or nectar collected by worker honeybees and stored in honeycomb cells as food for larvae and other bees.|The beekeeper examined the frames, noting the abundance of beebread stored for the winter. 养蜂人检查了蜂框，注意到储存了丰富的蜂粮过冬。|近义词：perga |反义词: None |Noun
