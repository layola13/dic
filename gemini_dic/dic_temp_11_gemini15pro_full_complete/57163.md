dowdy:/ˈdaʊdi/| adj.|过时老土的，衣着邋遢的|Unfashionable and not attractive; lacking smartness and freshness in style or appearance.|例句：She looked dowdy in that old dress. 她穿着那条旧裙子显得老土。|近义词：frumpy, shabby, unstylish, drab|反义词：stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant|词性:形容词
dowdy:/ˈdaʊdi/| n.|衣着邋遢的女人|A dowdily dressed woman.|例句：She's always been a bit of a dowdy. 她一直有点邋遢。|近义词：frump|反义词：fashionista|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dialectal *dowd (“slut, slattern”), of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to dad.
