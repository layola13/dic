genuses:/ˈdʒɛnəsɪz/
n.
属，种类
The plural form of "genus,"  referring to a taxonomic rank used in the biological classification of living and fossil organisms, ranking above species and below family.
Biologists have identified several distinct genuses of primates. 生物学家已经确定了几个不同的灵长类属。
The two plants belong to different genuses. 这两种植物属于不同的属。
synonyms: categories, classes, kinds, types
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a taxonomic rank)
