cosponsoring:/koʊˈspɑːnsərɪŋ/ /kəʊˈspɒnsərɪŋ/
|v.|共同赞助，联合主办|The two companies are cosponsoring a charity event.|两家公司共同赞助了一场慈善活动。|例句：The senator is cosponsoring a bill to protect the environment.  这位参议员共同发起了一项保护环境的法案。

例句：Several organizations are cosponsoring the conference.  几个组织共同主办这次会议。
|近义词：co-hosting, jointly sponsoring|反义词：solely sponsoring|词性：动词(present participle)

cosponsor:/koʊˈspɑːnsər/ /kəʊˈspɒnsə(r)/
|n.|共同赞助者，联合主办者|One of the cosponsors of the event pulled out at the last minute.|活动的一位共同赞助者在最后一刻退出了。|例句：The conference had several cosponsors from different industries.  这次会议有来自不同行业的几位共同赞助者。

例句：She was a cosponsor of the new legislation. 她是一位新立法的共同提案人。
|近义词：co-host, joint sponsor|反义词：sole sponsor|词性：名词

词源：co- (together) + sponsor
