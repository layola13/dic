nonabsolutistically:/nɒnˌæbsəˈluːtɪstɪkli/
adv. |非绝对地| In a way that is not absolute; in a manner that acknowledges nuances, exceptions, or degrees of certainty. |  She approached the issue nonabsolutistically, considering various perspectives and avoiding dogmatic pronouncements. | 近义词: relatively, contextually, conditionally | 反义词: absolutely, categorically, dogmatically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + absolute + -ist (suffix forming nouns and adjectives) + -ic (suffix forming adjectives) + -al (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs)
