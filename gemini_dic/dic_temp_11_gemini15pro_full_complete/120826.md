misauthorize:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəraɪz/
verb
未授权；错误授权
to give someone the wrong authority or permission to do something
The bank misauthorized a payment of $10,000.  银行错误授权了一笔1万美元的付款。
The clerk misauthorized the return of the defective merchandise. 店员错误地授权退回了有缺陷的商品。
近义词:  unauthorize, wrongly authorize
反义词: authorize, empower
词性: verb
