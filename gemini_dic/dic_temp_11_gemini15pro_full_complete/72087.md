floatiest:/ˈfloʊtiɪst/| adj.|最轻飘飘的，最漂浮的|Superlative form of floaty: most floaty.  Characterized by a light and airy quality, as if floating or drifting.|Her floatiest dress billowed in the summer breeze. 她最轻飘飘的裙子在夏天的微风中飘动。|Synonyms: airiest, dreamiest, ethereal, gossamer, diaphanous|Antonyms: heavy, weighty, substantial|adjective
