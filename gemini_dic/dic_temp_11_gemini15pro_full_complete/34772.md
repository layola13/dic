climacteric:/klaɪˈmæk.tər.ɪk/ /klaɪˈmæk.trɪk/
adjective|更年期的；关键的，转折点的|Relating to the menopause or marking a period of decrease of reproductive capacity; (of a plant) undergoing a process of ripening associated with increased production of ethylene; (of a change or event) causing a very important change and marking a stage of development|例句：She experienced the typical climacteric symptoms. 她经历了典型的更年期症状。|例句：The company is at a climacteric point in its development.  公司正处于发展的关键时期。|近义词：critical, crucial, decisive, pivotal, turning point|反义词:unimportant, trivial|词性:adjective
climacteric:/klaɪˈmæk.tər.ɪk/ /klaɪˈmæk.trɪk/
noun|更年期；转折点|The period in a woman's life in which menstruation ceases; A critical period or stage|例句：She's going through the climacteric. 她正在经历更年期。|例句：The battle marked the climacteric of the war. 这场战役标志着战争的转折点。|近义词：menopause, turning point, crisis|反义词: none|词性:noun
