desirous:/dɪˈzaɪərəs/
adjective
渴望的； desirous of sth
Having or characterized by desire.
She was desirous of a long vacation. 她渴望一个长假。
Synonyms: eager, keen, avid, longing, yearning
Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic, unenthusiastic
词性: 形容词
