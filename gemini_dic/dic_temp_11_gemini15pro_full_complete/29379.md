casuals:/ˈkæʒuəlz/| n.|便装；休闲服；便鞋；临时工|Casual clothes are clothes that are comfortable and not formal.  Casuals can also refer to workers who are employed on a temporary or irregular basis.|例句：He wore casuals to the party. 他穿着便服去参加了聚会。

例句：The company hired some casuals to help with the extra workload. 公司雇佣了一些临时工来帮忙处理额外的工作。|近义词：informal clothes, leisurewear, temporary workers|反义词：formal wear, permanent staff|词性:Noun
