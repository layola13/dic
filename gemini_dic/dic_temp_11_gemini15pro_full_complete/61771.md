empathizing:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
表示同情，感同身受
The act of understanding and sharing the feelings of another.
She was empathizing with the character's struggles in the novel. 她对小说中人物的挣扎感同身受。
近义词:  sympathizing, relating, understanding, commiserating, sharing
反义词:  apathy, indifference, detachment
