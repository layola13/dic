nonperceptible:/ˌnɑːnpərˈsɛptɪbəl/ /ˌnɒnpəˈsɛptɪbl/| adj.|不易察觉的；感觉不到的|Not perceptible; too slight or subtle to be noticed by the senses.|The changes were so gradual as to be nonperceptible.|Synonyms: imperceptible, unnoticeable, indiscernible, undetectable|Antonyms: perceptible, noticeable, discernible, detectable|adjective
nonperceptible:|/ˌnɑːnpərˈsɛptɪbəl/ /ˌnɒnpəˈsɛptɪbl/|adj.|不易察觉的；感觉不到的|Not perceptible; too slight or subtle to be noticed by the senses.  From non- + perceptible, from Latin percept-, past participle stem of percipere "to perceive," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to seize, take."|The changes were so gradual as to be nonperceptible. 这些变化非常缓慢，不易察觉。|Synonyms: imperceptible, unnoticeable, indiscernible, undetectable|Antonyms: perceptible, noticeable, discernible, detectable|adjective
