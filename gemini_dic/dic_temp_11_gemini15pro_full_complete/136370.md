nonresponses:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːnsɪz/ | n. |未答复，无反应|The survey had a high rate of nonresponses, making the results less reliable.  这项调查的无回复率很高，使得结果不太可靠。|  The researcher was concerned about the large number of nonresponses from the target demographic. 研究人员对目标人群中大量的未回复感到担忧。|Synonyms:  lack of response, silence, no reply |Antonyms: responses, answers, replies|noun

nonresponse:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːns/ | n. |未答复，无反应|  The nonresponse to the email campaign was disappointing.  对电子邮件活动的无响应令人失望。| We sent out 200 questionnaires, but the nonresponse rate was high. 我们发出了 200 份问卷，但未回复率很高。|Synonyms:  lack of response, silence, failure to respond |Antonyms: response, answer, reply|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + response.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," and response means "an answer or reaction."
