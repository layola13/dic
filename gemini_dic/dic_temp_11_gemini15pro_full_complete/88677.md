hoaxes:/ˈhoʊksiz/| n.|骗局，恶作剧|A deliberate act intended to deceive or trick. |The Piltdown Man was one of the most famous hoaxes in scientific history. 皮尔当人事件是科学史上最著名的骗局之一。|synonyms:  frauds, scams, tricks, deceptions, ruses|antonyms: truths, realities|noun

hoax:/hoʊks/| n. vt.|骗局，恶作剧；欺骗|A deliberate act intended to deceive or trick. |He hoaxed the public with a fake photograph. 他用一张假照片欺骗了公众。|synonyms:  deceive, trick, dupe, defraud, swindle|antonyms:  be honest, tell the truth|noun, verb
