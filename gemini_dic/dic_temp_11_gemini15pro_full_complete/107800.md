lapsed:/læpst/
adjective
失效的，过期的；不再有效的；废弃的
no longer effective or valid because the time limit has passed.
My membership has lapsed because I haven't paid my fees. 我的会员资格已经失效，因为我没有缴纳费用。
The license has lapsed, so he can't legally drive. 他的驾照过期了，所以他不能合法驾驶。
synonyms: expired, invalid, void, terminated, defunct
antonyms: valid, current, active, renewed
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lapsus*, past participle of *labi* "to slip, glide, fall, sink, decline."
