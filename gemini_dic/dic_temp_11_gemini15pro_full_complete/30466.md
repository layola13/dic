centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)| n. v.|离心机，离心分离机；用离心机分离|A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate substances of different densities.|The biologist used a centrifuge to separate the blood components. 生物学家使用离心机分离血液成分。|近义词：separator, extractor|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)| v.|用离心机分离|To spin something in a centrifuge to separate its components.|They centrifuged the sample to isolate the DNA. 他们用离心机分离样品以提取DNA。|近义词：spin, separate, whirl|反义词: N/A|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
