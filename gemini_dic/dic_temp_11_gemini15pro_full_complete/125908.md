muse:/mjuːz/ | n. vi.| 缪斯；沉思；冥想 |A source of inspiration, especially in artistic or creative endeavors.  A person or personified force who is the source of inspiration for a creative artist. To become absorbed in thought; to ponder. | She found her muse in the beauty of nature. 她在大自然的美景中找到了灵感。 He mused on the meaning of life. 他沉思着生命的意义。| 近义词：contemplate, ponder, meditate, ruminate, inspiration | 反义词: distraction | 词性: 名词, 动词

PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？
what for = 为了什么
What did you do that for? 你为什么那样做？
no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. 无论发生什么事，我都会在你身边。
what's more = 而且；更重要的是
He's a good singer, and what's more, he can dance. 他唱歌很好，而且，他还会跳舞。
what about = ...怎么样
What about going to the cinema tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？
