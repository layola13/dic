nausea:/ˈnɔːziə/ (US) /ˈnɔːsiə/ (UK)| n. |恶心，作呕|A feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit.|例句：The smell of the hospital made her feel nausea. 医院的气味让她感到恶心。|例句：He experienced nausea after the roller coaster ride.  坐过山车后他感到恶心。|近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting (the act of), retching|反义词: wellness, comfort|词性: noun


nauseam:/ˈnɔːziəm/ (US) /ˈnɔːsiəm/ (UK)| adv. |(与ad用在一起构成固定搭配 ad nauseam)令人作呕地，冗长乏味地|Used to describe something that has been done or repeated so often that it has become annoying or tiresome.|例句：He talks about football ad nauseam. 他喋喋不休地谈论足球，令人厌烦。|例句：The same commercials are repeated ad nauseam.  同样的广告令人厌烦地重复播放。|近义词：excessively, tediously, repeatedly|反义词: moderately, briefly|词性: adverb (only used in the phrase "ad nauseam")
