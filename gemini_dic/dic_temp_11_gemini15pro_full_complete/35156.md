clovers:/ˈkloʊvərz/| n. |三叶草；苜蓿|A small wild plant with usually three leaves on each stem, often considered to be lucky.  Clover is a common fodder crop.|例句：The field was covered in clovers.  田野里长满了三叶草。

例句：He found a four-leaf clover. 他找到了一株四叶草。|近义词：trefoil, shamrock|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
