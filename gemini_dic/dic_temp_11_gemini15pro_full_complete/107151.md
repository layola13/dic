laetere: /laeˈteːreɪ/ /ləˈtiːriː/
verb
rejoice, be glad
From Latin laetārī ("to rejoice").  It is the second-person singular present active imperative form of laetor.  It commands someone to rejoice.
Laetere, amice! Rejoice, friend!
近义词: rejoice, exult, celebrate
反义词: mourn, grieve
词性: verb
