overman:/ˈoʊvərˌmæn/| n.|超人， Übermensch (德语)|A superior man, one who transcends the limitations of ordinary human morality and creates new values.  This concept is central to the philosophy of Friedrich Nietzsche.|例句：Nietzsche's concept of the overman has been interpreted in many ways. 尼采的超人概念已被以多种方式解读。

例句：He strove to become an overman, free from the constraints of conventional morality. 他努力成为一个超人，摆脱传统道德的束缚。|近义词：superman, superior being|反义词:underman, common man|词性:noun
