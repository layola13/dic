macerator:/ˈmæsəreɪtər/| n.|浸渍器，研磨机|A machine that reduces solids to a pulp or slurry by soaking or grinding.  It's often used in waste disposal and food processing.|The macerator quickly broke down the food waste.  浸渍器迅速分解了食物垃圾。|Synonyms: grinder, pulverizer, mill|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "assembler" in a metaphorical sense)|noun

macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/| v.|浸渍，软化|To soften or break down something by soaking it in liquid.  Can also mean to cause something to become thin and weak.|The chef macerated the fruit in wine before adding it to the dessert.  厨师在将水果加入甜点之前先将其浸泡在葡萄酒中。|Synonyms: soak, steep, soften|Antonyms: dry, harden, dehydrate|verb
