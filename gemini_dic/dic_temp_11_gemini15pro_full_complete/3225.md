affectively:/əˈfɛktɪvli/
adverb
情感地；表达感情地
In a way that relates to moods, feelings, and attitudes.  Relating to, arising from, or influencing feelings or emotions.
She spoke affectively about her childhood. 她深情地谈起了她的童年。
Synonyms: emotionally, feelingly, passionately, sentimentally, tenderly
Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, unemotionally
word type: adverb

affectively:/əˈfɛktɪvli/
adverb
有效地
producing a desired result or effect
The medicine worked affectively to reduce the fever.  这种药有效地降低了发烧。
Synonyms: effectively, efficiently, successfully, potently
Antonyms: ineffectively, inefficiently, unsuccessfully
word type: adverb
