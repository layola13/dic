hashimoto:/ˌhɑːʃiˈmoʊtoʊ/ |n.| 桥本氏病；桥本甲状腺炎 | Hashimoto's thyroiditis, also known as chronic lymphocytic thyroiditis, is an autoimmune disease in which the thyroid gland is gradually destroyed by the body's own immune system. | Hashimoto's disease is the most common cause of hypothyroidism in the United States. 桥本氏病是美国甲状腺功能减退症最常见的原因。|  My aunt was diagnosed with Hashimoto's a few years ago.  我阿姨几年前被诊断出患有桥本氏病。 | synonyms: Hashimoto's thyroiditis, chronic lymphocytic thyroiditis | antonyms:  N/A (It's a disease; it doesn't have a direct opposite.) | proper noun
