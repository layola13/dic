leonine:/ˈliːənaɪn/| adj.|狮子的，像狮子的；威严的，庄严的|Resembling or characteristic of a lion; majestic and imposing.|例句：He had a leonine mane of hair. 他有着一头像狮子鬃毛一样的头发。|例句：She possessed a leonine dignity. 她拥有一种像狮子般的尊严。|近义词：lionlike, majestic, regal, stately, noble|反义词：meek, humble, timid|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *leonīnus*, from *leō* (“lion”).
