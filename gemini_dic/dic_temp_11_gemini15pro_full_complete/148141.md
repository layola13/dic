oversensibly:/ˌōvərˈsensəbli/ | adv. | 过于明智地，过分理性地 | Excessively or overly sensible; in a way that relies too much on reason and practicality, often at the expense of emotion or intuition.  | He acted oversensibly in the situation, neglecting the emotional needs of his friend. 他在这种情况下表现得过于理性，忽略了他朋友的情感需求。|  She oversensibly dismissed the idea of a romantic getaway, focusing solely on the cost. 她过于理性地否定了浪漫之旅的想法，只关注成本。 | 近义词: too practically, excessively rationally | 反义词: foolishly, impulsively | 词性: 副词
