nan:/næn/ |  adjective, noun |  非数字（Not a Number） |  In computing, NaN (Not a Number) is a value of a numeric data type that represents an undefined or unrepresentable value, such as the result of dividing by zero.  It can also be used as a placeholder for missing or invalid data. | Example: The result of 0/0 is NaN. 0/0的结果是NaN。| Synonym: undefined, invalid | Antonym: valid, defined | abbreviation

nan:/næn/ | noun |奶奶（非正式）|  Informal term for grandmother, primarily used in British English.  | Example: My nan makes the best cookies. 我的奶奶做的饼干最好吃。| Synonym: grandma, granny, grandmother | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | informal
