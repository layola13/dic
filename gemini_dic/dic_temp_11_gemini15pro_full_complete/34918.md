clivity: /ˈklɪvɪti/| n. |斜坡，倾斜|An upward slope.  A slope, especially a steep one.|例句：The car struggled up the steep clivity. 汽车艰难地爬上陡峭的斜坡。

例句：The goats grazed on the clivity. 山羊在斜坡上吃草。|近义词：slope, incline, gradient, acclivity|反义词: declivity, descent|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *clīvitās, from clīvus (“slope”).
