coffre:/kɔfr/ (French), /ˈkɒfər/ (English)| n.|箱子，保险箱，(汽车后部的)行李箱|A strongbox or chest, especially one for valuables; In a car, the trunk or boot.|例句：She kept her jewels in a locked coffre. 她把珠宝锁在一个箱子里。

例句：The coffre of the car was full of luggage. 汽车的后备箱装满了行李。|近义词：chest, trunk, box, safe|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French coffre, from Latin cophinus (“basket”), from Ancient Greek κόφινος (kóphinos, “basket”).
