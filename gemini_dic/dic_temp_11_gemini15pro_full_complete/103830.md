joyfullest:/ˈdʒɔɪfələst/| adj.|最快乐的|The superlative form of "joyful," meaning experiencing or expressing great happiness.  It suggests the highest degree of joy. |She had the joyfullest smile I'd ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最快乐的笑容。|The joyfullest day of my life was my wedding day.  我一生中最快乐的日子是我的婚礼。|近义词：happiest, most cheerful, most joyous|反义词：saddest, most miserable, most unhappy|词性:形容词
词源: From "joy" (from Old French "joie," ultimately from Latin "gaudia") + "-ful" (suffix indicating "full of") + "-est" (superlative suffix).
