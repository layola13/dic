crustated:/ˈkrʌsteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有壳的；甲壳类的 | Covered with a crust or shell, typically referring to animals. | Example: The lobster is a crustated creature. 龙虾是一种甲壳类动物。
| Example: We found a variety of crustated animals on the beach. 我们在海滩上发现了各种各样的甲壳类动物。 | Near synonyms: shelled, exoskeletal | Antonyms: soft-bodied, shell-less | adjective


crustacean:/krʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 甲壳类动物 | Any of a large group of arthropods, such as crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles, having a hard, segmented shell. | Example:  Crabs are a type of crustacean. 螃蟹是一种甲壳类动物。
| Example:  Many crustaceans are scavengers. 许多甲壳类动物是食腐动物。 | Near synonyms: shellfish | Antonyms: (no direct antonym, could use a specific non-crustacean animal like mammal or insect) | noun


词源:
From Latin *crustatus*, past participle of *crustare* "to encrust," from *crusta* "crust, rind, shell."
