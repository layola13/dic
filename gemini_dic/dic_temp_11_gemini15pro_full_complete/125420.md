multiperils: /ˌmʌltɪˈpɛrəlz/ | n. | 多重风险，综合险 |  Insurance coverage against loss from two or more perils, such as fire, windstorm, hail, explosion, riot, aircraft damage, vehicle damage, smoke, etc. |  Example: He purchased multiperils insurance for his home to protect against a variety of potential disasters.  他为自己的房屋购买了综合险，以防范各种潜在的灾害。| Example: Multiperils insurance policies are often more cost-effective than purchasing individual policies for each peril.  综合险保单通常比为每种风险单独购买保单更具成本效益。 | Synonyms: all-risk insurance, comprehensive insurance | Antonyms: single-peril insurance, specific-peril insurance | noun
