blusterer:/ˈblʌstərər/ (US) /ˈblʌstərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |  咆哮者；吹牛大王；虚张声势的人 | Someone who talks loudly and aggressively, but rarely acts on their threats. |  The blusterer threatened to sue, but everyone knew he was all talk.  那个咆哮者威胁要起诉，但大家都知道他只是说说而已。 | synonyms: braggart, loudmouth, bully, swaggerer | antonyms: meek person, quiet person | noun

bluster:/ˈblʌstər/ (US) /ˈblʌstə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 虚张声势；咆哮；狂妄自大地说话 | To talk loudly and aggressively, typically with little effect. | He blustered about how important he was, but nobody believed him. 他吹嘘自己多么重要，但没有人相信他。 | synonyms: rant, rave, swagger | antonyms: whisper, murmur | verb
