gaggled:/ˈɡæɡ(ə)ld/
verb
成群结队地走动; (鹅)嘎嘎叫
moved or sounded like a gaggle of geese
A gaggle of tourists gaggled through the museum. 一群游客叽叽喳喳地穿过博物馆。

近义词: flocked, swarmed, thronged
反义词: dispersed, scattered
词性: verb (used without object), past participle


gaggle:/ˈɡæɡ(ə)l/
noun
(鹅的)一群; 嘈杂的人群
a flock of geese; a disorderly or noisy group of people
A gaggle of reporters surrounded the actress. 一群记者围住了这位女演员。

近义词: group, crowd, flock, herd
反义词: individual, loner
词性: noun


词源:
From Middle English *gagelen, frequentative of gaggen (“to cackle”), of imitative origin.
