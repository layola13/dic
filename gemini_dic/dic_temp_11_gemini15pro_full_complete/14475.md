avanguard:/ˈævəŋɡɑːrd (US), ˈævənɡɑːd (UK)| n. adj.| 先锋，前锋，先驱；先锋部队，先头部队|  a position at the front of an army or fleet or movement; leading position in a trend or movement.  |The artist's work was considered avant-garde at the time.  这位艺术家的作品在当时被认为是先锋派。|  The company is at the avant-garde of technological innovation.  这家公司处于技术创新的最前沿。 |近义词：forefront, leading edge, pioneers, innovators|反义词：rearguard, laggards, conservatives, traditionalists|词性: 名词，形容词

Etymology:
From French *avant-garde*, from *avant* ("before") + *garde* ("guard"). Ultimately from Frankish *wara* (“protection”)
