metanephros:/ˈmɛtənɛfrɒs/ /ˈmɛtənɛfrɑːs/
noun
后肾
The definitive kidney of amniotes, developing from the posterior portion of the nephrogenic cord.  It replaces the earlier mesonephros.
The metanephros gives rise to the adult kidney. 后肾会发育成为成体的肾脏。
Metanephric development is essential for urinary system formation. 后肾发育对泌尿系统的形成至关重要。
