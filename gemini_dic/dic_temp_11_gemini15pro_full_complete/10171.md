apophyllite:/əˈpɒfɪlaɪt/| n. |鱼眼石|A hydrous potassium calcium silicate mineral, typically occurring as transparent or translucent crystals.|The apophyllite crystals shimmered in the dim light of the cave. 鱼眼石晶体在昏暗的洞穴灯光下闪烁。|The museum's collection boasts a stunning specimen of green apophyllite.  博物馆的藏品中有一块令人惊叹的绿色鱼眼石标本。|近义词:zeolite(沸石，泛指一类矿物，鱼眼石有时被归类于此)|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἀπό (apó, “off, away from”) + φύλλον (phúllon, “leaf”), because it exfoliates when heated, due to the loss of water.
