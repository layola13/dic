berretta:/bəˈrɛtə/ | n. | 贝雷帽，方形帽 | A square cap, sometimes having three projecting pieces stiffened with cardboard, worn by Roman Catholic clergy.  A similar cap worn by academics. | The cardinal donned his scarlet berretta.  红衣主教戴上了他的猩红色贝雷帽。

The new graduate proudly wore his berretta. 这位新毕业生自豪地戴着他的方形帽。| 近义词: cap, biretta | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian berretta,  from Late Latin birrus “a hooded cloak,” of uncertain origin.
