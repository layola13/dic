halul:/ˈhɑːluːl/| n.|亵渎，玷污，损害| desecration, profanation; violation; damage. |The halul of the sacred site angered many. 对圣地的亵渎激怒了许多人。|近义词：desecration, profanation, violation, defilement|反义词: reverence, veneration, respect|词性:名词


词源:

From Arabic *ḥalūl* (حَلول), verbal noun of *ḥalila* (حَلَّ), meaning "to become or be lawful, permissible".  The original meaning refers to the lifting of a ritual prohibition, but in Hebrew and some other contexts, it has taken on a negative connotation, referring to the improper lifting of a prohibition or a violation of something sacred.
