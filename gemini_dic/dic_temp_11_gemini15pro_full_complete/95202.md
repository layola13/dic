inauthenticities:/ˌɪnɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪtiz/
n.|不真实，虚假，伪造|The proliferation of inauthenticities online makes it difficult to discern truth from fiction. 网上充斥着各种虚假信息，让人难以辨别真伪。|例句：The museum discovered several inauthenticities in their collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆在他们的古代文物收藏中发现了几件赝品。

例句：He pointed out the inauthenticities in her story, proving she wasn't being truthful. 他指出了她故事中的不真实之处，证明她没有说实话。
|近义词：fakes, forgeries, falsehoods, misrepresentations|反义词：authenticity, genuineness, truths, realities|词性: 名词 (plural)
