breakpoints:/ˈbreɪkpɔɪnts/| n. |断点，中断点|A point in a computer program where interruption of normal processing occurs for a special purpose, such as debugging.|The debugger allows you to set breakpoints in your code.  调试器允许你在代码中设置断点。|Synonyms: stopping point, pause point, checkpoint|Antonyms: continuation point|noun
