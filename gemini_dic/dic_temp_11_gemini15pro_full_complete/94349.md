impious:/ˈɪmpiəs/| adj.|不敬的，不虔诚的|Not showing respect or reverence, especially for a god.|His impious remarks shocked the devout worshippers. 他不敬的言论震惊了虔诚的信徒们。|近义词：irreverent, profane, blasphemous, ungodly|反义词：pious, devout, reverent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *impius*, from *in-* (not) + *pius* (pious, dutiful).
