imputable:/ɪmˈpjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 可归咎的，可归因的 | Ascribable, attributable, chargeable.  Something that can be attributed or assigned to a particular cause, source, or person. |  The accident was imputable to driver negligence. 这起事故可归咎于驾驶员的疏忽。|  The success of the project is imputable to the team's hard work.  这个项目的成功归功于团队的辛勤工作。 |  attributable, ascribable, chargeable | non-imputable, unattributable | adjective
