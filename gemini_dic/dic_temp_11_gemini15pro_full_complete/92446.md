hypophrygian:/ˌhaɪpəˈfrɪdʒən/| adj.|希波弗里吉亚调式的；忧郁的|Relating to or characteristic of the Hypophrygian mode, a musical mode in ancient Greek music.  Also used figuratively to describe something melancholic or mournful.|The piece had a distinctly hypophrygian feel, evoking a sense of sadness and longing. 这首曲子有一种明显的希波弗里吉亚调式的感觉，唤起了一种悲伤和渴望的感觉。|The melody sounded almost hypophrygian in its mournful descent. 这旋律的悲哀下降听起来几乎像是希波弗里吉亚调式的。|近义词：melancholy, mournful, plaintive|反义词：joyful, cheerful|词性:adjective
