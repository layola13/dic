drawing-boards:/ˈdrɔɪŋ bɔːrdz/
n.|绘图板，策划阶段|A drawing board is a smooth, flat surface used for drawing or designing, often made of wood or plastic.  The phrase "back to the drawing board" means to start planning something again because the first plan failed. |The project failed, so it's back to the drawing boards.  项目失败了，所以得重新开始规划。|synonyms:  drafting boards, design tables|antonyms: N/A|noun


drawing-boards:/ˈdrɔɪŋ bɔːrdz/
n.|策划阶段，重新规划|Figuratively, "drawing boards" refers to the planning or development stage of a project. "Back to the drawing boards" means to return to the planning stage.  |After the client rejected our proposal, we had to go back to the drawing boards.  客户拒绝了我们的提案后，我们不得不重新进行规划。|synonyms: planning stage, initial phase|antonyms: execution, implementation|noun
