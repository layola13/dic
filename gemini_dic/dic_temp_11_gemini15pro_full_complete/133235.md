nonexculpable:/ˌnɒnɪksˈkʌlpəbl/ | adj. | 不可原谅的，不可开脱的 | Not able to be excused or justified; inexcusable. |  The judge deemed the defendant's actions nonexculpable due to the overwhelming evidence.  法官认为被告的行为由于证据确凿而不可原谅。

The company's nonexculpable negligence led to a massive oil spill. 该公司不可推卸的疏忽导致了大规模的石油泄漏。 | 近义词: inexcusable, unjustifiable, unforgivable | 反义词: excusable, justifiable, forgivable | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- (not) + exculpable (from Latin exculpare, meaning "to clear from blame," from ex- "out of" + culpa "blame").
