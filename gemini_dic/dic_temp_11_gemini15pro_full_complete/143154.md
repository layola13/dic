outchattering: /ˌaʊtˈtʃætərɪŋ/
verb
喋喋不休地说过; 比...说得更多
talking more or longer than someone else.
例句：
He outchattered everyone at the party.
他在聚会上比任何人都健谈。

She outchattered her rival in the debate.
在辩论中，她比对手说得更多。

近义词：outtalking, outscoring
反义词：being quiet, listening

词性：v.

词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "chatter" (verb meaning "to talk rapidly or incessantly about unimportant matters")
