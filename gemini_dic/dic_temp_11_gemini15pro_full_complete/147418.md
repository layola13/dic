overnourishing:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃɪŋ/
|adj.|
|过度营养的；营养过剩的|

|The overnourishing diet led to weight gain.  过度营养的饮食导致体重增加。|
|Excessive consumption of processed foods can lead to an overnourishing lifestyle.  过度食用加工食品会导致营养过剩的生活方式。|
|overfed, overnourished|
|undernourished, malnourished|
|adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix):  from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above, beyond."
nourish (verb): from Old French norrir "to nourish, nurture, educate," from Latin nutrire "to suckle, nourish."
