downriver:/ˌdaʊnˈrɪvər/| adv. adj.|顺流而下，在下游的|Located or situated along a river in the direction of its flow; further down a river than a specified point.|例句：We traveled downriver by boat. 我们乘船顺流而下。|近义词：downstream|反义词:upriver|词性:副词，形容词

downriver:/ˌdaʊnˈrɪvər/| n.|下游地区|The area downstream from a specified point on a river.|例句：The downriver towns were flooded. 下游的城镇被洪水淹没了。|近义词：lower reaches|反义词:upper reaches|词性:名词
