allurement:/əˈlʊəmənt/ | n. |诱惑，魅力|Something that tempts or entices. |The allurement of the big city drew many young people from rural areas.  大城市的诱惑吸引了许多来自农村地区的年轻人。|Synonyms: attraction, temptation, lure, charm, enticement|Antonyms: repulsion, deterrent, discouragement|noun

allurement:/əˈlʊəmənt/ | n. |诱惑力，吸引力|The power to attract or charm.|The allurement of a high salary convinced him to take the job. 高薪的诱惑力说服他接受了这份工作。|Synonyms: appeal, fascination, magnetism |Antonyms:  repulsiveness, unattractiveness|noun
