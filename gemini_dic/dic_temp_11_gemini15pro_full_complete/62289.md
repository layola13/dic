endall:/ˈɛndɔːl/ |n.| 结束；终结；万灵丹 |A final solution or remedy for a problem or difficulty. |The new policy was hailed as the endall of poverty. 这项新政策被誉为消除贫困的最终方案。| 近义词: panacea, cure-all, solution, remedy | 反义词: problem, difficulty, issue, start, beginning | 词性: 名词

endall:/ˈɛndɔːl/ |adj.| 最终的；终极的；包罗万象的 |Something that is considered the ultimate or best of its kind.  |This isn't the endall, be-all of smartphones, but it's pretty close. 这不是智能手机的终极形态，但已经很接近了。 |近义词: ultimate, best, supreme, definitive, conclusive |反义词: mediocre, average, ordinary, inferior |词性: 形容词
