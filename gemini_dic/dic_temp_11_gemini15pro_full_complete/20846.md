blastosphere: /ˈblæstəˌsfɪr/ (US) /ˈblɑːstəsfɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. |囊胚|An early stage of embryonic development in animals, characterized by a hollow, fluid-filled sphere of cells. |The blastosphere forms after the morula and before gastrulation.| The blastosphere is a crucial stage in embryonic development. 囊胚是胚胎发育的关键阶段。

The study focused on the formation of the blastosphere in mammals. 这项研究的重点是哺乳动物囊胚的形成。


近义词: blastula, embryoblast| 反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider later developmental stages like gastrula)|词性: noun
