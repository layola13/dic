harpooned:/hɑːrˈpuːnd/
verb
用鱼叉刺；捕获；抨击
struck with or caught by a harpoon; criticized sharply
The whalers harpooned the massive whale. 捕鲸人用鱼叉刺中了那头巨大的鲸鱼。
The politician was harpooned by the media for his controversial comments.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而受到媒体的抨击。
近义词:speared, caught, criticized, attacked
反义词:released, praised
词性:verb

词源: From harpoon + -ed,  "harpoon" comes from  French "harpon," from Middle Dutch "harpe," meaning "harp" (referring to the shape).
