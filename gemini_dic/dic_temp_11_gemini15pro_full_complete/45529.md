curialist: /ˈkjʊəriəlɪst/
noun
教廷官员；罗马教廷律师
An official of the papal court, especially a lawyer.
Curialists were often involved in complex legal and diplomatic negotiations.  教廷官员经常参与复杂的法律和外交谈判。
The curialist delivered the Pope's message to the assembled ambassadors.  这位教廷官员向聚集的大使们传达了教皇的讯息。
Synonyms:  papal official, Vatican official, canon lawyer
Antonyms:  layperson, secular official
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *curiālis, from Latin cūria (“court”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱowH-riy-eh₂ (“assembly of men”).
