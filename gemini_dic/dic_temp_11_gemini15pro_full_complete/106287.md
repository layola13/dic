knuckleheaded:/ˈnʌkəlˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻头傻脑的|Stupid or foolish; lacking in intelligence or common sense.|He made a knuckleheaded decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他做了傻瓜似的决定，把所有钱都投资在一支股票上。|My knuckleheaded brother locked his keys in the car again. 我那傻兄弟又把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：stupid, foolish, dim-witted, idiotic|反义词：intelligent, smart, bright, clever|词性:形容词

词源:
From "knuckle" (指关节) + "head" (头), 字面意思是“指关节头”, 引申为“用指关节思考的人”,  暗指愚蠢或缺乏思考。
