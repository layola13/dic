monotoned:/ˈmɒnətəʊnd/ | adj. |单调的，无变化的 | Having a single, unchanging tone or pitch; lacking in variation or interest. | Her voice was monotoned, making the lecture difficult to follow. 她的声音单调，使讲座难以理解。| The monotoned sound of the dripping faucet drove me crazy. 水龙头滴水单调的声音让我抓狂。| 近义词: tedious, dull, boring, monotonous | 反义词: varied, diverse, interesting, exciting | 词性: 形容词

monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ | n. |单调，单调的声音 | A single, unchanging tone or pitch; a lack of variation or interest. | The teacher's monotone made the lesson boring. 老师单调的声音使课很无聊。| The constant monotone of the engine lulled me to sleep.  发动机持续不断的单调声音让我昏昏欲睡。| 近义词: tediousness, dullness, boredom, monotony | 反义词: variety, diversity, interest, excitement | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *monotone*, from Ancient Greek *μονότονος* (monótonos, “having one tone”), from *μόνος* (mónos, “single, alone”) + *τόνος* (tónos, “tone, pitch”).
