nonsympathy:/ˌnɒnˈsɪmpəθi/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪmpəθi/| n. |缺乏同情，不同情|Lack of sympathy; absence of shared feelings or understanding.|She showed a complete nonsympathy for his situation. 她对他的处境完全缺乏同情。|His nonsympathy made her feel even worse. 他的冷漠让她感觉更糟了。|近义词：indifference, apathy, coldness, unfeelingness|反义词：sympathy, compassion, empathy|词性:名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + sympathy (from Greek syn "together" + pathos "feeling")
