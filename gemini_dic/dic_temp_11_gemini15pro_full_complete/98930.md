intentness:/ɪnˈtɛntnɪs/| n. |专注，专心，一心一意|The state of being intent; fixed attention or purpose.|She listened with intentness. 她专心致志地听着。|近义词：concentration, focus, absorption, attention, diligence|反义词：distraction, inattention, negligence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *intentnes, from Old French *entencion (Modern French intention),  from Latin intentiō (“a stretching out, exertion, effort, attention”), from intendō (“stretch out, strain, exert, intend”), from in (“in, on, upon”) + tendō (“stretch, extend”).
