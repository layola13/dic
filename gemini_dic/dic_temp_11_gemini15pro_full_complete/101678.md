irreligious:/ˌɪrɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
不虔诚的，无宗教信仰的，不敬神的
Not religious; showing a lack of religion.
He made some irreligious comments that offended many people.  他发表了一些不敬的言论，冒犯了许多人。
The irreligious atmosphere of the club made him uncomfortable.  俱乐部里不虔诚的氛围让他感到不舒服。
近义词:godless, ungodly, profane, impious, secular
反义词:religious, devout, pious
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + religious.
