moldery:/ˈməʊldəri/ (UK) /ˈmoʊldəri/ (US)| adj.|发霉的；腐朽的；陈腐的|Overgrown with mold; musty and decaying.  Characterized by or suggestive of decay or decline, especially in thought or practice.|The old books in the attic were moldery and smelled of damp.  阁楼里的旧书发霉了，有一股潮湿的气味。|近义词: musty, mildewy, decaying, rotting, stale|反义词: fresh, new, clean, pristine|词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *moldery*, from *mold* (“mold, humus”) + *-ery* (suffix forming adjectives). Cognate with Dutch *mulderig*, German *mulderig*.
