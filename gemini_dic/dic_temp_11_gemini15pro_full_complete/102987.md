jeepers:/ˈdʒiːpərz/
interjection
哎呀；我的天哪
An exclamation of surprise, astonishment, or alarm.
Jeepers, that was close! 哎呀，好险！
Jeepers creepers, where'd you get those peepers? 天哪，你的眼睛是从哪儿弄来的？(这是首老歌歌词)
近义词: gosh, gee, wow, oh my
反义词: (感叹词通常没有明确的反义词)
词性: interjection
