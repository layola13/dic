huddler:/ˈhʌdlər/| n.| 挤作一团的人；蜷缩者；参加小组会议的人|A person who huddles, especially to keep warm or to confer privately.|The huddled masses yearning to breathe free.  渴望呼吸自由的 huddled masses.|近义词：crowder, squeezer, conferrer|反义词: loner, individualist|词性:noun

huddler:/ˈhʌdlər/| n.|参加小组会议的人|A person who participates in a huddle, a small group meeting for private discussion.|The marketing team held a quick huddler to brainstorm new campaign ideas.  营销团队举行了一个快速的小组会议来集思广益新的活动方案。|近义词：participant, team member, discussant|反义词: outsider, non-participant|词性:noun
