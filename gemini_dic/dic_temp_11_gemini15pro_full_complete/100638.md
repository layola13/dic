intolerance: /ɪnˈtɒlərəns/ (UK) /ɪnˈtɑːlərəns/ (US)| n. |不宽容，偏狭|Lack of tolerance; unwillingness or refusal to tolerate or respect contrary opinions or beliefs, persons of different races, etc. |例句：He showed intolerance towards people of different religions. 他对不同宗教信仰的人表现出不宽容。|例句：The government has pledged to fight against intolerance and discrimination. 政府已承诺打击不容忍和歧视。|近义词：bigotry, prejudice, discrimination, bias|反义词：tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness|词性:名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from medieval Latin intolerantia, from in- ‘not’ + tolerantia (see tolerance).
