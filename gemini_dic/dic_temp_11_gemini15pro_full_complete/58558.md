dukee:/ˈduːkiː/ | n. |  公爵（非正式，通常带有讽刺或幽默意味）| A humorous or ironic term for a duke. | Example: "He acts like such a dukee, expecting everyone to cater to him." 他表现得像个公爵似的，期望每个人都迎合他。| Example: "Look at Mr. Dukee over there, flashing his cash." 看看那边那位“公爵先生”，炫耀他的钱。 | 近义词: duke, nobleman, lord | 反义词: commoner, peasant | 词性: noun
