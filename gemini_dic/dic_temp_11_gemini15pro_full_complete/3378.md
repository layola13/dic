affrontive: /əˈfrʌntɪv/ | adj. | 侮辱性的，冒犯性的 | Causing offense; insulting.  |  His affrontive language caused a fight to break out. 他的侮辱性语言引发了一场斗殴。 |  insulting, offensive, disrespectful, abusive, provocative | respectful, polite, courteous, considerate, deferential | adjective

Etymology:  From French *affrontif, -ive*, from *affronter* "to confront, insult," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) "to" + *front* "forehead," from Latin *frons*.  The original sense was of striking someone on the forehead, a grave insult.
