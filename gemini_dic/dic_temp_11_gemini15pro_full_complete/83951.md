half-conquered:/ˌhɑːf ˈkɒŋkəd/ (US) /ˌhæf ˈkɒŋkəd/ (UK)
adjective
半征服的，未完全征服的
Partially conquered; not completely subdued or defeated.  Implies a state of being under partial control, but not fully defeated.
The half-conquered city was still a dangerous place.  半征服的城市仍然是一个危险的地方。
The army pushed forward into the half-conquered territory.  军队向前推进到半征服的领土。
近义词: partially subdued, partly defeated, incompletely conquered
反义词: fully conquered, completely subdued, totally defeated
词性: adjective
词源: "half" + "conquered" -  "half" (Old English healf) meaning "one of two equal parts"; "conquered" (from Old French conquerre, from Latin conquirere) meaning "to gain by force, subdue".
