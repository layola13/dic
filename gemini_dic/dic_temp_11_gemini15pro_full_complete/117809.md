meliphagan: /ˌmɛlɪˈfeɪɡən/ | n. | 吸蜜鸟 | A bird of the family Meliphagidae, found in Australasia and the Pacific islands, characterized by a long, curved bill and a brush-tipped tongue for feeding on nectar. |  The meliphagan flitted from flower to flower, sipping nectar. 吸蜜鸟在花丛中飞来飞去，吸食花蜜。| synonym: honeyeater | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps insectivore) | noun
