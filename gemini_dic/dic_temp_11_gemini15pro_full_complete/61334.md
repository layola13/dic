embattlement:/ɪmˈbæt(ə)lmənt/
noun
城垛；雉堞；防御工事
A parapet having a series of indentations or crenels, originally for use by defenders firing from a protected position.
The castle's embattlements offered a commanding view of the valley. 城堡的城垛可以俯瞰整个山谷。
synonyms: parapet, battlement, rampart, fortification
antonyms: opening, breach, vulnerability
n.
