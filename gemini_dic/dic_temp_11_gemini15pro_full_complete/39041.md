confluence:/ˈkɒnfluːəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfluːəns/ (US)| n. |汇合；汇流；汇合处；（河流的）交汇处；（事物的）融合，汇集|The confluence of the two rivers creates a fertile delta. 两条河流的汇合处形成了一个肥沃的三角洲。|例句：

The city is located at the confluence of two major rivers.  这座城市位于两条主要河流的交汇处。

There was a confluence of fortunate events that led to her success. 一系列幸运事件的汇合促成了她的成功。|近义词：convergence, junction, meeting, union, intersection|反义词：divergence, separation|词性: 名词

confluent:/ˈkɒnfluːənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfluːənt/ (US)| adj. |汇合的；汇流的；融合的|Confluent smallpox is a severe form of the disease.  融合性天花是该疾病的一种严重形式。|例句：

The confluent rivers created a powerful current. 汇合的河流形成了一股强劲的水流。

The artist's work represents a confluent stream of ideas.  这位艺术家的作品代表了融合的思想潮流。|近义词：converging, merging, joining|反义词：diverging, separating|词性: 形容词
