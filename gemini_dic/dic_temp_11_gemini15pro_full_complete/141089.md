onrush:/ˈɒnrʌʃ/ (UK) /ˈɑːnrʌʃ/ (US)| n.|猛攻，突击，奔流|A sudden forceful forward rush or flow.|The onrush of the river swept away the bridge. 河水的奔流冲垮了桥梁。|近义词：surge, influx, rush, onslaught|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词

Etymology: From on + rush.  "On" in this context intensifies the meaning of "rush."
