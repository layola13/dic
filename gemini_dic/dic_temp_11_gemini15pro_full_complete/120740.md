misanalyzed:/ˌmɪsˈænəlaɪzd/
verb
错误分析的
incorrectly analyzed; analyzed wrongly.
The data was misanalyzed, leading to flawed conclusions. 数据被错误分析，导致结论有缺陷。
The scientist misanalyzed the results of the experiment.  科学家错误地分析了实验结果。

近义词: misinterpreted, misconstrued, misread
反义词: correctly analyzed, accurately interpreted
