antireductionist:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃənɪst/
| adj. n.| 反还原论的；反还原论者 |  Opposed to reductionism; characterized by the belief that a complex system is more than the sum of its parts and cannot be fully understood by analyzing its individual components. |  "His antireductionist approach to the study of consciousness emphasizes the importance of emergent properties." 他对意识研究的反还原论方法强调了涌现属性的重要性。|  holistic, synthetic | reductionist | adjective, noun
