bushnells:/ˈbʊʃnəlz/| n.|布什内尔（复数）|A unit of liquid volume equal to eight gallons, used especially for berries and fruits.  It's primarily a historical term, less common in modern usage.|The farmer sold his strawberries by the bushnel.|Near synonyms: peck, bushel (though these are dry measures) |Antonym: N/A (units of volume don't typically have direct antonyms)|Noun
