conventionalisms:/kənˈvɛnʃənəlɪzəmz/
|n.|习俗；惯例；传统做法|Behaviors or practices that are widely accepted and followed within a particular group or society.  These are often unwritten rules or guidelines that dictate how people should behave in certain situations.|The debutante ball is full of silly conventionalisms.  名媛舞会充满了愚蠢的习俗。|conventions, customs, traditions, norms|unconventionalities, eccentricities|noun
