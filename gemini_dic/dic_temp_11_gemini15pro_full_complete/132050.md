noncorruptness:/ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptnəs/
n.
廉洁; 清廉; 不腐败
The state of being not corrupt; integrity.
The judge was known for his noncorruptness and impartiality.  这位法官以其廉洁公正而闻名。
Synonyms: integrity, honesty, probity, rectitude, uprightness
Antonyms: corruption, dishonesty, venality, depravity
词性: Noun
