downcast:/ˈdaʊnkæst/| adj.|沮丧的，垂头丧气的|Feeling or showing unhappiness and discouragement.|She looked downcast after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后显得很沮丧。|近义词：dejected, depressed, gloomy, sad|反义词：cheerful, happy, optimistic|adjective

downcast:/ˈdaʊnkæst/| vt.|使沮丧，使低落|To lower (something, especially one's eyes or head) in sadness or shame.|He downcast his eyes in shame. 他羞愧地低下了头。|近义词：lower, droop|反义词：raise, lift|verb
