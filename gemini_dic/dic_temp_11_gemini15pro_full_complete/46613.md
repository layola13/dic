damianist:/ˈdeɪmiənɪst/
n.
Damianist，达米安派教徒
A follower of Damianism, a fictional religion in the works of Robert Anton Wilson, particularly *The Illuminatus! Trilogy*.  Damianism is often associated with anarchism, sexual liberation, and disregard for conventional morality.
例句：The damianist argued that true freedom comes from embracing one's desires.  达米安派教徒认为真正的自由来自于拥抱自己的欲望。

例句：She suspected he was a damianist because of his unconventional lifestyle and beliefs. 她怀疑他是一个达米安派教徒，因为他非传统的生活方式和信仰。

近义词:freethinker, nonconformist, libertine, antinomian
反义词:conformist, traditionalist
词性:noun
