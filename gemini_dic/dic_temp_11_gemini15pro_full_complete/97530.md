ingraft:/ɪnˈɡræft/ /ɪnˈɡrɑːft/
verb
嫁接；灌输；移植
to insert or implant (something) as if by grafting
例句：
The gardener ingrafted a branch from a pear tree onto an apple tree.
园丁将梨树的枝条嫁接到苹果树上。

例句：
He tried to ingraft new ideas into the minds of his students.
他试图将新的思想灌输到学生的头脑中。

近义词：graft, implant, embed, instill
反义词：remove, extract, uproot
词性：v.
