half-pleasing:/ˌhæfˈpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
半令人愉悦的；不太令人满意的
Partially pleasing; not entirely satisfactory.
Her performance was half-pleasing, showing some promise but ultimately falling short. 她的表演差强人意，展现了一些潜力，但最终还是有所不足。
The food was half-pleasing; the appetizer was delicious, but the main course was bland.  这顿饭不太令人满意；开胃菜很美味，但主菜很清淡。
near synonyms:moderately pleasing, somewhat satisfying, half-satisfying
antonyms:displeasing, unsatisfying, fully pleasing
词性:adjective
