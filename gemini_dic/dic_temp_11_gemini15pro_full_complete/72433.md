fluidity:/fluːˈɪdəti/ /fluˈɪdədi/| n. |流动性，流畅性，易变性|The fluidity of the situation made it difficult to plan ahead.  局势的变幻莫测使得难以提前计划。|例句：The fluidity of his movements was mesmerizing. 他的动作流畅得令人着迷。|近义词：smoothness, flexibility, adaptability, volatility|反义词: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility|词性:noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin fluiditas, from Latin fluidus (see fluid).
