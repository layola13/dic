daguerreian:/dəˈɡɛriən/
adjective
达盖尔银版法的；早期照相术的
Relating to the daguerreotype process, an early photographic process.
The daguerreian portrait captured the subject's likeness with remarkable detail. 这幅达盖尔银版肖像以惊人的细节捕捉了拍摄对象的模样。

adjective
(of a photograph) taken by the daguerreotype process.
(指照片)用达盖尔银版法拍摄的
The museum exhibited a collection of daguerreian images from the 19th century.  博物馆展出了一批19世纪的达盖尔银版照片。

noun
a daguerreotype.
达盖尔银版照片
The daguerreian showed a stern-looking man in a top hat. 这张达盖尔银版照片展示了一位戴着高顶礼帽、表情严肃的男人。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Louis Daguerre (1787–1851), French artist and inventor of the daguerreotype + -ian.

SYNONYMS->
(adjective) early photographic, primitive photographic
(noun) daguerreotype, photograph


ANTONYMS->
(adjective) modern photographic
(noun)  No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with "digital photograph" or other modern photographic methods.
