fraternation:/ˌfrætərˈneɪʃn/ |n.| 兄弟会，博爱，友爱 |A group of people sharing a common profession or interests, or a local branch of a larger organization. |例句：He joined the fraternity in his sophomore year. 他在大二那年加入了兄弟会。

例句：The spirit of fraternity united the workers.  友爱的精神团结了工人们。

近义词：brotherhood, fellowship, sodality, association |反义词：sorority, enmity, hostility |词性：noun

fraternity:/frəˈtɜːrnəti/ |n.| 兄弟会，博爱，友爱 |The state or quality of being brothers or a brother; brotherhood. |例句：The fraternity of the legal profession.  法律行业的兄弟情谊。

例句：There is a strong sense of fraternity among the team members. 团队成员之间有强烈的兄弟情谊。
