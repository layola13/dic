extinguished: /ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/
adjective
熄灭的；消灭的；破灭的
put out (a fire or light); quench.
The fire was quickly extinguished.  火很快就被扑灭了。
近义词: quenched, smothered, doused, suppressed
反义词: ignited, kindled, lit, burning
词性: adjective


extinguish: /ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
熄灭；压制；偿清
put out (a fire or light); quench.
Firefighters worked hard to extinguish the blaze. 消防队员努力扑灭大火。
近义词: quench, smother, douse, suppress
反义词: ignite, kindle, light
词性: verb


词源: From Latin extinguere (to quench, extinguish), from ex- (out) + stinguere (to quench).
