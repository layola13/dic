consenting:/kənˈsɛntɪŋ/
adjective
同意的，愿意的
Agreeing to something or giving permission for something to happen.
She is a consenting adult and can make her own decisions. 她是一个成年人，可以自己做决定。
The study was conducted with the consenting participation of all subjects. 这项研究是在所有受试者同意参与的情况下进行的。
近义词:agreeing, compliant, approving
反义词:objecting, opposing, dissenting
词性:adjective

consenting:/kənˈsɛntɪŋ/
verb (present participle of consent)
同意，允许
Giving permission for something to happen.
By consenting to the terms and conditions, you agree to abide by the rules. 通过同意条款和条件，您同意遵守规则。
He is consenting to the medical procedure after being fully informed of the risks. 在充分了解风险后，他同意进行医疗程序。
近义词:agreeing, acquiescing, complying
反义词:refusing, declining, objecting
词性:verb
