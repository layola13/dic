complicit:/kəmˈplɪsɪt/| adj.|有同谋关系的，串通的|Involved with others in an illegal activity or wrongdoing.|例句：She was complicit in the robbery. 她参与了抢劫。

例句：Several officials were complicit in the cover-up. 一些官员参与了掩盖真相。

近义词：involved, implicated, accessory, guilty, culpable|反义词：innocent, uninvolved|词性:形容词
