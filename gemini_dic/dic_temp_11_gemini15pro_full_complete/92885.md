ideality:/aɪˌdiˈælɪti/| n.|理想性；完美性；理想状态|The ideality of the design was compromised by practical considerations.  这个设计的理想性因为实际的考虑而打了折扣。|例句：The painting captures the ideality of the rural landscape. 这幅画捕捉到了乡村景色的理想状态。

例句：He was striving for ideality in his art, seeking to represent perfect beauty. 他在艺术中追求理想化，力求展现完美的美。|近义词：perfection, ideal, utopianism|反义词：imperfection, reality, practicality|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idealitas, from Latin idealis (“ideal”) + -itas.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἰδέα (idéa, “form, pattern, idea”).
