gobbler:/ˈɡɑːblər/ (US) /ˈɡɒblə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 火鸡（尤指雄火鸡），贪婪的家伙 | A male turkey, especially a large one.  Also, a person who eats and drinks greedily. |  He shot a wild gobbler on Thanksgiving Day. 他在感恩节那天猎杀了一只野火鸡。


The competitive eater was a real gobbler, finishing five plates of food.  这位竞食者真是个贪吃鬼，吃了五盘食物。| 近义词：turkey (for the bird), glutton, gourmand (for the person) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "moderate eater") | 词性: noun
