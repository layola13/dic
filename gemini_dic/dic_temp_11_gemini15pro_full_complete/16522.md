barkbound:/ˈbɑːrkbaʊnd/
adj.
被树皮束缚的；被树皮包裹的；(比喻)思想狭隘的，保守的
Enclosed or covered with bark; (figuratively) narrow-minded, inflexible, or resistant to change.
The barkbound tree struggled to grow. 这棵被树皮束缚的树难以生长。
His barkbound views prevented him from accepting new ideas. 他保守的观点使他无法接受新的想法。
近义词:hidebound, narrow-minded, inflexible, conservative, rigid
反义词:open-minded, liberal, progressive, flexible
词性:形容词

词源:
From bark (n.) + bound (adj.).  "Bound" in this context means enclosed or restrained.
