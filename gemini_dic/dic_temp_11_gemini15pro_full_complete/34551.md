clear-headedly:/ˌklɪəˈhɛdɪdli/
| adv. |清醒地，头脑清晰地|In a way that shows clear thinking; rationally.|She clear-headedly assessed the situation and made a quick decision. 她头脑清晰地评估了情况，并迅速做出了决定。|例句：He clear-headedly addressed the complex issue. 他清晰地阐述了这个复杂的问题。|近义词：rationally, lucidly, logically|反义词：confusedly, irrationally, hazily|词性:副词
Etymology: clear + -headed + -ly.  From "clear-headed" (having a clear mind) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Old English "clæne" (clean, pure) and "hēafod" (head).
