humidification:/hjuːˌmɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 加湿，增湿 | The process of increasing the humidity (moisture content) of the air. |  The humidifier provides humidification for the dry winter air.  加湿器为干燥的冬季空气提供湿度。| synonyms: moisturizing, damping | antonyms: dehumidification, drying | noun

humidification:/hjuːˌmɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 加湿，增湿 | The act of making something more humid. | Proper humidification is essential for preventing respiratory problems in infants. 适当的加湿对于预防婴儿呼吸道问题至关重要。| synonyms: wetting | antonyms: desiccation | noun
