moistnesses:/ˈmɔɪstnəsɪz/
noun
潮湿，湿润（复数）
The state of being slightly wet; dampness.  The various degrees of moisture present in different samples.
The moistnesses of the different soil samples were carefully recorded.  对不同土壤样本的湿度进行了仔细记录。
Synonyms: dampness, humidity, wetness
Antonyms: dryness, aridity
词性: 名词
