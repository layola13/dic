noniridescently:/ˌnɒnɪrɪˈdɛsəntli/ | adv. | 非虹彩地，不闪光的 |  Not iridescent; lacking a play of lustrous colors like those of the rainbow. | The beetle's shell was noniridescently black.  甲虫的外壳是不闪光的黑色。| 近义词: dully, plainly | 反义词: iridescently | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not".
iridescent (adjective):  from Latin iris, iridis "rainbow",  + -escent (suffix) meaning "beginning to be" or "slightly".
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives.
