jalapin:/ˈdʒæləpɪn/ | n. |泻药，哈拉帕根辣椒|A purgative drug obtained from the tuberous roots of the Mexican scammony, *Ipomoea purga*. Also, sometimes, the root itself.  Now chiefly historical.  Sometimes refers to the related, similarly purgative, *Ipomoea jalapa* (also called jalap). Occasionally also refers to the hot pepper, *Capsicum jalapeno*.|He took a dose of jalapin to relieve his constipation. 他服用了一剂泻药来缓解便秘。|The apothecary sold jalapin and other herbal remedies. 药剂师出售泻药和其他草药。|近义词: purgative, cathartic, laxative |反义词: antidiarrheal, constipating |词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish *jalapa*, from Nahuatl *xalapa* (the name of the Mexican city of Xalapa, from which the drug was originally exported).
