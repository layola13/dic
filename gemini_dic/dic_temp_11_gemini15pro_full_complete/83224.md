gybed:/dʒaɪbd/ | v. | 改变航向；(使帆船)顺风转向 | To change the course of a sailboat so that the wind crosses the stern from one side to the other. | The captain gybed the boat to avoid the rocks. 船长改变航向以避开礁石。| The sailor skillfully gybed the yacht around the buoy.  水手熟练地操纵帆船绕过浮标。| 近义词：jibe | 反义词: tack | 词性: 动词
