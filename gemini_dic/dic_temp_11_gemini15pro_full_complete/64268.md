equipping:/ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ/
verb (present participle of equip)
装备，配备
the process of providing someone or something with the necessary tools or skills
The school is equipping students with the skills they need for the future.  这所学校正在为学生配备未来所需的技能。
synonyms: furnishing, providing, supplying, outfitting
antonyms: stripping, depriving, divesting
verb
