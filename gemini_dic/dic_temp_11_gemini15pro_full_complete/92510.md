hypothesizes:/haɪˈpɒθɪsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 假设，假定 |  Puts forward a proposition or supposition, typically as a basis for further investigation or argument. | Scientists hypothesize that the universe began with a big bang. 科学家们假设宇宙起源于一次大爆炸。|  Theorizes, postulates, proposes, suggests, speculates, surmises |  Proves, confirms, establishes | verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hypothesizare, from Ancient Greek ὑποτίθεσθαι (hupotíthesthai, “to suppose”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + τίθημι (títhēmi, “I put, place”).
