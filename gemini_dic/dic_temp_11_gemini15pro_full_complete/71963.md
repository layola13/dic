flightlessness:/ˈflaɪtlɪsnəs/
n.
无法飞行;飞不起来的状态
The inability to fly.
例句：The flightlessness of penguins is a well-known adaptation to their aquatic lifestyle.  企鹅不会飞是它们适应水生生活方式的一个众所周知的特征。

例句：The dodo's flightlessness contributed to its extinction. 渡渡鸟不会飞是导致其灭绝的原因之一。
近义词：inability to fly, winglessness
反义词：flight, ability to fly
词性：名词
