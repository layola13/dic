一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he bought from the store? = 他从商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你这周一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was he thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if I press this button? = 如果我按下这个按钮会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 你今天结束前会完成什么？
What will have changed by the year 2050? = 到2050年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for by the time you graduate? = 到你毕业的时候，你将已经学习什么多久了？
What will they have been working on for five years by next month? = 到下个月他们将已经做了这件事五年了？
