fall-pipe:/ˈfɔːlpaɪp/ | n. | 落水管；排泄管 | A pipe, usually external to a building, for carrying rainwater down from the gutters/eaves. |Rainwater gurgled down the fall-pipe. 雨水沿着落水管汩汩流下。| The fall-pipe was blocked with leaves. 落水管被树叶堵住了。 | synonym: downpipe, drainpipe, rainwater pipe | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "up-pipe" in a specific industrial context) | noun
