calamistrate: /kəˈlæmɪˌstreɪt/ | v. |  使（头发）卷曲；用卷发钳烫发 | To curl or wave (hair), especially using a heated iron. | She calamistrated her hair for the special occasion. 她为这个特殊场合烫卷了头发。| Synonym: curl, wave, crimp | Antonym: straighten | verb

calamistration: /kəˌlæmɪˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 卷发；烫发 | The act or process of curling or waving hair. | The calamistration of her hair took hours.  她烫头发花了几个小时。| Synonym: curling, waving, crimping | Antonym: straightening | noun

词源:

From Late Latin *calamistrātus, past participle of calamistrō (“I curl or wave the hair”), from Latin calamistrum (“curling iron”), diminutive of calamus (“reed”).
