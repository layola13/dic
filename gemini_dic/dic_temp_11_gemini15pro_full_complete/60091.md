eellike:/ˈiːlˌlaɪk/
adjective|像鳗鱼的；鳗鱼似的|Resembling an eel in form or movement.|The eellike creature slithered through the mud.  这条像鳗鱼一样的生物在泥里蜿蜒滑行。|例句：The eellike robot navigated the narrow pipe with ease.  这个像鳗鱼一样的机器人轻松地在狭窄的管道中航行。|近义词：serpentine, sinuous|反义词:straight, rigid|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From eel +‎ -like.  eel 来自古英语 *ǣl,  -like 来自古英语 līc.
