irrigating:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
灌溉，浇灌
Supplying land with water by artificial means.
The farmer is irrigating his fields.  农民正在灌溉他的田地。
The system is used for irrigating crops.  这个系统用于灌溉农作物。
watering, showering
draining, drying
