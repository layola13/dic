outwalking:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːkɪŋ/
v.
胜过…的步行; 比…走得更远
Surpassing someone in walking; walking further or for a longer duration than someone else.
He outwalked his companions, leaving them far behind. 他比同伴走得更远，把他们远远地甩在了后面。
outwalk:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːk/
verb
胜过…的步行; 比…走得更远
Surpassing someone in walking; walking further or for a longer duration than someone else.
She easily outwalked everyone in the group. 她轻松地比团队里的每个人都走得远。
近义词: outpace, outhike, outstride
反义词: lag behind
