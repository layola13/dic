matriarchies:/ˈmeɪtriˌɑːrkiːz/
n.
母系社会
A social system in which descent and inheritance are traced through the female line.
The Mosuo people of China are often cited as an example of a matriarchy.  中国摩梭人常被认为是母系社会的例子。
matriarchal societies, matriarchal systems
