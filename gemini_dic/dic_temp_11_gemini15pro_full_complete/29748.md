catoptrics:/kəˈtɒptrɪks/ /kæˈtɑːptrɪks/
n.
反射光學
The branch of optics that deals with the reflection of light from mirrors or other polished surfaces.
Catoptrics played a crucial role in the development of early telescopes. 反射光學在早期望遠鏡的發展中起到了至關重要的作用。
近义词:reflectivity, reflection optics
反义词:dioptrics
词性:noun
