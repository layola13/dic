microacoustic:/ˌmaɪkrəʊəˈkuːstɪk/ | adj. |微声的，微音响的 | Relating to sounds of very low intensity, or the devices used to produce or measure them. | Microacoustic sensors are used in a variety of applications. 微声传感器应用于各种领域。|  Microacoustic technology has revolutionized the hearing aid industry. 微声技术彻底改变了助听器行业。| synonyms: low-volume, faint, soft, quiet | antonyms: loud, noisy, boisterous | adjective
