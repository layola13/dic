frontline:/ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ (US), /ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ (UK)| n. adj.|前线，第一线；战线|The area or position closest to the fighting in a war, or the people who are involved in a difficult or dangerous situation.|The soldiers in the frontline are bravely defending their country.  前线的士兵们正在英勇地保卫他们的国家。|synonyms: forefront, vanguard, battlefront, leading edge|antonyms: rear, back, support|noun

frontline:/ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ (US), /ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ (UK)| adj.|前线的，第一线的|Relating to or involved in the most important or dangerous part of a situation or campaign.|Frontline workers like doctors and nurses are essential during a pandemic.  在一场大流行病期间，像医生和护士这样的前线工作者至关重要。|synonyms: foremost, leading, primary|antonyms: secondary, supporting, auxiliary|adjective
