orthodiagonal:/ˌɔːrθoʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/| adj.|正交对角线的，正交对角的|Relating to or denoting a diagonal which is perpendicular to another diagonal, especially in a rhombus or kite.  In geometry, it describes diagonals that are at right angles to each other.|The orthodiagonal quadrilateral has perpendicular diagonals.|Near synonyms: perpendicular, orthogonal|Antonyms: non-orthogonal, oblique|adjective

orthodiagonal:/ˌɔːrθoʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/| n.|正交对角线|A diagonal that is perpendicular to another diagonal, especially in a rhombus or kite.|The length of the orthodiagonal is crucial for calculating the area.|Near synonyms: perpendicular diagonal|Antonyms: non-orthogonal diagonal|noun
