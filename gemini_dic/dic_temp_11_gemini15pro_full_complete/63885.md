epiloguing:/ˈepɪlɒɡɪŋ/ | v. | 作结语；写结语；尾声；后记 | delivering or writing an epilogue; concluding |  He was epiloguing his speech with a call to action.  他以行动号召作为演讲的结语。 |  concluding, summarizing, finishing, winding up |  commencing, beginning, initiating | verb

epilogue: /ˈepɪlɒɡ/ | n. | 结语；尾声；后记 | a concluding section added to a literary work, as a novel or play | The epilogue of the movie tied up all the loose ends.  电影的尾声把所有未交代的情节都串联起来了。 |  conclusion, afterword, postscript, coda |  prologue, preface, introduction | noun
