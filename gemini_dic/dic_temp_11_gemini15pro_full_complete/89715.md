hoped-for:/ˈhoʊpt fɔːr/| adj.|期望的，盼望的|Something that is hoped for is something that someone wants to happen or to have.  It's formed by combining the verb "hope" (past participle "hoped") with the preposition "for". This creates an adjective describing something desired or anticipated.|We achieved our hoped-for outcome.  我们取得了我们期望的结果。|Synonyms: desired, wished-for, anticipated, expected|Antonyms: unexpected, undesired, unanticipated|adjective
