overcleans:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːnz/
verb
过度清洁；清洗得过分
to clean something too much
She overcleans her house to the point where it's sterile. 她把房子打扫得过于干净，简直到了无菌的地步。
The detergent can overclean and damage delicate fabrics.  这种洗涤剂可能会过度清洁并损坏精致的织物。
overwash, overscrub
