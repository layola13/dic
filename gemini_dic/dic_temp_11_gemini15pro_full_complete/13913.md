augustulus:/ɔːˈɡʌstjʊləs/
noun
小奥古斯都，末代皇帝
A diminutive of Augustus, used specifically to refer to Romulus Augustulus, the last Western Roman Emperor.
Romulus Augustulus, often called "Augustulus," was deposed in 476 AD.  罗慕路斯·奥古斯都，通常被称为“小奥古斯都”，于公元476年被废黜。
He was just a boy, earning him the unflattering nickname "Augustulus." 他只是个孩子，这为他赢得了不光彩的绰号“小奥古斯都”。
synonym: last emperor, final emperor
antonym: first emperor, founding emperor
