brokennesses:/ˈbroʊkənɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
破碎，破损；弱点，缺点
The state of being broken, either physically or emotionally; multiple instances of being broken.
The brokennesses of the system were apparent to everyone.  这个系统的缺陷对每个人来说都很明显。
The therapist helped her address the brokennesses in her past.  治疗师帮助她解决了过去的创伤。
frailty, fragility, weakness, vulnerability
strength, resilience, wholeness
noun
