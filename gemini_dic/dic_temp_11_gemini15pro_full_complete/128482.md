newspaperese:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərˌiːz/ /ˈnuːzˌpeɪpərˌiːz/
noun
报纸体；报刊语言；报刊文体
A style of writing typical of newspapers, often considered clichéd, sensationalized, or overly formal.  It is characterized by short sentences, colloquialisms, and a focus on conveying information quickly.
The article was full of newspaperese, making it difficult to read. 这篇文章充满了报刊语言，读起来很困难。
synonyms: journalese, tabloidese, press jargon
antonyms: literary style, academic writing
词性: 名词
