hafniums:/ˈhæfniəmz/| n.|铪（元素Hf）|A chemical element with the symbol Hf and atomic number 72. It is a lustrous, silvery gray, tetravalent transition metal, chemically similar to zirconium and found in zirconium minerals.|Hafniums are used in nuclear reactors due to their excellent neutron absorption properties.|Synonym: (none -  as it's a specific element)|Antonym: (none - as it's a specific element)|noun
