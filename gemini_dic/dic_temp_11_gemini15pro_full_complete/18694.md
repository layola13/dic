berate:/bɪˈreɪt/ | v. | 斥责，责骂 | To criticize or scold someone angrily and at length. | The coach berated the players for their poor performance. 教练严厉斥责球员们的糟糕表现。

The teacher berated the student for being late. 老师责骂学生迟到。

| 近义词：scold, rebuke, reprimand, chide, chastise | 反义词：praise, commend, compliment |词性: 动词
