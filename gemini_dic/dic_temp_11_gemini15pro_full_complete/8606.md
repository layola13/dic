anticly:/ˈæntɪkli/
adverb
古怪地；滑稽地；
In an antic manner; playfully eccentric or grotesque.  Acting in a bizarre or ludicrous fashion.
He danced anticly around the room. 他古怪地在房间里跳舞。
Synonyms: playfully, whimsically, fantastically, grotesquely, bizarrely, ludicrously
Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, gravely, soberly
词性: adverb

anticly:/ˈæntɪkli/
adjective
古怪的；滑稽的；
Playfully eccentric or grotesque.
Her anticly behavior amused everyone. 她滑稽的行为逗乐了所有人。
Synonyms: playful, whimsical, fantastical, grotesque, bizarre, ludicrous
Antonyms: serious, solemn, grave, sober

ETYMOLOGY->
From antic + -ly.  "Antic" itself comes from Middle French *antique* and ultimately from Latin *antiquus* (ancient, old).  Over time, the meaning shifted from "ancient" to "grotesque figures in old art" and then to "bizarre behavior."
