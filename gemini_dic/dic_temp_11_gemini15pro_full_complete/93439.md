illuminational:/ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adj.
启发的，启蒙的；照明的
Relating to illumination; enlightening.
The illuminational power of the lecture opened my mind to new possibilities. 这场讲座的启发性力量让我对新的可能性敞开了心扉。
The illuminational design of the stage created a magical atmosphere. 舞台的照明设计营造了一种神奇的氛围。
近义词: enlightening, illuminating, revelatory
反义词: obscuring, confusing, misleading
词性: adjective


illumination: /ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
照明，照亮；启发，启迪
The act or process of illuminating; the state of being illuminated.
The streetlights provided ample illumination for pedestrians. 路灯为行人提供了充足的照明。
The teacher's explanation provided illumination on the complex topic. 老师的解释阐明了这个复杂的话题。
近义词: enlightenment, insight, understanding
反义词: darkness, ignorance, confusion
词性: noun


词源: From Late Latin illuminatio, from illuminare “to illuminate,” from Latin in- “in” + luminare “to light,” from lumen “light.”
