churchly:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃli/| adj.|教堂的；似教堂的；虔诚的|Resembling or suggesting a church; befitting a church. Also, excessively pious or devout.|例句：The music had a churchly feel.  这音乐有一种教堂的感觉。|例句：His churchly demeanor impressed the congregation. 他虔诚的举止给会众留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：religious, devout, pious, reverent|反义词：secular, irreligious, irreverent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *chirchely*, equivalent to church +‎ -ly.
