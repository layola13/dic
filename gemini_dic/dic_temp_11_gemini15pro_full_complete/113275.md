lute:/luːt/
n.|鲁特琴；琵琶|A stringed musical instrument having a long neck bearing frets and a rounded body with a vaulted back.|The musician played a beautiful melody on the lute. 音乐家在鲁特琴上演奏了一首优美的旋律。|Synonyms: guitar, mandolin|Antonyms: None|Noun
