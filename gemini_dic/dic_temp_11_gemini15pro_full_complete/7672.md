anodize:/ˈænədaɪz/| vt.|阳极氧化，阳极化|To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide film by an electrolytic process in which the metal forms the anode.|The aluminum parts were anodized to prevent corrosion. 铝制零件经过阳极氧化处理以防止腐蚀。|Synonyms: oxidize, coat, plate|Antonyms: reduce, strip|verb
