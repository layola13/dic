ascents:/əˈsɛnts/ | n. | (地位、权力等的)提高，提升 |  An ascent can also refer to an advancement in social status or power. |  Her rapid ascent to the CEO position surprised everyone. 她迅速晋升到首席执行官的职位，让每个人都感到惊讶。| synonyms: advancement, promotion, rise | antonyms: demotion, decline | noun
