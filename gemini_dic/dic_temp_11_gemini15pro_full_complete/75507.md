fsb:/ɛfɛsˈbiː/| n. |前端总线（Front Side Bus）|The front-side bus (FSB) is a communication interface (bus) that was often used in personal computers from about 1992 to 2009.  It typically carries data between the central processing unit (CPU) and a northbridge or memory controller hub.  | The computer's FSB speed significantly affects its overall performance. 这台电脑的前端总线速度显著影响其整体性能。| 近义词:system bus, front-side bus| 反义词:N/A| noun
