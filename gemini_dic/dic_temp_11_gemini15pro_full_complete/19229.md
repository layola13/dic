bezpopovtsy: /ˌbɛspəˈpɔːftsɪ/ | n. | 无司祭派 | Members of a sect of Old Believers who reject priests and all church hierarchy.  They believe that the true church ceased to exist after Nikon's reforms in the 17th century and that any priestly authority is invalid. | The Bezpopovtsy faced persecution from both the official church and the Tsarist government.  无司祭派面临着来自官方教会和沙皇政府的迫害。| Synonym: priestless Old Believers | Antonym: popovtsy (priestly Old Believers) | Noun
