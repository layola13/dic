endowed:/ɪnˈdaʊd/ | adj. | 赋予的，被赋予的 | Provided or supplied or equipped with (especially a quality or ability) |  She was endowed with both beauty and intelligence. 她兼具美貌与智慧。|  gifted, talented, blessed | deprived, lacking | adjective

endowed:/ɪnˈdaʊd/ |  adj. | 捐赠的，资助的 | Given a large sum of money that is used to pay for the continuing cost of something | The university is well endowed. 这所大学资金雄厚。 | funded, financed, supported |  underfunded, impoverished | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *endowen, from Old French endouer (“to provide with a dowry, to give, to bestow”), from Latin indotare (“to endow with a dowry”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + dos (“dowry”).
