flout:/flaʊt/| v.|蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗|To show contempt for something, typically a law, rule, or convention, by openly disobeying or defying it.|The company flouted planning regulations and built the factory anyway.  这家公司无视规划规定，执意建造了工厂。|Synonyms: defy, scorn, disregard, disobey|Antonyms: obey, comply with, respect, observe|verb

flout:/flaʊt/| n.|蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗（较少用作名词）|An act of flouting; a contemptuous disregard.|His flout of the rules angered the committee. 他对规则的蔑视激怒了委员会。|Synonyms: defiance, scorn, disregard, disobedience|Antonyms: obedience, compliance, respect, observance|noun
