gearhead:/ˈɡɪrˌhɛd/| n. |汽车迷；机械爱好者|A person who is enthusiastic about cars, motorcycles, or other machinery, especially one who enjoys working on them.|He's a real gearhead, always tinkering with his car in the garage. 他是个真正的汽车迷，总是在车库里摆弄他的车。|Synonyms: motorhead, car enthusiast, mechanic, tinkerer|Antonyms: technophobe|noun
