coalesce: /ˌkoʊəˈlɛs/ | v. | 合并，联合 | to come or grow together to form one thing or system | The two small companies coalesced to form a larger corporation. 两家小公司合并成了一家大公司。|  The various groups coalesced into a strong political force. 不同的团体联合起来形成了一股强大的政治力量。| synonyms: merge, unite, combine, fuse, amalgamate | antonyms: separate, divide, split, disintegrate | verb

coalesced: /ˌkoʊəˈlɛst/ | adj. | 合并的，联合的 | having come or grown together to form one thing or system | The formerly separate departments have now coalesced into a single unit.  先前独立的部门现在已经合并成一个单元。| The coalesced forces of the two armies were unstoppable. 两军联合后的力量势不可挡。| synonyms: merged, united, combined, fused, amalgamated | antonyms: separate, divided, split, disintegrated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coalescere, from com- 'together' + alescere 'to grow up', from alere 'to nourish'.
