cushions:/ˈkʊʃənz/ | n. |垫子，靠垫，缓冲物|A soft bag filled with air, feathers, or other materials, used for sitting, lying, or kneeling on, or for softening the impact of a blow.|She plumped up the cushions on the sofa. 她拍蓬了沙发上的垫子。|Synonyms: pillows, pads, bolsters|Antonyms: hard surface|Noun

cushion:/ˈkʊʃən/ | v. |缓冲，减轻，缓和|To make something softer or less painful, or to protect someone or something from the effects of a fall, blow, or shock.|The thick carpet cushioned his fall. 厚厚的地毯缓冲了他摔倒的冲击力。|Synonyms: soften, protect, shield|Antonyms: aggravate, worsen|Verb
