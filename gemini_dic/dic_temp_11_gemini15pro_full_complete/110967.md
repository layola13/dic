liquescent:/lɪˈkwɛsənt/| adj.|液化的；融化的；易液化的|becoming liquid; melting|The liquescent lava flowed down the side of the volcano.  熔化的熔岩顺着火山的一侧流下。|The warm sun made the ice cream liquescent. 温暖的阳光使冰淇淋融化了。|近义词：melting, dissolving, liquefying, fluid|反义词：solid, solidifying, hardening|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *liquescent- (stem of liquescens), present participle of liquescere "to become liquid," from lique- (variant of stem of liquere "to be liquid," related to liquidus "liquid") + -escens, present participle ending.
