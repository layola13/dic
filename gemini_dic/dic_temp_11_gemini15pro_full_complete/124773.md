/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ | pron. | 什么 | used to ask for information about something | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | which, who |  | pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你这周一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她几点到的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I came? = 我来之前你做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议之前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed majors? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had she been doing for a living before she retired? = 她退休前一直做什么工作？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你会取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the year? = 到年底会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做这件事五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
