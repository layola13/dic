disflesh:/dɪsˈflɛʃ/
verb
剥去…的皮肉；使…脱肉
To remove the flesh from; to strip of flesh.
The warrior disfleshed his enemy's skull as a trophy.  战士将敌人的头骨剥去皮肉作为战利品。
近义词: excarnate, flay
反义词: embody, incarnate
词性: verb

词源：dis- (prefix meaning "apart, away, or reversing") + flesh (noun meaning "the soft substance of a human or animal body")
