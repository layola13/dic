calcareousness:/ˌkælˈkɛəriəsnəs/| n. |钙质; 含钙量|The calcareousness of the soil makes it ideal for growing grapes. 土壤的钙质使其成为种植葡萄的理想选择。|The high calcareousness of the water contributes to the formation of stalactites and stalagmites in the cave.  水中高含钙量促成了洞穴中钟乳石和石笋的形成。|近义词：chalkiness, alkalinity|反义词: acidity|词性:noun
calcareous:/kælˈkɛəriəs/| adj. |钙质的; 含钙的|Limestone is a calcareous rock. 石灰石是一种钙质岩石。|Many marine organisms have calcareous shells. 许多海洋生物有钙质壳。|近义词：chalky|反义词: siliceous|词性:adjective
