corruptness:/kəˈrʌptnɪs/
n.
腐败；贪污；堕落
The state of being corrupt; dishonesty or wickedness.
The corruptness of the officials shocked the public. 官员们的腐败震惊了公众。
Synonyms: corruption, depravity, dishonesty, venality, turpitude
Antonyms: integrity, honesty, probity, uprightness
