bowl:/boʊl/ (US) /bəʊl/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|碗；木球；球状物；投球；打保龄球|A round, deep dish used for food or liquids.  A delivery of a ball in cricket or bowling.  To roll a ball.|She ate a bowl of cereal. 她吃了一碗麦片。 He bowled a perfect game. 他打出了一个全中。|近义词：dish, basin, container (n.); roll, pitch (v.)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the noun.  For the verb, perhaps "catch")|noun, verb

PHRASE->
bowl someone over:  使某人印象深刻/征服某人  Her performance completely bowled me over. 她的表演完全征服了我。
bowl of cherries:  美好的事物/轻松愉快的生活  Life isn't always a bowl of cherries. 生活并不总是轻松愉快的。
bowling alley: 保龄球馆  Let's go to the bowling alley this weekend.  这周末我们去保龄球馆吧。
bowl a maiden over:  (板球)投出一个处女球  The bowler bowled a maiden over. 投球手投出了一个处女球。
full as a tick/bowl: 非常饱 I'm full as a tick after that Thanksgiving dinner.  感恩节晚餐后我吃得饱饱的。
