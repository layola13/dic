cornrows:/ˈkɔːrnroʊz/
noun
玉米辫；玉米垄式发型
A hairstyle, originating in Africa, in which the hair is braided very close to the scalp, creating a raised row that resembles a row of corn.
She wore her hair in cornrows. 她把头发扎成了玉米辫。
近义词：braids, plaits
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps loose hair or unbraided hair)
词性: noun
