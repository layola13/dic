overappraisal:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzəl/| n. |高估，过高评价|An act of overestimating the worth or value of something.|The real estate agent's overappraisal of the house led to a bidding war that drove the price up even further. 房地产经纪人对房屋的估价过高，导致了一场竞价战，进一步推高了价格。|Overestimation, overvaluation|Undervaluation, underestimation|noun

overappraise:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪz/| vt. |过高估计，过高评价|To estimate the worth or value of something too highly.|The company overappraised the value of its assets, leading to an inaccurate financial report.  这家公司高估了其资产的价值，导致财务报告不准确。|Overestimate, overvalue|Undervalue, underestimate|verb
