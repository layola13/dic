bell:/bel/ | n. |铃铛；钟声；钟形物|A hollow metal object, typically cup-shaped, that makes a ringing sound when struck.| The church bell rang out across the valley. 教堂的钟声响彻山谷。 |synonyms: chime, gong, ringer| antonyms: silence, mute | noun

bell:/bel/ | v. |系铃于；使成钟形| To attach a bell to something, or to cause something to take on a bell shape.|  They belled the cat. 他们在猫身上系了铃铛。| synonyms: attach a bell, ring | antonyms: unbell, detach a bell | verb
