cloyed:/klɔɪd/| adj.|厌腻的，腻烦的|Feeling sick or disgusted because you have had too much of something pleasant, especially rich food.|After two weeks of vacationing in five-star hotels, she felt cloyed by luxury.  在五星级酒店度假两周后，她对奢华感到厌倦。|近义词：satiated, surfeited, jaded|反义词：unsatisfied, wanting|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *cloien, from Old French cloer (“to nail, fix, pierce, stick, stop up, block up”), from Vulgar Latin *clāvāre, from Latin clāvus (“nail”). More at clew.
