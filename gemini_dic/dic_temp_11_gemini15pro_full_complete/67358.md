extension: /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (UK), /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (US) | n. | 延长，延伸；延期；扩展；电话分机 | An increase or enlargement in size, scope, or duration. |  My visa got a three-month extension. 我的签证延期了三个月。


extension: /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (UK), /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (US) | n. | 延伸部分；增建部分 |  A part that is added to something to enlarge or prolong it. | The new extension to the house provided much needed space.  房子的新扩建部分提供了急需的空间。


extension: /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (UK), /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (US) | n. | 电话分机 | A telephone line connected to the main line within a building or organization.  | Please dial extension 201 for sales. 请拨分机201 找销售部。


extension: /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (UK), /ɪkˈstenʃən/ (US) | n. | 扩展名（计算机文件） | A set of characters added to the end of a filename to indicate its format. | The file has a .pdf extension.  这个文件扩展名是 .pdf。

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin extensio(n-), from the verb extendere (see extend).

近义词: expansion, enlargement, prolongation, add-on, annex, supplement

反义词: reduction, shortening, curtailment, decrease

词性: noun
