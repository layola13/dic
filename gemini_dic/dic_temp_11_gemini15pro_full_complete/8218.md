anti-freudians:/ˌæntiˈfrɔɪdiənz/ | n. | 反弗洛伊德主义者 |  Those who disagree with the theories of Sigmund Freud, the founder of psychoanalysis.  This can include those who reject his theories entirely, or those who adapt and modify them. |  Many anti-Freudians believe that his emphasis on sexual drives was exaggerated. 许多反弗洛伊德主义者认为他对性驱力的强调过于夸大。

The anti-Freudians offered alternative explanations for human behavior.  反弗洛伊德主义者提供了对人类行为的替代解释。 | 近义词: critics of Freud, dissenters from Freudianism | 反义词: Freudians, proponents of Freudianism | 词性: 名词(plural)
