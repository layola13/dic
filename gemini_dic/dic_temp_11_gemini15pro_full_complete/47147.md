davos:/ˈdaːvɔs/| n.|达沃斯|A town in eastern Switzerland, known for hosting the World Economic Forum (WEF), an annual meeting of global political and business leaders. |Every year, world leaders gather in Davos to discuss important economic issues. 每年，世界各国领导人齐聚达沃斯，讨论重要的经济问题。|Synonyms:  alpine resort, conference venue|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology->
From Romansh *Tavau, from Latin *ad tavos (“at the taverns”).
