nonbeatifically:/ˌnɒnˌbiːəˈtɪfɪkəli/
adverb
非祝福地；不快乐地；不狂喜地
in a way that does not suggest supreme happiness or blessedness.
He sighed nonbeatifically as he realized the enormity of the task ahead.  他意识到 앞으로의 과제의 엄청남을 깨닫고 행복하지 않은 듯 한숨을 쉬었다.
She accepted the award nonbeatifically, her face betraying a hint of disappointment. 她脸上带着一丝失望的表情，不快乐地接受了这个奖项。
近义词:unhappily, joylessly, miserably, gloomily
反义词:beatifically, blissfully, joyfully, ecstatically
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + beatifically (from beatific + -ally). Beatific comes from Late Latin beatificus, from Latin beatus (blessed) + -ficus (making).
