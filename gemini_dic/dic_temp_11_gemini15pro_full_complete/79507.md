ETYMOLOGY->
From globe + flower.  "Globe" refers to the round, globe-like shape of the flower.  The genus name *Trollius* is believed to be derived from the German word "Trollblume," meaning "troll flower."  In German folklore, trolls were often associated with magical plants and flowers.
