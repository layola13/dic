keramographic:/ˌkɛrəməˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |陶瓷显微的，陶瓷照相的 |Relating to keramography, the study of the microstructure of ceramic materials.  |Keramographic analysis revealed the presence of porosity in the sample. 陶瓷显微分析揭示了样品中存在孔隙。| Synonym: ceramographic, metallographic (when applied to metals)| Antonym: N/A |adjective

keramography:/ˌkɛrəˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |陶瓷显微术，陶瓷照相术|The study of the microstructure of ceramic materials, typically using microscopy. |Keramography is essential for understanding the properties of ceramic materials. 陶瓷显微术对于理解陶瓷材料的特性至关重要。|Synonym: ceramography, metallography (when applied to metals)| Antonym: N/A |noun
