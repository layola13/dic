fustians:/ˈfʌstiənz/
noun
浮夸的言辞；粗斜纹棉布
pompous or pretentious language in speech or writing; a kind of coarse twilled cotton cloth.
His speech was full of fustians and empty promises. 他的演讲充满了浮夸的言辞和空洞的承诺。
The actor delivered his lines with such fustians that the audience laughed. 演员的台词过于夸张，以至于观众都笑了。
近义词：bombast, rhetoric, rant, cant
反义词：sincerity, simplicity, conciseness
词性:n.

fustyan:/ˈfʌstiən/
adjective
浮夸的；陈腐的；粗斜纹棉布制的
pompous or pretentious in style; old-fashioned and stuffy; made of fustian.
The fustyan prose of the 18th century is not easy to read today. 18 世纪浮夸的散文如今读起来并不容易。
He wore a fustyan old coat. 他穿着一件陈旧的粗斜纹棉布外套。
近义词：pompous, pretentious, bombastic, overblown
反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, natural
词性:adj.
