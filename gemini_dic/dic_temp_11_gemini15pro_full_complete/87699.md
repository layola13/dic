hexane:/ˈhɛkseɪn/ | n. |己烷|A colorless, flammable liquid alkane with the chemical formula C6H14, used as a solvent and in gasoline.|Hexane is commonly used in laboratories as a non-polar solvent. 己烷通常在实验室用作非极性溶剂。|Hexane is a component of gasoline. 己烷是汽油的一种成分。|Synonyms: n-hexane |Antonyms: None |noun
