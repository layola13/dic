measuring:/ˈmeʒərɪŋ/
adjective|测量的，计量的|the act of ascertaining the size, amount, or degree of something.|They were busy measuring the area of the room. 他们正忙着测量房间的面积。|mensural, quantifying|immeasurable, incalculable
verb|(measure的现在分词) 测量，估量|present participle of "measure," meaning to determine the size, amount, or degree of something.|He is measuring the ingredients for the cake. 他正在测量蛋糕的配料。|calculating, gauging|guessing, estimating
noun|测量，尺寸|the process of determining the size, quantity, or extent of something.|The accurate measuring of ingredients is crucial in baking.  在烘焙中，准确测量配料至关重要。|measurement, dimension|guess, estimate
