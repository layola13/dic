outpeopled:/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəld/
verb
人口超过，居民数量胜过
To exceed in population; to surpass in the number of inhabitants.
The indigenous tribes were eventually outpeopled by the settlers.  这些土著部落最终在人口数量上被定居者超过。
The small island nation was quickly outpeopled by immigrants. 这个岛国的人口很快被移民超过。
Synonyms: outnumbered, surpassed
Antonyms: underpopulated, depopulated
词性: 动词
