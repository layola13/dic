conjector:/kənˈdʒɛktər/| n.|推测者，猜想者|Someone who forms an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information.|The conjector guessed the answer correctly.  这位推测者猜对了答案。|近义词：guesser, speculator, hypothesizer|反义词：knower, expert|noun
