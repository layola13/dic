anteing:/ˈæntiɪŋ/
verb
下注；付赌注金
The act of placing an ante or bet, especially in poker.
He was anteing up $100 in each round of poker. 他在每一轮扑克中都下注 100 美元。
Synonyms: betting, wagering, staking
Antonyms: withdrawing, folding
词性:动词

Etymology:
From ante (preposition and noun), from Latin ante (“before, in front of”).  The verb sense developed from the noun sense of the initial bet or stake in poker.
