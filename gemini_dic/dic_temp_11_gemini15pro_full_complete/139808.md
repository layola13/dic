octobers:/ɑːkˈtoʊbərz/
n.
十月（复数）

The Octobers of my childhood were always filled with colorful leaves.  我童年的十月总是充满了五彩缤纷的叶子。
We've had some beautiful Octobers in recent years. 近些年我们经历了一些美丽的十月。
