anti-dynastical:/ˌænti-daɪˈnæstɪkəl/
adjective
反王朝的；反对王朝统治的
Opposed to dynasties or dynastic rule; advocating against the rule of families or hereditary succession of power.
The anti-dynastical movement gained momentum after the king's death.  反王朝运动在国王死后获得了动力。
The protestors carried anti-dynastical banners. 抗议者举着反王朝的旗帜。
Synonyms: anti-monarchical, anti-hereditary
Antonyms: dynastic, monarchist, hereditary
词性: adjective
