accented: /əkˈsɛntɪd/ | adj. | 带重音的；强调的；口音的 | Pronounced or spoken with an accent; emphasized or stressed. |  The accented syllables in this poem create a rhythmic effect. 这首诗中带重音的音节创造了一种节奏感。| stressed, emphasized, highlighted | unaccented, unemphasized | adjective

accented: /əkˈsɛntɪd/ | adj. | 带有…口音的 | Speaking with a particular accent. | She spoke English with a heavily accented French accent. 她说着带有浓重法国口音的英语。 | foreign-sounding, dialectal | native, standard | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accentuatus, past participle of accentuare (“to accentuate”), from Latin accentus (“accent”), from ad (“to”) + cantus (“song,” “chant”).
