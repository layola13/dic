dissymmetrical: /ˌdɪsɪˈmetrɪkəl/ | adj. | 不对称的，不对称的 | Lacking symmetry; not identical on both sides of a dividing line.  Not symmetrical, but not necessarily asymmetrical. Dissymmetrical objects can have rotational symmetry but lack mirror symmetry. | The crystal structure is dissymmetrical.  这种晶体结构是不对称的。| The artist intentionally made the sculpture dissymmetrical to create a sense of imbalance.  艺术家故意使雕塑不对称，以营造一种不平衡感。 |  asymmetrical, unsymmetrical | symmetrical | adjective
