antiphilosophical:/ˌæntiˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl/ |adj.|反哲学的，反对哲学的|Opposed to or mistrustful of philosophy; rejecting the value or relevance of philosophical inquiry.|His antiphilosophical stance surprised many of his colleagues. 他的反哲学立场让很多同事感到惊讶。|Example: Some scientists hold an antiphilosophical view, believing that empirical data trumps abstract theorizing. 一些科学家持有反哲学的观点，认为经验数据胜过抽象理论。|Synonyms: unphilosophical, nonphilosophical |Antonyms: philosophical, pro-philosophical|adjective
