disquisitional:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃənəl/
adjective
论述的，探讨的
Given to or characterized by formal, lengthy, and often learned discussion or argument.
His writing style is dense and disquisitional. 他的写作风格浓重而充满论述。
The professor delivered a disquisitional lecture on the history of philosophy.  教授就哲学史发表了一场充满论述的演讲。
近义词:argumentative, analytical, critical, scholarly, academic
反义词: concise, brief, superficial, simplistic
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *disquisitionālis, from Latin disquisitiō ("inquiry, investigation"), from disquisītus, perfect passive participle of disquīrō ("seek out, inquire, examine, discuss"), from dis- ("apart, thoroughly") + quaerō ("seek, inquire").
