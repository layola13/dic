french-kissed:/ˈfrɛntʃ kɪst/
| adj. |法式轻吻的，(食物)略带焦糖色的|Having a light brown color or slightly caramelized, as if lightly kissed by heat, often used to describe food.  It can also refer to a style of kissing.|The french-kissed bread had a delightful crunch.  法式轻吻的面包口感香脆。|Synonyms: caramelized, browned, lightly toasted|Antonyms: raw, uncooked|adjective

french-kissed:/ˈfrɛntʃ kɪst/
| v. |(被)法式轻吻|To kiss in the French style; or to be lightly browned or caramelized, usually by heat.|The chef french-kissed the scallops to perfection.  厨师将扇贝轻煎至完美焦糖色。|Synonyms:  lightly sear, caramelize, brown|Antonyms: undercook, blanch|verb
