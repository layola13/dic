dodd:/dɑːd/ | n. | 道德，正直的人；古怪的人；怪人 | A person of integrity or high moral character; an eccentric or peculiar person. | He was a real dodd, always wearing mismatched socks. 他真是个怪人，总是穿着不搭的袜子。| 近义词: eccentric, character, individual | 反义词: conformist | 词性: 名词

词源:  Possibly a variant of "dod" meaning a small lump or piece, implying someone small or insignificant but with hidden depths. The precise etymology is uncertain.
