discerningly:/dɪˈsɜːrnɪŋli/
adverb
敏锐地；有识别力地；有眼力地
In a way that shows good judgment, especially about style and quality.
She dressed discerningly in simple but elegant clothes. 她穿着简单而优雅的衣服，很有品味。
Synonyms: astutely, perceptively, shrewdly, wisely, judiciously
Antonyms: indiscriminately, uncritically, blindly, foolishly
词性: 副词

discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/
verb
辨别；看出；察觉
Recognize or find out.
I could just discern a figure in the darkness. 我隐约能辨认出黑暗中的一个人影。
Synonyms: perceive, detect, recognize, distinguish, make out
Antonyms: overlook, miss, ignore
词性: 动词
