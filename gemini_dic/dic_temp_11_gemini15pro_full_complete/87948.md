high-born:/ˈhaɪˈbɔːrn/| adj.|出身高贵的，贵族出身的|Born into a family of high social rank.  Of noble birth.|She was a high-born lady, accustomed to a life of luxury. 她是一位出身高贵的女士，习惯了奢华的生活。|例句：His high-born manner betrayed his aristocratic origins. 他高贵的举止暴露了他的贵族出身。|近义词：noble, aristocratic, high-ranking, blue-blooded|反义词：low-born, common, plebeian|词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *hihborn*, equivalent to *high* + *born*.  Cognate with Dutch *hooggeboren*, German *hochgeboren*.
