luckily:/ˈlʌkɪli/| adv.|幸运地，幸亏，好运地|It means fortunately; by good luck.  It expresses that something good or fortunate has happened.|Luckily, I had a spare tire in the car. 幸好，我的车里有个备胎。|Luckily, the rain stopped just before the picnic. 幸好，野餐前雨停了。|fortunately, thankfully, happily|unfortunately, unluckily|adverb
