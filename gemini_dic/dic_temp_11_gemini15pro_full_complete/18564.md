benen:/ˈbɛnən/| n.|骨头; 腿骨 (尤指动物或鸟的)|Bones, especially the larger ones in the legs or wings of an animal or bird.|The dog buried its benen in the garden. 狗把它的骨头埋在了花园里。|近义词：bone, skeleton|反义词:flesh, muscle|noun

benen:/ˈbɛnən/| v.|剥去…的骨; 从…剔去骨头|To remove the bones from (meat, poultry, or fish).|The butcher benened the chicken before roasting it. 屠夫在烤鸡前把鸡骨剔除。|近义词：debone|反义词: N/A|verb
