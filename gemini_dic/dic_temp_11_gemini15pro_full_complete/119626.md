middle-agednesses:/ˌmɪdlˈeɪdʒɪdnəsɪz/| n. |中年时期（复数，罕用）|The state of being middle-aged (plural form, rarely used).  This word is grammatically correct, but its plural form is rarely used in modern English. People usually use "middle age" to refer to this period of life. |Example: The author explored the middle-agednesses of various characters in the novel. (作者在小说中探讨了不同人物的中年时期。)
