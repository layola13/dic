franchisability:/ˌfræn.tʃaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可特许经营性，可加盟性 | The quality of being suitable for franchising; the ease with which a business can be franchised. | The franchisability of a business model depends on its replicability and scalability.  一个商业模式的可特许经营性取决于它的可复制性和可扩展性。| 近义词: franchise potential | 反义词:  non-franchisable | 词性: 名词
