foregoers:/ˈfɔːrˌɡəʊərz/ (US) /ˈfɔːrˌɡoʊərz/| n. |前辈，先驱|People who came or lived before you, especially those who prepared the way for later developments or discoveries.|例句：Our forefathers fought for the freedoms we enjoy today. 我们的先辈为我们今天享有的自由而战。

例句：The museum honors the forefathers of aviation.  这家博物馆向航空先驱致敬。

近义词：predecessors, ancestors, pioneers|反义词:descendants, successors|词性:名词
