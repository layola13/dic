oops-a-daisy:/ˌuːps ə ˈdeɪzi/ | int. |哎呀; 哎哟 (表示轻微的错误或事故)| An exclamation of mild apology or sympathy when someone has slightly tripped, stumbled, or dropped something. | Oops-a-daisy! I almost dropped the vase. 哎呀！我差点把花瓶摔了。 | 近义词: whoops, whoopsie-daisy, oops | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词) | 词性:感叹词

词源:  This expression likely originated as a euphemism for a mild curse or oath. The "daisy" part possibly softens the exclamation, similar to other expressions like "sugar" or "honey."  The exact origin is uncertain.
