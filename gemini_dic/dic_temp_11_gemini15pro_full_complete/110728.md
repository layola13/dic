linesmen:/ˈlaɪnzmən/| n.|边线裁判；线路工人|An official who assists the referee in a game like soccer or football, by judging whether the ball has gone out of play at the side of the field, and also by signaling offsides. Also, a person who installs or repairs electrical power lines or telephone lines.|The linesman raised his flag to signal offside. 边线裁判举旗示意越位。|近义词：assistant referee, linesperson|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps referee as a contrasting role)|noun


linesmen:/ˈlaɪnzmən/| n.|线路工人|A person who installs or repairs electrical power lines or telephone lines. |The linesmen worked through the night to restore power after the storm. 线路工人们通宵工作以在暴风雨后恢复电力。|近义词：lineman, power lineman, telephone lineman|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
