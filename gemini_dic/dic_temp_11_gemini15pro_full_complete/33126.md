christly:/ˈkraɪstli/| adj.|像基督的，神圣的，虔诚的 (有时用作感叹词，表示惊讶或恼怒)|Of or relating to Christ; resembling Christ; pious.  Can also be used as an interjection expressing surprise or annoyance.|例句：He spoke with a christly tone. 他说话的语气很虔诚。|近义词：holy, godly, pious, sacred|反义词：unholy, ungodly, impious, profane|词性:形容词

christly:/ˈkraɪstli/| interj.|（感叹词）我的天啊！|Used as a mild oath expressing surprise or annoyance.|例句：Christly, what a mess! 我的天啊，真是一团糟！|近义词：Gosh, My goodness, Heavens|反义词: (无明确的反义词，因为是感叹词)|词性:感叹词
