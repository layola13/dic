bespattering:/bɪˈspætərɪŋ/
verb
溅污，玷污
The act of splashing small drops of liquid or mud onto something.
The car was bespattering mud on the pedestrians as it sped through the puddle. 汽车飞速穿过水坑，把泥水溅到了行人身上。
splashing, splattering, spraying, spotting
covering, shielding, protecting
v.
