first-degree:/ˌfɜːrst dɪˈɡriː/ /ˌfɝːst dɪˈɡriː/
adjective
第一级的；一级；最严重的；直接的
Relating to or denoting the most serious category of a crime (such as murder).
例句：He was convicted of first-degree murder. 他被判一级谋杀罪。

例句：She suffered first-degree burns on her arms. 她的手臂有一级烧伤。

近义词：primary, initial, basic, fundamental
反义词：second-degree, third-degree, minor, secondary
词性：adjective
