metis: /ˈmiːtɪs/ (US), /ˈmiːtɪs/ (UK)| n.| 智慧，机智，才智；(希腊神话) 智慧女神| Practical skill and cleverness:  She showed great metis in solving the puzzle. 她展现出非凡的智慧，解决了这个难题。|例句：His metis allowed him to succeed where others failed. 他的智慧让他在别人失败的地方取得了成功。

例句：The ancient Greeks revered Metis, the goddess of wisdom and cunning. 古希腊人崇敬墨提斯，智慧和狡猾的女神。

近义词：wisdom, ingenuity, cunning, acumen, intelligence|反义词: stupidity, foolishness|词性: 名词
