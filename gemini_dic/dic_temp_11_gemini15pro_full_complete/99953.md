interobservers:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvərz/ | n. | 观察者之间 |  People who observe the same phenomenon or event.  This word emphasizes the presence of multiple observers and the potential for differences in their observations. | The interobservers agreed on the general behavior of the chimpanzees, but disagreed on the details of their social interactions. 观察者们就黑猩猩的大体行为达成了一致，但在其社会互动细节上存在分歧。| Synonyms: raters, judges, assessors | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

interobserver:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvər/ | adj. | 观察者间的 | Existing or occurring between observers.  This adjective describes something related to the comparison or agreement between multiple observers. | Interobserver reliability is crucial for ensuring the validity of the data. 观察者间信度对于确保数据的有效性至关重要。| Synonyms: interrater, between-observers | Antonyms: N/A | Adjective
