bestiary: /ˈbɛstiəri/ | n. |动物寓言集，怪兽图鉴 | A collection of descriptions of real and fabled animals, often illustrated, and typically giving symbolic interpretations of the beasts' moral or allegorical significance. |  He was fascinated by the medieval bestiaries and their fantastical creatures.  他被中世纪的动物寓言集及其奇幻生物深深吸引。 | Example: The illuminated manuscript contained a bestiary depicting both real and mythical creatures. 这份泥金装饰手抄本包含一部动物寓言集，描绘了真实的和神话中的生物。 | synonym:  menagerie, compendium,  catalog | antonym:  None (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
