corvée:/kɔːrˈveɪ/ /kɔːrˈviː/
noun
徭役；义务劳动；苦差事
A day’s unpaid labor owed by a vassal to his feudal lord.  Forced labor exacted in lieu of taxes, especially for highway construction or repair. An unpleasant or arduous task.
The villagers resented the corvée imposed by the local lord.  村民们怨恨当地领主强加的徭役。
Synonyms:  forced labor, statute labor, compulsory labor, unpaid work, drudgery, toil, chore
Antonyms: leisure,  recreation,  rest,  vacation
n.
