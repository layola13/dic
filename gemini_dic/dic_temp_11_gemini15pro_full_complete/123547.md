monozoa: /ˌmɒnəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 单生动物 |  A subkingdom of animals comprising the protozoa characterized by a single cell or a colony of similar cells.  | Monozoa, like amoebas, are single-celled organisms. 像变形虫一样，单生动物是单细胞生物。 |  Protozoa, protist | Metazoa | noun
