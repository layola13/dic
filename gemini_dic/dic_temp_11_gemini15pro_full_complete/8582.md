anticlassicalness:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəlnəs/| n. |非经典性，反经典性|The state or quality of being not classical; deviation from classical principles or styles.|  The artist's embrace of anticlassicalness is evident in his rejection of traditional forms.  这位艺术家对非经典性的拥护体现在他对传统形式的拒绝上。|近义词：nonclassicality, unconventionality,  modernism|反义词: classicism, traditionalism|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposite of (from Greek *anti-)
classical (adjective): relating to the first class or rank, of the highest quality, traditional and long-established in form or style (from Latin classicus)
-ness (suffix): forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition
