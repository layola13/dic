overseership:/ˈoʊvərˌsiːərˌʃɪp/ |n.|监督；监管；指导|The overseership of the project was given to the most experienced engineer.  这个项目的监督工作交给了最有经验的工程师。| Overseership involves ensuring that tasks are completed efficiently and effectively. 监督涉及确保任务有效地完成。|近义词：supervision, management, superintendence|反义词：neglect, abandonment|词性: 名词

词源:  From "overseer" + "-ship."  "Overseer" comes from the Old English "ofersēon," meaning "to look over" or "supervise."  The suffix "-ship" denotes a state, office, or quality.
