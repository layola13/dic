moretto:/məˈrɛtoʊ/
noun
小黑人；黑皮肤的小男孩（尤指绘画或雕塑作品中的形象）；一种深色甜咖啡
A dark-skinned boy, especially as represented in painting or sculpture; a type of dark sweet coffee.
The artist was known for his paintings of moretti playing musical instruments.  这位艺术家以其描绘黑人小男孩演奏乐器的画作而闻名。
The cafe's signature drink was a rich, flavorful moretto.  这家咖啡馆的招牌饮品是一种浓郁美味的深色甜咖啡。
synonyms:dark-skinned boy,  blackamoor (somewhat archaic and potentially offensive)
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "fair-skinned boy" for the artistic representation sense)
n.
