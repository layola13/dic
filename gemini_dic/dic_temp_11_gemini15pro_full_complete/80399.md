golob:/ˈɡɔːlɔːb/| n.|鸽子（尤其指斯洛文尼亚语中的鸽子）|A golob is a dove or pigeon, particularly used in Slovenian. |The golob landed on the windowsill.  鸽子落在了窗台上。|  The president released a white golob as a symbol of peace. 总统放飞了一只象征和平的白鸽。 | synonyms: dove, pigeon | antonyms: hawk, falcon | noun
