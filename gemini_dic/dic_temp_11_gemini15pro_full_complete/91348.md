hydroponicists: /ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪsɪsts/ | n. | 水培种植者；水耕栽培者 | A person who practices or studies hydroponics, the method of growing plants without soil, using mineral nutrient solutions in a water solvent. |  The hydroponicists monitored the pH levels of the nutrient solution carefully. 水培种植者仔细监测营养液的pH值。

The conference attracted hydroponicists from all over the world. 这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的水培种植者。 | 近义词:hydroponics grower,  hydroculture practitioner | 反义词: soil farmer, traditional farmer | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hydroponics + -ist.  Hydroponics itself comes from Greek *hydro-*, "water," and *ponos*, "labor."  Thus, "working with water."
