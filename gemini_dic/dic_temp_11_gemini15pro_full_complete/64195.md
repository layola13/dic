equianalgesic: /ˌiːkwiˌænəlˈdʒiːzɪk/ /ˌiːkwiˌænəlˈdʒiːsɪk/
adjective
等效镇痛的
Having equal analgesic (pain-relieving) effects.  Relating to doses of different analgesic drugs that produce the same degree of pain relief.
Equianalgesic doses of morphine and hydromorphone were administered.  给予等效镇痛剂量的吗啡和氢吗啡酮。
近义词: equipotent
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "dissimilar analgesic effect" or "variable analgesic effect")
词性: adjective


词源:
From equi- (equal) + analgesic (pain-relieving).  The prefix "equi-" comes from Latin "aequus" meaning "equal," and "analgesic" comes from Greek "an-" (without) + "algesis" (sense of pain).
