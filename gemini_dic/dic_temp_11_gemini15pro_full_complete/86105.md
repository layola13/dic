heartache:/ˈhɑːrteɪk/ (US) /ˈhɑːteɪk/ (UK)| n.| 心痛，悲痛|A feeling of sadness and emotional pain, typically caused by the loss of someone or something loved.|She suffered terrible heartache after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。|Example: The news of his betrayal caused her deep heartache.  他背叛的消息使她心痛不已。|Synonyms: grief, sorrow, anguish, agony|Antonyms: joy, happiness|Noun
