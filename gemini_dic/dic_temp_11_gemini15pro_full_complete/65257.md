etymon:/ˈɛtɪmɒn/ | n. |词源；词根| A word or morpheme from which a later word is derived. | The etymon of "psychology" is the Greek words "psyche" and "logia".  “心理学”的词源是希腊语“psyche”和“logia”。| synonym: root, origin, derivation | antonym: derivative, offspring | noun
