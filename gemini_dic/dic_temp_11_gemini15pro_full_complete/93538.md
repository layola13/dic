imaginariness:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɛrɪnɪs/
n.|想象力，虚构性|The quality of being imaginary; the state of being imagined or conceived rather than real.|The imaginariness of his fears became apparent when the monster didn't appear. 他恐惧的虚幻性在怪物没有出现时变得显而易见。|Synonyms: unreality, fiction, fantasy, illusion, dream, make-believe|Antonyms: reality, actuality, factuality|Noun
