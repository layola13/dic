| adj.|立方的，三次幂的|raised to the third power|The recipe calls for a cup of diced, cubed potatoes.  食谱需要一杯切丁的土豆块。|cubical, three-dimensional|planar|adjective

cubed:/kjuːbd/
| v.|使成立方体；求...的立方|past tense and past participle of cube|He cubed the number five. 他计算了5的立方。|calculate the cube of, raise to the third power|---|verb
