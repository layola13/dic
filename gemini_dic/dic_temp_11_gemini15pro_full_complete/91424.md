hyetographic:/ˌhaɪɛtəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 雨量分布图的；雨量记录的 | Relating to or depicting the distribution of rainfall. |  A hyetographic map shows the rainfall patterns of a region. 雨量分布图显示了一个地区的降雨模式。|  The hyetographic data revealed a significant increase in precipitation this year. 雨量记录数据显示今年降水量显著增加。| synonyms: pluviographic, ombrographic | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "arid" in a descriptive sense) | adjective
