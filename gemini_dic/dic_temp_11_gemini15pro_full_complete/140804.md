on-draft:/ˌɑːn ˈdræft/ | adj. | 随时可取用的；散装的；正在酝酿中的 | Available from a cask or keg rather than in bottles or cans; being formulated or developed; not yet in final form. |  The pub has a wide selection of beers on-draft. 这家酒吧有很多种散装啤酒。| 近义词：draught, on tap | 反义词：bottled, canned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "on" + "draft." "Draft" in this context originally referred to drawing liquid from a cask.
