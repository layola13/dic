defying:/dɪˈfaɪɪŋ/| adj.| 违抗的，蔑视的，挑战的 | resisting or disobeying something, especially a rule, law, or authority |  The protesters were defying the government's ban on public gatherings. 抗议者们无视政府禁止公众集会的禁令。

The climber defied death by scaling the treacherous peak.  登山者攀登险峻的山峰，蔑视死亡。 | synonyms: resisting, disobeying, challenging, opposing, flouting | antonyms: obeying, complying, submitting, yielding | Word Class: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desfier "renounce faith in, challenge," from des- "away" (from Latin dis-) + fier "to trust" (from Latin fidere).
