birthstones:/ˈbɜːrθˌstoʊnz/
n.
生日石，诞生石
A gemstone traditionally associated with the month of a person's birth.
She wears a ring with her birthstone, a ruby. 她戴着一枚镶着她生日石——红宝石的戒指。

近义词: birth gems
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "non-birthstone gemstones")
词性: noun
