capuchin:/ˈkæpjutʃɪn/ /ˈkæpjuːʃɪn/ | n. | капуцин (俄语), 卡布奇诺咖啡,  卷尾猴 |A brown or black hooded cloak, traditionally worn by Capuchin friars.  Also refers to a type of monkey with a cap-like tuft of hair and a coffee drink (cappuccino) named after the color of the friar's habit. | The Capuchin monkey is known for its intelligence.  卷尾猴以其聪明才智而闻名。 | synonyms: Capuchin monkey, cappuccino,  friar's habit | antonyms: N/A | noun

capucins:/ˈkæpjutʃɪnz/ /ˈkæpjuːʃɪnz/ | n. | 卡布奇诺咖啡（复数）, 卷尾猴（复数）| Plural form of "capuchin," referring to multiple Capuchin monkeys or multiple servings of cappuccino.  Less commonly, it could refer to multiple Capuchin cloaks. | The capucins swung through the trees.  卷尾猴在树间荡来荡去。 | synonyms: Capuchin monkeys, cappuccinos | antonyms: N/A | noun
