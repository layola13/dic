cascading:/kæˈskeɪdɪŋ/| adj.|倾泻的，层叠的|arranged or occurring in a series or in succession of stages so that each stage derives from or acts upon the product of the preceding.|The cascading waterfall was a breathtaking sight. 倾泻而下的瀑布景象令人叹为观止。|近义词：flowing, tumbling, descending, overflowing|反义词：ascending, rising|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From cascade (n.), from Italian *cascata*, from cascare "to fall,"  from Vulgar Latin *casicare, frequentative of Latin cadere "to fall."
