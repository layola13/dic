introspective:/ˌɪntrəˈspektɪv/ | adj. | 内省的，反省的 | Characterized by or given to introspection; examining one's own thoughts and feelings. |  She spent a quiet afternoon in introspective thought. 她用一个安静的下午进行内省的思考。 |  He became more introspective after the life-altering experience.  在改变人生的经历之后，他变得更加内省了。 | 近义词: reflective, contemplative, pensive, self-examining | 反义词: unreflective, extroverted, outgoing | 词性: adjective

introspection:/ˌɪntrəˈspekʃn/ | n. | 内省，反省 | The examination or observation of one's own mental and emotional processes. |  After much introspection, she decided to change careers. 经过多次反省之后，她决定换工作。 |  He practiced daily introspection as part of his spiritual journey. 他把每天的反省作为精神之旅的一部分。 | 近义词: self-examination, reflection, contemplation | 反义词: extrospection | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *introspectīvus, from introspicere "to look within," from intro- "within" + specere "to look."
