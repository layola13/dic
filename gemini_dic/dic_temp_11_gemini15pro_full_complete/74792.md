frejol:/freˈxol/| n. |菜豆，四季豆 (主要指拉丁美洲西班牙语国家)|A common term for beans in several Latin American Spanish-speaking countries, particularly Peru, Ecuador, and Bolivia.  It usually refers to the common bean (Phaseolus vulgaris).|例句：In Peru, frejoles are a staple food. 在秘鲁，菜豆是主食。

例句：She made a delicious stew with frejoles and rice. 她用菜豆和米饭做了一道美味的炖菜。|近义词：bean,  habichuela (Spanish), poroto (Spanish)|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
