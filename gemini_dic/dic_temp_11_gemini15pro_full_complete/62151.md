enclosed:/ɪnˈkləʊzd/ /ɛnˈkloʊzd/ | adj. | 被附上的；封闭的 |  Surrounded or closed off on all sides.  |

例句：
I have enclosed a copy of my resume with this letter.
我随信附上我的简历复印件。

例句：
The garden was enclosed by a high wall.
花园被高墙围了起来。

近义词：attached, included, confined, surrounded
反义词：open, exposed, unenclosed
词性：形容词
