ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Blende*, from *blenden* "to blind, deceive," from Middle High German *blenden*, from Old High German *blantan*.  The mineral was so called because it resembled galena (lead ore) but yielded no lead, thus appearing to "deceive" miners.
