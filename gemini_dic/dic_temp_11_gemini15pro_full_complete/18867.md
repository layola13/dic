besetment:/bɪˈsɛtmənt/| n. |困境，围攻，困扰|The state of being beset; a state of difficulty, trouble, or harassment.|The small company was facing besetment from all sides: dwindling sales, rising costs, and increasing competition. 这家小公司面临着来自各方的困境：销售额下降、成本上升以及竞争加剧。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, harassment, affliction, plague|反义词:peace, tranquility, calm, ease|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + set, meaning "to surround, to attack from all sides".  The suffix "-ment" forms a noun indicating the state or result of the verb's action.
