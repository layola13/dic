nonponderous:/ˌnɒnˈpɒndərəs/| adj.|轻巧的，不笨重的|Not ponderous; light or lively.|Her writing style is nonponderous and easy to read. 她的写作风格轻快易读。|The dancer's movements were nonponderous and graceful. 舞者的动作轻盈优雅。|近义词：light, nimble, agile, graceful, airy|反义词：ponderous, heavy, weighty, cumbersome, clumsy|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
ponderous (adjective): From Late Latin ponderosus "weighty," from Latin pondus (genitive ponderis) "weight," from pendere "to weigh, hang, cause to hang."
