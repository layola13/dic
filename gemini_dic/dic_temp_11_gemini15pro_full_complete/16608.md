barometries: /ˌbærəˈmɛtriz/
n.
气压测定法; 气压测量学
The study of barometric pressure and its variations, especially for weather forecasting.  It can also refer to the collection of barometric readings over a period of time.
The barometries of the region showed a significant drop in pressure, indicating an approaching storm. 该地区的气压测量显示气压显著下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。
Near synonyms:  barometry, aerometry
No direct antonym.
