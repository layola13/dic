moodier:/ˈmuːdiər/ | adj. | 心情更易变化的，更加喜怒无常的 | Comparative form of 'moody': More subject to unpredictable changes of mood, especially to periods of gloominess or sullenness. |  He's been much moodier lately, I wonder what's wrong.  他最近情绪更加反复无常，我想知道出了什么问题。| Example: The weather has been moodier this week, shifting from sunshine to rain several times a day.  这周的天气变化无常，一天之内阳光和雨水交替了好几次。| Synonyms: gloomier, sulkier, grumpier, more temperamental | Antonyms: cheerier, happier, more stable, more even-tempered | adjective
