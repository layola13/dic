lingism:/ˈlɪŋɡɪzəm/ | n. | 语言歧视；语言主义 | Discrimination based on language, especially against those who do not speak the dominant language. |  Many immigrants face lingism in the workplace, being passed over for promotions because of their accent. 许多移民在工作场所面临语言歧视，因为他们的口音而被晋升。 | 近义词: language discrimination, linguistic discrimination | 反义词: linguistic tolerance, language acceptance | 词性: noun

词源:  A blend of "linguistic" and "-ism," indicating a system of discrimination or prejudice. 由“linguistic”和“-ism”混合而成，表示歧视或偏见的体系。
