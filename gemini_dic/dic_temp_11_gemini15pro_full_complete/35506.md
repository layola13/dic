coadjuvancy: /ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːvənsi/
n.
辅助；协助；协作
The act or quality of assisting or helping; assistance.
The coadjuvancy of the vice president was essential to the president's success. 副总统的协助对总统的成功至关重要。

近义词: assistance, aid, help, support
反义词: hindrance, obstruction, opposition

coadjuvant: /ˌkoʊˈædʒʊvənt/
adj.
辅助的；协作的
Serving to assist or help; auxiliary.
The coadjuvant therapy helped boost the patient's immune system. 辅助疗法帮助增强了病人的免疫系统。
n.
助手；辅助物
A person or thing that assists or helps; an assistant.
The new medication acted as a coadjuvant to the existing treatment. 新药作为现有治疗的辅助药物。

近义词: auxiliary, supplementary, supporting
反义词: primary, main, principal


词源:
From Late Latin *coadjuvans, present participle of coadjuvare "to help," from Latin com- "with" + adjuvare "to help."
