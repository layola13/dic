congressionalists:/kənˈɡrɛʃənəlɪsts/
noun
国会议员；拥护国会的人
Members of Congress or those who strongly support the role and authority of Congress.
The congressionalists were locked in a fierce debate over the new healthcare bill. 国会议员们就新的医疗保健法案展开了激烈的辩论。
Synonyms:  congressmen, congresswomen, legislators, representatives
Antonyms:  executive branch officials, lobbyists (sometimes)
n.
