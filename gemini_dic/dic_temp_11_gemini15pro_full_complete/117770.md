melchite:/ˈmɛlkaɪt/| n. adj.|美尔基特教徒，美尔基特教会的|A member of a Christian community of the Byzantine rite, especially in the Near East, in communion with the Roman Catholic Church.  Also used as an adjective to describe things related to this church.|例句：The Melchite priest celebrated the Divine Liturgy. 美尔基特神父举行了神圣礼仪。|近义词：Catholic, Eastern Catholic|反义词：Protestant, Orthodox (in some contexts)|词性:名词, 形容词
melchite:/ˈmɛlkaɪt/| adj.|美尔基特教会的|Relating to the Melchite Church. |例句：The Melchite cathedral is a beautiful example of Byzantine architecture. 美尔基特大教堂是拜占庭建筑的一个美丽典范。|近义词：Byzantine (in some contexts)|反义词：Latin (in some contexts)|词性:形容词
