overargues:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuːz/
verb
过度争论，争论过久
to argue for too long or too forcefully
He overargued his case and lost the jury's sympathy. 他过度争论自己的案件，失去了陪审团的同情。
Synonyms: overstate, belabor, overemphasize
Antonyms: understate, downplay
词性: 动词
