disobeyers:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪərz/
n.|不服从者，违抗者|People who refuse or fail to obey rules or commands are disobeyers.|The disobeyers were punished for their actions. 违抗者因其行为受到了惩罚。|Synonyms: rebels, nonconformists, delinquents, insubordinates|Antonyms: conformists, followers, obedients|Noun

disobey:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/
v.|不服从，违抗|To refuse or fail to obey rules or commands is to disobey.|Children should not disobey their parents. 孩子们不应该不服从父母。|Synonyms: defy, resist, violate, infringe|Antonyms: obey, comply, conform, submit|Verb

disobedient:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiənt/
adj.|不服从的，违抗的|Not obeying rules or commands is disobedient.|The disobedient child was sent to his room. 不听话的孩子被送回了房间。|Synonyms: rebellious, unruly, defiant, wayward|Antonyms: obedient, compliant, submissive, docile|Adjective
