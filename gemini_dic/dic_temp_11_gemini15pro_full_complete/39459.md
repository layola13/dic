connex:/kəˈnɛks/| adj.| connected, joined; related | Having a connection or relationship; joined together. | The two rooms are connex, with a doorway between them. 这两个房间是相连的，中间有一个门口。| The chapters of the book are all connex, dealing with different aspects of the same theme. 书中的章节都是相互关联的，讨论同一主题的不同方面。| 近义词: connected, related, linked, joined | 反义词: disconnected, separate, unrelated | 词性:形容词

connex:/ˈkɒnɛks/| n.| a connected group or series | A group of things connected or related in some way. | The islands form a connex, each accessible from the others by boat. 这些岛屿形成了一个相互连接的群岛，每个岛屿都可以通过船只从其他岛屿到达。| The various social groups within the city form a complex connex. 城市内的各种社会群体形成了一个复杂的联系体。| 近义词: network, system, complex, web | 反义词: disjunction, separation | 词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *connexus, past participle of connectere "to connect," from com- "together" + nectere "to bind."
