internists:/ɪnˈtɜːrnɪsts/ | n. |内科医生|A physician who specializes in internal medicine.  A doctor who focuses on the diagnosis and non-surgical treatment of diseases in adults.|例句：My family doctor referred me to an internist for further evaluation. 我的家庭医生让我去看内科医生做进一步检查。

例句：The internists diagnosed the patient with pneumonia. 内科医生诊断该病人患有肺炎。|近义词：physician, doctor, general practitioner|反义词: surgeon, specialist (in a surgical field) |词性: Noun
