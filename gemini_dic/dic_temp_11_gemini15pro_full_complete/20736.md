blameworthy:/ˈbleɪmwɜːrði/
adjective
应受谴责的；该受责备的
Deserving blame; culpable.
He was found blameworthy for the accident. 他被认为应对这起事故负责。
近义词: culpable, guilty, reprehensible, censurable
反义词: blameless, innocent, commendable, praiseworthy
词性: adjective

词源:
From blame + -worthy.  "-worthy" is a suffix meaning "deserving of," from Old English "-wyrðe."
