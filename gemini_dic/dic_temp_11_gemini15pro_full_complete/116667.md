mattis:/ˈmætɪs/| n.|垫子;草席;褥子|(From Latin *mattea, from a non-Indo-European language, possibly Punic; compare Hebrew *mittah "bed") A coarse woven or plaited fabric used as a floor covering, especially in the tropics.  Originally referred to a kind of rush or reed used for weaving mats. Now can refer to any type of mat, including sleeping mats, floor mats, etc.|He slept on a mattis on the floor.|近义词:mat, rug, pad|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
