misstyled:/ˌmɪsˈstaɪld/
adjective
样式错误的，名称不当的
incorrectly or inappropriately styled or named
The book was misstyled "A History of the World." 这本书被错误地命名为“世界史”。
misnamed, wrongly designated, incorrectly labeled
correctly styled, aptly named
adjective
