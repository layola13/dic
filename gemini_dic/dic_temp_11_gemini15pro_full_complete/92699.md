icehouses:/ˈaɪsˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
冰窖，冰库
A building used to store ice, especially in the past, before refrigeration.
The ice harvested in winter was stored in icehouses for use throughout the year.  冬季采集的冰块储存在冰窖中，以备全年使用。
refrigerators, freezers, cold storages
warm places, ovens
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *īs-hūs (attested only as ísenhūs 'iron house'), from ís 'ice' + hūs 'house'.
