confiscators:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪtəz/ (US) /ˈkɒnfɪskeɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |充公者；没收者|People or entities that confiscate something, meaning they seize it, typically by authority.|The confiscators seized the illegal goods. 充公者没收了非法物品。|The government officials acted as confiscators of the smuggled artifacts. 政府官员充当了走私文物的没收者。|Synonyms: seizers, appropriators|Antonyms: restorers, returners|Noun

confiscate:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ (UK)| v. |没收；充公|To seize (something) by authority.|The police confiscated the drugs. 警察没收了毒品。|The government confiscated the rebel's assets. 政府没收了叛军的资产。|Synonyms: seize, impound, appropriate|Antonyms: return, restore|Verb
