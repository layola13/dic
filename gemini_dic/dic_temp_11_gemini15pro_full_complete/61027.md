elite:/ɪˈliːt/ /eɪˈliːt/
n.|精英；精华；中坚分子|A select group that is superior in terms of ability or qualities.|The elite of the armed forces were invited to the ceremony.  武装部队的精英被邀请参加了仪式。|近义词: best, finest, cream, pick, choice|反义词: dregs, masses, riffraff|词性:名词

elite:/ɪˈliːt/ /eɪˈliːt/
adj.|精英的；精锐的；高级的|Superior in terms of ability or qualities.|Elite athletes train for years to reach their level of performance. 精英运动员需要训练多年才能达到他们的竞技水平。|近义词: select, exclusive, privileged|反义词: ordinary, common, average|词性:形容词

词源: From French élite, from Old French eslite "chosen, selected," past participle of elire "to elect, choose," from Latin eligere "pick out, select," from ex- "out" + legere "to choose, gather, read."
