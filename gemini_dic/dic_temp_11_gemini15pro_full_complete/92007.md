hyperorganic:/ˌhaɪpərɔːrˈɡænɪk/
adjective
超有机的；极度有机的
Excessively or extremely organic; relating to or characterized by an exaggerated emphasis on organic principles or qualities.  Often used ironically or humorously to suggest something artificial masquerading as natural.
The marketing campaign for the new energy drink touted its hyperorganic ingredients, even though it was clearly full of artificial sweeteners.  这款新能量饮料的营销活动吹捧其超有机的成分，即使它显然充满了人工甜味剂。

The influencer's hyperorganic lifestyle, complete with a perfectly arranged vegetable garden and flawlessly rustic kitchen, felt strangely inauthentic.  这位网红超有机的的生活方式，配上一个排列完美的菜园和完美质朴的厨房，感觉异常不真实。

近义词:excessively organic, artificially natural (ironic)
反义词:inorganic, synthetic, artificial
词性:adjective
