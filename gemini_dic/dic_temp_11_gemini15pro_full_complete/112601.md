lovingnesses:/ˈlʌvɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
慈爱，深情，爱意
The state or quality of being loving; a display of love or affection.
Her lovingnesses towards her children were evident in every interaction. 她对孩子们的爱在每一次互动中都显而易见。
Grandmother showered her grandchildren with lovingnesses.  祖母对她的孙子孙女充满了爱意。
近义词:affection, tenderness, fondness, devotion
反义词: coldness, indifference, hostility
词性:名词

词源: From loving + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition).  来源于loving加上-ness（构成表示状态、性质或条件的名词的后缀）。
