highballing:/ˈhaɪˌbɔːlɪŋ/| n. vi.|快速行驶；豪饮；(美俚)加足马力干；|Traveling or drinking rapidly; (US slang) working at full speed. |例句：The train was highballing across the plains. 火车飞驰穿过平原。


例句：They were highballing through the project to meet the deadline.  他们为了赶截止日期，正在全力以赴地进行这个项目。


近义词：speeding, rushing, guzzling, chugging|反义词:crawling, dawdling, sipping|词性:名词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From "highball", a railroad signal for full speed ahead, later also a mixed alcoholic drink consumed quickly.  源于铁路术语“highball”，指全速前进的信号，后来也指快速饮用的混合酒精饮料。
