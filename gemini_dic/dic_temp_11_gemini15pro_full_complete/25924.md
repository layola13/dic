burnishable:/ˈbɜːrnɪʃəbl/| adj.|可抛光的，可磨光的|Able to be polished by rubbing, especially with a burnisher.  This creates a smooth, shiny surface. |The old silver tray was tarnished but still burnishable.  这旧银盘虽然失去光泽，但仍然可以抛光。| The wood is burnishable to a high gloss. 这种木材可以抛光到很高的光泽度。|近义词：polishable, buffable|反义词:unpolishable, dull, matt|词性:形容词
词源: From burnish + -able. Ultimately from French brunir, "to make brown," from brun, "brown."
