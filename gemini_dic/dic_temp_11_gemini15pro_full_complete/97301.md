infomania:/ˌɪnfəˈmeɪniə/ | n. |信息癖；信息强迫症 | An excessive enthusiasm for acquiring and consuming information, especially news and current affairs, often through electronic devices.  A compulsive desire to check and share information, often to the detriment of other activities. | He suffers from infomania and constantly checks his phone for news updates. 他患有信息癖，不停地查看手机上的新闻更新。|  information overload, news addiction | information apathy, indifference to information | noun
