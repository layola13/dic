ephyra:/ˈɛfɪrə/ | n. |水螅，幼体|A small, free-swimming coelenterate larva, typically having a cylindrical or ovoid body and a ring of cilia at one end, characteristic of cnidarians. It is the free-swimming stage of a hydrozoan or scyphozoan. | The ephyra drifts in the ocean currents.  水螅在洋流中漂流。| The tiny ephyra eventually develops into a medusa.  微小的水螅最终会发育成水母。 | larva, polyp |imago| noun
