bunodonts:/ˈbjuːnəˌdɑːnts/ | n. |丘齿兽；低冠齿兽| Animals possessing bunodont teeth, characterized by low, rounded cusps on the molars, suitable for crushing and grinding.  These teeth are adapted for an omnivorous or herbivorous diet. | Example: Bunodonts like pigs are able to process a variety of food sources.  丘齿兽，例如猪，能够处理各种食物来源。

Example:  Ancient bunodonts occupied a diverse range of ecological niches. 古代丘齿兽占据了各种各样的生态位。 | 近义词:  omnivores, herbivores (depending on the specific bunodont)| 反义词:  carnivores, selenodonts, hypsodonts | 词性: noun
