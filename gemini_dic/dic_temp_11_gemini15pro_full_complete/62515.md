endpoint:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ | n. | 终点，端点 | A point marking the limit of something; the point at which a line, process, or activity terminates. | The endpoint of the trial is to determine the efficacy of the drug.  这个试验的终点是确定药物的疗效。| synonym: terminus, termination, conclusion | antonym: beginning, start, origin | noun

endpoint:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ | n. |（计算机网络）端点 | In computer networking, a communication endpoint or endpoint is a node or logical entity that serves as the source or destination of a communication flow.  |  The server acts as an endpoint for client connections. 服务器充当客户端连接的端点。 | synonym: node, terminal | antonym: hub, center | noun
