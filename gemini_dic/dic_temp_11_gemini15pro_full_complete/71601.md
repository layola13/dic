flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ (UK) /ˈflætˌpæk/ (US)| adj. n.|扁平包装的，扁平包装的家具|Furniture or other objects that are sold unassembled in flat boxes for easy transportation and storage, requiring the buyer to assemble them at home.|We bought a flat-pack wardrobe from IKEA.|Synonyms:  knock-down, ready-to-assemble, self-assembly|Antonyms: assembled, pre-built|adjective/noun

flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ (UK) /ˈflætˌpæk/ (US)| v.|扁平包装|To package something in a flat pack.|They flat-packed the furniture for shipping.|Synonyms: disassemble, knock down|Antonyms: assemble, build|verb
