metallography:/məˈtæləɡrəfi/
n.|金相学|The study of the structure of metals and alloys, typically using microscopic examination.|Metallography revealed the presence of micro-cracks in the weld. 金相分析揭示了焊缝中存在微裂纹。|近义词:materialography|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From meta- (beyond, after, with, across) + -graphy (writing, recording, description).  Ultimately from Greek metallon (metal) + -graphia (writing).
