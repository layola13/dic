lapilli:/ləˈpɪlaɪ/ /ləˈpɪli/ | n. |火山砾，火山豆|Small fragments of lava or rock ejected during a volcanic eruption, typically ranging in size from 2 to 64 millimeters in diameter. |The eruption showered the surrounding area with lapilli and ash. 火山爆发后，周围地区落满了火山砾和火山灰。

The geologists analyzed the lapilli to determine the nature of the eruption. 地质学家分析了火山砾，以确定喷发的性质。

|近义词：tephra, volcanic fragments|反义词:|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lapillus*, diminutive of *lapis* ("stone").
