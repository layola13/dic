half-questioning:/ˌhɑːfˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/
| adj. | 半信半疑的，略带疑问的 | Expressing slight doubt or uncertainty; questioning something tentatively. |  She gave him a half-questioning look. 她半信半疑地看了他一眼。| 近义词：doubtful, skeptical, uncertain, hesitant | 反义词：certain, confident, sure | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
half- (prefix) + questioning (present participle of question)
"half-" means partly or incompletely. "questioning" refers to the act of expressing doubt or uncertainty.
