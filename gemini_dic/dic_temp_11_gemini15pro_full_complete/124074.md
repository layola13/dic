morphs:/mɔːrfs/ | n. | 词素 | A morph is the smallest unit of meaning in a language.  It can be a word, like "cat," or part of a word, like the "un-" in "unhappy." |  The word "unbreakable" contains three morphs: "un-", "break", and "-able".  这个单词“unbreakable”包含三个词素：“un-”，“break”和“-able”。 | synonyms: morpheme | antonyms: N/A | noun
