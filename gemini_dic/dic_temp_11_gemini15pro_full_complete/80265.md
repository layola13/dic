goggliest:/ˈɡɒɡliɪst/ | adj. |  瞪大眼睛的，眼珠凸出的 |  Having or showing large, staring eyes, often in a way that suggests surprise, fear, or excitement.  Derived from "goggle" which means to stare with wide eyes.  The "-iest" suffix indicates the superlative form, meaning "most goggly." |  The child looked at the magician with the goggliest eyes, completely mesmerized by the disappearing act.  这个孩子瞪大眼睛看着魔术师，完全被消失的表演迷住了。| synonyms: wide-eyed, staring, pop-eyed | antonyms: uninterested, bored | adjective
