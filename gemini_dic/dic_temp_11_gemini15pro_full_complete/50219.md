depatriate:/diːˈpeɪtriˌeɪt/ /diːˈpætrieɪt/ | v.  n. |遣返；移居国外者|To remove (someone) from their native country; to leave one's native country to live elsewhere.|The company is repatriating all its overseas staff. 该公司正在遣返所有海外员工。|
depatriate:/diːˈpeɪtriˌeɪt/ /diːˈpætrieɪt/ | n. |移居国外者；被遣返者|A person who has left their native country to live elsewhere.|Many expatriates return home after a few years abroad. 许多移居国外者在国外待了几年后回国。|
depatriate:/diːˈpeɪtriˌeɪt/ /diːˈpætrieɪt/ | v. |遣返|To send or bring someone back to their own country.|The government repatriated the refugees. 政府遣返了难民。|


近义词:expatriate, exile, banish, deport|
反义词:repatriate, immigrate|
词性:verb, noun|
