keeldragged:/ˈkiːldræɡd/
verb
拖着龙骨；使受尽屈辱；严厉惩罚
to punish or criticize someone severely, especially in public
The CEO was keeldragged by the media after the scandal broke.  首席执行官在丑闻曝光后遭到了媒体的严厉批评。
criticize, castigate, reprimand, censure
praise, commend, laud
v.
