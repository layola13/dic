aeon:/ˈiːən/ (US) /ˈiːɒn/ (UK)| n.|亿万年，极漫长的时期，时代|An aeon is an indefinitely long period of time, often a period of a million years.|例句：It took aeons for the canyon to be carved by the river. 这条峡谷被河流雕琢而成花费了亿万年。

例句：The dinosaurs lived in a past aeon. 恐龙生活在过去的时代。|近义词：era, epoch, age|反义词:instant, moment|词性:名词
