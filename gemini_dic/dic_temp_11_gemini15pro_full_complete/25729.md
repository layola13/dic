bunyips:/ˈbʌnjɪps/ | n. | 澳洲水怪，邦尼普 | A mythical Australian creature said to inhabit swamps and billabongs.  Often described as having a large body, a dog-like face, dark fur, a horse-like tail, and flippers. |  例句：Stories about bunyips have been told by Aboriginal Australians for generations.  澳大利亚原住民世代相传关于邦尼普的故事。

例句：Many people search for evidence of bunyips, but none has ever been found. 许多人寻找邦尼普存在的证据，但从未找到过。 | 近义词：cryptid, monster | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a mythical creature) | 词性: noun
