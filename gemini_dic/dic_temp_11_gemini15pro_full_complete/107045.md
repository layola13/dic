lactoscope:/ˈlæktəskoʊp/| n. |乳汁计，乳浓计|An instrument for measuring the richness of milk, especially by determining the opacity.  It assesses milk quality based on its light-transmitting properties.|The farmer used a lactoscope to check the quality of the cow's milk.|近义词: lactometer|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps instruments measuring other properties of milk) |词性: noun
