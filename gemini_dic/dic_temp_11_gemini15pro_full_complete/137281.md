nonsynchronically:/ˌnɒnˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪkli/| adv.|非同步地，不同步地|Not occurring at the same time; asynchronously.  It refers to events or processes that do not happen together or at the same rate.|The two systems updated nonsynchronically, leading to data inconsistencies.|近义词: asynchronously, out of sync, independently|反义词: synchronously, concurrently, simultaneously|词性:副词
词源: non- (not) + synchronically (adverb form of synchronous, from Greek syn- (together) + chronos (time))
