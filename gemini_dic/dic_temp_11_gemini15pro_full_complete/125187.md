multicavous: /ˌmʌltɪˈkeɪvəs/
adjective
多洞的，多孔的
Having many cavities or hollows.
The multicavous rock provided a home for numerous small creatures.  这块多孔的岩石为许多小生物提供了家园。
The speleologist explored the multicavous cave system. 洞穴学家探索了这个多洞穴的洞穴系统。

近义词: porous, cavernous, honeycombed
反义词: solid, dense, compact
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *multicavus, from multi- 'many' + cavus 'hollow'.
