goonda:/ˈɡuːndə/ (UK), /ˈɡuːndɑː/ (US)| n.|暴徒，恶棍，打手|A hired thug or bully, often involved in criminal activities.  Derived from Hindi गुण्डा (guṇḍā), meaning "scoundrel" or "ruffian."|The local goondas were extorting money from shopkeepers. 当地的暴徒向店主勒索钱财。|Synonyms: thug, hooligan, hoodlum, gangster, ruffian|Antonyms: law-enforcer, police officer|noun
