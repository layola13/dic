hippophagy: /ˌhɪpəˈfɒdʒi/ | n. | 食马肉 | The practice of eating horse meat. | 例句：Hippophagy is common in some cultures but taboo in others.  在某些文化中食用马肉很普遍，但在其他文化中却是禁忌。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ἱπποφάγος (hippopʰágos, “horse-eating”), from ἵππος (híppos, “horse”) + φαγεῖν (phageîn, “to eat”).
