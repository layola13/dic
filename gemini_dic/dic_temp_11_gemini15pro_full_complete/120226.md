minette:/mɪˈnɛt/ | n. | 细粒碱性岩, 类似于霞石岩 | A dark, fine-grained igneous rock similar to syenite, typically containing biotite and orthoclase feldspar. | This minette dike cuts through the older limestone formation. 这条细粒碱性岩脉穿过了较老的石灰岩地层。| The quarry yields a rare type of minette rich in potassium.  这个采石场出产一种富含钾的稀有细粒碱性岩。 | 近义词: syenite, lamprophyre | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps granite as a contrasting igneous rock) | 词性: noun

minette:/miˈnɛt/ | n. | (旧) 黑猫 | (Archaic) A black cat. | The old woman's minette purred contentedly by the fire. 老妇人的黑猫在火炉边满足地咕噜着。|  Legends say a witch's minette is her familiar. 传说女巫的黑猫是她的魔宠。| 近义词: black cat | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
