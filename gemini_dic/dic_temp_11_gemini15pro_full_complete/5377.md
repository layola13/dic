alluviation:/əˌluːviˈeɪʃən/
n.
冲积; 冲积层; 冲积作用
the process of depositing alluvium; the alluvium deposited
例句：The alluviation of the Nile delta has created fertile farmland for centuries. 尼罗河三角洲的冲积作用几个世纪以来创造了肥沃的农田。

例句：The archaeologist studied the layers of alluviation to understand the history of the region. 考古学家研究了冲积层，以了解该地区的历史。
近义词：deposition, sedimentation, accretion
反义词：erosion
词性：noun
