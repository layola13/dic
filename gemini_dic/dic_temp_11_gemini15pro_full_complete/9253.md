antipatriarch:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriɑːrk/ | adj. n. | 反父权制的，反父权主义者 | Opposed to or rejecting patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power. |  She identifies as an antipatriarchal feminist. 她自认为是一个反父权的女权主义者。 | The group staged an antipatriarchal protest.  这个团体组织了一场反父权抗议。 | 近义词: feminist, egalitarian | 反义词: patriarchal, sexist | 词性: 形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + patriarch (male head of a family or tribe).  The prefix "anti-" is Greek in origin, meaning "against" or "opposite." "Patriarch" comes from the Greek "patriarkhēs," meaning "father of a family" or "ruler of a family."
