misunderstand:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/| v. |误解，误会|To interpret (something, such as a person's words or actions) incorrectly.|She completely misunderstood my intentions. 她完全误解了我的意图。|近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue|反义词：understand, comprehend|词性:动词
