expediential:/ˌɛkspɪˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|经验的，体验的，基于经验的| Relating to or based on experience; providing experience.  It emphasizes learning through doing and direct observation, rather than just theoretical study.|例句：

Expediential learning is more engaging than traditional lectures.
体验式学习比传统的讲座更引人入胜。

The program offers students an experiential understanding of the challenges faced by refugees.
该项目让学生体验式地理解难民面临的挑战。

|近义词：empirical, practical, hands-on, first-hand|反义词:theoretical, academic, abstract|词性:adjective
