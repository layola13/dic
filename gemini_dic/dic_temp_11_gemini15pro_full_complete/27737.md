caniniform:/ˈkeɪnaɪnɪfɔːrm/ /ˈkænɪnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|犬形的，似犬的|Resembling or characteristic of a dog, especially in having a pointed muzzle.|The animal had a distinctly caniniform skull.  这种动物有着明显的犬形头骨。|The fossil revealed a caniniform jaw structure. 这块化石展现了一个犬形的颌骨结构。|近义词：dog-like, canine, lupoid|反义词:feline|词性：形容词
