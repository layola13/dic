nudicaul:/njuːdɪˈkɔːl/| adj.|裸茎的，无茎叶的|Having the stem apparently without leaves, or with leaves much reduced in size.  A plant with its flowers appearing to rise directly from the ground or crown of the root.|The botanist identified several nudicaul species in the field.  植物学家在田野里识别出几种裸茎植物。|近义词:acaulescent, stemless|反义词: leafy, caulescent|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *nudicaulis*, from *nudus* "naked" + *caulis* "stem".
