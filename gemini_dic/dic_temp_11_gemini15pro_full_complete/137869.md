nonvirtuousness:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːrtʃuːəsnəs/
n.
lack of virtue; wickedness; immorality
The nonvirtuousness of the dictator was evident in his cruel policies.  这位独裁者的邪恶体现在他残酷的政策中。
The play explored the themes of virtue and nonvirtuousness.  这出戏探讨了美德与邪恶的主题。
Synonyms: wickedness, immorality, depravity, vice, iniquity
Antonyms: virtue, righteousness, morality, goodness
noun
