cheraki: /tʃɛˈrɑːki/ | n. | 水烟管；水烟 | A type of water pipe used for smoking, especially in the Middle East.  A long-necked water pipe used to smoke flavored tobacco, also known as a hookah or narghile. | Example:  He relaxed in the cafe, smoking a cheraki. 他在咖啡馆里放松，抽着水烟。

Example: The sweet smell of the cheraki filled the air. 水烟的甜味弥漫在空气中。 | Near synonyms: hookah, narghile, water pipe | Antonym: None (no direct opposite for a specific smoking device) | Noun
