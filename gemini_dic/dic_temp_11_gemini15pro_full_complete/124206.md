mosher:/ˈmɒʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈmɑːʃər/ (UK)| n.| 猛撞的人；激烈跳舞的人；疯狂的粉丝|A person who dances energetically or wildly in a mosh pit, often bumping into other dancers.|例句：The moshers went wild when the band started playing. 乐队开始演奏时，疯狂的粉丝们变得狂野起来。

例句：He’s a dedicated mosher and goes to every metal concert he can. 他是一个忠实的疯狂粉丝，只要他能去，每场金属音乐会他都会参加。|近义词：fan, enthusiast, headbanger|反义词:wallflower|词性:noun
