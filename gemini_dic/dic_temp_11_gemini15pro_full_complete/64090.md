epone:/ˈɛpəʊn/ | n. |  叙事诗 | A long narrative poem, often heroic and dealing with a serious subject. |  The Iliad is a famous example of an epone. 伊利亚特是一部著名的叙事诗。 | 近义词: epic, saga | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly short story, lyric) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *épopée*, from Latin *epopoeia*, from Ancient Greek *ἐποποιία* (epopoiía, “epic poetry”), from *ἔπος* (épos, “word, story”) + *ποιέω* (poiéō, “I make, I do”).
