embezzler:/ɪmˈbɛzələr/
|n.|盗用者，挪用公款者|A person who illegally takes money that they have been entrusted with. |The embezzler was caught and sentenced to prison. 盗用者被抓获并被判处监禁。|
|近义词: thief, defrauder, swindler|
|反义词: donor, philanthropist|
|词性: noun|


词源:
From Old French *embesler "to destroy," from en- "in" + besler "to sweep away.”  The meaning shifted over time from "make away with" to the specific sense of misappropriating funds.
