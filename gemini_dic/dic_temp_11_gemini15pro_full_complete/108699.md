leam:/liːm/ | n. | 粘土，泥土；泥浆 | A type of clay or other earth material that is sticky and plastic when wet, used for making pottery, bricks, etc. | The potter shaped the leam into a beautiful vase. 陶工把粘土塑造成一个漂亮的花瓶。| synonyms: clay, loam, mud | antonyms: sand, gravel | noun

leam:/liːm/ | v. | 闪光；闪烁；发出微光 | To emit a faint, unsteady light; to gleam. | A single candle leamed in the darkness. 一支孤零零的蜡烛在黑暗中闪烁着。| synonyms: gleam, glimmer, shimmer | antonyms: darken, extinguish | verb
