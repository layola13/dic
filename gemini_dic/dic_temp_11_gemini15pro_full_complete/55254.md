dissimulative:/dɪˈsɪmjʊlətɪv/
adjective
掩饰的，假装的
concealing or disguising one's true thoughts, feelings, or character.
He had a dissimulative nature, never revealing his true intentions. 他生性掩饰，从不透露自己的真实意图。
The spy maintained a dissimulative exterior, blending seamlessly into the crowd.  这个间谍保持着伪装的外表，完美地融入了人群。

近义词: deceptive, deceitful, mendacious, artful, cunning, sly, furtive, secretive
反义词: honest, frank, candid, open, forthright, ingenuous, artless

词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin dissimulatus, past participle of dissimulare “to disguise, conceal,” from dis- “not” + simulare “to simulate, copy, resemble.”
