heterothermic:/ˌhɛtərəˈθɜːrmɪk/ | adj. |变温的，异温的 | Having a body temperature that varies with the environment, intermediate between poikilothermic and homeothermic. | Example: Bats are heterothermic animals, meaning their body temperature can fluctuate depending on the surrounding environment.  蝙蝠是异温动物，这意味着它们的体温会根据周围环境而波动。 | Example:  Many desert animals exhibit heterothermic characteristics to cope with extreme temperatures. 许多沙漠动物表现出异温特性以应对极端温度。 | 近义词: poikilothermic, ectothermic | 反义词: homeothermic, endothermic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hetero- (different, other) + -thermic (relating to heat).  Hetero- comes from Greek heteros "other, different," and -thermic comes from Greek thermē "heat."
