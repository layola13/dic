flatiron:/ˈflætˌaɪərn/| n. |熨斗；三角形的建筑物|A flatiron is a clothes iron with a flat, usually pointed base.  It also refers to a building, typically triangular or wedge-shaped, situated on a narrow block formed by the intersection of two streets.|She carefully pressed the seams with a hot flatiron. 她小心地用热熨斗熨烫接缝。|The iconic Flatiron Building is a quintessential example of early skyscraper architecture.  标志性的熨斗大厦是早期摩天大楼建筑的典型例子。|近义词：(for the appliance) iron; (for the building) wedge-shaped building, triangular building|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flat + iron.  The term for the building shape is derived from its resemblance to the household appliance.
