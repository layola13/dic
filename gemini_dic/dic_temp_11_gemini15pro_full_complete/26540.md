cabined:/ˈkæbɪnd/
adjective
被限制的，受局限的; 狭窄的
Confined, restricted, or cramped.
The writer felt cabined by the strict rules of the sonnet form.  这位作家感到被十四行诗的严格规则所限制。
He lived a cabined life, rarely leaving his small apartment.  他过着一种受局限的生活，很少离开他的小公寓。
Synonyms: confined, restricted, cramped, limited, constricted
Antonyms: free, liberated, unrestricted, spacious, expansive
词性: adjective


cabined:/ˈkæbɪnd/
verb (past participle of cabin)
住在小屋里;  把…关在小屋里
To confine or restrict to a cabin.
Feeling cabined by their small apartment, they decided to move to a house. 感到被他们的小公寓限制住了，他们决定搬到一栋房子里去。
The sailors were cabined below deck during the storm.  在暴风雨期间，水手们被关在甲板下面。
Synonyms: confined, restricted, imprisoned, secluded
Antonyms: liberated, freed, released
词性: verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From cabin + -ed.  "Cabin" ultimately comes from the Old French word "cabine," meaning "hut" or "small room."
