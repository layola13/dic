dinosaurian:/ˌdaɪnəˈsɔːriən/| adj. n.|恐龙的，恐龙;|Of or relating to the dinosaurs.  Pertaining to or characteristic of dinosaurs or the Dinosauria. Also, a dinosaur.|The museum's dinosaurian exhibit was very popular.  博物馆的恐龙展览非常受欢迎。|The Tyrannosaurus Rex is a well-known dinosaurian. 霸王龙是一种著名的恐龙。|近义词：dinosaur-like, reptilian|反义词:mammalian|词性:adjective, noun


dinosaurian:/ˌdaɪnəˈsɔːriən/| n.|恐龙;|A dinosaur.|Many dinosaurians roamed the earth millions of years ago.  数百万年前，许多恐龙在地球上漫游。|The fossilized remains of a dinosaurian were discovered in the valley. 在山谷中发现了恐龙的化石遗骸。|近义词：dinosaur|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider modern animals like mammals or birds)|词性:noun
