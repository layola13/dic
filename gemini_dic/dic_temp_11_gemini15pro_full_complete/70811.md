fingle:/ˈfɪŋɡəl/| v. n.|拨弄；摸索；小题大做；虚构的故事|To touch or manipulate something nervously or idly with the fingers; a trivial or invented story.|He finkled with the loose threads on his jacket. 他紧张地拨弄着夹克衫上的线头。|近义词：fiddle, fidget, twiddle, tamper, fabricate|反义词：leave alone, ignore|词性：动词，名词

fingle:/ˈfɪŋɡəl/| n.|虚构的故事；琐碎的事|A trivial or invented story; a small, insignificant thing.|He told us a fingle about losing his wallet. 他编了个丢钱包的故事。|近义词：fib, fabrication, trifle|反义词：truth, fact|词性：名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "finger,"  suggesting the action of using one's fingers nervously or idly.  Its meaning as "a trivial or invented story" likely developed from the sense of "to fabricate" or "to make up."
