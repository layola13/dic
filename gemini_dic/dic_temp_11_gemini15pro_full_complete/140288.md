oftentimes:/ˈɔːfənˌtaɪmz/ (US), /ˈɒfənˌtaɪmz/ (UK)| adv.|经常，常常|Oftentimes is an adverb that means frequently or many times. It's a more formal and less common way of saying "often."|Oftentimes, she would walk to work to save money. 她经常步行上班以省钱。|She oftentimes forgets her keys. 她常常忘记带钥匙。|近义词：often, frequently, regularly|反义词:rarely, seldom, infrequently|词性:副词
