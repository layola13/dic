luggaged:/ˈlʌɡɪdʒd/
adjective
有行李的，携带行李的
carrying or encumbered with luggage.
She arrived at the airport, luggaged and ready for her trip. 她带着行李到达了机场，准备开始她的旅行。
The travelers, luggaged and tired, finally reached their destination.  旅客们带着行李，疲惫不堪，终于到达了目的地。
近义词:burdened, laden
反义词:unburdened, unladen
词性:adjective

词源: From luggage + -ed.  "Luggage" itself comes from the verb "lug" meaning "to carry with difficulty," ultimately from a Scandinavian source.
