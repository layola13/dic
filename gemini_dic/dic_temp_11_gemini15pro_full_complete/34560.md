clear-the-air:/ˌklɪr ði ˈɛr/
verb phrase
消除误会；改善关系；坦诚交谈
To remove suspicion or bad feeling between people by talking openly about a problem.
Example: We had a big argument, but then we sat down and cleared the air. 我们大吵了一架，但后来我们坐下来坦诚相待，消除了误会。

Example:  It's time to clear the air between us and start over. 是时候消除我们之间的隔阂，重新开始了。

Synonym:  talk things out, have it out, resolve a misunderstanding, make peace, reconcile, patch things up
Antonym:  worsen relations, escalate conflict, avoid confrontation, bottle things up
