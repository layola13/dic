afrikanerdoms: /ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnərˌdɒmz/ | n. | 南非白人文化（尤其指布尔人文化）| The ideology or cultural values of Afrikaners, especially emphasizing their language, history, and perceived right to self-determination. |  The museum exhibit explored the complex history of Afrikanerdoms in South Africa.  博物馆的展览探索了南非白人文化（尤其指布尔人文化）的复杂历史。| synonyms: Afrikaner nationalism, Afrikaner culture | antonyms: N/A | noun


Etymology:  From Afrikaans *Afrikanerdome*, plural of *Afrikanerdom*.  *Afrikaner* refers to a descendant of Dutch settlers in South Africa, and *-dom* is a suffix denoting state, condition, or domain (cognate with English *-dom* as in kingdom).
