inculcation:/ɪnˌkʌlˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|灌输；谆谆教诲|The inculcation of moral values is the duty of parents and teachers. 对道德价值观的灌输是父母和老师的责任。|例句：The constant inculcation of company values helps create a unified work environment. 不断灌输公司价值观有助于创造统一的工作环境。

例句：Through careful inculcation, he instilled a love of learning in his students. 通过细心的教导，他向学生灌输了对学习的热爱。


近义词：instillation, indoctrination, implantation, infusion|反义词:discouragement, dissuasion|词性:名词
