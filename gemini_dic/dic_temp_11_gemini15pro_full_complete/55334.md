dissuasive:/dɪˈsweɪsɪv/
adjective
劝阻的，阻止的
tending to dissuade; deterring
例句：The high price of the tickets was dissuasive.  票价很高，令人却步。

例句：The government launched a dissuasive campaign against smoking. 政府发起了一场劝阻吸烟的运动。

近义词：deterrent, discouraging, off-putting
反义词：encouraging, persuasive, inviting
词性：adjective
