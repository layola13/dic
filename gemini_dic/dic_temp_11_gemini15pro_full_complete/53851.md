discontentednesses:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
不满，不满足
The state of being discontented; lack of contentment; dissatisfaction.
The discontentednesses of the workers led to a strike. 工人们的不满导致了罢工。
Synonyms: discontents, dissatisfaction, unhappiness, malcontent, disaffection
Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, happiness, pleasure

n.

discontented:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd/
adj.
不满的，不满足的
Not content; dissatisfied; uneasy.
He felt discontented with his current job.  他对目前的工作感到不满。
Synonyms: dissatisfied, unhappy, restless, malcontent
Antonyms: content, satisfied, happy, pleased
Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + content (satisfied) + -ed (forming adjectives) + -ness (forming nouns).  Ultimately from Latin contentus (content).
