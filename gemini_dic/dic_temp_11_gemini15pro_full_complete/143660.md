outlabored:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbərd/
verb
过度劳累的，因过度劳动而筋疲力尽的
Worked harder and longer than.  Exhausted or worn out from excessive work.
The team was outlabored and outmatched in the final game.  该队在决赛中过度劳累，实力不济。
overworked, exhausted, spent
rested, refreshed
