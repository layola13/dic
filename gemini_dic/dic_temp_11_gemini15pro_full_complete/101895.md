ish:/ɪʃ/ | adj. | 大约, 有点儿, 像…一样 |  A suffix added to adjectives to form new adjectives that indicate that something is approximately or somewhat like the original adjective.  It can also mean "having the characteristics of" a particular nationality or group. | The water is bluish. 水有点蓝。| 近义词: -ly (e.g., reddish, slightly), kinda, sorta | 反义词: pure, completely | 词性: 后缀

ish:/ɪʃ/ | adj. | （用于年龄）…岁左右 | Used after a number to indicate approximate age. |  She's fortyish. 她四十岁左右。| 近义词: approximately, about, around | 反义词: exactly | 词性: 后缀
