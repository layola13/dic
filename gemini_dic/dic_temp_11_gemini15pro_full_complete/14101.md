auteurs:/oʊˈtɜːrz/| n. |作者（尤指电影导演）|A filmmaker who has a personal and recognizable style and exerts considerable control over their films.  A term borrowed from French. |例句：The festival celebrates the work of great auteurs.  这个电影节庆祝伟大导演的作品。

例句：Many consider Quentin Tarantino a modern auteur. 很多人认为昆汀·塔伦蒂诺是一位现代导演。 |近义词：filmmaker, director, artist |反义词: N/A |词性: Noun
