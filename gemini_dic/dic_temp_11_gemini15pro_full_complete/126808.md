nap's:/næps/| n.| 小睡，打盹 |It's the possessive form of "nap," meaning "belonging to a nap" or, more commonly, used as a contraction of "nap is."  It generally refers to a short period of sleep, typically taken during the day. | The nap's over.  小睡时间结束了。| synonyms:  rest, siesta, doze | antonyms: wakefulness, activity | noun
