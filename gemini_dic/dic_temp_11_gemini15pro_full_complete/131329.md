nonchaotic:/ˌnɑːnkeɪˈɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 非混沌的，有序的 | Not chaotic; exhibiting order and predictability. | The system, despite its complexity, remained remarkably nonchaotic.  这个系统尽管很复杂，但仍然保持着非凡的有序性。

The data analysis revealed a surprisingly nonchaotic pattern. 数据分析揭示了一个令人惊讶的有序模式。| 近义词：orderly, organized, predictable, stable, regular | 反义词：chaotic, disordered, unpredictable, unstable, irregular | 词性：形容词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + chaotic (from Greek khaos, meaning "void, vast chasm")
