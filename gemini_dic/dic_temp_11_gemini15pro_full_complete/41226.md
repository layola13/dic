coralligenous:/ˌkɒrəˈlɪdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 珊瑚的，产珊瑚的 | Relating to or consisting of coralligenous habitats, which are biogenic reefs built by algae and other organisms, often found in the Mediterranean Sea.  |  The coralligenous reefs are a biodiversity hotspot.  珊瑚礁是生物多样性的热点地区。 |  coral, reef-building | barren | adjective

coralligena:/kɒrəlˈɪdʒɪnə/ | n. | 珊瑚藻群落 | A hard substrate in the Mediterranean Sea and nearby Atlantic Ocean formed by calcareous algae on which diverse sessile organisms live. It's similar to a coral reef, but built by algae rather than corals. | Divers explored the vibrant coralligena, discovering numerous species of fish and invertebrates. 潜水员探索了充满活力的珊瑚藻群落，发现了许多种类的鱼类和无脊椎动物。 | reef, biogenic habitat | open water | noun

词源: From Italian *coralligeno*, from *corallo* ("coral") + *-geno* ("producing"). Ultimately from Latin *coralium* ("coral").
