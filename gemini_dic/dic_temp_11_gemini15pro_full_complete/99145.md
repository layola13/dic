intercessorial:/ˌɪntərˈsɛsərɪəl/ | adj. |  代祷的，说情的 | Relating to or characterized by intercession; acting as an intercessor. | The priest offered intercessorial prayers for the sick. 神父为病人做了代祷。|  The nun's intercessorial role brought comfort to the grieving family. 修女的说情角色给悲痛的家庭带来了安慰。 | 近义词: mediating, pleading, intervening | 反义词: accusing, condemning | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intercessorius, from Latin intercessor + -orius.  Intercessor is from intercedere "to intervene, go between," from inter- "between" + cedere "to go."
