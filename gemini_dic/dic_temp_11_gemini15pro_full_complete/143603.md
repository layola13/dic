outings:/ˈaʊtɪŋz/
noun
郊游，外出；短途旅行
A short trip or excursion, especially one taken for pleasure.
We enjoyed several outings to the beach during our vacation.  我们在假期期间几次到海滩游玩。

近义词: excursions, trips, expeditions, journeys
反义词: homecomings, returns

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From the verb "out," meaning "to go out," + "-ing," forming a noun.  源自动词“out”（意为“外出”）加上“-ing”构成名词。
