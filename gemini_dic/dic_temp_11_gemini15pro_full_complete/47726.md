decadist:/ˈdɛkədɪst/| n.|十年主义者；提倡十年为一个周期的改革者|A person who advocates a period of ten years as a cycle of reform or change.|The decadist believed that societal change should be implemented gradually over a decade.  这位十年主义者认为，社会变革应该在十年内逐步实施。|近义词：reformer, progressive|反义词：conservative, reactionary|词性:noun
