cancel:/ˈkænsəl/ (US) /ˈkænsl/ (UK)| v. n.|取消，撤销; 作废; 抵消|To decide that an arranged event will not take place; to order that something already arranged or advertised should not happen; to stop an order for goods or services that you no longer want.|I had to cancel my trip because I got sick. 我不得不取消我的旅行因为我生病了.|Synonyms: abandon, abolish, revoke, annul, call off|Antonyms: approve, authorize, confirm, continue, establish|verb

cancel:/ˈkænsəl/ (US) /ˈkænsl/ (UK)| n.|取消，撤销|The act of cancelling something.|The cancellation of the flight caused a lot of inconvenience. 航班取消造成很多不便.|Synonyms: annulment, revocation|Antonyms: approval, confirmation|noun
