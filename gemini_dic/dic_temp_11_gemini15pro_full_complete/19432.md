bicoastal:/ˌbaɪˈkoʊstəl/
adjective
双海岸的；来往于两岸的
Living or working on both the East and West Coasts of the United States, especially between New York and Los Angeles.
My sister lives a bicoastal life, working in New York and Los Angeles.
我的姐姐过着双海岸的生活，在纽约和洛杉矶工作。
The company has bicoastal offices, facilitating communication and collaboration between teams.
这家公司在东西海岸都设有办事处，方便团队之间的沟通和协作。
Synonyms: transcontinental
Antonyms: regional, local
词性: adjective
