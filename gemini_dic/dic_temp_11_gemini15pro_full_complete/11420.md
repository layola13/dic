argillaceous:/ˌɑːrdʒɪˈleɪʃəs/| adj.|黏土的，含黏土的|Consisting of or containing clay; resembling clay.|The soil in this area is argillaceous and difficult to work with. 这个地区的土壤是黏土质的，很难耕作。|Near synonyms: clayey, clayish, loamy|Near antonyms: sandy, gravelly|adjective
