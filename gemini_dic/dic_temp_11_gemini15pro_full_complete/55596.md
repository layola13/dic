disunites:/ˌdɪsjuˈnaɪts/
verb
使分裂，使不团结
to cause (people or things) to be no longer united
The issue of immigration has disunited the country. 移民问题使该国分裂。
近义词: divide, separate, disjoin, sever
反义词: unite, join, connect
