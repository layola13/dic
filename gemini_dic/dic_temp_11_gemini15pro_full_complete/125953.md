musician:/mjuˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 音乐家；乐师 | A person who plays a musical instrument, especially as a profession, or is musically talented. |  She's a very talented musician. 她是一位非常有才华的音乐家。|  He's a professional musician. 他是一位职业音乐家。 | synonyms: instrumentalist, player, performer, artist | antonyms: amateur, non-musician | noun
