functionary:/ˈfʌŋkʃəˌnɛri/ | n. | 官员，公务员，职员 | A person who holds or performs a particular function within an organization or government.  A person who performs official duties; an official. | The government functionaries were busy preparing for the upcoming summit. 政府官员们正忙于准备即将召开的峰会。| Synonyms: official, bureaucrat, administrator, officer | Antonyms: civilian, layman | noun

functionary:/ˈfʌŋkʃəˌnɛri/ | adj. |  职能的，公务的 | Relating to or characteristic of a functionary or their duties. (Less common usage) | The report detailed the functionary responsibilities of each department. 报告详细说明了每个部门的职能职责。| Synonyms: official, bureaucratic, administrative | Antonyms: unofficial, informal | adjective
