mtm:/ˌɛm tiː ˈɛm/ | abbreviation | 每月交易百万美元 (Million Transactions per Month) |  A metric used in the financial industry, particularly in payment processing, to measure the volume of transactions processed each month. |  The company's MTM reached a record high in December.  这家公司的每月交易量在12月达到了历史新高。 | synonyms: transaction volume, monthly volume | antonyms: N/A | noun

mtm:/ˈɛm tiː ˈɛm/ | abbreviation |  标记到市场 (Mark-to-Market) | An accounting practice that involves adjusting the value of an asset to reflect its current market price. | The bank uses MTM accounting for its trading portfolio. 该银行对其交易投资组合采用按市值计价的会计方法。 | synonyms: fair value accounting | antonyms: historical cost accounting | noun
