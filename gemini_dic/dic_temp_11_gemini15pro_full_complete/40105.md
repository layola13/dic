contrast:/ˈkɑːntræst/ (US) /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (UK)| n. v.|对比，对照；差异，差别|a difference between two or more things| The contrast between the two brothers is striking. 这两兄弟之间的反差很明显。|synonyms: difference, distinction, disparity| antonyms: similarity, resemblance| noun

contrast:/ˈkɑːntræst/ (US) /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (UK)| v.|对比，对照|to compare two or more things to show the differences between them|  She contrasted the two poems in her essay. 她在论文中比较了两首诗。|synonyms: compare, differentiate, juxtapose| antonyms: equate, assimilate| verb
