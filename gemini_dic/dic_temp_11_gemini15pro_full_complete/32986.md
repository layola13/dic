choreography:/ˌkɔːriˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 编舞，舞蹈设计，舞蹈动作 | The art or practice of designing sequences of movements of physical bodies (or their depictions) in which motion, form, or both are specified. | The choreography of the fight scene was very well done.  打斗场面的编排非常好。| synonym: dance design, movement composition | antonym: improvisation | noun
