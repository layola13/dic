flatware:/ˈflætˌweər/| n.|餐具，尤指刀、叉、匙|Knives, forks, and spoons used for eating.  Flatware is typically made of metal, such as stainless steel or silver.|We need to buy new flatware for our dinner party. 我们需要为我们的晚宴购买新的餐具。|The restaurant's flatware was elegant and heavy. 这家餐厅的餐具优雅而厚重。|cutlery, silverware|crockery (餐具通常不包括餐具)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *flat* (meaning "smooth and level") + *ware* (meaning "manufactured goods").  The term originated in the early 20th century. 由*flat*（意为“光滑且平坦”）+ *ware*（意为“制成品”）组成。该词起源于 20 世纪初。
