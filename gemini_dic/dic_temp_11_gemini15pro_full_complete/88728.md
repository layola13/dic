hobnobber:/ˈhɒbnɒbə(r)/ (US: /ˈhɑːbnɑːbər/)| n.| 交际花；与要人交往的人；攀附权贵者|A person who spends time with important or famous people, especially in order to gain advantage.|She's a real hobnobber – always at parties with politicians and celebrities. 她是个真正的交际花，总是和政客和名人一起参加派对。|Synonyms: social climber, name-dropper|Antonyms: recluse, introvert|Noun
hobnobs:/ˈhɒbnɒbz/ (US: /ˈhɑːbnɑːbz/)| v.| (与要人)亲密来往，过从甚密|To spend time with someone in a friendly way, especially someone who is important or famous.|He hobnobs with royalty. 他与皇室成员过从甚密。|Synonyms: socialize, mingle, fraternize|Antonyms: avoid, shun|Verb
