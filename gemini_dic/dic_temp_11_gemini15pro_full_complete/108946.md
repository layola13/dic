leep:/liːp/ | v. | 跳跃；飞跃 | To jump or spring a long way. | He lepped over the fence. 他跳过了篱笆。| She lepped across the stream. 她跳过了小溪。 | 近义词: jump, leap, bound, spring | 反义词: crawl, creep | 动词

词源:  From Middle English *lēpen, from Old English hlēapan (“to leap, jump, run, spring”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaipaną (“to run, jump”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱley- (“to lean, incline”). Cognate with Scots loup (“to leap”), West Frisian liepe (“to run”), Dutch lopen (“to walk, run”), German laufen (“to run”), Icelandic hlaupa (“to run”).
