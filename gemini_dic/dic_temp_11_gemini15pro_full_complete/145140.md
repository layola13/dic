overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/| v. |过度自信地断言；过分强调；|to assert something too strongly or too confidently|He overasserted his authority and alienated his staff. 他过分强调自己的权威，疏远了他的员工。|Example: She overasserted her point, making her seem aggressive. 她过分强调自己的观点，让她显得咄咄逼人。

Example:  He tends to overassert his opinions in meetings, which can be off-putting. 他倾向于在会议上过分强调自己的意见，这可能会让人反感。|近义词：overemphasize, overstate|反义词: understate, downplay|词性:动词
