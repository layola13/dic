gristmill:/ˈɡrɪstˌmɪl/| n. | 磨坊；谷物磨坊 | A building equipped with machinery for grinding grain into flour. | The old gristmill by the river has been restored and is now a museum. 河边的老磨坊已经修复，现在成了博物馆。| synonyms: flour mill, mill | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gristmille*, from Old English *grist* ("grinding") + *mylen* ("mill").  *Grist* ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *greustaną* ("to grind"), and *mylen* from Proto-Germanic *mulinō*, ultimately from Latin *molīna* ("mill").
