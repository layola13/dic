fuscine:/ˈfʌsɪn/| adj.|暗褐色的，深褐色的|Of a dark brownish color, resembling the color of fuscin.|The scientist observed the fuscine pigment under the microscope. 科学家在显微镜下观察了这种深褐色的色素。|Near synonyms: brown, dark brown, dusky, sepia|Near antonyms: light, bright, pale|adjective

fuscin:/ˈfʌsɪn/| n.|褐色素|A dark brown pigment found in the retinal epithelium.|The presence of fuscin in the eye plays a crucial role in vision. 眼睛中褐色素的存在对视觉起着至关重要的作用。|Near synonyms: pigment, melanin|Near antonyms: None (lack of pigment would be the opposite concept)|noun
