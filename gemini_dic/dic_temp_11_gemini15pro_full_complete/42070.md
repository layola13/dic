coterminously:/ˌkoʊˈtɜːrmɪnəsli/
adverb
同时地，共同存在地
Existing or occurring at the same time or during the same period; concurrently.
The two empires existed coterminously for several decades. 这两个帝国同时存在了几十年。
coterminously:/ˌkəʊˈtɜːmɪnəsli/
adverb
同时地，共同存在地
Existing or occurring at the same time or during the same period; concurrently.
Their reigns were coterminous. 他们的统治时期是同时的。
近义词:concurrently, simultaneously, at the same time
反义词:separately, asynchronously, at different times
词性:adverb

词源:
From Latin co- (together) + terminus (end, limit) + -ous (forming adjectives) + -ly (forming adverbs).
