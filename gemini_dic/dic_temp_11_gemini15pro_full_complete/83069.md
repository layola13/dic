gurnet:/ˈɡɜːrnɪt/ | n. | 角鱼；鲬鱼；红娘鱼 | A type of bottom-dwelling fish with a large head and spiny fins, often reddish-brown in color.  Several species are valued as food fish. |  We caught a gurnet while fishing off the pier. 我们在码头钓鱼时钓到了一条角鱼。 | synonyms:  gurnard, sea robin, tubfish | antonyms: None | noun


gurnet:/ˈɡɜːrnɪt/ | n. | 笨蛋;傻瓜 |  A foolish or stupid person (informal, dated). | That gurnet forgot his own birthday! 那个笨蛋连自己的生日都忘了！| synonyms: idiot, fool, dunce | antonyms: genius, mastermind | noun
