abrogate:/ˈæbrəɡeɪt/| v. |废除，取消|To officially end a law, agreement, or custom.|The treaty was abrogated in 1998. 该条约于1998年被废除。|近义词：repeal, revoke, annul, abolish|反义词：enact, establish, institute|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *abrogatus*, past participle of *abrogo* (“I repeal”), from *ab* (“from, away”) + *rogo* (“I ask, propose a law”).
