droll:/droʊl/
adjective
古怪滑稽的；诙谐古怪的
Curious or unusual in a way that amuses; dryly humorous.
He had a droll way of telling stories. 他讲故事的方式很古怪滑稽。
synonyms: amusing, funny, comical, whimsical, quirky
antonyms: serious, solemn, normal, conventional
词性: 形容词
