filled-up:/fɪldˈʌp/| adj.| 充满的，吃饱的 |Full; having no more space. filled-up:/fɪldˈʌp/| verb (past participle)| 加满，填满 | To make something full; to fill something completely.  |  The gas tank was filled-up before the road trip.  公路旅行前油箱加满了。 | I filled-up the application form online. 我在网上填完了申请表。 | synonyms: topped off, filled, completed | antonyms: emptied, drained, incomplete | verb
