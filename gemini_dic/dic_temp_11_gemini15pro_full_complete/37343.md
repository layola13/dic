commendablenesses:/kəˈmɛndəbəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 值得称赞的品质；优点 | The commendablenesses of her character are numerous, including her kindness, integrity, and perseverance. | Her selfless dedication to helping others is one of her many commendablenesses. | 近义词: merits, virtues, strengths | 反义词: flaws, shortcomings, weaknesses | 词性: 名词

commendable: /kəˈmɛndəbl/ | adj. | 值得称赞的；值得推荐的 | His commendable bravery saved many lives. | The student's commendable effort in the project earned him an A+. | 近义词: praiseworthy, laudable, admirable | 反义词: deplorable, reprehensible, blameworthy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *commendabilis, from commendare "to entrust, recommend, praise," from com- "together" + mandare "to entrust."
