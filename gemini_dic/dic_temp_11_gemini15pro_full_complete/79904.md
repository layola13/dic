gnawers:/ˈnɔːərz/ | n. | 啮齿动物 | Animals that gnaw, especially rodents. | Squirrels are common gnawers in many urban parks. 松鼠在许多城市公园里是常见的啮齿动物。 | The beavers, industrious gnawers, built a dam across the stream. 勤劳的海狸，这些啮齿动物，在溪流上筑起了一座水坝。 | 近义词：rodents, nibblers | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 predators 捕食者) | 词性: noun

gnaw:/nɔː/ | v. | 咬，啃 | To bite or chew on something persistently. | The dog gnawed on the bone for hours. 狗啃了几个小时的骨头。 | The mouse gnawed a hole in the wall. 老鼠在墙上啃了个洞。| 近义词：chew, nibble | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: verb
