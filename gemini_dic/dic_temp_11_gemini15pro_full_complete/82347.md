growan: /ˈɡroʊən/ (primarily Scottish and dialectal) | vi. | 呻吟，抱怨 | To moan, groan, or complain. |  He lay on the floor, growaning in pain. 他躺在地板上，痛苦地呻吟着。

The old man was growaning about his aching back. 老人在抱怨他的背痛。


近义词: moan, groan, complain, grumble, whine
反义词: rejoice, celebrate
词性: verb (不及物动词)
