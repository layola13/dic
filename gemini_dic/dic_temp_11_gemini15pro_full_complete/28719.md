carmined:/ˈkɑːrmaɪnd/ /ˈkɑːrmɪnd/| adj.|深红色的，胭脂红的|Deep red or crimson in color, like the pigment carmine.|Her lips were carmined, a stark contrast to her pale face. 她的嘴唇是深红色的，与她苍白的脸形成了鲜明的对比。|The carmined sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 深红色的日落将天空染成了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：crimson, ruby, scarlet, vermilion|反义词:pale, light|词性:形容词
词源:From French *carminé*, past participle of *carminer*, from Italian *carminare* "to card wool," from *carmino* "carmine," ultimately from Arabic *qirmiz* "kermes," referring to a dye derived from insects.
