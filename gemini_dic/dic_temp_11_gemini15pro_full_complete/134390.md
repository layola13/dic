nonintoxicating:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈtɑːksɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
无毒的；不致醉的
Not causing intoxication; not containing alcohol or other intoxicating substances.
Non-alcoholic beer is a popular nonintoxicating beverage.  无酒精啤酒是一种受欢迎的无毒饮料。
This herbal tea is completely nonintoxicating.  这种凉茶完全无毒。
近义词:non-alcoholic, sober
反义词:intoxicating, alcoholic
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + intoxicating.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "intoxicating" means "causing intoxication."
