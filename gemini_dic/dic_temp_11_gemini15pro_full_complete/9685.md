antitheologian:/ˌæntiˌθiːəˈloʊdʒən/
n.|反神学家，反对神学的人|A person who opposes theology or the influence of theologians.|He was a staunch antitheologian, questioning the very foundations of religious dogma. 他是一位坚定的反神学家，质疑宗教教条的根基。|Near synonyms:  atheist, skeptic, freethinker, unbeliever, heretic|Near antonyms: theologian, believer, devotee, pious|noun
