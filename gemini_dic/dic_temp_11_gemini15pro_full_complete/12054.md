artier:/ˈɑːrtiər/| adj.|更具艺术性的，更矫揉造作的|More artistic or affected in manner.|Her style became artier as she spent more time in Paris. 她在巴黎待的时间越长，风格就越矫揉造作。|近义词：affected, pretentious, mannered|反义词：natural, unaffected, artless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *artificiel*, meaning "artificial," ultimately from Latin *ars*, *artis* meaning "art, skill." The comparative form "artier" developed in English.
