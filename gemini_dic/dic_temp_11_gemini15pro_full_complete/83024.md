gunsmithing:/ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ/
noun
枪械制造;枪械修理;修枪业
The making and repairing of firearms.
例句：He learned gunsmithing from his father. 他从他父亲那里学习了枪械制造。
例句：Gunsmithing is a skilled trade.  枪械制造是一项熟练的技艺。
近义词：armory, firearms maintenance, gun repair
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

gunsmithing:/ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ/
verb (gerund)
制造或修理枪支
The act of making or repairing firearms.
例句：He enjoys gunsmithing in his spare time. 他喜欢在业余时间制造和修理枪支。
例句：Gunsmithing requires precision and patience. 制造和修理枪支需要精确性和耐心。
近义词：making guns, repairing guns, firearms work
反义词: N/A
词性: verb (gerund)

Etymology:
From gunsmith + -ing. Gunsmith from gun + smith (meaning a metal worker).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *smithaz.
