molten:/ˈmoʊltən/ (US), /ˈmɒltən/ (UK)| adj.|熔化的，熔融的|Made liquid by heat; melted.  Derived from the past participle of the verb "melt".|The molten lava flowed down the volcano. 熔岩从火山上流下来。|The blacksmith shaped the molten metal. 铁匠塑造熔化的金属。|近义词：melted, liquefied, fused|反义词：solid, frozen|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *molten*, past participle of *melten* ("to melt"), from Old English *meltan* ("to melt"), from Proto-Germanic *meltaną* ("to melt"), from Proto-Indo-European *mél-/*mól- ("soft").  Cognate with Dutch *melten* ("to melt"), German *schmelzen* ("to melt"), Latin *mollis* ("soft").
