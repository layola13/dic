anathemata:/əˈnæθəˌmɑːtə/ | n. | 诅咒物，被诅咒的人或物；献祭物 |  Objects or people that have been cursed or excommunicated, or things devoted to destruction.  It can also refer to votive offerings. | The museum displayed ancient anathema, objects cursed and buried long ago. 博物馆展出了古代的诅咒物，很久以前被诅咒并埋葬的物品。|  The heretic was declared anathema by the church.  这个异教徒被教会宣布为被诅咒的人。|  Synonyms: curse, ban, excommunication, abomination, taboo | Antonyms: blessing, benediction | Noun

anathemata:/əˈnæθəˌmɑːtə/ | n. | 宗教仪式中使用的物品 |  Objects used in religious ceremonies. |  The priest prepared the anathema for the ritual.  神父准备了仪式用的物品。 | The anathema included sacred oils and incense. 这些宗教仪式中使用的物品包括圣油和香。| Synonyms: relics, artifacts, sacred objects | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
