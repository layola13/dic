contrastedly:/kənˈtrɑːstɪdli/ | adv. | 对比地，截然不同地 | In a way that shows a marked difference; by contrast. | The two sisters dressed contrastedly; one in bright colours, the other in black.  这对姐妹的穿着形成了鲜明的对比；一个穿着鲜艳的衣服，另一个穿着黑色的衣服。 | Synonyms: differently, distinctly, strikingly, sharply | Antonyms: similarly, alike, identically | Adverb

contrastedly:/kənˈtrɑːstɪdli/ | adv. | 对比地，截然不同地 |  Their reactions to the news were contrastedly different; he was elated, while she was devastated. 他们对这个消息的反应截然不同；他兴高采烈，而她却悲痛欲绝。 | Synonyms: diversely, variously, disparately | Antonyms: uniformly, homogeneously, consistently | Adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
