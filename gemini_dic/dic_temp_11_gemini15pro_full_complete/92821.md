iconographs:/ˈaɪkɒnəɡrɑːfs/
noun
图像志，图像学
The study or interpretation of the subject matter of representational works of art, especially with reference to their historical or cultural significance.
The iconographs of the ancient Egyptian tombs tell stories of the afterlife.  古埃及墓葬的图像讲述了来世的故事。
