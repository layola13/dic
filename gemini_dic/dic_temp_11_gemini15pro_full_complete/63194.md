entanglingly:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlɪŋli/ | adv. | 缠绕地，纠缠地 | In a way that causes something to become entangled or intertwined; in a complicated or confusing manner. | The vines grew entanglingly around the old oak tree. 藤蔓缠绕着老橡树生长。|  The plot of the novel was entanglingly complex. 小说的情节错综复杂，令人迷惑。 | 近义词: intricately, complexly, convolutedly | 反义词: simply, clearly, straightforwardly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From entangle + -ing + -ly.  "Entangle" comes from the Old French word "entangler," meaning "to entrap in a net."
