orientalism:/ˌɔːriˈentəlɪzəm/
n.|东方主义|A way of seeing, representing, or thinking about Eastern cultures that distorts, stereotypes, and exoticizes them, often from a Western perspective.  It often involves patronizing or romanticized representations of "the Orient."|Example:  Edward Said's book *Orientalism* critiques the Western construction of the East.|Synonym: exoticism, stereotyping|Antonym: multiculturalism|noun
