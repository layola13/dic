ithaca:/ˈɪθəkə/| n.|伊萨卡|A small island in the Ionian Sea, off the west coast of Greece, famous as the home of Odysseus in Homer's Odyssey.|Odysseus longed to return to his beloved Ithaca. 奥德修斯渴望回到他挚爱的伊萨卡。|近义词：homeland, birthplace|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered "foreign land" or "exile")|noun
