defoliant:/diːˈfoʊliənt/ | n. | 落叶剂 | A chemical substance that causes leaves to fall off plants. |  The military used a powerful defoliant during the war.  军方在战争期间使用了一种强效落叶剂。| synonyms: herbicide, phytotoxin | antonyms: fertilizer, growth stimulant | noun

defoliate:/diːˈfoʊlieɪt/ | v. | 使落叶；用落叶剂喷洒 | To cause leaves to fall off, especially prematurely, by the use of chemicals or disease. | The chemical defoliated the trees within days.  这种化学物质几天内就使树木落叶了。 | synonyms: strip, denude | antonyms:  reforest, cultivate | verb
