nondenseness:/ˌnɒnˈdɛnsnɪs/ | n. | 非稠密性 | The property of a set not being dense in a given space.  It means there are "gaps" in the set. | The nondenseness of the rational numbers in the real numbers is a fundamental concept in analysis. 有理数在实数中的非稠密性是分析学中的一个基本概念。| sparseness, discontinuity | denseness | noun
