imbosom:/ɪmˈbʊzəm/ | vt. | 拥抱；怀抱；隐藏；珍藏；使…紧贴 | To enclose or cherish in the bosom; to hold dear; to embrace.  Also, to embed; to cover deeply; to bury. | She imbosomed the secret in her heart. 她把这个秘密藏在心里。 | 近义词：cherish, embrace, harbor, conceal | 反义词：reveal, expose | 词性：动词

词源:
From in + bosom.  "Bosom" originally referred to the breast or chest, and later expanded metaphorically to the heart or innermost feelings. Thus, "imbosom" literally means "to place in the bosom."
