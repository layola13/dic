megalopolistic:/ˌmɛɡəˌlɒpəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
大都市的；特大城市的
Relating to or characteristic of a megalopolis (a very large, heavily populated city or urban complex).
The megalopolistic sprawl of the Northeast Corridor stretches from Boston to Washington, D.C. 美国东北部走廊的大都市蔓延区从波士顿延伸到华盛顿特区。
The study analyzed the megalopolistic trends of urbanization in the 21st century. 这项研究分析了21世纪城市化的大都市趋势。
近义词: metropolitan, urban
反义词: rural
