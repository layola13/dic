indepth:/ˌɪnˈdɛpθ/| adj.|深入的，彻底的|thorough, detailed, and comprehensive|例句：We need an indepth analysis of the problem. 我们需要对这个问题进行深入的分析。|例句：She conducted indepth research on the topic. 她对这个主题进行了深入的研究。|近义词：thorough, comprehensive, exhaustive, detailed|反义词：superficial, cursory, shallow|词性:形容词
词源: From "in" + "depth".  "In" meaning "into" or "within" and "depth" referring to the extent or thoroughness of something.
