falafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ (US), /fəˈlæfəl/ (UK)| n.|炸鹰嘴豆丸子，沙拉三明治|A Middle Eastern dish of spiced mashed chickpeas or fava beans formed into balls or patties and deep-fried, usually eaten in pita bread.|例句：I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸鹰嘴豆丸子三明治。

|例句：Falafels are a popular street food in many Middle Eastern countries. 炸鹰嘴豆丸子是许多中东国家的流行街头食品。

|近义词：felafel (variant spelling)|反义词:|名词


falafils:/fəˈlɑːfəlz/ (US), /fəˈlæfəlz/ (UK)| n.|炸鹰嘴豆丸子（复数）|The plural form of falafel.|例句：We ordered a plate of falafils to share. 我们点了一盘炸鹰嘴豆丸子一起吃。
