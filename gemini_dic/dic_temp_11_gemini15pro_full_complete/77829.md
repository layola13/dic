generativism:/ˈdʒɛnərətɪvɪzəm/
n.
生成主义
A theory of language acquisition that argues that humans are born with an innate capacity for language, and that language develops through the interaction of this innate capacity with environmental input.
Generativism proposes that children are born with an innate knowledge of the basic structure of language.  生成主义认为儿童天生就具备对语言基本结构的认知。
Chomsky's work has had a profound impact on the field of linguistics, particularly in the area of generativism.  乔姆斯基的著作对语言学领域，尤其是在生成主义领域，产生了深远的影响。
近义词:nativism, innatism
反义词:empiricism, behaviorism
词性:noun
