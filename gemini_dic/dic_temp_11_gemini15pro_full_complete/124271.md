motelier:/moʊˈtɛlˈjeɪ/ /məʊtelˈɪə(r)/
n.
汽车旅馆老板，汽车旅馆经营者
A person who owns or manages a motel.
The motelier greeted us warmly at the reception desk. 汽车旅馆老板在接待处热情地迎接我们。
The motelier upgraded our room to a suite. 汽车旅馆老板将我们的房间升级为套房。

近义词: innkeeper, hotelier, hotel manager
反义词: guest, customer


词性: noun

词源:  From motel (motor hotel) + -ier (a suffix indicating a person engaged in a particular occupation or activity).
