bechuanas:/ˌbɛtʃuˈɑːnəz/
noun
茨瓦纳人
A member of a Bantu-speaking people of Botswana and neighboring areas.
The Bechuanas have a rich cultural heritage. 茨瓦纳人拥有丰富的文化遗产。
Bechuanas traditionally live in rural areas. 茨瓦纳人传统上居住在农村地区。
Synonym: Tswana
Antonym: N/A (It's a proper noun referring to a specific group of people, so a direct antonym doesn't exist.)
词性: noun
