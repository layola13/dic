monophyllous:/ˌmɒnəˈfɪləs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈfɪləs/| adj. |单叶的|Having only one leaf or leaf-like part.|Example: Some orchids are monophyllous, producing a single leaf each year.  一些兰花是单叶的，每年只长出一片叶子。
Example: The monophyllous plant stood out amongst the others with their abundant foliage.  这株单叶植物在其他枝繁叶茂的植物中显得格外突出。|近义词:unifoliate, single-leaved|反义词:polyphyllous, multifoliate|词性:形容词
