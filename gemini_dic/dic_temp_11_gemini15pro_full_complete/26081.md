bushwhacking:/ˈbʊʃˌwækɪŋ/
n.|丛林穿越；开路；艰难跋涉|The act of traveling through dense forest or brush, where there are no trails.|例句：Bushwhacking through the dense Amazon rainforest was a challenging experience.  在茂密的亚马逊雨林中艰难跋涉是一次充满挑战的经历。|近义词：hiking, trekking, exploring|反义词：strolling, sauntering|词性:名词

bushwhacking:/ˈbʊʃˌwækɪŋ/
v.|在丛林中开路；穿越丛林|To travel through dense forest or brush, where there are no trails.|例句：We spent days bushwhacking through the undergrowth. 我们花了几天时间在灌木丛中开路。|近义词：hiking, trekking, exploring|反义词：strolling, sauntering|词性:动词

词源:  "bushwhack" 最早出现在19世纪中叶的美国英语中，可能源自荷兰语 "boschwachter"，意思是 "forest guard" 或 "bush beater"。后来，它的意思演变为在没有道路或小径的情况下穿越茂密的灌木丛或森林。
