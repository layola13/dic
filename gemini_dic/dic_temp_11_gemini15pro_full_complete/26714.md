cadet's:/kəˈdɛts/
n.
学员的，学生军官的
Possessing form of cadet; belonging to a cadet.
This is the cadet's uniform.  这是那位学员的制服。
The cadet's rifle was meticulously cleaned.  这名学生军官的步枪被一丝不苟地擦拭干净了。
Synonyms: student's, trainee's
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider) officer's, veteran's
noun, possessive
