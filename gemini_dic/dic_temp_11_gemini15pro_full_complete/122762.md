moisturizes: /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
滋润，保湿
Makes something less dry, especially skin.
This cream moisturizes and softens the skin.  这款面霜滋润并软化肌肤。
hydrates, softens
dries, dehydrates
