again:/əˈɡen/ /əˈɡɛn/| adv.|再一次，又一次，此外，再则|Used to indicate repetition or return to a previous state or activity.|I'll see you again soon. 我很快会再见到你。|Synonyms: once more, anew, afresh|Antonyms: never, no more|Adverb

USAGE->

I read this book again every year. 我每年都会重读这本书。
He always forgets his keys, so he has to go back again. 他总是忘记钥匙，所以不得不再次回去。
