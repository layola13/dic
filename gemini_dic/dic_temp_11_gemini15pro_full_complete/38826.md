concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/ (US), /kənˈsɜːn/ (UK)| vt.| 关心, 关注, 涉及; 使担心|To be about something; to involve someone or something; to cause worry to someone.|  The news concerns the safety of the children. 这条新闻事关孩子们的安全。|Synonyms: involve, affect, worry, trouble|Antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *concerner, from Latin concernere "to mix together, sift, concern," from con- "together" + cernere "to separate, distinguish, perceive." The notion is of things being "sifted together."
