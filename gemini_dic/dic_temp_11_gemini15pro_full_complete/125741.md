muntjac:/ˈmʌntdʒæk/ | n. | 麂，赤麂 | A small deer native to southern Asia, with small antlers and tusks in the males. | 例句：The muntjac darted across the forest path.  赤麂飞快地穿过森林小路。

例句：We saw a muntjac grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一只麂在草地上吃草。

近义词：barking deer, rib-faced deer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal species) | 词性: noun
