denominating:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |命名，称呼；以…为单位的|Expressing a value in a specified unit.  |例句1：The bond is denominated in US dollars.  这债券是以美元计价的。

例句2：Coins denominated in cents. 以分计价的硬币。 |近义词：naming, designating, specifying, expressing |反义词：N/A |词性:形容词

denominate:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪt/
| vt. |命名，称呼；以…为单位|To give a name or designation to something; to express or state the value of something in a specified unit. |例句1：The new element was denominated Californium. 这种新元素被命名为锎。

例句2：The currency is denominated in euros.  这种货币以欧元计价。 |近义词：name, call, designate, term, entitle, label|反义词：N/A |词性:动词


词源：
From Latin *denominatus*, past participle of denominare (“to name, designate”), from de- (“down, from, away”) + nomen (“name”).
