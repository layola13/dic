otopathy:/oʊˈtɑːpəθi/
n.
耳病，耳痛
A disease or disorder of the ear.
Otopathy can cause hearing loss. 耳病会导致听力损失。
He suffers from otopathy. 他患有耳病。
近义词: ear disease, ear disorder
反义词: ear health
