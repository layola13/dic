antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/| adj. n.|防腐的，抗菌的；防腐剂，抗菌剂|Preventing the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.|例句：She cleaned the wound with an antiseptic solution. 她用防腐溶液清洗了伤口。|例句：The antiseptic properties of the herb helped to prevent infection. 这种草药的抗菌特性有助于防止感染。|近义词：disinfectant, germicidal, antimicrobial|反义词: septic, infected|adjective, noun
