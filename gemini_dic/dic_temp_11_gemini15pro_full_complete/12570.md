aspiring:/əˈspaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. |有抱负的，渴望成功的，有志向的 | Directing itself towards a specific result or achievement; desiring and working towards a goal. |  He is an aspiring writer, hoping to publish his first novel soon. 他是一位有抱负的作家，希望很快出版他的第一部小说。

She's an aspiring actress, taking classes and auditioning for roles. 她是一位有抱负的女演员，正在上课并试镜争取角色。| 近义词: ambitious, hopeful, striving | 反义词: unambitious, apathetic, indifferent | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "aspire" which comes from Old French aspirer, from Latin aspirare "to breathe upon, long for," from ad- "to" + spirare "to breathe."
