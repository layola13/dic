indefinites:/ɪnˈdɛfɪnɪts/| n. |不定代词；不定量词|Words like "some," "any," "several," or "many" that do not refer to specific things or amounts.|Several students were late for class.  几个学生上课迟到了。|近义词：non-specifics|反义词:definites|noun


indefinite:/ɪnˈdɛfɪnɪt/| adj. |不确定的；无限期的；模糊的|Not clearly defined or determined; lasting for an unknown or unstated length of time.|The meeting is scheduled for an indefinite time in the future. 会议安排在将来的某个不确定时间。|近义词：vague, uncertain|反义词:definite|adjective
