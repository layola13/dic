ornithotomical:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈtɒmɪkəl/ | adj. | 鸟类解剖的 | Relating to the dissection or anatomy of birds. |  The ornithotomical study revealed the bird's unique skeletal structure. 鸟类解剖学研究揭示了这种鸟独特的骨骼结构。

|  The museum houses a collection of ornithotomical specimens. 博物馆收藏了一系列鸟类解剖标本。
| 近义词: anatomical (of animals in general) | 反义词:  None | 词性:  adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *ornitho-* (bird) + *-tomical* (cutting, dissection).  Ultimately from Greek *ornis* (bird) and *temnein* (to cut).
