eclecticism: /ɪˈklɛktɪsɪzəm/
noun|折衷主义；博采众长|Her decorating style was an eclecticism of Asian and European influences. 她的装饰风格融合了亚洲和欧洲的影响。|
eclecticism: /ɪˈklɛktɪsɪzəm/
noun|折衷主义；博采众长|例句: His musical tastes are quite eclectic, ranging from classical to hip-hop. 他的音乐品味相当兼收并蓄，从古典到嘻哈都有。|近义词: pluralism, mix, assortment|反义词: purity, homogeneity|

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *éclectisme*, from Ancient Greek *ἐκλεκτικός* (eklektikós, “selective”), from *ἐκλέγω* (eklégō, “I pick out, select”).
