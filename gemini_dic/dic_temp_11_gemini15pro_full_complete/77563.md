gearboxes:/ˈɡɪərbɒksɪz/ (UK), /ˈɡɪərbɑːksɪz/ (US)| n. |变速箱，齿轮箱|A gearbox is a casing containing gears and other parts that transmit power from an engine to the driving wheels of a vehicle or other machine. It changes the speed and torque of the engine's output to suit the driving conditions.|The car's gearbox needs repairing. 这辆车的变速箱需要修理。|
The truck's gearbox failed on the steep hill. 这辆卡车的变速箱在陡峭的山坡上失灵了。|近义词：transmission, transaxle|反义词: None|词性: Noun
