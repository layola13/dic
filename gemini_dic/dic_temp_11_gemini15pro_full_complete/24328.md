brilliants:/ˈbrɪljənts/| n.|钻石，光辉灿烂的事物|Diamonds or other gems, especially when cut and polished.  Can also refer to exceptionally intelligent or talented individuals.|She wore a necklace of brilliants. 她戴着一条钻石项链。|近义词：gems, diamonds, stars|反义词:dullards, mediocrities|词性:noun

brilliants:/ˈbrɪljənts/| n.|才华横溢的人|Exceptionally intelligent or talented individuals.|The university attracts brilliants from all over the world. 这所大学吸引了来自世界各地的优秀人才。|近义词：geniuses, prodigies, intellectuals|反义词:dullards, mediocrities|词性:noun


词源: From the Old French "briliant," present participle of "briller" (to shine), from the Latin "beryllare" (relating to beryl, a precious stone).
