enround:/ɪnˈraʊnd/ | v. | 包围，环绕 | To surround or enclose something completely. | The soldiers enrounded the enemy camp. 士兵们包围了敌军的营地。| The mountains enround the valley. 山脉环绕着山谷。| 近义词：surround, encompass, encircle | 反义词: expose, free | 动词

词源: From en- + round, influenced by surround.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "to put into."  "Round" refers to a circular shape.  Therefore, "enround" literally means "to put into a round" or "to surround."
