loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtə/ (UK)| v. |闲逛，徘徊，逗留|To stand or wait around idly or without apparent purpose.|例句：A group of teenagers were loitering outside the shop. 一群青少年在商店外面闲逛。|近义词：linger, dawdle, hang around|反义词:hurry, hasten|词性:动词

loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtə/ (UK)| n. |闲逛，徘徊|The act of loitering.|例句：Loitering is prohibited in this area. 此区域禁止闲逛。|近义词：lingering, dawdling|反义词:hastening|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle Dutch *loteren "to shake, dangle," from Proto-Germanic *lut- "to bend down, stoop."
