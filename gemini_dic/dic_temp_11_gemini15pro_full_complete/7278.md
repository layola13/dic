angoumois:/ɑ̃.ɡu.mwa/| n. |昂古莱姆蛾|A small moth (Sitotroga cerealella) whose larva feeds inside kernels of stored grain, especially wheat.  It is a major pest of stored grains worldwide, originating from the Angoumois region of France. |The angoumois moth infestation ruined the entire grain harvest. 昂古莱姆蛾虫害毁掉了整个谷物收成。|近义词：grain moth, weevil|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
