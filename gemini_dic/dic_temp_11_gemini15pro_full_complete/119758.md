mien: /miːn/
noun
外表；样子；风采
A person's look or manner, especially one of a particular kind indicating their character or mood.
She had a serious mien. 她神情严肃。
synonyms: appearance, look, aspect, air, aura, demeanor, bearing, countenance, expression
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for mien, but one could consider opposites like "disguise" or words suggesting a lack of characteristic expression like "blankness")
