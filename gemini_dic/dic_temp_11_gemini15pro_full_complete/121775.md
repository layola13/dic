misreaders:/ˌmɪsˈriːdərz/
noun
误读者，误解者
People who misread or misinterpret something, especially text.
Many misreaders of the article took the author's satire as serious commentary.  许多误读这篇文章的人把作者的讽刺当成了严肃的评论。
近义词: misinterpreters, malinterpreters
反义词: accurate readers, comprehenders
词性: noun
