ahistoricisms:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəmz/
n.
缺乏历史感；非历史性；脱离历史
Interpretations of the past that are anachronistic or lack historical context.
The film was criticized for its ahistoricisms, such as depicting characters using technology that hadn't been invented yet. 这部电影因其缺乏历史感而受到批评，例如描绘角色使用尚未发明的技术。

ahistoricisms:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəmz/
词性: 名词 (plural)
中文翻译: 缺乏历史感；非历史性；脱离历史；对历史的无知
英文解析:  The quality or state of being ahistorical; a lack of historical perspective or context. Specifically, interpretations or representations of the past that are anachronistic or disregard historical accuracy.  It can also refer to instances of ignoring or distorting historical facts.
例句：
The professor criticized the student's essay for its ahistoricisms. 教授批评了学生的论文缺乏历史感。

Her ahistoricisms made it difficult to take her analysis seriously. 她对历史的无知使得人们很难认真对待她的分析。
近义词: anachronisms, inaccuracies, distortions
反义词: historical accuracy, contextualization
