antiphilosophies:/ˌænti fɪˈlɒsəfiz/ |n.| 反哲学，非哲学 |Systems of thought or belief that oppose or reject the fundamental principles of philosophy, often emphasizing practicality, skepticism, or religious dogma over rational inquiry.|  He dismissed their arguments as mere antiphilosophies, lacking any real intellectual rigor. 他认为他们的论点不过是反哲学，缺乏真正的学术严谨性。|Synonyms: counterphilosophies, unphilosophical doctrines| Antonyms: philosophies, philosophical systems| Noun
