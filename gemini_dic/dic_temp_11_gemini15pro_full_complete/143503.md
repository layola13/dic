outglowing: /ˌaʊtˈɡloʊɪŋ/
adjective
发出更强光芒的；胜过…的光芒的
Surpassing in brightness; radiating a stronger light than something else.
The outglowing beauty of the sunset captivated everyone. 落日胜过一切的光芒，令人着迷。
Near Synonyms: outshining, outradiating, eclipsing
Antonyms: dull, dim, faint
词性: 形容词
