offer:/ˈɒfə(r) (British), ˈɔːfər (American)| n. vt.|提议，提供；报价；出价|An offer is a proposal to do something or to give something to someone.|
He made an offer to buy the house. 他出价要买那栋房子。
She received an offer of a job in London. 她收到一份在伦敦的工作邀请。

offer:/ˈɒfə(r) (British), ˈɔːfər (American)| vt.|提供，给予；提出；出价|To offer is to present something for acceptance or rejection.|
He offered me a ride home. 他提出载我回家。
I offered her a cup of tea. 我给她倒了一杯茶。


近义词:propose, tender, present, suggest
反义词:refuse, decline, reject
词性:noun, verb
