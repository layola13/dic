jugum:/ˈdʒuːɡəm/| n.|轭，连接，一对|A yoke or connecting structure.  A pair of opposite leaflets of a pinnate leaf.|The oxen strained against their jugum. 牛费力地拉着它们的轭。|Synonyms: yoke, link, couple, pair|Antonyms: separation, division|noun
