denotation's:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 字面意义；指称意义 |  The denotation of a word is its literal meaning, the 'dictionary definition', as opposed to its connotation. | The denotation of "home" is "a place where one lives," but its connotations can include warmth, family, and security. | 近义词: meaning, signification, sense | 反义词: connotation, implication | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin denotatio(n-), from the verb denotare (see denote).
