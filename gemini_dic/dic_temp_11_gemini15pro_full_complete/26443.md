byroad:/ˈbaɪˌroʊd/ | n. | 小路，僻静的道路 | A small, less-traveled road, typically one that branches off a main road. | We took a scenic byroad through the countryside. 我们走了一条穿过乡村的风景优美的小路。 | Example: The byroad led us to a charming village. 这条小路把我们带到一个迷人的村庄。| Synonyms: back road, side road, lane, track | Antonyms: highway, main road, expressway | noun
