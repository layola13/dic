fork-over:/fɔːrk ˈoʊvər/ | phrasal verb | 交出，支付（尤指不情愿地） | To hand over or relinquish something, especially money, often reluctantly. |  例句：I had to fork over a hundred dollars for that speeding ticket. 我不得不为那张超速罚单支付一百美元。

例句：He finally forked over the money he owed me. 他终于把他欠我的钱还给我了。

近义词：hand over, cough up, shell out, pay up | 反义词：keep, withhold | 词性：动词短语
