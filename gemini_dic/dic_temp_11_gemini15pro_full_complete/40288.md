continuers:/kənˈtɪnjuːərz/
noun
继续者；持续者
Those who continue a particular activity or process are continuers.
The continuers of the family business have maintained its traditions.  家族企业的继承者们保持着它的传统。
synonyms: successors, maintainers, propagators
antonyms: quitters, abandoners
