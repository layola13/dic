crossway:/ˈkrɒsweɪ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsweɪ/ (US)| n. | 十字路口；交叉路；人行横道 |A place where two or more roads meet and cross each other; a paved path for pedestrians to cross a road. |例句：The accident happened at the crossway. 事故发生在十字路口。

例句：Be careful when crossing the crossway. 过马路时要小心。

近义词：intersection, crossing, junction |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'straight path') |词性: noun
