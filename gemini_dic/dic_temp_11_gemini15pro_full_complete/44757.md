crumbly:/ˈkrʌmbli/| adj.|易碎的，脆的|Easily broken into small fragments; friable.|The cake was delicious but a little too crumbly.  这蛋糕很美味，但有点太碎了。|The cheese was dry and crumbly. 奶酪又干又碎。|近义词：brittle, friable, flaky|反义词：moist, firm, compact|词性:形容词
crumbly:/ˈkrʌmbli/|詞源: From crumb + -ly|用法: 用于描述容易破碎成小块的东西，例如食物、土壤等。|例句：The soil was dry and crumbly. 土壤干燥易碎。|The cookies were perfectly crumbly. 饼干酥脆得恰到好处。|近义词：brittle, friable|反义词：moist, firm|词性:形容词
