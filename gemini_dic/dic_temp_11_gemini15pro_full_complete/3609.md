词性: 名词

词源:  From agedness + possessive suffix 's. Agedness is derived from aged + -ness. Aged comes from Middle English aged, from Old French aagé, past participle of aager "to age," from Vulgar Latin *aticare, from Latin aetas "age." The suffix -ness forms nouns meaning "the state or quality of being." Thus, agedness's refers to "the state of being aged," belonging to something.
