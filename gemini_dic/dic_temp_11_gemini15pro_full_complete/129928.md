non-linearity:/ˌnɑːnˌlɪnˈiːærəti/ | n. | 非线性 |The quality or state of being non-linear.  A relationship between two quantities that is not a straight line when graphed. |The system's non-linearity makes it difficult to predict its behavior.  这个系统的非线性使得很难预测它的行为。| nonlinear behavior, nonlinear response | linearity | noun

non-linear:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪniər/ | adj. | 非线性的 |Not linear; not denoting, involving, or arranged in a straight line. Not proportional. |The graph shows a non-linear relationship between the two variables.  图表显示了两个变量之间的非线性关系。|non-proportional, curved, asymmetrical | linear, straight, proportional | adjective
