myronic:/maɪˈrɒnɪk/
adjective
芥子油苷的; 芥子苷的
Relating to or derived from myrosin.  A glycoside, found in certain plants, that yields a pungent oil (such as mustard oil) on hydrolysis by myrosin.
Myronic acid is a precursor to the pungent oil found in mustard seeds.  芥子酸是芥菜籽中辛辣油的前体。
Myronic acid is hydrolyzed by the enzyme myrosinase. 芥子酸被芥子酶水解。
Synonyms: glucosinolate
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for this technical term)
word type: adjective
