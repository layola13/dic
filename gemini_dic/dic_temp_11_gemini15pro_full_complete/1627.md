acidulent:/əˈsɪdʒʊlənt/
adjective
酸的；尖刻的；带讽刺意味的
Slightly sour or sharp in taste or manner.  Characterized by a pleasantly sharp quality or by subtle sarcasm.
Her acidulent wit made her a formidable debater. 她尖刻的才智使她成为一位难对付的辩论者。
acid, tart, sour, sharp, biting, caustic, acrid
sweet, bland, saccharine, mild
