hamet:/ˈhæmɪt/| n.|小村庄，小市镇|A small village or town, typically one smaller than a village.|例句：The hamet nestled among the rolling hills. 小村庄依偎在起伏的山峦中。

例句：They grew up in a remote hamet. 他们在偏远的小村庄长大。|近义词：village, hamlet, town|反义词:city, metropolis|词性:名词
