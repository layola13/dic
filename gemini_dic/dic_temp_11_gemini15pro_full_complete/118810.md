metamorphism:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |变质(作用); 变形; 变态 |A change of the form or nature of a thing or person into a completely different one, by natural or supernatural means.| Marble is formed by the metamorphism of limestone. 大理石是由石灰岩变质形成的。|近义词：transformation, alteration, modification, mutation |反义词：stasis, stagnation |词性:noun

metamorphosis:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/| n. |变质(作用); 变形; 变态 |The process of transformation from an immature form to an adult form in two or more distinct stages.|  The butterfly undergoes metamorphosis. 蝴蝶经历变态。|近义词：transformation, alteration, modification, mutation |反义词：stasis, stagnation |词性:noun
