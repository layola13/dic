concordat:/kənˈkɔːrdæt/| n.|协定，一致，和约|A formal agreement between two or more parties, especially one between the Vatican and a secular government relating to matters of mutual interest.|The concordat between the Church and the State established the Church's right to appoint bishops.|Synonyms: agreement, treaty, pact, covenant|Antonyms: disagreement, discord, conflict|noun

concordat:/kənˈkɔːrdæt/| n.|和谐，协调|A state of agreement or harmony.|The two sides reached a concordat after months of negotiations.|Synonyms: harmony, agreement, accord, unity|Antonyms: discord, disharmony, conflict|noun
