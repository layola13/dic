doormen: /ˈdɔːrmən/ (US), /ˈdɔːmən/ (UK)| n. |门卫，门房|A person employed to open and close doors, especially in a hotel or apartment building, and to assist guests or residents.|The doormen at the Ritz are always impeccably dressed. 丽兹酒店的门卫总是穿着无可挑剔。|Synonyms: porter, attendant, concierge|Antonyms: resident, tenant|Noun
