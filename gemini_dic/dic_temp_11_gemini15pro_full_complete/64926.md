estacade:/ɛstəˈkeɪd/ |n.| 木桩栅栏，栈桥，(尤指)突堤|A row of stakes driven into the seabed or riverbed to support a bridge or other structure, or to protect against erosion or collisions.| The ships were moored to the estacade. 船只停泊在栈桥边。|Synonyms: pier, jetty, wharf, dock, breakwater|Antonyms: open water|noun
