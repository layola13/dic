drm:/ˌdiːˌɑːrˈɛm/ /ˈdiːɑːrˈɛm/ | n. | 数字版权管理 (Digital Rights Management) |  A system used to protect copyrighted digital media (such as music and video) from unauthorized duplication.  It controls access and usage rights of digital content. |  The drm on this music file prevents me from copying it to another device.  这个音乐文件的数字版权管理阻止我将其复制到另一个设备。 | Example:  Many ebooks are protected by drm to prevent piracy. 许多电子书都受到数字版权管理的保护，以防止盗版。 | Synonyms:  copy protection, content protection, access control | Antonyms:  freeware, open source | noun
