cohesivenesses:/koʊˈhiːsɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
凝聚力；团结；粘结性
The state of being cohesive; the quality of forming a united whole.  The various cohesivenesses of the different social groups were analyzed.
The team demonstrated remarkable cohesiveness throughout the project.  团队在整个项目中展现出非凡的凝聚力。
The cohesiveness of the community was strengthened after the disaster.  灾难过后，社区的凝聚力增强了。

近义词:unity, solidarity, cohesion, togetherness
反义词:division, disunity, fragmentation, discord

词性:noun
