logs:/lɒɡz/ (UK), /lɔːɡz/ (US)| n.|原木，日志，记录|A section of a tree trunk that has fallen or been cut down. Also refers to a record of events or data.|The lumberjack chopped down the tree and cut it into logs. 伐木工人砍倒了树，并把它锯成原木。|

The captain kept a detailed log of the voyage. 船长详细记录了航行的日志。|近义词：timber, record, journal|反义词: None|Noun
