misarticulates:/ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjəleɪts/
| v. |发音错误，吐字不清|To pronounce incorrectly; to articulate poorly. |She sometimes misarticulates words when she's nervous. 她紧张的时候有时会发音错误。|He misarticulated the name of the street, leading us to the wrong place. 他念错了街道的名字，把我们带到了错误的地方。|近义词：slur, mumble, mispronounce|反义词: articulate, pronounce, enunciate|词性:动词

Etymology:
mis- (prefix): wrong, bad, badly
articulate (verb): to express (something, such as an idea) clearly and effectively
