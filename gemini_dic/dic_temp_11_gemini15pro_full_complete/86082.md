hearsay:/ˈhɪrseɪ/ (US) /ˈhɪəseɪ/ (UK)| n.|传闻；道听途说|Information received from other people which one cannot adequately substantiate; rumour.|The court cannot accept evidence based on hearsay. 法庭不接受基于传闻的证据。|Synonyms: rumor, gossip, report, tale|Antonyms: fact, truth, evidence|noun
