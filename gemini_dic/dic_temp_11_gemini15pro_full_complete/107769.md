lapidific:/ˌlæpɪˈdɪfɪk/
adjective
使成石的；石化的
transforming into stone; petrifying.
The lapidific process can turn organic matter into fossils over millions of years.  数百万年的石化过程可以将有机物变成化石。
The ancient spring had lapidific properties, turning anything that fell into it to stone.  这处古老的泉水具有石化特性，任何落入其中的东西都会变成石头。
Synonyms: petrifying, fossilizing, lithifying
Antonyms: dissolving, melting, liquefying
adjective
