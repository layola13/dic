infused:/ɪnˈfjuːzd/ | adj. | 注入的，灌输的，浸渍的 | Filled or permeated with something, typically something non-material. |  The air was infused with the scent of pine. 空气中弥漫着松树的香味。|  The tea is infused with herbs.  这茶里注入了草药。 | imbued, steeped, permeated, saturated |  devoid, lacking | adjective

infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/ | v. | 注入，灌输，浸渍 | To fill or pervade something with a quality or substance. | She infused the room with her cheerful personality. 她用她开朗的性格感染了整个房间。 | He infused the tea with mint leaves. 他在茶里加入了薄荷叶。| imbue, instill, inject, permeate, saturate | drain, extract | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *infusus*, past participle of *infundere* "to pour in," from *in-* "in" + *fundere* "to pour."
