USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? 她最近在忙什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading recently? 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? 她参加聚会时穿了什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? 他们在看电影之前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? 他们几个小时以来一直在讨论什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? 他们在会议上将要展示什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? 到周五你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底他们将取得什么成就？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for by the time you graduate? 到你毕业的时候你将已经学习什么多久了？
What will she have been doing for the past ten years? 过去十年她一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? 如果我不在那里会发生什么？


被动语态:
What is being done about the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了些什么？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you thinking about? 你在想什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want here. 这里没有什么我想要的。
