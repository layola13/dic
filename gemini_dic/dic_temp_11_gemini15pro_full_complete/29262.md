cassiterite:/kəˈsɪtəraɪt/ | n. |锡石|A dark, heavy, metallic mineral consisting of tin dioxide, SnO2, and being the chief source of tin. |Cassiterite is the most important ore of tin. 锡石是最重要的锡矿石。|Cassiterite is found in alluvial deposits and hydrothermal veins. 锡石存在于冲积矿床和热液矿脉中。|Synonyms: tinstone|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
