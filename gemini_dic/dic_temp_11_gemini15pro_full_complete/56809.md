dorics: /ˈdɔːrɪks/
n.|多立克式，多立克柱式|Relating to or in the Doric order of classical architecture, characterized by a plain and sturdy column. |The temple was built in the Doric style. 这座寺庙是以多立克风格建造的。|Near synonyms: Doric order, Doric style|Antonyms: Ionic, Corinthian|noun
