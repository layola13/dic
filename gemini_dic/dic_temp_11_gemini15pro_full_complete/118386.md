mermaid:/ˈmɜːrmeɪd/ | n. | 美人鱼 | A mythical sea creature with the head and upper body of a woman and the tail of a fish. |  The little girl dreamt of becoming a mermaid and swimming with dolphins. 小女孩梦想着成为美人鱼和海豚一起游泳。

|例句： Sailors used to believe that mermaids lured ships onto rocks.  水手们过去相信美人鱼会引诱船只撞到岩石上。


|近义词：siren, sea nymph |反义词: (No direct antonym exists, possibly land creature) |词性: noun
