What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
