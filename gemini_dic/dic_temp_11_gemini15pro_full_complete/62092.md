enchafe:/ɪnˈtʃeɪf/| v. |激怒，使恼火，擦伤|To irritate or chafe; to make sore by rubbing.|The tight collar enchafed his neck.  紧绷的衣领擦伤了他的脖子。|Synonyms: irritate, vex, gall, annoy, abrade|Antonyms: soothe, calm, mollify|Verb

enchafe:/ɪnˈtʃeɪf/| n. |擦伤，恼怒|Soreness or irritation caused by rubbing.|The enchafe from the saddle made it difficult to ride. 马鞍造成的擦伤使骑马变得困难。|Synonyms: irritation, chafing, soreness|Antonyms: comfort, relief|Noun
