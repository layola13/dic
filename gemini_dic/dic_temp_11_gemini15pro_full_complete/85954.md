headmould:/ˈhɛdməʊld/ (UK) /ˈhɛdˌmoʊld/ (US)| n. |头形，头型，头模|The shape of a baby's head, especially a newborn, often molded by passage through the birth canal.|The baby's headmould was slightly elongated after birth.  婴儿出生后头形略微拉长。|Synonym: head shape, cranial mold|Antonym: N/A (shape is not typically antonymous)|noun
