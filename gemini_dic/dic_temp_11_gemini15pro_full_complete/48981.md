degerm:/diːˈdʒɜːrm/
verb
去除胚芽
to remove the germ from (grain)
The wheat is degermed before it is ground into flour. 小麦在磨成面粉之前要去除胚芽。
近义词:hull, husk
反义词:germinate
词性:v.


degerm:/diːˈdʒɜːrm/
noun
去胚芽处理
the process of removing the germ from grain
Degerming is a crucial step in the production of white flour. 去胚芽处理是生产白面粉的关键步骤。
近义词:husking, shelling
反义词:germination
词性:n.
