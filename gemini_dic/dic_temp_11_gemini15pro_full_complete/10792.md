aquatain:/ˈækwəteɪn/
noun
水性涂料，防水涂料
A coating that makes a surface water-resistant.
The boat was coated with aquatain to protect it from the elements.  这艘船涂上了水性涂料以抵御风雨。
近义词:waterproof coating, sealant
反义词:None
词性:n.

aquatain:/ˈækwəteɪn/
verb
涂上防水涂料
To coat with a water-resistant substance.
They aquatained the deck to prevent water damage.  他们给甲板涂上了防水涂料以防止水损。
近义词:waterproof, seal
反义词:None
词性:v.
