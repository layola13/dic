manifestation:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃən/| n.| 表现；显示；示威；显灵|an event, action, or object that clearly shows or embodies something, especially a feeling, attitude, or quality. |The painting is a manifestation of the artist's grief. 这幅画是艺术家悲伤的体现。|synonyms: display, demonstration, expression, exhibition, revelation|antonyms: concealment, suppression, hiding|noun
