emissive:/ɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
发射的，散发（光、热、气体等）的
emitting something, such as light, heat, or gas
例句：The emissive properties of the material make it ideal for use in lighting. 这种材料的发光特性使其非常适合用于照明。

例句：The sun is an emissive body that radiates heat and light. 太阳是一个散发热量和光的发射体。

近义词：radiant, luminous, bright
反义词：absorbent, non-emissive, dark
词性: adjective
