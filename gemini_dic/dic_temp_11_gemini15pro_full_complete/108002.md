lasso:/ˈlæsoʊ/ (US) /ˈlæsəʊ/ (UK)| n. vt.|套索；以套索套捕|A rope with a loop at one end, used for catching horses and cattle.  The cowboy lassoed the calf.|例句：The cowboy swung his lasso and caught the stray steer. 牛仔挥动套索套住了那头走失的小公牛。

例句：She lassoed a passing waiter to ask for more coffee. 她套住一个路过的服务员，要求再来点咖啡。|近义词：rope, noose, snare|反义词: release, free|词性:noun, verb

Etymology: From Spanish *lazo*, from Latin *laqueus* ("noose, snare").
