electroscopic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 验电的；静电的 | Relating to or detected by an electroscope; indicating the presence of an electric charge. |  The electroscopic measurements showed a significant charge buildup. 验电测量显示电荷积聚显著。| Near synonyms: electrostatic, charged | Antonyms: uncharged, neutral | adjective

electroscopic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 可用验电器测量的 | Measurable by an electroscope. | The experiment demonstrated an electroscopic difference between the two materials.  这个实验证明了两种材料之间存在着用验电器可以测量的差异。 | Near synonyms: detectable, measurable | Antonyms: undetectable, immeasurable | adjective
