overbounteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəsnəs/ | n. | 过分慷慨，过度丰富 | The state of being excessively generous or abundant. |  His overbounteousness led him to donate most of his fortune to charity. 他的过度慷慨使他把大部分财产都捐给了慈善机构。 |  Overabundance, profusion, lavishness, excessiveness, extravagance |  Meanness, stinginess, parsimony, frugality | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + bounteous + -ness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much". "Bounteous" means "generous" or "abundant".  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
