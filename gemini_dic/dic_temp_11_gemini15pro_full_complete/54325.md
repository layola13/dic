disheartened:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
Having lost determination or confidence; discouraged.
The team was disheartened after losing the championship game.  球队在输掉冠军赛后感到沮丧。
He felt disheartened by the lack of progress. 他因缺乏进展而感到灰心。
近义词: discouraged, demoralized, dejected, downhearted
反义词: encouraged, heartened, optimistic, cheerful
词性: adjective
词源: From dis- + heartened, past participle of hearten (“to give courage to”).  由 dis- + hearten 的过去分词构成，hearten 意为“鼓励”。
