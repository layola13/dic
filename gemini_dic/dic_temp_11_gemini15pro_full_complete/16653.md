barracks: /ˈbærəks/ (US) /ˈbærəks/ (UK)| n. |兵营；营房|A building or group of buildings used to house soldiers.|The soldiers returned to their barracks after a long day of training. 士兵们经过一天的训练后回到了他们的营房。|
例句：
The barracks were old and overcrowded. 营房又旧又拥挤。
New barracks are being built to accommodate the increasing number of soldiers. 新兵营正在建造中，以容纳越来越多的士兵。|
近义词：quarters, accommodations, housing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "home" in a civilian context)|词性: noun

barracks: /ˈbærəks/ (US) /ˈbærəks/ (UK)| n. |(通常用作复数)临时住房|Temporary housing, often simple or crude.|The construction workers lived in barracks on the site. 建筑工人们住在工地上的临时住房里。|
例句：
The earthquake victims were housed in temporary barracks. 地震灾民被安置在临时营房里。
The barracks were basic but provided adequate shelter. 营房很简单，但提供了足够的住所。|
近义词：quarters, accommodations, lodging|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "home" or "hotel")|词性: noun
