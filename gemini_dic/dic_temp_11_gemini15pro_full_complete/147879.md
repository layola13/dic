overreactions:/ˌoʊvərriˈækʃənz/ | n. | 过度反应 | An instance of reacting too strongly or intensely to something. | The market's overreaction to the news caused a temporary dip in stock prices. 市场对新闻的过度反应导致股价暂时下跌。|  The media's overreaction to the incident blew it out of proportion. 媒体对这一事件的过度反应使其被夸大了。 | synonyms: overresponses, exaggerations | antonyms: underreactions, calmness | noun
