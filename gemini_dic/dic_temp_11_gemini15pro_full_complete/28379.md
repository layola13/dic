carbfix: /ˈkɑːrbfɪks/
noun
碳固定
A method of permanently storing carbon dioxide emissions by reacting them with basalt rock formations.
Carbfix is a promising technology for mitigating climate change. 碳固定是一项很有前景的缓解气候变化的技术。
Carbfix has successfully stored thousands of tons of CO2 underground. Carbfix 已经成功地将数千吨二氧化碳储存在地下。
synonyms: carbon capture and storage, CCS, mineral sequestration
antonyms: carbon emission, carbon release
