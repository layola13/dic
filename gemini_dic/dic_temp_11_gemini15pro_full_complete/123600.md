montego:/mɒnˈtiːɡoʊ/ (US), /mɒnˈtiːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|蒙特哥贝 (牙买加一城市)|A city in Jamaica known for its beaches and tourism.|We spent our honeymoon in Montego Bay. 我们在蒙特哥贝度蜜月。|近义词:resort town, tourist destination|反义词:rural area|词性:名词

Etymology: From the Spanish "manteca" (lard, butter), possibly referring to the export of lard from the area in earlier times.  词源：源自西班牙语“manteca”（猪油，黄油），可能指的是早期该地区猪油的出口。
