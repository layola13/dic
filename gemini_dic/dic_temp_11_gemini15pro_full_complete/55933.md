DIYs /ˌdiː aɪ ˈwaɪz/
n.
DIY projects; things you make or repair yourself
Do-it-yourself projects or activities.
例句：I spent the weekend working on some DIYs around the house. 我周末在家做了一些DIY项目。
例句：She sells her DIYs on Etsy. 她在Etsy上出售她的DIY作品。
近义词：crafts, hobbies, projects
反义词: professionally made items, store-bought items
词性: noun (plural)


DIY /ˌdiː aɪ ˈwaɪ/
n.  adj. adv.
Do-it-yourself
The activity of making, repairing, or decorating things yourself, instead of paying someone else to do it.
例句：I enjoy DIY projects like building shelves and painting furniture. 我喜欢DIY项目，比如搭建架子和粉刷家具。
例句：He's very good at DIY. 他非常擅长DIY。
近义词：self-made, homemade
反义词: professional, store-bought
词性: noun, adjective, adverb
