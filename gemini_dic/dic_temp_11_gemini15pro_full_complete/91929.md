hypermedia:/ˌhaɪpərˈmiːdiə/| n.|超媒体|Hypermedia is an extension of the term hypertext, which refers to text containing links to other text. Hypermedia, on the other hand, refers to a combination of text, graphics, audio, video, and other media formats linked together in a non-linear fashion, allowing users to navigate between different pieces of content by clicking on links or icons.  It's essentially a web of interconnected information that can be accessed non-sequentially.|The museum's exhibit used hypermedia to present information about ancient artifacts, combining text, images, and videos.  博物馆的展览使用超媒体来展示古代文物的信息，结合了文字、图像和视频。
