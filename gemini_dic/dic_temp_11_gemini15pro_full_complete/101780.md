irreversibilities:/ˌɪrɪˌvɜːrsəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
不可逆性（复数）
The quality or state of being irreversible; the fact that a process cannot be reversed.
例句：The irreversibilities of the aging process are a constant reminder of the passage of time.  衰老过程的不可逆性不断提醒着时间的流逝。

例句：The scientist discussed the thermodynamic irreversibilities in the system. 科学家讨论了系统中的热力学不可逆性。

近义词：irreversibleness, immutability, unchangeableness, permanence
反义词：reversibility, mutability, changeability
词性：n. (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From irreversible + -ity.
Irreversible: from in- (not) + reversible (from Latin revertere, to turn back).
