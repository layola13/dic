enviousness:/ˈɛnviəsnəs/| n.|羡慕，嫉妒|The state or feeling of being envious.  A resentful or unhappy feeling caused by wanting something that someone else has.|Her lavish lifestyle fueled the enviousness of her colleagues. 她的奢华生活方式激起了同事们的嫉妒。|近义词：jealousy, envy, covetousness|反义词：generosity, admiration, contentment|词性:noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from envious + -ness.
