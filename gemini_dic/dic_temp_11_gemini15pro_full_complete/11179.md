archil:/ˈɑːrkɪl/ | n. |地衣红，紫染料|A purplish-red dye obtained from various lichens, especially Roccella tinctoria. |The ancient Egyptians used archil to dye fabrics. 古埃及人用地衣红来染布。|Synonyms: orcein, cudbear|Antonyms: None|Noun


archil:/ˈɑːrkɪl/ | n. |染紫色的苔藓|Any of various lichens, especially Roccella tinctoria, from which the dye archil is obtained. |Archil was harvested from rocky coastlines. 地衣红是从岩石海岸线上采集的。|Synonyms: orseille, orchil|Antonyms: None|Noun
