epitaphists:/ˈɛpɪˌtæfɪsts/
n.
墓志铭撰写者；碑文作者
A person who writes epitaphs.
The epitaphists of the Victorian era often employed flowery language.  维多利亚时代的墓志铭撰写者经常使用华丽的语言。

近义词: inscription writers, engravers (for those who carve the epitaphs)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
