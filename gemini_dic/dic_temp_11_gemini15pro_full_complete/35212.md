cluckiest:/ˈklʌkiɪst/| adj.|最爱咯咯叫的；最母性的|This hen is the cluckiest of the bunch, always fussing over her chicks.| This describes a hen (or sometimes a person) who is excessively or demonstrably motherly, especially in a fussy or overprotective way.  It stems from the "clucking" sound hens make, particularly when caring for their chicks.|例句：She's the cluckiest mother I've ever met, constantly worrying about her children. 她是我见过的最爱操心的母亲，总是担心她的孩子们。

例句：The cluckiest hen in the coop kept her chicks tucked under her wings. 鸡舍里最母性的母鸡把小鸡们藏在翅膀下。|近义词：motherly, overprotective, fussy, doting|反义词：neglectful, indifferent|词性：adjective
