cognizant:/ˈkɒɡnɪzənt/ (US) /ˈkɑːɡnɪzənt/| adj.|知道的；认识到的；意识到的|Having knowledge or being aware of something.|The government is cognizant of the problem. 政府已经认识到这个问题了。|aware, conscious, mindful|ignorant, oblivious, unaware|adjective

cognizant:/ˈkɒɡnɪzənt/ (US) /ˈkɑːɡnɪzənt/| adj.|负责的，掌管的 (较少用)|Having responsibility or jurisdiction over something. |She was cognizant of the department's budget. 她负责该部门的预算。|responsible for, in charge of|not responsible for|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cognizant-, present participle stem of *cognizāre "to get to know," from Latin cognōscere "to get to know, learn," from com- "together" + gnōscere "to know."
