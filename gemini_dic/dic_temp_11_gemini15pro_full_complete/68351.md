fallaciously: /fəˈleɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 谬误地，不合理地 |  In a fallacious manner; based on a fallacy.  Characterized by or containing a fallacy; logically unsound. | He argued fallaciously, basing his conclusion on a false premise. 他谬误地进行辩论，其结论建立在一个错误的前提上。| The politician fallaciously claimed that the new tax would not affect the poor.  这位政客错误地声称新税不会影响穷人。| synonym: erroneously, misleadingly, falsely, deceptively | antonym: correctly, accurately, validly, truthfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin fallaciosus, from Latin fallacia (see fallacy).
