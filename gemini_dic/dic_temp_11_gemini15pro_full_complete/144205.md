outsinging:/ˌaʊtˈsɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的歌声; 比…唱得好
to sing better than someone else
例句：She easily outsang all the other contestants. 她轻松地胜过所有其他参赛选手。

例句：He outsang everyone in the choir. 他的歌声在唱诗班里无人能及。

近义词：surpass, outdo, excel
反义词：be outsang

词性：v.
