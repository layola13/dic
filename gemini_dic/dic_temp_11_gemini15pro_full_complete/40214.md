/wɒt/ |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么，|Used to ask for information about something.|What did you say? 你说什么了？|Which, how|Nothing (as a direct antonym, "what" doesn't have a true opposite)|pronoun, determiner, adverb

Etymology: From Old English *hwæt*, neuter of *hwā* ("who").  Cognate with German *was*, Dutch *wat*.

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done to his hair? = 他对他的头发做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has she been reading lately?  = 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she said before the meeting started? = 会议开始前她说了什么？


Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？


Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？


Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will she have been working on for so long? = 到那时她将已经做了那么长时间什么了？


Interrogative:
What is the meaning of life? = 生命的意义是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末的计划是什么？

Negative:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 他不明白我在说什么。
