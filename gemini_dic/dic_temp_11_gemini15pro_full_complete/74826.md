frenetic:/frəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|疯狂的，狂热的，激动暴乱的|Characterized by fast and disordered activity; frantic.|The frenetic pace of modern life can be overwhelming. 现代生活的快节奏可能让人难以承受。|The atmosphere in the trading room was frenetic. 交易室里的气氛十分狂热。|近义词：frantic, frenzied, hectic, wild|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *freneticus, from Latin phreneticus (“delirious, frantic”), from Ancient Greek φρενιτικός (phrenitikós), from φρήν (phrḗn, “mind, heart, diaphragm”). Doublet of frantic.
