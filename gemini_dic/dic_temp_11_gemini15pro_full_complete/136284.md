nonreprehensible:/ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbl/
adjective
无可指责的; 无可非议的
Not deserving of blame or censure.  Free from fault or blame.
His conduct throughout the entire affair has been nonreprehensible.  他在整个事件中的行为无可指责。
Her actions, though unconventional, were entirely nonreprehensible. 她的行为虽然不落俗套，但完全无可非议。

近义词: blameless, irreproachable, unimpeachable, faultless, impeccable
反义词: reprehensible, blameworthy, culpable, censurable

词性: adjective
词源:  From non- (not) + reprehensible (deserving censure). Reprehensible comes from Late Latin reprehens- , past participle stem of reprehendere ‘reprove’, from re- ‘back, again’ + prehendere ‘seize’.
