nouns:/naʊnz/| n. |名词|A word used to identify any of a class of people, places, or things ( common nouns ), or to name a particular one of these ( proper nouns ).|例句： "Cat," "tree," and "house" are common nouns.  "猫"、"树"和"房子"是普通名词。

例句： "London," "Mary," and "Microsoft" are proper nouns. "伦敦"、"玛丽"和"微软"是专有名词。|近义词： names, appellations|反义词:  None (Nouns don't have direct opposites.)|词性: Noun
