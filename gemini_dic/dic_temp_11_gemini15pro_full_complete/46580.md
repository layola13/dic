damageableness:/ˈdæmɪdʒəbəlnəs/| n. | 可损害性；易受损性 | The damageableness of the ecosystem is a serious concern.  The vulnerability or susceptibility to being damaged. | 例句：The damageableness of the coastal ecosystem to rising sea levels is alarming.  沿海生态系统对海平面上升的脆弱性令人担忧。

例句：The damageableness of the old bridge led to its closure. 老桥的易损性导致其关闭。 | 近义词：vulnerability, fragility, susceptibility, weakness | 反义词：durability, resilience, robustness, strength | 词性: 名词

词源:  From damageable + -ness.  Damageable comes from damage + -able. Damage ultimately derives from the Old French word *damager*, meaning "to inflict loss or injury upon."
