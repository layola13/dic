bailage:/ˈbeɪlɪdʒ/| n.|保释金，保证金；保释责任；保释期|The act of giving bail for another person or oneself.  A pledge or surety given to liberate a person from custody, and to secure his appearance when required. The state of being released on bail. The district under the jurisdiction of a bailiff.|He was released on £5,000 bailage.|近义词：bail, surety, security|反义词: imprisonment, incarceration|noun
