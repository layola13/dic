achromatize:/eɪˈkroʊmətaɪz/ |v. | 使脱色；使消色差|To remove color from; to make achromatic. |The photographer achromatized the image to create a vintage effect. 摄影师对图像进行了消色差处理，以营造复古效果。|Synonyms: decolorize, bleach, neutralize |Antonyms: colorize, tint, dye|verb
achromatic:/ˌeɪkrəˈmætɪk/ |adj. | 无色的；消色差的|Without color; transmitting light without separating it into constituent colors. |The achromatic lens produced a clear image. 消色差透镜产生了清晰的图像。|Synonyms: colorless, neutral, monochrome |Antonyms: chromatic, colorful, polychromatic|adjective
