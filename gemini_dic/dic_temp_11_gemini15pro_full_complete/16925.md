basos:/ˈbeɪsoʊs/| n. |嗜碱性粒细胞|A type of white blood cell that is part of the body's immune system.  Basophils release histamine and other chemicals that contribute to allergic reactions. |  High basophil levels were found in his blood test, suggesting an allergic reaction. 他的血液检查发现嗜碱性粒细胞水平很高，表明有过敏反应。 |Synonyms: basophilic granulocyte | Antonyms: N/A | noun
