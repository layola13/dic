multiracialist: /ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəlɪst/
n.|多种族主义者；多种族融合拥护者|A person who believes in or advocates for multiracialism, the acceptance and promotion of societies with people of diverse racial backgrounds.|She considers herself a multiracialist, embracing the richness of all cultures.|近义词: integrationist, pluralist|反义词: segregationist, racist|词性: 名词

multiracialist: /ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəlɪst/
adj.|多种族主义的；多种族融合的|Relating to or characterized by multiracialism.|The organization promotes a multiracialist vision of society.|近义词: integrated, diverse|反义词: segregated, homogenous|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "many, much"
racial (adjective): from race + -ial (suffix forming adjectives)
-ist (suffix): forms agent nouns or adjectives from verbs or other nouns
