monty:/ˈmɒnti/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnti/ (US)| n. |蒙蒂，(通常指)男性名字；(非正式)胡说八道；废话|A common male name, often short for Montgomery. Also informally used to mean nonsense or rubbish. |例句：His name is Monty. 他的名字是蒙蒂。

例句：That's a load of monty! 那是一派胡言！

近义词：(for name) Montgomery, (for nonsense) rubbish, nonsense, baloney,  hogwash|反义词: (for nonsense) sense, truth|词性:noun
