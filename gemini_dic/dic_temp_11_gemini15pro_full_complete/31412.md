chargrilled:/ˈtʃɑːrɡrɪld/
adjective
烤的; 炭烤的
cooked over or under a very strong heat, grilling
例句：We had some delicious chargrilled vegetables. 我们吃了一些美味的烤蔬菜。

例句：The chargrilled steak was perfectly cooked.  炭烤牛排做得恰到好处。

近义词：grilled, broiled, barbecued
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly boiled, steamed)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From char (to burn or scorch) + grilled (cooked on a grill)
