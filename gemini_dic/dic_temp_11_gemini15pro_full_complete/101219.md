Characterized by a blend of Germanic and Roman influences, especially in art and architecture. | The church features some beautiful invisigothic arches. 这座教堂有一些美丽的西哥特式拱门。| Near synonyms: Visigothic, Germanic, post-Roman  | Antonyms: modern, contemporary | adjective
