filching:/ˈfɪltʃɪŋ/
verb
偷窃，窃取
The act of stealing something, typically of small value, in a sneaky or furtive way.
He was caught filching candy from the store. 他被抓到从商店里偷糖果。
synonyms: stealing, pilfering, purloining, pinching, swiping
antonyms: returning, giving, donating
v.
