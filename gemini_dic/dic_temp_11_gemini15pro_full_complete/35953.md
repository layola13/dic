code-names:/ˈkoʊdˌneɪmz/ |n.|代号，代码名称|A system of words or other symbols substituted for real names, especially to ensure secrecy.  Used for spies, secret operations, or products under development.  Derived from "code" (system of signals) and "names" (identifiers).|例句：The spies used code-names to protect their identities.  间谍使用代号来保护他们的身份。

例句：The project's code-name was "Aurora."  这个项目的代号是“极光”。|近义词：aliases, codenames, pseudonyms|反义词：real names, true identities|词性:noun
