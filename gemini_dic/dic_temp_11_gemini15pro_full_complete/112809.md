lucernal:/luːˈsɜːrnl/| adj.|灯的；照明的；像灯笼的|Of or relating to a lucerne or lamp.  Pertaining to, or resembling, a lantern.  |The lucernal glow of the streetlights illuminated the foggy night. 街灯像灯笼一样的光芒照亮了雾蒙蒙的夜晚。|The lucernal chamber housed a single, flickering candle. 灯室里只有一支闪烁的蜡烛。|Synonyms: lamplike, lantern-like|Antonyms: dark, unlit|adjective
