indigestibly:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstɪbli/ | adv. | 难以消化地；难理解地 | in a way that is difficult or impossible to digest; in a way that is difficult to understand or assimilate. | The textbook was written so indigestibly that the students struggled to comprehend the material. 这本教科书写得很难理解，学生们很难理解其中的内容。 |  The dense legal jargon made the contract indigestibly complex. 这些密集的法律术语使合同复杂得难以理解。 | 近义词: incomprehensibly, unintelligibly | 反义词: digestibly, clearly, comprehensibly | 词性: 副词
