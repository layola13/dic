anointed:/əˈnɔɪntɪd/
adjective
受膏的，被涂油的
Consecrated with oil; chosen by or as if by divine intervention.
The anointed king was crowned in a lavish ceremony.  受膏的国王在盛大的仪式中加冕。
The team's anointed leader struggled to inspire his players.  这个团队指定的领导者难以激励他的球员。
Near Synonyms: chosen, designated, selected, appointed, ordained
Antonyms: unchosen, rejected, common, ordinary
Word type: adjective

anointed:/əˈnɔɪntɪd/
noun
受膏者，被选中的人
A person who has been anointed, especially a king or priest.
He considered himself the anointed, destined to lead the people. 他认为自己是天选之人，注定要领导人民。
The anointed gathered for the sacred ritual. 受膏者聚集在一起进行神圣的仪式。
Near Synonyms: chosen one, elect, designated leader, appointed one
Antonyms: outcast, rejected, commoner
Word type: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *anoynten*, from Old French *enoindre* "to smear with oil, anoint", from Latin *inungō*, from *in-* + *unguō* "to anoint".
