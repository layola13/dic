overmerited: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪtɪd/
adjective
过分褒奖的，名不副实的
Having received more praise or reward than is deserved.
His overmerited promotion caused resentment among his colleagues. 他的晋升过分褒奖，引起了同事们的不满。

近义词: overpraised, overrated, undeserved
反义词: underappreciated, undervalued, merited, deserved
词性: adjective
