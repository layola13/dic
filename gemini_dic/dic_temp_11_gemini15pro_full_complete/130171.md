non-revocation:/ˌnɒnˌrɛvəˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|不可撤销，不可废除|The non-revocation of the certificate is crucial for the security of the system.  该证书的不可撤销性对于系统的安全至关重要。|The contract guaranteed the non-revocation of the license. 合同保证了许可证的不可撤销性。|irrevocability, non-cancellation|revocation, cancellation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + revocation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "revocation" comes from the Latin word "revocatio," meaning "a calling back."
