foresty:/ˈfɒrɪstri/ (US) /ˈfɔːrɪstri/ (UK)| n. |林学；森林管理；林业|The science, art, and practice of managing and using forests and their resources.  It encompasses a wide range of activities, including planting, cultivating, harvesting, and conserving trees and forest ecosystems.|
例句：He studied forestry at university. 他在大学学习林学。

例句：Sustainable forestry practices are crucial for the health of our planet. 可持续的林业实践对我们星球的健康至关重要。

近义词：silviculture, arboriculture, forest management|
反义词:deforestation|
词性:noun|
词源:From French *foresterie*, from forest. Ultimately from Late Latin forestis "out of doors".|
