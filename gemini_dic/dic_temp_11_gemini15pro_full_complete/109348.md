leninites: /ˈlɛnɪnaɪts/
| n. | 列宁主义者；列宁主义拥护者 | A follower or supporter of Leninism, the political and economic theories of Vladimir Lenin. |  The leninites believed in a centralized, one-party state.  列宁主义者相信集权的一党制国家。| synonyms: communist, Bolshevik, Marxist-Leninist | antonyms: capitalist, anti-communist | noun
