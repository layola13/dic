nonuisance:/ˈnʌnˌjuːsəns/ |n.| 无公害；不构成妨害；不构成滋扰|Something that is not a nuisance; something that does not cause trouble or annoyance. | The new factory is designed to be a nonuisance to the environment.  这家新工厂的设计宗旨是不会对环境造成危害。|The quiet operation of the new air conditioner makes it a real nonuisance. 新空调运行安静，真正做到了无扰。 | 近义词: benefit, advantage | 反义词: nuisance, annoyance | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "nuisance" (from Old French "noisance," meaning "harm" or "injury").
