overfreedoms:/ˌoʊvərˈfriːdəmz/
|n.| 过度的自由|An excess of freedoms, often leading to negative consequences due to lack of structure or regulation.|The concept of overfreedoms in modern society is a complex one, with some arguing it leads to societal breakdown. 现代社会中过度自由的概念是复杂的，有些人认为它会导致社会崩溃。|近义词: excess of liberties, unrestrained freedoms|反义词: restrictions, limitations|词性:名词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "freedom" (Middle English fredom, from Old English frēodōm "freedom, liberation," from frēo "free" + -dōm "-dom").  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or surplus.
