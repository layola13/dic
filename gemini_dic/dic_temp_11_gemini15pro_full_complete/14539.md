averroist:/əˈvɛroʊɪst/ | n. adj. | 阿威罗伊主义者；阿威罗伊派的 | A follower of Averroes (Ibn Rushd), a 12th-century Spanish-Arab philosopher who sought to reconcile Aristotelian philosophy with Islamic theology. Averroists held, among other things, the doctrine of the unity of the intellect, which implied that individual human intellects are merely temporary manifestations of a single, universal intellect. |  He was accused of being an Averroist, a charge that carried serious implications in the medieval world. 他被指控为阿威罗伊主义者，这一指控在中世纪世界具有严重的影响。|  Averroist doctrines were condemned by the Church. 阿威罗伊主义的教义被教会谴责。| 近义词: Aristotelian, scholastic | 反义词: Thomist | 词性: 名词, 形容词
