aberration: /ˌæbəˈreɪʃn/ (US) /ˌæbəˈreɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |异常，偏差，失常|A departure from what is normal, usual, or expected, typically an unwelcome one.|例句：His outburst was an aberration from his normally calm demeanor. 他的爆发是他通常冷静举止的一次失常。|近义词：anomaly, deviation, irregularity, abnormality|反义词：norm, normalcy, regularity|词性: 名词

aberration: /ˌæbəˈreɪʃn/ (US) /ˌæbəˈreɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |(光学)像差|A defect in a lens or mirror that causes light rays to deviate from their intended path, resulting in a distorted image.|例句：The telescope's lens had an aberration that blurred the images. 望远镜的镜头有像差，使图像模糊。|近义词：distortion, defect|反义词: clarity, precision|词性: 名词
