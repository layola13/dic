generating:/ˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. v.|产生的，生成的; 发电的|Producing something; creating something; or relating to the production of electricity.|The generating station provides power to the entire city.  发电站为整个城市供电。| synonyms: producing, creating, originating, developing | antonyms: destroying, eliminating, consuming, extinguishing |词性:  adjective, present participle of generate

generating:/ˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | v.|产生，发生; 引起|To produce or cause something to exist.|The solar panels are generating electricity. 太阳能电池板正在发电。| synonyms: producing, creating, causing, originating | antonyms: destroying, eliminating, preventing, hindering |词性: verb, present participle of generate


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *generātus*, past participle of generāre (“to beget, produce”), from genus (“birth, race, stock, kind”).
