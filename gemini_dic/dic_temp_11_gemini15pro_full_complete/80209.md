godmothers:/ˈɡɒdmʌðəz/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdmʌðərz/ (US)| n. |教母们|A woman who sponsors a child at baptism.  A female equivalent to a godfather.  Often used in the plural to refer to multiple godmothers.|My daughter has three godmothers. 我的女儿有三个教母。|My godmothers gave me a beautiful necklace for my christening. 我的教母们在我受洗时送了我一条漂亮的项链。|近义词：female sponsors, mentors (in a broader sense)|反义词: N/A|noun
