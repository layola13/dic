misbefitting:/ˌmɪsbɪˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj.|不合适的，不相称的|Not suitable or appropriate; ill-fitting.  It describes something that is inappropriate or unsuitable for a particular person, occasion, or situation.  It often implies a mismatch between the item and the context in which it is used.|Her casual clothes were misbefitting for such a formal occasion. 她休闲的服装在这种正式场合下很不合适。|The king wore a misbefitting crown, too small for his head. 国王戴着一顶不合适的王冠，对他来说太小了。|近义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, unbecoming, incongruous|反义词：appropriate, suitable, fitting, apt|词性:形容词
