enterorrhaphy: /ˌɛntəˈrɒrəfi/
n.
肠缝合术
The surgical suturing of a wound or tear in the intestines.
The patient underwent an enterorrhaphy after suffering a ruptured bowel.  病人肠破裂后接受了肠缝合术。

Etymology:  From entero- (intestine) + -rhaphy (suturing).  源自 entero-（肠）+ -rhaphy（缝合）。

Synonyms: intestinal suture, bowel repair
Antonyms:  enterotomy (incision into the intestine)
