jay:/dʒeɪ/| n.|樫鳥，松鴉；喋喋不休的人|A brightly colored bird, typically with blue markings, found in most parts of the world.|Example: The jay flew from branch to branch, its bright blue feathers flashing in the sunlight.  松鴉在樹枝間飛來飛去，它亮藍色的羽毛在陽光下閃爍。

Example: He's such a jay, always chattering about nothing important. 他真是個喋喋不休的人，總是嘮叨些無關緊要的事情。|近义詞：blue jay, chatterbox|反义詞: (No direct antonym, perhaps "quiet person" for the figurative meaning)|Noun
