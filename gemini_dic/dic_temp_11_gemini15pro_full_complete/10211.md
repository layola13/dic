apostle:/əˈpɒs(ə)l/ (US: /əˈpɑːs(ə)l/)| n. |使徒；倡导者；先驱|A person sent on a mission, especially one of the twelve disciples sent out by Jesus to preach the gospel; a vigorous and pioneering advocate of a particular cause or policy.|例句：The apostles spread Christianity throughout the world. 使徒们将基督教传播到世界各地。

例句：He is considered an apostle of free speech. 他被认为是言论自由的倡导者。|近义词：disciple, follower, advocate, champion, pioneer|反义词：opponent, enemy|词性: 名词
