nomadism: /ˈnoʊmæˌdɪzəm/ | n. | 游牧；流浪；游牧生活 | The way of life of people who travel from place to place rather than living in one place all the time. |  The documentary explored the challenges of nomadism in the modern world.  这部纪录片探讨了现代世界游牧生活所面临的挑战。 |  nomadic lifestyle, wandering, migration, itinerancy | sedentism, settled life | noun

nomadic: /noʊˈmædɪk/ | adj. | 游牧的；流浪的 |  Relating to nomads or their way of life; wandering. |  The nomadic tribes moved their herds to fresh pastures each season.  游牧部落每个季节都会把他们的牲畜迁移到新的牧场。| migratory, itinerant, wandering, roving | settled, sedentary, stationary | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nomadisme*, from Ancient Greek *νομάς* (nomás, “roaming, wandering”) + *-ισμός* (-ismós).
