cholera:/ˈkɑːlərə/ | n. |霍乱|An acute intestinal infection caused by ingestion of contaminated water or food, characterized by profuse watery diarrhea, vomiting, and muscle cramps.|例句：Cholera is a serious disease that can be fatal. 霍乱是一种严重的疾病，可能会致命。

例句：The cholera outbreak spread rapidly through the refugee camp. 霍乱疫情在难民营迅速蔓延。|近义词：dysentery, gastroenteritis|反义词: health, wellness |词性:noun
