definitional:/ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃənəl/| adj.|定义的，限定的|Of or relating to a definition; serving to define or specify something.  Characterized by or dependent on definition.|The definitional features of a democracy include free and fair elections. 民主制度的定义性特征包括自由公正的选举。|synonyms:  defining, specifying, descriptive, explanatory|antonyms: ambiguous, unclear, vague|adjective
