hysteron proteron:/ˌhɪstərɒn ˈprɒtərɒn/ |n.| 后置先行，逆序法 |A rhetorical device in which the logical order of events is reversed.  A figure of speech in which what naturally comes last is put first. |The phrase "put on your shoes and socks" is an example of hysteron proteron.  穿上你的鞋子和袜子（这是一种后置先行的用法）. | 近义词: inversion, anastrophe | 反义词: natural order | noun
