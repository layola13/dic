capistrate:/ˈkæpɪstreɪt/| adj.|  被束缚的，被控制的 |  Bound or restrained, especially with a halter or muzzle.  Having a headstall. | The farmer capistrated the unruly bull.  农民用笼头控制住了那头不听话的公牛。 |Synonyms: restrained, muzzled, controlled, curbed | Antonyms: unrestrained, free, liberated | adjective

capistrate:/ˈkæpɪstreɪt/| v.|  束缚，控制(尤指用笼头或口套) | To restrain or control, especially with a halter or muzzle. To put a headstall on. | They capistrated the dog to prevent it from biting.  他们用口套控制住那只狗，防止它咬人。 |Synonyms: restrain, muzzle, control, curb | Antonyms: unleash, free, liberate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capistratus, past participle of capistrare (“to halter, muzzle”), from Latin capistrum (“halter, muzzle”), from caput (“head”).
