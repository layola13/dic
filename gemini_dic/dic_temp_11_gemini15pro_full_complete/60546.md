elaoptene:/ɪˈleɪɒptiːn/ | n. |香油;液态烃|The liquid portion of an essential oil, as distinguished from the stearoptene. |The elaoptene of rose oil is highly valued in perfumery. 玫瑰油中的香油在香水制造业中备受推崇。|Synonyms: liquid component, essential oil fraction |Antonyms: stearoptene| noun
