moodle:/ˈmuːdl/| n.| 模块化对象导向动态学习环境 (Modular Object-Oriented Dynamic Learning Environment), 心情, 情绪 |A learning platform designed to provide educators, administrators and learners with a single robust, secure and integrated system to create personalised learning environments.  Also, a verb meaning "to be in a thoughtful or meditative state".|例句：The university uses Moodle for all its online courses. 这所大学所有在线课程都使用 Moodle。|近义词：platform, LMS (Learning Management System), contemplate, ponder|反义词: (For the LMS meaning) None. (For the verb meaning) act, decide |词性:noun, verb


moodle:/ˈmuːdl/| v.| 沉思, 冥想 |To think or meditate in a quiet, contemplative way. |例句：He moodled over the problem for hours. 他对着这个问题沉思了几个小时。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, muse, reflect|反义词: act, decide |词性:verb
