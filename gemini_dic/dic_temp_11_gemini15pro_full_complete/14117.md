authenticity:/ˌɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪti/ /ˌɑːθɛnˈtɪsɪti/
n.|真实性；可靠性|The painting was tested for authenticity. 这幅画被测试了真伪。|例句：The museum guarantees the authenticity of all its exhibits.  博物馆保证其所有展品的真实性。|近义词：genuineness, validity, credibility|反义词：falseness, inauthenticity|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin authenticitas, from authenticus ‘authoritative’.
