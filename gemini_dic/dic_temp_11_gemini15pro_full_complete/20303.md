bisects:/baɪˈsekts/ /bɑɪˈsekts/| v. |二等分，平分|divides something into two equal parts|The line bisects the angle. 这条线平分这个角。|近义词：divide, halve|反义词: join, unite|动词

bisect:/baɪˈsekt/ /bɑɪˈsekts/| n. |平分线|A line segment that divides another line segment into two equal parts|The perpendicular bisector of a line segment passes through its midpoint.  线段的垂直平分线过其中点。|近义词：divider, separator|反义词: connector, uniter|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bisecare*, from bi- (“two”) + secare (“to cut”).
