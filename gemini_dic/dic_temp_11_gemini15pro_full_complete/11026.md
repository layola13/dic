arcaded:/ɑːrˈkeɪdɪd/
adj.
拱廊式的；有拱廊的；
Having arches or arcades.
The grand hall was arcaded and spacious, filled with natural light.  宏伟的大厅是拱廊式的，很宽敞，充满了自然光。
The street was lined with arcaded shops, providing shade from the sun.  街道两旁林立着拱廊式的商店，遮挡着阳光。
synonyms: arched, vaulted, covered
antonyms: open, exposed

Etymology:
From French *arcadé*, past participle of *arcader* "to provide with arcades," from *arcade* "arcade," from Italian *arcata* "arch, arcade," from *arco* "arch," from Latin *arcus*.
