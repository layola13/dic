antitakeovers:/ˌæntiˈteɪkoʊvərz/
noun
反收购
measures taken by a company to prevent another company from taking it over.
The company adopted several antitakeovers measures to protect itself from hostile bids.  这家公司采取了几项反收购措施来保护自己免受恶意收购。
synonyms: anti-acquisition, takeover defense
antonyms: acquisition, merger
