delegated:/ˈdɛlɪɡeɪtɪd/| adj.|委派的，授权的|Having been given the responsibility or authority to act on behalf of another person or group. |The manager delegated the task of scheduling meetings to his assistant. 经理把安排会议的任务委托给了他的助手。|Synonyms: assigned, entrusted, commissioned|Antonyms: retained, withheld, assumed|Adjective


delegated:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪt/| v.|委派，授权|To give (control, responsibility, authority, etc.) to another person or group. |He delegated the task to his assistant. 他把任务委托给了他的助手。|Synonyms: assign, entrust, commission|Antonyms: retain, withhold, assume|Verb
