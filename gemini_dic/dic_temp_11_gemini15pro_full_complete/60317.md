ego-tripping:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 自我陶醉；自吹自擂 | An act of indulging in self-centered or boastful behavior, often focused on one's own perceived importance or coolness. | He went on an ego trip after winning the award, bragging about it to everyone. 他获奖后就开始自我陶醉，逢人就吹嘘。 |  近义词：boasting, bragging, showing off, self-aggrandizement | 反义词：humility, modesty | 词性: 名词

ego-tripping:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpɪŋ/ | adj. |  自我陶醉的；自吹自擂的 | Characterized by self-centered or boastful behavior.  |  His ego-tripping behavior alienated his friends. 他自吹自擂的行为疏远了他的朋友们。| 近义词：boastful, bragging, narcissistic | 反义词：humble, modest | 词性: 形容词
