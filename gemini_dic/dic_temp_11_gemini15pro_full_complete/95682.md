inconclusively:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
不确定地，无结果地
without a clear result or conclusion
The investigation ended inconclusively. 调查最终没有定论。
The experiment ended inconclusively, providing no clear answers.  实验无结果地结束，没有提供明确的答案。
Near synonyms: indecisively, uncertainly, ambiguously
Near antonyms: conclusively, decisively, definitely
词性: 副词
