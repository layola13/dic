come-up-with:/kʌm ˈʌp wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 想出，提出，提供 | To produce or find something, such as an idea, a plan, or a sum of money. |  I need to come up with a solution to this problem. 我需要想出一个解决这个问题的办法。

|  She came up with a brilliant idea for the new marketing campaign. 她为新的营销活动想出了一个绝妙的主意。 | 近义词：devise, conceive, formulate, generate | 反义词：forget, overlook | 词性：动词短语
