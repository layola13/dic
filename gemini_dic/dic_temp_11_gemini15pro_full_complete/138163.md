northward:/ˈnɔːrθwərd/ (US) /ˈnɔːθwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|向北的，朝北地|Toward the north.  Moving or situated in a northerly direction.|例句：The birds flew northward in the autumn. 秋天鸟儿向北飞去。|近义词：north, northerly|反义词:southward|词性:形容词, 副词

northward:/ˈnɔːrθwərd/ (US) /ˈnɔːθwəd/ (UK)| n.|北方|The direction or region to the north.|例句：They journeyed into the northward. 他们向北方旅行。|近义词：north|反义词:south|词性:名词
