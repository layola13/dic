doughtily:/ˈdaʊtɪli/| adv.|勇敢地，坚决地|In a brave and resolute manner; courageously and persistently.|The knights fought doughtily against the dragon. 骑士们勇敢地与巨龙搏斗。|近义词:bravely, valiantly, courageously, resolutely, stoutly, fearlessly|反义词:cowardly, timidly, fearfully|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doughteli, from Old English *dēahtiglīce (“bravely, valiantly”), from dēahtig (“doughty, valiant, strong, powerful”) +‎ -līce (adverbial suffix).  The Old English adjective is from Proto-Germanic *dauhtijaz (“suitable, fitting, useful”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰew- (“to be suitable, to be useful, to be proper”).
