fruiting:/ˈfruːtɪŋ/| adj. n.|结果实的，果期; 结果实|The fruiting bodies of mushrooms are often the only visible part of the organism.  蘑菇的子实体通常是该生物唯一可见的部分。|
The apple tree is fruiting heavily this year. 今年这棵苹果树结果很多。|近义词: ripening, maturing, bearing fruit|反义词: barren, sterile|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From the verb "fruit," which comes from Old French "fruit" (fruit, profit, enjoyment), ultimately from Latin "fructus" (fruit, enjoyment, profit). The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, which can function as an adjective or noun.
