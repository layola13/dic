bicrescentic: /ˌbaɪkrɪˈsɛntɪk/
adjective
新月形的；双新月形的
having the shape of two crescents placed back to back or joined at the points.
The bicrescentic shape of the amulet was believed to ward off evil spirits.  护身符的新月形被认为可以避邪。
The architect incorporated a bicrescentic design into the building's facade.  建筑师将双新月形设计融入建筑的立面。
Near synonyms: crescent-shaped, lunate, half-moon shaped
Antonyms: circular, round, spherical
word type: adjective
