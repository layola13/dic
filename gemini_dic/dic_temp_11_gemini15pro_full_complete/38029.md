component:/kəmˈpoʊnənt/ /kəmˈpəʊnənt/| n. adj.|组成部分，成分；部件，元件|A part or element of a larger whole, especially a machine or system.|例句：The engine is a vital component of the car. 引擎是汽车的重要组成部分。|近义词：element, part, ingredient, constituent, factor|反义词：whole, entirety|noun

component:/kəmˈpoʊnənt/ /kəmˈpəʊnənt/| adj.|组成的，构成的|Forming part of a larger whole; constituent.|例句：The component parts of the machine are very intricate. 这台机器的组成部件非常复杂。|近义词：constituent, integral|反义词：separate, independent|adjective
