departable:/dɪˈpɑːrtəbl/
adjective
可离开的，可出发的
able to depart; leaving
The flight is departable at 3 pm.  这趟航班下午3点起飞。
The train is departable from platform 4. 火车将从4号站台出发。
近义词:leaving, outgoing
反义词:arriving, incoming
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From depart + -able.  Depart comes from Old French departir, from Latin dispartire "to divide, distribute, separate," from dis- "apart" + partire "to divide."  The suffix -able forms adjectives meaning "capable of being."
