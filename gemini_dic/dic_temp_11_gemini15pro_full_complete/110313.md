lightable:/ˈlaɪtəbl/
adjective
可点燃的;可照亮的
capable of being lit; able to be illuminated.
The candles are lightable even after being dropped in water. 即使蜡烛掉进水里了，也是可以点燃的。
The room is lightable with just one lamp. 只需一盏灯就能照亮这个房间。
近义词: flammable, ignitable, illuminable
反义词: nonflammable, unlightable
词性:adjective

词源:  From "light" (verb, meaning to ignite or illuminate) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being"). The suffix "-able" is derived from the Old French "-able" and ultimately from the Latin "-abilis".
