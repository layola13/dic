blitzkrieging:/ˈblɪtsˌkriːɡɪŋ/
v.
闪电战，闪电式攻击
Engaging in a swift and overwhelming military offensive, resembling a blitzkrieg.
The army was blitzkrieging through the enemy territory.  军队正以闪电战的方式穿过敌人的领土。
The company blitzkrieged the market with their new product. 公司以新产品闪电式地攻击市场。
近义词:attacking, assaulting, overwhelming
反义词:defending, retreating, withdrawing
词性:动词(v.)

blitzkrieg:/ˈblɪtsˌkriːɡ/
n.
闪电战
A swift, intense military offensive, designed to achieve a quick victory.
The blitzkrieg was successful in capturing the capital city. 闪电战成功地占领了首都。
The marketing team launched a blitzkrieg of advertising.  营销团队发动了一场广告闪电战。
近义词:offensive, assault, attack
反义词:defense, retreat, withdrawal
词性:名词(n.)

词源: From German Blitzkrieg, from Blitz ‘lightning’ + Krieg ‘war’.  来自德语 Blitzkrieg，由 Blitz “闪电” + Krieg “战争”组成。
