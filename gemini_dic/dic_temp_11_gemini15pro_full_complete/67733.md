eye:/aɪ/ | n. | 眼睛；视力；眼光；见解 | An organ of sight.  The ability to see. A way of looking at something. | She has beautiful blue eyes. 她有一双美丽的蓝眼睛。

He has a good eye for detail. 他很注重细节。| synonyms: sight, vision, gaze, view | antonyms: blindness | noun

eye:/aɪ/ | v. | 注视；审视；细看 | To look at someone or something carefully. | He eyed the cake hungrily. 他饥饿地盯着蛋糕。

The security guard eyed the suspicious package. 保安人员仔细查看了可疑包裹。| synonyms: watch, observe, scrutinize, gaze | antonyms: ignore, overlook | verb
