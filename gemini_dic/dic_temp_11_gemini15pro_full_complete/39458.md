conner:/ˈkɒnər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnər/ (US)| n. | 康纳（男子名）;角落; 锥形物 |A male given name, a variant of Connor. Also can refer to a corner, or a cone-shaped object (though this usage is less common).|例句：Conner is a popular name in Ireland. 康纳在爱尔兰是一个很受欢迎的名字。

例句：He bumped into the conner of the table. 他撞到了桌角。|近义词：corner (for the meaning of a corner), cone (for the cone-shaped object)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a name) center (opposite of corner) |词性:Noun
