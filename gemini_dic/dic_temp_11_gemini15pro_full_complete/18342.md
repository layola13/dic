belligerent: /bəˈlɪdʒərənt/ | adj. n. | 好战的；交战的；交战国 | inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.  A nation or person engaged in war or conflict. |  例句：His belligerent attitude made it difficult to work with him.  他好斗的态度使人很难与他共事。| 近义词：aggressive, hostile, warring, combative | 反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable | 词性：adjective, noun

belligerent: /bəˈlɪdʒərənt/ | n. | 交战国；交战者 | a nation or person engaged in war or conflict. |  例句：The belligerents finally agreed to a ceasefire. 交战双方最终同意停火。| 近义词：combatant, adversary, opponent | 反义词：ally, friend | 词性：noun
