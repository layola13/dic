decimation:/ˌdesɪˈmeɪʃn/
n.
大批杀害，严重毁坏
The decimation of the population by the plague. 瘟疫对人口的大批杀害。
The hurricane caused widespread decimation of the coastal towns. 飓风对沿海城镇造成了大范围的破坏。
destruction, devastation, ruin
preservation, protection
noun

decimation:/ˌdesɪˈmeɪʃn/
n.
抽杀十分之一，大幅度削减
The decimation of the army's ranks by disease. 疾病使军队的人数大幅减少。
The company faced decimation of its profits due to the recession. 由于经济衰退，公司面临利润大幅下降。
reduction, decrease, curtailment
increase, growth
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *decimatio*, from decem (“ten”). Originally referred to the Roman military practice of killing every tenth man in a mutinous legion.
