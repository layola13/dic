marconigram:/ˌmɑːrkəˈnɪɡræm/ | n. | 马可尼电报；无线电报 | A message sent by Marconi's wireless telegraphy system.  A telegram sent by radio. | Example:  The ship sent a marconigram to report its position. 这艘船发送了一份马可尼电报报告它的位置。

Example:  Marconigrams were an important form of communication in the early 20th century.  马可尼电报是20世纪早期的一种重要通讯方式。| 近义词: radiogram, wireless, telegram | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly letter, phone call) | 词性: noun
