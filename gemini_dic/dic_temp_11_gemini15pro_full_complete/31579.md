chasm: /ˈkæzəm/ | n. | 深渊，裂缝，巨大差距 | A deep fissure in the earth, rock, or another surface. chasm: /ˈkæzəm/ | n. | 巨大分歧，意见不合  | A marked difference of opinion, feeling, or interest.  |  There was a chasm between the two political parties on the issue of healthcare.  两个政党在医疗保健问题上存在巨大分歧。| synonyms: discord, disagreement, disharmony | antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord | noun
