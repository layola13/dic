intestacy: /ɪnˈtɛstəsi/ | n. | 无遗嘱死亡; 无遗嘱继承 | The state of dying without having made a valid will.  The condition of an estate of a person who dies without leaving a valid will. |  His intestacy caused complications for his family. 他的无遗嘱死亡给他的家人带来了麻烦。|  He died in intestacy, leaving his affairs in disarray. 他死时没有留下遗嘱，身后事一片混乱。|近义词：testatorless, will-less|反义词: testacy | noun
