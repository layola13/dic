nonindependent:/ˌnɑːnɪndɪˈpɛndənt/
|adj.|非独立的，不独立的|Not independent; lacking independence.  This term describes something or someone that is not capable of functioning or existing autonomously. It relies on something else.|The study involved nonindependent variables, making it difficult to isolate the causal factors.|近义词:dependent, reliant, interconnected, interrelated|反义词:independent, autonomous, self-sufficient, self-reliant|词性:adjective
