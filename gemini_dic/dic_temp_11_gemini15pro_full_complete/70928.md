firebreak:/ˈfaɪərbreɪk/| n.|防火带，隔离带|A barrier, such as a strip of cleared land, intended to check the spread of fire.|Firefighters created a firebreak to stop the wildfire from spreading to the town. 消防队员开辟了一条防火带，以阻止野火蔓延到城镇。|近义词：firestop, fire line, barrier|反义词:fuel, accelerant|noun


firebreak:/ˈfaɪərbreɪk/| n.|阻碍物，障碍|Something that checks or hinders the spread or progress of something undesirable.|The new regulations are intended to act as a firebreak against the spread of the disease. 新法规旨在作为阻止疾病蔓延的防火墙。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier|反义词:catalyst, accelerator|noun
