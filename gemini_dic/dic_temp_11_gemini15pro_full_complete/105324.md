kiche:/ˈkiːtʃeɪ/ | n. | 基切语; 基切人 | A Mayan language spoken in the highlands of Guatemala.  It is also used to refer to the people who speak this language. |  Many kiche people maintain their traditional weaving practices. 很多基切人保留着他们传统的编织做法。 | synonyms: Quiché (alternative spelling) | antonyms: N/A | noun
