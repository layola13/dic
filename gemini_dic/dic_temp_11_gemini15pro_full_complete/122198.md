misus:/ˈmɪsjuːs/| n. |误用，滥用|Misuse of prescription drugs is a serious problem. 处方药的滥用是一个严重的问题。|Misuse of public funds led to his downfall. 公款的滥用导致了他的垮台。|近义词：abuse, misuse|反义词：proper use, correct application|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *misusen, from mis- + usen (“to use”), equivalent to mis- + use. Ultimately from Latin ūsus.
