gluttonish:/ˈɡlʌtnəs/ | adj. | 贪吃的；贪婪的；过量的 | Excessively greedy; given to overindulgence, especially in eating. | He has a gluttonous appetite for both food and information. 他对食物和信息都有着贪婪的胃口。 |  She ate a gluttonous amount of cake. 她吃了过量的蛋糕。 | synonyms: greedy, voracious, ravenous, insatiable | antonyms: temperate, moderate, abstemious | adjective
