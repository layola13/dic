oblivion: /əˈblɪviən/| n.|遗忘; 被遗忘的状态; 无知觉; 赦免|The state of being completely forgotten or unknown; the state of being unconscious.|例句：His name has faded into oblivion. 他的名字已渐渐被人遗忘。

例句：He drank himself into oblivion. 他喝得烂醉如泥，不省人事。


近义词：forgetfulness, amnesia, unconsciousness, pardon|反义词：remembrance, memory, consciousness|词性: 名词
