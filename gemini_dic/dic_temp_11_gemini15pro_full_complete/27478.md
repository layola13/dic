campanili: /ˌkæmpəˈniːliː/ /ˌkæmpəˈniːlaɪ/
n.
钟楼；钟塔
A bell tower, especially one freestanding from the main church building.
The campanili of Venice are famous landmarks. 威尼斯的钟楼是著名的地标。
