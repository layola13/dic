airy-fairy:/ˈɛəri ˈfɛəri/ | adj. | 不切实际的，异想天开的，空想的 | Not practical or realistic; whimsical or fanciful.  Suggestive of having one's head in the clouds. |  He has all these airy-fairy ideas about starting a business, but no real plan. 他对创业有很多不切实际的想法，但没有真正的计划。| Example:  She dismissed his suggestions as airy-fairy nonsense. 她认为他的建议是异想天开的废话。 |Synonyms: impractical, unrealistic, fanciful, whimsical, dreamy, utopian, quixotic | Antonyms: practical, realistic, down-to-earth, pragmatic | adjective
