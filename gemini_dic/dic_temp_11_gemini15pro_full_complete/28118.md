capitalness:/ˈkæpɪtəlnəs/
n.
资本性；资本的性质或状态
The capitalness of the investment was assessed before proceeding.  这项投资的资本性在继续进行之前得到了评估。
The report analyzed the capitalness of various assets.  该报告分析了各种资产的资本性。
Etymology:  From "capital" (meaning wealth in the form of money or other assets owned by a person or organization or available for investment) + "-ness" (a suffix forming nouns chiefly from adjectives, denoting a state, condition, or quality).  源自“capital”（意为个人或组织拥有或可用于投资的金钱或其他资产形式的财富）+“-ness”（主要由形容词构成的后缀，表示状态、条件或性质）。
Synonyms:  capital nature, investment quality
Antonyms:  expenditure, consumption
词性: 名词
