overassertivenesses: /ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 过分自信; 过分断言 | The state of being excessively assertive, often to the point of aggression or arrogance.  |  Her overassertivenesses in meetings alienated her colleagues. 她在会议上的过分自信疏远了她的同事。 | Synonyms: aggressiveness, pushiness, arrogance, presumptuousness | Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence, submissiveness | noun

Etymology:  From "over" (excessive) + "assert" (to state confidently) + "-ive" (adjective suffix) + "-ness" (noun suffix) + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Assert" comes from Latin "asserere" (to claim, maintain).
