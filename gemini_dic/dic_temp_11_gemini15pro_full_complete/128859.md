nihilist: /ˈnaɪɪlɪst/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪst/ (UK)| n.| 虚无主义者； нигилист|A nihilist believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles. |例句：He became a nihilist after witnessing the horrors of war.  他目睹了战争的恐怖后变成了一个虚无主义者。

|近义词：pessimist, cynic|反义词: optimist, believer|词性: noun


nihilists: /ˈnaɪɪlɪsts/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪsts/ (UK)| n.| 虚无主义者们； нигилисты |Plural form of nihilist, referring to a group of people who believe life is meaningless. |例句：The nihilists in the novel rejected all societal norms.  小说中的虚无主义者们拒绝所有社会规范。
