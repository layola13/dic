banan:/bəˈnæn/ | n. | 香蕉 | A long curved fruit with a yellow skin that grows in bunches on a tropical plant. | Example: I eat a banana every morning for breakfast. 我每天早上早餐吃一根香蕉。| Example: Monkeys love to eat bananas. 猴子喜欢吃香蕉。 | Synonym: plantain (cooking banana) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a fruit) | noun
