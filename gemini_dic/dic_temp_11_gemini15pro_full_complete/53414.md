disanimation:/ˌdɪsænɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
丧失活力, 失去生气, 失去动画效果
lack of animation; lifelessness. The process of removing animation from something.
The character's face showed a disturbing disanimation. 这个角色的脸上呈现出一种令人不安的毫无生气。
The software allows for the disanimation of 3D models.  这款软件可以移除3D模型的动画效果。
近义词: lifelessness, inertia, torpor, apathy
反义词: animation, liveliness, vibrancy, energy
词性: 名词
