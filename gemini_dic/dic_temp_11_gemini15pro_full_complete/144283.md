outsparkling:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的光辉; 比…更闪亮
Surpassing in sparkling or brilliance.
Her dress outsparkled all the others at the gala. 她的礼服在晚会上比其他所有人的都闪亮。
outshine, outdazzle, eclipse
dim, dull
v.
