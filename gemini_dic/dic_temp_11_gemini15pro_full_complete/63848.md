epigram:/ˈɛpɪɡræm/| n.|警句，箴言，讽刺短诗|A concise, witty, and often paradoxical saying or remark.  A short, often satirical poem ending in a witty or ingenious turn of thought.| Oscar Wilde was famous for his epigrams. 奥斯卡·王尔德以他的警句而闻名。|synonyms: quip, witticism, aphorism, maxim, adage|antonyms: platitude, cliché, banality|noun
