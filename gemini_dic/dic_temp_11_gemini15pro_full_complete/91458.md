hyksos:/ˈhɪksɒs/| n.|喜克索斯人|A group of people of mixed Semitic and Asian descent who conquered and ruled ancient Egypt between c. 1650 and 1550 BC.|The Hyksos introduced the chariot to Egypt, revolutionizing warfare. 喜克索斯人将战车引入埃及，彻底改变了战争方式。|Synonym:  Asiatics (in some contexts)|Antonym:  Native Egyptians|Noun
