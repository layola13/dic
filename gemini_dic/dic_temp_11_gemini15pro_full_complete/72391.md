fluctuating:/ˈflʌktʃueɪtɪŋ/
adjective
波动的，起伏的
rising and falling irregularly in number or amount.
The fluctuating price of gold makes it a risky investment. 黄金价格的波动使其成为一项风险投资。
fluctuating temperatures make it difficult to dress appropriately.  气温波动不定，很难穿得合适。
近义词: varying, changing, oscillating, unstable, erratic
反义词: stable, steady, constant, fixed, unchanging
词性: adjective

fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/
verb
波动，起伏
to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount.
Stock prices fluctuate daily. 股票价格每天都在波动。
His mood fluctuates between happiness and despair. 他的情绪在快乐和绝望之间波动。
近义词: vary, change, oscillate, waver, swing
反义词: stabilize, steady, remain constant
词性: verb

词源: From Latin fluctuatus, past participle of fluctuare "to wave, undulate," from fluctus "wave, billow."
