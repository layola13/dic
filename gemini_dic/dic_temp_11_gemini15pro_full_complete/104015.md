juicily:/ˈdʒuːsɪli/
adverb
多汁地，津津有味地，生动地
In a juicy manner; succulently.
The steak was cooked juicily.  牛排做得很多汁。
The gossip columnist described the scandal juicily.  八卦专栏作家绘声绘色地描述了这桩丑闻。
近义词:succulently, lushly, vividly
反义词:dryly, blandly
词性:adverb
