lay-by:/ˈleɪbaɪ/ (UK), /ˈleɪˌbaɪ/ (US)| n.|分期付款，预留商品|A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the retailer until the final payment is made.|I put the TV on lay-by and paid it off over three months. 我把电视机分期付款，三个月付清了。|She's using lay-by to buy Christmas presents. 她正在用分期付款的方式购买圣诞礼物。|近义词: installment plan, layaway (US)|反义词: outright purchase|词性: noun

lay-bys:/ˈleɪbaɪz/ (UK), /ˈleɪˌbaɪz/ (US)| n.|分期付款，预留商品（复数）|Plural form of lay-by, referring to multiple lay-by agreements.|She has two lay-bys at the moment, one for a new fridge and one for a washing machine. 她现在有两笔分期付款，一笔是买新冰箱，一笔是买洗衣机。|The store offers lay-bys on most of its products. 这家商店的大部分商品都可以分期付款。|近义词: installment plans, layaways (US)|反义词: outright purchases|词性: noun
