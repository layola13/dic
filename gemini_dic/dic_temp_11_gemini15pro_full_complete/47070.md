dates:/deɪts/| n.|日期; 约会; 枣; (枣椰树的)果实|A date is a particular day of the month or year.  日期是指月份或年份中的特定日子。|例句：What's the date today? 今天几号？|近义词：appointments, engagements (for romantic dates)|反义词: None (for the 'date' meaning 'a specific day')|noun
dates:/deɪts/| v.|确定…的年代; 显示…的年代; 属于…的年代; 注明日期; 过时; 和…约会|To date something means to mark it with a date. 确定某物的年代是指用日期标记它。  To date someone means to go on dates with them. 和某人约会是指和他们出去约会。|例句：The document is dated 1876. 这份文件注明日期为 1876 年。|近义词：go out with (for the 'date' meaning 'go on dates')|反义词: None|verb
