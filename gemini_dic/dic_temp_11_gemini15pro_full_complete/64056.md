epitomises:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzɪz/
verb
集中体现;是…的典型;
be a perfect example of a quality or type of thing
Her work epitomises the struggle of women artists to gain recognition. 她的作品是女性艺术家争取认可的斗争的缩影。
synonyms:typifies, embodies, exemplifies, represents
antonyms:misrepresents, distorts
词性:动词(第三人称单数)
