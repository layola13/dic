affronting:/əˈfrʌntɪŋ/
adjective
冒犯的，无礼的
Causing offense, insulting.
His affronting behavior made everyone uncomfortable. 他的无礼行为让每个人都不舒服。
The comedian's jokes were deliberately affronting. 这位喜剧演员的笑话故意冒犯人。
synonyms: insulting, offensive, disrespectful, insolent, rude, impertinent
antonyms: respectful, polite, courteous, gracious, flattering
词性:形容词
