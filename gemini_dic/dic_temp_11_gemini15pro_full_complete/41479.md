cornmeal:/ˈkɔːrnmiːl/ (US), /ˈkɔːnmiːl/ (UK)| n. |玉米粉，玉米面|A coarse flour made from dried corn kernels, used to make various foods such as cornbread, polenta, and tortillas.|She used cornmeal to make cornbread for breakfast. 她用玉米粉做了早餐的玉米面包。|Synonyms: maize meal, Indian meal|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider wheat flour, rice flour as alternatives)|noun
