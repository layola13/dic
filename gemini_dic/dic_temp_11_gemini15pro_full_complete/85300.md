harnessed:/ˈhɑːrnɪst/| v. adj.|利用，控制；套上挽具|To control and make use of (natural resources), especially to produce energy.  Put a harness on (a horse or other draft animal).|例句：They harnessed the power of the river to generate electricity. 他们利用河水发电。
|例句：The farmer harnessed the horses to the plow. 农民把马套在犁上。|近义词：utilize, exploit, employ, control, yoke|反义词:release, free|词性:动词/形容词

harnessed:/ˈhɑːrnɪst/| adj.|穿着挽具的，受控制的|Controlled or restrained, often used in the phrase "harnessed to".|例句：The horses, harnessed and ready, waited patiently.  套好挽具的马匹耐心地等待着。
|例句：His creativity was harnessed to the needs of the project. 他的创造力被用来满足项目的需求。|近义词：controlled, restrained, confined|反义词:free, unrestrained|词性:形容词
