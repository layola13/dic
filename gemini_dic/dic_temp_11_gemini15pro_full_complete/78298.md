germiparity:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈpærɪti/
n.
生殖；胚胎发育
The condition or mode of reproduction in which young develop from eggs that hatch within the body of the parent.
Guppies demonstrate germiparity, giving birth to live young.  孔雀鱼展现了卵胎生，产下活的幼鱼。
Synonyms: ovoviviparity, aplacental viviparity
Antonyms: oviparity, viviparity
noun
