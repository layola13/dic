exploding:/ɪkˈsploʊdɪŋ/| adj. | 爆炸的；激增的；爆发的 | Expanding or bursting with sudden violence; increasing rapidly. | The exploding population is putting a strain on resources.  人口激增给资源带来了压力。| synonyms: bursting, detonating, erupting, booming, surging | antonyms: imploding, shrinking, decreasing, contracting | adjective

exploding:/ɪkˈsploʊdɪŋ/| n. | 爆炸；激增 | The act of bursting or expanding violently; a rapid increase. | The exploding of the fireworks was spectacular. 烟花的爆炸非常壮观。 | synonyms: burst, detonation, eruption, boom, surge | antonyms: implosion, shrinking, decrease, contraction | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "explode," which comes from the Latin "explodere" (to drive out by clapping, hiss off the stage), from "ex-" (out) + "plaudere" (to clap).
