orneriness:/ˈɔːrnərinəs/
n.
固执，乖戾，暴躁
The state of being bad-tempered and difficult to deal with.
His orneriness made it difficult to work with him. 他的暴躁脾气让他很难与人共事。
stubbornness, cantankerousness,  irascibility,  cussedness
agreeableness, compliance, docility
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ornery + -ness.  Ornery itself is of uncertain origin, perhaps related to ordinary in the sense of "low, common, vulgar" (implying a lack of refinement and manners).
