bilaciniate:/baɪˌlæsɪniˈeɪt/
adjective

Having two whip-like appendages or extensions.  Shaped like two whips or lashes joined at the base.
例句：The bacterium has a bilaciniate tail, allowing it to move rapidly.  这种细菌有一条二叉的尾巴，使它能够快速移动。

例句：The artist created a sculpture with a bilaciniate form, resembling two intertwined flames. 艺术家创作了一个二裂形的雕塑，类似于两股交织的火焰。
