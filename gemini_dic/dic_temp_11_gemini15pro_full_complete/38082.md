compost:/ˈkɒmpɒst/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmpoʊst/ (US)| n. vt.|堆肥，混合肥料；施肥|A mixture of decayed organic matter, such as leaves and manure, used to improve soil fertility.  Also, the act of making or using compost.|例句：
The compost enriched the soil.  堆肥使土壤肥沃。

I compost all my kitchen scraps. 我把所有厨房垃圾都做成堆肥。|近义词：fertilizer, mulch|反义词:|n. & vt.
