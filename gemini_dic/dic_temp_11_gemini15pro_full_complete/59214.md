dysprosiums:/dɪsˈproʊziəmz/| n.|镝（元素符号Dy）|A soft, silvery metallic element of the lanthanide series.  It is used in some specialized alloys and magnets. |Dysprosiums are used in nuclear control rods because of their high neutron absorption cross-section. 镝因其高热中子吸收截面而用于核控制棒。

Dysprosium magnets are known for their high temperature stability. 镝磁铁以其高温稳定性而闻名。|近义词: (As an element, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but other lanthanides like holmium and erbium have similar properties.)|反义词: (None)|词性:noun
