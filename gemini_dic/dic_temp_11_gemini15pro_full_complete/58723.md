duncish:/ˈdʌŋkɪʃ/| adj.|愚钝的，迟钝的；昏庸的|Lacking intelligence; slow-witted; obtuse.  Relating to or characteristic of a dunce.|He made a rather duncish remark about the artwork, clearly not understanding its significance. 他对这件艺术品发表了相当愚蠢的评论，显然没有理解它的意义。|近义词：stupid, obtuse, doltish, slow-witted|反义词：intelligent, bright, clever, sharp|词性:形容词
词源: From "dunce" + "-ish."  "Dunce" itself derives from John Duns Scotus, a 13th-century theologian whose followers were later ridiculed as being intellectually inferior.
