nonracialism:/nɒnˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.
非种族主义
The belief that race is not a significant factor in human affairs, especially in social and political contexts.
Nelson Mandela championed nonracialism in South Africa.  纳尔逊·曼德拉在南非倡导非种族主义。
synonyms:  anti-racism, equality, integration
antonyms: racism, racialism, segregation
noun
