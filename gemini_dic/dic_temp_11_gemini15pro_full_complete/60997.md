eligibly:/ˈɛlɪdʒɪbli/ | adv. | 合格地，有资格地 | In a way that makes someone or something suitable for something; meeting the requirements. |  He is eligibly employed and can afford the rent. 他有资格就业，付得起房租。 |  She dressed eligibly for the job interview. 她穿着符合面试要求的服装。| Synonyms: suitably, appropriately, properly, lawfully | Antonyms: ineligibly, improperly, unsuitably | adverb

eligible: /ˈɛlɪdʒəbl/ | adj. | 合格的，有资格的 | Having the right to do or obtain something; satisfying the appropriate conditions. | He is eligible for a scholarship. 他有资格获得奖学金。|  Only residents are eligible to vote. 只有居民才有资格投票。 | Synonyms: qualified, entitled, suitable, fit | Antonyms: ineligible, disqualified, unsuitable | adjective
