coordinately:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnɪtli/| adv.|协调地，同步地|In a coordinated manner; together.  Working or acting together efficiently.|The dancers moved coordinately, their steps perfectly synchronized. 舞蹈演员们协调地移动，他们的舞步完美同步。|
The project was coordinately managed by several departments.  这个项目由几个部门协调管理。|近义词：jointly, synchronously, harmoniously|反义词：independently, separately, discordantly|词性:副词

coordination:/koʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|协调，配合|The organization or act of coordinating. The ability to move different parts of your body together smoothly and efficiently.|The coordination of the event was flawless. 活动的协调完美无瑕。|He demonstrated excellent hand-eye coordination. 他展现了出色手眼协调能力。|近义词：cooperation, collaboration, synchronization|反义词：discord, conflict, disorganization|词性:名词

coordinator:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtər/| n.|协调员，协调人|A person who coordinates something or someone.|The event coordinator ensured everything ran smoothly. 活动协调员确保一切顺利进行。|She works as a project coordinator for a large company. 她在一家大公司担任项目协调员。|近义词：organizer, manager, director|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "coordinate" + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Coordinate" comes from the Late Latin "coordinatus," past participle of "coordinare," meaning "to arrange in order, regulate," from "co-" (together) + "ordinare" (to arrange).  "Ordinare" comes from "ordo" (order).
