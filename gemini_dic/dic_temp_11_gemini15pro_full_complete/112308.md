lordliest:/ˈlɔːrdliɪst/| adj.|最高贵的，最傲慢的|Superlative form of lordly: most lordly.  Having or showing the proud and superior bearing or dignity of a person of high rank or social position. Can also imply arrogance.|He strode into the room with the lordliest air imaginable. 他以最傲慢的姿态大步走进房间。|近义词：haughtiest, proudest, most arrogant, most majestic|反义词：humblest, meekest, most modest|词性:形容词
