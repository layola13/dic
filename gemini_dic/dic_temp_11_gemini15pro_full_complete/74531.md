fraternization:/ˌfrætərnaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
友好往来， fraternization
The fraternization between officers and enlisted men is against regulations. 军官和士兵之间的友好往来是违反规定的。
Fraternization between the two groups led to a peaceful resolution.  这两个群体之间的友好往来促成了和平解决。
近义词：association, mingling, socializing, fellowship
反义词：estrangement, alienation, isolation
词性: noun


fraternize:/ˈfrætərnaɪz/
v.
亲如兄弟,  fraternize
The soldiers were warned not to fraternize with the enemy. 士兵们被警告不要与敌人 fraternize。
The company's policy discourages employees from fraternizing outside of work.  公司的政策不鼓励员工在工作之外 fraternize。
近义词：associate, mingle, socialize
反义词： shun, avoid
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fraterniser*, from Late Latin *fraternizare*, from Latin *fraternus* "brotherly," from *frater* "brother."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*bʰréh₂tēr* "brother."
