floatplane:/ˈfloʊtˌpleɪn/ | n. |水上飞机|An aircraft designed to take off and land on water. |The floatplane landed smoothly on the lake. 水上飞机平稳地降落在湖面上。|Example:  A floatplane offers a unique perspective of the coastline. 水上飞机提供了独特的海岸线视角。|Synonym: seaplane |Antonym: landplane |Noun
