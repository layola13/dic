jelled:/dʒeld/
verb
凝固, 成形
past tense and past participle of "jell" meaning to become firm or set, like jelly.
The mixture jelled overnight in the refrigerator. 混合物在冰箱里放了一夜就凝固了。
The plan finally jelled after weeks of discussion.  经过几周的讨论，计划终于成形了。
近义词: solidified, congealed, set
反义词: liquefied, melted
词性: verb
