cowish:/ˈkaʊɪʃ/| adj.|胆小的，懦弱的|Resembling a cow in meekness; timid.  Characterized by timidity or a lack of assertiveness. | He gave a cowish look and backed away from the confrontation. 他胆怯地看了一眼，然后从对抗中退缩了。|Synonyms: timid, timorous, fearful, pusillanimous, cowardly, craven, faint-hearted, spineless|Antonyms: brave, courageous, bold, daring, intrepid, valiant, fearless|词性:形容词
