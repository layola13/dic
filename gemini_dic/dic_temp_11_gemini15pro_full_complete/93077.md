idolous: /ˈaɪdələs/ | adj. | 偶像的；盲目崇拜的；过分喜爱的 | Given to excessive admiration or reverence; worshipping idols. |  She has an idolous devotion to her favorite band. 她对她最喜欢的乐队有着狂热的崇拜。|  He has an idolous love for his car. 他对他的车爱得痴迷。 | 近义词: idolatrous, worshipping, adoring | 反义词: critical, skeptical, disapproving | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French idoleus, from Latin idoleus ‘pertaining to idols’, from idolum.
