dambo:/ˈdæmboʊ/| n. |沼泽，湿地；季节性洪泛区|A dambo is a seasonally waterlogged, grassy depression in central, southern, and eastern Africa, typically found in headwater valleys.|The dambo provided water for the cattle during the dry season.  这片沼泽在旱季为牛群提供了水源。|近义词：marsh, swamp, wetland|反义词:desert, upland|noun
