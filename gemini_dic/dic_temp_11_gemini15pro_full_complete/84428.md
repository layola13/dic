hallucinating:/həˈluːsɪneɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 产生幻觉的；正在幻想的 | Experiencing hallucinations; seeing, hearing, or feeling things that are not really there. |  She was hallucinating due to the high fever. 她因高烧而产生幻觉。|  He claimed to be hallucinating after taking the new medication. 他声称服用新药后出现了幻觉。 | synonyms: delusional, visionary, imagining, fantasizing | antonyms: lucid, clear-headed, rational | verb (present participle)

hallucinate:/həˈluːsɪneɪt/| v.| 产生幻觉；幻想 | To experience hallucinations; to see, hearing, or feeling things that are not really there. |  The patient began to hallucinate after the surgery. 病人在手术后开始出现幻觉。| He hallucinated that he could fly. 他幻想自己能飞。 | synonyms:  see things,  have visions, trip | antonyms: perceive reality, be lucid | verb
