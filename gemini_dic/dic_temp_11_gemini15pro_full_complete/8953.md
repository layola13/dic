antihumanitarians:/ˌænˌtiˌhjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriənz/
| n. |反人道主义者|People who oppose humanitarian principles or actions; those who are cruel or inhumane.|The antihumanitarians were condemned for their brutal treatment of prisoners.|近义词：barbarians, brutes, savages|反义词:humanitarians, philanthropists|Noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposing") + humanitarian (noun meaning "a person who seeks to promote human welfare")
