delinquent:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənt/| n. adj.|违规者，不良少年；拖欠的，违法的|A person, usually young, who regularly performs illegal or immoral acts; failing in duty; overdue in payment.|例句：The delinquent teenagers vandalized the park. 这些不良少年破坏了公园。|近义词：offender, culprit, criminal, lawbreaker; overdue, unpaid, outstanding|反义词：compliant, law-abiding, responsible; paid, current|词性:名词，形容词
