dies:/daɪz/| v.|死亡，消逝，中止；(模具、冲模)冲压，压制|The flower dies in the winter.  这花在冬天枯死。|近义词：deceases, expires, perishes|反义词：lives, survives|verb

Etymology: From Old French *dier, from Latin *diere, present active infinitive of morī ("to die").
