damselfly:/ˈdæmzəlflaɪ/| n. |豆娘，蟌|A slender-bodied insect similar to a dragonfly but having broader, hindwings that are held together above the body when at rest.|例句：The damselfly hovered over the pond. 豆娘在池塘上盘旋。

例句：A bright blue damselfly landed on a reed. 一只亮蓝色的豆娘落在芦苇上。

|近义词：dragonfly|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
