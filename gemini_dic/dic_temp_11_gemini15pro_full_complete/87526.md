heterogeneous:/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异质的，由不同成分组成的| Diverse in character or content.| The city is a heterogeneous mix of cultures and ethnicities. 这座城市是文化和种族的异质混合体。|例句：The rock is heterogeneous, containing several different minerals.  这块岩石是异质的，包含几种不同的矿物。

例句：The student body is heterogeneous, with students from all over the world.  学生群体是异质的，有来自世界各地的学生。|近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar, miscellaneous|反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar, alike|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
