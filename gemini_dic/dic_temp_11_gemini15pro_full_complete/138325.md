/nəʊts/ |/noʊts/| n. |笔记，记录；注释；纸币；音符；(正式)照会，便笺| a brief record of something written down to aid the memory or for future reference |例句：I took notes during the lecture. 我在讲课期间做了笔记。|近义词：records, memoranda, annotations, remarks, observations|反义词： (For banknotes) coins |词性：名词

/nəʊts/ |/noʊts/| v. |注意，留意；指出；特别提到；记录| to notice or pay attention to something |例句：Please note that this offer is for a limited time only.  请注意，此优惠仅在限定时间内有效。|近义词：observe, perceive, remark, record|反义词：ignore, overlook |词性：动词
