bhakti:/ˈbʌkti/| n.|虔诚，奉献，热爱|Devotion, especially to a god or goddess, or a spiritual practice involving such devotion.|例句：Her bhakti for Krishna was evident in her daily prayers. 她对克里希纳的虔诚体现在她每天的祈祷中。

例句：Bhakti yoga is a path to enlightenment through devotion.  奉爱瑜伽是通过虔诚达到觉悟的途径。|近义词：devotion, piety, reverence, adoration|反义词：irreverence, disrespect, apathy|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Sanskrit भक्ति (bhakti), meaning "attachment, devotion, fondness, homage, faith or love, worship, piety".
