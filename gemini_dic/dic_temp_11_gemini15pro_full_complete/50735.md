derogatory:/dɪˈrɒɡət(ə)ri/ /dɪˈrɑːɡəˌtɔːri/
adjective
贬损的，贬低的
showing a critical or disrespectful attitude
例句：He made some derogatory remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌发表了一些贬损的言论。

例句：Using derogatory language is unacceptable. 使用贬损的语言是不可接受的。

近义词：disparaging, belittling, insulting, offensive, pejorative
反义词：complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *dērogātōrius, from Latin dērogātus (past participle of dērogāre, “to detract from, diminish, repeal partly”)  < dē- + rogāre (“to ask, propose a law”).
