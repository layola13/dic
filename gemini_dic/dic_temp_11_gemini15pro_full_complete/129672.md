non-demise:/nɒn-dɪˈmaɪz/ | adj. | 非终止的，非死亡的 |  Relating to a lease or agreement that does not terminate upon the death of a specified party.  It indicates the continuation of an obligation or right despite a death. |  The non-demise clause in the lease protects the tenant's rights even if the landlord dies.  该租赁协议中的非终止条款即使在房东去世后也能保护租户的权利。 |  continuing, enduring, ongoing | terminating, expiring | adjective

non-demise:/nɒn-dɪˈmaɪz/ | n. | 非终止，非死亡 | The state or quality of not being terminated or expiring, especially in legal contexts like leases. | The non-demise of the contract allowed the business to continue operating.  合同的非终止性允许企业继续运营。 |  continuation, persistence | termination, expiration | noun
