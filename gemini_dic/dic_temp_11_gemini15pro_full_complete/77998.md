gentleman's:/ˈdʒɛntlmənz/| n. (possessive form)|绅士的，先生的|A gentleman's agreement is one based on trust rather than a legal contract.  绅士协议是建立在信任而非法律合同基础上的协议。|例句：This is a gentleman's club, women are not allowed. 这是一个绅士俱乐部，女性不得入内。|近义词：men's, male's |反义词:lady's, women's|adjective, possessive
