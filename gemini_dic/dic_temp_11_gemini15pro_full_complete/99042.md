interanimated:/ˌɪntərˈænɪmeɪtɪd/
adjective
互相激励的，互相赋予活力的
given life or spirit mutually; mutually enlivened or animated
The two artists, interanimated by each other's passion, collaborated on a masterpiece. 这两位艺术家互相激发彼此的热情，合作创作了一幅杰作。
近义词：interconnected, interdependent, mutually stimulating
反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lifeless
词性：adjective

词源:
From inter- + animated.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "animated" means "full of life or spirit."
