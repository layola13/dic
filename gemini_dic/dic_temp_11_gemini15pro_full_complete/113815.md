madam:/ˈmædəm/ (US) /ˈmædəm/ (UK)| n.|女士，夫人|A polite term of address for a woman, equivalent to "Mrs." or "Ms."|例句：Excuse me, madam, could you help me?  打扰一下，女士，你能帮我吗？

例句：The letter was addressed to "Dear Madam and Sir". 这封信的抬头是“尊敬的女士及先生”。|近义词：ma'am, lady, Mrs., Ms.|反义词:sir, mister, gentleman|词性:noun
