bunfight:/ˈbʌnfaɪt/
n.
吵闹的争论；混战；聚会
A noisy argument or chaotic situation; a social gathering, often with refreshments.
The meeting descended into a bunfight, with everyone shouting at once. 会议变成了混战，每个人都在同时喊叫。
There was a bit of a bunfight at the bar last night. 昨晚酒吧里发生了一点争吵。
近义词：fracas, brawl, melee, free-for-all, party, gathering
反义词：agreement, harmony, calm

词源:  The origin is uncertain, but it's thought to be rhyming slang from the late 19th/early 20th century, possibly related to "bun and strife" (wife) and suggesting a domestic argument.  The meaning later broadened to encompass any noisy argument or chaotic situation.
