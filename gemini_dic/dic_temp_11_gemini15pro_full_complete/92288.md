ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ifination*, from Late Latin *hyphenātiō*, from Latin *hyphenātus*, past participle of *hyphenāre*, from Ancient Greek *ὑφενόω (huphenoō)*, from *ὑφ᾽ ἕν (huph' hen)*, meaning "under one" or "together".
