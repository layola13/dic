nonsensically:/ˌnɒnˈsensɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˈsensɪkli/| adv.|荒谬地，无意义地|In a manner that lacks sense or meaning; absurdly.|He was talking nonsensically about unicorns and dragons. 他胡言乱语地说着独角兽和龙的故事。|例句：The plot of the movie was nonsensically convoluted.  这部电影的情节荒谬地复杂。|近义词：absurdly, foolishly, meaninglessly, illogically|反义词：sensibly, rationally, logically|词性:副词

词源: From "nonsense" + "-ical" + "-ly". "Nonsense" comes from Middle English "nonsens," meaning "foolishness," from "non-" (not) + "sens" (sense).
