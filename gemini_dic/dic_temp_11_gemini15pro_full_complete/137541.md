/ˌnɒntrænˈskrɪpʃənz/ | n. | 非转录本; 未转录的内容 |  Material or information that has not been transcribed, typically from an audio or video recording. |  The researchers analyzed both the transcriptions and the nontranscriptions to ensure a comprehensive understanding of the data. 研究人员分析了转录本和非转录本，以确保对数据的全面理解。| non-textual data, untranscribed material | transcriptions, recordings | noun


Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix) from Latin "non" meaning "not."
"Transcription" from Latin "transcribere" meaning "to copy across, transfer."
