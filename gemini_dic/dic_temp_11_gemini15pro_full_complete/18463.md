bemuddle:/bɪˈmʌdəl/
verb
使迷惑；使糊涂
to confuse or bewilder someone
The instructions completely bemuddled me.  这些指示完全把我搞糊涂了。
confuse, bewilder, puzzle
clarify, enlighten
