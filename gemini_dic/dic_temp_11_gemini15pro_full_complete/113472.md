lyrebird: /ˈlaɪəbɜːrd/ (US), /ˈlaɪəbɜːd/ (UK)| n. |琴鸟|A bird native to Australia, known for its remarkable ability to mimic sounds, including other bird calls, human speech, and machinery noises.|The lyrebird's mimicry is so accurate it can even imitate the sound of a chainsaw. 琴鸟的模仿能力非常精确，甚至可以模仿电锯的声音。|Synonyms: mimicker, imitator|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific bird)|noun
