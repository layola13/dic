colonization:/ˌkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːlənaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |殖民化，殖民地化|The process of establishing a colony or colonies.| The colonization of Africa by European powers had a lasting impact. 欧洲列强对非洲的殖民化产生了持久的影响。|  The colonization of Mars is a long-term goal for some scientists.  一些科学家将火星殖民化作为长期目标。 |synonyms: settlement, occupation, peopling|antonyms: decolonization, independence|noun

colonize:/ˈkɒlənaɪz/ /ˈkɑːlənaɪz/| vt. |殖民，建立殖民地|To establish a colony or colonies in.| European powers colonized much of the world during the 19th century. 欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了世界的大部分地区。|  Scientists are exploring the possibility of colonizing other planets. 科学家们正在探索在其他星球上建立殖民地的可能性。| synonyms: settle, occupy, people| antonyms: decolonize, liberate|verb
