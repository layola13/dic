nontumultuous: /ˌnɒn.tʃuːˈmʌl.tʃu.əs/ | adj. | 非骚乱的；平静的 | Not tumultuous; peaceful and calm. | The meeting was surprisingly nontumultuous, despite the controversial topic.  这次会议出人意料地平静，尽管讨论的话题很有争议。| The transfer of power was nontumultuous. 权力交接平静地完成了。| 近义词: peaceful, calm, quiet, tranquil, serene | 反义词: tumultuous, turbulent, chaotic, riotous | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  (来自拉丁语 non)
tumultuous (adjective): characterized by uproar and confusion (来自拉丁语 tumultuosus, 来自 tumultus "uproar")
