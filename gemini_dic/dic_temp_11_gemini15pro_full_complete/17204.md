bauxite:/ˈbɔːksaɪt/| n.|铝土矿|A rock consisting mainly of hydrated aluminum oxides, formed by the weathering of aluminum-rich rocks in tropical and subtropical climates. It is the principal ore of aluminum.|Jamaica is a major producer of bauxite. 牙买加是铝土矿的主要产地。|
Bauxite is essential for aluminum production. 铝土矿对铝的生产至关重要。|
Synonyms: aluminum ore, laterite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
