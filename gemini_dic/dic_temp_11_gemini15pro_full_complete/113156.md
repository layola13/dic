lupine:/ˈluːpaɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪn/ (UK)| adj. n.|狼的；狼似的；贪婪的；羽扇豆；羽扇豆属植物|Of, relating to, or resembling a wolf, especially in being predatory or rapacious.  A plant of the genus Lupinus, typically having brightly coloured flowers.|例句：The lupine gaze of the hungry wolf made me shiver. 饥饿的狼贪婪的目光让我不寒而栗。

|例句：She planted lupines in her garden for a splash of vibrant colour. 她在花园里种了羽扇豆，增添了一抹鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：wolfish, ravenous, predatory (adj.); lupin (n.)|反义词:gentle, docile (adj.)|词性:adjective, noun
lupine:/ˈluːpaɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪn/ (UK)| n.|羽扇豆；羽扇豆属植物|A plant of the genus Lupinus, typically having brightly coloured flowers.|例句：The lupines bloomed in a riot of purples and blues. 羽扇豆开出了一片紫蓝相间的繁盛景象。
