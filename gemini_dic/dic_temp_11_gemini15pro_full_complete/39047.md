confocal:/kənˈfoʊkəl/| adj.|共焦的，共焦点的|Having the same focus; converging at the same point. Used especially of a microscope in which a pinhole aperture is used to eliminate out-of-focus light. |例句：Confocal microscopy allows for high-resolution imaging of thick specimens. 共聚焦显微镜可以对厚样本进行高分辨率成像。

例句：The confocal laser scanning microscope produces sharp images of a single plane within a thick specimen. 共聚焦激光扫描显微镜可以生成厚样本内单一平面的清晰图像。|近义词：cofocalized|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective

confocal:/kənˈfoʊkəl/| n.|共焦显微镜|A confocal microscope. |例句：The confocal revealed intricate details of the cell's structure. 共焦显微镜揭示了细胞结构的复杂细节。

例句：The researcher used a confocal to study the distribution of proteins within the tissue. 研究人员使用共聚焦显微镜研究组织内蛋白质的分布。|近义词：confocal microscope|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
