hyperventilates:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪts/| v. |过度换气，呼吸急促|breathes too quickly and deeply, causing too much oxygen to enter the blood and too much carbon dioxide to be removed.|She hyperventilated when she heard the bad news. 她听到坏消息后呼吸急促。|The runner hyperventilated after the strenuous race.  赛跑运动员在剧烈比赛后呼吸急促。|近义词：overbreathe|反义词:hypoventilate|词性:动词
