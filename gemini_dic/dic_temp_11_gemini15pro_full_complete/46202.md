cyrenaic: /ˌsaɪrəˈneɪɪk/ /ˌsɪrəˈneɪɪk/
cyrenaic: adj. |昔兰尼的；昔兰尼学派的；享乐主义的 |Relating to Cyrene, an ancient Greek city in North Africa, or its philosophy of pleasure-seeking.  |例句：He lived a cyrenaic lifestyle, indulging in every pleasure imaginable. 他过着享乐主义的生活，沉溺于所有可以想象的乐趣。|近义词：hedonistic, epicurean, pleasure-seeking|反义词: ascetic, abstemious|词性:形容词

cyrenaic: n. |昔兰尼人；昔兰尼学派信徒；享乐主义者| A follower of the Cyrenaic school of philosophy, which advocated pleasure as the highest good. |例句：The cyrenaic believed that the only true good was pleasure.  昔兰尼学派信徒认为唯一的真正好处是快乐。|近义词：hedonist, epicurean|反义词: ascetic|词性:名词

词源: From Greek *Kyrēnaikos*, from *Kyrēnē* (Cyrene).
