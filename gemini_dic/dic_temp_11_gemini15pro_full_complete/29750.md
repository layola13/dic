catoptron:/kəˈtɒptrən/ | n. |镜子；反射镜 | A polished surface, especially of metal, that forms images by reflection. |  The ancient catoptron reflected her face, distorted by the rippling water. 古老的镜子映照出她的脸，被涟漪的水面扭曲了。 | The catoptron shone brightly in the sunlight. 镜子在阳光下闪闪发光。 | synonym: mirror, reflector, speculum | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something that absorbs light: e.g., black hole, dark matter) | noun
