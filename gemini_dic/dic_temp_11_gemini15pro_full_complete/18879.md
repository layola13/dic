besides:/bɪˈsaɪdz/| prep.|除…之外（还包括）|Besides English, she speaks French and Spanish. 除了英语，她还会说法语和西班牙语。|近义词：in addition to, as well as|反义词:except, excluding, barring|词性:介词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bisides, from Old English *bī sīdan (“by the side of”), from bī (“by”) + sīdan (“side”).
