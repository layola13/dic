nonaerobiotic:/ˌnɒnˌɛərəʊˈbaɪɒtɪk/ | adj. | 非需氧的；厌氧的 |  Not requiring free oxygen for life or growth; anaerobic.  Relating to or involving organisms that do not require free oxygen for respiration. |  Certain nonaerobiotic bacteria thrive in environments without oxygen.  某些厌氧细菌在无氧环境中 thrive. | Synonyms: anaerobic, anoxic | Antonyms: aerobic, oxygen-requiring | adjective
