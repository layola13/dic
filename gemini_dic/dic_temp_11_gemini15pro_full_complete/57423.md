drabble:/ˈdræbəl/| n. v.| 短篇小说；涂鸦；潦草地写|A short piece of writing, typically of 100 words.  A short, often unfinished, piece of writing or art. To write or draw something quickly and carelessly. |She wrote a drabble for the online competition. 她为在线比赛写了一篇短篇小说。 He drabbled a quick sketch in his notebook. 他在他的笔记本上潦草地画了一幅速写。|近义词：doodle, scribble, short story|反义词：novel, epic|词性:名词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the science fiction fanzine Apa, first appearing in 1973. Originally a 50-word story, later standardized to 100 words.
