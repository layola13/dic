de-mobbing:/diːˈmɒbɪŋ/
n.|复员;遣散（尤指军人）; 解散队伍|The process of discharging soldiers from military service, especially at the end of a war.  The act of disbanding a group or team.|The de-mobbing of the troops took place after the armistice was signed.  停战协定签署后，部队进行了复员。|demobilization, discharge|mobilization, recruitment|noun

de-mobbing:/diːˈmɒbɪŋ/
v.|复员;遣散（尤指军人）;解散队伍|To discharge from military service.  To disband a group or team.|The government began de-mobbing the army after the war ended. 战争结束后，政府开始遣散军队。|demobilize, discharge|mobilize, recruit|verb
