governmentalized: /ˌɡʌvərnmɛntəlaɪzd/ | adj. |政府化的，国有化的|Converted into or managed by a government.  The process by which something becomes controlled or operated by the government.| Example: The formerly private healthcare system was governmentalized, leading to longer wait times.  前私有的医疗保健系统被国有化了，导致等待时间更长。 | Example:  The governmentalized industries struggled to compete in the free market. 国有化的产业在自由市场中难以竞争。 | Synonyms: nationalized, state-controlled, state-run | Antonyms: privatized, deregulated | word property: adjective
