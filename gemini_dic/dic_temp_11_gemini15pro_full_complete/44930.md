cryptogamist: /krɪpˈtɒɡəmɪst/ /krɪpˈtɑːɡəmɪst/
n.
隐花植物学家
A botanist specializing in the study of cryptogams (plants that reproduce by spores, such as ferns, mosses, algae, and fungi).
Cryptogamists have made significant contributions to our understanding of plant evolution. 隐花植物学家对我们理解植物进化做出了重大贡献。
The cryptogamist spent years cataloging the diverse mosses of the cloud forest.  这位隐花植物学家花了数年时间对云雾林中各种各样的苔藓进行编目。
近义词: botanist, plant scientist
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) phanerogamist (a specialist in flowering plants)
词性: noun
