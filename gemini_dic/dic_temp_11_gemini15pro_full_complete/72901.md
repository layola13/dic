followup:/ˈfɑːloʊʌp/ (US) /ˈfɒləʊʌp/ (UK)| n. adj.|后续行动，跟进；后续的，补充的|A followup is something done to continue or add to something that has already been started.| We need to schedule a followup meeting to discuss the project further. 我们需要安排一次后续会议来进一步讨论这个项目。|synonyms: follow-up, continuation, sequel, review, check-in|antonyms: prelude, beginning, start, initiation|noun

followup:/ˈfɑːloʊʌp/ (US) /ˈfɒləʊʌp/ (UK)| adj.|后续的，补充的|Following up on something previously done.|  This is a followup report on the incident. 这是一份关于该事件的后续报告。|synonyms: subsequent, additional, supplementary, continuing|antonyms: initial, original, primary, preceding|adjective
