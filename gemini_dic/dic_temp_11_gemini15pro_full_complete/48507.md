deergrass:/ˈdɪrˌɡræs/| n. |鹿草|A tall, tufted perennial grass (Muhlenbergia rigens) native to western North America, often used in landscaping for its attractive form and drought tolerance. It is also known as deer grass.|The deergrass provided excellent cover for the quail. 鹿草为鹌鹑提供了极好的掩护。| The landscape architect incorporated deergrass into the design for its drought tolerance. 景观设计师将鹿草纳入设计中，因为它具有耐旱性。 | 近义词:  purple muhly, Lindheimer's muhly | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: deer + grass.  The name "deergrass" comes from the fact that deer and other grazing animals often feed on it.
