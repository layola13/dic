extimulate:/ɛkˈstɪmjʊleɪt/
verb
刺激；激发
To excite or rouse; to stimulate intensely.
His words extimulated the crowd to a frenzy. 他的话煽动人群陷入疯狂。
近义词:stimulate, excite, arouse, provoke, incite
反义词:calm, soothe, pacify, quiet
词性:v.

Etymology: From Latin extimulāre, from ex- (“out, thoroughly”) + stimulāre (“to prick, goad, urge on”).
