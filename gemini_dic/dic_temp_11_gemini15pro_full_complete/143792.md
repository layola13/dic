outnoise:/ˌaʊtˈnɔɪz/ | v. |盖过…的声音；发出比…更大的噪音 |To make more noise than something else, so that it cannot be heard.| The band easily outnoised the crowd. 乐队的音量轻易盖过了人群的嘈杂声。| 近义词：drown out, overpower | 反义词：be drowned out, be subdued | 词性: 动词

词源:  out- + noise.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed."  "Noise" refers to sound, especially loud, unpleasant or disturbing sound.  Therefore, "outnoise" literally means to exceed or surpass in noise.
