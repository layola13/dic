candescence:/kænˈdɛsəns/| n.|白炽，炽热|The candescence of the molten metal was blinding. 熔融金属的白炽光令人目眩。|近义词：incandescence, glow, brilliance|反义词：darkness, dullness|noun
candescent:/kænˈdɛsənt/| adj.|白炽的，炽热的|The candescent filament in the light bulb glowed brightly. 灯泡里白炽的灯丝发出明亮的光芒。|近义词：incandescent, glowing, brilliant|反义词：dark, dull|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "candēscere" (to become white-hot), from "candēre" (to shine, glow).  Related to "candle."
