fission:/ˈfɪʃən/| n.|分裂，裂变|The fission of the atom produces tremendous energy.  原子的裂变产生巨大的能量。|例句：Nuclear fission is a powerful source of energy. 核裂变是一种强大的能源。|例句：The fission of the company into two separate entities was inevitable. 公司分裂成两个独立的实体是不可避免的。|近义词：splitting, division, separation|反义词：fusion, unification|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fissiō, from fissus, past participle of findere (“to split, cleave”).
