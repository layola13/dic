excisable:/ɛkˈsaɪzəbl/
adjective
可征税的；应纳消费税的
Subject to excise duty; liable to excise.
Excise taxes are levied on specific goods, such as gasoline, alcohol, and tobacco. 消费税是对特定商品征收的，例如汽油、酒精和烟草。
The government increased the tax on excisable goods. 政府提高了应税商品的税率。
Synonyms: taxable, dutiable
Antonyms: non-taxable, duty-free
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From excise + -able.  "Excise" itself comes from the Middle Dutch excijs, meaning "tax," ultimately from Late Latin accisa, "tax levied," from the past participle of accidere, meaning "to happen, befall," with the sense of "something that befalls one."
