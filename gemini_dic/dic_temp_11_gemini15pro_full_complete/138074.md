normalization:/ˌnɔːrmələɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 归一化，标准化 | The process of bringing or returning something to a normal condition or state.  The process of converting data to a common format or scale. |  Data normalization is crucial for accurate analysis. 数据标准化对于准确分析至关重要。 | synonyms: standardization, regularization, homogenization | antonyms: deviation, distortion, irregularity | noun
