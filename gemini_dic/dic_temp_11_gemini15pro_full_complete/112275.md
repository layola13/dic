loping:/ˈləʊpɪŋ/ (US) /ˈləʊpɪŋ/ (UK)| v. adj.|轻快地跑；跑；轻快的跑步；|present participle of lope|The dog was loping across the field. 狗轻快地跑过田野。|The rabbit loped away. 兔子轻快地跑开了。|近义词：cantering, bounding, trotting|反义词：sprinting, dashing, crawling|词性: verb (present participle), adjective

词源: From Middle English *lopen (“to leap, run”), from Old Norse hlaupa (“to run, leap, jump”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaną (“to run, leap, jump”). Cognate with Danish løbe (“to run”), Swedish löpa (“to run”), Icelandic hlaupa (“to run”).
