cross-sectoral:/ˌkrɒsˈsɛktərəl/ (US) /ˌkrɒsˈsɛktərəl/ (UK)| adj.|跨部门的，跨行业的|Involving or relating to different sectors of an economy, industry, or society.|例句：Cross-sectoral collaboration is essential to address complex social problems. 跨部门合作对于解决复杂的社会问题至关重要。

例句：The government is promoting cross-sectoral partnerships to boost innovation. 政府正在推动跨部门合作以促进创新。|近义词：interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, collaborative, joint, integrated|反义词：sector-specific, specialized, isolated|词性: adjective
