discontinue:/dɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ (US) /ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| vt. |停止，中断，终止|To stop doing, using, or providing something, especially something that has been done, used, or provided regularly.|The company discontinued the production of that model last year.  该公司去年停止了该型号的生产。|synonyms: cease, stop, terminate, abandon, halt, quit, drop, give up|antonyms: continue, start, begin, maintain, persist, resume|verb


discontinue:/dɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ (US) /ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| n. |停止，中断，终止|The act of discontinuing something.|The discontinuation of the service caused much inconvenience.  该服务的停止造成了很多不便。|synonyms: cessation, termination, abandonment, halt|antonyms: continuation, resumption, commencement|noun
