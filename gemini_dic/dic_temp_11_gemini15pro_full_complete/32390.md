chilled:/tʃɪld/ | adj. | 冷却的，冰镇的；冷静的，镇定的 |  Cooled, especially by refrigeration. Relaxed and calm. |  例句：I enjoyed a chilled glass of wine on the patio. 我在露台上享用了一杯冰镇葡萄酒。

例句：He remained remarkably chilled despite the chaos around him. 尽管周围一片混乱，他仍然保持着非凡的冷静。 | 近义词：cool, cold, relaxed, calm | 反义词：warm, heated, agitated, stressed | 词性: adjective

chilled:/tʃɪld/ | v. | （使）变冷，冷却；（使）冷静下来 | past tense and past participle of chill.  To make something cold. To become or cause someone to become calm.  | 例句：We chilled the champagne before serving it. 我们在上香槟之前先把它冰镇了一下。

例句：The news chilled her to the bone.  这消息让她毛骨悚然。 | 近义词：cool, refrigerate, soothe, relax | 反义词：heat, warm, agitate, excite | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "chill," which comes from Old English "ciele" meaning "cold," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*kaliz."
