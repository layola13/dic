accloy:/əˈklɔɪ/| vt.|使过饱，使腻烦，使厌倦|to fill or satiate to the point of loathing or disgust; surfeit|The rich food accloyed him.|近义词：satiate, surfeit, glut, cloy|反义词:starve, deprive|verb

词源:
From Middle English *acloyen, from Anglo-Norman acloier, from Old French encloer (“to nail up, plug up, stop up, clog”), from en- + clou (“nail”), from Latin clāvus.
