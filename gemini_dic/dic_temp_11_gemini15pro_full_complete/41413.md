corneocalcareous:/ˌkɔːrniːoʊkælˈkɛəriəs/
adjective
角质钙化的
Composed of horn and calcareous material; horny and calcareous.
The shells of some snails are corneocalcareous.  一些蜗牛的壳是角质钙化的。
Near Antonyms: fleshy, soft
Near Synonyms:  chitinous, shell-like
词性: adjective
