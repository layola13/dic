myrioscope:/mɪˈraɪəskoʊp/
noun
万花筒；千变万化的景象
An instrument which exhibits an endless variety of beautiful colours and symmetrical forms, chiefly by the multiplication of images of pieces of coloured glass, etc., in two mirrors inclined to each other at a small angle.  It also refers to anything that presents a constantly shifting scene or pattern.
Looking through the myrioscope was like peering into a kaleidoscope of infinite patterns.  透过万花筒就像凝视着无限图案的万花筒。
kaleidoscope, myriad
monotony
