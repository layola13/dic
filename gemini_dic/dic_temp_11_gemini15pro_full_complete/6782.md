anaphor:/ˈænəfɔːr/ (US), /ˈænəfɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|回指, 前指代词|A word or phrase that refers back to an earlier word or phrase in a text or conversation, especially a pronoun that substitutes for a noun phrase.|Example: The dog wagged its tail. It was happy.  狗摇着尾巴。它很高兴。|近义词：reference, antecedent|反义词:cataphor|noun
