globes:/ɡloʊbz/| n. |地球仪；球体；地球；世界|A spherical or rounded object; a representation of the earth or celestial sphere.|例句：She spun the globe, her finger landing on Africa. 她旋转地球仪，手指落在非洲上。

例句：The globes hanging in the classroom represented different planets. 教室里悬挂的球体代表不同的行星。|近义词：spheres, balls, worlds, earths|反义词: - |词性:noun
