compensation:/ˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmpenˈseɪʃn/| n.|补偿，赔偿，报酬|Something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss, injury, or suffering.|例句：He received compensation for the injuries he suffered in the accident. 他因事故受伤获得了赔偿。|近义词：remuneration, payment, recompense, restitution|反义词:penalty, forfeiture|名词

compensation:/ˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmpenˈseɪʃn/| v.|补偿，赔偿|Give (someone) something, typically money, in recognition of loss, suffering, or injury.|例句：They compensated him for the damage to his car.  他们赔偿了他汽车的损失。|近义词：reimburse, repay, indemnify|反义词: penalize, fine|动词
