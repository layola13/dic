animalism:/ˈænɪməˌlɪzəm/ | n. | 动物性；兽性；动物主义 | The doctrine that humans are merely animals with no spiritual dimension.  The state of being driven by basic instincts like hunger and aggression. |  His animalism was evident in his crude behavior. 他的兽性从其粗鲁的行为中显露无疑。 |  The philosophy of animalism denies the existence of a soul. 动物主义哲学否认灵魂的存在。 | 近义词： bestiality, savagery, brutality | 反义词： humanity, spirituality, civility | 词性: noun
