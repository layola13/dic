graani:/ˈɡrɑːni/ | n. | 谷粒，颗粒 | A small, hard seed, especially of a food plant like wheat or rye. |  The bird pecked at the graani scattered on the ground. 小鸟啄食散落在地上的谷粒。 | Synonyms: grain, kernel, seed | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a graani.  Could consider something large and indivisible, like a boulder.) | noun

Etymology:  From Hindi "grānī," ultimately from Sanskrit "grāṇi," meaning "grain," "kernel."
