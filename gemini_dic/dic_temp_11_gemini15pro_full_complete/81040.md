graffage:/ˈɡræfɪdʒ/ | n. |嫁接，接枝|The act or process of grafting, especially in horticulture.  The process of joining a scion (a cutting from one plant) onto a stock (a rooted plant) so that they grow together as one plant.| The gardener demonstrated the proper technique for graffage on apple trees. 园丁演示了苹果树嫁接的正确技巧。 | synonyms: grafting, implantation, insertion | antonyms: detachment, separation | noun
