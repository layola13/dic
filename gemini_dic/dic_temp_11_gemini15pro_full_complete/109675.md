leucophyll: /ˈljuːkəˌfɪl/ | n. | 白色叶; 白化叶 | A leaf, or part of a leaf, that is devoid of chlorophyll and consequently white or pale yellow. | Example: The leucophyll of the plant stood out against the green foliage.  这株植物的白化叶在绿叶中很显眼。| Example:  Genetic mutations can cause leucophyll in some plant species. 基因突变会导致某些植物物种出现白化叶。| Synonym: achlorophyllous leaf | Antonym: chlorophyllous leaf | noun

leucophyllous: /ˌljuːkəˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 白化叶的; 缺乏叶绿素的 | Characterized by or pertaining to leucophylls; lacking chlorophyll. | Example: The leucophyllous plant was unable to photosynthesize effectively.  这株白化叶植物无法有效地进行光合作用。 | Example: Some variegated plants have leucophyllous sections on their leaves. 一些杂色植物的叶子上有一些白化叶的部分。 | Synonym: achlorophyllous | Antonym: chlorophyllous | adjective
