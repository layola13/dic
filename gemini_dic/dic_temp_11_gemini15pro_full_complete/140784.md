omphalopsychite:/ˈɒmfələʊˈsaɪkaɪt/ | n. | 肚脐凝视者；沉思冥想者 | A person who engages in omphaloskepsis (contemplation of one's navel as an aid to meditation).  A mystic who attempts to achieve spiritual insight through contemplation of the navel. |  Example 1: The omphalopsychite spent hours in quiet contemplation, seeking enlightenment. 沉思冥想者花了几个小时静静地思考，寻求启迪。

Example 2:  He was known as an omphalopsychite, always lost in his own inner world. 他被称为肚脐凝视者，总是沉浸在自己的内心世界里。 | Synonym: mystic, contemplative, meditator | Antonym:  extrovert, materialist | noun
