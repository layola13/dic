fughetta:/fuˈɡɛtə/ | n. |赋格短曲，小赋格曲 |A short fugue.  A fugue is a musical composition in which one or two themes are repeated or imitated by successively entering voices. A fughetta is a shorter, simpler version of a fugue. | The composer included a delightful fughetta in the second movement. 作曲家在第二乐章中加入了一段令人愉悦的小赋格曲。| synonym: fuguelette, short fugue | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "extended fugue" or "complex fugue" could be considered contrasting concepts) | noun
