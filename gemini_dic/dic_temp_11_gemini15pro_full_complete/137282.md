nonsynchronous:/ˌnɒnˈsɪŋkrənəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪŋkrənəs/| adj.|非同步的，异步的|Not occurring at the same time; not synchronized.|The nonsynchronous nature of online learning allows students to study at their own pace.  在线学习的异步性使学生能够按照自己的进度学习。|例句：Nonsynchronous communication, like email, is becoming increasingly popular. 像是电子邮件这样的非同步沟通方式正变得越来越普及。|近义词：asynchronous, nonconcurrent|反义词: synchronous, concurrent|词性:形容词

nonsynchronous:/ˌnɒnˈsɪŋkrənəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪŋkrənəs/| n.|异步操作|An operation that does not complete immediately, but rather returns control to the caller while the operation continues in the background.|Nonsynchronous operations are essential for handling long-running tasks without blocking the main thread. 异步操作对于处理长时间运行的任务而不阻塞主线程至关重要。|例句：The system uses nonsynchronous data transfer to improve efficiency. 该系统使用异步数据传输以提高效率。|近义词：asynchronous operation|反义词: synchronous operation|词性:名词
