illacerable:/ɪˈlæsərəbəl/
adjective
不可撕裂的；无法摧毁的；不能根除的
not able to be torn, destroyed, or removed
例句：His reputation, once tarnished, proved illacerable.  他名誉一旦受损，就无法恢复了。

例句：The illacerable bond between mother and child.  母子之间牢不可破的联系。

近义词：indestructible, permanent, ineradicable
反义词：destroyable, eradicable, removable
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *illacerabilis, from in- "not" + lacerabilis "able to be torn," from lacerare "to tear, mangle," from lacer "torn, mangled."
