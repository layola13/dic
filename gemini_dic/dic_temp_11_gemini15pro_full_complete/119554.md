microsthene:/ˈmaɪkrəsˌθiːn/| n.|微斯登(磁力单位)|A unit of magnetic pole strength in the CGS electromagnetic system, equal to one-tenth of an abampere.  It represents the strength of a magnetic pole that exerts a force of one dyne on an equal pole placed 1 centimeter away in a vacuum.|The strength of the magnet was measured in microsthenes.  这块磁铁的强度是用微斯登来测量的。|近义词:unit of magnetic strength|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + -sthene, from Greek *sthénos "strength."
