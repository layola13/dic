nonhereditary:/ˌnɑːn.həˈred.ɪ.ter.i/
adjective
非遗传的
Not passed from parents to children through genes.
Nonhereditary conditions, such as obesity and high blood pressure, also increase a person's risk.  非遗传性疾病，例如肥胖和高血压，也会增加一个人的风险。
This form of diabetes is nonhereditary. 这种类型的糖尿病是非遗传性的。
Synonyms: acquired, non-genetic
Antonyms: hereditary, genetic, inherited
词性: 形容词
