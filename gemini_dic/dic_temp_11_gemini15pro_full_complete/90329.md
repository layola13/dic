housewright:/ˈhaʊsˌraɪt/| n.|房屋建造者，木匠|A skilled worker who builds or repairs wooden houses.  A carpenter, especially one who builds houses.|例句：The housewright carefully measured the timber before cutting it.  木匠在切割木材之前仔细地测量了它。

例句：The housewright's skill was evident in the intricate details of the roof.  木匠的技艺在屋顶复杂的细节中显而易见。|近义词：carpenter, builder, joiner|反义词:|n.
