inaccordant:/ˌɪnəˈkɔːrdənt/| adj.|不一致的，不和谐的，不协调的|Not in agreement or harmony; conflicting; discordant.  Characterized by a lack of agreement, correspondence, or harmony.|Her inaccordant views on the project caused friction within the team. 她在这个项目上不一致的观点导致了团队内部的摩擦。|Synonyms: discordant, dissonant, incompatible, conflicting, inconsistent, clashing, jarring|Antonyms: accordant, harmonious, consonant, compatible, consistent, agreeable|adjective
