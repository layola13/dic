The correct spelling is "crowd".

crowd: /kraʊd/
| n. |人群，群众；一堆，许多|A large number of people gathered together.|The crowd cheered for their team.  人群为他们的队伍欢呼。|近义词：throng, mob, horde, swarm|反义词: few, individuals|名词
crowd: /kraʊd/
| v. |拥挤，挤满；逼近|To fill a space with too many people or things.|People crowded around the stage. 人们挤在舞台周围。|近义词：pack, cram, throng|反义词: disperse, thin out|动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *crūdan, from Proto-Germanic *krūdaną (“to press, crowd, urge”).
