desertification: /dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 荒漠化 | the process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture. | Example: Desertification is a serious environmental problem in many parts of the world.  世界许多地方的荒漠化是一个严重的环境问题。| Example:  The ongoing desertification threatens the livelihoods of millions.  持续的荒漠化威胁着数百万人的生计。 | Synonym:  desertization, aridity, barrenness | Antonym:  afforestation, reforestation | Noun
