词源:  "kelp" + "ware".  "Kelp" refers to large brown seaweeds. "Ware" refers to manufactured articles of a particular type.  "Kelpware" is a relatively modern term combining these two elements.  “海带”指的是大型棕色海藻。“制品”指的是特定类型的制成品。“海带制品”是一个结合了这两个元素的相对现代的术语。
