graduating:/ˈɡrædʒueɪtɪŋ/| adj. | 毕业的，即将毕业的 | Completing an academic degree or program. |  例句：The graduating class of 2023 is very accomplished. 2023届毕业生非常有成就。

例句：She is graduating from college next year. 她明年大学毕业。| 近义词：finishing, completing, concluding | 反义词：commencing, beginning, starting | 词性：形容词
词源：From the Latin word "gradus," meaning "step" or "degree." It evolved through Old French and Middle English to become the modern English word "graduate." The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle form, denoting an ongoing action or state.
