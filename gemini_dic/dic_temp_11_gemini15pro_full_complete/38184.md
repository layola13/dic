comptroller:/kənˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK) /kənˈtroʊlər/ (US)| n.|主计长，审计官|A comptroller is a person who is in charge of the financial affairs of a company or government organization. The comptroller is responsible for ensuring that the organization's finances are managed effectively and efficiently.|The city comptroller announced the new budget.  市主计长宣布了新的预算。|Synonyms: controller, auditor|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

comptroller:/kənˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK) /kənˈtroʊlər/ (US)| n.|控制器|  In some contexts, comptroller can refer to a device or system that controls something. This usage is less common than the financial sense.|The temperature comptroller maintained a steady heat. 温度控制器保持稳定的热量。|Synonyms: regulator, governor|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
