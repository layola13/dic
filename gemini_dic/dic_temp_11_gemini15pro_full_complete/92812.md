iconoclastically:/aɪˌkɒnəˈklæstɪkli/
adverb
以打破传统的方式；反传统的
In a manner that attacks cherished beliefs or institutions.
He iconoclastically dismissed the sacred traditions of the art world. 他以打破传统的方式驳斥了艺术界的传统。
The artist iconoclastically shredded one of his own paintings in front of a shocked audience.  这位艺术家当着震惊的观众的面，以反传统的方式将自己的一幅画撕碎了。
Synonyms: rebelliously, subversively, heretically, unconventionally
Antonyms: traditionally, conventionally, orthodoxly, respectfully
词性: 副词
