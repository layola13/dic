gablet:/ˈɡeɪblɪt/| n.|三角墙，山墙|A small gable, especially a decorative one over a window or door.|The gablet above the window added a touch of charm to the cottage.  小屋窗户上方的小三角墙增添了一丝魅力。|Synonyms: gable end, pediment|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gablet:/ˈɡeɪblɪt/| n.|三角屋顶，小型山墙|A small, usually triangular portion of a wall at the end of a ridged roof, often containing a window.|Several gablets adorned the roofline of the historic building.  几座小三角墙装饰着这座历史建筑的屋顶线。|Synonyms: dormer, lunette|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gablel, diminutive of gable. Ultimately from Old Norse gafl.
