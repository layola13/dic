carpetway: /ˈkɑːrpɪtweɪ/ | n. | 地毯路; 铺着地毯的路 | A path or roadway covered with carpet. | The dignitaries walked along the carpetway to the entrance.  贵宾们沿着铺着地毯的路走向入口。 | 近义词: red carpet, aisle, pathway | 反义词: dirt road, trail | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound of "carpet" + "way".  "Carpet" comes from Old North French "carpite" (thick cloth), and "way" from Old English "weg" (road, path).
