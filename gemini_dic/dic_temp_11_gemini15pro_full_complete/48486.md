deepened:/ˈdiːpənd/ /ˈdiːpənd/| v. |加深，深化|The crisis deepened their love for each other. 这场危机加深了他们彼此的爱。|例句：His frown deepened. 他的眉头皱得更紧了。|例句：The mystery deepened when the police found a second body. 当警察发现第二具尸体时，这个谜团更加扑朔迷离了。|近义词：intensified, strengthened, escalated|反义词：lessened, decreased, diminished|词性:动词

Etymology->
From deep + -en, from Old English *dēopjan (attested only in past participle dēoped), from dēop "deep".
