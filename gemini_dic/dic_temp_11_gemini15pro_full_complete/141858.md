orchestrates:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪts/| v.|精心安排；策划；(使)协调|arranges or directs the elements of (a situation) so as to achieve a desired overall effect.|例句：He orchestrated the entire campaign. 他精心策划了整个竞选活动。

例句：The conductor orchestrated the various instruments beautifully. 指挥家优美地协调了各种乐器。

近义词：organizes, arranges, coordinates, manages, conducts, directs, masterminds|反义词:disorganizes, neglects|词性:动词
