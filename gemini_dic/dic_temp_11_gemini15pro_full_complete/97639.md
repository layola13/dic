inheritably:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəbli/| adv.|不可避免地，必然地|In a way that is impossible to avoid. |例句：Their actions will inheritably have consequences. 他们的行为必然会产生后果。|近义词：inevitably, necessarily, unavoidably|反义词: avoidably, contingently|词性:副词
词源: From inherit + -able + -ly.  "Inherit" comes from Old French "enheriter" (to receive as an heir), ultimately from Latin "haereditare" (to inherit).
