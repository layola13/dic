incorporal:/ɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/ |adj.|无形的，非物质的|Not consisting of a physical body; not material.|例句：Ghosts are often described as incorporeal beings.  鬼魂通常被描述为无形的生命体。

例句：The incorporeal nature of the soul has been debated for centuries.  灵魂的非物质性已经被争论了几个世纪。|近义词：immaterial, intangible, disembodied, spiritual|反义词：corporeal, material, physical, tangible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
Late 16th century: from late Latin incorporalis, from in- ‘not’ + corporalis (see corporeal).
