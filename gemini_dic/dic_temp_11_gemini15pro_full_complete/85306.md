harnesslike:/ˈhɑːrnɪsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像马具的，类似挽具的
Resembling a harness; similar in appearance or function to a harness used to control or restrain an animal, typically a horse.
The child wore a harnesslike device to prevent him from wandering off.  这个小孩穿戴着类似马具的装置以防他走丢。
The complex network of ropes looked harnesslike. 复杂绳索网络看起来像马具。
Near synonyms: harness-shaped, strappy, restrictive
Antonyms: loose, free, unrestrained
词性: 形容词
