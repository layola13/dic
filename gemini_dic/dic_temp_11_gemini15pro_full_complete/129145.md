no-fault:/ˌnoʊ ˈfɔːlt/ | adj. | 无过错的，免责的 |  Relating to or denoting a system of insurance or compensation in which blame or fault is not assigned, or a divorce in which neither party is held responsible for the breakdown of the marriage. |  "They have a no-fault divorce system in that state."  他们在那个州实行无过错离婚制度。 | Synonyms: blameless, guiltless | Antonyms: culpable, guilty | adjective

no-faults:/ˌnoʊ ˈfɔːlts/ | n. | 无过错保险（通常用单数） | A system of insurance or compensation in which blame or fault is not assigned.  |  "No-faults insurance can cover medical expenses regardless of who caused the accident." 无过错保险可以支付医疗费用，无论是谁造成了事故。| Synonyms: no-fault insurance | Antonyms: at-fault insurance | noun
