foreman:/ˈfɔːrmən/ (US), /ˈfɔːmən/ (UK)| n.|工头，领班|A worker who supervises and directs other workers, especially in a factory or on a construction site.|The foreman told the crew to start work at 7 a.m. 工头告诉工人早上7点开工。|近义词：supervisor, overseer, manager|反义词: subordinate, worker|noun

foreman:/ˈfɔːrmən/ (US), /ˈfɔːmən/ (UK)| n.|陪审团主席|A member of a jury who acts as its chairman or spokeswoman.|The foreman read the jury's verdict. 陪审团主席宣读了陪审团的裁决。|近义词：chairperson, leader|反义词: juror|noun
