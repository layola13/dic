costing:/ˈkɒstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɔːstɪŋ/ (US)| n. adj.|成本核算，成本计算; 估价; 耗费…的|The process of calculating the cost of something.  It can also be used as an adjective describing something related to cost calculation.|

例句：
The costing of the project took several weeks.  项目成本核算花了几个星期。

The costing exercise revealed some unexpected expenses. 成本核算工作揭示了一些意外支出。

近义词：estimation, valuation, pricing, appraisal|
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider 'profiting')|
词性: noun, adjective


costing:/ˈkɒstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɔːstɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|耗费…的|Causing something to cost a particular amount.|

例句：
That was a costing mistake. 那是一个代价高昂的错误。

This project is proving more costing than we anticipated. 这个项目比我们预期的耗费更大。

近义词：expensive, pricey|
反义词: inexpensive, cheap|
词性: adjective
