arenas:/əˈriːnəz/
n.
竞技场，舞台
a place or scene of activity, debate, or conflict
The political arena is a tough place to be. 政界是一个艰难的地方。

arenas:/əˈriːnəz/
n.
（尤指体育比赛用的）场地
a level area in the centre of an amphitheatre, in which contests and spectacles were held
The gladiators fought in the arena.  角斗士在竞技场战斗。


Etymology->
From Latin arēna (“sand, sandy place”), from PIE *h₂eh₁s- (“to burn”).  Originally referred to the sand-covered central area of an amphitheater.

Synonyms->
stadium, field, ground, venue, theater, stage

Antonyms->
stands, bleachers, audience, backstage
