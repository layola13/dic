mash:/mæʃ/ | n. vt. | 糊状物，麦芽浆；捣碎 | A soft pulpy mass. | The potatoes were mashed with butter and milk. 土豆泥是用黄油和牛奶捣成的。 | 近义词: pulp, puree, crush | 反义词: solidify, harden | 词性: 名词, 动词

mash:/mæʃ/ | vt. | 捣碎，压碎 | To crush or beat into a soft pulpy mass. | He mashed the bananas for the baby. 他把香蕉捣碎给婴儿吃。 | 近义词: crush, pulverize, squash | 反义词: solidify, harden | 词性: 动词

词源:  Old English *masc, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch mees ‘mash, curd’ and German Mais ‘maize’.
