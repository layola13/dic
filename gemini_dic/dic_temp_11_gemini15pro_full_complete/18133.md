behoovable:/bɪˈhuːvəbl/
adjective
適宜的；有益的；應該的
Suitable, proper, or fitting; incumbent (upon someone) as a duty or obligation.
It is behovable that he attend the meeting. 他应该参加这次会议。
近义词:appropriate, fitting, proper, suitable, incumbent, apt, seemly, decent, decorous, right
反义词:inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unfitting, unbecoming, indecent, indecorous, wrong
词性:adjective

词源:
From behoove + -able. Behoove comes from Old English *behōfian "to need, have need of, require," from Proto-Germanic *bihōfjan "to have need," from *bihōf "use, advantage, profit," from *bi- "by" + *hōf- "fitting, suitable."
