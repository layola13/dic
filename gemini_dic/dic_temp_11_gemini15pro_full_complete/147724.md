overpriced:/ˌoʊvərˈpraɪst/| adj.|定价过高，价格过高的|Charging too much; costing more than it is worth.|That restaurant is so overpriced; the food isn't that good. 那家餐馆定价过高；食物没那么好。|Example: The handbags in that boutique are ridiculously overpriced.  那个精品店里的手提包价格贵得离谱。|Synonyms: expensive, exorbitant, inflated, steep|Antonyms: cheap, inexpensive, reasonable, affordable|adjective
