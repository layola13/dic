outequivocate:/ˌaʊtɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/
verb
用模棱两可的话胜过；以含糊其辞的方式击败
to surpass in equivocating; to defeat by equivocation
He outequivocated the politician with his masterful use of vague language. 他以巧妙地运用模糊语言胜过了这位政客。
近义词:outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词:be straightforward, be direct
