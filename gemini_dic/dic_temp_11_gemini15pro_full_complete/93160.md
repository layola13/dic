ignite:/ɪɡˈnaɪt/| v. |点燃，引发，激起|To set on fire; to arouse or excite (feelings or passions).|The match ignited the dry leaves. 火柴点燃了干树叶。|The speaker's words ignited the crowd's enthusiasm. 演讲者的话激起了群众的热情。|近义词：kindle, inflame, spark|反义词：extinguish, quench|verb

ignite:/ɪɡˈnaɪt/| n. |点火，点燃|The act of igniting or the state of being ignited.|The ignition of the rocket was successful. 火箭点火成功。|Unfortunately, the ignition failed and the car wouldn't start. 不幸的是，点火失败了，汽车无法启动。|近义词：kindling, inflammation|反义词：extinguishing|noun
