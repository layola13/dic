hubspot:/ˈhʌbspɑːt/| n.|中心；枢纽；集聚地；（尤指公司业务的）核心；（软件） HubSpot|A software platform designed for inbound marketing, sales, and customer service.|HubSpot is a powerful tool for managing customer relationships. HubSpot是一个强大的客户关系管理工具。|synonyms: center, core, hub, focal point|antonyms: periphery, edge, margin|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"HubSpot" is a portmanteau of "hub" and "spot".  "Hub" refers to a central point of activity or interest, while "spot" suggests a specific location. The name implies that the software acts as a central point for all marketing, sales, and customer service activities.
