groundednesses:/ˈɡraʊndɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
扎根，根基，稳定性
The state of being grounded or stable, both literally and figuratively.
Her groundedness helped her through the difficult time.  她的稳定性帮助她度过了困难时期。
