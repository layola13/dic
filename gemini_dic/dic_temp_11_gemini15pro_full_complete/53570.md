disbelief:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːf/ | n. | 不相信，怀疑 | The feeling that something is not true. | She stared at him in disbelief. 她难以置信地盯着他。| synonyms: doubt, skepticism, incredulity | antonyms: belief, faith, trust | noun
