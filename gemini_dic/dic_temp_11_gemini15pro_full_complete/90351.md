hoveling:/ˈhʌvəlɪŋ/ | n. |  窝棚；简陋的住所；(尤指)悲惨的境况 |  A small, mean, or wretched dwelling. Also, a miserable or degrading situation. | They were hoveling in a one-room shack. 他们蜗居在一个单人间的小棚屋里。 | synonyms: shack, hut, slum, hovel | antonyms: mansion, palace, villa | noun

hovel:/ˈhɒvəl/ | n. | 窝棚；简陋的住所 | A small, squalid, or poorly built house. |  He lived in a tiny hovel with a dirt floor. 他住在一个地板是泥土的小棚屋里。 | synonyms: shack, hut, shanty | antonyms: mansion, palace, villa | noun

hovel:/ˈhɒvəl/ | vi. |  (尤指在肮脏或不舒服的地方)居住，蜗居 | To dwell in a small, squalid, or poorly built house. | They were hoveling in a cramped apartment. 他们蜗居在一间狭小的公寓里。 | synonyms:  squat, lodge, reside (in a poor dwelling) | antonyms: reside (in a comfortable dwelling) | verb
