OPEC:/ˈoʊpɛk/| n. |石油输出国组织|Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries.  A group of countries that export large amounts of petroleum and cooperate to set oil prices.|例句：OPEC's decision to cut oil production caused prices to rise. 石油输出国组织决定减产导致油价上涨。

例句：Many countries are not members of OPEC. 许多国家不是石油输出国组织的成员。|近义词：cartel, organization|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "independent oil producers")|词性:noun
