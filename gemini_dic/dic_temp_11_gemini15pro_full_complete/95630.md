incompetent: /ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənt/ (British) /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənt/ (American)| adj. |无能的；不称职的|not having the ability to do something successfully; not fit or suitable for a particular purpose|He was fired for being incompetent. 他因不称职而被解雇。|inept, unskilled, unqualified, incapable|competent, capable, skilled, qualified|adjective
