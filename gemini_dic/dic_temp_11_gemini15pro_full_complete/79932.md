gnomological:/ˌnɒməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
格言学的；箴言学的
Relating to gnomology, the study of gnomes or aphorisms.
Gnomic wisdom is often expressed in short, pithy sayings. 格言智慧通常以简短精辟的谚语来表达。
The professor's lecture was filled with gnomological pronouncements about the nature of truth. 教授的讲座充满了关于真理本质的格言式论断。
近义词: aphoristic, proverbial, sententious
反义词: verbose, prolix
