monophthong:/ˈmɒnəfθɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnəfθɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|单元音|A vowel sound that is pronounced with one unchanging sound quality, as opposed to a diphthong which changes sound quality during pronunciation.|The word "feet" has the monophthong /iː/.  "feet"这个单词包含单元音/iː/.|近义词:pure vowel|反义词:diphthong|词性:noun
