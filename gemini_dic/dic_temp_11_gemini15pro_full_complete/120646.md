mis-routed:/ˌmɪsˈruːtɪd/
adjective
中文翻译：路由错误的，发送错误的
英文解析：Incorrectly routed; sent to the wrong destination.  Formed by the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "routed" (past participle of "route," meaning to send something along a particular path).
例句：The mis-routed package finally arrived a week late.  路由错误的包裹最终晚了一周才到达。

例句：A mis-routed email led to a confidential document being sent to the wrong person. 一封路由错误的电子邮件导致一份机密文件被发送给了错误的人。
近义词：misdirected, wrongly addressed, wrongly sent
反义词：correctly routed, properly addressed
词性: adjective
