outwood:/ˈaʊtwʊd/
noun
外林地，郊区树林
A wood or forest situated outside a town or village.
The children played in the outwood all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在郊区树林里玩耍。
Synonyms: woodland, forest, woods
Antonyms: city, town
