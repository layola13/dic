misvalued:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuːd/
adjective
估值过低的，评价过低的
Incorrectly judged or assessed the value of something.
The company's assets were misvalued, leading to significant financial losses. 该公司的资产被低估了，导致了巨大的经济损失。
The painting was misvalued at auction and sold for much less than it was worth. 这幅画在拍卖会上被低估了，售价远低于其实际价值。

近义词: undervalued, underestimated, underrated
反义词: overvalued, overestimated, overrated
词性: adjective

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrongly, badly. From Old English, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly."
value (verb): to assess the worth of something. From Old French valoir, from Latin valēre "be strong, be worth."
-ed (suffix): forms the past participle of regular verbs, and also many adjectives.
