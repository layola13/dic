inextensible:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 不可伸展的，不能扩展的 | Not able to be extended or stretched; unyielding. |  The laws of physics are inextensible – they cannot be broken. 物理定律是不可伸展的——它们不能被打破。

The inextensible rope held the boat firmly in place.  不可伸展的绳索将船牢牢地固定在原地。 | 近义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding | 反义词: elastic, flexible, extensible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inextensibilis*, from in- "not" + extensibilis "stretchable," from extendere "to stretch out," from ex- "out" + tendere "to stretch."
