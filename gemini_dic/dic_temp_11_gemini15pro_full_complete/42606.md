counterquestion:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛstʃən/ /ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛsʧən/| n.|反问; 反诘|A question asked in response to another question, often to avoid answering the original question.|例句：When asked about his plans, he responded with a counterquestion: "Why do you want to know?" 当被问及他的计划时，他反问了一句：“你为什么想知道？”

例句：She deflected the interviewer's probing question with a clever counterquestion. 她用一个巧妙的反问避开了采访者尖锐的问题。|近义词：retort, comeback, riposte|反义词:answer, reply|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + question.  "Counter-" acts as a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to."  The word "question" comes from Old French "question" and Latin "quaestio," meaning "an inquiry."
