elinguate: /iːˈlɪŋɡweɪt/
verb
使…丧失语言能力；使…说不出话来
to deprive of the power of speech; to make speechless
She was so shocked by the news that she was completely elinguated. 她被这个消息震惊得完全说不出话来。
The accident elinguated him for the rest of his life.  这场事故使他余生都无法说话了。
synonyms: silence, dumbfound, stun
antonyms: articulate, express, vocalize
词性: 动词
