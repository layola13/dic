crock:/krɑːk/ /krɒk/| n. |瓦罐，破罐子；无用的人/物；废话；慢性病患者|An earthenware pot or jar.  A person or thing that is old and decrepit or useless. Nonsense. crock:/krɑːk/ /krɒk/| v. |（使）破裂；（使）虚弱|To break or crack. To become or cause to become weak or ill.|例句：The vase crock ed when it fell.  花瓶掉下来摔裂了。|近义词：break, crack, weaken, deteriorate|反义词:strengthen, improve, mend|词性:verb
