misassigns:/ˌmɪsəˈsaɪnz/
verb
错误分配，分配错误
to assign wrongly or incorrectly.
The algorithm misassigns some data points to the wrong cluster.  这个算法将一些数据点错误地分配到了错误的集群。
The teacher misassigned the essays, giving the wrong ones back to the students. 老师发错了作文，把错误的作文发回给了学生。
misallocate, misattribute
assign correctly
v.
