dryly:/ˈdraɪli/ /ˈdraɪlɪ/ | adv. |干燥地；冷淡地；枯燥无味地| in a dry manner; without emotion or humor|  "She dryly remarked that the weather was unpleasant." 她冷淡地说天气不好。 |Synonyms: aridly, dully, flatly, impassively, unemotionally | Antonyms: emotionally, expressively, humorously, warmly, wetly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Middle English *driliche, from Old English *dryglic, from dryge (dry) + -lic (like).
