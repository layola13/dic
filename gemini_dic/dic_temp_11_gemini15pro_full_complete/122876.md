molls:/mɑːlz/ | n. | 女朋友；女性同伙（尤指罪犯的）|  A girlfriend, especially of a gangster.  Sometimes used as a term of endearment, but can also be derogatory. | He took his moll to the fancy restaurant. 他带他的女朋友去了那家高档餐厅。| synonyms: girlfriend, sweetheart, lady friend, accomplice, partner (in crime) | antonyms: enemy, opponent | noun


moll:/mɑːl/ | n. | 女性罪犯 | A female criminal. | The moll was arrested for her involvement in the robbery.  这名女罪犯因参与抢劫而被捕。| synonyms: criminal, gangster, crook | antonyms: law enforcement officer, police officer | noun
