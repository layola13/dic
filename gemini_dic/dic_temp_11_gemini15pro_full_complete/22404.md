boogying: /ˈbuːɡiɪŋ/ | v. | 跳布吉舞，尽情摇摆 | Dancing energetically to rock music, typically in a disco or nightclub. |  例句：They were boogying the night away at the club. 他们在俱乐部里尽情摇摆，度过了一个夜晚。

例句：He was boogying on the dance floor. 他在舞池里尽情摇摆。

近义词：dancing, grooving, jiving | 反义词：sitting, standing still | 词性：动词
