book-up:/bʊk ˈʌp/
verb phrase
预订满，预约满
to reserve something, such as a hotel room or a ticket, in advance, so that it is not available for anyone else.  This often implies that everything is fully reserved.
The hotel is booked up for the weekend.  这家酒店周末已被预订满。
All the flights to London are booked up for the holiday season. 所有飞往伦敦的航班在节假日都已订满。
近义词: reserve, sell out, fully booked
反义词: available, vacant
词性: verb phrase
