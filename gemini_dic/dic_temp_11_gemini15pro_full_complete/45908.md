cutlet:/ˈkʌtlət/| n. | 肉片，炸肉排 | A small, thin piece of meat, typically veal, lamb, or pork, that is usually fried or grilled. |  I ordered a veal cutlet for dinner. 我晚餐点了一份小牛肉片。

She prefers chicken cutlets to beef steaks. 她喜欢鸡排胜过牛排。

| 近义词:chop, escalope, fillet | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "roast" as a contrasting cooking method) | 词性: noun
