cyclonoscope:/saɪˈkləʊnəˌskoʊp/| n.|气旋镜，旋风镜|An instrument for detecting the presence of cyclones, especially tornadoes.|例句：The cyclonoscope was invented in the late 19th century to provide early warning of approaching tornadoes. 气旋镜发明于19世纪后期，用于提供龙卷风逼近的早期预警。

例句：While not widely adopted, the cyclonoscope represented an early attempt to predict severe weather. 虽然没有被广泛采用，但气旋镜代表了早期预测恶劣天气的尝试。|近义词：tornado detector, anemometer|反义词: None|词性: Noun
