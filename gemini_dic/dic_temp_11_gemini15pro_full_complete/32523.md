chintzy: /ˈtʃɪnzi/ | adj. | 廉价的，俗气的 | Cheaply made or gaudy; of poor quality or taste.  Showing a lack of taste; tawdry. | The decorations were rather chintzy and looked like they came from a dollar store.  这些装饰相当俗气，看起来像是从一元店买的。

The fabric felt chintzy and thin. 这布料摸起来很廉价很薄。 | 近义词: tacky, gaudy, cheap, tawdry | 反义词: elegant, refined, sophisticated, expensive | 词性: adjective
