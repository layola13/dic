nonsocietal:/nɒnˌsəˈsaɪətl/
adjective
非社会的
Not relating to society; not connected with the organization of society.
The hermit lived a nonsocietal existence, far removed from the hustle and bustle of city life.  这位隐士过着非社会化的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。
This research focuses on nonsocietal factors influencing individual development. 这项研究关注的是影响个人发展的非社会因素。
Near Antonyms: social, societal
Antonyms: n/a
词性: adjective
