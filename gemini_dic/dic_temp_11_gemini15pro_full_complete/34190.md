clamp-down:/ˈklæmp daʊn/ (US), /ˈklæmp daʊn/ (UK)| n.|压制，取缔，打击|A sudden and strict action taken to stop or control something, often illegal or undesirable activity.|The government imposed a clamp-down on illegal immigration. 政府对非法移民进行了打击。|近义词：crackdown, suppression, repression|反义词:leniency, permissiveness|noun

clamp-down:/ˈklæmp daʊn/ (US), /ˈklæmp daʊn/ (UK)| v.|压制，取缔，打击|To suddenly and strictly take action to stop or control something, often illegal or undesirable activity.|The authorities clamped down on drug trafficking. 当局打击了贩毒活动。|近义词：crack down on, suppress, repress|反义词:tolerate, permit|verb
