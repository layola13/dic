malayic:/məˈleɪɪk/ | adj. | 马来语的；马来人的；马来文化的 | Relating to the Malay language, people, or culture. |  The malayic script is derived from Arabic. 马来文字源于阿拉伯文。| synonyms: Malay, Malaysian | antonyms: non-Malayic | adjective


malayic:/məˈleɪɪk/ | n. | 马来人；马来语 | A member of a people inhabiting the Malay Peninsula and parts of the western Malay Archipelago or their language. |  She is a native Malayic speaker. 她是一位马来语母语人士。 | synonyms: Malay, Malaysian | antonyms: non-Malayic | noun
