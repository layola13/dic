alkalimetry: /ˌælkəˈlɪmɪtri/ | n. | 碱量滴定法；酸碱滴定法 | The process of measuring the amount of alkali in a solution. | Alkalimetry is a common technique in analytical chemistry. 碱量滴定法是分析化学中常用的技术。 | The student learned the principles of alkalimetry in the lab. 学生在实验室学习了碱量滴定法的原理。 | Synonyms: acidimetry | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French alcalimètre, from alcali (“alkali”) + -mètre (“-meter”).  Alkali ultimately comes from Arabic *al-qalīy (“ashes of saltwort”). -meter comes from Greek métron (“measure”).
