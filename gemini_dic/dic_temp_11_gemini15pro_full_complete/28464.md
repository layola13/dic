carburize:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/ /ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪz/| v. |渗碳，使碳化|To introduce carbon into (a metal, especially iron or steel) in order to harden or strengthen it. |The gears were carburized to increase their durability. 齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐用性。

The process carburizes the surface of the steel. 这个过程使钢的表面渗碳。|近义词：carbonize, cement|反义词：decarburize|词性：动词
