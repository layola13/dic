ascococcus:/ˌæskəˈkɒkəs/| n. |囊球菌属|A genus of bacteria in the family Micrococcaceae, characterized by spherical cells occurring in irregular masses.  These bacteria are typically found in freshwater and soil environments.|Ascococcus are known to produce carotenoid pigments.|近义词:Micrococcus (more general),  Planococcus|反义词:None (specific genus, no direct opposite)|noun
