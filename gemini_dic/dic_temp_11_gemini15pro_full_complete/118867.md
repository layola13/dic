metapterygium:/ˌmɛtəpˈtɛrɪdʒɪəm/
noun
后鳍基骨; 基鳍骨
The basal internal skeletal element of the pectoral fin of fishes, or the homologous element in other vertebrates.
The metapterygium is a crucial part of a fish's fin structure. 后鳍基骨是鱼鳍结构的关键部分。
Sharks have a well-developed metapterygium.  鲨鱼有发达的后鳍基骨。
Synonyms: basal fin support, fin base
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, as it is a specific anatomical term.)
