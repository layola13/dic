focalize:/ˈfoʊkəlaɪz/
verb
聚焦；集中
to focus or concentrate (something, such as attention) on something
The photographer focalized the lens on the flower. 摄影师把镜头对准了花朵。
Synonyms: focus, concentrate, center
Antonyms: diffuse, scatter, disperse
词性:v.
