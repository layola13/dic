miscut:/mɪsˈkʌt/
noun
错误切割，裁切错误
an inaccurate or faulty cut, especially of a sheet of material or a playing card.
The miscut on the stamp made it more valuable.  这张邮票的裁切错误反而使它更有价值。
mis-cut
verb
错误地切割，裁切错误
to cut something inaccurately or faultily.
The printer miscut the sheets of paper.  打印机错误地裁切了纸张。
miscutting
adjective
错误切割的，裁切错误的
cut inaccurately or faultily.
The miscut cards were discarded.  裁切错误的卡片被丢弃了。
