eighteenths:/ˌeɪˈtiːnθs/
|n.|十八分之一|Represents one of eighteen equal parts of a whole.  It is the fractional form of 1/18.|One eighteenth of the pizza was left. 披萨还剩十八分之一。|近义词: one part out of eighteen|反义词: whole, entirety|词性: noun


eighteenth:/ˌeɪˈtiːnθ/
|adj.|第十八的；十八分之一的|Used to indicate the position of something in a series of eighteen or to represent one of eighteen equal parts.|This is the eighteenth time I've told you!  这是我第十八次告诉你！ He ate one eighteenth of the cake. 他吃了蛋糕的十八分之一。|近义词: (as ordinal) following seventeenth; (as fraction) one out of eighteen|反义词: first, whole|词性: adjective or noun (when referring to the fraction)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *eahtatēoða (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *ahtōtaihunda, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *oḱtṓw + *-to + *-ko-. Related to eight and -th (suffix indicating ordinal numbers or fractions).
