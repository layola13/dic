mistrial: /ˈmɪsˌtraɪəl/ | n. |无效审判，流产的审判 | A trial that is not considered valid because of a fundamental error in procedure, or because the jury cannot agree on a verdict. | The judge declared a mistrial after a juror was found to have been influenced by outside information. 法官宣布审判无效，因为发现一名陪审员受到了外界信息的影响。

The case ended in a mistrial due to a hung jury.  由于陪审团无法达成一致意见，此案以无效审判告终。| 近义词: aborted trial, invalid trial | 反义词: successful trial, valid trial | 词性: 名词

词源: mis- (wrong, bad) + trial (judicial examination)  来自古法语 *mes-  (wrongly) 和拉丁语 *trialis (pertaining to a trial)
