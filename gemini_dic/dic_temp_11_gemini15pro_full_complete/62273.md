encyclopedical:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪkəl/ /ɪnˌsaɪkloʊˈpiːdɪkəl/
adjective
百科全书式的；包罗万象的
covering a wide range of knowledge, like an encyclopedia.
The professor's knowledge of history was truly encyclopedical.  这位教授对历史的了解真是百科全书式的。
His encyclopedical knowledge of wine impressed everyone. 他对葡萄酒的渊博知识给每个人都留下了深刻印象。
comprehensive, exhaustive, wide-ranging, vast
limited, narrow, restricted
词性: 形容词
