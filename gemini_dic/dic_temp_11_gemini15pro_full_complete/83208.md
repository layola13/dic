guzzler:/ˈɡʌzlər/| n.|耗油量大的汽车；贪吃的人；狂饮者|A vehicle that consumes large quantities of fuel. A person who eats or drinks something greedily or in large amounts.|That old car is a real gas guzzler. 那辆旧车真是个耗油大户。|近义词：gas hog, glutton, heavy drinker|反义词:fuel-efficient car, moderate eater, light drinker|词性:名词
