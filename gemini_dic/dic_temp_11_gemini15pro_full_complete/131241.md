noncarbohydrate:/ˌnɒnˌkɑːbəʊˈhaɪdreɪt/ /ˌnɑːnˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt/| adj. n.|非碳水化合物的，非碳水化合物|A substance that is not a carbohydrate, especially one (such as a fat or protein) used by the body for producing energy.|例句：A noncarbohydrate diet is a diet that restricts the intake of carbohydrates. 非碳水化合物饮食是一种限制碳水化合物摄入的饮食。

例句：Noncarbohydrate sweeteners are used as sugar substitutes. 非碳水化合物甜味剂用作糖的替代品。|近义词：non-saccharide|反义词:carbohydrate|词性:形容词，名词
