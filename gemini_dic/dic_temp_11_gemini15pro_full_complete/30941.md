chairmen: /ˈtʃɛəmən/ | n. |主席，董事长|The plural form of "chairman," referring to multiple people holding the position of chairman.  It is now often considered outdated and gender-biased. |The chairmen of the various committees met to discuss the issue. 各个委员会的主席开会讨论这个问题。|Synonyms: chairs, chairpersons, presidents, heads | Antonyms: subordinates, members | Noun
