adopts:/əˈdɒpts/ (UK), /əˈdɑːpts/ (US)| v.| 采用，采取；收养；正式通过| To take and use as one's own; to formally accept or approve.| The company adopts a new technology.  这家公司采用了一项新技术。| synonyms: embraces, accepts, chooses, approves | antonyms: rejects, abandons, discards | verb

adopts:/əˈdɒpts/ (UK), /əˈdɑːpts/ (US)| v.| 收养| To legally take another's child and bring it up as one's own.| They adopted a baby girl from China. 他们从中国收养了一个女婴。| synonyms: fosters, takes in | antonyms: gives up, returns | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *adopten*, from Latin *adoptāre* (“to choose for oneself, adopt”), from *ad* (“to”) + *optāre* (“to choose, wish”).
