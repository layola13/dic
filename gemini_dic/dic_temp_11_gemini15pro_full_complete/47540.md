词源:

From Old English *dēaðlīcnes, from dēaðlīc (“deadly”) + -nes, equivalent to deadly +‎ -ness. Cognate with Old Frisian dādliknes, Old Saxon *dōdliknissa, Old High German tōtlihnissi.
