ordering:/ˈɔːrdərɪŋ/ | n. |排序，订购，点菜|The process of arranging things in a systematic way, or the act of requesting goods or services.|例句：The ordering of the files is alphabetical. 文件的排序是按字母顺序排列的。 |例句：Online ordering is convenient. 在线订购很方便。| 近义词：arrangement, organization, sequencing, booking, purchasing | 反义词：disorder, chaos, cancellation | noun


ordering:/ˈɔːrdərɪŋ/ | adj. |订购的，排序的 | Relating to the act of ordering or arranging. | 例句：The ordering process was simple and straightforward. 订购过程简单明了。| 例句：He has an ordering mind. 他思维很有条理。 | 近义词：arranging, organizing, sequencing | 反义词：disordering, chaotic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *orderynge*, from Old French *ordener* (to order), from Latin *ōrdināre* (to arrange, regulate), from *ōrdō* (order, rank).
