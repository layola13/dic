naggers:/ˈnæɡərz/| n.|唠叨的人；爱抱怨的人|People who constantly nag or complain, especially about minor things.  They can be irritating and tiresome.|例句：My parents are such naggers about my messy room. 我的父母总是对我凌乱的房间唠叨个不停。

例句：He's a real nagger about keeping the kitchen clean. 他真是个爱抱怨厨房不干净的家伙。|近义词：complainers, whiners, scolds|反义词：approvers, praisers|词性：名词
