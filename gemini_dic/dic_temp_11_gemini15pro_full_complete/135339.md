nonpenetrable:/nɒnˈpɛnɪtrəbl/ /nɑːnˈpɛnɪtrəbl/| adj.|不可穿透的，难以理解的|Not able to be penetrated, pierced, or entered; impervious. nonpenetrable:/nɒnˈpɛnɪtrəbl/ /nɑːnˈpɛnɪtrəbl/| adj.|难以理解的|Difficult or impossible to understand; inscrutable.|His motives were nonpenetrable. 他的动机难以理解。|Synonyms: inscrutable, incomprehensible, unfathomable, mysterious|Antonyms: understandable, comprehensible, clear, lucid|adjective
