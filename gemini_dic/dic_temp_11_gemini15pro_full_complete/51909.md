dhoti:/ˈdoʊti/| n. |缠腰布，男士围裙|A garment worn by men in India, Nepal, and Bangladesh, consisting of a long piece of cloth wrapped around the waist and legs. | He wore a crisp white dhoti. 他穿着一件干净的白缠腰布。|  He wrapped the dhoti around his waist. 他把缠腰布围在腰间。|近义词：lungi, mundu|反义词:trousers, pants| noun
