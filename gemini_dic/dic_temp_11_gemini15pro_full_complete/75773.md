ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fulsomnes*, from Old English *fūlsumnes*, from *fūlsum* (“foul, loathsome, excessive”) + *-nes*. More at fulsome.  来自中古英语 *fulsomnes*，源自古英语 *fūlsumnes*，由 *fūlsum*（“污秽的，令人讨厌的，过多的”）+ *-nes* 组成。更多信息请参见 fulsome。
