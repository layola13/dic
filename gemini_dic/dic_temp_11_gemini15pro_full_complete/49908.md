demystify:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 揭秘，使不再神秘 | to make something easier to understand by explaining it in a clear way | The book aims to demystify the world of finance. 这本书旨在揭开金融世界的神秘面纱。 |  The speaker demystified the complex scientific concepts for the audience. 演讲者为观众揭开了复杂的科学概念的神秘面纱。 | synonyms: clarify, explain, simplify, elucidate | antonyms: obfuscate, confuse, complicate | verb

Etymology:
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + mystify (from mystic + -fy).  Ultimately from Greek μύστης (mustēs, "initiate into mysteries")
