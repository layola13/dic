anemograph:/ˈænɪməɡrɑːf/|/ˈænɪməɡræf/| n. |风速计；风力自记器|An instrument that records the speed and direction of the wind.  It creates a permanent graphical record of wind speed variations over time.|The anemograph showed a sharp increase in wind speed just before the storm hit. 风速计显示，就在暴风雨来临之前，风速急剧上升。

The anemograph at the airport registered gusts of up to 70 miles per hour. 机场风速计记录到阵风高达每小时70英里。|近义词：wind gauge, wind meter|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *anemos* (wind) + *graphein* (to write)
