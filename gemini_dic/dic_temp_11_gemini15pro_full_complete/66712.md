exophthalmos:/ˌɛksɒfˈθælməs/| n. |眼球突出|Abnormal protrusion of the eyeball or eyeballs.|例句：Graves' disease can cause exophthalmos.  格雷夫斯病会导致眼球突出。

例句：The doctor noticed the patient's exophthalmos and suspected a thyroid problem. 医生注意到病人的眼球突出，怀疑是甲状腺问题。|近义词：proptosis|反义词:enophthalmos|词性:noun

exophthalmy:/ˌɛksɒfˈθælmi/| n. |眼球突出|Synonym of exophthalmos, referring to the abnormal protrusion of one or both eyeballs.|例句：Exophthalmy is a common symptom of hyperthyroidism. 眼球突出是甲状腺功能亢进症的常见症状。

例句：The patient's exophthalmy was causing him discomfort and affecting his vision. 病人的眼球突出使他感到不适，并影响了他的视力。|近义词：proptosis, exophthalmos|反义词:enophthalmos|词性:noun
