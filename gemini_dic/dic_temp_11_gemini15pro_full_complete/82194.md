Often refers to language or behavior that is considered offensive or inappropriate in polite society.  Can also describe something that is roughly or coarsely made.|He made a grossier joke that offended everyone. 他开了一个粗俗的玩笑，冒犯了所有人。|Synonyms: vulgar, crude, coarse, indecent, obscene, ribald, bawdy|Antonyms: refined, polite, elegant, sophisticated, cultured|adjective
