depainter:/diːˈpeɪntər/| n.|去涂料器，去漆器；图像修复工具（去除图像中不需要的部分）|A software or tool that removes unwanted elements from an image, often used for image restoration or editing.  It "de-paints" the image.|例句：The depainter tool helped me remove the graffiti from the photo of the historic building. 去涂料工具帮助我去除了那张历史建筑照片上的涂鸦。

例句：I used a depainter to remove the distracting power lines in the landscape picture. 我使用了一个去漆器去除了风景画中碍眼的电线。|近义词：inpainter, image restoration tool, image editing software|反义词:painter, image creation tool|noun
