atopic:/əˈtɑːpɪk/ | adj. |特应性的，异位的|Relating to or caused by atopy, a genetic tendency to develop allergic diseases such as eczema, asthma, and hay fever.  Characterized by a localized allergic reaction to a substance that does not normally cause a reaction in most people.|  Example: Atopic dermatitis is a common skin condition. 特应性皮炎是一种常见的皮肤病。|Synonym: allergic, hypersensitive|Antonym: non-atopic, non-allergic|adjective

atopic:/əˈtɑːpɪk/ | n. |特应性个体|A person with atopy, a genetic tendency to develop allergic diseases.| Example:  Many atoptics suffer from seasonal allergies. 许多特应性个体患有季节性过敏。|Synonym: allergic individual|Antonym: non-atopic individual|noun
