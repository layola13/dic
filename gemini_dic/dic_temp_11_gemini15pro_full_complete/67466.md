extolment:/ɪkˈstəʊlmənt/ /ɪkˈstoʊlmənt/
noun
赞美；颂扬；称赞
The act of praising something or someone highly.
The extolment of the king's virtues was common in the court.  国王的品德在朝廷上受到普遍赞扬。
The poem was an extolment of nature's beauty.  这首诗是对自然之美的赞美。
近义词: praise, laudation, commendation, acclaim, eulogy
反义词: criticism, condemnation, denunciation, censure
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *extolment*, from Anglo-Norman *extolement*, from Old French *extolement*, from *extoller* (“to extol, praise”), from Latin *extollō* (“I raise, lift up; elevate, exalt; praise”), from *ex-* (“out, up”) + *tollō* (“I raise, lift up”).
