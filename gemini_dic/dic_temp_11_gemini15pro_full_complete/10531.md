appositional:/ˌæpəˈzɪʃənl/
adjective
同位语的
Relating to or characterized by apposition, a grammatical construction in which two nouns or noun phrases are placed side by side, with one identifying or explaining the other.
My friend, a doctor, helped me with my injury. 我的朋友，一位医生，帮助我处理了我的伤。
The capital city, London, is very busy. 首都伦敦非常繁忙。

synonyms: juxtaposed, coordinating, equivalent
antonyms: disparate, unrelated, disconnected
adjective
