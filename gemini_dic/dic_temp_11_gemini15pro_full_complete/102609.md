jailless:/ˈdʒeɪllɪs/
adjective
无监禁的，免于监禁的
Free from imprisonment; not subject to imprisonment.
The judge granted the defendant a jailless sentence. 法官判处被告无监禁的刑罚。
The program offers jailless alternatives for non-violent offenders.  该计划为非暴力犯罪者提供无需监禁的替代方案。
近义词:unimprisoned, unconfined, free
反义词:imprisoned, jailed, incarcerated
词性:adjective

Etymology:
"jail" + "-less"  "jail" comes from Old French "jaiole," ultimately from Latin "cavea" (cage).  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
