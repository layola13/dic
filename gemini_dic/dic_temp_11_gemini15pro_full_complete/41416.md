corner-shop:/ˈkɔːnə ʃɒp/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrnər ʃɑːp/ (US)| n. |街角商店；街角小店|A small shop, often on a street corner, selling groceries and other everyday items. |例句：I popped down to the corner shop for some milk. 我去街角小店买些牛奶。

例句：The corner shop sells everything from newspapers to bread. 这家街角小店出售从报纸到面包的各种商品。|近义词：convenience store, newsagent, bodega (US), mini-mart|反义词：supermarket, hypermarket|词性：名词
