countercycle:/ˈkaʊntərˌsaɪkəl/| n. |反周期，逆周期|A countercycle is a trend or movement that goes against the prevailing economic cycle. It often involves investing in assets that perform well when the broader economy is struggling. |During the economic downturn, the company adopted a countercycle investment strategy. 在经济低迷时期，该公司采取了反周期投资策略。|近义词:contracyclical|反义词:procyclical|名词

countercycle:/ˌkaʊntərˈsaɪklɪkəl/| adj. |反周期的，逆周期的|Relating to or going against the economic cycle.  It often describes policies or investment strategies designed to mitigate the effects of economic fluctuations. |The government implemented countercyclical fiscal policies to stimulate the economy. 政府实施了反周期财政政策以刺激经济。|近义词:contracyclical|反义词:procyclical|形容词
