chapeau:/ʃæˈpoʊ/ (US) /ʃaˈpəʊ/ (UK)| n.|帽子; (尤指) 女帽; 敬意, 称赞|A hat, especially a woman's hat. An expression of admiration or approval.|She wore a wide-brimmed chapeau to the garden party. 她戴着一顶宽边帽去参加了花园派对。|  I take my chapeau off to her; she did a fantastic job. 我向她致敬，她做得非常出色。|synonyms: hat, bonnet, millinery|antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical object like a hat.  For the figurative meaning, one could consider "criticism" or "disapproval")|noun
