ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, lack, or reversal) + satisfaction.  The word "satisfaction" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French satisfaccion, from late Latin satisfactio(n-), from the verb satisfacere ‘do enough, satisfy,’ from satis ‘enough’ + facere ‘do.’
