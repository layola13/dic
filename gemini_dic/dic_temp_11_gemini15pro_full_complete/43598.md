creamily:/ˈkriːmɪli/| adv.|乳脂状地，柔滑地|In a smooth, rich way, like cream.  The sauce coated the pasta creamily.|例句：The soup was creamily blended. 汤被搅拌得很柔滑。

The chocolate mousse was incredibly rich and creamily smooth.  巧克力慕斯浓郁无比，柔滑如奶油。|近义词：smoothly, richly, luxuriously|反义词:thinly, coarsely|词性:副词
Etymology->
From cream + -ily.  "Cream" originates from Old French "cresme," ultimately from Latin "chrisma" meaning "ointment."  The suffix "-ily" transforms adjectives into adverbs, indicating manner.
