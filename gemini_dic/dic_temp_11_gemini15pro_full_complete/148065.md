oversanguineness:/ˌoʊvərˈsæŋɡwɪnɪsnəs/
|n.| 过度乐观，过于自信|The state of being excessively hopeful or confident; unrealistic optimism.|His oversanguineness about the company's future led to some risky investments. 他对公司前景的过度乐观导致了一些冒险的投资。| 近义词：optimism, hopefulness, buoyancy|反义词：pessimism, cynicism, despondency|词性: noun
Etymology: From over- + sanguine + -ness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Sanguine" comes from Latin "sanguis" meaning "blood,"  referring to the medieval belief that a cheerful disposition was caused by an abundance of blood. "-ness" forms a noun denoting a state or quality.
