klipfish:/ˈklɪpfɪʃ/ | n. | 咸鱼干 | Dried and salted cod, especially from Norway. |  We had klipfish for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了咸鱼干。| 近义词: salt cod, dried cod | 反义词: fresh cod | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Norwegian *klippfisk*, from *klippe* "cliff, rock" + *fisk* "fish".  Originally referring to cod dried on cliffs.
