hyperdelicateness:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkətnəs/
|n.|过度精致; 过分讲究; 过于敏感|The hyperdelicateness of the fabric made it unsuitable for everyday wear.  这种织物过于精致，不适合日常穿着。|Extreme delicacy, over-refinement, fastidiousness|She handled the antique vase with hyperdelicateness, fearing it would break. 她小心翼翼地拿着古董花瓶，生怕它会碎。|近义词: fastidiousness, over-refinement, sensitivity, fragility|反义词: robustness, coarseness, crudeness|词性:noun

Etymology:
From hyper- + delicateness.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive." "Delicateness" comes from the Old French "delicat" and ultimately from the Latin "delicatus," meaning "delightful, alluring, dainty."
