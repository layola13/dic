dialectology:/ˌdaɪəlekˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌdaɪəlekˈtɑːlədʒi/
n.
方言学
The scientific study of linguistic dialect, a sub-field of sociolinguistics.
Dialectology investigates the geographical and social distribution of different varieties of a language. 方言学研究一种语言不同变体的地域和社会分布。

Dialectology helps us understand how language changes over time and across different regions. 方言学帮助我们理解语言是如何随着时间和跨不同地区变化的。


Synonym:
linguistic geography, dialectography

Antonym:
(No direct antonym, but perhaps consider concepts like "standardization" or "linguistic homogeneity")

词性: noun
