disformity:/dɪsˈfɔːrmɪti/| n.|畸形，残疾|A disformity is a difference in the shape or appearance of a body part that is considered abnormal.  It can be congenital (present at birth) or acquired (developed later in life).|The child was born with a disformity of the foot.  这个孩子出生时脚有畸形。|Synonyms: deformity, malformation, abnormality, imperfection|Antonyms: normality, perfection|noun
