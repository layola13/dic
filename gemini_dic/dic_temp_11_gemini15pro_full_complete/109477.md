leprechaunish:/ˌlep-rəˈkɒn-ɪʃ/ | adj. | 小妖精似的，淘气的，恶作剧的 | Resembling or characteristic of a leprechaun, especially in being mischievous or tricky. |  The child had a leprechaunish glint in his eye as he hid his sister's toy.  这孩子藏起姐姐的玩具时，眼里闪过一丝恶作剧的光芒。 | synonyms: mischievous, impish, playful, puckish, roguish, tricky, waggish | antonyms: well-behaved, serious, solemn, angelic | 词性: 形容词
