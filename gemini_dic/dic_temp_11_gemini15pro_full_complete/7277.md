angostura:/ˌæŋɡəˈstʊərə/ | adj. | 安古斯图拉的 | Relating to or derived from angostura bark. | The angostura bitters added a unique flavor.  安古斯图拉苦酒增添了独特的风味。| Synonyms: (No direct synonyms, could use descriptive phrases like "bitter-tasting," "aromatic") | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms) | adjective
