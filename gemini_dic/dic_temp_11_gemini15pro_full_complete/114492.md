malacosteon:/ˌmæləˈkɒstiən/
n.
软骨
softened bone; bone affected with malacosteon
The doctor diagnosed the patient with malacosteon. 医生诊断病人患有软骨症。
近义词:osteomalacia, soft bone
反义词:hard bone
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Greek *malakos* (soft) + *osteon* (bone)
