fluky:/ˈfluːki/ | adj. | 侥幸的，意外的，偶然的 | Happening by chance rather than skill or planning; unexpected and fortunate. |  She won the lottery – it was a completely fluky win. 她中了彩票——这完全是侥幸的胜利。|  He made a fluky shot that won the game. 他侥幸投中一球赢得了比赛。 |  fortunate, lucky, accidental, chance |  unlucky, planned, intentional | adjective

fluke:/fluːk/ | n. | 侥幸，意外的成功 | An unlikely chance occurrence, especially a surprising piece of luck. | His success was just a fluke. 他的成功只是侥幸。 | By some amazing fluke, I ran into my old friend on the streets of Paris.  真是太巧了，我在巴黎的街道上遇到了我的老朋友。|  stroke of luck, windfall, godsend | misfortune, bad luck | noun
