overcareful: /ˌoʊvərˈkɛrfəl/ | adj. | 过分小心; 过于谨慎的 | Excessively careful; too cautious and anxious about detail.  Characterized by excessive attention to detail or an undue concern about potential problems. | She was overcareful in checking every detail, which slowed down the whole process. 她过分小心地检查每一个细节，这拖慢了整个过程。| He's overcareful with his money, rarely spending anything on himself. 他对自己的钱过于谨慎，很少在自己身上花钱。| 近义词: cautious, meticulous, scrupulous, fastidious | 反义词: careless, reckless, negligent | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "careful"
