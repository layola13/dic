daguerreotyped:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpt/
adjective
银版照相的；像银版照片的
photographed or represented by a daguerreotype; resembling or characteristic of a daguerreotype.
The daguerreotyped image was remarkably clear. 这张银版照片非常清晰。
Synonyms: photographed, pictured, imaged, portrayed
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider 'modernly photographed', 'digitally captured')
词性: adjective


daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpt/
noun
银版照片；银版照相法
a photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.
The museum displayed a collection of daguerreotypes from the 19th century.  博物馆展出了一批19世纪的银版照片。
Synonyms: photograph, picture, image, portrait
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider 'digital photo', 'modern photograph')
词性: noun


daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpt/
verb
用银版照相法拍摄
to photograph by the daguerreotype process.
He daguerreotyped many famous people of his time. 他用银版照相法拍摄了许多他那个时代的著名人物。
Synonyms: photograph, picture, image, portray
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider 'photograph digitally', 'capture modernly')
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre (1787–1851), French artist and inventor, + -type.
