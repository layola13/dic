The dog growled menacingly, a menaceful glint in its eyes. 那条狗发出威胁的低吼，眼中闪烁着危险的光芒。| 近义词: threatening, ominous, menacing, dangerous, sinister | 反义词: harmless, safe, benign | 词性: 形容词
