contractility:/ˌkɑːntrækˈtɪləti/ /ˌkɒntrækˈtɪləti/
n.|收缩性；收缩力|The ability of muscle tissue to shorten forcefully. |The heart's contractility is essential for pumping blood throughout the body. 心脏的收缩性对于将血液泵送到全身至关重要。|
例句：
The contractility of the uterus is crucial during childbirth. 子宫的收缩力在分娩过程中至关重要。

Smooth muscle contractility plays a role in regulating blood pressure. 平滑肌的收缩性在调节血压方面起作用。
|近义词：contractile force, contractile power|反义词:dilatability, expansibility|词性:noun
