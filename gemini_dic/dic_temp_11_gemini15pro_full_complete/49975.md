denigrator:/ˈdɛnɪɡreɪtər/| n.|诽谤者，诋毁者|A person who denigrates someone or something; a critic or attacker of the reputation of someone or something.|The politician's denigrators seized on his misstatement to attack his character. 这位政客的诋毁者抓住他的错误言论来攻击他的人品。|Synonyms: critic, detractor, slanderer, backbiter, defamer|Antonyms: admirer, supporter, praiser, upholder|Noun

denigrate:/ˈdɛnɪɡreɪt/| v.|诋毁，贬低|To criticize unfairly; disparage.|He was quick to denigrate anyone who challenged his authority.  他很快就会诋毁任何挑战他权威的人。|Synonyms: belittle, disparage, deprecate, decry, malign|Antonyms: praise, extol, laud, commend|Verb
