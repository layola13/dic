intermeshes:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃɪz/
verb
啮合，紧密交织
become entangled or intertwined
The gears intermesh smoothly. 齿轮平稳啮合。

intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃɪz/
noun
啮合，紧密交织
the act of becoming entangled or intertwined
The intermesh of the branches created a dense canopy. 树枝的交织形成了茂密的树冠。

近义词:intertwine, interlock, mesh, engage
反义词:disengage, separate, disentangle
词性:v. n.
