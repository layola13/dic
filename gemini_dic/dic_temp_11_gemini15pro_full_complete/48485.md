deepen:/ˈdiːpən/ /ˈdiːpn/ | v. | 加深，深化，变深 | To make or become deeper. | The river deepens as it flows towards the sea.  这条河在流向大海的过程中逐渐变深。

The crisis has deepened the divide between the rich and the poor. 这场危机加剧了贫富差距。 | 近义词：intensify, strengthen, enhance, augment | 反义词：lessen, reduce, diminish, weaken | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old English *dēopnian, from dēop (deep).
