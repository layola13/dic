词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  来自拉丁语 non "not".
proportionate (adjective): corresponding in size, degree, or intensity. 来自拉丁语 proportionatus, 过去分词 of proportionare "to make proportionate," from pro "to, for" + portion "share, part".
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.  来自古英语 -nes, -nis.
