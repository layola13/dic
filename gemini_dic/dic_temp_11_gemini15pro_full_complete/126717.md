namespace:/ˈneɪmˌspeɪs/| n.|命名空间，名称空间|A namespace is a designated area within a program where identifiers (like variable names) have meaning. This helps prevent naming conflicts, especially in large projects.|The namespace 'std' in C++ contains many standard functions.|近义词：scope, area, domain|反义词:|名词
