antinationalistic:/ˌæntiˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反民族主义的；反对民族主义的
Opposed to nationalism or national interests.
His antinationalistic views made him unpopular. 他的反民族主义观点使他不受欢迎。
Synonyms: antipatriotic, unpatriotic
Antonyms: nationalistic, patriotic
词性: 形容词
