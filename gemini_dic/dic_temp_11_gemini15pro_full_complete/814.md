abstemiously: /æbˈstiːmiəsli/ | adv. | 节制地，适度地 | In a sparing or moderate manner, especially in eating and drinking.  Characterized by abstinence. | He ate abstemiously, mindful of his health. 他饮食节制，注意自己的健康。|  She lived abstemiously, avoiding excesses of all kinds. 她生活俭朴，避免各种过度。 | 近义词: moderately, temperately, sparingly | 反义词: excessively, immoderately, gluttonously | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin *abstemius* "abstaining from strong drink," from *abs-* (from, away from) + *temetum* strong drink.
