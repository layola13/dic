expede:/ɪkˈspiːd/ | v. | 加快，促进 | To speed up or facilitate the process of something. |  例句：The new software is expected to expede the workflow.  这款新软件有望加快工作流程。

例句：They are looking for ways to expede the delivery of goods. 他们正在寻找加快货物交付的方法。 | 近义词：accelerate, hasten, expedite | 反义词：delay, hinder, impede | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *expedire, from ex- "out" + pes "foot" + -ire (infinitive suffix). Originally meaning "to free the feet," hence "to extricate, set free," later "to dispatch, perform quickly."
