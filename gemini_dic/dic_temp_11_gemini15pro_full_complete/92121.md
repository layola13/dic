hypersceptically:/ˌhaɪpərˈskeptɪkli/ | adv. | 极度怀疑地 |  In an extremely skeptical manner, doubting or questioning everything. | He looked at the proposal hypersceptically, questioning every detail. 他极度怀疑地看着这份提案，质疑每一个细节。 | She examined the evidence hypersceptically, refusing to accept anything at face value. 她极度怀疑地审查了证据，拒绝接受任何表面价值的东西。 | 近义词: extremely skeptically, doubtfully, cynically | 反义词: trustingly, credulously, naively | 词性: 副词

词源:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
skeptical (adjective): from Greek "skeptikos" meaning "inquiring, reflective"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
