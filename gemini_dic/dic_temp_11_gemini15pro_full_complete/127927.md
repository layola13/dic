neon:/ˈniːɒn/ (UK) /ˈniːɑːn/ (US)| n. |氖, 霓虹灯|A chemical element that is a rare gas, has the symbol Ne, and is used in electric signs because it glows brightly red-orange when an electric current is passed through it.|
The sign was made of bright neon.  这个招牌是用明亮的霓虹灯制成的。

Neon lights illuminate the bustling city streets. 霓虹灯照亮了繁华的城市街道。

近义词: (for neon lighting) fluorescent light|反义词: None (as a chemical element)|词性:noun
