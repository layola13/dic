objective:/əbˈdʒektɪv/| n. adj.|目标，目的；客观的|Something that you are trying to achieve.  |例句：The main objective of this meeting is to discuss the budget. 这次会议的主要目标是讨论预算。|近义词：goal, aim, target, purpose|反义词:subjective|词性:名词

objective:/əbˈdʒektɪv/| adj.|客观的，不带偏见的|Not influenced by personal feelings or opinions in considering and representing facts. |例句：We need an objective assessment of the situation. 我们需要对情况进行客观的评估。|近义词：impartial, unbiased, neutral|反义词:subjective, biased|词性:形容词
