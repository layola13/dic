overcomer:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmər/| n.|克服者，战胜者|Someone who overcomes adversity or difficulty.  A person who prevails.|例句：She is an overcomer who has faced many challenges in her life. 她是一位克服了人生诸多挑战的战胜者。

例句：He proved himself an overcomer by winning the race despite his injury. 尽管受伤，他还是赢得了比赛，证明了自己是一个战胜者。|近义词：victor, champion, conqueror, survivor, winner|反义词:loser, defeated, vanquished|词性:noun
