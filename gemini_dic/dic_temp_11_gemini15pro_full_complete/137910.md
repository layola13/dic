nonvocalization:/ˌnɒnvəʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌnɑːnvəʊkələˈzeɪʃən/| n.| 不发声；默读；无声化|The practice of reading or studying without uttering the words aloud.  The nonvocalization method is often used in speed reading techniques.|He practiced nonvocalization to improve his reading speed. 他练习默读以提高阅读速度。|Synonyms: silent reading, subvocalization|Antonyms: vocalization, articulation|noun

nonvocalize:/ˌnɒnvəʊkəlaɪz/ /ˌnɑːnvəʊkələˌaɪz/| v.| 不发声；默读；无声化|To read or study without uttering the words aloud.|He learned to nonvocalize to increase his reading comprehension. 他学习默读以提高阅读理解能力。|Synonyms: read silently, subvocalize|Antonyms: vocalize, articulate|verb
