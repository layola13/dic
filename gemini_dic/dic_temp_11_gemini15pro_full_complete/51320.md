detainment:/dɪˈteɪnmənt/
n.
拘留，扣押
The act of detaining someone or something.
The police held him in detainment for questioning. 警方将他拘留问话。
近义词：custody, confinement, imprisonment, arrest
反义词：release, liberation, freedom
词性：名词

detainment:/dɪˈteɪnmənt/
n.
延误，耽搁
The state of being delayed or held back.
The detainment of the flight was due to bad weather. 航班延误是由于恶劣天气造成的。
近义词：delay, postponement, hold-up
反义词：advancement, expedition
词性：名词

词源：
From the Old French word "deteiner," meaning "to hold back" or "to retain," derived from the Latin word "detinere," which is composed of "de-" (meaning "away" or "from") and "tenere" (meaning "to hold").
