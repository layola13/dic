nonpertinent:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrtnənt/ | adj. | 无关的，不相关的 | Not relevant or applicable to a particular matter; irrelevant. |  His comments were considered nonpertinent to the discussion and were ignored. 他的评论被认为与讨论无关，因此被忽略了。

|  She asked a lot of nonpertinent questions, wasting everyone's time. 她问了很多无关紧要的问题，浪费了大家的时间。 | 近义词: irrelevant, immaterial, extraneous, inapplicable | 反义词: relevant, pertinent, applicable, germane | 词性: 形容词
