demanded:/dɪˈmændɪd/
verb
要求, 请求
past tense and past participle of demand
The workers demanded better pay. 工人们要求提高工资。
近义词: requested, insisted, required
反义词: offered, supplied, conceded
词性: verb (past tense)

demanded:/dɪˈmændɪd/
verb
索取, 汲取
past tense and past participle of demand
The task demanded all his attention. 这项任务需要他全力以赴。
近义词:  needed, took, consumed
反义词: gave, offered, provided
词性: verb (past tense)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *demanden, from Old French demander, from Latin dēmandāre (“to entrust, commit, enjoin, order, command”), from dē (“from, down from”) + mandāre (“to entrust, commit, order”).

PHRASE->
demand for (对…的需求):  The demand for housing is high. 对住房的需求很高。
in demand (需求量大): Skilled workers are in demand.  熟练工人需求量很大。
on demand (一经要求):  The service is available on demand. 该服务可按需提供。
meet the demand (满足需求):  They are struggling to meet the demand. 他们正在努力满足需求。
demanding job (要求高的工作): Being a surgeon is a demanding job.  做外科医生是一项要求很高的工作。
