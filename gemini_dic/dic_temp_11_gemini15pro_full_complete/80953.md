graceful: /ˈɡreɪsfəl/ | adj. | 优雅的；优美的 | Having or showing grace or elegance in movement, form, or proportion. |  She moved with a graceful fluidity. 她以优雅流畅的动作移动。 | The swan is a graceful bird. 天鹅是一种优雅的鸟。 | 近义词: elegant, stylish, poised, refined | 反义词: awkward, clumsy, ungainly | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *gracieux*, from Latin *gratiōsus* (“pleasing, agreeable, charming”), from *grātia* (“grace, favor, kindness”).
