ETYMOLOGY->
"Moosewood" is a compound word combining "moose" and "wood."  "Moose" refers to the large North American deer (Alces alces), and "wood" refers to the hard fibrous material that forms the main substance of trees and shrubs. The restaurant's name likely refers to the wooded area where moose might be found, or simply a whimsical name.
