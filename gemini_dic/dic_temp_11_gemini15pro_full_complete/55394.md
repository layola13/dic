distenders:/dɪˈstɛndərz/| n.|扰乱者，引起不安的人或事物|People who disrupt the peace or cause anxiety can be called distenders.|The constant construction noise outside was a major distender to her concentration.  外面不断的施工噪音严重干扰了她的注意力。|Synonyms: disruptors, agitators, troublemakers|Antonyms: pacifiers, soothers|noun


Etymology->
From the verb "distend" (meaning to swell, expand, or stretch) + "-er" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  Ultimately from Latin "distendere"  (to stretch out, extend).
