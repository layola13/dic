hemispheres:/ˈhemɪsfɪərz/| n.|半球|Each hemisphere of the brain controls different functions.  大脑的每个半球控制着不同的功能。|The Earth is divided into two hemispheres, the Northern and the Southern. 地球被分成两个半球，北半球和南半球。|halves, sides|quarters|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hémisphère*, from Latin *hēmisphaerium*, from Greek *ἡμισφαίριον* (hēmisphairion), from *ἥμισυς* (hēmisys, “half”) + *σφαῖρα* (sphaira, “sphere, ball”).
