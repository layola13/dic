overchange:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | v. n. | 多找钱，多收的钱 | To give someone too much change back after they have paid for something; the amount of money that is given back in excess. |  The cashier overcharged me by $5. 收银员多收了我5美元。 | Synonyms: overprice, gouge | Antonyms: undercharge, discount | verb

overchange:/ˈoʊvərˌtʃeɪndʒ/ | n. | 多找的零钱 | The amount of money given back in excess of the correct change. | I received $10 in overchange. 我多收了10美元的零钱。 | Synonyms: excess change | Antonyms: underchange, due change | noun
