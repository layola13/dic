chlorinations:/ˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃənz/
noun
氯化（作用）；加氯（处理）
The process of adding chlorine to something, typically water, to disinfect it or for other chemical processes.
The chlorinations of the swimming pool take place weekly.  游泳池每周进行一次氯化消毒。
The city's water supply undergoes several chlorinations before reaching consumers. 城市供水在到达消费者之前要经过多次氯化处理。
近义词: disinfection, sterilization
反义词: dechlorination
