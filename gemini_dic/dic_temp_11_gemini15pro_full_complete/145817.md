overdeepens:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpənz/
verb
过度加深，过度深化
makes something excessively deep, deeper than is usual or necessary
The glacier overdeepened the valley floor. 冰川过度加深了谷底。
近义词: excavates, gouges
反义词: fills, shallows
词性:v.


Etymology:
From over- + deepen. Over- is a prefix meaning "excessively," and deepen comes from the Old English dēopian, "to make deep."
