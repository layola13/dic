maases:/ˈmɑːsəs/ | n. | 善行，功德，施舍物 (尤指犹太教) |  Charitable donations or good deeds, especially in Jewish tradition.  Derived from Hebrew "ma'asim" (מעשים), meaning "deeds" or "actions". | The rabbi praised the congregation for their maases during the High Holidays. 拉比赞扬了会众在犹太教重大节日中的善行。 |  benevolence, charity, alms, contributions |  malevolence, cruelty, stinginess | noun
