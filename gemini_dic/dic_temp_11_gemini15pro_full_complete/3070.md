aerologist:/ˌɛəˈrɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 气象学家；高空气象学家 | A scientist who studies the atmosphere, especially its physical characteristics and motions. | The aerologist released a weather balloon to gather data.  气象学家放飞了一个气象气球来收集数据。 | Meteorologist, atmospheric scientist |  | Noun
Etymology->
From aero- + -logist.  Aero- comes from Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”). -logist denotes "one who studies".
