depressors:/dɪˈprɛsɚz/
n.
抑制剂；减压器；降压药；（尤指下唇或嘴角的）降肌
agents that reduce activity or force; muscles that draw down a part, especially a depressor muscle of the lower lip or corner of the mouth.
The doctor prescribed depressors to lower her blood pressure. 医生开了降压药来降低她的血压。
Facial depressors are responsible for frowning. 面部降肌负责皱眉。
synonyms: inhibitors, reducers, suppressants, lowering agents, downers (informal)
antonyms: stimulants, activators, elevators, enhancers
noun
