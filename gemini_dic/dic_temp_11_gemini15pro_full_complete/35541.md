coaled:/koʊld/ | v. |加煤；供以煤；涂以煤；|Supplied or covered with coal.  The ship was coaled in port. 这艘船在港口加了煤。 |近义词：fueled, supplied|反义词: |v.
Etymology: From coal + -ed, the past participle suffix. The word "coal" itself comes from Old English "col," which in turn is from Proto-Germanic "*kula," and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*g(e)u-lo-," meaning "glowing ember."
