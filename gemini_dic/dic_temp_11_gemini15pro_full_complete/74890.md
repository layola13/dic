freshest:/ˈfrɛʃɪst/| adj.|最新鲜的|Superlative form of fresh.  Meaning the most recently made, produced, or obtained; newest. |The baker uses only the freshest ingredients in his cakes.  面包师在他的蛋糕中只使用最新鲜的食材。

The freshest news is available online. 最新的新闻可以在网上找到。|近义词：newest, latest, recent, pristine, untouched|反义词：oldest, stalest, most decayed, rotten|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *fersc "not salted, fresh, vigorous," from Proto-Germanic *friskaz "fresh" (source also of Old Saxon fersk, Old Frisian fersk, Old Norse ferskr, Dutch vers, Old High German frisc, German frisch "fresh"), from PIE root *preg- "fresh, cool, clear."  The suffix -est is the superlative ending in English.
