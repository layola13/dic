nibble:/ˈnɪbl/| v. n.|细咬，轻咬；少量地吃|To eat or bite something in small amounts. It can also mean to show a slight interest in something, like a business deal.|例句：He nibbled the edge of the cookie. 他轻咬了饼干的边缘。|例句：Several companies are nibbling at our offer. 几家公司对我们的报价略感兴趣。|近义词：bite, peck, sample|反义词：gulp, devour|词性: verb, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *knibblen*, frequentative of *nibben* "to nip, peck," probably of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch *knibbelen* and German *knibbeln*.
