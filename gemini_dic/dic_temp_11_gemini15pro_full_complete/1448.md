aceldama:/əˈkɛldəmə/| n. |血田；凶地|Akeldama, a place near Jerusalem associated with Judas Iscariot's death.  It is derived from the Aramaic words meaning "field of blood".|Peter in his speech to the apostles referred to Aceldama, the field purchased with the price of Judas' betrayal. 彼得在对使徒们的讲话中提到了用犹大背叛的钱买下的血田。

The guide pointed out the desolate area known as Aceldama, a chilling reminder of the consequences of greed. 导游指出了被称为血田的荒凉地区，令人不寒而栗地提醒人们贪婪的后果。|近义词:field of blood|反义词:sacred ground|词性:noun
