aimfully:/ˈeɪmfəli/| adv.|有目的地，目标明确地|In a manner that suggests a clear intention or purpose; purposefully.|She walked aimfully towards the library, determined to finish her research. 她目标明确地走向图书馆，决心完成她的研究。|近义词：purposefully, intentionally, deliberately, resolutely|反义词：aimlessly, randomly, haphazardly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From aim + -ful + -ly.  "Aim" comes from Old French "esmer," meaning "to estimate" or "to aim." "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
