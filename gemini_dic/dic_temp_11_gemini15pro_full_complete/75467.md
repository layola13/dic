frump:/frʌmp/| n.|邋遢的女人；古板的人|a woman who wears old, unfashionable clothes and does not take care of her appearance; a dull, old-fashioned person|例句：She looked like a frump in that old dress. 她穿着那件旧裙子看起来像个邋遢的女人。

例句：He considered her a frump and a bore. 他认为她既邋遢又无聊。|近义词：dowdy, drab, frumpy, old-fashioned|反义词:stylish, fashionable, chic|词性:noun
