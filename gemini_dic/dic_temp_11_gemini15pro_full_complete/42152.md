couchancy:/ˈkaʊtʃənsi/| n.|躺卧姿势；(尤指鹿)伏卧不动|The deer's couchancy allowed the hunter to get a clear shot.  鹿的伏卧姿势让猎人得以清晰地瞄准。|例句：The photograph captured the lion in a state of couchancy.  这张照片捕捉到了狮子伏卧的姿态。|近义词：recumbency, lying down|反义词:standing, upright|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *cochant “lying down,” present participle of coucher “to lie down,” from Vulgar Latin *colcare, frequentative of Latin collocare “to place, set, arrange.”
