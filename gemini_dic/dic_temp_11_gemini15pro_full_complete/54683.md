disorderednesses:/dɪsˈɔːrdərdnəsɪz/
|n.|混乱，紊乱，失调（状态）|The state of being disordered; lack of order or regularity.|His room was in a state of utter disorderedness. 他的房间一片混乱。|近义词：disorder, chaos, mess, confusion, disarray|反义词：order, organization, tidiness, neatness|词性:名词

词源:
From disordered + -ness.  "Disordered" comes from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") added to "ordered" (meaning arranged in a systematic way).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
