homeboys:/ˈhoʊmˌbɔɪz/| n.|老朋友；哥们；老乡|Close friends, especially male friends from one's hometown or neighborhood.  Often used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).|例句：He went out with his homeboys last night. 他昨晚和他的哥们出去了。

例句：My homeboys always have my back. 我的老乡们总是支持我。|近义词：friends, buddies, pals, mates|反义词:enemies, rivals|noun
