inklings:/ˈɪŋklɪŋz/
n.
暗示; 跡象; 模糊的想法
a slight knowledge or suspicion; a hint
She had no inkling of what was going on. 她对正在发生的事情一无所知。

例句：
I had an inkling that something was wrong. 我隐约觉得有些不对劲。
He had no inkling of his future success.  他对未来的成功毫无预感。


近义词：
hints, clues, suggestions, suspicions,  premonitions, glimpses

反义词：
certainties, knowledge, proofs, confirmations


词性
noun (plural)
