galena:/ɡəˈliːnə/ | n. | 方铅矿 | A common lead sulfide mineral (PbS) that is the chief ore of lead. It is a soft, heavy, metallic mineral with a cubic crystal structure and a distinctive lead-gray color and streak. | Galena is the most important ore of lead. 方铅矿是最重要的铅矿石。| The miners discovered a rich vein of galena. 矿工们发现了一条丰富的方铅矿脉。 | synonyms: lead glance, blue lead | antonyms: N/A | noun
