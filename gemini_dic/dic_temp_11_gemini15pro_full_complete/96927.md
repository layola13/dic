inexpressive:/ˌɪnɪkˈspresiv/
adjective
缺乏表情的；不善表达的
not showing feelings or emotions; not able to express feelings or emotions clearly
Her face was inexpressive, giving no clue to her thoughts. 她的脸上毫无表情，让人猜不透她的心思。
Synonyms:  unexpressive, impassive, blank, stoic, deadpan
Antonyms: expressive, communicative, animated, demonstrative
词性: 形容词
