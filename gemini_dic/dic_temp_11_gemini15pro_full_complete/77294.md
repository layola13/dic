gatefolds:/ˈɡeɪtfəʊldz/
noun
折叠页;  跨页;  (唱片)折叠式封面
A gatefold is a piece of paper, especially the cover of a record album, that is folded in half to make it larger.
The double album came in a beautiful gatefold sleeve. 这张双唱片装在一个漂亮的折叠式封套里。
synonyms: foldout, centerfold
antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly "single page" or "unfolded")
