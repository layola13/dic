outlustre:/ˌaʊtˈlʌstər/
verb
胜过；使黯然失色
to surpass in brilliance or splendor; eclipse
Her performance outlustred all the others. 她的表演胜过了所有其他人。
outshine, surpass, eclipse
overshadow, dim
