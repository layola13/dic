incriminates:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪts/
|vt.|控告；暗示…有罪；牵连|To make (someone) appear guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; to show evidence of involvement in a crime or wrongdoing.|The evidence found at the scene incriminates him in the robbery.  现场发现的证据表明他参与了抢劫。|Synonyms:  implicate, accuse, charge, indict|Antonyms:  exonerate, acquit, absolve|Verb
