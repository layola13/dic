dingbat:/ˈdɪŋˌbæt/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋；古怪的人；装饰符号 |A silly or eccentric person. Also, a decorative typographic character. |  She acted like a complete dingbat, forgetting her own phone number. 她表现得像个十足的傻瓜，竟然忘记了自己的电话号码。 | That website uses too many dingbats, it's distracting.  那个网站使用了太多的装饰符号，让人分心。 | 近义词：fool, idiot, nitwit, oddball, character (typographic) | 反义词: genius, sage | 词性: 名词
