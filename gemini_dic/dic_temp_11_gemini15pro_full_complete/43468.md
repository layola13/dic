craspedote:/kræˈspiːdoʊt/| adj.|有缘饰的，有边饰的|Having a craspedon; bordered or fringed.  Referring specifically to the wing of an insect when the edge is darker or thicker than the rest of the wing.|The butterfly's craspedote wings were a beautiful sight to behold. 这只蝴蝶有缘饰的翅膀非常美丽。|Synonyms: fringed, bordered, margined|Antonyms: unbordered, plain, simple|adjective
