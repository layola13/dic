batty:/ˈbæti/| adj.|疯狂的，古怪的，糊涂的|Mentally unstable; eccentric or slightly crazy.  Originating from 'bats in the belfry', referring to the erratic flight of bats.|He's gone completely batty since he lost his job. 他自从失业后就变得疯疯癫癫的。|Synonyms: crazy, mad, insane, eccentric, daft|Antonyms: sane, rational, sensible|adjective

batty:/ˈbæti/| n.|蝙蝠 (复数)|Multiple bats.  Less common than the singular 'bat'.|Look at all those batty flying around! 看所有那些蝙蝠飞来飞去！|Synonyms: bats|Antonyms: N/A|noun
