diarist:/ˈdaɪərɪst/ | n. |日记作者；日记体作家|A person who writes a diary.  A diarist records events, thoughts, and feelings in a personal journal. |Anne Frank is one of history's most famous diarists. 安妮·弗兰克是历史上最著名的日记作者之一。

|Samuel Pepys' diary offers a fascinating glimpse into 17th-century London.  萨缪尔·佩皮斯的日记让我们得以一窥17世纪伦敦的迷人景象。| synonyms: journalist, recorder, chronicler | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fabricator" if the diary is fictional) | noun
