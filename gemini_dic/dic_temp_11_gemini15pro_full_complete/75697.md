fulimart: /ˈfuːlimɑːrt/ | n. | 腐利超市（粉丝向同人作品贩售活动）| A fan-run event, often online, where fan-made works, especially doujinshi (fan comics) and merchandise, are sold, often focusing on BL (Boys' Love) themes.  Derived from "fuli" (福利, meaning benefits or perks, often referring to fan service) and "market". | Example: I'm going to the fulimart this weekend to buy some new doujinshi.  这个周末我要去腐利超市买一些新的同人志。| Example:  Many artists sell their fanworks at fulimarts. 很多艺术家在腐利超市出售他们的同人作品。 | 近义词: doujinshi market, fan market | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
