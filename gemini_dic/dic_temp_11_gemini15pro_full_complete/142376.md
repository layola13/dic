orthopterous:/ɔːˈθɒptərəs/| adj.|直翅目的|Belonging or pertaining to the order Orthoptera, which includes grasshoppers, crickets, locusts, and katydids.|例句：Grasshoppers are orthopterous insects. 蚱蜢是直翅目昆虫。

例句：The orthopterous insect chirped loudly.  这只直翅目昆虫大声鸣叫。|近义词：None (because it refers to a taxonomic order)|反义词：None|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὀρθόπτερος (orthópteros, “straight-winged”), from ὀρθός (orthós, “straight”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
