crustific:/krʌˈstɪfɪk/ | adj. | 壳状的，硬皮的 | Forming or having a crust; crust-like.  | The crustific exterior of the dried mud made it difficult to break apart.  干泥的壳状外壳使其难以破碎。 |  The crustific coating protected the delicate interior. 壳状涂层保护了精巧的内部。 | 近义词: crusty, crusted, hardened | 反义词: soft, pliable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *crustīficus, from crusta "crust" + -ficus "-making, -producing" (related to facere "to make, to do").
