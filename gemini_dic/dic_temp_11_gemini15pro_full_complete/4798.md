algological:/ˌælɡəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ |adj.| 藻类的；藻类学的 |Relating to the study of algae. |例句：Algological research is crucial for understanding aquatic ecosystems. 藻类学研究对于理解水生生态系统至关重要。

例句：She specialized in algological studies during her postgraduate degree.  她在研究生阶段专攻藻类学研究。|近义词：phycological|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


词源:  From  algology + -ical.  Algology is from  French *algologie*,  from  Greek *álogos*  "seaweed" + *-logía* "study of".
