humbleness:/ˈhʌmbəlnəs/| n.|谦逊，谦卑|The quality of being humble; modesty.|She accepted the award with humbleness and grace. 她以谦逊和优雅的态度接受了奖项。|Near Synonyms: modesty, humility, meekness, unpretentiousness|Antonyms: arrogance, pride, haughtiness|noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *humblenesse, equivalent to humble +‎ -ness.
