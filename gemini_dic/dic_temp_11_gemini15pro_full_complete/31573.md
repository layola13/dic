chasing: /ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| v. | 追逐，追赶 | The act of pursuing someone or something. | The dog was chasing a squirrel in the park. 狗在公园里追逐一只松鼠. |  He spent his life chasing fame and fortune. 他一生追求名利. | pursuing, following, hunting | fleeing, escaping, avoiding | verb

chasing: /ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 追逐的 |  Descriptive of the act of pursuing.  | This is a thrilling chasing scene in the movie. 这是电影里一个惊心动魄的追逐场景. | pursuing, hunting |  passive, static | adjective


词源: From Middle English *chasen*, from Anglo-Norman *chacer*, *cacier*, from Vulgar Latin *captiāre*, from Latin *captāre* “to try to seize,” frequentative of *capere* “to take, seize.”
