cirrigerous:/sɪˈrɪdʒərəs/
adjective
卷云的；产生卷云的
Producing cirrus clouds; characterized by cirrus clouds.
例句：The cirrigerous atmosphere hinted at an approaching change in weather. 卷云密布的天空暗示着天气即将发生变化。

例句：Cirrigerous clouds often precede a warm front. 卷云通常出现在暖锋之前。


近义词：cirrus-producing, cirriform
反义词：None (lack of cirrus clouds wouldn't have a specific descriptive term)
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *cirriger* "cirrus-bearing," from *cirrus* "curl, tuft of hair" + *-ger* "carrying, bearing."
