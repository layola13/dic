hyperconformists:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈfɔːrmɪsts/
|n.|超级顺从者；极端墨守成规者|People who conform to an excessive degree, often beyond what is considered normal or necessary.  They adhere rigidly to rules and norms, even when those rules are harmful or illogical.|The hyperconformists in the company never questioned the CEO's decisions, even when they were clearly detrimental to the business.| 近义词:conformists, sheep, followers|反义词:nonconformists, rebels, mavericks|Noun
