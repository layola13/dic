krispies:/ˈkrɪspiz/| adj. n.|脆的，脆米，爆米花|Small, crisp pieces of food, typically cereal.  Refers to the sound they make when eaten.|例句：I love the sound of Rice Krispies in milk. 我喜欢脆米泡在牛奶里的声音。

例句：These krispies are perfectly golden brown. 这些脆米呈完美的金棕色。|近义词：crispy, crunchy, crackly|反义词:soggy, soft|词性: 主要作形容词，也可作名词
