boondoggle:/ˈbuːndɒɡəl/ (US) /ˈbuːndɔːɡl/ (UK)| n. | 浪费时间或金钱的琐碎工作；无用的东西；华而不实的装饰品 | A project or activity that is wasteful or pointless but gives the appearance of having value.  It can also refer to a braided cord worn by Boy Scouts, originally used for various practical purposes. |  The new airport terminal was a complete boondoggle, costing millions but serving very few passengers.  新航站楼完全是浪费钱，耗资数百万却只服务了很少的乘客。| synonyms: waste, extravagance, folly, triviality, frippery, gewgaw | antonyms: necessity, essential, useful, practical, valuable | noun
