emeer:/əˈmɪər/ | n. |酋长，埃米尔，统治者|A title of various Muslim (chiefly Arab) rulers, commanders, or high officials.  It signifies a leader, commander, or prince.| The emeers of the various tribes met to discuss the treaty. 各部落的酋长们开会讨论条约。|Synonyms: chief, chieftain, sheikh, ruler, leader, prince| Antonyms: follower, subject | noun
