loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ (US) /ˈlɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|闲逛，徘徊，游荡|To stand or wait around idly or without apparent purpose.|例句：He was loitering near the school. 他在学校附近闲逛。|近义词：linger, dawdle, hang around|反义词：hurry, rush|词性:动词/名词

loitering:/ˈlɔɪtərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈlɔɪtərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|闲逛，游荡|The act of loitering.|例句：Loitering is prohibited in this area.  此区域禁止闲逛。|近义词：lingering, dawdling|反义词：rushing|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle Dutch *loteren "to shake, dangle".
