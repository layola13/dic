contactable:/ˈkɒntæktəbl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntæktəbl/ (US)| adj.|可联系的，可接触的|Able to be contacted or reached, especially by phone or email.|例句：Is he contactable at this number?  他可以用这个号码联系到吗？

例句：She made sure she was contactable at all times during the conference.  她确保在会议期间随时可以联系到她。|近义词：reachable, accessible, available|反义词：uncontactable, unreachable, unavailable|词性:形容词

contact:/ˈkɒntækt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntækt/ (US)| n. v.|接触，联系|The state or process of communicating or meeting with someone.|词源: From Latin *contactus*, past participle of *contingere* "to touch, meet," from *com-* "together" + *tangere* "to touch".|词性:名词，动词

-able:/əbl/| suffix.|可...的，能...的|Forms adjectives meaning "able to be," "capable of," "worthy of," "tending to," "given to."|词源: From Middle English *-able, -ible*, from Old French *-able*, from Latin *-abilis*.|词性:后缀
