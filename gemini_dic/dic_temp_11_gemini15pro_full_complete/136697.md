nonsegmentation:/ˌnɒnˌsɛɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非分割; 不分段;  |The absence of segmentation or division into separate parts. | The nonsegmentation of the worm's body makes it difficult to determine its exact length.  这种蠕虫的身体不分段，因此很难确定它的确切长度。| The nonsegmentation of the market makes it difficult to target specific customer groups.  市场的非分割使得很难针对特定的客户群体。|  synonyms: continuity, wholeness, unity | antonyms: segmentation, division, fragmentation | noun
