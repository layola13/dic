mollusk:/ˈmɑːləsk/ (US) /ˈmɒləsk/ (UK)| n. |软体动物|Any of a large phylum (Mollusca) of invertebrate animals (such as snails, clams, or squids) with a soft unsegmented body usually enclosed in a calcareous shell broadly : shellfish.|例句：We found a variety of mollusks on the beach. 我们在海滩上发现了各种各样的软体动物。

例句：The octopus is a highly intelligent mollusk. 章鱼是一种非常聪明的软体动物。|近义词：shellfish, invertebrate |反义词: vertebrate |词性: noun
