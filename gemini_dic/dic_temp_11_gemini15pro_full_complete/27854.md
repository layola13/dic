canonized:/ˈkænənaɪzd/| adj.|被封为圣徒的；被神圣化的；奉为经典的；被尊崇的|Officially declared (a dead person) to be a saint.  Elevated to a state of revered acceptance.|Joan of Arc was canonized in 1920.  圣女贞德于1920年被封圣。|Shakespeare's works are practically canonized in English literature.  莎士比亚的作品在英国文学中几乎被奉为经典。|Synonyms: sanctified, consecrated, beatified, exalted, venerated|Antonyms: condemned, denounced, vilified|adjective
