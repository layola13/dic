misconceivers:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvərz/
n.
误解者；持有错误观念的人
Those who misconceive something; people who hold incorrect beliefs or understandings.
The article was misinterpreted by many misconceivers who lacked the necessary background knowledge.  许多缺乏必要背景知识的误解者曲解了这篇文章。
Misconceivers of the new policy protested loudly. 对新政策的误解者大声抗议。
近义词:misinterpreters, malinterpreters
反义词:interpreters, understanders
词性:noun
