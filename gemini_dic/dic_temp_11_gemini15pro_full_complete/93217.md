iguanodons:/ɪˌɡwɑːnəˌdɒnz/ | n. |禽龙（复数）|A large herbivorous dinosaur of the Cretaceous period, with a large spike on its thumb.|Iguanodons were widespread in the Early Cretaceous period. 禽龙在早白垩世时期分布广泛。| The museum displayed a complete iguanodon skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的禽龙骨架。| 近义词: dinosaur | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑用predator 捕食者) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Iguanodon*, from *iguana* + Ancient Greek *odoús* (tooth).  Named for its teeth which resembled those of an iguana.
