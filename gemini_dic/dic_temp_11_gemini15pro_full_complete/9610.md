antispiritualism:/ˌæntiˈspɪrɪtʃʊəlɪzəm/
n.
反唯灵主义，反精神主义
Opposition to or rejection of spiritual or religious beliefs and values, emphasizing material or worldly concerns.
The rise of antispiritualism in the 20th century coincided with advancements in science and technology. 20世纪反唯灵主义的兴起与科学技术的进步相吻合。
His antispiritualism led him to reject any notion of an afterlife.  他的反精神主义使他拒绝任何来世的概念。
近义词: materialism, secularism
反义词: spiritualism, religiosity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek ἀντί (anti))
spiritualism (noun): the belief that spirits of the dead exist and can communicate with the living
-ism (suffix): forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, or practices
