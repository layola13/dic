apheliotropism:/ˌæfiːliˈɒtrəpɪzəm/ |n.|背日性|The tendency of a plant to turn away from the sun.|Sunflowers exhibit negative apheliotropism during some growth stages. 向日葵在某些生长阶段表现出负背日性。| antonyms: heliotropism, phototropism | synonyms: negative heliotropism | noun
