monocotyledon:/ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ /ˌmɑːnəˌkɑːtɪˈliːdən/| n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocotyledons constitute the smaller of the two great divisions of flowering plants.|例句：Corn is a monocotyledon. 玉米是单子叶植物。

例句：Orchids are a diverse family of monocotyledons. 兰花是种类繁多的单子叶植物科。|近义词：monocot|反义词:dicotyledon, dicot|词性:noun


monocotyle: /ˈmɒnəʊkɒt/ /ˈmɑːnəˌkɑːt/ | n. |单子叶植物|Shortened form of monocotyledon.|例句：Grasses are a common type of monocotyle. 草是一种常见的单子叶植物。
