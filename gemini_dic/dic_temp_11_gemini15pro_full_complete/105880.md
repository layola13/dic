kitschiness:/ˈkɪtʃɪnəs/
n.
庸俗；媚俗；低级趣味
The kitschiness of the souvenirs made her cringe. 那些纪念品的庸俗让她感到难堪。
例句：The sheer kitschiness of the décor was overwhelming.  这装饰的十足庸俗感让人难以忍受。

例句：He embraced the kitschiness of the theme park, finding joy in its over-the-top artificiality. 他欣然接受了主题公园的媚俗，并在其夸张的人工造作中找到了乐趣。

近义词：tackiness, gaudiness, vulgarity
反义词：sophistication, elegance, refinement
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Kitsch*, meaning "trash," "rubbish," or "gaudy trash."  The word entered English in the 1920s.
