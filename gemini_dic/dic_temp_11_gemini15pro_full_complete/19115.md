betul: /bəˈtul/ (Malay)| adj.|正确的，真实的，准确的|Correct, true, accurate.  It is used primarily in Malay and Indonesian.  The English word "correct" is a good equivalent.|The answer is betul. 答案是正确的。|Synonym: correct, right, accurate, true|Antonym: wrong, incorrect, false|adjective

betul: /bəˈtul/ (Malay)| adv.|真的，确实|Truly, really, indeed. It is used primarily in Malay and Indonesian. The English word "really" is a good equivalent in this adverbial sense.|Betul, I saw it with my own eyes! 真的，我亲眼所见！|Synonym: really, truly, indeed|Antonym: not really, not at all|adverb
