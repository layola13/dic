captaincies: /ˈkæptənsiz/
n.
队长职位；队长身份；（葡萄牙殖民时期巴西的）辖区
The position or rank of captain, especially of a team or ship; or, historically, a subdivision of Portuguese Brazil governed by a captain.
During the colonial era, Brazil was divided into various captaincies. 在殖民时代，巴西被划分为不同的辖区。
The captaincies were responsible for their own defense. 这些辖区负责自身的防卫。
近义词:  leaderships, commands
反义词: subordination
