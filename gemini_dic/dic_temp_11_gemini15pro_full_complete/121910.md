misseldine:/ˈmɪsəltəʊn/ (UK), /ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ/ (US)| n.|槲寄生|An evergreen parasitic plant with white berries, traditionally hung at Christmas.  It is considered good luck to kiss someone standing beneath it.|We hung misseldine in the doorway.|近义词:None (in the context of the plant itself, although other festive decorations could be considered synonyms in a broader sense).|反义词:None|noun
