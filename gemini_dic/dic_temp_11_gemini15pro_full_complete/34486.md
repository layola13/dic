claylike:/ˈkleɪlaɪk/
adjective
像黏土的；似陶土的
Resembling clay in appearance, texture, or consistency.
The soil was heavy and claylike. 土壤很重，像黏土一样。
近义词:clayey, argillaceous
反义词:sandy, gravelly
词性:adjective
