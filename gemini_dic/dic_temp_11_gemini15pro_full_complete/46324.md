dabblingly:/ˈdæblɪŋli/| adv.| 浅尝辄止地，戏耍地 | in a dabbling manner; superficially. | He dabbled dabblingly in painting, never truly committing to the art form. 他只是浅尝辄止地涉猎绘画，从未真正投入到这种艺术形式中。| She played the piano dabblingly, more for amusement than serious practice. 她只是随便弹弹钢琴，更多的是为了娱乐而不是认真练习。 | 近义词：superficially, casually, amateurishly | 反义词：seriously, dedicatedly, professionally | 词性: 副词

dabble: /ˈdæbəl/ | v. |  涉猎，浅尝 | 1. immerse (one's hands or feet) partially in water and move them around gently. 2. take part in an activity in a casual or superficial way. |  词源:  Probably of imitative origin. |
