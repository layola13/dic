arguable:/ˈɑːrɡjuəbl/| adj.|可争辩的，可论证的|Open to argument or debate; debatable.  Something that is arguable can be supported by evidence and reasoning, but there may be other valid viewpoints.|例句：It's arguable whether his methods were justified. 他的方法是否合理有待商榷。

例句：It is arguable that this is the best solution.  可以说这是最好的解决方案。|近义词：debatable, disputable, contestable, questionable|反义词：undeniable, indisputable, irrefutable|词性:adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from argue + -able.
