clickstreams:/ˈklɪkˌstriːmz/
noun
点击流
A series of mouse clicks made while using a website or the Internet.  It typically includes information about which pages were visited, in what order, and how long each page was viewed.
Example: Analyzing clickstreams can help businesses understand user behavior on their websites.  分析点击流可以帮助企业了解用户在其网站上的行为。

Example: E-commerce sites use clickstreams to recommend products to customers.  电子商务网站使用点击流向客户推荐产品。
Synonyms: click path, click history, web browsing history
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym)
词性: 名词
