ineffectivenesses:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|无效; 无能; 低效|The ineffectivenesses of the current system are apparent.  当前系统的无效性显而易见。|例句：The report highlighted the ineffectivenesses of the new policy.  报告强调了新政策的无效性。

The multiple ineffectivenesses of the various approaches led to frustration. 各种方法的多种无效性导致了挫败感。|近义词：failures, shortcomings, deficiencies, inefficiencies|反义词：effectiveness, efficacy, success, potency|词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From ineffective + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Ineffective comes from in- (not) + effective (producing a desired result).  Effective ultimately derives from Latin *effectivus, from efficere "to work out, accomplish."
