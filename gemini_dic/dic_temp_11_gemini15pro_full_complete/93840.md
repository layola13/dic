immitigable:/ɪˈmɪtɪɡəbl/
adjective
无法缓解的，不能缓和的
Not capable of being mitigated or softened; unyielding.
例句：His grief was immitigable after the loss of his child. 他失去孩子后悲痛欲绝，无法缓解。

例句：The immitigable pain in her back made it difficult to sleep.  她背部无法缓解的疼痛让她难以入睡。

近义词：unappeasable, relentless, implacable, inexorable
反义词：mitigable, appeasable, placable, flexible
词性：adjective
