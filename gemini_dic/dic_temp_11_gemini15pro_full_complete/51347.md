detector:/dɪˈtɛktər/ | n. | 探测器；检测器；发现者 | a device or instrument used to detect or locate something, such as metal, smoke, or radiation. | The metal detector beeped loudly. 金属探测器发出响亮的哔哔声。| The smoke detector alerted the family to the fire. 烟雾探测器提醒了这家人注意火灾。| synonyms: sensor, scanner, monitor | antonyms: concealer | noun
