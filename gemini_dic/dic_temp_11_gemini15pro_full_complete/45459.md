curations:/kjʊˈreɪʃənz/| n.|策展，管理，精选集|The museum's curations have been praised by critics.  博物馆的策展受到评论家的赞扬。|近义词：collections, selections, exhibitions|反义词: disorder, chaos|名词

词源:
From the Latin word "cura" meaning "care".  源自拉丁语单词 "cura"，意为“关怀”。

用法说明:
"Curations" is the plural form of "curation," referring to the act of carefully selecting, organizing, and presenting items, often in a museum, gallery, or online platform.  "Curations" 是 "curation" 的复数形式，指的是仔细选择、组织和展示物品的行为，通常在博物馆、画廊或在线平台上。
