nonpenetration:/ˌnɒnpɛnɪˈtreɪʃn/
n.|非渗透，不穿透|The nonpenetration of the protective coating led to rust. 保护涂层的非渗透性导致生锈。|例句：The nonpenetration of the firewall prevented the hackers from accessing sensitive data.  防火墙的不穿透性阻止了黑客访问敏感数据。|近义词：impermeability, impenetrability|反义词: penetration, permeability|词性:名词
Etymology: From non- + penetration.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "penetration" comes from the Latin "penetrat-" meaning "entered, pierced."
