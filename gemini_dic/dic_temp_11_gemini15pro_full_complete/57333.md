downwardly:/ˈdaʊnwərdli/
adverb
向下地，下降地
moving or extending downwards.
例句：The graph shows a downwardly trend in sales.  图表显示销售额呈下降趋势。

例句：The stock market spiraled downwardly. 股票市场螺旋式下降。

近义词：downwards, descending
反义词：upwardly, ascending
词性：副词


downward:/ˈdaʊnwərd/
adjective
向下的
moving or extending downwards.
例句：A downward slope. 向下的斜坡。

例句：The downward pressure on prices. 价格的下行压力。

近义词：descending
反义词：upward, ascending
词性：形容词


downwards:/ˈdaʊnwərdz/
adverb
向下
towards a lower place or level.
例句：He looked downwards and saw the valley below. 他向下看去，看到了下面的山谷。

例句：The path sloped downwards towards the river. 小路向下倾斜，通向河流。

近义词：downwardly, descending
反义词：upwards
词性：副词


词源:
From "downward" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Downward" itself comes from Old English "dūnewearde," from "dūne" (down) + "wearde" (toward).
