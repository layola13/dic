embryotomy:/ˌɛmbrɪˈɒtəmi/ | n. | 碎胎术，胚胎毁损术 | The surgical destruction of a fetus in utero, especially to facilitate delivery.  |  Embryotomy was performed to save the mother's life.  为了挽救母亲的生命，进行了碎胎术。 | Synonyms: fetotomy, craniotomy (when specifically referring to the head) | Antonyms: N/A (The opposite would be procedures preserving the fetus, like a C-section) | noun
