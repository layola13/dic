blocking-out:/ˈblɒkɪŋ aʊt/ (UK) /ˈblɑːkɪŋ aʊt/ (US)| n. | 阻挡；遮蔽；草拟；排练；封锁（感情）| The act of blocking something out, either physically or mentally.  This can refer to blocking light, sound, emotions, or planning a schedule. |  He was blocking out the sun with his hand. 他用手挡住了阳光。 | synonyms: obscuring, outlining, sketching, suppressing, repressing | antonyms: revealing, exposing, expressing, admitting | noun
