his-nibs:/hɪzˈnɪbz/ | n. |这家伙，那家伙；大人物，要人 | A humorous or slightly disrespectful term for a person, often one in authority.  It can also be used affectionately. | His-nibs over there thinks he can tell us what to do. 那边的家伙认为他可以告诉我们该做什么。|  His-nibs decided to give everyone a raise. 那位要人决定给大家加薪。| Synonyms: bigwig, VIP, boss, the man, top dog | Antonyms: underling, nobody | noun
