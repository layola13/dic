endangerer:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərər/ | n. | 危害者，使…陷入危险的人 |Someone or something that puts someone or something in danger. | Pollution is an endangerer of public health. 污染危害公众健康。 |  The reckless driver was an endangerer to everyone on the road. 这个鲁莽的司机对路上的每个人都是一种威胁。| synonyms: threat, menace, hazard, risk, peril | antonyms: protector, guardian, savior | noun

endanger:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒər/ | v. | 危及，危害 |To expose to danger; imperil. |  Smoking endangers your health. 吸烟危害你的健康。| The oil spill endangered marine life. 漏油事件危及海洋生物。| synonyms: jeopardize, imperil, threaten, compromise, risk | antonyms: protect, safeguard, secure | verb
