odoured:/ˈəʊdəd/ (UK) /ˈoʊdərd/ (US)| adj.|有气味的，有臭味的|Having a smell, often unpleasant.|The odoured air made me gag.  这难闻的空气让我作呕。|Synonyms: smelly, fragrant, aromatic, perfumed (depending on the type of smell)|Antonyms: odourless, scentless|adjective
