nucleobranchiata:/ˌnjuːkliːoʊˈbræŋkiˌɑːtə/
| n. | 海天使，裸鳃亚目 |  A taxonomic group of pelagic marine snails, commonly known as sea angels. They are characterized by their wing-like parapodia used for swimming and their lack of a shell in the adult stage.  | Sea angels, members of the Nucleobranchiata, are fascinating creatures. 海天使，裸鳃亚目的成员，是迷人的生物。 |  Synonyms: Pteropoda (partially), sea angels | Antonyms: (No direct antonym; could use benthic organisms as a contrasting group - 底栖生物) | noun
