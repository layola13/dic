juiciness:/ˈdʒuːsinəs/
n.
多汁性，津津有味，生动有趣
The juiciness of the steak was incredible. 牛排鲜嫩多汁，令人难以置信。
The juiciness of the gossip made it irresistible. 这条八卦津津有味，让人欲罢不能。
The juiciness of his writing style captivated readers. 他生动有趣的写作风格吸引了读者。
Synonyms: succulence, moistness, richness, vibrancy
Antonyms: dryness, blandness, dullness
