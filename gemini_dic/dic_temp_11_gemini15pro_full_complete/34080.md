civilities:/səˈvɪlətiz/| n.| 礼貌；客气话；礼节；寒暄|Acts of courtesy or politeness, or the expressions of such acts.  The formal or conventional requirements of polite social behavior.|She exchanged the usual civilities with her neighbors. 她和邻居们互相寒暄了几句。|Synonyms: courtesies, politeness, manners, etiquette, decorum|Antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, discourtesy|Noun
