coal-fired:/ˈkoʊlˌfaɪərd/| adj.|燃煤的|Powered by burning coal.  Referring to something, typically an industrial facility like a power plant, that uses coal as its fuel source.|The coal-fired power plant was a major source of pollution.  这家燃煤发电厂是主要的污染源。|Near synonyms: coal-burning, coal-powered|Near antonyms:  gas-fired, oil-fired, nuclear-powered|adjective
