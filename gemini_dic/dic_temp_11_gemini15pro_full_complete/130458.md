nonabsorption:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːrpʃn/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrpʃn/| n. |不吸收，非吸收|The nonabsorption of nutrients can lead to health problems. 营养物质的不吸收会导致健康问题。|例句：The nonabsorption of certain vitamins can cause deficiencies.  某些维生素的不吸收会导致缺乏症。

例句：The doctor diagnosed the problem as a nonabsorption of iron. 医生诊断该问题为铁的非吸收。|近义词：non-assimilation, malabsorption|反义词: absorption, assimilation|词性:名词
