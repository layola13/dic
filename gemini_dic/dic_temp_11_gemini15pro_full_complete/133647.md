nonformatively:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːmətivli/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɔːrmətɪvli/| adv.|非形式地，非构成性地|In a way that does not contribute to the formation of a word or its meaning.  Not relating to the formation of words or morphemes.|The prefix 'un-' is sometimes added nonformatively, as in 'unloose', which doesn't mean the opposite of 'loose'. “un-”前缀有时是非形式地添加的，例如在“unloose”中，它并不意味着“loose”的反义词。|Near Synonyms: irregularly, derivatively (somewhat), informally|Antonyms: formatively, regularly|词性:副词
