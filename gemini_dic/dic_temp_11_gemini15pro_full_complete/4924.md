alioth:/ˈælɪɒθ/| n.| 天枢星 (北斗七星的第五颗星)|Alioth is the fifth brightest star in the constellation Ursa Major, and part of the Big Dipper asterism.  它是大熊星座中第五亮的星，也是北斗七星的一部分。|Alioth, a star in the Big Dipper, is easily visible in the night sky. 天枢星，北斗七星中的一颗星，在夜空中很容易看到。|Synonyms: Epsilon Ursae Majoris|Antonyms: N/A|noun
