nanotechnologist: /ˌnænəʊtekˈnɒlədʒɪst/ (British) /ˌnænəʊtekˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ (American) | n. | 纳米技术专家 | A scientist or engineer specializing in nanotechnology. |  Dr. Lee is a leading nanotechnologist in the field of materials science. 李博士是材料科学领域领先的纳米技术专家。|  Nanotechnologists are working on developing new materials with unique properties. 纳米技术专家正在致力于开发具有独特性能的新材料。 | synonyms: nanoscientist, nanotechnician | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "nano-" (prefix denoting one billionth) + "technology" + "-ist" (suffix denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something).  "Nano-" comes from the Greek word "nanos," meaning "dwarf."
