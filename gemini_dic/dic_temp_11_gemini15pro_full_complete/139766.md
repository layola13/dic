octagons:/ˈɒktəɡɒnz/ (UK), /ˈɑːktəɡɑːnz/ (US)| n. |八边形，八角形|A plane figure with eight straight sides and eight angles. |例句：The stop sign is in the shape of an octagon.  停车标志是八边形的。

例句：The building's foundation was an octagon. 这座建筑的地基是八边形的。|近义词：polygon 多边形|反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps circle or other shapes with fewer sides) |词性: Noun
