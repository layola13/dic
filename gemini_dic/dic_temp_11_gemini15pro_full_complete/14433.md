auxesis:/ɔːkˈsiːsɪs/
|n.|
|增大，增强；夸张|
|An increase in intensity; amplification.  A rhetorical term for a gradual increase in intensity of meaning with words arranged in ascending order of force or importance.|
|The poet employed auxesis to emphasize the hero's bravery.|诗人运用递增法来强调英雄的勇敢。|
|augmentation, amplification, intensification, exaggeration, hyperbole|
|diminution, understatement, meiosis|
|noun|
