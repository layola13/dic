dehydrated:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtɪd/
adjective
脱水的，干燥的
removed water from; dried
例句：The dehydrated hikers were suffering from severe thirst.  脱水的徒步旅行者口渴得厉害。

例句：Dehydrated fruits are a healthy snack option.  脱水果干是一种健康的零食选择。

近义词：desiccated, dried, parched
反义词：hydrated, saturated, moist
词性：adjective


dehydrate:/diːˈhaɪdreɪt/
verb
脱水，使干燥
to remove water from; to dry
例句：The hot sun quickly dehydrated the ground.  炎热的太阳很快使地面脱水。

例句：You need to dehydrate these vegetables before storing them.  你把这些蔬菜存放之前，需要先脱水。

近义词：desiccate, dry, parch
反义词：hydrate, moisten, saturate
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + hydrate.  De- is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse." Hydrate comes from the Greek "hydōr" meaning "water."
