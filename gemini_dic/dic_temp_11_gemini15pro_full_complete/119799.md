migratorial:/ˌmaɪɡrəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
迁徙的；洄游的
Relating to migration, habitually moving from one region to another.
Swallows are migratory birds. 燕子是候鸟。
The salmon is a migratory fish. 鲑鱼是一种洄游鱼类。
近义词:migrant, nomadic
反义词:resident, settled
词性:adjective

migratory:/ˈmaɪɡrətɔːri/
adjective
迁徙的；洄游的
Relating to migration, habitually moving from one region to another.
Monarch butterflies are known for their long migratory journeys. 黑脉金斑蝶以其长途迁徙而闻名。
Many species of whales are migratory. 许多种类的鲸鱼都是迁徙的。
近义词:migrant, nomadic, traveling
反义词:resident, settled, stationary
词性:adjective


词源:  From Late Latin *migratorius, from Latin migrat-, past participle stem of migrare “to migrate.”
