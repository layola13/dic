eremitic:/ˌɛrəˈmɪtɪk/
adjective
隐居的；遁世的；像隐士的
Characterized by solitude and seclusion, like a hermit.
He lived an eremitic life in the mountains. 他在山中过着隐居的生活。
近义词：reclusive, solitary, secluded
反义词：gregarious, sociable
词性: adjective

eremite:/ˈɛrəˌmaɪt/
noun
隐士；遁世者
A person who lives in solitude, often for religious reasons.
The old hermit lived in a cave.  这位老隐士住在山洞里。
近义词：hermit, recluse, anchorite
反义词：socialite
词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin *eremiticus, from Greek erēmitēs "desert dweller," from erēmia "desert, solitude," from erēmos "lonely, desolate, deserted."
