fleabag:/ˈfliːbæɡ/| n. |流浪汉；邋遢的人；低级旅馆；<非正式>猫,狗|A person or animal infested with fleas; a dirty, rundown place; (informal) a cat or a dog. |例句：That motel was a real fleabag. 那家汽车旅馆真是个肮脏的破地方。|例句：He looked like a fleabag after sleeping on the streets. 在街上睡了一晚后，他看起来像个流浪汉。|近义词：tramp, vagrant, down-and-out, dive, dump|反义词: posh hotel, luxurious accommodation|词性:名词

fleabag:/ˈfliːbæɡ/| adj. |破旧的；肮脏的|Dirty and rundown. |例句：The fleabag motel was the only place we could afford. 这家破旧的汽车旅馆是我们唯一能负担得起的地方。|例句：She inherited a fleabag apartment from her uncle. 她从叔叔那里继承了一间破旧的公寓。|近义词：seedy, dilapidated, run-down|反义词:clean, well-maintained|词性:形容词
