observability:/əbˌzɜːrvəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可观察性 | The ability to infer internal states of a system by observing its external outputs.  | Example: The observability of the software system allows engineers to quickly identify the root cause of errors.  软件系统的可观察性使工程师能够快速识别错误的根本原因。

Example:  Improved observability is key to managing complex distributed systems.  提高可观察性是管理复杂分布式系统的关键。 | Near synonyms:  monitorability, visibility, trackability | Antonyms: opacity, obscurity | Noun
