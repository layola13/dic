baalite:/ˈbeɪəˌlaɪt/| n. |镁橄榄石|A mineral of the olivine group, (Mg,Fe)2SiO4, containing more magnesium than iron. It is typically olive-green in color and found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|Baalite is a common component of peridotite. 镁橄榄石是橄榄岩的常见成分。|The geologist identified the green crystal as baalite. 地质学家将绿色晶体鉴定为镁橄榄石。|Synonyms: forsterite, olivine|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
