loudliest:/ˈlaʊdɪst/| adj.|最响亮的，声音最大的|superlative form of loud;  used to compare three or more things and indicate the one producing the most sound.|The loudest cheer came from the home team's fans.  主队球迷的欢呼声最大。|synonyms: noisiest, strongest, |antonyms: quietest, softest, faintest|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *loudest, from Old English hlūdast, superlative of hlūd (“loud”).
