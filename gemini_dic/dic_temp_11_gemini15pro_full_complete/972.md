acalysinous: /ˌækælɪˈsaɪnəs/ | adj. | 不脱落的，不凋谢的 | Not falling off or fading; persistent.  | The acalysinous flowers of the plant remained vibrant throughout the winter.  这种植物不凋谢的花朵整个冬天都保持着活力。 |  近义词: persistent, enduring, unfading, everlasting | 反义词: deciduous, caducous, ephemeral, transient | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek *a- "not" + *kalyein "to cover, conceal" + -ous (adjective suffix).  The original meaning relates to a flower's calyx not enclosing the developing fruit and not falling off as the fruit matures.
