correctress: /kəˈrɛktrɪs/
n.
女校正员，女修改者
A female corrector; a woman who corrects or amends.
She worked as a correctress for a publishing house. 她在一间出版社担任女校对员。
The correctress meticulously reviewed the manuscript. 女校对员一丝不苟地审阅了手稿。
近义词: proofreader, editor, reviser
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider words like "author" or "writer" as someone creating the original content needing correction)
词性: noun
