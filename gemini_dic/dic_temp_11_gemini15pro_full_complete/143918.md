outpricing:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪsɪŋ/
verb
定价低于…; 比…售价低
The act of selling goods or services at a lower price than competitors.
Example: They are outpricing their competitors by 10%. 他们将价格定得比竞争对手低 10%。
Example: The company's strategy is to outprice all rivals. 该公司的策略是定价低于所有竞争对手。
Synonym: undercutting, underselling
Antonym: overpricing, inflating prices
verb

outpricing:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪsɪŋ/
noun
低价销售; 低价竞争
The practice or policy of selling goods or services at a lower price than competitors.
Example: The outpricing of smaller businesses is a serious concern.  小型企业的低价竞争是一个严重的问题。
Example:  Their aggressive outpricing led to increased market share.  他们激进的低价策略带来了市场份额的增长。
Synonym: underpricing, price war
Antonym: price gouging, premium pricing
noun
