jived:/dʒaɪvd/
verb
跳吉特巴舞，符合，欺骗
agreed, corresponded, danced the jive
The band played a fast number and we jived. 乐队演奏了一首快节奏的曲子，我们跳起了吉特巴舞。
近义词: danced, agreed, corresponded, harmonized, tallied, matched
反义词: disagreed, clashed
词性: verb

jived:/dʒaɪvd/
adjective
兴奋的，充满活力的
excited, energetic
The crowd was jived about the upcoming concert.  观众对即将到来的音乐会感到兴奋。
近义词: excited, energetic, lively, animated
反义词: bored, apathetic
词性: adjective
