felinities:/fɪˈlɪnɪtiz/
n.
猫的特性；猫科动物的特征
the characteristic qualities of cats; cat-like characteristics
Her elegant movements and aloof demeanor suggested a certain felinity. 她优雅的动作和冷漠的态度暗示着她具有某种猫的特性。

近义词:cattishness, feline qualities
反义词:caninity, dog-like qualities
词性:noun
