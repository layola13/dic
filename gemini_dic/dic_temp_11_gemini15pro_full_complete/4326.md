ajowan:/əˈdʒoʊən/ | n. | 香旱芹籽，阿魏 | An annual herb (Trachyspermum ammi) of the parsley family, native to India and widely cultivated for its aromatic seeds, which are used as a spice. |  Ajowan seeds are often used in Indian cuisine. 香旱芹籽经常用于印度菜肴。| The ajowan gave the dish a distinct flavor.  香旱芹给这道菜增添了独特的风味。 | synonyms: bishop's weed, carom, thymol seed | antonyms: N/A | noun
