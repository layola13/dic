out-cue: /ˈaʊtˌkjuː/
noun
提示；结束提示；出场提示
A signal (usually non-verbal) to a performer to end a scene, piece of music, or speech, or to exit the stage.
The director gave the actor an out-cue by dropping his hand. 导演放下手，给了演员一个出场提示。
