coccolith:/ˈkɒkəlɪθ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkəlɪθ/ (US)| n. |颗石|A tiny calcareous plate, typically elliptical, formed by certain unicellular algae (coccolithophores) and found in large numbers in chalk and some other rocks.|Coccoliths are so small that they can only be seen with a microscope. 颗石非常小，只能用显微镜才能看到。|Near synonyms:  nannofossil, microfossil|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
