learner:/ˈlɜːrnər/ (US) /ˈlɜːnə(r)/ (UK)| n. |学习者，学生|Someone who is learning something, especially a skill or a language.|Example: He's a quick learner and picks up new languages easily. 他学东西很快，能轻松掌握新语言。|Example: The program is designed for adult learners. 这个项目是为成年学习者设计的。|近义词：student, pupil, apprentice|反义词：teacher, instructor, expert|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English lernere, from Old English leornere (“learner, disciple, scholar”), equivalent to learn + -er. Cognate with Old Frisian lirnere (“learner”), Old Saxon liornari (“learner”), Old High German lirnāri (“learner”), German Lerner (“learner”).
