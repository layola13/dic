creosote: /ˈkriːəˌsoʊt/ (US) /ˈkriːəsəʊt/ (UK)| n. |木馏油；杂酚油|A dark brown oily liquid distilled from coal tar, used as a wood preservative.|The fence posts were treated with creosote.  栅栏柱子用木馏油处理过。|Synonyms: wood preservative, tar oil|Antonyms: None|noun


creosote: /ˈkriːəˌsoʊt/ (US) /ˈkriːəsəʊt/ (UK)| vt. |用木馏油处理|To treat (wood) with creosote.|They creosoted the railway sleepers. 他们用木馏油处理了铁路枕木。|Synonyms: preserve (wood), treat (wood)|Antonyms: None|verb
