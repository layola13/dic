monoglacialist:/ˌmɒnəˈɡleɪʃəlɪst/
n.
冰期学家；专研一次冰期的人
A person who specializes in the study of a single glacial period.
The monoglacialist presented her research on the Illinoian Stage.  这位冰期学家展示了她对伊利诺伊冰期的研究。
The monoglacialist argued that the evidence pointed to a single, intense period of glaciation.  这位冰期学家认为，证据指向一次单一而剧烈的冰期。
近义词: glacialist, ice age specialist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a specialist in non-glacial periods)
