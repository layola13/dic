ngo:/ˌɛnˌdʒiˈoʊ/ | n. | 非政府组织 (non-governmental organization) | A non-profit organization that operates independently of any government, typically one whose purpose is to address a social or political issue. |  Example: Many NGOs are working to improve access to clean water in developing countries. 许多非政府组织正在努力改善发展中国家清洁水的供应。

Example: The NGO provides medical care to refugees.  这个非政府组织向难民提供医疗服务。

| 近义词: non-profit, charity, voluntary organization | 反义词: government organization, for-profit organization | 词性: noun
