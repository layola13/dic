brattish:/ˈbrætɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，任性的，孩子气的|Resembling or characteristic of a brat; spoiled, ill-mannered.  Behaving in a childish, petulant way.|例句：He was acting rather brattish about not getting his way. 他因为没有得到自己想要的东西而表现得相当幼稚。

例句：Her brattish behavior embarrassed her parents. 她孩子气的行为让她的父母很尴尬。|近义词：spoiled, childish, immature, petulant, churlish|反义词：mature, well-behaved, polite, courteous|词性:形容词
