deleteriously:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəsli/
adverb
有害地
in a way that is harmful and damaging.
High levels of unemployment are having a deleterious effect on the economy. 高失业率正对经济产生有害影响。
Synonyms: harmfully, damagingly, detrimentally, injuriously, adversely
Antonyms: beneficially, advantageously
词性: 副词


deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
有害的
causing harm or damage.
The chemical has a deleterious effect on the environment. 这种化学物质对环境有有害影响。
Synonyms: harmful, damaging, detrimental, injurious, adverse
Antonyms: beneficial, advantageous
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēlēteriālis, from dēlēre (“to destroy, wipe out, erase”), from dē- (“down, away”) + lēre (“to smear”).
