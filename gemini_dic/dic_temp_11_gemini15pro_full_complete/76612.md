gallnut:/ˈɡɔːlnʌt/ | n. | 瘿(yǐng)；五倍子 |A hard, round outgrowth on a tree, especially an oak tree, caused by a gall wasp depositing its eggs in the tree's tissue.  It is rich in tannic acid and used in tanning, dyeing, and making ink.|Oak gallnuts are used in traditional medicine. 橡树瘿在传统医学中使用。|Synonym: gall, oak apple |Antonym: N/A | noun

gallnut:/ˈɡɔːlnʌt/ | n. | 胆汁; 怨恨；恼怒 | Figuratively, something bitter or irritating. |  His constant criticism was a gallnut to her.  他不断的批评让她很恼火。 | Synonym: bitterness, resentment | Antonym: solace, comfort | noun
