colour-coded:/ˈkʌlə(r) koʊdɪd/| adj.|颜色编码的，用颜色标记的|Something that is colour-coded uses different colours to represent different things, making it easier to organize or understand information.  This system often employs a key or legend to explain the meaning of each colour.|The wires are colour-coded for easy identification. 电线用颜色编码以便于识别。

The files are colour-coded according to their importance. 文件根据其重要性用颜色编码。| color-coded, color-differentiated | N/A | adjective
