calcites:/ˈkælˌsaɪts/| n. |方解石|A common crystalline form of calcium carbonate, CaCO3, found in various forms, including limestone, marble, and chalk. It is typically colorless or white but can be tinted by impurities.|The cave was filled with beautiful calcites of various shapes and sizes. 这个洞穴里充满了各种形状和大小的美丽的方解石。|Synonyms: calcite crystal, calcium carbonate|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
