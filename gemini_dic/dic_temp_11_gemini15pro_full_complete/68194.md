fair-haired:/ˌfeərˈhɛəd/ | adj. | 金发的，浅色头发的 | Having light-colored hair, typically blond. |  She was a fair-haired girl with blue eyes. 她是一个金发碧眼的女孩子。 |  blond, light-haired, golden-haired | dark-haired, brunette | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "fair" (attractive, beautiful) + "haired" (having hair of a specified kind).  "Fair" comes from Old English "fæger" (beautiful, pleasing). "Haired" comes from Old English "hǣr" (hair).
