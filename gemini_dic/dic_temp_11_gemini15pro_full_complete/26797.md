caffeine:/ˈkæfiːn/ /kæˈfiːn/| n. |咖啡因；咖啡碱|A crystalline compound that is found especially in tea and coffee plants and is a stimulant of the central nervous system.| I try to avoid caffeine after lunch.  我尽量在午饭后避免摄入咖啡因。|Synonyms: stimulant, energizer|Antonyms: depressant, sedative|noun
