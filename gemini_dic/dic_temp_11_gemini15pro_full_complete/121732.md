mispronounced: /ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊnst/ | adj. | 发音错误的；读音不准的 |  Incorrectly pronounced; said the wrong way. | He mispronounced my name. 他把我的名字念错了。|  mispronounced the word "February" 把“February”这个词的发音发错了。| 近义词: misarticulated, misspoken | 反义词: correctly pronounced, articulated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From mis- + pronounce + -ed.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Pronounce" comes from Old French "prononcer," ultimately from Latin "pronuntiare," meaning "to proclaim" or "to pronounce."
