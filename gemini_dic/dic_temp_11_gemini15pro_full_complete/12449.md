aslug: /əˈslʌɡ/ | n. | 鼻涕虫；懒汉；少量烈酒 | A small, shell-less terrestrial gastropod mollusk; a slow-moving, lazy person; a small amount of liquor, especially swallowed quickly. | Example 1: The garden was infested with aslugs after the rain. 雨后花园里到处都是鼻涕虫。

Example 2: He's such an aslug; he never helps out around the house. 他真是个懒汉，从来不帮忙做家务。| 近义词: slug, snail (for the mollusk); lazybones, idler (for the person); shot, nip (for the liquor) | 反义词: (For the person) energetic, active | 词性: noun
