get-across:/ɡɛt əˈkrɒs/ (British) /ɡɛt əˈkrɔːs/ (American)| phrasal verb |使…被理解；把…讲清楚|To successfully communicate an idea or message so that it is understood. |例句：I tried to get my point across, but they just wouldn't listen. 我试图把我的观点讲清楚，但他们就是不听。

例句：She's a brilliant scientist, but she has trouble getting her ideas across to a non-scientific audience. 她是一位杰出的科学家，但她很难向非科学界的观众解释清楚她的想法。|近义词：communicate, convey, explain, express|反义词：misunderstand, confuse, obscure|词性:动词短语
