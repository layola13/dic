eyren:/ˈaɪərən/| n.| runner; messenger; errand-runner (obsolete)|A person sent on a message or errand, especially in medieval times. |He sent an eyren to deliver the important missive. 他派了一个信使去传递重要的信件。|近义词：messenger, courier, envoy|反义词:receiver, recipient|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *eirande-renner*, from Old English *ǣrende* (errand) + *rinnan* (to run).
