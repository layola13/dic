chocolate: /ˈtʃɒklət/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːklət/ (US) | n. adj.| 巧克力；巧克力色的 | A food made from roasted and ground cacao seeds, that can be eaten as a sweet or used as a flavoring.  Also, a dark brown color. |  例句：I love dark chocolate. 我喜欢黑巧克力。| 近义词：cacao, cocoa, candy | 反义词: None (no direct opposite for a food) | 词性: Noun, Adjective

chocolate: /ˈtʃɒklət/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːklət/ (US) | adj.| 巧克力色的 | Having the color of chocolate; dark brown. | 例句：She wore a chocolate brown dress. 她穿了一件巧克力棕色的连衣裙。 | 近义词：brown, dark brown | 反义词: None (no direct opposite for a color) | 词性: Adjective
