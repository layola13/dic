batiking:/bæˈtiːkɪŋ/
n.
蜡染
The process of creating designs on fabric using wax and dye.
She spent the afternoon batiking a silk scarf. 她下午花了时间蜡染一条丝巾。
dyeing, printing
---
詞性: noun
中文翻譯: 蜡染（名词）
英文解析: The process of producing colored designs on textiles by dyeing them, having first applied wax to the parts not to be dyed.
例句:
She enjoys batiking in her free time.  她空闲时间喜欢蜡染。
The intricate designs on the batik fabric were stunning.  蜡染织物上复杂的图案令人惊叹。

近义词:  dyeing, wax printing, textile printing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) bleaching, plain weaving
