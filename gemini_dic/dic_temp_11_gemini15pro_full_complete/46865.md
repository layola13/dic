daredevils:/ˈdɛəˌdɛvəlz/ (US) /ˈdeəˌdevlz/ (UK)| n.| 胆大妄为的人；冒险家|A person who enjoys taking physical risks or engaging in dangerous activities. |例句：The daredevils performed breathtaking stunts on their motorcycles.  那些胆大妄为的人骑着摩托车表演了惊险的特技。

例句：The group of daredevils climbed the skyscraper without any safety equipment. 这群冒险家在没有任何安全设备的情况下爬上了摩天大楼。|近义词：thrill-seekers, risk-takers, adventurers|反义词：cautious people, timid people|词性:名词

daredevil:/ˈdɛəˌdɛvəl/ (US) /ˈdeəˌdevl/ (UK)| adj.| 胆大妄为的；冒险的|Recklessly daring; adventurous. |例句：He made a daredevil escape from the burning building. 他从燃烧的建筑物里进行了大胆的逃脱。

例句：Her daredevil attitude made her popular amongst her peers. 她大胆的态度使她在同龄人中很受欢迎。|近义词：reckless, audacious, bold|反义词：cautious, timid, careful|词性:形容词
