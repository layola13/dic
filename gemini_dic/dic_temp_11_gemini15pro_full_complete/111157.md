lithiasis:/lɪˈθaɪəsɪs/
noun
结石病
The formation of stony concretions (calculi) in the body, most commonly in the urinary system (kidney stones), gallbladder (gallstones), or salivary glands.
He suffered from renal lithiasis. 他患有肾结石。
Synonyms: stone formation, calculi, concretion
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a disease process, but one could consider "stone dissolution" as a contrasting process)
