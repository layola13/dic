impactite:/ˈɪmpæktaɪt/| n. |撞击岩，冲击岩|A rock created or modified by the impact of a meteorite or other extraterrestrial body. impactites:/ˈɪmpæktaɪts/| n. |撞击岩，冲击岩（复数）|Rocks created or modified by the impact of a meteorite or other extraterrestrial body.
