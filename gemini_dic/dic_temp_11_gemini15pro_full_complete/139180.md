objected:/əbˈdʒɛktɪd/ | v. | 反对，不赞成 |  Expressing disapproval or opposition to something. |  He objected to the proposal, citing potential risks. 他反对这项提议，理由是存在潜在风险。 |  opposed, protested, demurred | agreed, consented | verb

objection:/əbˈdʒɛkʃən/ | n. | 反对，异议 | An expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition; a reason for disagreeing. |  Her main objection to the plan was the cost. 她对该计划的主要反对意见是成本问题。 |  opposition, protest, dissent | agreement, consent | noun
