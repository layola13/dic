hydrothorax:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɔːræks/ | n. | 胸腔积液 | An accumulation of serous fluid in the pleural cavity. | The patient presented with shortness of breath due to hydrothorax. 病人因胸腔积液而出现呼吸急促。|  Example 2:  The hydrothorax was a complication of his congestive heart failure. 胸腔积液是他充血性心力衰竭的并发症。| 近义词: pleural effusion | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From hydro- + thorax, from Greek hydrōr, hydrōros "dropsy, watery swelling" + thōrax "chest, breastplate."
