anandrous:/ænˈændrəs/
adjective
无雄蕊的；雄蕊缺失的
Lacking stamens; having no stamens.
The anandrous flowers of this species rely entirely on wind pollination.  这种物种的无雄蕊花完全依靠风媒授粉。
This mutant strain produces anandrous flowers, making it sterile. 这个突变株产生无雄蕊的花朵，使其不育。
Near synonyms: stamenless, male-sterile
Antonyms: staminate, polyandrous
词性: adjective
