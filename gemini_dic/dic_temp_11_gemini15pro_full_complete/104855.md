keelrakes:/ˈkiːlˌreɪks/
noun
龙骨耙，船底刮刀
A tool, typically with long handles and multiple scraping blades, used to remove marine growth from the keel of a ship or boat.
The divers used keelrakes to clean the barnacles off the ship's hull.  潜水员用龙骨耙清理船体上的藤壶。

近义词:  barnacle scraper, hull scraper
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps fouling agent - something that causes growth on a hull)
词性:  n. (noun)
