armourless:/ˈɑːmələs/| adj.|无装甲的，无防备的|Without armour; unprotected.  Lacking defensive covering.|The armourless knight was vulnerable to attack. 无装甲的骑士很容易受到攻击。|近义词：defenseless, unprotected, vulnerable, exposed|反义词:armoured, protected, shielded|词性:形容词
词源: From "armour" (protective covering) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Armour" ultimately derives from Old French "armure."
