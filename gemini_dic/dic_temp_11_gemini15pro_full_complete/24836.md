browne:/braʊn/| adj. n.|棕色的，棕色人种；姓氏|Of a dark yellowish-brown color.  A person of mixed European and Black African or Caribbean descent.|例句：He has browne hair. 他有棕色的头发。|例句：Ms. Browne is a renowned lawyer. 布朗女士是一位著名的律师。|近义词：brown, chestnut, auburn (for hair color)|反义词:blond, white, fair|词性:形容词，名词
词源: Middle English: variant of broun.
