beyond-shadow-of-a-doubt:/biˈjɒnd ˈʃædəʊ əv ə daʊt/ (UK) /biˈjɑːnd ˈʃædoʊ əv ə daʊt/ (US)| idiom |毫无疑问；毋庸置疑|Without any doubt; with absolute certainty.  This phrase emphasizes the complete absence of doubt or uncertainty.|例句：He was guilty beyond a shadow of a doubt. 他毫无疑问是有罪的。|例句：The evidence proves her innocence beyond a shadow of a doubt. 证据确凿地证明了她的清白。|近义词：undoubtedly, certainly, definitely, absolutely, incontestably|反义词：doubtfully, questionably, possibly|词性: idiom
