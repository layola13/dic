aeronautically:/ˌɛrəˈnɔːtɪkli/
adverb|航空地；飞行地|In a way that relates to aeronautics; with respect to the design and construction of aircraft.|The plane was aeronautically advanced for its time. 这架飞机就其时代而言在航空技术上是先进的。|
近义词:aviationally, airwise
反义词:n/a
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From aeronautic + -ally.  Aeronautic comes from French aéronautique, from aéro- + -nautique (from Greek nautikē, "art of navigation").
