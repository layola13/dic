algific:/ælˈdʒɪfɪk/ | adj. | 产冷的；致冷的 | Producing or causing coldness; cold. |例句：The algific cave is known for its consistently cold temperatures. 这个产冷洞穴以其持续的低温而闻名。

例句：Algific waters flow from the spring, creating a unique microclimate.  冰冷的泉水从泉眼中流出，创造了独特的微气候。 | 近义词：frigid, icy, chilling | 反义词：caloric, heating, warming | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *algificus, from algēre (“to be cold”) + -ficus (“-making”).
