analyticities:/ˌænəlɪˈtɪsɪtiz/| n. |解析性（复数）|The state or quality of being analytic.  It often refers to the properties of complex functions that are differentiable at every point in a given open set. |The analyticity of the function allowed for further mathematical manipulation. 该函数的解析性允许进一步的数学操作。|近义词: differentiability, holomorphism (for complex functions)|反义词: non-analyticity|noun
