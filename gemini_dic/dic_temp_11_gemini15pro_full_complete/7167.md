angerful: /ˈæŋɡərfl/
adj.
充满愤怒的，易怒的
feeling or expressing anger readily.
He gave her an angerful look. 他愤怒地瞪了她一眼。

近义词: angry, enraged, furious, wrathful, indignant, irate, cross, choleric
反义词: calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, placid, content

词性: 形容词 (Adjective)

词源:  "anger" (名词，愤怒) + "-ful" (后缀，充满...的)
