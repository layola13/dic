beaujolais: /boʊʒoʊˈleɪ/ | n. | 博若莱葡萄酒；博若莱地区| A red wine made in the Beaujolais region of France. |  I enjoy a glass of Beaujolais with dinner. 我喜欢晚餐时喝一杯博若莱葡萄酒。|  Synonym: red wine, Burgundy (sometimes) | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps white wine as a contrasting category) | noun

Etymology: From French, named after the Beaujolais region in France.
