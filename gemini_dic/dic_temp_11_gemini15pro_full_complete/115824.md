marketable:/ˈmɑːrkɪtəbl/ /ˈmɑːrkɪdəbl/| adj.|适销对路的；有销路的|Able to be sold; in demand.|例句：This course is designed to make you more marketable to potential employers. 这门课程旨在让你对潜在雇主更具吸引力。

例句：The company produces marketable products at competitive prices. 该公司以具有竞争力的价格生产适销对路的产品。|近义词：salable, merchantable, vendable|反义词：unmarketable, unsalable|词性:adjective

Etymology->
From market + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *mercatus* "trade, market".
