infatigable:/ɪnˈfætɪɡəbl/ | adj. |不知疲倦的，不屈不挠的 | Showing sustained enthusiastic energy; tireless.  |  She was an indefatigable campaigner for human rights. 她是一位不知疲倦的人权运动家。 |  tireless, relentless, persistent, unflagging, persevering | weary, tired, exhausted, fatigued | adjective

Etymology:
From French *infatigable*, from Latin *infatigabilis*, from *in-* (not) + *fatigabilis* (capable of being tired), from *fatigare* (to tire).
