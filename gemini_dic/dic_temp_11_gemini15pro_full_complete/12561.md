aspire-after: /əˈspaɪər ˈæftər/ | phrasal verb | 渴望；追求；向往 | To have a strong desire to achieve or obtain something, especially something considered to be of high value or difficult to attain. |  She aspired after a career in medicine. 她渴望从事医学事业。 |  He aspired after fame and fortune. 他追求名利。 | 近义词: yearn for, long for, crave, desire, strive for | 反义词:  disregard, neglect, ignore | 词性: phrasal verb
