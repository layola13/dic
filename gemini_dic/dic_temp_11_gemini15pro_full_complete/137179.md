nonsuction:/ˌnɒnˈsʌkʃən/
adjective
非吸入式的；非抽吸的
Relating to or denoting something that does not involve suction.
The wound was dressed with a nonsuction bandage.  伤口用非吸入式绷带包扎。
Nonsuction wound dressings are preferred for some types of injuries. 某些类型的损伤更适合使用非吸入式伤口敷料。
近义词:non-aspirating, non-sucking
反义词:suction, aspirating
词性:adjective
