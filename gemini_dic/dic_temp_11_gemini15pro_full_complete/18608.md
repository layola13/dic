benny:/ˈbɛni/| n.|安非他明，苯丙胺 (俚语);福利救济金(主要指英国);|A tablet of amphetamine or a related drug.  Also: money received as unemployment benefit (chiefly Brit.).|Example: He popped a benny to stay awake. 他吞了一粒安非他明以保持清醒。

Example: He's been on the bennies for months. 他已经领了好几个月的失业救济金了。|近义词：amphetamine, dole (UK), welfare|反义词: None (for drug meaning); income, earnings (for benefit meaning)|词性:noun
