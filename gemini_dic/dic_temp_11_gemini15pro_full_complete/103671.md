jolsons:/ˈdʒoʊlsənz/| n.|乔森一家 (指美国艺人艾尔·乔森及其家人)|Referring to Al Jolson, a famous American entertainer, and often his family, particularly in the context of his performances.|He put on a real "Jolsons" show. 他上演了一场真正的“乔森一家”式的演出。|近义词: entertainers, performers|反义词: audience, viewers|词性: 名词

词源: This word is derived from the surname of Al Jolson (Asa Yoelson), a popular American singer, comedian, and actor. It's a proper noun turned common noun, used to describe a style of entertainment reminiscent of his energetic and emotional performances.
