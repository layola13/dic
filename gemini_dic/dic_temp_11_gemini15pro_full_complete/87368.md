herologist:/ˌhɪərɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 英雄学家；英雄研究者 | A person who studies heroes, their lives, and their impact on society.  | Example: The herologist gave a lecture on the archetypes of heroism in Greek mythology. 英雄学家做了一个关于希腊神话中英雄原型的讲座。 | Example:  The herologist analyzed the cultural significance of folk heroes in different societies.  英雄学家分析了民间英雄在不同社会中的文化意义。 | Synonym:  hero scholar, mythologist (partially) | Antonym:  villainographer (a humorous or fictional antonym) | noun
