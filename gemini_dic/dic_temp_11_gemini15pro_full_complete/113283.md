luteo:/ˈluːtioʊ/| adj.|黄色的，黄体的|Of or relating to the corpus luteum (the yellow endocrine gland that forms in the ovary of mammals after ovulation).  Derived from the Latin "luteus" meaning "yellow".|The luteo phase of the menstrual cycle is characterized by high progesterone levels. 月经周期的黄体期以高孕酮水平为特征。|The doctor monitored the patient's luteo function during pregnancy. 医生在怀孕期间监测了病人的黄体功能。|Synonyms: yellow, yellowish|Antonyms:  No direct antonyms, consider colors like blue, green, etc.|adjective
