ghoulishnesses:/ˈɡuːlɪʃnəsɪz/| n. |令人毛骨悚然，恐怖 |The quality of being ghoulish; morbid fascination with death or horror. |The ghoulishnesses of the haunted house sent shivers down my spine. 鬼屋的恐怖气氛让我脊背发凉。| 近义词：gruesomeness, macabre, creepiness | 反义词：pleasantness, cheerfulness | 词性：名词

词源: From ghoulish + -ness.  "Ghoulish" comes from "ghoul,"  ultimately from Arabic غُول (ghūl), a desert demon believed to rob graves and feed on corpses.
