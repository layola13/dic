flirter:/ˈflɜːrtər/ (US) /ˈflɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|调情者，轻浮的人|A person who flirts; someone who behaves as though attracted to someone, but without serious intentions.|She's a terrible flirt, she flirts with everyone! 她是个很轻浮的人，她和每个人都调情！|He's a known flirt around the office. 他是办公室里有名的花花公子。|Synonyms: coquette, tease, trifler|Antonyms: prude, wallflower|noun

flirt:/flɜːrt/ (US) /flɜːt/ (UK)| v.|调情，玩弄，轻率地对待|To behave as though attracted to someone, but without serious intentions; to trifle with.|He was flirting with the waitress. 他在和女服务员调情。|She flirted with the idea of moving to Paris. 她一时兴起想过要搬到巴黎去。|Synonyms: toy, dally, trifle|Antonyms: ignore, shun|verb
