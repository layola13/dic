procrastination: /prəˌkræstɪˈneɪʃən/ /proʊˌkræstɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 拖延，拖延症 |  the act of delaying something that must be done, often because it is unpleasant or boring |  I know I should start working on this project, but procrastination keeps getting in the way. 我知道我应该开始做这个项目了，但是我一直拖延。| synonyms: delaying, postponing, stalling | antonyms: diligence, promptness | noun

procrastinate: /prəˈkræstɪneɪt/ /proʊˈkræstɪneɪt/ | v. | 拖延 | to delay doing something that must be done |  I've been procrastinating on writing this paper for weeks. 我已经拖延写这篇论文好几个星期了。 | synonyms: delay, postpone, stall | antonyms: expedite, hasten | verb
