overpensive:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪv/| adj.|过度沉思的，忧思的|Characterized by excessive pensiveness; deeply or excessively thoughtful, often in a melancholy way.  Given to or marked by excessive or undue contemplation or serious thought.|She sat by the window, overpensive and staring at the rain. 她坐在窗边，忧心忡忡地望着雨。|近义词：contemplative, meditative, brooding, pensive, thoughtful|反义词：carefree, lighthearted, frivolous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From over- + pensive.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Pensive" comes from Old French "pensif," ultimately from Latin "pensare" (to think, consider, weigh).
