napierian:/nəˈpɪəriən/ | adj. | 纳皮尔式的，自然对数的 | Relating to or involving logarithms to the base *e* (also called natural logarithms), especially as developed by John Napier. | The napierian logarithm of 10 is approximately 2.302585.  纳皮尔对数10约等于2.302585。 |  Napierian constant is represented as 'e'. 纳皮尔常数用'e'表示。| 近义词: natural (logarithmic) | 反义词: common (logarithmic) | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From John Napier (1550-1617), a Scottish mathematician who invented logarithms. 源于约翰·纳皮尔（1550-1617），一位发明了对数的苏格兰数学家。
