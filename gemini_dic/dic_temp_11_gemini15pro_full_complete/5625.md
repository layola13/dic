alternativity:/ˌɔːltərˈnætɪvɪti/ | n. | 交替性，选择性，二择一性 | The state or quality of being alternative; the fact of offering a choice between two or more things. |  The alternativity of the treatment options allowed patients to choose the best fit for their lifestyle. 治疗方案的选择性使患者能够选择最适合他们生活方式的方案。| synonyms: choice, option, alternative, selection | antonyms: necessity, obligation, compulsion | noun

alternativity:/ˌɔːltərˈnætɪvɪti/ | n. | 交替性，选择性，二择一性 | The state or quality of being mutually exclusive; the fact that only one of two or more possibilities can be true or realized at the same time. | The alternativity of the hypotheses meant that only one could be correct. 这些假设的互斥性意味着只有一个是正确的。 | synonyms: mutual exclusivity, incompatibility, disjunction | antonyms: compatibility, conjunction, concurrence | noun
