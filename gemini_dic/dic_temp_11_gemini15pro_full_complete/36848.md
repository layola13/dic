colonnaded: /ˌkɒləˈneɪdɪd/ | adj. |有柱廊的，有列柱的 | Having a row of columns, usually supporting a roof or other structure.  |  The colonnaded walkway provided welcome shade from the midday sun. 有柱廊的走道提供了正午阳光下的阴凉。 | The grand colonnaded entrance impressed all the visitors. 雄伟的柱廊入口给所有游客留下了深刻的印象。 | synonyms: arcaded, pillared | antonyms: open, exposed | adjective
