alligatoring: /ˈælɪˌɡeɪtərɪŋ/
n.
鳄纹裂缝;  油漆或清漆表面出现像鳄鱼皮一样的裂纹
The development of cracks in a coating of paint or varnish, resembling the hide of an alligator.
The old paint on the door was alligatoring badly.  门上的旧油漆严重龟裂。
Synonyms:  cracking, crazing, checking
Antonyms: smooth, unblemished
noun
