bargained:/ˈbɑːrɡɪnd/ | v. | 讨价还价；商定；达成协议 |  past tense and past participle of "bargain" meaning to negotiate the terms and conditions of a transaction. |  We bargained with the street vendor for a lower price. 我们和街边小贩讨价还价，以求更低的价格。|  negotiated, haggled, dealt | conceded, surrendered | verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bargaignier (modern French barguigner), of uncertain origin.
