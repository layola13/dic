hominians:/ˈhɒmɪnɪnz/ | n. | 人族；人亚科动物 |  Members of the subfamily Homininae, which includes humans, chimpanzees, gorillas, and their extinct ancestors.  This term emphasizes the evolutionary relationship between humans and other great apes. |  Scientists study hominians to understand human evolution.  科学家研究人族以了解人类进化。 |  hominids, great apes |  (No clear antonym) | noun
