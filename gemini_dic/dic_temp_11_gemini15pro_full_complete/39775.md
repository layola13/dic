consommé:/kənˈsʌmeɪ/ | n. |清汤，浓缩肉汤 |A clear soup made from richly flavored stock that has been clarified, often garnished with vegetables or meat. | She served a delicate consommé as a starter. 她以清淡的浓汤作为开胃菜。| The consommé was rich and flavorful. 这汤味道浓郁鲜美。 | 近义词: broth, bouillon, stock | 反义词: thick soup | 词性: noun

consommés:/kənˈsʌmeɪz/ | n. |清汤（复数）| Plural form of consommé, referring to more than one clear soup made from richly flavored stock. | The chef prepared several consommés for the tasting menu.  这位厨师为品尝菜单准备了几种清汤。 | They offered a choice of two consommés: beef and chicken. 他们提供了两种清汤选择：牛肉和鸡肉。 | 近义词: broths, bouillons, stocks | 反义词: thick soups | 词性: noun
