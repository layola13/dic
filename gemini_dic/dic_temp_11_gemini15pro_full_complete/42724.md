counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/ | n. | 平衡重，平衡力，抗衡力 |  An object used to balance another weight, or a force or influence that balances or offsets another. | The crane uses a huge concrete block as a counterweight.  起重机使用一个巨大的混凝土块作为平衡重。 |  counterbalance, offset |  force, impetus | noun


counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/ | v. | 平衡，抵消 | To act as a counterweight to something. |  The government's spending on social programs is counterweighted by its cuts to other areas. 政府在社会项目上的支出被其在其他领域的削减所抵消。 | counterbalance, offset |  force, impetus | verb
