deterred:/dɪˈtɜːrd/ |adj.| 阻止的，威慑的，吓住的 | discouraged from doing something by fear of the consequences. |  The heavy fines deterred many drivers from speeding.  重罚阻止了许多司机超速行驶。| synonyms: discouraged, dissuaded, prevented, hindered | antonyms: encouraged, emboldened, incited, urged | word type: adjective

deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/ |verb|  阻止，威慑，吓住 | to discourage someone from doing something by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences. | High prices are deterring many young people from buying houses. 高昂的价格让很多年轻人不敢买房。| synonyms: discourage, dissuade, prevent, hinder | antonyms: encourage, embolden, incite, urge | word type: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin deterrēre, from de- + terrēre "to frighten."
