nonadmissibly:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪsɪbli/ | adv. | 不可接纳地，不被承认地 | Not in a way that is admissible; in an inadmissible manner.  It describes something that cannot be accepted as valid, particularly in a legal or formal context.  Derived from "admissible" with the prefix "non-". |  The evidence was nonadmissibly obtained and therefore couldn't be used in court.  证据是以不可接受的方式获取的，因此不能在法庭上使用。 |  The lawyer argued that the confession was nonadmissibly obtained due to coercion.  律师辩称，供词是由于胁迫而以不可接受的方式获得的。 | Synonyms: improperly, invalidly, wrongly, illegally (in some contexts) | Antonyms: admissibly, properly, legally, validly | 词性: 副词
