outlets:/ˈaʊtlɪts/ | n. | 出口，排放口；经销店，批发商店；插座 | A place or opening through which something, such as water, air, or electricity, is let out or discharged; a store that sells goods, especially at a reduced price from the manufacturer or supplier; a point on a wiring system at which current can be taken to supply an appliance. |  例句：The factory's outlets discharged polluted water into the river. 工厂的排放口将污水排入河中。

例句：She bought a discounted dress at a designer outlets. 她在一家名牌折扣店买了一条打折的连衣裙。

近义词：exits, vents, stores, shops, retailers, sockets, receptacles | 反义词：inlets, sources | 词性: 名词
