buffalo:/ˈbʌfəloʊ/ (US) /ˈbʌfələʊ/ (UK)| n. |水牛；野牛|A large bovine mammal of the ox family.  It is found in North America and Europe. |例句：The buffalo roamed the plains. 野牛在平原上漫游。

例句：The herd of buffalo was massive.  这群野牛数量庞大。|近义词：bison, ox |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

buffalo:/ˈbʌfəloʊ/ (US) /ˈbʌfələʊ/ (UK)| vt. |恐吓；迷惑| To intimidate, confuse, or baffle. |例句：He was buffaloed by the complex legal jargon. 他被复杂的法律术语搞糊涂了。

例句：Don't let them buffalo you into making a decision. 不要让他们吓唬你做决定。|近义词：intimidate, bewilder, confuse |反义词:encourage, clarify |词性:verb
