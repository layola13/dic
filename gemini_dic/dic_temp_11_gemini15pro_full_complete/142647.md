ostracizing: /ˈɒstrəsaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːstrəsaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
ostracizing: v.
ostracizing: 排斥；放逐
ostracizing: The act of excluding someone from a society or group.
ostracizing: He was ostracized by his colleagues for his controversial views.  他因为有争议的观点而被同事排斥。

ostracizing: The community ostracized her for breaking their traditions.  她因为违反了社区的传统而被排斥。

ostracizing: excluding, shunning, banishing, blacklisting
ostracizing: including, welcoming, embracing, accepting
ostracizing: verb
