indictable:/ɪnˈdaɪtəbl/| adj.|可起诉的，可被控告的|Subject to being formally accused of a crime in a court of law.  Relating to a crime serious enough to be tried in a higher court.|The offense is indictable in the Crown Court. 这项罪行可在刑事法庭起诉。|Synonyms: chargeable, prosecutable, actionable|Antonyms: non-indictable, unchargeable|adjective
