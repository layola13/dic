groaningly:/ˈɡroʊnɪŋli/| adv.|呻吟地，抱怨地|In a way that expresses or suggests a groan, indicating pain, annoyance, or displeasure.|He groaningly lifted the heavy box. 他呻吟着抬起了沉重的箱子。|近义词：complainingly, grumblingly, reluctantly|反义词：willingly, enthusiastically|词性:副词
Etymology: From the verb "groan" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Groan" comes from Old English "granian."
