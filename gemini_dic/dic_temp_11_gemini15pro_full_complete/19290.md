biaswise:/ˈbaɪəswaɪz/
adjective
按偏差方向的；与偏差有关的
Relating to or in the direction of bias.  Specifically used in machine learning contexts to refer to operations or analyses performed separately for each bias term in a model.
例句：Biaswise gradient updates can improve training stability.  按偏差方向的梯度更新可以提高训练稳定性。

例句：They analyzed the model's performance biaswise to identify specific issues.  他们按偏差方向分析了模型的性能，以找出具体问题。
近义词：per-bias, individually for each bias
