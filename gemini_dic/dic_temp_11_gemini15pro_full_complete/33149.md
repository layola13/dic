chromatical:/krəˈmætɪkl/| adj.|半音的，色彩的|Relating to chromatic scales or music that uses notes not belonging to the diatonic scale of the key; relating to color or colors.|The composer's use of chromatic harmonies created a sense of unease. 作曲家对半音和声的使用营造了一种不安的感觉。|The painting was a riot of chromatic brilliance. 这幅画色彩斑斓，光彩夺目。|近义词：colorful, vibrant, iridescent|反义词：monochromatic, achromatic|词性: adjective
