grassiness:/ˈɡræsɪnɪs/
n.|草地茂盛,青草覆盖,草地状态|The grassiness of the field was inviting.  田野里绿草茵茵，十分诱人。|English Explanation: The state or quality of being covered with grass; the extent to which an area is covered in grass. |例句：The sheep grazed on the lush grassiness. 羊群在茂盛的草地上吃草。|近义词：verdancy, lushness, greenness|反义词：barrenness, aridity|词性：名词

Etymology: From grassy + -ness.  "grassy" comes from Old English "græsig," meaning covered with grass.  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
