nonhabitablenesses:/ˌnɒnhæbɪtəbəlˈnɛsɪz/
n.
不适宜居住性；非宜居性
The nonhabitablenesses of these planets make them unsuitable for human life. 这些行星的不适宜居住性使它们不适合人类居住。
例句：The extreme temperatures contribute to the nonhabitablenesses of the planet. 极端的温度导致了这颗行星的不适宜居住。

The nonhabitablenesses of Mars have been a challenge for scientists seeking signs of life. 火星的不宜居性一直是科学家寻找生命迹象的挑战。


近义词：uninhabitability, inhospitability
反义词：habitability, suitability for life
词性：名词(plural)



ETYMOLOGY->
From nonhabitable + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Habitable comes from the Latin habitabilis, meaning "inhabitable," from habitare "to inhabit."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
