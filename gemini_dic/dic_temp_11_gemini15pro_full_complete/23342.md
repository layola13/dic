bozo:/ˈboʊzoʊ/| n.|笨蛋，蠢货|A foolish or incompetent person.|He's such a bozo, he locked his keys in the car again.|fool, idiot, clown, dunce|genius, mastermind|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From uncertain origin, possibly from Italian *bozzo* ("lump, swelling"), implying a clumsy or oafish person.  First recorded in American English in the early 20th century, popularized by circus clown Bozo the Clown.
