emancipatory:/ɪˌmænsɪˈpeɪtəri/ | adj. | 解放的，使人获得自由的 | Relating to or promoting emancipation; setting free from legal, social, or political restrictions. |  The Emancipation Proclamation was an emancipatory document for enslaved African Americans. 《解放奴隶宣言》是非裔美国奴隶的解放文件。

The feminist movement has had many emancipatory effects on women's lives. 女权运动对女性的生活产生了许多解放性的影响。 | 近义词: liberating, freeing, releasing | 反义词: restrictive, oppressive, enslaving | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin emancipatorius, from emancipare ‘set free’.
