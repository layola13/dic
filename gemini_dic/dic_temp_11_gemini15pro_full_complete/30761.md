cessavit:/sɛˈseɪvɪt/
verb
停止；中止(尤指封建制度下因土地持有者未履行义务而丧失土地)
cessavit is a legal term, derived from the Latin "cessavit de redditu," meaning "he has ceased from the rent." It refers to a procedure whereby a lord could recover land from a tenant who had failed to perform services or pay rent for a certain period.
The lord brought a writ of cessavit against the tenant. 领主对佃户提起停止支付租金的诉讼。
Near synonyms: ceased, stopped, discontinued
Antonyms: continued, maintained, resumed
词性: verb
