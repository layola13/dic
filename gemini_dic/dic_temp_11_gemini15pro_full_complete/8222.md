anti-germanic:/ˌænti-dʒɜːˈmænɪk/ | adj. | 反日耳曼的，仇视日耳曼的 | Opposed to or hostile towards Germanic peoples, their languages, or their culture. |  The anti-Germanic sentiment was strong after the war.  战后反日耳曼情绪高涨。| synonyms: anti-Teutonic | antonyms: pro-Germanic | adjective

anti-germanic:/ˌænti-dʒɜːrˈmænɪk/ | n. | 反日耳曼主义者 | A person who is opposed to or hostile towards Germanic peoples, their languages, or their culture. (Less common usage) | He was known as a staunch anti-Germanic. 他被称为坚定的反日耳曼主义者。 | synonyms: Germanophobe | antonyms: Germanophile | noun
