eyr:/ɛər/ /ɪər/ | n. | year (in documents) |  Abbreviation used on machine-readable travel documents (like passports) to indicate the year of expiry.  It represents the year in which the document expires. | Example:  My passport's eyr is 2025. 我的护照有效期到2025年。| Example: Ensure your eyr is valid for at least six months beyond your travel dates. 确保你的护照有效期至少比你的旅行日期晚六个月。| Synonym: expiry year, expiration year | Antonym: issue year | abbreviation
