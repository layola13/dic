osteogenetic:/ˌɒstiːəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
成骨的; 生骨的
Relating to or causing the growth of bone tissue.
Osteogenetic cells are responsible for bone formation. 成骨细胞负责骨骼的形成。
Bone morphogenetic proteins (BMPs) are osteogenetic. 骨形态发生蛋白 (BMPs) 具有成骨作用。
近义词: bone-forming, osteogenic
反义词: osteoclastic
词性: adjective
