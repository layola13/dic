feminists:/ˈfɛmənɪsts/ | n. | 女权主义者 | A person who believes in the social, political, and economic equality of the sexes.  | 例句：The feminists organized a protest against gender discrimination. 女权主义者组织了一场抗议性别歧视的示威活动。| 近义词：gender equality advocates, egalitarians | 反义词: sexists | Noun
