keenly:/ˈkiːnli/| adv.|敏锐地，强烈地，热切地|In a keen manner; sharply; intensely; eagerly.  It describes the way something is done or felt with a high degree of intensity, alertness, or enthusiasm.|She listened keenly to the lecture, absorbing every word. 她专心致志地听讲座，每个字都吸收进去了。|sharply, intensely, eagerly, acutely|mildly, indifferently, apathetically|adverb
