morosities:/məˈrɒsɪtiz/
n.
郁闷，闷闷不乐，阴郁
Gloominess and sullenness of temper; sulkiness.
The persistent morosity of the old man made him difficult to be around.  这位老人的持续郁闷让他很难相处。
sullenness, gloominess, dejection, depression
cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness
noun
