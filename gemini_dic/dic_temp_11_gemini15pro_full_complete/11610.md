arm-twisting:/ˈɑːrmˌtwɪstɪŋ/
n.
施压，强迫
the action of forcing someone to do something they do not want to do
He accused the company of using arm-twisting tactics to acquire the land. 他指责该公司采用施压策略来收购土地。
pressure, coercion, intimidation
persuasion, encouragement
noun
