kara:/ˈkɑːrə/| n.| 卡拉OK，卡拉|A form of entertainment originating in Japan, in which people sing along to recorded music using a microphone and lyrics displayed on a screen.  Karaoke is a contraction of the Japanese words "karappo" (empty) and "okesutora" (orchestra).| Karaoke is very popular in Japan.  卡拉OK在日本很流行。| We went to a karaoke bar last night. 我们昨晚去了卡拉OK酒吧。| synonyms: singalong, karaoke singing | antonyms: N/A | noun
