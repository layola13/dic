intercontradictions:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkʃənz/ | n. | 内部矛盾 |  The inherent conflicts or inconsistencies within a system, idea, or argument.  It refers to contradictions that exist within a single entity rather than between separate entities. | The theory suffers from several intercontradictions that make it difficult to accept.  该理论存在一些内部矛盾，使其难以被接受。| synonyms: internal conflicts, inconsistencies, discrepancies,  inner contradictions | antonyms: consistencies, harmonies, agreements | noun
