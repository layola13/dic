chinwagging:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡɪŋ/
noun
闲聊，聊天
the act of chatting or gossiping.
They spent the afternoon chinwagging about their neighbors. 他们下午一直在闲聊邻居的事情。
近义词：chatting, gossiping, talking
反义词：silence, reticence
词性: n. (noun, gerund)

chinwag:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡ/
noun
闲聊，聊天
a friendly chat.
We had a good chinwag over a cup of tea. 我们边喝茶边愉快地聊了聊。
近义词：chat, gossip, talk
反义词：silence
词性: n. (noun)

chinwag:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡ/
verb
闲聊，聊天
to chat or gossip.
They were chinwagging away in the corner. 他们在角落里聊得热火朝天。
近义词：chat, gossip, talk
反义词：be silent
词性: v. (verb)

词源：
Chinwag is believed to have originated in British slang in the late 19th century. It's thought to be imitative, suggesting the movement of the jaw during talking.  "Chin" refers to the jaw, and "wag" implies movement back and forth.
