insurant:/ɪnˈʃʊərənt/ | n. | 投保人；被保险人 | A person who takes out or is covered by an insurance policy. | Example 1: The insurant paid his premiums on time.  投保人按时支付了他的保费。

Example 2: The insurant was relieved that the damage was covered by the policy. 被保险人感到欣慰的是，损失在保单的承保范围内。 | Synonym: insured, policyholder | Antonym: insurer, underwriter | noun
