ambulance-chasing:/ˈæmbjələns ˌtʃeɪsɪŋ/
| n. | 救护车追逐；唯利是图的律师行为 |The practice of lawyers or their agents soliciting business from accident victims at the scene of an accident or shortly thereafter.|  The lawyer was accused of ambulance-chasing after he showed up at the hospital to solicit clients.  这个律师在医院出现招揽客户后，被指控追逐救护车。| 近义词：soliciting, touting | 反义词: ethical legal practice | 词性: 名词
