dependently:/dɪˈpɛndəntli/| adv.|依赖地，依靠地|In a dependent manner; relying on someone or something else for support or direction.|He lived dependently on his parents until he was 25.  他25岁之前一直依赖父母生活。|近义词：reliantly, contingently|反义词：independently, autonomously|词性:副词
Etymology: From the adjective "dependent" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Dependent" comes from the Latin "dependens," present participle of "dependere" (to hang down, to depend on).
