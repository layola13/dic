karate: /kəˈrɑːti/ /kəˈræti/ | n. | 空手道 | A Japanese martial art whose basic forms are kata (set routines of offensive and defensive postures) and kumite (sparring). |  Example: He has a black belt in karate. 他有空手道黑带。

Example:  She's been practicing karate for five years. 她练习空手道已经五年了。 | 近义词: martial arts, self-defense | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps pacifism) | 词性: noun
