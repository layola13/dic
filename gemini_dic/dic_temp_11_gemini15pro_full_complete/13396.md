atomicities:/ˌætəˈmɪsətiz/| n. |原子性（复数）|The quality of being atomic; indivisibility.  It often refers to operations in computer science that are guaranteed to either complete fully or not at all, preserving data integrity.|Database transactions require atomicity to ensure data consistency.|近义词：indivisibility, unity, wholeness|反义词：divisibility, separability|词性:noun
