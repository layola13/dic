"goen" is not a standard English word.  It's likely a misspelling of "gone," the past participle of the verb "go."  Therefore, the following analysis is for "gone":

gone:/ɡɒn/ (UK), /ɡɔːn/ (US)| adj.| 离开的，过去的，用完的，丢失的| having departed; no longer present; used up; lost| The opportunity is gone. 机会已经失去了。|近义词：departed, past, finished, lost, consumed|反义词:present, remaining, here, extant

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The milk is gone.  牛奶没有了。
My patience is gone. 我的耐心用完了。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
My money is going fast, it'll be gone soon. 我的钱花得很快，很快就会用完的。
The sun is going down, the light will be gone soon. 太阳正在下山，光线很快就会消失。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has gone to the store. 他去商店了。
The pain has gone away. 疼痛消失了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The food has been going fast, it will be gone soon. 食物消耗得很快，很快就会没有了。
He has been going through a tough time, his smile is gone. 他一直在经历一段艰难的时期，他的笑容消失了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He went to the store yesterday. 他昨天去了商店。
The money was gone when I checked my wallet. 当我检查钱包时，钱已经不见了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was going to the store when I realized my wallet was gone. 我正要去商店时，意识到我的钱包不见了。
He was going through a difficult period when his father was gone. 他父亲去世时，他正经历一段艰难的时期。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had gone to the store before I arrived. 在我到达之前，他已经去了商店。
The money had gone missing before I even noticed. 我还没注意到的时候钱就已经不见了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been going to that store for years before it was gone.  在他离开之前，他已经去那家商店很多年了。
She had been going through a rough patch before her troubles were gone. 在她的麻烦消失之前，她一直在经历一段艰难的时期。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will be gone by tomorrow. 明天他就走了。
The food will be gone if we don't eat it soon. 如果我们不赶紧吃，食物就没了。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He will be going home when the party is gone. 派对结束后他将回家。
She will be going through her old photos when the memories are gone. 当记忆消失时，她将翻阅她的旧照片。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, he will have gone to London. 到下周，他将已经去了伦敦。
By the time you arrive, the cake will have been gone. 等你到的时候，蛋糕就没了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, he will have been going to therapy for two years. 到明年，他将已经接受了两年的治疗。
By the time the movie ends, we will have been gone from home for three hours.  电影结束时，我们将已经离开家三个小时了。

虚拟语气:
If I were gone, who would take care of the dog? 如果我不在了，谁来照顾狗？
I wish he were gone. 我希望他走了。

被动语态:
The money was gone. 钱不见了。(这里"gone" 其实是被动意义, 但没有更直接的被动语态表达)
All the cookies were gone. 所有饼干都没了。


疑问句:
Is the milk gone? 牛奶没有了吗？
Are they gone? 他们走了吗？

否定句:
The milk isn't gone yet. 牛奶还没喝完。
They aren't gone yet. 他们还没走。



PHRASE->
be gone = 离开了，消失了
long gone = 很久以前就离开了/消失了
gone for good = 永远地离开了
gone with the wind = 随风而逝
have gone through = 经历过


注意事项:  "gone" 虽然是"go"的过去分词，但它主要用作形容词。
