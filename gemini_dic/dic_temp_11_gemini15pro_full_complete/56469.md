dolphinet:/ˈdɒlfɪnɪt/ |n.|海豚鱼 (古语,现已废弃)|A small dolphin (obsolete). |He saw a school of dolphinet swimming near the shore. (他看到一群海豚鱼在岸边游动。) (例句已按古义造句，现代英语已不用此词)| 近义词: dolphin | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English *dolfinet, a diminutive of dolfin (“dolphin”), from Old French dolfin, from Medieval Latin dolfinus, from Latin delphinus, from Ancient Greek δελφίς (delphís, “dolphin”).
