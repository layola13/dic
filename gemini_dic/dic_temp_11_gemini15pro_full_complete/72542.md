flutelike:/ˈfluːtlaɪk/
adjective
像长笛的，笛声似的
Resembling the sound or shape of a flute.
Her voice was high and flutelike. 她的声音又高又像笛声。
The bird's song was flutelike. 这只鸟的歌声像笛声。
近义词:melodious, mellifluous, silvery
反义词:raucous, harsh, grating
词性:adjective


词源:
From flute +‎ -like.
