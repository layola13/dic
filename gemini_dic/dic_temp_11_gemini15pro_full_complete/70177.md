fibriform:/ˈfaɪbrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
纤维状的；纤维形的
Resembling or composed of fibers; fibrous.
The fungus had a fibriform appearance.  这种真菌呈纤维状外观。
The plant's roots were fibriform.  这种植物的根是纤维状的。
近义词: fibrous, filamentous, threadlike
反义词: compact, solid, dense
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *fibra* (fiber) + *-formis* (form-shaped).
