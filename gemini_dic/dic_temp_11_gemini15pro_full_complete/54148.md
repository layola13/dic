disenjoy:/dɪsɪnˈdʒɔɪ/| v. |不喜欢，不享受|To not enjoy; dislike.| I disenjoyed the movie, it was too long. 我不喜欢这部电影，它太长了。|近义词：dislike, hate, detest|反义词:enjoy, like, love|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + enjoy.  The prefix "dis-" comes from Latin and means "apart," "asunder," "away," "utterly," or having a privative, negative, or reversing force. "Enjoy" comes from Old French "enjoir" meaning "to give joy to, delight, please."
