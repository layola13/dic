glitching:/ˈɡlɪtʃɪŋ/
n.|小故障，失灵|A malfunction or error in a system, typically electronic.  A temporary malfunction or irregularity.|The computer suffered a glitch and lost all my data. 电脑出现故障，我所有的数据都丢失了。|近义词：malfunction, bug, error, fault|反义词:smooth operation, perfection|词性:名词

glitching:/ˈɡlɪtʃɪŋ/
v.|发生小故障，失灵|(of a system, typically electronic) to malfunction or experience a temporary irregularity.|The screen kept glitching, making it difficult to watch the video. 屏幕一直闪烁，很难观看视频。|近义词：malfunctioning, bugging, erring|反义词:working properly, functioning smoothly|词性:动词
