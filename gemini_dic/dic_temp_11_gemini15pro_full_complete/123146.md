monitorship:/ˈmɒnɪtəʃɪp/ (US) /ˈmɒnɪtəʃɪp/ (UK)| n. |监督；监视（职位或时期）；班长职位|The position or period of time during which someone monitors something or someone.  The position of being a monitor (e.g., in a school).|She held the monitorship of the program for three years.|Synonyms: supervision, oversight, surveillance, observation, scrutiny|Antonyms: neglect, disregard|Noun
