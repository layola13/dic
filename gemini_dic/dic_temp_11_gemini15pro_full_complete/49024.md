degree: /dɪˈɡriː/ /dəˈɡriː/ | n. | 度，等级，学位 | a unit for measuring temperature or the size of an angle; a level in a scale of how serious something is; a qualification given to students who successfully complete a university course |  She has a degree in economics. 她拥有经济学学位。|  The temperature reached 30 degrees Celsius. 温度达到了30摄氏度。| synonyms: level, rank, qualification, extent | antonyms: insignificance, unimportance | noun
