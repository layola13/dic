darkens:/ˈdɑːrkənz/ | v. |(使)变暗，(使)变黑 | Makes something less light or more dark. | The sky darkens as the storm approaches.  天空随着暴风雨的临近而变暗。| synonyms: dims, shadows, obscures | antonyms: brightens, lightens | verb

darkens:/ˈdɑːr.kənz/ | v. | (使)变得阴沉，(使)变得忧郁 | Makes something more gloomy or depressing. | His mood darkens with the bad news. 他的心情随着坏消息而变得阴沉。| synonyms: saddens, worsens, deteriorates | antonyms: cheers, brightens, improves | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *deorcnian "to grow dark," from deorc "dark."
