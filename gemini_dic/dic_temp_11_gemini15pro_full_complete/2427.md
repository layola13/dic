adjudger:/əˈdʒʌdʒər/
noun
裁决者，评判员
A person who formally judges and decides a disputed issue.
The adjudger listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making a decision.  裁决者在作出决定前仔细听取了双方的论点。
The adjudger awarded the prize to the young artist. 评判员把奖项颁给了这位年轻的艺术家。
Synonyms: judge, arbiter, referee, umpire
Antonyms: defendant, plaintiff
