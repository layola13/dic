orthodoxality:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksəlɪti/
n.
正统性，正统观念
The quality or state of being orthodox; adherence to established doctrines, especially in religion.
The orthodoxality of his views was questioned by the reformists. 他的观点的正统性受到了改革派的质疑。
Near Synonyms: orthodoxy, conventionality, traditionalism
Antonyms: heterodoxy, unorthodoxy, unconventionality
