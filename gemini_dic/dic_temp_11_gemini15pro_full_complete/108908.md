ledgier:/ˈlɛdʒɪər/
adjective
多岩架的; 多暗礁的
Having many ledges.  A ledgier coastline is dangerous for ships.
The route became increasingly ledgier and difficult to traverse. 路线变得越来越多岩架，难以通行。
近义词: rocky, craggy, cliffy
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective
词源: From ledge + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ledge itself comes from Old North French *legiere.
