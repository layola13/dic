doubtfulness:/ˈdaʊtfəlnəs/
n.|怀疑；可疑性|The doubtfulness of the evidence led to the case being dismissed. 证据的可疑性导致案件被驳回。|
英文解析:The state or quality of being doubtful; uncertainty.|
例句：The doubtfulness of his story made me hesitate. 他的故事的可疑性让我犹豫了。
例句：There is considerable doubtfulness surrounding the project's success.  这个项目的成功存在相当大的不确定性。

近义词：uncertainty, dubiousness, questionableness
反义词：certainty, sureness, confidence
词性:noun
