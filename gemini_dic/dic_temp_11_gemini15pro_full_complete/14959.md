B-picture:/ˈbiː ˈpɪktʃər/ |n.|B级片，低成本电影|A low-budget, second-feature film, often the second movie in a double feature.  These films were often characterized by genre themes, exploitation, and lesser-known actors.|Example:  Many classic horror films started out as B-pictures. 许多经典恐怖电影最初都是B级片。

Example: The studio churned out B-pictures to fill the bottom half of double bills. 电影制片厂大量制作B级片，以填补双片连映的下半场。|近义词:low-budget film, exploitation film, cheapie, programmer|反义词:A-picture, blockbuster|词性:名词
