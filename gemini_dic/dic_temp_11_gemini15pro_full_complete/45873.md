cutchery: /kʌtʃəri/ | n. |法庭，审判室，县政府; (印度)县政府所在地|A place where legal cases are heard, especially in India.  Often also refers to the administrative offices of a district. |The cutchery was crowded with people seeking justice.  县政府挤满了寻求正义的人们。| The land dispute was settled in the local cutchery. 土地纠纷在当地县政府得到解决。 | synonyms: court, courthouse, tribunal, district office | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "outdoors," "field," signifying a place *not* for legal proceedings) | noun
