macking:/ˈmækɪŋ/| v. |调情，勾搭；闲逛（尤指无所事事）|To flirt, hit on, or chat someone up; also, to hang out or loiter, especially aimlessly. |He was macking on every girl at the party. 他在派对上和每个女孩调情。|近义词：flirting, chatting up, cruising, hanging out|反义词：ignoring, shunning|词性：动词

macking:/ˈmækɪŋ/| n. |调情，勾搭|The act of flirting or hitting on someone. |His constant macking was starting to annoy her. 他不停地勾搭让她开始感到厌烦。|近义词：flirting, seduction|反义词：rejection|词性：名词


词源:  Likely derived from the pimp or "mack" who controls prostitutes, implying a manipulative or controlling aspect to the flirting.  The sense of hanging out may have developed later, possibly influenced by the image of a pimp idly observing the street.
