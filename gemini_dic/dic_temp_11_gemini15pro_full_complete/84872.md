hanging-out:/ˈhæŋɪŋ aʊt/| n.|闲逛，出去玩|The act of spending time relaxing or socializing with friends, typically in an informal setting.|例句：We spent the afternoon hanging out at the mall. 我们下午在商场闲逛。

例句：They enjoy hanging out at the beach on weekends.  他们喜欢周末去海滩玩。|近义词：relaxing, chilling, socializing, spending time, kicking back|反义词:working, studying|词性:名词 (gerund)
