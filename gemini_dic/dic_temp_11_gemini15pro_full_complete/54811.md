dispersable:/dɪˈspɜːrsəbl/
adjective
可分散的；可驱散的；可消散的
Capable of being dispersed or scattered.
The powder is dispersable in water. 这种粉末可分散在水中。
The seeds are light and dispersable by the wind. 这些种子很轻，可以随风飘散。
近义词: scatterable, diffusible, distributable
反义词: non-dispersable, cohesive, concentrated
词性: adjective


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
verb
分散；驱散；消散
To distribute or spread over a wide area.
The police dispersed the crowd. 警察驱散了人群。
The wind dispersed the clouds. 风吹散了云朵。
近义词: scatter, disseminate, distribute
反义词: gather, collect, concentrate
词性: verb


dispersal:/dɪˈspɜːrsl/
noun
分散；驱散；消散
The action or process of distributing or spreading things or people over a wide area.
The dispersal of the seeds is essential for the plant's survival.  种子的散播对植物的生存至关重要。
The dispersal of the crowd was peaceful. 人群的疏散是和平的。
近义词: scattering, dissemination, distribution
反义词: gathering, collection, concentration
词性: noun
