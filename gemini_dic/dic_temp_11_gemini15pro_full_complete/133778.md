nongeminates:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɛmɪneɪts/ | n. |非重叠音，非双元音|  Sounds or letters that are not doubled or lengthened.  Refers to consonants that are pronounced with a single, short articulation, unlike geminates, which are held for a longer duration. | The word "better" contains two nongeminates, /t/ and /r/.  "Better"这个词包含两个非重叠音，/t/ 和 /r/。 | synonyms: singletons (less common) | antonyms: geminates | noun
