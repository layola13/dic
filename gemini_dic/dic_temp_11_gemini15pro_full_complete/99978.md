interosseous:/ˌɪntərˈɒsiəs/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈɒsiəs/ (UK)| adj.|骨间的；骨间的|Situated between bones; connecting bones.|The interosseous membrane connects the radius and ulna bones in the forearm.|近义词:intervening, connecting|反义词:superficial, external|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *interosseus*, from *inter-* (between) + *osseus* (bony), from *os* (bone).
