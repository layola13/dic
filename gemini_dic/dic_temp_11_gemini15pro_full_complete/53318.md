disabusal:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːzl/
noun
觉悟；使醒悟；纠正
The act of freeing someone from a misconception or deception.
He experienced a rude disabusal when he discovered the truth about his idol.  他发现偶像的真相后，经历了一次残酷的清醒。
synonyms: disillusionment, enlightenment, awakening, revelation
antonyms: deception, illusion, delusion
