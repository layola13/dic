hieroglyph:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪf/ | n. | 象形文字 |A picture or symbol used in hieroglyphic writing, representing a word, syllable, or sound.| The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphs. 罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。| synonym: ideogram, symbol | antonym: letter, alphabet | noun

hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的 | Relating to or written in hieroglyphs.|  Hieroglyphic writing is a complex system. 象形文字是一种复杂的系统。| synonym: symbolic, pictorial | antonym: alphabetic | adjective
