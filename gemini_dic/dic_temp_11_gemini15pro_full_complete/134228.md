nonindustriousness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs/
noun
不勤奋，懒惰
lack of diligence or industry; laziness.
His nonindustriousness led to his failure in the exam.  他的懒惰导致他考试不及格。
Synonyms: laziness, indolence, idleness, inactivity, sloth, shiftlessness
Antonyms: industriousness, diligence, assiduity, activity, energy
