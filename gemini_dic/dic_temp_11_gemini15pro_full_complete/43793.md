cremocarp: /ˈkrɛməʊkɑːrp/ | n. | 双悬果 | A dry fruit that splits into two one-seeded segments when ripe, characteristic of the parsley family (Umbelliferae/Apiaceae). | The fennel plant produces a cremocarp. |茴香植物会结出双悬果。| mericarp, schizocarp |  None | noun

Etymology:
From Greek *kréma* (κρέμα, "something suspended") + *karpós* (καρπός, "fruit").
