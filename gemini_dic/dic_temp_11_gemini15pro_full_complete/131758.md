noncongruent:/ˌnɒnkənˈɡruːənt/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈɡruːənt/| adj.|不全等的，不一致的|Not congruent; not having the same size and shape (in geometry) or not matching or agreeing (in other contexts).|The two triangles are noncongruent because their corresponding sides have different lengths.|近义词：dissimilar, different, incompatible, inconsistent|反义词: congruent, identical, similar|词性:形容词

noncongruent:/ˌnɒnkənˈɡruːənt/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈɡruːənt/| n.|不全等的事物|Something that is not congruent.|The architect pointed out several noncongruents in the blueprint.|近义词：discrepancy, disparity, variance|反义词: congruence, agreement, correspondence|词性:名词
