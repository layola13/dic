isometrics:/ˌaɪsəˈmetrɪks/ | n. | 等长运动，等容运动 | Exercises in which muscles contract but do not change length, such as holding a weight in a steady position. | Example: Isometrics are a good way to build strength without putting too much stress on your joints. 等长运动是一种很好的锻炼方式，可以在不给关节带来太大压力的情况下增强力量。 | Example:  She incorporated isometrics into her workout routine. 她将等长运动纳入了她的日常锻炼中。 | Synonym: static exercise, resistance training | Antonym: isotonic exercise, dynamic exercise | noun


isometrical:/ˌaɪsəˈmetrɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 等距的，等量的|  Relating to or denoting muscular action in which tension is developed without contraction of the muscle. | Example: Isometrical exercises can be helpful for rehabilitation. 等距运动有助于康复。 | Example: He used an isometric projection to draw the cube. 他使用等轴投影来绘制立方体。 | Synonym: equilateral, equidimensional | Antonym: anisometric | adjective
