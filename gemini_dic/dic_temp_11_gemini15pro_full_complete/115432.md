mantling:/ˈmæntlɪŋ/| n. v.|覆盖，披风，（鸟类）张开翅膀炫耀，（液体）覆盖表面，（脸部）泛起（如红晕）|The act of covering something, as with a mantle; something that covers or envelops; in birds, a display of spread wings and tail; the spreading of a liquid over a surface; a suffusion of the face (as with a blush).|
例句：
The snow mantling the trees created a beautiful winter scene.
白雪覆盖着树木，构成了一幅美丽的冬日景象。


例句：
The hawk was mantling its prey.
那只鹰张开翅膀炫耀它的猎物。


近义词：covering, enveloping, cloaking, draping, shrouding, suffusing|
反义词：uncovering, exposing, revealing|
词性：noun, verb|


mantle:/ˈmæntl/| n. v.|斗篷，覆盖物，地幔|A loose sleeveless garment worn over other clothes; something that covers, envelops, or conceals something.|
例句：
She wore a mantle of green velvet.
她穿着一件绿色天鹅绒的斗篷。


例句：
A mantle of snow covered the ground.
一层雪覆盖了地面。


近义词：cloak, cape, shawl, covering, layer|
反义词：uncovering, exposing|
词性：noun, verb|

词源：
From Old French *mantel (modern French manteau), from Late Latin mantum, mantus “cloak, short cape”.
