词源：Auburn comes from the Old French word *alborne*, meaning "whitish, blonde."  This word is related to the Latin *albus*, also meaning "white." The meaning shifted over time, influenced by the word *brun*, meaning "brown."  The current meaning, reddish-brown, emerged in the 16th century.
