v. | surpass in fame; outdo in renown | 超过…的名声；声誉胜过 |  He outfamed all his contemporaries. 他比所有同時代的人都出名。 | 近义词：outshine, eclipse, surpass | 反义词：obscure, overshadow | 词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "fame" (noun).  The verb form is derived by adding "-ed" to the past participle of "fame".
