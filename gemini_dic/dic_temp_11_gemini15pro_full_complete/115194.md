manicured:/ˈmænɪkjʊərd/| adj.|修剪整齐的，精心修饰的|Having nails that are cared for and polished; generally well cared for and meticulously maintained.  Refers to something (usually lawns, gardens, or nails) that has been neatly trimmed and groomed. |例句：She had perfectly manicured nails. 她有着完美修剪的指甲。

例句：The golf course was manicured and pristine. 高尔夫球场修剪得整整齐齐，一尘不染。|近义词：groomed, trimmed, neat, tidy, polished|反义词: unkempt, overgrown, neglected|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the past participle of *manicure*, which comes from French *manucure*, from Latin *manus* (hand) + *cura* (care).
