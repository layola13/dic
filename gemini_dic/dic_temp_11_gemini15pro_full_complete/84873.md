hangingly:/ˈhæŋɪŋli/ | adv. | 悬挂地，垂悬地 | in a hanging manner; so as to hang down | The vines hung hangingly from the pergola. 藤蔓垂悬在凉棚上。| 近义词: droopingly, pendulously | 反义词: uprightly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "hanging" + "-ly".  "Hanging" comes from the Old English "hangian," meaning "to hang."  The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
