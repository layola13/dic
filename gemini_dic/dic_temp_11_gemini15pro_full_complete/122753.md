moistures:/ˈmɔɪstʃərz/
n.
水分，湿气
Water or other liquid diffused in a small quantity as vapor, within a solid, or condensed on a surface.
The morning dew left moistures on the grass. 早晨的露水在草地上留下了湿气。
近义词: dampness, humidity, wetness
反义词: dryness, aridity
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *moistour, from Anglo-Norman *moisteur, from Old French moisteor, from moiste (“moist”), from Vulgar Latin *musteus, from Latin musteus (“new, fresh, young”), from mustum (“must, new wine”).  The plural form is regular.
