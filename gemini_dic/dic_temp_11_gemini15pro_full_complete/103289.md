jibbas:/ˈdʒɪbəz/| n.| 吉布斯（一种技巧，尤指在滑板运动中）| A jib is a skateboarding trick performed on a non-skating obstacle, such as a rail or ledge.  It involves grinding or sliding along the obstacle. | He landed a sick jib on that handrail. 他在那个扶手上做了一个很酷的吉布斯动作。 |近义词：grind, slide, trick|反义词: - | noun
