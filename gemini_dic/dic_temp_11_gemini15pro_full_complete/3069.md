aerological:/ˌɛərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
高空气象学的
Relating to the study of the atmosphere, especially its higher regions.
Aerological studies are crucial for understanding weather patterns.  高空气象学研究对于理解天气模式至关重要。
The aerological balloon carried instruments to measure temperature and humidity.  高空气象气球携带仪器测量温度和湿度。
近义词: atmospheric, meteorological
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective
词源: From aero- (relating to air) + -logical (relating to the study of).  源自 aero-（与空气有关）+ -logical（与研究有关）。
