harped-on:/ˈhɑːrpt ɑːn/ | adj. | 反复提及的，唠叨的 |  Constantly talked about or mentioned, often to the point of annoyance.  Derived from the verb "harp on," meaning to dwell on tediously. The image is of someone repeatedly plucking the same string on a harp, creating a monotonous and irritating sound. | He's always harped-on about his health problems. 他总是反复唠叨他的健康问题。|  She gets really annoyed when people harp-on about her age. 当人们反复提及她的年龄时，她真的很生气。 | synonyms: dwelled on, overemphasized, reiterated, belabored | antonyms: ignored, neglected, overlooked | adjective
