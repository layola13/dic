commemorators:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪtərz/
n.
纪念者，庆祝者
People who commemorate something; those who participate in a ceremony of remembrance.
The commemorators gathered at the monument to pay their respects. 纪念者们聚集在纪念碑前表达敬意。
Thousands of commemorators attended the Remembrance Day service. 数千名纪念者参加了阵亡将士纪念日仪式。
近义词： celebrants, observers, participants, attendees
反义词： protestors, objectors
