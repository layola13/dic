halysites:/ˈhælɪˌsaɪts/| n. |链珊瑚|An extinct genus of tabulate coral characterized by interconnected corallites forming chain-like or net-like patterns.  These corals lived from the Ordovician to the Silurian periods.|The museum displayed a beautiful fossil of Halysites.  博物馆展出了一块美丽的链珊瑚化石。|Synonym: chain coral|Antonym: N/A|noun
