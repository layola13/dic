maintainer:/meɪnˈteɪnər/ | n. |维护者，保养者|A person or organization that maintains something, such as equipment, software, or a website. | The software maintainer released a new version with bug fixes.  该软件维护者发布了一个包含错误修复的新版本。| Synonyms: custodian, keeper, conservator, upholder | Antonyms: destroyer, demolisher | noun

maintainer:/meɪnˈteɪnər/ | n. |支持者，维持者|A person who provides support, especially financial support, for someone or something.  |  He is the maintainer of his family. 他是他家的支柱。 | Synonyms: supporter, provider, backer | Antonyms: opponent, adversary | noun
