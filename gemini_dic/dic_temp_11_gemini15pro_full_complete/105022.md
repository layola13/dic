kenyanize:/ˌkɛnɪənaɪz/
verb
肯尼亚化
To make Kenyan in character; to adapt to Kenyan culture or conditions.
The government is trying to Kenyanize the education system. 政府正在努力使教育系统肯尼亚化。
The company Kenyanized its management team.  公司将其管理团队肯尼亚化了。
