bombazette:/ˌbɒmbəˈzɛt/ /ˌbɑːmbəˈzɛt/ | n. |轻薄柔软的棉织物; 薄软织物 |A thin, soft cotton fabric; a fine, soft material.|She wore a dress made of bombazette. 她穿着一件薄软织物做的连衣裙。|例句：The curtains were made of a delicate bombazette. 窗帘是用一种精致的薄软织物制成的。|近义词：batiste, muslin, voile, gossamer|反义词:canvas, denim|词性:名词

Etymology: From French <i>bombasin</i>, from Italian <i>bambagia</i>, from Late Latin <i>bombax</i>, from Greek <i>bombyx</i>, meaning "silk" or "silkworm".  The suffix "-ette" indicates a smaller or finer version.
