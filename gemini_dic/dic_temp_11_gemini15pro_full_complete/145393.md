overcaustic:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːstɪk/
adjective
过于刻薄的；过分尖刻的
Excessively sarcastic or critical; too harsh.
Her overcaustic remarks alienated many of her friends. 她过于刻薄的言辞疏远了许多朋友。
His overcaustic wit made him unpopular. 他过分尖刻的才智使他不受欢迎。
近义词:acerbic, biting, sardonic, scathing, harsh, vitriolic
反义词:kind, gentle, mild, complimentary
词性:adjective
