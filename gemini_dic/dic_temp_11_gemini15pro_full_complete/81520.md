gravimetric:/ˌɡrævɪˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|重量分析的，重量的|Relating to or determined by weight; based on the measurement of weight.|The gravimetric analysis determined the amount of iron in the sample. 重量分析法测定了样品中铁的含量。|Near synonyms: ponderal, quantitative|Antonym: volumetric|adjective
