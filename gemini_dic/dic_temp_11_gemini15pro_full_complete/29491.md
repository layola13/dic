catapult:/ˈkætəpʌlt/ /ˈkætəpʊlt/| v. n.|投射，弹射； catapult: 弹弓,投石器|The novel catapulted her to fame. 这部小说使她一举成名。|近义词：hurl, launch, propel, project|反义词: restrain, hold back|词性:动词,名词

Etymology: From Old French *catapulte, from Latin catapulta, from Greek katapeltēs, from kata- ‘down’ + pellō ‘I drive, I hurl’.
