cerebellum:/ˌserəˈbeləm/| n. |小脑|The part of the brain at the back of the skull in vertebrates. Its function is to coordinate and regulate muscular activity.|The cerebellum plays a crucial role in motor control. 小脑在运动控制中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: hindbrain |Antonyms: cerebrum|noun
