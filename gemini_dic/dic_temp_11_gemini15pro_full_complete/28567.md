care-for:/ˈker fɔːr/ /ˈker fɔːr/| v.|照顾；喜欢；想要|To look after someone or something; to like or want something.|She cared for her elderly mother for many years. 她多年来照顾着她年迈的母亲。|近义词:look after, tend, like, fancy|反义词:neglect, dislike, hate|词性:动词

care-for:/ˈker fɔːr/ /ˈker fɔːr/| n.|照顾；喜爱|The act of looking after someone or something; fondness or liking for someone or something. |The children were in the care of their grandparents. 孩子们由他们的祖父母照顾。|近义词:charge, custody, fondness, liking|反义词:neglect, dislike, hatred|词性:名词
