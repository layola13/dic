lapland: /ˈlæplənd/ | n. | 拉普兰（北欧地区）| A region in northern Europe, largely within the Arctic Circle, traditionally inhabited by the Sami people. It includes parts of Norway, Sweden, Finland, and Russia. |  Many reindeer are herded in Lapland.  许多驯鹿在拉普兰放牧。| synonyms: Sápmi | antonyms: N/A | noun
