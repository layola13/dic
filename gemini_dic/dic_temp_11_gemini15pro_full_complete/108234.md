laughing-at:/ˈlɑːfɪŋ æt/ |prepositional phrase, adjective|嘲笑，因…而发笑|The act of finding amusement or ridicule in something or someone.  It describes the action of laughing directed at a particular target.|例句：They were laughing at his clumsy attempt to dance. 他们嘲笑他笨拙的舞步。|近义词：mocking, ridiculing, deriding|反义词：admiring, respecting, praising|phrase
