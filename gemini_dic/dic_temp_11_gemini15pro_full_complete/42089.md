cotransduces:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈdjuːsɪz/
|v.|共同转导|To transfer genetic material from one bacterial strain to another simultaneously with other genetic material, often using a bacteriophage as a vector.|The bacteriophage cotransduces the genes for antibiotic resistance and lactose fermentation.|近义词: cotransfer|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "prevent transduction")|verb
