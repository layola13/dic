疑问句:
Does he like pizza? 他喜欢披萨吗？
Does she speak French? 她会说法语吗？

否定句:
He does not like spinach. 他不喜欢菠菜。
She does not work here anymore. 她已经不在这里工作了.

虚拟语气:
If he did his homework, he would get better grades. 如果他做作业，他的成绩会更好。(Note: This uses "did," the past tense of "do.")
I wish she did more to help. 我希望她能多帮帮忙。(Note: This uses "did," the past tense of "do.")

被动语态:
(Does is not used in passive voice.  The passive voice of "do" is "is/are/was/were done.")
The work is done by him. 这工作是他做的。
The dishes were done after dinner. 晚饭后洗了碗。
