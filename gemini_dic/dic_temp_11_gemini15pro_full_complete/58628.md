dumb:/dʌm/
adj.|愚蠢的，哑的，沉默的|lacking the power of speech; mute; unable or unwilling to speak; temporarily speechless, as from shock; stupid or senseless|例句：He was born dumb. 他生来就是哑巴。|近义词：stupid, foolish, mute, silent|反义词：intelligent, smart, articulate, talkative
词性: 形容词

dumb:/dʌm/
vt.|使沉默，使安静|to make silent or dumb; to make quiet; to still|例句：The news dumbed him. 这消息使他哑口无言。|近义词：silence, quiet, still|反义词：enliven, arouse, excite
词性: 动词
