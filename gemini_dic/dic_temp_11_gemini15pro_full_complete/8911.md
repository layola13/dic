antigraph:/ˈæntɪɡræf/| n. |反图|A graph where any two vertices connected by an edge do not share a common neighbor.|The Kneser graph KG(5,2) is an example of an antigraph.  克内泽尔图 KG(5,2) 是一个反图的例子。|Near Antonym: graph |Antonym: complete graph|noun
Etymology-> From Ancient Greek ἀντί (antí, “against, opposite”) + graph.
