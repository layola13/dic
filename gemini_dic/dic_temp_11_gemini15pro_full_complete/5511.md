alpestrine:/ælˈpɛstraɪn/
adjective
高山生长的；高山的
Growing or thriving in high mountains, above the timberline.
The alpestrine flowers were a vibrant splash of color against the gray rocks.  高山上的花朵在灰色的岩石映衬下，色彩鲜艳。
Near synonyms: alpine, montane, high-altitude
Antonyms: lowland, valley
