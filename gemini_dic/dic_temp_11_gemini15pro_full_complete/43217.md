crackaloo:/krækəˈluː/
n.
疯狂的行为；古怪的事件；疯狂的人；古怪的人
A crazy or eccentric person or thing; a wild or outlandish event or action.
He's a real crackaloo, always up to something strange. 他真是个怪人，总是做些奇怪的事情。
The party was a real crackaloo, with people dancing on tables and singing at the top of their lungs.  派对真是疯狂，人们在桌子上跳舞，放声高歌。
synonyms: nut, kook, eccentric, oddball, weirdo, character, card, wacko, crackpot
antonyms: normal person, conventional person, conformist
noun
