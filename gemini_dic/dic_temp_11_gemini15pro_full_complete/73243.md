fordo:/fɔːrˈduː/| vt.|毁坏，放弃，毁灭|To utterly ruin or destroy; to abandon or give up (something).|He fordoed his chances of promotion by being constantly late. 他因经常迟到而毁掉了自己的晋升机会。|例句：Stress and lack of sleep fordoed her health.  压力和睡眠不足毁掉了她的健康。|近义词：destroy, ruin, wreck, devastate|反义词：build, create, restore, repair|词性:动词
