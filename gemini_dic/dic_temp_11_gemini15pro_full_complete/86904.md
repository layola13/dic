hemitropous:/ˌhɛmɪˈtrɒpəs/
adjective
半倒生的，半转生的
(of a plant ovule) having the body of the ovule inverted so that the micropyle lies close to the funiculus.
例句：The hemitropous ovule is a common type in flowering plants. 半倒生胚珠是被子植物中常见的一种类型。

例句：The orchid has a hemitropous ovule.  这株兰花有半倒生胚珠。

近义词：campylotropous (slightly different orientation)
反义词：orthotropous, anatropous
词性：adjective
