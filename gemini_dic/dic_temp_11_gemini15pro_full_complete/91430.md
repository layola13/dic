hygeists:/ˈhaɪdʒiɪsts/
n.
卫生学家；保健专家
Experts in hygiene and public health.
Hygeists work to promote public health and prevent disease. 卫生学家致力于促进公共卫生和预防疾病。
The hygeists inspected the restaurant's kitchen. 卫生学家检查了餐厅的厨房。
近义词：sanitarians, public health officials
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) disease promoters
