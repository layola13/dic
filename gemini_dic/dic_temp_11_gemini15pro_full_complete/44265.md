croma:/ˈkrəʊmə/ (UK) /ˈkroʊmə/ (US)| n.| 色度，色调，音符|A term used in music, particularly in solfège systems, to refer to a specific note, often corresponding to middle C. It is also used in certain contexts to refer to the quality of color, especially its saturation or purity.|In the movable-do solfège system, "croma" is always do.  |近义词：tone, note, hue, chroma, saturation|反义词: achromatic |noun
