jailhouses:/ˈdʒeɪlˌhaʊzɪz/
noun
监狱（jailhouse的复数）
places of confinement for those accused or convicted of crimes.
He spent many years in various jailhouses across the state. 他在该州各地的监狱里度过了许多年。
synonyms: prisons, penitentiaries, correctional facilities
antonyms: freedom, liberty
