overrulers:/ˈəʊvərˌruːlərz/ | n. |统治者，支配者|Those who rule over others, often in a tyrannical or oppressive way.  A person or group that holds power or authority over another.|The overrulers of the small nation were overthrown in a popular uprising. 这个小国的统治者在一次民众起义中被推翻了。|Synonyms: dictators, tyrants, despots, oppressors, autocrats|Antonyms: subjects, citizens, the ruled, the oppressed|Noun

overrule:/ˌəʊvəˈruːl/ | vt. |推翻，否决；统治|To reject or disallow a decision, opinion, or argument; to have more power or authority than someone or something.|The judge overruled the lawyer's objection. 法官否决了律师的反对意见。|Synonyms: overturn, reject, disallow, override, invalidate|Antonyms: uphold, sustain, support, approve, ratify|Verb
