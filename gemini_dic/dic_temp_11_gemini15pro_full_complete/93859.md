immobilizing:/ɪˈmɔːbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 使不动，固定 | making something or someone unable to move | The doctor immobilized his broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了他断掉的腿。| synonyms: restricting, confining, crippling | antonyms: mobilizing, freeing, releasing | adjective

immobilize:/ɪˈmɔːbəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 使不动，固定 | to prevent something or someone from moving | The police immobilized the suspect with handcuffs. 警察用手铐制服了嫌疑人。| synonyms: restrain, restrict, disable | antonyms: mobilize, free, release | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
