megasthene:/məˈɡæsθiːn/ | n. |麦加斯梯尼|  A Greek ethnographer and explorer, ambassador of Seleucus I Nicator, one of the Diadochi (successors of Alexander the Great), to the court of Chandragupta Maurya in India.
