fold:/foʊld/ (US) /fəʊld/ (UK)| n. v.|折叠，褶皱；羊圈；信徒；倍数|A fold is a bend in a piece of paper, cloth, etc., or the shape that it makes. As a verb, to fold means to bend something over on itself.|

例句：
The paper has a fold in it.
这张纸上有一个褶皱。

Fold the paper in half.
把纸对折。


近义词：crease, pleat, wrinkle, bend, double (v.)|
反义词: unfold, straighten, flatten (v.)|
词性: noun, verb|

例句：

鸟儿收拢了翅膀。


He folded his arms across his chest.
他双臂交叉抱在胸前。
