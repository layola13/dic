canonicalness:/kəˌnɒnɪkəlnəs/| n.|正典性，规范性|The canonicalness of the Bible is a matter of faith for many.  圣经的正典性对许多人来说是一个信仰问题。|The canonicalness of this text is debated among scholars.  这篇文本的规范性在学者间存在争议。|近义词：authenticity, authority, legitimacy, validity|反义词：spuriousness, illegitimacy, invalidity|词性:noun

Etymology:
From canonical + -ness.  Canonical from Late Latin canonicus "according to church law," from Greek kanonikos "according to rule," from kanōn "rule, standard."
