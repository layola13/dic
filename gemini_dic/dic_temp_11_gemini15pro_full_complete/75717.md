full-of-beans:/ˌfʊl əv ˈbiːnz/| adj.|精力充沛的，活泼的|Full of energy and enthusiasm.|例句：The kids were full of beans after their nap. 孩子们午睡后精力充沛。|例句：He's always full of beans, even after a long day at work.  即使工作了一整天，他总是精力充沛。|近义词：energetic, lively, spirited, bouncy|反义词：lethargic, sluggish, tired, exhausted|词性:形容词

Etymology:
This idiom likely originated in the early 20th century. One theory suggests it relates to horses being fed beans, which gave them extra energy. Another connects it to the idea of being "full of piss and vinegar," with "beans" being a euphemism.  The exact origin remains uncertain.
