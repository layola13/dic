nonresonantly:/ˌnɒnˈrɛzənəntli/ /ˌnɑːnˈrɛzənəntli/
adverb
非共振地
Not in a resonant manner; without resonance.  The process occurs when the energy transfer is nonresonant.
The laser is coupled nonresonantly to the cavity. 激光非共振地耦合到腔体。
The energy is transferred nonresonantly to the molecule. 能量非共振地转移到分子上。
