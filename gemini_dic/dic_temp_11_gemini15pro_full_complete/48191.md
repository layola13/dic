decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs/ | v. | 减压，解压缩 | To release from pressure; to return to original size after being compressed. | After a long day at work, I like to decompress with a hot bath. 下班后，我喜欢用热水澡来放松一下。 |  I need to decompress this file before I can open it. 我需要先解压缩这个文件才能打开它。 | 近义词: relax, unwind, expand, inflate | 反义词: compress, constrict, tense | verb

decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 减压，解压缩 | The act or process of decompressing. |  The decompression of the diving tank was slow and careful. 潜水罐的减压过程缓慢而小心。| The decompression sickness can be very dangerous for divers. 减压病对潜水员来说非常危险。 | 近义词: relaxation, expansion, inflation | 反义词: compression, constriction | noun
