chemoreceptor:/ˈkɛməʊrɪˌsɛptə(r)/ /ˈkiːmoʊrɪˌsɛptər/ | n. | 化学感受器 | A sensory receptor that transduces a chemical signal into an action potential.  A chemoreceptor detects certain chemical stimuli in the environment and relays that information to the central nervous system. | The chemoreceptors in the carotid body detect changes in blood oxygen levels.  颈动脉体内的化学感受器检测血液中氧含量的变化。

The taste buds on your tongue are examples of chemoreceptors. 你舌头上的味蕾就是化学感受器的例子。 | 近义词: sensory receptor, receptor | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
