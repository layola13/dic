chinatown:/ˈtʃaɪnəˌtaʊn/ | n. | 唐人街；中国城 | A district of any non-Chinese city in which the population is predominantly of Chinese origin. |  We had dinner at a great restaurant in Chinatown. 我们在唐人街的一家很棒的餐馆吃了晚饭。

Chinatown is always bustling with activity during Chinese New Year.  中国新年期间，唐人街总是熙熙攘攘。
