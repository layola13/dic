crabbily:/ˈkræbɪli/ | adv. | 暴躁地，乖戾地 | In a bad-tempered or irritable way. | She answered crabbily, clearly annoyed by the interruption. 她暴躁地回答，显然被打扰了。| He stomped off crabbily after losing the game.  他输了比赛后暴躁地跺着脚走了。| 近义词：grumpily, irritably, sullenly | 反义词：cheerfully, pleasantly, amiably | 词性: 副词

词源:
From crab (n.) + -ily (adv. suffix).  The word's origin stems from the perceived grumpy and irritable nature of crabs.
