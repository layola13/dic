misleads:/ˌmɪsˈliːdz/| v.|误导，使误入歧途|To cause someone to have a wrong idea or impression, often intentionally.|The advertisement misleads consumers about the product's effectiveness. 这则广告误导了消费者对产品功效的认知。|近义词：deceive, trick, dupe, fool|反义词:guide, enlighten, inform|verb

misled:/ˌmɪsˈled/ |v. | 误导（mislead的过去式和过去分词） | Past tense and past participle of mislead. | The faulty map misled us and we ended up lost. 错误的地图误导了我们，我们最终迷路了。 | 近义词：deceived, tricked, duped, fooled | 反义词：guided, enlightened, informed | verb
