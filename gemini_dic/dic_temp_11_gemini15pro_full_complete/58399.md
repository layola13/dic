dubiosity:/ˌdjuːbiˈɒsɪti/ /ˌduːbiˈɑːsɪti/
|n.|可疑性，不确定性|The state of being dubious or uncertain; doubtfulness.|His story was filled with dubiosity, making it hard to believe.|近义词：doubt, uncertainty, suspicion, skepticism|反义词：certainty, assurance, conviction|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin *dubiosus* "doubtful," from *dubius* "wavering, moving two ways," figuratively "doubtful," from *duo* "two" + *via* "way."
