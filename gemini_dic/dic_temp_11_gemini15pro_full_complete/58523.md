duettino:/ˌduːɛˈtiːnoʊ/ /ˌdjuːɛˈtiːnoʊ/
noun
二重唱，二重奏
A short duet, typically one in an opera or other dramatic work.
The opera features a charming duettino between the two lovers.  这出歌剧以两位恋人之间迷人的二重唱为特色。
The duettino in the second act was the highlight of the performance. 第二幕的二重唱是演出的亮点。
synonyms: duet, duo
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
