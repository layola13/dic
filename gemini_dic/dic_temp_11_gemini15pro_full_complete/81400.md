grasshopper:/ˈɡræshɒpə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɡræshɑːpər/ (US)| n.|蚱蜢；蝗虫|A plant-eating insect with long back legs that enable it to jump very high.|The grasshopper hopped from one blade of grass to another. 蚱蜢从一片草叶跳到另一片草叶上。|Synonyms: locust, cricket|Antonyms: N/A|noun

catch a grasshopper:  做不可能的事情；徒劳无功
hear a grasshopper cough:  听得非常清楚；耳聪目明 (通常用于否定句)
