antispeculativeness:/ˌæntiˌspɛkjʊlətɪvnəs/
|n.| 反投机性，非投机性 |The antispeculativeness of the investment strategy appealed to conservative clients.  这种投资策略的反投机性吸引了保守的客户。|  The government praised the antispeculativeness of the market. 政府称赞了市场的非投机性。 |  practicality, pragmatism | speculation, risk-taking | noun


Etymology->
From anti- + speculative + -ness.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Speculative" comes from the Latin word "speculatus," meaning "to look at, observe, contemplate."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
