dependent: /dɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj. n. | 依赖的，依靠的；受抚养者 | relying on someone or something else for support, help, etc. |  He is still financially dependent on his parents. 他仍然在经济上依赖他的父母。| Synonyms: reliant, subject, subordinate | Antonyms: independent, autonomous | adjective

dependent: /dɪˈpɛndənt/ | n. | 受抚养者 | someone who relies on someone else for financial support | She claimed her children as dependents on her tax return. 她在报税表上申报了她的孩子作为受抚养人。| Synonyms: ward, charge | Antonyms: guardian, provider | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *dependaunt*, from Old French *dependant*, present participle of *dependre* (“to hang down from, depend on”), from Latin *dēpendere* (“to hang down, depend”), from *dē* (“down, from”) and *pendēre* (“to hang”).
