atomists:/ˈætəmɪsts/| n.|原子论者|People who believe in atomism, the theory that all matter is composed of tiny indivisible particles called atoms.|例句：The ancient Greek atomists, such as Democritus and Leucippus, developed a philosophical theory of the universe. 古希腊的原子论者，例如德谟克利特和留基伯，发展了一种宇宙哲学理论。

例句：Modern physicists, while building on ancient atomism, have discovered that atoms are not indivisible but composed of smaller particles. 现代物理学家在古代原子论的基础上，发现了原子并非不可分割，而是由更小的粒子组成。|近义词：materialists, physicists (depending on context)|反义词：dualists, idealists (depending on context)|词性:noun
