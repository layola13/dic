clouded:/ˈklaʊdɪd/| adj.|阴云密布的，模糊不清的，黯然的|overcast or obscured by clouds; (of someone's face or eyes) showing worry, sorrow, or anger; (of a liquid) not clear; opaque|例句：The sky was clouded and grey. 天空阴云密布，一片灰蒙蒙。|近义词：overcast, murky, obscure, gloomy, sullen|反义词：clear, bright, sunny, cheerful|词性:形容词

clouded:/ˈklaʊdɪd/| v.|（使）变得阴沉，（使）变得模糊不清，（使）黯然失色|(of the sky) become overcast with clouds; make or become less clear or transparent.|例句：Her eyes clouded with tears. 她的眼里噙满了泪水。|近义词：obscure, dim, blur|反义词：clarify, brighten|词性:动词
