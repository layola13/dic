lousiest: /ˈlaʊziɪst/ | adj. | 最糟糕的，最差劲的 | Superlative form of lousy: worst, of the poorest quality. |  This is the lousiest movie I've ever seen. 这是我看过的最烂的电影。|  The hotel room was absolutely lousiest - dirty and small.  这旅馆房间糟透了——又脏又小。 | 近义词: worst, terrible, awful, dreadful | 反义词: best, greatest, finest | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From lousy + -est. "Lousy" itself originated in the early 20th century, likely from "louse" (referring to a parasitic insect), implying something infested and unpleasant.  Over time, the meaning broadened to encompass anything of poor quality or low value.
