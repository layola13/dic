numerosities:/ˌn(j)uːmərˈɒsətiz/
|n.|数量众多；众多；大量|The numerousness or multitudinousness of something; the fact of being numerous.|The numerosity of the stars in the night sky is overwhelming. 夜空中星星的数量之多令人震撼。|
|近义词:abundance, multitude, multiplicity, plenitude, profusion|
|反义词:scarcity, paucity, lack, dearth|
|词性:noun|
