fulminuric:/ˌfʊlmɪˈnjʊərɪk/ | adj. | 雷酸的，异氰尿酸的 | Relating to or derived from fulminic acid. | Fulminuric acid is a chemical compound. 雷酸是一种化合物。|  Fulminuric acid can form explosive salts. 雷酸能形成爆炸性盐。| synonymous: explosive, unstable | antonymous: stable, inert | adjective

fulminurate:/ˈfʊlmɪnəˌreɪt/ | n. | 雷酸盐 | A salt or ester of fulminic acid. | Silver fulminate is a primary explosive. 雷酸银是一种起爆药。 |  Mercury fulminate is also highly sensitive. 雷酸汞也高度敏感。 | synonymous: explosive salt | antonymous:  (no direct antonym, could consider inert compound) | noun
