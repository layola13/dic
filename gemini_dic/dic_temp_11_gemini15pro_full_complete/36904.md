coloristic:/ˌkʌləˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
色彩的；着色的；用色的
Relating to or characterized by color or color effects, especially in a striking or unusual way.
Her paintings are known for their bold, coloristic effects. 她的画作以其大胆的色彩效果而闻名。

The film's coloristic style is reminiscent of early Technicolor movies. 这部电影的色彩风格让人想起早期的彩色电影。

colorful, vibrant, chromatic
drab, dull, monochrome
词性: 形容词
