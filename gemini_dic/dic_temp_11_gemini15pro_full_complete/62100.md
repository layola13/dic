enchantedly:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪdli/ | adv. | 着迷地，欣喜若狂地 | In a manner filled with delight and wonder; magically. | She listened to the music enchantedly. 她着迷地听着音乐。|  Example: The children gazed enchantedly at the fireworks. 孩子们着迷地凝视着烟花。| 近义词: delightedly, rapturously, magically | 反义词: indifferently, apathetically | 词性: 副词

enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/ | vt. | 使着迷，施魔法 | To fill with delight and wonder; to put under a spell. | The magician enchanted the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法使观众着迷。| Example: The beautiful scenery enchanted us.  美丽的景色使我们着迷。| 近义词: charm, captivate, fascinate | 反义词: repel, disgust | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *enchanter, from en- (“in, on”) + chanter (“to sing, chant”), from Latin cantare (“to sing”).  The original meaning was "to put under a spell by singing or chanting".
