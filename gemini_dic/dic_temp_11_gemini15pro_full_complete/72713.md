focusing:/ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | v. | 聚焦，集中 | The photographer is focusing the camera. 摄影师正在调焦。 |  例句：She is focusing on her studies. 她正专注于学习。 | 近义词：concentrating, honing, zeroing in | 反义词：distracting, blurring | verb

focusing:/ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | n. |  聚焦，集中 | The focusing of the lens is crucial for a sharp image. 镜头的聚焦对于清晰的图像至关重要。 | 例句：The focusing of attention on this issue is important.  将注意力集中在这个问题上很重要。 | 近义词：concentration, attention | 反义词：distraction, diffusion | noun

词源: From the verb "focus," which comes from the Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace."  The meaning shifted from the central point of a fire to the central point of anything.
