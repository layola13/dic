mortalities: /mɔrˈtælɪtiz/ | n. |死亡人数，死亡率| the number of deaths in a given period or from a particular cause | The war caused huge mortalities among civilians. 这场战争造成了大量的平民死亡。| synonyms: deaths, fatalities, losses | antonyms: births, survivals | noun

mortality: /mɔrˈtælɪti/ | n. |死亡率，必死性| the state of being subject to death |  Child mortality rates are falling.  儿童死亡率正在下降。| synonyms: death rate, fatality rate | antonyms: birth rate, survival rate | noun
