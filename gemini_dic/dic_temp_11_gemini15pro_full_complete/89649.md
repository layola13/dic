hooklike:/ˈhʊklaɪk/| adj.|钩状的，像钩的|Resembling a hook in shape or form.|The plant had hooklike thorns that caught on my clothes.  这种植物长着钩状的刺，钩住了我的衣服。|hook-shaped, hooked, curved|straight|adjective
Etymology->From 'hook' + '-like'.  'Hook' comes from Old English 'hōc', meaning 'hook, angle, bend'.  '-like' is a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'resembling', from Old English '-līc'.
