hyperaggregated:/ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度聚集的，过度凝集的 |  Characterized by excessive aggregation or clumping, typically referring to platelets or other blood components.  It describes a state where these components have clustered together more than usual. |  The patient's blood showed hyperaggregated platelets, suggesting a risk of thrombosis. 病人的血液显示血小板过度聚集，提示有血栓形成的风险。

The hyperaggregated neutrophils contributed to the inflammation. 过度聚集的嗜中性粒细胞导致了炎症。 | 近义词：clumped, aggregated | 反义词：dispersed, disaggregated | 词性：形容词
词源：hyper- (Greek, meaning "over, excessive") + aggregated (from Latin aggregare, "to add to a flock")
