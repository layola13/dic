birdieing: /ˈbɜːrdiɪŋ/ | v. | 小鸟球；低于标准杆一杆 |  The act of scoring a birdie (one stroke under par) on a hole in golf. |  He was birdieing every other hole. 他每隔一个洞就打出小鸟球。|  She finished the round by birdieing the 18th. 她在第18洞打出小鸟球结束了这一轮。| 近义词: scoring a birdie | 反义词: bogeying | 词性: 动名词 (gerund)
