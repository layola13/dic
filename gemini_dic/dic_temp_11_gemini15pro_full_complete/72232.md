floricultural:/ˌflɔːrɪˈkʌltʃərəl/
adjective
花卉栽培的；花卉文化的
Relating to the cultivation of flowers.
例句：The floricultural industry is a significant part of the economy in many countries.  花卉栽培业在许多国家都是经济的重要组成部分。

例句：She took a floricultural course to learn more about growing roses. 她参加了花卉栽培课程，以了解更多关于种植玫瑰的知识。

近义词：horticultural
反义词: None

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *floricultura*, from *flor-*, stem of *flos* ("flower") + *cultura* ("cultivation").
