epistolographic:/ɪˌpɪstəˈlɒɡræfɪk/
adjective
书信体的；与书信有关的
Relating to the writing of letters; contained in or carried on by letters.
Her research focuses on the epistolographic conventions of the 18th century. 她的研究集中在18世纪的书信写作惯例。
The novel is structured as a series of epistolographic exchanges between the two main characters. 这部小说是以两个主要人物之间的一系列书信往来为结构的。
近义词:letter-writing,  epistolary
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider) non-epistolary, conversational
词性: adjective
