malleation:/ˌmæliˈeɪʃən/
n.
捶打, 锻造, 延展性
the act of hammering or beating into shape; the state of being hammered or beaten into shape.
The malleation of the gold into intricate jewelry demonstrated the artisan's skill.  工匠将黄金捶打成复杂的珠宝，展现了他的技艺。
The blacksmith's malleation of the iron created a strong and durable horseshoe.  铁匠捶打铁块，打造出一个结实耐用的马蹄铁。
synonyms: hammering, forging, shaping, beating
antonyms:  hardening, solidifying, rigidifying
noun
