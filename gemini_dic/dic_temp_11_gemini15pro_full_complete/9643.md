antistudents:/ˌæntiˈstjuːdənts/ |n.|反学生者；反对学生的人|People who are opposed to students or student activism.  Those who actively express hostility or opposition towards students as a group, their activities, or their interests. |The antistudents protested loudly outside the university gates. 反学生者在大学门外大声抗议。| The petition was signed by a group of self-proclaimed antistudents. 请愿书是由一群自称反学生的人签署的。|Near synonyms: student antagonists, opponents of students |Antonyms: student supporters, pro-student advocates|noun
