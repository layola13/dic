marmorated: /ˈmɑːrməˌreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 大理石纹的，斑驳的 | Having a veined or mottled appearance like marble. |  The marmorated countertop added a touch of elegance to the kitchen.  大理石纹的台面为厨房增添了一丝优雅。 | Example: The stink bug, Halyomorpha halys, is commonly known as the brown marmorated stink bug due to its mottled markings. 这种椿象，Halyomorpha halys，由于其斑驳的斑纹通常被称为棕色大理石纹椿象。| synonyms: mottled, veined, marbled, variegated | antonyms: plain, uniform, solid | adjective

词源: From Latin *marmoratus, past participle of marmorare (“to cover with marble”), from marmor (“marble”).
