bruisings:/ˈbruːzɪŋz/
noun
瘀伤，青肿
Injuries to the body, usually caused by impact, that appear as discolored areas on the skin.
The boxer's face was covered in bruisings after the fight. 拳击手赛后脸上满是瘀伤。
近义词:contusions,  hematomas,  marks,  spots
反义词: healing, recovery
词性:n.

bruise:/bruːz/
verb
使…出现伤痕；撞伤
To injure the body without breaking the skin, causing discoloration.
She bruised her knee when she fell down the stairs. 她从楼梯上摔下来，擦伤了膝盖。
近义词:  injure,  contuse,  mark
反义词: heal, soothe
词性:v.

bruise:/bruːz/
noun
瘀伤；青肿
An injury appearing as an area of discolored skin on the body, caused by a blow or impact rupturing underlying blood vessels.
He had a nasty bruise on his arm from the fall. 他手臂上因摔倒而有一块严重的瘀伤。
近义词: contusion,  hematoma,  mark
反义词:none
词性:n.


词源: From Middle English *brusen, from Old French bruisier, of Germanic origin.
