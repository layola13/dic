overliberalized: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度自由化的
Having been made excessively liberal, especially in economic or political policies.  Relaxed or removed restrictions or regulations to an excessive degree.
The overliberalized financial market led to a speculative bubble.  过度自由化的金融市场导致了投机泡沫。
The country's overliberalized immigration policies have been a source of debate.  该国过度自由化的移民政策一直是争论的焦点。
Synonyms: deregulated, unrestricted, laissez-faire
Antonyms: regulated, controlled, restricted
词性: adjective
