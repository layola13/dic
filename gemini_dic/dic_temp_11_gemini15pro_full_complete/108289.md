laurate: /ˈlɔːreɪt/ /ˈlɒrɪt/ | n. adj.|月桂酸盐；月桂酸酯；受桂冠表彰者|A salt or ester of lauric acid.  A person who has been honored with an award, especially the Nobel Prize.|例句：Sodium laurate is a common ingredient in soap. 月桂酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。

例句：He is a Nobel laureate in physics. 他是诺贝尔物理学奖获得者。|近义词：winner, recipient (for laureate as a person)|反义词: loser (for laureate as a person) |词性: noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *laurātus*, past participle of *laurāre* (“to crown with laurel”), from *laurus* (“laurel”).
