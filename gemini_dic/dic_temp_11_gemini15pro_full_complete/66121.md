excentral:/ɛkˈsɛntrɪkəl/
adjective
偏心的；远离中心的
situated away from a center or axis; not central
The excentral point of a triangle is the center of the excircle of the triangle. 三角形的偏心点是三角形外接圆的圆心。
近义词: peripheral, outer, external
反义词: central, concentric, inner
词性: adjective
