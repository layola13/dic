firelights:/ˈfaɪə(r)ˌlaɪts/
n.
火光，炉火的光
the flickering light of a fire, especially in a fireplace.
The firelights danced on the walls, casting eerie shadows.  炉火的光芒在墙上跳动，投下怪异的影子。
The room was lit only by the soft firelights. 房间里只有柔和的炉火的光芒。
近义词:flicker, glow, embers
反义词:darkness, shadow
词性:名词


Etymology:
From "fire" + "light"
"Fire" comes from Old English "fyr", from Proto-Germanic "*fōr".
"Light" comes from Old English "lēoht", from Proto-Germanic "*leukhtam".
