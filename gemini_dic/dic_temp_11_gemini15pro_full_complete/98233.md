insense:/ɪnˈsɛns/ | vt. | 激怒；使发怒 | To make someone very angry. |  Example: His condescending remarks incensed her. 他的傲慢言辞激怒了她。

Example: The decision to close the library incensed the local community. 关闭图书馆的决定激怒了当地社区。


近义词: enrage, infuriate, anger, provoke | 反义词: soothe, calm, appease, placate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin incensare "to set on fire," figuratively "to inflame," from Latin incendere "to kindle, burn," from in- "in" + candere "to shine." The figurative sense of "to enrage" developed in English.
