nonimmanence:/ˌnɒnɪˈmənəns/| n.| 非内在性，非固有性|The concept of nonimmanence suggests a reality that transcends the material world.|  God's nonimmanence means he is separate from the creation. 上帝的非内在性意味着他与创造物是分离的。|近义词：transcendence, externality|反义词：immanence, inherence|noun
