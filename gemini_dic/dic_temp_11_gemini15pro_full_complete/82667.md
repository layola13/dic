guesses:/ˈɡesɪz/| n. |猜测，推测|An educated guess is a guess based on knowledge or experience. 有根据的猜测是基于知识或经验的猜测。|近义词：conjectures, estimations, assumptions, suppositions, hypotheses|反义词:certainties, facts, knowledge|noun
guesses:/ˈɡesɪz/| v. |猜测，推测|I guessed her age correctly. 我正确地猜出了她的年龄。|近义词：conjecture, estimate, assume, suppose, hypothesize, surmise, predict|反义词:know, ascertain|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gessen*, from Old Norse *geta* (“to get, obtain, beget”), from Proto-Germanic *getaną* (“to get, obtain”).  Cognate with Scots *get* (“to get”), West Frisian *gette* (“to beget”), Dutch *gissen* (“to guess”), German *gissen* (“to guess, suppose”), Danish *gisne* (“to guess, suppose”), Swedish *gissa* (“to guess, suppose”), Icelandic *geta* (“to get, beget, guess”).  More at get.
