be-hard-on:/biː hɑːrd ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 对…苛刻，对…严厉 | To treat someone severely or critically, often more than is deserved. |  例句：Don't be too hard on him; he's still learning.  不要对他太苛刻；他还在学习。|例句：The coach was hard on the players after they lost the game. 教练在他们输掉比赛后对球员们很严厉。| 近义词：criticize, scold, reprimand, be strict with | 反义词：be lenient with, be easy on, praise, encourage | 词性: 短语动词
