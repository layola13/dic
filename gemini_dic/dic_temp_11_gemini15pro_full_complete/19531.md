biflagellate:/baɪˈflædʒəˌleɪt/| adj. n.|双鞭毛的，双鞭毛生物|Having or possessing two flagella (whip-like appendages used for locomotion).|Example 1:  The biflagellate algae moved swiftly through the water.  双鞭毛藻在水中快速移动。

Example 2:  Researchers studied the movement patterns of several biflagellate species.  研究人员研究了几种双鞭毛物种的运动模式。|近义词：bi-flagellated|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps aflagellate - lacking flagella)|词性: adjective, noun

biflagellate:/baɪˈflædʒəˌleɪt/| n.|双鞭毛生物|An organism that has two flagella.|Example 1: The biflagellate was observed under a powerful microscope.  这种双鞭毛生物是在高倍显微镜下观察到的。

Example 2: Many biflagellates are found in marine environments.  许多双鞭毛生物都生活在海洋环境中。|近义词：bi-flagellated organism|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps aflagellate organism)|词性: noun
