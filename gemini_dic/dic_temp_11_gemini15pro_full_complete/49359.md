delinting:/diːˈlɪntɪŋ/
n.
去除棉绒，代码清理
The process of removing lint. In software development, it refers to the process of identifying and removing stylistic errors, inconsistencies, and potential bugs from source code.
The programmer spent hours delinting the code before submitting it for review. 程序员在提交代码审核前花了几个小时清理代码。
cleaning, tidying, purifying
contaminating, messing
