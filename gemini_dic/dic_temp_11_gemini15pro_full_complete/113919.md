maelstrom:/ˈmeɪlstrəm/ /ˈmɑːlstrəm/| n. |大漩涡；混乱；动乱|A powerful whirlpool in the sea or a river. Also used metaphorically to describe a situation of great confusion or upheaval.|The ship was caught in the maelstrom. 这艘船被卷入了大漩涡。|近义词：whirlpool, vortex, chaos, turmoil, turbulence|反义词：calm, order, peace, tranquility|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Dutch *maalstroom*, from *malen* "to grind, whirl" + *stroom* "stream".
