deontological:/ˌdiːɒnˈtɒlədʒɪkəl/| adj.|义务论的，道义论的|Relating to deontology, the ethical theory that the morality of an action should be based on whether that action itself is right or wrong under a series of rules, rather than based on the consequences of the action.|例句：A deontological approach to ethics emphasizes rules and duties. 义务论的伦理学方法强调规则和义务。

例句：She made a deontological decision, even though it resulted in an undesirable outcome. 她做出了一个道义论的决定，即使它导致了不理想的结果。|近义词：principled, moralistic, duty-based|反义词：consequentialist, teleological|词性:adjective
