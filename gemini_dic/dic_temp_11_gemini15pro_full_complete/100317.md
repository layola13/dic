intersow:/ˌɪntərˈsoʊ/
verb
间作，套种
To sow (a crop) between rows of another crop.
The farmer decided to intersow beans between the rows of corn.  农民决定在玉米行间套种大豆。
The carrots were intersown with onions. 胡萝卜和洋葱间作。
Synonyms: intercrop, interplant
Antonyms: monoculture
词性: verb
