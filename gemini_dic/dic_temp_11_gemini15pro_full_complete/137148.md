nonsubstitutional:/ˌnɑːnsʌbstɪˈtuːʃənəl/ |adj.|非取代的，非替代的|Not able to be substituted or replaced; not involving substitution.  Relating to a process or reaction where one atom or group is not replaced by another.|The nonsubstitutional binding of the drug makes it less likely to cause side effects. 这种药物的非取代结合使其不太可能引起副作用。|non-replaceable, irreplaceable, essential|substitutable, replaceable|adjective
