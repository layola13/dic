gamophyllous:/ˌɡæməˈfɪləs/| adj.|合瓣的，花瓣合生的|Having petals united or fused, at least at the base, forming a tube or cup.|Example: The bell-shaped flowers are gamophyllous, forming a single, unified structure.|Synonym: sympetalous|Antonym: choripetalous|Adjective
