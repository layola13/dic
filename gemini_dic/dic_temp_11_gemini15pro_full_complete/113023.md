lumen:/ˈluːmən/| n.|流明 (光通量单位)|A lumen is a unit of measurement for luminous flux, which is the total amount of visible light emitted by a source per unit of time.  It measures how much light is being output.|This bulb emits 1600 lumens. 这个灯泡发出1600流明。|近义词: candela (坎德拉, 发光强度单位), lux (勒克斯, 光照度单位) |反义词:  N/A (光通量单位没有直接的反义词)|noun
