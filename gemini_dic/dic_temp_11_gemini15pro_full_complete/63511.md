enumerative:/ɪˈnjuːmərətɪv/ | adj. | 枚举的，列举的 | Serving to enumerate; counting off or listing details.  |  She gave an enumerative list of all the reasons she couldn't attend. 她列举了所有她不能参加的理由。 |  The contract included an enumerative list of services to be provided. 合同中包含一份列举的服务清单。 |  Near synonyms: detailed, itemized, specific, exhaustive, comprehensive | Near antonyms: general, vague, unspecific | adjective
