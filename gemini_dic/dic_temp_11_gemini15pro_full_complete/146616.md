overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl/ (UK)| v. n.|彻底检修，大修；全面改革|To examine carefully and thoroughly and make any necessary changes or repairs; a thorough examination and repair.|例句：The engine needed a complete overhaul. 发动机需要彻底检修。|近义词：renovate, repair, service, maintain|反义词：neglect, damage|词性：动词，名词

overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl/ (UK)| n.|彻底检修，大修；全面改革|A thorough examination and repair.|例句：The car is due for an overhaul. 这辆车该大修了。|近义词：renovation, repair, service, maintenance|反义词：neglect, damage|词性：名词

词源: From "over" + "hale" (to pull, haul). Originally meant to haul a ship over for inspection and repair.
