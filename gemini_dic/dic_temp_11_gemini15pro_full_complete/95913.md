increpate:/ɪnˈkrɛpeɪt/| v. |斥责，责骂，严厉指责|To criticize or rebuke somebody severely or angrily. |例句：The teacher increpated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉斥责学生考试作弊。

例句：He was increpated for his negligence in allowing the escape. 他因玩忽职守导致犯人逃跑而受到严厉斥责。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, censure, reproach, scold, berate, castigate, admonish, chide|反义词: praise, commend, laud, compliment |词性:动词

词源: From Latin increpāre, from in- ‘in, towards’ + crepāre ‘to rattle, creak’.
