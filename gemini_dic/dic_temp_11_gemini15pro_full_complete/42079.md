cotillion:/koʊˈtɪljən/ | n. | 社交舞会，方舞；(尤指少女初次参加的)正式舞会 | A formal ball, especially one at which young ladies are presented to society. |  The debutantes were presented at the annual cotillion.  初次进入社交界的年轻女子在一年一度的社交舞会上被介绍给大家。| Synonyms: ball, formal dance, prom | Antonyms: informal gathering, casual party | noun

cotillion:/kəˈtɪljən/ | n. | 方舞 (一种起源于18世纪的舞) | A type of dance that originated in the 18th century, typically danced by four couples in a square or rectangular formation. |  They danced a lively cotillion at the wedding reception.  他们在婚礼招待会上跳了一支活泼的方舞。| Synonyms: quadrille, square dance | Antonyms: waltz, tango | noun
