desertedly:/dɪˈzɜːtɪdli/
adv.
荒凉地，无人居住地
In a deserted manner; lonely; solitary.
The streets were desertedly quiet after the curfew.  宵禁之后，街道空无一人，一片寂静。
The old house stood desertedly on the hill.  那栋老房子孤零零地矗立在山上。
近义词:solitarily, lonely, emptily
反义词:crowdedly, busily, populatedly
词性:副词

desert:/ˈdezərt/ | n. |沙漠；荒地|A barren or desolate area, especially one with little or no vegetation.|The Sahara Desert is vast and unforgiving. 撒哈拉沙漠广袤无垠，环境恶劣。|近义词:wasteland, wilderness|反义词:oasis, paradise|词性:名词
desert:/dɪˈzɜːrt/ | v. |抛弃；遗弃|Abandon (a person, cause, or organization) in a way considered disloyal or treacherous.|He deserted his family and ran off with another woman. 他抛弃了家人，和另一个女人私奔了。|近义词:abandon, forsake|反义词:support, stay with|词性:动词

词源: From the adjective "deserted" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Deserted" comes from the past participle of the verb "desert," ultimately from Latin "deserere" (to abandon).
