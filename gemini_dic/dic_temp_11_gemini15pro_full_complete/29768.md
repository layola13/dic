cattery:/ˈkætəri/ | n. |猫舍，养猫场 | A place where cats are bred or boarded. |  例句：She took her cat to the cattery while she was on vacation. 她去度假时把猫送到了猫舍。

例句：The cattery housed a variety of breeds, from Persians to Siamese.  这家猫舍养了各种各样的品种，从波斯猫到暹罗猫。| 近义词：cat shelter,  cattery, boarding kennel (for cats) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
