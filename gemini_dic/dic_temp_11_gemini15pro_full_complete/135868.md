nonprotruding:/ˌnɒnprəˈtruːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 不突出的，不伸出的 | Not projecting or sticking out; recessed. | The screws were nonprotruding, making the surface smooth.  螺丝不突出，使表面光滑。| The buttons on the remote are nonprotruding and difficult to feel in the dark.  遥控器上的按钮不突出，在黑暗中很难摸到。 | 近义词：recessed, flush, sunken | 反义词：protruding, projecting, sticking out | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not".
protrude (verb): from Latin protrudere "to thrust forth," from pro- "forth, forward" + trudere "to thrust, push."
-ing (suffix): forms the present participle of verbs and adjectives from verbs.
