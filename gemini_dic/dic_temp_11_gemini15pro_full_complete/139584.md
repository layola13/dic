obviation:/ˌɒbviˈeɪʃən/ /ˌɑːbviˈeɪʃən/
n.|消除，避免，排除|The obviation of the need for surgery is a major advantage of this treatment. 这种治疗的主要优点是避免了手术的需要。|Example: The new software obviates the need for manual data entry.  新软件避免了手动输入数据的需要。|Synonyms: elimination, prevention, avoidance|Antonyms: necessity, requirement|noun

obviate:/ˈɒbvieɪt/ /ˈɑːbvieɪt/
v.|消除，避免，排除|Regular exercise can obviate the need for medication in some cases. 在某些情况下，经常锻炼可以避免药物治疗的需要。|Example: The invention of the automobile obviated the need for horse-drawn carriages. 汽车的发明取代了对马车运输的需求。|Synonyms: preclude, prevent, avert|Antonyms: necessitate, require|verb
