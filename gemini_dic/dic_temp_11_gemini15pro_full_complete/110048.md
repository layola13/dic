licenses:/ˈlaɪsənsɪz/
n.|许可证，执照|A legal document giving official permission to do something.|You need a license to drive a car. 你需要驾照才能开车。|permit, authorization|prohibition|noun


license:/ˈlaɪsəns/
n.|许可证，执照|A legal document giving official permission to do something.|He obtained a license to sell alcohol. 他获得了销售酒类的许可证。|permit, authorization|prohibition|noun
v.|许可，授权|To give official permission to do something.|The software is licensed under the GNU General Public License.  这款软件是在GNU通用公共许可证下授权的。|authorize, permit|prohibit, forbid|verb

词源：From Old French "licence," from Latin "licentia" meaning "freedom, liberty," from "licere" meaning "to be permitted."
