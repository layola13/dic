disjointly:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪntlɪ/
|adv.|不相交地，互斥地|Having no elements in common; mutually exclusive.  Referring to sets or events that do not overlap or share any common members. |The two events occurred disjointly. 这两个事件互斥发生。|The two groups lived disjointly, rarely interacting.  这两个群体互不交织地生活，很少互动。|separately, independently, distinctly|conjointly, together, concurrently
|adverb|
