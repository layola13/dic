northwards:/ˈnɔːθwədz/ | adv. | 向北 |  Toward the north; in a northerly direction. | The birds migrated northwards for the summer. 鸟儿们夏天向北迁徙。| 近义词:northward, northerly | 反义词:southwards | 词性:副词

Etymology: From "north" + "-ward" (a suffix indicating direction).  "North" comes from Old English "norþ," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*norþuz," meaning "north." "-Ward" comes from Old English "-weard," meaning "toward."
