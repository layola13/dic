corrigibility:/ˌkɔːrɪdʒəˈbɪləti/
n.
可纠正性，可改正性
The quality of being capable of being corrected, improved, or reformed.
Her corrigibility allowed her to learn from her mistakes and become a better person.  她的可纠正性使她能够从错误中学习，成为一个更好的人。
correctability, amendability, reformability
incorrigibility, intractability, unamendability
noun
