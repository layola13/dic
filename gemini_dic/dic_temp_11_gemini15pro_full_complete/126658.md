nakedness:/ˈneɪkɪdnəs/| n.|裸体；赤裸；无遮蔽；缺乏掩饰|The state of being without clothing or covering; exposure to view; lack of concealment or disguise.|例句：The painting celebrates the nakedness of the human form. 这幅画赞美了人体的裸露。

例句：He spoke with a nakedness of emotion that was both shocking and moving. 他以一种赤裸裸的情感表达，既令人震惊又令人感动。|近义词：nudity, bareness, exposure, vulnerability|反义词：concealment, covering, clothedness|词性:noun

词源: From Old English "nǣcedness," from "nǣced" (naked) + "-ness" (suffix denoting state or quality).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*nakwa-" (naked).
