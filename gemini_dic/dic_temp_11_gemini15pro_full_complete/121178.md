misdirecting:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛktɪŋ/
verb
误导；指错方向
leading someone in the wrong direction or giving them incorrect information.
The misleading sign misdirected many tourists.  误导性的标志使许多游客走错了路。
misleading, misguiding
guiding, directing
v.
