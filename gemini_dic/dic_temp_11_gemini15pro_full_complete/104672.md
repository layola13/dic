kaross:/kəˈrɒs/| n. |卡罗斯，一种用动物皮毛制成的斗篷或毯子|A kaross is a cloak or blanket made of animal skins, traditionally worn by the Khoikhoi and San people of southern Africa. |例句：The old man wrapped himself in his kaross to ward off the evening chill. 老人用卡罗斯裹住自己以抵御夜晚的寒意。

例句：She treasured the kaross, a gift from her grandmother, made from the skin of a springbok. 她珍藏着这件卡罗斯，这是她祖母送的礼物，由跳羚皮制成。|近义词：cloak, blanket, robe|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern clothing")|词性:noun
