ferrugo:/fəˈruːɡoʊ/ /fɛˈruːɡoʊ/
n.|铁锈；锈病；锈菌|A reddish-brown coating formed on iron or steel by oxidation, especially in the presence of moisture.|The old gate was covered in ferrugo.|近义词：rust, corrosion, oxidation|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"polish", "shine" 表示没有锈的状态)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *ferrūgō*  "rust," from *ferrum* "iron."
