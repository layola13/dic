curialistic:/ˌkjʊəriəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
教廷式的；罗马教廷的；(尤指)教皇至上论的
Relating to or characteristic of the Roman Curia (the administrative body of the Holy See); specifically, advocating or supporting the supremacy of the pope.
例句：His curialistic views alienated many members of the church. 他的教廷式观点疏远了许多教会成员。

例句：The archbishop's curialistic pronouncements sparked controversy among the clergy. 大主教的教皇至上论言论在神职人员中引发了争议。

近义词：papal, Vatican, hierarchical
反义词：secular, laity-oriented

词性: adjective
