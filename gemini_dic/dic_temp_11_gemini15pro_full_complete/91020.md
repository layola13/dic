hushed:/ˈhʌʃt/ | adj. |安静的，寂静的|Characterized by quiet and stillness, often due to awe, reverence, or fear.  It implies a deliberate suppression of sound. |The room fell hushed as the speaker began. 房间里随着演讲者开始讲话而变得鸦雀无声。|Near synonyms: quiet, silent, still, muted, subdued|Antonyms: loud, noisy, boisterous|adjective

hushed:/ˈhʌʃt/ | v. |使安静，使沉默|To make quiet or silent, often by quieting others or oneself. |She hushed the baby with a lullaby. 她用摇篮曲让婴儿安静下来。|Near synonyms: quiet, silence, still, mute, subdue|Antonyms: encourage, incite, rouse|verb
