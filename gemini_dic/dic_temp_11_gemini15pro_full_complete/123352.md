monologian: /ˌmɒnəˈləʊɡɪən/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɔːɡiən/
monologian: n.
monologian: 中文翻译：独白者；擅长独白的人；喜欢发表长篇大论的人
monologian: 英文解析：A person who delivers a monologue; a person who is given to making long speeches.
monologian: 例句：The monologian held the audience captive with his fascinating story.  这位独白者用他引人入胜的故事吸引了观众。

monologian: 例句：He's a bit of a monologian, so be prepared for a long conversation. 他有点喜欢长篇大论，所以准备好进行长时间的对话吧。
monologian: 近义词：monologist, speaker, orator
monologian: 反义词：listener, audience
monologian: 词性：名词
