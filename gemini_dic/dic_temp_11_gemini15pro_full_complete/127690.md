negatively:/ˈneɡətɪvli/| adv.|消极地，负面地，否定地|in a way that expresses "no" or refusal; in a way that is not hopeful or encouraging; in a way that is harmful or bad|The news affected him negatively.  这消息对他产生了负面影响。|The test results were negatively impacted by the faulty equipment.  测试结果受到了故障设备的负面影响。|近义词：badly, unfavorably, adversely, pessimistically|反义词：positively, favorably, optimistically|词性:副词
negatively:|词源：From Latin *negativus, from negare (“to deny”), from nec (“not”) + aiō (“I say”).|
