defeated:/dɪˈfiːtɪd/| adj.|被打败的，挫败的，沮丧的|Defeated means having lost a battle, game, or contest, or feeling discouraged and demoralized.|例句：The defeated army retreated.  战败的军队撤退了。|近义词：conquered, vanquished, beaten, overwhelmed, thwarted, frustrated, discouraged|反义词：victorious, triumphant, successful, winning|词性:形容词
defeat:/dɪˈfiːt/| vt. n.|击败，战胜；失败，败北|To defeat someone means to win against them in a battle, game, or contest. As a noun, defeat means the act of losing or the state of having lost.|例句：They defeated their rivals in the final. 他们在决赛中击败了对手。|近义词：conquer, vanquish, overcome, subdue, overthrow|反义词：victory, triumph, success|词性:动词,名词

词源: From Old French *defeire, *desfaire "undo, destroy," from Latin dis- "apart" + facere "to do, make."
