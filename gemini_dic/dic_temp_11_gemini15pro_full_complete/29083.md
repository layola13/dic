carvist: /ˈkɑːrvɪst/ | n. | 雕刻家 | A person who carves wood, stone, or other materials into sculptures or decorative objects. |  Example 1: The carvist skillfully transformed a block of marble into a beautiful statue.  雕刻家巧妙地将一块大理石变成了一座美丽的雕像。

Example 2:  The museum exhibited a collection of intricate carvings by a renowned carvist.  博物馆展出了一位著名雕刻家创作的复杂雕刻作品集。 | 近义词: sculptor, engraver | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  From the verb "carve" + "-ist" (a suffix forming agent nouns).  "Carve" comes from Old English "ceorfan," meaning "to cut."
