overpensiveness:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪvnəs/| n.|过度沉思，忧思过度|The state of being excessively pensive; deep thoughtfulness, often tinged with melancholy.|Her overpensiveness made her seem distant and aloof. 她过度沉思让她显得疏远而冷漠。|近义词：brooding, contemplation, pensiveness, preoccupation|反义词：lightheartedness, cheerfulness|词性:名词

overpensive:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪv/| adj.|过度沉思的，忧思过度的|Excessively or unduly pensive; given to or marked by excessive brooding or melancholy thought.|He sat by the window, overpensive and withdrawn. 他坐在窗边，过度沉思且沉默寡言。|近义词：brooding, contemplative, pensive, preoccupied|反义词：lightheartedness, cheerful|词性:形容词
