kex:/kɛks/
noun
密钥交换
a process by which cryptographic keys are exchanged between two parties, allowing use of a cryptographic algorithm
The kex algorithm negotiated was diffie-hellman-group1-sha1.  密钥交换算法协商为 diffie-hellman-group1-sha1。
近义词: key exchange
反义词: (No direct antonym for a process)
