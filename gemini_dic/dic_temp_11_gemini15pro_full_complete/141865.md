orchid:/ˈɔːrkɪd/ (US) /ˈɔːkɪd/ (UK)| n. |兰花；淡紫色|A plant with brightly coloured flowers of unusual shapes. Orchids are grown in many countries for their beauty.|She loves orchids and has a large collection of them. 她喜欢兰花，收藏了很多。|Synonyms: flower, blossom|Antonyms: weed|Noun

orchid:/ˈɔːrkɪd/ (US) /ˈɔːkɪd/ (UK)| adj. |淡紫色的|Of a pale purple colour.|She wore an orchid dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|Synonyms: lavender, lilac|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the color)|Adjective
