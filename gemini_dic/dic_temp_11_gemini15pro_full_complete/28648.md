caricaturable:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊərəbl/ |adj.|可加以漫画化的，可被夸张描绘的|Suitable for caricature; easily caricatured.  Able to be represented in a caricature, typically by exaggerating particular features.|例句：His large nose and bushy eyebrows made him highly caricaturable. 他的大鼻子和浓密的眉毛使他很容易被画成漫画。|近义词：lampoonable, satirizable, mockable|反义词:unremarkable, nondescript|词性:adjective
