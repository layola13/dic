integropallial:/ɪnˌtɛɡroʊˈpæliəl/| adj.|完整的；全缘的；外套膜边缘完整的|Having a smooth and continuous pallial line, without a sinus or indentation.  This refers specifically to the pallial line on the inner surface of a bivalve shell, indicating the attachment point of the mantle.  The presence or absence of a pallial sinus is a key characteristic used in bivalve classification.|The clam exhibits an integropallial shell margin.  这只蛤蜊展现出完整的壳缘。|The Venus clam has an integropallial shell.  帘蛤的壳是全缘的。|近义词：entire, complete, whole|反义词：sinupalliate|词性:adjective
