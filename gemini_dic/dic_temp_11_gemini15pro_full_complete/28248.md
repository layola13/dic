captained:/ˈkæptɪnd/| v.|担任队长，指挥|She captained the team to victory. 她带领队伍取得了胜利。|近义词：led, commanded, headed|反义词: followed, obeyed |verb (past tense)

captained:/ˈkæptɪnd/| adj.|被指挥的，由...担任队长的|The captained ship sailed smoothly.  由...担任队长的船航行顺利。|近义词：led, commanded|反义词: uncontrolled, unguided |adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From captain + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *capitaneus "chief, head," from caput "head."
