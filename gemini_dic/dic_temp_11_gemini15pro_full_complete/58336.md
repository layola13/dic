drywall: /ˈdraɪwɔːl/ (US) /ˈdraɪwɔːl/ (UK)
n.|石膏板，干墙|A panel made of gypsum plaster pressed between two thick sheets of paper, used for interior walls and ceilings.|The contractor used drywall to construct the interior walls of the house. 承包商用石膏板建造房屋的内墙。|Synonyms: plasterboard, gypsum board, sheetrock|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps brick, concrete, wood)|noun
