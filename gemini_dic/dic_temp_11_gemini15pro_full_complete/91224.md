hydrocarbons:/ˈhaɪdrəʊˌkɑːrbənz/ (UK) /ˈhaɪdroʊˌkɑːrbənz/ (US)| n. |碳氢化合物|A compound of hydrogen and carbon, such as any of those which are the chief components of petroleum and natural gas.|Petroleum and natural gas are primarily composed of hydrocarbons. 石油和天然气主要由碳氢化合物组成。|Synonyms:  alkanes, alkenes, alkynes|Antonyms: None|Noun

hydrocarbon:/ˈhaɪdrəʊˌkɑːrbən/ (UK) /ˈhaɪdroʊˌkɑːrbən/ (US)| n. |碳氢化合物|Any of a class of organic compounds composed only of hydrogen and carbon.|Methane is the simplest hydrocarbon. 甲烷是最简单的碳氢化合物。|Synonyms:  alkane, alkene, alkyne|Antonyms: None|Noun
