outscent:/aʊtˈsɛnt/
verb
胜过…的嗅觉；嗅觉比…灵敏
to surpass in scent; to have a keener sense of smell than.
The bloodhound outscentsed the other dogs and quickly found the missing child.  警犬的嗅觉胜过其他狗，很快就找到了失踪的孩子。
近义词: outsniff, surpass in smell
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "to be outsmelled by")
词性: verb
