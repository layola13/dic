mono-substitution:/ˌmɒnəʊˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən/ |n.|单取代，单置换|The reaction undergoes mono-substitution, where only one hydrogen atom is replaced by a chlorine atom.|该反应进行单取代，其中只有一个氢原子被氯原子取代。|Example 1: Mono-substitution of benzene with nitric acid produces nitrobenzene.  苯与硝酸单取代生成硝基苯。

Example 2:  The mono-substitution reaction is highly selective.  这个单取代反应具有高度选择性。|synonyms: single substitution, single displacement|antonyms: poly-substitution, multiple substitution|noun
