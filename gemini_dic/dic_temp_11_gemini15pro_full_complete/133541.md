nonfilter:/nɒnˈfɪltər/ | adj. | 无滤镜的，未过滤的 |  Not having been subjected to a filter, either literally (like a photograph) or figuratively (like a person's thoughts or emotions).  |  She posted a nonfilter selfie on Instagram.  她在Instagram上发布了一张未经滤镜的自拍照。 | synonyms: unfiltered, raw, natural, authentic | antonyms: filtered, edited, altered, refined | adjective

nonfilter:/nɒnˈfɪltər/ | n. | 无滤镜的东西/人 | Something or someone that is unfiltered or raw. |  He's a nonfilter; he just says whatever comes to mind. 他是一个不加掩饰的人；想到什么就说什么。| synonyms: straight shooter, unedited version | antonyms: censored version, edited version | noun
