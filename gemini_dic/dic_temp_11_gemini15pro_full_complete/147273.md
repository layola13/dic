overmitigate: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/
verb
过度缓解；过度减轻
To mitigate something excessively, to reduce the severity of something more than is necessary or desirable.
The government's attempts to overmitigate the economic downturn led to unintended consequences. 政府试图过度缓解经济衰退导致了意想不到的后果。
The company overmitigated the risks, stifling innovation in the process.  这家公司过度降低风险，扼杀了创新进程。
近义词: overcompensate, overdo
反义词: undermitigate, exacerbate
词性: verb

Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mitigate" (from Latin "mitigare," meaning "to soften, make mild").
