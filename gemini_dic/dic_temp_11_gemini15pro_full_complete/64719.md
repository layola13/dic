eschaunge:/ɛsˈtʃɔːndʒ/ /ɛksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | n. | 交換；交易；交易场所 | An act of giving one thing and receiving another (especially of the same type or value) in return. | He made an eschaunge of prisoners with the enemy. 他与敌人交换了俘虏。 |  近义词: exchange, barter, trade, swap | 反义词: retain, keep | 词性: noun

eschaunge:/ɛsˈtʃɔːndʒ/ /ɛksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | vt. | 交換；交易 | Give something and receive something of the same kind in return. | They eschaunged gifts at Christmas. 他们在圣诞节交换了礼物。 | 近义词: exchange, barter, trade, swap | 反义词: retain, keep | 词性: verb
