lithologies:/lɪˈθɑːlədʒiz/
noun
岩性（复数）
The study of rock types, especially in the context of their formation and composition.  It refers to the physical characteristics of rocks, such as color, texture, grain size, and mineral composition.
The geologist studied the lithologies of the region to understand its geological history.  地质学家研究了该地区的岩性以了解其地质历史。
rock types, rock formations
---
Etymology:
From litho- +‎ -logy, from Ancient Greek  λίθος (líthos, “stone”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
