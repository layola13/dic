mysis: /ˈmaɪsɪs/ | n. | 幼虫；糠虾 | The larval stage of certain crustaceans, especially shrimps.  It's a small, shrimp-like creature. |  Many fish feed on mysis in their early life stages. 许多鱼类在生命早期以糠虾为食。

Mysis are an important food source for many marine animals. 糠虾是许多海洋动物的重要食物来源。 | synonyms: larva,  juvenile,  shrimplet | antonyms: adult, mature | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μῦς (mûs, “mouse”)  + -sis.  The name refers to the small, mouse-like appearance of the mysis larva.
