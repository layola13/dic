anticensorial:/ˌæntiˌsɛnˈsɔːriəl/
adj.
反审查的，反对审查制度的
Opposed to censorship; against the suppression of speech, writing, or other forms of expression.
The journalist wrote an anticensorial article criticizing the government's new media laws.  这位记者写了一篇反审查的文章，批评政府新的媒体法律。
The group held an anticensorial protest against the banning of the book.  该团体举行了一场反审查抗议活动，反对禁书。
近义词:  anti-censorship,  uncensored
反义词:  pro-censorship, censorious
词性: 形容词
