overdepressing: /ˌōvərdiˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 极其压抑的，非常沮丧的 | Extremely depressing; causing a great deal of sadness or dejection. | The news of the layoffs was overdepressing for the entire company.  公司裁员的消息让整个公司都感到极其沮丧。 |  The constant rain and gray skies were overdepressing.  连绵不断的雨和灰色的天空令人非常沮丧。 | 近义词: extremely depressing, overwhelmingly sad, deeply disheartening | 反义词: uplifting, encouraging, exhilarating | 词性: adjective

overdepressing:  |  |  | Formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) to the adjective "depressing". "Depressing" itself comes from the verb "depress," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "deprimere" meaning "to press down." |  |  |  |
