forboden:/fərˈboʊdən/
| adj. | 被禁止的；禁忌的 | Forbidden; prohibited.  Derived from the past participle of the verb "forbid". Often implies a strong moral or legal prohibition. |  Access to this area is strictly forbidden.  进入该区域是严格禁止的。 |  The forbidden fruit tasted sweet. 禁果尝起来很甜。| 近义词:prohibited, banned, outlawed, taboo | 反义词: permitted, allowed, legal | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *forboden*, from Old English *forboden*, past participle of *forbēodan* (“to forbid”), from *for-* (“against, away”) + *bēodan* (“to command, offer”).  Cognate with German *verboten*.
