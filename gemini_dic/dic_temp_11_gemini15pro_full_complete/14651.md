avowableness:/əˌvaʊəbəlˈnɛs/
noun
可公开承认; 可承认
The quality or state of being avowable; the ability to be openly acknowledged or declared.
His avowableness of his mistakes earned him respect. 他公开承认自己的错误为他赢得了尊重。
Synonyms: acknowledgeability, confessability, declarability
Antonyms: unavowableness, undisclosability, inconfessability
词性: 名词

avow:/əˈvaʊ/
verb
公开承认；公开宣称
To declare or admit (something) openly and unashamedly.
He avowed his love for her. 他公开宣称对她的爱。
Synonyms: declare, admit, confess, proclaim
Antonyms: deny, disavow, repudiate
词性: 动词
