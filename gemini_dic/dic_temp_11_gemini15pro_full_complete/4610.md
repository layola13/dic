alcyonoid:/ælˈsaɪənɔɪd/ | adj. n. |  海鸡冠目的；软珊瑚目的；(似)海鸡冠珊瑚的；(似)软珊瑚的 |  Relating to or denoting an order (Alcyonacea) of corals, including the sea fans, soft corals, and sea pens.  As a noun, it refers to a coral of this order. | The alcyonoid corals are known for their vibrant colors.  海鸡冠珊瑚以其鲜艳的色彩而闻名。

The diver observed a beautiful alcyonoid swaying gently in the current. 潜水员观察到美丽的软珊瑚在水流中轻轻摇曳。 | 近义词: soft coral, octocoral | 反义词: hard coral (in a broader sense, as alcyonoids are also corals) | 词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *alkyōn* (kingfisher, also halcyon) + *-oeidēs* (-like, resembling), ultimately related to *alkyōn* (kingfisher), which in Greek mythology was associated with a period of calm seas.  The connection likely stems from the perceived resemblance of some alcyonoids to a kingfisher's nest.
