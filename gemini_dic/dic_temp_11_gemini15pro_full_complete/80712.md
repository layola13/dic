gorinesses:/ˈɡɔːrinɪsɪz/
noun
血腥，残忍
The state or quality of being gory; excessive violence and bloodshed.
The gorinesses of the movie made her stomach churn. 这部电影的血腥程度让她反胃。
近义词: bloodshed, carnage, brutality, gruesomeness
反义词: pleasantness, peacefulness, tranquility
词性: noun


Etymology:
From gory + -ness + -es.  "Gory" comes from the Old English "gor," meaning filth or dung, which later evolved to mean clotted blood.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality. The suffix "-es" creates the plural form.
