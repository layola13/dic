dismayedness:/dɪsˈmeɪdɪdnəs/
|n.|沮丧，惊慌；灰心丧气|The news of the company's closure was met with widespread dismayedness among the employees. 公司倒闭的消息在员工中引起了广泛的沮丧。|The dismayedness in her voice was palpable. 她声音里的沮丧显而易见。|近义词：dismay, consternation, alarm, apprehension|反义词：calmness, composure, equanimity|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From dismayed + -ness.  Dismay comes from the Old French *desmayer (Modern French désmayer),  from des- (expressing reversal) + -mayer (to strike senseless).
