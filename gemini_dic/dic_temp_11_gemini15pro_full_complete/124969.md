muggar:/ˈmʌɡər/ | n. | 抢劫犯;行凶抢劫者 | A person who attacks and robs someone in a public place. | He was attacked by a muggar in the park. 他在公园里被一个抢劫犯袭击了。| synonyms: robber, attacker, assailant, bandit | antonyms: victim, law enforcer, police officer | noun

muggar:/ˈmʌɡər/ | v. | 行凶抢劫 | To attack and rob someone in a public place. | He was mugged on his way home from work. 他在下班回家的路上被抢劫了。| synonyms: rob, attack, assault | antonyms: protect, defend | verb
