brodekin:/ˈbrɒdɪkɪn/ | n. | 半统靴，短靴 | A kind of soft leather shoe or half-boot, like a buskin, worn in the 15th and 16th centuries. |  He wore a pair of fine brodekins, suitable for dancing. 他穿着一双精致的半统靴，适合跳舞。 | Example:  The young nobleman sported fashionable brodekins.  这位年轻的贵族穿着时髦的短靴。| Synonym: buskin, half-boot, ankle boot | Antonym:  None (lack of a direct opposite for this historical footwear) | noun
