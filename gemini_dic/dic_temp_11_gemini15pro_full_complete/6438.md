ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *ample*, from Latin *amplus* ("large, spacious, of large extent, great, ample, abundant, magnificent, splendid"), related to *ambi-* ("around") and possibly to Greek *ἀναπτύσσω* (*anaptyssō*, "to unfold").
