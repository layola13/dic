kle:/kleɪ/| n.| 粘土; (尤指用于制作砖、瓦等的)粘土 | A type of stiff, sticky earth that is used for making bricks, pottery, etc. | The house was built of kle. 这房子是用粘土建造的。| 近义词: clay, loam, earth | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *cleye, from Old English clǣġ (“clay”), from Proto-Germanic *klaiwaz (“clay”), from Proto-Indo-European *gley- (“to glue, stick”). Cognate with Scots cley (“clay”), West Frisian klaai (“clay”), Dutch klei (“clay”), German Klei (“clay”), Danish ler (“clay”), Swedish lera (“clay”).
