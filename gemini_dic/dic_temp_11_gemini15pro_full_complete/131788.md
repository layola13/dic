n.
非婚性，非婚姻状态
The state of not being married or in a marriage-like relationship.
His nonconnubiality allowed him to pursue his career without the constraints of family life.  他的非婚状态使他能够在没有家庭生活限制的情况下追求自己的事业。

近义词: singlehood, celibacy
反义词: marriage, matrimony
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + connubial + -ity.  Connubial comes from Latin *connubialis, from con- "together" + nubere "to marry, veil oneself".
