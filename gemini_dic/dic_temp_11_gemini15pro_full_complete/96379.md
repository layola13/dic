indiscreet:/ˌɪndɪˈskriːt/
adjective
不慎重的，轻率的，鲁莽的
Not careful about what you say or do, especially when this could cause trouble or upset people.
例句：It was indiscreet of him to mention her divorce. 他提及她的离婚是轻率的。

例句：She made some indiscreet remarks about his private life. 她对他私生活进行了一些轻率的评论。


近义词：imprudent, unwise, careless, tactless, impolitic
反义词：discreet, prudent, careful, tactful
词性：adjective
