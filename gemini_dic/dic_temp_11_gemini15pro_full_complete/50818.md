descried:/dɪˈskraɪd/
verb
察觉；发现；看出
To catch sight of (something distant or obscure) after looking carefully; discern.
例句：From the top of the hill, they descried a small village nestled in the valley below. 从山顶上，他们看到山谷里有个小村庄。

例句：The sailor descried a ship on the horizon. 水手在地平线上发现了一艘船。

近义词：spotted, observed, perceived, discerned, sighted, espied
反义词：missed, overlooked
词性：v.
