antiliberal:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəl/
adjective
反自由主义的；反对自由主义的
Opposed to liberal principles or policies.
The antiliberal policies of the government sparked protests.  政府的反自由主义政策引发了抗议。
The senator's antiliberal stance alienated many voters.  这位参议员的反自由主义立场疏远了许多选民。
Near Antonyms: liberal, progressive, leftist
Near Synonyms: conservative, reactionary, illiberal
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + liberal.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". "Liberal" comes from the Latin "liberalis" meaning "of freedom, pertaining to a freeman, noble, generous".
