emburse: /ɪmˈbɜːrs/ /ɪmˈbɝːs/
| vt. | 报销，偿还 | To repay (someone) for expenses incurred. | Example 1: The company will emburse you for your travel expenses. 公司会报销你的差旅费。

Example 2:  I will emburse him for the cost of the materials. 我会偿还他材料的费用。 | Synonyms: reimburse, compensate, repay | Antonyms: charge, bill, debit | Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emborser, from en- "in" + borse "purse".  Literally, "to put back in the purse."
