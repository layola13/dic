blasphemy: /ˈblæsfəmi/ (US), /ˈblæsfɪmi/ (UK) | n. |亵渎，亵渎神明|The act of insulting or showing contempt or lack of reverence for God or sacred things. |His words were considered blasphemy by the church. 他的话被教会认为是亵渎神明的。|
blasphemy: /ˈblæsfəmi/ (US), /ˈblæsfɪmi/ (UK) | n. |  irreverent or impious act or utterance | The comedian's jokes about religion were seen as blasphemy by some.  一些人认为这个喜剧演员关于宗教的笑话是亵渎。|近义词：profanity, sacrilege, impiety, irreverence |反义词：reverence, piety |词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *blasfemie*, from Late Latin blasphemia, from Greek βλασφημία (blasphēmia), from βλάσφημος (blasphēmos) "evil-speaking," from βλάπτω (blaptō) "I injure, harm" + φήμη (phēmē) "speech, utterance, report."
