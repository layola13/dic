outperforms:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrmz/
verb
胜过，优于
to perform better than someone or something else
例句：The new model outperforms the old one in fuel efficiency.  新型号在燃油效率方面胜过旧型号。

例句：Our team consistently outperforms our competitors. 我们的团队一直都比竞争对手表现出色。

近义词：exceeds, surpasses, beats, trumps
反义词：underperforms, lags behind
词性：v.
