misinstructing:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌktɪŋ/
verb
错误指导，误导
Giving incorrect information or instructions to someone, leading them to believe or do something wrong.
The teacher was misinstructing the students about the historical facts. 老师错误地指导学生关于历史事实。
He was misinstructing his team on the new software, leading to a lot of confusion. 他错误地指导他的团队使用新软件，导致了很多混乱。
misleading, misguiding, misdirecting
instructing, guiding, directing correctly
