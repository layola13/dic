multimodal:/ˌmʌltiˈmoʊdəl/ | adj. | 多模态的 |  Combining or involving several different modes, especially of communication or transportation. | This multimodal transportation system uses trucks, trains, and ships.  这个多式联运系统使用卡车、火车和轮船。 |  intermodal, multimedia | unimodal | adjective

multimodal:/ˌmʌltiˈmoʊdəl/ | n. | 多模态 | A system or approach that combines multiple modes, especially of communication or transportation.  | The conference focused on the latest developments in multimodal AI.  这次会议的重点是多模态人工智能的最新发展。| combined, integrated | singular | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
