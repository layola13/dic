dismissively:/dɪsˈmɪsɪvli/
adverb
轻蔑地，不屑一顾地
in a manner showing that you do not think something is worth considering; scornfully
He waved his hand dismissively. 他轻蔑地挥了挥手。
Synonyms: contemptuously, scornfully, disdainfully
Antonyms: respectfully, attentively, thoughtfully
词性: 副词
