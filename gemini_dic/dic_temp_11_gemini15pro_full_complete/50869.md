desensitises:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/
verb
使脱敏；使不再敏感
to make someone or something less sensitive to something
Repeated exposure to violence desensitises people to it.  反复接触暴力会使人们对暴力变得麻木。
近义词: numb, dull, inure
反义词: sensitise, heighten
词性: verb


desensitise:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
verb
使脱敏；使不再敏感
to make someone or something less sensitive to something
The vaccine desensitises the body to the allergen. 这种疫苗使身体对过敏原不再敏感。
近义词: numb, dull, inure
反义词: sensitise, heighten
词性: verb


词源: de- (Latin prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + sensitise (from Latin sensus "feeling")
