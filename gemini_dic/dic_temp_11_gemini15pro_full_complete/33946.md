cirrocumulus:/ˌsɪroʊˈkjuːmjələs/| n. |卷积云|A type of high-altitude cloud characterized by small, white patches or ripples arranged in regular patterns, often resembling fish scales or a flock of sheep.|例句：The cirrocumulus clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 卷积云懒洋洋地飘过天空。|例句：The presence of cirrocumulus clouds often indicates a change in weather. 卷积云的出现通常预示着天气变化。|近义词：mackerel sky (非正式)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 nimbostratus 雨层云, 代表完全不同的云类型)|词性:名词
