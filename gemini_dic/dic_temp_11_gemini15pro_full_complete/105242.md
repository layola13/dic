khakis:/ˈkɑːkiz/| n.|卡其裤；卡其布服装|A pair of loose-fitting trousers made of khaki cloth.  Typically light brown or beige in color.  Often refers to more than one pair.| He wore his khakis to the barbecue. 他穿着卡其裤去烧烤了。|近义词：chinos, slacks|反义词: (No direct antonym; perhaps "formal wear")|Noun

kaki:/ˈkɑːki/| n. adj.|卡其色；卡其布|A light brown or beige color. Also refers to the sturdy twilled cloth of this color.| The desert camouflage was a khaki color. 沙漠迷彩服是卡其色的。|近义词：beige, tan|反义词: (No direct antonym for the color; perhaps "dark")|Noun, Adjective
