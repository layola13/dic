nonbinding:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪndɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈbaɪndɪŋ/
adjective
不具约束力的；无约束力的
Not legally or morally obligatory.
例句：The agreement is nonbinding and can be changed at any time.  这项协议不具约束力，可以随时更改。

例句：They signed a nonbinding letter of intent. 他们签署了一份不具约束力的意向书。

近义词：nonobligatory, optional, voluntary
反义词：binding, mandatory, compulsory
词性：adjective
