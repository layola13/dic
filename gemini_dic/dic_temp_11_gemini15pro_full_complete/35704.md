cobwebs:/ˈkɑːbwɛbz/ (US) /ˈkɒbwɛbz/ (UK)| n. |蜘蛛网，蛛丝；陈腐的东西；混乱的思绪|A network of thin threads spun by a spider to catch its prey, often found in neglected places. Also used metaphorically to describe something old, dusty, or neglected, or a confused state of mind.|例句：The attic was filled with cobwebs. 阁楼里满是蜘蛛网。

例句：His mind was full of cobwebs after the long journey. 长途旅行后，他的思绪一片混乱。|近义词：spiderwebs, gossamer, dust, clutter|反义词:cleanliness, order|词性:名词
