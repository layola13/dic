chamberers:/ˈtʃeɪmbərərz/
n.
实习律师，见习律师
Lawyers who are undertaking pupillage (a period of practical training after completing a law degree).
The chamberers were busy preparing for their first court appearance.  实习律师们正忙着准备他们的第一次出庭。
Synonyms:  pupils, trainees, interns
Antonyms:  qualified lawyers, barristers, solicitors
noun
