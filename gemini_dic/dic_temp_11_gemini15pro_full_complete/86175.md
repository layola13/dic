heartthrob: /ˈhɑːrtθrɒb/ (US) /ˈhɑːtθrɒb/ (UK)| n. |万人迷；令人倾慕的人|A person, typically a celebrity, who inspires adoration and romantic interest, especially among young people.  Someone who causes hearts to throb.|例句：He was a teenage heartthrob in the 1990s. 他是90年代的青少年偶像。|例句：The young actor quickly became a heartthrob after his first film.  这位年轻演员在第一部电影之后迅速成为了万人迷。|近义词：idol, crush, darling, sweetheart|反义词: bore, nobody|词性: noun
