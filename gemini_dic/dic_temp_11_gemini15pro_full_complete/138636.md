nucleus:/ˈnjuːkliəs/ (US), /ˈnjuːklɪəs/ (UK)| n. |原子核; 细胞核; 核心; 中心|The central and most important part of an object, movement, or group, forming the basis for its activity and growth. |The nucleus of an atom contains protons and neutrons. 原子的原子核包含质子和中子。|Synonyms: core, center, heart, kernel |Antonyms: periphery, edge, margin|noun
