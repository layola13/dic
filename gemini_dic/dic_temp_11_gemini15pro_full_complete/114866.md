mammon: /ˈmæmən/ | n. | 财富，钱财（尤指被认为是罪恶之源的） | Wealth regarded as an evil influence or false object of worship and devotion. | He was so consumed by mammon that he neglected his family and friends. 他被金钱蒙蔽了双眼，以至于忽略了家人和朋友。 |  synonyms: riches, wealth, money, lucre | antonyms: poverty, destitution | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mammona, from Ecclesiastical Greek mamōnās, from Aramaic māmōnā (“wealth, riches”).
