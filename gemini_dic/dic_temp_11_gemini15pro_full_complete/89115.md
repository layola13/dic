hombres:/ˈɔmbreɪs/ (Spanish)| n.|男人，人们 (plural)|Men, people (masculine plural in Spanish).  This word is the plural form of "hombre" (man) in Spanish. It refers exclusively to males or a group of people where the masculine form is grammatically required. |例句：Los hombres están trabajando en el campo. The men are working in the field.|近义词：(depending on context) chicos (guys, informal), varones (males, more formal), personas (people, gender-neutral)|反义词:mujeres (women)|词性:Noun
