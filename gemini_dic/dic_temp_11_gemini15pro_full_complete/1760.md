acquisition:/ˌækwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 获得, 获取, 习得, 收购 |The acquisition of a new language requires dedication. 获取一门新的语言需要奉献精神。|  例句：The company's latest acquisition is a small software firm.  这家公司最新的收购是一家小型软件公司。

例句：Language acquisition is a complex process. 语言习得是一个复杂的过程。


| 近义词：gain, attainment, purchase, procurement | 反义词：loss, disposal, sale | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *acquisition (modern French acquisition), from Latin acquisītiō (“a getting, obtaining”), from acquisītus, past participle of acquīrere (“to acquire”).
