appeasements:/əˈpiːzmənts/| n. |安抚，姑息，绥靖|The policy of appeasement failed to prevent the war. 绥靖政策未能阻止战争。|例句：The appeasements offered to the aggressor only emboldened him.  提供给侵略者的安抚只会让他更加胆大妄为。

例句：Many historians criticize the appeasements made to Hitler in the 1930s. 许多历史学家批评20世纪30年代对希特勒的绥靖政策。|近义词：concessions, placations, pacifications|反义词:provocations, aggressions|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *apaisier "pacify, appease," from a- "to" (see ad-) + pais "peace," from Latin pacem (nominative pax; see peace). The -ment suffix creates nouns from verbs.
