dinners:/ˈdɪnərz/
|n.|晚餐，正餐(复数)|The main meal of the day, eaten in the evening.|We had delicious dinners at that restaurant every night. 我们每晚都在那家餐厅吃美味的晚餐。|近义词：meals, suppers|反义词：breakfasts, lunches|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French disner (meaning "breakfast"), from desjeuner (meaning "to break one's fast").  The meaning shifted to the main meal of the day, which was originally eaten at midday but gradually moved later.
