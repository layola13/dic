approximate: /əˈprɒksɪmeɪt/ | v. | 接近，近似 | to come close or be similar to something in quality, quantity, or degree. |  His description approximated the truth. 他的描述接近事实。 | approach, resemble, near | diverge from, differ from | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *approximatus*, past participle of *approximare*, "to approach," from Latin *ad* ("to") + *proximus* ("nearest").
