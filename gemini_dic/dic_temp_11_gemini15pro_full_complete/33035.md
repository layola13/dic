chosenness:/ˈtʃoʊzənnɪs/
n.|被选中,  被选择的状态|The state of being chosen or selected. |She felt a sense of chosenness, as if destiny had picked her for this task. 她感到一种被选中的感觉，仿佛命运选择她来完成这项任务。|Synonyms: selection, election, appointment|Antonyms: rejection, exclusion|noun

chosenness:/ˈtʃoʊzənnɪs/
n.|神选, 上帝的拣选|The state of being chosen by God or a higher power.|He believed in his own chosenness, convinced he was destined for greatness. 他相信自己是神选之人，确信自己注定要成就伟业。|Synonyms: predestination, divine election|Antonyms: damnation, reprobation|noun
