councilors:/ˈkaʊnsələrz/ (US) /ˈkaʊnsələz/ (UK)| n. |市议员，顾问|A member of a council, especially a city or town council.  A person who gives advice.|例句：The councilors voted to approve the new budget. 市议员投票通过了新的预算。

|例句：The company hired councilors to advise on the merger.  这家公司聘请了顾问来为合并提供建议。|近义词：advisor, consultant, representative|反义词:None|noun
