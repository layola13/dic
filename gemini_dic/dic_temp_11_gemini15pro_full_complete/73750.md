forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/ /fɔːrˈlɔːrn/| adj.|被遗弃的，孤独凄凉的，绝望的| Appearing sad and lonely; marked by or expressing hopelessness.|例句：The forlorn dog sat by the side of the road waiting for its owner. 那只被遗弃的狗坐在路边等待它的主人。|近义词：abandoned, desolate, deserted, lonely, miserable, wretched|反义词：cheerful, happy, hopeful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *forloren*, past participle of *forlesen* (“to lose completely”), from Old English *forlēosan* (“to lose, destroy, forfeit”), from Proto-Germanic *fra-liusan*, equivalent to *for-* + *lose*. Cognate with Dutch *verliezen* (“to lose”), German *verlieren* (“to lose”), Swedish *förlora* (“to lose”).
