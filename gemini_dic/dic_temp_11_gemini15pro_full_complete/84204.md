half-revealed:/ˌhæf rɪˈviːld/| adj.|半显露的，部分揭示的|Partially revealed; not fully shown or disclosed.  Something that is half-revealed is visible or known only in part.  It suggests an element of mystery or intrigue, as the full extent or nature of the thing remains hidden.|The archaeologist unearthed a half-revealed mosaic, promising further treasures beneath the sand. 考古学家发掘出一幅半显露的马赛克画，预示着沙子下面还有更多宝藏。|Synonyms: partially disclosed, partly visible, glimpsed, hinted at|Antonyms: fully revealed, completely visible, exposed, unconcealed|adjective
