mooruk:/ˈmʊərʊk/| n.|摩洛加鸡，一种印度尼西亚的鸡|A mooruk is a large, flightless bird native to the Indonesian islands of Halmahera, Morotai, and Bacan.  It is also known as the Moluccan megapode or the dusky megapode.|We saw a mooruk during our visit to Halmahera. 我们在访问哈马黑拉岛时看到了一只摩洛加鸡。|Synonym: Moluccan megapode, dusky megapode|Antonym: N/A|Noun
