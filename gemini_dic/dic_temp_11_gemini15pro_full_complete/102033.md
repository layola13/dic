isolated: /ˈaɪsəleɪtɪd/ (US) /ˈaɪsəˌleɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj.| 孤立的，隔离的，单独的|Separated from others; alone and without help or support.  Origin: From the Italian word *isolato*, past participle of *isolare* "to isolate," from *isola* "island," from Latin *insula*.|The isolated village was cut off from the rest of the world by heavy snow.  这个偏僻的村庄被大雪与世隔绝。|The scientist studied the isolated bacteria in the lab.  科学家在实验室里研究这种分离出来的细菌。|近义词：remote, secluded, solitary, lonely, detached|反义词：connected, integrated, social, surrounded|词性: 形容词
