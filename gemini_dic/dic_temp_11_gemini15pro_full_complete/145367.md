overbuy:/ˌoʊvərˈbaɪ/| v.|过度购买；买得过多|To buy more than is needed or can be used.|例句：I tend to overbuy groceries when I'm hungry. 我饿的时候往往会买太多食品杂货。

例句：She overbought on shoes this season and now has no money left. 她这季买太多鞋子了，现在没钱了。|近义词：overspend, splurge|反义词:underbuy, skimp|词性:动词
