drossiest:/ˈdrɒsiɪst/| adj.|最瞌睡的，最困倦的|Most sleepy or drowsy.|例句：After a long day of travel, I felt drossiest than I had ever felt before.  长途旅行一天后，我觉得比以往任何时候都困。

例句：The drossiest student in the class kept nodding off during the lecture. 班上最困倦的学生在讲课时不停地打瞌睡。|近义词：sleepiest, most tired, most fatigued|反义词：most alert, most awake, wide awake|词性:形容词|词源: From drowsy + -est (superlative suffix).  Drowsy itself comes from Old English *drusian, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse *drusi "sleepiness," and ultimately perhaps to "dregs," suggesting the sediment-like heaviness of sleep.
