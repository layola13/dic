impurity:/ɪmˈpjʊərɪti/ | n. |杂质，不纯；不洁；[道德方面] 污秽，邪恶 |The impurity of the water made it unsafe to drink.  水的杂质使其不安全饮用。|Synonyms: contaminant, adulterant, pollutant, defilement | Antonyms: purity, cleanliness, immaculateness | noun

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French impureté, from late Latin impuritas, from impurus ‘impure’.
