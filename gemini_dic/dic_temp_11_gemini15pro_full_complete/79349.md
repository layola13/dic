gleet:/ɡliːt/| n.|慢性尿道炎; (尤指淋病)白浊; |A thin, watery discharge from the urethra, especially as a chronic symptom of gonorrhea.|The doctor diagnosed the patient with gleet based on the persistent discharge.|近义词:discharge, exudate, secretion|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle English *glet*, from Old French *glette*, from Late Latin *glitta*, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Frankish *gleiza* (“glue”).
