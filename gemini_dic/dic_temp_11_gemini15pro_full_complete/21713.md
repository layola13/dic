boardlike:/ˈbɔːrdlaɪk/
adjective
像木板的；僵硬的；呆板的
Resembling a board, especially in being stiff, flat, and unyielding.
Her face was boardlike with shock. 她的脸因震惊而僵硬得像块木板。
The patient lay on the bed, boardlike and unresponsive. 病人躺在床上，身体僵硬，毫无反应。
近义词:stiff, rigid, inflexible, wooden
反义词:flexible, supple, pliant
词性:adjective
