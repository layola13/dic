intrinse:/ɪntrɪnz/, /ɪnˈtrɪnsɪk/ | adj. | 固有的，内在的，本质的 | Belonging naturally; essential. |  Her intrinsic kindness made her popular. 她与生俱来的善良使她很受欢迎。|  intrinsic motivation 内在动机 | synonyms: inherent, innate, immanent, built-in, ingrained | antonyms: extrinsic, external, acquired | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intrinsecus (“inwardly”), from Latin intrā (“within”) + sēcūs (“following, along”).  Compare extrinsic.
