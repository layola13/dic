borraginaceous:/ˌbɒrədʒɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
紫草科的
Relating to or characteristic of the borage family (Boraginaceae), typically having hairy leaves and small, five-petaled flowers.
The borage plant, with its vibrant blue star-shaped flowers, is a classic example of a borraginaceous species.  紫草科植物，以其鲜艳的蓝色星形花朵而闻名，是紫草科植物的典型例子。
