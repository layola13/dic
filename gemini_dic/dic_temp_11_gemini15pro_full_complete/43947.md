cribbers:/ˈkrɪbərz/| n.|抄袭者；作弊者；剽窃者|People who copy or cheat, especially in exams.  Those who plagiarize.|The teacher caught the cribbers red-handed during the exam. 老师在考试中当场抓住了作弊者。|Synonyms: cheaters,  copycats, plagiarists, frauds|Antonyms: honest students,  ethical individuals|noun

crib:/krɪb/| n. v.|婴儿床；食槽；剽窃；抄袭|A small bed for a baby; a manger; to copy or cheat.|The baby slept soundly in its crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。 He cribbed his essay from the internet. 他从网上抄袭了他的论文。|Synonyms: (n.) cot, cradle, manger; (v.) cheat, copy, plagiarize|Antonyms: (v.) originate, create|noun, verb
