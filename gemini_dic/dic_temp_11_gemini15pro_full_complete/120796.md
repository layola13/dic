misascribe:/ˌmɪsəˈskraɪb/| vt.|错误地归于，误认为|To wrongly attribute something to someone or something else. |例句：The painting was misascribed to Rembrandt for many years. 这幅画多年来被误认为是伦勃朗的作品。

例句：He misascribed his success to luck, ignoring all the hard work he had put in. 他把他的成功归因于运气，忽略了他所付出的所有努力。|近义词：misattribute, misassign|反义词：ascribe, attribute, credit|词性: 动词
