equipotential:/ˌekwɪpəˈtenʃəl/
adjective|等势的，等位的| having the same electric or gravitational potential at every point|The equipotential lines on the map showed areas of equal elevation.|Synonyms: isopotential, level, constant|Antonyms: unequal, varying|adjective

equipotential:/ˌekwɪpəˈtenʃəl/
noun|等势面，等位面|a line or surface in a field of force on which the potential is everywhere equal|The electric field is always perpendicular to the equipotential surfaces.|Synonyms: isopotential surface, level surface|Antonyms: None|noun
