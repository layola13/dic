deepener:/ˈdiːpənər/| n.|加深器；深化器|Something that makes something else deeper, either literally or figuratively.  It can refer to a tool that makes a hole deeper, a substance that intensifies a color, or something that enhances understanding or emotion.|The farmer used a subsoiler as a deepener to improve drainage.|近义词：intensifier, enhancer, amplifier|反义词:reducer, diminisher|noun
