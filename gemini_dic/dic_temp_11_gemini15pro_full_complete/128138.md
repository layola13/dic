netiquette: /ˈnetɪket/ /ˈnetɪˌkɛt/ | n. | 网络礼仪 | The correct or acceptable way of communicating on the Internet. |  Example 1: It's important to follow netiquette when posting in online forums.  遵守网络礼仪在在线论坛发帖很重要。

Example 2: Using all caps in an email is considered bad netiquette.  在电子邮件中使用全部大写字母被认为是不良的网络礼仪。 | Synonyms: online etiquette, internet etiquette | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but concepts like rudeness or cyberbullying represent the opposite behavior) | noun
