congregate: /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (British) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (American)| v. |聚集，集合|To come together in a group or crowd.|Birds congregate on the telephone wires. 鸟儿聚集在电话线上。|The students congregated in the hall for the assembly. 学生们聚集在大厅里参加集会。|近义词：assemble, gather, cluster|反义词：disperse, scatter|词性：动词

congregate: /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (British) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (American)| adj. |聚集的，集合在一起的|(less common) Gathered together into a crowd or mass.|a congregate flower head 由许多小花聚集而成的花序|This type of congregate living can be lonely. 这种群居生活可能会很孤独。|近义词：assembled, gathered, clustered|反义词：dispersed, scattered|词性：形容词
