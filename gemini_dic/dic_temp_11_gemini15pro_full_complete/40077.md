contagiousness:/kənˈteɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. | 传染性，蔓延性 | The quality of being contagious; the ease with which a disease spreads.  | The contagiousness of the new virus strain is alarming.  这种新病毒株的传染性令人担忧。 | Synonyms:  infectivity, transmissibility, communicability | Antonyms: non-contagiousness, resistance | noun

contagiousness:/kənˈteɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. | 传染性，蔓延性 |  The state of being easily spread from one person or organism to another. | The contagiousness of laughter is well-documented. 笑声的感染力是有据可查的。 | Synonyms: catching, infectious, spreading | Antonyms: non-infectious, contained | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From contagious + -ness. Contagious comes from Late Latin contagiosus "communicable by contact," from Latin contingere "to touch, seize, pollute," from com- "together" + tangere "to touch."
