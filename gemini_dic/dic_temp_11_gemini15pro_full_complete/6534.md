amygdaline:/əˈmɪɡdəlɪn/ /əˈmɪɡdəlin/ | adj. n. | 杏仁的；苦杏仁苷 | Relating to or resembling almonds; a white crystalline glucoside, C20H27NO11, extracted from the seeds of the bitter almond, apricot, and other fruits of the rose family. It yields hydrocyanic acid upon hydrolysis and is therefore highly poisonous. |

例句：
The amygdaline scent of the almond extract filled the kitchen.
厨房里充满了杏仁提取物的杏仁香味。

The cake was flavored with amygdaline.
蛋糕用杏仁調味。
