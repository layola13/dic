noncontinentals:/ˌnɒnkɒntɪˈnɛntlz/
noun
非大陆的；非大陆居民
Not of or relating to a continent; people who do not live on a continent.
The noncontinentals of the islands relied heavily on fishing. 岛上的非大陆居民主要依靠捕鱼为生。
Synonyms: islanders, island dwellers
Antonyms: continentals
词性: 名词

noncontinental:/ˌnɒnkɒntɪˈnɛntəl/
adjective
非大陆的
Not of or relating to a continent.
The noncontinental climate of the islands was more temperate. 岛屿的非大陆性气候较为温和。
Synonyms: insular, island
Antonyms: continental
词性: 形容词
