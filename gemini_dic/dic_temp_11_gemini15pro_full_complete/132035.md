noncorrosive:/ˌnɑːnkəˈroʊsɪv/ /ˌnɒnkəˈrəʊsɪv/| adj.|非腐蚀性的|Not corrosive; not causing corrosion.  It does not gradually damage or destroy something.|This paint is noncorrosive and safe for use on metal surfaces.  这种油漆是非腐蚀性的，可以安全地用于金属表面。|The noncorrosive properties of this material make it ideal for underwater applications.  这种材料的非腐蚀性使其成为水下应用的理想选择。|近义词：inert, non-reactive, resistant|反义词:corrosive, caustic|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
corrosive (adjective): From Latin corrodere "to gnaw to pieces," from com- "together" + rodere "to gnaw."
