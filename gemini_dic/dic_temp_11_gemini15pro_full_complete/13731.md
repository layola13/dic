auberge:/oʊˈbɛrʒ/ | n. | 小旅馆，客栈 | A small inn or hotel, typically one in a rural area, offering lodging and meals. |  We stayed at a charming auberge in the French countryside. 我们在法国乡村一个迷人的小旅馆住了下来。

The auberge offered a stunning view of the valley. 这家客栈可以欣赏到山谷的壮丽景色。

| 近义词: inn, hotel, lodge, guesthouse | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly 'home' or 'residence' in a contextual sense) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *alberge*, from Vulgar Latin *heribergum*, from Frankish *heriberga* "lodging, shelter," from *heri* "army" + *berg* "protection, shelter". Cognate with German *Herberge*.
