enhancing:/ɪnˈhænsɪŋ/ | adj. | 增强，提高，改进 | Increasing or improving the quality, value, or desirability of something. |  例句：Regular exercise is enhancing to overall health.  规律的运动可以增强整体健康。

例句：The new software is enhancing the productivity of the team. 新软件正在提高团队的生产力。| 近义词：improving, boosting, strengthening, augmenting | 反义词：diminishing, reducing, weakening, impairing | 词性: adjective

詞源->
From Old French "enhaucer" meaning "to raise, elevate," ultimately from Latin "altiare"  meaning "to make high."  It shares a root with "altus," meaning "high."
