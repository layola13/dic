antimediaeval:/ˌæntiˌmiːdiˈiːvəl/ | adj. | 反中世纪的，反对中世纪的 | Opposed to or rejecting the values, customs, or beliefs of the Middle Ages. |  The antimediaeval sentiment was strong during the Renaissance. 文艺复兴时期反中世纪的情绪很强烈。| synonyms: anti-medieval, modern, progressive | antonyms: medieval, mediaeval, archaic | adjective

antimediaeval:/ˌæntiˌmiːdiˈiːvəl/ | n. | 反中世纪主义者 | A person who opposes or rejects the values, customs, or beliefs of the Middle Ages. |  He was considered an antimediaeval for his embrace of humanist philosophy. 他因信奉人文主义哲学而被认为是反中世纪主义者。| synonyms: modernist, reformer, iconoclast | antonyms: traditionalist, conservative | noun
