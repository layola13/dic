hydroponicum:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪkəm/ | adj. | 水培的，无土栽培的 | Relating to hydroponics, the cultivation of plants in nutrient solutions without soil. |  Lettuce grown hydroponically is often more tender. 水培生菜通常更嫩。| Synonyms: soilless, water-cultured | Antonyms: soil-based, earth-grown | adjective

hydroponicum:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪkəm/ | n. | 水培植物 | A plant grown hydroponically. | This hydroponicum thrives in a nutrient-rich solution.  这种水培植物在营养丰富的溶液中茁壮成长。| Synonyms: hydroponic plant | Antonyms: soil-grown plant, earth-grown plant | noun
