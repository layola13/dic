mucro:/ˈmjuːkroʊ/| n. |尖端，尖头|A sharp, short point; especially, a short, straight, pointed tip, as of a leaf.|The mucro of the leaf was surprisingly sharp.  这片叶子的尖端出奇地锋利。|近义词：tip, point, apex, cusp|反义词:base, bottom|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *mucrō, *mucrōnis (“a sharp point, edge”), related to mucrōnus (“sharp, pointed”).
