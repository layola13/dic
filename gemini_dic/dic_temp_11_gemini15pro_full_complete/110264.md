lift:/lɪft/| n. v.|举起，抬起；电梯；搭便车|An act of lifting something; a device that carries people or goods between different levels of a building; a free ride in someone else's vehicle.|
The weightlifter lifted the barbell. 举重运动员举起了杠铃。
I took the lift to the tenth floor. 我乘电梯到了十楼。
Can I give you a lift to the station? 你能让我搭个便车去车站吗？|近义词：raise, elevate, hoist, boost, ride|反义词:lower, drop, descend|词性:名词, 动词

lift:/lɪft/| n. v.|心情好转，鼓舞；偷窃；抄袭|An improvement in mood or spirits; an act of stealing; plagiarism.|
The good news gave her spirits a lift.  好消息让她精神振奋。
Shoplifting is a serious crime. 商店行窃是一种严重的犯罪行为。
He lifted several paragraphs from my article. 他从我的文章中抄袭了几段。|近义词：boost, steal, plagiarize|反义词:depress, return|词性:名词, 动词
