carplike: /ˈkɑːrpˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 鲤鱼似的，像鲤鱼的 | Resembling a carp in shape or characteristics. | The fish had a carplike mouth and large scales.  这条鱼有像鲤鱼一样的嘴和大鳞片。 | Similar: cyprinoid, carp-shaped | Opposite: unlike a carp | adjective

Etymology:  From "carp" + "-like." "Carp" comes from Old French "carpe," from Late Latin "carpa," probably of Germanic origin. "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
