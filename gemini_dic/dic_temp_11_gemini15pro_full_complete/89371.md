homonymy:/həˈmɒnɪmi/ /ˈhɑːmənɪmi/| n. |同音异义；同形异义|The words "bat" (a nocturnal animal) and "bat" (a piece of sporting equipment) are examples of homonymy. |例句：The homonymy of "bank" (river bank) and "bank" (financial institution) can sometimes cause confusion. “bank”（河岸）和“bank”（金融机构）的同音异义有时会引起混淆。

例句：The poet uses homonymy to create layers of meaning in his work. 这位诗人用同音异义词在他的作品中创造多层含义。|近义词：polysemy|反义词:|词性:noun
