g'day:/ɡəˈdeɪ/
interjection
你好； 喂； 日安 (Australian informal greeting)
A contraction of "good day," used as an informal greeting, particularly in Australia.
G'day, mate! How's it going?  你好，伙计！最近怎么样？
G'day, everyone! Welcome to the barbecue.  大家好！欢迎来到烧烤派对。
近义词: hello, hi, howdy (especially in US)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a greeting)
词性: interjection

注意事项:
G'day is primarily used in informal settings in Australia. While understood elsewhere, it might sound out of place in formal situations or outside of Australia.  "G'day"主要在澳大利亚的非正式场合使用。虽然在其他地方也能理解，但在正式场合或澳大利亚以外的地方可能会显得不合适。
