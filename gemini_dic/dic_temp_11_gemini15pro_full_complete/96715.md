ineffectualnesses:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəlnəsɪz/
n.
无效；徒劳；无用
the state of being ineffectual; lack of power to produce the desired effect.
His ineffectualnesses in leadership led to the team's failure.  他领导上的无能导致了团队的失败。

The ineffectualnesses of the new policy were quickly apparent. 新政策的无效性很快就显现出来了。

近义词:futility, uselessness, impotence, inefficacy
反义词:effectiveness, efficacy, potency
