bascinet:/ˈbæsɪnɪt/| n.|盆盔，轻盔|A medieval helmet resembling a bowl, often with a visor.  It provided good protection for the head and neck. |The knight wore a gleaming bascinet into battle. 骑士戴着闪亮的盆盔上战场。|近义词：helmet, helm|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unprotected head  无保护的头部)|noun
