nonnormalities: /ˌnɒnˌnɔːrˈmælɪtiz/ | n. | 非正态性；异常情况；不正常现象 |  Deviations or departures from what is considered normal or standard.  It often refers to statistical data that doesn't follow a normal distribution, but can also be used more generally. | The researcher identified several nonnormalities in the data set. 研究人员在数据集中发现了一些异常情况。 |  irregularities, anomalies, deviations, abnormalities |  normalities, regularities, standards | noun
