directive:/dəˈrɛktɪv/, /daɪˈrɛktɪv/ | adj. n. | 指示的，指导的；指令，指示 | Giving instructions or directions.  A specific instruction or order. | The CEO issued a new directive about company spending. 首席执行官发布了关于公司支出新指令。| synonyms: instruction, order, command, decree, mandate | antonyms: suggestion, request, plea | adjective

directive:/dəˈrɛktɪv/, /daɪˈrɛktɪv/ | n. | 指令，指示 | A specific instruction or order. | The government issued a directive banning large gatherings. 政府发布了禁止大型集会的指令。 | synonyms: instruction, order, command, decree, mandate | antonyms: suggestion, request, plea | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *directivus, from Latin directus (past participle of dirigere ‘to direct’).
