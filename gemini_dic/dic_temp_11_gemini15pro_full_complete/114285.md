mainlining:/ˈmeɪnlaɪnɪŋ/
|n. / v.| (毒品)静脉注射; (比喻)沉迷; 专注于…|The act of injecting a drug directly into a vein, or figuratively, engaging intensely with something.| He was arrested for mainlining heroin. 他因静脉注射海洛因被捕。| 近义词: injecting, shooting up (drugs), absorbing, immersing oneself in|反义词: abstaining, moderating|词性: 名词/动词

mainlining:/ˈmeɪnlaɪnɪŋ/
|n. / v.|  (比喻) 沉迷；专注于…|Figuratively, engaging intensely with something, often to the exclusion of other things.|She's been mainlining Netflix all weekend. 她整个周末都在狂看Netflix。|近义词: binging, focusing intensely on, devoting oneself to|反义词: dabbling, taking a break from|词性: 名词/动词


Etymology:
From "main line," referring to the primary vein used for injection, later extended metaphorically.  源自“main line”，指的是用于注射的主要静脉，后来被比喻地扩展。
