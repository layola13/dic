hbm:/eɪtʃ biː ɛm/ |abbr.| 高带宽内存 (High Bandwidth Memory)| High Bandwidth Memory is a high-performance RAM interface used in graphics cards and other high-performance computing applications. It offers significantly higher bandwidth than traditional GDDR5 memory. |  HBM is used in many high-end GPUs.| 近义词: HMC (Hybrid Memory Cube)| 反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider older memory technologies like GDDR5 as conceptually opposite)| abbreviation
