fortitude:/ˈfɔːrtɪtjuːd/ (US) /ˈfɔːtɪtʃuːd/ (UK)| n. |毅力，坚韧，勇气|Mental and emotional strength in facing difficulty, adversity, danger, or pain courageously.|例句：She endured the long illness with remarkable fortitude. 她以非凡的毅力忍受了漫长的病痛。|例句：It took great fortitude to speak out against the injustice.  公开反对不公正需要极大的勇气。|近义词：courage, bravery, resilience, resolve, strength, grit, determination|反义词：cowardice, fear, timidity, weakness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *fortitude*, from Latin *fortitūdō* ("strength, firmness, force"), from *fortis* ("strong, brave").
