irrepealable:/ˌɪrɪˈpiːləbl/
adjective
不可废除的；不可撤销的
not capable of being repealed; unalterable
The constitution declares certain rights to be irrepealable. 宪法宣布某些权利是不可废除的。

The judge declared the sentence irrepealable. 法官宣布判决不可撤销。

Synonym: irreversible, unalterable, permanent, immutable
Antonym: repealable, revocable, reversible
词性: adjective
