overornament:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrnəmənt/ |v.|过度装饰；装饰过分|To decorate excessively; to adorn with too much ornamentation.|The architect overornamented the building with unnecessary details.  建筑师在建筑物上添加了不必要的细节，使其装饰过度。|The cake was overornamented with frosting and figurines.  蛋糕上装饰了过多的糖霜和小雕像。|近义词：overdecorate, embellish excessively|反义词: underdecorate, simplify|词性: verb

overornament:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrnəmənt/ |n.|过度装饰；过多的装饰|Excessive ornamentation; too much decoration.|The overornament of the church made it feel gaudy rather than grand. 教堂的过度装饰让它显得俗气而不是宏伟。|The overornament in the room was distracting. 房间里过多的装饰物让人分心。|近义词：excessive decoration, overdecoration|反义词: simplicity, minimalism|词性: noun
