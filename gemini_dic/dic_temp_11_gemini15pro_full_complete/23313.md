boycotter:/ˈbɔɪkɒtər/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːtər/ (US)| n.|抵制者，联合抵制者|A person who refuses to buy, use, or participate in something as a way of protesting.|Many boycotters refused to buy grapes during the farmworkers' strike. 许多抵制者在农场工人罢工期间拒绝购买葡萄。|Example: The boycotters picketed the store, urging others not to shop there. 抵制者在商店外抗议，敦促其他人不要在那里购物。|Synonyms: protester, objector, dissenter|Antonyms: supporter, participant, endorser|Noun
