aforehand:/əˈfɔːrˌhænd/| adv.|预先，事先|in advance, earlier, previously|例句：I booked the tickets beforehand. 我提前订了票。|近义词：previously, in advance, ahead of time|反义词:afterwards, later, subsequently|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *aforehand, equivalent to afore + hand.  Afore itself comes from Old English on foran, meaning "in front, before."
