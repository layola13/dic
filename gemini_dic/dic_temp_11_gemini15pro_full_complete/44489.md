crosser:/ˈkrɒsər/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsər/ (US)| n.|穿越者，杂交者|Someone or something that crosses, often referring to a person or animal that travels across something or a plant or animal that is a hybrid.|The crosser reached the other side of the river safely.  这个穿越者安全地到达了河的另一边。| The breeder developed a new crosser of roses.  育种家培育出了一种新的玫瑰杂交品种。|近义词：hybrid, traveler, migrant|反义词:|noun
