angulated:/ˈæŋɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
有角的，成角的
Having angles or sharp corners; angled.
The building has an angulated facade.  这栋建筑有一个有角的外立面。
The insect's legs were angulated.  这昆虫的腿是成角的。
近义词: angled, cornered, pointed
反义词: rounded, curved
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *angulatus, from angulus (“corner, angle”).
