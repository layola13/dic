dissolubility:/ˌdɪˌsɒljʊˈbɪləti/
noun
可溶性，溶解度
The quality or state of being dissoluble; capability of being dissolved.  The dissolubility of sugar in water is high. 糖在水中的溶解度很高。
The dissolubility of the compound in alcohol is low.  该化合物在酒精中的溶解度很低。
solubility, dissolvability
insolubility, indissolubility
n.


Etymology->
From Late Latin dissolubilitās, from dissolubilis ("dissoluble") + -itās.  Equivalent to dissoluble +‎ -ity.
