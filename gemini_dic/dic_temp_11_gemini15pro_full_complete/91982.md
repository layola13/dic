hypernaturally:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃərəli/
| adv. |超自然地，极不自然地|Existing or occurring in a way that exceeds the bounds of nature or what is considered natural. |Her acting felt hypernatural, almost too perfect to be believable. 她的表演感觉极不自然，几乎完美到令人难以置信。| The colors in the painting were hypernaturally vibrant, as if glowing from within. 这幅画中的颜色异常鲜艳，仿佛是从内部发光。 | 近义词:  supernaturally, unnaturally, preternaturally | 反义词: naturally, normally |词性:副词
