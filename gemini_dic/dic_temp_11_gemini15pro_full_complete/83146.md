gutsiness:/ˈɡʌtsinəs/| n. |勇气，胆量，魄力|The quality of being gutsy; courage and determination.|She showed great gutsiness in starting her own business. 她创办自己的公司展现出了巨大的勇气。|Synonyms: courage, bravery, boldness, nerve, pluck, grit, determination, resolve, spirit, mettle|Antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness|noun

gutsiness:/ˈɡʌtsinəs/| n. |胆识，韧性|The quality of being gutsy; courage and determination, often in the face of adversity.|He admired her gutsiness in speaking out against injustice. 他钦佩她敢于直言不讳地反对不公正。|Synonyms: fortitude, resilience, tenacity|Antonyms: weakness, fragility, vulnerability|noun

Etymology:
From gutsy + -ness.  Gutsy itself likely originates from "gut" referring to the intestines, implying deep-seated courage or resolve.
