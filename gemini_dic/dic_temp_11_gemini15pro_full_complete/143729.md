outliving:/ˌaʊtˈlɪvɪŋ/
verb
比...活得长;  比...持久
living longer than; surviving or existing longer than
She outlived her husband by twenty years. 她比她丈夫多活了二十年。
Synonyms: survive, outlast
Antonyms: predecease
