nonsentences:/ˈnɒnˌsɛntɪnsɪz/ | n. |  无意义的话；废话 |  Plural form of nonsentence, meaning utterances that do not form grammatically correct sentences or lack coherent meaning. |  The politician's speech was full of nonsentences.  这个政客的演讲充满了废话。 |  gibberish, babble, drivel |  sense, meaning | noun


Etymology: non- + sentence

non-:  from Latin *non, from Old Latin noenum "not one," from ne "not" + oinos "one."

sentence: from Old French sentence, from Latin sententia "feeling, thought, opinion, judgment, decision, vote, meaning," from sentire "to feel, perceive, think."
