词源:  "Ego-tripper" is a compound noun formed from "ego" and "tripper." "Ego" comes from Latin, meaning "I," and refers to the self. "Tripper" originally referred to someone who takes a trip or excursion. In the context of "ego-tripper," "trip" takes on the figurative meaning of being on an ego trip, an indulgence in self-importance and self-admiration.
