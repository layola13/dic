labyrinths:/ˈlæbərɪnθs/| n.|迷宫; 复杂难懂的事物|A complex network of paths or passages, often with many dead ends, designed as a puzzle or for amusement; something that is intricate and confusing.|The old city's narrow streets formed a labyrinth.  这座老城的狭窄街道形成了一个迷宫。|The bureaucratic procedures were a labyrinth of paperwork.  这些官僚程序就是一堆文书工作的迷宫。|maze, network, tangle|clarity, simplicity|noun
