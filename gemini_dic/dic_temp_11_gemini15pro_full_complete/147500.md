overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/
verb |过度包装|To wrap or enclose (something) in more layers of packaging than are necessary. |The company was criticized for overpackaging its products. 该公司因产品过度包装而受到批评。|近义词:overwrap|反义词:underpackage|词性:v.
overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/
noun |过度包装|The use of more packaging than is necessary. |Overpackaging is a major environmental problem. 过度包装是一个主要的环境问题。|近义词:excess packaging|反义词:minimal packaging|词性:n.
overpackaged:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒd/
adjective |过度包装的|Wrapped or enclosed in more layers of packaging than are necessary. |The overpackaged gift looked ridiculous.  过度包装的礼物看起来很荒谬。|近义词:excessively packaged|反义词:minimally packaged|词性:adj.
