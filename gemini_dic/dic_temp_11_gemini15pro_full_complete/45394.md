cupcakes:/ˈkʌpˌkeɪks/ (US), /ˈkʌpkeɪks/ (UK)| n. |纸杯蛋糕|A small cake baked in a cup-shaped container, often iced and decorated.|I baked a batch of cupcakes for the school bake sale. 我为学校的烘焙义卖烤了一批纸杯蛋糕。|近义词：fairy cakes (UK), muffins|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large cake)|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cup" + "cake".  The name derives from the fact that they were originally baked in small cups.
