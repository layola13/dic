litigious:/lɪˈtɪdʒəs/
adjective
好诉讼的；爱打官司的
prone to engage in lawsuits; inclined to sue
例句：The United States is known as a litigious society. 美国以爱打官司的社会而闻名。

例句：He has a litigious personality and seems to enjoy conflict. 他性格好斗，似乎很享受冲突。

近义词：argumentative, contentious, quarrelsome
反义词：conciliatory, peaceful, amicable
词性：adjective
