hydrating:/ˈhaɪdreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
保湿的，补水的
Causing or promoting hydration; increasing the water content of something.
This hydrating face mask leaves my skin feeling soft and supple.  这款保湿面膜让我的肌肤感觉柔软有弹性。
The athlete drank a hydrating sports drink after the marathon.  这位运动员在马拉松比赛后喝了补水运动饮料。
近义词:moisturizing, replenishing
反义词:dehydrating, drying
词性:adjective

hydrating:/ˈhaɪdreɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of hydrate)
使…水合，给…补水
The act of adding water to something, or becoming hydrated.
I'm hydrating my body by drinking plenty of water. 我正在通过喝大量的水来补充水分。
The lotion is hydrating my dry skin.  这款乳液正在滋润我干燥的皮肤。
近义词:moisturizing, replenishing
反义词:dehydrating, drying
词性:verb (present participle)

词源: From hydrate + -ing.  From Latin hydrāt- ‘watered’, from the verb hydrāre, from Greek hudōr ‘water’.
