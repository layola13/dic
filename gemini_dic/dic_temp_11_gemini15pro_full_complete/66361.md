excusation:/ˌɛkskjuˈseɪʃən/| n.|辩解，托辞，借口|An act of excusing or a plea offered in excuse.  The act of offering an excuse or apology for something.|He offered a weak excusation for his lateness. 他为他的迟到提出了一个苍白的借口。|近义词：excuse, apology, justification, plea, pretext|反义词：accusation, blame, condemnation|词性:noun
