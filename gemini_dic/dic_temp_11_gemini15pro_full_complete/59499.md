eastern:/ˈiːstən/ | adj. | 东方的，东部的 | Situated in, directed toward, or coming from the east. |  The eastern sky was beginning to lighten. 东方天空开始变亮。| 近义词: oriental, easterly | 反义词: western | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Old English *ēastern, from ēast (east) + -ern (suffix denoting direction).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *aus-teraz, from *aus- (out) + *-teraz (suffix).  The original meaning referred to the direction of the rising sun.
