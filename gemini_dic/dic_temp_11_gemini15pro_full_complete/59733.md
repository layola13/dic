eco-efficient:/ˈiːkəʊɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|生态效率高的，环保高效的|Relating to products, processes, or services that provide maximum value while minimizing environmental impact.  Designed to maximize the use of resources while producing minimal waste and pollution.|例句：Eco-efficient appliances can save you money on your energy bills. 生态高效的电器可以节省你的能源费用。

例句：The company is committed to developing eco-efficient manufacturing processes. 该公司致力于开发生态高效的制造工艺。|近义词：green, sustainable, environmentally friendly|反义词：wasteful, inefficient, polluting|词性:adjective
