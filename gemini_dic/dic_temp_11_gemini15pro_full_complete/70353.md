fiendlike:/ˈfiːndlaɪk/| adj.|恶魔般的，凶恶的|Resembling or characteristic of a fiend; extremely wicked or cruel.|He had a fiendlike grin on his face. 他脸上带着恶魔般的狞笑。|The dictator enforced his rule with fiendlike cruelty.  独裁者以恶魔般的残暴来执行他的统治。|近义词：diabolical, demonic, hellish, satanic, devilish|反义词：angelic, benevolent, kind, compassionate|词性:形容词
词源: From fiend +‎ -like. Fiend comes from Old English *fēond (attested as fēogan "to hate") akin to Old High German fīant "enemy," from Proto-Germanic *fijandaz, from Proto-Indo-European *pi-yont-, from the root *peyə- "to revile, criticize."
