initiations:/ɪˌnɪʃiˈeɪʃənz/
n.
入会仪式，启蒙，开始，入门
The process of formally admitting someone into a group or organization, often through a ceremony or ritual. Also, the act of beginning something.
The initiations into the secret society were shrouded in mystery.  入会仪式笼罩在神秘之中。
The initiations of new projects often require significant planning. 新项目的启动往往需要大量的计划。
近义词:beginnings, commencements, introductions, inductions
反义词:conclusions, endings, terminations
词性:名词
