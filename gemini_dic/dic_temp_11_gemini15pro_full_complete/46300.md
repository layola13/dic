d-lock:/ˈdiːlɒk/| n.|D型锁，U型锁|A D-shaped padlock used to secure bicycles and other items, often to fixed objects.|I secured my bike with a sturdy d-lock. 我用结实的D型锁锁住了我的自行车。|近义词：U-lock, bike lock, padlock|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps key?)|noun


Etymology->
"D-lock" is a descriptive term derived from the shape of the lock, resembling the letter "D".  The term "lock" comes from the Old English "loc," meaning fastening or enclosure.
