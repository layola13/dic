congregation:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 会众；人群；（教堂的）教友 | A group of people assembled for religious worship; a gathering or assembly of people. | The congregation stood to sing the hymn. 教堂的会众起立唱赞美诗。| synonyms: gathering, assembly, audience, flock | antonyms: dispersal, scattering | noun
