extirpable:/ɪkˈstɜːr.pə.bəl/
adjective
可根除的，可消灭的
capable of being extirpated or destroyed completely.
The doctor assured him that the tumor was extirpable. 医生向他保证肿瘤是可以根除的。
The weeds proved surprisingly extirpable. 这些杂草出乎意料地容易根除。

近义词:removable, eradicable, exterminable
反义词:ineradicable, incurable, permanent
词性:adjective
