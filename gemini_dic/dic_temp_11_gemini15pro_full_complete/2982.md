aegrotat:/ˌiːɡrəˈtæt/ /ˌeɪɡrəˈtæt/
n.
特许考试及格证书；因病准予考试及格
A certification that a student has been excused from an examination due to illness.  This allows them to pass the course or year without taking the exam.
He received an aegrotat after being hospitalized during exam week. 他在考试周住院后获得了特许考试及格证书。
