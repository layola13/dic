ethenyl:/ˈɛθənɪl/ | n. |乙烯基|A univalent hydrocarbon radical, -CH=CH2, derived from ethylene by removal of one hydrogen atom.  It's a common building block in polymers and other organic compounds. |Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) is made from the ethenyl chloride monomer. 聚氯乙烯 (PVC) 由氯乙烯单体制成。| synonym: vinyl | antonym: N/A | noun
