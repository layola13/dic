dreadlessness:/ˈdredləsnəs/| n.|无畏，无所畏惧|The state of being without dread; fearlessness.|He faced the challenge with utter dreadlessness. 他以完全无畏的精神迎接挑战。|近义词:fearlessness, intrepidity, courage, boldness|反义词:fear, dread, anxiety, apprehension|词性:名词

词源:
From dread + -less + -ness.  Dread comes from Old English *drǣdan "to advise, warn, urge," later "to fear, dread."  The suffix -less means "without," and -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
