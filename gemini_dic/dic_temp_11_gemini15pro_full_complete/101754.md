irrespectively:/ˌɪrɪˈspɛktɪvli/
adverb
不顾；无论；不考虑
regardless of; without regard to
He helped all the victims of the earthquake, irrespectively of their race or nationality. 他帮助了所有地震灾民，不分种族和国籍。
regardless,  without regard to
