chondrology:/kɒnˈdrɒlədʒi/
n.
软骨学
The study of cartilage.
Chondrology is important for understanding joint health.  软骨学对于理解关节健康很重要。
Chondrology helps us understand the development and diseases of cartilage. 软骨学帮助我们理解软骨的发育和疾病。
Synonym:  cartilage biology
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider bone study/osteology as a contrasting field)
词性: noun
