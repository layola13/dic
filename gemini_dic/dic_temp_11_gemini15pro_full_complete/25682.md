bungling:/ˈbʌŋɡlɪŋ/ | adj. n.|笨拙的，拙劣的；笨手笨脚的；失败|Showing or marked by clumsiness or incompetence.  A bungled action or piece of work.| His bungling attempt to fix the sink resulted in a flooded kitchen. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理水槽，结果厨房被水淹了。 |Synonyms: clumsy, inept, awkward, incompetent, maladroit|Antonyms: skillful, adept, competent, proficient|adjective

bungling:/ˈbʌŋɡlɪŋ/ | n.|笨拙的处理；拙劣的工作|A bungled action or piece of work.|The bungling of the investigation led to the real culprit going free. 对调查的拙劣处理导致真正的罪犯逍遥法外。|Synonyms: mismanagement, botch, mess|Antonyms: success, triumph, proficiency|noun
