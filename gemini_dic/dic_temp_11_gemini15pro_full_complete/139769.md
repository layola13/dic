octahedrite: /ˌɒktəˈhiːdraɪt/ | n. | 八面体铁陨石 | A type of iron meteorite that exhibits an octahedral crystalline structure when etched.  It is the most common structural class of iron meteorites. |  This octahedrite meteorite is a beautiful example of the Widmanstätten pattern. 这块八面体铁陨石是维斯台登构造的美丽例子。

The museum acquired a rare octahedrite from the Campo del Cielo meteorite field.  博物馆从坎波德尔谢洛陨石场获得了一块稀有的八面体铁陨石。 | 近义词: iron meteorite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
