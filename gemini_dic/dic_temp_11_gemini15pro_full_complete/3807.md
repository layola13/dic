agnosias:/æɡˈnoʊziəz/| n. |失认症|Loss of the ability to interpret sensory stimuli, such as sounds or images, although the specific sense is not defective and there is no significant memory loss.|The patient exhibited visual agnosias, unable to recognize familiar objects despite having perfect eyesight.|近义词:agnosia (singular form)|反义词:recognition|词性:noun
