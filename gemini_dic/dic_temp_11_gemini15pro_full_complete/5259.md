allogamous:/əˈlɒɡəməs/| adj.|异花授粉的，异体受精的|Refers to plants that are fertilized by pollen from a different plant, rather than self-fertilization.|Allogamous plants rely on pollinators like insects or wind. 异花授粉植物依靠昆虫或风等传粉者。|The apple tree is allogamous and requires cross-pollination. 苹果树是异花授粉的，需要异花授粉。|近义词：cross-fertilizing, outcrossing|反义词：autogamous, self-fertilizing|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek "allos" (other) + "gamos" (marriage). 来自希腊语“allos”（其他） + “gamos”（婚姻）。
