indorser:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər/ | n. | 背书人；签署人；认可者 | Someone who signs their name on the back of a check or other document, usually to transfer ownership or guarantee payment.  This act is called endorsement. |  The indorser of the check was John Smith. 支票的背书人是约翰·史密斯。| synonyms: signer, endorser, guarantor, supporter | antonyms: opponent, objector | noun

indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. | 背书；签署；认可；赞同 | To sign one's name on the back of a check, bill of exchange, or other negotiable instrument, usually to transfer ownership or guarantee payment.  Also, to express approval or support of something. |  She indorsed the check and deposited it. 她背书了支票并存入了银行。 He indorsed the candidate for mayor. 他认可了这位市长候选人。 | synonyms: sign, endorse, approve, support, sanction, ratify | antonyms: oppose, reject, disapprove | verb
