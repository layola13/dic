engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ (US) / /ɛnˈɡeɪdʒ (UK)/| v. | 参与，从事；雇佣；吸引；订婚 | To participate or become involved in; to hire or employ; to attract and hold the attention of; to become betrothed. |  She engaged in a lively debate. 她参与了一场激烈的辩论。 | synonyms:  involve, participate, occupy, hire, employ, attract, captivate | antonyms:  disengage, detach, ignore, dismiss, repel | verb

engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ (US) / /ɛnˈɡeɪdʒ (UK)/| adj. | 已订婚的 | Betrothed, promised in marriage. | They are an engaged couple.  他们是订婚的一对。 | synonyms:  betrothed, promised | antonyms:  single, unattached | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enga(i)gier "pledge, promise, engage,"  from en- "in" + gage "pledge," from Frankish *wadi "pledge," from Proto-Germanic *wadi.
