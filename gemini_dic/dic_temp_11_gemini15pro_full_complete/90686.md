Deceptive or misleading words or actions intended to trick someone.
The salesman's promises were nothing but a bunch of humbuggeries.  这个销售员的承诺不过是一堆骗人的鬼话。

synonyms:  trickery, deception, fraud, chicanery, shenanigans, scams
antonyms:  honesty, truth, sincerity, genuineness
