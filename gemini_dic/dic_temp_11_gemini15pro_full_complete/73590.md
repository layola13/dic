foretellable:/fɔːrˈtɛləbl/
adjective
可预言的，可预测的
able to be foretold; predictable.
The ending of the movie was entirely foretellable.  这部电影的结局完全可以预料。
The economic downturn was foretellable given the market conditions. 考虑到市场情况，经济衰退是可以预见的。
近义词:predictable, foreseeable, anticipated
反义词:unpredictable, unforeseen, unexpected
词性:adjective
词源:fore- (before) + tell + -able (capable of being)
