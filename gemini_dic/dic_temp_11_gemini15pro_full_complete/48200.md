decompressors:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsərz/| n.|减压器，解压缩器|A device or program that reduces the pressure of a gas or expands compressed data.|The divers used decompressors after their deep dive. 潜水员在深潜后使用了减压器。|synonyms: extractors, expanders|antonyms: compressors, compactors|noun
decompressor:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsər/| n.|减压器，解压缩器|A device or program that reduces the pressure of a gas or expands compressed data.|The file was too large, so I used a decompressor.  这个文件太大了，所以我使用了一个解压缩器。|synonyms: extractors, expanders|antonyms: compressors, compactors|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + compress + -or (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or device).  Compress comes from Latin "compressus," past participle of "comprimere" (to press together).
