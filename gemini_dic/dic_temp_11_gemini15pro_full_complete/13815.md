auditees:/ˌɔːdɪˈtiːz/
noun
被审计方，受审核者
People or organizations that are being audited.
The auditees were nervous during the external audit. 被审计方在外部审计期间很紧张。
Internal auditees are required to cooperate fully with the audit team.  内部被审计方必须与审计团队充分合作。

synonym: inspected parties, subjects of audit
antonym: auditors, examiners
词性: 名词
