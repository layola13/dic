harrowingly:/ˈhærəʊɪŋli/ (US) /ˈhærəʊɪŋli/ (UK)| adv.|令人痛苦地，令人 harrowing 的地|in a way that makes you feel very upset or frightened because you are experiencing something unpleasant or witnessing something unpleasant happening to someone else|The film harrowingly depicts the plight of refugees. 这部电影令人痛苦地描绘了难民的困境。|Example:  The survivors told a harrowingly vivid account of the disaster. 幸存者讲述了这场灾难的令人痛苦的生动描述。

Example: The book harrowingly portrays the horrors of war. 这本书令人痛苦地描绘了战争的恐怖。|Synonyms: distressingly, disturbingly, agonizingly, traumatically|Antonyms:  comfortingly, soothingly, pleasantly|Word Class: Adverb
