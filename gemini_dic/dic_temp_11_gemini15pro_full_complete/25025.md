bruv:/brʌv/| n.|哥们儿，兄弟 (主要用于英国，非正式)|A shortened form of "brother," used as a term of address for a male friend or acquaintance.|例句：Alright, bruv?  好的，哥们儿？

例句：What's up, bruv?  怎么了，兄弟？|近义词：mate, pal, dude, bro|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a term of endearment)|Noun
