imbecile: /ˈɪmbəsɪl/ (US), /ˈɪmbəˌsiːl/ (UK)| n. adj.|低能者，愚蠢的人；愚蠢的，低能的|A person who behaves in a stupid or childish way; stupid and childish.|He called me an imbecile for forgetting the keys. 他因为我忘了钥匙就叫我低能儿。|近义词：fool, idiot, moron, stupid, foolish|反义词：genius, intelligent, smart|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From French imbécile, from Latin imbecillus "weak," from in- "not" + baculum "staff." Originally referred to physical weakness, later evolving to denote mental deficiency.
