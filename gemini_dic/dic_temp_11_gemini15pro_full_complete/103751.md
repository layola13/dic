joule:/dʒuːl/
noun
焦耳
The SI unit of work or energy, equal to the work done by a force of one newton when its point of application moves one meter in the direction of action of the force, equivalent to one 3600th of a watt-hour.
One joule is the amount of energy required to lift a small apple one meter vertically from the Earth's surface.
一焦耳是将一个小苹果从地球表面垂直向上提升一米所需的能量。
Synonyms: energy unit, work unit
Antonyms: N/A (Units typically don't have direct antonyms)
