disconsolate:/dɪsˈkɒnsələt/ (US) /ˌdɪskənˈsəʊlət/ (UK)| adj.|  heartbroken, dejected, inconsolable, cheerless, desolate, sorrowful, sad, unhappy| Without consolation or comfort; hopelessly unhappy; cheerless.|

例句：
He was disconsolate after his team lost the championship game.
他输掉了冠军赛后非常沮丧。

She wandered the streets, disconsolate after losing her job.
她丢了工作后，在街上游荡，伤心欲绝。


近义词：
heartbroken, dejected, inconsolable, cheerless, desolate, sorrowful

反义词：
cheerful, happy, joyful, elated, contented

词性:
adjective
