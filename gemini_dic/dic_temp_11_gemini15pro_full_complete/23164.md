bovine:/ˈboʊvaɪn/ (US), /ˈbəʊvaɪn/ (UK)| adjective, noun|牛的；迟钝的；似牛的；牛科动物|Relating to cows or cattle; sluggish and dull.|例句：The farmer owned a large herd of bovine animals. 农场主拥有一大群牛。|近义词：cowlike, oxlike, stolid, placid|反义词：alert, lively, intelligent, quick-witted|词性: adjective, noun

bovine:/ˈboʊvaɪn/ (US), /ˈbəʊvaɪn/ (UK)| noun|牛科动物|A bovine animal, such as a cow, bull, or ox.|例句：The veterinarian specialized in the care of bovines.  这位兽医专门负责牛科动物的护理。|近义词：cattle, cow, ox, bull|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bovinus*, from *bos, bovis* "ox, cow."
