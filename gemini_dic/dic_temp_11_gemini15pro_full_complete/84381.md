halituous:/həˈlɪtʃuəs/| adj.|蒸汽的；呼出的；雾状的；恶臭的|Of, relating to, or resembling a vapor or exhalation; vaporous.  Often used to describe unpleasant smells or atmospheres.|The halituous swamp air hung heavy and thick. 蒸汽弥漫的沼泽空气又湿又闷。|近义词：vaporous, gaseous, miasmic, noxious|反义词:fresh, clean, pure|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *halitus* "breath, exhalation, vapor," related to *halare* "to breathe."
