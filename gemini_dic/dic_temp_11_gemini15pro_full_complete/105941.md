clicks:/klɪks/ | n. v. |点击，点击声，发出咔哒声|A click is a short, sharp sound, such as the sound made by a switch or a key on a computer keyboard when it is pressed.  As a verb, it means to make such a sound or to press a button on a computer mouse. |
The mouse clicks registered on the screen. 鼠标点击在屏幕上显示出来。|
He clicked his fingers to get the waiter's attention. 他打了个响指，以引起服务员的注意。 |
近义词：taps, snaps, sounds, presses (v.) |
反义词： drags (v., for mouse actions) |
词性：noun, verb
