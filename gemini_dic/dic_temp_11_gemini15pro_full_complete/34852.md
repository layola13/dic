clinkering:/ˈklɪŋkərɪŋ/
n.|熔渣形成；烧结|The process of forming clinkers, hard masses of fused material. |The clinkering of the ash made it difficult to remove from the furnace. 灰渣的烧结使其难以从炉中清除。|Synonyms: sintering, agglomeration, fusion|Antonyms: disintegration, separation|noun

clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/
n.|熔渣；熟料|A hard, stony mass formed by the fusion of material during heating, especially in a furnace.|The blacksmith removed the clinker from the forge. 铁匠从锻炉中取出了熔渣。|Synonyms: slag, cinder, dross|Antonyms: ore, raw material|noun

clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/
v.|（使）熔渣形成；（使）烧结|To form clinker; to become fused together into a hard mass.|The coal clinkered in the bottom of the furnace. 煤在炉底烧结了。|Synonyms: sinter, fuse, agglomerate|Antonyms: disintegrate, separate|verb
