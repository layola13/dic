frigefactive:/ˌfraɪdʒɪˈfæktɪv/| adj.|致冷的，产生寒冷的|Causing or marked by coldness; chilling.  Relating to the production of cold.|The frigefactive effect of the menthol spray soothed the burn.|近义词:cooling, chilling, refrigerant|反义词:heating, warming|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *frīgefactīvus, from frīgefactus (past participle of frīgefaciō, “I make cold”), from frīgus (“cold”) + faciō (“I make, I do”).
