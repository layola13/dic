leatherwood: /ˈleðərwʊd/ | n. | 皮革木；瑞香科植物 | An evergreen shrub or small tree (Eucryphia lucida) of Tasmania, having fragrant white flowers and yielding a dark honey.  A type of honey produced from the nectar of the leatherwood tree. |  The leatherwood tree is native to Tasmania. 皮革木树原产于塔斯马尼亚。  Leatherwood honey has a unique, spicy flavor. 皮革木蜂蜜有一种独特的辛辣味。 | Synonyms: leatherwood tree,  Eucryphia lucida | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
