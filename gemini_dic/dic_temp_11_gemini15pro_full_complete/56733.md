doorless:/ˈdɔːrlɪs/
adjective
无门的
lacking a door; without a door
The doorless entrance to the cafe made it feel open and inviting.  这家咖啡馆没有门的入口让人感觉开放和诱人。
The old shack was little more than a doorless room with a dirt floor.  这间旧棚屋只不过是一间没有门、泥土地板的房间。
synonyms: open, unobstructed, ungated
antonyms: gated, closed, secured
词性: adjective
