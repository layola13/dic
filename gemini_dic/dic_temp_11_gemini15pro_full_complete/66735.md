exorcisers:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzərz/ | n. |驱魔人|People who expel or attempt to expel evil spirits from a person, place, or thing. |The exorcisers performed a ritual to cleanse the house. 驱魔师们举行了一个仪式来净化这栋房子。|Synonyms:  expellers of demons, ghostbusters, spiritual cleansers|Antonyms: summoners, conjurers | noun

exorcisers:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzərz/ | n. |驱邪者|People who expel or attempt to expel evil spirits from a person, place, or thing. |The exorcisers chanted ancient prayers to banish the malevolent entity. 驱邪者吟唱古老的祈祷文来驱逐恶灵。|Synonyms:  casters out, demonologists|Antonyms: necromancers | noun
