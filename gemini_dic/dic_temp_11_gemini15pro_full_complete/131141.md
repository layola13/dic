nonblooming:/ˌnɒnˈbluːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 不开花的，未开花的 | Not blooming; failing to bloom. | The nonblooming orchid was a disappointment. 这株不开花的兰花真令人失望。| The nonblooming rose bush didn't add much color to the garden. 这棵不开花的玫瑰丛没有给花园增添多少色彩。 | 近义词: flowerless, barren, sterile | 反义词: blooming, flowering, blossoming | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not".
blooming (present participle of bloom):  from Old Norse *blōma "flower, blossom".
