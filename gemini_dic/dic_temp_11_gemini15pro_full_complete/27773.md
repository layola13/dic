cannibalises:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪzɪz/| v. |蚕食，削弱，拆用...的配件| To reduce the sales of one product by introducing a similar, newer product; to take parts from one machine to repair another.| The introduction of the new phone model cannibalised sales of the older one. 新手机型号的推出蚕食了旧型号的销量。| synonyms: undermine, erode, damage, hurt, weaken | antonyms: support, strengthen, enhance, boost | verb
