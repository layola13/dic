freezingly:/ˈfriːzɪŋli/| adv.|极其寒冷地，冰冷地|In an extremely cold manner.  It describes something that is so cold it causes a freezing sensation.|The wind blew freezingly across the plains.  风冰冷地吹过平原。|近义词：bitterly, icily, frigidly|反义词:warmly, heatedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "freezing" (present participle of "freeze") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Freeze" comes from Old English "freosan."
