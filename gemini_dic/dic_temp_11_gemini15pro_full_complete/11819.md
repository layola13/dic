arrayers:/əˈreɪərz/| n. |排列器，阵列器|People or machines that arrange things, especially in arrays.  This word is not commonly used, and "array" is typically used as a verb when referring to the action of arranging something.|The arrayers quickly organized the troops into formation. 阵列器迅速地将部队组织成队形。|The automatic arrayers positioned the components on the circuit board. 自动阵列器将元件放置在电路板上。|近义词：arrangers, organizers|反义词: disorganizers, scatterers| noun
