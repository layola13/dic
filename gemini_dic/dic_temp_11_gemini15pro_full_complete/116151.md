mascagnite:/mæˈskænɪt/ | n. | 马斯卡尼石，硫酸铵矿 | A naturally occurring mineral form of ammonium sulfate, (NH4)2SO4. It is typically found around volcanic fumaroles and in burning coal dumps. | Mascagnite is a common mineral found near volcanoes. 马斯卡尼石是一种常见于火山附近的矿物。 | Mascagnite deposits can be a source of ammonium sulfate for industrial use. 马斯卡尼石矿床可以作为工业用硫酸铵的来源。 |  Synonyms: ammonium sulfate | Antonyms: None | noun
