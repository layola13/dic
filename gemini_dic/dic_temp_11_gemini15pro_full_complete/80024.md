go-to:/ˈɡoʊ tuː/ (American) /ˈɡəʊ tuː/ (British)| adj.|首选的，必备的，得力的|A go-to person or thing is someone or something that you can rely on or use in a particular situation.|例句：He's my go-to guy for computer problems. 他是我解决电脑问题的得力助手。|近义词：reliable, dependable, favorite|反义词：unusable, unreliable|词性:形容词

go-to:/ˈɡoʊ tuː/ (American) /ˈɡəʊ tuː/ (British)| n.|首选，必备之物|Something that is regularly or readily used or relied on.|例句：This website is my go-to for news. 这个网站是我获取新闻的首选。|近义词：staple, essential|反义词：alternative|词性:名词
