disallowances:/dɪsəˈlaʊənsɪz/
n.
不允许; 扣除额; 折扣; 不认可
Amounts that are not allowed or that are deducted.  The act of disallowing something.

例句：
The company's disallowances for travel expenses were very strict.
这家公司对差旅费的扣除非常严格。

The tax disallowances reduced his net income.
税务扣除额减少了他的净收入。


近义词：deductions, reductions, rejections
反义词：allowances, approvals, permissions
词性: 名词(plural)
