oreosoma:/ˌɔːriːoʊˈsoʊmə/ | n. |  矿体;矿石体 | A body of ore; a mineral deposit. |  The geologists discovered a large oreosoma of copper. 地质学家发现了一个大型铜矿体。| synonym: ore deposit, orebody, lode | antonym: gangue, waste rock | noun

oreosoma:/ˌɔːriːoʊˈsoʊmə/ | n. | 矿石体;矿脉 | A naturally occurring solid material from which a metal or valuable mineral can be profitably extracted.  | The oreosoma was rich in gold. 这条矿脉富含黄金。| synonym: vein, deposit, seam | antonym: barren rock | noun
