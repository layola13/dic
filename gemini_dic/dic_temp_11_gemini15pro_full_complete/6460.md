例句：Her amplitudinous gown flowed gracefully behind her.  她宽大的礼服在她身后优雅地飘动。|近义词：vast, spacious, immense, capacious, extensive|反义词：small, narrow, limited, restricted|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin "amplitudo" (width, breadth, size) + "-inous" (suffix forming adjectives).
