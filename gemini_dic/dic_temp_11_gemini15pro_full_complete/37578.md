communalized:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzd/
v. | 使公有化，使集体化，使共有 | To make something communal; to transfer something to communal ownership or control. | The government communalized the farmland. 政府将农田公有化了。| collectivized, socialized | privatized | verb

communalized:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzd/
adj. | 公有化的，集体化的，共有的 | Characterized by communal ownership or control. | The communalized farming system failed to produce enough food. 公有化的农业系统未能生产足够的粮食。| collective, shared | private | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From communal + -ize.  Communal comes from Late Latin *communalis, from Latin communis "common."  The suffix -ize comes from French -iser, from Latin -izare, from Greek -izein.
