bad-mouths:/ˌbædˈmaʊðz/ | v. | 诋毁，说坏话 | To criticize someone or something severely and unfairly, often spreading negative information about them. | He's always bad-mouthing his competitors. 他总是诋毁他的竞争对手。|  She bad-mouthed her ex-husband to anyone who would listen. 她向任何愿意听的人说她前夫的坏话。| 近义词：criticize, denigrate, slander, malign, defame | 反义词：praise, commend, compliment | 词性: 动词

bad-mouth:/ˌbædˈmaʊθ/ | n. | 诋毁，说坏话（名词形式较少用） | The act of criticizing someone or something unfairly. |  His constant bad-mouth was starting to annoy everyone. 他不断的诋毁开始惹恼每个人了。 | 近义词：criticism, denigration, slander, defamation | 反义词：praise, commendation, compliment | 词性: 名词
