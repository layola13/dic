day-release:/ˈdeɪ rɪˈliːs/
n.|日间释放；半脱产|A system where someone works or studies for part of the week and is in prison or another institution for the rest.|He's on day-release from prison, working at a local garage. 他从监狱获得日间释放，在当地一家汽车修理厂工作。|
|近义词: part-time study, work-release|反义词: full-time imprisonment, full-time study|


day-release:/ˈdeɪ rɪˈliːs/
adj.|日间释放的；半脱产的|Relating to or involving a system where someone works or studies for part of the week and is in prison or another institution for the rest.|He's a day-release student at the local college. 他是当地学院的半脱产学生。|
|近义词: part-time, semi-detached|反义词: full-time, resident|
