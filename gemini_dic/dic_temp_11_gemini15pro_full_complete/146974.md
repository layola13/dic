overjoyful: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfəl/ | adj. | 欣喜若狂的，极度高兴的 | Extremely happy; full of joy. | She was overjoyful at the news of her son's graduation. 她听到儿子毕业的消息欣喜若狂。|  The children were overjoyful to see the snow. 孩子们看到雪欣喜若狂。 | 近义词：ecstatic, elated, jubilant, thrilled | 反义词：dejected, miserable, sorrowful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From over- + joyful.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "to a great degree." "Joyful" comes from "joy" (from Old French "joie") + "-ful" (meaning "full of").
