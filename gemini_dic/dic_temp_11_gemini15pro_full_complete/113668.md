machos:/ˈmɑːtʃoʊz/| adj.|大男子主义的，男子气概的（复数）|Having or showing qualities traditionally associated with men, especially physical strength, courage, and assertiveness.|They have very macho attitudes. 他们有大男子主义的态度。|Synonyms: manly, masculine|Antonyms: feminine, effeminate|Adjective
