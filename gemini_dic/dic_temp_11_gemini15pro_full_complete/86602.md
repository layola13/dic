heliometer:/ˌhiːliˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 日冕仪，太阳测量仪 | An instrument for measuring the diameter of the sun, or, more commonly, small angular distances between celestial objects.  It works by splitting the image of a celestial body with a split lens, and then adjusting the two halves until the images coincide. | Astronomers used a heliometer to precisely measure the diameter of Venus during its transit across the sun. 天文学家用日冕仪精确测量了金星凌日时的直径。

The heliometer provided crucial data for calculating the astronomical unit. 日冕仪为计算天文单位提供了关键数据。| synonyms: astrometer | antonyms: N/A | noun
