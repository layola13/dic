endanger:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒər/ | v. | 危害，使处于危险 | To expose to danger; imperil. |例句：Smoking endangers your health. 吸烟危害你的健康。

例句：The oil spill endangered thousands of birds. 石油泄漏危及数千只鸟类。

近义词：imperil, jeopardize, threaten, risk | 反义词：protect, safeguard, secure | 词性: 动词
