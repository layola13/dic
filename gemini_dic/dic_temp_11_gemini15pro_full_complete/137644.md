nonunanimous:/ˌnɑːnjuˈnænɪməs/ | adj. |不一致的，非一致通过的 |Not unanimous; not having the agreement of all. | The jury returned a nonunanimous verdict. 陪审团作出了非一致性裁决。| The decision was nonunanimous, with two members dissenting.  这项决定并非一致通过，有两名成员表示异议。 |Near synonyms: divided, dissenting, disagreeing, discordant| Antonyms: unanimous, united, agreed, concordant |adjective
