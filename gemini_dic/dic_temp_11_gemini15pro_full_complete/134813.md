nonmillionaire:/ˌnɒnˌmɪljəˈneər/ | n. | 非百万富翁，非富翁 |  A person who is not a millionaire, i.e., does not possess a net worth of one million dollars (or equivalent in another currency). |  He considered himself a nonmillionaire, even though he owned a successful small business. 他认为自己不是百万富翁，即使他拥有一家成功的小企业。 |  commoner, average person | millionaire, billionaire | noun
