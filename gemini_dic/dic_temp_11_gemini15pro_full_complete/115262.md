manipulator:/məˈnɪpjəleɪtər/| n.|操纵者，控制者|A person who controls or influences others skillfully, often unfairly or unscrupulously.|The politician was a master manipulator of public opinion. 这位政治家是操纵公众舆论的大师。|Synonyms: operator, controller, machinator, intriguer, schemer|Antonyms: pawn, puppet, dupe|Noun

manipulators:/məˈnɪpjəleɪtərz/| n.|操纵者们，控制者们|Plural form of "manipulator," referring to multiple individuals who control or influence others.|The manipulators behind the scenes were finally exposed. 幕后操纵者终于被曝光了。|Synonyms: operators, controllers, machinators, intriguers, schemers|Antonyms: pawns, puppets, dupes|Noun
