nonpracticalness:/ˌnɒnˈpræktɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 不实用性，不切实际 | The nonpracticalness of his invention was evident from the start.  他的发明从一开始就不实用。 | Examples: His plan was dismissed due to its sheer nonpracticalness. 他的计划因为完全不切实际而被驳回。  The nonpracticalness of wearing a ball gown to a hiking trip was obvious. 穿着舞会礼服去徒步旅行显然是不切实际的。 | Synonyms: impracticality, unfeasibility, uselessness | Antonyms: practicality, feasibility, usefulness | noun
