acceleratedly: /əkˈsɛləreɪtɪdli/
adverb
加速地，加快地
In a manner that increases speed or rate.
The car acceleratedly sped down the hill. 汽车加速地冲下山坡。
The project progressed acceleratedly after the new manager took over.  新经理接手后，项目进展加快。
近义词: rapidly, swiftly, quickly, speedily, hastily
反义词: slowly, gradually, leisurely
词性: adverb

词源:
From "accelerated" + "-ly."  "Accelerated" comes from the Latin "acceleratus," the past participle of "accelerare," meaning "to hasten," from "ad-" (to) + "celer" (swift).
