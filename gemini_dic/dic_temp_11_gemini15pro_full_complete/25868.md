burkini: /bərˈkiːni/ /bɜːrˈkiːni/ | n. |  布基尼泳衣 | A swimsuit that covers the whole body except the face, hands and feet, worn by some Muslim women. |  Example: She wore a burkini to the beach. 她穿着布基尼泳衣去了海滩。| Example:  The burkini allows her to swim while adhering to her religious beliefs. 布基尼泳衣让她可以在遵守宗教信仰的同时游泳。 | Synonym: full-body swimsuit, modest swimwear | Antonym: bikini, swimsuit | noun
