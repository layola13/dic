miaow's:/ˈmiːaʊz/| n.|猫叫声|The possessive form of "miaow," indicating the sound belongs to a cat (or perhaps metaphorically, to something cat-like). It signifies the act of meowing as belonging to a specific cat or entity.  |例句：The kitten's miaow's were barely audible. 小猫的叫声几乎听不见。

例句：I could hear the cat's miaow's from outside. 我能听到猫从外面传来的叫声。|近义词：meows, purrs (if soft), cries, calls|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider silence, barks, howls)|词性:noun
