interlocker:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːkər/ | n. | 联锁装置，联锁器 | A safety device that prevents a machine from operating unless certain conditions are met, often used in railway signaling and industrial settings. | The interlocker prevented the train from entering the occupied section of track.  联锁装置阻止火车进入已被占用的轨道部分。|  safeguard, safety mechanism | bypass | noun
interlock:/ˌɪntərˈlɒk/ | v. | (使)互相连接，(使)扣住 | To fit or be fitted together securely, typically by means of projections and recesses.  | The two pieces of the puzzle interlocked perfectly.  这两块拼图完美地扣合在一起。| connect, join, link | separate, disconnect | verb
