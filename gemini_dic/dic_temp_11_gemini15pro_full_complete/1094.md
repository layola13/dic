access:/ˈækses/ (US) /ˈæksɛs/ (UK)| n. vt.|通路，入口；使用权；机会；读取（计算机信息）|The ability or right to enter, approach, or use something; the act of obtaining or retrieving information from a computer system.|She has access to confidential information. 她可以接触到机密信息。|He accessed the files on his computer. 他读取了电脑上的文件。|近义词：approach, entry, admittance, retrieval|反义词：restriction, denial, prohibition|词性:名词, 动词

access:/ˈækses/ (US) /ˈæksɛs/ (UK)| v.|进入；使用；到达；读取（计算机信息）|To gain entry to; to make use of; to reach; to retrieve information from a computer system.|She accessed the files on her computer. 她读取了电脑上的文件。|He accessed the building using a key card. 他用门卡进入了大楼。|近义词：enter, use, reach, retrieve|反义词：exit, leave, depart|词性:动词
