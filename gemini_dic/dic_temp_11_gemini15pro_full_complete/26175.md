butcherliness:/ˈbʊtʃərlɪnɪs/
noun
残暴; 血腥; 屠夫的特性
The quality of being brutal, bloody, or characteristic of a butcher.
The butcherliness of the scene shocked even the seasoned detectives.  这血腥的场面即使是经验丰富的老练侦探也感到震惊。
Near synonyms: brutality, savagery, barbarity, cruelty, bloodthirstiness
Near antonyms:  kindness, gentleness, compassion, humanity, mercy
