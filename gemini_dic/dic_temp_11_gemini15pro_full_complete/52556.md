diffractive:/dɪˈfræktɪv/| adj.|衍射的；绕射的| Relating to or causing diffraction (the spreading of waves, such as light or sound, when they pass through an opening or around an obstacle).|The diffractive properties of the grating create a rainbow pattern. 光栅的衍射特性会产生彩虹图案。|The scientists studied the diffractive behavior of light waves. 科学家们研究了光波的衍射行为。|近义词: scattering, dispersive|反义词: non-diffractive|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From diffract + -ive.  Diffract comes from Latin diffractus, past participle of diffrangere "to break apart," from dis- "apart" + frangere "to break."
