nonavoidances:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdənsɪz/ | n. | 不可避免的事情；无法回避的事情 |  The state or quality of being unavoidable; things that cannot be avoided.  This word emphasizes the plural nature of unavoidable situations or events. |  The report detailed the nonavoidances of climate change, such as rising sea levels and extreme weather events. 报告详细描述了气候变化不可避免的后果，例如海平面上升和极端天气事件。

The nonavoidances of aging, like wrinkles and grey hair, are a natural part of life.  衰老的不可避免之处，如皱纹和白发，是生命中自然的一部分。| 近义词: inevitabilities, necessities | 反义词: avoidances (though less common), options, choices | 词性: 名词
