nonhierarchic:/ˌnɒnhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪk/ | adj. | 非等级的，无等级的 |  Characterized by the absence of hierarchical organization or structure; not arranged in ranks or levels. |  The company adopted a nonhierarchic structure to encourage collaboration.  这家公司采用了非等级结构来鼓励合作。 |  The team had a nonhierarchic decision-making process, with everyone's input valued equally.  团队采用非等级的决策过程，每个人的意见都得到同等重视。 |  non-hierarchical, egalitarian, democratic | hierarchical, stratified | adjective
