magnetizable:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzəbl/
adjective
可磁化的
Capable of being magnetized; susceptible to magnetism.
Iron is magnetizable. 铁是可以磁化的。
The material is highly magnetizable.  这种材料很容易被磁化。
近义词:magnetic, ferromagnetic
反义词:non-magnetic
词性:adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From magnetize + -able.  Magnetize comes from magnet + -ize. Magnet ultimately derives from the Greek "Magnes lithos" meaning "Magnesian stone," referring to lodestone found in Magnesia, a region of ancient Greece.
