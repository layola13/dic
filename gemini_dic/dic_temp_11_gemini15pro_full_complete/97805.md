injuriousnesses:/ɪnˈdʒʊəriəsnɪsɪz/| n. | 伤害，损害（复数） | The state or quality of being injurious; harmfulness.  The cumulative effect of multiple injuriousnesses can be devastating. 多种伤害的累积效应可能是毁灭性的。|例句：The injuriousnesses of the accusations were significant. 这些指控的危害性很大。|近义词：harmfulness, detrimentalness, noxiousness, perniciousness |反义词: harmlessness,  benefit, helpfulness| noun
