liberal: /ˈlɪbərəl/ (US), /ˈlɪbrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|自由主义的；慷慨的；自由的|Favoring individual liberty, free trade, and moderate political and social reform.  Generous or given freely.|例句：He has a liberal attitude towards immigration. 他对移民持开放态度。|近义词：progressive, open-minded, generous, tolerant|反义词：conservative, illiberal, stingy, intolerant|词性: adjective, noun

liberal: /ˈlɪbərəl/ (US), /ˈlɪbrəl/ (UK)| n.|自由主义者|A person with liberal views.|例句：He considers himself a liberal. 他自认为是一个自由主义者。|近义词：progressive, reformer|反义词：conservative, reactionary|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *liberal*, from Old French *liberal*, from Latin *līberālis* (“noble, generous, pertaining to a freeman”), from *līber* (“free”), related to *libet* (“it is pleasing”).
