convalescent:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesənt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesənt/| adj. n.|恢复期的，康复中的；恢复期病人|Recovering from an illness or medical treatment; a person who is recovering.|例句：The convalescent patient was gradually regaining his strength.  恢复期的病人逐渐恢复了体力。|近义词：recovering, recuperating|反义词：sick, ill|词性:形容词,名词

convalescent:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesənt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesənt/| n.|恢复期病人|A person who is recovering from an illness or medical treatment.|例句：The convalescent home provided a peaceful environment for healing.  疗养院提供了一个宁静的康复环境。|近义词：patient, invalid|反义词：healthy person|词性:名词


Etymology: From Late Latin *convalescentem, present participle of convalescere "to grow strong," from com- "together" + valescere "to grow strong."
