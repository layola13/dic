initiatively:/ɪˈnɪʃətɪvli/
adverb|主动地，积极地|In a way that demonstrates initiative; proactively. |She initiatively organized the meeting. 她主动组织了这次会议。|Near Synonyms: proactively, dynamically, enterprisingly, resourcefully|Near Antonyms: passively, reluctantly, apathetically|词性:副词

initiatively:/ɪˈnɪʃətɪvli/
adjective|主动的，积极的 (较少使用)|Showing or characterized by initiative. |She has an initiatively spirit and always looks for ways to improve things. 她有一种积极主动的精神，总是想方设法改进事情。|Near Synonyms: proactive, enterprising, self-motivated, resourceful|Near Antonyms: passive, hesitant, unmotivated|词性:形容词
