osmazome: /ˈɒzməˌzoʊm/ /ˈɑːzməˌzoʊm/ | n. | 肉味, 肉香, 肉汁 |  An extract of meat containing the flavoring constituents; a name formerly given to the water-soluble portion of meat extract, believed to be the source of the characteristic meat flavor.  | The rich osmazome of the roast filled the kitchen. 烤肉浓郁的肉香味弥漫了整个厨房。 | 近义词: aroma, flavor, savor | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider blandness, tastelessness) | 词性: noun

词源:  From French *osmazôme*, from Ancient Greek ὀσμή (*osmḗ*, “smell, odor”) + ζωμός (*zōmós*, “broth, soup”).
