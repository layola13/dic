nonconduciveness:/ˌnɑːnkənduːsɪvnəs/| n. | 不利，不适宜，不导电性 | The state or quality of not being conducive; unfavorableness. | The nonconduciveness of the environment to business growth caused many companies to relocate.  环境不利于企业发展，导致许多公司搬迁。 |  The nonconduciveness of the material made it unsuitable for electrical wiring. 这种材料的不导电性使其不适合用于电线。 | synonyms: unfavorableness, disadvantageousness, inauspiciousness, unsuitability, inappositeness | antonyms: conduciveness, favorableness, advantageousness, suitability, appropriateness | noun
