moguls: /ˈmoʊɡəlz/ (US) /ˈməʊɡəlz/ (UK) | n. | 大亨；巨头；雪丘； moguls |  Powerful and influential people, especially in the business or entertainment industry. Also refers to bumps on a ski slope. |  The moguls of Hollywood control vast media empires. 好莱坞的巨头们控制着庞大的媒体帝国。| synonyms: tycoons, magnates, VIPs, bigwigs, bosses | antonyms:  underlings, subordinates | noun

moguls: /ˈmoʊɡəlz/ (US) /ˈməʊɡəlz/ (UK) | n. | 雪丘 | Mounds of snow formed on a ski slope, especially by skiers making turns. | Skiing the moguls is a challenging and exciting experience. 滑雪道上的雪丘是一项具有挑战性和刺激性的体验。| synonyms: bumps, mounds | antonyms: smooth slope | noun
