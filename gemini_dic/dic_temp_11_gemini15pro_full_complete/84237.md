half-shouted:/ˌhɑːfˈʃaʊtɪd/ | adj. | 半喊，低声叫喊 |  Said in a voice louder than speaking but not as loud as shouting.  It implies a voice raised in urgency, excitement, or slight anger.  Formed by combining "half" with the past participle of "shout". | "He half-shouted a warning as the car veered towards the pedestrian." 他朝着那辆驶向行人的汽车半喊着发出警告。 | near synonyms:  almost shouted, muttered loudly, called out | antonyms: whispered, murmured, muttered softly | participle
