macintosh:/ˈmækɪntɒʃ/ | n. | 麦金塔电脑 | A brand of personal computer manufactured by Apple Inc., known for its user-friendly interface and innovative design. Often shortened to Mac. | 例句：She bought a new Macintosh last week. 她上周买了一台新的麦金塔电脑。

例句：The Macintosh revolutionized the personal computer industry. 麦金塔电脑彻底改变了个人电脑行业。 | 近义词：Mac, Apple computer | 反义词：PC (personal computer), Windows computer | 词性:noun


词源：Named after Jef Raskin, an Apple employee, who initially conceived the project. The name was inspired by his favorite variety of apple, the McIntosh.  However, the spelling was changed to "Macintosh" for legal reasons.
