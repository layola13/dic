clanny:/ˈklæni/| n.|克兰尼安全灯|A type of early safety lamp for miners, invented by William Clanny.  It was designed to prevent explosions caused by methane gas (firedamp).|The clanny lamp was an improvement on earlier designs, but it was still relatively heavy and cumbersome.|近义词:safety lamp, Davy lamp, Geordie lamp|反义词:N/A|noun
