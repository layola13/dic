ctenophora:/tɪˈnɒfərə/ /tɪˈnɑːfərə/
noun
栉水母动物门
A phylum of invertebrate animals, commonly known as comb jellies, characterized by eight rows of cilia they use for locomotion.
Ctenophora are found in all oceans, from the surface to the deep sea. 栉水母动物门存在于所有海洋中，从海面到深海。
Ctenophores, unlike jellyfish, do not sting. 栉水母不像水母那样会蜇人。
近义词: comb jellies
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a biological phylum)
词性:n.
