overheaping:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 过多的，堆积如山的 | Filled to overflowing; excessively full. | The table was laden with an overheaping platter of food. 桌子上摆满了堆积如山的食物。| The charity received an overheaping amount of donations.  这个慈善机构收到了堆积如山的捐款。| 近义词：overflowing, abundant, excessive, copious | 反义词：scant, meager, sparse | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "over-" (excessively) + "heap" (a pile).  "Heap" comes from Old English "hēap."
