annoyed:/əˈnɔɪd/| adj.|恼怒的，生气的|Feeling slightly angry or impatient.|例句：I was annoyed by his constant interruptions. 我被他不断的打断弄得很恼火。|例句：She was annoyed with herself for forgetting her keys. 她因为忘了带钥匙而生自己的气。|近义词：irritated, vexed, bothered, irked|反义词：pleased, delighted, happy|词性:形容词

annoy:/əˈnɔɪ/| vt.|使恼怒，打扰|To make someone slightly angry or impatient.|例句：His constant complaining annoyed me. 他不停的抱怨让我很恼火。|例句：It annoys me when people talk loudly on their phones in public.  在公共场合大声打电话的人让我很烦。|近义词：irritate, bother, vex, irk|反义词：please, delight|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *enoier, from Vulgar Latin *inodiare, from Latin inodiōsus (“causing hatred, hateful, offensive”), from in (“in, on”) + odium (“hatred”).
