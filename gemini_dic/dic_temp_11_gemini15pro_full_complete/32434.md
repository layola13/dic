chimera:/kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪərə/| n.|嵌合体；虚构的怪物；妄想|A fire-breathing female monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail in Greek mythology.  希腊神话中狮头、羊身、蛇尾的喷火女怪。|例句：The chimera of economic prosperity soon dissolved. 经济繁荣的幻想很快就破灭了。

例句：Genetic engineering has made the creation of chimeras a reality. 基因工程使创造嵌合体成为现实。|近义词：illusion, fantasy, delusion, monster|反义词：reality|词性：noun


chimera:/kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪərə/| adj.|幻想的，妄想的|Existing only as an illusion; impossible to achieve. 只存在于幻想中；不可能实现的。|例句：She chased a chimera of finding true love. 她追求着找到真爱的幻想。

例句：His plans for early retirement are completely chimerical. 他提前退休的计划完全是妄想。|近义词：illusory, unreal, fantastic, impractical|反义词：realistic, practical|词性：adjective


词源：From Late Latin chimaera, from Latin chimaera, from Greek khimaira (“she-goat,” literally “yearling she-goat”), from kheima (“winter”). 源自晚期拉丁语 chimaera，来自拉丁语 chimaera，来自希腊语 khimaira（“母山羊”，字面意思是“一岁的母山羊”），来自 kheima（“冬天”）。
