glunch:/ɡlʌntʃ/
n.|早午餐; 午餐|A combination of "lunch" and "glum," referring to a lunch eaten with little enthusiasm or in a state of sadness or disappointment.|I had a glunch today after hearing the bad news.  今天听到坏消息后，我吃了一顿没精打采的午餐。|Synonyms: dreary lunch, joyless meal|Antonyms: celebratory feast, delightful lunch|noun
