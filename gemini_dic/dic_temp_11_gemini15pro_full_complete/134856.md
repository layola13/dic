nonmodifying:/nɒnˈmɒdɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 非修饰性的；不修饰的 | Not able to modify or qualify something else; used to describe a word or phrase that does not change the meaning of the word it is associated with.  | Example:  The word "the" is a nonmodifying word.  这个词 "the" 是一个非修饰词。 | Synonym: nonrestrictive, descriptive, attributive | Antonym: modifying, restrictive, limiting | Word type: Adjective
