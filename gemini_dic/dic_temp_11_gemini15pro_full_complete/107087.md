ladening:/ˈleɪdnɪŋ/
noun
装载, 装货
The process of loading cargo onto a ship or other vehicle.
The ladening of the ship took several days.  这艘船的装载花了几天时间。
loading, freighting
unloading, discharging
n.

ladening:/ˈleɪdnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
装载，装货
The act of putting a load onto something.
They were ladening the truck with supplies. 他们正在往卡车上装载物资。
loading, filling
unloading, emptying
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From lade + -ing.  From Middle English laden, from Old English hladan (“to load, heap up, draw water”), from Proto-Germanic *hlōþaną (“to load, draw water”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear, incline”). Cognate with Dutch laden, German laden.  The -ing suffix forms the present participle and gerund of verbs.
