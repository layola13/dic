forgivingness:/fərˈɡɪvnɪs/| n.|宽恕，原谅|The quality of being willing to forgive someone who has offended you. |She showed great forgiveness towards her abuser. 她对虐待她的人表现出了极大的宽恕。|Synonyms: pardon, clemency, absolution, mercy|Antonyms: unforgiveness, resentment, rancor|noun

forgivingness:/fərˈɡɪvnɪs/| n.|宽容，谅解|The ability to overlook or excuse someone's faults or mistakes.|He admired her forgivingness and understanding. 他钦佩她的宽容和理解。|Synonyms: tolerance, acceptance, leniency|Antonyms: intolerance, condemnation, censure|noun
