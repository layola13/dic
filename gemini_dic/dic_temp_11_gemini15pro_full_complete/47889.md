decemvirship: /ˌdɛsɪmˈvɜːrʃɪp/
n.
十人执政官的职位或任期; 十人委员会
The office or term of office of a decemvir; a board of ten men.

The decemvirship was a period of political upheaval in ancient Rome.
十人执政时期是古罗马政治动荡的时期。

The decemvirship was ultimately abolished due to the tyranny of its members.
十人委员会最终因其成员的暴政而被废除。

Synonyms: commission, board, council
Antonyms: monarchy, autocracy
