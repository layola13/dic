intellect: /ˈɪntəlekt/ (US) /ˈɪntɪlekt/ (UK)| n. |智力，理解力，知识分子|The capacity for reasoning, understanding, and abstract thought.  The faculty of thinking and acquiring knowledge.  A person of high intellectual ability.|
He possesses a keen intellect. 他拥有敏锐的智力。

A man of great intellect and learning.  一位才智过人、学识渊博的人。

synonym: intelligence, mind, reason, understanding, brainpower
antonym: ignorance, stupidity, incomprehension
词性: noun
