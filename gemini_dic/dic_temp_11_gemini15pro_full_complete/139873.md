oculistic: /ˌɒkjuˈlɪstɪk/ (US) /ˌɒkjuːˈlɪstɪk/ (UK)
adjective
眼科的；关于眼睛的
Relating to the study and treatment of diseases of the eye.
例句：He sought oculistic care after experiencing blurry vision.  他视力模糊后寻求了眼科护理。

例句：Oculistic surgery has advanced significantly in recent years.  近年来，眼科手术取得了显著进展。

近义词：ophthalmic, ophthalmological
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *oculisticus, from oculus ("eye").
