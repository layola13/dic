misbestow:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊ/
verb
误给；错授；滥用
to give something, especially love or praise, to an unsuitable person or thing
He misbestowed his affections on a woman who did not deserve them. 他错把感情给了不值得爱的女人。
misplace, misuse, squander, waste
bestow appropriately, use wisely, allocate correctly
