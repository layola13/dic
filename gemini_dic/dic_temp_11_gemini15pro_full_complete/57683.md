drawlers:/ˈdrɔːlərz/ | n. | 抽屉（复数） | Drawers are a type of furniture consisting of boxes or compartments that slide in and out of a frame. They are typically used for storing clothes, linens, or other items. |  I put my socks in the top drawer. 我把袜子放在最上面的抽屉里。

My desk has three drawers. 我的桌子有三个抽屉。 | 近义词：chest of drawers, dresser, bureau | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider "open shelves" as a contrasting storage solution.) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *dragan (to draw, pull) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting instruments or agents).  The word originally referred to something that could be drawn or pulled out, hence its application to the sliding compartments in furniture.
