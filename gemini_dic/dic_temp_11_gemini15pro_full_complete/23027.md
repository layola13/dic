bouldery:/ˈboʊldəri/| adj.|多岩石的，遍布巨砾的|Covered with or characterized by large rocks or boulders.  Characterized by many large boulders.|例句：The bouldery terrain made hiking difficult. 多岩石的地形使徒步旅行变得困难。

例句：The riverbed was bouldery and slippery. 河床遍布巨砾，很滑。|近义词：rocky, stony, craggy|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词
