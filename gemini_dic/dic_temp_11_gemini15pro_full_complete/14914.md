azimuths: /ˈæzɪməθs/ | n. | 方位，方位角 |  The horizontal angle or direction of a compass bearing.  It is usually measured clockwise from north. | The surveyor measured the azimuths of several landmarks. 测量员测量了几个地标的方位角。| The azimuth of the rising sun is an important factor in navigation.  初升太阳的方位角是航海中的一个重要因素。 | synonyms: bearings, directions, headings | antonyms: N/A | noun
