antilegomena:/ˌæntɪlɪˈɡɒmɪnə/ |n.| 次经;有争议的经卷;次经书目|Books or passages of the Bible whose canonical status has been disputed.|  The Second Epistle of Peter is considered by some to be antilegomena. 彼得后书被一些人认为是次经。|  The antilegomena of the New Testament include books like 2 Peter and Jude. 新约中的次经包括彼得后书和犹大书等。 | synonyms: disputed books, apocrypha | antonyms: canonical books | noun
