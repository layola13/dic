neatify:/ˈniːtɪfaɪ/| vt.|使整洁；使整齐|To make neat and orderly; to tidy up.|She neatified her desk before leaving the office. 她离开办公室前整理了她的桌子。| He neatified the garden by mowing the lawn and trimming the hedges. 他通过修剪草坪和树篱来整理花园。|近义词：tidy, organize, arrange, clean, straighten|反义词:mess up, clutter, disorganize|词性:verb
词源: From neat + -fy.  "Neat" comes from Old French "net" meaning clean, pure, or clear.
