cushily:/ˈkʊʃɪli/
adverb
舒适地，安逸地
in a comfortable and relaxed way
She was sitting cushily on the sofa, reading a book. 她舒服地坐在沙发上看书。
comfortably, snugly, cozily
uncomfortably, awkwardly, restlessly
词性: 副词

cushy:/ˈkʊʃi/
adjective
舒适的，安逸的
comfortable and easy
He has a cushy job with a high salary. 他有一份轻松又高薪的工作。
comfortable, easy, snug
uncomfortable, difficult, demanding
词性: 形容词
