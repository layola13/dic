fusillis:/fuːˈsiːliː/ /fjuːˈsiːliː/
noun
螺旋面, 螺旋粉, 螺旋通心粉
A type of pasta that is shaped like a short, thick spiral.  It's a thicker version of rotini.
I made a delicious salad with fusilli, tomatoes, and mozzarella.  我做了一份美味的螺旋粉沙拉，配上西红柿和马苏里拉奶酪。
The fusilli pasta held the creamy pesto sauce perfectly.  螺旋粉完美地裹住了奶油香蒜酱。
synonyms: rotini, spirals, pasta
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a pasta shape)
n.
