convokes:/kənˈvoʊks/
verb
召集，召开
To call together or summon, especially for a formal meeting.
The president convokes Congress every year. 总统每年都会召开国会会议。
The chairman convoked a meeting of the board. 主席召集了董事会会议。
summon, assemble, convene
dismiss, adjourn
v.
