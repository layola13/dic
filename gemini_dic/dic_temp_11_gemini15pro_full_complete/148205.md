overshort:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɔːrt/| adj.| 过短的，长度不足的|Too short; shorter than required or desirable.  The curtains were overshort and didn't reach the floor.|例句：The dress was overshort for her. 这条裙子对她来说太短了。|近义词：too short, short, undersized|反义词:overlong, too long, oversized|词性:adjective

overshort:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɔːrt/| n.|  短缺，不足|A deficiency or shortage, especially in financial accounts.  There was an overshort in the cash register.|例句：The overshort in the inventory was significant. 库存短缺很严重。|近义词：shortfall, deficit, shortage|反义词:surplus, excess|词性:noun
