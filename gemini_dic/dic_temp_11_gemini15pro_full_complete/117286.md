mechanography:/ˌmɛkəˈnɒɡrəfi/ /ˌmɛkəˈnɑːɡrəfi/
n.
机械制图，打字
The process of producing text or other graphical representations using a machine, especially a typewriter or computer.
Mechanography played a vital role in office administration before the widespread use of computers.  机械制图在计算机普及之前在办公室管理中发挥了至关重要的作用。

She studied mechanography and shorthand at secretarial college. 她在秘书学院学习了机械制图和速记。

近义词:typing, keyboarding
反义词:handwriting, calligraphy
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mécanographie*, from *mécano-* (from *mécanique* "mechanical," from Latin *mechanicus*, from Greek *mēkhanikos*) + *-graphie* (from Greek *-graphia* "writing").
