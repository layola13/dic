bukater:/ˈbuːkeɪtər/| n.|  屠夫; 肉贩; 刽子手 | A person who slaughters animals for meat or a cruel or bloodthirsty person. |  The bukater sharpened his knife.  屠夫磨快了他的刀。| synonyms: butcher, slaughterer, meat cutter| antonyms: vegetarian, vegan | noun

bukater:/ˈbuːkeɪtər/| n.| 刽子手; 残酷的人 | A person who inflicts pain or death, especially in a violent or cruel way. |  The dictator was seen as a bukater by his people.  独裁者被人民视为刽子手。| synonyms: executioner, murderer, assassin | antonyms: savior, protector | noun
