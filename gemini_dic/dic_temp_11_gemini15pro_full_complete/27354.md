camboge:/kæmˈboʊdʒ/ | n. |藤黄，深黄色  |A gum resin obtained from various southeast Asian trees of the genus *Garcinia*, used as a pigment and purgative. |  The artist used a vibrant shade of gamboge in his painting. 艺术家在他的画作中使用了鲜艳的藤黄。

The antique chest was inlaid with intricate patterns of ebony and gamboge. 古董箱子镶嵌着乌木和藤黄的复杂图案。


|近义词：yellow, deep yellow, saffron, golden |反义词: - |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gomme gutte*, literally "gum gutta," referring to the resinous sap of the gamboge tree.  Ultimately from Khmer (Cambodian) *kâmpuciə*, referring to the country of Cambodia.
