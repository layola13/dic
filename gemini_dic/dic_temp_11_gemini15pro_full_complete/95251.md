incandescents:/ɪnkænˈdɛsənts/| n.|白炽灯，白炽物|Emitting light as a result of being heated.  Objects heated until they glow.|The soft glow of the incandescents created a warm atmosphere. 白炽灯柔和的光芒营造了温暖的氛围。|近义词：bulbs, lights|反义词: LEDs, fluorescents|Noun

incandescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/| adj.|白炽的，发白热光的；感情强烈的|Emitting light as a result of being heated.  Figuratively, characterized by strong emotion or brilliance.|The metal became incandescent in the furnace. 金属在熔炉里变得白热。|近义词：glowing, luminous, brilliant|反义词: dull, dark|Adjective
