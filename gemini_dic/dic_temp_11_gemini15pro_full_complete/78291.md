germinated: /ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtɪd/
verb, adjective
发芽的，萌发的
having developed into a plant from a seed
The seeds germinated quickly in the warm soil.  种子在温暖的土壤中迅速发芽。
近义词: sprouted, developed
反义词: dormant, undeveloped
词性: 过去分词/形容词


Etymology:
From Latin germinatus, past participle of germinare "to sprout," from germen "bud, sprout."
