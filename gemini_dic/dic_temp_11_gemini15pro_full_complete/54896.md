disporous:/daɪˈspɔːrəs/ /dɪˈspɔːrəs/
adjective
分散的，散生的
Occurring in two distinct places or forms. Existing separately or in scattered groups.
Example: The disporous distribution of the plant species made it difficult to study.  这种植物物种的分散分布使其难以研究。
Example: The fungus has a disporous life cycle, existing in both aquatic and terrestrial environments.  这种真菌具有分散的生活周期，在水生和陆生环境中都存在。
Near Synonyms: scattered, dispersed, separate, distinct
Antonyms: clustered, concentrated, aggregated
Word Origin: From Greek *di- 'two' + sporos 'seed'
