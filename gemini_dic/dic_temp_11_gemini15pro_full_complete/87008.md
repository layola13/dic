henries:/ˈhɛnriz/| n.|亨利（电感的单位）|The plural form of henry, the unit of inductance.  It represents the inductance of a circuit in which an electromotive force of one volt is produced by a current varying at the rate of one ampere per second.|The inductor has an inductance of several henries.|近义词: (No direct synonym as it's a unit of measurement) |反义词: (No antonym as it's a unit of measurement)|noun
