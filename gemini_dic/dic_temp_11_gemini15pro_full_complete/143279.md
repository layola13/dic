outdoing:/ˌaʊtˈduːɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing, excelling, exceeding in performance or achievement
He is outdoing all his competitors in sales this quarter. 他本季度的销售额超过了所有竞争对手。
surpass, excel, exceed, outstrip, outperform
be inferior to, fall short of
词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *outdoen, from Old English *ūtdōn (unattested), from out- + dōn (“to do”). Cognate with Dutch uitdoen (“to extinguish, undress”), German austun.
