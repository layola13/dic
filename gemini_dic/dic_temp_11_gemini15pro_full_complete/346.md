abhorring:/əˈbɔːrɪŋ/
adjective
憎恶的，厌恶的
Feeling or showing strong hatred or disgust.
She has an abhorring attitude towards violence. 她对暴力深恶痛绝。
近义词：detesting, loathing, hating, despising
反义词：loving, adoring, cherishing, approving
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *abhorren*, from Old French *abhorrer*, from Latin *abhorrēre* (“to shrink back from, shudder at, have an aversion to, detest”), from *ab* (“away from”) + *horrēre* (“to bristle, shudder, tremble”).
