Niger River: /ˈnaɪdʒər ˈrɪvər/ | n. | 尼日尔河 | A major river in West Africa, extending about 4,180 km (2,600 mi). It is the third-longest river in Africa, after the Nile and the Congo River. | The Niger River is a vital source of water for agriculture and fishing. 尼日尔河是农业和渔业的重要水源。 | The Niger River Delta is a biodiversity hotspot. 尼日尔河三角洲是一个生物多样性热点地区。 | 近义词: (No direct synonym for a specific river) | 反义词: (No antonym for a specific river) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Portuguese *Níger*, from a native name, probably Tuareg *gher n gheren* 'river of rivers'.  The name has sometimes been connected, by folk etymology, with Latin *niger* "black," but the river's name predates extensive European contact.
