nonflatulences:/ˌnɒnˌflætʃʊlənsɪz/
noun
无胀气；没有肠胃气体
Absence of flatulence; the state of not experiencing excessive gas in the digestive tract.
My improved diet has resulted in a welcome period of nonflatulences.  我的改良饮食带来了令人欣喜的无胀气时期。
The yoga instructor assured us the poses would promote digestion and nonflatulences. 瑜伽教练向我们保证，这些姿势会促进消化，避免胀气。
near synonyms: absence of gas, freedom from flatulence
antonyms: flatulence, gas, bloating
