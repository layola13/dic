irresilient:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪliənt/
adjective
不具弹性的，缺乏韧性的，脆弱的
Not resilient; unable to recoil or spring back into shape after bending, stretching, or being compressed.  Unable to recover quickly from difficulties; fragile.
The economy proved surprisingly irresilient to the shock.  这个经济体对冲击的抵抗力出奇地弱。
近义词：fragile, weak, vulnerable, brittle, susceptible
反义词：resilient, strong, tough, robust, elastic
词性: adjective
