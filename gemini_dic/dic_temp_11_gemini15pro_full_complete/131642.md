noncompulsion:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpʌlʃən/ | n. | 非强迫，非强制 | The principle of noncompulsion ensures individual autonomy. |  This policy operates on the basis of noncompulsion.  | synonyms: voluntariness, free will, optionality | antonyms: compulsion, coercion, obligation | noun

Etymology:  From non- + compulsion.  "Compulsion" comes from the Old French "compulsion" and ultimately from the Latin "compulsio," meaning "a driving or forcing together."
