dogged:/ˈdɒɡɪd/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡɪd/ (US)| adj. |顽强的，坚持不懈的，不屈不挠的|Having or showing tenacity and grim persistence.|She was dogged in her pursuit of the truth. 她坚持不懈地追求真相。|Synonyms: persistent, tenacious, determined, resolute, persevering|Antonyms: irresolute, wavering, hesitant, half-hearted|adjective

doggedly:/ˈdɒɡɪdli/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡɪdli/ (US)| adv. |顽强地，坚持不懈地|In a dogged manner; persistently.|He doggedly pursued his goal, despite the obstacles. 尽管有重重障碍，他仍然顽强地追求自己的目标。|Synonyms: persistently, tenaciously, resolutely, determinedly|Antonyms: hesitantly, half-heartedly|adverb
