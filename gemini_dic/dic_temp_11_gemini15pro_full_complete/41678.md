correspondency:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndənsi/ /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənsi/
n.
通信；信件；一致；符合
Communication by exchanging letters; the letters exchanged.  A close similarity, connection, or equivalence.
例句：I have a large volume of correspondence to deal with. 我有大量的信件要处理。

例句：There is a close correspondence between the two maps. 这两张地图非常吻合。

近义词：communication, letters, mail, agreement, conformity, similarity
反义词：disagreement, disparity, difference

词性: noun
