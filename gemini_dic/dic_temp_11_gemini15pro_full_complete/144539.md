outvoice:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪs/| v.|压倒…的声音；盖过…的声音；比…声音大；比…更响亮|to speak louder than someone or something else, so that they cannot be heard|The singer was outvoiced by the cheering crowd. 歌手的歌声被欢呼的人群盖过了。|近义词：drown out, overpower, outshout|反义词: be drowned out, be overpowered|verb

outvoice:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪs/| n.|（尤指比他人更响亮的）发言权，表达权|the right or opportunity to express your opinions, especially when this is louder or more effective than other people's|Marginalized communities are fighting for their outvoice in the political arena. 边缘化社区正在争取他们在政治舞台上的发言权。|近义词：voice, say, platform|反义词: silence, suppression|noun
