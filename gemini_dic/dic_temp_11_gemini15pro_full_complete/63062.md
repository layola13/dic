enroll:/ɪnˈroʊl/ (US) /ɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK)| v. & n.|注册，登记；招收，吸收|To officially register as a member of an institution or a student on a course.|例句：I enrolled in the online course last week. 我上周注册了在线课程。|近义词：register, sign up, join|反义词:withdraw, unregister|verb

enroll:/ɪnˈroʊl/ (US) /ɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK)| n.|注册，登记；入学|The act of enrolling or being enrolled.|例句：Enrollment in the program has increased this year.  今年该项目的注册人数增加了。|近义词：registration, admission|反义词:withdrawal|noun
