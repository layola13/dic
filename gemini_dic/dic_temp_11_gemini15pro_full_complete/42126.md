cottoned:/ˈkɒtnd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtnd/ (US)| v. |喜欢；理解；亲近|To take a liking to; to become fond of. To understand or grasp the meaning of something. To become friendly or close with someone.|例句：She quickly cottoned to the new puppy. 她很快就喜欢上了那只新小狗。

例句：He didn't cotton to the idea at first, but he came around eventually. 他起初不喜欢这个主意，但最终还是接受了。

近义词：like, fancy, take to, understand, grasp, get along with|反义词:dislike, hate, misunderstand|词性:动词

cottoned:/ˈkɒtnd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtnd/ (US)| adj. |(主要用于美式英语，非正式)棉制的|Made of cotton.|例句：He wore a cottoned shirt in the summer heat.  他在夏天的酷暑中穿着一件棉布衬衫。


例句：She prefers cottoned sheets because they are breathable. 她更喜欢棉质床单，因为它们透气。

近义词：cotton|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他材质，例如：silk, nylon)|词性:形容词
