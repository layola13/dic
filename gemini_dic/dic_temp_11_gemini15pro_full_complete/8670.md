anticontagious:/ˌæntiːkənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|抗传染的，防止传染的|Preventing contagion; checking the spread of disease.|This new mask claims to be anticontagious and can filter out 99% of airborne particles.|近义词：antiinfective, prophylactic|反义词:contagious, infectious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From anti- + contagious.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Contagious" comes from the Latin "contagiosus," meaning "touching," from "contingere" - "to touch."
