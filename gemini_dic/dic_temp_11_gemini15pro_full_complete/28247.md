captaincy: /ˈkæptɪnsi/ (UK) /ˈkæptənsi/ (US)| n. |队长职位；队长身份；队长任期|The position or rank of captain, or the period of time during which someone is captain.| He held the captaincy of the team for five years. 他担任了五年的队长。|近义词：leadership, command |反义词: subordination |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From captain + -cy.  "Captain" comes from Old French "capitain" (modern French "capitaine"), from Late Latin "capitaneus" meaning "chief, head man," ultimately from Latin "caput" meaning "head."  The suffix "-cy" forms abstract nouns usually denoting rank, office, or condition.
