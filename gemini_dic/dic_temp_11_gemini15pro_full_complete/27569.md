canalize:/ˈkænəlaɪz/ /ˈkænəˌlaɪz/| v.|引导；疏导；形成管道|to direct something into a particular course, or to become directed into a particular course; to make a channel or channels in something|The government is trying to canalize the flow of refugees.|近义词：channel, direct, guide, constrain|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词

Etymology: From French *canaliser*, from *canal* (“channel”).
