embellish:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/| vt.|装饰，修饰，润色|To make something more attractive or interesting by adding details, often decorative ones.  To enhance with fanciful details or fictitious additions.|She embellished the story with details about pirates and buried treasure. 她用海盗和埋藏宝藏的细节润色了这个故事。|近义词：adorn, decorate, ornament, enhance, elaborate, exaggerate|反义词：simplify, strip, understate|动词

embellishment:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃmənt/| n.|装饰，修饰，润色|A detail, especially one that is decorative, added to something to make it more attractive or interesting.|The carvings on the furniture were a beautiful embellishment. 家具上的雕刻是很漂亮的装饰。|近义词：decoration, ornamentation, adornment|反义词：simplification|名词
