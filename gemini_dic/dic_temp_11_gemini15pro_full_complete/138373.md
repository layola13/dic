notionless:/ˈnəʊʃənləs/ /ˈnoʊʃənləs/| adj.|静止的，不动的|Without any movement; still.|The deer stood motionless, watching us from the edge of the woods. 鹿一动不动地站在树林边上看着我们。|The flag hung motionless in the still air. 旗帜一动不动地悬挂在静止的空气中。|近义词：still, immobile, stationary, static|反义词：moving, active, dynamic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From notion + -less.  "Notion" originally meant "mental apprehension, idea," evolving to also encompass "inclination, desire to move," hence "motion, movement."  The suffix "-less" negates the presence of the quality.
