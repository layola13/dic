casteism:/ˈkɑːstiɪzəm/ /ˈkæstiɪzəm/
n.
种姓制度，种姓主义
Prejudice or discrimination on the grounds of caste.
Casteism is a major social problem in India.  种姓制度是印度的一个主要社会问题。
The politician's speech was criticized for promoting casteism.  这位政客的演讲因宣扬种姓主义而受到批评。
近义词：discrimination, prejudice, bigotry, bias
反义词：equality, tolerance, acceptance
词性：noun
