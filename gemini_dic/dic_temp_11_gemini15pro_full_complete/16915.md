basketwork:/ˈbɑːskɪtwɜːrk/ /ˈbæskɪtwɜːrk/
n.|编织品；篮子工艺|Objects made of interwoven twigs, reeds, or other flexible material, often used for making baskets or other containers.|The basketwork on display at the craft fair was intricate and beautiful.  工艺品博览会上展出的编织品精美绝伦。|wickerwork, wattle| N/A |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From basket + work.  Basket from Old French *baskete, diminutive of Old North French basse "basket," perhaps from Gaulish (Celtic) *bascauda. Work from Old English weorc "work, action, labor, military fortifications," from Proto-Germanic *werkan- "to perform, work" (source also of Old Saxon werk, Old Frisian werk, Dutch werk, Old High German werc, German Werk, Old Norse verk).
