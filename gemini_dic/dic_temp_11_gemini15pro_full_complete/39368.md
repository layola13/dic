conjugative:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡətɪv/ /ˈkɑːndʒəɡeɪtɪv/| adj.| 共轭的；结合的 |Relating to or denoting a type of bacterial reproduction in which genetic material is transferred from one bacterium to another by direct contact.|Bacterial conjugation is a conjugative process. 细菌接合是一个共轭过程。|近义词: transferable, transmissible |反义词: non-conjugative |词性: adjective

conjugation:/ˌkɒndʒʊˈɡeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːndʒəˈɡeɪʃən/| n.| 接合；结合 |The temporary union of two bacteria or unicellular organisms for the exchange of genetic material.|Bacterial conjugation is a mechanism of horizontal gene transfer. 细菌接合是水平基因转移的一种机制。|近义词: union, joining |反义词: separation, division |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conjugativus, from conjugat- "joined together," from the past participle stem of conjugare "to yoke together."
