amphiarthrosis:/ˌæmfiɑːrˈθroʊsɪs/| n. | 半关节 |A type of joint that allows slight movement, intermediate between a synarthrosis (immovable joint) and a diarthrosis (freely movable joint).  The joint surfaces are connected by fibrocartilage or hyaline cartilage. |The pubic symphysis is an example of an amphiarthrosis. 耻骨联合是半关节的一个例子。|  Synonym: slightly movable joint | Antonym: synarthrosis, diarthrosis | noun
