balneography: /ˌbælniˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 温泉疗法；温泉学； balneotherapy | The study of the therapeutic use of baths, especially mineral baths. |  Balneography is a traditional practice used in many cultures. 温泉疗法是许多文化中使用的传统疗法。 | Balneography has been practiced for centuries to treat various ailments.  几个世纪以来，温泉疗法一直被用于治疗各种疾病。 |  近义词: balneotherapy, hydrotherapy | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *balnéographie*, from *balnéo-* (from Latin *balneum* "bath") + *-graphie* (from Greek *-graphía* "writing").
