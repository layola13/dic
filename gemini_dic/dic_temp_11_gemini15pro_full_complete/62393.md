endocrinologists:/ˌɛndəkrɪˈnɒlədʒɪsts/
| n. |内分泌学家|A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the endocrine system, which includes glands that produce hormones.|
|例句：Endocrinologists treat a wide range of hormonal imbalances. 内分泌学家治疗各种激素失衡。|

|例句：I have an appointment with my endocrinologist next week. 我下周要和我的内分泌学家预约。|
|近义词：hormone specialist|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From  "endo-" (within) + "crin-" (to separate) + "-o-" + "-logist" (one who studies).  Ultimately from Greek "endon" (within) + "krinein" (to separate, secrete).
