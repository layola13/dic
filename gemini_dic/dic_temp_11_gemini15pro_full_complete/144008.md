outrebound:/ˌaʊtˈriːbaʊnd/ | adj. | 外跳的，向外的，超越界限的 |  Traveling or directed away from a central point or area, especially beyond established limits or boundaries.  It often implies exceeding typical bounds or going further than expected in an outward direction. | The company's outrebound marketing strategy reached customers in new international markets.  这家公司向外的营销策略触及了新的国际市场客户。

The satellite's outrebound trajectory took it beyond the moon's orbit. 这颗卫星向外的轨迹使其飞越了月球轨道。 | 近义词: outbound, outward, external | 反义词: inbound, inward, internal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Outre-" (meaning beyond or exceeding) + "bound" (meaning limited or having a boundary).  The prefix "outre-" is derived from French and ultimately from Latin "ultra" (beyond).
