interradiate:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdieɪt/ | v. |互相照射，互相辐射；使混合 | To illuminate each other mutually; to blend or mix together. | The two searchlights interradiated, creating a bright zone in the sky. 两束探照灯互相照射，在天空中创造出一个明亮的区域。 |  The different cultures interradiated, creating a vibrant and diverse community. 不同的文化相互融合，创造了一个充满活力和多元化的社区。 | 近义词: intermingle, blend, fuse, merge | 反义词: separate, divide, isolate | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Latin *interradiatus*, past participle of *interradiare*, from inter- "between" + radiare "to shine, radiate."
