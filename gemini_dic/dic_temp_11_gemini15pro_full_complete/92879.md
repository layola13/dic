idealism:/aɪˈdiːəlɪzəm/ /ɪˈdiːəlɪzəm/
n.|理想主义；唯心主义|The pursuit of noble goals and principles, often without regard to practical considerations; the philosophical theory that ideas are the only reality.|He was known for his youthful idealism and commitment to social justice. 他以年轻时的理想主义和对社会正义的承诺而闻名。|optimism, utopianism|realism, pragmatism|noun
