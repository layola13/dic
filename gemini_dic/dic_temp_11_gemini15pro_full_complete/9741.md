antivivisection:/ˌæntiˌvɪvɪˈsɛkʃən/
n.
反对活体解剖
opposition to the act of performing experiments on live animals.
The growth of antivivisection sentiment in the 19th century led to tighter regulations on animal research. 19世纪反活体解剖情绪的高涨导致对动物研究的更严格的规定。
The group held an antivivisection protest outside the laboratory.  该组织在实验室外举行了反活体解剖抗议。
近义词:animal rights activism, animal welfare advocacy
反义词:pro-vivisection, vivisectionism
词性:noun
