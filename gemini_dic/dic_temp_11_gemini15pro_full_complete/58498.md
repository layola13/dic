dueling:/ˈduːəlɪŋ/ /ˈdjuːəlɪŋ/ | n. |决斗，斗争|The practice of fighting a duel with deadly weapons, typically as a formal means of settling a dispute.|例句：Dueling was once common among gentlemen. 决斗曾经在绅士中很常见。

例句：The dueling pianos provided lively entertainment.  决斗钢琴提供了生动的娱乐。|近义词：fighting, combating, contesting, clashing|反义词：cooperation, agreement, harmony|词性:名词
