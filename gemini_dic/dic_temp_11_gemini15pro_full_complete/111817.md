logbooks: /ˈlɒɡbʊks/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡbʊks/ (US) | n. |航海日志，日志，记录簿|A book in which measurements or observations are recorded, especially a ship's log. |例句：The captain meticulously recorded every detail in the logbook. 船长一丝不苟地在航海日志中记录了每一个细节。

例句：The pilot checked the aircraft's logbook before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前检查了飞机的日志。|近义词：journals, diaries, records, registers |反义词: None |词性: Noun
