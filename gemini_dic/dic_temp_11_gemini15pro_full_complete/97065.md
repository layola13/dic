inferiority:/ɪnˌfɪəriˈɔːrəti/ | n. | 自卑，劣等 | a feeling that you are not as good, intelligent, or important as other people |  He had a deep sense of inferiority. 他有很深的自卑感。| synonyms: inadequacy, inferiority complex, low self-esteem | antonyms: superiority, confidence, self-esteem | noun

inferior:/ɪnˈfɪəriər/ | adj. | 较差的，次的，下级的 | lower in rank, status, or quality |  This product is inferior to the other one. 这个产品比另一个差。| synonyms: lesser, poorer, substandard | antonyms: superior, better, excellent | adjective
