assistantships:/əˈsɪstəntˌʃɪps/ | n. |助教职位；研究生助教奖学金|A form of financial aid given to graduate students, often in exchange for teaching or research duties.  It usually includes a tuition waiver and a stipend.|She received a teaching assistantship that covered her tuition and living expenses. 她获得了一个助教职位，涵盖了她的学费和生活费。|Synonym: fellowship, scholarship|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps self-funded studies)|Noun
