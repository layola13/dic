nonsensuousnesses:/nɒnˈsɛnʃʊəsnɪsɪz/
noun
无感觉；非感官性
lack of sensuous quality; absence of appeal to the senses.
His art strives for a nonsensuousnesses, a detachment from the physical world. 他的艺术追求一种非感官性，一种脱离物质世界的超然。
近义词:abstractness, immateriality, incorporeality
反义词:sensuousness, physicality, materiality
词性:n.


Etymology:
From non- (not) + sensuousness (the quality of affecting or appealing to the senses).  Ultimately from Latin *sensus (sense)
