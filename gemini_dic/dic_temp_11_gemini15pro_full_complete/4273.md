airtightly:/ˈɛərtʌɪtli/| adv.|密封地，不透气地|In a manner that prevents air from entering or escaping.|The jar was sealed airtightly to preserve the contents. 罐子被密封以保存里面的东西。|例句：The food was stored airtightly to prevent spoilage.  食物被密封保存以防止腐败。

例句：The windows were closed airtightly against the cold wind. 窗户被关得严严实实以抵御寒风。|近义词：hermetically, tightly, securely|反义词: loosely, openly
词性: 副词
