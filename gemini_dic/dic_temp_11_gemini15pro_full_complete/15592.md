bai: /baɪ/
determiner, pronoun|哪一个，哪一些|Used to ask questions about one or more people or things from a limited set. |Which dress do you prefer, the red one or the blue one? 你更喜欢哪条裙子，红色的还是蓝色的？|近义词: which|反义词: none, neither|词性: determiner, pronoun

bai: /baɪ/
noun|再见，告别| An informal way of saying goodbye. |"Bai!" she shouted as she ran out the door. “再见！”她一边喊，一边跑出门外。|近义词: goodbye, farewell, bye|反义词: hello, greetings|词性: noun
