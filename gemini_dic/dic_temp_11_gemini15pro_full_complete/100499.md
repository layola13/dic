interventional:/ˌɪntərˈvɛnʃənəl/
adjective
介入的，干预的
Relating to or involving intervention, especially medical intervention.
Interventional cardiology uses catheters and other small instruments inserted into blood vessels to diagnose and treat heart conditions.  介入性心脏病学使用插入血管的导管和其他小型器械来诊断和治疗心脏疾病。
The hospital has a new interventional radiology suite. 这家医院有一个新的介入放射科套件。
Synonyms: invasive, operative, surgical
Antonyms: non-invasive, non-surgical, preventative
词性: adjective
