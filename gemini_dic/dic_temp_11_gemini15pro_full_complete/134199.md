nonincrimination:/ˌnɑːnɪnˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
不自证其罪，豁免自证其罪
The principle that a person cannot be forced to testify against themselves.
The Fifth Amendment of the US Constitution guarantees the right of nonincrimination. 美国宪法第五修正案保障了不自证其罪的权利。
The suspect invoked his right to nonincrimination and refused to answer any questions. 嫌疑人援引了不自证其罪的权利，拒绝回答任何问题。

immunity from self-incrimination, privilege against self-incrimination
