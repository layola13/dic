hilltops:/ˈhɪlˌtɑːps/ | n. | 山顶，山峰 | The highest hilltops offered breathtaking views of the valley below. 最高山峰的景色令人叹为观止，可以俯瞰下面的山谷。|  The climber finally reached one of the hilltops after a long and arduous journey. 经过漫长而艰辛的旅程，登山者终于到达了其中一个山顶。 | synonyms: summits, peaks, crests, apexes | antonyms: valleys, bottoms, bases | noun

Etymology: From "hill" (Old English *hyll) + "top" (Old English topp)
