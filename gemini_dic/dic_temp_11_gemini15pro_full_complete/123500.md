monotheist:/ˈmɒnəθiːɪst/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəθiːɪst/| n. |一神教信徒，一神论者|A person who believes in the existence of only one god.|例句：She is a devout monotheist. 她是一位虔诚的一神教信徒。

例句：The community is comprised primarily of monotheists. 这个社区主要由一神教信徒组成。

近义词：believer (in one god)|反义词: polytheist, pagan|词性:noun
