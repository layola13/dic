amputators: /ˈæmpjʊˌteɪtərz/
n.
截肢者，截肢手术者
People who perform amputations are called amputators.
进行截肢手术的人被称为截肢者。
The amputators prepared the patient for surgery.
截肢医生让病人做好手术准备。
Synonyms: surgeons, surgical team
Antonyms: healers, reconstructive surgeons
