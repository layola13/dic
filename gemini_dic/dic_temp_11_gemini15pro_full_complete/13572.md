attenders:/əˈtɛndərz/
n.
参加者；出席者；出席人
People who attend a meeting, event, etc.
The attenders of the conference were impressed by the keynote speaker.  会议的出席者对主旨发言人印象深刻。

The wedding attenders celebrated late into the night. 婚礼的参加者们庆祝到深夜。
participants, attendees, guests
absentees
noun
