dogmatically:/dɒɡˈmætɪkli/ /dɑːɡˈmætɪkli/
adjective
教条地，武断地
In a manner asserting opinions as undeniably true, without consideration of evidence or the opinions of others.
He dogmatically asserted that the earth was flat. 他武断地断言地球是平的。
Synonyms: assertively, peremptorily, authoritatively, dictatorial, doctrinaire
Antonyms: open-mindedly, flexibly, liberally
词性: adjective
