matriculation:/məˌtrɪkjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 入学，注册，录取 |The act of enrolling or being enrolled in a college or university. |  Example: Her matriculation at Harvard was a proud moment for her family. 她进入哈佛大学就读是她全家人的骄傲时刻。

Example: The university's matriculation ceremony was held in the grand hall. 大学的入学典礼在大厅举行。


近义词: enrollment, registration, admission | 反义词: withdrawal, graduation | 词性: 名词

matriculations:/məˌtrɪkjuˈleɪʃnz/ | n. |  入学，注册，录取（复数）| The acts of enrolling or being enrolled in a college or university. | Example:  The matriculations of the new students will take place next week. 新生的入学手续将于下周办理。

Example: The university saw a rise in matriculations this year. 今年大学的入学人数有所增加。

近义词: enrollments, registrations, admissions | 反义词: withdrawals, graduations | 词性: 名词
