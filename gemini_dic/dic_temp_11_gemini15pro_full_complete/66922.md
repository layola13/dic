expendable:/ɪkˈspendəbl/ /ekˈspendəbl/| adj. n.| 可牺牲的，可消耗的；消耗品| Something that is expendable is not considered important and can be sacrificed to achieve a goal.  Expendables are items that are used up and replaced.|The troops were regarded as expendable. 这些部队被认为是可以牺牲的。|Synonyms: dispensable, nonessential, unimportant, replaceable,sacrificial |Antonyms: essential, indispensable, crucial, vital|adjective


expendable:/ɪkˈspendəbl/ /ekˈspendəbl/| n.| 消耗品| An expendable is an item that is used up and replaced. |Office expendables include pens, paper, and staples. 办公室消耗品包括笔、纸和订书钉。|Synonyms: supplies, consumables, provisions|Antonyms: durables|noun
