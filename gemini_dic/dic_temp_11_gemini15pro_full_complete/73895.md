forsake:/fərˈseɪk/ | vt. |放弃，抛弃，摒弃|To abandon or leave someone or something, especially when they need you or you have a responsibility towards them.|He forsook his family for a new life in the city. 他为了在城市开始新生活而抛弃了家人。

She forsook her career to raise her children. 她放弃了事业来抚养孩子。|近义词：abandon, desert, renounce, relinquish|反义词：cherish, embrace, keep, maintain|词性：动词

词源:
Old English *forsacan "oppose, refuse, renounce, deny," from for- "away" + sacan "contend, strive, dispute, quarrel" (related to seek). The original sense was "to resist, contend against," but this was superseded by the notion of "renounce, give up."
