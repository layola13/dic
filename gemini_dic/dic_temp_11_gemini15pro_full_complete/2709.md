adsorb:/ædˈsɔːrb/ /ədˈzɔːrb/
|v.|吸附|To attract and hold (molecules of a gas or liquid) to a surface, forming a thin film.|Activated charcoal can adsorb impurities from water.  活性炭可以吸附水中的杂质。|
|v.|吸附|The fabric adsorbs sweat easily. 这种布料很容易吸汗。|
|近义词：absorb, accumulate|反义词:desorb|词性:动词

PHRASE->
adsorb onto (吸附到): The molecules adsorb onto the surface of the material. 分子吸附到材料的表面。
adsorb from (从...吸附):  The filter adsorbs impurities from the water. 过滤器从水中吸附杂质。
adsorption capacity (吸附容量):  The adsorption capacity of the material is high.  这种材料的吸附容量很高。
adsorption isotherm (吸附等温线): The adsorption isotherm describes the relationship between the amount adsorbed and the concentration. 吸附等温线描述了吸附量和浓度之间的关系。
adsorption rate (吸附速率): The adsorption rate is affected by temperature and pressure. 吸附速率受温度和压力的影响。
