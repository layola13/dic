anodization:/ˌænədaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
阳极氧化，阳极化处理
The electrochemical process of forming a protective oxide layer on a metal, especially aluminum.
The anodization of the aluminum parts increased their resistance to corrosion.  铝零件的阳极氧化增加了它们的耐腐蚀性。

anodize:/ˈænəˌdaɪz/
v.
阳极氧化，阳极化处理
To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.
The company anodizes aluminum parts to protect them from corrosion.  这家公司对铝零件进行阳极氧化处理，以防止腐蚀。

anodic:/əˈnɑːdɪk/
adj.
阳极的
Relating to or occurring at the anode of an electrolytic cell.
The anodic reaction involves the oxidation of the metal.  阳极反应涉及金属的氧化。


近义词:oxidation (for the general process), electrolyzing
反义词:cathodization (though less commonly used)
词性: anodization (noun), anodize (verb), anodic (adjective)
