overargumentative: /ˌoʊvərˌɑːrɡjʊˈmentətɪv/ | adj. | 过于争辩的，好辩的 | Given to excessive arguing; disputatious.  Inclined to argue or debate excessively, often to the point of being unreasonable or contentious. | He became overargumentative after a few drinks, challenging everyone's opinions. 他几杯酒下肚后就变得过于好辩，质疑每个人的观点。| Example:  She is so overargumentative that no one wants to have a discussion with her. 她太好辩了，以至于没有人愿意和她讨论。 | Synonyms: disputatious, contentious, quarrelsome, argumentative | Antonyms: agreeable, amenable, conciliatory, compliant | word property: adjective
