generosity:/ˌdʒenəˈrɒsəti/ /ˌdʒenəˈrɑːsəti/ | n. |慷慨，大方|The quality of being kind, understanding, and not selfish; willingness to give money, help, etc., especially more than is usual or expected.|例句：Her generosity knows no bounds. 她的慷慨无边无际。

例句：He was known for his generosity to the poor. 他以对穷人的慷慨而闻名。

近义词：liberality, munificence, bounty, magnanimity|反义词：stinginess, meanness, miserliness, parsimony|词性:名词
