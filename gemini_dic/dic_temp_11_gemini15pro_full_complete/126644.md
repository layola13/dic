naivete:/naɪˈiːvteɪ/ /nɑːˈiːvteɪ/| n. | 天真;幼稚;轻信 | Lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment.  |  Her naivete about the dangers of the city was alarming. 她对城市危险的无知令人担忧。 |  innocence, credulity, simplicity, ingenuousness |  sophistication, worldliness, cynicism, shrewdness | noun

naive:/naɪˈiːv/ /nɑːˈiːv/| adj. | 天真的;幼稚的;轻信的 | Showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment. | She was so naive that she believed everything he said.  她太天真了，竟然相信他所说的一切。 | innocent, credulous, simple, ingenuous | sophisticated, worldly, cynical, shrewd
