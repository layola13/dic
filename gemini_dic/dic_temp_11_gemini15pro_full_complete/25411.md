bulimia nervosa: /ˌbuːˈlɪmiə nɜːrˈvoʊsə/ (US), /ˌbjuːˈlɪmiə nɜːˈvəʊsə/ (UK) | n. | 神经性贪食症 | An eating disorder characterized by binge eating followed by purging (self-induced vomiting, misuse of laxatives, diuretics, or enemas, or excessive exercise) to prevent weight gain. |  She was diagnosed with bulimia nervosa after years of struggling with her weight. 她与体重抗争多年后被诊断出患有神经性贪食症。

| 近义词: eating disorder,  | 反义词: anorexia nervosa (although both are eating disorders) | 词性: noun
