avant:/əˈvɑːnt/ | adj. | 前卫的；先锋的 |  Unusually new and different, especially in the arts, or of people supporting such ideas.  |  The restaurant's decor was very avant-garde.  这家餐厅的装潢非常前卫。 | synonyms:  advanced, progressive, innovative, cutting-edge, experimental, unconventional, pioneering | antonyms: traditional, conventional, conservative, old-fashioned | adjective


avant:/əˈvɑːnt/ | n. | 先锋；前锋 |  The advance part of an army; vanguard.  (This meaning is less common in modern English.) |  The avant of the army scouted the territory ahead.  军队的先锋侦察了前方的领土。 | synonyms: vanguard, forefront, pioneers, leaders | antonyms: rear, rearguard | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *avant-* "before," from Latin *abante* (from *ab* "from" + *ante* "before").
