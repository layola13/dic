loquat:/ˈloʊkwɑt/ (US), /ˈləʊkwɒt/ (UK)| n. |枇杷|A small, yellow, oval fruit with a soft, juicy flesh and a large seed. It is native to southeastern China.|例句：The loquats are ripe and ready to be picked. 枇杷熟了，可以摘了。

例句：She made a delicious loquat jam. 她做了一罐美味的枇杷酱。


近义词：medlar (somewhat similar fruit)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
