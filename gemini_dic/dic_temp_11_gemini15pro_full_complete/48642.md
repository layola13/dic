defenestration:/ˌdiːˌfɛnɪˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 扔出窗外; 开窗抛物 | The act of throwing someone or something out of a window. |  The rebels celebrated their victory by the defenestration of the former dictator. 反叛者通过将前独裁者扔出窗外来庆祝他们的胜利。

|  The defenestration of his laptop was an accident caused by his clumsiness. 他把笔记本电脑扔出窗外是由于他的笨拙造成的意外。 | 近义词：ejection, expulsion | 反义词: admittance, inclusion | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēfenestrātiō*, from dē- (from, away from) + fenestra (window).  Ultimately from Proto-Italic *fenes-tra, meaning “opening, window.”
