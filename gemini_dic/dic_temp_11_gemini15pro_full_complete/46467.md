daikons: /ˈdaɪkɒnz/ (UK) /ˈdaɪkɑːnz/ (US)| n. |萝卜（大根，通常指日本白萝卜）|A long, white, Asian radish.  It is milder and less pungent than the small red radish.  It is often used in salads and as a garnish.|例句：I added some sliced daikons to my salad for a bit of crunch. 我在沙拉里加了一些萝卜片，让口感更脆。

例句：Daikons are a common ingredient in Japanese cuisine. 白萝卜是日本料理中常见的食材。|近义词：radish, mooli|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
