lumpens:/ˈlʌmpənz/| n.| 流氓无产者，社会渣滓| People marginalized from society, often unemployed and considered unproductive.  They are typically disengaged from mainstream social and political structures.|  The economic downturn created a large class of lumpens who were easily manipulated by demagogues. 经济衰退造成了大量的流氓无产阶级，他们很容易被煽动家操纵。| synonyms: dregs, riffraff, underclass, outcasts, vagrants | antonyms: elite, aristocracy, upper class | noun
