overencouraging: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
过度鼓励的
Encouraging someone excessively, to the point where it may have negative consequences.  This can lead to unrealistic expectations or a lack of resilience in the face of challenges.
His overencouraging parenting style made his son believe he was entitled to everything.  他过度鼓励的育儿方式让他儿子认为他什么都应该得到。
The teacher's overencouraging feedback led the student to overestimate his abilities. 老师过度鼓励的反馈导致学生高估了自己的能力。
近义词: overpraising, oversupportive
反义词: discouraging, under-supportive
词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "encouraging" (present participle of "encourage," from Old French "encoragier," meaning "to make strong, to hearten")
