auget:/ˈɔːɡɛt/| n.|水管，水槽，沟渠|A conduit, pipe, or channel for conveying water; a trough or gutter.|The Roman auget channeled water to the baths.|近义词：channel, conduit, pipe, trough, gutter|反义词: blockage|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *auctus*, past participle of *augēre* "to increase," related to Greek *auxein* "to increase."  The original sense likely referred to a channel that increased the flow of water.
