hareld:/ˈhærəld/ | n. | 传令官；使者；先驱 |  A herald is an official messenger, especially one representing a monarch or a state, often with ceremonial duties. It can also refer to something that precedes or foreshadows something else. | The king sent a herald to announce the birth of his son. 国王派了一名传令官去宣布他儿子的出生。

The robin is a herald of spring. 知更鸟是春天的先驱。 | synonyms: messenger, envoy, precursor, harbinger | antonyms: follower, successor | noun
