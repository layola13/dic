encamp:/ɪnˈkæmp/ | v. |扎营，宿营 | To set up camp; to pitch tents for temporary lodging. | The soldiers encamped near the river. 士兵们在河边扎营。

The travelers encamped for the night in a clearing in the forest. 旅行者们在森林中的一块空地上扎营过夜。

| 近义词: camp, bivouac, tent | 反义词: decamp, break camp | 词性: 动词

encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/ | n. | 营地，宿营地 | The act of encamping or the place where a camp is set up. | The refugees set up an encampment on the outskirts of the city. 难民们在城市郊区建立了一个营地。

The annual encampment of the Boy Scouts was held in the mountains. 童子军的年度露营活动在山上举行。

| 近义词: camp, campsite, bivouac | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词
