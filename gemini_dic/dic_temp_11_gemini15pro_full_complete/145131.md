overarrange:/ˌoʊvərəˈreɪndʒ/| v. |过度安排，安排得过分|To arrange something excessively, often to the point of being fussy or unnecessary.|She overarranged the flowers, making the bouquet look stiff and unnatural. 她把花安排得过分了，让花束看起来僵硬不自然。|例句：
The wedding was overarranged, leaving the couple exhausted and stressed. 婚礼安排得过分了，让这对夫妇筋疲力尽，压力很大。

He tends to overarrange his travel itineraries, leaving no room for spontaneity. 他倾向于过度安排他的旅行行程，没有留下任何即兴发挥的空间。|近义词：overorganize, overplan|反义词: underarrange, underplan|词性:动词
