countryish:/ˈkʌntriɪʃ/
adjective
乡村风的；略带乡村气息的；有点土气的
Having some of the characteristics of the countryside or its people, often in a pleasant or naive way.
Her style is very countryish, with lots of floral prints and gingham. 她的风格非常乡村风，有很多碎花印花和格子图案。
synonyms:rustic, rural, pastoral, countrified
antonyms:urban, sophisticated, cosmopolitan
词性:形容词


countryish:/ˈkʌntriɪʃ/
adjective
有点土气的; 乡巴佬的
Slightly unsophisticated or unfashionable, suggesting a rural background.
He had a slightly countryish accent and manner. 他口音和举止略显土气。
synonyms:unsophisticated, naive, simple, hick
antonyms:sophisticated, urbane, worldly
