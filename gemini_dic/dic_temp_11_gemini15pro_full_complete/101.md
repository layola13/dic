abaciscus:/ˌæbəˈsɪskəs/
n.
小方砖，方形镶嵌片
A small tile or square compartment in a mosaic pavement.
The floor of the Roman villa was decorated with intricate abacisci. 这座罗马别墅的地板用复杂的方形镶嵌片装饰。
synonyms: tessera, tile, square
antonyms:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "irregular shape" in the context of mosaics)
noun
