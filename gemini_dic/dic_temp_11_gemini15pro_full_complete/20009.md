biologist:/baɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ (US) /baɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ (UK)| n.|生物学家|A scientist who studies living organisms.|例句：The biologist spent years studying the behavior of primates.  这位生物学家花了数年时间研究灵长类动物的行为。

例句：She's a renowned biologist specializing in marine life. 她是一位著名的海洋生物学家。

近义词：life scientist, naturalist, zoologist (for animal biologists), botanist (for plant biologists)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + -logist. Bio- comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life". -logist is a suffix denoting "one who studies".  Therefore, a biologist is "one who studies life".
