curtle:/ˈkɜːrtl/| v.|缩短；截短；简略|To cut short; abbreviate. |He curtled his speech when he realized how late it was.  他意识到时间很晚了，于是缩短了讲话。

She curtled the story to fit the time allotted. 她为了适应规定的时间而简略了故事。|近义词：abbreviate, shorten, truncate, condense|反义词: lengthen, extend, expand|词性:动词
