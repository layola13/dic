journaled:/ˈdʒɜːnəld/ | adj. | 已记录的，已写入日志的 |  Recorded in a journal; having a journal feature. |  The data is journaled to ensure consistency in case of system failure.  数据被记录下来以确保在系统故障的情况下保持一致性。 |  Data is journaled to a separate log file. 数据被记录到一个单独的日志文件中。 | Synonyms: logged, recorded, documented | Antonyms: unrecorded, undocumented | adjective

journaled:/ˈdʒɜːnəld/ | v. |  记录，写入日志 | Past participle of "to journal".  | She has journaled her thoughts and feelings for many years. 她多年来一直记录着她的想法和感受。 | He journaled his experiences during his travels abroad. 他在国外旅行期间记录了他的经历。 | Synonyms: logged, recorded, documented, chronicled | Antonyms:  N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "journal" + "-ed".  "Journal" comes from Old French "journal" (daily), ultimately from Late Latin "diurnalis" (daily), from Latin "diurnus" (of the day), from "dies" (day).
