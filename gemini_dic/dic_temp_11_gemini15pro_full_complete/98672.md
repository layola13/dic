insulating:/ˈɪnsjəleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
绝缘的，隔热的
Preventing the passage of heat, electricity, or sound.
The house has good insulating walls, keeping it warm in winter and cool in summer. 这座房子的墙壁隔热性能良好，冬天保暖，夏天凉爽。
The insulating layer protects the wires from damage. 绝缘层保护电线免受损坏。

近义词: nonconducting, non-conductive, isolating
反义词: conducting, conductive

词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "insulate," which comes from the Late Latin "insulare" meaning "to make into an island," from "insula" meaning "island." The idea is that insulation creates a barrier, like an island separated from the mainland.
