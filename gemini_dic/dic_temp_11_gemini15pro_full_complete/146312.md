overfeed: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːd/ /ˌəʊvəˈfiːd/
verb|过量喂食|To give too much food to someone or something.|I think I've overfed the dog. 我想我给狗喂得太多了。|近义词：stuff, gorge|反义词：starve, underfeed|词性:v.

overfeed: /ˌoʊvərˈfɛd/ /ˌəʊvəˈfɛd/
adjective|吃得太饱的，被过度喂食的|Having been given too much food.|an overfed cat 一只吃得太饱的猫|近义词：stuffed, gorged|反义词：starved, underfed|词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + feed.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above, beyond, excessively." "Feed" comes from the Old English "fēdan," meaning "to nourish, give food to."
