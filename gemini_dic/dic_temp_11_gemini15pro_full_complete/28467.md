carburizers:/ˈkɑːrbjəˌraɪzərz/
n.
渗碳剂
Substances used to introduce carbon into the surface of steel or other metals during carburizing.
The carburizers used in this process are a mixture of charcoal and barium carbonate.  这种工艺中使用的渗碳剂是木炭和碳酸钡的混合物。
Solid carburizers are packed around the steel components. 固体渗碳剂填充在钢部件周围。
synonyms: case hardening agents, carbonizing agents
antonyms: decarburizers
