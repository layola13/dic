handrails:/ˈhændˌreɪlz/ | n. |扶手，栏杆|A rail fixed to posts or a wall for people to hold on to for support, especially on stairs or ramps. |Make sure you hold onto the handrails when going down the stairs. 确保下楼梯时抓住扶手。| The handrails were newly polished and gleamed in the sunlight. 扶手刚擦过，在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: railing, banister |Antonyms: None | Noun
