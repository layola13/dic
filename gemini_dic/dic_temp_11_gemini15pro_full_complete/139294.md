obliviously:/əˈblɪviəsli/ | adv. | 显然地；不在意地；健忘地 | in a way that shows you are not aware of something | He walked obliviously into the pole. 他毫无察觉地撞到了杆子上。 |  Synonyms: unmindful, unaware, clueless, incognizant | Antonyms: mindfully, aware, conscious, alert | adverb

oblivious:/əˈblɪviəs/ | adj. | 遗忘的；不在意的；未察觉的 | not aware of or not concerned about what is happening around one. | He was oblivious to the danger. 他没有意识到危险。 | Synonyms: unaware, unconscious, unmindful, inattentive | Antonyms: aware, conscious, mindful, attentive | adjective
