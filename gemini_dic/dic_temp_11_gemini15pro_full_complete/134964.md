nonnationals:/nɒnˈnæʃənəlz/ /ˌnɑːnˈnæʃənəlz/
noun
非国民；外国人
people who are not citizens of a particular country
例句：The nonnationals were required to register with the local authorities.  非国民被要求向地方当局登记。

例句：Nonnationals do not have the right to vote in this country.  非国民在这个国家没有投票权。

synonym:foreigners, aliens, immigrants
antonym:nationals, citizens
