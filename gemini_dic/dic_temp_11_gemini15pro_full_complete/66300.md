excretion:/ɪkˈskriːʃən/ /ɛkˈskriʃən/| n. |排泄，排泄物|The process of eliminating or expelling waste matter from the body.  The removal of metabolic waste from the body.|The kidneys play a vital role in excretion. 肾脏在排泄中起着至关重要的作用。| The excretion of toxins from the body is essential for health.  从体内排出毒素对健康至关重要。|近义词：elimination, discharge, expulsion|反义词: absorption, retention|词性:noun
