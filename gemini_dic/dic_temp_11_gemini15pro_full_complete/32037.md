cherishingly:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃɪŋli/
adverb
珍爱地，爱惜地
In a way that shows affection and care.
She held the baby cherishingly in her arms. 她珍爱地把婴儿抱在怀里。
cherishingly:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃɪŋli/
adverb
珍爱地，爱惜地
Showing great affection and care.
He looked at the old photograph cherishingly. 他珍爱地看着那张旧照片。
近义词:affectionately, tenderly, lovingly
反义词:neglectfully, carelessly, indifferently
词性:adverb
词源:From cherish + -ing + -ly. Cherish comes from Old French cherir "to hold dear," from Late Latin cariscus "precious," of uncertain origin.
