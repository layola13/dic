maasai:/mɑːˈsaɪ/| n. adj.|马赛人，马赛人的|A member of an East African ethnic group inhabiting southern Kenya and northern Tanzania.|例句：The Maasai are known for their distinctive customs and dress. 马赛人以其独特的习俗和服饰而闻名。

例句：Maasai culture is rich in tradition. 马赛文化富有传统。|近义词：Nilotic (related ethnic group)|反义词: None (no single antonym for an ethnic group)|词性:noun, adjective
