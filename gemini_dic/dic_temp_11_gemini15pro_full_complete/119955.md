millefiore:/ˌmɪliˈfjɔːreɪ/ /ˌmɪləˈfjɔːri/
n.
千花玻璃；千花蜜饯；千花粉
A glassmaking technique in which rods of colored glass are fused together to create patterns, especially floral ones, which are then sliced to reveal the design. Also, a similar technique used in candy making.
例句：The artist created a beautiful millefiore paperweight.  艺术家创作了一个美丽的千花玻璃镇纸。

例句：She gave me a box of delicious millefiore candies for my birthday. 她送给我一盒美味的千花蜜饯作为生日礼物。

近义词：mosaic, tessellated
反义词: (No direct antonym for this artistic technique or type of candy)
词性: noun
