bezoartical:/ˌbezɔːrˈtɪkəl/
adjective

Of, relating to, or resembling a bezoar.
The museum displayed a fascinating collection of bezoartical formations.  博物馆展出了一系列令人着迷的胃石状构造。
The hardened mass in the animal's stomach appeared to be bezoartical.  动物胃里的硬块看起来像是胃石。
Synonyms:  calculus-like, concretionary
Antonyms:  dissolved, fluid
词性: adjective
