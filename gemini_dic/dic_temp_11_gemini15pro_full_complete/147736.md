overprocesses:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsesɪz/
verb
过度加工
To subject to excessive or unnecessary processing.
The factory overprocesses the food, stripping it of nutrients.  工厂过度加工食品，使其失去了营养。
Overprocessing can make images look artificial. 过度处理会使图像看起来不自然。
Synonyms: overcook, overdo, overwork
Antonyms: underprocess, simplify
词性: 动词

overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/
noun
过度加工
The act of subjecting something to excessive or unnecessary processing.
The overprocess of the data led to inaccurate results.  数据的过度处理导致了不准确的结果。
The overprocess of the coffee beans ruined the flavor. 咖啡豆的过度处理破坏了味道。
Synonyms: overcooking, overdoing, overworking
Antonyms: underprocessing, simplification
词性: 名词
