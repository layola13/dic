coleads:/ˈkoʊliːdz/| n.|共同领导，共同负责人|People who share leadership responsibilities.  They jointly lead a team, project, or organization.|Example: The project has two coleads to ensure diverse perspectives are considered.  这个项目有两个共同负责人，以确保考虑到不同的观点。

Example:  The coleads presented the proposal to the board.  共同负责人向董事会提交了提案。|近义词：co-leaders, joint leaders, shared leadership|反义词:follower, subordinate|noun
