nondynastically:/ˌnɒnˌdaɪˈnæstɪk(ə)li/ /ˌnɑːnˌdaɪˈnæstɪk(ə)li/
adv.
非王朝地，不属于王朝地
Not related to, or characteristic of, a dynasty or line of hereditary rulers.
The throne was passed nondynastically to a chosen successor. 王位非王朝地传给了选定的继承人。
Synonym: non-hereditarily
Antonym: dynastically
词性: 副词
