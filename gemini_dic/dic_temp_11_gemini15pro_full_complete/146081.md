overeditorializes:/ˌoʊvərˌedɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度编辑；过分评论
Edits or comments on something excessively, often adding unnecessary or subjective opinions.
The newspaper overeditorializes its articles, often inserting the writer's personal biases. 这家报纸过度编辑文章，经常加入作者的个人偏见。
The professor overeditorialized my paper, making changes that weren't necessary and didn't improve it.  教授过度修改了我的论文，做了一些不必要的修改，并没有改进它。
Synonyms: overedits, overcomments, interjects, interferes
Antonyms: underedits, summarizes, condenses
词性: 动词
