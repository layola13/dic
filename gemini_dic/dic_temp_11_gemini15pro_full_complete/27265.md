calque:/kælk/ | n. | 仿造词，直译词 | A word or phrase borrowed from another language and translated literally. |  The English word "superman" is a calque of the German word "Übermensch."  英语单词“超人”是德语单词“Übermensch”的仿造词。

The expression "skyscraper" is a calque from a French term.  “摩天大楼”这个表达是从法语直译过来的。 | 近义词: loan translation, borrowing | 反义词: coinage, neologism | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *calque*, from *calquer* "to copy, trace," from Italian *calcare* "to tread, press, trace," from Latin *calcare* "to tread on, trample," from *calc-* "heel."
