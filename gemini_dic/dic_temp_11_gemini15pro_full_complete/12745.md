*Note:*  The word "assident" is often confused with "accident" and "assiduous".  While similar in spelling, they have very different meanings.  "Accident" refers to an unfortunate incident that happens unexpectedly and unintentionally, typically resulting in damage or injury. "Assiduous" refers to showing great care and perseverance.  Make sure you use the correct word based on the intended meaning.
