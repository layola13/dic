counter-accusation:/ˌkaʊntərˌækjuˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |反控，反指控 | An accusation made in response to a previous accusation.  It's a claim that the original accuser is actually the guilty party or is also guilty of wrongdoing. |  The defendant's counter-accusation surprised the prosecution.  被告的反控令控方感到意外。

The politician responded to the bribery allegations with a counter-accusation of conspiracy.  这位政治家对贿赂指控的回应是反控阴谋。 | synonyms: countercharge, counterclaim | antonyms: admission, confession | noun
