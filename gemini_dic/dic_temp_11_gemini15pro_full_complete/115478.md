manumission:/ˌmænjuˈmɪʃən/ | n. | 释放，解放（尤指奴隶） | The act of freeing slaves from slavery.  |  The manumission of the slaves was a cause for celebration.  奴隶的解放是一个值得庆祝的理由。 |  emancipation, liberation, release, freedom | enslavement, captivity, subjugation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *manūmissiō*, from *manūmittere* "to release from slavery," from *manu* "from the hand" + *mittere* "to send, let go."
