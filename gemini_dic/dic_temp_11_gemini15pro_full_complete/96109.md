indehiscent:/ˌɪndiˈhɪsənt/| adj.|不开裂的，不裂开的|Not opening spontaneously at maturity to release seeds, as a fruit or anther.|The indehiscent fruits of the lotus remain on the dried stalk throughout winter.  莲的闭果整个冬天都留在干枯的茎上。|近义词：closed, sealed|反义词：dehiscent, splitting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *indehiscens*, present participle of *indehiscere*, from *in-* (not) + *dehiscere* (to gape open). 最终源自拉丁语 *dehiscere*（裂开）。
