enaliosaur:/ɛˈneɪliəˌsɔːr/ |ɛˈniːlioʊˌsɔːr/ | n. | 蛇颈龙 | An extinct marine reptile of the order Plesiosauria, with a long neck, paddle-like limbs, and a short tail.  They lived during the Mesozoic Era. |  Example: The enaliosaur fossils found in Kansas suggest it was once covered by a vast inland sea.  在堪萨斯州发现的蛇颈龙化石表明，它曾经被一片广阔的内陆海覆盖。 | Example: The museum displayed a complete enaliosaur skeleton, showcasing its impressive size.  博物馆展出了一具完整的蛇颈龙骨架，展示了它惊人的体型。 |  Synonyms: plesiosaur, plesiosaurian | Antonyms: None (extinct animal, no direct opposite) | Noun
