nonmysticism:/ˌnɒnˈmɪstɪsɪzəm/
noun|非神秘主义|The rejection of mystical beliefs or practices.  It emphasizes rational thought and observable evidence over spiritual or supernatural explanations.|His nonmysticism led him to seek scientific explanations for phenomena others attributed to divine intervention.|近义词:rationalism, empiricism, skepticism|反义词:mysticism, spiritualism|n.
