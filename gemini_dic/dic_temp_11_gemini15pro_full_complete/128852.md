nigua:/niˈɡwɑː/ /nɪˈɡwɑː/
n.
沙蚤，恙螨，沙蚤病
A tropical flea (Tunga penetrans) that burrows into the skin, especially of the feet, causing intense itching and inflammation. Also called jigger, chigoe, chigger.
The nigua burrowed into his foot, causing a painful lesion.  沙蚤钻进了他的脚里，引起了疼痛的病灶。

近义词: jigger, chigoe, chigger
反义词: (No direct antonym for a parasitic insect)
词性: noun
