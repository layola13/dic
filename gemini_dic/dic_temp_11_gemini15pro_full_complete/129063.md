nitrocalcite:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈkælˌsaɪt/| n. |硝石灰|A mineral consisting of hydrous calcium nitrate, typically found as an efflorescence in dry areas.  It is a form of calcite containing nitrogen, usually as nitrate.|Efflorescences of nitrocalcite were observed on the cave walls.|Synonyms:  calcium nitrate, nitrate of lime|Antonyms: None|noun
