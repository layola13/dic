dub-out:/dʌbˈaʊt/ | v. | 剔除（声音），删除（录音片段），配音 | To remove or replace a section of an audio or video recording, especially a soundtrack. |  The producers dubbed out the offensive language from the film. 制片人从电影中删除了冒犯性的语言。

The band dubbed out the guitar solo and re-recorded it. 乐队删除了吉他独奏并重新录制。 | 近义词: remove, delete, erase, censor, replace | 反义词: add, insert, include | 词性: 动词
