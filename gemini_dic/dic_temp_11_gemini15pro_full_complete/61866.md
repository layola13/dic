emporium:/ɪmˈpɔːriəm/ | n. | 商场，大商店；贸易中心 | A large retail store, often selling a wide variety of goods; a center of commerce. |  She bought a beautiful silk scarf at the emporium.  她在商场买了一条漂亮的丝巾。

The city was known as an emporium for exotic spices. 这座城市以其异国香料贸易中心而闻名。 | 近义词: store, shop, market, bazaar | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly small shop or kiosk) | 词性: noun
