gracies:/ˈɡreɪsiz/ | n. |感谢; 谢谢（gracia的复数形式）| A Portuguese and Spanish word meaning "thanks," "graces," or "thank you."  It's the plural form of "gracia."  Often used in martial arts contexts. | Example:  After the jiu-jitsu class, the students said "gracies" to their instructor. 课后，学生们对他们的教练说“谢谢”。| Example:  He offered his gracies for the meal. 他对这顿饭表示感谢。| Synonym: thanks, thank you | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an expression of gratitude) | Noun
