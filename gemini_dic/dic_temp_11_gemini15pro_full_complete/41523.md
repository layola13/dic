coronamen:/ˌkɔːrəˈneɪmən/ /ˌkɑːrəˈneɪmən/
n.
冠;顶;冠状物
The topmost part of something, especially a structure or natural formation, resembling a crown.
The coronamen of the tooth is covered by enamel.  牙齿的冠部被牙釉质覆盖。
apex, crown, crest, summit
base, bottom, foot
