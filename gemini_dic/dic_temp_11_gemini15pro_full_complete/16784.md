basam:/ˈbeɪsʌm/ | n. |香料，调味品 (especially in South Asian cooking) |A fragrant spice or seasoning, especially used in South Asian cuisine. It is often a blend of various spices and herbs.|  She added basam to the biryani for an aromatic flavor. 她往印度香饭里加了巴萨姆以增加香味。| synonyms: spice, seasoning, masala, condiment | antonyms: blandness, insipidity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi/Urdu _basam_ (بسَم), ultimately from Persian _basm_ (بسم), meaning "fragrance," "perfume," or "balm."
