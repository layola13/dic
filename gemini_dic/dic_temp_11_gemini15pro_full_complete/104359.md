如果你是想了解疑问代词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) |pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. |

英文解析: "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, and adverb to ask questions about things, actions, or qualities.
