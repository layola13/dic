lingered:/ˈlɪŋɡərd/| v. |徘徊，逗留，留恋|To stay somewhere longer than necessary or expected, often because of reluctance to leave.| The smell of smoke lingered in the air. 空气中弥漫着烟味。|近义词：stayed, remained, tarried|反义词:left, departed|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English *lingren, from Old Norse lengja (“to lengthen, prolong”), from langr (“long”).
