brigadier:/ˌbrɪɡəˈdɪər/| n.| 准將; 陸軍準將|An army officer of relatively high rank, typically holding the rank of a one-star general.|The brigadier commanded the entire armored division.|Synonyms: general, commander|Antonyms: private, soldier|noun
