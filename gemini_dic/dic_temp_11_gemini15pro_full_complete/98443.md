insperse:/ɪnˈspɜːrs/ | v. | 散布，点缀，穿插 | To distribute something at intervals among other things. |  He interspersed jokes throughout his lecture to keep the audience engaged. 他在整个演讲过程中穿插了笑话，以保持观众的参与度。

The author interspersed personal anecdotes throughout the novel. 作者在整部小说中穿插了个人轶事。


|近义词：scatter, sprinkle, distribute, interweave, pepper |反义词：concentrate, gather, collect | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inspersus*, past participle of *inspergere* "to sprinkle upon," from *in-* "on, upon" + *spargere* "to scatter."
