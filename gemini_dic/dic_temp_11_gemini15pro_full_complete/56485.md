domains:/dəˈmeɪnz/, /doʊˈmeɪnz/| n.|领域，范围，域名|A domain is a specific sphere of activity or knowledge.  A domain name is the address of a website on the internet.|例句：He's an expert in the domain of astrophysics. 他是天体物理学领域的专家。|例句：She registered the domain name for her new business. 她为她的新业务注册了域名。|近义词：fields, areas, realms, territories, website addresses|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly limitations)|词性:noun
