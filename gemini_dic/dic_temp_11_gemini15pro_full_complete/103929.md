judgers:/ˈdʒʌdʒərz/
n.
法官; 裁判员; 评价者; 批评者
People who form opinions or deliver verdicts, often critically.
The judgers awarded first prize to the youngest contestant. 评委们把一等奖颁给了最年轻的选手。

The judgers were impressed by her performance. 评委们对她的表演印象深刻。

近义词: critics, assessors, evaluators, arbitrators
反义词: supporters, defenders, advocates
词性: noun (plural)

judger:/ˈdʒʌdʒər/
n.
法官; 裁判员; 评价者; 批评者
A person who judges.
He is a harsh judger of other people's work. 他对别人的工作苛刻评价。

She doesn't want to be a judger, but she can't help noticing the flaws. 她不想做一个评判者，但她忍不住注意到那些缺陷。

近义词: critic, assessor, evaluator, arbitrator
反义词: supporter, defender, advocate
词性: noun (singular)
