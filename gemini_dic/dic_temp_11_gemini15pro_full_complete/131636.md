noncomprehensive:/ˌnɒnˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˌkɑːmprɪˈhensɪv/| adj.|不全面的，不广泛的，不详尽的|Not including all aspects or elements; incomplete; not thorough.|The report provided a noncomprehensive overview of the market, omitting key details about emerging trends.|近义词：incomplete, partial, limited, restricted|反义词：comprehensive, thorough, complete, exhaustive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + comprehensive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "comprehensive" comes from the Late Latin *comprehensivus*, from *comprehendere* "to grasp, comprehend."
