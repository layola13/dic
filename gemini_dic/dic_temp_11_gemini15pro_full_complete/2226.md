addresses:/əˈdrɛsɪz/ | n. |地址；演说；称呼；处理；致辞|A location of a place or building.  The act of speaking to someone or a group. The process of dealing with a problem or issue.|
例句：Please write your address clearly on the envelope. 请在信封上清楚地写下你的地址。

例句：The President's address to the nation was broadcast live. 总统的全国讲话进行了现场直播。

近义词：location, speech, handle, tackle, salutation
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)

addresses:/əˈdrɛsɪz/ | v. | 演说；写姓名地址；向…讲话；称呼；处理；致辞|To speak to someone or a group. To write the name and address on an envelope or package. To deal with a problem or issue. To direct a remark or written statement to.|
例句：She addressed the crowd with passion and conviction. 她充满激情和信念地向人群发表了演说。

例句：He addressed the letter to his lawyer. 他把信寄给了他的律师。

近义词：speak, direct, handle, tackle, greet
反义词: ignore, neglect
词性: verb (third person singular present)
