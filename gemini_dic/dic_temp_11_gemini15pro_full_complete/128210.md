neurobiology:/ˌnʊroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒi/ /ˌnjʊərəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/
n.|神经生物学|The study of the nervous system, including its anatomy, physiology, and biochemistry.|She's majoring in neurobiology. 她主修神经生物学。|近义词: neuroscience|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
neuro- (from Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve") + -biology (from Greek "bios" meaning "life" + "-logia" meaning "study of")
