appenders:/əˈpɛndərz/| n.|附录，附件，阑尾|Something added to the end of a book or document; a supplementary section.  In anatomy, a vestigial organ attached to the large intestine.|例句：The author included several appendices at the end of the book, containing additional data and charts. 作者在书的末尾附上了几个附录，其中包含额外的数据和图表。

例句：The appendix in the report provided further details on the methodology used. 报告中的附录提供了关于所用方法的进一步细节。|近义词：addendum, supplement, attachment, annex|反义词:preface, introduction|词性:noun
