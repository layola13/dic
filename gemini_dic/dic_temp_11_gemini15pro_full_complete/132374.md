nondeliverance:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪvərəns/ (美式) /ˌnɒndɪˈlɪvərəns/ (英式)
noun
未交付，未履行
Failure to deliver something or someone, or a state of not being rescued or liberated.
The nondeliverance of the promised goods led to a lawsuit.  未交付承诺的货物导致了一场诉讼。
The hostages prayed for nondeliverance from their captors. 人质祈祷不要被俘虏者释放。（此处nondeliverance语义反转，表示不被释放）
近义词: nonperformance, default, failure
反义词: delivery, performance, fulfillment
词性: 名词
