cataphoras:/kəˈtæfərəz/ | n. |前指|A cataphor is a word or expression that refers forward to another word or expression that will be used later in the text. |Near the end of the driveway sat *it*: a shiny, red sports car. (Here, "it" refers to the "shiny, red sports car" mentioned later.)|近义词:forward reference|反义词:anaphor|词性:noun
