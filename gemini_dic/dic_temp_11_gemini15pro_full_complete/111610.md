loca's: /ˈloʊkəz/ | n. | 地方；餐馆（尤指拉丁美洲餐馆）|  Short for "local's," often referring to a restaurant or bar frequented by locals, particularly those serving Latin American cuisine. It's informal and often seen on signs or in colloquial speech.  | Example: "Let's go grab some tacos at the loca's." 咱们去那家当地餐馆吃点玉米饼吧。| Example: "The loca's down the street has the best burritos." 街那头的当地餐馆有最好吃的墨西哥卷饼。| Synonym: restaurant, eatery, joint, cantina (especially for Mexican/Latin American restaurants) | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with "tourist trap") | Noun
