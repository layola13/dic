flageolet:/ˌflædʒəˈlɛt/| n.|六孔竖笛；哨笛；白扁豆|A small fipple flute with six finger holes.|He played a lively tune on his flageolet. 他用六孔竖笛吹奏了一首欢快的曲子。|近义词：recorder, flute, pipe|反义词:|noun

Etymology:
From French *flageolet*, diminutive of *flageo*, from Old French *flageol* (“pipe, flute”), from *flajol*, from Vulgar Latin *flautiolus*, diminutive of Latin *flauta* (“flute”).
