apriority:/ə praɪˈɔːrəti/ |n.|优先事项，优先权|something that is considered more important than other things|例句：Finishing the project is our top priority. 完成这个项目是我们的首要任务。|例句：Road safety is a priority for the government. 道路安全是政府的优先事项。|近义词：precedence, preference|反义词：unimportance, insignificance|noun

apriority:/ə praɪˈɔːrəti/ |adj.|优先的，在前的|considered more important than other things|例句：The hospital gives priority treatment to emergency cases. 医院优先处理急诊病例。|例句：Priority mail is guaranteed to arrive within two days. 优先邮件保证在两天内送达。|近义词：preferred, favored|反义词：subordinate, secondary|adjective
