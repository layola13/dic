eukaryote: /juːˈkæriəʊt/ (UK) /juˈkæriˌoʊt/ (US) | n. | 真核生物 | An organism whose cells contain a nucleus surrounded by a membrane and whose DNA is bound together by proteins (histones) into chromosomes.  | Eukaryotes include animals, plants, and fungi. 真核生物包括动物、植物和真菌。 | Example 1: The amoeba is a single-celled eukaryote. 变形虫是单细胞真核生物。

Example 2:  Eukaryotic cells are generally much larger and more complex than prokaryotic cells. 真核细胞通常比原核细胞更大更复杂。 | synonym: eucaryote | antonym: prokaryote | noun
