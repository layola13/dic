gerry:/ˈdʒɛri/| v. n.|不公正地划分选区；划分选区的舞弊行为；不公正划分选区的选区|To manipulate the boundaries of (an electoral constituency) so as to favor one party or class.|例句：The state legislature gerrymandered the district to ensure the incumbent's reelection. 州议会为了确保现任议员连任，对该选区进行了不公正的划分。

例句：The gerrymandered district snaked across three counties.  这个不公正划分的选区蜿蜒穿过三个县。|近义词：manipulate, rig|反义词:fair districting|词性：动词，名词
