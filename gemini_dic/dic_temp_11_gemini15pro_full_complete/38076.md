compositor:/kəmˈpɒzɪtər/ (UK) /kəmˈpɑːzɪtər/ (US)| n.|排字工人；排版工人；合成器；作曲家|A person who arranges type for printing or prepares material for other forms of visual presentation, or a device used for this purpose.  Also, a person who composes music.|The compositor carefully arranged the text for the newspaper headline. 排字工人仔细地排列了报纸标题的文字。|Synonyms: typesetter, layout artist, designer|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

compositor:/kəmˈpɒzɪtər/ (UK) /kəmˈpɑːzɪtər/ (US)| n.|作曲家|A person who writes music, especially classical music.|The compositor of the opera received a standing ovation. 歌剧的作曲家获得了全场起立鼓掌。|Synonyms: composer, musician|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
