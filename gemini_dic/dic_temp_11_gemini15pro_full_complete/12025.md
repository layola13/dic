arthurian:/ɑːrˈθʊəriən/
adjective|亚瑟王的；亚瑟王时代的|Relating to King Arthur and the knights of the Round Table.|例句：Arthurian legends are full of magic and chivalry. 亚瑟王传说充满了魔法和骑士精神。|近义词：Camelot, legendary|反义词：modern, contemporary
adjective|亚瑟王的；亚瑟王时代的|Of or relating to King Arthur of Britain and the knights of the Round Table, or to the time in which they are supposed to have lived.|例句：The Arthurian romance is a popular theme in literature. 亚瑟王式的浪漫故事是文学中的一个流行主题。|近义词：chivalric, medieval|反义词：modern, contemporary


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arthur + -ian.  Ultimately from Latin Artorius (a Roman family name).
