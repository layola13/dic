commercializer:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪzər/
n.
商业化者；商品化者
A person or company that brings a product or service to market.
The university partnered with a commercializer to bring its groundbreaking research to the public.  大学与一家商业化公司合作，将其突破性的研究成果推向公众。
entrepreneur, marketer, developer, promoter
traditionalist,  conservationist
noun
