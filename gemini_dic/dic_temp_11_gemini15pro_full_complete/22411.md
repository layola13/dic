book: /bʊk/ | n. v. | 书; 预订; 登记 | a written or printed work consisting of pages glued or sewn together along one side and bound in covers.  To reserve (accommodations, a place, etc.); buy (a ticket) in advance. | I am reading a book. 我正在读一本书。 I booked a flight to London. 我预订了去伦敦的航班。 |  近义词: volume, publication, reserve, schedule | 反义词: cancel | noun, verb
