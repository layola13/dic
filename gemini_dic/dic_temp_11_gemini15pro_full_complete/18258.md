believes:/bɪˈliːvz/| v.|相信，认为|accepts something as true; holds an opinion; has faith in something|She believes that hard work will lead to success. 她相信努力工作会带来成功。|近义词：thinks, trusts, accepts, supposes|反义词：doubts, disbelieves|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *bileven*, from Old English *belīefan* (“believe, entrust”), from *be-* + *līefan* (“allow, permit; believe, trust”) (akin to Old High German *gilouben*, *giloubēn* (“believe”)). The second element is related to Old English *lēof* (“dear, beloved; agreeable; pleasant”).
