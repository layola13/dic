buxom:/ˈbʌksəm/| adj.|丰满的，体态匀称的|Full-bosomed; plump and healthy-looking.  Generally used to describe women.  Implies an attractive fullness of figure.|例句：The buxom waitress served us our drinks.  丰满的女服务员给我们上了饮料。|例句：Renaissance paintings often depict buxom women as symbols of fertility. 文艺复兴时期的绘画经常描绘丰满的女性作为生育的象征。|近义词：curvaceous, plump, ample, full-figured|反义词：slim, thin, slender|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *buhsom*, from Old English *būhsum* (“pliant, obedient, submissive”), from *būgan* (“to bend, bow, stoop”) + *-sum*.  The meaning shifted over time from "obedient, yielding" to "healthy, vigorous," and then to its modern sense referring to a full figure.
