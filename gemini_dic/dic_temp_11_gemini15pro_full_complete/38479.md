conchinine:/ˈkɒŋkɪˌniːn/ /ˈkɑːŋkɪˌniːn/
n.
奎宁碱; 辛可宁
A white crystalline alkaloid, C₂₀H₂₄N₂O₂, obtained from cinchona bark and isomeric with quinine, but less active medicinally.
Conchinine is sometimes used as an antimalarial drug, although it is less effective than quinine. 奎宁碱有时被用作抗疟疾药物，尽管它的效果不如奎宁。
Conchinine can be extracted from the bark of the cinchona tree. 奎宁碱可以从金鸡纳树的树皮中提取。

近义词:quinidine, cinchonine
反义词:None

词性:noun
