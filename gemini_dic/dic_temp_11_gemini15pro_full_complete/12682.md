assented:/əˈsɛntɪd/
verb
同意，赞成
agreed to something, especially after thoughtful consideration
The king assented to the new law. 国王同意了新法律。
近义词：agreed, consented, concurred
反义词：dissented, objected, refused
词性: v. (past tense and past participle of assent)

assent:/əˈsɛnt/
noun
同意，赞成
agreement to or approval of something
She gave her assent to the proposal. 她同意了这个提议。
近义词：agreement, approval, consent
反义词：dissent, objection, refusal
词性: n.

assent:/əˈsɛnt/
verb
同意，赞成
to express agreement or approval
He assented to their request. 他同意了他们的请求。
近义词：agree, consent, concur
反义词：dissent, object, refuse
词性: v.
