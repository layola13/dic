largish:/ˈlɑːrdʒɪʃ/| adj.|相当大的，颇大的|Relatively large; somewhat large.  It describes something that is bigger than average, but not huge.|The house has a largish garden. 这房子有一个相当大的花园。|近义词：large, sizable, substantial|反义词:small, tiny, little|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From large + -ish. The suffix "-ish" indicates a moderate degree of the adjective.
