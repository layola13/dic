gnathite: /ˈnæθaɪt/
noun
颚肢
A mouthpart of an arthropod, especially one of a pair of appendages modifying to assist with feeding.
The gnathite helps the crayfish manipulate food towards its mouth.  小龙虾的颚肢帮助它将食物送入口中。

gnathite: /ˈnæθaɪt/

Etymology: From Greek *gnath- (jaw) + -ite (suffix forming nouns).

Synonyms:  mouthpart, appendage (in this context)
Antonyms:  N/A (lacks a direct opposite)
Word type: Noun
