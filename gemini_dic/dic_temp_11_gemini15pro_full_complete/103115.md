jerkier:/ˈdʒɜːrkiər (US) /ˈdʒɜːkɪər (UK)| adj.|更急促的，更颠簸的，更抽搐的|Comparative form of jerky: more jerky.  Characterized by sudden, sharp movements. |The ride in the old jeep was jerkier than I expected. 这辆旧吉普车的行驶比我预想的更颠簸。|The animation was jerkier in the earlier version of the film.  这部电影早期版本的动画更不流畅。|近义词：abrupt, spasmodic, uneven, fitful|反义词：smooth, fluid, continuous|词性:形容词
词源: From *jerk* + *-ier* (comparative suffix).  *Jerk* itself comes from a imitative origin, referring to a sudden sharp movement.
