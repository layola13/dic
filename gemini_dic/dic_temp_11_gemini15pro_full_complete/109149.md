legislators:/ˈledʒɪsleɪtərz/
n.
立法者，立法委员
People who make laws; members of a legislative body.
The legislators debated the new healthcare bill for months. 立法者就新的医疗保健法案辩论了数月。
The state legislators voted to increase funding for education. 州立法委员投票决定增加教育经费。
lawmakers, representatives, senators, congressmen/women, assemblymen/women
dictators, autocrats
noun
