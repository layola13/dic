antimechanistic:/ˌæntiˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/
| adj. | 反机械论的 | Opposed to or rejecting the explanation of phenomena by reference to purely mechanical principles.  It suggests a belief in something beyond the purely physical or material in nature or in human behavior. |  Many psychologists in the early 20th century held antimechanistic views, believing the mind to be more than just a complex machine.  许多20世纪早期的心里学家持有反机械论的观点，相信心灵不仅仅是一台复杂的机器。 | vitalistic, holistic, organic, teleological | mechanistic, reductionist | adjective
