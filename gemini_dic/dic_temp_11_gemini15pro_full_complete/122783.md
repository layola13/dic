molasse:/məˈlæs/ /moʊˈlæs/| n.|  软砂岩，磨拉斯|A sedimentary rock formed by the consolidation of materials eroded from mountains and deposited in a nearby basin.  It typically consists of poorly sorted conglomerate, sandstone, and shale.|The Swiss Plateau is largely underlain by molasse deposits.|Synonyms: conglomerate, sandstone, sedimentary rock|Antonyms: igneous rock, metamorphic rock|noun
