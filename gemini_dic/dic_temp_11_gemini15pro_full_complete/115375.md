mansard:/ˈmænsɑːrd/ (US), /ˈmɑːnsɑːd/ (UK)| n. | 屋顶；阁楼 |A roof with two slopes on each of its four sides, the lower slope being steeper than the upper.| The house has a distinctive mansard roof. 这座房子有一个独特的曼萨德屋顶。| synonyms: roof, attic, garret | antonyms: floor, foundation | noun

mansard:/ˈmænsɑːrd/ (US), /ˈmɑːnsɑːd/ (UK)| adj. | 曼萨德式的 | Relating to or designating a mansard roof.| The mansard windows offered a charming view of the city. 曼萨德式的窗户提供了迷人的城市景观。| synonyms: sloped, angled | antonyms: flat, level | adjective
