droit:/drwɑː/ (US), /drɔɪt/ (UK)| n.|权利，法律，正义|A right or legal entitlement.|He claimed his droit to speak freely. 他声称自己有言论自由的权利。|Synonyms: right, entitlement, privilege, prerogative, claim|Antonyms: duty, obligation|noun

droit:/drwɑː/ (US), /drɔɪt/ (UK)| adj.| 正确的，合法的 (古语，现已少用) |Legally correct; just.|It was considered a droit action in those times. 在那个时代，这被认为是合法的行为。|Synonyms: right, lawful, legitimate, proper|Antonyms: wrong, unlawful, illegitimate, improper|adjective
