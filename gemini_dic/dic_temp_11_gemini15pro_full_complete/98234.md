insensibilities:/ɪnˌsɛnsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
麻木，无感觉；冷淡，漠不关心
lack of awareness or feeling; unresponsiveness
He showed a chilling insensibility to the suffering of others. 他对别人的痛苦表现出令人心寒的漠不关心。

The drug induced a state of insensibility to pain. 这种药物能使人对疼痛麻木。

His insensibilities towards his family shocked everyone.  他对家人的冷漠震惊了所有人。

近义词：apathy, indifference, numbness, unresponsiveness, coldness
反义词：sensitivity, responsiveness, compassion, empathy, warmth
词性：名词（plural noun）


词源：Late Middle English: from Old French insensibilité, from insensible (see insensible).
