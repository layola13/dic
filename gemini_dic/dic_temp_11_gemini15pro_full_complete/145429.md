overcharitableness:/ˌoʊvərˌtʃærɪtəblnəs/
n.
过度仁慈；过分宽厚
Excessive charitableness; being too charitable or forgiving.
He showed overcharitableness towards his enemy, forgiving him completely. 他对敌人表现出过度的仁慈，完全原谅了他。
Near Synonyms: overleniency, overindulgence, excessive forgiveness
Antonyms:  unforgivingness, ruthlessness, severity
词性: noun
