monotonously:/məˈnɒtənəsli/ | adv. | 单调地，乏味地 | In a monotonous manner; in a way that lacks variation and is tedious. |  He spoke monotonously, and I almost fell asleep. 他说话单调乏味，我差点睡着了。|  The rain droned monotonously against the windowpane. 雨单调地敲打着窗玻璃。 |  tediously, dully, boringly, repetitively | interestingly, engagingly, excitingly, variously | adverb

Etymology->
From Late Latin *monotonus + -ous + -ly. From Ancient Greek μονότονος (monótonos, “monotonous”), from μόνος (mónos, “single, alone”) + τόνος (tónos, “tone, pitch”).
