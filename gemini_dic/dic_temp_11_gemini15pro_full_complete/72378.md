flubbed:/flʌbd/
verb
搞砸了，弄糟了
failed to do something properly or successfully
He completely flubbed his lines in the play. 他在剧中完全搞砸了他的台词。
近义词：botched, messed up, bungled, muffed
反义词：succeeded, aced, nailed
词性：动词
