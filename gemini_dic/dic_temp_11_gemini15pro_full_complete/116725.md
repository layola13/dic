maudlinwort:/ˈmɔːdlɪnwɜːrt/ | n. | 春飞蓬; 春飞蓬属植物 | A European plant ( *Chrysanthemum leucanthemum*, family Asteraceae, synonym *Leucanthemum vulgare*) of the daisy family, with white ray flowers and a yellow center.  It is also known as ox-eye daisy, moon daisy, and dog daisy. |  Maudlinwort grows profusely in meadows. 春飞蓬在草地上茂盛地生长。|Synonyms: ox-eye daisy, moon daisy, dog daisy, marguerite, Leucanthemum vulgare | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
