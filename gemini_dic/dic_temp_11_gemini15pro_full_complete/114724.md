mallotus:/məˈləʊtəs/| n. |红树科植物；野桐属|A genus of plants in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae, many of which are known for their medicinal properties and use in traditional remedies.  Many species are also important sources of timber and dyes.|The bark of the Mallotus philippensis tree is used to make a traditional dye. 野桐属植物的树皮被用来制作传统染料。|近义词：spurge (泛指大戟科植物)|反义词:  (无明确反义词，可以考虑其他植物科属)|词性:noun
