nonsensibly:/nɑnˈsɛnsɪbli/ /nɒnˈsɛnsɪbli/
adverb
无意义地，荒谬地
in a way that is not sensible; foolishly
He acted nonsensibly by investing all his money in a single stock. 他把所有的钱都投资在一只股票上，这样做很不明智。
Synonym: foolishly, absurdly, irrationally, ridiculously
Antonym: sensibly, wisely, rationally, prudently
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + sensible + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "sensible" means "having or showing good sense or judgment," and "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs.
