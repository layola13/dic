inefficacy:/ˌɪnˈɛfɪkəsi/ | n. | 无效；无效力；无能 |lack of power to produce an effect; failure to produce the desired effect | The inefficacy of the drug was apparent after several trials.  这种药物在几次试验后显现出无效。 | synonyms: ineffectiveness, impotence, futility, uselessness | antonyms: efficacy, effectiveness, potency, usefulness | noun
