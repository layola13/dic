blunger:/ˈblʌndʒər/| n.|搅拌机，混合器，调浆机|A machine with rotating blades used for mixing or grinding materials, especially in the ceramics industry, by blending them with a liquid.|The blunger mixed the clay and water into a smooth slip. 这台搅拌机将黏土和水混合成光滑的泥浆。|近义词：mixer, blender, agitator|反义词:|名词
