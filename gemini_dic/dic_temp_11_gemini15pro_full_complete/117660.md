megera:/məˈɡɪərə/ | n. |泼妇，悍妇|A shrewish or сварливая woman; a termagant. |She's a real megera, always yelling and causing trouble. 她真是个泼妇，总是大喊大叫，惹是生非。|  Example: The market stall owner was a megera, shouting at anyone who dared to touch her produce.  那个摊主是个泼妇，对任何敢碰她产品的人都大喊大叫。|Synonyms: shrew, virago, harpy, termagant, vixen|Antonyms: angel, saint, sweetheart|noun
