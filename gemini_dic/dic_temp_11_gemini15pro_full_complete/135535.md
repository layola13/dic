nonpliant:/nɒnˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 不顺从的，不服从的；倔强的；不易弯曲的 | Not easily bent or flexible; unyielding; resistant to being influenced or controlled. |  The nonpliant material made it difficult to shape. 这种不顺从的材料使它很难成形。

The nonpliant child refused to listen to his parents.  这个倔强的孩子拒绝听父母的话。 | 近义词: inflexible, rigid, unyielding, stubborn, obstinate, recalcitrant | 反义词: flexible, pliable, compliant, obedient, docile | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From non- (not) + pliant (bending readily; easily influenced).  "Pliant" comes from Old French "pliant" present participle of "plier" (to bend), from Latin "plicare" (to fold).
