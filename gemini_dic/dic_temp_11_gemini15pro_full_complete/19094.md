bets:/bets/ | n. | 赌注，打赌 | Wagers placed on an outcome. | He placed several bets on the horse race. 他在赛马上下了几注。 |  I lost all my bets on the football game. 我在足球比赛中输掉了所有的赌注。 | synonyms: wagers, stakes | antonyms: guarantees, assurances | noun

bet:/bet/ | n. | 打赌，赌注 | An agreement in which people risk money on an outcome. | He made a bet with his friend about the game's score. 他和他的朋友就比赛比分打了个赌。 | The bet was for $100.  赌注是100美元。 | synonyms: wager, gamble | antonyms:  concession, forfeit | noun
bet:/bet/ | v. | 打赌，敢说 | To risk money on an outcome. | I bet you $20 that it will rain tomorrow. 我跟你赌20美元，明天会下雨。 |  She bet him that she could finish the race first. 她跟他打赌，她能第一个完成比赛。 | synonyms: wager, gamble | antonyms: abstain, concede | verb
