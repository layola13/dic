blencher's:/ˈblɛntʃərz/
n.
漂白工的; 漂白剂的
belonging to or associated with a blencher (a person or machine that bleaches something).
The blencher's gloves were stained with bleach. 漂白工的手套沾满了漂白剂。
synonyms: bleacher's
antonyms: dyer's
noun
