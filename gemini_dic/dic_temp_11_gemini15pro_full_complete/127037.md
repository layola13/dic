nassa:/ˈnæsə/
noun
鱼梁，一种锥形的捕鱼陷阱
A nassa is a conical wicker fish trap used in shallow water.
Fishermen set several nassas in the lagoon hoping for a good catch. 渔民在泻湖里放置了几个鱼梁，希望能有个好收成。
近义词: fish trap, fish weir, fyke net
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open water")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nassa, from Greek νάσα (nasa).
