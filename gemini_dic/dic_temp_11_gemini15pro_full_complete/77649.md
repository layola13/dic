gelatinousnesses:/dʒɪˈlætɪnəsnəsɪz/
n.
胶状, 粘稠
the quality or state of being gelatinous.
The gelatinousnesses of the substance made it difficult to handle.  这种物质的胶状特性使其难以处理。
The various gelatinousnesses of the alien lifeforms were unsettling. 这些外星生命形式的各种胶状物质令人不安。
viscosity, jellylike consistency, sliminess
firmness, solidity, hardness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gelatinous + -ness.  Gelatinous comes from gelatin, from French gélatine, from Italian gelatina, from Late Latin gelata, from gelatus (frozen), past participle of gelare (to freeze), from Latin gelu (frost).
