cumulostratus:/ˌkjuːmjʊləˈstreɪtəs/ /ˌkjuːmjʊloʊˈstreɪtəs/
n.
层积云
A principal cloud type (genus) of the family of medium clouds, of moderate vertical development in the form of turrets or towers, the upper parts often having a fibrous texture and sometimes resembling cirrus. Cumulostratus is formed by the spreading out of cumulus; it may be transformed from altostratus or nimbostratus.
例句：The cumulostratus clouds filled the sky, hinting at the approaching storm. 层积云布满了天空，预示着暴风雨即将来临。

例句：The pilot reported seeing cumulostratus formations during the flight. 飞行员报告在飞行过程中看到了层积云的形成。

近义词：stratocumulus, altostratus
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a cloud type.  One might consider clear skies as a contrasting condition.)
