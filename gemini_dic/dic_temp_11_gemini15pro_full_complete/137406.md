nonterminal:/nɒnˈtɜːmɪnəl/ | adj. | 非终结符；非终止的 | Not terminal; not ending; in linguistics, designating a constituent of a phrase structure grammar that can be rewritten as other constituents (opposed to terminal).  |  In computer science, a nonterminal symbol can be replaced by other symbols, unlike a terminal symbol which represents a concrete value. 在计算机科学中，非终结符可以被其他符号替换，不像终结符那样代表一个具体的值。 |  Nonterminal nodes in a parse tree represent phrases, while terminal nodes represent words. 在解析树中，非终结符节点表示短语，而终结符节点表示单词。 | synonyms: intermediate, transitional | antonyms: terminal, final | adjective
