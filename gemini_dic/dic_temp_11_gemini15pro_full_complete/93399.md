illish:/ˈɪlɪʃ/ | adj. | 略有不适的；有点不舒服的 |  Slightly unwell or uncomfortable. | I felt a bit illish after eating that seafood. 吃了那个海鲜后我感觉有点不舒服。| 近义词:queasy, unwell, peaky | 反义词:well, healthy, comfortable | 词性:形容词

词源: "illish" is derived from the adjective "ill" with the addition of the suffix "-ish," which indicates a lesser degree or quality of the root word.  "Ill" itself has a long history, originating from the Old English word "ill," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*illaz," meaning "bad, evil."
