buscon:/ˈbʌskən/ | n. | 探矿者，搜索者 | A person who searches or explores, especially for minerals or other valuable resources. |  He was a buscon, always searching for the next big gold strike. 他是一位探矿者，总是在寻找下一个大的金矿。|  prospector, explorer, searcher |  idler, loafer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *buscón*, from *buscar* "to search," ultimately from Late Latin *buscare*.
