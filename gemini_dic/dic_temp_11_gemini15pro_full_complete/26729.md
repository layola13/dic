cadging:/ˈkædʒɪŋ/
verb
乞讨，索取
the act of asking for something, typically food or money, from someone.
He was cadging cigarettes from passersby. 他在向路人乞讨香烟。
synonyms: begging, panhandling, scrounging, sponging
antonyms: giving, donating, providing
v.

cadge:/kædʒ/
verb
乞讨，索取
to ask for and get something from someone, typically food or money.
He managed to cadge a lift home. 他设法讨到一个搭便车回家的机会。
synonyms: beg, panhandle, scrounge, sponge
antonyms: give, donate, provide
v.
