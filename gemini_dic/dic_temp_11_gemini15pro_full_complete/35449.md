co-tenancy:/koʊˈtɛnənsi/ | n. | 共同租賃；共同保有；联权共有 | The situation where two or more tenants lease or own a property together. |  Example 1: The co-tenancy agreement clearly outlined each party's responsibilities. 共同租赁协议明确规定了各方的责任。

Example 2:  Co-tenancy can be complicated if the tenants disagree on how to use the space. 如果租户对空间的使用方式意见不一致，共同租賃可能会变得复杂。 | Synonyms: joint tenancy, joint ownership, shared tenancy | Antonyms: sole ownership, sole tenancy | noun
