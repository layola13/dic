overintellectual:/ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
adjective
过度理智的；过分强调智力的
Thinking too much about abstract or complex ideas, often at the expense of practical considerations.  Characterized by excessive intellectual activity or emphasis on intellectual matters, sometimes to the point of impracticality or detachment from reality.
He was so overintellectual that he couldn't hold a normal conversation. 他太过于理智，以至于无法进行正常的对话。
近义词: cerebral, highbrow, intellectualistic, eggheaded
反义词: simple, unintellectual, pragmatic
