ordinaryship:/ˈɔːrdɪnɛrɪʃɪp/
n.
普通职位；牧师职位
the office or position of an ordinary
He was appointed to the ordinaryship of St. Paul's Cathedral. 他被任命为圣保罗大教堂的牧师。
synonyms: pastorate, incumbency, chaplaincy
antonyms: extraordinary position, special appointment
词性: noun
