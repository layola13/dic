disinfecting:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/
|v.|消毒，杀菌|The process of cleaning something to destroy bacteria and other harmful organisms.|例句：They are disinfecting the hospital rooms. 他们正在给医院房间消毒。|近义词：sterilizing, sanitizing|反义词:contaminating, polluting|Verb


disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt/
|n.|消毒剂|A substance used to disinfect.|例句：Bleach is a common disinfectant. 漂白剂是一种常见的消毒剂。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide|反义词:contaminant|Noun
