mumblement:/ˈmʌmblmənt/ | n. | 喃喃自语；含糊不清的话 |The mumblement of the crowd was barely audible. 人群的喃喃低语几乎听不见。|  A mumblement of prayers followed the sermon. 布道结束后，人们低声祈祷。 | 近义词: muttering, murmuring, whisper, undertone | 反义词: shout, yell, proclamation | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "mumble" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns of action or result). "Mumble" comes from Middle English *mommelen*, probably of imitative origin.
