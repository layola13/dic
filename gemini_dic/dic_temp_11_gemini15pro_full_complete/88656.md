hoarfrost:/ˈhɔːrfrɔːst/| n. |白霜|A white, feathery deposit of ice crystals formed on the ground or other surfaces when the temperature of the air falls below freezing.|The grass was covered in hoarfrost. 草地上覆盖着白霜。|近义词：frost, rime|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑dew露水，表示温度在冰点以上)|名词
