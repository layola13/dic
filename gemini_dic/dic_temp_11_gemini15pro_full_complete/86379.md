hederiferous:/ˌhedərˈɪfərəs/
adj.
有篱笆的，多篱笆的; 产常春藤的
Bearing or producing ivy.  Covered with ivy.
The hederiferous walls of the ancient castle evoked a sense of mystery.  古老城堡爬满常春藤的墙壁唤起了一种神秘感。
近义词:ivy-clad, ivy-covered
反义词:bare, uncovered
词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *hederifer, from hedera ("ivy") + -fer ("bearing").
