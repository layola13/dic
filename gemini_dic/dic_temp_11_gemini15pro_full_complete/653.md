abroad:/əˈbrɔːd/
adv.
在国外，到国外
in or to a foreign country or countries
例句：She worked abroad for a year. 她在国外工作了一年。
例句：He plans to go abroad next summer. 他计划明年夏天出国。
近义词：overseas, internationally
反义词：domestically, at home
词性：副词

abroads:  This word is not standard English.  The correct form is "abroad".  "Abroads" is sometimes seen, but it's considered archaic or dialectal and should be avoided in modern standard English.  Therefore, there are no further entries for this non-standard word.
