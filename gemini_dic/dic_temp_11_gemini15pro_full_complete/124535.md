mountainscape:/ˈmaʊntɪnskeɪp/ /ˈmaʊntənˌskeɪp/| n. |山景，山地景观|A view or depiction of mountains, especially a panoramic one.|例句：The mountainscape was breathtaking, with snow-capped peaks and verdant valleys. 山景令人叹为观止，白雪皑皑的山峰和翠绿的山谷。

例句：The artist captured the beauty of the mountainscape in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了山景之美。|近义词：mountain view, mountain scenery, panorama, vista|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 lowlands, plains, flatlands)|词性:noun
