chili: /ˈtʃɪli/ (US), /ˈtʃɪli/ (UK)| n.|辣椒，红辣椒，辣椒粉|A small, hot-tasting pod of a variety of capsicum pepper. |例句：I like chili in my tacos. 我喜欢玉米卷里有辣椒。

例句：He added a pinch of chili to the soup. 他在汤里加了一撮辣椒粉。

近义词：chilli, pepper, cayenne|反义词:bland, mild|词性:noun


chili: /ˈtʃɪli/ (US), /ˈtʃɪli/ (UK)| adj.|辣的，加了辣椒的|Containing or flavored with chili peppers.|例句：This chili sauce is very hot. 这辣椒酱很辣。

例句：I ordered the chili cheese fries. 我点了辣味芝士薯条。

近义词：spicy, hot, peppery|反义词:bland, mild|词性:adjective


词源: From Nahuatl *chīlli.
