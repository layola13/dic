ironworking:/ˈaɪərnˌwɜːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 铁工, 铁艺, 铁制品 | The art or craft of creating objects from wrought iron.  It encompasses a wide range of skills and techniques, including forging, welding, and shaping iron. | Blacksmithing is a traditional form of ironworking.  铁匠是一种传统的铁工形式。 | The Eiffel Tower is a testament to the skill of 19th-century ironworking.  埃菲尔铁塔证明了19世纪铁工的技艺。 | synonyms: blacksmithing, metalworking, forging | antonyms:  N/A | noun
