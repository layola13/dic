descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/| n.|后裔，后代|A person who is related to someone who lived a long time ago; someone who is a later member of the same family.|例句：She is a descendant of Queen Victoria. 她是维多利亚女王的后裔。|例句：Many Americans are descendants of immigrants. 许多美国人是移民的后代。|近义词：offspring, heir, progeny|反义词:ancestor, forebear|词性:名词

descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/| adj.|下降的，遗传的 (较少用)|Moving or directed downwards. Inherited from an ancestor.|例句：The family possesses a descendant estate. 这个家族拥有一处祖传的房产。(这里指房产是祖先传下来的)|例句：Descendant traits can be observed in the offspring.  后代身上可以观察到遗传的性状。|近义词：inherited, derived|反义词:ancestral|词性:形容词
