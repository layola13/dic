nondiffuse:/ˌnɒndɪˈfjuːs/
adjective
非弥漫性的
Not spread out or diffused; concentrated or localized.
The pain was nondiffuse, located just below his ribcage.  疼痛不是弥漫性的，而是集中在他的肋骨下方。
The light source was nondiffuse, creating a sharp, focused beam. 光源是非弥漫性的，产生了一束清晰、聚焦的光束。
近义词: localized, concentrated, focal, specific
反义词: diffuse, spread out, dispersed, scattered
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + diffuse.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "diffuse" comes from the Latin "diffusus," past participle of "diffundere" meaning "to pour out" or "to spread."
