fittingnesses:/ˈfɪtɪŋnɪsɪz/
|n.| 恰当性；适当性；合适性|The plural form of fittingness, referring to multiple instances of suitability or appropriateness.|The fittingnesses of his remarks were questioned by many. 他的言论的恰当性受到了很多人的质疑。|Near Synonyms: suitabilities, aptnesses, proprieties|Antonyms: unsuitabilities, inappropriatenesses, improprieties|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fitting + -ness + -es.  "Fitting" comes from the Old English "fitt" (a division or section of a song or poem).  The meaning evolved to encompass suitability and appropriateness.  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing state or quality. "-es" is a plural marker.
