backpacker:/ˈbækˌpækər/| n.|背包客，背包旅行者|A person who travels or hikes carrying their belongings in a backpack.|例句：The backpacker hostel was full of young travelers from all over the world.  这家背包客旅馆住满了来自世界各地的年轻旅客。

例句：She spent the summer working as a waitress to save money for her backpacking trip across Southeast Asia. 她整个夏天都在当服务员，为了攒钱去东南亚背包旅行。|近义词：hiker, trekker, traveler|反义词:resident, local|词性:noun
