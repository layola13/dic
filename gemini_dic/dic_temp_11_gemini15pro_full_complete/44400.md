cross-handed:/ˌkrɒsˈhændɪd/ /ˌkrɔːsˈhændɪd/| adj.|交叉手的，反手的|With hands crossed, typically the left hand performing the action normally associated with the right and vice versa. | He plays the guitar cross-handed.  他反手弹吉他。| 近义词: awkward, clumsy | 反义词: dexterous, skillful |词性: 形容词

cross-handed:/ˌkrɒsˈhændɪd/ /ˌkrɔːsˈhændɪd/| adv.|交叉手的，反手的|In a cross-handed manner. | She batted cross-handed. 她反手击球。| 近义词: awkwardly, clumsily | 反义词: dexterously, skillfully |词性: 副词
