nitrogenous:/naɪˈtrɒdʒənəs/
adjective
含氮的
Containing nitrogen or a nitrogen compound.
例句：Plants need nitrogenous fertilizers for healthy growth.  植物需要含氮肥料才能健康生长。

例句：Amino acids are nitrogenous compounds. 氨基酸是含氮化合物。

近义词：nitrogen-bearing, nitrogen-containing
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly "non-nitrogenous")
词性：adjective
