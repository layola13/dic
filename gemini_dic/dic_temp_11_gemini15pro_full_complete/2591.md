admonitory:/ədˈmɒnɪtəri/ (US) /ədˈmɒnɪtri/ (UK)| adj.|警告的；劝诫的；告诫的|Serving to warn; expressing reproof or admonition especially in a gentle, earnest, or solicitous manner.|The teacher gave the students an admonitory glance before they started the test. 老师在学生开始考试前给了他们一个警告的眼神。|The librarian cast an admonitory look at the noisy group. 图书管理员向吵闹的群体投去警告性的一瞥。|Synonyms: warning, cautionary, reproving, reprovingly, admonishing, monitory|Antonyms: encouraging, praising, commending|adjective
