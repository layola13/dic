apyrexy:/ˌeɪpaɪˈrɛksi/ | n. | 无热；退热 | Absence of fever; the intermission or subsidence of fever. | The apyrexy lasted for several days, giving the patient a much-needed respite.  退热持续了好几天，让病人得到了急需的休息。 |  The doctor noted the patient's apyrexy in the chart. 医生在病历表上记录了病人的退热情况。 | Synonyms: afebrile, feverless | Antonyms: febrile, feverish | noun
