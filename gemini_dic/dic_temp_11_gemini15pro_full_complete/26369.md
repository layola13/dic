by-hook-or-by-crook:/baɪ hʊk ɔːr baɪ krʊk/
idiom
不择手段;千方百计
By any means necessary, whether legitimate or illegitimate.
She was determined to win the competition, by hook or by crook. 她决心要不择手段赢得比赛。
近义词: by any means necessary, at any cost, by fair means or foul
反义词: honestly, ethically
