barers:/ˈbɛərərz/| n.| 持票人，不记名证券持有人，抬棺者 |People who present a cheque, document of title, etc. especially in order to obtain payment of the sum stated on it. /  Those who carry a coffin at a funeral. |The barers of the check will receive the money.  持票人将收到这笔钱。

The barers carefully carried the coffin. 抬棺者小心翼翼地抬着棺材。 |近义词：holders, carriers (for physical objects), bearers (for abstract things like news), pallbearers (specifically for coffin bearers)|反义词: issuers (for financial instruments) |词性: 名词
