forebrains:/ˈfɔːrbreɪnz/ /ˈfɔːrbreɪnz/
| n. |前脑|The anterior part of the brain, including the cerebrum, thalamus, and hypothalamus.  它是脑的前部，包括大脑、丘脑和下丘脑。|Damage to the forebrains can result in severe cognitive impairment.  前脑损伤会导致严重的认知障碍。|近义词：cerebrum, telencephalon|反义词:None|名词
