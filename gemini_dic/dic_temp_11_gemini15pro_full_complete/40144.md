contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ /kənˈtɛmpəˌrɛri/| adj. n.|当代的，同时代的；同时代的人|Existing or occurring at the same time.|例句：Contemporary art is often challenging. 当代艺术通常具有挑战性。|近义词：modern, current, present-day|反义词：ancient, old, past|词性:形容词

contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ /kənˈtɛmpəˌrɛri/| n.|同龄人，同时代的人|A person or thing living or existing at the same time as another.|例句：He was a contemporary of Shakespeare. 他是莎士比亚的同龄人。|近义词：peer|反义词：predecessor, ancestor|词性:名词
