daredevilism:/ˈdɛəˌdɛvəlɪzəm/
n.
蛮勇，鲁莽，冒险主义
Reckless behavior marked by a willingness to take risks, often involving physical danger.
He was known for his daredevilism, constantly pushing the boundaries of extreme sports. 他以其冒险精神而闻名，不断挑战极限运动的极限。
The stuntman's daredevilism was breathtaking.  这位特技演员的胆大妄为令人叹为观止。
近义词: recklessness, audacity, temerity, foolhardiness
反义词: caution, prudence, timidity
词性: 名词

词源:
From daredevil + -ism.  Daredevil (n.) dates back to the 18th century, meaning "a recklessly daring person," from dare (v.) + devil (n.). The suffix "-ism" forms nouns meaning "a doctrine, belief, or practice."
