enthusing: /ɪnˈθjuːzɪŋ/ | v. | 使充满热情；使热衷 |  The coach was enthusing about the team's prospects for the upcoming season. 教练对球队即将到来的赛季前景充满热情。 |  The new project is really enthusing me.  这个新项目真的让我很兴奋。 | 近义词：exciting, inspiring, motivating | 反义词：discouraging, disheartening | 词性：动词 (present participle of enthuse)

enthuse: /ɪnˈθjuːz/ | v. | 使充满热情；使热衷 | He was enthused by the idea of starting his own business.  创业的想法让他充满热情。 | She enthused about the benefits of a vegetarian diet. 她热情地讲述素食的好处。 | 近义词：excite, inspire, motivate | 反义词：discourage,  dampen | 词性：动词

词源: From en- + Greek *thoústhai ‘be inspired’.
