efficaciousnesses:/ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəsnɪsɪz/
|n.| 功效，有效性|The plural form of efficaciousness, referring to multiple instances of the quality of being effective or producing the desired result.|The various efficaciousnesses of the different treatments were compared in the study.  这项研究比较了不同治疗方法的各种有效性。| 近义词: effectiveness, potency, efficacy |反义词: ineffectiveness, inefficacy, impotence|词性: 名词
