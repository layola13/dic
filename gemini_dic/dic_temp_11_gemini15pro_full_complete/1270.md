accordment:/əˈkɔːrdmənt/
n.
给予; 授予; 同意; 一致
The act of according or granting something.  An agreement or harmony.
例句：The accordment of funds for the project was delayed.  该项目的资金拨付被推迟了。

例句：The accordment between the two parties was short-lived. 双方之间的一致是短暂的。
近义词：agreement, concord, granting, bestowal
反义词：disagreement, discord, refusal, denial
词性: noun
