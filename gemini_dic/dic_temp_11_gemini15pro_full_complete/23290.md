boxiness:/ˈbɑːksɪnɪs/
n.
箱状，四四方方
The quality of being boxy; resembling a box in shape.
The car's boxiness made it less aerodynamic. 这辆车的方正形状使其空气动力学性能较差。
blockiness, chunkiness, squareness
sleekness, streamlinedness
noun
