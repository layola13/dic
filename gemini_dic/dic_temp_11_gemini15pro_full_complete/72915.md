fond:/fɑːnd/ (US) /fɒnd/ (UK)| adj.|喜爱的；爱好的；宠爱的；溺爱的|Having a liking for or affection for someone or something.|例句：She is fond of chocolate. 她喜欢巧克力。

例句：He's fond of telling stories about his childhood. 他喜欢讲他童年的故事。

近义词：affectionate, loving, attached, keen, partial|反义词：disinclined, averse, indifferent|词性:形容词
