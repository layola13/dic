acknowledgers:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒərz/
n.
承认者，致谢者
People who acknowledge something or someone.  Those who express gratitude or recognition.
The acknowledgers in the preface thanked everyone who helped with the project.  序言中的致谢者感谢了所有帮助该项目的人。
synonyms: recognizers, appreciators, thankers
antonyms: deniers, disputers
noun
