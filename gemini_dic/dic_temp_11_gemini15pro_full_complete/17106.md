battable:/ˈbætəbl/| adj.|可击球的，可打击的|Able to be batted; suitable for batting.  Relating to the ability of a batter to hit a baseball.|The pitch was low and outside, barely battable.  那个球又低又偏外，几乎打不到。|近义词：hittable|反义词：unhittable|词性:形容词
