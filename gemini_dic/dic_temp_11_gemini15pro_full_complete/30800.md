cetrarin: /sɛˈtrærɪn/| n.|石蕊酸|A white crystalline bitter-tasting compound C21H20O8 present in various lichens, especially Iceland moss.|Cetrarin is a known antibiotic and antiviral.|近义词: usnic acid (similar properties, also found in lichens)|反义词: None (no direct chemical opposite)|词性: Noun
Etymology: From "cetraria" (the genus of Iceland moss) + "-in" (a suffix used to form names of chemical substances).
