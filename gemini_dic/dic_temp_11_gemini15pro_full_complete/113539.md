maca:/ˈmɑːkə/ | n. |玛咖，秘鲁人参|A Peruvian plant (Lepidium meyenii) cultivated for its fleshy hypocotyl, which is used as a root vegetable, a medicinal herb, and a supposed aphrodisiac. |  Maca is known for its purported health benefits. 玛咖以其所谓的健康益处而闻名。| synonym:Peruvian ginseng | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "placebo" in the context of its purported effects) | noun

maca:/ˈmɑːkə/ | n. |玛咖粉|Powdered form of the maca root.| Maca powder can be added to smoothies or baked goods.  玛咖粉可以添加到冰沙或烘焙食品中。| synonym:maca root powder | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
