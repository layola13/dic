flyest:/ˈflaɪɪst/ | adj. | 最酷的，最棒的，最时尚的 | Superlative form of fly (slang): extremely stylish, fashionable, or impressive.  Derived from African American Vernacular English (AAVE). | Example: She has the flyest sneakers in school.  她拥有学校里最酷的运动鞋。| Example: That car is the flyest thing I've ever seen. 那辆车是我见过的最棒的东西。| 近义词: coolest, best, dopest, freshest, illest | 反义词: worst, lamest, uncool | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From fly (adjective, slang) + -est (superlative suffix). The slang term "fly" meaning "stylish" or "attractive" originated in African American Vernacular English (AAVE) in the mid-20th century.  It initially referred to someone who was "high-class" or "living well," and later evolved to encompass fashionable appearance and impressive skills.
