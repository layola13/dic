lonesome:/ˈloʊnsəm/ (US) /ˈləʊnsəm/ (UK)| adj.|寂寞的，孤独的| Experiencing or causing a feeling of sadness because one is alone; solitary.|例句：She felt lonesome after her children moved out. 她孩子们搬出去后感到很寂寞。|例句：The lonesome howl of a wolf echoed through the valley.  狼孤独的嚎叫声在山谷中回荡。|近义词：lonely, solitary, isolated, alone|反义词：accompanied, sociable, gregarious|词性:形容词
