electives:/ɪˈlek.tɪvz/ | n. | 选修课 |  Courses that students can choose to take, rather than being required to take them.  Derived from "elect" meaning to choose. |  Students have a wide range of electives to choose from. 学生们有各种各样的选修课可供选择。| synonyms: optional courses, choices | antonyms: required courses, compulsory courses | noun

elective:/ɪˈlek.tɪv/ | adj. | 选修的 |  Allowing a choice; optional.  Derived from "elect" meaning to choose. |  Students can take elective courses in their final year. 学生可以在最后一年选修课程。| synonyms: optional, voluntary | antonyms: required, compulsory, mandatory | adjective
