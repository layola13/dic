miscovet: /ˌmɪsˈkʌvɪt/
verb
妄求；贪图；觊觎
To desire wrongly or inappropriately, especially something that belongs to another.
He miscoveted his neighbor's new car. 他觊觎邻居的新车。
She miscoveted her friend's position at work. 她贪图朋友在工作中的职位。
Near Synonyms: covet, crave, desire, yearn for
Antonyms: renounce, relinquish, forgo
词性: 动词
