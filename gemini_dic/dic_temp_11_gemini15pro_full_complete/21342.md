bloomingly:/ˈbluːmɪŋli/| adv.|开花地，兴旺地，繁盛地|In a flourishing or thriving manner; like a flower in bloom.  It describes something happening in a vibrant and healthy way, often with a sense of beauty and growth.|The roses were bloomingly beautiful in the garden.  花园里的玫瑰开得非常漂亮。|The business was bloomingly successful in its first year. 这家公司在第一年就非常成功。|近义词：flourishingly, thrivingly, exuberantly|反义词：witheringly, declining, failing|词性:副词
