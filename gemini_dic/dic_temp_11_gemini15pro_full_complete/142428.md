hosanna:/hoʊˈzænə/ |interjection, noun| 和散那（欢呼声，赞美诗） | A shout of praise or adoration.  Derived from Hebrew, originally a cry for help, but in usage became an exclamation of praise and joy, especially in religious contexts. | Example: The crowd cried "Hosanna!" as the king entered the city.  人群高呼“和散那！”，迎接国王进城。 | Example:  The choir sang hosannas during the Palm Sunday procession. 唱诗班在棕枝主日游行时唱着和散那。 | Synonym:  praise, acclamation, laudation | Antonym:  curse, condemnation, denunciation | part of speech: interjection, noun
