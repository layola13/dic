isodiametric:/ˌaɪsəʊdaɪəˈmetrɪk/
adjective
等径的; 正相反的
having equal diameters; diametrically opposed
The two viewpoints are isodiametric. 这两个观点截然相反。
近义词: diametrically opposed, contrary, antithetical
反义词: similar, identical, alike
词性: adjective

词源:  From iso- (equal) + diametric (relating to a diameter).  Ultimately from Greek *isos* (equal) and *diametros* (diameter).
