congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 意气相投的；合意的；适宜的；友好的 |  (of a person) pleasant because of having similar tastes and interests; (of a thing) pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination |  We spent a relaxed evening with congenial friends. 我们和志趣相投的朋友们一起度过了一个轻松的夜晚。| synonyms: agreeable, pleasant, amiable, compatible, kindred, sympathetic | antonyms: uncongenial, disagreeable, incompatible, hostile | adjective

congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | (指环境)适宜的，宜人的 | (of a thing) pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination | The town is a congenial place for raising a family. 这座城镇是养育家庭的宜居之地。| synonyms: suitable, agreeable, pleasant, favorable, appropriate | antonyms: unsuitable, disagreeable, unpleasant, unfavorable, inappropriate | adjective
