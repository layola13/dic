ectorganism:/ˈɛktɔːɔːrɡənɪzəm/
n.
外生物；体外生物
An ectoorganism is an organism that lives on the outside surface of another organism.
Fleas and lice are examples of ectoorganisms. 跳蚤和虱子是外生物的例子。
synonyms: epizoan, external parasite
antonyms: endoparasite, endorganism
noun
