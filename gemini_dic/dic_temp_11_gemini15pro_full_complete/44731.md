cruisingly:/ˈkruːzɪŋli/
adverb
悠闲地，轻松地
in a relaxed and unhurried way, as if cruising.
He cruised through the exam cruisingly. 他轻松地完成了考试。
Synonyms: effortlessly, easily, smoothly, calmly
Antonyms: laboriously, strenuously, with difficulty
词性: 副词
