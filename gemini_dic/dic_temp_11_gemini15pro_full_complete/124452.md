motorway:/ˈməʊtəweɪ/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərˌweɪ/ (US)| n. |高速公路|A road, especially a major road, designed for fast traffic, typically with multiple lanes and limited access. |例句：We drove on the motorway for hours. 我们在高速公路上开了几个小时。

例句：The motorway was congested due to the accident. 由于事故，高速公路拥堵。|近义词：freeway, expressway, highway, turnpike|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "local road" or "side street")|词性:noun
