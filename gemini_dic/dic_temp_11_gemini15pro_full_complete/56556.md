doming:/ˈdoʊmɪŋ/| n.|穹顶化，圆顶化; 制作圆顶|The process of forming a dome or rounded shape, often on a watch crystal or electronic device screen.  It can also refer to the dome itself.|Example 1: The doming on the watch face gives it a vintage look.  手表表面上的圆顶赋予它复古的外观。

Example 2: The company specializes in doming stickers and labels.  这家公司专门制作圆顶贴纸和标签。|近义词:  arching, rounding, curving|反义词: flattening|词性: noun
