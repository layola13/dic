nonnotification:/ˌnɑːnˌnoʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
未通知，不通知
lack of notification; failure to notify.
The nonnotification of the change in policy caused confusion.  这项政策变更的未通知造成了混乱。
The company's nonnotification of the data breach led to a lawsuit.  该公司未通知数据泄露事件导致了一场诉讼。
近义词:lack of notification, failure to inform, omission of notice
反义词:notification, announcement, communication, disclosure
