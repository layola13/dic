grou:/ɡruː/ | n. | 组，群，团体 | A number of things or people considered as a unit. |  Example:  A small grou of islands.  一小群岛屿。| Synonyms: group, cluster, collection | Antonyms: individual, single | noun

grou:/ɡruː/ | v. | 分组，聚集 | To form or arrange into a group. | Example: The children were grouped by age. 孩子们按年龄分组。 | Synonyms: gather, assemble, cluster | Antonyms: separate, disperse, scatter | verb
