adsorbers:/ədˈsɔːrbərz/
noun
吸附器；吸附剂
Substances that attract and hold other substances on their surface.
Activated carbon is a common adsorber used in water filters.  活性炭是水过滤器中常用的吸附剂。
The adsorbers in the air purifier remove pollutants. 空气净化器中的吸附器可以去除污染物。
Synonyms: absorbent, adsorbent
Antonyms: desorber
