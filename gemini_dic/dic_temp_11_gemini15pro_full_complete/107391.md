lamina:/ˈlæmɪnə/ /ˈlæmənə/
n.
薄片，薄层；叶片
A thin layer, plate, or scale of something, typically one of many layered on top of one another.
The lamina of a leaf is the blade. 叶子的叶片就是薄片。
The artist glued thin lamina of gold onto the sculpture. 艺术家把金箔粘在雕塑上。
layer, sheet, film, scale
---
lamina:/ˈlæmɪnə/ /ˈlæmənə/
n.
（脊椎动物的）椎弓板
The part of a vertebra that forms the roof of the spinal canal.
A fracture of the lamina can cause spinal cord compression.  椎弓板骨折会导致脊髓受压。
The surgeon carefully removed the bone fragment pressing on the lamina. 外科医生小心地移除了压迫椎弓板的骨碎片。
vertebral arch, neural arch
