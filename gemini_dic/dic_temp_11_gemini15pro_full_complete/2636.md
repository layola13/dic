adoption: /əˈdɑːpʃən/ (US) /əˈdɒpʃən/ (UK)| n.|收养，采用，采纳|The process of legally taking a child into one's family as one's own. Also, the act of accepting or starting to use something new.|例句：The adoption of the new technology improved efficiency. 采用新技术提高了效率。|近义词：acceptance, embrace, approval|反义词：rejection, refusal|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *adoptionem (nominative adoptio) "a choosing, adoption," noun of action from past-participle stem of adoptare "to choose for oneself, adopt," from ad- "to" + optare "to choose, wish, desire"
