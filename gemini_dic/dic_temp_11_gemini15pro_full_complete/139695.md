oceanias:/ˌoʊʃiˈeɪniəz/ | n. | 大洋洲（尤指太平洋岛屿）|  A geographical region comprising Melanesia, Micronesia, Polynesia, and sometimes Australasia, and conventionally, but incorrectly, Australia. It is used more commonly outside Australia to denote the islands of the Pacific Ocean. | Many indigenous cultures thrive in the oceanias. 许多土著文化在大洋洲繁荣发展。 | The oceanias offer a diverse range of marine life. 大洋洲拥有种类繁多的海洋生物。 | 近义词: Pacific Islands, Oceania | 反义词: None | noun
