kingling:/ˈkɪŋlɪŋ/
n.
小国王；幼主；王子
A young king, prince, or ruler, often used in a playful or affectionate way.
The kingling, barely ten years old, sat upon the throne.  这个小国王，仅仅十岁，就坐在了王位上。
Synonyms: prince, young king, heir apparent, royal child
Antonyms: commoner, subject
