incongruity:/ˌɪnkənˈɡruːɪti/ | n. |不协调，不一致，不相称|lack of harmony or agreement; something that is not suitable or proper in the circumstances|例句：The incongruity of his formal clothes with the casual atmosphere of the party was apparent. 他的正式服装与聚会的随意气氛格格不入，这很明显。

例句：There's an incongruity between the simple exterior of the building and its lavish interior. 这栋建筑朴素的外观和奢华的内部很不协调。|近义词：disharmony, incompatibility, inconsistency, disparity, clash, conflict|反义词：harmony, congruity, consistency, agreement, suitability|词性:名词
