glare:/ɡlɛər/| n. vi. | 怒视，瞪眼；刺眼的光 |A harsh, uncomfortably bright light.  A fierce or angry stare.| The sun glared down on the desert. 太阳炙烤着沙漠。|近义词：stare, scowl, blaze, dazzle |反义词: glimpse, glance |词性:名词,动词

glare:/ɡlɛər/| v. | 怒视，瞪眼；发出刺眼的光 |To stare angrily or fiercely. To shine with a harsh, uncomfortably bright light.| He glared at me across the table. 他隔着桌子怒视着我。|近义词：stare, scowl, blaze, dazzle |反义词: glimpse, glance |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glaren*, from Old Norse *glora* (“to stare”), from Proto-Germanic *glōrōną* (“to glow, stare”). Related to glow.
