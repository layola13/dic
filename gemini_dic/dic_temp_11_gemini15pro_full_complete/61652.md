emission:/ɪˈmɪʃən/ | n. | 排放；散发；发出；射出 | the production and discharge of something, especially gas or radiation | The emission of greenhouse gases is a major contributor to climate change. 温室气体的排放是气候变化的主要原因。|  discharge, release, ejection | absorption, suppression | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *emissioun, from Late Latin ēmissiō, ēmissiōnem, from Latin ēmittō (“I send forth, let go, discharge”), from ē (“out, out of, from”) + mittō (“I send, let go”).
