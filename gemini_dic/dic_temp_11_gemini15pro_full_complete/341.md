abhorrent-to:/əˈbɔːrənt tuː/
adjective
令人憎恶的；厌恶的
Inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant.
Racism is abhorrent to a civilized society. 种族主义对于文明社会来说是令人憎恶的。
近义词:repugnant, detestable, loathsome, odious, hateful, abominable, execrable
反义词:admirable, delightful, pleasing
词性:adjective
