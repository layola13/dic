coleopteran:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərən/| n. |鞘翅目昆虫，甲虫|An insect of the order Coleoptera, characterized by hard, shell-like front wings that cover and protect the membranous hind wings used for flight.  Coleopterans include beetles, weevils, and fireflies.|例句：The ladybug is a common coleopteran found in gardens. 瓢虫是在花园中常见的鞘翅目昆虫。

例句：Coleopterans are the most diverse order of insects, with over 400,000 known species. 鞘翅目是昆虫种类最多的目，已知有超过40万种。|近义词：beetle|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑其他昆虫种类，例如 lepidopteran 鳞翅目昆虫)|词性: 名词
