influential:/ˌɪnfluˈenʃl/| adj.|有影响的；有权势的|Having the power to cause changes; producing effects|Her work has been highly influential in the field of education. 她的工作在教育领域具有很高的影响力。|Nelson Mandela was an influential figure in the fight against apartheid. 纳尔逊·曼德拉是反种族隔离斗争中一位有影响力的人物。|Synonyms: powerful, potent, dominant, impactful|Antonyms: powerless, insignificant, unimportant|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *influencial*, from Late Latin *influentiālis* (“flowing in, exerting influence”), from Latin *influō* (“flow into”), from *in-* (“in, into”) + *fluō* (“flow”).
