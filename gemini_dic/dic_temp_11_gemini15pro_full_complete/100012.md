interpenetrating:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
相互渗透的，互相穿透的
mutually penetrating each other; permeating one another.
The two cultures are so interpenetrating that it's hard to distinguish them. 这两种文化相互渗透，很难区分。
The artist created a sculpture of interpenetrating geometric forms. 艺术家创作了一个相互穿透的几何形状的雕塑。
intertwined, interlaced, intermingled, pervasive
discrete, separate, distinct
adjective
