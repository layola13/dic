implies:/ɪmˈplaɪz/| v.|暗示，意味着|To suggest or convey an idea or feeling indirectly, without stating it explicitly. |Silence often implies consent.  沉默通常意味着同意。|His tone implied disapproval. 他的语气暗示着不赞成。|近义词：suggest, indicate, infer, connote|反义词：state, declare, express|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin implicare, from Latin in- ‘in’ + plicare ‘to fold’.
