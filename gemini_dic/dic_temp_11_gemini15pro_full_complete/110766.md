linguals:/ˈlɪŋɡwəlz/
n.
舌音;舌面音;舌音字母
sounds articulated with the tongue.  Specifically, speech sounds produced by placing the tip or blade of the tongue against or near the teeth or the alveolar ridge.
The "l" sound in "linguals" is itself a lingual sound. 舌音字母“l”本身就是一个舌音。
consonants, speech sounds, phonemes
gutturals (sounds produced in the throat)
