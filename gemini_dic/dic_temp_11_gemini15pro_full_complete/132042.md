noncorruptibilities:/ˌnɒnkərʌptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
不可腐败性；廉洁性
The quality or state of being incorruptible; inability to be corrupted, especially by bribery.
The judge was known for his noncorruptibility and impartiality.  这位法官以其廉洁公正而闻名。
Synonyms: incorruptibility, integrity, probity, uprightness, honesty
Antonyms: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality, unscrupulousness
noun
