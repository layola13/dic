morticing:/ˈmɔːrtɪsɪŋ/| n. |榫眼加工，开榫|The process of creating a mortise (a hole or recess) in a piece of wood or other material, usually to receive a tenon (a projecting member) as part of a joint.|He spent hours morticing the timbers for the new roof. 他花了几个小时为新屋顶的木材开榫眼。|近义词:grooving, slotting|反义词: N/A|noun

mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/| v. |开榫，凿榫眼|To cut a mortise (a hole or recess) in a piece of wood or other material, usually to receive a tenon (a projecting member) as part of a joint.|He carefully mortised the door frame to fit the hinges. 他仔细地在门框上凿出榫眼以安装铰链。|近义词:groove, slot|反义词: N/A|verb
