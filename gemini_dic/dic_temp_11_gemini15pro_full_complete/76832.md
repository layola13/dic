gandhian:/ɡɑːnˈdiːən/ | adj. n. | 甘地的；甘地主义的；非暴力抵抗的；甘地主义者 | relating to or characteristic of Mahatma Gandhi or his ideas, especially the principle of nonviolent resistance; a believer in or follower of Gandhi's ideas. |  例句：His gandhian principles guided his life.  他的人生以甘地主义原则为指导。| 近义词：pacific, pacifist, nonviolent | 反义词：violent, aggressive | 词性: adjective, noun
gandhian:/ɡɑːnˈdiːən/ | n. | 甘地主义者 | a person who follows the principles of Mahatma Gandhi, especially those of nonviolent resistance. | 例句：He is a devout gandhian. 他是一位虔诚的甘地主义者。| 近义词：pacifist, peacemaker | 反义词：warmonger, aggressor | 词性: noun
