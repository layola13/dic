guiltiest:/ˈɡɪltiɪst/| adj.|最有罪的；最内疚的|Feeling the most guilt; most culpable.|例句：He felt like the guiltiest man alive. 他觉得自己是世上最有罪的人。|例句：She had the guiltiest look on her face. 她脸上带着非常内疚的表情。|近义词：most culpable, most ashamed, most remorseful|反义词：most innocent, blameless|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *gilti, from Old English *gyltig, from Proto-Germanic *skulþigaz (“guilty, liable, owing”). Cognate with West Frisian skuldich (“guilty”), Dutch schuldig (“guilty, indebted”), German schuldig (“guilty, indebted, owing”), Swedish skyldig (“guilty, indebted”).  The superlative form "-iest" is a common Germanic suffix indicating the highest degree of an adjective.
