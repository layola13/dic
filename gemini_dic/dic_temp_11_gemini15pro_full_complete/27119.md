caliper:/ˈkælɪpər/ (US), /ˈkælɪpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|卡尺，测径器，钳子|A measuring instrument with two adjustable jaws, used to measure the thickness or diameter of an object.|The mechanic used a caliper to measure the brake pads. 机械师用卡尺测量刹车片。|近义词：gauge, micrometer|反义词: None|noun

caliper:/ˈkælɪpər/ (US), /ˈkælɪpə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|用卡尺测量|To measure something with a caliper.|He calipered the diameter of the pipe. 他用卡尺测量了管子的直径。|近义词：gauge, measure|反义词: None|verb
