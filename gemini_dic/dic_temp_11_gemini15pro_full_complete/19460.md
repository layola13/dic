bicyclic:/baɪˈsaɪklɪk/| adj.|双环的；二环式的|Having two rings or cycles, especially of atoms in a molecule.|This molecule is bicyclic, meaning it has two rings in its structure.  这个分子是双环的，意味着它的结构中有两个环。|The compound has a bicyclic structure. 该化合物具有双环结构。|cyclic, polycyclic|monocyclic, acyclic|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + cyclic (cyclic).
