frauds:/frɔːdz/
|n.|欺诈，诈骗；骗子|A dishonest act intended to trick someone out of something valuable.|Many elderly people fall victim to online frauds.|synonyms: scams, deceptions, hoaxes, cheats, swindles|antonyms: honesty, integrity|noun

frauds:/frɔːdz/
|n.|冒牌货，赝品|Something that is not what it is claimed to be.|The painting was later discovered to be a fraud.|synonyms: fake, counterfeit, sham, imitation|antonyms: genuine, authentic|noun
