nonfugitive:/ˌnɑːnˈfjuːdʒətɪv/ | adj. | 非逃亡的，不挥发的 | Not fleeing from justice; not evaporating readily. |  The police are searching for the nonfugitive suspect. 警方正在搜寻这个没有潜逃的嫌疑犯。 | The chemist studied the nonfugitive properties of the compound. 化学家研究了该化合物的非挥发性。 | 近义词:permanent, stable, enduring | 反义词:fugitive, volatile, evaporative | 词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + fugitive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fugitive" comes from the Latin "fugitivus," meaning "fleeing."
