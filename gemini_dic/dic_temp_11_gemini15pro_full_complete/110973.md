liquidate:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪt/| v. |清算， ликвидировать|To settle a debt, claim, or other obligation by converting (assets) into cash; to eliminate, typically by violent means; to kill.| The company was forced to liquidate its assets to pay off its debts.  该公司被迫清算资产以偿还债务。|  The dictator ordered his troops to liquidate the opposition leaders.  独裁者命令他的军队消灭反对派领导人。|synonyms: dissolve, clear up, pay off, eliminate, kill, abolish|antonyms: establish, found, create, build, save|verb

liquidation:/ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃn/| n. |清算， ликвидация|The act or process of liquidating.|The liquidation of the company's assets took several months.  公司资产的清算花了几个月的时间。|The liquidation of the enemy soldiers was swift and brutal. 对敌方士兵的清除迅速而残酷。|synonyms: dissolution, clearance, settlement, elimination, killing, abolishment|antonyms: establishment, foundation, creation, building, salvation|noun
