acclamatory:/əˈklæməˌtɔːri/
adjective
欢呼的，喝彩的
expressing or showing great praise or approval
The audience gave the performers an acclamatory ovation. 观众对表演者报以热烈的掌声。
近义词：laudatory, approving, celebratory, commendatory
反义词：critical, disapproving, condemnatory
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin acclamare "to shout at, applaud," from ad- "to" + clamare "to cry out."
