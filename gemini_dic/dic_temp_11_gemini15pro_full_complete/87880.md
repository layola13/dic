hidrosis:/hɪˈdroʊsɪs/ /haɪˈdrəʊsɪs/
n.
出汗，盗汗
The process of sweating.
Excessive hidrosis can be a medical problem.  过多的出汗可能是一个医学问题。
Hidrosis is a natural bodily function. 出汗是人体的自然机能。
近义词：sweating, perspiration
反义词：anhidrosis
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑδρώς (hudrṓs, “sweat”) +‎ -osis.
