amphipodous:/æmˈfɪpədəs/| adj.|片脚类的，端足类的|Having feet or legs of two kinds, adapted for walking or swimming; relating to or belonging to the Amphipoda.|Amphipodous crustaceans are common in aquatic environments.|近义词:crustacean|反义词:(None specific, possibly terrestrial)|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀμφί (amphí, “on both sides”) + ποδ- (pod-, “foot”) + -ous (English adjectival suffix).
