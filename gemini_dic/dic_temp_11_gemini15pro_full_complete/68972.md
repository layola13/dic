fathomless:/ˈfæðəmləs/| adj.|深不可测的，不可理解的|Something that is fathomless is so deep that it cannot be measured. It can also describe something that is impossible to understand.|The ship sank into the fathomless depths of the ocean.  这艘船沉入了深不可测的海洋深处。

The artist's fathomless eyes seemed to hold a universe of secrets.  这位艺术家深不可测的眼睛里似乎藏着一个充满秘密的宇宙。|近义词：unfathomable, immeasurable, bottomless, profound, abysmal|反义词：shallow, measurable, comprehensible, superficial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English fæthm "embrace, length of the outstretched arms," (related to Old Norse faðmr "embrace, bosom") + -less.  Originally literal; the figurative sense "incomprehensible" dates from the late 16th century.
