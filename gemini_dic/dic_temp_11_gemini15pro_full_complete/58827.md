duper:/ˈduːpər/ | n. | 骗子；欺骗者 | Someone who dupes or deceives others. | He's a real duper, always trying to con people out of their money. 他是个十足的骗子，总是试图骗取别人的钱财。 |  近义词: swindler, con artist, cheat, fraudster | 反义词: dupe (as a noun - the victim of a deception) | 词性: noun

dupe:/duːp/ | n. | 受骗者；易受骗的人 | Someone who is easily tricked or deceived. | He was a dupe in their scheme and lost all his savings. 他是他们阴谋中的受骗者，失去了所有的积蓄。| 近义词: victim, sucker, chump | 反义词: duper, swindler | 词性: noun

dupe:/duːp/ | vt. | 欺骗；愚弄 | To deceive or trick someone. | They duped him into signing the contract. 他们骗他签了合同。| 近义词: deceive, trick, fool, con, swindle | 反义词: enlighten, inform | 词性: verb
