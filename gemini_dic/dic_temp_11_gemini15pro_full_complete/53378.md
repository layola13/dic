ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + agreeable + -ity (forming abstract nouns).  Agreeable comes from Old French agreable, from agreer "to please," from a- "to" + gre "pleasure, liking," from Latin gratus "pleasing, thankful."
