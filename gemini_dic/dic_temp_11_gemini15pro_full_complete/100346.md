interstates:/ˈɪntərˌsteɪts/
noun
州际公路；州际公路系统
A system of limited-access highways connecting major cities across the United States.  A highway that is part of this system.
We drove on the interstates for most of our trip. 我们大部分的旅程都在州际公路上行驶。
The interstate is closed due to an accident.  由于事故，州际公路关闭了。
synonyms: highways, freeways, expressways, turnpikes, thruways
antonyms: local roads, back roads, surface streets
