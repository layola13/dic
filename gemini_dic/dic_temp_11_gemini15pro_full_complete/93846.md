immobile:/ɪˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|不动的，静止的，固定的|Unable to move or be moved.|The patient was completely immobile after the surgery.  手术后病人完全不能动弹。|The statue stood immobile in the square.  雕像一动不动地矗立在广场上。|近义词：motionless, static, fixed, stationary|反义词：mobile, movable, active|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin immobilis, from in- ‘not’ + mobilis (from movere ‘to move’).
