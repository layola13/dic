disveil:/dɪsˈveɪl/
verb
揭露，揭开，使…显露
to uncover or reveal something that was hidden or secret
The report disveiled a shocking level of corruption within the government. 这份报告揭露了政府内部令人震惊的腐败程度。
The artist disveiled his latest masterpiece at the gallery opening.  这位艺术家在画廊开幕式上揭开了他最新杰作的面纱。
synonyms: reveal, uncover, expose, unveil
antonyms: conceal, hide, cover, obscure
