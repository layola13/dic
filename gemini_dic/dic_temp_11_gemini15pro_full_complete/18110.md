behemoth:/bɪˈhiːməθ/ /biːˈhiːmɒθ/| n.|巨兽，庞然大物|Something of immense size or power. |例句：The corporation is a behemoth in the tech industry.  这家公司是科技行业的巨头。|例句：The new aircraft carrier is a true behemoth of the seas. 这艘新的航空母舰是真正的海上巨兽。|近义词：monster, giant, leviathan|反义词:dwarf, pygmy|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *behemot*, from Biblical Hebrew *bəhēmōṯ* (בְּהֵמוֹת), meaning "beasts," plural of *bəhēmā* (בְּהֵמָה), meaning "beast, cattle."  The word is used in the Book of Job to describe a large, powerful creature, often interpreted as a hippopotamus or elephant.
