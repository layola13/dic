nonrailroad:/ˌnɑnˈreɪlroʊd/
adjective
非铁路的；非铁路运输的
Not related to or involved with railroads.
The company specializes in nonrailroad transportation, focusing on trucking and shipping.  这家公司专门从事非铁路运输，专注于卡车运输和航运。
The nonrailroad industries in the town are thriving. 该镇的非铁路行业蓬勃发展。
Synonyms: non-railway
Antonyms: railroad, railway
word type: adjective
