nonoverheads:/ˌnɑːnˈoʊvərˌhɛdz/ | n. | 非经常性开支;非间接费用 | Costs that are not related to general business expenses, such as rent, utilities, or administrative salaries.  These are typically direct costs associated with a specific project or product. | The project budget includes all nonoverheads, such as materials and labor.  项目预算包括所有非经常性开支，例如材料和人工。| The company managed to reduce nonoverheads by streamlining the production process. 公司通过简化生产流程设法减少了非经常性开支。 | direct costs, operating expenses | overheads, indirect costs | noun
