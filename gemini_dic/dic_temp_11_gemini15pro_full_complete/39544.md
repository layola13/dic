consanguinity:/ˌkɑːnsæŋˈɡwɪnɪti/
n.
血缘关系，血亲
Blood relationship; kinship.
The two families were linked by consanguinity. 这两个家族因血缘关系而联系在一起。
consanguineous kinship, blood tie, close relationship
distance, unrelatedness
noun
