chimneys:/ˈtʃɪm.niz/| n. |烟囱|A vertical channel or pipe that conducts smoke and combustion gases up from a fireplace, furnace, or other device.|Smoke billowed from the chimneys of the factory. 工厂的烟囱里冒出浓烟。|近义词：stacks, flues|反义词: None|Noun
