coltsfoot:/ˈkoʊltsˌfʊt/| n.|款冬|A European plant (Tussilago farfara) with yellow flowers appearing before the leaves, which are hoof-shaped. It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine, especially for coughs.|例句：Coltsfoot is known for its cough-suppressing properties. 款冬以其镇咳特性而闻名。

例句：She made a tea from coltsfoot to soothe her sore throat. 她用款冬泡茶来舒缓喉咙痛。|近义词：butterbur, coughwort|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fola-fot (literally 'foal's foot'), referring to the shape of the leaf.
