nonspecification:/ˌnɑːnˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 未指明，未具体说明 | The contract's nonspecification of delivery dates caused problems.  该合同未指明交货日期，导致了问题。 |  The nonspecification of the software requirements led to delays in the project.  软件需求未具体说明导致项目延误. | 近义词: omission, lack of specification, indefiniteness | 反义词: specification, definition, clarity | 词性: 名词

词源: non- + specification. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "specification" refers to a detailed description or statement of requirements.  来自"non-"（意为“不”）和 "specification"（意为“具体说明”）的组合。“Non-”是一个表示“不”的前缀，“specification”指的是对需求的详细描述或陈述。
