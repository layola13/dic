aspergillosis:/æsˌpɜːrdʒɪˈloʊsɪs/ | n. | 曲霉病 | A disease caused by the fungus Aspergillus, typically affecting the respiratory system. |  Aspergillosis is a serious condition that can be life-threatening. 曲霉病是一种严重的疾病，可能危及生命。

Aspergillosis can range from mild allergic reactions to invasive infections. 曲霉病的症状从轻微的过敏反应到侵入性感染不等。 | 近义词:  pulmonary mycosis (肺真菌病 - broader term) | 反义词: None (diseases don't typically have antonyms) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *aspergillosis*, from Aspergillus + -osis.  Aspergillus itself comes from Latin aspergillum "holy water sprinkler," because the fungus resembles the shape of this device.
