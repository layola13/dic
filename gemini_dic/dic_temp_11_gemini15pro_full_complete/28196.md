capricioso:/kəˌprɪtʃˈoʊsoʊ/ /kəˌprɪtʃiˈəʊsəʊ/
adj.
反复无常的；任性的；情绪化的
Given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior.
She's such a capricious child; one minute she's happy, the next she's crying. 她真是个反复无常的孩子；一会儿高兴，一会儿哭。
The capricious weather made it difficult to plan a picnic.  变化无常的天气使得很难计划野餐。
近义词: fickle, impulsive, erratic, whimsical, mercurial
反义词: steady, consistent, predictable, stable
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Italian *capriccioso*, from *capriccio* "sudden start or motion," from *capro* "goat" (from Latin *caper*) + *-iccio* (suffix forming nouns). The notion is of "goatlike" behavior, hence "erratic, unpredictable."
