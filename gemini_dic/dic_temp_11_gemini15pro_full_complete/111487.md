loadstone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/ | n. |天然磁石，磁铁矿；吸引人的东西|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  Something that attracts strongly. | The sailors used a loadstone to help them navigate.  水手们使用天然磁石帮助他们导航。

The new shopping center is a loadstone for teenagers.  新的购物中心对青少年很有吸引力。 | 近义词:magnet, lodestar | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lode-ston*, from Old English *lād*, meaning "way, journey, course" + *stān*, meaning "stone".  Thus, a "leading stone" or a stone that shows the way (because of its magnetic properties used in compasses).
