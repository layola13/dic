cineas:/ˈsɪniəs/| n. |西尼阿斯|A Thessalian orator and politician, a friend and minister of Pyrrhus, king of Epirus. He tried to persuade the Romans to make peace with Pyrrhus after the Battle of Heraclea (280 BC).|Cineas was known for his diplomatic skills and persuasive eloquence. 西尼阿斯以其外交技巧和有说服力的口才而闻名。|近义词:diplomat, negotiator, orator|反义词:warmonger|Noun
