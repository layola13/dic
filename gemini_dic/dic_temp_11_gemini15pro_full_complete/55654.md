dithyrambic:/ˌdɪθəˈræmbɪk/ /ˌdaɪθəˈræmbɪk/
adjective
狂热的；酒神赞歌式的；热情奔放的
Wildly enthusiastic; ecstatic; extravagant in language, style, or manner, like a dithyramb (an ancient Greek hymn sung in honor of Dionysus, the god of wine and revelry).
The poet’s dithyrambic language conveyed the intensity of his love. 这位诗人用热情奔放的语言表达了他强烈的爱。
近义词: ecstatic, rhapsodic, passionate, fervent, effusive, exuberant
反义词: restrained, subdued, calm, unemotional, impassive
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *dithyrambos*, a hymn sung in honor of Dionysus.
