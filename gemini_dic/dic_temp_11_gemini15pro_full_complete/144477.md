outthreatening:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtnɪŋ/
|adj.|更具威胁性的，威胁更大的|posing a greater threat than something/someone else; surpassing in threat|The new virus strain is outthreatening the previous one due to its higher transmissibility.  这种新的病毒株比以前的病毒株更具威胁性，因为它的传播性更强。| The dictator's outthreatening rhetoric stoked fears of war.  独裁者极具威胁的言论激起了人们对战争的恐惧。|近义词: more menacing, more dangerous, more intimidating|反义词: less threatening, benign, harmless|词性: adjective

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "threatening" (present participle of "threaten," from Old English *þreatnian, related to threat)
