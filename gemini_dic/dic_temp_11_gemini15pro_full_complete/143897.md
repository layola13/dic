outpowering:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərɪŋ/
v.
胜过，压倒，超过…的能力
overcoming something or someone by having greater power or strength
The new engine is outpowering the old one by a significant margin.  这台新引擎的动力明显超过了旧引擎。
Synonyms: overpowering, exceeding, surpassing, outmatching, outstripping
Antonyms:  succumbing to, yielding to, being defeated by
词性: 动词
