jetsam:/ˈdʒɛtsəm/| n.|海上漂流物，废弃物|Goods thrown overboard from a ship to lighten it in distress, or washed ashore after such jettison.|例句：The beach was littered with jetsam from the wrecked ship.  海滩上散落着失事船只的漂流物。

例句：After the storm, jetsam and flotsam covered the shore.  暴风雨过后，海岸上覆盖着各种漂流物和残骸。|近义词：flotsam, debris, wreckage|反义词:treasure, salvage|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Anglo-Norman *getaison, from Old French getaison, from jeter "to throw," from Latin iactare, frequentative of iacere "to throw."
