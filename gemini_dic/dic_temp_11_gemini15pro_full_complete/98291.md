insiccation:/ˌɪnsɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
干燥，脱水
the state of being dried; dryness
The insipient desiccation of the lake bed is threatening the wildlife. 湖床的初期干燥正在威胁野生动物。

近义词: dehydration, drying, exsiccation
反义词: hydration, moistening

词性: 名词
