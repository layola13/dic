abysmal:/əˈbɪzməl/| adj.| 极坏的；糟透的；深不可测的| Extremely bad; appallingly low; extremely deep.| The food at the restaurant was abysmal.  这家餐馆的食物糟透了。| The company's profits have fallen to an abysmal level. 这家公司的利润已经跌到了极低的水平。| synonyms: dreadful, terrible, awful, appalling, atrocious, very bad, extremely bad | antonyms: superb, excellent, wonderful, outstanding | adjective

abysmal:/əˈbɪzməl/| adj.| 深不可测的 | Immeasurably deep.|  The ocean's depths are abysmal. 海洋的深度深不可测。|  She stared into the abysmal darkness. 她凝视着深不见底的黑暗。| synonyms: profound, immeasurable, unfathomable, bottomless | antonyms: shallow, superficial | adjective
