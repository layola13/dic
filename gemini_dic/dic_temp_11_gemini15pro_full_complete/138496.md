noviceship: /ˈnɒvɪsʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvɪsʃɪp/ (US)| n.|见习期；学徒期；新手阶段|The state or period of being a novice.  The period of time someone spends learning a skill or trade.|He successfully completed his noviceship and became a journeyman carpenter. 他成功地完成了他的学徒期，成为了一名熟练的木匠.|Synonyms: apprenticeship, traineeship, probation, initiation|Antonyms: mastery, expertise|Noun
