calorifacient:/ˌkæləriˈfeɪʃənt/
adjective
产热的；生热的
Producing heat; causing warmth.
例句：This calorifacient food helps to keep you warm in cold weather.  这种产热食物有助于你在寒冷的天气里保暖。

例句：Ginger is known for its calorifacient properties. 姜以其生热特性而闻名。
近义词：heating, warming, thermogenic
反义词：cooling, refrigerant
词性：adjective

calorifacient:/ˌkæləriˈfeɪʃənt/
noun
产热剂；生热剂
A substance that produces heat or raises body temperature.
例句：The doctor prescribed a calorifacient to help raise the patient's body temperature. 医生开了一种产热剂来帮助提高病人的体温。

例句：Capsaicin, found in chili peppers, acts as a calorifacient.辣椒中发现的辣椒素，起到产热剂的作用。
近义词：stimulant, heating agent
反义词：coolant, refrigerant
词性：noun
