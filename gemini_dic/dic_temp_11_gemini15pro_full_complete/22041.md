bolden:/ˈboʊldən/ (US) /ˈbəʊldən/ (UK)| v. |使加粗；使大胆；使更清晰|To make something bolder, more noticeable, or more prominent.  It can refer to visually making something darker or thicker, or figuratively making something more confident or courageous.|例句：The editor boldened the headings to make them stand out. 编辑将标题加粗以使其突出。

例句：His recent successes have boldened him to take more risks.  他最近的成功使他更有胆量去冒险。|近义词：emphasize, highlight, strengthen, embolden|反义词：weaken, soften, diminish, subdue|词性：动词

词源: From "bold" + "-en" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").  "Bold" itself comes from Old English "bald," meaning "brave, confident."
