glutton:/ˈɡlʌtn/| n.|暴食者，贪吃的人|Someone who eats and drinks excessively or has a great capacity for something.|He's such a glutton when it comes to desserts.  他吃甜点真是个贪吃鬼。|Synonyms: gourmand, overeater, gorger|Antonyms: ascetic, abstainer|noun

gluttonous:/ˈɡlʌtnəs/| adj.|贪吃的，暴食的|Excessively greedy; given to overindulgence.|She has a gluttonous appetite for gossip. 她对八卦有着贪得无厌的胃口。|Synonyms: greedy, voracious, ravenous|Antonyms: temperate, moderate|adjective
