homotypic:/ˌhɒməʊˈtɪpɪk/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈtɪpɪk/| adj.|同型的，同类型的|Of the same type or structure.  Relating to or being a chromosome or chromosome segment identical with another in terms of morphology and gene loci.|Homotypic cell fusion is important in development.|近义词：isomorphic, homologous|反义词:heterotypic|词性:adjective
