mandelic:/mænˈdɛlɪk/ | adj. | 杏仁的；扁桃酸的 |  Relating to or derived from mandelic acid, a type of alpha hydroxy acid (AHA). | Mandelic acid is a popular ingredient in chemical peels for treating acne.  扁桃酸是治疗痤疮的化学换肤术中的一种流行成分。|  Synonyms:  amygdalic (less common) | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

mandelic acid:/mænˈdɛlɪk ˈæsɪd/ | n. | 扁桃酸 |  An aromatic alpha hydroxy acid (AHA) with the chemical formula C₈H₈O₃. It is used in skincare products for its exfoliating and antibacterial properties. | Mandelic acid peels are gentler than glycolic acid peels. 扁桃酸换肤比乙醇酸换肤更温和。| Synonyms: phenylglycolic acid, amygdalic acid (less common) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
