infiltrated: /ˈɪnfɪltreɪtɪd/
verb
渗透，潜入
(of a liquid) permeate (something) by filtration.  (of a group of people) enter or gain access to (an organization, place, etc.) surreptitiously and gradually, especially in order to acquire secret information or cause disruption.
The rebels infiltrated the government.  叛军渗透了政府。
Spies infiltrated the enemy's camp.  间谍潜入了敌营。
Synonyms: permeate, penetrate, pervade, seep into,  insinuate oneself into
Antonyms:  extract, withdraw, leave
