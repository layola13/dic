disaffectednesses:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛktɪdnɪsɪz/
| n. |不满，不平；叛逆|The state of being disaffected; alienation or discontent.  A plural form of disaffectedness, referring to multiple instances or types of disaffection.|The growing disaffectednesses among the workers led to a strike. 工人们日益增长的不满导致了罢工。|Near synonyms: discontents, disaffections, alienations|Near antonyms: loyalties, attachments, contentments
