crystalloid:/ˈkrɪstəlɔɪd/| n. adj.|晶体；晶质的|A substance that, when dissolved, forms a true solution that can pass through a semipermeable membrane.  A substance that resembles a crystal.|The doctor administered crystalloids to the dehydrated patient. 医生给脱水的病人注射了晶体溶液。|Crystalloid solutions are commonly used in intravenous therapy. 晶体溶液常用于静脉治疗。|近义词：crystalline, solution|反义词：colloid|词性：noun, adjective
