noncharitableness:/ˌnɒnˌtʃærɪtəbəlnəs/
|n.|缺乏仁慈；刻薄；不宽容|The noncharitableness of her remarks shocked everyone. 她言辞的刻薄让每个人都感到震惊。|English Explanation:  Lack of charity or kindness; an unwillingness to forgive or tolerate the failings of others; meanness or spitefulness.|例句：His noncharitableness towards the homeless was appalling. 他对无家可归者的刻薄令人震惊。

例句：Her noncharitableness made her unpopular. 她的不宽容让她不受欢迎。

|近义词：unkindness, meanness, harshness, cruelty, intolerance|反义词: charity, kindness, compassion, tolerance, benevolence|词性:名词
