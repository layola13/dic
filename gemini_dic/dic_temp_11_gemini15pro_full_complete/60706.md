electro-acupuncture:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈækjʊpʌŋktʃər/
noun
电针疗法
A form of acupuncture where a small electric current is passed between pairs of acupuncture needles.
Electro-acupuncture can be used to treat chronic pain. 电针疗法可以用来治疗慢性疼痛。
Electro-acupuncture is often used in conjunction with traditional acupuncture.  电针疗法常与传统针灸疗法结合使用。
acupuncture, electrostimulation
