meatlessness:/ˈmiːtləsnəs/
n.
无肉; 素食; 不吃肉的状态
The state or quality of being without meat.
The restaurant caters to the growing trend of meatlessness.  这家餐厅迎合了日益增长的素食主义趋势。
Vegetarianism is a form of meatlessness. 素食主义是一种无肉的状态。
近义词:vegetarianism, veganism
反义词:carnivory
