neoprene: /ˈniːəpriːn/ (US) /ˈniːəpriːn/ (UK)| n.|氯丁橡胶，合成橡胶|A synthetic rubber that is resistant to water, heat, and chemicals.|例句：Wetsuits are often made of neoprene.  潜水衣通常由氯丁橡胶制成。

例句：Neoprene gloves provide good protection against chemicals. 氯丁橡胶手套可提供良好的化学防护。|近义词：polychloroprene, synthetic rubber|反义词:natural rubber|词性:noun

Etymology:  A proprietary name coined from chloro- + the second part of isoprene.
