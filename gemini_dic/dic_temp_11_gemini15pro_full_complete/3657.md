agglomerate: /əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK) /əˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (US)
agglomerate: n.|聚集体，团块|A mass of things clustered together.|The volcanic rock was an agglomerate of various minerals.|近义词: cluster, collection, aggregate, mass|反义词: dispersal, scattering|词性:名词

agglomerate: v.|聚集，结块|To gather into a mass or ball.|The dust particles agglomerated into larger clumps.|近义词: aggregate, coalesce, amass, cluster|反义词: disperse, separate, scatter|词性:动词

agglomerate: adj.|聚集的，成团的|Gathered together into a mass or cluster.|The agglomerate rock formations were impressive.|近义词: clustered, aggregated, amassed|反义词: dispersed, scattered|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin agglomerātus, past participle of agglomerāre (“to roll into a ball, gather into a heap”), from ad (“to”) + glomerāre (“to wind into a ball”), from glomus (“ball”).
