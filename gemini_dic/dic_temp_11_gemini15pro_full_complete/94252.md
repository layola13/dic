imperiwigged:/ˌɪmpɛrɪˈwɪɡd/
adjective
戴假发的；戴着假发的
Wearing a periwig (an old-fashioned word for a wig).  Formally and elaborately dressed, especially in the style of the 17th or 18th century.
He cut an imposing figure, tall and imperiwigged. 他身材魁梧，高大，戴着假发，给人留下深刻的印象。
The imperiwigged judge entered the courtroom.  戴着假发的法官走进了法庭。

Synonyms: wigged, bewigged, periwigged
Antonyms: unwagged, wigless
