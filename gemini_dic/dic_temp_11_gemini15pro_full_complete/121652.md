mispersuasion:/ˌmɪspərˈsweɪʒn/
noun
误导，错误的劝说
The act of persuading someone to believe something that is not true.
例句：His mispersuasion led her down a dangerous path. 他的误导使她走上了一条危险的道路。

例句：The advertisement was a clear case of mispersuasion, promising unrealistic results.  这则广告明显是误导，承诺了不切实际的效果。

近义词：misleading, deception, misdirection
反义词：persuasion, enlightenment, education
词性：n.
