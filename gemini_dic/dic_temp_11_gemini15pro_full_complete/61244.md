emanation:/ˌeməˈneɪʃn/
n.
流出物，散发物；放射物
Something that originates or issues from a source.
A mysterious emanation filled the room.  一股神秘的气体弥漫了整个房间。

The strange emanation from the substance made the scientists cautious.  这种物质散发出的奇怪气体使科学家们小心翼翼。

emission, effluence, exhalation
absorption, intake
