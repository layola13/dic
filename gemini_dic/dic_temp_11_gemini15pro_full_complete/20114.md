biotite:/ˈbaɪəˌtaɪt/| n. |黑云母|A common rock-forming mineral of the mica group, typically black or dark brown in color. It is a phyllosilicate mineral composed of layers of silica tetrahedra and octahedral sheets containing iron and magnesium.|Biotite is a common constituent of igneous and metamorphic rocks. 黑云母是火成岩和变质岩的常见成分。| The granite contained large flakes of biotite.  这块花岗岩含有大量的黑云母薄片。|Synonyms: mica, black mica|Antonyms: N/A|noun
