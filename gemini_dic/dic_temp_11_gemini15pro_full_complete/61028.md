elite's:/ɪˈliːts/
noun
精英们的；上层人士的
Possessive form of "elite," referring to something belonging to or associated with the elite.
The elite's opinions often shape public discourse. 精英们的观点经常塑造公众舆论。
The elite's vacation homes are often located in exclusive resorts. 上层人士的度假屋通常位于专属度假胜地。
Synonyms: upper class's, privileged's, aristocracy's
Antonyms: masses', commoners', proletariat's
词性: 名词所有格
