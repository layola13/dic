novae's:/ˈnoʊviːz/| n.|新星的|The possessive form of "novae," meaning belonging to or associated with novae (plural of nova, a star that suddenly increases greatly in brightness and then slowly returns to its original state over several months).|The astronomer studied the novae's spectra. 天文学家研究了新星的光谱。|近义词:supernovae's|反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive form.  Perhaps conceptually "dwarf star's")|词性:Possessive Noun
