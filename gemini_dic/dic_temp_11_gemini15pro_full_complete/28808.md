carrousel:/ˌkærəˈsel/ | n. |旋转木马；行李传送带|A merry-go-round, a revolving circular platform with seats shaped like animals, often horses, on which people ride for amusement. Also, a moving conveyor system, typically at an airport, for retrieving luggage. |例句：The children loved riding on the carrousel at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐场坐旋转木马。

例句：I collected my suitcase from the baggage carrousel. 我从行李传送带取走了我的行李箱。 |近义词：merry-go-round, roundabout (for amusement ride); conveyor belt, baggage claim (for luggage retrieval) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


carrousel:/ˌkærəˈzel/ | n. |骑兵的竞技表演；(旧)骑兵部队| A tournament on horseback, a military exercise involving displays of horsemanship and combat skills. Also, historically, a body of cavalry troops. | 例句：The king held a grand carrousel to celebrate his victory. 国王举行了一场盛大的骑兵竞技表演来庆祝他的胜利。

例句：The carrousel charged into battle with their lances raised.  骑兵部队举起长矛冲锋陷阵。| 近义词：tournament, joust (for competition); cavalry, squadron (for troop) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
