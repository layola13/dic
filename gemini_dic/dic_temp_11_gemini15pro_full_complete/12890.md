assumers:/əˈsuːmərz/| n.|假设者，臆断者|People who make assumptions without sufficient evidence are often called assumers.  没有充分证据就做出假设的人通常被称为臆断者。|Those assumers were wrong about the outcome of the election. 那些臆断者对选举结果的预测是错误的。|近义词：presumers, conjecturers|反义词：skeptics, doubters|noun
词源->
From assume (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents).  来自assume（动词）+ -er（构成表示行为者的名词的后缀）。
