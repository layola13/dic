intertubercular:/ˌɪntərtuːˈbɜːrkjʊlər/
adjective
结节间的
Situated between tubercles, particularly referring to the groove between the greater and lesser tubercles of the humerus.
The intertubercular groove houses the tendon of the long head of the biceps brachii muscle.
近义词: intertubercular sulcus
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


intertubercular:/ˌɪntərtuːˈbɜːrkjʊlər/
noun
结节间沟
The groove between tubercles, specifically the intertubercular sulcus of the humerus.
The surgeon carefully identified the intertubercular, ensuring not to damage the biceps tendon.
近义词: intertubercular sulcus, bicipital groove
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


词源:
From Latin *inter* (between) + *tuberculum* (small swelling, tubercle).
