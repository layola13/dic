grubble:/ˈɡrʌbəl/ | v. | 翻找；摸索；搜尋 | To search or rummage about, especially blindly or awkwardly. |  She grubbled in her purse for her keys. 她翻找着钱包找钥匙。

He grubbled around in the dark for the light switch. 他在黑暗中摸索着寻找电灯开关。| 近义词：rummage, fumble, search, grope | 反义词: organize, arrange | 词性: 动词
