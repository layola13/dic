greenliest:/ˈɡriːnɪst/
adjective
最绿的
superlative adjective of green; having the most green color.
The greenliest hills are found in the spring. 春天的小山最绿。
近义词:lushest, verdant
反义词:barrenest, brownest
词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *greneliest, from Old English grēnliost, superlative of grēnlic (“green”).  Equivalent to green +‎ -est.
