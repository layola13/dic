detergency:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒənsi/
noun
去垢力；洗涤力；清洁力
The quality of being a detergent; cleansing power.
This new laundry detergent boasts superior detergency.  这种新的洗衣液拥有卓越的去污力。
The detergency of this soap is not very good.  这种肥皂的去污力不太好。
synonyms: cleansing power, cleaning power, washing power, cleaning ability, washing ability
antonyms: dirtiness, filthiness
n.
