concessives:/kənˈsɛsɪvz/ | n. |让步状语从句; 让步; 让步从句|Clauses that introduce information that contrasts with information in the main clause, but does not prevent the main clause from being true. They often begin with words like "although," "though," "even though," "while," "whereas,"  "despite," "in spite of," etc.| Although it was raining, we went for a walk. 尽管下雨了，我们还是去散步了。|近义词: concessions, allowances |反义词: demands, requirements |词性: noun
