coulee:/ˈkuːliː/
noun
深谷，熔岩沟，峡谷
A deep ravine or gulch, often dry, especially one carved by a stream. Also, a stream of lava.
The Grand Coulee is a spectacular example of a coulee carved by glacial floodwaters.  大古力是一个由冰川洪水雕刻而成的峡谷的壮观例子。

The solidified coulee of lava snaked down the mountainside. 凝固的熔岩沟沿着山坡蜿蜒而下。

近义词: canyon, ravine, gulch, gorge
反义词: ridge, hill

词性: n.
