bibliophile:/ˈbɪbliəˌfaɪl/ | n. | 藏书家；爱书的人 | A person who collects or has a great love of books. |  He was a bibliophile with a library of over 10,000 volumes. 他是一位藏书家，拥有超过一万册的藏书。 |  bibliomaniac, bookworm, book lover | book hater, anti-bibliophile | noun

bibliomaniac:/ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 嗜书狂；藏书癖 | A person who has a passionate, excessive, and sometimes irrational love for collecting and possessing books. | She's a bibliomaniac, buying books she'll never read just to own them. 她是个藏书癖，买了很多书，只是为了拥有它们，却从来不读。 | bibliophile, bookworm | book hater, anti-bibliophile | noun

bibliotaphist:/ˌbɪbliəˈtæfɪst/ | n. | 藏书癖者；一个把书藏起来的人（尤指珍本或稀有书籍）| A person who hoards and hides books, often rare or valuable ones, usually without reading them. | The bibliotaphist kept his prized first edition locked away, never to be seen. 这位藏书癖者将他珍贵的第一版书籍锁了起来，从不让别人看到。 | book hoarder, book collector | bibliophile, book lover | noun
