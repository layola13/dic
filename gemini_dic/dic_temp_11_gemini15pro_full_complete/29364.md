castrato:/kæˈstrɑːtoʊ/ (plural: castrati)
|n.|阉伶歌手|A male singer who was castrated before puberty to preserve his high voice.|The castrati were highly prized in 18th-century opera. 18世纪的歌剧中，阉伶歌手备受推崇。|Synonyms: eunuch singer|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps baritone, bass)|Noun
