outsearched:/ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃt/
verb
搜索胜过，在搜索方面超过
to surpass in searching; to find more effectively through searching
例句：Google outsearched its competitors and became the dominant search engine. 谷歌在搜索方面胜过了它的竞争对手，成为了主要的搜索引擎。

例句：She outsearched everyone in the library, finally finding the obscure document. 她在图书馆里搜索胜过所有人，最终找到了那份晦涩的文件。
近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed
反义词：underperform, lose to
词性：v.
