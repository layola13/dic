hyperfunctioning:/ˌhaɪpərˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/
adjective
机能亢进的；过度活跃的
functioning at an abnormally high level of activity.
例句：Her hyperfunctioning thyroid was causing her heart to race.  她的甲状腺功能亢进导致心跳加速。

例句：The hyperfunctioning immune system can lead to autoimmune diseases.  过度活跃的免疫系统会导致自身免疫性疾病。

近义词：overactive, hyperactive
反义词：hypofunctioning, underactive
词性：adjective
