abrogable:/ˈæbrəɡəbl/
adj.
可废除的；可取消的
That which can be abrogated; repealable.
The contract contains several abrogable clauses.  该合同包含几个可废除的条款。
The law is abrogable. 这项法律是可以废除的。
近义词: repealable, revocable, annullable, rescindable
反义词: irrevocable, unalterable, permanent
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *abrogabilis, from Latin abrogare (“to repeal”) + -bilis (“-able”).  来自晚期拉丁语 *abrogabilis，源自拉丁语 abrogare（“废除”）+ -bilis（“-able”）。
