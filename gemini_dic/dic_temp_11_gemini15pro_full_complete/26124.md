bustee: /ˈbʌstiː/
n.
贫民窟；棚户区
A slum area or shantytown.
例句：The bustee was overcrowded and lacked basic sanitation.  这个贫民窟人满为患，缺乏基本的卫生设施。

例句：Many families in the bustee struggled to find enough food each day.  贫民窟里的许多家庭每天都在为寻找足够的食物而苦苦挣扎。

近义词：slum, shantytown, ghetto
反义词: affluent area, wealthy neighborhood
词性: noun
