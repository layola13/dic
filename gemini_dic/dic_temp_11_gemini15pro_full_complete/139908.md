ode's:/oʊdz/
n.
颂诗的(复数)
plural form of ode, meaning poems that express noble feelings, often about a specific person, thing, or event.
例句：Keats's odes are some of the most beautiful poems in the English language.  济慈的颂诗是英语中最美丽的诗歌之一。

例句：She wrote several odes celebrating the beauty of nature. 她写了几首颂扬自然之美的颂诗。
近义词：poems, lyrics, verses
反义词: (Ode's as a plural noun doesn't have a direct antonym.)
词性:Noun (plural)
