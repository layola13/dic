mincer:/ˈmɪnsər/| n.|绞肉机；碎肉机；切碎机|A machine used to mince food, especially meat.|She used a mincer to grind the beef for the burgers. 她用绞肉机把牛肉绞碎做汉堡。|近义词：grinder, chopper|反义词: N/A|名词

mince:/mɪns/| v.|切碎；剁碎；绞碎|To cut or chop food, especially meat, into very small pieces.|He minced the garlic finely before adding it to the pan. 他把大蒜切得很碎才放到锅里。|近义词：chop, grind, dice|反义词: N/A|动词

词源: From Middle English *mincen, from Old French mincier, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Vulgar Latin *minutiare, from Latin minutus (“small, minute”).
