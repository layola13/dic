activated:/ˈæktɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 激活的，启动的 | made active or operative; stimulated | The security system is activated by a motion sensor.  该安全系统由运动传感器激活。|  例句：The charcoal filter is activated by heating it.  木炭过滤器通过加热来激活。| 近义词: triggered, switched on, enabled, started, initiated | 反义词: deactivated, disabled, off, inactive | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *activātus, past participle of activāre (“to make active”), from activus (“active”).
