freestyler:/ˈfriːstaɪlər/| n.|自由式选手，即兴表演者|A person who performs freestyle, especially in rap, sports, or other creative activities.  A freestyler improvises their performance rather than following a fixed routine.|He's an amazing freestyler, able to rap off the top of his head for hours. 他是一位令人惊叹的自由说唱歌手，能够连续几个小时即兴说唱。|Synonyms: improviser, ad-libber|Antonyms: planner, traditionalist|noun

freestyler:/ˈfriːstaɪlər/| n.|自由式滑雪者|A person who performs freestyle skiing. |She's a competitive freestyler hoping to make the Olympics. 她是一位有竞争力的自由式滑雪运动员，希望能参加奥运会。|Synonyms: freestyle skier |Antonyms: N/A|noun
