nonclaimable:/ˌnɒnˈkleɪməbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈkleɪməbl/ (UK)
adjective
不可索赔的，不能认领的
Not able to be claimed or requested, especially of expenses, benefits, or property.
This parking fee is nonclaimable on expenses. 这笔停车费不能报销。
The lost luggage was deemed nonclaimable.  丢失的行李被认为无法认领。
Synonyms: unrecoverable, irretrievable, nonrefundable
Antonyms: claimable, recoverable, refundable
词性: 形容词
