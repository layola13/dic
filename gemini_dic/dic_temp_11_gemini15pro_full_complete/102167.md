英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection.  As a pronoun, it asks for information about something. As a determiner, it modifies a noun to indicate which thing or things are being referred to. As an adverb, it modifies an adjective or another adverb to indicate degree or extent. As an interjection, it expresses surprise or disbelief.

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？

What book are you reading? = 你在读什么书？
What color is your car? = 你的车是什么颜色的？
