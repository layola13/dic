exinanition:/ɪɡˌzɪnˈeɪʃən/ |n.|精疲力竭，极度衰弱|The state of being completely exhausted or emptied of strength.  The marathon runner collapsed in a state of utter exinanition.|例句：The hikers reached the summit in a state of exinanition.  登山者们精疲力竭地到达了山顶。

例句：He felt a deep exinanition after days without food or sleep.  几天没吃没睡后，他感到极度虚弱。|近义词：exhaustion, depletion, prostration, enervation|反义词：vigor, energy, strength|词性:名词
