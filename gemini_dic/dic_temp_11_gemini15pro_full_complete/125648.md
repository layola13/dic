mummification:/ˌmʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|木乃伊化；干尸化|The process of preserving a body by embalming it or drying it out, especially as practiced in ancient Egypt.|The mummification process involved removing the internal organs and wrapping the body in linen bandages.|近义词:embalming, preservation|反义词:decomposition, decay|noun
