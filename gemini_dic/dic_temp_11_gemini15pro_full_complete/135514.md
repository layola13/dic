nonplausibilities:/ˌnɑːnˌplɔːzəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.| 不可信性；难以置信之处；不合情理之处|The nonplausibilities in his story made it difficult to believe.  他故事中的不可信之处让人难以相信。|
|英文解析:The state or quality of being implausible; something that is implausible.|例句：The detective pointed out several nonplausibilities in the suspect's alibi.  侦探指出了嫌疑人不在场证明中的几个疑点。|近义词：implausibilities, improbabilities, unlikelihoods|反义词：plausibilities, probabilities, likelihoods|词性:noun (plural)
