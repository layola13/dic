baking: /ˈbeɪkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbeɪkɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|烘焙，烘烤; 烘烤的|The process of cooking food by dry heat, especially in an oven. Also used as an adjective to describe items related to baking.|
例句：
The baking of the bread took longer than expected.
面包的烘烤比预期的要长。

I love the smell of baking bread.
我喜欢烤面包的味道。

近义词：roasting, cooking|反义词:freezing, chilling|词性: noun, adjective

baking: /ˈbeɪkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbeɪkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|灼热的，酷热的|Extremely hot.|
例句：
It was a baking hot day.
今天天气酷热。

The baking sun beat down on us.
灼热的太阳照在我们身上。

近义词：scorching, burning|反义词:freezing, cold|词性: adjective
