backslapping: /ˈbækˌslæpɪŋ/
backslapping: n.
backslapping: 过分亲昵的恭维；互相吹捧；拍背恭维
backslapping: The act of slapping someone's back, often as a gesture of congratulation or camaraderie, but sometimes perceived as excessive or insincere.
backslapping:  There was a lot of backslapping and cheering after the team won the championship. 比赛结束后，队员们互相拍背欢呼庆祝胜利。

backslapping: 近义词: praise, flattery, commendation,  mutual admiration
backslapping: 反义词: criticism, condemnation, disapproval

backslapping: 词性: 名词 (noun)
