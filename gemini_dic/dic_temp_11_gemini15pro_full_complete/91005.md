husbander:/ˈhʌzbəndər/ | n. | 节俭的人，管理者，节约者 |  Someone who manages resources carefully and avoids waste.  A person who conserves or uses sparingly. | The farmer was a good husbander of his land, rotating crops and using sustainable practices.  这位农民精耕细作，轮作作物，采用可持续的耕作方式。| 近义词：economizer, conserver, manager, steward | 反义词：spendthrift, waster, squanderer | 词性: 名词

husbander:/ˈhʌzbəndər/ | vt. | 节约，管理，精打细算 |  To manage resources economically; to use sparingly; to conserve. |  She carefully husbandered her small income. 她精打细算地管理她微薄的收入. | 近义词：economize, conserve, manage, save | 反义词：waste, squander, dissipate | 词性: 动词
