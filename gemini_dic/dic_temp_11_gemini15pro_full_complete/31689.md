chaus:/ʃoʊz/ | n. | 鞋子，袜子 (尤指旧的或破旧的) | Worn-out shoes or socks.  Comes from French "chausses," meaning "hose" or "leggings." Originally referred to a kind of close-fitting breeches covering the legs and feet.  Over time, its meaning narrowed to footwear, particularly worn ones. |  He discarded his old chaus. 他丢弃了他的旧鞋子。| synonyms: footwear, shoes, socks (when context implies worn condition) | antonyms: new shoes | noun
