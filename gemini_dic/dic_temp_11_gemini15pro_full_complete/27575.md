canals:/kəˈnælz/
n.
运河；水道
Artificial waterways constructed to allow the passage of boats or ships inland or to convey water for irrigation.
The Panama Canal connects the Atlantic and Pacific oceans. 巴拿马运河连接大西洋和太平洋。
channels, waterways
rivers, streams
