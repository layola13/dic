incertitude:/ɪnˈsɜːrtɪtjuːd/ | n. |不确定性，犹豫，疑惑|a state of uncertainty or hesitation|例句：The incertitude surrounding the election results created anxiety. 选举结果的不确定性造成了焦虑。

例句：He expressed incertitude about the future of the company. 他对公司的未来表示不确定。

近义词：uncertainty, doubt, hesitation, ambiguity, vagueness|反义词：certainty, assurance, confidence, conviction|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incertitudo, from Latin incertus (“uncertain”) + -tudo (“-tude”). Equivalent to in- + certitude.
