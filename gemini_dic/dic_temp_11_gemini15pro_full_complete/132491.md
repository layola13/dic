nondesecrations: /ˌnɒnˌdɛsɪˈkreɪʃənz/ | n. | 未亵渎，未玷污 | The preservation of ancient artifacts relies on their nondesecrations.  保护古代文物依赖于它们不被亵渎。|  The museum prides itself on the nondesecrations of its exhibits.  博物馆以其展品未受玷污而自豪。 | 近义词: preservation, respect, reverence | 反义词: desecration, profanation, violation | 词性: 名词

词源:  "nondesecrations" comes from "non-" (a prefix meaning "not") + "desecration" (from Latin "desecrare," meaning "to divest of sacred character").
