orthodoxian:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksiən/
n.|正统基督教信徒|A believer in Orthodox Christianity.|He considered himself an orthodoxian, adhering strictly to the doctrines of the Eastern Orthodox Church. 他认为自己是一位正统基督教信徒，严格遵守东正教的教义。|近义词:Orthodox Christian, Eastern Orthodox believer|反义词:heterodox, heretic|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Orthodox + -ian (suffix forming nouns denoting persons belonging to or associated with a specified thing, system, or place).  "Orthodox" itself comes from Greek "orthos" (straight, right) + "doxa" (opinion, belief).
