loover:/ˈluːvər/| n.|百叶窗，散热片，通风装置|A louver is a window blind or shutter with horizontal slats that can be adjusted to admit light and air while keeping out rain and direct sunshine.  A louver can also refer to a vent or other opening with similar slats for ventilation or to control airflow.|The louvers on the windows were closed to keep out the heat. 窗户上的百叶窗关上了以阻挡热量。|The computer's cooling system uses a series of louvers to dissipate heat. 电脑的冷却系统使用一系列散热片来散热。| synonyms: blind, shutter, vent, grille| antonyms: (For window covering) curtain, drape |noun
