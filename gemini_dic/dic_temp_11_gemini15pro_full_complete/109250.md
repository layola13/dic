lekgotla: /ləˈxɔtɬɑː/
noun
南非的部落会议; 议事会
A traditional Tswana assembly or council.  Specifically, a tribal, village, or community meeting or court in Southern Africa, particularly Lesotho and Botswana.
The village elders convened a lekgotla to discuss the water shortage. 村里的长老们召集了一个部落会议来讨论缺水问题。
The lekgotla decided to impose a fine on anyone caught wasting water. 部落会议决定对任何被发现浪费水的人处以罚款。
synonym: council, assembly, meeting, court, gathering
antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dispersal," "separation")
