amissibility:/əˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/
n.
可接纳性，可容许性
The quality of being admissible; admissibleness.  The capability of being admitted or allowed.
The amissibility of evidence in court is crucial.  法庭上证据的可采纳性至关重要。

近义词:acceptability, permissibility, allowability
反义词:inadmissibility, unacceptability
