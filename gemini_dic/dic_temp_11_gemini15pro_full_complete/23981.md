breathalyse:/ˈbrɛθəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v. |进行呼吸测试；对…进行酒精测试| To test someone's breath to see how much alcohol they have drunk. |The police officer breathalysed the driver at the scene of the accident.  警察在事故现场对司机进行了呼吸测试。

The driver was breathalysed and found to be over the limit.  司机接受了呼吸测试，发现超过了限制。|近义词：test for alcohol,  check for alcohol |反义词: N/A |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From breath + -alyse (analyze).  "Analyse" is the British spelling of "analyze", meaning to examine something methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it.  Thus, "breathalyse" literally means to analyze someone's breath.
