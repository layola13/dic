overflattens:/ˌoʊvərˈflætənz/
verb
过度展平，过度压平
to flatten something excessively, beyond what is necessary or desirable
The baker overflattened the dough, resulting in a thin and crispy crust.  面包师把面团擀得过薄，导致面包皮又薄又脆。
The artist overflattened the clay, losing the desired texture.  艺术家把黏土压得太扁，失去了想要的质感。
synonyms: flatten excessively, over-compress
antonyms: puff up, inflate
词性:v.
