hanabi:/hɑːˈnɑːbiː/| n.|花火，烟火，烟花|A Japanese word meaning "fireworks."  It refers to the traditional Japanese fireworks displays, often held in summer festivals.|例句：The hanabi display over the Sumida River was breathtaking.  隅田川上的烟火表演令人叹为观止。

例句：We watched the hanabi light up the night sky. 我们看着烟花照亮夜空。|近义词：fireworks, pyrotechnics|反义词: None|词性:noun
