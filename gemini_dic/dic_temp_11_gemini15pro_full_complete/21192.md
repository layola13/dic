blockages:/ˈblɒkɪdʒɪz/ (UK) /ˈblɑːkɪdʒɪz/ (US)| n.|阻塞；堵塞|Something that stops something else from moving through or along something else. |The recent heavy rains have caused blockages in the city's drainage system. 最近的大雨导致城市排水系统堵塞。

The doctor found several blockages in the patient's arteries. 医生在病人的动脉中发现了多处堵塞。|近义词：obstructions, barriers, impediments|反义词: openings, clearances|词性:名词
