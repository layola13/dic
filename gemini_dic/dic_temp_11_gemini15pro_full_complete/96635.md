induplicative:/ɪnˌdjuːplɪˈkeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 重复的，双重的 | Characterized by reduplication; formed by or involving reduplication.  |  The word "choo-choo" is an induplicative word, formed by repeating "choo".  火车“choo-choo”是一个重复的词，由重复“choo”构成。 | synonyms: reduplicated, repetitive, iterated | antonyms: unique, singular, non-repetitive | adjective
