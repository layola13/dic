alveolar:/ælˈviːələr/ (US), /ælˈviːələ(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.| 肺泡的；齿龈音的；牙槽的 |Relating to or resembling alveoli (small air sacs in the lungs). Also refers to sounds produced by placing the tongue against the alveolar ridge (the ridge behind the upper teeth).  Can also refer to the jaw section containing the teeth sockets.|The alveolar sacs are crucial for gas exchange. 肺泡囊对于气体交换至关重要。

He has difficulty pronouncing alveolar consonants. 他发齿龈音辅音有困难。

The dentist examined the alveolar bone around the tooth. 牙医检查了牙齿周围的牙槽骨。|近义词：(for adj.) pulmonary, dental (depending on context) |反义词: (no direct antonym, depends on specific meaning)|词性: adjective, noun


alveolus:/ælˈviːələs/ (US), /ælˈviːələs/ (UK)| n. | 肺泡；牙槽|A small air sac in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place. Also refers to a tooth socket.|The alveoli are tiny air sacs in the lungs. 肺泡是肺部微小的气囊。

The tooth was extracted, leaving an empty alveolus. 牙齿被拔掉了，留下了一个空的牙槽。|近义词: (for lung meaning) air sac |反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alveolus* "small cavity, hollow," diminutive of *alveus* "hollow, cavity, trough, tub, boat."
