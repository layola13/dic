beady:/ˈbiːdi/| adj.|小而明亮的（眼睛）；（目光）锐利的，机警的|Small, round, and shiny (typically used to describe eyes); alert and watchful, often suggesting shrewdness or suspicion.|He looked at me with beady eyes. 他用小而闪亮的眼睛看着我。|The bird has beady black eyes. 这只鸟有一双乌黑发亮的小眼睛.|Synonyms: bright, gleaming, sharp, observant|Antonyms: dull, unobservant|adjective
