obsessives: /əbˈsɛsɪvz/

adjective|痴迷的，强迫性的|Excessively preoccupied with something or someone. |She has an obsessive fear of germs. 她对细菌有一种强迫性的恐惧。|近义词: compulsive, fixated, fanatical|反义词: casual, moderate, indifferent|词性:adj.
