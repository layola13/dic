hydroid:/ˈhaɪdrɔɪd/ | n. | 水螅体；水螅型的 |A small, marine animal related to jellyfish, typically having a polyp stage and a medusa stage in its life cycle.  A member of the class Hydrozoa. |  The hydroid colony attached itself to the rock.  水螅群体附着在岩石上。| Synonyms: polyp, hydrozoan | Antonyms: medusa (in some contexts, as they are alternating life stages) | Noun

hydroid:/ˈhaɪdrɔɪd/ | adj. | 水螅型的；似水螅的 | Relating to or resembling a hydroid.  Having the characteristics of a hydroid. | The hydroid form is sessile. 水螅型是固着的。 | Synonyms: hydrozoan, polyp-like | Antonyms: medusoid (referring to the free-swimming medusa form) | Adjective
