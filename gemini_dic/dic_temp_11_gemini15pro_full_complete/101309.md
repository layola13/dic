inwardnesses:/ˈɪnwərdnɪsɪz/
n.
内心深处；隐秘的想法；心灵的深处
the quality of having thoughts and feelings that are not expressed or shown to other people
He explored the inwardnesses of human nature in his novels. 他在小说中探索了人性的内心深处。

近义词:  interiority, subjectivity, privacy, introspection
反义词:  exteriority, outwardness
