minimizations:/ˌmɪnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |最小化，减少，缩小|The process of reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  The act of minimizing something. |The company focused on cost minimizations to improve profitability. 公司专注于成本最小化以提高盈利能力。|The minimizations of risk is a key concern for investors.  风险最小化是投资者主要关注的问题。|synonyms: reductions, diminutions, curtailments, decreases|antonyms: increases, expansions, augmentations|noun
