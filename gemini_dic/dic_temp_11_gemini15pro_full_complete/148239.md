oversimplistic: /ˌōvərˌsɪmplˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于简化的，过于简单化的 |Characterized by extreme simplicity; tending to simplify to such an extent as to distort or misrepresent. | The explanation was oversimplistic and failed to address the complexities of the issue.  这个解释过于简单化，未能解决问题的复杂性。 | Synonyms: simplistic, naive, reductive, shallow | Antonyms: nuanced, sophisticated, complex, comprehensive | Word type: adjective

Etymology:
From over- + simplistic.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessive." "Simplistic" is an adjective meaning "characterized by extreme simplicity."
