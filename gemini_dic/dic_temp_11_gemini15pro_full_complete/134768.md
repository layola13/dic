nonmercenaries:/ˌnɑnˈmɜːrsənɛriz/ | n. | 非雇佣兵 |  People who are not soldiers for hire; those who fight or work for reasons other than financial gain. |  The resistance movement was made up largely of nonmercenaries, fighting for their homeland. 抵抗运动主要由非雇佣兵组成，他们为自己的家园而战。| synonyms: volunteers, patriots, conscripts, draftees | antonyms: mercenaries, soldiers of fortune, hired guns | noun

nonmercenary:/ˌnɑnˈmɜːrsənɛri/ | adj. | 非雇佣的 | Not motivated by or involving payment; not mercenary. |  Her motives for helping were entirely nonmercenary; she simply wanted to do the right thing. 她帮助别人的动机完全是非雇佣性质的；她只是想做正确的事。| synonyms: altruistic, selfless, charitable, philanthropic | antonyms: mercenary, venal, greedy, avaricious | adjective
