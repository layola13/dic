legitimates:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪts/
|v.|使合法化；证明…为合法|To make something legal or lawful; to show or prove that something is justified or reasonable.|The government legitimized the use of force. 政府使武力的使用合法化。|validates, authorizes, justifies|invalidates, delegitimizes|verb
legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
|adj.|合法的；正当的；合理的|Conforming to the law or to rules; able to be defended with logic or justification; genuine.|Her claim to the throne is legitimate. 她对王位的要求是合法的。|legal, valid, lawful|illegal, invalid, unlawful|adjective
legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
|n.|合法的东西|Something that is considered legal, valid, or acceptable.|The lawyer argued that the contract was a legitimate document. 律师辩称该合同是合法文件。|legal entity, valid item|illegal item, invalid entity|noun
