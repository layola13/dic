myopic:/maɪˈɒpɪk/ /maɪˈɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 近视的；目光短浅的 | Lacking foresight or intellectual insight; short-sighted.  |  He has a myopic view of the world, failing to see the bigger picture. 他对世界的看法很狭窄，看不到全局。

He made a myopic decision to sell his stocks when the market dipped slightly. 他做了一个短视的决定，在市场略微下跌时就卖掉了他的股票。 | 近义词: short-sighted, nearsighted, narrow-minded, unthinking | 反义词: far-sighted, prescient, visionary | 词性: adjective
