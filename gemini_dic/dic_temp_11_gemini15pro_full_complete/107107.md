ladlefuls:/ˈleɪdlfʊlz/
noun
一勺勺；几勺的量
The amount held by several ladlefuls.  She served the soup in generous ladlefuls.
She served the soup in generous ladlefuls. 她盛汤时，每一勺都盛得满满的。
The stew was ladled out in generous ladlefuls. 炖菜被慷慨地一勺勺舀了出来。
