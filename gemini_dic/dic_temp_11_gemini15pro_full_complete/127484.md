necessity:/nəˈsesəti/ /nɪˈsesəti/| n.|必要性，必需品|Something that is necessary; an essential requirement.|例句：Water is a necessity of life.  水是生命必需品。|近义词：essential, requirement, need, requisite|反义词:luxury, nonessential|词性:noun
necessity:/nəˈsesəti/ /nɪˈsesəti/| n.|必然性，不可避免性|The state or quality of being necessary; inevitability.|例句：The necessity of change became apparent. 变革的必要性变得显而易见。|近义词：inevitability, certainty|反义词:impossibility, chance|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *necessite (Modern French nécessité), from Latin necessitas (“necessity, constraint, compulsion, fate, destiny”), from necessarius (“necessary, unavoidable, inevitable”), from necesse (“unavoidable, inevitable”), from ne- (“not”) + cedere (“to yield”).
