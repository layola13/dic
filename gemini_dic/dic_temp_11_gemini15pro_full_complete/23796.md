bray:/breɪ/
verb
大声而刺耳地叫；发出驴叫声
to utter a loud harsh cry, characteristic of a donkey
The donkey brayed loudly. 驴子大声地叫。
近义词:hee-haw, shriek, yell
反义词:whisper, murmur
词性:v.

bray:/breɪ/
noun
驴叫声
the loud harsh cry of a donkey
The bray of the donkey echoed through the valley. 驴的叫声在山谷中回荡。
近义词:hee-haw
反义词:silence
词性:n.
