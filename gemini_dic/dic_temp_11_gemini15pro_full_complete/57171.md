dowelled:/ˈdaʊəld/| adj.|销钉连接的，用木钉固定的|Joined or fastened with dowels.  A dowel is a small cylindrical rod, usually made of wood, plastic, or metal, used for joining two pieces of material together.|The two pieces of wood were dowelled together.  这两块木头是用木钉连接在一起的。|近义词：pinned, pegged, joined|反义词：unjoined, separated|adjective
