近义词: innovative, pioneering, revolutionary, original, novel
反义词: traditional, conventional, unoriginal, outdated, antiquated
词性: adjective, noun

词源:  From "ground" + "breaking," literally referring to the act of breaking the ground to begin construction. The figurative meaning of "innovative" developed later.
