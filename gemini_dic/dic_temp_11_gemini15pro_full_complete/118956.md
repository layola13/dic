meteoromancy:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒmənsi/
n.|流星占卜|Divination by observing meteors or shooting stars.  The practice of attempting to foretell the future by observing meteors. |Ancient cultures practiced meteoromancy, believing that shooting stars held messages from the gods.| 近义词:aeromancy, divination |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "scientific forecasting")|词性:noun
