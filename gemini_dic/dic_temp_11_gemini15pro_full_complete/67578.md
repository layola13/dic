extrapunitively:/ˌekstrəˈpʌnɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 外罚性地；向外攻击地 | Directing one's anger or aggression outward, towards others or the environment, rather than inward towards oneself.  |  Example: He reacted extrapunitively to the criticism, blaming his colleagues for his own shortcomings. 他以向外攻击的方式回应批评，将自己的缺点归咎于同事。 | Example:  Extrapunitively blaming the government for all her problems, she refused to acknowledge her own role in her difficulties.  她将自己所有的问题都归咎于政府，拒绝承认自己在困境中扮演的角色。 | Synonym: outwardly, aggressively,  | Antonym: intropunitively, inwardly | adverb
