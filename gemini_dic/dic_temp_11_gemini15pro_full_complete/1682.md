acolyte: /ˈækəlaɪt/ | n. |侍僧，助手，追随者 |  A person who assists in a religious ceremony or rite.  More broadly, it refers to a devoted follower or assistant. | The priest was assisted by his acolytes during the Mass.  神父在弥撒期间由他的侍僧协助。 | The CEO's acolytes quickly carried out his every command.  首席执行官的助手们迅速执行了他的每一项命令。 | synonyms: assistant, follower, supporter, disciple, adherent | antonyms: leader, opponent, adversary | noun
