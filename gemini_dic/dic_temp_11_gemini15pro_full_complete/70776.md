finfoot:/ˈfɪnˌfʊt/| n.| 鳍足鸟；非洲鹃 |A tropical freshwater bird with lobed feet, similar to a grebe or coot, and a sharp beak. They are found in Africa, Asia, and South America. |例句：The finfoot dived expertly into the murky water. 鳍足鸟熟练地潜入浑浊的水中。

例句：We spotted a masked finfoot foraging along the riverbank. 我们看到一只戴着面具的鳍足鸟在河岸边觅食。|近义词：grebe, coot, sungrebe|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *fin* (referring to the lobes on their feet) + *foot*.
