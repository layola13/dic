non-verbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/ (US)| adj.|非言语的；不用语言的|Not involving or using words; communicating without speech.|She gave me a non-verbal cue to be quiet. 她用非语言的暗示让我安静。|Synonyms: unspoken, wordless, silent, unsaid, mute|Antonyms: verbal, spoken|adjective

non-verbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/ (US)| n.|非言语交际；非言语行为|A way of communicating without speaking, such as through gestures, facial expressions, or body language.|Body language is a powerful form of non-verbal communication.  肢体语言是一种强有力的非语言交流形式。|Synonyms: body language, nonverbal communication|Antonyms: verbal communication|noun
