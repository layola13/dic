arabesque:/ˌærəˈbɛsk/ | n. v. adj. |蔓藤花纹，阿拉伯式花纹；（芭蕾舞姿）阿拉贝斯克|An arabesque is an ornamental design consisting of intertwined flowing lines, typically based on plant forms.  As a verb, it means to form or decorate with arabesques. As an adjective, it describes something decorated with or resembling arabesques. |The dancer held a graceful arabesque. 舞蹈演员保持着优雅的阿拉贝斯克舞姿。| 近义词: flourish, ornamentation, embellishment (noun); adorn, decorate (verb); ornate, decorative (adjective) | 反义词: plain, simple (adjective) | 词性: noun, verb, adjective

arabesqued:/ˌærəˈbɛskt/ | adj. | 用蔓藤花纹装饰的 | Decorated with arabesques. | The ceiling was arabesqued with intricate gold patterns. 天花板装饰着复杂的金色蔓藤花纹。 | 近义词: ornamented, decorated, embellished | 反义词: plain, simple, undecorated | 词性: adjective


词源: From French *arabesque*, from Italian *arabesco* "Arabian," from *Arabo* "Arab." Ultimately from Arabic *ʻarab*.
