checkrein: /ˈtʃɛkˌreɪn/ | n. |  (马的)限制缰绳; 约束; 限制 | A short rein attached to the bridle or headstall and running to a harness saddle, used to prevent a horse from lowering its head. | The horse was fitted with a checkrein to keep its head high. 这匹马被装上了限制缰绳以保持头部抬高。|Synonyms: curb, restraint, restriction | Antonyms: freedom, liberty | noun

checkrein: /ˈtʃɛkˌreɪn/ | v. |  控制; 约束 | To control or restrain something. |  The government attempted to checkrein the press. 政府试图控制新闻界。 | Synonyms: restrain, curb, control | Antonyms: free, liberate | verb
