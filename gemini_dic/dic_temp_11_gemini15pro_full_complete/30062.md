ccps:/ˌsiː siː piː ˈɛs/ |abbr.|连续循环生产系统 (Continuous Cyclic Production System)| A manufacturing system characterized by continuous flow, standardized work, and a focus on minimizing waste. | Example: The CCPS implementation significantly reduced lead times. 连续循环生产系统的实施显著缩短了交付周期。 | Synonym:  continuous flow manufacturing, lean manufacturing | Antonym: batch production |abbreviation
