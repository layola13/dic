hemelytrum:/ˈhɛmɪˌlaɪtrəm/| n. |半鞘翅|The hardened, leathery forewing of a true bug, such as a stink bug, that covers the membranous hindwing when at rest.|The stink bug's hemelytrum provided excellent camouflage.  椿象的半鞘翅提供了极好的伪装。|forewing, tegmen|hindwing|noun
hemelytra:/ˈhɛmɪˌlaɪtrə/| n. |半鞘翅（复数）|The hardened, leathery forewings of true bugs, such as stink bugs, that cover the membranous hindwings when at rest.|The hemelytra of the assassin bug are adapted for grasping prey. 刺客虫的半鞘翅适合抓捕猎物。|forewings, tegmina|hindwings|noun
