masio:/ˈmɑːsioʊ/ | n. | 揉捏，按摩 |  A kneading or massaging, especially of dough or clay.  Derived from Italian "masio,"  meaning "kneading trough." |  The baker gave the dough a thorough masio before shaping it into loaves.  面包师在把面团揉成面包之前彻底揉捏了一番。| Synonyms: kneading, massage, manipulation | Antonyms:  flattening, smoothing | noun
