cameras:/ˈkæmərəz/ (US), /ˈkæmrəz/ (UK)| n. |照相机，摄像机|A device for recording images, either as photographs or moving videos.|例句：The security cameras captured the entire incident. 安全摄像头拍下了整个事件。|近义词：recorders, video recorders|反义词: None|noun


cameras:/ˈkæmərəz/ (US), /ˈkæmrəz/ (UK)| n. |摄影机|A device used for making films or television programmes.|例句：Several cameras were used to film the concert.  几台摄像机被用来拍摄音乐会。|近义词：film cameras, video cameras|反义词: None|noun
