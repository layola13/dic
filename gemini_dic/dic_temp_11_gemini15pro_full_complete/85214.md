harkeners:/ˈhɑːrkənərz/
n.
倾听者，听众
Those who listen attentively.
The harkeners waited with bated breath for the storyteller to continue. 倾听者屏息等待着讲故事的人继续讲下去。
The politician addressed the harkeners with a passionate speech.  这位政治家向听众发表了热情洋溢的讲话。
listeners, audience
speakers, talkers
noun
