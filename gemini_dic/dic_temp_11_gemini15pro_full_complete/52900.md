dim:/dɪm/| v. |使变暗，使模糊|To make or become less bright or distinct.|The fog dimmed the headlights. 雾使车头灯变暗了。|近义词：darken, obscure, cloud, dull|反义词:brighten, illuminate, lighten|verb

dim:/dɪm/| adj. |昏暗的，模糊的，暗淡的|Not bright or distinct; faint or obscure.|The room was dimly lit. 房间里光线昏暗。|近义词：dark, faint, obscure, shadowy|反义词:bright, clear, distinct, vivid|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dimmen "to grow dark," from Proto-Germanic *dimmaz. Related to Dutch dimmen "to steam, exhale," German dämmern "to dawn, grow twilight." The original sense was likely "to become misty or vaporous."
