noncomplicity:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplɪsɪti/ | n. | 不参与；不共谋；不连累 | The state of not being involved in or associated with wrongdoing or harmful activity.  It implies a conscious choice to remain uninvolved, often in situations where others are complicit. |  Example 1: Her noncomplicity in the scandal protected her reputation. 她没有参与丑闻，从而保住了自己的名声。

Example 2: He maintained his noncomplicity despite pressure to join the conspiracy. 尽管面临加入阴谋的压力，他仍然保持不参与。 | 近义词: innocence, neutrality, noninvolvement, non-participation | 反义词: complicity, collusion, involvement, participation | 词性: 名词
