gibbsite:/ˈɡɪbsaɪt/
n.
三水铝石，水铝矿
A mineral form of aluminum hydroxide, Al(OH)₃, the most common constituent of bauxite.
Gibbsite is the most common form of aluminum hydroxide found in nature. 三水铝石是自然界中最常见的一种氢氧化铝形式。
Bauxite, the principal ore of aluminum, consists largely of gibbsite. 铝土矿，铝的主要矿石，主要由三水铝石组成。
近义词: hydrargillite (同义词,也是三水铝石的矿物学名称)
反义词: 无
词性: noun
