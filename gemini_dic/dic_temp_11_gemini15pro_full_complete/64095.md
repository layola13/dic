eponymist:/ˈɛpənɪmɪst/ | n. |命名者；名字来源者|A person after whom something is named, or a person who gives a name to something. |Example 1:  The scientist became the eponymist of the newly discovered element.  这位科学家成为了新发现元素的命名者。

Example 2: The eponymist of the disease was the doctor who first identified it.  这种疾病的命名者是第一个发现它的医生。|Synonym: namesake, name-giver |Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym) | noun
