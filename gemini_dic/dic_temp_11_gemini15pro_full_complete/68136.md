fahrenheit:/ˈfærənhaɪt/ | n. |华氏度；华氏温标|A temperature scale in which water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees.|The temperature today is 70 degrees Fahrenheit. 今天气温是70华氏度。|Near synonyms: Celsius, Centigrade |Antonyms: Celsius, Centigrade |noun
