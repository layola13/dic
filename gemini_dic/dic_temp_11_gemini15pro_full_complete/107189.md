lagoon:/ləˈɡuːn/| n.|泻湖；环礁湖|A lagoon is a shallow body of water separated from a larger body of water by a narrow landform, such as reefs, barrier islands, or a sandbar.|We kayaked across the beautiful lagoon. 我们乘皮划艇穿过美丽的泻湖。|The resort was built around a picturesque lagoon.  这个度假村建在一个风景如画的泻湖周围。|近义词：bay, inlet, sound|反义词:|词性:名词
