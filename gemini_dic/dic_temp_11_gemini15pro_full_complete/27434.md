camleted:/ˈkæmlɪtɪd/| adj.|毛料制的，用驼绒做的|Made of camel's hair or resembling it.  This refers specifically to a fabric made from camel hair, not just the color. |例句：She wore a camleted cloak to protect herself from the cold. 她穿了一件驼绒斗篷御寒。

例句：The antique chair was upholstered in a luxurious camleted fabric.  这把古董椅子是用奢华的驼绒面料装饰的。|近义词：camel-hair, woolen|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps: synthetic, cotton, linen) |词性: adjective
