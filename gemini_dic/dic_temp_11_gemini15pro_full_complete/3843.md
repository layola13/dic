agonothetic:/ˌæɡənoʊˈθɛtɪk/

Relating to contests or struggles, especially in intellectual or philosophical contexts.  Characterized by argument or debate.
Example: The agonothetic nature of the philosophical debate made it both stimulating and frustrating.  哲学辩论的竞争性质使其既刺激又令人沮丧。

Synonym:  contentious, polemical, argumentative, disputatious
Antonym:  conciliatory, cooperative, harmonious, agreeable
词性:  adjective
