deduce:/dɪˈd(j)uːs/ /dɪˈduːs/
verb
推断，演绎
To reach a conclusion from something known or assumed; infer.
Sherlock Holmes deduced from the evidence that the murderer was left-handed.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯从证据中推断出凶手是左撇子。

The police deduced that the robbers had entered through the back window. 警方推断劫匪是从后窗进入的。

infer, conclude, reason, gather
surmise, conjecture, guess, speculate
