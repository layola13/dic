astute:/əˈstʃuːt/ | adj. | 精明的，敏锐的，狡猾的 | Having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations or people and turn this to one's advantage. |  She made an astute observation about the company's financial situation. 她对公司的财务状况做出了精辟的观察。 |  shrewd, sharp, clever, perceptive, insightful |  naive, foolish, gullible, unintelligent | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *astutus*, from *astus* "craft, cunning."
