on-the-cards:/ˌɒn ðə ˈkɑːdz/ /ˌɑːn ðə ˈkɑːrdz/
adjective
可能的，预料之中的
likely to happen; expected
例句：A rise in interest rates is on the cards. 利率上升是预料之中的事。

例句：With the team playing so well, victory is definitely on the cards. 球队表现如此出色，胜利是必然的。

近义词：likely, probable, expected, anticipated
反义词：unlikely, improbable, unexpected

词性:adjective

词源: This idiom originates from card games, where the cards that are dealt determine the possible outcomes.  "On the cards" therefore refers to something being among the possible outcomes, making it likely.
