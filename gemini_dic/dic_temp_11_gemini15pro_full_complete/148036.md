overrude: /ˌoʊvərˈruːd/
adjective
过于粗鲁的，极其无礼的
Excessively rude; impolite to an extreme degree.
例句：His overrude behavior shocked everyone at the party. 他极其粗鲁的行为让派对上的每个人都感到震惊。

例句：It was overrude of him to interrupt the speaker like that. 他那样打断演讲者真是太无礼了。

近义词：insolent, impertinent, disrespectful, impolite, rude
反义词：polite, courteous, respectful


词性: adjective

词源:  A combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and the adjective "rude."  "Rude" comes from Middle English "rude," ultimately from Old French "rude" and Latin "rudis" meaning "rough, unpolished, crude."
