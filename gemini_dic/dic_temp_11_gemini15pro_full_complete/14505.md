avenger:/əˈven(d)ʒər/| n.|复仇者|Someone who takes vengeance for a wrong done to themselves or another person.|例句：He became an avenger, determined to punish those who had wronged him. 他成为了一个复仇者，决心惩罚那些冤枉他的人。

例句：The movie portrays her as an avenger, seeking justice for her family. 这部电影将她描绘成一个复仇者，为她的家人寻求正义。|近义词：retaliator, vindicator|反义词:forgiver, pardoner|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avengeor, from avengier, from Latin vindicare "to claim, avenge".
