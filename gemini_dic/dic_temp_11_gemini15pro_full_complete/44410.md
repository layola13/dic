cross-license:/ˌkrɔːs ˈlaɪsns/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈlaɪsns/ | n. v.|交叉许可，交叉授权|An agreement between two or more parties where they grant each other the right to use their respective intellectual property.|Example: The two companies signed a cross-license agreement to share their patented technologies.  两家公司签署了一项交叉许可协议，以共享其专利技术。|Synonyms: cross-licensing agreement, reciprocal license|Antonyms: exclusive license|Noun
cross-license:/ˌkrɔːs ˈlaɪsns/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈlaɪsns/ | v.|交叉许可，交叉授权|To grant a license to another party in exchange for a license from that party.|Example: They decided to cross-license their software to avoid potential legal battles.  他们决定交叉授权他们的软件，以避免潜在的法律纠纷。|Synonyms: reciprocally license|Antonyms: exclusively license|Verb
