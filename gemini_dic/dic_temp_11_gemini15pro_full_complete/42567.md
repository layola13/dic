counterparty:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɑːrti/ /ˈkaʊntərˌpɑrti/| n. |交易对手，对方|A counterparty is the other party involved in a financial contract or transaction.|例句：The bank acts as a counterparty in many financial transactions.  这家银行在许多金融交易中充当交易对手。

例句：It's important to assess the creditworthiness of your counterparty before entering into a contract. 在签订合同之前，评估交易对手的信用状况很重要。|近义词：opposite number, other party, participant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

counterparty:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɑːrti/ /ˈkaʊntərˌpɑrti/| n. |对应人员，对应机构|A counterparty can also refer to a person or organization that has a corresponding role or function in another organization or system.|例句：My counterparty in the negotiations was very experienced. 我在谈判中的对应人员非常有经验。

例句：The US agency works closely with its counterparties in Europe. 这家美国机构与其在欧洲的对应机构密切合作。|近义词： counterpart, equivalent, parallel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
