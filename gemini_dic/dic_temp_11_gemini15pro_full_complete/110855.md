linum:/ˈlaɪnəm/| n. |亚麻；亚麻属植物|A genus of about 200 species of herbs and subshrubs, mostly with narrow leaves and showy blue, yellow, white, or red flowers.  It is cultivated for its seeds (flaxseed) and for the fibers of its stem (flax).|Flaxseed oil comes from the linum plant. 亚麻籽油来自亚麻属植物。|近义词：flax|反义词: N/A|noun
