compassionates:/kəmˈpæʃənɪts/| adj.|富有同情心的，表示怜悯的|Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others.|She compassionates the homeless and often volunteers at a soup kitchen. 她同情无家可归的人，经常在施粥处做义工。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring|反义词：cruel, heartless, indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French compassionat, from late Latin compassionatus ‘moved with pity’, past participle of compati ‘suffer with’.
