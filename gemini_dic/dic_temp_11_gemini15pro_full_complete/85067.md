hard-fought:/ˌhɑːrdˈfɔːt/ | adj. | 艰苦奋斗的，奋力争取的 | Achieved or won after a lot of effort and struggle. | The team earned a hard-fought victory in the championship game.  这支队伍在冠军赛中取得了来之不易的胜利。 |

Example:
After a hard-fought battle, they finally secured the contract.  经过一番艰苦的斗争，他们最终拿下了合同。


Synonyms:  strenuously contested, closely fought, bitterly contested, fiercely contested, hardly won, arduously achieved
Antonyms:  easily won, effortlessly achieved, walkover

Etymology:
Compound of "hard" (Old English heard "firm, solid, severe") + past participle of "fight" (Old English feohtan "to contend in battle or single combat").
