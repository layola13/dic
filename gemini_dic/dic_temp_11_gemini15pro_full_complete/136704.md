nonsegregative:/ˌnɒnˈsɛɡrɪɡətɪv/ | adj. |非隔离的，不分离的 | Not segregating or separating; tending to remain integrated or mixed. |  This paint has nonsegregative properties, ensuring a uniform color throughout.  这种油漆具有非隔离特性，确保颜色始终均匀。

| Example 2: Nonsegregative social policies promote inclusion and diversity. 非隔离的社会政策促进包容性和多样性。 |  Near synonyms: integrated, unified, homogenous, unseparated | Antonyms: segregative, separated, divided, partitioned | Adjective
