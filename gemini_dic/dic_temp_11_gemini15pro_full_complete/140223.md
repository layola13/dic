officiates:/əˈfɪʃieɪts/
verb
主持；担任裁判；行使职责
acts as an official in a ceremony or other public event, especially a religious one; performs the duties of an official
The bishop officiated at the wedding. 主教主持了婚礼。
preside over, conduct, administer, direct, manage, supervise, control
