busier:/ˈbɪziər/ (US) /ˈbɪziə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更忙的|Comparative form of "busy," meaning engaged in activity; occupied.|She's busier now with two kids. 她现在有两个孩子，更忙了。|He's much busier than he used to be. 他比以前忙多了。|近义词：more occupied, more engaged|反义词：less busy, freer|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *bisiere, compartive of *bisi (“busy”), from Old English *bysig (“busy, anxious, diligent, occupied”).
