allay:/əˈleɪ/
verb
减轻，缓和
to diminish or put at rest (fear, suspicion, or worry).
The government is trying to allay public fears about the spread of the virus. 政府正在努力减轻公众对病毒传播的恐惧。
calm, ease, soothe, alleviate, mitigate
aggravate, exacerbate, intensify
词性:动词

allay:/əˈleɪ/
verb
使平静，使安静
to subdue or reduce in intensity or severity : ALLEVIATE.
allay the pain/suffering 减轻疼痛/痛苦
calm, ease, soothe, alleviate, mitigate
aggravate, exacerbate, intensify
词性:动词
