outplotted:/ˌaʊtˈplɒtɪd/
verb
智胜，以计谋胜过
defeated someone by planning or scheming more cleverly than they did
She outplotted her rivals and won the contract. 她智胜了竞争对手，赢得了合同。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted
词性: 动词
