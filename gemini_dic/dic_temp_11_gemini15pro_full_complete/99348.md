interdenominational:/ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənl/ /ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənl/
adjective
跨教派的；不限于某一教派的
Involving or relating to different Christian denominations.
The interdenominational service brought together people of various faiths.  跨教派的礼拜仪式将不同信仰的人们聚集在一起。
The interdenominational choir included singers from several churches.  这个跨教派的唱诗班包括来自几个教堂的歌手。
近义词: ecumenical, nonsectarian, interfaith
反义词: denominational, sectarian
词性: adjective

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
denominational (adjective): relating to a particular Christian denomination (from denomination, ultimately from Latin denominare, "to name")
