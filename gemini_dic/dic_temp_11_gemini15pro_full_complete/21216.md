blocklike:/ˈblɑːkˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像块状的，像积木的 | Resembling a block in shape or structure; solid and heavy-looking. | The building had a blocklike appearance, with its stark, rectangular shape. 这栋建筑外观像块状的，有着鲜明的矩形形状。 | The sculpture was a blocklike mass of granite. 这座雕塑是一块块状的花岗岩。 | 近义词: blocky, chunky, solid | 反义词: slender, delicate, intricate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  block + -like.  "Block" comes from Middle Dutch "blok" meaning a piece of wood. "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
