nationalistically:/ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. |民族主义地; | In a way that shows strong support for the interests of one's own nation, sometimes to the exclusion or detriment of the interests of other nations. | He spoke nationalistically about the country's glorious past. 他以民族主义的口吻谈论国家光荣的过去。 | patriotically, chauvinistically |  internationally, globally | adverb
