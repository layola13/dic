cablelaid:/ˈkeɪbəlˌleɪd/
adjective
绞合的，绳状的
made of strands twisted together like a cable; ropelike.
The cablelaid rope was strong and durable.  绞合的绳子结实耐用。

The bridge was supported by thick, cablelaid steel wires.  这座桥由粗大的绞合钢丝支撑。
近义词:twisted, stranded, ropelike, cordlike
反义词:straight, unbraided, untwisted
词性:adjective
