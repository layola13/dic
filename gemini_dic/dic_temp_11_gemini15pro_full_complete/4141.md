air-mails: /ˈeərˌmeɪlz/ (US), /ˈeəˌmeɪlz/ (UK)
| n. | 航空邮件 | Mail that is transported by airplane. | I sent the package by air-mail to ensure it arrived quickly. 我通过航空邮件寄送包裹，以确保它能快速到达。| Example: She received several air-mails from her pen pal overseas. 她收到了几封来自海外笔友的航空邮件。 | Synonym: airmail,  | Antonym: surface mail | noun
