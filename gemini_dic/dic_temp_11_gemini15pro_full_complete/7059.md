anemometric:/ˌænɪməˈmetrɪk/
adjective
风速测量的，风力计的
relating to or measured by an anemometer (an instrument for measuring the speed of the wind).
The anemometric data showed a significant increase in wind speed during the storm.  风速测量数据显示风暴期间风速显著增加。
The researchers used an anemometric device to record wind velocity. 研究人员使用风速测量装置记录风速。
Synonyms: wind-related, wind-measuring
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
