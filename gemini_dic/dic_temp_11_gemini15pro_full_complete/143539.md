outgush:/ˈaʊtɡʌʃ/| n. vi.|涌出，喷出|A sudden, copious outflow, especially of a fluid.|例句：There was an outgush of lava from the volcano. 火山喷出了熔岩。|近义词：gush, eruption, outburst, overflow|反义词:trickle, seep|词性:名词, 动词
