demographic:/ˌdɛm·əˈɡræf·ɪk/ /ˌdiːməˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. n.|人口统计学的；人口统计数据；特定人群|relating to the study of changes in the characteristics of a population such as size, distribution and vital statistics.|The demographic trend is towards an older population. 人口趋势是人口老龄化。|Synonym: population, statistical |Antonym: - |adjective

demographic:/ˌdɛm·əˈɡræf·ɪk/ /ˌdiːməˈɡræfɪk/ | n.|人口统计数据；人口统计资料；特定人群|a particular sector of a population|The products are designed to appeal to a young demographic. 这些产品旨在吸引年轻人群。|Synonym: group, sector, segment |Antonym: - |noun
