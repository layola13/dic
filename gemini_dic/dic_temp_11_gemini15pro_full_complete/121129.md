misdeem:/ˌmɪsˈdiːm/
verb
误判，错认
To judge or consider wrongly; to have an erroneous opinion of.
He misdeems their silence as consent. 他误认为他们的沉默是同意。
misjudge, misinterpret
judge correctly, interpret correctly
