internalize:/ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪz/ |/ɪnˈtɝːnəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 内化；使内在化；吸收 | To make (attitudes or behavior) part of one's nature by learning or unconscious assimilation. 将（态度或行为）通过学习或无意识的同化成为自身性格的一部分。|  He had not yet internalized the values of the company. 他还没有将公司的价值观内化。 | internalise (British), incorporate, absorb, assimilate, integrate | externalize | verb
