eugenol:/ˈjuːdʒɪnɒl/ (UK) /ˈjuːdʒɪnɔːl/ (US)| n. |丁香酚；丁香油酚|A pale yellow aromatic liquid extracted from clove oil and other essential oils, used as a local antiseptic and analgesic, and in perfumes and flavorings.|Eugenol is the main component of clove oil. 丁香酚是丁香油的主要成分。| The dentist used eugenol to soothe the patient's toothache. 牙医用丁香酚来缓解病人的牙痛。|Synonyms:  caryl acetate, allylguaiacol |Antonyms: None|noun
