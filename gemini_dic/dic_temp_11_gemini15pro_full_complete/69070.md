faultlessly:/ˈfɔːltləsli/| adv.|完美地，无瑕疵地|Without any faults; perfectly.|She played the piece faultlessly. 她完美地演奏了这首曲子。|Synonyms: perfectly, impeccably, flawlessly, immaculately|Antonyms: imperfectly, flawedly, defectively|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From faultless + -ly.  Faultless from fault + -less.  Fault from Old French faute "lack, want, default, failure, offense" from Vulgar Latin *fallita, from Latin fallere "to deceive, fail, disappoint, trick, escape the notice of."
