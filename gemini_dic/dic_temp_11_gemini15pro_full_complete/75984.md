funner:/ˈfʌnər/ | adj. | 更有趣的 | A nonstandard comparative form of fun, considered incorrect by many.  While becoming more common in informal speech, it is generally advisable to use "more fun" in formal writing or speech. | That ride was funner than the last one. 那个游乐设施比上一个更有趣。| The party last night was way funner than I expected. 昨晚的派对比我预想的要有趣得多。| 近义词：more enjoyable, more entertaining | 反义词：less fun, more boring | 词性：形容词

词源:  A comparative form of *fun*, from the noun *fun*. While *fun* has been used as an adjective since the 18th century, the comparative *funner* and superlative *funnest* are relatively recent formations and are still considered nonstandard by many.
