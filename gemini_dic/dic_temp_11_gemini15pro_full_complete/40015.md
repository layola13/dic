consumable:/kənˈsuːməbl/ | adj. n. | 可消耗的；消耗品 | Something that can be consumed; a commodity designed to be used up relatively quickly. |  Office supplies like paper and pens are typical consumables. 办公室用品，例如纸和笔，是典型的消耗品。| synonyms: expendable, disposable, nondurable | antonyms: durable, permanent, lasting | adjective

consumable:/kənˈsuːməbl/ | n. | 消耗品 | A commodity designed to be used up relatively quickly. |  The factory produces a range of consumables, from printer ink to cleaning fluids. 这家工厂生产一系列消耗品，从打印机墨水到清洁液。| synonyms: supply, good, product | antonyms: capital good, durable good | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *consumabille*, from Latin *consumabilis*, from *consumō* (“I consume, use up”).  The noun form emerged later, derived from the adjective.
