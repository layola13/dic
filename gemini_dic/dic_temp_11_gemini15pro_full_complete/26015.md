busbies: /ˈbʌzbiz/
noun
熊皮帽 (复数)
Tall, furry hats worn by certain regiments in the British army.
The guardsmen wore their busbies with pride.  卫兵们自豪地戴着熊皮帽。
The busbies made the soldiers look taller and more imposing.  熊皮帽使士兵们看起来更高大威武。
Synonyms: bearskins, shakos
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of hat)
