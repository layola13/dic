bromoiodized:/ˌbroʊmoʊˈaɪəˌdaɪzd/
adjective
溴碘化的
Containing both bromine and iodine.
Bromoiodized vegetable oil was used as a density adjusting agent. 溴碘化植物油被用作密度调节剂。

近义词: halogenated
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "unbrominated" and "uniodized" separately)
词性: adjective
