grecianized:/ˌɡriːʃənaɪzd/
adjective
希腊化的
Made Greek in character or style; Hellenized.
The architecture of this building is distinctly grecianized, with its elegant columns and intricate friezes. 这座建筑的建筑风格明显是希腊化的，有着优雅的柱子和复杂的雕饰。
近义词:Hellenized, Atticized
反义词:Romanized, Latinized
词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek + -ize.  The suffix "-ize"  means "to make or become like".  Therefore, "Grecianized" literally means "made to resemble or become Greek."
