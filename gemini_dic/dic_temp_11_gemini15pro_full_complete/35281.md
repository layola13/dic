clutchingly:/ˈklʌtʃɪŋli/
adverb|紧紧地，抓紧地|In a way that involves holding tightly onto something, often out of fear or anxiety.|She held onto his arm clutchingly as they crossed the busy street. 她紧紧地抓住他的胳膊，穿过繁忙的街道。|tightly, firmly, desperately|loosely, gently|词性: 副词

clutchingly:/ˈklʌtʃɪŋli/
Etymology: From "clutching" + "-ly". "Clutching" is the present participle of the verb "clutch," which comes from the Middle English "clucchen," meaning "to clench."|词源：来自 "clutching" + "-ly"。"Clutching" 是动词 "clutch" 的现在分词，它来自中古英语 "clucchen"，意思是 "握紧"。
