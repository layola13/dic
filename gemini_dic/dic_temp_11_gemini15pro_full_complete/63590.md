envisioned:/ɪnˈvɪʒənd/ | v. | 设想，展望 |  To have formed a mental picture of something, especially something that you hope will exist or happen in the future. |  She envisioned a future where everyone had access to clean water. 她设想了一个每个人都能获得清洁水的未来。| imagine, foresee, contemplate, visualize | disregard, ignore | verb

envisioned:/ɪnˈvɪʒənd/ | adj. (rarely used) | 设想的，展望的 |  Existing or happening only in someone's mind or imagination. | The envisioned project was ambitious and groundbreaking.  这个设想的项目雄心勃勃，具有开创性。 | imagined, conceived, projected |  real, actual | adjective
