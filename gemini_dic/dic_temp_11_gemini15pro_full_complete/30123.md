cedilla:/səˈdɪlə/| n.| 分音符|A diacritical mark (¸) placed beneath the letter "c" (ç) to indicate that it is pronounced as /s/ before the vowels "a," "o," or "u."  Without the cedilla, "c" before these vowels would typically be pronounced as /k/.|The word "façade" uses a cedilla to indicate the soft "c" sound.|近义词:  (none - it's a unique diacritical mark)|反义词: (none)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cédille*, a diminutive of *zède* (obsolete name of the letter Z), from Spanish *ceda* or *zeda* "Z", from Latin *zēta*, from Greek *zēta*.
