intermaze:/ˌɪntərˈmeɪz/ | v. | 使…如迷宫般错综复杂；使…困惑 | To make intricate or confusing like a maze. | The dense jungle intermazed the explorers, making it difficult to find their way out. 茂密的丛林使探险家们迷失了方向，难以找到出路。|  The twisting corridors intermazed the visitors, leaving them disoriented. 蜿蜒的走廊使游客们迷失了方向。| 近义词: confuse, bewilder, perplex | 反义词: clarify, simplify | 词性: 动词

词源:  From inter- (between) + maze (迷宫).  The prefix "inter-" suggests the action of becoming entangled or intertwined like the paths within a maze.
