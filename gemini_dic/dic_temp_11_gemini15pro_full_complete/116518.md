mateys:/ˈmeɪtiz/| n.|伙计们，朋友们（主要用于海盗或航海语境）|A term of address used by sailors, especially pirates, to refer to their companions.  It's an informal and often jovial way of saying "friends" or "companions," specifically within a nautical context. |"Ahoy, mateys! Let's set sail for treasure!" 喂，伙计们！我们起航寻宝吧！|"Shiver me timbers, mateys, there's a storm brewin'!" 我的老天，伙计们，暴风雨要来了！|近义词：friends, companions, buddies, pals|反义词:enemies, foes|名词
