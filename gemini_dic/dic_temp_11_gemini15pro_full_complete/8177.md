anti-degradation:/ˌæntiˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| n. |反退化，防止恶化|The prevention of a decline in environmental quality; maintaining or improving existing environmental conditions.|The Clean Water Act includes anti-degradation policies to protect existing high-quality waters.| 近义词: preservation, conservation, protection| 反义词: degradation, deterioration| noun

anti-degradation:/ˌæntiˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| adj. |反退化的，防止恶化的|Relating to policies or measures designed to prevent a decline in environmental quality.|Anti-degradation provisions are crucial for maintaining the ecological integrity of national parks. |近义词: preservative, conservative, protective| 反义词: degradative, deteriorative| adjective
