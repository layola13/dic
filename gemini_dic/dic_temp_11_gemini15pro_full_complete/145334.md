overbrutalize:/ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəˌlaɪz/| vt.|过度残暴对待；过分粗暴地对待|To treat with excessive brutality; to brutalize beyond what is necessary or justifiable.|The dictator's regime overbrutalized the protesting citizens, resorting to torture and unnecessary violence.|近义词：maltreat, abuse, mistreat, manhandle|反义词:coddle, protect, cherish|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "brutalize" (from "brutal" + "-ize"). "Brutal" comes from the French "brutal," ultimately from Late Latin "brutalis" meaning "of beasts," from Latin "brutus" meaning "dull, senseless, irrational."
