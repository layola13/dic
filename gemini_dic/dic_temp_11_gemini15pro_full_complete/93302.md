ill-concealed:/ˌɪlkənˈsiːld/
adjective
掩饰不住的，不加掩饰的
badly hidden; not effectively disguised.
Her ill-concealed anger was evident in her tone. 她掩饰不住的愤怒在她的语气中显而易见。
Synonyms: barely hidden, thinly veiled, poorly disguised, transparent, obvious, evident, unconcealed
Antonyms: well-concealed, hidden, disguised, masked, secret
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill- + concealed.  "Ill" acts as an adverb modifying "concealed," meaning "badly" or "poorly."  "Concealed" comes from the Old French "conceler" meaning "to hide."
