misreform:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrm/
verb
错误地改革；改革失败
to reform something badly or incorrectly, resulting in a worse situation than before.
The government's attempt to misreform the healthcare system led to increased costs and reduced access for many citizens. 政府试图错误改革医疗保健系统，导致成本增加，许多公民的医疗保健机会减少。
The education misreform resulted in lower test scores and demoralized teachers.  教育改革失败导致考试成绩下降，教师士气低落。

Synonym: bungle, mismanage, mishandle
Antonym: improve, enhance, ameliorate
词性: 动词
