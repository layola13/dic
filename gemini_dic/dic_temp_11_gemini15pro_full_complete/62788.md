engrossed:/ɪnˈɡroʊst/ |ɪnˈɡrɑːst/| adj.|全神贯注的，专注的|Completely occupied or absorbed in something.|She was so engrossed in her book that she didn't hear the phone ring. 她如此专注于她的书，以至于没有听到电话响。|He was completely engrossed in the video game. 他完全沉浸在电子游戏中。|近义词：absorbed, preoccupied, captivated, immersed|反义词：distracted, inattentive, bored|词性:形容词

词源: From the Old French word "engrossir," meaning "to make thick or large," later evolving to mean "to occupy fully."  The original sense related to writing, where being engrossed meant writing in large characters to fill a page.
