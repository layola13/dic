floridity:/flɔːˈrɪdəti/ /flɔrˈɪdəti/
n.
华丽；绚丽；辞藻华丽；花哨
the quality of being florid, especially excessively so.
His writing was known for its floridity. 他的写作以华丽的辞藻而闻名。

The floridity of his prose sometimes obscured his meaning. 他华丽的散文有时掩盖了他的意思。

近义词:ornateness, flamboyance, elaborateness, extravagance
反义词:simplicity, plainness, austerity
词性:noun
