droughted: /ˈdraʊtɪd/ | adj. | 干旱的，受旱灾的 | Affected by drought; parched. |  The droughted land cracked and crumbled.  干旱的土地龟裂破碎。 | The droughted fields yielded a poor harvest. 干旱的田地收成很差。|  arid, parched, dry |  lush, fertile, well-watered | adjective

Etymology: From drought + -ed.  "Drought" comes from Middle English *drought, *drougth, from Old English *drugaþ, meaning "dryness, drought."
