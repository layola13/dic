deliration:/dɪˈlɪəriəm/| n.| 谵妄，神志不清 |A state of being unable to think or speak clearly because of fever or mental confusion.|  He suffered from delirium caused by a high fever. 他因高烧而出现谵妄。|  The patient experienced delirium after the surgery. 病人在手术后经历了谵妄。|近义词：madness, insanity, frenzy |反义词： lucidity, sanity |词性: noun

词源: From Latin dēlīrium "going out of the furrow (in plowing)," figuratively "madness," from dēlīrāre "to deviate from a straight line, be crazy," from de- "from, out of" + līra "furrow, ridge between furrows."
