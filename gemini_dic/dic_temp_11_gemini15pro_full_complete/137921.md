nonvolatility:/ˌnɒnvɒləˈtɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnvɑːləˈtɪləti/
n.|非挥发性；非易失性|The quality of not being easily evaporated or vaporized.  This term is often used in finance to describe assets that are not subject to large price swings.|Bitcoin's nonvolatility is a key factor in its appeal to investors. 比特币的非波动性是其吸引投资者的关键因素。|persistence, stability, constancy|volatility, instability, fluctuation|noun
