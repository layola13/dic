anthophore:/ˈænθəˌfɔːr/ | n. | 花托；花柄 | The part of a flower stalk that bears the petals, sepals, and stamens, situated above the receptacle but below the ovary.  | The anthophore elevates the flower above the foliage. 花托将花朵抬高到叶子之上。| The anthophore of this particular flower is unusually long. 这种花的的花柄异常长。 |近义词: stalk, peduncle, pedicel |反义词: N/A | noun
