dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/ /dɪsˈmɑːntl/ | vt. | 拆除，拆卸 | To take apart a machine or structure so that it is in separate pieces. |  The workers began to dismantle the old bridge. 工人们开始拆除旧桥。

The mechanic dismantled the engine to find the problem.  机械师拆卸了发动机以找出问题所在。 | synonyms: disassemble, take apart, break down, deconstruct | antonyms: assemble, construct, build, put together | verb

dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/ /dɪsˈmɑːntl/ | vt. |  (逐渐地)取消，废除 | To gradually get rid of a system or organization. | The government is dismantling the public health service. 政府正在逐步取消公共卫生服务。

They are dismantling the old regulations. 他们正在废除旧的规章制度。| synonyms: abolish, eliminate, phase out, do away with | antonyms: establish, institute, set up, implement | verb
