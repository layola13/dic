nonolfactories:/ˌnɒnɒlˈfæktəriːz/ | n. | 非嗅觉器官 |  Relating to senses other than smell; organs or structures not involved in the sense of smell.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "olfactory" (relating to the sense of smell). | The fish uses its nonolfactories to detect changes in water pressure.  鱼利用它的非嗅觉器官来探测水压的变化。 | Synonyms: non-smelling organs, other sensory organs | Antonyms: olfactory organs, olfactory system | Noun
