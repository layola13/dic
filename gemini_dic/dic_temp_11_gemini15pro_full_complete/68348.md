fall-upon:/ˌfɔːl əˈpɒn/ (British), /ˌfɔːl əˈpɑːn/ (American)| phrasal verb | 袭击；偶然碰到；突然想到；求助于 | To attack someone violently and suddenly; to discover or meet something unexpectedly; to have an idea suddenly; to resort to something for help | The wolves fell upon the lone traveler.  狼群袭击了那个独自旅行的人。

The archaeologists fell upon an ancient tomb during their excavation. 考古学家在挖掘过程中偶然发现了一座古墓。


The idea to write a novel fell upon me during my vacation.  度假期间，我突然想到要写一部小说。


With no other options left, she fell upon her family for support.  没有别的选择，她只好向家人求助。


近义词：attack, encounter, hit upon, resort to, turn to
反义词: avoid, ignore, overlook


词性: phrasal verb
