clamatorial:/kləˈmætɔːriəl/
adjective
喧闹的；吵嚷的；适合大声喊叫的
Characterized by loud cries or calls, especially of birds or animals.  Relating to or resembling a clamator, a type of bird known for its loud calls.
The clamatorial calls of the monkeys echoed through the jungle. 猴子喧闹的叫声在丛林中回荡。
The bird emitted a series of clamatorial cries. 这只鸟发出了一连串刺耳的叫声。
近义词: noisy, loud, vociferous, clamorous
反义词: quiet, silent, hushed
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin "clamor" (shout, uproar) + "-atorial" (relating to, characterized by).
