neorama:/ˌniːəˈrɑːmə/ |n.|新全景画；新全景电影；新全景放映机|A new form of panoramic entertainment, combining aspects of cinema, theatre, and various special effects, often presented on a large, curved screen.|The neorama provided a breathtakingly immersive experience, transporting the audience to another world.|近义词:panorama, diorama, cyclorama|反义词:None|词性:名词

Etymology:  "Neo-" (new) + "-rama" (view, spectacle). From Greek "neo-" (new) and "-rama" from "horan" (to see).
