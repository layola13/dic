memorialised: /məˈmɔːriəlaɪzd/ /məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪzd/
verb
纪念，缅怀
To preserve the memory of someone or something, especially by creating a monument or writing a biography.
His life was memorialised in a biography published soon after his death.  他的一生被记载在他去世后不久出版的传记中。
The war dead are memorialised by this monument.  这座纪念碑是为了纪念战争中的死难者。
Synonyms: commemorate, remember, celebrate
Antonyms: forget, ignore
词性: 动词
