afk:/ˌeɪ ef ˈkeɪ/ | abbreviation | 暂时离开，离开键盘 | Away From Keyboard. Used in online chat and gaming to indicate that the user is temporarily not at their keyboard. | I'll be afk for a few minutes, brb. 我会暂时离开几分钟，马上回来。 |  I'm afk, grabbing a drink. 我去拿杯饮料，暂时离开一下。 | 近义词: away, unavailable | 反义词: present, available | abbreviation
