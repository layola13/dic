heptarchy:/ˈhɛptɑːrki/| n. |七王国时代; 七个王国组成的政体|A period in English history, traditionally dated from the late 5th to the mid-9th century, when England was divided into seven kingdoms.|The heptarchy was a complex and turbulent period in English history. 七王国时代是英国历史上一个复杂而动荡的时期。| The Anglo-Saxon heptarchy eventually coalesced into a single kingdom.  盎格鲁-撒克逊七王国最终合并成一个单一的王国。 |synonyms:  seven kingdoms |antonyms: monarchy, empire |noun
