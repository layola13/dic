denned:/dend/
v.|居住；藏身；(动物)穴居|The foxes denned in the hillside. 狐狸在山坡上筑巢穴居。|近义词：lived, resided, sheltered, nested|反义词:emerged, left|词性：动词

denned:/dend/
adj.|窝居的；居住的|(rare, usually of animals) having a den; inhabiting a den  （罕见，通常指动物）有窝的；居住在窝里的|The denned badger rarely emerged during daylight.  穴居的獾白天很少出来。|近义词：sheltered, nestled|反义词:exposed|词性：形容词

词源：From "den" + "-ed."  "Den" comes from Old English "denn" meaning "lair, cave."
