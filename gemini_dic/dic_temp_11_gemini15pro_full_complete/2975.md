aegean:/ɪˈdʒiːən/| adj. n.|爱琴海的；爱琴海|Relating to the Aegean Sea, a body of water between Greece and Turkey.  The Aegean civilization flourished in the Bronze Age.|The Aegean Sea is known for its beautiful islands. 爱琴海以其美丽的岛屿而闻名。|The Aegean civilization was a Bronze Age society. 爱琴海文明是一个青铜时代的社会。|近义词：Mediterranean, Greek|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑内陆的 inland) |词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Latin *Aegaeum (mare) "Aegean (sea)", from Greek *Aigaion (πέλαγος) "Aegean (sea)", of uncertain origin.  Possibly from the Greek city of Aegae, or from a mythological queen named Aegaea.
