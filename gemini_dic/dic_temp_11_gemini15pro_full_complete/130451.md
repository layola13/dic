nonabsorbability:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːrbəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 非吸收性；不吸收性 | The nonabsorbability of the material makes it ideal for outdoor use. 这种材料的不吸收性使其非常适合户外使用. |  The coating has high nonabsorbability, preventing water damage.  这种涂层具有很高的不吸收性，可以防止水损害。| 近义词：impermeability, impenetrability | 反义词: absorbability, permeability | 词性: noun

词源: non- (not) + absorb (to take in or soak up) + -ability (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)  来自拉丁语 absorbēre “to suck up,”  非- + 吸收 + -性 = 非吸收性
