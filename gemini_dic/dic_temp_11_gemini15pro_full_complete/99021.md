interagency:/ˌɪntərˈeɪdʒənsi/
adjective
机构间的；跨部门的
Relating to or involving two or more government agencies.
The interagency task force is working to combat drug trafficking.  跨部门工作组正在努力打击毒品贩运。
The report called for greater interagency cooperation.  该报告呼吁加强机构间合作。
Synonyms: collaborative, joint, combined, cooperative
Antonyms: independent, separate, individual
词性: adjective
