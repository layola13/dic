harnessless:/ˈhɑːrnɪslɪs/
adjective
无安全带的；无挽具的
without a harness.  A harness is a set of straps and fastenings by which a horse or other draught animal is attached to a cart, plough, etc. Thus, harnessless means without this equipment.
The rider rode his horse harnessless, a risky endeavor.  骑手不带挽具骑马，这是一项冒险的尝试。
The old wagon, harnessless and abandoned, sat in the field.  那辆旧马车，没有挽具，被遗弃在田野里。
近义词:unharnessed, unrestrained
反义词:harnessed, restrained
词性:adjective
