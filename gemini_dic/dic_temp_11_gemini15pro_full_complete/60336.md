egolessness:/ˈiːɡəʊləsnəs/| n. |无私；忘我|The state of being without ego; lack of egotism or self-importance.  A state of humility and selflessness.|例句：Her egolessness allowed her to truly listen to others. 她的无私让她能够真正倾听他人。

例句：Egolessness is a key trait of effective leaders. 无私是有效领导者的一个关键特征。|近义词：humility, selflessness, modesty, altruism|反义词: egotism, narcissism, self-centeredness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + -less + -ness.  "Ego" comes from the Latin word for "I".  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without". "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
