cochairing:/ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrɪŋ/ | n. v.| 共同主持 | The act of presiding over a meeting or event alongside another person. |  例句：They are cochairing the conference this year. 今年他们共同主持这次会议。| 近义词：jointly chairing, co-presiding | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun, verb (present participle)

cochair:/ˈkoʊtʃɛr/ | n. v.| 共同主席，共同主持 | A person who presides over a meeting or event alongside another person. | 例句：She is the cochair of the fundraising committee. 她是募款委员会的共同主席。 | 近义词：joint chair, co-president | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun, verb

词源: "co-" (together) + "chair" (preside over)
