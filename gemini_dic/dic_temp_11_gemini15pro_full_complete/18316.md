belletristic:/ˌbɛləˈtrɪstɪk/
adjective
文学的；文艺的
Relating to or characteristic of belles-lettres, which is literature regarded as a fine art, especially as having aesthetic value.
Her belletristic style made even mundane topics sound poetic. 她富有文学性的写作风格使得即使是平凡的话题也听起来像诗歌一样。
近义词:literary, artistic, aesthetic, refined
反义词:functional, utilitarian, prosaic, mundane
词性:adjective

belletrism:/ˌbɛləˈtrɪzəm/
noun
纯文学；美文学；文学研究
The genre of belles-lettres; writing or appreciation of literature of high aesthetic quality; an interest in literature.
He devoted his life to belletrism, constantly seeking beauty in the written word. 他一生致力于纯文学，不断在文字中寻找美。
近义词:literature, belles-lettres, literary study
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *belles-lettres* ("fine letters"), from *belle* ("beautiful") + *lettres* ("letters"). Ultimately from Latin *bellae litterae*.
