execratory:/ˈɛkskɹɪtɔːri/ /ˈɛkskrɪˌtɔri/ | adj.| 排泄的；排泄器官的| Relating to or functioning in excretion, the process of eliminating waste products from the body.| The kidneys are important execratory organs. 肾脏是重要的排泄器官。| The skin also plays an execratory role. 皮肤也起着排泄作用。|近义词：eliminative, excretory |反义词: retentive|词性: adjective
etymology: From Latin *excrētōrius*, from excrētus (past participle of excernere ‘to sift out, separate’), from ex- ‘out’ + cernere ‘to sift’.
