outroad:/ˈaʊtroʊd/| n.| 外路，偏僻的道路|A remote or less traveled road. |We took the outroad to avoid the traffic. 我们走了偏僻的道路以避开交通。|例句：The outroad was bumpy and difficult to navigate. 这条外路崎岖不平，难以通行。|近义词：backroad, byroad, side road|反义词：highway, main road|词性：名词

词源:  From 'out' + 'road'.  'Out' meaning away from the center or main area, and 'road' referring to a path for vehicles.
