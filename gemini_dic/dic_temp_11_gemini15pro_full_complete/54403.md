disimprovement:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpruːvmənt/
n.
恶化，退步，变坏
a worsening or decline in quality, performance, or condition.
The disimprovement in his health worried his family. 他的健康状况恶化，使他的家人很担心。

The recent disimprovement in the team's performance has led to speculation about the coach's future.  该队最近的表现下滑引发了人们对其教练未来的猜测。

近义词:decline, deterioration, worsening, regression, decay
反义词:improvement, betterment, enhancement, progress, advancement
