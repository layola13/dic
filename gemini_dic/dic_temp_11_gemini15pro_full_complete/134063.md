nonignitible:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈnaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可燃的，不易燃的 | Not capable of being ignited or easily set on fire. |  This material is nonignitible and safe for use near heat sources. 这种材料不可燃，可在靠近热源的地方安全使用。

The nonignitible fabric is ideal for firefighters' uniforms. 这种不可燃织物是消防员制服的理想选择。

| 近义词: fireproof, flame-resistant, incombustible | 反义词: flammable, inflammable, combustible | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (not) + ignite (to set on fire) + -ible (capable of being)
