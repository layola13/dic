boroughmaster: /ˈbʌrəˌmæstər/ | n. | 市政官，镇长 | A boroughmaster is a chief magistrate or governing official of a borough.  This term is primarily archaic and rarely used in modern English. | The boroughmaster called a town meeting to discuss the new bridge. 市政官召集了镇民大会讨论新桥的问题。| The boroughmaster decreed a new tax on all businesses. 市政官颁布了对所有企业征收新税的法令。 |Synonyms: mayor, magistrate, councilman, alderman | Antonyms: citizen, resident | Noun
