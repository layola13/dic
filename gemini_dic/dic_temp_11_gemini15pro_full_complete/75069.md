frights:/fraɪts/
n.
惊吓，恐惧
Sudden, intense fear.
The movie was full of frights and jump scares. 这部电影充满了惊吓和突如其来的恐怖场景。
近义词: scares, terrors, alarms
反义词: calmness, tranquility
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From Middle English *frightes, plural of *fright, from Old English *fryhto (attested in oblique cases only), from Proto-Germanic *frihtō, abstract noun from *frihtaną (“to frighten”). Cognate with West Frisian frychte (“fear”), Dutch vrees (“fear”), German Furcht (“fear”), Swedish fruktan (“fear”). More at frighten.
