nullah:/ˈnʌlə/| n.|沟壑，旱谷|A ravine or gully, especially a dry riverbed or watercourse in India or Pakistan.|The villagers cross the nullah every day to reach the fields. 村民们每天都要穿过旱谷才能到达田地。|近义词：ravine, gully, wadi, arroyo|反义词:ridge, hill|noun

nullah:/ˈnʌlə/| n.|沟壑，旱谷|A watercourse, especially a small stream or brook.|The nullah was dry except for a few stagnant pools.  除了几个死水潭外，这条小溪已经干涸了。|近义词：stream, brook, creek|反义词:mountain, peak|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi and Urdu  _nallā_  meaning "ravine," "watercourse." Ultimately from Sanskrit _nāla_  meaning "reed," "hollow stalk," hence "channel," "watercourse."
