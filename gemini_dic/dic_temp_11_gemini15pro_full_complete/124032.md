moroxite:/ˈmɒrɒksaɪt/| n. |绿松石|A bluish-green variety of apatite, often used as a gemstone.  It's named after Moroxite, a locality in Arendal, Norway, where it was first discovered.|The moroxite pendant shimmered in the sunlight. 绿松石吊坠在阳光下闪闪发光。|The museum displayed a collection of rare moroxites.  博物馆展示了一系列稀有的绿松石。|Synonyms: apatite, turquoise|Antonyms: N/A|noun
