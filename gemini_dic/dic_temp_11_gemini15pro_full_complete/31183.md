changelessly:/ˈtʃeɪndʒləsli/
adverb
不变地，永恒地
in a way that does not change
The stars shone changelessly in the night sky.  星星在夜空中永恒地闪耀。
The river flowed changelessly towards the sea.  河流恒定地流向大海。
近义词:invariably, constantly, perpetually, eternally
反义词:variably, erratically, inconsistently
词性:adverb

词源:
From "changeless" + "-ly".  "Changeless" comes from "change" (from Old French "changier", ultimately from Latin "cambiare" - to barter, exchange) + "-less" (Old English "-lēas" - without).
