cryonicist:/ˌkraɪɒˈnɪsɪst/ | n. |人体冷冻学家，低温生物学家| A person who advocates or practices cryonics, the freezing of the bodies or heads of the recently deceased in the hope that future scientific advances will allow them to be revived. | He's a cryonicist who believes that freezing bodies is the key to immortality. 他是一位人体冷冻学家，相信冷冻尸体是获得永生的关键。|  The cryonicist explained the process of vitrification to the potential client.  这位低温生物学家向潜在客户解释了玻璃化冷冻的过程。 | 近义词: cryobiologist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
