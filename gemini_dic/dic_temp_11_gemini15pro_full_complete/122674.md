modulative:/ˈmɒdjʊlətɪv/ | adj. | 调制的，调节的 | Relating to modulation; having the power to modulate. | The modulative effects of hormones on behavior are complex. 激素对行为的调节作用很复杂。 | The singer's modulative voice captivated the audience.  这位歌手抑扬顿挫的嗓音吸引了观众。 | 近义词: modulating, adjusting, regulating | 反义词: static, unchanging | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *modulativus, from Latin modulatus, past participle of modulari "to regulate, measure rhythmically," from modulus "measure."
