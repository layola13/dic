disenter:/dɪˈsɛntər/ | v. |  退出；脱离；放弃 | To formally leave or withdraw from an organization, association, or agreement. |  He disentered from the competition due to injury.  他因伤退出了比赛。| synonyms: withdraw, secede, resign, leave, quit | antonyms: join, enter, enroll | verb

disenter:/dɪˈsɛntər/ | n. |  退出者；脱离者 |  A person who formally leaves or withdraws from an organization, association, or agreement. | The dissenters formed their own political party.  退出者组成了自己的政党。| synonyms: separatist, dissident, nonconformist, renegade | antonyms: conformist, adherent, loyalist | noun
