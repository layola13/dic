multisyllable:/ˌmʌltiˈsɪləbl/ | adj. | 多音节的 |  Having more than one syllable.  A word like "cat" is one syllable, while a word like "computer" is multisyllable. |  The word "unconstitutional" is quite multisyllable and difficult to pronounce.  这个单词“unconstitutional”是多音节词，很难发音。 | polysyllabic | monosyllabic | adjective
