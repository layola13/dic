hadj/hædʒ/| n.|朝圣，麦加朝圣|A pilgrimage to Mecca, especially one performed by a Muslim.|例句：He made his hadj to Mecca last year. 他去年去了麦加朝圣。

例句：Every Muslim is expected to make the hadj at least once in their lifetime.  每个穆斯林一生中至少要进行一次朝圣。|近义词：pilgrimage|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
