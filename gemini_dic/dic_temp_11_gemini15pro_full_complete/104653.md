karen:/ˈkɛrən/ (US), /ˈkærən/ (UK)| n. |凯伦（人名），贬义指蛮横无理的白人中年女性|A pejorative slang term for a white woman perceived as entitled or demanding beyond the scope of what is considered appropriate or necessary.|例句：The karen demanded to speak to the manager.  那个凯伦要求和经理说话。

例句：Don't be such a karen! 别那么蛮横无理！|近义词：entitled, demanding, shrew|反义词: accommodating, understanding| Noun
