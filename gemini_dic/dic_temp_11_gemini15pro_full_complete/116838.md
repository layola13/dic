maximalist:/ˈmæksɪməlɪst/
n.|极繁主义者；最大化主义者|a person who holds extreme views and advocates maximal demands or measures.|The maximalist demanded complete and immediate reform.|近义词：extremist, radical|反义词:minimalist, moderate|词性:名词

maximalist:/ˈmæksɪməlɪst/
adj.|极繁主义的；最大化主义的|favoring maximal demands or measures.|Her maximalist approach to interior design resulted in a room overflowing with patterns and textures.|近义词：extreme, radical|反义词:minimalist, moderate|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From maximal + -ist. Maximal from Latin maximus (greatest) + -al.
