iou:/ˈaɪ oʊ ˈjuː/ | n. | 欠条，借据 | A written acknowledgment of debt; a promise to pay. | Example 1: He gave me an IOU for $50. 他给了我一张50美元的欠条。

Example 2:  She accepted an IOU instead of cash. 她收下了一张欠条而不是现金。| Synonym:  promissory note, note of hand | Antonym: receipt, payment | Noun
