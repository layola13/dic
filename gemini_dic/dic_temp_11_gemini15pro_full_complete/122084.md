misthrows:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊz/
noun
投掷失误，投掷错误
An act of throwing something incorrectly or inaccurately.
The quarterback's misthrows cost them the game. 四分卫的投掷失误让他们输掉了比赛。
Synonyms: bad throws, errant throws, inaccurate throws
Antonyms: accurate throws, good throws, perfect throws
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + throw (verb meaning 'to propel something through the air').
