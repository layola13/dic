besought:/bɪˈsɔːt/
verb
恳求；乞求
Asked earnestly or begged.
The knight besought the king for mercy. 骑士恳求国王开恩。
近义词：implored, pleaded, begged
反义词：demanded, commanded
词性：v.

词源：From Middle English *bisechen*, from Old English *besēcan* (“to beseech, implore, beg, ask, seek after, visit”), from *be-* + *sēcan* (“to seek”). Cognate with Dutch *bezoeken* (“to visit”), German *besuchen* (“to visit”).
