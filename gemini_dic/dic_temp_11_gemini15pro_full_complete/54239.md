digest:/daɪˈdʒɛst/ /dɪˈdʒɛst/| v. n.|消化，领悟；摘要|v. To break down food in the stomach and intestines.  To absorb information or ideas. n. A summary or condensation of a longer work.|例句：The stomach digests food. 胃消化食物。 I need time to digest the information. 我需要时间来消化这些信息。|近义词：absorb, comprehend, summarize, compendium|反义词:reject, excrete|verb

digest:/ˈdaɪdʒɛst/| n.|摘要，文摘|A condensed version of a longer text.|例句：The Reader's Digest is a popular magazine. 《读者文摘》是一本很受欢迎的杂志.  She read a digest of the news. 她读了一份新闻摘要。|近义词：summary, abstract, synopsis|反义词:expansion, elaboration|noun
