instinctive:/ɪnˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.|本能的，天生的，直觉的|Arising from instinct; done automatically without thinking.|例句：Cats have an instinctive fear of water. 猫天生就怕水。

例句：Her instinctive reaction was to run. 她本能的反应就是跑。|近义词：innate, inherent, natural, automatic, intuitive, spontaneous|反义词：learned, acquired, conscious, deliberate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *instinctīvus, from Latin instinctus (“impulse, instigation”), past participle of instingō (“to incite, instigate”), from in- + stingō (“to prick, goad”).
