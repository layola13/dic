gavialoid:/ˈɡeɪviəlɔɪd/| adj. n.|  Gavialoids,  似长吻鳄的, 长吻鳄类|Resembling or related to the gavial, a crocodilian with a long, slender snout.  像或与长吻鳄有关，长吻鳄是一种有着长而细的鼻子的鳄鱼。|The gavialoid skull is easily distinguished by its elongated snout. 长吻鳄的头骨很容易通过其细长的鼻子来区分。|The false gharial is a gavialoid crocodilian found in Southeast Asia.  马来长吻鳄是一种发现于东南亚的长吻鳄类鳄鱼。|近义词：crocodilian, long-snouted|反义词: (无明确反义词，可以考虑short-snouted 短吻的)|词性:  adjective, noun
