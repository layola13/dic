cilene: /saɪˈliːn/ | n. |香茅醛；香茅醇| An acyclic monoterpenoid, and an aldehyde.  It is a colorless liquid with a fresh, lemon-like scent.  It occurs naturally in citronella oil and is used in perfumery and flavoring. | Cilene is a common ingredient in insect repellents. 香茅醛是驱虫剂的常见成分。|  Citronellal is often used in soaps and perfumes for its pleasant scent. 香茅醛因其宜人的香味常用于肥皂和香水。| synonyms: citronellal, lemonal | antonyms: N/A | noun
