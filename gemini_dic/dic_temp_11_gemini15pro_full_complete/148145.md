oversensitize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 过度敏感化，使过敏 | To make someone or something excessively sensitive or easily offended. | The media coverage has oversensitized the public to this issue. 媒体报道使公众对这个问题过度敏感。| The child was oversensitized to certain foods after the illness.  这个孩子病后对某些食物过敏。 |  sensitize, heighten sensitivity | desensitize, numb | verb

oversensitive: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过度敏感的，神经质的 | Excessively sensitive or easily offended. | She's oversensitive to criticism. 她对批评过度敏感。| He's oversensitive about his weight. 他对自己的体重很敏感。 | sensitive, touchy | insensitive, thick-skinned | adjective

词源:

over- (prefix): excessive, too much
sensitive (adjective): readily or excessively affected by external agencies or influences
-ize (suffix): to make or become; to cause to be or conform to or resemble
