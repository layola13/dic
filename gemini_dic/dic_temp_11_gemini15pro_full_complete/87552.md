heteromorphy:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |异形性，异态性 |  The condition of having different forms at different stages of development or in different parts of an organism. |  The butterfly exhibits heteromorphy, going from a caterpillar to a winged adult. 蝴蝶展现出异形性，从毛毛虫变成有翅膀的成虫。|  polymorphism, heteromorphism |  homomorphy, isomorphy | noun

heteromorphy:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |异质同晶 | The property of having different crystal structures despite having the same chemical composition. |  Calcium carbonate exhibits heteromorphy, existing as both calcite and aragonite. 碳酸钙表现出异质同晶，同时以方解石和文石的形式存在。| polymorphism | isomorphism | noun
