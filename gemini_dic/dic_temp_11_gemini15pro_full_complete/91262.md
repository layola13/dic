hydrofracking:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfrækɪŋ/ | n. | 水力压裂 | The process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to force open existing fissures and extract oil or gas. | Hydraulic fracturing, or fracking, is a controversial method of extracting natural gas. 水力压裂，或称压裂，是一种有争议的天然气开采方法。|  Hydrofracking can release trapped natural gas. 水力压裂可以释放被困的天然气。 | synonyms: fracking, hydraulic fracturing | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional drilling") | noun


hydrofrackings: /ˌhaɪdrəˈfrækɪŋz/ | n. | 水力压裂（复数）| Multiple instances of the process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks to extract oil or gas. |  The hydrofrackings in this area have caused concern among residents.  这个地区的水力压裂活动引起了居民的担忧。| The numerous hydrofrackings have significantly increased gas production. 大量的水力压裂显著提高了天然气产量。| synonyms: frackings, hydraulic fracturings | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
