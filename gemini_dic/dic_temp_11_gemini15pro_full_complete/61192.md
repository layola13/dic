elute:/iˈluːt/ | n. | 洗脱物；洗出液 | The substance removed by elution. | The elute was then analyzed by mass spectrometry. 洗脱物随后用质谱法分析。| synonyms: extract, eluate | antonyms: adsorbate | noun
