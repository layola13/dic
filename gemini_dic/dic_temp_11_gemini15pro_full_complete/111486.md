loadstar:/ˈloʊdstɑːr/ |n.|指路明灯；指导原则；北极星|Something that serves as a guide or inspiration.|例句：The North Star has always been a loadstar for navigators. 北极星一直是航海家的指路明灯。

例句：Her unwavering faith served as a loadstar during difficult times.  她坚定的信念在困难时期成为她的指路明灯。|近义词：guiding light, polestar, beacon, lodestar|反义词：misguidance, distraction|词性：名词

词源：From Middle English lode sterre, from Old English lād-stēorra (“leading star, North Star”), equivalent to lād (from *lǣdan (“to lead”)) + stēorra (“star”). Cognate with Old High German leit-sterro (“guiding star”). Related to lead. Doublet of lodestar.
