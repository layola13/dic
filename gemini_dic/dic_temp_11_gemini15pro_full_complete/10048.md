aplotomy:/æˈplɒtəmi/
n.
单倍体化
The process by which the chromosome number of a cell is reduced by half.
Aplotomy is an important process in the life cycle of many organisms. 单倍体化是许多生物生命周期中的一个重要过程。
The aplotomy of the diploid cell resulted in haploid gametes.  二倍体细胞的单倍体化产生了单倍体配子。
近义词: haploidization, meiosis
反义词: polyploidization
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἁπλόος (haplóos, “single”) + -τομία (-tomía, “cutting”).
