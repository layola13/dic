chuck:/tʃʌk/| v. |抛；扔；丢弃；解雇；放弃；轻拍；停止；呕吐|To throw or toss something casually. To discard or get rid of something. To dismiss or fire someone from a job. To give up or abandon something. To give someone a light pat or tap. To stop or quit something. To vomit or be sick.|He chucked the ball to his dog. 他把球扔给了他的狗。|近义词：toss, throw, discard, fire, dismiss, vomit|反义词：hire, keep, retain|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chukken*, perhaps of imitative origin. Related to chock.
