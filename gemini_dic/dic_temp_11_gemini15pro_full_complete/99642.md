interlard:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːrd/ | /ˌɪntərˈlɑrd/ | vt. |  插入；点缀；（尤指）在…中穿插（另一事物） | To insert or introduce something between other things, especially between parts of a conversation or piece of writing. | He interlarded his speech with anecdotes. 他在演讲中穿插着一些奇闻轶事。 | synonyms: intersperse, interweave, mix, blend, insert, introduce | antonyms: separate, extract, remove | verb

interlard:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːrd/ | /ˌɪntərˈlɑrd/ | n. | （穿插在其他事物中的）点缀物；穿插物 | Something inserted or introduced between other things. | The anecdotes were an amusing interlard to the serious lecture. 这些奇闻轶事是对严肃讲座的有趣的点缀。 | synonyms: insertion, addition, admixture | antonyms: removal, extraction | noun
