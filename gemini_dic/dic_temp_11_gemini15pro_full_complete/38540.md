concluded:/kənˈkluːdɪd/
verb
结束；推断出；决定
brought to an end; decided; inferred
The meeting concluded at 3 p.m. 会议在下午3点结束。
The jury concluded that he was guilty. 陪审团认定他有罪。

conclude:/kənˈkluːd/
verb
结束；推断出；决定
bring to an end; decide; infer
We concluded the meeting with a vote of thanks. 我们以表决感谢结束了会议。
From the evidence, we can conclude that he is guilty. 从证据来看，我们可以断定他有罪。

Synonyms: finish, end, terminate, deduce, infer
Antonyms: begin, start, commence, initiate
