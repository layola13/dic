ligand:/ˈlɪɡænd/, /ˈlaɪɡænd/ | n. | 配体 | An ion or molecule that binds to a central metal atom to form a coordination complex. A ligand donates a pair of electrons to the central metal atom. |  The cyanide ion is a strong ligand. 氰离子是一种强配体。| synonyms: complexing agent, chelating agent, coordinating group | antonyms: N/A | noun

ligand:/ˈlɪɡænd/, /ˈlaɪɡænd/ | n. | 配位体 |  A molecule that binds specifically to a receptor site of another molecule. | In biochemistry, a ligand might refer to a hormone binding to a receptor. 在生物化学中，配体可能指与受体结合的激素。| synonyms: binding molecule, effector molecule | antonyms: N/A | noun
