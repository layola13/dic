clownfish: /ˈklaʊnˌfɪʃ/ (US), /ˈklaʊnˌfɪʃ/ (UK)| n. |小丑鱼，海葵鱼|A brightly colored fish that lives among sea anemones, with which it has a symbiotic relationship.  The anemone's stinging tentacles protect the clownfish from predators, and the clownfish helps clean the anemone and provides it with nutrients.|例句：The clownfish darted among the tentacles of the sea anemone. 小丑鱼在海葵的触须间快速穿梭。

例句：Clownfish are popular additions to saltwater aquariums.  小丑鱼是海水水族馆里受欢迎的 additions。

近义词：anemonefish|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
