homotropal:/ˌhɒməˈtrəʊpəl/| adj.|同向的，顺生的|Directed or turned towards the same side; facing the same way. Specifically (Botany): (of an ovule) curved in such a way that the micropyle is near the funicle.|The homotropal ovules are characteristic of certain plant families.|近义词：anatropous (somewhat), epitropous (somewhat)|反义词：antitropous, heterotropous|词性: adjective
