innovators:/ˈɪnəveɪtərz/| n. |创新者，革新者|A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.|例句：The innovators in the tech industry are constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible. 科技行业的创新者不断突破可能的界限。

例句：Innovators are essential for driving economic growth and societal progress. 创新者对于推动经济增长和社会进步至关重要。|近义词：pioneers, inventors, creators, groundbreakers|反义词：followers, imitators, traditionalists|词性:Noun

Etymology: From the Late Latin word "innovatorem,"  accusative of "innovator,"  from "innovare" meaning "to renew, alter, change,"  from "in-" (into) + "novus" (new).
