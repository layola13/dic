corporal:/ˈkɔːrpərəl/ (US) /ˈkɔːprəl/ (UK)| n. adj.|下士；肉体的，身体的|A non-commissioned officer ranking above a private in the army, marines, or air force.  Relating to the body.|例句：He was promoted to corporal after two years of service.  他服役两年后被提升为下士。|近义词：NCO (non-commissioned officer), physical, bodily|反义词:spiritual, mental|词性: noun, adjective

corporals:/ˈkɔːrpərəlz/ (US) /ˈkɔːprəlz/ (UK)| n.|下士们|Plural form of corporal, referring to multiple non-commissioned officers of that rank.|例句：The corporals instructed the new recruits.  下士们指导新兵。|近义词：NCOs|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plural noun denoting a specific rank)|词性: noun
