covinous:/ˈkʌvɪnəs/| adj.|欺骗的；诡诈的；阴谋的|Characterized by or engaged in plotting; deceitful; treacherous.|例句：The covinous plot to overthrow the government was discovered just in time.  这个推翻政府的阴谋被及时发现。

例句：They formed a covinous alliance to manipulate the stock market. 他们结成一个阴谋联盟来操纵股票市场。|近义词：deceitful, treacherous, conspiratorial, clandestine, insidious|反义词：honest, truthful, forthright, aboveboard|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *covinous*, from Old French *covineus*, from *covine* ("fraud, deceit"), from Latin *convenire* ("to come together, assemble").  The sense evolved from "meeting together" to "meeting together secretly to plot".
