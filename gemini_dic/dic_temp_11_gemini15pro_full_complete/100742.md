intransitively:/ˌɪntrænˈzɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 不及物地 |  The verb "go" can be used intransitively. 动词“去”可以不及物使用。 |  |  | adverb



ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + transitive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *transīre* (to go across).
