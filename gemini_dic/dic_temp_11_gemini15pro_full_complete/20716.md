blahs:/blɑːz/ /blɑːz/
n.
a feeling of boredom and lack of energy; a general feeling of discomfort, unhappiness, or depression
中文翻译：无聊；没精打采；闷闷不乐
英文解析：A mild, temporary, and nonspecific state of malaise or discomfort.
例句：I've got the blahs today and don't feel like doing anything. 我今天感觉没精打采，什么也不想做。

例句：She's had the blahs ever since she lost her job. 自从失业以来，她一直闷闷不乐。

近义词：blues, doldrums, ennui, lethargy, malaise
反义词：energy, enthusiasm, excitement, vigor
词性：名词
