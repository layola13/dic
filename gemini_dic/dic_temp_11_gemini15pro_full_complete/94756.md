improvisatrice:/ɪmˈprɒvɪzəˌtriːs/ /ɪmˈprɑːvɪzəˌtriːs/
noun
即兴女诗人；即兴女作曲家；即兴女表演者
A female performer who improvises, especially in music, poetry, or drama.
She was a renowned improvisatrice, captivating audiences with her spontaneous verses. 她是一位著名的即兴女诗人，用她自发的诗歌吸引着观众。
The improvisatrice wove a tale of love and loss, accompanying herself on the lute.  这位即兴女表演者边弹琵琶边讲述了一个关于爱与失去的故事。
Synonyms:  extemporizer, ad-libber, improviser
Antonyms: planner,  preparer
词性: 名词
