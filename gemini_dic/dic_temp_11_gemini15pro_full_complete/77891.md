geniality:/ˌdʒiːniˈælɪti/ | n. |亲切，和蔼，友好 | The quality of being friendly and cheerful. |  She exuded warmth and geniality. 她散发着温暖和亲切。| Synonyms: affability, amiability, cordiality, warmth | Antonyms: coldness, hostility, unfriendliness | noun

genial:/ˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 和蔼的；亲切的；友好的 | Friendly and cheerful. | He was a genial host. 他是一位和蔼可亲的主人。| Synonyms: friendly, affable, amiable, cordial, warm | Antonyms: unfriendly, cold, hostile, aloof | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin genialis "festive, joyous, pertaining to marriage," also "pleasant, kindly, genial," from genius "guardian deity or spirit which watches over each person from birth; spirit, incarnation; wit, talent," in its extended sense of "inborn nature or character" (see genius).
