ascarid:/ˈæskərɪd/| n. |蛔虫|A parasitic nematode worm of the Ascarididae family, which typically lives in the intestines of vertebrates.  It can infect humans, causing ascariasis.|The doctor diagnosed the child with an ascarid infection.  医生诊断这个孩子感染了蛔虫。|Synonyms: roundworm, nematode|Antonyms: N/A|noun
