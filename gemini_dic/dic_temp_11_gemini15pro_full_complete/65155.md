ethnical: /ˈeθɪk(ə)l/
adjective
伦理的；道德的
Relating to moral principles or the branch of knowledge dealing with these.
例句：The ethical implications of this decision are enormous. 这个决定的伦理含义是巨大的。

例句：Is it ethical to keep animals in zoos?  把动物关在动物园里合乎道德吗？


近义词：moral, virtuous, righteous
反义词：unethical, immoral, wrong
词性：adjective
