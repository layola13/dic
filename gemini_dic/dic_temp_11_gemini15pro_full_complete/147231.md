overmedicate: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪt/
verb
过度用药
To prescribe or administer too much medication to someone.
The doctor was accused of overmedicating his patients.  医生被指控给他的病人过度用药。
Some elderly people are overmedicated, leading to various health problems. 一些老年人被过度用药，导致各种健康问题。
近义词: overprescribe, overdose
反义词: undermedicate
词性: verb


overmedication: /ˌoʊvərˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/

近义词: overdosing, overprescription
反义词: undermedication
