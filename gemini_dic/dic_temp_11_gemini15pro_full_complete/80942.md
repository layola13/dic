grabbed:/ɡræbd/ | v. | 抓住了，夺取了 | past tense and past participle of "grab" which means to take hold of something suddenly and roughly | He grabbed his coat and ran out the door. 他抓起外套，跑出门外。| seized, snatched, grasped | released, dropped | verb

grabbed:/ɡræbd/ | adj. (informal) |  (informal) used to describe something that has been quickly and eagerly taken or obtained | I managed to get a grabbed lunch between meetings. 我在会议间隙匆匆吃了个午饭.  | snatched, hastily obtained |  | adjective (informal)
