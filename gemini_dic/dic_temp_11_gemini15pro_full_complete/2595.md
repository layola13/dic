adnascent:/ædˈnæsənt/ | adj. | 刚附着的；初生的；萌芽的 | Growing on or attached to something; just beginning to exist or develop. | The adnascent leaves clung tightly to the stem. 这些初生的叶子紧紧地附着在茎上。|  The adnascent political movement quickly gained momentum.  这个新兴的政治运动迅速获得了动力。 | 近义词: nascent, incipient, budding, emerging | 反义词: mature, developed, established | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin adnascens, present participle of adnascor (to grow upon, be born to).  "ad-" (to, towards) + "nascor" (to be born).
