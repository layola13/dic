bomas:/ˈboʊməs/| n. |牲畜围栏；村落（尤指东非地区）|An enclosure for livestock, especially in East Africa. Also, a Maasai village, traditionally enclosed by such a fence.|The tourists visited a traditional Maasai bomas. 游客参观了一个传统的马赛村落。|The cattle were herded into the bomas for the night. 牛群被赶进牲畜围栏过夜。|近义词：enclosure, kraal, village|反义词:|名词
