distorting:/dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋ/
verb|扭曲，歪曲|Changing the shape, appearance, or sound of something so that it is no longer accurate or true. |The loud music was distorting the sound of her voice. 吵闹的音乐扭曲了她的声音。|misrepresenting, twisting, warping|clarifying, representing accurately|v.
distorting:/dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋ/
adjective|扭曲的，歪曲的|Having the effect of distorting. |a distorting mirror 哈哈镜|misrepresentative, twisted, warped|accurate, truthful|adj.
