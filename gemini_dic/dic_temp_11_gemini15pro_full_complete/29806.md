caudicula:/kɔːˈdɪkjʊlə/| n.|小柄，尾状体；(特别是兰科植物花粉块的) 花粉梗|A slender stalk-like appendage, particularly the stalk of a pollen mass in orchids.|The caudicula of the orchid is sticky, allowing it to adhere to pollinators.|近义词:stalk, pedicle, filament|反义词:N/A|noun
