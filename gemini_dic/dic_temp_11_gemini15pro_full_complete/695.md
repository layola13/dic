absconsion: /æbˈskɒnʃən/ (UK) /æbˈskɑːnʃən/ (US)
n.
潜逃，逃匿，躲藏
the act of leaving secretly, often to avoid arrest or prosecution
The company director's sudden absconsion left investors wondering about the firm's future. 公司董事的突然潜逃让投资者对公司的未来感到疑惑。
He was charged with absconsion from bail. 他被指控弃保潜逃。

近义词: flight, escape, desertion, decampment
反义词: appearance, surrender

词性: noun

词源: From Latin abscondere (to hide, conceal)
