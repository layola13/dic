overpester:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛstər/| v. |过度纠缠，烦扰|To annoy or bother excessively; to pester beyond endurance.|He overpestered her with requests for dates until she finally agreed.|近义词：badger, harass, hound, plague, torment|反义词: leave alone, ignore|词性:动词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "pester" (probably from Old French empestrer "to entangle, embarrass," from Vulgar Latin *impastoriare "to hobble (a horse)," from Latin pastorium "fetter, hobble")
