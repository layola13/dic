denoted:/dɪˈnoʊtɪd/
verb
表示，指示
signified, represented, indicated.
The red light denotes danger.  红灯表示危险。
The symbol denotes peace. 这个符号代表和平。
synonym: signified, indicated, represented, symbolized
antonym: concealed, hidden, obscured
verb

denoted:/dɪˈnoʊtɪd/
adjective
表示的，指示的
indicated, signified, represented.
The denoted meaning of the word is different from its implied meaning.  这个词的字面意思与其隐含意思不同。
The area denoted on the map is a nature reserve. 地图上标出的区域是一个自然保护区。
synonym: indicated, signified, represented
antonym: concealed, hidden, obscured
