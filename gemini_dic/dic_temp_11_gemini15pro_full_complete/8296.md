anti-military:/ˌæntiˈmɪləteri/
adj.
反军事的，反对军国主义的
Opposed to military policies, actions, or institutions; pacifistic.
He has strong anti-military views and believes in peaceful conflict resolution. 他有强烈的反军事观点，并相信和平解决冲突。

The protest attracted many anti-military activists.  这次抗议吸引了许多反军事活动家。

近义词:pacifist, antimilitarist, peace-loving
反义词:pro-military, militarist, hawkish
词性:adjective
