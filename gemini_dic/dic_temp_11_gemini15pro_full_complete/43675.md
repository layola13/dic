credendum:/krɪˈdɛndəm/
n.
信条；教义；要旨
A statement of the beliefs or aims which guide someone's actions.
例句：The company's credendum is to provide excellent customer service. 该公司的信条是提供卓越的客户服务。

例句：One of the central credenda of the organization is environmental protection.  该组织的核心信条之一是环境保护。

近义词：creed, doctrine, dogma, tenet
反义词：heresy, unbelief

词性：名词

词源：From Latin *credendum*, neuter gerundive of credere "to believe."
