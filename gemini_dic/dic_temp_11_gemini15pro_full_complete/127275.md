navel-gazing:/ˈneɪvəl ɡeɪzɪŋ/
noun
沉思；自省；专注于自身
Excessive contemplation of oneself or one's own thoughts and feelings, often to the exclusion of the outside world.
He was accused of navel-gazing and ignoring the real problems facing the country.  他被指责只顾沉思，而忽视了国家面临的真正问题。
introspection, self-absorption, self-examination
extrospection, outward focus
n.
