breakthrough:/ˈbreɪkθruː/| n.|突破；重大进展；突破性|A sudden, important discovery or development that helps to solve a problem or understand something.|Scientists have made a major breakthrough in cancer research. 科学家们在癌症研究方面取得了重大突破。| synonyms: advance, development, progress| antonyms: setback, failure, obstacle |noun


break through:/breɪk θruː/| v.|突破；突围；克服|To force your way through something that is blocking you.|The soldiers broke through the enemy lines. 士兵们突破了敌人的防线。|synonyms: penetrate, breach, overcome|antonyms: retreat, surrender, yield|verb
