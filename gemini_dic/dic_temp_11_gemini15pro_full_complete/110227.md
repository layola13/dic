lifeguard:/ˈlaɪfɡɑːrd/| n.|救生员|A person employed to rescue people in danger of drowning, especially at a beach or swimming pool.|The lifeguard jumped into the pool to save the struggling child. 救生员跳进游泳池去救那个挣扎的孩子。|近义词：rescuer, lifesaver|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "drowner" but that refers to someone who causes drowning)|noun
