counterstate:/ˈkaʊntərˌsteɪt/
n.
对立国家；对抗国
a state or entity politically opposed to another
例句：The two counterstates engaged in a prolonged cold war. 这两个对立国家进行了长期的冷战。

例句：The small counterstate posed a surprising threat to its larger neighbor.  这个小对抗国对其较大的邻国构成了惊人的威胁。

近义词：rival state, adversary, opponent, enemy
反义词：ally, partner, friend

词性: 名词
