hocus-pocus:/ˌhəʊkəs ˈpəʊkəs/| n. |戏法；胡言乱语；骗人的把戏|Meaningless words used by magicians, or words used to deceive someone.  It refers to a deceptive trick, illusion, or nonsense.|例句：The magician waved his wand and uttered some hocus-pocus, and the rabbit disappeared. 魔术师挥舞着魔杖，念叨着咒语，兔子就消失了。

例句：He promised to double my money, but it was all just hocus-pocus. 他承诺让我的钱翻倍，但这只不过是骗人的把戏。|近义词：trickery, deception, sleight of hand, mumbo jumbo|反义词: reality, truth, honesty|词性:noun


hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| interj. |胡言乱语（魔术师用语）|Used as an exclamation during the performance of a trick.|例句：Hocus-pocus! Now watch carefully as the coin disappears.  胡言乱语！现在仔细看着硬币消失。

例句：With a wave of his hand and a cry of "hocus-pocus," the magician made the dove vanish.  魔术师一挥手，喊了一声“胡言乱语”，鸽子就消失了。|近义词：abracadabra, presto|反义词: - |词性:interjection
