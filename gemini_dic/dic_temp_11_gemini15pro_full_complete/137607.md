nontronite:/ˈnɒntrəˌnaɪt/| n. |绿脱石，一种粘土矿物|A clay mineral of the smectite group, containing iron and characterized by its greenish color.  It's formed by the weathering of basaltic rocks and other iron-rich materials.|例句1：Nontronite is commonly found in soils derived from volcanic rocks. 绿脱石通常存在于火山岩衍生的土壤中。
