dicker:/ˈdɪkər/ | n. |讨价还价；小生意；十个 |The act of bargaining, especially over a price; a small deal or transaction; a group of ten. |The dicker over the antique table lasted for an hour.  关于古董桌子的讨价还价持续了一个小时。 |Synonyms: bargaining, haggling, negotiation, wrangling |Antonyms: agreement, concession, acceptance |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *dīker, from dīc (“dike”), referring to transactions at a dike market.  Originally referred to a set of ten hides or skins.
