nonnegligible:/ˌnɒnˌnɛɡlɪdʒɪbəl/ |adj.| 不可忽略的；显著的 |The results showed a nonnegligible improvement in performance. 结果显示性能有显著提高。| significant, substantial, appreciable, considerable | insignificant, negligible, trivial | adjective


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
negligible (adjective): from Latin negligibilis "that may be disregarded," from negligere "to neglect," from nec "not" + legere "to gather, choose, read."
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
