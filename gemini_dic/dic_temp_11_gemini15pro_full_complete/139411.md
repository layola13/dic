observership:/əbˈzɜːrvərʃɪp/ /əbˈzɜːrvərˌʃɪp/
noun
观察员实习; 见习期
A period of time spent observing a skilled professional at work in order to gain practical experience and knowledge.
Dr. Smith completed an observership in pediatric cardiology at Boston Children's Hospital. 史密斯医生在波士顿儿童医院完成了小儿心脏病学的见习。
synonyms: apprenticeship, internship, practicum, training
antonyms:  employment, job, position
n.
