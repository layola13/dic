fantasia:/fænˈteɪʒə/ /fænˈteɪziə/| n. |幻想曲，幻想作品；奇想，幻想|A musical composition of irregular form, typically one based on improvisation or the imaginative depiction of a nonmusical idea.  A creation of the fancy; a whimsical or visionary notion.| The orchestra played a beautiful fantasia.  管弦乐队演奏了一曲优美的幻想曲。|Synonyms: fantasy, daydream, reverie, vision, dream, illusion, hallucination|Antonyms: reality, actuality|noun
