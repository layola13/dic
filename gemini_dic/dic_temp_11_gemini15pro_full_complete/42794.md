coupler:/ˈkʌplər/| n.|耦合器，连接器|A device that connects two things, especially two vehicles or pieces of machinery.|The train separated from the coupler. 火车与耦合器分离了。|synonyms: connector, link, joiner, adapter|antonyms: separator, disconnector|noun
coupler:/ˈkʌplər/| n.|联络人，中间人|A person who links two or more people or groups.|He acted as a coupler between the two warring factions. 他充当了两个交战派系之间的联络人。|synonyms: liaison, intermediary, mediator|antonyms: divider, separator|noun
