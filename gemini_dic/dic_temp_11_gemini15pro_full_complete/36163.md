coffeehouse:/ˈkɒfihaʊs/ (UK), /ˈkɔːfihaʊs/ (US)| n. |咖啡馆，咖啡店|A place where coffee and other beverages are sold, often providing a social gathering place.|例句：We met at the coffeehouse to discuss our project. 我们在咖啡馆见了面，讨论了我们的项目。|例句：The coffeehouse is a popular spot for students to study. 这家咖啡馆是学生们学习的热门地点。|近义词：cafe, coffee shop|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑bar 酒吧)|词性:noun
