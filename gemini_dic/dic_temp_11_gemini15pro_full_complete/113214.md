lusciousnesses:/ˈlʌʃəsnɪsɪz/
n.
美味；甘美；芬芳；性感
the quality of being luscious; deliciousness, sweetness, richness, or sensuality.
The lusciousnesses of the ripe peaches were irresistible.  熟透的桃子的美味令人无法抗拒。
近义词:deliciousness, sweetness, richness, attractiveness, allure
反义词:unpleasantness, bitterness, blandness, repulsiveness
词性:名词

lushness:/ˈlʌʃnəs/
n.
茂盛；葱茏；丰富；华丽
the quality of being lush; abundance, richness, or luxuriance.
The lushness of the tropical vegetation was breathtaking.  热带植被的茂盛令人叹为观止。
近义词:abundance, richness, luxuriance, lushness
反义词:scarcity, barrenness, sparseness
词性:名词

luscious:/ˈlʌʃəs/
adj.
美味的；甘美的；芬芳的；性感的
pleasingly rich, sweet, or attractive; delicious, fragrant, or sensual.
The cake was moist and luscious.  蛋糕湿润可口。
近义词:delicious, sweet, rich, attractive, alluring
反义词:unpleasant, bitter, bland, repulsive
词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *luscious, from Anglo-Norman luscious, lucius (“delicious, pleasant”), and Old French lascif, loscif (“lascivious”), from Latin lascivus (“wanton, playful”).  The semantic shift from "lascivious" to "delicious" occurred in English.
