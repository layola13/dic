enjambement:/ɪnˈdʒæmbmənt/ | n. | 行间转韵；跨行连续 | The continuation of a sentence without a pause beyond the end of a line, couplet, or stanza. | Example 1:  The poet used enjambement to create a sense of urgency in the poem. 诗人用跨行连续来营造一种紧迫感。

Example 2:  The enjambement in this stanza emphasizes the connection between the two lines.  这一节诗中的跨行连续强调了两行诗之间的联系。 |  Synonyms: run-on line,  verse overflow | Antonyms: end-stopped line | noun
