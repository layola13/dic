ionizers:/ˈaɪənaɪzərz/
n.
电离器; 离子发生器
A device that produces ions, which are electrically charged atoms or molecules.  These devices can be used for various purposes, including air purification.
The air purifier uses ionizers to remove dust and other particles from the air.  这个空气净化器使用电离器去除空气中的灰尘和其他颗粒物。
synonyms: ion generators, ionization devices
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider "deionizers" for the opposite effect)
noun
