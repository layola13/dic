counterfleury:/ˈkaʊntərˌflɜːri/ /ˈkaʊntərˌflʊri/| n.| 反花饰，反叶饰|An architectural ornament resembling a stylized, inverted fleur-de-lis.|The counterfleury design was a common motif in Gothic architecture. 反花饰设计是哥特式建筑中常见的图案。|Near synonyms: Inverted fleur-de-lis, counterflory|Antonyms: Fleur-de-lis|Noun
