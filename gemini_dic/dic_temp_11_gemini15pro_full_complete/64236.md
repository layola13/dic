equinoxes: /ˈiːkwɪnɒksiːz/ (UK), /ˈiːkwɪnɑːksiːz/ (US)| n.|分点；昼夜平分点|The time or date (twice each year) at which the sun crosses the celestial equator, when day and night are of equal length (especially about 21 March and 23 September).|The spring equinox occurs around March 20th each year. 春分大约在每年3月20日。

The autumnal equinox marks the beginning of fall. 秋分标志着秋天的开始。|近义词: equinoctial points|反义词: solstices|词性: Noun (plural)
