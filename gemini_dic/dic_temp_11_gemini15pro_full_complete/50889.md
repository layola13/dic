desertification:/dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
荒漠化，沙漠化
The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.
The desertification of the Sahel region has caused widespread famine. 撒哈尔地区的沙漠化造成了 widespread famine.
Desertification is a serious threat to agriculture. 荒漠化是对农业的严重威胁。
近义词：aridification, degradation
反义词：reforestation, afforestation
词性：noun

Etymology:
From French *désertification*, from *désertifier*  "to make into a desert," from *désert* "desert".  Ultimately from Latin *desertus* "abandoned, deserted, desolate," past participle of *deserere* "to abandon, desert," from *de-* "away" + *serere* "to join, link, bind."
