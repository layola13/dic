counteradvertising:/ˌkaʊntərˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ/
|n.|反广告，对抗性广告|The use of advertising to counteract the effects of previous advertising. |Example: The company launched a counteradvertising campaign to address the misleading claims made by its competitor.  该公司发起了一场反广告活动，以应对其竞争对手提出的误导性声明。|Example:  Counteradvertising can be an effective way to educate the public about the dangers of smoking. 反广告可以成为教育公众吸烟危害的有效途径。|Synonyms: counter-promotion, counter-marketing|Antonyms: advertising, promotion|noun
