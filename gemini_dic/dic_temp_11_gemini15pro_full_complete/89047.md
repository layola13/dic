holodomor:/ˌhɒləˈdɒmɔːr/ |n.|大饥荒，乌克兰大饥荒|A man-made famine in Soviet Ukraine in 1932-33, caused by Stalin's collectivization policies.|The Holodomor was a devastating tragedy that claimed millions of lives.  乌克兰大饥荒是一场毁灭性的悲剧，夺走了数百万人的生命。

The Holodomor is a stark reminder of the dangers of totalitarian regimes.  乌克兰大饥荒是对极权主义政权危险性的严厉提醒。|近义词：famine, genocide|反义词:abundance, feast|词性:noun
