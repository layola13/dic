hoodlumism:/ˈhuːlɪɡənɪzəm/
n.
流氓行为；暴徒行为
Disorderly and destructive behavior typical of hoodlums.
The city has seen a recent increase in hoodlumism.  这座城市最近流氓行为有所增加。
近义词：vandalism, hooliganism, thuggery
反义词：lawfulness, order, peacefulness
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hoodlum + -ism.  Hoodlum (1870s, American English) is of uncertain origin.
