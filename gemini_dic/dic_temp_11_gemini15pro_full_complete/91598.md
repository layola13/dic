hyperaggregating:/ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |过度聚集的，超聚集的 | Characterized by excessive aggregation or clumping, typically of cells like platelets.  Refers to an increased tendency of platelets to clump together, which can lead to the formation of blood clots. |  The patient's blood showed signs of hyperaggregating platelets. 患者的血液显示出 platelets 过度聚集的迹象。

The hyperaggregating effect of this drug needs further investigation.  这种药物的超聚集作用需要进一步研究。 | 近义词：overaggregating, excessively clumping | 反义词：disaggregating, dispersing | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"Hyper-" (Greek):  prefix meaning "over," "excessive," "beyond"
"Aggregating" (Latin aggregare, from ad- 'to' + grex 'flock, herd'):  present participle of "aggregate," meaning "to gather into a mass or whole."
