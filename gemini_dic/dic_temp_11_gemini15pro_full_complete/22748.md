boroughmonger:/ˈbʌrəˌmʌŋɡər/
noun
市镇议员买卖者；操纵市镇选举者
A person who uses corrupt means to control elections in a borough, especially by bribing voters or controlling nominations.
In the 19th century, boroughmongers held significant political sway in England. 19世纪，市镇议员买卖者在英国拥有重要的政治影响力。
Rampant corruption allowed boroughmongers to dominate Parliament.  猖獗的腐败使得市镇议员买卖者得以控制议会。
近义词:political manipulator, election rigger
反义词:honest politician, reformer
