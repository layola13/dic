noninclusively:/nɒnɪnˈkluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 非包含地，不完全地 | Not including all members or elements; excluding some.  |  The prices quoted are noninclusively of tax. 报价不含税。 | 近义词: exclusively, partially | 反义词: inclusively, totally | 词性: 副词

词源:  From non- + inclusive + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Inclusive" comes from the Latin "includere," meaning "to shut in, enclose, include."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
