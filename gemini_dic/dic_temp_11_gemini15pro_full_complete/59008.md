DVD-R: /ˌdiː viː ˈdiː ɑːr/ (US), /ˌdiː viː ˈdiː ɑː(r)/ (UK) | n. |  可录制DVD光盘 | A type of DVD disc that can be written to once, but not rewritten.  It stands for "Digital Versatile Disc - Recordable". | I bought a pack of DVD-Rs to back up my photos. 我买了一包可录制DVD光盘来备份我的照片。 | Synonyms: DVD+R, recordable DVD | Antonyms: DVD-ROM, DVD+ROM | noun
