changed /tʃeɪndʒd/ | v. | 改变，变化 | past tense and past participle of "change," meaning to make or become different. | My plans have changed due to the weather.  我的计划因为天气原因改变了。| The city has changed dramatically over the past decade. 这座城市在过去十年发生了巨大的变化。 | alter, modify, transform | remain, stay | verb

changed /tʃeɪndʒd/ | adj. | 改变了的，变化了的 |  modified or transformed; different from a previous state. |  He was a changed man after the experience.  经历过这件事后，他变成了一个不同的人。| The restaurant has a changed menu. 这家餐厅的菜单变了。| altered, modified, different | same, unchanged | adjective
