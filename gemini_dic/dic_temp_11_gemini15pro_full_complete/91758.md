hyperemotionality:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊʃəˈnælɪti/
n.|过度情绪化；情感亢进|A state of excessive or heightened emotional reactivity, characterized by exaggerated emotional responses to stimuli.|She exhibited hyperemotionality after the accident, crying and laughing uncontrollably.|近义词: emotional lability, excessive emotionality, emotional instability|反义词: emotional stability, emotional control|词性: noun

Etymology:  From hyper- (meaning "excessive, over") + emotionality (from emotion + -ality, a suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  Ultimately from the Latin *emovere* "to move out, remove, agitate."
