over-exacting:/ˌoʊvər ɪɡˈzæktɪŋ/
adjective
过分苛求的，过于严厉的
Making excessive demands; requiring too much of others.
The over-exacting teacher assigned a ten-page essay over the weekend.  这个过于严苛的老师布置了周末写一篇十页的论文。
He found his new boss over-exacting and difficult to please. 他发现他的新老板过分苛刻，很难取悦。
近义词: demanding, fastidious, meticulous, rigorous, stringent
反义词: lenient, tolerant, easygoing, undemanding
词性: adjective
词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "exacting" (demanding). "Exacting" comes from the verb "exact" (to demand and obtain something from someone), which ultimately derives from the Latin "exigere" (to drive out, demand).
