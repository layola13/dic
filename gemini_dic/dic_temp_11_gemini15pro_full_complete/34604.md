cleavelandite:/ˈkliːvˌlændˌaɪt/| n. |钠长石，透长石|A platy variety of albite, a plagioclase feldspar mineral. It is typically white or colorless and often occurs in thin, tabular crystals.|Cleavelandite is a common constituent of pegmatites.  钠长石是伟晶岩的常见成分。|Cleavelandite can exhibit beautiful iridescent colors. 钠长石可以展现美丽的彩虹色。|Synonyms: albite, plagioclase|Antonyms: None|Noun
