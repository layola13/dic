branchiomerism: /ˌbræŋkiˈɒmərɪzəm/ | n. |鳃节性，分节性|The serial repetition of structures associated with the branchial or pharyngeal arches in vertebrates.|The branchiomerism of the fish is clearly visible in its gill structure.  鱼的鳃节性在其鳃结构中清晰可见。|Synonyms: metamerism, segmentation|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider homogeneity or uniformity)|noun
