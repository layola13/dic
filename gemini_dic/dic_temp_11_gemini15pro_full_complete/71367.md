flagellates:/ˈflædʒəˌleɪts/ | n. |鞭毛虫|A group of single-celled organisms characterized by the presence of one or more whip-like appendages called flagella, which are used for locomotion. |Example 1:  Flagellates are found in a variety of aquatic environments. 鞭毛虫存在于各种水生环境中。

Example 2: Some flagellates are parasitic, causing diseases in humans and animals. 一些鞭毛虫是寄生的，会导致人类和动物的疾病。|近义词:mastigophora (obsolete), protozoa|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

flagellate:/ˈflædʒəˌleɪt/ | v. |鞭打; 鞭笞 | To whip or flog someone.  This usage is less common than the noun form referring to the organisms. | Example 1: The cruel master would flagellate his slaves for minor infractions. 残酷的主人会因为轻微的违规而鞭打他的奴隶。

Example 2:  The prisoner was publicly flagellated as punishment for his crimes.  这名囚犯因其罪行被公开鞭打作为惩罚。|近义词: whip, flog, scourge, lash |反义词: N/A |词性:verb
