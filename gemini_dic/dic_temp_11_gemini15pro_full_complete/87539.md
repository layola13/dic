heteroglossic:/ˌhɛtərəˈɡlɒsɪk/ /ˌhɛtərəˈɡlɑːsɪk/
adjective
多种语言的; 杂语的
Characterized by a diversity of voices, styles, or viewpoints within a text or discourse.  Relating to or characterized by heteroglossia.
Example: The novel's heteroglossic nature reflects the diverse social and cultural backgrounds of its characters. 这部小说的杂语性质反映了其人物多样化的社会和文化背景。

Example:  Bakhtin's theory of heteroglossia explores the multiple voices and perspectives present in any language. 巴赫金的杂语理论探讨了任何语言中存在的多种声音和视角。
近义词:polyglot, multilingual, multivocal
反义词:monoglot, monolingual, univocal
词性:adjective
