ignitions:/ɪɡˈnɪʃənz/
n.
点火，点燃；着火，燃烧；引发，激起
the process of something starting to burn or explode, or a device that starts this process
The car's ignition failed, and we were stranded. 汽车的点火装置坏了，我们被困住了。
ignition system 点火系统
ignition key 点火钥匙
combustion, firing, kindling
extinguishing, smothering
noun
