debellate:/dɪˈbɛleɪt/
verb
征服，击败，彻底战胜
to subdue or vanquish by war; to overcome or crush completely
例句：The Roman legions debellated the rebellious tribes. 罗马军团征服了叛乱的部落。

例句：The government aimed to debellate the insurgency through a combination of military force and political negotiation. 政府旨在通过军事力量和政治谈判相结合的方式来平息叛乱。

近义词：conquer, vanquish, subdue, overcome, defeat, crush
反义词：surrender, yield, lose

词源:  From Latin *debellare*, from *de-* (“down, completely”) + *bellare* (“to wage war”), from *bellum* (“war”).

词性: verb
