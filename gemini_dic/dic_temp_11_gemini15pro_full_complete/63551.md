envenom:/ɪnˈvɛnəm/ /ɛnˈvɛnəm/| vt.|使毒化；毒害；加毒于；使恶化|To impregnate with venom; to poison; to embitter.|The politician's speech was clearly intended to envenom the already tense situation.  这位政治家的演讲显然是为了加剧本已紧张的局势。|The spider envenomed its prey. 蜘蛛给它的猎物注入了毒液。|近义词：poison, taint, embitter, infect|反义词：heal, soothe, cure|词性:动词

Etymology->
From en- + venom.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put in or into," and "venom" refers to a poisonous substance.
