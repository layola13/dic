abases:/əˈbeɪsɪz/
|v.|降低，贬低；使谦卑|To lower in rank, prestige, or esteem; to humble. |He abased himself before the king, begging for mercy. 他在国王面前卑躬屈膝，乞求怜悯。|近义词：humiliate, degrade, demean|反义词：exalt, elevate, praise|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *abasen, from Old French abaisser, from a- (from Latin ad 'to, towards') + baissier 'to lower', from Vulgar Latin *bassare, from Late Latin bassus 'low'.
