civilest:/ˈsɪvɪlɪst/
adjective
最有礼貌的，最客气的
Most polite and courteous.
She gave the civilest response possible, despite being insulted.  她受到了侮辱，却仍然给出了尽可能最有礼貌的回应。
近义词:  most courteous, most polite, politest
反义词: rudest, most impolite, uncivil
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *civile*, from Old French *civil*, from Latin *civilis* "relating to citizens, pertaining to the state, courteous,"  from *civis* "citizen."  The *-est* suffix is the superlative form.
