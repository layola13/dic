clink:/klɪŋk/| n. v.|叮当声，碰撞声；(使)发出叮当声|A light sharp ringing sound, such as that made by striking glasses together.|The coins clinked in his pocket. 硬币在他的口袋里叮当作响。|He clinked the coins together. 他把硬币叮当作响地碰在一起。|近义词：jangle, ring, chime, chink|反义词:silence, hush|词性:名词, 动词

词源:  From Middle English *clinken, likely of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch klinken (“to clink, sound, ring”).
