germinations:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.
萌芽，发芽；发生
the process of germinating; the beginning of vegetation or growth from a seed or spore; the first development of something
例句：The germinations of seeds require warmth and moisture. 种子的萌发需要温暖和水分。

例句：We are witnessing the germinations of a new political era. 我们正在见证一个新的政治时代的萌芽。
近义词：sprouting, budding, beginnings, origins, inception
反义词：dormancy, decay, withering
词性：名词 (Noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *germinationem, from Latin germinare ("to sprout"), from germen ("bud, sprout").
