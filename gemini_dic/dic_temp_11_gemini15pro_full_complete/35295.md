clysmian: /klɪzˈmiːən/ | adj. |  灾难性的，大动荡的 | Relating to or characteristic of a cataclysm; disastrous. | The clysmian event reshaped the landscape.  这场灾难性事件改变了地貌。| 近义词: catastrophic, cataclysmic, disastrous, apocalyptic | 反义词: beneficial, fortunate, advantageous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Greek *kataklysmos* "deluge, flood," from *kataklyzein* "to inundate, deluge," from *kata-* "down" + *klyzein* "to wash, dash over."
