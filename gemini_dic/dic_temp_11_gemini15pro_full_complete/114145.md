Etymology:
From Mahatma + -ism.  "Mahatma" is a Sanskrit title of respect for a person regarded as of great soul or saint, applied especially to Gandhi. "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns denoting action or practice, state or condition, principles, doctrines, a usage or characteristic, etc.
