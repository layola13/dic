|The destructivenesses of the hurricanes left the coastal towns in ruins. 飓风的破坏性使沿海城镇沦为废墟。
|The report detailed the destructivenesses of several invasive species.  这份报告详细描述了几种入侵物种的破坏力。
|近义词: damage, devastation, ruin, havoc
|反义词: constructiveness, creativity, productivity
|词性: 名词


词源:
From destructive + -ness + -es.  Destructive comes from the Latin word *dēstruere* (to destroy), from *de-* (down, away) + *struere* (to build).
