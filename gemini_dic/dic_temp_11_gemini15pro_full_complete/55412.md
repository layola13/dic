distichously: /dɪˈstɪkəsli/ | adv. | 二列地，排成两列地 | Arranged in two rows or ranks on opposite sides of a stem or axis. |  The leaves are arranged distichously along the stem. 叶子沿着茎二列排列。| synonym: two-ranked | antonym: spirally, alternately | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *δίς* (dis, "twice") + *στίχος* (stichos, "row, line").
