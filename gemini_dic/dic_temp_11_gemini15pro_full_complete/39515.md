nominees:/ˌnɑːmɪˈniːz/
n.
被提名者；候选人
People who have been nominated for an award or position.
The nominees for Best Picture were announced. 最佳影片的提名名单已经公布。
近义词: candidates, contenders
反义词: electors, voters
词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:
From the Latin word "nominare," meaning "to name."  The suffix "-ee" indicates the person who receives the action, thus "nominee" is the person who is named or nominated.
