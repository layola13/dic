kibbled:/ˈkɪbəld/| adj.| kibbled (for animals);kibbles (for humans)|磨碎的；小块的（用于动物食品）；kibbles 指供人类食用的细碎食物|The cat ate kibbled dry food. 猫吃了磨碎的干粮。|Synonyms: ground, crushed, crumbled|Antonyms: whole, intact|adjective

kibble:/ˈkɪbəl/| n.| kibble (for animals);kibbles (for humans)|（动物的）粗磨食物；(供人食用的)细碎食物|The dog's kibble contains a mix of meat and grains. 狗粮里混合了肉和谷物。|Synonyms: feed, fodder, pellets (for animals);  granules, bits (for humans)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

kibble:/ˈkɪbəl/| v.| 磨碎；弄碎|She kibbled the biscuits for the baby. 她把饼干弄碎给婴儿吃。|Synonyms: grind, crush, crumble|Antonyms: N/A|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kibelen, frequentative of *kiben "to chop," of uncertain origin.
