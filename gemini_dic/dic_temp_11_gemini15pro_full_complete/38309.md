concealedly:/kənˈsiːlɪdli/
|adv.|隐蔽地，秘密地|in a concealed manner; secretly; not openly. |He concealedly slipped the note into her hand. 他偷偷地把纸条塞到她手里。|
|例句：The documents were concealedly stored in a hidden compartment. 文件被秘密地藏在一个隐藏的隔间里。|
|例句：She smiled concealedly, pleased with her secret. 她偷偷地笑了，对自己的秘密感到满意。|
|近义词：secretly, covertly, furtively, stealthily|
|反义词：openly, publicly, visibly|
|词性: 副词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From conceal + -ed + -ly.  Conceal comes from Old French *conceler, from Latin concēlāre "to hide, conceal," from com- "together" + cēlāre "to hide."
