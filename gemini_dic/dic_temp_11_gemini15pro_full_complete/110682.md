line-out:/ˈlaɪn aʊt/ | n. |线路输出; (体育)列队出场; (橄榄球)列队; |A connection to a sound system that sends audio signals. In sports, it refers to players lining up, especially in rugby for a line-out. | The band's line-out wasn't working, so the audience couldn't hear the music. 乐队的线路输出坏了，所以观众听不到音乐。| The rugby team prepared for the line-out. 橄榄球队准备列队。 | synonyms: output, lineup, alignment | antonyms: input | noun
