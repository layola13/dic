murnival:/ˈmɜːrnɪvəl/ | n. | 一组四个人; 四人一组 | A company or set of four persons. |  In medieval times, the term murnival designated a set of four dice used in gambling.  Later, it came to refer to any group of four. |  The king's murnival of advisors always accompanied him on important journeys. 国王的四位顾问总是陪他进行重要的旅行。| quartet, foursome, tetrad |  N/A | noun
