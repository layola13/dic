dapper:/ˈdæpər/| adj.|衣冠楚楚的；整洁漂亮的；短小精悍的|Neat and trim in dress and appearance;  stylish.  Also implies being small and active.|He looked very dapper in his new suit. 他穿着新西装，看起来非常衣冠楚楚。|近义词：stylish, smart, elegant, chic, spruce|反义词：sloppy, unkempt, scruffy|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Probably from Dutch *dapper, meaning "brave, valiant," later acquiring the sense of "nimble, quick," which may have influenced the development of the meaning related to neat appearance.
