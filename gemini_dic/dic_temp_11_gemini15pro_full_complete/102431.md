jackboots:/ˈdʒækbuːts/| n.|长筒靴；马靴；军靴|Heavy, sturdy, leather boots that reach to the knee or higher, often associated with military or authoritarian figures.|The soldiers wore highly polished jackboots. 士兵们穿着擦得锃亮的军靴。|synonyms: combat boots, riding boots, military boots|antonyms: slippers, sandals|noun

jackboots:/ˈdʒækbuːts/| n.|压制；暴政 (Figurative meaning)|Symbolic of oppressive or authoritarian rule, often used figuratively.|The jackboots of fascism were crushing dissent. 法西斯主义的铁蹄正在粉碎异议。|synonyms: oppression, tyranny, authoritarianism|antonyms: freedom, liberty, democracy|noun
