graecomania:/ˌɡriːkəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 希腊狂热，崇尚希腊文化 | An excessive fondness or enthusiasm for Greece, its people, or its culture. |  He had a bad case of graecomania, filling his house with Greek statues and artifacts. 他得了严重的希腊狂热症，把他的房子里摆满了希腊雕像和文物。 |  Example:  Her graecomania led her to study ancient Greek language and philosophy.  她的希腊狂热使她学习古希腊语和哲学。 |  Synonyms:  Hellenomania, philhellenism | Antonyms:  None | noun
