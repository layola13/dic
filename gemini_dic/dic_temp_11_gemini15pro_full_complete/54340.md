dishes:/ˈdɪʃɪz/ | n. | 盘子；菜肴 | plates, bowls, cups, etc., used for serving or eating food; food prepared as part of a meal |  She washed the dishes after dinner. 她晚饭后洗了碗。| synonyms: plates, crockery, tableware, cuisine, food | antonyms:  (For tableware) none; (For food) hunger, starvation | noun

dishes:/ˈdɪʃɪz/ | v. | 盛菜；提供信息 | to serve food onto plates; to provide information, especially gossip | He dished out the stew. 他盛出炖菜。| synonyms: serve, distribute, dispense, reveal, divulge | antonyms: withhold, conceal | verb


Etymology: From Old French *dische* (modern French *disque*), from Latin *discus* "disk, quoit."
