inefficaciousness:/ˌɪnɛfɪˈkeɪʃəsnəs/
n.
无效；无用；无能
Lack of power to produce the desired effect.
The inefficaciousness of the drug led to the discontinuation of the trial.  这种药物的无效导致试验中止。
近义词：ineffectiveness, futility, uselessness, impotence
反义词：effectiveness, efficacy, potency
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + efficacious (effective) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin ex- (out) + facere (to do/make).
