kersey:/ˈkɜːrzi/ /ˈkɝːzi/
n.
卡西粗呢；卡西裤子
A coarse, heavy woolen cloth, usually ribbed.  A pair of trousers made from this cloth.
例句：He wore a pair of sturdy kersey trousers. 他穿了一条结实的卡西裤子。

例句：The kersey fabric was rough to the touch. 卡西布料摸起来很粗糙。

近义词：baize, broadcloth, twill
反义词：silk, satin
词性：名词
