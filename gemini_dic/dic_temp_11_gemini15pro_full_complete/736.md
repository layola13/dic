absinthiate: /ˌæbsɪnθiˌeɪt/
verb
沉溺于苦艾酒; 饮苦艾酒
to drink absinthe excessively.
He absinthiated until he hallucinated. 他沉溺于苦艾酒直到出现幻觉。
近义词: overindulge, drink heavily
反义词: abstain
词性: verb

absinthiate: /ˌæbsɪnθiˌeɪt/
noun
苦艾酒爱好者
a person who drinks absinthe habitually.
He was a known absinthiate in the local bars. 他是当地酒吧里有名的苦艾酒爱好者。
近义词: drinker, alcoholic
反义词: teetotaler
词性: noun
