outsells:/ˌaʊtˈsɛlz/
verb
胜过，销量超过
sells more than something else.
例句：Product A consistently outsells product B. 产品A的销量一直超过产品B。
例句：This new phone model is outselling all others on the market.  这款新手机型号的销量超过了市场上所有其他型号。
近义词：surpasses, exceeds, overtakes
反义词：is outsold by, lags behind
词性:v.
