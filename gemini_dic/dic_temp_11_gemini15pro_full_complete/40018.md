consumed:/kənˈsuːmd/ | v. | 消耗，吃掉，吞噬，充满（某种情感） |  completely destroyed or used up something | The fire consumed the entire building. 大火吞噬了整栋建筑。| synonyms: devour, absorb, engulf, use up, exhaust | antonyms: preserve, conserve, save | verb

consumed:/kənˈsuːmd/ | adj. | 被…充满的，被…吞噬的 | dominated by a powerful feeling | He was consumed by guilt. 他被内疚感吞噬。 | synonyms: obsessed, preoccupied, engrossed, captivated | antonyms: indifferent, apathetic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin consumere, from Latin con- (thoroughly) + sumere (to take, use up).
