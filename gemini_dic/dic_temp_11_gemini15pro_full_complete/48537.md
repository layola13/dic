defacers:/dɪˈfeɪsərz/| n.|污损者，涂鸦者|People who deface public or private property, often with graffiti.  那些污损公共或私人财产的人，通常指涂鸦者。|The defacers were caught on camera spraying paint on the side of the building.  这些涂鸦者在建筑物侧面喷漆时被摄像头拍到。|Synonyms: vandals, graffiti artists (sometimes), taggers|Antonyms: restorers, conservators|noun

deface:/dɪˈfeɪs/| vt.|污损，涂鸦，丑化|To spoil the surface or appearance of something, often by writing or drawing on it. 损坏某物表面或外观，通常指在其上书写或绘画。|Someone defaced the poster with offensive slogans.  有人用攻击性标语污损了海报。|The statue was defaced with graffiti.  雕像被涂鸦污损了。|Synonyms: mar, spoil, damage, vandalize|Antonyms: restore, beautify, enhance|verb
