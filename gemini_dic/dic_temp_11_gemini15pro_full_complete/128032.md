nerdiest:/ˈnɜːrdiɪst/ | adj. | 最书呆子气的 |  Superlative form of nerdy: most nerdy.  It describes someone who exhibits the qualities of a nerd to the highest degree, often characterized by an intense interest in intellectual or obscure pursuits, sometimes combined with social awkwardness. |  He was voted the nerdiest kid in school.  他被选为学校里最书呆子气的孩子。 |  Example:  Even among the computer club members, he stood out as the nerdiest of the bunch, always tinkering with his Raspberry Pi. 即使在计算机俱乐部成员中，他也以最书呆子气的形象脱颖而出，总是摆弄他的树莓派。 | Synonyms: geekiest, dorkiest | Antonyms: coolest, most popular | word type: adjective
