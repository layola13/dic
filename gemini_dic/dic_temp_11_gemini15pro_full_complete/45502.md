curdly:/ˈkɜːrdli/ | adj. | 凝乳状的；像凝乳的 | Resembling curd; like curd in appearance or consistency. | The sauce had separated and become slightly curdly.  酱汁已经分离，变得有点凝乳状了。| The spoiled milk was thick and curdly.  变质的牛奶又浓又稠，像凝乳一样。 |  近义词：coagulated, clotted | 反义词: smooth, liquid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From curd + -ly.  来自 curd（凝乳）+ -ly（形容词后缀）。
