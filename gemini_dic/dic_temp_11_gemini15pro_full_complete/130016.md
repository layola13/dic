non-offer:/ˌnɒn ˈɒfər/ |n.| 非邀约；非报价；未发出要约 |A statement or action that does not constitute a legally binding offer.  Something presented or suggested that does not meet the criteria of a formal offer.| The company's initial statement was considered a non-offer, so we continued negotiations. 公司最初的声明被认为是非邀约，所以我们继续谈判。| near synonyms:  declaration, proposal (that is not a formal offer), suggestion, indication,  | antonyms: offer, bid, proposal (that *is* a formal offer) | noun
词性: noun
