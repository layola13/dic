infantine:/ˈɪnfənˌtaɪn/ /ˈɪnfənˌtin/ | adj. |幼稚的，孩子气的|Characteristic of or resembling an infant; childish.| Her infantine behavior embarrassed everyone at the dinner party. 她的幼稚行为让晚宴上的每个人都感到尴尬。|例句：The girl, though six years old, still had an infantine lisp.  虽然这个女孩已经六岁了，但她说话仍然带着孩子气的口齿不清。

例句：He showed an infantine delight in his new toy. 他对他的新玩具表现出孩子般的喜悦。

近义词：childish, babyish, juvenile, puerile |反义词: mature, adult, grown-up |词性: adjective

词源: From French *infantin, ultimately from Latin *infans* (infant).
