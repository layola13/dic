mammals:/ˈmæməlz/| n.|哺乳动物|Animals that have mammary glands (which produce milk to feed their young) and are warm-blooded, typically having hair or fur.|例句：Whales are mammals, even though they live in the ocean. 鲸鱼是哺乳动物，即使它们生活在海洋里。

例句：Humans are mammals too. 人类也是哺乳动物。|近义词：mammalian (adjective form)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider reptiles, birds, fish, amphibians as contrasting animal groups) |词性:noun
