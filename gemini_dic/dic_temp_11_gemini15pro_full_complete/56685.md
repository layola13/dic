doolally:/duːˈlɑːli/
adjective
疯狂的，精神错乱的
mentally deranged; crazy.
He went completely doolally after losing his job. 他失业后完全疯了。
近义词:crazy, mad, insane, bonkers
反义词:sane, rational
词性:adjective
词源:From Deolali, a town in India where British soldiers were often treated for mental illness before being sent home.
