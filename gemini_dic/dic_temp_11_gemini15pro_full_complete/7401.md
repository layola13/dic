animateur:/ˌænɪməˈtɜːr/ (UK) /ˈænɪmeɪtər/ (US)| n.|主持人，活跃气氛的人，领队|A person who leads and encourages activities, especially in a holiday resort or club, or on a cruise ship.  An animateur creates a lively and entertaining atmosphere.|例句：The animateur organized games and activities by the pool.  游泳池边的领队组织了游戏和活动。

例句：The cruise ship's animateur was energetic and kept everyone entertained.  游轮上的主持人精力充沛，让每个人都玩得很开心。|近义词：host, compere, MC, entertainer, leader, facilitator|反义词:bore, killjoy|词性:noun
