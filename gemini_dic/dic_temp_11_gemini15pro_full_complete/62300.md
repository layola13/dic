endangers:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərz/
verb
危及，使处于险境
puts (someone or something) at risk or in danger.
Pollution endangers marine life. 污染危及海洋生物。
近义词: jeopardizes, threatens, imperils, risks
反义词: protects, safeguards, secures
词性: verb
词源: From Old French *endamager, from en- (“in, into”) + damage (“damage”).  Ultimately from Latin damnum (“loss, damage”).
