misconnection:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkʃən/ | n. | 错过联系；失联；接错| A failure to connect, especially of people traveling on different flights or trains who intend to meet or transfer luggage. | Example 1:  Due to a misconnection in Chicago, I missed my connecting flight to London. 由于在芝加哥错过了联程航班，我没赶上飞往伦敦的航班。

Example 2: There was a misconnection with our luggage, and it ended up in Denver instead of Dallas. 我们的行李接错了，结果到了丹佛而不是达拉斯。 | Near synonyms: missed connection, connection failure, travel mishap | Antonyms: connection, successful transfer | noun
