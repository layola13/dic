destiny:/ˈdɛstɪni/ /ˈdɛstəni/| n.|命运，天命，天数|The inevitable or necessary fate or end to which people or things are believed to be destined.|She believed it was her destiny to become a great artist. 她相信成为一名伟大的艺术家是她的命运。|synonyms: fate, fortune, lot, doom, kismet|antonyms: chance, free will|noun

例句：
His destiny was to become a king.
他的命运是成为国王。

It was her destiny to change the world.
她的天命是改变世界。
