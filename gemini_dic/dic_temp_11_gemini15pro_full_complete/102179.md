itacolumite:/ˌɪtəkɒləˈmaɪt/ | n. |伊塔科鲁米石，柔性砂岩 |A porous, flexible sandstone that occurs in Brazil. It is composed primarily of quartz grains cemented by mica and iron oxides. The flexibility is due to the interlocking arrangement of the quartz grains and the nature of the cementing material. | Itacolumite is a rare and interesting type of rock. 伊塔科鲁米石是一种稀有而有趣的岩石。| Itacolumite is the bedrock on which the city of Ouro Preto is built.  伊塔科鲁米石是奥鲁普雷图市的地基。 |Synonyms: flexible sandstone | Antonyms: N/A | noun
