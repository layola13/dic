epithalamium:/ˌɛpɪθəˈleɪmiəm/ | n. | 婚礼颂歌，结婚诗 | A song or poem written in honor of a bride and groom. |  Example 1: The poet composed a beautiful epithalamium for his friend's wedding.  这位诗人为他朋友的婚礼创作了一首美丽的婚礼颂歌。

Example 2:  The epithalamium was read aloud during the wedding reception. 婚礼颂歌在婚宴上被朗读出来。 | Synonym:  nuptial song, wedding poem, hymeneal | Antonym:  elegy, lament, dirge | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epithalamion, from Ancient Greek ἐπιθαλάμιον (epithalámion), from ἐπί (epí, “upon, at”) + θάλαμος (thálamos, “bridal chamber, bedroom”).
