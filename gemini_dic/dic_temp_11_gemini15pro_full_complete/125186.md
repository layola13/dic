multicausal:/ˌmʌltiˈkɔːzəl/ | adj. | 多因素的，多原因的 | Having many causes; caused by many factors. |  Heart disease is often multicausal, involving genetics, lifestyle, and environmental factors. 心脏病通常是多因素引起的，涉及遗传、生活方式和环境因素。

The report suggests that the economic downturn is multicausal, stemming from both domestic and international pressures. 报告表明，经济衰退是多因素造成的，源于国内和国际的双重压力。 |  multifactorial, multifaceted | single-cause, monocausal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (prefix meaning "many") + causal (adjective meaning "relating to or acting as a cause"). The prefix "multi-" originates from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many."  The word "causal" comes from Late Latin "causalis," from Latin "causa" meaning "cause, reason."
