actualism:/ˈæktʃuəlɪzəm/
n.
现实主义;均变论
The doctrine that existing processes acting in the same manner and with essentially the same intensity as at present are sufficient to account for all geological changes.
The geologist explained the formation of the canyon using the principle of actualism.  地质学家用现实主义原则解释了峡谷的形成。
近义词：uniformitarianism
反义词：catastrophism
词性：noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin actualis "active, pertaining to action," from actus "a doing, a driving, an impulse," from agere "to drive, lead, act," + -ism.
