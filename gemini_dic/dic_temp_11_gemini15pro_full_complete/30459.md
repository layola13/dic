centrifuge: /ˈsentrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsentrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)
| n. | 离心机 | A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids. | The lab uses a centrifuge to separate blood components.  实验室用离心机分离血液成分。| synonyms: separator, extractor | antonyms: N/A | noun
centrifuge: /ˈsentrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsentrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)
| v. | 用离心机分离 | To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge.  |  The blood samples were centrifuged to isolate the plasma.  血液样本被离心以分离血浆。| synonyms: spin, whirl, rotate | antonyms: N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centrum* (center) + *fugere* (to flee).
