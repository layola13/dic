instigation:/ˌɪnstɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n. |煽动，唆使，刺激|The instigation of violence is a serious crime. 煽动暴力是一项严重的罪行。|The investigation revealed that he was the instigation behind the riot. 调查显示他是暴乱的幕后煽动者。|近义词：incitement, provocation, goading|反义词: dissuasion, deterrence|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin instigationem, accusative of instigatio, from Latin instigare "to incite, urge forward," from in- "toward" + stigare "to prick, goad."
