obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ (UK), /ˈɑːbdərət/ (US)| adj.|顽固的，执拗的，冷酷无情的|stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.|He remained obdurate, refusing to admit he was wrong. 他依然固执己见，拒绝承认自己错了。|Synonyms: stubborn, inflexible, adamant, unyielding, intractable|Antonyms: compliant, yielding, amenable, flexible|adjective
