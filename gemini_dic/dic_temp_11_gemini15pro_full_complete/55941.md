dizzily: /ˈdɪzɪli/ | adv. | 头晕地，眩晕地 | In a dizzy manner; in a way that causes dizziness.  |  She spun around dizzily. 她头晕目眩地转着圈。 |  The room spun dizzily around her. 房间在她周围天旋地转。 | 近义词: giddily, lightheadedly | 反义词: steadily, firmly | 词性: 副词
词源：From dizzy + -ly.  Dizzy itself comes from Old English *dysig, related to Old High German tusig "confused, foolish."
