neem:/niːm/| n.|印度楝树；楝树油|A fast-growing tropical evergreen tree (Azadirachta indica) native to India, valued for its medicinal and insecticidal properties.|例句：Neem oil is a natural insecticide. 楝树油是一种天然杀虫剂。

例句：The neem tree is highly valued in India. 印度楝树在印度非常受重视。|近义词：margosa|反义词:|noun
