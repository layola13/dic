complyingly:/kəmˈplaɪɪŋli/
adverb|顺从地，服从地|in a way that obeys a rule, request, or command|She complyingly handed over her passport. 她顺从地交出了护照。|近义词：obediently, submissively, meekly|反义词：defiantly, rebelliously|词性:副词
词源: From comply + -ing + -ly. Comply comes from Old French complaire, from Latin complēre "to fulfill," from com- "with" + plēre "to fill."
