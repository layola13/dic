nonsecrets:/ˈnɑːnˌsiːkrɪts/ /ˈnɒnˌsiːkrɪts/ | n. | 非秘密，公开信息 | Information that is not confidential or classified; matters of public knowledge. |  The company's financial reports are nonsecrets, available to anyone who wants to see them. 该公司的财务报告不是秘密，任何想看的人都可以看到。 | synonyms: public information, open knowledge, common knowledge, disclosed information | antonyms: secrets, classified information, confidential information | noun
