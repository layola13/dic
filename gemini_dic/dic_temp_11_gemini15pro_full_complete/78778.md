gilled:/ɡɪld/
adjective
有鳃的；
Having gills, respiratory organs used by aquatic animals.
The gilled mushroom was poisonous.  这种有鳃的蘑菇有毒。
近义词:lamellate, lamelliform
反义词:agill
词性:adjective

gilled:/ɡɪld/
adjective
(of a coin) having grooved edges.
Having a series of fine grooves, especially on the edge of a coin.
The gilled edge of the coin prevented counterfeiting.  硬币的滚花边缘防止了伪造。
近义词:grooved, milled
反义词:smooth
词性:adjective
