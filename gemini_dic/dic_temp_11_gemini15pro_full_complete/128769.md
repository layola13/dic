nighest:/ˈnaɪɪst/| adj.|最近的；最亲近的|Nearest, closest. Refers to physical proximity or close relationship.|例句：The nighest town is about ten miles away. 最近的城镇大约十英里远。|近义词：nearest, closest|反义词：farthest, furthest|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *nighest, a contraction of nigh, meaning "near" + est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Old English neah.
