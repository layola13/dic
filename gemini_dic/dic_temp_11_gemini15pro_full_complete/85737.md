hawkey:/ˈhɔːki/| adj.|敏锐的，有眼光的|Having or showing keen perception, especially in business or financial matters.|He has a hawkey eye for a bargain. 他对便宜货有敏锐的眼光。|近义词：sharp, astute, perceptive, shrewd|反义词：naive, gullible, unobservant|词性:形容词

词源:  From "hawk" (the bird) + "-ey" (suffix meaning "having the qualities of").  Hawks are known for their sharp eyesight, hence the adjective meaning "sharp-sighted" or "perceptive."
