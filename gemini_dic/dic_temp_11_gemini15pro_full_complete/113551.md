macadamize: /məˈkæd.ə.maɪz/ | v. | 用碎石铺路 | To cover a road or path with small broken stones in a way that forms a smooth, hard surface. |  The road was macadamized last year. 去年这条路被铺上了碎石。| Synonyms: pave, surface, asphalt | Antonyms: dig up, excavate | verb

macadamization: /məˌkæd.ə.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 碎石铺路 | The process of covering a road or path with small broken stones. | The macadamization of the road improved travel conditions.  这条路的碎石铺设改善了交通状况。| Synonyms: paving, surfacing | Antonyms: excavation | noun
