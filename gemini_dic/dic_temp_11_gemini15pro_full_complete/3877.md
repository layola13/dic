agrarianize:/əˈɡrɛəriənaɪz/
verb
使农业化，把…变成农业区
To make agrarian; to convert (land) to agricultural use; to divide (land) among peasant farmers.
The government's policy is to agrarianize the large estates and distribute the land to the peasants. 政府的政策是将大型庄园农业化，并将土地分配给农民。
The formerly industrial region was agrarianized after the factory closures. 工厂关闭后，以前的工业区变成了农业区。
近义词: ruralize, agriculturize
反义词: industrialize, urbanize
词性: verb

agrarian:/əˈɡrɛəriən/
adjective
土地的，耕地的；农业的；农民的
Relating to cultivated land or the cultivation of land; relating to landed property; pertaining to farmers or their way of life.
The agrarian reform movement seeks to redistribute land ownership. 土地改革运动旨在重新分配土地所有权。
The country's economy is primarily agrarian, relying heavily on agricultural exports. 该国的经济以农业为主，严重依赖农产品出口。
近义词: agricultural, rural, farming
反义词: industrial, urban
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin agrarius (“of fields or land”), from Latin ager (“field”).  The suffix -ize/-ise comes from Greek -ίζειν (-ízein), which forms verbs meaning "to make or become like".
