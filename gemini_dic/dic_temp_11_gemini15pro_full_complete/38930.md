confetto:/kənˈfɛtoʊ/ (plural confetti) | n. | 糖果，彩屑 | A small sweet, especially an almond coated with sugar; a sugared almond. Also (especially in plural) small pieces of colored paper thrown during celebrations.  | The bride and groom were showered with confetti as they left the church. 新郎新娘离开教堂时，人们向他们抛洒彩屑。 | Synonym: sweet, candy, sprinkle, spall, dragee | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain almond" in the singular sense) | Noun


confetti:/kənˈfɛti/ | n. | 彩屑，五彩纸屑 | Small pieces of colored paper thrown during celebrations. |  Confetti rained down on the parade. 彩屑飘落在游行队伍上。 | Synonym: sprinkles, streamers | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | Noun
