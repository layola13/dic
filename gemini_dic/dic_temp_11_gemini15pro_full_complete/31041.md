chamberlet: /ˈtʃeɪmbərlɪt/
n.
小房间，小卧室
A small chamber; a little room, especially a bedroom.
The chamberlet was cozy and perfect for a single guest.  这个小房间很舒适，非常适合单人客人入住。
The maid tidied the chamberlet each morning. 女佣每天早上都会整理小房间。
Synonym: cubicle, closet, small room, chamber
Antonym: hall, auditorium, large room
Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English chambrelet, diminutive of chambre (“chamber”), from Old French chambrette, diminutive of chambre, from Latin camera (“vault, arched roof, room”).
