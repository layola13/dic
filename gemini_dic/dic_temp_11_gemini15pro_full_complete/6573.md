anabaptize:/ˌænəˈbæptaɪz/ | vt. | 再洗礼；给…重新施洗 | to baptize again, especially an adult who was baptized as a child |  The preacher anabaptized the new converts. 传教士为新皈依者重新施洗。 | rebaptize |  N/A | verb

anabaptize:/ˌænəˈbæptaɪz/ | n. | 再洗礼主义者；主张再洗礼的人 | a member of a radical Protestant sect that arose in Europe during the Reformation, emphasizing adult baptism and rejecting infant baptism |  He was considered an anabaptize because he rejected the validity of infant baptism. 他被认为是再洗礼派教徒，因为他反对婴儿洗礼的有效性。 | rebaptizer |  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anabaptizare, from Greek anabaptizein, from ana- (“again”) + baptizein (“to baptize”).
