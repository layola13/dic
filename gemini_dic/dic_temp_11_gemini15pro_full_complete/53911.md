discourageable:/dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒəbl/
adj.
易灰心的，容易气馁的
Easily discouraged or disheartened; susceptible to losing confidence or enthusiasm.
The team was discourageable after their third consecutive loss.  这支队伍在连续三次失利后变得容易灰心。
He's a discourageable young man; he gives up too easily. 他是一个容易气馁的年轻人；他太容易放弃了。
近义词: disheartened, demoralized, easily discouraged, faint-hearted
反义词:  determined, resolute, persistent, tenacious
词性: adjective


discourage:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/
vt.
使灰心，使气馁；劝阻
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to prevent (something) by expressing disapproval or raising objections.
The rain discouraged us from going on a picnic.  下雨让我们打消了去野餐的念头。
The teacher discouraged the students from cheating on the exam. 老师劝阻学生不要在考试中作弊。
近义词: dissuade, deter, demoralize, dishearten
反义词: encourage, inspire, motivate, hearten
词性: verb


词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + courage + -able (forming adjectives).  来源于dis-（表示否定）+ courage（勇气）+ -able（构成形容词）。
