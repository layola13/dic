adjective
燃烧的；引起大火的
Relating to or causing a large destructive fire.
The conflagrative nature of the dry brush made it easy for the fire to spread.  干燥灌木丛易燃的特性使火势很容易蔓延。

近义词:incendiary, inflammatory
反义词:fire-resistant, nonflammable
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin conflagratus, past participle of conflagrare (“to burn completely”), from con- (“completely”) + flagrare (“to burn”).
