lignitic:/lɪɡˈnɪtɪk/
adjective
褐煤的；含褐煤的
Relating to, or resembling lignite; consisting of lignite.
Lignitic coal is a soft, brownish-black coal. 褐煤是一种柔软的棕黑色煤。
The power plant burns lignitic coal.  这家发电厂燃烧褐煤。
近义词:brown coal
反义词:anthracite, bituminous
词性:adjective
