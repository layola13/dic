euthanasiast: /ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒɪəst/ | n. |  安乐死支持者/实施者 | A person who believes in or practices euthanasia. |  He was a known euthanasiast and advocated for the right to die. 他是一位知名的安乐死支持者，并倡导死亡权。 | synonyms:  proponent of euthanasia, advocate of assisted suicide | antonyms: opponent of euthanasia | noun
