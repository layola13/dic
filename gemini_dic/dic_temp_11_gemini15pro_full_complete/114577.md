malaysian:/məˈleɪʒən/ /məˈleɪziən/| adj. n.|马来西亚的；马来西亚人|Relating to Malaysia, its people, or their language.  A person from Malaysia, or of Malaysian descent.|例句：She is a Malaysian citizen. 她是一位马来西亚公民。
