cutting-edge:/ˈkʌtɪŋ ɛdʒ/| adj.|尖端的，前沿的，最先进的|The most advanced or innovative stage of development in a particular field.  Relating to or representing the most advanced developments in a particular field.|This company is known for its cutting-edge technology. 这家公司以其尖端技术而闻名。|Synonyms: advanced, innovative, state-of-the-art, leading-edge, pioneering|Antonyms: outdated, obsolete, antiquated, backward, traditional|adjective
