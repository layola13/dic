overskirt: /ˈoʊvərˌskɜːrt/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌskɜːt/ (UK)
n.
长罩裙，外裙
An outer skirt worn over another skirt.
She wore a beautiful overskirt over her silk gown. 她穿着一件漂亮的罩裙套在她丝绸长袍外面。

The queen's overskirt was embroidered with gold thread.  女王的长罩裙绣着金线。

近义词: tunic, overdress, drapery
反义词: underskirt, petticoat
词性: noun

词源:  From "over" + "skirt".  "Over" indicating position above or outside, and "skirt" referring to the garment.  The word signifies a skirt worn over another.
