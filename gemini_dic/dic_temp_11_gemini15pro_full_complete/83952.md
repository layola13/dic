half-conscious:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəs/ | /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəs/ | adj. | 半清醒的；神志不清的 | Partly conscious; not fully awake or aware. | The patient was half-conscious after the surgery.  手术后病人处于半昏迷状态。|  Near-synonyms: semiconscious, dazed, groggy, sleepy, drowsy | Antonyms: conscious, alert, awake, fully aware | adjective

half-conscious:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəs/ | /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəs/ | adj. | 模糊地意识到的 | Vaguely or dimly aware. |  He was half-conscious of the noise outside. 他模模糊糊地听到了外面的噪音。 | Near-synonyms:  partly aware, vaguely aware | Antonyms: fully aware, oblivious | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "conscious". "Half" meaning one of two equal parts, from Old English "healf". "Conscious" from Late Latin "conscius" meaning "knowing with others, privy to,"  from "com-" (together) + "scire" (to know).
