acquiescingly: /ˌækwiˈɛsɪŋli/ | adv. | 顺从地，默许地 | in a way that shows you agree with something or accept it even if you do not want to |  She acquiescingly agreed to his request, though she had other plans. 她顺从地同意了他的请求，尽管她另有安排。|  He acquiescingly nodded his head, even though he disagreed. 即使他不同意，他还是顺从地点了点头。|Synonyms: submissively, compliantly, obediently | Antonyms: defiantly, rebelliously, resisting | Word Type: Adverb
