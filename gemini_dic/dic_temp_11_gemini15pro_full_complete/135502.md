nonphysicians:/ˌnɒn fɪˈzɪʃənz/ (US) /ˌnɒn fɪˈzɪʃənz/ (UK)
noun
非医生；非内科医生
People who are not licensed medical doctors.  This term encompasses a wide range of healthcare professionals, such as nurses, physician assistants, therapists, and technicians.
Many nonphysicians play crucial roles in patient care. 许多非医生在病人护理中扮演着至关重要的角色。
Nonphysicians, such as nurses and physician assistants, are essential members of the healthcare team.  非医生，例如护士和医生助理，是医疗保健团队的重要成员。
Synonyms: non-doctors, paramedical staff
Antonyms: physicians, doctors
词性: 名词
