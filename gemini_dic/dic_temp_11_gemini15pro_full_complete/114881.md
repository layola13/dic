mammoths:/ˈmæməθs/
n.
猛犸象（复数）
Extinct elephant-like mammal of the Pleistocene epoch, typically hairy with a sloping back and long curved tusks.
例句：Mammoths roamed the earth during the Ice Age. 猛犸象在冰河时代漫游在地球上。

例句：Scientists are studying the DNA of mammoths. 科学家正在研究猛犸象的DNA。

近义词：mastodon, behemoth (figurative)
反义词：(No direct antonym, as it's an extinct species.  Could use "small creature" for figurative contrast)
词性：noun
