mesmerization:/ˌmezməraɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 迷惑；催眠；着迷 |The act of mesmerizing someone; the state of being mesmerized.  The process of captivating or enthralling someone completely. |  The magician's performance was a mesmerizing display of skill and illusion. 魔术师的表演是对技巧和幻觉的迷人展示。| synonyms: fascination, captivation, enchantment | antonyms: boredom, disinterest | noun

mesmerize:/ˈmezməraɪz/ | vt. | 使着迷；催眠；迷惑 |To hold the attention of (someone) to the exclusion of all else or so as to transfix them.  To hypnotize or fascinate. | The swirling patterns mesmerized the audience. 旋转的图案使观众着迷。| synonyms: captivate, enthrall, hypnotize | antonyms: bore, repel, disenchant | verb
