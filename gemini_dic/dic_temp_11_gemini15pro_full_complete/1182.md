acclimatizable:/əˈklaɪməˌtaɪzəbl/
adjective
可适应气候的; 可驯化的
Capable of becoming acclimatized; adaptable to a new climate or environment.
The plant is highly acclimatizable and can thrive in various temperature ranges. 这种植物适应性很强，可以在不同的温度范围内茁壮成长。
Synonyms: adaptable, adjustable, malleable
Antonyms: unadaptable, inflexible, rigid
词性: adjective


acclimatize:/əˈklaɪməˌtaɪz/
verb
适应气候; 驯化
To become accustomed to a new climate or environment.
It took me a while to acclimatize to the high altitude. 我花了一段时间才适应高海拔地区。
Synonyms: adapt, adjust, accommodate
Antonyms: unadapt
词性: verb
