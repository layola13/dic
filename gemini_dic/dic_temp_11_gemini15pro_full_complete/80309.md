goldbeckian: /ɡəʊldˈbɛkiən/ | adj. |戈德贝克式的，戈德贝克风格的| Relating to or characteristic of the architectural style of Walter Goldbeck (1882-1961), particularly his use of reinforced concrete. | The Goldbeckian design of the factory emphasized functionality and durability. 这家工厂的戈德贝克式设计强调功能性和耐用性。|  Synonyms: concrete, modernist, functional | Antonyms: ornate, traditional, decorative | adjective
