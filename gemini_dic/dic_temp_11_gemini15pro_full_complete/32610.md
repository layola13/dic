chirpier:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪər/ | adj. | 更活泼的，更快乐的 |Comparative form of chirpy: more chirpy.  Showing or characterized by a lively and cheerful manner. | She seemed much chirpier after her vacation. 她度假回来后显得更加活泼快乐。|  Synonyms: more cheerful, more buoyant, perkier, brighter, sunnier | Antonyms: gloomier, more depressed, more melancholic | adjective

chirpy:/ˈtʃɜːrpi/ | adj. | 活泼的，快乐的 | Characterized by a lively and cheerful manner. |  He had a chirpy personality. 他性格活泼。 | Synonyms: cheerful, buoyant, perky, bright, sunny | Antonyms: gloomy, depressed, melancholic | adjective
