auricle: /ˈɔːrɪk(ə)l/ (US) /ˈɔːrɪkl/ (UK)
n.
耳廓；心房
the externally visible cartilaginous structure of the external ear; the atrium of the heart.
The auricle collects sound waves and directs them into the ear canal. 耳廓收集声波并将其导入耳道。
The left auricle receives oxygenated blood from the lungs. 左心房接收来自肺部的含氧血液。
Synonym: pinna (for external ear), atrium (for heart)
Antonym: ventricle (for heart)
noun
