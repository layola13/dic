organisations:/ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənz/ /ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənz/
| n. |组织；机构；团体|A group of people who work together in a structured way to achieve a particular aim.|例句：Many international organisations are working to improve global health. 许多国际组织正在努力改善全球健康。|例句：The two organisations merged to form a larger company. 这两个组织合并成了一家更大的公司。|近义词：institutions, groups, associations, bodies|反义词:individual, disorder|词性:名词


organisation:/ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |组织；机构；团体; 组织工作; 安排; 筹备|The action of organizing something.|例句：The organisation of the event took months of planning.  这次活动的组织工作花了几个月的时间来计划。|例句：The wedding organisation was flawless. 婚礼的安排完美无瑕。|近义词：arrangement, planning, structuring, formation|反义词:disorder, chaos|词性:名词
