Etymology: From Old English *brēþan (attested only in derived forms), from Proto-Germanic *brēþaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰés- (“to breathe, blow”). Cognate with Old Saxon brēthan, Old High German brātan, Old Norse blása. Related to blast.
