incorrespondence:/ˌɪnkɔːrəˈspɑːndəns/
noun
不一致,不符,不协调
lack of agreement or harmony
The figures are in incorrespondence with the facts. 这些数字与事实不符。
近义词:disagreement, discord, disparity, variance
反义词:agreement, correspondence, harmony, conformity
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- "not" + correspondence "agreement."
