mandiocas:/ˌmændiˈoʊkəz/ | n. | 木薯（复数） | The plural form of mandioca, a starchy tuberous root of a tropical tree, used as food in tropical countries.  It is the source of tapioca. | Cassava, also known as manioc and mandioca, is a starchy root vegetable that is a major source of carbohydrates for people in many parts of the world, particularly in Africa, Asia, and South America.  Mandiocas are a staple food in many developing countries. 木薯，也称为树薯和木薯，是一种淀粉根茎类蔬菜，是世界许多地区，尤其是非洲、亚洲和南美洲人民的主要碳水化合物来源。木薯是许多发展中国家的主食。 | 近义词：cassavas, yuccas | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
