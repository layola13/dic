exotery:/ˈɛksəˌtɛri/ | n. | 外传；公开的教义；通俗易懂的知识 |  The doctrine or practice of what is generally known or accessible, as opposed to esotery. |  Many ancient philosophies had an exoteric dimension for the general public and an esoteric one reserved for initiates.  许多古代哲学都对外行人有一个外传维度，而对入门者有一个秘传维度。 | synonyms: popularization, vulgarization | antonyms: esotery, mystery, arcane | noun
