co-star:/ˈkoʊ stɑːr/ (US) /ˈkəʊ stɑːr/ (UK)| n. v.|联合主演；共同出演|A performer who shares a leading role with another actor or actress. |例句：She co-starred with Tom Hanks in the movie. 她和汤姆·汉克斯共同主演了这部电影。|例句：He co-starred with her in several Broadway plays. 他和她一起主演过几部百老汇戏剧。|近义词：supporting actor, featured player|反义词：lead, star|词性：noun, verb
