coble:/ˈkəʊb(ə)l/| n. |小渔船，平底船|A small flat-bottomed fishing boat.|例句：The coble was pulled up onto the beach. 这艘小渔船被拉上了海滩。

例句：Fishermen used cobles to catch herring. 渔民们用平底小渔船捕鲱鱼。


近义词：boat, skiff, dinghy|反义词:|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cobil, from Old North French coble, caupble, from Medieval Latin caupulus, diminutive of Latin caupolus (“small boat, skiff”), alteration of scaphula, diminutive of scapha (“skiff, small boat”), from Ancient Greek σκάφη (skáphē, “anything dug or scooped out; trough, bowl, skiff”).
