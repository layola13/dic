The lawyer argued that the police search was nongratuitous, as they had probable cause. 律师辩称，警方的搜查并非无故，因为他们有合理的理由。 | 近义词: justified, warranted, reasonable, motivated | 反义词: gratuitous, unnecessary, unwarranted, unjustified | 词性: adjective
