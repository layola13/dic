navigators:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtərz/| n. |领航员，航海家，导航器|A person who plans and directs the route or course of a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle, especially by using instruments and maps. |例句：The navigators used the stars to guide their ships across the ocean. 领航员利用星星引导他们的船只穿越海洋。

例句：Modern aircraft rely on sophisticated navigators for accurate flight paths. 现代飞机依靠精密的导航器来确定精确的飞行路线。|近义词：pilot, guide, helmsman, explorer|反义词:passenger, follower|词性:noun
