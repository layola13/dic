bahadur:/bəˈhɑːdʊr/| n. adj.|勇士，勇敢的|A title of honor, often used in India, signifying "brave" or "heroic."  It can also be used as an adjective to describe someone courageous.  Derived from Persian بهادر (bahādur), meaning "brave," "valiant," or "hero."|例句：He was known as Bahadur for his fearless actions in battle. 他因在战斗中的无畏行为而被称为勇士。

|例句：The bahadur knight charged into the fray. 勇敢的骑士冲进了战斗。|近义词：hero, champion, valiant, courageous, brave|反义词:cowardly, timid|词性:noun, adjective
