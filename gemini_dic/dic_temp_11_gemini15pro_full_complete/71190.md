fisticuffs:/ˈfɪstɪkʌfs/| n.|互殴，拳斗|A fight with the fists.|They settled their argument with fisticuffs.|Synonyms: brawl, fight, scrap, scuffle|Antonyms: peace, truce, agreement|noun

Etymology:
From fist + -i- + cuff + -s.  "Cuff" here refers to a blow with the hand.  So, literally, blows with fists.
