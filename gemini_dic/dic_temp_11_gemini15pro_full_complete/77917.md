genitive:/ˈdʒenɪtɪv/| adj. n.|属格的，表示所属关系的；属格|Of, relating to, or designating a case that expresses possession, origin, or source.|The genitive case in English is typically marked by 's or of.  英语中的属格通常用 's 或 of 表示。|The possessive form "John's book" is an example of the genitive case.  所有格形式“John's book”是属格的一个例子。|近义词：possessive|反义词: nominative|词性:形容词,名词
