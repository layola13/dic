indisciplines:/ˌɪnˈdɪsəplɪnz/ | n. | 缺乏纪律；不守纪律的行为（通常用复数形式） |  Lack of discipline; acts of not following rules or guidelines.  Usually used in the plural form. |  His constant indisciplines led to his expulsion from school. 他不断的违纪行为导致他被学校开除。|Synonyms: misbehaviors, misconduct, disobedience | Antonyms: discipline, obedience, compliance | noun

indiscipline:/ɪnˈdɪsəplɪn/ | n. | 缺乏纪律，不守纪律 | Lack of control, order, or adherence to rules. | The teacher struggled to control the indiscipline in the classroom. 老师努力控制课堂上的纪律混乱。| Synonyms: disorder, chaos, lawlessness | Antonyms: discipline, order, control | noun
