digestively:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪvli/
adjective
消化地；与消化有关地
Relating to digestion, the process of breaking down food in the body.
Eating slowly and chewing your food thoroughly aids digestion digestively. 细嚼慢咽有助于消化。
近义词:gastrically, intestinally
反义词:None
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "digestive" + "-ly." "Digestive" comes from Late Latin *digestīvus*, from Latin digerere "to separate, distribute, arrange, digest," from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear."
