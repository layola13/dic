histrionicism:/ˌhɪstriˈɒnɪsɪzəm/ | n. |  装腔作势；戏剧性；表演性 | Exaggerated dramatic behavior designed to attract attention. | Her histrionicism was evident in her loud sobs and dramatic pronouncements. 她的装腔作势在她响亮的哭泣和戏剧性的声明中表现得很明显。|  例句：He displayed a great deal of histrionicism after the minor accident.  他在轻微事故后表现得非常夸张。| 近义词：dramatics, theatricality, melodrama, affectation | 反义词：understatement, reserve, restraint | 词性: 名词

histrionic:/ˌhɪstriˈɒnɪk/ | adj. |  做作的；装腔作势的；戏剧性的 | Overly theatrical or melodramatic in character or style. |  She had a histrionic outburst when she didn't get her way. 她没能如愿以偿时，就大发脾气。| 例句：His histrionic reaction to the news was embarrassing. 他对这个消息的夸张反应令人尴尬。 | 近义词：dramatic, theatrical, melodramatic, affected | 反义词：understated, reserved, restrained | 词性: 形容词
