deniably:/dɪˈnaɪəbli/| adv.|不可否认地，无可争辩地|In a way that cannot be denied; undoubtedly.|Her performance was undeniably brilliant. 她的表演无可否认地精彩。|近义词：undoubtedly, indisputably, unquestionably|反义词：questionably, arguably, disputably|词性:副词
词源: From deny + -able + -ly.  "Deny" comes from Old French "denier"  ultimately from Latin "denegare" (to refuse, deny), itself from "de-" (away from) + "negare" (to say no).
