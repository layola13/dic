fortunate:/ˈfɔːrtʃənət/ (US) /ˈfɔːtʃənət/ (UK)| adj.|幸运的，侥幸的|Bringing good luck; lucky.  Characterized by or resulting from good fortune.|She was fortunate to escape injury in the accident. 她很幸运，在事故中没有受伤。|Synonyms: lucky, blessed, providential, auspicious|Antonyms: unfortunate, unlucky, hapless, ill-fated|adjective
