lowest-common-denominator:/ˈloʊɪst ˈkɑːmən dɪˈnɑːmɪneɪtər/ |n.| 最小公分母;  最大公约数 (比喻); 最低标准; 平庸的诉求| something of low quality or intelligence that is designed to appeal to a large number of people.  It can refer to a literal mathematical concept, or, more commonly, a figurative one, representing the lowest level of taste or opinion. | Politicians often appeal to the lowest common denominator to attract the most votes. 政客们经常迎合最普遍的诉求以吸引最多的选票。 | least common multiple, greatest common divisor (in math); base level, common ground (figurative) | highest common factor (in math); sophisticated, refined (figurative) | noun
