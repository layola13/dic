boon:/buːn/
n.|恩惠，福利，好处|something that is helpful or beneficial; a blessing or advantage|The good weather was a boon to the farmers.|近义词：blessing, advantage, benefit, gift, windfall|反义词：bane, curse, disadvantage, hardship|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old Norse *bón "a petition, prayer," from Proto-Germanic *bōniz (source also of Old English bēn "prayer, petition, request," Old High German bōn "prayer, request"). The sense shifted from "something prayed for" to "a thing granted in answer to prayer" (mid-14c.), then to "benefit, favor" (late 14c.).
