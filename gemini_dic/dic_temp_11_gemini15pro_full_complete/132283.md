nondefenses:/ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛnsɪz/ | n. | 无防御；不设防；缺乏防御|  Lack of defensive measures or strategies; vulnerability. | The team's nondefenses led to a crushing defeat.  该队缺乏防守导致惨败。| 近义词: vulnerability, weakness, exposure | 反义词: defenses, protection, security | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "defenses" (plural of "defense").  由表示“不”的前缀"non-"和名词"defense"的复数形式"defenses"构成。
