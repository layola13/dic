outscores:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrz/
verb
超过，胜过
To score more points or goals than (an opponent).
The Lakers outscoring the Warriors in the 4th quarter. 湖人队在第四节超过了勇士队的得分。
outperforms, surpasses, beats, exceeds
is outscored by, loses to, trails
