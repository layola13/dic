overshrinking:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ/
|adj.|过度缩水，过度收缩|The act of shrinking something excessively, typically fabric.|The wool sweater overshrunk in the hot wash.  羊毛衫在热水洗涤中过度缩水了。|Synonyms: overcontracting, excessively shrinking|Antonyms: stretching, expanding|adjective
overshrinks:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋks/
|v.|过度缩水，过度收缩|(third-person singular simple present overshrinks, present participle overshrinking, simple past and past participle overshrunk)|To shrink something excessively.|He overshrinks all his clothes by putting them in the dryer on high heat. 他把所有衣服都用高温烘干，导致过度缩水。|Synonyms: overcontracts, shrinks excessively|Antonyms: stretches, expands|verb
overshrunk:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋk/
|adj.|过度缩水，过度收缩的|(past participle of overshrink)|Having shrunk excessively.|My favorite shirt is now overshrunk and unwearable. 我最喜欢的衬衫现在缩水太厉害，不能穿了。|Synonyms: overcontracted, excessively shrunk|Antonyms: stretched, expanded|adjective
