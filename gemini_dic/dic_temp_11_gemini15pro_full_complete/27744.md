canker:/ˈkæŋkər/ | n. | 溃疡; 弊病; 害虫 |A destructive fungal disease of trees and other plants that causes localized lesions. A sore in the mouth or on other mucous membrane. Something that corrodes, corrupts, or destroys. | The canker on the apple tree spread quickly. 苹果树上的溃疡迅速蔓延。| synonyms: ulcer, sore, lesion, blight, disease, corruption | antonyms: health, well-being | noun

canker:/ˈkæŋkər/ | v. | 溃烂; 腐蚀; 败坏 | To become infected with a canker. To corrode, corrupt, or destroy. |  Resentment cankered their relationship. 怨恨腐蚀了他们的关系。| synonyms: corrode, erode, corrupt, fester, decay | antonyms: heal, restore, improve | verb
