neglectfully:/nɪˈɡlɛktfəli/
adverb
疏忽地，忽略地
in a neglectful manner; showing or characterized by neglect.
He neglectfully left the gate open, and the dog ran away. 他疏忽地忘记关门，结果狗跑了。
She tossed her coat neglectfully onto the chair. 她漫不经心地把外套扔到椅子上。
近义词: carelessly, heedlessly, irresponsibly, negligently
反义词: carefully, attentively, responsibly, diligently
词性: adverb

词源: From neglect + -ful + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *neglegere "disregard," from nec "not" + legere "gather, choose, read."
