gainer:/ˈɡeɪnər/| n.|获得者；增益器；盈利股票；(体操、跳水)向前翻腾一周|A gainer is someone or something that gains or earns something, especially profit or an advantage. In gymnastics and diving, it refers to a forward somersault or dive.|He was a big gainer in the stock market last year. 去年他在股市上获利颇丰。|The diver performed a beautiful gainer.  跳水运动员完成了一个漂亮的向前翻腾一周。|Synonyms: winner, beneficiary, profiteer|Antonyms: loser|noun

gainer:/ˈɡeɪnər/| n.|增重的人；增肥食品|Refers to a person who is intentionally gaining weight, often in the context of bodybuilding. It can also refer to a food or supplement designed to help with weight gain.|He's been using a gainer protein shake to bulk up. 他一直在用增肌蛋白奶昔来增重。|This gainer supplement is packed with calories.  这款增肥补充剂富含卡路里。|Synonyms: weight gainer, bulker|Antonyms: dieter, slimmer|noun
