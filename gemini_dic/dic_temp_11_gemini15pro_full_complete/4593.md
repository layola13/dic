alcoholometry:/ˌælkəˈhɒləmɪtri/| n. |酒精测量法；酒精测定法|The process of determining the proportion of alcohol in a liquid.  It involves measuring the specific gravity or density of the liquid, as the presence of alcohol affects these properties.|Alcoholometry is essential for determining the strength of alcoholic beverages. 酒精测量法对于确定酒精饮料的浓度至关重要。

The distillery uses precise alcoholometry to ensure consistent product quality.  这家酿酒厂使用精确的酒精测量法来确保产品质量始终如一。|近义词：alcohol testing, alcohol measurement|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  From French *alcoo(l)ométrie*, from *alcool* (“alcohol”) + *-métrie* (“-metry”).  Ultimately from Arabic *الكُحُول* (*al-kuḥūl*, “kohl”) + Ancient Greek *-μετρία* (*-metria*, “process of measuring”).
