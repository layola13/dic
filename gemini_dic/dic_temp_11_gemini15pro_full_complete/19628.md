bijou:/ˈbiːʒuː/ (US), /ˈbiːʒuː/ (UK)| n.| 小饰品；珍品；小巧精致之物|A small, delicate, and exquisitely wrought trinket, often jeweled.|She wore a simple black dress accented by a diamond bijou.|近义词: trinket, bauble, gem, jewel, ornament|反义词: monstrosity, eyesore|词性:名词

词源: From French bijou, ultimately from Breton bizou ("finger ring").
