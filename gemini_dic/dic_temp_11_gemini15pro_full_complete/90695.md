humdrumness:/hʌmˈdrʌmnɪs/
noun
单调乏味；平淡无奇
The state of being humdrum; monotonous routine.
The humdrumness of his life was starting to get to him.  他生活的单调乏味开始让他感到沮丧。
monotony, dullness, tediousness
excitement, thrill, stimulation
