houseful:/ˈhaʊsfʊl/| n. |客满；满座；满屋子的人|A houseful of people means that a house is full of people.  It suggests a large gathering, often implying a lively or crowded atmosphere.|例句：We had a houseful of guests for Thanksgiving.  我们感恩节时家里挤满了客人。|例句：The party was a success, with a houseful of laughing children.  派对很成功，满屋子都是欢笑的孩子。|近义词：packed, crowded, full|反义词:empty, vacant|词性:名词
