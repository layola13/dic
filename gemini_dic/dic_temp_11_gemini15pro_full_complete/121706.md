misprescribed:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪbd/
adjective
开错药方的；处方错误的
incorrectly prescribed.
例句：The patient suffered adverse effects from the misprescribed medication. 病人因处方错误的药物遭受了不良反应。

例句：Misprescribed antibiotics can contribute to antibiotic resistance.  滥用抗生素会导致抗生素耐药性。

synonym: wrongly prescribed, incorrectly prescribed
antonym: correctly prescribed, appropriately prescribed
词性: adjective
