bobolink: /ˈbɑːbəˌlɪŋk/ | n. | 燕雀, 巴布灵克鸟 | A migratory American songbird (Dolichonyx oryzivorus) of the family Icteridae, the male of which has striking black and white plumage in the breeding season. It is known for its cheerful, bubbling song. |  The bobolink's song filled the meadow. 燕雀的歌声充满了草地。  The male bobolink is easily recognizable by its distinctive plumage. 雄性燕雀很容易通过其独特的羽毛被认出来。 | 近义词: meadowlark, songbird | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) | 词性: noun
