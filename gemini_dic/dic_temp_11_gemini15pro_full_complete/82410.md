grudging:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋ/| adj.|吝啬的，不情愿的，勉强的|Given, granted, or allowed reluctantly or resentfully. |He gave a grudging apology. 他很不情愿地道歉了。|近义词：reluctant, unwilling, resentful|反义词：willing, enthusiastic, eager|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *grucchen*, from Old North French *groucier*, *grocier* (“to grumble, murmur”), from Vulgar Latin *grūtiāre* (“to murmur, grumble”), from Latin *grūtia* (“dregs, sediment”).
