impulse:/ˈɪmpʌls/ (US), /ˈɪmpʌls/ (UK)| n.|冲动，脉冲，刺激|A sudden strong and unreflective urge or desire to act.|On a sudden impulse, I bought a plane ticket to Hawaii. 我一时冲动买了一张去夏威夷的机票。|Synonyms: urge, whim, instinct, drive|Antonyms: hesitation, premeditation, deliberation|noun

impulse:/ɪmˈpʌls/ (US), /ɪmˈpʌls/ (UK)| vt.|推动，激励|To drive or urge forward; to impel.|The new engine impulses the car forward with greater speed. 新引擎推动汽车以更快的速度前进。|Synonyms: propel, drive, motivate|Antonyms: restrain, hinder, inhibit|verb
