nimby:/ˈnɪmbi/ | n. |邻避症患者；邻避主义者|A person who objects to the siting of something perceived as unpleasant or potentially dangerous in their own neighborhood, such as a landfill or a nuclear power plant, while raising no such objections to similar developments elsewhere.  |  The NIMBYs protested the construction of the new homeless shelter. 邻避症患者抗议建造新的流浪汉收容所。|Synonyms: parochialist, localist | Antonyms: cosmopolitan, globalist | noun

nimbys:/ˈnɪmbiz/ | n. |邻避症患者；邻避主义者（复数）|Plural form of NIMBY.  | The nimbys blocked the development of the wind farm. 邻避症患者阻止了风力发电场的开发。|Synonyms: parochialists, localists | Antonyms: cosmopolitans, globalists | noun
