doleful: /ˈdoʊlfəl/ (US), /ˈdəʊlfʊl/ (UK)| adj. | 悲哀的；忧郁的 | expressing sorrow; mournful |  She gave a doleful sigh. 她悲哀地叹了口气。

The dog gave a doleful whine. 那条狗发出一声悲哀的呜咽。

近义词: mournful, sorrowful, sad, melancholy, gloomy, depressed, dejected, downcast

反义词: cheerful, happy, joyful, merry, lighthearted, upbeat

词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *doleful, from dole (“grief, sorrow”) + -ful.  Ultimately from Old French doel, from Latin dolus (“pain, grief”).
