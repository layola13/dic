moonwalkers:/ˈmuːnwɔːkərz/
n.
月球漫步者；进行月球漫步的人
People who walk or seem to walk on the moon. Originally referring to astronauts, it later became associated with Michael Jackson's dance move.
Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin were the first moonwalkers.  尼尔·阿姆斯特朗和巴兹·奥尔德林是最早的月球漫步者。

The dancers amazed the audience with their impressive moonwalks.  舞者们以令人印象深刻的月球漫步让观众惊叹。

近义词: astronauts (specifically for those who walked on the moon), dancers (for those performing the dance move)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
