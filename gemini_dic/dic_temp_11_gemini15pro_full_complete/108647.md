leafcutter:/ˈliːfˌkʌtər/| n.|切叶蚁|An ant of tropical America and the southern US that cuts and carries pieces of leaves back to its nest, where they are used to cultivate fungi for food.|Leafcutter ants can defoliate a tree in a single night. 切叶蚁可以在一夜之间让一棵树落叶。|Synonyms:  ant,  parasol ant |Antonyms: N/A (Antonyms for an animal are not usually applicable)|noun


leafcutter:/ˈliːfˌkʌtər/| adj.|切叶的|Relating to or characteristic of leafcutter ants.|Leafcutter ant jaws are incredibly strong. 切叶蚁的颚非常强壮。|Synonyms:  leaf-cutting |Antonyms: N/A|adjective
