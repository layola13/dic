keelvat: /ˈkiːlvæt/ | n. | 平底驳船; 平底货船 | A keelvat is a type of flat-bottomed barge or cargo boat, typically used in canals or shallow waters. |  The keelvat transported goods along the canal. 平底驳船沿着运河运送货物。 |Synonyms: barge, lighter, flatboat | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'ship' for contrasting deep-water vessels) | noun

Etymology:  Likely from Dutch *kielvat, combining *kiel "keel" and *vat "vat, tub".  The term describes the vessel's shape resembling an upturned vat or tub with a keel.
