instinctual: /ɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəl/ /ɪnˈstɪŋktʃəwəl/
adjective
本能的；直觉的
Relating to or based on instinct; arising from impulse rather than conscious thought.
A mother’s instinctual love for her child is powerful.  母亲对孩子的本能的爱是强大的。
Birds have an instinctual drive to build nests. 鸟类有一种筑巢的本能驱动力。
synonyms: innate, inherent, instinctive, visceral, intuitive
antonyms: learned, acquired, reasoned, conscious, deliberate
词性: 形容词
