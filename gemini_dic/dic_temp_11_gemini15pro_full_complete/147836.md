overpunishment:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃmənt/
n.
过度惩罚，过分处罚
The act of punishing someone too harshly or severely for an offense.
The child received overpunishment for a minor infraction.  这个孩子因为一个小的违规行为受到了过度的惩罚。
Overpunishment can lead to resentment and rebellious behavior. 过度惩罚会导致怨恨和叛逆行为。
近义词: excessive punishment, disproportionate punishment
反义词: leniency, underpunishment
词性: 名词
