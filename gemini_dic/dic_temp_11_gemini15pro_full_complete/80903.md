governorships:/ˈɡʌvərnərʃɪps/
n.
总督职位；州长职位
The position or office of a governor.  The period during which one holds the position of governor.
She held two governorships during her career.  她在职业生涯中担任过两个州的州长。
synonyms: governorship, statehouse, captaincy, administration
antonyms:  subjection, dependency, subordination
noun
