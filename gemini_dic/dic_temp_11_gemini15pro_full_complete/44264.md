croaker:/ˈkroʊkər/| n. | 抱怨者；呱呱叫的动物（尤指蛙类）；旧式电话 |A person who complains habitually; an animal, especially a frog, that makes croaking sounds; an old-fashioned telephone.|He's such a croaker, always complaining about something. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨个不停。|Synonyms: complainer, grumbler, whiner|Antonyms: optimist, enthusiast|noun

croak:/kroʊk/| n. vi. vt.|呱呱叫；发出低沉沙哑的声音；死亡；告密；<俚>杀死|The deep hoarse sound made by a frog or a crow; to utter a low hoarse sound; to die; to inform (on someone) to the authorities; <slang> to kill|The frogs croaked loudly in the pond. 池塘里的青蛙大声呱呱叫。|Synonyms: groan, rasp, complain, die, inform|Antonyms: whisper, live, be silent|noun, verb, transitive verb, intransitive verb
