bifocals: /baɪˈfoʊkəlz/ | n. | 双光眼镜 | Eyeglasses with lenses that have two different sections, one for seeing close up and one for seeing far away. |  She needs bifocals to read and drive. 她需要双光眼镜才能阅读和开车。| My grandmother started wearing bifocals when she turned sixty. 我祖母六十岁时开始戴双光眼镜。 | 近义词: multifocal lenses, progressive lenses | 反义词: single-vision lenses | 词性: noun

Etymology: bi- (two) + focal (relating to a focus)
