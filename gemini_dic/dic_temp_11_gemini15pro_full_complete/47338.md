de-shelling:/diːˈʃɛlɪŋ/
noun
去壳; 脫殼
The act of removing the shell from something, typically nuts or shellfish.
例句：The de-shelling of the peanuts was done by a machine. 花生的去壳是由机器完成的。

例句：De-shelling the prawns before cooking saves time later.  在烹饪前将虾去壳可以节省以后的时间。

近义词：shelling, hulling, shucking
反义词：shelling (in the sense of adding a shell)
