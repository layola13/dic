overgo:/ˌoʊvərˈɡoʊ/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡəʊ/
verb
超过，胜过; 覆盖;  审查，检查; 经历，经受
To surpass or exceed; to cover completely; to examine or review; to undergo or experience.
The flood waters overwent the riverbanks. 洪水漫过了河岸。
His performance overgoes all expectations. 他的表现超出了所有人的预期。

overgo:/ˌoʊvərˈɡoʊ/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡəʊ/
verb
胜过
surpass, exceed, excel, outdo, transcend
The beauty of the sunset overgoes description. 日落的美胜过任何描述。

overgo:/ˌoʊvərˈɡoʊ/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡəʊ/
verb
经历，经受
experience, undergo, endure, suffer
They had to overgo many hardships during the war. 战争期间他们不得不经历许多苦难。
The company overwent a major restructuring. 这家公司经历了一次重大的重组。

overgo:/ˌoʊvərˈɡoʊ/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡəʊ/
verb
覆盖
cover, overlay, overspread
The field was overgone with snow. 田野被雪覆盖了。
A thick fog overwent the city. 一层浓雾笼罩着城市。


近义词: surpass, exceed, transcend, outstrip, eclipse
反义词: fall short of, be inferior to
