fidgets:/ˈfɪdʒɪts/| n. | 坐立不安，烦躁不安 |A feeling of restlessness and an urge to move about.|He had the fidgets and couldn't sit still. 他坐立不安，无法安静地坐着。|近义词：restlessness, unease, anxiety |反义词:calmness, tranquility| noun


fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| v. | 烦躁不安，坐立不安 |To make small movements, especially of the hands and feet, through nervousness or impatience.|She fidgeted nervously with her pen. 她紧张地摆弄着她的笔。|近义词：squirm, twitch, wriggle |反义词:relax, be still| verb
