biphasic:/baɪˈfeɪzɪk/
adjective
双相的，两相的
Having two phases or stages.
This medication has a biphasic effect, initially stimulating and then depressing the central nervous system. 这种药物具有双相作用，最初刺激中枢神经系统，然后抑制中枢神经系统。

The biphasic sleep pattern of the elderly is well documented. 老年人的双相睡眠模式已有充分的文献记载。

近义词: two-phase, dual-phase
反义词: monophasic
词性: adjective
