lockin's:/ˈlɒkɪnz/ | n. |锁定；锁定效应；锁定状态 (通常指复数形式，表示多次发生的锁定或锁定的多个实例) | Lock-in is a situation where a customer is dependent on a vendor for products and services, unable to use another vendor without substantial switching costs. Lockin's refers to multiple instances of this phenomenon. |  Many companies offer "free" services initially, leading to lockin's later as users find it difficult to switch. 许多公司最初提供“免费”服务，导致用户后来发现难以转换，从而造成锁定。| lock-ins, dependencies, captive markets | freedom, flexibility | noun
