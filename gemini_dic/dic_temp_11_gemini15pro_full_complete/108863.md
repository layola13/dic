lechery: /ˈlɛtʃəri/ (US), /ˈlɛtʃəri/ (UK) | n. |  好色；淫荡；纵欲 | Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness. | He was known for his lechery and extravagant lifestyle. 他因好色和奢侈的生活方式而闻名。 | The king's lechery was legendary. 国王的淫乱行为是传奇。 |  synonyms: lust, lasciviousness, lewdness, debauchery, licentiousness | antonyms: chastity, purity, abstinence, modesty | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *lecherie*, from *lecher* "to lick," from Latin *lingere* "to lick, linger." The original sense referred to gluttony, but the meaning shifted to sexual indulgence.
