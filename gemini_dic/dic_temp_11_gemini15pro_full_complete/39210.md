conglaciation:/kənˌɡleɪʃiˈeɪʃən/ /kənˌɡleɪsiˈeɪʃn/ | n. |冰川作用；冰期；冻结|The formation of a large ice sheet that covers a significant portion of a continent or land mass.  It refers to both the process and the resulting state of being covered by glaciers.|The last period of widespread conglaciation ended about 10,000 years ago.  最后一次大范围冰期大约在一万年前结束。|Synonyms: glaciation, icing over|Antonyms: deglaciation, melting|noun
