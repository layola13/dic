beal:/biːl/| n.| (1)脓疱; 丘疹; (2)小麦, 大麦等谷物外壳上的芒 | A pustule or pimple; also, the husk or awn of barley, wheat, rye, or oats. | The farmer examined the beal of the wheat to assess its ripeness. 农民检查了小麦的芒以评估其成熟度。 | The doctor lanced the infected beal. 医生切开了感染的脓疱。 | 近义词:pustule, pimple, awn, husk | 反义词: N/A | 词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *bele, from Old English *bēle (“bellows, blister”), from Proto-Germanic *bēlō (“bellows, blister, bag”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰōl- (“to swell, blow”). Cognate with Scots beil (“boil, fester”), West Frisian bêl (“blister, boil”), Dutch buil (“boil, blister, bump”), German Beule (“bump, dent, bulge”), Swedish bälg (“bellows”), Icelandic belgur (“bellows”). More at bellows.
