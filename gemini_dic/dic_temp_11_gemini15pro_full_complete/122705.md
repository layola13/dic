moguled:/ˈmoʊɡəld/ | v. |  成为大亨，称霸，发迹  | to live and behave like a mogul; to dominate or become powerful in a particular field, especially business or entertainment. | He moguled his way to the top of the tech industry. 他在科技行业一路攀升，最终成为巨头。| synonyms: dominated, controlled, reigned, ruled, commanded | antonyms: served, followed, submitted, obeyed | verb

mogul:/ˈmoʊɡəl/ | n. | 大亨，巨头 |  an important or powerful person, especially in the film or media industry. |  He's a media mogul with a vast empire. 他是一位拥有庞大帝国的媒体大亨。| synonyms: tycoon, magnate, baron, industrialist | antonyms: underling, subordinate, employee | noun
