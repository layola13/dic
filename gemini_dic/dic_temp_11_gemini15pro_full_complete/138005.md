nookiest:/ˈnuːkiɪst/
adj.
最隐蔽的，最角落的
Having the most nooks; most secluded or hidden away.
例句：She found the nookiest spot in the library to read her book. 她找到了图书馆里最隐蔽的角落来读书。

例句：The cat curled up in the nookiest part of the sofa. 猫蜷缩在沙发最角落的地方。
近义词：secluded, hidden, tucked away, cozy, snug
反义词：exposed, open, public, central
词性: adjective
