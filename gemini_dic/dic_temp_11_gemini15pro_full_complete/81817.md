grev: /ɡrɛv/
noun
伯爵; (瑞典) 郡长
A count; a chief magistrate of a district in Sweden.
The grev welcomed the visitors to his castle.  伯爵欢迎来访者来到他的城堡。
Synonyms: count, earl, nobleman
Antonyms: commoner, peasant
词性: 名词
