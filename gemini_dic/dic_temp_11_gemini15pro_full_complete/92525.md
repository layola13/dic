hypotyposis:/haɪpəˈtaɪpəsɪs/| n.| 形象描述，逼真描绘 |A vivid, graphic description of something, often used in rhetoric to evoke a clear picture in the mind of the audience. It involves detailed descriptions of scenes, persons, or events. | The author's hypotyposis of the battle scene was so vivid that I could almost smell the gunpowder. 作者对战斗场景的形象描述如此生动，我几乎能闻到火药味。| synonyms: imagery, depiction, portrayal, representation | antonyms: abstract, summary, generalization | noun
