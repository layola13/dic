nonexploitation:/ˌnɒnˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非剥削; 不剥削 | The absence of exploitation; the practice of not taking unfair advantage of someone or something. |  The company prides itself on its policy of nonexploitation of its workers.  这家公司以其不剥削工人的政策而自豪。| 近义词: fairness, justice, equity, ethical treatment | 反义词: exploitation, abuse, mistreatment | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- + exploitation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "exploitation" comes from the Late Latin "exploitationem," meaning "a using up."
