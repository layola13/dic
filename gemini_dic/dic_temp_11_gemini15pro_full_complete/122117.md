mistraining:/ˌmɪsˈtreɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 错误训练，训练不当 | The mistraining of the algorithm led to inaccurate predictions.  算法的错误训练导致了不准确的预测。 | Example: The mistraining of the new recruits resulted in several accidents. 新兵的训练不当导致了几起事故。 | Synonyms: misinstruction, miseducation, bad training | Antonyms: proper training, correct training | noun

mistrained:/ˌmɪsˈtreɪnd/ | adj. | 训练不当的，训练错误的 | The mistrained dog bit the mailman. 这只训练不当的狗咬了邮递员。 | Example: The mistrained model produced biased results.  训练不当的模型产生了有偏差的结果。 | Synonyms: badly trained, improperly trained, poorly trained | Antonyms: well-trained, properly trained, correctly trained | adjective
