cried:/kraɪd/| v.|哭泣，叫喊，大声说| to produce tears as the result of strong emotions, such as unhappiness, pain, or anger; to shout or say something loudly| The little girl cried when she lost her toy. 小女孩丢了玩具就哭了。|  He cried out in pain. 他疼得大叫。| synonyms: wept, sobbed, bawled, yelled, shouted | antonyms: laughed, rejoiced | verb

cry:/kraɪ/| v. n.|哭，喊，叫声|  v. to produce tears as the result of strong emotions; to shout or say something loudly. n. a loud utterance; a shout, especially of pain or grief. | The baby cried for its mother. 婴儿哭着要妈妈。| There was a loud cry from the street. 街上传来一声大叫。| synonyms: weep, sob, yell, shout, wail (v.); shriek, shout, yell (n.) | antonyms: laugh, rejoice (v.); silence (n.) | verb, noun
