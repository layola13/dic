heteroclitical:/ˌhɛtərəˈklɪtɪkəl/
| adj. |变格不同的，变位的|Having different forms of declension or conjugation, especially having a nominative singular of one gender and the other cases of another.  Referring to words whose grammatical gender, declension, or conjugation is irregular. |The word "alumnus" is heteroclitical, as its plural is "alumni".  "校友"这个词是变格不同的，因为它的复数是"alumni"。| 近义词: anomalous, irregular | 反义词: regular | 词性: adjective

heteroclitical:/ˌhɛtərəˈklɪtɪkəl/
| n. |变格不同的词，变位的词|A word that is heteroclitical. |"Alumnus" is a heteroclitical. "校友"是一个变格不同的词。| 近义词: anomalism, irregularity | 反义词: regularity | 词性: noun
