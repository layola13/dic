hingi: /ˈhɪŋi/ | n. | 请求，恳求 (especially in Maori culture) | A request, plea, or supplication, particularly within the context of Maori culture. | Example: He made a hingi to the elders for their blessing. 他向长者请求他们的祝福。 | Example: The hingi for assistance was met with generosity.  对援助的请求得到了慷慨的回应。 | Synonym: request, plea, appeal, petition | Antonym: demand, command | noun
