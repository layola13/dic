impersonative:/ɪmˈpɜːsənətɪv/ | adj. |无人称的；非人称的|Of, relating to, or characterized by the use of an impersonal verb or construction.  Used of a verb or other construction having no personal subject or having as subject a word (such as *it*) that does not refer to any particular person, thing, or other entity.|It is snowing heavily. (It is an impersonal subject.) 雪下得很大。（It 是一个非人称主语）|近义词: impersonal|反义词: personal|词性:adjective

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
It often rains in the summer here.  这里夏天经常下雨。
It seems that he is not coming. 看起来他不会来了。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It is raining heavily now.  现在雨下得很大。
It is getting darker. 天越来越黑了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It has rained a lot recently. 最近雨下得很多。
It has been a long day.  今天过得好漫长。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
It has been raining all day.  雨下了一整天。
It has been snowing for hours.  已经下雪好几个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
It rained yesterday. 昨天下了雨。
It seemed colder than usual.  好像比平时冷。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
It was raining when I left. 我离开的时候正在下雨。
It was getting dark. 天渐渐黑了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
It had rained before I arrived.  在我到达之前已经下过雨了。
It had been snowing for hours before the power went out.  停电前已经下雪好几个小时了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
It had been raining for days before the flood.  洪水来临之前，已经下了好几天的雨。
It had been snowing all night.  下了一整夜的雪。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
It will rain tomorrow. 明天会下雨。
It will be cold tonight. 今晚会很冷。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
It will be raining this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候会下雨。
It will be getting colder later. 稍后会变冷。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
It will have rained by tomorrow.  到明天为止就会下过雨了。
It will have been snowing for a week by then. 到那时就下了一周的雪了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
It will have been raining for three days straight. 到那时将会连续下三天雨了。
It will have been snowing for days by the time we arrive.  到我们到达的时候将会已经下雪好几天了。


虚拟语气:
If it were to rain, the picnic would be cancelled.  如果下雨，野餐就会取消。
It is important that it be done correctly.  重要的是要正确地完成它。


被动语态:
It is said that he is a genius. 据说他是个天才。
It was reported that the accident was caused by heavy fog. 据报道，事故是由大雾引起的。


疑问句:
Is it raining?  下雨了吗？
Was it cold yesterday?  昨天冷吗？

否定句:
It is not raining.  没有下雨。
It wasn't snowing when I left. 我走的时候没有下雪。

词源: From Late Latin *impersonalis*, from Latin *in-* (not) + *persona* (person).
