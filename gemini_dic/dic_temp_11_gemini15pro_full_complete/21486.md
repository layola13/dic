blubberer:/ˈblʌbərər/ | n. | 爱哭的人，哭鼻子的人 | A person who cries easily and excessively. | The little blubberer started crying when he dropped his ice cream.  小哭包冰淇淋掉了就哭了起来。| 近义词: crybaby, whiner, sniveller | 反义词: stoic | 词性: 名词

blubberer:/ˈblʌbərər/ | n. | 鲸脂 | The fat of sea mammals, especially whales. |  Whales rely on blubber for insulation in cold waters. 鲸鱼依靠鲸脂在寒冷的水域保暖。| 近义词: fat, adipose tissue | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
