microscopical: /ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪkəl/ (British) /ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑːpɪkəl/ (American)
|adj.|微观的；只能用显微镜看到的|Relating to or involving a microscope; so small as to be visible only with a microscope.|The bacteria were microscopical and could only be seen with a powerful lens. 细菌是微观的，只有用高倍透镜才能看到。|The intricate details of the carving were microscopical.  雕刻的复杂细节十分微小。|Synonyms: minute, tiny, minuscule, infinitesimal|Antonyms: macroscopic, gigantic, huge|adjective
