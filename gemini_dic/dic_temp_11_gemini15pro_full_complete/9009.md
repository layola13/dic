antilopine:/ˈæn.tɪ.ləˌpaɪn/ | adj. | 羚羊的；像羚羊的 |Resembling or characteristic of an antelope, especially in grace and speed. | The animal leaped with antilopine grace.  这只动物以羚羊般的优雅跳跃。| 近义词: gazelle-like, deer-like | 反义词: clumsy, awkward | 词性: 形容词
antilopine:/ˈæn.tɪ.ləˌpaɪn/ | n. | 羚羊类动物 | Any of various ruminant mammals of the family Bovidae, resembling antelopes. (Less common usage) |  Several antilopine grazed peacefully on the savanna. 几只羚羊类动物在草原上平静地吃草。 | 近义词: antelope, gazelle | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
