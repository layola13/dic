distorter:/dɪˈstɔːrtər/| n.|失真器，扭曲者，歪曲者|A person or thing that distorts something, especially sound or image.|The guitar's sound was altered by a distorter.  吉他的声音被失真器改变了。|Synonyms: deformer, falsifier, perverter|Antonyms: clarifier, purifier, rectifier|noun

distort:/dɪˈstɔːrt/| v.|扭曲，歪曲|To pull or twist out of shape; to give a misleading or false account or impression of.|The media distorted the facts about the incident. 媒体歪曲了事件的事实。|Synonyms: misrepresent, twist, warp|Antonyms: clarify, represent accurately, portray truthfully|verb
