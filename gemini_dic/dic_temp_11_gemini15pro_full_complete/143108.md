outbuildings:/ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋz/
noun
附属建筑；外屋
A structure detached from but located near or on the same property as the main building.
The farm has several outbuildings, including a barn and a shed.  这个农场有几个附属建筑，包括一个谷仓和一个棚屋。
synonyms:  annexes, dependencies, auxiliary buildings, secondary buildings
antonyms:  main building, primary structure
n.
