astony:/ˈstəʊni/ (UK) /ˈstoʊni/ (US)| adj.|冷淡的，无情的；石质的，多石的|Appearing to be made of stone; unfeeling or showing little emotion.|例句：His face was astony and unreadable. 他的表情冷漠，难以捉摸。|例句：The landscape was astony and barren. 这片土地荒凉多石。|近义词：stony, hard, impassive, emotionless, cold|反义词：warm, emotional, expressive, sympathetic|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *stānig, from stān "stone" + -ig. Cognate with Old Frisian stenich, Middle Dutch stēnich, Old High German steinag (German steinig "stony, rocky"), Old Norse steinn "stone."
