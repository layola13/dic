affidavit:/ˌæfɪˈdeɪvɪt/
n.
宣誓书，书面证词
a written statement confirmed by oath or affirmation, for use as evidence in court.
She signed an affidavit stating that she had witnessed the accident. 她签署了一份宣誓书，声明她目睹了这起事故。
The court required an affidavit from the witness. 法院要求证人提供宣誓书。
oath, deposition, testimony
n/a
noun
