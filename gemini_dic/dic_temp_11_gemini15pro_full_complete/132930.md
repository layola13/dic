nonelusiveness:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪvnəs/
n.
非难懂性；容易理解；清晰明了
The nonelusiveness of the instructions made the task easy to complete.  说明书浅显易懂，使得任务很容易完成。
The nonelusiveness of the evidence made the case straightforward.  证据确凿，使得案件一目了然。
Synonyms: clarity, perspicuity, obviousness, transparency, simplicity
Antonyms: elusiveness, obscurity, vagueness, ambiguity, complexity
noun
