non-randomly:/nɑːnˈrændəmli/ | adv. | 非随机地 |  In a manner that is not random; deliberately or by design.  It implies a specific purpose or intention behind the selection or arrangement of something. | The participants were non-randomly assigned to the two groups. 参与者被非随机地分配到两组。|  The books were non-randomly arranged on the shelves by color. 书架上的书是按颜色非随机排列的。 | deliberately, purposefully, intentionally, systematically | randomly | adverb
