demissionary:/ˌdɛmɪˈʃənəri/ /ˌdiːmɪˈʃənəri/| adj.|辞任的，辞职的，即将卸任的|Relating to or characteristic of resignation from office.  It implies that someone is still in office but about to leave. |The demissionary government remained in place until the new elections. 即将卸任的政府在新选举之前仍然在位。|The prime minister delivered his demissionary speech. 首相发表了卸任演说。|近义词：resigning, outgoing, caretaker|反义词：incoming, incumbent|adjective
