dians:/ˈdaɪænz/ /ˈdaɪənz/ | n. | 钻石; 菱形; <棒球>内场 |Diamonds are a girl's best friend. 钻石是女孩最好的朋友。| Diamonds are formed under immense pressure deep within the earth. 钻石在地球深处巨大的压力下形成。 | 近义词: gems, jewels | 反义词: coal, worthless stones | 词性: 名词
