mendings:/ˈmɛndɪŋz/
noun
修补物；缝补工作
The act of repairing something, especially clothes.  Things that have been mended.
She was busy with her mendings. 她正忙着缝补衣服。
synonyms: repairs, patches, darning
antonyms: damages, tears, breaks
词性: 名词
