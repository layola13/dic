biacid:/baɪˈæsɪd/| adj. n.|二酸的，二酸|A biacid is a chemical compound containing two acidic hydrogen atoms replaceable by basic or other positive atoms or radicals.|Citric acid is a tribasic acid, not a biacid. 柠檬酸是三元酸，不是二元酸。|Sulfuric acid is a common example of a biacid. 硫酸是二元酸的一个常见例子。|近义词：dibasic, diprotic|反义词: monobasic, monoprotic|词性: adjective, noun |词源: bi- (two) + acid
