misstep:/ˌmɪsˈstɛp/| n.|失误，过失，错误|A wrong or awkward step; a blunder.|例句：His biggest misstep was trusting the wrong person. 他最大的失误就是信任了错误的人。

例句：One misstep on this mountain could be fatal. 在这座山上一步走错就可能致命。|近义词：blunder, error, mistake, gaffe, slip-up|反义词：success, triumph, achievement|词性:名词

词源:  "misstep" comes from the combination of "miss" (meaning to fail to hit, reach, or get) and "step" (meaning a movement made by lifting the foot and setting it down in a different place).  Thus, a "misstep" literally means a misplaced step.
