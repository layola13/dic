half-expectant:/ˌhɑːf ɪkˈspektənt/
adjective
半期待的，略带期盼的
partly expecting something to happen, but also not completely sure that it will
She looked at him with a half-expectant, half-amused expression. 她带着一种半期待半好笑的表情看着他。
I waited, half-expectant, for the phone to ring.  我半期待着电话响起，等待着。

近义词: anticipatory, hopeful, apprehensive
反义词: indifferent, uninterested
词性: adjective
