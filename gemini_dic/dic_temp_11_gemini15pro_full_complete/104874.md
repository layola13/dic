keenness's:/ˈkiːnnəsɪz/
n.
敏锐度，热情，渴望
The quality of being keen; eagerness.
Her keenness's for learning impressed all the teachers. 她对学习的热情给所有的老师都留下了深刻的印象。
近义词：eagerness, enthusiasm, avidity
反义词：apathy, indifference, reluctance
词性:名词

词源:
From keen + -ness.  Keen traces back to Old English *cēne "bold, brave, wise, clever." The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
