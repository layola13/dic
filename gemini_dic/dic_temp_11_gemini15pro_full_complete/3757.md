agister:/ˈeɪdʒɪstər/ | n. | 放牧人；牧场管理员 | An official in charge of livestock grazing on common land, especially in a forest. | The agister collected fees from the farmers for allowing their cattle to graze in the forest. 放牧人向农民收取费用，允许他们的牛在森林里吃草。 |  近义词：herdsman, shepherd, grazier | 反义词: None | noun

agist:/əˈdʒɪst/ | v. | (牲畜)寄养；放牧(牲畜) | To take in and feed or pasture livestock for a fee. |  The farmer agisted his sheep on his neighbor's land during the drought.  干旱期间，农民把他的羊寄养在邻居的土地上放牧。| 近义词：pasture, graze | 反义词: None | verb
