freeze-frames:/ˈfriːz freɪmz/| n.|定格画面，冻结帧|A freeze-frame is a single frame of a video recording that is displayed for an extended period of time, appearing as a still image.|The director used freeze-frames to emphasize the character's emotions.|近义词：stills, snapshots|反义词：motion picture, video|词性:noun

freeze-frame:/ˈfriːz freɪm/| v.|使(图像)定格，冻结帧|To freeze-frame is to pause a video recording on a single frame, creating a still image.|He freeze-framed the video to get a better look at the suspect's face.|近义词：pause, stop|反义词：play, continue|词性:verb
