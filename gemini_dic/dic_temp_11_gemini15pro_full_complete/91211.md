hydriote:/ˈhaɪdriət/ | n. | 水螅体; 水螅虫 | An organism belonging to the class Hydrozoa, typically a small freshwater polyp. |  Hydriotes are simple animals related to jellyfish. 水螅体是与水母相关的简单动物。| synonym: polyp, hydrozoan | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a complex multicellular organism) | noun
