longboat:/ˈlɒŋbəʊt/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋboʊt/ (US)|n.|长艇，大型划艇|A long, narrow boat, usually propelled by oars, formerly used in warfare and for carrying cargo or passengers.|The Vikings used longboats to raid coastal settlements.  维京人使用长艇袭击沿海定居点。|The longboat ferried supplies to the larger ship. 长艇将补给品运送到较大的船上。|近义词：rowboat, galley|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps ship, as a larger vessel contrasting with a smaller boat)|noun
