achroodextrin:/ˌeɪkroʊˈdɛkstrɪn/
n.
消色糊精
A type of dextrin that does not produce a color with iodine.  It is formed by the extensive hydrolysis of starch.
Achroodextrin is a product of starch breakdown. 消色糊精是淀粉分解的产物。
Further hydrolysis of erythrodextrin yields achroodextrin.  赤糊精进一步水解生成消色糊精。
Synonym:  limit dextrin
Antonym:  starch, amylose
