oryal: /ˈɔːriəl/ | adj. |金色的，金黄色的|Of the colour of gold; golden.  Relating to gold. |The oryal hues of the sunset were breathtaking. 落日的金色光辉令人叹为观止。|近义词：golden, aureate, gold|反义词:silver, dark|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *orial, from or "gold" (from Latin aurum) + -ial (from Latin -alis).
