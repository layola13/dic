depositable:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəbl/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtəbl/
adjective
可存入的；可寄存的
Able to be deposited, especially of money in a bank account.
Example: The check is depositable at any branch.  这张支票可以在任何分行存入。
Example:  Are these coins depositable at the ATM? 这些硬币可以在自动取款机存入吗？
Synonyms: bankable, lodgeable
Antonyms: non-depositable, undepositable
词性: adjective
