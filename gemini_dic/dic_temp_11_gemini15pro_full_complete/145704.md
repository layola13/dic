overcourteous: /ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtiəs/ | adj. | 过分礼貌的，过分客气的 | Excessively polite or courteous, often to the point of being insincere or obsequious. | He was so overcourteous that it seemed unnatural. 他太客气了，显得不自然。|  She gave an overcourteous bow. 她行了一个过分谦恭的鞠躬。 | 近义词: obsequious, unctuous, fawning | 反义词: rude, impolite, discourteous | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "courteous" (from Old French "corteis," meaning "having courtly manners").  "Over-" 前缀表示“过度” + "courteous"（源自古法语 "corteis"，意为“具有宫廷礼仪的”）。
