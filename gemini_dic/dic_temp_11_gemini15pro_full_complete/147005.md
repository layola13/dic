overland:/ˈoʊvərˌlænd/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌlænd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|陆路的，经由陆路的；陆上地|Traveling across land rather than by sea or air.|We made an overland journey to Tibet. 我们经由陆路前往西藏。|近义词：across land, by land|反义词：overseas, by sea, by air|词性:形容词, 副词

overland:/ˈoʊvərˌlænd/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌlænd/ (UK)| n.|陆路旅程，陆路运输|A journey or transportation over land.|The overland was arduous and took many weeks. 这趟陆路旅程十分艰辛，耗时数周。|近义词：land journey, land route|反义词：sea voyage, air travel|词性:名词


USAGE->

Etymology: From "over" + "land". "Over" in this context means "across" or "upon".
