docilities:/dəˈsɪlətiz/
n.
温顺，顺从，可教性
The dog's docilities made him easy to train.  这条狗的温顺使它很容易训练。
英文解析:  Docility (singular) refers to the quality of being easily taught or managed; readily trained or disciplined; teachable; tractable; compliant. Docilities is the plural form, referring to multiple instances or aspects of this quality.
例句：The trainer praised the horse's docilities.  驯马师称赞了这匹马的温顺。
近义词：obedience, compliance, tractability, manageability
反义词：disobedience, defiance, resistance, obstinacy
词性：名词 (plural)

docile:/ˈdɑːsəl/
adj.
温顺的，易驯服的，可教的
She has a docile nature. 她性情温顺。
英文解析: Easily taught or managed; readily trained or disciplined; teachable; tractable; compliant.
例句：The docile child learned quickly. 这个温顺的孩子学得很快。
近义词：obedient, compliant, tractable, manageable
反义词：disobedient, defiant, resistant, obstinate
词性：形容词

词源: From Middle French *docile*, from Latin *docilis* "easily taught," from *docēre* "to teach."
