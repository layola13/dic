dtm:/ˈdiː tiː ɛm/ | abbreviation | 数字地形模型（Digital Terrain Model） | A digital representation of the bare earth topography, excluding trees, buildings, and other surface features. |  Example: The DTM was used to create a flood inundation map. 数字地形模型被用来创建洪水淹没图。| Synonym: DEM (Digital Elevation Model) | Antonym: N/A | noun
