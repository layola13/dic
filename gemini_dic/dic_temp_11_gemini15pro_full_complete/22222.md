bonbon:/ˈbɒnbɒn/ (UK), /ˈbɑːnbɑːn/ (US)| n. |糖果，甜食|A small piece of candy, often chocolate and usually individually wrapped. |例句：The children were given a bag of bonbons after their meal. 孩子们饭后得到了一袋糖果。

例句：She offered me a bonbon from a beautifully decorated box. 她从一个装饰精美的盒子里拿出一块糖果给我。

近义词：candy, sweet, confection, chocolate|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of food)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bonbon*, literally "good good," from *bon* "good."  The reduplication emphasizes the goodness.
