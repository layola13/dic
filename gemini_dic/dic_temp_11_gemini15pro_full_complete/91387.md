hydrotherapists:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɛrəpɪsts/
noun
水疗师
A hydrotherapist is a healthcare professional specializing in hydrotherapy, which is the use of water for pain relief and treatment.
Hydrotherapists work with patients of all ages and abilities, helping them to improve their mobility, strength, and overall well-being.
例句：The hydrotherapists designed a personalized exercise program for the patient's arthritis. 水疗师为病人的关节炎设计了一个个性化的锻炼计划。

例句：Hydrotherapists often work in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, and private clinics.  水疗师通常在医院、康复中心和私人诊所工作。

近义词：aquatic therapists, physical therapists (in some contexts)
反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists)
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
hydro- (Greek): water
therapy (Greek): treatment of disease
-ist (Greek): one who practices or is skilled in
