comprehensibly:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪbli/ | adv. | 可理解地，易懂地 |  In a way that can be easily understood. | She explained the complex issue comprehensibly, so everyone could follow. 她把这个复杂的问题解释得很清楚，所以每个人都能理解。| The instructions were written comprehensibly, even for a beginner.  说明书写得很容易理解，即使是初学者也能看懂。| clearly, intelligibly, understandably | obscurely, unclearly, confusingly | adverb
