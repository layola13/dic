mismanagement:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒmənt/| n.|管理不善，经营不善|The company's mismanagement led to its bankruptcy. 该公司的管理不善导致其破产。|The project's mismanagement resulted in significant delays and cost overruns.  这个项目的管理不善导致了严重的延误和成本超支。|近义词：misconduct, mishandling, maladministration|反义词：good management, efficient administration|词性：名词

mismanage:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒ/| vt.|管理不善，处理不当|He mismanaged the company's funds. 他管理不善公司的资金。|She mismanaged the situation and made it worse. 她处理不当这个情况，并使之恶化。|近义词：mishandle, misdirect|反义词：manage well, handle properly|词性：动词
