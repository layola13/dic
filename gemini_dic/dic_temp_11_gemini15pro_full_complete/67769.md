eyehole:/ˈaɪhoʊl/| n. |窥视孔，观察孔|A small hole or opening through which one can look; a peephole. |例句：He peered through the eyehole in the door. 他透过门上的窥视孔往里看。

例句：The voyeur spied on his neighbors through a hidden eyehole. 那个偷窥狂透过一个隐藏的窥视孔监视他的邻居。|近义词：peephole, spyhole, judas hole |反义词: None |词性:noun
