eschewal:/ɪˈʃuːəl/
noun
规避，避开
the act of deliberately avoiding or abstaining from something
His eschewal of caffeine was remarkable considering his previous coffee addiction. 他戒掉咖啡因很了不起，考虑到他以前对咖啡上瘾。
abstinence, avoidance, forbearance
indulgence, acceptance, embrace
