distemperance:/dɪsˈtɛmpərəns/
n.
放纵； intemperance; lack of moderation or restraint
Excessive indulgence in alcoholic beverages.
Lack of self-control; immoderation.
A disordered or morbid condition of the body or mind.

Distemperance is a lack of moderation or restraint, especially in relation to alcoholic beverages.
例句：His distemperance ultimately led to his downfall. 他的放纵最终导致了他的垮台。
例句：The preacher warned against the evils of distemperance. 传教士警告人们放纵的危害。

近义词：intemperance, excess, immoderation, overindulgence, dissipation
反义词：temperance, moderation, abstinence, self-control, restraint
