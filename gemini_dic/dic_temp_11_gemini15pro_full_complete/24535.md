broadleaf: /ˈbrɔːdˌliːf/ /ˈbrɑːdˌliːf/ | n. adj. |阔叶树；阔叶的| A tree or plant with broad, flat leaves, as opposed to needles or scales.  A broadleaf tree is typically deciduous.|
The maple is a common broadleaf tree. 枫树是一种常见的阔叶树。

The forest is filled with broadleaf trees and conifers. 这片森林里满是阔叶树和针叶树。

近义词: deciduous |反义词: conifer, needleleaf | 词性: noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From broad + leaf.  "Broad" meaning wide, and "leaf" referring to the foliage of a plant.  The term directly describes the physical characteristic of the leaves.
