microphotograph:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈfoʊtəɡræf/ | n. | 显微照片，微型照片 | A photograph taken through a microscope, typically at a very high magnification. |  This microphotograph shows the intricate structure of a snowflake. 这张显微照片展示了雪花的复杂结构。


The microphotograph revealed previously unseen details of the cell. 这张显微照片揭示了以前看不见的细胞细节。| 近义词: photomicrograph | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps macroscopic image) | 词性: noun
