crossbower:/ˈkrɒsbəʊər/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsboʊər/ (US)| n. |弩手；十字弓射手|A person who uses or shoots with a crossbow. |例句：The crossbowman took aim at the target. 弩手瞄准了目标。

例句：The skilled crossbower could hit a small target from a great distance.  熟练的弩手可以从很远的距离击中小目标。

近义词：archer, bowman|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *crosbowere*, from *crosbow* + *-ere*.  *Crosbow* is from Old French *crosbog*, from *cros* (cross) + *bog* (bow).  Ultimately from Latin *crux* (cross) and a Germanic source for "bow".
