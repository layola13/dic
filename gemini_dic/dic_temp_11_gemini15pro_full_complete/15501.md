bad:/bæd/ | adj. | 坏的，不好的，糟糕的 | Unpleasant, unfavorable, or harmful. | The weather was bad yesterday. 昨天天气很糟糕。| The food tasted bad. 食物尝起来很糟糕。 |  near synonyms: awful, terrible, horrible, lousy, poor, rotten | near antonyms: good, excellent, great, wonderful, pleasant | adjective

worse:/wɜːrs/ | adj. | 更坏的，更糟的；（病情）更重的 |  Comparative form of "bad".  Indicates a greater degree of "badness." Can also refer to a worsening condition. | The traffic is worse today than yesterday. 今天的交通比昨天更糟。 | His cough is worse this morning. 他的咳嗽今天早上更严重了。| near synonyms: poorer, inferior | near antonyms: better, superior | adjective
