codiscover:/ˌkəʊdɪˈskʌvər/ | v. | 共同发现 | To discover something simultaneously with another person or group. | Marie and Pierre Curie codiscovered radium. 玛丽和皮埃尔·居里共同发现了镭。

Copernicus codiscovered that the Earth revolves around the Sun. 哥白尼和其他人共同发现了地球绕太阳公转的事实。| 近义词: discover jointly, discover together | 反义词:  conceal, hide | 词性: 动词
