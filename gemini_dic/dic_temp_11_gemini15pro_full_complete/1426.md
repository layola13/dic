ace-in-the-hole:/eɪs ɪn ðə hoʊl/| n.|秘密武器；王牌；杀手锏|A secret advantage or resource kept hidden until needed.|He had an ace in the hole—a witness who could corroborate his alibi. 他有一个秘密武器——一个可以证实他不在场证明的证人。|Synonyms: trump card, secret weapon, hidden advantage|Antonyms: disadvantage, weakness|Noun

ace-up-his-sleeve:/eɪs ʌp hɪz sliːv/| idiom.|锦囊妙计；秘诀；暗藏的王牌|A secret plan or advantage that can be used when needed, typically in a difficult situation.|He had an ace up his sleeve and produced a document that proved his innocence. 他胸有成竹，拿出一份文件证明了他的清白。|Synonyms: ace in the hole, trump card, secret weapon|Antonyms: disadvantage, weakness|Noun
