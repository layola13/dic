meticulously:/məˈtɪkjələsli/
adjective
一丝不苟地，小心翼翼地
in a way that shows great attention to detail; very thoroughly and carefully.
She meticulously planned every detail of the wedding. 她一丝不苟地计划了婚礼的每一个细节。
The antique clock had been meticulously restored. 这座古董钟已被精心修复。
近义词:carefully, thoroughly, precisely, diligently, painstakingly
反义词:carelessly, negligently, sloppily
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin meticulosus, from metus "fear." Originally meant "timid, fearful," later evolving to mean "overly cautious, scrupulous."
