fertilitate:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪt/| vt.|使肥沃，使多产，使受精|To make fertile; to enrich or supply with nourishment for plants or crops, or to impregnate an ovum or female animal or plant.|Farmers fertilize their fields to improve crop yields.  农民给田地施肥以提高作物产量。|The flowers were fertilized by bees.  这些花被蜜蜂授粉了。|synonyms: enrich, nourish, inseminate, fecundate|antonyms: sterilize, barren|verb
