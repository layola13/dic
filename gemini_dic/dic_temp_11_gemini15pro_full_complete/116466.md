mateology: /ˌmeɪtiˈɒlədʒi/
n.
交配学，配偶选择学
The study of mating systems and mate choice in animals, including humans.  It examines factors influencing mate selection, such as physical attractiveness, resources, and behavior.
例句：Mateology helps us understand the complex dynamics of sexual selection in nature. 交配学帮助我们理解自然界中性选择的复杂动态。

例句：Researchers in mateology study a wide range of species from insects to primates. 交配学的研究人员研究了从昆虫到灵长类动物的各种物种。
近义词：sexual selection, mating behavior, courtship
反义词: None
