normand:/ˈnɔːrmən/| n. |诺曼人；诺曼底人|A member of a people of Viking origin who settled in northern France (Normandy) in the 10th century and who subsequently conquered England in 1066.|The Normans had a significant impact on English culture and language.|近义词：Norman French|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Anglo-Saxon as they were conquered by the Normans)|Noun
