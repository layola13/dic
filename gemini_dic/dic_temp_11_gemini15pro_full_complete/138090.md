normanist: /ˈnɔːrmənɪst/ | n. |诺曼主义者；诺曼底学派学者|A specialist in Norman history, language, or literature, or an adherent of the Normanist theory of the origin of the English language.| The normanist argued that the influence of Norman French was paramount in shaping the English language.  诺曼主义者认为，诺曼法语的影响在塑造英语的过程中至关重要。|Synonym: Norman scholar|Antonym: Saxonist|Noun

normanist: /ˈnɔːrmənɪst/ | adj. |诺曼底的；诺曼学派的|Relating to the Normans, their history, language, or culture, or to the Normanist theory.|  He took a normanist approach to the study of medieval English literature. 他采用诺曼学派的方法研究中世纪英国文学。|Synonym: Norman|Antonym: Saxon|Adjective
