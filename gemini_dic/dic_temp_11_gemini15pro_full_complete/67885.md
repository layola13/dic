fabulistic:/ˌfæbjəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 传奇式的，寓言般的，难以置信的 | Relating to or resembling a fable, especially in being unrealistic or improbable.  Characteristic of a fable; describing or containing fables. |  His account of the journey was quite fabulistic, full of talking animals and magical occurrences. 他对这次旅行的描述非常传奇，充满了会说话的动物和神奇的事件。| synonyms: mythical, legendary, fantastical, incredible, unbelievable, far-fetched, improbable, apocryphal | antonyms: realistic, factual, truthful, credible, believable, probable |词性: 形容词
