holder:/ˈhoʊldər/ (US) /ˈhəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 持有人，拥有者；支架，容器|Someone or something that holds or contains something.|例句：The passport holder is made of leather.  护照夹是皮革做的。

例句：She is the current holder of the world record. 她目前是世界纪录的保持者。

近义词：owner, possessor, keeper, container, support|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


holder:/ˈhoʊldər/ (US) /ˈhəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 持票人；股票持有人|Someone who owns a ticket, check, or other document that gives them the right to something.|例句：The ticket holder is entitled to a free drink. 持票人可以获得一杯免费饮料。

例句：He is a major holder of company stock. 他是公司股票的主要持有人。

近义词：bearer, owner|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
