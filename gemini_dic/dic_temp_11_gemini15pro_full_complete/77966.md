genteelnesses:/ˌdʒɛnˈtiːlnɪsɪz/
noun
文雅, 有教养, 上流社会的风度；彬彬有礼的行为（通常用复数）
The refined genteelnesses of the upper class. 上流社会优雅的风度。
She displayed the genteelnesses expected of a lady in those days. 她展现了当时淑女应有的优雅举止。
Synonyms: refinements, courtesies, politenesses, civilities
Antonyms: rudeness, vulgarity, coarseness, crudeness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From genteel + -ness + -es (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin gentilis (“of the same clan or race”)
