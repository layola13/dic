mind-bogglingly:/ˈmaɪndˌbɑːɡlɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人难以置信地；令人震惊地 | Extremely surprising and difficult to understand; so complex or confusing as to be overwhelming. | The universe is mind-bogglingly vast. 宇宙浩瀚得令人难以置信。 |  The complexity of the human brain is mind-bogglingly immense. 人脑的复杂性令人难以置信的巨大。 | Synonyms: astonishingly, incredibly, unbelievably, astoundingly | Antonyms: ordinarily, commonly, unsurprisingly | adverb

Etymology->
From mind-boggling + -ly.  Mind-boggling itself comes from mind + boggling (present participle of boggle), where boggle means "to overwhelm with wonder or bewilderment."
