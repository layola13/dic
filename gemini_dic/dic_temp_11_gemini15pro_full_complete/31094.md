champing:/ˈtʃæmpɪŋ/
v.|大声地嚼; 焦急地等待|The horse was champing at the bit, eager to start the race.  这匹马嚼着马衔，急于开始比赛。|English Explanation: To bite or chew on something noisily or impatiently, often used figuratively to describe eagerness or impatience.|例句：He was champing at the bit to get started on the new project. 他急于开始这个新项目。

例句：The dog was champing on its bone. 狗正在啃骨头。|近义词：chomping, gnawing, munching, chewing impatiently|反义词：nibbling, sipping|词性: 动词(v.)


ETYMOLOGY->
From  "champ" (v.),  frequentative of "chap" (v.), meaning "to jaw, to chatter with the teeth".  Ultimately of imitative origin.
