hubbub:/ˈhʌbʌb/ (US); /ˈhʌbʌb/ (UK)| n.|喧闹声；嘈杂声；混乱|A loud, confused noise, as of many voices talking at once; an uproar.|The hubbub in the marketplace made it difficult to hear my friend.  市场里的喧闹声让我很难听到朋友说话。|Synonyms: din, clamor, commotion, racket, uproar|Antonyms: quiet, silence, stillness|noun
