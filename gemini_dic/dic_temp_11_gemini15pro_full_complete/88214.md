hinder:/ˈhɪndər/ | v. |阻碍，妨碍|To create difficulties for someone or something, resulting in delay or obstruction.|The heavy traffic hindered our progress. 交通拥堵阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词: obstruct, impede, hamper, inhibit |反义词: help, aid, assist, facilitate |词性:动词

hinder:/ˈhɪndər/ | adj. (古语，罕用) |后面的，后方的|Located at the back or rear.|This usage is archaic and rarely encountered.| 近义词: rear, posterior |反义词: front, anterior | 词性:形容词 (罕用)
