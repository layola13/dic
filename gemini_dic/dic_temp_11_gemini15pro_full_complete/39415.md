connascent:/kəˈnæsənt/
adjective
同生的，共同成长的
growing together; united in origin; born at the same time
The connascent twins were attached at the hip. 这对同生的双胞胎臀部相连。
Synonyms: connate, cognate, congenital
Antonyms: separate, distinct, unconnected
词性: 形容词
