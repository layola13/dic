alienate:/ˈeɪliəneɪt/ /ˈælɪəneɪt/| vt.|使疏远，离间|to cause someone to feel that they are different and not part of a group|His comments alienated his colleagues. 他的言论疏远了他的同事们。|近义词：estrange, isolate, detach|反义词:reconcile, unite|动词

alienate:/ˈeɪliəneɪt/ /ˈælɪəneɪt/| vt.|转让，让渡（财产等）|to transfer ownership or control of something to someone else|He alienated his property to his son. 他把财产转让给了他的儿子。|近义词：transfer, convey|反义词:retain, keep|动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin alienatus, past participle of alienare "to estrange, make another's, transfer," from Latin alienus "of or belonging to another," from alius "other."
