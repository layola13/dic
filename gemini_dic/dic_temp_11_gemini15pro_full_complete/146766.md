overhydrated:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪdreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度水合的
Having consumed too much water, leading to a dilution of electrolytes in the body.
The marathon runner was overhydrated and experiencing nausea. 马拉松运动员过度水合，感到恶心。
She felt sluggish and disoriented, likely because she was overhydrated. 她感到迟钝和迷失方向，可能是因为她过度水合了。
近义词: waterlogged
反义词: dehydrated
词性: adjective
