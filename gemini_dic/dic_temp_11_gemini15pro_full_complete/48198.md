decompressive:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsɪv/
adjective
减压的;降压的
Relating to or causing decompression, a reduction in pressure.
The diver needed to undergo decompressive stops to avoid getting the bends. 潜水员需要进行减压停留以避免患上减压病。
Decompressive surgery was performed to relieve pressure on the spinal cord.  进行了减压手术以缓解脊髓的压力。
近义词:pressure-relieving, depressurizing
反义词:compressive
词性:adjective
