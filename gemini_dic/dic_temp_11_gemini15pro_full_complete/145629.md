overconstruct:/ˌoʊvər.kənˈstrʌkt/| v. |过度构建，过度设计|To build or design something in an excessively complex or elaborate way, often beyond what is necessary or practical.| The software was overconstructed and difficult to use.  这个软件过度构建，难以使用。| The bridge was so overconstructed it could withstand a hurricane. 这座桥被过度设计，可以抵御飓风。| 近义词：overengineer, overdesign, overbuild| 反义词: underdesign, simplify|词性:动词


overconstruction:/ˌoʊvər.kənˈstrʌk.ʃən/| n. |过度构建，过度设计|The act of building or designing something in an excessively complex or elaborate way.| The overconstruction of the website made it slow to load.  网站的过度构建导致加载速度缓慢。| The building's overconstruction led to unnecessary costs.  建筑物的过度设计导致了不必要的成本。| 近义词：overengineering, overdesigning, overbuilding| 反义词: underdesigning, simplification|词性:名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "construct" (from Latin "construere" - to build)
