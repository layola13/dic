She made a delicious gooseberry pie. 她做了一个美味的醋栗派。|
Synonyms: gooseberry (singular), gooseberry bush| Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a fruit) | noun

词源：From Middle English *gosberie, *groserberie, from Anglo-Norman groseillier and Old Northern French groseille, from Frankish *krusil, of unknown ultimate origin. Possibly related to Old High German krûsil "gooseberry." The "goose" part may be a corruption or may refer to the use of gooseberries in a sauce for goose.
