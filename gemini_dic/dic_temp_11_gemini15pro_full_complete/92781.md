icier:/ˈaɪsiər/| adj.|更冰冷的，更冷淡的|Comparative form of icy.  Colder in temperature or more unfriendly in manner. |The wind made the air feel even icier.  风使空气感觉更冷了。|The reception he received was icier than he expected. 他受到的接待比他预期的更冷淡。|近义词：frostier, colder, chillier, more frigid, more glacial|反义词：warmer, friendlier, more cordial|词性:形容词
词源: From icy + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *isaz.
