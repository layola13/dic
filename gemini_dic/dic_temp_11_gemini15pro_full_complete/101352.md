ionically:/aɪˈɒnɪkli/
adverb
离子地；通过离子
in a way that relates to ions or involves the formation of ions
例句：The compound is ionically bonded.  这种化合物是离子键合的。

例句：The two elements react ionically. 这两种元素发生离子反应。

近义词:electrostatically
反义词:covalently
词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ionic + -ally.  Ionic from ion + -ic. Ion from Greek *ion*, present participle of *ienai* "to go."
