bunty:/ˈbʌnti/| adj. n.|矮胖的，矮壮结实的; 矮胖结实的人; 兔子（苏格兰方言）|A bunty person is short and plump.  Bunty can also be used as a nickname for a rabbit, particularly in Scottish dialects.|He's a bit bunty, but very strong. 他有点矮胖，但很强壮。|The children were chasing a bunty rabbit in the garden. 孩子们在花园里追逐一只矮胖的兔子。|近义词：stocky, stout, chubby, dumpy (for a person); plump, round (for a rabbit)|反义词：tall, thin, slender, lanky (for a person); skinny, lean (for a rabbit)|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from dialectal *bunt, 'short and thick,' of unknown origin.  The use for rabbit is likely related to their round shape.
