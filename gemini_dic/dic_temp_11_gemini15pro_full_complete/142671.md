ostriferous:/ɒsˈtrɪfərəs/ | adj. | 产牡蛎的；多牡蛎的 | Producing or yielding oysters; abounding in oysters. | The bay was once ostriferous, providing a livelihood for many families.  这个海湾曾经盛产牡蛎，为许多家庭提供了生计。 |  例句：The shallow waters are particularly ostriferous.  这些浅水区尤其盛产牡蛎。| 近义词: oyster-bearing, oyster-rich | 反义词: barren, oysterless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *ostrifer, from ostrea (oyster) + -fer (bearing).
