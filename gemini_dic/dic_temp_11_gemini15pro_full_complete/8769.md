antidoting:/ˈæntidəʊtɪŋ/
verb
解毒; 抵消; 中和
counteracting or neutralizing the effects of a poison or other harmful substance.
The doctor is antidoting the patient for the snake bite. 医生正在为病人解蛇毒。
近义词: neutralizing, counteracting, remedying
反义词: poisoning, harming, exacerbating
词性: verb (动名词)

antidote:/ˈæntidəʊt/
noun
解毒剂; 矫正方法; 对抗手段
a medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison.
Kindness is the best antidote to anger.  仁慈是消除愤怒的最好方法。
近义词: remedy, cure, corrective
反义词: poison, toxin, venom
词性: noun
