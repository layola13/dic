klap:/klæp/ /klɑːp/| n. v.|拍手，鼓掌；轻拍；啪的一声|A quick, sharp sound like that made by two flat surfaces striking each other.  To strike the palms of one's hands together repeatedly, typically in order to applaud.|The audience gave a loud klap.  观众大声鼓掌。 He gave me a friendly klap on the back. 他友好地拍了拍我的背。|近义词：clap, applause, pat|反义词: silence|词性：名词，动词

Etymology:
From Middle Dutch *klappen, frequentative of *klappen (“to strike”). Cognate with Middle Low German klappen, German klappen.  Related to clap.
