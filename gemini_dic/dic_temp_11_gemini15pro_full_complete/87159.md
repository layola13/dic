herblike:/ˈɜːrblaɪk/
adjective
像草的；草本植物似的
Resembling or characteristic of an herb.
The plant has a delicate, herblike fragrance. 这种植物有一种淡淡的、像草一样的香味。
The leaves are small and herblike. 叶子小而像草。

近义词:herbaceous, grassy
反义词:woody, treelike
词性:adjective
