celestially:/səˈlɛstiəli/
adjective
天上地；神圣地
of or relating to the sky or the heavens; divine or supremely good.
The music was celestially beautiful.  这音乐美妙得如同天籁。
Synonyms: divine, heavenly, ethereal, angelic, sublime
Antonyms: earthly, mundane, terrestrial
词性: adjective
