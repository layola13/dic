nonetherealnesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈθɪəriəlnəsɪz/
noun
非物质性；非灵性
The lack of ethereal qualities; the state of being solid, concrete, or worldly.  This word emphasizes the absence of spiritual, heavenly, or otherworldly characteristics.
His nonetherealnesses was evident in his focus on practical matters. 他对实际问题的关注表明了他不具备空灵的特质。
Synonyms:  materiality, worldliness, concreteness, solidity
Antonyms: ethereality, spirituality, otherworldliness, immateriality
