mistemper:/mɪsˈtɛmpər/| n. vt.|坏脾气，怒气；使发脾气|A state of ill humor or anger. To put in a bad temper; irritate.|He was in a mistemper because of the traffic jam. 他因为交通堵塞而发脾气。|近义词：anger, irritability, bad mood, ill humor|反义词：good humor, calmness, placidity|n.
mistemper:/mɪsˈtɛmpər/| vt.|使发脾气；激怒|To irritate or annoy; to put in a bad mood.|The constant noise mistempered him.  持续不断的噪音让他很烦躁。|近义词：anger, irritate, annoy, provoke|反义词：calm, soothe, placate|vt.
