externalizes:/ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzɪz/
v.
使外化；使客观化；投射
To express (a thought or feeling) outwardly.  To perceive (something internal, such as a feeling) as having an external cause or origin. To put (a service or function) outside the company that used to provide it.
He externalizes his anger by yelling. 他通过大喊大叫来发泄他的愤怒。
The company externalized its IT department.  该公司将其IT部门外包。
Synonyms: express, project, vent, outsource
Antonyms: internalize, repress, suppress
词性:动词
