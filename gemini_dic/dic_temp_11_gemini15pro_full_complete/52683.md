dignifiedly:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪdli/| adv.|庄重地；有尊严地|In a manner befitting one's dignity; in a stately and composed way.  It describes an action performed with grace, composure, and a sense of self-respect.|She accepted the award dignifiedly, even though she was clearly disappointed.|Synonym:gracefully, stately, nobly, regally|Antonym:undignifiedly, shamefully, basely|adverb
