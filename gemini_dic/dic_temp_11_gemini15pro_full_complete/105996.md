knappish:/ˈnæpɪʃ/| adj.|小气的，吝啬的；脾气暴躁的|Inclined to be stingy or short-tempered; curt.  Characterized by a curt, snappish, or surly manner.|He gave a knappish reply to her request for help. 他对她寻求帮助的请求作了粗鲁的答复。|近义词：curt, snappish, surly, grumpy, irritable|反义词：generous, kind, affable, good-natured|词性: adjective
