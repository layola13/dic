heptandrous:/hɛpˈtændrəs/
adjective
七雄蕊的
Having seven stamens.
Hepandrous flowers are characteristic of certain plant families. 七雄蕊的花是某些植物科的特征。
The Trientalis borealis is a heptandrous flower. 七瓣莲是七雄蕊的花。
synonym:septemandrous
antonym:(no common direct antonym, could use terms like "few-stamened," "oligandrous" depending on the context)
词性:adjective
