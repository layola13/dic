intermediator:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiˌeɪtər/| n.|中间人，调解人，媒介物|A person or thing that acts as a link between people or things.|The lawyer acted as an intermediator between the buyer and the seller. 律师在买方和卖方之间充当了中间人。|synonyms: mediator, negotiator, go-between, broker, middleman|antonyms: opponent, adversary|noun

intermediary:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiˌeri/ | adj. |中间的，媒介的|Acting as a mediator.|He played an intermediary role in the negotiations. 他在谈判中扮演了中间人的角色。|synonyms: mediating, intervening, conciliatory|antonyms: direct|adjective
