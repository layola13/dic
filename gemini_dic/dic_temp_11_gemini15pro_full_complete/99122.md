intercelebration: /ˌɪntərˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən/
n.
共同庆祝；互相庆祝；相互庆祝
A celebration between different groups, cultures, or nations.  A celebration shared or enjoyed by different groups.
例句：The intercelebration of the two festivals brought the communities closer. 两个节日的共同庆祝拉近了社区之间的距离。

例句：The intercelebration of diverse cultures enriched the city's social fabric.  多元文化的相互庆祝丰富了城市的社会结构。

近义词：joint celebration, shared celebration, mutual celebration, combined celebration
反义词:  segregated celebration, separate celebration

词性: noun
