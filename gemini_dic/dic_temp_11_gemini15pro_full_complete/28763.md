carnivals: /ˈkɑːrnɪvlz/ (US) /ˈkɑːnɪvəlz/ (UK)
n.
嘉年华，狂欢节
A period of revelry, typically associated with Shrove Tuesday, usually involving a public procession, feasting, and merrymaking.
The carnivals in Rio de Janeiro are world-famous. 里约热内卢的嘉年华世界闻名。
festival, celebration, fete, fiesta
fasting, abstinence
noun
