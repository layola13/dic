archangelically:/ˌɑːrkænˈdʒɛlɪkli/ | adv. |像大天使一样地；极其善良地；非常纯洁地|In a manner resembling or befitting an archangel; with extreme goodness, purity, or benevolence. |She smiled archangelically, radiating warmth and kindness. 她像大天使一样微笑着，散发着温暖和善良。|例句：He sang archangelically, his voice pure and clear. 他唱得像天使一样，声音纯净清澈。|近义词：divinely, seraphically, angelically|反义词：diabolically, devilishly|词性:副词
