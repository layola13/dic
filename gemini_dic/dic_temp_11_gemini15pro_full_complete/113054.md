lumpen:/ˈlʌmpən/| adj. n.|成块的，笨重的；流氓无产者|Of or relating to the Lumpenproletariat; unorganized and unintelligent.  A Lumpen person often lacks class consciousness.|The economist described the chronically unemployed as the lumpen elements of society. 经济学家将长期失业者描述为社会的流氓无产阶级分子。|近义词：impoverished, degraded, proletarian|反义词：bourgeois, elite, aristocratic|词性: adjective, noun

lumpenproletariat:/ˌlʌmpənproʊlɪˈtɛəriət/| n.|流氓无产阶级|The class of society composed of the lowest elements, such as beggars, vagrants, and criminals.|Marxist theory often discusses the role of the lumpenproletariat in social upheaval.  马克思主义理论经常讨论流氓无产阶级在社会动荡中的作用。|近义词：rabble, underclass, dregs|反义词：bourgeoisie, aristocracy|词性: noun
