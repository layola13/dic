neighbourliness:/ˈneɪbərlɪnɪs/
noun
睦邻友好；邻里间的亲善
The quality or state of being neighbourly; friendly or helpful behaviour towards people who live near you.
The village was known for its neighbourliness.  这个村庄以其睦邻友好而闻名。
Our new neighbours showed us a lot of neighbourliness. 我们的新邻居对我们表现得非常友好。
近义词: friendliness, amity, sociability, kindliness, geniality
反义词: hostility, unfriendliness
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From neighbour + -liness.  Neighbour comes from Old English *nēahgebūr meaning "near dweller," from *nēah "near" + *gebūr "dweller."
