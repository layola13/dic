nonconcessions:/nɒnkənˈseʃənz/ (UK) /nɑːnkənˈseʃənz/ (US)| n. |非让步，不妥协，不让步的事项|The union's list of nonconcessions included maintaining current healthcare benefits and a guaranteed annual pay raise.  工会的非让步清单包括维持现有的医疗福利和保证每年的加薪。|Example: The negotiations stalled due to the nonconcessions on both sides.  由于双方都不让步，谈判陷入了僵局。|Synonyms: intransigence, inflexibility,  unyieldingness|Antonyms: concessions, compromise, flexibility|Noun
