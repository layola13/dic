bites:/baɪts/| n. v.| 咬；叮；刺痛；(食物)小块；少量食物；上钩；受骗 |A bite is the act of using your teeth to cut into something.  A bite can also refer to a small piece of food, or a light meal. In informal language, a bite can also mean an insect sting or a sharp pain. As a verb, it means to use your teeth to cut into something.|

例句：The mosquito bites itch. 蚊子叮咬的地方很痒。

例句：He took a bite of the apple. 他咬了一口苹果。

近义词：nibbles, nibbling, snaps, sting, pinch|
反义词:release|
词性:noun, verb
