loyalties:/ˈlɔɪəltiz/
noun
忠诚；忠心；忠贞
The state of being loyal; allegiance.
Her loyalties were divided between her family and her career. 她对家庭和事业的忠诚难以取舍。
allegiance, fidelity, devotion
treachery, disloyalty
