flayed:/fleɪd/
verb
剥皮的; 鞭打的
Stripped off the skin; whipped.
The hunter flayed the deer. 猎人剥了鹿的皮。
近义词:skinned, excoriated, whipped
反义词:covered, protected


flayed:/fleɪd/
adjective
被剥皮的; 被鞭打的
Having had the skin stripped off; having been whipped.
The flayed carcass was left for the scavengers. 被剥了皮的尸体留给了食腐动物。
近义词:skinned, excoriated, whipped
反义词:covered, protected



词性: Verb/Adjective
中文翻译:  (v.) 剥皮，鞭打; (adj.) 被剥皮的，被鞭打的
英文解析: (v.) To strip off the skin of; to whip or lash; (adj.) Having had the skin stripped off; whipped.
词源: From Old English *flēan, from Proto-Germanic *flahaną (“to flay”). Cognate with Old Norse flá (“to flay”), Dutch vlaaien (“to skin, flay”), German flåen (“to flay”).
