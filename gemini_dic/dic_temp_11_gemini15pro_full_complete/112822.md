lucidly:/ˈluːsɪdli/| adv.|清晰地，明白地|In a clear and easily understood manner, intelligently.|She explained the complex theory lucidly, making it easy for everyone to grasp. 她清晰地解释了这个复杂的理论，使每个人都容易理解。|clearly, cogently, intelligibly|obscurely, vaguely|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "lucidus" (bright, shining, clear), from "lux" (light).  The connection between light and clarity of thought is a common metaphorical theme across languages.
