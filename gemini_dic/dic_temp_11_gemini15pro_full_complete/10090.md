apodan:/ˈeɪpɒdən/ /əˈpoʊdən/| adj. n.|无足的，无足动物|Relating to or belonging to the Apoda, an order of amphibians that are limbless and resemble worms or snakes; a limbless amphibian.|例句：The apodan burrowed into the soft earth.  这种无足动物钻进了松软的泥土里。

例句：Scientists discovered a new species of apodan in the Amazon rainforest.  科学家们在亚马逊雨林中发现了一种新的无足动物。|近义词：limbless, snake-like, caecilian|反义词: legged, limbed|词性: 形容词, 名词
Etymology: From Greek *a- "without" + *pous* "foot".
