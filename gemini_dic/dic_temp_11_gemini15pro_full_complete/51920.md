dhuti:/ˈduːtiː/
n.
缠腰布，男士围裙
A length of cotton cloth worn as a loincloth by Hindu men, especially in Bengal.
He wrapped the dhuti around his waist. 他把缠腰布裹在腰上。
近义词:dhoti, lunghi
反义词:trousers, pants
词性:名词
