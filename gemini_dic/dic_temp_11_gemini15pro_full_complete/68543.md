fandoms:/ˈfændəmz/ | n. | 粉丝圈；粉丝群体 | A group of fans of a particular person, team, fictional series, etc., especially one exhibiting intense enthusiasm and a sense of community.  Fans collectively, especially when organized online. |  Example: The Star Wars fandoms are incredibly active online.  星球大战的粉丝圈在网上非常活跃。

Example:  She's deep into several different fandoms. 她深深地沉浸在几个不同的粉丝圈中。 | 近义词: fanbase, following, supporters | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "detractors" in a specific context) | 词性: noun
