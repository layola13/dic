misrelies:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪz/
verb
错误地依赖；误信
To rely on someone or something mistakenly or inappropriately.
She misrelied on the information she found online and made a costly mistake. 她错误地依赖了她在网上找到的信息，犯了一个代价高昂的错误。
He misrelied on his friend's advice and ended up losing his job. 他误信了朋友的建议，最终丢了工作。
近义词: depend incorrectly, trust mistakenly
反义词: trust correctly, depend reliably
词性: verb

词源:
"mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "rely" (verb meaning to depend on with full trust or confidence)
