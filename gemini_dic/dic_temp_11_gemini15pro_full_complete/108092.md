lathe: /leɪð/ | n. | 车床 | A machine for shaping wood, metal, or other material by rotating it against a cutting tool. |  The carpenter used a lathe to create the table legs. 木匠用车床制作桌腿。| synonyms: turning machine, turning lathe | antonyms: None | noun

lathe: /leɪð/ | vt. | 用车床加工 | To shape something on a lathe. | He lathed the piece of wood into a smooth cylinder. 他用车床把那块木头加工成一个光滑的圆柱体。| synonyms: turn, shape, machine | antonyms: None | verb
