overattentions:/ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnʃənz/| n. |过度关注；过分关心|Excessive attention or attentiveness, often to the point of being intrusive or overwhelming. |例句：Her overattentions to detail sometimes slowed down the project. 她对细节的过度关注有时会拖慢项目的进度。

例句：The child's overattentions toward the new toy quickly waned. 孩子对新玩具的过度关注很快就消失了。|近义词：fussiness, solicitude, overcarefulness |反义词:neglect, indifference|noun
