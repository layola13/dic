interpollinating:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |异花授粉的 |Transferring pollen from one flower to another, especially of a different plant, or (of plants) being pollinated in this way. | The bees were busy interpollinating the apple blossoms. 蜜蜂正忙着给苹果花异花授粉。 |  cross-pollinating, cross-fertilizing | self-pollinating | adjective

interpollinate:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləˌneɪt/ | v. |异花授粉 |To transfer pollen from one flower to another, especially of a different plant. | The gardener interpollinated the roses to create a new hybrid. 园丁对玫瑰进行异花授粉以创造新的杂交品种。 | cross-pollinate, cross-fertilize | self-pollinate | verb
