alpinely:/ælˈpaɪnli/ | adv. |高山地；高海拔地；像高山一样地| In a manner characteristic of high mountains; like the Alps. |  The house was alpinely situated, offering breathtaking views. 这座房子位于高山地带，可以欣赏到令人叹为观止的景色。|  The peaks rose alpinely against the clear blue sky.  山峰高耸入云，映衬着湛蓝的天空。| 近义词:mountainously, highly, loftily | 反义词:lowly, basely |词性:副词
