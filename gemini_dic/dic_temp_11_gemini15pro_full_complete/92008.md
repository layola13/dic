hyperorthodox:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɑːks/| adj.|极端正统的，极端保守的|Extremely orthodox; excessively rigid in adherence to religious principles.|Example 1: The hyperorthodox community shunned all forms of modern technology. 极端正统的社区排斥一切形式的现代科技。

Example 2: His hyperorthodox views on marriage alienated many of his friends. 他对婚姻的极端正统的观点疏远了他的许多朋友。|近义词：ultra-orthodox, fundamentalist, rigid, strict|反义词：liberal, progressive, reformist|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + orthodox.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessively," derived from the Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ). "Orthodox" comes from the Greek "orthos" (ὀρθός), meaning "right, straight, true," and "doxa" (δόξα), meaning "opinion, belief."
