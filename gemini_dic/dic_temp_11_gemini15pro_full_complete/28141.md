capitulary: /kəˈpɪtʃʊləri/ | n. | 章程；法令；主教训令；教堂会议记录 | A collection of ordinances, especially ecclesiastical ones, in chapters.  A code of laws or regulations. | The capitulary issued by Charlemagne in 802 addressed issues of justice and administration.  查理曼大帝在802年颁布的法令涉及司法和行政问题。

The capitulary forbade the practice of divination. 这项法令禁止占卜行为。 | synonyms: decree, edict, ordinance, statute, regulation | antonyms:  allowance, permission | noun
