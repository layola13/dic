hymenium:/haɪˈmiːniəm/
noun
子实层
The fertile, spore-bearing layer of cells in fungi and lichens.
The hymenium of the mushroom releases spores for reproduction.  子实层的蘑菇释放孢子进行繁殖。

The hymenium of this species is smooth.  这个物种的子实层是光滑的.


Synonym:  sporophore, fruiting layer
Antonym:  (No direct antonym as it's a specific anatomical feature)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑμήν (humḗn, “membrane”) + -ium.
