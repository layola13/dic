dastardness:/ˈdæstərdnəs/| n. |卑鄙；懦弱；|Cowardice or wickedness. Extreme meanness or cruelty.  The quality of being a dastard. |His dastardness was evident in his betrayal of his closest friends. 他的卑鄙在他背叛最亲密的朋友时显露无疑。|Synonyms: cowardice, villainy, baseness, meanness, cruelty|Antonyms: courage, bravery, nobility, kindness, generosity|noun
