improvidentially:/ˌɪmprɑːvɪˈdɛnʃəli/
adverb
中文翻译：不谨慎地；不顾后果地；鲁莽地
英文解析：In a way that lacks foresight or careful planning; rashly.
例句：He improvidentially spent all his savings on a risky investment. 他不谨慎地把所有的积蓄都花在了一项风险投资上。

例句：She improvidentially quit her job without having another lined up. 她不顾后果地辞掉了工作，却没有找到另一份工作。
近义词：rashly, recklessly, thoughtlessly, imprudently, unwisely
反义词：prudently, wisely, cautiously, carefully, thoughtfully
词性：adverb
