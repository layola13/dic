eternalizes:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使永恒化，使永垂不朽
To make something eternal; to cause something to last forever.
The artist eternalizes the beauty of nature in his paintings.  这位艺术家在他的画作中使自然之美永垂不朽。
The museum eternalizes the memory of the war heroes.  博物馆使战争英雄的记忆永垂不朽。
immortalizes, perpetuates, enshrines
annihilates, destroys
词性: 动词
