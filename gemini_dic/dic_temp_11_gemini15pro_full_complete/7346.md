anhydrous:/ænˈhaɪdrəs/
adj.
无水的
lacking water; especially :  not containing water of crystallization
Anhydrous copper sulfate is white. 无水硫酸铜是白色的。

Anhydrous ammonia is used as a fertilizer. 无水氨用作肥料。
近义词:dry, desiccated, waterless
反义词:hydrous, hydrated
词性:adjective
