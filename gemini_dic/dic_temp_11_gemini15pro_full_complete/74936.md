fretworks:/ˈfrɛtwɜːrks/
noun
(通常复数)格状装饰, 格子细工,  格子状的东西
Ornamental work consisting of frets combined in geometric patterns.  Usually used in architecture or furniture.

The fretworks on the balcony were intricate and beautiful.  阳台上的格子细工复杂而美丽。

latticework, grillwork, tracery
--- (no clear antonym)
