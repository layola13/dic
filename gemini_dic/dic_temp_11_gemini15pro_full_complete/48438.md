deductive:/dɪˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|演绎的，推论的|Of, relating to, or provable by deduction, characterized by or based on the inference of particular instances from a general law.|Sherlock Holmes is famous for his deductive reasoning.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯以其演绎推理而闻名。|近义词：inferential, reasoning, logical|反义词：inductive|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin deductivus, from deducere (see deduce).
