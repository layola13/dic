hyporhachis:/ˌhaɪpəˈreɪkɪs/ | n. |叶轴下部；小穗轴|The lower part of the main axis of an inflorescence, especially in grasses, bearing the spikelets or secondary branches.|The hyporhachis of the wheat plant is strong and flexible.|Synonym:rachilla|Antonym:N/A|noun
