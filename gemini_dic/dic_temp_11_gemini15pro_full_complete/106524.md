krill:/krɪl/| n.|磷虾|Small, shrimp-like crustaceans found in all oceans, often forming dense swarms and serving as a major food source for many marine animals.|例句：Whales feed on massive amounts of krill.  鲸鱼以大量的磷虾为食。

例句：Krill oil is a popular dietary supplement. 磷虾油是一种流行的膳食补充剂。|近义词：plankton|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
