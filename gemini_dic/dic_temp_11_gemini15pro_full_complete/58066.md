droner:/ˈdroʊnər/| n. |嗡嗡叫的人；无人机驾驶员；持续低沉的声音|A person who speaks in a monotonous tone; a person who pilots a drone; a continuous low humming sound.|例句：The droner kept talking for hours, and everyone was bored. 这个嗡嗡叫的人连续说了几个小时，大家都厌烦了。

例句：He's a skilled droner, capable of navigating the drone through complex terrain. 他是一位熟练的无人机驾驶员，能够驾驶无人机穿越复杂的地形。|近义词：monotonist, drone pilot, hum, buzz|反义词:dynamic speaker, lively sound|词性:名词
