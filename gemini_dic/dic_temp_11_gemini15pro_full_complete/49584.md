demerges:/diːˈmɜːrdʒɪz/
| v. | 分拆，合并解除 | To separate a previously merged company into its original constituent companies. | The company demerged its telecommunications division last year.  该公司去年分拆了其电信部门。| synonyms: separate, split, divide, disentangle | antonyms: merge, combine, unite | verb

demerger:/diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
| n. | 分拆，合并解除 | The act of separating a previously merged company into its original constituent companies. | The demerger created two independent companies. 这次分拆创建了两家独立的公司。 | synonyms: separation, split, division, disentanglement | antonyms: merger, combination, union | noun
