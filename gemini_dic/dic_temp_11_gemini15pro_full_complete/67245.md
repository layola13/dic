expulsive:/ɪkˈspʌlsɪv/
adjective
排出性的；驱逐的；冲出的
Tending or serving to expel; having power to expel.
The expulsive power of the heart. 心脏的搏出力量。
The cough was harsh and expulsive. 咳嗽很剧烈，感觉要把东西咳出来。

近义词:ejective,  discharge
反义词:retentive, receptive
词性:adjective
