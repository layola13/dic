chondrification:/ˌkɒndrɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 软骨形成；软骨化 | The process by which cartilage is formed.  The development of cartilage. |  Example 1:  Chondrification is essential for the growth of long bones in children.  儿童长骨的生长离不开软骨形成。

Example 2: The study examined the factors influencing chondrification in the embryonic skeleton.  这项研究调查了影响胚胎骨骼软骨形成的因素。 |Synonyms: cartilage formation, gristle development | Antonyms:  bone resorption, cartilage degradation | noun
