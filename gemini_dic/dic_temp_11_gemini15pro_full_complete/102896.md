ETYMOLOGY->
From "jaw-dropping" + "-ly".  "Jaw-dropping" is a compound adjective formed from "jaw" (referring to the lower jaw) and "dropping" (the present participle of "drop"). The image is of someone's jaw falling open in amazement.
