diselenide:/ˌdaɪˈsɛlənaɪd/| n.|二硒化物|A chemical compound containing a Se-Se bond, often represented as R-Se-Se-R, where R can be an organic group or a hydrogen atom.|Diselenides are important in biochemistry and organic synthesis. 二硒化物在生物化学和有机合成中很重要。|Diphenyl diselenide is used as a catalyst. 二苯基二硒化物用作催化剂。|近义词: disulfide|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially reducing agents)|词性:noun

diselenide:/ˌdaɪˈsɛlənaɪd/| n.|二硒化物|Formed from two selenol groups (RSeH) by oxidation, analogous to the formation of disulfides from thiols.  类似于硫醇形成二硫化物，二硒化物由两个硒醇基团 (RSeH) 氧化形成。|Dimethyl diselenide has a strong odor. 二甲基二硒化物有强烈的气味。|近义词: disulfide|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially reducing agents)|词性:noun
