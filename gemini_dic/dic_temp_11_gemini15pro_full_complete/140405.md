What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？

What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？


Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？

What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？


Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？

What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？


Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？

What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？

What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？

What had they discussed before I arrived? = 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？


Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？

What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？


Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？

What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？


Future Perfect:
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? = 年底前你将完成什么？

What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by graduation? = 到毕业时你将已经学习什么五年了？

What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将一直在做什么？



Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. = 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。

I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？

What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


Interrogative Sentence:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？

What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？


Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。

She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
