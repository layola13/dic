old masters:/ˌoʊld ˈmæstərz/| n.|古代大师（尤指欧洲文艺复兴时期以前的画家）|A term used to describe renowned European painters, especially those active before the 18th century.  These artists are considered to have mastered their craft and produced works of exceptional quality. |The museum has a wonderful collection of old masters.  这家博物馆收藏了大量古代大师的作品。| synonyms: old masters (often used as its own synonym), classical masters, Renaissance masters| antonyms: modern artists, contemporary artists| noun
