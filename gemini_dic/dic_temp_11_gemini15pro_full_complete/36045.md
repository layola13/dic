codon:/ˈkoʊdɑːn/ (US), /ˈkəʊdɒn/ (UK)| n. |密码子|A sequence of three nucleotides which together form a unit of genetic code in a DNA or RNA molecule.|The codons in mRNA specify which amino acid will be added next during protein synthesis.|密码子在mRNA中指定在蛋白质合成过程中接下来要添加哪个氨基酸。|Synonyms: triplet, code, nucleotide triplet|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-coding sequence")|Noun
