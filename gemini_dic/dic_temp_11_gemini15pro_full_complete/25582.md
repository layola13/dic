bumblings:/ˈbʌmblɪŋz/
n.
笨拙的举动；踉跄；语无伦次的讲话
the act of speaking or moving in a clumsy, awkward way.
His first attempts at public speaking were marked by nervous bumblings. 他第一次尝试公开演讲的特点是紧张的笨拙。
近义词：blunders, stumbles, fumbles, awkwardness
反义词：grace, skill, fluency, dexterity
词性：名词
