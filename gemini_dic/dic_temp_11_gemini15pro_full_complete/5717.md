aluvial:/əˈluːviəl/| adj.|冲积的；淤积的|Relating to or derived from alluvium; deposited by running water.|The farmer's fields were rich with alluvial soil.  农民的田地富含冲积土。|Synonyms: deposited, sedimentary, fluvial|Antonyms: igneous, volcanic|adjective

alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/| n.|冲积层；冲积土|A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil.|The river deposited a thick layer of alluvium on the floodplain. 河流在泛滥平原上沉积了一层厚厚的冲积层。|Synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt|Antonyms: bedrock|noun
