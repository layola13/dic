nemes:/ˈniːmiːz/
n.
headcloth, headdress
A striped headcloth worn by pharaohs in ancient Egypt.
The pharaoh's nemes, with its distinctive stripes, symbolized his royal authority.  法老的nemes，以其独特的条纹，象征着他的王权。
Synonyms: headdress, crown, diadem
Antonyms: N/A (Lack of a direct antonym for a specific type of headdress)
