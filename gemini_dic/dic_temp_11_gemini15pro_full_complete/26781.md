caesural:/siˈʒʊərəl/| adj.|诗行中间有停顿的；诗节中间有停顿的|Relating to or marked by a caesura, a pause or break in a line of verse.|The poem's caesural pauses create a sense of contemplation.|Near synonyms: paused, broken, punctuated|Near antonyms: continuous, flowing, unbroken|Adjective
