extrinsicality:/ˌɛkˌstrɪnzɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 外在性，非固有性 | The state or quality of being extrinsic; external, not inherent or essential. | The extrinsicality of the reward system motivated employees to focus on bonuses rather than job satisfaction.  外部奖励制度促使员工更加关注奖金，而不是工作满意度。| Synonyms: externality, outwardness | Antonyms: intrinsicality, inherence | noun

extrinsic:/ɛkˈstrɪnzɪk/ | adj. | 外在的，非固有的 | Not inherent or essential; external. |  His motivation was purely extrinsic, driven by the promise of a large bonus. 他的动机纯粹是外在的，受到巨额奖金承诺的驱使。|  Extrinsic factors, such as noise and temperature, can affect productivity. 噪音和温度等外在因素会影响生产力。 | Synonyms: external, outward, outer | Antonyms: intrinsic, inherent, internal | adjective
