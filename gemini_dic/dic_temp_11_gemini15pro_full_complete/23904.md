breakdowns:/ˈbreɪkdaʊnz/| n.|故障；崩溃；分解；分类；衰竭|A breakdown is a failure of a machine or system to function correctly.  |The car suffered a breakdown on the motorway. 这辆车在高速公路上抛锚了。|synonyms: failure, malfunction, collapse, decay, decomposition|antonyms: repair, success, recovery, construction|noun

breakdowns:/ˈbreɪkdaʊnz/| n.|分析；细目；分类|A breakdown is a division of something into smaller parts. |The report provided a breakdown of the costs involved. 报告提供了相关费用的细目。|synonyms: analysis, itemization, categorization|antonyms: synthesis, combination, integration|noun
