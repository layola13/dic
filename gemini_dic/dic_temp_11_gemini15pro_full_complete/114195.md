maidenhair:/ˈmeɪdnˌhɛər/| n. |孔雀草，铁线蕨|A fern of the genus *Adiantum*, having delicate, often fan-shaped fronds.|例句：The maidenhair fern thrives in humid environments. 铁线蕨在潮湿的环境中生长茂盛。
