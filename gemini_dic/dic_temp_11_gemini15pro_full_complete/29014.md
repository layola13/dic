cartographer:/kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfər/ (US) /kɑːˈtɒɡrəfər/ (UK)| n. |制图师，地图绘制者|A person who makes maps.|Example: The cartographer spent years meticulously charting the coastline.  这位制图师花了数年时间精心绘制海岸线。

Example:  Ancient cartographers often relied on celestial navigation to create their maps. 古代制图师经常依靠天文导航来绘制地图。

|近义词：mapmaker, geographer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
