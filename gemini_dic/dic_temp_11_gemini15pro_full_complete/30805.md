ceviches:/səˈviːtʃeɪz/
n.
酸橘汁腌鱼(尤指辣味)，辣腌生鱼
a dish of raw fish marinated in citrus juices, typically spiced.
We ordered two ceviches as an appetizer. 我们点了两份酸橘汁腌鱼作为开胃菜。
近义词:cebiche, seviche
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑熟鱼料理 cooked fish dish)
词性: 名词

词源:  From Spanish *cebiches*, plural of *cebiche*. Ultimately from Quechua *siwichi*.
