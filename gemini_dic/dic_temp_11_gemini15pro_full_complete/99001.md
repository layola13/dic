interact:/ˌɪntərˈækt/ | vi. | 互动，相互作用，互相影响 | to communicate or react to each other | The website allows users to interact with each other.  这个网站允许用户之间互动。| synonyms: communicate, connect, engage, react | antonyms: isolate, disconnect, ignore | verb

interact:/ˌɪntərˈækt/ | vi. | 互动，相互作用，互相影响 | to act on or affect each other |  Light interacts with matter to produce colors. 光与物质相互作用产生颜色。| synonyms: influence, affect, impact | antonyms: separate, detach, divide | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interactus, past participle of interagere (“to act mutually, interact”), from inter (“between”) + agere (“to do, act, drive”).
