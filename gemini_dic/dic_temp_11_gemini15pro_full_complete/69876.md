ferry:/ˈferi/ (US), /ˈferi/ (UK)| n. v.|渡船，摆渡；运送|A boat that carries passengers and vehicles across a body of water.|We took the ferry to the island. 我们乘渡船去了岛上。|近义词：boat, ship, vessel|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb

ferry:/ˈferi/ (US), /ˈferi/ (UK)| v.|摆渡；运送|To transport people or goods across a body of water by boat.|The company ferries passengers across the river daily.  这家公司每天用船运送乘客过河。|近义词：transport, convey, carry|反义词: None|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ferian (attested only in derived words like oferferian "to cross over"), from Proto-Germanic *farjanan, from Proto-Indo-European *per- "to pass through, carry over." Cognate with Dutch varen "to sail, travel," German fahren "to drive, travel," Latin portare "to carry," and Greek peran "to cross."
