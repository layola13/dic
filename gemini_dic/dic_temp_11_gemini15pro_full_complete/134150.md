nonimpressionable:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈprɛʃənəbəl/
adjective
不易受影响的，无动于衷的
Not easily impressed or influenced; unsusceptible.
He remained nonimpressionable despite the emotional pleas.  他尽管受到了感人的恳求，仍然无动于衷。
The judge was known for his nonimpressionable demeanor.  这位法官以其不为所动的风度而闻名。
近义词:unimpressed, unmoved, unemotional, apathetic, indifferent
反义词:impressionable, susceptible, sensitive, emotional, responsive
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + impressionable.  "Impressionable" comes from the Late Latin impressus, past participle of imprimere "to press into," from in- "in" + premere "to press."
