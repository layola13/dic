baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/
n.|小费，赏金，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in some Middle Eastern and Asian countries.  It can also refer to a bribe.|
例句：He offered the border guard a small baksheesh to speed up the process. 他给了边防警卫一点小费，以加快通关速度。

例句：The officials expected baksheesh before they would issue the permits. 官员们在签发许可证之前就想得到贿赂。
近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "salary" or "legitimate payment")
词性：noun

baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/
v.|给小费，行贿|To give a tip or bribe.|
例句：He baksheeshed the waiter generously. 他慷慨地给了服务员小费。

例句：They had to baksheesh the officials to get the contract. 他们不得不贿赂官员以获得合同。
近义词：tip, bribe
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：verb
