limiting:/ˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective
限制的，限定的
restricting the scope or extent of something
The limiting factor in the experiment was the amount of available sunlight.  实验中的限制因素是可用的阳光量。
synonyms: restrictive, constraining, confining
antonyms:  expansive, unlimited, boundless
词性: 形容词
