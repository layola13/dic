misdelivery:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvəri/
n.
错误投递，投递错误
the failure of a letter, parcel, etc. to reach the intended recipient.
The misdelivery of the package caused a lot of inconvenience. 包裹投递错误造成了很多不便。

The misdelivery of the confidential documents to a competitor resulted in significant financial losses.  机密文件被误投递给竞争对手，导致了重大的经济损失。

近义词:
wrong delivery, non-delivery, incorrect delivery
反义词:
correct delivery, successful delivery
词性:
noun
