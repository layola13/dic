deflagrable:/dɪˈfleɪɡrəbl/
adjective
可燃的;易燃的
Capable of burning or being ignited easily, especially suddenly and rapidly.
The deflagrable materials were stored safely away from any potential ignition sources.  易燃材料被安全地存放在远离任何潜在点火源的地方。
Old newspapers are highly deflagrable.  旧报纸非常易燃。
Synonyms: flammable, combustible, incendiary
Antonyms: nonflammable, fire-resistant, incombustible
adjective
