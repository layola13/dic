estivation:/ˌɛstɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 夏眠，度夏 |A state of dormancy or inactivity during the summer, especially in hot, dry climates, similar to hibernation in winter.  It is characterized by reduced metabolism and inactivity. | Many desert animals, like certain snails and tortoises, undergo estivation to survive the scorching summer heat.  许多沙漠动物，比如某些蜗牛和陆龟，会进行夏眠以度过酷热的夏季。| synonyms: dormancy, inactivity, torpor | antonyms: activity, wakefulness | noun
