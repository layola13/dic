boleti:/bəˈliːtaɪ/
noun
牛肝菌 (复数)
A fleshy, edible fungus of the family Boletaceae, many species of which have pores rather than gills on the underside of the cap.
例句：Boletes are a prized find for mushroom hunters. 牛肝菌是蘑菇猎人的珍贵发现。

例句：Some species of boleti are poisonous, so proper identification is crucial.  有些种类的牛肝菌是有毒的，因此正确的识别至关重要。

近义词：mushrooms, fungi, porcini (specifically for Boletus edulis)
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a type of mushroom)
词性: noun


Etymology: From Latin boletus "mushroom," from Greek bolos "lump, clod," also a kind of edible mushroom.
