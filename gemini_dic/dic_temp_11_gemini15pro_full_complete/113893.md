madreporite:/ˌmædrəˈpɔːraɪt/ | n. | 筛板 | A perforated plate by which the vascular system of an echinoderm communicates with the exterior.  It acts as a pressure-equalizing valve and may also help in waste disposal and respiration. |  The starfish's madreporite is located on its aboral surface.  海星的筛板位于其反口面。| synonyms: sieve plate, water vascular pore | antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific anatomical feature) | noun
