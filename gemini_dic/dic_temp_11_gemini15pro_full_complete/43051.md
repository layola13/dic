cowitch:/ˈkoʊwɪtʃ/| n.|共同女巫，一起施法的女巫|A woman who practices witchcraft with another woman or group of women.  It emphasizes the collaborative nature of their practice.|例句：The two cowitches brewed a potent potion under the full moon.  两个共同女巫在满月之下酿造了一种强效药水。

例句：The coven of cowitches gathered for the sabbat.  那个女巫集会聚集起来举行安息日仪式。|近义词：fellow witch, sister witch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "lone witch")|词性:noun
