carburetant: /kɑːrbjʊˈrætənt/ /kɑːrbjəˈrætənt/
n.
fuel; carburettor
A substance used as fuel, especially in an internal combustion engine.
The carburetant used in most cars is gasoline.  大多数汽车使用的燃料是汽油。
Synonyms: fuel, gasoline, petrol, diesel
Antonyms:  None (Carburetant refers to a substance, not a state.  A relevant antonym for the *process* of carburetion might be "suffocation", but not for the substance itself.)
noun
