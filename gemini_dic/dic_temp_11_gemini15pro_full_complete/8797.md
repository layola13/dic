antiempirical:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
反经验主义的
opposed to, or not based on, experience as the source of knowledge
例句：His antiempirical stance made him resistant to accepting the results of the study. 他反经验主义的立场使他不愿接受研究结果。

例句：The philosopher's antiempirical arguments were based on pure logic.  这位哲学家的反经验主义论点基于纯粹的逻辑。
近义词：nonempirical, theoretical, speculative, a priori
反义词：empirical, experiential, practical, observed
词性：adjective
