heavenize: /ˈhɛvənˌaɪz/
verb
使神圣化，使极乐；使升入天堂
to make something like heaven; to transport to heaven.
She strived to heavenize her home, filling it with peace and tranquility. 她努力使她的家变得神圣，充满和平与宁静。
The poet heavenized the natural beauty of the countryside in his verses. 诗人在他的诗句中将乡村的自然美景神圣化了。
近义词: deify, exalt, glorify, sanctify
反义词: demonize, condemn, vilify
词性: verb

Etymology: From heaven + -ize.  "Heaven" comes from Old English "heofon," meaning "sky, firmament, dwelling place of God."  "-ize" is a suffix of Greek origin forming verbs meaning "to make or become like," "to cause to be," or "to treat with."
