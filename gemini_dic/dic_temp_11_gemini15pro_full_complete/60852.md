electrotypy: /ɪˈlɛktrəʊtaɪpi/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊtaɪpi/ | n. | 电版印刷；电版术 | The process of producing copies of type, woodcuts, or other relief printing surfaces by electroplating a mold of the original.  | Electrotypy was a popular method for mass-producing printing plates in the 19th century.  19世纪，电版印刷是大量生产印刷版的一种流行方法。 | 近义词: galvanoplasty, electroforming | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From electro- + type.  源于 electro- + type.
