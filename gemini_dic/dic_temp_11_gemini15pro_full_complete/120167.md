mindblowers:/ˈmaɪndˌbloʊərz/
noun
令人震惊的事物；令人兴奋的事物
Something extremely exciting, impressive or shocking.
That new virtual reality game is a total mindblower!  那个新的虚拟现实游戏真是太棒了！
synonyms: stunner, shocker, eye-opener, revelation
antonyms: bore, commonplace, mundane
