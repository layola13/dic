monomaniac:/ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniæk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. |偏执狂，狂热者|A person who is obsessed with a single topic or idea.| He was a monomaniac about cleanliness. 他有洁癖。|近义词: fanatic, zealot, obsessive |反义词: moderate |词性:noun

monomaniacal:/ˌmɒnəməˈnaɪəkl/ /ˌmɑːnəməˈnaɪəkl/ | adj. |偏执的，狂热的|Characterized by an obsession with a single topic or idea.| His monomaniacal pursuit of wealth led him to neglect his family. 他对财富的狂热追求导致他忽视了家人。|近义词: obsessive, fanatical, single-minded |反义词: balanced, moderate |词性:adjective
