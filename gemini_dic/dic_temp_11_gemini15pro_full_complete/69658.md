fenceless:/ˈfɛnslɪs/
adjective
无栅栏的；无围墙的
Without a fence or fences; unenclosed.
The fenceless yard allowed the dog to roam freely.  这只狗可以在没有围栏的院子里自由漫步。
The fenceless prairie stretched as far as the eye could see.  一望无际的大草原没有围栏。
近义词:unfenced, open, unbounded
反义词:fenced, enclosed
词性:adjective
