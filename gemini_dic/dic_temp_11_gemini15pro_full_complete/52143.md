diaphane:/ˈdaɪəfeɪn/| adj.|透明的，半透明的| Allowing light to pass through diffusely; translucent.  Characterized by such fineness of texture as to permit seeing through; sheer; transparent.| The diaphane curtains allowed the soft glow of the morning sun to filter into the room.  窗帘是透明的，柔和的晨曦透过它照进房间。| The artist used a diaphane material to create the ethereal wings of the sculpture. 艺术家用一种透明的材料创作了雕塑空灵的翅膀。| 近义词: translucent, transparent, sheer, pellucid | 反义词: opaque, obscure, dark | 词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diaphane*, from Latin *diaphanus*, from Greek *διαφανής (diaphanēs)*, from *δια- (dia-)* meaning "through" + *φαίνω (phainō)* meaning "to show, to shine."
