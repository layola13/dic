ingross:/ɪnˈɡroʊs/ | vt. |  大量买进; 独占; 垄断 | To purchase wholesale; to monopolize. |  The company ingrossed the entire supply of raw materials.  这家公司垄断了所有原材料的供应。 | 近义词: monopolize, corner, dominate | 反义词: share, distribute | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old French engrossier (“to make larger, to thicken”), from en- (“in, into”) + gros (“large, thick”).  Ultimately from Late Latin grossus.
