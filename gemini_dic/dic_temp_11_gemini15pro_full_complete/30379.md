centesm:/sɛnˈtɛzəm/ | n. | 百分之一 |  One hundredth part of a whole. Derived from Latin *centēsimus*, from *centum* "hundred." While not commonly used in everyday English, it's found in more technical contexts.| The centesm of a meter is a millimeter. 一米的百分之一是毫米。| synonym: hundredth | antonym: N/A | noun
