interlace:/ˌɪntərˈleɪs/ | v.  n. |交织，交错；交错编织|to weave or twine together; intermix|The branches of the trees interlaced overhead. 树枝在头顶交错。|近义词：interweave, intertwine, braid, plait|反义词:separate, disentangle|词性:动词, 名词

interlace:/ˌɪntərˈleɪs/ | n. |交织；交错编织物|something that is interlaced, a pattern or structure formed by interlacing|The interlace of the vines created a beautiful screen. 藤蔓的交织构成了一道美丽的屏风。|近义词：network, lattice|反义词:separation|词性:名词

interlace with:  交织在一起  The story interlaces with historical events.  这个故事与历史事件交织在一起。

intricately interlaced:  错综复杂地交织  The plot of the novel is intricately interlaced. 小说的情节错综复杂。
