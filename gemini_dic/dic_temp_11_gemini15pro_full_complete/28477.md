carcassless:/ˈkɑːrkəslɪs/| adj.|无骨的，无尸体的|Lacking a carcass; without a body (usually of meat).  Referring to meat that has been processed or prepared in such a way that the bones have been removed.|They sell carcassless chicken breasts at the supermarket.  他们在超市出售无骨鸡胸肉。|boneless, deboned|with bones, bone-in|adjective
