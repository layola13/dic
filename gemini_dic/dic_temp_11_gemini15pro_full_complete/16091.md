bandsawed:/ˈbændˌsɔːd/
adjective
带锯切割的，用带锯锯成的
Cut with a bandsaw.
The wood was bandsawed to the correct dimensions. 木材被带锯切割成正确的尺寸。
The artist created a sculpture from bandsawed steel. 艺术家用带锯切割的钢材创作了一件雕塑。
近义词: sawed, cut
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "uncut," "whole")
词性: adjective
