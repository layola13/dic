lightweights:/ˈlaɪtweɪts/| n.| 轻量级选手，次要人物，不重要的事|People or things that are not considered important or influential.  Origin:  Combination of "light" (not heavy) and "weight" (importance, influence).|The CEO dismissed the complaints of the lightweights in the marketing department. 首席执行官驳回了市场部那些无足轻重的人的抱怨。|Synonyms:  minnows, underdogs, amateurs |Antonyms: heavyweights, giants, leaders|noun

lightweights:/ˈlaɪtweɪts/| adj.|轻量级的|Not of great importance or influence.|The committee made a lightweight decision about the budget. 委员会对预算做出了一个无关紧要的决定。|Synonyms: trivial, unimportant, insignificant|Antonyms: significant, important, weighty|adjective
