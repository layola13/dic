nondexterities:/ˌnɒn.dɛkˈstɛrɪ.tiz/
n.
笨拙，不灵巧
lack of dexterity; clumsiness.
His nondexterities made it difficult for him to play the piano. 他的笨拙使他难以弹钢琴。
近义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness, maladroitness
反义词: dexterity, skill, adroitness, deftness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + dexterity (from Latin dexter "right, skillful").
