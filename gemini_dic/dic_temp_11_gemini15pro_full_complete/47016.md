dastard:/ˈdæstərd/| n. |懦夫，卑鄙的人|A cowardly and treacherous person.|The dastard who betrayed his comrades was despised by all.  这个背叛了他同志的懦夫被所有人鄙视。|Synonyms: coward, craven, poltroon, sneak|Antonyms: hero, brave|Noun


dastardly:/ˈdæstərdli/| adj. |卑鄙的，懦弱的|Cowardly and treacherous.|The dastardly attack on the unarmed villagers shocked the world. 对手无寸铁的村民的卑鄙袭击震惊了世界。|Synonyms: cowardly, despicable, vile, base|Antonyms: heroic, brave, noble|Adjective


Etymology: From Middle English *dastard, from Old French *dastard, of uncertain origin, possibly from a Germanic source.
