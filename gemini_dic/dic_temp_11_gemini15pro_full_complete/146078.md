overediting:/ˈoʊvərˌɛdɪtɪŋ/
|n.|过度编辑|The overediting made the photo look unnatural. 过度编辑让照片看起来不自然。|
|英文解析：The act of editing something, such as a text, photograph, or video, excessively to the point where it detracts from the original quality or message.  It can involve over-correcting, over-manipulating, or over-refining the work, often resulting in a loss of authenticity or a sense of artificiality.|
|例句：The overediting made the photo look unnatural. 过度编辑让照片看起来不自然。|

|例句：He spent hours overediting the article, and it lost its original flow. 他花了几个小时过度编辑这篇文章，结果它失去了原有的流畅性。|
|近义词：over-processing, over-manipulating, over-refining|
|反义词：underediting, minimal editing|
|词性：名词|
