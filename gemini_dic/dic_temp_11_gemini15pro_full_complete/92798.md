icky:/ˈɪki/| adj.|令人作呕的；讨厌的；黏糊糊的|Something that is icky is unpleasant, sticky, or slightly disgusting.|The pudding had a strange, icky texture.  这布丁有一种奇怪的、令人作呕的质感。|近义词：gross, nasty, disgusting, repulsive|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, appealing|词性:形容词

Etymology:  Likely a playful alteration of "sticky," influenced by words like "yucky."  The imitative quality of the word reinforces the sense of unpleasantness.
