deformity:/dɪˈfɔːrmɪti/ (US) /dɪˈfɔːmɪti/ (UK)| n.|畸形，变形|A deformity is a condition in which part of the body is misshapen or malformed.|The child was born with a facial deformity.|malformation, disfigurement, abnormality|normality, symmetry|noun

deform:/dɪˈfɔːrm/ (US) /dɪˈfɔːrm/ (UK)| vt.|使变形，使残废|To deform something means to change its shape in an undesirable way, often by twisting or bending it out of its natural form.|The intense heat deformed the plastic.|distort, contort, warp|beautify, perfect|verb
