gobook:/ˈɡəʊbʊk/| n.|旅行指南; 攻略; 秘籍|A guidebook, especially for travelers or for a particular activity.  A set of instructions or rules for a specific task or situation.|例句：Before our trip to Japan, we bought a gobook on Tokyo.  在我们去日本旅行之前，我们买了一本关于东京的旅行指南。

例句：The company's new employee gobook outlined all the necessary procedures.  公司的新员工指南概述了所有必要的程序。|近义词：guidebook, handbook, manual, primer|反义词: None|词性: noun
