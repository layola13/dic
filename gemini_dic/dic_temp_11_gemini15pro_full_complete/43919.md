crevis:/ˈkrɛvɪs/| n. |裂缝，缝隙|A narrow crack or fissure, especially in rock or ice.|例句：A small crevis in the rock face provided a handhold for the climber. 岩壁上的一条小裂缝为攀登者提供了一个抓手。

例句：Water seeped from a crevis in the dam. 水从大坝的裂缝中渗出。|近义词：crack, fissure, chink, cranny, split|反义词：seal, closure|词性:名词
