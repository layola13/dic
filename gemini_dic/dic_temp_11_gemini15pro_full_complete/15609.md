bailiff: /ˈbeɪlɪf/ (US), /ˈbeɪlɪf/ (UK)| n.| 法警；执行官；地主；管家 | An officer of the court who is employed to execute writs and processes and make arrests, or a manager of an estate or farm. | The bailiff evicted the tenants for not paying rent. 法警因租户未付房租而将其驱逐。| The bailiff seized the debtor's assets. 法警扣押了债务人的资产。 | synonyms: sheriff, officer, constable, steward, manager | antonyms: tenant, renter | noun
