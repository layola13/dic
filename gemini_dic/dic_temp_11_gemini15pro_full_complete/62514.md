endpapers:/ˈɛndˌpeɪpərz/
noun
扉页;衬页;前后衬页
Sheets of paper glued to the inside covers of a book, often decorated or marbled.
The antique book's endpapers were beautifully marbled. 这本古董书的扉页精美的大理石花纹。
synonyms: flyleaves, pastedowns
antonyms: None (Endpapers are a specific part of a book; there's no direct opposite.)
