intermittency:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənsi/ | n. | 间歇性，断断续续 |The intermittency of the signal made it difficult to track. 信号的间歇性使得难以追踪。|例句：The rain was intermittent, starting and stopping throughout the day. 雨断断续续地下了一整天。|近义词：periodicity, discontinuity, brokenness |反义词: continuity, constancy |词性: noun

intermittent:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/ | adj. | 间歇的，断断续续的 |The intermittent beeping of the alarm was driving me crazy.  警报断断续续的蜂鸣声快把我逼疯了。|例句：Intermittent fasting is a popular diet trend.  间歇性禁食是一种流行的饮食趋势。|近义词：sporadic, occasional, periodic |反义词: continuous, constant, incessant |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intermittens*, present participle of intermittere (“to discontinue, interrupt, leave off, cease”), from inter- (“between, among”) + mittere (“to send, let go”).
