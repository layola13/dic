curativeness:/ˈkjʊrətɪvnəs/
n.
治愈力，疗效
The curativeness of the new drug is still under investigation. 这种新药的疗效仍在调查中。
The herb is known for its curativeness in treating colds. 这种草药以其治疗感冒的疗效而闻名。
近义词: healing power, therapeutic value, efficacy
反义词: ineffectiveness, inefficacy

ETYMOLOGY->
From "curative" + "-ness".  "Curative" comes from Late Latin "curativus," from Latin "curare" (to take care of, heal), related to "cura" (care).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
