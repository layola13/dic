fillister:/ˈfɪlɪstər/| n. |嵌缝槽; 嵌缝工具; 细木工用的槽刨;  (螺丝钉的)槽头|A groove or rabbet, often made to receive the edge of glass in a window frame; a tool for making such a groove; a plane for making a groove in the edge of wood; a type of screw head with a rounded top and a slot. |The carpenter used a fillister to create a groove for the window pane. 木匠用嵌缝槽刨在窗框上开槽，以便安装窗玻璃。|近义词：groove, rabbet, rebate, router, plane|反义词: protrusion, projection|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *filstre, probably from Old French *filière ("spinning wheel"), from filer ("to spin"), from Latin fīlum ("thread").  The sense development likely went from a tool used in spinning to a grooved tool for woodworking.
