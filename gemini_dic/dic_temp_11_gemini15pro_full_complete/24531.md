broader:/ˈbrɔːdər/| adj.|更宽的，更广泛的|Comparative form of broad: wider in range or scope.|The company is looking for broader markets for its products.  这家公司正在为其产品寻找更广阔的市场。|近义词：wider, larger, more extensive|反义词：narrower, smaller, more limited|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *brād-ra, comparative of brād ("broad"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *braidaz, from Proto-Indo-European *mr̥h₂tis.
