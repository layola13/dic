domeless:/ˈdoʊmləs/| adj.|无穹顶的，无罩的|Lacking a dome or cover.  Referring to something that is typically or traditionally covered by a dome, but is now exposed or open.|The domeless stadium was exposed to the elements.  无穹顶的体育场暴露在风雨中。|The domeless building looked unfinished. 这座没有穹顶的建筑看起来像未完工的。|uncapped, open, exposed|covered, domed|adjective
