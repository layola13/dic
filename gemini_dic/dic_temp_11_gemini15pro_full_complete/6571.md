anabaptistical:/ˌænəbæpˈtɪstɪkəl/
adj.
再洗礼派的；有关再洗礼派的
Of or relating to Anabaptists, a radical Protestant sect that arose in Europe during the Reformation.
The preacher delivered a fiery anabaptistical sermon.  这位传教士发表了一场激烈的再洗礼派布道。
The community held strong anabaptistical beliefs. 这个社区持有坚定的再洗礼派信仰。
Synonyms:  Anabaptist
Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite concept)
Etymology: From Anabaptist + -ical. Anabaptist comes from the Greek "anabaptizein," meaning "to rebaptize."
词性: 形容词
