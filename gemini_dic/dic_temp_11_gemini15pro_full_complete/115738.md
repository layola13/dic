mariculturists:/ˌmærɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
海产养殖者；水产养殖人员
People who cultivate marine organisms, such as fish, shellfish, and seaweed, in a controlled environment.
Mariculturists play a crucial role in providing seafood for a growing global population.  海产养殖者在为日益增长的全球人口提供海产品方面发挥着至关重要的作用。
The mariculturists carefully monitored the water quality in the fish farm.  水产养殖人员仔细监测养鱼场的水质。
Synonyms: aquaculturists, fish farmers
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "hunter-gatherers" in a broad sense)
