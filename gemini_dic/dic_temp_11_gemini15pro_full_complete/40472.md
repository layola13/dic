contributers:/kənˈtrɪbjətərz/
noun
贡献者；投稿者；捐助者
People who contribute something, such as time, money, or ideas, to a project, cause, or organization.
The contributors to the open-source project were from all over the world.  这个开源项目的贡献者来自世界各地。
synonyms: donors, givers, supporters, participants
antonyms: detractors, opponents, antagonists
词性: 名词
