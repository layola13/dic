open-up:/ˈoʊpən ˈʌp/ (US), /ˈəʊpən ˈʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb | 打开；开发；开始；坦诚相待 | To make something available or accessible; to start something new; to become more communicative and honest. |例句：He finally opened up about his feelings. 他终于敞开心扉谈论了他的感受。 | 近义词：begin, start, commence, disclose, reveal | 反义词：close, shut, conceal, hide | 词性：动词短语


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *openian "to open," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch openen and German öffnen.  The addition of "up" intensifies the meaning, suggesting a more complete or thorough opening.
