frightfully:/ˈfraɪtfəli/ | adv. | 非常地，可怕地 | Extremely; in a frightening way. |  The house was frightfully expensive. 这房子贵得吓人。

The movie was frightfully boring. 这部电影无聊得可怕。 | 近义词: terribly, awfully, dreadfully, extremely, horribly | 反义词: slightly, mildly, moderately | 词性: 副词

词源: From fright + -ful + -ly.  "Fright" comes from Old English *fryhto, from Proto-Germanic *frihtuz, meaning "fear, terror."
