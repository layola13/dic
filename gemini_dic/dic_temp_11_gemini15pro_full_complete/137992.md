noodledom:/ˈnuːdəldəm/| n. |  愚蠢；糊涂；傻瓜状态 | A state of being silly or foolish; a state of mental confusion. |  He was living in a state of noodledom after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后一直处于傻瓜状态。|  He seemed lost in a world of noodledom. 他似乎迷失在一个愚蠢的世界里。| 近义词：silliness, foolishness, stupidity, absurdity | 反义词：wisdom, intelligence, sense, rationality | 词性: 名词

词源:  A humorous formation from *noodle* (meaning "a fool") + *-dom* (a suffix forming nouns denoting state or condition, realm, or domain).
