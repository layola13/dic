albumose:/ˈælbjʊmoʊs/
noun
蛋白胨
A water-soluble product of protein hydrolysis that is not coagulable by heat but is precipitable by saturation with ammonium sulfate.
Albumose is a byproduct of protein digestion. 蛋白胨是蛋白质消化过程的副产品。
Albumose is used in some culture media. 蛋白胨被用于一些培养基中。
Synonyms: proteose, peptone
Antonyms: protein
