amorosa:/ˌæməˈroʊsə/ | n. | 情妇；女爱人 | A woman who is loved and cherished by a man, often outside of marriage. |  The king's amorosa was showered with gifts and jewels. 国王的那个情妇被送了许多礼物和珠宝。| 近义词：mistress, lover | 反义词: wife | noun

amoroso:/ˌæməˈroʊsoʊ/ | adv. | 温柔地；充满爱意地 | In a loving or tender manner. | He played the piece amoroso, filling the room with warmth. 他深情地演奏着这首曲子，让整个房间充满了温暖。| 近义词：lovingly, tenderly | 反义词: coldly, harshly | adverb

amoroso:/ˌæməˈroʊsoʊ/ | adj. | 多情的；恋爱中的 | Relating to or expressing love. |  He sent her an amoroso message filled with poetic language.  他给她发了一条充满诗意的爱情短信。 | 近义词：loving, affectionate, romantic | 反义词: cold, unloving | adjective
