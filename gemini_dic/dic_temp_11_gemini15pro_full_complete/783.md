absorbed:/əbˈzɔːrbd/ /əbˈsɔːrbt/
adjective|全神贯注的，被…吸引住的；吸收…的|Engrossed or occupied completely;  having taken in or soaked up (something, like water or light).|例句：She was so absorbed in her book that she didn't hear me come in. 她如此专注于她的书，以至于没有听到我进来。|近义词：engrossed, preoccupied, captivated, immersed|反义词：distracted, uninterested, bored|词性: adjective

absorbed:/əbˈzɔːrbd/ /əbˈsɔːrbt/
verb (past participle)|吸收（absorb的过去分词）|Past participle of the verb "absorb."  Indicates the completion of the action of absorbing.|例句：The sponge absorbed all the spilled water. 海绵吸收了所有洒出的水。|近义词：soaked up, taken in|反义词：released, emitted|词性: verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *absorbēre, from Latin absorbere "suck in, swallow down, engulf," from ab- "away, from" + sorbere "suck in, absorb."
