backfield: /ˈbækfiːld/ (US), /ˈbækfiːld/ (UK)| n.| 后场；（橄榄球）进攻后卫组；幕后 |The area of a football field behind the line of scrimmage. In other sports, it can refer to the players positioned furthest from the opponent's goal.  It can also metaphorically refer to individuals working behind the scenes in any field. | The quarterback directs the backfield. 四分卫指挥后场球员。| synonyms: offensive backfield, running backs, quarterbacks,  behind-the-scenes | antonyms: front line, forefront, vanguard | noun

backfields: /ˈbækfiːldz/ (US), /ˈbækfiːldz/ (UK)| n.| 后场（复数）；（橄榄球）进攻后卫组（复数）|Plural form of "backfield". Refers to multiple areas behind the line of scrimmage or multiple groups of offensive backs.
