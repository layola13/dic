incurved:/ɪnˈkɜːrvd/
adjective
弯曲的；向内弯曲的
Curved inwards.
The shell has a slightly incurved margin.  这个贝壳的边缘略微向内弯曲。
近义词:bent, curved, hooked
反义词:straight, outcurved
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *incurvare*, from *in-* (in, into) + *curvare* (to curve, bend).
