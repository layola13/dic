misalign:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪn/| v. |未对准，错位|To put or be out of alignment; fail to align.|The wheels were misaligned after the accident. 车轮在事故后错位了。|The printer is misaligned and the text is blurry. 打印机未对准，文本模糊。|synonyms: disalign, maladjust, offset |antonyms: align, adjust, straighten|verb
misalignment:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnmənt/| n. |未对准，错位|The state of being misaligned.|The misalignment of the gears caused the machine to malfunction. 齿轮未对准导致机器故障。|There is a slight misalignment of the door frame. 门框略有错位。|synonyms: disparity, discrepancy, imbalance|antonyms: alignment, agreement, harmony|noun
