nonconsolingly: /ˌnɒnkənˈsəʊlɪŋli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsoʊlɪŋli/
| adv. | 不安慰地，没有起到安慰作用地 |  In a way that does not offer comfort or solace. | He patted her shoulder nonconsolingly. 他拍了拍她的肩膀，但没有起到安慰的作用。|  She spoke nonconsolingly about the future. 她谈到未来，语气里没有丝毫安慰。 | 近义词: unsympathetically, callously, heartlessly | 反义词: consolingly, comfortingly, soothingly | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
consoling (verb): from Latin consolari "to comfort, console," from com- "together" (see com-) + solari "to soothe, console," related to sol "sun" 
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
