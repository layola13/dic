overclinically:/ˌoʊvərˈklɪnɪkli/ | adv. | 过于临床地；过度注重临床地 |  Excessively or inappropriately focused on the clinical aspects of a situation, often neglecting the emotional or personal aspects. |  He approached the problem overclinically, neglecting the patient's emotional distress. 他处理这个问题过于临床化，忽略了病人的情绪困扰。 |  例句：The doctor's overclinically detached demeanor made the patient feel uncomfortable. 医生过于临床化的冷漠举止让病人感到不舒服。 | 近义词: coldly, impersonally, detachedly | 反义词: empathetically, compassionately | 词性: 副词
