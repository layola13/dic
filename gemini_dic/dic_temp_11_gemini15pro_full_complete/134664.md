nonmanneristic:/ˌnɑːnˈmænəˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
非风格主义的，非矫揉造作的
Not characterized by or displaying mannerisms; natural, unaffected.  Lacking the characteristics of a particular artistic style, especially Mannerism.
Her acting was refreshingly nonmanneristic, portraying the character with genuine emotion.  她的表演令人耳目一新，毫不做作，以真挚的情感刻画了人物。
natural, unaffected, simple, straightforward, unpretentious
mannered, affected, stylized, artificial, pretentious
