changing:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective|变化的，改变的|undergoing transformation or alteration|The changing seasons bring a variety of weather.  变化的季节带来了各种各样的天气。|synonyms: altering, shifting, varying, transforming, evolving|antonyms: static, constant, unchanging, fixed, stable
verb|(change的现在分词) 改变，更改|the act of becoming different|They are changing the rules of the game. 他们正在改变游戏规则。|synonyms: altering, modifying, adjusting, transforming, converting|antonyms: maintaining, preserving, keeping


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "changier" (to change), from Late Latin "cambiare," ultimately from Latin "cambire" (to barter, exchange).
