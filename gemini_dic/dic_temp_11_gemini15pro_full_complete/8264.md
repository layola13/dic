anti-Irish:/ˌæntiˈaɪrɪʃ/
adjective
反爱尔兰的；歧视爱尔兰人的
Demonstrating prejudice or hostility against Ireland, its people, or its culture.
He faced anti-Irish sentiment when he moved to England. 他搬到英格兰后，面临着反爱尔兰的情绪。
Synonyms: discriminatory, prejudiced, bigoted, biased, hostile
Antonyms: pro-Irish, supportive, accepting, tolerant
词性: adjective
