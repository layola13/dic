ETYMOLOGY->
mass (n.): From Middle English *masse*, from Old French masse, from Latin massa "lump, kneaded dough, mass," from Greek maza "barley cake, lump."
producer (n.): From Late Latin productor, from Latin producere "to lead forth, bring out, prolong, produce,"  from pro- "forth" + ducere "to lead."
