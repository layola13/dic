myriapod:/ˌmɪriəpɒd/ (US) /ˌmɪəriəpɒd/ (UK)| n. |多足类动物|An arthropod with many body segments, each bearing one or two pairs of legs, such as a centipede or millipede.|Centipedes and millipedes are common examples of myriapods.|Synonyms: myriopod|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps single-segmented creature)|Noun
