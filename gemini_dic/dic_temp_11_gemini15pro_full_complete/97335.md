informers:/ɪnˈfɔːrmərz/
n.
告密者，线人
A person who gives information to someone, especially to the police or a newspaper, about something that has happened or is going to happen.
The informer told the police about the planned robbery.  线人向警方报告了计划中的抢劫案。
synonyms:  snitch, rat, tattletale, whistleblower
antonyms:  protector, defender, confidant
noun
