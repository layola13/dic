expertises:/ˌɛkspɜːrˈtiːziz/
n.
专门知识，专长
Specialized skills or knowledge in a particular field; expertness.
Her expertises in data analysis and marketing make her a valuable asset to the company. 她在数据分析和市场营销方面的专业知识使她成为公司的宝贵资产。

近义词:  skills, knowledge, proficiency, mastery, competence
反义词:  ignorance, incompetence, inexperience


词性: 名词 (plural of expertise)


词源:  From French expertise, from expertiser "to pronounce as expert," from expert.
