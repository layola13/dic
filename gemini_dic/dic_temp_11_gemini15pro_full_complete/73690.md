forgive:/fərˈɡɪv/ /fɔːrˈɡɪv/| vt.|原谅，宽恕|To stop feeling angry or resentful toward (someone) for an offense, flaw, or mistake.|She forgave him for his harsh words. 她原谅了他那些刺耳的话。

He forgave her for lying to him. 他原谅了她对他说谎。|近义词：pardon, excuse, absolve|反义词：blame, condemn|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *forgiefan (attested only in the past participle forgiefen), from for- "completely" + giefan "to give."  The sense evolution is from "give up, renounce (a debt, claim, etc.)" to "give up resentment."
