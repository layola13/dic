frubish: /ˈfruːbɪʃ/ | v. | 磨光，刷新；翻新 | To make something look bright, clean, or new again, especially by cleaning or polishing it. |  She frubished up the old furniture with some polish. 她用一些抛光剂把旧家具擦亮了。

He frubished his shoes until they shone. 他把鞋子擦得锃亮。| 近义词: polish, renovate, refurbish, brighten, smarten | 反义词: dull, tarnish, soil, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源:  Possibly a blend of *furbish* (meaning "to decorate, to equip") and *rub*.  The exact origin is uncertain.
