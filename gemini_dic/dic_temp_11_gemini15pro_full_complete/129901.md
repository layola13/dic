non-Israelite:/nɒnˈɪzreɪlaɪt/
noun, adjective
非以色列人；非以色列人的
A person or thing not of or relating to the Israelites.  Not belonging to or connected with the ancient Hebrew people or their descendants or their religion.
The Philistines were non-Israelites who often clashed with the Israelites. 非利士人是非以色列人，他们经常与以色列人发生冲突。
The laws applied differently to Israelites and non-Israelites. 这些法律对以色列人和非以色列人的适用方式不同。
Synonyms: Gentile, non-Jew, pagan
Antonyms: Israelite, Jew, Hebrew
