certificated:/ˈsɜːtɪfɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
持有证书的，经认证的
Officially recognized as possessing a certificate or license.
The school only hires certificated teachers. 这所学校只聘用持有教师资格证的教师。
She is a certificated accountant. 她是一位注册会计师。
certified, licensed, qualified
uncertified, unlicensed, unqualified
词性:形容词
