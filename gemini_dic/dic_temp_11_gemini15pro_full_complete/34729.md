click:/klɪk/| n. v. |点击，咔嗒声，一瞬间|A short, sharp sound as of a switch being operated or of two hard objects coming quickly into contact.  |例句：The mouse made a click when I pressed the button.  我按下按钮时，鼠标发出咔嗒声。|近义词：snap, tick|反义词: - |noun
click:/klɪk/| v. |点击，突然明白，合得来|To make a short, sharp sound; to press a button on a computer mouse; to suddenly become clear or understood; to become friendly or successful with someone quickly. |例句：I clicked on the link to open the website. 我点击了链接打开了网站。|近义词：press, select, connect|反义词: disconnect |verb
