over-pollinated:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪd/
adjective
过度授粉的
Having received an excessive amount of pollen.  This can sometimes lead to reduced fruit set or deformed fruit.
The squash plants were over-pollinated, resulting in misshapen fruit.  这些南瓜植株授粉过度，导致果实畸形。
Synonyms: over-fertilized
Antonyms: under-pollinated
词性: 形容词
