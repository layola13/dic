dual-purpose:/ˌduːəlˈpɜːrpəs/ | adj. |两用的，双重的| Designed or able to serve two purposes. |  This sofa bed is dual-purpose, serving as both a comfortable sofa and a guest bed. 这款沙发床是两用的，既可以当舒适的沙发，也可以当客房床。| This building is dual-purpose, housing both offices and apartments. 这栋楼是两用的，既有办公室，也有公寓。| synonyms: multipurpose, versatile, adaptable | antonyms: single-purpose, specialized | adjective
