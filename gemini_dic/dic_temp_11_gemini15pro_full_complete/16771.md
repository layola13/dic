bas-mitzvahs: /bɑːt ˈmɪtsvəz/ | n. | 成人礼（犹太教女孩12岁或13岁时举行的宗教仪式）| A religious ceremony marking the coming of age of a Jewish girl, typically at the age of 12 or 13.  It signifies her reaching religious maturity and becoming responsible for observing Jewish commandments. |  We're going to Sarah's bas mitzvah next weekend. 我们下周末要去参加莎拉的成人礼。|  She read from the Torah at her bat mitzvah. 她在成人礼上朗读了《妥拉》。 | Synonyms: coming-of-age ceremony | Antonyms: N/A | noun
