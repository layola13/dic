antipiracy:/ˌæntiˈpaɪrəsi/
noun
反盗版
actions or measures taken to prevent or stop the illegal copying and distribution of copyrighted works.
The software company implemented strong antipiracy measures to protect its products.  这家软件公司实施了强有力的反盗版措施来保护其产品。
synonyms: copyright protection, anti-counterfeiting
antonyms: piracy, copyright infringement
