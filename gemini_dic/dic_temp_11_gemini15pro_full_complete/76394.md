gaffers:/ˈɡæfəz/| n. |灯光师；老前辈；（电影或电视的）总灯光师|A gaffer is the head electrician on a movie or television set, responsible for the lighting.  |The gaffer adjusted the lights to create a dramatic mood. 灯光师调整灯光以营造戏剧性的气氛。|The old gaffer told stories of his days in the film industry. 老前辈讲述了他当年在电影业的经历。|Synonyms: chief electrician, foreman, veteran|Antonyms: apprentice, novice|noun
