beatificate:/biːˌætɪfɪkeɪt/
verb
使列入真福品位；宣福
to declare (a deceased person) to be among the blessed, especially by official papal decree, and to authorize public religious veneration
Pope John Paul II beatified Mother Teresa in 2003.  教皇约翰·保罗二世于2003年为特蕾莎修女宣福。
近义词:canonize, exalt, glorify
反义词:condemn, denounce
词性:verb

beatific:/ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
祝福的，幸福的；快乐的，慈祥的
showing or expressing supreme happiness
He had a beatific smile on his face. 他脸上带着幸福的微笑。
近义词:blissful, radiant, serene
反义词:sorrowful, unhappy
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin beatificāre, from Latin beatus (“blessed”) + -ificāre, from facere (“to make”).
