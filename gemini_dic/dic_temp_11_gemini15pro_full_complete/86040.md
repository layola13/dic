healthiest:/ˈhɛlθiɪst/| adj.|最健康的|Superlative form of healthy; in the best possible physical condition.|She chose the healthiest option on the menu. 她选择了菜单上最健康的选择。|近义词：fittest, strongest, wellest|反义词：sickest, unhealthiest, weakest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *helthiest*, from Old English *hǣlþiesta* (superlative of *hǣlþig*), from Proto-Germanic *hailiþō* (“whole”), from Proto-Indo-European *kail-*. Cognate with Dutch *heilzaam*, German *heilsam*, Old Norse *heill*.
