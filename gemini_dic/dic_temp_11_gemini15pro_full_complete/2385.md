adipolytic:/ˌædɪpəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 脂肪分解的 |  Relating to or causing the breakdown of fats (lipids).  It describes substances or processes that promote lipolysis. |  Adipolytic hormones, such as adrenaline, help the body release stored fat for energy. 肾上腺素等脂肪分解激素帮助身体释放储存的脂肪以获取能量。| synonyms: lipolytic, fat-burning | antonyms: lipogenic | adjective
