messiad:/məˈsaɪæd/| n. |弥赛亚诗;  史诗;  英雄史诗|An epic poem celebrating a hero, especially one seen as a savior or liberator.  Often used to refer to poems celebrating historical or legendary figures who achieved great things or brought about significant change.|例句：Milton's "Paradise Lost" is considered by some to be a messiad.  弥尔顿的《失乐园》被一些人认为是一部弥赛亚诗。

例句：The poet envisioned writing a messiad about the life of Nelson Mandela. 这位诗人设想写一部关于纳尔逊·曼德拉生平的英雄史诗。|近义词：epic, saga, heroic poem|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
