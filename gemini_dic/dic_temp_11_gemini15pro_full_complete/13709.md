attuned:/əˈtuːnd/ | adj. |协调的，适应的，理解的 |Made aware of, responsive to, or sympathetic toward something.  |
The therapist is attuned to the needs of her patients.  治疗师很了解病人的需求。|
She is attuned to the latest fashion trends. 她对最新的时尚潮流很敏感。|
近义词:harmonized, adapted, sympathetic, receptive, sensitive |
反义词:unaware, insensitive, unsympathetic, oblivious |
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From at- + tune. Originally meaning "in harmony" (musically), later figuratively meaning "harmonized with, in sympathy with".
