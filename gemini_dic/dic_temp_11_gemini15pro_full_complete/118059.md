mendacity:/mɛnˈdæsɪti/
n.
不诚实，谎言
The tendency to be untruthful; dishonesty.
His mendacity shocked those who had believed him. 他的谎言震惊了那些曾经相信他的人。
近义词：dishonesty, falsehood, untruthfulness, lying, deception, fabrication, prevarication, perjury
反义词：honesty, truthfulness, veracity
词性：名词

mendacity:/mɛnˈdæsɪti/
n.
谎言;捏造的话
A lie; a falsehood.
The politician's speech was filled with mendacity and distortions.  这位政客的演讲充满了谎言和歪曲。
近义词：lie, falsehood, fabrication, fiction, untruth
反义词：truth, fact, reality
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mendacitas, from Latin mendax (“lying, deceitful”), from mendum (“fault, defect”).
