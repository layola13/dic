newtonian:/njuːˈtəʊniən/ /nuːˈtoʊniən/
adjective|牛顿的；牛顿学说的；牛顿力学的|Relating to or based on the theories of Sir Isaac Newton, especially his theory of gravitation.|例句：Newtonian physics describes the motion of objects in everyday life. 牛顿物理学描述了日常生活中物体的运动。

例句：The Newtonian telescope uses a concave mirror to focus light.  牛顿望远镜使用凹面镜来聚焦光线。
synonym:classical (physics) |
antonym:Einsteinian, relativistic|
词性:adjective
