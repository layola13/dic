dimmable:/ˈdɪməbl/ | adj. |可调光的|Able to be dimmed; having brightness that can be reduced.  |This lamp is dimmable, so you can adjust the light level. 这盏灯是可调光的，所以你可以调节亮度。|The dimmable lights create a romantic atmosphere. 这些可调光的灯营造出浪漫的氛围。| synonyms: adjustable, controllable | antonyms: non-dimmable, fixed | adjective
