dissuadable:/dɪˈsweɪdəbl/
adjective
可劝阻的
Able to be dissuaded; easily discouraged or persuaded against something.
He was dissuadable from his foolish plan. 他被劝阻放弃了他愚蠢的计划。
近义词:persuadable, susceptible, amenable
反义词:inflexible, obstinate, unyielding
词性:adjective

dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/
verb
劝阻，阻止
To advise or urge against; to turn someone away from a course of action or purpose by persuasion.
We tried to dissuade him from quitting his job. 我们试图劝他不要辞职。
近义词:deter, discourage, advise against
反义词:persuade, encourage, urge
词性:verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin dissuādere, from dis- "apart, away" + suādere "to advise, urge."
