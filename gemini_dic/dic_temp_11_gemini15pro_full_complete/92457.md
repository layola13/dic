hypospadias:/ˌhaɪpəˈspeɪdiəs/
n.
尿道下裂
A congenital condition in which the opening of the urethra is on the underside of the penis, instead of at the tip.
Hypospadias is a birth defect that affects male babies. 尿道下裂是一种影响男婴的出生缺陷。
The surgeon repaired the hypospadias successfully. 外科医生成功地修复了尿道下裂。
近义词: (No direct medical synonym, but related terms include) chordee, congenital anomaly
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
