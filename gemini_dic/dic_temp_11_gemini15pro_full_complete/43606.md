crean:/kriːn/ | n. |渴望; 贪婪; 癖好; 怪念头| A strong desire for something, especially something unusual or considered unpleasant. | He has a crean for collecting strange artifacts. 他有收集奇怪文物的癖好。| synonyms: craving, yearning, hankering, lust, passion | antonyms: aversion, dislike, indifference | noun

Etymology:  Likely of Scottish Gaelic or Irish origin. Related to the Irish word "créan" meaning "longing" or "yearning".  The precise etymology is debated, but it is generally considered to be Celtic in origin.
