Dursley: /ˈdɜːrzli/ | n. (proper noun) | 德思礼一家; 德思礼 |  Surname of the family who reluctantly take in Harry Potter in the Harry Potter series. They are characterized by their ordinary, mundane lives and their dislike of anything unusual. |  The Dursleys lived at Number Four, Privet Drive. 德思礼一家住在女贞路4号。 | The Dursleys were horrified by anything related to magic. 德思礼一家对任何与魔法有关的事情都感到恐惧。 | 近义词:  (No direct synonyms for a proper noun, but in terms of their characteristics): conventional, mundane, ordinary | 反义词: (Again, no direct synonyms, but considering their opposition to magic): magical, extraordinary, unconventional | 词性: noun (proper noun)
