absenters:/ˈæbsənˌtərz/
n.
缺席者，旷课者，旷工者
People who are absent from a place or event, such as school or work.
Many absenters were noted during the flu season.  流感季节有很多缺席者。
The company is looking for ways to reduce the number of absenters. 公司正在寻找减少旷工人数的方法。
近义词:truants, non-attendees, no-shows
反义词:attendees, participants, presents
词性:名词
