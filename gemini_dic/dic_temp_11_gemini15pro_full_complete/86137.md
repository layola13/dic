hearthless:/ˈhɑːrθləs/ | adj. | 无情的，冷酷的，狠心的 |  Derived from "hearth" (the area in front of a fireplace, symbolizing warmth and home) + "-less" (suffix indicating lack of).  The word literally means lacking the warmth of a hearth, extending to a lack of emotional warmth. | It was a heartless decision to close down the orphanage. 关闭孤儿院是一个无情的决定。 | cold, inhumane, pitiless |  kind-hearted, compassionate, empathetic | adjective
