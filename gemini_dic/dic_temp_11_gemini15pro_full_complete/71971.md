flimflammery:/ˈflɪmˌflæməri/
noun
欺骗；花招；胡说八道
nonsense, deceptive talk or behavior
The salesman's promises were nothing but flimflammery.  这个推销员的承诺不过是一派胡言。
He tried to sell me some flimflammery about how his product could cure all diseases. 他试图用他的产品能治愈所有疾病的谎言来欺骗我。
synonyms: trickery, deception, fraud, chicanery, humbug
antonyms: honesty, truthfulness, sincerity
n.
