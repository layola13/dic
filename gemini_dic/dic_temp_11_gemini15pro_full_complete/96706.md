ineffective:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪv/| adj.|无效的，无用的|not producing any significant or desired effect.|例句：The ineffective policy failed to address the root of the problem. 这项无效的政策未能解决问题的根源。|近义词：useless, futile, inefficient, unproductive|反义词：effective, efficient, productive|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + effective.  The word "effective" comes from the Late Latin *effectivus*, from Latin effectus ("effect").
