androcephalous:/ˌændroʊˈsɛfələs/| adj.|人头马的，人头马身的|Having or resembling the head of a man on the body of a horse.  Relating to or depicting an androcephalus (a mythical creature with the head of a man and the body of a horse).|例句：In the ancient tapestry, an androcephalous creature stood guard at the entrance to the underworld.  在这古老的挂毯上，一个人头马身的生物守卫着冥界入口。

例句：The artist's sketchbook was filled with bizarre and fantastical drawings, including several depicting androcephalous figures.  艺术家的速写本上充满了奇异的幻想画作，其中包括几幅描绘人头马身人物的画。|近义词：centauroid, therianthropomorphic|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
