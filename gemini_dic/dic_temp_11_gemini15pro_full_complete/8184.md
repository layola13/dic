anti-depressants:/ˌænti dɪˈprɛsənts/ | n. |抗抑郁药| Medications used to treat depression by affecting neurotransmitters in the brain. |  My doctor prescribed me anti-depressants to help manage my depression. 我的医生给我开了抗抑郁药来帮助控制我的抑郁症。

Anti-depressants can have various side effects. 抗抑郁药可能有各种副作用。

|Synonyms: anti-depressant medication, mood elevators|Antonyms: stimulants, depressants|noun
