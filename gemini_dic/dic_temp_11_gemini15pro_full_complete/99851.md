intermobilities:/ˌɪntərmoʊˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |交互流动性，相互流动性| The study explores the intermobilities of people, goods, and ideas across borders.  这项研究探讨了人员、货物和思想跨境交互流动性。|  例句：The project focuses on the intermobilities of students between universities. 该项目关注学生在大学之间的交流流动.

例句：Intermobilities of researchers facilitate the sharing of knowledge and expertise. 研究人员的相互流动促进了知识和专业技能的共享。 |近义词：interactions, exchanges, movements|反义词: stagnation, immobility|词性:名词
