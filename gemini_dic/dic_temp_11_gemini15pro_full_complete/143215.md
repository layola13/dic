outcrying:/ˈaʊtkraɪɪŋ/
n.
大声疾呼，强烈抗议
a strong expression of public disapproval or anger
The outcrying over the new tax law was deafening. 新税法的出台引发了震耳欲聋的抗议。

近义词:protest, clamor, outcry, objection
反义词:acceptance, approval, support


词源: From Middle English *outcrien, from Old French escrier, from Vulgar Latin *excrīāre, present active infinitive of *excrīō, from Latin exclāmō (“I exclaim”). Equivalent to out- +‎ cry.
