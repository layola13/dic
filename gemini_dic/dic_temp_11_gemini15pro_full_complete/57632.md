draughtsman:/ˈdrɑːftsmən/ /ˈdræftsmən/| n. | 制图员，绘图员 | A person who makes detailed technical drawings or plans, especially of buildings or machinery. | The draughtsman meticulously rendered the blueprints for the new bridge.  制图员一丝不苟地绘制了新桥的蓝图。 |  synonyms: draftsman, draftsperson, designer, architect (for buildings), engineer (for machinery) | antonyms: N/A | noun

draughtsman:/ˈdrɑːftsmən/ /ˈdræftsmən/| n. | 起草人 | A person who drafts legal documents, speeches, etc. | The company hired a draughtsman to prepare the contract. 公司聘请了一位起草人来准备合同。 | synonyms: writer, author, composer, scrivener | antonyms: N/A | noun
