nonnaturalistic:/ˌnɒnˌnætʃrəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非自然主义的
Not based on or conforming to the principles of naturalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy; artificial, contrived, or affected.
Her nonnaturalistic acting style felt forced and unconvincing. 她非自然主义的表演风格让人感觉做作，缺乏说服力。

The play's nonnaturalistic dialogue, full of stylized pronouncements, seemed out of place in the gritty urban setting.  这部戏剧非自然主义的对话，充满了程式化的声明，在粗犷的城市背景下显得格格不入。


Synonym: artificial, contrived, affected, unnatural, synthetic
Antonym: natural, naturalistic, realistic, authentic, genuine
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
naturalistic (adjective): from natural (from Old French naturel, from Latin naturalis "of nature," from natura "nature") + -istic (suffix forming adjectives from nouns ending in -ic, from French -istique and/or directly from Latin -isticus)
