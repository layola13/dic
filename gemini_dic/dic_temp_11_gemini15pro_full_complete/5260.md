allogamy: /əˈlɒɡəmi/ /əˈlɑːɡəmi/ | n. |异花授粉；异体受精| Fertilization of a flower by pollen from another flower, especially one on a different plant.| The allogamy rate in this species of tree is very high.  这种树的异花授粉率很高。| Synonyms: cross-pollination, cross-fertilization | Antonyms: autogamy, self-pollination | Noun

allogamous: /əˈlɒɡəməs/ /əˈlɑːɡəməs/ | adj. |异花授粉的| Relating to or characterized by allogamy; cross-fertilizing.|  Allogamous plants rely on external factors for pollination. 异花授粉植物依靠外部因素进行授粉。| Synonyms: cross-pollinating, cross-fertilizing | Antonyms: autogamous, self-pollinating | Adjective
