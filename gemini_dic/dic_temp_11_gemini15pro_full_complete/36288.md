cohelper:/koʊˈhɛlpər/| n.|助手，协作者|Someone who helps another person, especially in a task or project.  They assist and contribute to a shared goal. |Example 1:  My cohelper on this project was instrumental in its success.  这个项目我的助手对它的成功起了重要作用。

Example 2: We worked together as cohelpers to finish the presentation. 我们作为合作者一起完成了演示。|近义词：assistant, collaborator, partner, teammate|反义词: opponent, adversary|词性:noun

词源:  "Co-" (prefix meaning "together" or "jointly") + "helper" (noun meaning "one who helps")
