idoloclast:/ˈaɪdəlɒˌklæst/ (US) /ˈaɪdəlɒklæst/ (UK)| n. |偶像破坏者；破除迷信者|A person who destroys religious images or opposes idolatry. |The idoloclst smashed the statue of the false god. 偶像破坏者砸碎了假神的雕像。|Synonyms: iconoclast, image-breaker|Antonyms: idolater, worshipper|Noun
