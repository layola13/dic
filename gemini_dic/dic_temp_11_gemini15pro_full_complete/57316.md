downtime:/ˈdaʊntaɪm/| n.|停机时间，故障时间，休息时间|A period of time when a system is unavailable or inactive, or when someone is not working or active.|The server experienced some downtime last night, causing website access issues. 服务器昨晚出现了一些故障时间，导致网站访问出现问题.|Synonyms: outage, inactivity, break, rest|Antonyms: uptime, activity, work|noun

downtimes:/ˈdaʊntaɪmz/| n.|停机时间，故障时间，休息时间（复数）|Periods of time when a system is unavailable or inactive, or when someone is not working or active.|Scheduled downtimes for system maintenance are necessary.  为了系统维护，预定的停机时间是必要的。|Synonyms: outages, inactivities, breaks, rests|Antonyms: uptimes, activities, work periods|noun
