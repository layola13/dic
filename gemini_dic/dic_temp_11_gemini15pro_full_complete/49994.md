denizened:/ˈdɛnɪzənd/ |adj.|居住的，栖息的|Inhabited or frequented by (animals or people).  Characterized by having denizens.|例句：The deep forest, denizenized by owls and wolves, felt eerie at night.  夜里，居住着猫头鹰和狼的深林感觉阴森森的。

例句：The once denizenized city now lay in ruins. 这座曾经人烟稠密的城市如今已成废墟。|近义词：inhabited, populated, occupied|反义词：deserted, uninhabited, vacant|词性:形容词

词源: From denizen + -ed.  Denizen comes from Old French *denezein "from within, native," from denz "within," from Latin de intus "from within."
