nubility:/njuːˈbɪləti/
n.|性成熟；适婚年龄；青春妙龄|The state of being nubile; marriageable age; the peak of youthful attractiveness.|Her nubility was evident in her radiant complexion and graceful movements. 她的青春妙龄在她容光焕发的肤色和优雅的举止中显而易见。|Synonyms: marriageability,  adolescence, puberty, youthfulness|Antonyms:  old age, infancy, childhood|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin *nubilitas, from nubilis ‘marriageable’, from nubere ‘to marry, veil oneself’.
