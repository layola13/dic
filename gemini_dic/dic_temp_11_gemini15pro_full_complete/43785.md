cremations:/krɪˈmeɪʃənz/
n.
火化，焚化；骨灰
The process of reducing a dead body to ashes by fire.
Many people choose cremations over traditional burials. 很多人选择火化而不是传统的土葬。
The cremations took place at the local crematorium. 火化仪式在当地火葬场举行。

近义词:incinerations
反义词:burials
词性:名词
