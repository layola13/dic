outbray:/ˌaʊtˈbreɪ/| v. |大声叫喊，胜过(尤指以更响亮的声音)|to shout louder than someone, especially to defeat them in an argument by doing so|The hecklers outbrayed the speaker, making it impossible for him to be heard.  喧闹声盖过了演讲者，让他无法被听到。|The donkey outbrayed its rivals. 这头驴的叫声盖过了它的对手。|近义词：outshout, outbellow, drown out|反义词: whisper, murmur|动词
