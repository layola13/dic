fithful:/ˈfɪθfʊl/| adj.|忠实的，虔诚的|Faithful means loyal and steadfast in allegiance or affection, especially to a person, cause, or belief. It can also describe something that is accurate and reliable.|She has been a faithful employee for over twenty years. 她是一位忠诚的员工，工作了二十多年。|The movie is a faithful adaptation of the novel. 这部电影是对小说的忠实改编。|近义词：loyal, devoted, constant, true, steadfast|反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *feithful, from Old English *fǣstfull (“faithful, pious”), equivalent to faith +‎ -ful. Cognate with Dutch getrouw (“faithful, loyal”), German treu (“faithful, loyal”), Swedish trogen (“faithful, loyal”).
