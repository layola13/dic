non-evaluative:/nɒn-ɪˈvæljuətɪv/ | adj. | 非评判性的，不带评价的 | Not making judgments about value or worth; descriptive rather than evaluative. | The therapist offered non-evaluative support, simply listening to the patient's concerns.  治疗师提供非评判性的支持，只是倾听病人的担忧。| Synonym: descriptive, objective, neutral, impartial | Antonym: evaluative, judgmental, critical, biased | adjective
