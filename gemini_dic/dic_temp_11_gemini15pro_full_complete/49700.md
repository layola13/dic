demobilised:/ˌdiːˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ /ˌdiːˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/
adjective
复员的，遣散的
Discharged from military service.
例句：The demobilised soldiers returned home to their families. 复员的士兵们回家与家人团聚。

例句：Many demobilised soldiers struggled to find work after the war. 许多复员士兵在战后难以找到工作。

近义词：discharged, released, mustered out
反义词：mobilised, enlisted
词性：adjective


demobilise:/ˌdiːˈməʊbəlaɪz/ /ˌdiːˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/
verb
复员，遣散
To discharge from military service.
例句：The government plans to demobilise thousands of troops next year. 政府计划明年复员数千名士兵。

例句：After the war ended, the soldiers were quickly demobilised. 战争结束后，士兵们很快就被遣散了。


近义词：discharge, release, muster out
反义词：mobilise, enlist
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + mobilise, from French mobiliser, from mobile (“movable”), from Latin mobilis (“movable”), from movēre (“to move”).
