crispness:/ˈkrɪspnɪs/| n. |酥脆，脆爽，干脆利落|The crispness of the autumn air was invigorating. 秋天的空气清爽宜人。|近义词：crunchiness, firmness, sharpness|反义词:sogginess, softness, limpness|词性:名词
Etymology: From crisp + -ness.  Crisp itself comes from Old English "crisp" meaning "curly," likely of imitative origin.
