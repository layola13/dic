marly:/ˈmɑːrli/| adj.|泥灰的，似泥灰的，含有泥灰的|Resembling marl; containing marl.  Consisting of or abounding in marl. |The farmer enriched his fields with marly soil.  农民用泥灰质土壤改良了他的田地。|近义词：clayey, calcareous|反义词：sandy, non-calcareous|词性:形容词
词源: From marl + -y. Ultimately from Old French marle.
