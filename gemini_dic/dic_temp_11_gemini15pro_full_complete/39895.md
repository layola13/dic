constitutionalist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪst/| n. adj.|宪政主义者；拥护宪法者|A person who strongly supports or advocates for a constitution, especially adherence to its principles and limitations on government power.  A constitutionalist may also be an expert in constitutional law.|He's a staunch constitutionalist who believes in limited government. 他是一位坚定的宪政主义者，信奉有限政府。|Synonyms: advocate, supporter, defender, proponent|Antonyms: dictator, authoritarian, tyrant|noun

constitutionalist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪst/| adj.|宪法的；符合宪法的；拥护宪法的|Relating to or supporting a constitution or constitutionalism.|The constitutionalist reforms were aimed at limiting the power of the monarchy.  这些宪政改革旨在限制君主的权力。|Synonyms: lawful, legal, legitimate|Antonyms: unconstitutional, illegal, illegitimate|adjective
