farewelled:/ˌfɛəˈwɛld/ |v.|告别，送别|To say goodbye to someone, especially formally or for a long time.  The community farewelled their beloved teacher. 社区告别了他们敬爱的老师。|Synonyms: bid farewell, see off, say goodbye|Antonyms: greet, welcome|verb

farewelled:/ˌfɛəˈwɛld/ |adj.(rarely used)|已告别的，已送别的|Having been said goodbye to.  The farewelled soldiers marched towards the ship.  已告别的士兵们向船走去。|Synonyms: departed, gone|Antonyms: arriving, incoming|adjective
