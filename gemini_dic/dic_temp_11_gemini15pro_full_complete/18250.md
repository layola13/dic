believability:/bɪˌliːvəˈbɪləti/| n.|可信度，可信性|The believability of her story was questioned. |例句：The believability of his alibi was weak.  他的不在场证明的可信度很低。

例句：The film lacked believability due to poor special effects.  由于特效差，这部电影缺乏可信度。|近义词：credibility, plausibility|反义词：unbelievability, implausibility|词性:名词

词源: From believe + -ability.  来源于动词believe（相信）加上名词后缀-ability（可...性）。
