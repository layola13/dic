laboratories:/ˈlæbrətɔːriz/ (US) /ləˈbɒrətriz/ (UK)| n. |实验室；研究室|A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching, or for the manufacture of drugs or chemicals.|
例句：Many important discoveries were made in these laboratories. 许多重要的发现是在这些实验室里完成的。

例句：The laboratories are well-equipped with the latest technology. 这些实验室配备了最新的技术。

近义词：labs, research facilities, research centers|
反义词:|
词性: noun (plural)

laboratories:/ˈlæbrətɔːriz/ (US) /ləˈbɒrətriz/ (UK)| n. |实验室；研究室|A place providing opportunity for experimentation and observation; a situation or set of circumstances in which something develops or is studied.|
例句：The new policy will be tested in a laboratory of four schools. 这项新政策将在四个学校组成的实验组中进行测试。

例句：The war-torn country became a laboratory for new weapons. 这个饱受战争蹂躏的国家成了新武器的试验场。

近义词：testing ground, proving ground|
反义词:|
词性: noun (plural)


词源: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin laboratorium ‘a place for work, a workshop’, from Latin labor ‘work’.
