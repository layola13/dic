ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *graspen, *grapsen, from Old Norse *grapsa (“to grasp”), from Proto-Germanic *graspaną (“to grasp, grope”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰrebʰ- (“to seize, grasp”). Cognate with Dutch grapsen (“to grasp”), German grapsen (“to grasp, grope”), Swedish grabba (“to grab”).
