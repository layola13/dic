hellebores:/ˈhɛlɪˌbɔːrz/ | n. |嚏根草；圣诞玫瑰|A perennial plant of the buttercup family, bearing cup-shaped flowers in winter or early spring.|例句：The hellebores are blooming beautifully in my garden. 我的花园里的嚏根草开得很漂亮。

例句：She received a bouquet of hellebores for her birthday. 她生日收到了一束嚏根草。|近义词：winter rose, Lenten rose|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *helleborus*, from Greek *helléboros* (ἑλλέβορος), perhaps from *heleîn* (ἑλεῖν) "to injure" + *bora* (βορά) "food," referring to its poisonous properties.
