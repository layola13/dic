counterplead:/ˌkaʊntərˈpliːd/
verb
反诉，提出反请求
to plead against (a claim made by an opposing party in a legal action).
The defendant counterpleaded that the plaintiff owed him money.  被告反诉原告欠他钱。
He counterpleaded, arguing that he had acted in self-defense. 他提出反诉，辩称他是出于自卫。
Synonyms: countersue, oppose
Antonyms: concede, admit
v.
