biddable:/ˈbɪdəbl/| adj.|顺从的，听话的|Easily led or influenced; obedient.  Disposed to comply with instructions or requests.|The children were surprisingly biddable and followed all the instructions. 孩子们出奇地听话，遵守了所有的指示。|Synonyms: obedient, compliant, docile, tractable, amenable|Antonyms: disobedient, unruly, wayward, intractable, rebellious|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bid (verb) + -able (suffix).  The verb "bid" in this context means "to command" or "to request."  The suffix "-able" transforms the verb into an adjective meaning "capable of being bidded" or "capable of being commanded/requested."
