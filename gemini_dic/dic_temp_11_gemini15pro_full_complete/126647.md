nak: /næk/ | interj. | 呸；表示厌恶或轻蔑的感叹词 |  An exclamation expressing disgust or contempt, similar to "yuck" or "ew". | Nak! That smells awful! 呸！那闻起来真难闻！| 近义词: yuck, ew, blech | 反义词: yum, delicious | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely imitative in origin, representing the sound of spitting or gagging.  Many similar words exist across different languages to express disgust, often with nasal sounds like "n" or "m".
