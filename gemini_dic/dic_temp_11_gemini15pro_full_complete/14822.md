axillary:/ˈæksɪləri/ (US), /ækˈsɪləri/ (UK)| adj.|腋窝的；腋生的|Relating to the armpit.  Of or pertaining to the axilla (armpit).|例句：The axillary artery supplies blood to the arm. 腋动脉为手臂供血。

例句：Axillary lymph nodes are often examined for signs of breast cancer. 腋窝淋巴结经常被检查以寻找乳腺癌的迹象。|近义词：underarm|反义词:|词性:adjective

axilla:/ækˈsɪlə/ (US), /ækˈsɪlə/ (UK)| n.|腋窝|The armpit; the cavity beneath the junction of the arm and shoulder.|例句：The doctor checked the patient's axilla for swelling. 医生检查了病人的腋窝是否有肿胀。

例句：Sweat glands are abundant in the axilla. 腋窝汗腺丰富。|近义词：armpit|反义词:|词性:noun
