nuns:/nʌnz/
n.
修女；尼姑
a member of a religious community of women, especially a cloistered one, living under vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience.
The nuns devoted their lives to prayer and helping the poor. 这些修女将她们的一生奉献给了祈祷和帮助穷人。
synonyms:sisters, religious women
antonyms:monks, friars
noun
