heterocyst:/ˈhɛtərəˌsɪst/ | n. |异形胞|A specialized cell in certain filamentous cyanobacteria that engages in nitrogen fixation.|Example 1: Heterocysts are essential for nitrogen fixation in some cyanobacteria. 异形胞对于某些蓝细菌的固氮至关重要。

Example 2: The development of heterocysts is triggered by nitrogen starvation. 氮饥饿会触发异形胞的发育。|近义词: specialized cell, nitrogen-fixing cell |反义词: vegetative cell| 词性: noun
