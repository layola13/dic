busked:/bʌskt/
|v.|街头卖艺；在街头表演|Performed music or other art in a public place for money or tips. |He busked in the subway to earn some extra cash. 他在地铁里卖艺赚些外快。|Synonyms: perform, play, entertain|Antonyms:  work (in a formal setting),  rehearse (privately)|verb
busked:/bʌskt/
|v.|（尤指为挣钱）沿街兜售；沿街叫卖 | Attempted to sell something, typically small items, by approaching people in public places. |She busked her handmade jewelry at the local market. 她在当地市场兜售她手工制作的珠宝。|Synonyms: peddle, vend, hawk|Antonyms:  buy, purchase, acquire|verb
