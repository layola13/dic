obstinateness:/ˈɒbstɪnətnəs/ (US) /ˈɒbstɪnɪtnəs/ (UK)| n. |固执，顽固|The quality of being unreasonably determined to act in a particular way and refuse to change one's mind.|His obstinateness made it difficult to reach a compromise. 他的固执使得很难达成妥协。|Synonyms: stubbornness, tenacity, persistence, inflexibility|Antonyms: flexibility, compliance, acquiescence|noun

obstinate:/ˈɒbstɪnət/ (US) /ˈɒbstɪnɪt/ (UK)| adj. |固执的，顽固的|Unreasonably determined to act in a particular way and refuse to change one's mind.|He remained obstinate in his refusal to apologize. 他仍然固执地拒绝道歉。|Synonyms: stubborn, headstrong, willful, unyielding|Antonyms: compliant, amenable, flexible|adjective
