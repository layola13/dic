cumulative: /ˈkjuːmjələtɪv/ (US) /ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|累积的；渐增的|increasing or increased in quantity, degree, or force by successive additions.|The cumulative effect of the small changes was surprisingly large. 小变化的累积效应出奇地大。|Synonyms: aggregate, amassed, collective, combined, increasing, snowballing|Antonyms: decreasing, diminishing, individual, separate|adjective


例句：

The cumulative effect of sleep deprivation can be devastating.
睡眠不足的累积效应可能是毁灭性的。

Her cumulative GPA over four years was 3.8.
她四年来的累计平均绩点是3.8。
