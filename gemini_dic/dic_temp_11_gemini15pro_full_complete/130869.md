nonarchitectures:/ˌnɒnˈɑːrkɪtɛktʃəz/
noun
非架构；非建筑；非体系结构
Structures or systems that are not considered to be formally designed or architecturally significant.  This term often refers to informal settlements, vernacular buildings, or systems lacking a deliberate design.
These nonarchitectures often emerge organically and reflect the needs and resources of the community. 这些非建筑通常是有机地出现的，并反映了社区的需求和资源。
non-building, informal structure
