faithfuls:/ˈfeɪθfʊlz/
noun
信徒，忠实信徒
A group of people who are loyal and devoted, especially to a religion or cause.
The pastor greeted the faithfuls after the service. 牧师在礼拜结束后向信徒们致意。
loyal followers, adherents, believers
nonbelievers, skeptics, apostates
