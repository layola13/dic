counterploy:/ˈkaʊntərplɔɪ/| n.|对策，反计，策略|A maneuver or action used in response to another's; a stratagem designed to counteract or thwart another.|例句：His counterploy was to leak the information to the press. 他的对策是将信息泄露给媒体。

例句：The company launched a counterploy to its competitor's aggressive marketing campaign.  这家公司针对竞争对手的激进营销活动发起了一项反击策略。|近义词：stratagem, tactic, maneuver, scheme, plan|反义词:cooperation, collaboration|词性:noun
