insomnias:/ɪnˈsɑːmniəz/ /ɪnˈsɒmniəz/| n. |失眠（复数）| Habitual sleeplessness; inability to sleep.|  She has been suffering from insomnias for months. 她已经失眠好几个月了。|  He often experiences insomnias when he's stressed. 他在压力大的时候经常失眠。| 近义词：sleeplessness, insomnia | 反义词: sleep, rest | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin īnsomnĭa, from Latin in- (“not”) + somnus (“sleep”).
