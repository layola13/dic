kips:/kɪps/| n. |千磅;  基普(老挝货币)|A kip is a unit of force equal to 1000 pounds-force.  A kip is also the currency of Laos. |The bridge can withstand a load of 100 kips. 这座桥可以承受100千磅的载荷。

The price is 10,000 kips. 价格是1万基普。|近义词：(for force unit) kilopounds |反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性:noun


kips:/kɪps/| v. (kip, kipped, kipping) |(英式非正式)睡觉|(British informal) To sleep, especially in a place that is not your home.  |I kipped on my friend's sofa last night. 我昨晚睡在我朋友的沙发上。

We kipped at a cheap hotel. 我们在一个便宜的旅馆过夜了。|近义词：sleep, doze, nap, crash (informal)|反义词: wake |词性:verb
