gilthead: /ˈɡɪlthɛd/ | n. | 金头鲷，金头海鲷 | A fish (Sparus aurata) of the sea bream family, found in the Mediterranean and eastern Atlantic, popular as a food fish because of its fine flavour. It is silvery grey with a golden band across the forehead. |  例句：We had grilled gilthead for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤金头鲷。

例句：Gilthead is a prized fish in Mediterranean cuisine. 金头鲷是地中海菜肴中的珍品。


近义词：sea bream, dorade | 反义词: N/A | 词性：noun
