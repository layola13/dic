bisegment:/ˈbaɪˌsɛɡmənt/| n. |二等分; 二分体|A segment that bisects something, especially a line segment that bisects an angle.|The bisegment divides the angle into two equal parts. 二等分线将角分成相等的两部分。|Example: Draw a bisegment of the angle ABC.  画出角ABC的二等分线。

Example: The bisegment of the line segment connected the midpoints of the two sides of the triangle. 这条线段的二等分线连接了三角形两边的中点。|近义词：bisector|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:  From bi- (meaning "two") + segment (meaning "part").
