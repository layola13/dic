endeixis:/ɛnˈdeɪksɪs/ | n. |指示; 指標; 迹象| An indication or sign of something. |  The high fever is an endeixis of infection. 高烧是感染的迹象。| Synonyms: indication, sign, symptom, evidence, manifestation | Antonyms: concealment, suppression | noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek  *ἔνδειξις (éndeixis)*  meaning "indication," derived from  *ἐνδεικνύναι (endeiknýnai)* meaning "to point out," from *ἐν (en)*  "in" + *δεικνύναι (deiknýnai)* "to show."
