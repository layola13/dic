everglade: /ˈɛvərɡleɪd/
n.
沼泽地，湿地; 大沼泽地（专指美国佛罗里达州南部一大片沼泽地）
A marshy grassland area, typically covered with tall grass.  Specifically refers to a large swampland in southern Florida, USA.
例句：The Everglades are home to a diverse range of wildlife.  大沼泽地是各种野生动物的家园。

例句：Alligators thrive in the Florida Everglades.  短吻鳄在佛罗里达大沼泽地繁衍生息。

近义词：swamp, marsh, wetland
反义词：desert, upland
