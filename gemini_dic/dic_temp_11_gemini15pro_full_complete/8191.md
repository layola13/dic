anti-dogmatical:/ˌænti-dɒɡˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 反教条的，非教条的 | Opposed to or free from dogma; not bound by authoritarian tenets or beliefs. |  He took an anti-dogmatical approach to religion, preferring personal experience over strict adherence to doctrine. 他对宗教采取了反教条主义的态度，更喜欢个人经验而不是严格遵守教义。

Anti-dogmatical teaching encourages students to question and explore. 反教条的教学鼓励学生质疑和探索。 | 近义词: open-minded, liberal, tolerant, flexible, undogmatic | 反义词: dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, authoritarian | 词性: 形容词
