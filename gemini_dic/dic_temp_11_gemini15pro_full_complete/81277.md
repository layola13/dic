grant:/ɡrɑːnt/ (US) /ɡrɑːnt/ (UK)| n. vt.| 拨款；授予；承认；允许|To agree to give or allow (something requested) to someone.|The government granted him permission to build a house. 政府批准他建房子。|Synonyms: award, bestow, concede, allow|Antonyms: refuse, deny, withhold|noun, verb

grant:/ɡrɑːnt/ (US) /ɡrɑːnt/ (UK)| n.| 拨款；补助金|A sum of money given by a government or other organization for a particular purpose.|He received a grant to study abroad. 他获得了出国留学的奖学金。|Synonyms: subsidy, award, donation|Antonyms:  debt, loan|noun
