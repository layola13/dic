cabins:/ˈkæbɪnz/| n. |小木屋，客舱，座舱|A small, simple house made of wood. A private room on a ship or boat. A compartment for passengers in an aircraft. |We rented a cozy cabin in the mountains for our vacation. 我们在山上租了一间舒适的小木屋度假。|Synonyms: huts, cottages, rooms, compartments|Antonyms: mansions, palaces|Noun
