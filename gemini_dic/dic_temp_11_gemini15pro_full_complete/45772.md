cusp:/kʌsp/
n.|尖端，尖头；边缘；关键时刻|A point or pointed end, especially of a tooth or crescent moon. A point of transition between two different states. |The country is on the cusp of a new era. 这个国家正处于一个新时代的尖端。|Synonyms: point, tip, apex, verge, brink, edge, turning point|Antonyms: nadir, bottom, center|noun

cusp:/kʌsp/
n.|牙尖|A pointed or rounded eminence on or near the masticating surface of a tooth.|The dentist examined the cusp of the molar. 牙医检查了臼齿的牙尖。|Synonyms: point, tip, apex|Antonyms: base, root|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cuspis* (point, spear), related to *cuspid-* (pointed).
