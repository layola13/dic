excursive: /ɪkˈskɜːrsɪv/ /ɪkˈskɜːrsɪv/
| adj. | 离题的；散漫的；游览的 | Deviating from the main subject; rambling; relating to a short journey or trip. |  The professor's lectures were often excursive, covering a wide range of topics.  教授的讲座经常离题，涵盖了广泛的主题。|  We took an excursive trip to the countryside. 我们进行了一次短途乡村旅行。 | 近义词: digressive, rambling, wandering, meandering, desultory, discursive | 反义词: concise, succinct, focused, relevant, pertinent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin excursus, from the verb excurrere ‘run out’, from ex- ‘out of’ + currere ‘to run’.
