decandrous:/dɪˈkændrəs/
adjective
十雄蕊的
Having ten stamens.
Example: The decandrous flower attracted many bees.  十雄蕊的花吸引了许多蜜蜂。

Example:  Botanists identified the plant as a decandrous species.  植物学家将这种植物鉴定为十雄蕊物种。

Synonym: ten-stamened
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "having fewer than ten stamens" or names of specific stamen counts like "pentandrous" - five stamens)
Word type: adjective
