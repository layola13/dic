countrywide:/ˈkʌntriwaɪd/
adjective
全国性的；遍及全国的
Extending throughout a whole country.
The company launched a countrywide advertising campaign.  这家公司发起了一场全国性的广告宣传活动。
The government implemented countrywide reforms. 政府实施了全国性的改革。
Synonyms: nationwide, national, across the country, all over the country
Antonyms: local, regional
词性: adjective
