grokked:/ɡrɑːkt/ | v. | 理解；透彻地了解；| To understand something deeply and intuitively.  It implies a level of comprehension beyond the superficial. |  I finally grokked the concept of relativity after reading several books on the subject. 我读了几本关于相对论的书后，终于理解了这个概念。

I haven't grokked the new software yet. 我还没搞懂这个新软件。| 近义词: understand, comprehend, grasp, get, perceive | 反义词: misunderstand, misinterpret | 词性: 动词
