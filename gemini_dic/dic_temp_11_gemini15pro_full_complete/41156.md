copulative:/ˈkɒpjʊlətɪv/ /ˈkɑːpjəleɪtɪv/ | adj. | 连接的；系动的；交配的 | Serving to connect, linking; pertaining to copulation or sexual intercourse. |  The word "and" is a copulative conjunction. “和”字是一个连接连词。| Synonyms: connecting, linking, conjunctive | Antonyms: disjunctive, separating | adjective


copulative:/ˈkɒpjʊlətɪv/ /ˈkɑːpjəleɪtɪv/ | n. | 系词 | A word or phrase that links the subject of a sentence to a predicate. | The verb "to be" is a common copulative.  动词“to be”是一个常见的系词。 | Synonyms: linking verb, copula | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form) | noun
