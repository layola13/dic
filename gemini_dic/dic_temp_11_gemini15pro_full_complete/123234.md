monocrotic:/ˌmɒnəˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. |单峰脉的| Having a single beat or pulse wave for each cardiac cycle. Characterized by a single upward stroke in the pulse tracing. |The patient's pulse was found to be monocrotic. 病人的脉搏是单峰脉。|The doctor noticed a monocrotic pulse, which can be a sign of certain medical conditions. 医生注意到单峰脉，这可能是某些疾病的征兆。| 近义词: single-peaked |反义词: dicrotic|词性:形容词
