overemoted: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊtɪd/
verb (past tense)|过度表演；表现得过分夸张|The actor overemoted in the death scene, making it seem melodramatic rather than tragic.  这个演员在死亡场景中表演得过分夸张，使之看起来更像是闹剧而不是悲剧。|例句：He overemoted and ruined the scene. 他表演得过火，毁了整个场景。|近义词：overacted, exaggerated, overplayed|反义词：understated, subtle|词性:动词

overemoted: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊtɪd/
adjective|表演过度的；过分夸张的|Her overemoted performance felt insincere. 她过分夸张的表演让人感觉不真诚。|例句：The audience found his overemoted portrayal of grief unconvincing. 观众觉得他对悲伤的过度演绎很不令人信服。|近义词：histrionic, melodramatic, theatrical|反义词：understated, restrained|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "emote" (verb meaning "to express emotion").  "Emote" itself comes from the French word "émotif," meaning "emotional."
