hyperimmunize:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊnaɪz/| vt.|过度免疫，超免疫|To induce an excessively high level of immunity in a person or animal, usually by repeated injections of an antigen.|The horses were hyperimmunized with the snake venom to produce antivenom.  这些马被反复注射蛇毒以产生抗蛇毒血清。

The researchers hyperimmunized the mice with the virus. 研究人员对老鼠进行了病毒的过度免疫。|近义词:immunize, vaccinate|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用weaken immunity 或 suppress immunity)|词性:动词
