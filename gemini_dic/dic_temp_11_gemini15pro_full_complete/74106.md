fou:/fuː/| adj.| 疯狂的，精神错乱的 | Insane; mentally deranged. | He acted fou after the accident. 他在事故后行为疯狂。| The trauma left him fou.  这场创伤使他精神错乱。| 近义词：insane, mad, crazy, demented | 反义词：sane, rational, lucid | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *fol, ultimately from Latin *follis, meaning "bellows, windbag."  The original meaning referred to someone whose head was filled with air, hence foolish or insane.
