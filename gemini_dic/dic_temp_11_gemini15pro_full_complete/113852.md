madeleines:/ˌmædˈleɪnz/| n. |玛德琳蛋糕|Small shell-shaped sponge cakes, often flavored with lemon zest and made with ground almonds or nuts.  They are associated with the French writer Marcel Proust and his novel "In Search of Lost Time," in which the taste of a madeleine dipped in tea evokes vivid childhood memories.|例句：The aroma of warm madeleines filled the kitchen.  温暖的玛德琳蛋糕的香味弥漫了整个厨房。

例句：She served tea and madeleines in the afternoon.  她在下午供应了茶和玛德琳蛋糕。|近义词：cookies, cakes, pastries |反义词: None. |词性:noun
