cluniacensian: /ˌkluːniˈænsɪən/ | adj. n. |克吕尼的；克吕尼修会的修士| Of or relating to the Cluny Abbey in France, or its monastic order.  A monk of the Cluny order. |  The cluniacensian reforms had a profound impact on monastic life in medieval Europe.  克吕尼改革对中世纪欧洲的修道院生活产生了深远的影响。|  He was a devout cluniacensian monk. 他是一位虔诚的克吕尼修会的修士。| 近义词: Benedictine (in a broader sense) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Cluny (Cluniacum in Latin), the location of the abbey in France + -ian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns).
