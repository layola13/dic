bannered:/ˈbænərd/| adj.|用横幅装饰的，张贴横幅的|Decorated or furnished with banners.|The streets were bannered for the festival. 节日的街道上挂满了横幅。|近义词：decorated, adorned, festooned|反义词:bare, plain|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the noun "banner" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Banner" comes from Old French "baniere" (flag, standard), ultimately from Frankish "*banni" (proclamation, summons).
