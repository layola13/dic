nonrebelliously:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈbɛl.i.əs.li/
adverb
非叛逆地；温顺地
in a way that is not rebellious; obediently.
The students listened nonrebelliously to the teacher's instructions. 学生们顺从地听老师的指示。
He accepted the decision nonrebelliously, though he disagreed with it. 虽然他不同意这个决定，但他还是温顺地接受了。
Synonyms: obediently, submissively, meekly, compliantly
Antonyms: rebelliously, defiantly, resisting, opposing
词性: 副词
