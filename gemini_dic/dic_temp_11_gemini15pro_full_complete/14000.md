aurigraphy:/ɔːˈrɪɡrəfi/
n.
耳廓描记法,耳写法
The study of the external ear, especially its configuration.  It sometimes refers to a method of identification based on the unique shape of each individual's ear.
Aurigraphy is a relatively new biometric identification method. 耳廓描记法是一种相对较新的生物识别方法。
The detective considered using aurigraphy to identify the suspect.  侦探考虑使用耳廓描记法来识别嫌疑人。
近义词: otography
反义词:  None
