cambridges:/ˈkeɪmbrɪdʒɪz/
n.
剑桥(大学的复数形式，或指多个名为剑桥的地方)
Plural form of Cambridge, referring to multiple places named Cambridge or, less commonly, multiple Cambridge Universities.
The Cambridges of the world attract some of the brightest minds.  世界各地的剑桥都吸引了一些最聪明的人才。
Several Cambridges exist in the United States. 美国有几个叫剑桥的地方。
近义词:university towns, academic centers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) rural areas, non-academic towns
词性:noun
