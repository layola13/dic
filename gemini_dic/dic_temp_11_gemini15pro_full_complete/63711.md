ephemeric:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/| adj.|短暂的，朝生暮死的|Lasting for a very short time.|The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral. 樱花的美丽是短暂的。|Synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived, momentary|Antonyms: enduring, lasting, permanent|adjective

ephemeral:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/| n.|短暂的事物|Something that lasts for a very short time.|Mayflies are ephemerals, living only for a day.蜉蝣是朝生暮死的生物，只活一天。|Synonyms: transient, fleeting thing|Antonyms: permanent thing|noun
