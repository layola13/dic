hyperadipose:/ˌhaɪpərˈædɪpoʊs/
adjective
过度肥胖的，脂肪过多的
excessively fat; characterized by excessive accumulation of adipose tissue.
例句：The hyperadipose mouse model is used to study obesity-related diseases.  过度肥胖的小鼠模型被用来研究与肥胖相关的疾病。

例句：Hyperadipose individuals are at increased risk for developing type 2 diabetes.  过度肥胖的人患2型糖尿病的风险增加。

近义词：obese, corpulent, overweight
反义词：lean, thin, slender
词性：adjective
