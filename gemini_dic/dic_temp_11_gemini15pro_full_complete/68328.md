fall-for:/fɔːl fɔːr/|phrasal verb|爱上，迷恋，上当|To become romantically attached to someone; to be deceived by something.|例句：He fell for her the moment he saw her. 他对她一见钟情。

例句：She fell for his sob story and gave him all her money. 她被他编造的悲惨故事骗了，把所有的钱都给了他。|近义词：fall in love with, be smitten with, be taken in by, be fooled by|反义词：dislike, hate, distrust|词性:动词短语
