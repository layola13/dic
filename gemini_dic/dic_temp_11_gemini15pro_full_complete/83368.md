gyromancy:/ˈdʒaɪrəˌmænsi/ | n. | 旋轉占卜 | A method of divination in which a person walks or spins around in a circle until they become dizzy and fall, and the direction of the fall is interpreted as an omen or prediction. |  The ancient Greeks practiced gyromancy to seek guidance from the gods. 古希腊人通过旋转占卜来寻求神的指引。 |  divination, augury |  - | noun
