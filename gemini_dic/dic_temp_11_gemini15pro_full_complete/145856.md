overdepressively:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪvli/
adverb
过度消沉地，极度沮丧地
in an extremely depressed manner; excessively sadly.
She sighed overdepressively and slumped onto the sofa. 她极其沮丧地叹了口气，瘫倒在沙发上。
He spoke overdepressively about his future prospects. 他极其沮丧地谈论着他的未来前景。
Synonym: despairingly, despondently, dejectedly, gloomily
Antonym: cheerfully, optimistically, joyfully, happily
词性: 副词
