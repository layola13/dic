corbeled:/ˈkɔːr.bəld/ /ˈkɔːr.bɛld/ | adj.| 筑于托臂上的，层层挑出的 |  Built on or supported by corbels; projecting in courses of masonry, each course projecting beyond the one below.  | The castle features a corbeled turret.  这座城堡有一个托臂支撑的塔楼。 | projecting, cantilevered, overhanging | recessed, flush, inset | adjective

corbel:/ˈkɔːr.bəl/ /ˈkɔːr.bɛl/ | n. | 托臂，托座 |  A structural piece of stone, wood, or brick jutting from a wall to carry a superincumbent weight, a projecting member in the face of a wall, supporting a weight. | The architect designed a series of decorative corbels to support the balcony.  建筑师设计了一系列装饰性的托臂来支撑阳台。 | bracket, support, console |  base, foundation | noun
