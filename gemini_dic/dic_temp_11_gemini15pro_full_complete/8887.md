antifundamentalism:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪzəm/
n.
反基要主义
Opposition to religious fundamentalism.  It encompasses a range of perspectives, from secular humanism to liberal interpretations of religion.
The rise of antifundamentalism in the 20th century coincided with increasing secularization.  20世纪反基要主义的兴起与日益世俗化同步。

Her book explored the complexities of antifundamentalism in the Middle East. 她的书探讨了中东地区反基要主义的复杂性。

近义词:secularism, liberalism, modernism
反义词:fundamentalism, orthodoxy, traditionalism
词性:noun
