farrowed:/ˈfæroʊd/
verb
产仔（尤指猪）; 生（小猪）
gave birth to piglets.
The sow farrowed a litter of 12 piglets. 母猪产下了一窝12只小猪。
近义词:  pigged, littered
反义词: None (生育没有明确的反义词)
词性: verb (动词 - 过去式和过去分词)

farrow:/ˈfæroʊ/
noun
一窝猪崽
a litter of pigs
The farmer proudly showed off his new farrow. 农民自豪地展示了他新的一窝猪崽。
近义词: litter
反义词: None
词性: noun (名词)

farrow:/ˈfæroʊ/
verb
(猪)产仔
(of a sow) give birth to piglets
The sow is expected to farrow next week.  这头母猪预计下周产仔。
近义词: pig, litter
反义词: None
词性: verb (动词)



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fearh, from Proto-Germanic *farxwą (“piglet”), from Proto-Indo-European *porko- (“pig”). Cognate with Old High German farah (“piglet”), Old Norse fær (“sow”), Latin porcus (“pig”).  The verb sense developed from the noun.
