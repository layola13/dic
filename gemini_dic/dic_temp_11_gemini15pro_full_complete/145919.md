overdiligentnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪlɪdʒəntnəsɪz/
noun
过度勤奋, 过度努力
Excessive diligence or conscientiousness.
His overdiligentnesses in proofreading sometimes slowed down the publication process. 他在校对方面过度 diligence, 有时会减慢出版进程。
近义词: assiduity, sedulousness
反义词: laziness, negligence
词性: noun

Etymology:
From "over" (excessive) + "diligent" (showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  "Diligent" comes from the Latin "diligens," present participle of "diligere" (to love, esteem, select, value), from "dis-" (apart, asunder) + "legere" (to choose, gather, read).
