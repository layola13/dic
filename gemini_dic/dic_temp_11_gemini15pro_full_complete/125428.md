multipicture:/ˈmʌltiˌpɪktʃər/| n.|多图; 多画面; 多图像|A single frame or display containing multiple pictures or images.|The photographer used a multipicture technique to capture the dancer's movements in a single frame.|近义词：collage, montage, composite image|反义词: single picture, individual image|词性: 名词

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
picture (noun): from Latin pictūra "painting, a painting," from pictus, past participle of pingere "to paint, embroider, adorn"
