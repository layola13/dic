misregulates:/ˌmɪsˈrɛɡjʊleɪts/ | v. | 失调；错误调节 | To regulate badly or incorrectly.  It implies a disruption of the normal regulatory process, often leading to negative consequences. | Stress can misregulate the immune system. 压力会导致免疫系统失调。| The government's attempt to misregulate the market led to chaos. 政府试图错误地调控市场，导致了混乱。 | dysregulate, malfunction | regulate properly, control effectively | verb
