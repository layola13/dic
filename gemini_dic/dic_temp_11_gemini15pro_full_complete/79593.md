Gloria in excelsis Deo (拉丁语) = Glory to God in the highest  愿荣耀归于至高之神
In gloria = In glory 在荣耀中
Gloria Patri = Glory be to the Father  愿荣耀归于圣父
Ascribe gloria to = To give praise and honor to  将荣耀归于
Sing someone's glorias = To praise someone highly  高度赞扬某人

词源： From Latin gloria "glory, fame, renown."
