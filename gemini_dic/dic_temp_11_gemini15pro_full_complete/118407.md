merriest:/ˈmeriɪst/| adj.|最快乐的，最愉快的|Superlative form of merry.  Meaning the most full of cheer and good spirits.|This is the merriest Christmas I've ever had. 这是我过得最快乐的圣诞节。|近义词：jolliest, happiest, gayest|反义词：saddest, gloomiest|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *merriest, superlative of *merrie, from Old English *myrge (“pleasant, sweet, agreeable, delightful, merry; famous, glorious; excellent, beautiful”), from Proto-Germanic *murgijaz (“short, brief, pleasant”). Cognate with Old Saxon murg “short,” Old Frisian myrre “merry,” Dutch moedig “courageous,” Old High German murg “short,” German mürbe “tender,” Gothic 𐌼𐌰𐌿𐍂𐌲𐌹𐌽𐍃 (maurgins, “morning”).  The semantic development is uncertain.
