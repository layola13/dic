nominalising:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | n. |名词化|The process of forming a noun from a verb or adjective.  The act of turning a word that is not a noun (like a verb or adjective) into a noun.|例句：Nominalising is common in academic writing. 名词化在学术写作中很常见。|近义词：noun formation, nominalization|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "verbalizing") |词性:noun
