foredoom:/fɔːrˈduːm/
verb
注定失败，预先注定
to make someone or something certain to fail, die, be destroyed etc.
His reckless driving foredoomed him to an early death. 他鲁莽的驾驶预示着他会早逝。

The project was foredoomed to failure from the start. 这个项目从一开始就注定要失败。

近义词: predestine, preordain, doom, condemn
反义词: bless, favor

词性: verb
