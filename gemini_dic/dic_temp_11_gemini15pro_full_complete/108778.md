leases:/ˈliːsiz/| n.|租赁，租约|A contract by which one party conveys land, property, services, etc., to another for a specified time, usually in return for a periodic payment.|We signed the leases for the new apartments. 我们签署了新公寓的租约。|近义词：rentals, agreements, tenancies|反义词：purchases, sales|noun

leases:/ˈliːziz/| v.|出租，租赁|To grant the temporary possession or use of (lands, tenements, etc.) to another, usually for compensation at a fixed rate.|The company leases its equipment to small businesses. 这家公司将其设备出租给小型企业。|近义词：rents, lets, hires out|反义词：borrows, buys|verb
