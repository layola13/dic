词源:  From Latin *adjectus, past participle of adjicere (“to throw to, add to”) (from ad (“to, towards”) + jacere (“to throw”)).  It entered Middle English in the late 14th century.


注意事项:
* "adject" 虽然可以作为名词使用，指代形容词，但在现代英语中，更常见的用法是使用 "adjective"。
*  While "adject" is grammatically correct, "adjective" is the preferred and more commonly used term.  Using "adject" might sound archaic or overly formal.
