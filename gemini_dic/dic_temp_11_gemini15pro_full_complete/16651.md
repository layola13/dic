barrackers:/ˈbærəkərz/
n.
支持者，喝彩者
People who shout encouragement and support for a team or player, especially at a sporting event.
The barrackers were cheering wildly as their team scored the winning goal.  支持者们在他们的球队攻入制胜一球时疯狂欢呼。
synonyms: supporters, fans, cheerleaders, rooters
antonyms: critics, detractors
noun
