congratulations: /kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 祝贺，恭喜 | an expression of pleasure at someone's success or good fortune. | Example: Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！

Example:  I offered my congratulations on her promotion. 我对她升职表示祝贺。

近义词: felicitations, best wishes, compliments | 反义词: condolences | 词性: noun
