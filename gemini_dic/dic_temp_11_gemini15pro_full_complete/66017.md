example:/ɪɡˈzæmpl/ /ɪɡˈzɑːmpl/ | n. vt.| 例子，范例；榜样，楷模|  something that is typical of the group or set to which it belongs; a pattern or model, especially one to be imitated |  For example, you could use a dictionary. 例如，你可以使用字典。| He set a good example for the other students. 他为其他学生树立了一个好榜样。 | synonym: instance, illustration, model, sample, paradigm | antonym: exception, anomaly | noun

example:/ɪɡˈzæmpl/ /ɪɡˈzɑːmpl/ | vt.|  to be or give an example of something | to illustrate by example | This painting exemplifies the artist's skill. 这幅画体现了这位艺术家的技巧。 |  His behavior exemplifies the values of our company. 他的行为体现了我们公司的价值观。 | synonym: illustrate, demonstrate, epitomize, typify | antonym:  obfuscate, conceal | verb
