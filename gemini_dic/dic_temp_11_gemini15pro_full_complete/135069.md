nonnovels:/nɒnˈnɒvəlz/
n.
非小说类作品;纪实文学
Works of nonfiction; factual writings rather than fiction.
Many bookstores have separate sections for novels and nonnovels. 许多书店将小说和非小说类作品分开摆放。
Nonnovels often deal with real-life events or provide factual information. 非小说类作品通常讲述真实事件或提供事实信息。
synonyms: nonfiction, factual writings, documentaries, biographies, autobiographies
antonyms: novels, fiction, fictional works
noun
