asphalts:/ˈæsfɔːlts/ /ˈæsfɔːlts/| n. |沥青|A dark, sticky substance obtained from the distillation of petroleum or coal tar, used for paving roads and roofing.|The road was newly paved with asphalt. 这条路新铺了沥青。|近义词:bitumen, tarmac|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑未铺砌的材质，例如) gravel, dirt|词性:noun

asphalts:/ˈæsfɔːlts/ /ˈæsfɔːlts/| vt. |用沥青铺|To cover with asphalt.|They asphalted the driveway. 他们用沥青铺了车道。|近义词:pave, surface|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑移除沥青的动作，例如) excavate, remove|词性:verb
