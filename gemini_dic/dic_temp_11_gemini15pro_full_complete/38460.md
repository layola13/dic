concessively:/kənˈsɛsɪvli/| adv.|让步地，退让地|In a way that acknowledges a point or fact, often reluctantly or with reservations.  承认某一点或事实，通常是不情愿地或有保留地。|例句：He conceded the point, somewhat concessively. 他勉强地承认了这一点。

例句：Concessively, she admitted that he might have a point.  她退一步承认，他可能有道理。|近义词：reluctantly, grudgingly, yielding, accommodatingly|反义词：insistently, adamantly, stubbornly|词性: 副词
