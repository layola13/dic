dangling participle:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ ˈpɑːrtɪsɪpl/ | n. | 悬垂分词 | A dangling participle is a participle that modifies the wrong noun or pronoun in a sentence. It "dangles" because it doesn't have a clear grammatical connection to the word it's supposed to modify. | Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful. (Incorrect - the trees weren't walking) |  misplaced modifier |  - | noun

dangling participle:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ ˈpɑːrtɪsɪpl/ | adj. (attributive) | 悬垂分词的 |  Used to describe a participle that is dangling. |  A dangling participle construction can make your writing unclear. | misplaced | correctly placed | adjective
