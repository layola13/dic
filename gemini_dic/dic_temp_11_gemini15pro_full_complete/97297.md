infographics:/ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪks/| n. |信息图表；信息图|A visual representation of information or data, e.g. as a chart or diagram.|Example: The infographics clearly showed the growth in sales over the past year. 信息图表清楚地显示了过去一年销售额的增长。|Example:  Infographics are a great way to present complex data in an accessible format. 信息图表是将复杂数据以易于理解的格式呈现的好方法。|近义词：data visualization, charts, diagrams|反义词:text, prose|词性:noun

词源:
information + graphics
