hyperpatriotism:/ˌhaɪpərˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/| n.|极端爱国主义，过度的爱国主义|Excessive patriotism; fanatical patriotism.  A belief in and support for one's country so strong that it can lead to aggressive or discriminatory actions against other countries or people.|His hyperpatriotism led him to believe that any criticism of the government was treason.|Synonyms: ultranationalism, jingoism, chauvinism|Antonyms:  disloyalty, treason|noun
