bellyflop:/ˈbeliˌflɑp/ (US) /ˈbɛliˌflɒp/ (UK)| n. vi.| 肚子先着水|A fall onto the water surface with the front of the body flat, making a loud splashing sound.  A clumsy or unsuccessful action or event.|He did a painful bellyflop into the pool. 他肚子着水，重重地摔进了游泳池。

The new product launch was a total bellyflop.  新产品的发布完全失败了。|近义词：splash, flop, bomb (informal)|反义词: dive, graceful entry|名词, 动词
