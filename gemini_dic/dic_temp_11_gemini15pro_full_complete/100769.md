intravenously: /ˌɪntrəˈviːnəsli/ | adv. | 静脉注射地 |  into a vein; by injection into a vein.  Medication is often delivered intravenously for rapid effect. | The doctor administered the antibiotics intravenously. 医生通过静脉注射方式给病人注射了抗生素。| The patient received fluids intravenously. 病人通过静脉输液补充体液。 | Synonyms:  by injection, into a vein | Antonyms: orally, intramuscularly | adverb
