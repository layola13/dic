extant:/ˈekstənt/ /ɪkˈstænt/
|adj.|现存的，尚存的|Still in existence; surviving.|Several manuscripts of the play are extant. 这部戏剧的几份手稿尚存。|近义词：surviving, existing, remaining, abiding|反义词：extinct, lost, destroyed|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *extantem*, present participle of *exstare* "stand forth, be visible, exist," from *ex* "out" + *stare* "to stand."
