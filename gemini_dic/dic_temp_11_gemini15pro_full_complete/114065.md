magnetize:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/| vt.|磁化，使磁化|To give magnetic properties to; make magnetic. |例句：The iron filings were magnetized by the bar magnet. 铁屑被条形磁铁磁化了。

例句：He tried to magnetize the needle with a battery. 他试图用电池将针磁化。|近义词：attract, influence, mesmerize|反义词：demagnetize|词性: verb


magnetizee:/ˌmæɡnɪtɪˈziː/| n.|被磁化的物体；被吸引的人|A person or thing that is magnetized or attracted.|例句：The magnetizee, a small piece of iron, clung to the magnet. 被磁化的物体，一小块铁，紧紧地吸附在磁铁上。

例句：The charismatic speaker was a magnetizee for the audience's attention.  这位充满魅力的演说者吸引了观众的注意力。|近义词：target, subject, recipient|反义词：N/A|词性: noun


词源: From magnet + -ize.  Ultimately from the Greek  Magnes lithos, meaning "Magnesian stone," referring to magnetite, a magnetic iron ore found in Magnesia, a region of ancient Greece.
