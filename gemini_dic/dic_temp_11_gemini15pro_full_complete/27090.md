calico's:/ˈkælɪˌkoʊz/| n.|印花棉布(的)；印花棉布制品(的)；三色猫(的)|Calico's refers to items made from calico fabric or belonging to a calico cat.  It indicates possession. |The calico's vibrant pattern brightened the room.  这块印花棉布鲜艳的图案照亮了房间。|近义词：chintz, gingham|反义词:None|Noun

calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/| n. adj.|印花棉布；三色猫；杂色的|A plain-woven textile, typically brightly printed; a cat with a coat of three colours, typically white, black, and orange. |She wore a calico dress. 她穿着一件印花棉布连衣裙。  The calico cat sat on the windowsill. 三色猫坐在窗台上。|近义词：(n.) chintz, gingham (adj.) mottled, variegated|反义词:None|Noun, Adjective
