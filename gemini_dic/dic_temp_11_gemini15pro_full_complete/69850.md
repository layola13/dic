ferrites:/ˈfer.aɪts/ | n. | 铁氧体 | A ceramic-like material containing iron oxide combined with other metallic oxides.  They are magnetic but non-conductive, making them useful in various electronic applications. | Ferrites are used in the cores of transformers and inductors. 铁氧体用于变压器和电感器的磁芯。| Synonyms: ceramic magnets, ferromagnetic materials | Antonyms: paramagnetic materials, diamagnetic materials | noun

ferrite:/ˈfer.aɪt/ | n. | 铁素体 |  A solid solution of carbon in alpha-iron. It is the softest form of steel and is magnetic. | Ferrite is the primary constituent of low-carbon steel. 铁素体是低碳钢的主要成分。 | Synonyms: alpha-iron | Antonyms: austenite, cementite | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ferrite*, from Latin *ferrum* ("iron") + *-ite* (suffix forming nouns denoting minerals or fossils).
