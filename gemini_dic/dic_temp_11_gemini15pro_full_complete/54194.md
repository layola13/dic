disesteem:/ˌdɪsɪˈstiːm/
n.|轻视，蔑视|Lack of esteem; disrespect.|His constant criticism revealed his disesteem for his colleagues. 他不断的批评表明了他对同事的轻视。|近义词:contempt, scorn, disdain|反义词:esteem, respect, admiration|词性:名词
disesteem:/ˌdɪsɪˈstiːm/
vt.|轻视，不尊重|To regard with disrespect; to consider unworthy of esteem.|He disesteems those who are dishonest. 他轻视那些不诚实的人。|近义词:despise, scorn, disdain|反义词:esteem, respect, admire|词性:动词
