expatiation:/ɪkˌspeɪʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |详细解释；详述；细说 | a detailed explanation or discussion of a subject |  The professor's expatiation on the topic lasted for hours. 教授对这个主题的详细解释持续了几个小时。 |  She gave a lengthy expatiation on the history of the region. 她对该地区的历史进行了详细的阐述。 | synonyms: elaboration, exposition, discourse | antonyms: brevity, conciseness | noun
