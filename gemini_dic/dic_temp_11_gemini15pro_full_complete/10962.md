arbitral: /ˈɑːrbɪtrəl/ | adj. |仲裁的，裁决的|Relating to arbitration; involving or determined by arbitration. | The arbitral tribunal ruled in favor of the plaintiff. 仲裁庭裁定原告胜诉。| The company opted for arbitral proceedings instead of going to court. 公司选择仲裁程序而不是上法庭。|近义词：judicial, adjudicative, |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *arbitralis, from Latin arbiter (“judge, arbitrator”).
