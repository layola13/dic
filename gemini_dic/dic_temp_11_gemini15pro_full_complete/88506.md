history:/ˈhɪstri/ (US), /ˈhɪstri/ (UK)| n.|历史，历史学；履历；病历|A chronological record of events, as of the life or development of a people or institution, often including an interpretation of these events.|例句：The history of the world is full of conflict. 世界历史充满了冲突。|例句：She has a long history of heart problems. 她有长期的心脏病史。|近义词：past, chronicle, record, annals|反义词：future|词性:noun
