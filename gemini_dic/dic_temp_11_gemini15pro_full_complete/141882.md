what:/hwʌt/(hwɑːt) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something or someone.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which, how|反义词:|pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你通常早餐吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help the situation? 她做了什么来帮助解决这种情况？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been studying at university?  她在大学里一直在学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the meeting? 会议上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the lecture? 讲座期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? 中断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习什么多久了？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I can do about it. 关于这件事我无能为力。

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？
