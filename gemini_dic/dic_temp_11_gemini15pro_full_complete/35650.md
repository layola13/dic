coaxers:/ˈkoʊksərz/
n.
劝诱者；哄骗者
People who gently persuade or urge someone to do something.
The coaxers finally got the shy child to join the game. 劝诱者最终让害羞的孩子加入了游戏。
persuaders, wheedlers
bullies, intimidators
noun
