hara-kiri:/ˌhɑːrəˈkɪəri/ /ˌhærəˈkɪri/| n.|切腹自杀|Ritual suicide by disembowelment, formerly practiced by samurai in Japan.|Example:  He committed hara-kiri rather than face dishonor.  他宁愿切腹自杀也不愿面对耻辱。

Example:  Hara-kiri was a way for samurai to regain their honor.  切腹是武士恢复名誉的一种方式。|近义词：suicide, self-immolation|反义词: survival|词性:noun
