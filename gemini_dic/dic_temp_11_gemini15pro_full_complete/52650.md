digitalizes:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
数字化，将…数字化
Converts analog information into a digital format.
The company digitalized all its records.  这家公司将其所有记录数字化。
The library is digitalizing its collection of old manuscripts. 图书馆正在将其收藏的旧手稿数字化。
Near Synonyms: digitize, computerize, automate
Antonyms: analogize
