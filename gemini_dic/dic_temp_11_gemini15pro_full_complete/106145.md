knob-like:/ˈnɒbˌlaɪk/| adj.|像球形的，瘤状的|Resembling a knob; rounded or protuberant.|The plant had knob-like growths on its stem. 这种植物的茎上有瘤状的生长物。|The fungus had a knob-like cap.  这种真菌的菌盖呈球状。|近义词：bulbous, rounded, lumpy, bumpy, nodular|反义词：flat, smooth, even|词性:形容词
词源: knob + -like.  "Knob" comes from Middle Low German *knubbe. "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
