incondensible:/ˌɪnkənˈdɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 不可冷凝的 | Not able to be condensed; not capable of being made more dense or compact, especially of a gas or vapor that cannot be liquefied by cooling or compression. | The gas proved to be incondensible at room temperature.  这种气体在室温下被证明是不可冷凝的。|  The incondensible gases were vented from the system. 不可冷凝的气体从系统中排出。| 近义词: non-condensable, non-liquefiable | 反义词: condensable | 词性: adjective
