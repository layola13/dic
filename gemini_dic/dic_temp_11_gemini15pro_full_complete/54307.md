dishabille:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːl/ /ˌdɪsæˈbiːj/
noun
穿着随便；便服；家居服
The state of being dressed in a casual or lounging attire, typically at home.  A loose or negligent garment worn in this state.
She received visitors in dishabille. 她穿着家居服接待了客人。
Near synonyms:  undress, negligee, nightclothes
Antonyms:  formal wear, full dress
词性: 名词
