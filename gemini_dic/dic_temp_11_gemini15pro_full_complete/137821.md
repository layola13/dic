nonverticalnesses:/ˌnɒnvɜːrtɪˈkælɪsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非垂直性；不垂直的状态 | The nonverticalnesses of the leaning towers were a cause for concern. |  The state of not being vertical; deviations from verticality. | The architect noted the slight nonverticalnesses of the columns.  这些柱子轻微的不垂直引起了建筑师的注意。 |  inclination, slant, tilt, slope | verticality, uprightness | noun

Etymology: non- + vertical + -ness + -es

non-:  prefix meaning "not"
vertical: from Old French *vertical, from Late Latin verticalis "of the vertex," from Latin vertex (genitive verticis) "highest point, summit, crown of the head, pole of the heavens," from Proto-Italic *werteks, from Proto-Indo-European *wr̥texs-.
-ness: suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
-es: plural suffix.
