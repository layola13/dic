becomely:/bɪˈkʌmli/
adjective
合适的，得体的；漂亮的，美观的
Suitable or proper; pleasing in appearance; attractive.
She was dressed in a becomely manner for the occasion.  她为这个场合穿得很得体。
She was a becomely young woman with a pleasant smile. 她是一位漂亮的年轻女子，面带愉悦的微笑。
近义词:appropriate, fitting, graceful, elegant, attractive, pretty
反义词:unsuitable, inappropriate, ugly, unattractive, unbecoming
词性:adjective

词源:From Middle English *becomly, from Old English *becōmlīc (“becoming, fit, suitable”), equivalent to become +‎ -ly.  Cognate with Scots becomlie (“becoming”), West Frisian bikomlik (“becoming”), Dutch betamelijk (“proper, decent”).
