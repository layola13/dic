allodial:/ˌæləˈdaɪəl/ | adj. | 自由保有土地的；完全拥有的 |  Relating to land ownership that is free from any superior landlord or feudal lord.  The owner has complete and absolute ownership. |  He inherited an allodial estate from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里继承了一块完全拥有的地产。| synonyms: freehold, absolute, outright | antonyms: feudal, leasehold | adjective
