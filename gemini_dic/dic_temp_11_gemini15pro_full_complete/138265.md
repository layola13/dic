not turn a hair:/nɒt tɜːn ə heə(r)/ |idiom|  面不改色，毫不动容 | To show no reaction to something surprising, shocking, or upsetting; to remain calm and composed. |  例句：She heard the bad news, but didn't turn a hair. 她听到了坏消息，但面不改色。| 近义词：not bat an eye, keep one's cool, remain calm, stay composed | 反义词：panic, freak out, lose one's composure | idiom
