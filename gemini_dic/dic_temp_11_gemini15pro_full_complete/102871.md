jauntily:/ˈdʒɔːntɪli/ | adv. | 轻松活泼地，快活地 | In a light and cheerful way; briskly and self-confidently. | She walked jauntily down the street, whistling a merry tune. 她轻松活泼地走在街上，吹着欢快的口哨。|  He jauntily tipped his hat and strode off. 他轻快地挥了挥帽子，大步走开了。 |  cheerfully, briskly, merrily, lightheartedly |  gloomily, dejectedly, sadly, morosely | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From jaunty + -ly. Jaunty from French gentil "noble, high-born, handsome, elegant" influenced by Old French jante "elegant, jaunty". Ultimately from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family."
