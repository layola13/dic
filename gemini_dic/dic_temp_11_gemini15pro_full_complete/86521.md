heists:/heɪsts/
noun
抢劫(尤指银行)
An act of stealing something valuable, typically from a bank or other secure location.
The thieves made off with a million dollars in the daring heist.  这伙盗贼在这次大胆的抢劫中卷走了一百万美元。
robberies, thefts, burglaries
donations, gifts
n.
