insurance:/ɪnˈʃʊərəns/ (US), /ɪnˈʃɔːrəns/ (UK)| n.|保险|An arrangement by which a company or the state undertakes to provide a guarantee of compensation for specified loss, damage, illness, or death in return for payment of a specified premium.|I have health insurance through my employer. 我通过我的雇主获得了健康保险。|Synonyms: assurance, indemnity, protection, safeguard|Antonyms: risk, vulnerability, jeopardy|noun

insurance:/ɪnˈʃʊərəns/ (US), /ɪnˈʃɔːrəns/ (UK)| n.|保险费|The money paid to an insurance company for coverage.|The insurance on my car is very expensive. 我的汽车保险费很贵。|Synonyms: premium, payment|Antonyms: compensation, benefit|noun
