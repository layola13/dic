nonpecuniary:/ˌnɒnpɪˈkjuːniəri/ /ˌnɑːnpɪˈkjuːnieri/
adjective
非金钱的；非财产的
Not consisting of or relating to money.
例句：
He received nonpecuniary damages for his emotional distress.
他因精神痛苦获得了非金钱赔偿。

She sought nonpecuniary rewards, such as job satisfaction and recognition.
她寻求非金钱的回报，例如工作满意度和认可。

近义词：nonfinancial, nonmonetary
反义词：pecuniary, monetary, financial
词性：adjective
