cocreates:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪts/
verb
共同创造
To create something together with another or others.
The artist cocreates with the community to produce a unique mural. 艺术家与社区共同创作了一幅独特的壁画。
Collaborates, co-authors, co-designs, develops jointly
Creates alone, works independently
v.
