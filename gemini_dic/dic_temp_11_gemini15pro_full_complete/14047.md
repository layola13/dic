austereness:/ɔːˈstɪərnəs/| n.| 严肃性，朴素，简朴 |The austerity of the monks' lives was remarkable.  僧侣们生活的简朴令人瞩目。|  Severity, strictness, simplicity |  Luxury, extravagance, elaborateness| noun
austere:/ɔːˈstɪr/| adj.| 严肃的，朴素的，简朴的|He lived an austere life, devoid of luxury. 他过着简朴的生活，没有任何奢侈。|Stern, strict, simple| Luxurious, extravagant, elaborate| adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *austere*, from Latin *austērus* ("sour, harsh, rough, severe, grave, stern"), perhaps related to *aurōra* ("dawn").  The original meaning referred to a harsh or sour taste, later developing into the metaphorical sense of strictness and simplicity.
