archtreasurer:/ˌɑːrtʃˈtrɛʒərər/ | n. | 大司库，财务主管 | A chief treasurer, especially of a cathedral or other religious institution. | The archtreasurer oversaw the finances of the entire diocese. 大司库负责整个教区的财务。| The archtreasurer presented the annual budget to the chapter. 大司库向全体教士提交了年度预算。 |Synonyms: treasurer, bursar, cashier | Antonyms: debtor | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *arch- "chief" + treasurer.  The "arch-" prefix comes from the Greek *arkhos* meaning "leader," "ruler," or "chief."  "Treasurer" comes from the Old French *tresorier*, ultimately from the Latin *thesaurius* meaning "treasure" or "treasury."
