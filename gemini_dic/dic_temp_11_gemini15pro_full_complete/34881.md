clip-ons:/ˈklɪp ɑːnz/ (US) /ˈklɪp ɒnz/ (UK)| n.|夹式; 夹扣式|Small detachable items that are clipped onto something else, especially earrings, sunglasses or a bow tie.|She prefers clip-on earrings because her ears aren't pierced. 她更喜欢夹式耳环，因为她的耳朵没有打耳洞。|He bought a pair of clip-on sunglasses to wear over his prescription glasses. 他买了一副夹式太阳镜戴在他的处方眼镜上。|近义词:attachable, removable|反义词:permanent, fixed|词性:名词

clip-on:/ˈklɪp ɑːn/ (US) /ˈklɪp ɒn/ (UK)| adj.|夹式的; 夹扣式的|Designed to be clipped onto something.|These are clip-on earrings.  这些是夹式耳环。|He has a clip-on bow tie. 他有一个夹式领结。|近义词:attachable, removable|反义词:permanent, fixed|词性:形容词
