historiography:/ˌhɪstəˈriˈɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 历史编纂学 | The study of how history is written, including the methods and approaches used by historians. | Example 1:  A course in historiography might examine how different historians have interpreted the American Civil War.  历史编纂学课程可能会研究不同的历史学家是如何解释美国内战的。

Example 2:  Feminist historiography has challenged traditional narratives by focusing on the experiences of women.  女性主义历史编纂学通过关注女性的经历，挑战了传统的叙事。 | Synonyms: historical writing, historical scholarship | Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a field of study) | noun
