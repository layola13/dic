epenthetic:/ˌɛpənˈθɛtɪk/| adj.|插音的，附加的|Relating to or denoting a sound inserted in the middle of a word, especially a vowel sound where a consonant cluster is perceived as difficult to pronounce.|The “b” in “thimble” is an epenthetic consonant.|近义词：intrusive, inserted|反义词：original, inherent|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epenthesis* "insertion," from *epi* "in, into" + *en* "in" + *thesis* "a placing."
