indicible:/ɪnˈdɪsɪbl/ | adj. | 不可言喻的，难以形容的 | Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. | The beauty of the sunset was indicible. 夕阳的美丽难以言喻。 |  The pain he felt was indicible. 他感受到的痛苦难以名状。 | 近义词: inexpressible, unutterable, ineffable | 反义词: expressible, describable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *indicibilis, from in- "not" + dicibilis "speakable," from dicere "to say."
