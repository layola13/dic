non-existentialism:/ˌnɒnɪɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/ | n. |非存在主义 |The philosophical doctrine that denies the existence of existentialism.  It rejects the emphasis on individual existence, freedom, and choice that characterizes existentialist thought. |  Non-existentialism posits a reality external to individual consciousness. 非存在主义假设存在一个外在于个体意识的现实。|  He argued for a form of non-existentialism, emphasizing social structures over individual experience. 他主张一种非存在主义的形式，强调社会结构而非个人经验。 | Near Antonyms: existentialism | Antonyms: N/A | noun
