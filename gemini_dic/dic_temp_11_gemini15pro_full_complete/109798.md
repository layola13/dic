levynite:/ˈlɛvɪnaɪt/| n. | 钙沸石 |A mineral, hydrous calcium aluminium silicate, occurring in white or gray crystals.|Levynite is often found in volcanic rocks.  钙沸石常发现于火山岩中。|近义词：zeolite（沸石 - 更广泛的类别）|反义词: 无|名词
