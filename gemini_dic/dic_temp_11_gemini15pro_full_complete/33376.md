chunked:/tʃʌŋkt/| adj.|分成块的；分块传输的|Divided into chunks; transmitted in chunks.|The data was chunked into smaller packets for transmission. 数据被分成更小的数据包进行传输.|
例句：The file was too large to send in one piece, so it was chunked into smaller parts.  这个文件太大，无法一次发送，所以被分成了几个小部分。

例句：The application downloads the video in chunked format to allow for smoother playback.  这个应用程序以分块格式下载视频，以便更流畅地播放。


近义词：segmented, partitioned, divided|反义词: whole, complete, undivided|词性:形容词
