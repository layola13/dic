converging:/kənˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
会聚的，收敛的
moving towards the same point
例句：The converging lines of the drawing created a sense of depth.  绘图中汇聚的线条营造出一种纵深感。

例句：The two armies are converging on the enemy capital. 两支军队正向敌方首都集结。

近义词：meeting, joining, uniting, merging, coalescing
反义词：diverging, separating
词性: adjective


converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/
verb
会聚，集中，趋于一致
(of lines) tend to meet at a point. (of people or vehicles) move towards a place from different directions. (of opinions or policies) become similar or unified.
例句：The paths converge at the top of the hill. 几条小路在山顶汇合。

例句：All the evidence converges on the same conclusion. 所有证据都指向同一个结论。

近义词：meet, join, unite, merge, coalesce
反义词：diverge, separate
词性: verb
