melini:/məˈlini/| n. |蜜里尼（一种甜葡萄酒）|Melini is a type of sweet wine produced in Italy, typically made from partially dried grapes. |例句：This Melini is exquisitely sweet and pairs well with dessert. 这款蜜里尼甜而不腻，与甜点搭配相得益彰。

例句：He brought a bottle of Melini as a gift for the host. 他带了一瓶蜜里尼作为给主人的礼物。|近义词：Vin Santo, Moscato|反义词:Dry wine (干葡萄酒)|词性:noun
