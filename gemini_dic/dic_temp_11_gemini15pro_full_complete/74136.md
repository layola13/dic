foundationally:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənəli/
adv.
基础地，根本地
In a foundational way; in terms of foundations.  Relating to or based on foundations.
This argument is foundationally flawed. 这个论点从根本上来说是有缺陷的。
近义词: fundamentally, basically, essentially
反义词: superficially
词性: 副词
