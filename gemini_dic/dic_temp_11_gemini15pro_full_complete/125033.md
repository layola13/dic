mukluks: /ˈmʌklʌks/ (US), /ˈmʌklʊks/ (UK)| n. |  毛皮靴，尤指因纽特人穿的用驯鹿皮或海豹皮做的靴子 |Soft boots, traditionally made of reindeer skin or sealskin, worn by Arctic peoples such as the Inuit. |  I bought a pair of mukluks to keep my feet warm in the winter. 我买了一双毛皮靴，冬天可以保暖。| synonyms: winter boots, snow boots | antonyms: sandals, flip-flops | noun
