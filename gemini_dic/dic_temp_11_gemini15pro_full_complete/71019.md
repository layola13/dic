firmer:/ˈfɜːmə(r)/ /ˈfɝːmər/| adj.|更坚固的，更坚定的，更稳定的|More firm; comparatively firm.  Relating to something that is more solid, stable, or resolute.|例句：The mattress felt much firmer than our old one.  这床垫比我们旧的床垫感觉要硬得多。|例句：Her voice grew firmer as she spoke. 她说话时，声音变得更加坚定。|近义词：stronger, steadier, more stable, more resolute|反义词:softer, weaker, less stable, less resolute|词性:形容词
firmer:/ˈfɜːmə(r)/ /ˈfɝːmər/| n.|使牢固的人(或物)|Something that makes something else firmer.|例句：Adding a firmer to the paint will help it last longer. 在油漆中加入固化剂可以使其更持久。|例句： This type of clay needs a firmer to hold its shape. 这种粘土需要一个固定器来保持它的形状。|近义词：stabilizer, strengthener|反义词:weakener, softener|词性:名词
