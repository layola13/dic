append:/əˈpɛnd/| vt.|附加，添加|To add something to the end of a written document or a list. |例句：The lawyer appended a codicil to the will. 律师在遗嘱上附加了一个附录。|例句：I appended a list of sources to my essay. 我在我的文章后面附加了一个参考文献列表。|近义词：add, attach, affix, annex|反义词:detach, remove|verb
