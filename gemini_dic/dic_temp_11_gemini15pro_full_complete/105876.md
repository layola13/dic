kitsch:/kɪtʃ/ | n. |庸俗艺术品；粗制滥造的艺术品；矫揉造作的艺术品| something that appeals to popular or lowbrow taste and is often of poor quality |  The souvenir shop was full of kitsch. 那家纪念品商店里满是庸俗的商品。| synonyms:  tackiness, triteness, gaudiness, corniness, sentimentality | antonyms:  sophistication, refinement, elegance, tastefulness | noun

kitsch:/kɪtʃ/ | adj. |庸俗的；粗制滥造的；矫揉造作的| considered to be in poor taste because of excessive garishness or sentimentality, but sometimes appreciated in an ironic or knowing way | The restaurant's decor was a bizarre mix of kitsch and elegance.  这家餐厅的装潢是庸俗和优雅的奇异组合。 | synonyms: tacky, gaudy, corny, sentimental | antonyms: sophisticated, refined, elegant, tasteful | adjective
