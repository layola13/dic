malmbrick:/ˈmɑːlmˌbrɪk/ /ˈmælmbɪk/| n. |马尔姆砖，一种瑞典产的粘土砖|A type of fired clay brick, typically pale yellow in color, traditionally made in the region of Malmö, Sweden, and widely used in construction there.|The house was built with traditional yellow malmbrick. 这座房子是用传统的黄色马尔姆砖建造的。|Synonyms: brick, facing brick|Antonyms: None|Noun
