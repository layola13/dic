deemphasized: /ˌdiːˈɛmfəsaɪzd/ | adj. |  不重要的，被淡化的 | Reduced in emphasis; made to seem less important. | The report deemphasized the negative aspects of the project.  这份报告淡化了该项目的负面影响。 | Synonyms: downplayed, minimized, underplayed | Antonyms: emphasized, highlighted, stressed | adjective

deemphasize: /ˌdiːˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | v. |  淡化，降低…的重要性 | To reduce the emphasis on something; to make something seem less important. | The company deemphasized the product's flaws in its advertising. 该公司在其广告中淡化了该产品的缺陷。 | Synonyms: downplay, minimize, underplay | Antonyms: emphasize, highlight, stress | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reduce, remove") + emphasize.  "Emphasize" comes from the Late Latin "emphasizāre," from the Greek "emphánein" meaning "to exhibit, display."
