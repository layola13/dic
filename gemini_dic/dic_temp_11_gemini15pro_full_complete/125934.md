mushing:/ˈmʌʃɪŋ/ | n. |雪橇犬拉雪橇旅行; 艰难跋涉 |The act of traveling over snow with a dog sled, especially as a sport or transport.  Also refers to difficult travel over snow or muddy ground.| Example 1: We went mushing through the Alaskan wilderness.  我们乘雪橇犬穿过阿拉斯加荒野。

Example 2: After the heavy rain, the hike turned into a real mushing experience.  大雨过后，徒步旅行变成了一次真正的泥泞跋涉。 | Synonym: dogsledding, sledding, trekking, slogging | Antonym: flying, driving (on a clear road) | Noun
