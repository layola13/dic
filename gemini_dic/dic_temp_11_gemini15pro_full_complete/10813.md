aquifer:/ˈækwɪfər/| n. |含水层；蓄水层|An underground layer of rock or soil that holds water.|The city draws its water supply from a large aquifer.  这座城市的水源来自一个大型含水层。|近义词:water table, groundwater reservoir|反义词: aquiclude, aquitard|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aqua* (water) + *-fer* (bearing).
