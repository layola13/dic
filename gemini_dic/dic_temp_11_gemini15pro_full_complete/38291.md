concaved:/kɒnˈkeɪvd/ /kɑːnˈkeɪvd/| adj.|凹的，凹面的|Curved inward, like the inner surface of a bowl.|The mirror was concaved, distorting the reflection.|近义词：hollow, sunken, indented|反义词:convex|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *concavātus*, past participle of *concavāre*, from Latin *concavus* (“hollow”), from *com-* (“together”) + *cavus* (“hollow”).
