lotong:/ˈloʊtɒŋ/| n.| 懒猴，婴猴 |A small, nocturnal primate with large eyes, found in Southeast Asia.  A type of primate found in Southeast Asia, known for its slow movements and large eyes.|The lotong is a nocturnal animal. 懒猴是一种夜行动物。| The sanctuary protects several species of lotong.  这个保护区保护着好几种懒猴。 | synonyms: slow loris, slender loris | antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider diurnal primates like monkeys) | noun
