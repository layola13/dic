contemperation:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 适度，节制；调和；同时发生 | A state of being moderate or temperate; moderation.  The act of tempering or moderating something.  The simultaneous occurrence of events. | The contemperation of his anger was remarkable considering the provocation. 他在如此挑衅下还能克制怒火，真是了不起。 | 近义词: moderation, temperance, restraint, composure | 反义词: excess, immoderation, intemperance | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *contemperatio, from Latin contemperare ("to temper, moderate"), from com- ("together") + temperare ("to mix, blend, moderate").
