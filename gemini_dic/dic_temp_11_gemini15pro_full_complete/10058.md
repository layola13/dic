apocalypse:/əˈpɒkəlɪps/ (UK) /əˈpɑːkəlɪps/ (US)| n.|天启，启示，世界末日|A disclosure or revelation of something hidden or unknown. Also, a prophetic revelation, especially concerning a cataclysm in which the forces of good permanently triumph over the forces of evil.  Often used to refer to any widespread destruction or disaster.|The movie depicts a post-apocalypse world.|catastrophe, doomsday, cataclysm|renaissance, revival|noun
apocalyptic:/əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪk/ (UK) /əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪk/ (US)| adj.|天启的，启示录的，预示灾难的|Describing or prophesying a disastrous event, especially one signaling the end of the world.  Also, resembling the end of the world; momentous or catastrophic.|The apocalyptic imagery in the book was quite disturbing.|catastrophic, doomsday, disastrous|auspicious, promising|adjective
