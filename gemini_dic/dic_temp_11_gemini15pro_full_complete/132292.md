nondefensiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. | 不设防；不具防御性；坦然；不抗拒 | The state or quality of being nondefensive; a willingness to be open and receptive to feedback or criticism without feeling threatened or the need to justify oneself. |  Her nondefensiveness made the conversation much more productive. 她的不设防使得谈话更有效率。

| Example: His nondefensiveness allowed him to truly listen to his colleagues' concerns.  他的不设防使他能够真正倾听同事们的担忧。 | Synonym: openness, receptiveness, acceptance | Antonym: defensiveness, guardedness | noun
