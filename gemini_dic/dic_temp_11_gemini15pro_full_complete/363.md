abidingness:/əˈbaɪdɪŋnɪs/
n.
持久性；持续性；永恒性
The quality of being lasting or enduring.
His abidingness to his principles is admirable. 他对原则的坚持令人钦佩。
permanence, durability, steadfastness
transience, fleetingness
noun


abide:/əˈbaɪd/
v.
容忍；遵守；居留；停留
1. (v.) To remain; continue; stay; endure.  2. (v.) To put up with; tolerate.
1. She couldn't abide the noise any longer. 她再也无法忍受噪音了。

2.  He abides by the rules of the game. 他遵守游戏规则。
tolerate, bear, endure, obey, follow
disobey, break, violate
verb
