inoperably:/ɪnˈɒpərəbli/
adjective
不能手术地，无法运作地
Not able to be operated on or repaired; incurable.  The patient's condition was deemed inoperably advanced. 病人的病情被认为是晚期，无法手术。
The machine is inoperably damaged.  这台机器损坏严重，无法修复。
Synonyms: incurable, irremediable, irreparable, terminal
Antonyms: operable, curable, repairable
词性: adjective
