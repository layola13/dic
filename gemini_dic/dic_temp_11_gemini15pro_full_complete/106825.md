laboratory: /ˈlæbrətɔːri/ (US) /ləˈbɒrətri/ (UK)| n. |实验室；研究室|A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching, or for the manufacture of drugs or chemicals.|The scientists conducted experiments in the laboratory.|Synonyms: lab, workshop, studio|Antonyms: field, nature|noun

lab: /læb/| n. |实验室|Short for laboratory.|The lab results came back negative.|Synonyms: laboratory, workshop|Antonyms: field, nature|noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *laboratorium*, from Latin *laborare* "to labor," + *-torium*, a suffix forming nouns of place.
