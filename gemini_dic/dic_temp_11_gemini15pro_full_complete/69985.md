feta:/ˈfɛtə/ | n. |羊乳酪，菲达奶酪|A white salty Greek cheese made from ewe's milk or goat’s milk.  |例句：I love a Greek salad with feta cheese. 我喜欢加菲达奶酪的希腊沙拉。

例句：Feta cheese crumbles easily and adds a salty tang to dishes. 菲达奶酪很容易碎，并为菜肴增添咸味。|近义词：goat cheese,  |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of cheese) |词性: noun
