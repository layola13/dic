cowquake:/ˈkaʊkweɪk/| n.|牛震，牛群集体奔腾|A cowquake is a seismic event, usually minor, purportedly caused by a large herd of cattle moving together.  It's often used humorously or figuratively.|The farmer joked that the cowquake from his herd was bigger than the earthquake last week. 农民开玩笑说，他牛群造成的“牛震”比上周的地震还要大。|近义词：stampede, rumble|反义词: stillness, quiet|词性:名词
