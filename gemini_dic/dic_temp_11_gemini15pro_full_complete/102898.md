jawans:/dʒəˈwɑːnz/
|n.|士兵，兵员（尤指印度或巴基斯坦的）|Soldiers, especially in India or Pakistan.|The jawans marched bravely into battle. 士兵们勇敢地投入战斗。|troops, soldiers, servicemen|officers (sometimes), civilians|noun


jawan:/dʒəˈwɑːn/
|n.|士兵，兵员（尤指印度或巴基斯坦的）|A soldier, especially in India or Pakistan.|The young jawan was proud to serve his country.  这位年轻的士兵很自豪能为国家服务。|soldier, trooper, serviceman|officer (sometimes), civilian|noun

词源: From Hindi जवान (javān), from Persian جوان (javān, “young”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂yew- (“vital force”).
