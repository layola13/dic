gandhiesque:/ˌɡɑːnˈdiːɛsk/
adjective
甘地式的；具有甘地精神的
Characteristic of or resembling Mahatma Gandhi, especially in advocating nonviolent resistance.
Her gandhiesque approach to conflict resolution impressed everyone. 她甘地式的解决冲突的方法给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
Gandhi's gandhiesque philosophy inspired millions. 甘地的甘地式哲学激励了数百万人。
近义词:nonviolent, peaceful, pacifistic
反义词:violent, aggressive, militant
词性:adjective

词源: From the name of Mahatma Gandhi + -esque (a suffix meaning "in the style of; resembling").  The term emerged in the mid-20th century, likely shortly after Gandhi's death in 1948.
