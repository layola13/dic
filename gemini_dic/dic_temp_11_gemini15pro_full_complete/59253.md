e-tailing:/ˈiːteɪlɪŋ/
noun
电子零售
The sale of goods and services through the internet.
Many traditional retailers are now investing heavily in e-tailing. 许多传统零售商现在都在大力投资电子零售。
E-tailing offers consumers greater convenience. 电子零售为消费者提供了更大的便利。
近义词:online retailing, internet retailing
反义词:brick-and-mortar retailing
词性:n.
