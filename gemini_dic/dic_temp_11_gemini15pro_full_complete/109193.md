legroom:/ˈleɡruːm/ /ˈlɛɡˌrum/| n.|腿部空间|The amount of space available for your legs, especially when seated in a vehicle or aircraft. |The seats on the plane were cramped with very little legroom. 飞机上的座位很拥挤，腿部空间很小。|Synonyms:  seat space,  foot space,  knee room|Antonyms:  cramped space,  confined space|noun
