interwedded:/ˌɪntərˈwɛdɪd/
adjective
交织的，紧密结合的
closely joined or combined, typically through marriage or by sharing a common aim
The two families were interwedded for generations. 这两个家族世世代代联姻。
Synonyms: intertwined, intermingled, interwoven, fused, blended
Antonyms: separate, detached, disconnected
词性:形容词
