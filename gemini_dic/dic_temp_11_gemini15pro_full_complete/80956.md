gracefully:/ˈɡreɪsfəli/| adv.|优美地，优雅地，得体地|In a graceful manner; with elegance and poise.  Showing or having grace of movement, form, or proportion.|She danced gracefully across the stage. 她优雅地在舞台上翩翩起舞。|Synonyms: elegantly, smoothly, fluidly, nimbly|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, ungainly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From grace (n.) + -fully (adv. suffix).  "Grace" comes from Old French "grace"  ultimately from Latin "gratia" meaning "favor, goodwill, thanks."
