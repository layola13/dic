amok: /əˈmʌk/ | adv. & adj. | 狂乱地，发狂地；狂乱的，发狂的 |  Out of control and violently attacking people or things; in a state of murderous frenzy.  Origin: Malay <em>amuk</em> 'frenzied'. | The man ran amok with a knife.  那个人拿着刀乱砍乱杀。| 近义词: berserk, frenzied, wild | 反义词: calm, peaceful, controlled | 词性: 副词 & 形容词
