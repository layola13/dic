addling:/ˈædliŋ/| n.|腐坏，混乱|The process of rotting, especially of eggs; confusion or muddling.|The addling of the evidence made it impossible to solve the case.  证据的混乱使得案件无法侦破。|近义词：decay, rot, confusion|反义词：preservation, clarity|词性:名词

词源:  From Old English *ādlōd, past participle of *ādlian "to make ill, diseased," from ādl "disease."
