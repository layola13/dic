misoccupies:/ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪz/ | v. | 占用错误，误用，滥用 | To occupy wrongly or improperly; to put to a wrong or improper use. |  The company misoccupies the land designated for public park.  这家公司误占了指定用于公园的土地。

The squatters misoccupied the abandoned building. 擅自居住者误占了废弃的建筑物。 | 近义词: misuse, abuse, misapply | 反义词: use correctly, apply properly | 词性: 动词
