bepaint:/biːˈpeɪnt/
verb
涂抹颜料于；用颜料装饰
to cover with paint; to portray with paints
The walls were bepainted with murals depicting scenes from mythology.  墙壁上涂抹着描绘神话场景的壁画。
近义词: paint, decorate, color
反义词: strip, clean
