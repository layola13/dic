moonish:/ˈmuːnɪʃ/| adj.|梦幻般的；多变的；有点傻的；像月亮的|Resembling the moon, especially in being pale, dreamy, or changeable.  Also, slightly foolish or whimsical.|例句：He had a moonish look in his eyes. 他的眼神有些梦幻。|例句：Her moonish demeanor made her seem otherworldly. 她梦幻般的举止让她看起来像来自另一个世界。|近义词：dreamy, whimsical, capricious, vacillating, lunatic|反义词：practical, sensible, down-to-earth|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From moon + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of."
