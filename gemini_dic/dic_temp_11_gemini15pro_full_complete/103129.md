jerky:/ˈdʒɜːrki/ (US) /ˈdʒɜːki/ (UK)| adj. n.|急促的，颠簸的；牛肉干|Characterized by sudden, sharp movements; dried strips of meat, usually beef.|

例句：
The car ride was jerky because of the bumpy road.
因为道路颠簸，汽车行驶得很颠簸。

The jerky movements of the robot made it seem almost human.
机器人急促的动作使它看起来几乎像人类。

He enjoyed eating beef jerky as a snack.
他喜欢吃牛肉干作为零食。

The jerky texture of the dried fruit was unpleasant.
这种干果的粗糙质地令人不快。


近义词：abrupt, spasmodic, uneven (adj.);  beef jerky, biltong (n.)|
反义词：smooth, fluid, continuous (adj.)|
词性：adjective, noun|

jerky:/ˈdʒɜːrki/ (US) /ˈdʒɜːki/ (UK)| n.|牛肉干|Dried strips of meat, usually beef.|
