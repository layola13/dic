novelish:/ˈnɒvəlɪʃ/ (US) /ˈnɒvəlɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|新奇的；像小说的；不寻常的|Resembling or characteristic of a novel, especially in being unusual, exciting, or romantic.  Having the quality of being somewhat like a novel; fictional seeming. |Her life took a novelish turn when she inherited a castle in Scotland. 她的人生在她继承了苏格兰的一座城堡后发生了戏剧性的转变。|The plot was rather novelish and unrealistic.  这情节相当新奇且不切实际。|近义词：unusual, uncommon, extraordinary, romantic, fictional|反义词：ordinary, common, usual, realistic, mundane|词性: adjective
