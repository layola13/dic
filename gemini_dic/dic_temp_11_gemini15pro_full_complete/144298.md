outspirited:/ˌaʊtˈspɪrɪtɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，气馁的
dejected in spirit; discouraged.  Feeling downhearted or disheartened, especially after a loss or setback.
He felt completely outsprited after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到十分沮丧。
The team was outsprited after their crushing defeat.  球队惨败后士气低落。
近义词:discouraged, disheartened, demoralized, dejected, downhearted
反义词:encouraged, spirited, enthusiastic, optimistic
词性:adjective

词源:
From out- + spirited.  The prefix "out-" here intensifies the meaning of "spirited," suggesting a complete draining of spirit.
