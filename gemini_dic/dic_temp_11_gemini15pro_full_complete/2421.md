adjourned: /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (UK) /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (US)
verb
休会；延期；推迟
(of a meeting, formal gathering, or trial) break off proceedings with the intention of resuming them later.
The meeting adjourned until the following week.  会议延期到下周。
The judge adjourned the trial. 法官宣布休庭。
synonyms: suspend, postpone, defer, recess, prorogue, stay, interrupt
antonyms: convene, open, commence, begin, continue

adjourned: /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (UK) /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (US)
adjective
休会的；延期的；推迟的
(of a meeting, formal gathering, or trial) having had its proceedings temporarily suspended.
The adjourned meeting will take place on Monday.  休会后的会议将于星期一举行。
The case was adjourned until further notice.  案件延期至另行通知。
synonyms: suspended, postponed, deferred, recessed, prorogued, stayed, interrupted
antonyms: convened, opened, commenced, begun, continued
