intermomentary:/ˌɪntərˈməʊməntəri/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈmoʊmənteri/| adj. |瞬间的，片刻的|Existing or occurring between moments; very brief; momentary.|The pain was intermomentary, but sharp.  这阵疼痛很短暂，但很剧烈。|The flicker of light was intermomentary.  灯光闪烁只是一瞬间。|近义词：momentary, fleeting, transient, ephemeral|反义词：prolonged, lasting, enduring|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *inter* (between) + *momentum* (moment).
