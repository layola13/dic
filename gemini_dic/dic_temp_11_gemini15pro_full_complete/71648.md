flattening-out:/ˈflætənɪŋ aʊt/ | adj. |  变平的，平坦化的； (曲线)变缓的，(增长)放缓的 | The graph shows a flattening-out trend in sales. 图表显示销售额呈放缓趋势。 | 近义词: leveling-off, plateauing | 反义词: steepening, increasing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flatten" + "out".  "Flatten" comes from the Old Norse "flatr" meaning "flat". "Out" is a common English adverb and preposition with Germanic origins.
