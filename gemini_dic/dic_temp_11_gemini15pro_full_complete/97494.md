ingest:/ɪnˈdʒɛst/ | vt. | 摄取，吸收，咽下 | To take (food, drink, etc.) into the body by swallowing or absorbing it. |  The birds ingest small insects as their main food source. 鸟类以摄取小昆虫为主要食物来源。

The computer program can ingest data from multiple sources. 这个计算机程序可以从多个来源摄取数据。

近义词: consume, devour, absorb, take in | 反义词: excrete, expel, discharge | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ingestus*, past participle of *ingerere* "to carry in, introduce," from *in-* "in" + *gerere* "to carry, bear."
