howlers:/ˈhaʊlərz/| n.|嚎叫; 吼叫声; 滑稽可笑的错误;|A loud cry, especially of excitement, pain, or laughter. Also, a blunder that provokes amusement.|The fans let out a series of howlers when their team scored. 球迷们在他们的球队得分时发出了一阵阵的欢呼声。|The report was full of howlers. 报告里充满了滑稽的错误。|synonyms: yells, shouts, screams, roars, blunders, gaffes, mistakes|antonyms: whispers, murmurs, silence, successes, perfections|noun
