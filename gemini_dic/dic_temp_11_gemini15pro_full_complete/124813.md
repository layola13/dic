mucigen:/ˈmjuːsɪdʒən/
n.
粘蛋白原
A glycoprotein that is the principal constituent of mucus. It is secreted by mucous membranes and forms mucus when it mixes with water.
Goblet cells synthesize and secrete mucigen. 杯状细胞合成和分泌粘蛋白原。
近义词:mucin precursor
反义词:None
词性:noun
