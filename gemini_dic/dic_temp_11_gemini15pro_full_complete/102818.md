jargoons:/ˈdʒɑːrɡənz/ | n. | 行话，术语，黑话 |Technical or specialized terms used within a particular profession or group, often difficult for outsiders to understand. |例句：The lawyers spoke in legal jargoons, making it hard for the client to follow. 律师们用法律术语讲话，客户很难听懂。

例句：The computer programmers used so much technical jargon that I was completely lost. 计算机程序员用了太多专业术语，我完全听不懂了。 | 近义词：slang, cant, argot, terminology | 反义词：standard language, plain English | 词性: noun
