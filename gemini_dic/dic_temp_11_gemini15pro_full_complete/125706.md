mongoose:/ˈmɑːŋɡuːs/ | n. | 猫鼬 | A small carnivorous mammal with a long body and tail, native to Africa and Asia. It is noted for its ability to kill venomous snakes. | The mongoose quickly darted and dodged the cobra's strikes. 猫鼬快速地躲避着眼镜蛇的攻击。

The zookeeper fed the mongoose a small piece of meat.  动物园管理员给猫鼬喂了一小块肉。 | Synonyms: meerkat, suricate | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | noun
