counteruniversity:/ˌkaʊntərˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəti/ | n. | 反大学，另类大学 |  A counter-institution to a traditional university, often focusing on radical or alternative forms of education, community engagement, and social change. It often challenges traditional academic hierarchies and promotes self-directed learning. |  The counteruniversity offered workshops on community organizing and sustainable living, topics rarely found in a traditional university curriculum. 反大学开设了关于社区组织和可持续生活的研讨会，这些主题在传统的大学课程中很少见。

The activists established a counteruniversity to provide free education focused on social justice. 这些活动家建立了一所反大学，提供以社会正义为重点的免费教育。 | 近义词: free school, alternative school, people's university | 反义词: university, traditional university | 词性: 名词
