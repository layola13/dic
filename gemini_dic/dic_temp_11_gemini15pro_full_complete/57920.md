drillmaster:/ˈdrɪlˌmæstər/ | n. | 操练官；教官；训练员 | A person who instructs people rigorously, especially soldiers, in drills and exercises. | The drillmaster barked orders at the new recruits. 教官对着新兵大声吼叫着命令。|  The drillmaster put the soldiers through their paces. 教官让士兵们进行各种训练。 |Synonyms: instructor, trainer, coach, teacher, disciplinarian | Antonyms: student, trainee, pupil | Noun
