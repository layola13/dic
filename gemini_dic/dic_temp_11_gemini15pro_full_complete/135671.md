nonprelatic:/ˌnɒnˈprɛlətɪk/
adjective
非主教制的；非主教的
Not relating to or governed by prelates (high-ranking church officials, such as bishops).
The nonprelatic church structure allows for more democratic decision-making.  这个非主教制的教会结构允许更多的民主决策。
The small, independent church had a nonprelatic form of governance. 这个小型独立教会采用非主教制的管理形式。
Synonyms: nonhierarchical, congregational
Antonyms: prelatical, hierarchical
