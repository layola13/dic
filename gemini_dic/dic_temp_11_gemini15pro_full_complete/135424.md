nonpersecutory:/ˌnɒnpɜːrˈsɛkjʊtɔːri/ | adj. | 非迫害性的 |  Relating to or characterized by the absence of persecution; not involving or intended to persecute.  It describes something that does not involve harassing, oppressing, or unfairly treating someone. |  The judge ruled that the plaintiff's claim was nonpersecutory and therefore did not qualify for asylum. 法官裁定原告的主张并非基于迫害，因此不符合庇护资格。

The government offered nonpersecutory reasons for its decision to deny his visa. 政府就其拒签决定提供了非迫害性理由。 |  Near synonyms: non-oppressive, non-discriminatory, unbiased, impartial  | Antonyms: persecutory, discriminatory, oppressive, biased | adjective
