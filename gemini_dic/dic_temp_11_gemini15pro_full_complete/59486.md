easings:/ˈiːzɪŋz/
| n. |缓动; 缓和; 轻松; |A function that determines the rate of change of a property over time.  Used in animation and transitions to create smooth and natural-looking movements. |The animation uses cubic bezier easings for a smooth transition.| 近义词:transitions, animations, effects|反义词:jerks, abruptness|词性:名词
