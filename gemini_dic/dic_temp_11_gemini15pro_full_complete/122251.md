miter:/ˈmaɪtər/| n. v.|斜接；斜接处；主教冠；斜接；斜切|A miter is a joint made by beveling each of two parts to be joined, usually at a 45° angle, to form a corner, typically a 90° angle.|The carpenter used a miter saw to create a perfect miter joint for the picture frame.|近义词：bevel, angle|反义词:butt joint|词性:名词, 动词

miter:/ˈmaɪtər/| v.|斜接；斜切|To join two pieces of wood or other material at an angle, usually 45 degrees, to form a corner.|He mitered the edges of the molding before installing it.|近义词：bevel, angle|反义词:butt|词性:动词
