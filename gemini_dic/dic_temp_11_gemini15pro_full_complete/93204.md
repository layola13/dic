what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |  pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | 例句：What book are you reading? 你在读什么书？| 近义词: which | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she been up to? = 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been thinking about lately? = 他最近一直在想什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for dinner? = 他们晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by 2030? = 到2030年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years? = 到那时你将已经学习了五年什么？
What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将一直在做什么？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该做什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to think. = 我不知道该怎么想。
There's nothing what I want here. = 这里没有我想要的东西。
