incomplete:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːt/
adjective|未完成的，不完整的|Not finished; lacking some part.
The building project is incomplete due to a shortage of funds. |由于资金短缺，建筑项目未能完成。
Her application was rejected because it was incomplete. |她的申请因为不完整而被拒绝。
synonyms: unfinished, partial, lacking
antonyms: complete, finished, whole
词性:形容词

incomplete:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːt/
adjective|不完善的，有缺陷的|Not perfect or fully developed; defective.
The fossil record is incomplete, making it difficult to trace the evolution of certain species. |化石记录并不完整，使得追踪某些物种的进化变得困难。
His understanding of the subject was incomplete. |他对这门学科的理解并不完善。
synonyms: imperfect, deficient, faulty
antonyms: perfect, flawless, whole
词性:形容词


词源:  From in- (not) + complete.  The word "complete" comes from the Latin "complētus," past participle of "complēre" (to fill up, complete), from "com-" (together) + "plēre" (to fill).
