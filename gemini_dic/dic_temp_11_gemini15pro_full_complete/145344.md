overbulkily:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌlkɪli/| adv.|过于庞大地，过于笨重地|Excessively bulky; in a way that takes up too much space.|The furniture was overbulkily arranged, making the room feel cramped. 家具摆放得过于笨重，使房间感觉很狭窄。|The package was overbulkily wrapped and difficult to carry.  这个包裹包装得过于庞大，难以携带。|近义词:cumbersomely, awkwardly, clumsily|反义词:compactly, neatly, efficiently|词性:副词

词源:

over- (prefix):  from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above, beyond".  表示“过度”、“超过”。
bulky (adjective): from Middle English bulky, from bulk "size, mass". 指“庞大的”、“笨重的”。
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives. 将形容词变为副词。
