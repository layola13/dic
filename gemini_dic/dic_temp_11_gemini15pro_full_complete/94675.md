imprinted:/ɪmˈprɪntɪd/| adj.|印记的，铭刻的，深刻记住的|Marked or stamped with an impression; firmly fixed in the mind.|The image of the accident was imprinted on her memory.  事故的画面深深地印在了她的记忆里。|Synonyms: engraved, etched, stamped, fixed, ingrained|Antonyms: forgotten, erased|adjective

imprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/ | n. vt.|印记，痕迹；铭刻，使铭记|A mark made by pressing something onto a softer substance; to fix an idea, image, etc. firmly in someone's mind.|The dog's paws left imprints in the wet cement. 狗的爪子在湿水泥上留下了印记。  The experience was imprinted on his mind forever. 这段经历永远铭刻在他的心中。|Synonyms: mark, impression, stamp; impress, fix, instill|Antonyms: erase; forget|noun, verb
