fractionated:/ˈfrækʃəneɪtɪd/| adj.|分馏的，分级的|Separated into different components or fractions, typically by distillation or other physical processes.  Refers to a substance that has undergone fractionation.|The chemist analyzed the fractionated components of the crude oil. 化学家分析了原油的分馏成分。|fractional, divided, separated|whole, combined, complete|adjective

fractionate:/ˈfrækʃəneɪt/| vt.|分馏，分级|To separate something into different components or fractions, typically by distillation or other physical processes.|The crude oil was fractionated into gasoline, kerosene, and diesel. 原油被分馏成汽油、煤油和柴油。|separate, divide, distil|combine, mix, blend|verb
