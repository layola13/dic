misleaders:/ˌmɪsˈliːdərz/
noun
误导者，欺骗者
Those who deliberately give false or misleading information.
The article exposed the misleading advertisements of certain weight loss products and named the misleaders behind them. 这篇文章揭露了某些减肥产品的误导性广告，并指出了背后的误导者。
Synonyms: deceivers, defrauders, impostors, charlatans
Antonyms: guides, mentors, instructors
