necked:/nekt/ | adj. | 有...脖子的；瓶颈的 | Having a neck of a specified kind. |  The swan is a long-necked bird.  这只天鹅是一种长脖子的鸟。| long-necked, slender-necked | short-necked, thick-necked | adjective

necked:/nekt/ | adj. | 瓶颈的 |  Resembling a neck in being narrow or constricted. | The traffic flow is necked at the bridge.  桥梁处交通流量受限。| bottlenecked, constricted | wide, open | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hēafod-hnēcca (literally "head-neck") and *hnēcca (neck), from Proto-Germanic *knakkaz (neck).
