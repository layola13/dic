intercolline:/ˌɪntərˈkɒlaɪn/| adj.|丘陵间的，位于丘陵之间的|Situated between hills. |The intercolline valley was fertile and well-populated. 丘陵间的山谷肥沃，人口稠密。|The intercolline region is known for its vineyards.  丘陵地区以其葡萄园而闻名。|近义词：intermontane|反义词: peak, summit|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inter collēs*  "between hills," from *inter* "between" + *collēs*, plural of *collis* "hill."
