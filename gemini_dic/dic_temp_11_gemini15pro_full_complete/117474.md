medivacking:/ˈmɛdɪvækɪŋ/
|n.|医疗后送，医疗撤离|The act or process of evacuating someone, especially a wounded soldier, from a battlefield or other dangerous area to a place where they can receive medical care.|例句：The medivacking of the wounded soldier was successful. 受伤士兵的医疗后送成功了。

例句：They requested immediate medivacking for the injured climber. 他们请求立即对受伤的登山者进行医疗后送。|近义词：medical evacuation, airlift|反义词: N/A|词性: noun (gerund) derived from verb "medivac"


medivac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/
|n.|医疗后送的飞机（或直升机），医疗后送|An aircraft, especially a helicopter, used for medivacking. |例句：The medivac landed on the hospital roof. 医疗后送直升机降落在医院屋顶上。

例句：A medivac was dispatched to the scene of the accident. 一架医疗后送飞机被派往事故现场。|近义词：air ambulance|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


medivac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/
|vt.|用飞机（或直升机）医疗后送|To evacuate (someone) from a battlefield or other dangerous area to a place where they can receive medical care, especially by aircraft. |例句：The soldier was medivacked to a nearby hospital. 这名士兵被用飞机医疗后送到了附近的一家医院。

例句：They medivacked the injured civilians out of the war zone. 他们将受伤的平民用飞机撤离了战区。|近义词：airlift, evacuate|反义词: N/A|词性: verb

词源: Clipped form of medical evacuation.
