cadaveric:/kəˈdævərɪk/
adjective
尸体似的；像尸体的
Relating to a dead body, or resembling a corpse.
The cadaveric smell permeated the air.  尸体的气味弥漫在空气中。
The patient exhibited a cadaveric pallor. 病人脸色苍白，像死人一样。
Near synonyms: corpse-like, deathly, ghastly
Antonyms: lively, vibrant, living
词性: 形容词
