knaggy:/ˈnæɡi/| adj.|多节的，粗糙的，脾气暴躁的|Covered with hard or rough knobs; knotty; gnarled. Also, bad-tempered; irritable.|The old man had knaggy fingers, twisted with arthritis. 这位老人的手指粗糙多节，因关节炎而扭曲变形。|近义词：gnarled, knotty, lumpy, bumpy, rough, craggy, irritable, grumpy, cantankerous|反义词:smooth, even, pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *knag (n.), probably of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse *knag (peg, knot).
