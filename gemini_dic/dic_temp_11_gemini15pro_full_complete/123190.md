monocarpellary:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkɑːpəl(ə)ri/ /ˌmɑːnəʊˈkɑːrpəˌlɛri/| adj.|单心皮的|Having a single carpel (the female reproductive organ of a flower).|Example:  The pea family is characterized by its monocarpellary fruits. 豌豆科植物的特征是其单心皮果实。

Example: Monocarpellary flowers are simpler in structure than those with multiple carpels. 单心皮花朵的结构比多心皮花朵简单。|近义词:unicarpellate|反义词:polycarpellary, multicarpellary|词性:adjective
