debateful:/dɪˈbeɪtfʊl/
adjective
可争论的，值得讨论的
Open to debate; debatable.  Characterized by or prone to debate.
The effectiveness of the new policy is debateful.  这项新政策的有效性是有争议的。
近义词:debatable, arguable, disputable, controversial
反义词:undebatable, indisputable, unarguable, incontrovertible
词性:adjective
