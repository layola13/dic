flankerback:/ˈflæŋkərbæk/| n.|侧卫，边后卫|A rugby position, typically a fast and mobile player positioned on the flanks of the scrum.|例句：The flankerback made a crucial tackle, preventing a try.  这名侧卫完成了一次关键的擒抱，阻止了对方达阵。

例句：He's a talented flankerback, known for his speed and tackling ability. 他是一位才华横溢的侧卫，以速度和擒抱能力而闻名。|近义词：wing forward, flanker|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps prop or lock as they play different roles in the scrum)|词性:noun
