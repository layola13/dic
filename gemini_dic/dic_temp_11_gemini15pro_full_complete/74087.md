fossulate:/ˈfɒsjʊleɪt/ | v. | (鸟类)用喙挖掘；翻寻 | To dig or scratch with the beak, as a bird does, especially in search of food. | The hen fossulated in the dirt for grubs. 母鸡在泥土里翻寻蛴螬。| 近义词: rummage, forage, root | 反义词: bury, cover | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *fossulatus*, past participle of *fossulare* "to dig a little ditch," from *fossa* "ditch, trench."
