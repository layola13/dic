expansile:/ɪkˈspænsaɪl/
adjective
可膨胀的，可扩张的
Capable of expanding or becoming enlarged.
The tumor was expansile, pushing against the surrounding tissues.  肿瘤是膨胀性的，压迫周围组织。
The metal stent is expansile, allowing it to conform to the shape of the blood vessel.  金属支架是可膨胀的，使其能够适应血管的形状。
synonyms: expandable, distensible, inflatable
antonyms: contractile, shrinking, compressing
词性: 形容词
