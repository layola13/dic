estuary: /ˈestʃʊəri/ (US), /ˈestʃəri/ (UK)| n. |河口，江口|A partially enclosed coastal body of brackish water with one or more rivers or streams flowing into it, and with a free connection to the open sea.|The Chesapeake Bay is the largest estuary in the United States. 切萨皮克湾是美国最大的河口。|近义词：inlet, firth, bay|反义词:upstream|词性:名词

estuaries: /ˈestʃʊəriz/ (US), /ˈestʃəriz/ (UK)| n. |河口，江口（复数）|Plural form of estuary, referring to multiple partially enclosed coastal bodies of brackish water.|Many estuaries are important breeding grounds for fish. 许多河口是重要的鱼类繁殖地。|近义词：inlets, firths, bays|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
