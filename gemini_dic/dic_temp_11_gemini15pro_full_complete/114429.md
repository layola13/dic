make up for:/meɪk ˈʌp fɔːr/
phrasal verb
弥补；补偿
to compensate for something bad or wrong that has happened or that has been done or not done
例句：I'll work extra hours to make up for the time I missed. 我会加班来弥补我错过的时间。

例句：He bought her flowers to make up for being late. 他买了花来弥补迟到。

近义词：compensate for, atone for, rectify, redress
反义词：worsen, aggravate
词性：动词短语
