expanded:/ɪkˈspændɪd/| adj.|扩大的，展开的，膨胀的|Having been made larger or more extensive.|The company expanded its operations into new markets. 公司将其业务扩展到新的市场。|近义词：enlarged, widened, increased, extended, broadened, stretched, dilated, inflated|反义词：contracted, shrunk, reduced, diminished, compressed, deflated|adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *expandere*, from *ex- 'out' + pandere 'to spread'*.
