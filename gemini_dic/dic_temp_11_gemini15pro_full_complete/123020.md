monasticize:/məˈnæstɪsaɪz/
verb
使成为修道院生活;使过禁欲生活;使脱离尘世
To cause to adopt monastic practices or a monastic lifestyle; to seclude from worldly affairs.
He decided to monasticize his life after the death of his wife.  他妻子去世后，他决定过隐居生活。
The king monasticized the entire kingdom, forbidding all forms of entertainment. 国王将整个王国修道院化，禁止一切形式的娱乐。
Synonyms: cloister, seclude, isolate
Antonyms: secularize, socialize, worldlify
词性: 动词
