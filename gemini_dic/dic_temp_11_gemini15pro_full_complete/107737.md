lapa:/ˈlæpə/| n.|岩蹄兔，岩石缝隙|A hyrax, especially a rock hyrax, a small, thickset, herbivorous mammal native to Africa and the Middle East, living in colonies among rocks.|The lapa scurried into the crevice. 岩蹄兔 scurried 进了缝隙。|近义词：hyrax, dassie|反义词:|名词
