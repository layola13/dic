accusers:/əˈkjuːzərz/| n. |控告者，原告，指责者|A person who accuses someone of something, especially of a crime.  A person who formally charges another with a crime or offense.|The accusers presented compelling evidence against the defendant. 控告者提供了针对被告的有力证据。|synonyms: complainant, prosecutor, plaintiff, indicter|antonyms: defendant, accused, suspect|noun
