depreciatingly:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪtɪŋli/
| adv. |贬低地，轻蔑地|in a way that expresses that something is worthless or unimportant|He spoke depreciatingly of his own achievements. 他贬低自己的成就。|Synonyms: dismissively, contemptuously, scornfully|Antonyms: admiringly, respectfully, approvingly|词性:副词

depreciatingly:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪtɪŋli/
| adv. |轻视地，贬值地|in a way that makes something seem less valuable or important|She glanced depreciatingly at his worn-out shoes. 她轻蔑地瞥了一眼他那双破旧的鞋子。|Synonyms: belittlingly, disdainfully|Antonyms: appreciatively, flatteringly|词性:副词
