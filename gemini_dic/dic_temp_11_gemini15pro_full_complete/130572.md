nonactivist:/ˌnɒnˈæktɪvɪst/
adjective
非积极分子；不积极的
Not active, especially in political or social reform.
例句：The nonactivist investors were content to simply collect dividends. 这些不积极的投资者只满足于收取股息。

例句：He took a nonactivist approach to the problem, preferring to wait and see. 他对这个问题采取了不积极的态度，宁愿观望。

近义词：passive, inactive, uninvolved
反义词：activist, engaged, involved
词性：adjective
