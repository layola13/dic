obituary:/əˈbɪtʃuəri/ /oʊˈbɪtʃueri/ | n. |讣告，讣闻|A notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person.|例句：Her obituary appeared in the local newspaper. 她的讣告刊登在当地报纸上。

例句：The newspaper published a lengthy obituary for the renowned author. 这家报纸刊登了这位著名作家的长篇讣告。

近义词：death notice, necrology|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a factual notice) |词性: Noun


obituarial:/əˌbɪtʃuˈɛriəl/ /oʊˌbɪtʃuˈɛriəl/| adj. |讣告的，讣闻的|Relating to or resembling an obituary.|例句：He wrote an obituarial poem for his friend. 他为他的朋友写了一首悼亡诗。

例句：The obituarial tone of the article made it clear the subject had passed away. 这篇文章的讣告语气清楚地表明了主人公已经去世了。

近义词：valedictory, elegiac|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a descriptive term) |词性: Adjective
