half-praised:/ˌhæfˈpreɪzd/ | adj. |  受到一半称赞的；赞扬不足的 |  Partially or inadequately praised; praised with reservations.  It describes something or someone that has received some praise, but not as much as deserved or expected.  | The half-praised novel eventually gained recognition as a masterpiece. 这部受到一半称赞的小说最终被认为是一部杰作。 | The play received a half-praised review in the local paper.  这出戏在当地报纸上获得的评价褒贬不一。 | Near synonyms: underpraised, faintly praised, lukewarmly praised | Antonyms: highly praised, acclaimed, lauded | adjective
