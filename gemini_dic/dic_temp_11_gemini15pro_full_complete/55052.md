disquisitive:/dɪˈskwɪzɪtɪv/| adj.|好问的；爱钻研的|Inclined to ask many questions; intellectually curious.| She has a disquisitive mind and is always eager to learn new things. 她有着一颗求知若渴的心，总是渴望学习新事物。|例句：The child, naturally disquisitive, asked a barrage of questions about the museum exhibits.  这个孩子天生好奇，对博物馆的展品提了一连串的问题。

例句：He was known for his disquisitive nature, always delving into complex topics. 他以爱钻研的性格而闻名，总是深入研究复杂的话题。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, inquiring |反义词：apathetic, indifferent, incurious|词性:形容词
