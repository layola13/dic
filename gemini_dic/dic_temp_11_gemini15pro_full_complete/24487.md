brittlest:/ˈbrɪt(ə)ləst/| adj.|最脆的；最易碎的|superlative adjective of brittle; most brittle|The biscuits were so old they were the brittlest I'd ever seen.  这些饼干太老了，是我见过的最脆的。|The old bone was the brittlest part of the skeleton. 这块古老的骨头是骨骼中最易碎的部分。|近义词：fragile, frail, delicate, breakable|反义词：tough, strong, resilient, flexible|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *brittil, from Old English *bryttian (“to break, shatter”), from Proto-Germanic *breutaną (“to break”). Cognate with Dutch broos (“brittle”), German brüchig (“brittle”).  The -est suffix is the superlative ending.
