concessionaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/| n. |特许经营者， concession holder|A person or company that has been granted a concession, especially to operate a business within a particular area.|The concessionaire operates several restaurants in the national park.  这个特许经营者在国家公园里经营着几家餐馆。|Synonyms: licensee, franchisee, operator|Antonyms: licensor, franchisor|noun

concessionaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/| n. |受让人， concession holder|A person or company to whom something, typically a right or privilege, has been granted by a government, company, or other controlling body.|The concessionaire for the mining rights has begun exploratory drilling. 采矿权的受让人已开始探勘性钻探。|Synonyms: grantee, recipient, beneficiary|Antonyms: grantor|noun
