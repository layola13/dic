hasteful:/ˈheɪstfʊl/| adj.|匆忙的，草率的，仓促的|Characterized by excessive haste; done or acting with undue speed and often with insufficient thought or care.|She made a hasteful decision and later regretted it. 她做了一个草率的决定，后来后悔了。|He gave a hasteful glance at his watch and hurried out the door. 他匆匆瞥了一眼手表，然后冲出门去。|近义词：hurried, rushed, hasty, precipitate, impetuous|反义词：deliberate, careful, thoughtful, measured, cautious|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hastifull, from Old French hastif, from haste (“haste”) + -ful.
