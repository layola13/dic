cortical:/ˈkɔːrtɪkl/ (US), /ˈkɔːtɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|皮质的，皮层的|Relating to the outer layer of an organ, especially the brain.|The cortical areas of the brain are responsible for higher-level cognitive functions. 大脑皮层区域负责高级认知功能。|The doctor examined the cortical tissue under a microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了皮层组织。|近义词：cerebral, outer|反义词：medullary, inner|词性: adjective

cortex (n.) + -al (adj. suffix)  From Latin *cortex, cortic- "bark, rind, shell, husk"
