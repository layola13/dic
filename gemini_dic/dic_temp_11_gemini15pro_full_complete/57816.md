drenched:/dren(t)ʃt/
adjective
湿透的
soaked, saturated with liquid
例句：My clothes were drenched after walking in the rain.  我的衣服在雨中行走后湿透了。

例句：The dog was drenched from playing in the pond.  那只狗在池塘里玩耍后浑身湿透了。

近义词：soaked, saturated, sopping
反义词：dry, arid, parched
词性: adjective


drench:/dren(t)ʃ/
verb
使湿透
to wet thoroughly; to soak
例句：The sudden downpour drenched us to the bone.  突如其来的倾盆大雨把我们淋得透湿。

例句：I accidentally drenched myself with the garden hose. 我不小心用水管把自己浇透了。

近义词：soak, saturate, sop
反义词：dry, dehydrate, desiccate
词性: verb


词源: From Middle English *drenchen, from Old English drencan (“to give to drink, drench, submerge, drown”), from Proto-Germanic *drangkijaną (“to give to drink, cause to drink”), from Proto-Indo-European *dhrengʰ- (“to drink”). Cognate with Dutch drenken (“to give to drink, water, irrigate, soak, steep”), German tränken (“to give to drink, water, irrigate, soak, steep”), Icelandic drekkja (“to drown”).
