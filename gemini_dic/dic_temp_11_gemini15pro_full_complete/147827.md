overpump:/ˈoʊvərˌpʌmp/| v.|过度抽取，过量泵送|To pump excessively, often leading to depletion or damage.|The farmer overpumped the well, causing it to run dry. 农民过度抽取井水，导致井干涸了。|Overpumping groundwater can lead to land subsidence. 过度抽取地下水会导致地面沉降。|近义词：overdraw, deplete|反义词: underpump (非正式), conserve|词性:动词
