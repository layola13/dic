encapsulating:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 封装的；概括的；压缩的 | enclosing something in or as if in a capsule; expressing the essential features of something succinctly | The new design encapsulating the latest technology.  新设计囊括了最新技术。| synonyms: summarizing, epitomizing, compressing, condensing | antonyms: expanding, elaborating, detailing | adjective

encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | v. |  封装；概括；压缩 | express the essential features of (something) succinctly | This report encapsulates all the main points discussed in the meeting. 这份报告概括了会议上讨论的所有要点。| synonyms: summarize, epitomize, condense | antonyms: expand, elaborate, detail | verb
