inappreciable:/ˌɪnəˈpriːʃəbl/ | adj. | 微不足道的，难以察觉的 | Too small to be noticed or considered important; negligible. | The difference in taste between the two wines was inappreciable. 这两种葡萄酒的味道差异微乎其微。 | Synonyms: negligible, imperceptible, insignificant, minute, tiny | Antonyms: appreciable, considerable, significant, substantial | adjective

inappreciable:/ˌɪnəˈpriːʃəbl/ | adj. |  不感激的，不领情的 (obsolete, rare) | Not feeling or expressing gratitude. |  His inappreciable attitude towards her kindness hurt her deeply. 他对她的好意不领情，深深地伤害了她。(This usage is now rare and generally considered obsolete.) | Synonyms: ungrateful, unthankful | Antonyms: appreciative, grateful, thankful | adjective
