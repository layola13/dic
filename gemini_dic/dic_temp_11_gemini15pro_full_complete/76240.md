futuristically:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪkli/
adjective
未来式地；未来派地
in a way that suggests the future, especially in appearance or design
例句：The building was futuristically designed, with sleek lines and metallic surfaces.  这栋建筑采用未来主义设计，线条流畅，表面呈金属质感。

例句：Her clothes were always strikingly futuristic, incorporating unusual materials and shapes. 她的服装总是惊人地具有未来感，融合了不寻常的材料和形状。

近义词：advanced, modern, innovative, cutting-edge, avant-garde
反义词：old-fashioned, traditional, outdated, archaic, antiquated
词性: adjective
