cicutoxin:/sɪˈkjuːtɒksɪn/ | n. |毒芹毒素| A highly poisonous neurotoxin found in the water hemlock plant (Cicuta maculata).  It is considered one of the most potent plant toxins in North America. |  Cicutoxin can cause seizures and death if ingested.  毒芹毒素如果摄入会导致癫痫发作和死亡。 |  Synonyms:  water hemlock toxin, cicutol | Antonyms:  antidote, antitoxin | Noun
