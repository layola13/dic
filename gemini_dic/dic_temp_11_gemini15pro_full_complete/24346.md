brimstone:/ˈbrɪmˌstoʊn/| n. |硫磺；地狱之火|A yellow nonmetallic element that burns with a blue flame and a suffocating odor. Figuratively, it represents hellfire and punishment.|The air reeked of brimstone after the volcano erupted. 火山爆发后，空气中弥漫着硫磺味。|Synonyms: sulfur, hellfire|Antonyms: heaven, paradise|noun
