narcolepsy:/ˈnɑːrkəˌlɛpsi/| n. |嗜睡症，发作性睡病|A neurological disorder characterized by excessive daytime sleepiness, cataplexy (sudden muscle weakness), sleep paralysis, and hallucinations.|例句：Narcolepsy can make it difficult to stay awake during the day. 嗜睡症会让人难以在白天保持清醒。

例句：He was diagnosed with narcolepsy after years of unexplained sleep attacks.  经过多年的不明原因的睡眠发作后，他被诊断出患有嗜睡症。|近义词：hypersomnia, somnolence|反义词:insomnia|词性:noun
