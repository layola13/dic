browns:/braʊnz/| n. |棕色; 褐色; 焦糖色|A dark tertiary color with a tint of red and yellow or green.| The autumn leaves turned beautiful shades of browns. 秋天的叶子变成了美丽的棕色。|近义词：auburn, bronze, sepia, chestnut|反义词: white, light|词性:名词

browns:/braʊnz/| v. |(使)变成褐色; (使)变成棕色; 煎至焦黄|To cook something until it turns brown.| She browned the meat in a pan before adding the vegetables. 她先把肉在锅里煎成褐色，然后再加入蔬菜。|近义词：sear, roast, toast|反义词: bleach, whiten|词性:动词
