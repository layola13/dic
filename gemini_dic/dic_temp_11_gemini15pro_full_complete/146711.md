overhill:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪl/| adj.|在山的另一边，翻过山的|Situated or living on the other side of a hill; across the hill.|We hiked overhill to reach the next village. 我们翻过山到达了下一个村庄。|近义词：across the hill, beyond the hill|反义词: near, close|词性:形容词

词源:  From 'over' (meaning across or beyond) + 'hill'.  Simple and straightforward compound word.
