ledgiest:/ˈlɛdʒiɪst/
adjective
最险峻的，最危险的
Most precarious; having the most ledges.  Characterized by having numerous or prominent ledges, often implying a sense of danger or exposure.
The climber tackled the ledgiest part of the mountain. 攀登者应对了山上最险峻的部分。
The ledgiest cliffs offered spectacular views but were also terrifying.  最险峻的悬崖提供了壮观的景色，但也令人恐惧。
近义词:precipitous, sheer, steep, dangerous, perilous
反义词:safe, secure, flat
词性:adjective
