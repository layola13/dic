brake-in-on:/breɪkˈɪnˌɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 干扰，打断，插嘴 | To interrupt someone or something abruptly. | Example: He kept braking-in-on our conversation.  他不停地打断我们的谈话。| Example: She braked-in-on the meeting to announce the news. 她打断会议来宣布这个消息。 | Synonyms: interrupt, intrude, barge in, cut in | Antonyms: allow, permit, listen | verb phrase

brake-in-on:/breɪkˈɪnˌɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 强行介入，干涉 | To forcefully involve oneself in a situation, especially one that is not one's business.  | Example: He braked-in-on the argument between his siblings. 他强行介入了兄弟姐妹之间的争吵。 | Example: The government braked-in-on the negotiations between the two companies. 政府强行介入了两家公司之间的谈判。| Synonyms: intervene, interfere, meddle | Antonyms: abstain, withdraw | verb phrase
