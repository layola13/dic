anti-pragmatism:/ˈæntiˈpræɡmətɪzəm/| n.|反实用主义|A philosophical doctrine that opposes pragmatism, often emphasizing abstract principles, absolute truths, or the importance of theory over practical consequences.|His anti-pragmatism led him to reject any solution that didn't address the root cause of the problem. 他的反实用主义导致他拒绝任何不解决问题根源的方案。

Anti-pragmatism prioritizes theoretical consistency over empirical validation. 反实用主义优先考虑理论一致性而不是经验验证。|近义词：idealism, absolutism|反义词: pragmatism, empiricism|词性:noun
