bevel:/ˈbevəl/| n. v. |斜面，斜角；使成斜角|A sloping surface or edge.  To cut or shape to a sloping edge.|The picture frame had a decorative bevel.  这幅画的画框有一个装饰性的斜面。 He beveled the edges of the table. 他把桌子的边缘削成了斜角。|近义词:slope, slant, chamfer|反义词:straight, level|词性:名词, 动词

bevels:/ˈbevəlz/| n. v. |斜面，斜角；使成斜角|A sloping surface or edge.  To cut or shape to a sloping edge.|The mirror had decorative bevels around the edge. 这面镜子的边缘有装饰性的斜边。 He bevels the edges of the glass for safety. 他为了安全起见，把玻璃的边缘磨成斜角。|近义词:slopes, slants, chamfers|反义词:straight lines, levels|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bivel, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French beveau "drinking bout," from beivre "to drink," from Latin bibere.  The connection might be to the slanted edge of a drinking cup.
