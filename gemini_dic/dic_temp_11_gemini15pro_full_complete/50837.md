desecrate: /ˈdɛsɪkreɪt/ | vt. | 亵渎，玷污 | To treat (a sacred place or thing) with violent disrespect; violate. |  The vandals desecrated the church by spray-painting graffiti on the altar. 这些故意破坏者在祭坛上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了教堂。

They desecrated the graves by digging them up. 他们挖掘坟墓，亵渎了死者。


Synonym: profane, defile, violate, contaminate

Antonym: revere, honor, sanctify, consecrate

Etymology:  From Latin *desecrāre*, from *de-* (expressing reversal) + *sacrāre* (“to consecrate, dedicate”), from *sacr-* (“sacred”).

词性: 动词 (verb)
