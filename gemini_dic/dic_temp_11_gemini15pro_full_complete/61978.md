enables:/ɪˈneɪblz/| v.|使能够，使成为可能|This new software enables us to work more efficiently.  这个新软件使我们能够更高效地工作。|例句：The scholarship enabled her to study abroad.  奖学金使她能够出国留学。

例句：His wealth enables him to live a luxurious life. 他的财富使他能够过上奢侈的生活。|近义词：allows, permits, facilitates, empowers|反义词：prevents, hinders, restricts|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *enhablen*, from Old French *enhabile*, *enable*, from en- + habile (“able, apt, skillful”), from Latin habilis (“easily held, manageable, fit, suitable”), from habēre (“to have, hold”).
