aldermanlike:/ˈɔːldərmənˌlaɪk/
adjective
像市議員的；有市議員風度的
Resembling or characteristic of an alderman, typically suggesting dignity, pomposity, or self-importance.
He had an aldermanlike air of authority, even though he was just a volunteer. 他有一种像市議員般的權威氣度，即使他只是一名志願者。
The cat sat on the chair with an aldermanlike dignity. 那隻貓以一種像市議員般莊嚴的姿態坐在椅子上。
近义词:dignified, pompous, magisterial, self-important
反义词:humble, modest, unpretentious
词性:adjective

词源:
From alderman +‎ -like.  Alderman comes from Old English *ealdormann "chief, leader, headman," from ealdor "elder, chief, prince" (see elder (adj.)) + mann "man."  -like is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
