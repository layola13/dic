impermissibility:/ˌɪmpərˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可允许；不允许 |The impermissibility of such behavior is clear. 这种行为的不可允许性是显而易见的。| The judge stressed the impermissibility of using evidence obtained through illegal means. 法官强调了使用非法手段获取的证据的不可接受性。| 近义词：unacceptability, illegality, prohibition | 反义词：permissibility, allowability, legality | 词性: noun | 词源: From Latin *impermissibilis*, from *in-* (not) + *permissus* (permitted), past participle of *permittere* (to permit).
