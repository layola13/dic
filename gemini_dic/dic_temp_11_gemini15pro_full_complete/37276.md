commandant:/ˌkɒmənˈdɑːnt/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdænt/| n.| 司令官；指挥官； Commandant |A commandant is an officer who is in charge of a military organization or establishment.|The commandant of the Marine Corps base addressed the troops.  海军陆战队基地司令向部队发表了讲话。|Synonyms: commander, officer, chief, leader|Antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun

commandants:/ˌkɒmənˈdɑːnts/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdænts/| n.| 司令官们；指挥官们；Commandants |The plural form of commandant, referring to multiple officers in charge of military organizations or establishments.|The commandants of several bases met to discuss strategy.  几个基地的司令官开会讨论战略。|Synonyms: commanders, officers, chiefs, leaders|Antonyms: subordinates, followers|noun
