folivore:/ˈfɒlɪvɔːr/ (UK), /ˈfoʊləˌvɔr/ (US)| n. |食叶动物|An animal that feeds chiefly on leaves.|例句：The koala is a well-known folivore, subsisting almost entirely on eucalyptus leaves.  考拉是一种著名的食叶动物，几乎完全以桉树叶为生。

例句：Sloths are folivores and spend most of their time hanging in trees, munching on leaves.  树懒是食叶动物，大部分时间都挂在树上，咀嚼树叶。|近义词：herbivore, browser|反义词：carnivore, omnivore|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *folium* "leaf" + *-vorus* "-eating," from *vorare* "to devour."
