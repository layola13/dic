compote:/ˈkɒmpəʊt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmpoʊt/ (US)| n.|蜜饯，果盘；（肉或蔬菜的）炖菜|A dessert of fruit stewed in syrup.|We had apple compote for dessert. 我们甜点吃了苹果蜜饯。|近义词:stew, conserve, preserve|反义词:(None, as it's a specific type of food preparation)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *compote*, from Late Latin *composita*, feminine past participle of *componere* "to put together," from *com-* "together" + *ponere* "to put, place."
