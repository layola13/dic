nondiscernment:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɜːnmənt/ | n. |缺乏辨别力，不加区分|Lack of ability or willingness to distinguish between things.  |He showed a nondiscernment between right and wrong. 他表现出对是非缺乏辨别能力。|Synonyms: indiscrimination, undiscerningness, lack of judgment |Antonyms: discernment, discrimination, judgment |noun
