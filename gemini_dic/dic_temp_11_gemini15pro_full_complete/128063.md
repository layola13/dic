nerved:/nɜːrvd/
adjective
准备好的；鼓起勇气的；坚决的
strengthened and prepared to face a difficult situation.
例句：She was nerved for the challenge ahead. 她已准备好迎接未来的挑战。

例句：He nerved himself to ask for a raise. 他鼓起勇气要求加薪。


近义词：braced, prepared, resolute, determined, steeled
反义词：hesitant, timid, apprehensive, fearful, cowardly
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From nerve + -ed.  "Nerve" itself comes from Latin "nervus," meaning "sinew, tendon, nerve."
