doula:/ˈduːlə/| n. |导乐;陪产员|A trained professional who provides physical, emotional, and informational support to a pregnant person before, during, and shortly after childbirth.|例句：My doula was a huge help during labor. 我的导乐在分娩期间给了我巨大的帮助。

例句：Having a doula present can reduce the need for medical interventions during childbirth. 有导乐在场可以减少分娩期间医疗干预的需要。|近义词：birth companion, childbirth assistant, labor support person|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Ancient Greek word δούλα (doúla), meaning "female slave" or "female servant." The modern usage has evolved to reflect a supportive and empowering role.
