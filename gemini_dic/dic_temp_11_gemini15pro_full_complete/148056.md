oversale:/ˌoʊvərˈseɪl/ /ˌəʊvəˈseɪl/ | v. | 超售；过分推销 | To sell more of something, such as tickets, than is available or than can be delivered.  To promote something excessively enthusiastically. | The airline oversold the flight and had to bump some passengers.  航空公司超售了航班，不得不拒绝一些乘客登机。 |  overbook, oversell | undersell | verb

oversale:/ˈoʊvərˌseɪl/ /ˈəʊvəˌseɪl/ | n. | 超售；过分推销 | The act of overselling. The state of being oversold. | The oversale of tickets caused chaos at the concert.  门票超售导致音乐会现场一片混乱。 | overbooking | undersale | noun
