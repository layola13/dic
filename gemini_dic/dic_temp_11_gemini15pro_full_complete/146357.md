overfix:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪks/| v. |过度修理，过度矫正|To repair or adjust something excessively, often to the point of making it worse.|例句：He overfixed the engine and now it won't start. 他过度修理了引擎，现在它发动不了了。

例句：The therapist warned against overfixing the child's speech impediment. 治疗师警告不要过度矫正孩子的言语障碍。|近义词：overrepair, overdo, tamper (with)|反义词：underfix, neglect|词性:动词
