antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
n.|古物研究者，收藏家；古董商|A person who studies or collects antiquities.|He was a renowned antiquarian with a vast collection of Roman artifacts. 他是一位著名的古董收藏家，收藏了大量的罗马文物。|
近义词: archaeologist, collector, connoisseur
反义词: modernist
词性: noun

antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
adj.|古董的，古风的|Relating to or dealing in antiques.|She had an antiquarian interest in old books and manuscripts. 她对古籍和手稿有古董方面的兴趣。|
近义词: ancient, antique, archaic
反义词: modern, contemporary
词性: adjective
