besiege: /bɪˈsiːdʒ/| vt.|围攻，包围；困扰|The army besieged the city for months.  军队围攻了这座城市数月。|例句：The city was besieged by enemy forces. 这座城市被敌军围困。|近义词：surround, beleaguer, beset, harass|反义词：abandon, desert|verb

besiege: /bɪˈsiːdʒ/| vt.|（用请求、问题等）包围，困扰|The celebrity was besieged by reporters after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，这位名人被记者围攻。|例句：She was besieged with requests for help. 她被各种求助的请求所困扰。|近义词：overwhelm, inundate, bombard|反义词：ignore, neglect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French besieger, from be- "thoroughly" + siege "seat, siege."
