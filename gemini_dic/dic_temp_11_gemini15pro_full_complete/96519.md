indoctrinating:/ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪtɪŋ/ |v. (present participle)|灌输；教导；向…反复灌输|The process of teaching someone to fully accept a set of beliefs without questioning them.|They are indoctrinating their children with their religious beliefs. 他们正在向他们的孩子灌输他们的宗教信仰。|Synonyms: brainwashing, propagandizing, inculcating|Antonyms: enlightening, educating, liberating|Verb
