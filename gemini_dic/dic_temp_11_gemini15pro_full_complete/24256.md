briefless:/ˈbriːflɪs/
adjective
无案可辩的；没有客户的；失业的
Without clients or briefs; unemployed (especially of a barrister or lawyer).
He was a briefless barrister, struggling to make ends meet. 他是一位没有客户的律师，难以维持生计。
Synonyms: unemployed, jobless, out of work, idle, inactive
Antonyms: employed, busy, occupied
词性: adjective
