cheerers:/ˈtʃɪərərz/
n.
欢呼者；喝彩者；加油者
People who cheer, expressing encouragement, approval, or enthusiasm.
The cheerers in the stadium roared as the team scored.  体育场里的欢呼者在球队得分时大声喝彩。
supporters, fans, rooters
critics, detractors
noun
