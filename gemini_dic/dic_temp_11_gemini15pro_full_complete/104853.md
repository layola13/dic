keelrake:/ˈkiːlˌreɪk/
verb
彻底搜查；严厉批评；（船）翻覆
to search thoroughly; to criticize severely; (of a ship) to capsize
The police keelraked the house for evidence. 警察彻底搜查了这所房子寻找证据。
The coach keelraked the team after their poor performance. 教练在球队表现不佳后严厉批评了他们。
The storm keelraked the small sailboat.  暴风雨使这艘小帆船翻覆了。
近义词: ransack, scour, lambaste, castigate, overturn, capsize
反义词: praise, commend, right
词性: verb
