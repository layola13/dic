middle-tones:/ˈmɪdəl toʊnz/ | n. | 中音；中间色调 |  The range of frequencies or colors between the highest and lowest extremes, often considered the most important for conveying information or creating a balanced effect. | The photograph was beautifully composed with a range of subtle middle-tones. 这张照片构图精美，有一系列微妙的中间色调。| synonyms: mid-tones, mid-range, mid-frequencies | antonyms: highlights, shadows, lowlights | noun

middle-tone:/ˈmɪdəl toʊn/ | adj. | 中音的；中间色调的 |  Having a frequency or color value between the extremes. |  The artist used a middle-tone gray to create a sense of depth. 艺术家使用中灰色来营造一种深度感。| synonyms: mid-tone, mid-range | antonyms: high-pitched, low-pitched, bright, dark | adjective
