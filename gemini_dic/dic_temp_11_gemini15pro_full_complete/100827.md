introduction:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 介绍；引进；引言；序言 |  the act of making something known or starting something for the first time. |  The introduction of new technologies has changed the way we work. 新技术的引进改变了我们的工作方式。| synonyms: preface, foreword, preamble, prologue, opening, beginning | antonyms: conclusion, ending, epilogue | noun

introduction:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 入门；初阶 | a basic explanation or course about a subject. |  He attended an introduction to computer programming. 他参加了一个计算机编程入门课程。| synonyms: primer, basics, fundamentals | antonyms: advanced course, in-depth study | noun

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin introductio(n-), from the verb introducere (see introduce).
