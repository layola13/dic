makings:/ˈmeɪkɪŋz/
n.
素质，潜质；材料
the essential qualities or components needed for something to be successful or have the potential for success; the constituent elements of something.
He has the makings of a great leader. 他具备成为伟大领袖的潜质。
The ingredients have the makings of a delicious cake. 这些材料可以做成一个美味的蛋糕。
potential, qualities, attributes
flaws, deficiencies
noun
