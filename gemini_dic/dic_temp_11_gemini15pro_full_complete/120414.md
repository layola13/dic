minisummit:/ˈmɪniˌsʌmɪt/
noun
小型峰会；小型首脑会议
A small summit, often involving fewer participants or a more specific focus than a full summit.
The two leaders held a minisummit to discuss the trade agreement. 两位领导人举行了一次小型峰会，讨论贸易协定。
synonyms: small summit, meeting, conference
antonyms: large summit, major conference
n.
