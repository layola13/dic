chocoreto:/tʃɒkəˈrɛtoʊ/ | n. | 巧克力 |  A Japanese pronunciation of "chocolate," borrowed from the English word. It refers to the sweet treat made from cacao beans. |  Example:  Watashi wa chocoreto ga daisuki desu. 我非常喜欢巧克力。| Example: Kono chocoreto wa totemo oishii desu.  这款巧克力非常好吃。 | Synonyms: (In English) chocolate, cocoa | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
