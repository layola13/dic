fatwa:/ˈfætwɑː/| n. |宗教法令，裁决|A legal opinion or decree handed down by an Islamic religious leader or scholar.|The grand mufti issued a fatwa against smoking. 大穆夫提发布了一项禁止吸烟的宗教法令。|近义词：decree, ruling, edict|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "leniency," "tolerance," or the allowing of something previously forbidden) |名词
