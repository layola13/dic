kitbash:/ˈkɪtbæʃ/| n. v.|套件拼装，模型改造|The act of taking parts from commercial model kits (usually plastic model kits) and combining them to create a new model, or modifying an existing one.  It can also refer to the resulting model itself.|He kitbashed a spaceship model using parts from a tank and a plane kit. 他用坦克和飞机套件的零件拼装了一个宇宙飞船模型。|近义词：customize, modify, adapt|反义词:build from scratch|词性:名词, 动词
