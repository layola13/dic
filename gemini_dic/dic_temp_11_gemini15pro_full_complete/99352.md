interdental:/ˌɪntərˈdɛnt(ə)l/| adj.|齿间的，舌齿音的|Situated between the teeth; pronounced by placing the tip of the tongue between the upper and lower teeth.|Example 1:  The "th" sounds in English are interdental.  英语中的"th"音是齿间的。

Example 2:  He had difficulty pronouncing the interdental fricatives. 他发齿间擦音有困难。|近义词:dental|反义词: (No direct antonym, but possibly) labial, velar|词性:adjective
