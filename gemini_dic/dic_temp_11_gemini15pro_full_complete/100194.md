inter:/ɪnˈtɜːr/ (BrE) /ɪnˈtər/ (AmE)| v. |埋葬，安葬|To bury (a corpse).|例句：They interred the body in the family plot. 他们在家族墓地里安葬了遗体。

例句：The king was interred with great ceremony. 国王被隆重安葬了。

近义词：bury, entomb, inhume|反义词：exhume, disinter|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Old French *enterrer*, from Late Latin *interrare*, from Latin *in* (in, into) + *terra* (earth).
