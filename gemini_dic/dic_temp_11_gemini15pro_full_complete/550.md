aborsive:/əˈbɔːrsɪv/ | adj. | 流产的，堕胎的；引起流产的 | Causing or tending to cause abortion. |  This medication is known to be abortive in early pregnancy. 这种药物在怀孕早期是众所周知的堕胎药。

The abortive coup attempt led to many arrests.  这次失败的政变导致许多人被捕。| 近义词：miscarried, unsuccessful, failed | 反义词: successful, fruitful, productive | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *abortivus, from Latin abortus (“miscarriage, abortion”), past participle of aboriri (“to miscarry”).
