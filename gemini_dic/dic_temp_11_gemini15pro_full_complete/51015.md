deskilled:/diːˈskɪld/
| adj. |去技能化的；技能降低的|Reduced in skills, especially by the introduction of technology that simplifies tasks and reduces the need for specialized training. |Many factory workers feel deskilled by the automated production line. 许多工厂工人觉得自动化生产线降低了他们的技能。| The introduction of new machinery has deskilled many traditional crafts. 新机器的引进降低了许多传统工艺的技能要求。|近义词：unskilled, semi-skilled, downgraded|反义词：skilled, upskilled|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + skilled.  The term gained prominence in the late 20th century with discussions about the impact of automation on the workforce.
