non-arbitrary:/ˌnɒn ˈɑːbɪtrəri/ | adj. | 非任意的，非武断的 | Not random or based on personal whim; based on reason, logic, or evidence. |  The judge's decision was non-arbitrary, based on precedent and legal statutes. 法官的判决并非武断，而是基于先例和法律规定的。 | reasoned, logical, objective, principled, rational | arbitrary, random, capricious, whimsical | adjective
