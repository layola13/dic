observants:/əbˈzɜːrvənts/ | n. | 观察者；奉行者；遵守者 | People who pay close attention to details and follow rules or customs.  | The anthropologists were keen observers of the local customs.  这些人类学家是当地风俗的敏锐观察者。| synonyms: watchers, onlookers, followers, adherents | antonyms:  violators, nonconformists | noun

observant:/əbˈzɜːrvənt/ | adj. | 善于观察的；机警的；严格遵守的 | Quick to notice things; attentive; careful in observing a law, custom, or ritual. | She is an observant child and noticed the subtle changes in her mother's mood. 她是一个善于观察的孩子，注意到了母亲情绪的微妙变化。| synonyms: perceptive, attentive, watchful, mindful, heedful | antonyms: inattentive, unobservant, oblivious, heedless | adjective
