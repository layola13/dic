benthal:/ˈbenθəl/| adj.|深海底的，深海区的|Of, relating to, or occurring at the bottom of a body of water. |例句：Benthal organisms thrive in the deep ocean.  深海生物在深海中繁衍生息。

例句：The benthal zone is characterized by low light and high pressure. 深海区以光线弱、压力高为特征。|近义词：abyssal, deep-sea, submarine|反义词：pelagic, littoral|词性:形容词
benthos:/ˈbenθɒs/| n.|海底生物，底栖生物|The flora and fauna found on the bottom, or in the bottom sediments, of a sea, lake, or other body of water. |例句：The benthos of this lake is diverse and includes many species of invertebrates. 这个湖的底栖生物种类繁多，包括许多种无脊椎动物。

例句：Studying the benthos can provide valuable information about the health of the ecosystem. 研究底栖生物可以提供有关生态系统健康状况的宝贵信息。|近义词：bottom-dwelling organisms|反义词：plankton, nekton|词性:名词

词源: From Greek *benthos* meaning "depth of the sea."
