dubiousnesses:/ˈduːbiəsnəsɪz/
n.
疑惑；怀疑；不确定
The dubiousnesses surrounding the project made investors hesitant.  这个项目周围的种种不确定性使投资者犹豫不决。
例句：His dubiousnesses about the plan were well-founded. 他对该计划的怀疑是有根据的。

例句：The dubiousnesses surrounding her story made it hard to believe. 她故事周围的疑点使人难以相信。

近义词：doubts, uncertainties, misgivings, reservations, suspicions
反义词：certainties, assurances, convictions

词性: 名词 (Plural of dubiousness)

词源:  From dubious + -ness.  Dubious comes from Latin dubius "doubtful," from duo "two" (as in two minds, uncertain which way to go).
