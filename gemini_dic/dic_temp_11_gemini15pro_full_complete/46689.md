dams:/dæmz/| n.| 水坝；堤坝；障碍物| A barrier constructed to hold back water and raise its level, forming a reservoir used to generate electricity or as a water supply.|  The Hoover Dam is a marvel of engineering. 胡佛水坝是工程学的奇迹。|
The dams prevent flooding in the rainy season. 这些水坝防止了雨季的洪水。| synonyms: barriers, embankments, dikes, levees | antonyms: breaches, openings | noun
