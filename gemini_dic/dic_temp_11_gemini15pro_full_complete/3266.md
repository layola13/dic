affirmation:/ˌæfərˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 肯定；断言；主张；批准 |  The act of affirming or asserting something; a positive statement or declaration. |  Positive affirmations can improve your self-esteem.  积极的肯定可以提高你的自尊。 | synonyms: assertion, declaration, confirmation, avowal | antonyms: denial, negation, rejection, repudiation | noun

affirmation:/ˌæfərˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | (宗教、法律的) 证词；宣誓 | A solemn declaration made under penalty of perjury in lieu of an oath. | He made an affirmation of his belief in God. 他宣誓信仰上帝。| synonyms: attestation, testimony, avowal | antonyms: denial, negation, rejection | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affirmationem (nominative affirmatio), noun of action from past-participle stem of affirmare "to strengthen, confirm, declare," from ad "to" (see ad-) + firmare "to make firm, strengthen," from firmus "firm, strong" (see firm (adj.)).
