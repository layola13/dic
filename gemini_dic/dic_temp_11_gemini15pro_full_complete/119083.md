metrical:/ˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
格律的，韵律的
Relating to or composed in meter; measured.  Concerned with meter or versification.
The poem has a very regular metrical pattern. 这首诗的格律模式非常规整。
近义词: rhythmic, poetic
反义词: nonmetrical, prose
词性: adjective
