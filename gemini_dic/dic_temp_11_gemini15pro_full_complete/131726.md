nonconfidentiality:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnfɪˌdenʃiˈælɪti/
n.
缺乏保密性；不保密
lack of confidentiality; the state of not being confidential
Due to the nonconfidentiality of the information, it was leaked to the press. 由于信息的缺乏保密性，它被泄露给了媒体。
近义词: openness, disclosure, publicity
反义词: confidentiality, secrecy, privacy
