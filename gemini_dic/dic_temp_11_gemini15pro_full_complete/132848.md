noneffeteness:/ˌnɒnɪˈfɛktɪvnɪs/
n.
非柔弱；强健；活力
The state or quality of not being effete; vigor.
His noneffeteness was evident in his tireless work ethic and robust physique. 他的强健体魄和不知疲倦的职业道德显而易见。
The athlete's noneffeteness allowed him to compete at a high level for many years.  这位运动员的强健体魄使他能够多年来在高水平比赛中竞争。
synonyms: vigor, robustness, strength, hardiness, resilience
antonyms: effeteness, weakness, feebleness, fragility, debility
