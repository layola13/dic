aril:/ˈærɪl/| n.|假种皮|An extra seed covering, typically fleshy and brightly colored, found on some seeds, such as those of the yew and nutmeg.  It attracts animals which aid in seed dispersal.|The yew's red aril is the only part of the tree that isn't poisonous.| 近义词:seed covering|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
