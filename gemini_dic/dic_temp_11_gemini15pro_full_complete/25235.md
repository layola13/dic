budokan:/buːˈdoʊkɑn/| n.|武道馆|A large indoor arena in Tokyo, Japan, used for martial arts and other events.  It is often used as a venue for concerts.  It derives its name from the Japanese terms  "budō" (武道) meaning "martial arts" and "kan" (館) meaning "hall" or "building".|The Beatles played at the Budokan in 1966.  披头士乐队于1966年在武道馆演出。|Synonyms: arena, stadium, venue|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps: open-air venue)|noun
