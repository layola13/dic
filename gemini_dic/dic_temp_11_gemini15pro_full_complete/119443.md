microlite: /ˈmaɪkrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 微晶;微晶岩 | A minute crystal, usually of microscopic size, often occurring in glassy rocks. | The geologist identified microlites within the volcanic rock sample.  地质学家在火山岩样本中发现了微晶。| The rock is composed primarily of microlites. 这块岩石主要由微晶组成。 | synonym: microcrystal, crystallite | antonym: macrocrystal, megacryst | noun

Etymology:  From micro- + -lite, from Greek *lithos*, "stone."
