linearize:/ˈlɪniəraɪz/| v. |线性化，使线性化|To make something linear; to convert something to a linear form or to approximate something by a linear function.  |The engineer linearized the equation to simplify the calculations. 工程师将方程式线性化以简化计算。

The model linearizes the complex relationship between variables.  该模型将变量之间复杂的关线性化。|近义词：straighten, simplify |反义词: complicate, curve |词性:动词

Etymology: From linear + -ize.  "Linear" comes from Late Latin *linearis "belonging to a line," from Latin linea "line, thread, string."  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs from adjectives or nouns, meaning "to make or become (like) the thing specified."
