nonrehabilitative:/ˌnɒnˌriːhəˈbɪlɪtətɪv/
adjective
非康复性的；非改造性的
Not intended to rehabilitate; not contributing to rehabilitation.
The prison focused on punishment rather than nonrehabilitative measures.  这个监狱专注于惩罚而不是非改造措施。
The judge criticized the nonrehabilitative nature of the sentence.  法官批评了判决的非康复性质。
近义词:punitive, retributive
反义词:rehabilitative, restorative
词性:adjective
