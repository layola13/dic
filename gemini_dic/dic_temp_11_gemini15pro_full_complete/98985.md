inter-talking:/ˌɪntərˈtɔːkɪŋ/
| adj. |互相交谈的，穿插交谈的|Characterized by talking amongst each other, interrupting, or overlapping speech.|The meeting was plagued by inter-talking, making it difficult to follow the discussion. 会议中充满了互相交谈的声音，很难跟上讨论。| The inter-talking children made it hard for the teacher to be heard.  互相交谈的孩子们让老师很难被听到。 | 近义词: overlapping, interrupting, crosstalking | 反义词: orderly, sequential | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"talking": present participle of the verb "talk," from Middle English *talken*, of uncertain origin.
