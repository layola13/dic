eeliest:/ˈiːliɪst/
adjective
最令人毛骨悚然的
most eerie; strangest and most mysterious
The eeliest thing was the silence after the scream. 最令人毛骨悚然的事情是尖叫声过后的寂静。
近义词:creepiest, spookiest, most unsettling
反义词: most comforting, most reassuring
词性:形容词

词源:
From eerie + -est.  Eerie itself has uncertain origins, possibly from Old English *earg, meaning "cowardly."  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending.
