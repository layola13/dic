consecrater:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪtə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɑːnsɪkreɪtər/)| n.|祝圣者，圣化者|One who consecrates someone or something, especially a bishop who performs the ceremony of consecration.  A person who dedicates something to a sacred purpose.|The Archbishop was the consecrater at the ordination ceremony.  大主教是授任仪式的祝圣者。|近义词：ordainer, dedicator, sanctifier|反义词: desecrator, profaner|noun


consecrate:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ (US: /ˈkɑːnsɪkreɪt/)| vt.|祝圣，奉献，使神圣|To make or declare something sacred; to dedicate formally to a religious or divine purpose. To devote something to a special purpose.|The church was consecrated in 1850. 这座教堂于1850年祝圣。|近义词：hallow, sanctify, dedicate, devote|反义词: desecrate, profane|verb
