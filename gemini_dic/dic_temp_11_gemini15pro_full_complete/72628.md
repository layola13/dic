flyleaf:/ˈflaɪliːf/| n. |扉页；空白页|A blank page at the beginning or end of a book.|She wrote a dedication on the flyleaf of the book. 她在书的扉页上写了一段题词。|Synonyms: endpaper, blank leaf|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "printed page")|noun

flyleaf:/ˈflaɪliːf/| n. |衬页；衬纸|A piece of paper used to reinforce the first and last pages of a book.|The flyleaf was torn, exposing the stitching. 衬页被撕破了，露出了缝线。|Synonyms: endpaper, pastedown|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
