ledgment: /ˈlɛdʒmənt/
n.
ledge
壁架；突出物
A ledgment is a narrow horizontal surface projecting from a wall, cliff, or other vertical structure.
The climber rested on a small ledgment before continuing his ascent. 登山者在继续攀登之前在一个小壁架上休息了一下。
Synonyms: shelf, ledge, projection
Antonyms: recess, indentation
