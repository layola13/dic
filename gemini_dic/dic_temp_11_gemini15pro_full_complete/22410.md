booing:/ˈbuːɪŋ/| n.|嘘声，喝倒彩|The booing from the crowd was deafening.  观众的嘘声震耳欲聋。|例句：The comedian received a chorus of booing after his offensive joke.  那位喜剧演员讲了个冒犯性的笑话后，招来一片嘘声。

|近义词：jeering, hissing, catcalling|反义词：cheering, applause|词性:noun

boo:/buː/| v.|发出嘘声，喝倒彩|The audience booed the politician off the stage. 观众把政客嘘下了台。|例句：They booed him when he missed the penalty kick. 当他罚失点球时，他们对他发出了嘘声。|近义词：jeer, hiss, catcall|反义词：cheer, applaud|词性:verb
