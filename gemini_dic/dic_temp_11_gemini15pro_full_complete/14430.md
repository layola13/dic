autunite:/ɔːˈtjuːˌnaɪt/ | n. | 钙铀云母 | A bright-yellow-green, fluorescent mineral consisting of hydrated calcium uranyl phosphate, occurring in granite pegmatites and as a secondary mineral in oxidized uranium deposits. | Autunite is a radioactive mineral and should be handled with care.  钙铀云母是一种放射性矿物，应小心处理。 | Autunite specimens can be beautiful, but their radioactivity makes them unsuitable for casual handling. 钙铀云母标本可能很漂亮，但它们的放射性使其不适合随意处理。 |  uraninite, torbernite | - | noun
