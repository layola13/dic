immiscibility:/ˌɪmɪsɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可混溶性
The inability of two liquids to mix together to form a homogeneous solution.
Oil and water exhibit immiscibility. 油和水表现出不可混溶性。
The immiscibility of the two chemicals made them difficult to combine.  这两种化学物质的不可混溶性使得它们难以结合。
近义词：incompatibility, non-miscibility
反义词：miscibility, compatibility
词性：noun

Etymology:
From im- (not) + miscible (capable of being mixed) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Miscible comes from Latin miscere (to mix).
