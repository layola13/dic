overloyalties:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔɪəltiz/ | n. | 过度的忠诚，过分的忠心 | Excessive loyalty, often to the detriment of other loyalties or responsibilities. |  He felt torn between his overloyalties to his family and his duty to his country. 他感到在对家庭的过分忠诚和对国家的责任之间左右为难。 | 近义词: excessive loyalty, misplaced loyalty | 反义词: disloyalty, betrayal | 词性: noun

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "loyalty" (noun).  "Loyalty" comes from Old French "loialte," from "loial," meaning "loyal," ultimately from Latin "legalis," meaning "lawful."
