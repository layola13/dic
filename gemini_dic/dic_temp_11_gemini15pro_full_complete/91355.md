hydroquinone:/ˌhaɪdrəˈkwɪnoʊn/ | n. | 对苯二酚，氢醌 | a crystalline benzene derivative, C6H4(OH)2, used as a photographic developer and antioxidant, and in medicine as a topical application in some skin conditions.  | Hydroquinone is used in skin-lightening creams. 氢醌用于皮肤美白霜。|  Hydroquinone is a powerful reducing agent. 氢醌是一种强还原剂。 | Synonyms:  1,4-benzenediol, quinol | Antonyms: oxidizing agent | noun
