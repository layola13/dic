inspectability:/ɪnˌspɛktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可检查性，可检验性 | The quality of being easily inspected or examined.  The ease with which something can be inspected. |  The software's modular design improved its inspectability, making it easier to find and fix bugs.  该软件的模块化设计提高了它的可检查性，使查找和修复错误更容易。 | inspectability, checkability, examinability | non-inspectability, unexaminability | noun
