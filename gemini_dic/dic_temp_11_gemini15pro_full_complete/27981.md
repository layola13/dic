canvasback:/ˈkænvəsbæk/| n.| 帆布背潜鸭 |A North American diving duck (Aythya valisineria) having a reddish-brown head and a grayish-white back.| We saw a flock of canvasbacks on the lake. 我们在湖上看到了一群帆布背潜鸭。|近义词：duck, pochard |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From canvas + back, referring to the bird's back, which resembles coarse canvas.
