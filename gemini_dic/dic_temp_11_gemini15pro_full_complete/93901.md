immortalizes: /ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使永生，使不朽
makes something or someone famous or remembered forever
His bravery was immortalized in a song. 他的英勇事迹在一首歌中被传颂。
immortalise (British spelling)
verb
commemorates, eternalizes, remembers
verb
forgets, ignores
