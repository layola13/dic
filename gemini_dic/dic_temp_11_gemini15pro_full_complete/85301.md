harnesser:/ˈhɑːrnɪsər/ | n. | 利用者；治理者；驾驭者 |  A person or thing that harnesses something (especially natural resources or social or political forces). |  They were early harnessers of wind power. 他们是早期利用风能的人。 | near synonyms: utilizer, exploiter, controller | antonyms: waster, squanderer | noun
