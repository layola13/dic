antiphysical:/ˌæntiˈfɪzɪkəl/
adj.
反对物质的；非物质的；精神上的；违反自然规律的
Opposed to or rejecting the physical or material; spiritual; contrary to the laws of nature.
His antiphysical beliefs led him to reject modern medicine. 他反对物质的信仰使他拒绝现代医学。
Spiritual experiences are often described as antiphysical. 精神体验通常被描述为非物质的。
synonym: spiritual, immaterial, metaphysical, transcendental
antonym: physical, material, corporeal
词性: 形容词
