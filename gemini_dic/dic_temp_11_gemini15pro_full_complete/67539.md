extrajudicially: /ˌɛkstrədʒuˈdɪʃəli/
extrajudicially: adv.
extrajudicially: 在法庭外地，非经司法程序地
extrajudicially: Outside of court; without judicial proceedings.
extrajudicially: The matter was settled extrajudicially, saving both parties the time and expense of a trial.  这事情在庭外和解了，为双方节省了时间和诉讼费用。
extrajudicially: The dispute was resolved extrajudicially through mediation. 该纠纷通过调解在庭外得到解决。
extrajudicially: 近义词: out of court, informally, privately
extrajudicially: 反义词: judicially, legally
extrajudicially: 词性: 副词
