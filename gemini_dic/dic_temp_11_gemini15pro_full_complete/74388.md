frameo:/ˈfreɪmioʊ/
noun
数码相框;智能相框
A digital photo frame that allows users to share photos and videos remotely.
My grandma loves her Frameo, it's so easy for the family to send her pictures. 我的奶奶很喜欢她的 Frameo 相框，家人可以很轻松地把照片发送给她。
近义词:digital photo frame, smart photo frame, electronic photo frame
反义词:traditional photo frame, printed photo

词性:n.
