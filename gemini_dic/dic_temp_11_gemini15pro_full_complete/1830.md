acrobat:/ˈækrəˌbæt/ | n. | 杂技演员；立场观点善变的人 | a skilled performer of gymnastic feats, typically one who works in a circus.  A person who holds or appears to hold two or more conflicting beliefs, viewpoints, or allegiances. | The acrobats performed amazing feats of strength and balance. 杂技演员表演了惊人的力量和平衡技艺。 | The politician was accused of being an acrobat, constantly shifting his positions on key issues.  这位政治家被指责像个杂技演员，在关键问题上不断改变立场。 | synonyms: gymnast, tumbler, aerialist, contortionist | antonyms: clumsy person, couch potato | noun
