abstruseness:/əbˈstruːsnəs/
noun
深奥，难懂
The quality of being difficult to understand; obscurity.
The abstruseness of the philosophical treatise made it inaccessible to most readers. 这篇哲学论文的深奥性使得大多数读者难以理解。
近义词：complexity, difficulty, obscurity, profundity, incomprehensibility
反义词：clarity, simplicity, perspicuity, intelligibility
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Late Latin abstrusus "hidden, concealed," past participle of abstrahere "to draw away, hide," from ab- "away" + trahere "to draw."  The ending "-ness" forms nouns of state or quality.
