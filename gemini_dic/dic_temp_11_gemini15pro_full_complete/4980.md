alkalize: /ˈælkəlaɪz/ | v. |碱化，使成碱性 | To make something alkaline, or more alkaline.  |  We need to alkalize the soil for these plants to thrive. 我们需要碱化土壤以使这些植物茁壮成长。 | The gardener alkalized the soil with lime. 园丁用石灰碱化了土壤。 |  neutralize, raise the pH | acidify, lower the pH | verb

Etymology: From alkali + -ize.  Alkali comes from Arabic *al-qaly* "ashes of the saltwort plant".
