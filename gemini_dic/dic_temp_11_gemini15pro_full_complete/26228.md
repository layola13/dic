butteriness:/ˈbʌtərɪnɪs/
n.
油腻感；奶油味；黄油味
The quality of being buttery, resembling or containing butter.
The butteriness of the croissant was exquisite.  羊角面包的奶油味真是太棒了。
richness, creaminess, fattiness
leanness, dryness
noun
