lidocaine:/ˈlaɪdəkeɪn/ | n. |利多卡因|A local anesthetic (numbing medication). It works by blocking nerve signals in your body. It can also be used to treat irregular heartbeats.| The dentist used lidocaine before filling the cavity. 牙医在补牙之前使用了利多卡因。|  Local anesthetic was administered, 1% lidocaine with 1:100,000 epinephrine. 局部麻醉剂是1%利多卡因和1:100,000肾上腺素。 |Synonyms: Xylocaine, lignocaine| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific medication, but could consider "stimulant" in a broad sense) | noun
