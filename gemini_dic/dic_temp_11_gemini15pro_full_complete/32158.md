chewinesses:/ˈtʃuːɪnɪsɪz/
noun
咀嚼性；耐嚼性；嚼劲
The quality of being chewy; the plural form of chewiness.
These gummies have different chewinesses. 这些软糖有不同的嚼劲。
The various chewinesses of the different brands of taffy were compared.  比较了不同品牌的太妃糖的各种嚼劲。
synonyms: textures, consistencies, firmness, elasticity
antonyms: tenderness, softness, smoothness
n.

Etymology:
From chewy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Chewy" originates from "chew" which comes from Old English "cēowan".
