discouragingly:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地；灰心地
in a way that makes you feel less confident, enthusiastic, and positive about something, or less willing to do something
The team played discouragingly in the first half.  该队在上半场表现令人沮丧。
Synonyms: dishearteningly, depressingly, disappointingly
Antonyms: encouragingly, inspiringly, hopefully
词性: 副词
