noncomprehension:/ˌnɑːnkɑːmprɪˈhɛnʃən/ | n. | 不理解，不领悟 | Lack of understanding; failure to grasp the meaning of something. |  Her noncomprehension of the situation was evident. 她显然不理解情况。

The student's noncomprehension of the complex mathematical formula led to a poor grade on the exam.  这个学生对复杂的数学公式不理解，导致考试成绩很差。| 近义词: incomprehension, lack of understanding, bewilderment, confusion | 反义词: comprehension, understanding, grasp, apprehension | 词性: 名词
