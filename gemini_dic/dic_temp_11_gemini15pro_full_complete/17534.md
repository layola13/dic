beatboxing:/ˈbiːtˌbɒksɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbiːtˌbɒksɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|节奏口技；口技表演|The art of mimicking drum machines, other musical instruments, and turntablism using one's mouth, lips, tongue, and voice.|He showed off his amazing beatboxing skills at the party. 他在派对上展示了他惊人的口技技巧。|近义词：vocal percussion|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun

beatboxer:/ˈbiːtˌbɒksər/ (US) /ˈbiːtˌbɒksər/ (UK)| n.|节奏口技表演者；口技师|A person who performs beatboxing.|The beatboxer created a complex rhythm with his mouth.  这位口技师用他的嘴创造了一个复杂的节奏。|近义词：vocal percussionist|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
