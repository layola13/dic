out-thunder:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndər/
verb
以更大的雷声盖过；胜过
to surpass in loudness of thunder; to outdo in noise or vehemence.
The rock band out-thundered the fireworks display.  这支摇滚乐队的雷鸣般的音乐盖过了烟火表演的声音。
The speaker out-thundered his opponent, drowning out his arguments. 演讲者用更大的声音盖过了对手，淹没了他的论点。
近义词: out-roar, drown out, overpower
反义词: be drowned out, be subdued
词性: verb
