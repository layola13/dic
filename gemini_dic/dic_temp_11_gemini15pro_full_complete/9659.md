antisyndicalist:/ˌæntiˌsɪndɪkəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 反工团主义者；反工会主义的 | Someone who is opposed to syndicalism.  A person who believes that trade unions are harmful or undesirable. |  The factory owner was a staunch antisyndicalist and refused to negotiate with the workers. 工厂老板是一个坚定的反工团主义者，拒绝与工人谈判。|  The government adopted antisyndicalist policies to suppress labor movements. 政府采取了反工会主义的政策来压制劳工运动。 | synonyms: anti-unionist, anti-labor | antonyms: unionist, labor activist, pro-labor | noun, adjective
