ickinesses:/ˈɪkɪnɪsɪz/
n.
令人作呕的东西；黏糊糊的东西
the quality or state of being icky; unpleasant stickiness.
The ickinesses of the melted ice cream made me gag. 融化的冰淇淋的黏糊糊让我感到恶心。
近义词：stickiness, sliminess, gooiness, unpleasantness
反义词：cleanliness, dryness, pleasantness
词性：名词


词源：
From icky + -ness + -es.  "Icky" itself likely originated as a childish or informal term, possibly echoing words like "sticky" or "yucky."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality.  The "-es" makes the noun plural.
