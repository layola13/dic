medullary:/ˈmɛdəˌlɛri/ | adj. | 骨髓的；髓质的；延髓的 | Relating to, consisting of, or affecting the medulla of any organ or tissue. |  The medullary cavity of the bone contains marrow.  骨髓腔含有骨髓。

The medullary rays of the kidney extend from the cortex toward the sinus. 肾脏的髓放线从皮质延伸到肾窦。 | Synonyms: central, inner, core | Antonyms: cortical, peripheral, outer | adjective


medullary:/ˈmɛdəˌlɛri/ | n. | 髓质 | The inner part of an organ or tissue, specifically the medulla oblongata. | Damage to the medullary can cause respiratory and circulatory failure.  对延髓的损伤会导致呼吸和循环衰竭。 | Synonym: medulla | Antonym: cortex | noun
