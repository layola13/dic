nonnetworks:/nɒnˈnɛtwɜːks/ | n. | 非网络，非网络化系统 | Systems or entities that are not connected or organized into a network.  This term emphasizes the absence of interconnectedness and structured communication. |  Many rural communities still rely on nonnetworks for essential services. 许多农村社区仍然依赖非网络化的系统来获得基本服务。

The company's reliance on nonnetworks ultimately hindered its growth.  该公司对非网络化系统的依赖最终阻碍了其发展。 | 近义词: isolated systems, standalone systems, unconnected entities | 反义词: networks, interconnected systems | 词性: 名词
