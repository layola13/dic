clapboarded:/ˌklæpˈbɔːrdɪd/
adjective
用隔板覆盖的；用护墙板装修的
Covered with clapboards (overlapping horizontal wooden boards used as siding).
The old clapboarded house stood on a hill overlooking the town.  那栋古老的木板房矗立在可以俯瞰小镇的山丘上。
The newly clapboarded cottage looked charming. 新装上护墙板的小屋看起来很迷人。
Synonyms: weatherboarded, sided
Antonyms: unsided, exposed
