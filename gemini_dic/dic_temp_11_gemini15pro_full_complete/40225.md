contextualize:/kənˈtekstʃuəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 将…置于背景中; 将…联系起来 | To place (something, such as a word or an event) in context | The author contextualizes the novel by describing the political climate of the time. 作者通过描述当时的政治气候来 contextualize 这部小说。| contextualize the historical background of the event  将事件的历史背景联系起来 | synonyms: place in context, situate, relate, connect, explain, interpret | antonyms: decontextualize, isolate, abstract | verb

contextualize:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪz/ | v. | 置于语境中 | To place (a linguistic element, such as a word) in its linguistic context. |  It's important to contextualize new vocabulary words for language learners.  为语言学习者提供新词汇的语境是很重要的。| He contextualized his remarks by explaining the broader historical context. 他通过解释更广泛的历史背景来 contextualize 他的言论。 | synonyms: place in context, frame, situate | antonyms: decontextualize, isolate | verb
