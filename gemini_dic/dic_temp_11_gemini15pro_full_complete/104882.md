keep-out:/ˈkiːp aʊt/ | n. |禁区，禁止入内|An area or place where entry is forbidden. |There's a keep-out sign on the gate. 大门上有一个禁止入内的标志。|近义词: restricted area, off-limits, no entry|反义词: open access, unrestricted|词性: 名词

keep-out:/ˈkiːp aʊt/ | adj. |禁止入内的|Preventing entry; designed to exclude. |This is a keep-out zone for unauthorized personnel. 这是未经授权人员的禁区。|近义词: restricted, prohibited, forbidden|反义词: permitted, allowed, accessible|词性: 形容词


词源:  Compound formed from the verb "keep" and the adverb "out."  "Keep" originates from Old English *cēpan (to observe, attend to, care for), while "out" comes from Old English ūt (out, outside).
