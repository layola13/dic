halfnesses:/ˈhæf.nɪs.ɪz/
n.
半人；半身人；（尤指托尔金作品中的）霍比特人
The state or quality of being a halfling, a small humanoid creature, especially in the works of J.R.R. Tolkien.
The hobbits, or halflings, as they called themselves, lived quietly in the Shire. 霍比特人，或者他们自称的半身人，安静地住在夏尔。
The book delves into the halfnesses of the Shire and their simple lives.  这本书深入探讨了夏尔半人的生活和他们简朴的生活。

近义词:hobbits, halflings
反义词:giants
