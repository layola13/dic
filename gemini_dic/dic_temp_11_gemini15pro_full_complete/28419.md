carbonising:/ˈkɑːbənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 碳化，炭化 | The process of heating a substance in the absence of air to drive off volatile components and leave a residue of carbon. |  The wood was carbonised in a kiln to produce charcoal. 木材在窑中碳化以生产木炭。| Synonyms: charring, coking | Antonyms:  oxidizing, burning | verb
