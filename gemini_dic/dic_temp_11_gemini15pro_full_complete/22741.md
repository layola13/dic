boronic:/bɔːˈrɒnɪk/ |adj.|硼的，含硼的|Relating to or containing boron, especially in the +3 oxidation state.|Boronic acids are important reagents in organic synthesis. 硼酸是有机合成中的重要试剂。|近义词: boron-containing|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

boronic:/bɔːˈrɒnɪk/ |n.|硼酸|A class of organic compounds containing a boron atom bonded to two hydroxyl groups and an alkyl or aryl group.|Phenylboronic acid is a common example of a boronic acid. 苯硼酸是硼酸的常见例子。|近义词: organoboron|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
