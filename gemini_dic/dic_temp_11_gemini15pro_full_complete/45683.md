curtly:/ˈkɜːrtli/ (US) /ˈkɜːtli/ (UK)| adv.|唐突地，简略地，粗鲁地|In a short and rude way, often showing impatience.|She answered curtly and turned away. 她简短粗暴地回答后转身离开了。|近义词：abruptly, brusquely, rudely, shortly, tersely|反义词:politely, courteously, graciously|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *curtliche, from curt +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Latin curtus (“short”).
