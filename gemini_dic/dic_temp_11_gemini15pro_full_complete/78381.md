gesturers:/ˈdʒɛstʃərərz/
n.
做手势的人;打手势者
People who communicate or express themselves through gestures.
The gesturers on the stage conveyed the emotion of the story without saying a word.  舞台上的表演者不用说一句话就表达了故事的情感。
近义词:signers, motioners
反义词:speakers
