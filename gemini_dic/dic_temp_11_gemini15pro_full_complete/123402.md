monophonic:/ˌmɒnəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈfɑːnɪk/| adj.|单音的，单声道的|Consisting of a single melodic line, without harmony or counterpoint.  Characterized by a single channel of sound.|The old record player could only produce monophonic sound. 那台老式唱片机只能发出单声道的声音。|Synonyms: single-voiced, homophonic|Antonyms: polyphonic, stereophonic|adjective
