corgi: /ˈkɔːrɡi/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrɡi/ (US)| n. |柯基犬|A small, long-bodied, short-legged dog breed with pointed ears and a fox-like face, originating in Wales. It comes in two breeds: the Pembroke Welsh Corgi and the Cardigan Welsh Corgi.|例句：The Queen is famous for her love of corgis. 女王以爱柯基犬而闻名。

例句：My corgi loves to herd the children in the park. 我的柯基犬喜欢在公园里把孩子们赶到一起。|近义词：dog, canine|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific breed)|noun
