macrophysical:/ˌmækroʊˈfɪzɪkəl/ | adj. |宏观的，宏观物理的| Relating to large-scale physical properties and processes, as opposed to microscopic ones. |例句：Macrophysical properties like temperature and pressure are easier to measure than microscopic ones. 宏观物理性质，如温度和压力，比微观性质更容易测量。

例句：The macrophysical behavior of the system can be predicted using classical mechanics.  系统的宏观物理行为可以用经典力学来预测。| 近义词：macroscopic, large-scale | 反义词：microscopic, microphysical | 词性：形容词
