escambio:/esˈkambjo/ (Spanish), /ɪsˈkæmbioʊ/ (Anglicized)
n.|交换，交流，兑换|An act of exchanging one thing for another; trade, barter.  The exchange of goods and services. |We made an intercambio of language skills, I helped her with English and she helped me with Spanish. 我们交换了语言技能，我帮她学习英语，她帮我学习西班牙语。|synonyms: exchange, trade, barter, swap|antonyms: retention, keeping, hoarding|noun

escambio:/esˈkambjo/ (Spanish), /ɪsˈkæmbioʊ/ (Anglicized)
v.|交换，交流，兑换|To exchange one thing for another; to trade, to barter. To give and receive reciprocally.|They intercambiaron ideas about the project. 他们就这个项目交换了意见。|synonyms: exchange, trade, barter, swap|antonyms: retain, keep, hoard|verb

注意事项:

词源:  From Spanish *intercambio*, from Latin *intercambium*, from *inter-* (between) + *cambiare* (to change).

由于escambio是西班牙语词汇，在英语中使用时通常使用斜体或引号。  英语中更常用的对应词是"exchange".  上述例句中包含了在西班牙语语境下的例句和在英语语境下使用escambio的例句。

在英语语境下，使用动词形式"intercambio"比较少见，通常使用"exchange".
