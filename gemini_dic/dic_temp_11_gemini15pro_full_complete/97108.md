infidelities: /ˌɪnfɪˈdɛlətiz/
n.
不忠的行为，背叛；（尤指）婚外情
Acts of being unfaithful to a spouse or other sexual partner.
He confessed his infidelities to his wife. 他向妻子坦白了自己的不忠行为。

近义词: affairs, unfaithfulness, adultery, treachery, disloyalty
反义词: fidelity, faithfulness, loyalty
