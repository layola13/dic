nondiscursivenesses:/ˌnɑːndɪˈskɜːrsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
非散漫性；非离题性
The quality or state of being nondiscursive; conciseness, directness.  Lack of rambling or digression.
His nondiscursivenesses in the meeting were appreciated; he kept the discussion focused.  他在会议上的简洁性受到赞赏；他让讨论保持了重点。
近义词: conciseness, directness, brevity, succinctness
反义词: discursiveness, prolixity, verbosity, rambling
