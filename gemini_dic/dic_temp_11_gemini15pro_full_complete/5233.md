allmouth:/ˈɔːlmaʊθ/| adj.|夸夸其谈的，光说不做的|Someone who is all mouth talks a lot about doing things but never actually does them.  This word is often used in a disapproving way.|He's all mouth and no trousers – he keeps promising to fix the fence but never does.|近义词：boastful, bragging, talkative, empty talker|反义词：modest, humble, reserved, quiet|词性:adjective

词源:
This word is a compound word formed from "all" and "mouth."  It suggests someone who is all talk and no action, as if their mouth is the only active part of them.
