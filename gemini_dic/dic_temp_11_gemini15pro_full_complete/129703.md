non-drinker:/ˌnɒnˈdrɪŋkə(r)/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈdrɪŋkər/ (US)| n. |不饮酒的人；滴酒不沾的人|A person who does not drink alcoholic beverages. |例句：He's a non-drinker for religious reasons.  他因为宗教原因不喝酒。

|例句：The restaurant offered a good selection of non-alcoholic drinks for the non-drinkers. 这家餐厅为不喝酒的人提供了种类丰富的无酒精饮料。


|近义词：teetotaler, abstainer|反义词：drinker, alcoholic|词性:noun

non-drinker:/ˌnɒnˈdrɪŋkə(r)/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈdrɪŋkər/ (US)| adj. |不喝酒的|Used to describe someone who doesn't drink alcohol. |例句：She's a non-drinker, so she ordered a soda. 她不喝酒，所以点了一杯苏打水。

|例句：They hosted a non-drinker friendly party with plenty of juice and mocktails. 他们举办了一个对不喝酒的人友好的派对，提供了大量的果汁和无酒精鸡尾酒。

|近义词：teetotal, abstinent|反义词：drinking, alcoholic|词性:adjective
