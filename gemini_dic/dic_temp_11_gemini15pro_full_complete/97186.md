infixing:/ˈɪnfɪksɪŋ/| n.|中缀法, 插入|The process of inserting a morpheme (an affix) inside a word stem.  |Example:  "Fan-bloody-tastic" uses infixing.  "Bloody" is infixed into "fantastic."|近义词:tmesis (though not always synonymous)|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider prefixing or suffixing)|词性:noun
