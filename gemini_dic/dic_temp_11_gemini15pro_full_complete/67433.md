extinguishable:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
可熄灭的
able to be extinguished.
The fire was small and easily extinguishable. 这场火很小，很容易扑灭。
近义词:quenchable,smotherable
反义词: inextinguishable
词性: adjective
