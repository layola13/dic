lettuce:/ˈlɛtɪs/| n.|莴苣，生菜|A leafy green vegetable used in salads.|例句：I like lettuce in my sandwiches. 我喜欢在三明治里放生菜。|例句：Would you like some lettuce with your burger?  你的汉堡要加些生菜吗？|近义词：greens, salad greens|反义词:(none applicable)|词性:noun
Etymology->From Old French *laictuce, from Latin lactuca (“lettuce”), from lac (“milk”), referring to the milky sap.  The spelling with c was reintroduced in the 16th century, probably influenced by analogous words like practice.
