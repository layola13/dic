mandatorily: /ˈmændətɔːrɪli/ (US) /ˈmændətərɪli/ (UK)| adv.|强制地，必须地|In a mandatory manner; by requirement or by necessity.|Attendance at the meeting is mandatorily required for all staff. 所有员工都必须参加会议。|The new law mandatorily requires all drivers to wear seatbelts. 新法律强制要求所有司机系安全带。|近义词: obligatorily, compulsorily, necessarily|反义词: optionally, voluntarily|词性: 副词
词源: From Late Latin *mandatorius, from Latin mandatum (“mandate”).
