glutin:/ˈɡluːtɪn/| n.|谷蛋白，麸质|A protein composite found in wheat and related grains, especially barley and rye. Gluten gives elasticity to dough, helping it rise and keep its shape. |The bread rose beautifully because of the gluten in the flour.  面包发得很好，因为面粉里有麸质。|Synonym: glutenin|Antonym: None.|noun
