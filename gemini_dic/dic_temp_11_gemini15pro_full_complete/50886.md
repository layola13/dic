deserter:/dɪˈzɜːrtər/| n. |逃兵，放弃者|A person who leaves the armed forces without permission.|The soldier was labeled a deserter after leaving his post.|Synonyms: runaway, absconder, defector|Antonyms: loyalist, patriot|noun

deserter:/dɪˈzɜːrtər/| n. |放弃者，背弃者|A person who abandons a person, cause, or organization to which they owe allegiance or are bound.|He was considered a deserter by his former colleagues after switching companies.|Synonyms: renegade, traitor, quitter|Antonyms: supporter, adherent|noun
