intelligence: /ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. | 智力，情报，理解力 | The ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills. |  He possesses a high level of intelligence. 他拥有很高的智力。| synonyms: intellect, mind, brainpower, understanding, comprehension | antonyms: stupidity, ignorance | noun

intelligence: /ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. | 情报，信息 | Information concerning an enemy or competitor or a field of activity. |  The intelligence we gathered proved invaluable. 我们收集的情报证明是无价的。| synonyms: information, data, news, facts, reports | antonyms: misinformation, disinformation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "intelligence" (understanding, knowledge), from Latin "intelligentia" (understanding, power of discerning, knowledge), from "intellegens" (present participle of intellegere "to understand"), from "inter-" (between) + "legere" (to choose, gather, read).
