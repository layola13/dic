competitrix: /kəmˈpɛtɪtrɪks/
noun
女竞争者
A female competitor.
She saw her competitrix as a threat to her winning the championship. 她视她的女竞争对手为赢得冠军的威胁。
Mary was her main competitrix in the race. 玛丽是她在这场比赛中的主要竞争对手。
Synonyms: rival, opponent, adversary, contender
Antonyms: ally, teammate, partner
词性: 名词
