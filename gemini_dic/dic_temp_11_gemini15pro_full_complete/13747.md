auctionary:/ˈɔːkʃənəri/ | adj. | 拍卖的；关于拍卖的 | Of or relating to an auction. | The auctionary process can be quite exciting. 拍卖过程可能会非常刺激。 |  The auctionary firm handled the sale of the estate. 这家拍卖公司负责处理这处房产的出售。 | 近义词: auction, bidding | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑private sale 私人销售) | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *auctionarius, from auctio (“increase, auction”), from augere (“to increase”).
