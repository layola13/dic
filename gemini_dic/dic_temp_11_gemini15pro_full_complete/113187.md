lures:/lʊrz/ | n. | 诱惑物；诱饵；吸引力 | Something that tempts or attracts, especially with the promise of pleasure or reward. | The fisherman used brightly colored lures to attract fish. 渔夫用颜色鲜艳的诱饵来吸引鱼。| synonyms: bait, decoy, enticement, temptation | antonyms: deterrent, repellent | noun

lures:/lʊrz/ | v. | 引诱；诱惑；吸引 | To tempt or attract by offering something appealing. | The company lured customers with discounts and special offers.  这家公司用折扣和特价优惠来吸引顾客。| synonyms: entice, tempt, attract, seduce | antonyms: repel, deter, discourage | verb
