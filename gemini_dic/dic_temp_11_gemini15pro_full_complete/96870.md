inexhaustibly:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪbli/
adverb
取之不尽地，不知疲倦地
in an inexhaustible manner; tirelessly.  Derived from "inexhaustible" with the suffix "-ly".  The prefix "in-" negates "exhaustible," meaning "able to be used up," which comes from the Latin "exhaurire," meaning "to drain out."
He worked inexhaustibly on the project, driven by his passion.  他不知疲倦地致力于这个项目，受他的热情驱使。
The sun provides energy inexhaustibly to the earth. 太阳不断地为地球提供能量。

近义词:tirelessly, endlessly, incessantly, perpetually
反义词:wearily, intermittently, sporadically
词性:adverb
