antennas:/ænˈtɛnəz/| n. |天线|A metallic device (such as a rod or wire) for radiating or receiving radio waves.|The car's radio antenna was broken in the storm. 汽车的无线电天线在暴风雨中损坏了。|Synonyms: aerial, receiver, transmitter|Antonyms: N/A|noun

antennas:/ænˈtɛnəz/| n. |触角|Either of a pair of long, thin sensory appendages on the heads of insects, crustaceans, and some other arthropods.|The butterfly delicately moved its antennas. 蝴蝶轻轻地移动它的触角。|Synonyms: feelers, horns|Antonyms: N/A|noun
