americanizer:/əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzər/
|n.|美国化者，使美国化的人|A person or thing that Americanizes something or someone.|The americanizer sought to assimilate new immigrants into American culture.  这位美国化者试图让新移民融入美国文化。|近义词:assimilator, acculturator|反义词:traditionalist, nationalist|noun

americanize:/əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪz/
|v. (tr.)|美国化，使美国化|To make or become American in character; assimilate to the customs and institutions of the U.S.|Many immigrants were eager to americanize and embrace their new lives. 许多移民渴望美国化，拥抱他们的新生活。|They tried to americanize the school system. 他们试图将学校系统美国化。|近义词:assimilate, acculturate|反义词:nationalize, localize|verb
