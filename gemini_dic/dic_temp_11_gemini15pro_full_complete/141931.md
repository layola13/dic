ordinate:/ˈɔːrdɪnət/ |/ˈɔːrdɪnɪt/| n. |纵坐标；竖线 |The ordinate shows the value of the dependent variable. 纵坐标表示因变量的值。|例句：Plot the points on a graph, using the x-axis for time and the y-axis for the ordinate. 在图上绘制这些点，用 x 轴表示时间，用 y 轴表示纵坐标。

例句：The ordinate of the point (2,3) is 3. 点 (2,3) 的纵坐标是 3。|近义词：vertical coordinate, y-coordinate|反义词: abscissa, x-coordinate |noun


ordinate:/ˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ |/ˈɔːrdɪnɪt/| vt. |排列；规定；任命 |He was ordained as a priest last year. 他去年被任命为牧师。|例句：The king ordained that everyone should pay taxes. 国王下令每个人都应该纳税。

例句：She was ordained a minister in the Methodist church. 她在卫理公会教堂被任命为牧师。|近义词：appoint, order, decree|反义词: N/A |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ordinatus, past participle of ordinare "to set in order, arrange," from Latin ordo "row, rank, order."  The mathematical sense emerged in the 17th century.
