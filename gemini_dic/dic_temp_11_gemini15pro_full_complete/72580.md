fluxing:/ˈflʌksɪŋ/
n.|助熔, 熔化, 焊剂处理|The process of melting or fusing something, especially metal, often with the use of a flux.|The fluxing of the metal created a smooth, even surface. 金属的熔化形成了光滑、均匀的表面。|melting, fusing, soldering|solidifying, freezing|noun
fluxing:/ˈflʌksɪŋ/
v.|助熔, 熔化, 焊剂处理|The act of applying a flux to something, typically metal, to facilitate melting or joining.|He was fluxing the metal pipes together. 他正在用焊剂将金属管道焊接在一起。|melting, fusing, soldering|solidifying, freezing|verb
