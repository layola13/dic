gaugeable:/ˈɡeɪdʒəbl/
adjective
可测量的，可估计的
able to be gauged or measured.
The impact of the new policy is not easily gaugeable.  这项新政策的影响不容易评估。
The depth of the water was gaugeable with a sounding line.  水深可以用测深线测量。
measurable, assessable, estimable
immeasurable, incalculable, inestimable
adjective
