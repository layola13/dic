experimentals:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntlz/| n.|实验品；实验对象；实验性的事物|Things related to or used in experiments, especially those still in the testing phase. |The company is showcasing its new experimentals at the trade show.|近义词：prototypes, test subjects, pilots, trials|反义词:established products, proven methods|noun

Etymology: From "experimental" + "-s" (plural suffix). "Experimental" comes from Late Middle English "experimentall,"  from Medieval Latin "experimentalis,"  from Latin "experimentum"  meaning "a trial, proof, experiment," from "experiri" meaning "to try, test."
