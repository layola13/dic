atramental: /ˌætrəˈmɛntl/
adjective
墨水的；墨黑的；似墨的；忧郁的
Relating to, resembling, or consisting of ink; inky black.  Figuratively, it can also mean gloomy or melancholy.
The poet's atramental mood was reflected in his dark verses.  这位诗人忧郁的心情反映在他阴暗的诗句中。

The atramental fluid stained the ancient manuscript. 墨水弄脏了古老的手稿。

Synonym: inky, black, dark, somber, gloomy, melancholic
Antonym: bright, cheerful, colorful, light

词性: adjective
