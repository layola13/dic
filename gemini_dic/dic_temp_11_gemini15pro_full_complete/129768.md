non-extended:/ˌnɒn ɪkˈstendɪd/ | adj. | 非延伸的，未扩展的 | Not extended; not prolonged or lengthened.  | The non-extended version of the software has limited features.  非扩展版本的软件功能有限。 |  The meeting was non-extended and finished on time. 会议没有延长，按时结束。 | 近义词: short, brief, limited, unextended | 反义词: extended, prolonged, lengthened | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extended" (past participle of "extend," from Latin "extendere" - to stretch out).
