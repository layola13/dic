boorishly: /ˈbʊərɪʃli/ | adv. | 粗鲁地，粗野地 | In a rude and insensitive way, like a boor. | He boorishly interrupted the conversation, demanding to be heard. 他粗鲁地打断谈话，要求别人听他讲话。 | The drunken man boorishly shoved his way to the front of the line.  醉汉粗鲁地挤到队伍的最前面。| Synonyms: crudely, rudely, coarsely, uncivilly, loutishly | Antonyms: politely, courteously, graciously, respectfully | 词性: 副词
