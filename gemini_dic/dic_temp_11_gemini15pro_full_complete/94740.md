PHRASE->
improve on/upon something: 改进某事物
Her new book improves on her last one. 她的新书比上一本有所改进。

improve in something: 在某方面有所提高
He's been improving in his studies lately. 他最近学习有所进步。

room for improvement: 有改进的空间
There's still room for improvement in her writing. 她的写作还有改进的空间。

improve with age: 随着年龄增长而变得更好
This wine will improve with age. 这酒会越陈越好。

improve by leaps and bounds: 突飞猛进地改进
Her English has improved by leaps and bounds. 她的英语突飞猛进。

词源: From Old French *emprover (modern French emprunter), literally, "to put to profit," from en- "in" + prover "to prove, test, put to the test."  The sense shifted in French from “make a profit from, turn to one’s advantage” to “make better.”
