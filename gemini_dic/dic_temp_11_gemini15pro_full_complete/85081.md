hard-wearing:/ˈhɑːrdˈwɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 耐磨的，经穿的 | Able to withstand frequent use and resist damage or deterioration. | This carpet is very hard-wearing and will last for years. 这块地毯非常耐磨，可以用很多年。| These boots are hard-wearing and perfect for hiking.  这些靴子很耐穿，非常适合徒步旅行。| 近义词：durable, long-lasting, tough, resistant | 反义词：fragile, delicate, flimsy | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hard" + "wearing." "Hard" traces back to Old English "heard," meaning "firm, solid, resistant." "Wearing" comes from Old English "werian," meaning "to wear," related to the concept of enduring or lasting.  The combined term emphasizes resistance to the effects of wear and tear.
