greening:/ˈɡriːnɪŋ/| n. |绿化；环保；变得环保|The process of becoming green, environmentally friendly, or covered with vegetation.|例句：The greening of the desert is a long-term project. 沙漠绿化是一个长期项目。

例句：Urban greening can improve air quality. 城市绿化可以改善空气质量。|近义词：ecologization, environmentalization|反义词：browning, degradation|n.

greening:/ˈɡriːnɪŋ/| adj. |绿色的；环保的；变得环保的|Becoming green, environmentally friendly, or covered with vegetation.|例句：The company is adopting greening policies to reduce its carbon footprint. 公司正在采取环保政策以减少碳足迹。

例句：Greening initiatives are becoming increasingly popular. 环保倡议正变得越来越受欢迎。|近义词：ecological, environmental|反义词：polluting, harmful|adj.
