outmeasures:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərz/

| v. | 采取比...更有效的措施 | To take more effective measures than someone else. | The government outmeasured the rebels by cutting off their supply lines. 政府通过切断叛军的补给线，采取了比他们更有效的措施。 |
|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outperforms, outstrips|
|反义词：falls short of, underperforms, loses to|
