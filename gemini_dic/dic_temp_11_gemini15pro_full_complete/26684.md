cadaverousness:/kəˈdævərəsnəs/| n.| 尸体般的；憔悴的；苍白的|The state of looking like a corpse; extreme pallor and thinness, suggesting imminent death.  A ghastly appearance, as of a corpse.|His cadaverousness shocked his family, who hadn't seen him since he'd fallen ill.| 近义词：gauntness, emaciation, haggardness,  pallor|反义词: robustness, healthiness, vitality|词性:名词

cadaverous:/kəˈdævərəs/| adj.| 尸体般的；憔悴的；苍白的|Looking like a corpse; extremely pale and thin, suggesting imminent death. Resembling a corpse in being very pale, thin, or bony.|He had a cadaverous appearance after his long illness.| 近义词：gaunt, emaciated, haggard, pale|反义词: robust, healthy, vibrant|词性:形容词
