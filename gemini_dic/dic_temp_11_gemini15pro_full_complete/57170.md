doweled:/ˈdaʊəld/| adj.|销钉连接的；用木钉固定的|Joined or fastened with dowels.  A dowel is a small cylindrical rod, usually made of wood, plastic, or metal, used for joining two larger pieces.  The term "doweled" describes something that has been assembled or secured using these dowels.|The two pieces of wood were doweled together.  这两块木头是用销钉连接在一起的。|Synonyms: pinned, pegged, joined, fastened|Antonyms: separated, detached, loose|adjective

dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n. vt.|销钉；木钉；用销钉固定|A small cylindrical rod, usually made of wood, plastic, or metal, used for joining two larger pieces; to fasten or join with dowels.|He used a dowel to reinforce the joint. 他用销钉加固了接头。  She doweled the two pieces of wood together. 她用销钉把两块木头连接在一起。|Synonyms: pin, peg, rod|Antonyms: N/A|noun, verb
