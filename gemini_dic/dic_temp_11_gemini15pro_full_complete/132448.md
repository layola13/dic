nondepository:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːri/
adjective
非存款的
Not accepting or holding deposits of money, especially of the kind made by customers at a bank.
Nondepository institutions, such as insurance companies and pension funds, play a significant role in the financial system. 非存款机构，如保险公司和养老基金，在金融体系中扮演着重要角色。
Non-depository financial institutions offer various financial products but don't accept traditional deposits. 非存款金融机构提供各种金融产品，但不接受传统的存款。
near synonyms: non-bank, non-banking
antonyms: depository, banking
词性: adjective
