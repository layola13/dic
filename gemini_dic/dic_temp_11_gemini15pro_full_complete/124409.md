motorbiker:/ˈməʊtəˌbaɪkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərˌbaɪkər/ (US)| n. |摩托车手，骑摩托车的人|A person who rides a motorbike. |例句：The motorbiker sped down the highway. 摩托车手在高速公路上飞驰。

例句：The group of motorbikers waved as they passed.  这群摩托车手经过时挥手致意。

近义词：biker, motorcyclist|反义词:pedestrian, walker|词性:noun
