orthodome:/ˈɔːrθəˌdoʊm/| n. |正交晶系晶面的总称; 正交晶系晶面的一个|A crystal form consisting of two faces parallel to one of the crystallographic axes and meeting the other two axes at different distances.|The orthodome crystal form is characteristic of minerals in the orthorhombic system. 正交晶面晶形是正交晶系矿物的特征。|The mineral barite often exhibits well-developed orthodomes. 重晶石矿物通常表现出发育良好的正交晶面。|pinacoid, dome| N/A |noun
orthogonal:/ɔːrˈθɑːɡənəl/ |adj.|正交的；互相垂直的|Relating to or involving right angles; perpendicular.|The two lines are orthogonal. 这两条线互相垂直。|The vectors are orthogonal to each other. 这些向量彼此正交。|perpendicular, right-angled|oblique, skewed|adjective
