collembola:/kəˈlɛmbələ/
n.
springtails; collembolans
Collembola are a group of arthropods that are abundant in soil and leaf litter.
Collembola are important decomposers in many ecosystems.  跳虫在许多生态系统中是重要的分解者。
Collembola populations can be used as indicators of soil health. 跳虫种群数量可以作为土壤健康状况的指标。
synonyms: springtails
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a biological group)
noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek *kólla*  (glue) + *émbolos* (peg, wedge). Refers to the ventral tube (collophore), once thought to be an adhesive organ.
