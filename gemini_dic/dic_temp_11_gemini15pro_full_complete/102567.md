jaggers:/ˈdʒæɡərz/ | n. | 锯齿状边缘; 尖锐的突出物; (尤指)衣物的锯齿状装饰 | Sharp, irregular projections; especially, the notched edge of a garment. |  The dress's jaggers gave it a unique, edgy look. 这件衣服的锯齿状边缘赋予它独特的、前卫的外观。| synonyms: serrations, notches, teeth, points | antonyms: smoothness, evenness | noun


jagged:/ˈdʒæɡɪd/ | adj. | 锯齿状的; 凹凸不平的; 粗糙的 | Having sharp, irregular projections; unevenly notched or toothed. | The jagged rocks made the climb treacherous. 崎岖不平的岩石使攀登变得危险。 | synonyms: uneven, rough, serrated, notched | antonyms: smooth, even, level | adjective
