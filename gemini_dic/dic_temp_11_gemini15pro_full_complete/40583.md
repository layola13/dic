conus: /ˈkoʊnəs/ (US), /ˈkəʊnʌs/ (UK)| n.|圆锥体；圆锥形；（解剖学）锥体|A cone-shaped object or structure, especially in anatomy, referring to a cone-shaped part of the body, such as the conus medullaris (end of the spinal cord).|The venom of the conus snail is highly potent.  圆锥蜗牛的毒液非常厉害。

The conus arteriosus is part of the heart in some animals.  动脉圆锥是某些动物心脏的一部分。|Synonyms: cone, pyramid|Antonyms: none|Noun

conus: /ˈkoʊnəs/ (US), /ˈkəʊnʌs/ (UK)| n.|芋螺；鸡心螺|A genus of predatory sea snails, commonly called cone snails.|Several species of conus are found in tropical waters.  在热带水域可以找到几种芋螺。

The shell of a conus is often beautifully patterned.  芋螺的壳通常有美丽的图案。|Synonyms: cone snail|Antonyms: none|Noun
