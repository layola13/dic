believingly:/bɪˈliːvɪŋli/
adverb
相信地，深信不疑地
in a believing manner; with belief
She nodded believingly as he told his story. 她一边听他讲故事，一边深信不疑地点头。
近义词: trustingly, credulously, faithfully
反义词: skeptically, doubtfully, cynically
词性: adverb

词源:
From believe (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Believe" comes from Old English "belīefan"  meaning "to hold dear, value, trust," composed of "be-" (an intensifying prefix) and "lēof"  meaning "dear, beloved."
