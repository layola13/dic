lilacs:/ˈlaɪləks/| n.|丁香花|A shrub or small tree of the olive family, that has fragrant purplish or white flowers.|The air was filled with the scent of lilacs. 空气中弥漫着丁香花的香味。|Synonyms:flower, blossom|Antonyms:weed|noun

lilacs:/ˈlaɪləks/| adj.|淡紫色的|Of a pale pinkish-purple color.|She wore a lilacs dress. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙。|Synonyms:lavender, mauve|Antonyms:dark purple, black |adjective
