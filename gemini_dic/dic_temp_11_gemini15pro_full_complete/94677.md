imprinters:/ɪmˈprɪntərz/
n.
印刷机;压印机;印花机;铭刻机（imprinter 的复数）
Machines or tools used to impress a mark or design onto a surface.
The imprinters were used to stamp the company logo onto the promotional items.  这些压印机被用来将公司标志印在促销品上。
The imprinters created a detailed pattern on the leather.  压印机在皮革上创造了一个精细的图案。
synonyms: stamping machines, marking devices, embossers
antonyms: erasers
