hypercritical:/ˌhaɪpərˈkrɪtɪkl/ | adj. | 过分批评的，吹毛求疵的 | Inclined to find fault too readily; excessively critical. |  She gave him a hypercritical look that made him feel two inches tall. 她用一种极其挑剔的眼光看着他，让他感觉自己矮了两寸。

| Example: He's so hypercritical of everything I do, it's exhausting. 他对我做的每件事都吹毛求疵，真让人筋疲力尽。 | Synonym: fault-finding, nitpicking, captious, censorious | Antonym: uncritical, tolerant, forgiving | word property: Adjective
