ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *across, from Old English *on cros, from on (preposition, "on") + cros (noun, "cross"). Cognate with Dutch dwars ("across, athwart"), German quer ("across, oblique"), Swedish tvärs ("across, athwart").
