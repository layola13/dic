/ˈlɛksiːmz/ | n. | 词位，词素 |  Plural form of "lexeme," which refers to a basic unit of vocabulary, consisting of one word or several words, considered as an abstract unit, and applied to a family of words related by form or meaning. |  The lexemes "run," "runs," "running," and "ran" are all forms of the same lexeme.  词位“run”、“runs”、“running”和“ran”都是同一个词位的不同形式。| synonyms: vocabulary units, words | antonyms: N/A | noun
