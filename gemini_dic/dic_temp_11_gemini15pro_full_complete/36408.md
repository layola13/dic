cojoining:/koʊˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
verb
结合，连接
the act of joining together; connecting.
Cojoining clauses with a conjunction creates a compound sentence. 用连词连接子句构成复合句。
近义词: combining, uniting, linking, connecting
反义词: separating, dividing, disconnecting
词性: Verb (现在分词)

Etymology:
From co- + join + -ing.  "Co-" comes from Latin "com-" meaning "together," and "join" comes from Old French "joindre," ultimately from Latin "jungere" meaning "to join."
