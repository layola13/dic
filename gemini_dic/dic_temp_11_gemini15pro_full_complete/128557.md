niagara:/naɪˈæɡərə/ | n. |尼亚加拉|A place name, specifically referring to Niagara Falls, a group of massive waterfalls at the southern end of Niagara Gorge, spanning the border between the United States and Canada.  It can also refer to the river (Niagara River) and the region surrounding the falls.|例句：We visited Niagara Falls last summer. 我们去年夏天参观了尼亚加拉大瀑布。

|例句：The Niagara River flows north from Lake Erie to Lake Ontario. 尼亚加拉河从伊利湖向北流向安大略湖。|近义词：waterfall, cascade (for the falls themselves) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
