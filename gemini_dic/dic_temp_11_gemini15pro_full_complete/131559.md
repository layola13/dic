noncommunally:/ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnəli/
adverb
非宗派地，不分宗派的
Not pertaining to or involving different religious or ethnic groups within a community; in a way that does not promote communalism.
The funds were distributed noncommunally, benefiting people of all faiths.  资金的分配不分宗派，所有信仰的人都受益。
The organization works noncommunally to promote peace and understanding.  该组织不分宗派地致力于促进和平与理解。
近义词: inclusively, universally, impartially, neutrally
反义词: communally, sectarianly, divisively
