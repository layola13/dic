breastwheel:/ˈbrestˌwiːl/ | n. |水车|A water wheel having buckets fixed to the rim; the water enters at or near the top and turns the wheel by falling into the buckets.|The old mill was powered by a breastwheel. 那座老磨坊是由水车驱动的。|Synonym: water wheel|Antonym: turbine|noun

breastwheel:/brestˌwiːl/ | n. |胸轮，下冲式水车|A water wheel in which water enters the buckets at about the level of the axle.|The breastwheel was more efficient than the undershot wheel. 胸轮比下冲式水车效率更高。|Synonym: mid-shot water wheel|Antonym: overshot water wheel|noun
