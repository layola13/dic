bouncily:/ˈbaʊnsɪli/
adverb
活泼地，有弹性地
in a bouncy manner; springily.
She walked bouncily down the street. 她沿着街道蹦蹦跳跳地走。
近义词:springily, elastically, resiliently
反义词:stiffly, rigidly, sluggishly
词性:adverb

bounce:/baʊns/
verb/noun
弹跳，跳跃；反弹；弹回
(of an object, especially a ball) move quickly up, back, or away from a surface after hitting it; (of a person) jump repeatedly up and down; (of light, sound, or an electronic signal) be reflected back.
The ball bounced twice before he caught it.  球弹了两次他才接住。
近义词:leap, jump, spring, rebound
反义词:fall, drop, sink
词性:verb/noun

bouncing:/ˈbaʊnsɪŋ/
adjective
活泼的，有弹性的
(of a person) lively and active; (of an object) springing back into shape or position when squeezed or stretched.
The baby was a bouncing bundle of joy. 这个婴儿是一个活泼可爱的小家伙。
近义词:lively, energetic, vigorous, springy, resilient
反义词:lifeless, inert, sluggish, stiff, rigid
词性:adjective

bounciness:/ˈbaʊnsɪnəs/
noun
弹性，活泼
the quality of being bouncy.
The bounciness of the trampoline was exhilarating.  蹦床的弹性令人兴奋。
近义词:springiness, resilience, elasticity
反义词:stiffness, rigidity, inflexibility
词性:noun


词源:
From bounce + -ily.  Bounce ultimately comes from Middle Dutch *būnsen.
