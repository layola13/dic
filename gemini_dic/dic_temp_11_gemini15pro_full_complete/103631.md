jojobas:/həˈhoʊbəz/ | n. | 荷荷巴油（复数）|  A liquid wax obtained from the seeds of the jojoba plant, used in cosmetics and as a lubricant.  It's technically a liquid wax, not an oil. | Jojobas are widely used in skincare products. 荷荷巴油被广泛用于护肤产品。 |Synonyms: jojoba oil, jojoba wax | Antonyms: N/A | noun

jojoba:/həˈhoʊbə/ | n. | 荷荷巴（单数）| A shrub native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, from which jojoba oil is obtained.  | The jojoba plant thrives in arid climates. 荷荷巴植物在干旱气候中茁壮成长。 |Synonyms: Simmondsia chinensis (scientific name), goat nut, deer nut, pignut, wild hazel, coffeeberry, gray box bush | Antonyms: N/A | noun
