overharshness:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃnəs/| n.| 过于严厉，过度苛刻|The overharshness of the punishment sparked public outcry.  这项惩罚过于严厉，引发了公众的强烈抗议。|Overharshness of the criticism demoralized the team.  批评过于苛刻，打击了团队的士气。|severity, strictness, rigidity|leniency, mildness, gentleness|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + harshness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "harshness" refers to the quality of being unpleasant, severe, or cruel.
