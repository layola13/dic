leave:/liːv/
verb
离开，留下，剩下，使处于某种状态
Etymology: From Old English *lēofan (with a long vowel), from Proto-Germanic *laubjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *leubh- (“to love, like, desire”).
He left the house early this morning.  他今天早上很早就离开了家。
She left her umbrella on the bus. 她把伞落在公交车上了。
synonyms: depart, exit, quit, abandon
antonyms: arrive, stay, remain
词性: 动词
