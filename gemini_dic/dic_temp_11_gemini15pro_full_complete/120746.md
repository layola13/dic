misanthropical:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪkəl/ |adj.|厌世的；遁世的；憎恶人类的|Disliking humankind and avoiding human society.| He became increasingly misanthropical in his later years, refusing to see anyone.  他晚年变得越来越厌世，拒绝见任何人。| The hermit lived a misanthropical existence deep in the woods. 这位隐士在森林深处过着遁世的生活。 | synonyms: anti-social, cynical, pessimistic, reclusive, unsociable | antonyms:  philanthropic, sociable, gregarious | adjective
