assails:/əˈseɪlz/| v.|攻击，猛烈抨击|to attack violently, either physically or verbally; to criticize strongly|The army assailed the city walls.  军队猛攻城墙。

The senator was assailed with questions from reporters.  这位参议员遭到记者连珠炮似的提问。|近义词：attack, assault, bombard, criticize, lambaste|反义词：defend, protect, support|词性:动词
词源: From Old French *assalir*, from Latin *assilire* "to leap upon," from *ad-* "to" + *salire* "to leap."
