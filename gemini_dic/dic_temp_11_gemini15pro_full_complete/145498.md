overclosely:/ˌoʊvərˈkloʊsli/
adverb
过于密切地
Examining something overclosely can sometimes obscure the bigger picture.  过分仔细地检查有时会模糊大局。
She monitored her children overclosely, stifling their independence. 她过于严密地监视她的孩子们，扼杀了他们的独立性。
近义词: excessively closely, too closely, meticulously
反义词: superficially, cursorily, distantly
词性: adverb
