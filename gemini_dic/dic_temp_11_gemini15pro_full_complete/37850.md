competitiveness:/kəmˌpɛtɪtɪvnəs/
noun
竞争力
The quality of being able to compete successfully.
例句：The company's competitiveness has improved significantly in recent years. 该公司的竞争力近年来显著提高。
例句：Global competitiveness is becoming increasingly fierce. 全球竞争日益激烈。

近义词：rivalry, ability to compete
反义词：inability to compete, weakness

词性: noun

词源: From competitive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin competere "to strive together."
