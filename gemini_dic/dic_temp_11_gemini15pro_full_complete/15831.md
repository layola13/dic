balladiers:/ˌbæləˈdɪərz/
n.
民谣歌手；歌谣演唱者
A balladier is a singer, especially one who sings ballads.
The balladiers entertained the crowd with their soulful music.  民谣歌手们用他们深情的音乐娱乐大众。
The young balladier captivated the audience with his performance.  这位年轻的民谣歌手用他的表演迷住了观众。
singers, musicians, minstrels, troubadours
