helminthite:/ˈhɛlmɪnˌθaɪt/| n. |寄生虫（尤指肠虫）|A worm-like animal, especially one parasitic on the intestines of humans and other animals.  Specifically, it refers to a parasitic intestinal worm. |例句：The doctor diagnosed him with a helminthite infection. 医生诊断他感染了寄生虫。

例句：Helminthites can cause a variety of health problems. 寄生虫会导致各种健康问题。|近义词：worm, parasite, helminth |反义词: None (No direct opposite for a biological organism) |词性: Noun

词源: From Greek "helmins" (worm) + "-ite" (suffix denoting a mineral or fossil).
