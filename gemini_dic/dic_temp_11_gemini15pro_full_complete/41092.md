cobbler:/ˈkɑːblər/ | n. | 鞋匠；补鞋匠；水果馅饼 | A person who mends shoes.  A dish consisting of fruit baked with a thick, biscuit-like topping. | Example 1: The cobbler repaired my worn-out boots.  鞋匠修好了我破旧的靴子。

Example 2:  We had apple cobbler for dessert. 我们甜点吃了苹果馅饼。 | Synonyms: shoemaker, shoe repairer (for person); crumble, crisp (for dish) | Antonyms:  N/A (no direct opposite for a profession or a dish) | noun
