mushroomed:/ˈmʌʃ.rumd/ (US), /ˈmʌʃ.ruːmd/ (UK)| v. |迅速增长；迅速发展；如雨后春笋般涌现|To grow or expand rapidly, like mushrooms after rain. |例句：The town has mushroomed in recent years.  近年来，这个小镇迅速发展。

例句：New housing developments mushroomed on the edge of the city.  城市边缘的新住宅区如雨后春笋般涌现。|近义词：proliferate, burgeon, expand, grow rapidly|反义词:shrink, dwindle, contract, decrease|词性:动词
