claritude: /klærɪtjuːd/
n.
清晰，明晰，清楚
The state or quality of being clear and easily understood.
Her writing is admired for its exceptional clarity and claritude. 她的写作因其非凡的清晰度和明晰度而备受赞赏。
The claritude of the night sky allowed us to see the Milky Way. 晴朗的夜空让我们能够看到银河。
近义词: clarity, lucidity, perspicuity, transparency
反义词: obscurity, vagueness, ambiguity, confusion
词性: 名词

词源: From French "claritude," from Late Latin "claritudo," from Latin "clarus" (clear, bright).
