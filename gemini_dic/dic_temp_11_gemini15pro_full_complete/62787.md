engross:/ɪnˈɡrəʊs/ (US) /ɪnˈɡroʊs/| vt. |全神贯注，吸引；正式写成（决议等）|to occupy the mind or attention of someone completely|The new game engrossed him for hours.  这款新游戏让他着迷了好几个小时。|Synonyms: absorb, captivate, engage, immerse, preoccupy|Antonyms: bore, distract, tire|verb

engrossed:/ɪnˈɡrəʊst/ (US) /ɪnˈɡroʊst/| adj. |全神贯注的|completely occupied or absorbed|He was so engrossed in his book that he didn't hear me come in. 他全神贯注地看书，没听到我进来。|Synonyms: absorbed, captivated, engaged, immersed, preoccupied|Antonyms: bored, distracted, tired|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French engrosser "to make thick or fat, buy up wholesale," from en- "in" + grosser "to make large, buy up wholesale," from gros "large, big, thick."
