drosky:/ˈdrɒski/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːski/ (US)| n.|轻便马车，敞篷马车|A small, open horse-drawn carriage used in Russia and other countries.|例句：The drosky driver whipped his horse through the crowded streets.  轻便马车夫挥舞着鞭子，驾着马穿过拥挤的街道。

例句：We hired a drosky to take us to the summer palace. 我们雇了一辆轻便马车送我们去避暑山庄。|近义词：carriage, cab, fiacre|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian дрожки (drozhki), plural of дрожка (drozhka), diminutive of дорога (doroga, “road, way”).
