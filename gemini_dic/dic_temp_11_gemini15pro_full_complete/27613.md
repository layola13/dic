cancelli: /kanˈtʃɛli/
n.
gates, lattices, railings (plural)
A structure of metal bars or wires forming a barrier or enclosure.  Specifically refers to a lattice or grating, often used in architectural contexts. Derived from the Italian word "cancello" (singular).
The cancelli of the villa were intricately designed wrought iron.  别墅的栅栏是由精心设计的锻铁制成的。
synonyms:  gratings, grilles, railings, fences, barriers
antonyms: openings, entrances, gaps
