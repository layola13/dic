event:/ɪˈvent/ | n. |事件，大事，比赛项目 |Something that happens, especially something important or unusual. |The World Cup is a major sporting event. 世界杯是一项重要的体育赛事。

event:/ɪˈvent/ | n. |结果，后果 |The final result or consequence of a process or action. |In the event of a fire, break the glass. 如果发生火灾，请打破玻璃。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "evenement" (happening, adventure), from "evenir" (to happen), from Latin "evenire" (to come out, happen), from "ex-" (out) + "venire" (to come).


SYNONYMS->
occurrence, incident, happening, affair, occasion, circumstance

ANTONYMS->
non-event, nothing
