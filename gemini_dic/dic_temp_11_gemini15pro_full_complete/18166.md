bejitsu: /beɪdʒɪtsu/
n.|武术，兵法; 日本武术|Japanese martial arts.  A term encompassing a variety of martial arts and military strategies developed in Japan.|例句：He is a master of bejitsu. 他是一位武术大师。|例句：Bejitsu emphasizes both physical and mental discipline. 武术强调身心兼修。|近义词：martial arts, bujutsu|反义词: pacifism|词性:名词

词源: From Japanese  "兵術" (hyōjutsu, later changed to  "兵法" - heiho, then adapted as bejitsu), composed of 兵 (hei, "soldier; warfare; strategy; tactics") and 術 (jutsu, "art; technique; skill").  Originally referring to military tactics and strategy, it evolved to encompass a broader range of martial disciplines.
