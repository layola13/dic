omostegite:/ˈɒməʊˌstɛdʒaɪt/| n. |鳃盖, 覆盖鳃的甲壳|A plate-like or shield-like structure forming part of the carapace of certain crustaceans, and serving to protect the gills.|The omostegite of the shrimp was damaged, affecting its respiration.  虾的鳃盖受损，影响了它的呼吸。|Synonyms: branchiostegite (more common synonym), gill cover|Antonyms: None (lack of a structure doesn't have a specific antonym)|Noun
