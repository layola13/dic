fished-out:/ˈfɪʃtˌaʊt/
adjective
捕捞殆尽的，资源枯竭的
Depleted of fish or other marine life due to overfishing; exhausted or depleted of resources.
The lake is fished-out, there's barely anything left to catch.  这个湖里的鱼都被捕捞殆尽了，几乎没有什么可捕捞的了。
The gold mine was completely fished-out after years of exploitation.  经过多年的开采，金矿完全枯竭了。
Synonyms: depleted, exhausted, drained, overfished
Antonyms: abundant, plentiful, replete, well-stocked
词性: 形容词
