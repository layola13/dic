netball:/ˈnɛtbɔːl/| n.|篮球(尤指女子篮球)|A sport played, especially by women, with seven players on each team, in which goals are scored by throwing a ball through a raised hoop.|例句：She plays netball every Saturday. 她每个星期六都打篮球。
