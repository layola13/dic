making:/ˈmeɪkɪŋ/| n. |制造; 制作; 形成; 特质; 性格|The process of creating or forming something.  The act of producing something.|例句：The making of the film took two years.  这部电影的制作花了2年时间。|近义词：production, creation, formation, construction|反义词：destruction, demolition|词性:名词

making:/ˈmeɪkɪŋ/| adj. |构成…的; 形成…的; 促成…的|That constitutes or forms something; that helps to bring something about.|例句：Hard work is the making of a successful person. 努力工作是成功人士的必备条件。|近义词：constituent, formative, crucial, essential|反义词：unimportant, insignificant|词性:形容词
