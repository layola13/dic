etiquette: /ˈetɪkət/ (US), /ˈetɪket/ (UK)| n. |礼仪，礼节|The customary code of polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession or group. |  It's important to observe proper etiquette at a formal dinner. 在正式晚宴上遵守适当的礼仪是很重要的。| synonyms: decorum, protocol, manners, propriety | antonyms: rudeness, impropriety, disrespect, bad manners | noun
