cateringly:/ˈkeɪtərɪŋli/
adverb
迎合地，殷勤地
in a catering manner; so as to cater to someone's needs or desires.
She smiled cateringly at the demanding customer. 她殷勤地对这位苛刻的顾客笑着。
近义词: obligingly, solicitously, attentively
反义词: dismissively, indifferently
词性: adverb

cater:/ˈkeɪtər/
verb
提供餐饮服务；迎合
to provide food and drinks for a social event. / to try to satisfy a particular need or demand
They catered for the wedding reception. 他们为婚宴提供餐饮服务。
The magazine caters to a younger audience. 这本杂志迎合年轻读者的口味。
近义词: provide, supply, serve, pander to, indulge
反义词: neglect, ignore
词性: verb

-ing:/ɪŋ/
suffix
构成现在分词和动名词
Used to form the present participle and gerund of verbs.
Walking is good exercise.  散步是很好的运动。
He is reading a book. 他正在读书。
词性: suffix

词源: From the verb "cater," which comes from the Middle English "catour," meaning "buyer of provisions," ultimately from the Anglo-French "acatour." The "-ingly" suffix transforms the verb into an adverb indicating the manner in which something is done.
