identifications:/aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.| 鉴定，识别，认同；身份证明 |The identifications of the victims took several days. 受害者的身份鉴定花了几天时间。| Example: The police are seeking positive identifications of the suspects. 警方正在寻求对嫌疑人的明确身份认定。| Synonyms: recognition, verification, authentication | Antonyms: misidentification, anonymity | Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *identificationem,  from Latin identificare "to identify," from ident-, variant of idem "the same" + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make, do."
