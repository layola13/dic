bhistee: /bɪˈstiː/
noun
水夫，送水人
A person in India who carries water in a goatskin.
The bhistee filled the water jars from the well.  水夫从井里装满了水罐。
The bhistee sprinkled water on the dusty road. 水夫在尘土飞扬的路上洒水。
water carrier, water seller
