malfeasance:/ˌmælˈfiːzəns/
n.
渎职；不法行为；玩忽职守

The mayor was accused of malfeasance in office. 市长被指控渎职。

近义词:misconduct, wrongdoing, impropriety, corruption
反义词:lawful behavior, propriety
