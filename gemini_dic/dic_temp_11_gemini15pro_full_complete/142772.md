ouakari: /wɑːˈkɑːri/ | n. | 秃猴，吼猴 | A short-tailed South American monkey with a bald face and a loud howling call.  It is found in the Amazon rainforest. | 例句：The ouakari's bright red face is a striking feature.  秃猴鲜红色的脸是一个显著的特征。

Etymology: From the Tupi language.
