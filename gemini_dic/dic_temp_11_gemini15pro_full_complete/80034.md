go-together:/ˌɡoʊ təˈɡɛðər/ | adj. | 相配的，协调的，般配的；常在一起的，形影不离的 |  Usually found or used together; well-suited; compatible.  Also, frequently seen together; inseparable. |  That shirt and tie go together perfectly. 那件衬衫和领带非常相配。 |  matching, complementary, harmonious, compatible, inseparable |  clashing, discordant, mismatched | adjective
