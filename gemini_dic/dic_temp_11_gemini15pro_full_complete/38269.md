consoled:/kənˈsoʊld/
verb
安慰，抚慰
to comfort someone at a time of grief or disappointment.
例句：She consoled him after his mother died. 她在他母亲去世后安慰他。

例句：He tried to console her with a hug. 他试图用拥抱来安慰她。

近义词：comfort, soothe, solace
反义词：distress, upset, grieve
词性：v.


console:/ˈkɒnsəʊl/ /ˈkɑːnsoʊl/
noun
控制台，操纵台
a panel or unit accommodating a set of controls for electronic or mechanical equipment.
例句：The pilot checked the instrument console. 飞行员检查了仪表盘。

例句：A games console.  游戏机。

近义词：panel, dashboard, control panel
反义词：None
词性：n.
