fell-out:/ˈfɛl aʊt/ | adj. | 脱落的，散开的| Having fallen out or become separated. |  Her hair was fell-out and messy after the windy car ride.  坐完敞篷车后，她的头发散乱不堪。| Synonyms: loose, disheveled, unkempt | Antonyms: neat, tidy, ordered | adjective
