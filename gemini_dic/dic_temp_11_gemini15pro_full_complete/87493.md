heterocephalous: /ˌhɛtərəˈsɛfələs/
| adj. |异头的，头状花序上小花不同形的|Having flower heads with florets of different forms, as in some composite plants.|The heterocephalous inflorescence is a characteristic of this species.|近义词: dimorphic|反义词: homocephalous|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *héteros* (different) + *kephalḗ* (head)
