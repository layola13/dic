lesioning: /ˈliːʒənɪŋ/
n.
损害；毁损；切除
The process of producing lesions in the brain or other tissue, often for experimental purposes.
Lesioning specific areas of the brain can help researchers understand their function.  对大脑特定区域进行损害有助于研究人员了解其功能。
Experimental lesioning of the hippocampus in rats resulted in memory deficits.  对大鼠海马体进行实验性损害导致记忆缺陷。
Synonym: ablation, destruction
Antonym: repair, healing
noun
