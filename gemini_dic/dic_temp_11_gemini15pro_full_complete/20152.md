biplanar:/baɪˈpleɪnər/
adjective
双平面的
Relating to or situated in two planes.  Existing in two planes.
The biplanar X-ray imaging system allows for more precise measurements.  这个双平面X射线成像系统可以进行更精确的测量。
The fracture was biplanar, extending into two different anatomical planes.  骨折是双平面的，延伸到两个不同的解剖平面。
planar, two-dimensional
nonplanar, three-dimensional
