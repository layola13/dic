knitback:/ˈnɪtbæk/| n.|反面编织；反针织物|A knit stitch made by inserting the needle into the stitch from left to right (as opposed to right to left as in a regular knit stitch). This creates a slightly twisted stitch that appears different on the front and back of the fabric.  It is often used to create texture and patterns.|She used the knitback stitch to create a bumpy texture on the scarf.|近义词:purl, reverse knit|反义词:knit|词性:名词
