manerial:/məˈnɪəriəl/| adj.|领地的，庄园的|Relating to a manor or estate; characteristic of the landed gentry.|The family lived in a grand manerial home.  这个家庭住在一栋宏伟的庄园住宅里。|例句：The manerial system was prevalent in medieval Europe.  庄园制度在中世纪的欧洲很盛行。|近义词：estate, landed, feudal|反义词: urban, modern|词性:形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *manirialis, from Latin *maniarium "manor," from manus "hand" + -arium (noun suffix forming place nouns).  源自晚期拉丁语 *manirialis，来自拉丁语 *maniarium “庄园”，来自 manus “手” + -arium （构成地点名词的名词后缀）。
