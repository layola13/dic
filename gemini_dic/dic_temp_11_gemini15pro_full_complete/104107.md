jump cut: /ˈdʒʌmp kʌt/ | n. | 跳切 | An abrupt transition from one scene to another in a film, creating a sense of discontinuity. | Example: The director used jump cuts to create a fast-paced, energetic feel. 导演使用跳切来营造快节奏、充满活力的感觉。

Example:  The jump cut in that scene was jarring and took me out of the story.  那个场景中的跳切很突兀，让我出戏了。

| 近义词: cut, edit, transition | 反义词: dissolve, fade, match cut | 词性: noun
