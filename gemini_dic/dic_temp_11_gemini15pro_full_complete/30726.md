cervantite:/sərˈvæntaɪt/
n.
锑华，锑赭石
A mineral consisting of antimony tetroxide (Sb₂O₄), occurring as a yellow to white powder.
Cervantite is often found in the oxidized zones of antimony deposits. 锑华常发现于锑矿床的氧化带。
