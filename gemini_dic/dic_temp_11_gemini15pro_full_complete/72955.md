food:/fuːd/| n.|食物，食品|Any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorb in order to maintain life and growth.|例句：We need food to survive. 我们需要食物才能生存。

例句：The food at the restaurant was delicious.  这家餐厅的食物很美味。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, provisions, edibles|反义词:poison, toxin|Noun
