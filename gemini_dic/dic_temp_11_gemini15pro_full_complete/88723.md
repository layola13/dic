hobnail:/ˈhɒbneɪl/ (US) /ˈhɑːbneɪl/| n. adj.|鞋钉；带钉的；粗糙的|A short nail with a thick head, used to strengthen the soles of boots and shoes. Also used as an adjective to describe things studded with, or resembling, hobnails.|例句：The farmer wore hobnail boots.  农民穿着钉鞋。


例句：The hobnail boots protected his feet from the rough terrain.  钉鞋保护他的双脚免受崎岖地形的伤害。|近义词：stud, spike, cleat|反义词:smooth|词性:名词, 形容词
