biseptate:/baɪˈsɛpteɪt/
adjective|二分隔的；具二隔膜的|Divided into two parts or compartments by a septum or partition. |The biseptate uterus is a relatively uncommon uterine anomaly.|近义词：bilobed, divided|反义词：undivided, entire|词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *bi- "two" + septatus "divided by a septum," past participle of septare "to divide by a hedge or fence."
