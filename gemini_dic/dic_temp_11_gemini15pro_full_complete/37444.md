commiseratingly:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
同情地，怜悯地
in a way that expresses or shows sympathy or pity.
She patted his arm commiseratingly. 她同情地拍了拍他的手臂。
近义词：sympathetically, pityingly, compassionately
反义词：unsympathetically, callously, heartlessly
词性：adverb

词源:
From commiserate + -ing + -ly.  Commiserate comes from Late Latin commiserari "to pity," from com- "with" + miserari "to pity," from miser "wretched, unfortunate."
