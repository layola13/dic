bovinity:/boʊˈvɪnɪti/
n.
迟钝；愚蠢；牛性；像牛一样
The state of being bovine; dullness of intellect.
His bovinity made him an easy target for the con artist. 他的迟钝使他很容易成为骗子的目标。
Synonyms: stupidity, dullness, stolidity, apathy, phlegm
Antonyms: intelligence, brilliance, wit, alertness, vivacity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *bovīnitās, from Latin bovīnus (“of or pertaining to oxen or cows”), from bōs (“ox, cow”).
源自晚期拉丁语 *bovīnitās，来自拉丁语 bovīnus（“属于牛或与牛有关的”），来自 bōs（“牛”）。
