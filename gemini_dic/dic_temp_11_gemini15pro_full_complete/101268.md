involucral:/ɪnvəˈluːkrəl/| adj.|总苞的；苞片的|Relating to or resembling an involucre, a whorl or set of bracts surrounding a flower cluster or fruit.|The involucral bracts were covered in fine hairs.|近义词：bracteal, bracted|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ebracteate" which means lacking bracts)|词性: adjective
