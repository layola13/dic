heinousnesses:/ˈheɪnəsnəsɪz/
noun
极恶劣的行为；滔天罪行；罪大恶极
The extreme wickedness or egregiousness of something.  Plural of heinousness.
The heinousnesses of the crimes committed during the war shocked the world.  战争期间犯下的滔天罪行震惊了世界。
近义词:atrocities, enormities, wickedness, evil, depravity
反义词:virtues, goodness, righteousness
词性:noun

heinousness:/ˈheɪnəsnəs/
noun
极恶；凶残；滔天大罪
The quality of being utterly odious or wicked.
The heinousness of the crime warranted a severe punishment. 这起罪行的滔天罪行应该受到严厉惩罚。
近义词:atrocity, enormity, wickedness, evil, depravity
反义词:virtue, goodness, righteousness
词性:noun


词源:
From Middle English *heinouste, from Anglo-Norman *hainuste, from Old French haineus (“hateful, odious”), from haine (“hatred”), from Frankish *hagin (“hate, hatred”), from Proto-Germanic *hatiz (“hate, hatred”). Cognate with Old High German haz (“hate, hatred”, > German Hass), Old English hete (“hate, hatred”).
