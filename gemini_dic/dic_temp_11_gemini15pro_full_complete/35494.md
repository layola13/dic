coadaptation:/ˌkoʊˌædæpˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 共同适应 |The reciprocal evolutionary change of two or more interacting species or populations.|  The coadaptation of flowers and their pollinators is a classic example of evolutionary synergy. 花和它们的授粉者之间的共同适应是进化协同作用的经典例子。

The coadaptation of predator and prey often leads to an evolutionary arms race. 捕食者和猎物之间的共同适应通常会导致进化军备竞赛。


|近义词：coevolution, mutual adaptation|反义词: maladaptation|词性: noun
