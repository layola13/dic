circumfusile:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfjuːsɪl/ |adj.|可浇铸的，可熔化的|Capable of being poured or melted; that may be cast or fused.|Molten bronze is circumfusile and can be used to create intricate sculptures. 熔化的青铜是可浇铸的，可以用来创作复杂的雕塑。|The circumfusile material was poured into the mold.  这种可熔化的材料被倒入模具中。|Synonyms: fusible, meltable, molten, liquid|Antonyms: infusible, non-fusible, solid|adjective
