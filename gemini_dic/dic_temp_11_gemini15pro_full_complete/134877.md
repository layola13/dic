nonmortal:/nɒnˈmɔːrtl/ /nɑːnˈmɔːrtəl/| adj.|不朽的，非凡人的|Not subject to death; immortal.  Also, relating to or characteristic of gods or demigods.|The ancient Egyptians believed their pharaohs were nonmortal beings. 古埃及人相信他们的法老是不朽的存在。|Synonyms: immortal, eternal, undying|Antonyms: mortal, perishable, finite|Adjective
