flotsam:/ˈflɒtsəm/ (US) /ˈflɑːtsəm/ (UK)| n. |(海上)漂浮物，废料；(社会)流寇，流浪者|Floating wreckage of a ship or its cargo; refuse. People or things regarded as useless or unwanted.|例句：The beach was littered with flotsam and jetsam after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是漂浮物和残骸。

例句：The city's flotsam and jetsam gathered in the park. 城市的流浪者聚集在公园里。|近义词：debris, wreckage, jetsam, refuse|反义词:treasure, valuables|词性:名词
