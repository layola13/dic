blabby:/ˈblæbi/| adj.|多嘴的，饶舌的|Given to excessive talk; loquacious.|She's so blabby, she can't keep a secret. 她太嘴碎了，守不住秘密。|例句：He was quite blabby after a few drinks. 他喝了几杯酒后变得很健谈。|近义词：talkative, chatty, garrulous, loquacious|反义词：reticent, taciturn|词性:形容词
blabby:/ˈblæbi/| n.|饶舌的人|A person who talks excessively.|Don't tell her anything; she's a blabby. 别告诉她任何事；她是个大嘴巴。|例句：He's known to be a bit of a blabby, so be careful what you say. 他是出了名的爱说话，所以说话要小心。|近义词：gossip, chatterbox|反义词：quiet person|词性:名词
