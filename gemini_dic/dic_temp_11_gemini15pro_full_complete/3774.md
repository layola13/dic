agitatorial:/ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənl/
adjective
煽动的；鼓动的
Of, relating to, or characteristic of an agitator or agitation.
The agitatorial speeches roused the crowd to action.  煽动性的演讲激起了群众的行动。
The newspaper published agitatorial articles criticizing the government.  这家报纸发表了煽动性的文章批评政府。
Near synonyms: inflammatory, provocative, subversive, incendiary
Near antonyms: calming, soothing, pacifying, conciliatory
Word Origin: From agitate + -orial, ultimately from Latin agitare "to drive, stir."
