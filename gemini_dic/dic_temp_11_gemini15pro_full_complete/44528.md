crossruff: /ˈkrɒsˌrʌf/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌrʌf/ (US) | n. v. |交叉将牌,交叉将吃;垫牌| In card games, especially whist and bridge, to trump a card in one hand with a trump from the other hand when the first hand does not have a card of the suit led. |  He crossruffed the queen of spades with the three of hearts. 他用红桃3交叉将吃了黑桃Q。| 近义词: ruff | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "discard") |词性: 名词, 动词
