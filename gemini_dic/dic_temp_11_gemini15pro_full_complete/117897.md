meloplastic:/ˈmɛləˌplæstɪk/| adj.|旋律优美的，悦耳的|Relating to or characterized by melodious plasticity; having a pleasingly smooth and flowing melodic line.  It describes music that is both melodious (pleasant to listen to) and plastic (flexible, adaptable).|The composer's meloplastic style made the piece incredibly moving.  这位作曲家旋律优美的风格使这首曲子令人难以置信地感动。

The meloplastic phrasing of the singer captivated the audience. 歌手悦耳流畅的唱腔深深吸引了观众。|近义词：melodious, tuneful, euphonious|反义词：discordant, cacophonous|词性: adjective

Etymology:  "melo-" from Greek "melos" (song, melody) + "-plastic" from Greek "plastikos" (able to be molded, adaptable).
