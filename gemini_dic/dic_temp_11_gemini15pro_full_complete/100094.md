interposer:/ˈɪntərˌpoʊzər/| n. |插入器，介入器，调解人|Something that is interposed; a thing or person that comes between others.  A layer of packaging material placed between layers of fragile items to prevent breakage during shipping. In electronics, a thin electrical interconnect that connects a processor to memory or peripherals, often used to increase bandwidth or add functionality.  |例句：The interposer allowed for a higher density of connections between the chips.  插入器允许芯片之间更高的连接密度。

例句：He acted as an interposer between the warring factions. 他充当了交战派别之间的调解人。|近义词：mediator, intermediary, go-between, connector, adapter|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


interposers:/ˈɪntərˌpoʊzərz/| n. |插入器，介入器，调解人（复数）|Plural form of "interposer." Refers to multiple things or people that come between others, particularly in electronics, referring to multiple interposer connections. |例句：The new design utilizes multiple interposers for increased performance. 新设计利用多个插入器来提高性能。
