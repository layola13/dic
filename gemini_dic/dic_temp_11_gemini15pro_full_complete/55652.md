dithionic:/ˌdaɪθaɪˈɒnɪk/
adjective|二硫的；含二硫酸根的|Relating to or containing dithionic acid (H₂S₂O₆), which contains two sulfur atoms.  Specifically, it refers to the anion S₂O₆²⁻.|Dithionic acid is a relatively unstable oxoacid of sulfur.|近义词:disulfuric|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "monosulfuric" or names of other sulfur oxoacids like "sulfuric," "sulfurous," "thiosulfuric")
词性:adjective
