germer:/ˈɡɜːrmər/| n.| 格默,  (指物理学家Lester Halbert Germer)| Lester Halbert Germer (1896-1971) was an American physicist who, along with Clinton Davisson, confirmed the de Broglie hypothesis by demonstrating the wave nature of electrons through electron diffraction. This experiment provided crucial experimental confirmation of quantum mechanics.|The Davisson–Germer experiment proved the wave nature of electrons, as theorized by de Broglie. 戴维森-格默实验证明了电子的波动性，正如德布罗意所提出的理论。| synonym: physicist, scientist| antonym: N/A |noun
