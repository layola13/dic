capitulum:/kəˈpɪtjʊləm/| n. |小头状花序，骨骺|A small head, knob, or protuberance, especially the head of a bone or the dense cluster of flowers in a composite plant.|The capitulum of the dandelion contains hundreds of individual florets.|Synonyms: head, knob, condyle|Antonyms: base, foot|noun

capitulum:/kəˈpɪtjʊləm/| n. |章，回|A chapter or section of a book, especially a law or other formal document.|The capitulum on property rights was particularly complex.|Synonyms: chapter, section, division|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun
