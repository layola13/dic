lashed:/læʃt/| v. adj.| 鞭打，捆绑，猛烈抨击，（风、雨、浪等）猛烈地冲击，（睫毛）浓密的，涂了睫毛膏的|To strike or beat with or as if with a whip or rod; to fasten with a rope, cord, etc.; to criticize harshly; to move violently or suddenly; having long or thick eyelashes (often used with `with').|The sailor lashed the sails to the mast. 水手把帆绑在桅杆上。

The storm lashed the coast.  暴风雨猛烈地冲击着海岸。

The critic lashed the new play. 评论家严厉批评了这部新剧。

She lashed out at him with accusations. 她用指责猛烈抨击他。

She has long, lashed eyelashes. 她有着浓密的睫毛。 |近义词：whip, tie, bind, criticize, attack, batter,  dense (for eyelashes)|反义词：untie, release, praise, defend, calm, sparse (for eyelashes)|词性:动词，形容词
