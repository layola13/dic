omnipatient:/ˌɒmniˈpeɪʃnt/ | adj. | 全能的，包容所有病人的 | Having the capacity to treat or care for all types of patients or diseases.  This word is a neologism, likely combining "omni-" (all) with "patient". | The ideal doctor would be omnipatient, able to handle any medical issue. 理想的医生应该能够治疗所有病人，能够处理任何医疗问题。| 近义词: versatile, adaptable, comprehensive | 反义词: specialized, limited, narrow | 词性: adjective
