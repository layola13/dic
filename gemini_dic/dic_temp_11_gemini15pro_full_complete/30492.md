centrosphere:/ˈsɛntrəsfɪər/
n.
地核; 中心球
The central or innermost part of the Earth. The dense, solid inner part of the Earth.
The Earth's centrosphere is primarily composed of iron and nickel.  地球的中心球主要由铁和镍组成。
Seismic waves help scientists understand the properties of the Earth's centrosphere. 地震波帮助科学家了解地球中心球的特性。
Synonyms: core, inner core
Antonyms: crust, mantle
