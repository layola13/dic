obeyable:/əˈbeɪəbəl/ | adj. | 可服从的，顺从的 | Capable of being obeyed; submissive. | The dog is very obeyable and follows all commands. 这只狗很听话，会服从所有指令。| The children were surprisingly obeyable and followed all the rules. 孩子们出奇地听话，遵守了所有的规则。 | 近义词：compliant, docile, submissive, tractable | 反义词：disobedient, rebellious, intractable, defiant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From 'obey' + '-able'.  'Obey' comes from Old French *obeir*  (modern French *obéir*), from Latin *oboedire* "to obey," from *ob-"toward" + *audire* "to hear, listen to."
