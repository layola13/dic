braked:/breɪkt/
verb
刹车；制动
past tense and past participle of brake
The car braked sharply to avoid hitting the deer. 汽车急刹车以避免撞到鹿。
He braked hard when he saw the child run into the street. 当他看到孩子跑到街上时，他猛踩刹车。
近义词: slowed, stopped, decelerated
反义词: accelerated, speeded up
词性: verb
