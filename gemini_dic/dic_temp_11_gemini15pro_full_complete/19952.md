biodiversity:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | n. |生物多样性|The variety of life in the world or in a particular habitat or ecosystem.|例句：The Amazon rainforest is known for its incredible biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林以其令人难以置信的生物多样性而闻名。|例句：Protecting biodiversity is crucial for the health of our planet. 保护生物多样性对我们星球的健康至关重要。|近义词：variety, richness, |反义词: uniformity, homogeneity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- (life) + diversity.  "Bio" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life". "Diversity" comes from the Old French "diversité" meaning "difference, variety".
