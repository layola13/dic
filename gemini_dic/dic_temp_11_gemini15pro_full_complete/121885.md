miss-fire:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪər/ | v. n. | 失灵，不奏效；失败 |  (of a plan or action) fail to produce the intended result. miss-fire:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪər/ | n. | 失败；不发火 | An instance of failing to fire or explode properly. | The hunter heard the miss-fire of his rifle. 猎人听到了他的步枪哑火的声音。| synonyms: failure, misfire | antonyms: success, triumph | noun
