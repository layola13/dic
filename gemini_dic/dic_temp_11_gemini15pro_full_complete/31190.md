changen: /ˈtʃeɪndʒən/ | v. | 改变；变化；兑换 | To change is to make or become different. |  She changed her mind about going to the party. 她改变了去参加聚会的主意。 | synonyms: alter, modify, transform, shift, vary, convert, exchange | antonyms: remain, stay, preserve, maintain | verb
