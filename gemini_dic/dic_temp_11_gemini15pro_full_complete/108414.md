lawned:/lɔːnd/
adjective
有草坪的；草皮覆盖的
covered with a lawn; having a lawn
例句：The hotel has a large lawned area for guests to relax in.  这家酒店有一个很大的草坪供客人放松。

例句：They have a beautifully lawned garden. 他们有一个美丽的草坪花园。

近义词：grassy, turfed
反义词：bare, barren
词性：adjective
