deterrability:/dɪˌtɜːrəˈbɪləti/
n.
可威慑性,威慑力
The quality of being able to be deterred.  The ease with which someone or something can be discouraged or prevented from acting.
The country's nuclear deterrability relies on the certainty of retaliation. 这个国家的核威慑力依赖于报复的确定性。
The deterrability of the new security system was questioned after several break-ins. 在几次入室盗窃后，新安全系统的威慑力受到质疑。
synonyms: dissuadability, preventability, determent
antonyms:  inducement, encouragement, incitement
noun
