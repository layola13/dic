intercombine:/ˌɪntərkəmˈbaɪn/| v. |互相结合，相互联合|To combine or cause to combine with one another.|The two companies plan to intercombine their operations to create a more efficient system. 这两家公司计划将它们的业务相互结合，以创建一个更有效的系统。|例句：The different elements intercombine to form a complex whole. 不同的元素相互结合形成一个复杂的整体。|例句：The software allows users to intercombine different modules to create customized solutions.  该软件允许用户组合不同的模块来创建定制的解决方案。|近义词：merge, integrate, combine, unite|反义词：separate, divide, disintegrate|词性:动词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "combine" (join together).  The prefix "inter-" indicates a reciprocal action or relationship between entities.
