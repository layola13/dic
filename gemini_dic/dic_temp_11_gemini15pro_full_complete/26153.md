busybodies:/ˈbɪziˌbɑːdiz/
n.
爱管闲事的人；多嘴多舌的人
People who pry into the affairs of others; gossips.
She and the other neighborhood busybodies were always gossiping. 她和其他邻居的爱管闲事者总是八卦。
nosy parkers, gossips, meddlers
private individuals, mind their own business people
noun
