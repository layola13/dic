illacrymable:/ˌɪləˈkraɪməbəl/
adjective
不易落泪的; 不催人泪下的
not causing or tending to cause tears
The movie was surprisingly illacrymable, despite its tragic themes. 这部电影出人意料地不催人泪下，尽管它的主题很悲惨。
The comedian delivered an illacrymable performance, focusing more on wit than sentimentality.  这位喜剧演员的表演并不催人泪下，更多地侧重于机智而不是感伤。
近义词: unemotional, unmoving, unsentimental
反义词: tearjerking, moving, sentimental
词性: adjective
词源:  From in- (not) + lacrimable (tearful), from Latin lacrima (tear).
