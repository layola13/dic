meddlesomeness:/ˈmɛdl̩səmnəs/| n. |爱管闲事；多管闲事|The state of being meddlesome; interfering in other people's affairs without being asked or needed.|Her meddlesomeness has caused a lot of problems in the family. 她的爱管闲事给家庭带来了很多问题。|Synonyms: interference, intrusion, officiousness|Antonyms: respect, restraint, noninterference|noun

meddle:/ˈmɛdl/| v. |干涉；管闲事|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|Don't meddle in my affairs. 不要干涉我的事。|Synonyms: interfere, intrude, tamper|Antonyms: ignore, neglect, abstain|verb

meddlesome:/ˈmɛdl̩səm/| adj. |爱管闲事的；多管闲事的|Given to meddling; interfering.|He's a meddlesome old man. 他是个爱管闲事的老头。|Synonyms: interfering, intrusive, officious|Antonyms: respectful, restrained, uninvolved|adjective
