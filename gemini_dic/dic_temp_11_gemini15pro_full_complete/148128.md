overseeing:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːɪŋ/
verb
监督；监管；照管
The manager is responsible for overseeing the project.  经理负责监督这个项目。
Etymology: From "oversee,"  a combination of "over" (above, across) and "see." The "-ing" suffix transforms it into a present participle, often used as a gerund or in continuous tenses.
He was overseeing the construction of the new bridge. 他正在监督新桥的建造。
Synonyms: supervising, managing, monitoring, directing
Antonyms: neglecting, ignoring, abandoning
word class: verb
