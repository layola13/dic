megastar:/ˈmɛɡəstɑːr/ | n. |超级巨星；巨星|A very famous and successful performer, especially a singer, actor, or athlete. |  She's a global megastar with millions of fans worldwide. 她是一位拥有数百万粉丝的全球巨星。

| Example: The megastar arrived in a limousine, surrounded by paparazzi.  这位巨星乘坐豪华轿车抵达，周围簇拥着狗仔队。| Synonym: superstar, celebrity, icon, luminary | Antonym: nobody, unknown | Noun
