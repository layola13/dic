levo:/ˈliːvoʊ/| adj.|左旋的，左侧的|Relating to or designating the side of an asymmetric molecule that rotates the plane of polarized light to the left, counterclockwise as viewed by an observer facing the oncoming radiation.  It is also used in chemical nomenclature to distinguish one of two enantiomers.|Levo-dopa is a medication used to treat Parkinson's disease. 左旋多巴是一种用于治疗帕金森病的药物。|The levorotatory form of the molecule was more effective.  该分子的左旋形式更有效。|近义词:left, sinistral|反义词:dextro, right|词性:adjective
