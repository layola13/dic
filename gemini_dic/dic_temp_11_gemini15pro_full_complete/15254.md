backhandedly:/ˌbækˈhændɪdli/| adv.|拐弯抹角地，间接地，反讽地|In a manner that is indirect or seemingly insincere, often containing an implied criticism or insult.|She complimented him backhandedly, saying his tie was "certainly interesting." 她拐弯抹角地称赞他，说他的领带“确实很有趣”。|近义词：indirectly, sarcastically, subtly, obliquely|反义词：directly, frankly, sincerely|词性:副词

backhandedly:/ˌbækˈhændɪdli/| adv.|隐含批评地|Containing an implicit criticism or negative remark, often disguised as praise.|He backhandedly agreed to help, muttering about how busy he was. 他勉强同意帮忙，嘴里嘟囔着自己有多忙。|近义词：sarcastically, ironically, subtly|反义词：directly, sincerely, openly|词性:副词
