dunce:/dʌns/
n.
笨学生；傻瓜；蠢人
a stupid person who has difficulty learning
例句：He was treated like a dunce in school. 他在学校被当成笨蛋对待。

例句：She felt like a dunce when she couldn't answer the simple question.  当她无法回答这个简单的问题时，她觉得自己像个傻瓜。

近义词：fool, idiot, imbecile, dolt, simpleton
反义词：genius, scholar, intellectual
词性：名词

词源:
From Middle English *dunse, from the name of John Duns Scotus (c. 1266–1308), a Scottish scholastic philosopher and theologian, whose once-respected work later became a subject of ridicule.  来自中古英语 *dunse，源于苏格兰经院哲学家和神学家约翰·邓斯·司各脱（约 1266-1308 年）的名字，他的作品曾经受人尊敬，后来却成为嘲笑的对象。
