outdare:/ˌaʊtˈdɛər/
verb
超越…的胆量；比…更大胆
To surpass in daring; be bolder than.
He outdared his rivals by investing all his savings in the new venture. 他比他的竞争对手更大胆，把所有的积蓄都投资于新企业。
Synonyms: outbrave, surpass, eclipse
Antonyms: cower, flinch, recoil
词性: 动词
