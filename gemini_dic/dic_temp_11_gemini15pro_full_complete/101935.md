isles:/aɪlz/| n. |岛屿；小岛|A group of islands, often used poetically or archaically. |例句：The British Isles are a group of islands off the northwest coast of Europe.  不列颠群岛是欧洲西北海岸外的一群岛屿。

例句：The ship sailed towards the fabled isles of the west.  船向西边传说中的岛屿航行。|近义词：islands, archipelago|反义词:continent, mainland|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *isle (Modern French île), from Latin īnsula “island”.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *enésos “island”.
