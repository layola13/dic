congratulatory:/kənˌɡrætʃəˈleɪtəri/ | adj. | 祝贺的，庆贺的 | Expressing or conveying congratulations. |  She gave him a congratulatory hug after he won the race.  她在他赢得比赛后给了他一个祝贺的拥抱。|  Example: He sent a congratulatory telegram. 他发了一封贺电。| Synonyms: celebratory, complimentary,  laudatory, felicitous | Antonyms: critical, disapproving,  condemnatory | word property: adjective
