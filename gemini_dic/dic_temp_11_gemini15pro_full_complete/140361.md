oil-bearing:/ˈɔɪlˌberɪŋ/
adjective
含油的；产油的
Containing or yielding oil.
例句：The oil-bearing rock is a valuable resource.  这块含油的岩石是一种宝贵的资源。

例句：Oil-bearing shale formations are being explored as a new source of energy.  含油页岩地层正在被勘探作为一种新的能源。

近义词：petroliferous, oleiferous
反义词: barren, unproductive
词性: adjective
