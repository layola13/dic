garnishers:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃərz/| n. |装饰物，配菜|A garnish is something that is added to a dish to improve its appearance or taste. Garnishers are the people who add these items, or the items themselves (plural).|The garnishers carefully placed sprigs of parsley on each plate. 装饰厨师们小心地在每个盘子上放上欧芹枝。|The chef instructed the garnishers to add a lemon wedge to each serving.  厨师指示装饰员在每份菜上加一片柠檬角。|近义词：decorators, adornments|反义词:|名词
