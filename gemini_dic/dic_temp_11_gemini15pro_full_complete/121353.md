misgracious:/mɪsˈɡreɪʃəs/
adjective
不亲切的，粗鲁的，没礼貌的
lacking graciousness or courtesy; rude and unpleasant.
He gave a misgracious reply to her polite question. 他对她的礼貌提问给出了一个不礼貌的回答。
近义词:rude, impolite, ungracious, discourteous, churlish
反义词:gracious, polite, courteous, civil

词性:adjective
