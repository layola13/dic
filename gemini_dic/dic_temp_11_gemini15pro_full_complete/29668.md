cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/| adj. n.| 宣泄的；净化心灵的；通便的|Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; relating to catharsis.|例句：Crying can be a cathartic experience. 哭泣可以是一种宣泄的经历。|近义词：cleansing, purifying, releasing, therapeutic|反义词：repressive, suppressive|词性:形容词

cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/| n.| 宣泄剂；通便药|A substance that accelerates defecation.|例句：He took a cathartic to relieve his constipation. 他服用了泻药来缓解便秘。|近义词：laxative, purgative|反义词：None|词性:名词
