lionizer:/ˈlaɪənaɪzər/| n. |奉承者，吹捧者|A person who lionizes someone treats them as a very important and famous person.|She had many admirers and lionizers. 她有很多仰慕者和吹捧者。|近义词：flatterer, adulator, sycophant|反义词：critic, detractor|noun

lionize:/ˈlaɪənaɪz/| v. |吹捧，奉承|To lionize someone means to treat them as a very important and famous person.|The press lionized him after his victory.  他获胜后，媒体大肆吹捧他。|近义词：extol, celebrate, glorify|反义词：criticize, condemn|verb
