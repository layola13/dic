isenthalpic:/ˌaɪsɛnˈθælpɪk/ | adj. | 等焓的 |  Occurring or operating without change of enthalpy.  A process is isenthalpic if the enthalpy remains constant. |  The throttling process is often modeled as isenthalpic.  节流过程通常被建模为等焓过程。

The expansion of a gas through a porous plug is approximately isenthalpic. 气体通过多孔塞的膨胀近似于等焓过程。 | 近义词: adiabatic (in some cases) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek "iso-" (equal) + "enthalpy" (heat content).
