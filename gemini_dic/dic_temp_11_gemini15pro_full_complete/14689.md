awaken:/əˈweɪkən/ /əˈweɪkən/| v. |唤醒，觉醒|To rouse from sleep; to cause to become aware or conscious of something.|The loud noise awakened me. 巨大的噪音把我吵醒了。|The experience awakened her to the realities of war. 这次经历使她意识到了战争的残酷现实。|近义词：rouse, arouse, waken|反义词：sleep, slumber|词性:动词
