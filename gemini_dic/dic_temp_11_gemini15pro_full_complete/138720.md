numbed:/nʌmd/| adj.|麻木的，失去知觉的|Deprived of sensation, especially of feeling or pain.|My fingers were numbed with cold. 我的手指冻麻了。|Synonyms: deadened, desensitized, dulled|Antonyms: sensitive, responsive|adjective

numbed:/nʌmd/| v.|使麻木，使失去知觉|(past tense and past participle of numb) To make numb; deprive of feeling or responsiveness.|The tragic news numbed her senses.  这悲惨的消息使她麻木了。|Synonyms: deaden, desensitize, dull|Antonyms: sensitize, stimulate|verb
