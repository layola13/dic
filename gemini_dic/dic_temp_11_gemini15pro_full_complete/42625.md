counterrevolution:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 反革命，反革命运动 | A revolution against a government established by a previous revolution. |  The counterrevolution was swiftly suppressed by the new regime.  反革命活动很快被新政权镇压了。|  The government accused the protesters of counterrevolutionary activities. 政府指控抗议者从事反革命活动。 | synonyms: rebellion, revolt, uprising, insurrection | antonyms: revolution, reform | noun

counterrevolutionary:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃənɛri/ | adj. n. | 反革命的，反革命分子 | Of, relating to, or characteristic of a counterrevolution; counterrevolutionary person. | The counterrevolutionary forces were defeated. 反革命势力被击败了。 |  He was labeled a counterrevolutionary and imprisoned. 他被扣上反革命的帽子并被监禁。 | synonyms: reactionary, rebel, insurgent | antonyms: revolutionary, progressive | adjective, noun
