fungia:/ˈfʌndʒiə/
n.
蕈珊瑚, 菌珊瑚, 盘珊瑚
A genus of large solitary mushroom corals.  These corals are characterized by their large, circular or oval shape, resembling a mushroom cap.
Divers observed a large fungia on the reef. 潜水员在礁石上观察到一块巨大的蕈珊瑚。
The fungia coral provided shelter for small fish.  这块蕈珊瑚为小鱼提供了庇护所。
synonyms: mushroom coral, plate coral, disc coral
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider colonial corals as a contrasting form)
