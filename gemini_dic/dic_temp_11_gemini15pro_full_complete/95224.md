inbred:/ˈɪnˌbrɛd/| adj.|天生的；根深蒂固的|Produced by inbreeding; innate.|He had an inbred sense of caution. 他有一种与生俱来的谨慎感。|She displayed an inbred distrust of strangers. 她对陌生人表现出一种根深蒂固的不信任。|inherent, ingrained, congenital|acquired|adjective
