conestoga:/ˌkɒnɪˈstoʊɡə/| n. |康内斯托加马车；|A broad-wheeled covered wagon used for hauling freight across the prairies of the United States, especially in the 19th century.  It was developed in the Conestoga Valley of Pennsylvania. |The conestoga wagons carried pioneers westward. 康内斯托加马车载着拓荒者向西迁移。|Synonym: covered wagon, prairie schooner |Antonym: N/A |noun

conestoga:/ˌkɒnɪˈstoʊɡə/| adj. |康内斯托加的；|Relating to or characteristic of the Conestoga wagon or the Conestoga Valley in Pennsylvania. |The conestoga horses were known for their strength and endurance. 康内斯托加马以其力量和耐力而闻名。|Synonym: Pennsylvanian (in some contexts) |Antonym: N/A |adjective
