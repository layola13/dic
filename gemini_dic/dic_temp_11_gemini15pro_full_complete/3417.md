aforetime:/ˌeɪfɔːrˈtaɪm/ | adv. | 以前，从前 | formerly; in the past |  In aforetime days, knights wore shining armor. 从前，骑士们穿着闪亮的盔甲。 |  In aforetime, people communicated through letters.  从前，人们通过信件交流。| 近义词: formerly, previously, earlier, once, anciently | 反义词: now, currently, presently, latterly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *aforetime*, equivalent to *afore* (before) + *time*.
