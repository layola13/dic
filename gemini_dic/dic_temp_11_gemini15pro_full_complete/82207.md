grote:/ɣroʊtə/ | n. | 荷兰盾的辅币单位（等于荷兰盾的1/100） | A subunit of currency in the Netherlands, equal to one hundredth of a guilder. |  This old coin is worth one grote. 这枚旧硬币值一个格罗特。| synonym: cent, penny | antonym:  guilder (in a broad sense, as a higher unit) | noun

grote:/ɣroʊtə/ | adj. | 巨大的，伟大的 (古语，现已很少使用) | Great; large; grand (archaic and rarely used). | He was a grote and powerful king. 他是一位伟大而强大的国王。 | synonym: great, large, grand, huge, enormous | antonym: small, tiny, insignificant | adjective
