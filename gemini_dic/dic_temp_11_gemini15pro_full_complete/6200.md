ammonia:/əˈmoʊniə/ | n. | 氨; 氨水 | A colorless, pungent gas composed of nitrogen and hydrogen (NH3), which is highly soluble in water and used in cleaning products and fertilizers. |  Ammonia is used in many household cleaning products. 氨被用于许多家用清洁产品中。|  The strong smell of ammonia filled the air. 空气中弥漫着刺鼻的氨水味。| synonyms: azane, volatile alkali | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun
