horologies:/həˈrɒlədʒiz/
|n.|计时器，钟表学|The study and measurement of time, or the art of making clocks and watches.  The museum boasts a fascinating collection of antique horologies.|A collection of timekeeping devices, especially clocks and watches. The horologies on display spanned centuries of craftsmanship.|近义词:clocks, watches, timepieces, chronometers|反义词:None (lack of a concrete opposite)|词性:Noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *horologium*, from Ancient Greek ὡρολόγιον (hōrológion, “instrument for telling the hour”), from ὥρα (hṓra, “hour, time”) + -λόγιον (-lógion, suffix forming nouns).
