co-assessor:/koʊ-əˈsɛsər/
n.
共同评估人；联合估价员
A person who assesses something jointly with another person.
The co-assessor will help determine the value of the property. 联合估价员将帮助确定房产的价值。
The lead assessor worked closely with the co-assessor to finalize the report. 首席评估员与联合评估员密切合作，最终完成了报告。
synonyms: joint assessor, fellow assessor
antonyms: sole assessor
