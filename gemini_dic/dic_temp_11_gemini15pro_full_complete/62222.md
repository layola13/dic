encrinitic:/ɛŋkrɪˈnɪtɪk/
adjective
海百合的；由海百合组成的
containing, consisting of, or formed from the fossilized remains of crinoids (sea lilies).
The encrinitic limestone was full of tiny fossils. 这块海百合灰岩充满了微小的化石。
Synonyms: crinoidal
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "non-fossiliferous" or describing rocks formed from different materials, e.g., "igneous," "metamorphic.")
词性: adjective
