fusspot:/ˈfʌsˌpɒt/ (UK) /ˈfʌsˌpɑːt/ (US)| n.|大惊小怪的人；小题大做的人；神经质的人|A person who is easily upset or worried about small things, especially about cleanliness or tidiness.|例句：My grandmother is a real fusspot about her house being clean. 我祖母对她房子的清洁非常挑剔。

例句：He's such a fusspot about his food – everything has to be just so. 他对食物太挑剔了——每样东西都必须恰到好处。

近义词：worrier, nitpicker, perfectionist|反义词：easygoing person, relaxed person|词性：名词
