bastardised:/ˈbɑːstədaɪzd/ | adj. | 杂交的，劣化的，窜改的 | Changed from its original form in such a way as to lower its quality or value; corrupted. | The architecture of the building has been bastardised over the years with ill-matched additions.  这栋建筑的结构多年来因不匹配的增建而被破坏。| Synonyms: corrupted, debased, degraded, adulterated, distorted | Antonyms: purified, refined, improved, enhanced | adjective

bastardize:/ˈbɑːstədaɪz/ | vt. |  使杂交，使劣化，窜改 | To change something from its original form in such a way as to lower its quality or value; to corrupt.  |  They have bastardized the language with slang and jargon. 他们用俚语和行话败坏了这门语言。 | Synonyms: corrupt, debase, degrade, adulterate, distort | Antonyms: purify, refine, improve, enhance | verb
