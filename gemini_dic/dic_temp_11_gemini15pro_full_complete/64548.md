erraticism:/ɪˈrætɪsɪzəm/
n.
反复无常；不稳定性；古怪行为
Unpredictable and inconsistent behavior or characteristics.
His erraticism made it difficult to rely on him. 他的反复无常让人很难信赖他。
近义词：unpredictability, inconsistency, capriciousness, volatility
反义词：stability, consistency, predictability
词性：名词

erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/
adj.
不稳定的；古怪的；飘忽不定的
Not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable.
The weather has been erratic lately. 最近天气变化无常。
近义词：unpredictable, inconsistent, irregular, capricious
反义词：stable, consistent, predictable, regular
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin erraticus (“wandering, straying”), from Latin errare (“to wander, to err”), from Proto-Indo-European *ers- (“to move, wander”).
