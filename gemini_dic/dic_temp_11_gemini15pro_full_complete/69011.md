fattened:/ˈfætn̩d/| v. |使…长肥，养肥，|Past participle of "fatten".  It means to make something become fat, usually an animal, by feeding it a lot.|The farmer fattened the pigs for market. 农民把猪养肥了以备出售。|The goose was fattened for Christmas dinner. 这只鹅被养肥了准备圣诞晚餐吃。|近义词：plumped, enriched, nourished|反义词:slimmed, thinned, starved|词性:动词(过去分词)
