intermetropolitan:/ˌɪntərˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/| adj.|大都市间的，城市间的|Relating to or connecting two or more metropolitan areas.|例句：Intermetropolitan trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，城市间贸易显著增加。

例句：The intermetropolitan highway system facilitates travel between major cities. 城市间高速公路系统促进了主要城市之间的交通。|近义词：interurban, regional|反义词：intracity, local|词性:形容词

intermetropolitan:/ˌɪntərˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/| n.|城市间交通|Transportation system connecting metropolitan areas.|例句：The intermetropolitan railway is a crucial part of the national transportation network. 城际铁路是国家交通网络的重要组成部分。

例句：Investment in intermetropolitan infrastructure is essential for economic growth. 对城市间基础设施的投资对经济增长至关重要。|近义词：interurban transport, regional transit|反义词：local transport, city transit|词性:名词
