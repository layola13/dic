bridge-building:/ˈbrɪdʒ ˌbɪldɪŋ/
adj.|桥梁建设的；建立联系的|The conference played a bridge-building role between the two countries.  这次会议在两国之间发挥了桥梁作用。|近义词：connective, unifying|反义词：divisive, separating|词性:形容词
