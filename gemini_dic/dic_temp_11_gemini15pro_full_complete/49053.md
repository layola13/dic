dehortation:/ˌdiːhɔːrˈteɪʃn/
n.
劝阻，规劝；阻止
the act of trying to persuade someone not to do something
The teacher's dehortation against cheating was ignored by some students. 老师劝学生不要作弊，但有些学生置之不理。
近义词：dissuasion, admonition, caution, warning
反义词：encouragement, urging, exhortation
