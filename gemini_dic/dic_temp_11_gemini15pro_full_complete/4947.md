aliturgical:/ˌeɪlɪˈtɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adj.
非礼拜式的，不符合礼拜仪式的
Characterized by the absence of or departure from established liturgical forms.
The Quaker meeting, being aliturgical, followed no prescribed order of service.  贵格会的聚会是非礼拜式的，不遵循规定的仪式程序。
The group's aliturgical approach to worship emphasized spontaneous prayer and personal reflection.  该团体非礼拜式的崇拜方式强调自发的祈祷和个人反思。
近义词:non-liturgical, informal, unstructured
反义词:liturgical, formal, ritualistic
词性:adjective
