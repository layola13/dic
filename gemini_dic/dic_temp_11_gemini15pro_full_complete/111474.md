load-bearing:/ˈləʊdˌbeərɪŋ/ | adj. | 承重的，负重的 |  Able to support weight; structurally important. | The load-bearing walls of the house were reinforced with steel beams. 房子的承重墙用钢梁加固了。| Example:  The central pillar is load-bearing, so don't remove it.  中心柱是承重的，所以不要拆除它。| Synonyms: supporting, weight-bearing, structural | Antonyms: non-load-bearing, decorative | adjective
