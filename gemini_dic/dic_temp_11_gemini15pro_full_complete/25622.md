bumptiousness:/ˌbʌmpʃəsnəs/| n.|傲慢，自大，狂妄|The quality of being bumptious; offensively self-assertive.|His bumptiousness alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事。|Synonyms: arrogance, conceit, haughtiness, pomposity, superciliousness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness|Noun


Etymology->
From bumptious + -ness.  Bumptious itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from bump + -tious (as in factious, captions).
