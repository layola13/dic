mutchnik:/ˈmʊtʃnɪk/| n.|媒人；婚姻介绍人；(尤指犹太教的)婚礼司仪|A person who arranges marriages or, especially, a master of ceremonies at a Jewish wedding.|例句：The mutchnik introduced the bride and groom to each other. 媒人介绍了新娘和新郎互相认识。

例句：The mutchnik kept the wedding ceremony lively and entertaining. 婚礼司仪使婚礼仪式热闹而有趣。|近义词：matchmaker, marriage broker, wedding MC|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
