一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He is going to school every day.  他每天去上学。
She is not going to the party tonight. 她今晚不去参加派对了。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am going to the library now. 我现在正要去图书馆。
They are going home after work. 他们下班后正要回家。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has gone to London. 他去了伦敦。（已不在说话者所在地）
She has gone to the market. 她去市场了。（已不在说话者所在地）

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been going to that restaurant for years.  我们多年来一直去那家餐馆。
They have been going to the gym regularly. 他们一直定期去健身房。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I went to the park yesterday. 我昨天去了公园。
She went to the movies with her friends. 她和朋友一起去看电影了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was going to the store when I saw him. 我正要去商店的时候，我看到了他。
She was going home when it started to rain. 她正要回家的时候，开始下雨了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had gone to the airport before I arrived. 在我到达之前，他已经去了机场。
She had already gone home when I called. 我打电话时她已经回家了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been going to that gym for a year before it closed.  在那家健身房关闭之前，我已经去了一年了。
They had been going to the same doctor for years. 他们多年来一直去看同一个医生。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be going to the beach next weekend. 我下周末要去海滩。
She will go to the concert tomorrow. 她明天要去听音乐会。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be going to the library this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我将要去图书馆。
They will be going home at 5 pm. 他们下午5点将要回家。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have gone to Europe twice. 到明年，我将已经去过欧洲两次。
She will have gone to college by then. 到那时，她将已经上大学了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been going to this school for two years. 到下个月，我将已经在这所学校上了两年了。
She will have been going to therapy for six months by then. 到那时，她将已经接受了六个月的治疗。

虚拟语气:
If I were going to the party, I would tell you. 如果我要去参加派对，我会告诉你的。
I wish I were going on vacation with you. 我希望我能和你一起去度假。

被动语态 (going 不用于被动语态，此处用go的被动语态做示例):
The meeting is being gone to by everyone.  (不自然) 会议上每个人都去了。（这种被动语态不自然）
The place was gone to by many tourists.  (不自然) 这个地方被很多游客去过。（这种被动语态不自然）

疑问句:
Are you going to the party? 你要去参加派对吗？
Where are they going? 他们要去哪里？

否定句:
I am not going to the party. 我不去参加派对。
He is not going anywhere tonight. 他今晚哪儿也不去。
