bimo:/ˈbaɪmoʊ/ | n. |双月; 两月一次的出版物; |A publication issued every two months. |例句：The bimo is published six times a year. 这份双月刊一年出版六次。

例句：The club publishes a bimo for its members.  俱乐部为其成员出版双月刊。 |近义词：bi-monthly publication |反义词: N/A |词性:名词
