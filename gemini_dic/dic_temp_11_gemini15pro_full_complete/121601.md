misordered:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrdərd/
adjective
混乱的，排列错误的
not arranged in the correct order
The books on the shelf were completely misordered.  书架上的书完全是乱七八糟的。
The files were misordered, making it difficult to find the right one. 文件排列错误，很难找到正确的文件。
近义词:disordered, jumbled, chaotic, disorganized
反义词:ordered, organized, arranged, sorted
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- + ordered, past participle of order.  由mis-（表示错误、不当）+ order（秩序）的过去分词构成。
