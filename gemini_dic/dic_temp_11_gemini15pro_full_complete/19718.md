bilirophon:/bɪˌlɪrəfən/
n.
Bellerophon, 比勒罗丰
A hero in Greek mythology who slew the Chimera.
例句：Bellerophon, with the aid of Pegasus, was able to defeat the Chimera. 比勒罗丰借助飞马打败了奇美拉。

例句：The story of Bellerophon is a cautionary tale about hubris. 比勒罗丰的故事是一个关于傲慢的警示故事。
近义词：hero, champion
反义词：villain, coward
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek Βελλεροφῶν (Bellerophōn), perhaps meaning "slayer of Belleros".  The exact meaning and origin of "Belleros" are uncertain.
