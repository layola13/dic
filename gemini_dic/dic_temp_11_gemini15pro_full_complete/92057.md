hyperproducer:/ˈhaɪpərprəˌdusər/ | n. | 超级生产者，过度生产者 |  Someone or something that produces a large amount of something, often exceeding normal or expected levels.  A hyperproducer can refer to an individual, a company, a country, or even a biological organism. |  The factory became a hyperproducer of microchips, flooding the market.  这家工厂变成了微芯片的超级生产者，充斥着整个市场。 |Synonyms: overproducer, high-volume producer, prolific producer | Antonyms: underproducer, low-volume producer | noun
