aptate:/ˈæpteɪt/ | adj. | 适应的，适合的 | Adapted, suited, or fit, especially by modification or change. Often used in biology to describe structures or organisms modified for a particular function. | Example: The bird's beak is aptate for cracking seeds. 这只鸟的喙适合嗑种子。| Example:  The aptate leaves of the cactus help it conserve water. 仙人掌适应性强的叶子帮助它保存水分。| 近义词: adapted, modified, suited, fit | 反义词: unadapted, unsuitable, unfit | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *aptatus, past participle of *aptare “to fit,” from aptus “fit, suitable.”
