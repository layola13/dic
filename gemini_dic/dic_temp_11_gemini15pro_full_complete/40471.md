词源：From Latin *contribut-, past participle stem of contribuere "to bring together, bestow, contribute," from com- "together" (see com-) + tribuere "to grant, bestow" (from PIE root *treb- "dwelling, settlement").

注意事项:  虽然contributer这个拼写可以被接受，但contributor是更常用的拼写。
