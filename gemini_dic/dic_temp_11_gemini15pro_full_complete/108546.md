lazily:/ˈleɪzɪli/| adv.|懒惰地，缓慢地|in a lazy manner; with a lack of effort or exertion|He stretched lazily and yawned. 他懒洋洋地伸了个懒腰，打了个哈欠。|She lazily flipped through the pages of the magazine. 她懒洋洋地翻着杂志。|近义词：indolently, sluggishly, idly|反义词：energetically, diligently, actively|词性:副词
词源: From late Middle English *lasely, from lasy +‎ -ly. Equivalent to lazy +‎ -ly.
