gizzard:/ˈɡɪzərd/| n. | 砂囊，胗 |A muscular, thick-walled part of a bird's stomach that grinds food, often with the aid of ingested stones.  A similar organ in other animals. |The chicken's gizzard helped it digest the tough seeds.| 近义词:crop, maw |反义词: N/A| noun
