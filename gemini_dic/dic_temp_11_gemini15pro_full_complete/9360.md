antiprotons:/ˈæntiˌprəʊtɒnz/ (UK) /ˈæntiˌproʊtɑːnz/ (US)| n. |反质子|A subatomic particle with the same mass as a proton but opposite charge.|Scientists at CERN have successfully trapped antiprotons.  欧洲核子研究中心的科学家们已经成功地捕获了反质子。|
Antiprotons are used in certain types of medical imaging. 反质子被用于某些类型的医学影像。|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) antimatter particle, subatomic particle|反义词: proton|词性:noun
