mwini:/ˈmwini/ | n. | owner, proprietor |  A Bantu word (primarily Chichewa, but also found in other languages like Chitumbuka and Chiyao) meaning "owner" or "proprietor". Refers to the person who possesses or has rights over something. | The mwini of the land allowed us to camp there.  土地的主人允许我们在那里露营。 | synonyms: owner, possessor, landlord, proprietor | antonyms: renter, tenant | noun
