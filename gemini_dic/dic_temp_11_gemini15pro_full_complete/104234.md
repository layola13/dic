jurisdiction:/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃən/ | n. | 管辖区域，管辖范围 | The police have jurisdiction over this area. 警察对这一地区拥有管辖权。| territory, area, domain |  exclusion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French juridicion, from Latin iurisdictio ("administration of justice, jurisdiction"), from ius ("law, right") + dictio ("saying, declaration"), from dicere ("to say").
