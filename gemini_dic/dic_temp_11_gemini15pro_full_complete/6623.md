anaemically:/əˈniːmɪkli/| adv.|贫血地，无力地，没有生气地|In a manner characterized by or suggesting anaemia; weakly, feebly, without vitality.|The economy grew anaemically last year, barely registering any gains. 去年经济增长乏力，几乎没有任何增长。|Examples:

The stock market performed anaemically, with only minor fluctuations. 股市表现低迷，只有小幅波动。

He delivered the speech anaemically, lacking energy and enthusiasm.  他演讲得有气无力，缺乏活力和热情。


|近义词：weakly, feebly, listlessly, sluggishly|反义词: robustly, vigorously, energetically|词性: adverb
