appraising:/əˈpreɪzɪŋ/
adjective
评估的，估价的
assessing the value or quality of something
The jeweler was appraising the diamond. 珠宝商正在评估钻石。
近义词：evaluating, assessing, judging
反义词：disregarding, ignoring
词性: adjective

appraise:/əˈpreɪz/
verb
评估，估价
to assess the value or quality of something
The realtor appraised the house at $500,000. 房地产经纪人估计这栋房子价值50万美元。
近义词：evaluate, assess, judge
反义词：disregard, ignore
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apreisier, from a- "to" + preisier "to prize," from Latin pretium "price, worth, reward."
