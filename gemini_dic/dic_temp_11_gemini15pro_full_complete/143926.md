outpromising:/ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/
|v.|许诺过多，承诺过高|Making promises that are difficult or impossible to keep. |The company outpromised and underdelivered, leading to customer dissatisfaction. 这家公司承诺过多却交付不足，导致客户不满。|He outpromised on the project deadline and now he's scrambling to catch up. 他对项目截止日期的承诺过高，现在正忙于赶工。|近义词：overpromising, overcommitting|反义词：underpromising|词性: 动词
