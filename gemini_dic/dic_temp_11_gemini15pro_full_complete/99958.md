interoperable:/ˌɪntərˈɑːpərəbəl/ | adj. | 可互操作的，可互相协作的 | Able to operate or work with other systems or products, especially in computers or technology. |  Example 1: The new software is interoperable with older versions. 新的软件可以与旧版本兼容。

Example 2:  It's important to ensure that different healthcare systems are interoperable. 确保不同的医疗保健系统能够互操作非常重要。 | Near synonyms: compatible, interconnectable, interchangeable | Antonyms: incompatible, proprietary, closed | word type: adjective
