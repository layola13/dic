conclave:/ˈkɒŋkleɪv/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnkleɪv/ (US)| n. |秘密会议；教皇选举会议|A private or secret meeting, especially of cardinals to elect a new pope. |The cardinals met in conclave to choose the next pope.  红衣主教们举行秘密会议选举下一任教皇。

The company held a conclave to discuss the merger. 公司召开秘密会议讨论合并事宜。|近义词：meeting, assembly, gathering, council|反义词：public forum|词性：名词
