incite:/ɪnˈsaɪt/| vt.|煽动，激起|To encourage or stir up (violent or unlawful behavior).|The politician's speech incited the crowd to riot. 这位政治家的演讲煽动了群众暴乱。|例句：The ringleader incited the mob to violence.  头目煽动暴徒进行暴力活动。

例句：His words incited a riot. 他的话引发了一场骚乱。

近义词：provoke, instigate, stir up, urge, encourage, goad, spur|反义词:deter, dissuade, calm, pacify|词性:动词
