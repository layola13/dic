litter:/ˈlɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|垃圾，杂物；一窝（幼崽）；（猫砂等）垫窝材料；乱丢垃圾| Rubbish such as paper, cans, and bottles that is left lying in an open or public place. |The park was covered in litter. 公园里到处都是垃圾。| synonyms: rubbish, trash, garbage, debris, waste | antonyms: cleanliness, tidiness | noun

litter:/ˈlɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v.|乱丢垃圾；产（一窝幼崽）| To spread across an area or place untidily.
