diverberation:/dɪˌvɜːrbəˈreɪʃən/
n.
混响，残响
The gradual decay of sound in an enclosed space after the source has stopped.
The diverberation in the cathedral made the music sound ethereal.  教堂里的混响使音乐听起来空灵。
