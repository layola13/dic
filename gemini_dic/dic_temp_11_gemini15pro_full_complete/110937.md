lippiness:/ˈlɪpinɪs/
n.
lip肿胀; 唇肿
swelling or inflammation of the lip.
He experienced some lippiness after being stung by a bee. 他被蜜蜂蛰后嘴唇肿了起来。

近义词: swelling, inflammation, puffiness
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示正常状态的词) normal, healthy

词性: noun
