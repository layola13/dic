desoldered: /diːˈsɑːdərd/ /diːˈsɒldəd/ | v. |  拆焊，卸焊 | removed (a component) from a circuit board by melting the solder | The technician desoldered the faulty chip from the motherboard.  技术员从主板上拆焊了故障芯片。 | Example 2:  The old capacitor was carefully desoldered and replaced with a new one.  旧电容器被小心地拆焊下来，换上了一个新的。 | 近义词:  removed, detached, unsoldered | 反义词: soldered | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + solder + -ed.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove."  "Solder" refers to the metal alloy used to join metallic surfaces.  "-ed" is a past participle suffix.
