ergonomic:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪk/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪk/| adj.| 人体工学的，符合人体工程学的|Relating to or designed for efficiency and comfort in the working environment.|This chair is ergonomically designed to support your back. 这把椅子符合人体工程学设计，可以支撑你的背部。|Example:  The ergonomic keyboard helped reduce wrist strain.  人体工学键盘有助于减少手腕疲劳。|Synonyms: comfortable, user-friendly, efficient|Antonyms: uncomfortable, inefficient|adjective


ergonomics:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪks/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪks/| n.| 人体工程学|The study of people's efficiency in their working environment.|The office was redesigned with ergonomics in mind. 办公室的设计考虑到了人体工程学。|Example: She's taking a course in ergonomics. 她正在学习一门人体工程学课程。|Synonyms: human factors, human engineering|Antonyms: N/A|noun
