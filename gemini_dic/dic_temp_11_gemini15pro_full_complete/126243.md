mycomelic:/ˌmaɪkəˈmɛlɪk/ | adj. | 菌根的，由真菌和植物根系共生的 | Relating to or characteristic of mycorrhiza, a symbiotic association between a fungus and the roots of a vascular plant. |  Mycomelic associations are crucial for nutrient uptake in many plant species. 菌根关联对许多植物物种的营养吸收至关重要。

The researchers studied the mycomelic network in the forest. 研究人员研究了森林中的菌根网络。


近义词: mycorrhizal | 反义词: non-mycorrhizal | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek "mykēs" (fungus) + "rhiza" (root).  源自希腊语 "mykēs"（真菌）+ "rhiza"（根）。
