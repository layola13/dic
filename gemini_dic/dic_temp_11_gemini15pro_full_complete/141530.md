oppone:/əˈpoʊn/ | v. | 反对，对抗 | To place opposite or against something; to resist or counteract. | He opponed the new policy with all his might. 他竭尽全力反对这项新政策. |  He opponed the motion. 他反对这项动议。 | 近义词: resist, oppose, counteract, withstand | 反义词: support, advocate, endorse | 词性: 动词

**词源:**

From Latin *oppōnere* (to place against, oppose), from *ob-* (against) + *pōnere* (to put, place).
