atmologist: /ˌætmɒlədʒɪst/
n.
大气学家，气象学家
A scientist who studies the atmosphere, its phenomena, and especially the weather.
例句：The atmologist predicted heavy rainfall for the next few days.  大气学家预测未来几天将有强降雨。

例句：The atmologist studied the formation of hurricanes.  大气学家研究了飓风的形成。

近义词：meteorologist, climatologist
反义词: N/A
词性：noun
