nonkosher:/ˌnɑːnˈkoʊʃər/ | /ˌnɒnˈkəʊʃə(r)/| adj.|非犹太教规定的; 不合犹太教规的; 不正当的，不合法的|Not conforming to the requirements of Jewish law, especially dietary laws; not proper or legitimate.|  The restaurant serves nonkosher food. 这家餐厅供应非犹太洁食。|Synonyms:  non-kosher, treif (mainly used for food), unkosher, improper, illegitimate, unsuitable, unacceptable|Antonyms: kosher, proper, legitimate, suitable, acceptable|adjective
