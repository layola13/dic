objurgatory:/ˌɒbdʒɜːˈɡeɪtəri/ /ˌɑːbdʒɜːrˈɡeɪtɔːri/
adjective
责骂的；斥责的；严厉批评的
Expressing strong disapproval; rebuking sharply; censorious.
The teacher delivered an objurgatory speech to the misbehaving students. 老师对行为不端的同学们进行了严厉的批评。
His objurgatory tone made it clear he wasn't happy. 他责备的语气表明他很不高兴。
Near Synonyms:reproachful, scolding, castigating, rebuking, admonishing, upbraiding
Antonyms:laudatory, praising, complimentary, approving
词性:adjective
