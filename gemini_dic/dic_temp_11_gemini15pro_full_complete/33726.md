circles:/ˈsɜːrkəlz/ | n. |圆圈；圈子；循环；界；（具有共同兴趣或职业的）群体|A closed curve every point of which is equidistant from a fixed point within the curve.  A group of people with shared interests or professions.|The children sat in a circle. 孩子们围成一圈坐着。| synonyms: rings, loops, cycles, groups, societies | antonyms: lines, squares, individuals | noun

circle:/ˈsɜːrkəl/ | v. |环绕；盘旋；画圈|To move around something in a circle.|Vultures circled above the carcass. 秃鹫在尸体上空盘旋。| synonyms: revolve, rotate, orbit, surround, enclose | antonyms: straighten, linearize | verb
