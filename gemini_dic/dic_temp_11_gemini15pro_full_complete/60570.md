elasticness:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsɪsnɪs/ | n. |弹性，弹力|The quality of being elastic; the ability of something to stretch and return to its original shape. |The elasticness of the rubber band allows it to be stretched without breaking.  橡皮筋的弹性使它可以拉伸而不至于断裂。|近义词：flexibility, resilience, springiness |反义词: rigidity, stiffness | noun

elasticness:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsɪsnɪs/ | n. |灵活性，适应性|The ability to adapt or change easily. |The elasticness of the company's policy allows them to respond quickly to market changes.  公司政策的灵活性使他们能够快速响应市场变化。|近义词：adaptability, plasticity |反义词: inflexibility, rigidity | noun
