nondilatable:/ˌnɒndaɪˈleɪtəbl/
adjective
不可扩张的
Not able to be dilated or expanded.
The pupil of the eye was nondilatable due to the medication. 由于药物的作用，瞳孔不可扩张。
The artery was found to be nondilatable, increasing the risk of stroke.  这条动脉被发现是不可扩张的，增加了中风的风险。

近义词:nonexpandible, inflexible, constricted
反义词:dilatable, expandable, flexible
词性:adjective
