outdebate:/ˌaʊt dɪˈbeɪt/
verb
胜过…的辩论；辩论中战胜
To defeat someone in a debate; to argue more skillfully than an opponent.
He outdebated his opponent with a clear and logical argument. 他以清晰而有逻辑的论证在辩论中战胜了对手。
The experienced politician easily outdebated the young challenger.  经验丰富的政治家轻松地在辩论中击败了年轻的挑战者。

近义词: outargue, defeat in debate, best in argument
反义词: lose to in debate, be defeated in argument
词性: verb
