ignores:/ɪɡˈnɔːrz/| v.| 忽略，忽视，不理睬 |  To refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard intentionally. | She ignores all my advice. 她无视我所有的建议。| He ignored the warning signs and continued driving. 他无视警告标志，继续开车。 |  neglects, overlooks, disregards | acknowledge, heed, notice | verb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *ignoren, from Old French ignorer, from Latin ignorare (“not to know, to be ignorant of”), from in- (“not”) + gnōscere (“to know”).
