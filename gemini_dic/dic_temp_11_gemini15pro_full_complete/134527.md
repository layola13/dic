nonlicentiate:/ˌnɒnlaɪˈsɛnʃɪət/ |n.| 未获许可者；未授予执照者 |  A person who has not been granted a license, especially in a profession or trade. | Example: The nonlicentiate was barred from practicing medicine.  这个未获许可者被禁止行医。| Example:  The board refused to allow the nonlicentiate to operate the heavy machinery.  委员会拒绝允许这个未获许可者操作重型机械。 | Near Synonyms: unlicensed person, unqualified individual, unauthorized practitioner | Antonyms: licensee, qualified professional, certified practitioner | noun
