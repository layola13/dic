misdrawn:/ˌmɪsˈdrɔːn/
adjective
支取错误的；写错的；透支的
incorrectly drawn, as a check or other financial document.  A check is misdrawn when the amount written in numbers doesn't match the amount written in words, or when there are insufficient funds in the account to cover the amount.
The check was misdrawn, and the bank refused to honor it.  这张支票写错了，银行拒绝兑现。
Synonyms: incorrectly drawn, wrongly written, overdrawn
Antonyms: correctly drawn, properly written
