europeanised:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənaɪzd/ | adj. | 欧洲化的 | Having adopted or been influenced by European customs, ideas, or styles. |  Many formerly colonized countries are now highly Europeanised. 许多以前的殖民地国家现在已经高度欧洲化了。 | Example: The city's architecture became Europeanised after centuries of trade with Western nations.  这座城市的建筑在与西方国家几个世纪的贸易之后变得欧洲化了。| Synonyms: Westernized, Occidentalized | Antonyms:  indigenized, localized | adjective

europeanised:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənaɪzd/ | v. | 欧洲化 | To cause something to adopt or be influenced by European customs, ideas, or styles. |  The influx of European tourists has Europeanised the local culture.  欧洲游客的涌入使当地文化欧洲化了。 | Example: The government's policies are gradually Europeanising the education system. 政府的政策正在逐渐使教育体系欧洲化。 | Synonyms: Westernize, Occidentalize | Antonyms: indigenize, localize | verb
