disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/ | vt. | 取出内脏，剖腹 | To remove the internal organs of a body, especially as a method of torture or execution. | The defeated general was disemboweled by his enemies.  战败的将军被敌人剖腹。 |  The serial killer disemboweled his victims. 连环杀手将他的受害者开膛破肚。 | synonyms: eviscerate, gut | antonyms: - | verb


disembowelment:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbaʊəlmənt/ | n. | 剖腹，取出内脏 | The act of disemboweling. |  The disembowelment of the victim was a gruesome sight. 受害者被剖腹的景象十分可怕。| The ritual disembowelment was a part of the ancient ceremony.  仪式性的剖腹是古代仪式的一部分。 | synonyms: evisceration | antonyms: - | noun


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal) + embowel (to remove the bowels of).  Ultimately from Latin *exinvolvere, from ex- (out) + involvere (to wrap up, envelop).
