mistitle:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪtl/
verb
错误地命名；给…加错标题
To give a wrong or inappropriate title to.
The book was mistitled "A History of the World."  这本书被错误地命名为“世界史”。
The report was mistitled, leading to confusion.  报告的标题错了，导致了混乱。
Synonyms: misname, mislabel
Antonyms: correctly title, aptly name
词性: 动词

mistitle:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪtl/
noun
错误的标题
A wrong or inappropriate title.
The mistitle caused some embarrassment.  错误的标题引起了一些尴尬。
It was an honest mistitle, not intentional.  这是一个无意的错误标题，并非故意。
Synonyms: wrong title, incorrect title, inappropriate title
Antonyms: correct title, apt title
词性: 名词
