hoverers:/ˈhʌvərərz/
n.
盘旋者；徘徊者；悬停者
People or things that hover.  A device designed to hover.
Drones are sometimes called hoverers because of their ability to stay in one place in the air.  无人机有时被称为悬停器，因为它们能够停留在空中一个地方。
The hoverers lingered near the buffet table.  那些徘徊者在自助餐桌附近徘徊。
近义词：floaters,  lingering, dawdlers
反义词：speeders, rushers
