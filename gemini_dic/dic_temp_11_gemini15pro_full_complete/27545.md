can-doism: /ˈkænˌduːɪzəm/ /ˈkænˌduːɪzəm/
noun
积极进取的态度;乐观主义;实干精神
An attitude or spirit of optimism, confidence, and willingness to tackle challenges.
Example: The team's can-doism helped them overcome numerous obstacles and complete the project on time.  该团队的积极进取精神帮助他们克服了重重障碍，按时完成了项目。

Example:  Her can-doism is infectious and inspires everyone around her. 她的乐观主义精神很有感染力，激励着她周围的每个人。
Synonym: optimism, positive attitude, go-getter spirit, proactive approach, initiative
Antonym: pessimism, negativity, defeatism, apathy
词性: 名词
