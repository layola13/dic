nonmanufactures: /ˌnɒnˌmænjuˈfæktʃərz/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˌmænjuˈfæktʃərz/ (US)
|n.| 非制成品；非工业品
|Goods that are not manufactured; typically agricultural products, minerals, or other raw materials.|
|The trade deficit was offset by a surplus in nonmanufactures, such as agricultural goods.| 非制成品，例如农产品，抵消了贸易逆差。
|The country's economy relies heavily on the export of nonmanufactures like crude oil and timber.| 该国经济严重依赖原油和木材等非制成品的出口。
|近义词: commodities, raw materials, primary products|
|反义词: manufactures, manufactured goods|
|词性: noun|


nonmanufacture: /ˌnɒnˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ (US)
|n.| 非制造业
|An industry that does not involve manufacturing; typically services, agriculture, mining, etc.|
|Employment in nonmanufacture sectors has been steadily increasing.| 非制造业的就业人数一直在稳步增长。
|The government is investing heavily in nonmanufacture industries like tourism and education.| 政府正在大力投资旅游和教育等非制造业。
|近义词: service industry, non-manufacturing industry|
|反义词: manufacturing industry, manufacturing|
|词性: noun|


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "manufacture" (from Latin "manu factura" - "making by hand")
