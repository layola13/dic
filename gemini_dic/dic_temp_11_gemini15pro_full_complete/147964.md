词源：over- + resoluteness.  "Over-"  prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Resoluteness" is derived from "resolute," ultimately from Latin "resolutus," past participle of "resolvere" meaning "to loosen, dissolve, resolve."  Therefore, "overresoluteness" literally means "excessive resoluteness."
