glops:/ɡlɑːps/ /ɡlɒps/
n.
黏糊糊的东西；一团团的东西
a soft, sticky, shapeless mass
He dropped a glop of ketchup on his shirt. 他衬衫上掉了一团番茄酱。

近义词: blob, lump, gob, dollop
反义词:  (No clear antonym for a shapeless mass)
