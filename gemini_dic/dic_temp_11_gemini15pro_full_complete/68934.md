fatalnesses:/ˈfeɪtəlnəsɪz/
noun
致命性；致命度；必死性
The quality or state of being fatal; the inevitability of death.
The fatalnesses of the disease shocked the medical community.  这种疾病的致命性震惊了医学界。
The fatalnesses of the situation was apparent to everyone.  局势的严重性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
synonyms: lethality, deadliness, mortality
antonyms: harmlessness, safety, innocuousness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From fatal + -ness. Fatal comes from the Latin word "fatalis" meaning "destined by fate."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
