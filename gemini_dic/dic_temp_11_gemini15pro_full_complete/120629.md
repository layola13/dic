mis-connecting:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛktɪŋ/
adjective
未连接的，连接错误的
Failing to connect properly or as intended.
The mis-connecting flight caused me to miss my meeting. 连接错误的航班导致我错过了会议。
misconnected, wrongly connected, improperly connected
correctly connected, properly connected
adjective
