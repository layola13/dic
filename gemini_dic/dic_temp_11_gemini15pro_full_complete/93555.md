imagineering:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.
想象工程；幻想工程
The creation of fantastic or futuristic designs or concepts, especially in the fields of entertainment and amusement parks.
Disney is famous for its imagineering, creating immersive and magical experiences for visitors.  迪士尼以其想象工程而闻名，为游客创造身临其境的神奇体验。
Walt Disney Imagineering is the creative force behind Disney theme parks.  华特迪士尼幻想工程是迪士尼主题公园背后的创意力量。
synonyms: creative design, innovative engineering, blue-sky thinking
antonyms: practical engineering, conventional design
noun
