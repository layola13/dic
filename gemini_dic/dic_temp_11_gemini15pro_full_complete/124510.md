mounded:/ˈmaʊndɪd/
adjective
堆起的；隆起的
formed into a rounded pile or heap; heaped up
例句：The mounded earth marked the site of an ancient burial.  堆起的泥土标志着一处古代墓葬的地点。

例句：The snow was mounded high against the walls. 雪堆积在墙边很高。
synonym: heaped, piled, stacked
antonym: flat, level
词性: adjective
