ossein:/ˈɒsiːɪn/| n. |骨胶原；骨质|The organic matrix of bone, consisting mainly of collagen. 骨骼的有机基质，主要由胶原蛋白组成。|Ossein is the main component of bone tissue. 骨胶原是骨组织的主要成分。|Synonyms: collagen, bone matrix|Antonyms: None|Noun
