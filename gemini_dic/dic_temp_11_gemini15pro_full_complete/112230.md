loosened:/ˈluːsənd/| adj.|放松的，松开的|Made less tight or firm; relaxed. |The screws were loosened, and the board fell off. 螺丝松了，木板掉了下来。|The regulations have been loosened recently. 最近法规有所放宽。|近义词：relaxed, slack, loose, unfastened, unbound|反义词:tightened, tightened, firm, secure|v.
