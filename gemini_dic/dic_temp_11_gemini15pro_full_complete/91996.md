hypernym:/ˈhaɪpərnɪm/| n.|上位词，上义词|A word that is more general than another word.  A hypernym encompasses a range of hyponyms (more specific words). For example, "fruit" is a hypernym of "apple," "banana," and "orange."|Fruit is a hypernym, and apple is a hyponym.|近义词：superordinate, generic term|反义词:hyponym, subordinate term|词性:noun
