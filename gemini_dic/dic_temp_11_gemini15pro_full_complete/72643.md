flysheet:/ˈflaɪʃiːt/
n.
防水布；天幕；帐篷外层
A sheet of waterproof material used to cover a tent.
We put up the flysheet to protect us from the rain. 我们搭起了防水布来挡雨。

The flysheet ripped in the strong wind.  防水布在强风中撕裂了。

近义词:tarp, outer tent, rain fly
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun
