outrushing:/ˈaʊtrʌʃɪŋ/
adjective
涌出的，向外奔流的
moving outwards with great speed and force.
例句：The outrushing tide carried the boat out to sea. 向外奔流的潮水把船带到了海里。
例句：An outrushing wave of relief washed over her. 一股如释重负的感觉涌上她的心头。
近义词：gushing, surging, streaming
反义词：inflowing, incoming
词性: adjective
