inherence:/ɪnˈhɪərəns/ | n. | 内在性，固有性，天赋 |The inherent quality of something is a natural part of it and cannot be separated from it. |  Beauty is an inherence of art.  美是艺术的内在属性。| synonyms: innateness, intrinsicness, naturalness | antonyms:extrinsicness, acquiredness | noun

inherent:/ɪnˈhɪərənt/ | adj. | 内在的，固有的，与生俱来的 | Existing as a natural or basic part of something. | Risk is inherent in mountain climbing.  登山有固有的风险。| synonyms: intrinsic, innate, inborn, congenital | antonyms: extrinsic, acquired | adjective
