emolument:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt/ (US) /ɪˈmɑːljʊmənt/ (UK)| n.|报酬，薪酬，津贴|A salary, fee, or profit from employment or office.|The emoluments of the CEO include a company car and a generous pension plan. 首席执行官的薪酬包括一辆公司配车和丰厚的退休金计划。|Synonyms: compensation, remuneration, salary, wages, stipend|Antonyms: loss, expense|noun

emolument:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt/ (US) /ɪˈmɑːljʊmənt/ (UK)| n.|（尤指官职的）收入， emoluments指固定收入外的额外收入和好处，例如免费住宿。|The emoluments of the president include a large house and staff.|除了高薪，总统的额外收入包括一栋大房子和一些服务人员。|Synonyms: perquisites, perks, benefits|Antonyms: deductions|noun
