noncontagion:/ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒən/ | n. | 非传染性；非接触传染 | The doctor assured her that the rash was a noncontagion and posed no threat to others. 医生向她保证皮疹不具有传染性，不会对其他人构成威胁。 | Noncontagion refers to the inability of a disease or condition to be spread from one person to another. 非传染性指的是疾病或病症无法在人与人之间传播。 | 例句：
The doctor confirmed the rash's noncontagion. 医生确认了皮疹的非传染性。
His illness was diagnosed as a noncontagion. 他的病被诊断为非传染性疾病。

| 近义词：noncommunicable, noninfectious | 反义词：contagion, communicable, infectious | 词性：名词
