intracisternally:/ˌɪntrəsɪˈstɜːnəli/
adjective
池内的，脑池内的
Located or occurring within a cistern, especially referring to the subarachnoid cisterns of the brain.
Intracisternal injections are rarely used in modern medicine. 脑池内注射在现代医学中很少使用。
The drug was administered intracisternally.  这种药物是通过脑池内注射给药的。
近义词: intrathecally (although this refers specifically to the thecal sac surrounding the spinal cord)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps extracisternal, but this is not commonly used)
词性: adjective

intracisternally:/ˌɪntrəsɪˈstɜːnəli/
adverb
在脑池内
In or into a cistern, especially the subarachnoid cisterns of the brain.
The drug was injected intracisternally.  这种药物被注射到脑池内。
The sample was collected intracisternally. 样本是从脑池内采集的。
近义词: intrathecally (with the same caveat as above)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adverb
