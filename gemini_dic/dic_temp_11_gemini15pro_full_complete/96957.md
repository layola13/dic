infamies:/ˈɪnfəmiz/| n.|恶行，丑行，声名狼藉|Extremely wicked or outrageous acts; a state of extreme dishonor.|His name will live in infamy. 他的名字将遗臭万年。|近义词：atrocities, barbarities, villainies, outrages|反义词：honors, glories|词性: 名词 (plural noun)
Etymology: From Old French *infamie* (modern French *infamie*), from Latin *infamia* ("ill repute, dishonor, disgrace"), from *in-* ("not") + *fama* ("fame, reputation").
