mesohippus:/ˈmɛsoʊˌhɪpəs/| n. |中马|An extinct genus of prehistoric horse, existing from the Eocene to the Oligocene epochs, considered a transitional form between the earlier Orohippus and the later Miohippus. It was larger than Orohippus and had three toes on each foot, with the middle toe being larger and bearing most of the weight.|Mesohippus played a key role in the evolution of the horse. 中马在马的进化中扮演了关键角色。|近义词: Miohippus (later relative), Orohippus (earlier relative)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Greek μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + ἵππος (híppos, “horse”).
