applaud:/əˈplɔːd/| v. |鼓掌，称赞，赞赏|To show approval or appreciation, especially by clapping the hands.|The audience applauded the performance enthusiastically. 观众热情地为演出鼓掌。|近义词：praise, commend, acclaim|反义词:criticize, condemn|词性:动词
applause:/əˈplɔːz/| n. |鼓掌，掌声|The showing of approval or appreciation by clapping the hands.|The applause was deafening after the final song.  最后一首歌结束后，掌声震耳欲聋。|近义词：acclamation, ovation|反义词:criticism, disapproval|词性:名词
