dietary:/ˈdaɪəteri/| adj.|饮食的；规定饮食的|Relating to the food someone eats.|She follows a strict dietary regime. 她遵循严格的饮食方案。|例句：Dietary restrictions can make it difficult to eat out.  饮食限制可能使外出就餐变得困难。

例句：He needs to make some dietary changes to improve his health. 他需要改变一些饮食习惯来改善健康状况。|近义词：nutritional, alimentary|反义词: None|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diētārius, from Latin diēta (“manner of living, diet”), from Ancient Greek δίαιτα (díaita, “manner of living, diet”).
