/ˈbænərˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像旗帜的；像横幅的 | Resembling a banner in appearance, size, or shape. | The clouds stretched across the sky, bannerlike and majestic.  云彩像旗帜一样雄伟地横跨天空。 | The long, bannerlike scarf flowed behind her. 长长的、像横幅一样的围巾在她身后飘动。 | similar, flag-like | dissimilar, small, compact | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "banner" + "-like." "Banner" comes from Old French "baniere," meaning "flag" or "standard," ultimately from Frankish "*bandwo," meaning "sign" or "token." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
