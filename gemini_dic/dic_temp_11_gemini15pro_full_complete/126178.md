mutule:/ˈmjuːtʃuːl/ | n. |滴水板；突出的方块|A projecting block under the corona of the Doric cornice, usually decorated on the soffit with guttae. | The temple's cornice was adorned with a row of regularly spaced mutules.  这座寺庙的檐口装饰着一排等距排列的滴水板。| synonym: modillion | antonym: N/A | noun

mutule:/ˈmjuːtʃuːl/ | n. |(比喻)突出物；装饰|Something that projects or resembles a projecting block. |The rocky outcrops on the cliff face looked like mutules against the sky. 悬崖面上的岩石突出物看起来像天空中的滴水板。 | synonym: projection, protrusion | antonym: recess, indentation | noun
