nontarnishing:/ˌnɒnˈtɑːnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
不变色的，不失去光泽的
Resistant to tarnishing; not becoming dull or discolored, especially by exposure to air or moisture.
This nontarnishing silver necklace will remain beautiful for years to come.  这条不变色的银项链在未来几年都会保持美丽。
Near synonyms: stainless, corrosion-resistant, untarnished
Antonyms: tarnishable, oxidizing, corroding
词性: adjective
