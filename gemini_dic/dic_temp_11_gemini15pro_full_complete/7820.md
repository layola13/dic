antagonist:/ænˈtæɡənɪst/
n.|对手，敌手；拮抗肌|A person who actively opposes or is hostile to someone or something.|The antagonist in the play is a cruel and ruthless king. 剧中的反派是一个残暴无情的国王.|opponent, adversary, foe, rival|protagonist, supporter, ally|noun

antagonists:/ænˈtæɡənɪsts/
n.|对手，敌手；拮抗肌（antagonist 的复数形式）|Multiple people who actively oppose or are hostile to someone or something.|The antagonists in this story are a group of powerful corporations.  这个故事中的反派是一群强大的公司。|opponents, adversaries, foes, rivals|protagonists, supporters, allies|noun
