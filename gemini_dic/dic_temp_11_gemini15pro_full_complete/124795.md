muba: /ˈmuːbə/ | n. | 木坝, 木制水坝 | A dam made of wood or timber, used to control water flow in rivers or streams. | Example: The muba was built by the villagers to irrigate their crops. 村民们建造了木坝来灌溉庄稼。| Example: The old muba was no longer strong enough to hold back the floodwaters.  旧的木坝已经不够坚固，无法阻挡洪水了。 | Synonym: dam, weir, barrage | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open channel") | noun
