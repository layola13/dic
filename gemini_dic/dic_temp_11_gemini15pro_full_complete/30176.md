celeriac:/səˈleriæk/ (US), /sɪˈleəriæk/ (UK)| n. |芹菜根；块根芹|A root vegetable, also known as celery root, with a knobbly, rough skin and a distinctive, slightly nutty flavor.  It is used in soups, stews, salads, and purees.|This soup is made with celeriac and potatoes. 这汤是用块根芹和土豆做的。| Celeriac remoulade is a classic French dish. 块根芹泥是一道经典的法国菜。|Synonyms: celery root, knob celery|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
