noncontemplativenesses: /ˌnɒnkɒntɛmplətɪvnəsɪz/
noun
非沉思性，不沉思的状态
The state of not being contemplative; lack of contemplation or reflection.
His noncontemplativenesses led him to make rash decisions. 他的不沉思导致他做出了草率的决定。
Near synonyms: thoughtlessness, rashness, impulsiveness
Antonyms: contemplativeness, thoughtfulness, reflectiveness
词性: Noun
