doxological:/dɒksəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adj.
赞美的，颂歌的
Relating to or expressing praise, especially to God.
The congregation sang a doxological hymn.  会众唱了一首赞美诗。
近义词：laudatory, praising, hymnic
反义词：blasphemous, critical, defamatory
词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek "doxologia" (δοξολογία), from "doxa" (δόξα, "glory, praise") + "-logia" (-λογία, "speaking, discourse").
