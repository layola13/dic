humanlike:/ˈhjuːmənlaɪk/| adj.|像人的，似人的|Resembling a human being in appearance, behavior, or characteristics. |The robot had a humanlike face and could even smile.  这个机器人有一张像人的脸，甚至可以微笑。|近义词：humanoid, anthropoid|反义词：inhuman, beastly|词性:形容词
词源: human + -like.  From 'human' meaning 'of or characteristic of people' and '-like' meaning 'similar to'.
