noneloquently:/ˌnɒnˈɛləkwəntli/
adverb
不雄辩地，不流畅地
lacking eloquence; in a way that is not fluent or persuasive.
Her speech was delivered noneloquently, failing to captivate the audience. 她的演讲很不流畅，没能吸引住观众。
noneloquently expressed his dissatisfaction. 他笨嘴拙舌地表达了他的不满。
Synonyms: inarticulately, haltingly, clumsily, awkwardly
Antonyms: eloquently, fluently, articulately, persuasively
词性: adverb
