isomers:/ˈaɪsəmərz/| n. |异构体|Two or more compounds with the same formula but a different arrangement of atoms in the molecule and different properties.|Glucose and fructose are isomers. 葡萄糖和果糖是异构体。|synonyms: isomerides|antonyms: N/A|noun
