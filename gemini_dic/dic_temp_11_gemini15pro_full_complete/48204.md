deconflict:/diːˈkɒnflɪkt/ /diːˈkɑːnflɪkt/
verb
消除冲突，避免冲突
To resolve or avoid conflicts, especially between different groups or organizations.
The two departments needed to deconflict their schedules to avoid overlapping projects. 两个部门需要协调他们的日程安排，以避免项目重叠。
Military planners worked to deconflict airspace to prevent accidental collisions. 军事规划人员努力协调空域，以防止意外碰撞。
Synonyms: coordinate, harmonize, reconcile, integrate
Antonyms: clash, collide, conflict
词性: 动词

deconflict:/diːˈkɒnflɪkt/ /diːˈkɑːnflɪkt/
noun
消除冲突
The process of resolving or avoiding conflicts.
The deconflict of the flight plans was successful. 飞行计划的冲突消除是成功的。
The meeting was held for the deconflict of responsibilities between the two teams. 这次会议是为了消除两个团队之间的责任冲突。
Synonyms: coordination, harmonization, reconciliation, integration
Antonyms: clash, collision, conflict
词性: 名词
