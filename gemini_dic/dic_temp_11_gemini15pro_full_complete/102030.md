isokinetically:/ˌaɪsoʊkɪˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 等速地，等动力地 |  Characterized by or producing movement at a constant speed, regardless of the force applied.  It refers to a type of muscle contraction where the speed of the muscle shortening or lengthening is constant throughout the range of motion. | The physical therapist had him perform isokinetic exercises to strengthen his knee. 物理治疗师让他做等速运动来加强膝盖力量。|  The isokinetic dynamometer measured the strength of his quadriceps. 等速测力计测量了他的股四头肌的力量。 | Synonyms:  at a constant speed, uniformly | Antonyms:  isotonically, isometrically | Word type: adverb
