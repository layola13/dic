applotment:/əˈplɒtmənt/
n.
分配；拨款；指定
The act of allotting or distributing something.  The portion allotted.
The applotment of land to the refugees provided them with a place to live.  难民获得的土地分配为他们提供了一个居住的地方。

The applotment of funds for the project was insufficient.  该项目的资金分配不足。

近义词: allocation, assignment, distribution, allotment
反义词: confiscation, seizure, withholding
