aerobic:/ɛˈroʊbɪk/ /eəˈrəʊbɪk/| adj.|有氧的，需氧的|Relating to, involving, or requiring free oxygen.|Aerobic exercise is good for your heart. 有氧运动对你的心脏有好处。|近义词：oxygen-requiring, oxidative|反义词：anaerobic|词性:形容词

aerobics:/ɛˈroʊbɪks/ /eəˈrəʊbɪks/| n.|有氧运动|Exercises that strengthen the heart and lungs by making the body use oxygen more efficiently.|She does aerobics three times a week. 她每周做三次有氧运动。|近义词：cardio, fitness training|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Greek *aēr* (ἀήρ) "air" + *bios* (βίος) "life".
