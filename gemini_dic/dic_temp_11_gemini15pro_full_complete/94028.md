imparipinnate:/ˌɪmˌpærɪˈpɪneɪt/
adjective
奇数羽状的
Having an odd number of leaflets or pinnae, with a single terminal leaflet.  A leaf that is pinnately compound and has an unpaired terminal leaflet.
The leaves of the ash tree are imparipinnate. 白蜡树的叶子是奇数羽状的。
The rose bush has imparipinnate leaves. 玫瑰丛有奇数羽状复叶。

synonyms:odd-pinnate
antonyms:paripinnate, even-pinnate
词性:adjective
