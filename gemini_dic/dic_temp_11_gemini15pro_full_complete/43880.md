crestfallenly:/ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlənli/| adv.|垂头丧气地，沮丧地|In a dejected or disheartened manner, typically with head bowed.  It describes the way someone acts or behaves when they are feeling disappointed and discouraged.|He walked crestfallenly out of the room after hearing the bad news.  他听到坏消息后垂头丧气地走出了房间。|Synonyms: dejectedly, despondently, dispiritedly|Antonyms: cheerfully, joyfully, happily|adverb
