frail:/freɪl/| adj.| 脆弱的；虚弱的；易碎的 | Easily broken or damaged; physically weak and delicate. |  The frail old woman needed help crossing the street.  这位虚弱的老妇人需要帮助才能过马路。| synonyms: delicate, fragile, weak, feeble | antonyms: strong, robust, sturdy | adjective

frails:/freɪlz/| n.| 篮子（尤指装干果或葡萄干的）| Baskets, especially those used for dried fruit or raisins. | The merchant displayed his wares in several frails. 商人把他的货物陈列在几个篮子里。| synonyms: baskets, hampers | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps containers designed for heavy items) | noun
