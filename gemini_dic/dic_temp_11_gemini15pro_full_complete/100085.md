interpone:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊn/| vt.|插入，干预，提出|To place between; to insert; to interject.  It also means to introduce or interject (a comment, question, etc.) during a conversation or discourse.|The lawyer interposed an objection. 律师提出了反对意见。|Synonyms: insert, interject, introduce, inject, interpose|Antonyms: remove, extract, withdraw|verb

interpone:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊn/| n.|插入，干预|The act of interposing or the condition of being interposed.|His interpone stopped the argument. 他的介入阻止了争论。|Synonyms: intervention, mediation, arbitration|Antonyms: inaction, noninterference|noun
