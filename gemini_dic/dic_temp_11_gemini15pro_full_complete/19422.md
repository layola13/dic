bickerers:/ˈbɪkərərz/
noun
争吵者，爱吵架的人
People who habitually argue or quarrel about petty matters.
The bickerers finally agreed to a truce.  争吵者最终同意休战。
The constant bickerers disrupted the peace of the neighborhood.  不停争吵的人扰乱了社区的安宁。
synonyms: arguers, disputants, quarrelers, squabblers
antonyms: peacemakers, mediators, conciliators
