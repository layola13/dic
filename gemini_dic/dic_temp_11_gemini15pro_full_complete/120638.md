mis-measures:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒərz/
|n.| 错误措施，失策 | Incorrect or ill-conceived actions or steps taken. | The government's mis-measures in handling the economic crisis led to widespread unemployment. 政府在处理经济危机时的错误措施导致了广泛的失业。| mismanagement, blunders, mistakes | correct measures, appropriate actions | noun


mis-measure:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒər/
|v.| 测量错误，错误估计 | To measure incorrectly or inaccurately; to misjudge or miscalculate. |  The tailor mis-measured the fabric, resulting in a poorly fitting suit.  裁缝量错了布料，导致西装不合身。| miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate | measure correctly, assess accurately | verb
