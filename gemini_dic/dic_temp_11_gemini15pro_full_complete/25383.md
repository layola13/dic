bulbo:/ˈbʌlboʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌlbəʊ/ (UK)| n. |球茎；球状物|A rounded, swollen part of a stem, often underground, that stores food and from which new plants can grow.  It can also refer to any bulb-shaped object. |The tulip bulbs should be planted in the fall.  郁金香球茎应该在秋天种植。

The end of the thermometer was bulbo-shaped. 温度计的末端是球状的。|近义词：bulb, corm, tuber|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) stem, root|词性: noun
