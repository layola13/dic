depopulate:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪt/ |v. |减少人口，使人口减少|To reduce the population of (a place), especially by a large amount or by force.|例句：The war depopulated the countryside. 战争使农村地区人口减少。

例句：Disease depopulated the island. 疾病使岛上人口减少。|近义词：evacuate, deplete, decimate|反义词：populate, inhabit|词性：动词

depopulation:/ˌdiːˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃn/ |n. |人口减少，人口稀少|A reduction in population.|例句：The depopulation of rural areas is a serious problem. 农村地区人口减少是一个严重的问题。

例句：The depopulation of the island was caused by disease. 岛上人口减少是由疾病引起的。|近义词：decline in population, population decrease|反义词：population growth, population increase|词性：名词
