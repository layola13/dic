listening: /ˈlɪsənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɪsənɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 聆听，倾听 | The act of paying attention to sound; the process of hearing something with thoughtful attention. |  She improved her English by listening to podcasts. 她通过听播客提高了英语水平。

| 近义词: hearing, attending, heeding | 反义词: ignoring, disregarding | 词性: noun (名词)


listening: /ˈlɪsənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɪsənɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |  倾听的， 注意的 |  A listening ear is always appreciated. 善于倾听的人总是受到赞赏。

| 近义词: attentive, receptive | 反义词: inattentive, uninterested | 词性: adjective (形容词)
