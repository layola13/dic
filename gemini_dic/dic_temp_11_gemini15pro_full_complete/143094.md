(Since "outbreathes" is not commonly used in phrases, these examples illustrate common usage of "breathe" combined with prepositions.)

breathe in: 吸气  - Breathe in deeply and exhale slowly. 深吸气，慢慢呼气。
breathe out: 呼气 - Breathe out through your mouth.  从嘴里呼气。
breathe deeply: 深呼吸 -  Breathe deeply to relax. 深呼吸放松一下。
breathe a sigh of relief: 松一口气 -  She breathed a sigh of relief when she heard the good news. 听到好消息后，她松了一口气。
out of breath: 喘不过气 - He was out of breath after running up the stairs. 跑上楼梯后，他上气不接下气。
