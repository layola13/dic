forbearer's:/fɔːrˈbɛrərz/
|n.|祖先的，先辈的|belonging to or associated with a forbear (ancestor)|The forbearer's wisdom guided their decisions.  先辈的智慧指引着他们的决定。|
|近义词：ancestor's, forefather's|
|反义词：descendant's|
|词性：possessive noun (所有格名词)|
|词源：From "forbear" (ancestor) + "-'s" (possessive suffix).  "Forbear" comes from Old English "foreberan" (to endure, tolerate), later evolving to mean "ancestor."|
