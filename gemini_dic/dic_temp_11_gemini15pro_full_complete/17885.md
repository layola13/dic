beelzebub:/biːˈɛlzɪbʌb/ /biːlˈzɛbəb/| n. |别西卜，魔王|A name for the devil; Satan.  Derived from a Philistine deity, originally called Baal-zebub, meaning "lord of the flies."  It later became associated with Satan in Christian theology. |The preacher warned against the influence of Beelzebub. 传教士警告人们要提防别西卜的影响。

The exorcist claimed the man was possessed by Beelzebub. 驱魔人声称这个人被别西卜附身了。|近义词：Satan, Devil, Lucifer|反义词:God|词性:noun
