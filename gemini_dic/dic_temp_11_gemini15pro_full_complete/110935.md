lippier:/ˈlɪpiər/| adj.|更爱顶嘴的，更爱争辩的|More inclined to be impudent or disrespectful, especially in speech; more talkative in a sassy or cheeky way.  Comparative form of "lippy".|She's gotten a lot lippier since she started high school. 她自从上了高中以后变得更爱顶嘴了。|例句：He’s much lippier than his brother. 他比他哥哥更爱顶嘴。|近义词：impudent, sassy, cheeky, impertinent, disrespectful|反义词：respectful, polite, obedient, submissive|词性:形容词

lippier:/ˈlɪpiər/| adj.|嘴唇更丰满的|Having fuller or more prominent lips (less common usage). Comparative form of "lippy".|She looks different with lippier lipstick. 她涂了让嘴唇显得更丰满的口红，看起来不一样了。|例句：The actress had noticeably lippier lips after the cosmetic procedure.  这位女演员在整容手术后嘴唇明显变得更丰满了。|近义词：full-lipped, plump-lipped|反义词：thin-lipped|词性:形容词
