abysm:/əˈbɪzm/| n.|深渊，无底洞；地狱；混乱|A bottomless gulf or pit; a vast, immeasurable depth.  A state of profound or extreme misery or despair.  Chaos.|The climbers stared into the abysm. 攀登者凝视着深渊。|Synonyms: chasm, gorge, abyss, void, gulf, precipice|Antonyms: summit, peak, pinnacle|noun

abysmal:/əˈbɪzməl/| adj.|深渊的；极坏的；糟透的|Extremely bad; appalling.  Immeasurably deep.|The movie was abysmal. 这部电影糟透了。|Synonyms: dreadful, terrible, awful, appalling, atrocious|Antonyms: excellent, superb, wonderful|adjective
