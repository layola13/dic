declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/
verb
慷慨陈词；朗诵；演讲；谴责
to speak aloud in a rhetorical or impassioned manner, as if to an audience; to inveigh against or denounce publicly.
The actor declaimed his lines with great passion. 演员充满激情地朗诵着他的台词。
recite, orate, rant, inveigh
whisper, mutter
v.
