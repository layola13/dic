cothurnated:/kəˈθɜːrneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 穿高底靴的；悲壮的，夸张的 |  Wearing cothurni (high, thick-soled boots worn by tragic actors in ancient Greek theatre); elevated in style or tone; pompous or bombastic.  | The actor, cothurnated and masked, delivered his lines in a booming voice. 演员穿着高底靴，戴着面具，用洪亮的声音念着台词。 | 近义词:stilted, pompous, bombastic, inflated | 反义词:natural, simple, unpretentious | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *cothurnatus*, past participle of *cothurnare* "to wear cothurni," from *cothurnus* "buskin, tragic boot," from Greek *kothornos*.
