landslide:/ˈlændslaɪd/| n.|山体滑坡，压倒性胜利|A sudden and rapid movement of a mass of earth or rock down a slope; an overwhelming victory.|例句：The heavy rain caused a landslide that blocked the road.  暴雨引发了山体滑坡，阻塞了道路。

例句：The election resulted in a landslide victory for the ruling party. 选举结果是执政党取得压倒性胜利。|近义词：rockslide, avalanche, mudslide, rout|反义词:close contest, near defeat|noun
