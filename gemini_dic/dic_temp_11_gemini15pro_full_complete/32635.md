chisel-like:/ˈtʃɪzlˌlaɪk/| adj.|凿子般的；像凿子一样的|Resembling a chisel, especially in being sharp and pointed.|The bird had a chisel-like beak. 这只鸟的喙像凿子一样。|近义词：sharp, pointed, wedge-shaped|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:形容词
词源: From 'chisel' + '-like'. 'Chisel' comes from Old French 'cisel', from Vulgar Latin 'cisellum', diminutive of Latin 'caesum', past participle of 'caedere' (to cut).
