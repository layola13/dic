interpolar:/ˌɪntərˈpəʊlə(r)/ /ˌɪntərˈpoʊlər/ | adj. | 内插的，插值的 | Situated between two others; interpolated.  Relating to or involving interpolation. | Example: An interpolar position between two extremes.  在两个极端之间的中间位置。

Example: Interpolating data points to create a smoother curve.  插入数据点以创建更平滑的曲线。 | 近义词: interpolated, intervening | 反义词: extrapolated | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *interpolāre*, from inter- ‘between’ + polīre ‘to polish, refine’.
