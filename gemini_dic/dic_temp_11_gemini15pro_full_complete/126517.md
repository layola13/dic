naafi:/ˈnɑːfi/| adj.|有益的，有利的|Beneficial; advantageous; helpful.  Derived from the Swahili word "naafi" meaning "useful" or "profitable".  Originally used in East Africa in the context of military installations and services.|The naafi shop provided affordable goods for the soldiers.  军队福利社为士兵们提供价格合理的商品。|Synonyms: beneficial, advantageous, helpful, profitable, useful|Antonyms: detrimental, harmful, disadvantageous, unprofitable|adjective
