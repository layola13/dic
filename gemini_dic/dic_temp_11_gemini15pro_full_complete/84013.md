half-doors:/ˌhɑːf ˈdɔːrz/ | n. | 半门 | Doors that are divided horizontally in the middle, allowing the top half to be opened independently of the bottom half. |  The Jeep Wrangler is known for its half-doors, perfect for off-roading. 吉普牧马人以其半门而闻名，非常适合越野驾驶。

The farmer leaned on the bottom half of the half-doors and watched the sunset. 农夫倚在半门的下半部分，看着日落。 | 近义词: split doors, Dutch doors | 反义词: full doors | 词性: 名词
