baptizements: /bæpˈtɪzmənts/ | n. | 洗礼(仪式) |  The act of baptizing; a Christian sacrament marked by ritual washing with water, symbolizing purification and admission to the Christian Church.  It is derived from the Greek word "baptisma," meaning "immersion" or "washing." |  The baptizements took place in the river.  这些洗礼仪式在河里举行。|  The church held several baptizements on Sunday.  教堂在星期天举行了多次洗礼。 | synonyms:  baptisms, christenings | antonyms:  desecrations, profanations | noun
