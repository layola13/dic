brachycatalectic: /ˌbrækiˌkætəˈlɛktɪk/
| adj. | 短缺音节的；诗行末尾缺音节的 |  A line of verse lacking one or two syllables in the final foot. | The poem employed a brachycatalectic line for dramatic effect. 这首诗运用了一种短缺音节的诗行来达到戏剧性的效果。| Near Synonyms: catalectic, truncated | Antonyms: hypercatalectic, acatalectic | word property: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *brachykatalektikos*, from *brachys* "short" + *katalēktikos* "stopping short," from *katalēgein* "to leave off."
