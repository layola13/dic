getaways:/ˈɡɛtəˌweɪz/
n.
假期, 短途旅行, 逃跑, 逃亡
A short holiday or vacation, often to a place that is relaxing or different from one's usual environment; an escape or flight.
We're planning a weekend getaway to the mountains. 我们计划周末去山上度假。
The thieves made their getaway in a stolen car. 小偷们开着偷来的车逃跑了。
近义词:vacations, holidays, breaks, escapes, flights
反义词:homecomings, returns, arrivals
词性:名词(plural)
