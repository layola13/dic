antipoetically:/ˌæntiˌpəʊˈetɪkli/
adverb
反诗意地，缺乏诗意地
In a way that lacks poetic qualities; unpoetically.  Expressed in a way that is not poetic or romantic.
He described the beauty of the sunset antipoetically, focusing on the scientific explanation of the colors. 他反诗意地描述了日落的美丽，专注于色彩的科学解释。

The politician, known for his dry wit, described the budget crisis antipoetically, comparing it to balancing a checkbook.  这位以冷幽默闻名的政治家反诗意地描述了预算危机，将其比作平衡支票簿。

Synonym: prosaically, unromantically, matter-of-factly, plainly
Antonym: poetically, lyrically, romantically
词性: 副词
