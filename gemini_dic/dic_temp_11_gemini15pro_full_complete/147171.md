overlubricated:/ˌoʊvərˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度润滑的
lubricated excessively; having too much lubricant.
The gears were overlubricated, causing them to slip.  齿轮过度润滑，导致它们打滑。
The chain was so overlubricated that it flung grease everywhere. 链条过度润滑，以至于到处都是油脂。
synonyms: excessively lubricated, overly greased
antonyms: underlubricated, dry, insufficiently lubricated
word type: adjective
