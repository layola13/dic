displeasedness:/dɪsˈpliːzdnəs/| n. |不悦，不高兴|The state of being displeased; displeasure.|Her displeasedness was evident in her furrowed brow. 她的不悦从她紧皱的眉头中显露出来。|近义词：displeasure, dissatisfaction, annoyance|反义词：pleasure, satisfaction, contentment|词性:名词

Etymology:
From displeased + -ness.  Displeased itself comes from the prefix dis- (expressing negation) + pleased (from the verb please).  The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
