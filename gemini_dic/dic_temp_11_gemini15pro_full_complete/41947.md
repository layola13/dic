cosmopolitism:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtɪzəm/ | n. | 世界主义；世界性；四海一家 | The ideology that all human beings belong to a single community, based on a shared morality. |  New York is known for its cosmopolitism, embracing people and cultures from all over the world. 纽约以其世界主义而闻名，它包容来自世界各地的人们和文化。

Example: Her cosmopolitism was evident in her diverse group of friends and her interest in international affairs. 她的世界主义体现在她多元化的朋友圈和她对国际事务的兴趣上。

Synonyms:  internationalism, globalism, universality, multiculturalism
Antonyms:  nationalism, parochialism, insularity, provincialism
Word type: Noun
