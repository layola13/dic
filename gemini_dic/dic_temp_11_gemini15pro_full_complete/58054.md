dromatherium:/ˌdroʊməˈθɪəriəm/| n. |跑兽；锥齿兽|An extinct, shrew-like early mammal of the Late Triassic and Early Jurassic periods, considered to be one of the earliest known mammals.|Dromatherium was a small, insectivorous animal that lived alongside the dinosaurs. 跑兽是一种小型食虫动物，与恐龙生活在一起。|Near synonyms:  Morganucodon, Kuehneotherium|Antonyms:  N/A (extinct animal - no direct antonym)|noun
