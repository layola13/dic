overdilates:/ˌoʊvərˈdaɪˌleɪts/ | v. |过度扩张|To dilate excessively; to expand beyond normal or healthy limits. |The drug overdilates the pupils.  这种药会使瞳孔过度扩张。| High doses of the medication can overdilate blood vessels, leading to dizziness.  高剂量的药物会导致血管过度扩张，从而导致头晕。 |Synonyms: overexpand, distend excessively|Antonyms: constrict, contract|verb
