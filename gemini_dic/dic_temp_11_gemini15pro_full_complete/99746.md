intermeasurable:/ˌɪntərˈmeʒərəbl/
adjective
可互相测量的，彼此可比较的
Able to be measured in terms of each other; commensurable.  Two quantities are intermeasurable if one can be expressed as a multiple of the other.
The two lengths are intermeasurable because one is exactly twice the other. 这两个长度是可以互相测量的，因为一个是另一个的两倍。
interchangeable, comparable, commensurable
incommensurable, disparate, dissimilar
adjective
