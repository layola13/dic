dowy:/ˈdaʊi/| adj.|被露水打湿的；像露水一样新鲜的；柔和的；温顺的|Covered with or resembling dew; fresh as dew; gentle and meek. |The dewy grass glistened in the morning sun.  清晨的阳光下，沾满露水的草地闪闪发光。|近义词：dewy, moist, fresh, gentle, mild|反义词：dry, arid, harsh, severe|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dewy*, from Old English *dēawig* (“dewy”), from Proto-Germanic *dauwijaz (“dewy”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₁- (“dew”). Cognate with Dutch dauwig (“dewy”), German tauig (“dewy”), Icelandic döggvaður (“dewy”).
