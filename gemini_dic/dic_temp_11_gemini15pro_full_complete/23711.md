戴着黄铜帽的；高级军官的；高级官员的
Wearing a brass hat (figuratively, holding a high military rank); relating to or characteristic of high-ranking military officers.
The brasshatted generals made the decision to invade.  戴着黄铜帽的将军们做出了入侵的决定。
The brasshatted decision was met with resistance from the lower ranks. 高级军官的决定遭到了下级军官的抵制。
Synonyms: high-ranking, top-brass, senior
Antonyms: low-ranking, junior, subordinate
