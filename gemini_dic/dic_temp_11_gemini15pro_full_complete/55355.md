distance-education:/ˈdɪstəns ɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|远程教育|Education that takes place without the student being physically present in a traditional classroom setting. It often involves online learning, correspondence courses, and other forms of remote instruction.|Many universities now offer distance-education programs. 许多大学现在都提供远程教育课程。|E-learning, online learning, correspondence courses|Traditional education, face-to-face learning|noun
distance-learning:/ˈdɪstəns ˈlɜːrnɪŋ/ |n.|远程学习|The process of learning remotely, often through online platforms and resources.|Distance-learning allows students to study from anywhere in the world. 远程学习让学生可以从世界任何地方学习。|Online learning, e-learning, remote learning|Traditional learning, classroom learning|noun
