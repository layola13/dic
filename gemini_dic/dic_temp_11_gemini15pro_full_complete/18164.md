bejewel:/bɪˈdʒuːəl/
verb
用珠宝装饰;镶嵌宝石
To decorate or adorn something with jewels.
The queen's crown was bejeweled with diamonds and rubies. 女王的王冠上镶嵌着钻石和红宝石。
Her fingers were bejeweled with rings. 她的手指上戴满了宝石戒指。
adorn, decorate, ornament, embellish
strip, denude
v.
