misborn:/ˌmɪsˈbɔːrn/
adjective
生而不幸的；天生有缺陷的；畸形的
born with a physical defect; deformed.
The misborn child required extensive medical care.  这个天生有缺陷的孩子需要大量的医疗护理。
Synonyms: deformed, malformed, defective, imperfect
Antonyms: perfect, healthy, normal
词性: 形容词
