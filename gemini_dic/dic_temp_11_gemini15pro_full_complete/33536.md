ciderish:/ˈsaɪdərɪʃ/| adj.|似苹果酒的；微酸的；微醉的|Resembling cider in taste, smell, or effect; slightly sour; mildly intoxicated.|The drink had a ciderish tang to it.  这饮料有点苹果酒的味道。|近义词：tart, tangy, vinous|反义词:sweet, bland|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "cider" + "-ish" (suffix indicating resemblance or slight degree).  源自 "cider"（苹果酒）+ "-ish"（后缀，表示相似或轻微程度）。
