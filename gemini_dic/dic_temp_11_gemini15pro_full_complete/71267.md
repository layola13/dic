fix:/fɪks/| v. n.|修理，固定；确定，决定；准备，安排|To repair something that is broken or not working properly; to make something stable or firm; to establish something firmly; to prepare something, especially food.|I need to fix my bike. 我需要修理我的自行车。|近义词：repair, mend, secure, fasten, settle, decide, prepare, arrange|反义词：break, loosen, unsettle|verb

fix:/fɪks/| n.|困境，窘境；贿赂，非法操纵|A difficult situation; an act of bribery or illegal manipulation.|He's in a bit of a fix. 他有点进退两难。|近义词：predicament, dilemma, bribe|反义词：solution, advantage|noun
