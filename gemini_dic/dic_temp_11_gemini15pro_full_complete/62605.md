enfiled:/ɪnˈfaɪld/
verb
成串排列，排成一列，（尤指士兵）排成纵队
placed in a line, especially of soldiers
The troops were enfiled along the narrow road. 部队沿着狭窄的道路排成一列。
近义词：aligned, lined up, in formation
反义词：scattered, dispersed
词性：v.

enfiled:/ɪnˈfaɪld/
adjective
成串排列的，排成一列的
arranged in a line
The enfiled soldiers presented a formidable sight. 排成一列的士兵展现出一幅令人生畏的景象。
近义词：aligned, lined up, in formation
反义词：scattered, dispersed
词性：adj.

词源:
From en- + file (v.) "arrange in a line," from Old French filer "to string on a thread, arrange in a row"
