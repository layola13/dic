metagenesis:/ˌmɛtəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. | 世代交替 | The alternation of generations, especially the alternation of a sexual phase and an asexual phase in the life cycle of an organism. |  The jellyfish exhibits metagenesis, alternating between a polyp and medusa stage. 水母表现出世代交替，在水螅体和水母体阶段之间交替。 | alternation of generations | - | noun
