misshipped:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpt/
Verb
错运，误运
Shipped to the wrong destination.
The package was misshipped to California instead of Colorado. 包裹被错运到加利福尼亚州而不是科罗拉多州。
Near Synonyms: misrouted, wrongly shipped, mistakenly delivered
Antonyms: correctly shipped, delivered as intended
词性: 动词
