interlude:/ˈɪntərluːd/| n.|插曲，间奏；幕间休息；暂时中断|A piece of music played between the parts of a drama, movie, etc., or a short period of time between the parts of a performance, event, activity, etc.|The musical interlude provided a welcome break from the intense drama. 幕间演奏的音乐让紧张的戏剧得到了片刻喘息。|synonyms: break, interval, pause, respite|antonyms: continuation, prolongation|noun
