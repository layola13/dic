hypersocial:/ˌhaɪpərˈsoʊʃəl/| adj.|过度社交的，超社交的|Excessively or unusually sociable; marked by heightened sociability.|例句：Hypersocial individuals often thrive in group settings. 过度社交的人通常在群体环境中茁壮成长。

例句：Her hypersocial personality made her the life of the party. 她过度社交的性格使她成为派对的焦点。|近义词：gregarious, outgoing, sociable, extroverted|反义词：antisocial, introverted, withdrawn, reclusive|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + social.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive," derived from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond). "Social" comes from Latin "socialis" (pertaining to allies, social), from "socius" (companion, ally).
