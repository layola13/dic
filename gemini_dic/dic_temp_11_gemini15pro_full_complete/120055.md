millrind:/ˈmɪlrɪnd/
n.
磨坊的废料，谷糠层
the outer, coarse part of grain removed in milling.
The millrind was used as animal feed. 磨坊的废料被用作动物饲料。
近义词：bran, chaff
反义词：flour, endosperm
词性：名词
