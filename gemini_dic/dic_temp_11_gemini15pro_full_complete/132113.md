noncriticizings: /nɑːnˈkrɪtɪˌsaɪzɪŋz/ | n. | 不批评，不批判（的行为） |  The act of refraining from criticism or finding fault.  It refers to instances where one chooses not to express negative judgments or disapproval, even when they might feel it's warranted. |  His noncriticizings of her performance surprised everyone, given his usual high standards.  他不批评她的表现让每个人都很惊讶，因为他通常要求很高。| 近义词: tolerance, acceptance, forbearance | 反义词: criticisms, condemnations | 词性: 名词 (通常用作复数)

词源:  Derived from the adjective "noncritical" (not critical) with the addition of the suffix "-izing" to form a gerund, signifying the act of being noncritical.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not," and "critical" ultimately comes from the Greek word "kritikos" meaning "able to judge or discern."
