glass-jawed: /ˈɡlæsˌdʒɔːd/ | adj. | 玻璃下巴的，意志薄弱的 | Easily knocked out or discouraged; lacking resilience.  Having a weak chin, susceptible to being knocked out in boxing. |  He's a glass-jawed fighter; one punch and he's down. 他是一个玻璃下巴的拳击手，一拳就倒下了。|  The team was glass-jawed and folded under pressure.  这支队伍意志薄弱，在压力下崩溃了。| 近义词:fragile, vulnerable, weak, timid | 反义词: resilient, tough, strong, resolute | 词性: 形容词
