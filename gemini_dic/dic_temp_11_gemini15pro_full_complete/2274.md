adenitis:/ˌædɪˈnaɪtɪs/
n.
腺炎
Inflammation of a gland.
The doctor diagnosed her with adenitis after feeling the swollen glands in her neck. 医生摸了摸她脖子上的肿胀腺体后，诊断她患有腺炎。
近义词:glandular swelling, inflammation of the glands
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider health or absence of inflammation)
