flanking:/ˈflæŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 侧翼的，包抄的 |  Situated on or directed toward the flank; attacking from or protecting the flank.  | The enemy was flanking us on both sides. 敌人从两侧包抄我们。 | flanking maneuvers were employed to surround the enemy.  运用包抄战术包围敌人。|  近义词：surrounding, enveloping, outflanking | 反义词：frontal, head-on | 词性：adjective

flanking:/ˈflæŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 包抄，侧翼攻击 | The act of flanking, or attacking from the side. | The flanking maneuver was successful. 包抄行动很成功。| The general ordered a flanking attack. 将军下令进行侧翼攻击。| 近义词：envelopment, outflanking | 反义词：frontal attack | 词性：noun
