nonshrink:/ˈnɒnʃrɪŋk/ (British) /ˈnɑːnʃrɪŋk/ (American)| adj.|防缩的，不缩水的|Treated or made so as not to shrink.  Fabric that is specially treated so that it doesn't shrink when washed.|This shirt is made of nonshrink cotton. 这件衬衫是用防缩棉布做的。|The nonshrink wool is perfect for making sweaters. 这种不缩水的羊毛非常适合制作毛衣。|近义词：preshrunk, shrink-resistant, shrinkproof|反义词: shrinkable|词性:形容词
