interchanging:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
互相交换的，交替的
The two teams were interchanging positions constantly throughout the game.  两支队伍在整个比赛中不断交换位置。
Drivers were warned about interchanging lanes during rush hour.  司机们被警告在高峰时段不要变换车道。
近义词: exchanging, alternating, swapping, trading
反义词: fixed, static, unchanging
词性: adjective

interchange:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒ/
noun
交换，互换
There was a lively interchange of ideas at the meeting. 会议上进行了热烈的思想交流。
verb
交换，互换
The two players interchanged jerseys after the game. 比赛结束后，两位球员交换了球衣。
近义词: exchange, swap, trade, alternation
反义词: retain, keep
词性: noun, verb


词源:  From inter- (between) + change.  源自 inter-（在...之间）+ change（改变）。
