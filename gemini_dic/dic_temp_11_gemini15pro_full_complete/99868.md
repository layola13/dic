intermure:/ˌɪntərˈmjʊər/ | n. |  内墙，隔墙 | A wall between buildings or rooms, especially one that separates buildings or properties. |  例句：The intermure between our apartments is very thin. 我们公寓之间的隔墙很薄。

例句：The builders constructed a sturdy intermure to divide the property. 建筑工人建造了一堵坚固的隔墙来分隔房产。


近义词：partition, dividing wall, internal wall | 反义词: exterior wall, outer wall | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *intermurus, from Latin inter (between) + murus (wall).
