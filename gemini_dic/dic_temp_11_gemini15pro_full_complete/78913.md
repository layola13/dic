词源：
From Middle English *gipsiere, from Old North French *gipcier, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *cibīciārius (“provision bag”), from Latin cibārius (“relating to food”).  The word's evolution reflects a shift in meaning from a food bag to a money pouch.
