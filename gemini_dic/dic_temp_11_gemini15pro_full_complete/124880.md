muddies:/ˈmʌdiz/| v. |使浑浊，使模糊，使复杂化|Makes something unclear, confused, or more complicated. |The heavy rain muddied the water in the stream. 大雨使溪水变得浑浊。|近义词：confuses, obscures, complicates|反义词: clarifies, simplifies |verb (third-person singular simple present muddies, present participle muddying, simple past and past participle muddied)


muddy:/ˈmʌdi/| adj. |泥泞的，浑浊的，模糊的|Covered in or full of mud; not clear or bright; vague or confused.|The road was muddy after the rain. 雨后道路泥泞不堪。|近义词：dirty, murky, unclear|反义词: clean, clear |adjective
