interstriving: /ˌɪntərˈstraɪvɪŋ/
noun
内部竞争，内讧
Internal striving, conflict or competition within a group or organization.
例句：The interstriving between departments hindered the company's progress.  部门间的内讧阻碍了公司的发展。

例句：The team's success was threatened by constant interstriving among its members. 团队的成功受到成员之间持续内讧的威胁。

近义词：infighting, internal conflict, internal competition, dissension, discord
反义词：cooperation, collaboration, unity, harmony
