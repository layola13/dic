demonstrate:/ˈdemənstreɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛmənstreɪt/ (UK)| v. |展示，演示；证明；示威 | to show or make something clear; to prove something; to take part in a public meeting or march, usually as a protest | The teacher demonstrated how to solve the equation. 老师演示了如何解这个方程式。| show, exhibit, prove, display | conceal, hide | verb

demonstrate:/ˈdemənstreɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛmənstreɪt/ (UK)| n. |示威游行 | a public meeting or march, usually as a protest |  There was a large demonstrate against the new law. 有一场反对新法律的大型示威游行。| protest, rally, march |  | noun (rarely used)
