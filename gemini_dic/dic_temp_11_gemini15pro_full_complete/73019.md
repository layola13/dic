footbridges:/ˈfʊtˌbrɪdʒɪz/
n.
人行天桥
a bridge designed for pedestrians
The park has several footbridges over the stream. 公园里有几座横跨小溪的人行天桥。

近义词: pedestrian bridges, overpasses
反义词: underpasses
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From foot + bridge.  "Foot" meaning "for walking" and "bridge" from Old English "brycg" meaning a structure carrying a road or path over water or other obstacles.
