overdilate: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈleɪt/
verb
过度扩张
To dilate excessively; to expand beyond normal or healthy limits.
The doctor warned against using eyedrops that might overdilate the pupils. 医生警告不要使用可能导致瞳孔过度扩张的眼药水。

The drug can overdilate blood vessels, causing a drop in blood pressure.  这种药物会过度扩张血管，导致血压下降。

近义词: overexpand
反义词: constrict, contract
词性: verb
