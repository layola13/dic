dovetail:/ˈdʌvˌteɪl/| n. v.|鸠尾榫; 吻合; 使…吻合|A joint formed by one or more wedge-shaped projections (tenons) on one piece of wood that interlock with corresponding notches or recesses (mortises) in another. To fit together exactly; to connect or combine harmoniously.|The carpenter skillfully dovetailed the two pieces of wood. 木匠巧妙地将两块木头用鸠尾榫接合。|近义词：interlock, mesh, join, fit, coincide|反义词:clash, conflict, disagree|noun
dovetail:/ˈdʌvˌteɪl/| v.|鸠尾榫接合; 使…吻合|To join or fit together compactly or harmoniously.|The project manager ensured that all the different tasks dovetailed perfectly. 项目经理确保所有不同的任务完美地结合在一起。|近义词：interlock, mesh, join, fit, coincide|反义词:clash, conflict, disagree|verb
