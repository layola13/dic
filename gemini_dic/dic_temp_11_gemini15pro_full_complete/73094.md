footworn:/ˈfʊtwɔːrn/| adj.|磨损的，因行走过多而疲惫的|Worn or weary from much walking.|His footworn boots testified to the miles he had traveled.  他磨损的靴子证明了他走了很多路。|Synonyms: weary, exhausted, tired, worn out, fatigued|Antonyms: fresh, energetic, invigorated|adjective
