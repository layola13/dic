megaphone:/ˈmɛɡəˌfoʊn/ (US) /ˈmɛɡəfəʊn/ (UK)| n. |扩音器；喇叭|A portable, cone-shaped device used to amplify the voice.|The coach used a megaphone to address the team across the field. 教练用扩音器在球场上向队员们讲话。|
megaphones:/ˈmɛɡəˌfoʊnz/ (US) /ˈmɛɡəfəʊnz/ (UK)| n. |扩音器；喇叭（复数）|Plural form of megaphone.|The protesters carried megaphones to make their voices heard. 抗议者们带着扩音器，好让他们的声音被听到。|Synonyms: loudspeaker, amplifier, bullhorn|Antonyms: microphone (somewhat)|Noun
