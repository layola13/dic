fricassees:/frɪkəˈsiːz/| n. |奶油炖肉，炖菜|A dish of meat (especially chicken or veal) cut into small pieces, stewed in stock, and served in a thick white sauce.|She prepared a delicious chicken fricassee for dinner. 她晚餐准备了一道美味的鸡肉奶油炖菜。|近义词:stew, ragout|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "roast" as a contrasting cooking method)|noun

fricassee:/ˌfrɪkəˈsiː/| v. |用奶油炖，炖|To cook (meat, especially chicken or veal) by cutting it into small pieces, stewing it in stock, and serving it in a thick white sauce.|He fricasseed the rabbit with mushrooms and herbs. 他用蘑菇和香草炖了兔子。|近义词:stew, braise|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "roast" as a contrasting cooking method)|verb
