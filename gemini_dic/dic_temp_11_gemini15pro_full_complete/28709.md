carlton:/ˈkɑːrltən/| n.|卡尔顿|A surname, and also a place name found in several English-speaking countries.  It originates from the Old English words "ceorl" (freeman or peasant) and "tun" (settlement or farm).  Therefore, it likely meant "the freeman's farm" or "the peasant's farm".|Carlton is a common surname in England. 卡尔顿是英格兰的常见姓氏。

The Carlton Hotel is known for its luxurious accommodations. 卡尔顿酒店以其豪华的住宿而闻名。|近义词：(For place name) village, town, hamlet|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
