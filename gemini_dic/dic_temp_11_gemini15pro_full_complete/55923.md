divvying:/ˈdɪviɪŋ/
|v.|分摊，分配，分享|To divide or share something between a number of people.|We divvied up the candy among the children. 我们把糖果分给了孩子们。|Synonyms: distribute, allocate, share, split, apportion|Antonyms: hoard, keep, monopolize|verb

divvying:/ˈdɪviɪŋ/
|n.|分摊，分配，分享（这个词主要用作动名词，名词形式很少见）|The act of dividing or sharing something.|The divvying of the profits was a contentious issue. 利润的分配是一个有争议的问题。 （这个例句略有不自然，更常见的用法是动名词）|Synonyms: distribution, allocation, sharing, splitting, apportionment|Antonyms: hoarding, keeping, monopolization|noun
