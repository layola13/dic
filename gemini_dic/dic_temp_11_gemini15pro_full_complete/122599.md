modernities:/məˈdɜːrnɪtiz/
n.|现代性（复数）|The multiple and complex forms of modern social life, experience, and organization.  It acknowledges that "modernity" is not a singular, unified phenomenon but rather manifests in diverse and sometimes contradictory ways.|The rapid pace of technological change is one of the defining features of modernities.|近义词：modernisms, contemporary trends|反义词：traditions, archaisms|词性:名词

Etymology:
From modern + -ity + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *modernus*, from *modo* "just now".
