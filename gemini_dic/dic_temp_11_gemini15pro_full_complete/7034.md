anecdotal: /ˌænɪkˈdəʊtl/ (UK) /ˌænɪkˈdoʊtl/ (US)| adj.|轶事的；趣闻的；传闻的|Based on personal accounts rather than facts or research.  Relating to or consisting of anecdotes.|例句：Anecdotal evidence suggests that the medicine is effective.  传闻证据表明这种药有效。|例句：The book is full of anecdotal stories about famous scientists. 这本书充满了关于著名科学家的轶事。|近义词：informal, unofficial, casual, hearsay|反义词：scientific, empirical, statistical|词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek anekdota "things unpublished," neuter plural of anekdotos "unpublished," from an- "not" + ekdotos "published," from ekdidonai "to give out, publish."
