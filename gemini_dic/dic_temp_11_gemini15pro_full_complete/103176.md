jesters:/ˈdʒɛstərz/
n.
小丑；弄臣；爱开玩笑的人
A professional clown employed to entertain a king or nobleman.
The king's jester made everyone laugh. 国王的弄臣让每个人都笑了。

The jesters entertained the crowd with their antics. 小丑们用他们的滑稽动作娱乐人群。

近义词: clowns, fools, comedians
反义词: serious people
