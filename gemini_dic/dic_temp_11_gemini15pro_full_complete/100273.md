interseaboard:/ˈɪntərˈsiːbɔːrd/
adjective
跨海的；连接两个海域的
Connecting or relating to two or more seaboards.
The interseaboard railway connected the Atlantic and Pacific coasts.  跨海铁路连接了大西洋和太平洋沿岸。
Synonyms: transcontinental, cross-country
Antonyms: coastal, inland
词性: adjective
