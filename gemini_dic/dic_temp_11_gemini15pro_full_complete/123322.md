monographic:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 专著的；专题研究的 | Of or relating to a monograph; consisting of a monograph. |  This is a monographic study of the poet's early work. 这是对这位诗人早期作品的专题研究。|  The library holds a vast collection of monographic publications.  这个图书馆收藏了大量的专著出版物。|  specialized, in-depth, exhaustive | general, superficial, cursory | adjective
