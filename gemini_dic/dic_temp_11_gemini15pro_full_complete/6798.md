anapnograph:/ˈænəpnəɡrɑːf/
n.
呼吸描记器，呼吸描记图
An instrument for recording the movements of the chest and abdomen during respiration.  It also refers to the graphical record produced by such an instrument.
The doctor used an anapnograph to monitor the patient's breathing. 医生用呼吸描记器监测病人的呼吸。
The anapnograph showed a regular breathing pattern. 呼吸描记图显示呼吸模式正常。
近义词:pneumograph, respirograph
反义词: None
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *anapnoe* (breathing) + *-graph* (writing, recording).
