dregs:/drɛɡz/| n. |残渣，渣滓；沉淀物|the most worthless part or parts of something; the remnants of something after the best parts have been used up|The coffee was bitter, and there were dregs in the bottom of the cup. 咖啡很苦，杯底还有残渣。|synonyms: sediment, residue, lees, grounds|antonyms: cream, elite|noun

dregs:/drɛɡz/| n. |最坏的部分；社会渣滓|the worst and most undesirable parts of something|The dregs of society were often blamed for the city's problems.  社会渣滓经常被指责为城市问题的根源。|synonyms: riffraff, scum, outcasts|antonyms: elite, best|noun
