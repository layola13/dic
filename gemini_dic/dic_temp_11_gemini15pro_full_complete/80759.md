gossan:/ˈɡɒsən/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsən/ (UK)| n. |  铁帽，褐铁矿帽|A heavily oxidized, weathered or decomposed rock, usually the upper and exposed part of a sulfide ore deposit, often rich in iron oxides and sometimes containing valuable metals like gold and silver. It is formed by the weathering of sulfide minerals.| The prospector was excited to find a large gossan, hoping it indicated a rich gold deposit below. 探矿者兴奋地发现了一大片铁帽，希望它预示着下面有丰富的金矿床。| 近义词: caprock, iron hat | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider unweathered ore) | 词性: noun
