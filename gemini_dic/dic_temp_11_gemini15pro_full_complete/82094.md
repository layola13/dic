grobian: /ˈɡroʊbiən/ | n. | 粗鲁的人，粗野的人 | A rude, boorish, or unmannerly person. | He acted like a complete grobian, shouting and swearing in public. 他表现得像个十足的粗人，在公共场合大喊大叫，满嘴脏话。 |  Example: The grobian belched loudly during the elegant dinner.  这个粗鲁的家伙在优雅的晚宴上大声打嗝。 | Synonyms: boor, lout, churl, yahoo, oaf | Antonyms: gentleman, lady, refined person | noun

Etymology: From German *Grobian*, from *grob* "coarse, rough." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *graubaz*.
