ardent:/ˈɑːrdənt/| adj.|热心的；热烈的；激烈的|Characterized by warmth of feeling typically expressed in eager zealous support or activity.|She is an ardent supporter of animal rights. 她是一位热心的动物权利支持者。|近义词：passionate, fervent, enthusiastic, zealous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French "ardant" (present participle of "arder" - to burn), from Latin "ardentem" (nominative "ardens"), present participle of "ardere" - to burn, glow.
