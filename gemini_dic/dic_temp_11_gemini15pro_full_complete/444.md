ablegation:/ˌæblɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.|放弃；撤销；正式否认|The formal renunciation or rejection of a doctrine or belief.|He made a public ablegation of his former beliefs.|近义词：renunciation, repudiation, disavowal, denial, abjuration|反义词：affirmation, assertion, avowal|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ablegatio*, from Latin ablēgāre (“to send away, banish, delegate”), from ab (“away from”) + lēgāre (“to send with a commission, depute, bequeath”).
