hyposplenic:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈsplɛnɪk/ /ˌhaɪpəʊˈsplɛnɪk/
adjective
脾功能减退的；脾功能不全的
Having reduced or absent spleen function.  This can be due to surgical removal of the spleen (splenectomy), congenital absence of the spleen, or diseases that damage the spleen.
Hyposplenic patients are at increased risk of certain infections. 脾功能减退的患者更容易受到某些感染。
People who are hyposplenic should receive vaccinations against encapsulated bacteria. 脾功能低下的人应该接种针对荚膜细菌的疫苗。

近义词:asplenic
反义词:无

词性:adjective
