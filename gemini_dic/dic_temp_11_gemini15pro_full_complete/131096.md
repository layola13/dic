nonbiblical:/ˌnɒnˈbɪblɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈbɪblɪkəl/
adjective
非圣经的；世俗的
Not found in or not relating to the Bible.
The story contains many nonbiblical elements.  这个故事包含很多非圣经元素。
The museum houses a collection of biblical and nonbiblical artifacts.  博物馆收藏了圣经和非圣经的文物。
近义词：secular, worldly, profane
反义词：biblical, scriptural, religious
词性：adjective
