orthogamy:/ɔːrˈθɒɡəmi/ /ɔːrˈθɑːɡəmi/
noun
正配，同配生殖
The fusion of similar gametes, such as isogametes, that are not obviously different in size and structure from one another.
Example 1: Orthogamy is common in certain algae and fungi.  某些藻类和真菌中普遍存在正配生殖。
Example 2: The evolution of anisogamy from orthogamy is a key step in the evolution of sexual reproduction. 从同配到异配的进化是性生殖进化中的关键一步。
近义词: isogamy (in some contexts)
反义词: anisogamy, heterogamy
词性: n.
