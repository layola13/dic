alleged: /əˈlɛdʒd/ | adj. | 所谓的，声称的 |  Declared or stated to be as described; asserted. Often used when something hasn't been proven true. | The alleged thief was seen near the bank.  所谓的窃贼在银行附近被看到。| synonyms: supposed, purported, claimed, asserted | antonyms: proven, confirmed, verified | adjective

alleged: /əˈlɛdʒd/ | v. |  (主要用于法律)断言，声称；指控 | To assert or claim something without proof.  | The prosecution alleged that he committed the crime. 控方指控他犯了罪。 | synonyms: assert, claim, maintain, declare | antonyms: deny, refute, disprove | verb (past participle)
