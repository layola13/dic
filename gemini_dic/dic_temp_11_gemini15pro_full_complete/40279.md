continuative:/kənˈtɪnjuətɪv/
adjective
持续的，连续的
Continuing without interruption; expressing continuation.
The speaker made a continuative gesture to show he wasn't finished. 演讲者做了一个持续的手势，表示他还没有说完。
近义词: continuous, ongoing, uninterrupted, ceaseless
反义词: discontinuous, intermittent, sporadic
词性: adjective

continuative:/kənˈtɪnjuətɪv/
noun
延续词
A word or phrase that indicates continuation of a thought or action.
"And" and "then" are common continuatives. “和”和“然后”是常见的延续词。
近义词: connective, conjunction
反义词: (No clear antonym for the noun form)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *continuativus, from continuat- (stem of continuatus, past participle of continuare "to continue"), from Latin continuus "continuous" + -ivus (suffix forming adjectives).
