biosociological:/ˌbaɪoʊˌsoʊsiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
生物社会学的
Relating to the interaction of biological and social factors in determining individual or group behavior.
Biosociological research suggests a link between genetics and social status. 生物社会学研究表明遗传与社会地位之间存在联系。

Biosociological factors can influence health outcomes. 生物社会因素会影响健康结果。
Synonym: biosocial
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider solely biological or solely sociological)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- (life) + socio- (society) + -logical (study of)
