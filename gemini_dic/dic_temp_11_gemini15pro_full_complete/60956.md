eleviate:/ɪˈliːvieɪt/
verb
减轻，缓解
to make (something) less painful, difficult, or severe
The medication helped to elevate her pain. 药物帮助减轻了她的疼痛。
synonyms: alleviate, lessen, reduce, ease, mitigate
antonyms: increase, worsen, aggravate, exacerbate
词性:动词
