deerstalkers:/ˈdɪərstɔːkəz/
|n.|猎鹿帽；侦探帽|A soft woolen cap with visors front and back, worn especially for hunting.  A type of hat typically associated with Sherlock Holmes.|Sherlock Holmes is often depicted wearing deerstalkers.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯经常被描绘成戴着猎鹿帽。|Synonyms: hunting cap, Sherlock Holmes hat|Antonyms: baseball cap, fedora|noun
