fermentable:/fərˈmɛntəbl/ | adj. | 可发酵的 | Capable of undergoing fermentation. |  Grapes are fermentable and can be made into wine. 葡萄是可以发酵的，可以用来酿酒。|  The liquid is fermentable under the right conditions. 这种液体在合适的条件下可以发酵。 | synonyms: zymogenic, fermentescible | antonyms: non-fermentable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fermentable*, from Late Latin *fermentābilis*, from Latin *fermentāre* (to ferment) + *-bilis* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").
