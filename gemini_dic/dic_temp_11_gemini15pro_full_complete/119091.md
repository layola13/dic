metricity:/mɛˈtrɪsɪti/| n. |度量性；度量性质|The property of a space or manifold having a metric. It refers to the ability to measure distances and angles. |The metricity of this Riemannian manifold is essential for our calculations.  这个黎曼流形的度量性对我们的计算至关重要。|Synonyms: measurability, quantifiability|Antonyms: immeasurable, unquantifiable|noun
