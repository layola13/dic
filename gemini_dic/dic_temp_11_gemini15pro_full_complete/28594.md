caregivers:/ˈkerɡɪvərz/| n. |照顾者；护理员|A person who provides care for a child, an elderly person, or someone who is sick or disabled. |例句：The caregivers at the nursing home are very attentive to the residents' needs. 养老院的护理人员非常关注居民的需求。

例句：Family caregivers often face significant emotional and physical strain. 家庭护理员经常面临巨大的情感和身体压力。|近义词：attendant, nurse, guardian, custodian|反义词: ward, dependent|词性:noun
