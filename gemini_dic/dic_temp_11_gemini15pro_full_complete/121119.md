misdecision: /ˌmɪsdɪˈsɪʒən/ | n. | 错误的决定；失策 | A decision that turns out to be wrong or unfortunate. |  The company's misdecision to invest in the failing technology cost them millions. 该公司错误地决定投资于失败的技术，使他们损失了数百万美元。

The general's misdecision led to a disastrous defeat. 将军的错误决定导致了灾难性的失败。

| 近义词: bad decision, poor judgment, error in judgment, blunder, mistake | 反义词: good decision, wise decision, sound judgment | 词性: noun
