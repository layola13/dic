infrastructural:/ˌɪnfrəˈstrʌktʃərəl/
adjective|基础设施的，基础建设的|Relating to the basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g. buildings, roads, power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.|例句：The country needs to invest heavily in infrastructural improvements. 这个国家需要大力投资改善基础设施。|例句：Infrastructural development is crucial for economic growth.  基础设施建设对经济增长至关重要。|近义词：basic, fundamental, essential|反义词：superficial, cosmetic|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From infrastructure + -al.  "Infrastructure" comes from French, from infra- "below" + structure.  Ultimately from Latin infra "below" and structura "a building, structure."
