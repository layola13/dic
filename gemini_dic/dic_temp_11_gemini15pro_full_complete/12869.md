assuage:/əˈsweɪdʒ/| v. |减轻，缓和，平息|to make (an unpleasant feeling) less intense|The medicine assuaged his pain.  这药减轻了他的疼痛。|synonyms: relieve, ease, alleviate, mitigate, soothe, allay, mollify|antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify|verb

assuage:/əˈswedʒ/| v. |使满足，使满意|to satisfy (an appetite or desire)|Her hunger was assuaged by the hearty meal.  丰盛的饭菜使她饥饿感得到满足。|synonyms: satisfy, quench, gratify, appease|antonyms: dissatisfy|verb
