nonpenitents:/ˌnɒnˈpɛnɪtənts/ | n. | 不悔悟者；不忏悔者 |  People who do not feel or express regret for their sins or misdeeds.  Those who are unrepentant. | The preacher's words seemed to fall on deaf ears among the nonpenitents.  牧师的话似乎对那些不悔悟的人不起作用。

The nonpenitents continued their destructive behavior despite warnings. 不悔悟者们无视警告，继续他们的破坏行为。 | Near synonyms: unrepentant, impenitent, remorseless, uncontrite | Antonyms: repentant, contrite, remorseful | Noun
