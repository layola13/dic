baneful:/ˈbeɪnfəl/| adj.|有害的，恶毒的|Producing destruction or woe; seriously harmful; pernicious. |例句：The baneful effects of the pandemic were felt worldwide. 大流行的有害影响波及全球。

例句：Her baneful influence corrupted the entire organization. 她恶毒的影响腐蚀了整个组织。|近义词：pernicious, harmful, destructive, noxious, deleterious, malignant|反义词：beneficial, helpful, salutary, advantageous|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *baneful*, from Old English *bealuful* ("baleful, wicked, miserable"), equivalent to *bealu* ("bale, evil, woe") + *-ful*. Cognate with Old High German *baloful* ("wicked, harmful").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *balwaz* ("evil, pain, torment").
