lyric:/ˈlɪrɪk/ | n. adj. | 歌词；抒情的 |  A lyric is a short poem that expresses the writer's emotions, often set to music. As an adjective, it describes something expressing emotion in a beautiful and imaginative way. | The lyrics of the song are very moving. 这首歌的歌词很感人。 | synonyms: words, poem, verse, song, musical, poetic | antonyms: epic, narrative, prose | noun, adjective
