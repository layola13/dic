accusatives:/əˈkjuːzətɪvz/ | n. |宾格；宾格形式|The accusative case marks the direct object of a verb. 宾格标记动词的直接宾语。|The dog chased the cat (cat is in the accusative case). 狗追猫（cat 是宾格）。|Synonyms: objective case |Antonyms: nominative case |Noun
