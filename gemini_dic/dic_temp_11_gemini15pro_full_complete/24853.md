brownite:/ˈbraʊnaɪt/| n.|褐锰矿|A brownish-black mineral consisting of manganese oxide, important as an ore of manganese.|Brownite is a common manganese ore found in many parts of the world. 褐锰矿是一种常见的锰矿石，在世界许多地方都有发现。|Synonyms: manganite, hausmannite|Antonyms: N/A|noun

brownite:/ˈbraʊnaɪt/| n.|褐色的, 棕色的 (rare, used mainly in technical contexts)|Of a brown color.|The browner shades of brownite are less common. 褐锰矿中较棕色的色调不太常见。|Synonyms: brown, brownish|Antonyms: N/A|adjective (rare usage)
