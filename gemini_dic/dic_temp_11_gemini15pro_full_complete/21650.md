blushed:/blʌʃt/
verb
脸红；羞愧
become red in the face, typically as a result of shame or embarrassment
She blushed at the unexpected compliment. 她因意外的赞美而脸红了。
synonyms: reddened, flushed, colored
antonyms: paled, blanched
词性: 动词

blush:/blʌʃ/
noun
脸红；羞愧
a reddening of the face, typically as a result of shame or embarrassment
A blush crept up her cheeks.  一抹红晕爬上了她的脸颊。
synonyms: reddening, flush, rosiness
antonyms: pallor, paleness
词性: 名词

blush:/blʌʃ/
adjective
略带红色的
having a slightly red tinge
blush wine 淡红色葡萄酒
synonyms: reddish, pinkish, rosy
antonyms: pale
词性: 形容词
