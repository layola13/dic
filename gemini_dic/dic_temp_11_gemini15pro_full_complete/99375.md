interdetermined:/ˌɪntərdiˈtɜːrmɪnd/ | adj. |相互决定的，互相制约的|  Mutually determined or conditioned; reciprocally influencing each other.  The meaning of each part is dependent on the meaning of the others.| The interdetermined elements of the ecosystem make it difficult to isolate a single cause for the decline in the bee population. 生态系统中相互决定的因素使得很难找出蜜蜂数量下降的单一原因。| The success of the project is interdetermined with the level of funding received.  这个项目的成功与收到的资金水平相互决定。|  interconnected, interdependent, interrelated | independent, separate | adjective
