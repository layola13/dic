janky:/ˈdʒæŋki/| adj.|劣质的，不稳定的，不可靠的|Unreliable, of poor quality, or in bad condition.  Often used to describe something that is makeshift or improvised.|例句：The car was so janky, I was afraid it would fall apart on the way home. 这辆车太破旧了，我担心它会在回家的路上散架。

例句：The sound system was pretty janky, with wires hanging everywhere. 音响系统很糟糕，到处都是电线。|近义词：rickety, ramshackle, shoddy, flimsy, unreliable|反义词：stable, sturdy, reliable, robust|词性:形容词
