orthotropal:/ɔːrˈθɒtrəpəl/ | adj. | 正交各向异性的 | Having properties that differ along three mutually perpendicular axes, as in some crystals or wood. |  The orthotropal nature of wood makes it strong in one direction and weaker in others.  木材的正交各向异性使得它在一个方向上很强，而在其他方向上较弱。 | anisotropic | isotropic | adjective
