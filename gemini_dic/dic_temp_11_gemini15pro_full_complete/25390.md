bulbule:/ˈbʌlbjuːl/| n. |球茎；小球；水泡|A small bulb or bulb-like growth, especially on a plant; a rounded swelling.  It can also refer to a bubble in a liquid.|The orchid developed tiny bulbules at the base of its stem.| 近义词:bud, bulb, nodule, blister| 反义词:| noun
