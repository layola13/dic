ahistorical:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/
adjective
缺乏历史依据的；非历史的；不顾历史的
lacking historical perspective or context.
例句：An ahistorical approach to art. 对艺术的非历史方法。
例句：The film was criticized for its ahistorical portrayal of the events. 这部电影因其对事件的非历史性描述而受到批评。
近义词：unhistorical, anachronistic
反义词：historical
词性:adjective

Etymology->
From a- + historical.  "A-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without." "Historical" relates to history or past events.
