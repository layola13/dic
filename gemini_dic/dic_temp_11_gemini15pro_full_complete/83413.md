haberdashery:/ˈhæbərdæʃəri/
n.
男子服饰用品店;缝纫用品
Small items of clothing and sewing equipment, such as buttons, zips, and needles.
She bought some needles and thread at the haberdashery. 她在那家男子服饰用品店买了些针线。
近义词: notions, sewing supplies, small wares
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: hardware store, clothing store if referring to the place of purchase)
词性: noun
