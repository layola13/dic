monopolised:/məˈnɒpəlaɪzd/ /məˈnɑːpəlaɪzd/
|v.|垄断，独占|To obtain exclusive possession or control of (a trade, commodity, or service).|The company monopolised the market for computer chips.  这家公司垄断了电脑芯片市场。|
| | |The giant corporation has effectively monopolised the telecommunications industry. 这家巨型公司实际上已经垄断了电信行业。|
| | |synonyms: dominated, controlled, cornered, held|
| | |antonyms: shared, distributed, opened|
| |verb|
