gurney: /ˈɡɜːrni/ (US) /ˈɡɜːni/ (UK)| n. |轮床；担架|A wheeled stretcher used for transporting patients, especially in hospitals.|The paramedics wheeled the injured man out on a gurney.  护理人员用轮床把受伤的男子推了出来。|近义词：stretcher, cot |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From the proper noun Gurney, referring to the Gurney Carriage Company of Cincinnati, Ohio, which manufactured these stretchers.
