buckskin:/ˈbʌkskɪn/| n. adj.|鹿皮，鹿皮制的|A strong, soft leather made from the skin of a deer or similar animal.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something made of this leather.|例句：He wore a buckskin jacket. 他穿着一件鹿皮夹克。|近义词：deerskin, suede|反义词:None|Noun

buckskin:/ˈbʌkskɪn/| adj.|鹿皮色的，淡黄褐色的|Having a yellowish-brown color like buckskin.|例句：The horse was a buckskin color. 这匹马是鹿皮色的。|近义词：beige, tan|反义词:None|Adjective
