bismarck's:/ˈbɪzmɑːrks/
proper noun, possessive
俾斯麦的；属于俾斯麦的
Relating to or belonging to Otto von Bismarck, the first Chancellor of Germany.  It is the possessive form of "Bismarck,"  indicating ownership or association.

Bismarck's influence on European politics was immense. 俾斯麦对欧洲政治的影响是巨大的。
