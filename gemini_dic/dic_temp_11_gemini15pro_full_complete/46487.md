daintily:/ˈdeɪntɪli/| adv.|精致地；优雅地；美味地|In a delicate, refined, or elegant manner; fastidiously.|She daintily picked up the small cake with her fingertips. 她用指尖优雅地拿起小蛋糕。|delicately, gracefully, elegantly|clumsily, awkwardly|adverb

daintily:/ˈdeɪntɪli/| adv.|少量地；谨慎地|In small quantities; sparingly.|She ate daintily, picking at her food. 她吃得很少，只挑着食物吃。|sparingly, lightly|heavily, greedily|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dainty + -ly.  Dainty itself comes from Old French *daintie (variant of deintié), from Latin dignitatem (worth, dignity).
