karts:/kɑːrts/| n.|卡丁车，小型赛车|A small, open-wheeled racing car.|We raced karts at the weekend. 我们周末去玩卡丁车了。|Synonyms: go-karts, mini-racers|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "family car" as a contrast)|noun

Etymology->
Shortened form of "go-kart," which likely originated from the "go" of "go-devil," a small two-wheeled cart, and "cart."
