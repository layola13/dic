bluestocking: /ˈbluːˌstɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbluːˌstɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 蓝袜子；女学者；女才子|A woman with strong intellectual interests, especially one who displays her learning ostentatiously.|例句：She was considered a bluestocking because of her love of books and intellectual discussions. 她因为热爱书籍和学术讨论而被认为是蓝袜子。

|例句：The bluestockings of the 18th century challenged traditional gender roles. 18世纪的蓝袜子挑战了传统的性别角色。|近义词：intellectual, academic, learned woman|反义词：philistine, ignoramus|词性: noun
