hyperaccurateness:/ˌhaɪpərˈækjərətnəs/ | n. | 超级精确性，过度精确性 |  The state or quality of being extremely accurate, sometimes to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  |  His hyperaccurateness in record-keeping sometimes slowed down the project.  他的记录保存的超级精确性有时会减慢项目的进度。

Her hyperaccurateness bordered on obsession. 她的过度精确近乎强迫症。 | 近义词: precision, exactness, meticulousness | 反义词: inaccuracy, imprecision, carelessness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From "hyper-" (Greek:  ὑπέρ, hupér, 'over, beyond') + "accurate" (Latin: accuratus, past participle of accurare, 'to take care of') + "-ness" (Old English: -nes, forming nouns of state or quality).
