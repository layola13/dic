gangmaster:/ˈɡæŋˌmɑːstər/ | n. | 包工头；工头；劳务承包商 | A person who hires and controls groups of casual laborers, often exploiting them. | The gangmaster was accused of paying the workers less than minimum wage. 包工头被指控支付工人工资低于最低工资标准。| The gangmaster provided transportation for the workers to the fields each day.  包工头每天为工人提供到田间的交通工具。 | 近义词:labor contractor, crew leader, foreman | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "employee" in a contrasting sense) | 词性: noun
