docetism:/ˈdoʊsɪtɪzəm/| n. |幻影说，基督一性论|The belief that Jesus' physical body was an illusion and that he only appeared to be human.|Early Christians rejected docetism, asserting the reality of Jesus' incarnation.|近义词：illusionism, phantasmalism|反义词：realism, physicalism|noun
