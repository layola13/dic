mast's:/mæsts/ |n. |  桅杆；船桅（mast 的复数）| plural form of mast, referring to more than one mast | The ship's masts were damaged in the storm.  这艘船的桅杆在暴风雨中受损了。 | spars, poles |  | plural noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mæst (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mastaz, from Proto-Indo-European *mazdos. Cognate with Old Norse mastr, Old Frisian mast, Middle Dutch mast, Dutch mast, Old High German mast, German Mast.
