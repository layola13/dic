gangly:/ˈɡæŋɡli/ | adj. | 瘦长的；细长的；笨手笨脚的 | Tall, thin, and awkward in movements. |  The gangly teenager tripped over his own feet.  这个瘦高的少年被自己的脚绊倒了。 | synonyms: lanky, spindly, awkward, gawky | antonyms: graceful, coordinated, sturdy | adjective

Etymology:  Late 16th century (in the sense ‘straddling, having long legs’): perhaps from gang ‘go, walk’ + -ly.
