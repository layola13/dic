insipidnesses:/ɪnˈsɪpɪdnəsɪz/
|n.|乏味；无味；清淡无奇|The insipidnesses of the food made the meal a disappointment.  食物的乏味使得这顿饭令人失望。|
|英文解析:The quality or state of being insipid; lack of flavor, zest, or interest.|
|例句：The insipidnesses of his conversation bored everyone. 他谈话的乏味让每个人都感到无聊。|
|例句：She was tired of the insipidnesses of the television shows she was watching. 她厌倦了她正在看的电视节目的乏味。|
|近义词：blandness, dullness, vapidity, flatness|
|反义词：flavor, zest, piquancy, interest|
|词性: 名词|
