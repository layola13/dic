greencloth:/ˈɡriːnklɒθ/ /ˈɡriːnklɔːθ/| n. |绿呢;赌桌;赌博|A heavy woolen cloth, usually green, used for covering gambling tables. It can also refer to gambling itself.|The poker game was held on a greencloth in the back room.  扑克游戏是在后房间的一张铺着绿呢的桌子上进行的。

The detective knew the greencloth well from his undercover days.  那个侦探对赌博很熟悉，因为他做过卧底。|近义词：baize, felt|反义词: None|词性:noun
