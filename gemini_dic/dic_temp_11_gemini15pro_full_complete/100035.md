interphalangeal:/ˌɪntərˌfælənˈdʒiːəl/| adj.|指间的，趾间的|Situated between two phalanges (bones of the fingers or toes).|例句：Interphalangeal joints are the joints between the phalanges of the fingers or toes. 指间关节是手指或脚趾骨之间的关节。

例句：Arthritis can affect the interphalangeal joints, causing pain and stiffness.  关节炎会影响指间关节，引起疼痛和僵硬。|近义词：digital|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "proximal" in certain contexts) |词性: adjective
