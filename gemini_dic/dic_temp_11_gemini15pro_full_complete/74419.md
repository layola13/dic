franchisements:/ˈfræntʃaɪzmənts/
n.
特许经营权（复数）
The act of franchising or the state of being franchised.  A collective term referring to multiple franchise agreements or the businesses operating under them.
Many fast-food restaurants are franchisements. 许多快餐店都是特许经营的。
近义词:franchises, concessions
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "company-owned branches")
词性: noun
