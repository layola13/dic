drippiest:/ˈdrɪpiɪst/ | adj. | 最滴水的，最潮的 |  Superlative form of "drippy," meaning extremely fashionable or stylish, often in a showy or extravagant way. Originally referred to something literally dripping with liquid, but in modern slang, it describes someone with an exceptional sense of style. |  His outfit was the drippiest at the party, covered in diamonds and designer labels. 他在派对上的装扮是最潮的，全身都是钻石和名牌。|  stylish, fashionable, trendy, fly, fresh | drab, dull, unfashionable, plain | adjective
