bestir:/bɪˈstɜːr/
verb
激励；使奋起；唤醒
to rouse to action; stir up
He finally bestirred himself to go to work. 他终于奋起去工作了。
rouse, stir, awaken
idle, laze, procrastinate
词性: 动词
