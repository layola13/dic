bulwark:/ˈbʊlwərk/ | n. |壁垒，防御工事，保障；支柱|A defensive wall or rampart.  Something serving as a defense or safeguard. A strong support.|例句：The castle wall acted as a bulwark against the invaders. 城墙是抵御入侵者的壁垒。

例句：Free speech is a bulwark against tyranny. 言论自由是抵御暴政的保障。|近义词：rampart, fortification, defense, safeguard, bastion, support|反义词:weakness, vulnerability|词性:noun
