imperiously:/ɪmˈpɪəriəsli/| adv.|专横地；傲慢地；|In a domineering or haughty manner; commandingly.  Characterized by an arrogant or overbearing assertion of authority.|She imperiously demanded that he leave the room at once. 她专横地要求他立刻离开房间。|近义词：arrogantly, domineeringly, haughtily, overbearingly, dictatorially|反义词: humbly, meekly, submissively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imperiōsus* (from Latin *imperium* "command, power, empire") + *-ly*. *Imperium* is derived from *imperāre* "to command," from *in-* "in, on" + *parāre* "to prepare, procure."
