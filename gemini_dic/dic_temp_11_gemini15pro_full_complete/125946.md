musicalities:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkælɪtiz/| n.|音乐性，音乐才能|The sensitivity and skill with which music is played or composed, resulting in expressiveness and beauty. |Her playing has both technical brilliance and musicalities. 她的演奏兼具技巧的精湛和音乐性。|近义词：musicality, artistry, expressiveness|反义词：inartisticity, dullness|noun
