cinephile:/ˈsɪnəˌfaɪl/ | n. | 电影爱好者；影迷 | A person who is fond of or enthusiastic about cinema; a devotee of motion pictures. | She's a true cinephile, watching at least three films a week. 她是一位真正的电影爱好者，每周至少看三部电影。 |  He's such a cinephile that he has a room dedicated to his film collection. 他是一个十足的影迷，他有一个专门用来收藏电影的房间。 | Synonyms: film buff, movie lover, film fanatic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider someone who dislikes or is indifferent to films) | Noun
