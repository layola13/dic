apostematous: /ˌæpɒˈstɛmətəs/ /əˌpɑːstəˈmeɪtəs/ | adj. | 脓肿的，化脓性的 |  Characterized by the formation of an aposteme or abscess; pertaining to an aposteme. | The apostematous infection required immediate medical attention. 脓肿感染需要立即就医。| The doctor lanced the apostematous swelling. 医生切开了化脓的肿块。| 近义词: suppurative, purulent, abscessed | 反义词: healthy, non-infected | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Late Latin apostematosus, from Greek apostema "abscess," from aphistanai "to cause to revolt, separate," literally "to put away."
