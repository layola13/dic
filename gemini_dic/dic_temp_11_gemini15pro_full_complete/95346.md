incentively:/ɪnˈsɛntɪvli/
adverb|激励地，带有激励作用地|In a way that provides encouragement or motivation. |The program is designed to incentivly reward employees for their hard work.  这个项目旨在激励员工努力工作。|近义词: motivatingly, encouragingly|反义词: discouragingly, deterrently|词性:副词
词源: From "incentive" + "-ly."  "Incentive" comes from Late Latin "incentivus" meaning "inciting, setting the tune," from "incent-" (stem of "incendere" meaning "to kindle") + "-ivus" (suffix forming adjectives).
