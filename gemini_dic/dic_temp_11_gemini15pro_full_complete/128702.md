nidulant: /ˈnɪdjʊlənt/ | adj. | 蜷缩的；隐匿的；(尤指鸟类)伏窝的 | Lying snugly in a nest or other sheltered place; nestled.  Often used to describe birds sitting on eggs. | The nidulant bird patiently waited for her eggs to hatch. 伏窝的鸟耐心地等待着它的蛋孵化。 | 近义词: nestled, brooding, incubating | 反义词: exposed, uncovered | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *nidulans*, present participle of *nidulari* "to make a nest," from *nidus* "nest."
