overcommunicate:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
verb
过度沟通；过分沟通
To communicate excessively, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.  This can involve repeating information unnecessarily, providing too much detail, or using multiple channels of communication for the same message.
The manager overcommunicated the new policy, sending several emails, holding multiple meetings, and even posting flyers.  经理过度沟通了新政策，发送了几封电子邮件，举行了多次会议，甚至张贴了传单。

She tends to overcommunicate when she's nervous, explaining things multiple times. 她紧张的时候往往会过度沟通，把事情解释好几遍。

近义词: overexplain, overdo
反义词: undercommunicate
词性: verb
