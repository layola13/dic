garrot:/ˈɡærət/ /ɡəˈrɒt/| n. v. |绞索，止血带；用绞索勒死；用止血带止血|A device, usually a cord or wire, used for strangulation or for stopping bleeding by compressing a blood vessel.  It can also be the act of strangling someone with such a device or applying it to stop bleeding.|The assassin used a garrot to silently eliminate his target. 刺客用绞索悄悄地干掉了他的目标。|近义词：tourniquet, ligature, strangle, choke |反义词: release, loosen |词性: noun, verb
Etymology: From French garrot (“withers, stick, tourniquet”), from Old Provençal garrot (“stick, club”), from Late Latin garrum (“club, stick”). Ultimately from Gaulish.
