distractibility:/dɪˌstræktɪˈbɪləti/| n.|分心，注意力分散|The state of being easily distracted; inability to maintain attention.  容易分心的状态；无法保持注意力的状态。|His distractibility made it difficult for him to study. 他的注意力不集中使他难以学习。|The noise increased her distractibility. 噪音加剧了她的注意力分散。|近义词：inattentiveness, absent-mindedness, flightiness|反义词：focus, concentration, attentiveness|词性: noun

distractible:/dɪˈstræktɪbl/| adj.|易分心的，注意力不集中的|Easily diverted; susceptible to distraction. 容易分心；易受干扰。|He's very distractible and has trouble focusing on tasks. 他很容易分心，难以集中精力完成任务。|The distractible child couldn't sit still during class.  这个注意力不集中的孩子上课时坐不住。|近义词：inattentive, unfocused|反义词：focused, attentive, concentrated|词性: adjective
