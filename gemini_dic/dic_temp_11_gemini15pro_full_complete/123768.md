moorlands:/ˈmʊərləndz/
n.
荒野，高沼地，荒地
Open land, often high but poorly drained, with acidic peaty soil, typically covered with heather, coarse grasses, and bracken.
The hikers traversed the desolate moorlands.  徒步旅行者穿越了荒凉的荒野。
The novel was set in the bleak moorlands of Yorkshire. 这部小说的背景设定在约克郡荒凉的荒原。
近义词：moors, heathland, uplands
反义词: lowlands, valleys
词性: 名词
