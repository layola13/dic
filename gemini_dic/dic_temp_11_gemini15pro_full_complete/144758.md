over-compliant:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈplaɪənt/
adjective
过度顺从的，过分服从的
Excessively compliant; obeying rules or requests too readily, often to one's own detriment.
The over-compliant employee agreed to work overtime every day, even though it was affecting his health.  这个过度顺从的员工同意每天加班，即使这影响了他的健康。
He was so over-compliant with his girlfriend's demands that he lost all his friends. 他对女友的要求过于顺从，以至于失去了所有的朋友。
近义词:submissive, acquiescent, docile, obedient
反义词:rebellious, defiant, assertive, noncompliant
词性:adjective
