cartogram:/ˈkɑːrtəɡræm/| n. |统计地图；变形地图|A cartogram is a map in which the areas of regions are distorted proportionally to the value of a particular variable, such as population or gross domestic product.|The cartogram clearly showed the population density of different countries.|Synonym: thematic map, statistical map|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "standard map")|Noun
Etymology:  From French *cartogramme*, from *carte* ("map") + *-gramme* (from Greek *gramma* "something written or drawn").
