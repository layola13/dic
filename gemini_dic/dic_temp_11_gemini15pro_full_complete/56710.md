doomscrolling:/ˈduːmˌskroʊlɪŋ/
noun
末日刷屏，沉迷于浏览负面新闻
The act of spending an excessive amount of screen time devoted to absorbing negative news, typically about current events, even though that news is saddening, disheartening, or depressing.
例句：She was doomscrolling for hours, reading about the latest natural disasters. 她连续几个小时都在末日刷屏，阅读最新的自然灾害新闻。

例句：He knew he should stop doomscrolling, but the constant stream of bad news was strangely addictive. 他知道他应该停止末日刷屏，但源源不断的坏消息却奇怪地上瘾。

近义词：information overload, obsessive news consumption, morbid curiosity
反义词：digital detox, mindfulness, selective news consumption
词性：n.
