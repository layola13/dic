gluten:/ˈɡluːtn/ | n. | 谷蛋白，麸质 | A protein composite found in foods processed from wheat and related grain species, including barley and rye. |  I'm trying to avoid gluten. 我正在尝试避免麸质。 | Gluten gives bread its elasticity. 麸质赋予面包弹性。 | 近义词: protein (in a broad sense) | 反义词: None (Gluten is a specific protein, not a concept with an opposite.) | 词性: noun
