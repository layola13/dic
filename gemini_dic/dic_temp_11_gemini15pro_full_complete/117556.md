megacity:/ˈmɛɡəˌsɪti/ | n. | 大都市，特大城市 | A very large city, typically one with a population of over ten million people. |  Tokyo is a sprawling megacity with a population of over 37 million. 东京是一个拥有超过3700万人口的庞大都市。

The rapid growth of megacities presents numerous challenges. 特大城市的快速发展带来了诸多挑战。


| 近义词: metropolis, megalopolis | 反义词: village, small town | 词性: noun
