elytriform: /ɪˈlɪtrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
鞘翅状的，鞘状的
Resembling an elytron; shaped like a wing cover of a beetle.
The beetle's elytriform forewings protect its delicate hindwings.  这甲虫鞘翅状的前翅保护着它脆弱的后翅。
近义词: sheath-like, wing-covered
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "unprotected," "exposed" if referring to the function of elytra)
词性: adjective


elytriform: /ɪˈlɪtrɪfɔːrm/
noun
鞘翅状物
Something shaped like an elytron.
The archaeologist discovered an elytriform artifact. 考古学家发现了一个鞘翅状的文物。
近义词: elytron (when referring to the actual wing cover)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἔλυτρον* (elutron, “sheath, covering”) + Latin *-formis* (-form, “having the form of”).  The Greek *ἔλυτρον* itself is related to *εἰλύω* (eiluō, “to wrap, roll up”).
