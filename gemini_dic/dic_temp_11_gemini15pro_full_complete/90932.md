hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdi/| n. |手摇风琴；街头手摇风琴|A musical instrument with a droning sound, played by turning a crank that rubs against strings. It often has keys that change the melody.|The hurdy-gurdy player entertained the crowd with a lively tune. 手摇风琴演奏者用一首欢快的曲子娱乐了人群。|Synonym: barrel organ, wheel fiddle|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "silent instrument")|noun


hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdi/| adj. |(Informal) Repetitive and monotonous; noisy and confusing|The hurdy-gurdy rhythm of the factory machines was deafening. 工厂机器单调重复的节奏震耳欲聋。|Synonym: monotonous, repetitive, cacophonous|Antonym: varied, diverse, harmonious|adjective
