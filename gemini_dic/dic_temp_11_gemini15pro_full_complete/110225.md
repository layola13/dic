lifeguarded:/ˈlaɪfɡɑːrdɪd/
Verb|担任救生员；救生|The beach was lifeguarded during the summer months.  夏季的几个月里，海滩上有救生员值班。|例句：She lifeguarded at the local pool during the summer. 她夏天在当地游泳池当救生员。|近义词：guarded, protected, watched over|反义词: endangered, unprotected|词性:v.

lifeguard:/ˈlaɪfɡɑːrd/
Noun|救生员|The lifeguard saved the drowning child. 救生员救了溺水的孩子。|例句：He works as a lifeguard at the beach. 他在海滩上当救生员。|近义词：rescuer, lifesaver|反义词: N/A|词性:n.
