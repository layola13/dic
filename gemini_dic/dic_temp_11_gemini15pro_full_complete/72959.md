foodies:/ˈfuːdiz/| n.|美食家；吃货|A person who has a keen interest in food, especially in eating and cooking. |例句：The city is a haven for foodies, with its diverse range of restaurants. 这个城市拥有各种各样的餐馆，是美食家的天堂。

例句：Foodies will love the new farmers market. 美食家会喜欢新的农贸市场。|近义词：gourmet, epicure, gourmand|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以用缺乏对食物兴趣的人来表达，例如 indifferent eater)|词性:Noun
