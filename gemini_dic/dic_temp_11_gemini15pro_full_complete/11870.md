arriver:/əˈraɪv/ (US) /əˈrʌɪv/ (UK)| vi.|到达，抵达|To reach a place, especially at the end of a journey.|We arrived at the station early. 我们很早就到达了车站。|The package finally arrived after two weeks.  包裹最终在两周后到达了。|近义词：reach, get to, come to|反义词:depart, leave|verb
