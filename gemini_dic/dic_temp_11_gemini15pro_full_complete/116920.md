mayors:/ˈmeɪərz/| n.|市长们|The plural form of "mayor," referring to the elected heads of cities or towns.|The mayors of several cities met to discuss urban development. 几位城市的市长开会讨论城市发展。|近义词：city managers, council leaders|反义词:citizen, resident|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *maire*, from Latin *maior* (greater, elder).
