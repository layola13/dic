nonfossils:/nɒnˈfɒsɪlz/ | n. | 非化石 | Materials or organisms that are not fossils, typically referring to things that have not been mineralized or preserved over geological timescales. |  These nonfossils provide valuable data about the recent past. 这些非化石提供了关于近代的有价值的数据。| synonyms: recent remains, contemporary materials | antonyms: fossils | noun

nonfossil:/nɒnˈfɒsɪl/ | adj. | 非化石的 | Not fossilized; not having been transformed into a fossil. | The researchers studied both fossil and nonfossil pollen. 研究人员研究了化石和非化石花粉。| synonyms: unfossilized, contemporary | antonyms: fossil, fossilized | adjective
