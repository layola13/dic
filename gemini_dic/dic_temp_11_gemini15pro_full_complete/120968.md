mischarging:/ˌmɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
|n.|错误收费，收费错误
|The act of charging incorrectly, either by overcharging or undercharging.|
|The customer complained about the mischarging on their bill.|顾客投诉他们的账单收费错误。
|近义词：overcharging, undercharging, incorrect billing|
|反义词：correct charging, accurate billing|
|词性：名词|
