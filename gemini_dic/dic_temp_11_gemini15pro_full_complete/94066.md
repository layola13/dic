impassion:/ɪmˈpæʃən/ | vt. | 激起热情，使充满激情 | To fill with passion or intense feeling. |  His speech impassioned the crowd, leading to a standing ovation. 他的演讲激起了群众的热情，导致了全场起立鼓掌。

The music impassioned her and made her want to dance. 音乐激起了她的热情，让她想跳舞。| 近义词: arouse, inflame, excite, inspire | 反义词: calm, soothe, subdue | 词性: 动词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French *empassioner, from em- ‘in’ + passion.
