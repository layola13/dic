compares:/kəmˈpɛrz/ | v. | 比较，对照 |  To examine (two or more objects, ideas, people, etc.) in order to note similarities and differences | She compares her new house to her old one and finds the new one much better. 她把她的新房子和旧房子比较，发现新房子好多了。|  The report compares the different types of treatment available. 这份报告比较了不同类型的可用疗法。 | synonyms: contrasts, juxtaposes,  matches,  likens | antonyms: differentiates, distinguishes | verb

compare:/kəmˈpɛr/ | n. | 比较，对照 |  The act or instance of comparing. | There's no comparison between the two players - one is far better than the other. 这两个选手根本没有可比性——一个比另一个好得多。 |  The comparison between the two cities showed significant differences in population density. 对这两个城市的比较显示，人口密度存在显著差异。 | synonyms: analogy, simile, likeness | antonyms: difference, contrast | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comparen*, from Old French *comparer*, from Latin *comparāre* (“to couple, match, pair, compare”), from *com-* (“together”) + *parāre* (“to prepare, provide”).
