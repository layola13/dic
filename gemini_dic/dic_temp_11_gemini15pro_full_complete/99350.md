interdenominationalisms:/ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəlɪzəmz/
|n.|跨教派的主义，跨宗派的观点|The rise of interdenominationalisms reflects a growing desire for unity among Christians.|例句：The conference fostered interdenominationalisms by bringing together leaders from various Protestant sects.  这次会议通过召集来自各新教派的领导人，促进了跨教派的主义。

例句：Interdenominationalisms are becoming increasingly common as Christians seek common ground.  随着基督徒寻求共同点，跨宗派主义变得越来越普遍。|近义词：ecumenism, interfaith cooperation|反义词：sectarianism, denominationalism|词性:名词

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
denominational (adjective): relating to a particular Christian denomination
-ism (suffix): denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice
