impressionably:/ɪmˈprɛʃənəbli/
adjective
易受影响地，敏感地
Easily influenced by feelings and emotions; susceptible to impressions.
The young boy, impressionably, believed every word the magician said.  这个易受影响的小男孩相信了魔术师说的每一句话。
She listened impressionably to the stories of her grandparents' travels. 她听得入了迷，全神贯注地听着她祖父母旅行的故事。

近义词:susceptibly, sensitively, receptively
反义词:imperviously, unresponsively, insensitively
词性:adjective
