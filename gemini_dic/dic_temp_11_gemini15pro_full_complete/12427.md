askaris:/æsˈkɑːriːz/
n.
士兵，当地士兵，非洲士兵
Native soldiers in East Africa, often employed by European colonial powers.
The British employed askaris in their East African colonies.  英国在东非殖民地雇佣了阿斯卡利士兵。
The askaris were crucial in maintaining order during the colonial period.  在殖民时期，阿斯卡利士兵对维持秩序至关重要。
synonyms: soldiers, troops, mercenaries, native troops
antonyms: civilians, citizens, non-combatants
noun
