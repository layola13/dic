enface:/ɑ̃ˈfas/ /enˈfeɪs/ | adv. adj.|正面地，面对面地；正面的|Positioned so as to face the observer or with the face foremost.  Directly facing; opposite.|The baby was held enface so she could see her mother's face.  婴儿被抱着脸对着脸，这样她就能看到妈妈的脸了。|近义词：face-to-face, head-on, opposite|反义词:behind, back-to-back|词性:副词，形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *en face* ("in face, opposite"), from *en* ("in") + *face* ("face"). Ultimately from Latin *in* + *facies*.
