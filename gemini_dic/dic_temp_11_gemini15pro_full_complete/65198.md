ethylenes:/ˈɛθəlˌiːnz/ | n. |乙烯类|A group of unsaturated hydrocarbons with the general formula CnH2n, characterized by a carbon-carbon double bond.  Specifically, the simplest one is ethylene (C2H4).|Ethylene is commonly used to ripen fruits. 乙烯通常用于催熟水果。|Synonyms: alkenes, olefins|Antonyms: alkanes, paraffins|noun
