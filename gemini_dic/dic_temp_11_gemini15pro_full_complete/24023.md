breedable:/ˈbriːdəbl/
adjective
可繁殖的，适合育种的
Suitable for breeding; capable of being bred.
例句：These dogs are considered highly breedable due to their champion bloodline. 这些狗因为它们的血统优良而被认为非常适合繁殖。

例句：The farmer carefully selected only the most breedable animals for his stock.  这位农民仔细地只挑选了最适合繁殖的动物作为他的牲畜。

近义词：reproducible, procreative
反义词：infertile, sterile
词性：adjective

词源：From "breed" (v.) + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  "Breed" comes from Old English "brēdan," meaning "to cherish, nourish, keep warm, hatch, produce."
