baktun:/bɑːkˈtuːn/
n.
玛雅长纪年周期单位，约394年
A baktun is a period of 400 tun years in the Mayan Long Count calendar, equivalent to 144,000 days or approximately 394.26 solar years.
The 13th baktun ended on December 21, 2012, marking the end of a significant cycle in the Mayan calendar.
近义词:  cycle, period (in Mayan calendar context)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly katuns which are smaller units)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Yucatec Maya *bʼakʼtun, from bʼakʼ "four hundred" + tun "count, 360-day period."
