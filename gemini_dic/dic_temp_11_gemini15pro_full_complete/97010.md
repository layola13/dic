infatuated: /ɪnˈfætʃueɪtɪd/ | adj. | 迷恋的，着迷的 | Filled with a foolish or extravagant passion.  Driven by an intense but short-lived passion or admiration for someone or something. | She was completely infatuated with the pop star. 她完全迷恋上了那个流行歌星。 | He's infatuated with his new car. 他对他的新车着了迷。 |  Near Synonyms: obsessed, smitten, captivated, enamored | Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic, disenchanted | adjective
