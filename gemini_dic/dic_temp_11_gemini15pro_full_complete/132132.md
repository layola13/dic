nonculpable: /nɒnˈkʌlpəbl/ /nɑːnˈkʌlpəbl/
adjective
无罪的; 无可责备的
Not culpable; innocent; blameless.
He was found to be nonculpable in the accident. 他被认定在这起事故中无罪。
The court declared her nonculpable. 法院宣布她无罪。
近义词: innocent, blameless, guiltless, irreproachable
反义词: culpable, guilty, blameworthy

Etymology:
From non- + culpable, from Latin culpabilis "blameworthy," from culpare "to blame," from culpa "fault, blame."
