assemblies:/əˈsɛmbliz/ | n. | 集会，会议；装配，组装 |A group of people gathered together in one place for a common purpose. Also, the process of putting parts together to make a whole. | The school assemblies are held every Monday morning. 学校集会每周一早上举行。| synonyms: gatherings, meetings, congregations, conventions; constructions, installations | antonyms: dispersals, separations; dismantlings, deconstructions | noun

assemblies:/əˈsɛmbliz/ | n. | (尤指制造业)配件，部件 |A collection of parts that are put together to form a complete unit, especially in manufacturing. | The car assemblies were shipped from the factory. 汽车部件从工厂运出。| synonyms: components, units, modules, subassemblies | antonyms: parts, pieces, elements | noun
