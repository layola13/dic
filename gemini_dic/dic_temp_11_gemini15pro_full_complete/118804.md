metamerism:/ˈmɛtəmərɪzəm/| n.|异构，同分异构现象，分节同形|The phenomenon of having different spectral power distributions but appearing the same color under the same lighting conditions.  在相同光照条件下，具有不同光谱功率分布但呈现相同颜色的现象。|Example 1: The paint company had to deal with the issue of metamerism when matching colors across different paint bases. 这家油漆公司在匹配不同油漆基底的颜色时，不得不处理异构现象的问题。

Example 2: Metamerism is an important consideration in textile manufacturing, as fabrics that appear identical in one light may look different under another. 异构现象在纺织品制造中是一个重要的考虑因素，因为在一种光线下看起来相同的织物在另一种光线下可能看起来不同。|近义词：isomerism|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
