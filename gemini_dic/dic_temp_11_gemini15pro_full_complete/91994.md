hypernutrition:/ˌhaɪpərnuˈtrɪʃən/| n. |过度营养，营养过剩|A condition characterized by excessive intake of nutrients, leading to various health problems.  It's often associated with overeating and consuming a diet rich in calories, fats, and sugars.|Example: Hypernutrition can lead to obesity and other health complications. 过度营养会导致肥胖和其他健康并发症。

Example: Childhood hypernutrition is a growing concern in developed countries. 儿童过度营养在发达国家是一个日益严重的问题。|近义词: overnutrition |反义词: malnutrition, undernutrition |词性: noun
