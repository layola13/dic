cephalitis:/ˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs/
n.
脑炎
Inflammation of the brain, often due to infection.
The doctor diagnosed him with encephalitis after a series of tests. 医生经过一系列检查后诊断他患有脑炎。
Encephalitis can cause serious neurological problems. 脑炎会导致严重的神经系统问题。

近义词:brain inflammation
反义词:(No direct antonym for a medical condition)
词性:noun
