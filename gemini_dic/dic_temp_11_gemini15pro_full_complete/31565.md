chased:/tʃeɪst/| v.|追逐，追赶|The police chased the thief through the streets. 警察在街上追赶小偷。|例句：The dog chased the ball across the park. 狗追着球穿过公园。|近义词：pursue, follow, hunt|反义词:flee, escape|verb

词源: From Middle English *chasen, from Old French chacier (“to chase, hunt”), from Vulgar Latin *captiāre, frequentative of Latin capiō (“I take, seize, capture”).
