murmuration:/ˌmɜːrməˈreɪʃn/ | n. |低语声，喃喃声；(尤指鸟群的)群飞，盘旋 |The gentle murmuration of the stream was soothing. 小溪轻柔的潺潺流水声令人舒缓。|例句：The murmuration of starlings in the evening sky was mesmerizing.傍晚天空中椋鸟群的盘旋令人着迷。| 近义词：whisper, murmur, drone, swarm, flocking | 反义词: silence, stillness |词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *murmuratio, from Latin murmuratus, past participle of murmurare (“to murmur”).
