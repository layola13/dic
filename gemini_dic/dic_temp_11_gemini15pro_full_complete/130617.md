nonadjacent:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒeɪs(ə)nt/ | adj. |不相邻的，不毗连的 | Not immediately next to each other; separated. | The nonadjacent rooms offered more privacy. 这两个不相邻的房间提供了更多的隐私。| The islands are nonadjacent, separated by vast stretches of ocean. 这些岛屿不相邻，被广阔的海洋隔开。 | synonyms: disconnected, separate, discontiguous, apart, distant | antonyms: adjacent, adjoining, contiguous, abutting, neighboring | adjective
