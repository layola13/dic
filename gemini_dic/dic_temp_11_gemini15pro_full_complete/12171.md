aryans:/ˈɛəriənz/ | n. | 雅利安人 | A member of the peoples speaking Indo-European languages who invaded northern India c. 1500 BC, displacing the Dravidian inhabitants. Historically, especially in Nazi ideology, often taken to refer to a Caucasian people of Nordic type. |  The Nazis believed the Aryans were a superior race. 纳粹认为雅利安人是优等种族。| synonyms: Indo-European, Indo-Germanic, Caucasian | antonyms: non-Aryan, Dravidian | noun
