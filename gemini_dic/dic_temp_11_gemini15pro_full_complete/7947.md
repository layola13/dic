anthelix:/ˈænθɪlɪks/
n.
对耳轮
The curved prominence of cartilage parallel with and in front of the helix on the outer ear.
He has a piercing in his anthelix. 他在对耳轮上有一个穿孔。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀνθέλιξ (anthélix, “anything spiral, tendril”), from ἀντί (antí, “against, opposite”) + ἕλιξ (hélix, “spiral, anything twisted”).
