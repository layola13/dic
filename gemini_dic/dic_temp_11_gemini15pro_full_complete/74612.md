frecklier:/ˈfrɛklɪər/ | adj. | 更多雀斑的 | Having more freckles than before or than someone/something else.  Covered with a greater number of freckles. |  Example 1: After a week at the beach, she was noticeably frecklier.  一周在海滩度假后，她明显地多了很多雀斑。

Example 2: He's always been frecklier than his brother. 他一直比他哥哥雀斑多。 | Synonyms: more spotted, speckled | Antonyms: less freckled, clearer | Word Type: Comparative adjective


freckle:/ˈfrɛkl/ | n. | 雀斑 | A small, pale brown spot on the skin, especially on the face, caused by exposure to sunlight. | Example 1: She has a scattering of freckles across her nose. 她的鼻子上散布着一些雀斑。

Example 2:  He gets freckles easily in the summer. 他夏天很容易长雀斑。 | Synonyms: spot, speckle, lentigo | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could use "clear skin") | Word Type: Noun
