go-to-the-dogs:/ɡoʊ tə ðə dɒɡz/| idiom |每况愈下；走下坡路；堕落 |To deteriorate or decline in quality, condition, or status.  It implies a gradual process of worsening. |This company used to be great, but it's really gone to the dogs lately. 这家公司曾经很棒，但最近真的每况愈下了。|Synonyms: deteriorate, decline, worsen, degenerate|Antonyms: improve, flourish, thrive|idiom
