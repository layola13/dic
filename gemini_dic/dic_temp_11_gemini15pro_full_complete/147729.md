overprivileged:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd/
adjective
过于优越的；享有特权的
Having more advantages or privileges than are considered reasonable or necessary.
例句：Overprivileged children often don't appreciate what they have. 过于优越的孩子往往不懂得珍惜他们所拥有的。

例句：He comes from an overprivileged background and has never had to work for anything. 他出身于一个享有特权的家庭，从未为任何事情努力过。

近义词：privileged, advantaged, affluent, wealthy
反义词：underprivileged, disadvantaged, poor, impoverished
词性：adjective


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "privileged" (adjective from "privilege").  The word "privilege" comes from the Latin "privilegium" meaning "law for a private person,"  from "privus" (private) + "lex" (law).
