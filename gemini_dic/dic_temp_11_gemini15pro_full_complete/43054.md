cowleech:/ˈkaʊˌliːtʃ/
noun
放血者;兽医
a person who treats sick animals by bleeding them. In former times, often a farrier.
The farmer called for the cowleech when his prize cow fell ill.  农场主在他获奖的奶牛生病时叫来了放血者。
Synonyms: farrier, veterinarian (modern)
Antonyms: doctor (for humans), physician
