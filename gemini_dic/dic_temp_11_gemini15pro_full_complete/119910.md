militations:/ˌmɪlɪˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|斗争，战斗; 抵触;  不利因素|
|The continual militations between the two groups hindered progress. 这两个团体之间不断的斗争阻碍了进展。|
|His past mistakes militated against his chances of promotion. 他过去的错误对他升职的机会不利。|
|近义词: conflicts, struggles, objections, oppositions, factors|
|反义词: agreements, harmonies, supports, advantages|
|词性: 名词 (通常用复数形式)|


Etymology:
From Late Latin *militātiōn*, from Latin militāre (“to serve as a soldier”), from mīles (“soldier”).
