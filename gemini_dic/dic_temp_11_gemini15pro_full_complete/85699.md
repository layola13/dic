haver:/ˈheɪvər/ | n. |拥有者；持有人；<苏格兰>燕麦种植者 |One who has or possesses something; a possessor. | The haver of the winning lottery ticket is unknown. | 近义词: owner, possessor, holder | 反义词: loser, non-possessor | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *haver*, from Old Norse *hafr*, "he-goat".  The original meaning likely referred to a goatherd, someone who "has" goats. Later, the meaning broadened to "one who possesses anything."
