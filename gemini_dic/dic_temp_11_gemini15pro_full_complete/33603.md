cination:/səˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 着迷，迷恋 | The process of being fascinated or strongly attracted to something. | Her cination with ancient Egypt led her to study archaeology. 她对古埃及的迷恋使她学习了考古学。 | 近义词: fascination, captivation, infatuation | 反义词: apathy, indifference | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *cinationem, accusative of *cinatio ("reducing to ashes"), from Latin cinis ("ashes").  The sense shift likely occurred through association with the intense focus and "burning" desire associated with fascination.
