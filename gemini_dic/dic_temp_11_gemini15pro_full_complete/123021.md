monasticon:/məˈnæstɪkən/
n.
修道院志，修道院目录
A book or compilation listing monasteries, often with historical details and descriptions of their holdings.
The scholar consulted the monasticon to learn about the abbey's founding.  这位学者查阅了修道院志，以了解该修道院的创建。
近义词:  cartulary, register
反义词:  None
词性: Noun

monasticon:/məˈnæstɪkən/
n.
修道院的历史记载
A history or record of monasteries.
The monasticon detailed the lives of the monks and their contributions to scholarship.  修道院的历史记载详细描述了僧侣们的生活以及他们对学术的贡献。
近义词: chronicle, annals
反义词: None
词性: Noun
