infrastructures:/ˈɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃəz/
|n.|基础设施|The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g., buildings, roads, and power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.|例句：The country needs to invest in its infrastructures to support economic growth.  这个国家需要投资基础设施建设以支持经济增长。

|例句：Damaged infrastructures caused by the earthquake hampered rescue efforts. 地震造成的受损基础设施阻碍了救援工作。
|近义词：facilities, framework, base|反义词：superstructure|词性: 名词
