nonstyles:/ˈnɒnstaɪlz/
noun
无风格；非主流风格
Lack of a particular or distinctive style, or items that are considered to be outside of conventional fashion trends.
Many vintage shops specialize in nonstyles, offering unique and unconventional clothing. 许多复古商店专门经营非主流风格，提供独特且不落俗套的服装。
Near synonyms: unconventional styles, atypical fashions, unorthodox looks
Antonyms: styles, fashions, trends
