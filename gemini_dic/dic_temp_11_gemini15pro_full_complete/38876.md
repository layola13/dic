conferenced:/ˈkɒnfərənst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfərənst/ (US)
verb
已举行会议；已商议
having had a conference; having conferred
The board has conferenced and reached a decision.  董事会已举行会议并做出了决定。
The leaders conferenced for several hours before reaching an agreement.  领导们商议了几个小时才达成协议。
近义词: met, discussed, deliberated
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) avoided discussion, disagreed

词性: verb (past participle)
