henchboy:/ˈhɛntʃbɔɪ/ | n. | 帮凶; 打手; 喽啰; | A faithful follower, especially one prepared to use violence or intimidation on behalf of a powerful person. | The villain's henchmen surrounded the hero.  恶棍的帮凶包围了英雄。 |  The dictator relied on his henchmen to enforce his will. 独裁者依靠他的打手来执行他的意志。 | synonyms: minion, thug, goon, lackey, underling | antonyms: leader, boss, chief | noun
