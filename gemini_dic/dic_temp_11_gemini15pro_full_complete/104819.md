kecklish:/ˈkɛklɪʃ/
adjective
易呕吐的，想吐的，令人作呕的
causing or likely to cause vomiting; nauseating
The smell of the rotting food was kecklish. 腐烂食物的气味令人作呕。
近义词: nauseating, sickening, nauseous, queasy
反义词: appetizing, appealing
