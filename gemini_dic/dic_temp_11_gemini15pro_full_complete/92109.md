hyperromanticism:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmæntɪsɪzəm/
n.
过度浪漫主义；极端浪漫主义
An exaggerated or excessive expression of romanticism, characterized by a heightened emotional sensibility, an emphasis on individualism and imagination, and often a fascination with the exotic, the mysterious, or the morbid.
Her novels are full of hyperromanticism, with brooding heroes and passionate love affairs. 她的小说充满了过度浪漫主义，忧郁的英雄和充满激情的情事。
idealism, sentimentalism, emotionalism
realism, pragmatism
noun
