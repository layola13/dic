indiscernible:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnɪbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɝːnɪbl/
adjective
难以辨认的；无法察觉的
not able to be clearly seen, heard, or understood
The differences between the two versions are almost indiscernible. 这两个版本之间的差异几乎难以辨认。
近义词: imperceptible, undetectable, invisible, inaudible
反义词: discernible, perceptible, visible, audible
词性: adjective


indiscernibly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnib(ə)li/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɝːnɪbli/
adverb
难以辨认地；无法察觉地
in a way that is not able to be clearly seen, heard, or understood
The object moved almost indiscernibly slowly. 物体移动得非常缓慢，几乎难以察觉。
近义词: imperceptibly, undetectably
反义词: discernibly, perceptibly
词性: adverb


词源:
From Late Latin indiscernibilis, from in- "not" + discernibilis "distinguishable," from discernere "to separate, distinguish."
