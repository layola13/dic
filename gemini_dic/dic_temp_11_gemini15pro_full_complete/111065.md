listeners:/ˈlɪsənərz/| n.|听众，收听者|People who listen to something, such as a radio program or a speech.|The listeners were captivated by the speaker's story. 演讲者的故事吸引了听众。|近义词：audience, hearers|反义词:speakers, broadcasters|词性:名词

Etymology:
From listen (v.) + -er (agent suffix) + -s (plural suffix).  The verb "listen" comes from Middle English listnen,  from Old English hlysnan, of Germanic origin.
