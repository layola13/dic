bargainor:/ˈbɑːrɡɪnər/| n.|卖方；让与人|The bargainor is responsible for delivering the goods as agreed upon in the contract.  卖方有责任按照合同约定交付货物。|例句：The bargainor transferred ownership of the property to the bargainee. 卖方将财产所有权转给了买方。

例句：The bargainor agreed to sell the car for a reasonable price. 卖方同意以合理的价格出售汽车。


近义词：seller, vendor|反义词:bargainee, buyer|词性:noun
