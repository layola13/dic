deodorization:/diːˈoʊdəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|除臭，去味|The process of removing or masking unpleasant odors.|The deodorization of the refrigerator was necessary after the power outage.  冰箱断电后需要除臭。|近义词:odor removal, neutralization|反义词:aromatization, perfuming
词性:名词


deodorize:/diːˈoʊdəraɪz/
v.|除臭，去除…的气味|To eliminate or mask an unpleasant odor.|I need to deodorize the cat litter box. 我需要给猫砂盆除臭。|近义词:freshen, air out|反义词:scent, perfume
词性:动词

deodorized:/diːˈoʊdəraɪzd/
adj.|除臭的，去味的|Having had its odor removed.|The deodorized room smelled much better. 除臭后的房间气味好多了。|近义词:odorless, scentless|反义词:scented, perfumed
词性:形容词


词源:  From de- + odor + -ize + -ation.  "De-" prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse", "odor" meaning "smell", "-ize" suffix meaning "to cause to be", and "-ation" suffix forming nouns of action or process.
