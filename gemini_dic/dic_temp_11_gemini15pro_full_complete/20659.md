blacksmithery:/ˈblækˌsmɪθəri/ /ˈblækˌsmɪθri/
n.
铁匠铺；锻造术；铁匠工艺
The art or craft of a blacksmith; working with iron.  The place where a blacksmith works.
He learned blacksmithery from his father. 他从他父亲那里学习了锻造术。
The old blacksmithery stood abandoned on the edge of town.  古老的铁匠铺废弃在城镇边缘。
近义词: smithing, forging, metalworking
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "office work" in a contrasting context)
词性: noun
