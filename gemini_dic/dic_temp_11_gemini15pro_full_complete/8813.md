antiephialtic:/ˌæntiɛˈfaɪæltɪk/ | adj. | 预防噩梦的；止噩梦的 | Preventing or curing nightmares. |  She took an antiephialtic herb before bed hoping for a peaceful night. 她睡前服用了预防噩梦的草药，希望能睡个安稳觉。 |  Example: He searched for an antiephialtic remedy to combat his recurring nightmares. 他寻找一种止噩梦的疗法来对抗他反复出现的噩梦。 | Synonyms: nightmare-preventing, oneirotic | Antonyms: nightmare-inducing | adjective


Etymology:
From anti- "against" + epi- "upon, over" +  -altic from Greek *altikos "of leaping," *altos "leaping"
