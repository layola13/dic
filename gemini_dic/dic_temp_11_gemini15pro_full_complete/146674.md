overhead:/ˈəʊvəˌhed/(UK) /ˈoʊvərˌhed/(US)| adj. n.|经常性的费用；头顶上的；|Costs that do not vary with the level of production or sales, such as rent, rates, and administrative expenses.  A structure or fixture situated above head height.|例句：The rent for the office is a significant overhead expense. 办公室租金是一项重要的经常性开支。|近义词：expenses, costs, indirect costs, burden, on high, above|反义词：revenue, income, profit, below, underneath|词性:形容词/名词
overhead:/ˈəʊvəˌhed/(UK) /ˈoʊvərˌhed/(US)| adv.|在头顶上；|Above one's head; aloft.|例句：The plane flew overhead. 飞机在头顶飞过。|近义词：above, aloft|反义词：below, beneath|词性:副词
