donee:/dəˈniː/ /doʊˈniː/
n.
受赠人；受赠者
a person or organization that receives something, especially a gift of money or property
例句：The donee of the gift was a local charity. 这份礼物的受赠者是当地一家慈善机构。

例句：The museum is a grateful donee of the painting. 博物馆很感激地接受了这幅画的捐赠。

近义词：recipient, beneficiary
反义词：donor, grantor
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *done, past participle of doner “to give,” from Latin donare “to give, present,” from donum “gift.”
