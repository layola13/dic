nipping:/ˈnɪpɪŋ/| v.| 钳，夹，掐，咬; 阻止…的发生;  偷窃;  迅速前往|He nipped my finger with the pliers. 他用钳子夹了我的手指。|Synonyms: pinch, bite, snip, nip in the bud, steal |Antonyms: release, let go, encourage, promote| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *knippan "to pinch, squeeze," of uncertain origin. Related to Dutch knijpen, German kneifen.
