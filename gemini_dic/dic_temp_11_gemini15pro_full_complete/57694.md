drawn:/drɔːn/ | adj. | 疲惫的；憔悴的；紧张的；拉长的；拔出的；平局的 | looking strained and tense, often because of illness, tiredness, or worry |  例句：She looked drawn and tired after her long journey. 她长途旅行后看起来疲惫不堪。

例句：He had a drawn expression on his face. 他的脸上带着紧张的表情。| 近义词：haggard, gaunt, exhausted, tense, strained | 反义词：relaxed, refreshed, energetic | 词性：形容词

drawn:/drɔːn/ | v. |  画；拉；拖；吸引；抽出；提取；得出；招致；引来；(比赛)打成平局 | past participle of draw | 例句：The curtains were drawn. 窗帘拉上了。

例句：He drew his sword and charged. 他拔出剑冲锋陷阵。 | 近义词：pull, haul, attract, extract, deduce, incur | 反义词：push, repel | 词性：动词
