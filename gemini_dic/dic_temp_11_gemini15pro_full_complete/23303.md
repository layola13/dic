buoyancy /ˈbɔɪənsi/ | n. | 浮力，轻快，乐观 | The ability to float in water or other fluids. Also refers to a cheerful and optimistic disposition. | The ship's buoyancy kept it afloat.  这艘船的浮力使它漂浮着。| synonyms: floatability, lightness, cheerfulness, optimism | antonyms: heaviness, pessimism, depression | noun

buoyant /ˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. | 有浮力的，轻快的，乐观的 | Able to float or tending to rise in a fluid; cheerful and optimistic. | The buoyant balloons rose quickly into the sky. 这些充满浮力的气球迅速升上天空。  He was in a buoyant mood after receiving the good news. 他在收到好消息后心情很愉快。 | synonyms:  floating, light, cheerful, optimistic | antonyms: sinking, heavy, pessimistic, gloomy | adjective
