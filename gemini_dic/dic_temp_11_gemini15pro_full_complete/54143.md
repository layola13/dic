disengagement:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/
n.
脱离，解脱，分开，撤离
The disengagement of the troops from the war zone was a complex operation. 部队从战区的撤离是一项复杂的操作。
The process of disengagement from a stressful job can be challenging. 从一份压力大的工作中解脱出来的过程可能具有挑战性。
近义词:separation, detachment, withdrawal, extrication
反义词:engagement, involvement, connection
