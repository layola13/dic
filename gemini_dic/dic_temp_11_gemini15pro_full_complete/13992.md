auriculate: /ɔːˈrɪkjʊlət/ | adj. | 耳状的；有耳廓的 | Shaped like an ear or earlobe; having ear-shaped appendages. | The plant has auriculate leaves. 这种植物有耳状的叶子。|Synonyms: eared, auriform | Antonyms: earless | adjective

auriculate: /ɔːˈrɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. | 使成耳状 | To shape like an ear or earlobe. |  The craftsman auriculated the edges of the metal plate. 工匠把金属板的边缘做成了耳状。| Synonyms: shape, mold, fashion | Antonyms:  N/A | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *auriculātus*, past participle of *auriculāre* "to provide with ears," from *auricula* "little ear," diminutive of *auris* "ear."
