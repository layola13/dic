onocerin:/oʊˈnɒsərɪn/ | n. |  齐墩果烷-3β-醇; 白桦脂醇;  | A triterpenoid alcohol found in various plant resins.  A crystalline substance found especially in the corky outer bark of the cork oak.  |  Onocerin is a key component of cork oak bark. 白桦脂醇是栓皮栎树皮的关键成分。  Onocerin has been studied for its potential pharmaceutical properties. 白桦脂醇的潜在药用价值已得到研究。 | 近义词: betulin (桦木醇), lupeol (羽扇豆醇) | 反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: noun
