cotectic:/koʊˈtɛktɪk/
adjective|共结的，共晶的|Relating to or denoting the lowest temperature at which a particular mixture of substances can exist as a liquid.|The cotectic point on the phase diagram represents the composition and temperature at which two or more solid phases crystallize simultaneously from a melt.|The cotectic texture of the rock indicates simultaneous crystallization of two minerals.|近义词:eutectic (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically refers specifically to the lowest melting point of a mixture)|反义词:None|词性:adjective
cotectic:/koʊˈtɛktɪk/
noun|共结点，共晶点|The point on a phase diagram representing the composition and temperature at which two or more solid phases crystallize simultaneously from a melt.|The cotectic is reached when the melt cools to a specific temperature and composition.|The cotectic in this system is located at 70% A and 30% B.|近义词:eutectic (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically refers specifically to the lowest melting point of a mixture)|反义词:None|词性:noun
