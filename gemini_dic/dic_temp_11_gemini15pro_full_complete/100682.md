intra-linguistic:/ˌɪntrə lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/
adjective
语言内的；内部语言的
Relating to the internal structure of a language, rather than its relationship to other languages.
This dictionary focuses on intra-linguistic definitions, explaining words using other words within the same language. 这本词典侧重于语言内部的定义，用同一语言中的其他词语来解释词语。
Intra-linguistic variation can be observed in different dialects of the same language. 在同一语言的不同方言中可以观察到语言内部的变化。
近义词:endolinguistic
反义词:interlinguistic
词性:adjective
