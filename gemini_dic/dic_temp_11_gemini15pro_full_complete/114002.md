magistrateship: /ˈmædʒɪstreɪtʃɪp/
n.
地方法官的职位；地方治安法官的职权
The office or position of a magistrate.
She was appointed to the magistrateship last year. 她去年被任命为地方法官。
synonyms:  judgeship, justiceship, magistracy
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: dismissal, removal from office)
noun
