leatherette:/ˌlɛðəˈrɛt/| n.|人造革，仿皮|An artificial leather-like material, typically made from coated fabric or plastic.|The car seats were upholstered in a cheap leatherette.|近义词：faux leather, imitation leather, synthetic leather, vinyl|反义词:genuine leather, real leather|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From leather + -ette (a suffix forming diminutives, and also denoting imitation or substitute materials).
