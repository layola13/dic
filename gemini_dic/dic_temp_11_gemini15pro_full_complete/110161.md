liegeless:/ˈlaɪdʒləs/
adjective
无臣属关系的；无封地的；无附庸的
Without liegemen or vassals; not bound by feudal allegiance.
The king declared all the land liegeless after the rebellion. 国王在叛乱后宣布所有土地都无臣属关系。
The newly independent nation declared itself liegeless, free from any outside authority.  这个新独立的国家宣布自己无附庸，不受任何外部权威的约束。
近义词: independent, autonomous, free
反义词: subservient, dependent, vassal
词性: adjective

词源:  From liege + -less.  Liege comes from Old French and ultimately from Late Latin *laeticus, meaning "relating to a laetus" (a semi-free peasant, possibly of Germanic origin).  The suffix -less indicates the absence of something.
