idiomatical:/ˌɪdiəˈmætɪkl/
adj.
惯用的；符合语言习惯的
characteristic of a particular language; using or containing idioms
Her English is fluent and idiomatical. 她的英语流利且地道。
近义词:colloquial, informal, natural
反义词:formal, literal, stilted
词性:adjective
词源:Late 16th century: from French idiomatique or late Latin idiomaticus, from Greek idiōmatikos, from idiōma ‘peculiarity’ (see idiom).
