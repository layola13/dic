bussings:/ˈbʌsɪŋz/
|n.|公共汽车接送；（尤指）用校车接送学生|The practice of transporting students by bus to schools outside their neighborhoods, especially to achieve racial integration.  The bussing system is efficient but expensive. 校车系统效率很高，但也很昂贵。|例句：The bussing of children to different schools has been controversial.  用校车接送孩子去不同的学校一直备受争议。

例句：Bussings were organized to take the refugees to safer areas.  组织了公共汽车将难民送往更安全的地区。|近义词：transportation, busing|反义词：walking, cycling|词性：名词

|n.|公共汽车接送；（尤指）用校车接送学生|The practice of transporting students by bus to schools outside their neighborhoods, especially to achieve racial integration. 例句：The busing program helped to integrate the schools. 校车接送计划帮助学校实现了种族融合。|近义词：transporting, conveying|反义词：walking, cycling|词性：名词 (动名词形式，更常见)
