lamelliferous:/ˌlæməˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
薄片状的；由薄片组成的
bearing or composed of thin plates or lamellae
例句：The lamelliferous gills of the mushroom released spores.  蘑菇的薄片状菌褶释放了孢子。

例句：The lamelliferous structure of the mineral allowed it to easily cleave. 这种矿物的薄片状结构使其易于劈开。
synonym: laminar, foliated, scaly
antonym: non-lamellated, solid, compact
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lamellifer, from lamella (“small plate, thin layer”) + -fer (“bearing”).
