infirmest:/ɪnˈfɜːrmɪst/
adjective
最衰弱的，最不坚定的
Superlative form of infirm: most infirm.  Meaning extremely weak in body or health.  No longer in common usage; replaced by "most infirm."
He visited his infirmest relative in the hospital. 他去医院探望了他最虚弱的亲戚。
Near Synonyms: weakest, frailest, feeblest
Antonyms: strongest, healthiest, most robust
词性: 形容词
