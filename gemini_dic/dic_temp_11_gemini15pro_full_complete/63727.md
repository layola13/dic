ephraim:/ˈiːfreɪm/ | n. | 以法莲 | A masculine given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "fruitful" or "doubly fruitful". It is also used to refer to a tribe of Israel, descended from Ephraim, the younger son of Joseph. | Ephraim was a significant figure in the Old Testament. | 近义词: (as a name) Joseph, Benjamin | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun
