gymnodont:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌdɒnt/ (US) /ˈdʒɪmnəʊˌdɒnt/ (UK)| n. |鲀鱼；硬骨鱼纲鲀形目鱼类|A fish of the order Tetraodontiformes, characterized by a tough, often inflatable body and beak-like jaws.  These fish lack pelvic fins and usually have reduced or absent dorsal and/or anal fin spines.  Many species contain tetrodotoxin, a powerful neurotoxin.|例句：The pufferfish is a type of gymnodont. 河豚是鲀鱼的一种。

例句：Gymnodonts are known for their ability to inflate their bodies. 鲀鱼以其膨胀身体的能力而闻名。|近义词：pufferfish, blowfish, swellfish, tetraodontiform|反义词:  (No specific antonym exists, as it's a taxonomic classification) |词性:noun
