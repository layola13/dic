outpour:/ˈaʊtpɔːr/ (US), /ˈaʊtpɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|倾泻，涌出，流露|A sudden and abundant outpouring of emotion.  一阵突如其来的强烈情感的流露。|例句：There was an outpour of grief after the tragedy. 悲剧发生后，人们悲痛欲绝。|近义词：gush, surge, efflux, outburst, eruption|反义词：influx, intake|词性：名词

outpour:/ˈaʊtpɔːr/ (US), /ˈaʊtpɔː(r)/ (UK)| v.|倾泻，涌出，流露|The volcano outpoured lava and ash. 火山喷出熔岩和火山灰。|例句：Her heart outpoured with gratitude. 她的心中充满了感激之情。|近义词：gush, pour, spill, discharge|反义词：absorb, contain|词性：动词
