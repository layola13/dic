delegitimize:/diːləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/
verb
使失去合法性；剥夺合法地位；非合法化
To remove the legitimacy from something; to make something seem no longer valid or justifiable.
The opposition party tried to delegitimize the election results. 反对党试图使选举结果失去合法性。
The constant stream of fake news is designed to delegitimize the media. 不断涌现的假新闻旨在使媒体失去合法性。
synonyms: discredit, invalidate, undermine, devalue
antonyms: legitimize, validate, authorize, endorse
词性: 动词
