out-feeding: /ˌaʊtˈfiːdɪŋ/
|n.| 外部供料，向外觅食 |The act of feeding animals outside of their normal enclosure or habitat, or the act of one animal obtaining more food than another within the same environment.| The farmer practiced out-feeding to supplement the grazing of his cattle.  农民进行外部供料以补充牛的放牧。|近义词：supplementary feeding, foraging (in some contexts), provisioning|反义词：confining, restricting (in the context of animal movement)|词性：名词


out-feeding: /ˌaʊtˈfiːdɪŋ/
|v.| (指动物)胜过…觅食，比…吃得多|The act of one animal consuming more food than another in a competitive environment.|The dominant male out-fed the other members of the pack.  占统治地位的雄性比狼群中的其他成员吃得更多。|近义词：outcompete (in foraging), dominate (in feeding)|反义词：be outcompeted, lose out (in feeding)|词性：动词
