mercy:/ˈmɜːrsi/ (US) /ˈmɜːsi/ (UK)| n.|仁慈，怜悯，宽恕|Compassion or forgiveness shown toward someone whom it is within one's power to punish or harm.|The judge showed the defendant mercy and gave him a lighter sentence. 法官对被告表示了仁慈，并给予他较轻的判决。|Synonyms: compassion, clemency, leniency, pity, grace|Antonyms: cruelty, ruthlessness, severity, harshness|noun
