guardful:/ˈɡɑːrdfʊl/| adj.|警惕的，谨慎的|Watchful and cautious; vigilant.  Characterized by careful attention to potential dangers or threats.|The guardful dog barked at the approaching stranger.  警惕的狗对着走近的陌生人吠叫。|近义词：watchful, vigilant, cautious, wary, alert|反义词：careless, heedless, negligent, inattentive|词性:形容词

词源: From "guard" (meaning to protect or watch over) + "-ful" (a suffix indicating abundance or full of).  Ultimately from the Frankish *wardon, meaning "to watch."
