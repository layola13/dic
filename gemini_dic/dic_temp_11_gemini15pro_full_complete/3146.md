estuary:/ˈestʃʊəri/ (US), /ˈestʃəri/ (UK)| n. |河口，港湾|A partly enclosed coastal body of water with one or more rivers or streams flowing into it, and with a free connection to the open sea.  It's a transition zone between river environments and maritime environments.|The Thames estuary is home to a variety of wildlife. 泰晤士河口是各种野生动物的家园。

The city of New Orleans is located near the estuary of the Mississippi River. 新奥尔良市位于密西西比河河口附近。|近义词：inlet, firth, bay|反义词:upstream|词性:noun
