antihumanism:/ˈæntiˈhjuːmənɪzəm/| n.|反人道主义，反人文主义|A philosophical stance that questions or rejects the centrality of the human experience, especially the values, capacities, or perspectives of human beings.|Some critics see antihumanism in certain postmodern philosophies. 一些评论家在某些后现代哲学中看到了反人道主义。|近义词：misanthropy, inhumanity|反义词:humanism|词性:noun

antihumanism:/ˈæntiˈhjuːmənɪzəm/| adj.|反人道主义的，反人文主义的|Characterized by a philosophy that questions or rejects the centrality of the human experience.|The antihumanist stance often challenges anthropocentrism. 反人道主义立场经常挑战人类中心主义。|近义词：misanthropic, inhuman|反义词:humanistic|词性:adjective
