microcyte: /ˈmaɪkrəˌsaɪt/ | n. | 小红细胞 | A small red blood cell, smaller than normal.  Microyctes are commonly associated with iron deficiency anemia. |  The blood test revealed a high number of microcytes, indicating possible anemia. 血液测试显示小红细胞数量很高，表明可能患有贫血。 |  Microcytic red blood cells were observed on the blood smear. 在血涂片上观察到小红细胞。 | synonyms: microerythrocyte | antonyms: macrocyte | noun
