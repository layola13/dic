lacunose:/ləˈkjuːnəʊs/ /ləˈkjuːnoʊs/
adjective
多孔的；坑状的；有间隙的
Characterized by gaps, holes, or missing parts; having a pitted or perforated surface.
The lacunose surface of the moon is due to meteor impacts. 月球表面坑坑洼洼是由于陨石撞击造成的。
The cheese had a lacunose texture.  这种奶酪的质地有很多小孔。
porous, pitted, perforated, honeycombed, gap-ridden
smooth, solid, dense, compact
词性：形容词
