fellas:/ˈfeləz/| n.|伙计们，家伙们|An informal term of address for a group of men, often used to express camaraderie or familiarity.|例句：Hey fellas, what are you up to? 嘿，伙计们，你们在干什么？

例句：Alright fellas, let's get this show on the road. 好吧，伙计们，我们开始吧。|近义词：guys, dudes, boys, mates|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly ladies if referring specifically to a mixed group)|词性:名词(plural)

词源: Shortened form of "fellows," from Middle English *felawes*, plural of *felawe*, meaning "companion, partner, associate." Ultimately from Old Norse *félagi*.
