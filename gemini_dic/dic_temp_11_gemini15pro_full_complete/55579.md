disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/ (US), /dɪˈstɜːb/ (UK)| vt.|打扰，扰乱，使不安|To interrupt the peace, quiet, or regular course of something or someone; to cause someone to feel anxious or upset.|例句：The loud noise disturbed my sleep. 嘈杂的声音打扰了我的睡眠。

|例句：The news disturbed her greatly.  这个消息让她非常不安。|近义词：interrupt, bother, upset, agitate|反义词：soothe, calm, comfort|verb
