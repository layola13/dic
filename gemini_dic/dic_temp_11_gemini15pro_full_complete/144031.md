outrhymed:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmd/
verb
胜过韵律；以更优美的韵律盖过
to surpass in rhyming; to outdo or defeat in a rhyming contest.
Shakespeare outrhymed all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的韵律胜过他所有的同时代人。

outrhymed:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmd/
adjective
以更优美的韵律盖过的
surpassed in rhyming.
He felt outrhymed by the young poet. 他觉得自己被这位年轻诗人的韵律盖过了。



Etymology:
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "rhymed" (past participle of "rhyme")

Synonyms:
outshone, surpassed, excelled, exceeded, triumphed over (in the context of rhyming)


Antonyms:
outdone,  defeated (in the context of rhyming)
