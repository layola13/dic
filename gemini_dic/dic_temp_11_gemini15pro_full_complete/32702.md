chloridate:/ˈklɔːrɪdeɪt/| v. |氯化|To treat or combine with chlorine or a chlorine compound.|They chloridated the swimming pool to kill bacteria. 他们对游泳池进行了氯化消毒以杀死细菌。|The water supply is chloridated. 供水系统进行了氯化处理。|近义词：chlorinize|反义词: dechlorinate|词性:动词

词源: chlor- (from Greek khlōros, meaning "pale green," referring to the color of chlorine gas) + -ate (a suffix forming verbs denoting treatment with a substance).
