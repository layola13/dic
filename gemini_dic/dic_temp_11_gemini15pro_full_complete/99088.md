interbred:/ˌɪntərˈbrɛd/ |v. (interbreeds, interbreeding, interbred)|杂交，混种|To breed or cause to breed with members of another breed or group.|The two species of birds have interbred over the years, creating a hybrid population. 这两种鸟类多年来一直在杂交，创造了一个杂交种群。|Synonyms: crossbreed, hybridize, mix|Antonyms: purebred|verb

interbred:/ˌɪntərˈbrɛd/ |adj.|杂交的，混种的|Bred from parents of different breeds or groups.|The farmer raised interbred cattle for their increased hardiness.  这位农民饲养杂交牛以提高它们的耐寒性。|Synonyms: crossbred, hybrid, mixed|Antonyms: purebred|adjective
