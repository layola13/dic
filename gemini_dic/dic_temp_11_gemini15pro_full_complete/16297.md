bar-coding:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdɪŋ/ |n.|条形码编码；条码化|The process of representing data with bar codes.|Supermarkets use bar-coding to speed up checkout.|barcode scanning, barcode reading|N/A|noun
bar code: /ˈbɑːrˌkoʊd/ |n.|条形码|A machine-readable code in the form of parallel lines of varying widths, printed on and identifying a product.|The cashier scanned the bar code on the milk carton.|barcode, Universal Product Code (UPC)|N/A|noun

Etymology:
"bar" (referring to the lines) + "code" (system of symbols representing data) - First appeared in the mid-20th century.
