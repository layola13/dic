neoteric:/ˌniːəˈtɛrɪk/| adj.|新近的；现代的；新式的|Relating to or characteristic of the present; modern.  Of recent origin or introduction.|例句：The architect's neoteric design incorporated sustainable materials and smart technology.  这位建筑师的新式设计融合了可持续材料和智能技术。

例句：Her neoteric approach to teaching challenged traditional methods. 她新颖的教学方法挑战了传统方法。|近义词：modern, contemporary, recent, new, novel, innovative|反义词：old, ancient, traditional, outdated, archaic|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *neotericus*, from Ancient Greek *νεωτερικός* (neōterikós, “recent, modern”), from *νεωτερίζω* (neōterízō, “innovate”), from *νέος* (néos, “new, young”).
