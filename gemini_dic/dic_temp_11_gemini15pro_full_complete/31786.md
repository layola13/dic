checkering:/ˈtʃɛkərɪŋ/
n.
方格图案; 格子花纹; 交叉线
a pattern of squares, often used on cloth or wood
The stock of the rifle had elaborate checkering.  这支步枪的枪托有精致的格子花纹。

近义词:crosshatch, grid, lattice, tessellation
反义词:plainness, smoothness
