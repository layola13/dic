backache:/ˈbækˌeɪk/ | n. | 背痛 | A pain in the back. | I have a terrible backache from lifting heavy boxes. 我因为搬重箱子背疼得厉害。 | 近义词: back pain, dorsalgia | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 comfort, well-being 表示身体的舒适状态) | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From "back" + "ache". "Back" comes from Old English "bæc", and "ache" comes from Old English "acan".
