nonplasticities:/ˌnɒnplæˈstɪsɪtiz/
noun
非塑性
The lack of plasticity or the inability to be molded or deformed permanently.  This term refers to the properties of a material that prevent it from undergoing permanent deformation under stress.
The nonplasticities of the metal made it unsuitable for shaping.  这种金属的非塑性使其不适合塑形。
Near Antonyms:  malleability, ductility, plasticity
Antonyms: plasticity
