consecutively:/kənˈsɛkjʊtɪvli/
adverb|连续地，接连地|in a sequence; one after another without interruption.|The team won three games consecutively.  该队连续赢了三场比赛。|例句：He was elected for five consecutive terms. 他连续五届当选。|近义词：successively, sequentially, continuously|反义词:intermittently, sporadically|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *consecutivus, from Latin consequi (“to follow closely”), from con- (“together”) + sequi (“to follow”).
