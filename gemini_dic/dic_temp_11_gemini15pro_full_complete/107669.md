lani: /ˈlɑːniː/ /ˈleɪniː/
noun
天堂; 天上;  天空;  至高无上的幸福
heaven; celestial sphere; sky; supreme bliss.

Lani is a Hawaiian word that refers to heaven, the sky, or a state of supreme bliss. It is often used as a name.

例句：
The sunset painted the lani in vibrant hues.
日落将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。

Her name, Lani, reflects her parents' hope for a blissful life.
她的名字Lani反映了她父母对她幸福生活的期望。


近义词: heaven, paradise, sky, firmament
反义词: hell, earth

词性: noun
