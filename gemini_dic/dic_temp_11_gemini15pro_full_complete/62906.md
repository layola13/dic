enlisters:/ɪnˈlɪstərz/| n.|征募者；应募者；入伍者|People who enlist others, or who enlist themselves, especially into the armed forces.  Those who sign up or recruit. |The army enlisters were busy signing up new recruits. 陆军征兵人员正忙着招募新兵。|近义词：recruiters, enrollees, volunteers|反义词：dischargees, civilians|词性:名词
