over-emote:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊt/ | v. | 过度表情;表演过火 | To express emotions excessively or theatrically. | She tends to over-emote in dramatic situations, which can sometimes feel insincere.  她在戏剧性的情况下往往会表情过度，有时会让人觉得不真诚。| Example: He over-emoted during his audition, and it cost him the part. 他在试镜时表演过火，结果失去了这个角色。| synonyms: overact, exaggerate, dramatize | antonyms: understate, downplay | verb
