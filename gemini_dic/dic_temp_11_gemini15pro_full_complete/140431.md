oker:/ˈoʊkər/| n.|赭石，赭色|A natural earth pigment containing hydrated iron oxide, typically with clay, varying from light yellow to deep orange or brown.|The artist mixed oker with linseed oil to create a rich, earthy tone. 艺术家将赭石与亚麻籽油混合，创造出一种浓郁的泥土色调。|近义词：ochre, umber|反义词:|词性:noun
