nonvocationally: /ˌnɒnvoʊˈkeɪʃənəli/ /ˌnɑːnvoʊˈkeɪʃənəli/
adverb
非职业地；非业务地
in a way that is not connected with the work that you do in order to earn money
She paints nonvocationally. 她非职业地画画。
近义词:  amateurishly, avocationally
反义词:  professionally, vocationally
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + vocationally.  "Vocation" comes from the Latin "vocatio," meaning "a call" or "summons." The suffix "-al" forms adjectives, and "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.  Therefore, "nonvocationally" describes an action performed outside of one's calling or profession.
