dissemination:/dɪˌsemɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |传播，散布，宣传|The dissemination of information is crucial in a democracy. 信息的传播在民主社会中至关重要。|例句：The dissemination of the news through social media was rapid.  这条消息通过社交媒体迅速传播开来。

例句：The internet allows for the rapid dissemination of information globally. 互联网使得信息在全球范围内快速传播。

近义词：distribution, spreading, propagation, circulation |反义词：suppression, concealment, withholding |词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dis- (apart, in different directions) + seminare (to sow) + -tion (forming nouns of action).  Ultimately from the Latin word semen (seed).
