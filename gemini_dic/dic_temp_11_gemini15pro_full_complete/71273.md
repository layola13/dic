fixate:/ˈfɪkseɪt/| v. |注视，凝视；使固定；固着|To stare at intently; to focus one's gaze or attention on something. fixate:/ˈfɪkseɪt/| v. |(在…上)过分关注，念念不忘；执着于|To become obsessed or overly preoccupied with something or someone.|She fixated on the idea that she had been cheated. 她一心想着自己被骗了。|synonyms: obsess, dwell, brood, fret|antonyms: disregard, forget|verb
