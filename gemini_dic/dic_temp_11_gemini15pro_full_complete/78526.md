ghole:/ɡoʊl/| n.|洞穴，地洞; (尤指)老鼠洞; 骗局; 陷阱|A hole or burrow, especially one made by a small animal like a rat; a swindle or trap.|The rat disappeared into its ghole. 老鼠消失在它的洞穴里。|The investment scheme turned out to be a ghole. 这项投资计划结果是个骗局。|近义词：burrow, hole, den, pitfall, trap|反义词: mound, hill|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Marathi घोळ (ghoḷ) meaning "a hole", "a burrow". Possibly related to Sanskrit  गर्त (garta) meaning "pit", "hole".
