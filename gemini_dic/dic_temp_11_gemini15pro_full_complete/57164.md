dowdyish:/ˈdaʊdiɪʃ/ | adj. | 略显寒酸的，有点过时的 | Somewhat dowdy; shabby and unfashionable. |  She wore a dowdyish brown dress. 她穿着一件略显寒酸的棕色连衣裙。 |  The furniture was rather dowdyish. 家具显得有点过时。| 近义词: frumpy, shabby, unfashionable, outdated | 反义词: stylish, fashionable, chic, trendy | 词性: adjective

词源: From dowdy + -ish.  Dowdy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "dowd," meaning a dull, awkward woman.
