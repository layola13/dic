nonconspiratorial:/ˌnɑːnkənˌspɪrəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
非阴谋的; 非共谋的
Not involving or suggestive of a conspiracy; not secretive or clandestine.
The meeting had a nonconspiratorial atmosphere, with open discussion and transparent decision-making.  这次会议气氛不像是密谋，讨论公开，决策透明。
The witness gave a nonconspiratorial account of the events.  证人对事件的描述并不像合谋。
near synonym: open, aboveboard, forthright, candid
antonym: conspiratorial, clandestine, secretive, furtive
word origin:  From non- + conspiratorial, from conspiracy + -al.
