outking:/ˈaʊtkɪŋ/| v.|超越；胜过；比…更像国王|To surpass or defeat someone, especially in a way that demonstrates superior regal qualities or power. |例句：He outkinged all the other pretenders to the throne. 他胜过了所有其他王位觊觎者。

例句：She outkinged her rivals in terms of fundraising. 她在筹款方面胜过了她的对手。|近义词：surpass, outdo, outperform, eclipse, triumph over|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|词性:动词

词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning to surpass) and "king" (referring to a monarch).  The word suggests surpassing even a king in kingly attributes or actions.
