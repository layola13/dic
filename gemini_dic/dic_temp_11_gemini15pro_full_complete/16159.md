banjos:/ˈbændʒoʊz/ (US), /ˈbændʒəʊz/ (UK)
n.
班卓琴（复数）
A stringed musical instrument with a long neck and a round body like a tambourine, played by plucking or strumming the strings.
The banjos were tuned and ready for the bluegrass concert. 班卓琴已经调好音，准备 bluegrass 音乐会了。

banjo:/ˈbændʒoʊ/ (US), /ˈbændʒəʊ/ (UK)
n.
班卓琴（单数）
A stringed musical instrument with a long neck and a round body like a tambourine, played by plucking or strumming the strings.
He played a lively tune on his banjo. 他用班卓琴演奏了一曲欢快的曲子。

Etymology:  From  Kimbundu *mbanza.

Synonyms:  guitar, lute, mandolin (loosely)
Antonyms:  None (as it's a specific instrument)
