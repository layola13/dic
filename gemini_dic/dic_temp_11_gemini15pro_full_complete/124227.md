mosquitoes:/məˈskiːtəʊz/ (UK) /məˈskiːtोज/ (US)| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some types of mosquitoes spread serious diseases.|例句：Mosquitoes are a common nuisance in the summer. 夏天蚊子很常见。

例句：The mosquitoes were buzzing around my head all night. 蚊子整夜在我头上嗡嗡叫。


近义词：gnats|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a mosquito.  One might consider "dragonfly" as a predator of mosquitoes, representing an opposing force, but not a true antonym.)|词性: Noun (plural)
