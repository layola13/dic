nonreducibilities:/ˌnɒnrɪˌdjuːsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可约性(复数) | The state or quality of being nonreducible; the inability to be reduced or simplified further.  It often appears in mathematical or computational contexts. |  The nonreducibilities of certain computational problems make them difficult to solve efficiently.  某些计算问题的不可约性使它们难以有效地解决。|  irreducibilities, incompressibilities | reducibilities, simplicities | noun
