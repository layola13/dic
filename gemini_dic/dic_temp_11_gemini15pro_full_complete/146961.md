overirrigates: /ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪts/
verb
过度灌溉
To supply (land or crops) with too much water.
The farmer overirrigates his fields, leading to waterlogging.  农民过度灌溉了他的田地，导致土壤涝渍。
The automatic sprinkler system overirrigates the lawn if it's not adjusted for rainfall. 如果不根据降雨量进行调整，自动洒水系统会过度灌溉草坪。
synonyms: waterlog, flood, drench, soak
antonyms: underwater, dehydrate, parch
词性: 动词
