nonviscidnesses:/ˌnɒnvɪsˈkɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
无粘性，非粘稠性
The state or quality of being nonviscid; lack of viscosity.
The nonviscidnesses of the liquids allowed them to flow easily through the pipes.  这些液体的无粘性使它们很容易流过管道。
Near synonyms: fluidity, thinness
Antonyms: viscosity, stickiness, thickness
n.
