footloose:/ˈfʊtlus/| adj.|自由自在的，无拘无束的|Free to go or travel anywhere; not bound by responsibilities or commitments.|She's footloose and fancy-free, traveling the world with just a backpack.|近义词：free, unconstrained, independent, unattached|反义词：tied down, committed, restricted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *foot* (referring to freedom of movement) + *loose* (meaning unbound).
