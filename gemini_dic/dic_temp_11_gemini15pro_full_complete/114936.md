managery:/ˈmænɪdʒəri/ | n. | 管理人员；管理层；管理技巧 | The company's managery was praised for its effective leadership.  该公司的管理层因其有效的领导而受到称赞。 |  Example: The managery of the hotel was excellent.  这家酒店的管理非常出色。 | Synonym:  management, administration, leadership | Antonym: subordinates, staff, employees | noun

Etymology:  From "manage" + "-ery" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a place of activity, a state or condition, or a collection of things). "Manage" comes from Old French "menagier" (to handle), from "mener" (to lead), ultimately from Latin "manus" (hand).
