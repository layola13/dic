archaeolithic:/ˌɑːrkiːəʊˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. | 旧石器时代的 | Relating to the earliest period of the Stone Age, characterized by the use of rudimentary chipped stone tools. | The archaeologist discovered archaeolithic tools in the cave. 考古学家在山洞里发现了旧石器时代的工具。 |  The museum displayed several archaeolithic hand axes.  博物馆展出了几把旧石器时代的手斧。 | 近义词: Paleolithic | 反义词: Neolithic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *arkhaios* (ancient) + *lithos* (stone).
