harboring:/ˈhɑːrbərɪŋ/| v.|窝藏；怀有；庇护；包含|giving shelter to; holding (a thought or feeling)|The fugitives were harboring in a remote cabin. 逃犯们藏身在一个偏僻的小屋里。|近义词：sheltering, concealing, harbor, shielding, fostering|反义词：exposing, revealing|verb

harbor:/ˈhɑːrbər/| n. v.|港口；庇护；窝藏；怀有|a place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter, especially one protected from rough water by piers, jetties, and other artificial structures; to give shelter to; to hold (a thought or feeling)|The harbor was full of colorful fishing boats. 港口里满是色彩缤纷的渔船。|近义词：port, haven, shelter, conceal, foster|反义词：expose, reveal|noun, verb
