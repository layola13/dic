nonfrenetically: /ˌnɒnfrəˈnetɪkli/ (British) /ˌnɑːnfrəˈnetɪkli/ (American) | adv. | 非狂热地；平静地 |  In a way that is not frantic or marked by frenzy.  It describes an action or state that is calm, relaxed, and without excessive excitement or activity. |  He worked nonfrenetically, taking his time to ensure accuracy. 他工作起来不慌不忙，花时间确保准确性。| The crowd dispersed nonfrenetically after the concert.  演唱会结束后，人群平静地散去。| 近义词: calmly, peacefully, tranquilly, sedately | 反义词: frenetically, frantically, excitedly | 词性: 副词

词源:

* non-:  prefix meaning "not" (Latin origin)
* frenetic: adjective meaning "frantic; frenzied" (from Greek phrenitis, meaning "inflammation of the brain")
* -ally: adverbial suffix
