anti-dynastic:/ˌænti daɪˈnæstɪk/
adjective
反王朝的；反对世袭统治的
Opposed to the rule of a dynasty or hereditary succession of rulers.
The anti-dynastic movement gained momentum after the king's death.  反王朝运动在国王死后势头增强。
The protestors carried anti-dynastic banners. 抗议者们举着反王朝的旗帜。
近义词: anti-monarchical, anti-hereditary
反义词: dynastic, pro-monarchy
词性: adjective
