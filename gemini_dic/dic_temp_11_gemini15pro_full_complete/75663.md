fulciment:/ˈfʌlkrɪmənt/| n.|支点，支撑，支柱|A thing that provides support or acts as a foundation for something.|The central pillar acted as the fulciment for the entire structure. 中心柱子作为整个结构的支点。|Synonyms: support, prop, foundation, basis, underpinning|Antonyms: weakness, instability|noun

fulciment:/ˈfʌlkrɪmənt/| n.|支点，支撑，支柱|Something that serves as a prop or support.|Education is the fulciment of a strong society. 教育是一个强大社会的支柱。|Synonyms: cornerstone, linchpin, anchor|Antonyms: detriment, hindrance|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fulcimentum, from fulcire "to prop, support," from fulcrum "prop, support."
