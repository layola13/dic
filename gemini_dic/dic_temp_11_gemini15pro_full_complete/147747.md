overproduction:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdʌkʃən/
n.
过度生产，生产过剩
The overproduction of goods led to a fall in prices. 商品的过度生产导致价格下跌。
Overproduction can lead to waste and economic inefficiency. 过度生产会导致浪费和经济低效。
overproduction, surplus, glut
undersupply, shortage
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + production.  Over- means "excessive, too much". Production comes from the Latin prōdūcere, meaning "to lead forth, bring out, produce".
