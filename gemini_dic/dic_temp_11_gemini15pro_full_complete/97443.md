infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/ (US) /ɪnˈfjuːz/ (UK)| v. |灌输，注入；使充满|To fill something or someone with a particular feeling or quality.|例句：The coach infused the team with confidence. 教练给队员注入了信心。|例句：The tea is infused with herbs. 这茶里注入了草药。|近义词：instill, imbue, permeate, inspire|反义词：drain, deplete|词性：动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *infusus, past participle of infundere "to pour in," from in- "in" + fundere "to pour."
