credible: /ˈkrɛdəbəl/ (US) /ˈkrɛdɪbəl/ (UK) | adj. | 可信的，可靠的 |  Able to be believed; convincing. | The witness gave a credible account of the accident. 目击者对事故做了可信的描述。 |  believable, plausible, trustworthy |  unbelievable, implausible, dubious | adjective

credible: /ˈkrɛdəbəl/ (US) /ˈkrɛdɪbəl/ (UK) | n. | (罕见)可信的人或事 | Something or someone that is believable.  |  While the idea of time travel is not yet a credible, many scientists are working on the underlying theories.  虽然时间旅行的概念还不是一个可信的事实，但许多科学家正在研究其基本理论。 | believable thing, plausible thing | unbelievable thing, implausible thing | noun (rare usage)
