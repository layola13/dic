clickless:/ˈklɪklɪs/| adj.|无需点击的，无点击式的|Relating to a system or process that does not require clicking a mouse or other pointing device.  It typically describes user interfaces designed for touchscreens or voice commands.|Example:  The new clickless interface is very intuitive. 新的无点击界面非常直观。|Example: The clickless navigation makes browsing the website on my phone so much easier.  无点击导航使在我的手机上浏览网站变得更加容易。|近义词：touch-based, hands-free, automatic|反义词：click-based, manual|词性:形容词
