nonmitigations:/ˌnɒnˌmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. |  非缓解措施；未采取的缓解措施 |  Lack of actions taken to reduce the severity or impact of something, especially risks or negative consequences.  | The report highlighted the potential consequences of nonmitigations of climate change. 该报告强调了不缓解气候变化的潜在后果。 | The company's nonmitigations of cybersecurity risks led to a data breach.  该公司未采取网络安全风险缓解措施，导致数据泄露。|  inaction, failure to mitigate, lack of mitigation | mitigation, preventive measures | noun
