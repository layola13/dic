PHRASE->
Technically "in-back-of" is a phrase itself.  It is generally considered less formal than "behind".  Here are some phrases using "behind":

behind the times = 落后于时代
behind the scenes = 幕后
behind schedule = 落后于计划
fall behind = 落后
leave behind = 留下；遗留


注意事项:
"In-back-of" is more common in American English.  British English generally prefers "behind".
