misregistering:/ˌmɪsrɛˈdʒɪstərɪŋ/
verb
错误登记；登记错误；对位不准
The act of registering incorrectly or failing to align properly.
The printer was misregistering the colours, resulting in a blurry image. 打印机颜色对位不准，导致图像模糊。
misaligning, misplacing, incorrectly registering
correctly registering, aligning
