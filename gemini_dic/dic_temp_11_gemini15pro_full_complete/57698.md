drawrod:/ˈdrɔːrɒd/ (US) /ˈdrɔːrɒd/ (UK)| n.|拉杆，牵引杆|A metal rod, typically with a threaded end, used for pulling or tensioning something.|The drawrod on the wagon snapped, causing it to lose control. 马车的拉杆断了，导致它失去控制。|Synonyms: tie rod, tension rod, connecting rod|Antonyms: push rod|noun
