inquisitive:/ɪnˈkwɪzətɪv/| adj.|好奇的，爱打听的|Given to inquiry; eager for knowledge; curious.|例句：Children are naturally inquisitive. 孩子们天生好奇。|例句：She has an inquisitive mind and is always asking questions. 她有颗好奇的心，总是问问题。|近义词：curious, inquiring, questioning|反义词：apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
