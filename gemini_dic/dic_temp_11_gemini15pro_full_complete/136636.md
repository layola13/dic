nonscholar:/ˌnɒnˈskɒlə(r)/ | n. | 非学者；不爱学习的人 | A person who is not a scholar; someone who doesn't engage in scholarly pursuits or isn't known for academic achievement. | He was considered a nonscholar, preferring practical skills to book learning. 他被认为是一个非学者，更喜欢实践技能而不是书本学习。|  He was a nonscholar, more interested in sports than studies. 他对学习不感兴趣，是个非学者，更喜欢体育运动。 | 近义词: layman, nonacademic | 反义词: scholar, academic | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "scholar" (from Old French "escoler," ultimately from Latin "scholaris" meaning "belonging to a school")
