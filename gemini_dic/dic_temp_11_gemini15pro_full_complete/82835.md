词源: From Old English *gyldan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *gulþą (“gold”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰelh₃- (“yellow, green, gold”). Cognate with West Frisian goud, Dutch goud, German Gold, Danish guld, Swedish guld, Icelandic gull.
