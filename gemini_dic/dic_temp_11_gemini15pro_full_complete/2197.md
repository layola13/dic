addititious:/ˌædɪˈtɪʃəs/ | adj. | 附加的；外加的；添加的 | Introduced from outside; added, especially unnecessarily.  Something that is not inherent or essential but added extraneously. |  The cake had an addititious sweetness due to the artificial sweetener.  这个蛋糕由于添加了人工甜味剂，有一种外加的甜味。 | The addititious flavor made the dish taste artificial. 添加的调味料使这道菜尝起来像是人造的。 |  supplementary, additional, extraneous, extrinsic | inherent, intrinsic, essential | adjective
