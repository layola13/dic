duvet:/ˈduːveɪ/ /duːˈveɪ/| n.|羽绒被，鸭绒被|A soft quilt filled with down, feathers, or a synthetic fiber, used instead of an upper sheet and blankets.| I snuggled under the warm duvet. 我依偎在温暖的羽绒被下。|Synonyms: comforter, quilt, down comforter|Antonyms: sheet, blanket|noun

duvets:/ˈduːveɪz/ /duːˈveɪz/| n.|羽绒被(复数)|Plural form of duvet.|  We need new duvets for the guest bedrooms. 我们需要为客房准备新的羽绒被。|Synonyms: comforters, quilts, down comforters|Antonyms: sheets, blankets|noun
