Genghis Khan: /ˈɡɛŋɡɪs ˈkɑːn/ | n. | 成吉思汗 | The founder and first Great Khan of the Mongol Empire. |  Genghis Khan united the nomadic tribes of Northeast Asia. 成吉思汗统一了东北亚的游牧部落。| 近义词: (No direct synonym, but in terms of role: emperor, leader, conqueror) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology: Derived from the Mongolian "Chinggis Khaan," meaning "Oceanic Ruler" or "Universal Ruler."  The original Mongolian pronunciation is closer to "Chinggis."
