crabbiest:/ˈkræbiɪst/| adj.|最暴躁的，最易怒的|Superlative form of crabby: most crabby.  It means extremely irritable and bad-tempered.|例句：He was the crabbiest person I'd ever met. 他是我见过的脾气最坏的人。

例句：She was in a crabbiest mood this morning. 她今天早上心情非常糟糕。|近义词：grumpiest, grouchiest, surliest|反义词：pleasantest, happiest, cheerfullest|词性：形容词
Etymology: From crab + -y + -est.  The adjective "crabby" likely originated from the perceived sideways, irritable movements of crabs.
