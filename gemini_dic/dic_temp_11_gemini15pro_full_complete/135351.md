nonperceptibility:/ˌnɑːnpərˌsɛptɪˈbɪlɪti/
noun
非感知性；无法察觉
The nonperceptibility of the changes made it difficult to assess their impact. 这些变化难以察觉，因此难以评估其影响。
The drug's nonperceptibility makes it ideal for covert operations. 这种药物的不可感知性使其成为秘密行动的理想选择。
imperceptibility, invisibility, undetectability
perceptibility, visibility, detectability
