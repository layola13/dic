breakably:/ˈbreɪkəbli/| adv.|易碎地；脆弱地|In a way that is easily broken.  |The ornaments were packed breakably and several were damaged in transit.  这些装饰品包装得很不牢固，有几个在运输过程中损坏了。|The vase is breakably thin.  这个花瓶薄得易碎。|近义词：fragilely, delicately|反义词：sturdily, robustly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From break (v.) + -able (suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Break ultimately comes from the Old English *brecan. -able comes from the Old French -able and ultimately from the Latin -abilis. -ly comes from the Old English -lice.
