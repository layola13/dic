glaucine:/ˈɡlɔːsiːn/| n.|光彩碱，黄连碱|An alkaloid found in several plant species, including Glaucium flavum (yellow horned poppy), with anti-inflammatory, antitussive, and analgesic properties.|Glaucine is being investigated for its potential use in treating coughs. 光彩碱正在被研究用于治疗咳嗽的潜力。|Glaucine can be extracted from the yellow horned poppy. 光彩碱可以从黄罂粟中提取。|近义词: alkaloid (生物碱)|反义词:  (无明确的反义词)|词性:noun

词源: From *Glaucium* (the genus of the yellow horned poppy) + *-ine* (a suffix used to form names of alkaloids).
