irrespective:/ˌɪrɪˈspektɪv/| adj.|不考虑的，不顾的，无关的|Without taking something into account; regardless.|例句：The law applies to everyone, irrespective of race, creed, or color.  这项法律适用于所有人，不分种族、信仰或肤色。|例句：Irrespective of the cost, we must go ahead with the project. 不管费用多少，我们必须继续推进这个项目。|近义词：regardless, notwithstanding, no matter|反义词: considering, regarding|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + respective.  "In-" acts as a negating prefix, and "respective" relates to particular individuals or things.  Thus, "irrespective" means not relating to or dependent on particular individuals or things.
