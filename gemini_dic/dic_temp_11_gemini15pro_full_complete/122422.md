moaned:/moʊnd/ | v. | 呻吟，抱怨 | to make a long, low sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or sexual pleasure | He moaned in pain after he fell. 他摔倒后痛苦地呻吟。|近义词：groaned, complained, whined |反义词：rejoiced, cheered |词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *monen, from Old English mānan  "to complain, lament," from Proto-Germanic *mainaną (source also of Old High German meinen "to mean, think, complain," Old Norse meina "to harm, injure, complain of"), from Proto-Indo-European *moy-n-/*mey-n- "to make a sound, think."
