hooroo:/ˌhʊəˈruː/
interjection
再见，再会
An informal way of saying goodbye, originating in Australia.
例句：Hooroo, mate! See you later. 再见，伙计！回头见。

例句：Hooroo, everyone! Have a great time. 再见，各位！祝你们玩得开心。

近义词：goodbye, bye, see ya, cheerio
反义词：(None - it's a farewell)
词性：interjection
