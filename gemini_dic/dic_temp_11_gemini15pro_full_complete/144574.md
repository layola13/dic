outwards:/ˈaʊtwədz/ (UK) /ˈaʊtwərdz/ (US)| adv. adj.|向外地，外表的|Moving or directed away from a center or starting point; relating to the outside or exterior.|She pushed the door outwards. 她把门向外推。|Synonyms: outward, externally, outside|Antonyms: inwards, internally, inside|adverb/adjective

outward:/ˈaʊtwəd/ (UK) /ˈaʊtwərd/ (US)| adj. adv. n.|向外的，外表的，外面|Directed towards the outside; relating to the outside or exterior; the external appearance of something.|The outward appearance of the building was deceiving. 这栋楼的外观具有欺骗性。|Synonyms: external, outer, outside|Antonyms: inward, inner, inside|adjective/adverb/noun
