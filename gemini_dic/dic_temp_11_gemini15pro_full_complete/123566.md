monsterpiece:/ˈmɒnstəpiːs/ | n. | 杰作；巨著；非凡的作品 | A work of art, literature, or other creative endeavor of exceptional quality, often of great size or complexity.  It can sometimes have a negative connotation, suggesting something grotesque or excessive. |  His latest symphony is a true monsterpiece, spanning over three hours.  他最新的交响曲是一部真正的杰作，长达三个多小时。

|  The author's 1000-page novel is a monsterpiece of historical fiction.  这位作者的1000页小说是一部历史小说巨著。| 近义词：masterpiece, magnum opus, tour de force | 反义词：failure, debacle, flop | 词性: noun
