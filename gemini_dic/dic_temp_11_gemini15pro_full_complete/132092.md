noncreditors:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɛdɪtərz/
n.|非债权人|People or entities who are not owed money by a particular individual or company.  Those who do not have a financial claim against a debtor.|例句：The noncreditors were not involved in the bankruptcy proceedings. 非债权人没有参与破产程序。|例句：The distribution of assets will primarily benefit the creditors, not the noncreditors. 资产的分配将主要使债权人受益，而不是非债权人。|近义词：non-lenders|反义词:creditors
词性:名词
