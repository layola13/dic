n.
忽视者；不顾者；漠视者
People who disregard rules or advice are disregarders.
Those who disregard the warnings are disregarders of their own safety.
disregarders are those who ignore or pay no attention to something.
例句：The disregarders of the traffic laws caused the accident.  漠视交通法规的人造成了这起事故。

The disregarders of the doctor's advice suffered the consequences.  无视医生建议的人承担了后果。

近义词：ignorers, neglecters, slighters
反义词：observers, respecters, heeders
词性：名词(plural)
