noncontiguously:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪɡjuəsli/ | adv. | 不连续地，不相邻地 | Not touching; separated in space or time. | The data was stored noncontiguously across multiple servers. 数据不连续地存储在多台服务器上。| The islands are noncontiguously scattered across the ocean. 这些岛屿不连续地散布在海洋中。| 近义词: discontinuously, separately, intermittently | 反义词: contiguously, adjacently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From non- (not) + contiguous (touching; neighboring) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Contiguous" ultimately derives from the Latin "contingere" (to touch).
