flopper's:/ˈflɒpəz/ (US) /ˈflɒpəz/ (UK)| n. |失败者（的），笨拙者（的）|Something that belongs to or is associated with a flopper, someone who fails or is clumsy.  It's the possessive form of "flopper".|例句：The flopper's attempt at a graceful dive ended with a belly flop.  那个笨拙的家伙试图优雅地跳水，结果以一个肚子先着水的姿势结束。

例句：The flopper's excuses were as weak as his performance.  失败者的借口和他的表现一样无力。|近义词：loser's, klutz's, clumsy person's|反义词: winner's, expert's, adept's|词性:Possessive noun
