kriegers:/ˈkriːɡərz/| n.|战士们，勇士们|Kriegers is the plural form of Krieger, a German word meaning "warrior" or "soldier".  It refers to a group of warriors or soldiers.|The kriegers marched into battle. 勇士们投入战斗。|近义词：soldiers, warriors, fighters|反义词: pacifists|词性:名词

词源: From German *Krieger*, from Middle High German *krieger*, from Old High German *krīgāri*, from *krīg* (“strife, war”) + *-āri* (agent suffix). Cognate with Dutch * krijger*,  Swedish *krigare*.
