beatify:/biˈætɪfaɪ/ /biˈætəˌfaɪ/| vt.|使幸福；祝福；册封…为真福|To make supremely happy; to bless. In the Roman Catholic Church, to declare (a deceased person) to be among the blessed and thus entitled to specific religious honor.|Mother Teresa was beatified by the Catholic Church.  特蕾莎修女被天主教会册封为真福。|近义词：bless, exalt, glorify, sanctify|反义词：condemn, curse|verb
