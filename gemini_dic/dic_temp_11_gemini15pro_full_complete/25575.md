bumbled:/ˈbʌmbəld/ | v. | 踉跄；笨拙地行动；结结巴巴地说 | To speak or act clumsily or haltingly; to stumble. |  He bumbled his way through the speech, forgetting his lines several times. 他结结巴巴地做完了演讲，几次忘记了自己的台词。| stumble, fumble, stammer | articulate, eloquent | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From bumble (v.) + -ed.  The verb "bumble" likely originates from imitative or echoic origins, suggesting the sound of clumsy movement or speech.
