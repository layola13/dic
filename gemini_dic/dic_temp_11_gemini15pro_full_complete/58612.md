dullsome:/ˈdʌlsəm/| adj.|无聊的，沉闷的|Causing or marked by boredom; tedious.  Lacking interest or excitement.|The lecture was so dullsome that I nearly fell asleep.|近义词：boring, tedious, monotonous, uninteresting, tiresome|反义词：interesting, exciting, stimulating, engaging, captivating|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From *dull* + *-some*, a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "tending to".  *Dull* ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *dulą*, meaning "blunt" or "stupid".
