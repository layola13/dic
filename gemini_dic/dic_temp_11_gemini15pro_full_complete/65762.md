every-inch:/ˈevri ˈɪntʃ/
adjective
彻头彻尾的;不折不扣的
Thoroughly; completely; in every respect.  Used to emphasize the degree to which someone or something embodies a specified quality.
He was every inch a gentleman. 他是一位十足的绅士。
She was every inch the queen. 她举手投足都像个女王。
Synonyms: complete, total, absolute, thorough, utter, out-and-out, downright
Antonyms: partial, incomplete
词性: adjective
