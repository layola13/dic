dorps:/dɔrps/ | n. |村庄（尤指南非的）| A small village, especially in a rural area of South Africa.  Derived from Afrikaans. | Example:  Many people live in dorps scattered across the South African countryside. 许多人生活在散布在南非乡村的村庄里。| Example: The dorp had a small church and a general store.  这个村庄有一座小教堂和一家杂货店。|Synonym: village, hamlet, settlement | Antonym: city, metropolis | noun
