insignia:/ɪnˈsɪɡniə/ /ɪnˈsɪɡnjə/ | n. |徽章，标记，标志|A badge or distinguishing mark of military rank, office, or membership of an organization.|The president wore his insignia of office. 总统佩戴着他的职位徽章。|Synonyms: badge, emblem, symbol, crest, logo|Antonyms: anonymity, obscurity|noun

insignias:/ɪnˈsɪɡniəz/ /ɪnˈsɪɡnjəz/ | n. |徽章（insignia的复数形式）|Badges or distinguishing marks of military rank, office, or membership of an organization.|The soldiers proudly displayed their regimental insignias. 士兵们自豪地展示了他们团的徽章。|Synonyms: badges, emblems, symbols, crests, logos|Antonyms: anonymity, obscurity|noun
