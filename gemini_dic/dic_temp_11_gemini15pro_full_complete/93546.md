imaginations:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |想象力，想象物|The faculty or action of forming new ideas, or images or concepts of external objects not present to the senses.|Children have vivid imaginations. 孩子们有丰富的想象力。|Synonyms: fantasy, creativity, vision|Antonyms: reality, actuality|Noun

imaginations:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |幻想，空想|The ability of the mind to be creative or resourceful.|His head was full of romantic imaginations. 他满脑子都是浪漫的幻想。|Synonyms: fancies, illusions, daydreams|Antonyms: facts, truths|Noun
