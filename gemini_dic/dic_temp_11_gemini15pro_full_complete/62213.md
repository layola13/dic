encowl:/ɪnˈkaʊl/
verb
罩上；覆盖；用头巾包裹
to cover or enclose something, especially with a draped cloth or garment
The monk was encowled in a dark robe.  僧侣身披深色长袍。
近义词: cover, cloak, envelop, shroud
反义词: uncover, expose
词性:v.
