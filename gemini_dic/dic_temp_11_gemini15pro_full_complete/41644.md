correctly:/kəˈrektli/ | adv. |正确地；准确地| In a way that is true, factual or appropriate; free from error. | She answered all the questions correctly. 她正确地回答了所有问题。| accurately, properly, rightly | incorrectly, wrongly, inaccurately | adverb

词源:
From Middle English *correctli, from Old French correctement, from Late Latin *correctāmentum, from Latin corrēctus (past participle of corrigō (“I correct, set right; put straight; amend; improve”)), from com- (“together”) + regō (“I rule, guide, govern, direct”).
