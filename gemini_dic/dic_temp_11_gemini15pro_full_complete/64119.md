epsom:/ˈɛpsəm/| n. |埃普索姆(英国地名)；泻盐|A town in Surrey, England, famous for its racecourse and Epsom salts. Epsom salts are magnesium sulfate, used as a laxative and in bath salts. | Epsom is a popular destination for horse racing enthusiasts. 埃普索姆是赛马爱好者的热门目的地。| Epsom salts are known for their therapeutic properties.泻盐以其治疗特性而闻名。| synonym: magnesium sulfate (for Epsom salts) | antonym: N/A | noun
