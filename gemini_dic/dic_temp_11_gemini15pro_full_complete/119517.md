microprocessing: /ˌmaɪkrəʊˈprəʊsesɪŋ/ | n. |微处理| The execution of instructions by a microprocessor.  It encompasses the entire process of fetching, decoding, and executing instructions, as well as handling data and managing system resources. |Microprocessing is at the heart of modern computing. 微处理是现代计算的核心。| Modern smartphones rely heavily on microprocessing. 现代智能手机严重依赖微处理。 | 近义词: computing, data processing | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps manual processing) | 词性: noun
