non-communicating:/ˌnɒn kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |不交流的，不沟通的，不传达的| Not communicating; not exchanging information or expressing thoughts and feelings.  Relates to a lack of interaction or sharing, often in a social or emotional context, but can also apply to physical systems. | The patient was non-communicating after the stroke.  病人中风后无法交流。| The non-communicating vessels in the engine caused a pressure buildup.  发动机中不通的管道导致压力积聚。|近义词：uncommunicative, silent, withdrawn, unresponsive|反义词：communicative, talkative, responsive, interactive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
communicating (present participle): from Late Latin communicant-, present participle stem of communicare "to share, impart, communicate", from communis "common"
