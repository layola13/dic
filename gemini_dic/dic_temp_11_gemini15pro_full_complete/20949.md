bleedings:/ˈbliːdɪŋz/
n.
流血；出血
instances of losing blood
The patient suffered multiple bleedings after the surgery.  病人手术后多次出血。

The bleedings from the wound eventually stopped.  伤口处的出血最终停止了。

近义词：hemorrhages, losses of blood
反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 clotting 凝血)
词性：名词 (Noun)


词源: From the verb "to bleed" (Old English *blēdan), from Proto-Germanic *blōþjaną, from PIE *bʰlud-, extended form of *bʰel- ("to swell, bloom"). The "-ing" suffix forms a gerund or present participle, and the "-s" indicates plural.
