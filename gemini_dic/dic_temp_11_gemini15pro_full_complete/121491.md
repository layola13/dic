misleader:/ˌmɪsˈliːdər/ | n. | 误导者，骗子 | A person who misleads. |  The advertisement was a deliberate misleading of the public.  该广告故意误导公众。| synonyms: deceiver, liar, cheat, fraud, impostor | antonyms: guide, leader, mentor | noun

mislead:/ˌmɪsˈliːd/ | v. | 误导，欺骗 | To cause someone to have a wrong idea or impression. | The company misled investors about its financial situation.  该公司在财务状况方面误导了投资者。 | synonyms: deceive, delude, trick, fool, dupe | antonyms: guide, enlighten, inform, direct | verb
