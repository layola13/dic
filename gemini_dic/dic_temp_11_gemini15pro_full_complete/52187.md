diarrhetic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɛtɪk/| n.|泻药|A medicine that causes diarrhea.|He took a diarrhetic to relieve his constipation. 他服用了泻药来缓解便秘。|近义词:laxative, purgative, aperient|反义词:antidiarrheal, constipating|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diarrheticus, from Greek diarrhoetikos, from diarrhein "to flow through," from dia- "through" + rhein "to flow."
