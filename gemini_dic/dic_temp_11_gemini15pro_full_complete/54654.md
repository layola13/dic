disobediently:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiəntli/| adv.|不服从地，不顺从地|In a manner that shows a refusal to obey rules or instructions.|The child disobediently refused to go to bed.  这孩子不服从地拒绝上床睡觉。|Synonyms: defiantly, rebelliously, waywardly, naughtily|Antonyms: obediently, submissively, respectfully|adverb
