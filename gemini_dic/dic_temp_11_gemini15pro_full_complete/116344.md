masterstroke:/ˈmɑːstərstroʊk/ | n. | 妙计；高招；神来之笔 | A masterstroke is a brilliant action or plan that brings about a desired result, especially one that is both clever and unexpected. |  His resignation was a political masterstroke. 他的辞职是一项政治上的妙举。 | 近义词: coup, triumph, stroke of genius, brilliant move | 反义词: blunder, mistake, failure | 词性: 名词

词源:
From master + stroke.  "Master" implies skill and expertise, while "stroke" originally referred to a blow or an act of hitting.  Together, they signify a skillful and decisive action.
