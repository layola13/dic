cognate:/ˈkɒɡneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡneɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|同源的，同类的；同源词|Having the same linguistic derivation as another; related by descent from a common ancestor.  A word related to another by descent from a common ancestor.|The words "night" (English) and "Nacht" (German) are cognates.|近义词：related, similar, analogous, corresponding|反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, different|词性: adjective, noun
