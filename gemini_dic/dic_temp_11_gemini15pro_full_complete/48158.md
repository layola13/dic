decommercializes: /diːkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzɪz/
Verb
使非商业化；消除商业性质
To remove the commercial aspects or influences from something; to make something less commercial.
The new management team decommercialized the festival, focusing on community involvement rather than profit.  新的管理团队使节日去商业化，专注于社区参与而不是盈利。

The government is trying to decommercialize public broadcasting. 政府正试图将公共广播去商业化。
Synonym: demonetize, deprivatize
Antonym: commercialize, monetize
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): meaning "reverse" or "remove"  来自拉丁语，意思是“反转”或“移除”
commercialize (verb):  to make something commercial  使商业化
-ize (suffix): forms verbs from adjectives or nouns  构成动词的后缀
