carotte:/kɑːˈrɒt/ (UK), /ˈkærət/ (US)| n. |胡萝卜|A tapering orange-colored root eaten as a vegetable.|例句：I like to eat carrots raw. 我喜欢生吃胡萝卜。

例句：The rabbit nibbled on the carrot. 兔子啃着胡萝卜。|近义词：Daucus carota subsp. sativus (scientific name)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *carote (Modern French carotte), from Late Latin *carōta, from Latin carōta, from Greek karōton (καρωτόν), of unknown origin. Possibly related to the color, or to the texture.
