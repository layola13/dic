externalised:/ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪzd/
adjective
外化的；客观化的
expressed or made visible externally.
Her feelings were externalised in her art. 她的情感在她的艺术中得到了外化。
externalized:/ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪzd/
adjective
外化的；客观化的
expressed or made visible externally.
His anger was externalized in aggressive behavior. 他的愤怒表现在攻击性行为上。
synonym: expressed, manifested, external, outward
antonym: internalized, repressed, concealed
词性: adjective
