heel-in:/ˈhiːlɪn/| n. |假植；暂时栽植|The act of temporarily planting a seedling or young tree in a trench to protect its roots until it can be permanently planted.|We heeled in the saplings until we were ready to plant them in the orchard. 我们把树苗假植，直到我们准备好把它们种在果园里。|Near synonyms: temporary planting, trench planting|Antonyms: permanent planting|Noun

heel-in:/ˈhiːlɪn/| v. |假植；暂时栽植|To temporarily plant a seedling or young tree in a trench to protect its roots until it can be permanently planted.|We heeled in the rose bushes over winter. 我们在冬天把玫瑰丛假植。|Near synonyms: temporarily plant, trench plant|Antonyms: permanently plant|Verb
