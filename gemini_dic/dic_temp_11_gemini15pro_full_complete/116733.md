mauled:/mɔːld/| v. | 伤害，撕咬，粗暴地对待 |  To injure by beating and rough handling; to criticize harshly. | The hiker was mauled by a bear.  徒步旅行者被熊袭击了。 |  The critic mauled the new play in his review.  评论家在他的评论中严厉批评了这部新剧。 | savaged, mangled, battered, clawed, lacerated | healed, repaired, mended | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *mauler, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French mesler "to mix, meddle".
