acrimonies: /ˈækrɪmoʊniz/| n. | 尖刻的言辞；激烈争吵；恶言相向 | bitterness or ill feeling between people; bitter remarks or harsh criticisms | The divorce was finalized after months of acrimony.  这场离婚在数月的激烈争吵后最终敲定。|  The political debate descended into acrimony. 政治辩论沦为恶言相向。 | 近义词: bitterness, animosity, rancor, hostility, antagonism | 反义词: goodwill, harmony, cordiality, amity | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *acrimonia* "sharpness, pungency," from *acris* "sharp, pungent."
