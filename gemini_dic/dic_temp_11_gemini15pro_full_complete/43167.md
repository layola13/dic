cpio:/ˈsiːpaɪoʊ/| n. | 复制归档程序 | A program used to create and extract archive files.  It reads and writes archive files in cpio format. | Example:  I used cpio to backup my home directory. 我用cpio备份了我的家目录。 | Example: The cpio command is powerful for creating backups. cpio命令对于创建备份非常强大。 | Synonym: tar, pax | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a utility like cpio) | noun
