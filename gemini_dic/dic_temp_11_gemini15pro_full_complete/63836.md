epigenesis:/ˌepɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.|后成论，渐成论|The theory that an embryo develops by progressive differentiation of an initially undifferentiated entity, as opposed to preformationism.|The biologist explained the concept of epigenesis, detailing how complex organisms arise from simpler beginnings.|Synonyms:  development, evolution, ontogeny|Antonyms: preformationism|noun
