confider:/kənˈfaɪdər/ | n. | 知己，心腹，密友 |  A person with whom one shares a secret or private matter, trusting them not to repeat it to others. | She told her closest confider about her plans. 她把她的计划告诉了她最亲密的知己。 |  confidant, intimate, close friend | betrayer, enemy | noun

confide:/kənˈfaɪd/ | v. | 吐露；委托；信赖 | 1. Tell someone about a secret or private matter while trusting them not to repeat it to others. 2. Entrust something to someone for safekeeping. | 1. He confided his fears to his therapist. 他向治疗师倾诉了他的恐惧。 2. She confided her jewelry to the hotel safe. 她把她的珠宝委托给酒店保险箱保管。|  disclose, reveal, divulge, entrust | conceal, hide | verb
