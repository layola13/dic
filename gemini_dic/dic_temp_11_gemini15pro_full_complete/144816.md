over-hunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| v. |过度捕猎|To hunt a species so excessively that its population is severely depleted or it becomes extinct.|例句：They over-hunted the deer in that area, leading to a decline in their numbers. 他们在那个地区过度捕猎鹿，导致鹿的数量下降。

例句：Over-hunting of whales nearly drove them to extinction.  过度捕猎鲸鱼几乎使它们灭绝。|近义词：overexploit, deplete|反义词：conserve, protect|词性:动词

over-hunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| n. |过度捕猎|The act of hunting a species to excess.|例句：The over-hunt of passenger pigeons led to their extinction. 旅鸽的过度捕猎导致了它们的灭绝。

例句：The over-hunt decimated the local wolf population. 过度捕猎摧毁了当地的狼群。 |近义词：overexploitation, depletion|反义词：conservation, protection|词性:名词
