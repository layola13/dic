ceraceous:/sɪˈreɪʃəs/
adj.
蜡质的；似蜡的
of or resembling wax; waxy
The insect secreted a ceraceous substance that coated its body.  这种昆虫分泌出一种蜡质物质覆盖其身体。
近义词:waxy, waxlike
反义词:non-waxy
