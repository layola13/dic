involuntarily: /ɪnˈvɑːlənˌtɛrəli/ /ɪnˈvɒlənˌtɛrəli/
adverb
不由自主地; 非自愿地
done without will or conscious control.
She gasped involuntarily. 她不由自主地倒抽了一口气。
Synonyms: automatically, instinctively, reflexively, unconsciously, unwillingly
Antonyms: voluntarily, willingly, consciously
词性: 副词
