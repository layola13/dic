cauliform: /ˈkɔːlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
菜花状的；花椰菜状的
Resembling a cauliflower; having a cauliflower-like form, especially with compact, rounded masses.
The fungus had a cauliform appearance. 这种真菌外观呈菜花状。
近义词: cauliflower-shaped, cauliflower-like,  globular, rounded
反义词: flat, smooth
词性: adjective
