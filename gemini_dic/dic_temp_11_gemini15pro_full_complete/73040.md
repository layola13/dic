footier:/ˈfʊtiər/| adj.|更臭的，更难闻的| comparative adjective of footy; more footy.  Having a stronger or more unpleasant foot odor. |例句：His socks were footier than a locker room after a marathon. 他的袜子比马拉松比赛后的更衣室还要臭。

例句：My shoes are getting footier by the day. 我的鞋子一天比一天臭。|近义词：smellier, stinkier, funkier|反义词:fresher, cleaner|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From footy + -er (comparative suffix). Footy itself is likely a colloquial shortening of "foot odor," with "foot" ultimately from Old English *fōt (akin to Old Frisian fōt, Old Saxon fōt, Old High German fuoz, German Fuß, Gothic fotus), and "odor" from Latin odor.
