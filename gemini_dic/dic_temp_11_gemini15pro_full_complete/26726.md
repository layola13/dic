cadger's:/ˈkædʒərz/ |n. |乞丐的东西；骗子所得之物|Something belonging to a cadger; something obtained by cadging. | That broken bike is part of the cadger's haul.| 近义词: beggar's, sponger's | 反义词: donor's, giver's |词性:名词

词源:
From cadger + 's (possessive suffix).  "Cadger" itself likely comes from the Scottish Gaelic "cadgar," meaning a travelling tinker or peddler, often viewed with suspicion.
