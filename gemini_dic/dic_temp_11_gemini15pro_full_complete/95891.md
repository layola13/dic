例句：The incredibility of her escape from the burning building amazed everyone. 她从燃烧的建筑中逃脱的经历令人难以置信，震惊了所有人。|近义词：unbelievability, implausibility|反义词：credibility, believability|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French incredibilité, from late Latin incredibilitas, from incredibilis (see incredible).
