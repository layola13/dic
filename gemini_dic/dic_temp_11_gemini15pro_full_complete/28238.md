capsulated:/ˈkæpsjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被封装的，被包住的；概括的 | Enclosed in a capsule or as if in a capsule; summarized concisely. | The drug is capsulated for easy swallowing. 这种药被封装起来以便于吞咽。| The main points of the meeting were capsulated in a short report. 会议的要点被概括在一份简短的报告中。 |  Synonyms: enclosed, encased, summarized, condensed | Antonyms: exposed, open, expanded, detailed | word property: adjective

capsulate:/ˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | v. |  封装，包住；概括 | Enclose in a capsule; summarize concisely. | The machine encapsulates the pills in gelatin.  这台机器用凝胶封装药丸。 | The article encapsulates the main arguments of the book. 这篇文章概括了这本书的主要论点。 | Synonyms: enclose, encase, summarize, condense, epitomize | Antonyms: expose, open, expand, detail | word property: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "capsula" (small box, chest) + "-ate" (verb suffix) or "-ed" (past participle/adjective suffix).  最终来源于拉丁语 "capsa"，意思是盒子或容器。
