chalking:/ˈtʃɔːkɪŋ/| n. v.|粉化；用粉笔写或画|The act of rubbing chalk on a surface, or the marks made by doing this.  The process of a painted surface becoming powdery.|例句：The chalking of the blackboard created a cloud of dust.  黑板粉化产生了一团灰尘。|近义词：scribbling, sketching, marking|反义词:erasing, wiping|词性:noun, verb

chalking:/ˈtʃɔːkɪŋ/| v.|用粉笔写或画|To write or draw something with chalk.|例句：She was chalking a hopscotch grid on the sidewalk.  她正在人行道上用粉笔画跳房子格子。|近义词：drawing, writing, sketching|反义词:erasing, wiping|词性:verb
