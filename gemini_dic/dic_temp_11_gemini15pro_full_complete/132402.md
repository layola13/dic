nondenials:/ˌnɒndɪˈnaɪəlz/ |n.|不明确的否认；不置可否|An ambiguous denial; a statement that avoids directly confirming or denying something. |The spokesperson's nondenials about the CEO's resignation fueled further speculation.|Synonyms: equivocations, evasions, hedges|Antonyms: confirmations, admissions|noun
