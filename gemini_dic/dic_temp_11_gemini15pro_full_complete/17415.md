beaming:/ˈbiːmɪŋ/| adj.| 容光焕发的，喜气洋洋的，眉开眼笑的 | radiating happiness; showing great pleasure or satisfaction |  She had a beaming smile as she accepted the award. 她接过奖项时，脸上洋溢着灿烂的笑容。 | synonyms: radiant, bright, cheerful, joyful | antonyms: gloomy, sad, sullen, frowning | adjective

beaming:/ˈbiːmɪŋ/| v.| 发光，照射，播送 | to shine brightly; to smile radiantly | The sun was beaming down on us. 阳光照耀着我们。| synonyms: shining, radiating, glowing | antonyms: dimming, darkening, fading | verb
