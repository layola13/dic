larches:/ˈlɑːrtʃɪz/
noun
落叶松（larch的复数）
Plural form of larch, a type of deciduous conifer tree.
The golden larches on the mountainside were a breathtaking sight. 山坡上金色的落叶松景色 breathtaking。

The larches shed their needles every autumn. 每年秋天，落叶松都会落叶。

synonym: conifers, deciduous trees
antonym: evergreens
