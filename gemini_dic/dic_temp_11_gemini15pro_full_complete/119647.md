middlebrowism: /ˈmɪdəlˌbraʊɪzəm/ | n. | 中庸之道；中庸趣味；中等文化水平；迎合中产阶级趣味 |  A term used, often disapprovingly, to describe cultural interests or tastes considered to be conventional, unchallenging, and appealing to the middle class.  It often implies a lack of intellectual depth or sophistication. |  "The book club's selections were criticized for their middlebrowism."  这本书俱乐部的选择因其迎合中产阶级趣味而受到批评。 | Synonyms:  philistinism, conventionalism, mediocrity | Antonyms:  highbrowism, avant-garde, intellectualism | noun
