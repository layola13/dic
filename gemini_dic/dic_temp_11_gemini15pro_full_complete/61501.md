embroiler:/ɪmˈbrɔɪlər/| n.| 争吵者，卷入纷争的人；肉用仔鸡 |A person who embroils or involves others in conflict or difficulties; a young chicken raised specifically for meat. |The politician was a notorious embroiler, constantly stirring up controversy.  这个政客是一个臭名昭著的争吵者，不断挑起争论。| The supermarket sells fresh, plump embroilers. 超市出售新鲜肥美的肉鸡。|Synonyms: instigator, agitator, troublemaker; broiler |Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "embroil," which comes from Old French "embroillier" (to confuse, entangle), ultimately from "brooillier" (to mix, stir), from "brouiller" (to stir, mix).
