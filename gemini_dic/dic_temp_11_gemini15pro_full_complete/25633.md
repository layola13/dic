bunchiest:/ˈbʌntʃiɪst/
adjective
最成束的，最成群的
Most clustered or grouped together; forming the most bunches.
The bunchiest grapes were at the top of the vine.  最成串的葡萄在藤蔓的顶部。
The florist had the bunchiest bouquet of carnations I've ever seen.  花店里有我见过的最成束的康乃馨花束。
近义词:  thickest, densest, fullest
反义词:  sparsest, thinnest, scantiest
词性: adjective

词源:
From bunch + -y + -est.  "Bunch" comes from  Middle English *bunche, probably of Scandinavian origin.
