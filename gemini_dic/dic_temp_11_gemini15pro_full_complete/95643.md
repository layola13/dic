incompleteness:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːtnəs/
|n.|不完整，不完善|The incompleteness of the data made it difficult to draw conclusions.  数据的不完整性使得难以得出结论。|例句：She felt a sense of incompleteness in her life. 她感觉自己的生活不完整。|近义词：lack, deficiency, imperfection|反义词：completeness, wholeness|词性:名词

**词源:**

From *incomplete* + *-ness*. *Incomplete* comes from the Latin *in-* (not) + *completus* (complete), the past participle of *complere* (to fill up, complete).  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
