noncomplaisantly:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪzəntli/
adverb
不顺从地，不殷勤地
in a manner that is not compliant; stubbornly or ungraciously.
She noncomplaisantly refused to sign the document. 她不顺从地拒绝在文件上签字。
He noncomplaisantly rejected their offer of help. 他不情愿地拒绝了他们的帮助。
Synonyms:  unwillingly, reluctantly, defiantly, rebelliously, disobediently
Antonyms:  willingly, readily, obediently, submissively, cooperatively
词性: adverb
