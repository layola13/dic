barnstorm:/ˈbɑːrnstɔːrm/ (US) /ˈbɑːnstɔːm/ (UK)| v. n.|巡回演出；巡回演讲；作旋风式访问|To travel through rural areas, typically by plane, making brief appearances (especially to campaign for political office or to perform stunts). Originally referred to theatrical performances in barns.|例句：The candidate barnstormed across the state, hoping to garner support in rural communities.  候选人在全州进行旋风式访问，希望在农村社区获得支持。

例句：The pilot barnstormed the countryside, performing daring aerial acrobatics. 飞行员在乡村上空进行旋风式飞行表演，表演大胆的特技飞行。|近义词：tour, canvass, stump|反义词:|词性：verb, noun

barnstorming:/ˈbɑːrnstɔːrmɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɑːnstɔːmɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|巡回演出；巡回演讲；旋风式访问|The act of barnstorming.|例句：The barnstorming tour took the actors to small towns across the country.  这次巡回演出将演员们带到了全国各地的小城镇。

例句：His barnstorming speeches energized the crowds and boosted his popularity. 他充满激情的巡回演讲让群众充满活力，并提升了他的人气。|近义词：touring, canvassing|反义词:|词性：noun, adjective
