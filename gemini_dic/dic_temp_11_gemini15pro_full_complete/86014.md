heady:/ˈhɛdi/ | adj. | 令人兴奋的；醉人的；轻率的；强烈的 | Intoxicating; exhilarating; exciting; potent.  Causing feelings of dizziness or euphoria.  |  The champagne made her feel heady.  香槟让她感到微醺。| Synonyms: intoxicating, exhilarating, exciting, potent, dizzying, euphoric | Antonyms: sobering, calming, dull, mild | adjective

heady:/ˈhɛdi/ | adj. | 鲁莽的；冲动的；轻率的 | Impetuous; rash; reckless. Characterized by impulsiveness and a lack of careful consideration. | He made a heady decision to quit his job without another lined up. 他一时冲动，没有找到下家就辞掉了工作。| Synonyms: impetuous, rash, reckless, impulsive, hasty | Antonyms: cautious, careful, deliberate, prudent | adjective

heady:/ˈhɛdi/ | adj. | （香味）浓烈的 | (of a smell) strongly affecting the senses.  Having a powerful and pervasive aroma. | The heady scent of jasmine filled the air. 浓烈的茉莉花香弥漫在空气中。| Synonyms: strong, pungent, aromatic, intoxicating | Antonyms: faint, mild, subtle | adjective
