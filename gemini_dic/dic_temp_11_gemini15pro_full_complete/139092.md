obconic:/ɒbˈkɒnɪk/
adjective
倒圆锥形的
Shaped like an inverted cone; cone-shaped with the point downward.
The stalactite had an obconic form, wider at the bottom than at the top. 钟乳石呈倒圆锥形，底部比顶部宽。
近义词: inversely conic, obconical
反义词: conic, conical
词性: adjective

obconic:/ɒbˈkɒnɪk/
noun
倒圆锥体
A solid or figure shaped like an inverted cone.
The sculptor carved an obconic form from the block of marble.  雕塑家从大理石块上雕刻出一个倒圆锥体。
近义词: inverted cone
反义词: cone
词性: noun
