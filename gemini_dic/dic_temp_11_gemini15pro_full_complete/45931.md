cuttle:/ˈkʌtəl/| n.|乌贼；墨鱼|A marine mollusk with eight arms, two tentacles, and an internal shell called a cuttlebone. It is known for its ability to change color and squirt ink.|例句：The cuttlefish rapidly changed color to blend in with the coral.  乌贼迅速变色以融入珊瑚中。

例句：Cuttlebone is often given to pet birds as a source of calcium. 墨鱼骨经常被作为钙的来源给宠物鸟吃。|近义词：squid, cephalopod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
