half-divine:/ˌhæf dɪˈvaɪn/
adjective
半神性的；近乎神圣的
partially divine; sharing some qualities of divinity.
The hero of the epic poem was portrayed as half-divine, possessing incredible strength and wisdom.  这位史诗中的英雄被描绘成半神，拥有不可思议的力量和智慧。
The oracle was said to be a half-divine being, able to communicate with the gods.  据说神谕是一个半神，能够与神交流。
近义词:semi-divine, godlike
反义词:mortal, human
词性:adjective

half:/hæf/
noun, adjective, adverb
一半, 半数的
One of two equal parts of a whole.
She ate half the cake. 她吃了半个蛋糕。
Half the students failed the test. 一半的学生考试不及格。
近义词:one-half, moiety
反义词:whole, double
词性:noun, adjective, adverb

divine:/dɪˈvaɪn/
adjective, noun, verb
神圣的, 神, 占卜
Of, from, or like God or a god.
The music was simply divine. 这音乐简直是天籁之音。
He claimed to have divine powers. 他声称拥有神力。
近义词:holy, sacred, godly
反义词:unholy, profane, secular
词性:adjective, noun, verb

词源:

half: From Old English *healf, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *semis.

divine: From Old French devin, from Latin dīvīnus (“divine, pertaining to a god”), from dīvus (“god; godlike”), from Proto-Italic *deiwos, from Proto-Indo-European *deywós (“celestial, shining; god”).
