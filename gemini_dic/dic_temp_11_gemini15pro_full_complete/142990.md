outbartering:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtərɪŋ/
|v.| 以物易物胜过;在货物交换中胜过|To surpass or defeat someone in bartering; to get a better deal in an exchange of goods.|He outbartered the trader, getting two sheep for a single goat. 他在交易中胜过了商人，用一只山羊换来了两只绵羊。|近义词：outtrade, outsmart|反义词: be outdone, be outmaneuvered|词性: 动词
