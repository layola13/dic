loafs:/loʊvz/
n.
(一条条的)面包; 块状物
plural form of loaf
The baker sold several loaves of bread.  面包师卖了几条面包。
近义词:breads, chunks
反义词:slices, crumbs
词性:名词

loafs:/loʊvz/
v.
闲逛; 懒散度日 (third-person singular simple present indicative form of loaf)
He often loafs around the park instead of working. 他经常在公园里闲逛而不是工作。
近义词:idle, lounge
反义词:work, hustle
词性:动词
