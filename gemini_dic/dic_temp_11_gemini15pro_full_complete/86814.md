hematitic:/ˌhiːməˈtɪtɪk/ | adj. | 赤铁矿的；含赤铁矿的 | Relating to, resembling, or containing hematite. | The geologist identified the rock as hematitic due to its reddish-brown color.  地质学家根据其红棕色判断这块岩石含有赤铁矿。 | The hematitic ore was a rich source of iron. 赤铁矿矿石是丰富的铁来源。 | synonyms: ferruginous, iron-rich | antonyms: non-ferruginous | adjective

hematite:/ˈhiːməˌtaɪt/ | n. | 赤铁矿 | A reddish-black or gray mineral consisting of ferric oxide. It is an important ore of iron. | The mine produced large quantities of hematite.  这个矿场生产大量的赤铁矿。| Hematite is often used as a pigment. 赤铁矿常被用作颜料。| synonyms: iron ore, ferric oxide | antonyms: N/A | noun
