cheaters:/ˈtʃiːtərz/
n.
骗子；作弊者
People who act dishonestly or unfairly in order to gain an advantage, especially in a game, exam, or relationship.
The teacher caught the cheaters red-handed copying answers from each other. 老师当场抓住了互相抄答案的作弊者。
synonyms: frauds, deceivers, swindlers
antonyms: honest people, fair players
