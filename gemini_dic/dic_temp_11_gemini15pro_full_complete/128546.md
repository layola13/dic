ngoma:/ˈnɡoʊmə/| n.|鼓，舞蹈，庆典，仪式|(From Swahili) A traditional East African drum, dance, or celebration often associated with rituals or ceremonies.|例句：The ngoma lasted all night, filled with rhythmic drumming and vibrant dancing.  庆典持续了整夜，充满了节奏感强的鼓声和充满活力的舞蹈。

例句：The villagers gathered for the ngoma to celebrate the harvest. 村民们聚集在一起举行庆典来庆祝丰收。|近义词：drum, dance, celebration, ceremony, ritual|反义词:None|词性:noun
