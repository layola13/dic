fimbria:/ˈfɪmbriə/ (plural fimbriae /ˈfɪmbriˌiː/)
n.
纤毛，菌毛
A fringe of filaments or slender processes, especially around the opening of the fallopian tubes or on the surface of some bacteria.
The fimbriae of the fallopian tubes sweep the egg into the tube. 输卵管的伞部将卵子扫入管内。
The bacterial fimbriae help it adhere to surfaces.  细菌的菌毛帮助它粘附在表面上。

近义词:cilia, pilus
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth surface" in specific contexts)
词性: noun


fimbria:/ˈfɪmbriə/ (plural fimbriae /ˈfɪmbriˌiː/)
n.
(大脑)边缘，缘
A band of white matter along the medial edge of the hippocampus.
Damage to the fimbria can disrupt memory formation. 对边缘的损害会扰乱记忆的形成。

近义词: fornix (in some anatomical contexts)
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
