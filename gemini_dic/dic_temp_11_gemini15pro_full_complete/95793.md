inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/ | n. |不便，麻烦| something that causes trouble or difficulty; a slight trouble or problem |  The flight delay was a major inconvenience. 航班延误是一个很大的不便。| synonyms: trouble, difficulty, bother, hassle, nuisance | antonyms: convenience, ease, comfort | noun

inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/ | vt. |给…带来不便，打扰 | to cause trouble or difficulty for someone | I hope I'm not inconveniencing you by asking you to do this. 我希望我让你做这件事不会给你带来不便。| synonyms: bother, disturb, trouble, disrupt | antonyms: accommodate, assist, help | verb
