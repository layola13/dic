disproves:/dɪsˈpruːvz/
verb
反驳；证明…是虚假的
to prove that something is not true
New evidence disproves the defendant's alibi. 新证据反驳了被告不在场的申辩。
synonyms: refute, debunk, invalidate
antonyms: prove, confirm, validate
词性: 动词
