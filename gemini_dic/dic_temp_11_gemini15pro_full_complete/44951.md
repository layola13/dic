cryptopunks: /ˈkrɪptoʊˌpʌŋks/ | n. | 加密朋克 | A collection of 10,000 unique 24x24 pixel art images, generated algorithmically. They are non-fungible tokens (NFTs) on the Ethereum blockchain. |  Cryptopunks are considered one of the earliest examples of NFTs. 加密朋克被认为是最早的NFT的例子之一。 |  The value of some Cryptopunks has reached millions of dollars.  一些加密朋克的价值已经达到了数百万美元。 | synonyms: NFTs, digital collectibles, blockchain art | antonyms: physical art, traditional collectibles | noun
