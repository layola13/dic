amoebiform:/əˈmiːbɪfɔːrm/| adj.|变形虫状的，似变形虫的|Resembling an amoeba in shape or movement; amorphous.  Having no definite form; shapeless.|The creature moved in an amoebiform manner across the petri dish.|近义词：amorphous, shapeless, formless, indeterminate|反义词：formed, shaped, definite|词性: adjective
Etymology: From amoeba + -iform  (Amoeba from modern Latin,  from Greek amoibē ‘change’ + -iform from Latin -formis)
