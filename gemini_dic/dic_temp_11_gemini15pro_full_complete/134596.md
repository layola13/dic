The nonlucid dream was filled with strange and disjointed images.  那个不清醒的梦充满了奇怪而不连贯的图像。| 近义词：unconscious, delirious, confused, irrational | 反义词：lucid, clearheaded, rational, coherent | 词性: 形容词
