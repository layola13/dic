cornery: /ˈkɔːnəri/ | adj. | 角落的; 偏僻的; 隐蔽的 |  Having many corners; secluded or remote, like a corner. Often used to describe a place that feels small, cramped, or out-of-the-way. | The attic room was small and cornery, filled with old furniture. 阁楼房间又小又偏僻，里面堆满了旧家具。|  remote, secluded, cramped, small, confined | spacious, open, central | adjective
