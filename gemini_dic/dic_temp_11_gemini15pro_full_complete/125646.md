mummied:/ˈmʌmid/ | adj. | 木乃伊化的；像木乃伊的；干瘪的 |  Preserved or dried up like a mummy; resembling a mummy in appearance. | The explorers discovered a mummied cat in the ancient Egyptian tomb. 探险家们在古埃及陵墓中发现了一只木乃伊化的猫。 | The fruit had been left out in the sun too long and had become all mummied. 水果在阳光下放得太久，变得干瘪了。 | 近义词：mummified, desiccated, shriveled, withered | 反义词：fresh, plump, hydrated | 词性: 形容词

词源: From mummy + -ed.  Ultimately from Persian *mūmiya* (“bitumen, wax”).
