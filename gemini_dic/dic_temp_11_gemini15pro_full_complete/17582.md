beauteousness:/ˈbjuːtiəsnəs/
n.
美丽，美好
The quality of being beautiful; loveliness.
Her beauteousness captivated everyone in the room. 她的美丽吸引了房间里所有的人。
近义词:beauty, loveliness, attractiveness, charm
反义词:ugliness, unattractiveness, homeliness
词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *beauteousnes, equivalent to beauteous +‎ -ness.
