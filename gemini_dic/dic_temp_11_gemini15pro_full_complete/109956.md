liberalness:/ˈlɪbərəlnəs/
n.
慷慨；大方；宽宏大量；开放
The quality of being liberal; generosity, broad-mindedness, open-mindedness.
Her liberalness with her time and resources was greatly appreciated. 她对自己的时间和资源的慷慨大方受到了极大的赞赏。
Synonyms: generosity, magnanimity, tolerance, open-mindedness, broad-mindedness
Antonyms: stinginess, meanness, narrow-mindedness, intolerance, bigotry
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "liberal" + "-ness."  "Liberal" comes from Old French "liberal" and ultimately from Latin "liberalis" meaning "of freedom, pertaining to a freeman, generous, noble."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
