orbitosphenoidal:/ɔːrbɪtəʊsfiːnɔɪdəl/ | adj. | 眶蝶骨的 | Relating to the orbitosphenoid, a small bone forming part of the human skull.  It is situated between the presphenoid and the lesser wing of the sphenoid. | The orbitosphenoid bone forms a small portion of the posterior orbital wall. 眶蝶骨形成眼眶后壁的一小部分。|  The development of the orbitosphenoid bone is crucial for proper formation of the eye socket. 眶蝶骨的发育对眼窝的正常形成至关重要。 |  Synonyms:  sphenoidal (more general),  pertaining to the sphenoid | Antonyms: None (anatomical term, lacks a direct opposite) | adjective
