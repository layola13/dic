outspurns:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːrnz/
verb
超过，胜过
to surpass or exceed, especially in performance or achievement.
He easily outspurned his rivals in the final stretch of the race. 他在比赛的最后阶段轻松地超过了他的对手。
surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip
lag behind, fall short of
