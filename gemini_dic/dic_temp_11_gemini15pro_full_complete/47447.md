deafly:/ˈdɛfli/| adv.| 沉闷地；漠然地；像聋子一样地|In a manner resembling deafness; insensitively or indifferently.  Pertaining to or characteristic of deafness.|He spoke deafly, as if he couldn't hear his own voice. 他说话声音沉闷，好像听不到自己的声音。|近义词：indifferently, unresponsively, apathetically, dully|反义词：sensitively, responsively, alertly|词性: 副词

deafly:/ˈdɛfli/| adv.| 难以听见地；微弱地|In a way that is barely audible; faintly.|The music played deafly in the background.  背景音乐播放得难以听见。|近义词：faintly, softly, quietly, dimly|反义词：loudly, clearly, distinctly|词性: 副词
