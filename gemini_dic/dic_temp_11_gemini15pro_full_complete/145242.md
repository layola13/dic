overblame:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪm/| vt.|过度责备，过分责怪|To blame someone excessively or unfairly for something. | He tends to overblame himself for things that are not entirely his fault. 他倾向于为自己不完全是他的错的事情过度自责。|  She overblamed her son for breaking the vase, when it was actually the dog's fault. 她过分责备她儿子打碎了花瓶，而实际上是狗的错。|overcriticize, overcondemn|underblame, excuse|verb
