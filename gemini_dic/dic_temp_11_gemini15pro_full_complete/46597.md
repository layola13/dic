damasked:/ˈdæməskt/| adj.|锦缎似的，有锦缎花纹的|Woven or decorated with a pattern resembling damask.  Decorated with an intricate design.|例句：She wore a damasked gown of deep crimson. 她穿着一件深红色的锦缎长袍。|例句：The walls were covered in damasked wallpaper. 墙壁上贴着锦缎花纹的壁纸。|近义词：brocaded, patterned, figured|反义词:plain, simple|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From damask + -ed.  Ultimately from Damascus, the capital of Syria, where such fabrics were historically made.
