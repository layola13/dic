bawn:/bɔːn/
noun
围场，畜栏，奶牛场
An enclosed area or courtyard, especially in a castle or fortified house, typically used for keeping livestock.
The farmer kept his cows in the bawn. 农民把他的牛关在畜栏里。
enclosure, yard, paddock
field, open area
n.
