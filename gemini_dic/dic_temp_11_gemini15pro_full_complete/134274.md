noninformatively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɔːmətɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈfɔːrmətɪvli/| adv.|无信息地，没有提供信息的|In a way that does not provide useful or relevant information.  Not giving any useful information.|The study was conducted noninformatively, yielding no conclusive results. 这项研究进行得没有提供任何信息，没有得出任何结论性的结果。|Synonym: uninformatively, meaninglessly|Antonym: informatively, meaningfully|词性:副词
