bargee:/ˈbɑːrdʒiː/
n.
驳船夫，驳船工人
A person employed to work on a barge.
The bargee steered the barge skillfully through the narrow canal. 驳船夫熟练地驾驶驳船穿过狭窄的运河。
bargeman, boatman
captain, skipper
noun
