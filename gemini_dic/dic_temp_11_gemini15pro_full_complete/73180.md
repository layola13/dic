forbiddingnesses:/fərˈbɪdɪŋnɪsɪz/| n. |令人生畏， forbidding 的复数形式|The forbiddingnesses of the high mountains discouraged many climbers. 高山的险峻令许多登山者望而却步。|近义词：intimidation, dauntingness, unapproachability|反义词：welcomingness, approachability|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From forbidding + -ness.  Forbidding comes from the Middle English *forbeden*, from Old English *forbēodan*, meaning "to prohibit."  The suffix "-ness" transforms an adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
