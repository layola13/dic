airhostess: /ˈɛəhəʊstɪs/ (British English), /ˈɛrˌhoʊstɪs/ (American English) | n. | 空姐，空中小姐 | A female flight attendant who attends to passengers on an aircraft. | Example: The airhostess offered us drinks and snacks during the flight.  空中小姐在飞行期间给我们提供了饮料和小吃。

Example:  The airhostess demonstrated the safety procedures before takeoff.  空姐在起飞前演示了安全程序。

| 近义词: flight attendant, cabin crew, stewardess | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps passenger) | 词性: noun
