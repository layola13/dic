cogging:/ˈkɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. v. (present participle: cogging)|齿槽效应；磁阻转矩；作弊；欺骗|The act of manipulating something, often unfairly or dishonestly, or the uneven rotation of a motor due to magnetic forces.  Also refers to inserting teeth (cogs) into a wheel. |例句：The electric motor exhibited cogging at low speeds.  电机在低速时表现出齿槽效应。

The gambler was caught cogging the dice.  这个赌徒被抓到在骰子上做手脚。|近义词：cheating, manipulating, rigging, faking, doctoring (for dishonest manipulation);  pulsating, jerking (for uneven rotation)|反义词：fair play, honesty (for dishonest manipulation);  smooth, continuous (for uneven rotation)|词性:名词, 动词现在分词
