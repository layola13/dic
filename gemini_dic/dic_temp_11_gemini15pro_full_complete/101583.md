irradiance:/ɪˈreɪdiəns/ | n. |辐照度，辐射通量密度|The power of electromagnetic radiation per unit area incident on a surface.  The amount of radiant flux received by a surface per unit area.|The irradiance of the sun is greatest at noon.  中午的太阳辐照度最大。|近义词：radiation intensity, radiant flux density|反义词: - |noun
