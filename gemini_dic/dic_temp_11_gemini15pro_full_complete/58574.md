dulcet:/ˈdʌlsɪt/| adj.|甜美的，悦耳的，使人愉快的|Sweet and soothing (often used of sounds).|Her dulcet tones calmed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。|近义词：melodious, soothing, sweet, harmonious, pleasant|反义词：harsh, grating, discordant, strident, cacophonous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *doucet*, from Old French *doucet, dolcet* "sweet," from *dous* "sweet," from Latin *dulcis* "sweet."
