insufflation:/ˌɪnsəˈfleɪʃən/ /ˌɪnsʌˈfleɪʃən/
noun
吹入，注入；鼓舞
The act of blowing something (such as air, gas, or powder) into a body cavity. Also, the act of inspiring or animating.
The doctor performed an insufflation of the lungs.  医生对肺部进行了吹气。
The coach's speech was an insufflation of courage into the team. 教练的讲话给团队注入了勇气。

inspiration, inhalation, injection
exhalation
