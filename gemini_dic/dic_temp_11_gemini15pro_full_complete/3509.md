aftershocks:/ˈæftərʃɒks/ (UK), /ˈæftərʃɑːks/ (US)| n. |余震|A smaller earthquake following the main shock of a large earthquake.|例句：The city experienced several aftershocks following the initial earthquake.  这座城市在最初的地震后经历了几次余震。

例句：The aftershocks continued for days, making rescue efforts difficult.  余震持续了好几天，使得救援工作变得困难。|近义词：tremors, after-tremors|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "foreshock" but that's a different concept) |词性: Noun
