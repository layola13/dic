bacchius: /ˈbækiəs/ | n. | 长短格, 短长格 | A metrical foot consisting of a short or unstressed syllable followed by two long or stressed syllables. |  The poem is written in bacchius. 这首诗是以长短格写成的。|  The rhythmic pattern of the chant was based on the bacchius. 这段圣歌的节奏型是以短长格为基础的。| 近义词: amphibrach (sometimes) | 反义词: (No direct opposite, as it's a specific metrical foot) | 词性: noun
