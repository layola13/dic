mis-coordinated:/ˌmɪskəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
不协调的，动作不协调的
lacking coordination; poorly coordinated.
Her movements were mis-coordinated and clumsy. 她的动作不协调，笨手笨脚。
Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, ungainly, inept
Antonyms: coordinated, graceful, agile, deft
词性: 形容词

mis-coordinate:/ˌmɪskəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/
verb
使不协调，协调不良
to fail to coordinate properly.
Stress can mis-coordinate your muscles and make you more prone to injury. 压力会使你的肌肉协调不良，更容易受伤。
Synonyms: bungle, fumble, mess up
Antonyms: coordinate, synchronize, harmonize
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrongly' or 'badly') + coordinate (from Latin co- 'together' + ordinare 'to arrange').
