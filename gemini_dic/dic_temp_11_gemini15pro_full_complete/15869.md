ballister:/ˈbælɪstər/ (US), /ˈbælɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|栏杆，扶手；(弩炮的) 横梁|A vertical support for a railing, especially on a staircase or balcony. Also, a crossbar on a crossbow.|The ornate balusters of the grand staircase were a striking feature.  华丽的楼梯扶手是引人注目的特色。|Synonyms: banister, railing, balustrade|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun

ballast:/ˈbæləst/| n. vt.|压舱物；镇重物；稳定；使稳定|Heavy material, such as sand, gravel, or iron, placed low in a vessel to improve its stability.  Also, anything that gives mental or emotional stability.|The ship took on ballast in the harbor. 这艘船在港口装载了压舱物。 The steady routine of family life provided him with essential ballast. 稳定的家庭生活为他提供了必要的精神支柱。|Synonyms: counterweight, stabilizer, anchor|Antonyms: instability, volatility|noun, verb
