flatmate:/ˈflætmeɪt/ (UK), /ˈflætˌmeɪt/ (US)| n. |室友，合租人|A person who shares a flat (apartment) with another person or people.|例句：My flatmate and I share the rent and bills. 我和我的室友分摊房租和水电费。

例句：I'm looking for a new flatmate because my current one is moving out. 我在找一个新室友，因为我现在的室友要搬走了。|近义词：roommate, housemate|反义词: landlord, landlady|词性: noun
