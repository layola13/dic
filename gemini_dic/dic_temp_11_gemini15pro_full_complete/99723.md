interluding:/ˌɪntərˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
插入; 插播;
performing a short piece between the parts of a play, piece of music, etc.
The comedian interluded his monologue with songs.  这位喜剧演员在他的独白中穿插了歌曲。
intervening, inserting, intercalating
continuing, persisting
v.
