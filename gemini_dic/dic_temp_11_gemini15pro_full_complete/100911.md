intumescent:/ˌɪntʊˈmɛsənt/ | adj. |膨胀的，肿胀的|Becoming swollen, especially as a result of heat.  Characterized by swelling or enlarging, often due to heat exposure, as in fireproofing materials.|例句：The intumescent paint swelled and formed a protective char when exposed to the flames.  当暴露在火焰中时，膨胀型油漆膨胀并形成保护性炭层。

例句：Intumescent fire seals expand to block gaps and prevent the spread of fire and smoke. 膨胀型防火密封件膨胀以堵塞缝隙并防止火灾和烟雾蔓延。|近义词：expanding, swelling, bulging|反义词：shrinking, contracting|词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin intumescere "to swell up," from in- "in, on" + tumescere "to swell."
