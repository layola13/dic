defraying:/dɪˈfreɪɪŋ/
verb
支付，承担费用
The company offered to defray the travel expenses of the conference attendees.
该公司提出支付与会者的差旅费。
The grant helped defray the costs of her education.
这笔补助金帮助她支付了教育费用。
近义词: paying, covering, financing, subsidizing
反义词: charging, billing

词性: verb (动词)

词源: From Old French *desfraier, from des- (expressing removal) + fraier (to spend, consume).
