dotingly:/ˈdoʊtɪŋli/ | adv. | 溺爱地，宠爱地 | In a way that shows excessive fondness or love. | She looked at her grandson dotingly. 她宠溺地看着她的孙子。|  She smiled dotingly at her baby. 她对着她的宝宝宠溺地笑着。 | 近义词：affectionately, lovingly, fondly | 反义词：indifferently, coldly, neglectfully | 词性: 副词
词源: From the verb "dote" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Dote" comes from Middle English *doten*, of uncertain origin.
