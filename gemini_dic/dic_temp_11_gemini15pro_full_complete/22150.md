bomb-out:/ˈbɒm aʊt/ (British English), /ˈbɑːm aʊt/ (American English)| vi. n.| 失败；彻底失败；被炸毁；轰炸逐出|To fail badly or be forced to leave a place or situation, often due to bombing or other destruction.|The team bombed out in the first round of the competition.  该队在比赛的第一轮就惨败出局。|The family was bombed out of their home during the war.  这家人在战争期间被炸得无家可归。|近义词：fail, collapse, flop, flunk, wash out|反义词：succeed, triumph|verb, noun


bomb-out:/ˈbɒm aʊt/ (British English), /ˈbɑːm aʊt/ (American English)| n.| 失败；被炸毁的建筑物|A failure, especially a complete failure; a building that has been destroyed by bombs.|His first attempt at stand-up comedy was a complete bomb-out. 他的第一次单口喜剧尝试彻底失败了。|The city center was full of bomb-outs after the air raid. 市中心在空袭后到处都是被炸毁的建筑物。|近义词：failure, disaster, fiasco|反义词：success, triumph|noun
