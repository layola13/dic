abnormalnesses:/ˌæbnɔːrˈmælɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
异常，反常，变态
The state or quality of being abnormal; a collection of abnormal features or instances.
The doctor noted several abnormalnesses in the patient's blood work. 医生注意到病人的血液检查结果有几项异常。
synonyms: abnormalities, irregularities, deviations, anomalies, peculiarities
antonyms: normalities, regularities, standard, typicality
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From abnormal + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Abnormal itself comes from ab- (Latin prefix meaning "away from, off") + normal (from Latin normalis, "according to a carpenter's square," hence "regular").
