adjunctly:/əˈdʒʌŋktli/
adv.
附属地，补充地
in an adjunct manner; as a supplement or addition.
The information was adjunctly provided.  这些信息是补充提供的。
The topic was discussed adjunctly to the main presentation.  这个话题是作为主要演讲的补充进行讨论的。
近义词: additionally, supplementarily, incidentally
反义词: primarily, principally, chiefly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Latin *adjunctus*, past participle of *adjungere* "to join to," from *ad-* "to" + *jungere* "to join."
