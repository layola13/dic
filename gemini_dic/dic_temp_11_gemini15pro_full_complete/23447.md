brads:/brædz/| n.|细钉，无头钉|Small, thin nails with small heads, often used in finishing work. |He used brads to attach the molding to the wall. 他用无头钉把装饰线条固定在墙上。|近义词:nails, tacks|反义词:screws|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *brade*, from Old English *brǣd* (“a point, prick, sting, goad; a blade of grass”), from Proto-Germanic *bradą* (“point, sting”). Cognate with Dutch *braad* (“spit”), German *Bratspieß* (“spit”).
