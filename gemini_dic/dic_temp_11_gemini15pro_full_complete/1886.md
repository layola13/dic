acrotism:/ˈækrətɪzəm/
noun
无脉；脉搏缺失
Absence or imperceptibility of the pulse, especially at the wrist.
The doctor noted acrotism in the patient's wrist, indicating a circulatory problem. 医生注意到病人手腕处无脉，表明存在循环系统问题。
Near synonyms: pulselessness, asphyxia
Antonyms:  pulsation, palpable pulse
n.
