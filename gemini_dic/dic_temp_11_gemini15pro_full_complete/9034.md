antimaterialisms:/ˌænti məˈtɪəriəlɪzəmz/
|n.|反物质主义|The rejection of materialism, prioritizing spiritual or intellectual values over material possessions.  It emphasizes the importance of non-material things like relationships, experiences, and personal growth over acquiring wealth and possessions.|He embraced antimaterialisms and lived a simple life, focused on inner peace.|近义词：spiritualism, idealism, asceticism|反义词:materialism|Noun
