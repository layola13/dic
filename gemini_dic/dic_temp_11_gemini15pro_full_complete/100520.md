interviewing:/ˈɪntərvjuːɪŋ/| n. v.|面试；面谈；采访|The process of asking someone questions to assess their suitability for a job or course, or to gather information for a news report or article.|例句：The company is interviewing candidates for the sales manager position. 公司正在面试销售经理的候选人。|近义词：questioning, examining, assessing|反义词: N/A|词性: 动名词/现在分词

interviewing:/ˈɪntərvjuːɪŋ/| n.|面试；面谈|The act or process of interviewing.|例句：Interviewing is a crucial part of the hiring process. 面试是招聘过程中至关重要的环节。|近义词：assessment, evaluation|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词


Etymology: From interview + -ing.  "Interview" comes from the French "entrevue," meaning "a meeting between," from "entre" (between) and "vue" (view).
