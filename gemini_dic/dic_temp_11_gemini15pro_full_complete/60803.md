electronystagmogram: /ɪˌlektrəʊnɪˈstæɡməˌɡræm/ /ɪˌlekˌtroʊnɪˈstæɡməˌɡræm/ | n. |眼震电图；眼动电流描记图 | A graphical record of eye movements, especially involuntary ones, produced by electronystagmography.  It is used to diagnose balance disorders and other neurological conditions. |  The electronystagmogram showed evidence of vestibular dysfunction. 眼震电图显示前庭功能障碍的证据。

The doctor ordered an electronystagmogram to assess the patient's dizziness. 医生要求进行眼震电图检查以评估患者的眩晕。 | synonym: ENG (abbreviation),  eye movement recording | antonym:  N/A | noun
