enstatite:/ˈɛnstətaɪt/| n. |顽火辉石|A common rock-forming mineral belonging to the pyroxene group, typically grayish, greenish, or brownish in color. It is a magnesium endmember of the enstatite-ferrosilite solid solution series. | Enstatite is a common mineral in igneous and metamorphic rocks. 顽火辉石是火成岩和变质岩中常见的矿物。|  Enstatite can be found in meteorites. 顽火辉石可以在陨石中找到。|近义词：pyroxene 辉石|反义词: None|词性: noun
