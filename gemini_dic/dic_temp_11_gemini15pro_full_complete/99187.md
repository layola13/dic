intercitizenship:/ɪntərˈsɪtɪzənʃɪp/| n. |相互公民身份，跨国公民身份|A concept emphasizing the interconnectedness and shared responsibilities of individuals across national borders, often in the context of global issues or online communities.| Example: Intercitizenship promotes collaboration on climate change.  跨国公民身份促进在气候变化方面的合作。| Example:  The online forum fostered a sense of intercitizenship among its members.  这个在线论坛在其成员之间培养了一种跨国公民意识。| 近义词: global citizenship, transnational citizenship| 反义词: nationalism, parochialism| 词性: noun

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
citizen: a legally recognized subject or national of a state or commonwealth
-ship (suffix): state or condition of being
