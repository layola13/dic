NFTs:/ˌɛnˌɛfˈtiz/ | n. | 非同质化代币 | Non-Fungible Tokens, cryptographic tokens that represent something unique.  They are not interchangeable like cryptocurrencies. |  Example 1: He invested heavily in NFTs, hoping to make a profit. 他大量投资于NFT，希望能获利。

Example 2:  NFTs are changing the way digital art is bought and sold. NFT正在改变数字艺术的买卖方式。 | Near synonyms: digital assets, crypto collectibles | Antonym: fungible tokens | Noun
