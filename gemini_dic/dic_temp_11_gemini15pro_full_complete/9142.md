antinationalist:/ˌæntiˈnæʃ(ə)nəlɪst/
adjective
反民族主义的;反对民族主义的
Opposed to nationalism or nationalistic policies.
Example: The author presented an antinationalist argument against the war.  作者提出了反对战争的反民族主义论点。

Example:  Her antinationalist views were unpopular in the current political climate. 她在当前政治气候下反民族主义的观点并不受欢迎。

near synonym: internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist
antonym: nationalist, patriotic, chauvinist
word type: adjective
