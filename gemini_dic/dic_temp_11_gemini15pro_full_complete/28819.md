carousel:/ˌkærəˈsɛl/ | n. |旋转木马；行李传送带；图片轮播|A merry-go-round, a revolving machine with model horses or other animals on which people ride. Also, a conveyor belt at an airport baggage claim.  A rotating set of images, often used online.|例句：The children begged to ride the carousel. 孩子们恳求要坐旋转木马。

例句：My suitcase finally appeared on the baggage carousel. 我的行李终于出现在行李传送带上了。


近义词：merry-go-round, roundabout, conveyor belt, slider, image rotator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
