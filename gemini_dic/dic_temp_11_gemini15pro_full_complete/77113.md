garterless:/ˈɡɑːrtərləs/
adjective
无吊袜带的
Without garters; not wearing garters. Garters are bands worn around the leg to hold up stockings.
She wore a garterless stocking. 她穿了一只不带吊袜带的长袜。
Synonyms: stockingless, bare-legged
Antonyms: gartered
词性: adjective
