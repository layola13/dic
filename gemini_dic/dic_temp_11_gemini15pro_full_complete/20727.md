blamelessness:/ˈbleɪmləsnəs/
n.
无辜，清白，无可指责
The state of being free from blame or guilt.
His blamelessness was evident to everyone. 他的清白对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
The jury declared his blamelessness in the crime. 陪审团宣布他在该罪行中无罪。
innocence, purity, guiltlessness
guilt, culpability, fault
