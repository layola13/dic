forget:/fərˈɡet/ (US); /fəˈɡet/ (UK)| v. |忘记，遗忘|To lose the remembrance of; to be unable to recall.|I forgot my keys at home. 我把钥匙忘在家里了。|近义词：omit, neglect, overlook|反义词:remember, recall|verb

forget:/fərˈɡet/ (US); /fəˈɡet/ (UK)| n. |遗忘|The act or instance of forgetting.|It's all in the forget now.  现在一切都已忘记了。|近义词：oblivion|反义词:remembrance|noun
