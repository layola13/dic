disgruntledness:/dɪsˈɡrʌntldnəs/
n.
不满，不高兴
a feeling of sulky discontent.
The disgruntledness of the employees was evident in their poor performance. 员工的不满情绪体现在他们糟糕的表现上。
Near synonyms: dissatisfaction, displeasure, disaffection, discontentment, resentment
Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, happiness, pleasure
词性: 名词
