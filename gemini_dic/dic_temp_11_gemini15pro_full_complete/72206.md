florals:/ˈflɔːrəlz/
n.
花卉图案，花卉装饰
Flower patterns or designs, often used on fabrics or in decorating.
The dress featured delicate florals on a pale blue background.  这件连衣裙以浅蓝色为底，饰有精致的花卉图案。
She decorated the cake with delicate sugar florals. 她用精致的糖花装饰蛋糕。
近义词:flower prints, floral patterns, blossoms
反义词:solids, plain designs
词性:noun
