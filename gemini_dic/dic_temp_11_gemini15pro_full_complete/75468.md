frumpy:/ˈfrʌmpi/ | adj. | 邋遢的，老土的 | (of a woman or her clothes) dowdy and old-fashioned.  |  Example 1:  She looked frumpy in that old dress. 她穿着那件旧裙子看起来很邋遢。

Example 2: My aunt always wears such frumpy clothes. 我的阿姨总是穿着老土的衣服。 | 近义词: dowdy, drab, old-fashioned, unstylish | 反义词: stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
Origin uncertain; perhaps related to dialect frump “ill-tempered woman”
