anoplura:/ænəˈplʊərə/
n.
虱目; 吸虱类
Lice; an order of parasitic insects.
Anoplura are wingless insects that live as ectoparasites on mammals. 虱目是寄生在哺乳动物体外的无翅昆虫。
Human lice are classified under the order Anoplura. 人虱被归类于虱目。
Synonyms: lice, sucking lice
Etymology: From Greek *an* (without) + *hoplon* (weapon, armor) + *-oura* (tailed)
