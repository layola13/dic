what /wɒt/ (US) /hwɑːt/ |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask questions about something unknown or unspecified.|
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

近义词：which, how
反义词: (What doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast?  你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching right now? 你现在在看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? 做决定之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上将讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? 如果她知道真相她会说什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What time is the train leaving? 火车几点出发？
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是哪里？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She didn't say what she wanted. 她没说她想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about going to the movies? 去看电影怎么样？
What if it rains? 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? 为了什么？
So what? 那又怎样？
