bipedally:/baɪˈpɛdəli/
adv.
用两足；两足地
Using two feet; on two feet.
Humans typically walk bipedally. 人类通常用两足行走。
Chimpanzees can move bipedally for short distances. 黑猩猩可以短距离双足行走。
近义词: on two feet, upright
反义词: quadrupedally
词性: 副词
