clownishly:/ˈklaʊnɪʃli/ | adv. | 小丑般地，滑稽地，笨拙地 | In a manner resembling a clown; comically or foolishly. | He danced clownishly around the room, making everyone laugh. 他滑稽地在房间里跳来跳去，逗得大家哈哈大笑。|  He tripped clownishly over the rug. 他笨拙地被地毯绊倒了。 | 近义词: foolishly, ridiculously, comically, buffoonishly | 反义词: seriously, gracefully, solemnly | 词性: 副词
