coralloid:/ˈkɒrəlɔɪd/ /ˈkɔːrəlɔɪd/| adj.|珊瑚状的；珊瑚色的|Resembling coral, especially in structure or form; having a branched structure like coral.  Also, having the pink or reddish color of coral.|The fungus had a coralloid appearance. 这种真菌外观呈珊瑚状。|近义词：coral-like, branched, ramified|反义词:smooth, unbranched|词性:adjective

coralloid:/ˈkɒrəlɔɪd/ /ˈkɔːrəlɔɪd/| n.|珊瑚状物体|A thing resembling coral.|The divers found several coralloids on the seabed. 潜水员在海床上发现了几个珊瑚状物体。|近义词：coral-like structure|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
