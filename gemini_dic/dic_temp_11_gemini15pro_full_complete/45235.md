culpably:/ˈkʌlpəbli/| adv.|应受谴责地，该受责备地|In a manner deserving blame; blameworthily.  The driver was culpably negligent in causing the accident.|例句：He culpably neglected his duties. 他应受谴责地玩忽职守。

例句：She culpably withheld vital information from the police. 她该受责备地对警方隐瞒了重要信息。|近义词：blameworthy, guilty, reprehensible, censurable|反义词：blameless, innocent, laudable|词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *culpabilis, from Latin culpa (“fault, blame”) + -bilis, adjectival suffix.
