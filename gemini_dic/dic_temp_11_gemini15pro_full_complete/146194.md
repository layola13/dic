overexpand:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænd/ | v. | 过度扩张，过度膨胀 | To expand beyond safe or reasonable limits; to grow too large or too rapidly. | The company overexpanded during the boom years and eventually went bankrupt.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度扩张，最终破产了。 |  The balloon overexpanded and burst. 气球过度膨胀爆炸了。 | synonyms: overextend, overstretch, inflate, bloat | antonyms: contract, shrink, downsize | verb
