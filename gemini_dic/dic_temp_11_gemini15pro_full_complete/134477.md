nonlabeling:/ˌnɒnˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 非标记的，未贴标签的 | The process of not adding a label to something, often in a scientific or technical context. This could refer to not using radioactive isotopes in a biological experiment, not adding a brand name to a product, or not assigning a specific category or classification to something. | The researchers used a nonlabeling technique to study protein interactions. 研究人员使用非标记技术来研究蛋白质相互作用。 | The company decided on nonlabeling packaging for their generic product line.  该公司决定对其通用产品线使用非标记包装。| unlabeled, unmarked | labeled, marked | adjective
