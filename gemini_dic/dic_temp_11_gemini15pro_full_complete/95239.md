incalculable:/ɪnˈkælkjələbl/
adj.
不可估量的；无法计算的
too great to be calculated or estimated.
The damage caused by the earthquake was incalculable. 地震造成的损失不可估量。
近义词:immeasurable, inestimable, countless, infinite
反义词:calculable, measurable, estimable
词性:形容词

incalculable:/ɪnˈkælkjələbl/
adj.
极大的，巨大的
extremely large.
The project has brought incalculable benefits to the region. 这个项目给该地区带来了巨大的利益。
近义词:enormous, vast, immense, huge
反义词:small, tiny, minuscule
词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French incalculable, from in- ‘not’ + calculable (see calculate).
