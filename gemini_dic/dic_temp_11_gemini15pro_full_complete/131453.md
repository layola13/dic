noncogently:/ˌnɒnˈkəʊdʒəntli/ | adv. | 不令人信服地，没有说服力地 | Not in a cogent or convincing manner; lacking in persuasive force. |  The argument was presented noncogently, failing to persuade the audience.  论证没有说服力，未能说服听众。| Synonyms: unconvincingly, weakly, feebly, ineffectively | Antonyms: cogently, convincingly, persuasively, effectively | adverb
