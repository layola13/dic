bolete:/ˈboʊˌliːt/ /ˈbɒliːt/ | n. |牛肝菌；伞菌|A fleshy, edible fungus with a porous underside, typically found in woodland.  A type of mushroom.|例句：I found a beautiful bolete while hiking in the forest. 我在森林里徒步旅行时发现了一个漂亮的牛肝菌。

例句：The chef sautéed the boletes with garlic and butter.  厨师用大蒜和黄油煎了牛肝菌。|近义词：mushroom, fungus, cep|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
