catalepsy:/ˈkætəˌlɛpsi/| n. |僵住症，木僵|A medical condition characterized by a trancelike state of consciousness and postural rigidity.|The doctor diagnosed the patient with catalepsy after observing his rigid posture and unresponsive state. 医生观察到病人僵硬的姿势和无反应的状态后，诊断他患有僵住症。|Near synonyms: stupor, rigidity, trance|Antonyms: mobility, flexibility|Noun

catalepses:/ˈkætəˌlɛpsiːz/| n. |僵住症（复数）|The plural form of catalepsy, referring to multiple instances or cases of the condition.|The study examined several cases of catalepses in patients with schizophrenia. 这项研究调查了几个精神分裂症患者的僵住症病例。|Near synonyms: stupors, rigidities, trances|Antonyms: mobilities, flexibilities|Noun
