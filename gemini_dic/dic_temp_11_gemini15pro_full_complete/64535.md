errabund:/ˈerəbʌnd/ /ɪˈræbənd/
adjective
漫游的；流浪的；漂泊的
wandering; roving; nomadic; straying from the proper path or bounds
The errabund tribe moved from place to place in search of food. 漫游的部落为了寻找食物而四处迁徙。
The errabund thoughts drifted through his mind.  漂泊不定的思绪在他的脑海中游荡。
近义词: nomadic, wandering, roving, itinerant, migratory, peripatetic
反义词: settled, sedentary, stationary, resident
词性: adjective

词源: Latin *errabundus, present participle of errare "to wander, stray, err," from Proto-Italic *ersā-, from PIE root *ers- "to wander."
