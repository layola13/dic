back-date:/ˌbækˈdeɪt/| v.|回溯；追溯；倒填日期|To put an earlier date on a document or agreement than the actual date it was written or made.|The check was backdated to January 1st.  这张支票的日期被回溯到1月1日。|The contract was backdated to cover the period from June.  这份合同被倒填日期以涵盖从6月开始的期间。|Synonyms: antedate, postdate (with an earlier date)|Antonyms: postdate (with a later date)|verb
