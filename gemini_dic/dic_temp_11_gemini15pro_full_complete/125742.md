muntz:/mʌnts/
noun
蒙茨金属, 一种耐腐蚀的铜合金
A corrosion-resistant copper alloy.
The railing was made of muntz metal to withstand the salty air.  这个栏杆是用蒙茨金属制成的，以抵御咸咸的空气。
近义词:brass, bronze
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "corrodible metal")
词性:n.
