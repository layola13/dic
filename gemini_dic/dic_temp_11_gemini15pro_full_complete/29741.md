catnip: /ˈkætˌnɪp/ (US), /ˈkætˌnɪp/ (UK)| n. |猫薄荷|A plant (Nepeta cataria) of the mint family, whose leaves and stems produce a mildly intoxicating and euphoric effect on cats. |The cat rolled around ecstatically in the catnip.  这只猫欣喜若狂地在猫薄荷里打滚。|近义词：catmint|反义词: None|词性: noun
