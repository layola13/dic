inquisitorial:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
 inquisitorial; inquisitorially;
审讯式的；爱打听的
Resembling or characteristic of an inquisitor; given to rigorous and often harsh questioning.  Characterized by or resembling an inquisition.
例句：The police subjected him to an inquisitorial interrogation.  警方对他进行了审讯式的询问。

例句：Her inquisitorial nature made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她爱打听的性格让她不受同事欢迎。

近义词：probing, questioning, scrutinizing, inquiring, investigative, interrogative, prying, curious, nosy
反义词：uncurious, incurious, indifferent, apathetic

词性: adjective
