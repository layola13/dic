cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ /kjuˈniːəˌfɔrm/| adj. n.|楔形的；楔形文字|A system of writing used in ancient Mesopotamia, characterized by wedge-shaped marks impressed on clay tablets.  It is one of the earliest known forms of writing.|The museum has a fascinating collection of cuneiform tablets.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的楔形文字泥板。|近义词：wedge-shaped|反义词: (n.) alphabet, hieroglyphics|词性: adjective, noun
