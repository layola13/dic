isosulphocyanate: /ˌaɪsoʊˌsʌlfoʊˈsaɪəneɪt/ /ˌaɪsəʊˌsʌlfəʊˈsaɪəneɪt/
isosulphocyanate: n. |异硫氰酸酯|An ester of isosulphocyanic acid.  A compound containing the -N=C=S group. |例句：Allyl isothiocyanate gives mustard its characteristic flavor.  烯丙基异硫氰酸酯赋予芥末其特有的风味。

例句：Isothiocyanates are found in many cruciferous vegetables. 异硫氰酸酯存在于许多十字花科蔬菜中。
isosulphocyanate: |近义词：isothiocyanate|反义词: (No common chemical antonym)|词性: noun
