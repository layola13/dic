breakfasts:/ˈbrekfəsts/ (US) /ˈbrekfəsts/ (UK)| n. |早餐（复数）|The first meal of the day, typically eaten in the morning.|We enjoyed hearty breakfasts during our stay at the hotel. 我们在酒店期间享用了丰盛的早餐。|近义词：morning meals|反义词:dinners|Noun

词源: From Middle English *brekefast, from breken (“to break”) + fast (“fasting, abstinence from food”).  Literally "breaking the fast."
