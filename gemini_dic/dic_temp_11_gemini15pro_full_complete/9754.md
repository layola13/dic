antlerless:/ˈæntlər.ləs/
adjective
无角的
Lacking antlers.  Referring to female deer (does) or young male deer that have not yet grown antlers.
The regulations allow hunters to harvest one antlerless deer per season.  该法规允许猎人在每个季节捕猎一只无角鹿。
The hunter mistakenly shot an antlerless buck. 猎人错误地射杀了一只没有角的雄鹿。
Near Synonyms: hornless, without antlers
Opposite: antlered, horned
词性: adjective
