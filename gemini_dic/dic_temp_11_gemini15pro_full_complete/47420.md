deadlock:/ˈdedlɒk/ (UK) /ˈdɛdlɑːk/ (US)| n. v.|僵局，死锁|A situation, typically one involving opposing parties, in which no further progress can be made.|Negotiations have reached a complete deadlock. 谈判完全陷入了僵局。|近义词：standoff, stalemate, impasse|反义词：agreement, breakthrough, compromise|词性:名词, 动词

deadlock:/ˈdedlɒk/ (UK) /ˈdɛdlɑːk/ (US)| v. |(使)陷入僵局，(使)停顿|Cause (a situation or process) to become unresolvable.|The talks were deadlocked and no progress was made. 会谈陷入僵局，没有取得任何进展。|近义词：stall, block, hinder|反义词：resolve, facilitate, expedite|词性:动词
