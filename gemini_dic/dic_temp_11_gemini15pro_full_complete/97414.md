infructuose:/ˌɪnfrʊkˈtʃuːoʊs/
adjective
不结果实的;徒劳的;无益的
Not producing fruit; fruitless; unproductive.
His infructuose attempts to start a business left him in debt. 他徒劳的创业尝试让他负债累累。
近义词:fruitless, futile, unsuccessful, barren, unproductive, vain
反义词:fruitful, productive, successful, fertile
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *infructuosus, from in- (“not”) + fructuosus (“fruitful”), from fructus (“fruit”).
