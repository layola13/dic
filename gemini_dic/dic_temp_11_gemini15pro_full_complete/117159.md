meannesses:/ˈmiːnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
卑鄙行为；刻薄；吝啬
The state or quality of being mean, especially in character or behavior; acts of meanness.
His meannesses were well known throughout the town. 他在镇上以刻薄吝啬闻名。
synonyms: unkindness, cruelty, pettiness, stinginess, miserliness, niggardliness
antonyms: generosity, kindness, compassion, magnanimity, benevolence
词性: 名词(plural noun)
