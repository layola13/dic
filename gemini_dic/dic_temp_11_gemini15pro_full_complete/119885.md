militariness:/ˌmɪlɪˈtɛrɪnɪs/
n.
军国主义，军事化，好战性
The state of being military or warlike; a military character or quality.
The country's increasing militariness worried its neighbors.  这个国家日益增长的军国主义令其邻国担忧。
The government's militariness was evident in its increased defense spending. 政府的好战性在其增加的国防开支中显而易见。
近义词：bellicosity, aggressiveness, warmongering
反义词：pacifism, peacefulness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From military + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *militaris* "of a soldier," from *miles* "soldier."
