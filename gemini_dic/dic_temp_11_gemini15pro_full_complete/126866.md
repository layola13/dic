nappes:/ˈnæps/| n. |桌布；(尤指)餐桌的桌布；(地质)推覆体，逆掩层|A cloth spread over a table, especially during a meal; Also, in geology, a sheetlike body of rock that has been moved from its original location by faulting or folding.|The restaurant's white nappes gleamed in the candlelight. 餐厅里的白色桌布在烛光下闪闪发光。 The nappes of the Alps are complex and folded. 阿尔卑斯山的推覆体结构复杂且褶皱.|近义词：tablecloths, cloths (general), overthrusts (geology)|反义词: None (for tablecloths); autochthons (geology)|词性:noun


nappes:/ˈnæps/| n. |覆盖物，覆盖层 (更广泛的含义，可以指任何覆盖物)|Something that covers or overlays something else.|A thin layer of snow formed a delicate nappe over the fields. 薄薄的一层雪在田野上形成了精致的覆盖层。 The morning mist hung like a nappe over the valley. 晨雾像一层覆盖物笼罩着山谷。|近义词：covering, layer, blanket|反义词: None|词性:noun
