nonadherents:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪərənts/
n.
不信奉者；不遵守者；未加入者
People who do not adhere to a particular belief, rule, or group.
Many nonadherents of the religion still celebrate some of the holidays. 许多不信奉该宗教的人仍然庆祝一些节日。
The nonadherents to the treaty were criticized by the international community. 不遵守条约的国家受到了国际社会的批评。

synonyms:dissidents, objectors, opponents, rebels, nonconformists, heretics, apostates, dissenters, unbelievers, skeptics
antonyms:followers, adherents, believers, supporters, conformists, loyalists
noun
