manubrium:/məˈnjuːbriəm/
noun
胸骨柄
The uppermost part of the sternum, shaped like a knot of a tie.
The clavicles articulate with the manubrium.  锁骨与胸骨柄相连。
synonyms: handle, hilt
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for an anatomical term like manubrium)
