nonpermissibilities:/ˌnɒnpɜːrmɪsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不允许的事项；禁止的事项 |  The act of not permitting or the state of not being permitted.  Things that are not allowed or are forbidden.  | Due to various nonpermissibilities within the company's policy, employees are restricted from accessing certain websites. 由于公司政策中的各种不允许事项，员工被限制访问某些网站。

The contract clearly outlines the nonpermissibilities regarding the use of company property.  合同明确概述了关于公司财产使用的禁止事项。 | synonyms: prohibitions, restrictions, limitations, constraints | antonyms: permissions, allowances, authorizations | noun
