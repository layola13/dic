coruscatingly:/ˌkɔːrəˈskeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 闪耀地，闪烁地 |  Shining or flashing brilliantly; in a way that is dazzling or impressive. | Her diamond necklace coruscatingly reflected the light. 她的钻石项链闪耀地反射着灯光。 | The fireworks display was coruscatingly beautiful. 烟火表演闪耀夺目，美不胜收。 | 近义词：brilliantly, dazzlingly, sparklingly, glitteringly | 反义词：dully, dimly | 词性: 副词

coruscate: /ˈkɔːrəskeɪt/ | v. | 闪耀，闪烁 | To flash or sparkle. | The stars coruscated in the night sky. 星星在夜空中闪烁。|  His wit coruscated throughout the conversation. 他的机智在整个谈话中闪现。 | 近义词：sparkle, glitter, flash, shimmer | 反义词：dim, fade | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin "coruscāre" (to flash, glitter).
