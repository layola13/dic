digitalizations:/ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|数字化，数位化|The process of converting information into a digital format.  The digitalizations of library archives has made research much easier.|例句：The digitalizations of company records improved efficiency. 公司记录的数字化提高了效率。

例句：The museum is undergoing a period of rapid digitalizations. 博物馆正在经历一个快速的数字化时期。|近义词：digitization, computerization, automation|反义词:analogization|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From digitalize + -ation.  Ultimately from digit (finger, toe) + -al + -ize + -ation.  The word "digit" reflects the early use of fingers for counting.
