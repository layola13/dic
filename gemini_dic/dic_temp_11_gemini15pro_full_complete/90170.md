hotlist:/ˈhɒtlɪst/ (British) /ˈhɑːtlɪst/ (American)| n.| 热门名单；重点关注名单；优先处理名单|A list of people or things that are considered to be important or worthy of special attention.|例句：The police put him on the hotlist of suspects.  警方把他列入了嫌疑人名单。

例句：The book was immediately added to the bestseller hotlist. 这本书立刻被列入了畅销书排行榜。|近义词：shortlist, watchlist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
