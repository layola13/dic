colorimeter:/ˌkʌləˈrɪmɪtər/ | n. | 比色计，色度计 | An instrument used to measure the intensity of color, often used in chemistry and photography to determine the concentration of a substance or to analyze the color composition of a sample. | The chemist used a colorimeter to analyze the concentration of the dye in the solution. 化学家使用比色计分析溶液中染料的浓度。

The photographer used a colorimeter to calibrate their monitor for accurate color representation. 摄影师使用比色计校准他们的显示器以获得准确的色彩再现。 | color comparator, spectrophotometer | N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From color + -meter (a suffix meaning "measuring instrument," from Greek metron "measure").
