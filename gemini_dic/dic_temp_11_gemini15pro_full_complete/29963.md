cavers:/ˈkeɪvərz/| n. |洞穴探险者；洞穴探险家|People who explore caves, often for recreation or scientific study.  Those who engage in the activity of caving.|例句：The cavers discovered a new chamber deep within the cave system. 洞穴探险者在洞穴系统深处发现了一个新的洞室。
