overearnest:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrnɪst/
adjective
过度认真的，过分热心的
Excessively earnest; too serious and intense.
She delivered an overearnest speech about the importance of recycling. 她作了一个关于回收重要性的过分认真的演讲。

He was so overearnest in his apologies that it seemed insincere. 他道歉的态度过于认真，反而显得不真诚。

近义词:  serious, intense, fervent, zealous
反义词:  flippant, frivolous, casual, apathetic
词性: adjective
