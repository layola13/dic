coaxer:/ˈkoʊksər/| n.|哄骗者，劝诱者|Someone who coaxes; a person who persuades someone gently to do something.|She's a coaxer, not a forcer. 她是一个劝诱者，而不是强迫者。|近义词：persuader, wheedler, inducer|反义词：forcer, dictator|noun

coax:/ˈkoʊks/| v.|哄骗，劝诱|To persuade someone gently to do something.|He coaxed the cat down from the tree. 他哄着猫从树上下来。|近义词：persuade, wheedle, induce|反义词：force, compel|verb
