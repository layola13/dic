inter-sampled:/ˌɪntərˈsæmpəld/ | adj. |  间采样的，互相取样的 | Sampled at intervals or from different sources within a larger sample.  It involves taking samples from within a larger sample, often at regular intervals or according to a specific pattern. This can be done to reduce the overall number of samples required or to ensure representation from different parts of the population. | The data was inter-sampled to reduce processing time. 数据进行了间采样以减少处理时间。 | Inter-sampled data can be used to estimate population parameters. 间采样数据可用于估计总体参数。 |  sampled, cross-sampled | N/A | adjective
