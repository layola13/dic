andropogon:/ˌændrəˈpoʊɡən/ | n. |须芒草属；雄蕊草属|A genus of grasses, commonly known as bluestems, many of which are important forage crops.|Andropogon gerardii is a tallgrass prairie species. 雄蕊草是高草草原物种。|Andropogon virginicus is often used for erosion control.  维吉尼亚须芒草常用于防治水土流失。|近义词: bluestem, beardgrass |反义词:  (No specific antonym for a plant genus) |词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *ἀνδρ- (andr-)  "man" + *πώγων (pōgōn) "beard," referring to the hairy male florets.
