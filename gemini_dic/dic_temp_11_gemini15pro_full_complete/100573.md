interwreathes:/ˌɪntərˈriːðz/
verb
相互缠绕，交织
weave or twine together.
The roses and lilies were interwreathed around the columns. 玫瑰和百合花缠绕在柱子上。
intertwine, interlace, entwine
separate, disentangle
词性:v.
