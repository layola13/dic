cleft graft: /ˈklɛft ɡræft/ | n. | 劈接 | A grafting method in which a scion is inserted into a cleft cut in the stock. | The gardener used a cleft graft to join the apple scion to the rootstock. 园丁用劈接法将苹果接穗接到砧木上。|  The orchardist performed a cleft graft on the pear tree. 果农对梨树进行了劈接。 | synonyms: wedge graft | antonyms: N/A | noun


cleft: /klɛft/ | n. adj. |裂缝，裂口；劈开的 | A split or indentation, a space or opening made by cleavage; split, divided. |  There was a deep cleft in the rock face. 岩壁上有一道深深的裂缝。| A cleft palate is a birth defect. 腭裂是一种先天缺陷。| synonyms: fissure, crack, crevice, split | antonyms: closure, seal, whole | noun, adjective

graft: /ɡræft/ | n. v. |嫁接；移植；贪污 | (n.)
