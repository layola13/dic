drearihood: /ˈdrɪərihʊd/ | n. | 凄凉，沉闷，阴郁 | A state of dreariness; gloominess. |  The drearihood of the November landscape oppressed her. 十一月凄凉的景色让她感到压抑。 | 近义词：gloom, bleakness, desolation, dreariness | 反义词：cheerfulness, brightness, joy, happiness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From *dreary* + *-hood*. *Dreary* comes from Old English *drēorig*  meaning "sorrowful, mournful, bloodstained," related to *drēor* "gore, blood."  The suffix *-hood* forms nouns of state or condition.
