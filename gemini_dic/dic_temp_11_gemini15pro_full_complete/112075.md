longiloquence:/ˌlɒŋɡɪˈləʊkwəns/ /ˌlɔːŋɡɪˈloʊkwəns/| n. |冗长，啰嗦|The state or quality of being longiloquent; use of many words; verbosity.  The speaker's longiloquence made the audience restless.|例句：His longiloquence bored the audience. 他的冗长发言使听众感到厌烦。

例句：Her longiloquence made it difficult to understand her point. 她说话啰嗦，让人很难理解她的意思。|近义词：verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|反义词：brevity, conciseness, succinctness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *longiloquentia*, from longiloquus (“speaking at length”), from longus (“long”) + loqui (“to speak”).
