overpreach:/ˌoʊvərˈpriːtʃ/ | v. | 过度说教，讲道过多 | To preach excessively or to a tiresome extent, often focusing on minor details or repeating the same points. | The pastor tends to overpreach, and many parishioners find his sermons too long. 牧师倾向于过度说教，许多教区居民觉得他的布道太长了。

The politician overpreached his message, turning off potential voters.  这位政客过度宣扬他的理念，使潜在的选民感到厌烦。 | 近义词：moralize, sermonize, pontificate | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性：动词
