endosmose:/ˌɛndoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/ /ˌɛndɒzˈməʊsɪs/
noun
内渗
The inward passage of a fluid through a membrane toward a fluid of greater concentration.
Water enters the roots of plants by endosmose.  水通过内渗作用进入植物的根部。

absorption, osmosis, imbibition
exosmosis
