obturate:/ˈɒbtjʊreɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃəreɪt/ (UK)| v. | 阻塞，封闭 | To stop up; to close.  | The surgeon obturated the wound with a bandage.  外科医生用绷带封闭了伤口。 |  The dentist obturated the cavity with a filling. 牙医用填充物堵住了蛀牙洞。 |  close, block, occlude | open, unblock | verb
