debunking:/diːˈbʌŋkɪŋ/
n.|揭穿；揭露；驳斥|the act of exposing the falseness of a myth, idea, or belief.|The documentary focuses on debunking the myth of the Loch Ness Monster.|近义词: exposing, refuting, disproving|反义词: confirming, validating, verifying|词性: 名词 (Gerund/Verbal Noun)
debunked:/diːˈbʌŋkt/
v. (past tense & past participle)|揭穿；揭露；驳斥|to expose the falseness or hollowness of (a myth, idea, or belief).|Scientists have debunked the theory that eating carrots improves eyesight at night.|近义词: exposed, refuted, disproved|反义词: confirmed, validated, verified|词性: 动词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + bunk (nonsense)
