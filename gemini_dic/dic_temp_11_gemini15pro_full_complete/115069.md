manners:/ˈmænərz/| n. |礼貌；规矩；风俗习惯| Polite or well-bred social behavior; customary or traditional ways of behaving or speaking. |例句：It's bad manners to talk with your mouth full.  嘴里塞满食物说话是不礼貌的。

|例句：She has impeccable manners. 她举止无可挑剔。|近义词：etiquette, decorum, courtesy, politeness|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect|noun
