erythrophyllin: /ɪˌrɪθrəˈfɪlɪn/ | n. |  红藻素; 红藻叶绿素 | A red pigment found in certain red algae (Rhodophyta). It's a type of phycobilin, a class of accessory pigments in photosynthesis. | Red algae use erythrophyllin to absorb blue-green light, which can penetrate deeper into the water. 红藻利用红藻素吸收蓝绿光，这种光可以穿透到更深的水中。| The erythrophyllin in these algae allows them to thrive in deeper waters. 这些藻类中的红藻素使它们能够在更深的水域中茁壮成长。 | 近义词: phycoerythrin (藻红蛋白, 更广义的红藻中发现的色素) | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑 chlorophyll 叶绿素，因为功能相反) | 词性: noun
