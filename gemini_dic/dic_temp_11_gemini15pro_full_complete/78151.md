geophysicses:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪksɪz/ | n. |地球物理学(通常用单数)| The study of the physical properties of the Earth and its surrounding space environment, including the Earth's shape, internal structure, composition, and magnetic and gravitational fields. It also encompasses the study of geological phenomena such as earthquakes, volcanoes, and plate tectonics. |  Example 1: Geophysics plays a crucial role in understanding Earth's processes. 地球物理学在理解地球过程方面起着至关重要的作用。

Example 2:  He specialized in exploration geophysics, searching for oil and gas deposits. 他专门研究勘探地球物理学，寻找石油和天然气矿藏。 | Near Synonyms: geophysics, earth science | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider abstract concepts like "metaphysics") | noun
