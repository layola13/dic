headlight:/ˈhedˌlaɪt/| n.|前灯，车头灯|A light, usually one of two, at the front of a vehicle, such as a car, train, or bicycle.|The car's headlights cut through the thick fog. 汽车的前灯穿透了浓雾。|Synonyms: headlamp|Antonyms: taillight|noun

headlight:/ˈhedˌlaɪt/| n.|头灯，头戴灯|A light attached to a helmet or worn on the head, used especially by miners, cavers, and cyclists.|The miner's headlight illuminated the dark tunnel. 矿工的头灯照亮了黑暗的隧道。|Synonyms: headlamp, head lamp|Antonyms: N/A|noun
