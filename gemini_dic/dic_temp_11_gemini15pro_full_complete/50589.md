derainment:/diːˈreɪnmənt/
n.|脱轨；出轨；火车出轨|The derailment caused significant delays. 脱轨造成了严重的延误。|
例句：The derailment of the train caused several injuries. 火车脱轨造成数人受伤。

例句：Investigators are trying to determine the cause of the derailment.  调查人员正在努力确定脱轨的原因。

近义词：wrecking, crash, accident
反义词：(None - derailment describes a specific type of accident; there isn't a direct opposite)
词性:名词
