balayeuse:/balɛjøz/| n.|扫地机，清扫车|A machine, typically electrically powered, used for sweeping or cleaning floors.  It can refer to a manual or robotic device.|例句：The balayeuse made quick work of the spilled crumbs. 清扫车很快就把撒出来的面包屑清理干净了。

例句：She used the balayeuse to clean the garage floor. 她用扫地机清理了车库的地板。|近义词：sweeper, vacuum cleaner, cleaning machine|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From French "balayer" (to sweep) + "-euse" (feminine suffix denoting an instrument or agent). Ultimately from Vulgar Latin "balliare" (to sweep).
