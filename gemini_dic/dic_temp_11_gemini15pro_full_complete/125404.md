multiovulate: /ˌmʌltiˈɒvjuːleɪt/ | adj. | 多排卵的 | Producing or releasing multiple ova (eggs) during a single estrous or menstrual cycle.  |  Example: Multiovulate species, such as pigs and dogs, typically give birth to litters.  多排卵的物种，例如猪和狗，通常会产下多胎。 | Example: The researcher studied the reproductive cycle of multiovulate mammals. 研究人员研究了多排卵哺乳动物的生殖周期。 | Synonym: polyovular | Antonym: monovular | adjective
