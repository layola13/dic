cormac:/ˈkɔːrmæk/ | n. |科马克（男子名）|An Irish Gaelic name meaning "charioteer" or "son of the charioteer."  Derived from the Old Irish name *Corbmac*.| Cormac McCarthy is a renowned American novelist. 科马克·麦卡锡是一位著名的美国小说家。| My friend Cormac is a talented musician. 我的朋友科马克是一位才华横溢的音乐家。|Synonyms: (Name, no direct synonyms) Perhaps similar names in meaning like "driver" or "leader" could be considered. |Antonyms: N/A (Name) | noun
