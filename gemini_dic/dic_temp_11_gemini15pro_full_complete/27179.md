calligraphically:/ˌkælɪˈɡræfɪkli/
adv.
书法地，用书法
in a way that relates to calligraphy; in a beautiful and decorative handwriting style.
She signed her name calligraphically on the certificate.  她在证书上用书法签上了自己的名字。
近义词：beautifully, elegantly, artistically
反义词：crudely, sloppily, messily
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From calligraphy + -ically. Calligraphy comes from Greek kalligraphia "beautiful writing," from kallos "beauty" + graphein "to write."
