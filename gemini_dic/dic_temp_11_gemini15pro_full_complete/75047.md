frigga:/ˈfrɪɡə/| n.|弗丽嘉|Frigga is a Norse goddess, wife of Odin and mother of Baldr. In some interpretations, she is associated with marriage, motherhood, and prophecy.  She is a powerful figure in Norse mythology, often depicted as a wise and benevolent queen.|Frigga mourned deeply when her son Baldr died. 弗丽嘉在儿子巴尔德尔死后深切哀悼。|Synonyms: Freya (sometimes conflated with Frigga), Mother Goddess|Antonyms: N/A|noun
