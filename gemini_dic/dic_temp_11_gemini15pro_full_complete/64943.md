esteemed: /ɪˈstiːmd/ | adj. | 受人尊敬的，受敬重的 |  Highly regarded and respected.  |例句：The esteemed professor gave a lecture on quantum physics. 受人尊敬的教授做了一场关于量子物理学的讲座。

例句：She is an esteemed member of the community. 她是社区里受人尊敬的成员。

近义词：respected, admired, honored, revered | 反义词：despised, scorned, disrespected | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle French *estimer*, from Latin *aestimare* "to value, appraise," from *aes-* "copper, money" + *-timare* "to estimate." Originally meaning "valued," the sense shifted to "respected" over time.
