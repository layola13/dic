isomorphous:/ˌaɪsəˈmɔːrfəs/ | adj. |同形的，同晶的 | Having the same or closely similar form, structure, or appearance, especially of crystals having the same lattice structure but different chemical compositions. |  Calcite and sodium nitrate are isomorphous. 方解石和硝酸钠是同晶的。| synonymous: isomorphic, analogous, similar | antonymous: heteromorphous, dissimilar | adjective


isomorphism:/ˌaɪsəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. |同形，同晶 | The quality of being isomorphous; similarity of form. | The isomorphism between the two structures allows for easy data transfer. 这两种结构之间的同构性使得数据传输变得容易。| synonymous: similarity, analogy, correspondence | antonymous: heteromorphism, dissimilarity | noun
