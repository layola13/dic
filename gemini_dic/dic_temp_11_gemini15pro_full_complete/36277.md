cohabitor:/koʊˈhæbɪtər/ /kəˈhæbɪtə(r)/ |n.|同居者|A person who lives with another, especially in a sexual relationship, without being married to them. |例句：My cohabitor and I share the rent and household chores. 我和我的同居者分摊房租和家务。

例句：They were cohabitors for five years before they decided to get married.  他们在结婚前同居了五年。|近义词：partner, roommate, live-in lover|反义词：spouse, husband, wife|词性:noun
