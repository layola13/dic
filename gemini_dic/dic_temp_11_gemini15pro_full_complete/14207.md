autocentric:/ˌɔːtəˈsɛntrɪk/
| adj. |以自我为中心的，自私的|Considering oneself and one's own interests as primary; self-centered.|例句：His autocentric worldview prevents him from empathizing with others. 他以自我为中心的世界观使他无法同情他人。

|例句：She has a very autocentric personality and always puts her own needs first. 她性格非常以自我为中心，总是把自己的需求放在第一位。
|近义词：egocentric, self-centered, narcissistic, selfish|反义词：altruistic, selfless, other-centered|词性:形容词
