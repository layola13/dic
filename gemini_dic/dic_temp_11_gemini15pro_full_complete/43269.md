craftiest:/ˈkræf.ti.ɪst/ | adj. | 最狡猾的，最灵巧的 |  Superlative form of crafty: most crafty.  It describes someone who is extremely skilled at deceiving others or achieving their goals through trickery or clever manipulation. It can also mean exceptionally skilled in a particular craft. |  He was the craftiest politician I'd ever met. 他是我见过的最狡猾的政治家。 | synonyms:  sly, cunning, wily, artful, ingenious, shrewd | antonyms:  honest, straightforward, naive, guileless | adjective
