expurgatorious:/ˌekspərˈɡeɪtɔːriəs/
adjective
净化（不良内容）的;删除（令人反感内容）的
Tending to expurgate; expurgatory.  Removing or tending to remove matter thought to be objectionable or unsuitable.
The editor made several expurgatorious changes to the novel before it was published.  编辑在小说出版前对小说做了一些净化处理，删除了一些令人反感的内容。
近义词:  bowdlerizing, censoring, cleansing, purifying, sanitizing
反义词:  unabridged, unexpurgated, uncensored
词性: adjective
