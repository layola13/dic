mineralized:/ˈmɪnərəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 矿化的；含矿物的 | Containing or impregnated with minerals; converted into a mineral. | The bones were mineralized over time, turning them into fossils.  这些骨头随着时间的推移矿化，变成了化石。 | The soil is highly mineralized and rich in nutrients. 土壤高度矿化，富含营养物质。 | 近义词: fossilized, petrified, ore-bearing | 反义词: demineralized, unmineralized | 词性: 形容词

词源: From mineral + -ize + -ed.  来自 mineral（矿物） + -ize （使…化） + -ed （过去分词后缀）
