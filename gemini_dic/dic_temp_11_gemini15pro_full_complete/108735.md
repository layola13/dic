leapfrogger:/ˈliːpˌfrɒɡər/ (US) /ˈliːpˌfrɒɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|跳跃者；超越者；跳背游戏者|A person who leapfrogs, either literally in the children's game, or figuratively, by surpassing others in rank or achievement. |The company acted as a leapfrogger in the technology market, quickly surpassing its established competitors.  这家公司在技术市场上扮演了跳跃者的角色，迅速超越了老牌竞争对手。|Synonyms: overtaker, outstripper, surpasser|Antonyms: laggard, straggler|noun

leapfrogger:/ˈliːpˌfrɒɡər/ (US) /ˈliːpˌfrɒɡə(r)/ (UK)| v.|跳跃；超越|To surpass or overtake, especially in rank, achievement, or development. |Several developing countries have leapfrogged the industrialized nations in mobile technology adoption.  几个发展中国家在移动技术采用方面已经超越了工业化国家。|Synonyms: overtake, bypass, outstrip|Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind|verb
