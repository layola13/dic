dullest:/ˈdʌlɪst/| adj.|最迟钝的，最无聊的，最阴沉的|The dullest knife I've ever used.  我用过的最钝的刀。|近义词：most boring, most tedious, most gloomy, most uninteresting|反义词：brightest, sharpest, most exciting, most interesting|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dullest, superlative of dul, from Old English dol “foolish, dull,” of Germanic origin.  Related to Old Norse dullr “cloudy, dark; sad, downcast,” Dutch dol “mad, foolish,” German toll “mad, furious, wild.” The original sense was “foolish, slow-witted,” which broadened to “lacking sharpness or brightness” and then to “boring, tedious.”
