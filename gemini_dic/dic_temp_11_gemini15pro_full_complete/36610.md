collectedly:/kəˈlɛktɪdli/
adverb
共同地，集体地
in a collective manner; together
例句：The students collectedly decided to organize a protest. 学生们共同决定组织抗议。

例句：They collectedly agreed to contribute to the fund. 他们一致同意向基金捐款。

近义词：jointly, together, unitedly, concertedly, as a whole
反义词：individually, separately, singly, apart, independently
词性：副词
