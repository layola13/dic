bibliophilies: /ˌbɪbliəˈfɪliz/
n.
藏书癖；爱书癖；书籍收藏
A great love of books; bibliophilism.  The collecting of books, especially rare or fine books.
He indulged his bibliophilies by spending hours in dusty old bookshops. 他流连于尘封的旧书店，沉迷于自己的藏书癖。
近义词：bibliophilism, book-collecting, bibliomania
反义词：bibliophobia (fear of books)
词性：名词 (plural)

词源:
From French *bibliophilie*, from biblio- + -philie (from Greek *philía* “love”).
