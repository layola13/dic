charitableness:/ˈtʃærɪtəblənəs/
n.
仁慈，慈善，宽厚
The quality of being charitable; kindness and tolerance in judging others.
Her charitableness extended to all creatures, great and small. 她对所有生物，无论大小，都非常仁慈。
Synonyms: benevolence, generosity, kindness, compassion, tolerance, magnanimity
Antonyms:  malice, malevolence, cruelty, intolerance, stinginess, meanness
