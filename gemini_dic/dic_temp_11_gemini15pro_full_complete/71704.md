flavoring:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋ/ | n. |调味品；调味料|Something added to food or drink to give it a particular flavor. |  The chef added a special flavoring to the soup.  厨师在汤里加了一种特殊的调味料。| synonym: seasoning, condiment, spice, additive | antonym: blandness | noun

flavoring:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋ/ | adj. |调味的|Used to flavor food or drink. |  The flavoring agent gave the dish a unique taste.  这种调味剂赋予了这道菜独特的风味。| synonym: seasoning, aromatic, savory | antonym: bland, unflavored | adjective
