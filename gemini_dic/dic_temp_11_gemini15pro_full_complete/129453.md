non-addict:/ˌnɒnˈædɪkt/ | n. | 非成瘾者 | A person who is not addicted to a substance or activity. |  He considered himself a non-addict, only having a casual drink now and then. 他认为自己不是成瘾者，只是偶尔喝一杯。| Synonyms: non-user, abstainer | Antonyms: addict, user | Noun


non-addict:/ˌnɒnˈædɪkt/ | adj. | 非成瘾的 | Not addicted to a substance or activity. | She described herself as having a non-addict personality, not prone to compulsive behaviors. 她形容自己性格上不沉迷，不容易出现强迫行为。 | Synonyms: non-dependent, non-addictive | Antonyms: addictive, dependent | Adjective
