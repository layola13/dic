nonspiny:/ˌnɒnˈspaɪni/ | adj. | 无刺的，没有刺的 | Not having spines; spineless. From "non-" (not) + "spiny" (having spines).  Ultimately from Latin "spina" (thorn). |  This variety of rose is nonspiny, making it ideal for gardens frequented by children.  这种玫瑰没有刺，非常适合有小孩的花园。| 近义词: smooth, thornless | 反义词: spiny, thorny, prickly | 词性: 形容词
