almanzor: /ˌælmənˈzɔːr/ | n. | 阿尔曼佐 | A Spanish given name, from Arabic المنصور (al-manṣūr) meaning "the victorious".  It is most famously associated with Almanzor, a powerful 10th-century military leader in Islamic Spain. | Example: My grandfather's name was Almanzor.  我的祖父名叫阿尔曼佐。 | Example: Almanzor led his troops to victory. 阿尔曼佐带领他的部队取得了胜利。 | Near synonyms: victor, champion | Antonyms: loser, defeated | Noun
