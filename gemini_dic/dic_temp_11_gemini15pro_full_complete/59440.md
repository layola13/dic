earthshot:/ˈɜːrθʃɒt/ (US) /ˈɜːθʃɒt/ (UK)| n.|地球目标，重大环保目标|An ambitious goal, especially one to protect the environment.|例句：The Earthshot Prize rewards people working on solutions to environmental problems. 地球目标奖奖励致力于解决环境问题的人们。

例句：Their latest earthshot is to reduce carbon emissions by 50% in the next decade. 他们的最新地球目标是在未来十年内将碳排放量减少 50%。|近义词：goal, objective, target, aim|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示不作为的词) inaction, apathy|词性: noun
