metalinguist:/ˌmɛtəˈlɪŋɡwɪst/ | n. |元语言学家；语言金属学家 | A metalinguist is a person who studies metalanguage, which is language or symbols used to describe another language. |  A prominent metalinguist explored the relationship between language and thought. 一位著名的元语言学家探讨了语言与思维之间的关系。|  The metalinguist analyzed the grammar rules of the language. 这位元语言学家分析了该语言的语法规则。 | synonyms: linguist, language scholar | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "illiterate" in a broad sense, but not precise) | noun
