maxest: /ˈmæksɪst/
adjective
最大的，最高的 (non-standard, humorous or dialectal)
A superlative form of "max," used informally to mean "maximum" or "most."  It implies taking something to the absolute highest degree.
例句：He gave it his maxest effort.  他付出了最大的努力。

例句：That's the maxest speed this car can go. 这辆车能达到的最高速度就是这样了。

近义词：maximum, greatest, highest, utmost
反义词：least, lowest, minimum, smallest
词性: adjective
