kaleidophon:/kəˈlaɪdəfəʊn/
noun
万花筒音箱；音色万花筒
A toy or instrument that produces changing patterns of sound and light, often by reflecting sounds onto a vibrating surface.
The kaleidophon created a mesmerizing display of sound and light.  万花筒音箱创造了迷人的声音和光线的展示。
kaleidoscope, sound visualizer
