nonpacifists:/ˌnɒnˈpæsɪfɪsts/ /ˌnɑːnˈpæsɪfɪsts/
n.|非和平主义者|People who do not believe in pacifism (the belief that war and violence are unjustifiable).|The nonpacifists argued for stronger military action.|Synonyms: warmongers, hawks, belligerents|Antonyms: pacifists, peacemakers|noun
