cuckoldly: /ˈkʌkəldli/ | adj. | 妻子有外遇的，戴绿帽子的 |  Of, relating to, or characteristic of a cuckold; ignominious.  Describing a man whose wife has been unfaithful, or the humiliating situation he is in. | He felt cuckoldly when he saw his wife flirting with another man. 他看到妻子与另一个男人调情，感到自己被戴了绿帽子。 |  The cuckoldly husband plotted revenge.  这位被戴绿帽子的丈夫策划复仇。 | 近义词: humiliated, shamed, dishonored | 反义词: respected, honored | 词性: 形容词

词源: From cuckold + -ly.  "Cuckold" comes from Old French cucuault, from cucu, echoing the cuckoo's habit of laying its eggs in other birds' nests.
