matron:/ˈmeɪtrən/ | n. | 女舍监，护士长，主妇 | A woman who is in charge of domestic and medical arrangements at a school or other institution, or a senior nurse. | The matron of the boarding school was very strict.  寄宿学校的女舍监非常严格。| synonyms: superintendent, supervisor, housekeeper, head nurse | antonyms: student, patient, subordinate | noun

matron:/ˈmeɪtrən/ | n. | （尤指体态丰满的）已婚妇女，中年妇女 | A married woman, usually a dignified older one, or sometimes used humorously or slightly offensively to refer to a woman who is thought to be too concerned about behaving correctly. |  She's the matron of the village. 她是村里的主妇。 | synonyms: wife, lady, dame | antonyms: spinster, bachelor, girl | noun
