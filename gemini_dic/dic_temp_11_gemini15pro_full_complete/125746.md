muraena:/məˈriːnə/| n. |海鳝，鮣|A large, voracious, chiefly tropical marine eel of the family Muraenidae, having a long, snakelike body, sharp teeth, and no pelvic fins. |例句：The muraena lurked in the coral reef, waiting to ambush its prey.  海鳝潜伏在珊瑚礁中，等待伏击猎物。

例句：Divers should be cautious around muraenas, as their bite can be dangerous.  潜水员在海鳝周围应该小心，因为它们的咬伤可能很危险。|近义词：moray eel, eel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
