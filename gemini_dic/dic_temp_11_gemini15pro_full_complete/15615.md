bailiwick: /ˈbeɪlɪwɪk/ | n. | 管辖范围，势力范围；本行，专长 | One's area of skill, knowledge, authority, or work.  | The education of children is not within the bailiwick of the police. 教育儿童不在警方的管辖范围之内。 |  Example:  Cybersecurity is her bailiwick. 网络安全是她的专长。 | Synonyms: domain, province, jurisdiction, sphere, purview, area of expertise | Antonyms:  None | noun

bailiwick: /ˈbeɪlɪwɪk/ | n. |  管辖区域 | The district or jurisdiction of a bailiff. (This meaning is now archaic.) | The sheriff was responsible for all the bailiwicks in the county. 县治安官负责该县的所有辖区。(古语) | Example: The bailiff patrolled his bailiwick.  法警巡逻他的辖区。(古语) | Synonyms: jurisdiction, district | Antonyms: None | noun
