flicking:/ˈflɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 轻弹；轻拂；快速翻阅 | A quick, sharp movement, often with a finger, to hit or move something small and light. |  He was flicking through the channels on TV. 他正在快速翻阅电视频道。| She flicked the dust off her coat. 她轻弹掉外套上的灰尘。 | 近义词：flip, tap, brush, skim | 反义词：press, hold | 词性：动词

flicking:/ˈflɪkɪŋ/ | adj. |  轻弹的；闪烁的 |  Used to describe a quick, light movement. | The flicking flames of the candle created a warm glow. 闪烁的烛光营造出温暖的光芒. |  The lizard's flicking tongue caught the fly. 蜥蜴快速弹出的舌头抓住了苍蝇. | 近义词：darting, flashing | 反义词：steady, constant | 词性：形容词 (less common)
