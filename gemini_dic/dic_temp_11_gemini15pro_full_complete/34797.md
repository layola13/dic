climaxless:/ˈklaɪmæksləs/
adjective
无高潮的，平淡的
lacking a climax; anticlimactic.
The movie was climaxless and disappointing. 这部电影没有高潮，令人失望。
The play was strangely climaxless, leaving the audience feeling unfulfilled.  这出戏奇怪地缺乏高潮，让观众感到不满足。

近义词: anticlimactic, underwhelming, flat, uneventful, monotonous
反义词: exciting, thrilling, climactic, dramatic

词性: adjective
