ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bylan (intransitive) and byl(i)an (transitive), from Proto-Germanic *buljaną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewl-, *bʰuH₂l- (“to bubble, swell”). Cognate with Dutch builen (“to swell”), German beulen (“to dent, bulge”), Old Norse bólgna (“to swell”). The noun is from the verb.
