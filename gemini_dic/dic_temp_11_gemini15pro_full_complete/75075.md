frigidly:/ˈfrɪdʒɪdli/| adv.|冷淡地，冷漠地|In a frigid manner; coldly.|She stared frigidly at him, showing no emotion. 她冷冷地盯着他，毫无表情。|He frigidly dismissed her suggestion. 他冷淡地拒绝了她的建议。|近义词：coldly, icily, aloofly, distantly|反义词：warmly, kindly, affectionately|词性:副词

词源: From frigid + -ly.  Frigid comes from Latin frigidus "cold, chill, stiff, languid," from frigere "to be cold, to roast, to fry."
