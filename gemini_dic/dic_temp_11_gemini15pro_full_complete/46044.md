cycles:/ˈsaɪkəlz/ (US), /ˈsaɪklz/ (UK)| n.|循环，周期，自行车|A series of events that are regularly repeated in the same order.|The life cycle of a butterfly is fascinating. 蝴蝶的生命周期令人着迷。|synonyms: revolutions, rounds, series, phases|antonyms: chaos, disorder|noun

cycles:/ˈsaɪkəlz/ (US), /ˈsaɪklz/ (UK)| v.|循环，骑自行车|To move in or pass through a cycle.|The washing machine cycles through wash, rinse, and spin. 洗衣机循环进行洗涤、漂洗和脱水。|synonyms: rotate, revolve, circle, recur|antonyms: halt, stop|verb
