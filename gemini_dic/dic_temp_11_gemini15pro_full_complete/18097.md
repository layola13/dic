behaviouristically: /bɪˈheɪvjərɪˈstɪkli/ | adv. | 行为主义地 | In a way that is related to or based on the principles of behaviourism. |  His theories were interpreted behaviouristically. 他的理论被从行为主义的角度进行了解释。|  She approached the problem behaviouristically, focusing on observable actions. 她从行为主义的角度处理这个问题，关注可观察的行为。 |  Synonyms: behaviorally | Antonyms: cognitively, subjectively | word type: adverb
