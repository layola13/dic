co-inherence:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɪrəns/
|n.|共同存在，相互依存|The co-inherence of mind and body is a central tenet of some philosophies.  身心相互依存是一些哲学的核心原则。|Co-inherence emphasizes the interconnectedness of elements within a system. 共同存在强调系统内各要素的相互联系。|
|近义词：interdependence, interconnectedness, interrelation, symbiosis|反义词：independence, separation, disconnection|noun|

co-inherent:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɪrənt/
|adj.|共同存在的，相互依存的|The co-inherent properties of light and energy are studied in physics. 光和能量相互依存的特性是物理学的研究对象。|The report highlighted the co-inherent relationship between economic growth and environmental protection.  报告强调了经济增长与环境保护之间相互依存的关系。|
|近义词：interdependent, interconnected, interrelated, symbiotic|反义词：independent, separate, disconnected|adjective|
