irrecusable:/ɪrɪˈkjuːzəbl/ /ˌɪrɪˈkjuːzəbəl/
adjective
不可拒绝的；不容置疑的
Not able to be refused or rejected; undeniable.
His offer was irrecusable, so I accepted. 他的提议不容拒绝，所以我接受了。
The evidence presented in court was irrecusable. 在法庭上提出的证据是确凿无疑的。
近义词:incontestable, undeniable, irrefutable, unchallengeable
反义词:refusable, questionable, disputable
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *irrecusabilis, from in- ‘not’ + recusare ‘refuse’ (from re- ‘back’ + causa ‘cause, reason’).
