lmk:/ˌɛlɛmˈkeɪ/|abbr.|让我知道|Short for "let me know". It's used informally, primarily in online communication or texting, to request information or a response.|例句：LMK when you're free. 让我知道你什么时候有空。

例句：Just lmk if you need anything. 如果你需要什么就告诉我。|近义词：tell me, inform me, keep me posted, keep me updated|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "don't tell me")|词性:abbreviation
