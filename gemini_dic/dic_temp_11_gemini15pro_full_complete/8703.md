anticreativenesses:/ˌæntiˌkriːeɪˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏创造力；反创造性
The state or quality of being anticreative; instances of this.  The stifling of creativity; actions or attitudes that discourage or suppress creative expression.
His anticreativenesses stifled the team's innovative spirit. 他的缺乏创造力扼杀了团队的创新精神。
The government's policies promoted anticreativenesses in the arts. 政府的政策抑制了艺术领域的创造力。
Synonym: uncreativeness, unimaginativeness, sterility
Antonym: creativity, inventiveness, originality
