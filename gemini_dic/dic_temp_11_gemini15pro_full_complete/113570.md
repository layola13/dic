macauco:/məˈkɔːkoʊ/| n. |环尾狐猴，又称狐猴|A type of lemur, specifically the ring-tailed lemur (Lemur catta), native to Madagascar. It is known for its long, bushy, ringed tail.| The macauco is easily recognized by its distinctive tail. 环尾狐猴很容易通过其独特的尾巴辨认出来。

The zoo has a family of macaucos.  这家动物园有一群环尾狐猴。|近义词：ring-tailed lemur, lemur|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Malagasy *makomoko* or *makoko*, ultimately of imitative origin, referring to the animal's calls.  词源：来自马达加斯加语 *makomoko* 或 *makoko*，最终是拟声词起源，指的是这种动物的叫声。
