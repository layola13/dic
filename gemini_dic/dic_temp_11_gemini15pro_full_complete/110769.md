linguidental:/ˌlɪŋɡwɪˈdɛntəl/| adj.|舌齿音的；与舌和牙齿有关的|Relating to or produced by the tongue and teeth.  It describes sounds made with the tongue touching the teeth.|The sound /θ/ as in "thin" is a linguidental sound.  θ在“thin”这个单词中的发音是舌齿音。|近义词:dental, interdental|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps velar or glottal depending on the specific context)|词性:adjective
