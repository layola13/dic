lapling: /ˈlæplɪŋ/
noun
幼畜（尤指小动物，依偎在母兽怀中）, 被溺爱的人
a young animal (especially a small dog) that is held lovingly in someone's lap.  A person who is pampered or overprotected.
The tiny lapling nestled contentedly in its mother's fur.  这只幼小的动物依偎在母亲的皮毛里，心满意足。
He's a bit of a lapling, always relying on his parents for everything. 他有点被宠坏了，总是依赖父母做任何事。
近义词:  nestling,  pet
反义词:  adult, independent
词性: n.
