kneads:/niːdz/
verb
揉捏，捏合
works dough or clay into a uniform mixture by pressing, folding, and stretching.
The baker kneads the dough for the bread.  面包师揉捏做面包的面团。
近义词: massages, works, molds
反义词: flattens, stretches (in a single direction)
词性: verb (third-person singular simple present)
