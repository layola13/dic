biopsy: /ˈbaɪˌɑːpsi/ (US), /ˈbaɪɒpsi/ (UK)| n.|活组织检查；活检|The removal and examination of a sample of tissue from a living body for diagnostic purposes.|The doctor ordered a biopsy to determine if the lump was cancerous. 医生下令进行活检以确定肿块是否是癌性的。|The results of the biopsy came back negative. 活检结果呈阴性。|近义词：tissue sampling, examination|反义词: None|词性:noun

biopsy: /ˈbaɪˌɑːpsi/ (US), /ˈbaɪɒpsi/ (UK)| vt.|对…进行活组织检查|To perform a biopsy on (someone or something).|The surgeon biopsied the suspicious lesion.  外科医生对可疑的病灶进行了活检。|They biopsied the tumor to determine its nature. 他们对肿瘤进行了活检以确定其性质。|近义词：examine, test|反义词: None|词性:verb
