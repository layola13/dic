non-evasive:/ˌnɒn ɪˈveɪsɪv/| adj.|非侵入性的，非伤害性的|Not involving the introduction of instruments into the body, or not likely to cause offence or distress.|例句：The doctor recommended a non-evasive procedure to diagnose the problem. 医生建议采用非侵入性手术来诊断问题。|例句：She asked non-evasive questions about his personal life. 她问了他一些不涉及隐私的个人生活问题。|近义词：non-intrusive, non-aggressive, gentle|反义词:invasive, aggressive, intrusive|词性:形容词
