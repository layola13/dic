concertino:/kɒntʃəˈtiːnəʊ/ (UK), /kɑːntʃərˈtiːnoʊ/ (US)
n.
小协奏曲；独奏（或小合奏）小组
a short concerto, or a small group of solo instruments
The orchestra performed a delightful concertino for flute and oboe. 管弦乐队演奏了一首令人愉悦的长笛和双簧管小协奏曲。
synonyms:serenade, intermezzo
antonyms:concerto, symphony
noun

concertino:/kɒntʃəˈtiːnəʊ/ (UK), /kɑːntʃərˈtiːnoʊ/ (US)
n.
独奏（或小合奏）小组
a small group of solo instruments
The concertino consisted of two violins and a cello.  这个小合奏组由两把小提琴和大提琴组成。
synonyms:ensemble, group
antonyms:orchestra, band
noun
