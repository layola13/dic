alkanes:/ˈælkeɪnz/ | n. | 烷烃 |  A homologous series of saturated hydrocarbons with the general formula CnH2n+2.  They are also known as paraffins.  | Methane, ethane, and propane are simple alkanes. 甲烷、乙烷和丙烷是简单的烷烃。 |  saturated hydrocarbons, paraffins | alkenes, alkynes | noun
