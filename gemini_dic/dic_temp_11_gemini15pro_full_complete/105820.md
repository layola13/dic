kistvaen:/ˈkɪstvɛn/
n.
石棺，石槨
A prehistoric burial chamber typically consisting of a small rectangular or square box-like structure made of stone slabs, often covered with an earth mound.
The archaeologists discovered a well-preserved kistvaen containing the remains of a Bronze Age chieftain. 考古学家发现了一个保存完好的石棺，里面装着青铜时代酋长的遗骸。
近义词: dolmen, tomb, burial chamber
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open grave" in contrast to contained burial)
词性: 名词
