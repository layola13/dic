anchoretish:/ˌæŋkəˈrɛtɪʃ/ | adj. |隐士般的，遁世的|Like a hermit; characterized by solitude and contemplation.| He lived an anchoretish existence in the remote mountains. 他在偏远的山区过着隐士般的生活。| 近义词: hermitical, ascetic, reclusive, solitary | 反义词: gregarious, sociable | 词性:形容词

词源: From Late Middle English *ankeretisch, from Old French ancre + -ish. Ultimately from Late Latin anachorēta, from Greek anakhōrētēs (“hermit”), from anakhōreō (“I withdraw, retire”).
