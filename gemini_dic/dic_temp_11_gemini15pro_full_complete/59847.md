ectoplasmic:/ˌɛktəˈplæzmɪk/
adjective
外质的; 由外质组成的
Relating to ectoplasm, a viscous substance supposed to emanate from the body of a medium during a trance.
The medium claimed to produce ectoplasmic material during the séance.  灵媒声称在降神会期间产生了外质材料。
The ghostly figure appeared to be made of a shimmering, ectoplasmic substance.  幽灵般的身影似乎是由一种闪闪发光的外质构成的。
近义词:spiritual, ethereal
反义词:material, physical
词性:adjective

词源:  From ecto- + -plasm + -ic.  Ecto- comes from Greek *ektós "outside."  -plasm comes from Greek *plasma "something molded or formed."
