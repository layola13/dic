break-down:/ˈbreɪkdaʊn/| n. |故障，崩溃，分解，分类|a failure to function or work properly, a collapse of a relationship or system, the separation of something into constituent parts, an itemized analysis|The car suffered a breakdown on the highway. 汽车在高速公路上抛锚了。|synonyms: failure, malfunction, collapse, decomposition, analysis|antonyms: success, repair, construction, synthesis|noun

break-down:/breɪk ˈdaʊn/| v. |分解，发生故障，失败，（情感）崩溃，分类，细分|to separate something into constituent parts, to stop functioning, to fail, to lose control of one's emotions, to categorize, to divide into smaller parts|The peace talks broke down after just two days. 和谈仅仅两天后就破裂了。|synonyms: decompose, malfunction, fail, collapse, categorize, analyze|antonyms: repair, succeed, construct, synthesize|verb
