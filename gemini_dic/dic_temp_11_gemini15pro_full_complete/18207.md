belch:/beltʃ/| n. v.|打嗝，喷出|A belch is a noisy escape of gas from the stomach through the mouth. 打嗝是指气体从胃中经口发出响声的排出。|The baby belched after his feed. 婴儿吃完奶后打了个嗝。|近义词：burp, eructate|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|noun, verb

belch:/beltʃ/| n. v.|（火山、烟囱等）喷出，冒出|To belch means to suddenly send out something, like smoke or fire, in a large amount. 指突然大量喷出某物，如烟或火。|The volcano belched out a cloud of ash. 火山喷出一团火山灰。|近义词：spew, emit, erupt|反义词: absorb, inhale|noun, verb
