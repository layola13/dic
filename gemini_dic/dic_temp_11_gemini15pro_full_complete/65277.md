euchologion:/ˌjuːkəˈlɒdʒiən/
n.
祈祷书, 礼拜仪式书
A book containing the liturgical prayers and rites of a church, especially in the Eastern Orthodox Church.
The priest read from the euchologion during the Divine Liturgy.  神父在神圣礼拜仪式上诵读祈祷书。
The euchologion contained prayers for various occasions and feasts. 这本祈祷书包含了各种场合和节日的祈祷文。
prayer book, liturgical book, service book
missal, breviary
