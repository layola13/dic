clamatores:/klɑːməˈtɔːreɪz/
|n.| shouters, criers (plural)|呼喊者，叫喊者（复数）|They were the clamatores of their generation, voicing the concerns of the marginalized. 他们是他们那一代的呼喊者，表达了边缘化群体的担忧。|近义词:criers, yellers, shriekers|反义词:whisperers, murmurers|词性: Noun (Plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "clamātor,"  nominative plural "clamātōrēs," meaning "shouter," "crier," from the verb "clamō" meaning "to shout," "to cry out."
