celebrant: /ˈsɛləbrənt/ (US), /ˈselibrənt/ (UK)| n.| 庆祝者；(尤指)主祭神父；司仪|A person who celebrates something, especially a priest who performs a religious ceremony. |例句：The celebrant led the congregation in prayer. 主祭神父带领会众祈祷。

例句：The celebrants raised their glasses in a toast. 庆祝者们举杯祝酒。

近义词：officiant, minister, priest, participant, reveler |反义词: mourner|词性: noun
