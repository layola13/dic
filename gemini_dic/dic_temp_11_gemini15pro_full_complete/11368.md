areometrical:/ˌɛəriəˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
面积测量的；比重测量的
Relating to or determined by areometry (the measurement of the specific gravity of liquids).
This areometrical method allows for precise determination of the liquid's density. 这种面积测量法可以精确地测定液体的密度。
The scientist used an areometrical instrument to measure the density of the fluid.  科学家使用比重计测量液体的密度。
近义词: densitometric, gravimetric
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *aréométrique*, from *aréomètre* (“hydrometer”) + *-ique* + *-al*, from Ancient Greek ἀραιός (araios, “thin, rare”) + μέτρον (metron, “measure”).
