impulsiveness:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/| n. |冲动，鲁莽|The tendency to act or decide without thinking carefully about the possible consequences.|His impulsiveness often got him into trouble. 他的冲动经常给他带来麻烦。|近义词：rashness, recklessness, spontaneity|反义词: caution, prudence, deliberation|noun


impulsive:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj. |冲动的，易冲动的|Acting or done without forethought.|She made an impulsive decision to quit her job. 她一时冲动决定辞掉工作。|近义词：rash, reckless, spontaneous|反义词: cautious, prudent, deliberate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impulsivus, from Latin impulsus (past participle of impellere “to push, drive on”), from in- “in” + pellere “to push, drive.”
