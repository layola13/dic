misdevelops:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvɛləps/
verb
发育不良，发展不正常
To develop badly or incorrectly; to fail to develop properly.
The child misdeveloped due to malnutrition.  由于营养不良，这个孩子发育不良。
The company's new product misdeveloped and failed to meet market demands.  这家公司的新产品开发不当，未能满足市场需求。
Synonyms: malfunction, fail, go wrong
Antonyms: thrive, flourish, prosper
词性: 动词
