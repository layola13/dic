oleraceous:/ˌɒləˈreɪʃəs/ /ˌoʊləˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
蔬菜的；似蔬菜的
of, relating to, or resembling vegetables; having the characteristics of vegetables, especially such as are eaten.
Example: The garden was full of oleraceous plants.  花园里种满了蔬菜。

Example: The smell of oleraceous soup filled the kitchen.  蔬菜汤的香味弥漫了整个厨房。

近义词:vegetable, vegetal
反义词:meaty, fleshy
词性:adjective
