overprocessing:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəˈprəʊsesɪŋ/
| n. |过度加工; 过度处理|The act of processing something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative consequences.  It can refer to food, data, information, or even emotions.|
|例句：Overprocessing of food can strip it of its nutrients. 过度加工食品会使其失去营养。|

|例句：The overprocessing of data can lead to information overload.  数据的过度处理会导致信息过载。|
|近义词：overdoing, overhandling, overmanipulation|
|反义词: underprocessing, simplifying|
|词性: 名词|


overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/ /ˌəʊvəˈprəʊses/
| v. |过度加工; 过度处理|To process something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative consequences.|
|例句：They overprocessed the image, making it look artificial. 他们过度处理了图像，使其看起来很假。|

|例句：Be careful not to overprocess the meat, or it will be tough.  小心不要过度加工肉类，否则会很硬。|
|近义词：overdo, overhandle, overmanipulate|
|反义词: underprocess, simplify|
|词性: 动词|
