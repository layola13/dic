artotype:/ˈɑːrtoʊtaɪp/| n.|艺术复制品；照相版画|A photographic print made using a collotype process, typically on a gelatin-coated plate.|This museum has a stunning collection of artotypes from the 19th century.  这家博物馆收藏了19世纪令人惊叹的照相版画。|Synonyms: collotype, photogravure, reproduction|Antonyms: original, master|noun
