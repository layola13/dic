obviator:/ˈɒbvieɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈɑːbvieɪtər/| n. |消除者，排除者|Something that obviates or removes a difficulty or need.|The invention of the car obviated the need for horse-drawn carriages. 汽车的发明消除了对马车运输的需求。|近义词：eliminator, remover, preventer|反义词:necessitator, creator|noun
