isolux:/ˈaɪsəlʌks/ |n.|等照度线；等照度曲线|A line or curve on a map or chart connecting points of equal illumination. |The isolux diagram shows the distribution of light intensity across the room. 等照度线图显示了整个房间的光强度分布。|The architect used isolux lines to determine the optimal placement of lighting fixtures. 建筑师使用等照度线来确定灯具的最佳位置。| synonym: isophote| antonym: N/A| noun
