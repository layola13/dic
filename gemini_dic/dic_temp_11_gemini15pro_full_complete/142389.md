orthotomic:/ˌɔːrθəˈtɒmɪk/| adj.|正交切割的，正交的|Relating to or involving orthotomy; intersecting or dividing at right angles.|The orthotomic circles of a triangle intersect at right angles.|近义词：perpendicular, orthogonal|反义词: oblique, skewed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *orthos* "straight, right" + *tomē* "cutting".
