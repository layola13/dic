licensors:/ˈlaɪsənsərz/
n.
授权者，许可人
People or organizations that grant licenses to others to use their intellectual property, such as patents, trademarks, or copyrights.
The licensors receive royalties for the use of their intellectual property. 授权商因其知识产权的使用而获得版税。
The licensors agreed to extend the licensing agreement for another five years. 许可方同意将许可协议再延长五年。
Synonyms: grantor, authorizer
Antonyms: licensee, recipient
noun
