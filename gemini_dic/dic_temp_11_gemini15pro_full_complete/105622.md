kinesthetically:/ˌkɪnɪsˈθɛtɪkli/ /ˌkɪnəsˈθɛtɪkli/
| adv. | 感觉运动地；动觉地 | Relating to or based on kinesthesia; involving or depending on the awareness of the position and movement of the parts of the body by means of sensory organs (proprioceptors) in the muscles and joints. | She learns kinesthetically, preferring hands-on activities. 她以动觉方式学习，更喜欢动手活动。|  kinesthetic, sensori-motor | N/A | adverb
