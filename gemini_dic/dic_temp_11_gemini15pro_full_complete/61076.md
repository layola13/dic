ellipsoids:/ɪˈlɪpsɔɪdz/ | n. |椭球体；椭圆体 |A three-dimensional shape resembling a stretched or compressed sphere.  All plane cross sections of an ellipsoid are ellipses or circles. |The Earth is not a perfect sphere, but rather an ellipsoid. 地球不是一个完美的球体，而是一个椭球体。|
|Synonyms: spheroid, oval |Antonyms: sphere, cube | Noun
