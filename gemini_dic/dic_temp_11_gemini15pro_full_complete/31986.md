chemosynthesis:/ˌkɛmoʊˈsɪnθəsɪs/| n.| 化合作用；化学合成|The process by which some organisms, such as certain bacteria, use chemical energy to produce carbohydrates.|Bacteria near hydrothermal vents use chemosynthesis to convert chemicals into energy. 深海热泉附近的细菌利用化合作用将化学物质转化为能量。| 近义词: biosynthesis| 反义词:  photosynthesis| noun
