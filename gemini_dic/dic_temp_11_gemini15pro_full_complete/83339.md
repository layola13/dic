gypsying:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪŋ/ | n. | 流浪，漂泊;  (像吉普赛人一样)四处游荡 | The act of traveling from place to place, often living in a caravan or similar temporary accommodation, like the traditional lifestyle of the Roma people. |  They spent the summer gypsying through the countryside. 他们在乡间流浪了一个夏天。 | 近义词: roaming, wandering, traveling, nomadism | 反义词: settling down, staying put | 词性: 名词
