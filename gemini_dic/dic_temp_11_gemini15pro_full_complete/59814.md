ectad:/ˈɛktæd/
n.
兴奋，狂喜
A state of extreme happiness or elation.
She was in a state of ectad after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。
近义词：elation, euphoria, rapture, bliss
反义词：despair, misery, sadness
词性：名词

Etymology: From Latin ēctase, from Greek ἔκτασις (éktasis), meaning "extension, stretching out," from ἐκτείνω (ekteínō), meaning "I stretch out," from ἐκ (ek), meaning "out," and τείνω (teínō), meaning "I stretch."
