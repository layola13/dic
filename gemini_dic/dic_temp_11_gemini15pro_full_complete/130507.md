nonaccidentals:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛntlz/ | n. |非意外事件；必需品；（音乐）基本音级|Things that are essential or unavoidable, especially in music referring to the notes of a key or mode.| The composer used only nonaccidentals in the first movement.  作曲家在第一乐章只使用了基本音级。|Synonyms: essentials, necessities, fundamentals (in music: diatonic notes)|Antonyms: accidentals (in music: chromatic notes)|noun


nonaccidental:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛntəl/ | adj. |非偶然的；必然的|Not occurring by chance; deliberate or inevitable.|The similarities between the two paintings are nonaccidental. 这两幅画的相似之处并非偶然。|Synonyms: deliberate, intentional, purposeful, inevitable|Antonyms: accidental, chance, random, incidental|adjective
