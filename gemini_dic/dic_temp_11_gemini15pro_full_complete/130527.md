nonaccrual:/ˌnɑːnəˈkruːəl/
n.|未累计收入，非应计项目|A loan on which interest is no longer accruing because the borrower is delinquent.  A loan is typically placed on nonaccrual status when it is 90 days past due.|The bank placed the loan on nonaccrual status after the borrower missed three consecutive payments.| 近义词:nonperforming, delinquent|反义词:performing, current|词性:名词

nonaccrual:/ˌnɑːnəˈkruːəl/
adj.|未累计的，非应计的|Relating to a loan on which interest is no longer accruing because the borrower is delinquent.|The nonaccrual loan portfolio has increased significantly this quarter.|近义词:nonperforming, delinquent|反义词:performing, current|词性:形容词
