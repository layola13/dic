A sound that is not a sibilant. | The word "flower" exhibits nonsibilancy.  花这个词展现了非咝音性。 |The phoneme /m/ is an example of nonsibilancy. 音素/m/是非咝音的一个例子。 | 近义词: non-sibilant (adjective form) | 反义词: sibilancy | 词性: noun
