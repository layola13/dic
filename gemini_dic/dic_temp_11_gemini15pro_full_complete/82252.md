groundedly:/ˈɡraʊndɪdli/
adjective
有根据地；理由充分地
Based on sound reasoning or evidence.
He groundedly argued his case, citing numerous precedents. 他有理有据地陈述了他的案例，引用了许多先例。
She groundedly rejected the proposal, explaining its flaws in detail. 她有充分理由地拒绝了该提议，并详细解释了它的缺陷。
近义词: justifiably, reasonably, validly, soundly
反义词: unreasonably, unjustifiably, baselessly
