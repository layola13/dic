nonimprovements:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpruːvmənts/ | n. | 非改进，未改进之处，没有改进的地方 |  Lack of improvements or things that do not constitute an improvement.  | The report highlighted the nonimprovements in the system despite the investment.  这份报告强调了尽管进行了投资，但系统并没有改进。| The consultant pointed out several nonimprovements in the company's workflow.  顾问指出了公司工作流程中的几个未改进之处。| 近义词: shortcomings, deficiencies, flaws | 反义词: improvements, enhancements, advancements | 词性: 名词


词源:

non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, meaning "not".
improvement (noun): From the verb "improve," which comes from Old French "emprouwer," meaning "to make a profit," later "to make better."
