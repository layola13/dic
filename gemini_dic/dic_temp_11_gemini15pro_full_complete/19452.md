bicuspidate:/baɪˈkʌspɪdeɪt/| adj.|二尖瓣的；有两尖的|Having two cusps or points, particularly referring to a tooth or the mitral valve of the heart.|The bicuspid valve regulates blood flow between the left atrium and left ventricle of the heart.|近义词：bicuspid, mitral (when referring to the heart valve)|反义词:tricuspid|词性:adjective

bicuspid:/baɪˈkʌspɪd/| n. adj.|二尖瓣；二尖牙；二尖的|A tooth with two cusps, typically a premolar; also refers to the mitral valve of the heart.|The dentist examined the patient's bicuspids.|近义词：bicuspidate, premolar (when referring to a tooth), mitral (when referring to the heart valve)|反义词:tricuspid|词性:noun, adjective
