nonbenevolently: /ˌnɒnbɪˈnevələntli/ | adv. | 非仁慈地，恶毒地 |  In a way that is not kind or well-meaning; unkindly or maliciously.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "benevolent" (kind, well-meaning) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). | The king nonbenevolently dismissed his loyal advisor. 国王恶毒地解雇了他忠诚的顾问。|  The dictator ruled nonbenevolently, suppressing dissent and punishing his opponents cruelly.  独裁者实行暴虐统治，压制异见，残酷地惩罚他的反对者。 | Synonyms: unkindly, maliciously, cruelly, harshly, callously | Antonyms: kindly, benevolently, compassionately, generously | 词性: 副词
