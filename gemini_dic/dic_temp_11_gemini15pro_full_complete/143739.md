outluster: /ˌaʊtˈlʌstər/
verb
胜过，使黯然失色
to surpass in brightness or splendor; eclipse
Her performance outlustered all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
近义词: outshine, eclipse, surpass, overshadow
反义词: pale in comparison, be overshadowed by
词性: verb
