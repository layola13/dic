babel:/ˈbeɪbl/ (US), /ˈbæbl/ (UK)| n.|巴别塔，混乱，嘈杂声|A confusion of sounds or voices, especially when several people are speaking at once; a scene of noise and confusion.|The construction site was a babel of shouts and the clang of machinery.  工地上一片嘈杂的喊叫声和机器的叮当声。|近义词：confusion, disorder, chaos, pandemonium|反义词：order, harmony, peace, quiet|词性:名词

Etymology: From the biblical story of the Tower of Babel (Genesis 11:1-9), where God confused the language of the people building the tower, preventing them from understanding each other and completing their project.  源自圣经故事中的巴别塔（创世记11:1-9），上帝在那里混淆了建造塔的人们的语言，使他们无法相互理解并完成他们的项目。
