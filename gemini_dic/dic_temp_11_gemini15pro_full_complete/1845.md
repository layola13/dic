acrolithic:/ˌækrəˈlɪθɪk/
adjective
用石头和木头混合制成的，(雕像)头部和四肢用石头制成，躯干用木头制成
Made of stone and wood, with the head and extremities of stone and the trunk of wood.
The acrolithic statue of Athena was a prominent feature of the temple. 雅典娜的木石结合雕像曾是神庙的一大特色。
composite, mixed-material
solid, monolithic
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akrólithos*, from *ákros* (at the extremity, outermost) + *líthos* (stone)
