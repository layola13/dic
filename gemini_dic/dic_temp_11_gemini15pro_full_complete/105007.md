kenorland:/ˈkiːnɔːrlænd/| n. |基诺兰大陆|A supercontinent that existed in the Neoarchean Era, from approximately 2.72 to 2.45 billion years ago.|Kenorland was one of Earth's earliest supercontinents. 基诺兰大陆是地球最早的超大陆之一。

Kenorland broke apart into smaller continents. 基诺兰大陆分裂成较小的陆块。|近义词：supercontinent, landmass|反义词: (No direct antonym for a geological landmass)|noun
