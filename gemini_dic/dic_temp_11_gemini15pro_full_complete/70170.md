fibonacci:/ˌfɪbəˈnɑːtʃi/| adj. n.|斐波那契，斐波那契数列的|Relating to or involving a sequence of numbers in which each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, typically starting with 0 and 1.  This sequence is often used in mathematics, computer science, and even appears in natural phenomena.|The arrangement of the florets on a sunflower follows a Fibonacci sequence.|近义词：None (highly specific term)|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun
