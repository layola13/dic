octanoate:/ˌɒkˈtæneɪt/ /ˌɑːkˈtæneɪt/
noun
辛酸盐; 辛酸酯
A salt or ester of octanoic acid.  Octanoic acid is a saturated fatty acid with eight carbon atoms.
Sodium octanoate is commonly used as a food additive.  辛酸钠通常用作食品添加剂。
Potassium octanoate is used in some soaps. 辛酸钾用于一些肥皂中。
