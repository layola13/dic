outwishing:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的愿望; 在许愿上超过
To surpass someone in the act of wishing, often implying a more fervent or powerful desire.
She outwished him for good fortune, her prayers echoing in the ancient temple. 她比他更渴望好运，她的祈祷声在古老的寺庙中回荡。
He outwished his rivals for the coveted prize, visualizing his victory with unwavering intensity. 他比他的对手更渴望得到梦寐以求的奖品，以坚定不移的强度想象着自己的胜利。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: being surpassed, losing to
