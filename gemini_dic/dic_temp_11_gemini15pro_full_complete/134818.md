nonmineralogical:/ˌnɒnˌmɪnərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非矿物学的
Not related to or consisting of minerals; not pertaining to mineralogy.
The study also considered nonmineralogical factors influencing rock strength. 这项研究还考虑了影响岩石强度的非矿物学因素。
Near synonyms: non-mineral, abiotic
Antonyms: mineralogical, mineral
词性: 形容词
