USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I can speak English. 我会说英语。
He can play the piano. 他会弹钢琴。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I can be reached by phone.  我可以用电话联系到。 (表示一种状态，can be doing 很少用)
He can be working on the project now. 他现在可能正在做这个项目。(表示推测)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been able to finish the report. 我已经能够完成报告了。(用be able to 代替 can)
She has been able to drive since she was 18. 她自从18岁起就会开车了。(用be able to 代替 can)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I haven't been able to reach him all day. 我一整天都联系不上他。(用be able to 代替 can)
They haven't been able to agree on a solution. 他们一直没能就解决方案达成一致。(用be able to 代替 can)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I could swim when I was five. 我五岁时就会游泳。
He could speak French fluently. 他能流利地说法语。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was able to finish the report yesterday. 我昨天能够完成报告。(用be able to 代替 could)
She was able to drive to work despite the snow. 尽管下雪，她还是能开车去上班。(用be able to 代替 could)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had been able to finish the report before the deadline. 我在截止日期前已经能够完成报告了。(用be able to 代替 could have)
She had been able to live on her own since she graduated. 她毕业后就一直能够独立生活。(用be able to 代替 could have)


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been able to work on the project for a few weeks before I got sick.  我生病前已经能够做这个项目几个星期了。(用be able to 代替 could have been)
They hadn't been able to find a solution for months.  几个月来他们一直没能找到解决方案。(用be able to 代替 could have been)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be able to help you tomorrow. 我明天可以帮助你。（用be able to 代替 will can）
He will be able to speak English fluently after a year of study.  经过一年的学习，他将能流利地说英语。（用be able to 代替 will can）

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be able to answer your questions tomorrow during the meeting. 明天开会的时候我可以回答你的问题。（用be able to 代替 will can）
He will be able to assist you with this project next week. 他下周可以协助你完成这个项目。（用be able to 代替 will can）

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have been able to finish the project by next month. 到下个月我将能够完成这个项目。（用be able to 代替 will can）
She will have been able to save enough money for a down payment by next year.  到明年，她将能够存够首付的钱。（用be able to 代替 will can）

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been able to live in this city for ten years. 到明年，我将能够在这个城市生活十年了。(用be able to 代替 will have been)
By the time she graduates, she will have been able to study English for five years. 到她毕业时，她将能够学习英语五年了。(用be able to 代替 will have been)
