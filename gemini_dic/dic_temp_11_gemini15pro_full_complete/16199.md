banner:/ˈbænər/| adj.|杰出的，出色的，一流的|Exceptionally good or successful.|It was a banner year for the company. 对于公司来说，这是业绩辉煌的一年。|synonyms: excellent, outstanding, exceptional, first-rate|antonyms: poor, bad, terrible|adjective
