comprehensives:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪvz/ | n. (usually plural) | 综合学校；综合考试 |  A comprehensive school is a state school that does not select its intake on the basis of academic achievement or aptitude.  A comprehensive exam covers a broad range of topics. |  The government is investing in comprehensives to improve educational standards. 政府正在投资综合学校以提高教育水平。 | My son just took his comprehensives at the end of the year. 我儿子年末的时候参加了综合考试。| synonyms: all-inclusive, complete, thorough | antonyms: partial, incomplete, limited | noun


comprehensible:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensəbl/ | adj. | 可理解的，易懂的 |  Able to be understood; intelligible. |  The instructions were clear and comprehensible. 说明清晰易懂。|  His explanation was comprehensible even to a beginner. 他的解释即使是初学者也能理解。 | synonyms: understandable, clear, lucid, intelligible | antonyms: incomprehensible, unclear, confusing, obscure | adjective
