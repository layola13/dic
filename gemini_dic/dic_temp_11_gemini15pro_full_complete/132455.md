nondeprecatingly:/ˌnɒndɪˈprɛkətɪŋli/ | adv. | 不贬低地，不谦虚地，自信地 |  In a manner that doesn't belittle or express disapproval of oneself or one's achievements. It implies a sense of confidence and acceptance of one's worth. | He spoke nondeprecatingly about his accomplishments, highlighting his hard work and dedication.  他毫不谦虚地谈论自己的成就，强调自己的努力和奉献精神。| Synonyms: confidently, assertively, proudly, unassumingly | Antonyms: self-deprecatingly, modestly, humbly | adverb
