erratic: /ɪˈrætɪk/ | adj. |古怪的；飘忽不定的；不稳定的；无规律的| Not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable. | The weather has been erratic lately. 最近天气很不稳定。| Synonyms: unpredictable, inconsistent, irregular, variable, capricious | Antonyms: predictable, consistent, regular, stable, steady | adjective
