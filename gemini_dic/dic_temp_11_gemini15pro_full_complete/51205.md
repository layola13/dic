destinist: /ˈdɛstɪnɪst/
n.|宿命论者|A person who believes in destiny; a fatalist.|He's a destinist and believes everything is predetermined.|近义词: fatalist, determinist|反义词: voluntarist, free-willist|词性: 名词

Etymology: From destiny + -ist. Destiny comes from Old French destinee, from Latin destinata, feminine past participle of destinare "to determine, establish," from de- "fully" + stinare "to set up, fix," related to stare "to stand."
