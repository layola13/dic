outmaneuver: /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/ /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/
verb
智胜，以策略取胜
To use clever tactics or superior skill to gain an advantage over someone, especially in a competitive situation.
The company outmaneuvered its rivals by offering a lower price.  这家公司通过提供更低的价格战胜了竞争对手。

The chess player skillfully outmaneuvered his opponent and won the match.  这位棋手巧妙地战胜了对手，赢得了比赛。

Synonyms: outwit, outsmart, outfox, circumvent
Antonyms: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted

词性: verb
