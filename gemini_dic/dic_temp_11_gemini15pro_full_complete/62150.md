enclose:/ɪnˈkloʊz/ |v.|包围，围住；附上，随信附上|To surround or close off on all sides; to include something with a letter or package.|Please enclose a check with your application. 请随申请书附上支票。|Synonyms: surround, encompass, include, attach|Antonyms: open, release, exclude|verb

enclose:/ɪnˈkloʊz/ |v.|封闭，封入|To shut in; to confine.|The garden is enclosed by a high wall. 花园被高墙围了起来。|Synonyms: confine, imprison, shut in|Antonyms: free, release, liberate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "enclore"  and ultimately from Latin "includere" (to shut in), from "in-" (in) + "claudere" (to close).
