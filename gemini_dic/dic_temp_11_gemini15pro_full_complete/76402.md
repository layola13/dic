gaga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| adj.|痴呆的；疯狂的；热衷的；为…而着迷的|Infatuated with or enthusiastic about someone or something.|例句：She's gaga over the new baby. 她对这个新生儿非常着迷。

例句：He's gone completely gaga over that girl. 他完全被那个女孩迷住了。

近义词：infatuated, obsessed, crazy, mad, enthusiastic|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性:形容词

gaga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| n.|痴呆；疯狂；废话|Senility; foolishness; nonsense.|例句：The old man was starting to talk gaga. 老人开始胡言乱语了。

例句：That's a load of gaga!  那是一派胡言！

近义词：nonsense, gibberish, drivel|反义词：sense, wisdom|词性:名词
