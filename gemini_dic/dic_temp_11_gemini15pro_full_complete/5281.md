allophones:/ˈæləfoʊnz/ (US) /ˈæləfəʊnz/ (UK)| n. |音位变体|One of two or more variants of the same phoneme. Allophones do not change the meaning of a word.  They are perceived as the same sound by native speakers.|The sounds /p/ in "pin" and "spin" are allophones; the first is aspirated, the second is not, but both are perceived as the same sound /p/.|近义词：variant, variation|反义词:phoneme|词性:noun
