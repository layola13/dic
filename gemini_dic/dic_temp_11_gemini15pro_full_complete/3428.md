african-trypanosomiasis:/ˌæfrɪkən trɪˌpænəˌsoʊmaɪˈeɪsɪs/ | n. | 非洲锥虫病 | A parasitic disease, also known as sleeping sickness, that is caused by trypanosomes and transmitted by the tsetse fly.  It affects humans and other animals. | Example: African trypanosomiasis is a major public health concern in sub-Saharan Africa. 非洲锥虫病是撒哈拉以南非洲地区的一个主要公共卫生问题。 | Example:  Early diagnosis and treatment of African trypanososomiasis are crucial to prevent serious neurological complications.  非洲锥虫病的早期诊断和治疗对于预防严重的神经系统并发症至关重要。 | Synonym: sleeping sickness | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a disease) | Noun
