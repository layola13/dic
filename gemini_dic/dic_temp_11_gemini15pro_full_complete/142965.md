outawe:/ɔːˈtɔː/ | vt. | 使敬畏；使惊叹 | To inspire awe in; to overcome with respect or reverence. | The sheer size of the mountains outawed her.  群山的巨大让她敬畏不已。| Synonyms: amaze, astound, impress, overawe | Antonyms: disappoint, bore | verb

outawed:/ɔːˈtɔːd/ | adj. | 充满敬畏的；感到惊叹的 | Filled with awe; overwhelmed with respect or reverence. |  He was outawed by the beauty of the cathedral. 他被大教堂的美丽所震撼。| Synonyms: awestruck, amazed, astonished | Antonyms: unimpressed, bored | adjective
