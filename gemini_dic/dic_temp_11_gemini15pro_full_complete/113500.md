lythontriptic:/ˌlɪθənˈtrɪptɪk/
adjective
溶石的，碎石的
Capable of dissolving or breaking down calculi (stones), especially in the kidneys or gallbladder.
This medication is a lythontriptic agent used to dissolve kidney stones. 这种药物是一种溶石剂，用于溶解肾结石。
Synonyms:  antilithic, litholytic
Antonyms:  lithogenic
词性: adjective
