exhibitionists:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪsts/
n.
暴露狂，爱出风头的人
A person who behaves in an extravagant way in order to attract attention.
The exhibitionist jumped on the table and started singing.  那个暴露狂跳到桌子上开始唱歌。
近义词:show-off, attention-seeker
反义词:introvert, wallflower
词性:noun

exhibitionist:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst/
adj.
爱出风头的
Relating to or characteristic of an exhibitionist.
His exhibitionist behavior embarrassed his family. 他爱出风头的行为让他的家人很尴尬。
近义词:ostentatious, flamboyant
反义词:modest, reserved
词性:adjective
