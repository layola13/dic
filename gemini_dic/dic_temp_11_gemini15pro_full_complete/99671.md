interlinears:/ˌɪntərˈlɪniərz/ | n. |行间注释；逐行对照的译文；对照课文|A text with interlinear glosses, particularly used in language learning.  It provides a translation or explanation of each word or phrase in the original language.|Example: The student used an interlinear Bible to help understand the Hebrew text. 学生使用了一本带逐行对照译文的圣经来帮助理解希伯来文本。

Example: Interlinears can be helpful for beginners learning a new language. 逐行对照的译文对初学者学习新语言很有帮助。|Synonyms: glosses, annotations, translations, cribs|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
