overcivilize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪvəlaɪz/
|v.|过度文明化；过分教化|To civilize to an excessive degree, often resulting in a loss of naturalness, spontaneity, or vigor.|Modern society has, in some ways, overcivilized us, making us less resilient to hardship.|近义词: refine excessively, overrefine|反义词: barbarize|词性:动词

overcivilized: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪvəlaɪzd/
|adj.|过度文明化的；过分教化的|Having been civilized to an excessive degree, often implying a loss of naturalness, spontaneity, or vigor.|The overcivilized urban dwellers found it difficult to adapt to the rugged wilderness.|近义词: overrefined, excessively refined|反义词: barbaric, primitive|词性:形容词
