catadioptrics:/ˌkætədaɪˈɒptrɪks/
|n.|反射光學；反光鏡|The study of optical systems that use both reflection and refraction, such as reflecting telescopes and cat's eyes.|Catadioptrics played a crucial role in the development of modern telescopes. 反射光學在現代望遠鏡的發展中發揮了至關重要的作用。|Synonyms: reflex optics|Antonyms: dioptrics|noun
