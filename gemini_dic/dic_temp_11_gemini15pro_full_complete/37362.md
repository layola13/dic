commensal:/kəˈmɛnsəl/ | n. adj.| 共生者，共生的 |An organism that lives with another organism without harming or benefiting it.  A commensal relationship.|例句：The barnacles are commensals on the whale's skin. 藤壶是鲸鱼皮肤上的共生生物。|近义词：symbiont, freeloader |反义词: parasite, pathogen |词性: noun, adjective

commensal:/kəˈmɛnsəl/ | adj.| 共生的 |Characterized by commensalism; living in a relationship in which one organism derives food or other benefits from another organism without hurting or helping it.|例句：The commensal bacteria in our gut do not harm us. 我们肠道中的共生细菌不会伤害我们。|近义词：symbiotic |反义词: parasitic, pathogenic |词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *commensalis*, from *com-* (together) + *mensa* (table).  Literally "eating at the same table."
