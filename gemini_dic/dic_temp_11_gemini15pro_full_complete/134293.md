noninhibitory:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɪbɪtəri/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈhɪbətɔːri/
adjective
非抑制性的
Not inhibitory; that does not inhibit (a process or function).
The drug has a noninhibitory effect on the enzyme.  这种药物对酶有非抑制性作用。
Noninhibitory signals promote growth and development. 非抑制性信号促进生长发育。
Synonyms:  stimulatory, promoting, activating, excitatory
Antonyms: inhibitory, repressive, suppressive
词性: adjective
