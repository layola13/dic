function's:/ˈfʌŋkʃənz/| n.|功能；职责；函数；仪式|The function's primary purpose is to filter data.  这个函数的主要目的是过滤数据。|例句：The liver's function is to cleanse the blood. 肝脏的功能是净化血液。

The social function's purpose was to raise money for charity.  这次社交活动是为了给慈善机构筹款。|近义词：purpose, role, duty, responsibility, ceremony|反义词:dysfunction|词性:名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French fonction, from Latin functio(n-), from fungi ‘perform’.
