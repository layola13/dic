copps:/kɑːps/ | n. |警察 (非正式，通常带有轻蔑意味) | Slang, often derogatory, for police officers.  Derived from "copper," itself likely originating from the copper buttons on early police uniforms.|Example: "The copps were all over the place after the riot."  暴乱后到处都是警察。|近义词：police, cops, law enforcement, the fuzz (slang) |反义词: criminals, lawbreakers | noun
