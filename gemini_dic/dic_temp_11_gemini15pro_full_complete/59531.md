eat your words:/ˌiːt jər ˈwɜːrdz/
verb phrase|收回前言；承认错误|To admit that something you said was wrong.|例句：He had to eat his words when the project proved to be a success.  当项目证明成功时，他不得不收回前言。|例句：She boasted that she would win, but she had to eat her words after losing the race. 她吹嘘自己会赢，但在输掉比赛后不得不承认错误。|近义词：retract, apologize, back down|反义词：insist, persist, maintain|词性：动词短语
