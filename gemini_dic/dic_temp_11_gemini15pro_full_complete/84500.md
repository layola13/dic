halwa:/ˈhɑːlvə/ /ˈhæl və/| n. |哈尔瓦，一种甜食|A dense, sweet confection made from flour, butter, sugar, and often nuts or spices.  It is popular in many parts of the Middle East, South Asia, and Eastern Europe.| My grandmother makes delicious halwa during festivals.  我祖母在节日期间会做美味的哈尔瓦。

She brought a box of semolina halwa from India. 她从印度带了一盒粗面粉哈尔瓦。 |近义词：sweet, dessert, confection|反义词: None|词性:noun

From Arabic حَلْوَىٰ (ḥalwā), from حُلْو (ḥulw), meaning "sweet".
