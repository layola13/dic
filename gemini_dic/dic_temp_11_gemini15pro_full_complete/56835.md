dornick:/ˈdɔːrnɪk/ | n. | 多尼克粗麻布；一种粗麻布 | A coarse linen cloth, originally made in Dornick (now Tournai, Belgium). |  She used a dornick cloth to cover the table. 她用一块多尼克粗麻布盖住了桌子。 | The dornick was rough but durable. 这块粗麻布很粗糙但很耐用。 | 近义词: linen, burlap, hessian | 反义词: silk, satin | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle Dutch 𝑑𝑜𝑜𝑟𝑛𝑖𝑗𝑘, from 𝐷𝑜𝑜𝑟𝑛𝑖𝑗𝑘, the former name of Tournai, Belgium.
