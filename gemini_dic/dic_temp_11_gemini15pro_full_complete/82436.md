gruff:/ɡrʌf/| adj.|粗暴的，生硬的，（声音）粗哑的|Rough or surly in manner or speech; (of a voice) low and harsh.|He answered in a gruff voice. 他用粗哑的声音回答。|Synonyms: surly, brusque, curt, abrupt, grumpy, harsh, hoarse|Antonyms: gentle, kind, polite, courteous, smooth, soft|Etymology:  Probably of imitative origin.|
