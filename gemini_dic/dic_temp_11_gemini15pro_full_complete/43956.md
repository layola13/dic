cribrose:/ˈkrɪbroʊs/ | adj. | 多孔的，篩狀的 |  Resembling a sieve; perforated with small holes. | The cribrose plate of the echinoderm allows water to circulate.  这种棘皮动物的多孔板允许水循环。 | perforated, porous, sieve-like | solid, impermeable, dense | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *cribrōsus*, from *cribrum* ("sieve").
