jockey:/ˈdʒɒki/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːki/ (US)| n. v.|骑师；驾驶员；（用不正当手段）谋取；|A person who rides horses in horse races, especially as a profession.  Also, to maneuver or manipulate something skillfully or strategically.|例句：The jockey urged his horse towards the finish line.  骑师催促他的马冲向终点线。|近义词：rider, horseman, pilot, driver|反义词:passenger|词性:noun, verb

jockey:/ˈdʒɒki/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːki/ (US)| v.|（用不正当手段）谋取；驾驶；|To maneuver or manipulate something skillfully or strategically, often to gain an advantage.|例句：He jockeyed for position in the crowded elevator. 他在拥挤的电梯里抢占位置。|近义词：maneuver, manipulate, scheme, contrive|反义词:yield, submit|词性:verb
