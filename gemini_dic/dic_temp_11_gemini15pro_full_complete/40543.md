controversiality:/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃiˈælɪti/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃiˈælɪti/
n.|争议性，引起争论的性质|The controversiality of the new law has led to widespread protests. 这项新法律的争议性引发了广泛的抗议。| English Explanation: The state or quality of being controversial; the capacity to provoke disagreement or debate. |例句：The controversiality of the topic made it difficult to discuss calmly.  这个话题的争议性使得很难平静地讨论。|近义词：disputability, contentiousness|反义词：agreement, harmony|词性: noun

controversial:/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəl/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəl/
adj.|有争议的，引起争论的| Abortion is a highly controversial issue. 堕胎是一个极具争议的话题。 | English Explanation: Giving rise or likely to give rise to public disagreement. | 例句：His controversial remarks sparked a heated debate. 他备受争议的言论引发了一场激烈的辩论。| 近义词：disputed, debatable|反义词：uncontroversial, undisputed| 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *controversia, from Latin controversus (“turned against, disputed”), from contra (“against”) + versus, perfect passive participle of vertere (“to turn”).
