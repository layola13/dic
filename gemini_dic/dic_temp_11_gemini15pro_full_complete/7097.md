anesthetization:/ˌænɪsθətɪˈzeɪʃən/ /əˌnɛsθətɪˈzeɪʃn/
noun
麻醉；麻醉状态
The process of inducing anesthesia. The state of being anesthetized.
The patient underwent anesthetization before the surgery. 病人在手术前进行了麻醉。
The anesthetization was successful, and the patient felt no pain during the procedure. 麻醉成功了，病人在手术过程中没有感到疼痛。
synonyms: narcosis, numbness
antonyms: sensation, consciousness
