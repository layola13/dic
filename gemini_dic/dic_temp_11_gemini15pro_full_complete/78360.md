gestation:/dʒɛˈsteɪʃən/
n.|怀孕期，妊娠期；酝酿期|The period of development of a fetus in the womb from conception to birth; the development of something over a period of time.|The horse's gestation period is about 11 months. 马的妊娠期大约是11个月。|Synonyms: pregnancy, incubation, development|Antonyms:  birth, delivery|noun
