lixiviation:/ˌlɪksɪˈveɪʃən/| n.| 沥滤，浸析|The process of separating soluble substances from a solid by dissolving them in a liquid.  The separation of soluble components from a mixture by percolating a solvent through it.|The lixiviation of gold from the ore was a complex process. 从矿石中浸析金是一个复杂的过程。|Synonyms: leaching, percolation, extraction|Antonyms: precipitation, deposition|noun
