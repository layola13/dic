dedesignate:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/
verb
取消指定；撤销指定
To remove the official designation from something or someone.
The area was dedesignated as a national park.  该地区被取消了国家公园的称号。
The committee voted to dedesignate him as chairman. 委员会投票决定撤销他的主席职务。
近义词: revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw
反义词: designate, appoint, assign
词性: verb

词源:
From de- + designate.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse." "Designate" comes from the Latin word "designare," meaning "to mark out."
