noncoherence:/nɒnkəʊˈhɪərəns/ (British) /ˌnɑːnkoʊˈhɪrəns/ (American)| n. |不连贯，不一致|The state or quality of being noncoherent; lack of coherence or cohesion.  A lack of logical or meaningful connection between ideas or elements.|The noncoherence of his speech made it difficult to understand his point. 他的讲话不连贯，很难理解他的意思。|Synonyms: incoherence, disunity, disjointedness, inconsistency|Antonyms: coherence, cohesion, unity, consistency|noun
