fuze:/fjuːz/ | n. v. |引信，导火索；熔断器；保险丝 |A cord of readily combustible material that is used to ignite an explosive charge; a device, such as a length of wire, that melts and interrupts an electric circuit when overloaded.| The bomb's fuze was faulty. 炸弹的引信坏了。 |Light the fuze and run! 点燃导火索快跑！| synonyms: fuse, detonator, primer | antonyms: extinguisher | noun, verb

fuze:/fjuːz/ | v. |熔合；融合；合并 |To blend or become blended together by or as if by melting; to equip with a fuze.| The two companies fuzed to become a giant corporation. 这两家公司合并成了一家巨型企业。 |  They fuzed the metal parts together. 他们把金属部件熔合在一起。| synonyms: merge, combine, blend | antonyms: separate, divide | verb
