composing:/kəmˈpəʊzɪŋ/ (UK) /kəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ (US)| v. |创作;组成;使平静|The act of creating or writing something, especially music or literature; the act of forming something by putting parts together; calming oneself.|He spent the afternoon composing a letter to his parents. 他用了一下午的时间给父母写信。|Synonyms: writing, creating, forming, constructing, calming, soothing|Antonyms: destroying, dismantling, agitating, disturbing|Verb

composing:/kəmˈpəʊzɪŋ/ (UK) /kəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |组成的;构成的;使平静的|Constituting or forming part of something; having a calming or soothing effect.|The composing elements of the painting are color, line, and form. 这幅画的构成元素是色彩、线条和形式。|Synonyms: constituent, component, calming, soothing|Antonyms: separate, individual, agitating, disturbing|Adjective
