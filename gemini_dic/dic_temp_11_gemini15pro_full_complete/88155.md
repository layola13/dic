hilled:/hɪld/
adjective
多山的; 丘陵起伏的
covered with hills; having many hills
例句：The hilled countryside was a beautiful sight.  丘陵起伏的乡村景色很美。

例句：They hiked through the hilled terrain. 他们徒步穿越了丘陵地带。
近义词：mountainous, undulating, rolling
反义词:flat, level
词性:adjective

词源: From hill + -ed,  Old English *hyll + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulni- (hill, elevation).
