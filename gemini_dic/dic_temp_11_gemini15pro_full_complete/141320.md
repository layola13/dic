opencast: /ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst/ (UK) /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ (US)
adjective, noun
露天开采的，露天矿
(adjective) mined in the open air, not underground
(noun) an opencast mine
Opencast mining can have a significant environmental impact. 露天开采会对环境产生重大影响。
The opencast was a scar on the landscape.  这个露天矿是景观上的一个伤疤。
近义词: surface, strip
反义词: underground, deep
词性: adjective, noun


opencast: /ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst/ (UK) /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ (US)
verb
露天开采
to mine (coal, etc.) by opencast methods
They plan to opencast the area for coal. 他们计划在该地区露天开采煤炭。
The company opencasted the entire valley.  这家公司露天开采了整个山谷。
近义词: surface mine, strip mine
反义词: deep mine
词性: verb
