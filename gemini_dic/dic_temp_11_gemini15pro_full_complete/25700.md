bunking:/ˈbʌŋkɪŋ/
verb|逃课，躲避，逃避|The act of avoiding something, particularly school or work, often by hiding.|He was caught bunking off school and got detention.|近义词:skipping, dodging, avoiding|反义词:attending, facing|词性:动名词 (Gerund)

词源:  From the verb "bunk," which originated in the 19th century as student slang.  It likely comes from "bunk off," meaning to sleep in a bunk instead of attending class.
