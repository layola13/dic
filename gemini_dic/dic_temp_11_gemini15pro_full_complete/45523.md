curfew: /ˈkɜːrfjuː/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrfjuː/ (US)| n.|宵禁; 晚钟; 规定熄灯时间|A regulation requiring people to remain indoors between specified hours, typically at night.|The city is under curfew.  这座城市处于宵禁状态。|例句：The curfew was lifted after a week.  宵禁一周后解除了。|近义词：restriction, prohibition|反义词：freedom, liberty|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Old French *cuevrefeu, literally "cover fire," from cuevre (Modern French couvre), imperative of cuevrir "to cover," and feu "fire."  This referred to the medieval practice of covering fires at night to prevent the spread of fire.
