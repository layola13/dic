overdeepening:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpənɪŋ/
n.
过度深化；过度挖掘
The process by which a glacier erodes a valley floor below sea level, creating a basin that can become a lake after the glacier retreats.
The overdeepening of the valley resulted in a fjord after the ice age.  这个山谷的过度深化在冰河时代之后形成了一个峡湾。
近义词: overexcavation
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shallowing or aggradation)
词性: Noun

overdeepen:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpən/
v.
过度深化；过度挖掘
To erode (a valley floor, for example) to an excessive depth.
Glaciers can overdeepen valleys, creating basins that fill with water. 冰川会过度深化山谷，形成充满水的盆地。
近义词: overexcavate
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shallow or aggrade)
词性: Verb

词源： "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "deepen" (verb meaning "to make or become deep").
