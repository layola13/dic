overdefensive:/ˌoʊvərdiˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 过于防御的，过度防卫的 | Excessively defensive; reacting too strongly to perceived threats or criticism.  Characterized by excessive sensitivity to criticism and a tendency to perceive attacks where none are intended. | He became overdefensive when asked about his past. 当被问及他的过去时，他变得过于防卫。|  She's so overdefensive about her work that she takes any suggestion as a personal attack. 她对自己的工作过于防卫，以至于把任何建议都视为针对她个人的攻击。 | Near Synonyms: insecure, sensitive, touchy, thin-skinned | Antonyms: confident, secure, thick-skinned | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + defensive.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Defensive" comes from the Latin word "defendere," meaning "to ward off, protect."
