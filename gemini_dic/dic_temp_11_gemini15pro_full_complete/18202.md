belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| v. n.|保护，固定，停止，阻止|To secure a climber with a rope; to stop or interrupt.|The climber was belayed by his partner. 攀登者被他的同伴用绳索保护着。|近义词：secure, fasten, anchor, stop, halt|反义词:release, free|verb, noun

belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| n.|保护点，固定点|A secure point to which a climber's rope is attached.|The belay was strong and reliable. 保护点坚固可靠。|近义词：anchor, fastening|反义词: N/A|noun
