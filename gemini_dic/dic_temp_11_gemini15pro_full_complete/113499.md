lythonthriptic:/ˌlɪθɒnˈθrɪptɪk/ | adj. | 碎石的；溶石的 |  Relating to or causing the dissolving of stones, especially calculi (stones formed in the body, such as kidney stones or gallstones). Formed from "litho-" (stone) + "-thriptic" (crushing or dissolving). |  The lythonthriptic medication helped dissolve the kidney stones. 这种溶石药物帮助溶解了肾结石。 | 近义词: litholytic, antilithic | 反义词: lithogenic | 词性: 形容词
