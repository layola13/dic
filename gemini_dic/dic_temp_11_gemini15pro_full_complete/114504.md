maladjusted:/ˌmæləˈdʒʌstɪd/
adjective
失调的，适应不良的
Failing to cope with the demands of society or of a particular situation.
例句：He was a maladjusted child who had difficulty making friends. 他是一个适应不良的孩子，很难交朋友。

例句：The maladjusted teenager acted out in class.  这个适应不良的青少年在课堂上捣乱。

近义词：disturbed, troubled, unstable, antisocial, dysfunctional
反义词：well-adjusted, stable, adapted
词性: adjective

maladjusted:/ˌmæləˈdʒʌstɪd/
词源:  From mal- (badly) + adjusted (adapted).
