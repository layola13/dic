democrat-wagons:/ˈdɛməˌkræt ˌwæɡənz/ | n. |民主黨支持者的汽車; 載著民主黨支持者去投票的車|  A somewhat derogatory term referring to vehicles, often minivans or station wagons, stereotypically associated with Democratic voters.  It implies a suburban, family-oriented lifestyle often associated with the Democratic Party. | Example: The parking lot was full of democrat-wagons, suggesting a strong Democratic turnout. 停车场停满了民主党支持者的车，表明民主党投票率很高。

Example:  He joked about trading in his sports car for a democrat-wagon now that he had a family.  他开玩笑说，既然现在成家了，要把他的跑车换成一辆家用旅行车。| Near Synonyms:  Volvos (sometimes used humorously/derogatively in a similar context),  Suburban assault vehicles (more humorous and less political), family cars | Antonyms:  None (the term is more about a stereotype than a specific vehicle type) | Noun
