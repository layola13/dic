hairlines:/ˈhɛəlaɪnz/| n.|发际线；极细的线条；细微的差别|very fine lines; the edge formed by the hair on a person's forehead|例句：His hairlines are receding. 他的发际线正在后退。

例句：The map showed the coastlines and rivers in hairlines. 地图用极细的线条标示了海岸线和河流。|近义词：edges, outlines, contours|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


hairline:/ˈhɛəlaɪn/| adj.|极细的；细微的|extremely thin or fine|例句：A hairline fracture in his leg sidelined him for the season.  他腿上的细微骨折让他缺席了整个赛季。

例句：There's a hairline crack in the vase. 花瓶上有一条细微的裂纹。|近义词：thin, fine, slight|反义词:thick, wide|词性:adjective


词源:  "hairline" comes from "hair" + "line". It literally refers to the line formed by the hair, especially on the forehead.  The figurative meaning of "a very fine line" or "slight difference" developed from this literal sense.
