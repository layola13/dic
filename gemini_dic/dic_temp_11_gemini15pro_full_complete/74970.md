fricasseeing:/ˈfrɪkəsiːɪŋ/ | v. | 油炸，煎炒（尤指鸡肉或小牛肉）|  cooking (especially chicken or veal) by frying and then stewing in a sauce. |  She was fricasseeing some chicken for dinner. 她正在煎炒一些鸡肉做晚餐。| synonyms: frying, sauteing, braising | antonyms: boiling, steaming | verb

fricassee:/ˈfrɪkəsiː/ | n. | 油炸菜肴（尤指鸡肉或小牛肉）| a dish of chicken or other meat stewed in a white sauce |  Chicken fricassee is one of my favorite dishes. 鸡肉油炸菜是我最喜欢的菜肴之一。| synonyms: stew, ragout | antonyms: roast, grill | noun
