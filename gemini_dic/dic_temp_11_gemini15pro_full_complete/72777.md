fohns:/fɜːnz/ | n. |焚风，暖风 | A warm dry wind developing on the leeward side of a mountain range. | The fohns melted the snow quickly. 焚风迅速融化了雪。| The fohns dried out the crops. 焚风使庄稼干枯了。| 近义词: chinook, warm wind | 反义词: cold wind | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Föhn*, from Latin *favonius* (a west wind).
