debriefing:/diːˈbriːfɪŋ/
n.|汇报; 汇报会; 任务报告|An official report of a mission or project. A meeting for giving and receiving information after a mission or project. |The pilot underwent a debriefing after the flight. 飞行员在飞行后接受了汇报。|synonyms: report, briefing, review, assessment|antonyms: briefing (can be considered an antonym in some contexts as it precedes the action, while debriefing follows)
词性:名词
