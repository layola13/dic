The child's fawnier behavior embarrassed his parents.  孩子幼稚的行为让他的父母很尴尬。 | 近义词：younger, more naive, paler, lighter | 反义词：older, more mature, darker | 词性:形容词

词源: From "fawn" (a young deer) + "-er" (comparative suffix).  最终源自拉丁语 "fetus" (offspring).
