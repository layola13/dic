nonthermally:/nɒnˈθɜːməli/
adverb
非热地；非热力地
Relating to or denoting a process that does not involve heat or changes in temperature.
The food was nonthermally processed to preserve its nutrients.  这种食物经过非热加工以保留其营养成分。
The material was nonthermally modified using high pressure.  这种材料使用高压进行了非热改性。
Synonym: athermally
Antonym: thermally
词性: 副词
