elongating:/ɪˈlɔːŋɡeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|延长，拉长|The process of making something longer. |例句：The artist is elongating the neck in the portrait. 艺术家正在拉长肖像画中人物的脖子.|近义词：lengthening, extending, stretching, protracting|反义词: shortening, contracting, compressing|Verb

elongating:/ɪˈlɔːŋɡeɪtɪŋ/
|adj.|伸长的，延长的|Used to describe something that is being made longer or that has the effect of making something longer. |例句：The elongating shadows of the late afternoon created a dramatic effect. 傍晚拉长的影子营造出一种戏剧性的效果.|近义词：lengthening, extending, stretching|反义词: shortening, contracting|Adjective
