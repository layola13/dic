licenseless:/ˈlaɪsənsləs/
adjective
无执照的，未经许可的
without a license; not authorized.
He was caught driving a licenseless vehicle. 他被抓到驾驶无照车辆。
The licenseless vendors were asked to leave the market.  无照商贩被要求离开市场。
近义词:unauthorized, illegal, unlawful, illicit, illegitimate
反义词:licensed, authorized, legal, lawful, legitimate
词性:adjective

词源:
From license + -less.  "license" 源自法语 "licence," 最终源自拉丁语 "licentia"，意思是“自由，许可”。 后缀 "-less" 表示“没有，缺乏”。
