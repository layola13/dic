griming:/ˈɡraɪmɪŋ/| v. |使污秽，使肮脏|Making something dirty or covered with grime.|例句：The constant rain was griming the windows of the bus.  不断下雨使公共汽车的窗户变得很脏。|例句：Years of neglect were griming the once-grand facade of the building.  多年的疏忽使曾经宏伟的建筑正面变得污秽不堪。|近义词：dirtying, soiling, staining, besmirching|反义词：cleaning, cleansing, purifying|词性:动词(present participle of grime)

grime:/ɡraɪm/| n. |污垢，尘垢|Dirt ingrained on the surface of something, especially skin, clothes, or buildings.|例句：The mechanic's hands were covered in grime. 技工的手上沾满了油污。|例句：There was a layer of grime on the kitchen floor. 厨房地板上有一层污垢。|近义词：dirt, filth, soot, muck|反义词：cleanliness|词性:名词
