antisensuousness:/ˌæntiˈsɛnʃuːəsnɪs/
n.
反感官性; 禁欲主义
The quality or state of being opposed to or indifferent to sensory stimulation or pleasure.
His antisensuousness was reflected in his plain clothing and austere lifestyle. 他的反感官性体现在他朴素的衣着和简朴的生活方式上。
Near Synonyms: asceticism, austerity, puritanism, self-denial
Antonyms: sensuality, hedonism, indulgence
noun
