chilblain:/ˈtʃɪlbleɪn/ (US), /ˈtʃɪlbleɪn/ (UK)| n. |冻疮|A painful, itchy swelling on the fingers, toes, or ears, caused by repeated exposure to cold but not freezing temperatures.|  I got chilblains on my toes last winter from wearing thin socks. 去年冬天我因为穿了薄袜子，脚趾上长了冻疮。|Synonyms: pernio, frostbite (in severe cases)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one might consider "warmth" or "healthy circulation")|Noun
