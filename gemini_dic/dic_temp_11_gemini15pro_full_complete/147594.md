overplanning:/ˌoʊvərˈplænɪŋ/
noun
过度计划；过分计划
The act of planning something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or causing stress.
He was paralyzed by overplanning and never actually started the project. 他因过度计划而停滞不前，从未真正启动过这个项目。
overthinking, micromanaging
underplanning, improvising
n.
