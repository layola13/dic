overexpansions:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænʃənz/
|n.|过度扩张；过度膨胀|Excessive expansions, often referring to economic or business growth beyond a sustainable level.  The act of expanding too much or too quickly.|The company's overexpansions into new markets led to its downfall.  公司过度扩张到新市场导致了它的垮台。|overextensions, overgrowth|contraction, downsizing|noun
