contrarily:/kənˈtrɛərɪli/ | adv. | 相反地，反之 | In a contrary manner; opposite to what was expected or previously stated. |  She expected him to be angry, but, contrarily, he was quite calm. 她预料他会生气，但相反，他非常平静。| Contrarily to expectations, the team lost the game. 与预期相反，球队输掉了比赛。 |  conversely, inversely, on the contrary, on the other hand | similarly, likewise | adverb
