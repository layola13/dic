heat:/hiːt/| n. v.|热，热度，加热|The heat from the fire warmed the room. 火的热量温暖了房间。|例句：The summer heat was intense. 夏天的酷暑十分难耐。|近义词：warmth, temperature|反义词:cold, coolness|词性:noun

heat:/hiːt/| v.|加热，使激动|Please heat the oven to 350 degrees.  请将烤箱加热到350度。|例句：The argument heated up quickly. 争论迅速升温。|近义词：warm, cook|反义词:cool, chill|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hætu (attested only in derivatives), from Proto-Germanic *haitō (source also of Old Saxon heta, Old Frisian hēte, Middle Dutch hiete, Dutch hitte, Old High German heizi, German Hitze, Old Norse hiti), from Proto-Indo-European *kay- (“to glow, burn”).
