out-trade:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪd/
verb
胜过；在贸易上超过
to surpass in trading; to be more successful in trading than another
China has out-traded the US in many sectors. 中国在许多领域都超过了美国。
surpass, beat, outdo
be out-traded by, lose to
v.
