cathartin:/kəˈθɑːrtɪn/| n.|导泻剂，泻药|A cathartic is a substance that accelerates defecation. It is medically used to treat constipation.|The doctor prescribed a cathartic to relieve the patient's constipation. 医生给病人开了泻药以缓解便秘。|Synonyms: laxative, purgative, aperient|Antonyms: antidiarrheal, constipating|Noun

cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/| adj.|宣泄的，净化心灵的|Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; causing catharsis.|Watching a sad movie can be a cathartic experience. 看一部悲伤的电影可以是一种情感宣泄的体验。|Synonyms: cleansing, purifying, releasing|Antonyms: repressive, suppressive|Adjective
