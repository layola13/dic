gratitude:/ˈɡrætɪtjuːd/ /ˈɡrætɪtuːd/| n.|感激，感谢|the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation for and to return kindness.|I felt a deep gratitude for his help. 我对他提供的帮助深表感激。|Synonyms: thankfulness, appreciation, gratefulness|Antonyms: ingratitude, ungratefulness|noun
