missioner:/ˈmɪʃənər/| n. |传教士；使节；特使|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country.  A person sent on a specific mission, often of a diplomatic or political nature.|例句：The missionaries traveled to remote villages to spread their faith.  这些传教士前往偏远村庄传播他们的信仰。

例句：The government sent a missioner to negotiate a peace treaty. 政府派了一名特使去谈判和平条约。|近义词：apostle, envoy, delegate, representative|反义词:None|词性:Noun
