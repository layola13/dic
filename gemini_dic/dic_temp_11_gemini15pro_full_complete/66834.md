expatiations: /ɪkˌspeɪʃiˈeɪʃənz/ /ekˌspeɪʃiˈeɪʃənz/
| n. | 详细阐述；详尽的解释；冗长的讨论 | A lengthy discussion of a subject, often used in a disapproving way to suggest that it is unnecessarily long or tedious. |  He launched into lengthy expatiations on his favorite subject, the history of model trains. 他开始冗长地阐述他最喜欢的主题——火车模型的历史。| synonyms:  discourses, elaborations, expositions | antonyms: summaries, abridgments, condensations | noun

expatiate: /ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/ /ekˈspeɪʃieɪt/
| v. | 详述；细说 | To speak or write at length or in detail. | The professor expatiated on the importance of the upcoming exam. 教授详细说明了即将到来的考试的重要性。 | synonyms: elaborate, discourse, expand | antonyms: summarize, condense, abbreviate | verb
