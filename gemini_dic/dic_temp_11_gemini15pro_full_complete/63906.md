epiphany:/ɪˈpɪfəni/| n.|顿悟；突然的理解；显现（尤指神的显现）|A moment of sudden and great revelation or realization.|例句：She had an epiphany that she was wasting her life. 她顿悟到自己一直在浪费生命。

例句：The doctor's diagnosis was an epiphany for the patient, who finally understood the cause of his illness.  医生的诊断对病人来说是一个顿悟，他终于明白了自己病因。|近义词：realization, insight, revelation, inspiration|反义词:confusion, misunderstanding|词性:noun
