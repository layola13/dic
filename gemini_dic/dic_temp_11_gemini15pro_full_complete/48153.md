decolorize:/diːˈkʌləraɪz/
v.
使脱色；漂白
To remove the color from something.
The chemist decolorized the solution using activated charcoal. 化学家用活性炭使溶液脱色。

近义词:bleach, whiten, fade
反义词:color, dye, stain
词性:动词

词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + color + -ize (suffix forming verbs)
