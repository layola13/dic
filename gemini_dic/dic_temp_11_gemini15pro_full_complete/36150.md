coexists:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪsts/
verb
共存，共存
exists at the same time or in the same place as something else.
Polar bears and seals coexist in the Arctic. 北极熊和海豹共存于北极。
近义词：co-occur, exist together
反义词：conflict, clash
词性：v.
