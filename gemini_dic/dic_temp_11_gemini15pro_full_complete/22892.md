botchy:/ˈbɒtʃi/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtʃi/ (US)| adj.|拙劣的；粗制滥造的；笨手笨脚的|badly or clumsily done; showing a lack of skill or care.|The paint job was botchy and uneven. 油漆活儿做得拙劣不均匀。|synonyms: bungled, clumsy, messy, sloppy, inept, awkward|antonyms: skillful, expert, proficient, adept, masterful|adjective

botchy:/ˈbɒtʃi/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtʃi/ (US)| adj.|（尤指手术）失败的| (especially of surgery) unsuccessful.|The operation was botchy and resulted in complications. 手术失败了，导致了并发症。|synonyms: failed, unsuccessful, flawed, imperfect|antonyms: successful, perfect, flawless|adjective
