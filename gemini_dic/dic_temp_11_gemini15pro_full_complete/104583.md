kanjis:/ˈkɑːndʒiːz/| n. |汉字|Japanese characters used in writing Japanese.  Derived from Chinese characters (Hanzi).|例句：She is studying kanjis. 她正在学习汉字。

例句：He can read and write over 2000 kanjis. 他能读写超过2000个汉字。|近义词：characters, ideographs|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "kana" which is another Japanese writing system) |词性: Noun
