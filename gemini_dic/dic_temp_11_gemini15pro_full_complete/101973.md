isocalorically:/ˌaɪsoʊkəˈlɔːrɪkli/ | adv. | 等热量地，同卡路里地 |  Containing or supplied with the same number of calories.  | She replaced the sugary snacks in her diet isocalorically with healthier options. 她用更健康的选择等热量地取代了饮食中的含糖零食。|  Example: He isocalorically substituted the white bread with whole wheat bread. 他用全麦面包等热量地替换了白面包。 | Synonyms:  equi-caloric, with the same caloric value | Antonyms:  with different caloric values | part of speech: adverb
