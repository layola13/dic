nasolachrymal:/ˌneɪzoʊˈlækrɪməl/ | adj. | 鼻泪管的 | Relating to the nose and the lacrimal apparatus (tear ducts). | The nasolachrymal duct carries tears from the eye to the nose. 鼻泪管将眼泪从眼睛输送到鼻子。| The doctor examined the patient's nasolachrymal system. 医生检查了病人的鼻泪系统。 |  Synonyms: tear, ocular, ophthalmic | Antonyms: None | adjective

nasolachrymal:/ˌneɪzoʊˈlækrɪməl/ | n. | 鼻泪管 | The duct that carries tears from the lacrimal sac to the nasal cavity. |  Blockage of the nasolachrymal duct can cause watery eyes. 鼻泪管阻塞会导致眼睛流泪。| The surgeon repaired the damaged nasolachrymal duct. 外科医生修复了受损的鼻泪管。| Synonyms: tear duct | Antonyms: None | noun


Etymology:
From naso- (combining form meaning "nose") + lacrimal (relating to tears).  Ultimately from Latin nasus "nose" and lacrima "tear".
