archaeologically:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
考古学地，从考古学的角度
in a way that relates to the study of cultures of the past, or to the scientific study of the remains of past cultures
例句：The site was archaeologically significant. 该遗址在考古学上意义重大。

例句：The findings were interpreted archaeologically. 这些发现是从考古学角度进行解读的。

近义词：archaeologically speaking, from an archaeological standpoint
反义词: N/A
词性: adverb
