heterogeneousnesses:/ˌhɛtərəʊdʒɪˈniːɪtnəsɪz/
noun
异质性，多样性
The state or quality of being heterogeneous; diversity.
The heterogeneousnesses of the student population enriched the campus experience.  学生群体的多样性丰富了校园体验。
diversity, variety, heterogeneity, multifariousness, dissimilarity
homogeneity, uniformity, similarity, sameness
