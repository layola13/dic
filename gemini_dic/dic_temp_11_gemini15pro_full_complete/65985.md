exaltedly:/ɪɡˈzɔːltɪdli/
adverb
兴奋地；得意洋洋地；狂喜地
in a very happy, excited, and proud way

She spoke exaltedly of her plans for the future.
她兴奋地谈论着她对未来的计划。

He raised his hands exaltedly in victory.
他得意洋洋地举起双手庆祝胜利。


Etymology:
From  exalted + -ly.  Exalted from Latin exaltatus, past participle of exaltare "to raise, elevate,"  from ex- "out, up" + altus "high."

Synonyms:
joyfully, triumphantly, ecstatically, rapturously, jubilantly

Antonyms:
dejectedly, despondently, gloomily, miserably, sadly

Part of Speech:
adverb
