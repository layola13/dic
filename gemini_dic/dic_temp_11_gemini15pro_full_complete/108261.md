launchpads:/ˈlɔːntʃpædz/ (US), /ˈlɔːntʃpædz/ (UK)| n. |发射台，起跑点，跳板|A starting point or platform for something, especially a project or career.  A place from which spacecraft are launched. |例句：The program serves as a launchpad for young entrepreneurs. 这个项目是年轻企业家的跳板。

例句：The Kennedy Space Center is a famous launchpad for space missions. 肯尼迪航天中心是著名的航天发射场。|近义词：springboard, platform, base, starting point|反义词: culmination, finish, end|词性:noun
