miscellanies:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪniːz/
noun
杂集；杂录；杂项
A collection of various items or subjects, especially literary works.
The author published his miscellanies posthumously.  作者在他死后出版了他的杂文集。
synonyms: collection, anthology, assortment, mixture, medley, compilation, potpourri, hodgepodge
antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity
