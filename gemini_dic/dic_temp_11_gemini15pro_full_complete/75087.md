frillery:/ˈfrɪləri/| n.|装饰; 花边; 不必要的装饰; 华而不实的东西|Ornamental trimming or decoration, especially of cloth, typically consisting of gathered or pleated strips; unnecessary ornamentation; showy or frivolous things.|The dress was simple, but the frillery around the neckline added a touch of elegance. 这件衣服很简单，但领口周围的装饰增添了一丝优雅。|Synonyms: ornamentation, decoration, adornment, embellishment, trimming, furbelow, flounce|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun

frill:/frɪl/| n. vt.|褶边，装饰，荷叶边; 给…加上褶边|A ruffled, gathered, or pleated strip of cloth used as a decoration or trimming; an unnecessary extra feature or embellishment; to decorate with a frill.|The curtains had a delicate frill along the bottom edge. 窗帘的底边有一圈精致的褶边。|She frilled the edges of the tablecloth. 她给桌布的边缘加上了褶边。|Synonyms: ruffle, flounce, furbelow, gather, pleat, ornamentation, decoration, embellishment, trimming|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun, verb
