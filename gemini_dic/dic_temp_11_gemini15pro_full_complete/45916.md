cuts:/kʌts/
noun|削减，裁员，切口|reductions, layoffs, incisions|The company announced deep cuts in staffing. 公司宣布大幅裁员。
cuts:/kʌts/
verb|切割，剪切，削减|to divide something with a sharp-edged tool; to reduce the amount of something|He cuts the wood with a saw. 他用锯子锯木头。
cuts:/kʌts/
noun|（电影或录像的）剪辑|edited pieces of film or video|The director reviewed the cuts from the latest scene. 导演回顾了最新场景的剪辑。


Etymology: From Old French *colper, ultimately from Latin culpare "to prune, blame". Related to "culprit".
