all-that:/ɔːl-ðæt/| adj.|那么…|Used to emphasize the extent or importance of something, often implying that it is less than might be expected. |She's not all that smart. 她没那么聪明。|Synonym: not very, not so, not particularly |Antonym: very, extremely|adjective

all-that:/ɔːl-ðæt/| pron.|所有那些|Everything of a particular kind. (Often used with a negative to mean 'not everything'.) |That's all that matters. 那就是所有重要的。|Synonym: everything, all, the whole lot |Antonym: nothing|pronoun
