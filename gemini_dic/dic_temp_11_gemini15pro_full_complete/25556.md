bulu:/ˈbuːluː/
n.
绒毛，毛，羽毛
fine, soft hair or fur, especially the first growth of feathers on a young bird.
The chick's bulu was soft and downy. 小鸡的绒毛柔软蓬松。
近义词：down, fuzz, plumage, feather
反义词:bareness
