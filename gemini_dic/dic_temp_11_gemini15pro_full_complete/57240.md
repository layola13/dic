downiest:/ˈdaʊniɪst/| adj.|最柔软的，绒毛最多的|Covered with the softest, finest down.  The downiest feathers are used to make the most luxurious pillows.|例句：The baby bird had the downiest feathers I've ever seen.  这只幼鸟的羽毛是我见过的最柔软的。

例句：She wrapped herself in the downiest blanket she owned. 她把自己裹在她拥有的最柔软的毯子里。|近义词：softest, fluffiest, featheriest|反义词：coarsest, roughest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From down (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by') + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *dūnn.
