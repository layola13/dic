bums: /bʌmz/
n.
流浪汉，游民；懒汉；屁股
People who have no homes and live on the streets. / Lazy people. / Buttocks.
The city has a problem with bums sleeping in doorways. 这个城市有很多流浪汉睡在门口。
There are too many bums in this town. 这个城镇里有很多懒汉。
He fell on his bums. 他摔了个屁股蹲儿。
近义词: tramps, vagrants, loafers, idlers, buttocks, behinds
反义词: workers, employed
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


bums: /bʌmz/
v. (bums, bumming, bummed)
乞讨；蹭（烟、酒等）；使不安
To ask for something, typically money or food, without intending to repay it. / To get something from someone else for free. / To make someone feel uneasy or upset.
He bummed a cigarette from me. 他向我讨了一根烟。
The news bummed me out. 这个消息让我很不安。
He bums around the country, doing odd jobs. 他在全国各地流浪，做些零工。
近义词: cadge, scrounge, sponge, borrow, upset, disturb
反义词: give, earn, comfort, reassure
词性: 动词
