leggiest:/ˈlɛɡiɪst/| adj.|腿最长的；腿最多的|Having the most or longest legs.  Characterized by having conspicuously long legs. |例句：She was the leggiest girl in the class. 她是班上腿最长的女孩。

例句：The leggiest spider I've ever seen scurried across the floor. 我见过的腿最多的蜘蛛在地板上快速爬行。|近义词：long-legged, spindly, gangly|反义词：short-legged, stubby|词性:形容词

词源: leg + -y + -est. From "leg" meaning the limb used for walking,  "-y" forms an adjective meaning "having the quality of", and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
