burier:/ˈberiər/ | n. | 埋葬者 | A person who buries something, especially a dead body. | The burier placed the coffin gently into the grave. 埋葬者轻轻地把棺材放进坟墓里。| The ancient burier performed the ritual with reverence. 古代的埋葬者虔诚地执行着仪式。 |  synonyms: intermenter, undertaker, sexton | antonyms: exhumator, disinterrer | noun


burier:/ˈbɛriər/ | adj. (comparative burier, superlative buriest) | 更隐蔽的，更秘密的 (rare usage) | More buried or concealed; deeper. |  This secret is burier than the last one.  这个秘密比上一个更隐蔽。 | The treasure was burier than anyone could have imagined. 宝藏比任何人想象的都更深。| synonyms: more concealed, more hidden, deeper | antonyms: more exposed, shallower, more obvious | adjective
