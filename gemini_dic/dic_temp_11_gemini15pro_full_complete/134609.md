nonluminescences:/ˌnɒnˌluːmɪˈnɛsənsɪz/
n.
无发光; 不发光
Absence or lack of luminescence; the state of not emitting light.
The nonluminescences of the material made it unsuitable for use in the display. 这种材料的不发光特性使其不适合用于显示器。
The experiment focused on the nonluminescences of certain deep-sea creatures.  该实验的重点是某些深海生物的不发光性。
近义词: non-emission, darkness, opacity
反义词: luminescence,  emission,  glow
词性: 名词


词源:
non- (prefix):  not, absence of (Latin origin)
luminescence (noun): emission of light not caused by incandescence (from Latin "lumen" meaning light)
-ence (suffix): forming nouns expressing state or quality
