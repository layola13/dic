deplorability:/dɪˌplɔːrəˈbɪləti/
n.|可悲性，悲惨性|The state or quality of being deplorable; wretchedness. |The deplorability of the situation moved many to tears.  这种情况的悲惨性使许多人感动得流泪。| 近义词: wretchedness, misery, pitifulness | 反义词:  excellence, merit, worthiness|词性:名词

deplorable:/dɪˈplɔːrəbl/
adj.|可悲的，悲惨的|Deserving strong condemnation; shockingly bad in quality. |The living conditions in the refugee camp were deplorable.  难民营的生活条件极其恶劣。|近义词: terrible, awful, dreadful | 反义词: admirable, excellent, commendable|词性:形容词
