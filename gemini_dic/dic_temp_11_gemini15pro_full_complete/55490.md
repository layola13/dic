distracting:/dɪˈstræktɪŋ/| adj.|分散注意力的，使人分心的|Causing one to lose focus or concentration; diverting attention away from something.|The noisy children were very distracting while I was trying to work.  吵闹的孩子们在我努力工作时非常令人分心。|Synonyms: disturbing, diverting, interrupting|Antonyms: focusing, concentrating, absorbing|adjective


distract:/dɪˈstrækt/| vt.|分散(注意力)，使分心|To draw someone's attention away from something; to divert.|The loud music distracted me from my studies.  大声的音乐分散了我学习的注意力。|Synonyms: divert, sidetrack, perturb|Antonyms: focus, concentrate, engage|verb
