divination:/ˌdɪvɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|占卜，预言|the practice of seeking knowledge of the future or the unknown by supernatural means.  The act or practice of attempting to foretell future events or discover hidden knowledge by occult or supernatural means.|She practices divination using tarot cards. 她用塔罗牌占卜。|synonyms: prophecy, prediction, soothsaying, forecasting, augury, fortune-telling|antonyms: calculation, logic, reasoning|noun
