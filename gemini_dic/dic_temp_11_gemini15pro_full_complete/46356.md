dactylitis:/ˌdæktɪˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |指炎，趾炎|Inflammation of a finger or toe, typically resulting in a sausage-shaped appearance.|Example: The child presented with dactylitis, a common symptom of sickle cell disease.  这名儿童表现出指炎，这是镰状细胞病的常见症状。

Example: Dactylitis can be painful and limit movement of the affected digit. 指炎会很痛，并限制受影响手指或脚趾的活动。|Synonym: sausage digit,  |Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider using terms describing healthy digits like "normal digit", "unaffected digit")|Noun
