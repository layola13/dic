dined:/daɪnd/
verb
用过正餐；吃饭
past tense and past participle of dine
We dined at a fancy restaurant. 我们在一家高档餐厅用餐。
近义词: ate, lunched, supped
反义词: fasted, starved
词性: verb

词源: From Old French disner (Modern French dîner), from Vulgar Latin *disjūnāre "to break one's fast," from Latin dis- "apart, away" + jējūnāre "to fast."
