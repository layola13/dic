outbargain:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡɪn/
verb
胜过…的讨价还价能力；以较低价格成交
To surpass or defeat someone in bargaining; to negotiate a lower price than someone else.
He outbargained the street vendor and got the souvenir for half the original price. 他在讨价还价中胜过街头小贩，以原价的一半买到了纪念品。
近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outtrade
反义词: be outbargained, be outsmarted
词性: verb
