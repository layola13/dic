fragrant:/ˈfreɪɡrənt/| adj.|芬芳的，香的|Having a pleasant or sweet smell.|The air was fragrant with the scent of jasmine. 空气中弥漫着茉莉花的香味。|近义词：aromatic, perfumed, sweet-smelling|反义词：foul, musty, fetid|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fragraunt*, from Old French *fragrant*, from Latin *fragrāns*, present participle of *fragrāre* (“to emit a sweet smell, to be fragrant”), from *fragrum* (“strawberry”) + *-āre* (present active infinitive suffix).
