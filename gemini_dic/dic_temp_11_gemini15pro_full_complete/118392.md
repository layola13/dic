meroblast:/ˈmɛrəˌblæst/
n.
部分卵裂的卵；卵裂不完全的卵
An egg in which cleavage is restricted to a portion of the cytoplasm; incomplete cleavage.
The zebrafish embryo is a meroblast.  斑马鱼的胚胎是一个部分卵裂的卵。
Discoidal meroblastic cleavage occurs in birds. 盘状不完全卵裂发生在鸟类中。

From Greek *μέρος (méros, “part”) + *βλαστός (blastós, “germ, sprout”).
