eor:/eɪˈɔːr/ | conj. |或者；否则|A Boolean operator that returns true if either operand is true, but not both.  It is sometimes represented as XOR (exclusive OR). |You can have tea eor coffee, but not both. 你可以喝茶或咖啡，但不能两者都喝。|Synonyms: or, either...or |Antonyms: and, both |Conjunction

注意事项:

eor is generally used in more formal or technical contexts, especially in logic and computer programming.  In everyday conversation, "or" or "either...or" are more common.
