overpampering: /ˌoʊvərˈpæmpərɪŋ/ | n. | 过分溺爱，娇惯 | The act of pampering someone excessively, often to their detriment.  It implies spoiling someone by indulging their every whim and shielding them from difficulties. |  His overpampering of his children has resulted in them being unable to cope with everyday challenges. 他对孩子的过度溺爱导致他们无法应对日常挑战。 | 近义词: coddling, spoiling, indulging | 反义词: neglecting, disciplining | 词性: noun

overpamper: /ˌoʊvərˈpæmpər/ | v. | 过分溺爱，娇惯 | To pamper someone excessively. |  She overpampered her dog, feeding it gourmet meals and dressing it in silly outfits. 她过度溺爱她的狗，给它吃美食，还给它穿滑稽的衣服。 | 近义词: coddle, spoil, indulge | 反义词: neglect, discipline | 词性: verb

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "pamper" (from Middle English *pampren*, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French *pamprer* "to cover with vine leaves").
