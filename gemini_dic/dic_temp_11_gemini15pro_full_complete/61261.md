emancipist:/ɪˈmænsɪpɪst/
n.
被解放的人；(尤指)已获自由的囚犯；(澳大利亚史)被赦免的流放犯
a person who has been freed from slavery or other forms of legal restrictions, especially a convict who has been pardoned.
The emancipists played a crucial role in the development of early Australian society.  这些被赦免的犯人在澳大利亚早期社会的发展中扮演了至关重要的角色。
synonyms: freedman, libertee
antonyms: slave, captive
noun
