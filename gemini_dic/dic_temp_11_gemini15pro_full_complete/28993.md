cartesians:/kɑːrˈtiːʒənz/ | n. |  笛卡尔主义者；笛卡尔哲学信徒；笛卡尔坐标 | People who subscribe to the philosophical tenets of René Descartes, or who use Cartesian coordinates. | Many mathematicians today are Cartesians, in the sense that they use Cartesian coordinates. | 近义词:  Descartes' followers, rationalists | 反义词: empiricists | 词性: 名词(plural)

cartesians:/kɑːrˈtiːʒən/ | adj. | 笛卡尔的；笛卡尔哲学的；笛卡尔坐标的 | Relating to the philosophy of René Descartes, or pertaining to Cartesian coordinates. |  The Cartesian coordinate system is fundamental to much of modern mathematics. | 近义词:  Descartes', Cartesian coordinate | 反义词:  (For coordinates) polar, spherical | 词性: 形容词
