bourgeoisified:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 中产阶级化的，变得庸俗的 | Having adopted the values and lifestyle of the bourgeoisie; made middle-class in character.  Often used derogatorily to imply a loss of creativity or radicalism. |  The artist felt his work had become bourgeoisified after he started selling to commercial galleries.  这位艺术家觉得自从他开始向商业画廊出售作品后，他的作品变得庸俗了。| The once radical playwright's work has become disappointingly bourgeoisified. 这位曾经激进的剧作家的作品变得令人失望地中产阶级化了。 | 近义词：conventional, middle-class, gentrified | 反义词：radical, bohemian, avant-garde | 词性: adjective

词源: From bourgeois + -ify + -ed.  Bourgeois comes from Old French burgeis, meaning "citizen of a town."
