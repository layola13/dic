bibirine:/ˈbɪbɪriːn/| n.|比比林|An alkaloid with antipyretic and antimalarial properties, extracted from the bark and roots of several plants of the genus *Nectandra*.|Bibirine was once used as a substitute for quinine. 比比林曾经被用作奎宁的替代品。|Synonyms: bebeerine, nectandra|Antonyms: None|noun

bibirine:/ˈbɪbɪriːn/| adj.|比比林的|Relating to or containing bibirine.|Bibirine bark was a common ingredient in 19th-century tonics.  19世纪的滋补品中，比比林树皮是常见的成分。|Synonyms: bebeerine, nectandra-derived|Antonyms: None|adjective
