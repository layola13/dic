crestfallennesses:/ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlənɪsɪz/
n.
沮丧，垂头丧气
The state of being crestfallen; dejection.
His crestfallenness was evident after he lost the match.  他输了比赛后，沮丧之情溢于言表。
Near Synonyms: dejection, despondency, discouragement
Antonyms: elation, jubilation, cheerfulness
