clawless:/ˈklɔːləs/| adj.|无爪的；没有爪子的|Lacking claws; having no claws.|The clawed frog, despite its name, is actually clawed only on its hind feet; the front feet are clawless. 尽管名字叫有爪蛙，它实际上只有后脚有爪子；前脚是无爪的。|Near Synonyms: claw-free, unclawed|Antonyms: clawed, clawed|adjective
