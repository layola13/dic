narky:/ˈnɑːrki/ (UK) /ˈnɑːrki/ (US)| adj.|暴躁的，易怒的，尖刻的|Bad-tempered and critical; sarcastic.  It suggests a petulant or irritable tone and a tendency to make sharp, often sarcastic remarks.|例句：He made a narky comment about her dress. 他对她的裙子做了一个尖刻的评论。

例句：She was narky with me all morning. 她整个上午都对我暴躁。

近义词：irritable, grumpy, bad-tempered, sarcastic, snappy, curt, testy|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, good-natured, cheerful|词性:adjective
