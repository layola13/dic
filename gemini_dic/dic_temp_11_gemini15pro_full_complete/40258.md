contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/| n. | 意外事件；偶然事件；可能性；（复数）应急费用 | a future event or circumstance which is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty | The plan includes contingency for bad weather. 该计划包括了应对恶劣天气的应急措施。| synonyms: possibility, eventuality, uncertainty, risk | antonyms: certainty, guarantee | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contingentia, from Latin contingens, present participle of contingere "to touch, befall," from com- "with, together" + tangere "to touch."
