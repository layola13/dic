jove:/dʒoʊv/| int.|我的天哪！ (表示惊讶或感叹)|Used as an exclamation of surprise or astonishment.|例句：Jove! I hadn't expected to see you here. 我的天哪！我没料到会在这里见到你。|近义词：Goodness, heavens, my word|反义词:N/A|感叹词
