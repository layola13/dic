intangibles:/ɪnˈtændʒəblz/ | n. | 无形资产，无形的东西 | Assets that are not physical in nature, such as goodwill, brand recognition, and intellectual property. |  The company's intangibles, like its reputation and customer loyalty, are key to its success.  这家公司的无形资产，如声誉和客户忠诚度，是其成功的关键。 | synonyms: non-physical assets, abstract assets | antonyms: tangibles, physical assets | noun

intangible:/ɪnˈtændʒəbl/ | adj. | 无形的，难以捉摸的，难以理解的 |  Unable to be touched or grasped; not having physical presence. | The benefits of a good education are sometimes intangible. 良好教育的益处有时是无形的。 | synonyms: impalpable, imperceptible, ethereal | antonyms: tangible, concrete, palpable | adjective
