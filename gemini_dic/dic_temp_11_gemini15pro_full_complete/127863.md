neocerebellum:/ˌniːoʊˌsɛrəˈbɛləm/ | n. | 新小脑 |  The newest part of the cerebellum, involved in planning and executing complex movements and cognitive functions. | The neocerebellum receives input from the cerebral cortex and is crucial for motor learning. 新小脑接收来自大脑皮层的输入，对运动学习至关重要。|  cerebellar cortex, posterior lobe |  anterior lobe, paleocerebellum | noun
