heavenwardly:/ˈhɛvənwərdli/| adv.|向天堂地，朝天；向上地|Moving or directed toward heaven.  Its usage suggests a spiritual or uplifting movement or aspiration.|Her eyes gazed heavenwardly, seeking divine guidance. 她虔诚地仰望着天空，寻求神的指引。|Synonyms: skyward, upward, heavenwards|Antonyms: downward, earthward|Adverb
