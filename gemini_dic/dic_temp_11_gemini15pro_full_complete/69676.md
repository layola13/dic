fender:/ˈfɛndər/ (US), /ˈfendə(r)/ (UK)| n.|挡泥板；防护板；（船的）护舷|A part of a vehicle, such as a car or bicycle, that arches over the wheel to protect the vehicle and people from dirt or water thrown up by the wheel.  Also, a cushion-like device along the side of a boat or dock to protect it from damage.|The car's front fender was dented in the accident. 汽车的前挡泥板在事故中凹陷了。|synonyms: mudguard, wing (UK), bumper|antonyms: N/A|noun

fender:/ˈfɛndər/ (US), /ˈfendə(r)/ (UK)| n.|（壁炉等的）栏杆|A low metal guard placed in front of a fireplace to prevent burning coals from falling out.|The fender prevented the embers from rolling onto the carpet.  栏杆防止了余烬滚到地毯上。|synonyms: fireguard, firescreen|antonyms: N/A|noun
