consigne:/kɒnˈsaɪn/ /kənˈsaɪn/| n.|寄售货物，委托物；收货人；密码|Goods delivered to a person or agent to be sold.|The consignee received the shipment of textiles. 收货人收到了纺织品货物。|synonyms: shipment, consignment, delivery|antonyms:  dispatch, shipment (as the sender's perspective)|noun

consigne:/kɒnˈsaɪn/ /kənˈsaɪn/| v.|寄售，委托；交付|To deliver goods to a person or agent to be sold.|The company consigned its surplus inventory to an auction house. 这家公司将其剩余库存委托给了一家拍卖行。|synonyms: entrust, commit, deliver|antonyms: retain, withhold, keep|verb
