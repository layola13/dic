Assuming the intended word is "eating":

eating: /ˈiːtɪŋ/
noun/adjective
吃，进食 /  吃的，食用的
the action of consuming food; used to describe something related to eating.
Eating too much can be unhealthy.  吃太多可能不健康。
This is an eating apple, not a cooking apple. 这是一个食用苹果，不是烹饪苹果。
近义词: consuming, dining
反义词: fasting, starving
词性: noun, adjective
