ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ (US) /ˈɛkstəsi/ (UK)| n. |狂喜；入迷；忘形|A state of overwhelming happiness and joy.|She was in an ecstasy of delight after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。| The music transported him to a state of ecstasy.  音乐让他进入了一种忘我的境界。| 近义词: euphoria, rapture, bliss, elation |反义词: misery, despair, agony, depression|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin extasis, from Ancient Greek ἔκστασις (ékstasis, “displacement, trance”), from ἐξίστημι (exístēmi, “I displace, put out of its place”), from ἐκ (ek, “out”) + ἵστημι (hístēmi, “I stand”).
