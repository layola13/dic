cupellation:/ˌkʌpəˈleɪʃən/
n.
灰吹法；试金法
The process of assaying gold or silver by heating the sample with lead in a porous cupel. The lead oxidizes and is absorbed by the cupel, leaving a bead of pure metal.
The cupellation process revealed the true gold content of the ore. 灰吹法揭示了矿石的真实黄金含量。
assaying, refining

noun
