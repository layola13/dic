intergraft: /ˌɪntərˈɡræft/
n. |相互嫁接；相互移植；相互结合|The intergraft of different cultures can lead to a richer and more diverse society. 不同文化的相互结合可以导致一个更丰富和更多元化的社会。|
v. |相互嫁接；相互移植；相互结合|They intergrafted the two companies to create a stronger entity.他们将两家公司相互结合，以创建一个更强大的实体。|例句：The surgeon intergrafted skin from the patient's leg onto the burned area.  外科医生将病人腿上的皮肤移植到烧伤部位。|近义词：combine, merge, integrate, fuse|反义词：separate, divide, disconnect|词性：名词, 动词


Etymology:
From inter- + graft.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "graft" refers to the process of transplanting living tissue.
