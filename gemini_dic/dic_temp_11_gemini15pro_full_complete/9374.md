antiquarianism:/ˌæn.tɪˈkwɛər.i.ən.ɪ.zəm/
n.
古物研究；古物收藏；对古物的爱好
The study or collection of antiquities or a love of antiquities.
He had a passion for antiquarianism, spending hours in dusty old bookshops. 他对古物研究充满热情，常常在满是灰尘的旧书店里待上几个小时。
Her antiquarianism led her to a career in archaeology. 她对古物的热爱引导她从事了考古学。
Synonyms: archaism, antiquity
Antonyms: modernity, contemporary
noun


antiquarianisms:/ˌæn.tɪˈkwɛər.i.ən.ɪ.zəmz/
n.
古物研究（复数）；古物收藏（复数）；对古物的爱好（复数）
The plural form of antiquarianism, referring to multiple instances of the study or collection of antiquities, or a love for them.
His antiquarianisms filled his house with fascinating objects. 他的古董收藏让他的房子里充满了迷人的物品。
The various antiquarianisms of the Victorian era reflected a growing interest in the past. 维多利亚时代对古物的各种爱好反映了人们对过去日益增长的兴趣。
Synonyms: archaisms, antiquities
Antonyms: modernities, contemporaries
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From antiquarian + -ism.  Antiquarian comes from Late Latin antiquarius "copyist of old books," from Latin antiquus "old, ancient."
