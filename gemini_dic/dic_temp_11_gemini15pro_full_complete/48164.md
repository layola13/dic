decomp:/ˌdiːkəmˈpəʊz/ (UK) /ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊz/ (US)| v. n.|分解，腐烂| To break down or cause something to break down into smaller or simpler parts; the act or process of decomposing.| The gardener added compost to help decomp the leaves. 园丁添加堆肥以帮助树叶分解。|The body had started to decomp. 尸体已经开始腐烂。|近义词：decay, rot, break down, disintegrate|反义词: synthesize, compose, build up|词性:动词/名词

词源: From de- (prefix meaning "down, away") + compose (from Latin componere "to put together").  The noun form is a back-formation from the verb.
