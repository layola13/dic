noncombative:/ˌnɒnkəmˈbætɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈbætɪv/
adjective
非战斗性的；不具攻击性的；不爱争斗的
Not inclined to fight or argue; peaceful.
例句：He adopted a noncombative stance during the negotiations.  他在谈判过程中采取了非战斗姿态。

例句：Her noncombative nature made her a good mediator. 她不爱争斗的天性使她成为了一名优秀的调解员。
近义词：peaceful, pacifistic, nonaggressive, conciliatory, cooperative
反义词：aggressive, combative, belligerent, hostile, antagonistic
词性：adjective
