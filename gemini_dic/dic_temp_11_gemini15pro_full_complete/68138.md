fahrenheit:/ˈfærənˌhaɪt/ /ˈfɑːrənhaɪt/| n. adj.|华氏度；华氏温标|A temperature scale in which the freezing point of water is 32 degrees and the boiling point is 212 degrees.|例句：The temperature today is 70 degrees Fahrenheit. 今天气温是70华氏度。|近义词：Celsius, centigrade|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 形容词

fahrenheit:/ˈfærənˌhaɪt/ /ˈfɑːrənhaɪt/| n. adj.|华氏温度计|A thermometer that measures temperature in Fahrenheit.|例句：Use a Fahrenheit thermometer to measure the temperature of the water. 用华氏温度计测量水的温度。|近义词：Fahrenheit scale|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 形容词
