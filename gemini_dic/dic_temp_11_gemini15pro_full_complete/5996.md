ambushment's:/ˈæmbʊʃmənts/ |n.| 伏击，埋伏；埋伏地点|The ambushment's location was carefully chosen. 埋伏地点是精心挑选的。|The soldier survived the ambushment's dangers. 这名士兵在埋伏中幸存了下来。|Synonyms: ambush, trap, snare|Antonyms:  exposure, open ground|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *embuschement, from Old French embuschement, from embuschier (“to ambush”), from en- (“in”) + busche (“bush, wood”).
