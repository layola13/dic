impire:/ɪmˈpaɪər/ | v. | 损害，削弱；侵犯，侵害 | to weaken or damage something, especially gradually |  The constant stress of the job began to impire her health.  工作上的持续压力开始损害她的健康。|  His reputation was impired by the scandal.  丑闻损害了他的名誉。| 近义词: undermine, impair, weaken, damage | 反义词: strengthen, enhance, improve | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Late Latin *impirare, from Latin in- + pirare "to try, attempt," perhaps related to periculum "danger, trial."  源自晚期拉丁语 *impirare，来自拉丁语 in- + pirare “尝试，试图”，可能与 periculum “危险，审判” 相关。
