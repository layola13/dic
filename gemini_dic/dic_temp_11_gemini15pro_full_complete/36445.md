colcothar: /kɒlkəθɑːr/ /kɑːlkoʊθɑːr/
n.
红氧化铁，铁丹，煅赭石
A reddish-brown powder of ferric oxide, obtained by heating ferrous sulfate.  Used as a pigment and a polishing agent.
Colcothar is often used as a pigment in paints and primers. 铁丹常被用作油漆和底漆中的颜料。
This antique clock was polished with colcothar. 这座古董钟是用铁丹抛光的。
近义词: rouge, iron oxide, ferric oxide
反义词: None
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Medieval Latin *colcotar, from Arabic قولقطار (qūlqutār), from Greek χάλκιον (khalkion, “copperas, vitriol”) + Arabic قطران (qiṭrān, “tar”).  Originally referred to the residue left after distilling vitriol (ferrous sulfate).
