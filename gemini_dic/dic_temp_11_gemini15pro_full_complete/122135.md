mistreat:/ˌmɪsˈtriːt/| vt.|虐待，苛待|To treat someone badly, cruelly, or unfairly.|He mistreated his animals and was reported to the authorities. 他虐待动物，被举报给了当局。|The company was accused of mistreating its workers. 这家公司被指控苛待员工。|近义词：abuse, maltreat, ill-treat|反义词：treat well, cherish, nurture|词性:动词
Etymology:  From mis- + treat.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Treat" comes from the Old French "traitier" meaning "to handle, deal with."
