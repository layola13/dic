deodand:/ˈdiːoʊdænd/
noun
抵命物; 凶器
An object or instrument which caused a person's accidental death and was forfeited to the Crown.
The cart that ran over the child was declared a deodand.  那辆辗过孩子的马车被宣布为抵命物。
Synonyms: forfeit, confiscate
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a legal term with a specific historical meaning.  One could consider "property of the deceased's family" an antonymous concept in practice.)
