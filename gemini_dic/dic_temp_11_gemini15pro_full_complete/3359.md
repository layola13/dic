affliction:/əˈflɪkʃən/ | n. |痛苦，折磨；苦难，灾难|Something that causes pain or suffering.|The affliction of poverty can be devastating. 贫穷的折磨可能是毁灭性的.|近义词：suffering, distress, hardship, tribulation, torment|反义词：comfort, solace, relief, ease|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French affliction, from Latin afflictio(n-), from the verb affligere, from ad- ‘towards’ + fligere ‘to strike’.
