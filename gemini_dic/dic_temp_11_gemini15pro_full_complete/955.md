academicians:/əˌkædəˈmɪʃənz/
n.
院士；学会会员；学究
A member of an academy, especially a society of scholars, artists, or scientists.
The academicians met to discuss the latest research.  院士们开会讨论最新的研究成果。
The Royal Academy of Arts elected new academicians this year.  今年皇家艺术学院选举了新的院士。
synonyms: scholar, intellectual, learned person, savant, pundit, expert, authority, specialist
antonyms: ignoramus, amateur, novice, dilettante
noun
