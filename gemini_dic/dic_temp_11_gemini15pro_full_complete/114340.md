majestical:/məˈdʒɛstɪkəl/| adj.|雄伟的，庄严的，威严的|Having or showing impressive beauty or dignity, suggesting royalty or great power.  Characteristic of or befitting a majesty.|The majestical Himalayas rise above the clouds. 雄伟的喜马拉雅山脉高耸入云。|近义词：august, grand, magnificent, stately, majestic|反义词：humble, lowly, insignificant, unimpressive|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French majestatic or late Latin majesticus, from majestas (see majesty).
