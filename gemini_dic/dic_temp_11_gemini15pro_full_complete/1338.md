accrued:/əˈkruːd/ | adj. | 已累计的，应计的 | accumulated or increased over time, especially through the addition of interest or other charges |  Interest accrued on the savings account. 利息在储蓄账户上累计。 | synonyms: accumulated, gathered, amassed | antonyms: depleted, reduced, diminished | adjective


accrued:/əˈkruːd/ | v. | 累计，应计 | (of a sum of money or benefits) be received by someone in regular or increasing amounts over time. |  Holiday pay accrues over the year. 假期工资全年累计。 | synonyms: accumulate, collect, gather | antonyms: decrease, diminish, dwindle | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "accrēscere"  ("to grow to," "be added to"), from "ad-" ("to") + "crēscere" ("to grow").
