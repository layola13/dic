greisen:/ˈɡraɪzən/| n. | 花岗岩化|A coarsely crystalline aggregate of quartz, mica, and topaz, formed by the alteration of granite by its own volatile constituents.|The greisen contained significant amounts of cassiterite.  这种花岗岩化含有大量的锡石。|Synonyms: altered granite, pneumatolytic rock|Antonyms: unaltered granite, fresh granite|noun
