cyclops:/ˈsaɪklɒps/ (UK), /ˈsaɪklɑːps/ (US)| n.|独眼巨人|A mythical giant with a single eye in the middle of the forehead.|例句：The cyclops Polyphemus imprisoned Odysseus and his men. 独眼巨人波吕斐摩斯囚禁了奥德修斯和他的同伴们。

例句：In Greek mythology, cyclopes were often depicted as skilled blacksmiths. 在希腊神话中，独眼巨人通常被描绘成技艺精湛的铁匠。|近义词：giant, monster|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *κύκλωψ* (kúklōps, “round-eyed”), from *κύκλος* (kúklos, “circle, wheel, eye”) + *ὤψ* (ōps, “eye, face”).
