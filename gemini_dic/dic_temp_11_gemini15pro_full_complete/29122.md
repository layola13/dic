cascarón:/kɑːskɑːˈroʊn/ (US), /kɑːskəˈrɒn/ (UK)| n. |彩蛋；空蛋壳|A hollowed-out chicken egg filled with confetti or small toys, typically used during celebrations like Easter or Carnival, and broken over someone's head for good luck.|例句：Children were throwing cascarones at each other during the Fiesta celebration. 孩子们在节日庆典上互相扔彩蛋。

例句：She made colorful cascarones for the children's Easter egg hunt. 她为孩子们的复活节寻蛋游戏制作了彩色的彩蛋。|近义词：confetti egg, party favor|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
