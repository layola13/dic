neighbourhood: /ˈneɪbərhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n.| 附近; 邻近; 街坊; |A neighbourhood is a particular area of a town.  一个neighbourhood指的是城镇中的一个特定区域。|例句：I grew up in a friendly neighbourhood. 我在一个友好的街区长大。|例句：The whole neighbourhood came to the street party. 整个街区的人都来参加了街头派对。|近义词：community, vicinity, area, district|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑remoteness, isolation)|词性: noun
