nonpropagandist:/ˌnɒnˌprɒpəˈɡændɪst/ | n. |非宣传者；不进行宣传的人|A person who does not create or spread propaganda. | Example: The journalist considered himself a nonpropagandist, striving for objective reporting.  这位记者认为自己是一个非宣传者，努力进行客观报道。| Example:  She preferred a nonpropagandist approach to educating the public. 她更喜欢用非宣传的方式来教育公众。 |Synonym: neutralist, impartial observer | Antonym: propagandist, advocate | noun


nonpropagandizing:/ˌnɒnˌprɒpəˈɡændaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |非宣传的；不进行宣传的 |Not engaging in or characterized by propaganda. | Example:  He took a nonpropagandizing stance on the controversial issue. 他在这个有争议的问题上采取了非宣传的立场。| Example:  The organization's nonpropagandizing approach earned them respect. 该组织的非宣传方式赢得了他们的尊重。 |Synonym:  unbiased, objective, neutral | Antonym: propagandizing, promotional | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non-, meaning "not".
propaganda: From Modern Latin *propaganda, meaning "propagation (of the faith)". Ultimately from the Latin verb propagare, meaning "to propagate, to extend, to spread".
-ist (suffix): From Latin *-ista and Greek *-istēs, denoting an agent or adherent of a particular doctrine or practice.
