antienvironmentalist:/ˌæntiɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪst/ | n. | 反环保主义者 |  A person who opposes environmentalism or environmental protection.  |  The antienvironmentalist lobby fought against the new emissions regulations.  反环保主义游说团体反对新的排放规定。 |  Anti-environment advocate, climate change denier. | Environmentalist, conservationist | noun

antienvironmentalist:/ˌæntiɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪst/ | adj. | 反环保的 | Opposing or hostile to environmentalism or environmental protection. | The politician's antienvironmentalist stance cost him the election.  这位政治家的反环保立场使他输掉了选举。 |  Anti-environment, environmentally unfriendly | Pro-environment, environmentally friendly | adjective
