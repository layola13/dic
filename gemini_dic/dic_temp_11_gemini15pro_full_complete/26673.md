cacuminate:/kəˈkjuːmɪneɪt/ |adj.|渐尖的，骤尖的|Tapering to a slender point; pointed.|The leaves of the tree are cacuminate. 这棵树的叶子是渐尖的。|近义词：acute, pointed, sharp, cuspidate|反义词:obtuse, rounded|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cacuminatus*, past participle of cacuminare (“to make pointed”), from Latin cacumen (“summit, peak, top point”).
