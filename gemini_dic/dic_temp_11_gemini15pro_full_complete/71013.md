firm-up:/fɜːrm ˈʌp/| phrasal verb | 确定；巩固；使坚挺 | To make something more definite, concrete, or solid. | We need to firm up the arrangements for the meeting. 我们需要确定会议的安排。|近义词：solidify, strengthen, consolidate, finalize |反义词: weaken, loosen, soften |词性:动词短语

ETYMOLOGY->
From "firm" (adjective, meaning solid or definite) + "up" (adverb, indicating completion or intensification).
