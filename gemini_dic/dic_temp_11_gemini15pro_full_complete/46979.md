darwinianism:/dɑːrˈwɪniənɪzəm/ | n. | 达尔文主义；达尔文进化论 | The theory of the evolution of species by natural selection advanced by Charles Darwin. | Example: Darwinianism revolutionized the study of biology. 达尔文主义彻底改变了生物学的研究。

Example:  Many scientists found compelling evidence to support Darwinianism. 许多科学家发现了令人信服的证据来支持达尔文主义。 | 近义词: evolutionism, natural selection | 反义词: creationism | 词性: noun
