non-verbally: /ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəli/ /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəli/
adverb
非语言地；不用语言地
Without using spoken language.
She communicated her discomfort non-verbally. 她非语言地表达了她的不适。
He nodded non-verbally in agreement. 他默默地点头表示同意。
近义词: silently, wordlessly
反义词: verbally, orally
词性: adverb

non-verbal: /ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/
adjective
非语言的；不用语言的
Not involving or using words.
Non-verbal communication is very important. 非语言交流非常重要。
Babies rely heavily on non-verbal cues. 婴儿非常依赖非语言的线索。
近义词: unspoken, unsaid
反义词: verbal, spoken
词性: adjective
