outbeam:/ˌaʊtˈbiːm/
verb
发出更强的光
to surpass in beaming; to radiate with greater brilliance.
The new lighthouse outbeamed the old one. 新灯塔的光比旧灯塔更强。
近义词: outshine, eclipse, surpass
反义词: dim, darken
词性: verb
