nonproportionally: /ˌnɒnˌprəˈpɔːʃənəli/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌprəˈpɔːrʃənəli/ | adv. | 非比例地，不成比例地 | Not in proportion; in a way that is not proportional. |  The cost increases nonproportionally with the size of the engine.  发动机尺寸越大，成本增加得不成比例。 |  The price of the item increased nonproportionally to its weight. 这件物品的价格与其重量不成比例地增长。| 近义词: disproportionately, unevenly | 反义词: proportionally, proportionately | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
* non- (prefix):  from Latin *non* "not."  Indicates negation.
* proportionally (adverb): from *proportional* + *-ly*.
* proportional (adjective): from Late Latin *proportionalis* "pertaining to proportion," from Latin *proportio* "comparative relation, analogy," from *pro* "for, in proportion to" + *portio* "share, part."
