inessential:/ˌɪnɪˈsɛnʃəl/
adjective
不重要的；非必要的
not absolutely necessary; not essential
Removing inessential programs can free up space on your hard drive. 删除不必要的程序可以释放硬盘空间。
The appendix is an inessential organ. 阑尾是一个非必要的器官。
synonym:unnecessary, dispensable, nonessential, superfluous
antonym:essential, necessary, crucial, vital
词性:形容词
