grandmotherly:/ˈɡrændˌmʌðərli/| adj.|慈祥的；像祖母的|Kind and caring, in a way that is typical of a grandmother. |例句：She had a grandmotherly smile that made everyone feel welcome. 她慈祥的笑容让每个人都感到宾至如归。


例句：She gave me some grandmotherly advice about my love life. 她给了我一些关于爱情生活的像祖母一样的建议。


近义词：kind, caring, maternal, nurturing|反义词：uncaring, cold, harsh|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From grandmother + -ly.  Grandmother from grand + mother. Grand ultimately from Latin "grandis" (great). Mother from Old English "mōdor", of Germanic origin.
