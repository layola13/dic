disembarkation:/ˌdɪsɪmbɑːrˈkeɪʃən/
n.
下船，登陆
The act of leaving a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.
Passengers began disembarkation as soon as the ship docked.  乘客们在船靠岸后立即开始下船。
The disembarkation process was delayed due to bad weather.  由于恶劣天气，下船过程被延误了。
Synonyms: landing, debarkation, egress
Antonyms: embarkation, boarding
词性: 名词


disembark:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/
v.
下船，登陆
To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.
Passengers disembarked at Southampton. 乘客们在南安普顿下船。
We disembarked the plane after a long flight.  长途飞行后，我们下了飞机。
Synonyms: debark, land, get off
Antonyms: embark, board
词性: 动词


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal) + embarkation (from em- (in, on) + bark (ship) + -ation (forming nouns of action)).  Ultimately from the Latin word "barca" (boat).
