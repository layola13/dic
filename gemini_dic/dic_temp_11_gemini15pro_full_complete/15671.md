bakingly:/ˈbeɪkɪŋli/
|adv.|
|烘烤地，像烘烤一样地|
|Describes something done in a manner resembling baking, often referring to heat or dryness.  It's not a commonly used word.|
|The sun beat down bakingly on the desert sand. 太阳烘烤着沙漠的沙子。|
|The air hung heavy and bakingly hot. 空气沉重而燥热，就像烤箱一样。|
|近义词: hotly, scorchingly, aridly|
|反义词: coolly, damply|
|词性: 副词|
