emboldens:/ɪmˈboʊldənz/
verb
使大胆；使有勇气
makes someone feel braver and more confident
His success emboldened him to try again. 他的成功鼓舞了他再次尝试。
Synonyms: encourage, hearten, inspire, strengthen
Antonyms: discourage, dishearten, intimidate, weaken
詞性: 動詞(verb)

Antonyms: deter, hinder


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + bold + -en.  "En-" acts as a prefix meaning "to cause to be," "bold" refers to being courageous and confident, and "-en" is a suffix that transforms an adjective into a verb.
