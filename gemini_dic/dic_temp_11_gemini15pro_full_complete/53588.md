disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/ /dɪsˈbɝːs/| vt.|支付，支出|to pay out (money) from a fund.|The company disbursed bonuses to its employees. 公司向员工支付了奖金。|Synonyms: pay out, expend, distribute|Antonyms: receive, collect, deposit|verb

disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/ /dɪsˈbɝːsmənt/| n.|支付，支出款|the act of paying out money|The disbursement of funds was delayed due to the holiday.  由于假期，资金的支付被推迟了。|Synonyms: payment, expenditure, outlay|Antonyms: receipt, collection, deposit|noun
