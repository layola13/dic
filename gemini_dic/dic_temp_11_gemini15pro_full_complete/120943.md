miscensure:/ˌmɪsˈsɛnʃər/ | n. |  误判；错误的判断；错误的责备 | An incorrect judgment or assessment; blaming wrongly. | He made a miscensure of the situation and acted rashly. 他对情况作出了错误的判断，行事鲁莽。 | Near synonyms: misjudgment, miscalculation, error, blunder | Antonyms:  accurate assessment, correct judgment | noun

miscensure:/ˌmɪsˈsɛnʃər/ | v. |  错误地判断；错误地责备 | To judge or assess incorrectly; to blame wrongly. |  She miscensured his motives, thinking he was acting out of selfishness. 她误判了他的动机，认为他是出于自私自利。 | Near synonyms: misjudge, miscalculate, err | Antonyms: judge correctly, assess accurately | verb
