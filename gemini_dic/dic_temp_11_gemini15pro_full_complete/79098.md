glad-handed: /ɡlædˈhændɪd/
glad-handed: adj.
glad-handed: 热情友好的；虚情假意的
glad-handed: Excessively or insincerely friendly and hearty.  Characterized by a show of excessive cordiality, often considered insincere.
glad-handed: The glad-handed politician worked the room, shaking hands and slapping backs.  这位热情过度的政客在房间里来回走动，握手拍背。

glad-handed: The salesman was so glad-handed I didn't trust him.  这个推销员太热情了，我反而不信任他。
glad-handed: 近义词:  affable, cordial, gregarious, ingratiating, unctuous
glad-handed: 反义词: aloof, cold, distant, reserved, unfriendly
