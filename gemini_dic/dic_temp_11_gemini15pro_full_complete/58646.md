dumbfoundingly:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋli/
adv.
令人惊奇地，使人目瞪口呆地
in a way that dumbfounds or amazes someone
The magician dumbfoundedingly pulled a rabbit out of his hat.  魔术师令人惊奇地从帽子里变出了一只兔子。
She dumbfoundedingly solved the complex puzzle in seconds. 她不可思议地在几秒钟内解开了复杂的谜题。
近义词: astonishingly, amazingly, surprisingly, incredibly
反义词: unsurprisingly, expectedly, predictably
词性: 副词

词源:
From dumbfounding + -ly.  Dumbfounding 来自于 dumbfound (使...目瞪口呆) + -ing. Dumbfound 源于 dumb (哑的) + confound (使困惑).
