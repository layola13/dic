boulderized: /ˈboʊldəraɪzd/
verb | 用巨石加固（路面、斜坡等）; 用巨石铺设; | The trail was boulderized to prevent erosion.  这条小路用巨石加固以防止水土流失。| 近义词: reinforced, paved, fortified | 反义词: excavated, weakened | 词性: v.
boulderized: /ˈboʊldəraɪzd/
adjective | 用巨石加固的; 用巨石铺设的; | The boulderized slope provided a stable foundation. 用巨石加固的斜坡提供了一个稳定的基础。| 近义词: reinforced, paved, fortified | 反义词: excavated, weakened | 词性: adj.


Etymology:  From "boulder" (large rock) + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become like").
