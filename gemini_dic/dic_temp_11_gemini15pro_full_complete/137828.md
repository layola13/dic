nonvexatiousness:/ˌnɒnˌvɛkˈseɪʃəsnəs/
noun
无骚扰性；不烦扰性
The state or quality of not being vexatious; absence of annoyance or harassment.
His nonvexatiousness made him a popular neighbor. 他的不烦扰性使他成为受欢迎的邻居。
Near Synonyms: peacefulness, tranquility, serenity, calmness
Antonyms: vexatiousness, annoyance, harassment
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + vexatious (causing annoyance or frustration) + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Vexatious comes from the Latin vexare (to shake, agitate, trouble).
