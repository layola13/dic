announcements:/əˈnaʊnsmənts/
n.
公告；通告；声明；宣布
a formal public statement about a fact, occurrence, or intention.
The company made several important announcements at the press conference.
公司在新闻发布会上发布了几项重要公告。
The president made an announcement about the new policy.
总统宣布了这项新政策。

synonyms: declaration, proclamation, notification, communiqué, bulletin, report
antonyms: secret, concealment, suppression
