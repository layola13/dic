engulfment:/ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt/ | n. |吞没，吞噬，淹没|The act of being engulfed, or the state of being overwhelmed or swallowed up. |The engulfment of the town by the floodwaters was a tragedy.  小镇被洪水吞没是一场悲剧。| 近义词: submergence, inundation, immersion, overwhelming | 反义词: emergence, surfacing | 词性: 名词

engulfment:/ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt/ | n. |陷入，沉浸|The state of being deeply involved or absorbed in something. |His engulfment in grief made it difficult for him to function. 他沉浸在悲痛中，难以正常生活。| 近义词: absorption, immersion, preoccupation | 反义词: detachment, disinterest | 词性: 名词
