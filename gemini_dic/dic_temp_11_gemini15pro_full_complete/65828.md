evince:/ɪˈvɪns/| vt.|表明；显示；证明|To show or demonstrate clearly; to make evident or manifest.|Her behavior evinced a complete lack of respect. 她的行为表明她完全不尊重别人。|近义词：demonstrate, exhibit, manifest, reveal, show|反义词：conceal, hide, mask|verb

evince:/ɪˈvɪns/| vt.|表明；显示；证明|To reveal the presence of (a quality or feeling).|He evinced great interest in the project. 他对这个项目表现出极大的兴趣。|近义词：express, display, indicate|反义词：suppress, hide|verb
