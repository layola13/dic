franchisement:/ˈfræntʃaɪzmənt/
n.
特许经营权
The act of granting a franchise.
He was granted the franchise for the soft drink company in the region. 他获得了该地区软饮料公司的特许经营权。

n.
选举权
The right to vote.
Women's suffrage, or the right of women to vote, is a relatively recent franchisement in many countries.  在许多国家，妇女选举权或妇女的投票权是相对较新的特许经营权。


n.
公民权; 公民身份
The rights and privileges of a citizen.
Gaining franchisement meant gaining access to a whole new set of rights and responsibilities. 获得公民权意味着获得一套全新的权利和责任。


近义词:suffrage, right, privilege, license, authorization
反义词:disenfranchisement


词性: noun
