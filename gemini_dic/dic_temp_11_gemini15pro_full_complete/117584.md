megalomaniacs:/ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniæks/| n. |夸大狂患者；极度自大的人|A person obsessed with their own power and importance, exhibiting delusions of grandeur.|例句：The megalomaniacs plotted to take over the world. 这些夸大狂密谋要接管世界。

例句：He was a megalomaniac, convinced of his own genius. 他是一个夸大狂，坚信自己是天才。|近义词：egomaniac, narcissist, egotist|反义词:humble person, modest person|词性:noun (plural)

megalomaniac:/ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniæk/| adj. |夸大狂的；极度自大的|Exhibiting delusions of grandeur; obsessed with power.|例句：His megalomaniac schemes threatened the stability of the country. 他那夸大狂的计划威胁到国家的稳定。

例句：She had a megalomaniac desire to control everything. 她有一种控制一切的夸大狂的欲望。|近义词：egomaniacal, narcissistic, egotistic|反义词:humble, modest|词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Greek *megalomania* (μεγαλομανία)  'delusions of grandeur,' from *megalo-* (μέγαλο-) 'great' + *mania* (μανία) 'madness, frenzy.'
