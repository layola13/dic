certificate:/sərˈtɪfɪkət/ | n. |证书，证明；执照|A document that officially states that something is true, especially that a person has achieved a particular standard or passed an exam. |She has a certificate in computer programming. 她有计算机编程证书。|diploma, license, credential | denial, disproof | noun

certificate:/sərˈtɪfɪkət/ | v. |发给…证书|To provide someone with a certificate. |The electrician is certified to work on high-voltage systems.  这位电工获得了高压系统工作的认证。|license, authorize, accredit | revoke, disqualify | verb
