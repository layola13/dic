outcompliment: /ˌaʊtˈkɑːmplɪmənt/
verb
胜过…的恭维; 比…更会恭维
to surpass someone in giving compliments; to compliment someone more effectively or excessively than another person.
She outcomplimented him by praising not only his work but also his charming personality. 她不仅称赞他的工作，还称赞他迷人的个性，胜过了他的恭维。
He tried to outcompliment her, but she had already received the most elaborate praise. 他试图胜过她的恭维，但她已经得到了最详尽的赞美。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel
Antonyms: understate, downplay
词性: 动词
