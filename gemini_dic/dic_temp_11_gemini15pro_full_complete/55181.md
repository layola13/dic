dissent-from:/dɪˈsɛnt frəm/| v.|不同意，持异议|To disagree with a majority opinion or decision.|Many members dissented from the official line. 许多成员对官方路线表示异议.|近义词：differ, disagree, object|反义词:agree, concur, assent|词性:动词

词源: From Latin "dissentire," from "dis-" (apart, away) + "sentire" (to feel).
