jumping:/ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 跳跃的，跳跃 |  The act of jumping; leaping. |  The children were jumping on the trampoline. 孩子们在蹦床上跳跃。 | synonyms: leaping, hopping, bounding | antonyms: landing, descending | adjective/noun

jumping:/ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/ | adj. | 跳跃的 |  Suddenly increasing by a large amount. |  There's been a jumping increase in prices. 价格飞涨。| synonyms: soaring, rocketing, escalating | antonyms: falling, declining, decreasing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *jumpinge, verbal noun from jumpen (“to jump”), from Old French jumper.
