fiasco:/fiˈæskoʊ/ (US), /fiˈæskəʊ/ (UK)| n. |惨败，彻底失败|a complete failure, especially a ludicrous or humiliating one|例句：The party was a complete fiasco.  派对彻底失败了。

例句：The new product launch was a fiasco, with technical glitches and poor marketing. 新产品的发布是一场惨败，技术故障和糟糕的营销都出现了。|近义词：failure, debacle, disaster, flop|反义词：success, triumph|词性:noun
