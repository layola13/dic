metonic:/məˈtɒnɪk/ /məˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj. |  默冬的，默冬周期的 | Relating to the Metonic cycle, a period of 19 years in which the phases of the moon recur on the same dates of the solar year. | The Metonic cycle is used to calculate the date of Easter. 默冬周期用于计算复活节的日期。 | The ancient Greeks used the metonic calendar. 古希腊人使用默冬历。 | 近义词: nineteen-year | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Metōnikos*, from *Metōn*, an Athenian astronomer of the 5th century BC who discovered the cycle.  源自希腊语 *Metōnikos*，来自 *Metōn*，一位公元前 5 世纪的雅典天文学家，他发现了这个周期。
