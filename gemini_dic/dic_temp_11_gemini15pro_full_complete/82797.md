guineans:/ˈɡɪniənz/
n.
几内亚人；几内亚猪
People from Guinea or things related to Guinea. Also, a breed of small rodent often kept as pets.
Guinea pigs are popular pets. 几内亚猪是很受欢迎的宠物。
The Guineans welcomed the visitors warmly. 几内亚人民热情地欢迎了来访者。
synonyms: Guinean people, citizens of Guinea (for people); Guinea pigs, cavies (for animals)
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for people. For the animals, perhaps "wild rodents" as a loose opposite.)
