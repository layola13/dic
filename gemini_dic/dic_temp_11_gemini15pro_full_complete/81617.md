grecian:/ˈɡriːʃən/ /ˈɡriːʃn/| adj. n.|希腊的；希腊式的；希腊人|Relating to Greece, its people, or its culture.  A native or inhabitant of Greece.|例句：The grecian urn was a beautiful piece of art.  这个希腊式骨灰瓮是一件美丽的艺术品。|例句：He studied grecian architecture in college. 他在大学学习希腊建筑。|近义词：Greek, Hellenic|反义词：Roman, barbarian|词性:形容词, 名词

词源:
From Middle French *grecien*, from Latin *Graecianus*, from *Graecus* (“Greek”).
