clarino:/kləˈriːnoʊ/ (US), /kləˈriːnəʊ/ (UK)
n.
高音小号，一种尖锐刺耳的小号；高音小号的声音
A small, high-pitched trumpet or bugle, especially one used in baroque music; the sound of this instrument.
The clarino's piercing tone cut through the orchestral texture. 高音小号刺耳的声音穿透了管弦乐的织体。
The composer wrote a challenging clarino part for the concerto. 作曲家为协奏曲创作了一个具有挑战性的高音小号部分。
近义词:trumpet, bugle
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps something like "bass trumpet" for contrasting sound)
词性:noun
