gastropoda:/ɡæˈstrɒpədə/ | n. | 腹足纲；腹足动物 |  A large class of mollusks, typically having a single spiral shell and a broad foot, including snails and slugs.  | Snails and slugs are both members of the class Gastropoda.  蜗牛和蛞蝓都是腹足纲的成员。 | synonyms: univalve, gastropod | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic class) | noun
