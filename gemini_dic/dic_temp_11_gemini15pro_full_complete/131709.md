nonconductibility:/ˌnɒnkɒndʌktɪˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːndʌkˈtɪvəti/
noun
非导电性，不导电性
The property of being unable to conduct heat, electricity, or sound.
Rubber has high nonconductibility, making it a good insulator. 橡胶具有很高的不导电性，使其成为良好的绝缘体。
nonconductivity, insulation
conductivity, conductance
