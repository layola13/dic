chauvinism: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪzəm/ | n. | 沙文主义，盲目爱国主义 | Excessive or prejudiced loyalty or devotion to one's own group, cause, gender, or nation. |  He displayed blatant chauvinism in his belief that his country was superior to all others. 他坚信自己的国家比所有其他国家都优越，表现出明显的沙文主义。

chauvinism: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪzəm/ | n. |  男性至上主义|  The belief that men are superior to women. | His chauvinism was evident in his refusal to let his wife work.  他的大男子主义体现在他不允许妻子工作。

chauvinism: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪzəm/ | n. | 偏袒，偏执 | Prejudiced belief in the superiority of one's own group. | Her artistic chauvinism led her to dismiss all modern art as rubbish. 她对艺术的偏执使她认为所有现代艺术都是垃圾。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Nicolas Chauvin, a legendary French soldier excessively devoted to Napoleon.  源自尼古拉斯·沙文，一个对拿破仑极度忠诚的传奇法国士兵。


SYNONYMS->
jingoism, nationalism, sexism, bigotry, fanaticism, prejudice, bias


ANTONYMS->
tolerance, open-mindedness, impartiality, objectivity, cosmopolitanism
