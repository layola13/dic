interpervasively:/ˌɪntərpərˈveɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 遍布地，弥漫地 | Extending or spreading throughout something or everywhere. | The theme of love interpervasively influences the entire novel.  爱的主题弥漫性地影响着整部小说。 | Synonyms: pervasively, ubiquitously, extensively, widely, thoroughly | Antonyms: locally, partially, limitedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "inter-" (between, among) + "pervasive" (spreading throughout) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Pervasive" comes from Latin "pervasus," past participle of "pervadere" (to pervade, go through), from "per-" (through) + "vadere" (to go).
