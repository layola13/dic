antireflective:/ˌæntiːrɪˈflektɪv/| adj.|防反射的，抗反射的|Designed to prevent or reduce reflection, especially of light.  It refers to a coating or surface treatment that minimizes the reflection of light.|
The camera lens has an antireflective coating.  相机镜头有防反射涂层。
Antireflective glasses are popular with drivers. 防反光眼镜在司机中很受欢迎。|
近义词:non-reflective, non-glare|反义词:reflective|词性:形容词
