nonprosecutions:/ˌnɒnˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃənz/
n.
不起诉，不予起诉
The act of not prosecuting someone for a crime.
The district attorney explained the reasons for the nonprosecutions in several high-profile cases. 地方检察官解释了在几个备受瞩目的案件中不起诉的原因。
The policy of nonprosecutions for minor drug offenses has been controversial.  对轻微毒品犯罪不予起诉的政策一直存在争议。
Synonym: dismissals, acquittals
Antonym: prosecutions, indictments
noun
