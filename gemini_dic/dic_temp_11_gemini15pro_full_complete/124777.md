what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (US) /wʌt; hwʌt/
|pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

**中文翻译:**  什么，多么，何等

**英文解析:**  Used to ask for information about something or someone.  Can also be used as a determiner before a noun, or as an adverb to emphasize an adjective or adverb.  Can also be used as an interjection expressing surprise or disbelief.

**例句:**

What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What time is it?
现在几点了？

What a beautiful day!
多么美好的一天！

What did you say?
你说了什么？


**近义词:** which, how

**反义词:**  (What doesn't have a direct antonym.  Its meaning depends on context.)

**词性:** pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
