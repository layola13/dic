flutier:/ˈfluːtiər/ (US), /ˈfluːtɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 更像长笛的，更流畅的| More flute-like, more flowing or mellow in tone. | Her voice became flutier as she sang the lullaby. 她的声音在唱摇篮曲时变得更加流畅。| The melody was flutier than I expected.  这旋律比我想象的更像长笛声。 |近义词：melodious, mellifluous, liquid |反义词：harsh, raucous, strident|词性:形容词

flutier: /ˈfluːtiər/ (US), /ˈfluːtɪə(r)/ (UK) | comparative adj. | 更像长笛的; 更圆润的 | More resembling the sound of a flute; more mellow and liquid in tone. | The second movement of the concerto was noticeably flutier than the first. 协奏曲的第二乐章明显比第一乐章更像长笛。|  His tone grew flutier as he reached the higher notes. 当他唱到高音时，他的音调变得更加圆润。|近义词：melodic, sweet, dulcet |反义词：shrill, harsh, grating| 词性:形容词 (比较级)


词源: Derived from "flute" + "-ier" (a comparative suffix).  源自“flute”（长笛）+“-ier”（比较级后缀）。
