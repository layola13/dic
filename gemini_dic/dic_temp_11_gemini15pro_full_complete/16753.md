bartram: /ˈbɑːrtrəm/ | n. |巴特拉姆（姓氏）；巴特拉姆草|  A surname, also the name of a genus of plants in the family Onagraceae (evening primroses).  It is named after the American botanist John Bartram. | John Bartram was a prominent early American botanist. 约翰·巴特拉姆是一位杰出的早期美国植物学家。 | The Bartram's Garden in Philadelphia is a National Historic Landmark. 费城的巴特拉姆花园是国家历史地标。 |  Synonyms: (for the person) botanist, naturalist; (for the plant) evening primrose (loosely) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
