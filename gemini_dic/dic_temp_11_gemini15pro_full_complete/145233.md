overbilled:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪld/| v. |过高收费，超额收费|To charge (someone) more than the proper amount.|例句：The contractor overbilled us for the renovations. 承包商对我们的装修费用多收了。|例句：I was overbilled by $50 on my phone bill. 我的电话账单多收了我50美元。|近义词：overcharge|反义词:undercharge|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + bill.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Bill" comes from Medieval Latin "billa," meaning "a sealed document," later coming to mean a list of charges or an invoice.
