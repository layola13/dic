anathematical:/ˌænəθəˈmætɪkl/
adj.
被诅咒的；可憎的；该诅咒的
Accursed; execrable; that ought to be anathematized.
He uttered an anathematical curse against his betrayer. 他对背叛他的人发出可憎的诅咒。
近义词: accursed, damned, cursed, execrable
反义词: blessed, sacred, holy
词性: adjective
