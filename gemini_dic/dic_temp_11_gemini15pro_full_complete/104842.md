keelhaul:/ˈkiːlhɔːl/| v.| 龙骨吊；严厉惩罚|To drag a person under a ship's keel as punishment or torture.|The captain threatened to keelhaul anyone caught stealing. 船长威胁要对任何被抓到偷窃的人处以龙骨吊。|Synonyms: punish, flog, torture|Antonyms: reward, praise|verb

keelhauling:/ˈkiːlhɔːlɪŋ/| n.| 龙骨吊；严厉惩罚|The act of dragging a person under a ship's keel.|Keelhauling was a common punishment in the 17th century. 龙骨吊是17世纪的一种常见惩罚。|Synonyms: punishment, torture|Antonyms: reward, praise|noun
