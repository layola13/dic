abray:/əˈbreɪ/| v.|惊吓；使惊恐；|To startle or frighten, especially by a sudden noise or sight.|The sudden loud noise abrayed the horses.|近义词：frighten, startle, scare, alarm, terrify|反义词：calm, soothe, comfort|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French *esbraier, from es- (out) + braire (to cry, to bray).  Ultimately from Latin *brācare, a frequentative of *brāre, related to Greek βράχω (brakhō, “I roar, I crash”).
