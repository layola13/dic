一般现在时 (Simple Present):
O homem trabalha todos os dias. = The man works every day.
Os homens jogam futebol. = The men play soccer.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
O homem está trabalhando agora. = The man is working now.
Os homens estão jogando futebol agora. = The men are playing soccer now.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
O homem já trabalhou hoje. = The man has already worked today. (This tense doesn't translate directly to Portuguese in the same way, this is a close equivalent using "já")
Os homens já jogaram futebol hoje. = The men have already played soccer today. (Same as above)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):  (This tense doesn't have a direct equivalent in Portuguese)
O homem está trabalhando há algumas horas. = The man has been working for a few hours. (This uses the present continuous with duration)
Os homens estão jogando futebol há uma hora. = The men have been playing soccer for an hour. (Same as above)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
O homem trabalhou ontem. = The man worked yesterday.
Os homens jogaram futebol ontem. = The men played soccer yesterday.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
O homem estava trabalhando quando cheguei. = The man was working when I arrived.
Os homens estavam jogando futebol quando choveu. = The men were playing soccer when it rained.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
O homem já tinha trabalhado quando cheguei. = The man had already worked when I arrived.
Os homens já tinham jogado futebol quando anoiteceu. = The men had already played soccer when it got dark.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous): (Doesn't have a direct equivalent)
O homem estava trabalhando há horas antes de eu chegar. = The man had been working for hours before I arrived. (Using past continuous with duration)
Os homens estavam jogando futebol há horas antes da chuva. = The men had been playing soccer for hours before the rain. (Same as above)


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
O homem trabalhará amanhã. = The man will work tomorrow.
Os homens jogarão futebol amanhã. = The men will play soccer tomorrow.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
O homem estará trabalhando amanhã a esta hora. = The man will be working tomorrow at this time.
Os homens estarão jogando futebol amanhã a esta hora. = The men will be playing soccer tomorrow at this time.


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
O homem terá trabalhado até amanhã. = The man will have worked by tomorrow.
Os homens terão jogado futebol até amanhã. = The men will have played soccer by tomorrow.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): (Doesn't have a direct equivalent)
O homem estará trabalhando há dez anos até lá. = The man will have been working for ten years by then. (Using future continuous with duration)
Os homens estarão jogando futebol há anos até lá. = The men will have been playing soccer for years by then. (Same as above)
