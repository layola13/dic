corol:/ˈkɒrəl/ (US) /ˈkɒrɒl/ (UK)| n.|花冠|The petals of a flower, collectively; the inner envelope of the perianth of a flower, composed of petals, usually of delicate texture and bright colors.|The corol of the rose was a deep crimson. 玫瑰的花冠是深红色的。|近义词：corolla|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps calyx - the outer protective layer)|noun
