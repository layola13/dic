coverage: /ˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|覆盖; 报道; 保险(范围)|The extent to which something is covered.  The reporting of news and current events.|例句：The news coverage of the election was extensive.  这次选举的新闻报道非常广泛。|近义词：reporting, reporting, insurance, protection|反义词:exposure, vulnerability|词性:noun

coverage: /ˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|覆盖范围; 涉及范围|The range or scope of something.|例句：The course offers broad coverage of the subject. 这门课程涵盖了该主题的广泛内容。|近义词：scope, range, extent|反义词:limitation, restriction|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cover" + "-age".  "Cover" comes from Old French "covrir," from Latin "cooperire" meaning "to cover completely."  The suffix "-age" forms nouns denoting an action, process, or result.
