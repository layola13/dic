heritability:/ˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 遗传性，遗传力 | The extent to which variation in a trait within a population is due to genetic differences rather than environmental factors. | The heritability of intelligence is a complex and controversial topic. 智力的遗传性是一个复杂且有争议的话题。|  The heritability of height is estimated to be around 80%. 身高的遗传力估计约为 80%。 | synonyms:  inheritability,  genetic variance,  genetic determination | antonyms: environmental variance | noun
