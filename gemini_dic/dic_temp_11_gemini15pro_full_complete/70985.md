firestone:/ˈfaɪərstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈfaɪrˌstoʊn/ (US)| n.| 火石；燧石；（Firestone）凡士通（轮胎品牌）|A piece of hard rock such as flint that produces sparks when struck with steel, used especially in the past for lighting fires; Also a brand name of tires.|例句：He struck the firestone to make a spark. 他敲击火石制造火花。

例句：I need new Firestone tires for my truck. 我的卡车需要新的凡士通轮胎。|近义词：flint, flintstone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fȳrstan, from fȳr "fire" + stan "stone."  Meaning "tire" is a proprietary name.
