crucificial: /ˌkruːsəˈfɪʃəl/ | adj. | 极其重要的，决定性的；磨难的，痛苦的 | Relating to or resembling crucifixion; extremely important or decisive; causing intense suffering or agony. |  This was a crucificial moment in his life, determining his future path. 这是他人生中一个至关重要的时刻，决定了他未来的道路。 |  The loss of his job was a crucificial blow to his self-esteem. 失去工作对他自尊心是一个沉重的打击。 | 近义词: vital, critical, decisive, agonizing, torturous | 反义词: unimportant, trivial, insignificant, painless | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *crucificialis, from Latin crucifixus (past participle of crucifigere, "to crucify"), from crux ("cross") + figere ("to fix, fasten").
