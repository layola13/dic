aspiringness:/əˈspaɪərɪŋnɪs/
n.
渴望，抱负，志向
The state of being aspiring; ambition.
He showed great aspiringness to become a successful entrepreneur. 他展现出成为一名成功企业家的强烈愿望。
Near Synonyms: ambition, aspiration, drive, eagerness, zeal
Antonyms: apathy, indifference, laziness, passivity
noun
