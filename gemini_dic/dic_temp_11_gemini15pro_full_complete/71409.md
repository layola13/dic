flagworm:/ˈflæɡwɜːrm/
noun
旗杆虫; 可能是指一种寄生虫，但非正式或科学术语
A hypothetical or fictional worm associated with flagpoles. There's no established scientific or common usage of this word. It likely refers to an imagined parasite or worm living in or on a flagpole.
Example:  "The old sailor told tales of the dreaded flagworm that infested the ship's mast." 老水手讲述了可怕的旗杆虫侵扰船桅的故事。

Example: "He jokingly blamed the tattered flag on a flagworm." 他开玩笑地把破烂的旗帜归咎于旗杆虫。
