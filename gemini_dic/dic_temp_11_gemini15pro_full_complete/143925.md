outpromises:/ˌaʊtˈprɑːmɪsɪz/
verb
做出比…更多的承诺;许诺过多
to make more or greater promises than (someone or something else)
The candidate outpromised his opponent, pledging lower taxes and better healthcare. 这位候选人比他的对手做出了更多承诺，保证降低税收和改善医疗保健。
outbid, outdo, surpass
underdeliver
v.
