fulnesses:/ˈfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
饱满，充实；丰富
The state of being full; abundance.
The garden was bursting with the fullness of summer.  花园里充满了夏日的繁茂。
abundance, completeness, plenitude, richness
emptiness, lack, scarcity
noun
