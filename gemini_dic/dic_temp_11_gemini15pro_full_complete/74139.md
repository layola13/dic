foundationless:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənləs/
adjective
无基础的，没有根据的
lacking a foundation; groundless; unsubstantiated
His argument was completely foundationless and based on hearsay. 他的论点完全没有根据，只是基于传闻。
近义词:baseless, groundless, unsubstantiated, unfounded
反义词:founded, grounded, substantiated, well-founded
词性:adjective
