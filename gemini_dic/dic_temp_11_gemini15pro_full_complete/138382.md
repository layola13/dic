notorhizal:/ˌnɒtəˈraɪzəl/| adj.|非菌根的|Not forming a mycorrhiza; lacking mycorrhizae.  A notorhizal plant does not have a symbiotic relationship with fungi in its roots.|Some plants, like members of the Brassicaceae family, are typically notorhizal.  十字花科的一些植物通常是非菌根的。|non-mycorrhizal|mycorrhizal|adjective
