equippers:/ɪˈkwɪpərz/
n.
装备者，配备者
People or entities that provide equipment.  Those who furnish others with what they need for a particular purpose.
The equippers provided the explorers with the latest gear.  装备者为探险者提供了最新的装备。
outfitters, suppliers, providers
dispossessors, removers


ETYMOLOGY->
From equip + -er + -s.
From the verb "equip," meaning to furnish or provide with what is needed, plus the suffix "-er" denoting a person who performs the action, and the plural suffix "-s." Ultimately from Old French "esquiper" (to embark, to equip a ship), of Germanic origin.
