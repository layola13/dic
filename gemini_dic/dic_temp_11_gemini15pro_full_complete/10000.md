aphtha:/ˈæfθə/ (US), /ˈæpθə/ (UK)| n. |阿弗他溃疡，口腔溃疡|A small, shallow ulcer that appears in the mouth or on the tongue, often white or yellowish in the center with a red border.  It is also called a canker sore.|例句：I have a painful aphtha on the inside of my cheek. 我的脸颊内侧有一个疼痛的口腔溃疡。

例句：Aphthae can be caused by stress, hormonal changes, or certain foods.  压力、荷尔蒙变化或某些食物都可能引起口腔溃疡。

近义词：canker sore, mouth ulcer|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
