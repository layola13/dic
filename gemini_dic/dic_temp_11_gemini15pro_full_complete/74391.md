framers:/ˈfreɪmərz/| n.| 制定者，创立者，构架者|Those who create or establish something, especially a system of rules or laws.  The framers of the Constitution were visionaries. |例句：The framers of the Constitution carefully considered the balance of power between the branches of government.  宪法的制定者仔细考虑了政府部门之间的权力平衡。

例句：The framers of the new policy hoped it would encourage economic growth. 新政策的制定者希望它能鼓励经济增长。|近义词：authors, creators, architects, designers, founders|反义词: destroyers, demolishers|词性:名词
