outsmell:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛl/
verb
胜过…的气味; 比…的气味更好闻
to have a better smell than something or someone else.
例句：The roses outsmell the lilies. 玫瑰花的香味胜过百合花。
例句：This perfume outsmells all the others. 这款香水比其他所有香水都好闻。
近义词：out-perfume, surpass in fragrance
反义词：be outsmelled by, have a worse smell than
词性：v.
