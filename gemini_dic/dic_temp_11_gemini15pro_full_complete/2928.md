advisory:/ədˈvaɪzəri/ | adj. n. | 咨询的，顾问的；报告；公告 | Giving advice; containing advice. A report or announcement that contains advice, information, or a warning. | The government issued a travel advisory warning citizens about the risks of visiting the region. 政府发布了旅行警告，提醒公民注意访问该地区的风险。|  The advisory board recommended against the proposed merger. 顾问委员会建议反对拟议的合并。 | 近义词: consultative, cautionary, warning, bulletin, notice | 反义词: mandatory, compulsory | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From the Late Latin *advisorius*, from *advis-*, past participle stem of *advidere* "to look at, consider," from *ad-* "to" + *videre* "to see."
