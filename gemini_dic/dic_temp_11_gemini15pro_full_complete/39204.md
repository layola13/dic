congested:/kənˈdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. | 拥挤的，堵塞的 | Overcrowded or blocked, especially with traffic or people. | The roads are very congested during rush hour.  高峰时段道路非常拥堵。| synonyms: overcrowded, blocked, jammed, packed | antonyms: clear, empty, uncongested | adjective

congested:/kənˈdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. | （身体部位）充血的 | (of a body part) abnormally full of blood; over congested. |  He had a congested nose and chest. 他鼻子和胸部充血。| synonyms: blocked, stuffy, stuffed-up | antonyms: clear | adjective
