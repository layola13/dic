monoglacialism:/ˌmɒnəˈɡleɪʃɪəlɪzəm/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡleɪʃɪəlɪzəm/
n.
单冰期论; 一次冰期论
The theory that there was only one ice age during the Pleistocene epoch.
The geologist refuted monoglacialism, citing evidence of multiple glacial advances and retreats.  这位地质学家驳斥了单冰期论，引用了多次冰川前进和后退的证据。
Near Antonyms: polyglacialism

Etymology:
mono- (one) + glacial + -ism (doctrine, belief)
