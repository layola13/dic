dose: /doʊs/ (US) /dəʊs/ (UK)| n. vt.|剂量; 一服; 一剂|A measured quantity of medicine, drug, or radiation administered or recommended to be taken at one time.|The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics. 医生开了剂量的抗生素。|近义词：amount, quantity, portion|反义词: overdose|noun
dose: /doʊs/ (US) /dəʊs/ (UK)| vt.|给…服药|To administer a dose of medicine or drug to.|The nurse dosed the patient with painkillers. 护士给病人服用了止痛药。|近义词：administer, give|反义词: withhold|verb
