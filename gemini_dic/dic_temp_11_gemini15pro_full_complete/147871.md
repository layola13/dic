overreacher:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃər/| n.|过分追求者；不自量力的人；野心勃勃的人|Someone who tries to reach too far, either literally or figuratively, especially by aiming for more than they are capable of achieving.|He was an overreacher who ultimately failed because of his excessive ambition. 他是一个野心勃勃的人，最终因为过度的野心而失败了。|近义词：ambitious, aspiring, presumptuous|反义词：underachiever, modest, humble|词性: noun

overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/| v.|过度伸展；不自量力；做得过头|To try to do more than you are able to do; to fail by reaching too far.|The company overreached itself by expanding too quickly and went bankrupt.  这家公司扩张过快，不自量力，最终破产了。|近义词：overextend, overstrain, exceed|反义词：underestimate, underachieve|词性: verb

Etymology:
From over- + reach.  "Over-" signifies "too much" or "beyond," while "reach" refers to extending or striving for something.  The combined meaning implies exceeding one's grasp or capabilities.
