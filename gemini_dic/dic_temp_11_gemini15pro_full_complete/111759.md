lodestone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n.|天然磁石，吸引人的东西|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  Something that strongly attracts people or things.|The lodestone attracted the iron filings. 这块天然磁石吸引了铁屑。|Synonyms: magnet, attraction, draw, lure|Antonyms: repellent, deterrent|noun
