autophagy:/ɔːˈtɒfədʒi/ /ɑːˈtɑːfədʒi/| n. |细胞自噬|The natural process by which cells break down and recycle their own components.  细胞通过分解和循环利用自身成分的自然过程。|例句：Autophagy plays a crucial role in maintaining cellular health. 细胞自噬在维持细胞健康方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Researchers are studying the role of autophagy in preventing cancer. 研究人员正在研究细胞自噬在预防癌症中的作用。|近义词：self-digestion, self-cannibalization|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
