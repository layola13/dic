lifebloods:/ˈlaɪfblʌdz/
n.
命脉，生命线，不可缺少的东西
The essential things that give something strength and vitality.
Tourism is the lifeblood of the local economy. 旅游业是当地经济的命脉。
essentials, necessities, mainstays
impediments, hindrances
noun
