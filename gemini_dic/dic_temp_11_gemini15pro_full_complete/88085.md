highnesses:/ˈhaɪnəsɪz/| n.|殿下们；高度，高处（highness的复数）|A term of address for royalty, especially plural of highness. 用于对皇室成员的称呼，尤指highness的复数形式。|Their Highnesses the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge attended the ceremony. 剑桥公爵和公爵夫人殿下出席了仪式。|The highnesses graced the occasion with their presence.  殿下们的光临使这次活动增色不少。|近义词：majesties, excellencies|反义词：lowliness, inferiority|词性:名词
