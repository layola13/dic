efficacious:/ˌefɪˈkeɪʃəs/ | adj. |有效的，灵验的 | Producing the desired result or effect. |  The new marketing campaign proved efficacious, significantly boosting sales.  新的营销活动证明是有效的，大大提高了销售额。| synonyms: productive, efficient | antonyms: inefficient, unproductive | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *efficāc-, stem of efficāx "effective, effectual," from Latin efficere "to work out, accomplish, produce," from ex- "out" + facere "to make, do."
