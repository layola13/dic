leninists:/ˈlɛnɪnɪsts/
noun
列宁主义者
Followers of the political theories of Vladimir Lenin, especially those advocating the creation of a vanguard party to lead the revolution.
Leninists believe in a strong centralized state and a planned economy. 列宁主义者相信强大的中央集权国家和计划经济。
The leninists formed a powerful faction within the socialist movement.  列宁主义者在社会主义运动中形成了一个强大的派系。
synonyms: communists, Marxists, Bolsheviks, revolutionaries
antonyms: capitalists, conservatives, liberals
