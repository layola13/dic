lenins:/ˈlɛnɪnz/| n.|列宁们的; 列宁的 (复数形式，通常指列宁的著作、思想或追随者)|Lenin's refers to things belonging to or associated with Vladimir Lenin, the leader of the Russian Revolution. It's often used in a plural form to denote his works, ideas, or followers.|例句：Lenin's writings inspired many revolutionaries. 列宁的著作激励了许多革命者。

|例句：Many young revolutionaries studied Lenin's theories diligently. 许多年轻的革命者勤奋地学习列宁的理论。

|近义词：Leninist, Bolshevik|反义词: Tsarist, Menshevik|词性:Noun (名词)
