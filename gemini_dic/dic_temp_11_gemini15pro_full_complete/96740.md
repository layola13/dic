ineligible:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
不合格的，没有资格的
Not qualified or entitled to something.
He was declared ineligible to compete because of his age. 他因年龄原因被宣布没有参赛资格。
ineligible for government funding. 没有资格获得政府资助。
synonym: disqualified, unsuitable, unentitled
antonym: eligible, qualified, suitable
词性: 形容词
