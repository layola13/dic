decantation:/ˌdiːkænˈteɪʃn/
noun
倾析，滗析
The process of gently pouring a liquid from one container into another, leaving sediment or other solids behind.
The wine underwent decantation before serving to remove any sediment.  这瓶酒在上桌前经过了倾析，去除了沉淀物。
decantation:/ˌdiːkænˈteɪʃn/
noun
(喻)提纯,精华
A carefully selected group or part.
This anthology represents a decantation of the best poetry of the last decade. 这本诗集是过去十年最佳诗歌的精华。


近义词:pouring, separation, clarification
反义词:mixing, agitation
