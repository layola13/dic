fissipation:/fɪsɪˈpeɪʃən/
n.
分裂，分散，浪费
the act of dissipating or dispersing something; wasteful expenditure or consumption.
The fissipation of the family's wealth on frivolous purchases left them in poverty.  这个家庭在轻浮的购物上挥霍财富，使他们陷入贫困。
dispersal, scattering, dissipation, waste, squandering
accumulation, gathering, conservation
noun
