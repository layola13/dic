edulcoration:/ɪˌdʌlkəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 甜化；净化；精制 | The process of making something sweeter or purer, or removing impurities. | The edulcoration of the water made it safe to drink.  水经过净化后可以安全饮用。 | 近义词: purification, refinement, sweetening | 反义词: contamination, pollution | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *ēdūlcōrātiōn- (stem of ēdūlcōrātiō), from Latin ēdūlcōrātus, past participle of ēdūlcōrāre ("to sweeten, to make pleasant"), from ē- (variant of ex- "out") + dūlcōrāre ("to make sweet"), from dūlcis ("sweet").
