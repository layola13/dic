fracture:/ˈfræktʃər/ (US) /ˈfræktʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|骨折；断裂；破裂|The act or process of breaking or the state of being broken, especially of a bone or hard material.  A break, crack, or rupture.|
例句：He suffered a fracture of the skull. 他颅骨骨折。
例句：The earthquake caused fractures in the earth's crust. 地震导致地壳断裂。
近义词：break, crack, rupture, split, shatter|
反义词：mend, repair, heal|
词性：noun, verb
