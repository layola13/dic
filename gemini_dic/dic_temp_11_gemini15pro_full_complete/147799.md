overproud:/ˈoʊvərpraʊd/| adj.| 过于骄傲的，自负的 | Excessively proud; having or showing too much pride in oneself or one's accomplishments.  Derived from "over" (excessively) + "proud" (feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of one's own achievements, qualities, or possessions or those of someone with whom one is closely associated). | He was overproud of his new car and boasted about it constantly. 他对自己的新车过于骄傲，不断吹嘘。|  She was overproud of her son's achievements and constantly bragged about them to her friends. 她对儿子的成就过于骄傲，经常向朋友吹嘘。 | 近义词：arrogant, haughty, conceited, vain | 反义词：humble, modest | 词性: adjective
