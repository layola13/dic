funk:/fʌŋk/
noun|恐慌，恐惧；怯懦；恶臭；懦夫|A state of fear, anxiety, or depression; cowardice; a strong unpleasant smell.|He was in a funk about the upcoming exam. 他对即将到来的考试感到恐慌。|Near synonyms: panic, fear, dread, terror, anxiety, apprehension|Antonyms: courage, bravery, confidence|n.

funk:/fʌŋk/
verb|逃避，畏缩；使沮丧|To avoid or shrink from a duty or responsibility out of fear; to cause to be depressed.|He funked the challenge of leading the project. 他因害怕而逃避了领导这个项目的挑战。|Near synonyms: evade, avoid, shirk, dodge, duck|Antonyms: confront, face, embrace|v.
