flak-vests:/ˈflæk vɛsts/| n.|防弹衣|A protective vest, usually made of layers of fabric or other materials, designed to stop or slow down bullets or other projectiles.  It is worn to protect the torso from injury.|The soldiers were equipped with flak-vests before entering the combat zone. 士兵们在进入战区前都穿上了防弹衣。

|Synonyms: bulletproof vests, body armor|Antonyms: None|Noun

flak-jacket:/ˈflæk ˌdʒækɪt/| n.|防弹衣|A protective jacket, similar to a flak-vest, designed to protect the torso from bullets or other projectiles.|The journalist wore a flak-jacket while reporting from the war-torn region. 记者在战乱地区报道时穿了防弹衣。

|Synonyms: bulletproof jacket, body armor|Antonyms: None|Noun

词源： flak 来自德语单词 Fliegerabwehrkanone，意思是“防空炮”。vest 来自古法语 veste。 flak-vest 指的是最初设计用来抵御炮弹碎片的背心，后来演变为指能够抵御子弹和其他弹丸的背心。
