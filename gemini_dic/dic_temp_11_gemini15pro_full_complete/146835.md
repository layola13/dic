overincline:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪn/| n.|过度倾斜|Excessive inclination or slope.|The overincline of the ramp made it difficult for wheelchairs.|近义词：excessive tilt, steep slope|反义词: level ground, flat surface|词性:名词
