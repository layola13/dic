nontortuous:/ˌnɒnˈtɔːrtʃuəs/
adjective
非曲折的；直接的
Not tortuous; straight or direct.
The highway was nontortuous, allowing for quick travel. 这条高速公路笔直，可以快速通行。
The lawyer's explanation was nontortuous and easy to understand. 律师的解释简单明了，易于理解。
近义词:straight, direct, straightforward
反义词:tortuous, winding, convoluted
词性:adjective
