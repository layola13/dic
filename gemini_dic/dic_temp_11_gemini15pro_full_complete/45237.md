culpatory: /ˈkʌlpətɔːri/
adjective
有罪的；应受谴责的
implying or assigning blame
例句：The culpatory evidence was overwhelming.  大量的证据表明他有罪。

例句：His culpatory silence spoke volumes. 他保持沉默，这表明他有罪。

近义词：incriminating, accusatory, blaming
反义词：exculpatory, exonerating, absolving
词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *culpatorius, from Latin culpator (“accuser, blamer”), from culpare (“to blame”), from culpa (“fault, blame”).
