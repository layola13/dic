finisher:/ˈfɪnɪʃər/| n.| 完成者; 终结者; 整理工; (尤指马拉松比赛的)最后阶段选手; (拳击)终结拳; 涂饰工; (竞赛)最后阶段; 抛光机; 肥育(动物); 定影液|A finisher is someone or something that finishes something, especially a race or other competition.|The marathon finisher crossed the line exhausted but elated.  马拉松选手精疲力竭但兴高采烈地越过了终点线。|He delivered a powerful finisher in the final round, knocking out his opponent.  他在最后一轮打出了一记强力终结拳，击倒了对手。|Synonyms: completer, concluder, ender|Antonyms: starter, beginner|noun
