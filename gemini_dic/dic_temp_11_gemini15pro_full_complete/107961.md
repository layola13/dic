lasciviousness:/ləˈsɪviəsnəs/
|n.|好色，淫荡|Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness.  The state of being lascivious; lewdness.|His lasciviousness was evident in his constant staring and suggestive comments. 他的好色表现在他不断的盯着别人看和带有暗示性的评论。|近义词：lust, lechery, lewdness, salaciousness|反义词：chastity, purity, decency|词性: noun

词源:
From Middle English *lasciviousnes, from Old French lasciviouste, from Late Latin lasciviosus (“lustful”) + -te. Ultimately from Latin lascivus (“loose, wanton, playful”) + -osus.
