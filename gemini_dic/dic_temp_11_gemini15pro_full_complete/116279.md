massest:/ˈmæsɪst/| adj.|最大的，质量最多的|(superlative of) Having the greatest mass.  This word is archaic and rarely used.  "Most massive" is generally preferred. |例句：Of all the planets in our solar system, Jupiter is the massest.  在我们太阳系的所有行星中，木星质量最大。

例句：He claimed to have seen the massest tree in the world. 他声称见过世界上最大的树。|近义词：most massive, largest, heaviest, bulkiest|反义词：smallest, lightest, tiniest|词性:形容词
