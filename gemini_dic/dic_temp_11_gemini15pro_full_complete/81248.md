grangerite:/ˈɡreɪndʒəˌraɪt/| n. |菱锰矿|A pinkish-brown manganese silicate mineral, (Mn,Fe)7Si5O15(OH)2. It is named after the American geologist Walter Granger (1872-1941).|Grangerite is often found in association with other manganese minerals. 菱锰矿经常与其他锰矿物伴生。|The museum's collection includes a rare specimen of grangerite.  博物馆的收藏包含一个稀有的菱锰矿标本。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral name)|Antonyms: None|Noun
