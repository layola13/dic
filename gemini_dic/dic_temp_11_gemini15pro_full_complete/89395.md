homopterous:/hɒˈmɒptərəs/ | adj. | 同翅目的 | Belonging or pertaining to the Homoptera, an order of insects (now generally considered a suborder of Hemiptera) characterized by sucking mouthparts, and including cicadas, aphids, leafhoppers, and scale insects. |  The homopterous insects are known for their plant-feeding habits. 同翅目昆虫以其以植物为食的习性而闻名。|  synonym: hemipteran (in broader sense) | antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it is a taxonomic classification) | adjective
