neoplastic:/ˌniːəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
新生物的；肿瘤的
Relating to or characteristic of neoplasia or neoplasms.  Pertaining to a new, abnormal growth of tissue; pertaining to or like a neoplasm.
例句：Neoplastic diseases, such as cancer, are a leading cause of death. 新生物疾病，例如癌症，是主要的死亡原因。

例句：The doctor discovered a neoplastic growth on the patient's lung. 医生在病人的肺部发现了一个肿瘤。
近义词：cancerous, tumorous, malignant
反义词：benign, healthy
词性：adjective
