habitation:/ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
居住；住所；居住地
the act of living in a place; a place to live in
例句：The cave provided habitation for the early humans. 这个洞穴为早期人类提供了住所。

例句：The dilapidated building was no longer fit for human habitation. 这栋破旧的建筑不再适合人类居住。

近义词：residence, dwelling, abode, domicile
反义词：abandonment, desertion
