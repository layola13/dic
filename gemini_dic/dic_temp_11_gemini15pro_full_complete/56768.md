He was arrested for selling dope.  他因贩卖毒品而被捕。

The athletes were accused of using performance-enhancing dope.  这些运动员被指控使用兴奋剂。

He gave me the inside dope on the company merger. 他给了我公司合并的内幕消息。

Don't be such a dope! 别这么傻！

They doped the racehorses to make them run faster. 他们给赛马服用兴奋剂，让它们跑得更快。

This new phone is dope! 这款新手机太棒了！

近义词：drug, narcotic, information, tip, fool, idiot, excellent, awesome, cool|
反义词：smart, intelligent, bad, terrible|
词性：名词；形容词；动词|
