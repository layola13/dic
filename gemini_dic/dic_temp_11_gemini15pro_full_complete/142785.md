oughtness:/ˈɔːtnəs/
n.|应然性；义务性；道义；当然|The state or quality of being obligatory; moral obligation.|He felt a strong sense of oughtness to help his family. 他感到一种强烈的义务感要帮助他的家人。|近义词：duty, obligation, responsibility|反义词：option, choice|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ought (past tense of owe) + -ness.  The word "ought" itself comes from Old English *āhte, the past tense of āgan "to own, possess, owe." The sense shifted from owing a debt to owing a duty.
