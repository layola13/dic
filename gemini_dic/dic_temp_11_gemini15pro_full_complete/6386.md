amphigen:/ˈæmfɪdʒɛn/ | n. |两性生殖体; 两性配子;雌雄同体|An organism or cell that produces both male and female gametes.|The amphigen reproduced both sperm and eggs.  两性生殖体同时产生精子和卵子。| synonym: hermaphrodite, monoecious | antonym: dioecious | noun

amphigenic:/ˌæmfɪˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |两性的; 雌雄同体的|Produced by the union of male and female gametes; bisexual.|The amphigenic plant self-pollinated. 两性植物自花授粉。| synonym: bisexual, hermaphroditic | antonym: unisexual | adjective
