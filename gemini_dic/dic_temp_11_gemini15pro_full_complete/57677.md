drawings:/ˈdrɔːɪŋz/
noun
图画，图纸，素描
A picture or diagram made with a pencil, pen, or crayon rather than paint.
The architect showed us the drawings for the new building.  建筑师给我们看了新大楼的图纸。
sketches, illustrations, diagrams, pictures
