glazers:/ˈɡleɪzərz/| n.|上釉工人，玻璃工，玻璃安装工|A person whose job is glazing, especially a window glazier. |例句：The glazers installed the new windows in the skyscraper.  玻璃安装工们在大楼里安装了新的窗户。

例句：The glazers carefully applied the final coat of glaze to the pottery. 上釉工仔细地给陶器上了最后一层釉。|近义词：glassworkers, window fitters|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
