chart:/tʃɑːrt/ (US), /tʃɑːt/ (UK)| n. v. |图表; 海图; 排行榜|A graphical representation of data, often using lines, bars, or other visual elements to show relationships or trends.  Also, a map for navigation, especially at sea.
