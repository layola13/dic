headstone:/ˈhedˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈhedˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|墓碑|A slab of stone set up at the head of a grave, usually bearing an inscription.|例句：The headstone was weathered and difficult to read. 墓碑被风化了，难以辨认。

例句：She laid flowers at the foot of her mother's headstone. 她在她母亲的墓碑前献上鲜花。

近义词：gravestone, tombstone, monument|反义词: None|词性:noun
