outbuildings:/ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋz/
n.
附属建筑；外屋
A structure detached from but associated with a main building, such as a shed or barn.
The outbuildings included a stable, a workshop, and a woodshed. 附属建筑包括一个马厩、一个车间和一个柴房。

近义词: annex, extension, outhouse, shed, barn
反义词: main building
