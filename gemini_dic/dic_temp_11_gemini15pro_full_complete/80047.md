goad:/ɡəʊd/| n. vt.|刺激; 刺激物; 尖棒|Something that encourages, urges, or drives someone to do something; a pointed stick used to prod animals.|The promise of a promotion was a goad to work harder.  升职的承诺是努力工作的动力。|He goaded the bull with a sharp stick. 他用尖棍刺激公牛。|近义词:spur, incentive, prod, stimulus|反义词:deterrent, discouragement|noun, verb


goads:/ɡəʊdz/| n.|刺激; 刺激物; 尖棒（goad的复数形式）|The plural form of "goad," referring to multiple things that encourage, urge, or drive someone to do something, or multiple pointed sticks used to prod animals.|The constant goads of his conscience made him confess.  他良心的不断谴责使他坦白。|The farmer used goads to keep the oxen moving.  农夫用尖棒驱赶牛前进。|近义词: spurs, incentives, prods, stimuli|反义词: deterrents, discouragements|noun
