artoid:/ˈɑːrtɔɪd/| adj.|似艺术的，仿造艺术品的|Resembling or imitating art, but lacking originality or depth; artificial, contrived.  Appearing like art, but not genuine art. |The museum shop sold many artoid reproductions of famous paintings.  博物馆的商店出售许多名画的仿制品。|The decorations were cheap and artoid, looking mass-produced rather than handcrafted. 装饰品廉价且仿造艺术品，看起来像是批量生产的，而不是手工制作的。|Near synonyms:  artificial, pseudo-artistic, imitation, ersatz,  factitious, spurious, sham|Antonyms:  genuine, authentic, original, real|Adjective
