crutch: /krʌtʃ/ | n. |拐杖; 支柱; 依靠; |A support, typically one of a pair, used by a person with an injury or disability to assist in walking.  Something that provides support or assistance but may also become a dependency.| He walked with the aid of crutches after breaking his leg. 他腿断了之后拄着拐杖走路。| synonyms: support, prop, aid, staff | antonyms: hindrance, obstacle | noun

crutch: /krʌtʃ/ | v. |支撑; 扶持|To support with a crutch. To prop up, support, or sustain. |  He crutched the injured bird's wing with a splint. 他用夹板支撑着受伤鸟儿的翅膀。 | synonyms: support, prop, brace | antonyms: weaken, undermine | verb
