lecherousness:/ˈletʃərəsnəs/ |n. |好色，淫荡 |Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness.  |His lecherousness was evident in his constant staring at women. 他的好色表现在他不断地盯着女人看。|Synonyms: lust, lasciviousness, lewdness, salaciousness |Antonyms: chastity, purity, decency | Noun

Etymology:
From lecherous + -ness.  Lecherous comes from Middle English *lecherous*, from Old French *lechier* "to lick," from Latin *lingere* "to lick, linger."
