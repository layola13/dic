foresleeve: /ˈfɔːrsliːv/
n.
前袖，护袖
The part of a sleeve that covers the forearm.
Her foresleeves were embroidered with intricate designs. 她的前袖绣有复杂的图案。

The knight polished the metal foresleeves of his armor. 骑士擦亮了他盔甲上的金属护袖。

近义词: sleeve, cuff
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "sleeveless")
