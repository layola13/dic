overpotency: /ˌoʊvərˈpoʊtənsi/ | n. | 过效力；过强；超效；药力过强 |  Excessive potency or strength, especially of a drug or other chemical substance.  It refers to a situation where a substance has a greater effect than intended or expected.  | The overpotency of the new medication led to several patients experiencing adverse side effects.  这种新药的药效过强导致几名患者出现不良副作用。 | Synonyms: overdose, excess strength, hyperpotency | Antonyms: underpotency, weakness, ineffectiveness | noun

overpotency: /ˌoʊvərˈpoʊtənsi/ | adj. (less common) | 过效力的；过强的 | Having excessive potency or strength. | The overpotency mixture caused the reaction to proceed too quickly.  这个过强混合物导致反应进行得太快。 | Synonyms: excessively potent, overstrong | Antonyms: underpotent, weak, ineffective | adjective
