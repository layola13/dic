conservant:/kənˈsɜːrvənt/| n. adj.|防腐剂，保存剂；防腐的，有保存力的|A substance used to preserve something from decay.|This jam contains no artificial preservatives.  这种果酱不含人工防腐剂。|近义词：preservative, additive|反义词:decay agent, decomposer|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *conservans*, present participle of *conservare* "to preserve," from com- "together" + servare "to keep, guard."
