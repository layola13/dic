numbedness:/ˈnʌmdnəs/| n.|麻木，麻痹|A state of reduced sensation or feeling, often caused by cold, pressure, or injury.|The numbness in my fingers made it difficult to button my coat. 我的手指麻木了，很难扣上外套扣子。|近义词：insensibility, paralysis, torpor|反义词：sensitivity, feeling|词性:名词

词源: From numb + -ness.  "numb" comes from Old English *numen, past participle of *niman "to take," related to German nehmen "to take."  The original meaning was "taken away, seized," as by cold or fear, hence "benumbed."
