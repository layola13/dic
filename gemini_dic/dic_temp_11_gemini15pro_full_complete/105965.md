klutziest: /ˈklʌtsiɪst/ | adj. | 最笨拙的 | superlative form of klutzy; most awkward and clumsy. |  He's the klutziest person I've ever met, he always trips over his own feet. 他是我见过最笨拙的人，总是被自己的脚绊倒。|  She's so klutziest that she spilled coffee all over herself. 她笨手笨脚的，把咖啡洒得自己满身都是。| clumsy, awkward, ungainly | graceful, coordinated, deft | adjective

Etymology: From klutz + -y + -est.  "Klutz" is Yiddish, from German Klotz "block, lump."
