co-ops:/ˈkoʊˌɑːps/ (US), /ˈkəʊˌɒps/ (UK)| n.|合作社，合作商店；合作公寓|A business or organization owned and operated by the people who work there or use its services, or housing units owned and operated by a cooperative corporation.|例句：We buy our groceries from the local co-op. 我们从当地的合作社购买食品杂货。|例句：They live in a co-op apartment building. 他们住在合作公寓楼里。|近义词：cooperatives, collectives|反义词:corporations, conglomerates|词性:名词

co-op:/ˈkoʊˌɑːp/ (US), /ˈkəʊˌɒp/ (UK)| n.|合作，协作；合作教育项目|Short for cooperative; a program where students work in jobs related to their field of study.|例句：She's doing a co-op this semester at a software company. 她这学期在一家软件公司进行合作教育。|例句：The engineering co-op program is highly competitive. 工程合作教育项目竞争非常激烈。|近义词：internship, work experience|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
