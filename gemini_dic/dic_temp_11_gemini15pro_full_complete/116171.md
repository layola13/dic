masculinising:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
男性化的；使男性化的
Causing someone or something to have or appear to have more typically masculine qualities.
Steroids can have a masculinising effect on women. 类固醇会对女性产生男性化的影响。

近义词:virilizing, male-making
反义词:feminizing
词性:adjective
