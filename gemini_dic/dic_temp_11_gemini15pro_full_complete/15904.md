ballparks: /ˈbɔːlˌpɑːrks/ (US), /ˈbɔːlˌpɑːks/ (UK)| n.|棒球场；大致范围，约略估计|A ballpark is a stadium where baseball is played.  Figuratively, it refers to an approximate range of values. |The new stadium is one of the best ballparks in the league.  新体育场是联盟中最好的棒球场之一。

The cost of the project is in the ballpark of $1 million. 这个项目的成本大约在100万美元左右。|近义词：stadiums (for literal meaning), estimates, approximations (for figurative meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "precise figure" for the figurative meaning) |词性: noun


ballpark: /ˈbɔːlˌpɑːrk/ (US), /ˈbɔːlˌpɑːrk/ (UK)| adj.|大概的，粗略估计的|Relating to a rough estimate.  Usually used with "figure" or "estimate".|He gave me a ballpark figure for the cost of repairs. 他给了我一个大概的修理费用。|近义词：approximate, rough|反义词:precise, exact|词性: adjective
