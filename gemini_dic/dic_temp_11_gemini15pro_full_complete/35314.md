cnc:/ˌsiːˌɛnˈsiː/ | n. | 电脑数控（Computer Numerical Control）|  A method of automating machine tools through the use of software programs and electronic controllers. | The factory uses CNC machines to produce precision parts.  工厂使用电脑数控机床生产精密零件。|  近义词: automated machining, computer-aided manufacturing (CAM) | 反义词: manual machining | noun
