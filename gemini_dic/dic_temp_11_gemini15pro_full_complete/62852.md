enjoin:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪn/ | v. | 命令；禁止；嘱咐 | to instruct or urge (someone) to do something: The judge enjoined them to refrain from any further contact with the witness. 法官命令他们不要再与证人联系。 |例句：The doctor enjoined his patient to rest. 医生嘱咐他的病人休息。

例句：The king enjoined his knights to be brave. 国王命令他的骑士要勇敢。|近义词：command, order, direct, instruct, urge, admonish, exhort |反义词：allow, permit, forbid (when "enjoin" means prohibit) |词性：动词
