capric:/ˈkæprɪk/| adj.|  羊的；山羊的；有山羊气味的；反复无常的|Relating to or resembling a goat, especially in smell; characteristic of a goat.|The cheese had a distinct capric aroma.  这种奶酪有一种明显的山羊气味。|Synonyms: goatlike, hircine|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "pleasant-smelling" if referring to the smell)|adjective


capric:/ˈkæprɪk/| adj.|反复无常的; 任性的|Characterized by impulsive, erratic behavior; whimsical.|Her capric nature made it difficult to predict her reactions. 她反复无常的性格让人很难预测她的反应。|Synonyms: fickle, whimsical, mercurial, erratic, capricious|Antonyms:  stable, steady, consistent, predictable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caprinus* "of goats," from *caper* "goat."  The chemical sense (capric acid) refers to its presence in goat's milk.
