cybersquatting:/ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtɪŋ/
|n.|域名抢注；网络蟑螂|The act of registering a domain name that is the same as, or confusingly similar to, a well-known trademark, with the intent to profit from it.  注册与知名商标相同或容易混淆的域名，意图从中获利。| Many companies have been victims of cybersquatting. 很多公司都成为域名抢注的受害者。|  domain name hijacking,  domain grabbing |  domain relinquishment | noun
