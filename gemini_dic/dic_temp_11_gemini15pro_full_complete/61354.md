embered:/ˈɛmbərd/| adj.|燃烧的，闷烧的，带有余烬的| Covered with or containing embers; still glowing or smoldering.  Figuratively, it can mean something that is still faintly present, like a memory or emotion.|The embered logs glowed softly in the fireplace. 壁炉里闷烧的木头发出柔和的光芒。|近义词：glowing, smoldering, burning, live, alight|反义词：extinguished, dead, cold|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "ember" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Ember" comes from Old English æmyrge, from Proto-Germanic *aimuzjo, from Proto-Indo-European *aydh- (to burn).
