ministries:/ˈmɪnɪstriz/
n.
政府部门；部；（全体）牧师
A government department headed by a minister.  / The clergy.
The Ministry of Defence has announced cuts in military spending. 国防部宣布削减军费开支。
Many ministries are involved in dealing with the crisis. 许多政府部门参与处理这场危机。
The local ministries organized a community outreach program. 当地的牧师们组织了一个社区外展项目。
synonyms: departments, agencies, clergy, priesthood
antonyms: laity
