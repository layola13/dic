inclusion:/ɪnˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 包含，包容；内含物 |The inclusion of diverse perspectives is crucial for innovation.  包容不同的观点对创新至关重要。| Examples: The price includes breakfast. 价格包含早餐。/  Her inclusion in the team was a surprise. 她被纳入团队令人惊讶。 | Synonyms: incorporation, integration, addition | Antonyms: exclusion, omission | noun

inclusion:/ɪnˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 包含物，夹杂物 | The diamond had a small inclusion that reduced its value. 这颗钻石有一个小的内含物，降低了它的价值。| Examples: The geologist studied the inclusions in the rock. 地质学家研究了岩石中的夹杂物。/  The amber contained an inclusion of a prehistoric insect.  琥珀中包含一只史前昆虫。| Synonyms:  impurity,  foreign body | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inclusionem,  from Latin inclusus, past participle of includere "to enclose," from in- "in" + claudere "to shut."
