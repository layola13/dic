meditatingly:/ˈmɛdɪteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
沉思地，冥想地
in a meditating manner; thoughtfully; contemplatively
She gazed meditatingly at the painting. 她沉思地凝视着那幅画。
Synonyms: thoughtfully, pensively, contemplatively, reflectively
Antonyms: thoughtlessly, unthinkingly, impulsively
词性: 副词
