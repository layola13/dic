afro:/ˈæfroʊ/| n.|非洲式发型|A hairstyle in which the hair is shaped into a large rounded mass.|She wore her hair in a magnificent afro. 她留着一个很漂亮的非洲式发型。|近义词:natural, 'fro|反义词:(None specific, perhaps straightened hair)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened from Afro-hairstyle, from Afro- + hairstyle.  Afro- is a combining form representing Africa or African, used especially in nouns and adjectives.  Ultimately from Africa, from Latin.
