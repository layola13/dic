masticator:/ˈmæstɪkeɪtər/| n.| 咀嚼器，研磨器；咀嚼肌|A masticator is a muscle or a tool used for chewing or grinding. |The cow uses its strong masticators to grind the tough grass.  牛用它强壮的咀嚼肌来磨碎坚韧的草。|The industrial masticator quickly ground the plastic into small pieces.  这台工业研磨机迅速将塑料磨成小块。|synonyms: grinder, mill, chewer, jaw muscles|antonyms: N/A|noun
