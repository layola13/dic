overdrained:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnd/
adjective
过度排水的；排水过度的；过度消耗的
Exhausted or depleted, as resources or finances.  Depleted of moisture through excessive drainage.
The farmer's field was overdrained, leading to poor crop yields. 农田过度排水，导致作物产量低。
The overdrained soil cracked under the intense summer heat. 排水过度的土壤在酷暑下开裂了。
近义词: depleted, exhausted, impoverished
反义词: saturated, waterlogged
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "drained" (past participle of "drain," from Old English *drēahnian "to filter, strain," related to Old Norse draugr "dregs").
