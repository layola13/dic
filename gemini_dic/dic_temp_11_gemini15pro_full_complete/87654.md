hexadactylism:/ˌheksəˈdæktɪlɪzəm/
|n.|六指畸形，多指畸形
|A congenital anomaly characterized by the presence of six digits on one or both hands or feet.|
|Hexadactylism is a relatively common birth defect.|六指畸形是一种相对常见的出生缺陷。
|Polydactyly is a condition where a person has more than five fingers or toes on one, or on each, hand or foot. Hexadactyly is the specific type of polydactyly where there are six digits on a hand or foot. In most cases of hexadactyly, the extra digit is rudimentary.|多指畸形是指一个人在一只或两只手上或脚上有多于五个手指或脚趾的情况。六指畸形是多指畸形的一种特殊类型，其中一只手或一只脚上有六个指头。在大多数六指畸形病例中，多余的指头是发育不全的。


|近义词：polydactyly|
|反义词：(None -  absence of this condition isn't a named condition)|
