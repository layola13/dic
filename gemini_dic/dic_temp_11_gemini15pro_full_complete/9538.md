antisensuous:/ˌæntiˈsɛnʃuəs/
adjective
反感官的；禁欲主义的；抑制感官的
opposed or hostile to the gratification of the senses; ascetic.
The monk led an antisensuous life, dedicating himself to prayer and fasting.  这位僧侣过着禁欲的生活，致力于祈祷和禁食。
The philosopher advocated for an antisensuous approach to life, believing that physical pleasures hindered spiritual growth. 这位哲学家提倡一种禁欲的生活方式，认为肉体上的快乐会阻碍精神成长。
近义词:ascetic, austere, abstinent
反义词:sensuous, hedonistic, indulgent
词性:adjective

antisensuous:/ˌæntiˈsɛnʃuəs/
词源:anti- (against) + sensuous (related to the senses)
From Latin *antisensus*, from anti- "against" + sensus "sense".  源自拉丁语 *antisensus*，由 anti- “反对” + sensus “感觉” 组成。
