misunderstanding: /ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændɪŋ/ | n. | 误解，误会 | A failure to understand something correctly; a disagreement or quarrel. | There must have been some misunderstanding. I never said that. 一定是有什么误会。我从没那么说过。 | Synonyms: misconception, misinterpretation, misapprehension | Antonyms: understanding, agreement, accord | noun

misunderstand: /ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/ | v. | 误解，误会 | To fail to understand something correctly; to interpret something wrongly. | I think you misunderstood my intentions. 我想你误解了我的意图。 | Synonyms: misinterpret, misconstrue, misapprehend | Antonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp | verb
