noncounteractive:/ˌnɒnˌkaʊntərˈæktɪv/
adjective
非对抗性的，不抵消的
Not acting to neutralize or oppose the effects of something else.
His noncounteractive approach to the conflict only exacerbated the situation. 他对冲突不抵消的做法反而加剧了局势。
The medication had a noncounteractive effect, meaning it didn't interfere with other treatments. 这种药物具有非对抗作用，意味着它不会干扰其他治疗。
近义词: nonresistant, passive, inert
反义词: counteractive, opposing, neutralizing
词性: adjective
