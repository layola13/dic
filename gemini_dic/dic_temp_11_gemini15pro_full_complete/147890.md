overrealistic:/ˌoʊvərˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过于逼真的；过分现实的
Representing or attempting to represent something in a way that is excessively detailed or accurate, often to the point of being unnatural or unsettling.
The special effects in the movie were overrealistic and made some scenes disturbing.  这部电影的特效过于逼真，让一些场景令人不安。
The wax figures were so overrealistic that they looked like real people.  这些蜡像栩栩如生，看起来像真人一样。
近义词: hyperrealistic, too realistic, excessively realistic
反义词: unrealistic, idealized, romanticized
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "realistic" (adjective from "real" + "-istic" suffix)
