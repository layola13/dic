host:/hoʊst/ (US) /həʊst/ (UK)| n. vt.| 主人，主持人，东道主，主办，大量，主持，做东 | A person who receives or entertains other people as guests.  |  The host greeted us at the door. 主人在门口迎接我们。 | synonyms: entertainer, emcee, landlord, innkeeper | antonyms: guest, visitor | noun
host:/hoʊst/ (US) /həʊst/ (UK)| vt.| 主持，主办，做东 | To act as host at (an event). | They hosted a dinner party for their friends.  他们为朋友们举办了一个晚宴。 | synonyms: organize, stage, present | antonyms: attend, visit | verb

Etymology: From Old French *host, hoste (“guest, host, army, large number”), from Latin hospes (“guest, host; stranger, foreigner”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰóstis (“stranger, guest, host”).
