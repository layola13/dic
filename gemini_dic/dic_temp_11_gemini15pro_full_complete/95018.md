inablement:/ɪˈneɪblmənt/| n.|赋能，使能，授权|The process of giving someone the authority or means to do something.  Providing tools, resources, or training to empower someone to achieve a specific goal.|The company's focus on inablement has led to increased productivity.  这家公司对赋能的重视导致了生产力的提高。|近义词：empowerment, facilitation, enabling|反义词:restriction, hindrance, disablement|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "enable" + suffix "-ment". "Enable" comes from Old French "enabler" meaning "to make able," from "en-" (meaning "in" or "make") and "able". The suffix "-ment" forms nouns from verbs and means "the act, result, or means of".
