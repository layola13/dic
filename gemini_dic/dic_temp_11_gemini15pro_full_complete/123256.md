monodelphian: /ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfiən/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdɛlfiən/
adjective | 单孔目动物的| Belonging to the Monodelphia, a subclass of mammals characterized by having a single opening in the female genital tract, encompassing most mammals except monotremes and marsupials. |  Most placental mammals are monodelphian. 大多数胎盘哺乳动物都是单孔目动物的。| Near Synonyms: placental, eutherian | Antonym:  metatherian (marsupial), prototherian (monotreme)
noun | 单孔目动物| A mammal of the Monodelphia subclass. |  Humans are monodelphians. 人类是单孔目动物。| Near Synonyms: placental mammal, eutherian | Antonym: marsupial, monotreme
