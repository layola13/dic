disembarking:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrkɪŋ/
verb
下船，下飞机
the act of leaving a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle
Passengers are disembarking from the plane. 乘客正在下飞机。
近义词:debarking, landing, exiting
反义词:embarking, boarding
v.
