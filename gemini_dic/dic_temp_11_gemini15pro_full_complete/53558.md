disbarring:/dɪsˈbɑːrɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
取消律师资格
The act of officially preventing a lawyer from practicing law.
He faces disbarring for misconduct. 他因行为不端面临被取消律师资格的处罚。
Synonyms:  expelling, debarring, suspending
Antonyms:  admitting, reinstating
词性: 动词(现在分词)

Synonyms:  expel, debar, suspend
