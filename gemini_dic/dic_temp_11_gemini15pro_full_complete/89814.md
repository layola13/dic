hornblendic:/ˌhɔːrnˈblɛndɪk/| adj.|角闪岩的，含角闪石的|Containing or composed largely of hornblende.  A dark,  igneous rock.|The geologist identified the sample as a hornblendic rock. 地质学家鉴定该样本为角闪岩。|Synonyms: amphibolitic|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use terms like felsic, non-hornblendic to describe contrasting rock types)|adjective
