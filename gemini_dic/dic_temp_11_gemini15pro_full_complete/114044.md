magnetical:/mæɡˈnɛtɪkəl/
adjective
磁的；有磁性的
Relating to or caused by magnetism; having the properties of a magnet.
The Earth has a magnetical field. 地球有一个磁场。
The magnetical force attracts iron. 磁力吸引铁。
近义词:magnetic
反义词:nonmagnetic
词性:adjective
