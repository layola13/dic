hebridian:/hɪˈbrɪdiən/
adjective
赫布里底群岛的；赫布里底群岛居民的
relating to the Hebrides, a group of islands off the west coast of Scotland
The Hebridean landscape is rugged and beautiful. 赫布里底群岛的风景崎岖而美丽。
The Hebridean people are known for their hospitality. 赫布里底群岛的人民以热情好客而闻名。
近义词:Scottish, Gaelic
反义词:None
词性:adjective


hebridian:/hɪˈbrɪdiən/
noun
赫布里底群岛居民
a person from the Hebrides
The Hebridean spoke Gaelic.  这位赫布里底群岛居民讲盖尔语。
Many Hebrideans rely on fishing for their livelihood. 许多赫布里底群岛居民依靠捕鱼为生。
