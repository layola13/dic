necessitated:/nəˈsesɪteɪtɪd/
verb
使成为必需；需要
Made necessary; required.
The increase in population necessitated a bigger school. 人口增长使得需要一所更大的学校。
Synonyms: required, demanded, obliged, compelled
Antonyms: obviated, precluded
verb

PHRASE->
Currently doesn't have any widely used phrasal verbs.  It's typically used as a standalone verb.  Therefore, common collocations are provided:
Costs necessitated by the project = 项目所需的成本
Changes necessitated by the new law = 新法律要求的变更
Actions necessitated by the crisis = 危机所需的行动
Measures necessitated by the situation = 局势所需的措施
Decisions necessitated by circumstances =  情况决定的决策

词源: From Latin *necessitatus*, past participle of *necessitare* "to compel, constrain," from *necessitas* "necessity."
