infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/ | vt. | 侵犯，违反 | to actively break the terms of a law, agreement, etc.; to violate |  The company infringed on their competitor's patent.  这家公司侵犯了其竞争对手的专利。 | synonyms: violate, breach, contravene | antonyms: comply with, obey, uphold | verb

infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/ | vi. | 侵犯，侵害 | to encroach or trespass | Building the fence infringed on his neighbor's property. 建造栅栏侵占了他邻居的财产。| synonyms: encroach, trespass, impinge | antonyms: respect, honor | verb
