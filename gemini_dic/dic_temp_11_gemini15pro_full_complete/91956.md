hypermodernities:/ˌhaɪpərməˈdɜːrnɪtiz/ | n. | 超现代性（复数） |  A term used to describe the intensified and accelerated aspects of modernity, often characterized by globalization, technological advancements, and cultural fragmentation.  It emphasizes the plural and diverse nature of contemporary experience. | The hypermodernities of globalized cities can be both exhilarating and alienating. 全球化城市的超现代性既令人兴奋，又令人疏离。 |  Late modernities, postmodernity, supermodernity |  --- | noun
