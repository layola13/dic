exterritoriality:/ˌɛkstɛrɪˌtɔːriˈælɪti/
n.
治外法权；领事裁判权
The state of being exempted from the jurisdiction of local law, usually as the result of diplomatic negotiations.
The embassy claimed exterritoriality for its staff.  大使馆要求其工作人员享有治外法权。
