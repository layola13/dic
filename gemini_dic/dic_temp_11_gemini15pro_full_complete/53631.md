discernibleness:/dɪˈsɜːrnɪbəlnəs/
n.
可辨别性，可识别性
The quality of being discernible; perceptibility.
The discernibleness of the trail in the fresh snow was reassuring.  在新鲜的雪地上，小路的清晰可见令人安心。
近义词：perceptibility, visibility, distinctness, clarity
反义词：invisibility, obscurity, vagueness, indistinctness
词性: noun

discernible:/dɪˈsɜːrnɪbəl/
adj.
可辨别的，可识别的
Able to be discerned; perceptible.
There is a discernible improvement in her handwriting. 她的书法有了明显的进步。
近义词：perceptible, visible, distinct, clear
反义词：invisible, obscure, vague, indistinct
词性: adjective

discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/
v.
辨别，识别
To perceive or recognize (something).
It is difficult to discern the truth in this complicated situation.  很难在这种复杂的情况下辨别真相。
近义词：perceive, recognize, distinguish, detect
反义词：miss, overlook, ignore
词性: verb


词源:
From Late Latin *discernibilitās, from Latin discernō (“distinguish, separate”) + -ibilis (“-ible”) + -tās. Equivalent to discernible +‎ -ness.
