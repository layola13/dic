ambrosia:/æmˈbroʊʒə/ /æmˈbroʊziə/ | n. | 神的食物；美味佳肴；香脂 |  In Greek mythology, ambrosia was the food of the gods, said to confer immortality.  It is often used figuratively to refer to something exceptionally delicious or fragrant. | The chef's latest creation was a true ambrosia, a delight to the senses.  主厨的最新作品是一道真正的美味佳肴，令人愉悦。| Synonyms: nectar, delicacy, manna | Antonyms: swill, garbage | noun
