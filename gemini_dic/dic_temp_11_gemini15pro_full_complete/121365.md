misguess:/ˌmɪsˈɡes/| v. |猜错，估计错误|To guess incorrectly; to make a wrong estimate or prediction.|I misguessed the amount of time it would take to finish the project. 我错误估计了完成这个项目所需的时间。|She completely misguessed his reaction to the news. 她完全猜错了 er他对这个消息的反应。|近义词：miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate|反义词：guess correctly, estimate accurately|词性:动词
