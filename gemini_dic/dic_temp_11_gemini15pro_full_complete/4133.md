air-drops:/ˈeərˌdrɒps/ /ˈɛrˌdrɑːps/ | n. |空投；空投物资；（加密货币的）空投| An act of dropping supplies or personnel from the air, typically by parachute. Also, in cryptocurrency, a distribution of a cryptocurrency token or coin, usually for free, to numerous wallet addresses. |  The military performed air-drops of food and medical supplies to the refugees.  军队向难民空投了食物和医疗用品。| synonyms: airlift, parachute drop | antonyms: ground delivery, pickup | noun
