gaffer:/ˈɡæfər/| n.|灯光师；领班；老家伙（尤指乡村老汉）|A gaffer is the chief electrician on a movie set or a foreman or overseer, especially in charge of a group of laborers.|The gaffer adjusted the lights to create a more dramatic effect. 灯光师调整灯光以营造更具戏剧性的效果。|Synonyms: foreman, overseer, boss, supervisor, chief electrician (in film)|Antonyms: subordinate, underling|noun

gaffer:/ˈɡæfər/| n.|老家伙（尤指乡村老汉）|An elderly man, especially a rustic one.|He was a kindly old gaffer, always ready with a story. 他是一位和蔼可亲的老家伙，总是准备好要讲故事。|Synonyms: old man, codger, geezer|Antonyms: youngster, youth|noun
