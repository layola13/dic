noddling: /ˈnɒdlɪŋ/ | n. | 摸鱼；非法捕鱼（尤指用手摸索捕捉鲶鱼） | The act of catching fish, especially catfish, with one's bare hands by probing underwater holes or crevices. |  He went noddling for catfish in the river. 他去河里摸鱼了。|  Synonym:  fishing (general), grabbing, groping (for fish) | Antonym: angling, netting | noun

noddling: /ˈnɒdlɪŋ/ | n. | 漫无目的地走动；闲逛 | Wandering around aimlessly; pottering. |  We spent the afternoon noddling around the market. 我们下午在市场闲逛。 | Synonym: wandering, strolling, meandering | Antonym: rushing, hurrying | noun
