deauration:/diːˌɔːˈreɪʃən/
n.
去金，脱金
The process of removing gold from a surface or object.
The deauration of old jewelry can reveal the base metal underneath.  旧珠宝的去金可以显露出下面的贱金属。
近义词: gold removal, extraction of gold
反义词: gilding, gold plating
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *deauratio, from Latin deaurare "to gild," from de- "from" + aurum "gold."  The English word is a back-formation from the verb "deaure" (to remove gold), which is now rarely used.
