insectile:/ɪnˈsɛktaɪl/ /ɪnˈsɛktl/
adjective
昆虫的；昆虫似的
Resembling or suggesting an insect, especially in a creepy or unpleasant way.  Having the qualities or characteristics of an insect.
The creature emitted a series of high-pitched, insectile chirps.  这生物发出了一系列高亢刺耳的、类似昆虫的鸣叫声。
Her thin, spidery fingers moved with an insectile precision. 她细长如蜘蛛的手指以昆虫般的精准度移动着。
近义词:insectlike, buggy
反义词:mammalian, vertebrate
词性:adjective
