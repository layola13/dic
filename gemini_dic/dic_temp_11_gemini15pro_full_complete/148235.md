oversimplifier:/ˈoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪər/| n. |过度简化者，过分简化的人|Someone who simplifies something too much, often distorting or misrepresenting it in the process. |He's an oversimplifier; the issue is far more complex than he makes it out to be. 他是一个过度简化者；这个问题比他所说的要复杂得多。|Synonyms: reductionist, misrepresenter, distorter|Antonyms: elaborator, expander, clarifier|noun


oversimplify:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/| v. |过度简化，过分简化|To simplify something too much, often distorting or misrepresenting it in the process. |It's easy to oversimplify complex issues, but that doesn't make them any easier to solve.  很容易过度简化复杂的问题，但这并不能使它们更容易解决。|Synonyms: reduce, misrepresent, distort|Antonyms: elaborate, expand, clarify|verb
