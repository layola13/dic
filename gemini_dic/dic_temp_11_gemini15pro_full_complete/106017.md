knaw:/nɔː/ | v. | 啃，咬，啮；折磨，使苦恼 | To bite or chew something persistently, especially something hard; to cause persistent distress or anxiety. | The dog gnawed on the bone. 狗啃着骨头。|  synonyms: bite, chew, gnaw at, nibble, erode | antonyms: soothe, comfort | verb

knaw:/nɔː/ | n. | 啃咬；痛苦，烦恼 |  The act of gnawing or the feeling of being gnawed at. |  The constant gnaw of guilt made him confess.  持续的内疚感折磨着他，使他坦白了。| synonyms: bite, pang, ache | antonyms: comfort, relief | noun
