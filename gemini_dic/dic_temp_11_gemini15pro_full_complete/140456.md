old-buzzard:/ˈoʊld ˈbʌzərd/| n. |老家伙（贬义）|An informal, often derogatory term for an elderly man, implying cantankerousness or stubbornness. It equates the man to an old buzzard (a type of vulture), suggesting he is unattractive or unpleasant. |例句：That old buzzard refused to move his car.  那个老家伙拒绝挪车。

例句：Don't listen to that old buzzard; he's just grumpy.  别听那个老家伙的，他只是脾气暴躁。|近义词：codger, geezer, curmudgeon|反义词:youngster, whippersnapper|词性:noun
