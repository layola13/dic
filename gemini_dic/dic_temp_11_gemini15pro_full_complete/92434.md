hyponym:/ˈhaɪpənɪm/| n.|下义词|A word whose meaning is included in the meaning of another more general word; a specific term within a broader category.  A hyponym shares a type-of relationship with its hypernym.|Example: "Rose" is a hyponym of "flower."  玫瑰是花的下义词。 Example: "Sparrow" is a hyponym of "bird." 麻雀是鸟的下义词。|Synonym: subordinate term, specific term|Antonym: hypernym, superordinate term|noun
