archaicisms:/ˈɑːrkeɪɪzəmz/ | n. | 古语；古体 |  Words and phrases that are no longer in common use but are sometimes used to give a sense of old-fashioned style. |  The author used several archaicisms, such as "thee" and "thou," to create a historical atmosphere. 作者使用了一些古语，例如“thee”和“thou”，以营造历史氛围。 | synonyms: antiquated language, obsolete words, old-fashioned expressions | antonyms: modern language, contemporary vocabulary, current expressions | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *archaismus, from Ancient Greek ἀρχαϊσμός (arkhaïsmós, “old-fashioned word or phrase”), from ἀρχαῖος (arkhaîos, “ancient”).
