highlighting:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 突出显示；强调；加亮 | the act of marking or emphasizing something important |  The highlighting of the main points made it easier to understand the lecture.  突出重点使讲座更容易理解。 | emphasis, stressing | downplaying, obscuring | noun

highlighting:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  突出显示的；强调的；加亮的 |  serving to emphasize or make prominent | The highlighting pen made the key words stand out.  荧光笔使关键词突出。| prominent, emphasized | inconspicuous, subtle | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "highlight," which combines "high" and "light."  "High" here refers to prominence or importance, and "light" suggests bringing something into clearer view.
