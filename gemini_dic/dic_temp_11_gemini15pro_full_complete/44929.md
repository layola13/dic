cryptogamic:/ˌkrɪptəˈɡæmɪk/ | adj. | 隐花植物的；隐孢子的 | Relating to cryptogams, plants that reproduce by spores, not seeds, and do not have flowers or seeds. |  Cryptogamic plants include ferns, mosses, algae, and fungi. 隐花植物包括蕨类植物、苔藓、藻类和真菌。| 近义词: spore-bearing, flowerless | 反义词:  phanerogamic (显花植物的) | 词性: 形容词

cryptogam:/ˈkrɪptəɡæm/ | n. | 隐花植物；隐孢子植物 | A plant that reproduces by spores, not seeds, and do not have flowers or seeds. | Ferns are cryptogams. 蕨类植物是隐花植物。| 近义词: spore-bearing plant, flowerless plant | 反义词: phanerogam (显花植物) | 词性: 名词
