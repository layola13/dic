asserting: /əˈsɜːtɪŋ/ | v. | 断言，维护，坚持 |  The act of stating something confidently and forcefully. |  He was asserting his right to free speech. 他在维护自己言论自由的权利。 | synonyms: declaring, maintaining, upholding, avowing, claiming, affirming | antonyms: denying, retracting, withdrawing, conceding | verb

asserting: /əˈsɜːtɪŋ/ | adj. |  断言的，维护的，坚持的 |  Characterized by stating something confidently and forcefully. |  She had an asserting tone in her voice. 她的语气很坚定。 | synonyms: assertive, forceful, confident, emphatic | antonyms: hesitant, timid, unsure, unconfident | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *asserere, frequentative of Latin asserō (“I claim, vindicate, assert, maintain”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + serō (“I join, bind”).
