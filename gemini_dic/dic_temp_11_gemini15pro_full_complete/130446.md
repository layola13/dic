nonabsolutist:/nɒnˈæbsəluːtɪst/
adjective
非绝对主义的
Not adhering to or believing in absolutism, which is the acceptance of or belief in absolute principles in political, philosophical, ethical, or theological matters.
The professor presented a nonabsolutist view of morality, arguing that ethical principles can be flexible depending on the context.  教授提出了一个非绝对主义的道德观，认为伦理原则可以根据具体情况而灵活变通。
The king, known for his nonabsolutist approach, often consulted with his advisors before making important decisions.  这位国王以其非绝对主义的方式而闻名，在做出重要决定之前经常咨询他的顾问。

synonym: relativistic, flexible, contextual, pragmatic
antonym: absolutist, dogmatic, rigid, inflexible
词性: 形容词
