danegeld:/ˈdeɪnɡɛld/
n.
保护费，赎金，贡金
A payment made to buy off invaders or to avoid attack, historically referring to the tribute paid by the English to the Danes.
The government was criticized for paying danegeld to the terrorists. 政府因向恐怖分子支付赎金而受到批评。
synonyms: tribute, ransom, protection money
antonyms: reward, compensation
noun

Appeasement may be seen as a form of modern danegeld. 绥靖政策可以被视为现代的一种贡金。
synonyms: appeasement, payoff
antonyms: resistance, defiance

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Denegeld "Dane-money," from Dene (genitive plural of Dena "Danes") + geld "payment, tribute."
