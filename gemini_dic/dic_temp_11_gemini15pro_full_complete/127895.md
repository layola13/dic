neographic:/ˌniːoʊˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|新字体的；新书写的；新近创造的|Relating to or denoting a new word or phrase, or a new meaning of an existing word or phrase.  Characterized by the use of neologisms.|The dictionary included several neographic terms related to internet culture. 这本词典收录了几个与互联网文化相关的新词。|Synonyms: new, modern, recent, novel, innovative|Antonyms: old, ancient, archaic, outdated|adjective

neographic:/ˌniːoʊˈɡræfɪk/| n.|新词；新语|A newly coined word or expression.|Many neographics arise from technological advancements. 许多新词源于技术进步。|Synonyms: neologism, coinage|Antonyms: archaism|noun
