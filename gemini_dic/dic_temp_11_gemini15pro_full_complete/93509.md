I'm'ing:/ˈaɪmɪŋ/ | contraction | 我正在... | Contraction of "I am" + "-ing" form of a verb, indicating the present continuous tense.  It represents an action happening now. | Example: I'm'ing walking to the store. 我正走去商店。| 近义词: I am ...ing | 反义词: N/A | contraction

PHRASE->
N/A - I'm'ing is a standalone contraction and doesn't typically form set phrases.


注意事项:  "I'm'ing" is very informal and generally only used in spoken or very casual written English.  It's better to use the full form "I am + -ing" in most situations.
