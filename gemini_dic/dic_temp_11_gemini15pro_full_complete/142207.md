ornithoidichnite:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɔɪdɪknʌɪt/
n.
鸟形足迹，鸟类足迹化石
A fossilized footprint resembling that of a bird.
The ornithoidichnite discovered in the Jurassic strata provided evidence of early bird-like dinosaurs. 在侏罗纪地层中发现的鸟形足迹为早期鸟类恐龙的存在提供了证据。

The paleontologist identified the track as an ornithoidichnite. 古生物学家将这条轨迹鉴定为鸟形足迹。

Synonyms: fossil footprint, bird track, dinosaur track
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, potentially "modern bird footprint")
