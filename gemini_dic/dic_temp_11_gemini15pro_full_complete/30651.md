cerite:/ˈsɛraɪt/| n. |硅铈石|A brownish-red mineral consisting of hydrous cerium silicate, found in Sweden. |The cerite was discovered in a Swedish mine.  硅铈石是在瑞典的一个矿井中发现的。|The museum's collection includes a rare sample of cerite.  博物馆的藏品中有一块稀有的硅铈石样本。|Synonyms:  cerium silicate|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Swedish *cerit*, from New Latin *cerium*,  named after the asteroid Ceres. 最终源自拉丁语Cerēs,  罗马神话中的谷物女神。
