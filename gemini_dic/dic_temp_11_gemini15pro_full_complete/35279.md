clutchiest:/ˈklʌtʃiɪst/
adjective
最关键的，最紧要的，最有用的
Superlative form of "clutch" (adjective): Performing reliably and successfully in a critical situation, especially one of high pressure.  It describes something or someone that is extremely dependable and effective when it matters most.
He made the clutchiest free throws of his career to win the game.  他投中了职业生涯中最关键的罚球，赢得了比赛。
Synonyms: crucial, decisive, pivotal, critical, essential
Antonyms: insignificant, unimportant, trivial, useless
