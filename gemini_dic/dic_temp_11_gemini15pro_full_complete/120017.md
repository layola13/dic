millings:/ˈmɪlɪŋz/
n.
金属屑，铣屑
small pieces of metal, wood, etc. that are produced when something is milled
The millings from the lathe were collected for recycling.  车床上的金属屑被收集起来回收利用。

近义词:shavings, chips, filings
反义词:(None -  lacks a direct opposite)
词性:noun
