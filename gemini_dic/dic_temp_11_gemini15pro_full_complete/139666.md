occupied: /ˈɒkjʊpaɪd/ (British) /ˈɑːkjəˌpaɪd/ (American)| adj.| 已占用的，忙碌的，被占领的 |Being used by someone.  Also, busy or active.| The bathroom is currently occupied. | 近义词: busy, engaged, in use, taken, full | 反义词: vacant, empty, free, available | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *occupatus*, past participle of occupare (“to seize, take possession of”), from ob (“against, towards”) + capere (“to take”).
