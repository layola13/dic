demissive:/dɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
轻蔑的，不屑一顾的
Treating something as unimportant, unworthy of serious consideration.
He gave a dismissive wave of his hand. 他轻蔑地挥了挥手。
近义词：contemptuous, disdainful, scornful
反义词：respectful, attentive, considerate
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *dismissivus, from Latin dismiss-, past participle stem of dimittere "to send away," from dis- "away" + mittere "to send."
