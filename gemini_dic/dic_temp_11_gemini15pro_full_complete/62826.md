enigma:/ɪˈnɪɡmə/ | n. | 谜; 难以理解的人(或事物); | something that is mysterious and seems impossible to understand completely |  The disappearance of the plane remains an enigma. 这架飞机的消失仍然是个谜。 | synonyms: mystery, puzzle, riddle, conundrum | antonyms: solution, explanation | noun

enigmatic:/ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 神秘的; 难以理解的; | mysterious and difficult to understand |  He gave an enigmatic smile. 他神秘地笑了笑。 | synonyms: mysterious, puzzling, cryptic, ambiguous | antonyms: clear, obvious, straightforward | adjective
