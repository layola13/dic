aggressor:/əˈɡrɛsər/| n.|侵略者；攻击者；挑衅者|A person or country that attacks another first.|例句：The aggressor in the conflict was quickly identified. 冲突中的侵略者很快就被确定了。

例句：The small nation was invaded by a powerful aggressor. 这个小国被一个强大的侵略者入侵了。


近义词：attacker, assailant, invader, offender|反义词:victim, defender, protector|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aggressor, from Latin aggressus, past participle of aggredi (“to attack”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + gradi (“to step, walk”).
