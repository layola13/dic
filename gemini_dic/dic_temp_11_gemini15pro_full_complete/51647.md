deviate:/ˈdiːviˌeɪt/ | v. | 偏离，背离 | To depart from an established course, accepted standard, or usual practice. | The plane deviated from its planned route due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，飞机偏离了预定航线。 | stray, digress, diverge | conform, adhere | verb

deviated:/ˈdiːviˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 偏离的，背离的 | Having departed from an established course, accepted standard, or usual practice. | The deviated septum can cause breathing problems.  偏离的鼻中隔会导致呼吸问题。 | aberrant, abnormal, irregular | normal, standard, usual | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin deviatus, past participle of deviare "to turn aside," from de- "off" + via "way, road."
