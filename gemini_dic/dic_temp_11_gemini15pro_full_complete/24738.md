brooded:/ˈbruːdɪd/| v. | 沉思；担忧；(鸟)孵蛋 | to think for a long time about something that makes you worried, angry, or unhappy; (of a bird) to sit on (eggs) to keep them warm until they hatch | She brooded over the problem for days. 她对这个问题苦思冥想了好几天。The hen brooded her eggs. 母鸡孵蛋。 |近义词：ponder, dwell, meditate, incubate |反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard| verb

brooding:/ˈbruːdɪŋ/| adj. | 沉思的；令人忧郁的 | appearing sad, worried, or threatening | He had a brooding expression on his face. 他脸上带着沉思的表情。The brooding storm clouds gathered overhead. 令人忧郁的暴风云聚集在头顶。 |近义词：pensive, thoughtful, contemplative, menacing |反义词：cheerful, lighthearted, carefree| adjective
