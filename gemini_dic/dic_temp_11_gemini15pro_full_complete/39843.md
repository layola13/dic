constable:/ˈkɒnstəbəl/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəbəl/ (US)| n.|治安官，警员|A police officer of the lowest rank.|例句：The constable arrested the thief. 治安官逮捕了小偷。|例句：The local constable is well-respected in the community. 当地的警员在社区很受尊敬。|近义词：police officer, policeman, policewoman, officer, cop|反义词:criminal, lawbreaker|noun

词源:
From Old French *conestable, from Latin comes stabuli (“count of the stable”), originally a high-ranking officer in charge of the royal stables.
