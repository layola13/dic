blotter:/ˈblɑːtər/ (US), /ˈblɒtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|吸墨纸，记事簿，(警方)罪犯记录簿|A sheet of thick absorbent paper used to dry wet ink.  A book or pad for writing notes or memoranda, especially one used by police for recording arrests and other incidents.|He used a blotter to dry the ink on the contract. 他用吸墨纸吸干合同上的墨水。|synonyms: absorbent paper, blotting paper, notepad, diary, journal|antonyms: N/A|noun

blotter:/ˈblɑːtər/ (US), /ˈblɒtə(r)/ (UK)| v.|吸干(墨水)，涂污，玷污|To dry wet ink with absorbent paper. To obscure or blemish.|She blotted the excess ink with a tissue. 她用纸巾吸干多余的墨水。|synonyms: dry, soak up, absorb, blur, blemish|antonyms: wet, soak, saturate|verb
