afforest:/əˈfɔːrɪst/ /æˈfɔːrɪst/
verb
植树造林;绿化
To establish a forest on land that has not been forested for a long time.
例句：The government plans to afforest large areas of wasteland. 政府计划在荒原上大面积植树造林。

例句：They are trying to afforest the desert regions to prevent further desertification. 他们正在努力绿化沙漠地区，以防止进一步的荒漠化。

近义词：reforest, plant, landscape
反义词：deforest, clear
词性：v.
