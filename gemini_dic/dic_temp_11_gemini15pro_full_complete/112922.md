luft:/lʊft/
n.
air, atmosphere
The fresh luft invigorated him.  新鲜的空气让他精神焕发。
The valley was filled with the crisp mountain luft. 山谷里充满了清新的山间空气。
近义词:air, atmosphere, breeze
反义词:(None - Luft refers to the air itself, not its qualities like stillness or staleness)
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Luft*, meaning "air."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *luftuz*, related to Old English *lyft* (air, sky), and further back to Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (to peel, rind, bark). The connection likely comes from the idea of something that covers or surrounds.
