nicer:/ˈnaɪsər/ | adj. | 更友善的，更美好的，更好的 | comparative form of "nice," meaning more pleasant, agreeable, or attractive. | The weather today is nicer than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天好。|  The new park is nicer than the old one. 新公园比旧公园好。 |近义词：better, finer, more pleasant, more agreeable|反义词：worse, less pleasant, meaner, nastier | 词性:形容词
