inter-clasping:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːspɪŋ/
adjective
互相紧握的，交握的
Characterized by clasping or holding each other tightly, typically with fingers or hands intertwined.
Her hands were inter-clasping in her lap, a sign of her nervousness. 她双手紧紧交握在膝盖上，表明她很紧张。
The sculpture depicted two figures with inter-clasping hands, symbolizing unity.  这座雕塑描绘了两个手握在一起的人物，象征着团结。
近义词: intertwined, interlocked, clasped
反义词: unclasped, separated, disengaged
词性: adjective

词源:
From "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "clasping" (present participle of "clasp," from Old English *clasp or *clæps, related to Middle Low German and Middle Dutch claspe "clasp, buckle").
