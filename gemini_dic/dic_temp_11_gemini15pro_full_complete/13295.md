atheroid:/ˈæθəˌrɔɪd/| adj.|  银汉鱼似的，似银汉鱼的|Resembling or related to silversides (small, slender, silvery fishes of the family Atherinidae).  These fish are characterized by their translucent bodies and schooling behavior.  Atheroid can describe the fish themselves, their relatives, or anything having similar characteristics.|The scientist studied the atheroid fish found in the local estuary.|近义词：silversides-like, atheriniform|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly dissimilar, unlike)|词性: adjective
