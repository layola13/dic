hyperidealistically:/ˌhaɪpərˌaɪdiːəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过分理想主义地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive or unrealistic idealism. | He hyperidealistically believed that world peace could be achieved overnight. 他过分理想主义地认为世界和平可以一夜之间实现。 |  Example: She hyperidealistically pursued her dream, ignoring all practical considerations. 她过分理想化地追求她的梦想，忽略了所有实际的考虑。 | Synonyms: excessively idealistically, unrealistically idealistically, quixotically | Antonyms: pragmatically, realistically, cynically |词性: 副词
