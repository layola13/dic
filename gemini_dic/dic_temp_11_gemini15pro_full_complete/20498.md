bivalves:/ˈbaɪvælvz/
n.
双壳类动物
Aquatic mollusks having a compressed body enclosed within a hinged shell, such as oysters, clams, mussels, and scallops.
Oysters and clams are bivalves.  牡蛎和蛤蜊都是双壳类动物。

近义词:pelecypods
反义词:(none specific - could be univalves for single-shelled mollusks)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bivalvis* (two-valved), from *bi-* (two) + *valva* (leaf of a folding door, valve).
