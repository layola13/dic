blindless:/ˈblaɪndləsnɪs/
n.
失明; 盲目
the state or condition of being unable to see; lack of understanding or foresight.
His blindness prevented him from driving. 他的失明使他无法开车。
The blindness of their actions will have dire consequences. 他们盲目的行为将会造成可怕的后果。
近义词: sightlessness, cecity, unawareness
反义词: sight, vision, awareness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From blind + -ness.  "Blind" traces back to Proto-Germanic *blindaz, meaning "blinded, deprived of sight." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns expressing state or condition.
