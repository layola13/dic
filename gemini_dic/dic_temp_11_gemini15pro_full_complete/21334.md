bloomed:/bluːmd/
verb
开花；繁荣；焕发青春
produced or yielded flowers; flourished; reached a state of full development or beauty
例句：The cherry trees bloomed early this year. 今年樱花开得早。

例句：Her talent bloomed under the guidance of her mentor. 在导师的指导下，她的才华得到了充分展现。
近义词：flowered, blossomed, thrived, flourished
反义词：wilted, withered, faded
词性：v.
