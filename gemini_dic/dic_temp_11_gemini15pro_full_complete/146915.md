overinsuring: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊrɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|过度投保|The act of insuring something for more than its value.|He was accused of overinsuring his house. 他被指控对他的房屋过度投保。|Synonyms: over-insuring|Antonyms: underinsuring
noun|过度投保|The state of being insured for more than something is worth.|Overinsuring your car can be a waste of money. 为你的汽车过度投保可能是浪费金钱。|Synonyms: overinsurance|Antonyms: underinsurance

词性:  verb, noun
