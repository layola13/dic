chanty's:/ˈʃæntiz/ | n. | 船歌酒馆；船歌演唱者(尤指过去航海时在甲板上领唱船歌的人)的(住所、所有物等) |  Belonging to or associated with a chanty or a person who sings chanties, especially one who led such singing on board ship in former times.  |  Example: The chanty's rough melody echoed through the harbor. 船歌粗犷的旋律在港口回荡。 | Synonym: sailor's (in some contexts), sea shanty's | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "landlubber's" as a loose contrast) | Noun

chanty:/ˈʃænti/ | n. | 船歌；劳动号子 | A song, especially a rhythmical work song, sung by sailors. | Example: They sang a chanty while they hoisted the sails. 他们一边升帆一边唱着船歌。 | Synonym: work song, sea shanty | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | Noun
