aquilon:/ˈækwɪlɒn/ (US), /əˈkwɪlən/ (UK)| n.|北风，寒冷的北风|A strong north wind, especially a cold one.  It is often used poetically or in literary contexts.|例句：The aquilon blew fiercely across the plains. 寒风凛冽地吹过平原。

例句：The biting aquilon chilled the travelers to the bone. 刺骨的北风让旅行者感到彻骨的寒冷。|近义词：north wind, boreal wind|反义词:south wind, auster|词性:noun
