animose:/ˈænɪmoʊs/| adj.|敌意的，怀有敌意的|Full of animosity; hostile.  Characterized by strong hostility or ill will.|The animose debate between the two candidates reflected the deep divisions within the party.  两位候选人之间充满敌意的辩论反映了党内根深蒂固的分歧。|近义词：hostile, antagonistic, belligerent, rancorous|反义词：friendly, amicable, benevolent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *animosus* "full of spirit, courageous," from *animus* "spirit, mind, courage."  The meaning shifted in English to focus on the negative aspects of strong feeling, becoming synonymous with "hostile."
