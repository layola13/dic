ebbed:/ɛbd/
verb
退潮；衰退；减弱
receded; declined; diminished
The tide ebbed. 潮水退了。
The pain slowly ebbed away. 疼痛慢慢消退了。
近义词:recede, decline, diminish, wane, abate
反义词:flow, rise, increase, surge, intensify
词性:verb
