effervescingly:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsɪŋli/
adverb
兴奋地；热情洋溢地
in a lively and enthusiastic manner, as if bubbling with excitement.
Her laughter bubbled effervescingly. 她的笑声热情洋溢地冒了出来。
Near Synonyms: excitedly, enthusiastically, buoyantly, vibrantly
Antonyms: listlessly, apathetically, dully
词性: 副词
