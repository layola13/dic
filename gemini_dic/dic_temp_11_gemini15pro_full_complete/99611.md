interject:/ˌɪntərˈdʒekt/ | v. |插话；打断|To say something abruptly, especially as an interruption. |  He interjected a question during the lecture. 他在讲座中插话提了一个问题。 | synonyms: interrupt, intervene, interpose | antonyms: N/A | verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is that noise? = 那是什么声音？
How do you feel today? = 你今天感觉如何？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
Why is she crying? = 她为什么哭？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What has happened here? = 这里发生了什么事？
What have you been up to? = 你最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all this time? = 你这段时间一直在做什么？
What has she been working on lately? = 她最近一直在忙什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？
What did you say to him? = 你对他说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was he thinking about all day? = 他整天都在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么事？
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do if it rains? = 如果下雨你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 你将在一天结束前完成什么？
What will have changed by next year? = 明年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？
