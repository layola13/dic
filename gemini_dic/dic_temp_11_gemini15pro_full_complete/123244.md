monoculturist:/ˈmɒnəʊkʌltʃərɪst/ /ˈmɑːnəʊkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. |单一栽培者；单一文化主义者 |A person who practices or advocates monoculture, the cultivation of a single crop in a given area. |The monoculturist focused solely on growing corn, neglecting the potential benefits of crop diversification.  单一栽培者只专注于种植玉米，而忽略了作物多样化的潜在好处。| Synonyms: specialist, single-crop farmer | Antonyms: polyculturist, diversified farmer | noun

monoculture:/ˈmɒnəʊkʌltʃə(r)/ /ˈmɑːnəʊkʌltʃər/ | n. |单一栽培；单一文化 |The cultivation of a single crop in a given area. |The reliance on monoculture can make crops vulnerable to disease. 依赖单一栽培会使作物容易受到病害的影响。|Synonyms: single cropping | Antonyms: polyculture, mixed cropping | noun
