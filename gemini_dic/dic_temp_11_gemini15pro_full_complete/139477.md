obstination:/ˌɒbstɪˈneɪʃn/ /ˌɑːbstəˈneɪʃn/| n. |固执，顽固，执拗|The quality of being obstinate; stubbornness.  An act or instance of being obstinate.|His obstination in refusing to admit he was wrong was infuriating. 他拒不承认自己错了，这种固执的态度让人很生气。|Synonyms: stubbornness, tenacity, persistence, pertinacity, doggedness, inflexibility, intransigence, pigheadedness|Antonyms: flexibility, compliance, acquiescence, yielding, docility|noun
