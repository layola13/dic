chalcanthite:/kælˈkænθaɪt/
noun
胆矾，蓝矾
A vividly blue, crystalline, water-soluble sulfate mineral CuSO4·5H2O, occurring in arid regions.  It is formed by the weathering of copper sulfide ores.
Chalcanthite is often found as stalactites in old copper mines.  胆矾常以钟乳石的形式出现在古老的铜矿中。
Chalcanthite crystals are beautiful, but they are also fragile and dehydrate easily. 胆矾晶体很漂亮，但也易碎且容易脱水。
Synonym: blue vitriol, copper sulfate pentahydrate
