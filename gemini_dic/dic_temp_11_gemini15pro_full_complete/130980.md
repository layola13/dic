nonathlete:/ˌnɒnˈæθliːt/ | n. | 非运动员 |  A person who is not an athlete; someone who does not participate in sports, especially competitively. | He considered himself a nonathlete, preferring books to basketball. 他认为自己不是一个运动员，更喜欢看书而不是打篮球。| My brother is a complete nonathlete; he’d rather play video games. 我弟弟完全不是运动员；他宁愿玩电子游戏。| 近义词: non-sportsman, non-sportswoman | 反义词: athlete, sportsman, sportswoman | 词性: noun
