isobarometric:/ˌaɪsoʊˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
等压的
Characterized by equal barometric pressure.  Relating to or designating lines on a map connecting places having the same average barometric pressure at a given time or for a specified period.
Isobarometric lines on a weather map can help predict future weather patterns.  等压线在地图上能帮助预测未来的天气模式。

Isobarometric conditions are ideal for certain scientific experiments.  等压条件对于某些科学实验是理想的。
synonym: isobaric
antonym: anisobaric
