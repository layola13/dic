overpark: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːrk/ | v. | 过度停车; 停车占用过多空间 | To park a vehicle in a way that takes up more space than necessary or allowed, often encroaching on another parking space. | He overparked and took up two spaces. 他停车占用了两个车位。| The SUV overparked, blocking part of the driveway. 这辆SUV过度停车，挡住了部分车道。| 近义词: park badly, park incorrectly | 反义词: park neatly, park correctly | 词性: 动词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much") + "park" (verb meaning "to leave a vehicle standing").  The prefix "over-" modifies the verb "park" to indicate an excessive or incorrect manner of parking.
