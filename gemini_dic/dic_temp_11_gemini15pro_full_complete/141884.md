ordainable:/ɔːrˈdeɪnəbl/
adjective
可授予圣职的; 可被任命的
Suitable or qualified to be ordained.
He felt he wasn't ordainable because of his past. 他觉得由于过去的经历，他不适合被授予圣职。
近义词:eligible, appointable
反义词:ineligible, unappointable
词性:adjective
词源:From "ordain" + "-able". "Ordain" comes from Old French "ordener," ultimately from Latin "ordinare" meaning "to set in order, arrange."
