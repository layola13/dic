derelictness:/ˌderəˈlɪktnəs/
n.
玩忽职守；怠忽；失职
The state of having been abandoned and become dilapidated.  A shameful failure to fulfill one's obligations.
The derelictness of the city council allowed the problem to escalate. 市议会的玩忽职守导致问题升级。
The derelictness of their duties led to serious consequences.  他们玩忽职守导致了严重的后果。
近义词: negligence, neglect, failure, omission, carelessness
反义词: diligence, attentiveness, responsibility, conscientiousness
词性: 名词

词源:  From derelict + -ness. Derelict itself comes from the Latin *derelictus*, the past participle of *derelinquere* "to abandon," from *de-* "completely" + *relinquere* "to leave behind."
