gusseting: /ˈɡʌsɪtɪŋ/
n.
加固片，三角形衬布， gusset的动名词形式
The act of adding gussets to something, or the gussets themselves.
The gusseting in the crotch of these jeans makes them more comfortable.  这些牛仔裤裆部的三角形衬布使它们更舒适。
The gusseting on the sides of the bag allows it to expand. 包侧面的加固片使其可以扩展。
reinforcement, strengthening, bracing
weakening
noun
