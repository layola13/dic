interplay:/ˈɪntərpleɪ/| n.|相互作用，相互影响|The interplay between light and shadow creates a dramatic effect. 光与影的相互作用创造了戏剧性的效果。|例句：The interplay of economic and social factors is complex. 经济和社会因素的相互作用是复杂的。

例句：The interplay between supply and demand determines the price. 供求关系的相互作用决定了价格。

近义词：interaction, reciprocity, exchange|反义词：separation, isolation|词性：名词

词源：From inter- + play.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "play" refers to action or activity.  So "interplay" literally means the action or activity between or among things.
