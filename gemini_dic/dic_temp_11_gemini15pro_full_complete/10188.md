aporosa:/æpəˈroʊsə/| adj.|无孔的，不开孔的；(植物)无气孔的|Lacking pores or openings; (of plants) lacking stomata.|The aporosa leaf surface prevents excessive water loss.  这种无孔的叶子表面可以防止水分过度流失。|Synonyms:  impermeable, nonporous|Antonyms: porous, permeable|adjective

aporosa:/æpəˈroʊsə/| n.|  无孔植物；闭孔木|A plant lacking pores, especially stomata.|Aporosa species are often found in dry climates. 闭孔木属植物通常生长在干燥的气候中。|Synonyms:  (No close synonyms for the noun form, may use specific plant names)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, may use general terms like "porous plant")|noun
