larva:/ˈlɑːrvə/ (US), /ˈlɑːvə/ (UK)| n.|幼虫；幼体|The immature, wingless, and often worm-like feeding form of many insects before metamorphosis.|The butterfly larva eats leaves voraciously.  蝴蝶幼虫贪婪地吃着树叶。|Synonyms: grub, caterpillar, maggot|Antonyms: adult, imago|noun
