increment:/ˈɪŋkrəmənt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkrɪmənt/ (UK)|v. |增加，递增|to increase by a fixed degree|例句：The program increments the counter by one each time the loop is executed.  程序每次执行循环时，计数器都会递增1。|近义词：increase, raise, augment|反义词:decrement, decrease, reduce|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incrementum, from Latin increment-, stem of incrementum "growth, increase," from past participle stem of increscere "to grow in or upon," from in- "in" + crescere "to grow."
