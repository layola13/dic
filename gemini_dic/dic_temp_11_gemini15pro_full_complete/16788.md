basc:/bæsk/ | adj. | 基本的，基础的 | Of or relating to the Basque language or culture.  Pertaining to or characteristic of the Basque people or their language. |  Example: He's studying the Basc language. 他正在学习巴斯克语。

Example: Basc culture is rich in traditions. 巴斯克文化有着丰富的传统。 | 近义词: basic, fundamental, elementary | 反义词: advanced, complex, sophisticated | 词性: adjective

词源: From French *basque*, from Spanish *vasco*, from Latin *Vasco*, of unknown ultimate origin.
