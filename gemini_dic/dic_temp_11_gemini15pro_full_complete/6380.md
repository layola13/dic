amphictyony:/æmˈfɪktiəni/
n.
religious league; an association of neighboring states for their common interests, especially to protect a religious shrine
An amphictyony was a league of Greek tribes formed for the protection of a sacred site.
The Delphic Amphictyony was one of the most important in ancient Greece.
league, alliance, confederation
