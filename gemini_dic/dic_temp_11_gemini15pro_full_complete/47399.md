deadheading:/ˈdɛdˌhɛdɪŋ/
n.
去除枯萎的花朵; 空载行驶
Removing dead flower heads to encourage further blooming;  Operating a vehicle without paying passengers or cargo.
The gardener spent the morning deadheading the roses. 园丁早上都在去除玫瑰的枯萎花朵。
The truck driver was deadheading back to the depot. 卡车司机空车返回仓库。
近义词： pruning, trimming (for plants); empty run, light run (for vehicles)
反义词：  (For plants) letting flowers go to seed; (For vehicles) carrying passengers/cargo
