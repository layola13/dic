overdogmatic: /ˌoʊvərˌdɔːɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 过于教条的，过于武断的 | excessively dogmatic; adhering rigidly to principles or doctrines, often without considering practical consequences or alternative viewpoints. |  He was criticized for being overdogmatic in his approach to education. 他因其在教育方法上过于教条而受到批评。|  The professor's overdogmatic pronouncements alienated many of his students.  这位教授武断的言论疏远了他的许多学生。 |  dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire | open-minded, flexible, tolerant, pragmatic | adjective
