cupel:/ˈkjuːpəl/ (US), /ˈkʌpəl/ (UK)| n.|灰皿，烤钵|A small, porous cup, typically made of bone ash, used in assaying to separate precious metals from base metals by oxidation.|The goldsmith used a cupel to refine the silver. 金匠用烤钵提炼银子。|Synonym: crucible, scorifier|Antonym: N/A|noun

cupel:/ˈkjuːpəl/ (US), /ˈkʌpəl/ (UK)| vt.|用灰皿精炼|To refine or assay (precious metals) in a cupel.|He cupeled the ore to determine its gold content. 他用灰皿精炼矿石以确定其金含量。|Synonym: assay, refine|Antonym: N/A|verb
