npu:/ɛn piː juː/ |abbr.|神经处理单元 (Neural Processing Unit)|A specialized circuit designed to accelerate machine learning workloads, especially those involving neural networks.  It is optimized for matrix multiplications and other operations common in deep learning.|Example: This new laptop has a dedicated NPU for faster AI processing. 这款新的笔记本电脑有一个专用的NPU，可以加快人工智能处理速度。|Example:  The NPU significantly improved the performance of our image recognition software.  NPU显著提高了我们图像识别软件的性能。|近义词:AI accelerator, TPU|反义词:CPU, GPU|Abbreviation
