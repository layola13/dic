henchman:/ˈhɛntʃmən/ | n. | 帮凶; 走狗; 亲信; | A faithful follower, especially one prepared to engage in crime or violence on behalf of their leader. | The dictator surrounded himself with armed henchmen.  独裁者身边围绕着一群武装的帮凶。| Synonyms: minion, lackey, accomplice, goon, thug | Antonyms: leader, boss, opponent, adversary | noun


henchman:/ˈhɛntʃmən/ | n. | 心腹; | A trusted follower or supporter, especially one who is prepared to engage in unscrupulous or illegal activities for their leader. | The politician relied on his henchmen to spread misinformation.  这个政客依靠他的心腹散布虚假信息。| Synonyms: confidant, right-hand man, assistant, aide | Antonyms: opponent, adversary, rival | noun
