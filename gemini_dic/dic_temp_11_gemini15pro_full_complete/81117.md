ETYMOLOGY->
From grammar + -ical + -ness.  "Grammar" comes from Old French gramaire, ultimately from Greek grammatikē (tekhnē) "art of letters," from gramma "letter," related to graphē "writing." "-ical" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns ending in -ic. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
