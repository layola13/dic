omnipresent:/ˌɑːmnɪˈprɛzənt/| adj.|无所不在的，普遍存在的|Present everywhere at the same time.|例句：God is considered omnipresent by many religions. 许多宗教认为上帝无所不在。

例句：The influence of social media is omnipresent in modern society. 社交媒体的影响在现代社会中无处不在。|近义词：ubiquitous, pervasive, universal|反义词：localized, limited, rare|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *omnipraesent-*, present participle of *omnipraeesse* "to be present everywhere," from *omni-* "all" + *praeesse* "to be present."
