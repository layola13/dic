overliberally: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəli/
adverb
过于自由地；过分慷慨地
in a way that is too liberal, especially in giving or spending
He spent overliberally on his hobbies, often leaving himself short of money. 他在爱好上花钱过于慷慨，经常让自己缺钱。
近义词: excessively, too generously, extravagantly
反义词: stingily, frugally, parsimoniously
词性: adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "liberally" (adverb from "liberal," ultimately from Latin "liber" meaning "free")
