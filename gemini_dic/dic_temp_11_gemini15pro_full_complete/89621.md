hoofinesses:/ˈhuːfinəsɪz/
noun
蹄状物；蹄的特性
The quality or state of being hoofy; resemblance to a hoof, especially in hardness or shape.
The farrier assessed the hoofinesses of the horse's hooves after the long ride.  马蹄铁匠在长途骑行后评估了马蹄的蹄状程度。
His boots had developed a certain hoofinesses from years of walking on hard ground.  由于多年在坚硬的地面上行走，他的靴子已经形成了一种类似蹄子的硬度。
Synonyms: hardness, solidity, firmness
Antonyms: softness, flexibility
