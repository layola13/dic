dative:/ˈdeɪtɪv/| adj. n.|与格的，与格|Relating to or denoting a case of nouns (typically marking the indirect object or recipient of an action), or a word in that case.  A noun or pronoun in the dative case.|She gave him the book. (indirect object - *him* is in the dative case in some languages, represented by word form or preposition).  In German, "ihm" is the dative form of "er" (he).|近义词:recipient, beneficiary|反义词: (No direct antonym for a grammatical case)|noun, adjective
