ambush's:/ˈæmbʊʃɪz/
noun
埋伏者的；埋伏的
belonging to an ambush; of or relating to an ambush
The ambush's location was carefully chosen. 埋伏地点是精心挑选的。
近义词:attacker's, assailant's
反义词:defender's, protector's
词性:noun (possessive form)
