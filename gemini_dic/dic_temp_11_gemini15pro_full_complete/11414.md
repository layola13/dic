argentino:/ˌɑːr.dʒənˈtiː.noʊ/ /ˌɑːr.dʒənˈtiː.nəʊ/
noun|阿根廷人|A person from Argentina.
Many Argentinos emigrated to Europe during the economic crisis.|许多阿根廷人在经济危机期间移民到欧洲。
He is an Argentino, proud of his heritage.|他是一位阿根廷人，为自己的传统感到自豪。
近义词:Argentinian
反义词:None
词性:noun


词源: From Spanish *argentino*, from Latin *argentum* (silver).  The region was believed to be rich in silver.
