nonartistical: /ˌnɒnɑːˈtɪstɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnɑːrˈtɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 非艺术的，缺乏艺术性的 | Not artistic; lacking artistic qualities. |  Her nonartistical approach to interior design resulted in a rather bland and uninspired space. 她非艺术性的室内设计方法导致空间相当平淡无奇，缺乏灵感。| The report was factual and nonartistical, focusing solely on data and statistics. 这份报告是事实性的、非艺术性的，只关注数据和统计。| 近义词: inartistic, unartistic, nonaesthetic | 反义词: artistic, aesthetic | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "artistic" (adjective meaning "relating to art").  "Artistic" ultimately comes from the Latin "ars" meaning "art" or "skill."
