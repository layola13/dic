classy:/ˈklæsi/| adj.|优等的；高级的；漂亮的；时髦的|Stylish and sophisticated; elegant.|例句：She wore a classy black dress. 她穿着一件优雅的黑色连衣裙。|近义词：elegant, stylish, sophisticated, chic, posh|反义词：tacky, vulgar, gaudy, tasteless|词性:形容词
词源: From class + -y.  Originally meaning 'pertaining to social class', later evolving to mean 'having the qualities associated with high social class', such as elegance and sophistication.
