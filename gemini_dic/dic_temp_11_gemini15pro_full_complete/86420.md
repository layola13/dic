heedily:/ˈhiːdɪli/| adv.|轻率地，不注意地|He heedily signed the contract without reading it carefully. 他不加注意地签了合同，没有仔细阅读。|例句：She heedily crossed the street without looking both ways. 她不注意地过了马路，没有左右看。|近义词：rashly, carelessly, recklessly, thoughtlessly|反义词：carefully, cautiously, prudently|词性:副词

词源: From heed (注意) + -ily (副词后缀). Heed comes from Old English *hēdan, from Proto-Germanic *hauzijan.
