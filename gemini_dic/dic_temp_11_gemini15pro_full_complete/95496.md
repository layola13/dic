inclementnesses:/ɪnˈklɛməntnəsɪz/
n.
(plural of inclementness) periods of inclement weather; harsh weather conditions.
The inclementnesses of the winter made travel difficult. 冬天的恶劣天气使旅行变得困难。
近义词: severities, harshness, roughness, inclemencies
反义词: mildness, calmness, serenity, pleasantness
词性: noun


Etymology:
From inclement + -ness.  Inclement itself comes from in- (not) + clement (mild, merciful), from Latin clemens.
