miscreate:/ˌmɪskrɪˈeɪt/
verb
错误地创造；拙劣地模仿
To create in a deformed or defective manner; to make badly or improperly.
例句：The artist felt he had miscreated the portrait and destroyed it.  这位艺术家觉得他把这幅肖像画画坏了，于是把它毁掉了。

例句：Some believe that humans were miscreated, flawed from the beginning.  有些人认为人类是被错误地创造出来的，从一开始就有缺陷。

近义词：deform, distort, mangle, corrupt, spoil
反义词：create, perfect, refine
词性：v.
