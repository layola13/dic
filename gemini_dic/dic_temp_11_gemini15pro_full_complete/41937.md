cosmonauts:/ˈkɒzmənɔːts/ | n. | 宇航员，航天员 | A person trained to travel in a spacecraft; especially one from Russia or the former Soviet Union. |  Yuri Gagarin was the first cosmonaut to orbit the Earth. 尤里·加加林是第一个绕地球飞行的宇航员。| synonyms: astronaut, spaceman, space traveler | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian *kosmonavt*, from *kosmo-* (combining form of *kosmos* "cosmos, universe") + *navt* "sailor, navigator"
