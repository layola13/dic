hypermodernists:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdənɪsts/ | n. | 超现代主义者 |  A proponent or practitioner of hypermodernism, a cultural or artistic movement characterized by an extreme emphasis on modern or futuristic aesthetics, technology, and social values, often rejecting traditional forms and embracing radical experimentation. | The hypermodernists of the 1920s embraced new technologies and materials in their art.  20世纪20年代的超现代主义者在他们的艺术中采用了新技术和新材料。|  modernists, avant-gardists | traditionalists, conservatives | noun
