betacism:/ˈbeɪtəsɪzəm/
n.
混淆b和v的发音；β化；β音化
The pronunciation of 'v' as 'b', or the reverse.  This is common in some Spanish dialects.
Many speakers of Spanish exhibit betacism, pronouncing both "b" and "v" as a bilabial fricative.  许多西班牙语使用者都表现出β化现象，将“b”和“v”都发成双唇擦音。
Betacism is a common feature of several Romance languages. β化是几种罗曼语的共同特征。
近义词: lenition (though broader)
反义词:  (No direct antonym; perhaps hypercorrection in some cases)
