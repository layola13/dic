oscillaria:/ˌɒsɪˈleəriə/ | n. |颤藻属| A genus of filamentous cyanobacteria (blue-green algae) that are common in freshwater environments. They are characterized by their ability to oscillate or move back and forth. | Example: Oscillaria is often found in stagnant water. 颤藻通常在死水中发现。| Example: The bloom of Oscillaria can cause water quality problems. 颤藻的大量繁殖会导致水质问题。|Synonym: blue-green algae| Antonym: N/A | Noun
