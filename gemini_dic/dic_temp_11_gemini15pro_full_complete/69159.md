fawner:/ˈfɔːnər/ (US), /ˈfɔːnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|奉承者，拍马屁的人|A person who behaves in a servile way to win favor from someone.|He's such a fawner, always trying to get on the boss's good side. 他真是个马屁精，总是想讨好老板。|Synonyms: sycophant, flatterer, toady, yes-man|Antonyms: critic, detractor|Noun
