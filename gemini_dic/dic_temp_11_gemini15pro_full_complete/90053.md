hospitalized: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzd/ (US)
|v. | 住院，留院观察 |Admitted to a hospital for treatment or observation.| She was hospitalized for a week after the accident. 她因事故住院一周。|  例句：He was hospitalized with pneumonia. 他因肺炎住院。| 近义词：admitted, interned | 反义词: discharged, released |词性: Verb (past participle)

hospitalized: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzd/ (US)
|adj. | 住院的 |Receiving treatment in a hospital.| The hospitalized patient was recovering well. 住院病人恢复得很好。| 例句：My grandmother is currently hospitalized. 我的祖母目前住院了。 | 近义词：inpatient, confined | 反义词: outpatient, discharged |词性: Adjective
