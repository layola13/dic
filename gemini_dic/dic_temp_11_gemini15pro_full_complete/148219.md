overshrunken:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋkən/
adjective
过度缩水的；缩水过度的
having shrunk excessively, usually from being washed in water that is too hot.
The sweater was overshrunken and no longer fit.  这件毛衣缩水过度，再也穿不上了。
My favorite wool socks are overshrunken. 我最喜欢的羊毛袜子缩水过度了。
近义词: shrunken, too small
反义词: oversized, too big
词性: adjective

overshrunk:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋk/
verb (past participle of overshrink)
过度缩水
having shrunk excessively, usually from being washed in water that is too hot. (This form is less common than the adjective "overshrunken.")
The sweater was overshrunk in the wash. 这件毛衣在洗涤过程中缩水过度了。
The shirt had been overshrunk and was now child-sized. 这件衬衫缩水过度，现在只有小孩的尺寸了。
近义词: shrunk, reduced in size
反义词: stretched, enlarged
词性: verb (past participle)

词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "shrink" (verb)
"Over-" denotes an excess or going beyond a normal limit.
"Shrink" comes from Old English "scrincan," meaning "to contract" or "wither."
