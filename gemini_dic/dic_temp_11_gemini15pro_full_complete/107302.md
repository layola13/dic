lamarckism:/ləˈmɑːrkɪzəm/| n. |拉马克主义|A theory of organic evolution asserting that acquired characters are inherited by succeeding generations.|Lamarckism is no longer a widely accepted theory. 拉马克主义不再是一个被广泛接受的理论。|Near synonyms:  inheritance of acquired characteristics, soft inheritance|Antonyms: Darwinism, neo-Darwinism|noun
