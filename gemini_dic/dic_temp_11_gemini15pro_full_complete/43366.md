cranioclasm: /ˈkreɪniəˌklæzəm/ | n. | 碎颅术，颅骨压碎术 | The crushing of a fetal skull to facilitate delivery in cases of obstructed labor.  This procedure is now obsolete and considered dangerous. | Cranioclasm was a last resort in difficult childbirth before the advent of modern obstetrical techniques.  碎颅术是现代产科技术出现之前难产时的最后手段。 |  Synonyms: cephalotripsy | Antonyms: Cesarean section | noun

cranioclast: /ˈkreɪniəˌklæst/ | n. | 碎颅器 | An instrument used in cranioclasm. | The cranioclast was a feared instrument in the 19th century. 19世纪，碎颅器是一种令人恐惧的工具。| Synonyms: cephalotribe | Antonyms: None | noun
