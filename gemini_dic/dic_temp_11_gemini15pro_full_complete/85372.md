harrumphing:/hɑːˈrʌmfɪŋ/ /hɑrˈʌmpfɪŋ/
| n. |  不满的哼哼声，表示不悦的嘟囔 | An act of harrumphing; a sound made to express displeasure or disapproval. | He greeted the news with a loud harrumph. 他大声哼了一声，以此回应这个消息。|近义词：grumbling, muttering, complaining |反义词: praising, approving
| v. |  发出不满的哼哼声，表示不悦地嘟囔 | To make a sound expressing displeasure or disapproval. | She harrumphed and walked away. 她哼了一声，走开了。 | 近义词：grumble, mutter, complain | 反义词: praise, approve
