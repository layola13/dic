multiple:/ˈmʌltɪpl/| adj. n.| 多个的，多重的；倍数；<数>倍数 |A multiple is a number that can be divided by another number a certain number of times without a remainder.| This store sells multiple brands of shoes.  这家商店销售多个品牌的鞋子。| synonyms: numerous, many, several | antonyms: single, few, individual | adjective

multiple:/ˈmʌltɪpl/| n.| 倍数 |A multiple of a number is the product of that number and any integer.| 12 is a multiple of 3. 12是3的倍数。| synonyms: product, factor | antonyms: divisor, factor | noun
