nonprovocations:/ˌnɒnˌprɒvəˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
无挑衅行为；非挑衅行为
absence of anything that provokes or incites.
The ceasefire held despite minor nonprovocations across the border. 尽管边境发生了一些小的非挑衅行为，停火协议仍然有效。
Synonyms:  peacefulness, tranquility, calmness, quiet
Antonyms: provocations, incitements, aggressions, hostilities
noun


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + provocation (from Late Middle English provocacioun, from Old French provocacion, from Latin provocatio, from provocare "to challenge").
