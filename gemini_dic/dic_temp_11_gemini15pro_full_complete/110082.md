lichenography:/laɪkəˈnɒɡrəfi/
noun
地衣学
The study of lichens.
Lichens are fascinating organisms, and lichenography helps us understand their diversity and ecological roles.  地衣是迷人的生物体，地衣学帮助我们了解它们的多样性和生态作用。
Lichenography is a specialized field within botany. 地衣学是植物学中的一个专门领域。
Synonym: lichenology
