homies:/ˈhoʊmiz/| n.|哥们儿，朋友，老乡 (Informal)|Close friends, often used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).  It conveys a strong sense of camaraderie and loyalty.|例句：My homies and I are going to the game tonight.  我和我的哥们儿今晚要去看比赛。|近义词：friends, pals, buddies, mates|反义词:enemies, foes|名词
