cliffy: /ˈklɪfi/ | adj. | 陡峭的；悬崖的 | Characterized by cliffs; steep. | The road became increasingly cliffy as we approached the coast.  这条路随着我们靠近海岸变得越来越陡峭。 |  The cliffy terrain made hiking difficult. 悬崖般的地形使徒步旅行变得困难。 | 近义词: steep, precipitous, sheer | 反义词: gentle, sloping, flat | 词性: 形容词

词源: From cliff + -y.  来自 cliff (悬崖) + -y (形容词后缀，表示“具有…性质的”)
