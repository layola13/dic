acrobatism: /ˈækrəˌbætɪzəm/
n.
杂技；特技；惊险动作
The performance featured incredible feats of acrobatism.
演出展现了令人难以置信的杂技技艺。
Acrobatism requires strength, balance, and coordination.
杂技需要力量、平衡和协调。
Etymology: From French *acrobatisme*, from *acrobate* (acrobat) + *-isme* (-ism). Ultimately from Greek *akrobatēs* "walking on tiptoe, rope-dancer," from *akron* "summit, extremity" + *bainein* "to go, walk, step."
Synonyms:  stunt, feat, maneuver, gymnastics
Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness
