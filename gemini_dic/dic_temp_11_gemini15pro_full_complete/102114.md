isotonic:/ˌaɪsəˈtɒnɪk/ /ˌaɪsoʊˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|等渗的，等张的|Having the same osmotic pressure as another solution, especially one in a cell or a body fluid.|例句：An isotonic sports drink can help rehydrate the body after exercise.  等渗运动饮料可以帮助运动后身体补充水分。|近义词：isosmotic|反义词：hypotonic, hypertonic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- "equal" + Greek tonos "tension".
