indention:/ɪnˈdɛnʃən/ | n. | 缩进，凹陷 |  The act of indenting something; the state of being indented.  A blank space left at the beginning of a line of print or writing. | The first line of each paragraph should have an indention. 每段的第一行应该缩进。 |  indentation, recess,  depression | protrusion, projection | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indentationem, accusative of *indentatio, from indentatus, past participle of indentare "to notch," from in- "in" + dens (genitive dentis) "tooth."
