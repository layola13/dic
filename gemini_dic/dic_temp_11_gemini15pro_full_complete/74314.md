fracturer:/ˈfræktʃərər/| n.|骨折者；造成骨折的人或物|A person or thing that fractures something.|The earthquake was the fracturer of many buildings. 地震是许多建筑物倒塌的原因。|The bone fracturer was a fall from a horse. 造成骨折的原因是从马上摔下来。| synonyms: breaker, splitter | antonyms: mender, healer | noun

fracture:/ˈfræktʃər/| n. v.|骨折；断裂；破裂|
(n.) The breaking of a bone or other hard material.  (v.) To break (a bone or other hard material).|
(n.) He suffered a fracture in his leg. 他的腿骨折了。
(v.) He fractured his arm in the accident. 他在事故中摔断了胳膊。|
synonyms: (n.) break, crack, rupture; (v.) break, crack, shatter | antonyms: (n.) mend, repair; (v.) mend, repair, heal | noun, verb
