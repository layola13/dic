fatiloquent:/fætˈɪləkwənt/| adj.|多言的，废话连篇的|Speaking or writing at tedious length; using many words where few would do; verbose; prolix.|例句：The fatiloquent speaker droned on for hours, boring the audience to tears.  这位废话连篇的演讲者喋喋不休地讲了几个小时，把听众都烦得要命。

例句：His fatiloquent prose style made it difficult to extract the main points of his argument.  他冗长散漫的散文风格让人很难提取他论证的要点。|近义词：verbose, prolix, long-winded, garrulous, loquacious|反义词：concise, succinct, terse, laconic|词性：形容词
