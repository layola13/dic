megaflora:/ˈmɛɡəˌflɔːrə/ | n. | 巨型植物群 |  The large-sized flora of a particular region or geological period.  Specifically, plants of a size larger than trees, such as giant ferns, club mosses, and horsetails that were common in the Carboniferous period. | The Carboniferous period is known for its lush megaflora. 石炭纪以其繁茂的巨型植物群而闻名。

The museum's exhibit showcased the megaflora of the prehistoric era. 博物馆的展览展示了史前时代的巨型植物群。| 近义词: macroflora | 反义词: microflora | 词性: noun
