exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/| n. |恶化，加剧|An increase in the severity of a disease or its signs and symptoms.|The smoky conditions exacerbated her asthma. 烟雾弥漫的环境加剧了她的哮喘。|Synonyms: aggravation, worsening, intensification|Antonyms: improvement, amelioration, alleviation|noun

exacerbate:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪt/| v. |使恶化，加剧|To make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse.|The conflict was exacerbated by the media. 媒体加剧了这场冲突。|Synonyms: aggravate, worsen, intensify|Antonyms: improve, ameliorate, alleviate|verb
