hypochondriacal:/ˌhaɪpəʊkɒnˈdraɪəkl/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəʊkɑːnˈdraɪəkl/| adj.|疑病的，患疑病症的|Excessively or abnormally concerned with one's health; having imaginary illnesses.|例句：She's always been a bit hypochondriacal, rushing to the doctor for every little ache or pain. 她一直有点疑病症，每次有点小病小痛就去看医生。|近义词：neurotic, anxious, obsessive|反义词:healthy, well|词性:形容词
