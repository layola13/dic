aimlessness:/ˈeɪmləsnəs/
noun
漫无目的；无目标
lack of aim or purpose
He drifted through life in a state of aimlessness. 他漫无目的地过着生活。

近义词: purposelessness, drifting, lack of direction, indecision, vacillation
反义词: purposefulness, determination, resolve, direction, focus
词性: noun

词源：
From aimless + -ness.  Aimless comes from the Old Norse *aumr meaning "out of the way,"  combined with the Old English -lēas  meaning "without."  Thus, aimless literally means "without direction."
