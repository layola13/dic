accordionists:/əˈkɔːrdiənɪsts/
noun
手风琴演奏者，手风琴家
A person who plays the accordion.
The accordionists played a lively polka.  手风琴演奏者们演奏了一首活泼的波尔卡。
Synonyms: accordion player, musician
Antonyms: listener, audience
词性: 名词
