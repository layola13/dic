graffer:/ˈɡræfər/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːfər/ (UK)| n. |嫁接者；贪污受贿者；警探；（尤指铁路的）连接工|A person who grafts plants; a person who takes bribes; a detective, especially in the railway police; a person who connects things, especially railway carriages.|

例句：
The skilled graffer successfully joined the two branches of the apple tree.
这位熟练的嫁接者成功地将苹果树的两个树枝嫁接在一起。

The newspaper exposed the graffer who had been accepting bribes from construction companies.
报纸曝光了那个一直收受建筑公司贿赂的贪污者。

近义词：bribe-taker, corrupt official (for graffer as "one who takes bribes"); detective, investigator (for graffer as "detective"); joiner, connector (for graffer as "one who connects")|
反义词：honest official (for graffer as "one who takes bribes")|
词性：noun
