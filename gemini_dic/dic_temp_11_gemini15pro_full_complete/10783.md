aquarial:/əˈkwɛəriəl/ | adj. | 水族的；水族馆的；像水族馆的 | Relating to aquariums or the keeping of fish and other aquatic life in tanks. | The store specializes in aquarial supplies. 这家商店专门经营水族用品。 |The local university has a fascinating aquarial research lab.  这所地方大学有一个很棒的水族研究实验室。| 近义词: aquatic, marine | 反义词: terrestrial | 词性: 形容词

aquarial:/əˈkwɛəriəl/ | n. | 水族馆；养鱼池；玻璃缸 | A building or tank for keeping and displaying fish and other aquatic life. |  We took the children to the aquarial for the afternoon. 我们下午带孩子们去了水族馆。| We have a small aquarial in our living room. 我们的客厅里有一个小鱼缸。 | 近义词: aquarium, fish tank | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'terrarium') | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *aquariālis*, from *aquārium* (“reservoir, water-pipe”)  + *-ālis*.
