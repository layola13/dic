antipatriot:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriət/ |n.|反爱国者，不爱国的人|A person who does not love or support their own country. |例句：The antipatriot publicly denounced the country's policies.  这个反爱国者公开谴责国家的政策。

例句：He was accused of being an antipatriot for criticizing the government. 他因为批评政府而被指责为不爱国者。


|近义词：traitor, dissident, defector|反义词：patriot, nationalist|词性：名词
