actinometry:/ˌæktɪˈnɒmɪtri/
noun
光化學測量；光能測定；光化測量學
The science of measuring the intensity of radiant energy, especially that of the sun, by chemical effects.
Actinometry is used to measure the intensity of sunlight. 光化學測量用於測量太陽光的強度。
The researchers used actinometry to determine the UV exposure. 研究人員使用光化學測量法來確定紫外線照射量。
synonyms: radiometry, photometry
antonyms: N/A
