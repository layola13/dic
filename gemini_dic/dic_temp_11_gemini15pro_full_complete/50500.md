depressurizing:/ˌdiːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb|减压，卸压|The act of reducing pressure, especially in a sealed container or aircraft cabin.|The pilot began depressurizing the cabin as we approached our destination. 飞行员在我们接近目的地时开始给机舱减压。| depressurization, decompression | pressurization, compression |v.

depressurize:/ˌdiːˈprɛʃəˌraɪz/
verb|减压，卸压|To reduce the pressure in a sealed container or aircraft cabin.|They depressurized the aircraft cabin before landing. 他们在着陆前降低了机舱的压力。|depress, release pressure| pressurize, compress|v.

depressurization:/ˌdiːˈprɛʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun|减压，卸压|The process of reducing pressure.|Rapid depressurization can be dangerous. 快速减压很危险。|decompression|pressurization, compression|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + pressurize (from pressure + -ize (suffix meaning "to cause to be")).  The root word "pressure" comes from the Latin "pressura," meaning "a pressing."
