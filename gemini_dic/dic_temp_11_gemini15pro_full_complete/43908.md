crev:/krɛv/ | n. |裂缝，缝隙|A narrow opening or fissure, especially in rock or ice.|例句：The climbers found a crev in the rock face and used it as a handhold. 登山者在岩壁上发现了一条裂缝，并用它作为支撑点。

例句：Water seeped through the crev in the dam. 水从大坝的裂缝中渗出。|近义词：crack, fissure, crevice, chink, gap|反义词：seal, closure|词性：名词
