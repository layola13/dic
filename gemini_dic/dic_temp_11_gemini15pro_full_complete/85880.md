headborrow:/ˈhɛdˌbɒroʊ/ (美式) /ˈhɛdˌbɒrəʊ/ (英式)
|n.|借款；贷款；借入|The act of borrowing, especially money.|He took out a headborrow to start his business. 他借了一笔钱来创业。|近义词：loan, borrowing|反义词：lending|词性：名词
Etymology:  A blend of "head" and "borrow," likely implying borrowing a significant sum, perhaps enough to cover one's "head" (expenses).  This word is not standard English and is likely a neologism or a regionalism.  Its usage should be approached with caution in formal settings.
