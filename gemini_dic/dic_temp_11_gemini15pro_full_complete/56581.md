don't-know:/ˌdoʊnt ˈnoʊ/ |  phrase | 不知道 |  A contraction of "do not know," indicating a lack of knowledge or understanding. | Example: I don't know the answer to that question. 我不知道那个问题的答案。 | Synonym:  unaware, ignorant, uncertain | Antonym: know, aware, certain | phrase

don't-know:/ˌdoʊnt ˈnoʊ/ | adjective (informal) | 未知的，不明的 | Used to describe something that is not yet known or identified. | Example: The effects of the new policy are still don't-know. 新政策的影响仍然未知。| Synonym: unknown, undetermined, unspecified | Antonym: known, determined, specified | adjective
