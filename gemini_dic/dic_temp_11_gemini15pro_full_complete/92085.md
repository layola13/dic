hyperreactors:/ˌhaɪpərriˈæktərz/| n. |过度反应者|People who respond to stimuli with exaggerated or excessive emotional or physical reactions.  Those who hyperreact often display heightened sensitivity and intensity in their responses, which can manifest in various ways depending on the individual and the triggering situation.|例句：The hyperreactors in the audience screamed and fainted at the slightest shock.  观众中那些过度反应的人在最轻微的惊吓下尖叫和晕倒。

例句：Hyperreactors might interpret a minor disagreement as a major betrayal.  过度反应者可能会将一个小小的分歧理解为重大的背叛。|近义词：overreactors, alarmists|反义词:underreactors, stoics|词性:noun
