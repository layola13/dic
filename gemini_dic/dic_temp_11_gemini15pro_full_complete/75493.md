fruticose:/ˈfruːtɪkoʊs/ | adj. | 灌木状的；像灌木的 | Resembling a shrub; shrubby.  Having a bushy, branching growth habit. |  Example: The lichen had a fruticose growth form, branching out like a miniature tree.  地衣呈灌木状生长，像一棵微型树一样分支出来。

Example:  Fruticose lichens are often found hanging from trees in humid forests.  灌木状地衣经常出现在潮湿森林的树上。 | 近义词: shrubby, bushy, branching | 反义词: crustose, foliose | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fruticōsus, from frutex (“shrub”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of," "abounding in").
