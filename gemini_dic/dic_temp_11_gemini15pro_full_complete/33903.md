circumvent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvent/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvɛnt/
verb
绕开，规避
to avoid something, especially cleverly or illegally
例句：They found a way to circumvent the law. 他们找到了规避法律的方法。

例句：The company circumvented the trade embargo by shipping goods through a third country.  这家公司通过经由第三国运输货物来规避贸易禁运。

近义词：avoid, evade, bypass, dodge, sidestep
反义词：confront, face, comply with
词性：verb
