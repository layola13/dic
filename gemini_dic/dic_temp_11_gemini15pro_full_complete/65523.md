eurythmy:/ˈjʊərɪθmi/ | n. |优律思美，律动 |A system of expressive movement, often to music, developed by Rudolf Steiner and used in Waldorf education. It aims to make visible the inner life of music and speech.| The children performed a beautiful eurythmy piece at the Waldorf school festival. 孩子们在华德福学校的节日里表演了一段优美的优律思美。| synonyms: expressive movement, artistic movement, interpretive dance | antonyms: stillness, immobility | noun
