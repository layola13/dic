derivational:/ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃənəl/ |adj.| 衍生的，派生的 |Relating to or resulting from derivation.  A derivational affix changes the part of speech of the word it attaches to or significantly changes the meaning of the word. |The word "happiness" is a derivational formation from "happy".  “快乐”这个词是从“幸福”派生出来的。 | The derivational suffix "-ness" changes the adjective "happy" into the noun "happiness".  派生后缀“-ness”将形容词“happy”变为名词“happiness”.| derivative, derived | - | adjective
