dreadless:/ˈdrɛdlɪs/| adj.|无畏的，无所畏惧的|Free from dread; fearless; undaunted.  Not feeling dread; without fear or apprehension.|He walked dreadless into the dark forest. 他无所畏惧地走进了黑暗的森林。|Synonyms: fearless, undaunted, intrepid, brave, valiant|Antonyms: fearful, afraid, apprehensive, timid, cowardly|adjective
