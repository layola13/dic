conducible:/kənˈdjuːsɪbl/
adjective
有益的，有助于…的
tending to promote or assist; helpful
例句：Regular exercise is conducive to good health.  规律的运动有益于健康。

例句：A quiet environment is conducive to studying. 安静的环境有利于学习。

近义词：helpful, beneficial, advantageous, favorable
反义词：detrimental, harmful, unfavorable, hindering
词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin conducibilis, from conducere ‘bring together, contribute’, from con- ‘together’ + ducere ‘to lead’.
