fleeing:/ˈfliːɪŋ/ | v. | 逃跑，逃离 | Running away from danger or pursuit. | The thief was seen fleeing the scene.  小偷被看到逃离了现场。| synonyms: escaping, absconding, running away | antonyms:  approaching, confronting, staying | verb

fleeing:/ˈfliːɪŋ/ | adj. | 逃亡的，逃跑的 |  Describing someone or something that is running away. | They helped the fleeing refugees. 他们帮助了逃亡的难民。| synonyms: escaping, runaway | antonyms:  approaching, returning | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēon, from Proto-Germanic *fleuhaną, from Proto-Indo-European *pleuk-. Cognate with Dutch vlieden, German fliehen.
