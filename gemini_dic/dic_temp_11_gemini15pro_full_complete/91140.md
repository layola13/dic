hybridized:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzd/| adj.|杂交的，混合的|Combined or mixed different elements, often referring to species in biology or concepts in other fields.|例句：The hybridized plant showed characteristics from both parent species. 杂交植物显示了来自两个亲本物种的特征。

例句：The car uses a hybridized engine, combining gas and electric power. 这辆车使用混合动力发动机，结合了汽油和电力。|近义词：mixed, combined, crossbred, blended|反义词：pure, unmixed|词性:adjective

hybridize:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/| v. |杂交，混合|To combine or mix different elements, often referring to species in biology or concepts in other fields.|例句：Scientists hybridized the two species of roses to create a new variety.  科学家将两种玫瑰杂交以创造一个新品种。

例句：The artist hybridized different painting styles in his work. 这位艺术家在他的作品中混合了不同的绘画风格。|近义词：mix, combine, crossbreed, blend|反义词：separate, purify|词性:verb
