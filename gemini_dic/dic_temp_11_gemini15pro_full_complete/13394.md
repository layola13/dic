atomician:/ˌætəˈmɪʃən/ | n. |原子论者;原子学家|A person who believes in or studies atomism (the theory that all matter is composed of tiny indivisible particles called atoms). | Democritus was an ancient Greek atomician. 德谟克利特是一位古希腊原子论者。| The atomician explained the properties of matter in terms of atoms and their interactions. 原子论者用原子及其相互作用来解释物质的性质。 |synonyms: atomist, physicist (in some contexts) | antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone who believes in a continuous theory of matter) | noun
