ifying:/ɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 使...化的，具有...性质的 |  A suffix that forms adjectives from verbs, indicating something that causes or has the quality of the verb's action.  | The pacifying effect of the music calmed the baby.  音乐的抚慰作用使婴儿平静下来。| calming, soothing, quieting | agitating, exciting | suffix
