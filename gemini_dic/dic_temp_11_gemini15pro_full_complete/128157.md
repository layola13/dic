nettle:/ˈnɛtl/ (US), /ˈnetl/ (UK)| v.  adj.| 激怒，惹恼；荨麻|To irritate or annoy someone.|例句：His constant criticism nettled her. 他不断的批评惹恼了她。|近义词：irritate, annoy, vex, provoke, irk|反义词: please, soothe, calm|词性:动词

nettle:/ˈnɛtl/ (US), /ˈnetl/ (UK)| adj.| 恼怒的；被激怒的|Irritated; annoyed.|例句：She gave him a nettled look. 她恼怒地瞪了他一眼。|近义词：irritated, annoyed, vexed, provoked, irked|反义词: pleased, soothed, calmed|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *netele, from Proto-Germanic *natilō, from Proto-Indo-European *nad-.  Cognate with Dutch netel, German Nessel.  The verb sense "to irritate" arose from the stinging hairs of the nettle plant.
