crassest:/ˈkræsɪst/ | adj. | 最愚钝的；最粗俗的；最粗鲁的 |  Superlative form of crass: showing no intelligence or sensitivity.  | His crassest joke offended everyone in the room. 他最粗俗的笑话冒犯了房间里的每个人。 | Synonyms: insensitive, stupid, unrefined, vulgar, boorish | Antonyms: refined, sophisticated, sensitive, intelligent, cultured | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *crassus* "thick, fat, dense," figuratively "gross, coarse."
