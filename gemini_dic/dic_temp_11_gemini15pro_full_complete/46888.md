Dario:/ˈdɑːrioʊ/ (US), /ˈdærioʊ/ (UK)| n. |达里奥|A male given name, the Italian, Spanish, and Portuguese form of Darius, meaning "possessing good" or "upholding good".  It originates from the Old Persian name *Dārayavauš*.|Dario is a popular name in several European countries.|近义词:Darius, Darrius|反义词:N/A|Noun
