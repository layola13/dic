eelbuck: /ˈiːlbʌk/
noun
鲌鱼的一种
A freshwater fish of the carp family, found in Europe and Asia.  Specifically refers to the common bleak (Alburnus alburnus).
例句：The eelbuck shimmered as it swam through the clear river.  鲌鱼在清澈的河流中游动时闪闪发光。

例句：Fishermen often use eelbuck as bait for larger fish. 渔民经常用鲌鱼作为诱饵来钓更大的鱼。
近义词：bleak, minnow
