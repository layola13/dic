allotment: /əˈlɒtmənt/ (UK) /əˈlɑːtmənt/ (US)| n. |分配；份额；小块菜地；拨款|A portion or share of something allocated to someone or something.  A plot of land rented by an individual for growing vegetables and fruit.|例句：The allotment of funds for the project was insufficient.  这个项目的资金分配不足。

例句：He spends his weekends tending his allotment. 他周末都在照料他的小块菜地。

近义词：allocation, portion, share, plot, patch|反义词:confiscation, seizure|词性:noun
