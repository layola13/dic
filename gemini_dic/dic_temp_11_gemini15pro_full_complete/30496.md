cents:/sɛnts/ | n. | 分，分币 | A monetary unit equal to one hundredth of a dollar. |  Example 1: The candy costs fifty cents. 这颗糖果要五十美分。

Example 2:  He found a few cents on the sidewalk. 他在人行道上发现了几个分币。 | synonyms: pennies, coins | antonym: dollar | noun
