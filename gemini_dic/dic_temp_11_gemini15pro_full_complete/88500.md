historiographies: /ˌhɪstɔːriˈɒɡrəfiz/ /ˌhɪstɔːriˈɑːɡrəfiz/ | n. | 史学；史学史；历史编纂学 | The study of the writing of history and of written histories.  It considers the various approaches used by historians over time and how these approaches have shaped our understanding of the past. |  Example 1:  Modern historiographies challenge the traditional narratives of colonialism. 现代史学对传统的殖民主义叙事提出了挑战。

Example 2: He is specializing in the historiographies of the French Revolution. 他专门研究法国大革命的史学。 | 近义词: historical writings, historical scholarship | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a field of study) | 词性: noun
