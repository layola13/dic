compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənət/| adj.|有同情心的，表示怜悯的|Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others.|She is a compassionate nurse who always puts her patients first. 她是一位富有同情心的护士，总是把病人放在第一位。|例句：The compassionate judge gave the young offender a light sentence. 富有同情心的法官对年轻的罪犯从轻判决。

例句：He felt compassionate towards the homeless man. 他对这个无家可归的人感到同情。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring, humane|反义词：cruel, heartless, indifferent, callous|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *compassionat, from compassion.
