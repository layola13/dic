bar:/bɑːr/ (US) /bɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|酒吧；条，杆；障碍；律师界|A place where alcoholic drinks are served; a long, rigid piece of metal or wood; something that obstructs or prevents progress or achievement; the legal profession.|例句：
The hotel has a bar and a restaurant.  这家酒店有一个酒吧和一个餐厅。

He lifted the heavy metal bar. 他举起了沉重的金属杆。

Poverty is a bar to education. 贫穷是教育的障碍。

He was called to the bar last year. 他去年获得了律师资格。

|近义词：pub, saloon, rod, stick, obstacle, hindrance, legal profession|反义词: allowance, aid, pass, opening|词性:名词, 动词
