monometallic:/ˌmɒnəməˈtælɪk/ | adj. |单金属的|Consisting of a single metal; made of only one metal.|The catalyst used in the experiment was monometallic, composed solely of platinum.|近义词: single-metal, unimetallic |反义词: bimetallic, polymetallic |词性:形容词
Etymology:  "mono-" (Greek: μόνος, mónos, "alone", "single") + "metallic" (from "metal," ultimately from Greek μέταλλον, métallon, "mine," "quarry," "metal").
