hydrogalvanic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊɡælˈvænɪk/
adjective
水流电的；由水流产生的电流的
Relating to or denoting electricity generated by the action of water, especially in a stream or waterfall.
Hydrogalvanic power is a relatively unexplored source of renewable energy. 水流发电是一种相对未开发的可再生能源。
The hydrogalvanic effect can be observed in certain geological formations. 在某些地质构造中可以观察到水流发电效应。
近义词: hydroelectric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly thermoelectric)
词性: adjective
