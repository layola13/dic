deprecate:/ˈdeprɪkeɪt/ | v. | 反对；贬低；轻视 | To express disapproval of. |  The teacher deprecated the student's disruptive behavior. 老师批评了那个学生扰乱课堂秩序的行为。 | criticize, condemn, denounce |  praise, commend | verb
