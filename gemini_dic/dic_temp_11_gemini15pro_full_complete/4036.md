aic:/eɪ aɪ ˈsiː/ |abbr.|AIC|Akaike information criterion, 赤池信息量准则|AIC is a metric used to evaluate the relative quality of statistical models for a given set of data.  AIC is used to select the best model among a finite set of candidate models. |The AIC of this model is lower than the previous one, suggesting it's a better fit.  这个模型的AIC比前一个低，表明它更拟合。 |近义词:BIC (Bayesian information criterion)|反义词: N/A | abbreviation
