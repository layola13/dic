eckerd: /ˈɛkərd/ | proper n. | 埃克德 | Eckerd is primarily known as the name of a former American drugstore chain, Eckerd Drugs. It was founded by Jack Eckerd in 1952 and was once one of the largest retail pharmacy chains in the United States.  The name can also refer to Eckerd College, a private liberal arts college in St. Petersburg, Florida, named after Jack Eckerd. | Jack Eckerd founded the Eckerd drugstore chain. 杰克·埃克德创立了埃克德连锁药店。

Eckerd College is known for its marine science program. 埃克德学院以其海洋科学课程而闻名。 | synonyms: (For Eckerd Drugs) pharmacy, drugstore, chemist's (British) | antonyms: N/A (Proper nouns typically don't have antonyms) | noun
