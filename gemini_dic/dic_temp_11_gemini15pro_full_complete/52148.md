diaphanometer:/ˌdaɪəfəˈnɒmɪtər/| n. |透光计，透明度计|An instrument for measuring the transparency of the atmosphere, especially as regards mist or fog.| The diaphanometer showed a significant decrease in atmospheric transparency due to the heavy fog. 透光计显示，由于浓雾，大气透明度显著下降。|Synonym: transparency meter|Antonym: opacity meter|noun


diaphaneity:/ˌdaɪəfəˈniːɪti/| n. |透明度，透光性|The quality of being diaphanous; transparency.| The diaphaneity of the fabric allowed the sunlight to filter through.  这种织物的透明度让阳光可以穿透。|Synonym: transparency, translucence|Antonym: opacity|noun


diaphanous:/daɪˈæfənəs/| adj. |透明的，半透明的|Light, delicate, and translucent.| She wore a diaphanous gown that flowed gracefully in the breeze. 她穿着一件透明的礼服，在微风中飘逸。|Synonym: sheer, translucent, transparent|Antonym: opaque, thick|adjective
