arbitraged:/ˈɑːrbɪtɑːʒd/ | v. | 套利；进行套利交易 |  The act of buying an asset in one market and simultaneously selling it in another market at a higher price, thereby profiting from the price difference. |  He arbitraged the stock by buying it on the New York Stock Exchange and selling it on the London Stock Exchange. 他通过在纽约证券交易所买入股票，然后在伦敦证券交易所卖出，从而进行了套利。 | Synonyms: profited from price difference, exploited market inefficiency | Antonyms: lost money on price difference | verb
