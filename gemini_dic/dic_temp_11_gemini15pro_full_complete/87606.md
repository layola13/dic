heterotrophic:/ˌhɛtərəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 异养的 | An organism that cannot produce its own food, instead taking nutrition from other sources of organic carbon, mainly plant or animal matter. | Example: Most animals and fungi are heterotrophic organisms. 大多数动物和真菌是异养生物。

Example:  Heterotrophic bacteria play a vital role in decomposition. 异养细菌在分解过程中起着至关重要的作用。 | Synonym:  heterotrophic, organotrophic | Antonym: autotrophic | adjective
