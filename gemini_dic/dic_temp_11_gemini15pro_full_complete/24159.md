brick:/brɪk/| n. adj.|砖; 砖块; 积木; (像砖的) 长方体;|A small rectangular block typically made of fired or sun-dried clay, used in building.|例句：The house is built of brick. 这房子是用砖建造的。

例句：He threw a brick through the window. 他向窗户扔了一块砖。|近义词：block, tile|反义词:(无直接反义词)|n.

brick:/brɪk/| adj.|砖似的; 砖红色的;|Made of brick or resembling brick in color or form.|例句：The building has a brick facade. 这座建筑有砖砌的外墙。

例句：She wore a brick-red dress. 她穿着一件砖红色的连衣裙。|近义词：brick-colored, reddish-brown|反义词:(无直接反义词)|adj.
