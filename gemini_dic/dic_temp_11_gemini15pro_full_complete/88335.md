hippocratism:/hɪˌpɒkrətɪzəm/ | n. | 俱樂部狀指 | Clubbing of the fingers and toes, a symptom of certain diseases, especially of the heart and lungs.  It is characterized by a bulbous enlargement of the terminal phalanges of the fingers and toes. | The doctor noticed hippocratism in the patient's fingers, which suggested a possible lung problem. 医生注意到病人的手指有杵状指，这提示可能存在肺部问题。 | The patient exhibited marked hippocratism, a classic sign of long-standing cyanotic heart disease. 病人表现出明显的杵状指，这是长期紫绀性心脏病的典型症状。 | Synonyms: clubbing, digital clubbing | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but the absence of clubbing would be the opposite condition.) | noun
