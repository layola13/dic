chassepot:/ʃæs.poʊ/ (US), /ˈʃæs.pɒt/ (UK)| n. |夏塞波步枪|A type of breech-loading rifle adopted by the French army in 1866. It was a significant improvement over previous muzzle-loading rifles.|The Chassepot rifle was a formidable weapon during the Franco-Prussian War. 夏塞波步枪在普法战争中是一件令人生畏的武器。|Synonym: rifle|Antonym: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific type of rifle)|noun
