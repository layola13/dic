nonreproduction:/ˌnɑːnˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən/
n.|非繁殖，不复制|The nonreproduction of the endangered species is a major concern.  濒危物种的无法繁殖是一个主要问题。|The museum prohibits the nonreproduction of its artifacts.  博物馆禁止复制其文物。|近义词: nonproliferation, sterility, barrenness|反义词: reproduction, propagation, fertility|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not"
reproduction (noun): from Late Latin reproductionem, from reproducere "to produce again," from re- "again" + producere "to bring forth"
