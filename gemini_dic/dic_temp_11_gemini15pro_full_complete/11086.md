archaism: /ˈɑːrkeɪɪzəm/ | n. | 古语；古体 |  A word or phrase that is no longer in common use but is sometimes used to give a sense of old-fashioned language. |  The author used archaisms like "thee" and "thou" to create a sense of antiquity.  作者使用 "thee" 和 "thou" 等古语来营造一种古代的感觉。 | synonyms: antiquated word, obsolete word, old-fashioned word | antonyms: neologism, modernism | noun
