countersign:/ˈkaʊntərˌsaɪn/ | n. vt.| 副署，连署；口令 |A signature added to another signature to attest to its authenticity.  To sign jointly with another or others.  A secret signal, password, or watchword. |例句：The check requires a countersign. 这张支票需要副署。| 近义词：endorse, co-sign, corroborate, confirm, validate, ratify | 反义词：reject, refuse, repudiate | n.

countersign:/ˈkaʊntərˌsaɪn/ | vt.| 副署，连署；用口令答复 |To sign jointly with another or others.  To authenticate by adding one's signature after another's; attest. | 例句：He countersigned the treaty. 他在条约上副署。 | 近义词：endorse, co-sign, corroborate, confirm, validate, ratify | 反义词：reject, refuse, repudiate | v.
