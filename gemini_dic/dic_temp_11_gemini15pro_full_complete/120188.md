mindset:/ˈmaɪndset/| n.|心态，思维模式|A mindset refers to a set of beliefs, assumptions, and attitudes that someone holds about something.  It influences how a person perceives, interprets, and responds to the world around them.|A growth mindset is essential for learning.  成长型思维对于学习至关重要。|synonyms: attitude, outlook, perspective, frame of mind|antonyms: none (mindset describes a neutral state, not inherently positive or negative)|noun
