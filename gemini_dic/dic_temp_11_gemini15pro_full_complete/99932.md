interning:/ˈɪntɜːrnɪŋ/ | adj. | 实习的 | Relating to or characteristic of an intern. | Example: He's currently in his interning phase of medical school. 他目前正处于医学院的实习阶段。| Synonyms: probationary, trainee | Antonyms: professional, experienced | adjective
