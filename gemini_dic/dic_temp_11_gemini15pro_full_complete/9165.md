antinepotism:/ˈæntiˈnɛpətɪzəm/| n.|反裙带关系；反对任人唯亲|The policy of antinepotism prevents the hiring of relatives.|公司反对任人唯亲的政策禁止雇佣亲属。|Example: The company has a strict policy of antinepotism to ensure fair hiring practices. 这家公司有严格的反裙带关系政策，以确保公平的招聘流程。

Example:  Antinepotism rules prevent managers from hiring their own family members.  反裙带关系的规定阻止了经理们雇佣自己的家庭成员。|近义词：impartiality, fairness, meritocracy|反义词：nepotism, cronyism|词性: noun
