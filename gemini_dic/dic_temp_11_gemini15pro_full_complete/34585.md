clearnesses:/ˈklɪərnɪsɪz/
n.
清晰度，明晰，清楚
The clearness of the water allowed us to see the bottom.  水的清澈度让我们能够看到底部。
The clearness of her explanation made the complex topic easy to understand. 她清晰的解释使得这个复杂的话题容易理解。
clarity, lucidity, transparency, distinctness
opacity, vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity
noun
