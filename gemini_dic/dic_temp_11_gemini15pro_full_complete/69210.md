fearer:/ˈfɪərər/| n.|害怕的人；恐惧者|Someone who fears something or someone.|例句：He is a fearer of heights. 他有恐高症。|近义词：coward, timid person|反义词:brave person, daredevil|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *ferer, from Old English *fǣrere, from fǣran (“to frighten, terrify, scare”) + -ere (“-er”).  Cognate with Dutch verer (“one who puts to flight, hunts”), German Ferer (“one who makes to flee”).
