bulimia:/buˈlɪmiə/ /bʊˈliːmiə/| n. |贪食症；暴食症|An emotional disorder involving distortion of body image and an obsessive desire to lose weight, in which bouts of extreme overeating are followed by depression and self-induced vomiting, purging, or fasting. |例句：Bulimia can have severe health consequences. 贪食症会对健康造成严重后果。

例句：She struggled with bulimia for years. 她与贪食症斗争了多年。|近义词：binge eating disorder, hyperorexia|反义词: anorexia (although not a direct opposite, it represents an opposite extreme in eating disorders)|词性:noun
