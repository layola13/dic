lament:/ləˈment/ | n. v. | 悲叹，哀悼；挽歌 | An expression of grief or sorrow, especially a song or poem. |  She lamented the death of her friend. 她哀悼她朋友的去世。

lament:/ləˈment/ | n. v. | 悲叹，哀悼；挽歌 |  To express grief or sorrow. |  He lamented his lost youth. 他哀叹自己逝去的青春。

近义词:mourn, grieve, bewail | 反义词:rejoice, celebrate | 词性:noun, verb

词源: From Old French *lamenter, from Latin lāmentārī (“to bewail, lament”), from lāmentum (“wailing, moaning”), from PIE *lā-ment- (“cry, wail”).
