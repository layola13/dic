misdeliveries:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvəriz/
n.
错误投递；误送
The act of delivering something to the wrong person or address.
Several customers complained about misdeliveries of their packages.  几个顾客抱怨他们的包裹被投递错误。
misdelivery,  wrong delivery, incorrect delivery
correct delivery, successful delivery
noun
