fossorial:/fɒˈsɔːriəl/ /fɑːˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
穴居的；掘地的
Adapted to digging and living underground.
The mole is a fossorial animal, spending most of its life beneath the surface. 鼹鼠是一种穴居动物，一生中的大部分时间都在地下度过。

The fossorial forelimbs of the badger are powerful digging tools. 獾的穴居前肢是强有力的挖掘工具。
近义词:burrowing, subterranean, subterraqueous
反义词:surface, aboveground
词性:adjective
