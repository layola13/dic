hightail:/ˈhaɪteɪl/| v. | 迅速离开，逃之夭夭 | To leave rapidly; to run away quickly. |  The robbers hightailed it out of the bank when the alarm went off. 银行警报响起时，劫匪们迅速逃离了银行。

The cat hightailed it up a tree when it saw the dog.  这只猫看到狗后迅速爬上了树。

| 近义词：flee, escape, run away, bolt, scram | 反义词：approach, arrive, stay |词性:动词
