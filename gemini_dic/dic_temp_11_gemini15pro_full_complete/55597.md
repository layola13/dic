disunities:/dɪsˈjuːnɪtiz/
|n.|不团结，分裂，不一致|lack of unity or agreement; discord; division.|The disunities within the party led to its downfall. 党内的不团结导致了它的垮台。|近义词:discord, division, disharmony, friction, schism, split|反义词:unity, harmony, agreement, concord, solidarity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From disunity + -ies (plural suffix).  Disunity itself comes from dis- (prefix indicating negation or reversal) + unity.  Ultimately from Latin *ūnus* (one).
