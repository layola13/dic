overflooding:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪŋ/ | n. | 泛滥，过多 | The act of flooding excessively; an overwhelming amount or influx. | The overflooding of the river caused significant damage to the surrounding areas. 河流泛滥对周围地区造成了重大破坏。| The overflooding of information on the internet can be overwhelming.  互联网上信息的泛滥可能会让人不知所措。| 近义词: inundation, deluge, overflow | 反义词: drought, scarcity | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From over- + flooding, the present participle of flood.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, indicating an excessive degree of flooding.  "Flood" itself comes from Old English "flōd," meaning "a flowing, stream, flood, mass of water."
