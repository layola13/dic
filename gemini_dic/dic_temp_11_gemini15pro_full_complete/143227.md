outcutting:/ˈaʊtkʌtɪŋ/
verb
外切，切出
The process of cutting something out, especially from a larger piece of material.
The surgeon performed an outcutting procedure to remove the tumor.  外科医生进行了一次外切手术以切除肿瘤。
excise, resect
incise, insert
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out + cutting (present participle of cut).
