negritude:/ˈnɛɡrɪtjuːd/ | n. | 黑人性; 黑人文化 | The quality or fact of being of black African origin; the affirmation or consciousness of the value of black or African culture, heritage, and identity. |  The concept of negritude emerged in Paris in the 1930s.  黑人性概念于 20 世纪 30 年代在巴黎出现。| Synonym: blackness, Afrocentrism | Antonym: whiteness, Eurocentrism | noun

negritude:/ˈnɛɡrɪtjuːd/ | n. | 黑人尊严;种族自豪 | Pride in being black; a belief in the cultural identity of black people. | The poetry of the Negritude movement celebrated black beauty and heritage. 黑人性运动的诗歌歌颂了黑人的美丽和传统。| Synonym: black pride, self-determination | Antonym: self-hatred, racial inferiority | noun
