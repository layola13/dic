armozeen:/ɑːrˈmoʊziːn/
n.
armozeen (fabric)
一种厚实的丝绸织物，通常有条纹或图案，用于服装和室内装饰。
A heavy plain-woven silk fabric, typically striped or patterned, used for clothing and upholstery.
The curtains were made of a rich armozeen. 窗帘是用一种华丽的厚丝绸制成的。

近义词:silk, satin, damask, brocade
反义词:cotton, linen, wool
