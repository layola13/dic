neuvaine:/nʊˈveɪn/ | n. | 九日敬礼；九日祈祷 | A series of prayers or services performed for nine consecutive days, especially in the Roman Catholic Church. |  She attended the neuvaine for her sick grandmother. 她参加了她祖母的九日祈祷。 | Synonym: devotion, prayer | Antonym: blasphemy, irreverence | noun
