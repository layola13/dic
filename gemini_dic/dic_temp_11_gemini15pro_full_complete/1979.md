activity: /ækˈtɪvɪti/ (US) /ækˈtɪvəti/ (UK)| n.| 活动；活跃；行动|A thing that a person or group does or has done.|例句：The park offers a wide range of activities for children.  这个公园为孩子们提供各种各样的活动。

例句：His suspicious activities caught the attention of the police.  他可疑的活动引起了警方的注意。|近义词：action, movement, operation, work, pursuit|反义词：inactivity, idleness, stillness, passivity|noun


activity: /ækˈtɪvɪti/ (US) /ækˈtɪvəti/ (UK)| n.| 活跃；活力|The condition in which things are happening or being done.|例句：There’s been a lot of activity in the housing market recently. 最近房地产市场非常活跃。
