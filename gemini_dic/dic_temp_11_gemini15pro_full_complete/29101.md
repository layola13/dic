casanova:/ˌkæsəˈnoʊvə/ /ˌkæzəˈnəʊvə/ | n. | 花花公子；风流浪子 | A man who is known for seducing many women.  A charismatic and charming man, often with a reputation for being a womanizer. | 例句：He's a real casanova, always surrounded by beautiful women. 他真是个花花公子，身边总是围绕着美女。

例句：He had a reputation as a bit of a casanova. 他有点花花公子的名声。

近义词：womanizer, philanderer, Don Juan, Romeo, lothario | 反义词：faithful husband, loyal partner | 词性：noun
