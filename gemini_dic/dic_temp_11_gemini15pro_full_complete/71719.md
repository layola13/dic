flavoury:/ˈfleɪvəri/ (US) /ˈfleɪvəri/ (UK)| adj.|美味的，可口的，有香味的|Having a pleasant and distinct taste or smell.|The stew had a rich, savoury aroma. 这道炖菜有着浓郁的香味。|近义词:tasty, delicious, appetizing, savory (US)|反义词:bland, tasteless, insipid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *flavouri*, from Anglo-Norman *flavourus*, from Old French *savouros*, from *saveur* (“savor, flavor”), from Latin *sapor* (“taste, flavor”).  The spelling with *l* is influenced by *flavour*.
