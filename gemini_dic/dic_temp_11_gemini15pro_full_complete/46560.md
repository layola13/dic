dallying:/ˈdæliɪŋ/| n. vi.|闲荡，玩耍，拖延|The act of wasting time or delaying something intentionally.  It can also refer to a lighthearted or playful interaction, especially in a romantic context.|He lost his job due to his constant dallying. 他因为经常拖延而丢了工作。|Synonyms: lingering, loitering, tarrying, trifling, flirting|Antonyms: hastening, hurrying, expediting|verb (present participle)
