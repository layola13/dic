corbelling:/ˈkɔːrbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 叠涩 | The masonry technique of offsetting successive courses of stone or brick to create a projecting structure. | The arch was supported by intricate corbelling.  拱门由复杂的叠涩支撑。| Synonym:  oversailing, cantilevering | Antonym:  flushwork | noun


corbel:/ˈkɔːrbəl/ | n. | 叠涩; 突出物; 托架 | A projection jutting out from a wall to support a weight above, often elaborately carved or molded.  |  The gargoyle perched on a decorative corbel.  滴水嘴兽栖息在一个装饰性的托架上。| Synonym: bracket, support, console | Antonym: recess | noun
