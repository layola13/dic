intensively:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪvli/| adv.|密集地，集中地，深入地|With great concentration and effort over a short period of time.|The company is investing intensively in research and development. 该公司正在大力投资研发。|closely, rigorously, thoroughly|superficially, lightly|adverb
Etymology->
From intense + -ly.  "Intense" comes from the Late Latin "intensus" (past participle of "intendere" meaning "to stretch out, strain"), from "in-" (in, into) + "tendere" (to stretch).
