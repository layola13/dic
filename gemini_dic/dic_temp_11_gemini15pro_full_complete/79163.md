glamorised: /ˈɡlæməraɪzd/ | adj. | 被美化的，被赋予魅力的 | Made to seem more attractive or exciting than it really is. |  The movie glamorised the life of a rock star.  这部电影美化了摇滚明星的生活。| 近义词： romanticized, idealized, glorified | 反义词： deglamorised, realistic | 词性: 形容词

glamorised: /ˈɡlæməraɪzd/ | v. | 美化，赋予魅力 | To make something seem more attractive or exciting than it really is. | The advertisement glamorised the product to appeal to a younger audience.  这则广告美化了产品以吸引年轻的观众。| 近义词： romanticize, idealize, glorify | 反义词： deglamorize, portray realistically | 词性: 动词
