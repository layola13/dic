debruised:/diːˈbruːzd/
adjective
无瘀伤的；（精神上）无打击的
free from bruises; (figuratively) free from damage or hurt
The boxer, though victorious, was not debruised.  这位拳击手虽然获胜了，但并非毫发无损。
The debruised apple was perfect for the pie.  这个没有瘀伤的苹果非常适合做馅饼。
近义词:unblemished, undamaged, uninjured, unscathed
反义词:bruised, damaged, injured
