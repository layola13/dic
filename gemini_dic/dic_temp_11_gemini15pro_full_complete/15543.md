baggier:/ˈbæɡɪər/| adj.|更宽松的，更袋状的|Comparative form of baggy: more baggy.  Meaning even more loose and hanging than something already baggy.|Her jeans were much baggier than mine.|近义词：looser, slouchier, roomier|反义词:tighter, snugger, fitted|词性:adjective
baggier:/ˈbæɡɪər/| adj.|（尤指眼睛下）肿胀的，浮肿的|Informal use, referring to eyes that are puffy and swollen, often due to lack of sleep or illness.|He looked tired and his eyes were baggier than usual.|近义词：puffier, swollen|反义词:taut, firm|词性:adjective
