orthopedic:/ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/| adj. n.|骨科的，矫形的；矫形外科医生|Relating to the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles.|例句：She went to see an orthopedic surgeon about her knee pain. 她去看骨科医生治疗膝盖疼痛。|例句：He needs orthopedic shoes to support his arches. 他需要矫形鞋来支撑他的足弓。|近义词：musculoskeletal, orthopaedic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
