elitism:/ɪˈliːtɪzəm/ |n.|精英主义；杰出人物统治论|The belief that society should be led by an elite.  A system that gives power and advantage to a small group of people, considered to be superior.|  Many accuse private schools of fostering elitism. 很多人指责私立学校助长精英主义。|  The elitism of the ruling class is evident in their policies. 统治阶级的精英主义在他们的政策中显而易见。|  snobbishness, exclusiveness, snobbery| egalitarianism, populism |noun
