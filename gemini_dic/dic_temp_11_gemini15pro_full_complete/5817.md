amassment:/əˈmæsmənt/| n.|积累，积聚|The archaeologist was amazed by the sheer amassment of artifacts in the tomb. 考古学家对墓中大量的文物感到惊奇。|例句：The amassment of wealth should not be the sole goal in life.  积累财富不应该是人生唯一的目标。

例句：His amassment of knowledge over the years was truly impressive. 多年来他积累的知识确实令人印象深刻。|近义词：accumulation, collection, gathering, hoard|反义词:dispersal, distribution|词性:noun
