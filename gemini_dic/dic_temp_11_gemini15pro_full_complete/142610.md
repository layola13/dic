osteopathist:/ˌɒstiˈɒpəθɪst/ | n. | 整骨师，整骨医生 | A practitioner of osteopathy, a system of complementary medicine involving the treatment of medical disorders through the manipulation and massage of the skeleton and musculature. |  I went to see an osteopathist for my back pain. 我去看了一位整骨医生治疗我的背痛。|  Synonym: bonesetter, osteopath | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider a conventional medical doctor as a contrasting profession) | noun
