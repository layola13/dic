minaret:/ˌmɪnəˈrɛt/ | n. | 尖塔，光塔 | A tall slender tower, typically part of a mosque, with a balcony from which a muezzin calls Muslims to prayer. |  The mosque's minaret dominated the skyline. 清真寺的尖塔耸立于天际线上。

The call to prayer echoed from the minaret.  祈祷的召唤声从尖塔回荡。 | 近义词:spire, tower | 反义词:base, foundation | 词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *minaret*, from Turkish *minare*, from Arabic *manārat*  "lighthouse, place of fire," from *nāra* "fire."  Originally referred to a lighthouse or beacon, later applied to mosque towers.
