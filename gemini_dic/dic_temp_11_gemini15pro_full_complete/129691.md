non-disjunctional:/ˌnɒn dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃənəl/ |adj.| 非分离的，非互斥的 |Relating to or characterized by nondisjunction, the failure of homologous chromosomes or sister chromatids to separate properly during cell division. |Non-disjunctional errors during meiosis can lead to aneuploidy. 减数分裂过程中非分离错误会导致非整倍性。|Synonym: aneuploid, aberrant |Antonym: euploid, normal |adjective

non-disjunction:/ˌnɒn dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ |n.| 非分离 |The failure of homologous chromosomes or sister chromatids to separate properly during cell division. |Nondisjunction is a common cause of Down syndrome. 非分离是唐氏综合征的常见原因。|Synonym: nondisjunction event |Antonym: disjunction |noun
