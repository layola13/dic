codirector:/koʊˌdaɪˈrɛktər/ /kəʊˌdaɪˈrɛktə(r)/| n.|联合导演，共同执导者|A person who directs something jointly with another or others.|例句：The codirectors of the film shared the award.  这部电影的联合导演分享了这个奖项。

例句：She and her husband are codirectors of the company. 她和她的丈夫是公司的联合董事。|近义词：joint director, co-leader|反义词:sole director|词性:名词
