antimodernists:/ˌæntiˈmɑːdɜːrnɪsts/ | n. | 反现代主义者 | People who oppose or resist modernism, especially in the arts, culture, or society.  They often advocate for traditional values and ways of life. | The antimodernists criticized the new architecture as cold and impersonal. 反现代主义者批评新建筑冷漠而没有人情味。|  The rise of social media has given a platform to some antimodernists. 社交媒体的兴起为一些反现代主义者提供了平台。 |  traditionalists, conservatives | modernists, progressives | noun
