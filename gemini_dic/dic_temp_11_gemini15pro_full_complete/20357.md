bison:/ˈbaɪsən/ (US), /ˈbaɪzn/ (UK)| n. |野牛；美洲野牛|A large, shaggy-haired, hoofed mammal of North America and Europe.  It is closely related to the cow. |The bison roamed the plains in vast herds. 野牛成群结队地在平原上漫游。

The European bison is smaller than its American cousin. 欧洲野牛比它的美洲表亲小。|近义词：buffalo, wisent|反义词: N/A (Animals don't typically have direct antonyms)|词性:noun
