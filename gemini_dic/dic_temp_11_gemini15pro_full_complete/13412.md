atomizer:/ˈætəˌmaɪzər/ (US), /ˈætəmaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n. |喷雾器，雾化器|A device that converts a liquid into a fine spray or mist.|例句：She used an atomizer to spray perfume. 她用喷雾器喷香水。

例句：The paint was applied using an atomizer for an even coat.  油漆是用喷雾器喷涂的，以获得均匀的涂层。


近义词：sprayer, nebulizer, vaporizer|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
