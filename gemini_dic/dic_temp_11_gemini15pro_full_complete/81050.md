grafts:/ɡræfts/ | n. | 嫁接，移植，贪污，贿赂 |A piece of living tissue that is transplanted surgically.|The surgeon performed several skin grafts on the burn victim. 外科医生对烧伤病人进行了多次皮肤移植。| synonyms: transplant, implant, insertion | antonyms: removal, excision | noun

grafts:/ɡræfts/ | v. |嫁接，移植，贪污，贿赂 | To insert (a graft) surgically. To join or unite closely. |  He grafted the apple branch onto the pear tree. 他把苹果枝嫁接到梨树上。| synonyms: transplant, implant, insert | antonyms: remove, excise | verb
