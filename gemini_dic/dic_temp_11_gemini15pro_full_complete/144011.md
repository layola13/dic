outrebounds:/ˌaʊtˈriːbaʊndz/
n.
篮板球（尤指进攻篮板球）
the act of gaining possession of the basketball after a missed shot, especially on offense
The Lakers dominated the outrebounds, grabbing 15 offensive boards.  湖人队在篮板球方面占据了主导地位，抢下了15个进攻篮板。

近义词:rebounds, boards
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "giving up rebounds")
词性:noun
