devouringly:/dɪˈvaʊərɪŋli/| adv.|狼吞虎咽地，贪婪地|In a way that shows great eagerness or enthusiasm, especially for food or information.  It describes the manner in which something is consumed, whether literally (like food) or figuratively (like information).|He read the book devouringly, finishing it in a single day. 他狼吞虎咽地读着那本书，一天就看完了。|近义词：voraciously, ravenously, greedily|反义词：moderately, sparingly|词性:副词

devour:/dɪˈvaʊər/| vt.|吞食，毁灭|To eat something quickly and hungrily; to destroy something completely.|The flames devoured the building.  火焰吞噬了建筑。|近义词：consume, gobble, engulf|反义词：nibble, savor|词性:动词
