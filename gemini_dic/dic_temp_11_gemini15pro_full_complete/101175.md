invigorate:/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/| vt.|使精力充沛，使充满活力|To give strength and energy to.|A brisk walk can invigorate you.  快走能让你精神焕发。|近义词：energize, revitalize, stimulate, refresh|反义词: weaken, enervate, exhaust|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *invigoratus, past participle of invigorare (“to give vigor to”), from in- (“in, into”) + vigor (“vigor, strength”) + -are (verb-forming suffix).
