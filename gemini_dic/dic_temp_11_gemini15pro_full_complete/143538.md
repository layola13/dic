outguns:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌn/
verb
胜过，以火力压制
defeat or overcome by having better guns or other weapons.
The rebels outgunned the government troops. 叛军以火力压制了政府军。
outmatch, surpass, eclipse, outstrip
be outgunned by, be inferior to
