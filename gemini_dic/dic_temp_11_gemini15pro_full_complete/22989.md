boudoirs:/ˈbuːdwɑːz/ (US), /ˈbuːdwɑːrz/ (UK)| n. |闺房，化妆室|A woman's private sitting room or bedroom, often used for dressing and entertaining close friends.|例句：She retreated to her boudoir to prepare for the evening. 她回到自己的闺房准备晚上的活动。

例句：The boudoir was decorated with luxurious silks and velvets. 这间闺房用奢华的丝绸和天鹅绒装饰。


近义词：bedroom, dressing room, private room|反义词:public room|词性:noun
