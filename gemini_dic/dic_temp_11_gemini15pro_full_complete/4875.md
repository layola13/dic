aliform:/ˈælɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 翼形的，翅状的 | Having the shape of a wing; wing-shaped. | The aliform appendages of the insect helped it to glide. 昆虫的翼状附属物帮助它滑翔。| Near synonyms: winged, wing-like, pinnate | Antonyms: wingless, apterous | Adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aliformis*, from ala ("wing") + -formis ("-shaped").
