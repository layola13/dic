disillusioned:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənd/ | adj. | 失望的，幻想破灭的 |  Freed from illusion; disenchanted.  Feeling disappointed because something is not as good as one believed it to be. |  He became disillusioned with politics after seeing the corruption firsthand.  他亲眼目睹了腐败之后对政治感到失望。| disappointed, disenchanted, jaded | hopeful, optimistic | adjective

disillusion:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒən/ | vt. | 使幻想破灭，使清醒 | To free from illusion or belief; disenchant. | The harsh realities of war disillusioned many young soldiers. 战争的残酷现实使许多年轻士兵的幻想破灭。 | disappoint, disenchant | encourage, hearten | verb
