intertouched:/ˌɪntərˈtʌtʃt/
adjective
相互接触的，互相影响的
Having made physical contact with each other; mutually affected or influenced.
Their hands intertouched briefly as they passed each other.  他们擦肩而过时，手短暂地接触了一下。
The two cultures were so intertouched that it was difficult to tell where one began and the other ended. 这两种文化相互影响如此之深，以至于很难分辨彼此的界限。
近义词:intertwined, interconnected, intermingled
反义词:separate, detached, distinct
词性:adjective

词源:
From inter- + touched, the past participle of touch.  由前缀inter-(相互) + touch(接触)的过去分词touched构成。
