deactivator:/diːˈæktɪveɪtər/
n.|去活化剂；使无效的物质|A deactivator is a substance that renders something inactive. |The enzyme deactivator prevented further reaction.|近义词: inhibitor, neutralizer|反义词: activator, catalyst|词性:名词
deactivate:/diːˈæktɪveɪt/
v.|使无效；使不活动；停用|To deactivate something means to make it inactive or no longer working.|She deactivated her Facebook account.|近义词: disable, neutralize|反义词: activate, enable|词性:动词
deactivation:/diːˌæktɪˈveɪʃən/
n.|去活化；使无效；停用|Deactivation is the process of making something inactive.|The deactivation of the alarm system took several minutes.|近义词: inactivation, neutralization|反义词: activation, enablement|词性:名词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + activate + -or (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or instrument)  Ultimately from Latin *activus*, from actus "act".
