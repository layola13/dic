metamorphist:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪst/| n. |变质学家|A scientist who studies metamorphism, the process by which rocks are transformed by heat, pressure, or other natural agencies.|The metamorphist examined the rock sample under the microscope. 变质学家在显微镜下检查了岩石样本。|近义词：geologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
From metamorphism + -ist.  Metamorphism comes from Greek *metamorphōsis* "transformation," from *metamorphoun*  "to transform," from *meta-* "change" + *morphē* "form."
