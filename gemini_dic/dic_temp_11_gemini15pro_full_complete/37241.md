comfortingly:/ˈkʌmfərtɪŋli/
adverb
令人欣慰地，安慰地
in a way that makes someone feel less worried, upset, or frightened
Her hand rested comfortingly on his shoulder. 她把手安慰地放在他的肩膀上。

近义词:reassuringly, soothingly, supportively
反义词:distressingly, upsettingly, alarmingly
词性:adverb
