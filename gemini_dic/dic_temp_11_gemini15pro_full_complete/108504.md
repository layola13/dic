layman:/ˈleɪmən/| n.|外行，门外汉|a person who is not trained in or does not have a detailed knowledge of a particular subject|例句：The book is written for the layman. 这本书是为外行写的。

例句：It's difficult to explain this concept to a layman.  很难向外行解释这个概念。

近义词：nonprofessional, amateur, novice|反义词:expert, professional, specialist|词性:noun
