onecore:/ˈwʌnˌkɔːr/| n.|单核; 一核|A term used within Microsoft to refer to the shared core of Windows 10 across devices (PCs, tablets, phones, Xbox One, and HoloLens).|Example:  OneCore allows developers to create universal Windows apps that can run on a variety of devices.|Synonym: shared core, common core|Antonym: N/A|noun

onecore:/ˈwʌnˌkɔːr/| adj.|单核的; 一核的|Describing something related to or based on Microsoft's OneCore platform.|Example:  OneCore development is crucial for creating a unified Windows experience.|Synonym: unified, shared|Antonym: separate, fragmented|adjective
