chivalry: /ˈʃɪvəlrɪ/ (US), /ˈʃɪvəlri/ (UK)| n.|骑士精神，骑士制度|The ideals of chivalry include courage, honor, and courtesy. 骑士精神的理想包括勇气、荣誉和礼貌。|He behaved with the utmost chivalry towards her. 他对她表现出极度的骑士风度。|Synonyms: courtesy, gallantry, courtliness|Antonyms: rudeness, boorishness, discourtesy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French chevalerie "knighthood, cavalry," from chevalier "knight," from Late Latin caballarius "horseman," from Latin caballus "horse"
