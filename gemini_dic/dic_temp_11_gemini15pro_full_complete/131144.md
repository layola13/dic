nonblunderingly:/ˌnɒnˈblʌndərɪŋli/
adverb
没有错误地；无差错地
Without making blunders; flawlessly; accurately.
He navigated the complex situation nonblunderingly. 他巧妙地处理了这个复杂的情况。
The surgeon performed the operation nonblunderingly.  外科医生完美地完成了手术。
近义词: accurately, flawlessly, precisely, expertly, skillfully
反义词: blunderingly, clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + blunder (a stupid or careless mistake) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
