derisory:/dɪˈraɪsəri/
adj.
嘲笑的，嘲弄的，不足挂齿的
Expressing contempt or ridicule; ridiculously small or inadequate.
例句：He made a derisory offer for the house.  他出价极低要买这栋房子。

例句：She gave a derisory snort. 她轻蔑地哼了一声。

近义词：contemptuous, mocking, scornful, ridiculous, laughable, paltry, insignificant
反义词：respectful, admiring, substantial, significant
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dērīsōrius, from Latin dērīdeō (“I mock, ridicule, laugh at”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *dērīsōrius，来自拉丁语 dērīdeō（“我嘲笑，嘲弄，取笑”）。
