huffier:/ˈhʌfiər/ | adj. | 更生气的，更易怒的 |Comparative form of huffy: more huffy.  Meaning more easily offended; touchy. | He was huffier than usual this morning. 他今天早上比平时更容易生气。| Synonyms: touchier, more irritable, testier, more petulant | Antonyms: calmer, more patient, less irritable | adjective

huffier:/ˈhʌfiər/ | adj. | 更傲慢的，更目中无人的 |Comparative form of huffy: more huffy. Meaning more arrogant or self-important. |  She gave a huffier sniff and turned away. 她傲慢地哼了一声，转过身去。 | Synonyms: haughtier, more supercilious, more disdainful | Antonyms: humbler, more modest, less arrogant | adjective
