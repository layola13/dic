citigradae: /ˌsɪtɪˈɡreɪdiː/ | n. |  猫形总科 | A superfamily of mammals, traditionally including the families Felidae (cats), Hyaenidae (hyenas), Herpestidae (mongooses), and Viverridae (civets). Recent classifications often exclude the hyenas. |  Citigradae includes cats, mongooses, and civets. 猫形总科包括猫、獴和灵猫。 | 近义词: Feloidea (猫型总科 - a more modern and commonly used term) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Latin "citis" (swift) + "-gradae" (walking), referring to the typically agile movement of these animals.
