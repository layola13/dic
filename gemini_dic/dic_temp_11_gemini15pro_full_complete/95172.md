inattentive:/ˌɪnəˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 不专心的，疏忽的 | Not paying attention; distracted. | He was inattentive during the meeting and missed important information. 他在会议期间心不在焉，错过了重要信息。| The inattentive driver almost caused an accident.  心不在焉的司机差点酿成事故。| 近义词: distracted, heedless, unmindful, negligent | 反义词: attentive, alert, focused, observant | 词性: adjective

inattention:/ˌɪnəˈtɛnʃ(ə)n/ | n. |  不注意，疏忽 | Lack of attention. | Her inattention to detail led to several errors in the report. 她对细节的疏忽导致报告中出现几个错误。| The student's inattention in class was a constant source of frustration for the teacher.  这个学生在课堂上的心不在焉让老师一直很沮丧。 | 近义词: negligence, carelessness, heedlessness | 反义词: attention, attentiveness, mindfulness | 词性: noun


词源:  Late 16th century: from in- (expressing negation) + attentive.
