adc:/ˌeɪ diː ˈsiː/ | n. | 模拟数字转换器; ADC | Analog-to-Digital Converter, a system that converts an analog signal, such as a sound picked up by a microphone or light entering a digital camera, into a digital signal. |  The adc in this sound card is high quality.  这个声卡里的模数转换器质量很高。| synonym: A/D converter | antonym: DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converter) | noun

adc:/ˌeɪ diː ˈsiː/ |abbr. | (游戏)攻击伤害输出;  | Attack Damage Carry, in online gaming, a character who deals physical damage and is typically a team's main source of damage output. | The adc on our team carried us to victory.  我们队伍的ADC带领我们走向了胜利。| synonym: carry, DPS | antonym: support, tank | abbreviation
