appointer:/əˈpɔɪntər/| n.|任命者，指定者|A person who appoints someone to a position or office.  The appointer is responsible for selecting the right candidate. |The president is the appointer of Supreme Court justices.|近义词：nominator, selector, designator|反义词:dischargee, deposer|noun
