denominationalism:/dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |宗派主义，教派主义|The condition of being divided into denominations; adherence to a particular denomination.  The tendency to emphasize the distinctions between denominations.|
The rise of denominationalism in the 19th century fragmented the Christian church. 19世纪宗派主义的兴起使基督教会分裂。

Denominationalism can sometimes lead to conflict between different religious groups.  教派主义有时会导致不同宗教团体之间的冲突。
|近义词：sectarianism, factionalism|反义词：ecumenism, unity|noun
