galactophagist:/ɡəˌlæktəˈfeɪdʒɪst/
noun
食乳者；嗜乳成癖的人
A person who drinks milk, especially one who consumes a large quantity of milk.
Galactophagists rejoice!  A new dairy farm has opened nearby. 食乳者欢欣鼓舞！附近开了一家新的奶牛场。
synonyms: milk drinker, milk lover
antonyms: lactose intolerant (person)
n.
