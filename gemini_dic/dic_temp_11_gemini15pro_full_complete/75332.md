frosteds:/ˈfrɔːstɪdz/
noun
(通常复数)糖霜食品；(尤指)蛋糕上的糖霜
A sweet food, especially a cake, covered with frosting.
We ordered two frosteds and a coffee. 我们点了两份糖霜蛋糕和一杯咖啡。
Near Synonyms:  iced cakes, frosted cakes, frosted pastries
Antonyms: unfrosted cakes, plain cakes
词性: 名词


frosting:/ˈfrɔːstɪŋ/
noun
糖霜; (糕饼上的)奶油糖衣
A sweet creamy mixture used to cover or decorate cakes.
She piped the frosting onto the cupcakes. 她把糖霜挤到纸杯蛋糕上。
Near Synonyms: icing, glaze, topping
Antonyms: (none specific - perhaps "filling" if contrasting with a cake's interior)
词性: 名词


frost:/frɔːst/
noun
霜
Ice crystals that form on a surface when the temperature falls below freezing.
The grass was covered with a thick layer of frost. 草地上覆盖着一层厚厚的霜。
Near Synonyms: rime, hoarfrost
Antonyms: thaw, melt
词性: 名词
frost:/frɔːst/
verb
结霜; (使)蒙上霜
To become covered with frost; to cover something with frost.
The windows were frosted over. 窗户上结满了霜。
Near Synonyms: ice over, freeze over
Antonyms: defrost, de-ice
词性: 动词
