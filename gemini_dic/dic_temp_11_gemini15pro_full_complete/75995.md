funny-bones:/ˈfʌni bəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈfʌni boʊnz/ (US)| n.| 麻筋；肘部尺骨端；|The spot on the elbow where the ulnar nerve passes close to the surface, causing a tingling sensation when struck.  This isn't a bone, but part of the humerus where the ulnar nerve is exposed.|He bumped his funny-bone and yelped in pain. 他撞到了麻筋，疼得叫了起来。|近义词：crazy bone|反义词: None|词性: noun

funny-bone:/ˈfʌni bəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈfʌni boʊnz/ (US)| n.| 滑稽的骨头(比喻义，指幽默感)；|Figuratively, a sense of humor.  This usage is less common than the literal meaning.|He's got a funny-bone, always cracking jokes. 他很有幽默感，总是讲笑话。|近义词：sense of humor|反义词: None|词性: noun
