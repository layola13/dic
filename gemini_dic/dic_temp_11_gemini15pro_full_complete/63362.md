ETYMOLOGY->
From French *entomologique*, from *entomologie* (“entomology”) + *-ique* (“-ic”, adjectival suffix), from Ancient Greek *ἔντομον* (éntomon, “insect”) + *-λογία* (-logía, “-logy”), from *λέγειν* (légein, “to speak”).
