nonstriped:/ˌnɒnˈstraɪpt/ | adj. | 无条纹的 | Not having stripes or bands of different colors. | The nonstriped shirt looked plain compared to the others.  这件没有条纹的衬衫与其他的相比看起来很朴素。| The cat was nonstriped and completely black. 这只猫没有条纹，全身都是黑色的。 | Synonyms: plain, solid, unstriped | Antonyms: striped, banded | adjective
