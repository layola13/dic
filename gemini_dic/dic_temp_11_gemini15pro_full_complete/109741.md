levering:/ˈliːvərɪŋ/ /ˈlevərɪŋ/
n.
杠杆作用; 杠杆操作; (用杠杆)撬动; 举; 抬; 撬
The action of using a lever to lift or move something.  Also, the action of using something to maximum advantage.
The levering of the heavy rock revealed a hidden chamber.  撬动沉重的岩石，露出了一个隐藏的房间。
They used levering to gain access to the treasure chest. 他们用杠杆来打开宝箱。
leverage, lifting, raising, prying
lowering, dropping
noun
