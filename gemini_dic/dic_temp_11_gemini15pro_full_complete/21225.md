bloggable:/ˈblɒɡəbəl/ (US) /ˈblɒɡəbl/ (UK)| adj.| 可写成博客的，适合写博客的 | Suitable for a blog post; interesting or noteworthy enough to be written about in a blog. | Example: Her trip to Iceland was very bloggable. 她的冰岛之旅非常适合写成博客。| Example: The conference provided lots of bloggable content.  这次会议提供了很多可以写进博客的内容。| 近义词:sharable, postable, tweetable | 反义词:unremarkable, uninteresting | 词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From blog + -able.  "Blog" is a shortened form of "weblog," a combination of "web" and "log."
