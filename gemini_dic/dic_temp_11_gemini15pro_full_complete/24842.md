brownfield:/ˈbraʊnˌfiːld/ | adj. | 棕地的 | Relating to or designating abandoned or underused industrial or commercial sites, often contaminated and requiring cleanup before redevelopment. | The brownfield site posed a significant environmental challenge. 这个棕地带来了重大的环境挑战。| Synonyms: derelict, contaminated, abandoned | Antonyms: greenfield, undeveloped | adjective
