matriculating:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
入学；注册
The act of enrolling or registering in a college or university.
She is matriculating at Harvard University this fall. 她今年秋季将在哈佛大学入学。
John matriculated at the university last week.  约翰上周在大学注册了。
近义词: enrolling, registering, joining
反义词: withdrawing, leaving, graduating
词性: verb (动词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "matriculatus," past participle of "matriculare" ("to enroll," "to register"), from "matricula" ("register," "roll").
