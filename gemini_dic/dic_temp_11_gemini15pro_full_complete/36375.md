coinhabitant:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhæbɪtənt/| n.|同居者，共同居住者|A person or animal that lives in the same place as another.|例句：The two species are coinhabitants of the forest. 这两个物种共同生活在这片森林里。

例句：She became my coinhabitant after we signed the lease. 我们签了租约后，她成了我的同居者。|近义词：roommate, housemate, cohabitator, resident, dweller|反义词:None|词性:noun
