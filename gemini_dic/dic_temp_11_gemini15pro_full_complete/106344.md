koikoi:/ˈkɔɪkɔɪ/| interj. n.|再来一次，纸牌游戏名|A Japanese word, primarily used as an interjection in the Hanafuda card game to indicate a desire to continue playing the hand for more points. It is also the name of the game itself.|例句：Player 1: Koikoi! 玩家1：再来一次！|近义词: (as an interjection) none; (as a game) Hanafuda|反义词: (as an interjection)  pass|词性: interjection, noun

koikoi:/ˈkɔɪkɔɪ/| n.|恋恋(こいこい) (Japanese)|The Japanese word for "come come," often used romanized. It can also refer to the feeling of longing or yearning for someone.|例句：I felt a sense of koikoi for my hometown. 我对我的家乡感到一种恋恋不舍的感觉。|近义词: longing, yearning|反义词: apathy|词性: noun
