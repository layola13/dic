decylic: /dɪˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. | 十碳的，癸的 | Relating to or containing ten carbons.  Specifically refers to decanoic acid (also called capric acid), a saturated fatty acid with a ten-carbon chain. | Decylic acid is found in coconut oil and palm kernel oil.  癸酸存在于椰子油和棕榈仁油中。| 近义词: capric, decanoic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, would need to specify a different carbon chain length, e.g., nonylic, undecylic) | 词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *deka* "ten" + *kyl-* (a variant of *hyl-* "matter, wood") + *-ic*.
