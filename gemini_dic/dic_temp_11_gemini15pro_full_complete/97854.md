in league:/ɪn liːɡ/
adj. |勾结的，串通的| secretly working with someone else, usually to do something dishonest |The two companies were in league to fix prices.  这两家公司串通操纵价格。|
例句：
They were in league with the enemy. 他们与敌人勾结。
It is suspected that the police officer was in league with the criminals. 怀疑这个警察和罪犯串通一气。
近义词：colluding, conspiring, collaborating|反义词：opposed, against|词性：形容词


词源:
From Middle English *in lige*, from *in* (“in”) + *lige* (“allegiance, liege”).  Meaning shifted from "owing allegiance to the same lord" to "allied, united in a common cause."
