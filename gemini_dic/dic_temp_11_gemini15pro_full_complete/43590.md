creamed:/kriːmd/
v. |搅拌成奶油状；彻底打败；|To have beat thoroughly and decisively.  To have mixed something with cream until smooth.|The chef creamed the butter and sugar together.  厨师将黄油和糖搅拌成奶油状。| whipped, blended | separated, curdled |verb
creamed:/kriːmd/
adj. |奶油状的；加奶油的；|Containing or resembling cream.|She ordered a creamed spinach dish. 她点了一份奶油菠菜。| creamy, buttery | watery, thin |adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From cream (n.) + -ed.  The verb sense of "to beat thoroughly" arose in American English slang, likely from the image of whipping cream into a frothy, airy state, suggesting complete domination.
