disincentive: /ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛntɪv/ | n. | 阻碍；抑制；不利因素；起阻碍作用的事物 | Something that discourages or deters people from doing something. |  High taxes are a disincentive to investment. 高税收不利于投资。| synonyms: deterrent, discouragement, impediment, obstacle | antonyms: incentive, encouragement, motivation | noun

disincentive: /ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛntɪv/ | adj. | 阻碍的；抑制的 | Tending to discourage or deter. (Less common usage) | The disincentive effect of the new policy was immediately apparent. 新政策的抑制作用立即显现出来。 | synonyms: discouraging, deterring | antonyms: incentivizing, encouraging | adjective
