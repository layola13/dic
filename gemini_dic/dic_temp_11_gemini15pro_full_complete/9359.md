antiproton:/ˈæntiˌproʊtɒn/ | n. |反质子|A subatomic particle with the same mass as a proton but opposite charge (negative).  It is the antiparticle of the proton.|Physicists created antiprotons in a particle accelerator. 物理学家在粒子加速器中创造了反质子。|Synonym: negative proton |Antonym: proton |noun
