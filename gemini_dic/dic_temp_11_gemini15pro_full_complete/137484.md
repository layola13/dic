nontonic:/ˌnɒnˈtɒnɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|非主音的，无重音的|Relating to or denoting a syllable or tone that is not stressed or accented.  It's often used in linguistics and phonetics.|The nontonic syllables in the word "water" are the "a" and the "er". “water”这个词中非重读音节是“a”和“er”。|unstressed, weak|tonic, stressed|adjective
