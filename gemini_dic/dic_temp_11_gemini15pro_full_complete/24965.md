brushy:/ˈbrʌʃi/| adj.|灌木丛生的，茂密的；粗糙的，毛茸茸的|Covered with bushes or brush; resembling a brush; shaggy.|例句：The trail led through a brushy area. 这条小径穿过一片灌木丛生的地区。|近义词：bushy, scrubby, overgrown, shaggy, hairy|反义词：clear, bare, smooth|词性:形容词

brushy:/ˈbrʌʃi/| adj.|（尤指声音）沙哑的，粗糙的| (especially of a voice) rough and harsh|例句：His voice was brushy from years of smoking. 多年的吸烟使他的声音变得沙哑。|近义词：raspy, hoarse, gravelly|反义词：smooth, clear, melodious|词性:形容词
