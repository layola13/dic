distributable:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtəbl/
adjective
可分配的；可散发的；可分发的
Capable of being distributed or apportioned.
The profits are distributable among the shareholders. 利润可在股东之间分配。
distributable assets 可分配资产
synonyms: allocable, apportionable, divisible, sharable
antonyms: indivisible, non-distributable
词性: 形容词
