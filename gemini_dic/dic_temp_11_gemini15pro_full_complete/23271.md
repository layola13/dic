box-out:/ˈbɒks aʊt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːks aʊt/ (US)| n. v.|卡位，抢篮板球前的卡位动作|The act of positioning oneself between an opponent and the basket to gain better rebounding position.  It involves widening your stance, using your body to create space, and focusing on blocking out the opponent rather than solely going for the ball.|Example:  The coach emphasized the importance of boxing out for every shot.|近义词: block out, screen out|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but failing to box out could lead to being out-rebounded)|词性: noun, verb
