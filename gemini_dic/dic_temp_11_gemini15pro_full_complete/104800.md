kbps:/ˈkeɪbiːpiːˈɛs/| n. | 千比特每秒 | kilobits per second, a unit of data transfer rate equal to 1,000 bits per second. It is commonly used to measure the speed of internet connections or data transfer speeds in general. |  My internet connection speed is 50 kbps. 我的网速是50千比特每秒。| synonym:  kilobits/second | antonym: Mbps (megabits per second) | abbreviation
