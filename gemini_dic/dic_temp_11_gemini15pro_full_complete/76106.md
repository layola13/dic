furtherances:/ˈfɜːrðərənsɪz/
n.
推进，促进，增进
the act of furthering or helping something to progress
例句：The furtherances of peace and understanding between nations is a noble goal.  促进各国和平与理解是一个崇高的目标。

例句：He dedicated his life to the furtherance of medical knowledge. 他毕生致力于增进医学知识。

近义词：advancement, promotion, progress, development
反义词：hindrance, obstruction, impediment
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
