manuductor:/ˌmænjʊˈdʌktər/ | n. | 引导者，领导者 |  A person who leads or guides, especially by the hand.  This word is quite rare and often considered archaic.  It emphasizes the physical act of leading someone. |  The manuductor led the blind man across the busy street. 引导者领着盲人穿过繁忙的街道。 | Near synonyms: guide, leader, escort, conductor | Antonyms: follower | Noun
