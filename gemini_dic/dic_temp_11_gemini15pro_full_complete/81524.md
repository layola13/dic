gravitate:/ˈɡrævɪteɪt/| vi.|被吸引；受吸引移动|To move or be drawn toward something, especially by natural or physical forces, or to tend toward a particular activity, interest, or group of people.|Young people often gravitate towards the cities in search of work. 年轻人往往会为了寻找工作而涌向城市。|Dust particles gravitate towards the earth. 灰尘颗粒被吸引到地球上。|Synonyms:  be drawn to, be attracted to, incline towards, tend towards|Antonyms: repel, repulse|Verb
