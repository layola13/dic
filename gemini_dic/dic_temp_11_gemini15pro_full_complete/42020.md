costive:/ˈkɒstɪv/ (US) /ˈkɑːstɪv/| adj.|便秘的；(思想、语言)迟缓的，保守的|Relating to or causing constipation; slow or reluctant to act or speak, especially about one's feelings. |例句：The patient complained of costive bowels. 病人抱怨便秘。

例句：His costive personality made it difficult for him to express his emotions. 他保守的性格让他难以表达自己的情感。|近义词：constipated, slow, reticent, reserved|反义词：laxative, open, communicative|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *costif*, from Old French *costiv*, ultimately from Latin *constipatus*, past participle of *constipare* ("to press together, cram, condense, constipate"), from *con-* ("together") + *stipare* ("to press, pack, cram").
