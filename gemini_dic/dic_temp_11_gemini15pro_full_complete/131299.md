nonceremoniously:/ˌnɒnsɛrəˈməʊniəsli/
adverb
不拘礼节地，随便地
without ceremony; informally.
He was nonceremoniously bundled out of the room. 他被 unceremoniously 地赶出了房间。
The waiter nonceremoniously dumped the food on the table. 服务员 unceremoniously 地把食物扔在桌子上。
Synonyms: informally, casually, plainly, simply, abruptly, rudely, unceremoniously
Antonyms: formally, ceremoniously, respectfully, politely
词性: adverb
