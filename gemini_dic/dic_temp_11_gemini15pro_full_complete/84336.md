halfhearted:/ˌhæfˈhɑːr.tɪd/ | adj. | 不热心的，敷衍的，三心二意的 | Showing little enthusiasm or interest; lacking conviction. |  He made a halfhearted attempt to apologize. 他敷衍地試圖道歉。 |  The team played a halfhearted game and lost badly. 球队比赛时心不在焉，输得很惨。 | apathetic, indifferent, lukewarm, unenthusiastic | enthusiastic, wholehearted, passionate, committed | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "hearted."  "Half" meaning partly or incompletely, and "hearted" referring to the heart as the seat of emotions and commitment. Thus, "halfhearted" describes something done without full emotional engagement.
