haversack:/ˈhævərsæk/| n.|背包，干粮袋|A bag, typically made of canvas or other strong material, carried on the back or over the shoulder and used for carrying supplies, especially by soldiers or hikers. |例句：The soldier packed his haversack with rations for the three-day march. 士兵把他的背包里装满了三天的行军口粮。

例句：The hiker's haversack was overflowing with trail mix, water bottles, and a first-aid kit.  徒步旅行者的背包里装满了什锦干果、水瓶和一个急救包。|近义词：backpack, rucksack, knapsack|反义词: None|词性:noun
