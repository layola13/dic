miswiring:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪərɪŋ/
noun
错误布线
incorrect wiring; faulty connections in an electrical system or circuit.
The miswiring caused a short circuit and a power outage. 错误的布线导致了短路和停电。
近义词: faulty wiring, incorrect wiring, bad wiring
反义词: correct wiring, proper wiring
词性: noun

miswiring:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪərɪŋ/
noun
神经线路错误
incorrect connections in the nervous system, often referring to the brain.
Some scientists believe miswiring in the brain can contribute to certain mental disorders. 一些科学家认为大脑中的线路错误会导致某些精神疾病。
近义词: faulty connections, incorrect connections
反义词: correct connections, proper connections
词性: noun
