ageists:/ˈeɪdʒɪsts/
noun
年龄歧视者
People who discriminate against others based on their age.
Many companies have policies in place to prevent ageists from discriminating against older workers. 许多公司都有防止年龄歧视者歧视老年工人的政策。
近义词：discriminators, bigots
反义词: egalitarians
