defuels:/diːˈfjuːəlz/| v. |卸除燃料，去除燃料| To remove fuel from something, such as a vehicle or aircraft. |  The aircraft was defueled after the emergency landing.  飞机紧急着陆后被卸除了燃料。

The crew began defueling the rocket once the launch was scrubbed.  发射取消后，工作人员开始从火箭中卸除燃料。| synonyms: drain, empty | antonyms: fuel, refuel | verb
