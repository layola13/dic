malmsey:/ˈmɑːmzi/ | n. | 马姆齐酒（一种甜葡萄酒） | A sweet white wine originally from Greece, similar to Madeira. |  例句：The king enjoyed a glass of malmsey after dinner. 国王晚饭后享用了一杯马姆齐酒。

|例句：Malmsey was a popular drink in medieval Europe. 马姆齐酒在中世纪的欧洲是一种很受欢迎的饮品。 | 近义词：Madeira, sweet wine | 反义词: dry wine | noun


Etymology->

From Middle English *malvesie, from Old French malvoisie, from Medieval Latin malvasia, from the name of the port of Monemvasia (Μονεμβάσια) in Greece.
