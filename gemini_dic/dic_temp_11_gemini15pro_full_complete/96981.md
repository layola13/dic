infantilises:/ɪnˈfæn.tɪ.laɪ.zɪz/
verb
使幼稚化;把…当作小孩看待
treats (someone) as a child or in a way that denies their maturity.
He infantilises her by constantly reminding her of her age. 他总是提醒她的年龄，把她当孩子看待。
synonyms: patronize, condescend, demean, belittle
antonyms: empower, respect, validate
词性: 动词
