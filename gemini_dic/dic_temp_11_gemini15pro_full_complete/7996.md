anthomania:/ˌænθəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 对花的狂热；爱花癖 | An excessive enthusiasm for flowers. | She has a true anthomania, her house is overflowing with blooms. 她是一个真正的爱花之人，她的房子里摆满了鲜花。|  Example 2: His anthomania led him to cultivate a rare orchid collection. 他的爱花癖使他培育了一个珍稀的兰花收藏。 | 近义词: florimania | 反义词: flower aversion/apathy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek ἄνθος (ánthos, “flower”) + μανία (manía, “madness, frenzy”).
