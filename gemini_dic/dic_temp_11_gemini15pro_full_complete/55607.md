disutilize:/dɪsˈjuːtəlaɪz/
verb
使失效，使闲置
To make something no longer useful or in use; to stop using something.
The factory was disutilized after the company moved its operations overseas.  这家公司将业务转移到海外后，工厂就闲置了。
The government disutilized the old power plant. 政府停止使用旧的发电厂。
近义词:deactivate, idle, retire
反义词:utilize, employ, activate
词性:v.
