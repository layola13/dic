invocation's:/ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 祈求，调用，召唤 |  The possessive form of "invocation," referring to something belonging to or associated with an act of invoking.  It indicates ownership or a close relationship. | The invocation's power was palpable.  这祈求的力量是显明的。| The priest's invocation's solemnity moved the congregation.  牧师严肃的祈祷感动了会众。 | Synonyms:  appeal's, call's, summoning's, supplication's | Antonyms: dismissal's, rejection's | POS: noun (possessive)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *invocacioun*, from Old French invocation, from Latin invocātiō (“a calling upon, invocation”), from invocāre (“to call upon”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + vocāre (“to call”).
