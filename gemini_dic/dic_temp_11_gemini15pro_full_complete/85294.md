harmonometer:/ˌhɑːrməˈnɒmɪtər/ | /ˌhɑːrməˈnɑːmɪtər/ | n. | 和声计，谐音器 | An instrument for measuring the harmonic relations of sounds. | The physicist used a harmonometer to study the vibrations of the strings. 物理学家使用谐音器来研究弦的振动。 | The harmonometer helped the musician fine-tune the instrument.  谐音器帮助音乐家微调乐器。 | 近义词: tonometer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἁρμονία (harmonía, “harmony, concord of sounds”) +‎ -meter.
