gymnastical:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
体操的；体育的
Relating to gymnastics or physical exercise.
She demonstrated gymnastical skills on the balance beam. 她展示了在平衡木上的体操技巧。
The athlete's gymnastical prowess was impressive.  这位运动员的体操能力令人印象深刻。
近义词:athletic, gymnastic
反义词:clumsy, awkward
词性:adjective
