fulminating:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 爆炸的；怒斥的；猛烈的 | exploding suddenly and violently; expressing vehement protest; severe and sudden | The general let out a fulminating attack against the enemy's tactics. 将军对敌人的战术发起了猛烈的抨击。 |  The doctor diagnosed a fulminating appendicitis. 医生诊断为急性阑尾炎。 | 近义词：explosive, thundering, raging, furious | 反义词：mild, gentle, calm | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin fulminare ("to strike with lightning"), from fulmen ("lightning").
