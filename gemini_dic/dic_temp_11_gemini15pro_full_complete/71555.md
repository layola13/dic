flashback:/ˈflæʃbæk/| n. |闪回；倒叙|A sudden and vivid memory of a past event, especially one that is traumatic.|例句：The film uses flashbacks to tell the story of the protagonist's childhood. 这部电影用闪回讲述了主人公童年的故事。

例句：She experienced disturbing flashbacks of the accident. 她经历了事故的令人不安的闪回。|近义词：recollection, memory, reminiscence|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "present moment")|词性：名词

flashback:/ˈflæʃbæk/| v. |闪回；倒叙|To have a sudden and vivid memory of a past event.|例句：The movie flashbacks to the hero's youth to explain his current motivations.  这部电影闪回到主人公的青年时代，以解释他目前的动机。

例句：He kept flashbacking to the moment he heard the news. 他不断回想起听到这个消息的那一刻。|近义词：remember, recall, reminisce|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "forget")|词性: 动词
