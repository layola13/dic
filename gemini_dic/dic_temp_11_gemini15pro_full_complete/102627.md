Jakie decyzje zostały podjęte na spotkaniu? = What decisions were made at the meeting?

否定句:

PHRASE->
Jakie masz wieści? = What's up?/ What's new?

Bez względu na wszystko... = No matter what...



注意事项:

"jakie" is primarily used in Polish, not English. The provided examples use Polish sentences with English translations to illustrate usage within the Polish language context. There are no direct English equivalents that function grammatically in the same way.  The closest English equivalent depends heavily on context and can be "what," "which," or "what kind of."  This explanation focuses on its use *within Polish*.
