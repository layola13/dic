hopscotch:/ˈhɒpskɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːpskɑːtʃ/ (US)| n. |跳房子|A children's game played by hopping into and over squares marked on the ground.|例句：The children were playing hopscotch in the playground. 孩子们正在操场上玩跳房子。

例句：She drew a hopscotch grid with chalk on the sidewalk. 她在人行道上用粉笔画了一个跳房子格子。|近义词：jump rope, tag|反义词: None|noun
