disprofess:/ˌdɪsprəˈfɛs/| vt.|否认；放弃；拒绝承认|To deny knowledge of, connection with, or belief in; renounce; repudiate.| He disprofessed any involvement in the crime. 他否认参与了这起犯罪。|近义词：deny, renounce, repudiate, disavow, disclaim|反义词：profess, affirm, avow, acknowledge|词性:动词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French desprofesser, based on Latin profiteri ‘declare publicly’.
