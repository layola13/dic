homebody:/ˈhoʊmˌbɑːdi/| n.|宅男，宅女；喜欢待在家里的人|A person who prefers to stay at home and rarely goes out for social events.|例句：She's a real homebody and prefers a quiet night in to a party. 她是个十足的宅女，喜欢安静的夜晚待在家里而不是参加派对。

例句：He's become a bit of a homebody since he started working from home. 自从他开始在家工作以来，他变得有点宅了。|近义词：stay-at-home, introvert, recluse|反义词：social butterfly, extrovert|词性:noun
