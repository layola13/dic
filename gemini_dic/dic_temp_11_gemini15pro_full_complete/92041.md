hyperphalangia:/ˌhaɪpərˈfæləndʒiə/
n.
多指（趾）畸形
A congenital condition characterized by the presence of extra phalanges in the fingers or toes.
Polydactyly is a form of hyperphalangia.  多指畸形是多指（趾）畸形的一种形式。
Whales exhibit hyperphalangia in their flippers. 鲸鱼的鳍状肢表现出多指（趾）畸形。
近义词: polydactyly,  
反义词: oligodactyly, ectrodactyly
