greenswarded:/ˈɡriːn.swɔːr.dɪd/
adjective
绿草覆盖的
covered with green grass
The greenswarded hills rolled down to the sea.  绿草覆盖的山丘绵延至大海。
The poet described a greenswarded valley. 诗人描绘了一个绿草如茵的山谷。
近义词:grassy, verdant
反义词:barren, arid
词性:adjective

词源:
From "green" + "sward" + "-ed."  "Green" refers to the color green. "Sward" refers to a stretch of grassy ground.  "-ed" is a suffix used to form adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
