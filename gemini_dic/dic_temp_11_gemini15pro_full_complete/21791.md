bobbling:/ˈbɒblɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːblɪŋ/ (US)| v. n. adj.|摇晃，摆动，晃动;  摇晃的动作或状态|A bobbling motion/movement refers to a jerky, unsteady up-and-down or side-to-side movement.  As a verb, it means to move in this way.  As an adjective, it describes something that bobs.|The bobbing of the small boat on the waves made me feel seasick. 小船在波浪上的晃动让我感到晕船。

The fishing float was bobbing gently on the surface of the water.  鱼漂在水面上轻轻地上下摆动。

She wore a hat with a bobbling feather. 她戴着一顶帽子，上面有一根摇晃的羽毛。|近义词：shaking, wobbling, oscillating, quivering|反义词:still, steady|词性:verb (present participle), noun, adjective
