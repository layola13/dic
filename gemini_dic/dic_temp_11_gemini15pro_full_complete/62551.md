enemy: /ˈɛnəmi/ (US) /ˈɛnɪmi/ (UK)| n.| 敌人，仇敌，敌军，有害之物|A person who feels hatred for, and wants to harm, another person. |例句：He made many enemies during his political career.  他在政治生涯中树敌众多。

例句：The two countries were bitter enemies during the war. 这两个国家在战争期间是死敌。

近义词：foe, adversary, opponent, rival|反义词: friend, ally, supporter|词性: noun
