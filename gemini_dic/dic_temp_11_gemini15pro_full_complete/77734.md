gemstones:/ˈdʒɛmˌstoʊnz/
n.
宝石
A precious or semiprecious stone, especially one cut, polished, and used in jewelry.
She wore a necklace of sparkling gemstones. 她戴着一条闪闪发光的宝石项链。
Synonyms: jewels, gems, precious stones
Antonyms: rocks, pebbles
noun
