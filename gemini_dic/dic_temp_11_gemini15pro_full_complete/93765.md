immediate:/ɪˈmiːdiət/ | adj. |立即的，直接的，最接近的|happening or done without delay; existing now and needing attention quickly; nearest in time, relationship, or rank|例句：We need an immediate response. 我们需要立即回复。|例句：The immediate cause of the accident is unknown. 事故的直接原因不明。|近义词：instant, prompt, direct, next|反义词：delayed, later, distant, eventual|词性:形容词

词源:  From Late Middle English *immediat, from Late Latin immediātus (“not mediated, immediate”), from Latin in- (“not”) + mediātus (“mediated”).
