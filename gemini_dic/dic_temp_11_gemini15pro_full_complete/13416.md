atoms:/ˈætəmz/ | n. |原子|The basic unit of a chemical element. |Everything is made up of atoms.  一切都是由原子构成的。| synonym: particle, element | antonym: molecule, compound | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄτομος (átomos, “indivisible”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + τέμνω (témnō, “I cut”).
