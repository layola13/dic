infamous:/ˈɪnfəməs/ | adj. | 声名狼藉的，臭名昭著的 | Widely known for something bad; having a very bad reputation. |例句：The infamous criminal was finally caught.  臭名昭著的罪犯终于被抓住了。|近义词：notorious, disreputable, ignominious |反义词：famous, celebrated, renowned |词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin *infamosus, from in- ‘not’ + famous ‘famous’. The original sense was ‘having lost civil or other rights as a result of conviction for crime’, hence ‘publicly disgraced’.
