muff:/mʌf/| v. | 笨拙地处理，失误，搞砸 | To handle something clumsily or fail to catch, hold, or accomplish something. |  She completely muffed her lines in the play. 她在剧中完全搞砸了台词。| 近义词：bungle, botch, flub | 反义词: succeed, achieve | verb
