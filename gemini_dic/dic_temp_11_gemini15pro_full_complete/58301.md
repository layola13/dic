dry-cleaner's: /ˈdraɪˌklinərz/ (US), /ˈdraɪˌkliːnəz/ (UK)
noun
干洗店
A shop where clothes are cleaned using chemicals rather than water.
I took my suit to the dry-cleaner's. 我把我的西装送到干洗店去了。
The dry-cleaner's is on the corner of Main Street. 干洗店在主街的拐角处。
Synonyms: dry-cleaning establishment, cleaners
Antonyms: laundry, laundromat
词性: 名词
