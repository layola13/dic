colead:/ˈkoʊliːd/| n. v.|共同领导，共同主持人|A person who shares leadership or主持 responsibilities with another.|例句：The project had two coleads to ensure its success.  这个项目有两个共同负责人以确保其成功。|近义词：co-leader, joint leader, shared leader|反义词:|n. v.


colead:/ˈkoʊliːd/| v.|共同领导，共同主持|To lead or host something jointly with another person.|例句：They colead the weekly team meeting.  他们共同主持每周的团队会议。|近义词：co-manage, co-direct, co-present|反义词:|v.
