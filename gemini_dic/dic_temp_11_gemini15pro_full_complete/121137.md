misdelivering:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/
verb
递错，投递错误
Failing to deliver to the intended recipient or destination.
The package was misdelivered to the wrong address. 包裹被投递错了地址。
misdirecting, misrouting
correctly delivering, delivering appropriately
verb
