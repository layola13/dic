inconsolably:/ˌɪnkənˈsoʊləbli/ | adv. | 无法安慰地，悲痛欲绝地 | In a way that cannot be consoled or comforted; beyond comfort. | She wept inconsolably after the death of her pet. 她心爱的宠物去世后，她悲痛欲绝地哭泣。|  The child cried inconsolably when his toy broke.  当孩子的玩具坏了时，他哭得无法安慰。 | Near Synonyms: despairingly, hopelessly, disconsolately, heartbrokenly | Near Antonyms: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | Part of Speech: Adverb
