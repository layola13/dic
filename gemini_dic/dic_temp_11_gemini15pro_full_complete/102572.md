jaghirdar: /dʒɑːˈɡɪərdɑːr/ | n. | 地主，采邑主 | A holder of a jagir (a feudal land grant conferring the right to collect revenue and administer justice). |  The jaghirdar ruled his territory with absolute authority.  这位地主以绝对的权力统治着他的领地。

 The jaghirdar was responsible for collecting taxes and maintaining order.  这位地主负责收税和维持秩序。| Synonym: landlord, feudal lord, zamindar | Antonym: peasant, serf | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Persian جاگیردار (jāgirdār), from جاگیر (jāgīr, “land grant”) + دار (dār, “holder”).  Ultimately from Sanskrit स्थान (sthāna, “place”).
