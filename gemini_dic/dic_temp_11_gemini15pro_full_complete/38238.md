computerises:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪz/
verb
计算机化；使…计算机化
converts (a system or process) to one controlled by or involving computers.
The factory has been fully computerised.  这家工厂已经完全计算机化了。
Many records are now computerised. 现在很多记录都已计算机化。
computerize, automate, digitalize
manualize, decomputerize
v.
