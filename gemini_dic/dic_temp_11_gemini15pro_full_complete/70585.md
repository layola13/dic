fillip:/ˈfɪlɪp/| n. vt.|刺激,激励;弹指|Something that acts as a stimulus or boost to an activity or interest.  A flick or snap with the finger.|The news gave a fillip to the telecommunications sector.  这消息刺激了电信行业。 He gave the spinning top a fillip. 他弹了一下旋转的陀螺。|近义词：stimulus, boost, incentive, encouragement, flick, snap|反义词：deterrent, discouragement, hindrance|noun
fillip:/ˈfɪlɪp/| vt.|轻弹,轻击|To strike or propel something with a flick or snap of the finger.|He filliped the coin across the table. 他用手指轻弹硬币穿过桌子。|近义词：flick, snap, flip|反义词：-|verb
