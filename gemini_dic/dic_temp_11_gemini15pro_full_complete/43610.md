creased:/kriːst/ | adj. | 有褶皱的，起皱的 | Having lines or folds on the surface, typically as a result of being folded or crushed. |  The shirt was badly creased after being in the suitcase.  这件衬衫在行李箱里放久了，皱得很厉害。| synonyms: wrinkled, folded, lined, rumpled | antonyms: smooth, ironed, flat | adjective

crease:/kriːs/ | n. | 褶皱，折痕 | A line or ridge produced on paper or cloth by folding, pressing, or crushing. |  He smoothed the creases out of his jacket. 他把外套上的褶皱抚平了。| synonyms: wrinkle, fold, line, furrow | antonyms: smoothness, flatness | noun

crease:/kriːs/ | v. |  使起皱；起皱 | To make a crease or creases in (something); to become creased. |  He creased his brow in concentration. 他皱着眉头，聚精会神。| synonyms: wrinkle, crinkle, rumple | antonyms: smooth, iron, flatten | verb
