autopoietic:/ˌɔːtəʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk/ | adj. | 自创生的，自组织的 |  Referring to a system capable of maintaining and reproducing itself by creating its own parts and components.  It emphasizes self-organization and self-maintenance. |  Example:  Living organisms are considered autopoietic systems because they continuously regenerate and repair themselves. 生物被认为是自创生系统，因为它们不断地再生和修复自己。

Example:  Maturana and Varela's theory of autopoiesis revolutionized the understanding of biological systems.  马图拉纳和瓦雷拉的自创生理论彻底改变了对生物系统的理解。 | 近义词: self-producing, self-organizing, self-maintaining | 反义词: allopoietic | 词性: adjective
