knee-high:/ˈniːhaɪ/| adj.|齐膝高的；很矮的|Reaching up to the knees.  Also used figuratively to describe something small or insignificant. |The children were playing in the knee-high grass. 孩子们在齐膝高的草丛里玩耍。|Synonyms: short, low|Antonyms: tall, high|adjective

knee-high:/ˈniːhaɪ/| n.|齐膝高的事物；及膝长靴|Something that reaches the knees. |The snowdrifts were knee-high in places. 有些地方的雪堆积到齐膝高。|Synonyms: boots|Antonyms: N/A|noun
