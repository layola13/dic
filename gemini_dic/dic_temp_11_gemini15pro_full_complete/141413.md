ophiophagous:/ˌɒfiˈɒfəɡəs/| adj.|食蛇的，以蛇为食的|Feeding on snakes; snake-eating.  Derived from Greek *ophis* "snake" + *-phagos* "eating".|The king cobra is an ophiophagous reptile, often preying on other venomous snakes.|Synonyms: snake-eating|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
