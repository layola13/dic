chronic's: /ˈkrɒnɪks/ | n. | chronicles（chronic 的复数形式） |  The plural form of "chronic," meaning a record of events in chronological order. It can also refer to something that is persistent or long-lasting (though this usage is less common for the plural form). | He kept detailed chronicles of his travels. 他详细记录了他的旅行。 | 近义词: annals, records, histories | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun


词源:

From Late Latin *chronicus, from Ancient Greek χρονικός (khronikós, “of time, concerning time”), from χρόνος (khrónos, “time”).
