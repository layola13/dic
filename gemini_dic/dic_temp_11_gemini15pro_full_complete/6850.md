anatiferous:/ˌænəˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
产鸭子的；长出像藤壶一样的东西的
Producing ducks; bearing barnacles or similar organisms.
The myth of the barnacle goose arose from the observation of anatiferous barnacles attached to driftwood. 藤壶鹅的神话源于人们观察到附着在浮木上的产鸭子的藤壶。
The anatiferous tree was believed to grow geese. 人们相信产鸭子的树会长出鹅。
近义词:barnacle-bearing
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "barren")
