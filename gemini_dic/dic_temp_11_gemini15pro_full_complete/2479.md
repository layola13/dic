adjust: /əˈdʒʌst/ | v. | 调整，调节；适应 | To change something slightly, especially to make it more correct, suitable, or effective; to become more familiar with a new situation. |  例句：She adjusted her glasses before reading. 她阅读前调整了一下眼镜。| 近义词：adapt, modify, alter, regulate, tune | 反义词：disturb, upset, worsen | 词性: 动词

adjust: /əˈdʒʌst/ | v. | 校正；校准 | To change something slightly, especially to make it more correct, accurate, or effective. |  例句：He adjusted the focus of the camera. 他调整了相机的焦距。| 近义词：correct, rectify, fine-tune | 反义词：distort, misalign | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adjustare, from Latin ad- "to" + justus "right, just."
