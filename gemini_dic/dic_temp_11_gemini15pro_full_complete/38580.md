concordal:/kənˈkɔːrdəl/| adj.|和谐的，一致的，相符的|Being in agreement or harmony; corresponding.  Relating to or based on a concordance.|The concordal passages in the Gospels tell the same story. 福音书中一致的段落讲述了同一个故事。|近义词：harmonious, agreeable, consistent, corresponding|反义词：discordant, conflicting, dissonant|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concordalis*, from Latin concord-, concors "agreeing, harmonious," from com- "together" + cord-, cor "heart."
