depending:/dɪˈpɛndɪŋ/| prep.|取决于，视…而定|depending is a preposition that introduces a condition or factor that influences something else. It is always followed by "on".|The price will vary depending on the size of the house. 价格会根据房屋大小而有所不同。|近义词：conditional on, subject to, contingent on|反义词:independent of|词性:介词

词源: Middle English: present participle of depend, from Old French dependre, from Latin dependere ‘hang down from’.
