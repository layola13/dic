calvary: /ˈkælvəri/ (US), /ˈkælvəri/ (UK)| n. |髑髅地; 苦难的经历|A place of skull; an experience of intense suffering. |例句：His life has been a long calvary. 他的一生充满了苦难。

例句：They reached the calvary after a long and arduous journey. 经过漫长而艰辛的旅程，他们到达了髑髅地。|近义词：ordeal, trial, tribulation, suffering, agony|反义词:joy, bliss, comfort, ease|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *Calvaria, from Latin calvaria (“skull”), from calvus (“bald”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (“bald, bare”).
