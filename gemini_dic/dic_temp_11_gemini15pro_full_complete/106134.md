knitted:/ˈnɪtɪd/| adj.| 编织的，针织的| made by knitting; close and intimate |  例句：She wore a knitted scarf. 她戴着一条针织围巾。| 近义词：woven, crocheted | 反义词: unraveled, loose | 词性:形容词

knitted:/ˈnɪtɪd/| v.| 编织，针织 (knit的过去式和过去分词)| to make (a garment, fabric, etc.) by interlocking loops of yarn or thread with needles | 例句：She knitted a sweater for her grandson. 她为孙子织了一件毛衣。| 近义词：woven, crocheted | 反义词: unravel | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cnyttan, from Proto-Germanic *knuttijaną (“to knot”), from *knuttō (“knot”). Cognate with Scots knit (“to knit”), West Frisian knetje (“to knit”), Dutch knutten (“to knit, tie”), German knütten (“to tie, knot”), Danish knytte (“to knit, tie”), Swedish knyta (“to tie, knit”). Related to knot.
