attaining:/əˈteɪnɪŋ/

attaining:/əˈteɪnɪŋ/
adj. |达到的，获得的|Used to describe something that is in the process of being achieved or reached. |Her attaining mastery of the piano took years of practice. 她获得钢琴的精通花了好几年的练习。 |achieving, reaching, accomplishing, gaining, obtaining|failing, missing, losing|adjective
