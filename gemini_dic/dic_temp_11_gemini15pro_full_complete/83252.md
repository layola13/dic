gymnoblastic:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈblæstɪk/ | adj. | 裸芽的，裸生的 |  (of certain hydroids) having the gonophores unprotected by a gonotheca. |  Gymnoblastic hydroids are characterized by their exposed reproductive structures. 裸芽水螅的特征是其生殖结构暴露在外。| Synonym: achlamydeous | Antonym: calyptoblastic | word type: adjective

gymnoblastic:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈblæstɪk/ | n. | 裸芽类 | A gymnoblastic hydroid. |  The study focused on the diversity of gymnoblastic hydroids in the region. 这项研究的重点是该地区裸芽水螅的多样性. | Synonym:  Anthomedusae (sometimes used for the order containing many gymnoblastic hydroids) | Antonym: Leptomedusae (often associated with calyptoblastic hydroids) | word type: noun
