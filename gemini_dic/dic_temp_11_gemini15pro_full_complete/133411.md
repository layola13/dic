nonfalsifiable:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːlsɪfaɪəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɔːlsɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可证伪的 | Not capable of being proved false;  A statement, hypothesis, or theory is nonfalsifiable if it cannot be disproven by any possible empirical observation or experiment. |  His theory about the origin of the universe is nonfalsifiable, making it difficult to test scientifically.  他的关于宇宙起源的理论是不可证伪的，因此难以用科学方法检验。

| Example: The claim that "invisible fairies paint the leaves in autumn" is nonfalsifiable because no evidence could disprove it.  “看不见的仙女在秋天为树叶涂色”的说法是不可证伪的，因为没有任何证据可以反驳它。

| Synonym:  untestable, unprovable, unverifiable | Antonym:  falsifiable, testable, verifiable | word type: adjective
