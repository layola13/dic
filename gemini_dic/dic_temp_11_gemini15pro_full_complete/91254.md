hydrofluate:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfluːeɪt/
n.
氢氟酸盐; 氟化物
A salt of hydrofluoric acid.  A compound containing fluorine and one other element.
Hydrofluates are used in various industrial processes. 氢氟酸盐用于各种工业过程。
Potassium hydrofluate is a common example. 氟化钾是一个常见的例子。
Synonyms: fluoride
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)
