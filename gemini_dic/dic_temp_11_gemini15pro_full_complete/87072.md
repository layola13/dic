heptangular:/hɛpˈtæŋɡjʊlər/
adjective
七角形的；七邊形的
Having seven angles and seven sides.
A heptangular prism is a three-dimensional shape with two heptangular bases and seven rectangular sides.  一个七角棱镜是一个三维形状，有两个七角形的底面和七个长方形的侧面。

The fortress was built on a heptangular plan.  这座堡垒是按照七边形平面图建造的。

synonym:septagonal
antonym:circular, round

词性:adjective
