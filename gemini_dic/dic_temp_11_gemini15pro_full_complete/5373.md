alluvial's:/əˈluːviəl/ |adj. | 冲积的；由冲积物形成的 | Relating to or deposited by flowing water. |  The alluvial soil in the delta is incredibly fertile. 三角洲的冲积土非常肥沃。 | The farmers relied on the alluvial deposits left by the river each spring. 农民们依靠每年春天河流留下的冲积物。 | 近义词：fluvial, sedimentary | 反义词： igneous, volcanic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *alluvialis*, from Latin alluvius (“wash, inundation”), from alluere (“to wash against”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + luere (“to wash”).
