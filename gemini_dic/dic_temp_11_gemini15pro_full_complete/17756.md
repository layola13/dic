bedfellows:/ˈbedˌfeləʊz/ (US) /ˈbedˌfeləʊz/ (UK)| n. |同床者；伙伴；盟友|People allied or associated with each other, especially in a surprising or unusual way.|例句：Politics makes strange bedfellows. 政治会促成奇怪的联盟。

例句：He found himself bedfellows with former enemies. 他发现自己与昔日的敌人成了盟友。


近义词：associates, allies, partners, collaborators|反义词：enemies, opponents, adversaries|词性:名词
