despect:/dɪˈspekt/ | v. | 轻视，蔑视 | To regard with contempt or scorn; to look down upon. | He despected those who were less educated than him. 他蔑视那些比他受教育程度低的人。| She despected his attempts at flattery. 她鄙视他试图奉承她的举动。 | 近义词: disdain, scorn, despise, contemn | 反义词: respect, admire, esteem | 词性: 动词


词源: From Latin *despectus*, past participle of *despicere* "to look down on," from *de-* "down" + *specere* "to look at."
