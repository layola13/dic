axai: /ˈæksaɪ/
noun| 可解释人工智能 (Explainable Artificial Intelligence)| Artificial intelligence (AI) in which the results of the solution can be understood by humans.  It contrasts with the "black box" model of AI, where even its designers cannot fully explain why it arrived at a specific decision or result. | Example:  Researchers are increasingly focusing on axai to build trust and transparency in AI systems. 研究人员越来越关注可解释人工智能，以建立人工智能系统的信任和透明度。 | Example: Axai is crucial for applications like medical diagnosis where understanding the reasoning behind a decision is paramount.  对于医疗诊断等应用，可解释人工智能至关重要，因为理解决策背后的推理至关重要。 | Synonyms:  interpretable AI, transparent AI | Antonyms: black box AI |n.
