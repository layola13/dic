ardentest:/ˈɑːrdəntɪst/| adj.|最热烈的，最热情的|Superlative form of "ardent": characterized by warmth of feeling typically expressed in eager zealous support or activity.|She was his ardentest admirer, attending every concert and buying all his albums.|近义词：most passionate, most enthusiastic, most fervent|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle French *ardant*, from Old French *ardant*, present participle of *arder* (“to burn”), from Latin *ārdēns*, present participle of *ārdere* (“to burn”).  The *-est* suffix is the superlative ending in English.
