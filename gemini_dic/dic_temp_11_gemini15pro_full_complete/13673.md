attractively:/əˈtræktɪvli/
adverb
动词
迷人地，有吸引力地
in a way that attracts interest or pleasure
She was attractively dressed in a simple blue dress. 她穿着一条简单的蓝色连衣裙，十分迷人。
The food was attractively presented. 食物摆放得很诱人。
近义词: appealingly, charmingly, enchantingly, pleasingly
反义词: unattractively, repellently
词性: 副词
