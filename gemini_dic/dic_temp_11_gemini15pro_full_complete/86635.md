hell-like:/ˈhɛlˌlaɪk/
adjective
地狱般的; 如地狱的
resembling or suggestive of hell; infernal.
The air was thick with smoke, and the heat was hell-like. 空气中弥漫着浓烟，热得像地狱一样。
The refugees described hell-like conditions in the camp. 难民描述了营地里地狱般的状况。
infernal, hellish, diabolical
heavenly, paradisiacal
adjective
