maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ (US), /məˈnuːvə/ (UK)| n. v. |策略；调遣；演习；操纵|A cleverly planned scheme or action designed to achieve a particular goal.|例句：The company's latest marketing maneuver is a social media campaign. 该公司最新的营销策略是在社交媒体上开展宣传活动。|近义词：tactic, strategy, operation, ploy, scheme|反义词：inaction, idleness|词性：名词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manœuvrer*, from Old French *manovre*, *maneouvre* "manual labor, work done by hand," from *manœuvrer*, *maneovrer* "to work by hand," ultimately from Latin *manu operare* "to work by hand."
