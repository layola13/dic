gentilize:/ˈdʒɛntɪlaɪz/| v. |使文雅；使高贵；使温和；使有教养|To make something or someone more refined, polite, or cultured.  To render (a plant, animal, etc.) less adapted to its natural environment and more suited to human needs.|She tried to gentilize the rough-mannered children by teaching them etiquette. 她试图通过教他们礼仪来使这些举止粗鲁的孩子变得文雅。|近义词：refine, civilize, polish, cultivate|反义词：coarsen, brutalize|verb
