bin:/bɪn/| n. |垃圾桶；容器；储藏室|A container for storing things, especially rubbish/garbage.|例句：Please put the empty bottles in the recycling bin. 请把空瓶子放到回收箱里。

例句：The apples are stored in bins in the cellar. 苹果储藏在地窖的箱子里。|近义词：container, receptacle, box, crate|反义词: N/A|名词

bin:/bɪn/| vt. |丢弃；把…扔进垃圾桶|To throw something away.|例句：He binned the old newspapers. 他把旧报纸扔掉了。

例句：I binned the idea after I realized it wouldn't work.  在我意识到这个想法行不通之后，我就放弃了它。|近义词：discard, throw away, dispose of|反义词: keep, save|动词
