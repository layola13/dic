counterrioters:/ˌkaʊntərˈraɪətərz/ | n. | 反暴乱者；镇暴者 | People who oppose rioters, often by taking actions to stop or suppress a riot.  |  The counterrioters clashed with the protesters in the streets.  反暴乱者与抗议者在街上发生了冲突。| Synonyms: vigilantes, opponents, resisters | Antonyms: rioters, insurgents, rebels | noun

counterrioters:/ˌkaʊntərˈraɪətərz/ | n. |反抗暴动的人|Those who act against rioters, often to maintain order or suppress the riot. | The police acted as counterrioters to quell the unrest. 警方充当了反抗暴动的人的角色来平息骚乱。 | Synonyms:  peacekeepers, law enforcers | Antonyms: rioters, agitators | noun
