diktat:/dɪkˈtæt/ /ˈdɪkteɪt/| n. |独裁命令，勒令；规定|An order or decree imposed by someone in power without popular consent.  A dogmatic decree. | The company president issued a diktat that all employees must work overtime. 公司总裁下令所有员工必须加班。|Synonyms: decree, edict, mandate, fiat, order, command, injunction|Antonyms: request, plea, suggestion|noun

diktat:/dɪkˈtæt/ /ˈdɪkteɪt/| n. |独裁统治|Rule by decree or arbitrary order.| The country suffered under the diktat of a ruthless dictator. 这个国家遭受着无情独裁者的独裁统治。|Synonyms: autocracy, despotism, tyranny|Antonyms: democracy, self-government|noun
