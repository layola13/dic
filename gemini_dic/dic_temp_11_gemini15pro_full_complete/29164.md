cash-starved: /ˈkæʃ stɑːrvd/ | adj. | 资金匮乏的，缺乏现金的 | Experiencing a severe lack of cash or having very little money available.  | The cash-starved company was forced to lay off workers. 这家资金匮乏的公司被迫裁员。 | The cash-starved startup struggled to meet its payroll obligations. 这家资金短缺的初创公司难以履行其工资义务。 | Synonyms: impoverished, impecunious, broke, strapped for cash | Antonyms: affluent, wealthy, prosperous | adjective

词源:  "cash" + "starved".  "Cash" refers to money, especially readily available money. "Starved" means suffering or dying from hunger.  The combination creates the meaning of lacking necessary cash flow.
