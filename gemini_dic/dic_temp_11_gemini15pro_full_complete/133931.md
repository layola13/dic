nonhappening:/ˌnɑːnˈhæpənɪŋ/
noun
无事发生；平淡无奇的事情
an event that was planned but that did not take place; something dull and uninteresting
The party was a total nonhappening.  那场派对真是无聊透顶。
The much-anticipated concert turned out to be a nonhappening.  备受期待的音乐会结果却平淡无奇。
synonyms: nonevent, dud, flop
antonyms: event, happening, success
n.
