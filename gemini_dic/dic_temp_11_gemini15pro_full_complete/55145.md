dissection's:/dɪˈsekʃənz/| n.|解剖；剖析；仔细分析|The dissection's findings revealed the cause of death.  解剖结果揭示了死因。|例句：The frog dissection's purpose was to study the amphibian's internal organs.  解剖青蛙的目的是为了研究两栖动物的内脏。|近义词：analysis, examination, scrutiny|反义词: synthesis, construction|词性:名词
词源: From Late Latin dissectionem,  accusative of dissectio (“a cutting apart”), from dissecare (“to cut apart”), from dis- (“apart”) + secare (“to cut”).
