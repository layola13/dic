manicurists:/ˈmænɪkjʊrɪsts/
n.
美甲师；指甲修饰师
A person who gives manicures.  A person who cleans, shapes, and polishes fingernails and toenails.
例句：The manicurists at the salon are highly skilled.  这家沙龙的美甲师技术很高。

例句：She trained as a manicurist after leaving school. 她毕业后接受了美甲师培训。

近义词：nail technician, beautician
反义词: None (没有明确的反义词)
词性: Noun
