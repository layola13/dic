deduplicate:/diːˈduːplɪkeɪt/
verb
去重，删除重复项
To remove duplicate copies of something, usually data.
The software can deduplicate files to save disk space.  这个软件可以去重文件以节省磁盘空间。
The system automatically deduplicates entries. 系统自动删除重复的条目。
Synonyms: remove duplicates, eliminate duplicates, weed out duplicates, cleanse
Antonyms: duplicate, replicate, copy
词性: 动词
