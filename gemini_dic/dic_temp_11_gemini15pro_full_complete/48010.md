decipheress:/dɪˈsaɪfərəs/
noun
女译解员；女破译员
A woman who deciphers something, especially a coded message or a difficult handwriting.
The decipheress worked tirelessly to crack the enemy's code. 女译解员不知疲倦地工作，以破解敌人的密码。
Near Synonyms: decoder, cryptanalyst, interpreter
Antonyms: encoder, encryptor
词性: 名词
