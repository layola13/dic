grey-haired:/ˈɡreɪˌhɛəd/| adj.|灰白的，头发花白的|Having grey or white hair.  This adjective describes someone whose hair has turned grey, typically due to age.|例句：The grey-haired gentleman sat on the park bench. 那位头发花白的先生坐在公园长椅上。

例句：My grey-haired grandmother told me stories of her childhood. 我头发花白的祖母给我讲了她童年时代的故事。|近义词：hoary, silver-haired, white-haired|反义词:young, youthful|词性:adjective

词源:
From 'grey' + 'haired'.  'Grey' comes from Old English 'græg', of Germanic origin. 'Haired' comes from Old English 'hǣred', also of Germanic origin.
