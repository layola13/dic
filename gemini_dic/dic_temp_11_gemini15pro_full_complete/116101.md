martyrological:/ˌmɑːrtərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 殉道者的；殉道学上的；殉道录的 | Relating to martyrs or the study of martyrs; resembling or characteristic of a martyr |  His book took a martyrological tone, portraying the victims as innocent sufferers.  他的书采用了殉道者的口吻，把受害者描绘成无辜的受难者。

The museum houses a martyrological collection of artifacts related to the early Christian martyrs.  这个博物馆收藏了与早期基督教殉道者相关的殉道文物。| 近义词: hagiographic, saintly | 反义词: secular, worldly | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek "martyr" (witness) + "-logos" (study of) + "-ical" (adjective suffix)
