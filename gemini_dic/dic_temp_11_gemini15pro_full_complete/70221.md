fibulae: /ˈfɪbjʊliː/
n.
腓骨（复数）
The outer and usually smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle in vertebrates.  The plural form of fibula.
Both fibulae were fractured in the accident.  事故中两根腓骨都骨折了。
近义词: calf bone (口语)
反义词: tibia (胫骨)
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

fibula: /ˈfɪbjʊlə/
n.
腓骨
The outer and usually smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle in vertebrates.
The fibula is thinner than the tibia. 腓骨比胫骨细。
近义词: calf bone (口语)
反义词: tibia (胫骨)
词性: 名词 (noun)
