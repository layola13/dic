materials:/məˈtɪəriəlz/ /məˈtɪriəlz/ | adj. | 物质的；肉体的 | relating to physical matter rather than mind or spirit. | The material world is all around us. 物质世界就在我们身边。 | synonyms: physical, tangible, corporeal | antonyms: spiritual, immaterial, intangible | adjective
