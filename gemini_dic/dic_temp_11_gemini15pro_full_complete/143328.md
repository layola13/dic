outechoes:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊz/ | v. | 超越回声；胜过；盖过 |  to surpass in echoing; to outdo in sound or resonance; to exceed in reverberation or impact. | The singer's powerful voice outechoed the accompanying instruments. 歌手浑厚的嗓音盖过了伴奏乐器的声音。 | synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, transcend | antonyms:  be subdued by, be overshadowed by | verb

outechoes:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊz/ | n. |  胜过的声音；压倒性的回声 | a sound that surpasses another in volume or resonance; a reverberation that exceeds others in impact or duration. | The outechoes of the explosion could be heard for miles. 爆炸的巨大回响几英里外都能听到。 | synonyms: reverberations, echoes, resonance | antonyms: whispers, murmurs, faint sounds | noun
