interdiffusion:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |相互扩散，互相扩散|The process by which atoms or molecules of different substances mix together as a result of random thermal motion.  This mixing occurs across an interface or within a single phase.|例句：Interdiffusion of nickel and chromium is crucial in the formation of certain alloys. 镍和铬的相互扩散对于某些合金的形成至关重要。

例句：The interdiffusion of dopants in semiconductors influences device performance. 半导体中掺杂剂的相互扩散会影响器件性能。|近义词：diffusion, intermixing |反义词: segregation |词性:noun
