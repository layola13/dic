fizzier:/ˈfɪziər/ | adj. |更起泡的，更兴奋的|More bubbly; more effervescent.  A comparative form of "fizzy".|The champagne was fizzier than the sparkling wine. 这香槟比气泡酒更起泡。|The crowd became fizzier as the concert started. 随着演唱会的开始，人群变得更加兴奋。|近义词：bubbly, effervescent, sparkling, lively|反义词：flat, still, dull|词性:形容词
