nonportrayal:/ˌnɑːnpɔːrˈtreɪəl/ | n. | 未描绘; 未刻画;  |The nonportrayal of women in leadership roles in the media perpetuates stereotypes. | 媒体中对女性领导角色的未描绘使刻板印象永久化。 | Synonyms: omission, exclusion, absence | Antonyms: depiction, portrayal, representation | noun
Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "portrayal" (from "portray," from Old French "portraire" - to paint, draw).
