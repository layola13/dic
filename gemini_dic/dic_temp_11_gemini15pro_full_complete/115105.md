manganous:/ˈmæŋɡənəs/ |adj.|亚锰的，二价锰的|Containing manganese with a valence of 2.  Relating to or containing manganese, especially with valence 2.|Manganous sulfate is used as a fertilizer. 硫酸亚锰用作肥料。|近义词: manganese(II), divalent manganese|反义词: manganic (referring to manganese with a valence of 3 or 4)|词性: adjective
