ICBM:/ˌaɪ siː biː ˈɛm/| n. |洲际弹道导弹|Intercontinental ballistic missile, a guided missile with a minimum range of 5,500 kilometers (3,400 mi) primarily designed for nuclear weapons delivery.  |The threat of ICBM attacks during the Cold War was a constant fear. 冷战期间，洲际弹道导弹攻击的威胁一直令人恐惧。

|Example: The country developed its own ICBM program. 这个国家发展了自己的洲际弹道导弹计划。|Synonym: ballistic missile, nuclear missile|Antonym: N/A|noun
