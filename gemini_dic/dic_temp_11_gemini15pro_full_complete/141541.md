opportunist:/ˈɒpətjuːnɪst/ (UK), /ˌɑːpərˈtuːnɪst/ (US)| n.|机会主义者；投机取巧者|A person who exploits circumstances to gain immediate advantage rather than being guided by consistent principles or plans.|He's a ruthless opportunist who will stop at nothing to get what he wants. 他是一个无情的机会主义者，为了得到他想要的东西会不择手段。

He saw the crisis as an opportunity, but others called him an opportunist. 他将危机视为机遇，但其他人称他为机会主义者。


|近义词：exploiter, self-seeker, pragmatist|反义词:principled person, idealist|noun|
