ichthyophagous:/ɪkθiˈɒfəɡəs/| adj.|食鱼的，鱼食性的|Feeding on fish; fish-eating.  Derived from Greek *ikhthús* (fish) + *-phagos* (eater).|例句：Penguins are ichthyophagous birds.  企鹅是食鱼的鸟类。

例句：Many marine mammals are ichthyophagous.  许多海洋哺乳动物都是食鱼的。|近义词：piscivorous|反义词:herbivorous, carnivorous (if not specifically fish-eating)|词性:adjective
