hawed:/hɔːd/| v. | 左转舵；转向逆风方向 | To turn a ship or boat away from the wind.  The captain hawed the ship to avoid the rocks. 船长左转舵以避开礁石。| 近义词: turn, veer | 反义词: jibe, wear | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *hauwen, from Old Norse *háfa (“to heave, lift”).  Related to heave.
