antefix:/ˈæntɪfɪks/
n.
垂饰，瓦当
An ornament, often of terra cotta, fixed vertically along the eaves of a classical building.
The antefixes on the temple were elaborately decorated with floral motifs.  这座寺庙的瓦当装饰着精致的花卉图案。
Synonyms:acroterion,  eave tile,  ornament
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "plain roof" as a contrasting concept)
