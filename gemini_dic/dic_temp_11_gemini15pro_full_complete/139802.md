octic:/ˈɒktɪk/ | adj. n.| 八次的，八进制的；八项式 |Of or relating to the eighth degree, order, or power.  Also, a mathematical expression of the eighth degree. |An octic equation is one in which the highest power of the unknown quantity is the eighth power. 八次方程是未知数最高次幂为八次幂的方程。| The octic polynomial had complex roots.  这个八次多项式有复根。 |  eighth-degree, eighth-order |  - | adjective, noun
