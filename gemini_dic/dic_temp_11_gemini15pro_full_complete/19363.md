bibliopolistic:/ˌbɪblɪəpəˈlɪstɪk/
bibliopolistic| adj.|书商的；贩书的；爱书的；藏书的|Relating to booksellers, bookselling, or bibliophiles; pertaining to the love or collection of books.|He inherited his grandfather's bibliopolistic tendencies and amassed a huge library. 他继承了祖父爱书的癖好，积累了大量的藏书。|
bibliopolistic|近义词：bookish, bibliophilic|
bibliopolistic|反义词：apathy towards books|
bibliopolistic|词性: adjective|
bibliopolistic|词源: From Greek biblio- (book) + -polistic (selling).|
