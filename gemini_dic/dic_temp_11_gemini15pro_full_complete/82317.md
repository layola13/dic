grouters:/ˈɡraʊtərz/| n.|灌浆工；灌浆机|People who or machines that apply grout (a thin mortar) to fill gaps in tiles, bricks, etc.  A grouter fills the spaces between tiles with grout.|The tilers finished laying the tiles, and the grouters came in the next day.|近义词：tile setters, cementers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "demolishers" in a very loose sense if they remove grouted material)|词性:noun
