microsurgical: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
显微外科的
Relating to or involving microsurgery.  Performed using a microscope and specialized instruments.
Microsurgical techniques are used to reattach severed limbs.  显微外科技术用于重新连接断肢。
Microsurgical instruments are extremely delicate.  显微外科器械极其精细。
synonyms:  microscopic, fine, delicate, precise
antonyms:  macroscopic, gross, crude
