frothiness:/ˈfrɒθɪnɪs/ /ˈfrɑːθɪnɪs/
noun
泡沫性；起泡沫的性质
the quality of being frothy; full of or covered with froth.
The frothiness of the cappuccino was perfect.  卡布奇诺的泡沫非常完美。
Near synonyms: foaminess, bubbliness, effervescence
Antonyms: flatness, stillness
词性: 名词


frothy:/ˈfrɒθi/ /ˈfrɑːθi/
adjective
起泡沫的；多泡的
full of or covered with froth.
He poured me a frothy beer. 他给我倒了一杯泡沫丰富的啤酒。
Near synonyms: foamy, bubbly, effervescent
Antonyms: flat, still
词性: 形容词
