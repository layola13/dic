nonconsolidated:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd/
adjective
未合并的，未整合的
not combined into a single unit; not consolidated.
The company released nonconsolidated financial results for its subsidiaries.  这家公司发布了其子公司的未合并财务报表。
The nonconsolidated subsidiaries operate independently.  这些未合并的子公司独立运营。
近义词:unconsolidated, separate, independent
反义词:consolidated, combined, merged
词性:adjective
