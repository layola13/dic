fade-out:/ˈfeɪd aʊt/| n.|淡出，渐隐|A gradual decrease in the visibility or audibility of something, especially a scene in a film or a piece of music.|The movie ended with a slow fade-out to black. 电影以缓慢的淡出到黑色结束。|近义词：fade away, die away|反义词：fade-in|词性：名词

fade-out:/ˈfeɪd aʊt/| v.|（使）淡出，（使）渐隐|To cause something to gradually decrease in visibility or audibility.|The director faded out the music as the scene ended. 导演在场景结束时逐渐减弱了音乐。|近义词：fade away, die away|反义词：fade-in|词性：动词

词源：  "Fade" comes from the Old French word "fader," meaning "to wither" or "to grow weak."  The addition of "out" emphasizes the gradual disappearance. “Fade”来源于古法语单词“fader”，意思是“枯萎”或“变弱”。添加“out”强调逐渐消失。
