The intreatable problem plagued the company for months.  这个棘手的问题困扰了公司好几个月。| 近义词: incurable, untreatable, irremediable, hopeless | 反义词: treatable, curable, manageable, solvable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  in- (not) + treatable (capable of being treated)
