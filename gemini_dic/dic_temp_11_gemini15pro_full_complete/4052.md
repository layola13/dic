aidful:/ˈeɪdfəl/| adj.|有帮助的；有益的|Providing help or assistance; useful.|例句：Her advice was very aidful. 她的建议很有帮助。|近义词：helpful, assisting, supportive, useful|反义词：unhelpful, useless, hindering|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "aid" + "-ful." "Aid" comes from Old French "aider" (to help), ultimately from Latin "adjutare" (to help). "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
