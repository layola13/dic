marsipobranch:/ˌmɑːrsɪpəˈbræŋk/ | n. |圆口纲动物|A member of the superclass Agnatha, characterized by a jawless, sucking mouth and a cartilaginous skeleton. These are primitive fish-like vertebrates, including lampreys and hagfish.|Lampreys and hagfish are examples of marsipobranchs. 七鳃鳗和盲鳗是圆口纲动物的例子。|近义词: agnathan, cyclostome |反义词: gnathostome|词性:noun

Etymology: From  Latin *marsupium* "pouch" + Greek *bránchia* "gills"
