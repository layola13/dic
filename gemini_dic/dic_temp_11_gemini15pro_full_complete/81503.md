gravestone: /ˈɡreɪvˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈɡreɪvˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |墓碑|A stone that marks a grave. |例句：The gravestone was weathered and worn. 墓碑已经风化磨损了。

例句：She laid flowers on her mother's gravestone. 她把花放在了她母亲的墓碑上。


近义词：tombstone, headstone|反义词: None|词性: Noun
