centistere: /ˈsɛntɪˌstɪər/ | n. |厘立方米；百分之一立方米| A unit of volume equal to one hundredth of a stere or 0.01 cubic meters. |Example: The volume of the small box is measured in centisteres.  小盒子的体积是用厘立方米测量的。

|Example:  A centistere is a relatively small unit of volume. 一厘立方米是一个相对较小的体积单位。|近义词: cubic centimeter (cm³)|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement.  Perhaps kilostere or cubic meter for a much larger unit) |词性: noun
