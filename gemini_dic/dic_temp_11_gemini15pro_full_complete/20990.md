blenk:/blæŋk/ | adj. n. v. |空白的；空虚的；茫然的；空白；空格；空白表格|A blank space, surface, or sheet of paper is empty and has nothing written or printed on it.  A blank expression shows no feeling, understanding, or interest.|

例句：
My mind went completely blank.
我的脑子一片空白。

He handed me a blank sheet of paper.
他递给我一张白纸。


近义词：empty, vacant, void, expressionless, unfilled, bare
反义词：full, complete, filled, expressive

词性:
adjective, noun, verb
