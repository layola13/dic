luwak:/ˈluːwak/ | n. | 麝香猫; 麝香猫咖啡 | A nocturnal, cat-like mammal native to Southeast Asia, also known as the Asian palm civet. Also refers to the coffee that includes part-digested coffee cherries eaten by the animal. |  The luwak is known for its role in producing Kopi Luwak coffee. 麝香猫以其在生产猫屎咖啡中的作用而闻名。

The luwak is a shy, nocturnal creature. 麝香猫是一种害羞的夜行动物。 | 近义词: civet, palm civet | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | 词性: noun
