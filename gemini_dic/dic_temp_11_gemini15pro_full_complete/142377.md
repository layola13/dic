orthopyroxene:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈpaɪrəˌksiːn/ /ˌɔːθəʊˈpaɪrəksiːn/
orthopyroxene|n.|斜方辉石|A group of rock-forming inosilicate minerals of the general formula (Mg,Fe)2Si2O6.  They crystallize in the orthorhombic system.|Orthopyroxene is a common mineral in mafic and ultramafic igneous rocks.|近义词:enstatite, hypersthene|反义词:None|noun
