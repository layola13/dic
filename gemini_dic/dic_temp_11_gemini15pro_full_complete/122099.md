mistiming:/mɪsˈtaɪmɪŋ/
n.
错误的时机;不合时宜
an act of choosing the wrong moment for something
The mistiming of the announcement could cost the company millions.  公告发布的时机不当可能会让公司损失数百万美元。

近义词:bad timing, poor timing, wrong timing
反义词:good timing, perfect timing
词性:名词
