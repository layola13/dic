merchantableness:/ˈmɜːrtʃəntəbəlnəs/
n.
可销售性；适销性
The quality of being fit for sale; the state of being easily sold.
This new type of apple has increased merchantableness due to its sweeter taste and longer shelf life. 这种新品种苹果由于味道更甜、保质期更长，增加了适销性。
Synonym: marketability, salability
Antonym: unmarketability, unsalability
