leatherbound: /ˈleðə(r)baʊnd/
adjective
皮革装订的；精装的
Bound in leather; having a leather binding.
She treasured her leatherbound first edition of Jane Austen's "Pride and Prejudice". 她珍藏着她那本皮革装订的《傲慢与偏见》第一版。
My grandfather left me his leatherbound journals. 我的祖父留给了我他的皮革装订的日记本。
Synonyms: leather-covered, hardback, hardcover
Antonyms: paperback, softcover, softbound
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From leather + bound (past participle of bind).  "Leather" comes from Old English "leþer,"  from Proto-Germanic "*leþrą."  "Bind" comes from Old English "bindan," from Proto-Germanic "*bindaną."
