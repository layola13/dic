cape:/keɪp/| n.|斗篷；披肩；海角|A piece of clothing worn around the shoulders, typically longer than a shawl, or a piece of land jutting into the sea or other large body of water.|She wore a long, flowing cape. 她穿着一件飘逸的长斗篷。|The ship rounded the cape. 船绕过了海角。|近义词：cloak, shawl, promontory, headland|反义词:bay, gulf|noun
