boxiest:/ˈbɒksiɪst/
adjective
最像盒子的;最四四方方的
most like a box in shape; most square or cubic
That's the boxiest car I've ever seen. 那是迄今为止我见过的最像盒子的车。
The building was boxiest and least attractive of all the designs. 这栋楼是所有设计中最四四方方、最不吸引人的。
近义词: squarest, cuboid, blocky
反义词: rounded, curved
词性: adjective
