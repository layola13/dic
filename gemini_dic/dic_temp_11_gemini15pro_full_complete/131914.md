noncontinence:/ˌnɒnkɒntɪnəns/ | n. | 失禁，不能自制 | Inability to control excretory functions, especially urination or defecation. |  She experienced noncontinence after the surgery.  她手术后出现了失禁。| synonyms: incontinence, inability to hold urine/feces | antonyms: continence, control | noun


noncontinences:/ˌnɒnkɒntɪnənsiz/ | n. |  失禁（复数形式，较少使用）|  Multiple instances or types of incontinence. | The doctor discussed various noncontinences with the patient. 医生和病人讨论了各种失禁情况。 | synonyms: incontinences | antonyms: continences | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + continence (self-restraint, ability to control bodily functions). Continence comes from Latin continentia, from continens, present participle of continēre (to hold together, contain), from con- (together) + tenēre (to hold).
