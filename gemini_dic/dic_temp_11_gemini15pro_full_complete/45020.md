cta:/ˈsiː tiː ˈeɪ/ | n. |  行动号召; 行动呼吁 | Call to action. A piece of content intended to induce a viewer, reader, or listener to perform a specific act, typically taking the form of an instruction or directive (e.g. buy now, learn more). |  The website's cta button is bright red and says "Sign Up Now".  网站的行动号召按钮是亮红色的，上面写着“立即注册”。 | synonyms: prompt, invitation, appeal | antonyms: discouragement, deterrent | noun
