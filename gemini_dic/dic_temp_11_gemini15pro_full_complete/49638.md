demilitarise:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| v. |使非军事化；解除武装|To remove military forces or weapons from a place; to prohibit military activity in a place.|例句：The treaty requires both countries to demilitarise the border zone.  该条约要求两国对边界地区进行非军事化。

例句：They are campaigning to demilitarise the entire region. 他们正在开展运动，争取使整个地区非军事化。|近义词：disarm, neutralise|反义词:militarise, arm|词性:动词

demilitarisation:/diːˌmɪlɪtərəˈzeɪʃən/| n. |非军事化；解除武装|The act or process of demilitarising a place or thing.|例句：The demilitarisation of the zone was a key part of the peace agreement. 该区域的非军事化是和平协议的关键部分。

例句：The government has begun the demilitarisation of the former military base. 政府已经开始对以前的军事基地进行非军事化。|近义词：disarmament, neutralisation|反义词:militarisation, armament|词性:名词
