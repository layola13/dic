maunderers:/ˈmɔːndərərz/ (US) /ˈmɔːndərəz/ (UK)| n. | 漫步者；唠叨者 | People who maunder, often complaining or talking in a rambling way. | The maunderers complained about the heat, the dust, and the length of the journey.  漫步者抱怨天气炎热、尘土飞扬，以及旅程的漫长。| synonyms: ramblers, drivelers, wanderers, dawdlers | antonyms: hustlers, go-getters | noun

maunder:/ˈmɔːndər/ (US) /ˈmɔːndə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 漫步；唠叨 | To talk in a rambling or incoherent way, often complaining. | He maundered on about his childhood for hours. 他絮絮叨叨地讲了他几个小时的童年往事。| synonyms: ramble, drivel, wander, dawdle | antonyms: hustle, focus | verb
