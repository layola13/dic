greyhounds:/ˈɡreɪhaʊndz/
n.
灰狗（复数）
A breed of tall, slender dog, noted for its great speed and keen sight, often used for racing.
Greyhounds are known for their incredible speed. 灰狗以其惊人的速度而闻名。
Two greyhounds chased the mechanical rabbit around the track. 两只灰狗追逐着赛道上的机械兔。
近义词:whippets, lurchers
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑体型小的狗 chihuahua, pug)
词性:noun
