cagit:/ˈkædʒɪt/| n.|笼子；监狱；电梯|A cage-like enclosure, often used for confining animals or as a lift in a mine.  It can also metaphorically refer to a restrictive or confining situation.|例句：The miners descended in the cagit. 矿工们乘着矿井里的升降机下去。

例句：He felt trapped in a cagit of his own making. 他感觉被困在自己制造的牢笼里。|近义词：cage, lift, elevator, prison|反义词:freedom, liberty|词性:名词
