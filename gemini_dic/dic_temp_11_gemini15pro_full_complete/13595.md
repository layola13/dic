attenuation:/əˌtɛnjuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 衰减；减弱；稀释 | the reduction of the force, effect, or value of something | The attenuation of the signal was due to the long cable. 信号由于电缆过长而衰减。 | synonyms: weakening, reduction, lessening, diminution, decrease | antonyms: amplification, strengthening, intensification | noun

attenuate:/əˈtɛnjuˌeɪt/ | v. | 衰减；减弱；使稀薄 | to reduce the force, effect, or value of something | The thick walls attenuated the noise from the street. 厚厚的墙壁减弱了街道上的噪音。| synonyms: weaken, reduce, lessen, diminish, decrease | antonyms: amplify, strengthen, intensify | verb
