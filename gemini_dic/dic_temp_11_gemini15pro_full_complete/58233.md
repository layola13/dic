druidess: /ˈdruːɪdɪs/ | n. | 女祭司，女德鲁伊 | A female druid, a priestess of the ancient Celtic religion. |  例句：The druidess performed the ritual under the oak tree. 女祭司在橡树下举行了仪式。

例句：Legends tell of powerful druidesses who could heal the sick and command the elements. 传说中，强大的女祭司可以治愈病人，并掌控元素。| 近义词：priestess, sorceress, witch | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps layman/laywoman for non-religious counterpart) | 词性: noun
