countermine:/ˈkaʊntərmaɪn/ | v. n. | 反击，对抗；坑道爆破 | To thwart or defeat by secret or indirect means; a mine or tunnel dug to intercept or destroy an enemy's mine. | The general countermined the enemy's attack by sending troops around the flank. 将军通过派兵绕过侧翼来反击敌人的进攻。 | Synonyms: thwart, frustrate, foil, obstruct, undermine | Antonyms: support, aid, assist | verb

countermine:/ˈkaʊntərmaɪn/ | n. | 反击手段；对抗措施；坑道 | A means of counteracting or opposing something; a mine or tunnel dug to intercept or destroy an enemy's mine. |  The spy's countermine was to leak false information to the enemy. 间谍的反击手段是向敌人泄露虚假信息。| Synonyms: countermeasure, counterplot, counteractant | Antonyms:  support, aid | noun
