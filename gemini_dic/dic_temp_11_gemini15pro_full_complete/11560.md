aristocratism:/əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪzəm/
n.
贵族主义，贵族统治；贵族作风，贵族气派
The belief in or advocacy of aristocracy or government by an elite or privileged upper class.  A system of government in which power is held by the nobility.  Behavior or attitudes characteristic of the aristocracy.
He exhibited a certain aristocratism in his mannerisms and speech.  他的言谈举止流露出某种贵族气派。
Synonyms:  elitism, snobbery, aristocracy, nobleism
Antonyms:  egalitarianism, democracy, populism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aristocratisme*, from *aristocrate* (aristocrat) + *-isme* (-ism).  Ultimately from Greek *aristokratía* (rule of the best).
