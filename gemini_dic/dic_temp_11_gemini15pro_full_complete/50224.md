depeculation:/dɪˌpekjʊˈleɪʃən/
n.
盗用公款; 贪污; 挪用
The fraudulent appropriation of funds or property entrusted to one's care.
The investigation revealed widespread depeculation of public funds.  调查揭露了公共资金普遍被盗用的情况。

The mayor was accused of depeculation and abuse of power. 市长被指控挪用公款和滥用职权。

Synonyms: embezzlement, misappropriation, defalcation, peculation
Antonyms:  donation, contribution, restitution

词性: noun
