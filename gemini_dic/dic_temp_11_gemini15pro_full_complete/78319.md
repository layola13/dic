gerontologist:/ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌdʒɛrənˈtɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |老年学家；老年医学专家|A specialist who studies aging and the problems of the elderly.|例句：The gerontologist recommended regular exercise for the elderly patients. 老年学家建议老年患者定期锻炼。

例句：My grandmother consults a gerontologist for advice on maintaining her health. 我的祖母咨询老年学家，以获取有关保持健康的建议。

近义词：geriatrician|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
