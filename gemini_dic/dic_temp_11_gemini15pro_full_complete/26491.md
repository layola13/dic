cabalism:/ˈkæbəlɪzəm/| n.|秘传主义，神秘学，阴谋|A system of esoteric beliefs and practices, often associated with Jewish mysticism but also used more generally to refer to any occult doctrine or secret intrigue.|The group's cabalism included strange rituals and secretive meetings.  这个团体的秘传主义包括奇怪的仪式和秘密会议。|近义词：occultism, mysticism, esotericism, intrigue, conspiracy|反义词：exotericism, openness, transparency|noun

cabalistic:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|秘传的，神秘的，阴谋的|Relating to or characteristic of cabalistic doctrines or practices; secret and intriguing.|The symbols found on the walls seemed cabalistic and mysterious. 在墙上发现的符号看起来神秘而诡秘。|近义词：occult, mystical, esoteric, secretive, cryptic|反义词：exoteric, open, transparent|adjective
