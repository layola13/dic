flannelettes:/ˌflænəˈlets/ | n. | 法兰绒布（尤指棉质的）| A soft, slightly napped cotton fabric, typically used for nightclothes or sheets.  A type of soft cotton cloth with a fuzzy surface, often used for making clothes like pajamas. |  She snuggled into the warm flannelettes. 她依偎在温暖的法兰绒布里。|  The baby's pyjamas were made of soft flannelettes. 婴儿的睡衣是用柔软的法兰绒布做的。 | 近义词: flannel, cotton | 反义词: silk, satin | 词性: 名词

词源:  From flannel + -ette (a diminutive suffix).  源自 flannel（法兰绒） + -ette（表示小的后缀）。
