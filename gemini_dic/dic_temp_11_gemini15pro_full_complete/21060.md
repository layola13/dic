blindfolder:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊldər/| n.|眼罩；蒙眼布|A piece of cloth used to cover someone's eyes so they cannot see.|The magician tied a blindfolder around the volunteer's head.|Near synonyms: blindfold, eye mask, blinder|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "revealing" in a metaphorical sense)|noun

blindfold:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊld/| vt.|蒙住…的眼睛；使…看不见|To cover someone's eyes with a blindfold.|She was blindfolded and led into the room.|Near synonyms: cover the eyes, mask the eyes|Antonym: uncover the eyes|verb

blindfold:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊld/| adj.|被蒙住眼睛的|Having one's eyes covered with a blindfold.|The blindfolded hostage was led away.|Near synonyms: sightless, unseeing (in this context)|Antonym: seeing|adjective
