institutive:/ˈɪnstɪtjuːtɪv/
adjective
设立的，制定性的，制度性的
having the power to establish or create something; relating to the act of establishing or creating something
例句：The institutive act established the new university.  设立法案建立了这所新的大学。

例句：The government played an institutive role in the creation of the national park. 政府在建立国家公园方面发挥了设立性作用。
近义词：founding, establishing, constitutive, formative
反义词：destructive, abolishing
词性：adjective
