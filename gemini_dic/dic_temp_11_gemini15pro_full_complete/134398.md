nonintrovertedness:/ˌnɒnɪntrəˈvɜːtɪdnəs/ | n. | 外向性，不内向 | The state or quality of not being introverted; extroversion. |  Her nonintrovertedness made her a natural leader. 她的外向性使她成为天生的领导者。|  He showed his nonintrovertedness by initiating conversations with strangers.  他通过主动与陌生人交谈来展现他的外向性。 | 近义词: extroversion, sociability | 反义词: introversion, shyness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + introverted (adjective meaning "turned inward" or "shy") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin introvertere ("to turn inward").
