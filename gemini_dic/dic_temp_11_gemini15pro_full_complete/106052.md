knee-jointed:/ˈniːˌdʒɔɪntɪd/
adjective
膝关节连接的；有膝关节的
Having joints like knees; articulated.
The robot had knee-jointed legs, allowing it to climb stairs.  这个机器人有类似膝盖的关节腿，可以爬楼梯。
knee-jointed legs allowed the robot to navigate uneven terrain.  膝关节连接的腿使机器人能够在不平坦的地形上航行。
Near synonyms: articulated, jointed
Antonyms: rigid, inflexible
词性: 形容词
