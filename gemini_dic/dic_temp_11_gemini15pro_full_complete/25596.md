bumming around:/ˈbʌmɪŋ əˈraʊnd/
|adjective (informal)|懒散的，无所事事的|Describing someone who is spending their time idly. |He has a rather bumming-around lifestyle. 他过着一种相当懒散的生活方式。|lazy, idle, shiftless, indolent|industrious, diligent, hardworking|adjective
