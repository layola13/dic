meditatively:/ˈmedɪtətɪvli/ | adv. | 沉思地，冥想地 | in a meditative manner; thoughtfully and reflectively | He sat meditatively by the fire, pondering the events of the day. 他沉思地坐在火炉旁，思考着当天发生的事情。 | She gazed meditatively out the window. 她若有所思地凝视着窗外。| 近义词: thoughtfully, pensively, contemplatively | 反义词:  rashly, impulsively | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *meditativus, from Latin meditatus (past participle of meditari “to meditate”) + -ivus.
