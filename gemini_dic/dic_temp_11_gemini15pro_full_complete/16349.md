barbary:/ˈbɑːrbəri/| n. |巴巴里；巴巴里海岸；北非|A region of North Africa, historically comprising the coastal regions from Egypt to the Atlantic Ocean.|The Barbary pirates operated in the Mediterranean Sea for centuries. 巴巴里海盗在地中海活动了几个世纪。|Synonyms: North Africa, Maghreb|Antonyms: None|noun


barbary:/ˈbɑːrbəri/| adj. |巴巴里的；北非的|Relating to or characteristic of the Barbary Coast or its inhabitants.|Barbary horses are known for their endurance and agility. 巴巴里马以其耐力和敏捷性而闻名。|Synonyms: North African, Maghrebi|Antonyms: None|adjective
