castor /ˈkæstər/ (US) /ˈkɑːstər/ (UK)| n.|蓖麻; 蓖麻油; 蓖麻籽;  Castor(双子座α星); (某些动物的)香腺;|A tropical plant cultivated for its seeds which yield an oil used as a lubricant, in medicine, etc. Also refers to this oil, or to a small wheel attached to the bottom of furniture legs.|The castor oil plant is native to Africa. 蓖麻原产于非洲。|synonyms: castor bean, castor-oil plant|antonyms: N/A|noun

castor /ˈkæstər/ (US) /ˈkɑːstər/ (UK)| n.|脚轮; 万向轮;|A small wheel attached to the bottom of furniture legs to make it easy to move.|The table has castors so it can be easily moved. 这张桌子装有脚轮，可以轻松移动。|synonyms: wheel, roller, caster|antonyms: N/A|noun
