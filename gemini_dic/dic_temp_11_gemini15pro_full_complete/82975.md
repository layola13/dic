gunmanship:/ˈɡʌnmənʃɪp/
n.
枪法; 射击技术
skill in shooting, especially with a handgun
His gunmanship was legendary in the Wild West. 他在西部荒野的枪法堪称传奇。

近义词:marksmanship, shooting skill, accuracy
反义词:inaccuracy, clumsiness

词性:noun

词源:gunman (持枪歹徒，枪手) + -ship (构成名词，表示技巧，技艺，状态)
