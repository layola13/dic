outvie:/ˌaʊtˈvaɪ/
verb
超越，胜过
to surpass or defeat someone in competition or rivalry
He outvied his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中胜过对手。
近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform, eclipse
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：动词
