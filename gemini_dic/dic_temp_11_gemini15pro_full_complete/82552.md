guanine:/ˈɡwɑːniːn/
n.
鸟嘌呤
A purine base found in DNA and RNA.  It pairs with cytosine.
Guanine is one of the four main nucleobases found in the nucleic acids DNA and RNA. 鸟嘌呤是DNA和RNA核酸中发现的四个主要核碱基之一。

Guanine forms base pairs with cytosine. 鸟嘌呤与胞嘧啶形成碱基对。


近义词: (没有直接的近义词，可以列出其他三种碱基) adenine, cytosine, thymine
反义词: (无)
词性: noun
