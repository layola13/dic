over-expresses:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛsɪz/ | v. | 过度表达，过量表达 | To express (a gene or protein) at a higher level than normal.  It signifies increased production of a gene product, such as RNA or protein, compared to typical levels. | The cancerous cells over-express a particular protein. 癌细胞过度表达一种特定的蛋白质。 | The gene over-expresses in response to stress. 该基因在应激反应中过度表达。 | Synonyms: upregulate, overproduce | Antonyms: downregulate, underexpress | verb
