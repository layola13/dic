immigrated: /ˈɪmɪɡreɪtɪd/
verb
移民, 移居
past tense of immigrate, meaning to come to live permanently in a foreign country.
My grandparents immigrated to the United States from Italy in 1920. 我的祖父母于1920年从意大利移民到美国。
He immigrated to Canada seeking better job opportunities. 他为了寻找更好的工作机会移民到了加拿大。
Synonyms: migrated, relocated, resettled
Antonyms: emigrated, repatriated
词性: verb
