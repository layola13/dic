aerodromes:/ˈɛərəˌdroʊmz/ /ˈɛərəˌdrəʊmz/| n. |飞机场；机场|A place where aircraft regularly take off and land, with facilities for handling passengers and cargo.  It's a more formal or British English term for "airport".|The pilot landed the plane safely at the aerodrome. 飞行员安全地将飞机降落在机场。|Synonym: airport, airfield|Antonym: None|Noun
