gibbous:/ˈɡɪbəs/| adj.|凸圆的，隆起的（尤指月亮等天体）|Swelling out on one side; convex.  Specifically used to describe the moon when it appears more than half full but less than full.|例句：The gibbous moon shone brightly in the night sky. 凸圆的月亮在夜空中闪耀。|例句：The photograph captured the gibbous phase of the moon perfectly. 这张照片完美地捕捉到了月亮的凸圆阶段。|近义词：convex, bulging, rounded|反义词：concave, crescent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gibbosus* "humpbacked," from *gibbus* "hump, protuberance."
