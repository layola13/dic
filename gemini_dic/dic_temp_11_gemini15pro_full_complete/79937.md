gnomonically:/nɒˈmɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 日晷地，日晷式地，根据日晷 |  Relating to or measured by a gnomon (the part of a sundial that casts the shadow).  It can also be used figuratively to describe something that reveals information indirectly or incrementally, like the movement of a shadow on a sundial. | The sundial gnomonically marked the passage of time. 日晷以日晷的方式标记时间的流逝。 | The author gnomonically hinted at the true nature of the villain. 作者间接地暗示了反派的真实本性。|  indirectly, incrementally, obliquely | directly, explicitly | adverb
