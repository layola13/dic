KPIs:/ˌkeɪ piːˈaɪz/| n.|关键绩效指标|Key Performance Indicators.  Metrics used to track and measure progress towards achieving a specific goal or objective. |We need to identify the KPIs that are most relevant to our business objectives. 我们需要确定与我们的业务目标最相关的关键绩效指标。|Synonyms: metrics, measures, benchmarks|Antonyms: N/A|noun
