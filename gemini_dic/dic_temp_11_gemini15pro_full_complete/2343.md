adhesiveness:/ədˈhiːsɪvnəs/
noun
粘附性，粘着性
The quality of being adhesive; stickiness.
The adhesiveness of the glue made it difficult to remove. 胶水的粘性使它难以去除。
stickiness, tackiness, gumminess
slipperiness, smoothness
n.
