extradition:/ˌɛkstrəˈdɪʃən/ | n. | 引渡 | The act of handing over a person accused or convicted of a crime to the jurisdiction of another country or state where the crime was committed. | The government is seeking the extradition of the suspected terrorist. 政府正在寻求引渡这名恐怖嫌疑人。| synonyms: surrender, deportation, rendition | antonyms: asylum, refuge | noun

extradite:/ˈɛkstrəˌdaɪt/ | vt. | 引渡 | To hand over (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another country or state. | The suspect was extradited to the United States to face trial.  嫌疑犯被引渡到美国受审。| synonyms: deport, hand over, surrender | antonyms: harbor, protect | verb
