foxily:/ˈfɒksɪli/ (British), /ˈfɑːksɪli/ (American)| adv.|狡猾地，诡诈地|In a cunning or sly manner, resembling the characteristics of a fox.|She smiled foxily, knowing she had outwitted him. 她狡黠地一笑，知道自己已经智胜了他。|The lawyer foxily maneuvered his client out of the charges. 律师狡猾地使他的客户摆脱了指控。|近义词：craftily, slyly, cunningly|反义词：honestly, openly, frankly|词性:副词

词源:
From fox + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs from adjectives.  "Fox" comes from Old English "fox," which in turn comes from Proto-Germanic "*fuhsaz."
