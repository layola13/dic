greve:/ɡrev/ (primarily British English) /ɡrɛv/ (American English, less common)| n.|罢工; 停工;  |A concerted cessation of work by employees as a protest against working conditions, low pay, etc.|例句：The miners' greve lasted for several months. 矿工的罢工持续了几个月。

例句：The transport workers voted to hold a greve. 交通运输工人投票决定举行罢工。|近义词：strike, walkout, stoppage, industrial action|反义词:work, operation|词性:noun
