intendant:/ɪnˈtɛndənt/| n.|监督官，主管，负责人|An official appointed to oversee a district or province, especially in France or one of its colonies. |例句：The intendant was responsible for collecting taxes and enforcing the law.  监督官负责收税和执法。

例句：The king appointed a new intendant for the northern provinces. 国王为北部省份任命了一位新的监督官。|近义词：administrator, superintendent, overseer, supervisor, manager, director, governor, commissioner|反义词: subordinate, employee|词性:noun
