dramatically:/drəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 戏剧性地，显著地 | In a dramatic or theatrical manner; to a striking extent. | The landscape changed dramatically after the flood.  洪水过后，地貌发生了巨大的变化。

The price of oil has risen dramatically this year. 今年油价剧烈上涨。

近义词:sharply, significantly, considerably, greatly, drastically | 反义词: slightly, gradually, subtly, imperceptibly | 词性:副词
