demilune:/ˌdɛmiˈluːn/ /ˌdɛmaɪˈluːn/| n. |半月形，半月形物，新月形面包|A crescent-shaped figure or object, especially a pastry.|The chef prepared a delicious demilune of flaky pastry filled with cream cheese.  厨师准备了一个美味的半月形酥皮糕点，里面填满了奶油奶酪。|Synonyms: crescent, half-moon|Antonyms: full moon|Noun

demilune:/ˌdɛmiˈluːn/ /ˌdɛmaɪˈluːn/| adj. |半月形的|Shaped like a half-moon or crescent.|The demilune window offered a picturesque view of the garden. 半月形的窗户提供了花园如画的景色。|Synonyms: crescent, lunate|Antonyms: circular, round|Adjective
