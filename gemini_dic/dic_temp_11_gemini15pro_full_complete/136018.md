nonrealism:/nɒnˈriːəlɪzəm/ /nɑːnˈriːəlɪzəm/| n.|非现实主义，非写实主义|A genre or style, especially in literature, art, or film, that does not represent things as they are in real life but presents imagined worlds, characters, and events, often symbolic or metaphorical in nature.|The play was a bold experiment in nonrealism, with fantastical creatures and dreamlike sequences.|近义词：fantasy, surrealism, idealism, romanticism|反义词：realism, naturalism|词性:名词

词源: non- + realism.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "realism" refers to the accurate depiction of reality.
