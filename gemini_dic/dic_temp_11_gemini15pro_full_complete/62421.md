endomorph:/ˈɛndəˌmɔːrf/| n.|内胚层体型者，矮胖型体质者|A person with a soft, round body build and a tendency to gain weight easily.  This body type is characterized by a predominance of fat tissue.|Example:  Endomorphs often excel at strength-based sports. 内胚层体型的人通常擅长力量型运动。

Example:  He recognized himself as an endomorph and adjusted his diet accordingly. 他意识到自己是内胚层体型，并相应地调整了饮食。|近义词:mesomorph (partially)|反义词:ectomorph|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *éndon* "within" + *morphḗ* "form"
