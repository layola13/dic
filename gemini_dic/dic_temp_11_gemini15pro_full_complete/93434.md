illuminati:/ɪˌluːmɪˈnɑːti/ | n. | 光明会；先觉者 |  persons claiming special intellectual or spiritual enlightenment.  A name that has been given to various groups, both real and fictitious. Historically, the name usually refers to the Bavarian Illuminati, an Enlightenment-era secret society founded on May 1, 1776. |  Many conspiracy theories center around the supposed influence of the Illuminati. 很多阴谋论都围绕着所谓的光明会的影响。 | synonyms: enlightened, cognoscenti, initiates | antonyms: ignorant, unenlightened | noun
