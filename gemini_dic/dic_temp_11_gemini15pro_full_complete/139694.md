oceanians:/ˌoʊʃiˈeɪniənz/
noun
大洋洲人
People from Oceania, a region centered on the islands of the tropical Pacific Ocean.
The Oceanians have a rich cultural heritage.  大洋洲人民有着丰富的文化遗产。
Islanders from Fiji are considered Oceanians.  来自斐济的岛民被认为是大洋洲人。
近义词:Pacific Islanders
反义词: (No direct antonym, could list people from other continents, e.g., Europeans, Asians)
词性:n. (noun)
