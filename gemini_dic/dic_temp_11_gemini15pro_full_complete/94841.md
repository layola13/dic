impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/ | vt. | 归咎于，归罪于；认为…是…的起因 | to attribute or ascribe (something dishonest or dishonorable, such as a criminal offense or a base motive) to a person | The police impute the accident to the driver's negligence. 警方将事故归咎于司机的疏忽。 | synonyms: ascribe, attribute, assign, credit, charge | antonyms: exculpate, absolve, exonerate | verb

impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/ | vt. |  估算，推算（缺失值）| To calculate or estimate the value of something, especially when the value is not readily apparent or is missing from the data. | Statisticians can impute missing values in a dataset using various methods. 统计学家可以使用各种方法估算数据集中缺失的值。| synonyms: estimate, calculate, assess, appraise, infer | antonyms: N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imputare "to reckon, charge, ascribe," from Latin in- "in" + putare "to think, to reckon."
