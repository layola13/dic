equitable:/ˈekwɪtəbl/| adj.|公平的，公正的|Characterized by equity or fairness; just and impartial.|例句：An equitable distribution of resources is essential for social harmony. 资源的公平分配对于社会和谐至关重要。|例句：The judge made an equitable decision in the case. 法官在这个案件中做出了公正的判决。|近义词：fair, just, impartial, unbiased|反义词：unfair, unjust, biased, inequitable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *equitable, from Old French equitable, from Latin aequitābilis (“just, fair”), from aequitās (“equality, fairness”), from aequus (“equal, level, just, calm”).
