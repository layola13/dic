epigastrium:/ˌepɪˈɡæstriəm/| n. |上腹部，心窝|The epigastrium is the upper central region of the abdomen. |He felt a sharp pain in his epigastrium. 他感到上腹部一阵剧痛。|Synonyms: upper abdomen, pit of the stomach|Antonyms: hypogastrium|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐπί (epí, “upon, on”) + γαστήρ (gastḗr, “belly, stomach”).
