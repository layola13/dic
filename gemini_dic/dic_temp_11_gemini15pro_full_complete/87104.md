heraldists:/ˈherəldɪsts/
n.
纹章学家，纹章研究者
Experts in heraldry, the study of coats of arms and other heraldic symbols.
The heraldists authenticated the family's crest.  纹章学家鉴定证实了这个家族的族徽。
The heraldists debated the origins of the symbol. 纹章学家们对该标志的起源进行了辩论。
Synonyms: armorists, genealogists
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "amateur historians" in some contexts)
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From herald + -ist.  Herald comes from Old French herault, ultimately from Frankish *hariwald, "army commander".
