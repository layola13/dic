idea:/aɪˈdɪə/ | n. |理想；典范|an excellent or perfect example of something.| That's my idea of a perfect vacation. 那是我理想中的完美假期。| synonym: ideal, exemplar, paradigm | antonym: imperfection, flaw | noun
