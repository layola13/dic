empowers:/ɪmˈpaʊərz/| v.|授权，使能够，赋予权力|To give someone the authority or power to do something; to make someone stronger and more confident, especially in controlling their life and claiming their rights.|Education empowers people to make informed decisions. 教育使人们能够做出明智的决定. The movement actively empowers women and minorities. 这项运动积极赋予妇女和少数族裔权力。|近义词：authorize, enable, strengthen, facilitate|反义词: restrict, weaken, disempower|词性:动词

Etymology:
From en- (“in, into”) +‎ power, ultimately from Late Latin potere (“to be able”)
