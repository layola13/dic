economizes:/iˈkɒnəmaɪzɪz/
verb
节省，节约
spends less; reduces expenses.
She economizes by making her own clothes. 她通过自己做衣服来节省开支。
The company is economizing on staff costs. 公司正在削减员工成本。
save, cut back, reduce, conserve
spend, waste, squander
v.
