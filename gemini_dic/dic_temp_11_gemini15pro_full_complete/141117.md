ontological:/ˌɒntəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɑːntəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
本体论的
Relating to the branch of metaphysics dealing with the nature of being.
例句：The book delves into complex ontological questions about the nature of reality. 这本书深入探讨了关于现实本质的复杂本体论问题。

例句：His ontological argument for the existence of God was based on the idea of a perfect being. 他的关于上帝存在的本体论论证是基于完美存在的概念。

近义词：metaphysical, philosophical, existential
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "empirical" as focusing on observable reality rather than abstract being)
词性: adjective
