gingerness:/ˈdʒɪndʒərnəs/
n.
谨慎,小心,胆小
The quality or state of being ginger; cautiousness or timidity.
He approached the task with a degree of gingerness, unsure of the outcome.  他小心翼翼地处理这项任务，不确定结果会如何。
She showed gingerness in expressing her opinion, fearing it might offend someone. 她小心翼翼地表达自己的观点，担心可能会冒犯别人。
近义词:caution, wariness, timidity, hesitancy
反义词:boldness, recklessness, audacity
词性:名词
