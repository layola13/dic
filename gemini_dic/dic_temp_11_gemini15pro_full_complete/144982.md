overacidities:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɪdətiz/
n.
过酸；酸过多
Excessive acidity, typically in the stomach.
The patient complained of overacidities and heartburn. 病人抱怨胃酸过多和烧心。
近义词: hyperacidities
反义词: achlorhydria (胃酸缺乏)
词性: 名词

词源:
over- (prefix) + acidity (noun)
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"acidity" comes from Late Latin "aciditas," from Latin "acidus" meaning "sour, sharp, tart."
