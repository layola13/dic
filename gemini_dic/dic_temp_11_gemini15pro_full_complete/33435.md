churlish:/ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃ/ (US), /ˈtʃɜːlɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|粗鲁的，无礼的；脾气暴躁的|Rude in a mean-spirited and surly way.  Derived from "churl," meaning a peasant or person of low social standing, reflecting the perceived rudeness of the lower classes by the upper classes.|He responded with a churlish grunt. 他粗暴地咕哝了一声作为回应。|The waiter's churlish manner ruined our dining experience. 服务员粗鲁的态度破坏了我们的用餐体验。|近义词：rude, impolite, surly, boorish, grumpy, ill-mannered|反义词：polite, courteous, gracious, amiable, affable|词性: adjective
