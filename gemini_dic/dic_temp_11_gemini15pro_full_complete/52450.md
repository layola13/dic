diegesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒiːsɪs/ /ˌdaɪəˈdʒisɪs/
n.
叙事，故事世界
The narrative presented by a narrator or implied by the author.  The world of the story.
The film's diegesis is meticulously constructed. 这部电影的叙事世界构建得非常细致。
narrative, story, plot, fabula
non-diegetic


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek διήγησις (diḗgēsis, “narration, narrative”), from διηγέομαι (diēgéomai, “I narrate, recount”), from διά (diá, “through”) + ἡγέομαι (hēgéomai, “I lead, guide”).
