heat-proof:/ˈhiːtˌpruːf/
adjective
防热的，耐热的
Impervious to heat; able to withstand high temperatures without damage.
This pan is heat-proof, so you can put it in the oven.  这个锅是耐热的，所以你可以把它放进烤箱。
The firefighters wear heat-proof clothing. 消防员穿着耐热的衣服。

近义词:fire-resistant, heat-resistant, fireproof
反义词:flammable, combustible
词性:adjective
