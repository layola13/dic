enfeeblers:/ɪnˈfiːblərz/
| n. |使人衰弱的人或物|Something that weakens or makes feeble.|The constant stress and lack of sleep were enfeeblers that gradually eroded her health.  持续的压力和睡眠不足逐渐削弱了她的健康。|Synonyms: weakeners, debilitators|Antonyms: fortifiers, strengtheners|Noun
