collodiotype:/kəˈloʊdiətaɪp/ |n.| 火棉胶照片，珂罗版照片 |A photographic process using a glass plate coated with collodion as a support for light-sensitive silver salts. It was widely used in the mid-19th century.| Example: The museum displayed a collection of exquisite collodiotypes from the 1860s.  博物馆展出了一批1860年代的精美珂罗版照片。 | Example:  Collodiotypes were known for their sharp detail and clarity. 珂罗版照片以其清晰的细节和清晰度而闻名。|Synonym: ambrotype, tintype |Antonym:  digital photograph |noun
