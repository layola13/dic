antisocialities:/ˌæntiˈsoʊʃiˈælɪtiz/| n. |反社会行为（复数）|The plural form of "antisociality," referring to multiple instances or types of behavior that are harmful or disruptive to society or social norms.  It describes a pattern of behaviors characterized by a disregard for others' rights and feelings.|His various antisocialities, including vandalism and petty theft, landed him in juvenile detention.| 近义词： antisocial behaviors,  deviant behaviors,  unsocial conduct | 反义词： prosocial behaviors,  sociable conduct| noun

antisociality:/ˌæntiˌsoʊʃiˈælɪti/| n. |反社会行为|Behavior that is harmful or disruptive to society or social norms.  It describes a pattern of behaviors characterized by a disregard for others' rights and feelings.  While often associated with Antisocial Personality Disorder, antisociality itself is a broader term.|Her antisociality manifested in her constant lying and manipulation of others.| 近义词： antisocial behavior,  deviance,  unsociability | 反义词： prosocial behavior,  sociability| noun
