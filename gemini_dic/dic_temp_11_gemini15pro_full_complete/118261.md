merchandising:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 商品推销，商品销售 | The process of promoting and selling goods, especially in a retail setting.  It involves various strategies to optimize the presentation and sale of products. | Example 1: The company invested heavily in visual merchandising to attract more customers. 这家公司在视觉营销方面投入巨资以吸引更多顾客。

Example 2: Effective merchandising can significantly increase sales. 有效的商品推销可以显著提高销售额。 | Synonyms: marketing, sales promotion, retailing | Antonyms: procurement, purchasing | noun
