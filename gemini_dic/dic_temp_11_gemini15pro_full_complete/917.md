abusivenesses:/əˌbjuːsɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|辱骂; 虐待; 滥用|The repeated abusivenesses finally led to the breakdown of their relationship.  反复的辱骂最终导致他们关系的破裂。|例句：The levels of abusivenesses in online comments are alarming. 网上评论中的辱骂程度令人担忧。|近义词：maltreatment, mistreatment, cruelty, violence, abuse|反义词：kindness, respect, care, support|词性: 名词

词源:  From abusive + -ness + -es (plural).  来源于形容词abusive（辱骂的，虐待的），加上名词后缀-ness构成名词abusiveness（辱骂，虐待），再加-es构成复数形式abusivenesses.
