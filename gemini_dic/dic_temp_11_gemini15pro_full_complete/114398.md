make-belief:/ˈmeɪk bɪˌliːf/
adjective
假装的；虚构的
An activity in which you imagine that something is true, although it is not.
例句：The children were lost in a world of make-belief. 孩子们沉浸在一个虚构的世界里。

例句：They spent hours playing make-belief games. 他们花了几个小时玩假装游戏。

近义词：pretend, imaginary, fictional, fantasy
反义词：real, actual, factual
词性：adjective

make-belief:/ˈmeɪk bɪˌliːf/
noun
假装；虚构
The act of imagining that something is true, although it is not.
例句：It's all just make-belief. 这都只是假装的。

例句：Children often engage in make-belief play. 孩子们经常进行假装游戏。

近义词：pretense, fantasy, fiction
反义词：reality, truth, fact
词性：noun
