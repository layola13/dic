co-attest:/ˌkoʊəˈtest/| v. |共同证明，联证|To attest to something together; to certify or affirm something jointly with another or others.|例句：Two witnesses co-attested the signing of the will. 两名证人共同证明了遗嘱的签署。

例句：The document was co-attested by the CEO and CFO. 该文件由首席执行官和首席财务官共同证明。|近义词：corroborate, verify, confirm, authenticate|反义词：deny, contradict, refute|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + attest (to bear witness).  "Attest" comes from the Latin "attestari," meaning "to bear witness to."
