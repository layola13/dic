multiexhaust:/ˈmʌltiɪɡˈzɔːst/ | adj. | 多排气的 | Having or involving multiple exhaust pipes or outlets.  A system designed to expel gases from multiple sources or in multiple directions.  Often used in contexts relating to engines, vehicles, or industrial processes. |  The multiexhaust system on the sports car provided impressive performance.  这辆跑车上的多排气系统提供了令人印象深刻的性能。 | synonyms: multi-outlet exhaust, multiple exhaust | antonyms: single exhaust | adjective
