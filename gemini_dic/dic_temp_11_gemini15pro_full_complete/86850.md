hemicardia:/ˌhɛmɪˈkɑːrdɪə/ | n. | 半心畸形 | A severe congenital anomaly in which only half a heart is formed. |  The baby was born with hemicardia, a condition incompatible with life.  这个婴儿出生时患有半心畸形，这是一种与生命不相容的疾病。 | Synonym:  Half a heart (descriptive), cardiac anomaly | Antonym:  Normal heart development | noun

hemicardia:/ˌhɛmɪˈkɑːrdɪə/ | n. | 半心畸形 | A congenital malformation where only one side of the heart develops. | The fetus was diagnosed with hemicardia during the ultrasound scan.  胎儿在超声波扫描中被诊断出患有半心畸形。 | Synonym:  Unilateral heart development (descriptive) | Antonym:  Normal heart formation | noun
