antireactions:/ˌænti riˈækʃənz/| n. |逆反应，反作用，对抗反应|An action or actions taken in opposition to another action or set of actions; an opposing reaction.|The government's proposed tax increases sparked antireactions from businesses and citizens. 政府提出的增税引发了企业和公民的反对。|The antireactions to the new drug were mild.  这种新药的副作用很轻微。|Synonyms: counteractions, opposition, resistance|Antonyms: reactions, support, agreement|noun
