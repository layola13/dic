conniving:/kəˈnaɪvɪŋ/
adjective
诡计多端的，阴谋的，暗中算计的
(adj.) Scheming, secretive, and dishonest; given to or involved in conspiring to do something immoral, illegal, or harmful.
She gave him a conniving look. 她给了他一个诡计多端的眼神。
The conniving villain plotted to overthrow the king.  这个阴险的恶棍密谋推翻国王。
近义词:scheming, devious, crafty, sly, underhand, deceitful
反义词:honest, forthright, ingenuous, aboveboard

词性:adjective
