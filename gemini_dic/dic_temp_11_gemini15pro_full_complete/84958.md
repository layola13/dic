haplessnesses:/ˈhæplɪsnəsɪz/| n. |不幸；厄运|The state of being consistently unlucky or unfortunate.|His life was marked by a series of haplessnesses, one misfortune after another.|近义词：misfortunes, calamities, adversities|反义词:fortunes, blessings, successes|词性:名词

Etymology:
From hapless + -ness.  Hapless itself comes from hap (meaning chance, luck, fortune) + -less (meaning without).  The word "hap" has Old Norse origins.
