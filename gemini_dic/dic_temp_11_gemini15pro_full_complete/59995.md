editorialised: /ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzd/ | v. | 添加评论；发表社论 | to express opinions rather than just report facts, especially in a news report |  The news report was heavily editorialised, making it difficult to discern the objective facts. 这篇新闻报道添加了大量的评论，很难分辨出客观事实。|  The anchor editorialised about the recent political scandal.  主持人就最近的政治丑闻发表了评论。 | synonyms: commented, opined, interpreted, slanted | antonyms: reported, documented, factualized | verb
