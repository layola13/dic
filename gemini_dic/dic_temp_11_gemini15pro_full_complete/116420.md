matali:/mɑːˈtɑːliː/
adjective
醉人的，陶醉的
intoxicated, especially with alcohol; exhilarated
例句：He felt matali after drinking a bottle of wine. 他喝了一瓶酒后感到陶醉。

例句：The matali crowd cheered loudly for the band. 陶醉的人群大声地为乐队欢呼。

近义词：intoxicated, drunk, tipsy, exhilarated, elated
反义词：sober, clear-headed


词性: adjective

词源：From Sanskrit मतवाला (matavālā), meaning "intoxicated," "drunk," or "exhilarated."
