highboys:/ˈhaɪbɔɪz/
noun
高脚柜，高大衣柜
A tall chest of drawers on legs.
The antique highboy in the bedroom was a family heirloom. 卧室里的古董高脚柜是传家宝。
synonyms: chest of drawers, tallboy, bureau
antonyms: lowboy, dresser (though not exact opposites, they represent different furniture styles)
n.
