cross-promotion:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊʃən/ (US) /ˌkrɑːs prəˈmoʊʃən/| n.|交叉推广，联合促销|The act of promoting two or more products or services together, typically benefiting all parties involved.  It leverages the existing customer base of each partner to reach a wider audience.|The movie studio engaged in cross-promotion with a fast-food chain, offering themed toys in kids' meals.  这家电影制片厂与一家快餐连锁店进行了交叉推广，在儿童套餐中提供主题玩具。|The two companies agreed on a cross-promotion to boost sales. 这两家公司同意进行交叉推广以促进销售。|joint promotion, cooperative marketing| N/A |noun
