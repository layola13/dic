embalmings: /ɪmˈbɑːmɪŋz/
n.
防腐处理；尸体保存；（尤指）涂香油防腐
The process of preserving a corpse from decay.
The museum houses several Egyptian mummies, testaments to the ancient art of embalming.  这个博物馆收藏了几具埃及木乃伊，证明了古代防腐技术的精湛。
preservation, mummification
decay, decomposition
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embaumer, from en- "in" + baume "balm".  Ultimately from Latin balsamum, from Greek βάλσαμον (balsamon).
