ostiole:/ˈɒstiəʊl/ (UK), /ˈɑːstioʊl/ (US)| n. |小孔，气孔|A small opening or pore, especially one in a plant or animal, serving as a mouth, an entrance, or an exit.|The ostiole of the fig allows wasps to enter and pollinate the flowers.|Synonym: pore, opening, aperture, orifice|Antonym: closure, blockage|noun
