favela:/fəˈvɛlə/| n.|贫民窟|A shantytown in or near a Brazilian city, especially Rio de Janeiro or São Paulo.  It is often characterized by densely packed housing, poverty, and lack of infrastructure.|The documentary offered a glimpse into the daily lives of the people living in the favela.|slum, shantytown|mansion, affluent neighborhood|noun
