leed:/liːd/| n.|  (印刷或书写的)行距|The leed of the document is too narrow.  这份文件的行距太窄了。| synonym: leading, linespacing | antonym: (无明确的反义词) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *leed, *led, from Old English lēad (“lead”), from Proto-Germanic *laudaʐ (“lead”), from Proto-Indo-European *lAudh- (“lead”). Cognate with West Frisian lead (“lead”), Dutch lood (“lead”), German Lot (“solder, plummet, lead”), Danish lod (“lead”), Swedish lod (“lead, weight, solder”), Icelandic lóð (“lead”).
