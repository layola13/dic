overprotecting:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪŋ/| adj.| 过度保护的|Protecting someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence and development.  Shielding someone too much from potential harm or challenges.|例句：Her overprotecting nature made it difficult for her son to develop self-reliance. 她过度保护的天性使她儿子难以培养自立能力。

例句：They were overprotecting their children, never letting them out of their sight. 他们过度保护他们的孩子，从不让孩子们离开他们的视线。|近义词：smothering, coddling, shielding, sheltering|反义词：neglectful, inattentive|词性: adjective
