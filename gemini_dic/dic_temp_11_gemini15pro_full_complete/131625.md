noncomposure:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpoʊʒər/ | n. |  不安；失态；慌张 | Lack of composure; agitation. |  Her noncomposure during the crisis was surprising.  她在危机中的慌张令人惊讶。| 近义词: anxiety, unease, perturbation, disquiet, agitation | 反义词: composure, calmness, tranquility, serenity | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (not) + composure (calmness)  来自拉丁语 componere,  com- (together) + ponere (to place).
