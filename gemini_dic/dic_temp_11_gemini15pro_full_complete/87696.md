hexandria: /hɛkˈsændriə/
noun
六雄蕊；六雄蕊植物
Having six stamens in each flower.  Relating to or denoting plants of the class Hexandria (in the Linnaean system), which have six stamens of equal length.
例句：Hexandria is a class in the Linnaean system of plant classification.  六雄蕊是林奈植物分类系统中的一个类别。

例句：Many members of the lily family fall into the hexandria class.  许多百合科植物都属于六雄蕊类。

From Greek hex (ἕξ) "six" + andros (ἀνήρ, genitive ἀνδρός) "man," used botanically to mean "stamen."
