macroseism:/ˈmækroʊsaɪzəm/ | n. | 大地震，强震 | A large-scale earthquake that can be felt over a wide area. |  The macroseism caused significant damage to the region.  大地震对该地区造成了重大破坏。 | synonym: earthquake, temblor | antonym: microseism | noun

macroseism:/ˈmækroʊsaɪzəm/ | n. | 地震主震 | The main shock of an earthquake sequence. | The macroseism was followed by a series of aftershocks. 主震之后发生了一系列余震。 | synonym: mainshock, principal shock | antonym: aftershock, foreshock | noun
