delices:/ˈdelɪkəsiːz/ (UK), /ˈdelɪkəsiːz/ (US)| n. |美味佳肴；珍馐；|Pleasures, especially of the senses, particularly relating to food.  Derived from the French word "délices," meaning "delights." Often used in the plural form.|例句：The chef prepared a plate of delices for the special guests.  厨师为特殊的客人准备了一盘美味佳肴。

例句：The bakery is known for its delices, especially the pastries. 这家面包店以其美味佳肴而闻名，尤其是糕点。|近义词：delicacies, treats, goodies, delights|反义词:disgusts, horrors|词性:noun
