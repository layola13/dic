librarians:/laɪˈbreəriənz/
n.
图书管理员
A person trained in library science and engaged in library services.
The librarians helped the students find research materials. 图书管理员帮助学生们寻找研究资料。

librarians:/laɪˈbreəriənz/
n.
图书管理员们
Plural form of librarian.
The librarians are organizing a book fair. 图书管理员们正在组织一个书展。


Etymology:
From Old French *librarie, from Latin librarius (“copyist, bookseller”), from liber (“book”).

Synonyms:
library staff, information specialists, archivists (in some contexts)

Antonyms:
N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
