cc'ed:/ˈsiːsiːd/ /ˈkɑːpid/ | v. |抄送，发送副本|A shortened form of "carbon copied" referring to the practice of sending a copy of a letter or email to someone else.  Originally from the use of carbon paper to create copies of typed documents. |I cc'ed John on the email so he's aware of the situation. 我把这封邮件抄送给了约翰，让他知道情况。|Synonyms: copied, sent a copy to |Antonyms: excluded, omitted |verb
