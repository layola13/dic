exasperatedly:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪdli/ /ɛɡˈzæspəreɪtɪdli/| adv.|恼怒地，愤怒地|In a manner that shows strong annoyance or irritation.  Derived from "exasperate" (to irritate intensely) + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "exasperatus," past participle of "exasperare" (to roughen, irritate).|She sighed exasperatedly and threw her hands up in the air. 她恼怒地叹了口气，并将双手举向空中。|He exasperatedly slammed the door shut. 他愤怒地把门关上。|近义词：angrily, furiously, impatiently, irritably|反义词：calmly, patiently, peacefully|词性:副词
