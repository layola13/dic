breadlines:/ˈbredˌlaɪnz/ | n. | 救济粮领取队伍；面包线 | A line of people waiting to receive free food, typically during a time of economic hardship. | During the Great Depression, breadlines were a common sight in many cities. 大萧条时期，在许多城市都能看到排队领取救济粮的人群。| synonyms: queues, food lines | antonyms: abundance, prosperity | noun

breadlines:/ˈbredˌlaɪnz/ | n. | (比喻) 贫困线；赤贫状态 | A state of poverty or near poverty. | Many families are living just above the breadlines. 许多家庭的生活水平 barely above the breadlines. 许多家庭勉强维持生计， barely above the breadlines.| synonyms: poverty, destitution | antonyms: wealth, affluence | noun
