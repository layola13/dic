ixtil: /ˈiʃtil/ | n. | 伊斯特尔（一种传统的玛雅蓝色染料）|  A traditional Mayan blue dye made from the leaves of the añil plant (Indigofera suffruticosa) and palygorskite clay.  It was used extensively in pre-Columbian Mesoamerica for painting murals, ceramics, and textiles. | Example:  Archaeologists discovered remnants of ixtil pigment on ancient Mayan pottery. 考古学家在古代玛雅陶器上发现了伊斯特尔颜料的残留物。| Synonym:  Maya blue, indigo | Antonym: N/A | noun

ixtil: /ˈiʃtil/ | adj. | 伊斯特尔蓝色的 | Colored with ixtil, a traditional Mayan blue dye. | Example: The ixtil murals in the temple were remarkably well-preserved. 寺庙里伊斯特尔蓝色的壁画保存得非常好。 | Synonym: indigo-colored, Maya blue | Antonym: N/A | adjective
