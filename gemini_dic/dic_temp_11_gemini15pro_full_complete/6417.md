amphitheatric:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrɪk/
adj.
圆形剧场的，圆形剧场式的
Resembling or characteristic of an amphitheater; having the form of an amphitheater, especially in being semicircular or oval.
The seating arrangement was amphitheatric, allowing everyone a good view of the stage. 座位的安排是圆形剧场式的，让每个人都能很好地看到舞台。
近义词:semicircular, oval, arena-like
反义词:linear, straight
词性:adjective


amphitheatrical:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrɪkəl/
adj.
圆形剧场的，圆形剧场式的
Relating to or resembling an amphitheater.
The valley had an amphitheatrical shape, with the mountains rising around it. 山谷呈圆形剧场状，周围群山环绕。
近义词:amphitheatric, arena-like, circular
反义词:linear, straight
词性:adjective
