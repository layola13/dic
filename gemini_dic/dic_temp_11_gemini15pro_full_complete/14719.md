awarenesses:/əˈwɛərnɪsɪz/
n.
意识，认识；觉悟
The state of being aware; knowledge or perception of a situation or fact.
She had a growing awareness of the problem. 她对这个问题的认识越来越深刻。
consciousness, recognition, understanding, perception
ignorance, unawareness, insensitivity
noun
