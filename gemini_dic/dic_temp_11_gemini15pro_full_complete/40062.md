contacted:/ˈkɒntæktɪd/ /ˈkɑːntæktɪd/
verb
联系过，接触过
past tense and past participle of "contact" which means to communicate with someone, typically in order to give or receive specific information.
I contacted the company about the job opening. 我联系了那家公司询问职位空缺。
The virus is spread when an infected person is contacted by others. 当感染者与他人接触时，病毒就会传播。
Synonyms: reached, communicated with, corresponded with, got in touch with
Antonyms: ignored, avoided, shunned
词性: 动词
