blarneyed:/ˈblɑːr.nid/| adj.|花言巧语的，甜言蜜语的|Persuaded by flattery or artful talk.  Influenced by smooth, flattering talk; charmed.|He blarneyed his way into her good graces. 他用花言巧语赢得了她的好感。|Synonyms: sweet-talked, cajoled, flattered, wheedled|Antonyms: straightforward, blunt, honest, frank|adjective


blarney:/ˈblɑːr.ni/| n.|奉承，甜言蜜语|Smooth, flattering talk; cajolery.|He used a lot of blarney to get what he wanted. 他用了很多甜言蜜语来得到他想要的东西。|Synonyms: flattery, cajolery, sweet talk, smooth talk|Antonyms: honesty, frankness, bluntness, directness|noun


词源: From Blarney Stone, a stone in Blarney Castle, Ireland, said to give the gift of eloquence to those who kiss it. 源自爱尔兰布拉尼城堡中的一块石头——布拉尼石，据说亲吻这块石头的人会获得雄辩的才能。
