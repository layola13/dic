asynchronous: /eɪˈsɪŋkrənəs/ /ˌeɪsɪŋˈkrɑːnəs/ | adj. | 异步的，非同步的 | Not occurring at the same time; independent of the time in which another event occurs. |  Data is transferred asynchronously between the peripheral and the central processor. 外围设备和中央处理器之间异步传输数据。|  Asynchronous learning allows students to study at their own pace. 异步学习允许学生按照自己的进度学习。 | 近义词：non-concurrent, non-simultaneous | 反义词：synchronous, concurrent | 词性:形容词

Etymology->

From a- (not) + synchronous (occurring at the same time). Synchronous comes from the Greek syn- (together) and chronos (time).
