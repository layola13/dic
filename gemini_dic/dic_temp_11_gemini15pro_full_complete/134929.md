nonmutinousnesses:/ˌnɒnˌmjuːtɪnəsnəsɪz/
n.
不服从，不叛变（的状态）
The state or quality of being not mutinous; lack of mutiny.
The captain praised the crew's nonmutinousnesses during the difficult voyage. 船长赞扬了船员在艰难航行中的服从精神。
Synonyms: obedience, compliance, docility, loyalty
Antonyms: mutiny, rebellion, revolt, disobedience
