hematin:/ˈhiːmətɪn/ | n. |血红素，正铁血红素|A dark bluish or brownish pigment containing iron, derived from oxidized heme.  It is found in aged bloodstains and can be used in forensic investigations.|The forensic scientist identified hematin in the bloodstain. 法医在血迹中发现了血红素。|Synonyms: ferriheme, oxidized heme |Antonyms: heme (in its reduced form)|noun
