floristries: /ˈflɔːrɪstriːz/
n.
花店; 花艺; 花卉栽培;
The business of selling or arranging flowers; the art of arranging flowers.
She owns a chain of floristries in the city. 她在这个城市拥有一连锁花店。
The floristries offer a wide variety of bouquets for all occasions.  这些花店提供各种场合的各式花束。
flower shops, flower arranging, floral design
