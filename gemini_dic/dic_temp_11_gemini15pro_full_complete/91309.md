hydrometer:/haɪˈdrɒmɪtər/ (US) /haɪˈdrɑːmɪtər/ (UK)| n.|液体比重计；比重瓶|An instrument used for measuring the relative density of liquids, especially to determine the alcoholic strength or specific gravity of a liquid.|The winemaker used a hydrometer to check the sugar content of the grape juice.|Synonyms: areometer, densimeter|Antonyms: None|noun
