immedicable:/ɪˈmɛdɪkəbl/
adjective
无法治愈的; 不可救药的
Incapable of being healed or cured.
His immedicable disease caused him great suffering. 他无法治愈的疾病给他带来了巨大的痛苦。

The damage to their relationship seemed immedicable.  他们关系中的裂痕似乎无法弥合。

近义词: incurable, irremediable, terminal, hopeless
反义词: curable, treatable, remediable
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *immedicabilis, from in- "not" + medicabilis "curable," from medicare "to heal."
