externalizing: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɛkˈstɝːnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|外化；使外在化|To express (feelings, for example) outwardly, rather than repressing them.|He externalized his anger by yelling at the wall. 他对着墙大喊，以此来发泄他的愤怒。|近义词: express, vent, manifest, project|反义词: internalize, repress, suppress|词性: 动词

externalizing: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɛkˈstɝːnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|adj.|外化的；外向的|Relating to or characterized by externalization.|Externalizing behaviors can be disruptive in a classroom setting. 外化行为在课堂环境中可能具有破坏性。|近义词: outward, expressive|反义词: inward, internalized|词性: 形容词

externalizing: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɛkˈstɝːnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|n.|外化；外向化|The act or process of externalizing.|The externalizing of his grief helped him to cope with his loss.  他将悲伤外化，这帮助他应对失去亲人的痛苦。|近义词: expression, manifestation|反义词: internalization, repression|词性: 名词


词源: From the verb externalize, which comes from the adjective external, from Latin externus "outward," from exter "outside."
