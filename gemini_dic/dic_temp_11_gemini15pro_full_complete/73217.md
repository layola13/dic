forceps:/ˈfɔːrˌsɛps/| n. | 镊子；钳子 |A hinged instrument with two blades, used for grasping and holding objects, especially in surgery or other delicate operations. |The surgeon used forceps to remove the splinter.  外科医生用镊子取出了碎片。

Forceps are commonly used in childbirth. 钳子常用于分娩。|近义词：tongs, pincers, tweezers |反义词: N/A |名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *forcipes "pair of tongs," plural of *forcep, from formus "warm" + capere "to take"
