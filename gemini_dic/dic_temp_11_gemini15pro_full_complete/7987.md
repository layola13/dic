anthologist:/ænˈθɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
选集编纂者；诗集编者
A person who compiles an anthology.
She is a renowned anthologist of 20th-century poetry. 她是一位著名的20世纪诗歌选集编纂者。
compiler, editor
 
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀνθολογία (anthología, 'collection of flowers, garland of flowers, anthology')  < ἄνθος (ánthos, 'flower, bloom') + -λογία (-logía, '-logy').
