fugacious:/fjuːˈɡeɪʃəs/
adjective
短暂的；易逝的；转瞬即逝的
lasting for a short time; fleeting
例句：The beauty of the cherry blossoms is fugacious. 樱花的美丽是短暂的。

例句：Youth is fugacious, so enjoy every moment. 青春是易逝的，所以要享受每一刻。

近义词：ephemeral, fleeting, transient, momentary, evanescent
反义词：permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual, eternal
词性: adjective
