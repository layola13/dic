circuited:/ˈsɜːrkɪtɪd/
adjective|电路连接的；短路的|Connected by a circuit; short-circuited.
The circuited wires caused a power outage.|电路连接的电线导致了停电。
近义词:connected, wired
反义词:disconnected, unwired
词性:adjective
词源:From "circuit" + "-ed." "Circuit" comes from Old French "circuit" (going around), ultimately from Latin "circuitus" (a going around), past participle of "circumire" (to go around).
