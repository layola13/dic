doggednesses:/ˈdɒɡɪdnəsɪz/ /ˈdɑːɡɪdnəsɪz/
n.
顽强，坚持不懈，不屈不挠
The quality of being dogged; persistence, tenacity.
His doggedness eventually paid off, and he won the case. 他的坚持最终得到了回报，他赢得了这场官司。
persistence, tenacity, determination, resolve, perseverance
irresolution, vacillation, weakness, hesitation
noun
