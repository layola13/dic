fatiguingly:/fəˈtiːɡɪŋli/
adverb
令人疲倦地
in a way that makes you feel very tired
The journey was fatiguingly long. 这趟旅程累人地漫长。
近义词:exhaustingly, tiringly, wearisomely
反义词:refreshingly, invigoratingly
词性:adverb

词源: From fatigue + -ing + -ly.  Fatigue comes from the French word "fatigue," ultimately derived from the Latin "fatigare" meaning "to tire out."
