ceasefire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ | n. |停火；停火协议|A temporary suspension of fighting, typically by agreement between opposing forces. |例句：The two sides agreed to a ceasefire. 双方同意停火。

例句：The ceasefire is fragile and could break down at any time.  停火协议很脆弱，随时可能破裂。

近义词：truce, armistice |反义词: hostility, conflict |词性: noun

ceasefire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ | v. |停火|To stop fighting.  |例句：The rebels have ceased fire. 叛军已经停火。

例句：Both countries agreed to ceasefire immediately. 两国同意立即停火。

近义词：halt, suspend |反义词: attack, fight |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cease" (from Old French "cesser," from Latin "cessare" - to stop, cease) + "fire" (from Old English "fyr," meaning fire).
