oppositeness:/ˈɒpəzɪtnəs/ | n. | 对立性，相反性 | The state or quality of being opposite or contrary. | The oppositeness of their views led to a heated debate. 他们的观点的截然相反导致了一场激烈的辩论。 | synonyms: contrariety, antithesis, opposition, contrast | antonyms: similarity, likeness, sameness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From opposite + -ness.  "Opposite" comes from Old French "oposite" and ultimately from Latin "oppositus," the past participle of "opponere" (to place against, oppose).  "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
