karenina:/kɑːrəˈniːnə/ | n. | 卡列尼娜 |  Refers to the protagonist Anna Karenina in Leo Tolstoy's novel of the same name. It can also be used more generally to refer to a woman trapped in an unhappy marriage or a woman who defies social conventions for love. | Example: Anna Karenina is a complex and tragic character.  安娜·卡列尼娜是一个复杂而悲剧的人物。| Example:  Her story is often compared to Anna Karenina.  她的故事经常被比作安娜·卡列尼娜。 | Synonym:  adulteress (in some contexts), tragic heroine | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific context) | noun
