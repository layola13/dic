hastier:/ˈheɪstiər/ | adj. | 更匆忙的，更草率的 | Comparative form of hasty: done with excessive speed or urgency; hurried. | He made a hastier decision than usual due to the time constraint.  由于时间限制，他做出了比平时更草率的决定。 | Example: The hastier the project, the more likely mistakes will happen. 项目越仓促，就越容易出错。| Synonyms: quicker, faster, more hurried, more rushed | Antonyms: slower, more deliberate, more cautious | adjective
