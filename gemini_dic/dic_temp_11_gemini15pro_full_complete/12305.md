ascomycetes:/ˌæskəˈmaɪsiːts/
n.
子囊菌
a large class of higher fungi that have septate hyphae and spores formed in asci.
Morels and truffles are ascomycetes. 羊肚菌和松露都是子囊菌。
近义词:sac fungi
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑其他真菌类别，例如担子菌 Basidiomycetes)
词性: noun
