artistic:/ɑːrˈtɪstɪk/ | adj. | 艺术的；有艺术才能的 | Having or revealing natural creative skill. | She has an artistic temperament. 她有艺术家的气质。|  The painting is very artistic. 这幅画很有艺术性。| 近义词: creative, imaginative, aesthetic, artful | 反义词:inartistic, uncreative, unimaginative | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French artistique, from Latin artisticus, from ars, art- ‘art’.
