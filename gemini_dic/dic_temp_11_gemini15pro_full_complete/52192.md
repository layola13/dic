diarthrodial:/ˌdaɪɑːrˈθroʊdiəl/| adj.|滑膜关节的|Relating to a freely movable joint; synovial.  A diarthrodial joint allows for a wide range of motion.|The knee is a diarthrodial joint, allowing for flexion and extension.|近义词：synovial|反义词:synarthrotic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (through, across) + arthro- (joint) + -al (relating to).  Ultimately from Greek *diá* (through) + *árthron* (joint).
