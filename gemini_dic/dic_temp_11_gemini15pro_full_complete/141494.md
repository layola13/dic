opisthobranch:/əˈpɪsθəˌbræŋk/ | n. | 后鳃类动物 |  A marine gastropod mollusk of the subclass Opisthobranchia, which includes sea slugs, sea hares, and bubble snails.  These mollusks are characterized by having their gills behind their heart and often having reduced or absent shells. |  Many colorful opisthobranchs can be found in coral reefs. 许多色彩鲜艳的后鳃类动物可以在珊瑚礁中找到。|  The opisthobranch grazed on the algae.  这只后鳃动物吃着海藻。 | synonym:  gastropods, sea slug (informal), nudibranch (for some types) | antonym: (No direct antonym,  perhaps "prosobranch" which is another subclass of gastropods) | noun


opisthobranchiate: /əˌpɪsθəˈbræŋkiət/ | adj. | 后鳃类的 | Belonging to or characteristic of the Opisthobranchia, a subclass of marine gastropod mollusks. | The opisthobranchiate sea slug displayed vibrant colors. 这只后鳃亚纲的海蛞蝓展现出鲜艳的色彩。 |  Researchers studied the unique respiratory system of the opisthobranchiate snail. 研究人员研究了这种后鳃类蜗牛独特的呼吸系统。 | synonym:  opisthobranch (as an adjective) | antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially "prosobranch" as an adjective) | adjective
