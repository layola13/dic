eryngo:/ɪˈrɪŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪˈrɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|刺芹, 海冬青, 海蓬子|A plant of the parsley family, typically growing in coastal areas, with spiny leaves and blue or white flower heads. The roots of some species are edible.|例句：The eryngo grew wild along the dunes. 刺芹沿着沙丘生长。|例句：He added chopped eryngo root to the salad. 他在沙拉里加了切碎的刺芹根。|近义词：sea holly, beach thistle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *eringo*, from Old French *eringo*, from Late Latin *eringium*, from Latin *eryngium*. Ultimately from Greek *ἠρύγγιον* (*ērúngion*), name of a plant, perhaps the sea holly.
