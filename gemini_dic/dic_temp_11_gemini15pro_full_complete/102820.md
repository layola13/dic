jarl:/jɑːrl/ | n. |伯爵；酋长|A Scandinavian nobleman, especially a chief in Norway before the 13th century, ranking just below the king. |例句：The jarl ruled over a vast territory.  伯爵统治着广阔的领土。

例句：The sagas tell of the bravery of many jarls.  萨迦讲述了许多伯爵的英勇事迹。|近义词：chieftain, earl, nobleman|反义词: commoner, peasant|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old Norse *jarl, from Proto-Germanic *erlaz, meaning "man, warrior, nobleman". Cognate with Old English eorl.
