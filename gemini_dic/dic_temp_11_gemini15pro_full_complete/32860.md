choline:/ˈkoʊliːn/ /ˈkəʊliːn/| n.|胆碱|An essential nutrient, usually grouped with the B vitamins, that is important for liver function, normal brain development, nerve function, muscle movement, supporting energy levels, and maintaining a healthy metabolism.|Choline is vital for maintaining healthy cell membranes. 胆碱对维持健康的细胞膜至关重要。|Synonyms:  vitamin B4 (sometimes considered)|Antonyms: None|noun

choline:/ˈkoʊliːn/ /ˈkəʊliːn/| n.|胆碱|A quaternary ammonium compound involved in many biological processes, including the synthesis of the neurotransmitter acetylcholine and the maintenance of cell membrane structure.|Adequate choline intake is important during pregnancy for fetal brain development.  充足的胆碱摄入在怀孕期间对胎儿大脑发育很重要。|Synonyms:  vitamin B4 (sometimes considered)|Antonyms: None|noun
