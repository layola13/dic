kolarian:/koʊˈlɛəriən/| adj. n.| 科尔语系的，科尔语系的人|Relating to or denoting a family of languages spoken in central and eastern India, or a member of a people speaking these languages.  It's a now largely obsolete term, considered inaccurate and potentially offensive, previously used to group certain indigenous languages of India. |The term "kolarian" was once used to describe the Munda languages. | 近义词: Austroasiatic (more accurate), Munda (more accurate) | 反义词:  None (as it's a language family classification) | 词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Kol, a name used for certain tribal groups in India.
