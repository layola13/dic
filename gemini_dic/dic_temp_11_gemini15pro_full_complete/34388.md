classifying: /ˈklæsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 分类，归类 | The process of arranging things into groups or categories based on shared characteristics. |  Librarians are classifying books according to genre. 图书管理员正在根据类型对书籍进行分类。

The scientist is classifying the plants based on their medicinal properties.  科学家正在根据植物的药用特性对它们进行分类。 | synonyms: categorizing, sorting, grouping, arranging | antonyms:  mixing, combining, jumbling | verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *classificare, from Latin classis (“class, division, fleet”) + -ficare (from facere (“to make, do”)).
