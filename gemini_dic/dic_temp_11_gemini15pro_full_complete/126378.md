myrmecophyte:/ˌmɜːrmɪˈkɒfaɪt/ | n. |蚁栖植物|A plant that lives in a mutualistic relationship with ants, providing shelter and/or food for the ants, and in turn receiving protection from herbivores or other benefits from the ants.|Example 1:  The acacia tree is a well-known myrmecophyte, offering ants hollow thorns for shelter and nectar as food.  金合欢树是一种著名的蚁栖植物，它为蚂蚁提供空心的刺作为庇护所，并提供花蜜作为食物。

Example 2: Some myrmecophytes have specialized structures to house ant colonies. 一些蚁栖植物有专门的结构来容纳蚁群。|近义词: ant plant |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
