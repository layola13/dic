goriest:/ˈɡɔːriɪst/ /ˈɡɔːriəst/| adj.|最血腥的，最残暴的|Superlative adjective of gory; involving or showing a great deal of bloodshed and violence.|The movie had the goriest scene I have ever witnessed. 这部电影有我见过的最血腥的场景。|The goriest battle of the war was fought on this very ground. 这场战争中最血腥的战斗就在这片土地上进行。|近义词：bloodiest, most violent, gruesome, grisly|反义词：cleanest, most peaceful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *gore* (blood) + *-y* (adjective suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix).  The word "gore" comes from Old English *gor*, meaning "filth, dung, mud," later evolving to mean clotted blood.
