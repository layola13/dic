abetters:/əˈbɛtərz/
| n. |教唆犯；帮凶；煽动者|People who assist or encourage someone to do something wrong, especially in a crime.|The abetters of the robbery were caught and arrested. 抢劫案的帮凶被抓捕归案。|Synonyms: accomplices, accessories, collaborators|Antonyms: hinderers, obstructers|noun
abet:/əˈbɛt/
| vt. |教唆；怂恿；煽动|To encourage or assist someone to do something wrong, especially in a crime.|He was accused of abetting the murder. 他被指控教唆谋杀。|Synonyms: incite, instigate, encourage|Antonyms: deter, discourage, prevent|verb
