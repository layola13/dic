leghorn:/ˈleɡɔːrn/ | n. | 莱ghorn鸡; (尤指) 白色莱ghorn鸡; 莱ghorn草帽 | A breed of domestic chicken, originally from Italy, that is a prolific layer of white eggs. Also, a straw hat made in Leghorn, Italy. |  We raise leghorns for their eggs. 我们饲养莱ghorn鸡是为了它们的蛋。 | Synonyms: (for the chicken) White Leghorn, Italian hen | Antonyms: (no direct antonym for a breed) | noun
