nonimputative:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪv/
adjective
非归罪的；不归咎于…的
Not involving imputation or attribution, especially of sin or guilt.  Not holding someone morally responsible for something.
His nonimputative approach to therapy helped the client feel safe and accepted. 他非归罪性的治疗方法帮助来访者感到安全和被接纳。
Near Synonyms: non-blaming, forgiving, absolving, exonerating
Antonyms: imputative, blaming, accusing, incriminating
词性: adjective
