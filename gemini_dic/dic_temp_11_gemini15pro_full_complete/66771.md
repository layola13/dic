exoterics:/ˌɛksəˈtɛrɪks/| n. |秘传的教义；奥秘；秘术| doctrines or practices of a mystical or esoteric nature, especially those made accessible to the initiated.|  The exoterics of the ancient mystery cults were often revealed only to a select few.  古代神秘教派的秘传教义通常只对少数人透露。|  近义词: arcana, mysteries, secrets | 反义词: exotericism | 词性: 名词

exoteric:/ˌɛksəˈtɛrɪk/| adj. | 外传的；公开的；通俗易懂的 | intended for or likely to be understood by the general public.| The philosopher's later works were more exoteric than his earlier, more complex writings. 这位哲学家的后期作品比他早期更复杂的作品更容易理解。| 近义词: public, common, popular, accessible | 反义词: esoteric, arcane, occult | 词性: 形容词
