defined:/dɪˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 被定义的；清晰的；明确的 | Clearly characterized or delimited; explained or described. |  The terms of the contract are clearly defined. 合同条款明确规定。 | synonyms: specific, precise, exact, explicit, clear, determined, fixed, set, established | antonyms: undefined, vague, ambiguous, unclear, nebulous | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *definen*, from Old French *definir*, from Latin *definire* (“to limit, determine, explain”), from *de* (“completely”) + *finire* (“to finish, limit”), from *finis* (“end, limit, boundary”).
