nonaccedences:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛnsɪz/ | n. | 未发生的事情；意外事故 |  Things that have not happened; unforeseen or unexpected events.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or nonstandard. It's likely a back-formation from "accidence," referring to things that *do* happen. | There were many nonaccedences that could have altered the course of the battle. 许多未发生的事情可能会改变战役的进程。| Near synonyms: non-events, unforeseen circumstances, contingencies | Antonyms: happenings, occurrences | noun
