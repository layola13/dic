outstartle: /ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtl/ /ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtəl/
verb
使更加吃惊，胜过…的吃惊
to surprise or frighten someone more than they have already been surprised or frightened
The sudden noise outstartled everyone in the room. 突然的噪音让房间里的每个人都大吃一惊。
近义词: amaze, astound, shock, stun
反义词: calm, soothe, comfort
词性: verb
