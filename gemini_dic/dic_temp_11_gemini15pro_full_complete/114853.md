mammilla:/məˈmɪlə/ | n. | 乳头，乳头状突起 | A nipple, or a nipple-shaped projection. | The mammilla of the volcano was clearly visible from the airplane. 从飞机上可以清楚地看到火山的乳头状突起。 | Example 2: The architect incorporated mammillae into the building's design. 建筑师将乳头状突起融入建筑设计中。 | 近义词: nipple, teat, papilla | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源: From Latin *mammillatus*, past participle of *mammillare* "to furnish with nipples," from *mammilla* "nipple," diminutive of *mamma* "breast."
