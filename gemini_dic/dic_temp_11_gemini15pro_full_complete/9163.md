antinephritic:/ˌæntiːnɪˈfrɪtɪk/
adjective
抗肾炎的;治疗肾炎的
Relating to or used to treat nephritis (inflammation of the kidneys).
This medicine has antinephritic properties and can help reduce kidney inflammation.  这种药具有抗肾炎的特性，可以帮助减少肾脏炎症。
The doctor prescribed an antinephritic drug for the patient's kidney condition. 医生给病人开了抗肾炎药来治疗他的肾病。
近义词:  renal-protective, nephroprotective
反义词:  nephritic (in some contexts)
词性: adjective
