bested:/ˈbɛstɪd/| v.|战胜，击败|To defeat or surpass someone in a competition or struggle.  It is the past tense and past participle of the verb "best".|He bested his rival in the final round. 他在最后一轮击败了他的对手。|近义词：defeated, conquered, overcame, triumphed over, outdid, surpassed|反义词：lost to, defeated by, succumbed to|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *besten*, from Old English *betst* (superlative of *gōd* (“good”)), + *-ian* (suffix forming verbs). Cognate with Scots *best* (“to defeat”).  The superlative form *best* became a verb meaning "to get the better of."
