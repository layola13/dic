commigration:/ˌkɒmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/ |n.|共同迁徙，共迁；集体移居|The commingration of different species to the same breeding grounds is a fascinating phenomenon. 不同物种共同迁徙到同一繁殖地是一个迷人的现象。|例句：The commingration of these two tribes led to the formation of a new, larger community. 这两个部落的共同迁徙导致了一个新的、更大的社群的形成。

例句：Scientists are studying the commingration patterns of birds to understand their navigation skills. 科学家们正在研究鸟类的共同迁徙模式以了解它们的导航技能。|近义词：migration, joint migration, collective migration|反义词：emigration, immigration|词性: noun
