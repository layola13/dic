kerbed:/kɜːbd/ | adj. | 有路缘的；装有路缘的 | Having a kerb or kerbs; furnished with a kerb. | The newly kerbed road improved the safety for pedestrians. 新铺设路缘的道路提高了行人的安全性。| The kerbed driveway prevented the car from rolling onto the lawn.  有路缘的车道防止汽车滚到草坪上。 | 近义词: curbed | 反义词: unkerbed | 词性: 形容词

词源: From kerb + -ed.  Kerb itself comes from the earlier curb, from the Old French *courbe, meaning "curved," ultimately from Latin curvus.
