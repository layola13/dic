mainprise:/meɪnˈpraɪz/
noun
保释，担保；释放
The act of taking responsibility for someone accused of a crime, guaranteeing their appearance in court.
He was released on mainprise. 他被保释释放了。
Synonyms: bail, surety, guarantee
Antonyms: imprisonment, detention, confinement
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mainprise*, from main (hand) + prise (a taking, seizing).  Ultimately from Latin manus (hand) and prehendere (to seize).
