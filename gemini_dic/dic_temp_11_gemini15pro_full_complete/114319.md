maintenances:/ˈmeɪntənənsɪz/
n.
维护, 保养
the process of keeping something in good condition by regularly checking and repairing it
例句：The maintenances of the old building cost a fortune.  维护这栋老建筑花费了一大笔钱。

例句：Regular maintenances are essential to keep the machinery running smoothly.  定期维护对于保持机器平稳运行至关重要。

近义词：upkeep, repair, service
反义词：neglect, deterioration
词性: 名词
