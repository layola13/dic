convulse:/kənˈvʌls/| v. |抽搐，痉挛，剧烈震动|To shake violently and uncontrollably.|例句：The patient convulsed violently during the seizure. 病人在癫痫发作时剧烈抽搐。

例句：Laughter convulsed his whole body. 笑声使他全身震动。|近义词：shake, tremble, shudder, spasm, writhe|反义词：calm, soothe, steady|词性：动词

convulsed:/kənˈvʌlst/| adj. | 震动的，抽搐的 | Affected by convulsions; shaken violently. | 例句：Her convulsed body lay on the floor. 她抽搐的身体躺在地板上。

例句：The country was convulsed by political unrest.  这个国家因政治动荡而震动。|近义词：shaking, trembling, shuddering |反义词：calm, still, peaceful|词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *convulsus*, past participle of *convellere* "to pull violently," from *com-* "together" + *vellere* "to pluck, tear."
