gnomology:/nɒˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. | 格言学；箴言集 | The study of maxims or proverbs; a collection of maxims or proverbs. |  He was a scholar of gnomology, collecting and analyzing proverbs from various cultures. 他是一位格言学家，收集和分析来自不同文化的谚语。 |  Example: Her dissertation focused on the gnomology of ancient Greece. 她的论文集中研究了古希腊的格言学。 | Synonyms:  proverbiology, paremiology | Antonyms:  None | noun
