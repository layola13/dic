agitative:/ˈædʒɪˌteɪtɪv/
adjective
煽动的；使人激动的
Causing or tending to cause agitation.
The speaker's agitative words roused the crowd to action. 演讲者煽动性的话语激起了群众的行动。
近义词: inflammatory, provocative, stirring
反义词: calming, soothing, pacifying
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agitativus, from Latin agitatus (past participle of agitare "to set in motion, drive, urge, rouse"), frequentative of agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
