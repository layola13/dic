nonconstraining:/ˌnɒnkənˈstreɪnɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈstreɪnɪŋ/| adj.|非约束性的，非强制性的|Not imposing restrictions or limitations; allowing freedom of action or choice.|The guidelines are nonconstraining and allow for flexibility in implementation.  这些指导方针是非约束性的，允许在实施过程中有一定的灵活性。|Example:  A nonconstraining contract allows both parties significant latitude.  一个非约束性合同允许双方有很大的自由度。|Synonyms: flexible, permissive, open, optional, voluntary|Antonyms: restrictive, binding, mandatory, compulsory, obligatory|Word type: adjective
