golding:/ˈɡoʊldɪŋ/| n.|戈尔丁; 金色; 金色的东西;|Golding refers to William Golding, a British novelist, playwright, and poet, best known for his novel Lord of the Flies. It can also refer to anything made of or resembling gold, or the act of gilding.|例句：William Golding won the Nobel Prize in Literature. 威廉·戈尔丁获得了诺贝尔文学奖。
