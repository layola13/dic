outburn:/ˈaʊtbɜːrn/
verb
烧尽; 烧坏
To burn longer or more intensely than something else.
The bonfire outburned the smaller fire.  篝火比小火烧得更久更旺。
The larger candle outburned the smaller one.  大蜡烛比小蜡烛烧得更久。
synonyms: outlast, survive
antonyms: extinguish, smother
词性: 动词
