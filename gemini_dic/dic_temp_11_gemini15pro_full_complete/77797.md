generalization:/ˌdʒɛnərələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 概括，归纳；普遍化；一般化 | A generalization is a broad statement or idea that applies to a group of people or things. |  He made a sweeping generalization about women drivers. 他对女司机做了一个笼统的概括。| synonyms: simplification, abstraction, summarization, synthesis | antonyms: specification, particularization | noun

generalization:/ˌdʒɛnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 概括，归纳；普遍化；一般化 | The act or process of forming a general notion or concept. | The generalization of this mathematical principle is complex. 这个数学原理的推广很复杂。| synonyms: induction, inference, extrapolation | antonyms: specialization, specification | noun
