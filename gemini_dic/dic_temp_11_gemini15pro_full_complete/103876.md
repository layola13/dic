jubilate:/ˈdʒuːbɪleɪt/| v. | 欢庆，庆祝；感到欣喜 | To express great joy, especially due to a success or victory.  |  They jubilated over their team's victory. 他们为团队的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。

|近义词：rejoice, celebrate, exult |反义词: mourn, grieve |词性: verb

jubilation:/ˌdʒuːbɪˈleɪʃən/| n. | 欢庆，庆祝；欣喜 | A feeling of great joy, especially due to a success or victory. | The news was met with jubilation.  这个消息受到了热烈的欢迎。

|近义词：rejoicing, celebration, exultation |反义词: grief, sorrow |词性: noun
