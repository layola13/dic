moc:/mɑːk/ | n. |模拟考试，模拟操作|A simulated test or operation, often used for training or assessment.|例句：The pilots practiced landing in a flight moc. 飞行员在飞行模拟器中练习着陆。|例句：The disaster relief team ran a moc of the earthquake scenario. 灾难救援队进行了一次地震场景的模拟演练。|近义词：simulation, drill, exercise, rehearsal|反义词：reality, actual event|词性: noun

词源:  MOC is an abbreviation, likely derived from "mock," meaning to imitate or simulate something.  It doesn't have a complex etymological history like words with roots in older languages.
