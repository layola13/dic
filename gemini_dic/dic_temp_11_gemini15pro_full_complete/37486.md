committed: /kəˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 坚定的，尽心尽力的，已承诺的 | dedicated, devoted, pledged | She is a committed teacher who always puts her students first. 她是一位尽职尽责的老师，总是把学生放在第一位。 |  committed to, committed relationship | uncommitted, indifferent | adjective

committed: /kəˈmɪtɪd/ | v. (past tense and past participle) | 犯（罪），承诺，把…托付给 | perpetrate, pledge, entrust | He committed a serious crime. 他犯了重罪。 | commit to, commit suicide | abstain, refrain | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *committen*, from Latin *committere*, from *com-* (together) + *mittere* (to send).
