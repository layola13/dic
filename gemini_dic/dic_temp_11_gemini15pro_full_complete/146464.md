overfruitful: /ˌoʊvərˈfruːtfəl/
adjective
过度结果实的;产量过高的
Producing or yielding too much fruit.
The apple tree was overfruitful this year, and the branches were bending under the weight. 今年苹果树结果过多，树枝都被压弯了。
The vineyard was overfruitful, resulting in smaller grapes than usual.  葡萄园产量过高，导致葡萄比往年小。
synonyms: prolific, abundant, copious
antonyms: barren, unproductive, infertile
adjective
