outhumored:/ˌaʊtˈhjuːmərd/
|adj.| 比…更幽默; 胜过…的幽默 |Having a better sense of humor than someone else; surpassing someone in humor.| He outhumored his opponent in the debate and won over the audience. 他在辩论中以幽默胜过对手，赢得了观众的支持。| 近义词: wittier, funnier, more humorous | 反义词: less humorous, dull, witless | 词性: adjective
词源: out- + humored.  The prefix "out-" indicates exceeding or surpassing, while "humored" relates to having a sense of humor.  Therefore, "outhumored" means to surpass someone in humor.
