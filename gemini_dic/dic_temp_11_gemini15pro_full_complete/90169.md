hotlink:/ˈhɑːtlɪŋk/| n. v.|热链接，盗链|A link from a Web page to another Web site's content, such as an image or a music file, usually done without permission.  It consumes bandwidth and resources of the linked site without their consent. |Example: The website owner discovered numerous hotlinks to their images on other sites, draining their server resources. 该网站所有者发现其他网站上有大量指向其图片的热链接，这耗尽了他们的服务器资源。

Example: Some websites prevent hotlinking by using techniques like .htaccess files. 一些网站通过使用 .htaccess 文件等技术来防止盗链。|近义词：inline linking, direct linking, bandwidth theft|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym exists for the technical term "hotlink")|词性: noun, verb
