nuttily:/ˈnʌtɪli/
adverb
疯狂地；古怪地；神经质地
In a way that suggests mental instability or eccentricity.
He grinned nuttily and began to sing. 他傻笑着开始唱歌。
近义词：crazily, insanely, eccentrically, bizarrely
反义词：sanely, rationally, normally
词性：副词

词源: From nut (n.) + -ily (adverbial suffix).  "Nut" in this context originally referred to the kernel of a nut, and later came to mean "head" or "brain,"  eventually leading to slang usages related to craziness.
