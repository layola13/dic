monopolist:/məˈnɑːpəlɪst/ /məˈnɒpəlɪst/| n. |垄断者，独占者|A person or company that has a monopoly.| The monopolist controlled the entire market for that particular product.  这个垄断者控制了那种特定产品的整个市场。|Synonym: trust, cartel, magnate|Antonym: competitor, rival|noun


monopolize:/məˈnɑːpəlaɪz/ /məˈnɒpəlaɪz/| v. |垄断，独占|To have or take control of the largest part of something so that other people are prevented from sharing it.| The company monopolized the banana trade. 该公司垄断了香蕉贸易。|Synonym: dominate, corner, control|Antonym: share, distribute|verb


monopolistic:/məˌnɑːpəˈlɪstɪk/ /məˌnɒpəˈlɪstɪk/| adj. |垄断的，独占的|Relating to or involving a monopoly.|  The monopolistic practices of the company were investigated by the government.  该公司的垄断行为受到了政府的调查。|Synonym: exclusive, restrictive, controlling|Antonym: competitive, open, shared|adjective
