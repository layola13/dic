bumpinesses:/ˈbʌmpɪnəsɪz/
noun
崎岖不平，颠簸
the state or quality of being bumpy; unevenness.
The bumpinesses of the road made the journey uncomfortable. 路面的颠簸使得旅程很不舒服。
Synonyms: unevenness, roughness, irregularity
Antonyms: smoothness, evenness, flatness

bumpy:/ˈbʌmpi/
adjective
崎岖不平的，颠簸的
having a rough or uneven surface.
The bumpy road made the car shake. 颠簸的道路使汽车摇晃。
Synonyms: uneven, rough, irregular
Antonyms: smooth, even, flat
词性: 形容词

bump:/bʌmp/
noun, verb
撞击, 肿块; 碰撞
noun: a protuberance, swelling, or lump.  verb: to knock or run into something with a jolt.
The car hit a bump in the road. 汽车撞到了路上的一个凸起。
He bumped his head on the low ceiling. 他的头撞到了低矮的天花板上。
Synonyms: (n.) lump, protuberance, swelling; (v.) knock, hit, collide
Antonyms: (n.) hollow, indentation; (v.) avoid, miss
词性: 名词, 动词
