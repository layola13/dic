alleviatory:/əˈliːviəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 减轻的，缓和的 | Making something less painful, severe, or serious.  |  The doctor prescribed alleviatory medication for her back pain. 医生给她开了缓解背痛的药物。 | synonyms: soothing, palliative, mitigating, relieving | antonyms: aggravating, exacerbating, intensifying | adjective

alleviatory:/əˈliːviəˌtɔːri/ | n. | 缓解物，缓和剂 | Something that alleviates pain or suffering. |  A warm bath can be an effective alleviatory for sore muscles.  热水澡可以有效缓解肌肉酸痛。| synonyms:  remedy, balm,  analgesic | antonyms: irritant, stimulant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *alleviatorius, from Latin alleviatus (past participle of alleviare “to lighten, relieve”), from ad- “to” + levis “light”.
