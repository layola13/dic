ostrich:/ˈɒstrɪtʃ/ (UK) /ˈɑːstrɪtʃ/ (US)| n.|鸵鸟|A large flightless bird native to Africa, with a long neck and legs and the ability to run at high speeds. It is the largest living bird species.|The ostrich buried its head in the sand. 鸵鸟把头埋在沙子里。|Synonyms: ratite,  |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal species)|noun
