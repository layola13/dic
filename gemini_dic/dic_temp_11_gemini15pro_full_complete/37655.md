comose:/ˈkəʊməʊs; US ˈkoʊ-/| adj.| 多毛的，毛簇状的，彗星状的 | Having a coma; tufted; resembling a comet. |  The dandelion seed head is comose. 蒲公英的种子头是毛簇状的。

The comet had a bright, comose tail. 这颗彗星有一条明亮的彗星状尾巴。


|近义词：hairy, tufted, plumose|反义词:bald, glabrous|词性:adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cōmōsus* ("hairy, covered with hair"), from *coma* ("hair of the head, mane, foliage") + *-ōsus* (adjective-forming suffix).
