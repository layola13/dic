nonsecessions:/ˌnɒnsɪˈseʃənz/
noun
非脱离
the state of not seceding or withdrawing formally from membership of a federal union, an alliance, or a political or religious organization.
The treaty ensured the nonsecession of the territories. 该条约确保了这些领土不会脱离。
non-withdrawal, non-separation
secession, withdrawal
