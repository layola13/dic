biotype:/ˈbaɪ.əʊ.taɪp/ | n. |生物型，生物类型|A group of organisms having the same genotype. A group of organisms sharing a particular combination of characteristics.  A population of organisms of similar genetic constitution.|例句：Scientists have identified several distinct biotypes of this bacteria. 科学家们已经鉴定出这种细菌的几种不同的生物型。

|例句：This particular biotype of wheat is resistant to drought.  这种特殊类型的小麦具有抗旱性。|近义词：genotype, phenotype, strain, variety|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
