nonfan:/ˌnɑːnˈfæn/ | n. | 非粉丝，不喜欢某事物的人 |  A person who is not a fan of a particular person, team, or thing. | She's a nonfan of reality TV. 她不喜欢真人秀。 | Near synonyms:  detractor, critic, skeptic | Antonyms: fan, enthusiast, admirer | noun

nonfan:/ˌnɑːnˈfæn/ | adj. | 非粉丝的 | Not being a fan of something; characterized by a lack of enthusiasm or interest. | He gave a nonfan-like response to the news. 他对这个消息的反应不像粉丝。| Near synonyms:  unenthusiastic, indifferent, apathetic | Antonyms:  fanatical, enthusiastic, devoted | adjective
