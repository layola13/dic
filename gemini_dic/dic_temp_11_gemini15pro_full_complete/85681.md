have-a-shot-at:/hæv ə ʃɒt æt/ (British) /hæv ə ʃɑːt æt/ (American)| v.|尝试；试一试；争取|To attempt something, especially something that is challenging or unlikely to succeed.|例句：I've never climbed a mountain before, but I'd like to have a shot at it. 我以前从未爬过山，但我想尝试一下。

例句：He decided to have a shot at applying for the scholarship, even though he wasn't sure he'd get it. 他决定试着申请奖学金，尽管他不确定自己是否能得到。

近义词：try, attempt, give it a go, take a crack at, have a go at|反义词: avoid, abstain from, refrain from|词性:动词短语
