digesting:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪŋ/ /dɪˈdʒɛstɪŋ/| v. |消化; 领悟; 整理; 忍受|The process of breaking down food in the body.  Also, the process of understanding or absorbing information.|例句：The baby is digesting her milk.  婴儿正在消化她的牛奶。

例句：I'm still digesting the news of his resignation. 我还在消化他辞职的消息。|近义词：absorbing, assimilating, processing, comprehending|反义词: excreting, regurgitating|词性: Verb (present participle of digest)


Etymology: From Late Latin *digestare, frequentative of Latin digerere "to separate, distribute, arrange, digest," from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear."
