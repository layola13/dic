adelantadillo:/a.ðɛ.lɑn.tɑˈði.ʎo/ | adj. |  precocious, forward, early, premature (especially referring to fruits or crops) |  This Spanish word describes something that is ahead of its time or develops earlier than expected, often used in the context of agriculture to describe early ripening fruits or crops. It can also be used to describe a person, particularly a child, who is precocious or forward.  | El melón está adelantadillo este año.
