exclusivistic: /ɪkˌskluːsɪˈvɪstɪk/ | adj. | 排外的；具有排他性的；限于少数人的 | Characterized by exclusiveness; tending to exclude others. |  The country club had a reputation for being exclusivistic and only accepting members from wealthy families.  这个乡村俱乐部以排外而闻名，只接受来自富裕家庭的会员。

|  Her exclusivistic social circle consisted primarily of artists and musicians. 她的排外社交圈主要由艺术家和音乐家组成。 | 近义词: exclusive, restrictive, cliquish, selective | 反义词: inclusive, open, welcoming, egalitarian | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From exclusive + -istic.  "Exclusive" comes from the Latin "excludere" meaning "to shut out." "-istic" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
