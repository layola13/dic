lanneret:/ˈlænərɪt/
n.
雄隼;
A male lanner falcon.
The lanneret soared high above the desert. 雄隼在沙漠上空翱翔。

近义词:falcon, hawk
反义词:prey


词性:noun

词源: From Old French *laneret, diminutive of lanier (“lanner falcon”), from Medieval Latin lanarius, from Latin lanius (“shrike, butcher-bird”). Lanner is ultimately of Germanic origin.
