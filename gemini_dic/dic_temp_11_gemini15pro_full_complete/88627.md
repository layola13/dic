human:/ˈhjuːmən/ | adj. n. | 人类的；人 |  Relating to or characteristic of people or mankind. A human being, especially a person as distinguished from an animal. |  Humans are social creatures. 人类是群居动物。|  She showed her human side by crying at the movie.  她在电影院哭了，展现了她人性的一面。| synonyms: mortal, person, individual, being, soul | antonyms: inhuman, animal, divine | adjective, noun
