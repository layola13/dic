carboy:/ˈkɑːrˌbɔɪ/| n. |大玻璃瓶，大肚瓶|A large glass bottle with a narrow neck, encased in wicker or other protective material, used for holding liquids, especially acids or corrosive substances.  A carboy is typically used for transporting or storing large volumes of liquids.|The chemist carefully poured the acid into the carboy. 化学家小心地将酸倒入大肚瓶中。

The winery stored its wine in large carboys. 酿酒厂将葡萄酒储存在大肚瓶中。|近义词：demijohn, flagon, jug|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps vial or flask for indicating smaller size) |词性:noun
