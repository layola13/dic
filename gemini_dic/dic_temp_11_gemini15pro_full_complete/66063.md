The council exauthorated the mayor after the scandal.  丑闻之后，市议会剥夺了市长的权力。


近义词: depose, dethrone, oust, remove, dismiss | 反义词: empower, authorize, install |词性: 动词


词源:  From Late Latin *exauthoratus, past participle of *exauthorare, from Latin ex- 'out of' + auctor 'author, originator, creator'.
