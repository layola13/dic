misliking:/mɪsˈlaɪkɪŋ/
n.
不喜欢，厌恶
a feeling of aversion or antipathy
She had a strong misliking for loud noises. 她非常讨厌噪音。

近义词:dislike, aversion, antipathy, distaste
反义词:liking, fondness, affection

词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *mislikyng, equivalent to mis- + liking.  Compare Dutch mislijking (“displeasure”).
