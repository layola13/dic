exsufflate:/ˌɛksˈsʌfleɪt/
verb
吹出；呼出；通过吹气排出
to blow out; to expel by blowing.
The doctor exsufflated the patient's lungs to remove the blockage. 医生通过吹气排出病人肺部的阻塞物。
The diver exsufflated all the air from his lungs before surfacing. 潜水员在浮出水面之前把肺里的空气全部呼出。
近义词: exhale, expel, blow out
反义词: inhale, inspire
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exsufflare, from ex- (out) + sufflare (to blow, puff), from sub- (under) + flare (to blow).
