intermittent:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/| adj.|断断续续的|stopping and starting at irregular intervals.|The intermittent rain showers made it difficult to plan a picnic. 断断续续的阵雨使得难以计划野餐。|近义词：irregular, fitful, broken|反义词:continuous, constant, persistent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intermittens, present participle of intermittere "to discontinue, interrupt, leave undone," from inter- "between" + mittere "to send, let go."
