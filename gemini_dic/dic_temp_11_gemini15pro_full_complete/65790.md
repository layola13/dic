evict:/ɪˈvɪkt/| v. |驱逐，逐出|To force someone to leave a place, especially a property they are renting or occupying illegally.|The landlord threatened to evict the tenants for not paying rent. 房东威胁要驱逐不付房租的租客。|The family was evicted from their home after failing to make mortgage payments.  这家人因未能支付抵押贷款而被赶出家门。|synonyms: expel, oust, remove, dispossess|antonyms: admit, welcome, house|verb
