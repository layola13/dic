collegiate:/kəˈliːdʒiət/| adj.|大学的；学院的；大学生的|Relating to a college or its students.|例句：She participated in collegiate sports. 她参加了大学运动。|近义词：academic, university|反义词:non-collegiate, professional|词性:adjective

collegiate:/kəˈliːdʒiət/| n.|大学生|A student in a college.|例句：The collegiates gathered for the homecoming game.  大学生们聚集在一起参加返校节比赛。|近义词：undergraduate, student|反义词:graduate, professor|词性:noun
