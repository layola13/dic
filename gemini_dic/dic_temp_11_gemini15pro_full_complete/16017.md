banam:/ˈbænəm/| n.|禁令，禁止，逐出教会|A ban is an official or legal prohibition. |The city has a ban on smoking in public places.  该市禁止在公共场所吸烟。|Example: There is a ban on using cell phones during the exam.  考试期间禁止使用手机。|Synonyms: prohibition, embargo, restriction, interdiction|Antonyms: permission, allowance, authorization|noun
