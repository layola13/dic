monogoneutic:/ˌmɒnəɡəˈnjuːtɪk/
adjective
单性生殖的
Reproducing asexually, with the offspring developing from a single cell.
例句：Some species of aphids exhibit monogoneutic reproduction during certain parts of the year. 一些蚜虫种类在一年中的某些时候会表现出单性生殖。

例句：Monogoneutic reproduction allows for rapid population growth in favorable conditions.  单性生殖有利于在适宜条件下种群的快速增长。

近义词：asexual, parthenogenetic
反义词：sexual, digenetic
词性：adjective
