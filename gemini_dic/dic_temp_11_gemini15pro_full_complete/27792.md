cannister:/ˈkænɪstər/ (US), /ˈkænɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |罐，小罐，筒|A metal container, typically cylindrical and airtight, used for storing dry goods, especially tea, coffee, or flour.|例句：The tea is kept in a cannister. 茶叶放在一个罐子里。

例句：She opened the cannister of flour. 她打开了面粉罐。

近义词：container, canister, tin, box, jar |反义词: None|词性: noun
