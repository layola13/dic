advocatrice: /ˌædvəˈkeɪtrɪs/ | n. | 女拥护者，女提倡者 | A female advocate; a woman who pleads the cause of another, especially in a court of law. |  She was a fierce advocatrice for women's rights. 她是一位激烈的女权拥护者。|  The advocatrice presented a compelling case for her client.  这位女辩护人为她的客户提出了令人信服的案例。| 近义词: advocate, champion, supporter, proponent | 反义词: opponent, antagonist, detractor | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *advocatrice*, feminine of *avocat* (“advocate”), from Latin *advocatus*, past participle of *advocare* (“to call to one's aid”).  Ultimately from *ad-* ("to") + *vocare* ("to call").
