intergossips: /ˌɪntərˈɡɑːsɪps/ | n. | 私下闲聊，流言蜚语 |  Exchange of gossip between people, especially in a secretive or private manner.  This word is not standard English and is likely a neologism formed by combining "inter-" (between) and "gossips" (plural of gossip).  It suggests a reciprocal exchange of gossip. |  "The intergossips amongst the staff created a tense atmosphere." 员工之间的私下闲聊制造了紧张的气氛。| 近义词: whispers, rumors, backbiting, tattling | 反义词: open communication, transparency | 词性: 名词
