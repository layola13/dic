menorah:/məˈnɔːrə/| n.|烛台；九枝烛台；七枝烛台|A candelabrum with seven or nine branches, used in Jewish worship, especially during Hanukkah.|The family lit the menorah on the first night of Hanukkah.  家人在光明节的第一晚点燃了烛台。|The menorah is a symbol of Jewish faith. 烛台是犹太信仰的象征。|synonyms: candelabrum, candelabra|antonyms: None|noun
