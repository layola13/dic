mosaical:/məʊˈzeɪɪkəl/ | adj. | 马赛克的；拼花的；五彩缤纷的 | Resembling or relating to a mosaic; composed of diverse elements. | The artist created a mosaical pattern using tiny pieces of colored glass. 艺术家用小块彩色玻璃创作了一个马赛克图案。 | The church's altar features a mosaical design depicting biblical scenes. 教堂的祭坛上有一幅描绘圣经场景的马赛克图案。 | synonyms: tessellated, inlaid, variegated, kaleidoscopic | antonyms: uniform, homogeneous, monochrome | adjective
