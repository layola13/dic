flighting:/ˈflaɪtɪŋ/| n.| 试播;  断续性广告;  (鸟类)飞翔, (尤指)迁徙|A period of advertising for a product or service, interspersed with periods of no advertising at all.  The act of birds flying, especially migrating.|例句：The company uses flighting as an advertising strategy.  这家公司采用断续性广告作为广告策略。

例句：The flighting of geese south for the winter is a spectacular sight.  大雁南飞过冬的景象蔚为壮观。|近义词：advertising campaign, scheduling, migration (for birds)|反义词:continuous advertising,  hibernation (for some animals)|词性:noun
