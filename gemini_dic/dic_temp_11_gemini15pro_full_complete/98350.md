insipidness:/ɪnˈsɪpɪdnəs/| n. |平淡无味；乏味；无趣|Lack of flavor, zest, or interest; dullness.|The insipidness of the movie made me want to leave early. 这部电影的乏味让我想要早点离开。|Synonyms: blandness, dullness, vapidity, flatness|Antonyms: excitement, zest, flavor, piquancy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From insipid + -ness.  Insipid comes from Latin *insipidus, from in- "not" + sapidus "savory, tasty," from sapere "to taste."
