buoyancy:/ˈbɔɪənsi/ | n. | 浮力，轻快，乐观 | The ability to float in water or other fluids; a feeling of cheerfulness or optimism. | The boat's buoyancy kept it afloat. 这艘船的浮力使它漂浮着。| synonyms: lightness, floatability, cheerfulness, optimism | antonyms: heaviness, negativity, pessimism | noun

buoyant:/ˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. | 有浮力的，轻快的，乐观的 | Able to float easily; cheerful and optimistic. | The buoyant economy boosted investor confidence.  蓬勃发展的经济提振了投资者信心。| synonyms: floating, lighthearted, cheerful, optimistic | antonyms: sinking, heavy, depressed, pessimistic | adjective
