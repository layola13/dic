asiphonata:/ˌeɪsaɪfəˈneɪtə/ | n. |無管類|A taxonomic group of insects, specifically mollusks, characterized by the absence of a siphon.  They are a subclass of the class Gastropoda.|Asiphonata are a diverse group, including many common land snails.|近义词:Pulmonata (sometimes used synonymously, but technically different)|反义词:Siphonata|noun
