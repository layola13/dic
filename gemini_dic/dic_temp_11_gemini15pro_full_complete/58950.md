dustiest:/ˈdʌstiɪst/| adj.|最多灰尘的，最脏的|Covered with the most dust; most dirty.|The attic was the dustiest place in the whole house. 阁楼是整栋房子里灰尘最多的地方。|The old bookshop had a dustiest corner filled with forgotten treasures.  这家旧书店里有一个满是灰尘的角落，里面堆满了被遗忘的宝藏。|近义词：dirtiest, grimiest|反义词：cleanest, purest|词性:形容词
词源: From dust + -y + -est.  "Dust" comes from Old English *dust, of Germanic origin. The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
