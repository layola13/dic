intracultural:/ˌɪntrəˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|文化内的，同一文化内的|Occurring within a single culture; involving members of the same culture.|例句：Intracultural communication is communication between people from the same culture.  文化内交流是指来自同一文化的人们之间的交流。

例句：Intracultural marriages are more common than intercultural marriages.  文化内婚姻比跨文化婚姻更普遍。|近义词：domestic, within a culture|反义词：intercultural, cross-cultural|词性:adjective

Etymology:
intra- (Latin) = within + cultural (English) = relating to culture
