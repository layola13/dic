geomagnetic:/ˌdʒiːoʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
地磁的
Relating to the magnetic field of the Earth.
The geomagnetic storm disrupted radio communications.  地磁暴扰乱了无线电通讯。

The geomagnetic field protects us from harmful solar radiation. 地磁场保护我们免受有害的太阳辐射。
Synonym: terrestrial magnetic, Earth's magnetic
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-geomagnetic" could be used contextually)
词性: adjective
