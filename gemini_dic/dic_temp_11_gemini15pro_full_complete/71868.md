fleshliness:/ˈflɛʃɪnɪs/| n.|肉感; 豐滿 |The fleshliness of the fruit suggested it was ripe.  这种水果的肉感表明它已经成熟了。|The painting captured the fleshliness of the human form. 这幅画捕捉到了人体的肉感。|近义词：plumpness, fullness, succulence|反义词：thinness, leanness, boniness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fleschlinesse*, from Old English *flæsclīcnes*, from *flæsc* (flesh) + *-līc* (-ly) + *-nes* (-ness).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flaiskaz* (flesh), from Proto-Indo-European *plēḱ-sk-/*pleḱ-sk-* (flat, thin piece, board).
