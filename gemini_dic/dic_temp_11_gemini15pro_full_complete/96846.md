inexactitude:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzæktɪtjuːd/

不精确，不准确
lack of exactness or precision.
例句：The inexactitude of the measurements led to flawed results. 测量的不准确导致了有缺陷的结果。

例句：There's a degree of inexactitude in these figures. 这些数字有些许不精确。

近义词：inaccuracy, imprecision, vagueness, error
反义词：accuracy, precision, exactness, correctness
