inclusive-of:/ɪnˈkluːsɪv ˌʌv/ | adj. | 包含的，包括在内的 | Including; taking everything into account.  | The price is inclusive of tax. 价格包含税款。 |  The rent is inclusive of all utilities. 租金包含所有水电费。 | 近义词: including, comprising, encompassing | 反义词: exclusive-of, excluding | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "includere" (to shut in, enclose) + "-ive" (forming adjectives) + "of".
