Botswanan: /ˌbɒtsˈwɑːnən/ | adj. n. | 博茨瓦纳的；博茨瓦纳人 |  Relating to Botswana, its people, or their language.  A person from Botswana or of Botswanan descent. | Example:  The Botswanan landscape is stunning. 博茨瓦纳的风景令人惊叹。  She is a Botswanan citizen.  她是博茨瓦纳公民。 | Synonym: Motswana (for a person),  | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a nationality) | Adjective, Noun

Etymology: Derived from "Botswana," the name of the country.  "Botswana" itself comes from "Batswana," the name of the dominant ethnic group, the Tswana people.
