dweeby:/ˈdwiːbi/| adj.|呆子气的，书呆子气的|Someone who is dweeby is considered socially awkward, unfashionable, or overly intellectual.  They might be intensely interested in hobbies or academic pursuits that are not considered mainstream.|例句：He was a dweeby kid who loved computers. 他是个喜欢电脑的呆子小孩。

例句：She had a dweeby obsession with collecting stamps. 她痴迷于集邮，显得有点呆。|近义词：nerdy, geeky, awkward, uncool|反义词：cool, popular, stylish|词性:形容词
