cottonwoods:/ˈkɒtnˌwʊdz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtnˌwʊdz/ (US)| n.|棉白杨，三叶杨|A fast-growing North American poplar tree with seeds covered in white cottony hairs.|例句：The cottonwoods along the river shimmered in the breeze. 河边的棉白杨在微风中闪闪发光。

cottonwoods:| n.|棉白杨林|A stand or group of cottonwood trees.|例句：We picnicked in the shade of the cottonwoods. 我们在棉白杨林的树荫下野餐。

cottonwood:/ˈkɒtnˌwʊd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtnˌwʊd/ (US)| n.|棉白杨木|The wood of the cottonwood tree.|例句：Cottonwood is a lightweight wood often used for making boxes. 棉白杨木是一种轻质木材，常用于制作盒子。
