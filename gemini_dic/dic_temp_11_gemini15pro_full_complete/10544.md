appraisements: /əˈpreɪzmənts/ | n. | 评估，估价，评价 | An official valuation of something. The act of assessing the value or quality of something. |  The insurance company ordered appraisements of the damaged property. 保险公司要求对受损财产进行估价。

The appraisements of the antique furniture varied widely. 古董家具的估价差异很大。| 近义词: valuations, assessments, estimations | 反义词:  None (lack of appraisal isn't a direct opposite) | 词性: Noun
