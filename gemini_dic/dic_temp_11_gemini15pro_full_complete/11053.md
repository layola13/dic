arch-flamen: /ˈɑːrtʃˈfleɪmən/ | n. | 祭司长；大祭司 |  The chief priest of a particular Roman deity.  It combines "arch-" (chief) and "flamen" (priest). | The arch-flamen of Jupiter was the most important priest in ancient Rome.  古罗马的朱庇特大祭司是最重要的祭司。 | Synonym: high priest, chief priest | Antonym: layman, layperson | Noun
