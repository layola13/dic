corroborators:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtərz/
|n.|证实者；确证者；支持者
|People who corroborate something; those who provide supporting evidence or confirmation.|
|Several corroborators came forward to support her alibi.  几位证人出面证实了她的不在场证明。|
|The police are looking for corroborators to verify his story.  警方正在寻找证人来证实他的说法。|
|witnesses, verifiers, supporters, confirmers|
|contradictors, disputants, challengers|
