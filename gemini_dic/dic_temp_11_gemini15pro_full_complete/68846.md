fashionableness:/ˈfæʃənəblnəs/
n.
时尚；流行；时髦
The state or quality of being fashionable.
Her fashionableness was evident in her stylish attire. 她的时髦体现在她时尚的着装上。
近义词：style, trendiness, chic, vogue
反义词：unfashionableness, dowdiness, frumpiness


ETYMOLOGY->
From fashionable + -ness.  "fashionable" comes from the Old French "faschonable," ultimately derived from Latin "factio" (a making, a doing).
