nonfissility:/ˌnɒnfɪsɪləti/
n.
不可裂变性
The property of not being fissile, meaning the material cannot sustain a nuclear chain reaction.
The nonfissility of the material made it safe for transport.  这种材料的不可裂变性使其可以安全运输。
Uranium-238 has low nonfissility and can be used in nuclear reactors. 铀238具有低的不可裂变性，可用于核反应堆。
inertness, stability
fissility, fissile
noun
