dissenters:/dɪˈsentərz/ | n. |持异议者，反对者|A person who disagrees with a prevailing opinion, belief, or policy.  Those who refuse to conform to an established doctrine, especially in religion.|例句：The dissenters were imprisoned for their beliefs. 这些持异议者因其信仰而被监禁。

例句：The government cracked down on the dissenters. 政府镇压了异议人士。|近义词：objectors, nonconformists, protesters, rebels, dissidents |反义词：conformists, supporters, adherents|词性:名词
