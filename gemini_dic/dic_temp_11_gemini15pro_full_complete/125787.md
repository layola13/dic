muriatiferous:/ˌmjʊəriˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含氯化物的；产盐的
Containing or producing chlorides, especially common salt (sodium chloride).
The muriatiferous springs in the region are known for their high salt content.  该地区的含氯化物泉水以其高盐含量而闻名。
The geologist identified the rock formation as muriatiferous. 地质学家将岩层确定为含氯化物的。
近义词: saline, salt-bearing
反义词: freshwater


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *muriātus* (containing brine, pertaining to brine) + -ferous (bearing). *Muriātus* is derived from *muriēs* (brine, seawater).
