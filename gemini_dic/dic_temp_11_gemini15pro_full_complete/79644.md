glossist:/ˈɡlɒsɪst/ | n. |注释者；词汇表编纂者；解释古文难字的人| A person who writes glosses or explanations of words or texts, especially of difficult or obscure words or passages in old or foreign writings.| The glossist provided helpful annotations for the medieval manuscript.  这位注释者为中世纪的手稿提供了有益的注释。| synonyms: commentator, annotator, expounder, interpreter | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps obfuscator - someone who makes things unclear) | noun
