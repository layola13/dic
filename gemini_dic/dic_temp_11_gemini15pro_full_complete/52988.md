dinkly:/ˈdɪŋkli/| adj.|小的，整洁的，精致的|Small, neat, and often fashionable or stylish.  Often used to describe things that are considered cute or charming in a slightly old-fashioned way.|She wore a dinkly little hat to the garden party.|近义词：trim, neat, tidy, dainty, cute|反义词:messy, untidy, sloppy|词性:形容词
词源:Likely a playful formation, possibly influenced by words like "dinky" (small and insignificant) and "twinkly" (sparkling).  The exact origin is uncertain.
