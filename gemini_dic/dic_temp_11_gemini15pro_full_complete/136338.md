nonresidual:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈzɪdʒuəl/
adjective
非残留的，非剩余的
Relating to or denoting a component of a model or measurement that is not explained by the other components.  The nonresidual part of the data represents the systematic effect.
The nonresidual effects of the treatment were minimal. 治疗的非残留效应很小。
non-residual, essential, intrinsic
residual, remaining
