fluorescent:/flɔːˈres(ə)nt/ /flʊˈres(ə)nt/| adj.|荧光的，发荧光的|Emitting light when struck by radiation, especially ultraviolet light.  A fluorescent object absorbs light of one wavelength and emits light of a longer wavelength.|The fluorescent lights buzzed overhead. 顶上的荧光灯发出嗡嗡声。|The fish's scales were fluorescent under the black light. 在黑光灯下，鱼鳞发出荧光。|近义词：luminous, bright, glowing|反义词：dull, dark|词性:形容词

fluorescent:/flɔːˈres(ə)ns/ /flʊˈres(ə)ns/| n.|荧光|The emission of light by a substance that has absorbed light or other electromagnetic radiation.|The scientist studied the fluorescence of the mineral under UV light. 科学家研究了紫外光下矿物的荧光。|The fluorescence of the jellyfish was mesmerizing. 水母的荧光很迷人。|近义词：luminescence, glow|反义词：darkness, opacity|词性:名词
