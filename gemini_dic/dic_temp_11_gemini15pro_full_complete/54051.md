disdainful:/dɪsˈdeɪnfʊl/ /dɪsˈdeɪnf(ə)l/| adj.|轻蔑的，鄙视的，不屑的|Showing contempt or lack of respect.|She cast a disdainful glance at the cheap jewelry. 她轻蔑地瞥了一眼那廉价的珠宝。|The actor gave a disdainful laugh. 演员轻蔑地笑了笑。


|近义词：contemptuous, scornful, haughty, arrogant, supercilious|反义词：respectful, admiring, appreciative, humble, modest|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French desdeigneux, from desdeignier "to disdain," from des- "away" + deignier "to deign," from Latin dignari "to consider worthy," from dignus "worthy."
