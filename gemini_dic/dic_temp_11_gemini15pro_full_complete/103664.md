It describes an action carried out in a cheerful and festive way.|He sang jolliment at the Christmas party. 他在圣诞派对上快乐地唱歌。|The children played jolliment in the snow. 孩子们在雪地里玩得很开心。|近义词：merrily, cheerfully, gaily, joyfully|反义词：sadly, gloomily, morosely|词性:副词

词源:
