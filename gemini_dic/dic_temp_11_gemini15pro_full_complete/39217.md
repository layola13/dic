conglomerate: /kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (UK), /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (US) | n.  | 聚集体，企业集团，砾岩 |A thing formed by conglomeration; a mass or collection. A large corporation formed by merging several smaller and different firms. A coarse-grained sedimentary rock composed of rounded fragments. | The company is a large conglomerate with holdings in various industries. 这家公司是一个大型企业集团，在各个行业都有控股。| synonym: aggregate, corporation, combine, mixture | antonym: component, element, individual | noun

conglomerate: /kənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK), /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (US)| v. |  聚集，凝聚，使…结块 | To gather into a rounded mass or ball; to form or cause to form into a conglomerate. | The company conglomerated several smaller businesses into a single entity.  这家公司将几家小企业合并成一个实体。| synonym: amass, accumulate, consolidate | antonym: disperse, scatter, separate | verb
