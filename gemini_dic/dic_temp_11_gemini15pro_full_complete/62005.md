enamelled:/ɪˈnæmld/ | adj. | 搪瓷的；涂珐琅的；光滑的 | Coated with enamel; having a smooth, glossy surface. | The artist created a beautiful enamelled brooch.  艺术家创作了一枚美丽的珐琅胸针。 |  The enamelled surface of the bathtub was easy to clean. 浴缸的搪瓷表面很容易清洁。 | 近义词: glazed, varnished, lacquered, polished | 反义词: dull, rough, matte | 词性: 形容词

enamel:/ɪˈnæml/ | n. | 珐琅；搪瓷；指甲油 | A glossy substance used to coat metal or pottery for protection or decoration;  a hard, glossy paint used on fingernails. | The antique vase was decorated with intricate enamel work.  这个古董花瓶装饰着复杂的珐琅工艺。| She applied a coat of pink enamel to her nails. 她给指甲涂了一层粉色指甲油。 | 近义词: glaze, lacquer, varnish, polish | 反义词: (n.) none | 词性: 名词

enamel:/ɪˈnæml/ | vt. | 涂珐琅；使光滑 | To coat or decorate with enamel. | The jeweler enamelled the pendant with a vibrant blue.  珠宝商在吊坠上涂上了鲜艳的蓝色珐琅。| The factory enamels thousands of bathtubs every year. 这家工厂每年生产数千个搪瓷浴缸。 | 近义词: glaze, varnish, lacquer, polish | 反义词: (vt.) none | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Old French *esmaillier (Modern French émailler), from Germanic *smaltojan, from *smaltaz "smalt" (a deep blue pigment used in enamel).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *smalt-, related to melt.
