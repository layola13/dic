laxativeness:/ˈlæksətɪvnəs/
noun
通便, 泻药性, 缓泻性
The quality of tending to loosen the bowels.
This tea has a mild laxativeness.  这种茶有轻微的通便作用。
近义词:purgative effect, cathartic effect, aperient effect
反义词:costiveness, astringency
词性:noun

laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/
noun
泻药
A medicine or agent for relieving constipation.
He took a laxative to relieve his constipation. 他服用了泻药来缓解便秘。
近义词:aperient, purgative, cathartic
反义词:antidiarrheal
词性:noun

laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/
adjective
通便的，有泻药作用的
Having the power to loosen the bowels.
Prunes are known for their laxative effect. 西梅以其通便作用而闻名。
近义词:aperient, purgative, cathartic
反义词:constipating
词性:adjective


词源: From Late Latin *laxativus, from Latin laxare "to loosen," from laxus "loose."
