overplans:/ˈoʊvərplænz/ | v. | 过度计划，计划过多 | to plan excessively or in too much detail |  She overplanned the trip, leaving no room for spontaneity. 她把旅行计划得过于详细，没有留下任何即兴发挥的空间。|  He overplanned his weekend and ended up exhausted from trying to do everything. 他把周末计划得满满当当，结果因为想做所有事情而筋疲力尽。| synonyms: overschedule, overorganize | antonyms: underplan, improvise | verb

overplan:/ˈoʊvərplæn/ | n. | 过度计划，过多的计划 | a plan that is excessive or too detailed | Her overplan for the party included a minute-by-minute schedule. 她为派对制定的过度计划包括一个精确到分钟的时间表。 | The overplan was so complex that it became difficult to manage.  这个过度计划太复杂了，难以管理。 | synonyms: overschedule | antonyms: underplan | noun
