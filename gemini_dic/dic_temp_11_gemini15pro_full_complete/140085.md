ofen:/ˈoʊfən/ | adv. | 经常，常常 | Frequently, many times; habitually. | I often go to the library on weekends. 我周末经常去图书馆。 |  She often visits her grandparents. 她经常去看望她的祖父母。| 近义词: frequently, regularly, habitually, commonly | 反义词: rarely, seldom, infrequently | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *often, *ofte, from Old English oft, from Proto-Germanic *uftą (“often”), from Proto-Indo-European *up- (“up; over”). Cognate with Old Frisian oft, Old Saxon oft, Old High German oft, Old Norse opt, Gothic 𐌿𐍆𐍄𐌰 (ufta).
