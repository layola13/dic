heavenwardness:/ˈhɛvənwərdnɪs/
n.
天堂般，天国，向上，升天
The state or quality of being directed toward heaven; spiritual aspiration.
His paintings conveyed a sense of heavenwardness, a yearning for the divine. 他的画作传达了一种天堂般的感觉，一种对神圣的渴望。
近义词：godliness, piety, spirituality, devoutness
反义词：worldliness, materialism, impiety
