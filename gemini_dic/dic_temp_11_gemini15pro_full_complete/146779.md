overidentification:/ˌoʊvərɪˌdentɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |过度认同|The process of associating oneself too closely with another person or group, often leading to the adoption of their values, beliefs, and behaviors, sometimes to an excessive or unhealthy degree.  It can also refer to a statistical bias in econometrics.|
例句：
His overidentification with his favorite sports team led him to paint his entire house in their colors.
他过度认同他最喜欢的运动队，以至于把整个房子都漆成了他们的颜色。

例句：
The therapist suggested that her overidentification with her mother's struggles was hindering her own personal growth.
治疗师认为，她过度认同母亲的挣扎阻碍了她自身的成长。

近义词：empathy, identification, internalization|
反义词: detachment, disassociation|
词性:noun
