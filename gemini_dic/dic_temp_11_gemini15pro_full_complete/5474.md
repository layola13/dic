alon:/əˈlɒn/ | adj. | 独自的，单独的 | Alone means being without other people. |  She lives alone. 她独自一人生活。|  He felt alone in the crowd. 他在人群中感到孤独。 | 近义词：solitary, lonely, lonesome | 反义词: together, accompanied | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *aloon, *al one, from Old English all āna (“quite alone, all one”), equivalent to all + one. Cognate with Scots alane (“alone”), West Frisian allinne (“alone”), Dutch alleen (“only, alone”), German allein (“alone”). More at all, one.
