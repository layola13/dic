copy-the-mail:/ˈkɑːpi ðə meɪl/ |n.|复制邮件|The act of duplicating an email message.  It involves creating an identical copy of the original email, including the sender, recipient, subject, body text, and any attachments.|Please copy-the-mail to everyone in the department. 请将这封邮件复制给部门里的每个人。|近义词:duplicate the email, forward the email|反义词:delete the email|noun
