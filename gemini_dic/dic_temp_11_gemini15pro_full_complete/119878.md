militantly:/ˈmɪlɪtəntli/
词性:adverb
中文翻译:好战地， воинственно (Russian), militantemaniera (Italian)
英文解析:Fighting aggressively for a cause; in a combative manner.
例句:The protestors marched militantly through the streets. 抗议者 воинственно (Russian) 走上街头。

例句:She militantly defended her beliefs. 她好战地捍卫自己的信仰。
近义词:aggressively, combatively, belligerently, fiercely
反义词:peacefully, passively, nonviolently



词源: From the late Middle English word *militaunt* (adjective) meaning "engaged in warfare,"  derived from Latin *militantem*, the present participle of *militare* "to serve as a soldier," from *miles* "soldier." The adverbial form *militantly* emerged later.
