abnegation's:/ˌæbnɪˈɡeɪʃənz/
n.
放弃，克制，自制，舍己
The act of denying oneself something; self-denial.
例句：Her abnegation's depth surprised everyone. 她的舍己程度让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：The monk's life was one of abnegation's purest forms.  这位僧侣的生活是最纯粹的舍己形式之一。

近义词：self-denial, renunciation, sacrifice, forbearance
反义词：indulgence, gratification, self-indulgence
词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:
From Late Latin "abnegationem" (nominative abnegatio), noun of action from past-participle stem of "abnegare" "to deny, renounce," from "ab-" "away" + "negare" "to deny."
