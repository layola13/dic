fog-bound:/ˈfɒɡˌbaʊnd/ (US) /ˈfɒɡˌbaʊnd/ (UK)| adj.|被雾笼罩的；困于雾中的|Unable to move or operate because of thick fog.  Enveloped or surrounded by fog.|The airport was fog-bound for several hours, delaying many flights. 机场被大雾笼罩了几个小时，导致许多航班延误。|The fog-bound ship was unable to enter the harbor. 被雾困住的船无法进入港口。|近义词：fogged in, stranded, delayed|反义词：clear, unhindered|词性:形容词
