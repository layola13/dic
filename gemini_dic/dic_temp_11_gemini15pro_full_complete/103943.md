judicative:/ˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
审判的；具有判断力的
Relating to judgment or the administration of justice.  Pertaining to the act of judging or the power to judge. Having the power to judge.
The judicative functions of the government are performed by the courts. 政府的审判职能由法院执行。
The judge made a judicative decision. 法官做出了审判决定。

近义词:judicial, judgmental
反义词:non-judgmental, impartial
词性:adjective
