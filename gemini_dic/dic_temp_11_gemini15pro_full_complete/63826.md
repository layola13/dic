epidural:/ˌɛpɪˈdjʊərəl/| n. adj.|硬膜外麻醉；硬膜外的|An injection of anesthetic into the space around the spinal cord.  It numbs the lower part of the body and is often used during childbirth.|She had an epidural during labor. 她分娩时使用了硬膜外麻醉。|近义词：spinal block|反义词: general anesthesia|词性:名词,形容词
epidurals: /ˌɛpɪˈdjʊərəlz/ |n.|硬膜外麻醉（复数）|Multiple injections of anesthetic into the space around the spinal cord.|Many women opt for epidurals during childbirth. 许多妇女在分娩时选择硬膜外麻醉。|近义词：spinal blocks|反义词: general anesthetics|词性:名词
