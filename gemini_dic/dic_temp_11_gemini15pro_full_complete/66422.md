executrix: /ɪɡˈzekjətrɪks/ | n. | 女遗嘱执行人 | a woman appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of the will. |  My aunt was named executrix of my grandmother's will. 我的姑姑被指定为我祖母的遗嘱执行人。 |  The executrix is responsible for distributing the assets of the estate. 女遗嘱执行人负责分配遗产的资产。| 近义词: executor (if gender-neutral or male), administrator | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps beneficiary) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English executrice, from Old French executrice, from Latin exsecūtrīx (“female executor”), from exsecūtor (“executor”), from exsequor (“I follow out, execute”), from ex (“out”) + sequor (“I follow”).
