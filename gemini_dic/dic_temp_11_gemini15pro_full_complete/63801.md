epidemiologies:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒiz/
| n. |流行病学|The study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in specified populations, and the application of this study to the control of health problems. |
|例句：Researchers in various epidemiologies are working to understand the spread of the virus. 各个流行病学领域的研究人员正在努力了解这种病毒的传播方式。|
|例句：The epidemiologies of heart disease and cancer are complex and involve many factors. 心脏病和癌症的流行病学是复杂的，涉及许多因素。|
|近义词：epidemiology (singular form)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun (plural)
