chiffon:/ʃɪˈfɒn/ (UK), /ʃɪˈfɑːn/ (US)| n. adj.|雪纺绸，薄绸；雪纺蛋糕|A sheer, lightweight fabric, typically made of silk or nylon. Also, a light and airy cake made with eggs, oil, and flour.|She wore a beautiful chiffon dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的雪纺连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：silk, gauze, muslin|反义词:heavy fabric, thick material|词性:noun, adjective

chiffon:/ʃɪˈfɒn/ (UK), /ʃɪˈfɑːn/ (US)|adj.|雪纺绸制的|Made of chiffon fabric.|The chiffon curtains billowed in the breeze. 雪纺窗帘在微风中飘动。|近义词：silky, gauzy|反义词:thick, heavy|词性:adjective
