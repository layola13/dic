CFC:/ˌsiːɛfˈsiː/| n. |氯氟烃|Chlorofluorocarbon, a type of gas that was used in aerosols and refrigerators, which damages the ozone layer.|The use of CFCs has been banned in many countries because they damage the ozone layer. 氯氟烃在许多国家已被禁用，因为它们会破坏臭氧层。|Synonym: freon|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but alternatives include HFCs and HCFCs)|noun
