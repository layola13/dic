de-intensify:/diːɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪ/
verb
降低强度；缓和；减弱
To reduce the intensity or severity of something.
The government is trying to de-intensify the conflict. 政府正在努力缓和冲突。
The storm began to de-intensify as it moved inland.  风暴向内陆移动时开始减弱。
synonyms: lessen, reduce, diminish, mitigate, alleviate, ease, moderate, temper, subdue, tone down, soften, weaken
antonyms: intensify, escalate, heighten, aggravate, exacerbate
词性: 动词
