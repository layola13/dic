noncommunion:/ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnjən/ | n. |  不圣餐; 不领圣餐; 拒绝圣餐;  |  Abstention from receiving Holy Communion.  It can refer to the state of being excluded from Communion or the act of voluntarily abstaining. |  His noncommunion was a protest against the church's stance on social issues.  他的不领圣餐是对教会在社会问题上立场的抗议。

The church imposed noncommunion on members who refused to adhere to its doctrines. 教会对拒绝遵守其教义的成员施加了不圣餐的处罚。 | 近义词: excommunication, exclusion | 反义词: communion, participation | 词性: 名词
