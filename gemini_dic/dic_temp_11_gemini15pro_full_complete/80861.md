gourmet:/ˈɡʊrmeɪ/ (US) /ˈɡɔːmeɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.|美食家; 美味的|A connoisseur of good food and drink; of high quality and often expensive food and drink.|例句：He is a true gourmet, always seeking out the finest restaurants. 他是一位真正的美食家，总是寻找最好的餐厅。

例句：She prepared a gourmet meal for her guests. 她为客人准备了一顿美味佳肴。|近义词：epicure, connoisseur, foodie|反义词：glutton, gourmand (although gourmand can sometimes be used similarly, it often implies excessive eating)|n. adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gourmet*, from Old French *groumet* "wine merchant's assistant," originally "boy who tasted wine," from *groume* "boy, lad," probably of Germanic origin. The meaning shifted in the 18th century to "connoisseur of good food and drink."
