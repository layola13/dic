lagger:/ˈlæɡər/| n.|落后者，迟缓者；网络延迟者|Someone or something that lags behind or is slow to respond, especially in online gaming or computing.  It can refer to a person experiencing high latency in a video game, causing their actions to be delayed, or to a computer or software that is slow to process information.|He's such a lagger in this game, always a few steps behind. 他在这款游戏中真是个落后者，总是慢几拍。|近义词：straggler, slowpoke, dawdler|反义词：leader, frontrunner|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "lag," meaning "to fall behind."  "Lag" itself likely comes from Old Norse "lakra," meaning "to move slowly."
