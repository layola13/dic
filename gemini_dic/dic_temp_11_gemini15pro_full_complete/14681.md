awaited:/əˈweɪtɪd/| adj.|期待的，等待的|Something that is awaited is expected or looked forward to.|The long-awaited sequel finally hit theaters.  这部期待已久的续集终于上映了。|Example:  The awaited announcement came at last. 姗姗来迟的公告终于发布了。|Synonyms: expected, anticipated, hoped-for|Antonyms: unexpected, unanticipated, unforeseen|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From await + -ed.  Await comes from Middle English awaiten, from a- (prefix meaning "on" or "in") + waiten (to wait).
