overhumanize:/ˌōvərˈhjuːməˌnaɪz/| v. |过度人性化，过分赋予人性|To ascribe human characteristics or motivations to something that is not human, often to an excessive or inappropriate degree.|例句：Some pet owners overhumanize their animals, treating them like children.  一些宠物主人过度人性化他们的动物，把它们当作孩子对待。

例句：Critics argue that the movie overhumanizes the robot, making it too relatable and sentimental.  评论家认为这部电影过度人性化了机器人，使其过于贴近人心和感伤。|近义词：anthropomorphize, personify|反义词:dehumanize, objectify|词性：动词
