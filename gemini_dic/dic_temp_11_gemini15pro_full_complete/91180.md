hydrarthrosis:/ˌhaɪdrɑːrˈθroʊsɪs/| n. |关节积水|An abnormal accumulation of watery fluid in a joint cavity.|Example: The doctor diagnosed hydrarthrosis in her knee after the injury.  医生诊断她受伤后膝盖积水。
Example:  Hydrarthrosis can be a symptom of several underlying conditions. 关节积水可能是几种潜在疾病的症状。|近义词：joint effusion, water on the knee (specifically for the knee)|反义词: None|词性: noun

词源:
From hydro- + arthr- + -osis.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" meaning "water". "Arthr-" comes from Greek "arthron" meaning "joint". "-osis" is a suffix indicating a condition or process.
