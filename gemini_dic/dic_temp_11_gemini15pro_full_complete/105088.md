kermesse:/kərˈmɛs/| n.|乡村节日；游乐会；义卖市场|A kermesse is a local festival, often including entertainment, games, and stalls selling food and goods, typically held for fundraising or charitable purposes.|The church held a kermesse to raise money for repairs.|近义词：fair, fete, carnival, festival|反义词:|noun
