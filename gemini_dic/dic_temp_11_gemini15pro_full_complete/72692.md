focalises:/ˈfoʊkəlaɪzɪz/
verb
聚焦，集中
concentrates attention on a particular aspect or point of view
The novel focalises on the experiences of a single character.  这部小说集中讲述了一个人物的经历。
focuses, centers, concentrates
scatters, diffuses
