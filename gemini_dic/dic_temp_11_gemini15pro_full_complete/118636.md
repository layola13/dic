messily:/ˈmesɪli/| adv.|凌乱地，杂乱地|In a messy or disordered manner.  Characterized by a lack of neatness or organization.|He ate messily, dropping crumbs everywhere. 他吃得很邋遢，到处掉面包屑。|近义词：sloppily, untidily, carelessly|反义词：neatly, tidily, orderly|词性:副词

Etymology: From "messy" + "-ly."  "Messy" comes from the Old French word "messier," meaning "to mix, stir, or jumble."
