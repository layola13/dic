obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/| n.|障碍，阻碍，阻塞; 障碍物|Something that blocks or gets in the way; the act of blocking something.|The fallen tree caused an obstruction in the road. 倒下的树造成了道路阻塞。|近义词：obstacle, barrier, hindrance, impediment|反义词:clearance, aid, assistance|noun

obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/| vt.|阻碍，妨碍，阻塞|To block or get in the way of something.|The protesters obstructed the entrance to the building. 抗议者堵住了大楼的入口。|近义词：block, hinder, impede, prevent|反义词:aid, assist, clear, facilitate|verb
