darer's:/ˈdɛərərz/
n.
挑战者们的；敢于冒险的人的
belonging to or associated with someone who dares (to do something).
The darer's reward was substantial. 挑战者的奖励是巨大的。
近义词:risk-taker's, adventurer's
反义词:coward's, timid person's
词性:名词所有格


词源:
From "darer" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Darer" comes from the verb "dare," from Old English "durran," meaning "to dare."
