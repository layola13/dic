misreports:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrts/
|v. (third-person singular simple present misreports, present participle misreporting, simple past and past participle misreported)|错误地报告；误报|To give a false or inaccurate account of something.|The media misreported the facts of the case. 媒体错误地报道了案件的事实。|misstates, falsifies, distorts|reports accurately, represents truthfully|verb


misreports:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrts/
|n. (plural misreports)|错误报告；误报|A false or inaccurate account of something.|The newspaper issued a retraction for the misreports. 报纸就错误报道发布了撤回声明。|misstatements, inaccuracies, distortions|accurate reports, truthful accounts|noun
