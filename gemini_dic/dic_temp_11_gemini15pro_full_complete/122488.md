moccasined:/ˈmɒkəsɪnd/ /ˈmɑːkəsɪnd/ | adj. | 穿着鹿皮鞋的，软皮鞋的 | Wearing moccasins; (figuratively) moving lightly and silently. |  He walked moccasined through the forest. 他穿着软皮鞋走过森林。| 近义词：shod, slippered | 反义词：barefoot | 词性:形容词

moccasin:/ˈmɒkəsɪn/ /ˈmɑːkəsɪn/ | n. | 鹿皮鞋，软皮鞋 | A soft leather shoe, originally worn by Native Americans, typically made of deerskin or other soft leather and consisting of a sole and sides made of a single piece of leather stitched together at the top. | He bought a new pair of moccasins. 他买了一双新的软皮鞋。 | 近义词：slipper, shoe | 反义词：boot | 词性:名词
