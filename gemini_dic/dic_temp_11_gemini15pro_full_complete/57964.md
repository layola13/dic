drippings:/ˈdrɪpɪŋz/
n.
油滴，滴下的液体
fat and juices that drip from roasting meat
The roast chicken produced delicious drippings. 烤鸡流出了美味的油滴。

近义词: drippings, leavings, remnants
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solids" in a cooking context)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From drip (v.) + -ing + -s (plural).  The verb "drip" comes from Old English *dryppan, from Proto-Germanic *druppan, of uncertain origin.
