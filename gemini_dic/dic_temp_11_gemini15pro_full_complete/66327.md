excretive:/ɪkˈskriːtɪv/
adjective
排泄的; 分泌的
related to or involved in the process of excretion or secretion
The kidneys play an excretive role, filtering waste products from the blood. 肾脏起着排泄的作用，过滤血液中的废物。
近义词: secretory, excretory
反义词: retentive
词性: adjective

excretion:/ɪkˈskriːʃn/
noun
排泄; 排泄物
the process of eliminating or expelling waste matter from the body
The excretion of toxins is essential for maintaining health. 排出毒素对维持健康至关重要。
近义词: elimination, discharge
反义词: absorption, retention
词性: noun
