cerebritis: /ˌsɛrɪˈbraɪtɪs/
noun
脑炎
Inflammation of the cerebrum, the largest part of the brain.
The patient presented with symptoms suggestive of cerebritis, including headache, fever, and altered mental status.  病人出现提示脑炎的症状，包括头痛、发烧和精神状态改变。

encephalitis, brain inflammation
