outvanishes:/ˌaʊtvæˈnɪʃɪz/
verb
消失得比...更快；在消失方面胜过
to vanish or disappear more completely or quickly than something else
The magician outvanished his rival, leaving the audience completely baffled.  魔术师比他的对手消失得更快，让观众完全迷惑了。
近义词:eclipse, surpass, outdo
反义词:appear, materialize
