As a noun, it refers to the Baroque style or period. | The palace is a masterpiece of baroque architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克建筑的杰作。 | synonyms: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant | antonyms: simple, plain, austere | adjective, noun

It eventually became associated with the artistic style considered overly ornate and extravagant by later critics.  |  The composer's baroko compositions are known for their complex harmonies. 这位作曲家的巴洛克作品以其复杂的和声而闻名。 | synonyms: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant | antonyms: simple, plain, austere | adjective, noun
