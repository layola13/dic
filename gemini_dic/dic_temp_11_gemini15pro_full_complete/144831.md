over-literal:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪtərəl/| adj.|过于字面的，过度照字面解释的|Taking something too literally; interpreting something word for word without considering the context or intent.|She has an over-literal interpretation of the rules and misses the point sometimes. 她对规则的理解过于字面，有时会忽略重点。|例句：He's so over-literal, he doesn't understand sarcasm. 他太拘泥于字面意思了，不懂反讽。|近义词：literalistic, literal-minded|反义词：figurative, metaphorical|词性:形容词
