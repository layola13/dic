cassia: /ˈkæʃə/ /ˈkæsiə/ | n. | 桂皮；决明子；肉桂 | An evergreen tree or shrub of the genus Cinnamomum, whose bark is used as a spice.  A plant of the genus Cassia, especially the senna plant. | The cassia tree is native to Southeast Asia. 桂皮树原产于东南亚。
