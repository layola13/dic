gargantuan:/ɡɑːrˈɡæntʃuən/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Of immense size, volume, or capacity; gigantic; enormous.|例句：They had a gargantuan feast after the harvest.  他们收获后举行了一场盛大的宴会。

例句：The company faced a gargantuan task in rebuilding its reputation.  这家公司面临着重建声誉的艰巨任务。|近义词：gigantic, enormous, colossal, immense, massive|反义词：tiny, minuscule, small, diminutive|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From François Rabelais's Gargantua, a giant known for his enormous appetite, ultimately from Spanish/Italian *garganta ("throat").
