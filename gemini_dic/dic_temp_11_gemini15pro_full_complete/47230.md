dazzle:/ˈdæzl/ /ˈdæzəl/| v. n.|使目眩，耀眼；使惊叹|To impress or overwhelm with brilliance or splendor; to blind temporarily with a sudden bright light.|The fireworks dazzled the children. 烟花使孩子们眼花缭乱。|近义词：blind, amaze, impress, overpower|反义词:bore, disappoint|词性:动词, 名词

dazzle:/ˈdæzl/ /ˈdæzəl/| n.|耀眼的光；令人眼花缭乱的东西|A sudden bright light that blinds temporarily; something that impresses or overwhelms with brilliance.|The dazzle of the headlights made it hard to see.  车头灯的强光使人难以看清。|近义词：glare, brilliance, splendor|反义词:dullness, darkness|词性:名词
