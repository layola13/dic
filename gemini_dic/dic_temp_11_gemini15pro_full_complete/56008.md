do-goodings:/ˈduːɡʊdɪŋz/
|n.|做好事的人；爱管闲事的人|A person who tries to do good things, often in a way that is annoying or unwanted.|例句：The town was full of do-goodings trying to improve everyone's lives.  小镇上到处都是些爱管闲事，想要改善每个人生活的人。|近义词：altruist, philanthropist, humanitarian|反义词：malefactor, wrongdoer|Noun
