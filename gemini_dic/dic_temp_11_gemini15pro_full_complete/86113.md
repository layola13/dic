heartbroken:/ˈhɑːrtˌbroʊkən/ (US) /ˈhɑːtˌbrəʊkən/ (UK)| adj. | 心碎的，伤心的 | Overwhelmed by grief or disappointment. Feeling extreme sadness, especially due to a loss or rejection in love. | She was heartbroken when her fiancé left her. 她被未婚夫抛弃后心碎了。

He was heartbroken after his dog died.  他的狗死后，他非常伤心。

近义词：devastated, grief-stricken, inconsolable, crushed | 反义词：joyful, happy, elated | 词性: adjective
