adversifoliate:/ˌædvɜːrsɪˈfoʊliɪt/ | adj. | 对生叶的；背腹向的 | Having leaves arranged in pairs on opposite sides of the stem, but with successive pairs at right angles to each other. | The adversifoliate arrangement of the leaves allows for maximum light capture. 对生叶的排列方式可以最大限度地吸收光线。 | opposite-leaved, decussate | alternate, spiral | adjective
