illustrate:/ˈɪləstreɪt/ (US), /ˈɪləstreɪt/ (UK)| v. |说明，阐明；给…加插图|to make something clearer or easier to understand by giving examples or using pictures|The lecturer illustrated his point with a diagram. 讲师用图表来说明他的观点。|clarify, explain, demonstrate, exemplify|obscure, confuse|verb
illustrative:/ɪˈlʌstrətɪv/ (US), /ɪˈlʌstrətɪv/ (UK)| adj. |说明性的，解释性的|serving as an example or explanation|The examples given are illustrative of the problems faced by many companies.  给出的例子说明了许多公司面临的问题。|explanatory, exemplary|unrepresentative|adjective
illustration:/ˌɪləˈstreɪʃn/ (US), /ˌɪləˈstreɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |说明，例证；插图|an example that explains or makes something clear; a picture in a book, magazine, etc.|This is a clear illustration of the importance of regular exercise.  这清楚地说明了定期锻炼的重要性。|example, instance, picture, diagram|obscurity, confusion|noun
