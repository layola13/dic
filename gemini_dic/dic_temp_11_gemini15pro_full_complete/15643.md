baits:/beɪts/| n. v.|诱饵，饵；引诱|Something used to attract and catch fish or animals, or to lure someone into a trap. |The fisherman used worms as bait. 渔夫用蠕虫作诱饵。|Synonyms: lure, decoy, enticement|Antonyms: deterrent, repellent|noun
baits:/beɪts/| v.|装饵；引诱|To put bait on (a hook, trap, etc.); to lure or entice. |He baited the hook with a worm. 他用蠕虫装饵。|Synonyms: lure, entice, tempt|Antonyms: deter, repel|verb
