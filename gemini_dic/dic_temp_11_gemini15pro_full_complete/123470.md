monospore:/ˈmɒnəspɔːr/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəspɔːr/ (US)| n. |单孢子|A spore produced singly, not in groups or chains. A single spore that can develop into a new individual.|The fern reproduced via monospores.|近义词:spore|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "multicellular propagule")|词性:noun

monospore:|/ˈmɒnəspɔːr/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəspɔːr/ (US)| adj. |单孢子的|Producing or consisting of single spores.|The monospore culture allowed for isolation of specific genetic strains. |近义词:uniporous|反义词:multispored|词性:adjective
