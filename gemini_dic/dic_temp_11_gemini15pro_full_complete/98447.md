inspirable:/ɪnˈspaɪrəbl/| adj.|可激发灵感的；令人振奋的|Capable of being inspired; serving or tending to inspire.|Her courage is inspirable. 她的勇气令人振奋。|近义词：inspiring, motivating, uplifting|反义词：uninspiring, demotivating, depressing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From inspire + -able.  Inspire comes from Latin inspirare "to breathe into, inspire," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."
