overlargely:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdʒli/
adv.
过大地，过于广泛地
To an excessive degree; too extensively.
The issue was overlargely debated in the media.  这个问题在媒体上被过分广泛地讨论了。
The success was overlargely attributed to one person.  成功被过分地归功于一个人。
近义词: excessively, unduly, overly
反义词: insufficiently, inadequately
词性: adverb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "large" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
