chaetiferous:/kiːˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
具刚毛的；有刺毛的
Bearing bristles or setae.
例句：The chaetiferous caterpillar crawled across the leaf.  这浑身刚毛的毛毛虫爬过树叶。

例句：Chaetiferous plants can often be found in arid environments.  多刺毛的植物常见于干旱环境。

近义词：bristly, setaceous, hispid
反义词：smooth, glabrous
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *chaetifer* (bearing bristles), from *chaeta* (bristle) + *-fer* (bearing).  *Chaeta* is from Greek *khaitē* (flowing hair, mane).
