newspaperish:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərɪʃ/
adjective
像报纸的，有报纸风格的
resembling or characteristic of a newspaper, especially in style or content.
Her writing style is quite newspaperish, short and to the point. 她的写作风格很像报纸，简短扼要。
The website has a rather newspaperish layout.  这个网站的布局很像报纸。
synonyms: journalistic, reportorial, newsy
antonyms: literary, fictional, poetic
词性: adjective
