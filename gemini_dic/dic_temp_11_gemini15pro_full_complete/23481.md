brah:/brɑː/| n. |兄弟，哥们|A slang term used to address a male friend, similar to "bro" or "dude". It originated in surfer culture.|例句：Hey brah, what's up? 嘿，兄弟，怎么了？

例句：Brah, that wave was awesome! 哥们，那个浪太棒了！|近义词：bro, dude, pal, buddy, mate|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
