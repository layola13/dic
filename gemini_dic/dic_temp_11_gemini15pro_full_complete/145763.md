overcure: /ˌoʊvərˈkjʊr/ | v. n. | 过度固化，过度硫化；过度治疗 | To cure (rubber, plastic, etc.) for too long a time or at too high a temperature, resulting in undesirable properties.  As a noun, it refers to the state of being overcured. | The rubber seals were overcured and became brittle.  橡胶密封圈过度硫化变脆了。| 近义词: overbake, overcook | 反义词: undercure | 词性: 动词, 名词
