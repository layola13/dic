anthropophagi: /ˌænθrəˈpɒfədʒaɪ/
n.
食人者，食人族
People who eat human flesh; cannibals.
The isolated tribe was rumored to be anthropophagi, shunning all contact with the outside world.  这个与世隔绝的部落被传说是食人族，他们避开与外界的任何接触。
The ancient texts described gruesome rituals practiced by the anthropophagi.  古代文献描述了食人族进行的可怕仪式。
近义词: cannibals, man-eaters
反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a person who practices cannibalism)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *anthropophagos*, from *anthrōpos* "human being" + *phagein* "to eat".
