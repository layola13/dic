cyamellone:/saɪəˈmeləʊn/ | n. | 三聚氰酸三酰胺 | A white, crystalline, heterocyclic compound, C6H6N12O3, formed by condensation of cyanuric acid with melamine. |  Cyamellone is a condensation product of melamine and cyanuric acid. 三聚氰酸三酰胺是三聚氰胺和三聚氰酸的缩合物。|  Cyamellone can be used in the production of flame-retardant materials. 三聚氰酸三酰胺可用于生产阻燃材料。| Synonyms: Melamine cyanurate | Antonyms: None | Noun
