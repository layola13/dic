benthic:/ˈbɛnθɪk/| adj.|底栖的，水底的|Relating to the bottom of a body of water, such as a lake or ocean.|例句：Benthic organisms play a vital role in the aquatic ecosystem. 底栖生物在水生生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The study focused on the benthic community of the coral reef. 这项研究的重点是珊瑚礁的底栖生物群落。|近义词：demersal, bottom-dwelling|反义词：pelagic (远洋的)|词性:形容词
