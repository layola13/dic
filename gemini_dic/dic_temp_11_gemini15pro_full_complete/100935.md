inumbrate:/ɪˈnʌmbreɪt/ | vt. |  遮蔽，使阴暗；使蒙上阴影 | To cast a shadow over; to darken or obscure. |  The clouds inumbrated the valley.  乌云遮蔽了山谷。| synonyms: overshadow, darken, obscure, shade | antonyms: illuminate, brighten, light up | verb

inumbrate:/ɪˈnʌmbreɪt/ | vt. |  预示；暗示 | To foreshadow; to suggest or hint at something. | The king's illness inumbrated the coming war. 国王的病预示着即将到来的战争。 | synonyms: foreshadow, portend, presage | antonyms: clarify, reveal | verb
