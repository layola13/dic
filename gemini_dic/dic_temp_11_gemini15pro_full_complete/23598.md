brambled:/ˈbræmbəld/
adjective
荆棘丛生的；灌木丛生的；缠结的
Covered with or entangled by brambles.
The path was brambled and overgrown.  这条小路荆棘丛生，杂草丛生。
The brambled fence kept the animals in. 荆棘篱笆把动物关在里面。
近义词: thorny, prickly, overgrown, tangled
反义词: clear, open, untangled
词性: adjective

bramble:/ˈbræmbəl/
noun
荆棘；黑莓灌木
A prickly shrub, especially a blackberry bush.
The bramble tore at my clothes as I walked through the woods.  我穿过树林时，荆棘划破了我的衣服。
We picked blackberries from the bramble. 我们从荆棘丛中摘了黑莓。
近义词: thornbush, briar
反义词: (no clear antonym)
词性: noun

词源: From Old English *brǣmel, from Proto-Germanic *brā̄m-ilaz. Related to Dutch braam and German Brombeere.
