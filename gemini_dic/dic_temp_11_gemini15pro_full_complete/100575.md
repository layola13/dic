interxylary:/ˌɪntərˈzaɪləri/| adj.|木间生的，位于木质部之间的|Situated between the woody tissues of a plant, specifically within the xylem.|The interxylary phloem is a characteristic feature of certain plant stems.|近义词：intraxylary (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically refers to within the xylem, not between), intracambial|反义词: extraxylary|词性:形容词
