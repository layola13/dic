depositee:/ˌdɛpɒzɪˈtiː/
n.
受托人；存款人
a person or organization that money is deposited with
The bank acts as a depositee for its customers' funds.  这家银行充当其客户资金的受托人。

Example: The depositee is responsible for safeguarding the deposited funds. 受托人有责任保护存入的资金。

Example:  The depositee has the right to withdraw the money at any time. 存款人有权随时提取这笔钱。

Synonym: beneficiary, recipient, trustee
Antonym: depositor, payer

词性: noun
