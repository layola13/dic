kyrielle:/kɪˈriːəl, kɪˈrjɛl/
|n.|连串，一长串，大量|A long series of things, often of a similar kind.|例句：A kyrielle of complaints followed the announcement. 公告之后，随之而来的是一连串的抱怨。|例句：The museum boasts a kyrielle of Impressionist paintings.  这家博物馆以收藏大量印象派绘画而自豪。|近义词：litany, string, series, succession, abundance, multitude, profusion|反义词:lack, scarcity, dearth|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *kyrielle*, from Old French *kiriele*, *kireele*, from Medieval Latin *kyriele*, from Ecclesiastical Latin *Kyrie eleison* ("Lord, have mercy"), a liturgical prayer.
