croakily:/ˈkroʊkɪli/| adv.|嘶哑地，呱呱地|In a croaking voice; hoarsely.  Describes a sound that is rough, low, and broken, like the sound a frog makes.|The old man croakily recounted his tale of woe. 老人嘶哑地讲述了他的悲惨故事。|Synonyms: hoarsely, raspingly, huskily|Antonyms: clearly, smoothly, melodiously|Adverb
