ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gripan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *grīpaną (“to grip, seize”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰreib- (“to seize, grasp”). Cognate with Dutch grijpen (“to seize, grab”), German greifen (“to seize, grip”), Swedish gripa (“to seize, grip”).
