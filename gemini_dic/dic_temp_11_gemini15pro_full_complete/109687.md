leucoxene:/ˈluːkɒksiːn/| n. |白钛石，白钛矿|A general term for a mixture of titanium oxides (principally TiO2) derived from the alteration of ilmenite or other titanium-bearing minerals.|Leucoxene is a common accessory mineral in many igneous and metamorphic rocks.|近义词:ilmenite alteration product, titanium dioxide|反义词:|名词
