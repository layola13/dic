appraise: /əˈpreɪz/ (US) /əˈpreɪz/ (UK)| vt. | 评估，鉴定，估价 | To assess the value or quality of something. |  The bank will appraise the house before approving the loan. 银行会在批准贷款前对房屋进行估价。

The expert appraised the painting at $1 million. 专家估计这幅画价值100万美元。

近义词: assess, evaluate, estimate, judge | 反义词:  underestimate, undervalue |词性: 动词
