discage:/dɪsˈkeɪdʒ/
verb
释放，解雇；免除(义务)
To release from confinement or an obligation.
The king discaged the prisoner. 国王释放了囚犯。
synonyms: release, liberate, free, discharge
antonyms: imprison, confine, restrain
词性: 动词
