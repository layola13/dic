ecoterrorism:/ˈiːkəʊˈtɛrərɪzəm/ | n. |生态恐怖主义|The use of violence or criminal methods to damage the environment, often in order to further a political agenda.  | Environmental activists are sometimes accused of ecoterrorism.  环保主义者有时会被指控为生态恐怖主义。|Synonyms: environmental terrorism, green terrorism | Antonyms: environmental conservation, ecological preservation | noun

ecoterrorism:/ˈiːkoʊˈtɛrərɪzəm/ | n. |生态恐怖主义|The action of causing deliberate environmental damage in order to further a political aim. |The group was charged with ecoterrorism after setting fire to a logging company's warehouse. 该组织因放火焚烧一家伐木公司的仓库而被指控犯有生态恐怖主义罪。|Synonyms:  environmental extremism | Antonyms: environmental protection | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
eco- (from Greek oikos "house, dwelling") + terrorism (from French terrorisme, from Latin terror "great fear")
