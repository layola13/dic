fibroin:/ˈfaɪbrɔɪn/| n. |丝心蛋白|A fibrous protein that forms the main structural component of silk, secreted by spiders and insects such as silkworms.|Silkworms produce fibroin to create their cocoons. 蚕吐出丝心蛋白来制造茧子。| The fibroin fibers are strong and flexible. 丝心蛋白纤维既坚韧又有弹性。|Synonym: silk protein|Antonym: N/A|noun
