non-unitarians:/ˌnɒn juːnɪˈteəriənz/ | n. |非一神论者| People who do not believe in the doctrine of the Trinity (the belief that God is one being existing in three co-equal, co-eternal persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit). |  Many non-Unitarians found their theology challenged by Unitarian arguments. 许多非一神论者发现他们的神学受到一神论论证的挑战。| synonyms: antitrinitarians | antonyms: Trinitarians | noun
non-unitarian:/ˌnɒn juːnɪˈteəriən/ | adj. |非一神论的| Relating to or characterized by the rejection of the doctrine of the Trinity. | The non-Unitarian churches often clashed with Unitarian congregations. 非一神论教会经常与一神论会众发生冲突。| synonyms: antitrinitarian | antonyms: Trinitarian | adjective
