hyperrational:/ˌhaɪpərˈræʃənəl/ | adj. | 过度理性的，极端理性的 | Excessively or extremely rational; prioritizing logic and reason above all other considerations, often to a fault.  |  He made a hyperrational decision to sell his house, even though it held sentimental value. 他做了一个过度理性的决定，卖掉了他的房子，即使它具有情感价值。 | Synonyms: overrational, excessively logical, coldly logical | Antonyms: irrational, emotional, impulsive | adjective

hyperrational:/ˌhaɪpərˈræʃənəl/ | adj. | 过度理性的，极端理性的 |  Driven by logic and reason to an extreme degree, often neglecting emotional or intuitive factors. | She took a hyperrational approach to dating, creating spreadsheets to analyze potential partners.  她采取了一种过度理性的约会方式，创建电子表格来分析潜在的伴侣。 | Synonyms:  ultra-rational, overly analytical | Antonyms:  irrational, illogical, sentimental | adjective
