angelify:/ænˈdʒɛlɪfaɪ/
v.
使神圣化，使纯洁化，使像天使
To make something or someone like an angel, often in terms of purity, goodness, or beauty.
She tried to angelify her mischievous child by dressing him in a white robe and wings for the play. 她试图通过给她的顽皮孩子穿上白色长袍和翅膀来让他在剧中扮演天使，从而使他看起来神圣化。
The artist angelized the subject's features in the portrait.  艺术家在肖像中将人物的面部特征描绘得像天使一般。
近义词：purify, sanctify, exalt, idealize
反义词：corrupt, demonize, degrade
词性：verb (动词)
