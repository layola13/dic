drachma:/ˈdrækmə/ | n. | 德拉克马（古希腊的货币单位）;希腊货币单位| A drachma was a silver coin used in ancient Greece. 德拉克马是古希腊使用的一种银币。| The modern Greek drachma was replaced by the euro in 2002. 现代希腊德拉克马在2002年被欧元取代。 | 近义词:currency, money | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 debt 债务) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δραχμή (drakhmē, “handful, drachma”), from δράσσομαι (drássomai, “to grasp”). Originally a "handful" of six obols (small metal spits).
