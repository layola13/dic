maquette:/mæˈkɛt/ | n. | 模型，草图，原型 | A preliminary model of something, often smaller scale, used to visualize or plan the final product.  | Example 1: The architect presented a maquette of the proposed skyscraper to the city council.  建筑师向市议会展示了拟建摩天大楼的模型。

Example 2:  The sculptor created a maquette in clay before beginning work on the marble statue.  雕塑家在开始制作大理石雕像之前先用粘土制作了一个模型。 | Synonym: model, prototype, mock-up, miniature | Antonym: final product, finished work | noun
