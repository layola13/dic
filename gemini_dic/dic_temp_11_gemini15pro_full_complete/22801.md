bosky:/ˈbɒski/ (UK) /ˈbɔːski/ (US)| adj.|茂密的，多树的，荫蔽的|Covered with bushes and trees; wooded.  Relating to woods or forests; shady and secluded.|The garden path led into a bosky dell.  花园小径通向一个树木茂盛的小山谷。|Synonyms: wooded, forested, sylvan, leafy, verdant|Antonyms: bare, open, treeless|adjective
