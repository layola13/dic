bicorporate: /baɪˈkɔːrpərɪt/
adjective
双体的；有两部分组成的
Having two bodies.  A bicorporate entity would be one with two distinct bodies or organizational structures.
The mythical centaur is often depicted as a bicorporate creature, part horse and part human.  这神话中的半人马常常被描绘成一个双体生物，一部分是马，一部分是人。
The newly merged company operated as a bicorporate entity for a short period before fully integrating.  新合并的公司在完全整合之前，曾短暂地作为一个双体实体运营。
近义词: dual, twofold
反义词: unified, single
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin "bi-" (two) + "corporatus" (having a body), past participle of "corporare" (to embody).
