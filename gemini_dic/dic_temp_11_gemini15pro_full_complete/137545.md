nontransference:/ˌnɑːntrænsˈfɜːrəns/ | n. | 非转移; 非转嫁 |  The absence of transference, especially of feelings from one person to another, particularly in a therapeutic relationship.  It refers to a situation where the emotions and feelings associated with one person or situation are not projected or transferred onto another person or situation. |  The therapist's focus on nontransference allowed for a more objective analysis of the patient's issues.  治疗师对非转移的关注使得对病人问题的分析更加客观。  Her nontransference towards her new boss, despite resembling her previous one, was surprising.  尽管她的新老板和她之前的老板很像，但她对她没有移情，这很令人惊讶。 | 近义词: objectivity, detachment, neutrality | 反义词: transference | 词性: 名词
