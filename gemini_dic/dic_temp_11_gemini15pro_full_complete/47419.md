deadlocking:/ˈdɛdlɒkɪŋ/ | n. | 僵局，死锁 |A situation in which two or more processes are unable to proceed because each is waiting for the other to do something. | The two sides reached a deadlocking over the issue of pay raises. 双方在加薪问题上陷入了僵局。| synonyms: stalemate, standstill, impasse | antonyms: agreement, compromise, resolution | noun

deadlock:/ˈdɛdlɒk/ | n. v. | 僵局，死锁；使陷入僵局 |A situation, typically one involving opposing parties, in which no progress can be made.  To cause (a process or system) to become deadlocked. |  Negotiations have reached deadlock. 谈判陷入了僵局。  The system deadlocked when two processes tried to access the same resource. 当两个进程试图访问同一资源时，系统死锁了。 | synonyms: stalemate, standstill, impasse | antonyms: agreement, compromise, resolution | noun, verb
