archaically:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪkli/ | adv. | 古代地，古老式地 | In older times; in a way that is no longer current.  |  They spoke archaically, using words no longer common. 他们说话很古雅，使用了一些现在不再常用的词。 |  He dressed archaically in a doublet and hose.  他穿着古旧的紧身上衣和长袜。 | 近义词: anciently, obsoletely, old-fashionedly | 反义词: modernly, contemporarily | 词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *archaicus, from Greek arkhaïkos, from arkhaîos "ancient," from arkhe "beginning."
