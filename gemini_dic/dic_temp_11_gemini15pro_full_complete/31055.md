chameleonlike:/kəˈmiːliənˌlaɪk/| adj.|变色龙似的，善变的|Resembling a chameleon, especially in being able to adapt readily to different circumstances or blend in with different groups of people.  This describes someone or something that changes or adapts easily to different situations, like a chameleon changing its color to match its surroundings.|例句：He has a chameleonlike ability to fit in with any group of people. 他有一种变色龙般的融入任何人群的能力。

例句：Her chameleonlike acting skills allow her to portray a wide range of characters convincingly. 她变色龙般的演技使她能够令人信服地扮演各种角色。|近义词：adaptable, versatile, flexible, changeable, variable|反义词：inflexible, rigid, unadaptable, constant|词性:形容词

词源: From chameleon + -like.  "Chameleon" itself comes from the Greek "khamaileōn," meaning "ground lion."
