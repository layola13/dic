african-sleeping-sickness:/ˈæfrɪkən ˈsliːpɪŋ ˈsɪknɪs/ | n. | 非洲昏睡病 | A parasitic disease transmitted by the tsetse fly, causing fever, lethargy, and eventually coma and death if untreated. |  African sleeping sickness is a serious public health concern in sub-Saharan Africa. 非洲昏睡病是撒哈拉以南非洲地区严重的公共卫生问题。| Synonym: trypanosomiasis | Antonym: health | Noun
