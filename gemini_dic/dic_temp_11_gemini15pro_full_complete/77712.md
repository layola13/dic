gemmated:/ˈdʒɛmeɪtɪd/
adjective
发芽的；芽状的
Having produced buds; covered in buds.
The gemmated branches of the tree were covered in tiny, nascent leaves. 这棵树发芽的枝条上覆盖着微小的、初生的叶子。
The cactus was gemmated, showing signs of new growth. 仙人掌发芽了，显示出新的生长迹象。
Near synonyms: budding, sprouting, germinating
Antonyms: barren, withered, dead
词性: adjective
