enraptured:/ɪnˈræptʃərd/| adj.|狂喜的，着迷的|Filled with delight; captivated and enthralled.|She was utterly enraptured by the performance. 她完全被表演迷住了。|近义词：ecstatic, delighted, thrilled, captivated|反义词：bored, apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (expressing intensification) + rapture (from Latin raptura "a carrying off, seizure," from raptus, past participle of rapere "to seize, carry off")
