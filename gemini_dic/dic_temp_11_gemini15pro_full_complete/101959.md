isoantigenic:/ˌaɪsoʊˌæntiˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 同种抗原的 | Relating to or exhibiting isoantigenicity; denoting antigens present in some but not all members of a species and capable of inducing the formation of antibodies in other members of the same species. |  Isoantigenic differences can lead to transplant rejection.  同种抗原差异会导致移植排斥。| synonyms: allogeneic, homologous | antonyms: autologous, syngeneic | adjective

Etymology:  iso- (Greek isos "equal") + anti- (Greek anti "against") + -gen (Greek -genes "born, produced") + -ic (suffix forming adjectives)
