bibbing:/ˈbɪbɪŋ/ | n. |  流口水; 用围嘴吃饭; 轻微饮酒 | The act of dribbling saliva or drinking in small sips. | The baby's bibbing had soaked through her clothes. 婴儿的口水浸透了她的衣服。| 近义词: dribbling, sipping | 反义词: gulping | 词性: 名词

bibbing:/ˈbɪbɪŋ/ | v. |  流口水; 用围嘴吃饭; 轻微饮酒 |  Present participle of "bib".  |  The baby was bibbing all over her new dress.  婴儿把口水流得新裙子上到处都是。 | 近义词: dribbling, sipping | 反义词: gulping | 词性: 动词


词源: From the noun "bib," referring to a cloth placed under a baby's chin to catch dribble.  "Bib" comes from Middle English "bibe,"  probably of imitative origin.
