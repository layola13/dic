classes:/ˈklæsɪz/ (US), /ˈklɑːsɪz/ (UK)| n.|课程;班级;种类|A series of meetings in which students are taught a particular subject or activity; a group of students who are taught together; a set or category of things having shared characteristics.|例句：My history class is very interesting. 我的历史课很有趣。|近义词：course, lesson, group, category, type, kind, sort|反义词：individuality, uniqueness|noun


classes:/ˈklæsɪz/ (US), /ˈklɑːsɪz/ (UK)| v. (classes, classing, classed)|分类;归类|To regard something as belonging to a particular group or category.|例句：He was classed as a genius. 他被归类为天才。|近义词：categorize, classify, group, rank|反义词：separate, distinguish|verb
