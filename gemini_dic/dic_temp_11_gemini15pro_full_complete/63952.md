episcopant:/ɪˈpɪskəpənt/ | n. |主教,监督者|A bishop or overseer, especially in some branches of Christianity.| The episcopant visited the parishes under his care. 主教访问了他所管辖的教区。 | 近义词: bishop, overseer, prelate | 反义词: layman, parishioner | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *episcopans*, present participle of *episcopāre* "to act as a bishop," from Latin *episcopus* "bishop" (see bishop).
