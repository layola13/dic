bunker mentality:/ˈbʌŋkər mɛnˈtælɪti/
noun
碉堡心态；闭关自守的心态
A defensive and isolated mindset characterized by prioritizing self-preservation and resisting change or external influence, often seen in organizations or groups facing pressure or crisis.
The company developed a bunker mentality after the scandal, refusing to communicate with the press. 这家公司在丑闻之后形成了碉堡心态，拒绝与媒体沟通。
Synonyms: siege mentality, defensiveness, insularity, parochialism
Antonyms: openness, receptiveness, collaboration, adaptability
n.
