nonfutile:/ˌnɑːnˈfjuːtaɪl/
adjective
非徒劳的；有效的
Having some effect or achieving some purpose; not futile.
The doctor felt encouraged that the treatment was nonfutile and the patient was responding well.  医生感到鼓舞，因为治疗并非徒劳，病人反应良好。
The meeting proved nonfutile, as several key decisions were made. 会议最终并非徒劳无功，因为做出了一些重要的决定。
近义词:effective, productive, successful, worthwhile
反义词:futile, pointless, useless, ineffective
词性:adjective
