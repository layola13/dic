conciseness:/kənˈsaɪsnəs/| n.|简洁，简明|The conciseness of his writing style made it easy to understand. 他的写作风格简洁明了，易于理解。|例句：She expressed her ideas with admirable conciseness. 她以令人钦佩的简洁表达了她的想法。

例句：Conciseness is a valuable quality in academic writing.  简洁是学术写作中宝贵的品质。


近义词：brevity, succinctness, terseness|反义词:verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French concis, from Latin concisus ‘cut short’, past participle of concidere .
