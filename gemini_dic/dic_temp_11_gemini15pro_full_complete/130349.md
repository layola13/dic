non-trust:/ˌnɒnˈtrʌst/ | adj. |不信任的，非信任的 |Lacking trust; characterized by distrust or suspicion.  Relating to systems or environments where trust is not assumed or established. |  The non-trust environment of the internet requires strong security measures. 互联网的不信任环境需要强有力的安全措施。 | The team operated in a non-trust environment, constantly verifying each other's work.  团队在不信任的环境中运作，不断地核实彼此的工作。 |  distrustful, suspicious, skeptical | trusting, confident | adjective
