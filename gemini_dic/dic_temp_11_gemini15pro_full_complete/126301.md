myogram: /ˈmaɪəɡræm/ | n. | 肌电图 | A graphic representation of the electrical activity of muscles. |  The myogram showed a significant increase in muscle activity during the exercise. 肌电图显示运动期间肌肉活动显著增加。

| Example: The doctor studied the myogram to assess the patient's muscle function. 医生研究了肌电图以评估病人的肌肉功能。 | Synonyms: electromyogram, EMG | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms relating to lack of muscle activity like "paralysis" or "atony" but these aren't true opposites) | noun
