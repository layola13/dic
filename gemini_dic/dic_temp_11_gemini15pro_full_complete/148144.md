oversensitivities:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
过敏; 过度敏感
the state of being too easily upset or offended by things that people say or do
Example:  He suffers from a number of oversensitivities, including an allergy to peanuts.  他患有多种过敏症，包括花生过敏。

Example:  Her oversensitivities made social interactions difficult. 她过度敏感，使得社交互动变得困难。
synonyms: allergies, sensitivities, susceptibilities, vulnerabilities
antonyms: tolerances, immunities
noun
