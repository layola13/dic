outsmarts:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrts/
verb
智胜，比…更聪明
to defeat or get an advantage over someone by being more clever
He always outsmarts his opponents in negotiations. 他总能在谈判中智胜对手。
近义词: outwit, outmaneuver, surpass, defeat
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted, lose to
