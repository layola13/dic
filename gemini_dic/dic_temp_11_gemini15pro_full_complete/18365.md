bellwether:/ˈbelˌweðər/ (US), /ˈbelˌweðə/ (UK)| n. |领头羊，风向标，前兆|A bellwether is a sheep that leads the flock, often wearing a bell around its neck.  Figuratively, it refers to something that leads or indicates a trend. | The success of this product is a bellwether for the future of the company. 这个产品的成功是公司未来的风向标。| synonyms: leader, indicator, trendsetter, barometer | antonyms: follower, laggard | noun
