multitones:/ˈmʌltiˌtəʊnz/ | n. |复音; 多音; 多音调|A complex sound consisting of multiple frequencies or tones.|The multitones of the city created a vibrant soundscape.|近义词：polytones, overtones|反义词: monotone|词性:名词

Etymology:  "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many") + "tones" (from Latin "tonus" meaning "sound").  Essentially, "many sounds."
