dialysis: /daɪˈælɪsɪs/ /daɪˈæləsɪs/
noun
透析；分解；分离
The separation of smaller molecules from larger ones through a semipermeable membrane, as in kidney dialysis.
He is on dialysis three times a week.  他每周要进行三次透析。
synonyms: filtration, separation, purification
antonyms: combination, synthesis, fusion
n.
