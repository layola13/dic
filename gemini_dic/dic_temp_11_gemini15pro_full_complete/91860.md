hyperimmunizes:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊnaɪzɪz/
verb
过度免疫，高度免疫
To induce an abnormally high level of immunity in (a person or animal), usually by repeated injections of an antigen.
The horses were hyperimmunized with the venom to produce antivenom.  这些马被反复注射毒液以产生抗毒血清。
The lab hyperimmunizes rabbits to create antibodies.  实验室对兔子进行高度免疫以产生抗体。
synonyms: immunize, vaccinate
antonyms:  None (The opposite action would be to suppress the immune system, but there isn't a single direct antonym)
词性: 动词
