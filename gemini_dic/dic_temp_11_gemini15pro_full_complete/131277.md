noncaustic:/ˌnɒnˈkɔːstik/ | adj. | 非腐蚀性的；不刻薄的 | Not corrosive or burning; not sarcastic or biting. | This cleaning solution is noncaustic and safe for use on most surfaces.  这种清洁液是非腐蚀性的，可安全用于大多数表面。 | The teacher's criticism, though pointed, was noncaustic and helpful. 老师的批评虽然尖锐，但并不刻薄，而且很有帮助。 | 近义词: mild, gentle, harmless, benign | 反义词: caustic, corrosive, abrasive, biting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "caustic" (from Latin "causticus," burning).  "non-" 表示否定，"caustic" 源于拉丁语 "causticus"，意为燃烧的。
