koftgari:/kɒftˈɡɑːriː/
noun
金银错丝工艺; 金属丝镶嵌工艺
The art of inlaying gold or silver wire into steel, typically used for decorating weapons and other metal objects.
The koftgari on the sword hilt was intricate and beautiful.  这把剑柄上的金银错丝工艺复杂而精美。
inlay, damascening
