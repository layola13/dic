boof:/buːf/
verb
欺骗；愚弄；粗暴地对待
to deceive, cheat, or treat roughly.
He boofed the old lady out of her savings. 他骗走了老太太的积蓄。

honest, truthful

boof:/buːf/
verb
大量吸食（毒品，尤指大麻）
to inhale a large amount of a drug, especially marijuana.
He boofed the entire joint in one hit. 他一口吸完了整根大麻烟卷。

boof:/buːf/
noun
假货；劣质品
a fake or inferior product.
