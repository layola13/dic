non-homologous:/ˌnɒn həˈmɒləɡəs/ /ˌnɑːn həˈmɑːləɡəs/
adjective
非同源的
Not having the same evolutionary origin but sharing similar structure or function due to convergent evolution.
The wings of a bat and the wings of a butterfly are non-homologous structures.  蝙蝠的翅膀和蝴蝶的翅膀是非同源结构。
The fins of sharks and dolphins are non-homologous despite their similar appearance.  鲨鱼和海豚的鳍虽然外观相似，但并非同源。
近义词: analogous, similar (in function)
反义词: homologous
词性: adjective
