overbulk:/ˈoʊvərˌbʌlk/| v. | 过度膨胀；使体积过大 | To make something too large or bulky.  The packaging overbulked the shipment, increasing shipping costs. 包装使货物体积过大，增加了运输成本。| overpack, overload | underpack, reduce | verb
overbulk:/ˈoʊvərˌbʌlk/| n. | 过大体积；过剩容积 |  Excessive bulk or volume. The overbulk of the furniture made it difficult to move through the doorway. 家具的过大体积使得难以通过门口。| excess, surplus | deficit, shortage | noun
