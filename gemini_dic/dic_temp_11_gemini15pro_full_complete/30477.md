centripetency:/ˌsɛntrɪˈpɛtənsi/ | n. | 向心力，向心趋势 | The tendency of a body to move toward a center of attraction. | The centripetency of gravity keeps the planets in orbit around the sun. 向心引力使行星绕太阳运行。 |  near synonyms:  centripetal force, centralizing tendency, inward drive | antonyms: centrifugal force, centrifugal tendency, outward drive | noun

centripetal:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪpɪtl/ | adj. | 向心的 | Moving or tending to move toward a center. | The centripetal force keeps the object moving in a circular path. 向心力使物体沿圆周运动。| near synonyms:  central, converging, inward | antonyms: centrifugal, diverging, outward | adjective
