berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ | n. | 狂战士；暴怒的人 | A fierce warrior, especially one of the ancient Norse warriors legendary for working themselves into a frenzy before battle. | He fought like a berserker, fueled by rage and adrenaline. 他像个狂战士一样战斗，被愤怒和肾上腺素所驱使。|  Example: The Vikings were known for their berserker warriors who fought with unmatched ferocity. 维京人以其狂暴的战士而闻名，这些战士以无与伦比的凶猛战斗。| Synonyms:  wildman, madman, fanatic, fiend | Antonyms: pacifist, calm person | noun
