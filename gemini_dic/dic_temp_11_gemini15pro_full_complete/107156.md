laf: /læf/
noun|大笑，笑声|a loud or boisterous burst of laughter|The comedian's joke elicited a laf from the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引起了观众的一阵大笑。|近义词:guffaw, chuckle, roar|反义词:sob, cry|n.

verb|大笑，狂笑|to laugh loudly or boisterously|They laffed at his silly antics. 他们对他的愚蠢行为大笑。|近义词:guffaw, roar, chuckle|反义词:sob, cry|v.

Etymology:  "Laf" is a variant spelling of "laugh," likely influenced by dialectical pronunciation.  The word "laugh" comes from Old English *hleahtor, from Proto-Germanic *hlahtraz.  The verb form developed later.
