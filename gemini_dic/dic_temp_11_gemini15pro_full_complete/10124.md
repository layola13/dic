apollinarian:/əˌpɒlɪˈnɛəriən/ | n. | 阿波里拿留派信徒 | A follower of Apollinaris or Apollinarianism. | He was considered an Apollinarian for his beliefs. 他因其信仰被认为是阿波里拿留派信徒。 | Near Synonyms: heretic, dissenter | Antonyms: orthodox believer | noun
