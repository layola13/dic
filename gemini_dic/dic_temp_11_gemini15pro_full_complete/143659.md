outlabor:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbər/
verb
胜过工作; 比...更勤奋地工作
To work harder or longer than someone else.
The team outlabored their opponents and secured a victory.  这个团队比他们的对手工作更努力，并取得了胜利。
The small company outlabored the larger corporation and won the contract. 这家小公司比大公司工作更努力，赢得了合同。
近义词: outwork, surpass, outperform
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性: verb

词源: "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "labor" (verb meaning "to work")
