mindfulness:/ˈmaɪndflnəs/| n.|正念，专注力，觉察力|A mental state achieved by focusing one's awareness on the present moment, while calmly acknowledging and accepting one's feelings, thoughts, and bodily sensations, used as a therapeutic technique.|例句：Practicing mindfulness can reduce stress and improve focus.  练习正念可以减轻压力并提高注意力。|近义词：awareness, attention, concentration, presence|反义词：distraction, heedlessness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From *mind* (n.) + *-ful* (suffix) + *-ness* (suffix).  The concept originates from Buddhist traditions.
