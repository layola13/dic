macula:/ˈmækjʊlə/ (US), /ˈmækjələ/ (UK)| n. |斑点；污点；(尤指视网膜上的)黄斑|A small spot or blemish, especially on the retina of the eye.|The ophthalmologist examined the macula of her eye. 眼科医生检查了她眼睛的黄斑。|Synonyms: spot, blemish, blotch, speck, patch|Antonyms: clear, spotless, unblemished|Noun

macula:/ˈmækjʊlə/ (US), /ˈmækjələ/ (UK)| adj. |(解剖学)有斑点的|Spotted; having maculae or spots. |This rare butterfly has macula wings.  这种稀有的蝴蝶有斑点的翅膀。|Synonyms: spotted, speckled, mottled|Antonyms: unspotted, clear|Adjective
