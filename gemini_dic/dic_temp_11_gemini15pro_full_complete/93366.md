illegitimateness:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmɪtnəs/
n.
非法性；私生；不合法
The state or quality of being illegitimate.
The illegitimateness of his claim to the throne led to a civil war. 他对王位的非法性导致了一场内战。
Synonyms: illegality, unlawfulness, invalidity
Antonyms: legitimacy, legality, validity
词性: 名词


illegitimateness:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmɪtnəs/
n.
不正当；不合理
The state or quality of being illogical or unreasonable.
He argued the illegitimateness of the new policy. 他辩称新政策不合理。
Synonyms:  unreasonableness, illogicality, invalidity
Antonyms: reasonableness, logicality, validity
