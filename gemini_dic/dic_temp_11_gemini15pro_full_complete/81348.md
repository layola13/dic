graphitoid:/ˈɡræfɪˌtɔɪd/| adj.|石墨状的，类似石墨的|Resembling graphite; applied to a variety of pyrophyllite that has a greasy feel, like that of graphite.|The geologist identified the mineral as graphitoid due to its greasy texture.|Synonyms: graphitic|Antonyms: non-graphitic|adjective
