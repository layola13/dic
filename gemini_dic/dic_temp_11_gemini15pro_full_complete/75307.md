frontispiece:/ˈfrʌntɪspiːs/
n.
卷首插画;扉页
An illustration facing the title page of a book.
The frontispiece of the book was a beautiful engraving. 这本书的卷首插画是一幅美丽的雕刻画。
近义词: illustration, plate, engraving
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: back cover, epilogue)
词性: Noun

frontispieces:/ˈfrʌntɪspiːsiz/
n.
卷首插画;扉页（复数）
Illustrations facing the title pages of books.
The frontispieces in the antique book collection were meticulously preserved.  这套古籍收藏中的卷首插画都保存得非常完好。
近义词: illustrations, plates, engravings
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: Noun
