inexpiable:/ɪnˈɛkspiəbl/
adj.
不可饶恕的，不可赎罪的
Unable to be atoned for; unpardonable.
例句：His betrayal was an inexpiable offense against his family. 他对家人的背叛是不可饶恕的罪行。

例句：The dictator committed inexpiable crimes against humanity.  独裁者犯下了不可饶恕的反人类罪行。

近义词：unforgivable, unpardonable, irredeemable
反义词：forgivable, pardonable, excusable
词性: 形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin inexpialis, from in- ‘not’ + expiare ‘atone for’ (from ex- ‘out’ + piare ‘appease, purify,’ from pius ‘pious’).
