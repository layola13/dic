fascist:/ˈfæʃɪst/| n. adj.|法西斯主义者；法西斯的|A follower of a political philosophy characterized by authoritarian views and a strong central government, often involving repressive measures against political opponents and minorities.  A person who supports or advocates fascism. (As an adjective) Relating to or characteristic of fascism.|例句：He called his opponent a fascist. 他称他的对手为法西斯主义者。|例句：The fascist regime controlled every aspect of people's lives. 法西斯政权控制了人们生活的方方面面。|近义词：authoritarian, totalitarian, dictator, tyrant, despot|反义词：democrat, liberal, egalitarian|词性: noun, adjective

fascism:/ˈfæʃɪzəm/| n.|法西斯主义|A political philosophy, movement, or regime that exalts nation and often race above the individual and that stands for a centralized autocratic government headed by a dictatorial leader, severe economic and social regimentation, and forcible suppression of opposition.|例句：The rise of fascism in Europe in the 1930s led to World War II. 20世纪30年代欧洲法西斯主义的兴起导致了第二次世界大战。|例句：Fascism is often characterized by extreme nationalism and militarism. 法西斯主义的特征通常是极端的民族主义和军国主义。|近义词：authoritarianism, totalitarianism, Nazism|反义词：democracy, liberalism, socialism|词性: noun
