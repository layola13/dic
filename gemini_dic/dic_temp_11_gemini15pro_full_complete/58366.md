dualin: /ˈduːəlɪn/ | n. | 对偶线性函数; 对偶线性|A dual linear function is a linear function defined on the dual space of a vector space.  It's a key concept in linear algebra and functional analysis, often used in optimization and other areas of mathematics.|  The dualin of this vector space is isomorphic to the space itself.  这个向量空间的对偶与该空间本身同构。| synonyms: dual function, linear functional | antonyms: N/A | noun
