gourd:/ɡɔːrd/ (US), /ɡʊəd/ (UK)| n.|葫芦；瓜；瓠|A fleshy, typically large fruit with a hard rind, such as a pumpkin, squash, or melon.  Some gourds are used for decoration, and some are edible.|The farmer grew a giant gourd for the county fair. 农民为县集市种了一个巨大的葫芦。|Synonyms: calabash, pumpkin, squash, melon|Antonyms: None|Noun

gourd:/ɡɔːrd/ (US), /ɡʊəd/ (UK)| adj.|葫芦形的|Shaped like a gourd.|The vase had a gourd-like shape. 花瓶是葫芦形的。|Synonyms:  bulbous, rounded|Antonyms:  narrow, elongated|Adjective
