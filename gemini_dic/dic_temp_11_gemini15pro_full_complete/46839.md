dapperest:/ˈdæpərɪst/| adj.|最衣冠楚楚的，最整洁漂亮的|Superlative form of "dapper," meaning most stylish and neat in dress and appearance.|He was the dapperest man at the party, impeccably dressed in a tailored suit. 他是派对上最衣冠楚楚的男人，穿着一套剪裁得体的西装，无可挑剔。| The dapperest gentleman I've ever seen always wears a bow tie. 我见过的最衣冠楚楚的绅士总是戴着领结。| 近义词：smartest, most stylish, most elegant|反义词：scruffiest, sloppiest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dapper + -est.  "Dapper" itself likely comes from Dutch  "dapper," meaning brave or valiant, later evolving to describe a neat and elegant appearance.
