apprehend:/ˌæprɪˈhend/ | v. | 理解；逮捕；担心 | To understand something; to arrest someone; to fear that something bad will happen. | The police apprehended the suspect. 警察逮捕了嫌疑人。 |  Synonyms: understand, grasp, capture, arrest, seize, dread, fear | Antonyms: misunderstand, release, liberate, free | verb

apprehend:/ˌæprɪˈhend/ | v. | 忧虑；恐惧 | To feel anxious or fearful about something that might happen. | I apprehended that something terrible had happened to him. 我担心他发生了什么可怕的事情。| Synonyms: fear, dread, worry, anticipate (with anxiety) | Antonyms: hope, be confident | verb
