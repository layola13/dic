lymph's:/lɪmfs/| n.|淋巴（液）的|The possessive form of "lymph," referring to something belonging to or associated with lymph.  It indicates ownership or a close relationship.|The lymph's flow was obstructed. 淋巴的流动被阻碍了。|近义词: lymphatic (adjective) |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a possessive form) |词性:Possessive noun


词源:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin lympha ‘water,’ influenced by Latin nympha ‘nymph’ (on account of the purity of water).
