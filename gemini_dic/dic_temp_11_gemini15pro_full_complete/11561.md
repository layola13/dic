aristocratize:/əˌrɪstəˈkrətaɪz/
|v.|使贵族化；使带有贵族气息；变得高贵|To make aristocratic; to give aristocratic qualities or characteristics to; to become aristocratic.|The wealthy merchant attempted to aristocratize his family by sending his sons to prestigious boarding schools.  这位富商试图通过把他的儿子们送到著名的寄宿学校来使他的家庭贵族化。

The new country club quickly aristocratized the social scene. 这个新的乡村俱乐部迅速地使社交圈贵族化。
|近义词：refine, elevate, ennoble|反义词：democratize, vulgarize|词性：动词
