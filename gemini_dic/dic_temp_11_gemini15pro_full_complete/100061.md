interpolant:/ɪnˈtɜːrpələnt/ | n. | 插值；内插值；插值函数 | A function or value generated by interpolation, i.e., estimated between known data points. |  The interpolant smoothly connects the known data points. 插值函数平滑地连接已知数据点。

The software uses a cubic spline as the interpolant.  该软件使用三次样条作为插值函数。 | 近义词: interpolation function, interpolated value | 反义词: extrapolated value | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Latin "interpolare"  (to refurbish, alter, polish up),  from "inter" (between) + "polare" (to polish).  The mathematical sense developed from the idea of "filling in" gaps in data.
