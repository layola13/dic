agoraphobes:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbz/| n. |恐旷症患者，广场恐惧症患者|People who suffer from agoraphobia, a fear of open or crowded spaces.|Many agoraphobes find it difficult to leave their homes.|近义词：panic disorder sufferers|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "sociable people," "extroverts")|词性:noun


Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀγορά (agorá, “marketplace”) + -phobia.
