charily:/ˈtʃɛrɪli/ | adv. | 谨慎地，吝啬地 | cautiously; sparingly |  He spent his money charily, always mindful of the future. 他花钱很谨慎，总是考虑到未来。|  She charily offered her opinion, afraid of causing offense. 她小心翼翼地提出自己的意见，生怕得罪人。| 近义词: cautiously, sparingly, frugally, prudently | 反义词: lavishly, extravagantly, freely, generously | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Middle English *charily, from Old French *charier "to cherish, to hold dear," from Late Latin *cariare, from Latin carus "dear." The original sense was "dearly, at a high price," which evolved to "carefully, sparingly."
