cellular:/ˈsɛljələr/ (US), /ˈsɛljʊlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|细胞的；蜂窝状的；多孔的|Relating to or consisting of cells; resembling the structure of a cell; using a cellular phone network.|例句：Cellular respiration is the process by which organisms convert nutrients into energy. 细胞呼吸是有机体将营养物质转化为能量的过程。|例句：The new building has a cellular structure, making it lightweight and strong. 新建筑采用蜂窝结构，使其既轻便又坚固。|近义词：cell-like, mobile (phone)|反义词:noncellular, acellular|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *cellulāris*, from *cellula* ("small room, cell").
