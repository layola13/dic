deterioration:/dɪˌtɪəriəˈreɪʃn/| n. |恶化，变质|the process of becoming progressively worse|There has been a marked deterioration in his health. 他的健康状况明显恶化。|decline, decay, worsening|improvement, recovery

词源: From Latin *dēteriōrāre*, from *dēterior* "worse," comparative of *dē* "down from" + root of *terior* "situated behind," from PIE root *ters-* "to cross, pass over."
