condensation:/ˌkɒndenˈseɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 冷凝，凝结，浓缩，缩写 |The process of changing from a gas to a liquid or solid state. Also, a shortened version of something, like a book or play. |Example 1: Condensation formed on the cold window.  冷凝水在冰冷的窗户上形成。

Example 2:  He read a condensation of the lengthy report. 他读了一份长篇报告的缩略版。 | synonyms: liquefaction, precipitation, abridgment, summary | antonyms: evaporation, vaporization | noun
