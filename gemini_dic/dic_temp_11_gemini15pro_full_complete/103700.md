jonquil: /ˈdʒɒŋkwɪl/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːŋkwɪl/ (US)| n.|水仙花（一种黄水仙）|A fragrant, yellow-flowered daffodil (Narcissus jonquilla) native to southern Europe and southwestern Asia, with long, narrow, rushlike leaves.|The jonquils bloomed early this year. 今年水仙花开得很早。|近义词：daffodil, narcissus|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他花卉如rose)|词性:noun

jonquil: /ˈdʒɒŋkwɪl/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːŋkwɪl/ (US)| adj. |水仙黄色的|Having the color of a jonquil flower; a pale yellow.|She wore a jonquil dress to the spring party. 她穿着一件水仙黄色的连衣裙去参加春季派对。|近义词：yellow, primrose, lemon|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他颜色如blue)|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "jonquille," from Spanish "junquillo" (rush, jonquil), diminutive of "junco" (rush), from Latin "juncus."  The name refers to the plant's rush-like leaves.
