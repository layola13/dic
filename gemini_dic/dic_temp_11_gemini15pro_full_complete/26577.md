caboodles:/kəˈbuːdlz/| n. |大量，全部，所有物|a lot of things; the whole lot.  It often refers to a collection of miscellaneous items.| She brought the whole caboodle with her - clothes, shoes, and even her kitchen sink.| 近义词: lots, heaps, piles, oodles, tons, scads | 反义词: little, few, nothing | 词性: noun

Etymology: Origin uncertain. Perhaps related to *boodle*, meaning profits or loot.
