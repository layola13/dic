bedward:/ˈbɛdwərd/| adv.|朝床的方向，临睡前|Toward bed; in preparation for going to bed. |例句：She turned bedward, weary from a long day. 她朝床走去，漫长的一天让她疲惫不堪。|例句：The children were sent bedward at eight o'clock. 孩子们八点钟被赶去睡觉。|近义词：bedtime, sleepward|反义词：(No direct antonym, consider 'upward', 'outward' for opposite direction)|词性：副词
