annoyingnesses:/əˈnɔɪɪŋnəsɪz/
|n.|恼人之事；令人讨厌的事物|The constant dripping of the faucet was one of the annoyingnesses of the old apartment. 老公寓里不断滴水的水龙头是其中一件令人讨厌的事情。|例句：The sheer number of annoyingnesses in my day made me want to scream. 我一天中遇到的烦心事多得让我只想尖叫。

| | |Her constant complaining was one of her biggest annoyingnesses. 她不停地抱怨是她最大的缺点之一。|近义词：irritations, nuisances, vexations, bothers|反义词:pleasantries, comforts|词性:名词

annoying:/əˈnɔɪɪŋ/
|adj.|恼人的；讨厌的|The mosquito buzzing in my ear was incredibly annoying.  蚊子在我耳边嗡嗡叫，真是烦死人了。|例句：His annoying habit of tapping his pen drove me crazy. 他不停地敲笔的烦人习惯快把我逼疯了。

| | |Finding a parking spot can be incredibly annoying during rush hour. 在高峰时段找停车位非常烦人。|近义词：irritating, bothersome, vexing, troublesome|反义词:pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词

annoy:/əˈnɔɪ/
|v.|使恼怒；打扰|The loud music from the neighbors annoyed me all night. 邻居家吵闹的音乐让我整晚都不得安宁。|例句：It annoys me when people interrupt me while I'm speaking. 当我说话时有人打断我，我很生气。

| | |The constant barking of the dog annoyed the entire neighborhood. 狗不停地吠叫惹恼了整个社区。|近义词：irritate, bother, vex, pester|反义词:please, delight|词性:动词
