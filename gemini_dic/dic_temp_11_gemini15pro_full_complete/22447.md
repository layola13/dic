booking-out:/ˈbʊkɪŋ aʊt/
n.|退房；结账离开|The process of officially leaving a hotel or other accommodation. |例句：The booking-out time is 11 am. 退房时间是上午11点。|近义词：checking out, departure|反义词：checking in, arrival|noun

booking-out:/ˈbʊkɪŋ aʊt/
n.|签出；登记离开|The act of recording one's departure from a place, especially a workplace. |例句：Make sure you booking-out before you leave the office.  确保你在离开办公室之前签出。|近义词：signing out, clocking out|反义词：signing in, clocking in|noun
