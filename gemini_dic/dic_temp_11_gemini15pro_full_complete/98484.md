installing: /ɪnˈstɔːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ɪnˈstɔːlɪŋ/ (US)
verb
安装，设置
the act of putting something in place and making it ready to use
Example: The installing of the new software took several hours.  安装新软件花了几个小时。
Example: They are installing a new security system. 他们在安装新的安全系统。
Synonyms: setting up, fitting, implementing
Antonyms: removing, uninstalling, dismantling
词性:动词
