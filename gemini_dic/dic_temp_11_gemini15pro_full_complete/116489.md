materializes:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
实现；出现；突然显现
to become real or actual; to appear suddenly
例句：
The opportunity she had been waiting for finally materialized. 她一直等待的机会终于出现了。

My hopes for a promotion never materialized. 我升职的希望从未实现。

近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, take shape
反义词：disappear, vanish, dematerialize
词性: v.
