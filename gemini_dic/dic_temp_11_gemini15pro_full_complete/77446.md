gawky:/ˈɡɔːki/ | adj. |笨拙的，迟钝的 |Awkward and clumsy in movement or manner.  Characterized by ungainliness or awkwardness, often due to being tall and thin. | He was a gawky teenager, all arms and legs. 他是个笨手笨脚的青少年，又高又瘦。 |  The newborn giraffe was gawky and unsteady on its feet. 新生长颈鹿的腿很笨拙，站不稳。 | clumsy, awkward, ungainly, gangling | graceful, elegant, coordinated, poised | adjective
