immunization:/ˌɪmjuːnɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 免疫；免疫接种 | The act of making a person or animal immune to infection, typically by inoculation. |  Example: Childhood immunization is vital for preventing many serious diseases.  儿童免疫接种对于预防许多严重疾病至关重要。 | Example:  Immunization against measles is highly effective. 麻疹免疫非常有效。| Synonym: vaccination, inoculation | Antonym: infection, susceptibility | Noun
