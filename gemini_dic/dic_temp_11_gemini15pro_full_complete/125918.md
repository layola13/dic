musette:/mjuːˈzɛt/ | n. |  小风笛；轻便的背包；一种舞曲 | A small bagpipe; a small lightweight bag or satchel, often used by soldiers or cyclists; a type of French dance |  The soldier's musette contained his rations and a few personal items. 士兵的小背包里装着他的口粮和一些私人物品。 | synonym: bag, satchel, knapsack, haversack | antonym: suitcase, trunk | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *musette*, diminutive of *muse* "muzzle, snout," also "bagpipe," from Late Latin *musa*. The sense development likely went from "bagpipe" (due to its shape resembling a snout) to "small bag."
