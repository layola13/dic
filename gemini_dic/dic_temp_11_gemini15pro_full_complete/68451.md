famicom:/ˈfæmɪkɒm/ |n.| 家庭电脑（Family Computer的缩写），任天堂红白机|A Nintendo Entertainment System is often called a Famicom in Japan.|The Famicom was hugely popular in the 1980s. 红白机在20世纪80年代非常流行。|近义词: NES (Nintendo Entertainment System), console|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Japanese ファミコン (Famikon), a shortened form of ファミリーコンピュータ (Famirī Konpyūta), meaning "Family Computer".
