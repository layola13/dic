lubricant:/ˈluːbrɪkənt/| n.|润滑剂；润滑油|A substance, such as oil or grease, that reduces friction when applied to moving parts.|The mechanic applied lubricant to the gears. 技工给齿轮上了润滑油。|Synonyms: oil, grease, emollient|Antonyms: abrasive, desiccant|noun

lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/| v.|润滑；使…顺畅|To apply a lubricant to something in order to reduce friction.|He lubricated the chain on his bicycle. 他给自行车链条上了油。|Synonyms: oil, grease, smooth|Antonyms: seize, bind, stick|verb
