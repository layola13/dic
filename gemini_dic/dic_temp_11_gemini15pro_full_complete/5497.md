alopi: /əˈloʊpiː/
noun
秃头症，斑秃
Alopecia is a condition that causes hair to fall out.
Alopecia can affect men and women of all ages.
She suffers from alopecia. 她患有秃头症。
Stress can sometimes trigger alopecia. 压力有时会引发脱发。
synonyms: baldness, hair loss
antonyms: hair growth
