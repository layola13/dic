exact:/ɪɡˈzækt/ | adj. | 准确的，精确的 | Precisely accurate or correct. |  The exact time of the meeting is 3:00 PM.  会议的确切时间是下午3点。| synonyms: precise, accurate, correct | antonyms: inaccurate, imprecise, approximate | adjective

exact:/ɪɡˈzækt/ | vt. |  要求，强求；勒索 | To demand and obtain (something, especially a payment) from someone. | The gangsters exacted a heavy toll from local businesses.  歹徒向当地企业勒索巨额钱财。 | synonyms: demand, require, extort | antonyms: give, offer, donate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exactus*, past participle of *exigere* "to drive out, demand, enforce," from *ex* "out" + *agere* "to drive, lead, act."
