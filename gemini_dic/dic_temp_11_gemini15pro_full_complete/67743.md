eyeballer:/ˈaɪˌbɔːlər/| n.|眼球, 目击者, 观看者|A person who looks or stares, especially one who watches something closely or with interest.  Someone who attends an event or views something, often to assess it.|The art director needed a few eyeballersto give feedback on the new logo design. 艺术总监需要一些人来对新标志设计提供反馈。|Synonyms: viewer, observer, onlooker, spectator, witness|Antonyms: N/A|noun
