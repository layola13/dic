bidarkee: /bɪˈdɑːrki/ | n. | 比达基（一种澳大利亚土著的仪式用木棒）| A bidarkee is a carved wooden object used in ceremonies by some Aboriginal Australian peoples. It is typically long and slender, and may be decorated with carvings or paint.  |  The elders passed the bidarkee around the circle during the ceremony. 长老们在仪式中将比达基在圈内传递。|  Synonyms: ceremonial object, ritual stick | Antonyms: N/A | noun
