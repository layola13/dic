Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party?  她参加聚会时穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you went to the cinema? 你去看电影之前吃了什么？
What had she done before I arrived? 在我到达之前她做了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before you called me? 你打电话给我之前一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will she bring to the picnic? 她会带什么去野餐？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the wedding? 她婚礼上会穿什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by Friday? 到星期五你将完成什么？
What will she have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底她将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学了什么多久了？
