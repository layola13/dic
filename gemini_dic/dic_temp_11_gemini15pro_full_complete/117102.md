mealier:/ˈmiːliər/| adj.|更粉状的，更松软的，更像粗粉的|Containing more meal; resembling meal more closely. The potatoes were mealier than usual. |例句：The bread was surprisingly mealier than I expected. 这面包比我预想的要更松软。

例句：These potatoes are much mealier than the ones we had last week. 这些土豆比我们上周吃的要粉得多。|近义词：floury, powdery, crumbly, dry, soft|反义词：firm, dense, moist, waxy|词性：形容词

词源：From meal (n.) + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *malan (to grind).
