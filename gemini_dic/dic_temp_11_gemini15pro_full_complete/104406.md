kadder:/ˈkædər/| n.| 絮凝物，凝聚物；小块，小团| A flocculent mass, especially one formed in a solution or through coagulation; a small lump or clot. | The chemist observed small kadders forming in the beaker. 化学家观察到烧杯中形成了小的絮凝物。| 近义词：clot, lump, floccule, aggregate | 反义词: solution, liquid | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *kadder or *kader, likely related to *kad "lump, clod."  Possibly influenced by or related to  Middle Dutch *kadden "to coagulate."  The exact etymology is uncertain.
