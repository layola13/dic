grudgingness:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋnəs/| n.|勉强，不情愿，吝啬|Reluctance or unwillingness to give or admit something.|He paid the money with extreme grudgingness. 他极不情愿地付了钱。|近义词：reluctance, unwillingness, resentment|反义词：willingness, generosity, enthusiasm|词性:名词

Etymology:
From *grudging* + *-ness*.  *Grudging* comes from the Old North French word *grouchier* meaning "to grumble, complain."
