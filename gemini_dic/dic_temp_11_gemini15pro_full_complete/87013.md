henry:/ˈhenri/ (US) /ˈhɛnri/ (UK)| n.|亨利; 亨利 (电感单位)|A masculine given name, of Germanic origin, meaning "home ruler".  Also, the unit of inductance in the International System of Units (SI), named after American scientist Joseph Henry.|My son's name is Henry. 我的儿子叫亨利。|The inductance of the coil is 10 henries.  这个线圈的电感是10亨利。|近义词：(for given name) Harry, Hank|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
