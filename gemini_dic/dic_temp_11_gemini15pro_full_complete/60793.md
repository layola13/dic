electron-beam:/ɪˈlektrɒn biːm/ (US) /ɪˈlektrɒn biːm/ (UK)| n. |电子束|A stream of electrons focused into a narrow beam, often used in scientific instruments and electronic devices. |Electron-beam lithography is used to create very small patterns on semiconductor chips. 电子束光刻技术用于在半导体芯片上创建非常小的图案。|
Electron beams are used in some types of welding.  电子束用于某些类型的焊接。|synonyms: electron stream, cathode ray| antonyms: N/A |noun
