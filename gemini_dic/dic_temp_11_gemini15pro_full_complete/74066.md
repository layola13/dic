fossiliferous:/ˌfɑːsəˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
含化石的；多化石的
Containing fossils; rich in fossils.
The cliffs are fossiliferous, containing numerous ammonite and trilobite fossils. 这些悬崖富含化石，包含大量的菊石和三叶虫化石。
Synonyms: fossil-bearing, fossil-rich
Antonyms: unfossiliferous, barren


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fossilis "dug up" + -ferous "bearing."
