grandmamma:/ˈɡrændməˌmɑː/ /ˈɡrændmɑːmə/ | n. |奶奶；外婆|An informal and affectionate term for grandmother, often used by children.| Example: My grandmamma used to bake the best cookies. 我的奶奶/外婆以前烤的饼干最好吃。| Example:  Grandmamma told us wonderful stories before bedtime. 奶奶/外婆在睡前给我们讲精彩的故事。| 近义词: grandmother, grandma, granny, nan | 反义词: grandson, granddaughter | 词性: noun

词源: From French *grand-maman*, from *grand* "big" + *maman* "mama".
