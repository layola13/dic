interiorly:/ɪnˈtɪəriərli/
adverb
内部地；精神上地；内心深处地
Inwardly; in the inner parts; privately; mentally.
She smiled exteriorly, but interiorly she was fuming. 她表面上微笑着，但内心却怒火中烧。
Synonyms: inwardly, internally, within
Antonyms: exteriorly, outwardly, externally
词性: 副词
