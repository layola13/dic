overskeptical:/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪkəl/| adj.|过度怀疑的，怀疑过度的|Inclined to excessive skepticism; too ready to doubt or disbelieve.|He was overskeptical about the new project, raising objections at every turn. 他对新项目过度怀疑，处处提出反对意见。|She's so overskeptical that she doesn't believe anything anyone tells her. 她太怀疑了，以至于她不相信任何人告诉她的话。|近义词：cynical, distrustful, dubious|反义词：credulous, gullible, trusting|词性:形容词

词源:
From over- + skeptical.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Skeptical" comes from the Greek "skeptikos" meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful," referring to a philosophical school of thought that questioned the possibility of certainty.
