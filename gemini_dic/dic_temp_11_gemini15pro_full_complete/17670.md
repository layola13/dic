becomed:/bɪˈkʌmd/ | v. (archaic) | 变得，成为 | This word is archaic and no longer in common use. It was the past participle of the verb "become" in older forms of English. |  He becomed a knight. (Archaic - He became a knight.) 他成为了一名骑士。（古语 - 他成为了一名骑士。）|近义词: become, got, grew | 反义词:  | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *become*, past participle of *becumen* (“to happen, become”), from Old English *becuman* (“to arrive, come to pass, become”), from *be-* + *cuman* (“to come”). Cognate with Dutch *bekomen* (“to receive, get”), German *bekommen* (“to get, receive”).
