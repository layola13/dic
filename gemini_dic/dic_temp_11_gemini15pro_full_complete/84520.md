hambone:/ˈhæmbəʊn/ (US), /ˈhæmbəʊn/ (UK)| n.|猪腿；火腿骨；（非裔美国人俚语）手臂；黑人|A hambone is the end of a cured ham, containing the leg bone.  It's also used as slang, particularly in African American Vernacular English, to refer to the arm, or sometimes to a Black person (often considered offensive).|She made delicious soup from a smoked hambone. 她用熏猪腿骨做了一锅美味的汤。|Synonyms: ham hock, ham, (slang for arm) arm, limb|Antonyms: (no direct antonym, could consider lean meat as opposite of fatty hambone) |noun
