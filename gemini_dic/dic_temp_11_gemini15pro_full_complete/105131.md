kestrel:/ˈkɛstrəl/| n. |红隼|A small falcon with a reddish-brown back and tail, known for hovering in the air while hunting.|例句：A kestrel hovered over the field, searching for prey. 一只红隼在田野上空盘旋，寻找猎物。

例句：The kestrel's sharp eyesight allows it to spot small rodents from a great height. 红隼敏锐的视力使它能够从很高的地方发现小型啮齿动物。|近义词：falcon, hawk|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific animal) |词性:noun
