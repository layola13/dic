gasps:/ɡæsps/ | n. | 喘气，倒抽气 | sudden, short intakes of breath, often due to surprise, shock, or pain |  She let out a series of gasps after running the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后发出了一阵阵喘气声。| synonyms: breaths, pants, puffs | antonyms: exhales | noun

gasp:/ɡæsp/ | v. | 喘气，倒抽气 | to inhale suddenly and sharply, as in surprise or shock | He gasped when he saw the size of the spider. 当他看到蜘蛛的大小时，他倒吸了一口气。 | synonyms: pant, puff, choke | antonyms: exhale | verb
