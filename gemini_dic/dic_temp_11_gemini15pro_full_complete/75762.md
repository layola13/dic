fulminatory:/ˈfʊlmɪnətɔːri/ /ˈfʊlmɪneɪtɔːri/
adjective
爆发的；霹雳似的；迅猛的
coming on suddenly and with great severity; explosive
例句：The disease had a fulminatory course, rapidly progressing to a critical stage. 这种疾病发展迅猛，迅速恶化到危重阶段。

例句：The patient experienced fulminatory heart failure. 病人经历了爆发性心力衰竭。

近义词：explosive, sudden, violent, rapid, swift, impetuous, fierce
反义词：gradual, slow, lingering, chronic
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *fulminātōrius*, from fulmināre ‘to strike with lightning’, from fulmen, fulmin- ‘lightning bolt’.
