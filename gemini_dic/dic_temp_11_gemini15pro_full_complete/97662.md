inhibitingly:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋli/
| adv. |抑制地，约束地|In a way that restrains or hinders a process or activity.  Restricting or hindering something.  |Her shyness inhibitingly affected her performance. 她的羞怯抑制了她的发挥。|Synonyms: restrictively, repressively, suppressively|Antonyms: encouragingly,  stimulatingly
|词性: 副词

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/
| v. |抑制，约束|To hinder, restrain, or prevent (an action or process).|Fear inhibited him from speaking up.  恐惧阻止了他大声说话。|Synonyms: restrain, hinder, impede, check, curb|Antonyms: encourage, promote, stimulate
|词性: 动词

inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/
| n. |抑制，约束|A feeling that makes one self-conscious and unable to act in a relaxed and natural way.|She overcame her inhibitions and sang beautifully. 她克服了拘束，优美地唱了起来。|Synonyms: restraint, reserve, self-consciousness|Antonyms: boldness, confidence
|词性: 名词
