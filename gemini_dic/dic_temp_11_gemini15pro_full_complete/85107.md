hardenabilities:/ˌhɑːrdənəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可硬化性，硬化能力 | The ability of a metal, especially steel, to be hardened by heat treatment.  It refers to the depth to which a metal can be hardened. |  The hardenabilities of different steel alloys vary significantly. 不同钢合金的可硬化性差异很大。| near synonyms: hardenability, tempers | antonym: softness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From harden + -ability.  "Harden" comes from Old English "heardian," meaning "to make hard." "-ability" is a suffix denoting capability or capacity.
