neoplasm:/ˈniːəˌplæzəm/ | n. |肿瘤；赘生物 |An abnormal mass of tissue that forms when cells grow and divide more than they should or do not die when they should.  A neoplasm can be benign (not cancer) or malignant (cancer). |The doctor discovered a neoplasm in her lung. 医生在她的肺部发现了一个肿瘤。|  近义词:tumor, growth, lesion | 反义词: healthy tissue | 词性: noun
