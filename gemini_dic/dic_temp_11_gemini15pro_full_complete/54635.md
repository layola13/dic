dismission:/dɪsˈmɪʃən/
n.
解雇，免职，解散
The act of sending someone away or allowing them to leave.
His dismission from the company was unexpected. 他被公司解雇出乎意料。
The court ordered the dismission of the case. 法院下令驳回此案。
dismissal, discharge, release
appointment, retention
noun
