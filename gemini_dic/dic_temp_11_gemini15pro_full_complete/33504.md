cicada: /sɪˈkeɪdə/ /sɪˈkɑːdə/
cicada: n. | 蝉 | A large homopterous insect with long transparent wings, occurring chiefly in warm countries. The male produces a loud, shrill droning sound. | The cicada's song filled the summer air.  蝉鸣充满了夏日的空气。 | 近义词:  None | 反义词:  None | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Latin *cicada, from earlier *quīquēda, ultimately of imitative origin.
