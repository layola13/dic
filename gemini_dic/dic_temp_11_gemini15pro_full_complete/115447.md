"manua" 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想问 "manual"。如果是这样，以下是 "manual" 的解析：

manual:/ˈmænjuəl/| adj. n.|手工的；体力的；手册；指南|done or operated by hand rather than automatically. A book of instructions, especially for operating a machine or learning a subject.|Example:  The car has a manual transmission. 这辆车是手动挡的。|Synonym: handbook, guidebook, instruction book|Antonym: automatic, automated|adjective

manual:/ˈmænjuəl/| n.|手册；指南|A book of instructions, especially for operating a machine or learning a subject.|Example: I need to consult the computer manual. 我需要查阅电脑手册。|Synonym: handbook, guidebook, instruction book|Antonym: automatic, automated|noun
