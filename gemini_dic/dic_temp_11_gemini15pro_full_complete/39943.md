constructing:/kənˈstrʌktɪŋ/
v.
建造，构建；创立
Present participle of "construct".  The act of building or creating something.
They are constructing a new bridge across the river. 他们正在建造一座横跨河流的新桥。
The company is constructing a new factory.  这家公司正在建造一座新工厂。
Synonyms: building, erecting, assembling, creating, making
Antonyms: destroying, demolishing, dismantling
