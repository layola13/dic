half-deified:/ˌhæf ˈdiːɪfaɪd/
adjective
半神化的；被奉为半神的
partly made into a god; treated as partly divine
The Roman emperors were often half-deified by their subjects. 罗马皇帝经常被他们的臣民半神化。

The legendary hero was half-deified after his death.  这位传奇英雄死后被奉为半神。

synonym:semi-divine, partly deified
antonym:mortal, human
