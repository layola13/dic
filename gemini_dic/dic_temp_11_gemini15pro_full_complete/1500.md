acetic:/əˈsiːtɪk/ | adj. |醋的，乙酸的|Relating to or containing acetic acid|Vinegar has a strong acetic smell.  醋有很浓的醋酸味。| Acetic acid is the main component of vinegar. 乙酸是醋的主要成分。|近义词:vinegary|反义词: alkaline, basic|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *acēticus*, from *acētum* (“vinegar”)  源自拉丁语 *acēticus*，来自 *acētum*（“醋”）
