loki:/ˈloʊki/ (US), /ˈləʊki/ (UK)| n.|洛基|Loki is a Norse god known for his trickery and shapeshifting abilities. He is often portrayed as a villain or anti-hero in Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe.|Loki is often depicted as Thor's adopted brother and a constant source of chaos. 洛基经常被描绘成托尔的养兄弟，也是混乱的持续来源。

Loki’s mischievous nature often leads him into trouble. 洛基淘气的本性经常让他陷入麻烦。|近义词：trickster, mischief-maker, shape-shifter|反义词:hero, god of order|noun
