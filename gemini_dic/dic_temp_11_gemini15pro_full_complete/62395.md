endoctrine:/ˈɛndɒktraɪn/ (UK) /ˈɛndɑːktrɪn/ (US)| n. vt.|教义，主义；灌输|A system of beliefs accepted as authoritative by a group or movement.  To teach someone to accept a set of beliefs without questioning them.|
例句：The political endoctrine of communism. 共产主义的政治教义。

例句：Children are endoctrined from an early age to believe in the party.  孩子们从小就被灌输要信仰这个党。

近义词：dogma, creed, indoctrinate, instill, inculcate|
反义词：question, challenge, freethinking|
词性：noun, verb|
