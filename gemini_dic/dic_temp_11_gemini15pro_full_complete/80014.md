go-round:/ˈɡoʊ raʊnd/ |n.|旋转木马；一轮；一份；一圈|A carousel; a circular ride with wooden horses or other animals; a single complete sequence of a series of actions or events; a single portion or share of something distributed among a group.|The children loved riding the go-round at the carnival. 孩子们喜欢在嘉年华上玩旋转木马。|synonyms: carousel, merry-go-round, rotation, cycle, round, turn|antonyms: standstill, halt|noun

go-round:/ˈɡoʊ raʊnd/ |adj.|循环的；轮流的|Recurring; rotating; happening in a regular sequence.|There's a go-round system for using the computer. 使用电脑实行轮流制度。|synonyms: rotating, recurring, cyclical, circulating|antonyms: static, fixed|adjective
