activ:/ˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 活跃的；积极的；有效的；主动的；现役的 | characterized by energetic activity; engaged in action; brisk; lively; causing action or change; operative; in operation or use; currently serving in the armed forces |  The volcano is still active. 这座火山仍然很活跃。 | synonyms: energetic, vigorous, brisk, lively, dynamic, effective, operative | antonyms: inactive, passive, dormant, inert | adjective


/ˈæktɪv/ | n. | 积极分子；主动语态 | a person or thing that is active or lively;  the grammatical form of a verb in which the subject performs or causes the action expressed by the verb  |  The activists protested against the new law. 积极分子抗议新法律。  | synonyms: participant, doer | antonyms: bystander, observer | noun



Etymology:
From Late Latin *activus, from Latin actus (“act, action”), past participle of agō (“I do, act, drive”).
