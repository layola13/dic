fishwife:/ˈfɪʃˌwaɪf/| n.|鱼贩，卖鱼的女人；泼妇，悍妇|A woman who sells fish, often depicted as loud and quarrelsome.|The fishwife at the market had a booming voice that could be heard across the square.  市场上卖鱼的女人的声音洪亮，整个广场都能听到。|近义词：fishmonger (more formal), virago (for the "shrew" sense)|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly a quiet, demure woman)|noun

fishwoman:/ˈfɪʃˌwʊmən/| n.|鱼贩，卖鱼的女人；泼妇，悍妇|A woman who sells fish, often depicted as loud and quarrelsome.
