hooray:/hʊˈreɪ/ /hʊˈrɑː/ | int. n.|万岁；好哇；欢呼声|An exclamation of joy, approval, or encouragement.|例句：Hooray! We won!  好哇！我们赢了！

例句：The crowd erupted in hoorays.  人群爆发出欢呼声。

近义词：hurrah, yay, cheers, yes!|反义词:boo, hiss|词性:感叹词, 名词
