anxious: /ˈæŋkʃəs/ (US) /ˈæŋkʃəs/ (UK)| adj.|焦虑的，担忧的，渴望的|Experiencing worry, nervousness, or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome; greatly desiring something.|例句：I'm anxious about the upcoming exam. 我对即将到来的考试感到焦虑。|例句：She was anxious to hear from him. 她渴望收到他的来信。|近义词：worried, concerned, apprehensive, eager, keen|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, indifferent

anxious: /ˈæŋkʃəs/ (US) /ˈæŋkʃəs/ (UK)| n.|焦虑的人|A person who is anxious.|例句: Anxious people tend to overthink. 焦虑的人容易想太多. |例句:  The anxious in the waiting room paced nervously. 在候诊室里，焦虑的人来回踱步，显得非常紧张。|近义词: worrier|反义词: optimist


词性: adjective, noun
