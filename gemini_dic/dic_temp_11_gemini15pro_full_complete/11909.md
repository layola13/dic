arrowroot:/ˈærəʊˌruːt/ | n. | 竹芋，竹芋粉 | A tropical American plant with starchy edible roots.  The starch obtained from the root of this plant, used in cooking as a thickener. |  She used arrowroot to thicken the sauce. 她用竹芋粉使酱汁变稠。| synonyms: starch, tapioca | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a foodstuff) | noun

arrowroots:/ˈærəʊˌruːts/ | n. | 竹芋（复数），竹芋粉（复数）|  Plural form of arrowroot.  Refers to multiple arrowroot plants or multiple batches of the starch. |  She bought two bags of arrowroots. 她买了两袋竹芋粉。| synonyms: starches, tapiocas | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a foodstuff) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From the Carib *aru-aru, meaning "meal of meals".  Originally, the root was used to draw out poison from arrow wounds.
