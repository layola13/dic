authoress:/ˈɔːθərɪs/| n.|女作家；女作者|A female author.  A term now generally considered outdated and potentially sexist, as "author" is typically used for both male and female writers.|例句：The authoress of the novel was known for her sharp wit.  这部小说的女作者以其敏锐的机智而闻名。

例句：While the term authoress was common in the past, it's now considered preferable to use "author" regardless of gender. 虽然“authoress”这个词在过去很常见，但现在认为无论性别如何，使用“author”更好。|近义词：author, writer, novelist (if writing novels)|反义词: reader, audience|词性:noun
