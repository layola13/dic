oryza:/əˈraɪzə/ | n. |稻, 稻属|A genus of grasses having a terminal one-flowered spikelet, comprising the rice plants.  This is the scientific name for the rice genus.|Oryza sativa is the most commonly cultivated species of rice. 稻 sativa 是最常种植的稻米品种。|Synonyms: rice plant, paddy|Antonyms: N/A (It's a genus name; direct antonyms are not applicable.)|Noun
