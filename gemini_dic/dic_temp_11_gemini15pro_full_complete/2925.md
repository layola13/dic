advisories:/ədˈvaɪzəriz/| n.|公告，报告，建议，咨询|official warnings or announcements about a particular situation, often involving danger or risk.  These are typically issued by governments or other authorities to inform the public.|The government issued travel advisories warning citizens about the risks of visiting certain countries. 政府发布了旅行公告，警告公民访问某些国家的风险。|synonyms: warnings, cautions, notices, bulletins|antonyms: reassurances, encouragements|noun

advisories:/ədˈvaɪzəriz/| n.|报告，汇报|reports or briefings providing information or recommendations on a specific topic.|The company's financial advisories were bleak, predicting further losses in the coming quarter. 该公司的财务报告很不乐观，预测下一季度将进一步亏损。|synonyms: reports, briefings, summaries|antonyms: concealments, suppressions|noun
