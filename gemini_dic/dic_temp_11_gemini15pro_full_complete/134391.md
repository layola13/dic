nonintoxicatingly:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈtɑːksɪkeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
无醉意地；不使人陶醉地
In a way that does not cause intoxication.
This beverage is nonintoxicatingly delicious.  这种饮料美味而不醉人。
Near synonyms: soberly, temperately
Opposite: intoxicatingly
词性: 副词
