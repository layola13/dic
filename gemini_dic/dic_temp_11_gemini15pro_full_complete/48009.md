decipherers:/dɪˈsaɪfərərz/
n.
解密者，破译者
People who decipher codes or messages are called decipherers.
The decipherers worked tirelessly to break the enemy's code.  解密者不知疲倦地工作以破解敌人的密码。
近义词：cryptanalysts, codebreakers, decoders
反义词：encoders, encryptors
词性：名词
