hang-a-left:/ˌhæŋ ə ˈlɛft/ | phrasal verb | 左转 | To turn left.  This is an informal, colloquial expression. | Example: "Hang a left at the next intersection."  在下一个路口左转。 | Example: "When you see the big oak tree, hang a left." 当你看到那棵大橡树时，左转。 | Synonyms: turn left, make a left | Antonyms: turn right, make a right | verb phrase
