out-grossed:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊst/
verb
超过...的票房收入
to surpass in gross earnings, especially at the box office.
"The new superhero movie out-grossed the previous record holder." 这部新的超级英雄电影票房收入超过了之前的纪录保持者。
"Avatar out-grossed Titanic to become the highest-grossing film of all time." 《阿凡达》的票房超过了《泰坦尼克号》，成为有史以来票房最高的电影。
synonyms: out-earned, surpassed, exceeded
antonyms: underperformed, lagged behind
