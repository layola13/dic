divisibleness:/dɪˌvɪzəˈbɪlnəs/| n. |可分性，可除性|The quality or state of being divisible; the capacity of being divided without a remainder.|The divisibility of 12 by 3, 4, and 6 demonstrates its composite nature. 12 可以被 3、4 和 6 整除，这表明它是一个合数。|近义词：divisibility|反义词: indivisibility|词性:noun
