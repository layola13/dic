livingnesses:/ˈlɪvɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
生命力；活力；生动性
The quality or state of being alive or lively; vitality.
The painting captured the livingness of the natural world. 这幅画捕捉到了自然界的活力。
liveliness, vibrancy, animation
inertness, lifelessness
