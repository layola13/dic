dedicatory:/ˈdedɪkətɔːri/
adjective
奉献的，献给…的
Expressing or showing dedication.
This book is dedicatory to my parents. 这本书献给我的父母。
Near Synonyms:  commemorative, tribute, honoring, devoted
Antonyms:  irreverent, disrespectful
词性: 形容词


dedication:/ˌdedɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
奉献，献词
An act of dedicating something to a cause, person, or purpose; the inscription in a book dedicating it to someone.
The dedication of the new hospital was a momentous occasion. 新医院的落成典礼是一个重要的时刻。
Near Synonyms:  commitment, devotion, consecration
Antonyms:  abandonment, neglect
词性: 名词


dedicate:/ˈdedɪkeɪt/
verb
奉献，致力于
Set apart and consecrate to a deity or to a sacred purpose; devote.
He dedicated his life to helping others. 他一生致力于帮助他人。
Near Synonyms:  devote, commit, consecrate
Antonyms:  forsake, abandon
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dedicatorius "of dedication," from Latin dedicare "to dedicate," from de- "down, completely" + dicare "to proclaim, dedicate."
