ambassadrice:/æmˈbæsədrɪs/ | n. | 女大使；女使节 | A female ambassador; a woman who acts as an official representative of a country to another. |  The ambassadrice presented her credentials to the president. 女大使向总统递交了国书。|  The French ambassadrice hosted a reception at the embassy. 法国女大使在使馆举行招待会。 | Synonyms: ambassador (gender-neutral), envoy, diplomat, emissary | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific role, but perhaps: rebel, traitor, dissident - in the sense of opposing their own country) | noun
