concordance:/kənˈkɔːrdəns/ | n. | 一致，和谐；索引；词语索引；调和 | agreement or harmony between people or groups; an alphabetical list of the principal words used in a book or body of work, showing their context | The concordance between the two accounts of the incident is remarkable.  这两个对事件的描述非常一致。 | synonyms: agreement, harmony, unity, accord, consensus | antonyms: disagreement, discord, conflict, disharmony | noun
