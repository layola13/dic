odic:/ˈɒdɪk/ | adj. | 歌颂的；颂歌体的；有节奏的 | Relating to or in the form of an ode; having a rhythmical quality. | The poem had an odic quality, praising the beauty of nature. 这首诗具有颂歌的特质，歌颂了自然之美。| Example: His odic style is reminiscent of the Romantic poets. 他的颂歌风格让人想起浪漫主义诗人。| Synonyms: lyrical, poetic, rhapsodic | Antonyms: prosaic, mundane | adjective
