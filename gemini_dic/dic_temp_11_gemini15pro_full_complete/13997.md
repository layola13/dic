auriga:/ɔːˈraɪɡə/ | n. | 御夫座; (尤指古罗马的)双轮战车驭手 | A constellation of the northern hemisphere near Perseus and Gemini, containing the bright star Capella.  A charioteer, especially in ancient Rome. |  例句：Auriga is a prominent constellation in the winter sky. 御夫座是冬季夜空中一个显著的星座。

例句：The charioteer, an auriga of great skill, guided his horses expertly through the crowded streets. 那位驭手，一位技艺高超的御夫，熟练地驾驶着他的马匹穿过拥挤的街道。| 近义词：charioteer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *auriga*, from *aurea* (“bridle, reins”) + *-ga* (agent suffix), from *agō* (“I drive, lead”).
