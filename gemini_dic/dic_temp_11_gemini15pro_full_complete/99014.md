interacts:/ˌɪntərˈækts/
verb
互动，相互作用，互相影响
acts together or has an effect on something else.
The website interacts with the user to provide personalized recommendations.  该网站与用户互动，以提供个性化推荐。
Social media allows users to interact with each other online. 社交媒体允许用户在线互动。
近义词:communicates, reacts, engages, connects
反义词:ignores, avoids, disconnects
词性:v.
