innervate:/ˈɪnərveɪt/
verb
支配；使受神经支配；激励；赋予活力
to supply (a part of the body) with nerves; to stimulate to action
例句：The radial nerve innervates the dorsal aspect of the arm. 桡神经支配手臂的背面。

例句：The coach's speech innervated the team to play their best. 教练的讲话激励了队员们发挥出最佳水平。

近义词：stimulate, energize, invigorate, activate
反义词：weaken, enfeeble, debilitate
词性:v.
