half-blindly:/ˌhɑːf ˈblaɪndli/ | adv. | 半盲目地；不完全失明地；视而不见地 | Doing something without full awareness or attention, as if partially blind. It implies acting with limited perception or understanding of the situation. | He walked half-blindly into the room, not noticing the broken glass on the floor. 他半盲目地走进房间，没有注意到地板上的碎玻璃。

She half-blindly followed the crowd, not really knowing where she was going. 她茫然地跟着人群走，也不知道自己要去哪里。 | Near synonyms: partially blindly, unseeingly, heedlessly, inattentively | Near antonyms: attentively, carefully, mindfully, deliberately | adverb
