baldest:/ˈbɔːldɪst/| adj.|最秃的|Superlative form of bald: having the least hair on the head. |He was the baldest man I had ever seen. 他是我见过的秃顶最严重的人。|The baldest eagle perched atop the flagpole.  那只秃得最厉害的鹰栖息在旗杆顶上。|近义词：hairless, glabrous|反义词：hairiest, shaggiest|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *beald "shining, white," from Proto-Germanic *baldaz "white, shining," from PIE root *bhel- (1) "to shine, flash, burn." The sense shifted from "white patch on the head" to "hairless" probably by influence of Old French bald "white-faced, piebald."
