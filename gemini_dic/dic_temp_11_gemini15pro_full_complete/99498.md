interflowing:/ˌɪntərˈfləʊɪŋ/
adjective
相互流动的，交汇流动的
flowing together or into one another.
The two rivers are interflowing, creating a beautiful confluence. 这两条河流相互交汇，形成了美丽的汇流。
The interflowing streams created a network of waterways.  交汇的溪流形成了一个水系网络。
近义词: converging, merging, intermingling, blending
反义词: diverging, separating
词性: adjective

interflowing:/ˌɪntərˈfləʊɪŋ/
noun
相互流动，交汇
the act or process of flowing together or into one another.
The interflowing of the two cultures created a unique blend of traditions. 两种文化的交融创造了独特的传统融合。
The interflowing of ideas is essential for innovation.  思想的交流对创新至关重要。
近义词: confluence, convergence, merging, intermingling
反义词: divergence, separation
词性: noun
