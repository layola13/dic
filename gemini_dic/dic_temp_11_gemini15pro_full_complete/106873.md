laburnine:/ləˈbɜːrnɪn/| adj.|金链花的，金链花毒素的|Of or relating to laburnum; containing laburnine (a poisonous alkaloid).|The laburnine blossoms hung heavy on the branches. 金链花的花朵沉甸甸地挂在枝头。|The laburnine toxin can be dangerous to small children. 金链花毒素对幼儿可能有危险。|Synonyms: golden chain, cytisine|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

laburnine:/ləˈbɜːrnɪn/| n.|金链花毒素|A poisonous alkaloid present in laburnum.|Laburnine can cause vomiting and convulsions if ingested. 金链花毒素如果摄入会导致呕吐和抽搐。|The laburnine in the seeds is particularly potent. 种子中的金链花毒素尤其强烈。|Synonyms: cytisine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
