air-dropping:/ˈeə(r)ˌdrɒpɪŋ/ | n. |空投；空降|The act of delivering supplies or people to a location by parachute from an aircraft.|例句：The air-dropping of food and medical supplies saved many lives in the disaster zone.  灾区的食品和医疗用品空投拯救了许多生命。

例句：The military conducted an air-dropping of troops behind enemy lines.  军队在敌后进行了空投部队。|近义词：parachuting, airlift|反义词: ground transportation|词性:名词(gerund)


air-drop:/ˈeə(r)drɒp/ | v. |空投；空降|To deliver supplies or people to a location by parachute from an aircraft.|例句：They air-dropped food and water to the stranded hikers.  他们向受困的徒步旅行者空投了食物和水。

例句：The plane air-dropped humanitarian aid to the refugees. 飞机向难民空投了人道主义援助。|近义词：parachute, airlift|反义词: transport by land|词性:动词
