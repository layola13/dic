burbling:/ˈbɜːblɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɜːblɪŋ/ (UK)| v.  n. |发出汩汩声；潺潺流水；兴奋地闲聊|Making a gentle bubbling sound, like a brook. Also, talking rapidly and excitedly, often about unimportant things.|The brook burbled along its rocky bed. 小溪沿着岩石河床潺潺流过。

The baby was burbling happily in her crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里高兴地咿呀学语。|近义词：gurgling, babbling, chattering, prattling|反义词：silent, quiet|词性:动词, 名词

Etymology: From the verb "burble," which is imitative of the sound.  Likely related to Middle English "burbelen," frequentative of "burben" (to bubble).
