experiential:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəl/ /ɛkˌspɪriˈɛnʃəl/| adj.|经验的；体验的；基于经验的|Based on experience; derived from or providing experience.|例句：Experiential learning is more engaging than traditional lectures. 体验式学习比传统讲座更吸引人。

例句：The museum offers visitors an experiential journey through history.  这家博物馆为游客提供了一次穿越历史的体验之旅。

近义词：empirical, practical, hands-on, observed|反义词：theoretical, academic, hypothetical|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *experientialis, from Latin experientia "trial, proof, experience" + -al.
