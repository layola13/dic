call-a-day:/ˈkɔːl ə deɪ/| n.|一天一次的电话；每天一次的拜访；|A sales strategy involving contacting a potential customer once per day.|例句：The call-a-day approach can be effective, but also annoying if overdone. 每天一次的电话拜访策略可能有效，但如果做得过火也会令人反感。

例句：He started a call-a-day campaign to reach out to new clients. 他开始了一天一次的电话活动，以联系新客户。|近义词：daily contact, regular communication|反义词: infrequent contact|noun
