drawlingness:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋnəs/ | n. | 慢吞吞的说话方式；拖长腔调 | The quality of speaking in a slow, drawn-out manner, often considered lazy or affected. |  His drawlingness made it difficult to follow his story.  他慢吞吞的说话方式使他的故事难以理解。| synonyms: slowness of speech, prolonged articulation | antonyms:  briskness, rapidity of speech | noun


drawlingness:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋnəs/ | n. | 拖沓的语气；懒洋洋的腔调 | The quality of speaking in a slow, lengthened tone, often perceived as lazy or uninterested. | Her drawlingness suggested she wasn't taking the conversation seriously. 她拖沓的语气表明她没有认真对待这次谈话。| synonyms:  languor,  sluggishness in speech | antonyms:  animation,  liveliness in speech | noun
