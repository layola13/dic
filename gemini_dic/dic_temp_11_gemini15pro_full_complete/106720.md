kymnel:/ˈkɪmnɛl/ | n. | 基姆内尔（一种加香料的甜酒）| A liqueur flavored with caraway seeds, cumin, and fennel. |  例句：She offered him a glass of kymnel after dinner.  她晚饭后给他一杯基姆内尔酒。

例句：The kymnel had a warm, spicy aroma. 这基姆内尔酒有着温暖的辛辣香味。 | 近义词：liqueur, cordial | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of liqueur) | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English *kymmel, from Middle Dutch kummel, ultimately from Latin cuminum (“cumin”). Related to cumin and caraway.
