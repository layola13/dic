lead-times:/ˈliːd taɪmz/| n.|交付周期; 提前期; 前置时间|The time between initiating a process and its completion, often used in manufacturing and supply chain management.|The factory needs to shorten its lead-times to meet customer demand. 工厂需要缩短交货周期以满足客户需求。

The project was delayed due to long lead-times for key components.  由于关键组件的交付周期长，项目被推迟了。|近义词：delivery time, turnaround time, waiting time|反义词:|词性:noun
