harlo:/ˈhɑːrloʊ/| interjection|你好；喂（表示问候或引起注意）|A greeting or exclamation used to attract attention or express greeting, often considered informal or dialectal.  It seems to be a variation or misspelling of "hello".|Harlo, is anyone there? 喂，有人在吗？|近义词：hello, hi, hey|反义词: goodbye, farewell|词性:感叹词

词源: Likely a variant or misspelling of "hello," which itself has a complex etymology.  It may be influenced by earlier forms of greeting like "hallo" or "hullo," ultimately related to Old High German "halâ, holâ" (used to call attention).
