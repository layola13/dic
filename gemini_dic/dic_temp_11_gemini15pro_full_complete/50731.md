derogatory:/dɪˈrɒɡət(ə)ri/ /dɪˈrɑːɡətɔːri/ | adj. | 贬损的，诋毁的 |  Expressed as worthless or in negative terms; critical or disrespectful. |  She made some derogatory remarks about her ex-husband. 她对前夫说了一些贬损的话。| synonyms: disparaging, belittling, denigrating, demeaning, insulting, offensive, pejorative, slighting | antonyms: complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory | adjective

derogate:/ˈdɛrəˌɡeɪt/ /ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/ | v. | 贬低，诋毁 |  to cause to seem less serious; to detract from |  He never missed an opportunity to derogate his rivals. 他从不放过任何贬低对手的机会。| synonyms: belittle, disparage, denigrate, depreciate, detract from, diminish, minimize, underestimate, undervalue | antonyms: praise, extol, laud, commend, enhance, elevate | verb
