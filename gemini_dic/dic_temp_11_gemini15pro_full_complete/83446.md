habitator:/ˈhæbɪteɪtər/ | n. | 居住者，栖息动物 |  An inhabitant; a dweller.  One that inhabits or dwells in a place. |  The habitators of the forest include deer, squirrels, and various birds. 森林的栖息动物包括鹿、松鼠和各种鸟类。 | inhabitant, resident, dweller, occupant | nomad, transient | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *habitator, from Latin habitare "to inhabit," frequentative of habēre "to have, hold, possess."
