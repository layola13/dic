fender-bender:/ˈfɛndərˌbɛndər/| n.|轻微的交通事故，小碰撞|A minor traffic accident, typically involving minimal damage to the vehicles.|例句：I was involved in a fender-bender this morning, but luckily no one was hurt. 我今天早上卷入了一起轻微的交通事故，但幸运的是没有人受伤。

例句：The fender-bender only resulted in a few scratches on the car. 这起小碰撞只在车上留下几道划痕。|近义词：minor accident, collision, bump|反义词:major accident, crash|词性:noun
