catamaran:/ˌkætəməˈræn/ | n. |双体船，帆船|A boat with two parallel hulls.|We sailed around the bay on a catamaran. 我们乘双体船绕着海湾航行。| The catamaran sliced through the waves. 双体船破浪前进。 | 近义词：twin-hulled boat, outrigger canoe (somewhat) | 反义词: monohull | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil *kaṭṭumaram*  "tied wood," from *kaṭṭu*  "to tie" + *maram*  "tree, wood."  Originally referred to a type of raft made of logs tied together, used in southern India and Sri Lanka.
