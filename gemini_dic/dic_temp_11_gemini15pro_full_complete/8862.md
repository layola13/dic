antifeministic:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪstɪk/
adjective
反对女权主义的
Opposed to or hostile towards feminism and its goals of achieving political, social, economic, and personal equality of the sexes.
His antifeministic views alienated many of his female colleagues.  他的反女权主义观点疏远了他的许多女性同事。
She wrote an article criticizing the antifeministic backlash. 她写了一篇文章批评反女权主义的强烈反对。
Synonyms: misogynist, sexist, anti-feminist
Antonyms: feminist, pro-feminist
词性: adjective
