fissiparous:/fɪˈsɪpərəs/
adjective
分裂的，裂变的
Inclined to cause or undergo division into separate parts or groups.
The fissiparous tendencies of the political party led to its eventual downfall.  这个政党的内部分裂倾向导致了它最终的垮台。
The fissiparous nature of the single-celled organism allowed it to reproduce rapidly.  这种单细胞生物的分裂特性使其能够快速繁殖。
近义词: divisive, schismatic, factious
反义词: unifying, cohesive, integrating
词性: adjective

fissiparous:/fɪˈsɪpərəs/
adjective
(尤指原子核)易分裂的
(especially of atomic nuclei) tending to undergo fission.
Uranium-235 is a fissiparous isotope used in nuclear reactors. 铀-235是一种用于核反应堆的可裂变同位素。
Certain fissiparous materials are essential for the development of nuclear weapons. 某些易裂变材料对于核武器的研制至关重要。
近义词: fissionable
反义词: non-fissionable
词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *fissiparus (“splitting, dividing”), from fissus, perfect passive participle of findō (“I split, divide”) + -parus, from pariō (“I give birth, produce”).
