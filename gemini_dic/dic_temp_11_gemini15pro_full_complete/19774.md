billionths:/ˈbɪljənθs/
noun
十亿分之一
One part in a billion; 10⁻⁹.
The concentration of the pollutant was measured in billionths.  这种污染物的浓度是以十亿分之一来衡量的。
The margin of error is only a few billionths of a second. 误差幅度只有几百亿分之一秒。
Synonyms:  parts per billion (ppb)
Antonyms: N/A
