noni:/ˈnoʊniː/
n.
诺丽果，海滩桑葚，印度桑椹
A small evergreen tree (Morinda citrifolia) of the madder family, native to Southeast Asia and Australia and widely naturalized in tropical regions. Its fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a food and source of dyes.
The noni fruit has a strong smell and is often used in juices or supplements.  诺丽果气味浓烈，常用于果汁或补充剂中。
Noni juice is said to have many health benefits.  据说诺丽果汁有很多健康益处。
synonyms: Indian mulberry, beach mulberry, cheese fruit
antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a plant/fruit)
