herbages:/ˈhɜːrbɪdʒɪz/
n.
草，牧草，草本植物
Plants suitable for grazing animals; pasture; grass.
The farmer rotated his livestock through different herbages to ensure they had a varied diet.  农民轮流让牲畜在不同的牧草地上吃草，以确保它们的饮食多样化。
The rolling hills were covered in lush herbages.  起伏的山丘上覆盖着茂盛的草地。
近义词:pasture, grass, forage, fodder
反义词:barrenness, desert
