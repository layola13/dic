folksong:/ˈfoʊkˌsɔːŋ/ | n. |民歌|A song that originates in traditional popular culture or that is written in such a style. |例句：The singer performed a beautiful folksong about love and loss. 歌手演唱了一首关于爱与失去的优美民歌。

例句：Folksongs are an important part of many cultures' heritage. 民歌是许多文化遗产的重要组成部分。|近义词：traditional song, folk music|反义词: pop song, popular music|词性: noun
Etymology: folk + song.  "Folk" comes from Old English "folc" meaning "people, nation, tribe." "Song" comes from Old English "sang" meaning "song, poem."
