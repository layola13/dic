jibingly:/ˈdʒaɪbɪŋli/
adverb|嘲讽地，讥讽地|in a mocking or sarcastic manner
He jibingly referred to his rival as "the great pretender." 他讥讽地称他的对手为“伟大的冒牌货”。
She smiled jibingly at his clumsy attempt to dance. 她嘲讽地对他笨拙的舞步笑了笑。
近义词:mockingly, sarcastically, derisively, tauntingly
反义词:respectfully, sincerely, seriously
词性:adverb

词源: From jibe (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Jibe" likely comes from the Dutch "gijben," a nautical term referring to a sudden shift in a sail's position.  This sense of a sudden shift or change likely evolved into the meaning of a sharp or taunting remark.
