musicalization:/ˌmjuːzɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
音乐化;谱曲;配乐
The process of making something into a musical or adding musical elements to it.
The musicalization of the poem was well-received by the audience.  这首诗的音乐化受到了观众的好评。
The play's musicalization added a new dimension to the story.  该剧的配乐为故事增添了新的维度。
近义词：adaptation, arrangement, setting
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "de-musicalization" or "simplification")
