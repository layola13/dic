chimpanzees:/ˌtʃɪmpænˈziːz/| n. |黑猩猩|A small, very intelligent African ape with black or brown fur.|例句：Chimpanzees are known for their intelligence and social behavior. 黑猩猩以它们的智力和社会行为而闻名。

例句：Researchers study chimpanzees to learn more about primate evolution.  研究人员研究黑猩猩以了解更多关于灵长类动物进化的问题。|近义词：ape|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑human 人类) |词性:noun
