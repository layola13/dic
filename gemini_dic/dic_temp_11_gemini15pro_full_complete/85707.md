having:/ˈhævɪŋ/| adj.|富有的，有钱的|Used as an adjective, particularly in compound adjectives, meaning "possessing" or "characterized by."|He's a fun-having kind of guy. 他是个很会享受生活的人。|Synonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich|Antonyms: poor, impoverished|adjective
