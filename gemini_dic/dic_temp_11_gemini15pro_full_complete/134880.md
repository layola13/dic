nonmotile:/ˌnɒnˈməʊtaɪl/
adjective
不能动的，不运动的
Not able to move independently.  Lacking motility; not capable of self-propulsion.
Certain bacteria are nonmotile and rely on air or water currents for dispersal.  某些细菌不能动，依靠气流或水流传播。
non-motile:/ˌnɒnˈmoʊ.taɪl/ (American pronunciation)
adjective
不能动的，不运动的
Not able to move independently.  Lacking motility; not capable of self-propulsion.
The non-motile spores of this fungus are easily spread by the wind.  这种真菌不运动的孢子很容易被风传播。

近义词:immobile, sessile, stationary
反义词:motile, mobile

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "motile" (from Latin "motus," past participle of "movēre," meaning "to move").
