delegation: /ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 代表团，授权，委托 | A group of people chosen or appointed to represent a larger group, or the act of entrusting a task or responsibility to another person. |  The delegation from China arrived in Washington for trade talks. 中国代表团抵达华盛顿进行贸易谈判。|  He gave me the delegation to sign the contract. 他授权我签署合同。 | synonyms: mission, deputation, assignment, commission | antonyms: retention, revocation, refusal | noun

delegation: /ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 代表团 |A group of delegates. | The French delegation to the UN includes diplomats and experts.  法国驻联合国代表团包括外交官和专家。|  The company sent a small delegation to the conference. 公司派了一个小型代表团参加会议。 | synonyms:  contingent, representatives, envoys | antonyms:  individuals,  single person | noun
