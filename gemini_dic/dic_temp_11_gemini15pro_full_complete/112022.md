long-running:/ˈlɒŋ ˈrʌnɪŋ/ /ˈlɔːŋ ˈrʌnɪŋ/ | adj. | 持久的，长期的 | Continuing for a long time. | The company has a long-running dispute with the union.  这家公司与工会存在长期纠纷。

The play had a long-running success on Broadway.  这出戏在百老汇长期成功上演。

近义词: protracted, enduring, extended, continuous, ongoing, persistent
反义词: short-lived, brief, temporary, transient
词性: adjective
词源:  Compound adjective formed from "long" (Old English "lang," meaning "long" in space or time) and "running" (present participle of "run," from Old English "rinnan," meaning "to flow, to run").  The combined meaning refers to something that continues to operate or exist for an extended duration.
