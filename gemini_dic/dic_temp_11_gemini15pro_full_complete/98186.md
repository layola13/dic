insculpture:/ɪnˈskʌlptʃər/ |v. |雕刻；铭刻|To carve or engrave (something) into a surface.|  He insculptured his initials on the tree. 他把他的姓名首字母刻在树上。| The artist insculptured a delicate design onto the silver locket.  艺术家在银质小盒子上雕刻了一个精致的图案。 |近义词：carve, engrave, etch|反义词: erase, efface|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insculptura*, from *insculptus*, past participle of *insculpere* ("to carve in, engrave"), from *in-* ("in, on") + *sculpere* ("to carve").
