anaphylactic:/ˌænəfɪˈlæktɪk/| adj.|过敏性的，过敏反应的|Relating to or exhibiting anaphylaxis; a severe, potentially life-threatening allergic reaction.|She experienced an anaphylactic reaction after eating peanuts. 她吃完花生后发生了过敏性反应。|Synonyms: allergic, hypersensitive|Antonyms: non-allergic, tolerant|adjective

anaphylaxis:/ˌænəfɪˈlæksɪs/| n.|过敏性休克|A severe, potentially life-threatening allergic reaction.|The patient went into anaphylaxis after being stung by a bee. 病人在被蜜蜂蜇伤后发生了过敏性休克。|Synonyms: anaphylactic shock, allergic reaction|Antonyms: tolerance|noun
