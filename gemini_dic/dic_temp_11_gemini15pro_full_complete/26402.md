bycatches:/ˈbaɪkætʃɪz/
n.
兼捕，副渔获物
The unintentional capture of marine life during fishing for a different species.
Fishermen are increasingly concerned about the high number of bycatches they are seeing. 渔民越来越担心他们看到的兼捕数量很高。
Trawling often results in large quantities of bycatches. 拖网捕鱼通常会导致大量的副渔获物。
incidental catches, unintended catches
targeted catches
