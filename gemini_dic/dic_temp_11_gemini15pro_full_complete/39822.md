conspicuousnesses:/kənˈspɪkjuəsnəsɪz/
n.
显著，显眼，引人注目
the state or quality of being conspicuous; noticeability.
Her conspicuousness at the party made her the center of attention.  她在派对上的显眼使她成为关注的中心。
近义词：prominence, noticeability, visibility
反义词：inconspicuousness, obscurity, unnoticeability
词性: 名词

conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/
adj.
显著的，显眼的，引人注目的
standing out so as to be clearly visible.
He was conspicuous by his absence.  他的缺席很引人注目。
近义词：noticeable, prominent, outstanding
反义词：inconspicuous, unnoticeable, hidden
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conspicuosus, from Latin conspicere (“to look at attentively, observe, perceive”), from con- (“together”) + specere (“to look”).
