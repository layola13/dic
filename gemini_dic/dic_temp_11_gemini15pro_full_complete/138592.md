nubiferous:/njuːˈbɪfərəs/| adj.|多云的，乌云密布的|Bringing or carrying clouds; cloudy.  Characterized by clouds. |例句：The nubiferous sky threatened rain. 乌云密布的天空预示着即将下雨。

例句：The hikers were caught in a nubiferous storm.  徒步旅行者遭遇了乌云密布的风暴。|近义词：cloudy, overcast, sunless|反义词：clear, cloudless, sunny|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *nūbifer, from nūbēs (“cloud”) + -fer (“-bearing”).
