nonfuel:/ˌnɒnˈfjuːəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfjuːəl/| adj. n.|非燃料的，非燃料|Not of or relating to fuel; not used as fuel. | The nonfuel costs of operating a nuclear power plant are high. |近义词：non-combustible, nonflammable|反义词:fuel, combustible|形容词/名词

Etymology->
From non- + fuel.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Fuel" comes from Old French "feuaile" meaning "firewood," ultimately from Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace."
