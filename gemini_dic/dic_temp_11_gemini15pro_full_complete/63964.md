episodal:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/ (US) /ˌɛpɪˈzɒdɪkl/ (UK)| adj.| 情节式的；分集的；断断续续的|Occurring in episodes or marked by episodes; happening in parts or segments.|The pain she experienced was episodic, coming and going without warning. 她经历的疼痛是阵发性的，来来去去没有预兆。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, periodic|反义词：continuous, constant|词性:形容词

episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/ (US) /ˌɛpɪˈzɒdɪkl/ (UK)| n.|分集剧|A television series consisting of self-contained episodes.|Many streaming services are now producing episodic content. 许多流媒体服务现在都在制作分集剧。|近义词：series, serial|反义词：movie, film|词性:名词 (rarely used)
