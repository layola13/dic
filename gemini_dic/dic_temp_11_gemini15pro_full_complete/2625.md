adoors:/əˈdɔːrz/ | adv. | 在室内；在屋内 | Inside, indoors.  It is used chiefly in Scottish English and some dialects of English. |  It's cold outside, let's stay adoors.  外面很冷，我们待在室内吧。| 近义词: indoors, inside | 反义词: outdoors, outside | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From a- + doors, where a- is a reduced form of Old English *on (on, in).  Similar to aboard, ashore, afield.
