enterprise: /ˈɛntərpraɪz/ (US) /ˈentəpraɪz/ (UK) | n. | 企业; 事业; 进取心; 创业精神 |  A project or undertaking, typically one that is difficult or requires effort.  A company or business. Initiative and willingness to undertake new projects. |  例句：She showed great enterprise in starting her own business. 她创办自己的企业展现了非凡的进取心。

例句：The new enterprise quickly became successful. 这家新企业很快就获得了成功。 | 近义词：business, venture, undertaking, initiative, ambition, drive | 反义词：apathy, lethargy, inactivity, idleness | 词性: noun

etymology: Middle English entreprise, from Old French, from entreprendre to undertake, from entre- between + prendre to take, from Latin prehendere
