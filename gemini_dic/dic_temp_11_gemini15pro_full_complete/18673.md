bequeath:/bɪˈkwiːð/ /bɪˈkwiːθ/| vt.|遗赠；把…传下去|To leave (property) to someone by means of a will.  To pass (something) on to someone else.|He bequeathed his entire estate to his daughter. 他把全部财产遗赠给了女儿。|Synonyms:  leave, will, bestow, impart, transmit|Antonyms: inherit, receive|verb
