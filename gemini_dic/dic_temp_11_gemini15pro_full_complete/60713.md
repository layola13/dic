electro-oculogram:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɒkjʊləɡræm/ | n. |眼电图| A record of the electrical potential difference between the front and back of the eye, used in diagnosing eye disorders and in studying rapid eye movements during sleep.| The electro-oculogram showed abnormal activity during REM sleep.|Synonym: EOG|Antonym: N/A|noun
