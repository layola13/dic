allegheny:/ˌæləˈɡeɪni/ | n. adj.| 阿勒格尼|  A name derived from a Lenape word, believed to mean "fine river." It refers to the Allegheny River, the Allegheny Mountains, and various places named after them, such as Allegheny County, Pennsylvania.| The Allegheny River flows through western Pennsylvania and New York. 阿勒格尼河流经宾夕法尼亚州西部和纽约州。| synonym: Appalachian (for the mountains) | antonym: N/A | noun, adjective
