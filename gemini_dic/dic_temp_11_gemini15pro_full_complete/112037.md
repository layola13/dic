long-windedly:/ˌlɔːŋˈwɪndɪdli/| adv.|冗长地，啰嗦地|In a tediously lengthy manner; at great length.|He spoke long-windedly about his travels, boring everyone in the room. 他冗长地讲述他的旅行，让房间里的每个人都感到无聊。|近义词：tediously, verbosely, prolixly|反义词: concisely, succinctly, briefly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "long-winded" + "-ly."  "Long-winded" itself comes from "long" + "wind" (breath), suggesting someone who speaks for so long they use up a lot of breath.
