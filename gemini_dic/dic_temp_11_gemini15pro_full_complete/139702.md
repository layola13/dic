oceanographers:/ˌoʊʃəˈnɑːɡrəfərz/
noun
海洋学家
Scientists who study the physical, chemical, and biological aspects of the ocean.
Oceanographers study a wide range of topics, from the smallest plankton to the largest whales. 海洋学家研究范围广泛的主题，从最小的浮游生物到最大的鲸鱼。

Oceanographers use sophisticated equipment to map the ocean floor. 海洋学家使用精密的设备绘制海底地图。

近义词:marine scientists, ocean scientists
反义词:(None -  No direct opposite for a profession)
词性:Noun
