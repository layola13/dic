dsts:/ˌdiːɛsˈtiːɛs/| n. | Daylight Saving Times (plural) |夏令时（复数）| Daylight Saving Time is the practice of advancing clocks during summer months so that evening daylight lasts longer.  DSTs are observed in many countries.|  Many countries observe DSTs to save energy and make better use of daylight. 许多国家实行夏令时以节约能源并更好地利用日光。| synonym: summer time | antonym: standard time | noun
