monocotyl:/ˈmɑːnəˌkɑːtəl/ /ˈmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪl/| n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocots also typically have parallel-veined leaves, fibrous root systems, and flowers with parts in multiples of three.|Examples: Corn, wheat, and rice are monocots. 玉米、小麦和稻米都是单子叶植物。 Orchids and lilies are also examples of monocots. 兰花和百合也是单子叶植物的例子。|近义词:monocot|反义词:dicot|词性:noun

monocotyledon:/ˌmɑːnəˌkɑːtəlˈiːdən/ /ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/| n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf).  This is synonymous with monocot. |Examples:  The monocotyledon quickly sprouted. 这株单子叶植物很快就发芽了。  The botanist specialized in the study of monocotyledons. 这位植物学家专门研究单子叶植物。|近义词:monocot|反义词:dicot|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mono- (one) + cotyledon (seed leaf). Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and kotyledōn (cup-shaped cavity).
