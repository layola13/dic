connected:/kəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. | 连接的，有关联的 | joined or linked together; having a relationship or association |  The two towns are connected by a railway. 这两个城镇由一条铁路连接。| 近义词：related, linked, associated, joined | 反义词:disconnected, separate, unrelated | 词性:形容词

connected:/kəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. |  (人)有权势的，有影响力的 | having social or professional influence | He's a well-connected businessman. 他是一位人脉很广的商人。| 近义词：influential, powerful | 反义词:unknown, obscure | 词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *connectere, from Latin connectō (“I fasten together”), from con- (“together”) + nectō (“I bind, tie”).
