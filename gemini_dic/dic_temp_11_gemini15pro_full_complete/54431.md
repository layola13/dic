disinfectors:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktərz/
| n. |消毒员，消毒器|A person or device that disinfects something.|The disinfectors worked tirelessly to sanitize the hospital after the outbreak.  消毒员在疫情爆发后不知疲倦地对医院进行消毒。|近义词：sanitizers, sterilizers|反义词: contaminators|Noun


disinfector:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktər/
| n. |消毒器|A device that disinfects something.|The disinfector uses UV light to kill bacteria. 这款消毒器使用紫外线杀菌。|近义词：sterilizer, sanitizer|反义词: contaminator|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From disinfect + -or.  Disinfect comes from dis- (meaning "reversal" or "removal") + infect.  Infect ultimately derives from Latin inficere "to dip in, stain, taint, infect," from in- "into" + facere "to make, do."
