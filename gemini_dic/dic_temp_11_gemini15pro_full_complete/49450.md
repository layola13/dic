deltafication:/ˌdɛltɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|三角洲化，三角洲形成|The process of delta formation, involving the deposition of sediment carried by a river as the flow leaves its mouth and enters slower-moving or standing water.|例句：Deltafication of the Mississippi River has created a vast and fertile region. 密西西比河的三角洲化作用创造了一个广阔而肥沃的地区。

例句：The deltafication process is influenced by factors such as river discharge, sediment load, and wave action. 三角洲化过程受河流流量、沉积物负荷和波浪作用等因素的影响。|近义词：delta formation, alluvial deposition|反义词: erosion|词性:noun
