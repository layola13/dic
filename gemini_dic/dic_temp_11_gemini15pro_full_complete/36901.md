colorising:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 着色；彩色化 |  The process of adding color to a black and white image or film. |  They are colorising old black and white movies. 他们正在为黑白老电影上色。| synonyms: colorizing, coloring | antonyms: bleaching, desaturating | verb

colorising:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 着色；彩色化 | The act or process of adding color to something. | The colorising of the old photos brought them to life.  老照片的着色使它们栩栩如生。| synonyms: colorization, coloring | antonyms: bleaching, desaturation | noun
