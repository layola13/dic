carbuncular: /ˌkɑːrˈbʌŋkjələr/ | adj. |痈的；似痈的；长痈的|Of, relating to, or resembling a carbuncle (a severe abscess or multiple boil).  Characterized by or suffering from carbuncles.|He had a carbuncular infection on his neck. 他脖子上有一个痈样感染。|Synonyms:  inflamed, festering, purulent | Antonyms: healthy, clear | adjective

carbuncular: /ˌkɑːrˈbʌŋkjələr/ | adj. |红似红宝石的|Resembling the deep red color of a carbuncle (a gemstone, especially a garnet). |The wine had a rich, carbuncular hue.  这酒色泽浓郁，像红宝石一样。|Synonyms: ruby, crimson, scarlet | Antonyms: pale, colorless | adjective
