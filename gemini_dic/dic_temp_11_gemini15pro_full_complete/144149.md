outshape:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪp/
verb
胜过…的体型;在体型上优于
surpass in shape; be superior to in shape
The young athlete outshaped his older competitors.  这位年轻运动员的体型胜过他年长的竞争对手。
outshape:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪp/
verb
做得比…好; 技艺胜过…
to do better than; to excel
He outshaped all the other woodcarvers in the competition. 他在比赛中技艺胜过所有其他木雕家。
近义词:surpass, excel, outdo, outperform
反义词:underperform
