lattice:/ˈlætɪs/| n.|格子; 格子状结构|A structure of crossed wooden or metal strips usually arranged to form a diagonal pattern of open spaces between the strips.|The ivy grew up a wooden lattice. 常春藤沿着木格向上生长。|Synonyms: grid, network, mesh, framework|Antonyms: solid, mass|noun

lattice:/ˈlætɪs/| adj.|格子状的|Resembling a lattice.|a lattice window 格子窗|Synonyms: latticed, crisscrossed, gridded|Antonyms: solid, unlatticed|adjective
