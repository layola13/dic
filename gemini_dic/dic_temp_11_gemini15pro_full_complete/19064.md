betokened:/bɪˈtoʊkənd/
verb
预示，表示
indicated; was a sign of
His furrowed brow betokened his anxiety. 他紧锁的眉头表明他很焦虑。
Synonyms: signified, foreshadowed, portended, presaged, augured
Antonyms: concealed, masked, hid
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitokenen*, from Old English *betācnian* (“to signify, betoken, portend, indicate, show, demonstrate, prefigure, represent”), from *be-* + *tācnian* (“to make a mark or sign, to betoken”), from Proto-Germanic *taiknijaną* (“to show, designate”).
