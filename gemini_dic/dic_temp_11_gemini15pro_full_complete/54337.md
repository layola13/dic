disherit:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/
verb
剥夺继承权
To prevent someone from inheriting property or money, especially a member of one's family.
He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他剥夺了违背他意愿结婚的儿子的继承权。
synonyms: cut off, exclude from inheritance
antonyms: bequeath, endow
词性: 动词
