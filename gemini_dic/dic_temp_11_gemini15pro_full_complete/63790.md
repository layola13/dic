epicycloidal:/ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɔɪdəl/
adjective
外摆线的，外旋轮线的
Relating to or resembling an epicycloid; a curve traced by a point on a circle that rolls externally on another circle.
The clock uses an epicycloidal gear system. 这台钟使用外摆线齿轮系统。
The movement of the planet is epicycloidal.  这颗行星的运动是外摆线的。
近义词:cycloidal
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective
