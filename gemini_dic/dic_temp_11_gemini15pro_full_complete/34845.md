clinique:/klɪˈniːk/| n.|诊所，临床，克丽缇娜（化妆品品牌）|A place where people go to receive medical treatment, often specializing in a particular area. Also a well-known cosmetics brand.|例句：She works at a dermatology clinique. 她在一家皮肤科诊所工作。

例句：She uses Clinique moisturizer. 她用倩碧的保湿霜。|近义词：clinic, hospital, infirmary (for the medical sense), beauty counter, cosmetics brand (for the brand sense)|反义词:None (for the medical sense), drugstore (possibly, for the brand sense) |词性:Noun
