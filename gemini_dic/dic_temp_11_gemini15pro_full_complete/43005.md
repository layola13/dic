cowardize: /ˈkaʊərdaɪz/| vt.|使胆怯，使懦弱|To make someone cowardly; to destroy someone's courage.|His threats cowardized her into agreeing. 他的威胁使她胆怯而同意了。|The constant bullying cowardized him.  不断的欺凌使他变得懦弱。|近义词: intimidate, daunt, dishearten|反义词: embolden, encourage|词性:动词
Etymology: From coward + -ize.  源自coward（懦夫） + -ize（使...化）
