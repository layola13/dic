biaxial:/baɪˈæksiəl/
adjective
双轴的
Having two axes; especially, having two optical axes.  Relating to, or having, two axes.
The biaxial crystal is used in optical devices.  这种双轴晶体用于光学设备。

The material exhibits biaxial stress-strain behavior.  这种材料表现出双轴应力应变行为。
近义词:anisotropic, doubly refracting
反义词:uniaxial, isotropic
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + axial (relating to an axis).  Ultimately from Latin axis (axle, axis).
