calculably:/ˈkælkjələbli/| adv.|可计算地，可以预测地|In a way that can be calculated or estimated.  This means that something can be determined or figured out, often using mathematical or logical methods.|The risks were calculably small.  风险是可以估算到的，而且很小。|近义词：predictably, foreseeably, estimably|反义词:unpredictably, incalculably|词性:副词
