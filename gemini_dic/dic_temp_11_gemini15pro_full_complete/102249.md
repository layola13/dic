iterating:/ˈɪtəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 迭代的，重复的 | The act of repeating a process, often with slight changes each time, in order to improve or refine the outcome.  |  The program is iterating through the list of names. 程序正在遍历姓名列表。| synonyms: repeating, looping, cycling | antonyms: static, unchanging | Verb (present participle)

iterating:/ˈɪtəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 迭代，重复 | To perform or utter repeatedly. | She kept iterating the same point. 她不断重复同一个观点。| synonyms: repeat, reiterate, reprise | antonyms: single, once | Verb (present participle)
