highlighter:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtər/| n.|突出强调的事物|Something that emphasizes or draws attention to something else.|The colorful illustrations were the highlighter of the children's book.  这些彩色的插图是这本儿童书籍的亮点。|Synonyms: highlight, feature|Antonyms: obscurity, insignificance|noun
