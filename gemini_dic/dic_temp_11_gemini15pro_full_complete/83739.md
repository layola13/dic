hailers:/ˈheɪlərz/| n.|欢呼者，喝彩者，招呼者|People who hail someone or something, often expressing enthusiastic approval or greeting. |The hailers cheered as the team returned victorious.  欢呼者们在球队凯旋而归时欢呼雀跃。| The hailer waved his arms to get the driver's attention. 招呼者挥舞着手臂以引起司机的注意。| synonyms: cheerers, applauders, greeters, callers | antonyms: critics, dissenters | noun
