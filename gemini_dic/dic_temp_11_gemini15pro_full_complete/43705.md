creditorship:/ˈkrɛdɪtərʃɪp/
n.
债权人身份，债权
The status or position of being a creditor.
He assumed the creditorship of the bankrupt company. 他承担了破产公司的债权人身份。
近义词: creditor status, debt ownership
反义词: debitorship, indebtedness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From creditor + -ship.  Creditor comes from Latin crēditor "one who trusts," from crēdere "to believe, trust, entrust," related to crēdō "I believe." The suffix -ship denotes "state or condition of being."
