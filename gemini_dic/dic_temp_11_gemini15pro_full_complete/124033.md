moroxylate: /məˈrɒksɪleɪt/
noun
吗洛酯
An opioid analgesic drug.
Moroxylate is a potent opioid agonist used to treat moderate to severe pain.
Moroxylate was prescribed for the patient's post-surgical pain. 吗洛酯被用来治疗病人的术后疼痛。
The doctor administered moroxylate to alleviate the patient's discomfort. 医生使用吗洛酯来减轻病人的不适。
Synonyms: opioid, analgesic, painkiller
Antonyms: stimulant
