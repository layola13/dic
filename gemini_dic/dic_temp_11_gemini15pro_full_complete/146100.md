overelaborates:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪts/ | v. | 过分详尽阐述，过度修饰 |  explains or describes something in excessive detail, often making it more complicated than necessary | He overelaborates his points, making a simple idea sound convoluted. 他把自己的观点解释得过于详尽，使一个简单的想法听起来很复杂。| elaborate, overdo, belabor | simplify, condense | verb

overelaborate:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪt/ | adj. | 过分详尽的，过度修饰的 | excessively detailed or elaborate |  Her overelaborate explanation only confused the students. 她过于详尽的解释反而让学生们感到困惑。 | elaborate, complex, detailed | simple, concise | adjective
