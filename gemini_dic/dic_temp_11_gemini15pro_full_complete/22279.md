boneshaker: /ˈbəʊnˌʃeɪkə(r)/ (US) /ˈboʊnˌʃeɪkər/| n. |骨头震动器，旧式自行车|An early type of bicycle with solid rubber tires, so called because of the jarring ride it gave.  It refers to a bicycle, especially of the type used before the invention of pneumatic tires.|例句：Riding a boneshaker over cobblestones was a truly uncomfortable experience. 骑着骨头震动器穿过鹅卵石路面真是一次不舒服的体验。

例句：Boneshakers were a popular form of transportation in the late 19th century.  19世纪后期，骨头震动器是一种流行的交通工具。|近义词：bicycle, velocipede, ordinary (bicycle) |反义词: modern bicycle, car|词性:名词
