leot:/ˈliːɒt/ (UK), /ˈliːɑːt/ (US)| n. |紧身衣|A tight-fitting garment, usually made of stretchy material, that covers the body from the shoulders or neck to the thighs or knees. It is typically worn by dancers, gymnasts, acrobats, and other performers.|She wore a bright pink leot for her gymnastics routine. 她穿着亮粉色紧身衣进行体操表演。|The dancer's leot was adorned with sequins. 舞者的紧身衣上装饰着亮片。|近义词: bodysuit, unitard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing, sweatpants)|词性: noun
