acquisitively: /əˈkwɪzɪtɪvli/
adverb
贪婪地，渴求地
In a manner characterized by a strong desire to acquire or possess, often excessively; greedily.
He acquisitively eyed the rare antique. 他贪婪地盯着那件稀有的古董。
Synonyms: greedily, avariciously, covetously, rapaciously
Antonyms: generously, altruistically, charitably
Part of Speech: adverb
