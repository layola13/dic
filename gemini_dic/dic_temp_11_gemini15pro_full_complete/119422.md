microgametophyte:/ˌmaɪkroʊɡəˈmiːtəfaɪt/ | n. | 小配子体 | The male gametophyte of seed plants, containing the cells that develop into sperm cells. | The pollen grain of a flowering plant is a microgametophyte.  开花植物的花粉粒是一个小配子体。| The microgametophyte develops inside the pollen grain. 小配子体在花粉粒内发育。 | synonym: male gametophyte | antonym: megagametophyte | noun
