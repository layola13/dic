dodo:/ˈdoʊdoʊ/ (US), /ˈdəʊdəʊ/ (UK)| n. |渡渡鸟，愚笨的人|An extinct flightless bird of Mauritius, proverbial for its stupidity.  It became extinct in the late 17th century.|例句：The dodo is a symbol of extinction. 渡渡鸟是灭绝的象征。

例句：He's a bit of a dodo when it comes to technology. 他在科技方面有点像个笨蛋。|近义词：simpleton, fool, idiot|反义词:genius, intellectual|Noun
