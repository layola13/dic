karaite: /ˈkɛrəˌaɪt/| n. adj.|卡拉派教徒，卡拉派的|A member of a Jewish sect that rejects the authority of the oral law and adheres only to the written law (Torah).|Karaites believe only in the written Torah. 卡拉派教徒只相信成文的《妥拉》。|He is a Karaite and observes only the biblical commandments. 他是一位卡拉派教徒，只遵守圣经中的戒律。|近义词:Sadducee (in some aspects)|反义词:Rabbanite|词性:名词，形容词
