canopying:/ˈkænəpiɪŋ/
n.|天篷覆盖，形成天篷|The act of covering with or forming a canopy.
|例句：The canopying of the forest created a cool, shaded area. 森林的树冠形成了一个阴凉的区域。

|例句：The colorful canopying of umbrellas at the market was a vibrant sight. 市场上品种繁多的伞篷构成了一幅鲜艳的景象。
|近义词：covering, sheltering, overhanging|反义词:exposing
|词性:名词

canopying:/ˈkænəpiɪŋ/
v.|用天篷覆盖，形成天篷|To cover with or form a canopy.
|例句：The trees were canopying the path, creating a tunnel of green. 树木遮蔽着小路，形成了一条绿色的隧道。

|例句：The vines were quickly canopying the old wall. 藤蔓迅速覆盖了旧墙。
|近义词：covering, shading, overhanging|反义词:exposing
|词性:动词
