kickboxer:/ˈkɪkˌbɑːksər/| n.|跆拳道运动员，自由搏击运动员|A person who participates in kickboxing, a martial art combining boxing and kicking techniques.|例句：The kickboxer trained rigorously for the upcoming championship.  这位跆拳道运动员为即将到来的锦标赛进行了严格的训练。

例句：She's a skilled kickboxer with a powerful roundhouse kick. 她是一位技术娴熟的跆拳道运动员，回旋踢很有力。|近义词：fighter, martial artist|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider pacifist)|词性:noun
