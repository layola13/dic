darvaza: /dɑːrˈvɑːzə/
n.
地狱之门，达瓦札天然气燃烧坑
The Darvaza gas crater, also known as the "Door to Hell" or "Gates of Hell", is a natural gas field collapsed into a cavern in Derweze, Turkmenistan.
达瓦札天然气坑，也被称为“地狱之门”，是土库曼斯坦Derweze的一个天然气田坍塌成的一个洞穴。
The Darvaza crater has been burning continuously since 1971.
达瓦札火山口自1971年以来一直在持续燃烧。
近义词: crater, pit, abyss, hellhole
反义词: paradise, heaven
词性: 名词

词源:
Borrowed from Turkmen *darwaza (meaning "gate"), ultimately from Persian دروازه (darvāze, “gate”).
