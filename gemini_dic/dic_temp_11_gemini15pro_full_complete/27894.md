cantankerous:/kænˈtæŋkərəs/| adj.|脾气坏的，爱争吵的|Bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative.|例句：The cantankerous old man yelled at the children playing in his yard. 脾气暴躁的老人对着在他院子里玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。

例句：He became increasingly cantankerous as he grew older. 随着年龄的增长，他变得越来越暴躁。|近义词：bad-tempered, grumpy, irritable, quarrelsome, irascible|反义词：good-natured, affable, amiable, agreeable|词性:形容词
