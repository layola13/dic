disappearance:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪərəns/ | n. |消失，失踪|The sudden disappearance of the plane remains a mystery.  这架飞机突然消失仍然是一个谜。|例句：The magician amazed the audience with the disappearance of his assistant. 魔术师以他助手的消失使观众惊叹。|近义词：vanishing, absence, loss|反义词：appearance, arrival, emergence|词性:名词
Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + appearance.  Ultimately from Latin apparēre "to appear."
