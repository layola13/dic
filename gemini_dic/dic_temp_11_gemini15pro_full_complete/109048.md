legalise:/ˈliːɡəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈliɡəˌlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|使合法化；将…合法化|To make something legal; to give legal status or authorization to something that was previously illegal.|The government has decided to legalise same-sex marriage. 政府已决定将同性婚姻合法化。

The possession of small amounts of marijuana has been legalised in some states.  一些州已经将持有少量大麻合法化。|synonyms: legitimize, authorize, decriminalize|antonyms: prohibit, ban, outlaw|verb
