baristi:/bɑːˈriːsti/ | n. |咖啡师；咖啡调配师|A barista is a person who makes and serves coffee drinks, often in a coffee shop. | The barista made a perfect latte. 咖啡师做了一杯完美的拿铁。| 近义词: coffee maker, bartender | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun

baristas:/bɑːˈriːstəz/ | n. |咖啡师们；咖啡调配师们|Plural form of barista.  | The baristas were busy during the morning rush.  咖啡师们在早高峰时很忙。| 近义词: coffee makers | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian barista, ultimately from Greek βαρύς (barýs, “heavy”) + -ιστής (-istēs).  Originally, a barista in Italy served all kinds of drinks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic, at a bar. The term became associated primarily with coffee preparation in English-speaking countries.
