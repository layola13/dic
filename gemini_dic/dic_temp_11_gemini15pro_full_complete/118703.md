metacinnabarite:/ˌmɛtəsɪnəˈbɑːraɪt/| n. |黑辰砂|A black, tetragonal mineral form of mercury sulfide (HgS). It's a less common polymorph of cinnabar (which is red).|Metacinnabarite is often found in low-temperature hydrothermal veins. 黑辰砂常发现于低温热液矿脉中。|Metacinnabarite can alter to cinnabar over time. 黑辰砂会随着时间推移变成辰砂。|Synonym:  guadalcazarite (less common)|Antonym: cinnabar (different color, same chemical formula)|noun
