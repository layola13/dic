overbaking:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪkɪŋ/
|n.|过度烘烤|The overbaking resulted in a dry, crumbly cake. 过度烘烤导致蛋糕干燥易碎。|
|v.|过度烘烤（动名词形式）|I ruined the cookies by overbaking them. 我把饼干烤过头了，毁了它们。|
|英文解析|Baking something for too long, resulting in a dry or burnt outcome.|
|近义词|burning, scorching|
|反义词|underbaking|
|词性|动名词 (gerund), 名词(noun) |
|词源|over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + baking (from bake)|
