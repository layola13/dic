brainer:/ˈbreɪnər/| n. |容易的事情；不费脑筋的事|Something that requires very little thought; an easy decision or problem.|Choosing vanilla ice cream is a no-brainer for me.  对我来说，选择香草冰淇淋是不费脑筋的事。|Synonyms: cinch, piece of cake, easy decision, child's play|Antonyms: challenge, difficult decision, puzzle, conundrum|noun

brainer:/ˈbreɪnər/| n. |不用动脑筋的事|Something that is easy to understand or do.|This quiz was a total brainer. 这个测验非常简单。|Synonyms:  cinch, doddle, pushover, walkover|Antonyms:  challenge, puzzle, enigma|noun
