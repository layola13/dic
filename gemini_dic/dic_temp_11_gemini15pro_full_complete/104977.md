kendi:/ˈkɛndi/| pronoun|自己（斯瓦希里语）|Reflexive pronoun in Swahili, used to emphasize the subject performing the action.  Equivalent to "myself," "yourself," "himself," "herself," "itself," "ourselves," "yourselves," and "themselves" depending on the context.|Mtoto alijifunza kupika kendi. (The child learned to cook by himself/herself.)|近义词:mwenyewe (Swahili)|反义词: N/A|pronoun

PHRASE->

(Since "kendi" is a reflexive pronoun, it doesn't typically form standalone phrases.  However, it combines with verbs to express reflexive actions. Here are examples illustrating its usage within verb phrases.)

1. kujipika kendi (to cook for oneself)
2. kujisomea kendi (to read by oneself)
3. kujifunza kendi (to learn by oneself)
4. kujifanya kendi (to pretend to be oneself)
5. kujisaidia kendi (to help oneself)


注意事项:

"kendi" is specifically Swahili.  There isn't a direct equivalent with the same usage across all Bantu languages.  The examples provided utilize Swahili verb conjugations reflecting the reflexive nature of "kendi."
