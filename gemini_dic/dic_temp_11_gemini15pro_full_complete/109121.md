legible:/ˈledʒəbl/| adj.|清晰的，易读的|clear enough to read; easy to read|例句：Her handwriting is perfectly legible. 她的字迹非常清晰易读。|近义词：readable, clear, decipherable|反义词: illegible, unreadable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *legible*, from Late Latin *legibilis* (“that can be read”), from Latin *legō* (“to read”).
