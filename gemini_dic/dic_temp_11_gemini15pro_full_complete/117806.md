meliorator:/ˈmiːliəˌreɪtər/| n. |改良者，改进者|A person or thing that meliorates or improves something, especially land.|例句：The farmer acted as a meliorator of the soil, enriching it with compost. 这位农民充当了土壤的改良者，用堆肥丰富了土壤。

例句：New technologies can be powerful meliorators of existing processes. 新技术可以有效地改进现有流程。|近义词：improver, reformer, enhancer, ameliorator|反义词：worsener, degrader|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *meliorator, from Latin meliorare "to improve," from melior "better."
