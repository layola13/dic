insurrectionist:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪst/
n.
暴动者；叛乱者；起义者
A person who takes part in an insurrection; a rebel.
The insurrectionists stormed the capitol building. 暴动者冲进了国会大厦。
Synonyms: rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, mutineer, rioter
Antonyms: loyalist, conformist
词性: 名词

insurrectionist:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪst/
n.
暴乱者
a person who engages in armed resistance to a government or to the execution of its laws
Several insurrectionists were arrested after the failed coup. 几名暴乱者在未遂政变后被捕。
Synonyms: rebel, traitor, seditionist
Antonyms: patriot, loyalist
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insurrectionista, from Latin insurrectionem (nominative insurrectio) "a rising up, rebellion," noun of action from past-participle stem of insurgere "to rise up," from in- "up, upon" + surgere "to rise."
