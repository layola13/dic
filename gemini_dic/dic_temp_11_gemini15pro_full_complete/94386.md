impledge:/ɪmˈpledʒ/
verb
抵押；保证；承诺
to give something as a guarantee that you will do something, or that something is true
He impledged his house to the bank as security for the loan. 他把房子抵押给银行作为贷款担保。
promise, pledge, vow, guarantee
renege, default
词性: 动词
