overdepresses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪz/
verb
过度压抑；使过分沮丧
To cause someone to feel excessively depressed or disheartened.
The constant criticism from her boss overdepressed her. 她老板不断的批评让她感到非常沮丧。
The gloomy weather further overdepressed him. 阴沉的天气使他更加沮丧。
Synonyms: deject, dispirit, demoralize
Antonyms: encourage, uplift, cheer
词性: 动词
