misadapting:/ˌmɪsəˈdæptɪŋ/
verb
不适应，适应不良
Failing to adjust adequately to a new environment or situation.
The species is misadapting to the changing climate.  这个物种无法适应气候变化。
misadjusting, maladapting
adapting
