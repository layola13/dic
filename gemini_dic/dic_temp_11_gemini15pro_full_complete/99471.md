interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/ (US) /ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. |干涉，干预，妨碍|To become involved in a situation or activity when your involvement is not wanted or is not helpful.|例句：I don't want to interfere in their relationship. 我不想干涉他们的关系。|近义词：intervene, meddle, tamper|反义词:support, assist|verb

interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/ (US) /ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. |冲突，抵触|To prevent something from working effectively or from developing successfully.|例句：The noise interfered with my sleep. 噪音干扰了我的睡眠。|近义词：clash, conflict, hinder|反义词:harmonize, cooperate|verb
