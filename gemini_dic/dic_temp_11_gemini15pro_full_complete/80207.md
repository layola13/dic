godmothered:/ˈɡɒdˌmʌðəd/ | v. |  被施以魔法；像教母一样关爱 | To treat someone with excessive kindness and generosity, often in a way that is overwhelming or unwanted, or to magically transform something.  Derived from "godmother," implying a magical influence or transformation. | She felt completely godmothered by her aunt, who showered her with unwanted gifts and advice. 她觉得被她姑姑过分溺爱了，她姑姑给了她很多她不想要的礼物和建议。 |  The old house had been godmothered into a beautiful, modern home.  这栋老房子被神奇地改造成了一个漂亮、现代化的家。 | 近义词: pampered, spoiled, indulged, transformed | 反义词: neglected, ignored | 词性: 动词
