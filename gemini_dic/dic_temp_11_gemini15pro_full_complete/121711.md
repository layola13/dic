misprint:/ˈmɪsprɪnt/| n.|印刷错误，错字|A mistake in printing. |The book was full of misprints and had to be recalled. 这本书里到处都是印刷错误，不得不被召回。|近义词：typo, error, mistake|反义词:correctness, accuracy|noun
misprint:/ˌmɪsˈprɪnt/| vt.|印错，印坏了|To print (something) incorrectly. |He misprinted the date on the invitations. 他把请柬上的日期印错了。|近义词：print incorrectly, misspell|反义词:print correctly|verb
