banco:/ˈbæŋkoʊ/ (US) /ˈbaŋkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|长凳; (尤指公园或花园里的)长椅; 工作台; (尤指赌场的)庄家|A long seat, especially one in a park or garden; a workbench; (especially in gambling) the banker or dealer.|We sat on a banco in the park. 我们坐在公园的长椅上.|Synonyms: bench, seat, pew|Antonyms: N/A|noun

banco:/ˈbæŋkoʊ/ (US) /ˈbaŋkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|银行|A financial institution that accepts deposits and channels the money into lending activities.|He works at a banco. 他在一家银行工作.|Synonyms: bank, financial institution|Antonyms: N/A|noun
