letter-size:/ˈletər saɪz/| adj.|信纸大小的，标准尺寸的|Of or relating to a standard size of paper, typically 8.5 x 11 inches in the US and 210 x 297 millimeters (A4) elsewhere.  It refers to the dimensions of a piece of paper commonly used for letters and documents.|I need some letter-size paper for my printer. 我需要一些信纸大小的纸张打印机用。|Synonyms: standard-sized, A4 (outside US)|Antonyms: legal-size, oversized|adjective
词源:  Compound of "letter" (referring to its original primary use for correspondence) and "size".
