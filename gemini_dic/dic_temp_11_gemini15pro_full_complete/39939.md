constructor: /kənˈstrʌktər/ (US), /kənˈstrʌktə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 构造器，构造函数 |A special method within a class used to create and initialize objects of that class. It is automatically called when a new object is created.|  The constructor initializes the object's properties. 构造函数初始化对象的属性。| synonym: initializer, builder | antonym: destructor | noun
