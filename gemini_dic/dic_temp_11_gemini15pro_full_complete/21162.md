bloated:/ˈbloʊtɪd/ (US), /ˈbləʊtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|肿胀的，臃肿的；过分饱胀的；傲慢的，自负的|Swollen, distended; excessively full; pompous, arrogant|例句：My stomach felt bloated after the huge meal.  我的肚子在吃了大餐后感觉胀胀的。

例句：The company's bloated bureaucracy hindered innovation. 公司臃肿的官僚机构阻碍了创新。|近义词：swollen, distended, inflated, puffed up, pompous|反义词：deflated, shrunken, humble, modest|词性:形容词


bloat:/bloʊt/ (US), /bləʊt/ (UK)| v. n.|（使）膨胀，肿胀；（使）傲慢自大|To swell, to become inflated; to puff up with pride|例句：The dead fish bloated in the sun. 死鱼在阳光下膨胀了。

例句：His ego was bloated by his recent success. 他最近的成功让他变得自负。|近义词：swell, inflate, expand, puff up|反义词：deflate, shrink, contract|词性:动词/名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bloten, from Old Norse blautr (“soft, soaked”), from Proto-Germanic *blautaz (“soft, weak”). Related to Old English blǣt (“soft, flabby”).
