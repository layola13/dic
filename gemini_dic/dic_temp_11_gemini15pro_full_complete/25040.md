bryozoan: /ˌbraɪəˈzoʊən/ | n. |苔藓虫，苔藓动物|A small invertebrate animal that lives in colonies and forms a branching, mossy structure.  They are aquatic, mostly marine, filter feeders. |例句：Bryozoans are often mistaken for seaweed. 苔藓虫经常被误认为是海藻。

例句：The bryozoan colony was attached to the underside of the rock.  这个苔藓虫群体附着在岩石的底部。|近义词：moss animal |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

bryozoan: /ˌbraɪəˈzoʊən/ | adj. |苔藓虫的，苔藓动物的|Relating to or characteristic of bryozoans. |例句：The bryozoan skeleton is made of calcium carbonate. 苔藓虫的骨骼是由碳酸钙构成的。

例句：Bryozoan fossils are found in rocks dating back to the Ordovician period.  在可以追溯到奥陶纪时期的岩石中发现了苔藓虫化石。|近义词：N/A |反义词: N/A |词性:adjective
