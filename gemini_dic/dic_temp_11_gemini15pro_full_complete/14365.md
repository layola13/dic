autopneumatic:/ˌɔːtəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. |自动充气的；气动的| Operating by compressed air, especially automatically; self-inflating. | The life raft was autopneumatic, inflating as soon as it hit the water. 救生筏是自动充气的，一碰到水就充气了。 | The tires are autopneumatic and adjust pressure based on terrain. 这些轮胎是气动的，可以根据地形调整压力。 | 近义词: self-inflating, automatic, pneumatic | 反义词: manual | 词性: adjective
