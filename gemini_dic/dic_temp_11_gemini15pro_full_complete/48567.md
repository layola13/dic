default's:/dɪˈfɔːlts/| n.|默认值，违约，拖欠|Something that is usual or standard, especially when other things are not available or not done.  The failure to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in a court of law. |The printer's default setting is double-sided printing. 打印机的默认设置为双面打印。 He defaulted on his loan payments. 他拖欠了贷款还款。|近义词：standard, automatic, omission, failure, lapse|反义词:change, achievement, success|noun
