flatteries:/ˈflætəriz/ (US) /ˈflætəriz/ (UK)| n.|奉承；恭维话；阿谀|Excessive or insincere praise, given especially to further one's own interests.|He showered her with flatteries, hoping to gain her favor. 他对她大加奉承，希望获得她的好感。|Synonyms: compliments, praise, adulation, blandishments|Antonyms: criticisms, insults, reprimands|Noun
