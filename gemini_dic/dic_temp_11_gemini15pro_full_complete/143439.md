outflowed:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊd/
verb
流出, 外流
The past participle of "outflow."  It describes something that has already flowed out.
Large amounts of capital outflowed from the country during the financial crisis. 在金融危机期间，大量资金从该国流出。
The lava outflowed from the volcano, destroying everything in its path.  熔岩从火山中流出，摧毁了沿途的一切。
synonyms: discharged, emanated, effused, leaked
antonyms: inflowed, accumulated, gathered
词性: 动词(past participle)

outflow:/ˈaʊtfloʊ/
noun
流出, 外流
The act or process of flowing out.
The outflow of refugees from the war-torn country continues. 来自饱受战争蹂躏的国家的难民外流仍在继续。
The dam controls the outflow of water from the reservoir. 大坝控制着水库的出水量。
synonyms: efflux, discharge, emission, exodus
antonyms: inflow, influx, intake
词性: 名词
