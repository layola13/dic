lowfat:/ˈloʊˌfæt/ (American) /ˈləʊˌfæt/ (British)| adj.|低脂的|Containing a reduced amount of fat.  Designed or prepared so as to contain a lower than usual amount of fat.|I'm trying to eat lowfat yogurt these days. 我最近在尝试吃低脂酸奶。|synonyms:light, lean, diet, reduced-fat|antonyms:full-fat, high-fat|adjective
