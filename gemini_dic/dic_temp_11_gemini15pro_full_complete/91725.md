hyperdeify:/ˌhaɪpərˈdiːɪfaɪ/
verb
过度神化；极度崇拜
to deify excessively; to worship or revere someone or something to an extreme degree.
The fans practically hyperdeified their favorite singer, treating her like a goddess.  粉丝们实际上过度神化了他们最喜欢的歌手，把她当作女神一样对待。
The ancient Romans hyperdeified their emperors, ascribing divine powers to them. 古罗马人极度崇拜他们的皇帝，认为他们拥有神力。
近义词:idolize, worship, exalt, glorify, venerate
反义词:despise, scorn, denigrate
词性:v.
