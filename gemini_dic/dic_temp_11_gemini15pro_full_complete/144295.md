outspin:/ˌaʊtˈspɪn/
|v.|胜过旋转；超过…的旋转速度；|The new model of the turbine outspins the older one. 新型涡轮机的旋转速度比旧型快。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outperform|反义词: lag, trail|词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "spin" (verb meaning "to revolve rapidly").  Comes from Old English "spinnan."
