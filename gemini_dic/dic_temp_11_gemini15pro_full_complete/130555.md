nonacquiescing:/ˌnɒnˌækwiˈesɪŋ/
adjective
不默许的，不服从的
Refusing to accept or comply with something.
The nonacquiescing taxpayer challenged the IRS ruling in court.  这位不默许的纳税人向法院挑战了美国国税局的裁决。
The government's nonacquiescing stance on the treaty led to further negotiations. 政府在条约问题上不默许的立场导致了进一步的谈判。
近义词: dissenting, objecting, resisting, opposing
反义词: acquiescing, complying, agreeing, accepting
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (expressing negation) + acquiesce (to accept something reluctantly but without protest) + -ing (present participle suffix).  Acquiesce comes from Latin acquiescere "to rest, remain at rest," from ad- "to" + quiescere "to be quiet."
