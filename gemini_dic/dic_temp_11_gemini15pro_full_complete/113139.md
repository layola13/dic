lunkheaded:/ˌlʌŋkˈhɛdɪd/| adj.|愚笨的，笨拙的|Slow-witted, stupid, or unintelligent.|He made a lunkheaded decision to invest all his money in one stock. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|Synonyms: doltish, obtuse, dim-witted, thickheaded|Antonyms: intelligent, bright, clever, sharp|adjective

lunkhead:/ˈlʌŋkˌhɛd/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or slow-witted person.|Don't be such a lunkhead!  别这么傻！|Synonyms: dolt, oaf, blockhead|Antonyms: genius, intellectual|noun
