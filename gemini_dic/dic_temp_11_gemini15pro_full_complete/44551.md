crotaphitic:/ˌkroʊtəˈfɪtɪk/| adj.|颞的，颧弓的|Relating to the crotaphitic fossa, a shallow depression on the side of the skull, behind the zygomatic arch (cheekbone).|The crotaphitic muscle is located in the crotaphitic fossa. 颞肌位于颞窝内。|Synonyms: temporal, zygomatic|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

crotaphitic:/ˌkroʊtəˈfɪtɪk/| n.|颞肌|The temporalis muscle, a muscle of mastication (chewing) located in the crotaphitic fossa.|Damage to the crotaphitic can affect chewing ability. 对颞肌的损伤会影响咀嚼能力。|Synonyms: temporalis muscle|Antonyms: N/A|noun
