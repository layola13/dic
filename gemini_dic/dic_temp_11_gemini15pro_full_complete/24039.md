breezeless:/ˈbriːzlɪs/| adj.|无风的，平静的|Without a breeze; still, calm.|The air was hot and breezeless. 空气闷热无风。|近义词：calm, still, windless|反义词:windy, breezy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From breeze + -less.  "breeze" comes from the Spanish "briza," referring to a northeast wind.  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and indicates a lack or absence of something.
