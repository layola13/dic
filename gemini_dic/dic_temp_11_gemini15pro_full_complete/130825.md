The nonappealingnesses of the design turned off potential buyers.  这个设计缺乏吸引力，让潜在买家望而却步。| 近义词：unattractiveness, unpleasantness, undesirability | 反义词：attractiveness, appeal, desirability | 词性：名词

Etymology: non- + appealing + -ness + -es

"Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."

"Appealing" comes from the Old French "apeler" meaning "to call, appeal."

"-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.

"-es" is a plural suffix.
