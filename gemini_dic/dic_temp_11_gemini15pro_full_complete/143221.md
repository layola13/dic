outcursed:/ˌaʊtˈkɜːrst/
verb
超越诅咒; 诅咒得更厉害
To curse more effectively or intensely than someone else.
He outcursed the sailor with a string of vile epithets. 他用一连串恶毒的绰号咒骂水手，比水手骂得更厉害。
Near synonyms:  execrate, condemn, damn, anathematize
Antonyms: bless, praise, commend
词性: 动词
