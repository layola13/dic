booming:/ˈbuːmɪŋ/| adj.|繁荣的，兴旺的，迅速发展的；（声音）深沉的，响亮的|experiencing a period of great prosperity or rapid economic growth; (of a sound) deep and resonant|例句：The booming economy created many new jobs. 繁荣的经济创造了许多新的就业机会。|近义词：flourishing, thriving, prosperous, booming, resonant|反义词:declining, failing, weak, quiet, soft|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "boom," meaning "to make a deep resonant sound" or "to grow or develop rapidly," from Middle Dutch "bomen" and Old Norse "bauma."  The sense of rapid growth likely developed from the idea of a sudden loud noise indicating rapid expansion.
