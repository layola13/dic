hitchily:/ˈhɪtʃɪli/| adv.|断断续续地，不顺畅地|In a halting or jerky manner; with frequent interruptions or difficulties.|The old car moved hitchily down the road. 这辆旧车在路上颠簸地行驶。|Synonyms: jerkily, unevenly, intermittently, haltingly|Antonyms: smoothly, continuously, fluidly|词性:副词

hitch:/hɪtʃ/| n. vt. vi.|故障，障碍；搭便车；拴住，套住|A temporary problem or difficulty; to fasten or tie something to something else; to obtain a free ride in a passing vehicle.|There was a hitch in the plan. 计划中出现了一个小故障。 He hitched his horse to the fence post. 他把马拴在了栅栏柱上. She hitched a ride to the city. 她搭便车去了城里。|Synonyms: snag, glitch, problem, fasten, attach|Antonyms: solution, advantage, detach, unfasten|词性:名词,动词
