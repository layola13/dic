consult:/kənˈsʌlt/ /kɑːnˈsʌlt/| v. n.|咨询，商量，查阅|To seek information or advice from someone or something.|I consulted my doctor about the pain in my back. 我向我的医生咨询了我的背痛。|近义词: confer, advise, refer to|反义词: ignore, disregard|verb

consult:/ˈkɑːnsʌlt/| n.|咨询，磋商|A meeting to discuss something or to seek advice.|The doctors held a consult to discuss the patient's condition. 医生们举行了一次会诊，讨论病人的病情。|近义词: conference, meeting|反义词: N/A|noun
