noiance:/ˈnɔɪəns/| n.|噪音，喧闹声；讨厌的人或事|An annoying or unpleasant sound; a person or thing that is annoying or causes trouble.|The constant noiance from the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的持续噪音使人难以集中精力。|近义词：noise, racket, din, disturbance|反义词:quiet, silence, peace|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *noiance*, ultimately from Latin *nocēre* "to harm". The word entered English in the 15th century.
