melody:/ˈmɛlədi/ (US), /ˈmɛlədi/ (UK)| n. |旋律，曲调|A sequence of single notes that is musically satisfying.  A tune.|The melody of the song was haunting and beautiful. 这首歌的旋律萦绕心头，优美动听。|近义词：tune, air, theme, strain|反义词: dissonance, cacophony|noun
