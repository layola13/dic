kittle:/ˈkɪtəl/| adj. vt.|易怒的，难对付的；使发痒；使激动|easily tickled; easily roused to laughter or tears; difficult to deal with; unpredictable |The kitten is very kittle and starts purring whenever you stroke it. 小猫很怕痒，你一摸它就发出呼噜声。|近义词：ticklish, touchy, sensitive, irritable, fidgety, restless|反义词：calm, placid, stoic|词性:形容词, 动词


kittle:/ˈkɪtəl/| vt.|使发痒；使激动|to tickle; to excite; to provoke|He kittled the baby's feet and made her laugh. 他挠了挠婴儿的脚，把她逗笑了。|近义词：tickle, tease, provoke, stimulate|反义词：soothe, calm, pacify|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *kitelen, frequentative of kiten (“to tickle”), from Old Norse kitla (“to tickle”). Cognate with Icelandic kitla (“to tickle”), Swedish kittla (“to tickle”), Danish kildre (“to tickle”).
