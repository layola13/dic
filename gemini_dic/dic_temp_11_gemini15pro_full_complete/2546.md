admiring:/ədˈmaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
钦佩的；赞赏的
Feeling or showing respect and warm approval.
She gave him an admiring glance. 她向他投以赞赏的一瞥。
近义词: appreciative, respectful, impressed, approving
反义词: critical, disapproving, scornful
词性: adjective
词源: From the verb "admire," which comes from the Old French "admirer" (to wonder at, marvel at), ultimately from the Latin "admirari" (to wonder at).
