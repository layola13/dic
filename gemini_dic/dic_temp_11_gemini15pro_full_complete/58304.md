dry-eyed:/ˌdraɪˈaɪd/| adj.|无泪的，不哭的，冷静的|Showing no signs of weeping, even when sadness or grief might be expected. |例句：She remained dry-eyed throughout the funeral, surprising everyone. 她葬礼自始至终都没有哭，让每个人都很惊讶。

例句：Despite the heartbreaking news, he delivered the announcement dry-eyed. 尽管消息令人心碎，他还是面无表情地宣布了这个消息。|近义词：unemotional, stoic, composed, impassive|反义词：tearful, weeping, crying, emotional|词性:形容词
