allocatable:/ˈæləkeɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可分配的 | Able to be allocated or assigned. |  Fortran allows for allocatable arrays, meaning their size isn't fixed at compile time.  Fortran 允许可分配数组，这意味着它们的大小不是在编译时固定的。

The company has allocatable resources for new projects. 公司为新项目分配了可分配资源。 | synonyms: assignable, distributable, apportionable | antonyms: fixed, non-assignable, permanent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From allocate + -able. Allocate comes from Late Latin *allocare, from ad- "to" + locare "to place," from locus "place."  -able is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of, susceptible of, fit for, tending to, given to."
