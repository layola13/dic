discompliance:/ˌdɪskəmˈplaɪəns/
n.
不服从，不遵守
failure or refusal to comply with a rule or regulation.
The patient's discompliance with the prescribed medication regimen led to a worsening of his condition. 病人不遵守规定的用药方案导致病情恶化。
noncompliance, disobedience, insubordination
compliance, obedience, conformity
noun
