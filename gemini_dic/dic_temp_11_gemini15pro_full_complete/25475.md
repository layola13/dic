bulletless:/ˈbʊlɪtləs/| adj.|无弹的，没有子弹的|Without bullets; lacking ammunition.  It refers to a firearm or magazine that does not contain any bullets. It can also more generally describe something that lacks a key component or driving force.|The police confiscated a bulletless gun from the suspect. 警方从嫌疑人手中没收了一把没有子弹的枪。|Synonyms: unloaded, empty, unarmed|Antonyms: loaded, armed|adjective
