loosed:/luːst/
|v.|释放，解开，放任|Past tense of loose.  The past tense of to loose is loosed. It means to release something or someone from a constraint.|He loosed the arrow from his bow. 他从弓上射出了箭。|近义词：released, freed, unleashed|反义词：tightened, fastened, bound|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English *losen, from Old Norse *leysa (“to loosen, release”), from Proto-Germanic *lausjaną (“to loosen, set free”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lew- (“to loosen, separate”). Cognate with Danish løse (“to loosen, solve”), Swedish lösa (“to loosen, solve, redeem”), Icelandic leysa (“to loosen, solve, redeem”). Related to loose.
