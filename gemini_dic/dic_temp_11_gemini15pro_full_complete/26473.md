byzantines:/bɪˈzæn.tɪnz/ | n. |  拜占庭人；拜占庭式的物品；复杂的、阴谋诡计的 | People of or relating to the Byzantine Empire or its capital, Constantinople (modern Istanbul). Also used to describe something excessively complex, intricate, or devious. | The Byzantines were renowned for their intricate mosaics. 拜占庭人以其复杂的马赛克而闻名。|  Byzantine art is characterized by its rich colors and religious themes. 拜占庭艺术的特点是色彩丰富，主题宗教化。| 近义词: convoluted, intricate, complex | 反义词: simple, straightforward | 词性: 名词

byzantine:/bɪˈzæn.taɪn/ | adj. | 拜占庭的；拜占庭式的；复杂的、阴谋诡计的 | Relating to Byzantium, its people, or its culture; excessively complex, intricate, or devious. | The byzantine plot was difficult to follow.  这个复杂的阴谋难以理解。| Navigating the byzantine bureaucracy was a nightmare.  在复杂的官僚机构中摸索简直就是一场噩梦。| 近义词: convoluted, intricate, complex | 反义词: simple, straightforward | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Late Latin *Byzantinus, from Byzantium (the ancient name of Constantinople), from Greek Byzantion.
