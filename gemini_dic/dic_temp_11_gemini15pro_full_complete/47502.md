dearest:/ˈdɪərɪst/| adj. n.|最亲爱的，最珍爱的（人）；|Used as a term of affection, especially at the beginning of a letter.  Also used to refer to something most cherished or valued.|Dearest Mary, I hope this letter finds you well.  最亲爱的玛丽，我希望这封信能让你一切安好。|My dearest wish is to travel the world. 我最珍爱的愿望是环游世界。|近义词：beloved, darling, precious|反义词：despised, hated|词性:形容词/名词
词源: From Old English *dēorost, superlative of dēore (“dear, valuable, precious, costly”).
