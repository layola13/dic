descriptivenesses:/dɪˈskrɪptɪvnəsɪz/| n. |描述性，描写性（复数）|The quality of being descriptive; the degree to which something is descriptive.|The descriptivenesses of his narratives brought the characters to life. 他的叙述的描述性使人物栩栩如生。|近义词：details, vividness, particularity|反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity|词性:名词

词源:  From descriptive + -ness + -es (plural).  Descriptive comes from the Latin *describere*, "to write down," from *de-* "down" + *scribere* "to write".
