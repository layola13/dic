apoliticism:/ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪsɪzəm/| n.|政治冷淡，不关心政治|The apoliticism of the younger generation is a cause for concern. 年轻一代对政治的冷漠令人担忧。|Apathy towards politics; lack of interest in political affairs.  A state of being unconcerned with political matters.  The quality or state of being apolitical.|Her apoliticism made her an unlikely candidate for student government. 她的不关心政治使她不太可能成为学生会候选人。|近义词：indifference, apathy, neutrality|反义词：engagement, activism, partisanship|词性:noun

Etymology:
From a- (not) + political + -ism.  Ultimately from Greek *politikos* (of citizens or the state).
