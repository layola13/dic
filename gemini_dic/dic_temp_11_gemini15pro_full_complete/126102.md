mutating:/ˈmjuːteɪtɪŋ/| adj.|变异的，突变的|Changing or altering, especially genetically.|例句：The mutating virus poses a significant threat to public health. 变异的病毒对公众健康构成重大威胁。

例句：Some organisms are capable of mutating rapidly to adapt to new environments. 一些生物能够快速变异以适应新环境。|近义词：changing, altering, modifying, transforming, evolving|反义词：static, unchanging, fixed, stable|词性:形容词
