entrant:/ˈɛntrənt/| n.|参赛者；进入者；新成员|A person or group that enters a contest or competition; a person who becomes a member of a group or organization.|例句：The entrant paid the fee and joined the race. 参赛者支付了费用并参加了比赛。|例句：The company welcomed the new entrants to the team. 公司欢迎团队的新成员。|近义词：candidate, applicant, participant, newcomer|反义词:incumbent, veteran|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French entrant, present participle of entrer “to enter,” from Latin intrare “to enter,” from intra “within.”
