harvestings:/ˈhɑːrvɪstɪŋz/
n.
收成，收获（复数）
The process of gathering a ripe crop from the fields.  This word emphasizes the action or process of collecting the harvest, often implying multiple instances or a continuous activity.
The farmer was pleased with the harvestings this year. 今年农民对收成很满意。

近义词: harvests, crops, yields, gatherings
反义词: plantings, sowings

词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "harvest," which derives from the Old English "hærfæst" meaning "autumn."
