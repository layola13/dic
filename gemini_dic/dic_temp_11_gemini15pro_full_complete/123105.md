mongrelisms:/ˈmʌŋɡrəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 混杂语；混合语；杂交语 | A mixture of different languages or dialects, or words formed by such a mixture; hybrid language. |  The text was full of mongrelisms, making it difficult to understand for someone unfamiliar with the local dialects. 这篇文章充满了混杂语，使得不熟悉当地方言的人难以理解。 | Synonyms: hybridism, jargon, slang, argot | Antonyms: purity, standard language | noun
