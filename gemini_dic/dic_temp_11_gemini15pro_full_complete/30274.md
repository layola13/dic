cementation:/ˌsɛmənˈteɪʃən/| n.|胶结，水泥化，渗碳|The process of binding particles together by the deposition of mineral matter in their interstices.  The process of hardening iron or steel by heating it in contact with a substance containing carbon.|The cementation of the loose sediments formed a solid rock.  松散沉积物的胶结形成了坚硬的岩石。|consolidation, solidification, lithification|erosion, weathering|noun
