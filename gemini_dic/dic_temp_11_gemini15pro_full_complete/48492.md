deeper:/ˈdiːpər/| adj.|更深的，更深层次的| extending further down from the top or surface than another of the same kind; situated further inside or further back than another.|The river is much deeper here than it was upstream. 这条河在这里比上游深得多。|The deeper meaning of the poem is about loss and grief. 这首诗的更深层含义是关于失去和悲伤。|近义词：lower, further, profound, intense|反义词：shallower, higher, superficial|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *dēopra, comparative of dēop (“deep”). Cognate with Dutch dieper, German tiefer, Old Norse djōpri.
