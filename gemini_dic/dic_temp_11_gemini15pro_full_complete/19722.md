bilateralism:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəlɪzəm/| n. |双边主义| The conduct of political, economic, or cultural relations between two sovereign states.|  Example 1: The two countries signed a treaty to promote bilateralism in trade.  这两个国家签署了一项条约，以促进贸易双边主义。

 Example 2: Bilateralism has been the cornerstone of the two nations' relationship for decades.  几十年来，双边主义一直是两国关系的基石。|近义词：two-sidedness, duality |反义词: multilateralism, unilateralism|词性:noun
