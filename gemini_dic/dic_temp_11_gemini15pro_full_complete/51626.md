developmentalist: /dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəlɪst/ | n. | 发展主义者；发展心理学家 | A specialist in the study of development, often referring to child development but also applicable to other areas like economic or social development. |  例句：The developmentalist observed the child's interaction with its mother.  发展心理学家观察了孩子与母亲的互动。

例句：The renowned developmentalist argued that early childhood experiences shape adult personality.  这位著名的发展心理学家认为，童年早期的经历塑造了成年人的性格。 | 近义词：development expert, development specialist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
