outdancing: /ˌaʊtˈdænsɪŋ/
verb
比…跳得更好
surpassing someone in dancing skill or endurance.
He outdanced everyone at the party. 他在派对上跳得比所有人都好。
Synonyms: outshine, surpass, excel
Antonyms: underperform
词性: 动词
