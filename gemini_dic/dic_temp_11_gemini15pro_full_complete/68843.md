fashion-forward:/ˈfæʃən ˈfɔːrwərd/
| adj. |
|时尚前卫的；走在时尚尖端的|
|Interested in and following the latest fashions and trends.|
|
|Example 1:  She's a fashion-forward dresser, always sporting the latest styles. 她是一位走在时尚前沿的穿着者，总是穿着最新的款式。

|Example 2: The fashion-forward designs in this collection are sure to be a hit. 这个系列中时尚前卫的设计一定会大受欢迎。
|
|近义词: trendy, stylish, chic, avant-garde, cutting-edge|
|反义词: old-fashioned, outdated, traditional, conservative|
|词性: 形容词|

ETYMOLOGY->
"fashion" (from Old French "façon" meaning "making, shape, manner") + "forward" (from Old English "foreweard" meaning "toward the front"). The combination signifies being at the forefront of fashion trends.
