becuna:/bɪˈkjuːnə/
noun
金枪鱼，鲔鱼
A large, fast-swimming food and game fish found in warm seas. It is a type of tuna.
The becuna, a prized game fish, is known for its speed and strength.  金枪鱼是一种珍贵的游钓鱼，以其速度和力量而闻名。

The fishermen hauled in a massive becuna. 渔民们拖上来一条巨大的金枪鱼。
synonym: tuna, bonito, albacore
