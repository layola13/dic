anti-diabetic:/ˌæntiˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk/ | n. | 抗糖尿病药 | A drug or treatment that is effective against diabetes. | Example:  The doctor recommended a new anti-diabetic. 医生推荐了一种新的抗糖尿病药物。 | Synonyms: antihyperglycemic agent, antidiabetic medication | Antonyms: - | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + diabetic.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Diabetic" comes from the Greek "diabainein," meaning "to pass through," referring to excessive urination, a symptom of diabetes.
