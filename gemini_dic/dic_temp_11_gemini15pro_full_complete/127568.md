nectar:/ˈnɛktər/| n.|花蜜，甘露，美味的饮料|A sweet liquid secreted by flowers, collected by bees and other insects, and used to make honey. Also, any delicious drink.|The bees busily collected nectar from the blossoms. 蜜蜂忙着从花朵中采集花蜜。|例句：The hummingbird sipped nectar from the flower.  蜂鸟从花中吸取花蜜。|近义词：honeydew, ambrosia|反义词：poison|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nectar* (nominative *nectar, nectaris*), from Greek νέκταρ (néktar), the drink of the gods, probably from *νη- (nē-) + *κτείνω (kteínō)* "to kill," thus meaning "death-overcoming."
