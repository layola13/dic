commandable:/kəˈmændəbl/ | adj. | 可指挥的，可控制的 | Able to be commanded or controlled; capable of being ordered or directed. | The troops were highly disciplined and commandable. 这些部队训练有素，指挥得当。| The situation is no longer commandable. 局势已无法控制。 | 近义词: controllable, manageable, governable | 反义词: unmanageable, uncontrollable, unruly | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from command + -able.
