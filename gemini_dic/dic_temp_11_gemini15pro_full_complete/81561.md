graystone:/ˈɡreɪstoʊn/| n. |灰石，灰色石头|A type of building stone, typically limestone or sandstone, that is gray in color. It is often used in construction, particularly for walls and buildings.|The old manor house was built of graystone. 这座古老的庄园是用灰石建造的。|近义词：limestone, sandstone, flagstone|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps contrasting materials like brick, wood)|noun
