impostrix:/ɪmˈpɒstrɪks/ | n. | 女骗子，女冒名顶替者 | A female impostor; a woman who deceives others by pretending to be someone else. |  She was exposed as an impostrix, claiming to be a doctor when she had no medical training. 她被揭露是一个骗子，声称自己是医生，但她没有受过任何医学训练。

She posed as an heiress, but she was really an impostrix trying to marry into wealth. 她冒充女继承人，但她实际上是个骗子，试图嫁入豪门。| 近义词: impostor, charlatan, fraud, fake | 反义词: genuine person, authentic individual | 词性: noun
