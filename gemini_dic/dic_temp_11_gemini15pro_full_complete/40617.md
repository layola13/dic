convenience:/kənˈviːniəns/ | n. | 便利；方便；便利设施；舒适 |Something that is convenient is suitable for your needs and easy to do or use. |The house has every modern convenience. 这房子拥有所有现代化的便利设施。|  Synonyms: ease, comfort, handiness, accessibility | Antonyms: inconvenience, difficulty, hardship | noun

convenience:/kənˈviːniəns/ | n. | 便利的事物；方便的用具；（公共）厕所 |A convenience is something that is useful and makes life easier or more comfortable. |Public conveniences are located in the park. 公园里设有公共厕所。| Synonyms: amenity, facility, resource, appliance | Antonyms: inconvenience, obstruction, hindrance | noun
