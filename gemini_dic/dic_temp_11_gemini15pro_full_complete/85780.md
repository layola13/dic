haymaker:/ˈheɪmeɪkər/| n.| 挥动大镰刀; (尤指拳击中)大力的一击; 竭尽全力的尝试|A powerful blow, especially in boxing, aimed at knocking out an opponent; a forceful attempt to achieve something.|The boxer landed a haymaker that sent his opponent sprawling. 拳击手挥出一记重拳，把对手打得踉踉跄跄。|近义词：punch, wallop, knockout blow|反义词:jab, light punch|noun

haymaker:/ˈheɪmeɪkər/| n.| 干草机; 干草制造者|(dated) A person who makes hay.|The haymakers were out in the field all day. 干草工整天都在田里。|近义词：hay farmer, harvester|反义词: N/A|noun
