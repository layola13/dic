irrelevancies:/ɪˈrɛləvənsiz/| n. |不相干的事物；无关紧要的信息|Things that are not related to the subject being discussed; unimportant information.|The report was filled with irrelevancies and it was difficult to find the key information.  这份报告充满了无关紧要的内容，很难找到关键信息。|Synonyms:  extraneous details, nonessentials, trivia|Antonyms:  essentials, pertinent information, relevant details|noun
