conchyliologist:/ˌkɒŋkɪliˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌkɑːnkiːliˈɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
贝类学家
A person who studies shells, especially mollusks' shells.
She is a renowned conchyliologist with an extensive collection of rare shells. 她是一位著名的贝类学家，收藏了大量的稀有贝壳。

The conchyliologist identified the shell as belonging to a rare species of snail.  这位贝类学家鉴定出这个贝壳属于一种稀有的蜗牛。

近义词: malacologist (软体动物学家)
反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *conchylium* (shellfish, mussel, oyster) + -o- + -logist (one who studies).  *Conchylium* is from Greek *konchylion* (small shellfish, shell), diminutive of *konche* (mussel, cockle, shell).
