outarm: /ˌaʊtˈɑːrm/
verb
超过…的臂力范围;胜过;
to have longer arms than someone, or to be able to reach further than someone.  To surpass or outdo someone, especially in power, resources, or cunning.
The taller basketball player could easily outarm his opponent.  更高的篮球运动员可以轻易地超过对手的臂展。
He was able to outarm his rivals and secure the contract. 他能够胜过竞争对手，并获得合同。
近义词: outmaneuver, outwit, surpass, exceed
反义词: yield, surrender, succumb
