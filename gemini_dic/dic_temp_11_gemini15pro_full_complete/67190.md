expresses:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪz/
|v.|表达，表示|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "express." It signifies conveying a thought, feeling, or idea through words, actions, or artistic means. |She expresses herself beautifully through her paintings. 她通过她的画作优美地表达自己。|Synonyms: conveys, communicates, articulates, manifests|Antonyms: suppresses, conceals, hides|verb
