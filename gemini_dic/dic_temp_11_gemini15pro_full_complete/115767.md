marinating:/ˈmærɪneɪtɪŋ/| v.| 腌制，浸泡|The process of soaking food in a seasoned liquid, typically acidic, before cooking to enhance flavor and tenderness.|Example: She is marinating the chicken in a mixture of lemon juice, herbs, and spices. 她正在用柠檬汁、香草和香料的混合物腌制鸡肉。|Example: Marinating the steak overnight makes it more tender.  将牛排腌制过夜会使它更嫩。|近义词：soaking, immersing|反义词:drying|词性:动词 (verb)

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mariner*, "to pickle in sea water," from *marin*, "sea, brine," from Latin *marinus*, "of the sea," from *mare*, "sea."
