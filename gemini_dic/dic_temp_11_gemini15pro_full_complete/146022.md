overdrains:/ˈoʊvərˌdreɪnz/
n.|过量排水；排水暗管；排水系统|A system of pipes or channels installed beneath the surface of the ground to collect and remove excess water. |The overdrains prevented waterlogging in the field. 排水暗管防止了田地积水。|
Synonyms: drainage system, underdrains, French drains
Antonyms: irrigation system
词性: 名词

overdrain:/ˈoʊvərˌdreɪn/
v.|过量排水；过度消耗|To drain excessively; to deplete or exhaust.|Excessive pumping can overdrain the aquifer. 过度抽水会耗尽含水层。|
Synonyms: deplete, exhaust, overdraw
Antonyms: replenish, refill
词性: 动词
