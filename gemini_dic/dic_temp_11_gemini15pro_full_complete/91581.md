hyperactivity:/ˌhaɪpərækˈtɪvɪti/
n.|多动症，过度活跃|A condition characterized by excessive restlessness and movement.|The child's hyperactivity made it difficult for him to focus in class.  这个孩子的多动症使他难以集中注意力上课。|Synonyms: restlessness, fidgetiness, excitability|Antonyms: calmness, stillness, tranquility|noun

hyperactive:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪv/
adj.|多动的，过度活跃的|Excessively restless and active.|The hyperactive puppy was constantly running and jumping.  这只多动的小狗不停地跑来跑去、跳来跳去。|Synonyms: restless, energetic, lively|Antonyms: calm, quiet, still|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ὑπερ- (huper-, “over, beyond”) + Latin *activus (“active”)
