blackjack:/ˈblækˌdʒæk/ | n. |二十一点（一种纸牌游戏）;  黑色手杖 |A card game in which players try to acquire cards with a face value totalling 21 or as close as possible to 21 without going over. Also, a small, leather-covered club with a flexible handle, used as a weapon. |  He won a lot of money playing blackjack. 他玩二十一点赢了很多钱。| Synonyms: twenty-one (card game), cosh, sap (weapon) | Antonyms: None | noun

blackjack:/ˈblækˌdʒæk/ | vt. |用黑色手杖打; 胁迫 |To hit someone with a blackjack. Also, to coerce or extort. | The thugs blackjacked the shopkeeper and stole all the cash. 暴徒用黑色手杖打了店主，偷走了所有的现金。 | Synonyms: bludgeon, cosh, coerce, extort | Antonyms: None | verb
