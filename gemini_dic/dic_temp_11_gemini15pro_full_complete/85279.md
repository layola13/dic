harmonises:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzɪz/ /ˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪz/| v.|协调，使和谐，使一致|The new building harmonises well with its surroundings.  这座新建筑与其周围环境十分协调。|例句：The colours in the painting harmonise beautifully.  画中的色彩搭配得非常和谐。|近义词：blends, coordinates, matches, integrates, conforms|反义词：clashes, conflicts, disrupts, jars|词性：动词

harmonises:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzɪz/ /ˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪz/| v.|（使）相符，（使）一致，（使）相协调|His actions do not harmonise with his words. 他的言行不一。|例句：The government's policies don't always harmonise with public opinion. 政府的政策并不总是与公众舆论一致。|近义词：accord, agree, correspond, tally|反义词：contradict, clash, disagree|词性：动词

Etymology: From French *harmoniser*, from Late Latin *harmonizare*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονίζειν* (harmonizein), from *ἁρμονία* (harmonia, “harmony”).
