gesticulatory:/dʒɛsˈtɪkjʊlətɔːri/
adjective
做手势的；伴有手势的
Characterized by or involving gestures, especially dramatic ones.
The speaker's gesticulatory style captivated the audience. 演讲者富有表现力的手势吸引了观众。
gesticular, expressive, animated
reserved, restrained
adjective
