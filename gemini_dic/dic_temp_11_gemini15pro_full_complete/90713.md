humidifier:/hjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪər/ | n. | 加湿器 | A device that adds moisture to the air. |  I use a humidifier in the winter to prevent dry skin. 我在冬天使用加湿器以防止皮肤干燥。

|例句：The humidifier helps to alleviate the dryness in the air during winter. 加湿器有助于缓解冬季空气干燥。|近义词：vaporizer, atomizer |反义词: dehumidifier |词性: noun
