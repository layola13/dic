factitive:/ˈfæktɪtɪv/
adjective
使役的，致使的
Causing something to happen or exist; causative.
The verb "make" in the sentence "He made her cry" is used factitively.  他让她哭了，这里的“make”用作使役动词。
近义词: causative, effective
反义词: preventive, inhibitory
词性: adjective

factitive:/ˈfæktɪtɪv/
noun
使役动词，使役结构
A verb that causes something to happen or exist; a causative verb or construction.
"Make," "have," and "get" can be used as factitive verbs. “Make”、“have”和“get”可以用作使役动词。
近义词: causative
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *factitivus, from factus (past participle of facere ‘to do, make’), from the same Indo-European base as Greek the-, ti-thenai ‘to put, place’.
