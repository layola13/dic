antilibration:/ˌænti laɪˈbreɪʃən/
noun
月球天平动
The slight oscillatory motion of a celestial body about its center, relative to an observer, especially such a motion of the Moon or other satellite.
The spacecraft's orbit was designed to minimize the effects of antilibration.  该航天器的轨道设计旨在最大限度地减少天平动的影响。
libration, oscillation
fixity, stillness
