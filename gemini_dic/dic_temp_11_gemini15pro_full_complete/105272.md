kholah:/ˈkoʊlə/| n.|新娘价格，彩礼|Bride price, a sum of money or other valuables paid by the groom or his family to the bride's family upon marriage, primarily practiced in some Middle Eastern and African cultures.|The kholah for his bride was substantial, consisting of livestock and gold.|近义词：bride price, dowry (although dowry is paid by the bride's family), mahr (Islamic term)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
