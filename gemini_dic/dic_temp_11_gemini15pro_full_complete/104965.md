kempe:/kɛmp/ | n. | 冠军; 勇士; 战士; (尤指中世纪的)武士 | A champion; a warrior, especially a medieval soldier. |  Sir Gawain was a noble kempe of King Arthur's Round Table. 高文爵士是亚瑟王圆桌会议中一位高贵的武士。 | 近义词: champion, warrior, knight, soldier | 反义词: coward | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kempe, from Old Norse *kempa (“champion, fighter”), from Proto-Germanic *kampjoną (“fighter, warrior”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱemp- (“to fight”). Cognate with Old English cempa (“warrior, soldier”).
