centredness:/ˈsɛntədness/ | n. | 中心性；专注；镇定 | the state of being centred or focused; a feeling of calm and stability |  She maintained her centredness despite the chaos around her.  她尽管周围一片混乱，却依然保持镇定。 | synonyms: composure, calmness, equanimity, poise, self-possession | antonyms: anxiety, agitation, nervousness, unease, distress | noun

Etymology:  From "centred" + "-ness". "Centred" comes from the verb "centre" + "-ed". "Centre" originates from the Old French "centre" and Latin "centrum", meaning "center point".
