overcoach:/ˈoʊvərkoʊtʃ/| v.|过度指导，过度训练|To coach someone excessively, often to the point of detriment.  This can involve too much practice, too much instruction, or too much pressure to perform.|The team was overcoached and burned out before the playoffs even started.  这个队伍在季后赛开始前就被过度训练，精疲力竭了。

The swim coach overcoached his star pupil, leading to injury and burnout.  游泳教练过度训练了他的明星学生，导致受伤和倦怠。|近义词：overtrain, overwork|反义词: undercoach, neglect|词性:动词
