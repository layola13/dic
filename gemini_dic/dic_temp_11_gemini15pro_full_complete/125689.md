mundificant:/ˈmʌndɪfɪkənt/
adjective
清洁的，净化的
Having the power to cleanse or purify.
The shaman used a mundificant herb to purify the sacred space.  萨满用一种清洁的草药净化了圣地。
The spring water is reputed to have mundificant properties.  据说泉水具有净化特性。
近义词: cleansing, purifying, detergent, antiseptic
反义词: polluting, contaminating, soiling
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *mundificantem (nominative mundificans), present participle of mundificare "to make clean," from mundus "clean, neat, elegant" + -ficare (from facere "to make, do").
