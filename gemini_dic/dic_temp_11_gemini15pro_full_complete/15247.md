backgammon:/ˈbækɡæmən/| n. |西洋双陆棋|A board game for two players, played on a board with 24 points, where each player moves their 15 pieces according to the throw of the dice, the object being to bear off all one's pieces before the opponent.|I played backgammon with my grandfather every Sunday. 我每个星期天都和我的祖父玩西洋双陆棋。|近义词：board game, table game|反义词:None|词性:noun
backgammons:/ˈbækɡæmənz/| n. |(复数)西洋双陆棋|The plural form of "backgammon," referring to multiple games or instances of the game.|We played several backgammons that evening. 那天晚上我们玩了几盘西洋双陆棋。|近义词：board games, table games|反义词:None|词性:noun
