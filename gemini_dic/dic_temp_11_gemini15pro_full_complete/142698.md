otherguise:/ˈʌðərɡaɪz/
noun
伪装, 掩饰, 假扮
a different appearance or manner used to hide one's true identity or intentions
He attended the party in other guise, dressed as a pirate. 他伪装成海盗参加了派对。
synonyms: disguise, camouflage, masquerade, pretense
antonyms: true form, real identity, honesty, openness
