geomantical:/ˌdʒiːəˈmæntɪkəl/
adjective
地气的；风水术的；占卜的
Relating to or based on geomancy; pertaining to divination by geographic features or lines and figures.
The geomantical properties of the site were considered auspicious for building a temple.  这个地点的风水被认为是建造寺庙的吉兆。
The ancient emperor consulted a geomancer before making important decisions. 古代皇帝在做重要决定之前会咨询风水师。
近义词: divinatory, augural, prophetic
反义词: scientific, empirical
词性: adjective
