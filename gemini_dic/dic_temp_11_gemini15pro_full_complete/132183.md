nondamnation:/ˌnɑndæmˈneɪʃən/
n.
absence of damnation; salvation.
The state of not being condemned or punished, especially in a religious context.
His belief in nondamnation offered him comfort. 他对不被惩罚的信念给了他安慰。
Near Synonyms: salvation, redemption, absolution, exoneration, acquittal.
Near Antonyms: damnation, condemnation, conviction.
