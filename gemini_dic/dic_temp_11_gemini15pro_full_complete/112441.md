louche:/luːʃ/ | adj. |  不体面的；可疑的；声名狼藉的；不正经的 | Disreputable or shady; of questionable taste or morality; suggesting immorality or lack of respectability. |  He frequented louche nightclubs and gambling dens. 他经常光顾声名狼藉的夜总会和赌场。 | 近义词: shady, dubious, disreputable, seedy, sleazy, decadent | 反义词: respectable, reputable, decent, proper, moral | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From French *louche* "squinting, cross-eyed," figuratively "ambiguous, suspicious," from Latin *luscus* "one-eyed, blind in one eye." The semantic shift likely occurred because squinting can be associated with suspicion or shiftiness.
