filtrates:/ˈfɪltreɪts/ | n. | 滤液；滤出物 | A liquid that has passed through a filter. | The filtrates were collected and analyzed. 滤液被收集起来并进行了分析。|  The chemist carefully collected the filtrates from the experiment. 化学家小心地收集实验中的滤液。 | synonyms: filtrate, residue, effluent | antonyms: retentate, precipitate | noun

filter:/ˈfɪltər/ | n. vt. vi. | 过滤器；滤波器；筛选；过滤 | A device or material that allows liquid or gas to pass through it while removing solid particles or impurities. | The coffee filter separated the grounds from the liquid. 咖啡过滤器将咖啡渣和液体分离。 |  The photographer used a filter to enhance the colors of the sunset. 摄影师使用滤镜来增强日落时的色彩。 | synonyms: strainer, sieve, screen | antonyms:  block, obstruct | noun, verb
