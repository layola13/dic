adulteries: /əˈdʌltəriːz/
n.
通奸，婚外情
Voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and a person who is not their spouse.
He was accused of committing several adulteries. 他被指控犯有多起通奸罪。
Synonyms: infidelity, unfaithfulness, affair, cheating
Antonyms: fidelity, faithfulness
