coccosphere:/ˈkɒkəˌsfɪə(r)| n.|球石藻|A single-celled planktonic alga (specifically a coccolithophore) encased in a spherical shell of interlocking calcareous plates called coccoliths.|Coccospheres play a vital role in the ocean's carbon cycle. 球石藻在海洋碳循环中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: coccolithophorid,  nanoplankton|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider larger plankton like diatoms or dinoflagellates)|Noun
