honeybird:/ˈhʌniˌbɜrd/| n.|响蜜鸟|A bird, typically of the family Indicatoridae, that chatters loudly to attract the attention of a person or animal to a bees' nest, which it then raids after the honey has been taken.|The honeybird led us to a wild beehive.|近义词：honeyguide|反义词:|名词
