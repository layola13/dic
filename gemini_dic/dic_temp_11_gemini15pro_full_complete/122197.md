misurato:/miːzuˈrɑːtoʊ/ | adj. | 慎重的，精确的，有分寸的 | Measured; deliberate; careful.  Derived from the Italian "misurato," past participle of "misurare" (to measure). | The pianist played the piece with misurato elegance.  钢琴家以谨慎优雅的方式演奏了这首曲子。 | Synonyms: measured, deliberate, careful, precise, calculated, controlled | Antonyms: hasty, rash, impulsive, reckless, careless, impetuous | adjective

misurato:/miːzuˈrɑːtoʊ/ | adv. | 慎重的，精确的，有分寸的 | In a measured or deliberate manner.  Derived from the Italian "misurato," past participle of "misurare" (to measure).  Used less frequently as an adverb. | He spoke misurato, choosing his words carefully. 他说话谨慎，字斟句酌。 | Synonyms: measuredly, deliberately, carefully, precisely | Antonyms: hastily, rashly, impulsively, recklessly, carelessly | adverb
