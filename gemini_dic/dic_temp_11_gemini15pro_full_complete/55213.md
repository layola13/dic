disservices:/dɪsˈsɜːrvɪsɪz/
|n.| 损害，伤害，帮倒忙 |A disservice is an action that harms someone or something.|  His comments were a disservice to the company. 他的评论对公司有害。|  The portrayal of women in this movie is a disservice to their true capabilities. 这部电影中对女性的描绘是对她们真正能力的损害。 | synonyms: harm, damage, injury, detriment, prejudice | antonyms: service, benefit, help, aid, assistance | noun
