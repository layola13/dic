herte:/ˈhɛrtə/ (Middle English)| n.| 心脏；勇气；核心；鹿|A Middle English word meaning "heart," referring to the organ, but also figuratively to courage, the center of something, or a deer (specifically a male red deer over five years old).|He showed great herte in battle. 他在战斗中表现出极大的勇气。|近义词：heart, courage, core, stag|反义词:cowardice, periphery|词性:noun
