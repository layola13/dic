curcuma:/ˈkɜːrkjʊmə/ | n. | 姜黄，郁金 | A rhizomatous herbaceous perennial plant of the ginger family, native to Southeast Asia, having yellow flowers and a fleshy rhizome used as a spice and dyestuff. |  Turmeric is derived from the root of the curcuma plant. 姜黄是从姜黄植物的根部提取的。| Synonyms: turmeric, saffron (as a dye) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

curcuma:/ˈkɜːrkjʊmə/ | n. | 姜黄粉 | The spice obtained from the dried rhizome of the curcuma plant, typically ground into a deep orange-yellow powder. |  Curcuma adds a vibrant color and earthy flavor to curries. 姜黄粉为咖喱增添了鲜艳的色彩和泥土的味道。| Synonyms: turmeric powder | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
