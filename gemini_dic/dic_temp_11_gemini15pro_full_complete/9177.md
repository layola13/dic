antinihilists:/ˌæntiˈnaɪəlists/| n.|反虚无主义者|A person who opposes nihilism, the belief that life is meaningless.|The antinihilists argued that there was inherent value in human existence.|Synonyms:  optimist,  life-affirmer,  existentialist (sometimes)|Antonyms: nihilist, pessimist|noun
