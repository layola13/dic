capita:/kəˈpɪtə/ | n. | 人头; 个人; |  A person, especially when considered as one of a number, such as in a calculation of per capita income. | The tax is levied at a flat rate per capita.  这税是按人头征收的。| synonyms: individual, person, soul | antonyms: N/A | noun

capitatim:/ˌkæpɪˈteɪtɪm/ | adv., adj. | 按人头; 人均; |  Per head; for each person; by the head. Used especially of payments. | The doctor is paid capitatim.  医生按人头收费。| synonyms: per capita, per head, individually | antonyms: N/A | adverb, adjective
