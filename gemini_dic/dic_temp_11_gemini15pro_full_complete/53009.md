dinosauria:/ˌdaɪnəˈsɔːriə/| n. |恐龙类|A taxonomic clade of reptiles, often very large, that first appeared roughly 245 million years ago. Dinosaurs dominated the land for over 160 million years, before most species became extinct at the end of the Cretaceous Period.  |The Dinosauria includes diverse groups such as theropods (like Tyrannosaurus rex) and sauropods (like Brachiosaurus). 恐龙类包括各种各样的群体，例如兽脚亚目（如霸王龙）和蜥脚亚目（如腕龙）。| Dinosauria encompasses a vast array of prehistoric reptiles. 恐龙类包含了大量的史前爬行动物。|近义词:dinosaurs|反义词: (None - Dinosauria is a taxonomic classification, not a concept with an opposite.)|词性:noun
