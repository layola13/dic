horribly:/ˈhɒrəbli/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrəbli/ (US)| adv.|可怕地；非常；极其|in a horrible manner; to a dreadful extent|例句：The play was horribly boring. 这部戏无聊得可怕。|例句：She sang horribly out of tune.  她唱歌跑调得厉害。|近义词：awfully, terribly, dreadfully, frightfully|反义词：wonderfully, beautifully, delightfully|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *horribli, from Old French horrible, from Latin horribilis (“causing horror, dreadful, terrible, frightful”), from horrēre (“to bristle, shudder, tremble at, dread”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghers- (“to bristle”).
