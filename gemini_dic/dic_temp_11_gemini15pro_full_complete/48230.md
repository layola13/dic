deconstructor:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌktər/
n.
解构主义者，解构者
a person who deconstructs something, especially a text or a work of art
例句：The deconstructor argued that the text was full of inherent contradictions. 这位解构主义者认为文本充满了内在矛盾。

例句：Literary deconstructors analyze texts to reveal their underlying assumptions.  文学解构主义者分析文本以揭示其潜在的假设。

近义词：analyst, critic, interpreter
反义词：constructor, builder

词性: noun

词源: From deconstruct + -or.  Deconstruct itself comes from French déconstruire, from dé- + construire "to construct," from Latin construere.
