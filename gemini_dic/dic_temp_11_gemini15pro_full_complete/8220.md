anti-gallicanism:/ˌæntiˈɡælɪkənɪzəm/
n.
反高卢主义；反对法国或法国文化的影响
Opposition to French influence, especially in political or religious matters.
The rise of anti-gallicanism in 18th-century England led to boycotts of French goods. 18世纪英格兰反高卢主义的兴起导致了对法国商品的抵制。
Anti-gallicanism fueled English nationalism during the Napoleonic Wars.  拿破仑战争期间，反高卢主义助长了英国的民族主义。
Synonym: Francophobia
Antonym: Gallophilia
