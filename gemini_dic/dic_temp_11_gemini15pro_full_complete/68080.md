fade:/feɪd/| v. n.| 褪色，逐渐消失，衰落 |To lose freshness or intensity of color or appearance; to gradually disappear or diminish. |The flowers faded in the hot sun. 这些花在烈日下枯萎了。|synonyms: diminish, disappear, wane, wither |antonyms: brighten, intensify, flourish, strengthen |verb

fade:/feɪd/| n.| 逐渐消失，衰落 |The act or process of fading. |The fade of the music signaled the end of the show.  音乐的逐渐消失标志着演出的结束。|synonyms: decline, decay |antonyms: rise, growth |noun
