accreditable:/əˈkrɛdɪtəbl/
adjective
可 accreditation 的；可授权的；可认证的
able to be accredited; capable of being officially recognized or authorized.
The online course is accreditable by a recognized professional body.  这个在线课程可以被一个公认的专业机构认证。
This training program is accreditable for continuing education credits.  这个培训项目可以获得继续教育学分。
近义词:certifiable, attestable, verifiable, confirmable
反义词:unaccredited, uncertified, unverified
词性:adjective

词源:
From accredit + -able.  来自 accredit + -able。
