jibbing:/ˈdʒɪbɪŋ/
n.|拒绝，推诿；(尤指马)突然止步不前|Refusal to do something; especially of a horse, stopping suddenly and refusing to jump a fence.|The horse's jibbing cost him the race.  这匹马的止步不前让他输掉了比赛。|synonyms: balking, refusing, shirking|antonyms: complying, cooperating|noun

jibbing:/ˈdʒɪbɪŋ/
v.| (尤指马)突然止步不前; 拒绝，推诿|(especially of a horse) to stop suddenly and refuse to jump a fence; to refuse to do something|He jibbed at the last minute and didn't sign the contract.  他在最后一刻退缩了，没有签合同。|synonyms: balk, refuse, shirk|antonyms: comply, cooperate|verb
