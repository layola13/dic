leakless:/ˈliːklɪs/| adj.|无泄漏的，不漏的|Free from leaks; not allowing fluid to escape.|The new water bottle is completely leakless and can be tossed in a bag without worry.  这个新的水瓶完全不漏水，可以放心地扔在包里。|The submarine's leakless hull was crucial for its deep-sea missions.  这艘潜艇不漏水的船体对其深海任务至关重要。|近义词：tight, sealed, impermeable|反义词:leaky, permeable|词性:形容词
词源: leak (n. 漏洞) + -less (suffix forming adjectives meaning "without" or "free from")
