obstructs:/əbˈstrʌkts/
verb
阻碍，阻塞
blocks or closes up (something, such as a passage, road, or view)
The fallen tree obstructs the road.  倒下的树阻塞了道路。

近义词: blocks, hinders, impedes, bars, clogs
反义词: clears, aids, assists, facilitates, opens
词性: verb
