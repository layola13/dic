distractious: /dɪˈstræktɪʃəs/
adjective
易分心的，注意力不集中的
Easily distracted; tending to distract.
The child was distractious and had trouble focusing on his homework.  这个孩子很容易分心，很难集中精力做作业。
The open office environment proved to be too distractious for productive work. 开放式办公环境被证明太容易让人分心，不利于高效工作。
近义词: distractible, inattentive, unfocused
反义词: attentive, focused, concentrated
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From distract (v.) + -ious (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by").  Distract comes from Latin *distractus*, past participle of distrahere "to draw apart, divide, distract," from dis- "apart" + trahere "to draw."
