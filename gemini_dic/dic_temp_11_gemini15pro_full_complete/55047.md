disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/
n.
专题论文；学术讨论；详尽的探讨
A formal and lengthy discourse on a subject, often in writing.
The professor delivered a lengthy disquisition on the history of philosophy. 教授就哲学史发表了长篇专题论文。

The book is a disquisition on the nature of good and evil. 这本书是对善与恶的本质的详尽探讨。

近义词: discourse, treatise, essay, exposition, dissertation
反义词: summary, synopsis, abstract
词性: noun
