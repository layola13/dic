liberatory:/ˈlɪbərəˌtɔːri/
adjective
解放的，释放的
Tending to liberate; promoting liberation.  Setting free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression; providing freedom.
例句：The liberatory power of education. 教育的解放力量。
例句：The group engaged in liberatory actions to free political prisoners.  该组织参与解放政治犯的行动。
近义词：emancipatory, freeing, liberating, releasing
反义词：oppressive, restrictive, enslaving, confining
词性：adjective

liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/
verb
解放，释放
Set (someone or something) free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression; release.
例句：The troops liberated the city from the enemy.  部队将城市从敌人手中解放出来。
例句：She felt liberated after quitting her stressful job.  辞掉压力大的工作后，她感到如释重负。
近义词：free, release, emancipate, unchain
反义词：imprison, enslave, confine, restrict
词性：verb
