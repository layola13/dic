explicate:/ˈɛksplɪkeɪt/| v. |解释；说明；阐明|To analyze and develop (an idea or principle) in detail.|The professor explicated the poem line by line. 教授逐行解释了这首诗。|近义词：explain, clarify, interpret, elucidate|反义词：obfuscate, confuse, complicate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin explicatus, past participle of explicare (“to unfold, unravel, explain”), from ex- (“out”) + plicare (“to fold”).
