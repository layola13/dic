nimbostratus:/ˌnɪmboʊˈstrɑːtəs/ | n. | 雨层云 | A dark, gray cloud layer, thick enough to block out the sun and typically associated with continuous rain or snow. |  The nimbostratus clouds brought a steady drizzle all day. 雨层云带来了整天持续的毛毛雨。| Synonym: rain cloud | Antonym: clear sky | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nimbus* (rain cloud) + *stratus* (spread out).
