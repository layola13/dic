irrelapsable:/ˌɪrɪˈlæpsəbl/
adjective
不能复发的；不可逆转的
Not able to relapse; irreversible.
His condition was diagnosed as irrelapsable. 他的病情被诊断为不可逆转的。
The doctor assured him that his recovery was irrelapsable. 医生向他保证他的康复是不会复发的。
近义词: incurable, terminal, irreversible, permanent
反义词: relapsing, recurrent, temporary, curable
词性: adjective
