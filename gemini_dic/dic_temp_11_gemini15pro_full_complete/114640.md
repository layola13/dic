malevolent:/məˈlɛvələnt/| adj.|恶毒的；怀有恶意的；有害的|Wishing evil or harm to another or others; showing ill will; malicious.  Characterized by or indicative of ill will; arising from intense ill will or hatred.|He gave her a malevolent glare. 他恶狠狠地瞪了她一眼。|Synonyms: malicious, spiteful, wicked, evil, vicious, malign, sinister, hostile, baleful, venomous, poisonous, rancorous|Antonyms: benevolent, benign, kind, compassionate|adjective
