barken:/ˈbɑːrkən/ (US) /ˈbɑːkən/ (UK)| v.|吠叫；咆哮；厉声说话|to make a loud, rough noise, like the sound a dog makes; to shout at someone in a loud, angry voice|The dog barked at the stranger. 狗对着陌生人吠叫。
