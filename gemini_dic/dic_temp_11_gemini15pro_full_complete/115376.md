mansarded:/ˈmænsɑːrdɪd/
adjective
屋顶为曼萨德式的；有曼萨德屋顶的
Having a mansard roof; mansard-roofed.
The mansarded roof provided extra living space on the top floor.  这栋有曼萨德式屋顶的房子在顶层提供了额外的居住空间。
The hotel was a grand building with a mansarded roof and ornate balconies. 这家酒店是一座宏伟的建筑，带有曼萨德式屋顶和华丽的阳台。

近义词:mansard-roofed, sloped, angled
反义词:flat-roofed
