naturopathic:/ˌneɪtʃəˈræpɪk/ | adj. | 自然疗法的 | Relating to or using naturopathic medicine, a system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage. |  Example: She sought naturopathic treatment for her allergies. 她寻求自然疗法治疗她的过敏。 | Example: He's a qualified naturopathic practitioner. 他是一名合格的自然疗法医生。| Synonyms: holistic, natural, alternative | Antonyms: conventional, allopathic | adjective

Etymology:  From nature + -pathic (from Greek pathos "suffering").  The word "naturopathy" was coined in 1895 by John Scheel and popularized by Benedict Lust.
