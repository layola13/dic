funnily:/ˈfʌnɪli/| adv.|滑稽地，古怪地，奇怪地|In a funny or amusing manner; strangely or unusually.|He was funnily dressed for the occasion. 他穿的衣服在这个场合很滑稽。|She looked at me funnily. 她奇怪地看着我。|近义词：strangely, oddly, curiously, amusingly|反义词：seriously, normally, typically|词性:副词

词源: From "funny" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Funny" originates from the early 18th century, meaning "causing amusement or laughter," later developing senses of "strange" and "peculiar."
