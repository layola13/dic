mammose:/ˈmæməʊs/ (US) /ˈmæməʊs/ (UK)| adj.| 乳房状的，乳头状的| Having breast-like shapes; resembling a breast or nipple, especially in being rounded and protuberant. | The cactus had mammose protrusions all over its surface.  仙人掌的表面布满了乳头状的突起。| Synonyms: mammillary, mammiform, rounded, protuberant | Antonyms: flat, smooth | Adjective
