helpful:/ˈhɛlpfəl/| adj.|有帮助的；有益的|Giving or ready to give help. |例句：She gave me some helpful advice. 她给了我一些有用的建议。|例句：The staff were very helpful and friendly.  工作人员非常乐于助人，也很友好。|近义词：useful, beneficial, assisting, supportive|反义词：unhelpful, useless|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *helpeful, equivalent to help +‎ -ful.
