heatlike:/ˈhiːtlaɪk/
adj.
像热的; 似热的; 热似的
Resembling or characteristic of heat.
The sensation was heatlike, a spreading warmth across her skin. 这种感觉像热，一股暖流在她皮肤上蔓延开来。
The engine emitted a heatlike shimmer in the desert air. 引擎在沙漠的空气中发出像热一样的闪光。
近义词:  thermal, burning, fiery
反义词: cold, cool, icy
词性: 形容词


词源:  A combination of "heat" and the suffix "-like," meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of." "Heat" comes from Old English *hætu.  "-like" comes from Old English *līc.
