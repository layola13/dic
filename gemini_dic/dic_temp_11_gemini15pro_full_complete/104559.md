kamsin:/ˈkæmsɪn/| n. |卡辛风，热风|A hot, dry, southerly wind that blows in Egypt and the Sudan, often carrying sand and dust.|The kamsin blew relentlessly for days, making travel difficult and unpleasant. 卡辛风无情地刮了几天，使旅行变得困难和不愉快。|Synonyms: sirocco, simoom|Antonyms: breeze, zephyr|noun
