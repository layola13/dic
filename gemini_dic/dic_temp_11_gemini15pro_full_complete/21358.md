blosmy: /ˈblɒzmi/ (UK) /ˈblɑːzmi/ (US) | adj. | 盛开的，花团锦簇的；青春焕发的 | Covered in blossoms; looking healthy and attractive. | The apple trees were blosmy and fragrant in the spring sunshine. 春天的阳光下，苹果树开满了花，香气扑鼻。|  The children's faces were blosmy with health. 孩子们的脸上洋溢着健康的红润。 | 近义词: blooming, flourishing, blossoming | 反义词: withered, barren | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "blossom" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Blossom" itself comes from Old English *blōstm, related to *blōwan "to bloom," from Proto-Germanic *blōsman.
