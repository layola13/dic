cartloads:/ˈkɑːrtloʊdz/ | n. | 满载; 一车一车的 | A large quantity of something, enough to fill several carts. | The farmer brought cartloads of pumpkins to the market. 农民把一车车的南瓜运到市场。|  The market was overflowing with cartloads of fresh produce. 市场里堆满了新鲜农产品。| synonyms: abundance, heaps, lots, masses, piles | antonyms: little, scarcity | noun
