amanita:/ˌæməˈnaɪtə/ /ˌæməˈniːtə/ | n. | 鹅膏菌属；毒蝇伞 |A genus of poisonous mushrooms, including the death cap and the fly agaric.  These mushrooms are known for their often brightly colored caps and stems and are highly toxic, some being deadly if ingested.| The hiker identified the mushroom as an amanita and avoided it carefully.  徒步旅行者认出这种蘑菇是鹅膏菌，小心翼翼地避开了它。 |Synonym: death cap, toadstool, fly agaric | Antonym: edible mushroom | noun
