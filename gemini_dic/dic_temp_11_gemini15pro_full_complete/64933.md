estatement:/ɪˈsteɪtmənt/
n.
电子账单；电子结算单
An electronic version of a statement, typically a bank statement or other financial document.
例句：I prefer to receive my estatements online. 我更喜欢在线接收我的电子账单。

例句：The bank offers estatements to reduce paper usage. 银行提供电子账单以减少纸张使用。

近义词：e-statement, electronic statement, online statement
反义词：paper statement, printed statement
词性：名词
