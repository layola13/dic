deriver's:/dɪˈraɪvərz/| n.|  衍生者；推导者；导出者|Someone who derives something, especially a word or a meaning.  A deriver is one who obtains or receives something from a source.  It often refers to someone who creates a new word or meaning from an existing one.|The deriver's understanding of etymology was impressive.  这位词源推导者的词源学知识令人印象深刻。|近义词：originator, creator, inventor|反义词:imitator, copier|词性:名词
