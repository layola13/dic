courage:/ˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈkʌrɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. |勇气，胆量|The quality of mind or spirit that enables a person to face difficulty, danger, pain, etc., without fear; bravery.|He showed great courage in battle. 他在战斗中表现出极大的勇气。|近义词：bravery, valor, fortitude, boldness|反义词:fear, cowardice, timidity|名词
