词源:
The word "majun" comes from Traditional Chinese Medicine.  "Ma" (马) doesn't have a direct translation in this context but relates to the preparation process or ingredients.  "Jun" (骏) means excellent horse, but in the context of medicine, it refers to a superior or effective remedy. When used as a name, "骏" carries the meaning of strong and outstanding.
