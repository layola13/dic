chubbiest:/ˈtʃʌbiɪst/
adjective
最胖的，最圆乎乎的
Superlative form of chubby: most chubby.  It describes someone (or something) that is round and plump, especially in a cute or endearing way.
例句：He was the chubbiest baby in the nursery. 他是育儿室里最胖的宝宝。

例句：The chubbiest hamster stuffed its cheeks with seeds.  最胖的仓鼠把种子塞满了它的脸颊。

近义词：plumpest, roly-poly, tubbiest
反义词：thinnest, slimmest, skinniest
词性: adjective

chubby:/ˈtʃʌbi/
adjective
胖乎乎的，圆胖的
Pleasantly plump; slightly fat.  It often implies a cute or endearing kind of plumpness.
例句：She has chubby cheeks. 她有胖乎乎的脸颊。

例句：The toddler had chubby little legs.  那个蹒跚学步的孩子有一双胖乎乎的小腿。

近义词：plump, portly, rotund
反义词：thin, slim, skinny
词性: adjective
