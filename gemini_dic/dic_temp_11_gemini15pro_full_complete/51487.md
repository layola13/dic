PHRASE->
detract from something  从…中减损;  The graffiti detracts from the beauty of the building.  涂鸦破坏了建筑物的美观。
nothing detracts from  无损于; His bad temper does nothing to detract from his skill as a surgeon.  他的坏脾气丝毫没有影响他作为外科医生的技术。
detract from the importance of  贬低…的重要性;  The minister was accused of trying to detract from the importance of the report. 部长被指责试图贬低这份报告的重要性。
detract from the value of  降低…的价值; The scratches detract from the value of the antique table.  这些划痕降低了这张古董桌子的价值。
detract from the merits of  贬低…的优点;  The negative publicity does nothing to detract from the merits of her work.  负面宣传丝毫没有贬低她作品的优点。
