euromast:/ˈjʊərəʊmɑːst/ | n. | 欧洲之桅（荷兰鹿特丹一座高塔） | A tall tower in Rotterdam, the Netherlands, used as a television and radio transmission tower and offering panoramic views. |  We visited the Euromast and enjoyed the breathtaking views of Rotterdam. 我们参观了欧洲之桅，欣赏了鹿特丹令人叹为观止的景色。

The Euromast is a popular tourist attraction in Rotterdam. 欧洲之桅是鹿特丹一个受欢迎的旅游景点。 | 近义词: tower, observation tower | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源:  A blend of "Europa" (Europe) and "mast" (a tall upright spar on a ship).
