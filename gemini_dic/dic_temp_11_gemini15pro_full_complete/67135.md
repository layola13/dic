exporters:/ɪkˈspɔːrtərz/
|n.|出口商；出口国|People or companies that sell goods or services to other countries.|The exporters are facing challenges due to the rising shipping costs. 由于运输成本上升，出口商面临挑战。|近义词: sellers, vendors, traders|反义词: importers|词性: Noun

export:/ɪkˈspɔːrt/
|v.|出口|To send goods or services to another country for sale.|The company exports its products to over 50 countries. 这家公司向50多个国家出口产品。|n.|出口; 出口商品|The export of coffee is crucial to the country's economy. 咖啡出口对该国经济至关重要。|近义词: sell abroad, ship overseas|反义词: import|词性: Verb/Noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *exporten*, from Latin *exportāre* (“to carry out, bring out”), from *ex-* (“out of, from”) + *portāre* (“to carry”).
