missing:/ˈmɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 丢失的，缺少的，失踪的 | Absent; not found where expected or thought to be. |  Example: My keys are missing. 我的钥匙不见了。 | Example: The missing person was found safe and sound. 失踪的人安然无恙地被找到了。| Synonyms: absent, lost, lacking, unavailable | Antonyms: present, found, available | adjective

missing:/ˈmɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 缺少的东西，失踪的人 | Something that is absent or lost; a missing person. | Example: The police are still searching for the missing. 警方仍在搜寻失踪者。| Example: There are a few missings from the collection.  这套藏品缺少了几件。| Synonyms: absence, loss, lack | Antonyms: presence, finding | noun
