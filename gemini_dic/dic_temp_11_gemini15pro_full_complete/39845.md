constables:/ˈkɒnstəblz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəblz/ (US)| n.| 治安官，警官|A peace officer with limited policing authority, typically in a small town.|The constables patrolled the village regularly. 治安官定期在村里巡逻。|Synonyms: police officers, law enforcement officers, peacekeepers|Antonyms: criminals, lawbreakers|Noun
