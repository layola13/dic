kakistocracy: /ˌkækɪˈstɒkrəsi/ (US) /ˌkækɪˈstɔkrəsi/ (UK)| n. | 恶人政治；坏人当政；庸人统治 | Government by the worst people. |  The historian warned that the current political climate could lead to kakistocracy. 历史学家警告说，目前的政治气候可能导致恶人政治。|The country descended into kakistocracy after the coup. 政变后，这个国家陷入了坏人当政的局面。| 近义词: misrule, maladministration, kleptocracy | 反义词: meritocracy, aristocracy (in its ideal sense) | 词性: noun


Etymology:

From Greek *kakistos* (worst) + *-kratia* (rule, power).  *Kakistos* is the superlative of *kakos* (bad).
