nontransforming:/ˌnɒntrænsˈfɔːmɪŋ/
adjective
非转化性的，非变形的
Not undergoing transformation or change in form or nature.
The virus was found to be nontransforming, meaning it did not cause cancerous growth in the host cells. 这种病毒被发现是非转化性的，意味着它不会在宿主细胞中引起癌变。
non-oncogenic, non-carcinogenic
transforming, oncogenic, carcinogenic
