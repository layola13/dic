neglect:/nɪˈɡlɛkt/ | v. n. | 忽视，疏忽；忽略；疏于照顾 | Fail to care for properly. | He neglected his studies and failed the exam. 他忽视了学习，考试不及格。| synonyms: disregard, ignore, overlook, abandon | antonyms: attend to, cherish, nurture | verb, noun
