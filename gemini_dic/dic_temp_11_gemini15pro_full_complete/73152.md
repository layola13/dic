foray:/ˈfɒreɪ/ (UK) /ˈfɔːreɪ/ (US)| n.  vi.| 突袭，袭击；涉足，尝试|A sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory, especially to obtain something; a venture or an attempt to do something different.|The troops made a foray into enemy territory. 部队突袭了敌方领土。
