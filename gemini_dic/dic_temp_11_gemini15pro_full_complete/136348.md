nonresistance:/ˌnɑːnriˈzɪstəns/
n.|不抵抗，非暴力抵抗|The policy of nonresistance seemed to encourage further aggression. 不抵抗政策似乎鼓励了进一步的侵略。|Nonresistance is a philosophy that advocates not opposing evil, even through violence. 非暴力抵抗是一种主张不反抗邪恶，即使是通过暴力也不反抗的哲学。|例句：His nonresistance only emboldened the bully. 他的不抵抗只会让恶霸更加胆大妄为。|近义词：passivity, submission, acquiescence|反义词：resistance, defiance, opposition|词性：名词

nonresistant:/ˌnɑːnriˈzɪstənt/
adj.|不抵抗的，非暴力抵抗的|The nonresistant protesters were easily dispersed by the police. 不抵抗的抗议者很容易被警察驱散。|A nonresistant approach to conflict can sometimes be effective. 非对抗性的冲突解决方法有时会很有效。|例句：Their nonresistant attitude made them easy targets. 他们不抵抗的态度使他们成为容易攻击的目标。|近义词：passive, submissive, acquiescent|反义词：resistant, defiant, opposing|词性：形容词
