interpervades:/ˌɪntərpərˈveɪdz/ | v. | 弥漫，遍及，渗透 | to spread through and be noticeable in every part of something |  A feeling of sadness interpervades the film.  悲伤的情绪弥漫着整部电影。| synonyms: permeate, saturate, imbue | antonyms: dissipate, vanish | verb

interpervading:/ˌɪntərpərˈveɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 弥漫的，遍及的，渗透的 | spreading through and affecting every part of something | An interpervading sense of doom hung over the town. 一种弥漫的厄运感笼罩着整个小镇。| synonyms: pervasive, permeating, suffusing | antonyms: localized, limited | adjective
