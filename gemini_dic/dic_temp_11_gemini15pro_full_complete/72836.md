foliolate:/ˈfoʊliəˌleɪt/ /ˈfɒliəˌleɪt/ | adj. | 具小叶的；有叶片的 | Having leaflets; leafy. |  The fern is characterized by its finely foliolate fronds.  这种蕨类植物的特点是其精细的具小叶的叶子。| 近义词: leafy, frondose | 反义词: leafless | 词性: 形容词

foliolate:/ˈfoʊliəˌleɪt/ /ˈfɒliəˌleɪt/ | n. | 具小叶植物 | A plant having leaflets. |  Many foliolates thrive in tropical rainforests. 许多具小叶植物在热带雨林中茁壮成长。| 近义词: compound-leaved plant | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Latin *foliolatus, from folium (“leaf”) + -atus (suffix forming adjectives).
