interceptor:/ˌɪntərˈseptər/ | n. |拦截器；截击机；截击导弹|Something that intercepts, especially a fast aircraft or missile designed to intercept enemy aircraft or missiles.|The fighter jet acted as an interceptor, preventing the enemy bomber from reaching its target.|近义词：blocker, defender, intercepter|反义词：facilitator, enabler|noun
