caduceus: /kəˈdjuːsiəs/ /kəˈduːsiəs/ | n. | 双蛇杖，医学的象征 | A staff with two snakes coiled around it and two wings at the top.  In Greek mythology, it was carried by Hermes; in Roman mythology, by Mercury. It is often incorrectly used as a symbol of medicine.  | The hospital mistakenly used a caduceus on its logo instead of the Rod of Asclepius. 这家医院在其标志上错误地使用了双蛇杖，而不是阿斯克勒庇俄斯之杖。 | 近义词: staff, rod, symbol | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "misrepresentation" if used incorrectly as a medical symbol) | 词性: noun
