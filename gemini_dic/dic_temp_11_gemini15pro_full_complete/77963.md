genteelish:/ˌdʒɛnˈtiːlɪʃ/ | adj. | 装作上流社会的；假斯文的；附庸风雅的 | Appearing or trying to appear genteel but not genuinely so; affectedly refined. | She spoke in a genteelish accent, trying to impress her new neighbors. 她操着一口装腔作势的上流社会口音，试图给她的新邻居留下深刻印象。|  He had a genteelish manner that some found charming, others irritating. 他有一种假斯文的举止，有些人觉得很迷人，有些人觉得很烦人。 | 近义词: affected, pretentious, pseudo-intellectual, snobbish | 反义词: genuine, sincere, unaffected, down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From genteel + -ish.  Genteel itself comes from French gentil, ultimately from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family." The suffix "-ish" indicates a resemblance or slight degree of a quality.
