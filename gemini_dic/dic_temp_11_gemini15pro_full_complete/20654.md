blacksalter:/ˈblækˌsɔːltər/| n.|黑盐商，奸商；（尤指）食盐私贩|A person who illegally trades in salt, especially one who avoids paying taxes on it.  Historically, salt was a heavily taxed commodity, and blacksalters profited by smuggling or illegally producing it.|He was known as a blacksalter, always finding ways to undercut the legal salt merchants. 他被称为黑盐商，总是想方设法压低合法盐商的价格。|Synonyms: smuggler, profiteer, racketeer|Antonyms: honest merchant, law-abiding citizen|noun
