homaging:/ˈhɒmɪdʒɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːmɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
致敬，向…表示敬意
paying tribute to someone or something, often by imitating or referencing their work
The film homages the classic Hollywood musicals of the 1950s.  这部电影致敬了20世纪50年代的经典好莱坞音乐剧。
The director homaged his mentor by using a similar visual style. 这位导演用相似的视觉风格向他的导师致敬。

近义词:  tributing, honoring, saluting, celebrating
反义词:  disrespecting, insulting, mocking, denigrating
词性:  Verb (present participle)


词源:  From homage + -ing. Homage comes from Old French homage, ultimately from Latin homo "man."
