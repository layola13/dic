disblame:/dɪsˈbleɪm/ | v. | 免责；解除…的责备 | To clear from blame; exculpate. | He disblamed her for the accident, stating she wasn't at fault. 他免除了她对事故的责任， stating她没有过错。 | 近义词：exonerate, acquit, absolve | 反义词：blame, accuse, incriminate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From dis- + blame.  The prefix "dis-" negates the action of "blame".
