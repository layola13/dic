ogham:/ˈoʊəm/ |n.|欧甘字母；欧甘文|An ancient alphabet used in Ireland, consisting of a series of strokes and notches cut across a central line. |The ogham inscription on the stone was difficult to decipher.  这块石头上的欧甘铭文很难破译。| 近义词:script, alphabet |反义词: None|noun

oghams:/ˈoʊəmz/ |n.|欧甘字母（复数）|Plural form of "ogham", referring to multiple instances of the Ogham alphabet or inscriptions. |Several oghams were found carved into the ancient stones.  在古代的石头上发现了几个欧甘铭文。| 近义词:inscriptions, scripts, alphabets |反义词: None|noun
