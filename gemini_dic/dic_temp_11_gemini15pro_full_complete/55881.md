divisionary:/dɪˈvɪʒəneri/
adjective
分裂的，制造分歧的，转移注意力的
Causing disagreement or dissension; intended to distract attention from something else.
The government staged a divisionary war to distract from domestic problems. 政府发动了一场转移注意力的战争，以掩盖国内问题。
Synonyms: distracting, diverting, divisive, disruptive
Antonyms: unifying, conciliatory, harmonizing
词性: 形容词
