outgains:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnz/
verb
胜过，超过（在收益或增长方面）
surpasses or exceeds in earnings or growth
Apple outgained Microsoft in the last quarter.  苹果公司上季度的收益超过了微软。
Synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, outperforms, outstrips
Antonyms: loses to, underperforms
词性:v.
