interpenetrative:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪv/
adjective
相互渗透的，互相穿透的
mutually penetrating or pervading each other; extending into or through each other.
The two cultures have become so interpenetrative that it's hard to distinguish between them. 这两种文化已经变得如此相互渗透，以至于很难区分它们。
The artist created an interpenetrative sculpture using light and shadow. 艺术家用光和影创造了一个相互穿透的雕塑。
intertwined, interlaced, intermingled, pervasive
separate, distinct, independent
adjective
