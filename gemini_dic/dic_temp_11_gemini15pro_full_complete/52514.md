differentiates:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪts/| v. |区分，区别，使不同|distinguishes between, makes different|例句：This feature differentiates our product from the competition.  这个特点使我们的产品与竞争对手的产品区别开来。

例句：The company differentiates itself by offering exceptional customer service. 该公司通过提供卓越的客户服务来使其与众不同。|近义词：distinguishes, separates, contrasts, discerns|反义词：conflates, equates, assimilates|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin differentiatus, past participle of differentiare "to make different," from Latin different-, differens, present participle of differre "to set apart, differ," from dis- "apart" + ferre "to carry, bear."
