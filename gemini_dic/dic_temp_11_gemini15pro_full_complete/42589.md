counterpoise:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪz/ /ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪz/ | n. vt.|平衡物，平衡力，抵消；使平衡，使抵消|A counterpoise is a weight used to balance another weight, or a force or influence that balances or offsets another.|The government's spending cuts were a counterpoise to increased taxation. 政府的削减开支与增税相抵消。|近义词：balance, offset, counterbalance, compensate|反义词：imbalance, instability|词性:名词,动词

counterpoise:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪz/ /ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪz/ | vt.|平衡，抵消|To counterpoise something is to balance or offset it with something else.|He counterpoised the weight of the bucket with stones. 他用石头平衡了桶的重量。|近义词：balance, offset, counterbalance, compensate|反义词：imbalance, destabilize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *contrepois* "counterweight, equilibrium," from *contre* "against" (from Latin *contra*) + *pois* "weight" (from Latin *pensum*).
