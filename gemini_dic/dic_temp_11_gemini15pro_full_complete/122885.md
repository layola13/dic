molluscum:/məˈlʌskəm/| n. | molluscum contagiosum; 水痘|A viral skin infection characterized by small, raised, pearl-like bumps.  It is also known as molluscum contagiosum.| Example: The doctor diagnosed the child with molluscum contagiosum.|Synonym:  water warts| Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific disease)| Noun
