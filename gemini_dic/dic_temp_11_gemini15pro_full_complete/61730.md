emotiveness:/ɪˈmoʊtɪvnəs/| n. |情感性，情绪化 |The quality of expressing emotion readily; emotional character.  The state of being readily affected by emotion.| Her emotiveness made her a compelling speaker. 她的情感性使她成为一位引人入胜的演说家。|近义词：emotionality, expressiveness, sensitivity |反义词：stoicism, impassivity |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *emotive* + *-ness*. *Emotive* comes from French *émotif, -ive*, from *émotion* (“emotion”), from Latin *ēmovēre* (“to move out, remove, stir up”), from *ē-* (variant of *ex-*) + *movēre* (“to move”).  *-ness* is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, expressing a state or quality.
