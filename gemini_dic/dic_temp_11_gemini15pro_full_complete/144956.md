overabundances: /ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndənsɪz/
noun
过度丰富，过剩
An excessive amount of something.
The farmer experienced overabundances of zucchini this year. 今年这位农民的西葫芦产量过剩。
synonyms:  excesses, superfluities, surpluses, plethora, glut
antonyms:  scarcity, shortage, deficiency, lack, dearth
n.
