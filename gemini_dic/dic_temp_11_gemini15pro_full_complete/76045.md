furlong:/ˈfɜːrlɒŋ/ (US) /ˈfɜːrlɒŋ/ (UK)| n. |弗隆（长度单位，等于八分之一英里或201.17米）|A furlong is a unit of measurement representing one eighth of a mile or 660 feet (approximately 201.17 meters). It is now primarily used in horse racing. |The horse won the race by a furlong. 这匹马以一弗隆的优势赢得了比赛。

The track is six furlongs long.  这条跑道有六弗隆长。


|近义词：eighth of a mile, 220 yards, 660 feet|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
