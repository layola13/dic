decennovary:/ˌdɛsɛˈnoʊvəri/
n.
十九年期, 十九年的周期
A period of nineteen years.
The Metonic cycle, a period of nineteen years in which the phases of the moon recur on the same dates of the solar year, is a decennovary.  默冬章，一个十九年的周期，其中月相在太阳年的相同日期重复出现，就是一个十九年期。
Ancient astronomers recognized the decennovary as an important cycle for predicting eclipses. 古代天文学家认为十九年期是预测日食的重要周期。
近义词:  Metonic cycle
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially short period)
词性:  noun
