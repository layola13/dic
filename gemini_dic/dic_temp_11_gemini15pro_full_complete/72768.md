fogs:/fɒɡz/ /fɑːɡz/
n.
浓雾，雾气
A thick cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the atmosphere at or near the earth's surface that obscures or restricts visibility.
The heavy fogs made driving conditions treacherous. 浓雾使驾驶条件变得危险。

近义词: mists, haze, smog
反义词: clarity, clearness
词性: 名词(plural of fog)
