nonatrophic:/ˌnɒnəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 非萎缩的 | Not atrophic; without atrophy.  Characterized by the absence of atrophy (decrease in size or wasting away of a body part or tissue). | The biopsy revealed nonatrophic skin, indicating the disease hadn't progressed.  活检显示皮肤没有萎缩，表明疾病没有恶化。| The muscles remained nonatrophic after the rehabilitation program.  康复计划后，肌肉没有萎缩。 | 近义词: healthy, normal, undeveloped | 反义词: atrophic, wasted, shrunken | 词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
"atrophic": from Greek "a-" (without) + "trophikós" (nourishing), related to "trophē" (nourishment).
