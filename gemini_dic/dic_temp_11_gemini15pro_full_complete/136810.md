nonshippers:/ˌnɑːnˈʃɪpərz/ | n. | 非CP粉，不嗑CP的人 | People who do not engage in or approve of "shipping" fictional characters, celebrities, or real people.  Specifically, they do not imagine or support romantic relationships between them. | Example:  The nonshippers were annoyed by all the romantic fan fiction. 非CP粉对所有浪漫的同人小说感到厌烦。

Example:  Some nonshippers just enjoy the story without focusing on romantic pairings.  有些非CP粉只是单纯地享受故事，而不关注浪漫的配对。 | Near Synonyms:  antis, non-fans (in certain contexts) | Antonyms: shippers, fans (in certain contexts) | Noun
