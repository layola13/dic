aberrate:/ˈæbəreɪt/| v. |偏离；脱离常轨；反常|To deviate from the normal or usual course; to stray from the right or proper way.|The comet's orbit aberrated from its predicted path. 这颗彗星的轨道偏离了预定轨道。|The project aberrated from its original goals. 这个项目偏离了最初的目标。|近义词：deviate, stray, digress, diverge|反义词：conform, adhere, comply|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin aberrare "to stray, wander away," from ab "away" (see ab-) + errare "to wander" (see err (v.)).
