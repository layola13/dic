guaiacol:/ˈɡwaɪəkɒl/ (US) /ˈɡwaɪəkɔːl/ (UK)| n.|愈创木酚; 邻甲氧基苯酚|A colorless, oily liquid with a smoky, aromatic odor, extracted from guaiacum or wood creosote, or prepared synthetically. It is used in medicine as an expectorant and antiseptic.|Guaiacol is often used in cough syrups. 愈创木酚常用于止咳糖浆。|Synonyms:  o-methoxyphenol, 2-methoxyphenol|Antonyms: N/A|noun
