overarching:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrtʃɪŋ/| adj.|包罗万象的，支配一切的，总括的| encompassing or overshadowing everything else; dominant| The overarching theme of the conference was climate change. 会议的首要议题是气候变化。| The overarching goal of the project is to improve public health.  该项目的总体目标是改善公众健康。| synonyms: comprehensive, all-embracing, overall, general, umbrella| antonyms: specific, limited, narrow, restricted|词性:形容词

词源: From over + arching (present participle of arch, meaning to form an arch). 字面意思是“在...之上形成拱形”，引申为“覆盖一切”。
