mockadour:/ˈmɒkədɔːr/| n.|模仿者，滑稽模仿者|A person who mocks or mimics someone or something, often in a humorous or satirical way.  A mockadour is similar to a parodist or satirist, but often with a more lighthearted or playful approach.|He was a gifted mockadour, able to perfectly imitate the mannerisms of any politician.|Synonyms: mimic, impersonator, parodist, satirist|Antonyms: admirer, flatterer|noun
