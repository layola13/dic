half-waking:/ˌhɑːf ˈweɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 半醒的，似醒非醒的 | Being in a state between sleep and full wakefulness, partially aroused from sleep. |  I was in a half-waking state when I heard the phone ring. 我听到电话响的时候，正处于半睡半醒的状态。

She lay in a half-waking daze, unsure if she was dreaming. 她躺在那里，半梦半醒，不知道自己是不是在做梦。 | 近义词：semi-conscious, drowsy, half-asleep | 反义词：wide-awake, alert, fully conscious | 词性: adjective
