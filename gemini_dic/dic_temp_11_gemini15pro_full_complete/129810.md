non-gradable:/ˌnɒnˈɡreɪdəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|不可分级的，绝对的|An adjective that cannot be used with adverbs like "very" or "extremely" because it describes a quality that is absolute.  It represents a state or quality that is either present or absent, not something that can exist to varying degrees.|例句：The room was freezing. (Not *very freezing*). 房间很冷。（而不是*非常冷*).

例句：The answer is unique. (Not *extremely unique*). 答案是独一无二的。（而不是*极其独一无二*).|近义词：absolute, categorical|反义词：gradable|词性:adjective
