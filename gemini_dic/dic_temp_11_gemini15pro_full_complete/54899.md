disporting:/dɪˈspɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
嬉戏; 玩耍;  娱乐
Engaging in playful activity; amusing oneself; frolicking.
例句：The children were disporting themselves in the park. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏。
例句：The dolphins were disporting themselves in the waves. 海豚在海浪中嬉戏玩耍。
近义词：playing, frolicking, gamboling, cavorting, sporting
反义词：working, toiling, laboring
