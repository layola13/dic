arraignments:/əˈreɪnmənts/| n. |提审，传讯；指控，控告|The act of arraigning someone; a formal accusation of wrongdoing.  A calling into question or a finding fault, especially with respect to conduct.|The arraignments of the suspects were held in a closed courtroom. 嫌疑人的提审是在一个封闭的法庭进行的。|synonyms:accusations, charges, indictments, prosecutions|antonyms:acquittals, defenses, exonerations|noun
