grevy:/ˈɡreɪvi/| adj. n.|格雷维的，格雷维斑马|Referring to a species of zebra (Equus grevyi) characterized by its narrow stripes, large ears, and white belly.  It can also be used as a noun to refer to the zebra itself.|Example: The Grevy's zebra is the largest living wild equid. 格雷维斑马是现存最大的野生马科动物。

Example:  We saw a herd of grevy grazing in the grasslands. 我们看到一群格雷维斑马在草原上吃草。|近义词：imperial zebra (sometimes used, though not a precise synonym)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
