dog-paddle:/ˈdɒɡˌpædl/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡˌpædl/ (US)| n. vi.|狗刨式游泳|A swimming stroke resembling the paddling of a dog, characterized by short, quick movements of the hands and feet.|He swam across the pool using a dog-paddle. 他用狗刨式游过了泳池。|近义词: doggy paddle|反义词: crawl, breaststroke|词性:名词, 动词
