anamorphosis:/ˌænəmɔːrˈfoʊsɪs/| n. |变形，失真，歪像；变形术|A distorted projection or drawing that appears normal when viewed from a particular point or with a suitable mirror or lens.  A distorted image that appears in natural form when viewed in some unconventional way.|The artist created an anamorphosis of a skull on the floor.  这位艺术家在地板上创作了一幅骷髅的变形画。|Synonyms: distortion, deformation, warping|Antonyms:  normalization, regularization|noun

anamorphosis:/ˌænəmɔːrˈfoʊsɪs/| n. |变形，失真，歪像；(生物)变形，变态|A change in the form or structure of an organism, typically as part of its normal development.|The anamorphosis of the caterpillar into a butterfly is a fascinating process. 毛毛虫蜕变成蝴蝶是一个迷人的过程。|Synonyms: metamorphosis, transformation, transmutation|Antonyms:  stagnation,  invariance|noun
