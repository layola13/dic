associational:/əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənl/
|adj.|联想的，关联的|Relating to or based on association; connected in the mind or memory.|例句：The brain forms associational links between smells and memories. 大脑在气味和记忆之间形成联想联系。|例句：Associational learning is a powerful tool in education. 联想学习是教育中的一个强大工具。|近义词：connected, related, linked, correlated|反义词：unrelated, disconnected, independent|词性:形容词
|词源: From Late Latin *associationem*, from Latin *associare* "to join with," from *ad* "to" + *socius* "companion."|
