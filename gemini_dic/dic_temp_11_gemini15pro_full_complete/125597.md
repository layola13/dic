multivagant: /ˌmʌltɪˈveɪɡənt/
adjective
多方向游荡的；漫游的；漂泊不定的
Wandering or straying in many different directions or areas.
He led a multivagant life, traveling from country to country. 他过着漂泊不定的生活，从一个国家旅行到另一个国家。
Synonyms: nomadic, wandering, roving, itinerant, peripatetic
Antonyms: settled, sedentary, stationary
词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *multivagans, from multi- "many" + vagans, present participle of vagari "to wander".
