foraged:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒd/| v. adj.|觅食的，搜寻的|searched for food or provisions|例句：They foraged for berries in the woods. 他们在树林里寻找浆果。|近义词：searched, hunted, gathered, rummaged|反义词:planted, cultivated|词性:动词过去式/过去分词, 形容词

Etymology: From Old French *fourrager "to forage," from fourrage "fodder," from Old French feurre "straw" (see fodder).
