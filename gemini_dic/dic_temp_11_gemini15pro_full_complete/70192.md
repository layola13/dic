fibrinoplastic:/ˌfaɪbrɪnoʊˈplæstɪk/| adj.|促纤维蛋白形成的，纤维蛋白塑造的|Relating to or promoting the formation of fibrin, a protein involved in blood clotting.|Fibrinoplastic agents are crucial for wound healing. 促纤维蛋白形成剂对伤口愈合至关重要。|The surgeon used a fibrinoplastic substance to control the bleeding.  外科医生使用了一种促纤维蛋白物质来控制出血。|近义词：coagulant, hemostatic|反义词：anticoagulant, fibrinolytic|词性：形容词

fibrinoplastin:/ˌfaɪbrɪnoʊˈplæstɪn/ | n. |血纤蛋白原|A former name for fibrinogen, a soluble protein present in blood plasma that is converted to fibrin by the action of thrombin in the latter stages of blood coagulation.|Fibrinoplastin is essential for blood clotting. 血纤蛋白原对血液凝固至关重要.|Historically, fibrinoplastin was used to describe the substance now known as fibrinogen.  历史上，血纤蛋白原被用来描述现在被称为纤维蛋白原的物质。|近义词：fibrinogen|反义词：None|词性：名词
