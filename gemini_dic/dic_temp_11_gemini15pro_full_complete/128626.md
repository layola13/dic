nickeliferous:/ˌnɪkəˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
含镍的；产镍的
Containing nickel; yielding nickel.
The nickeliferous ore was mined and processed. 这种含镍的矿石被开采和加工。
The Sudbury Basin is known for its nickeliferous deposits. 萨德伯里盆地以其镍矿床而闻名。
nickel-bearing, nickel-rich
N/A
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *niccolum* (nickel) + -ferous (bearing).  *-ferous* comes from Latin *ferre* (to bear, carry).
