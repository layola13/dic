knapsacking: /ˈnæpsækɪŋ/ | n. | 背包问题，背包算法 | The act or process of packing a knapsack, especially in such a way as to maximize the value of the items carried, given a weight limit.  It often refers to the "knapsack problem" in computer science, a combinatorial optimization problem. | He studied different algorithms for knapsacking to optimize his hiking gear. 他研究了不同的背包算法，以优化他的徒步旅行装备。 | synonyms: packing, optimizing (in the context of the knapsack problem) | antonyms: unpacking, emptying | noun
