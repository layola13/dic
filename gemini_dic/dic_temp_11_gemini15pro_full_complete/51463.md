detonator:/ˈdɛtəneɪtər/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪtə/ (UK)| n. |引爆器，雷管|A device used to set off an explosive charge, such as dynamite.|The detonator was carefully inserted into the explosive.  雷管被小心地插入炸药中。|Synonyms: fuse, trigger, primer|Antonyms:  N/A (Detonators initiate explosions; there isn't a direct antonym.)|noun

detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (UK)| v. |引爆，爆炸|Explode or cause to explode.|The bomb detonated at precisely 6:00 am. 炸弹在早上六点整爆炸。|Synonyms: explode, blow up, go off|Antonyms: defuse, disarm|verb
