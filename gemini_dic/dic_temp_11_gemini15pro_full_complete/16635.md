baroqueness:/bəˈroʊknəs/
n.|巴洛克风格；华丽的装饰风格|The baroqueness of the music was overwhelming.  这音乐的巴洛克风格令人震撼。|Elaborateness, ornateness, extravagance|Simplicity, minimalism|noun
baroqueness:/bəˈroʊknəs/
n.|巴洛克式；复杂精美的风格|The architecture of the palace displayed a distinct baroqueness. 这座宫殿的建筑展现出独特的巴洛克风格。|Complexity, intricacy, flamboyance|Plainness, austerity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *baroque* (irregularly shaped pearl; whimsical, uneven) -> Italian *barocco* (bizarre, whimsical) ->  Possibly from Portuguese *barroco* (irregular pearl) -> Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly pre-Romance.  The term was initially used derogatively to describe the overly ornate style of art and architecture following the Renaissance, later becoming a neutral term.
