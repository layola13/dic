mamluk:/ˈmæmlʊk/| n.|马穆鲁克，奴隶兵|A soldier of slave origin, especially a member of one of the military castes which ruled parts of the Islamic world during the Middle Ages.|The Mamluks of Egypt were a powerful force in the region. 埃及的马穆鲁克是该地区的一支强大力量。|Synonyms: slave soldier, warrior, mercenary|Antonyms: freeman, citizen|Noun
