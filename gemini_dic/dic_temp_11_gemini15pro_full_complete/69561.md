fellowships:/ˈfeləʊʃɪps/ (UK) /ˈfɛloʊʃɪps/ (US)| n.|奖学金；研究员职位；会员资格；友谊；团体|A fellowship is a sum of money granted to a graduate student for advanced study or research.  A fellowship can also refer to a position as a member of a learned society. It can also mean companionship and camaraderie.|
She won a prestigious fellowship to study abroad. 她获得了出国留学的著名奖学金。

The fellowship provided him with a stipend and research facilities. 研究员职位为他提供了津贴和研究设施。

He valued the fellowship of his colleagues. 他珍视与同事之间的友谊。

The fellowship of writers met monthly. 作家团体每月聚会一次。

近义词:scholarship, grant, position, membership, companionship, camaraderie, association, society
反义词:isolation, loneliness, animosity

词性:noun
