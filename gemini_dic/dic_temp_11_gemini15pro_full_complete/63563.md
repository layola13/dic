envier:/ˈɛnviər/| n.|羡慕者, 嫉妒者|A person who envies someone or something. |She was an envier of her sister's success. 她嫉妒她姐姐的成功。|近义词: jealous person, covetous person |反义词: admirer |词性: 名词

envy:/ˈɛnvi/| n. vt.|羡慕, 嫉妒; 引起羡慕|n. A feeling of discontented or resentful longing aroused by someone else's possessions, qualities, or luck. vt. Desire to have a quality, possession, or other desirable attribute belonging to (someone else).|I envy her beauty. 我羡慕她的美貌。  He envied his friend's new car. 他嫉妒他朋友的新车。|近义词: jealousy, covetousness, resentment |反义词: admiration, generosity |词性: 名词, 动词
