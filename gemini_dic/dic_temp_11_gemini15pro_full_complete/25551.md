bulse: /bʌls/
n.
a swelling or protuberance; a blister or boil
A bulse formed on his heel after the long hike.
长途徒步旅行后，他的脚后跟起了个水泡。
The doctor lanced the infected bulse to drain the pus.
医生切开了感染的水泡以排出脓液。

近义词: blister, boil, swelling, lump
反义词: indentation, depression

词性: noun

词源:  From Middle English *bulse, from Old English *bylse, from Proto-Germanic *bulsō (“bubble, blister”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewl- (“to swell, blow”). Cognate with Scots bulse (“blister, boil”), West Frisian bulte (“boil, blister”), Dutch buil (“boil, swelling”), German Beule (“bump, dent”), Swedish bulnad (“swelling”).
