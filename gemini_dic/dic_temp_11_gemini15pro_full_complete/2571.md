admitting:/ədˈmɪtɪŋ/
verb
承认；允许进入；容纳
The act of confessing or acknowledging something. Also, the process of allowing entry.
He admitted breaking the window. 他承认打破了窗户。
The museum is admitting visitors for free today. 博物馆今天免费开放。
synonyms: confessing, acknowledging, conceding, granting entry, letting in
antonyms: denying, refusing, barring, excluding
词性: v.
