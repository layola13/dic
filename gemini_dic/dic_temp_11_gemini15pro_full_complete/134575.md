nonlogicality:/ˌnɒnˈlɒdʒɪˈkælɪti/
noun
非逻辑性；不合逻辑性
The state or quality of being nonlogical; lack of logic or logical connection.
His argument was riddled with nonlogicality, making it difficult to follow. 他的论证充满了非逻辑性，让人难以理解。
illogicality, irrationality, unreasonableness
logicality, rationality, reasonableness
