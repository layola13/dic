gazetting:/ɡəˈzɛtɪŋ/ | n. | 公告；刊登；任命 | The act of publishing or announcing something officially, often in a government gazette, also the act of appointing someone to a position officially. | The gazetting of the new law will take place next week.  新法律的公告将于下周进行。| synonyms: announcement, publication, proclamation, appointment | antonyms: concealment, suppression | noun

gazetted:/ɡəˈzɛtɪd/ | adj. | 被公告的；被刊登的；被任命的 | Officially published or announced, typically in a government gazette.  | The gazetted list of approved vendors is available online.  已批准的供应商的公告清单可在网上查阅。| synonyms: announced, published, proclaimed, appointed | antonyms: unannounced, unpublished, unproclaimed, unappointed | adjective
