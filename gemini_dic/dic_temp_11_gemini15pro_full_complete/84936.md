hanu:/ˈhɑːnuː/| n.|哈努人，哈努语|A member of a Sino-Tibetan people inhabiting southern China and northern Vietnam and Laos; the language of the Hanu people, belonging to the Tibeto-Burman family.|The Hanu people live in isolated villages. 哈努人居住在偏僻的村庄里。|Hanu is a tonal language. 哈努语是一种声调语言。|synonyms:  (for the people)  ethnic minority, hill tribe  (for the language)  Tibeto-Burman language|antonyms: N/A|noun
