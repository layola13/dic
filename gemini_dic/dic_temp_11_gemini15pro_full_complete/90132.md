hotbed:/ˈhɑːtbɛd/ | n. |温床；滋生地；热烈活动中心|A place or situation that favors rapid growth or development of something, especially something unwelcome.|Silicon Valley is a hotbed of innovation. 硅谷是创新的温床。|The internet has become a hotbed of conspiracy theories. 互联网已成为阴谋论的滋生地。| synonyms: breeding ground, seedbed, center, hub | antonyms:  suppressor, deterrent | noun
