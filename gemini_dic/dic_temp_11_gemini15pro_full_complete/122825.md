molehilly:/ˈmoʊlˌhɪli/| adj.|多鼹鼠丘的，鼹鼠丘遍布的|Covered with molehills; abounding in molehills.|The field was molehilly and uneven, making it difficult to walk across. 这片田地鼹鼠丘遍布，高低不平，很难穿过。|The pasture was so molehilly that it was impossible to play football. 这片牧场鼹鼠丘太多了，根本无法踢足球。|近义词：lumpy, bumpy, uneven|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mole + hill + -y.  "Mole" refers to the small burrowing mammal, and "hill" refers to the mounds of earth they create. The suffix "-y" transforms the noun phrase into an adjective.
