goldfish:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪʃ/| n. |金鱼|A small, freshwater fish, typically orange or red, popular as a pet. It is a member of the carp family.|例句：I won a goldfish at the fair. 我在集市上赢了一条金鱼。

例句：My goldfish swims around its tank all day. 我的金鱼整天都在鱼缸里游来游去。

近义词：carp (鲤鱼, 广义上的)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

goldfish:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪʃ/| adj. |金鱼（形容词，较少用）|Relating to or resembling a goldfish.|例句：She wore a goldfish-orange dress. 她穿了一件金鱼橙色的连衣裙。

例句：He has goldfish-like memory. 他的记忆力像金鱼一样差。(比喻用法，指记忆力差)

近义词：N/A|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
