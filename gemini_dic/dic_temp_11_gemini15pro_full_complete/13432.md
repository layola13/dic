atones:/əˈtoʊnz/| v.|赎罪，弥补|makes amends or reparation for (an offense or injury).|例句：He atoned for his sins by giving to charity. 他通过慈善捐款来赎罪。

例句：She atoned for her mistake by working extra hours. 她加班加点来弥补自己的错误。|近义词：expiates, makes amends, compensates, redeems|反义词：worsens, aggravates|词性:动词
