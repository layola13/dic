ambitionless:/æmˈbɪʃən.ləs/
adjective
没有雄心的；缺乏抱负的
Lacking ambition; not ambitious.
He was a quiet, ambitionless man who seemed content with his simple life. 他是一个安静、没有雄心的人，似乎对简朴的生活感到满足。
She's completely ambitionless - she just wants to stay home and watch TV. 她完全没有雄心壮志——只想待在家里看电视。
近义词:unambitious, unaspiring, apathetic
反义词:ambitious, aspiring, driven
词性:adjective
