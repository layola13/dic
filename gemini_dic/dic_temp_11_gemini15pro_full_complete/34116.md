clacked:/klækt/| v.|发出咔哒声|Made a sharp, short sound like hard objects hitting together.|The keys clacked as she typed furiously. 她快速打字时，键盘发出咔哒咔哒的声音。|近义词：clicked, clattered|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "silent," "muffled")|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clacken*, of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch *klakken*, German *klaken*, *klacken*.
