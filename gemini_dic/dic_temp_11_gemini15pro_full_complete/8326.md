anti-patriotism: /ˌæntiˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/ | n. | 反爱国主义；不爱国 | Lack of patriotism; opposition to patriotism.  The sentiment of not loving or supporting one's country. |  His anti-patriotism was evident in his constant criticism of the government. 他的反爱国主义体现在他对政府的不断批评。|  She was accused of anti-patriotism for protesting the war.  她因为抗议战争而被指责为不爱国。| 近义词: disloyalty, sedition, treason | 反义词: patriotism, nationalism, loyalty | 词性: 名词
