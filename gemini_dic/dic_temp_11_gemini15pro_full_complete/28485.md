carcinogen:/ˌkɑːrˈsɪnədʒən/ | n. |致癌物|A substance capable of causing cancer in living tissue.|例句：Exposure to certain carcinogens, such as asbestos, can significantly increase the risk of lung cancer.  接触某些致癌物，例如石棉，会显著增加患肺癌的风险。

例句：Many carcinogens are present in cigarette smoke. 许多致癌物存在于香烟烟雾中。|近义词：mutagen, cancer-causing agent|反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可用描述性词组例如：cancer-preventing agent) |词性: noun
