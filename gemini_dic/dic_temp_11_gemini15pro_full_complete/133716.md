nonfrozen:/ˌnɒnˈfroʊzn/ | adj. | 未冷冻的，非冷冻的 | Not frozen; in a liquid or unsolidified state. | The nonfrozen ground made it easy to dig. 未冻的地面很容易挖。|  The grocery store had a selection of nonfrozen concentrated juices.  这家杂货店有各种各样的未冷冻浓缩果汁。|近义词: unfrozen, thawed, liquid | 反义词: frozen, solid | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, from Old Latin noenum "not".  Indicates negation or absence.
frozen (adjective):  Past participle of freeze. From Old English *freosan, from Proto-Germanic *freusaną.
