deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/| vt.|耗尽，减少，使枯竭|To reduce something in size or amount, especially supplies of energy, money, etc.|The long drought has severely depleted the region's water resources. 长期的干旱严重耗尽了该地区的水资源。|synonyms: exhaust, drain, consume, use up, reduce, diminish, lessen, decrease|antonyms: augment, increase, replenish, refill|verb

depletion:/dɪˈpliːʃən/| n.|耗尽，减少，枯竭|The reduction of something by a large amount so that there is not enough left.|The depletion of the ozone layer is a serious environmental concern. 臭氧层的损耗是一个严重的环境问题。|synonyms: exhaustion, draining, consumption, reduction, diminution|antonyms: augmentation, increase, replenishment|noun
