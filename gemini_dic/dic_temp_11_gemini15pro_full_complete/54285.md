disguises:/dɪsˈɡaɪzɪz/
|n.|伪装，掩饰|Something that hides the usual appearance of something or someone, making it difficult to recognize. |He wore a disguise to avoid being recognized. 他乔装打扮以免被人认出来。|synonyms: camouflage, mask, cloak, cover, facade|antonyms: revelation, exposure|noun
disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/
|vt.|伪装，掩饰|To give a different appearance to something or someone, especially in order to hide its true form or identity. |She disguised her voice on the phone. 她在电话里伪装了她的声音。|synonyms: conceal, mask, hide, cloak, obscure|antonyms: reveal, expose, unmask|verb
