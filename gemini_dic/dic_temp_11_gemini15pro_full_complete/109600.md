letheonize: /ləˈθiːənaɪz/
verb
使麻木；使迟钝；使忘却
to cause someone to forget something unpleasant
例句：The drug letheonized the patient's pain.  这种药物使病人忘记了疼痛。

例句：The trauma of the accident letheonized her memory of the event. 事故的创伤让她忘记了这件事的记忆。
近义词：numb, dull, obliterate, erase
反义词：sensitize, sharpen, remember
词性：v.
