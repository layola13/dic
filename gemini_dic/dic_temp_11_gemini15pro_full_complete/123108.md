mongrelize: /ˈmʌŋɡrəˌlaɪz/ /ˈmɒŋɡrəˌlaɪz/
|v. |使杂交；使混杂；降低…的纯洁性|To cause something to become mixed or impure, especially by interbreeding different breeds or mixing different styles or elements.|The architect was criticized for mongrelizing the traditional design with modern elements.  这位建筑师因将传统设计与现代元素混杂而受到批评。|
| |They accused the company of mongrelizing the local culture with Western influences. 他们指责该公司用西方文化影响使当地文化变得不纯。|Synonym: hybridize, crossbreed, intermix, adulterate, corrupt|Antonym: purify, refine, homogenize|Verb|

mongrelization: /ˌmʌŋɡrələˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌmɒŋɡrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|杂交；混杂；降低纯洁性|The act or process of mongrelizing; the state of being mongrelized.|The mongrelization of the breed led to a decline in its overall health.  该品种的杂交导致其整体健康状况下降。|
| |The critic lamented the mongrelization of modern art.  评论家哀叹现代艺术的混杂。|Synonym: hybridization, crossbreeding, intermixture, adulteration, corruption|Antonym: purification, refinement|Noun|
