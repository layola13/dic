comfortlessly:/ˈkʌmfətləsli/
adverb|无慰藉地，不舒服地|Without comfort; in a way that provides no comfort or consolation.|She sat comfortlessly on the hard chair, worrying about her future. 她不安地坐在硬椅子上，担心着自己的未来。|例句：He slept comfortlessly on the floor. 他在地板上睡得很不舒服。|近义词：uncomfortably, miserably, wretchedly|反义词：comfortably, cozily, snugly|词性：副词
词源：comfort (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ly (suffix). From Middle English comforten, from Old French conforter, from Late Latin confortare "to strengthen much," from Latin com- "with" + fortis "strong."
