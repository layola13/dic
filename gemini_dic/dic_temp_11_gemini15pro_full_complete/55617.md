diswarn:/dɪsˈwɔːrn/
verb
取消警告，撤回警告
To retract or cancel a warning or caution previously given.
The general diswarned the troops about the impending attack, as new intelligence suggested it was a false alarm. 将军取消了对部队即将到来的袭击的警告，因为新的情报表明这是一个错误的警报。
The meteorologist diswarned the public about the hurricane, stating it had changed course. 气象学家撤回了对公众关于飓风的警告， stating it had changed course.
近义词: retract, countermand, revoke, rescind
反义词: warn, caution, forewarn
词性: verb
