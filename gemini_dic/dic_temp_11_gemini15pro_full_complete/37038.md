comanche:/kəˈmæntʃiː/ /kəˈmæntʃi/
noun|科曼奇人；科曼奇语|a member of a North American Indian people formerly inhabiting western Texas and adjacent parts of Oklahoma, New Mexico, and Colorado.  Also, the Uto-Aztecan language of this people.|The Comanche were renowned horsemen.|近义词：Shoshone, Ute (related tribes)|反义词: N/A|词性:n.
comanche:/kəˈmæntʃiː/ /kəˈmæntʃi/
adjective|科曼奇人的；科曼奇语的|relating to the Comanche people or their language.|He spoke fluent Comanche.|近义词：N/A|反义词: N/A|词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *Comanche*, from Ute *kɨmantʃi* (“enemy”).
