bedung: /bɪˈdʌŋ/
adjective
covered in dung
Covered or smeared with manure.
The farmer's boots were bedung after mucking out the stalls. 农民的靴子在清理畜栏后沾满了粪便。
近义词: dung-covered, besmirched, fouled
反义词: clean, pristine
词性: adjective
