expiable:/ˈɛkspiəbl/
adjective
可赎的，可补偿的
capable of being expiated or atoned for.
His sins are expiable through prayer and good works. 他的罪孽可以通过祈祷和善行来赎清。

近义词:atonable, redeemable, forgivable
反义词: inexpiable, unpardonable

词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin expiabilis, from expiare "to atone for," from ex- "out" + piare "to appease, purify," from pius "dutiful, devout."
