nonderelict:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɛlɪkt/ | adj. | 非荒废的，维护良好的 | Not derelict; maintained in good condition. | The nonderelict buildings in the historic district attracted many tourists. 历史街区维护良好的建筑吸引了许多游客。 |  The park was nonderelict, with well-tended gardens and freshly painted benches.  公园维护得很好，花园整洁，长椅也新刷了油漆。| 近义词：well-maintained, preserved, sound | 反义词：derelict, dilapidated, rundown | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + derelict.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "derelict" means abandoned or neglected.
