nonhunter:/ˌnɒnˈhʌntər/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈhʌntər/ (US)| n.|非猎人;不打猎的人|A person who does not hunt. |Example:  Many nonhunters enjoy observing wildlife in their natural habitat. 许多不打猎的人喜欢观察野生动物在其自然栖息地。

Example: The park is a popular destination for both hunters and nonhunters. 这个公园是猎人和非猎人共同的热门目的地。|近义词:non-sportsman, anti-hunter|反义词:hunter, sportsman|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix) + hunter (noun)

"non-" comes from Latin "non," meaning "not."
"hunter" comes from Old English "huntere," from "huntian," meaning "to hunt."
