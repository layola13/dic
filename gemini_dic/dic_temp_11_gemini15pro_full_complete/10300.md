apparent-magnitude:/əˈpærənt ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/ | n. |视星等| The measure of the brightness of a celestial object (such as a star or planet) as seen by an observer on Earth. |  The apparent magnitude of Sirius is -1.46.  天狼星的视星等是-1.46。| synonyms: visual magnitude, apparent brightness | antonyms: absolute magnitude | noun

apparent:/əˈpærənt/ | adj. |明显的，表面上的|  Seeming real or true, but not necessarily so. |  His apparent calmness hid his true feelings. 他表面上的平静掩盖了他真实的感受。| synonyms: obvious, evident, seeming | antonyms: hidden, real, actual | adjective

magnitude:/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/ | n. |大小，量级，震级|  The great size or extent of something. | The magnitude of the earthquake was 7.0 on the Richter scale. 这次地震的震级是里氏7.0级。 | synonyms: size, extent, scale, degree | antonyms: smallness, insignificance | noun
