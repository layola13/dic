invoking:/ɪnˈvoʊkɪŋ/| v.|调用，援引，祈求|The ritual involves invoking the spirits of their ancestors.  这个仪式包括祈求他们祖先的灵魂。|例句：He invoked the power of the law to protect himself. 他援引法律的力量来保护自己。

例句：She invoked the gods to help her. 她祈求神灵帮助她。

近义词：calling upon, appealing to, citing, summoning|反义词:dismissing, ignoring|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *invocare, from Latin in- (“in, on, upon”) + vocare (“to call”).
