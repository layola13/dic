cephalology:/ˌsefəˈlɒlədʒi/
n.
头部学；颅骨学
The study of the head, particularly its shape and size.
Cephalology is a branch of anthropology that studies the measurements of the human skull. 头部学是人类学的一个分支，研究人类头骨的测量。
The museum's exhibit on cephalology showcased skulls from different eras and cultures.  博物馆关于头部学的展览展示了来自不同时代和文化的头骨。
近义词:craniology
反义词:None
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek kephalē (head) + -logy (study of).
