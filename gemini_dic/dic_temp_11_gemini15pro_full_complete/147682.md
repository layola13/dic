overpowering:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋ/| adj.|压倒性的，势不可挡的，极强的|Having such force or intensity as to defeat or subdue completely; irresistible.|The heat was overpowering, and I had to step inside.  高温势不可挡，我不得不走进去。|The stench of the garbage was overpowering. 垃圾的恶臭令人窒息。|近义词： overwhelming, dominant, compelling, intense, strong|反义词： weak, mild, subtle|词性:形容词

overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/| vt.|制服，压倒，征服|To defeat or subdue by superior force.|The police overpowered the suspect. 警察制服了嫌疑犯。|Our team overpowered their defense and won the game. 我们的队伍战胜了他们的防守，赢得了比赛。|近义词： conquer, subdue, vanquish, defeat, overwhelm|反义词： surrender, yield, submit|词性:动词
