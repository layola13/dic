longnose:/ˈlɒŋnəʊz/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋnoʊz/ (US)| n. adj.|长鼻子,长鼻子的|A person or animal with a long nose.  It can also be used adjectivally to describe something having a long nose.|例句：The longnose gar is a primitive fish. 长吻雀鳝是一种原始鱼类。

例句：He's got a bit of a longnose. 他有点长鼻子。|近义词：long-nosed, beak, snout|反义词:short-nosed, snub-nosed|词性: noun, adjective
