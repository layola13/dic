hyke:/haɪk/ | n. v. |远足，徒步旅行；大幅提高（价格等）；拉起，提起|A long walk or journey, especially in the countryside; a sharp increase, especially in price; to pull or lift something sharply upwards. |例句：We went on a hyke in the mountains. 我们去山上远足了。|例句：The company announced a hyke in prices. 这家公司宣布提价。|近义词：hike, trek, tramp, raise, boost, increase |反义词：decrease, lower, decline|词性:名词,动词

词源:  From earlier hike (v.), of uncertain origin, possibly from dialectal hick "to hop, limp," related to hitch.  The noun is from the verb.
