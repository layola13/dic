frizzy:/ˈfrɪzi/| adj.|卷曲的，毛躁的|Characterized by or having small, tight curls that are dry and unruly.  Often used to describe hair.|例句：Her hair gets frizzy in humid weather. 她的头发在潮湿的天气里会变得毛躁。

例句：The humidity made my hair frizzy. 湿度让我的头发毛躁。|近义词：curly, kinky, unruly, fuzzy|反义词：straight, smooth, sleek|词性:形容词

词源: From frizz (v.) + -y (adjective suffix). Frizz itself is likely of imitative origin, suggesting the sound or appearance of something crinkling or crackling.
