incantators:/ɪnˈkænteɪtərz/| n.|念咒者，施法者|A person who incants; someone who uses spells or charms.|The incantators chanted ancient words, hoping to summon the rain. 念咒者吟唱着古老的咒语，希望能召唤来雨水。|Synonyms: spellcasters, magicians, sorcerers, wizards|Antonyms: None (lack of a single direct antonym; could consider "mundanes" or "ordinary people")|Noun
