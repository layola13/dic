destructibleness:/dɪˌstrʌktɪblˈnɛs/
n.
破坏性，可破坏性
The quality or state of being destructible; destructibility.
The destructibleness of the building was evident after the earthquake.  这栋建筑在地震后的破坏性显而易见。
The archaeologist lamented the destructibleness of ancient artifacts due to looting. 考古学家对古代文物因抢劫而遭到破坏感到惋惜。
destructibility, fragility, vulnerability
indestructibility, durability, permanence
noun
