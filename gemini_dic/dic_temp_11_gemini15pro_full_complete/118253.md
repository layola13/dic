merchandiser:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzər/ (US), /ˈmɜːtʃəndaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 商人；商品推销员；采购员|A person or company involved in buying and selling goods, especially one who deals in a particular commodity.  A person whose job is to buy and sell goods for a shop or company, or to help a shop sell goods by deciding how they should be displayed, advertised, etc.| The merchandiser decided to promote the new line of clothing with a social media campaign.  该采购员决定通过社交媒体宣传推广新的服装系列。| synonyms: retailer, trader, dealer, vendor, seller, salesman, saleswoman, salesperson, distributor | antonyms: buyer, purchaser, consumer, customer | noun
