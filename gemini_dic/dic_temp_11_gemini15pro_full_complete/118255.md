merchandises:/ˈmɜːrtʃənˌdaɪzɪz/
|n.| 商品；货物 | goods that are bought and sold | The store merchandises its products effectively to attract customers.  这家商店有效地推销其产品以吸引顾客。| synonyms: goods, wares, commodities, stock, products | antonyms: services | noun


merchandise:/ˈmɜːrtʃənˌdaɪz/
|v.| 推销；销售 | to promote the sale of (goods), especially by their presentation in retail outlets | The company merchandised the new toy line with television commercials and in-store displays.  该公司通过电视广告和店内展示来推销新的玩具系列。| synonyms: promote, market, sell, retail | antonyms: buy, purchase | verb


Etymology:
From Old French *marchandise "trade, commerce; merchandise," from marchand "merchant," from Late Latin mercatans, present participle of mercatare "to trade, traffic," frequentative of mercari "to buy, trade."
