foetation:/fiːˈteɪʃən/ /fiːˈteɪʃn/

the process of carrying a fetus or developing young in the womb; pregnancy; gestation
The mare's foetation period is approximately eleven months. 母马的妊娠期大约是11个月。
pregnancy, gestation, gravidity
parturition, birth
