breathily:/ˈbreθɪli/ | adv. | 气喘吁吁地，呼吸急促地 | In a way that involves breathing quickly and noticeably. | She spoke breathily, clearly exhausted from the run. 她气喘吁吁地说，显然是跑步累坏了。|例句：He whispered breathily in her ear. 他在她耳边气喘吁吁地低语。| 近义词：gaspingly, pantingly | 反义词: calmly, quietly | 词性: 副词

词源: From breath + -ly.  "Breath" comes from Old English "bræth."
