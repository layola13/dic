headshot:/ˈhɛdʃɒt/ (US) /ˈhedʃɒt/ (UK)| n.|爆头，头像|A photograph of a person's head, typically taken from the shoulders up, often used for professional profiles, acting portfolios, or other identification purposes. |例句：She updated her LinkedIn profile with a new professional headshot. 她用一张新的专业头像更新了她的领英个人资料。

例句：The actor's headshot was included with his resume.  这位演员的简历中附有他的头像照片。|近义词：portrait, profile picture, mugshot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
