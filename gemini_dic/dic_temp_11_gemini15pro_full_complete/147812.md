overproving:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvɪŋ/ | n. |过度发酵|Allowing dough to prove for too long, resulting in a collapsed or dense texture.| The bread didn't rise properly because of overproving.  面包没有发起来是因为过度发酵了。|Synonyms: over-fermentation, over-rising| Antonyms: underproving, under-fermentation | noun
