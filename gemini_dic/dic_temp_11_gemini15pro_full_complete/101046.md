inveigle:/ɪnˈveɪɡəl/
verb
诱骗，哄骗
persuade (someone) to do something by means of deception or flattery.
He inveigled her into coming on the trip. 他哄骗她一起去旅行。
Synonyms: coax, cajole, wheedle, entice, lure
Antonyms: dissuade, deter, discourage
词性: 动词
