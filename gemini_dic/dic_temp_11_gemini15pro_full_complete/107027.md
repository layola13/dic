lactide:/ˈlæktaɪd/| n. |丙交酯；交酯|A cyclic diester of lactic acid. It is used in the production of polylactic acid (PLA), a biodegradable polymer.|Lactide is a crucial intermediate in the synthesis of PLA. 丙交酯是合成聚乳酸的关键中间体。

Lactide can be polymerized to form PLA. 丙交酯可以聚合形成聚乳酸。|近义词: dilactide (although often used interchangeably)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  From lactic + -ide (a suffix used in the formation of nouns denoting chemical compounds).
