outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
verb
抢先报道；比...先得到独家新闻
to get a news story before (someone else)
The local newspaper outscooped the national media on the story.  当地报纸比国家媒体抢先报道了这则新闻。
synonyms: beat, best, scoop, outdo, surpass, top, trump
antonyms: be scooped, lose out
词性: 动词

outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
noun
抢先报道；独家新闻
an instance of getting a news story before someone else
It was a major outscoop for the small-town paper.  对这家小镇报纸来说，这可是个大新闻。
synonyms: scoop, exclusive, beat
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
