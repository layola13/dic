aboral:/æbˈɔːrəl/
aboral| adj.|反口的，远离口的|Situated or directed away from the mouth, as in the aboral surface of a starfish. |The aboral surface of a sea anemone contains the anus. 海葵的反口面有肛门。|近义词：dorsal, posterior|反义词：oral, anterior|词性:形容词
aboral| n.|反口面|The surface opposite to the mouth.|The starfish uses its tube feet on its aboral surface for locomotion. 海星用其反口面的管足来移动。|近义词：dorsal surface, posterior surface|反义词：oral surface, anterior surface|词性:名词
