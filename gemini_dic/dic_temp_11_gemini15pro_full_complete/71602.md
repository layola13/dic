flat-packed:/ˌflætˈpækt/ | adj. | 平装的；扁平包装的 |  Sold in a flat pack, requiring assembly by the purchaser.  (以扁平包装出售，需要购买者自行组装。) | 例句：We bought some flat-packed furniture from IKEA. 我们从宜家买了一些扁平包装的家具。| 近义词：unassembled, knockdown, ready-to-assemble | 反义词: assembled, pre-assembled | 词性: 形容词

flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ | v. | 平装；扁平包装 | To pack (something) flat, typically for ease of storage or transport. (将某物扁平包装，通常是为了便于储存或运输。) | 例句：The furniture was flat-packed for easy shipping.  家具被扁平包装以便于运输。| 近义词：disassemble, knockdown | 反义词: assemble, pre-assemble | 词性: 动词

flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ | n. | 扁平包装 | An item that is flat-packed. (扁平包装的物品) | 例句：The flat-pack arrived damaged. 扁平包装的物品到达时已损坏。| 近义词：kit, package | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
