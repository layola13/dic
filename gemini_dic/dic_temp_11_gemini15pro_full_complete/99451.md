interestingnesses:/ˈɪntrɪstɪŋnəsɪz/
n.
趣味性，引人入胜之处
The quality of being interesting; the power to excite attention or emotion.
The interestingnesses of the museum kept the children entertained for hours.  博物馆的趣味性让孩子们玩了几个小时。
近义词:attractiveness, fascination, appeal
反义词:dullness, boredom, monotony
词性:名词
