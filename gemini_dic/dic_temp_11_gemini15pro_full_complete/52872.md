diminishingly: /dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋli/
| adv. |逐渐减少地，递减地 | in a way that becomes gradually less or smaller |  The pain was diminishingly small. 疼痛逐渐减轻。 | 近义词: decreasingly, progressively less | 反义词: increasingly, growingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From diminish + -ing + -ly.  Diminish comes from Old French diminuer, from Latin diminuere "to lessen, break in pieces," from dis- "apart" + minuere "to lessen."
