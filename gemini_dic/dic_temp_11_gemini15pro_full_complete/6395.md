amphilogism:/æmˈfɪləˌdʒɪzəm/| n. |双关语；模棱两可的话|A statement or phrase that can be interpreted in two ways, often with humorous or contradictory meanings.|Her remark about "going out to get some air" was an amphilogism, as she was actually leaving to avoid an argument.|Synonyms: ambiguity, double entendre, equivocation|Antonyms: clarity, precision|noun
