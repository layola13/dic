bhaji:/ˈbɑːdʒiː/ /ˈbɑːʒiː/
n.
油炸蔬菜丸子; 蔬菜咖喱
A spicy Indian dish of vegetables deep-fried in batter.
例句：I ordered some bhajis as a starter. 我点了一些蔬菜丸子作为开胃菜。

例句：The bhajis were crispy and delicious. 这些蔬菜丸子酥脆可口。

近义词：pakora, fritter
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "boiled vegetables" as a contrasting dish)
词性: noun
