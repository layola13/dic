androphagous:/ænˈdrɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食人肉的；吃人的
Feeding on human flesh.
例句：
Ancient myths sometimes depict androphagous monsters.
古代神话有时会描绘吃人的怪物。

Some tribes were falsely accused of androphagous practices.
一些部落被诬告有吃人的习俗。

近义词：cannibalistic, anthropophagic
反义词：herbivorous, vegetarian
词性：adjective
词源：From Greek  *ἀνθρωποφάγος (anthrōpophagos)  'man-eating,'  from  *ἄνθρωπος (anthrōpos)  'man' +  *φαγεῖν (phagein)  'to eat.'
