forter:/ˈfɔːrtər/ | n. |堡垒建造者；要塞建造者| One who constructs or builds forts. | The forter oversaw the construction of the entire fortress.  要塞建造者监督了整个堡垒的建造。| 近义词: builder, constructor | 反义词: demolisher, destroyer | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forter, from Anglo-Norman *fortur, from Old French *fortier (“to fortify”), from Late Latin *fortiāre, from Latin fortis (“strong”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwer- (“firm, solid, strong”).
