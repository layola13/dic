go-against:/ɡoʊ əˈɡenst/ | phrasal verb | 违反，反对，不利于 | to oppose or contradict something; to be unfavorable to someone or something |  He decided to go against his parents' wishes and pursue a career in art. 他决定违背父母的意愿，从事艺术事业。

The current economic climate is going against small businesses. 当前的经济环境对小型企业不利。 | 近义词: oppose, contradict, defy, counter, resist | 反义词: support, agree with, comply with, conform to | 词性: 动词短语
