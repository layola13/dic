bolometer:/boʊˈlɒmɪtər/ |n.|辐射热测量计，测辐射热计|An instrument that measures the power of incident electromagnetic radiation via the heating of a material with a temperature-dependent electrical resistance.|The astronomers used a bolometer to measure the infrared radiation from the distant galaxy.|Synonyms: radiometer, actinometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
