musaceous:/ˌmjuːˈseɪʃəs/| adj.|芭蕉科的，像芭蕉的|Relating to or resembling plants of the Musaceae family (banana family), typically having large, oblong leaves.|The botanist identified the plant as a musaceous species.  植物学家将这种植物鉴定为芭蕉科植物。|Example: The large, paddle-like leaves were a clear indication of its musaceous nature.  这些巨大的桨状叶子清楚地表明了它芭蕉科植物的特性。|Synonyms: banana-like, plantain-like|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "unlike banana plants")|adjective
