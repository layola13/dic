browbeat:/ˈbraʊbiːt/| v. |恐吓，威胁，威逼|To intimidate or dishearten by stern, overbearing, or arrogant behavior; bully.|The boss browbeat his employees into working overtime. 老板威逼员工加班。|
Synonyms: bully, intimidate, cow, hector, coerce|Antonyms: encourage, support, praise|verb
browbeater:/ˈbraʊbiːtər/| n. |恃强凌弱者，恐吓者|A person who intimidates or disheartens by stern, overbearing, or arrogant behavior; a bully.|The school bully was a notorious browbeater. 学校恶霸是一个臭名昭著的恃强凌弱者。|Synonyms: bully, intimidator, tyrant|Antonyms: supporter, encourager|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From brow + beat.  Originally meaning "to strike or hit on the brow," it evolved to mean "to depress or bear down upon," and finally settled on its current meaning of "to intimidate."
