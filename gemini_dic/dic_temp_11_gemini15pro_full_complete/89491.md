honeymooning:/ˈhʌnimuːnɪŋ/
adj.|度蜜月的|Relating to or characteristic of a honeymoon.|They are in a honeymooning mood. 他们沉浸在新婚的甜蜜中。|近义词:nuptial, marital|反义词:routine|词性:形容词
