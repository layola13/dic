farcilite: /fɑːrˈsɪlaɪt/
n.
farcilite (岩石)
A rare, light-colored, intrusive igneous rock. 一种稀有的浅色侵入性火成岩。
Farcilite is found in the Monteregian Hills of Quebec.  法西岩发现于魁北克的蒙特雷吉安丘陵。
近义词: igneous rock,  syenite
