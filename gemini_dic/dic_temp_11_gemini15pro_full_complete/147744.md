overproduces:/ˌoʊvərprəˈduːsɪz/
verb
生产过剩，过度生产
produces more of something than is needed or wanted.
The factory overproduces these cheap toys, leading to a surplus in the warehouse.  这家工厂过度生产这些廉价玩具，导致仓库积压。
The dairy farm overproduces milk, which they then sell at a discount.  这家奶牛场牛奶生产过剩，然后他们打折出售。
overproduce, manufacture excessively, generate in excess, produce too much
