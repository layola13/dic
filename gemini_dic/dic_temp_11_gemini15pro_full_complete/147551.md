overpenalizations: /ˌoʊvərˌpiːnələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度惩罚，过度处罚 |  The act of penalizing someone excessively or too harshly.  It often refers to situations where the punishment is disproportionate to the offense committed. | The judge reduced the sentence, acknowledging the overpenalizations of the defendant. 法官减轻了判决，承认了对被告的过度处罚。|  The overpenalizations of minor offenses can lead to resentment and recidivism. 对轻微犯罪的过度处罚会导致怨恨和累犯。 | Synonyms: overpunishment, excessive punishment | Antonyms: leniency, clemency | Noun
