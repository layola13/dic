iotacism:/aɪˈoʊtəsɪzəm/| n. |ι化；希腊语中ι的读音混淆|The pronunciation of several vowels as "i" (iota), especially in Modern Greek.  The confusion or merging of vowel sounds, especially of iota with other vowels.|Example: The pronunciation of "ei" as "ee" in Modern Greek is an example of iotacism. 现代希腊语中将"ei"发成"ee"音就是ι化的一个例子。

Example: Iotacism has played a significant role in the evolution of the Greek language. ι化在希腊语的演变过程中发挥了重要作用。|近义词: None (highly specific term)|反义词: None (highly specific term)|词性:noun
