lossy:/ˈlɒsi/ (UK) /ˈlɔːsi/ (US)| adj.|有损的，失真的|Characterized by the loss of data or quality, especially during compression or transmission.|The image was saved in a lossy format, resulting in a noticeable reduction in quality. 这张图片以有损格式保存，导致画质明显下降。

MP3 files are lossy, meaning some audio data is discarded to reduce file size. MP3文件是有损的，意味着一些音频数据会被丢弃以减小文件大小。|近义词：imperfect, degraded, compressed|反义词:lossless, uncompressed|词性:形容词
