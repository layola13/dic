grousers:/ˈɡraʊzərz/| n. |抱怨者; 履带板上的防滑块|A grousers is someone who complains a lot, or the cleats on a continuous track (like a tank or bulldozer).|The construction workers were grousers about the long hours. 建筑工人抱怨工作时间太长.|The tank's grousers provided excellent traction in the mud.  坦克的履带防滑块在泥泞中提供了极佳的牵引力。|Synonyms: complainers, malcontents, cleats, lugs|Antonyms: optimists,  praisers|noun


grousers:/ˈɡraʊzərz/| n. |抱怨; 牢骚|Grousing refers to the act of complaining.|The soldiers' grousing about the food was constant. 士兵们一直在抱怨食物。|There was a lot of grousing about the new company policy.  对公司的新政策有很多抱怨。|Synonyms: complaining, grumbling, griping|Antonyms: praising,  complimenting|noun
