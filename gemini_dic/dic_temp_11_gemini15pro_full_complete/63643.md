eosaurus:/ˌiːoʊˈsɔːrəs/
n.
曙龙 (一种恐龙)
A genus of prosauropod dinosaur from the Late Triassic period.  It is one of the earliest known dinosaurs.
Eosaurus was a herbivore that lived during the Late Triassic. 曙龙是生活在晚三叠世的一种食草动物。
Eosaurus is considered an important find in understanding dinosaur evolution.  曙龙被认为是理解恐龙进化史上的一个重要发现。
近义词:Plateosaurus 板龙, Thecodontosaurus  槽齿龙 (These are related prosauropods)
反义词: N/A (It's difficult to have an antonym for a specific dinosaur genus)
词性: Noun
