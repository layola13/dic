bouncer:/ˈbaʊnsər/| n.|保镖，保安；(酒吧等的)门卫；跳跃的人或物|A person employed by a nightclub or similar establishment to prevent troublemakers from entering or to eject them from the premises.  A person or thing that bounces.|The bouncer wouldn't let us in.  保安不让我们进去。 The child enjoyed playing with her bouncy ball. 孩子喜欢玩她的弹力球。|synonyms: doorman, security guard, watchman, ejector |antonyms: patron, customer|noun

bouncer:/ˈbaʊnsər/| n.|（板球）弹地球；（棒球）高飞球|In cricket, a ball that bounces high off the pitch. In baseball, a fly ball.|The batsman was caught out from a bouncer.  击球手被一个弹地球接杀出局。 He hit a bouncer to center field. 他击出一个高飞球到中外野。|synonyms: fly ball (baseball), long hop (cricket) |antonyms: full toss (cricket)|noun
