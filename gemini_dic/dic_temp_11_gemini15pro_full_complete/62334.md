endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的；流行的 | Native or confined to a particular region or people; characteristic of or prevalent in a field. | Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 疟疾在许多炎热的国家流行。 | Synonyms: native, indigenous, aboriginal, restricted to | Antonyms: exotic, foreign, alien | adjective

endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | n. | 地方病 | A disease regularly found among particular people or in a certain area. |  The doctor diagnosed the endemic as a strain of malaria. 医生诊断这种地方病为一种疟疾。 | Synonyms: local disease, regional disease | Antonyms: pandemic | noun
