macramed:/ˌmækɹəˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 用绳结编织的；用花结装饰的 | Made using the macrame technique of knotting. |  She wore a macramed vest. 她穿着一件绳结编织的背心。 | Synonyms: knotted, woven, braided | Antonyms: plain, unadorned | word type: adjective

macrame:/ˈmækɹəmeɪ/ | n. | 花边结；绳结编织工艺 | The art of knotting cord or string in patterns to make decorative articles. |  She learned macrame at summer camp. 她在夏令营学会了绳结编织。 | Synonyms: knotting, knotwork | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "weaving" or "knitting" as alternative crafts) | word type: noun

macrame:/ˈmækɹəmeɪ/ | v. | 用绳结编织；用花结装饰 | To create something using the macrame technique. | She macramed a beautiful wall hanging. 她用绳结编织了一幅漂亮的壁挂。 | Synonyms: knot, braid, weave | Antonyms: untie, unravel | word type: verb

Etymology: from French *macramé*, from Turkish *makrame*, from Arabic *miqrama*, meaning "embroidered napkin" or "fringe."
