autarchically:/ˌɔːtɑːrˈkɪkli/| adv.|自给自足地，独立自主地|In a self-sufficient or independent manner, without external assistance or dependence.|例句：The small island nation functioned autarchically, producing all its own food and energy.  这个小岛国自给自足，自己生产所有的食物和能源。

例句：They lived autarchically, detached from the complexities of modern society. 他们过着自给自足的生活，远离现代社会的复杂性。|近义词：independently, self-sufficiently, autonomously|反义词：dependently, interdependently|词性:副词
