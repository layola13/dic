divinement:/diːˈviːnmɒ̃/ | adv. | 神圣地，极其地，绝妙地 | In a divine manner; exquisitely; superbly. |  She sang divinely, moving the audience to tears. 她唱得极其优美，感动得观众流下了眼泪。 | divinely beautiful scenery 绝妙的风景 | 近义词: exquisitely, superbly, wonderfully, beautifully, perfectly | 反义词: poorly, badly, awfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From French divinement, derived from the adjective divin ("divine"), ultimately from Latin dīvīnus.
