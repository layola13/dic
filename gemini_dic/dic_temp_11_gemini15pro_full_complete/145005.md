overadjusted:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstɪd/
adjective
过度调整的
Adjusted too much; excessively corrected or modified.
The economist argued that the market was overadjusted and predicted a correction soon. 经济学家认为市场过度调整，并预测很快就会出现回调。
The therapist felt the patient's medication was overadjusted, leading to new side effects.  治疗师认为病人的药物调整过度，导致了新的副作用。
近义词: overcompensated, overcorrected
反义词: underadjusted
词性: adjective
