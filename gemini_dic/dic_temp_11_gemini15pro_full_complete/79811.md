glycogeny:/ˈɡlaɪkədʒɛni/ /ˈɡlaɪkoʊdʒɛni/
noun
糖原生成，糖原形成
The formation of glycogen from glucose.
Glycogeny is an important process for storing glucose in the liver and muscles.  糖原生成是肝脏和肌肉储存葡萄糖的重要过程。
The athlete's diet was designed to maximize glycogeny.  这位运动员的饮食旨在最大限度地提高糖原生成。
近义词:glycogenesis
反义词:glycogenolysis
