not /nɒt/ /nɑːt/ | adv. | 不，没有 | Used to express negation, denial, or refusal. |  I do not like spinach. 我不喜欢菠菜。| 近义词: no, none | 反义词: yes | 词性: 副词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I do not eat meat.  我不吃肉。
She does not work here anymore. 她不再在这里工作了。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
They are not playing games. 他们没有在玩游戏。
He is not sleeping right now. 他现在没在睡觉。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
We have not finished the project yet. 我们还没有完成这个项目。
She has not seen him for years. 她已经好几年没见过他了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have not been feeling well lately. 我最近感觉不太好。
They have not been studying very hard. 他们最近学习不太努力。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He did not go to the party. 他没去参加派对。
We did not see the movie. 我们没有看那部电影。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
She was not watching TV when I called. 我打电话时她没在看电视。
They were not working on the project yesterday. 他们昨天没有在做这个项目。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had not eaten before the meeting. 开会前我没吃东西。
They had not finished the work before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前没有完成工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
She had not been sleeping well before the exam. 考试前她一直睡不好。
We had not been working on that project for very long. 我们做那个项目的时间不长。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will not go to the party tomorrow. 我明天不会去参加派对。
They will not be here next week. 他们下周不会在这里。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will not be working this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我不会在工作。
She will not be traveling next month. 她下个月不会去旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will not have finished the project by next year. 明年之前我不会完成这个项目。
They will not have arrived by then. 到那时他们不会到达。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will not have been working here for ten years by next year. 到明年我在这里工作还不到十年。
She will not have been studying English for very long by then. 到那时她学习英语的时间不会很长。

词源: Old English *nāwiht, *nōwiht, from nā no + wiht thing, creature.  类似于德语 nicht, 荷兰语 niet.
