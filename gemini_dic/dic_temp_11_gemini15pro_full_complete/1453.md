acephala:/əˌsefələ/| n.|无头类动物|Animals belonging to the Acephala group, characterized by the lack of a distinct head. These are typically marine invertebrates like clams, oysters, and mussels.|Oysters and clams are both acephala. 牡蛎和蛤蜊都属于无头类动物。|近义词:bivalve, mollusk|反义词:cephalopod|noun
