enchanting:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪŋ/ | adj. |迷人的，令人陶醉的 |Delightfully charming or attractive; captivating.| The enchanting music filled the air. 迷人的音乐充满了空气。|  The view from the mountaintop was enchanting. 从山顶望下去的景色令人陶醉。| 近义词: captivating, charming, fascinating, alluring, magical | 反义词: repulsive, unpleasant, boring, dull | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *enchanter (“to enchant”), from Latin incantare (“to chant or sing a magic formula over”), from in- (“in, on”) + cantare (“to sing”).
