like-mindedness:/ˌlaɪkˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n. |志同道合，相同想法|The group bonded over their like-mindedness on key political issues.  这个团体因为在关键政治问题上的相同想法而团结在一起。|例句：Their like-mindedness made them fast friends. 他们的志趣相投使他们很快成为朋友。|近义词：affinity, compatibility, concord, harmony, agreement, accord, unanimity|反义词：discord, disagreement, difference, disharmony|Noun


like-minded:/ˌlaɪkˈmaɪndɪd/| adj. |志同道合的，想法相同的|They are a group of like-minded individuals working towards a common goal. 他们是一群为共同目标而努力的志同道合的人。|例句：She found comfort in being surrounded by like-minded people. 她觉得被志同道合的人包围着很舒服。|近义词：similar, compatible, kindred, congenial|反义词：incompatible, uncongenial, dissimilar|Adjective
