likee: /ˈlaɪkiː/
noun|点赞; 喜欢的人或物|A person or thing that someone likes.  Specifically, a user who follows and interacts with content on a social media platform, particularly short-form video platforms like Likee (now known as Likee Video).  It can also generally refer to something someone enjoys.|She got a lot of likees on her latest video. 她在最新的视频中获得了许多点赞。|Synonyms: fan, follower, admirer|Antonyms: hater, critic|n.

likee: /ˈlaɪkiː/
verb|点赞; 表示喜欢|To express one's approval or enjoyment of something, typically on social media by clicking a "like" button or equivalent.  It can also mean to enjoy something in general.|I likeed her post on Instagram. 我在Instagram上点赞了她的帖子。|Synonyms:  approve, enjoy, favor, heart, upvote|Antonyms: dislike, downvote, disapprove|v.
