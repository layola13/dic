formatted:/ˈfɔːrmætɪd/ | adj. | 格式化的，已格式化的 | Arranged or prepared according to a particular format or structure. | The document was formatted according to the company's style guide.  该文件已根据公司风格指南进行了格式化。 | formatted text has been arranged according to a standard layout. 已格式化的文本已根据标准布局排列。 |  近义词: structured, arranged, organized | 反义词: unformatted, disorganized | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "format" + "-ed" suffix.  The verb "format" comes from the French "formater," from "format" (shape, size), ultimately from Latin "forma" (form, shape).
