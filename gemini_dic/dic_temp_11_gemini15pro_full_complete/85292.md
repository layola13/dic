harmonizes:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪz/
verb
协调，使和谐，使一致
to bring into or be in accord or agreement; make harmonious or consistent
The soft music harmonizes with the gentle swaying of the trees.  柔和的音乐与树木的轻柔摇曳相协调。
The new building harmonizes well with its surroundings.  新建筑与其周围环境非常协调。
harmonize, blend, coordinate, match, agree
clash, conflict, disagree, jar
v.
