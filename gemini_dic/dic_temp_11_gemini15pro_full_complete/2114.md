adaptable:/əˈdæptəbl/ | adj. | 适应性强的，能适应的 | Able to adjust to new conditions. | She's a very adaptable person and will quickly fit in here. 她适应性很强，很快就会融入这里。|  He is an adaptable employee and is comfortable working on a variety of projects.  他是一个适应能力强的员工，可以轻松胜任各种项目。 | 近义词：flexible, versatile, adjustable | 反义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From adapt + -able.  "Adapt" comes from the Latin word "adaptare," meaning "to fit."
