admired:/ədˈmaɪəd/ | adj. | 钦佩的，受人赞赏的 |  Regarded with respect, wonder, and approval. |  Her work is much admired by critics. 她的作品深受评论家们的赞赏。 | synonyms: respected, esteemed, revered, venerated | antonyms: despised, scorned, loathed, detested | adjective

admiring:/ədˈmaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 赞赏的，钦佩的 | Feeling or expressing admiration. | He gave her an admiring glance. 他向她投以赞赏的一瞥。 | synonyms: appreciative, approving, respectful | antonyms: critical, disapproving, scornful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *admirer*, from Latin *admirari* "to wonder at," from *ad-* "to" + *mirari* "to wonder."
