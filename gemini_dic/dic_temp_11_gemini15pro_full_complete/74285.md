fracid:/ˈfræsɪd/ | adj. | 松弛的；萎蔫的；无力的 | lacking firmness; flabby and soft; weak |  The flowers were fracid from lack of water. 这些花因为缺水而枯萎了。| synonyms: flabby, limp, drooping, withered | antonyms: firm, taut, turgid | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fracidus, from fracidus (“soft, mellow, putrid, rotten, crumbling”), from fracesco (“begin to decay”), from *frac-, related to frango (“break”).
