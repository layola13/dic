decorticate: /diːˈkɔːrtɪkeɪt/
verb
剥去…的皮；剥皮；去皮；除去（大脑等的）皮质；去皮层
To remove the bark, rind, husk, or outer layer from something.  To remove the cortex of (a brain, seed, etc.).
The chef decorticated the oranges before using the zest in the cake.  厨师在用橙皮做蛋糕之前先去掉了橙皮。
The neurosurgeon decorticated the patient's brain to relieve pressure. 神经外科医生切除了病人的大脑皮层以减轻压力。
近义词: peel, husk, pare, flay, excoriate
反义词: cover, coat
词性: verb

decorticate: /diːˈkɔːrtɪkeɪt/
adjective
去皮的；去皮层的
Having had the bark, rind, husk, or outer layer removed. Having had the cortex removed.
The decorticated wheat grains were ready to be processed. 去皮的小麦颗粒准备进行加工。
The study analyzed decorticated brain samples. 这项研究分析了去皮层的大脑样本。
近义词: peeled, husked, pared
反义词: covered, coated
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin decorticatus, past participle of decorticare (“to peel, husk”), from de- (“off, away from”) + cortex (“bark, rind, husk”).
