herbal:/ˈɜːrbl/ (US) /ˈhɜːbl/ (UK)| adj.|草本的；药草的|Relating to or made from herbs.|例句：She drinks herbal tea every evening. 她每晚都喝凉茶。|例句：He's an expert on herbal remedies. 他是草药疗法的专家。|近义词：botanical, plant-based|反义词：synthetic, chemical|词性：形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *erbal, from Anglo-Norman and Old French erbal, from Medieval Latin herbālis, from Latin herba (“herb, grass”).
