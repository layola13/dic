l-plates:/ˈel pleɪts/ | n. |  实习驾照；临时驾照 | Learner plates, displayed on a vehicle to show that the driver is a learner.  A sign displayed on the front and rear of a motor vehicle driven by a learner driver. |  Example: He still has his l-plates on his car. 他的车上仍然挂着实习驾照牌。
Example:  She nervously pulled away from the curb, her l-plates clearly visible. 她紧张地把车开离路边，她的实习驾照牌清晰可见。 | Synonym: learner plates, provisional plates | Antonym: None | Noun
