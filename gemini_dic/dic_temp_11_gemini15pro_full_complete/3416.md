aforethought:/əˈfɔːrθɔːt/
|n.|预谋，预先考虑|Premeditation; planning or consideration beforehand.|The murder was committed with malice aforethought. 这起谋杀是预谋的。|
|近义词：premeditation, forethought, planning|
|反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity|
|词性：名词|

Etymology:
From Middle English *aforethoght*, equivalent to afore +‎ thought.
