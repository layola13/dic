fervent:/ˈfɜːrvənt/ (US) /ˈfɜːvənt/ (UK)| adj.|热烈的，热情的，强烈的|Having or showing strong feelings; intensely devoted or enthusiastic.|She has a fervent belief in her religion. 她对自己的宗教信仰充满热情。|例句：He delivered a fervent speech about the importance of education. 他发表了一场关于教育重要性的热情洋溢的演讲。|近义词：passionate, ardent, zealous, enthusiastic|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lukewarm|词性:形容词

fervor:/ˈfɜːrvər/ (US) /ˈfɜːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|热情，热烈，热诚|Intense and passionate feeling.|The fervor of the crowd was contagious. 人群的热情具有感染力。|例句：She spoke with the fervor of a true believer. 她以一个真正信徒的热情讲话。|近义词：passion, ardor, zeal, enthusiasm|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fervent*, from Old French *fervent*, from Latin *fervens*, present participle of *fervere* (“to boil, glow”).  The noun form, *fervor*, also derives from Latin *fervor*.
