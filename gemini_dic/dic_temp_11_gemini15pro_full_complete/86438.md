heelspur:/ˈhiːlˌspɜːr/ (US), /ˈhiːlˌspəː(r)/ (UK)| n.|跟骨刺，足跟骨刺|A bony growth on the heel bone (calcaneus), typically causing pain when standing or walking.|The doctor diagnosed her heel pain as a heelspur.|Near synonyms:  bone spur, calcaneal spur|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: healthy heel, normal heel)|Noun
