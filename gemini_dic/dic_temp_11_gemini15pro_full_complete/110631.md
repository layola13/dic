limpingly:/ˈlɪmpɪŋli/| adv.|一瘸一拐地，蹒跚地|in a limping manner; with a limp.  The action or state of walking with difficulty because of an injured or stiff leg or foot.|He walked limpingly across the room. 他一瘸一拐地走过房间。|Synonyms: haltingly, falteringly, laboriously|Antonyms: steadily, smoothly, easily|Adverb
