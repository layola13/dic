abacuses:/ˈæbəkəsiːz/ /ˈæbəkəsɪz/
n.
算盘
A frame with rows of wires or grooves along which beads are slid, used for calculating.
She learned to use an abacus in her childhood. 她小时候学会了使用算盘。
The shopkeeper still used an abacus to calculate the total. 店主仍然用算盘计算总数。

slide rule
