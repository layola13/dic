misaffect:/ˌmɪsəˈfɛkt/
verb
使…不满意；使…不忠；使…疏远
To cause to be dissatisfied or discontented; to alienate the affections of.
The king's policies misaffeected many of his subjects. 国王的政策使许多臣民不满。
The new management's decisions have misaffeected the employees. 新管理层的决策疏远了员工。
近义词:alienate, disaffect, estrange
反义词: reconcile, appease, placate
词性:v.

Etymology:
From mis- (badly, wrongly) + affect (to act on, to influence). The prefix "mis-" adds a negative connotation to the verb "affect," implying a negative influence or impact.
