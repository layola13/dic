kneebrush:/ˈniːbrʌʃ/| n.|膝盖刷; 膝盖高度的灌木丛; (尤指儿童)及膝的裤子/裙子|A kneebrush is a growth of bushes or shrubs that reaches up to about knee height. It can also refer to pants or skirts that reach to the knees, especially for children.|The children played hide-and-seek in the kneebrush. 孩子们在及膝高的灌木丛中玩捉迷藏。|She wore a pretty kneebrush skirt. 她穿了一条漂亮的及膝裙。|近义词：knee-length, scrub, thicket|反义词：(For clothing) floor-length, ankle-length, maxi  (For vegetation) forest, woodland|词性:noun
