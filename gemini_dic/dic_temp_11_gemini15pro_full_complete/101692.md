irremovable:/ˌɪrɪˈmuːvəbl/
adjective
不可移动的;无法去除的
not able to be removed
The stain on the shirt was irremovable.  衬衫上的污渍无法去除。
The president is facing irremovable opposition. 总统正面临着无法消除的反对。
近义词:fixed, permanent, fast, inalienable
反义词:removable, detachable, mobile
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + removable.  "Removable" comes from the verb "remove," which ultimately derives from the Latin "removēre," meaning "to move back or away."
