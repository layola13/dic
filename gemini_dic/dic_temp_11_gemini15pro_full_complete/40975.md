coopt:/koʊˈɑːpt/ /kəʊˈɒpt/| vt.|吸收；拉拢；指派；选定|To add (someone) to a group by the votes of the existing members; To appoint to membership of a committee or other body by invitation of the existing members.|The rebels were eventually coopted into the government. 叛乱分子最终被拉拢加入了政府。

The committee coopted three new members. 委员会增选了三名新成员。|近义词：appropriate, elect, appoint, enlist, incorporate|反义词:exclude, reject|词性:动词

coopted:/koʊˈɑːptɪd/ /kəʊˈɒptɪd/| adj.|被吸收的；被拉拢的；被指派的|Having been added to a group by the votes of the existing members.|The coopted members brought a fresh perspective to the committee.  被吸收的成员给委员会带来了新的视角。

Many formerly coopted officials have now been elected. 许多以前被指派的官员现在已经被选出来了。|近义词：appointed, selected, chosen|反义词:rejected, excluded|词性:形容词
