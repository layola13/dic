heritable:/ˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. |可遗传的，可继承的 | Able to be passed from parent to offspring genetically.  |  Eye color is a heritable trait. 眼睛的颜色是可遗传的性状。 | Synonyms: inheritable, genetic, transmissible | Antonyms: acquired, non-heritable | adjective

heritable:/hɛˈrɪtəbl/ | adj. |可继承的（指财产、头衔等）| Able to be passed down by inheritance. | The estate is heritable only by male heirs.  这片地产只能由男性继承人继承。| Synonyms: inheritable, bequeathable, transmissible | Antonyms: non-inheritable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *eritable, from Old French eritable, from eriter (to inherit), from Latin hereditare, from heres (heir).
