mangou: /mæŋˈɡuː/ | n. |芒果 (指西非芒果树及其果实) |  A tropical African tree ( *Irvingia gabonensis*) bearing edible greenish-yellow fruits with a large, fibrous stone. The fruit is often used to make jams, jellies, and other preserves. The seeds can also be processed into a thickening agent for soups and stews. |  The mangou fruits are ripe and ready to be harvested. 芒果果实成熟了，可以收获了。 | Synonyms: dika nut, wild mango | Antonyms: N/A | noun

mangou: /mæŋˈɡuː/ | n. |迪卡果 (指西非芒果树的种子) |  The seed of the mangou tree (*Irvingia gabonensis*), also known as dika nut. These seeds are rich in fat and protein and are used in various culinary applications, including as a thickening agent. |  Dika butter, extracted from mangou seeds, is used in traditional African cuisine. 从芒果种子中提取的迪卡黄油用于传统的非洲烹饪。| Synonyms: dika nut, ogbono nut | Antonyms: N/A | noun
