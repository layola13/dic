metamer:/ˈmɛtəmər/| n. |同分异构体；体节；异构体|One of several similar segments into which the body of some animals is divided. A chemical compound having the same composition and molecular weight as another compound, but differing in the arrangement of atoms.|The earthworm's body is composed of many metamers. 蚯蚓的身体由许多体节组成。|Synonyms: isomer, segment, somite|Antonyms: whole, entire|noun

metamer:/ˈmɛtəmər/| n. |（颜色）同色异谱|Two colors that appear to be the same under one light source but different under another.  |These two fabrics are metamers; they match under fluorescent light but not in daylight. 这两块布料是同色异谱的；它们在荧光灯下看起来一样，但在日光下则不一样。|Synonyms: color match (under specific conditions)|Antonyms: spectral match|noun
