detonating:/ˈdɛtəneɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective
爆炸；引爆（动词）; 爆炸的；起爆的（形容词）
Exploding, causing something to explode (verb); related to explosion or causing explosion (adjective).
The detonating cord ignited the explosives. 引爆线点燃了炸药。
The detonating device was hidden in a briefcase.  起爆装置藏在一个公文包里。
Synonyms: exploding, blasting, igniting
Antonyms: defusing, neutralizing
