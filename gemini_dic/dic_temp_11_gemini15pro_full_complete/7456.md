anisopetalous:/ˌænɪsoʊˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 花瓣不等长的；不等瓣的 | Having petals of unequal size or shape.  | Example 1: The anisopetalous flower had petals of varying lengths. 这朵不等瓣的花的花瓣长度各不相同。

Example 2:  Anisopetalous flowers are a characteristic of certain plant families.  不等瓣的花是某些植物科的特征。 | Synonym: irregular, unequal | Antonym: regular, isopetalous | adjective
