cerebrin: /ˈsɛrɪbrɪn/
noun
脑苷
A fatty substance found in brain tissue.
Cerebrin is a component of myelin, the protective sheath around nerve fibers.
脑苷是髓磷脂的一个组成部分，髓磷脂是神经纤维周围的保护鞘。
synonyms: cerebroside
antonyms: None (It's a specific chemical compound, not a concept with an opposite)
