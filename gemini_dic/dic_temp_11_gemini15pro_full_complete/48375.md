decussate:/ˌdiːkəˈseɪt/| v. adj.|交叉, 交叉的|To cross in the form of an X; to intersect.|The optic nerves decussate in the brain.  视神经在大脑中交叉。|Near Antonyms: parallel, diverge|Near Synonyms: intersect, cross, crisscross|verb, adjective

decussation:/ˌdiːkəˈseɪʃn/| n.|交叉, 交叉点|The act or an instance of crossing, especially in the form of an X.|The decussation of the pyramids is a prominent feature of the medulla oblongata. 锥体交叉是延髓的一个显著特征。|Near Antonyms: parallelism, divergence|Near Synonyms: intersection, crossing, crisscrossing|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *decussatus*, past participle of *decusso*, "to divide into ten parts, mark with an X," from *decussis*, "a division of ten, the Roman numeral X," from *decem*, "ten."
