homotaxia:/ˌhɒməˈtæksiə/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈtæksiə/ | n. |同源异序; 同功异源 | Similarity in structure between parts of different organisms due to convergent evolution, not common descent.  The independent evolution of similar features in species of different lineages. |例句：The wings of bats and birds are an example of homotaxia. 蝙蝠和鸟类的翅膀是同功异源的一个例子。

例句：Homotaxia can make it difficult to determine evolutionary relationships. 同功异源会使确定进化关系变得困难。|近义词：analogy, convergence |反义词: homology |词性:noun
