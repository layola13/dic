lomonite:/ˈloʊməˌnaɪt/| n. |菱沸石|A zeolite mineral, hydrated calcium silicate.  It occurs as small white crystals and is often found in volcanic rocks.|Lomonite crystals were found in the basalt. 在玄武岩中发现了菱沸石晶体。|Synonyms: laumontite, leonhardite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
