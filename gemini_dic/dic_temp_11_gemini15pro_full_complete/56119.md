doctrinality:/ˌdɒktrɪˈnælɪti/ /ˌdɑːktrɪˈnælɪti/| n. |教义性，学理性|The state or quality of being doctrinal; adherence to doctrines or dogma.  The degree to which something is doctrinal. |The doctrinality of the statement was questioned by some members of the church.  该声明的教义性受到一些教会成员的质疑。| 近义词: dogmatism, rigidity, orthodoxy | 反义词: flexibility, pragmatism, heterodoxy |词性: noun
