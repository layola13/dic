nighttime:/ˈnaɪtˌtaɪm/ (US), /ˈnaɪtˌtaɪm/ (UK)| n.|夜间，晚上|The period of time between sunset and sunrise. |Owls are most active during the nighttime. 猫头鹰在夜间最活跃。|Synonyms: night, darkness, nightfall|Antonyms: daytime, daylight|noun


nighttime:/ˈnaɪtˌtaɪm/ (US), /ˈnaɪtˌtaɪm/ (UK)| adj.|夜间的|Relating to or occurring at night. |Nighttime temperatures can be significantly lower than daytime temperatures. 夜间温度可能明显低于白天温度。|Synonyms: nocturnal, nightly|Antonyms: diurnal, daytime|adjective
