amputee: /ˌæm.pjəˈtiː/ (US), /ˌæm.pjuːˈtiː/ (UK)| n. |截肢者|A person who has had a limb amputated (surgically removed).|例句：The amputee was fitted with a prosthetic leg.  这位截肢者安装了假肢腿。

例句：Support groups can be helpful for amputees adjusting to their new lives.  互助小组可以帮助截肢者适应他们的新生活。

近义词：disabled person, handicapped person|反义词: able-bodied person|词性: noun
