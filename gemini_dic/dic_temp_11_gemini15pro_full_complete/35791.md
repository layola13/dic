cockcrow:/ˈkɒkˌkrəʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkˌkroʊ/ (US)| n.|鸡鸣；黎明；破晓|The sound a rooster makes at dawn, also used figuratively to refer to the very early hours of the morning.|The farmer woke at cockcrow.  农民鸡鸣时醒来。|Synonyms: dawn, daybreak, sunrise|Antonyms: sunset, dusk, nightfall|noun

cock-a-doodle-doo:/ˌkɒk ə ˈduːdl ˈduː/ (UK) /ˌkɑːk ə ˈduːdl ˈduː/ (US)| n.|鸡鸣声|The onomatopoeic representation of a rooster's crow.|The cock-a-doodle-doo announced the start of a new day.  鸡鸣声宣告了新的一天开始。|Synonyms: cockcrow|Antonyms: None|noun
