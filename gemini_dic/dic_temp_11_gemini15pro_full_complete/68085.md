fade-out:/ˈfeɪdaʊt/ | n. | 淡出，渐隐 |A gradual decrease in the visibility or audibility of something, such as a scene in a film or a sound in a piece of music. | The movie ended with a slow fade-out to black.  这部电影以缓慢的淡出到黑色结束。| synonyms: dissolve, fade away | antonyms: fade-in, appear | noun

fade-outs:/ˈfeɪdaʊts/ | n. | 淡出，渐隐（复数） | Plural form of "fade-out," referring to multiple instances of gradual decreases in visibility or audibility. | The album uses fade-outs between several of the tracks. 这张专辑在几首曲目之间使用了淡出效果。| synonyms: dissolves, fade aways | antonyms: fade-ins, appearances | noun
