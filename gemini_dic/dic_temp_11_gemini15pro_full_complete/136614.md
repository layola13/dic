nonsatirical:/ˌnɒn səˈtɪrɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːn səˈtɪrɪkəl/| adj.|非讽刺的，不开玩笑的|Not satirical; serious.  Lacking satire or irony.|The documentary presented a nonsatirical account of the war.|近义词：serious, earnest, sincere, genuine|反义词：satirical, ironic, sarcastic|词性:形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "satirical" (from Late Latin "satiricus," from Greek "satirikos," relating to satyr plays, which were often comedic and critical).
