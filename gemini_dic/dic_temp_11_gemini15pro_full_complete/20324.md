bishopess:/ˈbɪʃəpɪs/
n.
女主教
a woman who holds the rank of bishop, especially in certain Protestant churches.
例句：The bishopess presided over the ordination ceremony. 女主教主持了授任仪式。

例句：The bishopess visited several parishes in her diocese. 女主教访问了她教区内的几个教区。

近义词：bishop (in some contexts), prelate
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly layman/laywoman)
词性：noun
