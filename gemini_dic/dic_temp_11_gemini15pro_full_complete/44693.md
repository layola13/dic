cruddy:/ˈkrʌdi/| adj.|肮脏的，邋遢的；劣质的；令人不快的|Something that is cruddy is dirty, of poor quality, or unpleasant.|"The hotel room was cruddy and smelled of stale cigarettes."  酒店房间很脏，一股陈腐的香烟味。|近义词：dirty, grimy, shabby, lousy, awful|反义词：clean, pristine, excellent, wonderful|词性:形容词
词源:Probably from 'crud' + '-y'.  'Crud' itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to 'curd'.
