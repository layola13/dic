adjourning:/əˈdʒɜːrnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|休会；延期|The meeting was adjourned until the following week. 会议延期到下周。|The judge adjourned the trial. 法官宣布休庭。|近义词: postponing, suspending, deferring|反义词: convening, commencing, starting
noun|休会；延期|The adjourning of the meeting was unexpected. 会议的休会出乎意料。|The adjournment lasted for several hours. 休会持续了几个小时。|近义词: postponement, suspension, deferment|反义词: commencement, start
