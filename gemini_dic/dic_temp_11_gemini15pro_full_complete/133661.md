nonfortifying: /ˌnɑːnˈfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. |  不加强的；不滋补的；不鼓励的 | Lacking the quality of fortifying; not giving strength or courage; not invigorating. | The news was nonfortifying, leaving her feeling drained and discouraged. 这个消息并不令人鼓舞，让她感到筋疲力尽，灰心丧气。|  The thin soup was nonfortifying and did little to satisfy his hunger. 这稀薄的汤不滋补，几乎不能满足他的饥饿感。 | 近义词: weakening, debilitating, discouraging, uninspiring | 反义词: fortifying, strengthening, encouraging, invigorating | 词性: adjective

词源:

"Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."

"Fortify" comes from the Old French "fortifier," from "fort" (strong) and "-fier" (to make).  Ultimately, it derives from the Latin "fortis" (strong).
