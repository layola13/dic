imperfectibility:/ˌɪmpərˌfɛktɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不能完美, 不完善
The state or quality of being imperfectible; inability to be made perfect.
The philosopher discussed the imperfectibility of human nature. 哲学家探讨了人性的不完美。
近义词:flawlessness, faultlessness, impeccability
反义词:perfectibility, flawfulness, faultiness
词性:noun

imperfectible:/ˌɪmpərˈfɛktɪbl/
adj.
不能完美的
Not capable of being made perfect.
Humans are imperfectible beings. 人类是不完美的生物。
近义词:flawed, faulty, imperfect
反义词:perfectible, flawless, faultless
词性:adjective
