fluke: /fluːk/| n.|意外；侥幸；偶然事件；尾片|an unlikely chance occurrence, especially a surprising piece of luck.  Also, a parasitic flatworm, especially one that infects the liver or intestines of vertebrates. Also, the part of an anchor that fastens into the ground or the lobes of a whale's tail.|He won by a fluke. 他侥幸获胜。|Synonyms: coincidence, accident, stroke of luck, windfall, godsend|Antonyms: plan, intention, design, purpose|noun


fluke: /fluːk/| v.|侥幸成功；意外地做成|to achieve something by luck or chance.|He fluked the exam. 他侥幸通过了考试。|Synonyms: chance, luck into, stumble upon|Antonyms: earn, achieve through hard work|verb

注意事项:
fluke 作为名词，还可以指“侥幸成功的人/事”或“意外的错误”。例如：He was a fluke, winning the lottery on his first try. （他真是个幸运儿，第一次买彩票就中了。）  或 The computer crash was a fluke. （电脑崩溃是个意外。）

fluke 还可以指某些寄生虫（吸虫）或鲸鱼尾部的水平叶片（尾片）。
