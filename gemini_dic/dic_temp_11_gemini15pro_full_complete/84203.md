half-republican:/ˌhæf rɪˈpʌblɪkən/
| adj. | 半共和的；部分拥护共和主义的 | Partly republican; supporting some but not all republican principles. |  The party adopted a half-republican platform, appealing to a broader range of voters.  该党采取了半共和主义的纲领，以吸引更广泛的选民。| Near synonyms: semi-republican, moderately republican | Antonyms: fully republican, completely republican, anti-republican | Word type: Adjective

half-republican:/ˌhæf rɪˈpʌblɪkən/
| n. | 半共和主义者; 部分拥护共和主义的人 | A person who supports some but not all republican principles. |  He considered himself a half-republican, agreeing with some of their fiscal policies but not their social views. 他认为自己是一个半共和主义者，同意他们的一些财政政策，但不同意他们的社会观点。 | Near synonyms: semi-republican, moderate republican | Antonyms: staunch republican, ardent republican, anti-republican | Word type: Noun
