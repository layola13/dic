ichthyophagy: /ɪkθiˈɒfədʒi/ | n. | 食鱼；食鱼习性 | The practice of eating fish.  Fish consumption as a dietary staple. | Piranhas are known for their ichthyophagy. 食人鱼以食鱼而闻名。 |  Many coastal communities rely on ichthyophagy for sustenance. 许多沿海社区依靠食鱼为生。 | 近义词: piscivory | 反义词:  herbivory | 词性: noun

词源:  From Ancient Greek  ἰχθυοφαγία (ikhthuophagia), from ἰχθύς (ikhthus, “fish”) + φαγεῖν (phagein, “to eat”).
