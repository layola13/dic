askewness:/skjuːnɪs/
n.|偏度，偏斜度|A statistical measure of the asymmetry of a probability distribution about its mean.  It describes the degree to which a distribution is skewed to the left (negative skew) or to the right (positive skew).|The data showed a significant positive askewness, indicating a concentration of values at the lower end. 数据显示出显著的正偏度，表明数值集中在低端。|
近义词:skewness, asymmetry|反义词:symmetry|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From skew + -ness.  Skew itself comes from Old North French eskiuer "to shy away, swerve."
