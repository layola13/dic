designative:/ˈdɛzɪɡnətɪv/| adj.|指定的，指名的|Serving to designate; indicative.  Having the quality of designating; appointing.|The designative characteristics of a species are those that distinguish it from other species.|近义词:  assigned, appointed, nominated, specified|反义词:  elective, unspecified, undesignated|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *designativus, from Latin designatus (past participle of designare ‘to mark out, designate’), from de- ‘down, completely’ + signare ‘to mark’.
