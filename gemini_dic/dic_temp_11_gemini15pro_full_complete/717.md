absenter:/ˈæbsənˌtər/ (US), /æbˈsɛntə(r)/ (UK)| n.|缺席者，旷工者|A person who is absent, especially from work or school without permission.|He was marked as an absenter in the company's records for three consecutive days. 他连续三天被公司记录为缺勤者。|
Example: The absenter was penalized for missing the important meeting.  缺席者因错过重要会议而受到处罚。
Example:  Frequent absentees can face disciplinary action.  经常缺勤的人可能会面临纪律处分。
|近义词：truant, non-attender|反义词：attendee, presentee|词性: noun
