blockading:/blɒˈkeɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |封锁的；实行封锁的|  Engaging in a blockade; obstructing or preventing passage or communication. | The blockading forces prevented any ships from entering the port. 封锁部队阻止任何船只进入港口。| 近义词: besieging, block,  | 反义词: opening, freeing | adjective

blockade:/blɒˈkeɪd/ | n. vt.|封锁；阻塞|The isolation of a place, such as a port or a nation, by troops or warships to prevent entrance or exit.| The naval blockade prevented essential supplies from reaching the city. 海军封锁阻止了必要的物资到达这座城市。|近义词: siege, obstruction | 反义词: access, opening | noun, verb

词源: From French *blocus*, from Dutch *blokhuis* "blockhouse," literally "log house," from *blok* "log" + *huis* "house."  The sense shifted from "fortification" to "siege" and then to the current meaning.
