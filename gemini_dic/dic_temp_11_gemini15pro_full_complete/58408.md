dubitative:/ˈduːbɪteɪtɪv/ /ˈdaʊbɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
怀疑的，犹豫的
Expressing or characterized by doubt.
He gave me a dubitative look when I told him my plan. 他听了我的计划后，给了我一个怀疑的眼神。
She spoke in a dubitative tone, unsure of her answer.  她说话的语气犹豫，对自己的答案不确定。
近义词：doubtful, hesitant, uncertain, skeptical
反义词：certain, sure, confident, convinced
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *dubitativus, from Latin dubitare "to doubt," from dubius "doubtful."
