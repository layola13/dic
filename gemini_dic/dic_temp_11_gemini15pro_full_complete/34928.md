cloaked:/kloʊkt/| adj.|披着斗篷的，隐藏的，秘密的|Covered with or as if with a cloak; disguised or hidden.|The spy's true intentions were cloaked in secrecy.  间谍的真正意图隐藏在秘密之中。|The wizard was cloaked in a long, dark robe.  巫师披着一件长长的黑色袍子。|近义词：hidden, concealed, masked, disguised, veiled|反义词：revealed, exposed, open, unconcealed|词性:形容词
词源: From the noun cloak + -ed.  来自名词cloak + -ed。 Cloak本身来自古法语 cloke，最终源自晚期拉丁语 *clocca “旅行斗篷”。
