countertenor:/ˈkaʊntərˌtenər/| n.|男高音，假声男高音|A male singer with a voice in the alto or soprano range, usually produced by falsetto.|The countertenor's voice soared above the orchestra.  男高音的歌声在管弦乐队之上翱翔。|近义词：alto, male soprano|反义词:bass, baritone|词性:noun

Etymology:
From counter- + tenor, from Old French *contre-tenor (unattested), modelled on Medieval Latin contratenor "one who sings against the tenor", from Latin contra "against" + tenor "a course, progress, tenor".
