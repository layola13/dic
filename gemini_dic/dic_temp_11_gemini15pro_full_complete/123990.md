morne:/mɔːrn/ | adj. | 凄凉的，阴郁的，荒凉的 | Dreary, gloomy, desolate, especially in appearance or character.  | The morne landscape stretched before them, a testament to the devastating drought.  这片凄凉的景象展现在他们面前，证明了这场毁灭性的干旱。

The old house had a morne atmosphere, as if shrouded in perpetual twilight.  这栋老房子有一种阴郁的气氛，仿佛永远笼罩在薄暮之中。 | 近义词: gloomy, dismal, bleak, desolate | 反义词: cheerful, bright, vibrant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French morne, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Old French morne “muzzle, snout” (hence, “sullen”), or to Old Occitan morn “blunt, dull.”
