obviousnesses:/ˈɑːbviəsnɪsɪz/ |n.|明显性；显而易见之处|The obviousnesses of the situation were overlooked.  这种情况的明显之处被忽略了。|The obviousnesses of his guilt were apparent to everyone.  他明显的罪行对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|synonyms: clarity, conspicuousness, evidence, patency |antonyms: obscurity, subtlety, vagueness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From obvious + -ness. Obvious comes from the Late Latin *obvius, from Latin obviam (in the way, meeting).
