hemline: /ˈhemlaɪn/ | n. |（裙子等的）长度；（价格、比率等的）水平|A particular level or limit. |  Stock prices continued to fall, testing new hemlines. 股价继续下跌，探底新低。| synonyms: level, limit, boundary | antonyms: peak, apex | noun
