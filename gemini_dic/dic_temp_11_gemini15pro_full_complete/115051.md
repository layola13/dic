mandrel:/ˈmændrəl/ (US), /ˈmændrəl/ (UK)| n.| 心轴，芯棒，顶针|A shaft or bar inserted into a hollow work piece to support it during machining or forming.  A metal rod on which something is shaped or formed.|

The blacksmith used a mandrel to shape the horseshoe. 铁匠用芯棒来塑造马蹄铁。

The glassblower used a mandrel to create the delicate vase. 玻璃吹制工用芯棒制作精美的花瓶。

|近义词：arbor, spindle, shaft, rod|反义词: N/A|词性: noun|
