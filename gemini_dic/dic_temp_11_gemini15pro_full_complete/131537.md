noncommendable:/ˌnɑːnˌkɑːˈmɛndəbəl/
adjective
不值得称赞的，不值得推荐的
Not deserving praise or recommendation; not commendable.
His noncommendable behavior led to his dismissal.  他的不值得称赞的行为导致他被解雇。
The restaurant's noncommendable service received many complaints. 这家餐厅糟糕的服务收到了很多投诉。

近义词:undesirable, objectionable, unacceptable, reprehensible, blameworthy
反义词:commendable, laudable, praiseworthy
词性:adjective
