mimed:/maɪmd/ | v. | 模仿；哑剧表演 | to act or tell something using only actions, not words | He mimed eating a banana. 他模仿吃香蕉的动作。|  acted, imitated, simulated | spoke, vocalized | verb


mime:/maɪm/ | n. | 哑剧；哑剧演员 | a play or other dramatic entertainment in which the actors use actions and gestures but no words; an actor who performs in mimes | The mime performed a scene about waiting for a bus.  这位哑剧演员表演了一个等公共汽车的场景。|  pantomime, charade | speech, dialogue | noun
