citrus:/ˈsɪtrəs/| n. adj.|柑橘属植物；柑橘类水果|A tree of the rue family that produces citrus fruit, such as oranges, lemons, grapefruits, and limes. Also, the fruit of any of these trees.|例句：The citrus trees in the orchard were laden with fruit. 果园里的柑橘树上果实累累。|例句：She enjoys the refreshing taste of citrus fruits. 她喜欢柑橘类水果清爽的味道。|近义词：lemon, orange, grapefruit, lime|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-citrus fruit)|词性: noun, adjective
