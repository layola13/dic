equitum:/ˈekʷɪtʊm/
noun
骑兵，骑士 (复数)
Genitive plural of eques, meaning "of the horsemen/knights".  It refers to a group of Roman cavalrymen or knights.
The consul addressed the equitum before the battle. 领事在战斗前向骑兵们讲话。
The bravery of the equitum was legendary. 骑兵的英勇是传奇性的。
synonyms: horsemen, cavalry, knights
antonyms: infantry, foot soldiers
