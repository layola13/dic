outgunned:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌnd/
adjective
火力不足的，武器装备不如对方的
defeated or surpassed by someone with superior weapons or resources
The small police force was completely outgunned by the heavily armed robbers. 这支小型警察部队的武器装备完全不如全副武装的劫匪。
outmatched, overpowered
surpassing, superior
adjective
