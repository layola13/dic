nosological:/ˌnɒsəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 病因学的，疾病分类学的 | Relating to nosology; classifying diseases. |  例句：The nosological classification of mental disorders is constantly evolving. 精神疾病的疾病分类学分类正在不断发展。

例句：His research focuses on the nosological challenges of diagnosing autoimmune diseases. 他的研究重点是诊断自身免疫性疾病的疾病分类学挑战。


| 近义词：classificatory, taxonomic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *nosos* (disease) + *-logia* (study of).
