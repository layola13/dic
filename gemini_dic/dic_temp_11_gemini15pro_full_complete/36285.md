coheiresses:/koʊˈɛərɪsɪz/
noun

A female who is a joint heir with one or more other persons.
The two sisters were coheiresses to their father's vast estate. 这两姐妹是她们父亲庞大遗产的共同继承人。

The coheiresses will split the inheritance equally. 这些女性共同继承人将平分遗产。

synonym: joint heirs, co-inheritors
antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "disinherited")
