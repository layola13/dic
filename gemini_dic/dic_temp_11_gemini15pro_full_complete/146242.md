overexpressiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspresivnəs/
n.|过度表达; 过分表现|The state or quality of expressing oneself excessively or dramatically.  This can manifest in exaggerated emotions, overly enthusiastic speech, or dramatic gestures.|Her overexpressiveness can be a bit overwhelming at times. 她的过度表达有时会让人难以招架。|Synonyms:  effusiveness, exuberance, demonstrativeness|Antonyms:  reserve, restraint, inhibition|noun
