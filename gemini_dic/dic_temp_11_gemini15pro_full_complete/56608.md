doncella:/donˈθeʎa/| n.|少女，闺女，未婚女子|A young unmarried woman; a maiden.|例句：The doncella was known for her beauty and grace.  这位少女以她的美丽和优雅而闻名。

例句：The king's doncella was chosen to be his queen. 国王的闺女被选为王后。|近义词：maiden, damsel, girl, lass|反义词：matron, crone|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Spanish *donzella, diminutive of dueña (“lady, mistress”), from Latin domina (“lady, mistress”)
