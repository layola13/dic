outbreast:/aʊtˈbrɛst/
verb
超过，胜过
surpass, excel, outdo, especially in courage or daring.
He outbreasted all competitors in the race. 他在比赛中胜过所有对手。
近义词: surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip, outvie
反义词: lag behind, fall behind
词性: verb


outbreast:/aʊtˈbrɛst/
verb
挺胸面对
face boldly or defiantly.
She outbreasted the storm of criticism. 她挺胸面对批评的风暴。
近义词: confront, face, defy, brave
反义词: cower, shrink from, avoid
词性: verb


词源:  "out-" (meaning exceeding) + "breast" (chest, signifying courage and boldness).  Dates back to the 16th century.
