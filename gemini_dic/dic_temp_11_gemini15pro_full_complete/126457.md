mythe:/miːθ/ |n.|神话，虚构的故事|A traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events. | Example: The myth of Icarus is a cautionary tale about the dangers of hubris. 伊卡洛斯的神话是一个关于傲慢危险的警示故事。

Example:  Many myths feature gods and goddesses with extraordinary powers. 许多神话都以拥有非凡力量的神和女神为主角。|Synonym: legend, fable, folklore, story, tale|Antonym: reality, truth, fact|noun
