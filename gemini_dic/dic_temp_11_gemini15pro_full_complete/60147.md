effectuates: /ɪˈfɛktʃueɪts/
verb
使生效；实现；实行
to put into effect; to cause something to happen or be accomplished
The new regulations will be effectuated next month.  新规将于下月生效。
The lawyer effectuated the transfer of the property. 律师使财产转移生效。
synonyms:  carry out, implement, execute, achieve, realize, complete, fulfill
antonyms:  obstruct, hinder, impede, prevent, block
词性: 动词
