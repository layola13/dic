diclinous:/daɪˈklaɪnəs/
adjective
雌雄异株的；单性花的
Having the male and female reproductive organs in separate flowers on separate plants.
例句：Diclinous plants, like holly, require both male and female plants for berry production.  雌雄异株的植物，像冬青，需要雄性和雌性植物才能结出浆果。

例句：The diclinous nature of the willow tree means that some trees produce only male catkins while others produce only female catkins. 柳树的雌雄异株特性意味着有些树只产生雄性柳絮，而另一些树只产生雌性柳絮。

近义词：unisexual, dioecious
反义词：monoclinous, hermaphrodite
词性: adjective
