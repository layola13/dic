osselet:/ˈɒsɪlɪt/ /ˈɑːsɪlɪt/ | n. |小骨，踝骨，玩踝骨游戏|A small bone, especially one of the bones in the ankle or wrist. Also refers to the game played with such bones.  |She found a small osselet on the beach. 她在海滩上发现了一块小踝骨。|  The children were playing osselets in the courtyard. 孩子们在院子里玩踝骨游戏。|Synonym: knucklebone, small bone| Antonym: (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider "large bone" for contrast.) | Noun
