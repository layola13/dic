chickening:/ˈtʃɪkənɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
退缩，胆怯
The act of losing one's courage or backing out of something due to fear.
He was going to bungee jump, but ended up chickening out at the last minute. 他本来打算蹦极跳，但最后却临阵退缩了。
近义词: flinching, balking, withdrawing, backing out
反义词: braving, daring, facing, persevering
词性: verb (present participle)

chickening out:/ˈtʃɪkənɪŋ aʊt/
phrasal verb
临阵退缩，打退堂鼓
To decide not to do something because you are afraid.
I was going to ask her out, but I chickened out. 我本来想约她出去，但我却退缩了。
近义词: backing down, wimping out, losing one's nerve
反义词: going for it, taking the plunge, being brave
词性: phrasal verb
