athleticisms:/æθˈlɛtɪsɪzəmz/
noun
运动能力，体育才能
The sheer athleticisms of the gymnasts was breathtaking. 体操运动员纯粹的运动能力令人叹为观止。
She possesses extraordinary athleticisms. 她拥有非凡的运动能力。
近义词:athletic ability, athletic skill, physical prowess, sportsmanship
反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness
词性:n.


词源: From athleticism + -s (plural suffix) .  "Athleticism" itself comes from "athletic" + "-ism" (suffix forming nouns of quality, state, or practice).  Ultimately from Greek "athletes" (contestant in games) from "athlon" (prize).
