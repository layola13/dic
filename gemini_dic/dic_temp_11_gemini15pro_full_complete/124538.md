mountaintop:/ˈmaʊntɪntɒp/ (UK) /ˈmaʊntənˌtɑːp/ (US)| n. |山顶，山頂|The highest point of a mountain. |例句：They reached the mountaintop at sunrise. 他们在日出时分到达了山顶。

例句：The view from the mountaintop was breathtaking. 从山顶望下去的景色令人叹为观止。

近义词：summit, peak, crest, apex |反义词:valley, base, bottom |词性:noun
