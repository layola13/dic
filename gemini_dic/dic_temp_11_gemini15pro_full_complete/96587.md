inductee:/ɪndʌkˈtiː/
n.
新成员；入选者；被征召者
a person who is formally introduced into an organization or group, especially one that is considered prestigious
The Hall of Fame inductees will be honored at a special ceremony. 名人堂的新成员将在一个特别的仪式上受到表彰。
synonyms: initiate, newcomer, recruit, entrant
antonyms: veteran, old-timer
noun
