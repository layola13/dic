anteflexion:/ˌæntiˈflɛkʃən/
n.
向前屈曲，前屈
the bending forward of an organ or part.
The patient presented with uterine anteflexion.  这位病人表现为子宫前屈。

The anteflexion of the uterus is normal. 子宫前屈是正常的。
synonym: bending forward,  forward curvature
antonym: retroflexion,  backward bending
noun
