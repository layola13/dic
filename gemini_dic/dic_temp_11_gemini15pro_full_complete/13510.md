attain:/əˈteɪn/| v. |获得，达到，实现|to succeed in getting something, usually after a lot of effort|She finally attained her goal of becoming a doctor. 她最终实现了成为一名医生的目标。|He attained the rank of captain. 他晋升到了上尉军衔。|近义词：achieve, accomplish, reach, obtain, gain|反义词:forfeit, lose, miss|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *ateindre*, from Latin *attingere* "to touch, reach, arrive at," from *ad* "to" + *tangere* "to touch."
