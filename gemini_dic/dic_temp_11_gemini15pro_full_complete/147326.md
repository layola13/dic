overmortgaged:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/
adjective
过度抵押的
Having mortgaged one's property for more than it is worth.
The overmortgaged house was eventually repossessed by the bank. 这栋过度抵押的房子最终被银行收回了。
近义词：deeply in debt, heavily mortgaged
反义词：debt-free, unmortgaged
词性: adjective

overmortgaged:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/
verb (past participle)
被过度抵押
The state of having a mortgage larger than the value of the property.
The family found themselves overmortgaged after the housing market crash. 房市崩盘后，这家人发现自己过度抵押了。

反义词：(n/a for verb participle)
