eyot:/ˈaɪət/ | n. |小岛，河中小岛|A small island, especially in a river or lake. |例句：A small eyot in the Thames provided a haven for wildlife. 泰晤士河中的一个小岛为野生动物提供了一个避难所。

例句：We moored our boat alongside the eyot and had a picnic. 我们把船停泊在小岛边上野餐。|近义词：isle, islet, ait |反义词:mainland |词性:noun
