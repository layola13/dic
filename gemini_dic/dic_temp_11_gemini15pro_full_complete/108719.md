leanness:/ˈliːnnəs/| n. | 瘦；精干；贫瘠；缺乏 |The state of being lean; thinness, especially from lack of fat; containing little or no fat.  Also, a lack of richness or abundance. |The leanness of the meat made it a healthy choice.  这块肉很瘦，因此是健康的选择。|近义词：thinness, slenderness, meagreness, spareness|反义词: fatness, obesity, richness, abundance|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *lenesse*, from Old English *hlǣnnes*, from *hlǣne* ("lean").
