misclassifying: /ˌmɪsˈklæsɪfaɪɪŋ/
Verb
错误分类，分类错误
The act of incorrectly assigning something or someone to a category.
The algorithm is misclassifying spam as legitimate email.  该算法将垃圾邮件错误地归类为合法邮件。
They are misclassifying workers as independent contractors to avoid paying benefits. 他们将工人错误地归类为独立承包商，以避免支付福利。
Synonyms: mislabeling, mistyping, categorizing incorrectly
Antonyms: classifying correctly, categorizing accurately
词性: 动词
