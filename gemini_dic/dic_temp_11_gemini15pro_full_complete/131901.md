noncontending:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtɛndɪŋ/
adjective
非竞争性的；无竞争力的；不争胜负的
Not competing or contending; not actively seeking to win or gain something.
The noncontending team played just for fun. 这支无竞争力的队伍只是为了好玩而比赛。
Synonyms: noncompetitive, uncompetitive, out of the running, not in contention
Antonyms: contending, competitive, vying, in the running
词性: 形容词
