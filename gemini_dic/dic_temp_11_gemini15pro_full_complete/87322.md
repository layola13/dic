herniotomy:/ˈhɜːrniˌɒtəmi/ (US) /ˈhɜːniˌɒtəmi/ (UK)| n.|疝修补术|Surgical repair of a hernia, typically involving pushing the herniated tissue back into place and reinforcing the weakened area of the abdominal wall.|The surgeon performed a herniotomy to repair the patient's inguinal hernia.  外科医生进行了疝修补术以修复病人的腹股沟疝。|Synonyms: herniorrhaphy|Antonyms: N/A|noun
