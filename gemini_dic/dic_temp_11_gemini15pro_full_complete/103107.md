jerichos: /ˈdʒɛrɪˌkoʊz/
词性: n.
中文翻译:耶利哥城（ Jericho 的复数形式）;  Jericho trumpets; 耶利哥喇叭
英文解析: Plural form of Jericho, a city in the West Bank. Can also refer to Jericho trumpets, a type of loud horn.
例句：The walls of the Jerichos crumbled. 耶利哥城的城墙倒塌了。

例句：The Jerichos sounded, announcing the arrival of the king. 耶利哥喇叭吹响，宣告国王的到来。

近义词: cities (when referring to the city itself) , trumpets, horns (when referring to Jericho trumpets)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a city name or a specific type of horn)
