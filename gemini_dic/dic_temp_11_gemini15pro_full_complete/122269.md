mitigates:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪts/| v. |减轻，缓和| Makes something less severe, serious, or painful.|例句：The medicine mitigates the pain.  这药减轻了疼痛。|近义词：alleviates, lessens, eases, diminishes, reduces|反义词:aggravates, exacerbates, intensifies|verb
