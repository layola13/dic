overhauler:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːlər/| n. |大修者；彻底检修者；革新者|A person who overhauls something, especially a machine or a system.|The engine overhauler discovered a major problem with the pistons.  发动机大修人员发现活塞存在重大问题。|Near Synonyms:  renovator, restorer, reformer, repairer|Near Antonyms: damager, saboteur|Noun

overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/| vt. |彻底检修；大修；改造；革新|To repair or improve something so that every part of it is in good condition; to make a thorough examination of a system and make changes to it in order to improve it.|They overhauled the engine of the car. 他们彻底检修了汽车的发动机。
The government plans to overhaul the healthcare system. 政府计划彻底改革医疗保健体系。|Near Synonyms: renovate, restore, refurbish, revamp, reform|Near Antonyms: damage, neglect, worsen|Verb
