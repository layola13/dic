bouffant:/buˈfɑːnt/ /buˈfænt/| adj. n.|蓬松的，鼓起的；蓬松发型|A bouffant hairstyle is one that is full and rounded, often styled with backcombing or teasing. |Her bouffant hairdo made her look taller.  她的蓬松发型让她看起来更高了。|She wore her hair in a bouffant style. 她梳了一个蓬松的发型。|近义词：full, puffy, voluminous|反义词：flat, sleek, smooth|词性:形容词, 名词|词源: From French *bouffant*, present participle of *bouffer*  'to puff, swell'.
