droughtiest: /ˈdraʊtiɪst/ | adj. | 最干旱的 | Superlative form of droughty: experiencing or susceptible to drought. Characterized by a prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water. | The droughtiest region in the country is experiencing severe water restrictions.  这个国家最干旱的地区正在经历严格的用水限制。 | Synonyms: aridest, driest, parched | Antonyms: wettest, soggiest | adjective
