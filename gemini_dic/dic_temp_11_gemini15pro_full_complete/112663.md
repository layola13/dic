lowercase:/ˈloʊərˌkeɪs/ | adj. n. | 小写的；小写字母 |  Of or relating to small letters, as opposed to capitals. Also, a small letter itself.  |  Please write your name in lowercase. 请用小写字母写你的名字。 | synonym: minuscule, small letter | antonym: uppercase, capital, majuscule | adjective, noun
