bunk:/bʌŋk/| n. vi.|床铺；铺位；废话；胡说；逃学；躲藏|A narrow bed that is fixed to a wall, especially on a ship, train, or in a prison.  Nonsense.  To sleep in a bunk or makeshift bed.  To stay away from school or work without permission.|例句：The children slept in bunks. 孩子们睡在双层床上。

例句：He dismissed her story as a load of bunk. 他认为她的故事一派胡言。

近义词：bed, berth, cot, nonsense, rubbish, baloney|反义词:sense, truth|词性:noun, verb
