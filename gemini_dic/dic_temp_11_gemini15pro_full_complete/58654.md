dumbos:/ˈdʌmboʊz/
| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜（复数）|A person or people who are considered stupid or foolish.  It's an informal and often derogatory term.|Those dumbos left the keys in the car! 那些笨蛋把钥匙落在车里了！|Synonyms: idiots, fools, nitwits|Antonyms: geniuses, intellectuals|Noun
