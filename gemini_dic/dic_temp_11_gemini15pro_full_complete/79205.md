glandage:/ˈɡlændɪdʒ/
noun
橡子饲养;橡子;橡树果实;橡子产量
The feeding of animals (especially swine) in the woods, where they eat acorns and other mast.  The acorns or other mast thus eaten. The yield of acorns.
例句：The farmers relied on glandage to fatten their pigs before slaughter. 农民们依靠橡子饲养来在宰杀前育肥他们的猪。

例句：The heavy glandage this year promises a good supply of pork. 今年丰富的橡子产量预示着猪肉供应充足。
近义词：mast, pannage
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "stall-feeding" as a contrasting practice)
词性:n.
