charted:/ˈtʃɑːtɪd/ | adj. |  已绘制地图的；已探明的；计划好的 | mapped; explored and recorded; planned |  例句：The expedition set off to explore uncharted territory. 探险队出发去探索未知的领域。 | 近义词：mapped, surveyed, explored, planned, outlined | 反义词：uncharted, unknown, unexplored | 词性:形容词

charted:/ˈtʃɑːtɪd/ | v. | 绘制…的地图；规划；记述；跟踪；详细记录 | to make a map of an area; to plan a course of action; to record or describe something in detail; to follow or track something; to document something carefully | 例句：They charted a new course for the company. 他们为公司规划了新的方向。 | 近义词：map, survey, plot, plan, outline, record, document, track | 反义词： -- | 词性:动词 (charted是chart的过去分词和过去式)


词源: From the verb "chart," which comes from Old North French "charte" (map, card), ultimately from Latin "charta" (paper, papyrus).
