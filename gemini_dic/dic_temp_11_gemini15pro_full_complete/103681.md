joltiest:/ˈdʒoʊltiɪst/
adjective
最颠簸的
Superlative adjective of jolty: most jolty.  Characterized by sudden, sharp, and repeated movements.
The joltiest ride I've ever been on was in a jeep over unpaved mountain roads. 我坐过最颠簸的车是在一辆吉普车上，行驶在未铺砌的山路上。
The old wooden roller coaster provided the joltiest, yet most thrilling, ride in the park.  这座古老的木制过山车提供了公园里最颠簸、但也最刺激的旅程。
jolting, bumpy, rough, jarring
smooth, comfortable, steady
词性: 形容词
