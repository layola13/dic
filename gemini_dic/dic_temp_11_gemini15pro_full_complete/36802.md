colonial: /kəˈlɒʊniəl/ (British) /kəˈloʊniəl/ (American)| adj. |殖民的，殖民地的|Relating to or characteristic of a colony or colonies.|例句：The building has a distinct colonial style. 这座建筑具有明显的殖民地风格。

例句：Many colonial powers exploited the resources of their colonies. 许多殖民列强都剥削其殖民地的资源。

近义词：imperial, dependent|反义词：independent, sovereign|词性: 形容词
