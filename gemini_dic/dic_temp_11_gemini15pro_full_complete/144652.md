outwrench:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃ/
verb
用力扭，猛扭；用力扳，猛扳
To wrench out or away with great force.
He outwrenched the steering wheel, trying to avoid the collision. 他猛扳方向盘，试图避免碰撞。
近义词:wrest, wrench, tear
反义词:insert, implant
词性:v.
