acrocephalic: /ˌækrəʊsəˈfælɪk/ /ˌækroʊsəˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 尖头的，头颅高的 | Having a high, pointed skull. |  Acrocephalic babies often require special medical care. 尖头婴儿通常需要特殊的医疗护理。

| The acrocephalic shape of the skull is a distinctive feature of this breed of dog.  这种狗的头骨尖头形状是其显著特征。 | 近义词: oxycephalic, turricephalic | 反义词: brachycephalic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *akros* "topmost, outermost, extreme" + *kephalē* "head."
