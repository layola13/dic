PHRASE->
kage bunshin no jutsu (Japanese) 影分身の術 (kage bunshin no jutsu)  Shadow Clone Technique (忍者常用的招式)
kage-musha 影武者 (kagemusha)  body double, political decoy
kage wo kakusu  影を隠す (kage o kakusu) to hide oneself; to conceal oneself; to go into hiding
kage ga usui 影が薄い (kage ga usui)  inconspicuous; low profile; unremarkable; bland; having little presence
kageguchi  陰口 (kageguchi)  backbiting; speaking ill of someone behind their back; slander


注意事项:

由于 kage 是一个日语借词，主要用于指代日本文化中的“影子”或“分身”概念。在其他语境中，更常用 shadow。
