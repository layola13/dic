caleb:/ˈkeɪləb/| n.|卡莱布（男子名）|A masculine given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "whole-hearted" or "devotion to God." It is derived from the Hebrew name כָּלֵב (Kalev).|Caleb was one of the twelve spies sent by Moses to explore the land of Canaan. 卡莱布是摩西派去探索迦南地的十二名探子之一。

Caleb is a popular name for boys in the United States. 卡莱布是美国男孩的流行名字。|近义词: (For meaning, not as a name) faithful, devoted, loyal|反义词: (For meaning, not as a name) disloyal, unfaithful|词性:noun
