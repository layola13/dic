orderliness:/ˈɔːrdərlɪnɪs/
n.
整洁；秩序井然
the quality or state of being orderly
The orderliness of her desk impressed her boss. 她桌子上的整洁给她的老板留下了深刻的印象。

近义词:tidiness, neatness, organization, system
反义词:disorder, chaos, messiness, untidiness
词性:名词
