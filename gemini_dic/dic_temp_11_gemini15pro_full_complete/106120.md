knightling: /ˈnaɪtlɪŋ/
n.
年轻的骑士，骑士侍从
A young knight; a knight's page.
The knightling, barely a man, prepared for his first tournament.  这个年轻的骑士，几乎还是个孩子，正准备他的第一次锦标赛。
The knightling swore fealty to his lord. 年轻的骑士向他的领主宣誓效忠。
Synonyms: page, squire, young knight
Antonyms: veteran, old knight
noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *knighteling, equivalent to knight + -ling.  -ling is a diminutive suffix.
