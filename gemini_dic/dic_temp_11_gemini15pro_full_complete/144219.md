outskirmishes:/ˈaʊtˌskɜːrmɪʃɪz/
noun
小规模冲突，前哨战
Minor battles or fights, especially those that take place before or separate from a main battle.
The army engaged in several outskirmishes before the main battle commenced.  军队在主要战役开始前进行了几次小规模冲突。
