monolithic:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|巨大的；整体的；单一的|Formed of a single large block of stone.  Relating to or resembling a monolith, especially in being massive, solid, uniform, and unyielding.|例句：The company's monolithic structure made it slow to adapt to change.  这家公司庞大的结构使其难以适应变化。|近义词：massive, huge, inflexible, rigid, unified, homogenous|反义词:flexible, adaptable, diverse, heterogeneous|词性:形容词

monolithic:|/ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪθɪk/| n.|巨石|A large single upright block of stone, especially one shaped into or serving as a pillar or monument.|例句：The ancient monolithic statues are a marvel of engineering.  这些古老的巨型石像是工程学的奇迹。|近义词：obelisk, megalith|反义词:|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μονόλιθος (monólithos, “made of one stone”), from μόνος (mónos, “alone, single, solitary”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”).
