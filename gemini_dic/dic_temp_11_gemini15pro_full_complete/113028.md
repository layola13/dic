luminance:/ˈluːmɪnəns/| n.|亮度，发光度|The luminance of the screen is adjustable. 屏幕的亮度可调。|例句：The luminance of the monitor was too high, causing eye strain. 显示器的亮度太高，导致眼睛疲劳。

例句：The astronomer measured the luminance of the distant star. 天文学家测量了那颗遥远恒星的亮度。|近义词：brightness, intensity, radiance|反义词：darkness, dimness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *luminantia, from luminant- (stem of luminans), present participle of luminare "to light up," from lumen "light."
