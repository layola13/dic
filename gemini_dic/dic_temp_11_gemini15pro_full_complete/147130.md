overlofty: /ˌoʊvərˈlɔːfti/ | adj. | 过于高傲的，过于崇高的 | Excessively lofty or elevated; pompous or pretentious.  Characterized by an inflated sense of self-importance or superiority. | His overlofty pronouncements alienated his colleagues.  他过于高傲的言论疏远了他的同事。|  The overlofty tone of the speech made the audience uncomfortable. 演讲中过于崇高的语气让观众感到不舒服。 | 近义词：haughty, arrogant, supercilious, pompous, pretentious | 反义词：humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "lofty" (adjective meaning "of imposing height; elevated in character or style").  From Middle English *overloft*, from Old English *oferlyft*.
