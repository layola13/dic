nonbreakable:/ˌnɒnˈbreɪkəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈbreɪkəbl/ | adj. | 不易碎的，牢不可破的 | Not easily broken; designed to resist breakage. | This cup is made of nonbreakable plastic.  这个杯子是用不易碎的塑料制成的。 | The nonbreakable bond between mother and child. 母子之间牢不可破的联系。 | 近义词: shatterproof, unbreakable, durable, tough | 反义词: breakable, fragile, brittle | 词性: 形容词

nonbreakable:/ˌnɒnˈbreɪkəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈbreɪkəbl/ | n. |  不可断裂空格（主要指计算机排版中的用法）| A character or code in digital typography that prevents a line break at its position. |  Use a nonbreakable space between the initials and the last name. 在首字母和姓氏之间使用不可断裂空格。 |  The nonbreakable ensured the name stayed on one line.  不可断裂空格确保了名字在一行中显示。 | 近义词: non-breaking space, no-break space | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but a regular space would be the contrasting element) | 词性: 名词
