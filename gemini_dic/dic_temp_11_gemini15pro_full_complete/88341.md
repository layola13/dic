hippodrome:/ˈhɪpədrəʊm/ (US) /ˈhɪpədroʊm/ (UK)| n. |跑马场；竞技场；古罗马的战车竞技场|An oval track for horse races and chariot races, especially in ancient Greece and Rome.  A building or place for equestrian and other performances.|The hippodrome was packed with cheering spectators. 跑马场挤满了欢呼的观众。|Synonyms:  circus, arena, stadium, track|Antonyms:  None|noun
