obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/| n.|障碍，阻碍，妨碍物|something that blocks or gets in the way of something else; the act of blocking something|The fallen tree was an obstruction in the road.  倒下的树是道路上的障碍物。|The obstruction of justice is a serious crime.  妨碍司法公正是严重的犯罪。|近义词：obstacle, barrier, impediment, hindrance|反义词：aid, assistance, help|noun
