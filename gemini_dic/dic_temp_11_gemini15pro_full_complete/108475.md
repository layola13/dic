lay-on-the-line:/ˌleɪ ɒn ðə ˈlaɪn/| idiom |摊牌，说出真相|To be completely honest and direct, revealing all the facts or opinions, even if they are unpleasant.| He laid it all on the line and told her exactly how he felt. 他摊牌了，告诉了她他的真实感受。|近义词：be upfront, be honest, tell the truth, come clean, level with, speak frankly|反义词：be evasive, beat around the bush, be indirect, conceal, hide|phrase

lay-on-the-line:/ˌleɪ ɒn ðə ˈlaɪn/| idiom |冒风险|To risk something, especially money or reputation.|He laid his job on the line to defend his friend. 他为了维护朋友，冒着丢掉工作的风险。|近义词：risk, gamble, endanger, jeopardize, put at stake|反义词：play it safe, protect, secure|phrase
