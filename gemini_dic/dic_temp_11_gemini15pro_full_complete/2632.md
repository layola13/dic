adoptees:/əˌdɒpˈtiːz/ /əˌdɑːpˈtiːz/
n.
被收养者；被领养人
A person who has been adopted.
例句：The adoptees searched for their biological parents.  被收养者寻找他们的亲生父母。

例句：The support group provides a safe space for adoptees to share their experiences.  这个互助小组为被收养者提供了一个安全的分享经验的空间。

近义词：adopted children, fostered children
反义词: biological children
词性: noun
