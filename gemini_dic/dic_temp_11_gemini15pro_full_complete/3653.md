aggeration:/ɪˌɡædʒəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 夸大；夸张；言过其实 | An exaggeration is a statement that represents something as better or worse than it really is. | He told us a story full of exaggerations. 他给我们讲了一个充满夸张的故事。|  synonyms: overstatement, hyperbole, magnification, amplification | antonyms: understatement, minimization, downplaying | noun
