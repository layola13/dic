gouge:/ɡaʊdʒ/| n. v.| 凿子；凿槽；欺诈；敲诈|A chisel with a concave blade for scooping or cutting holes in wood.|He used a gouge to carve the design into the wood. 他用凿子把图案刻在木头上。|synonyms: chisel, scoop, extort, swindle|antonyms: donate, give|noun, verb

gouge:/ɡaʊdʒ/| v.| 挖出；凿出；欺诈；敲诈|To cut or force something out roughly or brutally, especially with a gouge.|The thief gouged out his victim's eyes. 小偷挖出了受害者的眼睛。|synonyms: scoop, excavate, extort, swindle|antonyms: donate, give|verb
