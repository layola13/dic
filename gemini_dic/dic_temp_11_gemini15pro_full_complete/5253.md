allodialism:/ˌæləˈdaɪəlɪzəm/ | n. |完全保有制，自由保有制 |The system of land ownership in which land is held free and clear of any rent or service due to a superior.  A system where land ownership is absolute, not subject to any feudal superior. |Example 1: Allodialism is a cornerstone of property rights in many countries. 完全保有制是许多国家产权的基石。

Example 2: The concept of allodialism contrasts sharply with feudal land tenure. 完全保有制概念与封建土地所有制形成鲜明对比。 | 近义词: freehold, absolute ownership | 反义词: feudalism, leasehold | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From Medieval Latin *allodialis, from Frankish *allōd "full property," from Proto-Germanic *allōdiz "completely owned," from *allaz "all" + *ōþalan "to own."
