escalating: /ˈɛskəleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
逐步升级的；逐步上升的
increasing in intensity, seriousness, or scope
The escalating tension between the two countries led to war.  两国之间日益紧张的局势导致了战争。
The escalating cost of healthcare is a major concern.  医疗保健费用不断上涨是一个主要问题。
近义词: increasing, rising, growing, intensifying, worsening, deteriorating
反义词: decreasing, declining, diminishing, lessening, improving, ameliorating
词性: adjective
词源: From the Latin word "scala" meaning "ladder" or "stairs."  源自拉丁语单词“scala”，意思是“梯子”或“楼梯”。
