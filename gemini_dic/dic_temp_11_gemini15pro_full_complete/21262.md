bloodbaths:/ˈblʌdbɑːθs/
n.
大屠杀，血腥 massacre
A period or event characterized by widespread violence and bloodshed.
The revolution degenerated into a bloodbath. 这场革命演变成了血腥的大屠杀。

bloodbaths:/ˈblʌdbɑːθs/
n.
惨败，溃败 rout
A disastrous and overwhelming defeat or failure.
The election was a bloodbath for the ruling party. 这次选举对执政党来说是一场惨败。

bloodbaths:/ˈblʌdbɑːθs/
n.
血洗
An extensive, often violent, removal or purging of personnel, as in a company or organization.
There were fears of a bloodbath after the merger of the two companies. 两家公司合并后，人们担心会出现一场血洗。


近义词：massacre, slaughter, carnage, butchery
反义词：peace, truce, armistice
词性：noun
