lambasting:/læmˈbeɪstɪŋ/
verb
严厉批评，痛斥
criticizing someone or something severely
The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。
The critic lambasted the movie for its weak plot and unconvincing acting.  评论家严厉批评了这部电影的薄弱情节和不令人信服的演技。

近义词
criticizing, condemning, denouncing, berating, castigating, excoriating, upbraiding
反义词
praising, commending, lauding, applauding
词性
verb (动名词形式)


词源:
From the verb "lambaste," which originated in the early 19th century.  It's likely a blend of "lam" (to beat) and "baste" (to beat with a stick).
