atomised:/ˈætəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 原子化的，雾化的，分散的 | Reduced to atoms or minute particles; fragmented or dispersed.  Also, figuratively, utterly destroyed or defeated. | The paint was atomised and sprayed onto the car. 油漆被雾化并喷到车上。| The enemy's defenses were atomised by the relentless bombing. 敌人的防御工事被无情的轰炸彻底摧毁了。 | synonyms: vaporized, fragmented, dispersed, shattered, pulverized | antonyms: unified, combined, consolidated, whole | adjective

atomise:/ˈætəmaɪz/ | v. |  原子化，雾化，分散 | To reduce to atoms or minute particles; to fragment or disperse. Also, figuratively, to utterly destroy or defeat. | The perfume atomises into a fine mist. 香水雾化成细小的薄雾。 | The explosion atomised the building. 爆炸使建筑物化为齑粉。 | synonyms: vaporize, fragment, disperse, shatter, pulverize | antonyms: unify, combine, consolidate | verb

词源: From atom + -ize.  Ultimately from Greek *atomos* "indivisible".
