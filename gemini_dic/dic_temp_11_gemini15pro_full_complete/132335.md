nondeformity:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɔːmɪti/
noun
无畸形；不变形
The absence of deformity; the state of being normal in shape or form.
The surgeon confirmed the nondeformity of the limb after the cast was removed.  外科医生在拆除石膏后确认了肢体没有畸形。
The baby's nondeformity was a relief to the parents. 婴儿没有畸形，这让父母松了一口气。
synonyms: normality, soundness, integrity, wholeness
antonyms: deformity, malformation, distortion
