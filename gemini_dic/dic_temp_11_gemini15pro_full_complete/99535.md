intergossip:/ˈɪntərˌɡɑːsɪp/
n.|闲聊，流言蜚语|The act of gossiping among a group of people.|They spent the afternoon in intergossip, discussing the latest office rumors. 他们一下午都在闲聊，讨论办公室最新的流言蜚语。|近义词：chatter, gossip, banter|反义词:silence, reticence|词性:名词

词源:  'inter-' (between) + 'gossip' (idle talk)
