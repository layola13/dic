overexpectantly:/ˌōvərɪkˈspektəntli/| adv.|过分期待地，期望过高地|In a manner characterized by excessive or unrealistic expectations.  It describes an action performed with too much hope or anticipation, often leading to disappointment.|She waited overexpectantly for his call, her heart pounding with each ring. 她满怀期待地等着他的电话，每次铃声响起，她的心都怦怦直跳。|He looked overexpectantly at the unopened present. 他满怀期待地看着未打开的礼物。|近义词: hopefully, eagerly, anxiously|反义词: apathetically, indifferently|词性:副词

词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "expectant" (having expectations) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
