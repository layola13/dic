calceated: /ˈkælsieɪtɪd/
adjective
穿鞋的；戴着鞋的
Wearing shoes; shod.
The calceated individual stepped gingerly across the muddy field.  穿鞋的人小心翼翼地走过泥泞的田野。
近义词: shod, footed
反义词: barefoot, unshod
词性: adjective

calceate: /ˈkælsieɪt/
verb
给…穿鞋
To furnish with shoes.
He calceated his feet before venturing outside. 他在冒险外出之前穿上了鞋子。
近义词: shoe
反义词: unshoe
词性: verb

词源: From Latin *calceatus, past participle of calceare "to shoe," from calceus "shoe."
