hedger:/ˈhedʒər/| n.|套期保值者；规避风险者|A person or company that tries to reduce the risks of future price changes by buying or selling contracts for future delivery.|The company is a major hedger in the global oil market.  这家公司是全球石油市场的主要套期保值者。|Synonyms: speculator, arbitrageur|Antonyms: gambler|noun

hedger:/ˈhedʒər/| v.|进行套期保值；规避（风险）；避免正面回答|To protect oneself against financial loss by buying or selling futures contracts or other financial instruments.|The airline hedged against rising fuel prices. 这家航空公司对冲了不断上涨的燃油价格。|Synonyms: safeguard, insure, protect|Antonyms: risk, expose|verb
