bawler:/ˈbɔːlər/| n.|大声哭叫的人；爱哭鬼|A person who cries loudly or frequently, especially a child.|The little bawler wouldn't stop crying until he got his candy.  这个爱哭鬼直到拿到糖果才停止哭泣。|Synonyms: crybaby, whiner, sniveler|Antonyms: stoic|noun

bawl:/bɔːl/| v.|大声哭；放声痛哭；大声叫喊|To cry loudly and unrestrainedly; to shout or call out loudly.|The baby bawled all night. 婴儿哭了一整夜。|Synonyms: weep, wail, yell, shout|Antonyms: whisper, murmur|verb
