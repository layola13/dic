europeanization:/jʊərəpiːənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|欧洲化|The process by which a country or region adopts the social, political, and economic norms and practices of Europe.|例句：The europeanization of Turkey has been a long and complex process. 土耳其的欧洲化是一个漫长而复杂的过程。

例句：The impact of europeanization on national identity is a subject of ongoing debate. 欧洲化对国家认同的影响是一个持续争论的话题。|近义词：westernization, integration|反义词：isolation, nationalism|词性：名词
