outcompliment: /ˌaʊtˈkɒmplɪmənt/ (British) /ˌaʊtˈkɑːmplɪmənt/ (American)| v. | 超越，胜过（在赞美方面）| To surpass someone in giving compliments; to compliment someone more or better than someone else. | He outcomplimented everyone at the party with his charming words. 他在派对上用他迷人的话语胜过了所有人。|  She outcomplimented me by praising not only my dress but also my shoes and hairstyle. 她不仅称赞了我的裙子，还称赞了我的鞋子和发型，胜过了我。 | synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel | antonyms:  understate, downplay | verb
