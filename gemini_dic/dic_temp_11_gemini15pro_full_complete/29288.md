cast pearls before swine:/kæst pɜːrlz bɪˈfɔːr swaɪn/ | idiom | 对牛弹琴；把有价值的东西给不识货的人 | To offer something precious to someone who cannot appreciate its value. |  例句：Giving him that expensive wine was like casting pearls before swine; he wouldn't know a good vintage from vinegar.  给他那瓶昂贵的葡萄酒就像对牛弹琴；他根本分不清好年份的酒和醋。| 近义词：waste one's breath, preach to the deaf | 反义词: find the right audience, share with appreciative people | idiom

cast:/kæst/ | v. n. adj. | 投掷，演员阵容，铸造的 | throw (something) forcefully in a specified direction. |  例句：He cast the fishing line into the river. 他把鱼线抛进河里。| 近义词：throw, fling, hurl | 反义词: catch, receive | verb
pearls:/pɜːrlz/ | n. | 珍珠 | a hard, lustrous spherical gem formed within the soft tissue of a living shelled mollusk. |  例句：She wore a necklace of beautiful pearls. 她戴着一条漂亮的珍珠项链。| 近义词：gems, jewels | 反义词: - | noun
before:/bɪˈfɔːr/ | prep. adv. conj. | 在...之前 | during the period of time preceding (a particular event, date, or time). |  例句：I arrived before the meeting started. 我在会议开始前到达。| 近义词：prior to, earlier than | 反义词: after | preposition, adverb, conjunction
swine:/swaɪn/ | n. | 猪 | a pig. |  例句：The farmer raised swine for meat. 农民养猪是为了吃肉。| 近义词：pig, hog | 反义词: - | noun
