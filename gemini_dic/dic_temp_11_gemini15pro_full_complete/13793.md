audiologists:/ˌɔːdiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 听力学家；听力专家 | A healthcare professional specializing in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of hearing and balance disorders. |  Example 1: Audiologists conduct hearing tests to assess the extent of hearing loss.  听力学家进行听力测试以评估听力损失的程度。

Example 2: My grandmother consults an audiologist regularly for her hearing aids. 我祖母定期咨询听力学家关于她的助听器。 | 近义词: hearing specialist, hearing doctor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

audiologist:/ˌɔːdiˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 听力学家；听力专家 |  Derived from "audiology" (the study of hearing) + "-ist" (denoting a specialist).  "Audiology" comes from Latin "audīre" (to hear) + Greek "-logia" (study of). | Example 1:  The audiologist recommended hearing aids for my father. 听力学家建议我父亲佩戴助听器。

Example 2:  She became an audiologist after witnessing the impact of hearing loss on her family.  在目睹了听力损失对她家庭的影响后，她成为了一名听力学家。| 近义词: hearing specialist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
