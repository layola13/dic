adaptive /əˈdæptɪv/ | adj. | 适应的，适应性的 | Able to change or be changed in order to deal successfully with new situations. |例句：Adaptive learning software adjusts to the student's pace.  适应性学习软件会根据学生的进度进行调整。

例句：Animals in the desert have adaptive mechanisms for conserving water. 沙漠中的动物有适应性的机制来保存水分。 | 近义词：flexible, adjustable, adaptable, versatile | 反义词：inflexible, rigid, unadaptable, fixed | 词性: 形容词
