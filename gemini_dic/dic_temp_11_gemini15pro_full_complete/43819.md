creole:/ˈkriːoʊl/
noun
克里奥尔语；克里奥尔人；克里奥尔文化
A language that has developed from a pidgin and is used as a first language by a community.  A person of mixed European and black descent, especially in the Caribbean.
Haitian Creole is the most widely spoken Creole language. 海地克里奥尔语是使用最广泛的克里奥尔语。
