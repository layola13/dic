frontbenchers:/ˈfrʌntˌbɛntʃərz/ | n. | 前排议员（通常指主要政党在议会中的高级成员） |  Members of Parliament who sit on the front benches in the House of Commons or House of Lords, usually senior members of the governing or opposition parties. | The frontbenchers were locked in a heated debate. 前排议员们正在进行激烈的辩论。 | The Prime Minister reshuffled the frontbenchers. 首相改组了前排议员。 | 近义词：leading members, senior politicians | 反义词：backbenchers | 词性：名词
