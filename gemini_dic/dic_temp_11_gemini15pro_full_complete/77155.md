gasholder:/ˈɡæshəʊldər/ (UK) /ˈɡæshˌoʊldər/ (US)| n. |煤气罐，煤气贮存器|A large container for storing gas, typically one with a telescopic upper section that rises and falls according to the amount of gas inside.|The gasholder dominated the skyline of the industrial town.  煤气罐在工业城镇的天际线上占据主导地位。
