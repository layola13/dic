defenselessness:/dɪˈfɛnsləsnəs/| n.|无助，无力自卫，毫无防御能力|The defenselessness of the newborn kittens was heartbreaking.  新生小猫的无助令人心碎。|近义词：vulnerability, helplessness, weakness, impotence|反义词：protection, security, strength, power|词性:名词

Etymology: From "defenseless" + "-ness". "Defenseless" comes from "defense" (from Old French "defense") + "-less".  "Defense" ultimately derives from Latin "defendere" (to ward off).
