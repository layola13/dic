banović:/ˈbɑːnɔːvɪtʃ/ /ˈbænoʊvɪtʃ/ | n. | 村长; 巴诺维奇（姓氏） | A title used in some Slavic countries, similar to "village headman" or sometimes a term of respect for a prominent person in a village.  Also a surname. | Example: The banović welcomed the visitors to the village.  村长欢迎来访者来到村庄。

Example:  Mr. Banović is a respected member of the community. 巴诺维奇先生是社区里受人尊敬的成员。 | Synonym: headman, village elder, chief (in some contexts) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a title/surname) | Noun
