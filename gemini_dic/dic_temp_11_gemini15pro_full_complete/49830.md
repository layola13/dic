demonstratively:/ˌdemənˈstreɪtɪvli/
adverb
公开地；明显地；示范性地
in a way that clearly shows a particular feeling or quality
She wept demonstratively. 她放声大哭。
Synonyms: openly, plainly, evidently, obviously, visibly, strikingly, markedly, noticeably, conspicuously, patently, unmistakably, undeniably
Antonyms: subtly, quietly, secretly
词性: 副词
