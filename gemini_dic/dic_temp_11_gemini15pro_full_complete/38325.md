conceit:/kənˈsiːt/ /kɑːnˈsiːt/| n.|自负，骄傲；奇想，巧妙的构思|An excessive pride in oneself or one's abilities.  A fanciful notion or idea.|He was puffed up with conceit. 他自负得不得了。|Synonyms: vanity, arrogance, pride, egotism|Antonyms: humility, modesty|noun

conceit:/kənˈsiːt/ /kɑːnˈsiːt/| v.|幻想，认为|To form or hold an opinion.|He conceited himself to be a genius. 他自以为是天才。|Synonyms: fancy, imagine, believe|Antonyms: doubt, distrust|verb
