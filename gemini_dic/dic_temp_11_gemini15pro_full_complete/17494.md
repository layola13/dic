bearlike:/ˈbɛərˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像熊的；熊一样的 | Resembling a bear in appearance, actions, or qualities. | The man had a bearlike growl and a thick, shaggy beard.  那个男人有着像熊一样的咆哮声和浓密的胡须。|  The wrestler's bearlike physique intimidated his opponents.  这个摔跤运动员像熊一样强壮的体格吓倒了他的对手们。| 近义词：ursine, bearish | 反义词：N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: bear + like.  "Bear" comes from Old English "bera," and "like" comes from Old English "lic" meaning having the same form as.
