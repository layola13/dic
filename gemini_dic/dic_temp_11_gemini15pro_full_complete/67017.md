expirers:/ɪkˈspaɪrərz/
n.
到期者；期满者
Those whose terms or agreements are coming to an end.
The expirers of the contract will be notified by mail. 合同到期者将通过邮件收到通知。
synonyms: non-renewers, leavers
antonyms: renewers, continuers
noun

expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/
v.
期满；终止；失效
To come to an end; to cease to be valid.
My passport expires next month. 我的护照下个月到期。
synonyms: terminate, cease, lapse
antonyms: renew, extend, continue
verb
