microfibrillar: /ˌmaɪkrəʊfɪˈbrɪlər/ /ˌmaɪkroʊfaɪˈbrɪlər/ | adj. | 微原纤维的 | Composed of or pertaining to microfibrils, which are very fine fibrils or fiber-like structures. |  The cell walls of plants contain microfibrillar cellulose. 植物的细胞壁含有微原纤维纤维素. |  The tendon is composed of a microfibrillar network of collagen. 肌腱由胶原蛋白的微原纤维网络组成。 |  fibrous, filamentous | N/A | adjective
