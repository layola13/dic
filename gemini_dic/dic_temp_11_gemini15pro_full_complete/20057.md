biopics:/ˈbaɪoʊpɪks/ | n. |传记片| Biographical motion pictures, often focusing on the life of a famous person. | Example:  The biopic about the famous singer was a box office hit.  这部关于这位著名歌手的传记片是票房热门。

Example:  Many biopics take liberties with the facts of the subject's life. 许多传记片对主人公生平事迹进行了改编。| 近义词: biographical films, biographical dramas | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
