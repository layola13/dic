collar: /ˈkɒlə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːlər (US)/| n. v. |衣领；颈圈；控制；抓住|A band of material around the neck of a shirt, dress, coat, etc., or a separate band worn around the neck, often with a shirt.  To seize someone by the collar.|例句：The dog wore a red collar. 这只狗戴着红色的项圈。|近义词：neckband, lapel|反义词: (For the verb) release, free|词性: noun, verb

collar: /ˈkɒlə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːlər (US)/| v. |抓住；逮捕|To seize someone by the collar; to apprehend someone.|例句：The police officer collared the thief as he tried to escape. 警察抓住小偷，因为他试图逃跑。|近义词：apprehend, arrest, seize|反义词: release, free|词性: verb
