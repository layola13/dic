modder:/ˈmɒdə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdər/ (US)| n. |修改者，模组制作者|A person who modifies something, typically software or video games, to create custom content or features.|Example: The modder created a new level for the game.  这个模组制作者为游戏创建了一个新的关卡。|Example:  Modders are often very creative and skilled programmers. 模组制作者通常都很有创造力而且是技术娴熟的程序员。|近义词：tweaker, hacker (in a positive sense), customizer|反义词: purist, traditionalist|词性:noun
