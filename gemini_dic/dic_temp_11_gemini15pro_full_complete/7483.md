ankling:/ˈæŋklɪŋ/| v. |(尤指缓慢或吃力地)步行，走 |To walk, especially slowly or with difficulty. |例句：He was ankling along the beach. 他正沿着海滩慢慢地走着。

例句：She ankled painfully after twisting her ankle.  她扭伤了脚踝后，一瘸一拐地走得很痛苦。

近义词：walking, strolling, ambling, limping (if injured)|反义词: running, sprinting|词性:动词
