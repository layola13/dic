monotreme:/ˈmɒnətriːm/ | n. | 单孔目动物 | An egg-laying mammal, such as the echidna or platypus, of the order Monotremata.  These are the only mammals that lay eggs. |  The platypus is a fascinating monotreme.  鸭嘴兽是一种迷人的单孔目动物。

The echidna, another monotreme, is covered in spines.  针鼹，另一种单孔目动物，身上覆盖着刺。 | 近义词: prototherian (更学术) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑marsupial (有袋动物) 或 placental mammal (胎盘哺乳动物) 作为对比) | 词性: noun
