coverchief: /ˈkʌvərˌtʃiːf/
n.
头巾，头巾状物
A cloth worn over the head, often as a headdress.
She wore a brightly colored coverchief. 她戴着一块颜色鲜艳的头巾。
Synonyms: head covering, headscarf, kerchief
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly uncovered head)
noun
