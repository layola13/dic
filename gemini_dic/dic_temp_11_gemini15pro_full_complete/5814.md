amasses:/əˈmæsɪz/
verb
积聚，积累
gathers together or accumulates, often in a large quantity over a period of time.
The company amasses a fortune through smart investments.  该公司通过精明的投资积累了巨额财富。
He amasses a large collection of stamps. 他积累了大量的邮票收藏。
synonyms: gathers, accumulates, collects, stockpiles, hoards
antonyms: dissipates, scatters, squanders, distributes, spends
词性:动词
