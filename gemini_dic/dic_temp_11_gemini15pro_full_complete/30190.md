词源: From celestial + -ness + -es.  "Celestial" ultimately derives from Latin "caelestis," meaning "heavenly," related to "caelum," meaning "sky" or "heaven." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition. "-es" is a plural suffix.
