anticynical:/ˌæntiˈsɪnɪkəl/
adjective
反愤世嫉俗的;  不玩世不恭的
Opposed to or rejecting cynicism; hopeful and positive about the future.
She retained an anticynical belief in the goodness of people. 她对人性本善的信念始终未曾动摇，从未变得愤世嫉俗。
Near Synonyms: optimistic, hopeful, positive, idealistic, trusting
Antonyms: cynical, pessimistic, jaded, disillusioned, skeptical
词性: adjective
