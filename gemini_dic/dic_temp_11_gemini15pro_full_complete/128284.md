neuropsychology:/ˌnʊəroʊsaɪˈkɑːlədʒi/ /ˌnjʊərəʊsaɪˈkɒlədʒi/| n. |神经心理学|The study of the relationship between the brain and behavior, including cognitive, emotional, and motor functions.  It investigates how brain damage or dysfunction affects these functions.|Neuropsychology plays a crucial role in understanding the effects of stroke. 神经心理学在理解中风的影响方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：cognitive neuroscience|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
neuro- (from Greek "neuron," meaning "nerve") + psychology (from Greek "psyche," meaning "soul" or "mind," and "logia," meaning "study")
