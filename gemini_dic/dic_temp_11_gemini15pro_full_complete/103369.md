jingang:/ˈdʒɪŋɡæŋ/| n.|金刚|A transliteration of the Sanskrit word "vajra," meaning diamond or thunderbolt. It often refers to a wrathful deity or a guardian figure in Buddhism, particularly Vajrayana Buddhism.|Example: The jingang statue guarded the temple entrance.  金刚塑像守护着寺庙入口。|Example: He meditated on the image of the jingang. 他冥想金刚的形象。|近义词: protector, guardian, vajra|反义词: demon|词性:noun
