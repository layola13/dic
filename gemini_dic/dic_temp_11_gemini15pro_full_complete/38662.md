concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/ (US), /kənˈkʌʃn/ (UK)| n.|脑震荡；震动；冲击|A concussion is a temporary injury to the brain caused by a blow to the head. |He suffered a concussion after falling off his bike. 他从自行车上摔下来后脑震荡。|近义词：impact, shock, trauma|反义词: recovery, healing|noun


concuss:/kənˈkʌs/ (US), /kənˈkʌs/ (UK)| vt.|使脑震荡|To concuss means to cause a concussion.|The fall concussed him. 摔倒使他脑震荡。|近义词：injure, traumatize|反义词: heal, cure|verb
