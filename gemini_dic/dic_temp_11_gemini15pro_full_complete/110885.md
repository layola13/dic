lionly:/ˈlaɪənli/| adj.|像狮子的，雄壮的，勇敢的|Resembling a lion in appearance, action, or sound; majestic; brave.|He walked with a lionly gait, his head held high.  他昂首阔步，步伐像狮子一样威严。|The old warrior let out a lionly roar. 老战士发出像狮子一样的怒吼。|近义词：brave, courageous, majestic, regal, noble|反义词：cowardly, timid, meek|词性:形容词

词源:  From lion + -ly.  由"lion" (狮子) 和形容词后缀 "-ly" 组成。
