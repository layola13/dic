astrolabe:/ˈæstrəˌleɪb/| n.|星盘|An ancient astronomical instrument used for observing and calculating the positions of celestial bodies, typically consisting of a graduated circle with a rotating arm.|Sailors used astrolabes to navigate by the stars. 水手使用星盘根据星星导航。|近义词:armillary sphere, quadrant|反义词:None|noun
