non-traditionalist:/ˌnɒn trəˈdɪʃənəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 非传统主义者；非传统的 |  A person who rejects traditional beliefs, customs, or practices.  Someone who embraces new ideas and methods, especially in contrast to established norms. |  例句：The non-traditionalist artist used unconventional materials in her sculptures.  这位非传统主义艺术家在她的雕塑中使用了非传统的材料。

例句：As a non-traditionalist, she chose to elope rather than have a large wedding.  作为一个非传统主义者，她选择私奔而不是举行盛大的婚礼。 | 近义词：iconoclast, maverick, innovator, reformer, revolutionary, modernist | 反义词：traditionalist, conservative, conformist | 词性: 名词，形容词

non-traditional:/ˌnɒn trəˈdɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非传统的 | Not adhering to or in accordance with tradition.  Departing from established customs or beliefs. | 例句：They opted for a non-traditional wedding ceremony on the beach. 他们在海滩上选择了一个非传统的婚礼仪式。

例句：The company adopted a non-traditional approach to marketing.  这家公司采用了一种非传统的营销方式。| 近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, modern, contemporary, innovative | 反义词：traditional, conventional, orthodox | 词性: 形容词
