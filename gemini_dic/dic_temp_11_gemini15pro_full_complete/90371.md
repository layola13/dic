how-toer:/ˈhaʊˌtuːər/
noun
操作指南撰写者；指导者；实用技巧专家
A person who creates or provides instructions or guidance on how to do something, especially practical skills or tasks.
She's a popular how-toer on YouTube, with videos on everything from gardening to car maintenance. 她是一位YouTube上很受欢迎的操作指南撰写者，视频涵盖了从园艺到汽车保养的各种内容。
instructor, guide, expert, tutor
beginner, novice
