directively:/dɪˈrɛktɪvli/
adv.
指导地，指挥地
in a way that controls or influences something or someone.
The teacher directively managed the classroom activities. 老师指导性地管理课堂活动。
近义词: authoritatively, commandingly, dictatorially
反义词: passively, indirectly, subtly
词性: 副词
