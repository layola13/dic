admitted:/ədˈmɪtɪd/| v. adj.|承认；准许进入；录取|To confess to be true or to be the case; to allow entry, as to a place, fellowship, or the like; to receive as valid, as evidence.|He admitted his mistake. 他承认了他的错误。|Synonyms: acknowledged, confessed, granted, conceded|Antonyms: denied, refuted, rejected|verb

admitted:/ədˈmɪtɪd/| adj.|被承认的；公认的|Acknowledged; confessed; granted.|He is an admitted thief. 他是一个公认的小偷。|Synonyms: acknowledged, confessed, avowed|Antonyms: unacknowledged, denied, unconfessed|adjective
