distress:/dɪˈstrɛs/ | n. vt. | 痛苦，悲伤；危难，困境；（使）痛苦，（使）悲伤 |  A state of extreme sorrow, suffering, or pain; a condition of danger or difficulty; to cause someone to feel extreme sorrow, suffering, or pain. | The ship sent out a distress signal. 这艘船发出了求救信号。 | synonyms: anguish, anxiety, suffering, trouble, hardship | antonyms: comfort, peace, relief, happiness, calmness | noun
distress:/dɪˈstrɛs/ | vt. |（使）痛苦，（使）悲伤 | To cause someone to feel extreme sorrow, suffering, or pain. | It distressed him to see his wife in such pain. 看到妻子如此痛苦，他感到非常难过。 | synonyms: upset, worry, bother, trouble | antonyms: comfort, soothe, please, reassure | verb
