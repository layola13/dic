overresponded:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪd/ | v. | 反应过度 | reacted excessively or more strongly than necessary or appropriate |  The market overresponded to the news, causing a sharp drop in prices. 市场对这消息反应过度，导致价格暴跌。| overreacted, went overboard | underreacted | verb
overresponded:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪd/ | adj. | 反应过度的 | having reacted excessively or more strongly than necessary or appropriate | The child was overresponded to the loud noise and started crying. 孩子对巨大的噪音反应过度，开始哭泣。| oversensitive, hypersensitive | undersensitive, unresponsive | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + respond.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Respond" comes from the Latin "respondēre," meaning "to answer" or "to react."
