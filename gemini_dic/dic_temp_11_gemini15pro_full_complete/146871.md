overinflations: /ˌōvərɪnˈfleɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度膨胀；通货膨胀过度 |The act of inflating something too much, especially the overexpansion of a currency.  It refers to a situation where the money supply increases significantly faster than the supply of goods and services, leading to a rapid increase in prices. |  The Weimar Republic experienced catastrophic overinflations after World War I.  魏玛共和国在第一次世界大战后经历了灾难性的通货膨胀过度。 | overexpansion, inflation | deflation, disinflation | noun
