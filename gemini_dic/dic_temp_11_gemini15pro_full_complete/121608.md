misorganize:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
组织混乱，组织不善
to organize something badly or inefficiently
He had misorganized the files so badly that it took hours to find what we needed. 他把文件整理得乱七八糟，我们花了几个小时才找到需要的东西。
mismanage, disorganize
organize, systematize
v.
