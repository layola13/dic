bitterling: /ˈbɪtərliŋ/ | n. | 欧洲鰟鮍 | A small freshwater fish (Rhodeus amarus) of the carp family, native to Europe and Asia. The female deposits its eggs in the gills of a freshwater mussel, where they are fertilized and develop. |  The bitterling relies on mussels for reproduction.  欧洲鰟鮍依靠贻贝进行繁殖。 |  Bitterlings are fascinating creatures due to their unique reproductive strategy.  鰟鮍由于其独特的繁殖策略而成为引人入胜的生物。 | synonyms:  rose bitterling,  | antonyms: N/A | noun
