hognut:/ˈhɒɡnʌt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡnʌt/ (US)| n. |山核桃；（美）油炸圈饼；（俚）蠢人，笨蛋|A type of hickory tree nut, also known as a pecan in some regions. In the US, it can also refer to a type of deep-fried doughnut. Informally, it can be used as a derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person.|He cracked open the hognut and ate the kernel. 他敲开了山核桃，吃了果仁。

The bakery is famous for its delicious hognuts. 这家面包店以其美味的油炸圈饼而闻名。

Don't be such a hognut! 别这么笨！|近义词：(nut) pecan, hickory nut; (doughnut) donut, friedcake; (person) idiot, fool, moron|反义词：(person) genius, smart person|词性:noun
