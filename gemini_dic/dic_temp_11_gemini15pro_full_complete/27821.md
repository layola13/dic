canny:/ˈkæni/| adj.|精明的，谨慎的，节俭的|Showing good judgment, especially in practical matters; shrewd; careful; thrifty.|She made a canny investment in the stock market. 她精明地在股市进行了投资。|近义词：shrewd, astute, clever, sharp, wise, prudent, cautious|反义词：naive, foolish, gullible, imprudent, extravagant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *cannie* "pleasant, careful, gentle, shrewd," from *can* "to know, have skill in" + *-ie* (diminutive suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kunnan* "to know, be able." Related to English *ken* (obsolete) "to know."
