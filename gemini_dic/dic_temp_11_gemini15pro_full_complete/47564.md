debacle:/dɪˈbɑːkəl/ /deɪˈbɑːkl/
n.
彻底的失败；崩溃；灾难
a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco
The party's annual conference was a complete debacle. 该党的年会彻底失败了。
The economic summit ended in debacle. 经济峰会以失败告终。
近义词:fiasco, disaster, catastrophe, failure, collapse
反义词:success, triumph, victory
词性:名词
