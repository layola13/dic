cicisbeo:/ˌtʃiːtʃɪzˈbeɪoʊ/ /ˌtʃiːtʃɪzˈbeɪəʊ/
n.
男伴，情夫；(尤指已婚妇女的)陪伴，护花使者
A man who is the professed admirer of a married woman; a gallant.
In 18th-century Italy, a married woman would often have a cicisbeo who would escort her to social events and act as her companion.  在18世纪的意大利，已婚妇女通常会有一位cicisbeo陪伴她参加社交活动，并充当她的伴侣。
The countess was rarely seen without her cicisbeo. 女伯爵很少不和她的男伴一起出现。
近义词:lover, gallant, admirer, escort
反义词:husband
词性:noun
