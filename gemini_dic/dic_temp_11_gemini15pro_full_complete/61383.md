emblazoning:/ɪmˈbleɪzənɪŋ/
v.
装饰；纹章装饰；炫耀；使显著
The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上装饰着一只金色的狮子。
His name was emblazoned across the front page of the newspaper. 他的名字赫然印在报纸的头版。
decorate, adorn, ornament
conceal, hide
verb
