loftiness:/ˈlɔːftɪnɪs/
n.
崇高，高尚；高傲，傲慢
The loftiness of his ideals inspired many. 他的崇高理想激励了很多人。
He spoke with a loftiness that alienated some people. 他说话的傲慢态度疏远了一些人。
lofty ideals/principles/sentiments 崇高的理想/原则/情操
hauteur, arrogance, grandeur, nobility, majesty
humility, modesty, humbleness, meekness

ETYMOLOGY->
From lofty + -ness.  Lofty from Middle English *lofti, from Old Norse lopt "air, sky," related to Old English lyft "air, sky."
