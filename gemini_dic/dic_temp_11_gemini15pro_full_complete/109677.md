leucoplast: /ˈluːkəˌplæst/ | n. | 白色体 | A colorless plastid found in plant cells, used for storage of starch, oil, or protein. |  Leucoplasts can differentiate into other plastids, such as chloroplasts. 白色体可以分化成其他质体，例如叶绿体。 |  Amyloplasts are a type of leucoplast that stores starch.  淀粉体是一种储存淀粉的白色体。 | synonyms: plastid | antonyms: chromoplast | noun
