dolabriform:/doʊˈlæbrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|斧形的，似斧状的|Shaped like a hatchet or axe; axe-shaped. |The dolabriform leaves of the plant were its most distinctive feature. 这种植物斧形的叶子是它最显著的特征。|近义词：axe-shaped, hatchet-shaped|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like "lanceolate," "ovate," or "circular")|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dolabr(i)-, from dolabra (“mattock, pickaxe”) + -form.
