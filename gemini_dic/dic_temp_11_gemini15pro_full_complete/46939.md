darnel:/ˈdɑːrnəl/ | n. |毒黑麦，野麦，假小麦|A common weedy annual grass (Lolium temulentum) that grows in grainfields and whose seeds are poisonous. It is often mentioned in the Bible as a symbol of worthlessness.|The field was infested with darnel, choking the wheat.|Synonyms: tare, weed, ryegrass|Antonyms: wheat, grain, beneficial plant|noun

darnel:/ˈdɑːrnəl/ | n. |喻指坏人或有害之物|Something harmful or undesirable.|The politician denounced his opponent as a darnel in the field of public service.|Synonyms: blight, pest, nuisance|Antonyms: asset, benefit, advantage|noun
