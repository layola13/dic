bitonality:/baɪtoʊˈnælɪti/
n.
双调性
The simultaneous use of two different keys or tonalities.
The composer experimented with bitonality, creating a jarring yet intriguing effect.  作曲家尝试了双调性，创造了一种刺耳却又引人入胜的效果。
