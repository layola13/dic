ob-gyns:/ˌɒbˈɡaɪnz/ /ˌɑːbˈɡaɪnz/
noun
妇产科医生
Short for obstetrician-gynecologists, medical doctors specializing in women's reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth.
Many women see their ob-gyns for annual checkups. 许多女性每年都会去看妇产科医生做检查。
obstetrician-gynecologists, OB/GYNs
(No clear antonym for a medical specialist)
n.
