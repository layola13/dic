indecision:/ˌɪndɪˈsɪʒən/| n.|优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The state of being unable to make a decision.|His indecision cost him the opportunity. 他的犹豫不决让他错失了机会。|近义词：hesitation, doubt, vacillation|反义词: decisiveness, resolution|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin indecisio (“irresolution”), from Latin indecisus (“undecided”).  Equivalent to in- (“not”) + decisio (“decision”).
