investing:/ɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/| n.|投资，投资行为|The act of putting money or resources into something with the expectation of profit or material result.|He's considering investing in the stock market. 他正在考虑投资股票市场。|近义词：financing, funding, backing|反义词:divesting, withdrawing|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "invest," which comes from the Old French "investir" (to clothe, install in office) and the Latin "investire" (to clothe, surround, cover), from "in-" (in, into) + "vestire" (to clothe). The financial sense developed from the idea of "clothing" money in something else.
