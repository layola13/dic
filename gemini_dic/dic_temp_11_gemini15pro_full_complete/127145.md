nattery:/ˈnætəri/
adjective
唠叨的，琐碎的
given to excessive or frivolous chatter; chatty
例句：He had a nattery aunt who always called at the worst possible time. 他有个爱唠叨的姑妈，总是在最不合适的时候打电话来。

例句：The article was full of nattery gossip and little substance. 这篇文章充斥着琐碎的流言蜚语，没什么实质内容。
近义词：chatty, talkative, gossipy, garrulous
反义词：reticent, taciturn, quiet
词性：adjective

词源：From the noun "natter" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Natter" itself comes from the imitative verb "gnatter," frequentative of "gnaw," suggesting the persistent, sometimes irritating quality of idle chatter.
