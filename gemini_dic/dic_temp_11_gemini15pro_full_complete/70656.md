filtrating:/ˈfɪltreɪtɪŋ/
verb
过滤中, 渗透中
The process of a liquid passing through a filter.  A substance passing through or permeating something.
The coffee is filtrating through the grounds. 咖啡正在透过咖啡渣过滤。
Rainwater is slowly filtrating into the soil. 雨水正慢慢渗入土壤。
synonyms: percolating, permeating, seeping, oozing
antonyms: blocking, obstructing, solidifying
