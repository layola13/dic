acanthocephala:/əˌkænθəˈsɛfələ/| n. |棘头动物门|A phylum of parasitic worms characterized by a retractable proboscis armed with hooks, which they use to attach to the intestinal wall of their host.  They are commonly known as thorny-headed worms.|The acanthocephala are a group of parasitic worms that infect a wide range of vertebrates.|近义词:spiny-headed worms|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
