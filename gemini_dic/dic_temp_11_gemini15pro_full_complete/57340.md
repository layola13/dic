downweigh:/ˌdaʊnˈweɪ/
verb
降低…的权重；使…显得不重要；比…重要
To cause something to have less influence or importance than something else.
The committee downweighed the importance of individual complaints. 委员会降低了个人投诉的重要性。
Their evidence downweighed all other considerations. 他们的证据比所有其他考虑因素都重要。
synonym: devalue, minimize, diminish, reduce, detract from, depreciate, underemphasize, underestimate
antonym: overemphasize, exaggerate, overvalue, inflate, magnify
词性: 动词
