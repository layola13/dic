infrabranchial:/ˌɪnfrəˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
鳃下的；位于鳃下方的
Situated below the gills.
The infrabranchial muscles play a crucial role in the fish's respiration.  鳃下肌肉在鱼的呼吸中起着至关重要的作用。
The infrabranchial chamber houses important blood vessels. 鳃下腔容纳重要的血管。
近义词: subbranchial, hypobranchial
反义词: suprabranchial
