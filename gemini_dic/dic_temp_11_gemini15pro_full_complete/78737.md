gigging:/ˈɡɪɡɪŋ/
n.
演出;  演奏
The act of playing gigs (musical performances).
例句：He makes a living from gigging in local pubs. 他靠在当地酒吧演出谋生。
例句：She's been gigging around the country with her band. 她和她的乐队在全国各地巡回演出。
近义词：performing, playing
反义词:resting
