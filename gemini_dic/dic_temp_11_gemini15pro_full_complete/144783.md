over-feeling:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːlɪŋ/
adj.
过度感性的，过于情绪化的
Excessively emotional or sentimental; too readily affected by emotion.
She's a bit over-feeling and cries at everything. 她有点过度感性，什么事都会哭。
He gave an over-feeling performance that lacked sincerity. 他的表演过于情绪化，缺乏真诚。
近义词:oversensitive, sentimental, emotional
反义词:unemotional, stoic, apathetic
词性:形容词

词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "feeling" (noun/adjective). "Over-" denotes excess or being too much. "Feeling" refers to emotions or sensations.
