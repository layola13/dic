almacantar:/ˌælməkænˈtɑːr/ | n. | 等高环,地平圈 | A circle parallel to the horizon.  An instrument, consisting of a circle of metal, divided into degrees and minutes, used to take observations of the sun or a star in order to determine its altitude and azimuth, or the variation of the magnetic needle. |  The astronomer used an almacantar to measure the star's altitude. 天文学家用等高环来测量恒星的高度。| Synonym:  parallel of altitude | Antonym:  None | noun

almacantar:/ˌælməkænˈtɑːr/ | n. | 天体测量仪 | An astronomical instrument used to determine the altitude and azimuth of celestial bodies. |  The museum displayed a medieval almacantar, a testament to early astronomical ingenuity. 博物馆展出了一台中世纪的等高环，证明了早期天文学的独创性。| Synonym:  astrolabe (partially) | Antonym:  None | noun
