ineptical:/ɪˈnɛptɪkəl/
adjective
笨拙的，无能的
lacking skill or ability; clumsy.
例句：His ineptical attempt at fixing the sink resulted in a flood. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理水槽，结果导致了水灾。
例句：She was ineptical at sports, always tripping over her own feet.  她不擅长运动，总是绊倒自己的脚。
近义词：clumsy, awkward, incompetent, inept, unskilled
反义词：skillful, adept, competent, proficient
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *inepticus, from in- (“not”) + aptus (“fit, suitable”).
