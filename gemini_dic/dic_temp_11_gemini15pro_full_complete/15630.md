bairn:/bɛrn/| n.|孩子，小孩 (尤指苏格兰语)|A child, especially in Scotland or northern England. |例句：The farmer's bairn was playing in the hay. 农夫的孩子在干草堆里玩耍。

例句：She's a bonny bairn. 她是一个漂亮的孩子。|近义词：child, kid, youngster, tot, infant, wean (Scottish)|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:名词
