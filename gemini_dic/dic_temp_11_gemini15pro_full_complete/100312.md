intersocial:/ˌɪntərˈsoʊʃəl/
adjective
社会间的；社会群体之间的
Existing or occurring between different social groups.
例句：Intersocial communication is crucial for maintaining harmony. 社会群体间的交流对于维护和谐至关重要。

例句：The intersocial dynamics between the two communities were complex.  这两个社群之间的社会互动很复杂。

近义词：intergroup, intercommunal
反义词：intrasocial, within-group
词性: adjective
