homonyms:/ˈhɑːmənɪmz/, /ˈhɒmənɪmz/ | n. |同音异义词|Words that have the same pronunciation but different meanings, origins, or spellings.|The words "there," "their," and "they're" are homonyms. 这三个词"there"、"their"和"they're"是同音异义词。| synonyms: homophones, homographs | antonyms: heteronyms| noun
