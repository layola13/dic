cissies:/ˈsɪsiz/
noun
胆小鬼，懦夫
A derogatory term for someone considered weak, cowardly, or effeminate.
He called the boys who wouldn't play football a bunch of cissies. 他把那些不愿踢足球的男孩叫做一群胆小鬼。
Near Synonyms: cowards, wimps, milksops
Antonyms: brave, courageous, heroes
