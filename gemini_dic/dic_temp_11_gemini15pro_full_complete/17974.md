befriending:/bɪˈfrɛndɪŋ/
n.|交朋友，友好对待|The act of becoming friends with someone. |She dedicated her life to befriending the lonely and the needy. 她一生致力于与孤独和需要帮助的人交朋友。|
近义词: supporting, helping, assisting
反义词: antagonizing, alienating


befriending:/bɪˈfrɛndɪŋ/
v.|与…交朋友|The act of becoming friends with someone. |He befriended a stray dog and took it home. 他与一只流浪狗交朋友，并把它带回了家。|
近义词: befriending, supporting, helping
反义词: antagonizing, alienating

词性: 动名词 (present participle of the verb "befriend")

词源: From "be-" (making, causing to be) + "friend" (one attached to another by affection or esteem).  The Old English root for "friend" is *frēond*, related to words signifying "to love."
