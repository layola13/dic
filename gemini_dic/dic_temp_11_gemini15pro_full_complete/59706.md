eclampsias:/ɪˈklæmpsiəz/
noun
子痫
A serious condition in pregnancy characterized by convulsions and sometimes coma.
Eclampsia is a rare but serious complication of preeclampsia.  子痫是先兆子痫的一种罕见但严重的并发症。
Severe headaches are a possible sign of eclampsias. 剧烈头痛可能是子痫的征兆。
近义词: toxemia, convulsions
反义词: (None - This is a medical condition without a direct opposite.)
词性: noun

词源:
From Greek eklampsis "a shining forth," from eklampein "to shine forth," from ek- "out" + lampein "to shine."  The original meaning referred to the sudden onset of convulsions.  来自希腊语 eklampsis “闪耀”，来自 eklampein “闪耀”，来自 ek- “出来” + lampein “闪耀”。最初的意思是指突然发作的抽搐。
