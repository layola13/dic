nummulitic:/ˌnʌmjʊˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
货币虫的；含货币虫的
Containing nummulites (large, lens-shaped foraminifera).
The cliffs are formed of nummulitic limestone. 这些悬崖是由货币虫灰岩形成的。
近义词:foraminiferal
反义词: None

Etymology:
From Nummulites (genus of foraminifera) + -ic.  Nummulites comes from Latin nummulus "little coin" (diminutive of nummus "coin") + -ites (suffix used in forming the names of fossil organisms).
