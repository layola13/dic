herbivore:/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːr/ (US), /ˈhɜːbɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|食草动物|An animal that feeds on plants.|Cows are herbivores. 奶牛是食草动物。|Deer are herbivores and primarily eat leaves, bark, and twigs. 鹿是食草动物，主要吃树叶、树皮和嫩枝。|近义词:vegetarian, plant-eater|反义词:carnivore|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *herbivorus*, from *herba* (herb) + *vorare* (to devour).
