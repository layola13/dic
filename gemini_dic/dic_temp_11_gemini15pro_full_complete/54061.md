disdain:/dɪsˈdeɪn/| n. vt.|轻蔑，鄙视；不屑|To consider someone or something to be unworthy of one's consideration or respect.|He disdained to answer their questions. 他不屑于回答他们的问题。|近义词：scorn, contempt, despise|反义词：admiration, respect|noun, verb

disdain:/dɪsˈdeɪn/| v.|轻蔑，鄙视；不屑|To refuse or reject something because you consider it beneath you.|She disdained his offer of help. 她不屑于接受他的帮助。|近义词：spurn, snub, reject|反义词：accept, welcome|verb
