cherubim: /ˈtʃɛrəbɪm/ | n. |智天使，小天使（复数）| Angelic beings of high rank, often depicted as winged children.  The plural form of "cherub".  While "cherubs" is also used as a plural, "cherubim" is considered more traditional and sometimes preferred in religious contexts. | The cherubim guarded the entrance to the Garden of Eden. 智天使守护着伊甸园的入口。|  The artist depicted cherubim hovering above the Madonna and Child.  这位艺术家描绘了圣母玛利亚和圣子耶稣上方盘旋的智天使。 | 近义词: angels, seraphs | 反义词: demons, devils | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cherubin, from Greek *kheroubin, from Hebrew kərūḇīm, plural of kərūḇ.
