denudate:/dɪˈnjuːdeɪt/ /dɪˈnuːdeɪt/| vt.  adj.|使裸露，剥夺，脱去；裸露的|To strip bare, to deprive of covering, especially of vegetation; laid bare.|例句：The heavy rains denudated the hillside, leaving bare rock exposed.  暴雨冲刷了山坡，露出了光秃秃的岩石。

例句：The process of erosion has denudated the landscape over millions of years.  数百万年来，侵蚀过程使地貌变得光秃秃的。|近义词：strip, bare, expose, divest|反义词：cover, clothe, conceal|词性: verb, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *denudatus, past participle of denudare "to lay bare," from de- "off, away" + nudare "to make naked," from nudus "naked."
