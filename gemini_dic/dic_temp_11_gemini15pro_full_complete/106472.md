kowtow:/ˌkaʊˈtaʊ/| vi. n.|叩头，磕头；卑躬屈膝；唯命是从|To kneel and touch the ground with the forehead in worship or submission, or to act in an excessively subservient manner.|He kowtowed to the emperor. 他向皇帝叩头。|近义词：grovel, fawn,  toady, truckle|反义词：defy, resist, stand up to|verb, noun

kowtow:/ˌkaʊˈtaʊ/| n.|叩头，磕头|The act of kneeling and touching the ground with the forehead in worship or submission.|The emperor demanded a kowtow from his subjects. 皇帝要求臣民向他叩头。|近义词：prostration, obeisance|反义词： defiance, resistance|noun
