excuses:/ɪkˈskjuːsɪz/
noun
借口；辩解
Reasons or explanations given to justify a fault or mistake.
She made excuses for being late. 她为迟到找借口。
synonyms: apologies, justifications, pretexts, defenses
antonyms: reasons, explanations, admissions, confessions
n.

excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːs/
verb
原谅；宽恕
To forgive or overlook a fault or offense.
Please excuse my interruption. 请原谅我打断一下。
synonyms: pardon, forgive, absolve, condone
antonyms: blame, condemn, reprimand, accuse
v.

excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːs/
noun
借口；理由
A reason or explanation given to justify a fault or mistake.
His excuse for being late was that his car broke down. 他迟到的理由是他的车抛锚了。
synonyms: reason, explanation, justification, pretext
antonyms: accusation, blame, condemnation, reprimand
n.
