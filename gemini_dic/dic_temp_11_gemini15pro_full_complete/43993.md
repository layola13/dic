criminalises:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
将…定为犯罪；使…成为非法
Makes something illegal or a crime.
The new law criminalises certain types of online speech.  新的法律将某些类型的网络言论定为犯罪。
criminalize, outlaw, prohibit
legalize, decriminalize
v.
