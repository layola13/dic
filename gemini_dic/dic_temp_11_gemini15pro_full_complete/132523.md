nondetonating:/ˌnɒnˈdɛtəneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非爆震的，非起爆的
Not exploding or detonating; burning smoothly rather than exploding.
The nondetonating explosive is safer to handle.  这种非爆震炸药处理起来更安全。
Ammunition specialists carefully handled the nondetonating cord. 弹药专家小心翼翼地处理非爆震导火索。

Synonym:nonexplosive, stable
Antonym:detonating, explosive
词性: adjective
