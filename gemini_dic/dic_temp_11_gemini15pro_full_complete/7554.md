/əˈnaɪəˈleɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 灵魂湮灭论者 | A person who believes in annihilationism, the doctrine that the wicked will ultimately cease to exist rather than suffer eternal torment in hell. | Some theologians consider themselves annihilationists, believing that eternal damnation is incompatible with a loving God.  一些神学家认为自己是灵魂湮灭论者，他们相信永恒的诅咒与慈爱的上帝是不相容的。 |  The preacher's sermon focused on the concept of annihilationism, sparking a lively debate among the congregation. 牧师的布道集中在灵魂湮灭论的概念上，引发了会众的热烈讨论。 | 近义词: destructionist | 反义词: eternalist, immortalist | 词性: 名词

词源: From annihilation + -ist.  来自annihilation（毁灭） + -ist（表示人或主义的后缀）。
