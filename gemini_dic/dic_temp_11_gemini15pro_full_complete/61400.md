embleming:/ɪmˈblɛmɪŋ/
v.
象征;作为…的象征
Representing or symbolizing something.
The dove embleming peace. 鸽子象征和平。
近义词: symbolizing, representing, typifying
反义词: obscuring, hiding, concealing
词性: 动词 (verb)

embleming:/ɪmˈblɛmɪŋ/
n.
象征;标志
A symbol or representation.
The embleming of peace through the dove. 通过鸽子象征和平。
近义词: symbol, representation, emblem
反义词: concealment, disguise, camouflage
词性: 名词 (noun)
