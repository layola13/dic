fulcrate: /ˈfʌlkreɪt/ | adj. | 支点的，杠杆的；  | Having a fulcrum or acting as a fulcrum; levered. |  The fulcrate arm of the crane lifted the heavy load.  起重机支点臂吊起了重物。 | 近义词：leveraged, pivoted | 反义词：unleveraged, unsupported | 词性: adjective

fulcrum: /ˈfʌlkrəm/ | n. | 支点，杠杆支点 | The point on which a lever rests or is supported and on which it pivots. | The fulcrum of the seesaw allowed the children to go up and down. 跷跷板的支点可以让孩子们上下移动。 | 近义词：pivot, axis, support | 反义词：N/A | 词性: noun
