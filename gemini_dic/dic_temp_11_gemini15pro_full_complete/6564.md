an-arm-and-a-leg:/ən ˌɑːrm ənd ə ˈleɡ/ | idiom | 代价高昂；非常昂贵 |  This idiom means that something is very expensive. It implies that you would have to sacrifice a lot (like an arm and a leg!) to afford it. | That car cost me an arm and a leg. 那辆车花了我一大笔钱。| 近义词：a fortune, a king's ransom, a small fortune, a lot of money, expensive | 反义词：cheap, inexpensive, affordable | idiom
