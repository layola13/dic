ichthyomorpha:/ɪkθiːəˈmɔːrfə/ | n. | 鱼形类 |  An extinct subclass of tetrapods that includes the "fish-like" amphibians, representing a stage in the evolution of tetrapods from fishes.  |  Ichthyomorpha were important in the transition from aquatic to terrestrial life. 鱼形类在从水生到陆生生命的过渡中很重要。|  Near synonyms:  early tetrapods, stem tetrapods | Antonyms:  amniotes | Noun
