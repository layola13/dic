greenwood:/ˈɡriːnwʊd/| n.|绿林，树林|A greenwood is a forest that is green, especially with leaves. |Robin Hood lived in the greenwood. 罗宾汉住在绿林里。|The children played in the greenwood all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在树林里玩耍。|近义词：forest, woodland, wood|反义词:desert, barren land|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *grenewode*, from Old English *grēnwuda*, from *grēne* (“green”) + *wudu* (“wood”).
