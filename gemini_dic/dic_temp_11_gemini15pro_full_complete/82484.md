grunginess:/ˈɡrʌndʒɪnəs/| n.|肮脏，邋遢|The grunginess of the apartment was off-putting. 公寓的脏乱让人望而却步。|近义词：dirtiness, filthiness, squalor, grime|反义词:cleanliness, tidiness, neatness|词性:noun

Etymology: From grunge + -iness. Grunge itself originated in the 1980s as a term for a subgenre of alternative rock characterized by its distorted guitars and apathetic lyrics, often associated with a messy, unkempt aesthetic.  The term broadened to describe this general aesthetic of dirtiness and untidiness.
