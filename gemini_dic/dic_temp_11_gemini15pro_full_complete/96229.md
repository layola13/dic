indianeer:/ˈɪndiənɪr/| n.| 印度工程师 (尤指在IT行业工作的) |An Indian engineer, especially one working in the IT sector. | Many indianeers work in Silicon Valley. 许多印度工程师在硅谷工作。|  The company hired several indianeers for their new project.  这家公司为他们的新项目雇佣了几位印度工程师。|  Indian software engineer, IT professional from India | N/A | noun
Etymology: A portmanteau of "Indian" and "engineer".  This term arose due to the large number of Indian engineers, particularly in the software and IT sectors, working globally, especially in the US.
