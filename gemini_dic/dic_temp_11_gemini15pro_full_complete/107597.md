landslidden:/ˈlændslɪdn/| adj.|滑坡的；因山崩而毁坏的；受灾的；精神上受打击的|Covered with or damaged by a landslide; overwhelmed or devastated.|The village was landslidden after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，村庄被山体滑坡掩埋了。|近义词：devastated, ruined, overwhelmed|反义词:intact, unscathed|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From land + slidden, past participle of slide.  The word literally describes land that has slid.
