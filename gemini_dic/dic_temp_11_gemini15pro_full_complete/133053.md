nonepiscopal:/ˌnɒnɪˈpɪskəpəl/
adjective
非主教制的；非圣公会的
Not pertaining to or characteristic of an episcopal church government; not episcopal.
The nonepiscopal churches often have different organizational structures. 非主教制教会通常有不同的组织结构。
Many Protestant denominations are nonepiscopal. 许多新教教派是非主教制的。
近义词:non-episcopal, nonhierarchical
反义词:episcopal, hierarchical
词性:adjective
