adjuster:/əˈdʒʌstər/| n.| 调整者，调节器，理算员|A person who assesses the amount of compensation to be paid in an insurance claim.  A person who adjusts or regulates something.|The insurance adjuster came to assess the damage to the car. 保险理算员来评估汽车的损坏情况。|Synonyms: regulator, assessor, evaluator|Antonyms: disorganizer|noun
