chiffons:/ˈʃɪfɒnz/ (UK), /ʃɪˈfɑːnz/ (US)| n.|雪纺绸；薄纱；轻薄织物|A sheer, lightweight fabric typically made of silk, nylon, or rayon.|She wore a dress made of delicate chiffons. 她穿着一件精致的雪纺连衣裙。|例句：
The curtains were made of sheer chiffons. 窗帘是用透明的雪纺绸做的。
The dancer's scarf was a flowing piece of chiffons. 舞者的围巾是一块飘逸的雪纺绸。|近义词：gauze, voile, gossamer|反义词:heavy fabric, thick cloth|词性:noun
