disadvantageousnesses:/ˌdɪsˌædvænˈteɪdʒəsnəsɪz/
noun
不利之处，劣势（复数）
The state or quality of being disadvantageous; a collection of disadvantages.  This word is rarely used due to its awkwardness.
The disadvantageousnesses of the location outweighed the benefits.  这个地方的种种不利条件超过了它的好处。
Near synonyms: drawbacks, downsides, handicaps
Opposite: advantages, benefits
词性: 名词
