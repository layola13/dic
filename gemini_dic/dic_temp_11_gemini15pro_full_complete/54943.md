dispossessor:/dɪspəˈzɛsər/
n.|剥夺者，驱逐者|Someone who deprives another of land or property.|The dispossessors forced the family from their ancestral home.|Synonyms: expropriator, usurper, evictor|Antonyms: benefactor, donor, provider|Noun
