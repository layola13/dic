oppressively:/əˈprɛsɪvli/
adverb
压抑地；沉重地；暴虐地
In a manner that is harsh, cruel, or burdensome; in a way that weighs heavily on the mind or spirits.
The heat hung oppressively in the air.  闷热的天气令人窒息。
The government ruled oppressively, silencing all dissent. 政府实行暴虐统治，压制所有异议。
近义词:tyrannically, harshly, cruelly, brutally
反义词: gently, kindly, mildly, leniently
词性: adverb

词源: From the adjective "oppressive" + "-ly". "Oppressive" derives from the Latin "oppressus," the past participle of "opprimere" meaning "to press against, suppress."
