chiffoniere:/ˌʃɪfəˈnɪər/ | n. | 衣橱；矮柜 | A tall chest of drawers, often with a mirror attached. |  She kept her jewelry in the top drawer of the chiffoniere. 她把她的珠宝放在衣橱最上面的抽屉里。
