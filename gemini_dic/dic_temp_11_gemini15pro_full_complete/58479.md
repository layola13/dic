dudes:/duːdz/| n.|伙计们，哥们儿，家伙们|A group of (usually male) friends or associates.  A term of address, often used informally.|例句：Hey, dudes, what are you up to? 嘿，伙计们，你们在干嘛？

例句：Those dudes are always causing trouble.  那些家伙总是惹麻烦。

近义词：guys, fellows, pals, buddies|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "ladies" in a specific context)|词性:noun
