capsaicin:/kæpˈseɪsɪn/ | n. |辣椒素|A colorless, crystalline, pungent compound, C18H27NO3, found in various capsicums, especially chili peppers. It is the active component that produces the hotness sensation.|Capsaicin is used in pepper spray. 辣椒素用于胡椒喷雾。|Capsaicin is the chemical that makes chili peppers hot. 辣椒素是使辣椒辣的化学物质。|Synonyms: chili pepper extract, hot pepper extract|Antonyms: coolant|noun

capsicine:/kæpˈsiːɪn/ | n. |辣椒素|Alternative spelling of capsaicin.|Capsicine is the active component that makes chili peppers hot. 辣椒素是使辣椒辣的活性成分。|Capsicine creams can be used to relieve muscle pain. 辣椒素药膏可以用来缓解肌肉疼痛。|Synonyms: chili pepper extract, hot pepper extract|Antonyms: coolant|noun
