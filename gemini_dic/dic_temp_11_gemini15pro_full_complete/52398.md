didascalar: /ˌdɪdəˈskælər/ | adj. | 教学的；教导的；指导的 | Relating to teaching or instruction; intended for instruction. |  The didascalar method employed by the professor encouraged student participation. 教授采用的教学方法鼓励学生参与。|  The museum offers didascalar materials for school groups.  博物馆为学校团体提供教学材料。| 近义词: pedagogical, instructive, educational | 反义词: uninformative, unenlightening | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek διδασκαλικός (didaskalikos), from διδάσκαλος (didaskalos, “teacher”), from διδάσκω (didaskō, “I teach”).
