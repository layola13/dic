farinaceous:/ˌfɑːrɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|淀粉的，含淀粉的|Made of flour or containing starch.  Resembling flour or meal in texture.|例句：Farinaceous foods, such as bread and pasta, are a good source of carbohydrates.  含淀粉的食物，例如面包和意大利面，是碳水化合物的重要来源。|例句：The cake had a slightly farinaceous texture.  蛋糕的质地略带淀粉感。|近义词：starchy, mealy|反义词:non-starchy|词性:adjective
