heteroptics:/ˌhɛtərˈɒptɪks/ | n. |异向光学|The study of the interaction of light with anisotropic materials, especially in ways that change the polarization state of the light.|Heteroptics plays a crucial role in the development of advanced optical devices. 异向光学在先进光学设备的开发中起着至关重要的作用。|Near synonyms: anisotropic optics, polarization optics |Antonyms: isotropic optics |noun
