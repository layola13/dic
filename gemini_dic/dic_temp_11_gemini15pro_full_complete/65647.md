evaporable:/ɪˈvæpərəbl/
adjective
可蒸发的；可挥发的
able to evaporate; that can evaporate
例句：The liquid is highly evaporable.  这种液体很容易挥发。

例句：Evaporable substances should be stored in sealed containers. 易挥发物质应存放在密封容器中。

近义词：volatile, vaporizable
反义词：non-volatile, non-vaporizable
词性: adjective

词源: From evaporate + -able.  来自动词evaporate 加上形容词后缀 -able.
