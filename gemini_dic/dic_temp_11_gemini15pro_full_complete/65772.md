everyman:/ˈevriˌmæn/ | n. |普通人；典型人物；老百姓 | An ordinary individual; a typical or representative human being. |  Example: The play explores the struggles of an everyman facing mortality.  这出戏探讨了一个普通人面对死亡的挣扎。 | Example: He's the everyman hero, relatable and inspiring. 他是一位平民英雄，让人产生共鸣并鼓舞人心。 | Synonyms: common man, average person, ordinary individual | Antonyms: elite, aristocrat, superhero | noun

everyman:/ˈevriˌmæn/ | adj. | 适用于所有人的；通俗的；普通的 | Designed for or suitable for everyone; common; ordinary. | Example: The film's everyman appeal made it a box office hit.  这部电影对大众的吸引力使它成为票房热门。 | Example: It's an everyman story about love and loss. 这是一个关于爱与失去的普通人的故事。| Synonyms: common, ordinary, universal, general | Antonyms: exclusive, specialized, niche | adjective
