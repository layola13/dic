anticivic:/ˌæntiˈsɪvɪk/
adjective
反公民的; 反社会公德的; 不关心社会公益的
Opposing or harming the welfare of the community; detrimental to civic duty or responsibility.
His anticivic behavior, like littering and vandalizing public property, landed him in trouble with the law. 他反公民的行为，比如乱扔垃圾和破坏公共财产，让他陷入了法律的麻烦。
近义词: antisocial, uncivic, irresponsible, lawless
反义词: civic, public-spirited, responsible, law-abiding
词性: adjective
