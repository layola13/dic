aurelian:/ɔːˈriːliən/ | adj. n. |金色的，蝴蝶的；奥勒良的；蝴蝶收藏家 |Of or relating to gold or the color gold.  Relating to or characteristic of the Roman emperor Aurelian. A collector and student of butterflies, especially a lepidopterist. | The aurelian hues of sunset filled the sky. 夕阳的金黄色调布满了天空。 | He was a keen aurelian, with a vast collection of butterflies. 他是一位热衷于蝴蝶的收藏家，拥有大量的蝴蝶收藏。 | Near synonyms: golden, gold-colored,  butterfly enthusiast, lepidopterist. | Antonyms:  dull, dark, leaden.
词性: adjective, noun
