camphire: /ˈkæmfər/ (US), /ˈkæmfə(r)/ (UK)| n.|樟脑; 樟树|A white, crystalline, volatile substance with a strong, aromatic odor, obtained from the wood and bark of the camphor tree.  It is used in medicine and as an insect repellent.|The old chest smelled strongly of camphor.  那旧箱子散发着浓烈的樟脑味。|Synonyms: camphor, 2-bornanone|Antonyms: None|Noun

camphire: /ˈkæmfər/ (US), /ˈkæmfə(r)/ (UK)| adj. (archaic/poetic)|樟脑的; 像樟脑的|Relating to or resembling camphor.|The camphire-scented air filled the room. 樟脑味的空气充满了房间。|Synonyms: camphoraceous|Antonyms: None|Adjective
