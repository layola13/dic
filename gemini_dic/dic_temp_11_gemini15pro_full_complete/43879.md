crestfallen: /ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlən/ (US), /ˈkrɛstˌfɔːln/ (UK)| adj. | 沮丧的，垂头丧气的 |  Dejected and disappointed; dispirited.  It suggests a visible lowering of one's spirits, like a bird with drooping wings (crest). |  He looked crestfallen after his team lost the championship game. 他在球队输掉冠军赛后看起来很沮丧。 |  dejected, despondent, disconsolate, downcast |  elated, cheerful, jubilant | adjective
