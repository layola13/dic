nonpleadingly:/nɑːnˈpliːdɪŋli/
adverb
无辩护地；不作答辩地
Without making a plea or offering a defense.
He nonpleadingly accepted the judge's sentence. 他没有辩护地接受了法官的判决。
The defendant stood nonpleadingly before the court. 被告没有作答辩地站在法庭前。
近义词: silently, tacitly, wordlessly
反义词: pleadingly, defensively
