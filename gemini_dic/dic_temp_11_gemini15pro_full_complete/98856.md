intemperancy:/ɪnˈtɛmpərənsi/| n.|放纵；无节制；过度|Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in the consumption of alcohol; excessive indulgence.|His intemperancy ultimately led to his downfall. 他的放纵最终导致了他的垮台。|Synonyms: excess, immoderation, overindulgence, dissipation, profligacy|Antonyms: moderation, temperance, abstinence, self-control|noun
