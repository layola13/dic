boilers:/ˈbɔɪlərz/| n.| 锅炉; 烧水壶 |A closed metal container in which water is heated to provide steam or hot water, typically for heating a building or powering an engine.|The boilers in the old factory finally gave out.  旧工厂的锅炉终于坏了。|synonyms: heater, furnace, kettle|antonyms: cooler, refrigerator|noun
