missupposed:/ˌmɪsəˈpoʊzd/ | adj. | 误以为的，错误地认为的 | Incorrectly supposed or assumed; based on a false supposition. | She missupposed his intentions, thinking he was trying to help when he was actually hindering her. 她误解了他的意图，以为他是想帮忙，而实际上是在阻碍她。| Example:  He missupposed her silence for agreement. 他误以为她的沉默就是同意。| Synonym: mistaken, misinterpreted, misunderstood | Antonym: correctly understood, rightly assumed | word type: adjective
