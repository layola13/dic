impassionedly:/ɪmˈpæʃənɪdli/
adverb
热情地；慷慨激昂地
With great emotion and fervor; ardently.
She spoke impassionedly about the need for change. 她慷慨激昂地谈到了变革的必要性。
He argued impassionedly for the release of the prisoners. 他热情地为释放囚犯而辩护。
近义词: fervently, passionately, ardently, emotionally, vehemently
反义词: apathetically, indifferently, coldly
词性: adverb

词源:
From impassioned + -ly.  Impassioned itself comes from the Old French "empassioner,"  meaning "to inflame with passion," from "en-" (in) + "passion" (passion).
