eddies:/ˈɛdiz/| n.|漩涡，涡流|A circular movement of water, counter to a main current, causing a small whirlpool.|The kayak bobbed in the eddies downstream from the bridge. 皮划艇在桥下游的漩涡中颠簸。|近义词：whirlpools, swirls, vortexes|反义词:currents, streams|noun

eddies:/ˈɛdiz/| v.| (水) 旋转，起漩涡|(Of water, air, etc.) move in a circle, especially counter to the main current.|The wind eddied around the corners of the buildings. 风在建筑物的角落里盘旋。|近义词：swirl, whirl, spiral|反义词:flow, stream|verb
