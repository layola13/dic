monthlier:/ˈmʌnθliər/
adjective
更每月的；每月一次的
Happening, done, or payable every month, or more frequently than that.  More monthly than something else.
He seemed to be getting monthlier payments. 他似乎每月都在得到更多的付款。
The magazine became monthlier, publishing two issues a month. 这本杂志变得更频繁了，每月出版两期。
近义词:  more frequent,  bimonthly (if referring to twice a month)
反义词: less frequent,  annual, yearly
词性: adjective
