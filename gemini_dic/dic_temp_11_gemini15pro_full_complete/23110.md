bourbonism:/ˈbɜːrbənɪzəm/
n.
波旁主义；波旁王朝的拥护；保守主义
Support for the Bourbon dynasty in France, or a conservative political philosophy.
The resurgence of bourbonism after the revolution was short-lived.  波旁王朝在革命后的复辟是短暂的。
The family's staunch bourbonism led them to oppose any progressive reforms. 这个家族坚定的波旁主义使他们反对任何进步的改革。
synonyms: conservatism, traditionalism, reactionism
antonyms: liberalism, progressivism, radicalism
noun
