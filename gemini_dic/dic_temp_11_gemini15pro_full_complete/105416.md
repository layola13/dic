kidnaping:/ˈkɪdnæpɪŋ/ /ˈkɪdnæpɪŋ/| n.|绑架，拐骗，劫持|The act of abducting someone and holding them captive, typically to obtain a ransom. |The kidnaping of the businessman's daughter made headlines.  这名商人的女儿被绑架上了头条。|近义词：abduction, seizure, snatching|反义词:release, liberation| noun

kidnap:/ˈkɪdnæp/ /ˈkɪdnæp/| vt.|绑架，拐骗，劫持|To abduct someone and hold them captive, typically to obtain a ransom. |The gang kidnapped the child and demanded a ransom.  该团伙绑架了孩子并索要赎金。|近义词：abduct, seize, snatch|反义词:release, liberate| verb
