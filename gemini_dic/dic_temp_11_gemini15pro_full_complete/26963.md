calcified:/ˈkælsɪfaɪd/
adjective
钙化的；僵化的
Hardened by deposits of calcium salts.  Made rigid and unadaptable.
例句：The calcified artery caused a reduction in blood flow. 钙化的动脉导致血流量减少。

例句：His political views had become calcified over the years. 他的政治观点多年来已经变得僵化。

近义词：hardened, rigid, inflexible, ossified
反义词：flexible, adaptable, pliable
词性: adjective


calcify:/ˈkælsɪfaɪ/
verb
钙化；使僵化
To harden by deposits of calcium salts. To make or become rigid and unadaptable.
例句：The tissues calcified over time. 这些组织随着时间的推移而钙化。

例句：Their thinking has calcified over the years. 他们的思维多年来已经僵化。

近义词：harden, rigidify, ossify
反义词：soften, flexibilize
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcificāre, from calc-, calx "lime, limestone" + -ficāre "-fy".
