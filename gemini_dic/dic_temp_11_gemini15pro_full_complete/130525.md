nonaccretions:/ˌnɒnəˈkriːʃənz/
|n.|非增值，非积累|The report highlighted the company's nonaccretions in revenue this quarter. 报告强调了该公司本季度收入的非增长。|Nonaccretions refer to the absence of growth or accumulation, often in financial contexts, representing a stagnation or decline rather than an increase.  It can describe a situation where a company's earnings or assets do not increase as expected, or even decrease.  It's the opposite of accretion, which signifies growth or increase by gradual addition.|The company experienced nonaccretions in market share due to increased competition. 由于竞争加剧，该公司经历了市场份额的非增长。|Near synonyms: decline, decrease, stagnation, reduction, contraction|Antonyms: accretion, growth, increase, expansion, augmentation|noun
