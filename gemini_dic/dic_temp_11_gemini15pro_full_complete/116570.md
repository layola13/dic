matless:/ˈmætləs/
adjective
无褥垫的；没有垫子的
Without a mattress.
The floor was cold and hard, and he had to sleep matless.  地板又冷又硬，他不得不睡在没有褥垫的地方。
He spent an uncomfortable night in the matless bed. 他在没有褥垫的床上度过了一个不舒服的夜晚。
Near Synonyms:  unpadded, uncovered, bare
Antonyms:  mattressed, padded, cushioned
词性: adjective
