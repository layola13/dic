laconic: /ləˈkɒnɪk/ (UK) /ləˈkɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|简洁的，简明的|Using few words; expressing much in few words; concise.|例句：His laconic reply suggested he wasn't interested.  他简洁的回答表明他不感兴趣。

例句：She gave a laconic shrug. 她耸了耸肩，没多说什么。

近义词：brief, concise, terse, succinct|反义词：verbose, loquacious, prolix|词性: adjective
