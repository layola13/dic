Barrett's:/ˈbærɪts/| n.|巴雷特氏食管|A condition in which the flat pink lining of the esophagus becomes damaged by acid reflux, causing it to thicken and become red.  It is a precancerous condition.|He was diagnosed with Barrett's esophagus after years of chronic heartburn.|近义词: Barrett's esophagus (often used interchangeably)|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
