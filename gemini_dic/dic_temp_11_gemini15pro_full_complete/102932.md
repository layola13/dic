jaywalk: /ˈdʒeɪwɔːk/ | vi. | 乱穿马路 | To cross a street carelessly or illegally, especially by not using a crosswalk. |  He jaywalked across the busy street, narrowly avoiding a speeding car. 他乱穿繁忙的街道，差点被一辆飞驰的汽车撞到。


He was fined for jaywalking. 他因乱穿马路被罚款。


| 近义词: cross against the light, cross illegally | 反义词: cross at the crosswalk, cross legally | 词性: 动词 (verb)
