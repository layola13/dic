detractingly: /dɪˈtræktɪŋli/
adverb
贬低地，减损地
in a way that makes something seem less good or impressive
Her constant complaints were detractingly from the overall positive atmosphere. 她不断的抱怨降低了整体积极的氛围。
The chipped paint on the wall was detractingly, making the whole room look shabby.  墙上剥落的油漆 detractingly, 使整个房间看起来很破旧。
Synonyms: disparagingly, belittlingly, negatively
Antonyms: favorably, positively, enhancingly
