magnesial: /mæɡˈniːʒiəl/ | adj. | 镁的；含镁的 | Relating to or containing magnesium. | This rock is magnesial, containing a high percentage of magnesium. 这块岩石是镁质的，含有很高的镁。| The soil is magnesial and good for growing certain crops. 这种土壤富含镁，适合种植某些作物。 | synonyms: magnesian | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could be described as magnesium-deficient) | adjective
