overact:/ˌoʊvərˈækt/ | v. | 过度表演；夸张表演 | To act in an exaggerated or unrealistic way. |  He overacted the part of the villain, making him seem comical rather than menacing. 他把反派角色演得太过了，让他看起来滑稽而不是危险。

The lead actress overacted so dramatically that it ruined the entire scene. 女主角表演得过于夸张，毁了整个场景。

| 近义词：exaggerate, ham it up | 反义词：underact, downplay | 词性: 动词
