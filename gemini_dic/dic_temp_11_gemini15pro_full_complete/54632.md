dismissers:/dɪsˈmɪsɚz/
noun
解雇者；不予理会者；轻视者
People who dismiss something or someone, often without proper consideration.
The dismissers of the new technology were later proven wrong.  那些轻视新技术的人后来被证明是错的。
synonyms:  scorners, skeptics, detractors, rejectors
antonyms:  supporters, advocates, proponents
n.
