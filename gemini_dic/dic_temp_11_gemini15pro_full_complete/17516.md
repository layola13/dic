beat:/biːt/| v. n. adj.| 打败，敲打，节拍| To strike repeatedly, defeat, pulsate rhythmically. | The boxer beat his opponent. 拳击手打败了他的对手。| defeat, conquer, hit, pulse, rhythm | lose, fail, surrender | verb, noun, adjective

beat:/biːt/| v. n. adj.| 疲惫的，破旧的| Exhausted, worn out. | After a long day's work, I felt beat.  一天辛苦工作后，我感到筋疲力尽。| exhausted, tired, worn out | refreshed, energetic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bēatan "to beat," from Proto-Germanic *bautaną (source also of Old Norse bauta "to strike," Old High German bōzan "to beat, knock," Gothic bautan "to beat").  The sense of "pulsate" is from late 14c.
