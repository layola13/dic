nondepositions:/ˌnɒndɛpəˈzɪʃənz/| n.|未作证词；未提交证词（通常用复数形式）|The case was dismissed due to nondepositions from key witnesses.  由于关键证人未提交证词，案件被驳回。|The judge expressed frustration at the repeated nondepositions. 法官对反复未提交证词表示失望。|近义词：non-testimony, absence of testimony|反义词：depositions, testimonies|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + deposition.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "absence of". "Deposition" comes from the Late Middle English "deposicioun", from Old French "deposicion", ultimately from Latin "dēpositiō", "a putting down".
