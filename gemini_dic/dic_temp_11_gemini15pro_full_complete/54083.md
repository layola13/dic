disembarked:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrkt/
verb
下船，登陆
to leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle
The passengers disembarked at Southampton. 乘客们在南安普敦下船。
近义词: debark, land, go ashore
反义词: embark, board
