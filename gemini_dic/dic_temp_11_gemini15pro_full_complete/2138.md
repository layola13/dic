adaptness:/əˈdæptnɪs/
n.
适应性，适合性
The adaptness of the camel to desert conditions is remarkable. 骆驼对沙漠环境的适应性非常显著。
The adaptness of this program to our needs is questionable.  这个程序对我们需求的适应性值得怀疑。
近义词:adaptability, suitability, fitness
反义词:unsuitability, inaptness
词性:名词

Etymology: From adapt + -ness.  Adapt comes from Late Latin *adaptare, from Latin ad- ‘to’ + aptare ‘fit’.
