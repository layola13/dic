coder:/ˈkoʊdər/ (US) /ˈkəʊdə(r)/ (UK)| n. |程序员，编码员|A person who writes code for computer programs.  A coder translates software requirements into instructions that a computer can understand and execute.|She's a skilled coder who can write in multiple programming languages. 她是一位熟练的程序员，可以使用多种编程语言编写代码。|Synonyms: programmer, developer, software engineer|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
