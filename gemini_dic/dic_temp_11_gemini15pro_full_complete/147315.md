overmoral:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəl/| adj.|过分道德的，道德观念过强的|Excessively concerned with morals; moralistic to an excessive degree.  Having or showing an exaggerated sense of morality.| He was seen as overmoral by his peers, always judging their choices. 他被同龄人视为过分道德的人，总是评判他们的选择。| She had an overmoral attitude towards everything, making her difficult to be around. 她对每件事都抱有过度道德的态度，这让她很难相处。|近义词：moralistic, puritanical, self-righteous|反义词：amoral, immoral, unethical|词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "moral" (adjective related to principles of right and wrong)
