carnivora:/kɑːrˈnɪvərə/| n. |食肉目|An order of placental mammals characterized by their specialized teeth and claws for tearing flesh.  They are typically predators, though some are omnivorous.|Lions and tigers belong to the order Carnivora. 狮子和老虎属于食肉目。|synonyms: carnivores, flesh-eaters|antonyms: herbivora|noun
