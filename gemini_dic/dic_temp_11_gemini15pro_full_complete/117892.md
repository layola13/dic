melonist:/ˈmɛlənɪst/
noun|西瓜爱好者；特别喜欢西瓜的人|A person who loves melons, especially watermelons.|He's a true melonist; he eats watermelon every day. 他是一位真正的西瓜爱好者；他每天都吃西瓜。|近义词: watermelon lover, melon enthusiast|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly someone who dislikes melons)|n.
