inactivate:/ɪnˈæktɪveɪt/ | v. | 使不活跃，使失效，灭活 | To make something inactive or ineffective; to deprive of power or force. |  The vaccine inactivates the virus.  疫苗使病毒失活。

The enzyme is inactivated by heat.  酶被加热灭活了。 | 近义词: deactivate, disable, neutralize | 反义词: activate, enable | 词性: 动词
