mulligrubs:/ˈmʌlɪɡrʌbz/
noun
肚子不舒服；心情不好
A feeling of general discomfort or depression.
例句：He woke up with the mulligrubs after eating too much pizza last night. 他昨晚吃了太多披萨，醒来后觉得肚子不舒服。

例句：She had the mulligrubs all day because she didn't get the promotion. 她一整天心情都不好，因为她没有得到晋升。

近义词：blues, doldrums, sulks, ill humor
反义词：good spirits, cheerfulness, elation


Etymology:
Origin uncertain, perhaps a fanciful formation. First recorded in the mid-16th century. Possibly influenced by "mullygrubs," a now obsolete term for the intestines.
