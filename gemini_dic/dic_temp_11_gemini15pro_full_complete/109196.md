leguleian: /ləˈɡuːliən/
noun
讼棍，好诉讼的人，律師(贬义)
A lawyer, especially one who is perceived as being overly litigious or unscrupulous.
He's a real leguleian, always looking for a lawsuit. 他真是个讼棍，总想着打官司。
Synonyms: pettifogger, shyster, ambulance chaser, litigant
Antonyms: arbitrator, mediator
词性: 名词
