aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/
verb
加重，恶化
to make a bad situation, an illness, or an injury worse
Stress can aggravate an existing medical condition. 压力会加重现有的疾病。
worsen, exacerbate, intensify
improve, alleviate, relieve
词性:动词

aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/
verb
使恼火，激怒
to annoy someone
His constant complaining aggravated her. 他不停的抱怨让她很恼火。
irritate, annoy, provoke
soothe, calm, placate
词性:动词
