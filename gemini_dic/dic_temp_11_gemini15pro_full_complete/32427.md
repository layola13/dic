It is known for its scavenging habits and distinctive chestnut-colored wings.|The chimango soared effortlessly above the pampas. 栗翅鹰轻松地在潘帕斯草原上空翱翔。|The chimango perched on a fence post, watching for carrion. 栗翅鹰栖息在栅栏柱上，寻找腐肉。|Synonyms: caracara, hawk, kite|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific bird species, but could use terms like prey, dove for contrasting concepts)|Noun
