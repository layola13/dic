fretless:/ˈfrɛtlɪs/| adj.|无品的|Without frets (the raised metal strips on the fingerboard of stringed instruments like guitars or basses).|A fretless bass allows for a smoother, gliding sound.  无品贝斯可以发出更流畅、滑动的音色。|He prefers the warm tone of a fretless guitar. 他更喜欢无品吉他的温暖音调。|近义词:unfretted|反义词:fretted|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *fret* (a ridge on the fingerboard) + *-less* (suffix meaning "without").  The word "fret" comes from Old English *frett*, meaning "decoration, interlaced work."  Over time, the meaning evolved to refer specifically to the ridges on a fingerboard.
