nurtureless: /ˈnɜːrtʃərlɪs/
| adj. |缺乏养育的，未受良好教养的|Lacking nurture or upbringing; deprived of loving care and attention, especially during childhood.|The nurtureless child grew up to be withdrawn and distrustful.  这个缺乏养育的孩子长大后变得孤僻和多疑。|例句：The nurtureless child struggled to form healthy relationships.  这个缺乏养育的孩子难以建立健康的人际关系。|近义词：uncared for, neglected, deprived, abandoned|反义词：nurtured, cherished, cared for|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From nurture + -less.  "Nurture" comes from Old French "norture"  meaning "nourishment, education, breeding," from Latin "nutrire" "to nourish."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
