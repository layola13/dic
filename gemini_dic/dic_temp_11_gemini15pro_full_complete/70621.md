Filmstrasse:/ˈfɪlmˌʃtraːsə/| n. |电影街|A street associated with the film industry, often where studios or production companies are located.  It is a German word combining "Film" (film) and "Strasse" (street).|Berlin's Filmstrasse is home to many important film companies.|Synonym:  Cinema street (loosely), studio lot (related concept)|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology: German, from Film (film) + Straße (street)
