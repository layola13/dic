dramaturge:/ˈdræmətɜːrdʒ/ /ˈdrɑːmətɜːrɡ/ | n. | 剧作家；戏剧顾问；文学顾问 | A dramaturge is a literary editor on the staff of a theater who consults with authors and edits texts. | The dramaturge helped the playwright develop the characters and storyline.  剧作家帮助剧作家发展人物和故事情节。| synonym:  literary advisor, dramatic advisor | antonym:  None | noun


dramaturg:/ˈdræmətɜːrdʒ/ /ˈdrɑːmətɜːrɡ/ | n. | 剧作家；戏剧顾问；文学顾问 | A dramaturg is a literary editor on the staff of a theater who consults with authors and edits texts. | The dramaturg's feedback was invaluable to the production.  文学顾问的反馈对作品来说是非常宝贵的。| synonym:  script editor, theatre consultant | antonym:  None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dramaturge*, from German *Dramaturg*, from *Drama* + *-urg* (meaning "worker, doer"). Ultimately from Greek *δρᾶμα* (drama, "deed, act, play") + *-ουργός* (-ourgos, "worker").
