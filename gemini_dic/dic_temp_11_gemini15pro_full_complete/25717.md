bunted: /ˈbʌntɪd/ | v. | 触击；轻击；用头顶撞 | To deliberately hit or tap (a baseball) lightly so that it rolls slowly in the infield. | He bunted the ball down the first base line. 他触击球到一垒边线。|  The goat bunted me with its head. 山羊用头撞了我。 | synonyms: tapped, dabbed | antonyms: smashed, slammed | verb

bunted: /ˈbʌntɪd/ | adj. |  (船头)短而钝的，(船头)呈球形的 | Having a short, blunt, or rounded bow. |  a bunted trawler  一艘球鼻首拖网渔船 | synonyms: rounded, blunt | antonyms: pointed, sharp | adjective
