maharanees:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːniːz/
noun
王妃们（maharani 的复数形式）
Plural form of maharani, meaning the wife or widow of a maharaja.
The maharanees gathered for the annual festival.  王妃们聚集在一起参加年度节日。
The portraits of the maharanees adorned the palace walls.  王妃们的肖像装饰着宫殿的墙壁。
Synonyms: queens, princesses, royal consorts
Antonyms: commoners
词性: noun
