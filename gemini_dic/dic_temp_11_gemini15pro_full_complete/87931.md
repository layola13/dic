hierophant: /ˈhaɪərəfænt/ (US), /ˈhaɪərəfɑːnt/ (UK)| n. |教宗，解释神秘事物的人，|A person, especially a priest in ancient Greece, who interprets sacred mysteries or esoteric principles.  A person who explains or interprets arcane knowledge or practices. |例句：The hierophant revealed the secrets of the ancient ritual. 教宗揭示了古代仪式的秘密。

例句：The art critic acted as a hierophant, explaining the meaning behind the abstract painting.  艺术评论家扮演了解释者的角色，解释了抽象画背后的含义。|近义词：interpreter, expounder, oracle, guru, sage, priest |反义词:disciple, follower, student|词性: noun
