aerosol:/ˈɛrəsɒl/ (US) /ˈeərəsɒl/ (UK)| n.|气溶胶；喷雾剂|A colloid of fine solid particles or liquid droplets, in air or another gas.  A substance enclosed under pressure and able to be released as a fine spray, typically by means of a propellant gas.|例句：The air was thick with aerosol from the spray paint. 空气中充满了喷漆的气溶胶。|例句：She used an aerosol deodorant. 她使用了一瓶喷雾除臭剂。|近义词：spray, mist, fog|反义词:(No direct antonym)|noun
