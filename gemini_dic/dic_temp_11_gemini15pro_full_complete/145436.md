overcheaply:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃiːpli/
| adv. |太便宜地；过分便宜地|Too cheaply; at an excessively low price.|He bought the car overcheaply, which made him suspicious. 他以过低的价格买了这辆车，这让他起了疑心。|例句：The goods were sold overcheaply due to a clearance sale. 由于清仓甩卖，这些商品以极低的价格出售。|近义词：inexpensively, cheaply|反义词：expensively, dearly|词性:副词
Etymology: over- + cheaply.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "excessively, too much."  "Cheaply" comes from Old English "cēaplice," from "cēap" (cheap) + "-lice" (an adverbial suffix).
