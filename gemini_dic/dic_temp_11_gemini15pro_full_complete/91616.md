hyperaspist:/haɪpərˈæspɪst/| n.|超级捍卫者，超级保护者|A person or thing that provides exceptional protection or defense.  It's built from "hyper-" (meaning "over, beyond, excessively") and "aspist" (from the Greek "aspis," meaning shield).|The lawyer acted as a hyperaspist for his client, deflecting every accusation.|近义词: champion, defender, protector, guardian|反义词: assailant, attacker, aggressor|词性: noun
