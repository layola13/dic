adulterator:/əˈdʌltəreɪtər/
n.
掺假者；伪造者
Someone who adulterates something, especially food or drink.
The food adulterator was caught and fined heavily.  这个食品掺假者被抓获并被处以巨额罚款。
Synonyms: falsifier, debaser, corrupter
Antonyms: purifier, refiner
词性: 名词

adulterate:/əˈdʌltəreɪt/
v.
掺假；掺杂
To make something impure or weaker by adding something of inferior quality.
They adulterated the milk with water. 他们用水掺牛奶。
Synonyms: contaminate, taint, pollute
Antonyms: purify, refine
词性: 动词
