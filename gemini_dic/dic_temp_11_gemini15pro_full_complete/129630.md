non-contradiction:/ˌnɒn kɒntrəˈdɪkʃən/ /ˌnɑːn kɑːntrəˈdɪkʃən/ | n. | 不矛盾，无矛盾 | The principle of non-contradiction states that something cannot be both true and false at the same time and in the same respect.  无矛盾律指出，某事物不可能在同一时间、同一方面既为真又为假。 | The lawyer pointed out a non-contradiction in the witness's testimony. 律师指出了证人证词中的一个自相矛盾之处。 | synonyms: consistency, coherence, agreement, harmony | antonyms: contradiction, inconsistency, conflict, discrepancy | noun
