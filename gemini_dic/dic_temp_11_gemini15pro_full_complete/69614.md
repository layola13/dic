femininity:/ˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 女性气质，女人味 | The quality of being feminine; womanliness. | She exuded an aura of quiet femininity. 她散发着一种安静的女性气质。|  She dressed with classic femininity. 她穿着打扮充满了古典的女性气质。 | synonyms: womanliness, femaleness, ladylikeness | antonyms: masculinity, maleness | noun

Etymology:
From French *féminité*, from Latin *fēminīnitās* (“womanhood”), from *fēminīnus* (“female, feminine”), from *fēmina* ("woman").
