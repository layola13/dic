inflexibleness:/ɪnˈflɛksəbəlnəs/
|n.|不灵活性，僵硬，顽固|The inflexibleness of the rules made it difficult to adapt to changing circumstances. 规则的不灵活性使得难以适应变化的环境。|rigidity, stiffness, stubbornness|flexibility, adaptability, pliability|noun
inflexible:/ɪnˈflɛksəbəl/
|adj.|不灵活的，僵硬的，顽固的|His inflexible attitude prevented a compromise. 他不灵活的态度阻碍了妥协。|rigid, stiff, unyielding|flexible, adaptable, pliable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inflexibilis "unbending," from in- "not" + flexibilis "flexible," from Latin flectere "to bend"
