dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ /ˈdaɪˌleɪt/| v. |扩张，扩大|to make or become wider, larger, or more open|The pupils of your eyes dilate when you enter a dark room.  当你进入黑暗的房间时，你的瞳孔会放大。|
dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ /ˈdaɪˌleɪt/| v. |详述，细说|to speak or write at length or in detail|The professor dilated on the subject for over an hour.  教授详细阐述了这个主题一个多小时。|
dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ /ˈdaɪˌleɪt/| n. |扩张器|A surgical instrument used to enlarge an opening or cavity in the body|The surgeon used a dilate to widen the opening.  外科医生使用扩张器来扩大开口。|
dilator:/ˈdaɪˌleɪtər/| n. |扩张器，扩张肌|Something that dilates, especially a muscle that dilates a part of the body, or a surgical instrument used to enlarge an opening or cavity|The eye doctor used a pupillary dilator.  眼科医生使用了瞳孔扩张器。|


近义词:expand, enlarge, widen, stretch, amplify, elaborate (v.) expander, stretcher (n.)
反义词:contract, constrict, shrink, compress (v.) constrictor (n.)
词性:verb, noun


词源: From Latin dīlātāre, past participle of dīlātare (“to spread out, extend”), from dī- (“apart, asunder”) + lātus (“wide, broad”).
