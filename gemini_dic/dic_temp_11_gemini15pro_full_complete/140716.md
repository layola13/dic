omniformity:/ˌɒmnɪˈfɔːmɪti/
n.
全形性; 多样统一性
The state or quality of having every form or shape; infinite diversity in unity.
The artist's work explored the concept of omniformity, showcasing a multitude of styles and techniques within a single cohesive piece.  艺术家作品探索了全形性的概念，在单一的 cohesive 作品中展示了多种风格和技巧。
Omniformity is a key concept in some philosophical systems. 全形性在一些哲学体系中是一个关键概念。

近义词: universality, all-inclusiveness, comprehensiveness
反义词: uniformity, homogeneity, sameness
词性: noun
