mudding:/ˈmʌdɪŋ/| n.| 泥浆浴; 在泥地里玩耍; 在泥泞中行驶; (尤指越野车的) 泥地驾驶 |The act of driving a vehicle, especially an off-road vehicle, through mud for recreation or sport. Also, the application of mud, as in a mud bath, or simply playing in mud. |例句：The kids were covered in mud after an afternoon of mudding. 孩子们玩了一下午泥巴，浑身都是泥。

例句：Mudding is a popular pastime in some rural areas. 在一些农村地区，泥地驾驶是一种很受欢迎的消遣。|近义词：off-roading, four-wheeling, mud-bogging |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑paving, concreting - 指铺路)|词性: 名词
