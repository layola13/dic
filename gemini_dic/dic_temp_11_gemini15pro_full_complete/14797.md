awry:/əˈraɪ/
adjective
错误的，出错的；歪的，斜的
In a state of disorder, disarray, or error; wrong; askew.
The plan went awry when the funding fell through.  资金不足导致计划失败。
The picture hung awry on the wall.  画歪挂在墙上。
近义词: amiss, wrong, askew, crooked, faulty
反义词: right, correct, straight, orderly
词性: adjective

awry:/əˈraɪ/
adverb
错误地，出错地；歪地，斜地
In a disordered, disarrayed, or erroneous manner; wrongly; askew.
Something had gone awry with the engine.  引擎出了故障。
Her hat was perched awry on her head.  她的帽子歪戴在头上。
近义词: amiss, wrongly, askew
反义词: right, correctly, straight
词性: adverb
