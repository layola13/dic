batta:/ˈbætə/| n.|额外津贴，特别补贴；加班费；伙食费|An allowance of money given in addition to the regular pay, especially to soldiers or persons on special duty, or for extra work; additional pay; extra wages.  It can also refer to an allowance for food or board.|例句：He received a batta for working overtime. 他因加班得到了一笔津贴。

例句：The soldiers received a batta for their service in the harsh conditions. 士兵们因在艰苦条件下服役而获得津贴。|近义词：allowance, bonus, supplement, extra pay|反义词: deduction|词性:noun
