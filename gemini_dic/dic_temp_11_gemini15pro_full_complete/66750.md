exorhiza:/ˌɛksoʊˈraɪzə/| n. |外生根|A root that grows from a stem above ground level, as in some mangroves.  An aerial root that arises from the stem and grows downward into the ground.|The mangrove's exorhizas provided stability in the soft mud. 红树林的外生根在软泥中提供了稳定性。|近义词: aerial root, prop root|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subterranean root)|词性:noun
