anklet: /ˈæŋklɪt/
n.
脚链
An anklet is an ornament worn around the ankle.
She wore a delicate silver anklet. 她戴着一条精致的银脚链。

The dancer's anklets jingled as she moved.  舞者的脚链随着她的舞动发出叮当声。

近义词: ankle bracelet, ankle chain
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ankle + -et (diminutive suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *ancleow.
