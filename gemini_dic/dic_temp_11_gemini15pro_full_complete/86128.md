hearteners:/ˈhɑːrtnərz/
n.
鼓舞人心的人或事物
People or things that boost morale or spirits.
The coach's words were real hearteners for the team after their recent defeats. 教练的话在球队最近几次失利后真正鼓舞了士气。
synonyms: encouragements, inspirations, boosts, morale boosters
antonyms: discouragements, depressants, dampeners
noun
