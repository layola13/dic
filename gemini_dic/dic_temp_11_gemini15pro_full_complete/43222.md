cracked:/krækt/ | adj. | 破裂的，裂开的；声音沙哑的；精神失常的；技艺高超的 | broken, split; having a rough or harsh voice; mentally unstable; highly skilled | The vase is cracked.  花瓶破裂了。 | synonyms: broken, fractured, split, chipped, damaged, flawed, impaired, unsound, faulty | antonyms: intact, whole, undamaged, perfect, flawless, sound | adjective

cracked:/krækt/ | v. | （使）破裂；（使）发出爆裂声；砸开；破解（密码）；崩溃；变沙哑 | break, split; make a sharp snapping sound; hit forcefully; decipher (a code); break down; become hoarse | The ice cracked under the weight of the skaters. 冰在溜冰者的重量下破裂了。 | synonyms: break, fracture, split, shatter, snap, burst, splinter, chip | antonyms: mend, repair, fix, heal | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *craked, *kraked, from Old English cracian (“to crack, crash, resound”), from Proto-Germanic *krakōną (“to crack, break, make a noise”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱreg- (“to resound, rattle”). Cognate with West Frisian krake (“to crack”), Dutch kraken (“to crack”), German krachen (“to crash, crack”), Danish krakke (“to crack”), Swedish kraka (“to crack”), Icelandic krakka (“to crack”).
