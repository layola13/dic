indefensibility:/ˌɪndɪˌfɛnsəˈbɪləti/
noun
不可辩护，无法辩护
The quality or state of being impossible to defend or justify.
His indefensibility in the face of overwhelming evidence led to his conviction.  他在压倒性证据面前的不可辩护导致了他的定罪。
The report exposed the indefensibility of the government's policy. 该报告揭露了政府政策的不可辩护性。
近义词:untenable, unjustifiable, inexcusable
反义词: defensible, justifiable, excusable
词性:n.


indefensible:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbl/
adjective
不可辩护的，无法辩护的
Impossible to defend or justify.
His actions were utterly indefensible. 他的行为完全不可辩护。
The company's decision to pollute the river was indefensible.  该公司污染河流的决定是不可辩护的。
近义词:untenable, unjustifiable, inexcusable
反义词: defensible, justifiable, excusable
词性:adj.


词源:
From in- (not) + defensible, from defend (to protect) + -ible (capable of being). Ultimately from Latin defendere (to ward off).
