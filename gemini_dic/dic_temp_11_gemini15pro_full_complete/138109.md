norroy:/ˈnɔːrɔɪ/
n.
北方纹章院院长; 北方纹章官
The chief herald of the College of Arms in England, north of the River Trent.
The Norroy King of Arms is responsible for heraldry in the north of England.  北方纹章院院长负责英格兰北部地区的纹章事务。
Norroy granted the family a new coat of arms. 北方纹章官授予了这个家族一个新的盾形纹章。
近义词: herald, king of arms
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Anglo-Norman *norreis, *norrois (“northern”), from Old Norse norrænn (“northern”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nurþrą (“north”).
