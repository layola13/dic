词源:
From Middle English *clever, from Old English clæfer (“clay”), from Proto-Germanic *klaiwaz (“clay”). Cognate with Dutch klei (“clay”), German Klei (“clay”), Swedish klera (“clay”).  Originally referred to skill in working with clay, later generalized to mental agility.
