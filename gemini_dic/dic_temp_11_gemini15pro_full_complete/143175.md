outclimbed:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmd/
verb
胜过攀登，爬得比…高
surpassed in climbing; climbed higher or faster than.
The young mountaineer outclimbed his more experienced partner.  年轻的登山者比他更有经验的同伴爬得更高。
Synonyms:  surpassed, bested, outstripped, exceeded
Antonyms:  lost to, trailed, lagged behind
词性: 动词
