dispatching:/dɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
n.|派遣，调度，发送|The dispatching of troops to the border was swift. 部队迅速被派往边境。|近义词：sending, shipping, distributing|反义词：receiving, collecting, retaining|词性:名词

dispatching:/dɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
adj.|发送的，调度的|The dispatching office is very busy. 调度室非常忙碌。|近义词：sending, distributing|反义词：receiving, collecting|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French despescher, from des- (expressing reversal) + pescher "to fish out," from Late Latin piscare, from Latin piscis "fish."  Originally meaning to expedite, rid oneself of something. The meaning shifted to sending something off quickly.
