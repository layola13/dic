molybdenums:/məˈlɪbdənəmz/
|n.|钼（元素符号Mo）|A silvery-white, hard, transition metal, molybdenum is used in steel alloys. |Molybdenums are essential for the growth of some plants. 钼对某些植物的生长至关重要。|Synonym: Mo|Antonym: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *molybdaenum*, from Ancient Greek *μόλυβδος* (molybdos, “lead”), because molybdenite (molybdenum sulfide, MoS₂) was confused with lead ore (galena, lead sulfide, PbS).
