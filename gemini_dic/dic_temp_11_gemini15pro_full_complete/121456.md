misjoining:/ˌmɪsˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
|n.|错误连接，连接错误|The misjoining of the pipes caused a leak. 管道连接错误导致了泄漏。|
|英文解析: The act of joining something incorrectly, resulting in a faulty connection or combination.|例句：The misjoining of the wires caused the circuit to malfunction. 电线连接错误导致电路故障。|近义词：misconnection, misalignment|反义词：correct joining, proper connection|词性:n.
|词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "joining" (from the verb "join," ultimately from Latin "jungere," meaning "to join").|
