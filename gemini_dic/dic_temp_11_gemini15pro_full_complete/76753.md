gamekeeping:/ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɪŋ/
n.
狩猎看守；猎场管理；野生动物管理
The activity of managing land and wildlife in order to support hunting or shooting.
He enjoys gamekeeping on the large estate. 他喜欢在这片广阔的庄园里做狩猎看守工作。
近义词:wildlife management, hunting management
反义词:poaching
