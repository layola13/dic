USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she eaten today? = 她今天吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all day? = 他一整天都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? = 如果我不在那里会发生什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want. = 没有我想要的东西。
