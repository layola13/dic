against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/
|idiomatic phrase|违背意愿；格格不入；|Going against the grain means doing something that is difficult or unpleasant because it contradicts one's natural inclinations or beliefs.|例句：Asking for help goes against the grain for him.  寻求帮助违背了他的意愿。|近义词：against one's nature, against one's better judgment, counterintuitive|反义词: naturally, instinctively, willingly|phrase

against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/
|idiomatic phrase|违反常规；|Going against the grain means doing something that is different from the usual or accepted way of doing things.|例句：Her unconventional approach went against the grain in the conservative company. 她非传统的方式与保守的公司格格不入。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, atypical|反义词: conventional, traditional, typical|phrase
