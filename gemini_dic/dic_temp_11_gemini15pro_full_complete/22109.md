bolshies:/ˈbɒlʃɪz/| n.|布尔什维克，激进分子|A derogatory term for Bolsheviks, often used to describe anyone with politically radical or left-wing views.|Those bolshies are always causing trouble. 那些布尔什维克总是惹麻烦。|Synonyms: communists, radicals, revolutionaries |Antonyms: conservatives, reactionaries|noun


bolshie:/ˈbɒlʃi/| adj.|布尔什维克的，激进的|Relating to or characteristic of Bolsheviks; (informal) rebellious and difficult.|He's got a bolshie attitude. 他态度很叛逆。|Synonyms: rebellious, radical, revolutionary, defiant|Antonyms: compliant, obedient, conservative|adjective
