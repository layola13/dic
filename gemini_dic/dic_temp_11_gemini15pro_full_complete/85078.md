hard-rocker:/ˈhɑːrd ˈrɒkə(r)/ (US) /ˈhɑːrd ˈrɑːkər/| n. |硬摇滚乐手；硬摇滚乐迷|A musician or fan of hard rock music, a subgenre of rock music characterized by aggressive vocals and highly amplified electric guitars.|例句：The hard-rocker wore a ripped denim jacket and band t-shirt.  这位硬摇滚乐迷穿着破洞牛仔夹克和乐队T恤。

|近义词：metalhead, rocker, headbanger|反义词:pop fan, folk music enthusiast|词性:noun


hard-rocking:/ˈhɑːrd ˈrɒkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɑːrd ˈrɑːkɪŋ/| adj. |硬摇滚的|Characterized by or relating to hard rock music.|例句：They went to a hard-rocking concert last night. 他们昨晚去听了一场硬摇滚音乐会。

|近义词：heavy metal, loud, intense|反义词:soft, mellow, quiet|词性:adjective
