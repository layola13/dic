knee-deep:/ˈniː diːp/| adj.|齐膝深的；深深卷入的；埋头于…的|immersed to the knees; deeply involved or engrossed in something.|例句：The children were knee-deep in the stream, looking for tadpoles. 孩子们在小溪里水深及膝地寻找蝌蚪。

例句：He's knee-deep in debt. 他债台高筑。|近义词：immersed, engrossed, involved, overwhelmed|反义词：superficial, uninvolved|词性:形容词
