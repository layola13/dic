horsefish:/ˈhɔːrsfɪʃ/| n. |马面鱼；海鲂|Any of various elongate, compressed fishes of the family Congiopodidae, found in shallow temperate waters of the Southern Hemisphere and having a long dorsal fin and a horselike face.|The horsefish is a master of camouflage, blending seamlessly with the seabed.  这种马面鱼是伪装大师，能与海床完美融合。| Horsefish are often found near kelp forests. 马面鱼经常在海藻林附近被发现。|近义词：sea robin, gurnard (some species)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
