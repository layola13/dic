genealogy:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒi/ /ˌdʒɛniˈælədʒi/| n.|家谱；家系；宗谱；世系；起源；发展史|A genealogy is a line of descent traced continuously from an ancestor.|She can trace her genealogy back to the 17th century. 她可以追溯她的家谱到17世纪。|Synonyms: lineage, ancestry, descent, pedigree, family tree|Antonyms: N/A|noun

genealogy:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒi/ /ˌdʒɛniˈælədʒi/| n.|系谱学|Genealogy is the study of family history.|He's very interested in genealogy and has traced his family back hundreds of years. 他对系谱学很感兴趣，已经追溯了他的家族几百年的历史。|Synonyms: family history research|Antonyms: N/A|noun
