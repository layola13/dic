outthrew: /ˌaʊtˈθruː/
verb
投掷得比…更远；超过…的投掷距离
throw (something) further or with more force than.
例句：
He outthrew his opponent in the javelin competition.
他在标枪比赛中投掷得比他的对手更远。

例句：
She outthrew everyone on the team.
她比队里的任何人都投得远。

近义词：outdistance, surpass, exceed
反义词：fall short of

词性: verb (动词)

词源: out- + threw (past tense of throw)
