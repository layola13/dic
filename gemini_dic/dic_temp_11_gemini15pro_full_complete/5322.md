allowancing:/əˈlaʊənsɪŋ/
n.
定量供应；限额供应；配给；津贴
the process of giving someone a fixed amount of something, especially money, that they are allowed to use or have
例句：The allowancing of food during the war was strictly controlled.  战争期间食物配给受到严格控制。

例句：Strict allowancing of resources is necessary during a drought. 干旱期间必须严格控制资源配给。

近义词：rationing, allocation, allotment
反义词：unlimited supply, abundance

词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "allowance," which comes from Old French "aloue," meaning "something granted or permitted."  Ultimately derived from Latin "allocare,"  meaning "to place, assign."
