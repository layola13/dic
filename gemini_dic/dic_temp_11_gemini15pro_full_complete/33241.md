chronologies:/krəˈnɒlədʒiz/
n.|年表，大事记|A chronology is an arrangement of events in their order of occurrence in time.|The book presented a detailed chronology of the war.|近义词:timeline, history, record, annals|反义词:disorder, chaos|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin chronologia, from Ancient Greek χρόνος (khrónos, “time”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
