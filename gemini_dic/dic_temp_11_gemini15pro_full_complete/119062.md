metonymies:/ˌmɛtənɪmiːz/| n. |转喻|A figure of speech in which a thing or concept is referred to by the name of something closely associated with that thing or concept.|Hollywood is a metonymy for the US film industry. 好莱坞是美国电影业的代名词。|Synonyms:  figure of speech, trope, metaphor, synecdoche|Antonyms:  literal language|noun
