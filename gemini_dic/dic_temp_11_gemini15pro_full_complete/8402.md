anti-sovietism:/ˌæntiˈsoʊviətɪzəm/
n.|反苏主义，反苏情绪|Opposition to the Soviet Union, its ideology, and its influence.|Example:  Anti-sovietism was prevalent in the United States during the Cold War. 冷战期间，反苏主义在美国盛行。

Example:  His anti-sovietism led him to support dissident groups within the USSR.  他的反苏情绪使他支持苏联内部的异议团体。
|近义词：anti-communism, anti-stalinism|反义词:pro-sovietism, sovietism|词性:noun
