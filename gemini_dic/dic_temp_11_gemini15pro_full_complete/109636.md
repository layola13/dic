letting-up:/ˈlɛtɪŋ ˈʌp/ | n. | 停止，减弱，放松 | a decrease in intensity; a relaxation of effort or vigilance | The letting-up in the rain allowed us to finally go outside. 雨势减弱让我们终于可以出去了。 |  cessation, abatement, relaxation, easing | intensification, increase | noun

Etymology:  From the phrasal verb "let up," which is formed from the verb "let" (Old English *lætan, from Proto-Germanic *lētaną) and the adverb "up."
