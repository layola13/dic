horseplayful:/ˈhɔːrspleɪfʊl/| adj.|爱闹着玩的；嬉戏打闹的|Given to boisterous play, like a young horse; frolicsome. Characterized by rough, playful activity.|例句：The children were horseplayful in the park, running and chasing each other. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏打闹，跑来跑去，互相追逐。

例句：Their horseplayful behavior resulted in a broken lamp. 他们嬉闹的行为导致了一个灯被打碎。|近义词：boisterous, frisky, rambunctious, playful, mischievous|反义词：calm, quiet, subdued, well-behaved|词性:形容词
