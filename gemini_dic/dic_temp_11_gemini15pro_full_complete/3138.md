aesthetican: /ɛsˈθɛtɪkən/ | n. |美学家；审美家|A person who is knowledgeable about or sensitive to beauty, especially in art.|  She considers herself an aesthetican, appreciating the finer details in every painting. 她自认为是一位美学家，欣赏每幅画作中更精细的细节。 | 近义词: aesthete, connoisseur | 反义词: philistine | 词性: noun

词源: From aesthetic + -an.  "aesthetic" comes from Greek "aisthētikos" meaning "perceptive, sensitive,"  from "aisthanesthai" "to perceive."
