monking:/ˈmʌŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 做和尚; 修道士的生活;  | The act of behaving like a monk, especially by withdrawing from worldly pursuits.  It can also refer to the practice or profession of being a monk. | He tried monking for a while, but it wasn't for him.  他尝试过做一段时间的和尚，但这并不适合他。|  Example 2: The monking life appealed to him, with its emphasis on simplicity and contemplation. 修道士的生活吸引了他，因为它强调简单和沉思。 | synonyms: monasticism, religious seclusion | antonyms: worldliness, materialism | noun
