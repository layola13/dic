goutily:/ˈɡaʊtɪli/
adjective
痛风地；患痛风地
affected with gout; characteristic of gout.
He walked goutily, each step a small victory against the pain. 他走起路来像个痛风病人，每一步都像是战胜了疼痛。
近义词:arthritically
反义词:healthily
词性:adjective

goutily:/ˈɡaʊtɪli/
adverb
痛风似地
in a gouty manner.
His fingers were swollen and he moved them goutily. 他的手指肿胀，移动起来像痛风病人一样。
近义词:arthritically
反义词:healthily
词性:adverb
