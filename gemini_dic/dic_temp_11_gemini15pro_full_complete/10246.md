apothegmatic: /ˌæpəθəɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 格言式的；警句般的；简洁的 | Of the nature of or resembling an apothegm; concise and pithy. | Her style is terse and apothegmatic. 她的风格简洁而格言式。 |  Example: His apothegmatic pronouncements were often quoted. 他的格言式言论常被引用。

Example: The professor's lectures were dense with apothegmatic statements. 教授的讲座充满了格言式的陈述。 | Synonyms: pithy, concise, terse, succinct, epigrammatic, aphoristic | Antonyms: verbose, prolix, rambling, diffuse | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apothegmaticus*, from Greek *apothegmatikos*, from *apothegma* ("apothegm").
