grapefruit:/ˈɡreɪpˌfruːt/ (US) /ˈɡreɪpˌfruːt/ (UK)| n.|葡萄柚|A citrus fruit that is larger than an orange, with a slightly bitter yellow or pink flesh.|例句：I enjoy a grapefruit for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃一个葡萄柚。

例句：She cut the grapefruit in half. 她把葡萄柚切成两半。

近义词：pomelo, citrus|反义词:|词性:noun


grapefruits:/ˈɡreɪpˌfruːts/ (US) /ˈɡreɪpˌfruːts/ (UK)| n.|葡萄柚（复数）|Plural form of grapefruit.|例句：I bought a bag of grapefruits from the market. 我从市场买了一袋葡萄柚。

例句：These grapefruits are very juicy. 这些葡萄柚很多汁。

近义词：citrus fruits|反义词:|词性:noun
