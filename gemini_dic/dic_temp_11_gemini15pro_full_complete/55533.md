distributional:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənəl/| adj.|分布的，分配的|Relating to the way in which something is spread or shared over an area or between a group of people.|The distributional effects of the tax cuts were felt most by the wealthy.|Synonyms: dispersive, scattering, spreading|Antonyms: concentrated, localized|adjective
