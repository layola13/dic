overmitigated: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪd/
| adj. |过度缓解的，过度减轻的|Reduced or lessened to an excessive degree.  Having applied mitigation efforts that went too far, resulting in an unintended negative consequence.|The government's overmitigated response to the economic crisis led to a prolonged recession. 政府对经济危机的过度缓解措施导致了长期的经济衰退。|overcompensated, overcorrected|undermitigated|adjective
