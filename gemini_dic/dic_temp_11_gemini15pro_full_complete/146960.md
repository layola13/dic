overirrigated: /ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度灌溉的
Having received too much irrigation.  The land becomes waterlogged and the plants may die.
The overirrigated field was flooded and the crops were damaged.  过度灌溉的田地被淹没了，庄稼也受到了损害。
The farmer realized his mistake when he saw the overirrigated, soggy soil.  当农民看到过度灌溉的、湿漉漉的土壤时，他意识到了自己的错误。
近义词: waterlogged, saturated, flooded
反义词: underwatered, parched, arid
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "irrigate" (verb meaning "to supply water to land or crops by artificial means") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
