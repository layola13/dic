chronostratigraphical:/ˌkrɒnoʊˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/ /ˌkrɑːnoʊˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adj.
年代地层学的
Relating to chronostratigraphy, the branch of geology concerned with establishing the absolute ages of rock strata.
The chronostratigraphical classification of these rocks is complex. 这些岩石的年代地层分类很复杂。
近义词:geochronologic
反义词:None
词性:adjective
