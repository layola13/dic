nlife: /ˈɛnlaɪf/
noun
网络生活;  数码生活;  线上生活
Digital life; online presence and activities.
She documents her nlife extensively on social media. 她详细记录了她的网络生活在社交媒体上。
近义词: online life, digital life, cyberlife, virtual life
反义词: offline life, real life, analog life
词性: n.


Etymology:  A blend of "net" (referring to the internet) and "life."  While not found in traditional dictionaries, it's a neologism used informally, particularly online, to describe one's digital presence and activities.
