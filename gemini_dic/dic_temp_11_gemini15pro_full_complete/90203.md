hound's-tooth:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/| n. adj.|千鸟格，犬牙纹|A duotone textile pattern characterized by broken checks or abstract four-pointed shapes, often in black and white.|She wore a hound's-tooth jacket. 她穿了一件千鸟格外套。|近义词：check, plaid, dogtooth|反义词: solid, plain|词性:名词, 形容词


hound's-tooth check:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ tʃek/| n.|千鸟格纹|A specific type of hound's-tooth pattern, often used for clothing.|The suit was made of a classic hound's-tooth check. 这套西装是用经典的千鸟格纹制成的。|近义词：hound's-tooth, dogtooth check|反义词: solid, plain|词性:名词


词源:  The term "hound's-tooth" refers to the resemblance of the pattern to the jagged teeth of a dog, particularly a hound.  It emerged in 19th-century Scotland, initially in woven wool cloth.
