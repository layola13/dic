kibbutz:/kɪˈbuːts/ /kɪˈbʊts/| n. |集体农庄；基布兹|A kibbutz is a communal settlement in Israel, traditionally based on agriculture.|例句：My cousin lives on a kibbutz in the Negev Desert. 我的表兄弟住在内盖夫沙漠的一个基布兹里。

例句：Life on a kibbutz is based on shared work and resources.  基布兹的生活建立在共同劳动和资源共享的基础上。|近义词：commune, collective, cooperative|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) private farm, individual holding|词性: noun
