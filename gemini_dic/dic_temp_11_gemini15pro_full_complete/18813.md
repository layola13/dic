berth: /bɜːrθ/ (UK) /bɜːrθ/ (US) | n. v. | 卧铺；停泊处；职位；(船)停泊；(人)上床 | A berth is a bed on a train or ship, or a space for a ship to dock. As a verb, it means to moor a ship or to provide a sleeping place. |

例句：
She booked a berth on the overnight train. 她预订了夜班火车上的一个卧铺。

The ship berthed in the harbor. 这艘船停泊在港口。


近义词：dock, mooring, bunk, bed, position, appointment
反义词：(For verb) depart, sail, (For noun - docking space) open sea


词性: noun, verb
