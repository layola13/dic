discriminateness:/dɪˌskrɪmɪnɪtnəs/| n.|辨别力，识别力，鉴赏力|The quality of being discriminating; the ability to distinguish accurately, especially between subtle differences.|Her discriminateness in choosing friends is admirable. 她选择朋友的鉴赏力令人钦佩。|近义词：discernment, judgment, taste, refinement|反义词：indiscriminateness, indiscretion|词性:noun

词源:
From discriminate + -ness.  "Discriminate" comes from the Latin discriminare "to divide, separate, distinguish," from discrimen "separation, distinction."
