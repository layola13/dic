overinterpretations: /ˌəʊvərɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度解读，过度诠释 |  The act of interpreting something, such as a text or an event, in an excessive or unwarranted way, often finding meanings that were not intended. |  His overinterpretations of her comments led to a misunderstanding. 他对她的评论过度解读，导致了误解。| synonyms: misinterpretations, misreadings, distortions | antonyms: accurate interpretations, sound analyses | noun
