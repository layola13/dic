misdeclares:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleərz/
verb
错误地申报；虚报
To declare something incorrectly, especially in an official context.
The company misdeclared its profits to the tax authorities.  该公司向税务机关虚报了利润。
He misdeclared the value of the goods on the customs form. 他在报关单上错误申报了货物价值。
misstate, misrepresent
declare correctly, state accurately
