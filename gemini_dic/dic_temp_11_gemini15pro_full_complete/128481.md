newspapered:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərd/
v.
用报纸覆盖；用报纸包装；在报纸上刊登
Covered or wrapped with newspapers; publicized or reported in newspapers
The shelves were newspapered to protect them from dust.  架子上铺了报纸以防灰尘。
The scandal was newspapered across the country. 这桩丑闻被全国各地的报纸报道。
近义词: publicized, reported, covered
反义词: concealed, hidden
词性: 动词
