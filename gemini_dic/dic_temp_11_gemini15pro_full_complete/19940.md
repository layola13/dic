biodegradable:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|可生物降解的|Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.|Example: Biodegradable packaging helps reduce environmental pollution. 可生物降解的包装有助于减少环境污染。|Synonym: compostable, decomposable|Antonym: non-biodegradable|adjective

biodegradables:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəblz/| n.|可生物降解物|Substances that can be decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.|Example:  Many supermarkets now sell biodegradables. 许多超市现在出售可生物降解产品。|Synonym: compostables, decomposables|Antonym: non-biodegradables|noun
