cambria:/ˈkæmbriə/
noun
威尔士；坎布里亚郡；(印刷)坎布里亚字体
Cambria is a serif typeface designed by Ascender Corp. and hinted by Microsoft.  坎布里亚是一种由Ascender公司设计并由微软微调的衬线字体。
The rolling hills of Cambria are a sight to behold.  坎布里亚郡连绵起伏的丘陵是一道美丽的风景。
Wales, formerly known as Cambria, is a beautiful country. 威尔士，旧称坎布里亚，是一个美丽的国家。
synonyms: Wales, Cumberland
