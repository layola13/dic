nonseasonableness:/ˌnɒnˈsiːzənəblnəs/
| n. |不合时宜，不合时令|The nonseasonableness of the warm weather in December was unsettling. 十二月里温暖的天气不合时宜，令人不安。|The nonseasonableness of the snowstorm in May caused widespread damage to crops. 五月份的暴风雪不合时令，对农作物造成了广泛的损害。|近义词：untimeliness, inappropriateness, inopportuneness|反义词：timeliness, appropriateness, seasonableness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + seasonable + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Seasonable" comes from the Old French "saisonable," meaning "of a season, timely."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
