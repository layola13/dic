captivatingly:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪŋli/
adverb
迷人地；令人着迷地
in a way that holds the attention by being extremely interesting, beautiful, etc.
Her performance was captivatingly beautiful. 她的表演美得令人着迷。
近义词:enchantingly, alluringly, fascinatingly, charmingly
反义词:uninterestingly, dully, tediously, boringly
词性:adverb

captivate:/ˈkæptɪveɪt/
verb
迷住；使着迷
to hold the attention of someone by being extremely interesting, exciting, etc.
The children were captivated by the story. 孩子们被这个故事迷住了。
近义词:enchant, fascinate, charm, enthrall
反义词:bore, repel, disgust
词性:verb

captivating:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
迷人的；令人着迷的
holding the attention by being extremely interesting, beautiful, etc.
The speaker gave a captivating presentation. 演讲者做了一个引人入胜的演讲。
近义词:enchanting, alluring, fascinating, charming
反义词:uninteresting, dull, tedious, boring
词性:adjective
