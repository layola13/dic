abrasional:/əˈbreɪʒənəl/
adjective
磨损的; 引起的磨损的
Relating to or causing abrasion; wearing away by rubbing or scraping.
The abrasive action of the sand smoothed the rocks. 沙子的磨蚀作用使岩石变得光滑。
synonyms: abrasive, grinding, erosive, corrosive
antonyms: smooth, polished, protective
词性: 形容词
