hyperreal:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəl/ | adj. | 超真实的，极度逼真的 | Characterized by heightened or exaggerated realism, often to the point of being surreal or artificial.  Relating to or denoting a system of numbers that includes both infinitely large and infinitely small quantities.| The movie presented a hyperreal vision of the future, with flying cars and robot servants. 这部电影呈现了一个超真实的未来景象，有飞行汽车和机器人仆人。 | 近义词:surreal, exaggerated, heightened, artificial, simulated | 反义词:realistic, natural, authentic | 词性:形容词

Etymology:  From hyper- + real. "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, above, beyond."
