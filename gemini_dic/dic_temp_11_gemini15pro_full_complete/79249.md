glasseye:/ˈɡlæs aɪ/ |n.|玻璃眼，假眼|An artificial eye, usually made of glass or plastic, used to replace a missing eye.|She wore a glasseye after losing her eye in an accident. 她在一场事故中失去了一只眼睛后戴上了假眼。|Synonyms: prosthetic eye, artificial eye|Antonyms: natural eye|noun

glasseye:/ˈɡlæs aɪ/ |adj.|冷漠的，无表情的|Having a fixed, unblinking stare, like a glass eye; showing no emotion.|He gave me a glasseye stare. 他冷漠地盯着我。|Synonyms: expressionless, impassive, blank|Antonyms: expressive, emotional|adjective
