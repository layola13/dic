hauteur:/oʊˈtɜːr (US), həʊˈtɜːr (UK)| n.|高度，身高，傲慢|A quality of aloof arrogance; haughtiness.  The sheer hauteur of the minister when addressing the public was shocking. |例句：The artist gazed down at the crowd with an air of hauteur.  这位艺术家以一种傲慢的神态俯视着人群。

The building has a hauteur of 100 meters. 这座建筑的高度为100米。|近义词：arrogance, haughtiness, pride, loftiness|反义词：humility, modesty|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *hauteur*, from Old French *hauteor*, from Latin *altitūdo* (“height”).
