brewsterite:/ˈbruːstəraɪt/| n. |布鲁斯特沸石|A zeolite mineral, hydrated strontium and barium aluminosilicate.  It's named after Sir David Brewster, a Scottish physicist and mineralogist.|Brewsterite is a relatively rare mineral found in volcanic cavities. 布鲁斯特沸石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于火山腔中。|Synonym: zeolite |Antonym: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Sir David Brewster (1781-1868), Scottish physicist and mineralogist, + -ite (suffix forming names of minerals).
