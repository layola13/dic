full-time:/ˌfʊlˈtaɪm/ | adj. adv.| 全职的，全日制的；全职地，全日制地 |  Working or operating for the full number of hours considered normal or standard in a particular job or profession. | She has a full-time job as a teacher. 她有一份教师的全职工作。 |  full-time: He works full-time at the factory. 他在工厂全职工作。 |Synonyms: permanent, regular | Antonyms: part-time, temporary | adjective/adverb


full-time:/ˌfʊlˈtaɪm/ | n.| 全职工作；全日制 | A job or position requiring the full number of hours considered normal or standard in a particular field. |  She's looking for a full-time. 她在找一份全职工作。 | full-time: It's difficult to balance studies with a full-time. 平衡学习和全职工作很困难。 |Synonyms: full-time job, full-time position | Antonyms: part-time job, part-time position | noun
