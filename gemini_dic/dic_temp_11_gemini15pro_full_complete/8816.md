antiepileptics:/ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪks/| n.|抗癫痫药|Medications used to prevent or control epileptic seizures.|The doctor prescribed antiepileptics to manage her seizures. 医生给她开了抗癫痫药来控制她的癫痫发作。|Synonyms: anticonvulsants|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps proconvulsants in a research context)|noun

antiepileptic:/ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| adj.|抗癫痫的|Relating to or used in the treatment of epilepsy.|She is taking antiepileptic medication. 她正在服用抗癫痫药物。|Synonyms: anticonvulsant|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps proconvulsant in a research context)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + epileptic.  "Anti-" is a Greek prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". "Epileptic" derives from the Greek word "epilēptikos," meaning "seized,"  referring to the seizures characteristic of epilepsy.
