nag:/næɡ/| v. n.|不断抱怨；唠叨；批评；使烦恼；烦扰；纠缠；老马；驽马|to annoy someone by often complaining to them about their behaviour or urging them to do something|例句：She nagged him about his drinking. 她不断地抱怨他酗酒。|近义词：pester, harass, annoy, badger, henpeck|反义词：praise, commend|词性:动词/名词

nag:/næɡ/| n.|老马；驽马|a horse, especially one that is old or in poor condition|例句：He rode an old nag. 他骑着一匹老马。|近义词：hack, jade|反义词：steed, thoroughbred|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *naggen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse gnaga (“to gnaw”).
