counterinstitution:/ˈkaʊntərˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | n. | 对抗性机构；反体制机构 | An organization that is created to oppose or challenge an existing institution, often with the aim of promoting alternative values or practices. |  Example 1: The free clinic served as a counterinstitution to the expensive private hospitals in the area.  这个免费诊所是对该地区昂贵私立医院的一种对抗性机构。

Example 2:  Several counterinstitutions emerged during the civil rights movement, challenging segregation and discrimination.  在民权运动期间出现了几家对抗性机构，挑战种族隔离和歧视。 | Synonyms: alternative institution, opposing organization, rival institution | Antonyms: established institution, mainstream institution | noun
