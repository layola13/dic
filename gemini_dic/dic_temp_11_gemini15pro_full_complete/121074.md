miscontent:/ˌmɪskənˈtent/ | n. | 不满意; 不满 | Dissatisfaction; lack of contentment. | The new policy caused widespread miscontent among the employees. 新政策在员工中引起了广泛的不满。 |  近义词: discontent, dissatisfaction, displeasure | 反义词: contentment, satisfaction, pleasure | noun

miscontent:/ˌmɪskənˈtent/ | vt. | 使不满意; 使不满 | To make discontented. | The long hours and low pay miscontented the workers. 长时间的工作和低工资使工人感到不满。 | 近义词: displease, dissatisfy | 反义词: please, satisfy | verb
