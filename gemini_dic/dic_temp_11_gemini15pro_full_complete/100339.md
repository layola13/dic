intersprinkling:/ˌɪntərˈsprɪŋklɪŋ/ | n. |点缀，散布；零星分布|The poem is an intersprinkling of humor and pathos.|这首诗充满了幽默和悲伤的情感。|近义词：interspersion, scattering, sprinkling|反义词：concentration, consolidation|词性:名词

Etymology: From inter- + sprinkling, from sprinkle + -ing. "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," while "sprinkling" refers to the act of scattering small drops or particles.
