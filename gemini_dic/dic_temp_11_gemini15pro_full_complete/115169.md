maniac:/ˈmeɪniæk/ (US), /ˈmeɪniæk/ (UK)| n. |疯子；狂热者；癖好者|A person exhibiting extreme symptoms of mental illness, especially marked by violent behaviour; a person who has a very strong interest in a particular activity|He drove like a maniac. 他开车像个疯子。|近义词：lunatic, madman, psychopath, fanatic, enthusiast|反义词：sane person, moderate person|noun

maniacal:/məˈnaɪək(ə)l/ (US), /məˈnaɪək(ə)l/ (UK)| adj. |疯狂的；狂热的；着迷的|Exhibiting or suggestive of extreme symptoms of mental illness; characterized by excessive enthusiasm or obsession|His maniacal laughter echoed through the halls. 他疯狂的笑声回荡在大厅里。|近义词：insane, crazy, frantic, obsessive, fanatical|反义词：sane, rational, calm, moderate|adjective
