brickyards:/ˈbrɪkjɑːdz/
n.
砖厂，砖窑
A place where bricks are made.
The brickyards were busy in the summer months. 夏天的时候，砖厂都很忙碌。

The old brickyards are now a housing development. 老砖厂现在变成了住宅区。
近义词：brick factory, brickworks
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for a place.  Could consider words related to destruction like "demolition site" if contrasting the purpose.)
词性:noun
