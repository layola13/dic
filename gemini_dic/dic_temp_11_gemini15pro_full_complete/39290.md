conic:/ˈkɑːnɪk/ /ˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 圆锥的；圆锥形的；二次曲线的 | Relating to or shaped like a cone; a curve formed by the intersection of a cone with a plane. |  The conic shape of the volcano was striking against the sky.  火山圆锥形的轮廓在天空的映衬下格外醒目。 | The searchlight emitted a conic beam of light. 探照灯发出锥形的的光束。 | 近义词: conical | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "planar" or "flat" in certain contexts) | 词性: adjective
