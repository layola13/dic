crowd:/kraʊd/| n. vt. vi.|人群，群众；拥挤，挤满|A large number of people gathered together in a disorganized or unruly way.  A large number of things positioned close together.|The crowd cheered as the band took the stage.  人群在乐队上台时欢呼起来。|The fans crowded around the stage. 歌迷们挤在舞台周围。|近义词：throng, mob, swarm, horde|反义词:few, scattering|n. v.
