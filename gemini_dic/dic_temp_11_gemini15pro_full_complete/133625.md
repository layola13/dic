nonforeclosures:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈkloʊʒərz/ | n. | 非丧失抵押品赎回权 |  Properties that have not been subject to foreclosure.  This refers to homes or other real estate where the borrower has continued to make payments and the lender has not initiated foreclosure proceedings. | The report showed a significant increase in nonforeclosures this quarter, indicating a healthier housing market.  该报告显示本季度非丧失抵押品赎回权的数量显著增加，表明房地产市场更加健康。

The number of nonforeclosures is expected to rise as the economy improves. 预计随着经济好转，非丧失抵押品赎回权的数量将会上升。 | 近义词: retained properties, properties not in foreclosure | 反义词: foreclosures | 词性: 名词
