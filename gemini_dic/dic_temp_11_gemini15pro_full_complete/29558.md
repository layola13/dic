catchingly:/ˈkætʃɪŋli/| adv.|动人地；引人注目地；迷人地|In a way that is attractive or interesting and makes you want to know more.|Her brightly colored dress was catchingly displayed in the shop window. 她的颜色鲜艳的连衣裙在商店橱窗里引人注目地陈列着。|Synonyms: attractively, appealingly, enticingly, charmingly|Antonyms: unattractively, repellently, dully|adverb

Etymology->
From catch + -ing + -ly.  "Catch" in this sense means to attract attention or interest.
