chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ | n. |变色龙，见风使舵的人|A small lizard that can change the color of its skin.  A person who changes their opinions or behaviour according to the situation.|The chameleon blended in perfectly with the leaves. 变色龙与叶子完美地融合在一起。|He's a political chameleon, changing his views to suit the prevailing opinion. 他是一个政治变色龙，改变自己的观点以适应主流意见。|Synonyms: lizard, adapter, opportunist |Antonyms: nonconformist, maverick|noun
