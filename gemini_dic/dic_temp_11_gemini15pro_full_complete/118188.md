mentor:/ˈmɛntɔːr/ (US), /ˈmɛntɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. v. | 导师，顾问；指导 | A trusted counselor or guide.  |  She was a mentor to many young writers. 她是一位许多年轻作家的导师。| advisor, guide, tutor, counselor | protégé, mentee | noun, verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Méntōr*, the name of Odysseus's trusted advisor in Homer's *Odyssey*.
