outsourcing: /ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 外包，外购 | The practice of obtaining goods or services from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. | Many companies are outsourcing their IT operations to save money. 许多公司正在外包他们的IT运营以节省成本。|  The company decided on outsourcing its customer service. 公司决定将其客户服务外包。| 近义词: subcontracting, offshoring | 反义词: insourcing | 词性: 名词

outsourcings: /ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋz/ | n. |  外包（复数形式，较少使用） | Multiple instances of the practice of obtaining goods or services from an outside supplier. |  The company's recent outsourcings have led to job losses in the local community.  公司最近的几项外包导致了当地社区的失业。| The report detailed the various outsourcings undertaken by the corporation. 报告详细列出了公司进行的各种外包。 | 近义词:  subcontracts | 反义词: insourcings | 词性: 名词
