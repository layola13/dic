double-dated:/ˌdʌbəl ˈdeɪtɪd/
adjective
双重约会的；有双重日期的
Having two dates, appointments, or meanings.  Specifically, having two different dates written or printed on it, usually fraudulently.
The investigator found a double-dated receipt, proving the suspect was lying.  调查员发现了一张有双重日期的收据，证明嫌疑人在撒谎。
Near Synonyms:  misdated, falsified, fraudulent
Antonyms: accurately dated, valid
词性: 形容词


double-dated:/ˌdʌbəl ˈdeɪtɪd/
verb
加双重日期；同时约会两人
To put two different dates on something. Also, to date two different people at the same time.
He double-dated the check to avoid paying taxes. 他在支票上写了两个日期以逃避纳税。
Near Synonyms:  backdate, postdate
Antonyms: date correctly
词性: 动词
