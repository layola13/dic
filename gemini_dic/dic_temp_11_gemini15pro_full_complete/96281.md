indies: /ˈɪndiz/
n.
独立电影；独立音乐；独立开发者；独立游戏
independent films, music, developers, or games, especially those not produced or owned by large companies.
Example: He prefers watching indies to Hollywood blockbusters. 他更喜欢看独立电影，而不是好莱坞大片。
Example:  The festival showcases the best in new indies and foreign films.  这个电影节展示了最新的优秀独立电影和外国电影。

近义词: independent films/music/games, alternative, underground
反义词: mainstream, commercial
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
