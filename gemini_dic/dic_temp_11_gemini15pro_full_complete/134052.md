nonidiomatically:/ˌnɒnɪdiəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非惯用地，不自然地 | In a way that is not typical of the natural way in which a native speaker would express something; literally. | He translated the poem nonidiomatically, focusing on literal accuracy rather than natural flow. 他翻译这首诗时过于字面，注重字面准确性而不是自然流畅。 |  近义词: literally, awkwardly, unnaturally | 反义词: idiomatically, naturally, fluently | 词性: 副词
