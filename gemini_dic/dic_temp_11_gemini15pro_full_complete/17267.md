bayoneting:/ˌbeɪəˈneːtɪŋ/| n. / v.| 刺刀突击；用刺刀刺杀 |The act of attacking or killing someone with a bayonet. bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/ | n. / v. | 刺刀；用刺刀刺杀 | A blade that can be attached to a rifle for use in hand-to-hand fighting. He was bayoneted to death. 他被刺刀刺死了. | 近义词：blade, dagger | 反义词: (No direct antonym for the noun. For the verb, perhaps "disarm") | 词性: noun, verb

词源: From French *bayonnette*, from Bayonne, a city in southwestern France where bayonets were supposedly first made.
