apara:/əˈpɑːrə/ | n. | 衣服，服装; 装饰; 外表; (尤指妇女的)装饰品 | Clothing, especially outer garments; adornment; outward show; trappings, especially those of a woman. | She wore a beautiful apara to the festival. 她穿着漂亮的衣服去参加节日。| The peacock displayed its magnificent apara.  孔雀展示了它华丽的羽毛。 |Synonyms: attire, garments, clothing, raiment, vestments, costume, finery, array | Antonyms: nudity, bareness | noun

apara:/əˈpɑːrə/ | n. | (印度教) 宗教仪式 | (Hinduism) a religious ceremony |  They performed the apara with great devotion. 他们虔诚地举行了宗教仪式。 | The apara involved chanting and offerings of flowers.  这个仪式包括唱诵和献花。| Synonyms: ritual, ceremony, rite, observance | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly informality, secularism) | noun
