一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been studying at university?  她在大学里一直在学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by then? 到那时他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
