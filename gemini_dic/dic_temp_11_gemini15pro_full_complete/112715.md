loy:/lɔɪ/
词性:  adjective (形容词)
中文翻译: 忠诚的，忠贞的
英文解析:  Loyal and faithful, especially to a cause, ideal, custom, leader, or loved one.
例句：He remained loy to his principles. 他始终忠于自己的原则。
例句：She was a loy friend and supporter. 她是一位忠实的朋友和支持者。
近义词: faithful, true, devoted, constant, steadfast
反义词: disloyal, faithless, treacherous, unfaithful
词源:  From Middle English *loye, leie*, from Anglo-Norman *loi*, *lei*, from Latin *lēx* (“law”).
