diseducate:/ˌdɪsˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/
verb
中文翻译：误导；使失去教育；灌输错误思想
英文解析：To teach someone incorrect information or to give them a bad education, especially one that causes them to have wrong ideas about the world.  It can also imply the suppression of learning or critical thinking.
例句：Some argue that certain media outlets deliberately diseducate the public by spreading misinformation.  一些人认为某些媒体通过散布错误信息故意误导公众。

例句：The biased curriculum seemed designed to diseducate students about the true history of the conflict.  有偏见的课程似乎旨在让学生对冲突的真实历史产生错误的认识。
近义词：misinform, mislead, indoctrinate, corrupt
反义词：educate, enlighten, inform
词性：v.
