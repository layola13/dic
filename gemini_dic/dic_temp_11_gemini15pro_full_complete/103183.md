jesuit:/ˈdʒɛzjuɪt/ | n. |耶稣会士；耶稣会会员 |A member of the Society of Jesus, a Roman Catholic religious order founded by Ignatius of Loyola.|例句：He was educated by the Jesuits. 他是由耶稣会士教育的。

例句：The Jesuits are known for their scholarship. 耶稣会士以其学识而闻名。|近义词：Catholic, cleric |反义词：Protestant |词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *jésuite*, from Late Latin *Jesuita*, from *Jesus*. Ultimately from Hebrew *יֵשׁוּעַ* (*yēšūă*).
