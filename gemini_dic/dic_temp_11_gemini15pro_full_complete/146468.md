overfrustration: /ˌoʊvərfrʌˈstreɪʃən/
noun
过度沮丧，过分挫折
The state of being excessively frustrated.  Being frustrated beyond a normal or reasonable level.
He suffered from overfrustration after failing the exam multiple times.  他多次考试不及格后感到过度沮丧。
Overfrustration led him to quit his job. 过度的挫折感使他辞掉了工作。
近义词: extreme frustration, excessive frustration, intense frustration
反义词: contentment, satisfaction, fulfillment
词性: n.


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "frustration" (from Late Latin "frustrationem,"  meaning "a deceiving, disappointing, frustrating")
