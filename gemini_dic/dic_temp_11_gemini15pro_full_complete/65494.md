europeans:/jʊərəˈpiːənz/ | n. | 欧洲人 | People who originate from Europe. | Many Europeans travel within the continent. 许多欧洲人在欧洲大陆内旅行。| Europeans enjoy a variety of cultures and cuisines. 欧洲人享有各种文化和美食。|近义词：Continentals (more general), Westerners (if referring to Western Europe)|反义词: Non-Europeans | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Europe + -an (suffix denoting belonging to or originating from).  "Europe" itself ultimately derives from the Greek name "Eurṓpē," of uncertain origin, possibly from a Semitic word meaning "west."
