oleone:/ˈoʊliːoʊn/| n. |油酸甘油酯; 油精|A naturally occurring triglyceride of oleic acid, found in olive oil and other vegetable oils.|Olive oil is a rich source of oleone. 橄榄油富含油酸甘油酯。|Synonyms: triolein, glyceryl trioleate|Antonyms: None|noun
