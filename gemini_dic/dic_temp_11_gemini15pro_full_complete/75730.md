fullback:/ˈfʊlbæk/ | n. | 后卫；后卫球员 | A player positioned in the backfield of a football team, often responsible for blocking and carrying the ball. | The fullback made a crucial block that allowed the running back to score. 后卫做了一个关键的阻挡，让跑卫得分了。
The fullback powered through the defensive line for a short gain. 后卫强力突破防守线，取得了小幅推进。 | 近义词: defender, running back(in some contexts) | 反义词: forward, striker | 词性: 名词

fullback:/ˈfʊlbæk/ | n. | (橄榄球)后卫 | In (American) football, a player stationed behind the quarterback who is primarily a blocker and short-yardage runner.  | The fullback cleared a path for the halfback. 后卫为前卫开辟了道路。
The coach sent in the fullback for the goal-line stand. 教练派后卫上场进行达阵防守。 | 近义词:  blocking back | 反义词:  wide receiver | 词性: 名词
