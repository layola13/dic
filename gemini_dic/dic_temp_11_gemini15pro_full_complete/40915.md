cooler:/ˈkuːlər/ (US), /ˈkuːlə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|冷却器；冷藏箱；(非正式)监狱|A container filled with ice for cooling food and drinks; a device or substance for cooling something; (informal) a prison. |例句：We packed a cooler full of drinks for the picnic. 我们在野餐篮里装满了饮料的冷藏箱。

|近义词：refrigerator, chiller, icebox, jail (informal), prison (informal)|反义词: heater, warmer|词性:名词

cooler:/ˈkuːlər/ (US), /ˈkuːlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|较凉快的；较冷静的|Comparatively cool; calmer. |例句：It's much cooler in the shade. 在阴凉处凉快多了。

|近义词：chillier, colder, calmer, more relaxed|反义词: hotter, warmer, more agitated|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cool + -er (comparative suffix).  The noun sense developed from the adjective, referring to something that makes things cool.
