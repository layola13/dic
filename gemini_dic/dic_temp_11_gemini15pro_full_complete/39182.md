congener:/ˈkɒndʒɪnə(r)/ /ˈkɑːndʒənər/| adj. |同类的；同族的；同属的|Of the same kind or nature.  (This usage is less common than the noun form.)|例句：Congener elements share similar chemical properties. 同源元素具有相似的化学性质。

例句：The study compared the effects of the drug with those of its congener substances. 这项研究比较了这种药物与其同类物质的效果。|近义词：related, similar, analogous, homologous|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *congener* "of the same kind, of the same race," from *com-* "with, together" (see com-) + *genus* "birth, race, kind" (see genus).
