half-wild:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪld/ | adj. | 半野生的；半驯服的 | Partially wild; not completely domesticated.  | The farm had a few half-wild chickens that roamed freely.  农场里有一些半野生的鸡，可以自由活动。 |  The zookeepers were careful around the half-wild wolves.  动物园管理员对半野生的狼很小心。| Near synonyms: semi-wild, feral, untamed | Near antonyms: domesticated, tame, captive | Adjective


half-wild:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪld/ | n. | 半野生动物；半驯服动物 | An animal that is partially wild. | The half-wild mustangs were a magnificent sight. 半野生的野马是一幅壮丽的景象。 |  The rancher tried to capture the half-wild steer. 牧场主试图捕捉那头半野生的阉牛。| Near synonyms: semi-wild animal, feral animal | Near antonyms: domesticated animal, pet | Noun (rare usage)
