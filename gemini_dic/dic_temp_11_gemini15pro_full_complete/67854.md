fabaceous:/fəˈbeɪʃəs/| adj.|豆科的，豆类的|Relating to or belonging to the Fabaceae family (also known as Leguminosae), which includes plants such as beans, peas, and lentils.  Characterized by having pods as fruits and often having nitrogen-fixing bacteria in root nodules.|例句：The fabaceous plants are important for agriculture. 豆科植物对农业很重要。|例句：Many fabaceous species are used as cover crops. 许多豆科植物被用作覆盖作物。|近义词：leguminous|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-leguminous)|词性: adjective
