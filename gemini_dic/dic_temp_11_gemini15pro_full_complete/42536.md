countermark:/ˈkaʊntərmɑːrk/
noun
对戳，副戳，反标记，检验戳
A mark added to a coin or piece of pottery after its original production, usually indicating its reuse or a change in its value or status.
The coin had a countermark from a later ruler, suggesting it continued to circulate for many years. 这枚硬币上有后一位统治者的对戳，表明它继续流通了很多年。
synonyms: counterstamp, overstrike, control mark
antonyms: original mark
