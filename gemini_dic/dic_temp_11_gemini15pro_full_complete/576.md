abound:/əˈbaʊnd/| v. |大量存在，充满，富于|exist in large numbers or amounts|Fish abound in the lake.  湖里有很多鱼。|近义词：teem, swarm, overflow, proliferate|反义词:lack, be scarce|verb

abound:/əˈbaʊnd/| v. |充满，富于|be copiously supplied with something|The region abounds in/with natural resources. 这个地区自然资源丰富。|近义词：overflow, be filled|反义词:lack, be devoid of|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away from" (see ab-) + undare "to surge, overflow," from unda "wave."
