destabilising:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使不稳定的；破坏稳定的
tending to make a government, area, or political or economic system less strong or safe, by causing changes and problems
The constant threat of terrorism is destabilising the region.  持续不断的恐怖主义威胁正在破坏该地区的稳定。
The financial crisis had a destabilising effect on the global economy. 金融危机对全球经济产生了不稳定的影响。
destabilizing (American English)
近义词: disruptive, unsettling, undermining
反义词: stabilizing, strengthening, reinforcing
词性: adjective
