bolar:/ˈboʊlər/ | adj. | 土质的，粘土状的 | Of or relating to clay or bole. | The bolar soil was difficult to work with. 土质土壤很难耕作。| 近义词: clayey, argillaceous | 反义词: sandy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From bole (a type of clay) + -ar (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *bolus*, from Greek *βῶλος* (bōlos) meaning 'clod of earth'.
