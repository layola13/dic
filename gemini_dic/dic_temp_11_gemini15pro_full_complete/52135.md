diape:/ˈdaɪeɪp/| n. |幻灯片|A transparent photograph or picture, especially one mounted in a frame for projection on a screen.|例句：The diape showed a picture of a family vacation. 这张幻灯片展示了一张家庭度假照片。

例句：He carefully arranged the diapes for his presentation. 他仔细地排列幻灯片，为他的演讲做准备。|近义词：slide, transparency|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "print" or "physical photograph" in some contexts)|词性:noun
