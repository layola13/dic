misspeak:/ˌmɪsˈspiːk/
verb
说错话，讲错
to say something incorrectly, especially in a way that is embarrassing or offensive
I misspoke earlier when I said he was 25 – he's actually 26. 我之前说他 25 岁说错了——他实际上 26 岁。
近义词: blunder, err, slip up
反义词: articulate correctly
