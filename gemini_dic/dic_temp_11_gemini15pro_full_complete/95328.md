incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ /ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri/ | adj. n. |煽动性的；放火的；燃烧弹；纵火犯| (adj.) Tending to excite or inflame; pertaining to or involving arson. (n.) A person who deliberately sets fire to property; a substance or weapon used to start fires. |例句：His incendiary remarks ignited a riot. 他的煽动性言论引发了一场骚乱。|近义词：inflammatory, provocative, combustible, arsonist|反义词：conciliatory, calming, peaceful, extinguisher |词性: 形容词, 名词
