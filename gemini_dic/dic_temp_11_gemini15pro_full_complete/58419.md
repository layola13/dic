ducally:/ˈduːkəli/
adjective
公爵地；像公爵一样地
in a manner befitting a duke; like a duke.
He behaved ducally, dispensing favors and pronouncements with regal grace. 他举止像公爵一样，以王者风范施恩于民并发表声明。
近义词：aristocratically, nobly, regally
反义词：basely, humbly, meanly
词性：adjective

词源：
From duke + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin dux (leader).
