metate:/mɛˈtɑːteɪ/
noun
石磨，磨盘 (especially in Mexico and Central America)
A flat or slightly hollowed stone, usually rectangular, on which corn or other grains are ground using a mano (a cylindrical hand-held grinding stone).
The woman knelt by the metate, grinding corn for tortillas.  那个女人跪在石磨旁，磨玉米做玉米饼。

She used a mano and metate to grind the dried chilies into powder. 她用磨棒和石磨把干辣椒磨成粉末。

grinding stone, millstone
