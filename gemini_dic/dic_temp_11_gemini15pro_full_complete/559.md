aborting:/əˈbɔːrtɪŋ/| adj.|流产的，中止的|The aborting process took several hours.  中止过程花了几个小时。|近义词：failed, unsuccessful, terminated|反义词:successful, completed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abortus*, past participle of *aborīrī* ("to miscarry, fail to be born"), from *ab-* ("from, away") + *orīrī* ("to arise, be born").
