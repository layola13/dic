ETYMOLOGY->
From the Spanish *Alburquerque*, ultimately from the Latin *alba quercus* meaning "white oak".  The Spanish town of Alburquerque was named for the abundance of white oak trees in the area.  The spelling of the New Mexican city was altered at some point, dropping the first "r".
