claimer:/ˈkleɪmər/| n.| 要求者；认领者；索赔者|A person who makes a claim, especially in a legal context or for something lost or stolen.|The insurance company had to deal with numerous claimers after the flood.  洪水过后，保险公司不得不处理大量的索赔者。|例句：The claimer of the lost dog was overjoyed to be reunited with his pet. 丢失小狗的认领者非常高兴能与他的宠物团聚。


例句：Several claimers came forward, each insisting the valuable painting was theirs.  几位认领者站了出来，每个人都坚持说这幅珍贵的画是他们的。|近义词：applicant, claimant, petitioner|反义词:renouncer, abdicator|词性:noun
