beamforming:/ˈbiːmˌfɔːrmɪŋ/ | n. | 波束成形 | A signal processing technique used in antenna arrays to control the directionality of the transmitted or received signal. | The radar system uses beamforming to focus the signal on a specific target. 雷达系统使用波束成形将信号聚焦在特定目标上。| synonyms: spatial filtering, phased array processing | antonyms: omnidirectional radiation | noun
