aquaculturing:/ˈækwəkʌltʃərɪŋ/
n.
水产养殖
The practice of farming aquatic organisms, such as fish, shellfish, and seaweed.
Aquaculturing is becoming increasingly important as a source of protein.  水产养殖作为蛋白质的来源正变得越来越重要。
Fish farming is a common type of aquaculturing.  鱼类养殖是一种常见的水产养殖。
fish farming, aquaculture, mariculture
hunting, gathering
noun
