banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/
noun
女妖，报丧女妖
A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of an impending death in a family.
The old woman swore she heard a banshee wail the night before her husband died. 老妇人发誓说，在她丈夫去世的前一天晚上，她听到了女妖的哀号。
synonyms: spirit, wraith, phantom, ghost, specter
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a supernatural being.  One could consider "living being" or "mortal" as a loose contrast.)
