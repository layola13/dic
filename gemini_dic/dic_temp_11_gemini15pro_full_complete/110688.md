lineal:/ˈlɪniəl/| adj.|直系的；线性的；长度的|Relating to a line; descended from an ancestor in a direct line; of or relating to length or linear measurement.|例句：He is a lineal descendant of King George. 他是乔治国王的直系后裔。|近义词：direct, linear, hereditary|反义词：collateral, indirect|词性:形容词
lineal:/ˈlɪniəl/| n.|长度单位|A unit of length, especially one equal to one twelfth of an inch.|例句：The width of the fabric is three lineals. 这块布料的宽度是三里尼尔。|近义词：inch, unit|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
