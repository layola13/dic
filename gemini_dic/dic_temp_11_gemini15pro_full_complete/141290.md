open-airnesses:/ˌoʊpənˈɛərnɪsɪz/
|n.|露天场所；户外环境|The quality or state of being open to the air; exposure to the open air.  The vast open-airnesses of the Mongolian steppe.|
例句：
The festival embraced the open-airnesses of the park, with stages set amongst the trees.
这个节日利用了公园的露天环境，在树林间设置了舞台。

She loved the open-airnesses of the countryside, a stark contrast to her city life.
她喜欢乡村的户外环境，这与她的城市生活形成了鲜明的对比。
|近义词：outdoors, open air|反义词：indoors, enclosure|词性:名词
