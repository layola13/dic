monkery:/ˈmʌŋkəri/
noun
修道生活；僧侣的习俗；僧院
The practice or profession of being a monk; monastic life, especially its less strict or more worldly aspects; a monastery.
He abandoned the world and embraced monkery. 他放弃了世俗生活，皈依佛门。
synonyms: monasticism, religious life, asceticism, cloister
antonyms: worldliness, secularism
n.

monkery:/ˈmʌŋkəri/
noun
虚伪的行为；假装虔诚
Behavior that affects excessive piety or religious devotion; hypocritical displays of piety.
His monkery was just a show to impress others. 他的虔诚只不过是做给人看的。
synonyms: hypocrisy, sanctimony, pretense, affectation
antonyms: sincerity, genuineness, honesty
n.
