debaucher:/dɪˈbɔːtʃər/
noun
放荡者；腐蚀者；堕落者
Someone who leads another person into a life of debauchery; a corrupter.
He was known as a debaucher of young women. 他被称为年轻女性的腐蚀者。
Synonyms:corrupter, seducer, libertine
Antonyms:moralist, reformer, ascetic
词性: 名词

debauch:/dɪˈbɔːtʃ/
verb
使放荡；使堕落；腐蚀
To corrupt or lead astray morally; to seduce.
The emperor was debauched by power. 权力使皇帝堕落。
Synonyms:corrupt, pervert, deprave
Antonyms:purify, elevate, uplift
词性: 动词

debauched:/dɪˈbɔːtʃt/
adjective
放荡的；堕落的；腐化的
Morally corrupt; given to excessive indulgence in pleasure.
He led a debauched lifestyle. 他过着放荡的生活。
Synonyms:dissolute, dissipated, profligate
Antonyms:moral, upright, virtuous
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French desbaucher "to seduce, lead astray," from des- "away" + bauche "workshop, shed." The original meaning was "to lead someone away from their work," implying idleness and dissipation.
