coldfinch:/ˈkoʊldfɪntʃ/| n. |冷雀（指冷漠或无情的人）| A coldfinch is a person who is emotionally detached, uncaring, or unresponsive. This word is often used figuratively to describe someone who lacks empathy or warmth.| He's such a coldfinch; he didn't even offer condolences when he heard about my loss. 他真是个冷血动物，听到我的损失后甚至没有表示慰问。|近义词：unfeeling person, insensitive person, heartless person|反义词: warmhearted person, compassionate person|词性:名词
