asthma: /ˈæzmə/ (US), /ˈæsmə/ (UK)| n. |哮喘|A chronic respiratory condition marked by spasms in the bronchi of the lungs, causing difficulty in breathing.|例句：She suffers from asthma. 她患有哮喘。|例句：His asthma was triggered by dust. 他的哮喘是由灰尘引起的。|近义词：respiratory disease, bronchospasm|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἄσθμα (ásthma), from *ἀσθμαίνειν (ásthmaínein) "to breathe hard," from *ἆσθμα (âthma) "panting, gasping." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂enh₁- "to breathe."
