myography:/maɪˈɒɡrəfi/
noun
肌动描记术，肌电描记法
The recording of the mechanical activity of muscles.  The process of recording muscle contractions.
Electromyography is a type of myography used to diagnose neuromuscular disorders. 肌电图是一种用于诊断神经肌肉疾病的肌动描记法。
Intramuscular myography can measure muscle activity deep within the tissue. 肌内肌动描记术可以测量组织深处的肌肉活动。
synonym: electromyography
antonym: (No direct antonym exists, but consider terms like "muscle inactivity" or "paralysis" as conceptually opposite)
词性:n.
