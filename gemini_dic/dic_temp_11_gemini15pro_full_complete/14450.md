avail oneself of:/əˈveɪl wʌnˈsɛlf əv/
|v.|利用， воспользоваться|To make use of something; to take advantage of an opportunity.|She availed herself of the opportunity to study abroad. 她利用这个机会去国外学习。|近义词：utilize, make use of, take advantage of|反义词：forgo, neglect, ignore|词性:动词短语

词源: From the Old French *avaler (to be of use, to be worth) from Latin ad valere (to have value).
