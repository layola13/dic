overassists:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɪsts/
verb
过度辅助；过分帮助
To provide excessive assistance, often to the point of hindering development or independence.
The coach overassisted the young players, preventing them from learning to strategize on their own. 教练过度辅助年轻球员，阻止他们学习自己制定策略。
The helicopter parent overassisted her child with homework, to the point that the child couldn't complete assignments independently.  直升机父母过度帮助孩子做作业，以至于孩子无法独立完成作业。
近义词: overhelp, coddle, pamper
反义词: neglect, underassist
词性: verb
