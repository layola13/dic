branch-out:/bræntʃ aʊt/| phrasal verb |拓展，扩大，分支|To expand one's interests, activities, or business into new areas.|Example: The company decided to branch out into the software market.  这家公司决定拓展软件市场。|近义词：expand, diversify, broaden, develop|反义词: contract, narrow, specialize|词性:动词短语

branch-out:/bræntʃ aʊt/| n. | 分支机构；拓展业务|An expansion into a new area of activity, especially in business.|Example: The restaurant chain's new branch-out in the city center is proving successful. 这家连锁餐厅在市中心的新分支机构非常成功。|近义词：expansion, diversification, venture|反义词: contraction, specialization|词性:名词

词源:  From the literal sense of a tree branch growing outwards.  来自于树枝向外生长的字面意思。
