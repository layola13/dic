frame:/freɪm/ | n. vt.|框架，边框；体格；画面；组织，制定|A rigid structure that surrounds or encloses something.|The painting's frame was ornately carved. 这幅画的画框雕刻得很华丽。|synonyms: structure, framework, chassis, skeleton, casing |antonyms: content, filling, interior| noun
frame:/freɪm/ | vt.|给…装框；陷害；表达；制定|To enclose in or as if in a frame; to formulate or construct.|He was framed for a crime he didn't commit. 他被诬陷犯了他没有犯的罪。|synonyms: formulate, construct, devise, compose, design |antonyms: dismantle, destroy, ruin| verb
