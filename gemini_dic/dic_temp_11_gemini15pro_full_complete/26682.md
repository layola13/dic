cadaverous:/kəˈdævərəs/| adj.|形容词|憔悴的；苍白的；像尸体的|Resembling a corpse in being very pale, thin, or bony.  Appearance suggesting that of a corpse; gaunt and pallid.|例句：He had a cadaverous complexion. 他面色苍白如尸体。

例句：The illness had left him looking cadaverous.  疾病使他看起来面色苍白。|近义词：gaunt, haggard, pallid, ashen, ghastly, skeletal, emaciated|反义词：healthy, robust, ruddy, flourishing|词性:adjective
