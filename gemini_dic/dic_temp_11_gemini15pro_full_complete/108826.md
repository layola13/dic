leavening: /ˈlɛvənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɛvənɪŋ/ (UK)
n. |发酵；发酵剂；使发酵|The process of making something rise, especially bread, by adding a substance such as yeast or baking powder. Also, the substance itself that causes this rising.|The baker added the leavening to the dough. 面包师把发酵剂加到面团里。|Synonyms: yeast, baking powder, raising agent|Antonyms: deflation, flattening|noun
