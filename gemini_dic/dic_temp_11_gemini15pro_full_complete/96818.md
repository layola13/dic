inescapability:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可避免性；无法逃脱
The quality of being impossible to avoid or escape.
The inescapability of death is a difficult concept for many to grasp.  死亡的不可避免性对许多人来说是一个难以理解的概念。
The inescapability of her past haunted her. 她过去的阴影挥之不去，让她难以释怀。

近义词: inevitability, unavoidability, certainty
反义词: avoidability, escapability, uncertainty
词性: noun
