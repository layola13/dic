n.|日光反射信号器|A device that uses mirrors to reflect sunlight for sending signals over long distances.|例句：The heliograph proved essential for communication during the expedition. 日光反射信号器在探险过程中被证明是通讯的必要工具。|例句：The team used a heliograph to signal for help when they became stranded.  当团队被困时，他们使用日光反射信号器发出求救信号。|近义词：semaphore, signal lamp|反义词:|词性:noun
