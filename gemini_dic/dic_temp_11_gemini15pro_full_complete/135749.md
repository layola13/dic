nonproducing:/ˌnɒnprəˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | /ˌnɑːnprəˈduːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 非生产性的；不生产的 | Not producing anything; unproductive. |  The nonproducing oil wells were capped. 这些不产油的油井被封住了。| The company sold off its nonproducing assets.  这家公司出售了其非生产性资产。| 近义词：unproductive, barren, idle, inert | 反义词：productive, fertile, active | 词性: adjective

词源: non- + producing. "non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "producing" is the present participle of the verb "produce," which comes from the Latin "producere," meaning "to lead forth, bring forth, produce."
