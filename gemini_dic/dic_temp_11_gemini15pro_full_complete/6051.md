amends:/əˈmendz/
|n.|赔偿；补偿；赔罪|compensation or reparation for a wrong or injury|She tried to make amends for her mistake by apologizing. 她试图通过道歉来弥补自己的错误。|近义词:reparation, restitution, compensation|反义词:damage, injury|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amendes* (plural), "penalty, fine, compensation," from *amendre*, "to correct, improve, amend," from Latin *emendare*, "to correct, free from faults."  The singular form *amend* existed briefly in Middle English but died out, leaving only the plural form.
