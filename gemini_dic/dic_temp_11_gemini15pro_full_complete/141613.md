optative:/ˈɒptətɪv/ /ˈɑːptətɪv/ | adj. n. |  选择性的；愿望的；虚拟语气的；祈愿法 | expressing a wish or desire. |  例句：He used the optative mood to express his hope for peace. 他用虚拟语气表达了他对和平的渴望。| 近义词：desiderative, wishful | 反义词：indicative, imperative | 词性: adjective

optative:/ˈɒptətɪv/ /ˈɑːptətɪv/ | n. |  祈愿法；表示愿望的词 | a grammatical mood that expresses a wish or desire. |  例句：The optative mood is not commonly used in English. 祈愿法在英语中不常用。| 近义词：subjunctive | 反义词：indicative | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *optātīvus, from Latin optātus (past participle of optāre “to wish, choose, desire”), + -īvus.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ep- (“to work, take pains”).
