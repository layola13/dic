numismatology:/ˌn(j)uːmɪzˈmætɪkəlɒdʒi/ /ˌnuːmɪzˈmætɪkəlɑːdʒi/
noun
钱币学
The study or collection of coins, paper money, and medals.
He is a distinguished scholar of numismatology. 他是一位杰出的钱币学学者。

例句：
My grandfather's hobby is numismatology; he has a vast collection of old coins. 我的祖父的爱好是钱币学；他收藏了大量古钱币。

She took a course in numismatology to learn more about ancient currencies. 她学习了钱币学课程，以了解更多古代货币的知识。


近义词:
coin collecting,  currency study

反义词:
None (There isn't a direct antonym for a field of study)

词性:
n.
