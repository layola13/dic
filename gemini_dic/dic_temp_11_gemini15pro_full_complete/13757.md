audaciousness:/ɔːˈdeɪʃəsnəs/| n.|大胆，无畏，鲁莽|The audaciousness of the plan surprised everyone.  这个计划的大胆让每个人都感到惊讶。|例句：Her audaciousness in challenging the status quo was admirable. 她挑战现状的大胆令人钦佩。

例句：The sheer audaciousness of the heist made headlines around the world.  这起盗窃案的惊人胆量登上了世界各地的头条。|近义词：boldness, daring, temerity, nerve, chutzpah|反义词:timidity, cowardice, fearfulness|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin audacitas, from audax "bold,"  ultimately from audere "to dare."
