nonrepentantly:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpɛntəntli/ | adv. |  不后悔地，毫无悔意地 | without repentance; showing no regret or remorse for one's actions. | He nonrepentantly continued his disruptive behavior in class. 他毫不悔意地继续在课堂上进行破坏性行为。|  He nonrepentantly admitted to his mistakes, refusing to apologize.  他毫不后悔地承认了自己的错误，拒绝道歉。 | 近义词: unapologetically, impenitently, remorselessly | 反义词: repentantly, apologetically, remorsefully | 词性: 副词

词源:
From non- + repentantly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "repentant" comes from the Old French "repentant" and ultimately from the Latin "repaenitere" meaning "to feel remorse."
