overcapacities:/ˌoʊvər kəˈpæsɪtiz/
n.|过剩产能，超额容量|A situation where the production capacity exceeds the demand, leading to idle resources and potential financial losses.  The factory suffered from overcapacities after the economic downturn.|例句：The steel industry is currently struggling with significant overcapacities.  钢铁行业目前正与严重的产能过剩作斗争。

例句：Overcapacities in the automotive sector have led to price wars. 汽车行业的产能过剩导致了价格战。|近义词：excess capacity, surplus capacity, glut|反义词:undercapacity, shortage|词性:名词
