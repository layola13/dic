loveed:  (This is not a standard English word.  It appears to be a misspelling of "loved," the past tense of "love.")

/lʌvd/ | v. | 爱过，喜爱过 | Past tense of "love," meaning to have felt a deep affection or fondness for someone or something. |  例句：I loved the movie. 我很喜欢这部电影。


例句：She loved him dearly. 她深深地爱着他。


近义词：adored, cherished, liked, enjoyed | 反义词：hated, disliked, despised | 词性：动词
