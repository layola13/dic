bursitis:/bərˈsaɪtɪs/ /bɝːˈsaɪtɪs/
noun
滑囊炎
Inflammation of a bursa, a small fluid-filled sac that cushions the joints.
Example: He was diagnosed with bursitis in his shoulder. 他被诊断出患有肩部滑囊炎。

Example:  The repetitive motion caused bursitis in her elbow.  重复的动作导致她肘部滑囊炎。


近义词:inflammation, tendonitis
反义词:(None -  there's no direct opposite of a specific medical condition)
词性:n
