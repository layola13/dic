ochrea:/ˈɒkriə/ | n. |托叶鞘| A sheath formed from two stipules that surrounds the stem at the node in plants of the Polygonaceae family, such as rhubarb and knotweed. | The ochrea of the knotweed is quite prominent.  蓼科植物的托叶鞘很明显。| synonym: sheath, stipular sheath | antonym: (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific botanical term) | noun
