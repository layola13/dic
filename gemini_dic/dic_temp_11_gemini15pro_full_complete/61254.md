emancipating: /ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪŋ/
Verb (present participle)
解放的，使获得自由的
Freeing someone from slavery or other constraint.
例句：The Emancipation Proclamation was a document emancipating enslaved African Americans in the Confederate states.  解放黑奴宣言是一份解放南方邦联奴隶制非裔美国人的文件。

例句：The new laws are emancipating women from oppressive traditions. 新法律将妇女从压迫性的传统中解放出来。

近义词：liberating, freeing, releasing, unchaining, unshackling
反义词： enslaving, oppressing, restricting, confining
词性：v.
