lower chamber:/ˈloʊər ˈtʃeɪmbər/| n.|下议院，下院|The lower chamber of the parliament has the power to initiate financial bills. 议会的下议院有权提出财政法案。|The lower chamber approved the bill. 下议院批准了该法案。|Synonyms: House of Representatives, House of Commons, Assembly|Antonyms: upper chamber, Senate|noun

Etymology:
"lower" comes from Old English "lāwer" (comparative of "lāg," meaning "low").  "chamber" comes from Old French "chambre," ultimately from Latin "camera" (meaning "vaulted room").
