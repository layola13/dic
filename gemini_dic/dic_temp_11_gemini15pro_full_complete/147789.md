overprosperousness: /ˌoʊvərprɑːˈspɛrəsnəs/
n.
过度繁荣，过分兴旺
A state of excessive prosperity, often implying instability or unsustainability.
The overprosperousness of the Roaring Twenties ultimately led to the Great Depression.  二十年代的过度繁荣最终导致了大萧条。
The economist warned of the dangers of overprosperousness and the potential for a bubble to burst. 经济学家警告过度繁荣的危险以及泡沫破裂的可能性。

近义词: affluence, opulence, exuberance
反义词: poverty, scarcity, depression
