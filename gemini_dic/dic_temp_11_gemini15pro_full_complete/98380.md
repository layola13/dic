insociable: /ɪnˈsoʊʃəbl/
adjective
不善交际的；孤僻的
Not sociable; not enjoying the company of others.

例句：
He was an insociable man who preferred to live alone.
他是一个不善交际的人，喜欢独居。

She became increasingly insociable after the death of her husband.
丈夫去世后，她变得越来越孤僻。


近义词：
unsociable, antisocial, withdrawn, reclusive, aloof

反义词：
sociable, gregarious, outgoing, extroverted

词性
adjective


词源：
Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin insociabilis, from in- ‘not’ + sociabilis (see sociable).
