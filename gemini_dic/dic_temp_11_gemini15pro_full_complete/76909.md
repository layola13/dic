ganoidian:/ˈɡænɔɪdiən/ | adj. n.| 硬鳞鱼类的；硬鳞鱼|Relating to or characteristic of ganoid fishes, having thick, bony, rhombic scales.|Example: The ganoidian scales of the gar provided excellent protection.  雀鳝的硬鳞提供了极好的保护。|Example: The museum displayed a collection of ganoidian fossils.  博物馆展出了一批硬鳞鱼化石。|近义词: bony-scaled, |反义词: cycloid, ctenoid (referring to other scale types)|词性:形容词, 名词
