haplomi:/hæpˈloʊmaɪ/| n. |单倍体|A cell or organism having a single set of chromosomes.|The haplomi of this species has 12 chromosomes.|近义词:monoploid|反义词:diploid|词性:名词
Etymology:  From Greek  *haplóos* "single" + *-ōm(a)*  "mass, group"  + *-i*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sem- "one; together"*
