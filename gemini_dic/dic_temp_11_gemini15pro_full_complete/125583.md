multitube:/ˈmʌltiˌtjuːb/ /ˈmʌltiˌtuːb/| adj. n.|多管的；多管式|Having or consisting of multiple tubes.  A device with multiple tubes.|The chemist used a multitube apparatus for the experiment. 化学家用一个多管装置进行实验。|The multitube system allows for simultaneous testing of multiple samples. 多管系统允许多个样品同时进行测试。|近义词：multi-tubular, polytubular|反义词: single-tube|adjective, noun
