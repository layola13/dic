enroller:/ɪnˈroʊlər/ | n. | 登记员；注册人；招收者 |  A person who enrolls someone or something, especially a student in a school or course. | The enroller helped the new students with the registration process.  登记员帮助新生办理注册手续。 | synonyms: registrar, recorder, admitter | antonyms: discharger, expeller | noun

enrol:/ɪnˈroʊl/ | v. | 登记；注册；招收 | To officially register as a member of an institution or a student on a course. |  She enrolled in the online photography course. 她报名参加了在线摄影课程。| synonyms: register, sign up, join | antonyms: withdraw, unregister, drop out | verb
