jabbingly:/ˈdʒæbɪŋli/
adverb
刺戳地，戳刺般地
In a manner resembling a jab; sharply and repeatedly.
He spoke jabbingly, punctuating each word with a pointed finger. 他说话时咄咄逼人，用手指着重强调每个字。
The needle jabbingly pierced the fabric. 针尖猛地刺穿了布料。
sharply, pointedly, piercingly
softly, gently
词性: 副词
