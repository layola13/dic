dubnium: /ˈdʌbniəm/ | n. | 钅杜（一种人工合成的放射性化学元素）|  A synthetic radioactive chemical element with the symbol Db and atomic number 105.  It is extremely radioactive and has no stable isotopes. | Dubnium is a superheavy element created in a laboratory. 钅杜是一种在实验室里创造的超重元素。

Dubnium was named after the Russian city of Dubna. 钅杜是以俄罗斯城市杜布纳命名的。 | 近义词: (No close synonyms as it's a specific element) | 反义词: (No antonyms for chemical elements) | 词性: noun
