desertrice: /ˈdɛzərt raɪs/ | n. | 沙漠稻 | A type of rice that is grown in arid or semi-arid regions, often requiring less water than traditional rice varieties. | Example: Desert rice is a staple food in some parts of Africa.  沙漠稻是非洲一些地区的主食。

Example:  Researchers are developing new strains of desert rice to improve yields. 研究人员正在开发新的沙漠稻品种以提高产量。 | Synonym: drought-tolerant rice, arid-land rice | Antonym: paddy rice, wetland rice | noun
