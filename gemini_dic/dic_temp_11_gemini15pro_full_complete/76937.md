gapeworm:/ˈɡeɪpwɜːrm/| n. | 裂口线虫 | A parasitic nematode worm, specifically *Syngamus trachea*, that infects the respiratory tracts, particularly the trachea, of birds, especially young poultry, and some mammals.  It causes the host to gasp for air, hence the name. | Several chicks died due to a gapeworm infestation. 几只小鸡死于裂口线虫感染。 | Synonyms: red worm, forked worm | Antonyms: N/A | noun
