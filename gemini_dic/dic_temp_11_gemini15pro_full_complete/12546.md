aspidobranchia:/æˌspɪdəˈbræŋkiə/| n. | 盾鳃类 |A subclass of mollusks (gastropods) characterized by having a single or paired gills, often protected by a shell or mantle. This subclass is now considered obsolete and its members have been reclassified into other groups.|Aspidobranchia were once considered a distinct group, but modern taxonomy has superseded this classification.|近义词:Archaeogastropoda (partially), Vetigastropoda (partially) |反义词: (None, as it's an obsolete classification, no direct opposite exists)
词性:noun
