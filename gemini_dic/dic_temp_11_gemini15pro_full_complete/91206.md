hydrina:/haɪˈdraɪnə/ | n. |海螅|A small, solitary hydroid polyp, typically cylindrical and with a ring of tentacles around the mouth.|The hydrina attached itself to the rock. 海螅附着在岩石上。|近义词: polyp, hydroid|反义词: N/A|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ὕδρα (hýdra, “water serpent, Hydra”) +‎ *-ινος (-inos, adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wódr̥ (“water”).
