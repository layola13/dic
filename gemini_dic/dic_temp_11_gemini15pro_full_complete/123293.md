monogenetic:/ˌmɒnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌmɑːnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|单成因的，单基因的|Formed from a single source; having a single origin, especially of rocks formed from a single period of igneous activity or by a single eruption.  Also, caused by a single gene.|例句：The volcano is monogenetic, formed by a single eruption. 这座火山是单成因的，由一次喷发形成。|例句：Cystic fibrosis is a monogenetic disease, meaning it is caused by mutations in a single gene. 囊性纤维化是一种单基因疾病，这意味着它是由单个基因突变引起的。|近义词：unigenic|反义词: polygenetic|词性: adjective
