asymmetral:/ˌeɪsɪˈmetrɪkl/
adj.
不对称的，非对称的
Not symmetrical; lacking symmetry.
The asymmetral design of the building made it stand out.  这栋建筑不对称的设计使它脱颖而出。
The artist intentionally created an asymmetral sculpture.  这位艺术家特意创作了一个不对称的雕塑。
近义词: asymmetrical, dissymmetric, unbalanced
反义词: symmetrical, balanced, even
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *a-* "not" + "symmetrical."
