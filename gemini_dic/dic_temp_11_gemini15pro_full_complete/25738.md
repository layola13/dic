buoyantly:/ˈbɔɪəntli/
adverb
轻快地，乐观地
In a cheerful, optimistic, and resilient manner; in a way that suggests lightness and ease.
The team buoyantly celebrated their victory.  该队兴高采烈地庆祝他们的胜利。
She walked buoyantly into the room, full of good news. 她兴高采烈地走进房间，带来了好消息。
近义词:cheerfully, optimistically, blithely, lightheartedly
反义词:dejectedly, gloomily, despondently
词性:adverb

buoyant:/ˈbɔɪənt/
adjective
有浮力的，轻快的，乐观的
1. Able to float or keep things afloat. 2. Cheerful and optimistic. 3. (of an economy, market, etc.) strong and successful.
The boat remained buoyant even with the heavy load.  即使负载很重，船仍然保持漂浮。
He felt buoyant and hopeful about the future. 他对未来感到乐观和充满希望。
The buoyant economy created many new jobs.  蓬勃发展的经济创造了许多新的就业机会。
近义词:floating, cheerful, optimistic, resilient, thriving
反义词:sinking, gloomy, pessimistic, depressed, stagnant
词性:adjective

词源:From Middle French *boyant* "floating," present participle of *boyer* "to float," from Frankish or some other Germanic source akin to Old English *būian* "to dwell, live, inhabit," originally "to be, exist"
