ballyhooed:/ˌbæliˈhuːd/
adjective
大肆宣传的，大吹大擂的
Excessively or overly promoted; publicized with boisterous fanfare.
The ballyhooed new product turned out to be a disappointment.  这款大肆宣传的新产品结果令人失望。
The much ballyhooed movie premiere was a flop. 这部被大肆宣传的电影首映式失败了。
synonyms: hyped, publicized, touted, trumpeted
antonyms: understated, downplayed, ignored, obscure
词性:形容词
