bestrides:/bɪˈstraɪdz/
verb
跨越，横跨；支配，控制
To stand or sit with a leg on either side of something; to dominate or control.
The Colossus of Rhodes bestrode the harbor entrance.  罗德岛巨像跨立于港口入口。
His influence bestrides the entire music industry. 他的影响力遍及整个音乐产业。
Synonyms: straddles, spans, dominates, controls
Antonyms: submits, yields
词性: 动词
