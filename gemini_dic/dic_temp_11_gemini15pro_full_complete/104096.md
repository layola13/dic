jump-on-board:/ˌdʒʌmp ɒn ˈbɔːrd/ | phrasal verb | 加入；参与；赶快行动 | To join an activity, project, or cause, often enthusiastically or with a sense of urgency. | Example: We need more people to jump on board and help with the fundraising.  我们需要更多的人加入进来，帮助我们筹款。

Example: The company is encouraging its employees to jump on board with the new initiative. 公司鼓励员工积极参与这项新计划。 | Synonyms: join in, participate, get involved, sign up | Antonyms: opt out, withdraw, disengage | phrasal verb
