bergmaster:/ˈbɜːrɡˌmɑːstər/ | n. |矿山主管，矿长| A person in charge of a mine, especially in South Africa. | The bergmaster ensured the safety regulations were followed meticulously. 矿长确保安全规章得到严格遵守。| The bergmaster called for an inspection of the new tunnel. 矿长要求检查新的隧道。 | 近义词：mine manager, superintendent | 反义词: miner, laborer | 词性: noun

词源: From Afrikaans *bergmeester*, from *berg* ("mountain") + *meester* ("master").  Ultimately from Dutch.
