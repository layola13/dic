drib:/drɪb/| v. n.|运球，滴下|To fall or flow in small drops; to move a ball by bouncing it or giving it short kicks or pushes.|He dribbled the basketball down the court. 他运球过半场。|近义词：drip, trickle|反义词：gush, pour|verb
drib:/drɪb/| n.|运球，点滴|A small quantity of liquid; an act of dribbling a ball.|The rain came down in a light drib. 雨点稀稀落落地下着。|近义词：drop, trickle|反义词：flood, torrent|noun
