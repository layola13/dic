mesocaecum:/ˌmɛzoʊˈsiːkəm/| n. |中盲肠|The mesocaecum is a fold of peritoneum that attaches the cecum to the posterior abdominal wall.  It is part of the mesocolon.|The surgeon carefully examined the mesocaecum during the appendectomy. 外科医生在阑尾切除术中仔细检查了中盲肠。|Synonym: mesoappendix (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically refers to the mesentery of the appendix specifically)|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology: meso- (Greek: middle) + caecum (Latin: blind gut)
