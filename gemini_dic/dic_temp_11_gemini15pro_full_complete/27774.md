cannibalising:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/
v.|蚕食，内部竞争|Reducing sales or profits of one product by introducing a similar new product from the same company. |The new product line is cannibalising sales of the older models.  新产品线正在蚕食旧型号的销售。|近义词：undermining, eroding|反义词: boosting, supporting|词性:动词现在分词

cannibalise:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/
v.|蚕食，内部竞争|To reduce the sales or profits of one product by introducing a similar new product from the same company. |The company is worried that its new phone will cannibalise sales of its existing models. 公司担心它的新手机会蚕食现有型号的销售。|近义词：undermine, erode|反义词: boost, support|词性:动词

cannibalisation:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|蚕食，内部竞争|The reduction of sales or profits of one product by introducing a similar new product from the same company. |The launch of the new model led to significant cannibalisation of the older one. 新型号的推出导致旧型号的销售大幅下滑。|近义词：undermining, erosion|反义词: boosting, supporting|词性:名词

词源: From cannibal + -ize/-ise.  源于cannibal（食人者）+ -ize/-ise（动词后缀），最初指同类相食，后引申为内部竞争。
