dragooner:/drəˈɡuːnər/| n.|龙骑兵，迫害者|A soldier originally trained to fight on horseback but later also on foot.  A cavalryman; one who harasses or coerces.|例句：The dragooner charged across the battlefield. 龙骑兵冲过战场。

例句：The landlord was a real dragooner, constantly harassing his tenants for rent.  房东是个十足的迫害者，不断地骚扰房客交房租。|近义词：cavalryman, trooper, persecutor, oppressor|反义词:protector, defender|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *dragon*, ultimately from Latin *draco* "dragon," so called from the short musket (dragon) they originally carried.
