bespeak:/bɪˈspiːk/| vt.|预示，表明；预订，订购；显示；证明|The dark clouds bespeak a coming storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。|Synonyms: indicate, signify, suggest, portend, order, reserve |Antonyms: conceal, hide, belie|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English besprecan "to speak, speak to, address; speak about, discuss; accuse, betray; charm, conjure," from be- + sprecan "to speak."  The meaning "to order beforehand" emerged in the 16th century.
