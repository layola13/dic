nonorganizations:/ˌnɑːnˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|非组织; 非正式组织；非正式群体|Entities that are not formally structured or recognized as organizations. These can include informal groups, social movements, or loosely connected networks of individuals.|Example:  Nonorganizations, such as online communities, can exert significant influence.  线上社区等非组织可以发挥重大影响力。
Example:  The study focused on the role of nonorganizations in disaster relief. 这项研究的重点是非组织在灾难救济中的作用。|Synonym: informal groups,  unstructured groups,  loose collectives|Antonym: organizations, institutions, formal groups|noun
