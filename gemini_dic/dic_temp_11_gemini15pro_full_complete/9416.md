antiradicalism:/ˌæntiˈrædɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
反激进化；反极端主义
Opposition to radical political or social change.  The active process of preventing people from becoming radicalized.
The government has implemented new antiradicalism measures. 政府已经实施了新的反激进化措施。
Antiradicalism campaigns often target vulnerable youth. 反极端主义运动通常针对弱势青年。
近义词: counter-extremism, counter-radicalization, deradicalization
反义词: radicalism, extremism
词性: 名词
