dialypetalous:/ˌdaɪəliˈpɛtələs/| adj.|离瓣的；多瓣分离的|Having separate petals, not joined together.|The dialypetalous flower boasted vibrant, individually distinct petals.|近义词:polypetalous|反义词:gamopetalous, sympetalous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *dia-* "through, across" + *lyein* "to loose" + *-petalos* "having petals"
