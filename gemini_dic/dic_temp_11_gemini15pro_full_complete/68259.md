faithlessness:/ˈfeɪθləsnəs/
n.
不忠;背信;无信仰
Lack of faith or loyalty; treachery.
His faithlessness to his wife led to their divorce. 他对妻子的不忠导致了他们的离婚。
近义词：disloyalty, infidelity, treachery, perfidy
反义词：faithfulness, loyalty, fidelity
词性: 名词

faithlessness:/ˈfeɪθləsnəs/
n.
不信; 怀疑
Lack of religious belief.
Her faithlessness made it difficult for her to find comfort in times of trouble.  她的不信让她在困境中难以找到慰藉。
近义词：unbelief, skepticism, doubt, agnosticism
反义词：faith, belief, piety
词性: 名词
