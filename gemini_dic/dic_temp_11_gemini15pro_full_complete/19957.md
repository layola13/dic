bioelectric:/ˌbaɪoʊɪˈlɛktrɪk/| adj.|生物电的|Relating to bioelectricity; produced by or involving bioelectricity.  Pertaining to electrical phenomena in living organisms.|The researchers are studying the bioelectric signals of the brain. 研究人员正在研究大脑的生物电信号。|Near synonyms: bioelectrical|Near antonyms: None|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
bio- + electric
bio-: from Greek bios "life"
electric: from Late Latin electricus "produced from amber by friction," from Latin electrum "amber," from Greek elektron, also meaning "amber."
