boi:/bɔɪ/ | n. |男孩，家伙，(非正式)男人，(用于称呼)老兄|A slang term, often used online,  used to refer to a male, sometimes with affection or familiarity, sometimes ironically.  It can be seen as a more casual and playful version of "boy" or "guy". It's predominantly used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE). |例句：Hey boi, what's up? 嘿，老兄，怎么了？

例句：Look at that boi go! 看看那家伙！|近义词：guy, dude, fella, man, bro |反义词:girl, gal (informally) |词性:noun
