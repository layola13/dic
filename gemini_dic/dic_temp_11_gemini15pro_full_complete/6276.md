amoralities:/ˌeɪmɔːˈrælɪtiz/
n.
不道德行为；非道德性；无道德原则
lack of moral principles or standards of behavior; immoral practices.
The amoralities of the regime were widely condemned.  该政权的不道德行为受到广泛谴责。
近义词:immoralities, unethicalness, wrongdoing, improprieties
反义词:moralities, ethics, virtues
词性:名词 (plural noun)
