floppiness's:/ˈflɒpinəsɪz/
noun
松弛；无力；软弱
The state or quality of being floppy.  Possession of the quality of floppiness.
Her floppiness's apparent even in her handshake.  她的软弱无力甚至在握手时也很明显。
近义词:limpness, flaccidity, looseness
反义词:firmness, tightness, rigidity
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From floppy + -ness + -'s (possessive).  Floppy comes from flop (verb),  imitating the sound of something falling loosely.
