decidable:/dɪˈsaɪdəbl/| adj.|可判定的，可决定的|Able to be decided or determined; capable of being settled.|The question of whether or not to build a new library is decidable.  是否建造新图书馆的问题是可以决定的。|The halting problem is famously undecidable. 停机问题是著名的不可判定问题。|近义词:determinable, resolvable, solvable|反义词:undecidable, indeterminable, unsolvable|词性:形容词|词源:From decide + -able.  来自decide + -able.
