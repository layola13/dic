dolin:/ˈdoʊlɪn/ /ˈdɒlɪn/| n. |落水洞，溶蚀洼地，漏斗|A closed depression in a karst area.  A doline is a sinkhole or swallow hole.|The hikers stumbled upon a doline hidden amongst the trees.  徒步旅行者偶然发现树林中隐藏着一个落水洞。|The doline was so deep they couldn't see the bottom. 这个落水洞很深，他们看不到底部。|近义词：sinkhole, swallow hole|反义词: 无|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Slovene *dolina, from Proto-Slavic *dolьna (“valley”), from *dolъ (“valley”). Ultimately from the same Proto-Indo-European root as "dale".
