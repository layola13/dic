ether:/ˈiːθər/ (US) /ˈiːθə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 乙醚；以太；天空，苍穹 |A pleasant-smelling, colorless, volatile liquid that is highly flammable. Also, the upper regions of space; the sky.|例句：The patient was anesthetized with ether. 病人用乙醚麻醉了。

例句：The astronauts travelled into the vast ether. 宇航员进入了浩瀚的太空。|近义词：sky, heavens, firmament (for sky); anesthetic (for medical ether) |反义词:ground, earth (for sky)|noun
