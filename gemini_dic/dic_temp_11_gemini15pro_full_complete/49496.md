demagnetize:/dɪˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/
verb
消磁，去磁
To eliminate or reduce the magnetic properties of a material.
The watchmaker demagnetized the watch.  钟表匠给手表消了磁。

近义词:neutralize
反义词:magnetize
词性:v.
