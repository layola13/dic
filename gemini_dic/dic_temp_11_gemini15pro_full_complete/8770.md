antidromous:/ænˈtɪdrəməs/
| adj. |逆向的，逆行的|Conducting impulses in a direction opposite to the normal physiological direction; moving upstream.|Salmon are antidromous fishes, swimming upstream to spawn.|
|近义词：retrograde|反义词：orthodromic|词性: adjective
