interventionism:/ˌɪntərˈvɛnʃənɪzəm/
|n.|干涉主义，介入主义|The doctrine of or belief in intervening, especially government interference in economic affairs or political affairs of another country.|The US has a long history of interventionism in Latin American politics. 美国长期干预拉丁美洲政治。|近义词：interference, meddling, intrusion|反义词：non-intervention, neutrality|noun
