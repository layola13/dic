encephalopathy:/ɛnˌsɛfəˈlɒpəθi/ /ɛnˌsɛfəˈlɑːpəθi/
n.
脑病
Any diffuse disease of the brain that alters brain function or structure.
例句：The patient presented with symptoms of hepatic encephalopathy.  病人表现出肝性脑病的症状。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him with hypoxic encephalopathy. 医生诊断他患有缺氧性脑病。

近义词：brain disease, cerebropathy
反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  Health or normal brain function could be considered contrasting concepts.)
词性: noun
