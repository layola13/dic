nonfinitenesses:/ˌnɑːnfɪˈnɪtnɪsɪz/| n. |非限定性，非限定形式（尤指动词）|The state or quality of being nonfinite; the forms or categories of nonfinite verbs.  It refers to verb forms that do not show tense or person.  Examples include infinitives, participles, and gerunds.|His analysis of nonfinitenesses in the language was groundbreaking. 他对该语言中非限定形式的分析具有开创性。|近义词: nonfinite forms, verbals|反义词: finiteness, finite forms|词性: noun

词源: From nonfinite + -ness.  "Nonfinite" is derived from Latin "non" (not) + "finitus" (limited, bounded).
