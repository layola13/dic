cosmopolis:/kɒzˈmɒpəlɪs/ /ˌkɑːzˈmɑːpəlɪs/| n. |国际大都会；世界之都|A city inhabited by people from many different countries. |例句：New York is a true cosmopolis, a melting pot of cultures from around the world. 纽约是一个真正的国际大都市，是世界各地文化的熔炉。

例句：London is a bustling cosmopolis, attracting people from all walks of life.  伦敦是一个繁华的国际大都市，吸引着各行各业的人。|近义词：metropolis, megacity, urban center|反义词:village, hamlet, small town|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe, order”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).
