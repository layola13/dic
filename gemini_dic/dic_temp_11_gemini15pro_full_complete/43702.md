creditless:/ˈkrɛdɪtləs/
adjective
无字幕的，未署名的
Lacking credits; specifically (of a film or television program) lacking the usual introductory or concluding credits.
The studio released a creditless version of the trailer online.  工作室在网上发布了无片头片尾字幕的预告片版本。
近义词:uncredited, anonymous
反义词:credited
词性:adjective
