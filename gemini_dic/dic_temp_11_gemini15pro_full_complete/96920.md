inexplosive:/ˌɪnɪkˈspləʊsɪv/ /ˌɪnɪkˈsploʊsɪv/ | adj. | 非爆炸性的；不易爆炸的 | Not explosive; not tending to explode. |  The liquid is relatively inexplosive under normal conditions.  这种液体在正常条件下相对不易爆炸。

The demolition team used inexplosive techniques to dismantle the old bridge.  拆除小组使用非爆炸技术拆除了旧桥。 | 近义词：non-explosive, inert, stable | 反义词：explosive, volatile, flammable | 词性：形容词

Etymology:  in- (prefix meaning "not") + explosive.  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "explosive."
