cocreate:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪt/
verb
共同创造
To create something together with one or more other people.
We cocreated a new marketing strategy with our clients. 我们与客户共同创建了一个新的营销策略。
The artists cocreated a mural that reflected the community's history. 艺术家们共同创作了一幅反映社区历史的壁画。
collaborate, co-author, co-produce
invent, originate, produce
v.
