mechanist:/ˈmɛkənɪst/| n.|机械论者，机械主义者|A person who believes in or advocates the theory of mechanism, especially in philosophy or biology.  A mechanist views the universe and living beings as complex machines governed by physical laws.| Descartes was a prominent mechanist.  笛卡尔是一位著名的机械论者。|synonyms: materialist, determinist, reductionist|antonyms: vitalist, teleologist|noun
