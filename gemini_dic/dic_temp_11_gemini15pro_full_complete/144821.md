over-imports:/ˈoʊvərˈɪmpɔːrts/
noun
过度进口
The act of importing more goods than a country needs or can consume, often leading to a trade deficit.
The country's over-imports of luxury goods contributed to its economic woes.  这个国家奢侈品的过度进口导致了其经济困境。
synonyms: excess imports, surplus imports
antonyms: under-imports, limited imports
n.


over-import:/ˈoʊvərˈɪmpɔːrt/
verb
过度进口
To import more goods than necessary or desirable.
The government implemented tariffs to prevent businesses from over-importing cheap products.  政府实施关税以阻止企业过度进口廉价产品。
synonyms: import excessively, import too much
antonyms: under-import, restrict imports
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "import" (from Latin importare "to bring in," from in- "into" + portare "to carry")
