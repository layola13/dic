leadhillite:/ˈlɛdɪlaɪt/
noun
铅矾
A sulfate carbonate mineral of lead, typically occurring as colourless, white, or pale yellow monoclinic crystals.
Leadhillite is a rare mineral found in oxidized lead deposits. 铅矾是一种罕见的矿物，发现于氧化的铅矿床中。
Leadhillite crystals are often twinned. 铅矾晶体经常孪生。
Synonym: maxwelllite (a less common synonym)

词性: noun
