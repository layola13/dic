hypersexuality:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti/| n. |性欲亢进，性欲过盛|Excessive sexual desire or activity.|His hypersexuality led to numerous affairs. 他的性欲过盛导致了无数次的婚外情。|例句：Hypersexuality can be a symptom of certain medical conditions. 性欲亢进可能是某些疾病的症状。

例句：The patient exhibited signs of hypersexuality after starting the new medication.  病人开始服用新药后表现出性欲亢进的迹象。

|近义词：nymphomania (尤指女性), satyriasis (尤指男性), excessive libido|反义词：hyposexuality, low libido|词性：noun


Etymology:
From hyper- + sexuality.  "Hyper-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "over, beyond, excessive," and "sexuality" refers to the capacity for sexual feelings.
