nonproportionately: /ˌnɒnˌprəˈpɔːʃənətli/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌprəˈpɔːʃənətlɪ/ (UK)| adv.|不成比例地，不相称地|Not in proportion; to a disproportionate degree.  It describes something that does not increase or decrease at the same rate as something else.|The wealth is distributed nonproportionately among the population. 财富在人口中的分配不成比例。|Synonyms: disproportionately, unevenly, unequally|Antonyms: proportionately, proportionally, evenly|Adverb
