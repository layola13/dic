eyrie:/ˈɪəri/ |n.|鹰巢；高处危险的地方|A large nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle, typically built high in a tree or on a cliff; a high and exposed place; a lofty and often perilous dwelling or position. |The eagle's eyrie was perched precariously on the cliff face. 老鹰的巢穴危险地坐落在悬崖面上。|Synonyms: aerie, nest, perch, roost, ledge|Antonyms: ground, bottom, base|noun


eyrie:/ˈɪəri/ |adj.|（地方）高而险峻的；（空气）稀薄的|(of a place) high and exposed to strong winds; (of air) thin and difficult to breathe. |The eyrie air made it difficult to breathe. 稀薄的空气使人难以呼吸。|Synonyms: lofty, high, elevated, exposed, airy, thin|Antonyms: low, sheltered, thick, dense|adjective
