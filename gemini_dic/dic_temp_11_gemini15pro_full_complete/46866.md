daredeviltries:/ˈdɛəˌdɛvəltriz/
noun
大胆鲁莽的行为；冒险行为；不要命的举动
recklessly daring or dangerous feats; feats of daredeviltry.
His youthful daredeviltries had now been replaced by a more cautious approach. 他年轻时的鲁莽行为现在已经被一种更加谨慎的态度所取代。
synonyms:  recklessness, audacity, boldness, daring, exploits, escapades, stunts
antonyms: caution, prudence, timidity, restraint
