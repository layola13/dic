hardcovered:/ˈhɑːrdˌkʌvərd/| adj.|精装的，硬封面的|A book bound with stiff covers.|I prefer hardcovered books because they are more durable. 我更喜欢精装书，因为它们更耐用。|近义词：hardback, casebound|反义词：paperback, softcover|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"hard" + "cover" + "-ed"
"hard" (Old English heard) meaning "firm, solid, resistant"
"cover" (from Old French covrir, meaning "to cover")
"-ed" (suffix forming adjectives from nouns)
