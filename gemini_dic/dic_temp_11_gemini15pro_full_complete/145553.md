overcommunicative: /ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
过度沟通的；沟通过度的
Inclined to communicate excessively; sharing more information than necessary or desired.
She's so overcommunicative that it can be overwhelming at times. 她太爱沟通了，有时让人难以承受。
He was overcommunicative about his feelings, which made some people uncomfortable. 他过度表达自己的感受，让一些人感到不舒服。
近义词: talkative, loquacious, effusive, verbose
反义词: reticent, taciturn, reserved, laconic
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "communicative" (adjective from "communicate," from Latin communicare, meaning "to share")
