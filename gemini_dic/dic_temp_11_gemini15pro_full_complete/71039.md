firs:/fɜːrz/
n.
冷杉；冷杉木
evergreen trees of the pine family, typically having erect cones
The majestic firs stood tall against the winter sky. 庄严的冷杉在冬日的天空下巍然屹立。
近义词:pine, spruce
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑落叶树 deciduous tree)
词性:名词
