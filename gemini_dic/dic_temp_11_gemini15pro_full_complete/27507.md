campgrounds: /ˈkæmpɡraʊndz/
n.
露营地，营地
A place where people can camp, usually with facilities like restrooms and fire pits.
We stayed at several beautiful campgrounds during our road trip.  我们在公路旅行期间在几个美丽的露营地停留。
The campground offers stunning views of the mountains. 这个露营地可以欣赏到壮丽的山景。
近义词: campsites, camping areas
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) hotels, resorts
词性: 名词
