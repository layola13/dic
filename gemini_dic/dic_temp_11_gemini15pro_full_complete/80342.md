goldie:/ˈɡəʊldi/ (US), /ˈɡɒldi/ (UK)| n.|金鱼; 金发女郎; 受宠的人|A small golden-colored fish, often kept as a pet; a blonde woman; a person who is favored or beloved.|例句：The goldie swam lazily around the fishbowl. 金鱼懒洋洋地在鱼缸里游来游去。

例句：She was a stunning goldie, with hair like spun gold. 她是一位迷人的金发女郎，头发像金丝一样。


近义词：goldfish, blonde, darling, favorite|反义词:brunette (for blonde)|词性:noun

goldie:/ˈɡəʊldi/ (US), /ˈɡɒldi/ (UK)| adj.|金色的; 金黄色的|Having a golden color.|例句：The goldie sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 金色的夕阳将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。

例句：The goldie retriever wagged its tail excitedly. 金毛猎犬兴奋地摇着尾巴。

近义词：golden, gold, aureate|反义词:dark, dull|词性:adjective
