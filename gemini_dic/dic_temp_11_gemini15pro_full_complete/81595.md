greasy:/ˈɡriːsi/ /ˈɡriːzi/| adj.|油腻的；滑溜溜的；谄媚的|Covered with or full of grease; oily or slippery. Also, excessively or insincerely polite.|The mechanic's hands were greasy after working on the engine. 机修工修理完发动机后，手上满是油污。|The pizza was so greasy it made me feel sick.  这披萨太油腻了，让我觉得恶心。|近义词：oily, slippery, slimy, unctuous|反义词：clean, dry, non-greasy|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *gresi, from Old English *grēsig, from grēse (“grease”). Cognate with West Frisian griesich (“greasy”), Dutch griezig (“greasy”), German grießig (“gritty, grainy”).
