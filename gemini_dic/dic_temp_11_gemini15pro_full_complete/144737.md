over-annotates:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌteɪts/ | v. | 过度注释，过度注解 | Adds excessive annotations or notes to something, often to the point of obscuring the original content. | The professor over-annotates his student's papers, sometimes making it difficult to understand their original points. 教授过度注释了他学生的论文，有时让人难以理解他们原本的观点。

The editor over-annotates the manuscript, leaving little white space on the page. 编辑过度注释了手稿，页面上几乎没有空白。 | 近义词: over-explains, over-comments | 反义词: under-annotates, summarizes | 词性: 动词
