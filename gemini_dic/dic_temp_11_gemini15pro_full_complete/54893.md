disponer:/dɪspəˈnɛr/ /dɪspəˈnoʊr/
verb
安排，处置，布置
to arrange, to have available, to make provisions for
The general disposed his troops strategically. 将军战略性地部署了他的部队。
The company disposed of its assets. 公司处置了它的资产。
近义词: arrange, prepare, order, manage, handle
反义词: disorganize, neglect
词性: verb
