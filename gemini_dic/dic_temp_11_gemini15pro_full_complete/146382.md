overfloured:/ˌoʊvərˈflaʊərd/
adjective
撒了太多面粉的；裹了太多面粉的
Covered with too much flour.
The dough was overfloured, making it difficult to knead.  面团撒了太多面粉，很难揉。
The chicken was overfloured and the coating was too thick.  鸡肉裹了太多面粉，外皮太厚了。
overpowdered, over-floured
lightly floured
adjective
