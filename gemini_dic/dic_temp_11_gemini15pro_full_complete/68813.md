fascicule:/ˈfæsɪkjuːl/ (US), /ˈfæsɪkjuːl/ (UK)| n. |分册，分期，束，捆 |A section of a book or other written work published in installments; a small bundle or cluster.| The latest fascicule of the dictionary has just been published. 最新一期字典分册已经出版了。| synonym: installment, part, section, chapter, issue | antonym: whole, complete | noun

fascicule:/ˈfæsɪkjuːl/ (US), /ˈfæsɪkjuːl/ (UK)| n. |（解剖学）束 |A small bundle or cluster, especially of nerve or muscle fibers.|  The doctor identified the fascicule of nerves responsible for the pain. 医生确定了引起疼痛的神经束。| synonym: bundle, cluster, group | antonym: individual, separate | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fasciculus, diminutive of Latin fascis "bundle," of uncertain origin.
