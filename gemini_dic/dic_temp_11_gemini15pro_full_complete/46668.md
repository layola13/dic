damped:/dæmpt/ | adj. | 抑制的，减震的，阻尼的 | Reduced in extent or intensity, especially of oscillations or vibrations.  Characterized by a lack of enthusiasm or vigor. | The damped vibrations of the string gradually died away.  琴弦的减震振动逐渐消失了。 The party was a rather damped affair. 聚会的气氛相当沉闷。 | 近义词：suppressed, muted, subdued, softened, deadened, weakened | 反义词：amplified, enhanced, increased, strengthened | 词性: 形容词

damped:/dæmpt/ | v. |  抑制，减弱（damp 的过去式和过去分词） | Past tense and past participle of "damp." To make less strong or intense. | He damped the fire down with ashes. 他用灰烬把火扑灭了。| 近义词：suppress, mute, subdue, soften, deaden, weaken | 反义词：amplify, enhance, increase, strengthen | 词性: 动词


Etymology: From damp + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dampaz.
