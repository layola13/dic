magnetizer:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzər/| n. |磁化器，磁化者|A person or device that magnetizes something.  A device used to create a magnetic field in a material.|The magnetizer was used to create a strong magnetic field in the iron bar. 磁化器被用来在铁棒中产生强磁场。|Synonyms: magnetizing force,  magnetizing field|Antonyms: demagnetizer|noun
