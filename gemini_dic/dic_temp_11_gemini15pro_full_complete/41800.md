cortex: /ˈkɔːrteks/ (US) /ˈkɔːteks/ (UK) | n. | 皮层，皮质 | The outer layer of an organ or body part, especially the brain. | The cerebral cortex is responsible for higher-level cognitive functions. 大脑皮层负责高级认知功能。

The visual cortex processes information from the eyes.  视觉皮层处理来自眼睛的信息。

近义词: rind, bark, shell | 反义词: medulla, core | 词性: noun
