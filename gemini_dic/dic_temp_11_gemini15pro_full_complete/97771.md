injection:/ɪnˈdʒekʃən/ | n. | 注射；注射剂；注入，投入 | the act of forcing a liquid, especially a drug, into someone or something using a needle and syringe | The doctor gave him an injection to relieve the pain. 医生给他打了一针以减轻疼痛。| synonyms: shot, inoculation | antonyms: extraction, removal | noun

injection:/ɪnˈdʒekʃən/ | n. | (资金、想法等的)投入，注入 |  the introduction of something new that is intended to improve a situation or process |  The injection of new funds saved the company. 新资金的注入拯救了公司。| synonyms: infusion, investment | antonyms: withdrawal, divestment | noun
