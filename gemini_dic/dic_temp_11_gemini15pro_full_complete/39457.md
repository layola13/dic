conned:/kɑːnd/ /kɒnd/| v. |欺骗，诈骗|To persuade (someone) to do or give something by lying to them.|He conned me out of my life savings. 他骗走了我所有的积蓄。|Synonyms: tricked, swindled, defrauded, deceived|Antonyms:  honest, truthful|verb

con:/kɑːn/ /kɒn/| n. |骗局，诡计|A trick or deception.|The investment scheme turned out to be a con. 这项投资计划原来是个骗局。|Synonyms:  trick, scam, swindle, fraud|Antonyms: honesty, truth|noun
