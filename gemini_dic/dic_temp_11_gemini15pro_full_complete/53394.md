disallow:/dɪsəˈlaʊ/ | v. | 不允许，禁止 | To refuse to allow or accept something; to prohibit. | The judge disallowed the evidence. 法官不接受该证据。 | The school disallows cell phones during class. 学校不允许学生在上课期间使用手机。 | 近义词: prohibit, forbid, reject, refuse | 反义词: allow, permit, accept | 词性: 动词
词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + allow.  Ultimately from Latin *alligare "to bind to," from ad- "to" + ligare "to bind."
