brewhouse:/ˈbruːhaʊs/ | n. |酿酒厂，啤酒厂|A building or part of a building where beer is brewed.|The old brewhouse has been converted into a restaurant.  旧酿酒厂已被改建成一家餐馆。|例句：The brewhouse is filled with the aroma of hops. 啤酒厂里充满了啤酒花的香味。|近义词：brewery|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *brewhous, from Old English breáw-hūs (“brewhouse”), equivalent to brew +‎ house.
