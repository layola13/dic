overlarded:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdɪd/ | adj. | 过于油腻的，过于华丽的，过分修饰的 |  Covered or decorated excessively, especially with something rich or elaborate; overburdened.  This can refer to food made excessively rich or heavy with fat, or writing made overly elaborate or ornate with excessive detail or embellishment. | The steak was overlarded and left me feeling queasy.  这块牛排太油腻了，让我感到恶心。 | The prose was so overlarded with metaphors that the meaning was lost. 这篇文章充斥着比喻，以至于失去了意义。 | 近义词:  ostentatious, flamboyant, excessive, overdone, overwritten, rich, fatty | 反义词:  simple, plain, austere, understated | 词性: adjective
