insatiety:/ˌɪnseɪˈʃaɪəti/ |n.|不知足，贪得无厌|The state of being insatiable; inability to be satisfied.|His insatiety for power led to his downfall. 他对权力的贪得无厌导致了他的垮台。|近义词: greed, avarice, rapacity|反义词: contentment, satisfaction|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *insatietas, from Latin insatiatus ("unsatisfied"), from in- ("not") + satiatus ("satisfied"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sa- ("to satisfy").
