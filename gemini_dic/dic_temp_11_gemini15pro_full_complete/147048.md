overlaying:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪɪŋ/
v. |覆盖;叠加|The image of the map was overlaying the satellite photo. 地图图像叠加在卫星照片上。|superimposing, covering|revealing|verb
overlaying:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪɪŋ/
adj. |覆盖的;叠加的|The overlaying layers of rock showed different geological periods. 叠加的岩层显示了不同的地质时期。|superimposed, layered|separate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "overlay," which comes from the Old English "oferlecgan," meaning "to lay over or upon."  "Ofer" means "over" and "lecgan" means "to lay."
