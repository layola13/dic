on-the-ground:/ˌɒn ðə ˈɡraʊnd/ /ˌɑːn ðə ˈɡraʊnd/ | adj. |  实地的，现场的，实际的 |  Existing or happening in a place rather than being described or discussed |  例句：We need someone on-the-ground to assess the situation. 我们需要有人到现场评估情况。| 近义词：practical, realistic, hands-on | 反义词：theoretical, abstract | 词性: 形容词

on-the-ground:/ˌɒn ðə ˈɡraʊnd/ /ˌɑːn ðə ˈɡraʊnd/ |  adj. | 基层的，草根的 | Relating to the ordinary people in a society or an organization, rather than to the leaders | 例句：On-the-ground support for the policy is weak.  这项政策的基层支持很薄弱。| 近义词：grassroots, local | 反义词：top-level, leadership | 词性: 形容词


词源:  This hyphenated adjective is formed from the preposition "on" and the noun phrase "the ground." It literally refers to being situated on the surface of the earth.  The figurative meanings related to practical experience and grassroots level involvement developed from this literal sense.
