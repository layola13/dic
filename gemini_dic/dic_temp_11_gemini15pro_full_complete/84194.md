half-rebelliousness:/ˌhæf rɪˈbɛljəsnəs/
|n.| 半叛逆，略带叛逆 |A state of being partially or mildly rebellious; a tendency to resist authority or convention in a limited or moderate way.|She displayed a half-rebelliousness by wearing a slightly unconventional dress to the formal event. 她穿着略微非传统的服装出席正式活动，表现出一种半叛逆的态度。|Example:  He showed a half-rebelliousness by quietly questioning the teacher's instructions. 他默默地质疑老师的指示，表现出一种半叛逆的态度。|Synonyms: mild disobedience, partial defiance, slight insubordination|Antonyms: obedience, conformity, submission|noun

rebelliousness:/rɪˈbɛljəsnəs/
|n.|叛逆，反叛|The quality or state of being rebellious; a tendency to resist authority or control.|Example:  Teenage rebelliousness is often expressed through music and fashion. 青少年的叛逆往往通过音乐和时尚来表达。

Example:  Her rebelliousness led her to challenge the status quo. 她的叛逆精神使她挑战现状。|Synonyms: defiance, insubordination, resistance|Antonyms: obedience, conformity, submission|noun
