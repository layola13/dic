nontreated:/ˌnɒnˈtriːtɪd/ | adj. | 未经处理的，未经治疗的 | Not having been subjected to a treatment or process. | The study compared the nontreated group with the group receiving medication. 这项研究比较了未治疗组和接受药物治疗组。| The researchers analyzed nontreated wastewater samples. 研究人员分析了未经处理的废水样本。| 近义词: untreated, raw, natural | 反义词: treated, processed | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + treated.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "treated" is the past participle of the verb "treat."
