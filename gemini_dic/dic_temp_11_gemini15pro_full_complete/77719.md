gemminess:/ˈdʒɛmɪnɪs/
n.
宝石般的光辉; 宝石的性质; 璀璨
The quality of resembling a gem; brilliance.
The gemminess of the diamond caught everyone's eye.  这颗钻石的璀璨夺目吸引了所有人的目光。

近义词: brilliance, sparkle, radiance, luster
反义词: dullness, opacity
