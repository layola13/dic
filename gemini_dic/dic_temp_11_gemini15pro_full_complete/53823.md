disconnection:/ˌdɪskəˈnekʃən/ | n. | 断开，分离，中断 |A lack of connection or continuity; a disruption in the flow of something. |The disconnection of the power supply caused a blackout.  断电导致了停电。| synonyms: separation, detachment, severance, break, interruption | antonyms: connection, union, junction, linkage | noun

disconnection:/ˌdɪskəˈnekʃən/ | n. |  失去联系，疏远 | A state of being emotionally or socially detached. | He felt a growing disconnection from his family. 他感到与家人日渐疏远。 | synonyms: alienation, estrangement, isolation | antonyms: closeness, intimacy, connection | noun
