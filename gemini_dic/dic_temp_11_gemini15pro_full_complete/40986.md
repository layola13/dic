coordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
协调的，同等的
bringing different elements into a harmonious or efficient relationship
例句：She's responsible for coordinating the work of several committees. 她负责协调几个委员会的工作。

例句：The coordinating conjunction "and" connects two independent clauses. 并列连词“and”连接两个独立分句。

近义词：harmonizing, integrating, synchronizing, unifying
反义词：disorganizing, conflicting, clashing
词性：adjective


coordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
协调，调节
(of two or more people or things) working together well
例句：We are coordinating our efforts to provide better service. 我们正在协调我们的努力，以提供更好的服务。

例句：They are coordinating the relief efforts after the earthquake. 他们正在协调地震后的救援工作。

近义词：collaborating, cooperating, harmonizing, integrating
反义词：conflicting, clashing
词性: verb
