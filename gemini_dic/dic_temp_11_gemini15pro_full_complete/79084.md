glacial:/ˈɡleɪʃəl/ | adj. | 冰的，冰川的；冰冷的，冷淡的 | Relating to glaciers or ice; extremely cold or slow | The glacial landscape was breathtaking. 冰川景色令人叹为观止。| synonyms: icy, frigid, frosty, arctic, freezing | antonyms: warm, hot, tropical, torrid | adjective

glacial:/ˈɡleɪʃəl/ | adj. | 缓慢的 | Extremely slow | Progress on the project has been glacial.  这个项目的进展极其缓慢。| synonyms: slow, sluggish, tardy | antonyms: fast, rapid, swift, quick | adjective
