hunte:/hʌnt/ | v. | hunt (archaic/dialectal form) | To chase and kill wild animals for food or sport. | He hunted deer in the forest. 他在森林里猎鹿。|  近义词: chase, pursue, stalk | 反义词: protect, conserve | verb

hunt:/hʌnt/ | n. | hunt | The act of hunting; a search or pursuit. | The hunt for the missing treasure lasted for years.  对丢失宝藏的搜寻持续了多年。| 近义词: search, quest, pursuit | 反义词: surrender, abandonment | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *huntian, from Proto-Germanic *huntijōną (“to hunt”), from Proto-Indo-European *ken- (“to seek”).  Cognate with Dutch hunten, German hunten, Old Norse henta.
