bawdrick:/ˈbɔːdrɪk/| n.| 腰带；肩带；佩剑的挂带；绶带|A baldric or baldrick is a belt worn over one shoulder, or around the neck and one shoulder, that is typically used to carry a weapon (especially a sword) or other implement.  It can also be any belt-like article of attire, such as a ceremonial sash.|The knight wore his sword in a bawdrick. 骑士把剑挂在肩带上。|Synonyms: baldric, belt, sash, strap, girdle|Antonyms: None|noun
