continuous:/kənˈtɪnjuəs/ | adj. | 延伸的，广阔的 | Extending or prolonged without interruption in space. | The forest is continuous for miles. 这片森林绵延数英里。| a continuous stretch of beach 绵延不绝的海滩 | synonyms: extensive, vast, expansive | antonyms: limited, restricted, confined | adjective
