cross-fertilized:/ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzd/ | adj. |交叉受精的，异花受粉的 |  Made fertile by the transfer of pollen from a different plant or variety.  In a broader sense, it can also refer to the mixing of different ideas, influences, or styles. | The apple trees were cross-fertilized with pollen from a different variety to improve the fruit.  这些苹果树用不同品种的花粉进行异花授粉以改良果实。| Synonyms: hybridized, crossbred, interbred | Antonyms: self-fertilized, inbred | adjective


cross-fertilize:/ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ | v. |交叉受精，异花授粉 | To fertilize a plant with pollen from a different plant or variety.  Figuratively, it refers to the mixing of different ideas, influences, or styles, often leading to new and innovative results. |  Researchers cross-fertilized different strains of rice to develop a more drought-resistant variety. 研究人员将不同品种的水稻进行杂交以培育出更耐旱的品种。 | Synonyms: hybridize, crossbreed, interbreed | Antonyms: self-fertilize, inbreed | verb
