harlech: /ˈhɑːrlɛk/ (UK), /ˈhɑːrlɛx/ (US)| n.| 哈莱克 (威尔士的一个小镇) |A town in Gwynedd, northwest Wales, on the northern coast of Cardigan Bay. It is known for its imposing medieval castle, Harlech Castle.|Harlech Castle is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 哈莱克城堡是联合国教科文组织世界遗产地。| Harlech is a popular tourist destination. 哈莱克是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。| 近义词: town, village | 反义词: city, metropolis | 词性: 名词

词源: From Welsh *Harlleg, possibly from *hardd llech (“beautiful slate”).
