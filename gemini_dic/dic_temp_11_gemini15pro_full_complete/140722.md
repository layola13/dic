omnilucent:/ˌɒmniˈluːsənt/
adjective
全光透的；全透明的
Allowing light to pass through from all directions; transparent in every part.
例句：The artist created an omnilucent sculpture using a special type of glass. 艺术家用一种特殊的玻璃创作了一个全光透的雕塑。

例句：The omnilucent walls of the building allowed for a panoramic view of the city.  这栋建筑的全透明墙壁可以欣赏到城市的全景。
近义词：transparent, translucent, diaphanous
反义词：opaque
词性：adjective


omni-/ˈɒmni/  (prefix) all; every
-lucent/-ˈluːsənt/ (suffix) shining; giving off light
