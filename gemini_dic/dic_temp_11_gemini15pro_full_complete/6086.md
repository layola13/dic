americanised:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzd/
adjective
美式化的
Made or become American in character; adapted to or influenced by American customs, institutions, or culture.
McDonald's is a highly americanised version of a traditional German dish. 麦当劳是传统德国菜肴的高度美式化版本。
The spelling of "colour" has been americanised to "color". “colour”的拼写已被美式化为“color”。
近义词: westernized, modernized
反义词: traditional, indigenous
词性: adjective
