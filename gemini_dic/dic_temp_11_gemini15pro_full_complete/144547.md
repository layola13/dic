outvoyage:/ˈaʊtvɔɪɪdʒ/
noun
外出航行，出航
A journey outward from a port or country, especially by sea.
The outvoyage to Australia was long and arduous.  从英国到澳大利亚的航行漫长而艰辛。
近义词:outbound voyage, outward voyage
反义词:homeward voyage, return voyage
词性:n.

outvoyage:/ˈaʊtvɔɪɪdʒ/
verb (rarely used)
出航，外出航行
To travel outward from a port or country, especially by sea.
The ship outvoyaged from Plymouth. 这艘船从普利茅斯出航。
近义词:sail out, depart
反义词:return, come back
词性:v.
