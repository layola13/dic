legalese: /ˌliːɡəˈliːz/
|n.|法律术语；法律措辞|Technical jargon used in legal documents, often difficult for non-lawyers to understand.|The contract was full of legalese, making it hard for the average person to comprehend.|近义词: jargon, legalistic language, technical terminology|反义词: plain language, simple English|词性:名词


Etymology:  From legal + -ese (a suffix forming nouns meaning "language of," "style of," as in Chinese, journalese).
