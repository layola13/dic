cantle:/ˈkæntl/| n.| (马鞍的)后桥; (山丘的)顶部; 一小部分; |A piece of something, especially a small piece of a larger whole.  The back part of a horse's saddle.| The rider gripped the cantle of the saddle as the horse reared. 骑手在马后仰时紧紧抓住马鞍的后桥。|Synonyms: piece, portion, section, fragment |Antonyms: whole, entirety| noun


cantle:/ˈkæntl/| v.| 分割; 切下; |To cut off a piece of something.|  They cantled a small piece of land from the larger estate.  他们从更大的庄园中分割出一小块土地。|Synonyms: divide, separate, sever, cut off |Antonyms: join, unite, combine| verb
