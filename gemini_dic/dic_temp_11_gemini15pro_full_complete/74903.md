freshman:/ˈfrɛʃmən/ | n. |新生，一年级学生 |A student in the first year of high school or college. |例句：He's a freshman at Harvard University. 他是哈佛大学的一年级学生。

例句：The freshman class is very large this year. 今年的新生班级人数很多。|近义词：first-year student, newcomer, beginner |反义词：senior, graduate, upperclassman |词性：名词

freshmen:/ˈfrɛʃmən/ | n. |新生们，一年级学生们 (freshman的复数形式) | Plural form of freshman, referring to multiple students in their first year. |例句：The freshmen are still getting used to college life. 新生们还在适应大学生活。


例句：The freshmen were invited to a welcome reception. 新生们被邀请参加欢迎招待会。|近义词：first-year students, newcomers, beginners |反义词：seniors, graduates, upperclassmen |词性：名词


**词源:**

From fresh + -man.  Originally referred to a new entrant into any field, but now primarily used in an academic context.
