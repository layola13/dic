mittyish:/ˈmɪtiɪʃ/
adjective
空想的，不切实际的，爱幻想的
Having unrealistic or impractical ideas or fantasies, characteristic of Walter Mitty, a fictional character known for his elaborate daydreams.
He had a mittyish notion of becoming a famous rock star despite never having picked up a guitar. 他有一个不切实际的想法，那就是成为一个著名的摇滚明星，尽管他从未拿起过吉他。
She often gets lost in mittyish fantasies about traveling the world. 她经常沉浸在环游世界的幻想中。
近义词:fanciful, dreamy, imaginative, quixotic
反义词:realistic, practical, down-to-earth
词性:adjective
