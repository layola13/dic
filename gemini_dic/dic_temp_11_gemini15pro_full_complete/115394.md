manslaughters:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərz/| n. |过失杀人(复数)|The unlawful killing of a human being without malice aforethought.  It's a legal term for homicide that's less culpable than murder.|The jury found him guilty of two counts of manslaughter. 陪审团认定他犯有两项过失杀人罪。|Synonyms: homicide, killing, slaying|Antonyms: murder (sometimes), justifiable homicide|Noun
