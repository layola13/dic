gender-bendings:/ˈdʒɛndərˌbɛndɪŋz/| n.|性别转换，性别颠倒|The act of portraying a character as the opposite gender from their original depiction, often for humorous or exploratory purposes.  It can involve changing clothing, mannerisms, and social roles.|Fanfiction often features gender-bendings of popular characters.|近义词：gender-swapping, gender-flipping, crossdressing (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
