hypotenuse:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːz/ (US) /haɪˈpɒtənjuːs/ (UK)| n. |斜边|The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle. |The hypotenuse of this triangle is 5 cm long.  这个三角形的斜边长5厘米。|Synonyms: longest side |Antonyms: leg, cathetus|noun
