leonid:/ˈliːənɪd/| n. |狮子座流星雨；狮子座流星群中的流星|A meteor shower appearing to radiate from the constellation Leo.  It is associated with the comet Tempel-Tuttle. |例句：The Leonids are usually a modest meteor shower, but every 33 years, they produce a meteor storm.  狮子座流星雨通常是一个规模适中的流星雨，但每隔33年，它们就会产生一场流星暴。

例句：We stayed up late to watch the Leonids streak across the sky. 我们熬夜观看狮子座流星雨划过天空。|近义词：meteor shower, meteor storm (when exceptionally active)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
