adversary: /ˈædvərˌseri/ (US), /ˈædvəsəri/ (UK)| n.| 对手，敌手|Someone who opposes or fights against another in a contest, conflict, or dispute.|He saw her as his main adversary in the election. 他把她视为他在选举中的主要对手。|
近义词: opponent, rival, foe, enemy, antagonist|
反义词: ally, friend, supporter, partner|
词性: noun

adversary: /ˈædvərˌseri/ (US), /ˈædvəsəri/ (UK)| adj. (less common)| 对立的，敌对的|Characterized by opposition or hostility.|Adversary proceedings in court can be stressful. 法庭上的对抗性程序可能会很有压力。|
近义词: opposing, hostile, antagonistic, conflicting|
反义词: friendly, supportive, cooperative, allied|

词源:
From Old French adversaire, from Latin adversarius (“opposed, turned towards, facing”), from adversus (“turned towards, facing, opposite, hostile”), past participle of advertere (“to turn towards”), from ad- (“towards”) + vertere (“to turn”).
