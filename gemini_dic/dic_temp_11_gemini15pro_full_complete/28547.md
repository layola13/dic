cardioid:/ˈkɑːrdɪɔɪd/| n. | 心形线，心脏线 |A heart-shaped curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls around a fixed circle of the same radius. |The microphone has a cardioid polar pattern, meaning it picks up sound mostly from the front.  这个麦克风具有心形拾音模式，意味着它主要从正面拾取声音。

The shape of the pond resembles a cardioid.  池塘的形状像一颗心形线。|近义词: heart-shaped |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
