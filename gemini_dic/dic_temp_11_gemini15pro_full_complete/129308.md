noggin:/ˈnɒɡɪn/ (UK) /ˈnɑːɡɪn/ (US)| n. |脑袋；头；智慧；少量烈酒| a person's head, or their mind/intelligence; a small amount of alcoholic drink |例句：Use your noggin and figure it out!  动动脑筋，把它想出来！

|例句：He poured himself a noggin of whiskey. 他给自己倒了一小杯威士忌。|近义词：head, mind, intellect, brain, a drop, a nip|反义词：(For head/mind)  body; (For alcohol)  abstinence|词性: noun
