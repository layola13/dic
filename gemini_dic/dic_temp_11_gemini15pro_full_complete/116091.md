martyrization:/ˌmɑːrtərɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|殉道；殉难；牺牲|The process of being made a martyr, often through death or suffering for one's beliefs. |The film depicts the martyrization of early Christians. 这部电影描绘了早期基督徒的殉道。|Synonyms: sacrifice, immolation, victimization|Antonyms: glorification, celebration|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin martyrizatio, from martyrizare "to make a martyr," from martyr + -izare.  Ultimately from Greek martyr "witness."
