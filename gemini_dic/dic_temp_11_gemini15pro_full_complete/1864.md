acrophobia:/ˈækrəˌfoʊbiə/| n.| 恐高症 | An extreme or irrational fear of heights. |  She couldn't go on the rooftop because of her acrophobia. 她因为恐高症而无法上屋顶。

My acrophobia prevented me from enjoying the mountain view. 我的恐高症让我无法欣赏山景。


| 近义词: altophobia, height phobia | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a love of heights) | 词性: noun
