nominalise:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪz/ | v. |名词化，将…变成名词 | To convert (a word or phrase, such as an adjective or verb) into a noun. | The adjective "difficult" can be nominalised to form the noun "difficulty".  形容词“difficult”可以名词化形成名词“difficulty”。|  They nominalised the verb "fail" into the noun "failure". 他们把动词“fail”名词化成了名词“failure”。 | synonym: nounify | antonym: verbalize | verb
