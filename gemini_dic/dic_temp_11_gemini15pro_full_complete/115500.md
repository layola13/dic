manuscriptural:/ˌmænjuˈskrɪptʃərəl/
adjective
手写的，手稿的
Relating to or characteristic of a manuscript; written by hand.
The library holds a vast collection of manuscriptural documents dating back to the 12th century.  图书馆收藏了大量可追溯到12世纪的手写文件。
The manuscriptural evidence supports the theory. 手稿证据支持这一理论。
近义词: handwritten, hand-scribed, pen-written
反义词: printed, typed, digital

词性: adjective
