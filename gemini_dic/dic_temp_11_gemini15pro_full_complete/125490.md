multiracial:/ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəl/ | adj. |多种族的；多民族的 |  Of, relating to, or consisting of people of many different races. |  Example:  The United States is becoming increasingly multiracial. 美国正变得越来越多种族化。

Example:  She grew up in a multiracial neighborhood. 她在一个多种族混合的街区长大。 | 近义词: mixed-race, interracial | 反义词: monoracial, uniracial | 词性: 形容词
