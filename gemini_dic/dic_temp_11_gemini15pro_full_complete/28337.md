caramelizes:/ˈkærəməˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
焦糖化;使变成焦糖色
(of a food containing sugar) change in colour and flavour as a result of being heated.
The onions caramelized beautifully in the pan. 洋葱在锅里焦糖化得很漂亮。
近义词:brown, glaze
反义词:N/A
词性:v.


caramelize:/ˈkærəməˌlaɪz/
verb
焦糖化;使变成焦糖色
(of a food containing sugar) change in colour and flavour as a result of being heated.
The sugar caramelized over a low heat. 糖在小火上焦糖化了。
近义词:brown, glaze
反义词:N/A
词性:v.


caramelized:/ˈkærəməˌlaɪzd/
adjective
焦糖化的
having been caramelized.
The caramelized onions added a rich sweetness to the dish. 焦糖化的洋葱给菜肴增添了浓郁的甜味。
近义词:browned, glazed
反义词:N/A
词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French carameliser, from caramel ("burnt sugar"), from Spanish caramelo ("sugar cane"), probably from Late Latin cannamellis ("sugar cane"), from Latin canna ("cane") + mellis (genitive of mel, "honey").
