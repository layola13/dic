overburden:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdən/ | n. vt.| 过载，覆盖层，负担 | An excessive load or burden. The rock or soil overlying a mineral deposit.  To load with excessive weight or  burden. | The company was overburdened with debt.  这家公司负债累累。 The overburden was removed to expose the coal seam. 覆盖层被移除以露出煤层。  Don't overburden yourself with too much work. 不要让自己工作过度。 | 近义词：overload, overtax, burden, covering, mantle | 反义词：underload, lighten | 词性：名词，动词

overburden:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdən/ | vt.|  使负担过重，使过度劳累| To load with too much weight, work, or responsibility.|  He was overburdened with responsibilities. 他肩负着过多的责任。| 近义词：overload, overtax | 反义词：relieve, lighten | 词性：动词
