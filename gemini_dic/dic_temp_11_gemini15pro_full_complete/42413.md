counterconspiracy:/ˌkaʊntər kənˈspɪrəsi/
noun
反阴谋，对抗阴谋
A conspiracy designed to counteract another conspiracy.
The government uncovered a counterconspiracy aimed at destabilizing the regime. 政府发现了一个旨在破坏政权的对抗阴谋。
synonyms: counterplot, countermine
antonyms: conspiracy, plot
n.
