kickstarted:/ˌkɪkˈstɑːr.tɪd/ | adj. |启动的，开始的|Started or reinvigorated by an initial boost, impetus, or encouragement.|The kickstarted project is now showing promising results.  这个启动的项目现在正显示出可喜的成果。|Synonyms: launched, begun, initiated, boosted |Antonyms: halted, stopped, ceased, ended |adjective
