osteophone:/ˈɒstiəfəʊn/| n.|骨传导助听器，骨传电话|A device that transmits sound through the bones of the skull to the inner ear.|He used an osteophone to hear better as his outer ear was damaged. 他因为外耳受损，所以使用骨传导助听器来更好地听到声音。|Synonyms: bone conduction hearing aid|Antonyms: air conduction hearing aid|noun
