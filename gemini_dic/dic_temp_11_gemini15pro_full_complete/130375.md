non-user:/ˌnɒn ˈjuːzər/ (US) /ˌnɑːn ˈjuːzər/| n.|非用户|Someone who does not use a particular product, service, or system.|Example: The website is not designed for non-users.  这个网站不是为非用户设计的。|Example:  The survey also included a control group of non-users. 这项调查还包括一个非用户对照组。|Synonyms: non-member, outsider|Antonyms: user, member|noun
