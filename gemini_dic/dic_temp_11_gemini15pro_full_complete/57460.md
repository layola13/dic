drafted:/ˈdræftɪd/ /drɑːftɪd/
verb
起草的；应征入伍的；被选拔的
selected or chosen, especially for a special task or duty; conscripted for military service; having been drawn up in written form

例句：
He drafted a letter to the editor.
他给编辑草拟了一封信。

The quarterback was drafted first overall.
这名四分卫被选为状元。

He was drafted into the army.
他被征召入伍。

近义词：conscripted, enlisted, recruited, selected, chosen, composed, written
反义词：rejected, discharged, volunteered

词性：v.
