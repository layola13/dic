listless:/ˈlɪstlɪs/| adj.|无精打采的；倦怠的；冷漠的|Lacking energy and enthusiasm; apathetic.|例句：He sat there, listless and depressed. 他坐在那里，无精打采，沮丧不已。|例句：The heat made everyone feel listless. 炎热的天气让每个人都感到倦怠。|近义词：lethargic, apathetic, languid, inert, spiritless|反义词：energetic, enthusiastic, vigorous, lively|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From list (n.) "desire, inclination," + -less.  Originally meaning "having no desire or inclination," the sense shifted to "lacking energy or enthusiasm" in the 17th century.
