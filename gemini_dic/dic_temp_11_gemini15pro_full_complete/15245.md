backgammoned:/ˈbækɡæmənd/ | v. | 被彻底击败，被困住 | To defeat (an opponent in backgammon) so completely that the opponent is unable to remove any checkers from the board.  In a more general sense, it can mean to be thoroughly defeated or put in a hopeless position. |  He was backgammoned in the final game of the tournament. 他在锦标赛的最后一局比赛中被彻底击败了。|  She felt backgammoned by her circumstances. 她觉得自己的处境让她陷入绝境。 | Synonyms: trounced, routed, defeated, thwarted | Antonyms: victorious, triumphant | verb

backgammoning:/ˈbækɡæmənɪŋ/ | n. |  彻底击败，困住（对手）的行为 | The act of backgammoning someone, usually in the game of backgammon. | His backgammoning of his opponent was swift and decisive.  他迅速而果断地击败了对手。| The backgammoning left him feeling humiliated. 彻底的失败让他感到羞辱。 | Synonyms: routing, thrashing | Antonyms:  losing, surrendering | noun
