limberly:/ˈlɪmbərli/ | adv. | 灵活地，柔软地 | In a limber or agile manner; flexibly. | She danced limberly across the stage. 她轻盈地在舞台上舞动。| 近义词：flexibly, supplely, agilely, nimbly | 反义词：stiffly, rigidly | 词性：副词

词源:  From "limber" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Limber" comes from Middle English "limer," probably from Old English *lēmber.  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse *limpra, meaning "to soften."

what if = 如果...会怎么样

what for = 为了什么
What did you buy this tool for? 你买这个工具是为了什么？

what about = ...怎么样
I'm going to the cinema, what about you? 我要去看电影，你呢？
