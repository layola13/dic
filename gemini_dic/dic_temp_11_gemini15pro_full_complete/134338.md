nonintegrated:/ˌnɒnˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/
adjective
未整合的，未集成的
not integrated; not combined into a whole
The company's nonintegrated systems made data analysis difficult.  这家公司未整合的系统使得数据分析很困难。
The report highlighted the challenges of a nonintegrated approach to healthcare.  报告强调了医疗保健中未整合方法的挑战。
近义词:separate, disconnected, disjointed
反义词:integrated, unified, combined
词性:adjective
