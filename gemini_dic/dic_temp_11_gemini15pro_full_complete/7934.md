anteriorly:/ænˈtɪəriərli/
adverb
向前地，在前面
Situated toward the front; in front.
The sternum is located anteriorly to the heart.  胸骨位于心脏的前面。
The tumor was located anteriorly. 肿瘤位于前方。
近义词:frontally, forward, to the front
反义词:posteriorly, behind, to the rear
词性:adverb

词源:
From Latin *anterior* (comparative of *ante* "before, in front").
