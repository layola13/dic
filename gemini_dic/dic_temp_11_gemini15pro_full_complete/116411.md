matabele:/ˌmætəˈbiːliː/ /ˌmɑːtəˈbeɪleɪ/
noun|马塔贝莱人；恩德贝莱人（非洲南部的一个民族）|A member of a Bantu-speaking people of southwestern Zimbabwe and northeastern Botswana, traditionally a warrior people.  The Matabele are closely related to the Zulu people.|The Matabele established a powerful kingdom in the 19th century.|Synonym: Ndebele|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a people group)|n.
