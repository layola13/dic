intelligencer:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsər/| n.|情报人员；报信者；通报者|A person who gathers or provides intelligence, especially secret information.|The general sent an intelligencer to scout the enemy's position. 将军派了一名情报人员去侦察敌人的位置。|Synonyms: spy, informant, scout, agent|Antonyms: civilian, bystander|noun

intelligencer:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsər/| n.|情报刊物；新闻通讯|A publication, such as a newsletter or journal, that provides intelligence or news.|The weekly intelligencer provided updates on local business developments.  这份周刊提供了当地商业发展的最新消息。|Synonyms: newsletter, bulletin, journal, periodical|Antonyms: N/A|noun
