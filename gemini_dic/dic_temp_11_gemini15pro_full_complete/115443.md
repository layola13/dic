mantua:/ˈmæntʃuə/ | n. | مانت娅（一种女式宽松长外袍）；曼图亚（意大利城市） | A woman's loose gown worn over a corset and petticoat, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.  Also refers to the city of Mantua in Italy. |  The queen wore a beautiful mantua embroidered with gold thread. 女王穿着一件绣着金线的漂亮 مانت娅。 |  She visited Mantua during her trip to Italy.  她在意大利旅行期间参观了曼图亚。 |  gown, robe, dress | cloak, coat | noun
