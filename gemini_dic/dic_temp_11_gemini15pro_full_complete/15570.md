bagworm:/ˈbæɡwɜːrm/| n. |蓑蛾，袋虫|A moth larva that lives in a silken bag which it carries around as it feeds.|例句：The bagworm constructs its protective case from silk and bits of leaves. 蓑蛾用丝和碎叶建造它的保护壳。

例句：Bagworms can be a pest in gardens, damaging trees and shrubs. 蓑蛾在花园里可能成为害虫，损害树木和灌木。|近义词：casebearer, case moth|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bag + worm.  "Bag" refers to the protective casing the larva creates, and "worm" refers to the larva's worm-like appearance.
