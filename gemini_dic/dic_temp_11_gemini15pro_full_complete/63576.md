environmentalist:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪst/ | n. | 环保主义者；环境保护论者 | A person who is concerned about and advocates for the protection of the environment. | 例句：The environmentalist spoke out against the new dam project.  这位环保主义者公开反对新的大坝项目。

例句：She is a passionate environmentalist dedicated to saving endangered species. 她是一位充满激情的环保主义者，致力于拯救濒危物种。

近义词：conservationist, ecologist, nature lover | 反义词：polluter | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From environment + -al + -ist.  "Environment" comes from the Old French environer, meaning "to surround."  "-al" forms an adjective, and "-ist" denotes a person who practices or believes in something.
