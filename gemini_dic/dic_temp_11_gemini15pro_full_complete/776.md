absorb:/əbˈzɔːrb/ (US) /əbˈsɔːb/ (UK)| v. |吸收; 理解; 使全神贯注|to take something in, especially gradually; to understand facts or ideas completely and remember them; to take up someone's attention completely|The sponge absorbs water. 海绵吸水。|synonyms: soak up, take in, imbibe, assimilate, incorporate|antonyms: exude, emit, release|verb

absorb:/əbˈzɔːrb/ (US) /əbˈsɔːb/ (UK)| adj. |吸引人的|engrossing|The book was so absorb that I couldn't put it down. 这本书太吸引人了，我爱不释手。|synonyms: engaging, captivating, fascinating|antonyms: boring, tedious, uninteresting|adjective
