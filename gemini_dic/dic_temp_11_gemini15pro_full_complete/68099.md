fads:/fædz/
|n.|一时流行的风尚；时尚；一时的狂热|An intense and widely shared enthusiasm for something, especially one that is short-lived and without basis in the object's qualities; a craze.|例句：The latest fad among teenagers is collecting vintage sneakers.  青少年最近的流行趋势是收集复古运动鞋。

例句：He's always chasing the latest fads. 他总是追逐最新的潮流。
|近义词：crazes, trends, fashions, vogues|反义词：traditions, classics|Noun
