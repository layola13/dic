downdraught:/ˈdaʊndræft/ (US) /ˈdaʊndrɑːft/ (UK)| n.|下沉气流；下降气流；（尤指房间里的）穿堂风|A downdraught is a downward current of air.  A downdraught can extinguish a fire, or make flying difficult. |例句：The downdraught from the helicopter caused the small boat to rock violently. 直升机产生的下沉气流使小船剧烈摇晃。
