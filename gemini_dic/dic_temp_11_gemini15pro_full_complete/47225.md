dazedness:/ˈdeɪzɪdnəs/ /ˈdeɪzdnəs/ | n. | 茫然，恍惚；昏迷 | A state of being dazed; a feeling of being stunned or confused. |  He walked in a dazedness after being hit on the head.  他头部被击中后，茫然地走着。

She experienced a period of dazedness following the accident. 事故发生后，她经历了一段时间的茫然。| 近义词：confusion, disorientation, bewilderment, lightheadedness, stupor | 反义词：alertness, awareness, clarity | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dazed + -ness.  Dazed comes from the Old English *dǣsed, past participle of *dǣsan "to stupefy," of uncertain origin.
