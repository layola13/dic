daydreams:/ˈdeɪdriːmz/| n.|白日梦，幻想|A series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present.|She filled her notebooks with daydreams and stories. 她的笔记本里充满了白日梦和故事。|Synonyms: fantasies, reveries, musings|Antonyms: realities, actualities|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From day + dream.  First recorded in the late 16th century.  "Day" referring to waking hours, as opposed to dreams during sleep.
