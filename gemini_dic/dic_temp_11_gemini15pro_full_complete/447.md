ableist:/ˈeɪblɪst/| adj. n.|歧视残疾人的；歧视残疾人者|Discriminating against people with disabilities; characterized by or perpetuating such discrimination.  A person who holds such discriminatory views.|例句：That comment was very ableist. 那句话带有歧视残疾人的意味。|例句：He was accused of being an ableist. 他被指责歧视残疾人。|近义词：discriminatory, prejudiced, bigoted|反义词：inclusive, accepting|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology:  From "able" + "-ist."  The suffix "-ist" is used to form nouns and adjectives denoting a person who practices or adheres to a particular doctrine or system, or who holds a particular belief. In this case, it highlights a focus on "ableness" in a way that excludes or devalues those with disabilities.
