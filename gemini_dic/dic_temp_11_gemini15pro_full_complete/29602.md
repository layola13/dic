categorical:/ˌkætəˈɡɒrɪkl/ /ˌkætəˈɡɔːrɪkl/ | adj. |绝对的，明确的，无条件的；分类的| stated in a very clear and definite way, allowing no doubt or confusion; relating to or involving the classification of things into categories | The president issued a categorical denial of the rumors. 总统明确否认了这些谣言。| synonyms: absolute, definite, explicit, unconditional, unequivocal | antonyms: ambiguous, equivocal, vague | adjective

categorical:/ˌkætəˈɡɒrɪkl/ /ˌkætəˈɡɔːrɪkl/ | adj. |分类的| relating to or involving the classification of things into categories | Categorical data is often presented in bar charts.  分类数据通常用条形图表示。| synonyms: classified, taxonomic | antonyms: unclassified | adjective
