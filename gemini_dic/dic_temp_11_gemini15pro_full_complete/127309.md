navyist:/ˈneɪviɪst/
noun
海军主义者；海权主义者
A person who advocates or believes in the importance of naval power.
He was a staunch navyist, believing that a strong navy was essential for national security. 他是一位坚定的海军主义者，认为强大的海军对国家安全至关重要。
近义词:navalist, jingoist
反义词:pacifist
词性:n.

Etymology:
From "navy" + "-ist" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system).  "Navy" ultimately derives from Old French "navie" meaning "fleet of ships."
