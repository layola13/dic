entrustments: /ɪnˈtrʌstmənts/ | n. |委托；信托；托付| The act of entrusting something to someone.  The responsibility or item entrusted. |  She was given several important entrustments by her manager. 她被经理委以几项重要任务。|  The lawyer discussed the terms of the entrustment with his client. 律师与他的客户讨论了委托条款。|synonyms: commissions, delegations, assignments| antonyms: appropriations, seizures, usurpations| noun
