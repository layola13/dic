embushment:/ɪmˈbʊʃmənt/| n. |埋伏；伏击；埋伏地点|An ambushment is the act of ambushing someone or the state of being ambushed. It can also refer to the place where an ambush occurs.|The soldiers lay in ambushment, waiting for the enemy patrol. 士兵们埋伏起来，等待着敌人的巡逻队。|近义词：ambush, trap, snare|反义词: None|词性: Noun

embushment:/ɪmˈbʊʃmənt/| n. |埋伏；伏击；埋伏地点|The act of lying in wait to attack by surprise; or the state of being attacked by surprise from a concealed place.  It can also refer to the place of ambush. |They planned an ambushment in the dense forest. 他们在茂密的森林里策划了一次伏击。|近义词：ambush, ambuscade|反义词: None|词性: Noun
