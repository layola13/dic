noun
坚定性，坚固性
The quality of being firm or resolute; resoluteness.  The state of being solid or stable; stability.
The firmness of her handshake impressed me. 她的握手很有力，给我留下了深刻的印象。
The firmness of the ground made it ideal for building.  地基很坚固，非常适合建造房屋。
synonyms:  resoluteness, steadfastness, determination, resolve, strength, solidity, stability, hardness
antonyms:  weakness, instability, softness, flexibility, indecision
