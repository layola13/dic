hoity-toity:/ˌhɔɪtiˈtɔɪti/| adj.|傲慢的，自命不凡的|Thinking oneself superior to others; haughty or snobbish.|例句：She gave me a hoity-toity look. 她傲慢地看了我一眼。|例句：He has a hoity-toity attitude towards people he considers beneath him.  他对那些他认为不如他的人有一种自命不凡的态度。|近义词：snobbish, haughty, arrogant, supercilious, pompous|反义词：humble, modest, down-to-earth|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Likely a playful reduplication of "hoity," an obsolete adjective meaning "frolicsome" or "riotous," perhaps influenced by "highfalutin."
