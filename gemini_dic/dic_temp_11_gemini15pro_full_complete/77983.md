gentilish: /dʒɛnˈtiːlɪʃ/
adjective
有教养的；显得高贵的；上流社会的；装模作样的
Having or showing refinement and good manners; pretending or trying to appear upper-class.

例句：
She spoke in a rather gentilish accent.
她说话的口音有点装腔作势。

He affects a gentilish air, but his family is quite ordinary.
他装出一副上流社会的样子，但他的家庭很普通。


近义词：
refined, cultured, polished, sophisticated, pretentious, affected

反义词：
crude, vulgar, uncouth, unrefined, natural, genuine

词性
adjective
