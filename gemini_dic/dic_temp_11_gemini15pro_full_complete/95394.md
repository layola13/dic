inchoate:/ɪnˈkoʊeɪt/ /ɪnˈkəʊeɪt/| n.|未完成的事物|Something that is inchoate or just beginning.|The project was still an inchoate mass of ideas.|Synonyms: beginning, start, outset|Antonyms: completion, end, finish|noun
