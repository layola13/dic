|n.|轮回；灵魂转世|The doctrine of metempsychosis suggests that the soul is reborn into a new body after death.|例句：Some religions believe in metempsychosis, where the soul is reborn into another life form. 一些宗教相信轮回，灵魂会重生到另一种生命形式中。

例句：The concept of metempsychosis is central to many Eastern philosophies. 轮回的概念是许多东方哲学的核心。|近义词：reincarnation, transmigration, rebirth|反义词:|noun
