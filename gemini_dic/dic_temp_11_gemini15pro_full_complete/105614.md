kinesiatrics:/ˌkɪˌniːziˈætrɪks/| n.|运动疗法；运动治疗学|The study and application of movement, especially in the treatment of disease or injury. It focuses on improving physical function and promoting overall well-being through exercise and other movement-based therapies.|Kinesiatrics plays a vital role in rehabilitation after surgery.  运动疗法在术后康复中起着至关重要的作用。

Kinesiatrics is often prescribed for patients with back pain.  运动疗法通常用于治疗背痛患者。|近义词：physiotherapy, physical therapy, kinesitherapy|反义词:|noun
