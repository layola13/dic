nineteenth:/ˌnaɪnˈtiːnθ/ | adj. n.| 第十九的；十九分之一 |The nineteenth century witnessed significant industrial growth. 十九世纪见证了显著的工业增长。| The nineteenth of May is my birthday. 五月十九日是我的生日。 | Synonyms: 19th | Antonyms: first | adjective, noun

Etymology: From Old English *nīontiēnða (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *newuntēgandō, from *newun "nine" + *-tēgandō, ordinal suffix.
