nondenominational:/ˌnɑːndɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
非教派的；不属于任何宗派的
Not affiliated with or restricted to any particular religious denomination.
The couple opted for a nondenominational wedding ceremony. 这对夫妇选择举行一个非教派的婚礼。
近义词:interfaith, ecumenical, universal
反义词:denominational, sectarian
词性:adjective
