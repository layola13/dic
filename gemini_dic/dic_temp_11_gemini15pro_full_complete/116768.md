mausoleums:/ˌmɔːsəˈliːəmz/
n.
陵墓，陵寝
A large stately tomb or building housing tombs, especially of a family or other group.
The family's remains were interred in the mausoleum.  这个家族的遗体被安葬在陵墓中。
