misunderstandingly:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændɪŋli/
adv.
误解地，由于误解
in a way that shows or is caused by a failure to understand something correctly.
She misunderstandingly took his silence as a sign of disapproval. 她误解了他的沉默，认为这是不赞成的表示。
近义词: erroneously, mistakenly
反义词: correctly, accurately
词性: 副词

词源: misunderstanding (n.) + -ly (adv.)
misunderstanding 来自 mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly") + understanding (n.)
understanding 来自 understand (v.)
understand 来自 under- (prefix meaning "beneath, among") + stand (v.)
