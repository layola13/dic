abominably:/əˈbɒmɪnəbli/| adv.|讨厌地；非常地；极坏地|in a way that causes disgust or hatred; extremely badly|The weather was abominably cold. 天气非常冷。|近义词：horribly, terribly, awfully, dreadfully, appallingly|反义词：wonderfully, delightfully, excellently|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *abōminābilis, from Latin abōminārī (“to deprecate as ominous, to shudder at, to abominate”), from ab- (“away from”) + ōmen (“omen, foreboding”).
