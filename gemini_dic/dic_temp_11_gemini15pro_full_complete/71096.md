fish-slice: /ˈfɪʃ slaɪs/ | n. | 鱼铲，鱼刀 | A flat, slotted utensil used for serving and turning fish. It is designed to slide easily under delicate fish fillets without breaking them. |  She used a fish-slice to lift the salmon from the pan. 她用鱼铲把鲑鱼从锅里铲出来。

She carefully transferred the cooked fish to a serving platter with a fish-slice. 她小心地用鱼铲把煮熟的鱼放到一个餐盘里。


| 近义词: spatula, turner | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
