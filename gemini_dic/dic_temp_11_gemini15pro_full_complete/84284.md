half-tone's:/ˌhɑːfˈtoʊnz/ | n. | 半色调的 |  Relating to or using a halftone printing process, which simulates continuous tone imagery through the use of dots, varying either in size or in spacing. | The newspaper used a half-tone's image of the president. 这家报纸使用了总统的半色调图像。 | synonyms: screened, mezzotint | antonyms: continuous tone | adjective
half-tone:/ˌhɑːfˈtoʊn/ | n. | 半色调 | An image produced by a halftone process.  | The half-tone in the old photograph gave it a grainy appearance. 老照片中的半色调使其呈现出颗粒状外观。| synonyms: screened image, mezzotint | antonyms: continuous tone image | noun
