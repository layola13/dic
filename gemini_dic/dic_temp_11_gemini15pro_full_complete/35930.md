coculturing:/koʊˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 共培养，共同培养 | The process of growing two or more different types of cells together in the same environment. |  Example 1: Coculturing of different bacterial strains can reveal synergistic or antagonistic interactions. 不同细菌菌株的共培养可以揭示协同或拮抗作用。

Example 2:  Coculturing cancer cells with immune cells is a valuable research tool.  癌细胞与免疫细胞的共培养是一个有价值的研究工具。 | synonyms: co-cultivation | antonyms: monoculture | noun
