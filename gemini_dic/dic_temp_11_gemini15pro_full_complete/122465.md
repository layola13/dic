mobilizers:/ˈmoʊbɪˌlaɪzərz/ | n. |动员者；动员力；调动者|People or things that mobilize something or someone.|例句：The community mobilizers encouraged people to vote. 社区动员者鼓励人们投票。

例句：New technologies can be powerful mobilizers of social change. 新技术可以成为社会变革的强大推动力。|近义词：activists, campaigners, organizers|反义词：demobilizers, pacifiers|词性:noun (plural)

mobilize:/ˈmoʊbɪˌlaɪz/ | v. |动员；调动；集合|To organize and prepare something or someone for action.|例句：The government mobilized the army for war. 政府动员军队准备战争。

例句：They are mobilizing resources to help the victims of the hurricane. 他们正在调动资源来帮助飓风的受害者。|近义词：assemble, rally, marshal|反义词：demobilize, disband|词性:verb
