abjection:/əbˈdʒɛkʃən/| n.|卑贱，屈辱，堕落|The state of being extremely unhappy, weak, or helpless.|He lived in a state of abjection. 他生活在屈辱之中。|近义词：degradation, humiliation, shame, debasement|反义词:dignity, honor, pride|noun

abject:/ˈæbdʒɛkt/| adj.|卑贱的，凄惨的，绝望的|Experiencing or showing a complete lack of self-respect.|They live in abject poverty. 他们生活在赤贫之中。|近义词：wretched, miserable, pathetic, degraded|反义词:noble, dignified, proud|adjective
