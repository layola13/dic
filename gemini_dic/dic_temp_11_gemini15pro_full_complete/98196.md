insectan:/ɪnˈsɛktən/| adj. n.|昆虫的，昆虫；|Relating to or characteristic of insects; an insect.|例句：Insectan wings are fascinating structures. 昆虫的翅膀是迷人的结构。|例句：The museum has a vast collection of insectans.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的昆虫。|近义词：insect, bug|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑vertebrate脊椎动物)|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *īnsectānus, from Latin īnsectum (“insect”),  from īnsectāre (“to cut into, to divide”), from in- + sectāre, frequentative of secāre (“to cut”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sek- (“to cut”).
