dissolvable:/dɪˈzɒlvəbl/ (US) /dɪˈsɒlvəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可溶解的；可分解的|Able to be dissolved, typically in a liquid.|The tablets are dissolvable in water. 这些药片可溶于水。|The dissolvable stitches will disappear on their own. 这些可吸收的缝线会自行消失。|近义词：soluble, meltable, liquefiable|反义词：insoluble, indissoluble|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Middle English *dissolvabel, from Old French dissolvable, from Latin dissolubilis, from dissolvere (to dissolve).
