hadron:/ˈhædrɒn/ (UK) /ˈhædrɑːn/ (US)| n. |强子|A subatomic particle, such as a proton or neutron, that is made up of quarks and is subject to the strong force.  It's one of two categories of particles governed by the strong interaction (the other category being mesons).|Protons and neutrons are hadrons. 质子和中子是强子。|The Large Hadron Collider smashes hadrons together at high speeds. 大型强子对撞机以高速将强子撞击在一起。|Near synonyms: baryon, meson (though these are *types* of hadrons)|Antonym: lepton (a fundamental particle *not* subject to the strong force)|noun
