chronogrammatical:/ˌkrɒnəɡrəˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. | 按时间顺序排列的；年代学的 | Relating to or involving the dating of texts or events by the words contained in them, or to the use of dates expressed in this way. | The inscription on the tombstone was chronogrammatical, concealing the year of death within a coded phrase. 墓碑上的铭文是按时间顺序排列的，在一个编码短语中隐藏了死亡年份。|  This chronogrammatical poem cleverly reveals the date of its composition. 这首年代学诗巧妙地揭示了它的创作日期。| 近义词: chronological, dated | 反义词: undated, anachronic | 词性: adjective
