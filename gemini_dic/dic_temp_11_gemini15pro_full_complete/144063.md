outrode:/ˌaʊtˈroʊd/
verb
超越，胜过（骑马或骑自行车）
to ride faster or further than someone else
例句：He easily outrode his companions on the steep climb. 他在陡峭的爬坡中轻松地超过了他的同伴。

例句：She outrode the storm and reached shelter before nightfall. 她顶着暴风雨骑行，在天黑前到达了避难所。

近义词：outstrip, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag behind, trail

词性：v.

词源：out- + ride.  "Out-" prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding.
