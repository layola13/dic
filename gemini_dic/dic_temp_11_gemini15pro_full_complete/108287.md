laurasia:/lɔːˈreɪʒə/ | n. | 劳亚古陆 | A large, ancient continent of the Northern Hemisphere, comprising North America, Europe, and Asia (except peninsular India).  It existed from the late Paleozoic Era to the early Mesozoic Era. |  Laurasia eventually broke up into North America and Eurasia. 劳亚古陆最终分裂成北美和欧亚大陆。 |  The geological formations of Laurasia and Gondwana show many similarities.  劳亚古陆和冈瓦纳大陆的地质构造显示出许多相似之处。 | 近义词: Pangaea (as a related concept) | 反义词: Gondwana | 词性: noun
