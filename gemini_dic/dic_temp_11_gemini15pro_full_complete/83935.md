half-clear:/ˌhɑːfˈklɪr/ | adj. | 半晴朗的；部分清晰的；不太清楚的 | Partially clear; somewhat clear but not completely; not fully understood or explained. | The sky was half-clear, with some clouds obscuring the sun. 天空半晴朗，一些云遮住了太阳。| The instructions were half-clear, leaving me with some questions. 说明不太清楚，我還有一些疑问。 | 近义词: partly clear, hazy, slightly cloudy, vague | 反义词: overcast, cloudy, completely clear, distinct | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half" + "clear". "Half" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "one of two equal parts." "Clear" comes from Old French "cler," ultimately from Latin "clarus," meaning "bright, clear."
