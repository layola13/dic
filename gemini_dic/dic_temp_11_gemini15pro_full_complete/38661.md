concussing:/kənˈkʌsɪŋ/| v.|震荡，使脑震荡|The blow concussed him, leaving him dazed and confused. 这一击使他脑震荡，让他感到头昏眼花。|例句：The force of the impact was concussing. 撞击的力量造成了脑震荡。

The repeated blows were concussing the boxer.  反复的打击使拳击手脑震荡。 |近义词：shaking, jarring, jolting|反义词:stabilizing, calming|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *concuss-, past participle stem of concutere "to shake violently," from com- "together" (see com-) + quatere "to shake"
