moderator:/ˈmɑːdəreɪtər/ (US) /ˈmɒdəreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|主持人，调解人，版主|A person who presides over a discussion or meeting, keeping order and ensuring that all participants have a chance to speak.  A person who monitors content on a website or online forum, ensuring that it adheres to community guidelines.|The moderator kept the debate focused on the key issues.  主持人将辩论的重点放在了关键问题上。|The online forum moderator deleted several offensive posts.  在线论坛版主删除了几条攻击性帖子。|synonyms: chairperson, facilitator, adjudicator, arbiter, mediator, controller, administrator|antonyms: participant, instigator|noun
