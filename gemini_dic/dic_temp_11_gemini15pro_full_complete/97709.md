inimicous: /ɪˈnɪmɪkəs/
| adj. |
|敌意的，不友好的，有害的|
|Showing or feeling hostility; unfriendly.  Harmful or unfavorable.|
|The inimicous atmosphere in the meeting made it difficult to reach an agreement.  会议上充满敌意的气氛使得很难达成协议。|
|The desert climate is inimicous to most plants. 沙漠气候对大多数植物有害。|
|近义词：hostile, unfriendly, antagonistic, adverse, harmful|
|反义词：friendly, amicable, beneficial, favorable|
|词性：adjective|

inimicous: /ɪˈnɪmɪkəs/
|Etymology: From Latin *inimicus*, from *in-* (not) + *amicus* (friend).|
