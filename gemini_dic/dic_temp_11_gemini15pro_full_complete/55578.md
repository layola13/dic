disturbs:/dɪˈstɜːbz/| v. |打扰，扰乱，使不安|To interrupt the quiet, peace, or order of; to worry or make anxious.|The loud noise disturbed my sleep. 吵闹的声音打扰了我的睡眠。|Synonyms: bother, interrupt, upset, agitate|Antonyms: soothe, calm, comfort|verb

disturbing:/dɪˈstɜːbɪŋ/| adj. |令人不安的，扰乱的|Causing worry or anxiety.|The news was deeply disturbing.  这个消息令人深感不安。|Synonyms: troubling, upsetting, worrying|Antonyms: comforting, reassuring, calming|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *destorber, from Latin disturbare "throw into disorder," from dis- "apart, asunder" + turbare "to disturb, confuse," from turba "turmoil, crowd, throng."
