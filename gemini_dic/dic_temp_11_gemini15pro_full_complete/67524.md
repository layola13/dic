extracurricular:/ˌekstrəkəˈrɪkjələr/| adj.|课外的，课程以外的|Outside the regular curriculum of school or college.|例句：She participates in many extracurricular activities, such as playing the violin and volunteering at the local hospital. 她参加许多课外活动，例如拉小提琴和在当地医院做志愿者。|例句：Extracurricular activities are important for a well-rounded education. 课外活动对于全面教育很重要。|近义词：nonscholastic, outside, additional|反义词：curricular, academic|词性:形容词
词源:  From extra- + curricular. "Extra-" means outside or beyond, and "curricular" refers to the curriculum, or the courses offered by a school or college.
