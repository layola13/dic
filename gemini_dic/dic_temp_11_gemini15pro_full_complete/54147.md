disengaging:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
脱离，解脱，退出
releasing from an engagement or connection; becoming free from something
The soldiers are disengaging from the battle. 士兵们正在脱离战斗。
The company is disengaging from the failing project.  这家公司正从失败的项目中退出。
disconnecting, withdrawing, detaching
engaging, attaching, connecting
v.
