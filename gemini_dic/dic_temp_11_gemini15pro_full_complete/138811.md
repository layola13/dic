numskull:/ˈnʌmskʌl/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A foolish or stupid person.|例句：He's such a numskull, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be a numskull, think before you act! 别犯傻了，三思而后行！|近义词：blockhead, dunce, idiot, moron|反义词:genius, intellectual|名词
