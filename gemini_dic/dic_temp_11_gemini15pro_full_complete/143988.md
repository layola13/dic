outranted: /ˌaʊtˈræn.tɪd/
verb
胜过，以叫喊压倒
outtalk or outshout someone; defeat someone by ranting.
He outranted his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以叫喊压倒了他的对手。
Synonyms: outshouted, outvociferated, drowned out
Antonyms:  be quiet, listen
词性: 动词
