nonmeasurablenesses:/ˌnɒnˈmɛʒərəbəlnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɛʒərəbəlnəsɪz/| n. |不可测性；不可衡量性|The quality or state of being impossible to measure.  The concept deals with things that are difficult or impossible to quantify or assess using standard metrics.|The nonmeasurablenesses of human emotions make them difficult to study scientifically. 人类情感的不可测性使得它们难以用科学方法研究。|Near synonyms: immeasurability, imponderability, indefinability|Antonyms: measurability, quantifiability|Noun
