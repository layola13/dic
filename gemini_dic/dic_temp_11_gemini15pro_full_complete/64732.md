eschewment:/ɪsˈtʃuːmənt/
n.
戒绝，避免
the act of deliberately avoiding or abstaining from something.
He advocated the complete eschewment of violence. 他主张彻底戒绝暴力。
近义词：avoidance, abstinence, forbearance, shunning
反义词：indulgence, embrace, acceptance
词性：名词

Etymology:
From eschew + -ment.  Eschew comes from Old French *eschiver, from Frankish *skiuhan "to frighten, shy away," from Proto-Germanic *skeukhwōną "to be startled, shy away."
