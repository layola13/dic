cross-action:/ˈkrɒs ˌækʃən/ (US) /ˈkrɒs ˌækʃn/ (UK)| n.| 交叉动作，交错动作，（尤指电影中）交叉剪辑| An editing technique used in films to establish action occurring at the same time in two different locations.  It intercuts between two or more scenes to create suspense or excitement.| The director used cross-action to show the police chasing the robber while the hostages were trying to escape. 导演运用交叉剪辑同时展现了警察追捕劫匪和人质试图逃跑的场景。

The film's cross-action sequences were masterfully edited.  这部电影的交叉剪辑片段剪辑得非常出色。|近义词：intercutting, parallel editing|反义词: linear editing|词性:noun
