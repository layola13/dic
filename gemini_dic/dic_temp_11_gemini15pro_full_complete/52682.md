dignified:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪd/| adj.|有尊严的；高贵的；庄重的|Having or showing a composed or serious manner that is worthy of respect.|She maintained a dignified silence throughout the ordeal. 她自始至终都保持着庄重的沉默。|近义词：stately, noble, majestic, august, imposing, grand|反义词：undignified, common, low, base, vulgar|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *dignified*, past participle of *dignify* ("to give dignity to"), from Latin *dignificare*, from *dignus* ("worthy") + *-ficare* (suffix forming verbs).
