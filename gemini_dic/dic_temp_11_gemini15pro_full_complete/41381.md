cormophylogeny:/ˌkɔːrmɒfaɪˈlɒdʒəni/ | n. | 茎叶植物系统发育学 | The study of the evolutionary relationships among cormophytes (vascular plants, i.e., plants with stems and leaves).  |  Cormophylogeny helps us understand the diversification of land plants. 茎叶植物系统发育学帮助我们了解陆地植物的多样化。| 近义词: plant phylogeny | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *kormos* (trunk, stem) + *phylon* (tribe, race) + *-geny* (origin, development)
