backlog:/ˈbæk.lɒɡ/ (US) /ˈbæk.lɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|积压的工作，积压的订单|An accumulation of something, especially uncompleted work or matters needing to be dealt with.|The company has a huge backlog of orders.|近义词：accumulation, arrears, buildup|反义词：surplus, excess|noun
