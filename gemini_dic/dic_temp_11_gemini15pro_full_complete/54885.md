displosion:/dɪˈsploʊʒən/| n. |内爆；向内爆炸；剧烈收缩；崩溃|An implosion is a sudden collapse inwards.  内爆是一种突然向内的坍缩。|The demolition of the building was achieved by a controlled displosion. 这栋建筑的拆除是通过可控内爆实现的。|近义词：collapse, implosion, cave-in|反义词:explosion, burst, eruption|词性:名词

词源:  From dis- "apart, away" + explosion.  由dis-（分开，离开）+ explosion（爆炸）组成。
