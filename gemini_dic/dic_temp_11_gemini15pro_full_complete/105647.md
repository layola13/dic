kinger:/ˈkɪŋər/| n.|小孩，尤指男孩；年轻人|A child, especially a young boy; a youngster|He's a bright kinger, always asking questions. 他是个聪明的小伙子，总是问问题。|近义词：child, youngster, kid, lad|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *kinger, a derivative of *king (meaning "child, offspring") related to Old Norse *kyn ("family, kin").  The word is primarily used in dialects, especially in northern England and Scotland.
