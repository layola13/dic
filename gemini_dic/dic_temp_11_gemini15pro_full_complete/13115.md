astrut:/əˈstrʌt/| adj.|趾高气扬的，昂首阔步的|Walking with a proud, arrogant gait, often with the chest and head held high.|He strutted around the office like he owned the place. 他在办公室里昂首阔步地走来走去，好像他是老板一样。|Synonyms: swagger, prance, parade|Antonyms: slouch, cower|Adjective
