adenophorous:/ˌædɪˈnɒfərəs/
adjective
腺体的；有腺的
Bearing glands.  Specifically, in botany, bearing glands that secrete nectar.
例句：The adenophorous plant attracted many pollinating insects. 这种有腺的植物吸引了许多授粉昆虫。

例句：Adenophorous tissues are essential for the plant's reproductive cycle. 腺体组织对植物的繁殖周期至关重要。

近义词：glandular, glanduliferous
反义词：eglandular (without glands)
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *adēn* (gland) + *-phoros* (bearing).
