friz:/frɪz/| v. n.|卷曲，使卷曲，毛边，卷边|to make or become tightly curled, or form a tight curl; a frayed edge or curl.|Her hair frizzled in the humidity. 她的头发在潮湿的空气中卷曲了。 The fabric started to friz at the edges. 布料的边缘开始卷曲了。|近义词：curl, crimp, crinkle|反义词：straighten, smooth|词性：动词，名词

词源:  From Old French *frizer, from Frankish *frisōn.  Cognate with Old High German frisōn (“to curl”), Icelandic frysa (“to freeze”).  More at freeze.
