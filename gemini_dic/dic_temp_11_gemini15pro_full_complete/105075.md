kerchiefed:/ˈkɜːrtʃiːft/ /ˈkɜːrtʃiːfd/
adjective
戴着头巾的；用头巾包住的
Wearing or covered with a kerchief.
The kerchiefed woman smiled warmly. 戴着头巾的女人热情地微笑着。
The old woman, kerchiefed and aproned, bustled about the kitchen.  戴着头巾、系着围裙的老妇人在厨房里忙来忙去。
近义词: headscarfed, veiled
反义词: bareheaded, uncovered
词性: adjective

词源:  From kerchief + -ed.  Kerchief itself comes from Old North French couvrechief (covering for the head), from covrir (to cover) and chief (head).
