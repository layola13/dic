maximises:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
最大化
Makes as large or great as possible.
The company maximises profits by keeping costs low. 公司通过降低成本来最大化利润。
Synonyms: optimize, boost, amplify, enhance, increase, augment
Antonyms: minimize, reduce, decrease, diminish, lessen
词性: 动词
