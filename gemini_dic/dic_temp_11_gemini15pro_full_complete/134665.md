nonmanual:/ˌnɒnˈmænjʊəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈmænjuəl/| adj.|非手动的；非体力的|Relating to communication that does not involve the use of hands, such as facial expressions or body language.|Sign language uses both manual and nonmanual signals.  手语同时使用手动和非手动信号。|Nonmanual markers like raised eyebrows can change the meaning of a signed sentence. 像扬眉这样的非手动标记可以改变手语句子的含义。|近义词:nonverbal|反义词:manual|词性:形容词

nonmanual:/ˌnɒnˈmænjʊəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈmænjuəl/| n.|非体力劳动者|A person who does work that does not involve manual labor.|Many nonmanuals work in offices. 许多非体力劳动者在办公室工作。|The company is hiring more nonmanuals for its marketing department.  该公司正在为其营销部门招聘更多非体力劳动者。|近义词:white-collar worker|反义词:manual worker, blue-collar worker|词性:名词
