dipping:/ˈdɪpɪŋ/ | n. adj. | 浸渍，蘸酱; 下沉的 | the act of immersing something briefly into a liquid. | She enjoys dipping her fries in ketchup. 她喜欢把薯条蘸番茄酱。 |  近义词：immersing, submerging, sinking | 反义词：rising, ascending | n.
dipping:/ˈdɪpɪŋ/ | adj. | 下沉的 | sloping downwards. | The dipping sun cast long shadows.  落日投下了长长的影子。 | 近义词：descending, declining | 反义词：rising, ascending | adj.
