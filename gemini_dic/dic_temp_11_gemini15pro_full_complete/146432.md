overforward:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrwərd/| adj.|过度向前；过于积极| excessively forward; too eager or aggressive |  He was a bit overforward in his approach and it made her uncomfortable. 他在接近她的方式上过于急切，让她感到不舒服。| The overforward salesman annoyed the customers with his pushy tactics.  过于积极的推销员用他咄咄逼人的策略惹恼了顾客。|近义词：pushy, aggressive, forward, presumptuous|反义词：reserved, reticent, shy, timid|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "forward" (adjective meaning "bold, presumptuous, or advanced in position")
