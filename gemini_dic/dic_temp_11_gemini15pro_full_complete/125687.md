mandatory:/ˈmændətɔːri/ (US), /ˈmændətəri/ (UK)| adj.|强制性的，义务的|Required by law or rules; compulsory.|例句：Attendance at these meetings is mandatory. 参加这些会议是强制性的。|近义词：compulsory, obligatory, required|反义词：optional, voluntary|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *mandatorius, from Latin mandatum (“mandate”).
