heterochromic:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒmɪk/ | adj. | 异色的，杂色的 | Having different colors, especially of the irises of the eyes. |  Example 1: The cat was heterochromic, with one blue eye and one green eye. 这只猫异色瞳，一只眼睛是蓝色的，另一只眼睛是绿色的。

Example 2: Heterochromic iridium is a fascinating phenomenon. 异色虹膜是一种迷人的现象。 | Near synonyms: varicolored, multicolored, polychromatic | Antonym: homochromic | Adjective
