condensible:/kənˈdensəbl/ | adj. | 可冷凝的，可凝结的 | Capable of being condensed; able to be changed from a gas or vapor to a liquid. |  The steam is condensible into water.  蒸汽可以凝结成水。| synonyms: condensable, liquefiable | antonyms: non-condensible, non-liquefiable | adjective

condensible:/kənˈdensəbl/ | adj. | 可浓缩的 |  Capable of being made denser or more concentrated. | The syrup is condensible by evaporation.  糖浆可以通过蒸发来浓缩。| synonyms:  concentratable, reducible | antonyms:  non-reducible, non-concentratable | adjective
