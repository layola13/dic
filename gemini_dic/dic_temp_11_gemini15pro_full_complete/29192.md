cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ | n. |收银员| a person who collects payments in a shop, bank, etc.|  The cashier scanned my groceries. 收银员扫描了我的杂货。| synonyms: teller, clerk | antonyms: customer, client | noun

cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ | vt. |解雇，开除（尤指军官）| to dismiss from a position of responsibility or trust, especially in the military | The captain was cashiered for disobeying orders. 船长因不服从命令而被解雇。| synonyms: dismiss, discharge, sack | antonyms: employ, hire, promote | verb
