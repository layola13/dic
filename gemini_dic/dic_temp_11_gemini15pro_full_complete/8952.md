antihumanitarian:/ˌæntiˌhjuːmænɪˈtɛəriən/
adj.|反人道的，非人道的|Opposed to humanitarian principles; inhumane; cruel and brutal.|The antihumanitarian treatment of prisoners of war was condemned by international organizations.  战俘的反人道待遇受到国际组织的谴责。| 近义词: inhumane, cruel, barbaric, brutal, callous, merciless|反义词: humanitarian, humane, compassionate, merciful|词性: 形容词

antihumanitarian:/ˌæntiˌhjuːmænɪˈtɛəriən/
n.|反人道主义者|A person who opposes humanitarian principles.|The antihumanitarian was shunned by society for his cruel beliefs.  这个反人道主义者因其残忍的信仰而被社会所唾弃。|近义词: brute, savage, barbarian|反义词: humanitarian, philanthropist|词性: 名词
