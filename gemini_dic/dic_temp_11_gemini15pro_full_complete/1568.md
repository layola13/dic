aching:/ˈeɪkɪŋ/| n.|疼痛|A continuous or prolonged dull pain.|例句：The aching in my muscles wouldn't go away. 我肌肉的疼痛一直不消失。|近义词：pain, soreness|反义词：comfort, relief|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *acan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch aken and German achen.
