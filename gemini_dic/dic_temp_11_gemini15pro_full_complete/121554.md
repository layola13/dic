mismeshed:/mɪsˈmɛʃt/
adjective
啮合不良的，不协调的
not meshing or fitting together properly; out of sync or harmony.
The gears were mismeshed, causing a grinding noise. 齿轮啮合不良，发出刺耳的噪音。
misaligned, out of sync, incompatible, discordant
harmonious, compatible, synchronized, aligned
词性:形容词

mismeshed:/mɪsˈmɛʃt/
verb (past participle)
啮合不良
having failed to mesh or fit together properly.
Their personalities were mismeshed from the start, leading to constant arguments.  他们的性格从一开始就不合，导致不断争吵。
misaligned, out of sync, incompatible, discordant
harmonious, compatible, synchronized, aligned
词性:动词(过去分词)
