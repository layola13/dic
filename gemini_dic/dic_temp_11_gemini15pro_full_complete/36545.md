collagenase: /kəˈlɑːdʒɪneɪs/
n.
胶原酶
An enzyme that breaks down collagen.
Collagenase is used in some medical treatments to break down scar tissue.  胶原酶在一些医学治疗中用于分解疤痕组织。
Collagenase ointments can help with wound healing. 胶原酶药膏可以帮助伤口愈合。
近义词: protease, enzyme
反义词: collagenase inhibitor
词性: noun
