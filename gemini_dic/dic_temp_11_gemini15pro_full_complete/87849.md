hideboundness:/ˈhaɪdˌbaʊndnəs/
n.
狭隘；保守；顽固；墨守成规
The state of being hidebound; narrow-mindedness or unwillingness to change due to being excessively bound by tradition or prejudice.
His hideboundness prevented him from accepting new ideas. 他的保守使他无法接受新思想。
近义词：conservatism, inflexibility, rigidity, dogmatism, narrow-mindedness
反义词：open-mindedness, flexibility, progressiveness
词性：名词

hidebound:/ˈhaɪdˌbaʊnd/
adj.
顽固的；保守的；墨守成规的
Unwilling or unable to change because of tradition or convention.
They are hidebound by outdated rules and regulations. 他们被过时的规章制度束缚住了。
近义词：conservative, inflexible, rigid, dogmatic, narrow-minded
反义词：open-minded, flexible, progressive
词性：形容词

词源:
From "hidebound" + "-ness."  "Hidebound" literally means having a hide (skin) that is bound tightly, making movement difficult.  Figuratively, it refers to being restricted by tradition or prejudice.
