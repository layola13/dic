nonrectified:/ˌnɒnˈrɛktɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未精馏的，未整流的，未校正的 | Not rectified; not having been corrected or adjusted.  Specifically refers to alternating current that has not been converted to direct current. | The nonrectified AC signal caused the motor to vibrate. 未精馏的交流信号导致电机振动。| The nonrectified image showed distortions. 未校正的图像显示出失真。 | synonyms: uncorrected, unadjusted, raw, crude | antonyms: rectified, corrected, adjusted, refined | adjective
