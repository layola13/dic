dustbins:/ˈdʌstbɪnz/ (UK) /ˈdʌstbɪnz/ (US)| n. |垃圾桶；垃圾箱|A large container, typically made of metal or plastic, used for temporarily storing refuse and waste.|例句：The dustbins are overflowing. 垃圾桶都满了。

例句：Empty the dustbins regularly to avoid unpleasant smells.  定期清空垃圾桶以避免难闻的气味。
