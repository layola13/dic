degrader:/dɪˈɡreɪdər/| n.|降解剂，降解者，降解物|a substance or organism that degrades something, especially a pollutant|The researchers are developing a new degrader for plastic waste. 研究人员正在开发一种新的塑料垃圾降解剂。|近义词：decomposer, breaker-down|反义词:builder, constructor|noun

degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/| v.|降低，贬低，使降解|to reduce in rank, status, or quality; to decompose or break down (a substance)|Exposure to sunlight can degrade the quality of the paint. 阳光照射会降低油漆的质量。  The enzyme degrades the protein into smaller peptides. 这种酶将蛋白质降解成更小的肽。|近义词：deteriorate, worsen, corrupt, decompose|反义词:improve, enhance, elevate|verb
