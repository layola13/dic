jary:/ˈdʒeɪri/ | n. | 罐子；坛子；广口瓶 | A cylindrical or globular container with a wide mouth, typically made of glass or earthenware and used for storing food or other substances. |  She kept her homemade jam in a jary. 她把自制的果酱放在一个坛子里。


The jary was filled with pickled onions.  这个坛子里装满了腌洋葱。


近义词: jar, crock, pot | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *jari, from Anglo-Norman jare, Old French jarre (“jar”), of uncertain origin. Possibly from Arabic جَرَّة‎ (jarra, “jar, pitcher”), or perhaps ultimately from a pre-Roman substrate language.
