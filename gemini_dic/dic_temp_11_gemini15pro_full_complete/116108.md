marv:/mɑːrv/| v. |惊叹，感到惊奇；感到高兴|To be filled with wonder or astonishment; to rejoice greatly.  (Often used in a humorous or informal way.)|例句：I marveled at her skill with the violin. 我对她的演奏技巧感到惊叹。

例句：He marveled at the beauty of the sunset. 他对日落的美丽感到惊叹。|近义词：wonder, marvel, admire, be amazed, be astonished|反义词：disregard, ignore|词性：动词
