contributable:/kənˈtrɪbjətəbl/ | adj. | 可归因的，可归功于的 |  Capable of being attributed to a specific cause or source.  | The increase in sales is largely contributable to the new marketing campaign. 销售额的增长很大程度上归功于新的营销活动。 | Synonyms: attributable, ascribable, imputable | Antonyms: unattributable, non-contributory | adjective

contributable:/kənˈtrɪbjətəbl/ | adj. | 捐献的 |  Able to be contributed or donated. (Less common usage) |  The funds are contributable to the charity.  这些资金可以捐给慈善机构。| Synonyms: donatable, giftable | Antonyms: non-donatable | adjective
