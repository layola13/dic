diathermous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːməs/| adj.|透热的，易传热的|Allowing heat to pass through; readily permeable to radiant heat.  A diathermous wall allows heat to pass through it.|The scientist used a diathermous container for the experiment, ensuring efficient heat transfer.  科学家使用了一个透热的容器进行实验，以确保有效的热传递。|Synonyms:  heat-transmitting, thermally conductive|Antonyms:  adiabatic, heat-insulating|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek dia- "through" + thermos "hot, warm."
