dividends:/ˈdɪvɪdendz/
n.|股息；红利；回报；好处|A sum of money paid regularly (typically quarterly) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves).|The company declared a large dividend this year. 今年公司宣布了巨额股息。|synonyms: payout, distribution, return, earnings, gain, profit|antonyms: loss, debt|noun

dividends:/ˈdɪvɪdendz/
n.|股息；红利；回报；好处|Something that is gained as a result of an action or activity.|Investing in education pays rich dividends in the long term.  长期投资教育会带来丰厚的回报。|synonyms: reward, benefit, advantage, payoff|antonyms: disadvantage, drawback|noun
