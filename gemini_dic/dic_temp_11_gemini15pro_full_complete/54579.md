dismaler:/ˈdɪzmələr/ | adj. | 更凄凉的，更阴沉的 | More dismal; gloomier. | The weather became dismaler as the day went on. 天色随着时间的推移变得更加阴沉。| 近义词：gloomier, drearier, more depressing | 反义词：brighter, cheerier, more joyful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Comparative form of dismal. From Middle English *dismal*, from Anglo-Norman *dismal*, from Late Latin *diēs malus* (evil day).
