aphemia:/əˈfiːmiə/| n. |失语症 (一种运动性失语症，患者理解语言但无法说话)|Loss of the power of speech, due to a brain lesion, while the comprehension of language and the ability to write remain unimpaired.|例句：The stroke resulted in aphemia, leaving the patient unable to speak but able to understand everything. 中风导致了失语症，使病人无法说话，但能够理解一切。

例句：Although she suffered from aphemia, she could still communicate through writing. 虽然她患有失语症，但她仍然可以通过写作进行交流。|近义词：motor aphasia, anarthria|反义词: - |词性:noun
