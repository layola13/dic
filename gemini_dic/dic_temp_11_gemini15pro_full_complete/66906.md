expeditions:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃənz/| n. |探险；远征；考察|A journey undertaken by a group of people with a particular purpose, especially that of exploration, research, or war.|例句：The team embarked on a challenging expedition to the South Pole.  这个团队开始了一次具有挑战性的南极探险。

例句：Several expeditions were sent to explore the uncharted territory.  几支探险队被派去探索未知的领域。|近义词：journey, voyage, quest, adventure|反义词:stay, inaction|词性:名词
