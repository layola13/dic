blogroll:/ˈblɒɡrəʊl/ (US) /ˈblɑːɡroʊl/| n. |博客列表，友情链接|A list of links to other blogs, usually displayed on a blog's sidebar, that the blog's author recommends or finds interesting.|Example: I added your blog to my blogroll. 我把你的博客添加到我的博客列表里了。|Example:  Check out the blogroll for some interesting reads.  看看博客列表里的一些有趣的读物。|近义词：link list, blog list|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源：blog + roll.  "Blog" is short for "weblog," and "roll" refers to a list or catalog.  The term originated in the early days of blogging when bloggers would often list links to their favorite blogs on their own sites.
