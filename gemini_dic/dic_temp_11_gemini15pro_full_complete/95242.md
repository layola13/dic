incalescence:/ˌɪnkəˈlɛsəns/| n.|炽热，白热化；温度骤然升高|The incalescence of the metal during forging made it malleable.  锻造过程中金属的白热化使其具有延展性。|例句：The sudden incalescence of the wire indicated a short circuit. 电线突然变热表明发生了短路。|近义词：heating, incandescence|反义词:cooling, refrigeration|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incalescere,  "to grow hot," from in- "in, into" + calescere "to grow warm," from calere "to be warm."
