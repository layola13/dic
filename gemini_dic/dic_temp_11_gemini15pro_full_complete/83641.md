haemolysis:/hiːˈmɒlɪsɪs/ (US) /hiːˈmɑːləsɪs/| n. |溶血|The rupture or destruction of red blood cells.|例句：The venom of some snakes causes haemolysis.  某些蛇的毒液会导致溶血。
