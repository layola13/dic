outfishing:/ˌaʊtˈfɪʃɪŋ/
|v.|过度捕捞；在捕鱼方面胜过|The act of catching more fish than someone else or more fish than is sustainable.  It can also refer to depleting a fish population through excessive fishing.|They are outfishing their competitors. 他们在捕鱼方面胜过竞争对手。|overfishing, depleting|sustainable fishing|verb
