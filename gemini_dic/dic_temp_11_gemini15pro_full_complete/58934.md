duskly:/ˈdʌskli/| adv.|昏暗地；朦胧地；模糊地|In a dim or shadowy way, as if at dusk.  Relating to or resembling dusk.|The room was duskly lit by a single candle. 房间里只有一支蜡烛发出昏暗的光。|Near Antonyms: brightly, brilliantly|Far Antonyms: brightly, clearly|adverb

Etymology->
From dusk + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Dusk itself comes from Old English *dux, related to Dutch duister "dark" and German duster "dim, dusky".
