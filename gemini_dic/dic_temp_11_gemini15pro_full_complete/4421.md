alarmist:/əˈlɑːrmɪst/ | n. adj. | 危言耸听者；危言耸听的 | A person who exaggerates danger and causes unnecessary fear; creating or causing unnecessary fear or anxiety | The news report was criticized for being alarmist and causing undue panic. 这篇新闻报道因危言耸听，造成不必要的恐慌而受到批评。|  近义词：scaremonger, doomsayer, panicker | 反义词：optimist, realist | 词性: 名词, 形容词
