discontenting:/dɪskənˈtɛntɪŋ/
adjective
令人不满的；使人不愉快的
Causing discontent; dissatisfying.
The discontenting news of the factory closure spread quickly through the town. 工厂关闭的令人不满的消息迅速传遍了全镇。
The low salary was discontenting to the employees. 低工资让员工们感到不满。
synonyms:dissatisfying, displeasing, unsatisfactory, frustrating
antonyms:satisfying, pleasing, gratifying, fulfilling
词性:形容词
