incyst:/ɪnˈsɪst/ |v. |包囊，形成囊肿；使…被包在囊内|To enclose or become enclosed in a cyst. |The parasite will incyst in the host's tissues.  寄生虫会在宿主的组织中形成包囊。| The amoeba incysts to protect itself from harsh conditions.  变形虫形成包囊以保护自己免受恶劣环境的影响。 |Synonyms: encapsulate, encyst, enclose |Antonyms: excyst, emerge |verb
