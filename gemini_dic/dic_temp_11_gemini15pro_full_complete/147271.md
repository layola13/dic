overmines:/ˌoʊvərˈmaɪnz/| v. |过度开采，破坏(比喻义：削弱；暗中破坏)|To damage or weaken something, especially gradually or insidiously, often by undermining. |The constant criticism from her boss overmined her confidence.|近义词: undermine, sabotage, weaken, impair|反义词: strengthen, bolster, support|词性:动词

Etymology:  From *over-* + *mine*.  *Over-* here functions as an intensifier, suggesting an excessive or destructive degree of mining.  The verb *mine* itself comes from Old French *miner*, meaning "to dig a mine," ultimately from Late Latin *minare*.
