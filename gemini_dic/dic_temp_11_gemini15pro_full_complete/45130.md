cuda: /ˈkuːdə/ (US) /ˈkjuːdə/ (UK)| n. |  Compute Unified Device Architecture (计算统一设备架构)|A parallel computing platform and programming model developed by Nvidia for general computing on GPUs.|  Nvidia's CUDA platform allows developers to accelerate computationally intensive applications.  英伟达的CUDA平台允许开发者加速计算密集型应用程序。|Synonyms: GPGPU (General-Purpose computing on Graphics Processing Units), GPU computing|Antonyms: CPU computing|Noun
