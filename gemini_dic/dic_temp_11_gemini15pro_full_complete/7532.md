annexationist:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənɪst/
noun
兼并主义者；主张吞并领土者
A person who advocates or supports annexation, especially political annexation of territory.
The annexationist policies of the country led to several wars.  这个国家的兼并主义政策导致了几场战争。
Synonyms: expansionist, imperialist, jingoist
Antonyms: pacifist, anti-imperialist
n.

annexation:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃən/
noun
吞并；附加；合并
The act of annexing something, especially territory.
The annexation of Crimea by Russia caused international outrage. 俄罗斯吞并克里米亚引起了国际社会的愤怒。
Synonyms: seizure, takeover, appropriation
Antonyms: liberation, surrender
n.
