laudanum:/ˈlɔːdənəm/
noun
鸦片酊；鸦片酒
A tincture of opium.  A solution of opium in alcohol, formerly used as a narcotic painkiller.
Laudanum was a common remedy in the 19th century.  鸦片酊在19世纪是一种常见的药物。
