heightener:/ˈhaɪtnər/| n.|增强器；增高剂；加剧者|Something that increases height, intensity, or effect.|The use of contrasting colors is a heightener of visual appeal. 使用对比色可以增强视觉吸引力。|近义词：intensifier, enhancer, amplifier|反义词：reducer, diminisher, weakener|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From heighten + -er.  Heighten itself comes from Middle English heghten, from Old English hēahtan "to make high," from Proto-Germanic *hauhijaną "to make high," from *hauhaz "high."
