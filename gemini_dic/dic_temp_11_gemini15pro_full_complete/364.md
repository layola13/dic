abidingness's:/əˈbaɪdɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
持久性，永恒性
The quality or state of being abiding; lastingness.  This word emphasizes the enduring and unchanging nature of something, particularly feelings or qualities.
His abidingness's to his principles is admirable. 他对原则的坚持令人钦佩。
近义词: permanence, durability, steadfastness, constancy
反义词: transience, ephemerality, instability, fickleness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From abidingness + -'s (possessive suffix).  Abidingness is derived from abiding + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Abiding comes from Middle English abiden, from Old English ābīdan "to remain, continue, wait, endure," from ā- "on, in, at, to" + bīdan "to abide, wait."
