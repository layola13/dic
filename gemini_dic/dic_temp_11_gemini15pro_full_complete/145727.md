ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + credulous + -ness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Credulous" comes from the Latin "credulus," meaning "believing easily," from "credere" meaning "to believe." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
