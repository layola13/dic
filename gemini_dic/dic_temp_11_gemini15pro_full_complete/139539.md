obtruder:/əbˈtruːdər/| n.|闯入者，打扰者，不速之客|Someone who obtrudes, or forces themselves, into a place or situation where they are unwelcome.|The obtruder was quickly escorted out of the private party.  这个不速之客很快被护送出了私人聚会。|Synonyms: intruder, trespasser, interloper|Antonyms: guest, invitee|noun

obtrude:/əbˈtruːd/| v.|打扰，闯入，突出|To force oneself upon others; to impose or thrust forward; to become noticeable in an unwelcome way.|She tried not to obtrude her opinions on others. 她尽量不把自己的意见强加于人。  His large nose obtruded from his face. 他的大鼻子从脸上凸出来。|Synonyms: impose, intrude, interfere|Antonyms: withdraw, recede, retreat|verb
