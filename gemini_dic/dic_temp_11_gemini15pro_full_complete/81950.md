grimiest:/ˈɡrɪmiɪst/ | adj. | 最肮脏的 | Covered with grime; extremely dirty.  Superlative form of 'grimy'. | The grimiest corner of the city was filled with overflowing bins. 城市中最肮脏的角落里堆满了 overflowing 的垃圾桶。| The grimiest alley I've ever seen was behind that restaurant. 我见过的最肮脏的小巷就在那家餐馆后面。 | 近义词: dirtiest, filthiest, nastiest | 反义词: cleanest, purest, tidiest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From grime + -y + -est.  "Grime" comes from Middle English *gryme*, likely of Scandinavian origin.
