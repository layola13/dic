aep:/eɪ iː piː/ |abbr.| 年度执行计划 (Annual Execution Plan)| An abbreviation for Annual Execution Plan. It is a detailed plan that outlines the goals, objectives, and strategies an organization or individual intends to achieve within a year. | The team reviewed the AEP for the next fiscal year. 团队审查了下一财年的年度执行计划。| synonym: annual plan, yearly plan | antonym: N/A | abbreviation
