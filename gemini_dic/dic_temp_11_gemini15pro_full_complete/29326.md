castigative: /ˈkæstɪɡeɪtɪv/
adjective
惩罚的；责骂的；苛评的
Relating to or expressing severe criticism or punishment.
The teacher's castigative remarks made the student feel ashamed. 老师的责备让学生感到羞愧。
The castigative tone of the review discouraged other readers from buying the book. 这篇评论的批判性语气让其他读者打消了买这本书的念头。
近义词: critical, censorious, reproving, rebuking, admonishing
反义词: laudatory, complimentary, praising
词性: adjective

castigate: /ˈkæstɪɡeɪt/
verb
严厉批评；责骂；惩罚
To criticize or reprimand severely.
The coach castigated the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。
The newspaper castigated the government for its handling of the crisis. 报纸严厉批评了政府处理危机的方式。
近义词: reprimand, rebuke, admonish, censure, upbraid
反义词: praise, commend, laud
词性: verb

词源: From Latin castigatus, past participle of castigare (“to correct, chastise”), from castus (“pure”) + agere (“to drive, lead, do”).
