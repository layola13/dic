out-bellows:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊz/
v.
胜过…的吼叫声; 比…声音更大
To shout louder than someone or something else.
The angry foreman out-bellowed the noisy machinery.  愤怒的工头的声音盖过了嘈杂的机器声。
The opera singer out-bellowed the entire orchestra. 歌剧演唱家的声音盖过了整个管弦乐队。
近义词: outshout, drown out
反义词: be drowned out, be outshouted
词性: 动词
