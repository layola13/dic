openoffice:/ˈoʊpənˌɒfɪs/ /ˈoʊpənˌɑːfɪs/ | n. | 开放式办公室；OpenOffice（一款办公软件） | A suite of office productivity applications that includes a word processor, a spreadsheet, a presentation program, and more.  It is an open-source alternative to commercial office suites. | I use OpenOffice to write my documents and create spreadsheets. 我使用OpenOffice来编写文档和创建电子表格。|  OpenOffice is a free alternative to Microsoft Office. OpenOffice是微软Office的免费替代品。 | synonyms: LibreOffice, Apache OpenOffice, FreeOffice | antonyms: Microsoft Office (arguably, as a proprietary, closed-source counterpart) | noun
