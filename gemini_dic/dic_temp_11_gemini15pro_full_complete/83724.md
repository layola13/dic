hahaha:/hɑːˈhɑːˈhɑː/ /həˈhɑːhəˈhɑː/ | interjection | 哈哈/呵呵 (表示笑声) | An interjection used to represent laughter.  It's often used in informal writing, such as text messages or online chats, to express amusement or laughter. The more "ha"s are used, the longer or more intense the laughter is implied to be. | Example: Hahaha, that's a funny joke! 哈哈，那真是个好笑的笑话！| Example:  He told me about his embarrassing moment and I was like, "hahaha!" 他跟我讲了他的尴尬时刻，我就像，“哈哈哈！” | Near Synonyms: lol, lmao, hehe, giggle | Antonyms: (Laughter doesn't have a direct antonym, but words expressing sadness or displeasure could be considered opposites in certain contexts, such as) boo, hiss
词性: interjection
