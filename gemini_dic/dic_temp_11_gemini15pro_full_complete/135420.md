nonperpetuities:/ˌnɒnpərˈpɛtʃuɪtiz/
n.
非永久性资产/负债；非永续性
Financial instruments or obligations that have a limited lifespan, unlike perpetuities which are theoretically forever.
The company's balance sheet listed several nonperpetuities, including short-term loans and bonds with maturity dates.  公司资产负债表列出了几项非永久性资产/负债，包括短期贷款和有到期日的债券。
Synonyms:  temporary investments, finite obligations
Antonyms: perpetuities, permanent investments
词性: 名词
