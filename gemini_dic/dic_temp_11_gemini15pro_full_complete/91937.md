hypermetaphoric:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 超比喻的，极度比喻的 |  Excessively or elaborately metaphoric; using many metaphors, often layered or extended.  It describes language that is rich in metaphors, often to an exaggerated degree. |  Her writing style is hypermetaphoric, making it dense and challenging to read. 她的写作风格非常比喻化，使其内容密集且难以阅读。

The speaker's hypermetaphoric language painted a vivid, though somewhat overwhelming, picture of the situation. 演讲者极度比喻的语言描绘了一幅生动但又有点让人难以承受的画面。 | 近义词:  figurative, allegorical, symbolic, metaphorical | 反义词: literal, plain, direct | 词性: adjective
