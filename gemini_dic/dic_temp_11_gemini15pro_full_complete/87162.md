herborize:/ˈhɜːrbəraɪz/ | v. |  采集草药；研究植物；从事植物学研究 | To collect or study plants, especially medicinal herbs, in their natural habitat. |  She spent the summer herborizing in the mountains. 她夏天在山里采集草药。|  He enjoys herborizing and identifying different species of wildflowers. 他喜欢采集和识别不同种类的野花。 | synonyms: botanize, collect plants, forage | antonyms: cultivate, farm | verb
