ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *capitalisen*, from Medieval Latin *capitalizare*, from Latin *capitalis* "of the head," hence "chief, principal," from *caput* "head." The financial sense emerged in the 17th century, while the sense of using capital letters developed in the 19th century.
