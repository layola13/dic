diachronicness:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪknəs/ |n.|历时性|The quality or state of being diachronic; development through time.|The diachronicness of language is evident in the evolution of word meanings.| 近义词: historicity, temporal development, evolution | 反义词: synchronicity, simultaneity | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From diachronic + -ness.  Diachronic from Greek dia- "through, across" + chronos "time".
