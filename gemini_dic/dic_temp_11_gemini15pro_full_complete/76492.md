galantine:/ˈɡælənˌtiːn/ /ˈɡælənˌtaɪn/| n.|加兰廷，肉冻|A dish consisting of boned stuffed poultry or meat, poached and served cold, coated with aspic.  A galantine is typically made by deboning a chicken or other bird, stuffing it with forcemeat, rolling it up, poaching it, and then chilling it. The resulting dish is then often coated in aspic, a savory jelly.|

例句：
The chef prepared a beautiful galantine of pheasant for the banquet.
主厨为宴会准备了一道精美的野鸡加兰廷。

例句：
The galantine was decorated with elaborate patterns of aspic.
肉冻上装饰着精心制作的肉冻图案。
