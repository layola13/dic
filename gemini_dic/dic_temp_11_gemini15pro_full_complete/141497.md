opisthodome:/ˈɑːpɪsθəˌdoʊm/| n. |后房，后殿|A chamber at the rear of a Greek temple, often used as a treasury.  It's the opposite of the pronaos, which is at the front.|The opisthodome of the Parthenon housed the treasury of the Delian League.|Synonym: rear chamber, back room|Antonym: pronaos, front room|noun
