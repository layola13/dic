arroyos:/əˈrɔɪoʊz/
n.
溪流，小河，干谷
A watercourse in an arid or semiarid region, often dry except after heavy rain.
The desert landscape was crisscrossed by dry arroyos.  沙漠景观中纵横交错着干涸的河床。
gullies, washes, creeks, ravines
rivers, streams
