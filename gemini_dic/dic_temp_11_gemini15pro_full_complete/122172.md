mistuning:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnɪŋ/ | n. | 失谐，失调 |The act or process of tuning incorrectly; the state of being out of tune.  A slight difference in frequency or other characteristic between components of a system, especially when this has undesirable effects. |The mistuning of the engine caused it to vibrate.  发动机的失谐导致它振动。| near synonyms: detuning, discordance, dissonance | near antonyms: tuning, harmony, concordance | noun
