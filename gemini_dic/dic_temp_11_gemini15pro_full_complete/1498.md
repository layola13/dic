acetated:/ˈæsɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
醋酸盐的；醋酸化的
combined with or treated with acetic acid.
The fabric was acetated to improve its colorfastness.  这种织物经过醋酸处理以提高其色牢度。
近义词:acetylated
反义词:None
词性:adjective
