insected:/ɪnˈsɛktɪd/
adjective
昆虫切开的; 充满昆虫的
cut or divided into sections, like an insect's body; infested with insects.
The insected leaf showed clear signs of the larva's path. 这片被昆虫切开的叶子清楚地显示了幼虫的路径。
The old house was insected with termites. 这座老房子里到处都是白蚁。
近义词:segmented, infested
反义词:whole, uninfested
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *insectus*, past participle of *insecare* "to cut into," from *in-* "into" + *secare* "to cut."  The original meaning referred to the segmented bodies of insects.  The meaning "infested with insects" is a later development.
