circularise:/ˈsɜːrkjələraɪz/ /ˈsɜːrkjəleraɪz/| vt.|分发；传阅；使循环|To send something to a large number of people.|The company circularised a memo to all staff. 公司向所有员工传阅了一份备忘录。|distribute, circulate, disseminate| - |verb
circularise:/ˈsɜːrkjələraɪz/ /ˈsɜːrkjəleraɪz/| n.|分发；传阅；使循环|The act of sending something to a large number of people.|The circularisation of the newsletter was completed within a day.  通讯的发送在一天内完成。|distribution, circulation, dissemination| - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "circular" + "-ise". "Circular" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French circulaire or late Latin circularis, from Latin circulus ‘circle’.  "-ise" is a suffix forming verbs, meaning “to make or render”.
