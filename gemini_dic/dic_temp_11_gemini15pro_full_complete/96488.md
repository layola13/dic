indivisibleness:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbəlnəs/| n.|不可分割性|The quality or state of being indivisible; inability to be divided or separated.|The indivisibleness of the nation is crucial for its stability.  国家的不可分割性对其稳定至关重要。|Synonyms: unity, wholeness, integrity, inseparability|Antonyms: divisibility, separability|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + divisible + -ness.  Divisible comes from Late Latin divisibilis, from Latin dividere (to divide).
