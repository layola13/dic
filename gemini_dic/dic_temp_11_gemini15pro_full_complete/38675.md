condemner:/kənˈdemər/| n.|谴责者，责难者|A person who condemns someone or something.|The condemner of the policy faced strong opposition.  这个政策的谴责者面临强烈的反对。|Synonyms: critic, censurer, denouncer|Antonyms: supporter, advocate, defender|noun

condemn:/kənˈdem/| vt.|谴责，指责；宣告…不适用，定…罪|To express strong disapproval of; to pronounce judgment against.|The judge condemned the thief to five years in prison.  法官判处小偷五年徒刑。|Synonyms: denounce, censure, criticize|Antonyms: praise, commend, approve|verb
