outerness:/ˈaʊtənəs/
n.
外在性，表面性
The outerness of the building was impressive, but the interior was disappointing.
这栋建筑的外观令人印象深刻，但内部却令人失望。
The artist focused on the outerness of the subject, neglecting its inner meaning.
这位艺术家专注于主题的外在表现，而忽略了其内在含义。
exteriority, superficiality
interiority, essence
