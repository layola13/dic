词源:  From Middle English *develment, *deuilment, from Anglo-Norman develment, develement, from develer, deuler (“to hurt, injure, harm”).  Ultimately from Latin diabolus (“devil”).  The sense shifted from "harm, injury" to "mischief, playful troublemaking."
