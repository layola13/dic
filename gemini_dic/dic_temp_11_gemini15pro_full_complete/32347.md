childishly:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃli/| adv.|幼稚地；孩子气地|In a childish manner; like a child.  Characterized by immaturity or lack of seriousness.|He giggled childishly when he was tickled.  他被挠痒痒时孩子气地咯咯笑。|Synonyms: immaturely, naively, foolishly, playfully, simply|Antonyms: maturely, responsibly, seriously, soberly|Word type: adverb

childish:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的；孩子气的|Having or showing the qualities of a child; immature.|His childish behavior embarrassed his parents.  他幼稚的行为让他的父母很尴尬。|Synonyms: immature, naive, silly, infantile, puerile|Antonyms: mature, grown-up, adult, sophisticated, responsible|Word type: adjective
