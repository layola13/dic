disoblige:/dɪsəˈblaɪdʒ/
verb
怠慢，使不快
to offend or annoy someone, especially by not doing what they have asked you to do
例句：I wouldn't want to disoblige him. 我不想得罪他。

例句：She was unwilling to disoblige her aunt. 她不愿惹她姑妈不高兴。

近义词：offend, annoy, upset, displease, irritate, vex
反义词：please, gratify, oblige
词性：v.
