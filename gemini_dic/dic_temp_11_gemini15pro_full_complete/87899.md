hierarchist: /ˈhaɪərɑːrkɪst/ | n. | 等级主义者；等级制度拥护者 | A person who believes in or supports a hierarchy, especially in social or political systems. |  The hierarchist insisted on maintaining the traditional power structure.  等级主义者坚持要维持传统的权力结构。 |  The company president, a staunch hierarchist, rarely consulted with lower-level employees. 公司总裁是一个坚定的等级主义者，很少与低级别员工协商。 | Synonyms: authoritarian, elitist, traditionalist | Antonyms: egalitarian, democrat | Noun
