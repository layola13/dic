iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/| adj.|彩虹色的，闪光的|Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles.|The hummingbird's feathers are iridescent. 蜂鸟的羽毛是彩虹色的。|The oil slick on the water created an iridescent sheen. 水面上的油污形成了彩虹色的光泽。|近义词：opalescent, shimmering, pearly, lustrous|反义词：dull, matte|词性:形容词
iridescence:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəns/| n.|彩虹色，闪光|The quality of showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles.|The iridescence of the butterfly's wings was captivating. 蝴蝶翅膀的彩虹色令人着迷。|The soap bubble displayed a beautiful iridescence. 肥皂泡展现出美丽的彩虹色。|近义词：opalescence, shimmer, pearl, luster|反义词：dullness, matteness|词性:名词
