mongrel:/ˈmʌŋɡrəl/ | n. | 杂种狗，混血儿，混合物 | A dog of mixed breed.  A person of mixed origin. A mixture of different things. |  The rescue center took in a lonely mongrel.  (收容中心收留了一只孤独的杂种狗。) | Synonyms: hybrid, crossbreed, mutt (for dogs), mixed-blood | Antonyms: purebred, pedigree | noun

mongrel:/ˈmʌŋɡrəl/ | adj. | 杂种的，混血的 | Of mixed origin or breed. | The garden boasted a mongrel collection of flowers. (花园里种着各种各样的花。) | Synonyms: mixed, hybrid, crossbred | Antonyms: purebred, pedigree | adjective

词源：From Old French *mangoniel,  perhaps related to *mangerie "trade, traffic," from Latin mango "dealer, trader."  The original sense was probably "trader, huckster," later applied to dogs of no particular breed.
