gumshoeing:/ˈɡʌmˌʃuːɪŋ/
n.|侦探工作，暗中调查|The act of investigating something, especially secretly or discreetly.|The reporter did some gumshoeing to uncover the truth about the politician's shady dealings.  这名记者做了一些秘密调查，以揭露这位政客不正当交易的真相。|Synonyms: sleuthing, investigating, probing, detective work|Antonyms: inaction, idleness|noun


gumshoe:/ˈɡʌmˌʃuː/
n.|侦探，私家侦探|A detective, especially a private investigator.|He hired a gumshoe to follow his wife. 他雇了一个私家侦探跟踪他的妻子。|Synonyms: detective, investigator, sleuth|Antonyms: None|noun
