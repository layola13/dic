frightenedly:/ˈfraɪtndli/| adv.|害怕地，惊恐地|In a manner exhibiting fear or alarm.  Describes how an action is performed due to fear.|She looked at him frightenedly. 她惊恐地看着他。|近义词：fearfully, anxiously, nervously, timidly|反义词：bravely, boldly, courageously|词性: 副词
Etymology: From *frightened* (past participle of *frighten*) + *-ly* (adverbial suffix).  *Frighten* comes from Old English *fryhtan* "to frighten," from *fyrhtu* "fright, fear."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *furkht*.
