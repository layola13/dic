dislikes:/dɪsˈlaɪks/
noun|不喜欢，厌恶|a feeling of aversion or disapproval; something that one does not like|She expressed her dislikes for loud music and crowded places. 她表达了她对吵闹的音乐和拥挤的地方的厌恶。|Synonyms: aversion, distaste, antipathy|Antonyms: likes, fondness, preference|n.
dislikes:/dɪsˈlaɪks/
verb|不喜欢，厌恶|to have an aversion to; to regard with disapproval|He dislikes spicy food. 他不喜欢辛辣的食物。|Synonyms: detest, abhor, loathe|Antonyms: like, love, enjoy|v.
