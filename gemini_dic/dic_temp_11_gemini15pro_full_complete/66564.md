exhibitions:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənz/
n.
展览；展览会；展出
A public display of works of art or other items of interest held in an art gallery or museum or at a trade fair.
The museum is holding an exhibition of ancient Egyptian artifacts.  博物馆正在举办古埃及文物的展览。
The artist's work was featured in several exhibitions across the country.  这位艺术家的作品在全国多个展览中展出。
displays, shows, presentations
concealment, hiding
