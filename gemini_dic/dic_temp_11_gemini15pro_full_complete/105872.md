kitless:/ˈkɪtlɪs/
adjective
无装备的；缺少必备工具的
lacking a kit or the necessary equipment.
He arrived kitless and unprepared for the camping trip. 他到达露营地时没有带装备，也没有做好准备。
近义词: unequipped, unprepared
反义词: equipped, prepared
词性: adjective

kitless:/ˈkɪtlɪs/
adjective
无组件的
lacking components or parts.
The kitless furniture requires assembly.  这种无组件的家具需要组装。
近义词: unassembled, incomplete
反义词: assembled, complete
词性: adjective
