insouciance:/ɪnˈsuːsiəns/ /ɪnˈsuːʃəns/| n. | 无忧无虑；漫不经心；漠不关心 | Lack of concern; indifference; nonchalance. |  She displayed a surprising insouciance about the upcoming exams. 她对即将到来的考试表现出令人惊讶的漫不经心。

He met the news of his dismissal with insouciance. 他以漠不关心的态度面对被解雇的消息。 | 近义词：nonchalance, indifference, carelessness, lightheartedness | 反义词：anxiety, worry, concern, solicitude | 词性: 名词
