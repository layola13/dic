hurtled:/ˈhɜːrtld/ (US) /ˈhɜːtld/ (UK)| v.|猛冲，飞驰，猛投|To move with great speed, typically in a reckless or uncontrolled manner.  It also means to throw something with great force.|The car hurtled down the hill. 汽车飞驰下山。|The protestors hurtled rocks at the police. 抗议者向警察投掷石块。|synonyms: sped, rushed, dashed, zoomed, flew, hurled, flung|antonyms: crawled, crept, inched, dawdled|verb
