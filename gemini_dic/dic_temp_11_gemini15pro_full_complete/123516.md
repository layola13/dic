monotone:/ˈmɑːnəˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈmɒnətəʊn/ (UK)| n. adj.|单调，单调的声音；单调乏味的|A sound that stays at one pitch without going higher or lower; something that is boring because it stays the same.|The speaker's monotone made the lecture incredibly dull.|Synonym: drone, tedium|Antonym: variety, diversity|Noun/Adjective

monotones:/ˈmɑːnəˌtoʊnz/ (US) /ˈmɒnətəʊnz/ (UK)| n.|单调的声音，单调的说话方式|A way of speaking in which the voice stays at one pitch without going higher or lower, making it sound boring and uninteresting.|She spoke in monotones, and I almost fell asleep.|Synonym: drones, chanting|Antonym: inflections, modulations|Noun
