anacathartic:/ˌænəkəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adjective
宣泄的；净化心灵的
Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; causing catharsis.
After screaming into her pillow, she felt an anacathartic release of tension.  在对着枕头尖叫之后，她感到了一种宣泄的轻松感。
The therapy session proved anacathartic, allowing him to finally confront his grief.  治疗的过程证明是宣泄的，让他最终能够面对自己的悲伤。
近义词: cathartic, cleansing, purifying, liberating
反义词: repressive, inhibiting, stifling
词性: adjective

anacatharsis:/ˌænəkəˈθɑːrsɪs/
noun
情感宣泄；净化
The process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from, strong or repressed emotions.
Writing poetry can be a form of anacatharsis for some people.  写诗对某些人来说是一种情感宣泄的方式。
She experienced a sense of anacatharsis after confessing her secret.  坦白了她的秘密后，她体验到一种情感宣泄的感觉。
近义词: catharsis, release, purgation, venting
反义词: repression, suppression, inhibition
词性: noun
