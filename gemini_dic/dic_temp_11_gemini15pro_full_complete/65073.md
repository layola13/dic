etesian:/ɪˈtiːʒən/ | adj. | 夏季的，和风有关的，特指地中海地区夏季的干燥的北风 |  Relating to or caused by the etesian winds, which are dry northerly winds of the Aegean Sea, blowing in the summer. | The etesian winds bring welcome relief from the summer heat. 地中海夏季的和风带来了令人愉悦的凉爽。 | The etesian winds helped the sailors navigate the Aegean Sea. 和风帮助水手们在地中海航行。 | synonyms: northerly, summery,  meltemi (specifically for the Aegean Sea wind) | antonyms: southerly, wintery | Adjective
