cyanuric:/ˌsaɪəˈnjʊərɪk/ | adj. |氰尿的，氰尿酸的 | Relating to or derived from cyanuric acid. | Cyanuric acid is a chemical compound with the formula (CNOH)3. 氰尿酸是一种化学式为(CNOH)3的化合物。| Cyanuric chloride is a useful reagent in organic synthesis.  氰尿酰氯是有机合成中的有用试剂。 |  isocyanuric | N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *cyan* (blue) + *-uric* (relating to uric acid). The name comes from the blue color of copper cyanurate.
