ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "honky-tonk" is uncertain, but it likely emerged in the late 19th century in the American West and South. Some theories suggest it's onomatopoeic, mimicking the sounds of such establishments. Others connect it to the name of a specific saloon or type of piano.
