gregarinida:/ɡrɛɡəˈrɪnɪdə/| n. |簇虫类，グレガリナ|A large order of parasitic protozoa, belonging to the subclass Gregarinasina, of the class Sporozoa. They are common parasites of arthropods, mollusks, and annelids.|Gregarinida are often found in the guts of insects.|近义词:gregarine, sporozoan|反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)|noun
