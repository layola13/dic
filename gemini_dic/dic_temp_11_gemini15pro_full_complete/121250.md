misery:/ˈmɪzəri/| n.|痛苦，悲惨，不幸|A state of great suffering or unhappiness.|She lived a life of misery. 她过着悲惨的生活。|近义词：distress, suffering, anguish, torment, woe|反义词：happiness, joy, bliss|noun

miserable:/ˈmɪzərəbl/| adj.|悲惨的，痛苦的，可怜的|Wretchedly unhappy or uncomfortable.|The weather was miserable - cold and raining. 天气很糟糕——又冷又下雨。|近义词：unhappy, wretched, sorrowful, dejected|反义词：happy, cheerful, joyful|adjective
