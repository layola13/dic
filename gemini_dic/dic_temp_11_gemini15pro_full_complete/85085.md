hardbacks:/ˈhɑːrdbæks/ | n. |精装书|A book bound with stiff covers. |例句：I prefer hardbacks because they are more durable. 我更喜欢精装书，因为它们更耐用。

例句：The hardback edition is more expensive than the paperback. 精装版比平装版贵。|近义词：hardcovers, hardbound books|反义词：paperbacks|词性:名词

词源:  "hard" + "back"，"hard" 指的是坚硬的封面，"back" 指的是书的背面。
