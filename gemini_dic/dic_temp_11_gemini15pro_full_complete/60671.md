electr:/ɪˈlɛktr/ | n. | 電; 電子; 電力 |  A combining form meaning “electric, electrical,” used in the formation of compound words: electromagnet.  From Greek *ēlektron* amber. |  Electr- is used in words like electromagnet and electrocute.  | 近义词: electric, electrical | 反义词: none | 词性: combining form
