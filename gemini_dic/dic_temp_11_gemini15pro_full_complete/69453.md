feignedly:/ˈfeɪnɪdli/| adv. |假装地，虚伪地 | in a feigned manner; pretendingly; falsely.  It describes an action done with pretense or artificiality. | She feignedly smiled at him, hiding her true feelings. 她假装对他微笑，掩饰了她真实的感受。|近义词:pretendedly, falsely, deceptively, hypocritically |反义词:genuinely, sincerely, truly, honestly |词性:副词

feignedly:/ˈfeɪnɪdli/| adv. |假装地，虚伪地 |  Meaning "in a feigned manner," from feign + -ed + -ly.  Feign comes from the Old French *feindre, from Latin fingere "to touch, handle, mold, shape, fashion, form, invent, feign," related to figūra "shape, form." | He feignedly agreed to her request, though he had no intention of fulfilling it. 他假惺惺地同意了她的请求，尽管他并不打算兑现。|近义词:artfully, dissemblingly, insincerely |反义词:openly, frankly, candidly |词性:副词
