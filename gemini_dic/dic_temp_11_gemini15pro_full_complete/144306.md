outsport:/ˈaʊtspɔːrt/
verb
胜过；在体育运动上超过
To surpass or defeat in a sport or athletic competition.
He outsported his rival in the final match. 他在决赛中胜过了他的对手。
surpass, defeat, outdo, beat
be defeated by, lose to
