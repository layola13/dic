myrialitre:/ˌmɪriəˈliːtər/ |n.|兆升；京升|A unit of volume equal to 10,000 cubic meters (10^7 liters) or one hundredth of a megalitre.  It is not a commonly used unit.|The reservoir holds several myrialitres of water.|近义词: megalitre (ML), kilolitre (kL) |反义词: microlitre (µL), nanolitre (nL)|noun
