overassume:/ˌoʊvərəˈsuːm/| v. |过度假设，过分臆断|To assume too much; to make presumptions that go beyond what is warranted by the evidence or situation.|She overassumed his willingness to help and was disappointed when he refused. 她过分臆测他乐于助人，当他拒绝时她感到很失望。|例句：He overassumed her interest in him and was embarrassed when she rejected his advances. 他高估了她对他的兴趣，当她拒绝他的求爱时，他感到很尴尬。

例句：Don't overassume that everyone agrees with you; there might be dissenting opinions. 不要过分假设每个人都同意你的观点；可能会有不同的意见。|近义词：presume, suppose, conjecture, surmise|反义词：underestimate, undervalue|词性:动词
