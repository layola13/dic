filch:/fɪltʃ/| v.|偷窃，窃取|To steal something, especially something small and of little value, in a casual or sneaky way.|He filched a candy bar from the store. 他从商店里偷了一块糖果。|Synonyms: pilfer, swipe, pinch, purloin|Antonyms: return, give, offer|verb

filch:/fɪltʃ/| n.|偷窃行为|The act of stealing something small and insignificant.|The filch went unnoticed by the shopkeeper. 店主没有注意到这次偷窃行为。|Synonyms: theft, pilfering, swiping|Antonyms: return, restitution|noun
