chatoyant:/ʃəˈtɔɪənt/ /ʃæˈtɔɪənt/
adjective
adj. 变幻的，闪光的，猫眼状的
Having a changeable luster or color with an undulating narrow band of light, like that of a cat's eye.
The gemstone displayed a chatoyant shimmer as I turned it in the light. 这颗宝石在我转动它时展现出变幻的光芒。
The silk fabric had a chatoyant sheen that shifted with the movement of the wearer.  丝绸面料有一种闪光的光泽，随着穿着者的移动而变化。

近义词: shimmering, iridescent, opalescent, lustrous
反义词: dull, matte

词性: adjective

词源: From French chatoyant, present participle of chatoyer "to shimmer," from Old French chatoier "to flicker, sparkle," from chate "cat" (from Latin cattus) + -oyer, suffix denoting repeated or continuous action.
