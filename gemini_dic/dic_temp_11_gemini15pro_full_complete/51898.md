dharna:/ˈdɑːrnə/| n.| 静坐抗议，静坐示威|A form of non-violent protest in which people sit or stand in a public place and refuse to move until their demands are met.  It originated in India.|例句：The workers staged a dharna outside the factory gates. 工人们在工厂大门外举行了静坐示威。

例句：The dharna lasted for several days until the management agreed to negotiate. 静坐示威持续了几天，直到管理层同意谈判。|近义词：sit-in, protest, demonstration|反义词: acquiescence, compliance|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi धरना (dharnā), from Sanskrit धारणा (dhāraṇā, “holding, maintaining, keeping”), from the verb root धृ (dhṛ, “to hold, maintain, keep”).
