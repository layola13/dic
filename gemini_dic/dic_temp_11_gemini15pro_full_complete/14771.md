awl's:/ɔːlz/| n.| 锥子；尖钻 |A small pointed tool used for piercing holes, especially in leather or wood.| He used an awl to make holes in the leather for the stitching. 他用锥子在皮革上钻孔以便缝合。| synonyms: piercer,bradawl, gimlet | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Old English *æle, from Proto-Germanic *aliz, *aliso. Cognate with Old High German ala, Old Norse alr.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂el- (“awl, thorn”).
