detasseling:/diːˈtæsəlɪŋ/
n.| 除须; 去雄;|The process of removing the tassel from a corn plant.  去除玉米植株雄穗的过程。|Detasseling is a common summer job for teenagers in the Corn Belt. 在玉米带，除须是青少年常见的暑期工作。|Synonym: tassel removal|Antonym: pollination
词性: 名词
