handed:/ˈhændɪd/| adj.|惯用的；用…手的；有…只手的；交付的|Predominantly right-handed, she struggled with left-handed scissors.  她主要用右手，用左手剪刀很费劲。|例句：He's a left-handed pitcher. 他是一个左撇子投手。|近义词：dexterous, skillful (for skilled with hands)|反义词:clumsy, awkward (for unskilled with hands)|词性:形容词
handed:/ˈhændɪd/| v. (hand的过去式和过去分词) |递，交，给|例句：She handed me the book. 她把书递给了我。|近义词：gave, passed|反义词:took, received|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *handod, from hand + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *handuz.
