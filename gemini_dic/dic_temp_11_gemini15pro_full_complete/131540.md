noncommendably:/ˌnɑːnˌkɑːˈmɛndəbli/ | adv. | 不值得称赞地，不值得推荐地 | In a manner that is not worthy of praise or recommendation.  | He behaved noncommendably during the meeting, interrupting and dismissing others' opinions. 他在会议上的行为不值得称赞，打断别人并无视他们的意见。 | The project was managed noncommendably, resulting in significant cost overruns and delays.  这个项目管理得很差，导致成本严重超支和延误。| 近义词: reprehensibly, censurably, disapprovingly | 反义词: commendably, laudably, praiseworthily | 词性: 副词
