directionlessness:/dɪˈrɛkʃənləsnəs/
n.
lack of direction; aimlessness
The directionlessness of youth can be both exciting and daunting.  年轻人的茫然既令人兴奋又令人畏惧。
He felt a sense of directionlessness after losing his job.  失业后，他感到茫然。
近义词: aimlessness, purposelessness, drift, lostness
反义词: purposefulness, focus, determination, resolve
词性: noun

Etymology:
From direction + -less + -ness.  Direction from Old French, ultimately from Latin 'directus'. '-less' and '-ness' are native English suffixes.
