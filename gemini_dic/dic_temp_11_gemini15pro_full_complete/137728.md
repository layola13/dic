nonvagrantly:/nɒnˈveɪɡrəntli/ | adv. | 非流浪地，定居地 |  In a settled, non-nomadic manner; not wandering or roaming.  |  He lived nonvagrantly, remaining in the same town for his entire life. 他一生都定居地生活，一直住在同一个城镇。 |  settledly, permanently, stably | nomadically, wanderly, rovingly | adverb

Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix): Latin, meaning "not."
"Vagrant": From Old French "vagarant," present participle of "vaguer" ("to wander"), from Latin "vagari" ("to wander, roam, stray").
"-ly" (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
