electroactive:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 电活性的，电致活性的 |  Exhibiting a change of oxidation state as a result of an applied electrical current, or producing an electrical current in response to a chemical reaction.  |  This material is electroactive and can be used in sensors. 这种材料是电活性的，可以用于传感器。| synonyms: redox-active, electrochemically active | antonyms: electrochemically inert | adjective
