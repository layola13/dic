fourfold:/ˈfɔːrfəʊld/| adj. adv.|四倍的，四重的；四部分的|Multiplied by four; having four parts or elements.|例句：The company's profits increased fourfold this year.  今年公司的利润增加了四倍。

fourfold:/ˈfɔːrfəʊld/| n.|四倍|A quantity four times as great as another.|例句：The investment resulted in a fourfold return. 这项投资获得了四倍的回报。
