offsetted:/ɒfˈsɛtɪd/ | v. | 抵消，补偿 | To counterbalance or compensate for something. | The company offsetted its carbon emissions by planting trees. 公司通过植树来抵消其碳排放。 |  The gains offsetted the losses. 收益抵消了损失。 | 近义词: compensated, counterbalanced, balanced | 反义词: increased, aggravated | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the verb "offset," which is formed from the prefix "off-" (meaning "away from, detached") and the verb "set" (meaning "to place, put").  The "-ed" ending signifies the past tense or past participle form.

注意事项:
"Offsetted" is generally considered the standard past tense and past participle of "offset" in American English. However, "offset" is also sometimes used as the past tense and past participle, especially in British English.  Both are generally considered acceptable.
