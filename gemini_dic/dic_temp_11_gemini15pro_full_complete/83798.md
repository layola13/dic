hairpieces:/ˈhɛərˌpiːsɪz/| n. |假发，发片|A piece of artificial hair worn to cover baldness or to enhance one's natural hair.|She wore hairpieces to make her hair look thicker. 她戴了假发，让头发看起来更浓密。|Synonyms: wig, toupee, hair extension|Antonyms: natural hair|noun
