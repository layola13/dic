madreporite:/ˌmædrɪˈpɔːraɪt/| n. |篩板|A perforated plate by which the vascular system of echinoderms communicates with the exterior.|Sea stars use their madreporite to regulate their internal water pressure and for locomotion. 海星利用它们的篩板来调节内部水压和进行运动。|Synonyms: sieve plate, water pore|Antonyms: None|Noun
