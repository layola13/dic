meagre:/ˈmiːɡər/ (US) /ˈmiːɡə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|贫乏的，不足的，瘦的|Lacking in quantity or quality; insufficient. Also, thin or emaciated.|例句：The meagre rations were barely enough to survive on. 补给不足，几乎难以生存。|例句：He looked meagre and unwell after his illness.  他病后看起来很瘦弱，很不舒服。|近义词：scanty, sparse, insufficient, paltry, thin, lean, emaciated|反义词：abundant, plentiful, ample, sufficient, plump, substantial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *megre, from Old French maigre, from Latin macer (“lean, thin”), perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *mak- (“long, thin”).
