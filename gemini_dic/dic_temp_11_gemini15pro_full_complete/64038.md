epithesis:/ɪˈpɪθɪsɪs/| n. |附加物，添补物；修补，弥补（尤指身体部位的）|An artificial addition, especially to the body, such as a false eye, nose, or limb.  A restoring or supplying of what is wanting; substitution. |After losing his finger in the accident, John received an epithesis to help restore its functionality.  约翰在事故中失去了手指，他接受了添补物来帮助恢复其功能。|Synonyms: prosthesis, addition, supplement, implant, augmentation |Antonyms: removal, amputation, excision| noun

epithesis:/ɪˈpɪθɪsɪs/| n. |（语言学）音插入|The addition of a sound or letter within a word.|The word "athlete" sometimes undergoes epithesis, becoming pronounced as "athelete."  "athlete"这个词有时会发生音插入现象，发音变成"athelete"。|Synonyms: insertion, addition, epenthesis |Antonyms: elision, deletion, omission| noun
