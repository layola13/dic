builders:/ˈbɪldərz/| n.|建造者，建设者|People who construct or erect buildings or other structures.|The builders are working hard to finish the project on time. 建造者们正在努力工作以按时完成项目。|constructors, developers, erectors|destroyers, demolishers|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bilderes*, from Old English *byldere*, agent noun from *byldan* (“to build”).  Cognate with German *Bilder* (“images, pictures”).
