nonextensiveness:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnsɪvnəs/| n. |非广延性，非外延性|The property of a system where the total value of a property is not simply the sum of its parts.|The nonextensiveness of entropy is a key concept in statistical mechanics. 熵的非广延性是统计力学中的一个关键概念。|近义词：nonadditivity|反义词：extensiveness, additivity|词性:noun

nonextensive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnsɪv/| adj. |非广延的，非外延的|Describing a system where the total value of a property is not simply the sum of its parts.|Nonextensive thermodynamics is a generalization of standard thermodynamics. 非广延热力学是标准热力学的推广。|近义词：nonadditive|反义词：extensive, additive|词性:adjective
