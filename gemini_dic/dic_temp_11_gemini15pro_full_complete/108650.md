leaflet: /ˈliːflɪt/ (US also /ˈliːflət/) | n. | 传单；小册子；散页印刷品 | A printed sheet of paper containing information or advertising and usually distributed free. |  Example 1: They were handing out leaflets about the upcoming election. 他们正在分发有关即将举行的选举的传单。

Example 2: I picked up a leaflet about local museums. 我拿了一份当地博物馆的宣传单。 | Synonyms: pamphlet, flyer, brochure, handbill, circular | Antonyms: book, volume | noun
