baa's:/bɑːz/
noun
羊叫声; (尤指)咩咩叫声
the bleating of sheep, especially a lamb

The lamb's baas could be heard across the field.
小羊的咩咩叫声在田野里都能听到。

Synonyms: bleats, cries, calls
Antonyms: silence, quiet

Etymology:  Imitative origin.
