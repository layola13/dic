nondisastrously:/ˌnɒndɪˈzɑːstrəsli/ | adv. | 无灾无难地；平安地；顺利地 | In a way that avoids disaster or serious negative consequences. | The mission, though challenging, concluded nondisastrously. 虽然任务具有挑战性，但最终平安结束。| Synonyms: safely, successfully, uneventfully, smoothly | Antonyms: disastrously, catastrophically | adverb

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + disastrous + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Disastrous" comes from the Old Italian *disastroso, from dis- "away, apart" + astro "star," hence "ill-starred," "unlucky."
