deflationists:/dɪˈfleɪʃənɪsts/
n.
通货紧缩主义者，通货紧缩论者
Those who believe in or advocate for deflationary policies.  These individuals or groups often argue that falling prices are beneficial for the economy.
Deflationists argue that falling prices increase consumer purchasing power. 通货紧缩主义者认为，价格下跌会提高消费者的购买力。
Central banks are often wary of deflationists' arguments. 中央银行通常对通货紧缩主义者的论点持谨慎态度。
近义词:none (This word represents a specific economic viewpoint, and direct synonyms are rare.)
反义词:inflationists
词性:noun
