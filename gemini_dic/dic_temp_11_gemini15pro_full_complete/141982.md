organdy:/ˈɔːrɡəndi/ (US), /ˈɔːɡəndi/ (UK)| n. |薄纱，透明硬纱|A thin, stiff, transparent fabric of cotton or silk. |例句：She wore a beautiful organdy dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的薄纱连衣裙去参加聚会。

例句：The curtains were made of delicate organdy. 窗帘是用精致的薄纱制成的。


近义词： voile, muslin, gauze |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "heavy fabric" or "opaque fabric") |词性: noun
