nasalising:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
鼻音化
The act of making a sound nasal by lowering the soft palate so that air escapes through the nose.
She was nasalising her vowels, which made her difficult to understand. 她把元音鼻音化了，这让她很难被理解。
The singer is nasalising the final consonant. 歌手正在将最后一个辅音鼻音化。
