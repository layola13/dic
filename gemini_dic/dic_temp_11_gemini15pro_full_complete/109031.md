leg-pulling: /ˈleɡˌpʊlɪŋ/
noun
开玩笑；戏弄；哄骗
The act of teasing or tricking someone in a playful way.
He didn't realize it was all leg-pulling. 他没有意识到这只是一场玩笑。
My brother is always leg-pulling me. 我的兄弟总是爱戏弄我。
Synonyms: teasing, joking, kidding, ribbing, joshing, bantering, taking the mickey (UK), having someone on (UK), winding someone up (UK)
Antonyms: seriousness, sincerity
