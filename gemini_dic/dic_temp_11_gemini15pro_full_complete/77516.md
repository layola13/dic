gazumped:/ɡəˈzʌmpt/ | v. |  抬高价格成交; 哄抬物价成交；毁约加价转售(房屋) | to raise the price of something (such as a house) that you have agreed to sell after a buyer has offered the asking price, forcing the buyer to pay the higher price or lose the purchase.  |  They were gazumped at the last minute by a higher bidder.  他们最后一刻被一个出价更高的买家抬价成交了。 | synonyms: outbid, overbid | antonyms: underbid, undercut | verb

gazumping:/ɡəˈzʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 抬高价格成交; 哄抬物价成交；毁约加价转售(房屋) | the act of gazumping | The practice of gazumping is unfortunately common in this area. 在这个地区，哄抬房价的做法很常见。 | synonyms: outbidding, overbidding | antonyms: underbidding, undercutting | noun
