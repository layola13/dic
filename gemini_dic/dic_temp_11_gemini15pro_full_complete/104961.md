kemb:/kɛm/
n.|梳子 (尤指羊毛梳)|A toothed instrument used for disentangling, cleaning, or arranging hair, wool, or flax.|She used a kemb to straighten the tangled wool.|近义词:comb|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly tangle, snarl)|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old English *camb, from Proto-Germanic *kambaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gembh- (“tooth”). Cognate with German Kamm, Dutch kam.
