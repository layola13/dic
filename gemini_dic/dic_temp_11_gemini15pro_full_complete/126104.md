mutational:/mjuːˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
突变的

The mutational changes in the virus made it more resistant to the vaccine. 病毒的突变变化使其对疫苗更具抵抗力。
Synonyms: mutant, genetic, altered
Antonyms: stable, unchanging
