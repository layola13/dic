jinn:/dʒɪn/ | n. |精灵，神怪|A supernatural creature in Arabian and Muslim mythology, often depicted as having the ability to assume human or animal form and to influence human affairs. |The fisherman rubbed the lamp, and a jinn appeared in a puff of smoke. 渔夫擦了擦神灯，一个精灵从一团烟雾中出现。|
|近义词:genie, spirit, demon|反义词:angel, god|名词
