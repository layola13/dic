auditioning:/ɔːˈdɪʃənɪŋ/
n.|试镜，试音，试演|The act of performing a short piece, usually for a judge or panel, to demonstrate one's talent and suitability for a role or position.|She is auditioning for a part in the play. 她正在为戏剧中的一个角色试镜。|tryout, test, screening|rejection|noun

auditioning:/ɔːˈdɪʃənɪŋ/
v.|试镜，试音，试演|The act of participating in an audition.|He's auditioning for the lead singer role in the band. 他正在试音，争取成为乐队的主唱。|try out, test, screen|reject|verb
