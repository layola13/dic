manhunter:/ˈmænˌhʌntər/| n.|猎头; 追捕者; 捕猎者|A person who hunts and kills other people.|The police are searching for the manhunter who escaped from prison. 警方正在搜捕越狱的杀人凶手。|Synonyms: hunter, predator, pursuer|Antonyms: prey, victim|noun

manhunter:/ˈmænˌhʌntər/| n.|猎头(公司)|A company that finds people for senior jobs.|The company hired a manhunter to find a new CEO.  这家公司聘请了一家猎头公司来寻找新的首席执行官。|Synonyms: headhunter, recruiter|Antonyms: applicant, candidate|noun
