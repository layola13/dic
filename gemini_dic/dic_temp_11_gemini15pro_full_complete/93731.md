immarginate:/ɪˈmɑːrdʒɪnət/ | adj. | 无边缘的，无边框的 | Lacking a margin or border; not margined. | The immarginate petals of the flower gave it a unique appearance.  这朵花无边缘的花瓣使它具有独特的外观。 | 近义词: borderless, edgeless, unbordered | 反义词: marginate, bordered, edged | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *immarginatus*,  from in- 'not' + marginatus 'bordered', past participle of marginare 'to furnish with a border', from margin- 'border'.
