lexes:/ˈlɛksiːz/| n.| 词汇，词位| A lexeme is a basic unit of meaning in a language.  A lexe is a variant form of a lexeme.  Lexes are often considered in computational linguistics.|  The program analyzes the lexes in the sentence to determine its meaning. 程序分析句子中的词位以确定其含义。| synonyms: words, vocabulary, terms, lexicon | antonyms: none | noun
