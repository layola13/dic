insufferableness:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbəlnəs/
n.
难以忍受；令人厌恶
The quality or state of being insufferable; intolerableness.
Her insufferableness made it difficult to be around her for long periods of time. 她令人难以忍受，让人无法长时间和她待在一起。
近义词: intolerableness, unbearableness, obnoxiousness
反义词: pleasantness, agreeableness, tolerability
词性: 名词

insufferable:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbəl/
adj.
难以忍受的；令人厌恶的
Too extreme to bear; intolerable.
The heat was insufferable, making it impossible to work outdoors.  酷热难耐，根本无法在户外工作。
近义词: intolerable, unbearable, obnoxious
反义词: pleasant, agreeable, tolerable
词性: 形容词

词源: From in- + sufferable, from suffer + -able.  "In-" 表示否定，"sufferable" 意为可忍受的，因此 "insufferable" 表示不可忍受的。
