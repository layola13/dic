espousal:/ɪˈspaʊzəl/| n.|拥护，支持；信奉；结婚，订婚|An act of adopting or supporting a cause, belief, or way of life.  The act of becoming engaged to marry.|例句：His espousal of the radical cause surprised many. 他对激进事业的支持让很多人感到惊讶。

例句：Their espousal took place in a small chapel. 他们的订婚仪式在一个小教堂举行。|近义词：adoption, support, advocacy, championship, marriage, engagement|反义词：rejection, opposition, renunciation, divorce, separation|noun
