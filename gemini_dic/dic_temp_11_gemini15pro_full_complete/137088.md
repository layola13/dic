nonstudiousness:/ˌnɑːnˌstuːdiəsnəs/
n.
不勤奋，不爱学习
lack of studiousness; unwillingness to study.
His nonstudiousness resulted in poor grades. 他的不勤奋导致成绩很差。
Synonyms: indolence, laziness, apathy, indifference
Antonyms: diligence, studiousness, industriousness
