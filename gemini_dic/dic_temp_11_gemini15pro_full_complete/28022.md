capacious:/kəˈpeɪʃəs/| adj.|宽敞的，容量大的|Having a lot of space inside; roomy.|例句：The capacious bag held all of her groceries.  这个宽敞的袋子装下了她所有的杂货。

例句：The auditorium was capacious enough to hold the entire student body.  礼堂足够宽敞，可以容纳全体学生。|近义词：roomy, spacious, ample, commodious|反义词：small, cramped, limited, restricted|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *capaciosus*, from *capax* ("able to hold, spacious"), from *capere* ("to take, hold").
