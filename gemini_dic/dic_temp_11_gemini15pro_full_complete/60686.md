electricity: /ɪˌlekˈtrɪsəti/ | n. |电，电能 | A form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles (such as electrons or protons), either statically as an accumulation of charge or dynamically as a current. |  The electricity went out during the storm.  暴风雨期间停电了。| power, energy | darkness, non-electricity | noun
