enraging:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪŋ/| adj.|使人愤怒的，令人恼火的|Causing great anger; infuriating.|例句：The enraging traffic made me late for work. 令人恼火的交通让我上班迟到了。|近义词：infuriating, maddening, exasperating, annoying, irritating|反义词：calming, soothing, pleasing, delightful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From enrage + -ing.  Enrage comes from the Old French word enrager, from en- "make" + rage "rage".
