multicircuit's:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪts/ |adj.|多电路的|Belonging to or used by multiple circuits.  Derived from "multi-" (many) + "circuit" (a complete path for an electric current). The apostrophe-s indicates possession.| Example: The multicircuit's control panel managed power distribution efficiently. 多电路的控制面板有效地管理了配电。|近义词: multi-circuit, multiple-circuit|反义词: single-circuit|词性: adjective
