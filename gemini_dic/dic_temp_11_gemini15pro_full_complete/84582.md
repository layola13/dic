hammy:/ˈhæmi/| adj.|做作的，夸张的，过分戏剧化的|Excessively theatrical or dramatic in style, often to the point of being artificial or affected.  Originating from the theatrical tradition of amateur actors overacting, as if performing a "ham" role.|His performance was so hammy it was almost comical. 他的表演太做作了，几乎到了滑稽的地步。|例句：The villain's laugh was incredibly hammy.  反派的狂笑非常做作。|近义词：theatrical, melodramatic, overacted, exaggerated, affected|反义词：understated, subtle, nuanced, natural, realistic|词性:形容词
