chinking:/ˈtʃɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 填塞(缝隙)，嵌填材料；(带有强烈歧视意味的)对东亚人的蔑称 | The act of filling in chinks or cracks, typically with caulking or mortar. chinking:/ˈtʃɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. | (金属或玻璃碰撞的)叮当声 | A light ringing sound, as of metal or glass striking together. |  The chinking of glasses signaled the start of the celebration.  玻璃杯的叮当声标志着庆祝的开始。 | 近义词: clinking, jingling, tinkling | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun
