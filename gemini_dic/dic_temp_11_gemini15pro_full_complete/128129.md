netfish:/ˈnɛtˌfɪʃ/| n.|网鱼，用网捕到的鱼|A fish caught in a net.  This refers to any fish caught using a net, regardless of the species.|例句：The fishermen brought in a large haul of netfish. 渔民们带回了一大网鱼。

例句：The netfish were mostly herring and mackerel. 网捕到的鱼大多是鲱鱼和鲭鱼。|近义词：fish, catch|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a fishing method)|Noun
