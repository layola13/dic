onslaught:/ˈɑːnslɔːt/ (US), /ˈɒnslɔːt/ (UK)
noun
猛攻，攻击
a fierce or violent attack
The town suffered an onslaught of enemy fire.  这个城镇遭受了敌人的猛烈炮火袭击。

The company faced an onslaught of criticism after the product recall.  产品召回后，公司面临铺天盖地的批评。

近义词: attack, assault, barrage, bombardment
反义词: defense, resistance

词性: n.


词源: From Middle Dutch *ānslach, from aan- + slach, "stroke, blow."  源于中古荷兰语 *ānslach，由 aan- + slach（“击打，打击”）组成。
