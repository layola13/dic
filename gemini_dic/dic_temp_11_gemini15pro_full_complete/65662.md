evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ /ɪˈveɪzɪv/| adj.|逃避的，回避的，托辞的|Tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly.|The politician gave an evasive answer to the journalist's question. 这位政治家对记者的提问给出了一个回避的答案。|Synonyms: elusive, ambiguous, equivocal, vague|Antonyms: direct, forthright, honest, open|adjective
