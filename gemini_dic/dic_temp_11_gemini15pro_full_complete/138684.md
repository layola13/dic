nuisance:/ˈnjuːsns/ (US), /ˈnjuːs(ə)ns/ (UK)| n. | 讨厌的人（或事物）; 麻烦事 | Something or someone that causes trouble, annoyance, or inconvenience. |  The barking dog next door is a real nuisance. 隔壁狂吠的狗真是个讨厌鬼。

|近义词：annoyance, bother, pest, inconvenience, problem|反义词: pleasure, delight, joy | Noun


nuisance:/ˈnjuːsns/ (US), /ˈnjuːs(ə)ns/ (UK)| adj. |讨厌的;麻烦的;妨害公共利益的|(chiefly law) Causing inconvenience or annoyance.  |  Nuisance calls from telemarketers are a common problem.  来自电话推销员的骚扰电话是一个普遍问题。 | 近义词：annoying, troublesome, bothersome, inconvenient |反义词: pleasant, delightful, agreeable | Adjective

词源:
From Old French *nusance (whence also Modern French nuisance), from nuire “to harm, hurt, injure,” from Latin nocēre.


注意事项:
nuisance 既可以作名词，也可以作形容词，但形容词用法主要限于法律语境。
