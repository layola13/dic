outchattered:/ˌaʊtˈtʃætərd/
verb
胜过…的饶舌，比…更健谈
to surpass someone in chattering; to talk more or more rapidly than someone else.
She outchattered her friend during the entire lunch.  她在整个午餐时间里比她朋友说得更多。
He easily outchattered everyone at the party. 他在派对上轻松地比每个人说得都多。
近义词: outtalk, outspeak
反义词: be quiet, listen
