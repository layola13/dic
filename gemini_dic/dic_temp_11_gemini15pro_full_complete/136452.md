nonreversible:/ˌnɒnrɪˈvɜːsəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnrɪˈvɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 不可逆的，不能反转的 | Not able to be reversed or changed back to its original state. | The damage to the ecosystem is nonreversible.  这个生态系统的损害是不可逆转的。 |  The chemical reaction is nonreversible under these conditions. 在这些条件下，该化学反应是不可逆的。 | 近义词：irreversible, unchangeable, permanent | 反义词：reversible, changeable, temporary | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non, meaning "not"
reversible (adjective): from Late Latin reversabilis, from reversare "to turn back," from Latin re- "back" + versare "to turn"
