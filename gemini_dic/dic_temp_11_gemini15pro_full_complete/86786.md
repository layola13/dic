hemachrome:/ˈhiːməkrəʊm/ /ˈheməˌkroʊm/ | n. |血色素，血色蛋白|A colored protein containing heme, such as hemoglobin or myoglobin, that acts as a respiratory pigment. |Hemoglobin is a hemachrome responsible for oxygen transport in the blood. 血红蛋白是一种负责血液中氧气运输的血色素。|The hemachrome in muscle tissue is called myoglobin. 肌肉组织中的血色素叫做肌红蛋白。|近义词：hemoglobin, myoglobin|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "apoprotein" - the protein component without the heme group) |词性: noun
