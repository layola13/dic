confirm:/kənˈfɜːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɜːm/ (UK)| v. |确认；证实；批准；坚定|To establish the truth or correctness of something; to validate.  To strengthen or make more certain.|I called the airline to confirm my flight reservation. 我打电话给航空公司确认我的航班预订。|Synonyms: verify, corroborate, validate, authenticate, substantiate|Antonyms: deny, refute, contradict, disapprove|verb
