credulous: /ˈkredʒələs/ (US) /ˈkredjʊləs/ (UK)
adjective
轻信的，易受骗的
Having or showing too great a readiness to believe things.
She was so credulous that she believed his every word. 她太轻信了，以至于相信他说的每一句话。
He is a credulous person who will believe anything. 他是一个轻信的人，什么都会相信。

近义词:
gullible, naive, trusting, unsuspecting
反义词:
skeptical, cynical, incredulous, distrustful
词性:
adjective
