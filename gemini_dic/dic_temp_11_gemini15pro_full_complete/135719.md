nonprime:/ˌnɒnˈpraɪm/ | adj. | 非质数的，合数的 | Not a prime number; divisible by a number other than itself and one. |  Example 1:  Four is a nonprime number because it is divisible by two. 四是一个合数，因为它可以被二整除。

Example 2: The number 12 is nonprime, having factors of 2, 3, 4, and 6. 12是合数，有2、3、4和6等因子。 |  Synonyms: composite | Antonyms: prime | Adjective
