epilepsy: /ˈɛpɪˌlɛpsi/ (US), /ˈɛpɪˌlɛpsi/ (UK)| n. |癫痫，羊癫疯|A neurological disorder marked by sudden recurrent episodes of sensory disturbance, loss of consciousness, or convulsions, associated with abnormal electrical activity in the brain.| My uncle has epilepsy and has to take medication daily. 我的叔叔患有癫痫，必须每天服药。| Epilepsy can be a debilitating condition. 癫痫可能是一种使人衰弱的疾病。|近义词：seizure disorder, convulsive disorder|反义词: None|词性:noun

epileptic: /ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/ (US), /ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/ (UK)| adj. n. |癫痫的，患癫痫的；癫痫患者|Relating to or affected by epilepsy; a person with epilepsy.| He had an epileptic seizure. 他癫痫发作了。| She is epileptic and requires regular medical attention.  她是癫痫病患者，需要定期就医。|近义词：convulsive|反义词: None|词性:adjective, noun
