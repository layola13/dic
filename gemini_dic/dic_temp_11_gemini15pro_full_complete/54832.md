dispirit:/dɪˈspɪrɪt/
verb
使沮丧，使气馁
To make someone lose enthusiasm or hope.
The team was dispirited by their recent loss.  这支队伍因最近的失利而士气低落。

The constant rain dispirited the hikers.  持续的降雨使徒步旅行者感到沮丧。

近义词: discourage, deject, demoralize
反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + spirit (animating or vital principle).  Ultimately from Latin spiritus ("breath").
