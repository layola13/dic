manaker:/ˈmænəkər/| n.|经理，管理者|A person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff.|The manaker is responsible for the team's performance.  经理负责团队的绩效。|近义词：manager, administrator, executive, director, supervisor|反义词:subordinate, employee|名词

词源:  Likely a misspelling of "manager," which comes from the Old French "maneger" (to handle, manage a horse), from Late Latin "managiare"  and ultimately from Latin "manus" (hand).
