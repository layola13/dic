bobstay:/ˈbɒbˌsteɪ/ (US) /ˈbɒbˌsteɪ/ (UK)| n. |船首斜撑|A rope or chain used to steady the bowsprit of a sailing ship, running from the end of the bowsprit to the stem or cutwater.|The bobstay snapped under the strain of the heavy seas. 巨浪的压力下，船首斜撑断裂了。|The bobstay kept the bowsprit from lifting in the strong winds.  强风中，船首斜撑防止船首斜桅抬起。|近义词: forestay, martingale|反义词: N/A|名词
