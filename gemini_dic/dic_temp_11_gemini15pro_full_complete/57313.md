downtick:/ˈdaʊntɪk/| n.|下跌，小幅下降|A downtick is a small decrease in the price of a stock or other security.  It represents a decline in trading activity or market value.|The stock market experienced a slight downtick today. 股票市场今天略有下跌。|Synonyms: decline, decrease, dip, fall|Antonyms: uptick, increase, rise|noun

downtick:/ˈdaʊntɪk/| n.|不利因素，负面发展|A downtick can also refer to a negative development or a decline in something.|There has been a downtick in consumer confidence recently. 最近消费者信心有所下降。|Synonyms: downturn, setback, decline|Antonyms: upturn, improvement, rise|noun
