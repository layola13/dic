feudary:/ˈfjuːdəri/ /ˈfjuːdətɔːri/| n. adj.|封臣；封建的|A person who held land from a feudal lord in return for allegiance and service; relating to or characteristic of feudalism.|例句：The feudary swore allegiance to his lord.  封臣向他的领主宣誓效忠。

例句：The feudary system was prevalent in medieval Europe. 封建制度在中世纪的欧洲很普遍。|近义词：vassal, liegeman, tenant|反义词：lord, overlord|词性: 名词/形容词

feudal:/ˈfjuːdl/| adj.|封建的|Relating to, or characteristic of feudalism.|例句：Feudal society was strictly hierarchical. 封建社会等级森严。

例句：The feudal lord held vast estates.  封建领主拥有广阔的土地。|近义词：feudalist, feudatory|反义词：modern, democratic|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feudal, from Medieval Latin feudalis, from feudum "fief."
