categorises:/ˈkætəɡəraɪzɪz/
verb
分类，归类
divides things into categories; classifies.
The library categorises its books by genre.  图书馆按体裁对书籍进行分类。
She carefully categorises her expenses each month. 她每个月都仔细地分类她的开支。
classify, group, sort, order, arrange
decategorise, disorganise
词性:动词(第三人称单数)

categorise:/ˈkætəɡəraɪz/
verb
分类，归类
divides things into categories; classifies.
The library categorises its books by genre. 图书馆按体裁对书籍进行分类。
He categorises his music collection by artist. 他按艺术家对他的音乐收藏进行分类。
classify, group, sort, order, arrange
decategorise, disorganise, mingle
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *categorizare, from Ancient Greek κατηγορέω (katēgoreō, “I accuse, assert, predicate”), from κατηγόρος (katēgoros, “accuser”), from κατά (katá, “down, against”) + ἀγορεύω (agoreúō, “I speak in the assembly”).
