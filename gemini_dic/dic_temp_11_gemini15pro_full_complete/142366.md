orthophoria:/ˌɔːrθəˈfɔːriə/| n.|正视|The condition in which the visual axes of the two eyes are parallel when the muscles are relaxed, allowing for binocular vision without strain.  The eyes naturally point straight ahead at rest.|She was diagnosed with orthophoria, indicating perfect eye alignment.|Near synonyms: normal binocular vision|Antonyms: heterophoria, strabismus|noun
