loche:/lɒʃ/| n.|泥鳅，欧洲淡水鱼|A small freshwater fish of the loach family, found in Europe and Asia. It is often used as bait.|例句：The loche is a bottom-dwelling fish. 泥鳅是一种底栖鱼。

例句：He caught a loche while fishing in the river. 他在河里钓鱼时钓到了一条泥鳅。|近义词：groundling, mudfish|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French loche, from Frankish *lauka, from Proto-Germanic *laiko ("pond, pool"). Cognate with Old High German loh ("hole, cave"), German Lache ("puddle, pool").
