dermaptera:/dɜːrˈmæptərə/
n.
蠼螋，革翅目昆虫
An order of insects characterized by elongated bodies, short leathery forewings, and cerci modified into forceps-like structures at the tip of the abdomen.  They are also known as earwigs.
Earwigs (Dermaptera) are nocturnal insects found in moist environments. 蠼螋（革翅目）是一种夜行性昆虫，常见于潮湿的环境中。
Some species of dermaptera are known to exhibit maternal care. 一些种类的蠼螋表现出母性关怀。
近义词:earwig
反义词:None
词性:noun
