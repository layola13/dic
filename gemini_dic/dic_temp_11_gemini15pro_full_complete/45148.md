cudgeled:/ˈkʌdʒəld/ | v. | 棍棒殴打；反复思考 | To beat with a cudgel; to think about something deeply and repeatedly. | The witness testified that he saw the victim being cudgeled by the assailant.  证人作证说他看到受害者被袭击者用棍棒殴打。

He cudgeled his brains trying to remember where he had put the keys. 他绞尽脑汁想记起他把钥匙放在哪里了。 | 近义词：beat, batter, pummel, ponder, contemplate | 反义词：caress, soothe | 词性：动词

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/ | n. | 棍棒；短粗的棍子 | A short, thick stick used as a weapon. | The farmer used a cudgel to defend himself against the wild dog.  农民用棍棒保护自己免受野狗的攻击。| 近义词：club, stick, bludgeon | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *koggel*, from Old English *cycgel*, diminutive of *cucg*, a club.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kukjaz*.
