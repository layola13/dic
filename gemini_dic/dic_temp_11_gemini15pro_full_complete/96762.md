ineptly:/ɪˈnɛptli/
adverb
笨拙地，无能地
in an inept manner; clumsily; awkwardly.
He ineptly tried to fix the leaky faucet, making the problem even worse. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理漏水的水龙头，结果使问题更糟了。
近义词:clumsily, awkwardly, incompetently, foolishly
反义词:skillfully, adeptly, proficiently, expertly
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ineptus* "unfit, unsuitable," from *in-* "not" + *aptus* "fit, suitable."
