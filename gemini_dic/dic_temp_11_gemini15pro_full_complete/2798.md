advancers:/ədˈvænsərz/
n.
前进者；上升者；进展者
People or things that make progress or move forward.
The advancers in the race quickly took the lead.  比赛中领先的人迅速取得了领先地位。
The new technology benefited the advancers in the field.  这项新技术使该领域的领先者受益。
近义词：leaders, pioneers, innovators, developers
反义词：laggards, stragglers, decliners, retreaters
词性：名词

Etymology:
From advance (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).  Advance ultimately derives from Latin *abante, from ab- (from, away from) + ante (before).
