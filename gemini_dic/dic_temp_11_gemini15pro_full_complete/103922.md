judgelike: /ˈdʒʌdʒˌlaɪk/
adjective
像法官的；公正的
Resembling or characteristic of a judge, especially in being impartial and dignified.
He delivered his verdict in a judgelike manner.  他以法官般的方式宣布了他的判决。
近义词: judicial, impartial, objective, fair, unbiased
反义词: biased, prejudiced, partial, unfair, subjective
词性: adjective

judgelike: /ˈdʒʌdʒˌlaɪk/
noun
法官的举止；公正的行为
The manner or behavior characteristic of a judge, especially impartiality and dignity.
His judgelike demeanor commanded respect. 他的法官风范令人肃然起敬。
近义词: judiciousness, impartiality, objectivity, fairness
反义词: bias, prejudice, partiality, unfairness
词性: noun


词源:
From judge +‎ -like.  由judge（法官）和-like（像…的）组成。
