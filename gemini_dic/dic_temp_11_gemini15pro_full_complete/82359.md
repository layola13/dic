growlingly:/ˈɡraʊlɪŋli/
adverb|咆哮地，怒吼地|In a manner resembling a growl; angrily or menacingly. |The dog stared growlingly at the stranger.  狗咆哮着盯着陌生人。|近义词：angrily, menacingly, fiercely|反义词：gently, softly|词性: 副词
Etymology: From "growl" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Growl" ultimately comes from imitative roots.
