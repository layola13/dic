devotionary:/dɪˈvoʊʃəneri/ | adj. n. | 虔诚的；祈祷的；灵修的；祈祷书；灵修书 |  Relating to or used in religious worship; characterized by or expressing devotion; a book of prayers or religious meditations. | She reads her devotional every morning. 她每天早上读她的灵修书。| 近义词：religious, pious, devout, prayer book, spiritual | 反义词：irreligious, impious, profane, secular | 词性：adjective, noun

devotional:/dɪˈvoʊʃənl/ | n. | 祈祷式；灵修仪式；早祷会 | A religious service or meeting, especially one devoted to prayer or meditation. | The Sunday evening devotional was very inspiring.  星期天晚上的祈祷仪式非常鼓舞人心。| 近义词：service, prayer meeting, worship | 反义词：N/A | 词性：noun


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French devocionel, from late Latin devotionalis, from devotio(n-) ‘devotion’
