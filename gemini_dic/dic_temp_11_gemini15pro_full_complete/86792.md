hemodynamics:/ˌhiːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 血流动力学 | The study of the physical principles of blood circulation, including blood pressure, blood flow, and the resistance to blood flow. |  The patient's hemodynamics were unstable after the surgery.  手术后病人的血流动力学不稳定。| synonyms: circulatory dynamics | antonyms: N/A | noun
