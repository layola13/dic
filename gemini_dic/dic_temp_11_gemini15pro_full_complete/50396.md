deposing:/dɪˈpoʊzɪŋ/
verb
罢免; 免职; 废黜; 宣誓作证
The act of removing someone from a position of power, or the act of giving sworn testimony.
The king was deposed by his own son. 国王被他自己的儿子废黜了。

The witness is deposing in court. 证人在法庭上宣誓作证。

Synonyms: ousting, removing, dethroning, testifying, attesting
Antonyms: installing, appointing, electing, abstaining, recusing
