oligosiderite:/ˌɒlɪɡoʊˈsɪdəˌraɪt/
n.
橄榄陨铁，贫镍陨铁
An iron meteorite that is relatively poor in nickel.
Oligosiderite is a type of meteorite primarily composed of iron and nickel, but with a lower nickel content than other iron meteorites. 橄榄陨铁是一种主要由铁和镍组成的陨石，但其镍含量低于其他铁陨石。
Scientists discovered a large oligosiderite in the desert. 科学家在沙漠中发现了一块巨大的橄榄陨陨铁。
Synonym: iron meteorite, meteorite
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could use "terrestrial rock")
