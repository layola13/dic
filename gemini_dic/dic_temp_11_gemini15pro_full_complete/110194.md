lieve:/liːv/ |  suffix |相信, 亲爱的| part of words like "believe" and "relieve," meaning "to try to make something seem true or real," or "to free from." |  I believe in you. 我相信你。 | 近义词: (As a suffix, it doesn't have direct synonyms.  The words it forms have synonyms. E.g., for believe: trust, accept) | 反义词: (As a suffix, it doesn't have direct synonyms. The words it forms have antonyms. E.g., for believe: doubt, disbelieve)| suffix
