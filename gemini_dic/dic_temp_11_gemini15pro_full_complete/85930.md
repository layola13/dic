headlines:/ˈhedˌlaɪnz/ | n. |  新闻标题；头条新闻 |  Titles of news articles, often displayed prominently. | The headlines announced the president's resignation. 总统辞职的消息上了头条。 | synonyms: titles, captions, headings | antonyms: body text, details | noun

headlines:/ˈhedˌlaɪnz/ | v. |  担任主角；领衔主演 | To be the main performer or star in a show or event.  |  The band headlined the festival last year.  去年这支乐队是音乐节的领衔主演。 | synonyms: star, feature, top | antonyms: support, accompany | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "head" + "line," referring to the top line of a printed article.  源自“head”（头部）和“line”（线），指的是印刷文章的顶行。
