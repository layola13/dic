autowind:/ˌɔːtəˈwaɪnd/ | adj. n. |自动上链的；自动上弦的；自动上链装置|A mechanism, especially in a watch, that winds it automatically.| My new watch is autowind, so I don't have to wind it manually. 我的新手表是自动上链的，所以我不用手动上发条。 |Synonyms: self-winding, automatic | Antonyms: manual, hand-wound | adjective

autowind:/ˌɔːtəˈwaɪnd/ | n. |自动上链装置|A device or mechanism that winds something automatically, typically a watch.|The autowind on my watch broke, so now I have to wind it manually. 我手表的自动上链装置坏了，所以现在我必须手动上发条。|Synonyms: self-winding mechanism, automatic winding mechanism | Antonyms: manual winding mechanism| noun
