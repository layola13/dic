abandoned: /əˈbændənd/ | adj. | 被遗弃的，被抛弃的；放纵的 |  Forsaken or deserted.  No longer occupied or used.  Given up or discontinued. | The abandoned house stood on a lonely hill. 这栋废弃的房子矗立在荒凉的山丘上。| synonyms: deserted, forsaken, derelict, abandoned | antonyms: inhabited, occupied, cherished, claimed | adjective

abandoned: /əˈbændənd/ | n. |  被抛弃的人或物 |  Something that has been abandoned. (Often used in the plural.) | The streets were filled with the abandoned belongings of the refugees.  街道上到处都是难民遗弃的物品。| synonyms: rejects, castoffs, leftovers | antonyms: possessions, belongings | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abandoner*, from *a bandon* "at one's disposal, freely, entirely," literally "at the ban," from *ban* "proclamation, control, jurisdiction."
