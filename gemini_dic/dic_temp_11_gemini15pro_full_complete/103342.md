jilbab:/dʒɪlˈbɑːb/ | n. | 吉尔巴布，希贾布 | A long, loose-fitting outer garment worn by some Muslim women that covers the whole body except the face, hands, and feet. |  She wore a jilbab to the mosque. 她穿着一件吉尔巴布去了清真寺。

She chose a dark blue jilbab for everyday wear. 她选择了一件深蓝色的吉尔巴布作为日常穿着。 | 近义词: hijab, abaya, chador | 反义词: (No direct antonym, represents a choice of dress) | 词性: noun
