maladroit:/ˌmæləˈdrɔɪt/| adj.|笨拙的，不灵巧的|Lacking skill or dexterity; clumsy.|He made a maladroit attempt to catch the ball, tripping over his own feet. 他笨拙地试图接住球，结果被自己的脚绊倒了。|例句：His maladroit handling of the situation only made things worse. 他笨拙地处理情况，只会使事情变得更糟。

例句：She felt maladroit and awkward at the formal dinner. 在正式晚宴上，她感到笨手笨脚，很不自在。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, inept, ungainly|反义词：skillful, dexterous, adept, graceful|词性:形容词
