mimically:/ˈmɪmɪkli/| adv.|模仿地，模拟地|In a way that mimics or imitates something or someone else.  The action or behavior is done in a way that resembles or copies another.|She mimically repeated the teacher's gestures. 她模仿老师的动作。|Near Synonyms: imitatively, apishly, mockingly|Antonyms: genuinely, originally, sincerely|词性:副词
