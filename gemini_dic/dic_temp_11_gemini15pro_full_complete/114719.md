malleolus:/məˈliːələs/ (US), /məˈliːələs/ (UK)| n. |踝|A bony prominence on either side of the ankle.  It is the rounded distal end of the tibia or fibula.|
He fractured his lateral malleolus while playing basketball.|
近义词: anklebone|
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a body part)|
词性: noun


malleoli:/məˈliːəlaɪ/ (US), /məˈliːəlaɪ/ (UK)| n. |踝（复数）|The plural form of malleolus, referring to both bony prominences on either side of the ankle.|She injured both malleoli in a skiing accident.|
近义词: anklebones|
反义词: (No direct antonym for a body part)|
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *malleolus*, diminutive of *malleus* "hammer,"  referring to the hammer-like shape of the bone.
