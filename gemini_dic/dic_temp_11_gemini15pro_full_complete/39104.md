confrontationists:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənɪsts/
n.
对抗主义者；好斗分子
People who advocate or engage in confrontation, especially in political or social contexts.  Those who believe that social or political change should be achieved through direct action and challenging authority, often aggressively.
The confrontationists staged a protest outside the government building.  对抗主义者在政府大楼外举行了抗议。
The union leader was known for his confrontationist tactics.  这位工会领导人以其对抗性策略而闻名。
synonyms:  agitators, militants, firebrands, radicals, provocateurs
antonyms:  pacifists, mediators, conciliators, diplomats, moderates
noun

confrontationist:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənɪst/
adj.
对抗性的；好斗的
Characterized by or tending towards confrontation.  Describing an approach or attitude that is aggressive and seeks conflict.
The speaker's confrontationist tone alienated many in the audience.  演讲者对抗性的语气疏远了许多观众。
He adopted a confrontationist stance during the negotiations. 他在谈判中采取了对抗的姿态。
synonyms: aggressive, belligerent, combative, hostile, provocative
antonyms:  conciliatory, cooperative, diplomatic, peaceful, moderate
adjective
