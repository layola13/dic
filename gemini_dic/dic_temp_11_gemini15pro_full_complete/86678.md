hellhound:/ˈhelˌhaʊnd/| n. |地狱犬，猎犬|A mythical dog, often black and fiery, associated with the underworld.  It is often depicted as having glowing red eyes and supernatural powers.  It's a guardian of the underworld or a servant of the devil.|The hellhound guarded the gates of Hades, preventing any soul from escaping.|Synonyms: demon dog, Cerberus|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps guardian angel)|Noun
