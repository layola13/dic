greenkeeping: /ˈɡriːnˌkiːpɪŋ/
noun
草坪管理，草坪维护
The practice of maintaining grass surfaces, especially on golf courses or bowling greens, in good condition.
The greenkeeping staff do a fantastic job of maintaining the course.  草坪管理人员在维护球场方面做得非常出色。
The recent drought has made greenkeeping a real challenge.  最近的干旱使草坪维护成为一项真正的挑战。
近义词: turf management, groundskeeping
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑neglect of turf/grounds)
词性: n.

Etymology:
From green + keeping (from the verb keep).  "Green" in this context refers to a grassy area.
