grumped:/ɡrʌmpt/ | adj. |脾气暴躁的，抱怨的 | Feeling bad-tempered and irritable. | He grumped about the cold weather. 他抱怨天气冷。| 近义词：crabby, grumpy, irritable, sullen | 反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "grump" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The verb "grump" likely originated in the 19th century, possibly as an imitative word.
