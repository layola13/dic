anticlinal:/ˌæntiˈklaɪnəl/| adj. n.|背斜的，背斜|An upward fold in rock strata.  A geological structure in which rock layers are folded upwards, forming an arch-like shape.|例句：The anticlinal trap is a common place to find oil. 背斜圈闭是寻找石油的常见地点。

例句：The geologist identified an anticlinal fold in the mountain range. 地质学家在山脉中发现了一个背斜褶皱。|近义词：upfold, arch|反义词: synclinal|adjective, noun
