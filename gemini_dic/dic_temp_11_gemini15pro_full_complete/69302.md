febrifacient:/ˌfebrɪˈfeɪʃənt/| adj. n.| 发热的，致热的；发热剂 |Causing fever; an agent that produces fever. | Aspirin is a febrifacient. 阿司匹林是一种退烧药 (注意，这里虽然中文翻译是退烧药，但其英文释义是导致发烧的药剂，实际应用中阿司匹林是用来退烧的).  The doctor prescribed a febrifacient to induce a fever to fight the infection. 医生开了一种发热剂来引发发烧以对抗感染。 | 近义词: pyrogenic, fever-producing | 反义词: antipyretic, febrifuge | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From Latin *febrifaciens*, present participle of *febrifacere* "to make feverish," from *febris* "fever" + *facere* "to make."
