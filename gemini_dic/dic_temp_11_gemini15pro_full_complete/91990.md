hypernormally:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɔːrməli/ | adv. | 超常地，过度正常地 |  In a way that exceeds or goes beyond what is considered normal or typical.  It implies an exaggerated or heightened sense of normalcy, often to a point of artificiality or absurdity. |  The characters in the play behaved hypernormally, as if nothing was wrong, despite the impending doom.  剧中的人物表现得异常正常，好像什么也没错，尽管厄运即将降临。

The neighborhood was hypernormally quiet after the storm.  暴风雨过后，社区异常安静。 | 近义词: excessively, abnormally, unusually, extraordinarily | 反义词: normally, typically, ordinarily | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "hyper-" (Greek:  ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "normal" + "-ly".
