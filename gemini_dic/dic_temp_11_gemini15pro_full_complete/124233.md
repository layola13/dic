mossback: /ˈmɔːsbæk/ (US), /ˈmɒsbæk/ (UK)| n. |老顽固，守旧的人|A person who is stubbornly resistant to change and new ideas, often considered old-fashioned or out of touch.|例句：He's a real mossback, refusing to use a computer. 他真是个老顽固，拒绝使用电脑。

|例句：The mossbacks on the town council blocked the proposal for a new park. 市议会里的那些老顽固阻止了新建公园的提议。|近义词：diehard, reactionary, conservative, fuddy-duddy|反义词：progressive, liberal, innovator|词性: 名词
