inuredness:/ɪˈnjʊərdnɪs/ |n.|习惯；适应；麻木|The state of being inured, especially to hardship or pain; tolerance.  The soldiers showed remarkable inuredness to the harsh conditions.|He developed an inuredness to the constant criticism. 他对不断的批评逐渐麻木了。|近义词:tolerance, habituation, acclimatization,  hardiness|反义词:sensitivity, vulnerability, susceptibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inure (v.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Inure comes from Old French enurer "accustom, habituate," from en- "in" + user "to use."
