epoxying:/ɪˈpɒksiɪŋ/
n.|环氧树脂涂覆，环氧树脂粘合|The process of joining or coating something with epoxy resin.  |例句：The epoxying of the boat hull provided a waterproof seal.  船体环氧树脂涂层提供了防水密封。|近义词：gluing, bonding, adhering, cementing|反义词: detaching, separating
v.|用环氧树脂涂覆，用环氧树脂粘合|To join or coat something with epoxy resin. |例句：He was epoxying the two pieces of wood together. 他正在用环氧树脂将两块木头粘合在一起。|近义词：gluing, bonding, adhering, cementing|反义词: detaching, separating
