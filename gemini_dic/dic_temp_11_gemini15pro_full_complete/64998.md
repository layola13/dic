estrangednesses:/ɪˈstreɪndʒdɪsnɪsɪz/
|n.|疏远，隔阂|The state of being estranged; alienation.|例句：The estrangednesses between family members saddened everyone.  家庭成员之间的疏远让每个人都很难过。

例句：Years of silence had created an almost unbearable estrangedness between them.  多年的沉默在他们之间造成了几乎无法忍受的隔阂。|近义词：alienation, distance, remoteness, separation, disaffection|反义词：closeness, intimacy, nearness, togetherness|词性: 名词

词源: From estranged + -ness.  Estranged itself comes from the Old French estrange, meaning "foreign, strange."
