hed /hɛd/ | n. | 头部；头脑；领导；标题 | Head refers to the part of the body containing the brain, or the top part of something. It can also mean a leader or a heading. |  He hit his head on the low beam. 他撞到了低矮的横梁上。 | synonyms: chief, leader, mind, title | antonyms: tail, bottom, foot | noun

hed /hɛd/ | v. |  前往；率领；居…之首；用头顶 | Head means to go in a certain direction, to lead a group, to be at the top of something, or to hit something with your head. | They headed towards the mountains. 他们朝着山脉走去。 | synonyms: lead, direct, command, steer | antonyms: follow, trail, obey | verb
