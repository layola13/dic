outsonnets:/ˌaʊtˈsɑːnɪt/
verb
胜过…的十四行诗; 写十四行诗超过
To surpass in sonnet writing.
Shakespeare outsonneted all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的十四行诗超过了他所有的同时代人。
Near Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip
Antonyms:  be inferior to, fall short of
词性: verb
