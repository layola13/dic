gnathidium: /næˈθɪdiəm/
noun
颚基, 下颚
The chitinous basal part of the mouthparts of an insect or other arthropod.
The gnathiidium of this species of beetle is unusually large.  这种甲虫的颚基异常大。
近义词: mandible, jaw
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider anatomical opposites like "maxilla" - 上颚)
词性: n.
