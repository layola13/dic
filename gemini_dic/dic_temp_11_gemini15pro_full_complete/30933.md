chairman:/ˈtʃɛəmən/ (US), /ˈtʃɛəmən/ (UK)| n.|主席，董事长|A person who presides over a meeting, committee, board, etc.|The chairman called the meeting to order. 主席宣布开会。|近义词：president, chairperson, moderator|反义词:None|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ceapmann "merchant, trader" (attested only in compounds), from ceap "bargaining, business, price, purchase" + mann "man, person." The current sense, "presiding officer," arose in the 17th century.
