hypersentimental:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/| adj.|过度感伤的，过于多愁善感的|Excessively sentimental; overly emotional or nostalgic.|She wrote a hypersentimental poem about her childhood pet. 她写了一首关于她童年宠物的过度感伤的诗。|The movie was so hypersentimental that I couldn't help but roll my eyes. 这部电影太过于煽情，我不禁翻白眼。|近义词：oversentimental, mawkish, cloying, saccharine|反义词：unsentimental, pragmatic, stoic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + sentimental.  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ) meaning "over, above, beyond." "Sentimental" comes from the French "sentimental," ultimately from the Latin "sentire" (to feel).
