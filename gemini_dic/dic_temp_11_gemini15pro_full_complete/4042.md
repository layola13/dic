aidant:/eɪˈdɑːnt/ | adj. n. | 帮助的，辅助的；助手，护理员 |  A person who provides assistance and support to someone in need, especially with daily tasks due to age, illness, or disability.  | Example 1:  My grandmother has an aidant who helps her with bathing and dressing. 我祖母有一位助手帮助她洗澡和穿衣。

Example 2:  The aidant program provides much-needed support for families caring for elderly relatives.  护理员计划为照顾老年亲属的家庭提供了急需的支持。 | Synonym:  helper, assistant, caregiver, supporter | Antonym: antagonist, opponent, hinderer |  Word Type: Adjective, Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "aidant," present participle of "aider" (to help), from Latin "adiutare" (to help, assist).
