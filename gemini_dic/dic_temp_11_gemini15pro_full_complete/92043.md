hyperphalangy:/ˌhaɪpərˈfæləndʒi/
n.
多指（趾）畸形
The condition of having more than the normal number of phalanges (finger or toe bones).
Polydactyly is a form of hyperphalangy.  多指畸形是多指（趾）畸形的一种形式。
Whales exhibit hyperphalangy in their flippers.  鲸鱼的鳍状肢表现出多指（趾）畸形。
synonym: polydactyly,  
antonym: oligodactyly
noun
