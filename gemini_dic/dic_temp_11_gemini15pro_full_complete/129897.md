non-irishes:/nɒnˈaɪrɪʃɪz/ | n. |非爱尔兰人| People who are not Irish. |  Many non-irishes live in Ireland. 许多非爱尔兰人居住在爱尔兰。| synonyms: foreigners, non-natives | antonyms: Irish people, natives | noun

Etymology:
"non-" (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"Irish":  from Old English "Irisc," ultimately from Old Irish "Ériu" (name of Ireland)
"-es": plural suffix
