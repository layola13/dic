mindbendingly:/ˈmaɪndˌbɛndɪŋli/| adv.|令人费解地，极其复杂地|extremely complex and difficult to understand; requiring great mental effort.|The plot of the movie was mindbendingly intricate. 这部电影的情节错综复杂，令人费解。|近义词：bafflingly, bewilderingly, confusingly, perplexingly|反义词:simply, easily, clearly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "mind-bending" + "-ly". "Mind-bending" is a compound adjective formed from "mind" (referring to mental processes) and "bending" (from the verb "bend," meaning to curve or distort).  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.
