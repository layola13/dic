alunogen: /əˈluːnədʒən/ | n. | 毛矾石 | A hydrated aluminium sulfate mineral, Al2(SO4)3·17H2O, occurring as a white or tinted fibrous, granular, or massive efflorescence in shales and other rocks containing pyrite. | Alunogen is often found in areas with volcanic activity. 毛矾石经常在火山活动地区被发现。 | Synonyms: feather alum, hair salt | Antonyms: N/A | noun
