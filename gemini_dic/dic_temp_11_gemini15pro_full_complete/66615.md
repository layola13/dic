exiled:/ˈeksaɪld/| adj. v.|流亡的，放逐的；被流放的|forced to live in a foreign country|The exiled king lived a lonely life. 被流放的国王过着孤独的生活。|近义词：banished, deported, ostracized|反义词:repatriated|词性:形容词/动词

exile:/ˈeksaɪl/ | n. v. |流放，放逐；被流放者；流亡；放逐|the state of being barred from one's native country, typically for political or punitive reasons|He was forced into exile. 他被迫流亡。|近义词：banishment, deportation, expulsion|反义词:repatriation|词性:名词/动词

词源: From Old French exil, from Latin exilium (“banishment”), from exul (“exile”), from ex (“out of”) + solum (“soil, ground”).
