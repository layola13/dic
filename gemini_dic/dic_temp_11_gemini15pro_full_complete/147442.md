overobjectify:/ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/ | v. | 过度物化 | To treat someone (usually a woman) as an object, focusing excessively on their physical appearance and reducing them to their sexual attributes, rather than acknowledging their personality, intellect, or emotions. |  The advertising industry often overobjectifies women, portraying them as mere sex symbols to sell products. 广告业经常过度物化女性，将她们描绘成纯粹的性象征来销售产品。

| Example: Some critics argue that the film overobjectifies the female lead, focusing more on her body than her character development. 一些评论家认为这部电影过度物化了女主角，更多地关注她的身体而不是她的性格发展。 | Synonym: sexualize, objectify, dehumanize | Antonym: humanize, respect, value, personalize | verb
