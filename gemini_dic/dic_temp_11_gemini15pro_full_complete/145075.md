overangry: /ˌoʊvərˈæŋɡri/ | adj. | 过于愤怒的，暴怒的 | Excessively angry; angrier than is warranted or reasonable.  This word is not standard English and generally considered informal or nonstandard. It emphasizes the extreme nature of the anger. | He was overangry about the spilled milk. 他对洒出的牛奶过于生气了。|  She got overangry when her phone broke.  她手机坏了的时候勃然大怒。 | 近义词: furious, enraged, livid, infuriated | 反义词: calm, peaceful, tranquil | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "angry" (adjective).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "angry."
