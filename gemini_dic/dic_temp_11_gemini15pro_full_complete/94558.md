The painting was an imposture, a clever forgery of a Renaissance masterpiece. 这幅画是一幅赝品，是对文艺复兴时期杰作的巧妙伪造。| 近义词：fraud, deception, sham, charade, hoax | 反义词：honesty, genuineness, authenticity | 词性: 名词
