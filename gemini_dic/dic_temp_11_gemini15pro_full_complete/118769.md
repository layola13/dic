metallization:/ˌmɛtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|金属化，金属化处理|The process of coating a non-metallic surface with a layer of metal.  |The metallization of the plastic components improved their conductivity.  塑料部件的金属化提高了它们的导电性。|plating, coating|demetallization|noun


metallization:/ˌmɛtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|（天文学）金属丰度|The abundance of elements heavier than hydrogen and helium in a star or other celestial object. |The metallization of a star can provide clues to its age and formation history. 恒星的金属丰度可以提供关于其年龄和形成历史的线索。|abundance, enrichment|depletion|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From metal + -ization.  Metal comes from the Greek word "metallon," meaning "mine, quarry, metal."  The suffix "-ization" denotes the process of making or becoming something.
