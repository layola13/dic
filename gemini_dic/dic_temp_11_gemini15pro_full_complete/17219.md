bawdy: /ˈbɔːdi/ (US), /ˈbɔːdi/ (UK)| adj.|下流的，猥亵的|Indecent; dealing with sex in a way that is meant to be funny.| He told a bawdy joke. 他讲了一个下流的笑话。| 近义词:lewd, vulgar, obscene, indecent, ribald, coarse, crude, smutty, dirty| 反义词:clean, decent, proper, polite, refined|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *bawdy, from Old French baude (“bold, joyous”), from Frankish *bald (“bold, brave”). More at bold.  The semantic development to "lewd, obscene" is unclear, but possibly influenced by bawd (“prostitute, madam”).
