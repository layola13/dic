outreigns:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnz/
verb
比...统治时间长；统治时间超过
to reign longer than; to exceed in the length of one's reign
Queen Victoria outreigned all previous British monarchs. 维多利亚女王比以往任何一位英国君主的统治时间都长。
Synonyms: outlast, survive, surpass
Antonyms: precede, predate
词性:动词
