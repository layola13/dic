arboreality:/ˌɑːrbɔːriˈælɪti/
n.
树栖性，树栖生活
The state of living in trees.
Monkeys exhibit a high degree of arboreality. 猴子表现出高度的树栖性。
The arboreality of sloths allows them to access food sources unavailable to ground-dwelling animals.  树懒的树栖性使它们能够获得地面动物无法获得的食物来源。

近义词:tree-dwelling, arboreal lifestyle
反义词:terrestriality, ground-dwelling
词性:noun
