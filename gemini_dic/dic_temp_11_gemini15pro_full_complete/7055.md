anemography:/ˌænɪˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |风速风向记录；风向图；风力记录法|The study and recording of wind speed and direction, often with the use of instruments like anemometers and wind vanes.  It includes the creation and analysis of wind maps and charts.| The anemography of this region shows a consistent prevailing wind from the west. 这个地区的风向图显示盛行西风。|Synonyms:  wind recording, anemology |Antonyms: N/A | noun
