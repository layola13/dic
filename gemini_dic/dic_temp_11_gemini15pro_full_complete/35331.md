co-adore:/koʊ-əˈdɔːr/ | v. |共同崇拜，共同热爱 |To adore something or someone together with another person. |例句：They co-adore their favorite band and go to all their concerts together. 他们共同热爱他们最喜欢的乐队，一起去参加所有的演唱会。

例句：The twins co-adore their grandmother and call her every day. 这对双胞胎共同热爱他们的祖母，每天都给她打电话。 | 近义词：jointly adore, mutually adore | 反义词：mutually despise, jointly hate | 词性：动词


Etymology:  "Co-" (prefix) from Latin "com-" meaning "together" + "adore" from Old French "adorer"  and Latin "adorare" meaning "to worship."
