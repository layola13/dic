atonic:/əˈtɒnɪk/ /eɪˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|无重音的；不重读的；无力的|Lacking tone or accent; unaccented.  In phonetics and phonology, an atonic syllable is one that is not stressed.|The word "a" is often atonic in English sentences.|Synonyms: unaccented, unstressed, weak|Antonyms: tonic, stressed, accented|adjective

atonic:/əˈtɒnɪk/ /eɪˈtɑːnɪk/| n.|无重读音节；无力音|An unaccented syllable or word; a weak beat.|The atonic syllables in this verse create a sense of fluidity.|Synonyms: unstressed syllable|Antonyms: stressed syllable, tonic syllable|noun
