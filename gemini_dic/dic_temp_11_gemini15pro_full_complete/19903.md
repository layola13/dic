binoxide:/baɪˈnɒksaɪd/ /baɪˈnɑːksaɪd/
noun
二氧化物
a chemical compound containing two atoms of oxygen combined with another element or radical.
Manganese binoxide is used in dry cell batteries.  二氧化锰用于干电池。

Dioxide is a more frequently used and preferred term. Dioxide是一个更常用和更受欢迎的术语。

近义词: dioxide
反义词: 无

词性: noun (名词)
