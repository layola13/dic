nonelector:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktə(r)/
noun
非选举人，没有选举权的人
A person who is not entitled or allowed to vote in an election.
例句：Non-electors, such as felons and non-citizens, cannot participate in the democratic process. 非选举人，例如重罪犯和非公民，不能参与民主进程。

例句：The number of nonelectors in the country is surprisingly high due to various restrictions.  这个国家非选举人的人数由于各种限制而高得惊人。
近义词：disenfranchised, ineligible voter
反义词：voter, elector
词性：n.
