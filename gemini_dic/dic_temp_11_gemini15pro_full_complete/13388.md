atolls:/ˈætɒlz/ (British) /ˈætɑːlz/ (American)| n. |环礁，珊瑚岛|A ring-shaped coral reef including a coral rim that encircles a lagoon partially or completely.|例句：The Maldives are a chain of atolls in the Indian Ocean. 马尔代夫是印度洋上的一串环礁。

例句：Many atolls are found in the Pacific Ocean. 许多环礁都位于太平洋中。
