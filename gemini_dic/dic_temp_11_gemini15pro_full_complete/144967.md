overaccelerated: /ˌoʊvərəkˈsɛləˌreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度加速的；加速过快的 | Accelerated beyond a desirable or normal rate.  | The car overaccelerated, causing the driver to lose control. 汽车过度加速，导致司机失控。 |  The overaccelerated economic growth led to inflation. 过度加速的经济增长导致了通货膨胀。 | 近义词: excessively accelerated, overly accelerated | 反义词: decelerated, slowed | 词性: adjective

词源: over- + accelerated. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "accelerated" comes from the Latin "accelerare," meaning "to hasten."
