enfreeze:/ɪnˈfriːz/
verb
冻结，使凝固
to cause a liquid to become solid by cold; to solidify by freezing
The intense cold enfroze the lake. 严寒使湖水冻结了。

近义词:freeze, congeal, solidify
反义词:thaw, melt, liquefy
词性:v.
