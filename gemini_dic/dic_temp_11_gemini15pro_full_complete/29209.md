casino:/kəˈsiːnoʊ/ (US), /kəˈsiːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |赌场；娱乐场|A public building or room for gambling, especially one with roulette and other gambling tables.|例句：They spent the whole night at the casino. 他们在赌场待了一整晚。

例句：The casino offers a variety of games. 这家赌场提供各种各样的游戏。

近义词：gambling house, gaming house|反义词:|n.(名词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *casino* "small house," diminutive of *casa* "house," from Latin *casa* "hut, cottage." Originally referred to a small country villa, summerhouse, or social club. The meaning later expanded to include public buildings for music and dancing, and eventually to places for gambling.
