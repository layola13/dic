approvingly:/əˈpruːvɪŋli/
adverb
赞许地
In a way that shows approval.
She smiled approvingly at her son. 她赞许地对儿子笑了笑。
He nodded approvingly as he listened to the presentation. 他边听报告边赞许地点头。
近义词:favorably, positively, acceptingly
反义词:disapprovingly, critically, negatively
词性:adverb

词源:
From approve + -ly.  Approve comes from the Old French aprover, from Latin approbare, from ad- 'to' + probare 'prove, test'.
