contemporaneousness:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsnəs/ |n.|同时代；同时期；同期|The contemporaneousness of the two events is striking. 这两个事件的同时发生令人震惊。|English Explanation:  The state of existing or occurring at the same time.|例句：The contemporaneousness of the two writers' works, though they never met, is remarkable.  两位作家作品的同时期性，尽管他们从未谋面，也很引人注目。|近义词：simultaneity, synchronicity, coincidence|反义词：asynchrony, non-simultaneity|词性: noun

contemporaneous:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ |adj.|同时代的；同时期的；同期的|The two artists were contemporaneous. 这两位艺术家是同时代的。|English Explanation: Existing, occurring, or originating during the same time.|例句：The contemporaneous accounts of the battle differed widely.  关于这场战斗的同时期记载差异很大。|近义词：simultaneous, synchronous, coincident, concurrent|反义词：asynchronous, non-simultaneous|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contemporaneus, from con- 'together with' + tempus 'time'.
