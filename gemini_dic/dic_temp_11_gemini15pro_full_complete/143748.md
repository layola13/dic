outmanipulated:/ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjʊleɪtɪd/
verb
中文翻译：以策略胜过，精巧地控制
英文解析：To surpass or defeat someone by using more skillful or cunning manipulation or maneuvering.  It implies outsmarting someone through superior tactics, often in a competitive or strategic context.
例句：In the final round of negotiations, she outmanipulated her opponent and secured a favorable deal. 在最后一轮谈判中，她以策略胜过了对手，达成了有利的协议。

例句：The experienced chess player outmanipulated his younger rival, forcing him into checkmate. 经验丰富的棋手精巧地控制了他的年轻对手，迫使他将死。
近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted
词性：v.
