impracticable:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəbl/
adjective
不可行的，不切实际的
Not feasible or practical; impossible to carry out or put into practice.
The plan to build a bridge across the ocean proved impracticable.  建造一座跨越大洋的桥梁的计划被证明是不可行的。
Due to the heavy snow, the mountain pass became impracticable. 由于大雪，山口变得无法通行。
近义词: infeasible, unworkable, impossible, unattainable
反义词: feasible, practical, possible, achievable
词性: adjective

impractical:/ɪmˈpræktɪkl/
adjective
不切实际的，不实用的
Not suitable for use or action; not sensible or realistic.
He had lots of impractical ideas that would never work in real life. 他有很多不切实际的想法，在现实生活中永远行不通。
Wearing high heels while hiking is impractical.  徒步旅行时穿高跟鞋是不切实际的。
近义词: unrealistic, unreasonable, unsuitable
反义词: practical, realistic, sensible, suitable
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin impracticabilis, from in- 'not' + practicabilis 'able to be done'.
