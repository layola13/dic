objective: /əbˈdʒɛktɪv/ | adj. n. | 客观的；目标；物镜 | Based on facts rather than feelings or opinions; a goal or aim; a lens or system of lenses in a telescope or microscope. |  
例句：The journalist tried to write an objective account of the conflict. 记者试图对这场冲突进行客观的报道。
例句：Her main objective is to become a doctor. 她的主要目标是成为一名医生。

近义词：unbiased, impartial, fair, target, aim, goal | 反义词：subjective, biased, prejudiced | 词性: adjective, noun


objective: /əbˈdʒɛktɪv/ | n. | 目标 | A thing aimed at or sought; a goal. |
例句：Our objective is to win the championship. 我们的目标是赢得冠军。
例句：The company's primary objective is to increase profits. 公司的首要目标是增加利润。
