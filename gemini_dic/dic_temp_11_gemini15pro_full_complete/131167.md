nonbreach:/nɒnˈbriːtʃ/
n.
non-infraction,  observance (of a contract)
The contract included a clause addressing nonbreach.  该合同包含一项关于不违约的条款。
non-violation
The company's nonbreach of the agreement was confirmed. 公司对协议的遵守得到了确认。
近义词: compliance, observance, adherence
反义词: breach, violation, infringement
词性: 名词

Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "breach" (from Old French "breche," meaning "a break or rupture")
