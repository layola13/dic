commodious:/kəˈmoʊdiəs/
adjective
宽敞的，舒适的
(of a room or building) roomy and comfortable.
例句：The hotel room was surprisingly commodious.  酒店房间出乎意料地宽敞。

例句：They lived in a commodious house with a large garden.  他们住在一栋宽敞的房子里，带有一个大花园。

近义词：spacious, roomy, ample, capacious
反义词：cramped, small, confined, restricted
词性: adjective
