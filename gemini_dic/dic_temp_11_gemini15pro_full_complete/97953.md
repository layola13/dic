innuendo:/ˌɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ | n. | 含沙射影，影射，暗讽 | An indirect or subtle suggestion, remark, or hint, often with a derogatory or sexual connotation.  |  Her constant innuendos about his drinking problem made him uncomfortable. 她不断地暗示他的酗酒问题，让他很不舒服。 | Example:  The politician's speech was filled with innuendos about his opponent's personal life.  这位政治家的演讲充满了对他对手私生活的影射。 | Synonyms:  insinuation, implication, allusion, hint | Antonyms:  direct statement, explicit remark | noun
