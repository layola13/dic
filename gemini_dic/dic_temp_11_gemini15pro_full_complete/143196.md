outcountenance:/ˌaʊtˈkaʊntənəns/
verb
使...不安；使...愧色；使...胆怯
to make someone feel ashamed or embarrassed, especially by looking at them in a way that shows disapproval.
She was completely outcountenanced by his rudeness. 她被他粗鲁的举止弄得十分难堪。
He was outcountenanced by her stare. 她的凝视让他感到局促不安。
近义词: disconcert, abash, discomfit, rattle
反义词: embolden, encourage
词性: verb
