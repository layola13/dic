get-it-off: /ˌɡɛt ɪt ˈɒf/ (British) /ˌɡɛt ɪt ˈɔːf/ (American)
phrasal verb
摆脱，除去，完成，享受
To remove something, often with effort; to complete a task or duty; to experience pleasure or release.
I need to get this stain off my shirt. 我需要把这污渍从我的衬衫上弄掉。
He finally got the report off to his boss. 他终于把报告交给了他的老板。
She really got it off dancing at the club. 她在俱乐部跳舞很尽兴。
近义词: remove, finish, enjoy, complete
反义词: put on, start, begin
词性: phrasal verb
