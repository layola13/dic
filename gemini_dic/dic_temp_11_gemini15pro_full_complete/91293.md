hydrolyses:/haɪˈdrɒləˌsiːz/ /haɪˈdroʊləˌsiːz/| v.|水解，水解作用|The process by which a molecule is broken down into smaller molecules by reacting with water.|Complex carbohydrates are hydrolysed to simple sugars. 复杂碳水化合物被水解成单糖。| 近义词: decompose, break down | 反义词: synthesize, condense |词性: 动词 (verb)

hydrolysis:/haɪˈdrɒləsɪs/ /haɪˈdroʊləsɪs/| n.|水解，水解作用|The chemical breakdown of a compound due to reaction with water.|The hydrolysis of starch produces glucose. 淀粉的水解产生葡萄糖。|近义词: decomposition, breakdown | 反义词: synthesis, condensation |词性: 名词 (noun)
