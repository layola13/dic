comparableness:/kəmˈpɛərəblnəs/
n.
可比性
the quality of being comparable; similar enough to be compared
例句：The comparableness of the two products makes it difficult to choose. 这两种产品的可比性使得难以选择。

例句：The study examined the comparableness of different teaching methods. 这项研究检验了不同教学方法的可比性。

近义词：similarity, comparability, likeness
反义词：dissimilarity, difference, incomparability
词性：noun

词源：From comparable + -ness.  Comparable is derived from the Late Latin comparabilis, from comparare "to compare," from com- "together" + parare "to make ready, prepare."
