brazilin:/brəˈzɪlɪn/
noun
巴西木精；苏木色素
A red crystalline compound obtained from brazilwood, used as a dye and indicator.
Brazilin is a natural dye extracted from brazilwood. 巴西木精是从巴西木中提取的天然染料。
Brazilin is used as a pH indicator. 巴西木精被用作酸碱指示剂。
Synonyms: brazilwood extract, Natural Red 24
Antonyms: N/A
