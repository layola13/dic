blurbists:/ˈblɜːrbɪsts/
n.
吹捧者，广告宣传员
Someone who writes or publishes blurbs.
The blurbists hailed the new novel as a masterpiece.  吹捧者们称赞这部新小说是杰作。
The blurbists were enthusiastic about the author's latest work. 广告宣传员对这位作者的最新作品充满热情。
synonyms: publicists, promoters, hucksters
antonyms: critics, detractors
