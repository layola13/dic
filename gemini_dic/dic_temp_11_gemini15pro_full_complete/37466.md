commissioned: /kəˈmɪʃənd/ | adj.| 委任的；受委托的 | Officially appointed or authorized to perform a service or duty. | He is a commissioned officer in the army. 他是军队中一名受委任的军官。| synonyms: authorized, appointed, assigned | antonyms:  unofficial, unauthorized | adjective
