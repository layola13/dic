evocative:/ɪˈvɑːkətɪv/| adj.|唤起的，引起回忆的， gợi lên (ký ức, hình ảnh...)|Bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind.|The scent of woodsmoke is evocative of winter evenings by the fire. 木柴燃烧的气味让人想起冬夜围坐在火炉旁的情景。|近义词：reminiscent, suggestive, redolent|反义词：unremarkable, bland|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin ēvocātīvus, from ēvocāre (“to call out, summon”), from ē- (“out”) + vocāre (“to call”).
