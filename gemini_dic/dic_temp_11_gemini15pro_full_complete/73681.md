forgettable:/fərˈɡetəbl/| adj.|容易被忘记的；不值得记住的|Unlikely to be remembered; not important or special enough to be remembered.|The movie was so forgettable that I can't even remember the name of the main character. 这部电影太容易被忘记了，我甚至不记得主角的名字了。|Synonyms:unmemorable, insignificant, bland|Antonyms:memorable, unforgettable, noteworthy|adjective
