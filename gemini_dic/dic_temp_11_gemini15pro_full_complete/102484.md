jackstraws:/ˈdʒækˌstrɔːz/
noun
拾棍游戏；乱七八糟的东西
a game in which a pile of small sticks or thin strips of wood, bone, or plastic are dropped onto a table, and players try to remove them one by one without disturbing the others. Also refers to a confused or disordered mass of things.
The children were playing jackstraws on the floor. 孩子们在地板上玩拾棍游戏。
The contents of the suitcase were a jumble of jackstraws.  行李箱里的东西乱七八糟的。
synonyms: pickup sticks, spillikins, tumble-sticks, mess, jumble, tangle
antonyms: order, organization
词性: 名词
