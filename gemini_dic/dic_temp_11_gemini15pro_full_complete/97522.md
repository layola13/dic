ingorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ | v. | (on/with)  | 沉迷于，尽情享受 | to indulge oneself with something to excess | He ingorged himself on video games all weekend. 他整个周末都沉迷于电子游戏。| 近义词：indulge, revel | 反义词：abstain | verb
