indiums:/ˈɪndiəmz/| n. |铟（复数）|A soft, silvery-white metallic element, symbol In, atomic number 49.  It is a post-transition metal that is relatively rare in the Earth's crust. Indium is often used in LCD screens and solar cells. |Indiums are used as a coating for ball bearings. 铟被用作滚珠轴承的涂层。|Synonym: (No direct synonym for the element itself; can use "post-transition metal" as a category) |Antonym: (No direct antonym for an element)|noun
