flector:/ˈflektər/| n.|反射器;反射肌;屈肌|A device or surface that reflects something, especially light, heat, or sound. A muscle that bends a part of the body, such as a limb.|例句：The flector in the headlight directs the beam of light.  车前灯里的反射器引导光束。

例句：The flector muscles in his arm contracted, bending his elbow. 他手臂上的屈肌收缩，弯曲了他的肘部。|近义词：reflector, flexor|反义词:extensor|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flector, from flectere "to bend"
