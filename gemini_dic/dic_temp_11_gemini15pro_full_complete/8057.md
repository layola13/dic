anthropologist:/ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |人类学家| A person who studies humankind, especially its societies, cultures, and origins. |例句：The anthropologist spent years studying the tribe's customs.  这位人类学家花了数年时间研究该部落的习俗。

例句：Margaret Mead was a famous anthropologist known for her work in Samoa. 玛格丽特·米德是一位著名的人类学家，以其在萨摩亚的研究而闻名。|近义词：ethnologist, social scientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
