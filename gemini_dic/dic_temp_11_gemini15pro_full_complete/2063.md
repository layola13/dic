acutely:/əˈkjutli/
adjective
敏锐地，剧烈地
In a sharp or severe manner; intensely.
She felt the loss acutely. 她深深地感受到了失去的痛苦。
sharply, intensely, severely, keenly
mildly, slightly, gently, dully
词性:副词
