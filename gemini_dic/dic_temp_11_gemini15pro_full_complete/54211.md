disfellowship: /ˌdɪsˈfɛləʊʃɪp/ (UK), /ˌdɪsˈfɛloʊʃɪp/ (US)
n.
开除教籍；逐出教会；断绝关系
The formal act of expelling a person from a church or other religious community.
He was facing disfellowship from the church for his beliefs.  他因为自己的信仰面临被逐出教会。
The disfellowship caused a rift in the family.  这次开除教籍导致了家庭的分裂。
expulsion, excommunication, ostracism
inclusion, acceptance, fellowship
noun
