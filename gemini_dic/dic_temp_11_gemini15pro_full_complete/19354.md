bibliophilisms: /ˌbɪbliəˈfɪlɪzəmz/ | n. | 藏书癖；爱书癖；书籍收藏 | The love of books, especially as a collector or connoisseur.  It encompasses the collection, appreciation, and study of books, often with a focus on rare or beautiful editions. | He indulged his bibliophilisms by purchasing first editions of classic novels. 他通过购买经典小说的初版来满足他的藏书癖。 | Synonyms: book collecting, bibliomania | Antonyms: bibliophobia | noun
