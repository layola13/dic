cajole:/kəˈdʒoʊl/ /kəˈdʒəʊl/
verb
哄骗；劝诱
To persuade someone to do something by sustained coaxing or flattery.
He cajoled her into going to the party with him. 他哄骗她和他一起去参加派对。
She cajoled him into buying her a new car. 她劝诱他给她买一辆新车。
近义词: coax, wheedle, persuade, entice
反义词: dissuade, discourage, coerce
词性: verb

cajole:/kəˈdʒoʊl/ /kəˈdʒəʊl/
词源: From French cajoler, of uncertain origin.
