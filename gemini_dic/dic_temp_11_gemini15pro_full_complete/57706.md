drayage:/ˈdreɪɪdʒ/| n.|短途运输；搬运费|The cost of drayage from the port to the warehouse was significant. 从港口到仓库的短途运输费用很高。|Drayage involves transporting goods over a short distance, often within a port or terminal area. 短途运输是指在短距离内运输货物，通常是在港口或码头区域内。|例句：The drayage fee is included in the total shipping cost. 短途运输费包含在总运费中。

The company outsourced its drayage operations to a third-party logistics provider.  这家公司将其短途运输业务外包给第三方物流供应商。|近义词：cartage, haulage, transportation|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *drayage*, from *drayer* "to haul, to transport in a dray," from *dray* "a low cart for heavy loads," from Old North French *trai*, from Middle Dutch *trade*, from Old English *træde* "tread, step." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *tradjaną* "to tread."
