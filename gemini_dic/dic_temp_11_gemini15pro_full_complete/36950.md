colouring:/ˈkʌləriŋ/ (UK) /ˈkʌlərɪŋ/ (US)| n. |着色，涂色，染色；颜料，色素；（尤指食品的）色素，着色剂|The process of adding colour to something, or the colours added. |The children were enjoying their colouring. 孩子们很喜欢涂色。|近义词：coloring (US), pigmentation, dye, tint |反义词: bleaching, fading |词性: noun

colouring:/ˈkʌləriŋ/ (UK) /ˈkʌlərɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |着色的，涂色的|Used for colouring.  |Colouring pencils 彩色铅笔 |近义词：tinting, dyeing|反义词: bleaching, fading |词性: adjective
