middle-of-the-roadisms:/ˌmɪdl əv ðə ˈroʊdɪzəmz/
|n.|中间路线主义，中庸之道|The prevalence of middle-of-the-roadisms in political discourse can hinder progress. 政治话语中充斥着中庸之道可能会阻碍进步。|
|英文解析：Middle-of-the-roadisms refers to the tendency to avoid extreme positions and adopt moderate or centrist views, often characterized by vagueness and a lack of strong commitment. It can manifest in political discourse, personal opinions, or artistic expressions.|
|例句：
His middle-of-the-roadisms frustrated both sides of the debate. 他的中庸之道让辩论双方都感到沮丧。

The candidate's middle-of-the-roadisms failed to inspire voters.  这位候选人的中庸立场未能激励选民。
|近义词：centrism, moderation, neutrality|
|反义词：extremism, radicalism, partisanship|
|词性：名词|
