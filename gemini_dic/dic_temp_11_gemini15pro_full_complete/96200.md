indevote:/ˌɪndɪˈvoʊt/ | adj. |  挚爱的，忠诚的，全心投入的 | Dedicated or devoted to something, especially a cause or ideal.  Highly devoted; ardent. |  She is indevote to her work as a teacher. 她对教师工作充满热情。| 近义词: devoted, dedicated, committed, zealous | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From in- + devote.  The prefix "in-" in this case intensifies the meaning of "devote."  "Devote" comes from the Latin "dēvōtus," past participle of "dēvovēre"  meaning "to vow, dedicate."
