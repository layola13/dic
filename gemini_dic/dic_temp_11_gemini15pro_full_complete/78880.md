ginkgos: /ˈɡɪŋkoʊz/ | n. |银杏树（复数）| The plural form of "ginkgo," referring to more than one ginkgo tree.  Ginkgo trees are known for their unique fan-shaped leaves and medicinal properties. |  The ginkgos in the park turn a beautiful golden color in autumn. 公园里的银杏树在秋天变成了美丽的金色。|  Synonyms: ginkgo trees (more common in plural usage) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

ginkgos: /ˈɡɪŋkoʊz/ | n. |银杏果/白果（复数）| The plural form of "ginkgo," also referring to the seeds of the ginkgo tree, often used in Asian cuisine. | Ginkgos are a popular ingredient in some Chinese dishes. 白果是一些中国菜肴中常用的食材。 | Synonyms: ginkgo nuts, ginkgo seeds | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
