contractiblenesses:/kənˌtræktɪˈbɪlɪtɪsɪz/
n.
收缩性；收缩能力
The quality or property of being contractible; the capability of shortening, especially of muscle fibers.
Smooth muscle contractibleness plays a vital role in regulating blood flow. 平滑肌的收缩性在调节血流中起着至关重要的作用。

The heart's contractiblenesses are essential for pumping blood throughout the body.  心脏的收缩能力对于将血液泵送到全身至关重要。

近义词: contractility, contractile power
反义词: inextensibility, rigidity
词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From contractible + -ness.  "Contractible" comes from Late Latin *contractibilis, from Latin contractus (past participle of contrahere "to draw together, contract"), from com- "together" + trahere "to draw".  "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns of state or quality.
