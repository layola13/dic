doorstepping: /ˈdɔːrˌstɛpɪŋ/ | n. | 上门采访；上门推销 | The act of going to someone's home, often unexpectedly, to try to talk to them, especially a journalist or salesperson. | Reporters were doorstepping the politician at his home. 记者们在他家门口堵截采访这位政治家。| synonyms: canvassing, soliciting, cold-calling | antonyms: avoiding, ignoring | noun

doorstepping: /ˈdɔːrˌstɛpɪŋ/ | v. | 上门采访；上门推销 | To go to someone's home, often unexpectedly, to try to talk to them, especially a journalist or salesperson. |  The charity workers were doorstepping residents to ask for donations. 慈善工作者挨家挨户上门募捐。| synonyms: canvassing, soliciting, cold-calling | antonyms: avoiding, ignoring | verb
