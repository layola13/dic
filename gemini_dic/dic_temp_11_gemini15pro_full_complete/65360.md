eulogizer: /ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzər/
n.
颂扬者, 赞美者
One who delivers a eulogy; one who praises highly.
The eulogizer spoke eloquently about the deceased's contributions to society.  颂扬者雄辩地讲述了死者对社会的贡献。
The eulogizer painted a vivid picture of the deceased's life. 颂扬者生动地描绘了死者的一生。
Synonyms: praiser, encomiast, lauder
Antonyms: critic, detractor, slanderer
noun
