colonial:/kəˈloʊniəl/ | adj. n. |殖民的，殖民地的；殖民地居民|Relating to a colony or colonies; characteristic of a colony or colonies.  A person who lives in a colony.|例句：The British colonial empire was vast.  大英帝国幅员辽阔。|近义词：imperial, dependent, subject |反义词:independent, sovereign |词性:adjective, noun

colonial:/kəˈloʊniəl/ | n. |殖民地居民|A person who lives in a colony.|例句：Many colonials felt oppressed by the ruling power. 许多殖民地居民感到被统治势力压迫。|近义词：settler, colonist |反义词:native, indigenous |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coloniālis, from Latin colōnia (“farm, landed estate, settlement, colony”) + -ālis (adjectival suffix).  The Latin colōnia is from colōnus (“farmer, husbandman, settler, colonist”), from colō (“I till, cultivate, inhabit”).
