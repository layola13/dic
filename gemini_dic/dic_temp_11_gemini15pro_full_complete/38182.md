comptometer:/kəmˈpɑːmɪtər/
noun
计算器
A key-driven calculating machine that adds, subtracts, multiplies, and divides.
She used a comptometer to quickly add up the sales figures. 她用计算器快速地加总了销售额。
The accountant relied on his comptometer for accurate calculations. 会计师依靠他的计算器进行精确的计算。
synonyms: calculator, adding machine
antonyms: abacus, slide rule
n.
