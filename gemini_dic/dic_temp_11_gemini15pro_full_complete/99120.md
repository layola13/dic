intercedes:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdz/| v. |调解，斡旋，说情|to plead with someone in authority on behalf of another person, especially someone in trouble or disgrace; to intervene on behalf of another|例句：The lawyer interceded with the judge to get a lighter sentence for his client. 律师向法官求情，希望他的当事人能获得轻判。

例句：When the two boys were fighting, their teacher interceded to stop them. 当两个男孩打架时，他们的老师介入阻止了他们。|近义词：mediate, intervene, negotiate, arbitrate|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词
