extendedly:/ɪkˈstɛndɪdli/ | adv. | 延伸地，扩展地 | In a way that covers a large area or period of time; at length. | He spoke extendedly on the subject. 他就这个主题详细地进行了阐述。 | 近义词: lengthily, comprehensively, broadly, widely | 反义词: briefly, concisely, shortly | 词性:副词

词源:  From "extended" + "-ly". "Extended" comes from the Latin "extendere," meaning "to stretch out."
