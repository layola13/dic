hoodwinked:/hʊdˈwɪŋkt/
verb
欺骗，蒙蔽
deceived or tricked
The salesman hoodwinked us into buying a used car that didn't even run.  推销员骗我们买了一辆根本不能开的二手车。
Synonyms: deceived, tricked, duped, fooled, swindled
Antonyms: enlighten, inform, undeceive
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hood + wink, literally "to blindfold."  The "hood" part refers to a falcon's hood, used to cover its eyes and keep it calm.  The "wink" part refers to closing one's eyes.
