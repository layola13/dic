colluvies:/kəˈluːviːz/
noun
冲积物，崩积物
A heterogeneous mixture of rock fragments and soil deposited by rainwash, sheetwash, or slow continuous downslope creep, usually collecting at the base of slopes or in valley bottoms.
Heavy rains caused colluvies to flow down the mountainside.  暴雨导致崩积物顺着山坡流下。
近义词:talus, debris, scree
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps bedrock)
词性:n.


colluvies:/kəˈluːviːz/
noun
（医学）腐烂组织
Decomposed or disintegrated tissue. (Medical context)
The wound was filled with colluvies.  伤口里充满了腐烂的组织。
近义词:debris, detritus
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps healthy tissue)
词性:n.
