obvert:/əbˈvɜːrt/ /ɑːbˈvɜːrt/
verb
反转；变换；使转化
To transform (a proposition) into an equivalent proposition in which the subject and predicate are interchanged and the quality is changed. To turn inside out or upside down.
The proposition "No A is B" is obverted to "All A is non-B."
近义词: invert, reverse, transform, transpose
反义词: maintain, preserve, uphold
词性: verb
