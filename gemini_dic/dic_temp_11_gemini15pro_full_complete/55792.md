diverticular:/ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊlər/| adj.|憩室的|Relating to or characterized by diverticula (small pouches that can form in the lining of the digestive system, especially the colon).|Diverticular disease is a common condition affecting the large intestine.|近义词:pouched|反义词:smooth|词性:形容词

diverticulum:/ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊləm/| n.|憩室|A small pouch or sac that protrudes from a hollow organ, especially the colon.|The doctor found several diverticula in her colon during the colonoscopy.|近义词:pouch, sac|反义词:(None - anatomical structure)|词性:名词
