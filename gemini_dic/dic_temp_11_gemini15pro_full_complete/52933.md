dinaphthyl:/daɪˈnæfθɪl/| n. |二萘基|A radical formed from naphthalene by removal of a hydrogen atom, C₁₀H₇.  It forms dinaphthyl compounds, such as 1,1'-dinaphthyl.|1,1'-Dinaphthyl was used as a starting material in the synthesis of a new family of ladder polymers.  1,1'-二萘基被用作合成新型梯形聚合物家族的起始原料。

|近义词:binaphthyl (especially for 1,1'-dinaphthyl)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
