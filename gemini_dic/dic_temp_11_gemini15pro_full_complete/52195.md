diaspora:/daɪˈæspərə/ (US), /daɪˈæspərə/ (UK)| n. |大流散，散居群体|The dispersion or spreading of something that was originally localized, especially a scattering of people from their homeland.  It often refers to the Jewish diaspora, but can also be used more broadly. |The African diaspora has significantly influenced cultures around the world. 非洲大流散极大地影响了世界各地的文化。

The Armenian diaspora is spread across many countries. 亚美尼亚侨民遍布许多国家。|近义词：dispersion, scattering, migration, exile|反义词：concentration, gathering, repatriation|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *diaspora "a scattering, dispersion," from diaspeirein "to scatter," from dia- "across, through" + speirein "to sow, scatter."
