lucubration:/ˌluːkjuˈbreɪʃn/
n.
苦心研究；冥思苦想；(尤指夜晚)刻苦钻研的成果；学究式的著作
laborious study, especially at night; the product of such study, especially a scholarly or pedantic piece of writing
After years of lucubration, he finally finished his magnum opus. 经过多年的苦心钻研，他终于完成了他的巨著。
Synonyms: study, meditation, contemplation, deliberation, rumination
Antonyms: idleness, inactivity, procrastination
noun
