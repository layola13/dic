materiarian:/məˌtɪəriˈɛəriən/ | n. | 材料学家；物质主义者 | A person who studies or specializes in materials science or a person who believes that material possessions are the most important things in life. | 例句：The materiarian was fascinated by the properties of the new alloy.  这位材料学家对这种新合金的特性着迷。

例句：He's a real materiarian; he only cares about money and possessions. 他是个真正的物质主义者；他只在乎金钱和财产。 | 近义词：(for scientist) material scientist, engineer; (for philosophy) materialist, consumerist | 反义词：(for scientist) N/A; (for philosophy) spiritualist, ascetic | 词性: noun
