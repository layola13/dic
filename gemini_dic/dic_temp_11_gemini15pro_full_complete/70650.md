filthy:/ˈfɪlθi/| adj.|肮脏的，污秽的；卑鄙的|Extremely dirty and unpleasant; morally disgusting and offensive.|The kitchen was absolutely filthy. 厨房简直脏透了。|The politician used filthy language during the debate.  这个政客在辩论中使用了污言秽语。|近义词：dirty, foul, squalid, disgusting, vile|反义词：clean, pure, spotless|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *fylþig, from fylþ (“filth”). Cognate with West Frisian fylzich (“filthy”), Dutch vuil (“dirty”), German faul (“rotten, lazy”).
