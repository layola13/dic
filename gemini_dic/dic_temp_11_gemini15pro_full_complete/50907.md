deserved: /dɪˈzɜːrvd/ | adj. | 应得的，理所当然的 | Earned or merited; suitable as a consequence of one's actions or qualities. | He received the deserved praise for his bravery. 他因勇敢而获得了应得的赞扬。 | Synonyms: earned, merited, justified, due, right, appropriate | Antonyms: undeserved, unmerited, undue, unjustified | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *deserven*, from Old French *deserver*, from Latin *dēservīre* (“to serve well, deserve”), from *dē-* (“fully, completely”) + *servīre* (“to serve”).
