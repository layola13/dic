birectangular:/baɪˌrɛkˈtæŋɡjʊlər/
adjective
双矩形的；具有两个直角的
Having two right angles.  Specifically, in spherical geometry, a spherical triangle having two right angles.
A birectangular spherical triangle is formed by two meridians and the equator.  这个双矩形球面三角形是由两条子午线和赤道构成的。
The navigation problem involved calculations with a birectangular triangle.  这个导航问题涉及到双矩形三角形的计算。
近义词:right-angled (in plane geometry)
反义词:oblique
词性:adjective
