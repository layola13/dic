incommensurablenesses:/ɪnkəmɛnʃərəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
|n.|不可通约性；无法比较性|The state or quality of being incommensurable; inability to be measured by the same standard or compared on the same scale. |The incommensurablenesses of their worldviews made communication difficult. 他们的世界观不可通约，使得沟通变得困难。|Synonyms: disparity, incompatibility, incongruity, difference, divergence|Antonyms:  commensurability, compatibility, similarity, comparability|Noun


incommensurability:/ɪnkəmɛnʃərəˈbɪləti/
|n.|不可通约性；无法比较性|The state or quality of being incommensurable; inability to be measured by the same standard or compared on the same scale. |The incommensurability of their values led to conflict. 他们的价值观不可通约，导致了冲突。|Synonyms: disparity, incompatibility, incongruity, difference, divergence|Antonyms:  commensurability, compatibility, similarity, comparability|Noun


incommensurable:/ɪnkəmɛnʃərəbl/
|adj.|不可通约的；不能比较的|Not able to be judged by the same standard as something else; having no common standard of measurement. |The two systems of thought are incommensurable. 这两种思想体系是不可通约的。|Synonyms: incompatible, disparate, disproportionate, unequal, dissimilar|Antonyms: commensurable, comparable, similar, proportionate, equal|Adjective
