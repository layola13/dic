minimalist: /ˈmɪnɪməlɪst/ | n. adj. | 极简主义者；极简主义的 | A person who adopts a minimalist lifestyle, characterized by owning only essential possessions and living simply; or something characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. |  She's a minimalist and her apartment is sparsely furnished. 她是一位极简主义者，她的公寓里只有很少的家具。 | synonyms: ascetic, simple, essentialist | antonyms: maximalist, extravagant, lavish | noun

minimalist: /ˈmɪnɪməlɪst/ | adj. | 极简主义的 | Characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. | The minimalist design of the website makes it easy to navigate.  这个网站的极简主义设计使它易于浏览。 | synonyms: simple, uncluttered, spare | antonyms: ornate, elaborate, complex | adjective
