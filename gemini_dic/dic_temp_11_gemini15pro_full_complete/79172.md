glamorous: /ˈɡlæmərəs/ | adj. | 迷人的，富有魅力的 | Attractive in an exciting and special way |  She led a glamorous life, traveling the world and meeting famous people. 她过着光鲜亮丽的生活，周游世界，结识名人。|  例句：The actress looked glamorous in her designer gown.  这位女演员穿着她的名牌礼服，看起来光彩照人。| 近义词：alluring, charming, fascinating, stylish | 反义词：dull, drab, unglamorous | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "grammar" influenced by "glamour". Originally, "grammar" was associated with occult learning and magic, hence the connection.  "Glamour" itself comes from the Scots word "gramarye," meaning magic, enchantment, or spell.
