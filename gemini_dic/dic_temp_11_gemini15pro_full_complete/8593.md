anticlericalist:/ˌæntɪˈklɛrɪkəlɪst/
n.|反教权主义者；反对神职人员者|A person who opposes the power or influence of the clergy in secular affairs.|The anticlericalist movement gained momentum during the French Revolution. 法国大革命期间，反教权主义运动势头强劲。|近义词:secularist, freethinker|反义词:clericalist, devout|词性:名词
