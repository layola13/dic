deliberatively:/dɪˈlɪbərətɪvli/
adverb
故意地；慎重地；不慌不忙地
Done consciously and intentionally.
She deliberatively chose to ignore his advice. 她故意不理会他的建议。
He placed each piece of the puzzle deliberatively. 他不慌不忙地放好每一块拼图。
Synonyms: intentionally, purposely, carefully, slowly, cautiously
Antonyms: hastily, rashly, carelessly, impulsively
词性: 副词
