misjudger:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒər/| n.|误判者，判断错误的人|A person who judges wrongly or incorrectly. | He proved to be a misjudger of character, trusting the wrong people. 他证明了自己对性格判断错误，相信了错误的人。|She was a misjudger of talent, letting the real star slip away. 她对才能判断失误，让真正的明星溜走了。|近义词：misinterpreter, miscalculator|反义词: expert, connoisseur|词性: noun
