counter-elite:/ˌkaʊntərɪˈliːt/ | n. | 反精英；反建制派 | A group or movement that opposes the established elite or ruling class.  They often challenge the existing power structures and advocate for alternative forms of governance or social organization. | The rise of populist, counter-elite movements has disrupted traditional politics in many countries.  反民粹主义的反精英运动扰乱了许多国家的传统政治。

The counter-elite candidate promised to dismantle the corrupt political system.  这位反精英候选人承诺要瓦解腐败的政治体系。 | 近义词: anti-establishment, populist, insurgent | 反义词: establishment, elite, ruling class | 词性: 名词
