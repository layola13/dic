aerenchyma:/ˌɛərˈɛŋkɪmə/| n.|通气组织|A spongy tissue with large intercellular air spaces, found especially in many aquatic plants.  It allows for the exchange of gases between the shoot and the root.|例句：The aerenchyma in the stems of water lilies helps them float.  睡莲茎中的通气组织帮助它们漂浮。

例句：Mangroves rely on aerenchyma for gas exchange in their waterlogged roots.  红树林依靠通气组织在其浸水的根部进行气体交换。|近义词：aerating tissue|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + ἔγχυμα (énchyma, “infusion”).
