obmutescence:/ˌɒbmjuːˈtɛsəns/
n.
沉默，缄默，无声
The state of being silent or speechless; muteness.
After the shocking news, a heavy obmutescence fell over the room.  震惊的消息传来后，房间里一片沉寂。
The witness's sudden obmutescence during the trial was concerning.  审判期间证人突然沉默不语，令人担忧。
silence, muteness, speechlessness
loquacity, garrulity, talkativeness
