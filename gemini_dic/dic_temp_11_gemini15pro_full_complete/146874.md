overinfluences:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluənsɪz/
|v.|过度影响|To influence excessively or too much.|Social media overinfluences young people's fashion choices. 社交媒体过度影响了年轻人的时尚选择。|The constant barrage of advertisements overinfluences consumers' spending habits.  持续不断的广告轰炸过度影响了消费者的消费习惯。|近义词: dominates, controls, manipulates|反义词: underinfluences (not a standard word), neglects|verb

overinfluence:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluəns/
|n.|过度影响|Excessive influence.|The overinfluence of celebrities on public opinion is a concern. 名人对公众舆论的过度影响令人担忧。|Parents worry about the overinfluence of violent video games on their children. 父母担心暴力电子游戏对孩子的过度影响。|近义词: dominance, control, manipulation|反义词:  lack of influence, neglect|noun
