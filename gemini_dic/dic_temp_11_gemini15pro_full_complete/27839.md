canonical:/kəˈnɒnɪkəl/ | adj. |规范的；经典的；权威的；圣典的 | Following the accepted rules or standards; established as genuine or authentic; included in the biblical canon. |

例句：
The canonical Gospels are Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John.
中文翻译：规范的福音书是马太福音、马可福音、路加福音和约翰福音。

Shakespeare's plays are considered canonical works of English literature.
中文翻译：莎士比亚的戏剧被认为是英国文学的经典作品。

近义词：standard, accepted, authoritative, orthodox, established, authentic
反义词：unorthodox, apocryphal, unconventional, heterodox
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin canonicus ‘according to church law’, from Greek kanōnikos, from kanōn ‘rule’.
