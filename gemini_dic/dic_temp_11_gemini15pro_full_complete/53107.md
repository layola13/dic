diphthongous:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəs/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋɡəs/| adj.|双元音的；包含双元音的|Containing a diphthong; of the nature of a diphthong.|The word "loud" has a diphthongous sound.  “loud”这个词有一个双元音。|diphthongal|monophthongal|adjective
diphthong:/ˈdɪfθɒŋ/ /ˈdaɪfθɑːŋ/| n.|双元音|A sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another (as in coin, loud, and side ).|The word "coin" contains the diphthong /ɔɪ/. “coin”这个词包含双元音/ɔɪ/。|vowel combination, vowel glide||noun
