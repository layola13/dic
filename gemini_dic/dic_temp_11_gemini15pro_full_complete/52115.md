diamorphine:/ˌdaɪəˈmɔːrfiːn/
noun
二乙酰吗啡；海洛因
A highly addictive narcotic analgesic drug derived from morphine.  It is a controlled substance in most countries due to its potential for abuse and dependence.
Diamorphine is a powerful opioid painkiller. 二乙酰吗啡是一种强效的阿片类止痛药。
Diamorphine is illegal in many countries.  海洛因在许多国家是非法的。
Synonym: heroin, morphine
Antonym: naloxone (an opioid antagonist)
词性: noun
