diversifier:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪər/| n.|多元化者，多样化因素|Something that diversifies, especially a business or investment that reduces risk by spreading capital across different areas.|The company is a major diversifier in the agricultural sector, investing in everything from crops to livestock.|近义词:investor, stakeholder, distributor|反义词:specialist, concentrator|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "diversify," which comes from the Late Latin "diversificare," meaning "to make diverse" or "to vary," from "diversus" (diverse) + "-ficare" (making).
