gradient:/ˈɡreɪdiənt/| n.|梯度，坡度，倾斜度|A gradient is a slope or an incline. It can refer to the degree of inclination of a slope or the rate of change of a quantity (like temperature or pressure) over a distance or other variable. |The cyclist struggled up the steep gradient.  骑自行车的人艰难地爬上陡峭的斜坡。

The temperature gradient between the two rooms was significant. 两个房间之间的温度梯度很大。|synonyms: slope, incline, grade, slant|antonyms: level, flat|noun

gradient:/ˈɡreɪdiənt/| adj.(rare, usually technical)|渐变的，梯度的|Gradually changing; having a gradient. |The fabric had a gradient color, shifting from blue to green.  这种织物颜色渐变，从蓝色到绿色。|synonyms: graded, gradual|antonyms: uniform, constant|adjective
