overplanned:/ˌoʊvərˈplænd/| adj.|过度计划的，计划过密的|Planned in excessive detail or to an excessive degree.  The trip was overplanned, leaving no room for spontaneity.|例句：The itinerary was so overplanned that we felt rushed and didn't get to enjoy anything fully.  行程安排得过于紧密，我们感觉很匆忙，没能尽情享受任何东西。

例句：The wedding was overplanned and lacked the charm of a more spontaneous celebration. 婚礼计划过度，缺乏更自然庆祝的魅力。|近义词：overorganized, over-scheduled, regimented|反义词：underplanned, flexible, spontaneous|词性:形容词
