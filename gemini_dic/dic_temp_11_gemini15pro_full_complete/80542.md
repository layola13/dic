goodly:/ˈɡʊdli/| adj.|美好的，漂亮的；相当大的，相当多的|pleasing and wholesome in appearance; considerable in size, quantity, or extent|She inherited a goodly sum of money. 她继承了一大笔钱。|近义词：attractive, pleasant, substantial, considerable|反义词：unattractive, unpleasant, meager, insignificant|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *godeli, *goodeli, from Old English gōdlīc (“goodly, beautiful, excellent, virtuous, kind, gentle, noble, honorable, considerable, ample”), equivalent to good +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots guidlie (“goodly, handsome, pleasant”), West Frisian goedlik (“goodly, benevolent, kind”), Dutch goedertieren (“charitable”), German gütig (“kind”).
