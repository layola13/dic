atwixt:/əˈtwɪkst/
adverb|在...之间；在两者之间|between; in the midst of|例句：The house is situated betwixt the river and the road. 这座房子位于河流和道路之间。|近义词：between, amid, amongst|反义词:outside, beyond|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *atwix, from Old English betwux, betweox (“between”), from Proto-Germanic *betwehs (“between”). Cognate with Dutch betwixt (“between”), German zwischen (“between”).
