fawnlike:/ˈfɔːnlaɪk/| adj.|像小鹿的，温顺的，讨好的|Resembling a young deer in appearance or behavior, especially in being gentle, graceful, or timid; also, attempting to gain favor by flattery or servility.|例句：She had a fawnlike shyness that made her endearing. 她有一种像小鹿一样的羞涩，让她显得十分可爱。|近义词：timid, shy, gentle, graceful, meek, subservient, ingratiating|反义词：bold, assertive, aggressive, confident|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *fawn* (young deer) + *-like* (suffix indicating resemblance).  The word "fawn" itself comes from the Old French *faon*, ultimately from the Latin *fetus* (offspring).
