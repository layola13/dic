mixers:/ˈmɪksərz/ | n. |搅拌器; 混合器; 调酒师; （广播、电视或录音室里的）混音师| a machine used for mixing things, especially food; a person who mixes drinks, especially alcoholic drinks; a person who combines sounds from different sources, such as microphones and instruments, to produce a recording or a broadcast | The baker used an electric mixer to combine the ingredients. 面包师用电动搅拌机混合配料。

|近义词：blenders, combiners, minglers (for people)|反义词: separator| 词性: 名词

mixer:/ˈmɪksər/ | n. | 混合者; 社交场合; （尤指非正式的）联谊会| a person who mixes easily with others at social events; a social gathering, especially an informal one | She's a good mixer and enjoys parties. 她善于交际，喜欢参加聚会。|近义词：socializer, mingler|反义词: loner, introvert| 词性: 名词
