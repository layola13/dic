jobsworth:/ˈdʒɒbzwɜːθ/| n.|墨守成规的人；死板的人；只知道照章办事的人|A person who insists on sticking to rules and regulations, even when it is unhelpful or unnecessary. |"The librarian was a real jobsworth and wouldn't let me borrow a book because I was a day late returning the last one."  那个图书管理员真是个墨守成规的人，就因为我上次还书晚了一天，就不让我借书。|近义词：bureaucrat, stickler, martinet|反义词：flexible person, accommodating person|Noun
