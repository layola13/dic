outshoot:/ˌaʊtˈʃuːt/
verb
胜过；比...射得远；比...拍得好
to shoot better or farther than; to surpass in shooting or filming
The Celtics outshot the Lakers from the three-point line. 凯尔特人队的三分球命中率超过了湖人队。
outscore, surpass, exceed
