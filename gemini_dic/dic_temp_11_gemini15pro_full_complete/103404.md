jinx:/dʒɪŋks/| n. vt.|厄运，霉运；带来厄运的人或事物；使倒霉，诅咒|A jinx is something that is thought to bring bad luck.  A jinx can also be a person or thing that brings bad luck, or the act of bringing bad luck to someone or something.|例句：There's a jinx on this house!  这房子被诅咒了！

例句：He seemed to be jinxed when it came to relationships. 他在感情方面似乎总是很倒霉。|近义词：curse, hex, spell|反义词:blessing, charm|词性:名词, 动词
