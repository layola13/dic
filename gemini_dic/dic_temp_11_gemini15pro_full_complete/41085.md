coppiced:/ˈkɒpɪst/ | adj. | 修剪过的，矮林作业的 |Cut or harvested by coppicing.  A method of woodland management in which trees are periodically cut down to near ground level to stimulate growth. | The coppiced hazel trees provided a sustainable source of wood.  这些经过矮林作业的榛树提供了可持续的木材来源。| The path wound through a coppiced woodland. 这条小路蜿蜒穿过一片矮林。 |  managed, pruned, trimmed | overgrown, neglected | adjective
