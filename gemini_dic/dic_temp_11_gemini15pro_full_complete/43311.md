craic:/kræk/
n.
fun, enjoyment, news, gossip, especially good conversation
Irish word for fun, enjoyment, and good times. It can also refer to news, gossip, or especially good conversation.
"We had great craic at the pub last night." 昨晚我们在酒吧玩得很开心。
synonyms: fun, enjoyment, banter, amusement, entertainment
antonyms: boredom, dullness, tediousness, monotony
noun
