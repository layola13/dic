ambled:/ˈæmbəld/ (US), /ˈæmbld/ (UK)| v. | 漫步，缓行 | To walk at a slow, relaxed pace. |  He ambled down the lane, enjoying the scenery. 他沿着小路漫步，欣赏着风景。

The old horse ambled along the path. 老马沿着小路缓行。| 近义词：strolled, sauntered, meandered | 反义词：ran, sprinted, rushed | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *amblen, from Old French *ambler, ultimately from Latin ambulare ("to walk").
