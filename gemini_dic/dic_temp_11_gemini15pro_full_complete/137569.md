nontransparency:/ˌnɑːntrænsˈpærənsi/ | n. |不透明性；不透明度；非透明度|Lack of transparency; the condition of being not transparent.  This can refer to literal physical opacity or to figurative obscurity in processes, information sharing, or decision-making.| The nontransparency of the government's decision-making process led to public distrust. 政府决策过程的不透明导致了公众的不信任。|The frosted glass ensured the nontransparency of the bathroom window. 毛玻璃保证了浴室窗户的不透明性。| Synonyms: opacity, opaqueness, obscurity, cloudiness | Antonyms: transparency, clarity, lucidity, openness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + transparency (from trans- "through" + parere "appear").
