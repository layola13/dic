diuturnity:/ˌdaɪjʊˈtɜːrnɪti/ /ˌdjuˈtɜːrnɪti/
n.|持久, 长久|Long duration or lastingness; permanence.|The diuturnity of their friendship was a testament to their shared values. 他们的友谊的持久是对他们共同价值观的证明。|
近义词: longevity, permanence, durability, endurance
反义词: brevity, transience, ephemerality
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diuturnitas, from Latin diuturnus (“long-lasting”), from diu (“long”) + turnus (“circuit, course, period”).
