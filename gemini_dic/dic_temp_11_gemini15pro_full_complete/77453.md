gawping:/ˈɡɔːpɪŋ/| v.| 呆呆地看，目瞪口呆地注视 | Staring with your mouth open, usually in surprise or amazement. |  He stood there gawping at the accident. 他站在那里目瞪口呆地看着事故。| synonyms: staring, gaping, ogling | antonyms: glancing, ignoring | verb

gawping:/ˈɡɔːpɪŋ/| adj.| 目瞪口呆的 |  Characterized by staring with the mouth open. | She gave me a gawping stare. 她目瞪口呆地盯着我。| synonyms: staring, gaping, wide-eyed | antonyms: uninterested, indifferent | adjective
