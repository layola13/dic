calculated:/ˈkælkjəleɪtɪd/| adj.|计算的；预先计划的；故意的；精心安排的|carefully planned or worked out; done with a specific purpose in mind|例句：It was a calculated risk. 这是一个经过计算的风险。|近义词：deliberate, planned, intentional, premeditated|反义词:unintentional, accidental, unplanned, impulsive|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calculatus*, past participle of calculare (“to reckon, compute”), from calculus (“pebble, small stone”).  Originally, calculations were performed using pebbles or stones as counters.
