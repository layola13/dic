multigroup:/ˈmʌltiɡruːp/
| adj. n.|多组的，多群的；多组，多群|Consisting of or involving multiple groups; a collection of multiple groups. |The researcher conducted a multigroup analysis to compare the effectiveness of the treatment across different age groups. 研究人员进行了多组分析，以比较不同年龄组的治疗效果。|The multigroup study included participants from three different countries.  这项多组研究的参与者来自三个不同的国家。|近义词：multifaceted, multiple, diverse|反义词：single, uniform, homogenous|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: "multi-" (from Latin multus, "much, many") + "group" (from Old French groupe, "lump, cluster")
