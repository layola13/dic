chatter:/ˈtʃætər/ (US), /ˈtʃætə/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|喋喋不休，唠叨，（鸟）啁啾，（机器）嗒嗒声|a series of quick, short sounds, especially made by animals such as birds or monkeys, or a quick, informal conversation about unimportant things|The monkeys chattered in the trees. 猴子在树上吱吱喳喳地叫。|近义词：babble, prattle, jabber, twitter, chirp|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:noun, verb

chatter:/ˈtʃætər/ (US), /ˈtʃætə/ (UK)| vi. vt.|喋喋不休，唠叨，（鸟）啁啾，（机器）嗒嗒声|to talk quickly and continuously, especially about things that are not important|The girls chattered excitedly about the concert. 女孩们兴奋地谈论着音乐会。|近义词：babble, prattle, jabber, twitter, chirp|反义词:be quiet|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chateren, frequentative of chatten (“to chat”), of imitative origin.
