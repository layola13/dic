bromate:/ˈbrəʊmeɪt/ (US) /ˈbroʊmeɪt/| n.|溴酸盐|A salt or ester of bromic acid.  A chemical compound containing the bromate anion, BrO₃⁻.|Potassium bromate is sometimes added to flour as a dough conditioner. 溴酸钾有时被添加到面粉中作为面团调节剂。|近义词:oxidizing agent (氧化剂) |反义词:reducing agent (还原剂)|noun

bromate:/ˈbrəʊmeɪt/ (US) /ˈbroʊmeɪt/| v.|用溴酸盐处理|To treat or react with a bromate.|The flour was bromated to improve its baking qualities. 面粉用溴酸盐处理以改善其烘焙品质。|近义词:oxidize (氧化)|反义词:reduce (还原)|verb
