outearn:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrn/
verb
挣得比...多；收入超过
to earn more money than someone else
He outearns his wife by a considerable margin. 他挣的钱比他妻子多得多。

近义词:outstrip, surpass, exceed
反义词:underperform

词性:v.
