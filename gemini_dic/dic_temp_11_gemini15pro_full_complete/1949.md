actionable:/ˈækʃənəbl/| adj.|可操作的，可执行的，可起诉的|Capable of being acted on or providing grounds for legal action.|The report outlined several actionable recommendations. 报告概述了几个可行的建议。|synonyms: practical, feasible, viable, applicable, usable|antonyms: impractical, unfeasible, unusable, inapplicable|adjective

actionable:/ˈækʃənəbl/| adj.|可控告的，可提起诉讼的|(of a statement) giving sufficient reason to take legal action.|His defamatory remarks were deemed actionable. 他诽谤性的言论被认为是可起诉的。|synonyms: suable, litigable|antonyms: unactionable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From action + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for," or "worthy of."  "Action" comes from Old French "acion," ultimately from Latin "actio," meaning "a doing, a driving," from the verb "agere" meaning "to do, to drive."
