agriculturists:/ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
农业学家；农学家
People who work in agriculture, especially farmers and scientists who study farming.
例句：The agriculturists developed new techniques for growing crops in arid regions.  农业学家开发了在干旱地区种植作物的新技术。

例句：Many agriculturists are concerned about the effects of climate change on their crops. 许多农业学家担心气候变化对作物的影响。


近义词：farmers, growers, agriculturalists
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
