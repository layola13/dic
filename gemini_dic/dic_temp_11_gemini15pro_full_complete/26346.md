buzzsaw: /ˈbʌzsɔː/ (US) /ˈbʌzsɔː/ (UK)| n. | 嗡嗡作响的圆锯;  比喻具有毁灭性力量的事物 |A circular saw that makes a loud buzzing sound; figuratively, something destructive and difficult to stop.|  The project ran into a buzzsaw of bureaucratic regulations.  这个项目遇到了官僚规章制度的阻碍。| 近义词: circular saw,  obstacle, hurdle | 反义词:  assistance, help | 词性: 名词


Etymology:  buzz + saw. "Buzz" refers to the sound the saw makes, and "saw" refers to the cutting tool.  The word first appeared in the mid-19th century.
