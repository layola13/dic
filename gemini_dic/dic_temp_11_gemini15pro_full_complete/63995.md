epistolary: /ɪˈpɪstəˌlɛri/ /ɪˈpɪstəˌleri/
adjective
书信体的；用书信写成的
Relating to or denoting the writing of letters or literary works in the form of letters.
Example:  The novel is written in an epistolary style, using letters and diary entries to tell the story. 这部小说是以书信体写成的，用信件和日记来讲述故事。

Example: Mary Shelley's Frankenstein is a famous example of an epistolary novel.  玛丽·雪莱的《弗兰肯斯坦》是书信体小说的著名例子。

近义词: letter-based, correspondence-based
反义词: non-epistolary
