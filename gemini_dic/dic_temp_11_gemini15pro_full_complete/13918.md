auks:/ɔːks/
n.
海雀
A family of seabirds including puffins, guillemots, and razorbills.
Auks are excellent swimmers and divers, but they are clumsy on land.  海雀是优秀的游泳健将和潜水员，但在陆地上却很笨拙。
Auks build their nests on cliffs. 海雀在悬崖上筑巢。
近义词:sea birds, diving birds
词性:noun
