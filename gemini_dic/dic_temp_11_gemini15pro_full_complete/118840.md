metaphoricalness:/ˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 比喻性，隐喻性 | The quality of being metaphorical; the extent to which something is metaphorical. | The poem is notable for its metaphoricalness, with almost every line containing a figure of speech. 这首诗以其比喻性而著称，几乎每一行都包含一个修辞格。| synonyms: figurativeness, symbolism, allegory | antonyms: literalness, directness | noun
