ironness:/ˈaɪərnɪs/
noun
讽刺，冷嘲，反讽
A state of being ironic; a quality of irony.  The ironness of the situation was palpable. 情况的反讽意味非常明显。
The ironness of being rescued by the very person he had mocked was not lost on him. 被他嘲笑过的人救了他，这种讽刺意味他当然明白。
irony, sarcasm, sardonicism
sincerity, earnestness
