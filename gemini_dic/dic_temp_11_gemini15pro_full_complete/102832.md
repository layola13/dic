jarvey:/ˈdʒɑːrvi/| n.| 轻便马车；赶马车的人|A driver of a jaunting car, a light two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, especially in Ireland.|例句：The jarvey expertly navigated the narrow streets of Dublin. 赶马车的人熟练地驾驭着马车穿过都柏林狭窄的街道。

例句：We hired a jarvey to take us on a tour of the Ring of Kerry. 我们雇了一个赶马车的人带我们游览凯里环。|近义词：coachman, driver|反义词: passenger|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Irish *gearbhach ("horse-boy"). Ultimately from  *ger- ("to shout"), of imitative origin.
