adios:/ˌɑːdiˈoʊs/ | interjection | 再见，告别 | An informal word used to express goodbye, farewell. Originates from Spanish. | Example: "Adios, amigos!" 再见，朋友们！| Synonym: goodbye, farewell, so long, bye | Antonym: hello, greetings | Part of Speech: Interjection
