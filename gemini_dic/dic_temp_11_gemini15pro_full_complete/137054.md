nonstock:/ˌnɑːnˈstɑːk/
adjective
非股份的；非股票的
Not organized as a stock corporation; not issuing or trading stock.
Many nonstock corporations are nonprofit organizations. 许多非股份公司都是非营利组织。
This is a nonstock, nonprofit educational institution. 这是一所非股份的非营利教育机构。
近义词:non-profit, mutual
反义词:stock, for-profit
词性:adjective
