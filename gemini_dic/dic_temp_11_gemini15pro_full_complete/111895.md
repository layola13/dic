logroll:/ˈlɒɡrəʊl/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡroʊl/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|互惠互利；互相吹捧；滚圆木；圆木运动|An agreement to exchange support or favors, especially in politics.  Also, the act of rolling logs, often as a sport or competition.|The two senators agreed to logroll, each supporting the other's pet project. 这两位参议员同意互相支持，各自支持对方的重点项目。 | 近义词:back-scratching, quid pro quo, wheeling and dealing | 反义词:opposition, antagonism |词性:名词,动词
