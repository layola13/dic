ebonize: /ˈɛbənaɪz/ | vt. | 使乌黑；使成黑檀色；用黑檀木装饰 | To make black or dark; to give the appearance of ebony. |  The craftsman ebonized the wood to give it a rich, dark finish. 工匠将木头染黑，使其呈现出丰富的深色光泽。

The antique table was ebonized and polished to a high sheen. 这张古董桌子被染成黑檀色并抛光至高度光泽。 | 近义词：blacken, darken | 反义词：whiten, lighten | 词性: 动词
