kleptomanias:/ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniəz/| n. |盗窃癖，偷窃狂|An uncontrollable impulse to steal, typically without any economic motive.|She was diagnosed with kleptomania after being caught stealing small items from stores repeatedly. 她多次在商店偷窃小物品后被诊断为盗窃癖。|Synonyms: kleptomania, compulsive stealing|Antonyms: honesty, integrity|noun
