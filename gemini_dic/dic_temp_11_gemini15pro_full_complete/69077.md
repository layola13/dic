fauna:/ˈfɔːnə/ (US), /ˈfɔːnə/ (UK)| n. |动物群|The animals of a particular region, habitat, or geological period.|

例句：
The fauna of the Amazon rainforest is incredibly diverse.
亚马逊雨林的动物群极其多样化。

The island's fauna includes several endangered species.
该岛的动物群包括几种濒危物种。

近义词：wildlife, animals|
反义词：flora (植物群)|
词性：noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Fauna, the Roman goddess of animals and fertility, sister of Faunus, the Roman god of the wild.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dhew- (“to run, flow, rush”). Related to "fawn".|
