excoction:/ɪkˈskɒkʃən/ |n.| 煎煮物；提取物； concoction|  A medicinal preparation made by boiling herbs or other ingredients in water.  An extract obtained by boiling.  A concoction or mixture.| 例句：The excoction of herbs created a potent remedy.  草药的煎煮物制成了一种有效的药物。

例句：The excoction was bitter and unpleasant to drink.  这煎煮物很苦，很难喝。 |近义词：decoction, extract, infusion, concoction|反义词:  N/A|词性: noun
