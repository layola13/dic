confessionist:/kənˈfɛʃənɪst/
n.
忏悔者；认罪者；坦白者
a person who confesses something, especially their sins or crimes.
He was a confessionist who admitted to stealing the money. 他是一个忏悔者，承认偷了钱。
The confessionist sought absolution from the priest.  忏悔者寻求牧师的赦免。
synonyms: penitent, contriteness
antonyms: unrepentant, impenitent
noun
