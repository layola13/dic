brashest:/ˈbreɪʃɪst/| adj.|最轻率的；最鲁莽的；最厚颜无耻的|superlative adjective of brash: extremely self-assertive in a rude, noisy, or overbearing way|He was known for his brashest pronouncements, often offending those around him. 他以最轻率的言论而闻名，经常冒犯周围的人。| synonyms:  boldest, cockiest, most impudent, most arrogant, most audacious| antonyms:  shyest, humblest, most timid, most reserved| adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brasche, *brask (“rash, impetuous, brittle”), probably of imitative origin.  Cognate with Norwegian brask (“crackling, crash”), Swedish brasa (“fire”), Old Norse braşk (“crackling of fire”). More at brash.
