doubtable:/ˈdaʊtəbl/| adj.|可疑的，值得怀疑的| Open to doubt or suspicion; questionable.|Her claims seemed doubtable, given her past behavior.  鉴于她过去的行为，她的说法似乎值得怀疑。| The evidence is doubtable. 证据值得怀疑。|Synonyms: questionable, dubious, uncertain, debatable, disputable|Antonyms:  certain, indubitable, unquestionable|adjective
