muniments:/ˈmjuːnɪmənts/
n.
防御工事；契据；档案；（尤指）所有权证明文件
legal documents, especially title deeds, that serve as evidence of ownership or rights
The lawyer examined the ancient muniments to establish ownership of the land. 律师检查了古代契据，以确定土地的所有权。
近义词: deeds, records, archives, documents, title deeds
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用表示“伪造文件” forgery 或 “无效文件” invalid documents)
词性: 名词

词源:
From Old French *muniment, from Latin mūnīmentum "fortification, defense, protection," from mūnīre "to fortify," from moenia "city walls."
