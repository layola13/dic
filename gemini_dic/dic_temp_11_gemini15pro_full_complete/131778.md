nonconnectivities:/ˌnɒnkənɛkˈtɪvɪtiz/ | n. | 非连接性；不连通性 | The lack of connections or relationships between things or people.  This can refer to physical disconnections, like roads that don't meet, or abstract disconnections, like a lack of communication between departments. | The nonconnectivities in the transportation system caused significant delays. 交通系统的不连通性导致了严重的延误。 | The report highlighted the nonconnectivities between different government agencies.  报告强调了不同政府机构之间的缺乏联系。 | 近义词: disconnections, gaps, separations | 反义词: connections, links, continuities | 词性: 名词
