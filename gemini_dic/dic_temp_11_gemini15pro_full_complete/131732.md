nonconfined:/ˌnɒnkənˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 非受限的，无约束的 | Not confined or restricted; free. |  The nonconfined aquifer is susceptible to contamination from surface sources.  非承压含水层容易受到地表污染源的污染。 | The animals roamed nonconfined across the vast plains. 动物们在广阔的平原上自由漫步。 | 近义词: free, unrestricted, unbound, unconstrained | 反义词: confined, restricted, limited, bound | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + confined (from Latin confinis, meaning "having a common border")
