contrastiveness:/kənˈtræstɪvnəs/| n.|对比性，对照性|The contrastiveness of the two approaches is striking.  这两种方法的对比性很明显。|例句：The contrastiveness of the two paintings highlights the different styles of the artists.  这两幅画的对比性突出了艺术家们不同的风格。

The contrastiveness of the two proposals made it easy to choose the better one.  两个提案的对比性使得更容易选择更好的一个。|近义词：difference, distinction, contrast, dissimilarity|反义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From contrast + -ive + -ness.  Contrast ultimately comes from the Latin prefix *com-* (together, with) and *stare* (to stand).
