over-serves:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvz/
verb
过度服务；服务过量
To provide more service than is necessary or desired.
The restaurant over-served us with bread; we couldn't possibly eat it all.  这家餐馆给我们提供了过多的面包，我们根本吃不完。
The waiter over-served the customer with wine, and he became intoxicated. 服务员给顾客倒了太多的酒，他喝醉了。
