druggy:/ˈdrʌɡi/| adj. n.|吸毒的；沉迷毒品的；像吸毒者的；吸毒成瘾者|Relating to, characteristic of, or influenced by drugs, especially illegal drugs; a drug addict.|He had a druggy look in his eyes. 他的眼神看起来像吸毒的。|近义词：addicted, strung-out, stoned, high|反义词:sober, clean|词性:形容词, 名词

druggy:/ˈdrʌɡi/| n.|吸毒成瘾者|A habitual user of illicit drugs; a drug addict.|The police arrested a group of druggies in the park. 警察在公园里逮捕了一群吸毒者。|近义词：addict, junkie|反义词:teetotaler|词性:名词

词源:  From drug + -y.  The adjective first appeared in the late 19th century, and the noun followed in the early 20th century.
