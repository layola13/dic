misname:/ˌmɪsˈneɪm/
verb|错误命名；叫错名字|To give someone or something the wrong name, either accidentally or deliberately.|He was misnamed in the newspaper report. 他在报纸报道中被叫错了名字。|mislabel, miscall|correctly name|v.
misname:/ˌmɪsˈneɪm/
noun|错误的名字|A wrong name.|That's a misname; her real name is Elizabeth. 那是个错误的名字；她真正的名字是伊丽莎白。|misnomer, wrong name|correct name|n.
