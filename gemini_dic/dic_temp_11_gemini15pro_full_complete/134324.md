noninstructively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈstrʌktɪvli/
adv. |无指导地，无益地|  Without providing instruction or guidance; in a way that does not teach or inform. | The experiment was conducted noninstructively, leaving the participants to figure things out on their own.  这个实验是在没有指导的情况下进行的，让参与者自己去弄明白事情。| 近义词: unhelpfully, uselessly, pointlessly | 反义词: instructively, helpfully, usefully | 词性: 副词

词源:  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "instructive" (meaning providing instruction), and then adding the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "Instructive" comes from the Latin "instruere" (to build, equip, arrange, teach).
