nonministerially:/ˌnɒnˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəli/
adverb
非部长地；非官方地
Not done or carried out by a minister of state or in an official capacity.
The decision was made nonministerially, by a committee of experts.  这个决定是由专家委员会非官方做出的。
The funds were distributed nonministerially to various community organizations. 这些资金被非官方地分配给了各个社区组织。
Synonyms: unofficially, privately, independently
Antonyms: officially, ministerially
词性: 副词
