attacks: /əˈtæks/ (US) /əˈtæks/ (UK)| n. v.| 攻击，袭击; 发作; 侵袭|An act of using violence to try to hurt or kill someone or something. A sudden short period of suffering from an illness, especially one affecting the heart or brain.|

例句：
The enemy launched several attacks on the city.  敌人对这座城市发动了几次袭击。

The patient suffered a heart attack.  病人心脏病发作了。


近义词：assaults, offensives, strikes, onslaughts, seizures, fits|
反义词：defenses, protections|
词性：名词, 动词|
