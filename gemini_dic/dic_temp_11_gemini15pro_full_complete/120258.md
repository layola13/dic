mini-condominiums:/ˈmɪniˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (US) /ˈmɪniˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (UK)
n.
小型公寓，迷你公寓
Small condominium units, typically smaller than average condominiums, often designed for single occupancy or small families.
The developer is building mini-condominiums aimed at young professionals.  开发商正在建造针对年轻专业人士的小型公寓。
The mini-condominium was perfect for her minimalist lifestyle. 这套小型公寓非常适合她的极简生活方式。
Synonyms: small apartments, studio apartments, compact apartments
Antonyms: large apartments, mansions, spacious homes
Noun
