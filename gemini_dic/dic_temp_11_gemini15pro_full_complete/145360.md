overburned:/ˌōvərˈbərnd/
adj.
烧过头的，过度燃烧的
burned excessively; overcooked.
The overburned toast was inedible. 烤焦了的吐司不能吃。
近义词: burnt, scorched, charred
反义词: undercooked, raw
词性:形容词

词源:
From "over" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "burned" (past participle of "burn," from Old English beornan, from Proto-Germanic *brannjanan).
