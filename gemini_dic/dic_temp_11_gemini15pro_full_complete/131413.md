nonclearances:/ˌnɒnˈklɪərənsɪz/
noun
未清除物，未获批准的事项
The absence of clearances or authorizations; things or matters that have not been cleared or approved.
The project was delayed due to several nonclearances from the regulatory bodies.  这个项目由于监管机构的几项未批准事项而被推迟了。
Several nonclearances related to land ownership held up the construction project.  与土地所有权相关的几项未清除事项阻碍了建设项目的进行。
Synonyms:  unapproved items, outstanding issues, unresolved matters, pending approvals
Antonyms:  clearances, approvals, authorizations
