leg:/leɡ/ | n. | 腿；支柱；一段路程 | A limb of an animal used for supporting the body and for walking.  One of the supports of a piece of furniture. A stage or part of a journey or course. | She broke her leg skiing. 她滑雪时摔断了腿。The table has four legs. 这张桌子有四条腿。The last leg of our journey was by train. 我们旅程的最后一段是坐火车。| synonym:limb, support, stage | antonym: - | noun
