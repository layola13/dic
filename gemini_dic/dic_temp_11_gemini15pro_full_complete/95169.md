inattention:/ˌɪnəˈtɛnʃən/| n.|不注意，疏忽，注意力不集中|Lack of attention; failure to pay attention.|His inattention during the meeting resulted in him missing important information. 他在会议期间的注意力不集中导致他错过了重要信息。|近义词：negligence, carelessness, disregard|反义词:attention, diligence, heedfulness|词性:名词

Etymology:
Late Middle English: from in- (expressing negation) + attention.
