epidermic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 表皮的；流行性的 | Affecting the epidermis or skin; widespread, prevalent, affecting a large number of people. |  The rash was deemed epidermic, confined to the outer layers of skin. 皮疹被认为是表皮的，仅限于皮肤外层。

The epidermic spread of the flu caused widespread school closures. 流感的流行传播导致学校普遍停课。 | 近义词: cutaneous, dermal, superficial, widespread, rampant, pervasive | 反义词: deep-seated, internal, localized | 词性: 形容词
