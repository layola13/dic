cretinous:/ˈkriːtɪnəs/| adj.|白垩的；患呆小症的；愚钝的|Resembling chalk; suffering from cretinism; stupid.|例句：The cretinous child struggled with simple tasks.  这个呆小症儿童连简单的任务都很难完成。

例句：His cretinous remark offended everyone in the room. 他愚蠢的言论冒犯了房间里的每个人。|近义词：stupid, foolish, idiotic, imbecilic, moronic|反义词：intelligent, bright, smart, clever, astute|词性：形容词
词源：From French *crétin*, from Franco-Provençal *cretin*, from Vulgar Latin *christianus* (“Christian”), used euphemistically for someone with congenital defects.  Ultimately from Latin *Chrīstiānus*.
