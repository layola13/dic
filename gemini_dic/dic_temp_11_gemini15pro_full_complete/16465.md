baresthesiometer:/ˌbæriːsθiːziˈɒmɪtər/ /ˌbæriːsθiːziˈɑːmɪtər/
noun
触觉计，压觉计
An instrument for measuring sensitivity to pressure, especially touch.
The doctor used a baresthesiometer to test the patient's sensitivity to touch. 医生使用触觉计来测试病人的触觉敏感度。
