myoepithelial: /ˌmaɪoʊɛpɪˈθiːliəl/ | adj. | 肌上皮的 |  Relating to or consisting of myoepithelial cells, which are contractile cells found in glandular epithelium, such as sweat glands, salivary glands, and mammary glands.  They are typically located between the basement membrane and the luminal epithelial cells. | Myoepithelial cells play a crucial role in expelling secretions from glands. 肌上皮细胞在从腺体排出分泌物中起着至关重要的作用。 |  Myoepithelial carcinoma is a rare type of cancer. 肌上皮癌是一种罕见的癌症。 | Synonyms: None (highly specific term) | Antonyms: None | adjective
