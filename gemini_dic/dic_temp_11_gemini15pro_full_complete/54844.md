displacement's:/dɪsˈpleɪsmənts/
noun
位移的，取代的
belonging to or associated with displacement (the moving of something from its place or position).
The displacement's impact on the community was devastating.  这个位移对社区的影响是毁灭性的。
近义词: shifting's, movement's, relocation's
反义词: placement's, positioning's
词性: possessive noun (名词所有格)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "displacement,"  from displace + -ment.  Displace comes from the Old French desplacier,  from des- (expressing removal) + place (place).
