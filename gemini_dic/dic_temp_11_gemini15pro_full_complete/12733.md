assets /ˈæsɛts/
n.
资产，财产
Something valuable that a person or organization owns, especially property, investments, or money.
The company's assets include land, buildings, and machinery.  这家公司的资产包括土地、建筑和机械。
synonyms: property, possessions, holdings, resources, wealth
antonyms: liabilities, debts
