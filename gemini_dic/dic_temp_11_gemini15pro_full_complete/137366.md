nontelepathic:/ˌnɒnˌtɛləˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 非心灵感应的，非心电感应的 | Not involving or relating to telepathy; unable to communicate through thoughts. |  The message was sent by radio, a nontelepathic method of communication. 这条信息是通过无线电发送的，一种非心灵感应的通信方式。

The detective relied on evidence and questioning, rather than nontelepathic means, to solve the case.  侦探依靠证据和讯问，而不是非心灵感应的方式来破案。 | 近义词: non-psychic, mundane, ordinary | 反义词: telepathic, psychic | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "telepathic" (from Greek "tele-" meaning "distant" and "pathos" meaning "feeling").
