instigating: /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb, present participle
煽动；唆使；激起
Encouraging or urging (someone) to do something, especially something bad.
He was accused of instigating violence. 他被指控煽动暴力。
instigate: /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/
verb
煽动；唆使；激起
Bring about or initiate (an action or event).
They instigated a rebellion. 他们发动了叛乱。
近义词: incite, provoke, abet, foment, stir up, goad
反义词: deter, discourage, prevent, suppress, quell
词性: verb (动词)
