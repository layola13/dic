denomination's:/dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.
中文翻译：面额（尤指纸币或硬币）；命名；名称；教派
英文解析：A unit of value, especially of money; a name or designation; a religious group or sect.
例句：The cashier counted the bills of different denominations.  收银员数了不同面额的钞票。

例句：The denomination's beliefs are rooted in ancient traditions.  这个教派的信仰根植于古老的传统。
近义词：value, name, designation, sect, church, faith
反义词：(No direct antonym, depends on context - e.g., for value: worthlessness; for sect: mainstream)
词性：名词 (Noun - possessive form)
