ambitioning:/æmˈbɪʃənɪŋ/
v.
有雄心，追求，渴望
Having ambitious plans or desires; aspiring to.
He is ambitioning a leadership role in the company. 他渴望在公司担任领导职务。
近义词: aspiring, desiring, striving, aiming
反义词:  apathetic, indifferent, unmotivated
词性: 动词 (verb)
