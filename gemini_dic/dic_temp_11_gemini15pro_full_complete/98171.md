inscribing: /ɪnˈskraɪbɪŋ/
verb
铭刻，题写
The act of writing, engraving, or carving words or symbols onto a surface.
He was inscribing his name on the trophy. 他正在奖杯上刻他的名字。
inscribing: /ɪnˈskraɪbɪŋ/
adjective
题写的
Used to describe something that is being inscribed or has been inscribed.
The inscribing ceremony was held in the town square. 题字仪式在小镇广场举行。


Etymology:
From Latin "inscribere" (in + scribere - to write).

Synonyms:
Engraving, carving, etching, imprinting, marking

Antonyms:
Erasing, deleting, obliterating

Part of Speech:
Present participle of the verb "inscribe"
