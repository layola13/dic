antimonarchic:/ˌæntiˈmɑːnəkɪk/ | adj. | 反君主的，反对君主制的 | Opposed to monarchy; supporting the abolition of monarchy. |  The antimonarchic sentiment was strong in the country after the king's corruption was revealed.  这个国家在国王的腐败行为被揭露后，反君主的情绪高涨。| synonyms: republican, antiroyalist | antonyms: monarchist, royalist | adjective


antimonarchist:/ˌæntiˈmɑːnəkɪst/ | n. | 反君主主义者 | A person who opposes monarchy. | The antimonarchists protested outside the palace. 反君主主义者在宫殿外抗议。| synonyms: republican, antiroyalist | antonyms: monarchist, royalist | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + monarchic (relating to monarchy).  "Monarchic" ultimately derives from Greek monarkhia (sole rule), from monos (alone) + arkhein (to rule).
