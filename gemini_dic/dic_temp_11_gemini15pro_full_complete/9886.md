apassionata:/əˌpæʃəˈnɑːtə/
noun
热情奏鸣曲；热情地；充满激情地
A musical composition, typically for piano, characterized by passionate expression.  It also refers to the passionate quality itself.
Beethoven's Appassionata is a masterpiece. 贝多芬的《热情奏鸣曲》是一部杰作。
She danced with an appassionata that captivated the audience. 她充满激情地跳舞，深深吸引了观众。
synonyms: fervent, impassioned, ardent, passionate
antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unemotional
词性: noun
