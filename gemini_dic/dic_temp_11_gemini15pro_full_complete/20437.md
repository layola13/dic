bitted:/ˈbɪtɪd/| adj.|装上嚼子的，受控制的，|A horse controlled by a bit and bridle.  A person or entity controlled or restrained by something.|例句：The bitted horse responded readily to the rider's cues.  装上嚼子的马很容易对骑手的提示做出反应。

例句：He felt bitted by his responsibilities. 他感到被自己的责任束缚住了。|近义词：restrained, controlled, curbed|反义词：unbridled, free, unrestrained|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "bit" (to put a bit in a horse's mouth), from Old English "bītan" (to bite).
