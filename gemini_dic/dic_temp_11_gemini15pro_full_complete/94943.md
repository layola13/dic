PHRASE->
(This phrase is somewhat archaic and not commonly used in modern English, so providing common phrases using "in point of" is difficult.  Instead, offering modern alternatives is more helpful.)

Instead of phrases *using* "in-point-of", here are phrases that express *similar meanings* and are more common:

1. in terms of: In terms of cost, this option is the best. 就成本而言，这个选项是最好的。
2. regarding:  Regarding your question, the answer is yes. 关于你的问题，答案是肯定的。
3. concerning: Concerning the meeting, it has been postponed. 关于会议，它已被推迟。
4. with respect to: With respect to your concerns, we have taken appropriate measures. 关于你的担忧，我们已经采取了适当的措施。
5. as regards: As regards the project, we are on schedule. 关于这个项目，我们在按计划进行.
