brontozoum:/ˌbrɒntoʊˈzoʊəm/ | n. | 雷龙足迹，雷龙 | An extinct animal, believed to be one of the largest dinosaurs, known from its fossilized footprints. |  Example 1: The brontozoum trackway found in the Connecticut Valley is a remarkable testament to the size of these creatures.  康涅狄格山谷发现的雷龙足迹是这些生物体型巨大的显著证明。

Example 2:  Scientists debated whether the brontozoum tracks belonged to a dinosaur or another type of prehistoric reptile. 科学家们争论雷龙足迹是否属于恐龙或其他类型的史前爬行动物。 | synonym: dinosaur footprint, trackway (of a large dinosaur) | antonym: N/A (no direct opposite for a specific footprint) | noun
