diffracts:/dɪˈfrækts/| v. |衍射，绕射|When a wave encounters an obstacle, it bends around the edges of the obstacle. This bending is called diffraction. |Light diffracts as it passes through a narrow slit. 光线穿过狭缝时会发生衍射。|The sound diffracted around the corner, allowing me to hear the conversation. 声音绕过拐角发生衍射，让我听到了谈话。|Synonyms: scatter, disperse, spread|Antonyms: focus, concentrate|verb
