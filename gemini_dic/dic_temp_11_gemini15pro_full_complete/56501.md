domesticated: /dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 驯养的，家养的 | Adapted to living with humans and serving their purposes; tame. | Example: Domesticated animals like dogs and cats are common pets.  像狗和猫这样的家养动物是常见的宠物。| Synonyms: tame, trained, broken, docile | Antonyms: wild, feral, untamed | adjective

domesticated: /dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd/ | v. | 驯化，使习惯于家庭生活 | To tame (an animal) and keep it as a pet or for farm produce.  | Example:  Humans domesticated wolves thousands of years ago, leading to the evolution of dogs.  人类在几千年前驯化了狼，导致了狗的进化。| Synonyms: tame, train, break | Antonyms: release, free | verb
