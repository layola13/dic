kahuna:/kəˈhuːnə/ | n. |  祭司；专家；大师；内行 | A priest or shaman, especially in Polynesian cultures.  Also used informally to refer to an expert or leader in a particular field. |  He's the kahuna of computer programming. 他是计算机编程的大师。 | synonyms: expert, master, specialist, guru | antonyms: amateur, novice, beginner | noun

kahuna:/kəˈhuːnə/ | n. |  祭司；专家；大师；内行 |  A person who is an expert at something; a leader.
