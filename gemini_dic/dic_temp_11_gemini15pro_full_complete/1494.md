acetanilide:/ˌæsɪˈtænɪlaɪd/| n. |乙酰苯胺|A white crystalline solid, also known as N-phenylacetamide, used as a fever reducer and pain reliever, and in the manufacture of other chemicals.| Acetanilide was commonly used as a fever reducer in the past. 乙酰苯胺过去常被用作退烧药。|Synonyms: antifebrin, N-phenylacetamide|Antonyms: None (lacks a direct antonym, but could contrast with fever-inducing substances)|noun
Etymology: From acetic + aniline + -ide.
