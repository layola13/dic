euthanatizes:/ˌjuːθəˌnaɪˌzaɪz/ | vt. | 安乐死 | to kill (a person or animal) in a painless way, especially to end suffering from an incurable illness | The vet euthanized the injured horse. 兽医对受伤的马实施了安乐死。 | 近义词: put down, put to sleep | 反义词: resuscitate, revive | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  From Greek *eu- "good, well" + *thanatos "death."
