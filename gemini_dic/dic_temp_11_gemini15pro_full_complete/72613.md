flybane:/ˈflaɪbeɪn/| n.|灭蝇毒药；捕蝇纸；捕蝇器|A substance or device used to kill or trap flies.|The old house was filled with flybane, a testament to its long neglect.  这栋老房子里到处都是捕蝇器，证明它长期以来无人照料。|Example: The farmer hung flybane in the barn to control the fly population.  农夫在谷仓里挂了捕蝇纸来控制苍蝇的数量。|Synonyms: flypaper, flytrap, insecticide|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fly attractant")|noun
