aught:/ɔːt/
pron.|任何事物，任何东西|Anything; any part.|例句：For aught I know, he may be innocent. = 就我所知，他可能是无辜的。

例句：It matters not an aught. = 这无关紧要。
|近义词：anything, something, everything|反义词:naught, nothing|词性:代词

aught:/ɔːt/
n.|零|Zero; nothing.|例句：Set the counter to aught. = 将计数器设置为零。

例句：He knows aught about computers. =他对电脑一无所知。
|近义词：zero, cipher, nil|反义词:all, everything|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *āwiht, from ā ("ever") + wiht ("thing, creature").  Cognate with German "etwas" (something).  The pronunciation shifted over time.
