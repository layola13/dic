noncharismatic:/ˌnɒnˌkærɪzˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 没有魅力的；不具感召力的 | Lacking charisma; not inspiring enthusiasm or devotion. |  He delivered a noncharismatic speech that failed to engage the audience. 他发表了一场缺乏魅力的演讲，未能吸引观众。

The new CEO is efficient but noncharismatic. 新任首席执行官效率很高，但缺乏个人魅力。

| 近义词: uninspiring, dull, bland, tedious, boring | 反义词: charismatic, inspiring, magnetic, charming, captivating | 词性: 形容词
