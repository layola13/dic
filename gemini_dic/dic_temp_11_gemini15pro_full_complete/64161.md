equalness:/ˈiːkwəlnəs/
n.
平等；均等；相等
The state or quality of being equal.
例句：The equalness of opportunity is a fundamental principle of a just society.  机会平等是公正社会的基本原则。

例句：They strived for equalness in the workplace, ensuring everyone received fair treatment.  他们在工作场所争取平等，确保每个人都得到公平对待。

近义词：equality, parity, equivalence
反义词：inequality, disparity, imbalance
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From equal + -ness.  Equal comes from the Latin "aequalis" (meaning even, level, equal), derived from "aequus" (meaning level, even, plain, just).  The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
