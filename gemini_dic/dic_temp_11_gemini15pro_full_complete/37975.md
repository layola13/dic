compliantly:/kəmˈplaɪəntli/
adverb
顺从地，服从地
in a compliant manner; obediently.
The company compliantly agreed to the new regulations.  这家公司顺从地同意了新规定。
obediently, submissively, meekly, dutifully
rebelliously, defiantly, resistantly
词性: 副词
