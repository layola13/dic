douroucouli:/ˌdʊrʊˈkuːli/ | n. | 夜猴；猫头鹰猴 | A small, nocturnal, arboreal monkey with large eyes, found in South America.  It is also known as the owl monkey. |  The douroucouli is the only truly nocturnal monkey. 夜猴是唯一真正夜行性的猴子。 | The douroucouli uses its large eyes to navigate through the forest at night. 夜猴利用它的大眼睛在夜间穿越森林。 | Synonyms: owl monkey, night monkey | Antonyms: diurnal monkey | noun
