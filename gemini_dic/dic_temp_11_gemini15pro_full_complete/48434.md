deductible:/dɪˈdʌktɪbl/ | n. adj. |免赔额，可扣除的|The amount you have to pay out-of-pocket before your insurance coverage kicks in.  指保险生效前你需要自己支付的金额。|例句：My health insurance plan has a high deductible. 我的健康保险计划有很高的免赔额。|近义词：excess, out-of-pocket expense |反义词: benefit, coverage | noun

deductible:/dɪˈdʌktɪbl/ | adj. |可扣除的| Expenses that can be subtracted from your taxable income. 指可以从应税收入中扣除的费用。|例句：Charitable donations are often tax deductible.  慈善捐款通常可以免税。|近义词：subtractable, allowable |反义词: non-deductible, taxable | adjective
