entanglement:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlmənt/ | n. | 纠缠，缠绕；牵连；复杂的关系 |A complicated or compromising relationship or situation.  A state of being twisted together or entwined. In quantum mechanics, a phenomenon where two or more particles become linked and share the same fate, even when separated by large distances.|  例句：The entanglement of the fishing line made it impossible to cast. 鱼线缠在一起使它无法抛出。

| 近义词：tangle, complication, involvement, snarl, intricacy | 反义词：simplicity, order, clarity, disentanglement, separation | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "in, into") + tangle + -ment (noun-forming suffix).  "Tangle" comes from the Middle English "tanglen," of Scandinavian origin.
