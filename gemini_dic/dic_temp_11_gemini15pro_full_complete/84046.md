half-fictitious:/ˌhæf fɪkˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
半虚构的
partly imagined or invented; based partly on fact and partly on fiction
The story is half-fictitious, based on real events but with some embellishments.  这个故事是半虚构的，基于真实事件，但有一些润色。
half-true, semi-fictional
factual, real, true
