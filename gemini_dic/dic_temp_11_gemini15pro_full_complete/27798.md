cannonades:/ˌkænəˈneɪdz/ | n. | 炮轰，连串的炮火 | A period of continuous heavy gunfire; a barrage. | The cannonades from the fort shook the very ground.  从堡垒传来的炮轰声震动了大地。| bombardment, barrage, shelling | silence, peace | noun

cannonades:/ˌkænəˈneɪdz/ | n. |  (比喻) 大量的批评或责难  | A barrage of criticism or questions. | He faced a cannonade of questions from the reporters. 他面临记者的大量提问。 | barrage, onslaught, volley | quiet, calm | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *canonnade*, from *canonner* "to cannonade," from *canon* "cannon." Ultimately from Late Latin *canna* "reed, tube."
