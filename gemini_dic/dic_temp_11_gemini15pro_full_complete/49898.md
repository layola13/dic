demurring:/dɪˈmɜːrɪŋ/
adjective
表示反对的，犹豫的，迟疑的
raising doubts or objections or showing reluctance
例句：She offered him the job, but he demurring, unsure if he wanted to leave his current position.  他给她提供了这份工作，但他犹豫了，不确定是否想离开现在的职位。

例句：He gave a demurring look when asked to take on the extra responsibility. 当被要求承担额外责任时，他露出了犹豫的神情。

近义词：hesitant, reluctant, objecting, wavering, doubtful
反义词：agreeable, willing, enthusiastic, eager, consenting
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demorer "to linger, delay, tarry," from Latin demorari "to delay, linger, loiter," from de- "down, completely" + morari "to delay."  The sense shifted from "delaying" to "objecting" in the 16th century.
