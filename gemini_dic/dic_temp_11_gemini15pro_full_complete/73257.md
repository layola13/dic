forefending:/fɔːrˈfɛndɪŋ/
| adj. |防御的，保护的| Preventing something bad from happening; protective. |  The knight raised his shield, forefending the dragon's fiery breath. 骑士举起盾牌，抵挡恶龙的火焰吐息。 | Example:  A forefending wall was built around the city.  一座防御墙环绕着城市建了起来。| Synonyms: protective, defensive, shielding, guarding | Antonyms:  offensive, attacking,  vulnerable, exposing | Word type: adjective

forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/
| vt. |防御，防止，保护| Avert, prevent, especially something unpleasant. | God forefend! 上帝保佑！（表示不希望某事发生） | Example:  She held up her hands to forefend the blow. 她举起双手抵挡打击。| Synonyms: prevent, avert, obviate, preclude | Antonyms:  cause, incite, provoke, allow | Word type: verb
