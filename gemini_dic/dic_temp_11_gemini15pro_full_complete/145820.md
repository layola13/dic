overdefensiveness:/ˌəʊvədɪˈfɛnsɪvnəs/| n. |过度防御；过敏感；防御性过强|The state of being excessively defensive, often characterized by reacting disproportionately to perceived threats or criticism.|Her overdefensiveness made it difficult to have a constructive conversation. 她过强的防御心态使得很难进行建设性的对话。|近义词：sensitivity, touchiness, defensiveness|反义词：openness, receptiveness|noun
