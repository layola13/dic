half-written:/ˌhɑːf ˈrɪtn/ | adj. | 写了一半的，未完成的 |  Something that is half-written is only partially completed, with the writing process not yet finished. |  The novel was half-written when the author passed away. 这部小说在作者去世时只写了一半。| Synonyms: unfinished, incomplete, partial | Antonyms: finished, complete, whole | adjective

half-written:/ˌhɑːf ˈrɪtn/ | adj. | 半成品的 |  A project or piece of work that is half-written is still in progress and not yet finalized. |  He submitted a half-written report and promised to complete it later. 他提交了一份半成品的报告，并承诺稍后完成。| Synonyms: underdeveloped, rudimentary, in progress | Antonyms: finalized, completed, polished | adjective
