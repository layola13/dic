haemadynamometer:/ˌhiːməˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/| n. |血压计|An instrument for measuring the force of the blood current.  A sphygmomanometer is a common type of haemadynamometer.|The doctor used a haemadynamometer to check the patient's blood pressure.|Synonym: sphygmomanometer, blood pressure monitor, blood pressure gauge|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology:  From Greek *haima* (blood) + *dynamis* (power) + *metron* (measure).
