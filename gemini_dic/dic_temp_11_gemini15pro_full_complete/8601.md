anticling:/ˌæntiˈklɪŋ/| adj.|防粘的，抗粘连的|Resistant to clinging or sticking.  Treated or designed to prevent something from clinging or sticking to it.|This fabric is treated with an anticling agent to prevent static.  这种布料经过防静电处理以防止粘连。|The anticling film makes it easy to separate the layers.  防粘膜使层与层之间容易分离。|近义词：nonstick, cling-free, static-free|反义词：clingy, sticky|词性:形容词
