cauline:/ˈkɔːlaɪn/| adj.|茎生的；长在茎上的|Growing on the stem, as distinct from radical leaves which grow from the root.|The plant has small, cauline leaves.  这种植物有小的茎生叶。|近义词:stem-borne|反义词:radical, basal|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caulīnus, from caulis (“stalk, stem”).
