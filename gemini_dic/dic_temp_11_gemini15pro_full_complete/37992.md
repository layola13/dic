complice:/ˈkɑːmplɪs/ /ˈkʌmplɪs/ | n. |共犯，帮凶 |A person who helps another person commit a crime or do something wrong. |例句：He was convicted as an accomplice to murder. 他被判为谋杀案的同谋。

例句：She acted as his accomplice in the robbery.  她在抢劫案中充当了他的帮凶。

近义词：accessory, abettor, collaborator, partner in crime |反义词:victim, opponent, adversary |词性:noun
