alliaceous:/ˌælɪˈeɪʃəs/| adj.|似大蒜的，有蒜味的|Relating to or resembling garlic, onion, or other plants of the allium family, especially in smell or taste.|The air was thick with an alliaceous aroma emanating from the kitchen. 厨房里飘散着浓浓的蒜香味。|Synonyms: garlicky, oniony|Antonyms: bland, mild|adjective

alliaceous:/ˌælɪˈeɪʃəs/| adj.|蒜味的，大蒜似的|Having the smell or taste of garlic or onions.|The alliaceous scent filled the air as she sautéed the onions. 她炒洋葱时，空气中弥漫着蒜味。|Synonyms: pungent, savory|Antonyms: sweet, odorless|adjective
