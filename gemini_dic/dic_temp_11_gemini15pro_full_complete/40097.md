contaminated:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 受污染的，被污染的 | Made impure by contact or mixture with something unclean or poisonous. | The river was contaminated with chemicals from the factory. 这条河被工厂的化学物质污染了。 |  synonyms: polluted, tainted, infected, defiled | antonyms: pure, clean, unpolluted, pristine | adjective

contaminated:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/ | v. | 污染，弄脏 | To make something impure by exposure to or addition of a poisonous or polluting substance. | The oil spill contaminated the beach.  石油泄漏污染了海滩。 | synonyms: pollute, taint, infect, defile | antonyms: purify, cleanse, decontaminate | verb
