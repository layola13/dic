idyllic:/aɪˈdɪlɪk/ | adj. |田园诗般的，牧歌式的 | Extremely pleasant, peaceful, or picturesque. |  We spent an idyllic week in the countryside. 我们在乡下度过了一个田园诗般的一周。 |  The island was an idyllic paradise.  这个岛屿是一个田园诗般的乐园。| 近义词: peaceful, serene, tranquil, picturesque, charming, utopian | 反义词: unpleasant, chaotic, stressful, hectic, dystopian | 词性:形容词

词源: From Greek eidyllion ‘little picture, short poem,’ from eidos ‘form, shape.’
