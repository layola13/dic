mechanograph:/ˈmɛkənəɡrɑːf/
noun
机械记录器；机械描记器
An instrument that records movements or pressure mechanically.
The mechanograph recorded the subtle vibrations of the bridge.  机械记录器记录了桥梁的细微振动。

The doctor used a mechanograph to monitor the patient's breathing. 医生用机械描记器监测病人的呼吸。
synonym: recording instrument, measuring device
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps manual recording)
n.
