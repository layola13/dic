illimitation:/ɪˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. |无限，无限制|The state of being unlimited or unrestricted.  Absence of limitation.|His power seemed to have no illimitation. 他的权力似乎没有任何限制。|近义词:boundlessness, infinity, limitlessness|反义词:limitation, restriction|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *illimitatio*, from Latin *illimitatus* (unlimited), from *in-* (not) + *limitatus* (limited), past participle of *limitare* (to limit). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leyk-* (to bend, turn).
