如果你是想查询 "what" 这个单词，以下是分析：

what: /wɒt/ (British), /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 |  Used to ask a question about something unknown or something you want to know. | Example: What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ |  Example: What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！| Synonym: which, how | Antonym: (No direct antonym for "what") | pronoun, determiner, adverb
