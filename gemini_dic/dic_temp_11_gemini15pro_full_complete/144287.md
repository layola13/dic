outspeeded:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdɪd/
verb
超过，比…更快
To surpass in speed; to move faster than.
The cheetah outspeeded the gazelle. 猎豹的速度超过了瞪羚。
He was easily outspeeded by the younger runners.  他很容易就被年轻的跑步者超过了。
近义词: outrun, overtake, surpass
反义词: lag behind, trail

词性: verb (past tense)

词源:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "speed" (verb meaning to move quickly) + "-ed" (past tense suffix)
