adiactinic:/ˌeɪdaɪækˈtɪnɪk/ | adj. | 不透化学射线的 | Not transmitting chemically active rays. Specifically, not transmitting ultraviolet light. |  The adiactinic glass protected the chemicals from degradation.  这种不透化学射线的玻璃保护了化学物质免于降解。 | synonyms:  lightproof, opaque to ultraviolet | antonyms:  translucent to ultraviolet | etymology:  From Greek *a-* "not" + *dia-* "through" + *aktis* "ray" + *-inos* "relating to" + *-ic* "of, pertaining to"
