heirships:/ˈɛərʃɪps/
n.
继承权；继承资格
The legal right to inherit property or titles.
The complex rules of heirships determined who would inherit the estate.  复杂的继承权规则决定了谁将继承遗产。
He worried about the heirships of his vast fortune. 他担心自己巨额财产的继承问题。
synonyms: inheritance, legacy, succession, patrimony
antonyms: disinheritance
noun
