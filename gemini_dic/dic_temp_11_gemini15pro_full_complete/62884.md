enlay:/ɪnˈleɪ/ /ɛnˈleɪ/| n. vt.|镶嵌；镶嵌物；镶嵌工艺|To decorate (an object) by setting pieces of wood, ivory, etc., into its surface. |The cabinet was inlaid with ivory.  这个橱柜镶嵌了象牙。|近义词：insert, embed, implant|反义词:extract, remove|n.
