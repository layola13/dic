liabilities:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|负债，债务|Amounts owed to creditors.  These represent obligations of a company payable in money, goods or services.|The company's liabilities exceed its assets. 该公司的负债超过了它的资产。|Synonyms: debts, obligations, payables|Antonyms: assets, credits|noun

liabilities:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|责任，义务|Legal or other responsibilities or duties.|The company admitted liability for the accident.  该公司承认对事故负有责任。|Synonyms: responsibility, accountability, duty|Antonyms: exemption, immunity|noun
