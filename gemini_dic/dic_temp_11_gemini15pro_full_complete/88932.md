holdfast:/ˈhoʊldˌfæst/ (US), /ˈhəʊldˌfɑːst/ (UK)| n.|固着器；抓牢物；系缚物；(尤指海藻的)固着根|A holdfast is a structure, especially in a plant, that makes it firmly attached to something.|The kelp swayed gently, anchored by its strong holdfast. 海带轻轻摇曳，被其强壮的固着器固定着。|Synonyms: anchor, grip, fastener|Antonyms: release, detachment|noun

holdfast:/ˈhoʊldˌfæst/ (US), /ˈhəʊldˌfɑːst/ (UK)| adj. (obsolete, rare)|坚定的, 固定的|Firmly fixed; steadfast.|His holdfast nature kept him from abandoning his principles. 他坚定的本性使他不放弃自己的原则。|Synonyms: steadfast, resolute, firm|Antonyms: wavering, unsteady, irresolute|adjective
