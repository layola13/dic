caffeine:/ˈkæfiːn/ /ˈkæfiːɪn/| n.|咖啡因；咖啡碱|A crystalline compound that is found especially in tea and coffee plants and is a stimulant of the central nervous system.|I need caffeine to wake me up in the morning. 我早上需要咖啡因来唤醒我。|Synonyms: stimulant, energizer|Antonyms: depressant, sedative|noun
