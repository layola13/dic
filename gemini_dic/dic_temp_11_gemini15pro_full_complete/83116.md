gustiest:/ˈɡʌstiɪst/| adj.|阵风最大的；刮风最猛的|Characterized by or having the strongest gusts of wind.|The gustiest day of the year saw trees uprooted and fences blown down.  一年中风最大的那天，树木被连根拔起，栅栏也被吹倒了。|The gustiest winds are expected near the coast. 预计沿海地区将出现最强劲的阵风。|近义词：windiest, stormiest|反义词：calmest, stillest|词性:形容词
词源:  From gusty + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse gustr "gust of wind".
