chap:/tʃæp/| n.|小伙子，家伙；（尤指）男人，小伙子；皲裂，裂口|A man or boy, often a friendly form of address.|例句：He's a good chap. 他是个好小伙。|例句：The cold weather chapped my lips. 寒冷的天气让我的嘴唇皲裂了。|近义词：fellow, guy, bloke (British), lad|反义词: (For the "man" meaning) woman, girl; (For the "crack" meaning) heal|词性:名词

chap:/tʃæp/| v.|（使皮肤）皲裂，裂开|To become rough, cracked, or sore, especially from cold weather.|例句：My skin chaps easily in the winter. 我的皮肤在冬天很容易皲裂。|例句：The wind chapped his face. 风吹裂了他的脸。|近义词：crack, split, roughen|反义词: heal, soothe|词性:动词
