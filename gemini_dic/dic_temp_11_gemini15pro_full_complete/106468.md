kowtow: /ˌkaʊˈtaʊ/ (US), /ˌkaʊˈtaʊ/ (UK)| n. vi.|叩头，磕头；卑躬屈膝；唯命是从|act of kneeling and touching one's forehead to the ground in expression of deep respect, worship, or submission, as formerly done in China; also : an obsequious act|Example:  He kowtowed to the emperor. 他向皇帝叩头。

Example:  She refused to kowtow to the boss. 她拒绝向老板卑躬屈膝。|近义词：grovel, fawn, cringe|反义词：defy, resist|词性：noun, intransitive verb
