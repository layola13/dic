necessitates:/nəˈsɛsɪˌteɪts/
verb
使成为必要；需要
to make something necessary
例句：Increased traffic necessitates widening the road. 交通流量的增加使得拓宽道路成为必要。
例句：The project necessitates close collaboration between the two teams.  这个项目需要两个团队紧密合作。

近义词：requires, demands, calls for, entails
反义词：obviates, precludes

词性:v.
