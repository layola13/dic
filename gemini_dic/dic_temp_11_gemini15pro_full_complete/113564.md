macaroon:/ˌmækəˈruːn/ | n. |杏仁饼干；马卡龙|A small, sweet cookie or biscuit, typically made with egg whites, sugar, and ground almonds or coconut. It can be chewy and soft or crisp and shell-like, often brightly colored and filled with buttercream, ganache, or jam.| I bought a box of colorful macaroons from the bakery. 我从面包店买了一盒五颜六色的杏仁饼干。|She made delicate pink macaroons for the wedding. 她为婚礼做了精致的粉色马卡龙。| synonym:cookie, biscuit, confection | antonym: N/A (It's a type of food, not a concept with an easy opposite.) | noun
