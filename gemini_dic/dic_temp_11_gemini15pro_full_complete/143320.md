outearned:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnd/
verb
胜过…的收入; 比…挣得多
to earn more money than someone else
例句：She outearned her husband by a considerable margin. 她比她丈夫挣得多得多。

例句：He consistently outearned his colleagues. 他的收入一直高于他的同事。

近义词：outstrip, surpass, exceed
反义词：underperform, lag behind
词性：动词
