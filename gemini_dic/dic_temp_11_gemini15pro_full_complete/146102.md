overelaborations:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.| 过度阐述；过于详细的说明；|An overelaboration of details can make a simple story confusing. 过于详细的细节描述会使一个简单的故事变得混乱。|
|noun| 过度阐述；过于详细的说明；|The architect's overelaborations on the design made the project unnecessarily complex.  建筑师对设计的过度阐述使得项目变得不必要地复杂。|
|近义词：overkill, redundancy, prolixity|
|反义词：simplification, conciseness, brevity|


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + elaboration (from elaborate, from Latin elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out," from e- "out" + laborare "to work").
