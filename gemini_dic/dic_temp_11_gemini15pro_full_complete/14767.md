awkwardly:/ˈɔːkwərdli/ (US), /ˈɔːkwədli/ (UK)| adv.|笨拙地，尴尬地|In a way that is not graceful or elegant; clumsily.  Showing or causing a lack of social ease or grace.|He awkwardly stumbled over his words. 他笨拙地结结巴巴。|She smiled awkwardly. 她尴尬地笑了笑。|近义词：clumsily, gracelessly, ineptly, unskillfully|反义词：gracefully, elegantly, skillfully|词性: 副词
