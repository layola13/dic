axons:/ˈæksɒnz/ (UK) /ˈæksɑːnz/ (US)| n. |轴突|A long, slender projection of a nerve cell, or neuron, that typically conducts electrical impulses away from the neuron's cell body.|The axons of certain neurons are covered with a fatty myelin sheath.|近义词:nerve fibers|反义词:dendrites|词性:noun
