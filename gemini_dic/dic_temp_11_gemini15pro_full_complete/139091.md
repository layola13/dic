obcompressed:/ɒb.kəmˈprest/
adjective
扁压的；倒卵形的；倒心脏形的
Flattened dorsoventrally or from back to front; specifically, in botany, flattened contrary to the usual way; as, an obcompressed fruit.
The obcompressed seed capsules are a distinguishing feature of this plant. 这种植物的显着特征是扁压的种子荚。
近义词:compressed, flattened
反义词:inflated, swollen
词性:adjective
