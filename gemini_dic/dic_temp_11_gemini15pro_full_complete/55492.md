distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃən/| n.|分心，分散注意力的事物，娱乐|Something that prevents someone from giving their attention to something else.|The noise of the traffic was a constant distraction. 交通噪音一直让人分心。|Synonyms: interruption, disturbance, diversion|Antonyms: concentration, focus|noun

distract:/dɪˈstrækt/| vt.|使分心，转移(注意力)，打扰|To prevent (someone) from giving full attention to something.|The loud music distracted me from my work.  吵闹的音乐使我无法专心工作。|Synonyms: divert, sidetrack|Antonyms: focus, concentrate|verb
