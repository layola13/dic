blandisher:/ˈblændɪʃər/| n.|奉承者，甜言蜜语的人|A person who blandishes; one who flatters or coaxes with soft words.|He's a notorious blandisher, always flattering his superiors. 他是个臭名昭著的奉承者，总是奉承他的上司。|近义词：flatterer, sycophant, cajoler|反义词：critic, detractor|noun

blandish:/ˈblændɪʃ/| v.|奉承，哄骗，用甜言蜜语劝诱|To coax or influence by flattery or kind words; to persuade with flattery.|She blandished him into giving her a raise. 她用甜言蜜语劝他给她加薪。|近义词：flatter, cajole, coax, wheedle|反义词：criticize, condemn|verb
