catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
n.
情感宣泄，精神净化
the process of releasing strong or repressed emotions through a particular activity, such as writing or music, in a way that helps you to understand those emotions
She went running as a form of catharsis. 她去跑步作为一种情感宣泄的方式。
近义词：release, purgation, cleansing
反义词：repression, suppression
词性: 名词

catharses:/kəˈθɑːrsɪːz/
n.
情感宣泄，精神净化（catharsis的复数形式）
the plural form of catharsis, referring to multiple instances of emotional release or purification
The artist explored various catharses through their work.  这位艺术家通过他们的作品探索了各种情感宣泄。
近义词：releases, purgations, cleansings
反义词：repressions, suppressions
词性: 名词
