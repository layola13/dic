bowelled:/ˈbaʊld/
verb
开膛破肚，取出内脏; (板球)击倒 (保龄球) 瓶
to remove the bowels or entrails of; disembowel. (in cricket) to knock over (the stumps).
The hunter bowelled the deer. 猎人给鹿开了膛破肚。
The bowler bowled the batsman out first ball. 投球手第一球就把击球手淘汰出局。
近义词: disembowel, eviscerate (for the literal meaning); dismiss, knock over (for the cricket meaning)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for the literal meaning);  (No direct antonym for the cricket meaning)
词性:v.
词源: From bowel + -ed.
