dispense:/dɪˈspens/ | v. | 分配；分发；免除；施予；配（药）| to distribute or provide something, especially in a systematic or controlled way; to prepare and give out medicine | The machine dispenses snacks.  这台机器分发零食。| synonyms: distribute, allocate, allot, dole out, hand out, give out, share out, deal out, mete out, issue, administer | antonyms: withhold, retain, keep, hoard | verb

dispense with:/dɪˈspens wɪð/ | v. | 免除；取消；摒弃| to stop using something or someone because you no longer need them | They dispensed with his services. 他们不再需要他的服务。| synonyms: forgo, do without, manage without, get along without,  eliminate | antonyms: require, need, demand | verb
