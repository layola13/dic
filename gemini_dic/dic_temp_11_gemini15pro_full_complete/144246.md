outsmelt:/ˌaʊtˈsmelt/
verb
熔炼出; 冶炼出; 比...更会冶炼
to extract (metal) by smelting. To surpass in smelting.
The new process outsmelts the old one in terms of efficiency. 新工艺在效率方面胜过旧工艺。
Near synonyms: smelt, refine, extract
Antonyms:  N/A (The antonym would be a process that prevents smelting, but there isn't a single established word.)
词性: verb
