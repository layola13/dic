dismantlement:/dɪsˈmæntl̩mənt/
noun
拆除，拆卸
The act of taking something apart.
The dismantlement of the nuclear weapons is crucial for world peace.  核武器的拆除对世界和平至关重要。

The government ordered the dismantlement of the trade barriers. 政府下令拆除贸易壁垒。

近义词:disassembly, demolition, destruction
反义词:assembly, construction, establishment
词性:noun


dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/
verb
拆除，拆卸
To take something apart.
The workers dismantled the old machinery.  工人们拆除了旧机器。

They dismantled the stage after the concert.  音乐会结束后，他们拆掉了舞台。

近义词:take apart, disassemble, break down
反义词:assemble, construct, build
词性:verb
