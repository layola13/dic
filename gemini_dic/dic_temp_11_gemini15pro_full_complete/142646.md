ostracises:/ˈɒstrəˌsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 排斥，排挤；放逐 | to exclude someone from a group or society | He was ostracised by his colleagues for his controversial views.  他因其有争议的观点而被同事排斥。 |  exclude, banish, shun, blackball | include, welcome | verb

ostracism:/ˈɒstrəˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 排斥，排挤；放逐 | exclusion from a society or group | She suffered years of ostracism after the scandal.  丑闻过后，她遭受了多年的排斥。 | exclusion, banishment, shunning, blackballing | inclusion, acceptance | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek "ostrakismos" (ὀστρακισμός), from "ostrakon" (ὄστρακον) meaning "shell, potsherd," referring to the practice of voting with potsherds to banish someone from ancient Athens.
