entombing:/ɪnˈtuːmɪŋ/
verb
埋葬，掩埋
The eruption entombed the entire city in ash. 火山爆发将整个城市掩埋在火山灰下。
The workers were entombed in the collapsed mine. 工人们被困在倒塌的矿井中。
bury, inter, enshroud
exhume, unearth
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + tomb (meaning "a burial place").  Ultimately from Latin *tumulus* meaning "mound, burial mound".
