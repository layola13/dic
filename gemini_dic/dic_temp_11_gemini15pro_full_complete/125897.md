muscularly:/ˈmʌskjələrli/
adverb
肌肉发达地；强壮地
in a way that relates to muscles or uses muscle strength
He was muscularly built and moved with easy grace. 他身材健壮，动作优雅自如。
近义词:strongly, powerfully, robustly
反义词:weakly, feebly
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From muscular + -ly.  "Muscular" comes from the Late Latin "musculāris," meaning "of or pertaining to muscles," derived from "musculus" (muscle), a diminutive of "mūs" (mouse). The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
