deceivers:/dɪˈsiːvərz/
noun
欺骗者；骗子
a person who deceives others.
例句：The deceivers were eventually caught and punished. 那些骗子最终被抓获并受到了惩罚。

例句：Be wary of deceivers who promise easy money.  要小心那些承诺轻松赚钱的骗子。


近义词：liars, tricksters, fraudsters, impostors
反义词：honest people, truth-tellers
词性：noun (plural)
