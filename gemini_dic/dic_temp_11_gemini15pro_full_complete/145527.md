overcome:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkʌm/ (UK)| v. |克服，战胜，征服|To successfully deal with a problem or difficulty.|He overcame his fear of public speaking. 他克服了对公开演讲的恐惧.|近义词：conquer, defeat, surmount, triumph over|反义词：succumb to, yield to, be defeated by|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *ofercuman, from ofer- "over" + cuman "to come".
