outhustled:/ˌaʊtˈhʌsəld/ | v. | 超越，胜过，比...更努力 | To outperform or defeat someone through greater effort, energy, or determination. |  Example:  They were outhustled by a more energetic team.  他们被一支更有活力的队伍超越了。

Example:  She outhustled her competitors and won the contract. 她比竞争对手更努力，赢得了合同。 | 近义词: outwork, outmaneuver, surpass, beat | 反义词:  be outdone, lose to | 词性: 动词
