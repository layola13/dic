overbill:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪl/| vt. n.|过高收费，超额账单|To charge someone more than they should be charged.  To issue an invoice for an amount greater than what is owed.|The contractor overbilled us by $500.  承包商多收了我们500美元。|The overbill was a result of a clerical error. 这张超额账单是由于一个文书错误造成的。|overcharge, inflate|underbill, undercharge|verb, noun
