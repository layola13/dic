harmless:/ˈhɑːrmləs/| adj.|无害的，不致伤的|Not able or likely to cause harm.|The spider is harmless and won't bite you.  这只蜘蛛是无害的，不会咬人。|近义词：innocuous, safe, inoffensive|反义词：harmful, dangerous, noxious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From harm + -less.  Harm ultimately comes from the Proto-Germanic *harmaz.
