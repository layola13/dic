builtin:/ˈbɪltɪn/| n.|内置功能，内置组件|A builtin feature or component is one that is integrated into a system or structure.|This software has several useful builtins. 这个软件有几个有用的内置功能。|integrated feature, incorporated component|external device, add-on|noun
