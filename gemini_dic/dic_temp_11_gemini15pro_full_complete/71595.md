flat-footed:/ˌflætˈfʊtɪd/| adj.|笨拙的，措手不及的，扁平足的|Clumsy or awkward in movement; unprepared; having flat feet.  Derived from the literal meaning of having flat feet, which can hinder agility.|例句：The sudden question left him flat-footed. 突如其来的问题让他措手不及。|例句：The boxer was flat-footed and easily knocked down.  这个拳击手站得很笨拙，很容易就被击倒了。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, unprepared, caught off guard|反义词:agile, nimble, prepared, ready|词性:adjective

flat-footed:/ˌflætˈfʊtɪd/| adv.|措手不及地|In a clumsy or unprepared manner.|例句：He stood flat-footed, staring at the approaching car. 他笨拙地站着，盯着驶来的汽车。|例句：She was caught flat-footed by the news.  她被这个消息弄得措手不及。|近义词：awkwardly, clumsily|反义词:nimbly, deftly|词性:adverb
