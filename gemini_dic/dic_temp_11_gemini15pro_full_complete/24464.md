britishers:/ˈbrɪtɪʃəz/
noun
英国人（带有轻蔑意味）
A term used to refer to British people, often considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its historical association with colonialism.
例句：Some older texts refer to "britishers" travelling abroad.  一些年代较久的文本会提到“英国人”出国旅行。
例句：The term "britishers" is now largely replaced by "Britons" or "British people".  现在，“英国人”一词已基本被“Britons”或“British people”取代。
近义词：Britons, British people
反义词:foreigners
词性:n.
