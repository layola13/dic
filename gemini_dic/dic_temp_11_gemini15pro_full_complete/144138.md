outsell:/ˌaʊtˈsɛl/
verb
卖得比…多；销量超过
to sell more than something or someone else
例句：Product A outsells product B by a large margin. 产品A的销量远远超过产品B。

例句：This year, SUVs are outselling sedans. 今年，SUV的销量超过了轿车。
近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip
反义词：be outsold by
词性：v.
