enubilous:/ɪˈnjuːbɪləs/ | adj. | 无云的，晴朗的 | Free from clouds; clear. | The enubilous sky was a brilliant blue. 晴朗的天空是一片灿烂的蓝色。| 近义词：cloudless, clear, sunny | 反义词：cloudy, overcast | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *ēnūbilis (“cloudless”), from ē (“out of, from”) + nūbilis (“cloudy, covered with clouds”), from nūbēs (“cloud”).
