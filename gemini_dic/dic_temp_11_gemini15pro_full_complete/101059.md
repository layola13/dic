inventer:/ɪnˈvɛntər/| n.|发明家，发明者|A person who invents something, especially a machine or other device.|例句：Thomas Edison was a prolific inventer. 托马斯·爱迪生是一位多产的发明家。|例句：The inventer of the telephone, Alexander Graham Bell, revolutionized communication. 电话的发明者亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔彻底改变了通讯方式。|近义词：creator, innovator, originator|反义词：imitator, copier|词性: noun
Etymology: From Old French *inventere, from Latin invenire ("to come upon, find, discover, devise"), from in- ("in, on, upon") + venire ("to come").
