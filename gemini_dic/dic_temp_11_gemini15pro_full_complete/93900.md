immortalizers:/ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪzərz/
n.
使不朽的人或物
People or things that make someone or something immortal.
The photographer’s portraits immortalized the beauty of the era. 这位摄影师的肖像照使那个时代的美丽永垂不朽。
commemorators, in memoriam, enshriners
killers, destroyers
noun
