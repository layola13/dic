boskage:/ˈbɒskɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbɑːskɪdʒ/| n. |灌木丛；树丛；林地|A mass of trees and shrubs; woods or woodland.|例句：The deer disappeared into the boskage. 鹿消失在灌木丛中。

例句：The garden was a delightful mix of flowers and boskage. 花园里鲜花和灌木丛交相辉映，令人赏心悦目。|近义词：woods, woodland, thicket, shrubbery, undergrowth, underbrush|反义词：clearing, meadow, grassland|词性: noun
