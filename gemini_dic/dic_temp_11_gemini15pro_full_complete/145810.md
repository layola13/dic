overdedicating: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
|adj.|过度奉献的|dedicating too much time or effort to something|She was overdedicating herself to her work, neglecting her family. 她过度投入工作，忽略了家人。|overzealous, excessively dedicated|apathetic, indifferent|adjective


overdedicate: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪˌkeɪt/
|verb|过度奉献|to dedicate too much time or effort to something|He overdedicated himself to the project and burned out. 他在这个项目上过度投入，最终精疲力竭。|overexert, overwork|neglect, underwork|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix): from Old English *ofer, meaning "above, beyond, excessively"
dedicate (verb): from Latin dedicare "to proclaim, consecrate," from de- "down, completely" + dicare "to proclaim, dedicate"
