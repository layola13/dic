disperate:/ˈdɛspərət/ (US), /ˈdɛspərɪt/ (UK)| adj.|绝望的；极度渴望的；迥然不同的；孤注一掷的|So desperate were they for food that they ate grass. 他们极其渴望食物，以至于吃草。|例句：The family is in desperate need of help. 这个家庭急需帮助。

例句：He made a desperate attempt to save the drowning child. 他拼命地试图救起溺水的孩子。

近义词：despairing, frantic, different, diverse, dissimilar|反义词：hopeful, calm, similar, alike|词性：adjective


disparate:/ˈdɪspərət/ (US), /ˈdɪspərɪt/ (UK)| adj.|迥然不同的，完全不同的，不相干的|The two cultures were so utterly disparate that she found it hard to adapt. 这两种文化截然不同，她发现很难适应。|例句：The organization is made up of disparate groups.  这个组织由完全不同的群体组成。

例句：They gathered disparate pieces of information to solve the mystery. 他们收集了各种不同的信息来解开这个谜团。

近义词：different, diverse, dissimilar, various, distinct|反义词：similar, alike, homogeneous|词性：adjective
