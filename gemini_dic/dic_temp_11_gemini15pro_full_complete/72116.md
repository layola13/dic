PHRASE->
flog a dead horse:  做无用功  Trying to revive interest in something that has no chance of success.
flog something to death:  过度使用；滥用  To overuse or exploit something to the point of exhaustion or ruination.
flog off: 廉价出售  To sell something cheaply, often because it is unwanted.
flog oneself:  拼命工作  To work very hard, to exhaust oneself.
flog it:  快速移动  To move quickly, to run or drive fast. (primarily British slang)
