autological:/ˌɔːtəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ | adj. | 自指的，自身描述的 |  An autological word is one that describes itself.  For example, "short" is a short word, and "pentasyllabic" is a pentasyllabic word.  | The word "short" is autological because it is short.  短这个词是自指的，因为它很短。

The word "English" is autological because it's a word in the English language. "英语"这个词是自指的，因为它是一个英语单词。 | 近义词: self-descriptive, self-referential | 反义词: heterological | 词性: adjective
