enucleate: /ɪˈnjuːkliˌeɪt/ /iˈnuːklieɪt/
verb
取出…的核；去核；使…突出
To remove the nucleus of a cell or an organ.  To remove something, as if by gouging.
The surgeon enucleated the patient's eye due to the severe infection.  / 由于严重感染，外科医生摘除了病人的眼球。
The government tried to enucleate the rebel leaders by offering them amnesty. / 政府试图通过提供大赦的方式，将叛军领袖剔除出去。
近义词: remove, extract, excise, extirpate
反义词: implant, insert, embed
词性: verb
