i've:/aɪv/ | contraction | 我有（I have 的缩写） |  I've is a contraction of "I have". It's used in informal speech and writing. | I've got a new car. 我有一辆新车。|  I have, I possess | I lack, I don't have | contraction

词源:
"I've" comes from the contraction of "I have". "Have" comes from Old English "habban", from Proto-Germanic "*habjaną".  The contraction itself arose during the evolution of the English language, with the apostrophe representing the omitted letters.
