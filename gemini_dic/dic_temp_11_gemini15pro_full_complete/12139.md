arugula:/əˈruːɡələ/ (US), /əˈrʌɡələ/ (UK)| n. |芝麻菜，火箭菜|A peppery-tasting salad green with long, thin leaves.  It is also known as rocket.|例句：I added some fresh arugula to my salad. 我在我的沙拉里加了一些新鲜的芝麻菜。

例句：Arugula is a popular ingredient in Italian cuisine. 芝麻菜是意大利菜中很受欢迎的食材。|近义词：rocket (UK), rucola|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
