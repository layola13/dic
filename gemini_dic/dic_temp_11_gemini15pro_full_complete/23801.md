brazen:/ˈbreɪzn/| adj.|厚颜无耻的，肆无忌惮的；黄铜色的，像黄铜的|Bold and without shame.|He told a brazen lie. 他说了一个弥天大谎。|近义词：bold, shameless, impudent, audacious, brassy|反义词：shy, timid, modest, ashamed|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *brasen, *brasyn, from Old English bræsen (“of brass, brazen”), from bræs (“brass”). Cognate with Dutch brazijn (“to braze”), German brasen (“to braze”). More at brass.
