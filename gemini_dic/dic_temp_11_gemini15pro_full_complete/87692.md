hexametric:/hɛksəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
六音步的，六步格的
Consisting of six metrical feet, especially of hexameters.  Relating to or composed in hexameter verse.
The poem is written in hexametric verse. 这首诗是用六音步诗体写成的。

The epic poem is composed in stately hexameters. 这部史诗是用庄严的六步格写成的。

synonym: hexameter
antonym: (No direct antonym for a metrical form.  Could consider shorter forms like pentameter or tetrameter as contrasting examples)
part of speech: adjective
