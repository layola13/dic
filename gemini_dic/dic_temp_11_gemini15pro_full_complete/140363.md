oil-less:/ˈɔɪl.ləs/
adjective
无油的；不加油的
Containing no oil; not requiring oil.
This oil-less fryer uses air to cook food.  这个无油炸锅用空气来烹饪食物。
The oil-less compressor is more environmentally friendly.  无油压缩机更加环保。
synonym:  non-oiled, greaseless, dry-lubricated
antonym: oiled, lubricated
词性: 形容词
