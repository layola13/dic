diathesis:/daɪˈæθɪsɪs/ /daɪˈæθəsɪs/
n.
素质，体质；易患病的体质；倾向
A predisposition or tendency, especially one that makes someone susceptible to a particular disease or condition.
例句：He has a diathesis for depression. 他有抑郁症的倾向。

例句：The doctor said she had a diathesis for skin allergies. 医生说她有皮肤过敏的体质。

近义词：predisposition, tendency, susceptibility, proneness
反义词：immunity, resistance

词性: noun
