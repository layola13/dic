endnote:/ˈɛndˌnoʊt/| n.|尾注，脚注|A note, as of explanation, emendation, or the like, added at the end of a book, chapter, etc.|The endnotes provide additional information on the sources used in the research. 尾注提供了研究中使用的来源的补充信息。|The author included several endnotes to cite her sources. 作者在文末添加了几个尾注来引用她的资料来源。|synonym: footnote, afterword|antonym: foreword, preface|noun
