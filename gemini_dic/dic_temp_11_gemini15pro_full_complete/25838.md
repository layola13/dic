burgomaster:/ˈbɜːrɡəˌmæstər/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡəˌmɑːstə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 市长，镇长 | A mayor or chief magistrate of a town in some European countries, especially in the Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany. |  The burgomaster welcomed the visiting dignitaries. 市长欢迎来访的贵宾。

The burgomaster called a town meeting to discuss the new budget. 市长召集了镇民大会讨论新的预算。 | 近义词：mayor, chief magistrate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle Dutch *burgemeester, from burger "citizen" + meester "master."  Cognate with German Bürgermeister.
