hominy:/ˈhɑːməni/| n. |玉米粥，粗磨玉米粒|Kernels of corn that have been treated with an alkali, in a process called nixtamalization.  It can be eaten whole or ground into grits.|I love hominy with chili. 我喜欢吃玉米粥配辣椒。|Synonym: grits (especially when ground), posole (Mexican hominy stew)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps other grains like rice or wheat could be considered contrasting foods)|noun
hominy grits:/ˈhɑːməni ɡrɪts/| n. |粗磨玉米粒，玉米粥|Coarsely ground hominy.|Hominy grits are a popular breakfast food in the Southern United States. 粗磨玉米粒是美国南部很受欢迎的早餐食物。|Synonym: grits|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
