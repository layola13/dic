Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he think about the new policy? 他对新政策有什么看法？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner tonight? 她今晚晚餐做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? 他做了什么才配得上这个奖？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? 她整个下午都在看什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们会在下次会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么多久？

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, neuter of hwā "who," which is from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kwod.  Related to who, how, and why.
