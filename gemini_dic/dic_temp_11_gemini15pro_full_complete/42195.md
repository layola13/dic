coumarin:/ˈkuːmərɪn/ (US), /ˈkuːmərɪn/ (UK)| n. |香豆素|A fragrant crystalline solid found in tonka beans and other plants, used in perfumes and flavorings. Also, any of a class of similar substances.|Tonka beans are a natural source of coumarin.  香豆豆是香豆素的天然来源。|Coumarin is used to give some perfumes a sweet, hay-like scent. 香豆素用于赋予某些香水一种甜美的类似干草的香味。|Synonyms: benzopyrone|Antonyms: None|Noun
