determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/ | n. |行列式；决定因素；决定物 |A quantity obtained by the addition of products of the elements of a square matrix according to a given rule. Also, a factor which decisively affects the nature or outcome of something. |The determinant of the matrix is zero.  这个矩阵的行列式为零。|synonyms: decisive factor, determining factor,  |antonyms: inconsequential factor, unimportant factor |noun


determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/ | adj. |决定性的 |Serving to determine or decide something.  |Cost was the determinant factor in our decision.  成本是我们决定的决定性因素。|synonyms: crucial, decisive, pivotal  |antonyms: insignificant, unimportant |adjective
