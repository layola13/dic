bamboos:/bæmˈbuːz/
n.
竹子；竹竿
tall, tree-like, tropical grass with hard, hollow stems
The pandas eat bamboos. 熊猫吃竹子。
Near synonyms: canes, reeds
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a plant, could consider materials like steel, plastic as functional opposites)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier bambu, from Portuguese bambu, from Malay bambu, ultimately of Kannada origin.
