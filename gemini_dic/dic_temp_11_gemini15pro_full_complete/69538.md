fellinic:/fɪˈlɪnɪk/
adjective
次要的，胆汁酸的一种
relating to or denoting fellinic acid, a bile acid.
Chenodeoxycholic acid is a major primary bile acid, whereas fellinic acid is a minor one.  鹅去氧胆酸是一种主要的初级胆汁酸，而次级胆汁酸是一种次要的胆汁酸。
Fellinic acid is a bile acid produced by intestinal bacteria from chenodeoxycholic acid. 次级胆汁酸是由肠道细菌从鹅去氧胆酸产生的胆汁酸。
近义词:secondary (in the context of bile acids)
反义词:primary (in the context of bile acids)
词性:adjective
