distich:/ˈdɪstɪk/| n.|对句，双行诗|A distich is a unit of two lines of verse, usually rhyming.  A couplet.|例句：The poet used a distich to express a complete thought. 诗人用对句表达了一个完整的意思。

例句：The epitaph on the tombstone was a simple distich. 墓碑上的碑文是一首简单的对句诗。|近义词：couplet, verse|反义词:|noun
