non-localized:/ˌnɒnˈləʊkəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非局部的，未本地化的 | Not confined or restricted to a particular place or area; spread out or diffused. | The pain was non-localized, making it difficult to diagnose. 疼痛并非局部性的，因此难以诊断。 | The infection was non-localized and had spread throughout the body. 感染不是局部的，已经扩散到全身。 | 近义词: diffuse, widespread, general, global | 反义词: localized, confined, restricted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "localized" (past participle of "localize," from "local" + "-ize").  Ultimately derived from Latin "localis" meaning "of a place."
