attendances: /əˈtɛndənsɪz/
n.
出席人数；出席率；出席
The number of people present at an event or meeting.  The act of being present at a place or event.

The attendances at the football games have been declining this season.
这个赛季足球比赛的上座率一直在下降。

Regular attendance is essential for success in this course.
定期出席这门课程对于取得成功至关重要。

近义词: presence, turnout, participation
反义词: absence, nonattendance
词性: 名词
