overrepress:/ˌōvərɪˈprɛs/| v. |过度抑制，过度压制|To repress beyond normal or desirable limits; to excessively control or suppress (feelings, desires, or expression).|The government overrepressed dissent, leading to widespread unrest. 政府过度压制异议，导致了广泛的社会动荡。

The company overrepressed its employees' creativity, stifling innovation.  这家公司过度压制员工的创造力，扼杀了创新。|近义词：suppress, stifle, repress, constrain|反义词：encourage, promote, liberate, unleash|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + repress.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Repress" comes from the Latin "reprimere," meaning "to press back" or "to check."
