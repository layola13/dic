buzzword:/ˈbʌzwɜːrd/| n. | 流行语；时髦词；行话 |A word or phrase, often an item of jargon, that is fashionable at a particular time or in a particular context.|  "Cloud computing" has become a buzzword in the tech industry. “云计算”已经成为科技行业的流行语。| synonyms: jargon, slang, catchphrase, vogue word, trendy word | antonyms:  standard terminology,  common parlance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From buzz (n.) + word (n.).  "Buzz" in this sense means excited talk or activity, implying that the word is generating a lot of attention and discussion.
