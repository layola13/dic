hollowed:/ˈhɒləʊd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊd/ (US)| adj.|空的，掏空的，凹陷的|Having a hole or empty space inside; made hollow.  Formed or shaped like a bowl or cup.|The wind whistled through the hollowed branches of the old oak tree. 风从老橡树空心的树枝间呼啸而过。|Synonyms: concave, indented, excavated, sunken|Antonyms: convex, solid, filled, protruding|adjective
