hydrometallurgical:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˌmɛtəˈlɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
湿法冶金的
Relating to the extraction of metals from their ores by dissolving them in aqueous solutions.
Hydrometallurgical processes are used to extract gold from low-grade ores. 湿法冶金工艺用于从低品位矿石中提取金。
The hydrometallurgical extraction of copper is more environmentally friendly than traditional smelting. 铜的湿法冶金提取比传统的熔炼方法更环保。
近义词: chemical, extractive
反义词: pyrometallurgical
