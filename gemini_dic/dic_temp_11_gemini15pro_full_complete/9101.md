antimonate:/ˈæntɪməneɪt/ | n. |锑酸盐|A salt of antimonic acid.  A compound containing antimony in its +5 oxidation state, typically combined with oxygen and other elements.|Sodium antimonate is used as a flame retardant. 锑酸钠用作阻燃剂。|Synonyms:  stibnate | Antonyms: None | Noun

antimonate:/ˈæntɪməneɪt/ | adj. |锑酸的|Containing or relating to antimony in its +5 oxidation state. | Antimonate compounds are often used in industrial applications. 锑酸盐化合物常用于工业应用。 | Synonyms: stibnic, antimonic | Antonyms: None | Adjective
