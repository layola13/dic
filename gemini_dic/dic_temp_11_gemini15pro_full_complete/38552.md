conclusionally:/kənˈkluːʒənəli/
adverb|总结性地；最后地；作为结论|In conclusion, therefore, conclusionally, the experiment was a success. |最终，因此，总而言之，实验是成功的。|近义词：finally, ultimately, lastly, in conclusion, to conclude|反义词: initially, firstly|词性:副词

词源: conclusionally 源自名词 conclusion（结论），加上后缀 -al（形容词后缀）构成形容词 conclusional（结论的），再加后缀 -ly（副词后缀）构成副词 conclusionally。
