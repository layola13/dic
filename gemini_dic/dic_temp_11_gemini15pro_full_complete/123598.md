monte:/ˈmɒnteɪ/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnteɪ/ (US)| n.|山;赌场;一堆筹码|A mountain; a gambling establishment; a pile of gambling chips.|例句：They visited the famous Monte Carlo casino. 他们参观了著名的蒙特卡洛赌场。

例句：The hikers climbed the steep monte.  登山者爬上了陡峭的山。|近义词：mountain, hill, casino, pile, stack|反义词:valley, plain|noun
