diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːs/ (adj.) /dɪˈfjuːz/ (v.)
adjective|弥漫的，扩散的；散开的；冗长的；不清楚的|Spread out over a large area; not concentrated. |The light was diffuse and soft. 光线弥漫而柔和。| synonyms: spread out, dispersed, scattered, rambling, verbose | antonyms: concentrated, focused, concise, succinct |词性:形容词
diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːs/ (adj.) /dɪˈfjuːz/ (v.)
verb|扩散；传播；漫射；减弱；平息|To spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people. |The company diffused the news through a press release. 公司通过新闻稿发布了消息。| synonyms: spread, disseminate, disperse, scatter | antonyms: concentrate, gather, collect |词性:动词
