contorted:/kənˈtɔːrtɪd/ /kənˈtɔːrdɪd/
adjective
扭曲的，歪曲的
twisted out of shape or meaning; distorted
Her face was contorted with pain. 她的脸因疼痛而扭曲。
近义词:twisted, distorted, deformed, warped
反义词:straight, unbent, normal, natural
词性:adjective

contort:/kənˈtɔːrt/ /kənˈtɔːrd/
verb
扭曲，歪曲
to twist or bend out of its normal shape
His features were contorted in a grimace. 他的五官扭曲成怪相。
近义词:twist, distort, deform, warp
反义词:straighten, unbend
词性:verb
