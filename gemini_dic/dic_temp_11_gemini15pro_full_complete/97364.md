infractions:/ɪnˈfrækʃənz/
n.
违规，违反；侵害
a violation or breaking of a law or agreement.
The player received two infractions for unsportsmanlike conduct.  这名运动员因违反体育道德而受到两次处罚。
He was punished for several traffic infractions. 他因几项交通违规行为而受到处罚。
breaches, violations, offenses, transgressions
compliance, obedience
noun
