medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/ (US), /ˈmɛdɪvæk/ (UK)| n. v.|医疗后送；医疗后送飞机（直升机等）|The act or process of evacuating someone who needs medical care, especially from a remote area, typically by aircraft. Also refers to the aircraft used in such an evacuation.|例句：The soldier was medevaced after being injured in combat.  这名士兵在战斗中受伤后被医疗后送。

例句：The medevac helicopter landed on the hospital roof. 医疗后送直升机降落在医院屋顶上。|近义词：medical evacuation, air ambulance|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
