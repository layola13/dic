gyrencephala:/ˌdʒaɪrɛnˈsɛfələ/
| n. |  脑回动物|Animals (especially mammals) having a brain with convolutions (gyri) on the surface of the cerebral cortex.| Gyrencephala are characterized by their folded brains, which allow for a larger surface area of the cortex. 脑回动物的特点是大脑有褶皱，这使得皮层的表面积更大。| Most mammals, including humans, belong to the gyrencephala.  大多数哺乳动物，包括人类，都属于脑回动物。|  convoluted brain animals| lissencephala | noun
