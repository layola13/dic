complaintive: /kəmˈpleɪntɪv/ | adj. | 抱怨的；哀叹的；诉苦的 | Expressing or suggestive of complaint; whining. |  Her complaintive tone irritated me. 她抱怨的语气让我很恼火。| The dog gave a complaintive whine when its owner left. 主人离开时，狗发出一声哀鸣。 | 近义词: whining, querulous, plaintive, doleful | 反义词: cheerful, happy, contented, satisfied | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *complantivus, from Latin complanare (“to lament, bewail”), from com- (“together”) + planare (“to lament”).
