defen(s)able /dɪˈfɛnsəbl/
adj.
可辩护的，可防御的
Capable of being defended, justified, or protected.
His actions were not defensible in court. 他的行为在法庭上是站不住脚的。
justifiable, supportable, maintainable
indefensible, unjustifiable
adjective
