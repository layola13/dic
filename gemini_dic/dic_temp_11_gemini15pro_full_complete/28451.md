carburet:/ˈkɑːrbjʊret/ /ˈkɑːrbəret/ | v. | 汽化，使...与空气混合 | to mix (a gas, especially air) with a flammable gas or vapour to produce a mixture that will explode when ignited |  The mechanic will carburet the engine. 机械师将汽化发动机。 | 近义词: vaporize, atomize | 反义词: condense | verb

carburetor:/ˈkɑːrbjʊreɪtər/ /ˈkɑːrbəreɪtər/ | n. | 化油器 | a device in an internal combustion engine for mixing air with a fine spray of liquid fuel | The carburetor needs to be cleaned. 化油器需要清洗。 | 近义词:  fuel mixer | 反义词: fuel injector | noun
