flitch:/flɪtʃ/| n.|一块腌肉，侧腹肉，（树木的）原木|a side of cured bacon; the whole side of a pig, salted and cured. Also, a large piece cut lengthwise from a log of timber.|The butcher prepared a flitch of bacon for smoking. 肉贩准备了一块腌肉熏制。|Synonyms: slab, side (of bacon), piece|Antonyms: slice, sliver|noun

flitch:/flɪtʃ/| v.|把…切成大片|to cut (a log) lengthwise into flitches.|They flitched the oak tree into thick planks. 他们把橡树纵向锯成厚板。|Synonyms: saw, cleave, split|Antonyms: join, assemble|verb
