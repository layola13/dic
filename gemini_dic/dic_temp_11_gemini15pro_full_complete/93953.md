immure:/ɪˈmjʊr/| vt.|监禁，禁闭；把…嵌在墙上|To enclose or confine within walls; to imprison. |The king immured the traitor in a dungeon. 国王把叛徒囚禁在地牢里。|近义词： imprison, confine, incarcerate, enclose|反义词: liberate, free, release|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin immurare "to wall in," from Latin in- "in" + murus "wall."
