embracers:/ɪmˈbreɪsər z/
n.
拥抱者；信奉者；接受者
People who embrace a new technology or idea. 
Those who readily accept or adopt something new, such as a new technology, belief, or way of life.
例句：The early embracers of electric vehicles faced many challenges.  电动汽车的早期拥护者面临着许多挑战。

例句：The company is looking for embracers of change who are willing to adapt to new strategies.  该公司正在寻找愿意适应新战略的变革拥护者。

近义词：adopters, supporters, advocates, proponents
反义词：resisters, opponents, skeptics
词性：名词
