exscriptural:/ˌɛkˈskrɪptʃərəl/
adjective
非圣经的，圣经以外的
Not found in or not derived from the Bible; outside the canon of scripture.
His arguments were based on exscriptural traditions. 他的论点基于圣经以外的传统。
Near synonyms: extra-biblical, apocryphal, uncanonical
Antonyms: scriptural, biblical, canonical
词性: adjective
