lanceolate:/ˈlænsioʊleɪt/| adj.|披针形的，矛尖形的|Shaped like the head of a lance; tapering to a point at each end, especially at the apex.|The willow tree has lanceolate leaves. 柳树有披针形的叶子。|近义词：lance-shaped, spear-shaped, tapering|反义词:ovate, cordate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *lanceolatus*, from lanceola (“small lance”), diminutive of lancea (“lance”).
