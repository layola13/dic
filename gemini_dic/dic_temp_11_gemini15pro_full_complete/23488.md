brahman:/ˈbrɑːmən/ | n. | 婆罗门，梵，宇宙精神 | A member of the highest Hindu caste, that of the priesthood. Also, the ultimate principle or reality in the universe. |  The brahmans performed the sacred rituals. 婆罗门执行神圣的仪式。|  The ancient texts describe Brahman as the source of all creation. 古代文献将梵描述为所有创造的源泉。| synonyms: priest, cleric; absolute, ultimate | antonyms: layman, outcast; relative | noun

brahman:/ˈbrɑːmən/ | n. | (印度瘤牛) | A breed of Zebu cattle, originating in India. Distinguished by a large hump over the shoulders.  | The brahman is known for its heat tolerance. 瘤牛以其耐热性而闻名。| The farmer raised a herd of brahman cattle. 农场主养了一群瘤牛。| synonyms: Zebu, Bos indicus | antonyms: N/A | noun
