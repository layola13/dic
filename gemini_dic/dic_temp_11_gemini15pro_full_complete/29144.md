casemated:/ˈkeɪsmeɪtɪd/
adjective
设有炮台的；装入炮郭的；处于炮台中的
Provided with casemates; enclosed in a casemate.
The fort is casemated, providing protection for the artillery.  这个堡垒设有炮台，为炮兵提供保护。
The soldiers were safe in their casemated positions. 士兵们在炮郭阵地里很安全。
近义词: fortified, protected, shielded
反义词: exposed, vulnerable, unprotected
词性: adjective

casemate:/ˈkeɪsmeɪt/
noun
炮郭；炮台；装甲炮座
A fortified gun emplacement or armored housing for artillery.
The casemate protected the cannon from enemy fire. 炮郭保护大炮免受敌人炮火攻击。
The ship's main guns were housed in casemates.  舰艇的主炮都装在炮座里。
近义词: bunker, emplacement, fortification
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "open field")
词性: noun

词源: From French *casemate*, from Italian *casamatta*, literally 'slaughtered house', from *casa* 'house' + *matta* 'dark, dull, matted'.  Originally referred to a vaulted chamber in a fortification.
