nonjudicial:/ˌnɒn.dʒuːˈdɪʃ.əl/
adjective
非司法的；非裁判性的
Not judicial; not pertaining to the administration of justice.
The dispute was settled through nonjudicial means.  这个纠纷通过非司法途径解决了。
The organization provides nonjudicial conflict resolution services.  该组织提供非司法冲突解决服务。
近义词:extrajudicial, informal, nonlegal
反义词:judicial, legal
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + judicial.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "judicial" relates to the administration of justice.
