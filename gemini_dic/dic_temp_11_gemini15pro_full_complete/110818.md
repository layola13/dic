linnet:/ˈlɪnɪt/| n.|红雀，亚麻雀|A small brown and grey finch with a red breast and forehead, common in Europe.|例句：A linnet perched on the branch, singing its cheerful song. 一只红雀栖息在树枝上，唱着欢快的歌。

例句：The linnet's song is a complex melody of trills and whistles. 红雀的歌声是由颤音和哨音组成的复杂旋律。|近义词：finch, songbird|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|noun
