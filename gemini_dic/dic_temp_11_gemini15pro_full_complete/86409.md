hee:/hiː/
interjection
嘿；咴（赶牲口的声音）
A sound used to attract attention or to express mild surprise, triumph, or scorn.
例句：Hee, look at that! 嘿，看那个！
例句：Hee hee, I tricked him. 嘿嘿，我把他骗了。
近义词：ha, hey
反义词: (No direct antonym for this interjection)
词性：感叹词
