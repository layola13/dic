carburization:/ˌkɑːrbjʊraɪˈzeɪʃn/
|n.|渗碳，碳化|The process of increasing the carbon content of the surface of steel by exposing it to a carbonaceous atmosphere at elevated temperatures.|The carburization process hardened the surface of the gear. 渗碳工艺使齿轮表面硬化。|synonyms: carbonization, cementation|antonyms: decarburization|noun


carburise:/ˈkɑːrbjʊraɪz/
|v.|渗碳，使碳化|To increase the carbon content of the surface of (steel) by exposing it to a carbonaceous atmosphere at elevated temperatures.|The gears were carburised to improve their wear resistance. 这些齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐磨性。|synonyms: carbonize, cement|antonyms: decarburize|verb
