nonprovidently:/ˌnɒnˈprɒvɪd(ə)ntli/
adverb
不谨慎地，不节俭地，无远见地
in a way that lacks foresight or prudence; improvidently.
He had nonprovidently spent his inheritance. 他不谨慎地花光了遗产。
近义词: improvidently, rashly, imprudently, unwisely, foolishly
反义词: providently, prudently, wisely, carefully, thoughtfully
词性: adverb

nonprovident:/ˌnɒnˈprɒvɪd(ə)nt/
adjective
不顾将来的，不节俭的，无远见的
lacking foresight or thrift; improvident.
His nonprovident habits led him into debt. 他不顾将来的习惯让他负债累累。
近义词: improvident, imprudent, shortsighted, wasteful, extravagant
反义词: provident, prudent, farsighted, thrifty, frugal
词性: adjective


词源:
From non- + provident, from Latin provident- ‘foreseeing, providing’, from the verb providēre, from pro- ‘before’ + vidēre ‘to see’.
