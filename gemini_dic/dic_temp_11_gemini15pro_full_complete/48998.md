deglutination:/ˌdiːɡluːtɪˈneɪʃn/ /ˌdɛɡluːtɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |吞咽；咽下|The act of swallowing.  The process of passing food from the mouth, through the pharynx and esophagus, to the stomach.|The deglutination of the large pill was difficult.  吞下这颗大药丸很困难。|synonyms: swallowing, ingesting |antonyms: regurgitation, vomiting|noun

deglutination:/ˌdiːɡluːtɪˈneɪʃn/ /ˌdɛɡluːtɪˈneɪʃn/ | v. |吞咽；咽下|To swallow.|He deglutitated the medicine with a gulp of water. 他喝了一口水吞下了药。|synonyms: swallow, ingest |antonyms: regurgitate, vomit|verb
