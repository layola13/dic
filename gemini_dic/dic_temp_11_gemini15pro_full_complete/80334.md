goldfields:/ˈɡoʊldˌfiːldz/
n.
金矿区；金矿产地
A region where gold is mined.
The Australian goldfields attracted prospectors from all over the world in the 19th century. 19世纪，澳大利亚的金矿吸引了来自世界各地的探矿者。
The Klondike goldfields were the site of a major gold rush. 克朗代克金矿区是淘金热的中心地带。
synonyms: gold mines, gold regions, gold country
antonyms: barren lands, wasteland
noun
