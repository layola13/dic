blonds: /blɑːndz/ /blɒndz/
noun
金发女郎；金发男子（尤指女人）
People with blond/blonde hair, especially women.

She's a natural blonde. 她天生一头金发。
Many blondes have blue eyes. 许多金发的人都长着蓝眼睛。

近义词: fair-haired, golden-haired
反义词: brunette, dark-haired
词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Old French *blond, from Frankish *blund, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *blundaz (“blond”).
