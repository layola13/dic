hermetical:/hərˈmɛtɪkl/
adjective
密封的；炼金术的；神秘的
Completely sealed; airtight; relating to alchemy or the occult.
The tomb was hermetically sealed for centuries.  这座坟墓被密封了几个世纪。
近义词: airtight, sealed, impervious, impassable
反义词: open, porous, permeable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin hermeticus, from Greek Hermētikos, relating to Hermes Trismegistus, a legendary Hellenistic figure associated with alchemy and other occult sciences.  来自晚期拉丁语 hermeticus，源自希腊语 Hermētikos，与赫尔墨斯·特里斯墨吉斯忒斯有关，他是一位与炼金术和其他神秘科学相关的传奇希腊化人物。
