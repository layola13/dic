indoxylic: /ɪnˈdɒksɪlɪk/ | adj. | 吲哚基的，吲哚氧基的 | Relating to or containing the indoxyl group, a derivative of indole. |  Indoxylic acid is a metabolite of indole. 吲哚氧基酸是吲哚的代谢产物。| The indoxylic compounds are important in certain biological processes. 吲哚氧基化合物在某些生物过程中很重要。 | 近义词: indoxyl-related | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From indoxyl + -ic.  Indoxyl itself comes from  indigo + -yl.  Ultimately from Greek *Indikon (Indian dye)
