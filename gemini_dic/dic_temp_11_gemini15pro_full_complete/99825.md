interminglements:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡlmənts/ | n. | 交织，混合，交融 | The act or process of intermingling; the state of being intermingled.  A mixture of things that have become intermingled. | The complex interminglements of historical fact and fiction make it a challenging read.  这本小说中史实与虚构的复杂交织使它成为了一本富有挑战性的读物。 | 近义词: mixtures, blendings, combinations | 反义词: separations, divisions | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From intermingle + -ment. Intermingle comes from inter- + mingle. Inter- is a prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," or "reciprocally." Mingle comes from Old English mengan, meaning "to mix."  The suffix "-ment" forms nouns expressing the result or product of an action.
