granter:/ˈɡræn.tər/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːn.tər/ (UK)| n.| 授予者；批准者；让与人|Someone who grants something, such as a privilege, a property, or a right.|The granter of the license retains ownership of the software.  许可证的授予者保留软件的所有权。|Synonyms: giver, donor, bestower|Antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun
