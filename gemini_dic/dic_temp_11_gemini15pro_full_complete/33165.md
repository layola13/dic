chromatype:/ˈkrəʊməˌtaɪp/ | n. |染色体组型；染色体组型图；核型 | A photographic representation of the chromosomes of a single cell, arranged in order of decreasing size. | Example: The chromatype of a person with Down syndrome shows an extra copy of chromosome 21.  唐氏综合症患者的染色体组型图显示21号染色体多了一个副本。

Example:  Scientists analyze chromatypes to identify genetic abnormalities. 科学家分析染色体组型图来识别遗传异常。| Synonym: karyotype, karyogram | Antonym: N/A | Noun
