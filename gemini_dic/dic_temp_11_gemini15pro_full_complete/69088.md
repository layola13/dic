fausen:/ˈfaʊzən/| v. | 欺骗，哄骗 | To deceive or trick someone. | He tried to fausen the old lady out of her money. 他试图骗走老太太的钱。|

例句：
The con man fausened several people into investing in his fake company. 骗子哄骗几个人投资他的假公司。

The salesman fausened me into buying a car I couldn't afford.  销售员骗我买了一辆我买不起的车。


近义词：deceive, trick, cheat, swindle, defraud

反义词：be honest, be truthful

词性: verb


词源：From Middle High German *vüsen*, related to *fusen* (“to fart, break wind”).  The original meaning seems to have been "to impose empty promises," later generalizing to "deceive."
