bribeless:/ˈbraɪbləs/| adj.|不受贿的；廉洁的；清廉的|Not susceptible to bribery; incorruptible.|The judge was known for being a bribeless public servant.  这位法官以清正廉洁的公仆形象而闻名。|He has a reputation as a bribeless official. 他以廉洁的官员而闻名。|近义词：incorruptible, honest, upright|反义词：corrupt, venal, bribable|词性:形容词
bribe:/braɪb/| n. vt.|贿赂；贿赂物|Something, such as money or a favor, offered or given to a person in a position of trust to influence that person's views or conduct.|He offered me a bribe to keep quiet. 他贿赂我让我保持沉默。|They bribed the officials to get the contract. 他们贿赂官员以获得合同。|近义词：inducement, payoff|反义词：reward (for good deed)|词性:名词，动词
