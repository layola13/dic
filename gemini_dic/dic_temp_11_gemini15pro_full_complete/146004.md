overdosages:/ˈoʊvərˌdoʊsɪdʒɪz/
n.
过量；过量服用；过量用药
An excessive dose of a drug.  The act of taking an excessive dose of a drug. Instances of excessive doses of a drug.
The hospital reported several overdosages of painkillers last month. 上个月医院报告了几起止痛药过量服用的病例。
Overdosages can be accidental or intentional. 过量服用可能是意外也可能是故意的。
近义词: overdoses
反义词: underdoses
词性: 名词 (plural of overdose)


overdose:/ˈoʊvərdoʊs/
n.
过量；过量服用；过量用药
An excessive dose of a drug.
The musician died of a drug overdose. 这位音乐家死于药物过量。
He took an overdose of sleeping pills. 他服用了过量的安眠药。
近义词: excess
反义词: underdose
词性: 名词


overdose:/ˈoʊvərdoʊs/
v.
过量服用；过量用药
To take an excessive dose of a drug.
He overdosed on heroin. 他过量服用了海洛因。
She accidentally overdosed on her medication. 她不小心服用了过量的药物。
近义词: poison
反义词: underdose
词性: 动词

词源:  "overdose" comes from "over" (meaning "excessive") + "dose" (meaning "a measured quantity of medicine").  The word "dose" ultimately comes from the Greek word "dosis" meaning "a giving" or "gift."
