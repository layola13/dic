nonseclusion:/ˌnɑːnsɪˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 非隔离，非隐居 | The state of not being secluded or isolated. |  The patient thrived in an environment of nonseclusion, interacting regularly with staff and other patients.  病人在一个非隔离的环境中茁壮成长，定期与工作人员和其他病人互动。

| Example:  Her preference for nonseclusion led her to choose a bustling city over a quiet country life. 她对非隐居生活的偏爱使她选择了一个熙熙攘攘的城市而不是宁静的乡村生活。


| Synonym: integration, inclusion, social interaction, openness, accessibility | Antonym: seclusion, isolation, solitude, privacy, withdrawal | noun
