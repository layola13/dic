on-sale: /ˌɑːnˈseɪl/ /ˌɒnˈseɪl/
adjective
打折的，减价销售的
Available for purchase at a reduced price.
例句：The on-sale items were quickly snatched up by eager shoppers.  打折商品很快就被热切的购物者抢购一空。

例句：I bought this dress on-sale last week. 我上周打折买了这条裙子。

近义词：discounted, reduced, bargain, cheap
反义词：full-price, regular-price
词性：adjective
