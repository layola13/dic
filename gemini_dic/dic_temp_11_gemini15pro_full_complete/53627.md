discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/ /dɪˈsərn/| v. |辨别，察觉，识别|To perceive or recognize something, typically something subtle or obscure.|例句：I could discern a faint light in the distance. 我能辨认出远处微弱的光。

例句：It was difficult to discern his true motives. 很难辨别他的真实动机。

近义词：perceive, detect, distinguish, recognize, differentiate, make out|反义词：miss, overlook, ignore|词性：动词
