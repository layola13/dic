overcensors:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsərz/ | v. | 过度审查 | To censor excessively, often to the point of suppressing important or legitimate information. | The government overcensors the media, restricting freedom of speech. 政府过度审查媒体，限制了言论自由。|  The platform overcensors user-generated content, removing even harmless jokes.  该平台过度审查用户生成的内容，甚至删除了无害的笑话。 |  censor heavily, suppress, restrict, muzzle |  allow, permit, encourage free speech | verb
