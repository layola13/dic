falsifiabilities:/ˌfɔːlsɪfaɪəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|可证伪性(复数)|The quality of being able to be proven false; the susceptibility of a theory or hypothesis to being disproved by observation or experiment.|The scientist stressed the importance of falsifiabilities in any valid scientific theory.  科学家强调了任何有效科学理论中可证伪性的重要性。|Synonym: testability, refutability|Antonym: unverifiability, irrefutability|Noun
falsifiability:/ˌfɔːlsɪfaɪəˈbɪlɪti/
|n.|可证伪性|The quality of being able to be proven false; the susceptibility of a theory or hypothesis to being disproved by observation or experiment.|A key principle of the scientific method is the falsifiability of hypotheses. 科学方法的一个关键原则是假设的可证伪性。|Synonym: testability, refutability|Antonym: unverifiability, irrefutability|Noun
