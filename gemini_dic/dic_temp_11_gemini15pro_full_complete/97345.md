/ˈɪnfoʊz/ (US) /ˈɪnfəʊz/ (UK)| n.|信息，资料|Information, facts, or details about something.|例句：The infos on the website are outdated.  网站上的信息已经过时了。|近义词：data, facts, details, intelligence|反义词: disinformation, misinformation|noun

/ɪnˈfjuːz/ (US) /ɪnˈfjuːz/ (UK)| vt.|灌输，注入，泡制|To fill something or someone with a particular feeling or quality.  To steep (leaves, herbs, etc.) in liquid (such as water) without boiling, so as to extract the flavor or healing properties.|例句：He infused his students with enthusiasm. 他给学生们注入了热情。|近义词：instill, imbue, inspire, permeate, steep, brew|反义词: drain, extract|verb

词源: Old English _hwæt_, neuter of _hwā_ "who," related to _hwȳ_ "why."  The word has cognates in other Germanic languages.  The verb form "infuse" comes from Latin _infundere_, meaning "to pour into."  The noun form "infos" is a colloquial shortening of "information."
