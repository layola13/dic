birchest:/ˈbɜːrtʃɪst/ | adj. |  最多的桦树；最像桦树的 |  Most abounding in birches; most like a birch. |  The birchest path led through the woods.  这条最多桦树的小径穿过树林。 | 近义词:  birchy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider treeless, barren) | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From birch + -est (superlative suffix).  Birch comes from Old English *birce, from Proto-Germanic *birkjō, related to Old Norse bjǫrk.
