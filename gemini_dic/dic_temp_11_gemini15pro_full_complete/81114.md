grammatical: /ɡrəˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 语法的；符合语法规则的 | Relating to grammar; conforming to the rules of grammar. |

Example 1:  His grammatical explanation was very clear. 他的语法解释非常清楚。

Example 2: The sentence is not grammatical.  这个句子不符合语法。


Synonym: syntactic, well-formed

Antonym: ungrammatical, incorrect


Etymology:  Late Middle English: from late Latin grammaticus, from Greek grammatikos ‘of letters, of literature’, from gramma ‘letter of the alphabet’.
