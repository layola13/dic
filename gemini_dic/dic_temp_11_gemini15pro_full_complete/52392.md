didactic:/dɪˈdæktɪk/ | adj. | 教诲的，说教的 | Intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive.  |  The professor's lectures were didactic, focusing on conveying practical knowledge and ethical principles.  教授的讲座很有教益，侧重于传授实用知识和伦理原则。 | synonyms:  instructive, educational, pedagogic, preachy | antonyms:  uninformative,  non-instructive, entertaining | adjective
