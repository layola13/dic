comfreys: /ˈkʌmfriz/ | n. | 紫草属植物；聚合草 |  A perennial plant of the borage family, with hairy leaves and bell-shaped flowers, several kinds of which are traditionally used in herbal medicine. | Comfreys are known for their healing properties. 紫草以其愈合特性而闻名。 | synonyms: knitbone, boneset, bruisewort | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Old French *cunferie, from Latin confirma, meaning "to strengthen" due to its traditional use in bone healing.
