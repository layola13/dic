eastward:/ˈiːstwərd/ | adj. adv.|向东的，向东|Moving or situated in the direction of east. |The ship sailed eastward. 这艘船向东航行。|近义词：easterly, eastbound|反义词:westward|词性:形容词, 副词
eastward:/ˈiːstwərd/ | n.|东方|The direction or region lying to the east. |The sun rises in the eastward. 太阳从东方升起。|近义词：east|反义词:west|词性:名词

词源: Old English ēastweard, from ēast (east) + -weard (toward).
