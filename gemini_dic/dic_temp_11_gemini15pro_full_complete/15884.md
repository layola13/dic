ballooned:/bəˈluːnd/
v.|膨胀，激增|To increase rapidly or expand like a balloon. |The national debt ballooned in the last decade. 国家债务在过去十年里激增。|
近义词:increased, expanded, surged, swelled, escalated
反义词:shrunk, decreased, contracted, diminished
词性:动词

ballooned:/bəˈluːnd/
adj.|膨胀的|Having expanded like a balloon. |Her eyes ballooned with surprise. 她的眼睛因惊讶而睁得老大。|
近义词:expanded, inflated, swollen
反义词:deflated, shrunken
词性:形容词

PHRASE->
balloon out: 鼓胀起来  The sails ballooned out in the wind. 帆被风吹得鼓了起来。
balloon up: 迅速膨胀 The hot air balloon ballooned up quickly. 热气球迅速膨胀起来。
balloon to: 膨胀到… The company's debt ballooned to unprecedented levels. 公司的债务膨胀到前所未有的水平。
balloon with: 因…而膨胀  His chest ballooned with pride. 他的胸膛因自豪而挺了起来。
let something balloon:  放任…膨胀 They let the problem balloon until it was too late.  他们放任问题恶化，直到为时已晚。
