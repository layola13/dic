apostrophising: /əˈpɒstrəfaɪzɪŋ/
verb
用撇号表示省略; 省略;  将…变成所有格
Addressing or mentioning something by apostrophe.  Marking possession or omission with an apostrophe.
He was apostrophising the spirit of his dead wife. 他正在向他亡妻的灵魂倾诉。
She is apostrophising her book to her parents.  她将她的书献给她的父母。
Synonyms: addressing, invoking, appealing to
Antonyms: ignoring, neglecting
