cystidean: /sɪˈstɪdiən/
cystidean: | adj. n.| 囊胞海百合的；海囊百合纲动物 |Relating to or denoting an extinct echinoderm of the class Cystoidea, which existed from the Ordovician to Devonian periods and was characterized by a spherical or sac-like body. Also, a member of this class. | The cystidean fossils were remarkably well-preserved. 这些囊胞海百合化石保存得非常好。| The museum's collection boasts several rare cystidean specimens.  这家博物馆的收藏品中有一些珍稀的海囊百合纲动物标本。| 近义词: cystoid | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *Cystoidea*, from Ancient Greek *κύστις* (kústis, “bladder, pouch”) + *-oidea* (“resembling”).
