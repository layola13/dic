co-captain:/ˈkoʊˌkæptɪn/| n.|联合队长；副队长|A co-captain shares the responsibilities of leadership with another person.|Example 1:  Mary and John are co-captains of the debate team. 玛丽和约翰是辩论队的联合队长。

Example 2: The team elected two co-captains to share the workload.  团队选举了两名联合队长来分担工作。|近义词：assistant captain, joint captain|反义词:|noun
