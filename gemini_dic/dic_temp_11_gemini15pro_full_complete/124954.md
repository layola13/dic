muffler:/ˈmʌflər/ (US) /ˈmʌflə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消音器；围巾；头巾|A device used to deaden the sound of escaping gases from an engine or other machine. Also, a scarf worn around the neck for warmth.|例句：The car's muffler needs to be replaced.  这辆车的消声器需要更换。

例句：He wrapped a thick woolen muffler around his neck. 他用一条厚厚的羊毛围巾围住脖子。|近义词：silencer, scarf, neck warmer|反义词:amplifier (for the device sense)|noun
