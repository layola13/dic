deritualizing:/diːˈrɪtʃuəlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|去仪式化；使非仪式化|The process of removing the ritualistic elements from something; making something less formal or ceremonial.|Modern society is deritualizing many aspects of life, such as marriage. 现代社会正在使生活的许多方面去仪式化，例如婚姻。|The museum is deritualizing the way it presents its artifacts, focusing more on interactive experiences.  博物馆正在改变其展示文物的方式，更加注重互动体验。|secularizing, demystifying|ritualizing|verb
