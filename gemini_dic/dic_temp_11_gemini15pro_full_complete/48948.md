defy:/dɪˈfaɪ/| v.| 违抗，反抗，挑战；经受住，顶住；使不可能，使落空 | They defied the government's ban on public gatherings. 他们违抗了政府禁止公众集会的禁令。| 近义词:resist, oppose, challenge | 反义词:obey, comply, surrender |词性:动词

词源:  From Old French *desfier* "renounce, disavow, challenge," from *des-* "away, from" (from Latin *dis-*) + *fier* "to trust, confide in" (from Latin *fidare* "to trust").
