extolled:/ɪkˈstəʊld/ /ɪkˈstoʊld/
verb
赞美；颂扬；称赞
praised enthusiastically.
The critics extolled her performance as the best of the year.  评论家们称赞她的表演是年度最佳。
He extolled the virtues of a simple life. 他颂扬简朴生活的优点。

近义词: praised, lauded, acclaimed, celebrated, commended, glorified
反义词: criticized, condemned, denounced, disparaged, belittled

词性: verb (past tense and past participle of extol)

词源: From Late Middle English *extollen*, from Latin extollere (“to raise, lift up; to praise, extol”), from ex- (“out, up”) + tollere (“to raise, lift”).
