axiom:/ˈæksiəm/ | n. | 公理；格言；自明之理 | A statement or proposition which is regarded as being established, accepted, or self-evidently true. | Example 1:  It is a widely held axiom that governments should not negotiate with terrorists.  政府不应该与恐怖分子谈判，这是一个被广泛接受的公理。

Example 2:  One of the key axioms of the theory of relativity is that the speed of light is constant. 相对论的一个关键公理是光速恒定。 | synonyms:  principle, theorem,  truism,  postulate,  maxim,  dictum,  dogma | antonyms:  paradox, absurdity, fallacy | noun
