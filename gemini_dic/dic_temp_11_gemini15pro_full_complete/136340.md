nonresignation:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən/
n.
不辞职；不放弃
The state of not resigning from a position or office; refusal to give up.
His nonresignation surprised everyone, given the scandal.  他并没有辞职，鉴于丑闻缠身，这令每个人都感到惊讶。
The CEO's nonresignation sent a clear message to investors. 首席执行官的不辞职向投资者发出了明确的信息。
perseverance, tenacity, persistence
resignation, surrender
noun
