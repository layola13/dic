homegirl's:/ˈhoʊmɡɜːrlz/ |n. (possessive)|闺蜜的，女性朋友的|A term of endearment and solidarity among close female friends, indicating possession belonging to a homegirl.  It's often used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).| My homegirl's car is so cute! 我闺蜜的车好可爱！|Synonyms: friend's, pal's, bestie's|Antonyms: enemy's, rival's|Possessive Noun
