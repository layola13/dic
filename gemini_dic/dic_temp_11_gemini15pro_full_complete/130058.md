non-poisonous:/ˌnɒnˈpɔɪzənəs/ | adj. | 无毒的 | Not poisonous; harmless. |  These berries are non-poisonous and safe to eat. 这些浆果无毒，可以安全食用。

The snake is non-poisonous, so you don't have to worry.  这条蛇没有毒，所以你不必担心。 | 近义词: harmless, innocuous, safe | 反义词: poisonous, toxic, venomous | 词性: adjective

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + poisonous (from Old French poison + -ous)
