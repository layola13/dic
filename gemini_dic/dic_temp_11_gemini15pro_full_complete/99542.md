intergovernmentally:/ˌɪntərˌɡʌvərnˈmentəli/
adverb
政府间地；跨政府地
in a way that involves governments of different countries working together
例句：The issue was discussed intergovernmentally.  这个问题在政府间进行了讨论。

例句：The project is being funded intergovernmentally.  这个项目是由各国政府共同资助的。

近义词：multinationally, internationally
反义词: domestically, nationally
词性: adverb
