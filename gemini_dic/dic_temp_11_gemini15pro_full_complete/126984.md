narwhal:/ˈnɑːrwɔːl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n. |独角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale (Monodon monoceros) that possesses a large "tusk" from a protruding canine tooth, found in the Arctic waters.|例句：The narwhal is a fascinating creature of the Arctic. 独角鲸是北极一种迷人的生物。

例句：Researchers are studying the narwhal's unique tusk. 研究人员正在研究独角鲸独特的长牙。|近义词：unicorn whale, toothed whale|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal)|词性:noun
