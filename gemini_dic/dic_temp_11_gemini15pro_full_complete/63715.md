ephemerous: /ɪˈfɛmərəs/ | adj. | 短暂的，朝生暮死的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemerous. 樱花的美丽是短暂的。

The joy was ephemerous, soon replaced by a deep sadness.  快乐是短暂的，很快就被深深的悲伤所取代。


近义词: transient, fleeting, short-lived, momentary | 反义词: enduring, lasting, permanent, perpetual | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ephēmeros* "lasting only a day," from *epi* "on, for" + *hēmera* "day."
