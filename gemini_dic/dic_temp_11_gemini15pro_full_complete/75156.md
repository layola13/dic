fritterer:/ˈfrɪtərər/| n.|浪费者，挥霍者|Someone who fritters away time or money; a squanderer.|He was a fritterer of his inheritance, spending it all on frivolous pursuits. 他挥霍了继承的遗产，把所有的钱都花在了无谓的追求上。|Synonyms: squanderer, waster, profligate|Antonyms: saver, economizer|noun
fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/| v.|浪费，挥霍|To waste or squander (something, typically time or money) in a frivolous way.|She frittered away her allowance on candy. 她把零用钱都浪费在了糖果上。|Synonyms: squander, waste, dissipate|Antonyms: save, conserve, hoard|verb
