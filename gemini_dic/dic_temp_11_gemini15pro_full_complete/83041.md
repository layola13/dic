gupta:/ˈɡʊptə/| n. |古普塔|A common South Asian surname, derived from the Sanskrit word "goptr" meaning "protector" or "preserver".  It is also the name of an ancient Indian dynasty (c. 320-550 CE) known for its advancements in art, science, and literature.|例句：The Gupta empire was a golden age for India. 古普塔帝国是印度的黄金时代。

例句：Mr. Gupta is a renowned historian specializing in ancient India. 古普塔先生是一位著名的古代印度历史学家。|近义词：N/A (as a surname)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
