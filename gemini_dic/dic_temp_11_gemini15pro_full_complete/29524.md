catatonic:/ˌkætəˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|紧张性精神分裂症的；僵住症的；呆若木鸡的|Relating to or characterized by catatonia, a state of immobility and unresponsiveness.|He remained catatonic for days after the trauma. 他在遭受创伤后几天都处于紧张性精神分裂症状态。|The catatonic patient sat motionless, staring blankly ahead.  紧张性精神分裂症病人一动不动地坐着，茫然地盯着前方。|近义词：unresponsive, immobile, stuporous, inert|反义词：responsive, active, alert|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek *katatonos* "stretched tight," from *kata-* "down" + *teinein* "to stretch."
