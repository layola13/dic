delphinoidea:/ˌdɛlfɪˈnɔɪdiə/ | n. |海豚超科|A superfamily of aquatic mammals that includes oceanic dolphins, porpoises, and river dolphins.|Dolphins, porpoises, and river dolphins belong to the Delphinoidea superfamily. 海豚、鼠海豚和河豚都属于海豚超科。|近义词:Cetacea, Odontoceti |反义词:None |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin, from *Delphinus* (genus name for dolphins) + *-oidea* (superfamily suffix).  *Delphinus* ultimately derives from Ancient Greek δελφίς (delphís, “dolphin”).
