culvert:/ˈkʌlvərt/| n.|涵洞，暗渠，管道|A culvert is a tunnel carrying a stream or open drain under a road or railway.|The culvert under the highway allows the stream to flow unimpeded. 高速公路下的涵洞让溪流畅通无阻。|Synonyms: conduit, channel, duct, pipe, underpass|Antonyms: overpass, bridge|noun
