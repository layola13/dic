characterization:/ˌkærəktərəˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 描述；人物塑造 | The act of describing the distinctive nature or features of someone or something, especially in literature. | The author's characterization of the villain was brilliant. 作者对反派的刻画非常精彩。|  synonyms: portrayal, depiction, representation | antonyms: misrepresentation, distortion | noun

characterization:/ˌkærəktərəˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 特征;特性 | A description of the qualities or peculiarities of a person or thing. | The scientist gave a detailed characterization of the newly discovered species. 科学家对新发现的物种进行了详细的描述。| synonyms: description, account, definition | antonyms:  vagueness, ambiguity | noun
