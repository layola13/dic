irreversibly:/ˌɪrɪˈvɜːrsəbli/
ad.|不可逆转地|in a way that cannot be changed back to a previous state; permanently.|例句：The landscape has been irreversibly altered by human activity.  人类活动不可逆转地改变了地貌。|近义词：permanently, irrevocably, definitively|反义词：reversibly, temporarily|词性:副词
Etymology:  From "irreversible" + "-ly".  "Irreversible" comes from "in-" (not) + "reversible".  "Reversible" comes from "reverse" (to turn back) + "-ible" (capable of). The root "reverse" ultimately comes from Latin "revertere" (to turn back).
