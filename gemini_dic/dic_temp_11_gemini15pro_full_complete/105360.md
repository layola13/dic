kicking-in:/ˈkɪkɪŋ ɪn/| phrasal verb |开始生效；起作用；(指钱或帮助)到位；(尤指不愉快的事情)开始发生|The painkillers are starting to kick in. 止痛药开始起作用了。|近义词:take effect, begin working, come into effect|反义词:wear off, stop working|verb phrase

kicking-in:/ˈkɪkɪŋ ɪn/| noun |生效；起作用|(The kicking-in of the new regulations caused some confusion.) 新规定的生效引起了一些混乱。|近义词:implementation, commencement, onset|反义词:cessation, termination|noun

词源: "Kick in" originates from the literal sense of kicking a door inward to force entry.  Figuratively, it refers to something suddenly starting or becoming active.
