gate-legged:/ˈɡeɪtˌleɡɪd/| adj.|折叠式的，闸门腿式的|Having legs that swing out like gates to support a hinged leaf or drop leaf.  A gate-legged table has legs that swing out to support hinged leaves.|We have a beautiful antique gate-legged table in our dining room. 我们餐厅里有一张漂亮的古董折叠桌。|Near synonyms: drop-leaf, folding, hinged|Antonym: fixed|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "gate" (referring to the swinging motion of the legs) + "legged" (having legs).
