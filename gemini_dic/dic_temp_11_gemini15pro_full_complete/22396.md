boogie:/ˈbuːɡi/| n. vi.|布吉舞，即兴演奏|A lively, rhythmical dance to pop or rock music.  A style of blues played on the piano.|例句：Let's go boogie! 咱们去跳布吉舞吧！

例句：He played a boogie on the piano. 他用钢琴演奏了一段布吉乐。|近义词：dance, groove, jam|反义词:waltz, slow dance|词性:noun, verb


boogie:/ˈbuːɡi/| vi.|跳布吉舞，迅速离去|To dance the boogie. To leave quickly.|例句：They were boogying all night. 他们整晚都在跳布吉舞。

例句：I gotta boogie, I'm late for work! 我得赶紧走了，上班要迟到了！|近义词：depart, leave, rush|反义词:stay, remain|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From West African languages, perhaps through the French "bouger" (to move, stir). Originally referred to a rent party with dancing.
