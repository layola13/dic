dropsied:/ˈdrɒpsid/ | adj. | 水肿的，患水肿病的 | Affected with, or characteristic of, dropsy (an old term for edema, the swelling of soft tissues due to excess fluid).  |  The dropsied patient struggled to breathe. 水肿病人呼吸困难。| 近义词：edematous, swollen, bloated | 反义词: slim, thin | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From *dropsical*, from *dropsy* + *-ical*. *Dropsy* comes from Middle English *dropesie*, from Old French *ydropisie*, ultimately from Greek *hydrops* "dropsy," from *hydōr* "water."
