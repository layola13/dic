giving-back:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ bæk/| n.|回馈，回报|the practice of making charitable contributions of time, money, or resources to benefit the community or a particular cause.  It's often seen as a way to express gratitude for one's success or good fortune. |Many companies have giving-back programs that support local charities. 许多公司都有回馈当地慈善机构的项目。|synonyms: philanthropy, charity, contribution, reciprocation|antonyms: selfishness, greed, taking|noun
