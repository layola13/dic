ministerially:/ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəli/
adverb
部长地；官员地；形式上地
In a manner befitting a minister; officially; perfunctorily.
The documents were signed ministerially, without much thought given to their content. 文件只是例行公事地签署了，没有仔细考虑其内容。
The priest ministerially performed the wedding ceremony. 牧师照例举行了婚礼仪式。
Synonyms: officially, formally, routinely, perfunctorily
Antonyms: informally, personally, thoughtfully
