lowborn:/ˈloʊbɔːrn/| adj.|出身低微的，卑贱的|Born into a low social class; of humble origin.  Of low rank by birth.|He was lowborn but rose to become a respected leader. 他出身低微，但最终成为受人尊敬的领袖。|近义词：baseborn, humble, plebeian|反义词：noble, aristocratic, highborn|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *lēaggeboren, from lēage (“low”) + geboren (“born”).  Cognate with German *lägeborn (obsolete).
