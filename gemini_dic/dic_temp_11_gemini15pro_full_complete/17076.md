batik:/ˈbætɪk/| n.|蜡染布；蜡染技法|A method of dyeing fabric by covering parts of it with a coat of wax which prevents the dye from reaching the covered areas.|The museum has a fine collection of Indonesian batiks.|近义词：dye, textile, fabric|反义词:|noun

词源: Javanese word *mbatik* which means "to write".  The word refers to the process of drawing dots and lines with wax on cloth before dyeing, resembling a form of writing on the fabric.
