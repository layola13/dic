devanagaris:/ˌdeɪvənɑːˈɡɑːriːz/| n.|天城文，梵文|A script used for writing Sanskrit, Hindi, and other Indic languages. 天城文是一种用于书写梵语、印地语和其他印度语言的文字。|The Devanagaris script is known for its distinctive horizontal line at the top. 天城文以其顶部独特的水平线而闻名。|近义词:script, alphabet|反义词:None|词性:noun
