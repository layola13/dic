inclinational:/ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 倾斜的，倾向的 | Relating to inclination; having a leaning or tendency towards something. |  She has an inclinational preference for classical music. 她倾向于喜欢古典音乐。|  The tower has an inclinational angle of five degrees. 这座塔有一个五度的倾斜角。| 近义词: inclined, predisposed, prone, biased | 反义词: averse, disinclined, opposed | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "inclination" + "-al". "Inclination" comes from Late Latin "inclinatio," from Latin "inclinare" meaning "to bend, lean," from "in-" (towards) + "clinare" (to bend).
