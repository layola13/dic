bicolored:/baɪˈkʌlərd/
adjective
双色的，两色的
Having two colors.
The bicolored flag symbolized the unity of the two nations.  双色旗象征着两个国家的团结。
The bicolored flower attracted many bees.  这朵双色花吸引了许多蜜蜂。
Near Synonyms: two-colored, two-toned, dichromatic
Antonyms: monochromatic, single-colored
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + colored. "Bi-" comes from Latin, meaning "two". "Colored" comes from the past participle of the verb "color," ultimately from Latin "color".
