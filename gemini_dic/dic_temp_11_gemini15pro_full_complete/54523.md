disk-braked:/ˈdɪsk breɪkt/ | adj. | 盘式制动的 | Equipped with disk brakes.  A system where brake pads squeeze against a rotating disc to slow or stop a vehicle. |  The new car features disk-braked wheels for improved stopping power. 新车采用盘式制动车轮，以提高制动能力。| Synonym: disc-braked | Antonym: drum-braked | adjective
