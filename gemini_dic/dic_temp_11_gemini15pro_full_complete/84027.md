half-erased:/ˌhæf ɪˈreɪst/ | adj. | 半擦除的；几乎消失的 | Partially erased; almost gone. | The inscription on the tombstone was half-erased. 墓碑上的铭文已经半模糊了。 | The faded photograph showed a half-erased image of her grandmother.  褪色的照片上是她祖母几乎消失的影像。 | 近义词：faded, indistinct, faint, partially erased | 反义词：clear, distinct, visible | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "erase" (from Old French eraer "to scrape, scratch out," from Latin eradere "to scrape out," from ex "out" + radere "to scrape")
