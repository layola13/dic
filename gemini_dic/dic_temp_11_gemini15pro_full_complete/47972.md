deciles: /ˈdɛsaɪlz/ /ˈdɛsɪlz/ | n. |十分位数；十分位点|  Values that divide a data set into ten equal parts. Each part represents 1/10th (or 10%) of the data set. |  The income deciles show the distribution of income across the population. 收入十分位数显示了人口中的收入分配。|  Researchers analyzed the health outcomes across income deciles. 研究人员分析了不同收入十分位数的健康结果。|  Synonyms: quantiles, percentiles (specifically the 10th, 20th,...90th percentiles)| Antonyms: N/A | noun

decile: /ˈdɛsaɪl/ /ˈdɛsɪl/ | n. |十分位数；十分位点| Any of nine values that divide a frequency distribution into ten groups, each containing one tenth of the total population. | The fifth decile represents the median value. 第五个十分位数代表中位数。 | The government published data on income deciles. 政府公布了收入十分位数的数据。| Synonyms: quantile, percentile (specifically the 10th, 20th,...90th percentiles for the nine deciles)| Antonyms: N/A | noun
