overanalysis:/ˌoʊvərəˈnæləsɪs/
noun
过度分析
Excessive or obsessive analysis; analyzing something beyond a reasonable or productive degree.
He suffered from overanalysis and often second-guessed his decisions. 他过度分析，经常事后质疑自己的决定。

overanalysis:/ˌoʊvərəˈnæləsɪs/
noun
过度分析
Excessive or obsessive analysis; analyzing something to the point where it becomes counterproductive.
Her overanalysis of the situation led to paralysis by analysis. 她对情况的过度分析导致了分析瘫痪。

overanalysis:/ˌoʊvərəˈnæləsɪs/
noun
过度分析
The process of analyzing something too much, often to the point of hindering understanding or decision-making.
Overanalysis of the data can sometimes obscure the bigger picture. 对数据进行过度分析有时会掩盖全局。


近义词:overthinking, rumination, scrutiny
反义词:underestimation, cursory glance, superficial examination
词性:noun
