fermentability:/fɜːrmɛntəˈbɪləti/ /ˌfɜːrmɛntəˈbɪlədi/| n. |发酵性，可发酵性|The ability of a substance to undergo fermentation.  The ease with which a substance can be fermented.|The fermentability of different sugars varies. 不同糖类的发酵性各不相同。|Synonyms: fermentive capacity, fermentation potential|Antonyms: non-fermentability (not a commonly used word), resistance to fermentation|Noun
