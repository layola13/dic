instigant:/ˈɪnstɪɡənt/
adjective
煽动的；刺激的；教唆的
Something that instigates or incites an action or feeling.
The instigant speech riled up the crowd. 这番煽动性的演讲激怒了人群。
近义词：provocative, inciting, stimulating, abetting
反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying
词性: adjective

instigant:/ˈɪnstɪɡənt/
noun
煽动者；刺激物；教唆犯
A person or thing that instigates or incites an action or feeling.
The police are still searching for the instigant of the riot. 警方仍在搜寻骚乱的煽动者。
近义词：provocateur, agitator, inciter
反义词：peacemaker, mediator, conciliator
词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin *instigant- (nominative instigans), present participle of instigare "to incite, urge on," from in- "in, on" + stigare "to prick, goad."
