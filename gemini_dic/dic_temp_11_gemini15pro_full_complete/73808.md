formatter:/ˈfɔːrmætər/ | n. |格式化工具，格式器|A formatter is a program or a device that arranges data into a specific format, such as text, images, or other digital content.| The formatter automatically adjusted the margins of the document. 格式化工具自动调整了文档的页边距。 | synonym: styler, organizer | antonym: disorganizer, scrambler | noun

/fɔːrˈmæt/ | v. |格式化，安排格式 |To format means to arrange something, especially text or data, in a particular way or style.| He formatted the hard drive before installing the new operating system. 他在安装新的操作系统之前格式化了硬盘。| synonym: arrange, style, organize | antonym: disorganize, mess up | verb
