fixatives:/ˈfɪksətɪvz/
|n.|固定剂；定影剂|Substances used to preserve the structure of biological tissues or cells, preventing decay and maintaining their form for microscopic examination or other analysis.|The pathologist used fixatives to prepare the tissue samples for biopsy. 病理学家使用固定剂来制备组织样本用于活检。|synonyms: preservatives, stabilizers|antonyms: solvents, destroyers|noun
fixative:/ˈfɪksətɪv/
|n.|固定剂；定影剂|A substance used to preserve something, especially a biological specimen.|Formalin is a common fixative used in histology. 福尔马林是组织学中常用的固定剂。|synonyms: preservative, stabilizer|antonyms: solvent, destroyer|noun
