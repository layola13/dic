nonheritabilities:/ˌnɒnherɪtəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 非遗传性，非遗传率 |  The degree to which traits are not influenced by genetic factors, but rather by environmental factors or random chance. |  Studies suggest the nonheritabilities of certain personality traits are higher than previously thought. 研究表明，某些人格特征的非遗传性比以前认为的要高。| The nonheritabilities of some diseases are still under investigation. 一些疾病的非遗传性仍在研究中。| synonyms: non-heredity, acquired characteristics | antonyms: heritabilities, heredity | noun
