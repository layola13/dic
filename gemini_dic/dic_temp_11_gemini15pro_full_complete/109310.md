lending-libraries:/ˈlendɪŋ ˈlaɪbrɛriz/| n.|借阅图书馆|Libraries that lend books.|例句：Lending-libraries were popular before everyone could afford to buy books. 在每个人都能买得起书之前，借阅图书馆很受欢迎。

lend:/lend/| v.|借出|To give something to someone for a short time, expecting it to be returned.|词性:verb

library:/ˈlaɪbrɛri/| n.|图书馆|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|词性:noun
