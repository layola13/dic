misapprehensively:/ˌmɪsˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪvli/
adverb
误解地，错误地
in a way that misapprehends or misunderstands something.
She misapprehensively thought he was offering a compliment. 她误以为他在恭维她。
近义词: mistakenly, erroneously, wrongly
反义词: correctly, accurately, rightly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From misapprehensive + -ly.  Misapprehensive is from mis- + apprehensive.  Apprehensive comes from Late Latin apprehensivus, from Latin apprehendere "to seize, grasp," from ad- "to" + prehendere "to grasp, seize."
