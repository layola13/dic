cyclopedia:/ˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/| n.|百科全书|A book or set of books giving information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject, typically arranged alphabetically.|Example 1: He consulted the encyclopedia for information on the history of Egypt. 他查阅了百科全书，寻找有关埃及历史的信息。

Example 2:  The online encyclopedia is constantly updated with new information.  在线百科全书不断更新新信息。|近义词：lexicon, dictionary|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin encyclopaedia, from Ancient Greek ἐγκύκλιος παιδεία (enkýklios paideía, “general education”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + κύκλος (kýklos, “circle”) + παιδεία (paideía, “education”).  Originally referred to a complete course of instruction.
