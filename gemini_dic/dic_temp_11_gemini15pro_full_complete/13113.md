astrotheology:/ˌæstroʊθiˈɒlədʒi/
n.
星象神学，宇宙神学
The study of the astronomical and cosmological beliefs and symbolism of ancient cultures, often relating them to deities and religious practices.
Astrotheology explores how ancient civilizations incorporated celestial observations into their religious worldviews.
Astrotheology examines the astronomical origins of religious myths and symbols.
近义词:archaeoastronomy, cosmology of religion
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From astro- (from Greek ἄστρον ástron 'star') + theology (from Greek θεολογία theología 'study of God').
