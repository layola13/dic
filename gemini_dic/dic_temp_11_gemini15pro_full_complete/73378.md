forehammer:/ˈfɔːrˌhæmər/| n.|前锤，前落锤|A heavy hammer, often lifted by a cam and allowed to drop by gravity, used especially in crushing ore or forging metal.|The forehammer pounded the ore into smaller pieces.|Synonyms: drop hammer, trip hammer|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps a grinding mill which uses a different crushing method)|noun
