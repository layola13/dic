gestalts:/ɡəˈʃtɑːlts/ /ɡəˈʃtɔːlts/
n.
格式塔; 完形
an organized whole that is perceived as more than the sum of its parts.
The gestalt of the city changed dramatically after the earthquake.  这座城市的整体面貌在地震后发生了巨大的变化。
The painting has a powerful gestalt.  这幅画具有强烈的整体感。
configuration, form, whole, entirety
fragment, part, piece
noun
