indestructible:/ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktɪbl/
adjective
不可摧毁的；坚不可摧的
not capable of being destroyed
例句：Diamonds are considered virtually indestructible. 钻石被认为是几乎不可摧毁的。
例句：This new material is said to be indestructible. 据说这种新材料是坚不可摧的。
近义词：unbreakable,imperishable,invulnerable
反义词：destructible,fragile,breakable
词性：形容词

词源:
From in- (not) + destructible (capable of being destroyed), from Latin destructus, past participle of destruere (to destroy).
