jackstraw:/ˈdʒækˌstrɔː/| n.|稻草人；引火物；一种儿童游戏|A game in which a pile of straws or thin sticks is let fall in a heap, and each player in turn tries to remove one at a time without disturbing the others. Also, any of the straws or sticks used in this game.|The children were playing jackstraws on the floor. 孩子们在地板上玩杰克游戏。|Near synonyms: pickup sticks, spillikins|Near antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider organized games with specific rules as a loose contrast)|noun

jackstraw:/ˈdʒækˌstrɔː/| n.|杂乱无章的东西；凌乱的东西|Something in a state of disorder; a jumble.|His desk was a jackstraw of papers and pens. 他的桌子乱七八糟地堆满了纸和笔。|Near synonyms: jumble, mess, clutter, disarray|Near antonyms: order, tidiness, organization|noun
