campana:/kæmˈpɑːnə/ /kæmˈpeɪnə/| n.|钟，铃；钟形物|A bell, especially a large, deep-toned one.  It can also refer to a bell-shaped object.|The church campana rang out across the valley. 教堂的钟声响彻山谷。|Synonyms: bell, chime, gong|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

campana:/kæmˈpɑːnə/ /kæmˈpeɪnə/| n.|（尤指植物的）钟状部分|The bell-shaped part of a flower, especially a corolla.|The delicate campana of the lily was a pure white. 百合花的钟状花冠是纯白色的。|Synonyms: corolla, bell|Antonyms: N/A|noun
