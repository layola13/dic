crape:/kreɪp/| n.|绉布，绉纱，黑色绉纱(用于丧事)，可丽饼|A thin, light fabric with a wrinkled surface, often used for mourning clothes. Also, a thin pancake.|She wore a black crape dress to the funeral. 她穿着一件黑色绉纱连衣裙参加葬礼。|近义词:crepe, gauze|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crepe, from Old North French crespe, from Old French crespe, past participle of cresper (“to curl, crisp”), from Latin crispus (“curled, wrinkled”).
