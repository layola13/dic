cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ /kəʊˈhiːʒən/| n. |凝聚力；团结；结合；内聚力|The cohesion of the group was remarkable, especially given their diverse backgrounds.  这个团队的凝聚力非凡，特别是考虑到他们不同的背景。|The molecules are held together by forces of cohesion. 分子靠内聚力结合在一起。|近义词：unity, solidarity, coherence, adhesion|反义词：division, disunity, separation|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cohaesio, from Latin cohaes-, past participle stem of cohaerēre "to cleave together," from com- "together" + haerēre "to stick, cling"
