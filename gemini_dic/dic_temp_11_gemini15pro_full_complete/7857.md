ante-up:/ˌæntiˈʌp/| phrasal verb |下注；付钱；拿出(钱) | to pay (money); to put up (stakes, as in poker) | Example:  The dealer told the players to ante up.  庄家告诉玩家们下注。

Example:  We all had to ante up $10 for the pizza. 我们都得付10美元买披萨。| 近义词: pay, contribute, chip in | 反义词: withdraw, withhold | 词性: 动词短语

词源:  From the card game poker, where "ante" refers to a forced bet made by all players before the hand is dealt. "Up" indicates the action of putting forth the money.  来自扑克牌游戏，其中 "ante" 指的是所有玩家在发牌前下的强制赌注。"Up" 表示拿出钱的动作。
