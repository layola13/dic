nonatmospherical:/ˌnɒnˌætməsˈfɛrɪkəl/
adjective
非大气的
Not relating to, existing in, or caused by the atmosphere.
The experiment was conducted in a nonatmospherical environment to eliminate the effects of air pressure.  这个实验是在非大气环境中进行的，以消除气压的影响。
The spacecraft's nonatmospherical propulsion system allowed it to maneuver in the vacuum of space. 宇宙飞船的非大气推进系统使其能够在真空空间中进行机动。
synonyms: extra-atmospheric, exatmospheric
antonyms: atmospheric
