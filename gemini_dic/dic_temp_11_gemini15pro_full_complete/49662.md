demiscible:/ˌdɛmɪˈsɪbəl/
adjective
部分互溶的
Partially miscible; capable of being mixed to a limited extent.  Two liquids are demiscible if they can be mixed together to form a single phase over a certain range of concentrations, but not over all concentrations.
Oil and water are demiscible to a small extent, as they can form a cloudy mixture.  油和水在一定程度上是部分互溶的，因为它们可以形成浑浊的混合物。
Certain organic solvents are demiscible with water. 某些有机溶剂与水部分互溶。
