comart:/ˈkoʊmɑːrt/| n.|共同市场；共同体；贸易伙伴关系|A comart is a group of countries or businesses that have agreed to trade freely with each other, often without tariffs or other restrictions.|例句：The two nations formed a comart to boost their economies. 这两个国家组成了一个共同市场以促进它们的经济。|例句：The comart agreement facilitated trade between member states. 共同市场协议促进了成员国之间的贸易。|近义词：common market, trading bloc, trade association|反义词:|n.
