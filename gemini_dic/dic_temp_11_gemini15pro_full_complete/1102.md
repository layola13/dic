accesses:/ˈæksesɪz/
| n. (pl.) |访问; 使用; 通道; (情感的) 迸发| The lawyer requested access to the confidential files. 律师要求查阅机密文件。|近义词：entries, approaches|反义词: exits, departures|词性:名词复数

accesses:/ˈæksɛsɪz/
| v. (third-person singular simple present) |访问; 使用; 获取|She accesses the internet through a dial-up connection. 她通过拨号连接访问互联网。|近义词：uses, retrieves|反义词: blocks, restricts|词性:动词第三人称单数

词源: From Old French *acces, from Latin accessus (“approach, arrival, entrance”), past participle of accedere (“to approach”).
