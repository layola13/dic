monopolistic:/ˌmɒnəpəˈlɪstɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəpəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|垄断的，具有垄断性质的|Relating to or constituting a monopoly; having exclusive control of a commodity or service in a particular market.|The company engaged in monopolistic practices to control prices.  这家公司从事垄断行为以控制价格。|Synonyms: exclusive, controlling, dominant|Antonyms: competitive, open-market|adjective

monopolistic:/ˌmɒnəpəˈlɪstɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəpəˈlɪstɪk/| n.|垄断者|A person or business that has a monopoly.| Some argue that large tech companies are becoming monopolistics, stifling innovation.  一些人认为大型科技公司正在成为垄断者，扼杀创新。|Synonyms: monopolist|Antonyms: competitor|noun
