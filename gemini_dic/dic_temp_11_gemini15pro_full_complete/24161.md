brickbat:/ˈbrɪkbæt/| n.|砖块，辱骂，批评|A piece of brick, especially one used as a missile; a harsh or critical remark.|例句：He hurled a brickbat at the police car. 他向警车投掷了一块砖头。

例句：The politician received a barrage of brickbats from the press. 这位政治家受到了来自媒体的猛烈抨击。|近义词：insult, criticism, slur, barb|反义词：compliment, praise|noun
