flannels:/ˈflænəlz/ | n. | 法兰绒制品（尤指衬衫或睡衣裤）; 法兰绒布 | Soft, woven fabric, typically made of wool or cotton, known for its warmth and slightly napped surface. Often used for shirts, pajamas, and other clothing items. |  He always wears flannels in the winter. 他冬天总是穿法兰绒衬衫。| 近义词: pajamas, shirts | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

flannel:/ˈflænəl/ | n. | 法兰绒; 法兰绒布 | A soft, slightly napped fabric of wool or cotton or both. |  She bought some flannel to make a new blanket. 她买了一些法兰绒做新毯子。 | 近义词: fleece, suede | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Welsh *gwlanen (“woolen garment”), from gwlân (“wool”) +‎ *-en (diminutive suffix).
