dramaticule: /ˌdræməˈtɪkjuːl/
noun
小戏剧，短剧，滑稽短剧
A short, often humorous, dramatic piece.
She wrote a dramaticule for the school play. 她为学校戏剧表演写了一个滑稽短剧。
Synonyms: skit, sketch, vignette
Antonyms: epic, saga
n.
