isodimorphic:/ˌaɪsoʊdaɪˈmɔːrfɪk/
adjective
同二形的；类质异像的
Existing in two different crystalline forms, like two chemically distinct substances that crystallize in the same form.  Relating to or exhibiting isodimorphism.
Isodimorphic crystals can sometimes be mistaken for polymorphs. 同二形晶体有时会被误认为是多晶型物。
The two minerals are isodimorphic, meaning they share a similar crystal structure despite having different chemical compositions. 这两种矿物是同二形的，这意味着尽管它们的化学成分不同，但它们具有相似的晶体结构。
Synonyms: isomorphous (partially)
Antonyms:  anisomorphic, heteromorphic
词性: adjective
