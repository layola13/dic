overseasoned:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzənd/| adj.|调味过重的，加太多调料的|Containing too much seasoning.|The soup was overseasoned and too salty. 汤的调料放太多了，太咸了。|近义词：overspiced, too salty, heavily seasoned|反义词:underseasoned, bland, tasteless|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "over, above, beyond") + "seasoned" (from Old French saison, meaning "season, sowing time," ultimately from Latin sationem, meaning "a sowing").  The combination creates the meaning of "excessively seasoned."
