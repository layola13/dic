sticky:/ˈstɪki/ | adj. | 粘的；粘性的；棘手的 | covered with a sticky substance; difficult to deal with | The caramel made my fingers sticky.  焦糖糖浆让我的手指粘糊糊的。 | adhesive, gluey, gummy | slippery, smooth | adjective
Etymology: From Old English *stician "to stick, adhere," from Proto-Germanic *stik- (source also of Old Norse stika "to stick, pierce," Old High German stihhan "to stick, prick," Gothic stikan "to stick").
