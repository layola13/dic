overcrowdednesses:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
过度拥挤的状态
The state of being excessively crowded.
The overcrowdednesses of the refugee camps were appalling.  难民营人满为患的状况令人震惊。
近义词: congestion, overcrowding
反义词: spaciousness, roominess
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From overcrowded + -ness.  Overcrowded itself comes from over- + crowded, with crowded ultimately deriving from crowd.
