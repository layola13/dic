hindlimbs:/ˈhaɪndˌlɪmz/| n. |后肢|The hindlimbs of a kangaroo are much larger and stronger than its forelimbs. 袋鼠的后肢比前肢更大更强壮。|例句：The dog injured its hindlimbs in the accident. 这只狗在事故中伤了后肢。

The cheetah uses its powerful hindlimbs to accelerate quickly. 猎豹用它强有力的后肢快速加速。|近义词：rear legs, back legs|反义词：forelimbs, front legs|词性:noun

Etymology: From "hind" (meaning posterior) + "limbs" (meaning legs or arms).  "Hind" comes from Old English "hindan," meaning "behind," from Proto-Germanic "*hindaną." "Limb" comes from Old English "lim," of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "limar" (branches).
