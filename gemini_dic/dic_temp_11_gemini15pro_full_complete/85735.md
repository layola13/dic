hawker:/ˈhɔːkər/ (UK), /ˈhɔːkər/ (US)| n.|小贩，叫卖者|A person who travels around selling goods, typically by shouting at people to attract their attention.|The hawker was selling fresh fruit and vegetables in the market square.  市场广场上的小贩在卖新鲜的水果和蔬菜。|vendor, peddler, salesman|shopkeeper, retailer|noun

hawker:/ˈhɔːkər/ (UK), /ˈhɔːkər/ (US)| v.|叫卖，兜售|To sell goods by going from place to place and calling out to people.|He hawked his wares on the streets of London. 他在伦敦街头叫卖他的商品。|peddle, vend, sell|buy, purchase|verb
