docibility:/ˌdɑːsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌdəʊsəˈbɪləti/
noun
可教性，温顺，驯良
The quality of being easily taught or controlled; teachableness.
The dog's docility made him easy to train.  这只狗的温顺使它很容易训练。
Synonyms:  malleability, flexibility,  tractable, compliant,  amenable, biddable
Antonyms:  intransigence, obstinacy,  unruliness,  unyielding, recalcitrant
词性: 名词
