deploringly:/dɪˈplɔːrɪŋli/
adverb
悲叹地，痛惜地
in a way that shows you think something is very bad and you strongly disapprove of it
She shook her head deploringly. 她悲叹地摇了摇头。
近义词: regretfully, sadly, ruefully, lamentably
反义词: approvingly, gladly, joyfully
词性: adverb

词源:
From the verb "deplore" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Deplore" comes from the Latin "deplorare" meaning "to weep for," from "de-" (down, completely) + "plorare" (to cry out, weep).
