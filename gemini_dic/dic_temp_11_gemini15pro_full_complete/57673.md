drawing:/ˈdrɔːɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdrɔːɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.| 绘画，图画；绘图；抽签；提款 |A drawing is a picture or diagram made with a pencil, pen, or crayon rather than paint.  The act of producing such a picture. |例句：She showed me a drawing of her dream house. 她给我看了她梦想中的房子的图画。

|例句：He is skilled in the art of drawing. 他擅长绘画艺术。|近义词：sketch, illustration, picture, diagram, delineation, representation|反义词: N/A|词性:noun/verb
