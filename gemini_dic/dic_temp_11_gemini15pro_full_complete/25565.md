bumbag:/ˈbʌmˌbæɡ/ (US) /ˈbʌmˌbæɡ/ (UK)| n.|腰包，屁股包|A small bag worn around the waist, often used for carrying personal items like money, keys, and a phone.  Originally worn with the pouch at the back, hence the name, but now more commonly worn at the front.|She wore a bumbag to keep her valuables safe while travelling. 她旅行时戴着腰包来保管贵重物品。|近义词：fanny pack (US), waist pack, belt bag|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps backpack or suitcase as they offer larger storage)|noun
