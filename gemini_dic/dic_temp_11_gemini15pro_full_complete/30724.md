cerusite:/ˈsɛrəsaɪt/ /ˈsɪərəˌsaɪt/
n.|白铅矿|A mineral consisting of lead carbonate (PbCO3), and an important ore of lead. It is usually colorless or white, but may be gray or grayish black when impure.|Cerusite is a common secondary mineral found in the oxidized zone of lead deposits.|近义词:lead carbonate|反义词: None|词性: noun

词源: From German *Cerussit*, from Latin *cerussa* (“white lead”) + *-it* (mineral suffix).
