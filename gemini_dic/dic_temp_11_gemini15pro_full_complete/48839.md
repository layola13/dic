defoliators:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪtərz/
|n.|落叶剂; 食叶动物; 脱叶剂|An agent, such as a chemical spray or an insect, that causes defoliation.|The defoliators stripped the trees bare in a matter of weeks.  这些落叶剂在几周内就把树木的叶子剥光了。|Synonyms: leaf-strippers, defoliants|Antonyms: fertilizers, growth stimulants
|noun|


defoliator:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪtər/
|n.|落叶剂; 食叶动物; 脱叶剂|An agent, such as a chemical spray or an insect, that causes defoliation.|Caterpillars are common defoliators in gardens. 毛毛虫是花园里常见的食叶动物。|Synonyms: leaf-stripper, defoliant|Antonyms: fertilizer, growth stimulant
|noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (indicating removal) + foliate (to cover with leaves) + -or/-er (agent or one that performs an action). Ultimately from Latin *de- + folium (leaf).
