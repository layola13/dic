doni:/ˈdoʊni/ | n. | 印度尼西亚的一种小船 | A small Indonesian boat, typically used for fishing or transport. |  The fishermen used a doni to navigate the shallow waters. 渔民们使用多尼船在浅水区航行。

The doni was laden with spices and textiles. 这艘多尼船装满了香料和纺织品。 | 近义词: boat, perahu, prahu (Indonesian) | 反义词: ship (a larger vessel) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Indonesian *doni*.  Likely derived from Malay *perahu*, meaning "boat."  The specific etymology of *doni* within Indonesian is less clear, possibly a regional variation or a borrowing from another language within the Indonesian archipelago.
