outbuy:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪ/
verb
胜过购买，买得比…多
To purchase more than someone else, especially a competitor.
例句：The company outbought its rivals and secured the largest share of the market.  这家公司比竞争对手购买得更多，从而确保了最大的市场份额。

例句：They were able to outbuy all the other bidders at the auction. 他们在拍卖会上能够出价比其他所有竞标者都高。
近义词：outbid, overbid
反义词：underbid
词性：v.
