modernizes:/ˈmɒdənaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzɪz/ (US)| v.| 使现代化|To make something more modern; to update or improve something to conform to contemporary standards or practices.| The company is modernizing its factories with new equipment.  该公司正在用新设备使其工厂现代化。| synonyms: updates, upgrades, innovates, improves, renovates| antonyms: archaizes, outdates, preserves (in an outdated way)| verb
