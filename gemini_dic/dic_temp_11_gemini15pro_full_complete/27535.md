camshaft:/ˈkæmʃæft/| n. |凸轮轴|A rotating shaft within an engine that operates the intake and exhaust valves via cams. |The camshaft controls the timing of the engine's valves. 凸轮轴控制着发动机气门的正时。|The mechanic replaced the worn camshaft. 机械师更换了磨损的凸轮轴。|Synonym: cam axle|Antonym: None|noun
