injury's:/ˈɪndʒəriːz/
noun
伤害，损害（injury的名词所有格）
The extent of the injury's impact on his career is still unknown.  这个伤对他的职业生涯的影响程度仍是未知数。
近义词：harm's, damage's, wound's
反义词：benefit's, advantage's
词性：名词所有格


Etymology:
From Middle English *injure, from Anglo-Norman injurer, Old French injurier (“to insult, offend, injure”), from Latin injūria (“wrongdoing, injustice, insult, injury”), from in- (“not”) + jūs (“right, law”).
