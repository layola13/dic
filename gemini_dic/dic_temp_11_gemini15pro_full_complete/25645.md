bundesrath:/ˈbʊndəsˌrɑːt/| n.|联邦参议院（德国）|The Bundesrat is the upper house of the German parliament. 联邦参议院是德国议会的上议院。|例句：The Bundesrat represents the interests of the German states. 联邦参议院代表德国各州的利益。

例句：The Bundesrat can veto legislation passed by the Bundestag. 联邦参议院可以否决联邦议院通过的立法。|近义词：Senate, upper house|反义词:Bundestag (in a functional sense, not a direct opposite)|词性:noun
