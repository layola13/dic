noncruciform:/ˌnɒn kruːˈsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
非十字形的
Not having the shape of a cross; not cruciform.
The building has a noncruciform layout, unlike traditional churches.  这栋建筑的布局是非十字形的，不像传统的教堂。
The architect opted for a noncruciform design for the modern art museum.  这位建筑师为现代艺术博物馆选择了非十字形的设计。
Near synonyms: non-cross-shaped, non-cruciate
Antonyms: cruciform, cross-shaped
Word Origin:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "cruciform" (from Latin "crux" meaning "cross" + "-form" meaning "shaped")
