homie:/ˈhoʊmi/ | n. | 好友，哥们儿，老伙伴 | A close friend or companion, often used in a familiar and affectionate way. | Example: He's been my homie since we were kids.  他从小就是我的好哥们儿。

Example:  What's up, homie?  怎么了，哥们儿？

近义词: friend, pal, buddy, mate, dude | 反义词: enemy, foe | 词性: noun
