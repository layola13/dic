entozooelogist:/ˌɛntəzoʊəˈlɒdʒɪst/ | n. | 内寄生动物学家 | A scientist who studies entozoa, which are internal animal parasites. |  Example: The entozooelogist discovered a new species of worm parasitizing the fish. 内寄生动物学家发现了一种寄生在鱼身上的新蠕虫种类。

Example:  The entozooelogist presented their research on the life cycle of the parasite. 内寄生动物学家介绍了他们对寄生虫生命周期的研究。| Near Synonyms: parasitologist | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
