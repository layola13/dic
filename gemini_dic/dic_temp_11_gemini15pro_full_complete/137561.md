nontransitivenesses:/ˌnɒntrænˈzɪtɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|非及物性；非传递性|The nontransitivenesses of some verbs can be confusing for learners.  一些动词的非及物性可能会让学习者感到困惑。|The mathematical concept deals with the nontransitivenesses of certain relations. 这个数学概念处理某些关系的非传递性。|近义词:intransitivity, non-transitivity|反义词:transitivity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + transitive + -ness (state or quality of being) + -es (plural suffix).  The root word "transitive" comes from Late Latin *transitivus, from Latin transire "to go across," from trans- "across" + ire "to go."
