chushi:/ˈtʃuːʃiː/ | n. |厨师|A person who cooks professionally, especially the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel. |例句：The chushi prepared a delicious meal. 厨师准备了一顿美味的佳肴。

例句：He is a skilled chushi. 他是一位技艺娴熟的厨师。|近义词：cook, chef |反义词:N/A |词性:noun

chushi (from Chinese 廚師/厨师 chúshī)  The word "chushi" is a direct borrowing from Mandarin Chinese.  廚 (chú) means "kitchen" and 師 (shī) means "master," "teacher," or a skilled professional.  Together they form the word for "cook" or "chef."  It has become increasingly common in English, particularly in contexts related to Chinese cuisine.
