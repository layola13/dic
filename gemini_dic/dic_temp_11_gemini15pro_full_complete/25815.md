burghbrech: /ˈbɜːrɡbrɛç/ | n. | 违反城镇和平; 扰乱治安 |  An archaic Scots law term referring to a breach of the peace within a burgh or town.  This often involved violent or disruptive behavior. |  "He was charged with burghbrech for starting a brawl in the marketplace." 他因在市场引发斗殴而被指控扰乱治安。 | 近义词: breach of the peace, disorderly conduct, riot | 反义词:  peaceful conduct, law and order | 词性: 名词

词源: From Scots, from *burgh* (borough, town) + *breach* (violation).
