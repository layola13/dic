hospitality:/ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti/ /ˌhɑːspɪˈtælɪti/| n. |好客，热情，款待|The friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers.|例句：The hotel is known for its excellent hospitality. 这家酒店以其优质的款待而闻名。|近义词：welcoming, warmth, generosity, friendliness|反义词：hostility, unfriendliness, coldness|词性:名词

hospitality:|/ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti/ /ˌhɑːspɪˈtælɪti/| n. |招待，接待|The provision of food, drink, or lodging for guests, visitors, or strangers.|例句：We were grateful for their hospitality after our long journey.  漫长的旅程之后，我们非常感谢他们的招待。|近义词：entertainment, reception, catering|反义词：neglect, inhospitality|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hospitalite*, from Latin hospitālitās (“hospitality, friendliness”), from hospitālis (“hospitable”), from hospes (“host, guest”).
