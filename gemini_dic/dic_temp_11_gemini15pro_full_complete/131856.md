nonconstructiveness:/ˌnɑːnkənˈstrʌktɪvnəs/ |n.| 非建设性；无益 |Lack of helpfulness or positive contribution; behavior or comments that do not contribute to a productive outcome. |His nonconstructiveness during the meeting frustrated everyone. 他在会议期间的不合作态度让每个人都很沮丧。|Synonyms: negativity, unhelpfulness, obstructiveness |Antonyms: constructiveness, helpfulness, positiveness|noun

nonconstructive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈstrʌktɪv/ |adj.| 非建设性的；无益的 |Not helpful or likely to produce a good result. |Her nonconstructive criticism only made him feel worse. 她毫无建设性的批评只会让他感觉更糟。|Synonyms: unhelpful, negative, unproductive |Antonyms: constructive, helpful, productive|adjective
