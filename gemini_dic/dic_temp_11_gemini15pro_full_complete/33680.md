cinnamic:/sɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 肉桂的，肉桂酸的 | Relating to or containing cinnamic acid, a white crystalline organic acid found in cinnamon and other plants. |  Cinnamic acid is used in perfumes and flavorings.  肉桂酸用于香水和调味品。

Cinnamic aldehyde gives cinnamon its characteristic flavor.  肉桂醛赋予肉桂其特有的风味。 | 近义词: cinnamon-flavored, aromatic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
