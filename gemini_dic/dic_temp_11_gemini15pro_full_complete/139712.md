ocellated:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪd/ /ˈɑːsəleɪtɪd/| adj.|有眼状斑点的，有圆形斑点的|Having a pattern of eye-like spots or markings.|The ocellated turkey has beautiful, iridescent feathers with eye-like spots. 这只眼斑火鸡有着美丽的彩虹色羽毛，上面有眼状斑点。|spotted, ringed, eyed|plain, unspotted|adjective

ocellated:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪd/ /ˈɑːsəleɪtɪd/| adj.|（昆虫翅膀）有单眼状纹的|Having a simple eye-like marking (especially on an insect wing).|The butterfly's wings were ocellated, with a large spot resembling an eye. 这只蝴蝶的翅膀上有单眼状纹，有一个很大的斑点像眼睛。|spotted, ringed, eyed|plain, unspotted|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ocellatus*, past participle of *ocellare* "to furnish with little eyes," from *ocellus* "little eye," diminutive of *oculus* "eye."
