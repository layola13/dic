nonargument:/ˈnɑːnˈɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | 非论证，非争论点 | Something that is not a valid argument; a statement or point that does not support a particular position. |  The lawyer dismissed the statement as a nonargument, saying it lacked any legal basis.  律师驳回了该声明，认为它是非论证，缺乏任何法律依据。

The claim that the earth is flat is a nonargument in modern science. 声称地球是平的是现代科学中的一个非论证。 | 近义词: fallacy, irrelevancy, invalid point | 反义词: argument, proof, evidence | 词性: 名词
