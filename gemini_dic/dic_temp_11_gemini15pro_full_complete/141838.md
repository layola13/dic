orcein:/ˈɔːrsɪɪn/ |/ˈɔːrsiːɪn/| n. |地衣红；苔红|A natural dye obtained from certain lichens, typically producing a reddish-purple color. Used as a biological stain.|例句：Orcein is commonly used to stain chromosomes.  苔红常用于染色染色体。

例句：The elastic fibers were stained with orcein. 弹性纤维用苔红染色。|近义词：litmus, stain|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *orseille*, from Spanish *orchella*, ultimately from Arabic *أرشلة* (*ʾaršila*), from Medieval Greek *ἀρχίλλα* (*archilla*).
