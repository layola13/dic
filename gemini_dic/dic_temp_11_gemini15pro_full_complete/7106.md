anetic: /əˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
无风的；平静的；无风的
Relating to or characterized by a lack of wind or current.  Free from wind; calm.
例句：The anetic conditions made it perfect for sailing.  无风的状态使它成为帆船运动的完美条件。

例句：The lake was anetic, reflecting the clear blue sky.  湖水平静无波，倒映着清澈的蓝天。
近义词：calm, still, windless, airless
反义词：windy, breezy, gusty
词性: adjective
