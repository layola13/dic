coones:/kuːnz/ | n. |浣熊（复数，非标准拼写）|Raccoons (plural, non-standard spelling).  The standard spelling is "raccoons." This represents a dialectal or informal variation.| Example: I saw some coones in my backyard last night. 昨晚我在后院看到了一些浣熊。 | 近义词: raccoons | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: "Coones" is a non-standard plural form of "coon," a shortened and often derogatory term for "raccoon."  "Raccoon" itself comes from the Powhatan (Algonquian) word *araxúŋana, meaning  "he scratches with his hands."  The shortened form "coon" emerged in the 18th century.
