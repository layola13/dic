anticosmetics:/ˌæntiˌkɒzˈmetɪks/
n.
反化妆品；抵制化妆品的理念
Products or philosophies that oppose or reject the use of traditional cosmetics, often emphasizing natural beauty or challenging societal beauty standards.
Many young people are embracing the anticosmetics movement, opting for natural skincare routines instead of heavy makeup. 许多年轻人正在拥抱反化妆品运动，选择天然护肤程序而不是浓妆艳抹。
近义词：natural beauty products, skincare
反义词：cosmetics, makeup
词性：名词

anticosmetic:/ˌæntiˌkɒzˈmetɪk/
adj.
反化妆品的
Opposed to or rejecting the use of cosmetics.
She has an anticosmetic philosophy, believing that natural beauty is best. 她持有反化妆品的理念，相信自然美才是最好的。
近义词：natural, unadorned
反义词：cosmetic, beautifying
词性：形容词

词源:  "anti-" (Greek, meaning "against, opposite") + "cosmetics" (from Greek "kosmetikos," meaning "skilled in decorating or adorning").
