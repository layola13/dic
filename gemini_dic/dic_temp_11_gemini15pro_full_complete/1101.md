accessed:/ˈæksɛst/| v. |访问，获取，使用|To gain entry to or make use of something, especially information or a facility.|例句：I accessed the company database to find the client's contact details. 我访问了公司数据库以查找客户的联系方式。|例句：The website was accessed by millions of users worldwide. 该网站被全球数百万用户访问。|近义词：obtained, retrieved, used, entered, reached|反义词:blocked, restricted|词性:动词

词源: From the Latin "accessus," meaning "approach" or "admission."  It's the past participle of the verb "access."
