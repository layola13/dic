leotard: /ˈliːətɑːrd/ (US) /ˈliːətɑːd/ (UK)| n. |紧身衣，练功服|A close-fitting, one-piece garment, typically made of stretchy fabric, worn by dancers, gymnasts, acrobats, and other athletes.|例句：She wore a bright pink leotard for her gymnastics class. 她穿着亮粉色的紧身衣去上体操课。

例句：The ballerina's leotard sparkled under the stage lights.  芭蕾舞演员的紧身衣在舞台灯光下闪闪发光。

近义词：unitard, bodysuit|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing, street clothes) |词性: noun
