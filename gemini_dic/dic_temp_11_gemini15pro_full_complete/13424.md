atonalists:/eɪˈtoʊnəlɪsts/
n.
无调性音乐作曲家；无调性音乐支持者
Composers or advocates of atonal music, a style of music that avoids traditional tonal centers or keys.
Arnold Schoenberg was one of the pioneering atonalists of the 20th century.  阿诺德·勋伯格是20世纪无调性音乐的先驱之一。

The atonalists sought to break free from the constraints of traditional harmony.  无调性音乐家试图摆脱传统和声的束缚。

近义词:modernists, avant-gardists
反义词:tonalists
词性:noun
