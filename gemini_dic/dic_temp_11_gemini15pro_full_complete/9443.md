antireducing's:/ˌæntiɹɪˈduːsɪŋz/ | adj. | 抗还原的 |  Relating to or exhibiting the property of preventing reduction, often used in chemistry to describe substances that inhibit a chemical reaction where a substance gains electrons.  The 's is likely a possessive or pluralizing misuse, the correct form would generally be "antireducing". |  The antireducing agent protected the solution from oxidation. 抗还原剂保护溶液免受氧化。|  Near Synonyms: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | Antonyms: reducing, oxidative | adjective
