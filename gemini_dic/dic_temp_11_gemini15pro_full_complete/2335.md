adherers:/ədˈhɪrərz/
n.
追随者，拥护者
People who stick to a belief, cause, or leader.
The new religion quickly gained thousands of adherents.  这个新宗教很快就获得了成千上万的追随者。

followers, supporters, devotees
opponents, dissenters
noun
