counter-raid's:/ˈkaʊntərˌreɪdz/ | n. |反突袭，反击|A raid undertaken in retaliation for another raid.  A counter-raid is a military operation launched in response to an enemy raid.  It aims to disrupt or destroy the enemy's forces or infrastructure while they are still recovering from their initial attack. |The general ordered a counter-raid against the enemy encampment after their initial attack. 将军在敌人第一次袭击后下令对敌营进行反突袭。

The counter-raid's success depended on the element of surprise. 反突袭的成功取决于出其不意的因素。|近义词：counterattack, counteroffensive |反义词:raid, attack|词性:名词
