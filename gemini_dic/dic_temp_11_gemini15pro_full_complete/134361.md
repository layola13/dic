词源：
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "dependent" + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs).  The word "dependent" comes from the Latin "dependere" (to hang down, rely on).  Therefore, "noninterdependently" literally means "not relying on each other."
