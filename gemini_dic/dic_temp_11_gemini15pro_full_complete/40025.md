consumeristic:/kənˈsuːmərˈɪstɪk/
adjective
消费主义的
Characterized by or exhibiting consumerism; preoccupied with buying goods.
例句：
The consumeristic society often prioritizes material possessions over experiences.
这个消费主义社会往往将物质财富置于经验之上。

例句：
Her consumeristic habits led to a large amount of debt.
她消费主义的习惯导致了大量的债务。

近义词：materialistic, acquisitive
反义词：frugal, thrifty
词性: adjective
