overshadower:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædəwər/| n.| 使黯然失色的人或物；胜过者 |Something or someone that overshadows another makes it seem less important or impressive.|  Her new book was overshadowed by the controversy surrounding her personal life. 她的新书被围绕她个人生活的争议所掩盖。| synonyms: eclipse, outshine, dwarf, surpass | antonyms: highlight, emphasize | noun

overshadow:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊ/| vt.|  (1)遮蔽，使阴暗；(2)使黯然失色；(3)使蒙上阴影 | (1) To cast a shadow over; darken. (2) To make appear less important or impressive. (3) To make gloomy or ominous. | The tall buildings overshadowed the park. 高楼遮蔽了公园。| synonyms: eclipse, outshine, obscure, dim | antonyms: illuminate, brighten, highlight | verb
