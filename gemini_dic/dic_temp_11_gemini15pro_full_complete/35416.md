co-ordinateness:/kəʊˈɔːdɪnɪtnəs/| n. |协调性，配合性|The state or quality of being co-ordinated; harmonious or orderly arrangement or operation.|The co-ordinateness of the dancers' movements was breathtaking. 舞蹈演员动作的协调性令人叹为观止。|近义词：harmony, order, organization, coherence, integration, synchronization|反义词：discord, disorder, disorganization, chaos, incoherence|词性: noun

co-ordinate:/kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/| adj. |同等的，并列的；坐标的|Of the same order or degree; equal or parallel in rank or importance; relating to or using coordinates.|The x and y axes are co-ordinate axes. X轴和Y轴是坐标轴。|近义词：equal, equivalent, parallel, corresponding|反义词：unequal, different, dissimilar|词性: adjective

co-ordinate:/kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/| v. |协调，配合；使协调一致|Bring the different elements of (a complex activity or organization) into a relationship that will ensure efficiency or harmony.|We need to co-ordinate our efforts to achieve our goals. 我们需要协调我们的努力来实现我们的目标。|近义词：harmonize, organize, synchronize, integrate, arrange|反义词：disorganize, disrupt, separate|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin co- (together) + ordinatus (arranged, ordered).
