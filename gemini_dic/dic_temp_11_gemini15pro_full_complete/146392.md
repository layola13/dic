overflown:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊn/
|adj.| 溢出的，泛滥的; (飞机)飞越的; | Past participle of *overflow*.  Meaning something has been flooded or exceeded its capacity, or that a plane has flown over a certain area.| The river overflown its banks and flooded the fields. 河水漫过河岸，淹没了田野。| The plane had overflown enemy territory. 飞机飞越了敌方领土。| 近义词: flooded, inundated, flown over | 反义词: contained, drained | 词性: adjective

overflow:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊ/
|v. (overflows, overflowing, overflowed, overflown)| 溢出；泛滥；充满；挤满| To flow over the brim of a container; to be very full of something; to be filled with a strong emotion| The bathtub overflowed and soaked the carpet.  浴缸里的水溢了出来，把地毯弄湿了。|  His heart overflowed with gratitude. 他心中充满了感激。| 近义词: flood, inundate, brim over | 反义词: drain, empty | 词性: verb

词源: Old English *oferflowan*, from *ofer-* (over) + *flowan* (to flow).
