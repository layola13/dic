friendsourcing:/ˈfrɛndˌsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.
众包朋友; 朋友资源;
The act of soliciting information or advice from one's friends, especially via social media.  It relies on the collective knowledge and resources of a person's social network.
例句：She used friendsourcing to get recommendations for a good mechanic. 她利用朋友资源找到了一位优秀的机械师。

例句：Friendsourcing can be a quick way to get diverse perspectives on a problem.  众包朋友可以快速获得对问题的不同看法。

近义词：crowdsourcing, social networking
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps traditional research or professional consulting)
词性: noun
