astrometeorological:/ˌæstroʊˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
天体气象学的
Relating to the branch of meteorology that studies the supposed influence of celestial bodies on weather.
Astrometeorological research is often considered pseudoscience by mainstream scientists.  天体气象学研究经常被主流科学家认为是伪科学。
The astrometeorological forecast predicted heavy rainfall during the lunar eclipse.  天体气象学预测月食期间会有强降雨。

ETYMOLOGY->
From astro- (prefix meaning "star, celestial body") + meteorological.
"Astro-" comes from the Greek "astron" (star).
"Meteorological" comes from the Greek "meteōrologia" (study of things in the air), from "meteōros" (high in the air) + -logia (study of).
