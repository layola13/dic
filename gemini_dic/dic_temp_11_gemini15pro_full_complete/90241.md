houseboat:/ˈhaʊsboʊt/ (US) /ˈhaʊsbəʊt/ (UK)| n.|船屋；居住船|A boat that is designed and equipped for use as a dwelling.|We spent our vacation on a houseboat on the lake. 我们在湖上的船屋里度假。|
|The family lived on a houseboat for several years. 这家人在船屋里住了好几年。|
|近义词：barge, floating home|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps apartment, house)|词性: noun
