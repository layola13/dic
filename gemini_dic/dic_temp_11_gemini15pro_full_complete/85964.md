headrace: /ˈhɛdreɪs/ | n. |  引水渠，渠道，上游 | A channel carrying water to a mill or other water-powered installation. | The headrace channeled water to the turbine.  引水渠将水引入涡轮机。|
Synonyms: millrace, raceway, flume, aqueduct, channel | Antonyms: tailrace, outflow | noun

headrace: /ˈhɛdreɪs/ | n. |  赛艇的上游赛段 | The section of a river or other waterway upstream of a rowing or paddling race. | The headrace was choppy, making for a difficult start. 上游赛段波涛汹涌，比赛开始就很难。|
Synonyms: upstream, upper course | Antonyms: downstream, lower course | noun
