The naturalist observed an empusa, a fascinating type of mantis, camouflaged among the leaves.  博物学家观察到一只螳螂，一种迷人的螳螂，伪装在树叶中。| 近义词：demon, specter, phantom, mantis | 反义词: angel, deity | 词性: noun
