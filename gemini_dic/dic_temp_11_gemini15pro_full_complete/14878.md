如果你是想问 "what" 这个词，我可以提供以下解析：

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. int. | 什么，多么 |  Used to ask for information about something. |  What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | What did you say? 你说了什么？| which, who |  (No single antonym - depends on context) | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing? = 你在做什么？
What is he watching? = 他在看什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done? = 你做了什么？
What has she eaten? = 她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all day? = 你一整天都在做什么？
What has he been working on? = 他一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when I called? = 我打电话时你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? = 她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed? = 他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们会在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by graduation? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
