nonutilitarian:/ˌnɒnjuːˌtɪlɪˈteəriən/
adjective
非功利的，非实用主义的
Not having or intended to have a useful or practical purpose; not based on or concerned with usefulness.
例句：
She pursued nonutilitarian goals, like painting and writing poetry.
她追求非功利的目标，比如绘画和写诗。

He appreciated the nonutilitarian beauty of the intricate sculpture.
他欣赏这件复杂雕塑的非功利的美丽。

近义词：nonpractical, nonfunctional, aesthetic, decorative, ornamental
反义词：utilitarian, practical, functional, pragmatic

词性: adjective
