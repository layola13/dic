nonluminescence:/ˌnɒnljuːmɪˈnesəns/ /ˌnɑːnlʊmɪˈnɛsəns/| n. |无光，不发光|The absence of light emission; the inability to produce light.|The material exhibited nonluminescence even under UV irradiation. 这种材料即使在紫外线照射下也不发光。|近义词:non-emission, darkness, opacity|反义词:luminescence, emission, brilliance|词性:名词

nonluminescent:/ˌnɒnljuːmɪˈnesənt/ /ˌnɑːnlʊmɪˈnɛsənt/| adj. |不发光的|Not emitting light; lacking luminescence.|The nonluminescent paint was perfect for camouflage. 这种不发光的涂料非常适合伪装。|近义词:dark, non-emissive, dull|反义词:luminescent, bright, glowing|词性:形容词
