microbenthic:/ˌmaɪkrəʊˈbɛnθɪk/
adjective
微底栖的
Relating to or inhabiting the microbenthos, the microscopic organisms living in or on the seabed.
Microbenthic communities play a vital role in nutrient cycling. 微底栖生物群落在营养循环中起着至关重要的作用。
The study focused on the microbenthic algae of the intertidal zone. 这项研究的重点是潮间带的微底栖藻类。
近义词:benthic
反义词:pelagic
词性:adjective
