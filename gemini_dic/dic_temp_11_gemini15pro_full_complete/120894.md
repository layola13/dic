miscalculate:/ˌmɪskælkjəˈleɪt/ | v. | 计算错误，估算错 | To calculate or judge something incorrectly. |  I miscalculated the cost of the project and we went over budget. 我错误计算了项目成本，结果超出预算。

He miscalculated the distance and ran out of gas before reaching his destination. 他错误估计了距离，在到达目的地之前就没油了。 | 近义词: misjudge, underestimate, overestimate, err | 反义词: calculate correctly, estimate accurately | verb
