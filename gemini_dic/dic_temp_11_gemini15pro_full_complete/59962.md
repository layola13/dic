edifications:/ˌɛdɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
启迪，教诲；陶冶；(尤指道德、精神、智力方面的)提高
the improvement of the mind and character, especially by moral instruction
The pastor hoped his sermon would offer edification to his flock. 牧师希望他的布道能给他的教众带来启迪。
synonyms: enlightenment, instruction, betterment, improvement
antonyms: corruption, degradation, deterioration
noun
