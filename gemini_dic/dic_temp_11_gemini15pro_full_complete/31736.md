cheapness:/ˈtʃiːpnəs/| n.|便宜，廉价，低劣|The cheapness of the materials made the product unreliable. 材料的低廉使得产品不可靠。|例句：The cheapness of the flight was appealing, but the long layover was not. 这趟航班价格便宜很吸引人，但长时间的中途停留就不好了。

例句：He bought the car for its cheapness, not its quality. 他买这辆车是因为它便宜，而不是因为它的质量。|近义词：inexpensiveness, affordability, low cost|反义词：expensiveness, costliness, high price|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chepnes*, from Old English *cēapnes* (“cheapness, low price, meanness”), from *cēap* (“cheap”) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Cognate with German *Käuflichkeit* (“venality”).
