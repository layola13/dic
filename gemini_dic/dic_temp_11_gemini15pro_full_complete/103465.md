job-sharing: /ˈdʒɒb ʃɛərɪŋ/ (British) /ˈdʒɑːb ʃɛrɪŋ/ (American)| n. |工作分担，工作共享|The practice of two or more people occupying one job, often by splitting the hours of work.|Example: Job-sharing allows parents to balance work and childcare. 工作分担允许父母平衡工作和育儿。|Example:  They decided on job-sharing to reduce their workload. 他们决定工作分担以减少工作量。|近义词: work-sharing, co-working|反义词: full-time employment|词性: noun
