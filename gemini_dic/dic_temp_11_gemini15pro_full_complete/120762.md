misapply:/ˌmɪsəˈplaɪ/| vt.|误用，滥用，错用|To use something incorrectly or for the wrong purpose. |He misapplied the funds intended for the charity. 他把用于慈善的资金用错了。

The company misapplied the new technology, leading to significant losses.  这家公司误用了新技术，导致了重大损失。

|近义词：misuse, abuse, pervert|反义词：apply correctly, use properly|词性：动词
