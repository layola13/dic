churchless:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃləs/
adjective
没有教会的；不信教的；不参加教会活动的
Not belonging to or attending a church; not affiliated with a church.
Many young people today are churchless. 如今许多年轻人都不去教堂。
Near Synonyms: irreligious, secular, non-churchgoing, unchurched
Antonyms: religious, devout, churchgoing
词性: adjective

churchless:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃləs/
adjective
不受教会约束的；不受教会影响的
Not subject to church authority or influence.
A churchless society may have different moral values. 一个不受教会约束的社会可能有不同的道德价值观。
Near Synonyms: secular, non-denominational, independent
Antonyms: religious, denominational
词性: adjective
