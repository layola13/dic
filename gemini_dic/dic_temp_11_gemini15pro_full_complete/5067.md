all-the-same:/ɔːl ðə seɪm/| adv.|仍然，尽管如此，还是|Despite something that has just been said; nevertheless. |例句：I don't agree with you, but I appreciate your opinion all the same. 我不同意你的观点，但我仍然感激你的意见。|近义词：nevertheless, nonetheless, still, yet, however|反义词:therefore, consequently|词性:副词

all-the-same:/ɔːl ðə seɪm/| adj.|一样的，无差别的|Identical or alike; making no difference.|例句：The results were all the same, no matter which method we used. 不管我们用哪种方法，结果都是一样的。|近义词：identical, alike, similar|反义词:different, dissimilar|词性:形容词
