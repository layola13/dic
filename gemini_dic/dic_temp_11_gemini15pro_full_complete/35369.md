co-morbid:/ˌkəʊˈmɔːrbɪd/ /ˌkoʊˈmɔːrbɪd/ | adj. | 共病的，并发疾病的 | Existing simultaneously with and usually independently of another medical condition. |  The patient's depression is co-morbid with his diabetes.  这位病人的抑郁症与他的糖尿病并发。| Synonyms: coexisting, concomitant, concurrent | Antonyms: independent, separate | adjective

co-morbidity:/ˌkəʊmɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ /ˌkoʊmɔːrˈbɪdɪti/  | n. | 共病，并发疾病 | The simultaneous presence of two or more diseases or medical conditions in a patient. | The study examined the co-morbidity of anxiety and depression. 这项研究调查了焦虑和抑郁的共病情况。 | Synonyms: comorbidity, coexistence, concurrence | Antonyms: independence | noun
