nonpermanences:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːmənənsɪz/
n.
无常；非永久性；短暂性
The nonpermanences of life are something we all must face.  人生的无常是我们都必须面对的。
Life is full of nonpermanences, from the changing seasons to the fleeting moments of happiness.  生活充满了无常，从变化的季节到稍纵即逝的幸福时刻。
Synonyms: impermanence, transience, ephemerality, mutability
Antonyms: permanence, eternity, stability, immutability
noun


Etymology:
From non- (not) + permanence.  Permanence comes from the Late Latin  permanentia, from permanens (remaining, enduring), present participle of permanēre (to remain, endure).
