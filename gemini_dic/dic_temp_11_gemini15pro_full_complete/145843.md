overdemanding:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmændɪŋ/| adj.|要求过高的，苛求的|Making excessive or unreasonable demands; placing too many or too difficult demands on someone.|例句：He found the job overdemanding and eventually quit. 他觉得这份工作要求太高，最终辞职了。

例句：Her overdemanding boss made her work life miserable. 她那苛刻的老板让她苦不堪言。|近义词：exacting, stringent, rigorous, oppressive|反义词：undemanding, lenient, easygoing|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + demanding.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Demanding" comes from the verb "demand", which has roots in Old French "demander" and ultimately Latin "demandare" meaning "to entrust, commit, commend".
