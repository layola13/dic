exhibiting:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ/
|v. |展出；展示；表现出；显出|
|Exhibiting her paintings at the local gallery, she hoped to gain recognition for her artistic talent.  她在当地画廊展出她的画作，希望她的艺术才能得到认可。|
|The patient was exhibiting signs of improvement after the surgery.  手术后，病人表现出好转的迹象。|
|近义词：showing, displaying, presenting, demonstrating, revealing, manifesting|
|反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing|
|词性：动词(present participle of exhibit)|
|词源：From Latin *exhibitus*, past participle of *exhibere* "to hold out, show, display," from *ex-* "out" + *habere* "to hold."|
