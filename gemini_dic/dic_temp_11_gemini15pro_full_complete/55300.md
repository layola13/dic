dissolution:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən/
n.|分解；溶解；解散；终止|The dissolution of the company came as a shock to its employees. 该公司的解散令其员工感到震惊。|The dissolution of sugar in water is a physical change. 糖在水中溶解是物理变化。|
dissolution:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən/
n.|放纵；堕落|He lived a life of dissolution and excess. 他过着放纵和过度的生活。|The dissolution of morals in society is a serious concern. 社会道德的沦丧是一个严重的问题。|
dissolution:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən/
n.|（议会等的）解散|Parliament was dissolved before the general election. 在大选前议会解散了。|The dissolution of the assembly marked the end of an era.  议会的解散标志着一个时代的结束。|
近义词:disintegration, dissolving, termination, ending, decay, corruption
反义词:formation, unification, solidification
词性:noun
