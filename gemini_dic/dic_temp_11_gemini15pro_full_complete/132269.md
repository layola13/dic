nondeductively:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb|非演绎地|In a way that does not use deductive reasoning; based on inference rather than logical necessity.|He argued nondeductively, drawing upon examples and analogies.|近义词:inductively, inferentially|反义词:deductively|词性:副词

词源:
"non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "deductive" (from Latin "deducere" - to lead down, derive) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
