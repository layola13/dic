final hurrah:/ˈfaɪnl hʊˈrɑː/ /ˈfaɪnəl hʊˈrɑː/
noun
最后的欢呼；最后的努力；最后的辉煌
A final, often especially vigorous or enthusiastic, effort, performance, or attempt.
The retiring conductor gave his final hurrah with a performance of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony. 退休的指挥家以演奏贝多芬第五交响曲作为他的最后的辉煌。
swan song, last stand, final act
