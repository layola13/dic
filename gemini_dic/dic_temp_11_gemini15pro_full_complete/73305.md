forecastle: /ˈfɔːksl/ (US) /ˈfəʊksl/ (UK)| n. |前甲板; 水手舱|The forward part of a ship's upper deck, traditionally used as the sailors' living quarters.  It's also often used to refer to the crew themselves.|The sailors gathered on the forecastle to watch the sunset. 水手们聚集在前甲板上观看日落。|
The captain addressed the forecastle about the upcoming storm. 船长向前甲板上的水手们讲述了即将到来的风暴。|Synonyms:  foredeck, bows|Antonyms: quarterdeck, stern|noun
