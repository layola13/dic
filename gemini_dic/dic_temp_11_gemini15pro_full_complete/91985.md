hypernaturalnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəlnəsɪz/
|n.| 超自然性（复数）
|The quality or state of being beyond or exceeding what is natural; extreme artificiality.|
|The hypernaturalnesses of the computer-generated images were unsettling. 电脑生成图像的极度不自然感令人不安。
|The artist explored themes of artificiality and hypernaturalnesses in his work.  这位艺术家在他的作品中探索了人工化和超自然性的主题。|
|artificialities, unnaturalness, surrealism|
|naturalness, realism|
|noun|
