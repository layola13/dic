nachos: /ˈnɑːtʃoʊz/ (US) /ˈnɑːtʃəʊz/ (UK)| n. |玉米片, 通常搭配奶酪酱, 辣椒酱, 或其他配料|A dish of tortilla chips topped with melted cheese and often other savory toppings, such as chili, jalapenos, or beans. It originated in northern Mexico. |例句：We ordered nachos as an appetizer. 我们点了一份玉米片作为开胃菜。

例句：The nachos were loaded with cheese and jalapenos. 玉米片上堆满了奶酪和墨西哥辣椒。

近义词：tortilla chips, chips and salsa (when served with salsa)|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a food item)|词性:noun
