conclusive:/kənˈkluːsɪv/| adj.|结论性的；决定性的；无可置疑的|Serving to settle an issue; final.|The fingerprint evidence was conclusive and led to the conviction of the suspect. 指纹证据确凿，导致嫌疑人被定罪。|近义词：definitive, decisive, final, ultimate|反义词：inconclusive, ambiguous, debatable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin conclusivus, from concludere ‘to conclude’.
