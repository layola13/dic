disprovide:/ˌdɪsprəˈvaɪd/
verb
取消供应；使丧失
To deprive of something previously provided; to fail to provide.
The sudden budget cuts disprovided the school of essential resources. 突然的预算削减使学校失去了必要的资源。
The landlord disprovided the apartment of heat during the coldest week of the year. 在一年中最冷的一周，房东停止为公寓供暖。
近义词: deprive, strip, divest
反义词: provide, supply, furnish
词性: verb
