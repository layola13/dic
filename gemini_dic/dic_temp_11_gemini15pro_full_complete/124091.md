morse:/mɔːrs/ | n. | 摩尔斯电码 | A system of dots and dashes representing letters and numbers, used for sending messages by telegraph, radio, or flashing light. |  Example: The ship sent out an SOS message in Morse. 这艘船用摩尔斯电码发出SOS求救信号。 | Example: He learned Morse code when he was in the Boy Scouts. 他在童子军时学会了摩尔斯电码。 | Synonym: Morse code | Antonym: N/A | noun
