jewel:/ˈdʒuːəl/ (US), /ˈdʒuːəl/ (UK)| n.|宝石，珠宝；珍宝，贵重物品|A precious stone, especially when cut and polished or set into jewelry.  Something highly valued or prized.|She wore a dazzling jewel around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一颗耀眼夺目的宝石。|Synonyms: gem, gemstone, precious stone, treasure, ornament|Antonyms: trinket, bauble|noun
