glitz:/ɡlɪts/| n.  |浮华；炫耀；闪耀 |Superficial brilliance or glamour.| The Oscars were full of glitz and glamour. 奥斯卡颁奖典礼充满了浮华和魅力。| 近义词：glamour, glitter, sparkle, flashiness | 反义词：simplicity, modesty, understatement | 词性: noun

glitz:/ɡlɪts/| v. |使闪耀；使光彩夺目 |To make something glamorous or showy.|  They glitzed up the old building for the party.  他们为了派对把旧楼装饰得光彩夺目。| 近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish | 反义词：tone down, simplify | 词性: verb
