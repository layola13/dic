magnetizes:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
磁化；吸引
To make magnetic; to attract strongly, as if by magnetism.
The presence of the strong magnet magnetizes the iron filings. 强磁铁的存在磁化了铁屑。
