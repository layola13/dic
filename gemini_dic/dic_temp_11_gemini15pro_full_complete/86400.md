hedonics:/hɪˈdɑːnɪks/ /hiːˈdɒnɪks/
n.
享乐主义；快乐论；快乐经济学
The study of pleasure, happiness, and well-being; especially the branch of economics concerned with the quantification and measurement of happiness and well-being.
Hedonics plays a significant role in understanding consumer behavior.  享乐主义在理解消费者行为方面起着重要作用。
The government is increasingly incorporating hedonics into policy making. 政府越来越多地将快乐经济学纳入政策制定。
近义词:eudaemonism,  felicific calculus
反义词:dysphoria, suffering
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Greek  ἡδονικός (hēdonikos, “pleasant”), from ἡδονή (hēdonē, “pleasure”).
