chippings:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋz/| n. |碎屑，碎片，木屑|Small pieces of stone, wood, or other material that have been broken or cut off from a larger piece.|例句：Wood chippings covered the floor of the workshop.  木屑覆盖了车间的地板。

例句：The path was covered in chippings of slate.  这条小路铺满了板岩碎片。|近义词：fragments, bits, pieces, shavings (especially for wood)|反义词: whole, complete|词性:noun
