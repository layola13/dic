interattrition:/ˌɪntərəˈtrɪʃən/
n.
内部人员流失;内部损耗
The gradual reduction of a workforce within an organization due to employees leaving to take positions in other parts of the same organization.
Example: Interattrition can lead to knowledge loss in one department while strengthening another within the same company.  公司内部人员流失会导致一个部门的知识流失，而同时加强了同一公司内另一个部门。

Example:  The company's new restructuring plan inadvertently encouraged interattrition, with several key employees moving to different divisions. 公司新的重组计划无意中鼓励了内部人员流失，几个关键员工转到了不同的部门。
近义词: internal transfer, internal movement, internal mobility
反义词: external attrition, recruitment
