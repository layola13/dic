camouflager:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒər/ /ˈkæməˌflɑːdʒər/ | n. |伪装者；进行伪装的人或物|A person or thing that camouflages something. |The soldiers were expert camouflagers, blending seamlessly into the forest. 这些士兵是伪装专家，能完美地融入森林中。|Synonyms: disguiser, concealer |Antonyms: revealer, exposer |noun

camouflage:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ/ /ˈkæməˌflɑːdʒ/ | n. vt.|伪装，掩饰|The disguising of military personnel, equipment, and installations by painting or covering them to make them blend in with their surroundings; the act of concealing the identity of something by modifying its appearance. |The army used camouflage to hide their tanks. 军队使用伪装来隐藏他们的坦克。| The chameleon's skin acts as camouflage. 变色龙的皮肤起到了伪装的作用。|Synonyms: disguise, concealment, masking |Antonyms: exposure, revelation |noun, verb

camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/ /ˈkæməˌflɑːdʒd/ | adj.|伪装的，被掩饰的|Disguised or concealed by camouflage. |The camouflaged vehicle was nearly invisible against the desert background.  伪装的车辆在沙漠背景下几乎看不见。|Synonyms: disguised, concealed, masked |Antonyms: exposed, revealed |adjective
