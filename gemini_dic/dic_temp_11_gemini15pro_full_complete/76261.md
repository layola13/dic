fuzzes:/ˈfʌziz/| n. |绒毛，模糊不清的东西|A mass of fine, light fibers or hairs; something that is indistinct or unclear.|The camera couldn't focus properly and the image was all fuzzes. 相机无法正确对焦，图像全是模糊的。|近义词：fluff, down, blur|反义词: clarity, sharpness|词性:名词

fuzzes:/ˈfʌziz/| v. (fuzzes, fuzzing, fuzzed) |使模糊，变得模糊不清|To become or make something unclear or indistinct.|The old photograph had fuzzed with age.  这张老照片因年代久远而变得模糊不清了。|近义词：blur, obscure, confuse|反义词: clarify, sharpen|词性:动词
