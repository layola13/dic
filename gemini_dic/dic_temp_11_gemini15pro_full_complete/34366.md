classicism: /ˈklæsɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 古典主义，古典风格 | The principles or styles characteristic of the literature and art of ancient Greece and Rome, emphasizing order, balance, and simplicity.  |  The architecture of the building reflected a strong sense of classicism.  这座建筑的风格体现了强烈的古典主义。 |  近义词: traditionalism, formalism | 反义词: romanticism, modernism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French classicisme, from classique (“classic”), from Latin classicus (“of the first class”), from classis (“class”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kleH- (“to shout, call”).
