electro-coating: /ɪˌlɛktroʊˈkoʊtɪŋ/
n.
电泳涂装，电泳涂层
The process of coating a metal object with a paint or other finish by immersing it in a bath of the coating material and applying an electric current.  This causes the coating material to adhere to the object.
The car parts underwent electro-coating to prevent rust.  汽车零件经过电泳涂装以防止生锈。
electrophoretic deposition, electro-deposition
