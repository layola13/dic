illegitimation:/ɪˌlɪˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
非法化;褫夺合法地位;私生
the act of making something illegitimate, especially the act of declaring that someone's birth was illegitimate
The illegitimation of his birth caused him much pain throughout his life. 他出身的非法化给他的一生带来了很多痛苦。
近义词:bastardization, disinheritance
反义词:legitimation, validation
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French illégitimation, from illégitimer (to illegitimize), from in- (not) + légitime (legitimate), from Latin legitimus (lawful).
