libbard:/ˈlɪbərd/| n.|豹, 特指雄豹|A male leopard, especially one used in heraldry.  The term is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |例句：The libbard, rampant and regardant, adorned the knight's shield.  这只直立回首的雄豹装饰着骑士的盾牌。

例句：In medieval bestiaries, the libbard was often depicted as a cunning and fierce creature.  在中世纪的动物寓言集中，豹通常被描绘成狡猾而凶猛的生物。|近义词：leopard, panther|反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)|词性:noun
