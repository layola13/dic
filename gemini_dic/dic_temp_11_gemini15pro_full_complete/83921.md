half-broken:/ˌhæfˈbroʊkən/
adjective
半坏的；破损的；驯服了一半的
partially broken, damaged, or tamed; not completely broken, damaged, or tamed.
The old chair was half-broken and wobbly. 那把旧椅子半坏而且摇摇晃晃的。
The farmer tried to ride the half-broken horse. 农夫试图骑那匹驯服了一半的马。
Synonyms: partially broken, damaged, incompletely tamed
Antonyms: fully functional, intact, completely tamed
词性: 形容词
