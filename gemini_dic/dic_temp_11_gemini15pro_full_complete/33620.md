cincture:/ˈsɪŋktʃər/| n.| 腰带，束带；围绕物；包围|Something that encircles or confines, like a belt or a ring; an act of encircling or girdling.|The monk wore a simple cincture around his robe.  僧侣的长袍上系着一条简单的腰带。|Synonyms: belt, girdle, sash, band, ring, enclosure|Antonyms: opening, gap, separation|noun

cincture:/ˈsɪŋktʃər/| v.| 围绕，包围|To encircle or bind with a belt or band.|Mountains cincture the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。|Synonyms: encircle, surround, enclose, gird, encompass|Antonyms: open, expose, free|verb
