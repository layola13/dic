deftest:/ˈdɛftɪst/| adj.| 最灵巧的; 最熟练的 |  Superlative form of deft: most deft.  Meaning possessing great skill and dexterity. |  He was the deftest pickpocket in the city, able to lift a wallet without anyone noticing. 他是城里最熟练的扒手，能够在无人注意的情况下偷走钱包。 | 近义词：most adroit, most adept, most skillful | 反义词: clumsiest, most awkward | 词性: 形容词
