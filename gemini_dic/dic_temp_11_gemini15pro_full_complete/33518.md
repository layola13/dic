cicerone:/ˌsɪsəˈroʊni/ /ˌtʃɪtʃəˈroʊni/| n.|导游，向导，尤指对古迹或艺术品讲解的人|A person who acts as a guide, especially one who conducts visitors through museums or archaeological sites and explains matters of archaeological, historical, or artistic interest.|We hired a cicerone to show us around the ancient ruins. 我们雇了一个导游带我们参观古遗址。|近义词：guide, docent, escort|反义词: None|Noun
