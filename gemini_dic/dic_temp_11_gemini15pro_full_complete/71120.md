fishhawk:/ˈfɪʃˌhɔːk/| n.|鱼鹰|A large diurnal bird of prey that feeds on fish, also called an osprey.|The fishhawk plunged into the water and emerged with a wriggling trout in its talons. 鱼鹰一头扎进水里，爪子里抓着一条扭动的鳟鱼。|近义词：osprey|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From fish + hawk.  The word simply describes the bird's diet and appearance.
