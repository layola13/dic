cottonseed:/ˈkɑːtnˌsiːd/| n. |棉籽|The seed of the cotton plant, from which cotton fiber grows and which is processed to yield cottonseed oil and meal.|Cottonseed is a valuable byproduct of cotton production. 棉籽是棉花生产中的宝贵副产品。|The price of cottonseed has risen sharply this year.  今年棉籽的价格急剧上涨。|synonyms: cotton plant seed |antonyms: N/A|noun
