leglessness:/ˈleɡləsnəs/
n.
无腿；无足
The state of having no legs.
The leglessness of the snake allows it to move through tight spaces. 蛇的无腿特性使它能够穿过狭窄的空间。
近义词:apodia, absence of legs
反义词:leggedness
词性:名词
