business-to-business: /ˈbɪznəs tə ˈbɪznəs/
| adj. n.| 企业对企业的；B2B |  Relating to or involving commercial transactions between businesses, rather than between a business and individual consumers. |  Example: The company specializes in business-to-business software solutions.  这家公司专门提供企业对企业软件解决方案。| Synonym: B2B, inter-company | Antonym: business-to-consumer (B2C) | adjective, noun
