oversecularization:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛkjələrɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|
|过度世俗化|
|The excessive decline of religious belief and practice in a society. It refers to a situation where the influence of religion on various aspects of life, including social norms, politics, and individual behavior, has diminished significantly beyond what might be considered a normal or balanced level.|
|Some sociologists argue that modern Western societies are experiencing oversecularization, leading to a decline in moral values.|一些社会学家认为，现代西方社会正在经历过度世俗化，导致道德价值观下降。

|The rapid oversecularization of the region has resulted in a breakdown of traditional community structures.|该地区迅速的过度世俗化导致了传统社区结构的瓦解。
