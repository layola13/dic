discussionist: /dɪˈskʌʃənɪst/
n.
讨论者，辩论者
A person who participates in discussions, especially online.
He's a frequent discussionist on online forums. 他经常在网上论坛参与讨论。

近义词: debater, arguer, participant, contributor
反义词: listener, observer

词性: noun

词源: discussion + -ist
