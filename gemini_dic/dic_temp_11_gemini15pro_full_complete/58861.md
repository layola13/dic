durability:/djʊəˈrəˈbɪləti/ /ˌdjʊrəˈbɪləti/| n. |耐久性，耐用性|The ability of something to withstand wear, pressure, or damage over a long period of time.  |This jacket's durability makes it ideal for hiking. 这件夹克的耐用性使它非常适合徒步旅行。|Synonyms: resilience, strength, toughness, sturdiness|Antonyms: fragility, weakness, frailty|noun
