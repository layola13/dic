lichi:/ˈliːtʃiː/ /ˈlaɪtʃiː/
n.
荔枝
A small red or pink tropical fruit with sweet white flesh and a large seed.
例句：I love the sweet taste of fresh lichis. 我喜欢新鲜荔枝的甜味。

例句：Litchis are a popular fruit in many Asian countries. 荔枝在许多亚洲国家是一种很受欢迎的水果。

近义词：lychee, litchi
反义词: (No direct antonym for a fruit)
词性: noun
