abjugate:/ˈæbdʒʊˌɡeɪt/
verb
正式放弃，公开否认
to renounce or reject something formally
He abjured his former beliefs. 他公开否认了他以前的信仰。
近义词: renounce, repudiate, relinquish, forswear, disavow
反义词: embrace, uphold, maintain, affirm, avow
词性: verb

abjugate:/ˈæbdʒʊˌɡeɪt/
词源:  From Latin *abjugare (“to unyoke, detach”), from ab- (“away from”) + jugum (“yoke”).
词性: verb
