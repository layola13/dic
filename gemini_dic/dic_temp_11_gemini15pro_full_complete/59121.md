dynamicism:/ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/ /daɪˈnæmɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. |活力，劲头，动态，推动力|The dynamism of the city is contagious.  这座城市的活力很有感染力。| dynamism is the quality of being characterized by vigorous activity and progress. It refers to the energy, force, and constant change or progress that characterizes something or someone.|The dynamism of the tech industry is what attracts so many young people. 科技行业的活力吸引了众多年轻人。|近义词：energy, vigor, vitality, drive, momentum|反义词：inertness, stagnation, apathy|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dynamisme*, from Ancient Greek *δύναμις* (dúnamis, “power, might, strength”) + *-ισμός* (-ismós).
