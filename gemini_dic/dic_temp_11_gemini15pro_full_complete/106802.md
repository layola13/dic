lability:/ləˈbɪləti/
n.
易变性，不稳定性
The tendency to change rapidly and unpredictably, especially of mood or emotions.
Her emotional lability made it difficult for her to maintain stable relationships.  她的情绪不稳定使她难以维持稳定的关系。
The lability of the chemical compound made it difficult to store. 这种化合物的易变性使其难以储存。

近义词: instability, volatility, mutability, changeability
反义词: stability, constancy, permanence
词性: 名词
