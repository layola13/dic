addable:/ˈæd.ə.bəl/ | adj. | 可添加的，可加的 | Able to be added. |  The numbers are addable. 这些数字可以相加。|  The ingredients are addable to the mixture. 这些成分可以添加到混合物中。| synonyms: summable,  addible | antonyms: non-addable, indivisible | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From add + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for being," or "tending to." "Add" comes from the Latin "addere," meaning "to give to," formed from "ad-" (to) + "dare" (to give).
