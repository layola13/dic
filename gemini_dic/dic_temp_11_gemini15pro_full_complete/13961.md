aurate:/ˈɔːreɪt/ /ˈɑːrət/| adj. n.|镀金的，金酸盐|A compound containing gold, typically as an anion.  A gilded surface or object.|例句：The aurate frame shimmered in the candlelight.  镀金的画框在烛光下闪烁。|近义词：gilded, gold-plated|反义词:tarnished, dull|词性:adjective, noun
aurate:/ˈɔːreɪt/ /ˈɑːrət/| n.|金酸盐|A salt of auric acid.|例句：Sodium aurate is used in some electroplating processes. 金酸钠用于某些电镀工艺。|近义词：gold salt|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *auratus*, past participle of *aurare* "to gild," from *aurum* "gold."
