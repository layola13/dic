knockstone:/ˈnɒkˌstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈnɑːkˌstoʊn/ (US)| n.|敲石; 磨刀石; 试金石|A hard stone used for sharpening edged tools or for testing the quality of gold by observing the color of the streak it leaves on the stone.|The craftsman used a knockstone to sharpen his chisel. 工匠用敲石来磨他的凿子。|Synonyms: whetstone, touchstone, grindstone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
