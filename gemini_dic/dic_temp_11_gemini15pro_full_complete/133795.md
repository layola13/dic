nongenuinely:/nɒnˈdʒɛnjuɪnli/
adv.
不真诚地，不真实地
Not genuinely; in a way that is not sincere or authentic.
His smile seemed nongenuinely friendly. 他的笑容看起来并不真诚友好。

近义词: insincerely, falsely, artificially, deceptively, dishonestly
反义词: genuinely, sincerely, authentically, truly, honestly
词性: adverb

词源:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "genuinely" (adverb form of "genuine").  "Genuine" comes from Latin "genuinus" meaning "innate, native, natural," ultimately from "gignere" meaning "to beget."
