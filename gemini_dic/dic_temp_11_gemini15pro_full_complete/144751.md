over-chilled:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪld/
adj.
过冷的，冷却过度的
Cooled to an excessive degree.
The over-chilled wine lost some of its subtle flavors. 这瓶过冷的葡萄酒失去了一些微妙的味道。
近义词：overcooled, excessively chilled
反义词：overheated, warm, lukewarm
词性：形容词

over-chilled: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪld/
verb (past participle)
过冷的，冷却过度的
Having been cooled to an excessive degree.  (This functions as an adjective formed from the past participle.)
The champagne, over-chilled in the ice bucket, was less bubbly than expected. 在冰桶里冷却过度的香槟，不如预期中那么起泡。
近义词：overcooled, excessively chilled
反义词：overheated, warmed
词性：动词（过去分词，用作形容词）
