belch:/bɛltʃ/ /bɜːltʃ/
verb
打嗝；喷出
to release air from the stomach through the mouth, often with a loud noise; to emit something violently, like smoke or flames.
He belched loudly after drinking the soda.  他喝完汽水后大声地打嗝。
The volcano belched smoke and ash. 火山喷出烟雾和灰烬。
近义词:burp, eructate, emit, spew
反义词:suppress (a belch)

词性:verb

belch:/bɛltʃ/ /bɜːltʃ/
noun
打嗝；喷出物
an act of belching; something belched out.
He let out a loud belch. 他发出一声响亮的打嗝。
A belch of black smoke came from the chimney.  一股黑烟从烟囱里喷出来。
近义词:burp, eruption
反义词:(None for the noun form)

词性:noun

词源:Old English *bealcian (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *balgijaną "to swell," from *balg- "bag, swelling." Cognate with Old Norse belgia "to bellow."
