containerless:/kənˈteɪnərlɪs/
adj.
无容器的；非盛器式的
Relating to or denoting a process in which a material is melted and solidified without coming into contact with a container.
Containerless processing allows materials to be processed without contamination from a crucible.  无容器处理允许材料在不受坩埚污染的情况下进行加工。
Containerless solidification can produce materials with unique microstructures.  无容器凝固可以生产具有独特微观结构的材料。
近义词: crucible-free, free-floating
反义词: contained
词性: adjective
