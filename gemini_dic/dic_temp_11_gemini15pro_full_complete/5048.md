all-out:/ˌɔːl ˈaʊt/ | adj. | 全力的，竭尽全力的 | using all your strength or resources; complete and total | The team made an all-out effort to win the championship.  该队全力以赴赢得了冠军。| 近义词: wholehearted, exhaustive, maximum, comprehensive | 反义词: half-hearted, limited, minimal, restricted | 词性: 形容词

all-out:/ˌɔːl ˈaʊt/ | adv. | 全力以赴地，竭尽全力地 | with maximum effort or intensity |  They went all-out for the party decorations. 他们为派对装饰全力以赴。 | 近义词: completely, totally, fully, entirely | 反义词: partially, halfheartedly, minimally | 词性: 副词

词源:  "all out" 最初是一个航海术语，指的是船帆完全展开以获得最大的速度。后来，这个词语的含义扩展到其他领域，表示“全力以赴”或“竭尽全力”。
