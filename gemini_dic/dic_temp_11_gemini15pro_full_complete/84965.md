haply:/ˈhæpli/| adv.|或许，可能，偶然|Perhaps, by chance.|例句：Haply I may see him tomorrow. 也许我明天能见到他。|近义词：perhaps, possibly, maybe, perchance|反义词:certainly, definitely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *haply, *happely, from hap (chance, luck) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse happ (luck, chance).
