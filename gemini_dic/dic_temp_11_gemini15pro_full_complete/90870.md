hunker:/ˈhʌŋkər/| v.|蹲下，盘踞，固守|To squat or crouch down close to the ground.|例句：The soldiers hunkered down in the trenches. 士兵们蜷缩在战壕里。

例句：He hunkered down beside the fire to get warm. 他蹲在火堆旁取暖。|近义词：squat, crouch, huddle|反义词:stand, rise|词性:动词

hunker down:/ˈhʌŋkər daʊn/| v.| 蹲下；准备长期抵抗困境；安顿下来|To settle in for a period of resistance or stability.|例句：With the blizzard approaching, we hunkered down for the night. 暴风雪即将来临，我们安顿下来过夜。

例句：The company is hunkering down to weather the economic storm.  这家公司正在努力抵御经济风暴。|近义词：dig in, settle in, brace oneself|反义词: venture out, give up|词性:动词短语


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *hunkeren "to squat," likely of imitative origin.  First recorded in American English in the early 19th century. 最初记录于19世纪早期的美国英语中，可能源于荷兰语 *hunkeren “蹲下”，可能是拟声词。
