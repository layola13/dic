outspreading:/ˌaʊtˈsprɛdɪŋ/ | adj. |  展开的，伸展的 | Extending or spreading outwards. | The eagle soared with outspreading wings.  老鹰展开翅膀翱翔。 | 近义词: extended, stretched, widespread | 反义词: contracted, folded, closed | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From "out-" (meaning outward) + "spread" (from Old English "sprædan," meaning to scatter or stretch out).  词源：来自“out-”（意为向外）+“spread”（来自古英语“sprædan”，意为散布或伸展）。
