influentially:/ˌɪnfluˈenʃəli/| adv.|有影响地，有势力地|In a way that has a significant impact or effect.|Her work has influenced the field influentially. 她的工作对该领域产生了深远的影响。|近义词：powerfully, effectively, significantly|反义词：insignificantly, weakly|词性:副词
词源: From influence + -ial + -ly. Ultimately from Latin influere "to flow into."
