crossfit: /ˈkrɒsfɪt/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfɪt/ (US)| n. | 交叉健身 | A branded fitness regimen involving constantly varied functional movements performed at high intensity.  It is a strength and conditioning program consisting mainly of a mix of aerobic exercise, calisthenics (body weight exercises), and Olympic weightlifting. |  Example: She started CrossFit to improve her overall fitness. 她开始进行CrossFit以提高整体健康水平。 | Example: CrossFit is known for its challenging workouts.  CrossFit以其高难度的训练而闻名。 | 近义词:  functional training, high-intensity training, boot camp | 反义词:  low-intensity exercise, traditional weight training | 词性: noun


crossfit: /ˈkrɒsfɪt/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfɪt/ (US) | adj. | 交叉健身的 | Relating to the CrossFit fitness regimen. | Example: He follows a CrossFit training program. 他遵循一个CrossFit训练计划。 | Example: She wears CrossFit apparel to the gym. 她穿着CrossFit服装去健身房。 | 近义词:  functional fitness, high-intensity | 反义词:  traditional fitness, low-impact | 词性: adjective
