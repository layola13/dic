disklike:/ˈdɪsklaɪk/| adj.|圆盘状的，像圆盘的|Resembling a disk; flat and circular.|The spaceship was disklike in shape.  这艘宇宙飞船是圆盘状的。|近义词：disclike, discoid, circular, round, flat|反义词:spherical, globular|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From *disk* + *-like*.  *Disk* ultimately comes from the Latin *discus*, meaning "dish" or "quoit."  *-like* is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
