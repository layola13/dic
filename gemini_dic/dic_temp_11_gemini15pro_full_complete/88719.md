hobgoblin:/ˈhɒbɡɒblɪn/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbɡɑːblɪn/ (US)| n. | 鬼怪；妖怪；令人恐惧的事物 |A mischievous and often malicious goblin. A source of fear or anxiety. |例句：The children were afraid of the hobgoblins in the dark forest. 孩子们害怕黑暗森林里的妖怪。

例句：He let his hobgoblins of fear get the better of him. 他被恐惧的鬼怪吓坏了。|近义词：goblin, bogeyman, bugbear, phantom|反义词: comfort, reassurance | noun
