hypochondriasm: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæzəm/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndrɪæzəm/ (US)| n. |疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, even when there is no medical evidence to support the concern.  It involves misinterpreting minor bodily sensations or symptoms as signs of a grave condition.|例句：Her hypochondriasm led her to visit the doctor multiple times a month. 她的疑病症导致她每个月去看好几次医生。

例句：He was convinced his headache was a brain tumor, a classic example of hypochondriasm. 他确信他的头痛是脑瘤，这是疑病症的典型例子。|近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) robust health, well-being|词性: noun
