fogeyish:/ˈfoʊɡiɪʃ/| adj.|守旧的，古板的|Old-fashioned and resistant to change; having old-fashioned ideas and habits.|例句：His fogeyish views on technology made him seem out of touch. 他对技术的守旧观点让他看起来与时代脱节。

例句：She dismissed their concerns as fogeyish and irrelevant. 她认为他们的担忧是守旧且无关紧要的。|近义词：old-fashioned, antiquated, stuffy, conservative, fuddy-duddy|反义词：modern, progressive, contemporary|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fogey (an old-fashioned person) + -ish (suffix forming adjectives).  Fogey likely comes from the Scots word "fogie," meaning a soldier invalided out of service.
