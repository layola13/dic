intravenous:/ˌɪntrəˈviːnəs/| adj.|静脉内的；静脉注射的|Existing or taking place within, or administered into, a vein.|The patient received intravenous fluids. 病人接受了静脉输液。|Synonyms:  inside a vein, into a vein|Antonyms: extravenous (rarely used), intramuscular|adjective
