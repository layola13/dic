arminian:/ɑːrˈmɪniən/ | adj. n. |亚米念主义的；亚米念主义者 | Relating to or adhering to the doctrines of Jacobus Arminius, a Dutch theologian who rejected predestination and affirmed free will.  A follower of Arminius. |  He identified as an Arminian, believing in free will. 他自认为是亚米念主义者，相信自由意志。 | The church was split between Calvinists and Arminians.  这个教会分裂成加尔文主义者和亚米念主义者。 |近义词: free-will, |反义词: Calvinistic, predestinarian |词性:形容词，名词
