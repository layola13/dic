anamnestic:/ˌænæmˈnɛstɪk/ | adj. |记忆的；唤起记忆的；基于记忆的| Relating to or assisting memory, especially of past events; based on memory. |  The patient's anamnestic response helped the doctor diagnose the illness.  病人的记忆反应帮助医生诊断了疾病。| 近义词: mnemonic, reminiscent | 反义词: amnesic, forgetful |词性:形容词

anamnesis:/ˌænæmˈniːsɪs/ | n. |回忆；anamnesis | The act or process of recalling to mind past experiences, especially in medicine, the patient's account of their medical history. |  A thorough anamnesis is crucial for accurate diagnosis. 彻底的病史采集对于准确诊断至关重要。| 近义词: recollection, recall | 反义词: amnesia, forgetting |词性:名词
