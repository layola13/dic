interdifferentiate:/ˌɪntərˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪˌeɪt/
verb
相互区分，互相分化
To distinguish between; to make or become different from one another.
The cells begin to interdifferentiate and form distinct tissues. 这些细胞开始互相分化，形成不同的组织。
Differentiate, distinguish, separate
Confuse, merge
v.
