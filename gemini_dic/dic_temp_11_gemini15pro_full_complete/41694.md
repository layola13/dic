corrigendum:/ˌkɒrɪˈdʒɛndəm/ /ˌkɔːrɪˈdʒɛndəm/ | n. |勘误表；更正；|A printed or published correction of an error in a book or other printed text. | Example: A corrigendum was issued to correct the misprinted date in the first edition.  勘误表已发布，以纠正第一版中印刷错误的日期。 | Example:  The journal published a corrigendum in the next issue, acknowledging the error in the data. 该期刊在下一期发表了一份勘误表，承认了数据中的错误。 | Synonyms: correction, amendment, emendation, rectification | Antonyms: error, mistake | noun
