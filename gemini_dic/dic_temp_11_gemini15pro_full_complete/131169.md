nonbreaching:/nɒnˈbriːtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 未违约的，不违反的 | Not in breach of a contract or agreement; complying with the terms of a contract or agreement.  | The nonbreaching party is entitled to damages. 未违约方有权获得损害赔偿。| The nonbreaching party can terminate the contract. 未违约方可以终止合同。 | 近义词：compliant, conforming, observing | 反义词：breaching, violating, defaulting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "breaching" (present participle of "breach," from Old French "breche," meaning "a break or gap").
