minorite:/maɪˈnɔːrɪti/ /maɪˈnɔːrəti/
|n.|少数民族；少数派；未成年人|A group of people who differ racially or politically from a larger group of which it is a part; a group or member of a group that constitutes less than half of a population; a person under the legal age of majority.|例句：The new law protects the rights of minorities. 这项新法律保护少数群体的权利。|近义词：minority group, underaged|反义词：majority|词性: noun
