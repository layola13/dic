gwiniad:/ˈɡwɪniəd/| n. |白鲑鱼(一种生活在英国湖泊中的鱼)|A gwiniad is a type of freshwater whitefish found in certain lakes in the UK, particularly in Wales and the Lake District of England. It's known for its delicate flavour and is considered a prized catch by anglers.|例句：The gwiniad is a species of fish found in Bala Lake. 白鲑鱼是一种在巴拉湖中发现的鱼类。
