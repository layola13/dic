caravanists:/ˈkærəvænɪsts/
n.
旅行拖车度假者；房车旅行者
People who travel and live in caravans, especially for holidays.
Caravanists often enjoy the freedom of the open road.  旅行拖车度假者通常享受户外道路的自由。
Many caravanists choose to stay at campsites during their travels. 许多旅行拖车度假者在旅行期间选择住在露营地。
近义词：campers, RV enthusiasts, motorhomers
反义词: stay-at-home vacationers, hotel guests
词性: noun
