all-inclusive:/ɔːlɪnˈkluːsɪv/| adj.|全包的，包括一切的|Including everything, especially all the costs, charges, and services of a holiday or other purchase.|例句：We booked an all-inclusive holiday to the Caribbean. 我们预订了一个去加勒比海的全包假期。|例句：The price is all-inclusive, covering flights, accommodation, and meals.  这个价格是全包的，包括机票、住宿和餐饮。|近义词：comprehensive, complete, full|反义词:exclusive, limited, partial|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From all + inclusive.  "Inclusive" comes from the Latin "includere" meaning "to shut in, enclose."
