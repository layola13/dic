overmuchness:/ˌoʊvərˈmʌtʃnəs/| n.| 过多，过剩|An excessive amount or degree of something.  It implies a burdensome or overwhelming quality.| Her overmuchness of concern became suffocating. 她过多的关心令人窒息。|近义词：excess, superfluity, surfeit|反义词: lack, scarcity, insufficiency|词性:名词
Etymology:  From 'overmuch' (meaning excessively much) + '-ness' (a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). 'Overmuch' is from Old English 'ofermycel', from 'ofer' (over) + 'mycel' (much).
