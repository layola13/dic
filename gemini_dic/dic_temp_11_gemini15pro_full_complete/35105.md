clotter:/ˈklɑtər/ (US), /ˈklɒtə(r)/ (UK)| v. |凝块; 结块; 使…凝结|To cause (a liquid, such as blood) to form clots or to form clots.|The blood clotted quickly after the injury. 受伤后血液迅速凝结。|近义词：coagulate, thicken, congeal|反义词:thin, liquefy|verb


clotter:/ˈklɑtər/ (US), /ˈklɒtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |凝结剂|Something that causes clotting.| This chemical acts as a clotter. 这种化学物质起凝结剂的作用。|近义词：coagulant|反义词:anticoagulant|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clotteren, frequentative of clotten (“to clot”), from Old English clott (“lump, clod”).
