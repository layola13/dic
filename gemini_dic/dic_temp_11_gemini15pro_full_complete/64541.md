errantly:/ˈerəntli/ | adv. | 错误地，漫无目的地 | In a wandering or incorrect manner; mistakenly. | He errantly wandered into the restricted area. 他错误地走进了禁区。|  She errantly assumed he agreed with her. 她错误地认为他同意她的观点。 | 近义词: mistakenly, wrongly, incorrectly, astray | 反义词: correctly, accurately, rightly, properly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *errant- (stem of errans), present participle of errare "to wander, err," from Proto-Italic *ers- "to wander," from Proto-Indo-European *ers- "to move, set in motion."
