inscription:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |题词，铭文，刻字| something inscribed, as on a monument or in a book. | The inscription on the tombstone was barely legible. 墓碑上的铭文几乎难以辨认。 |synonyms: lettering, engraving, carving | antonyms: erasure, deletion | noun

inscription:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |登记，注册|  the act of inscribing. | His inscription in the guest book was dated July 10th. 他在留言簿上的登记日期是7月10日。 | synonyms: enrollment, registration | antonyms: deregistration, withdrawal | noun
