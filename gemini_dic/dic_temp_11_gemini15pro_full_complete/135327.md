nonpedagogically:/ˌnɑːnˌpɛdəˈɡɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 非教学地，非教育地 | In a manner that is not related to teaching or education; not in a way intended to instruct or educate. | He explained the complex theory nonpedagogically, using jargon and assuming prior knowledge. 他用术语解释了这个复杂的理论，并且假定了先验知识，完全没有教学法。

The scientist presented his findings nonpedagogically, making it difficult for the lay audience to understand.  这位科学家以非教学的方式展示了他的发现，这使得非专业人士很难理解。| 近义词: unacademically, informally | 反义词: pedagogically, didactically | 词性: 副词
