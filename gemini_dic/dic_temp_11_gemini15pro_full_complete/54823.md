dispersibilities:/dɪˌspɜːrsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
分散性，分散能力
The ability of a substance to be dispersed or spread out, especially in a liquid.
The dispersibilities of the pigments in the paint affect its overall quality.  颜料在油漆中的分散性会影响其整体质量。
The researchers are studying the dispersibilities of nanoparticles in different solvents. 研究人员正在研究纳米粒子在不同溶剂中的分散性。
synonyms: spreadability, distributability, diffusibility
antonyms: insolubility, immiscibility
noun


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
v.
分散，散布
To distribute or spread over a wide area.
The crowd dispersed after the concert.  音乐会结束后，人群散开了。
The wind dispersed the seeds across the field. 风把种子吹散到田野里。
synonyms: scatter, disseminate, distribute
antonyms: gather, collect, assemble
verb


dispersible:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪbl/
adj.
可分散的
Able to be dispersed or spread out.
The powder is dispersible in water.  这种粉末可分散在水中。
This type of paint is highly dispersible and easy to apply.  这种类型的油漆高度可分散，易于涂抹。
synonyms: spreadable, distributable, diffusible
antonyms: insoluble, immiscible
adjective
