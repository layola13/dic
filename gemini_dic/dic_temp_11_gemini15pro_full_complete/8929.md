antiherpes:/ˌæntiˈhɜːrpiːz/ | adj. |抗疱疹的|Used to describe drugs or treatments that are effective against herpes viruses.  It signifies something that combats or prevents herpes infections. |This cream is an antiherpes medication.  这种药膏是一种抗疱疹药物。| The doctor prescribed an antiherpes drug. 医生开了抗疱疹的药。|近义词:antiviral|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but potentially "proviral" in a specific scientific context)|词性:adjective
