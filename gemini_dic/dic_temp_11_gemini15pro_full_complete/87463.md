hessan:/ˈhɛsən/ | n. |粗麻布; 粗麻袋|A coarse fabric made of jute or hemp, used for sacks and wrapping.|The coffee beans were shipped in hessan sacks. 咖啡豆是用粗麻袋运来的。|近义词:burlap, sackcloth, gunny|反义词:silk, satin|词性:名词

词源: From Hessian, referring to the German state of Hesse, known for producing this fabric.  源自“Hessian”，指的是德国黑森州，该州以生产这种织物而闻名。
