aerology:/ˌɛərˈɒlədʒi/
n.
气象学，高空气象学
The study of the free atmosphere throughout its vertical extent, as distinguished from studies confined to the layer in contact with the earth's surface.
Aerology is crucial for understanding weather patterns.  气象学对于理解天气模式至关重要。
Balloons are used in aerology to collect data. 气球用于气象学收集数据。

近义词:meteorology, atmospheric science
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

词源:  From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
