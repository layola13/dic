keel-like:/ˈkiːlˌlaɪk/| adj.|龙骨状的；像龙骨的|Resembling a keel, the central structural member of a ship's hull that runs lengthwise along the bottom.|The bottom of the surfboard was keel-like, providing stability in the waves.  冲浪板的底部呈龙骨状，在波浪中提供稳定性。|similar, resembling, shaped like| dissimilar, unlike|adjective

Etymology:
From 'keel' (Old English *cēol, from Proto-Germanic *kēlaz) + '-like' (Old English -līc).
