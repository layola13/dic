husbands:/ˈhʌzbəndz/
n.
丈夫（复数）
The plural form of "husband," referring to more than one husband.
My two sisters both have wonderful husbands. 我的两个姐妹都有很棒的丈夫。
The husbands went golfing while their wives went shopping. 丈夫们去打高尔夫球，而妻子们去购物。
