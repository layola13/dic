linear-momentum:/ˈlɪniər məˈmɛntəm/ | n. | 线性动量；动量 |  The product of the mass and velocity of an object.  It is a vector quantity, possessing both magnitude and direction. | The linear momentum of the car was enormous due to its high speed. 汽车由于速度很快，所以线性动量很大。| synonyms: momentum, impulse | antonyms: inertia (in a sense, as resistance to change in momentum) | noun
