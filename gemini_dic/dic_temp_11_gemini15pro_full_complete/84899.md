hankeringly:/ˈhæŋkərɪŋli/| adv.|渴望地，热切地|In a way that shows a strong desire for something.|She looked hankeringly at the chocolate cake. 她渴望地看着巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：longingly, yearningly, wistfully|反义词：indifferently, apathetically|词性:副词
词源: From hankering + -ly. Hankering from hankeren (Middle English)  meaning "to hang about"
