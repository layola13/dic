holarctic:/ˌhɒlˈɑːktɪk/ | adj. n.| 全北区的；全北区 | Relating to or inhabiting the Holarctic region (the biogeographic realm that encompasses the majority of habitats found north of the tropics, or in other definitions, north of the Tropic of Cancer).  | The wolverine has a holarctic distribution. 狼獾的分布是全北区的。 | The Holarctic flora shares many species. 全北区植物群有很多相同的物种。 | 近义词: boreal, northern | 反义词: southern, tropical | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From  Holo- + Arctic.  "Holo-" from Greek hólos "whole," and "Arctic" referring to the Arctic region.
