indictees:/ɪnˌdaɪkˈtiːz/
n.
被起诉人，被告
People who have been formally charged with a crime.
The indictees pleaded not guilty. 被告不认罪。
近义词：accused, defendants
反义词：plaintiffs, complainants
词性: Noun (Plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *enditer, from enditer to indict, accuse in writing, from en- + dite saying, from Latin dictum thing said, from neuter past participle of dicere to say
