eddaic: /ˈɛdeɪɪk/
adjective
埃达的; 古冰岛诗歌的
Of or relating to the Eddas, the two collections of ancient Icelandic literature.
The poem is full of eddaic imagery. 这首诗充满了埃达式的意象。
Synonyms: Norse, Icelandic, Old Norse
Antonyms: modern, contemporary
word type: adjective
