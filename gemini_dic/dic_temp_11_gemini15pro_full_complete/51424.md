detersively:/dɪˈtɜːsɪvli/
adv.|去污地，清洁地|In a manner that cleanses or purifies.|She wiped the counter detersively. 她清洁地擦拭了柜台。|Synonyms: cleansingly, purifyingly|Antonyms: dirtily, filthily|
Etymology: From detersive + -ly. Detersive from Latin dētersīvus, from dētergēre "to wipe off," from dē- "off" + tergēre "to wipe."
