openbill:/ˈoʊpənˌbɪl/ | n. | 敞嘴鸟; 阔嘴鸟 | A type of wading bird characterized by a distinctive gap between the upper and lower mandibles of its bill. | The openbill uses its specialized bill to catch snails. 敞嘴鸟用它特有的喙捕捉蜗牛。 |  近义词: open-billed stork | 反义词: N/A | noun

openbill:/ˈoʊpənˌbɪl/ | adj. | 敞嘴的; 阔嘴的 | Having a bill that does not fully close, leaving a gap. |  The openbill stork is easily recognizable by its openbill. 敞嘴鹳很容易通过它的敞开的嘴被识别出来。 | 近义词: open-billed | 反义词: N/A | adjective
