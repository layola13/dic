draughtier:/ˈdrɑːftiər/ | adj. | 通风更强的；更冷的 | More draughty; letting in more currents of cold air. |  The old house was much draughtier in the winter than the new one.  这栋老房子冬天比新房子通风更厉害。| synonyms: airier, breezier, chillier | antonyms: less draughty, warmer, airtight | adjective


draughtier:/ˈdrɑːftiər/ | adj. | 更单调乏味的；更沉闷的 | More tedious and dull.  (Less common usage) | The second half of the play was even draughtier than the first.  这出戏的下半场比上半场更单调乏味。| synonyms: duller, more tedious, more boring | antonyms: more interesting, more exciting, more engaging | adjective
