bloomers:/ˈbluːmərz/ (US), /ˈbluːməz/ (UK)| n.|灯笼裤；女式短运动裤；开花期|Loose, baggy trousers gathered at the knee or just below, typically worn by women or girls for sports or as casual wear.  Also refers to the state of a plant being in bloom.|例句：She wore a pair of pink bloomers for the cycling race. 她穿着一条粉红色的灯笼裤参加自行车比赛。

例句：The apple trees are in full bloomers now. 现在苹果树正盛开着花。


近义词：shorts, knickers, pants, trousers (for clothing); blossom, flowering (for blooming)|反义词: (For blooming) dormancy, wilt|词性:noun


bloomer:/ˈbluːmərz/ (US), /ˈbluːməz/ (UK)| n.|大错；愚蠢的错误；失败|A complete failure or a spectacularly embarrassing mistake.|例句：He made a real bloomer when he forgot the president's name. 他把总统的名字忘了，真是个大错。

例句：The new product launch was a bit of a bloomer. 新产品的发布有点失败。

近义词：mistake, blunder, error, gaffe, howler|反义词:success, triumph|词性:noun
