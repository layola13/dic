grotty:/ˈɡrɒti/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːti/ (US)| adj.|肮脏的，邋遢的，令人不愉快的|Unpleasant, dirty, or of poor quality.  Referring to a place, object, or situation that is run-down, shabby, and generally unpleasant.|例句：The hotel room was grotty and smelled of damp.  酒店房间很脏，有股霉味。

例句：I feel a bit grotty today – I think I'm coming down with a cold. 我今天感觉有点不舒服——我想我快要感冒了。|近义词：dirty, filthy, shabby, squalid, unpleasant, nasty|反义词:clean, pleasant, comfortable|词性:adjective
词源:Probably from "grotesque," influenced by "gritty."
