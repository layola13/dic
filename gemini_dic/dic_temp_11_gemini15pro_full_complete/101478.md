iridescently:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəntli/
adverb
彩虹般地，闪着彩虹般的光
showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
The hummingbird's feathers shimmered iridescently in the sunlight.  蜂鸟的羽毛在阳光下闪耀着彩虹般的光芒。
The soap bubble floated iridescently in the air. 肥皂泡在空中闪耀着彩虹般的光芒。

近义词:prismatically, opalescently, shimmeringly
反义词:dully, plainly

词性:adverb

词源: From iridescent + -ly.  Iridescent comes from Latin iris, iridis "rainbow" + -escent "beginning to be."
