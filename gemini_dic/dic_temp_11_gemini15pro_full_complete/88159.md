hilliest:/ˈhɪliɪst/
adjective
最多山的
Having the most hills; most hilly.
The hilliest part of the journey was the last five miles.  旅途中最多山的部分是最后五英里。
近义词:mountainous, undulating
反义词:flat, level
词性:adjective
词源:From hill + -y + -est.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *hulniz.
