outmouth:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊθ/
verb
胜过…的口才; 比…更会说
to surpass in loudness or abusiveness of speech; to outdo in talking
He outmouthed his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过对手的口才。
近义词: outspeak, outtalk
反义词: be quiet, be silent
词性:v.

outmouth:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊθ/
noun
大嘴巴; 说话粗鲁的人
a person who speaks loudly or abusively
He's known as an outmouth in the community. 他在社区里以大嘴巴著称。
近义词: loudmouth, bigmouth
反义词: quiet person
词性:n.
