annularity:/ˌænjuˈlærɪti/
n.
环状；环形；环状性
The state or quality of being annular.  A ring-shaped object or formation.
The annularity of the eclipse was breathtaking.  这次日食的环状景象令人叹为观止。
近义词：circularity, ring-shapedness
反义词：linearity
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *annularitas, from Latin annularis (“ring-shaped”), from annulus (“ring”), diminutive of anus (“ring, circle”).
