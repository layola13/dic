amphitropal: /æmˈfɪtrəpəl/
adjective
双生胚珠的，两侧胚珠的
(of an ovule) curved so that the micropyle and chalaza are close together, almost at opposite ends of a diameter.
The amphitropal ovule is characteristic of some families of flowering plants.  双生胚珠是某些开花植物科的特征。
Near Antonyms:  anatropous, orthotropous, campylotropal
Etymology:  From Greek amphi- (around, both) + tropal (turning).
word type: adjective
