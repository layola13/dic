dutchy:/ˈdʌtʃi/| n.|公爵领地，公国 (尤指较小的)|A duchy is a territory or region ruled by a duke or duchess, especially a smaller one. |The small duchy was known for its beautiful vineyards.  这个小公国以其美丽的葡萄园而闻名。|Synonym: dukedom, principality|Antonym: kingdom, empire|noun
