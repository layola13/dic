USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? 他做了什么值得这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't take action? 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to do, I would tell you. 如果我知道该怎么做，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what was going on in her mind. 我希望我知道她心里在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to think. 我不知道该怎么想。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. 她不明白我在说什么。

PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？
What about going to the movies? 去看电影怎么样？
What if it rains? 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? 为了什么？
So what? 那又怎样？
