humored:/ˈhjuːmərd/ | adj. | 幽默的；诙谐的；迁就的 | Indulging or showing a tendency to indulge someone's whims or fancies. Also, having or showing a sense of humor. | She was always good-humored, even when things went wrong. 她总是很幽默，即使事情出了问题也一样。|  He humored her by pretending to believe her story. 他假装相信她的故事来迁就她。 | Synonyms: amused, cheerful, jovial, indulgent, lenient | Antonyms: ill-humored, grumpy, serious, strict | word property: adjective
