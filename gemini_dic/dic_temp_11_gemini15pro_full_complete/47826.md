decategorize:/diːˈkætəɡəˌraɪz/ | v. | 去分类，取消分类 | To remove something from a category or classification; to make something no longer fit into a particular category. | The library has decategorized its fiction section, allowing books to be shelved alphabetically by author. 图书馆取消了小说的分类，允许书籍按作者的字母顺序排列。

The museum decategorized its collection to encourage visitors to explore connections between different art forms.  博物馆取消了藏品的分类，以鼓励参观者探索不同艺术形式之间的联系。| synonyms: reclassify, regroup, rearrange, reorganize | antonyms: categorize, classify, group, order | verb
