flabellate:/ˈflæbəleɪt/| adj.|扇形的，扇状的|Shaped like a fan; expanding in a fan-like form.|The peacock displayed its flabellate tail. 孔雀展示了它扇形的尾巴。|近义词：fan-shaped, fan-like|反义词：linear, straight|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *flabellatus*, past participle of *flabellare* "to fan," from *flabellum* "fan," diminutive of *flabrum* "blast, breeze."
