incriminator:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪtər/
n.|控告者，指控者|Someone or something that incriminates another person or thing.  A person or thing that makes someone seem guilty, especially of a crime.| The leaked document was the incriminator that led to his arrest. 泄露的文件是导致他被捕的罪证。|近义词：accuser, prosecutor|反义词：exonerator, defender|词性:noun
