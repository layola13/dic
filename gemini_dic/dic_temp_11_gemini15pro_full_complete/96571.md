indubitable:/ˌɪndʊˈbɪtəbl/ | adj. |不容置疑的；确凿的| Certain and unquestionable; beyond doubt.| Her talent is indubitable. 她的才华不容置疑。| The evidence of his guilt was indubitable.  他犯罪的证据确凿无疑。| synonyms: unquestionable, undeniable, indisputable, incontrovertible, irrefutable | antonyms: doubtful, questionable, debatable, disputable | Adjective
