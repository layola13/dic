innocuity:/ɪˈnɒkjuɪti/ |n.|无害，无毒，安全性|The innocuity of the substance was confirmed by laboratory tests.  该物质的无害性已由实验室测试证实。|例句：The researcher stressed the innocuity of the new drug. 研究人员强调了这种新药的无害性。

例句：Despite its innocuity to humans, the chemical is deadly to insects. 尽管这种化学物质对人类无害，但对昆虫却是致命的。

|近义词：harmlessness, safety, innocence|反义词：harmfulness, toxicity, danger|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin innocuitās, from innocuus (“harmless”), from in- (“not”) + nocuus (“harmful”).
