bazooka:/bəˈzuːkə/ | n. |火箭筒；火箭炮 |A portable rocket launcher used against tanks and other armored vehicles. It was developed during World War II. |The soldier fired the bazooka at the enemy tank. 士兵用火箭筒向敌方坦克开火。

The bazooka was a game-changer in infantry warfare. 火箭筒改变了步兵战的格局。| synonym: rocket launcher, RPG | antonym: N/A | noun
