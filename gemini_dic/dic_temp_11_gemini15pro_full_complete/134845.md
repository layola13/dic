nonmoderate:/nɒnˈmɒdərət/ /nɑːnˈmɑːdərət/| adj.|非温和的，不节制的，过度的|Not moderate; excessive or extreme.|例句：The nonmoderate views of the fringe groups caused concern.  边缘群体的极端观点引起了人们的担忧。

例句：Her nonmoderate spending habits led her into debt. 她无节制的消费习惯让她负债累累。|近义词：extreme, excessive, immoderate, radical|反义词：moderate, temperate, reasonable|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non, from Old Latin *noenum, contraction of *ne oinom "not one."
moderate (adjective/verb): From Latin moderatus "kept within bounds, restrained," past participle of moderari "to regulate, control," from modus "measure, manner."
