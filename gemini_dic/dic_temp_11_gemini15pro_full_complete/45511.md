cureless:/ˈkjʊəlɪs/| adj.|无法治愈的，不可救药的|Incurable; not capable of being cured.  The disease is, unfortunately, cureless at this stage.  这个疾病很不幸在这个阶段是无法治愈的。|近义词：incurable, irremediable, terminal, hopeless|反义词：curable, treatable, remediable|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From cure + -less.  Cure comes from the Old French curer, meaning "to take care of, heal."  The suffix -less indicates a lack or absence of something.
