imaginant:/ɪˈmædʒɪnənt/
n.|想象出的事物；虚构的事物|Something imagined; a figment of the imagination.|The author populated her novel with strange imaginants.|近义词：figment, fantasy, chimera|反义词：reality, actuality|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin imaginans, present participle of imaginari (“to imagine”), from Latin imago (“image”).
