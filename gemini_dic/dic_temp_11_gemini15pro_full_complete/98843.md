| n. | 可理解性；清晰度 |The quality or state of being intelligible.  The plural form of intelligibility. |The intelligiblenesses of the various dialects varied greatly, making communication challenging. 不同方言的可理解程度差异很大，使得交流变得困难。|近义词：clarity, comprehensibility, understandability |反义词：unintelligibility, incomprehensibility, obscurity | noun
