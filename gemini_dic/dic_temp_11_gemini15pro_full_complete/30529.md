cephalism: /ˈsefəlɪzəm/ | n. | 头部集中化；头部化 | The concentration of sense organs, nervous control, etc., at the anterior end of the body, forming a head and brain, both during evolution and in the course of an embryo's development. |  Cephalism is a key characteristic of vertebrates. 脊椎动物的一个关键特征是头部集中化。|  The evolution of cephalism allowed for more complex behaviors. 头部化的进化使得更复杂的行为成为可能。 | synonym: cephalization | antonym: N/A | noun
