nonobjectifications:/nɒnəbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非对象化，非物化 | The act of not treating someone or something as a mere object; the avoidance of objectification.  This often involves recognizing the inherent worth, dignity, and agency of individuals or entities, rather than reducing them to instruments or commodities. |  Feminist philosophy often discusses nonobjectifications of women in media.  女权主义哲学经常讨论媒体中女性的非对象化。

The movement promotes the nonobjectification of animals, emphasizing their sentience and capacity for suffering.  该运动提倡动物的非物化，强调它们的感知能力和承受痛苦的能力。| 近义词: humanization, personification, subjectification | 反义词: objectification, dehumanization | 词性: 名词


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < *ne oinom (not one), from PIE *ne (not).
object (noun/verb):  From Latin objectum "something presented to the mind," past participle of obicere "to throw in the way of, oppose, present," from ob- "in the way of, against" + iacere "to throw."
-ification (suffix):  Forms nouns of action or process, from French -ification, from Latin -ificationem, accusative of -ificatio, from -ificare "to make," from -ficus "making, doing," related to facere "to do, make."
-s (suffix):  Plural marker.
