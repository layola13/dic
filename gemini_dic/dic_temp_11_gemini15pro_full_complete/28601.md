careline: /ˈkɛəˌlaɪn/ | n. | 关怀热线；服务热线 | A telephone number that people can call to get information or advice, especially about social or medical problems. |  You can call our careline for advice on caring for elderly relatives. 你可以拨打我们的关怀热线，获得照顾老年亲属的建议。

| Example:  The company has set up a careline for customers with questions about their products.  该公司为对产品有疑问的客户设立了服务热线。 | Synonym: helpline, hotline, support line | Antonym:  N/A | noun
