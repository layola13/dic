oaks:/oʊks/ | n. | 橡树；橡木 | A tree of the genus Quercus, bearing acorns as fruit.  The wood of the oak tree, used for furniture and building. |例句：The oaks in the park are hundreds of years old. 公园里的橡树有几百年的历史了。

例句：The table is made of solid oak. 这张桌子是用实心橡木做的。|近义词：tree |反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *okes, plural of *ok, from Old English āc (“oak, oak tree”), from Proto-Germanic *aikaz (“oak”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eyǵ- (“tree”). Cognate with Scots aik (“oak”), West Frisian iik (“oak”), Dutch eik (“oak”), German Eiche (“oak”), Danish eg (“oak”), Swedish ek (“oak”), Icelandic eik (“oak”), Latin aesculus (“Italian oak”).
