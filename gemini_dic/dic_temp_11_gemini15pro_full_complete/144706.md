ovaries:/ˈoʊvəriːz/
n.
卵巢
Female reproductive organs that produce eggs and hormones.
The ovaries release an egg each month during ovulation.  卵巢在排卵期每月释放一个卵子。
The ovaries produce estrogen and progesterone. 卵巢产生雌激素和孕激素。
Synonym:  (no direct synonym, but related terms include) female gonads, reproductive glands
Antonym:  (no direct antonym)
noun
