bloody-minded:/ˌblʌdi ˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|故意刁难的，固执的|Deliberately uncooperative or awkward.|Example: He was bloody-minded enough to refuse the offer just to spite them. 他固执到为了刁难他们而拒绝了这个提议。 |Example: Her bloody-minded attitude made it impossible to reach an agreement. 她固执的态度使得达成协议成为不可能。 |近义词:pigheaded, obstreperous, cantankerous|反义词:accommodating, flexible, yielding|词性:形容词

词源:  The word "bloody-minded" combines "bloody" (used as an intensifier and often considered mildly vulgar) with "minded" (having a particular mental disposition).  The "bloody" intensifier originated in British English.
