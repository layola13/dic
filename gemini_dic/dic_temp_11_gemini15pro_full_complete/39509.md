conodont:/ˈkɒnədɒnt/ (US) /ˈkoʊnəˌdɑːnt/| n. |牙形石|An extinct eel-like marine animal of the Paleozoic era, whose tooth-like microfossils are used for stratigraphic correlation.|
The conodonts helped geologists date the rock formations. 牙形石帮助地质学家确定了岩层的年代。|
Conodonts are useful index fossils. 牙形石是有用的标准化石。|
近义词: index fossil|
反义词: N/A|
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kōnos* "cone" + *odous* (genitive *odontos*) "tooth".
