bismuthinite:/ˈbɪzməθɪˌnaɪt/
n.
辉铋矿
A lead-gray mineral, Bi2S3, the principal ore of bismuth.
Bismuthinite is a common ore of bismuth. 辉铋矿是铋的一种常见矿石。
Bismuthinite occurs in hydrothermal veins. 辉铋矿产于热液矿脉中。

Synonyms: bismuth glance, bismuth sulfide
Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性: noun
