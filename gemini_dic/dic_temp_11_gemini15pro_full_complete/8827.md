antievolutionist:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/
n.
反进化论者
Someone who opposes the theory of evolution.
The antievolutionist argued against the teaching of evolution in schools.  反进化论者反对在学校教授进化论。
The museum exhibit sparked protests from antievolutionists. 博物馆的展览引发了反进化论者的抗议。
opponent of evolution, creationist
evolutionist, Darwinist
