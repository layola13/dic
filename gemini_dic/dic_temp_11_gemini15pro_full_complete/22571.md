booth:/buːθ/
n.|售货亭，摊位；电话亭；小隔间|a small enclosed space, such as one for selling goods or making telephone calls|例句：I ordered a coffee from the coffee booth. 我从咖啡售货亭点了一杯咖啡。|例句：He stepped into the phone booth to make a call. 他走进电话亭打电话。|近义词：stall, kiosk, cubicle, compartment|反义词:(none specific - perhaps open space)|词性:noun
