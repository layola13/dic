low-rises:/ˈloʊˌraɪzɪz/
noun
低层建筑
Buildings with a small number of floors, typically less than four or five stories.
Many people prefer living in low-rises for their close proximity to the ground and a sense of community. 许多人喜欢住在低层建筑里，因为它们离地面很近，而且有社区感。
low-rise buildings, low-rise apartments
high-rises, skyscrapers
