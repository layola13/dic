abstrusive: /əbˈstruːsɪv/ | adj. | 深奥的；难懂的；隐晦的 | Difficult to understand; obscure. |  The professor's abstrusive lectures left the students confused. 教授深奥的讲座让学生们感到困惑。

The abstrusive philosophical concepts were challenging to grasp. 这些深奥的哲学概念很难理解。


近义词: obscure, recondite, esoteric, arcane | 反义词: clear, simple, lucid, perspicuous | 词性: adjective


词源:  From Late Latin *abstrusus, past participle of abstrūdere (“to hide, conceal”), from ab- (“away from”) + trūdere (“to thrust, push”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *trewd- (“to push, press”).
