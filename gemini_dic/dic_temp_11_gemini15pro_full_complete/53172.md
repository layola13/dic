dippiest:/ˈdɪpiɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的，最笨的|Superlative adjective of "dippy," meaning extremely silly, foolish, or eccentric.|That was the dippiest thing I've ever heard. 那是我听过的最愚蠢的事情。|He made the dippiest excuse for being late. 他编了一个最愚蠢的迟到借口。|近义词：stupidest, silliest, foolish, idiotic|反义词：smartest, brightest, wisest|词性:形容词

词源:
From "dip" (meaning to briefly immerse) + "-y" (forming adjectives indicating a characteristic) + "-est" (superlative suffix). The original sense likely referred to someone whose mind was "dipped" or slightly impaired, hence foolish.
