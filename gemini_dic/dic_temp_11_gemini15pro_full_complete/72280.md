flote:/floʊt/ | v. |漂浮，浮动 |To rest or remain on the surface of a liquid; be buoyant. |The boat floated gently on the lake. 小船轻轻地漂浮在湖面上。|近义词：drift, float |反义词: sink |词性:动词

词源:  Middle English: from Old French *floter (verb), *flot (noun), of Germanic origin; related to fleet.
