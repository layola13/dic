gordiacea:/ɡɔːrˌdɪˈeɪʃə/ | n. | 绳虫纲; 铁线虫纲 | A class of parasitic worms, commonly known as horsehair worms or Gordian worms, characterized by their long, thin, thread-like bodies. | Horsehair worms, members of the Gordiacea class, are often found in water sources. 铁线虫，绳虫纲的成员，经常在水源中被发现。| 近义词: Nematomorpha (线形虫门)| 反义词: None | noun

gordiacean:/ɡɔːrˌdɪˈeɪʃən/ | adj.  n. |  绳虫纲的; 铁线虫纲的; 绳虫纲动物; 铁线虫纲动物 | Relating to or belonging to the Gordiacea class of parasitic worms; a member of the Gordiacea class. | The gordiacean worm wriggled in the water. 那条绳虫在水中扭动。 | 近义词: nematomorphan | 反义词: None | adjective, noun
