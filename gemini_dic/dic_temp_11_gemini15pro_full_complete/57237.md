downhill:/ˌdaʊnˈhɪl/| adj. adv.|下坡的；走下坡路的；越来越糟的|Going downhill means sloping downwards, or getting worse in condition or quality. |The road runs downhill to the village. 这条路下坡通向村庄。|近义词：descending, declining, worsening|反义词: uphill, ascending, improving|词性:形容词, 副词|词源: From 'down' + 'hill'. Literally meaning 'towards the bottom of a hill'.

downhill:/ˌdaʊnˈhɪl/| n.|下坡|A downhill is a slope that goes downwards.  (Less common usage)|It was a steep downhill all the way.  一路都是陡峭的下坡。|近义词：descent, slope|反义词: ascent, incline|词性:名词|词源: From 'down' + 'hill'. Literally meaning 'towards the bottom of a hill'.
