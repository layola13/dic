mercerize: /ˈmɜːrsəraɪz/ | v. | 丝光处理；使有光泽 | To treat (cotton thread or fabric) with caustic alkali to increase strength and give a lustrous appearance. | The company mercerizes cotton fabrics to give them a silky sheen.  这家公司对棉织物进行丝光处理，使其具有丝般的光泽。 |  The factory mercerized the thread to improve its strength. 这家工厂对线进行了丝光处理以提高其强度。 |  gloss, polish, sheen | dull, matte | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From John Mercer (1791–1866), British calico printer and chemist, who invented the process.
