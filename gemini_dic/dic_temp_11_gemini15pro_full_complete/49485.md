delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/ (US) /dɪˈljuːʒn/ (UK)| n. | 妄想，错觉 |A false belief, often of persecution or grandeur, that may accompany psychotic disorders.  A persistent false psychotic belief regarding the self or persons or objects outside the self that is maintained despite indisputable evidence to the contrary. | He suffers from delusions of grandeur, believing he is a king. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是一位国王。

She had delusions of being followed by the FBI. 她妄想被FBI跟踪。|近义词：hallucination, illusion, misconception, fallacy |反义词：reality, truth, fact |词性: noun
