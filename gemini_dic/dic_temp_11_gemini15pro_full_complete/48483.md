deep-water:/ˈdiːpˈwɔːtər/ | adj. |深水的，深海的 |Of, relating to, or inhabiting deep water.  |例句：Deep-water fishing is a popular sport. 深海钓鱼是一项很受欢迎的运动。|近义词：oceanic, pelagic |反义词:shallow-water, littoral |词性:形容词
deep-water:/ˈdiːpˈwɔːtər/ | n. |深水区 |An area of deep water.  |例句：The ship sailed into the deep-water. 船驶入了深水区。|近义词：ocean, sea |反义词:shallow, shoal |词性:名词


Etymology:
"deep" + "water"
"deep" (adj.): Old English dēop "deep," of Germanic origin; related to Old Saxon diop, Old Frisian diap, Old High German tiof, German tief, Old Norse djūpr, Gothic diups "deep."
"water" (n.): Old English wæter "water, liquid, moisture, rain; body of water, sea, river," of Germanic origin; related to Old Saxon watar, Old Frisian wetir, Dutch water, Old High German wazzar, German Wasser, Old Norse vatn, Gothic wato "water."
