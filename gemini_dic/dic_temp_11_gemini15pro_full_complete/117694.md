melain:/ˈmɛlənɪn/| n.|黑色素|A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for tanning of skin exposed to sunlight.|Melanin protects the skin from the harmful effects of UV radiation. 黑色素保护皮肤免受紫外线辐射的伤害。|Melanin gives skin its color. 黑色素赋予皮肤颜色。|近义词：pigment|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mélanine*, from Greek *μέλας* (mélas, “black, dark”).
