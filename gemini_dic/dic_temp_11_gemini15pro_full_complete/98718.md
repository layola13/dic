noun

belonging to or relating to the person who has insurance

近义词: policyholder's
反义词: insurer's
词性: possessive noun


insured's:/ɪnˈʃʊərdz/
adjective
被保险人的
belonging to or relating to the person who has insurance
The insurance company assessed the insured's claim. 保险公司评估了被保险人的索赔。
近义词: policyholder's
反义词: insurer's
词性: possessive adjective


词源:  From "insured" + -'s (possessive suffix). "Insured" comes from the verb "insure," which ultimately derives from Old French "enseurer"  (to make certain, guarantee).
