baller:/ˈbɔːlər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |高手；能人；富人；篮球高手|A person who is exceptionally good at something, especially in sports like basketball, or someone who is very wealthy and successful.  Originally referred to someone skilled at ball games.|He's a real baller on the court. 他在球场上真是个高手。|synonyms:  player, athlete, high-roller, big shot, mogul|antonyms: amateur, beginner, nobody|noun

baller:/ˈbɔːlər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |极好的；很棒的 (slang)|Excellent; impressive.  Used informally.|That new car is baller! 那辆新车太棒了！|synonyms: awesome, excellent, fantastic, amazing|antonyms: terrible, awful, bad|adjective
