ammoniacal:/ˌæməˈnaɪək(ə)l/
adjective
氨的；含氨的；像氨的
Of or relating to ammonia; containing ammonia or its properties.
The air was thick with an ammoniacal smell. 空气中弥漫着刺鼻的氨味。
Synonyms: ammoniated, ammonia-like
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a chemical property)
词性: adjective
