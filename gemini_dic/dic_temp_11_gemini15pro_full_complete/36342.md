coilability:/ˌkɔɪləˈbɪləti/
n.
可绕性，成卷性
The ability of a material, especially metal, to be wound or coiled without cracking or breaking.
This steel alloy has excellent coilability, making it ideal for springs. 这种钢合金具有优异的可绕性，使其成为制造弹簧的理想材料。
coil forming ability, windability
uncoilability, non-coilability
noun
