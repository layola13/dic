out-of-print:/ˌaʊt əv ˈprɪnt/ | adj. | 绝版的，脱销的 | No longer being printed or available for purchase from the publisher.  |  例句：The first edition of this book is out-of-print. 这本书的第一版已经绝版了。

例句：I'm looking for an out-of-print copy of "Moby Dick." 我正在寻找一本绝版的《白鲸》。

近义词：unavailable, discontinued | 反义词：in print, available | 词性：形容词
