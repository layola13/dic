hetaira:/hɪˈtaɪrə/
n.
高级妓女，艺妓
A highly educated and skilled courtesan in ancient Greece, often accomplished in music, poetry, and conversation.
Aspasia, the partner of Pericles, is a famous example of a hetaira. 阿斯帕西娅，伯里克利的伴侣，是一位著名的艺妓。
The hetaira entertained the men with her wit and knowledge.  这位艺妓以她的智慧和知识娱乐了男人们。
近义词：courtesan, concubine
反义词：wife, matron
词性: noun
