antihijacking:/ˌæntiˈhaɪdʒækɪŋ/
adjective
反劫机的
Designed to prevent or counter the hijacking of an aircraft or other vehicle.
The airport has implemented new antihijacking security measures. 机场实施了新的反劫机安全措施。
Near synonyms:  anti-piracy, counter-terrorism
Opposite: pro-hijacking (rare and generally inappropriate)
词性: adjective


antihijacking:/ˌæntiˈhaɪdʒækɪŋ/
noun
反劫机措施
Measures taken to prevent or counter hijacking.
Antihijacking procedures were reviewed after the incident. 事件发生后，对反劫机程序进行了审查。
Near synonyms: security measures, countermeasures
Opposite: N/A (no direct opposite for a noun referring to preventative measures)
词性: noun


Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix of Greek origin meaning "against, opposite of") + "hijacking" (from "hijack,"  verb meaning to seize or commandeer a vehicle by force)
