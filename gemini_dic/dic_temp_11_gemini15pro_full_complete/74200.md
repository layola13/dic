fourneau:/fuːrˈnoʊ/ (US), /fʊərˈnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|炉子，火炉，熔炉|A fourneau is an oven or furnace used for heating, cooking, or industrial processes.  It is an older term, more common in French, and less used in modern English. |The old bakery still used a wood-fired fourneau.  那家老面包店仍在使用烧木柴的炉子。|The iron ore was smelted in a large fourneau. 铁矿石在一个大型熔炉中熔炼。|Synonyms: oven, furnace, kiln, stove|Antonyms: refrigerator, freezer|noun
