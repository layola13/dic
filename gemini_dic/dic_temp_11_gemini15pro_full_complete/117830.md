mellifluous:/məˈlɪflʊəs/| adj.|流畅的；甜美的；悦耳的|Sweet or musical; pleasant to hear.|Her mellifluous voice soothed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音安抚了哭闹的婴儿。|The speaker had a mellifluous tone that kept the audience captivated. 演讲者流畅的语调吸引了观众的注意力。|近义词：euphonious, harmonious, melodious, sweet, smooth|反义词：cacophonous, harsh, grating, strident|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *mellifluus* (“flowing with honey”), from *melli-* (combining form of *mel*, “honey”) + *-fluus* (“flowing”), from *fluere* (“to flow”).
