drengage:/ˈdrɛnɪdʒ/ | n. | 排水；排水系统；排泄；（尤指情感的）发泄，宣泄 |The removal of surface or subsurface water from a given area by gravity or pumping.  The drainage system of the city needs to be improved. | The drainage system in the field wasn't adequate, leading to waterlogging. 这块田里的排水系统不够完善，导致了涝灾。 | 近义词: drainage system, sewer system, outflow, discharge | 反义词: irrigation, waterlogging | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Anglo-French *drenage, from Old French drenage, from drenar (“to drain”), from Vulgar Latin *drenāre, from Latin drēnare, present active infinitive of drēnō (“I drain, dry up”), from Proto-Italic *drēnō, from Proto-Indo-European *drēh₁nós (“dry”). Cognate with Italian drenare (“to drain”).
