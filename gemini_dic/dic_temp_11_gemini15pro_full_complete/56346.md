dogtooth:/ˈdɒɡtuːθ/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡtuːθ/ (US)| n. adj.|犬齿；犬牙；（建筑）锯齿饰；（植物）山慈菇|A pointed canine tooth, especially of a dog. Also, an architectural ornament resembling a dog's tooth, or a kind of early spring flower (Erythronium dens-canis).|The architect included a dogtooth pattern along the roofline. 建筑师沿着屋顶线加入了犬牙图案。|Synonyms: canine (tooth), hound's tooth (pattern), trout lily (flower)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a body part or architectural feature. For the flower, perhaps "cultivated flower" as a loose contrast.)|Noun, Adjective
