intellectually:/ˌɪntəˈlektʃuəli/ | adv. | 智力上地，理智地 | In an intellectual manner; with regard to the intellect. | She approached the problem intellectually, analyzing all the data before making a decision. 她理智地处理这个问题，在做决定之前分析了所有数据。|  She grasped the concepts intellectually but struggled to apply them practically. 她从智力上理解了这些概念，但在实际应用中却遇到了困难。 | 近义词: mentally, cerebrally, cognitively | 反义词: emotionally, instinctively, physically | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *intellectualiter, from Latin intellectualis (“intellectual”) + -iter (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from intellectus (“understanding, perception, comprehension”), from intellegere (“to understand”), from inter- (“between”) + legere (“to choose, gather, read”).
