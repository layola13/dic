n.
硼砂；硼酸盐
Borates are salts or esters of boric acid.
Borax is a common borate used in cleaning products. 硼砂是一种常用的硼酸盐，用于清洁产品。
The team discovered borates in the soil samples.  研究小组在土壤样本中发现了硼酸盐。
Synonyms: borate minerals, boric acid salts
