experimentative:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 实验性的，爱试验的 | Given to or characterized by experimentation; willing to try new things. |  She has an experimentative approach to cooking, always trying new recipes. 她做饭的方式很实验性，总是尝试新的食谱。 | The company has an experimentative phase in product development.  这家公司在产品开发阶段有一个试验阶段。| 近义词: experimental, innovative, empirical | 反义词: conservative, traditional | 词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from medieval Latin experimentativus, from experimentat- ‘tried by experiment’ (from the verb experiri) + -ivus.
