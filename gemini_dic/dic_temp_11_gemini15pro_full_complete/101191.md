invincibly:/ɪnˈvɪnsɪbli/
adverb
无敌地，不可战胜地
in a way that is too powerful to be defeated or overcome.
He fought invincibly throughout the tournament. 他在整个锦标赛中都表现得不可战胜。
Synonyms: unconquerably, insuperably, unbeatably
Antonyms: vulnerably, weakly, defenselessly
词性: 副词

invincible:/ɪnˈvɪnsɪbəl/
adjective
无敌的，不可战胜的
too powerful to be defeated or overcome.
She seemed invincible on the tennis court. 在网球场上，她似乎是不可战胜的。
Synonyms: unconquerable, insuperable, unbeatable
Antonyms: vulnerable, weak, defenseless
