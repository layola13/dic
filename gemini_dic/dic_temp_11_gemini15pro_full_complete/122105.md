mistitles:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪtlz/
verb|错误地命名，加错标题|To give a wrong or inappropriate title to something.
The librarian mistitled several books, leading to confusion among patrons.|图书馆管理员给几本书加错了标题，导致读者感到困惑。
misnames, mislabels|correctly titles, accurately names
