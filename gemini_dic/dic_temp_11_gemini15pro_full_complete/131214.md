noncabinet:/nɑːnˈkæbɪnɪt/
adj.
非内阁的
Not belonging to or part of a political cabinet.
The president appointed a noncabinet official to the task force. 总统任命了一位非内阁官员加入工作组。
近义词:non-ministerial
反义词:cabinet

词性: adjective
