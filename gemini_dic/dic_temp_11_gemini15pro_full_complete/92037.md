hyperperistalses:/ˌhaɪpərˌpɛrɪˈstɔːlsiːz/
| n. |肠道蠕动亢进|Increased frequency and strength of peristalsis, the wave-like muscle contractions that move food through the digestive tract.|The patient experienced hyperperistalses after eating the contaminated food. 病人吃了受污染的食物后，肠道蠕动亢进。|Near Synonyms: increased peristalsis, hypermotility |Antonyms: hypoperistalsis, hypomotility| noun
