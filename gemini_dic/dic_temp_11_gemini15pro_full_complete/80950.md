grace: /ɡreɪs/ | n. v. |优雅；恩典；魅力；宽限； grace (v.) 使优美；惠赐；装饰|A quality of moving in a smooth, relaxed, and attractive way. / To make something more attractive or beautiful. |She moved with effortless grace. 她举止优雅自然。 /  He graced the occasion with his presence.  他的出席使这个场合增色不少。|近义词：elegance, charm, poise, favor, kindness|反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness, disgrace|词性: noun, verb

词源: From Old French *grace*, from Latin gratia ("grace, gratefulness, agreeableness, pleasantness"), from gratus ("pleasing, agreeable, thankful, grateful").
