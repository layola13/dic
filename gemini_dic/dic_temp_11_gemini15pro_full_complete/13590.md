attenuate:/əˈtenjʊeɪt/ /əˈtɛnjuˌeɪt/| v. adj.|减少，减弱；使稀薄|to reduce the force, effect, or value of something|例句：The drug attenuates the effects of the virus. 这种药物减弱了病毒的作用。|近义词：weaken, diminish, lessen, reduce|反义词:strengthen, intensify, amplify|verb
attenuate:/əˈtenjʊeɪt/ /əˈtɛnjuˌeɪt/| adj.|减弱的，稀薄的|reduced in force, effect, or value|例句：The signal was attenuated by the thick walls. 信号被厚厚的墙壁减弱了。|近义词：weakened, diminished, lessened, reduced|反义词:strengthened, intensified, amplified|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin attenuatus, past participle of attenuare "to make thin, weaken," from Latin ad- "to" + tenuis "thin, rare, slight."
