embitterments: /ɪmˈbɪtərmənts/
n.
痛苦，辛酸；怨恨
The state of being embittered; bitterness.  Things that cause bitterness.
The constant criticism led to a growing sense of embitterment.  不断的批评导致越来越强烈的怨恨。
His embitterments stemmed from a difficult childhood. 他的痛苦源于艰难的童年。
近义词: bitterness, resentment, acrimony, rancor
反义词: contentment, happiness, satisfaction, joy
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

embitter: /ɪmˈbɪtər/
vt.
使怨恨；使痛苦
To make bitter; to cause someone to feel resentment or bitterness.
The loss of his job embittered him.  失去工作使他怨恨不已。
The experience embittered her against the world.  这段经历使她对这个世界充满了怨恨。
近义词: sour, poison, alienate
反义词: sweeten, gratify, please
词性: 动词

bitter: /ˈbɪtər/
adj.
苦的；痛苦的；充满仇恨的
Having a sharp, pungent taste or smell; causing pain or unhappiness; marked by resentment or cynicism.
The coffee was too bitter.  咖啡太苦了。
He felt bitter about the divorce.  他对离婚感到痛苦。
近义词: acrid, harsh, resentful
反义词: sweet, pleasant, happy
词性: 形容词
