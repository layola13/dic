inexecutable:/ɪnˌɛksɪˈkjuːtəbl/
adjective
不可执行的
Not able to be executed or carried out; not capable of being put into effect.
The code is inexecutable because of syntax errors. 这段代码由于语法错误而无法执行。
The plan was deemed inexecutable due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该计划被认为无法执行。
近义词:unworkable, impracticable, infeasible, unviable
反义词:executable, workable, feasible, viable
词性:adjective

in-/ɪn/  prefix. not, opposite of.  不，相反的
execute/ˈɛksɪˌkjuːt/  verb.  carry out, perform. 执行，完成
-able/əbl/ suffix.  capable of being.  可以…的
