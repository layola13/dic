deserts:/ˈdɛzərts/ (US), /ˈdɛzəts/ (UK)| n. |沙漠；荒漠；应得的惩罚或奖赏 (常用复数)|A large area of land that is very dry, with little or no vegetation, typically covered with sand or rocks. Also can refer to something deserved, whether reward or punishment (usually in plural).|The Sahara is the largest hot desert in the world. 撒哈拉沙漠是世界上最大的热沙漠。|synonyms: wasteland, wilderness, barrenness|antonyms: oasis, paradise, garden|noun

deserts:/dɪˈzɜːts/ (US), /dɪˈzɜːts/ (UK)| vt. |抛弃；离弃；擅离（职守）|To leave someone or something, especially when they need you; to abandon.  Also to leave a place or situation.|He deserted his wife and children. 他抛弃了他的妻子和孩子。|synonyms: abandon, forsake, leave|antonyms: support, stay with, remain loyal to|verb
