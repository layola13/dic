ousted:/ˈaʊstɪd/| v. |驱逐，罢免，革职|to force someone to leave a position of power, job, place, or competition|The president was ousted in a military coup. 总统在军事政变中被赶下台。|Synonyms: removed, expelled, deposed, overthrown, ejected|Antonyms:  reinstated, appointed, elected|verb

ousted:/ˈaʊstɪd/| adj. |被驱逐的，被罢免的|forced out of a position of power, job, place, or competition|The ousted CEO refused to comment on the allegations. 被罢免的首席执行官拒绝评论这些指控。|Synonyms: deposed, former, ex-|Antonyms: current, incumbent|adjective
