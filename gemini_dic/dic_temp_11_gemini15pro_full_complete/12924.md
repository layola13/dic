assyriology:/ˌæsɪriˈɒlədʒi/
n.|亚述学|The study of the language, history, and culture of ancient Assyria.|She is a leading scholar in the field of Assyriology. 她是一位亚述学领域的顶尖学者。|近义词: Near Eastern studies, ancient history|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Assyrian + -ology (study of).  Assyrian comes from Assyria, the ancient Mesopotamian kingdom.  The "-ology" suffix is derived from the Greek -λογία (-logia), meaning "study of," "theory," or "knowledge of."
