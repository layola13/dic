disreputation:/ˌdɪsˌrepjuˈteɪʃən/
n.
坏名声，声名狼藉
The state of being held in low esteem by the public.
His disreputation followed him wherever he went.  他的坏名声如影随形。
synonyms: disgrace, ignominy, ill repute, notoriety, opprobrium, bad name
antonyms: reputation, good name, esteem, honor
noun
