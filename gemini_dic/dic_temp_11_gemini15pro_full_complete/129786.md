non-finite:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪt/ /nɑːnˈfaɪnaɪt/| adj.|非限定的，非限定形式的|A non-finite verb form is not marked for tense or person. It cannot serve as the main verb in an independent clause.  Non-finite verbs include infinitives, participles, and gerunds.|She enjoys reading books. (reading is a non-finite verb, a gerund)|Near synonyms: infinite, unlimited|Antonyms: finite, limited|Grammar
