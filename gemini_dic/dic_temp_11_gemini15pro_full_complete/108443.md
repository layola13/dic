laxative: /ˈlæksətɪv/ | n. adj.|泻药; 通便的|A medicine or food that stimulates bowel movements and relieves constipation; having the effect of stimulating bowel movements.|The doctor prescribed a mild laxative. 医生开了一种温和的泻药。|近义词: purgative, cathartic, aperient |反义词: constipating | noun, adjective
