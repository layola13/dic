labour-saving:/ˈleɪbərˌseɪvɪŋ/| adj.|节省劳力的|Designed to reduce the amount of work needed to do something.|This new labour-saving device will cut your cleaning time in half.  这个新的节省劳动力的设备将使你的清洁时间减半。|Synonyms: efficient, timesaving, automated|Antonyms: labor-intensive, time-consuming|adjective

labour-saving:/ˈleɪbərˌseɪvɪŋ/| n.|节省劳力的设备或方法|A device or method that reduces the amount of work needed.|The washing machine was a great labour-saving invention. 洗衣机是一项伟大的节省劳力的发明。|Synonyms: appliance, gadget, tool|Antonyms: manual labor|noun
