implementing:/ˈɪmplɪmentɪŋ/
v.
实施，执行
putting a plan or system into effect
The company is implementing a new marketing strategy.  公司正在实施新的营销策略。
The government is implementing new regulations to protect the environment. 政府正在实施新的法规来保护环境。
近义词: carrying out, enacting, applying, executing
反义词: abandoning, halting, ceasing
