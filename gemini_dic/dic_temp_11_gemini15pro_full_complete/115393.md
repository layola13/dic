manslaughterous:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərəs/
adj.
过失杀人的；构成过失杀人的
Relating to, or constituting, manslaughter; suggestive of manslaughter.
The driver's manslaughterous actions led to the death of a pedestrian.  司机的过失杀人行为导致一名行人死亡。
The judge described his driving as manslaughterous. 法官称他的驾驶行为是过失杀人的。
近义词:culpable, criminal, unlawful
反义词:lawful, legal, innocent
词性:形容词

词源: From manslaughter + -ous.  来自manslaughter（过失杀人）+ -ous（形容词后缀）。  Manslaughter itself comes from Middle English manslaghter, from man + slaughter.
