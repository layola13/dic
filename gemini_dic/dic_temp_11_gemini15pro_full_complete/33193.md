chromoplast:/ˈkroʊməˌplæst/| n. |色素体|A plastid containing pigments other than chlorophyll, typically responsible for the yellow, orange, or red colors of fruits, flowers, and autumn leaves.|The bright red color of tomatoes is due to chromoplasts. 番茄鲜艳的红色是由于色素体造成的。|Synonyms: plastid, chromatophore|Antonyms: chloroplast, leucoplast|noun
