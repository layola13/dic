outfumble:/ˌaʊtˈfʌmbəl/
verb
失球；因笨拙失球；比…更笨拙地失球
to fumble the ball more often or more awkwardly than
例句：He outfumbled the other running backs and lost the starting job. 他比其他跑锋更容易失球，因此失去了首发位置。

例句：The team outfumbled their opponents and lost the game.  这个队比对手失球更多，输掉了比赛。

近义词： fumble, drop, mishandle
反义词： secure, hold, grasp
词性: verb
