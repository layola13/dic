nonadherence:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪərəns/| n.|不依从，不服从；不坚持|Lack of adherence or compliance, as with a medical regimen, a set of rules, or another expectation.|The patient's nonadherence to the prescribed medication led to a worsening of his condition. 病人的不依从医嘱服药导致病情恶化。|Example:  Nonadherence to the treatment plan can have serious consequences. 不遵守治疗方案可能会造成严重后果。|Synonyms: noncompliance, default, delinquency|Antonyms: adherence, compliance, obedience|noun
