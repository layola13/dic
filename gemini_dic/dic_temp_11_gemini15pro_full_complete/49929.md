denazification:/diːˌnɑːtsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
去纳粹化
The process of removing Nazi influences from German society and politics after World War II.
After World War II, the Allied powers initiated a program of denazification in Germany. 二战后，同盟国在德国启动了一项去纳粹化计划。

The denazification process was complex and controversial. 去纳粹化的过程复杂且充满争议。
Synonym: purging, cleansing
Antonym: Nazification
