helminthes:/hɛlˈmɪnθiːz/
noun
蠕虫类，肠虫
A group of invertebrate animals comprising the parasitic worms, especially the roundworms, flatworms, and thorny-headed worms.
Helminthes infections are common in developing countries.  蠕虫感染在发展中国家很常见。
synonyms: worms, parasites
antonyms: None (No direct antonym for a biological classification)
