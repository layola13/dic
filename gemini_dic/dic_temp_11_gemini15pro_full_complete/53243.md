directors-cuts: /dəˈrɛktərz kʌts/ | n. | 导演剪辑版 | A version of a film, usually longer than the theatrical release, that reflects the director's original vision.  It often includes scenes that were cut for time or other reasons. |  I finally got to see the director's cut of "Blade Runner," and it was amazing. 我终于看到了《银翼杀手》的导演剪辑版，太棒了。 | Example: The director's cut of the film included several deleted scenes that added depth to the story.  这部电影的导演剪辑版包含了几个被删除的场景，增加了故事的深度。| Synonyms:  director's edit, extended version | Antonyms: theatrical cut, abridged version | noun
