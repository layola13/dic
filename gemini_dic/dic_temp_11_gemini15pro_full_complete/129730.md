/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. int. adv.| 什么 |Used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or disbelief.|
What is your name? | 你叫什么名字？
What are you doing? | 你在做什么？
Synonym: which, whatever | Antonym: (No direct antonym for "what")
词性: pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb

/hwʌt/ (Occasionally, especially for emphasis, "what" can be pronounced with an initial /h/ sound)

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she eaten? = 她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday at noon? = 你昨天中午在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for dinner? = 他们晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 到星期五你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years by 2028? = 到2028年，你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
