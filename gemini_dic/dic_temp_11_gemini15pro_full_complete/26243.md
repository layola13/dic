butterweed:/ˈbʌtərˌwiːd/| n.|黄雏菊，猪草|A North American plant (Packera glabella, formerly Senecio glabellus) with yellow flowers, common in wet places and often poisonous to livestock. It is also known as cressleaf groundsel or ragwort.|The farmer was concerned about the butterweed growing in his pasture, as it is toxic to his cattle.|近义词：cressleaf groundsel, ragwort, groundsel|反义词:|noun
