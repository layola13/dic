backers:/ˈbækərz/
n.
支持者，赞助人
People or organizations who provide financial or other support to a project, cause, or individual.
The project's backers include several wealthy philanthropists.  这个项目的支持者包括几位富有的慈善家。

近义词:supporters, patrons, sponsors, investors
反义词:opponents, detractors

词性:名词


backers:/ˈbækərz/
n.
(尤指赌博中的)下注者
A person who places a bet on something.
The backers cheered as their horse crossed the finish line. 下注者们在他们的马冲过终点线时欢呼起来。

近义词:bettors, gamblers, punters
反义词:bookmakers


词源: From "back" (v.) meaning to support + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).  源自动词“back”（支持）+后缀“-er”（构成施动者名词）。
