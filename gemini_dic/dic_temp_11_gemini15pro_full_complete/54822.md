disperses:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪz/
verb
分散，散开
to spread across or move away over a large area, or to make something do this
The crowd dispersed after the concert. 音乐会结束后人群散开了。
近义词:scatter, disseminate, distribute
反义词:gather, collect, assemble
词性:verb

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
verb
分散，散开
to spread across or move away over a large area, or to make something do this

近义词:scatter, disseminate, distribute

词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere, from dis- "apart" + spargere "to scatter".
