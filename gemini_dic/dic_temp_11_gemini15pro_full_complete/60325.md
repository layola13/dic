egohood:/ˈiːɡoʊhʊd/
noun
自我邻域；以自我为中心的邻域
A neighborhood defined not by geographic boundaries but by the places one frequents and the people one interacts with regularly, essentially a personalized map of one's urban experience.
My egohood encompasses the coffee shop where I work, the gym I go to, and the park where I walk my dog. 我的自我邻域包括我工作的咖啡店、我去的健身房和我遛狗的公园。
near synonyms: personal space, activity space, life-world
antonyms: neighborhood (in the traditional sense), community (in the geographical sense)
n.
