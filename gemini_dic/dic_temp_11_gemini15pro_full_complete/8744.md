antidesiccant:/ˌæntiˈdesɪkənt/ | adj. | 抗干燥的，防干的 | Having the property of preventing drying or absorbing moisture. |  The antidesiccant properties of the cream kept my skin hydrated.  这款面霜的抗干燥特性使我的皮肤保持水润。| Synonyms: moisture-retaining, hydrating | Antonyms: drying, dehydrating | adjective
