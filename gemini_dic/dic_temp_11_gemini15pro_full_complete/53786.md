discomposed:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzd/ | adj. |  不安的，扰乱的，慌张的 |  Disturbed, upset, or agitated; lacking composure. | The sudden noise discomposed the speaker. 突如其来的噪音使演讲者感到不安。 |  The news of her brother's accident left her completely discomposed.  她哥哥出车祸的消息让她完全慌了神。 | 近义词: disturbed, upset, agitated, ruffled, flustered | 反义词: calm, composed, serene, tranquil | 词性: adjective

discompose:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊz/ | v. | 使不安，扰乱，使慌张 | To disturb the composure of; upset or agitate. | The unexpected question discomposed him.  这个意外的问题让他感到不安。| The sight of the blood discomposed her.  看到血让她感到慌乱。 | 近义词: disturb, upset, agitate, ruffle, flustered | 反义词: calm, compose, soothe, tranquilize | 词性: verb


词源:  From the French *descomposer*, from des- (expressing reversal) + composer "to compose."  源自法语 *descomposer*，由 des-（表示反转）+ composer “组成” 组成。
