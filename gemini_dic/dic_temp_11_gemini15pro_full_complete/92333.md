hypnotizing:/ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|催眠的，迷人的|Having the power to hypnotize; mesmerizing.|Her voice was soft and hypnotizing. 她的声音轻柔而迷人。|近义词:mesmerizing, captivating, enthralling, spellbinding|反义词:boring, tedious, dull|词性:形容词

hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪz/| vt.|催眠，使着迷|To put someone in a state of hypnosis; to fascinate or enthrall.|The magician hypnotized the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法迷住了观众。|近义词:mesmerize, captivate, enthrall|反义词:bore, tire|词性:动词

词源: From French *hypnotiser*, from *hypnose* "hypnosis" + *-iser* "-ize".  Ultimately from Greek *hypnos* "sleep".
