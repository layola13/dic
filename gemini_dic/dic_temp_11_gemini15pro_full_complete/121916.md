misses:/ˈmɪsɪz/| n. v. (third-person singular simple present)|小姐（用于未婚女性）、错过，想念|Misses is the plural of miss (as a title) or the third-person singular present form of the verb "miss".|She misses her family when she's away.  她不在家时很想念家人。|近义词：(for the verb) longs for, yearns for, lacks|反义词：(for the verb) hits, finds, meets|词性：noun, verb

misses:/ˈmɪsɪz/| n. |女士们（复数，用于称呼多位未婚女性）|Misses is the plural form of Miss, used to address or refer to more than one unmarried woman.|The two misses went shopping together.  两位小姐一起去购物了。|近义词: Ms. (plural: Mses.), ladies|反义词: Messrs. (for men)|词性: noun
