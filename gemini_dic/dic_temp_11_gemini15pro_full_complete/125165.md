multibillionaires:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪljəˌnɛrz/ | n. | 亿万富翁（复数） | People who have a net worth of multiple billions of dollars. |  The world's multibillionaires control a vast amount of wealth. 世界上的亿万富翁控制着巨额财富。| synonyms: billionaires, tycoons, magnates | antonyms: paupers, the poor | noun


multibillionaire:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪljəˌnɛr/ | n. | 亿万富翁（单数） | A person who has a net worth of multiple billions of dollars. | He's a multibillionaire who made his fortune in tech. 他是一位在科技领域发了财的亿万富翁。 | synonyms: billionaire, tycoon, magnate | antonyms: pauper, the poor | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
multi- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "many," "much," "multiple."
billion: from French billion, ultimately from Latin bis "twice" + million.
-aire (suffix):  from French -aire, indicating a person connected with something.
