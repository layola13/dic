brookable:/ˈbrʊkəbl/
adjective
可容忍的，可忍受的
able to be tolerated or allowed; bearable.
Her constant complaining was barely brookable. 她不停的抱怨几乎让人无法忍受。
近义词:tolerable, bearable, acceptable, endurable
反义词:intolerable, unbearable, unacceptable, unendurable
词性:adjective
词源:From brook (v.) + -able.  Brook meaning "to tolerate" comes from Old English *brucan "to use, enjoy, have the benefit of," from Proto-Germanic *brukanan "to make use of," from PIE root *bhrug- "to enjoy."
