left-wingers:/ˌleftˈwɪŋərz/
noun
左翼人士；左派
People who support left-wing political parties or hold left-wing political views, typically advocating social equality, government intervention in the economy, and progressive social policies.
The left-wingers in the party advocated for higher taxes on the wealthy.  该党内的左翼人士主张对富人增税。
The left-wingers organized a protest against the proposed cuts to social programs.  左翼人士组织了一场抗议削减社会福利项目的示威活动。
synonyms: liberals, progressives, socialists, radicals
antonyms: right-wingers, conservatives, reactionaries
