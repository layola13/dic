outrushes:/ˈaʊtrʌʃɪz/
noun
涌出；冲出；迸发
A sudden rapid flow or bursting forth.
例句：The outrushes of lava from the volcano were spectacular. 火山熔岩的喷涌景象蔚为壮观。

例句：There was an outrush of reporters as the president emerged. 总统出现时，记者们蜂拥而出。
synonym: surge, eruption, outburst, gush, flood
antonym: influx, inflow, trickle
词性: 名词 (n.)
