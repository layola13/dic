allusively:/əˈluːsɪvli/
|adv.|暗示地；引喻地；含蓄地|In a way that refers to something indirectly or by suggestion.|He smiled allusively, leaving her to wonder what he meant. 他意味深长地笑了笑，让她琢磨不透他的意思。|Synonyms: indirectly, implicitly, suggestively, obliquely|Antonyms: directly, explicitly, plainly, clearly|词性: 副词

allusive:/əˈluːsɪv/
|adj.|暗指的；引喻的；含蓄的|Containing or characterized by indirect references.|The poem is allusive and requires a deep understanding of mythology. 这首诗充满典故，需要对神话有深入的了解。|Synonyms: indirect, implicit, suggestive, oblique|Antonyms: direct, explicit, plain, clear|词性: 形容词

allusion:/əˈluːʒən/
|n.|典故；暗指；暗示|An expression designed to call something to mind without mentioning it explicitly; an indirect or passing reference.|The lyrics contain several allusions to classical literature. 歌词中包含了几个对古典文学的典故。|Synonyms: reference, intimation, suggestion, hint|Antonyms: statement, declaration, specification|词性: 名词
