outclasses:/ˌaʊtˈklæsɪz/
verb
胜过，优于
to be much better than someone or something
例句：The new model completely outclasses all its rivals.  这款新模型完全胜过所有竞争对手。

例句：He was completely outclassed by his opponent. 他被对手彻底击败了。
近义词：surpass, excel, outstrip, outdo, outperform
反义词：be inferior to, be outclassed by
词性：v.
