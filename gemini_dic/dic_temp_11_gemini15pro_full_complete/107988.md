laska:/ˈlæskə/ | n. | 亲爱的，宝贝; (捷克语)鼬鼠 |A term of endearment, similar to "darling" or "sweetheart"; also the Czech word for "weasel". |例句：He called her his laska. 他叫她亲爱的。

例句：V lese žije laska. (Czech) A weasel lives in the forest. 森林里住着一只鼬鼠。 |近义词：darling, sweetheart, honey, love (as terms of endearment); weasel, stoat (for the animal) |反义词: enemy, foe (as terms of endearment - context dependent) |词性:noun
