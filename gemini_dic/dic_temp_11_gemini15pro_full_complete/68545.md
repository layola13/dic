fanega:/fɑːnˈeɪɡə/ | n. | 法内加（西班牙等国的谷物和土地面积单位） | A unit of dry measure, varying in size from region to region, used especially in Spain and Spanish America for grain and also as a unit of land area. |  Example 1: The farmer harvested several fanegas of wheat this year. 今年农民收获了几法内加的小麦。

Example 2: The land he owned was measured in fanegas. 他拥有的土地是用法内加来衡量的。 | 近义词: bushel,  hectoliter (approximate equivalents depending on the region)| 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: Spanish, from Arabic *fanīqa*, from Greek *phoníkē*, "Phoenician (measure)."
