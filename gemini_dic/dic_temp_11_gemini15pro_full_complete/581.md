abounding:/əˈbaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
丰富的；大量的
present participle of abound
例句：
The river is abounding in/with fish.
这条河里鱼很多。

The garden was abounding with flowers.
花园里开满了鲜花。

近义词：teeming, overflowing, plentiful, copious, rife
反义词：scarce, lacking, deficient, meager
词性：adjective
