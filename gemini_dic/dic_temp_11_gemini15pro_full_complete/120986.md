miscitation: /ˌmɪsaɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
错误引用；误引
the act of quoting something incorrectly.
The essay was full of miscitations and factual errors.  这篇文章充满了错误的引用和事实错误。
He apologized for the miscitation in his previous article. 他为他之前文章中的错误引用道歉。
misquotation, incorrect citation
accurate citation
n.
