authorised:/ˈɔːθəraɪzd/ (UK) /ˈɔːθəˌraɪzd/ (US)| adj.|授权的，经批准的|Officially approved or allowed; given permission.|The authorised biography of the president was published last year.  去年出版了总统的授权传记。|近义词：approved, sanctioned, licensed, permitted|反义词：unauthorised, forbidden, prohibited|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "authorise," which comes from Old French "autoriser" and ultimately from Latin "auctorizare," meaning "to give authority to."
