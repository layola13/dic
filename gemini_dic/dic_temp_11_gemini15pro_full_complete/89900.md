horrified:/ˈhɒrɪfaɪd/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrɪfaɪd/ (US)| adj.|感到恐惧的，惊骇的|Filled with horror; extremely shocked and disgusted.|例句：I was horrified by the news of the accident. 我对事故的消息感到震惊。|例句：She was horrified to see the state of the house. 看到房子的状况，她吓坏了。|近义词：shocked, appalled, terrified, aghast|反义词：calm, unperturbed, delighted|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "horrify," which comes from the Latin "horrere" (to bristle, shudder) + "-fy" (to make).
