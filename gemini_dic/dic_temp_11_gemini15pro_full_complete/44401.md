cross-handled:/ˌkrɒsˈhændəld/ /ˌkrɑːsˈhændəld/| adj.|十字柄的，交叉柄的|Having handles that cross each other, typically referring to faucets or valves where the handles are arranged perpendicularly for easier control of hot and cold water.|The old bathtub had cross-handled faucets.|近义词:X-shaped, crossed|反义词:single-handled, straight-handled|词性:形容词

cross-handle:/ˌkrɒsˈhændəl/ /ˌkrɑːsˈhændəl/| v. |交叉处理，互相干扰|To interfere with or obstruct each other, often unintentionally; to create complications or difficulties through conflicting actions or interests.|Their conflicting instructions cross-handled the project and caused delays.|近义词:interfere, obstruct, hinder, complicate|反义词:facilitate, aid, assist|词性:动词
