embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| vt.|禁止，禁运|To officially ban trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The government embargoed all imports from that nation.|近义词：ban, prohibit, restrict, sanction|反义词:allow, permit|动词
