nectaried:/ˈnɛktərid/
adjective
充满花蜜的; 甜蜜的
Containing or yielding nectar; sweet.
The nectaried blossoms attracted hummingbirds. 充满花蜜的花朵吸引了蜂鸟。
近义词:honeyed, sweet, sugary
反义词:bitter, sour
词性:adjective

词源:
From nectar + -ed.  Nectar comes from Latin nectar, the drink of the gods, from Greek nektar.
