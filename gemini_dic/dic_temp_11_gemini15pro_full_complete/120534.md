mipgen:/ˈmɪpˌdʒɛn/ | n. |  Mipmap 生成器; 多级纹理生成器 | A tool or algorithm used to generate mipmaps. Mipmaps are pre-calculated, optimized sequences of images, each of which is a progressively lower resolution representation of the same image. They are used in 3D computer graphics to improve rendering speed and reduce aliasing artifacts. |  The game engine uses a mipgen to create mipmaps for all textures. 游戏引擎使用 mipgen 为所有纹理创建 mipmap。 | synonyms: mipmap generator, texture LOD generator | antonyms: N/A | noun
