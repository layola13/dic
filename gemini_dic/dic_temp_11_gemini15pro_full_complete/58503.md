duelled:/ˈdjuːəld/ (US) /ˈdʒuːəld/ (UK)| v.|决斗(ed)|fought a duel; engaged in a formal combat with someone. |The two knights duelled at dawn. 两位骑士在黎明决斗。|例句：They duelled for hours until one finally yielded. 他们决斗了几个小时，直到一方最终屈服。|近义词：fought, clashed, contended, competed|反义词: cooperated, yielded|词性:动词

词源: From the Old French "duel" (modern French "duel"), from the Latin "duellum" meaning "war," originally "war between two."
