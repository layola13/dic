linden:/ˈlɪndən/ (US), /ˈlaɪndən/ (UK)| n. |椴树；菩提树|A deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish flowers.  The wood is soft and easily carved.|The linden tree in the park provides welcome shade on a hot day. 公园里的椴树在炎热的天气里提供了受欢迎的阴凉。|Synonyms: lime tree, basswood|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

linden:/ˈlɪndən/ (US), /ˈlaɪndən/ (UK)| adj. |椴树的；菩提树的|Made from, or relating to, the linden tree.|Linden wood is often used for carving. 椴木经常用于雕刻。|Synonyms: lime, basswood|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
