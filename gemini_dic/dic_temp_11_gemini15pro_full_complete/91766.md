hyperemphasize:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | v. | 过分强调；过分重视 | To emphasize something excessively or to an unnecessary degree. |  The media tends to hyperemphasize the negative aspects of the news. 媒体倾向于过分强调新闻的负面。

The politician hyperemphasized the importance of his new policy.  这位政治家过分强调了他的新政策的重要性。


| 近义词: overemphasize, overstress, exaggerate | 反义词: downplay, understate, minimize | 词性: verb
