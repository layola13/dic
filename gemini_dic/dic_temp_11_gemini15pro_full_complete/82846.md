gulfstream: /ˈɡʌlfˌstriːm/ | n. | 墨西哥湾暖流，湾流 | A warm ocean current that flows from the Gulf of Mexico northward through the Atlantic Ocean. | The Gulf Stream keeps the climate of Europe warmer than it would otherwise be. 墨西哥湾暖流使得欧洲的气候比其原本应有的气候更温暖。

The Gulf Stream is a powerful and important ocean current.  墨西哥湾暖流是一股强大而重要的洋流。


近义词: current | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑cold current 寒流) | 词性: noun
