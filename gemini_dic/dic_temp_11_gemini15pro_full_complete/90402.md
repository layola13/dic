howlingly:/ˈhaʊlɪŋli/| adv.| 极度地，非常地，极其可笑地 |  In a way that suggests a howl; extremely; hilariously. | The plan failed howlingly.  这个计划彻底失败了。|  The comedian's jokes were howlingly funny.  这位喜剧演员的笑话滑稽极了。| 近义词: extremely, exceedingly, intensely, ridiculously, hilariously | 反义词: slightly, mildly, moderately | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "howl" (v.) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Howl" comes from Old English "howlian,"  of imitative origin.
