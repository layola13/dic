impassionable:/ɪmˈpæʃənəbl/ | adj. | 冷静的，不易动感情的 | Not easily aroused to emotion; impassive; calm. |  He delivered the news with an impassionable face, betraying no emotion. 他面无表情地宣布了这个消息，没有流露出任何情感。

|例句：
The judge listened to the testimony with an impassionable expression.  法官面无表情地听着证词。

Her impassionable demeanor made it difficult to know what she was thinking. 她冷静的举止让人难以捉摸她的想法。
|近义词：unemotional, apathetic, stoic, indifferent, phlegmatic, dispassionate |反义词：passionate, emotional, ardent, fervent, excitable |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impassionabilis, from in- "not" + passionabilis "capable of feeling," from Latin passio "suffering, feeling."  Ultimately from Latin pati "to suffer, endure, experience."
