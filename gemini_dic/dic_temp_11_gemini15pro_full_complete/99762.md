intermediaries:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiˌɛriz/| n.|中间人，调解人，媒介|A person who acts as a link between people or things; a mediator.|例句：The intermediaries helped to negotiate a peace treaty. 中间人帮助谈判达成了和平条约。

例句：Real estate agents act as intermediaries between buyers and sellers. 房地产经纪人在买卖双方之间充当中间人。|近义词：mediators, negotiators, agents, brokers, go-betweens|反义词：principals, direct parties|词性:Noun
