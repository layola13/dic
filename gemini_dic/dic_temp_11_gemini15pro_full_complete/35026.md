clootie:/ˈkluːti/| n.|破布；左脚；魔鬼|A piece of cloth, especially a rag or scrap; (Scottish) the left foot; (Scottish) the devil.|He tripped over an old clootie on the floor. 他被地板上的一块破布绊倒了。|近义词：rag, scrap, cloth (for the first meaning); devil, Satan (for the third meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the first meaning); God, angel (for the third meaning)|词性:noun

clootie:/ˈkluːti/| adj.|用布包裹的|Covered or wrapped in cloth.|A clootie dumpling is a traditional Scottish dessert. 布丁饺子是一种传统的苏格兰甜点。|近义词：cloth-covered, wrapped|反义词:uncovered, unwrapped|词性:adjective
