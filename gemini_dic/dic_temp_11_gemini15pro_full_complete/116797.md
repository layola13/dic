mawkish:/ˈmɔːkɪʃ/| adj.|多愁善感的；自作多情的；令人作呕的|Excessively sentimental to the point of being sickening or insipid. Characterized by sickly sentimentality or shallow emotion.|例句：The film was so mawkish I almost walked out.  这部电影太伤感了，我差点就走了。

例句：His mawkish poetry made me cringe.  他那矫揉造作的诗让我感到难堪。|近义词：sentimental, cloying, saccharine, maudlin|反义词：unsentimental, dry, cynical|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From obsolete *mawke "maggot" (from Old Norse mathkr "maggot"), + -ish. The original sense was "sickly, nauseated," later evolving to "oversweet, cloying," and finally to its current meaning of "excessively sentimental."
