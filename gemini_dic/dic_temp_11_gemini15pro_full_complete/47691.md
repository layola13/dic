debunk:/diːˈbʌŋk/ | v. | 揭穿；驳斥；暴露…的真相 | to expose the falseness or exaggeration of (a claim, belief, etc.) |  Scientists have debunked the myth that eating fat makes you fat. 科学家已经揭穿了吃脂肪会让人发胖的谬论。 | synonyms: expose, disprove, refute, discredit | antonyms: confirm, prove, validate | verb

debunk:/diːˈbʌŋk/ | v. | 揭穿；驳斥；暴露…的真相 | to show that something is not true | The article debunks the notion that money buys happiness. 这篇文章驳斥了金钱能买到幸福的观点。 | synonyms: explode, demystify | antonyms: substantiate, corroborate | verb
