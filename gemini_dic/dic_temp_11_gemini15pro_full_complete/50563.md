deputizing: /ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 代理；委任…为副手 | acting or serving as a deputy |  The manager is deputizing for the director during his absence. 经理在主管不在期间代理他的职务。| synonyms: substituting, acting for, standing in, covering for | antonyms:  leading, heading | verb

deputize: /ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪz/ | v. | 代理；委任…为副手 | to appoint someone to act as a deputy | I've deputized my assistant to run the meeting. 我已委派我的助手主持会议。| synonyms: appoint, delegate, commission, authorize | antonyms: dismiss, discharge | verb
