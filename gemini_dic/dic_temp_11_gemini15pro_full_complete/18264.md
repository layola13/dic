belime: /bɪˈlaɪm/
verb
污蔑，诽谤
to falsely and maliciously accuse; slander
例句：He belimed his rival to gain an advantage in the election. 他诽谤他的对手以在选举中获得优势。

例句：She was belimed by her co-workers, who spread rumors about her personal life.  她的同事诽谤她，散布关于她私生活的谣言。

近义词：slander, defame, libel, smear, calumniate
反义词：praise, commend, extol, laud
词性：v.
