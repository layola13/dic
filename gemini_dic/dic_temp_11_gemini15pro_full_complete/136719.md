nonsenses:/ˈnɒnsənsɪz/ /ˈnɑːnsɛnsɪz/| n.|胡说，废话|Speech or writing that is silly or meaningless. |That's absolute nonsense!  那纯粹是胡说八道！|synonyms: rubbish, balderdash, twaddle, gibberish, claptrap, drivel, hogwash, bunk, bullshit, piffle|antonyms: sense, truth, fact|noun

Etymology:  From Old French *nonsens "absurdity," from non "not" (from Latin non) + sens "sense, meaning" (from Latin sensus).
