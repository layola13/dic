back-to-back:/ˌbæk tə ˈbæk/ | adj. adv.| 背靠背的，连续的 | Occurring or done in immediate succession. | We had back-to-back meetings all day. 我们整天都在开连续的会议。| The team played back-to-back games last weekend.  该队上周末进行了背靠背的比赛。| consecutive, successive, continuous | alternating, intermittent | adjective, adverb

back-to-back:/ˌbæk tə ˈbæk/ | n.| 背靠背的比赛/事件 | Two games, events, etc., that occur on consecutive days. |  Playing three back-to-backs is tough on the players.  打三场背靠背的比赛对球员来说很艰难。| The conference featured several back-to-backs on cybersecurity.  这次会议有几个关于网络安全的背靠背会议。| consecutive games/events, doubleheader | separate events | noun
