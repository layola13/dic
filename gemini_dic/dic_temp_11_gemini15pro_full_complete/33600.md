cimmerian:/sɪˈmɪəriən/| n.|居住在黑暗中的人|A member of a mythical people described by Homer as living in a land of perpetual darkness.|Ancient Greeks believed Cimmerians lived in a land of perpetual twilight. 古希腊人认为辛梅里安人生活在一个永久的暮色之地。| Legends speak of the mysterious Cimmerians and their gloomy kingdom.  传说讲述了神秘的辛梅里安人及其阴暗的王国。|近义词： troglodyte, cave dweller|反义词：-|词性:名词
