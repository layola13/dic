irreparably:/ɪrˈɛpərəbli/
adverb
不能挽回地，无法弥补地
in a way that is impossible to repair or put right.
The vase was irreparably damaged when it fell.  这个花瓶摔落后彻底损坏了。
The bridge was irreparably damaged by the flood. 这座桥被洪水彻底摧毁了。
近义词:permanently, irretrievably, beyond repair
反义词:repairably, fixable
词性:副词
