frowningly:/ˈfraʊnɪŋli/ | adv. | 皱眉地，不悦地 | In a way that shows you are frowning, because you are annoyed, worried, or thinking hard. | She looked at him frowningly. 她皱着眉头看着他。|Synonyms: disapprovingly, sullenly, crossly | Antonyms: smilingly, cheerfully, happily | adverb

frown:/fraʊn/ | v. n. | 皱眉，蹙额 | To make a serious, angry or worried expression by bringing your eyebrows closer together so that lines appear on your forehead. | He frowned as he read the letter. 他读信时皱起了眉头。| Synonyms: scowl, glare, grimace | Antonyms: smile, grin, beam | verb, noun
