nonretroactivity:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊækˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊækˈtɪvɪti/
n.|不溯既往；非追溯性|The principle of nonretroactivity ensures that laws are not applied to past actions. 不溯既往原则确保法律不适用于过去的行为。|
The court's decision had nonretroactivity, affecting only future cases. 法院的裁决不溯及既往，只影响未来的案件。|
近义词:irretroactivity, prospectivity|反义词:retroactivity, retrospectivity|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
retro- (prefix): from Latin retro "backward, behind"
act: from Latin actus "a doing, a driving, an impulse," past participle of agere "to set in motion, drive, lead, do, act"
-ivity (suffix): from Latin -ivitas, related to -ivus "-ive"
