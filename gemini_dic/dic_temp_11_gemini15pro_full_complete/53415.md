disannex:/ˌdɪsəˈnɛks/
verb
取消兼并；脱离；分离
To separate (territory) from a country, state, etc.
The city voted to disannex the surrounding suburbs. 该市投票决定将周围的郊区分离出去。
synonyms: detach, separate, disconnect, cede
antonyms: annex, append, attach, unite
词性: 动词


disannex:/ˌdɪsəˈnɛks/
noun
脱离；分离
The act of separating territory.
The disannexation of the territory caused much controversy.  该领土的脱离引起了很多争议。
synonyms: separation, detachment, disconnection
antonyms: annexation,  union, attachment
词性: 名词
