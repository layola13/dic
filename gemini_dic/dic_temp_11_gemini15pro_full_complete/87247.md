hereunto:/ˌhɪərʌnˈtuː/
adv.
至此，于此
to this document or matter; attached to this
例句：We, the undersigned, have hereunto set our hands and seals. 我们，签署人，已在此签名盖章。

例句：The parties hereunto agree to the following terms and conditions.  双方在此同意以下条款和条件。

近义词：herewith, attached, appended
反义词： (No direct antonym exists, as it relates to document attachment)
词性：副词
