drawdown:/ˈdrɔːdaʊn/| n.|下降; 减少; 提取;  回撤|A drawdown is a reduction in the water level of a reservoir or lake. 回撤是指水库或湖泊水位的下降。|例句：The prolonged drought caused a significant drawdown of the reservoir. 长时间的干旱导致水库水位显著下降。


例句：The company experienced a drawdown in its cash reserves due to the economic downturn. 由于经济低迷，该公司现金储备减少了。|近义词：reduction, decrease, decline, withdrawal|反义词:increase, buildup, accretion|词性:名词

词源:  From "draw down,"  meaning to pull or lower something.  The noun form signifies the result of this action, a lowering or reduction.  Comes from the Old English "dragan" (to draw).
