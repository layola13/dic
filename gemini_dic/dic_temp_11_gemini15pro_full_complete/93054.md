idolatries: /aɪˈdɑlətriz/ | n. | 偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜 | The worship of idols; excessive admiration or devotion to something. |  Many ancient cultures practiced idolatries. 许多古代文化都有偶像崇拜的习俗。|  The fans' idolatry of the pop star was astounding.  歌迷对这位流行歌星的狂热崇拜令人震惊。 | synonyms:  worship, adoration, reverence, devotion, deification | antonyms:  disdain, contempt, irreverence, atheism | noun


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *idolatri(e), from Old French idolatrie, from Late Latin idolatria, from Greek eidololatreia, from eidolon (“image, idol”) + latreia (“worship”).  Ultimately from the Greek eidos (“form, shape”).
