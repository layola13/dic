fasting:/ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 禁食，斋戒 |  The act of abstaining from all or some kinds of food or drink, especially as a religious observance. |  She observed a strict fasting during Ramadan.  她在斋月期间严格禁食。 | synonyms: abstinence, abstention | antonyms: feasting, gorging | noun

fasting:/ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 禁食的，斋戒的| Relating to or involving fasting. |  The fasting month of Ramadan is a time for reflection.  斋月是一个反思的月份。 | synonyms: abstinent | antonyms: feasting | adjective
