folliculated:/fɒˈlɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  有小囊的，有滤泡的 | Covered with, or having, follicles.  | The hen's ovary was folliculated, indicating she was ready to lay eggs. 母鸡的卵巢有滤泡，表明它准备下蛋。 |  近义词: follicular, vesicular | 反义词: smooth, non-follicular | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "folliculus" (small bag, little sack) + "-ated" (forming adjectives meaning "having" or "provided with").
