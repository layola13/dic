desist:/dɪˈzɪst/ /dɪˈsɪst/| v. |停止，终止|to stop doing something|The company was ordered to desist from using the misleading advertisement.  该公司被勒令停止使用误导性广告。

The judge told him to desist from making further comments. 法官告诉他停止发表进一步评论。|近义词：cease, stop, discontinue, abstain, refrain|反义词：continue, persist, persevere|词性:动词
