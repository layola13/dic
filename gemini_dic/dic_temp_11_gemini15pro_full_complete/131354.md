nonchivalrousness:/ˌnɒnˈʃɪvəlrəsnəs/ | n. | 不骑士精神；缺乏绅士风度 | Lack of chivalrous behavior; discourtesy, especially towards women. | His nonchivalrousness was evident when he refused to hold the door open for her.  他的不骑士精神在他拒绝为她扶门时显而易见。 | 近义词:rudeness, discourtesy, ungentlemanliness | 反义词: chivalry, gallantry, courtesy | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + chivalrous (adjective meaning "courteous and gallant, especially toward women") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Old French chevaler ("knight").
