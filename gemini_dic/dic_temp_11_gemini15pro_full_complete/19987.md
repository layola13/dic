biograph: /ˈbaɪəɡræf/ | n. | 传记影片；电影放映机 | A motion picture that presents a biographical account of a person's life.  A device for projecting motion pictures. |  Example: The biograph of Abraham Lincoln was very informative.  亚伯拉罕·林肯的传记电影非常翔实。

Example: The old biograph flickered to life, projecting images onto the sheet.  老式电影放映机闪烁着生命，将图像投射到屏幕上。 | 近义词: biography film, biographical film, motion picture projector | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From bio- (life) + -graph (writing).  源自 bio-（生命）+ -graph（书写）。
