amongst:/əˈmʌŋst/ | prep. |在…之中；在…之间|Being situated within a group of things or people. |例句：He distributed the candy amongst the children. 他把糖果分给了孩子们。|例句：The decision was made amongst the members of the committee.  这个决定是由委员会成员共同作出的。| 近义词：among, amid, between, betwixt | 反义词: outside, beyond | 词性:介词

词源:
Middle English: variant of among, influenced by against.
