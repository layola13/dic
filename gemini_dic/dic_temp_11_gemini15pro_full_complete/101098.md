inversely:/ɪnˈvɜːrsli/ | adv. | 相反地，反之 | in an inverse manner; in reverse order or proportion |  The cost of living rises inversely to the value of the dollar. 生活费用与美元价值成反比。 | inversely proportional to | directly | adverb
Etymology->
From Late Latin *inversē, from Latin inversus (“inverted, turned about”), past participle of invertere (“to turn about, invert”). Equivalent to in- +‎ verse +‎ -ly.
