moghul:/ˈmoʊɡʌl/| n.|莫卧儿，大亨|A member of the Muslim dynasty of Mongol origin which ruled most of India from the 16th to the 19th century. Also used to refer to a powerful or influential person, especially in business or entertainment. |Example 1: The Moghul emperors built magnificent palaces and tombs. 莫卧儿皇帝建造了宏伟的宫殿和陵墓。

Example 2: He's considered a moghul in the film industry. 他被认为是电影界的大亨。|近义词：magnate, tycoon, emperor, ruler|反义词:pauper, commoner|词性:noun
