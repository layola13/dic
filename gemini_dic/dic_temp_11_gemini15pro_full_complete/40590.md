convalescence: /ˌkɒnvəˈles(ə)ns/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnvəˈles(ə)ns/ (US) | n. | 康复期；恢复期 |The period of gradual recovery of health and strength after illness or injury.  The time during which one recovers from an illness. |  After her surgery, she needed a period of convalescence.  她手术后需要一段时间的康复。| synonyms: recovery, recuperation, rehabilitation | antonyms: relapse, deterioration | noun

convalescences: /ˌkɒnvəˈles(ə)nsiz/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnvəˈles(ə)nsiz/ (US) | n. | 康复期；恢复期（复数形式，较少使用）| Periods of recovery after illnesses or injuries.  Multiple instances of the time during which one recovers from an illness. | The hospital specializes in convalescences after major surgeries. 这家医院专门从事大手术后的康复。 | synonyms: recoveries, recuperations, rehabilitations | antonyms: relapses, deteriorations | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French convalescence, from late Latin convalescentia, from convalescere ‘grow strong’, from con- (expressing intensive force) + valescere ‘become strong’.
