genethliatic:/ˌdʒɛnəˈθlɪætɪk/
adjective
算命的，占星的，关于出生的
Relating to or based on the position of the stars at one's birth; astrological.
Genethliatic astrology is a system of astrology based on the moment of birth.  基因占星学是一种基于出生时刻的占星系统。
Ancient astrologers used genethliatic charts to predict the future. 古代占星家使用出生星盘来预测未来。
近义词: astrological, natal
反义词: None
词性: adjective


词源:
From Greek *genethliakos*, from *genethlios* 'of or relating to birth', from *genesis* 'birth'.
