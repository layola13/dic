outlearned:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrnd/
|v.|超越，在学习上胜过|To surpass someone in learning or skill, especially through experience or practice.|The young apprentice quickly outlearned his master.  年轻的学徒很快就在学习上超过了他的师傅。|Synonyms: surpassed, eclipsed, exceeded|Antonyms: lagged behind, fell behind|verb

outlearned:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrnd/
|v.|学得比...多|To learn more than someone else, gaining a competitive edge.|She outlearned her competitors and secured the top position. 她学得比她的竞争对手多，并获得了最高职位。|Synonyms: outstripped, outmatched|Antonyms: underperformed, lagged|verb
