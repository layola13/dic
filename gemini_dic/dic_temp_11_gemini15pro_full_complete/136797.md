nonseverances:/ˌnɒnsɛˈvərənsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnsɛˈvɛrənsɪz/
noun
非割裂; 非分离;  不分割; (尤指)不遣散
The absence of severances, especially the continued employment of workers.  It implies a commitment to retaining employees, often in the context of mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.
The company's policy of nonseverances during the merger eased employee anxieties.  公司在合并期间的非遣散政策缓解了员工的焦虑。
Retention, continuation,  non-separation
Severance, dismissal, layoff
n.
