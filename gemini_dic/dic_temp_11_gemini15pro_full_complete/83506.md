hacks:/hæks/
|n.|技巧，诀窍；黑客行为；出租马车|A clever trick or shortcut; an act of gaining unauthorized access to a computer system; a horse-drawn carriage for hire|She has some useful hacks for cleaning the kitchen quickly. 她有一些快速清洁厨房的有用技巧。|
| |synonyms: tricks, tips, shortcuts, cheats, exploits (for computer hacks)|antonyms:  (For computer hacks) security measures, protections|noun|

hacks:/hæks/
|v. (third-person singular simple present hacks, present participle hacking, simple past and past participle hacked)|砍，劈；非法入侵（电脑系统）；难以胜任|To cut or chop roughly; to gain unauthorized access to a computer system; to manage or cope with something (often with difficulty)|He hacked the branch off the tree. 他把树枝砍了下来。|
| |synonyms: chop, cut, gash, breach, infiltrate (for computer hacking), cope, manage|antonyms: protect, secure (for computer hacking), fail|verb|
