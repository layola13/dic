nonintrovertedly:/ˌnɑːnɪntrəˈvɜːrtɪdli/
adverb
非内向地；外向地
in a non-introverted manner; not shyly or reservedly.
She spoke nonintrovertedly at the meeting, surprising her colleagues. 她在外向地在会议上发言，令她的同事们感到惊讶。
He presented his ideas nonintrovertedly, engaging the audience. 他外向地表达了自己的想法，吸引了观众。
近义词:extrovertedly, outgoingly, sociably
反义词:introvertedly, shyly, reservedly
词性:adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
introverted (adjective):  from Latin intro- "inward" + vertere "to turn"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
