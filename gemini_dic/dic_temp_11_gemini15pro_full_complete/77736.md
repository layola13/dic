gen:/dʒɛn/ | n. | generation; gene | A generation is all the people born and living at about the same time, regarded collectively. A gene is the basic physical and functional unit of heredity. | Example 1: The younger gen doesn't understand how we lived without the internet. 年轻一代不理解我们没有互联网是怎么生活的。

Example 2: Scientists are studying the gen responsible for eye color. 科学家正在研究负责眼睛颜色的基因。 | Synonym: generation (for gen meaning generation), DNA, chromosome (for gen meaning gene) | Antonym: None | Noun
