consolidation:/kənˌsɒlɪˈdeɪʃn/ | n. |巩固，合并，团结|The consolidation of several small companies into one large corporation.  几家小公司合并成一家大公司。|例句：The company is undergoing a period of consolidation.  这家公司正在进行一段时间的整合。

The consolidation of his power was achieved through strategic alliances. 他通过战略联盟巩固了自己的权力。|近义词：strengthening, reinforcement, unification, merging, combination |反义词: weakening, division, separation |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *consolidacioun*, from Late Latin consolidātiō, from Latin consolidātus, past participle of consolidāre (“to make solid, strengthen”), from con- (“together”) + solidus (“solid”).
