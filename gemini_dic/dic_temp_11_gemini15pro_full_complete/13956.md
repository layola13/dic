aurality:/ɔːˈrælɪti/
n.
听觉性；听觉特性
The quality of being perceived through the sense of hearing.
The composer explored the aurality of the piece, focusing on the interplay of different sounds.  作曲家探索了这首作品的听觉特性，重点关注不同声音之间的相互作用。
The aurality of the rainforest is overwhelming.  雨林的声音极其丰富。
auditory perception, sound quality
visual aspects
noun
