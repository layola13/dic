cop-a-plea:/ˈkɒp ə pliː/ (UK) /ˈkɑːp ə pliː/ (US)
n.|认罪协议；抗辩交易|An agreement in a criminal case whereby the defendant agrees to plead guilty to a particular charge in return for some concession from the prosecution.|He took a cop-a-plea to avoid a longer sentence. 他接受了认罪协议以避免更长的刑期。|plea bargain, plea agreement|confession, denial|noun
