microcellular:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛljələr/| adj.|微孔的，微蜂窝状的| Composed of or containing many tiny cells or compartments.| Microcellular foam is used in many applications due to its lightweight and insulating properties. 微孔泡沫因其轻质和隔热性能而被广泛应用。|例句：The new material is microcellular, making it incredibly lightweight.  这种新材料是微孔的，因此非常轻。

例句：Microcellular plastics are used in packaging to protect fragile items. 微孔塑料用于包装中以保护易碎物品。|近义词：porous, cellular, foam-like|反义词：dense, compact|词性:形容词
