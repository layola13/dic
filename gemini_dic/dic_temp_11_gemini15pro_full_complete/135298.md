nonpast:/nɑːnˈpæst/ | adj. | 非过去的，现在或将来的 | Referring to present or future time; not past. | The nonpast tense includes the present and future. 非过去时态包括现在时和将来时。|  She is focused on nonpast events. 她专注于现在或未来的事件。 | 近义词: present, future | 反义词: past | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "past" (adjective referring to a time before the present)

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I eat breakfast every day. = 我每天都吃早餐。
She works at a bank. = 她在银行工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am eating breakfast right now. = 我现在正在吃早餐。
She is working on a new project. = 她正在做一个新项目。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have eaten breakfast already. = 我已经吃过早餐了。
She has worked here for five years. = 她在这里工作了五年。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been eating breakfast for an hour. = 我已经吃了一个小时的早餐了。
She has been working on this project all week. = 她整个星期都在做这个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I ate breakfast yesterday. = 我昨天吃了早餐。
She worked late last night. = 她昨晚工作到很晚。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was eating breakfast when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候我正在吃早餐。
She was working on the report when the power went out. = 停电时她正在写报告。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had eaten breakfast before I left for work. = 我上班前已经吃过早餐了。
She had worked on the project before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前完成了项目。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been eating breakfast for an hour when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候我已经吃了一个小时的早餐了。
She had been working on the report for days before it was finally finished. = 这份报告她写了好几天才最终完成。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will eat breakfast tomorrow. = 我明天会吃早餐。
She will work on the weekend. = 她周末要工作。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be eating breakfast at 8am tomorrow. = 明天早上8点我将会在吃早餐。
She will be working on the presentation next week. = 她下周将准备报告。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have eaten breakfast by the time you arrive. = 你到达之前我将已经吃过早餐了。
She will have finished the project by the end of the month. = 她将在月底前完成这个项目。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been eating breakfast for an hour by the time you wake up. = 你醒来的时候我将已经吃了一个小时的早餐了。
She will have been working on this project for six months by the time it's launched. = 到项目启动时，她将已经在这个项目上工作了六个月。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would travel the world. = 如果我很有钱，我会环游世界。
If she had more time, she would learn a new language. = 如果她有更多的时间，她会学习一门新的语言。

被动语态:
The breakfast was eaten. = 早餐被吃掉了。
The project is being worked on. = 项目正在进行中。

疑问句:
Are you eating breakfast? = 你在吃早餐吗？
Is she working on the project? = 她正在做这个项目吗？

否定句:
I am not eating breakfast. = 我没有在吃早餐。
She is not working on the project. = 她没有在做这个项目。
