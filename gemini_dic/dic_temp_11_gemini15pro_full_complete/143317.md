outdwells:/ˌaʊtˈdwɛlz/
verb
居住得比…更久; 比...活得更长
to live longer than someone else.
He outdwelled all his siblings. 他比他所有的兄弟姐妹都活得长。
近义词: outlive, survive
反义词: predecease
词性: verb
