knocker:/ˈnɑːkər/ (US) /ˈnɒkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|敲门环；敲门者；门环|A person or thing that knocks, especially a device attached to a door for knocking.  A metal object, often shaped like a ring, fixed to a door and used for knocking.|The brass knocker on the front door gleamed in the sunlight.  阳光下，前门的黄铜敲门环闪闪发光。|
She gave the knocker a sharp rap. 她重重地敲了一下门环。|Synonyms: hammer, mallet, clapper, rapper |Antonyms: doorbell (in modern context)|noun
