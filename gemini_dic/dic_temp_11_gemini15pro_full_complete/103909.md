juddock:/ˈdʒʌdək/ | n. |红点海葵; 一种欧洲淡水鱼（通常指roach, 欧洲蟑螂）| A small European freshwater fish (roach). Also, a red anemone. | He caught a juddock while fishing in the river. 他在河里钓鱼时钓到了一条juddock。| The juddock clung to the rock in the fast-flowing stream.  红点海葵紧紧地贴在湍急溪流中的岩石上。 | 近义词: roach, rudd (for the fish), anemone (for the sea creature) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
Origin uncertain. Possibly related to the Dutch word 'jodokus', a personal name. For the fish, the term is mostly a regional variation for "roach." For the anemone, the etymology is less clear, possibly related to its appearance.
