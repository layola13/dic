lachrymal:/ˈlækrɪməl/ (US), /ˈlækrɪml/ (UK)| adj. n.|泪腺的；流泪的；泪管的；泪器| relating to tears or the glands that produce them|
The lachrymal glands produce tears. 泪腺产生眼泪。
She dabbed her lachrymal ducts with a tissue. 她用纸巾轻擦泪管。
近义词: tearful, weeping|反义词: joyful, cheerful|词性: adjective, noun

lachrymal:/ˈlækrɪməl/ (US), /ˈlækrɪml/ (UK)| n.|泪腺；泪器|the glands that secrete tears; tear ducts|
The doctor examined her lachrymal. 医生检查了她的泪腺。
Injury to the lachrymal can cause excessive tearing. 泪腺损伤会导致泪水分泌过多。
近义词: tear gland, tear duct|反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for the anatomical noun)|词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *lacrimālis, from Latin lacrima (“tear”).
