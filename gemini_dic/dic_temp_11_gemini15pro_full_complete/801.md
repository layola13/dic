absorptive:/əbˈsɔːrp.tɪv/ /əbˈzɔːrp.tɪv/
adjective
吸收性的；有吸收力的
Capable of absorbing or taking something in.
The absorptive capacity of the sponge is impressive.  这块海绵的吸收能力令人印象深刻。
The medication is applied as an absorptive cream.  这种药物是以吸收性乳膏的形式使用的。
近义词: absorbent, permeable, porous
反义词: impermeable, nonabsorbent
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *absorptīvus, from Latin absorptus (past participle of absorbēre “to suck in, swallow down, engulf”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + sorbēre (“to suck up, absorb”).
