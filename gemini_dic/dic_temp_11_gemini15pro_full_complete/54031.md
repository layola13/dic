discus:/ˈdɪskəs/ (US), /ˈdɪskəs/ (UK)| n. |铁饼；掷铁饼运动|A heavy, circular, and usually metal object thrown in track and field competitions.|The athlete hurled the discus with great force.  运动员使劲投掷铁饼。|Synonyms: disc, plate|Antonyms: None|Noun


discus:/dɪˈskʌs/ (US), /dɪˈskʌs/ (UK)| v. |讨论；谈论|To talk about a subject with someone or a group of people.|We need to discus the budget for next year. 我们需要讨论一下明年的预算。|Synonyms: talk over, debate, consider, deliberate, confer|Antonyms: ignore, avoid, disregard|Verb
