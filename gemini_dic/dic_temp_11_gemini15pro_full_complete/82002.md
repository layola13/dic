griot:/ˈɡriːoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡriːət/ (UK)| n. |吟游诗人，西非的口述历史学家|A West African storyteller, singer, musician, and oral historian who preserves and transmits the history and traditions of a community or family.|The griot told stories of ancient kings and heroes. 吟游诗人讲述了古代国王和英雄的故事。|近义词：storyteller, bard, historian, poet|反义词: N/A|noun
