banneret:/ˈbænərɪt/| n. |旗手；骑士；拥有自己旗帜的骑士|A knight who was entitled to lead a company of men under his own banner.|Sir Giles, a seasoned banneret, led his troops into battle.  老练的骑士吉尔斯爵士率领他的部队投入战斗。|Synonyms: knight, standard-bearer, ensign|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider "foot soldier," "commoner")|noun

banneret:/ˈbænərɪt/| n. |小旗；旗帜|A small banner or flag.|The banneret fluttered in the wind. 小旗在风中飘扬。|Synonyms: pennant, pennon, banderole|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
