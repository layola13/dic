non-starter:/ˌnɑːnˈstɑːrtər/ | n. | 无希望的人/事，不可能成功的事 |Something that has no chance of success.| The project was a non-starter due to lack of funding. 由于缺乏资金，该项目毫无希望。| synonyms: doomed project, hopeless case, dud | antonyms: promising venture, successful undertaking | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + starter,  meaning something that cannot or does not start.  The term originally comes from horse racing, referring to a horse that does not start a race.
