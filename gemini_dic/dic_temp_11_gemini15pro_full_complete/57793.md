dredge:/drɛdʒ/ | v.  n. | 疏浚，挖掘；挖泥船 | To remove mud, silt, or other unwanted material from the bottom of a body of water.  As a noun, it refers to a machine used for this purpose. | They dredged the harbor to make it deeper. 他们疏浚了港口以使其更深。 | synonyms: excavate, scoop, clear | antonyms: fill, deposit | verb

dredged:/drɛdʒd/ | adj. | 疏浚过的，挖掘出的 |  Covered or extracted by dredging. | The dredged material was used to create new land. 疏浚的材料被用来建造新的土地。 | synonyms: excavated, scooped | antonyms: filled, deposited | adjective
