gaekwar:/ˈɡeɪkwɑːr/ |n.| 土邦主，王公；（尤指印度西部）盖克瓦德 |A Gaekwar was a Maratha ruler, especially of Baroda in western India. |The Gaekwar commissioned a magnificent palace. 盖克瓦德委托建造了一座宏伟的宫殿。| synonym: raja, nawab | antonym: peasant, commoner | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Marathi गायकवाड (gāykwāḍ), ultimately from Sanskrit गोपालक (gopālaka, “cowherd”).  The Gaekwads were originally cowherds who rose to prominence in the Maratha Empire.
