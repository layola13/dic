co-ossify:/koʊˈɑːsɪfaɪ/
verb
共同骨化；愈合在一起
to become united by the growth of bony tissue; to fuse into one bone.
The two bones co-ossified over time due to the injury.  由于受伤，这两块骨头随着时间的推移愈合在一起了。
The separate bones in the skull of a baby eventually co-ossify. 婴儿头骨中分离的骨头最终会愈合在一起。
近义词: fuse, unite, consolidate, join
反义词: separate, divide
词性: verb

Etymology:
From co- (together) + ossify (to turn into bone).  'Ossify' comes from Latin 'os' (bone) + '-ify' (to make).
