discipline:/ˈdɪsɪplɪn/ /ˈdɪsəplɪn/| n. vt.|纪律；学科；训练；惩罚|A branch of knowledge, typically one studied in higher education.  The practice of training people to obey rules or a code of behavior, using punishment to correct disobedience.|例句：She studied the discipline of history at university. 她在大学学习历史这门学科。|近义词：order, training, control, instruction, punishment|反义词：disorder, chaos, negligence|n.

discipline:/ˈdɪsɪplɪn/ /ˈdɪsəplɪn/| vt.|训练；惩罚|To train someone to obey rules or a code of behavior, using punishment to correct disobedience.|例句：The teacher disciplined the student for talking in class. 老师因为学生上课讲话而惩罚了他。|近义词：punish, chastise, correct, train|反义词：praise, reward, indulge|vt.
