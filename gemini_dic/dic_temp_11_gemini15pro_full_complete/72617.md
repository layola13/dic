flyboat:/ˈflaɪboʊt/| n. |平底货船，内河驳船|A large, flat-bottomed boat used for carrying cargo, especially on canals and rivers.|例句：The flyboat was laden with timber and slowly made its way down the river. 这艘平底货船装满了木材，缓慢地沿着河向下游驶去。

例句：Flyboats were a common sight on the canals of Europe in the 17th and 18th centuries. 在17和18世纪，平底货船在欧洲的运河上是常见的景象。

近义词：barge, lighter|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a fast, modern cargo ship could be considered a contextual antonym)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *vlieboot*, from *vlie* "shallow-draught, flat-bottomed" + *boot* "boat."
