eye-rolling:/ˈaɪˌroʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 翻白眼 | The act of rolling one's eyes, typically expressing exasperation, disbelief, or contempt. |  She responded to his suggestion with an exaggerated eye-roll. 她对他提出的建议翻了个白眼作为回应。| Synonyms: eye-roll (noun form), rolling one's eyes | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical action, but antonyms for the implied sentiment could be agreement, enthusiasm) | noun
