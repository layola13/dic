evergreen:/ˈevərɡriːn/| adj. n.|常青的，常绿树; 始终流行的|A plant that retains green leaves throughout the year.  Something that remains popular or relevant over a long period of time. |例句：Pine trees are evergreens. 松树是常青树。|近义词：lasting, perennial, enduring|反义词:deciduous, ephemeral|词性:形容词, 名词

evergreen:/ˈevərɡriːn/| n.|常绿树，常青植物|A tree or plant that retains its green leaves throughout the year.|例句：The evergreen forests of the Pacific Northwest.  太平洋西北地区的常绿森林。|近义词：conifer, evergreen tree|反义词:deciduous tree|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ever + green.  "Ever" meaning always, and "green" referring to the color of the leaves.
