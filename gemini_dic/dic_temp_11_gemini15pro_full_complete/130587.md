nonacuteness:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːtnəs/ | n. |非锐度，不尖锐，迟钝|Lack of sharpness or acuteness; the state of not being acute.  It can refer to a lack of sharpness in a physical sense (like a dull blade) or in a mental sense (like a lack of quick understanding).|The nonacuteness of the blade made it difficult to cut the rope. 刀刃的钝度使其难以切割绳索。|Near synonyms: dullness, bluntness, obtuseness|Antonyms: acuteness, sharpness, keenness|noun
