churchman:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmən/| n. |教士；教会人员；虔诚的教徒|A man who is actively involved in the affairs of a church, especially a clergyman. |例句：He was a devout churchman all his life.  他一生都是虔诚的教徒。

例句：The churchman delivered a moving sermon.  这位教士发表了一篇感人的布道。|近义词：clergyman, ecclesiastic, parishioner (if lay member)|反义词:atheist, nonbeliever|词性:noun
