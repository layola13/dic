overcommercialized: /ˌoʊvərˌkəˈmɜːrʃəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度商业化的 |  Having been exploited or promoted excessively for profit. | The once charming town has become overcommercialized. 这个曾经迷人的小镇已经变得过度商业化了。| over-commercialised, overly commercial | understated, authentic | adjective
