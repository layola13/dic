caf:/kæf/ | n. | 咖啡馆，小餐馆 | A small, informal restaurant serving simple meals and drinks, often coffee. |  I met my friend at the caf for lunch. 我和朋友在咖啡馆吃午饭。

| 近义词: cafe, coffee shop, bistro | 反义词: restaurant (更正式的餐厅) | 词性: 名词


caf:/kæf/ | n. |  自助餐厅 | A cafeteria, especially in a school, college, or office building. | The caf in our office building has a good selection of salads. 我们办公楼的自助餐厅有很多沙拉可供选择。

| 近义词: cafeteria, canteen | 反义词: restaurant (更正式的餐厅) | 词性: 名词
