halichondriae:/ˌhælɪˈkɒndriˌiː/ /ˌhælɪˈkɑːndriˌaɪ/
n.
halichondrias (plural)
海绵骨针；硅质骨针
Spicules of siliceous sponges, usually of the order Halichondrida.  These spicules are made of silica and provide structural support for the sponge.
Halichondriae are found in the skeletal structure of many marine sponges.  海绵骨针存在于许多海绵的骨骼结构中。
These halichondriae are characteristic of this species of sponge. 这些硅质骨针是这种海绵的特征。
近义词: spicules
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soft tissue" in the context of sponge anatomy)
词性: noun
