overmystify: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪstɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使过于神秘化，故弄玄虚 | To make something unnecessarily mysterious or complicated; to obscure or confuse by making something seem more complex than it is. | The professor overmystified the simple concept, leaving the students more confused than before. 教授把简单的概念故弄玄虚，让学生们更加困惑了。|  The author overmystified the plot, making it difficult for readers to follow.  作者把情节弄得太神秘了，让读者难以理解。 | 近义词: obfuscate, bewilder, confuse, perplex | 反义词: clarify, simplify, explain, elucidate |词性: 动词
