apiologist:/ˌeɪpiˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.
养蜂人；蜜蜂学家
A person who studies bees or beekeeping.
例句：The apiologist inspected the hives for signs of disease.  养蜂人检查蜂箱是否有疾病迹象。

例句：The apiologist gave a lecture on the importance of bees to the ecosystem. 蜜蜂学家就蜜蜂对生态系统的重要性作了讲座。
