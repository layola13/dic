facetiae:/fəˈsiːʃiiː/
noun
滑稽言辞；妙语；俏皮话
Witty or humorous remarks or writings, especially of a somewhat indecent or coarse character.
He entertained the crowd with his facetiae. 他用他的妙语连珠娱乐了大众。
synonyms: witticisms, jests, quips, jokes, banter, badinage, repartee, drollery, humor, pleasantry
antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
n.

Etymology:
From Latin *facetiae*, plural of *facetia* "wittiness, humor," from *facetus* "witty, elegant, fine, courteous."
