evolutility:/iˌvɒl(j)uˈtɪlɪti/ | n. |进化性，演进能力| The capacity of something to evolve or adapt easily.  This refers to the ease with which a system, process, or product can be modified or improved over time. |  This software platform boasts impressive evolutility, allowing developers to easily add new features.  这个软件平台拥有令人印象深刻的进化性，允许开发人员轻松添加新功能。| synonyms: adaptability, flexibility, plasticity | antonyms: rigidity, inflexibility | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "evolution" + "-ility" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  "Evolution" comes from the Latin "evolutio," meaning "an unrolling."
