bugloss: /ˈbʌɡlɒs/ (UK), /ˈbʌɡlɔːs/ (US) | n. | 蓝蓟属植物; 牛舌草 | A plant of the genus *Anchusa*, having hairy leaves and bright blue, purple, or red flowers.  The name comes from the Greek words "bous" (ox) and "glossa" (tongue), referring to the shape and texture of the leaves. | The bugloss attracted bees to the garden.  蓝蓟吸引蜜蜂来到花园。| The vibrant blue bugloss added a splash of color to the meadow.  鲜艳的蓝色蓝蓟为草地增添了一抹色彩。 | Synonyms: alkanet, ox-tongue | Antonyms: N/A | noun
