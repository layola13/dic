nonconfrontational: /ˌnɒnkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnkɑːnfrənˈteɪʃənl/| adj.|不具对抗性的，不爱对抗的|Not tending to confront or challenge; avoiding conflict.|She has a nonconfrontational approach to management. 她采用了一种不具对抗性的管理方法。|He took a nonconfrontational stance during the negotiations. 在谈判过程中，他采取了不对抗的立场。|近义词:  cooperative, conciliatory, peaceful, diplomatic|反义词: confrontational, aggressive, hostile, belligerent|词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin non "not"
confrontational: from confront (verb) + -ational (suffix)
confront: from Old French confronter "to compare," from Latin confrontare "to bring face to face," from com- "together" + frons "forehead, front"
