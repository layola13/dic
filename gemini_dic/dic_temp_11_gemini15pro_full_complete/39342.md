conjoining:/kənˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
|v.|结合，连接|The report conjoins the findings of two separate studies. 这份报告结合了两项独立研究的结果。|
|例句：Conjoining clauses with "and" is common in English. 用“and”连接从句在英语中很常见。|近义词：combining, uniting, linking, joining|反义词：separating, dividing, disjoining|词性：动词
conjoining:/kənˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
|adj.|连接的，结合的|The conjoining roads created a busy intersection. 连接的道路形成了一个繁忙的十字路口。|
|例句：The conjoining of the two companies created a powerful corporation. 两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的公司。|近义词：connecting, linking, joining|反义词：separating, dividing, disjoining|词性：形容词

PHRASE->
conjoining with:  与...结合  Conjoining with other organizations, they were able to achieve their goal. 与其他组织结合，他们能够实现他们的目标。
conjoining and:  连接并且  Conjoining and analyzing the data is the next step. 连接并分析数据是下一步。
the act of conjoining:  结合的行为  The act of conjoining different elements created a unique style. 结合不同元素的行为创造了一种独特的风格。
conjoining of:  ...的结合 The conjoining of art and science often leads to innovation. 艺术和科学的结合通常会导致创新。
conjoining the efforts:  共同努力 Conjoining the efforts of different teams resulted in a successful project. 不同团队共同努力，最终项目取得了成功。
