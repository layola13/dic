crooked:/ˈkrʊkɪd/| adj.|弯曲的，歪的，不正当的|bent or twisted out of shape; dishonest or illegal|The picture hung crooked on the wall.  这幅画歪挂在墙上。 The politician was involved in a crooked business deal.  这个政客卷入了一桩不正当的商业交易。|近义词：bent, twisted, dishonest, corrupt, illegal|反义词：straight, honest, legal|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *croked, from Old Norse *krokitr (“crooked”), past participle of *krøkja (“to bend, crook”), from Proto-Germanic *kraukjaną (“to hook, bend”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”). Cognate with Dutch *kroke (“bend, crook”), German *Kroke (“hook”).
