outshrilled:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪld/
verb
(以更高的音调)盖过…的声音，比…叫得更响
To scream or shriek more loudly than someone or something else.
The soprano outshrilled the other singers in the chorus.  女高音的歌声盖过了合唱团里的其他歌手。
outscream, outyell
-
verb
词源:  "out-" (超过) + "shrill" (发出尖锐的声音)  -> "outshrill" (以更高的音调盖过…的声音)
