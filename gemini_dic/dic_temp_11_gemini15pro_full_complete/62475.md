endosmometric:/ˌɛndoʊzˈmɑːtrɪk/ | adj. | 内渗的 | Relating to or exhibiting endosmosis, the inward flow of a solvent through a semipermeable membrane toward a solution of higher solute concentration. |  The endosmometric pressure caused the cell to swell. 内渗压导致细胞膨胀。 |  Near synonyms: endosmotic | antonyms: exosmometric | adjective
