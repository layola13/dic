interwreathe:/ˌɪntərˈriːð/ |v. |互相缠绕，交织|To weave or twine together; interlace.|例句：The roses and lilies were interwreathed around the columns. 玫瑰和百合花缠绕在柱子上。|例句：Their destinies seemed to be interwreathed. 他们的命运似乎交织在一起。|近义词：intertwine, interlace, entwine|反义词：separate, disentangle|词性:动词

词源:
From inter- + wreathe.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Wreathe" comes from the Old English "writhan," meaning "to twist."
