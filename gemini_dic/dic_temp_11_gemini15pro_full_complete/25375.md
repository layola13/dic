bulbasaur:/ˈbʌlbəˌsɔːr/ | n. |妙蛙种子|A fictional creature from the Pokémon franchise. It is a Grass/Poison type Pokémon and is the first Pokémon in the National Pokédex.  Bulbasaur is a small, quadrupedal amphibian Pokémon that has a bulb-like plant growing on its back.  This bulb provides it with energy through photosynthesis and allows it to learn various Grass-type moves.|Ash's first Pokémon was a Bulbasaur. 小智的第一只宝可梦是妙蛙种子。|近义词: (No direct synonyms, as it's a proper noun.
