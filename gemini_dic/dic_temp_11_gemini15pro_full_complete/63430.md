entreat:/ɪnˈtriːt/ /ɛnˈtriːt/| v. |恳求，乞求|To ask someone earnestly or anxiously to do something.|She entreated him to stay. 她恳求他留下。| The villagers entreated the king for help. 村民恳求国王的帮助。|近义词：beg, implore, beseech, plead|反义词：demand, command|词性:动词

词源: From Old French entraiter (to treat, handle, deal with), from en- (in) + traiter (to treat).  The sense of "to ask earnestly" developed from the idea of "dealing with" someone in a pleading or persuasive way.
