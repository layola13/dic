affuse:/əˈfjuːz/ | v. | 洒，倾注；充满，弥漫 | To pour out or spread, as a liquid; to suffuse. |  The golden light of sunset affused the landscape. 夕阳的金光洒满了大地。| 近义词:suffuse, permeate, imbue | 反义词:drain, empty | 动词

词源:
From Latin affusus, past participle of affundere "to pour on, pour out," from ad "to, toward" + fundere "to pour."
