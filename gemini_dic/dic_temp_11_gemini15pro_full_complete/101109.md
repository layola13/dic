invertebrate:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrət/ /ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrɪt/| n. adj.|无脊椎动物；无脊椎动物的|An animal without a backbone or spinal column.  An invertebrate animal or species.|例句：Invertebrates, such as insects and mollusks, make up a vast majority of animal species. 无脊椎动物，如昆虫和软体动物，构成了绝大多数的动物物种。

例句：Spiders are invertebrate animals. 蜘蛛是无脊椎动物。|近义词：spineless creature|反义词: vertebrate|词性:名词、形容词
