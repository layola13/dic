nonprophetical:/ˌnɑːnprəˈfɛtɪkəl/
adjective
非预言性的，非先知的
Not prophetic; not foretelling the future.
Her novel, while imaginative, was entirely nonprophetical. 她的小说虽然富有想象力，但完全没有预言性。
Synonyms: nonpredictive, unprophetic
Antonyms: prophetic, predictive
词性: adjective
