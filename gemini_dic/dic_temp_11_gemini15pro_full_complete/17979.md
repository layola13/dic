befuddle:/bɪˈfʌdəl/| vt.|使迷惑，使糊涂|to confuse someone so much that they cannot think clearly|The complex instructions completely befuddled me. 复杂的指令让我完全糊涂了。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex, mystify|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, clear up|verb

befuddle:/bɪˈfʌdəl/| adj.(rare, informal) |困惑的，糊涂的|confused and unable to think clearly|He had a befuddled look on his face. 他脸上带着困惑的表情。|Synonyms: confused, bewildered, perplexed, mystified|Antonyms: clear-headed, lucid|adjective
