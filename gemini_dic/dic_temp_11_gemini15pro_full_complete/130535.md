nonaccumulativeness:/nɒnəkjuːmjʊlətɪvnəs/ | n. | 非累积性，非积累性 |  The property of not accumulating or building up over time.  It refers to something that doesn't increase in quantity or intensity with repeated occurrences. | The nonaccumulativeness of the drug means that it's safe to take daily.  这种药物的非累积性意味着每天服用是安全的。| non-cumulative nature, non-additive nature | cumulativeness, additive nature | noun
