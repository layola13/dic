anfractuous:/ænˈfræktʃuəs/
adjective
迂回的，曲折的
Characterized by winding and intricate passages; circuitous.
例句：The hikers struggled through the anfractuous mountain trails.  徒步旅行者艰难地穿过蜿蜒曲折的山路。

例句：Her explanation was so anfractuous that I quickly got lost. 她的解释太迂回了，我很快就搞糊涂了。

近义词：winding, tortuous, convoluted, circuitous, meandering
反义词：straight, direct, straightforward
词性: adjective
