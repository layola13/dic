blabbering:/ˈblæbərɪŋ/ | n. | 喋喋不休，胡言乱语 | The act of talking too much, especially about unimportant things or in a foolish way. | The child's blabbering was hard to understand. 孩子喋喋不休的话很难听懂。| 近义词: chattering, prattling, babbling | 反义词: silence, reticence | 词性: 名词

blabbering:/ˈblæbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 喋喋不休的，胡言乱语的 | Talking too much, especially about unimportant things or in a foolish way. |  He was blabbering on about nothing in particular. 他一直在胡言乱语，没有什么特别的内容。 | 近义词: talkative, garrulous, loquacious | 反义词: quiet, reserved | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *blaberen*, frequentative of *blaben* (“to blab”), of imitative origin.
