invulnerable:/ɪnˈvʌlnərəbl/
|adj.|无懈可击的；不会受伤害的；刀枪不入的|Impossible to harm or damage.|
|No system is completely invulnerable to attack.|没有哪个系统是完全不会受到攻击的。|
|She seemed invulnerable to criticism.| 她似乎不受批评的影响。|
|近义词: impenetrable, immune, invincible|
|反义词: vulnerable, susceptible, weak|
|词性: 形容词|


词源:
From Late Latin *invulnerabilis, from in- (“not”) + vulnerabilis (“vulnerable”). Equivalent to in- + vulnerable.
