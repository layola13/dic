arrowheads:/ˈæroʊˌhɛdz/| n.|箭头|The pointed tips of arrows.  These can be made of various materials like stone, metal, or bone.|The ancient arrowheads were made of flint. 这些古代箭头是用燧石做的。|synonyms: points, tips, barbs|antonyms: (no direct antonym, perhaps "shaft" or "nock" as parts of the arrow opposite the head) |noun
