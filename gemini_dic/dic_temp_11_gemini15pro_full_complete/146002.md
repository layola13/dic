overdominating: /ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度支配的，过分控制的
Excessively controlling or dominating; tending to exert too much influence or power over others.
例句：His overdominating personality made it difficult for others to contribute to the project. 他过度支配的性格使其他人难以对项目做出贡献。

例句：She found his overdominating behavior suffocating and decided to end the relationship. 她觉得他过分控制的行为令人窒息，于是决定结束这段关系。

近义词：controlling, domineering, authoritarian, overpowering, oppressive
反义词：submissive, passive, yielding, compliant

词性: adjective


Etymology:
From over- + dominating, the present participle of dominate. Dominate comes from the Latin word "dominari," meaning "to rule" or "to govern."
