hurrah:/hʊˈrɑː/ /həˈreɪ/| int. n.|欢呼；万岁；|A shout of joy or approval.|例句：Hurrah! We won! 万岁！我们赢了！

例句：The crowd let out a loud hurrah.  人群发出震耳欲聋的欢呼声。|近义词：hooray, cheers, yahoo|反义词:boo, hiss|词性:感叹词, 名词
词源: From German  hurra, from the exclamation hu!, probably of imitative origin.
