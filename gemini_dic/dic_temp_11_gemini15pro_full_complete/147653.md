overponderously:/ˌōvərˈpɑːndərəsli/ | adv. | 过于沉重地，过于 ponderous 地 |  In a manner that is excessively heavy, labored, or dull. | He overponderously explained the intricacies of the tax code, losing everyone's attention.  他过于沉重地解释了税法的复杂性，失去了所有人的注意力。 | 近义词：laboriously, tediously, ponderously, heavily | 反义词：lightly, quickly, easily, nimbly | 词性：副词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "ponderous" (heavy, weighty). "Ponderous" comes from the Late Latin "ponderosus," from "pondus" (weight), related to "pendere" (to hang, weigh).
