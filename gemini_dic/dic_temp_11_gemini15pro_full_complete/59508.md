eastness:/ˈiːst.nəs/
noun
东方性，东向性
The quality or state of being east or eastern.
The eastness of the rising sun was undeniable.  旭日东升的东方性是不可否认的。
The map highlighted the eastness of the region.  地图突出了该地区的东向性。
Synonym: easternness, orientality
Antonym: westness
