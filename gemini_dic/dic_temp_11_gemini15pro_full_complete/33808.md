circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleks/ (US) /ˈsɜːkəmfkeks/ (UK)| adj. n.|抑扬符，弯曲的|A circumflex is a diacritical mark (^) placed above a vowel in some languages to indicate pronunciation, length, or a combination of both. It can also refer to something bent or arched.|The word "crèche" uses a circumflex.|近义词：caret, arc, curve|反义词:straight|adjective, noun
