hono:/ˈhɒnəʊ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːnoʊ/ (US)| n. |荣誉，尊敬|A public acknowledgment or official reward given for an achievement.  It can also refer to a high respect earned through accomplishments or character.  Derived from the Latin word "honor".|例句：
They bestowed great honor upon her. 他们给予她极大的荣誉。
He is a man of honor. 他是一个正直的人。

|近义词：respect, esteem, reverence, dignity, distinction|反义词：dishonor, disgrace, shame, ignominy|词性:名词
