coynesses:/ˈkɔɪnɪsɪz/
n.
害羞;腼腆;忸怩作态;故作天真
A deliberate pretense of shyness or modesty that is intended to be alluring.
She affected a certain coyness when he complimented her singing. 当他称赞她的歌声时，她故作羞涩。
近义词：shyness, modesty, demureness, reserve, reticence
反义词：boldness, brazenness, forwardness
