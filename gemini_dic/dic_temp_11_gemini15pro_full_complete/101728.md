irresistible:/ɪˌrɪˈzɪstəbəl/ /ɪˌriːˈzɪstəbəl/| adj.|不可抗拒的；无法抵挡的|Too good to refuse; impossible not to like, want, or admire.|The chocolate cake was irresistible. 这块巧克力蛋糕太诱人了。|Synonyms: alluring, captivating, compelling, enticing, tempting|Antonyms: resistible, unappealing, unattractive|Word Class: Adjective

irresistible:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbli/ /ˌɪriːˈzɪstəbli/| adv.|不可抗拒地|In a way that is too strong to oppose or prevent.|She was irresistibly drawn to him. 她被不可抗拒地吸引到他身边。|Synonyms: overwhelmingly, powerfully, strongly|Antonyms: weakly, slightly|Word Class: Adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English:  irresistible, from Old French, or from late Latin irresistibilis, from in- ‘not’ + resistibilis ‘that can be resisted’ (from resistere ‘resist’).
