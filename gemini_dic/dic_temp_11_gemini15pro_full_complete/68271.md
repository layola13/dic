fakers:/ˈfeɪkərz/| n.|骗子；假冒者；伪造者|A person who pretends to be someone or something they are not, or who makes something that is not genuine.|Those fakers online pretend to be experts. 那些网上的骗子假装是专家。|synonyms: impostors, charlatans, fraudsters, pretenders|antonyms: genuine people, authentic individuals|noun

fake:/feɪk/| adj. n. v.|假的；伪造的；冒充的；赝品；冒充；伪造|Not genuine; imitation.  A thing that is not genuine; a forgery. To make something that is not genuine. |The painting was a fake. 这幅画是赝品。 He faked his own death. 他伪造了自己的死亡。|synonyms: counterfeit, forged, imitation, sham|antonyms: genuine, real, authentic|adjective, noun, verb
