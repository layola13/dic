deoxidation:/ˌdiːˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 脱氧，去氧化 | The process of removing oxygen from a substance, especially metal. |  Steel undergoes deoxidation during its production to remove impurities and improve quality. 钢铁在其生产过程中会进行脱氧，以去除杂质并提高质量。

The deoxidation of copper is a crucial step in its refining. 铜的脱氧是其精炼过程中的关键步骤。 | synonyms: reduction, oxygen removal | antonyms: oxidation | noun
