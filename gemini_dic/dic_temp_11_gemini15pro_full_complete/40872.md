cooker:/ˈkʊkər/ (UK) /ˈkʊkɚ/ (US)| n. | 炉灶；炊具 |A large piece of equipment for cooking food, typically using gas or electricity.  A device used to cook food. | I bought a new cooker last week. 我上周买了一个新的炉灶。

The cooker isn't working.  炉灶坏了。| 近义词：stove, oven, range | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "to cook" + "-er" (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "coquere" (to cook).
