arabesquely:/ˌærəˈbɛskli/ | adv. | 花饰般地；蔓藤花纹般地 | In an ornate or elaborate manner, reminiscent of the intricate designs of arabesques. | The dancer moved arabesquely across the stage. 舞蹈演员在舞台上花饰般地移动。 | The vine climbed arabesquely up the wall. 藤蔓花饰般地爬上墙壁。 | 近义词: ornately, elaborately, decoratively | 反义词: plainly, simply | 词性: 副词

词源: From French *arabesque*, from Italian *arabesco*, from *arabo* "Arabian," ultimately from Arabic *ʻarab* "Arab".  The term originally referred to a style of artistic decoration characteristic of Islamic art, featuring intricate patterns of interwoven lines and geometric forms.
