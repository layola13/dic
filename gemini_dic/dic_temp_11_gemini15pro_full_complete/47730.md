decagonal: /dɪˈkæɡənəl/
adjective

Having ten sides and ten angles.
The architect designed a decagonal gazebo for the park.  这位建筑师为公园设计了一个十边形的凉亭。
The crystal had a decagonal cross-section. 这颗水晶的横截面是十边形的。
synonym: ten-sided
antonym: (No direct antonym, consider shapes with different numbers of sides, e.g., triangular, square, pentagonal)
词性: adjective
