factionalisms:/ˈfækʃənəlɪzəmz/
noun
派系主义；派系斗争
The tendency to form or promote factions; the state of being divided into factions.
The factionalisms within the political party threatened to tear it apart.  党内的派系斗争有可能使其分裂。
Synonyms:  infighting, dissension, discord, strife, division
Antonyms: unity, harmony, agreement, solidarity
词性: noun
