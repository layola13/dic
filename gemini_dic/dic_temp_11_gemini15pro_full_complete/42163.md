couchlike:/ˈkaʊtʃlaɪk/
adj.
像沙发的；像躺椅的；懒散的
Resembling a couch; inviting relaxation or indolence.
The room had a couchlike feel, with its soft lighting and plush cushions. 房间有一种像沙发一样的感觉，灯光柔和，垫子柔软。
The beanbag chairs were couchlike and comfortable.  这些豆袋椅像沙发一样舒适。
近义词:comfortable, relaxing, loungelike, sofa-like
反义词:uncomfortable, stiff, formal
