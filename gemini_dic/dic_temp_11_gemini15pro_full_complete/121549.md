mismeasurement:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒərmənt/
n.
错误测量，测量不准
An act or instance of measuring incorrectly.
The mismeasurement of the ingredients resulted in a cake disaster.  配料测量错误导致蛋糕做坏了。
近义词:inaccurate measurement, faulty measurement
反义词:accurate measurement, precise measurement
词性:noun

Etymology:
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + measurement (from measure + -ment).
