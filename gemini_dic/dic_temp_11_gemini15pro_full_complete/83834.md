hakim:/ˈhɑːkiːm/ | n. | 法官；统治者；伊斯兰教法学家 | A person who acts as a judge or ruler, especially in Islamic countries; a Muslim legal scholar. | The hakim listened carefully to both sides of the dispute.  法官仔细听取了争议双方的意见。| Synonyms: judge, magistrate, ruler, leader, scholar, jurist | Antonyms: defendant, accused, subject | noun


hakim:/ˈhɑːkiːm/ | n. | 医师；医生（尤指印度、巴基斯坦等地） | A physician or doctor, especially in India, Pakistan, etc. | The hakim prescribed a herbal remedy for my cough.  医师给我开了草药来治疗我的咳嗽。| Synonyms: doctor, physician, healer | Antonyms: patient | noun
