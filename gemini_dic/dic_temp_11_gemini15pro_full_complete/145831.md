overdeliberates:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbəreɪts/
|v.|过度深思熟虑|To think about or consider something excessively or to an unnecessary degree.|He overdeliberates every decision, which leads to analysis paralysis.|近义词：overthink, ponder excessively, ruminate|反义词：act impulsively, decide quickly|词性:动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "deliberate" (verb meaning "to think carefully about").  The word "deliberate" comes from Latin "deliberare," meaning "to weigh in the mind, consider, ponder," from "de-" (from, out of) + "librare" (to weigh).
