chylifactive:/ˌkaɪlɪˈfæktɪv/ | adj. | 乳糜化的，促进乳糜形成的 | Relating to or promoting the formation of chyle (a milky fluid containing absorbed fats and other products of digestion) in the small intestine. |  The chylifactive process is essential for the absorption of fats. 乳糜化过程对于脂肪的吸收至关重要。|The drug has chylifactive properties that aid in digestion. 这种药物具有促进消化的乳糜化特性。|近义词:chyle-producing | 反义词: None | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From chylo- + -factive, from Latin chylus "chyle" (from Greek chylos "juice") + -factive (from Latin facere "to make").
