labile:/ˈleɪbaɪl/ /ˈleɪˌbaɪl/| adj.|不稳定的，易变的，易分解的| readily undergoing change or decomposition.  Subject to frequent change of emotion; emotionally unstable.|例句：The patient's blood pressure was labile. 病人的血压不稳定。

例句：He has a labile personality. 他性格易变。|近义词：unstable, volatile, fluctuating, changeable|反义词：stable, constant, fixed, immutable|词性:adjective

Etymology: From late 19th century: from French, from Latin labilis ‘liable to slip’, from labi ‘to slip’.
