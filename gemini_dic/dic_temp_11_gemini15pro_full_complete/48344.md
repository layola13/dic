decrumbs:/diːˈkrʌmz/
|n.(通常用复数形式)|面包屑；碎屑|Small fragments of bread or other food, or any small, loose particles.|The decrumbs from the toast fell on the floor. 烤面包屑掉在地上了.|crumbs, fragments, particles|whole, complete|noun
