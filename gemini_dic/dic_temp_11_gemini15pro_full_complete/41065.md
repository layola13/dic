copout:/ˈkɒpˌaʊt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpˌaʊt/ (US)| n.|逃避；推诿；回避|An act of avoiding or backing away from something, especially a commitment or responsibility.  A lame excuse.|He promised to help but copped out at the last minute. 他答应帮忙，但在最后一刻退缩了。|近义词：excuse, evasion, dodge, weasel out|反义词：commitment, follow-through|名词
