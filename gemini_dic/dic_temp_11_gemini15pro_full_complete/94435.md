implore:/ɪmˈplɔːr/ (US) /ɪmˈplɔː(r)/ (UK)| v. |恳求，哀求|To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|She implored him to stay. 她恳求他留下。|The refugees implored the soldiers for mercy.  难民们恳求士兵们发慈悲。| beseech, entreat, beg, plead|demand, command|verb
