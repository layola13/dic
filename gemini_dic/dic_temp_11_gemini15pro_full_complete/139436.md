obsidional:/əbˈsɪdiənəl/
adjective
围攻的，攻城的
Pertaining to a siege; used in or suitable for a siege.
The obsidional artillery bombarded the city walls. 攻城炮轰击了城墙。
The Roman army employed various obsidional tactics. 罗马军队使用了各种围攻战术。
Near synonyms: besieging, beleaguering
Antonyms: defensive, protective
词性: adjective
