furbishable:/ˈfɜːrbɪʃəbl/
adj.
可翻新的，可整修的
Capable of being refurbished; able to be restored to good condition.
The old house is furbishable, but it will take a lot of work. 这栋老房子可以翻新，但这需要很多工作。
The antique chair was furbishable and, with a little care, looked almost new. 这把古董椅子可以翻新，稍加修整，看起来几乎像新的一样。
近义词:renovatable, restorable, repairable
反义词:irreparable, unrepairable, ruined
词性:adjective
词源:From "furbish" (to brighten or polish) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  "Furbish" comes from Old French "fourbiss-"
