asymmetrical:/ˌeɪsɪˈmetrɪkl/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrɪkl/
adjective
不对称的；非对称的
Not identical on both sides of a central line; lacking symmetry.
Her dress had an asymmetrical hemline. 她的裙子有一个不对称的下摆。
uneven, lopsided, unbalanced, irregular
symmetrical, balanced, even, regular
词性: 形容词

asymmetry:/ˌeɪˈsɪmətri/ /ˌæsɪˈmətri/
noun
不对称；不均衡
Lack of equality or equivalence between parts or aspects of something; lack of symmetry.
The asymmetry of the building was deliberate. 这栋建筑的不对称是特意设计的。
imbalance, disproportion, unevenness, irregularity
symmetry, balance, evenness, regularity
词性: 名词
