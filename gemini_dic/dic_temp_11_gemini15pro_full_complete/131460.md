noncognizance:/nɒnˈkɒɡnɪzəns/
n.
不知; 未察觉; 不认知
lack of knowledge or awareness; failure to recognize something.
The defendant pleaded noncognizance of the charges against him.  被告声称对控告他的罪行不知情。
Near synonyms: ignorance, unawareness, incognizance, unconsciousness
Antonyms: awareness, knowledge, cognizance, recognition
noun
