outpowered:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərd/
Verb
胜过，压制，在力量上超过
defeated by having more power used against one.
Our team was completely outpowered by the opponents. 我们的队伍被对手完全压制了。
Synonyms: overpowered, overwhelmed, defeated, conquered, subdued
Antonyms: empowered, strengthened, aided, assisted
词性: 动词

outpowered:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərd/
Adjective
被压制的，力量不足的
defeated by having more power used against one.
The outpowered army retreated.  力量不足的军队撤退了。
Synonyms: overpowered, overwhelmed, defeated, conquered, subdued
Antonyms: empowered, strengthened, aided, assisted
词性: 形容词
