give: /ɡɪv/ (British) /ɡɪv/ (American) | v. |给，给予，赠送，提供，付出，捐献，举办，产生，让步，塌陷，弯曲|  To freely transfer the possession of (something) to (someone); to hand over to.| She gave me a book. 她给了我一本书。 | synonyms: offer, present, donate, provide, grant, bestow, confer, impart, yield, concede | antonyms: take, receive, keep, withhold, deny | verb

give: /ɡɪv/ (British) /ɡɪv/ (American) | n. |弹性，伸展性 | The ability of a material to bend or stretch. | The fabric had a lot of give. 这块布料很有弹性。 | synonyms: flexibility, elasticity, springiness | antonyms: rigidity, stiffness | noun


Etymology: From Old English *giefan, *gefan (West Saxon gifan), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch geven and German geben.
