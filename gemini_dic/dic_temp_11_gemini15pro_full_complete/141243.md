op-ed:/ˈɑːp ɛd/ | n. | 观点，评论文章 | An article that expresses the opinions of a writer who is not an employee of the publication.  It is typically placed opposite the editorial page. | The newspaper published an op-ed about the new healthcare law. 这家报纸发表了一篇关于新医保法的评论文章。 | synonym: opinion piece, commentary, column | antonym: news report, factual article | noun

op-ed:/ˈɒp ɛd/ | n. | 观点，评论文章 | An article that expresses the opinions of a writer who is not an employee of the publication.  It is typically placed opposite the editorial page. |  She wrote a compelling op-ed on climate change for the New York Times. 她为《纽约时报》撰写了一篇关于气候变化的引人注目的评论文章。 | synonym: viewpoint, essay, editorial | antonym: news report, factual article | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened from "opposite the editorial page."  Originally, these articles were literally placed on the page opposite the newspaper's own editorials.
