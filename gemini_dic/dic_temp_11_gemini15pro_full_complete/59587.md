ebrious:/ɪˈbriəs/| adj.| 醉醺醺的；兴高采烈的| Feeling or demonstrating an exaggerated sense of cheerfulness or wellbeing, often associated with drunkenness but not necessarily due to alcohol.|  He was ebrious after winning the lottery. 他中了彩票后欣喜若狂。|  She danced with ebrious abandon. 她狂喜地跳着舞。| 近义词：intoxicated, drunk, elated, exhilarated| 反义词：sober, depressed, sad| 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *ēbrius*, from *ē* ("out of") + *briāre* ("to be drunk"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\pih₁-* (“to drink”).
