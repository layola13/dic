overdebating:/ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度辩论，过度讨论
The act of debating or discussing something excessively, often to the point of being unproductive or counterproductive.
They were overdebating the merits of different coffee brands. 他们过度讨论不同咖啡品牌的优劣。
The committee was overdebating the issue and failed to reach a decision. 委员会过度讨论了这个问题，未能达成决定。
近义词: overdiscussing, overanalyzing
反义词: underdiscussing, skimping on debate
词性: Verb (present participle)
