outsumming:/aʊtˈsʌmɪŋ/
verb
超过…的总数；在…总数之外
surpassing in total; exceeding a sum
The benefits of the new system outsum the costs. 新系统的效益超过了成本。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, outstripping
反义词: falling short of, lagging behind
词性: 动词

词源:  "Out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "summing" (present participle of "to sum," meaning to calculate the total).  The word is formed by combining the prefix "out-" with the verb "summing," implying the act of surpassing a given sum or total.
