concur:/kənˈkɜːr/ (US), /kənˈkɜː(r)/ (UK)| v.| 同意，一致；同时发生|To agree with someone or something; to happen at the same time.|The committee concurred with the recommendations of the report. 委员会同意报告中的建议。

The two events concurred to produce a disastrous outcome. 这两个事件同时发生，导致了灾难性的后果。|近义词：agree, coincide, concur, converge|反义词：disagree, differ, clash|verb

concurrence:/kənˈkʌrəns/ (US), /kənˈkʌrəns/ (UK)| n.| 同意，一致；同时发生|Agreement or coincidence.|The concurrence of the board members was needed for the project to proceed.  项目进行需要董事会成员的一致同意。

The concurrence of several unfortunate events led to the crisis.  几个不幸事件的同时发生导致了危机。|近义词：agreement, coincidence, conjunction|反义词：disagreement, divergence|noun
