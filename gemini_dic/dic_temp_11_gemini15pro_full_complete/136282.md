nonreprehensibilities:/nɒnˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|无可指责性；无过失；清白|The state or quality of being nonreprehensible; blamelessness.|His nonreprehensibilities in the matter were eventually proven. 他在这件事上的清白最终得到了证明。|Synonyms: blamelessness, innocence, irreproachability, unimpeachability|Antonyms: culpability, guilt, blameworthiness|Noun

Etymology:

* **non-**:  Latin prefix meaning "not."
* **reprehensible**: From Late Latin *reprehensibilis*, from Latin *reprehendere* "to blame, censure," from *re-* "back" + *prehendere* "to grasp, seize."
* **-ity**: suffix forming nouns of quality or condition.
* **-ies**: suffix forming plural nouns.
