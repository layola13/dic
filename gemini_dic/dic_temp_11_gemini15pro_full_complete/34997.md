cloisterly: /ˈklɔɪstərli/ (US) /ˈklɔɪstəli/ (UK)| adj.| 修道院般的；隐居的；与世隔绝的|Resembling or suggestive of a cloister; secluded from the world; sheltered.|例句：He led a cloisterly life dedicated to scholarship.  他过着一种隐居的学术生活。

例句：The library had a cloisterly atmosphere, quiet and conducive to study.  图书馆里有一种修道院般的气氛，安静且有助于学习。|近义词：secluded, reclusive, solitary, withdrawn, isolated|反义词：gregarious, sociable, outgoing, extroverted|词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From *cloister* + *-ly*.  *Cloister* comes from Old French *cloistre*, from Latin *claustrum* "lock, bolt, bar, barrier," hence also "monastery, convent" (because of the enclosed life).
