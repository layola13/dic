blackball:/ˈblækˌbɔːl/| v. n.|投反对票；阻止；排斥；黑球|To reject (someone or something, such as an application) especially by secret vote. A negative vote cast by dropping a black ball into a ballot box; also : a ball so used.|The committee blackballed his application to join the club. 委员会投了他的反对票，阻止他加入俱乐部。|近义词：reject, veto, ostracize, exclude|反义词：accept, approve, admit, include|词性:动词/名词
blackballs:/ˈblækˌbɔːlz/| n. |反对票；黑球（复数）|Plural form of blackball, referring to the black balls used in a vote or the negative votes themselves.|Two blackballs were enough to prevent her from joining the society.  两张反对票就足以阻止她加入这个社团。|近义词：rejections, vetoes|反义词：approvals, acceptances|词性:名词

词源:
From the practice of using a black ball in secret voting to indicate opposition.  源于秘密投票中使用黑球表示反对的做法。
