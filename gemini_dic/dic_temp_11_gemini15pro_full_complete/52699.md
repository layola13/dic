digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n.|二合字母；双字母|A digraph is a combination of two letters representing one sound, such as "sh" in "ship" or "ea" in "head".|The digraph "ch" can be pronounced differently in different words.|近义词：ligature|反义词:|noun
