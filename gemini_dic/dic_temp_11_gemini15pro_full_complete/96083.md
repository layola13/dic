indefeasibility:/ˌɪndɪˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/| n.|不可废止，不可推翻，不可剥夺|The indefeasibility of title is a cornerstone of property law.  产权的不可剥夺性是财产法的基石。|The lawyer argued for the indefeasibility of his client's claim to the land. 律师辩称他的客户对这块土地的权利不可推翻。|近义词：incontestability, inviolability|反义词：defeasibility, vulnerability|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *indefeasibilitas, from indefeasibilis (“indefeasible”) + -itas (“-ity”).  Ultimately from Latin in- (“not”) + de- (“away, off”) + *facere (“to do, make”) (from Proto-Italic *fakō, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to set, put”)).
