leavened:/ˈlevənd/| adj.|发酵的，膨松的|Caused to rise and become light and porous by the action of yeast or other leavening agent.|The baker used leavened dough to make the bread. 面包师用发酵的面团做面包。|Synonyms: fermented, raised, aerated|Antonyms: unleavened, flat, dense|adjective
