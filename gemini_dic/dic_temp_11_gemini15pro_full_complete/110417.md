like-mindedly:/laɪkˈmaɪndɪdli/
|adv.|志同道合地；想法一致地|In a way that shows similar opinions or interests.  Sharing the same opinions and interests.|The group like-mindedly agreed to pursue the project.  该小组一致同意推进这个项目。|Synonym: harmoniously, unanimously, concordantly|Antonym: discordantly, differently, divergently|词性: 副词

like-minded:/laɪkˈmaɪndɪd/
|adj.|志同道合的；想法一致的|Having similar opinions or interests.|She found like-minded friends in the book club.  她在读书俱乐部找到了志同道合的朋友。|Synonym: compatible, kindred, congruent|Antonym: incompatible, uncongenial, dissimilar|词性: 形容词

词源：
like (similar) + minded (having a particular mind or opinion) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
