aluminiferous:/ˌæljʊmɪˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含铝的；产铝的
Containing alumina or aluminum.
例句：The soil in this region is aluminiferous, making it suitable for bauxite mining.  这个地区的土壤富含铝，适合开采铝土矿。

例句：Aluminiferous rocks are a primary source of aluminum ore.  含铝岩石是铝矿石的主要来源。

近义词：aluminum-rich, aluminum-bearing
反义词：non-aluminiferous (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aluminifer*, from *alumen* (alum) + *-fer* (bearing).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\h₂el-* (bitter, salt).
