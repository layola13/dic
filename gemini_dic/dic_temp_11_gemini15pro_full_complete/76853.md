Ganges:/ˈɡændʒiːz/
n.
恒河
A large river in northern India and Bangladesh, sacred to Hindus.
The Ganges is a sacred river for Hindus.  恒河是印度教徒的圣河。
The pollution of the Ganges is a serious problem. 恒河的污染是一个严重的问题。
近义词: Ganga (another name for the Ganges)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific river)
词性: noun
