affordably:/əˈfɔːrdəbli/
adverb|经济实惠地；能够负担得起地|In a way that is not expensive|We ate affordably at the local cafe. 我们在当地咖啡馆吃得很实惠。|cheaply, reasonably, economically|expensively, extravagantly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From afford + -ably.  "Afford" comes from Old English *geforðian "to advance, promote, perform, accomplish, execute," from forð "forth, forward."  The suffix "-ably" comes from Old French -able and Latin -abilis, meaning "capable of being."
