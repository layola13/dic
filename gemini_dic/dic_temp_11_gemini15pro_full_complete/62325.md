endeavour: /ɪnˈdɛvə(r)/ (British) /ɪnˈdɛvər/ (American)| n. v.|努力，尽力，尝试|An attempt to achieve a goal.|例句：He endeavoured to finish the project on time. 他努力按时完成项目。|近义词：attempt, try, strive, aspire|反义词：neglect, abandon|词性:名词, 动词

endeavours: /ɪnˈdɛvə(r)z/ (British) /ɪnˈdɛvərz/ (American)| n. |努力，尽力，尝试（复数）|Attempts to achieve a goal.|例句：Her endeavours to improve her skills were successful. 她努力提高技能的尝试成功了。|近义词：attempts, tries, efforts|反义词：failures, inaction|词性:名词

词源:  From Old French *endevoir (modern French devoir) "duty, that which one owes,"  from Vulgar Latin *de habere "to have from,"  from Latin debere "to owe." Originally meant "to put oneself under an obligation,"  later "to exert oneself, try hard."
