nakora: /nəˈkɔːrə/ | n. | 纳科拉鼓 |  A double-sided barrel drum from South India, used in Carnatic music.  It is similar to the tavil, but smaller and played with sticks rather than hands. | The nakora accompanied the vocalist beautifully. 纳科拉鼓优美地伴奏着歌手。 | Synonym: barrel drum, sometimes tavil (though not exactly the same) | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence or a different instrument like a flute) | noun
