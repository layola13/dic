nonpressure:/ˌnɒnˈprɛʃə(r)/ | adj. | 无压力的，非加压的 |  Characterized by or involving a lack of pressure, especially in a competitive or stressful situation. |  The team played a nonpressure exhibition game before the season started. 球队在赛季开始前进行了一场没有压力的表演赛。|  The atmosphere at the picnic was very nonpressure. 野餐的气氛非常轻松自在。| 近义词: relaxed, casual, informal, easygoing, low-key | 反义词: pressure, stressful, intense, demanding | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
pressure (noun): from Old French *presser "to press," from Latin pressare "to press, press down, squeeze," from premere "to press"
