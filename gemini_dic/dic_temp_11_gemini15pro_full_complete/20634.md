blackly:/ˈblækli/| adv.|阴郁地，恶狠狠地，不祥地|In a dark or threatening way; gloomily or angrily.  Derived from "black" with the adverbial suffix "-ly."  Old English *blæclic.|He stared blackly at the empty page. 他阴郁地盯着空白页。|Synonyms: gloomily, sullenly, darkly, morosely, ominously|Antonyms: brightly, cheerfully, hopefully|adverb
