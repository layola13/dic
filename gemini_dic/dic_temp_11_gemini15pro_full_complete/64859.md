espouse:/ɪˈspaʊz/ | v. | 支持；拥护；信奉（信仰、学说等）；嫁娶（古语） | to support or adopt a cause, belief, or way of life; to marry (archaic) |  He espoused the cause of animal rights. 他支持动物权利事业。 | synonyms: adopt, embrace, support, champion, advocate, marry | antonyms: reject, renounce, oppose, abandon | verb

espoused:/ɪˈspaʊzd/ | adj. | 被拥护的；被信奉的 | adopted or supported |  His espoused beliefs were often controversial. 他所信奉的理念经常引起争议。 | synonyms: adopted, embraced, supported | antonyms: rejected, renounced, opposed | adjective
