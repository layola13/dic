airflow:/ˈɛərfloʊ/ (US) /ˈɛəfləʊ/ (UK)| n.|气流|The movement of air, especially a current of air in a particular direction or within a particular space. |例句：The airflow through the vent was strong. 通风口的气流很强。

例句：The architect designed the building to maximize natural airflow. 建筑师设计了这座建筑以最大限度地利用自然气流。|近义词：draft, current, stream, ventilation|反义词:stasis, stagnation|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From air + flow.  "Air" comes from Old French "air" and ultimately from Latin "aer" (air). "Flow" comes from Old English "flowan" (to flow).
