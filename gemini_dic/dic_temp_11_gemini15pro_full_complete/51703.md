devis:/dəˈviː/ (UK), /dəˈvɪs/ (US)| n.|估价; 预算; 设计; (尤指)设备; (复数)devises|An estimate, especially of the cost of work to be done; a plan or design, especially one for a piece of equipment. |The builder gave me a devis for the renovations. 建筑商给了我一份翻修工程的估价单。|Synonyms: estimate, quotation, tender, proposal, plan, design, scheme, project|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider words like "improvisation" or "lack of planning" as contrasting concepts)|noun
