counteracting:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪŋ/
verb
抵消；中和；对抗
acting in opposition to something in order to reduce its force or effect.
The medication is designed to counteract the effects of the poison. 这种药物旨在抵消毒药的作用。
synonyms: neutralize, offset, counterbalance, negate
antonyms: reinforce, augment, enhance, exacerbate
词性: 动词

counteract:/ˌkaʊntərˈækt/
verb
抵消；中和；对抗
act against (something) in order to reduce its force or neutralize it.
Drinking plenty of water counteracts the dehydrating effects of hot weather.  多喝水可以抵消炎热天气造成的脱水作用。
synonyms: neutralize, offset, counterbalance, negate
antonyms: reinforce, augment, enhance, exacerbate
词性: 动词
