buckwheat:/ˈbʌkwiːt/ | n. |荞麦|A plant cultivated for its triangular seeds, which are ground into flour and used in pancakes, noodles, and other foods. Also refers to the flour itself.| I love buckwheat pancakes with maple syrup. 我喜欢枫糖浆荞麦煎饼。|Synonyms:  kasha (specifically roasted buckwheat groats) | Antonyms: wheat, rice (as alternative grains)|noun

buckwheat:/ˈbʌkwiːt/ | adj. |荞麦的|Made from or containing buckwheat.|  She bought a bag of buckwheat flour. 她买了一袋荞麦粉。|Synonyms:  kasha (specifically relating to roasted buckwheat groats) | Antonyms:  wheat, rice (as alternative grain adjectives - "wheaten", "rice")|adjective
