deputise:/ˈdɛpjʊtaɪz/
| v. | 代理；代表；替…执行任务 | acts or speaks for another person; substitutes for another person |  He deputised for the chairman at the meeting. 他在会议上代表主席。| synonyms: substitutes, stands in, covers, represents | antonyms: leads, directs, heads | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French député, from Latin deputatus, past participle of deputare (“to assign, appoint, select”), from de- (“down, away”) + putare (“to think, estimate, prune, reckon”).
