hasteners:/ˈheɪsənərz/ | n. |促进剂，催化剂|Substances that accelerate a process, particularly a chemical reaction or other natural process. |The use of hasteners in the concrete allowed it to set quickly. 在混凝土中使用促凝剂使其快速凝固。| The farmer used hasteners to speed up the ripening of the fruit. 农民使用催熟剂来加速水果的成熟。 | synonyms: accelerators, catalysts, expediters | antonyms: inhibitors, retarders | noun
