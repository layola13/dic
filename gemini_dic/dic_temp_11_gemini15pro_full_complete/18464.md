bemuffle:/bɪˈmʌfəl/
verb
使消声；使低沉；裹住；蒙住
to wrap or cover something so that it makes less noise or is less noticeable; to muffle someone's voice
例句：
The heavy curtains bemuffled the sound of the traffic outside.  厚重的窗帘使外面的交通噪音减弱了。

She bemuffled her baby in a warm blanket. 她用温暖的毯子把婴儿裹了起来。

近义词：muffle, stifle, deaden, mute
反义词：amplify, increase, enhance
词性:v.
