eschewer:/ɪˈʃuːər/ /ɛˈʃuːər/ | n. |回避者；避开者|A person who deliberately avoids or abstains from something.|He is an eschewer of all things technological. 他是一个回避所有科技事物的人。|近义词：avoider, shunner, abstainer|反义词：embracer, pursuer, seeker|词性:名词

eschew:/ɪˈʃuː/ /ɛˈʃuː/ | vt. |避免；避开；回避|Deliberately avoid using; abstain from.|She eschewed social media to focus on her studies. 她为了专注于学习而避免使用社交媒体。|近义词：avoid, shun, abstain from, forgo, renounce|反义词：embrace, pursue, seek, indulge in|词性:动词
