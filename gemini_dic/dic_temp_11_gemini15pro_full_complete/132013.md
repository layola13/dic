noncorrectives:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɛktɪvz/
noun
非矫正措施
Actions or measures that do not aim to correct a problem, but rather to manage or mitigate its effects.
The report suggested implementing several noncorrectives to minimize the impact of the delays.  该报告建议实施一些非矫正措施，以尽量减少延误的影响。
synonyms:  mitigating measures, palliative measures, preventative measures
antonyms:  corrective actions, remedial actions
词性: 名词
