defensibly:/dɪˈfɛnsɪbli/
adjective
可辩护地，可防御地
In a manner that can be justified or defended; in a way that can be protected from attack or criticism.
His actions, while controversial, were defensibly motivated by a desire to protect his family.  他行为虽然有争议，但出于保护家人的愿望，是情有可原的。

The city is defensibly located on a hilltop. 这座城市位于易守难攻的山顶上。
synonyms: justifiably, reasonably, legitimately, warrantably, vindicably
antonyms: indefensibly, unjustifiably, unreasonably
