giantest:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪst/ | adj. | 最巨大的 |  A non-standard superlative form of "giant," meaning extremely large or of great size. While grammatically incorrect, it's sometimes used for emphasis in informal contexts.  |  He claimed to have seen the giantest spider ever! 他声称见过最大的蜘蛛！|  largest, hugest, biggest, enormous, massive | smallest, tiniest, minute | adjective

Etymology:
From giant + -est (superlative suffix).  "Giant" comes from the Old French "geant," ultimately from the Greek "gigas."
