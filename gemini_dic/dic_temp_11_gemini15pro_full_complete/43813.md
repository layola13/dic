crenulation:/ˌkrenjʊˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 城垛；锯齿状；【生】圆锯齿 | A series of small, regular, rectangular projections on a wall or other surface, like the battlements of a castle. Also refers to a similar scalloped edge or pattern. | The crenulations of the castle walls provided excellent defensive positions for archers. 城堡城墙的锯齿状结构为弓箭手提供了绝佳的防御位置。 | 近义词: battlement, indentation, notching, scalloping | 反义词: smoothness | 词性: noun

crenulate:/ˈkrenjʊleɪt/ | v. | 使成锯齿状；用城垛围绕 | To furnish with crenulations; to make a scalloped edge or pattern. | The frost crenulated the edges of the leaves. 霜使叶子的边缘呈锯齿状。 | 近义词: notch, scallop, indent | 反义词: smooth | 词性: verb

crenulated:/ˈkrenjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 锯齿状的；有圆锯齿的 | Having crenulations; scalloped. | The crenulated coastline offered numerous sheltered coves. 锯齿状的海岸线提供了许多隐蔽的小海湾。 | 近义词: notched, scalloped, indented | 反义词: smooth | 词性: adjective
