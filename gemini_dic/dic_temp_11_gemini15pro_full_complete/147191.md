overlying:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 上覆的，覆盖的 | Situated on top of something else; superimposed. | The overlying rock formation was primarily shale. 上覆的岩层主要是页岩。|  The map shows the overlying vegetation.  地图显示了上面的植被。| 近义词:superjacent, incumbent, covering, overlying | 反义词:underlying, subjacent | 词性:形容词

词源: From overlie, from Old English oferliġan (“to lie over”).  "Over-" (OE ofer) + "lie" (OE licgan).
