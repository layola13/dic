doorkeeper:/ˈdɔːrˌkiːpər/ (US), /ˈdɔːˌkiːpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|门卫，看门人|A person who guards or controls access to a building or room.|例句：The doorkeeper wouldn't let us in without tickets.  门卫不让我们进去，因为我们没有票。

例句：The hotel doorkeeper greeted us warmly.  酒店的门卫热情地迎接了我们。

近义词：gatekeeper, guard, watchman, custodian|反义词:|词性: noun
