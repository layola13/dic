ETYMOLOGY->
"landline" comes from "land" + "line."  "Land" refers to the fixed location of the phone being connected to the earth, as opposed to mobile. "Line" refers to the physical telephone line. "Phone" is a shortened form of "telephone," which comes from Greek "tele" (far) + "phone" (sound).
