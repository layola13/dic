overextracting:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræktɪŋ/
|v.|过度萃取|The act of extracting too much of a substance, such as flavor from coffee or tea, or minerals from the earth.|例句：Overextracting the coffee grounds resulted in a bitter brew. 过度萃取咖啡粉导致咖啡很苦。

例句：The company was accused of overextracting resources from the rainforest. 这家公司被指控过度开采雨林资源。
|近义词：overdrawing, overuse, depleting|反义词:underextracting|词性:动词


overextracted:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræktɪd/
|adj.|过度萃取的|Describing something that has had too much of a substance extracted from it.|例句：The overextracted tea was weak and flavorless.  过度萃取的茶又淡又没有味道。

例句：The soil was overextracted and no longer fertile. 土壤被过度开采，不再肥沃。
|近义词：depleted, exhausted|反义词:underextracted|词性:形容词


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "extract" (from Latin "extractus," past participle of "extrahere" meaning "to draw out").
