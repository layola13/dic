libertys:/ˈlɪbərtiz/
n.
自由，自由权 (This is generally considered an incorrect plural form. The standard plural of "liberty" is "liberties")
 liberties are the state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions imposed by authority on one's way of life, behaviour, or political views.  (This definition is for the standard singular or plural form.)
 He fought for the liberties of his people. 他为人民的自由而战。
Synonym: freedoms, rights
Antonym: restrictions, constraints
Noun
