madbrain:/ˈmædbreɪn/| n.|疯狂的想法；疯狂的计划；不切实际的想法|A madbrain scheme or idea is impractical, crazy, or foolish. | He had a madbrain scheme to get rich quick. 他有一个快速致富的疯狂计划。

|近义词：crazy idea, harebrained scheme, wild scheme, foolish plan|反义词：sensible plan, practical idea, sound plan|词性:noun


madbrain:/ˈmædbreɪn/| adj.|疯狂的；不切实际的；愚蠢的|Characterized by being impractical, crazy, or foolish. | That's a madbrain idea, it will never work. 那是一个疯狂的想法，永远不会成功。

|近义词：crazy, foolish, harebrained, wild|反义词：sensible, practical, sound|词性:adjective


词源:  Compound of 'mad' (meaning insane or foolish) and 'brain' (the organ of thought).  This creates a straightforward and easily understood meaning.
