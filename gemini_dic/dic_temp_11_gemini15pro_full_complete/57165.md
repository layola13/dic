dowdyism:/ˈdaʊdiɪzəm/
n.
过时的衣着；衣着邋遢；缺乏时尚感
The state or quality of being dowdy; dowdiness.  A style or fashion that is dowdy.
Her dowdyism made her stand out in the fashionable crowd. 她的衣着邋遢让她在时尚的人群中显得格格不入。
近义词：dowdiness, frumpiness, shabbiness
反义词：stylishness, chic, elegance
词性：名词

dowdy:/ˈdaʊdi/
adj.
(尤指女人)衣着过时的，邋遢的；缺乏时尚的
(especially of a woman) not stylishly or fashionably dressed
She looked dowdy in that old dress. 她穿着那件旧裙子显得邋遢。
近义词：frumpy, shabby, unfashionable
反义词：stylish, chic, fashionable
词性：形容词


词源:  From dowdy + -ism.  Dowdy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to dialectal  dowd, meaning "a dull, heavy, or slovenly woman."
