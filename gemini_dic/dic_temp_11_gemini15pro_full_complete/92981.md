idiograph:/ˈɪdiəɡræf/ | n. | 表意文字，单字表意符号 | A written symbol that represents a complete word or phrase without indicating its pronunciation, as opposed to a phonogram. | Example 1: Chinese characters are considered idiographs.  汉字被认为是表意文字。

Example 2:  Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs are another example of an idiograph system. 古埃及象形文字是表意文字系统的另一个例子。 | Synonym: logogram, ideogram | Antonym: phonogram | noun
