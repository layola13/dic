agouti:/əˈɡuːti/ | adj. n. |  刺豚鼠色的，（毛发）尖端颜色较浅的，刺豚鼠 |  Of a brownish grey colour, typically with the hairs banded with black and yellow so that the overall effect is grizzled.  A rodent of the genus *Dasyprocta*, found in Central and South America and the West Indies. |  The agouti's fur is a beautiful mix of brown, black, and yellow. 刺豚鼠的皮毛是棕色、黑色和黄色的美丽混合。 | The agouti scurried through the undergrowth. 刺豚鼠窜过灌木丛。 | 近义词: grizzled, brindled (for coat color), rodent (for the animal) | 反义词: (No direct antonym for the color.  For the animal, perhaps "predator" in a loose ecological sense.) | 词性: adjective, noun
