nonpropagandists: /ˌnɑːnprɑːpəˈɡændɪsts/ | n. |非宣传者；不进行宣传的人| People who do not spread propaganda.  Those who do not engage in promoting particular doctrines or principles. |例句：The group consisted of journalists, academics, and other nonpropagandists.  这个团体由记者、学者和其他非宣传人员组成。

例句：Nonpropagandists often focus on objective reporting and analysis. 非宣传人员通常专注于客观报道和分析。| 近义词：neutrals,  objectivists | 反义词: propagandists, advocates | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "propagandist" (from "propaganda" ultimately from Latin "propagare" - to propagate or spread)
