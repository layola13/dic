devised:/dɪˈvaɪzd/
verb
设计；发明；策划；想出
Past tense and past participle of devise.  It means to create, invent, or plan something.
The engineer devised a new method for purifying water.  工程师设计了一种新的净水方法。
They devised a plan to escape from prison. 他们策划了一个越狱计划。

近义词:created, invented, planned, formulated, engineered, designed, conceived, developed
反义词:copied, imitated, borrowed, plagiarized
词性:Verb (Past Participle)
词源:From Old French deviser "to divide, distribute, arrange, contrive," from Late Latin divisare "to apportion, distribute."
