monoenergetic:/ˌmɒnəʊˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪk/ /ˌmɑːnoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
单能的;  单能量的
Having a single energy; consisting of particles or waves of a single energy.
Monoenergetic beams of electrons are used in some types of radiotherapy.  单能电子束用于某些类型的放射治疗。
Monoenergetic gamma rays were emitted from the source. 从源头发射出单能伽马射线。
近义词:homochromatic, monochromatic
反义词:polyenergetic
词性:adjective
