grindstones:/ˈɡraɪndˌstoʊnz/ (US), /ˈɡraɪndstəʊnz/ (UK)| n.|磨刀石|A thick revolving disk of stone or other abrasive material used for grinding, sharpening, or polishing metal objects.|He sharpened his axe on the grindstone. 他在磨刀石上磨斧头。|synonyms:  whetstone, hone, sharpener|antonyms: duller|noun
