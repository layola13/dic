cairn:/kɛrn/ | n. | 石冢，堆石路标 | A heap of stones piled up as a landmark, monument, or memorial. |  Hikers often use cairns to mark trails in mountainous areas.  登山者经常用石冢来标记山区的路径。| synonyms: monument, marker, memorial | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "path" or "road" as something that replaces the need for a cairn) | noun

cairn:/kɛrn/ | n. | (苏格兰)石堆，石屋 | (Scottish) A rough shelter of stones. |  They found refuge from the storm in a small cairn.  他们在一个小石屋里躲避了暴风雨。| synonyms: shelter, refuge | antonyms: exposure, unsheltered | noun
