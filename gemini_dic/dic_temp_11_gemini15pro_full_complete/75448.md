fruition:/fruːˈɪʃən/
n.
实现; 完成; 结果实; 成熟
the point at which a plan or project becomes real or complete
例句：After years of hard work, her plans finally came to fruition. 经过多年的努力，她的计划终于实现了。

例句：The project is finally coming to fruition. 这个项目终于要实现了。

近义词：realization, accomplishment, achievement, completion, fulfillment
反义词：failure, frustration, abortion

词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English fruicioun, from Old French fruicion, from Latin fructiō (“enjoyment, delight in”), from fructus (“fruit, enjoyment, profit”), from fruor (“I enjoy”).
