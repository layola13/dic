obtrusionist: /əbˈtruːʒənɪst/
obtrusionist: n.
obtrusionist: 闯入者，打扰者
obtrusionist: Someone who obtrudes, or forces their presence or opinions on others.
obtrusionist: The obtrusionist interrupted the meeting with irrelevant comments.  这个闯入者用不相关的评论打断了会议。
obtrusionist: The obtrusionist insisted on joining the private conversation.  这个打扰者坚持要加入私人谈话。
obtrusionist: intruder, interloper, meddler, busybody
obtrusionist: recluse, introvert
