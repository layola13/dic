odiums:/ˈoʊdiəmz/
n.
憎恶，仇恨
intense hatred or dislike directed toward someone or something considered despicable or vile.
The dictator incurred the odiums of the people for his tyrannical rule.  独裁者因其暴虐的统治而招致人民的憎恨。
The odiums he faced after the scandal forced him to resign.  丑闻发生后，他面临的仇恨迫使他辞职。
近义词: hatred, loathing, detestation, abhorrence, animosity
反义词: love, admiration, respect, esteem
