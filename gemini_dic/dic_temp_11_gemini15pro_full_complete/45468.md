curatorships:/kjʊˈreɪtərʃɪps/| n. |策展工作，策展职位|The position and responsibilities of a curator, often involving the acquisition, care, and interpretation of museum or gallery collections.  It can also refer to the period of time during which someone holds this position.|She held several prestigious curatorships throughout her career. 她在职业生涯中担任过多个享有盛誉的策展职位。|Synonyms: curation, custodianship, guardianship|Antonyms: neglect, abandonment|noun
