bisectionally: /baɪˈsɛkʃənəli/
adv. |二等分地，对半分地| In a manner that bisects something; dividing something into two equal parts. | The line bisects the circle bisectionally.  这条线二等分地平分了这个圆。| Equally, symmetrically |Unequally, asymmetrically |adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'bisection' + '-ally'.  'Bisection' comes from Latin 'bisectio', from 'bisecare' (to cut in two), from 'bi-' (two) + 'secare' (to cut). '-ally' is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
