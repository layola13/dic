antiphrastical: /ˌæntɪˈfræstɪkəl/
| adj. | 反语的，讽刺的 | Using words in a sense opposite to their proper meaning; ironic. |  He used the term "genius" antiphrastically to describe his clumsy friend. 他反讽地用“天才”来形容他笨拙的朋友。 | Example:  She described the messy room as "immaculately clean," clearly speaking antiphrastically. 她把凌乱的房间描述成“一尘不染”，显然是在说反话。| Synonyms: ironic, sarcastic, sardonic | Antonyms: sincere, genuine, earnest | word type: adjective
