euterpean:/juːˈtɜːrpiən/
adjective
优美的，和谐的，音乐的
Relating to music or the muse of music, Euterpe.  Inspiring or promoting happiness or optimism.
例句：The euterpean sounds of the orchestra filled the concert hall.  管弦乐队优美的乐声充满了音乐厅。

例句：Her euterpean nature made her a joy to be around. 她乐观的天性使人乐于和她相处。
近义词：musical, harmonious, melodious, sweet-sounding, euphonious, cheerful, optimistic
反义词：discordant, cacophonous, depressing, pessimistic
词性：adjective
词源：From Greek *Euterpē*, the Muse of music and lyric poetry.
