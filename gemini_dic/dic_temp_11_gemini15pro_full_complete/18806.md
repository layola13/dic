berserker:/ˈbɜːrsɜːrkər/ /bərˈzɜːrkər/| n.| 狂战士；暴怒的人|A fierce warrior, especially one of the ancient Norse warriors, who fought with frenzied rage.|He fought like a berserker, ignoring the pain of his wounds.|近义词：wildman, maniac, madman, fiend|反义词:pacifist, dove|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *berserkr, from bjǫrn "bear" + serkr "shirt, coat" (i.e., one who wears a bearskin).
