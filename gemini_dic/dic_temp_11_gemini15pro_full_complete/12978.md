astigmatism: /əˈstɪɡmətɪzəm/ | n. | 散光 | A defect in the eye or in a lens caused by a deviation from spherical curvature, which results in distorted images, as light rays are prevented from meeting at a common focus. |  I have astigmatism and need to wear glasses. 我有散光，需要戴眼镜。 | Myopia, nearsightedness, blurry vision |  Perfect vision, 20/20 vision | noun
