indomitable: /ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbəl/ (UK) /ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbl/ (US)| adj.|不屈不挠的，不可征服的|Incapable of being subdued or overcome; unconquerable.|例句：She showed indomitable spirit in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中表现出不屈不挠的精神。

例句：His indomitable courage inspired his followers. 他不屈不挠的勇气鼓舞了他的追随者。

近义词：unyielding, resolute, steadfast, tenacious|反义词：weak, yielding, submissive|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *indomitabilis*, from in- "not" + domitabilis "tamable," from domitare "to tame," frequentative of domare "to tame, subdue."
