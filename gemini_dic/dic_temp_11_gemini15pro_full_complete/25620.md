bumptious:/ˈbʌmpʃəs/| adj.|傲慢的，自以为是的| Offensively self-assertive; conceited.  Characterized by excessive and often noisy self-assertion. | He was so bumptious that nobody liked him. 他太傲慢了，所以没有人喜欢他。

|  The bumptious young man annoyed everyone with his bragging.  这个自以为是的年轻人吹嘘自己，惹恼了所有人。

|  Synonyms: arrogant, conceited, self-important, pompous, overbearing, swaggering, presumptuous | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, meek, diffident | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bump (meaning "to strike or collide with") + -tious (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "having the quality of").  The original sense was "apt to bump against others," hence "pushy, forward."
