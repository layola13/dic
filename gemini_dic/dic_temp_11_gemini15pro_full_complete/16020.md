bananas:/bəˈnænəz/| n.|香蕉|A long curved fruit with a yellow skin that grows in hot countries.  The fruit can be eaten raw or cooked.|例句：I eat two bananas every morning. 我每天早上吃两根香蕉。

例句：The monkeys love to eat bananas. 猴子喜欢吃香蕉。|近义词：plantain (cooking banana) |反义词: (No direct antonym for a fruit) |词性:noun


bananas:/bəˈnænəz/| adj. informal|疯狂的，精神错乱的|Insane, mad.|例句：He went bananas when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后疯了。

例句：This traffic is driving me bananas. 交通堵塞让我抓狂。|近义词：crazy, mad, insane|反义词:sane, rational|词性:adjective
