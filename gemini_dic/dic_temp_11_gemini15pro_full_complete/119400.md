microfabrication:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |微加工，微制造| The process of fabricating miniature structures of micrometer scales.  This involves techniques like photolithography, etching, and deposition.| Microfabrication techniques are crucial for creating microchips. 微加工技术对于制造微芯片至关重要。| 近义词:micromanufacturing, micromachining | 反义词:macrofabrication (if used, generally implies traditional manufacturing)| noun
