carry-the-ball:/ˈkæri ðə bɔːl/| idiom|承担责任，负起主要责任|To take responsibility for something and lead the way.|例句：He really carried the ball on that project. 他在那个项目中真的承担了主要责任。|近义词：take charge, take the lead, be responsible for|反义词：shirk responsibility, pass the buck| idiom
carry-the-ball:/ˈkæri ðə bɔːl/| idiom|推进，取得进展|To make progress, especially in a difficult situation.|例句：We need someone to carry the ball and get this project moving. 我们需要有人来推进这个项目，让它取得进展。|近义词：make progress, move forward, advance|反义词：stall, stagnate| idiom
