anagogics:/ˌænəˈɡɒdʒɪks/ | n. |神秘的解释，精神上的意义，尤其是指圣经经文的比喻性解释|The anagogics of the text revealed a hidden spiritual meaning. 这段文字的神秘解释揭示了一种隐藏的精神意义。|例句：The preacher focused on the anagogics of the story of Jonah. 传教士专注于约拿故事的神秘意义。

例句：Students of medieval literature often study the four levels of meaning in texts: literal, allegorical, moral, and anagogical. 中世纪文学的学生经常研究文本的四层含义：字面意义、寓言意义、道德意义和神秘意义。|近义词：mystical interpretation, spiritual meaning, allegorical interpretation|反义词：literal interpretation, surface meaning|词性: 名词
