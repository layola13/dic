go-between:/ˈɡəʊ bɪˌtwiːn/| n.|中间人，媒人|A person who communicates between two parties, often to help them reach an agreement or resolve a dispute.|She acted as a go-between for the two warring families. 她充当了两个交战家族的中间人。|Synonyms: intermediary, mediator, negotiator, liaison|Antonyms: adversary, opponent|noun


go-between:/ˈɡəʊ bɪˌtwiːn/| adj.|作为中间人的，媒介的|Acting as a link between two parties.|He played a go-between role in the negotiations. 他在谈判中扮演了中间人的角色。|Synonyms: intermediary, mediating|Antonyms: direct|adjective
