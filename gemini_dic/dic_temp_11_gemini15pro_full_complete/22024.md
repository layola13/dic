boisterous:/ˈbɔɪstərəs/ | adj. |喧闹的；狂暴的；充满活力的|Full of noisy enthusiasm and energy; rowdy.|  The children were boisterous during the party. 孩子们在聚会上很喧闹。|  The boisterous crowd cheered for their team. 喧闹的人群为他们的队伍欢呼。| 近义词: rowdy, noisy, rambunctious, loud, uproarious | 反义词: quiet, calm, peaceful, silent, subdued | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Middle English *boistous, *boistose, from Anglo-Norman boistous, alteration of Old French boisteus “lame, crippled,” of uncertain origin.  The sense shift from "clumsy" to "rough, noisy" occurred by the 16th century.
