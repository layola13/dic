fermata:/ferˈmɑːtə/ (US) /fɛrˈmɑːtə/ (UK)| n.|延长记号，休止|A symbol (𝄐) placed over a note or rest indicating that it is to be held for longer than its normal time value, the length of the extension being left to the performer's discretion.  It is also called a hold, pause, or grand pause. |The conductor indicated a fermata on the final chord. 指挥在最后一个和弦上标示了延长记号。|近义词：hold, pause, grand pause|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'release' in a musical context)|noun
