ait:/eɪt/
|n.|小岛；河中小岛|A small island, especially in a river or lake.|The boat landed on a small ait in the middle of the river. 船停靠在河中央的一个小岛上。|近义词:islet, island|反义词:mainland|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *eyte, from Old English *īgeþ, *īgoþ (“eyot”), from Proto-Germanic *aujō (“island in a river”). Cognate with Old Norse ey (“island”), Swedish ö (“island”), Danish ø (“island”). Doublet of eyot.
