boohoo:/ˈbuːhuː/| int. n.| 哭泣，假哭；痛哭流涕| An exclamation imitating the sound of crying, often used to express mock sympathy or derision.  A crying fit; a spell of weeping.|  例句： "Boohoo," she said sarcastically, "you'll get over it."  “哼哼，”她讽刺地说，“你会克服的。”

例句：He had a real boohoo after his favorite toy broke. 他心爱的玩具坏了之后，他痛哭了一场。|近义词：cry, weep, sob, whine, moan|反义词:laugh, rejoice, celebrate|词性:感叹词, 名词

词源:  Late 19th century, imitative.
