melanconiales:/ˌmɛlənˈkoʊniˌeɪliz/ /ˌmɛlæŋˈkəʊnɪˌeɪliːz/ | n. | 黑盘孢目 | An order of fungi in the class Dothideomycetes characterized by acervular conidiomata.  The conidia are produced in a slimy matrix and are often pigmented.  Many species are plant pathogens. |  Many plant diseases are caused by fungi in the order Melanconiales. 许多植物病害是由黑盘孢目的真菌引起的。|  The fungus *Colletotrichum gloeosporioides* belongs to the order Melanconiales. 胶孢炭疽菌属于黑盘孢目。 | synonyms: Melanconiaceae (sometimes used, but less precise) | antonyms: N/A | noun
