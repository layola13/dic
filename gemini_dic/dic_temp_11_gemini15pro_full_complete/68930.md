fatality:/fəˈtæləti/| n.|死亡；致命事故；灾祸；宿命；不幸；厄运|An event resulting in death; a death caused by an accident or disaster.  A predetermined and inevitable fate.|例句：The car crash resulted in two fatalities. 这场车祸造成两人死亡。

例句：He seemed resigned to his fatality. 他似乎听天由命了。|近义词：death, casualty, mortality, doom, disaster|反义词：survival, life|词性：名词

fatality:/fəˈtæləti/| n.|致命；宿命性；灾难性；不幸；厄运|The quality of being destined to occur. |例句：The fatality of the disease was shocking. 这种疾病的致命性令人震惊。

例句：She accepted the fatality of her situation. 她接受了自己处境的宿命。|近义词：lethality, deadliness|反义词：harmlessness|词性：名词
