outgrow:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊ/| v.| 长大不再需要；长得比…大；发展得比…快| to grow too large for (something, such as clothing); to grow taller or bigger than (someone); to grow beyond (something)|  She outgrew her childhood clothes quickly. 她很快就长得穿不下她小时候的衣服了。

He soon outgrew his older brother. 他很快就长得比他哥哥高了。| 近义词：outsize, surpass, transcend| 反义词：shrink, diminish| 词性: 动词
