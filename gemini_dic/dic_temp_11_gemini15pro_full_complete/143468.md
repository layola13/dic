outgain:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪn/
verb
胜过，超过（在收益或产量方面）
to surpass in gains or production
The company outgained its competitors by a large margin last quarter.  该公司上季度大幅超过了其竞争对手。
Our team outgained theirs by 100 yards. 我们队的进攻码数比他们队多100码。
surpass, exceed, outstrip, outperform
lag behind, fall short of
v.
