nonplurality:/ˌnɒnplʊˈrælɪti/ | n. | 非多数; 非多数状态; |  The state of not constituting a plurality or majority.  A situation where no single candidate or option receives more votes than any other, but less than half of the total votes cast. | In the election, the candidate won by a nonplurality, securing only 40% of the vote. 在选举中，候选人以非多数票获胜，仅获得40%的选票。| Near synonyms: minority, lack of majority | Antonyms: majority, plurality | noun
