fleshed:/fleʃt/
adj.
有血肉的，丰满的，充实的
covered with flesh; having a full, rounded form; developed or elaborated in detail
例句：The fleshed-out skeleton looked eerily lifelike.  这具添上血肉的骨架看起来异常逼真。

例句：The author fleshed out the characters in her novel with vivid descriptions.  作者用生动的描写充实了她小说中的人物。
近义词：full, plump, substantial, detailed, developed
反义词：thin, emaciated, skeletal, sketchy, undeveloped
词性: adjective


fleshed:/fleʃt/
v.
(flesh out) 充实，具体化
to add more details or information to something
例句：The screenwriter fleshed out the plot with more exciting scenes.  编剧为情节添加了更多精彩的场景。

例句：The teacher asked the students to flesh out their essays with more supporting evidence.  老师要求学生用更多的论据来充实他们的文章。
近义词：expand, elaborate, develop, enlarge
反义词：condense, shorten, abridge, summarize
词性: verb (past participle)


词源: From Old English *flǣscd, past participle of *flǣscan "to cover with flesh," from flǣsc "flesh."
