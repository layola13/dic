carbuncled: /ˈkɑːrbʌŋk(ə)ld/ | adj. | 长痈的；生疖子的；似痈的；似疖的 | Affected with carbuncles; resembling a carbuncle. | The old man's neck was carbuncled and sore.  这位老人的脖子长满了疖子，很疼。 | The carbuncled landscape was scarred and pitted.  这片像痈一样的土地布满了伤疤和凹坑。 | 近义词: festering, ulcerated, inflamed | 反义词: healthy, clear | 词性: 形容词

词源: From carbuncle + -ed.  Carbuncle comes from Middle English *carbuncle, from Old French carbuncle, from Latin carbunculus "small coal, ruby," diminutive of carbo "coal, charcoal."
