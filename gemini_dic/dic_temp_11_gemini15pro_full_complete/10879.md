arachnitis:/ˌærækˈnaɪtɪs/ |n.|蛛网膜炎|Inflammation of the arachnoid membrane, one of the three membranes that surround and protect the brain and spinal cord.|He was diagnosed with arachnitis after experiencing severe headaches and stiffness in his neck.|近义词:meningitis|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源: From Greek *aráchnē* (spider) + *-itis* (inflammation).  The arachnoid membrane is so named because its delicate, web-like appearance resembles a spider's web.
