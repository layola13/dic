aoudad:/ˈaʊˌdæd/ | n. | 北非蛮羊 | A wild sheep (Ammotragus lervia) of North Africa, having long, curved horns. |  例句：The aoudad skillfully navigated the steep cliffs. 北非蛮羊熟练地在陡峭的悬崖上行走。

例句：We spotted a herd of aoudad grazing on the sparse vegetation. 我们发现一群北非蛮羊在稀疏的植被上吃草。 | 近义词：Barbary sheep | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Berber (Tamazight)  *awdad, meaning "wild sheep".
