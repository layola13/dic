linkmen:/ˈlɪŋkmɛn/
n.
联络员；联系人
A person who acts as a liaison between different groups or individuals.
The linkman for the project kept everyone informed of the progress. 该项目的联络员让每个人都了解进展情况。

The company appointed a linkman to facilitate communication between the departments. 公司任命了一名联络员来促进部门之间的沟通。

近义词:liaison, contact, representative, intermediary, coordinator
反义词:(No direct antonym.  One could consider "obstructionist" if the linkman actively hindered communication.)
词性:noun
