ounce: /aʊns/| n.|盎司；少量|A unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound (approximately 28.35 grams).  A small amount of something. |例句：The recipe calls for four ounces of butter.  这个食谱需要四盎司黄油。

例句：She added just an ounce of sugar to her tea. 她往茶里加了一点糖。|近义词：unit, bit, dash, drop|反义词: pound, ton|词性:noun


ounce: /aʊns/| n. (fluid ounce) |液盎司|A unit of liquid volume equal to one sixteenth of a pint (approximately 29.57 milliliters).|例句：The baby drank four fluid ounces of milk. 婴儿喝了四液盎司牛奶。

例句：The bottle contains eight fluid ounces of juice.  这个瓶子装有八液盎司果汁。|近义词：unit|反义词: gallon, liter|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *unce, from Latin uncia (“twelfth part, ounce”), from PIE *oin-ko-, from *oinos (“one”).  Originally a twelfth part of a Roman pound or foot.
