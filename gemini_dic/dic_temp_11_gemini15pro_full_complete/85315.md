harpa:/ˈhɑːrpə/ (US), /ˈhɑːpə/ (UK)| n.|竖琴|A stringed musical instrument that has a triangular frame formed by a soundbox, a pillar, and a curved neck, and whose strings are plucked with the fingers.|She played a beautiful melody on her harpa. 她用她的竖琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。|harp|lyre, lute|||noun
