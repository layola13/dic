collapsibilities:/kəˌlæp.səˈbɪl.ɪ.tiz/| n. | 可折叠性；易倒塌性；易崩溃性 | The quality or state of being collapsibile; the tendency to collapse.  | The collapsibilities of these cheap tents are quite concerning. 这些廉价帐篷的易倒塌性令人担忧。| The engineer tested the collapsibilities of the bridge supports. 工程师测试了桥梁支撑的易倒塌性。| 近义词：fragility, instability, weakness | 反义词：strength, stability, robustness | 词性：名词

词源:
From collapsible + -ity.  "Collapsible" comes from the verb "collapse" + "-ible".  "Collapse" traces back to the Latin word "collapsus," the past participle of "collabi," meaning "to fall together" (from "com-" (together) + "labi" (to slip, slide, fall)).
