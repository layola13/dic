exceeding:/ɪkˈsiːdɪŋ/
adjective
超越的，非常的
Going beyond the limit of; very great.
Her performance was exceeding expectations. 她的表现超出了预期。
The cost was exceeding what we had budgeted for. 成本超过了我们的预算。
Synonyms: surpassing, excelling, outstripping, exceeding
Antonyms: inferior, inadequate, insufficient
词性:形容词
