kelvin:/ˈkɛlvɪn/| n.|开尔文，开氏度|A unit of absolute temperature, where 0 K is absolute zero (-273.15°C). The size of one kelvin is the same as one degree Celsius.|The boiling point of water is 373.15 kelvins.|近义词:K (abbreviation), absolute temperature|反义词:degrees Celsius, degrees Fahrenheit|词性:noun
