ostracizes: /ˈɒstrəsaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːstrəsaɪzɪz/ (US)
| v. |排斥，孤立，放逐| to exclude from a group or society by common consent | The community ostracizes anyone who challenges their beliefs.  这个社区排斥任何挑战他们信仰的人。|
| | |  He was ostracized by his colleagues for his unethical behavior.  他因为不道德的行为而被同事排斥。|
| | | synonym: exclude, shun, banish, blackball, blacklist | antonym: include, welcome, embrace, accept | verb
