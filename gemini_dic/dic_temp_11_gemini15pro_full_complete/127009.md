NASCAR: /ˈnæsˌkɑːr/
noun
纳斯卡赛车
National Association for Stock Car Auto Racing (the dominant governing body for stock car racing in the United States)
例句：NASCAR races are incredibly popular in the South.  纳斯卡赛车在南方非常受欢迎。

例句：He dreams of becoming a NASCAR driver. 他梦想成为一名纳斯卡赛车手。

近义词: stock car racing, auto racing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps other forms of motorsport like Formula 1 or IndyCar could be considered contrasting examples)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Acronym derived from National Association for Stock Car Auto Racing.  "Stock car" originally referred to cars that had not been modified for racing, but now refers to a specific type of race car.
