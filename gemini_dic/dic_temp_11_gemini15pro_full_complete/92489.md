hypothecation:/hɪˌpɒθɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 抵押，担保 | the act of offering something as security for a loan, without transferring possession to the creditor. |  The bank required hypothecation of the company's assets as collateral for the loan.  银行要求将公司的资产作为贷款的抵押品。

The hypothecation of the ship allowed the owner to obtain a loan for repairs.  船舶的抵押使得船主能够获得维修贷款。| 近义词：pledge, security, collateral | 反义词：release | 词性：名词

hypothecate: /haɪˈpɒθɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 抵押，担保 | to pledge something as security for a loan, without transferring possession to the creditor. | The farmer hypothecated his land to secure a loan for new equipment. 农民抵押了他的土地以获得购买新设备的贷款。

He hypothecated his house to start his own business. 他抵押了他的房子以创办自己的事业。 | 近义词：pledge, mortgage | 反义词：redeem | 词性：动词
