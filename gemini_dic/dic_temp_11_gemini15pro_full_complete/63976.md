epispore:/ˈɛpɪspɔːr/| n.|外生孢子|An outer coat or covering of a spore, especially in certain fungi.|The epispore of this fungus is thick and ornamented.  这种真菌的外生孢子很厚且有纹饰。|The tough epispore protects the spore from harsh environmental conditions. 坚韧的外生孢子保护孢子免受恶劣环境条件的影响。|近义词:perispore, exospore|反义词:endospore|词性:noun
