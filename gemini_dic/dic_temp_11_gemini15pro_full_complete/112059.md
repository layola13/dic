longevities: /lɒnˈdʒevɪtiz/ /lɑːnˈdʒevətiz/
noun
长寿，寿命
The plural form of longevity, referring to the length or duration of life of multiple individuals or things.  It emphasizes the lifespans of multiple entities.
Scientists are studying the longevities of different species of primates. 科学家正在研究不同灵长动物物种的寿命。
The museum exhibit showcased the surprising longevities of certain ancient artifacts.  博物馆展览展示了某些古代文物的惊人寿命。
synonyms: lifespans, durations, life expectancies
antonyms: brevities, short lifespans
词性: noun
