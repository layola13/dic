medicaster:/ˈmɛdɪˌkæstər/ | n. |庸医；江湖郎中|A person who pretends to be a physician; a quack. | He was denounced as a medicaster by the medical community. 他被医学界谴责为江湖郎中。| The villagers relied on the medicaster for their ailments, despite his lack of formal training.  村民们尽管这位江湖郎中缺乏正规训练，还是依靠他治疗疾病。 | 近义词: quack, charlatan, impostor | 反义词: physician, doctor, surgeon | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin medicus (“physician”) + -aster (pejorative suffix).  The suffix "-aster" denotes an inferior or spurious imitation, similar to "-ster" in words like "trickster."
