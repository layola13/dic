mordicant: /ˈmɔːrdɪkənt/ | adj. | 尖刻的，辛辣的，讽刺的 |  Biting and caustic in thought, manner, or style. Characterized by a sharp and often critical quality, especially in language or wit. | Her mordicant wit made her a popular, if sometimes feared, guest at parties. 她的尖刻的机智使她成为聚会上受欢迎的客人，尽管有时也让人害怕。 |  The critic's mordicant review of the play left the actors devastated.  评论家对这部戏剧的辛辣评论让演员们崩溃了。 |  Synonyms:  caustic, acrimonious, biting, trenchant, cutting, sarcastic, sardonic, scathing | Antonyms:  gentle, kind, mild, complimentary, bland | adjective
