geopolitics:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈpɑːlətɪks/ | n. |地缘政治学|The study of the influence of such factors as geography, economics, and demography on the politics and especially the foreign policy of a nation.|例句：Geopolitics plays a crucial role in international relations. 地缘政治在国际关系中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The current conflict is rooted in geopolitics. 当前的冲突根植于地缘政治。|近义词：geostrategy, political geography|反义词: N/A | Noun
