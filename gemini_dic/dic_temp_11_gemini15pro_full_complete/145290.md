overbrag:/ˌoʊvərˈbræɡ/ | v. | 过分吹嘘，夸大其词 | To brag or boast excessively, often to the point of being unbelievable or annoying. | He kept overbragging about his new car, which made everyone uncomfortable. 他不停地吹嘘他的新车，让每个人都很不舒服。| She overbragged about her cooking skills, but the meal was disastrous. 她过分夸大了她的厨艺，但这顿饭却糟透了。 | 近义词: boast, brag, exaggerate, show off | 反义词: downplay, understate, be modest | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "brag" (verb meaning "to boast").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "brag," indicating an excessive or exaggerated form of boasting.
