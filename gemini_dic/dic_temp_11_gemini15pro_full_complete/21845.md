bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiːˈsʌtvə/ | n. |菩萨|A being who has attained enlightenment but postpones nirvana in order to help others achieve it.  A person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings.|例句：The bodhisattva vowed to help all sentient beings. 菩萨发誓要帮助所有众生。|例句：Many Buddhists strive to cultivate the qualities of a bodhisattva. 许多佛教徒努力培养菩萨的品质。|近义词：saint, enlightened being|反义词: N/A | noun
