fiddliest:/ˈfɪdliɪst/| adj.|最繁琐的，最难摆弄的|Superlative form of fiddly: most fiddly.  Meaning requiring careful and delicate manipulation or attention to detail, often in a frustrating or time-consuming way.|Assembling this model airplane is the fiddliest job I've ever done.  组装这架模型飞机是我做过的最费劲的工作。|Synonyms:  most intricate, most complex, most awkward, most delicate, most fiddly|Antonyms: simplest, easiest, most straightforward|adjective
