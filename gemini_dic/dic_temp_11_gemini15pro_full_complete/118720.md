metafile:/ˈmɛtəˌfaɪl/| n. |元文件|A file containing information about other files, often used in computer graphics to store images in a format that can be scaled without loss of quality. It often includes both vector and raster graphics data.|The designer saved the logo as a metafile so it could be resized easily.|Synonyms: vector graphic, image file|Antonyms: raster graphic|noun
