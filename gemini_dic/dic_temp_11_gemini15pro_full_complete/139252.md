oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/ (US) /əˈblaɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|迫使；施恩于；帮忙；使感激|To compel or constrain someone to do something; to do a favor or service for someone; to make someone grateful.|例句：The law obliges parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送孩子上学。|近义词：compel, force, require, bind, favor, accommodate|反义词：excuse, release, free|verb

oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/ (US) /əˈblaɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|（用于礼貌的请求或邀请）请；务必|Used in polite requests or invitations.|例句：Oblige me by closing the door. 请帮我把门关上。|近义词：kindly, please|反义词：disoblige|verb
