lubrication: /ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 润滑，润滑作用 | The act of applying a substance such as oil or grease to a surface to reduce friction. |  Regular lubrication is essential for the smooth operation of machinery.  定期润滑对于机器的平稳运行至关重要。| synonyms: oiling, greasing, anointing | antonyms: friction, abrasion | noun
