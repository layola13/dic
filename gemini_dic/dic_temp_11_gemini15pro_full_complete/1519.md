acetous:/əˈsiːtəs/
adjective
酸的；醋的；似醋的
Having a sour or vinegary taste or smell; relating to or containing vinegar.
The acetous odor filled the kitchen. 厨房里充满了酸味。
synonyms: vinegary, sour, acidic, tart
antonyms: sweet, alkaline, mild
词性: 形容词
