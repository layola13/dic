leest:/liːst/ | adj. | 最小的；最少的 | smallest; least in size, amount, degree, or importance |  The leest of my worries is what to wear. 我最不担心的是穿什么。| 近义词: smallest, tiniest, minimum | 反义词: largest, biggest, maximum | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *leste, lest, superlative of litel (“little”), from Old English lǣst, lǣst, contraction of lǣsest, superlative of *lytel (“little”); akin to Old Frisian lest, Old Saxon lesto, Old High German lezisto, all from Proto-Germanic *laisistaz.  More at less.
