air-cooled:/ˈɛərˌkuːld/| adj.|风冷的，空气冷却的|Cooled by air, as opposed to water or other liquids.  A system or device designed to be cooled by the circulation of air.|This vintage car has an air-cooled engine. 这辆老爷车有一个风冷式引擎。|
The computer's processor is air-cooled, relying on a large fan to dissipate heat.  这台电脑的处理器是风冷的，依靠一个大风扇来散热。|Synonyms: air-conditioning, ventilated|Antonyms: water-cooled, liquid-cooled|adjective
