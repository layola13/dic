cholericly: /kəˈlɛrɪkli/ (US) /kəˈlɛrɪkli/ (UK)| adv.|暴躁地，易怒地|In a choleric or easily angered manner; angrily.  Derived from the word "choler," meaning one of the four bodily humors in ancient and medieval physiology, believed to cause anger and irritability.  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "choleric" into an adverb.|He responded cholericly to the slightest provocation. 他对最轻微的挑衅都暴跳如雷。|The old man cholericly shook his fist at the noisy children. 老人愤怒地挥舞着拳头对着吵闹的孩子们。|近义词：angrily, irately, furiously, wrathfully|反义词：calmly, peacefully, placidly|词性:副词
