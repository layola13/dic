byssoid:/ˈbɪsɔɪd/
adjective
丝状的，像棉絮的
Resembling a fine filamentous substance; like cotton or flax.
The byssoid mycelium of the fungus spread across the petri dish.  这种真菌的丝状菌丝体遍布整个培养皿。
The spider produced a byssoid web to catch its prey.  蜘蛛结了一张丝状的网来捕捉猎物。
近义词:filamentous, cottony, floccose
反义词:dense, compact
词性:adjective

byssus:/ˈbɪsəs/
noun
(某些双壳类动物的)足丝
A bundle of silky filaments by which certain bivalve mollusks, such as mussels, attach themselves to rocks, etc.
Mussels anchor themselves to rocks using a byssus.  贻贝用足丝把自己固定在岩石上。
The byssus of the pen shell is highly prized for making a fine, golden cloth.  海笔的足丝被高度重视，用来制作精美的金色布料。
近义词:beard (of a mussel)
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
