overinclined: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnd/
adjective
过度倾向的；过分偏爱的
Having a tendency to do something to excess; excessively inclined.
He was overinclined to offer advice, even when it wasn't asked for. 他总是倾向于给出建议，即使别人并没有请求。
She's overinclined to spend money on clothes. 她太爱买衣服了。
近义词: predisposed, prone, susceptible
反义词: averse, disinclined, resistant
词性: adjective
