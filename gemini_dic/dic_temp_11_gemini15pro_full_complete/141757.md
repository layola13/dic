orangite:/ˈɔːrəndʒaɪt/ | n. | 硅镁铀矿; 橙黄色水晶 | A mineral, a variety of thorite containing uranium. It is usually orange or yellow in color. | This orangite specimen exhibits a vibrant orange hue. 这块硅镁铀矿标本呈现出鲜艳的橙色。| The orangite crystal is highly radioactive. 这颗橙黄色水晶具有高放射性。 |Synonyms: thorite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
