Etymology->

From non- + transient + -ness + -es.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Transient" comes from the Latin word "transire" meaning "to go across" or "to pass."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
