nonbookishly:/ˌnɒnˈbʊkɪʃli/
adverb
非书呆子气地；不像书呆子；不迂腐地
in a way that is not typical of someone who spends a lot of time reading or studying; in a practical or worldly way.
He tackled the problem nonbookishly, with practical experience and common sense. 他以非书呆子的方式处理这个问题，凭借的是实践经验和常识。
Synonyms: practically, realistically, pragmatically, down-to-earth
Antonyms: bookishly, academically, theoretically, scholastically
词性: 副词
