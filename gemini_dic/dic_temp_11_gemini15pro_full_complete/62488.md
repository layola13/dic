endosteum: /ɛnˈdɒstiəm/ (British), /ɛnˈdɑːstiəm/ (American)| n. | 骨内膜 | A thin vascular membrane of connective tissue that lines the inner surface of the bony tissue that forms the medullary cavity of long bones. | The endosteum plays a crucial role in bone growth, repair, and remodeling. 骨内膜在骨骼生长、修复和重建中起着至关重要的作用。| Synonyms: medullary membrane | Antonyms: periosteum | Noun
