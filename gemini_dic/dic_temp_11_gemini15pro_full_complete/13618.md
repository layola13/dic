attests:/əˈtɛsts/
| v. |证明；证实；表明|provides clear evidence of; declares to be true|Her success attests to her hard work. 她的成功证明了她的努力。|Synonyms: confirms, verifies, corroborates, substantiates|Antonyms: denies, refutes, contradicts|verb
attests:/əˈtɛsts/
| v. |作证，证明（某事属实）|To declare to be true; to give evidence as a witness.|He attested to the validity of the signature. 他证实了签名的有效性。|Synonyms: testifies, affirms, swears, vows|Antonyms: denies, refutes, contradicts|verb
