interpolate:/ɪnˈtɜːrpəleɪt/ /ɪnˈtɝːpəleɪt/ | vt. |插入，篡改，添加|To insert (something of a different nature) into something else; to introduce (material) into a text or conversation.|例句：He interpolated a few remarks while I was speaking.  他趁我讲话时插了几句话。|近义词：insert, interject, introduce|反义词:delete, remove, extract|Verb

interpolated:/ɪnˈtɜːrpəleɪtɪd/ /ɪnˈtɝːpəleɪtɪd/ | adj. |插入的，添加的|Inserted, added, or introduced between other things or parts.|例句：The interpolated data helped complete the sequence.  插入的数据有助于完成序列。|近义词：inserted, added, introduced|反义词:original, unaltered|Adjective

interpolation:/ɪnˌtɜːrpəˈleɪʃn/ /ɪnˌtɝːpəˈleɪʃn/ | n. |插入，篡改，添加|The act of interpolating; something interpolated.|例句：The interpolation of the data was necessary for the analysis. 数据的插入对于分析是必要的。|近义词：insertion, addition, introduction|反义词:deletion, removal, extraction|Noun
