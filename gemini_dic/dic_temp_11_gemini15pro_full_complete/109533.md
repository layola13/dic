less-than:/ˈles ðæn/ | adj. | 少于，小于 |  Used to describe something that is smaller in quantity, value, or degree than something else.  Meaning "not as much as" or "smaller than". | He has less-than perfect eyesight. 他的视力不太好。| synonyms: under, below, inferior to | antonyms: greater than, more than, exceeding | adjective

less-than:/ˈles ðæn/ | prep. | 少于，小于 | Used as a preposition before a number or quantity. | The project took less than a week to complete. 这个项目不到一周就完成了。| synonyms: under, below | antonyms: over, above | preposition
