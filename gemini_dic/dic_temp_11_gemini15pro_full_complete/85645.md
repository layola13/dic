hauntingly:/ˈhɔːntɪŋli/ | adv. | 萦绕心头地；令人难忘地；不易忘怀地 | In a way that is disturbing and stays in the mind for a long time. | The music was hauntingly beautiful. 这音乐优美得令人难忘。|Synonyms: unforgettable, memorable, poignant, evocative | Antonyms: forgettable, unremarkable | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From haunt (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Haunt comes from Old French hanter "to frequent, resort to," which itself likely comes from a Germanic source.
