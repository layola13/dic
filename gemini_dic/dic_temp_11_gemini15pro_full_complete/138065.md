normalise:/ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US)| v. | 使正常化；使标准化；使合乎规范 |To make something conform to a standard or norm; to make something usual or typical. |The government is trying to normalise relations with its neighbours. 政府正试图使其与邻国的关系正常化。

normalise:/ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US)| v. | 标准化（数据）；正规化 |To adjust data to a common scale or standard. |The data was normalised to account for differences in population size.  数据被标准化以考虑人口规模的差异。

normalise:/ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US)| v. |（冶金学）使（金属）回复常态 |To restore (a metal) to its normal condition after some special treatment. |The steel was normalised by heating and cooling.  钢通过加热和冷却使其回复常态。


近义词: standardize, regulate, adjust, normalize (US spelling)
