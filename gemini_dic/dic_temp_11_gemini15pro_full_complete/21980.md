bohemianism:/boʊˈhiːmiənɪzəm/| n.|放荡不羁，波西米亚主义|A way of life characterized by unconventional behavior, artistic expression, and a disregard for conventional rules and norms.  It often involves a rejection of materialism and a pursuit of creative freedom and self-expression.|She embraced bohemianism and lived a life of travel and artistic pursuits. 她崇尚放荡不羁的生活方式，过着旅行和艺术追求的生活。|synonyms: unconventionality, nonconformity, counterculture|antonyms: conventionality, conformity, traditionalism|noun
