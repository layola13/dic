embryoplastic:/ˌɛmbrɪoʊˈplæstɪk/| adj.|胚胎成形的，胚胎塑造的|Relating to or involved in the formation of an embryo; capable of molding or shaping the embryo.|Embryoplastic factors play a crucial role in the development of the fetus. 胚胎成形因子在胎儿发育中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：embryogenic, embryogenetic|反义词: None|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From embryo + -plastic.  "Embryo" comes from the Greek ἔμβρυον (émbruon), meaning "young one," from ἐν (en, "in") + βρύειν (brúein, "to swell, grow"). "-Plastic" comes from the Greek πλαστικός (plastikós, "able to be molded"), from πλάσσειν (plássein, "to mold, shape").
