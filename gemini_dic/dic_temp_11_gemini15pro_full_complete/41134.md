copsing:/ˈkɒpsɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɑːpsɪŋ/| n. |窥探；窥视；监视(尤指警察秘密监视) |The act of watching or observing someone or something secretly, especially by the police. |例句：The police were accused of copsing on the protesters. 警方被指控对示威者进行监视。

例句：Undercover officers were copsing on the drug deal. 便衣警察正在秘密监视毒品交易。

近义词：surveillance, spying, monitoring, observing, watching, tailing, shadowing |反义词: ignoring, neglecting |词性: noun (名词)
