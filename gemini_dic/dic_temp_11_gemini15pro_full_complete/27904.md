canted:/ˈkæntɪd/ (US), /ˈkæntɪd/ (UK)| adj.|倾斜的，歪斜的；(尤指观点)故作姿态的，不真诚的|sloping; tilted; set at an angle;  Also, affectedly pious or moralistic|The picture hung canted on the wall.  这幅画歪挂在墙上。|Synonyms: tilted, sloping, inclined, slanted, askew, oblique, angled|Antonyms: straight, upright, level, vertical|adjective

canted:/ˈkæntɪd/ (US), /ˈkæntɪd/ (UK)| v.|使倾斜；使翘起 (past tense and past participle of *cant*)|to tilt or slope something|He canted the chair back on two legs. 他把椅子向后倾斜，只用两条腿支撑。|Synonyms: tilt, tip, slant, incline, lean|Antonyms: straighten, level, upright|verb
