nonprocurements:/ˌnɑːn prəˈkjʊrmənts/ | n. |非采购，非采购项目|  The act or process of not obtaining goods or services through purchase. It often refers to activities or acquisitions that don't involve the typical purchasing process, like donations, gifts, or internal transfers.  |  The university received several nonprocurements of lab equipment this year. 今年大学收到了几批非采购的实验室设备。| synonyms: non-purchases, acquisitions (in some contexts) | antonyms: procurements, purchases | noun

nonprocurement:/ˌnɑːn prəˈkjʊrmənt/ | n. |非采购，非采购项目| A single instance of not procuring something through the usual purchasing process. | The nonprocurement of the software led to delays in the project.  该软件的非采购导致了项目的延误。| synonyms: non-purchase, acquisition (in some contexts) | antonyms: procurement, purchase | noun
