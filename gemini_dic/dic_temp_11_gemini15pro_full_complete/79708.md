glucogenesis:/ˌɡluːkoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
糖异生；糖原异生
The formation of glucose within the animal body from precursors other than carbohydrates especially by the liver and kidney using amino acids, glycerol, or lactate.  
The liver plays a crucial role in glucogenesis. 肝脏在糖异生中起着至关重要的作用。
Gluconeogenesis is essential for maintaining blood glucose levels during fasting. 糖异生对于在禁食期间维持血糖水平至关重要。
Synonyms:  gluconeogenesis
Antonyms: glycolysis
