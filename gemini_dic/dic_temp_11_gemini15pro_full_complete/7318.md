angulating:/ˈæŋɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
成角的，弯曲的
forming an angle; bending.
The bone was angulating at an unnatural degree after the injury.  受伤后，骨头以不自然的角度弯曲。

近义词:bending, flexing, curving
反义词:straightening, extending

词性:verb

词源: From Late Latin angulatus, past participle of angulare “to make angular,” from Latin angulus “angle, corner.”
