inconceivably:/ˌɪnkənˈsiːvəbli/
adverb
难以置信地，不可思议地
not capable of being imagined or grasped mentally; unbelievably.
The universe is inconceivably vast. 宇宙浩瀚得不可思议。
Synonyms: unimaginably, unbelievably, incredibly, impossibly
Antonyms: conceivably, possibly, imaginably
词性: 副词
