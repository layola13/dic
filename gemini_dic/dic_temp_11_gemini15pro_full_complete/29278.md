cast-aside: /ˌkæst əˈsaɪd/ | adj. n. |被抛弃的，被遗弃的；被抛弃的人/物 |Something that is cast aside is no longer wanted or used. | The old toys were cast aside and forgotten. 旧玩具被丢弃一旁，被人遗忘了。 | The cast-aside clothes were donated to charity. 被丢弃的衣服被捐赠给了慈善机构。 | 近义词：discarded, abandoned, rejected, dumped | 反义词：cherished, valued, treasured | 词性：形容词, 名词


Etymology:  From the verb phrase "cast aside," literally "to throw to the side."  "Cast" comes from Old Norse *kasta "to throw," and "aside" from Old English on sīdan, meaning "on the side."
