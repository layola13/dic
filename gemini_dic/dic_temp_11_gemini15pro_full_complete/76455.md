gainsome:/ˈɡeɪnsəm/
adjective
有利可图的；合算的；有益的
Producing a gain or profit; advantageous; profitable.
He made a gainsome investment in the stock market. 他在股市进行了一项有利可图的投资。
近义词: profitable, lucrative, advantageous, beneficial
反义词: unprofitable, loss-making, disadvantageous, detrimental
词性: adjective

gainsome:/ˈɡeɪnsəm/
adjective
大量的；丰富的；很多的
Large in quantity or number; abundant. (This meaning is less common in modern usage.)
The farm had a gainsome harvest this year.  今年农场获得了丰收。（这个用法在现代英语中不太常见）
近义词: abundant, plentiful, copious, ample
反义词: scarce, meager, scanty, sparse
词性: adjective


词源:
From "gain" + "-some" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by," "tending to," or "productive of").
