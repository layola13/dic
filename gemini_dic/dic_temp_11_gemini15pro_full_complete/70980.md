firers:/ˈfaɪərərz/
n.
放火者，点火者，射击者
People who start fires, or those who operate firearms.
The firers escaped the scene before the authorities arrived. 放火者在当局到达之前逃离了现场。
The competition tested the skills of the firers. 比赛测试了射击者的技能。
synonyms: arsonists, incendiaries, shooters, gunmen
antonyms: firefighters, extinguishers, peacemakers
noun
