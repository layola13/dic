juice:/dʒuːs/ | n. |果汁，精力，活力|The liquid part of a fruit, vegetable, or meat that can be squeezed out.  Also can refer to electrical energy or vitality. |She squeezed the juice from an orange. 她从橙子里榨出了果汁。|  The project has lost its juice. 这个项目已经失去了活力。|synonyms: nectar, liquid, extract, sap, vigor, energy| antonyms: dryness, lethargy| noun
