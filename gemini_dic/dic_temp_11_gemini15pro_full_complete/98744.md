insurrectionists:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪsts/
n.
暴动者，起义者，叛乱者
A person who takes part in an insurrection; a rebel.
The insurrectionists stormed the capitol building. 暴动者冲进了国会大厦。
近义词: rebels, mutineers, revolutionaries
反义词: loyalists, conformists
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insurrectionistēs, from Latin insurgere  "to rise up".  The "-ist" suffix denotes a person who performs or adheres to a particular action or belief.
