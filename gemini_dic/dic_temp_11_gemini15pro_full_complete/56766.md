dopaminergic:/ˌdōpəməˈnɜːrdʒɪk/ | adj. | 多巴胺能的 | Relating to or involving dopamine or its effects. |  Parkinson's disease is associated with a loss of dopaminergic neurons. 帕金森氏病与多巴胺能神经元的丧失有关。

The study investigated the dopaminergic pathways in the brain. 这项研究调查了大脑中的多巴胺能通路。 | 近义词: dopamine-related, dopamine-mediated | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, would depend on the specific context.  Perhaps "anti-dopaminergic" in a pharmacology context) | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dopamine + -ergic.  Dopamine itself comes from dihydrOxyPhenylAlanine (its chemical precursor) + amine (a type of nitrogen-containing compound).
