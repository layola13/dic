grainy:/ˈɡreɪni/| adj.|颗粒状的；粗糙的；多粒的；模糊不清的|Having a granular or rough texture; composed of or resembling grains; unclear or indistinct, as a photograph or sound recording.|The photo was old and grainy. 这张照片很旧，而且颗粒感很强。|近义词：granular, coarse, rough, pixelated, fuzzy|反义词:smooth, clear, sharp|词性:形容词
词源:From grain + -y.  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *grǝ-n-o-, meaning "grain, seed."
