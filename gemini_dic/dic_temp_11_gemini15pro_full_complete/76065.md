furnishers:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃərz/
n.
家具商；家具店
People who supply furniture; shops that sell furniture.
The furnishers delivered the new sofa this morning.  家具商今天早上送来了新沙发。
synonyms: furniture dealers, furniture stores, furniture suppliers
antonyms: N/A (Lack of a direct antonym for a noun referring to a commercial entity)
