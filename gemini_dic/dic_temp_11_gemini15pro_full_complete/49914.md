denarcotize:/diːnɑːrkətaɪz/
verb
戒毒；消除麻醉作用
To free from the influence of narcotics; to counteract the effects of narcotics.
The clinic helps patients denarcotize and rebuild their lives.  这家诊所帮助病人戒毒并重建他们的生活。
The medication is used to denarcotize patients after surgery. 这种药物用于手术后消除病人的麻醉作用。
Synonyms: detoxify, wean, withdraw
Antonyms:  addict, drug
词性: 动词
