neurism: /ˈnjʊərɪzəm/ | n. |神经症; 神经过敏; 神经质 | A functional nervous disorder without demonstrable physical lesion.  A condition characterized by an undue tendency to nervousness or neurotic traits. |  He suffered from a neurism that caused him to twitch uncontrollably. 他患有一种神经症，导致他不受控制地抽搐。|  Her constant worrying was a sign of her neurism. 她不停地担心是神经质的表现。 | synonyms: neurosis, nervous breakdown, nervous prostration | antonyms: stability, calmness | noun
