glaucosis:/ɡlɔːˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |青光眼，房水过多|A disease of the eye marked by increased intraocular pressure.  It is characterized by a specific pattern of progressive optic neuropathy that can lead to blindness.|Glaucosis is a serious eye condition that requires immediate medical attention. 青光眼是一种需要立即就医的严重眼疾。|Synonyms: glaucoma|Antonyms: N/A|noun
