gallicize: /ˈɡælɪsaɪz/ (US) /ˈɡalɪsaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使法国化；使具有法国特点；法语化|To make something French in character or style; to adapt something to French customs or idiom.|The menu was gallicized with fancy French names for simple dishes.  菜单被法语化了，简单的菜肴都起了花哨的法语名字。|synonyms: frenchify, adapt, modify|antonyms: anglicize, americanize|verb

gallicization: /ˌɡælɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌɡalɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |法国化；法语化|The process of making something French in character or style.|The gallicization of Vietnamese cuisine during the colonial period is evident in dishes like banh mi.  殖民时期越南菜的法语化在banh mi等菜肴中很明显。|synonyms: frenchification|antonyms: anglicization, americanization|noun
