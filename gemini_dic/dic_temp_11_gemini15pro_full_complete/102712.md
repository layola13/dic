jangji: /ˈdʒɑːŋdʒi/ | n. | 장지 (Korean: burial ground; graveyard)| A Korean word referring to a burial ground or graveyard.  It's specifically a place where Koreans traditionally bury their dead. |  Example:  My grandmother is buried in the family jangji. 我的祖母葬在家族墓地。

Example:  Visiting the jangji during Chuseok is a common tradition.  在中秋节期间扫墓是一个普遍的传统。 | Synonym: cemetery, graveyard, burial ground | Antonym: N/A | Noun
