nicknamed:/ˈnɪkˌneɪmd/
adjective
绰号的，被叫做…的
Given or having a nickname; called by a nickname.
My cat, nicknamed "Tiger," is actually quite timid. 我的猫，绰号“老虎”，实际上非常胆小。
synonyms: called, dubbed, known as
antonyms: nameless, anonymous
词性: adjective
