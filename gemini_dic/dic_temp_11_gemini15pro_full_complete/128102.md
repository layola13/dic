nesters:/ˈnɛstərz/
n.
筑巢者；(尤指)留巢鸟；居家的一代；空巢老人(nester的复数)
Birds building nests are called nesters.  正在筑巢的鸟被称为筑巢者。
The nesters are busy preparing for their young.  这些筑巢的鸟正忙着为它们的幼鸟做准备。
近义词:residents, inhabitants, dwellers, occupants
反义词:migrants, nomads
词性:名词

Etymology: From "nest" + "-er" (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "nest".
