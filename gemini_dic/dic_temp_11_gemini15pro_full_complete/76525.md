galicians:/ɡəˈlɪʃənz/
n.
Galicians; Galician people
People from Galicia (a region in northwestern Spain).
Galicians are known for their bagpipes and Celtic traditions. 加利西亚人以其风笛和凯尔特传统而闻名。
