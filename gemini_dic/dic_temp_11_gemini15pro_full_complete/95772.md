incontestable:/ˌɪnkənˈtɛstəbl/ | adj. | 不可争辩的，无可置疑的 | Not able to be disputed or challenged; undeniable. |  His victory in the election was incontestable. 他在选举中的胜利是无可争辩的。

The evidence against him was incontestable.  针对他的证据是确凿无疑的。

| 近义词: undeniable, indisputable, irrefutable, unquestionable | 反义词: disputable, debatable, questionable, contestable | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *incontestabilis, from in- "not" + contestari "to call to witness, to bring an action against," from con- "together" + testari "to bear witness," from testis "witness."
来自晚期拉丁语 *incontestabilis，来自 in- “不” + contestari “传唤证人，对…提起诉讼”，来自 con- “一起” + testari “作证”，来自 testis “证人”。
