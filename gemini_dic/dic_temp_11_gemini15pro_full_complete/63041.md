enravishment:/ɪnˈrævɪʃmənt/ | n. | 狂喜；着迷；销魂 | A feeling of intense delight or joy; a state of being completely captivated. | She listened with enravishment to the music. 她听得如痴如醉。| 近义词: rapture, ecstasy, bliss, delight | 反义词: misery, despair, sorrow | 词性: 名词

enravish:/ɪnˈrævɪʃ/ | vt. | 使狂喜；使着迷 | To fill with intense delight; to captivate. | The beauty of the sunset enravished her. 日落的美丽使她陶醉。| 近义词: enchant, captivate, charm, fascinate | 反义词: repel, disgust, bore | 词性: 动词
