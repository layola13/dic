accusatorially:/ˌækjuˈzeɪtərəli/
adverb
指责地，控诉地
In a manner that expresses or implies an accusation.
He looked at her accusatorially. 他用指责的眼神看着她。
He pointed his finger accusatorially. 他指责地指着她。

Synonyms: accusingly, reproachingly, blamingly, incriminatingly
Antonyms: apologetically, forgivingly, sympathetically

词性: adverb
