non-fictitious:/ˌnɒn fɪkˈtɪʃəs/ (US) /ˌnɒn fɪkˈtɪʃəs/ (UK)| adj.|非小说的|Referring to literature or other works that are based on facts, not imagination.|例句：She prefers to read non-fictitious books. 她更喜欢读非小说类的书籍。|近义词：factual, real, true, authentic, genuine|反义词：fictitious, fictional, fabricated, invented, imaginary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + fictitious.  "Fictitious" comes from the Latin "ficticius" meaning "feigned, artificial," from "fingere" meaning "to form, shape, invent, devise."
