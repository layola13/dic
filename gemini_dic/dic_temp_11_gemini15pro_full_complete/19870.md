bingers:/ˈbɪndʒərz/| n.| 狂欢作乐的人; 放纵者;  (尤指)暴食者;  连续看剧的人 |People who engage in excessive consumption of something, especially food or entertainment, typically over a short period.  |例句：The Netflix bingers finished the entire series in one weekend.  那些连续看剧的人用一个周末看完了整部剧。

例句：The holiday bingers indulged in all sorts of treats.  假期里那些放纵者尽情享受各种美食。|近义词：overindulgers, addicts, gluttons (specifically for food), marathoners (specifically for entertainment) |反义词:moderates, abstainers|noun
