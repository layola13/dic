lopped:/lɒpt/ |v. |砍掉，修剪，|To cut off the top or branches of (a tree, plant, etc.).  |The gardener lopped the branches off the overgrown tree. 园丁砍掉了过度生长的树的树枝。

The storm lopped the tops off several trees.  暴风雨把几棵树的顶部刮掉了。|近义词：trim, prune, cut, chop, sever |反义词: grow, extend, lengthen |词性: 动词

lopped:/lɒpt/ |adj. |被砍掉的，修剪过的|Having had the top or branches cut off.  |The lopped branches lay scattered on the ground. 被砍掉的树枝散落在地上。

The lopped trees looked bare against the winter sky. 被修剪过的树木在冬日的天空下显得光秃秃的。|近义词：trimmed, pruned, cut, chopped, severed |反义词: untrimmed, unpruned, uncut |词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *loppen, from Old English *loppan (“to leap, run”), of uncertain origin.  Related to leap.  The sense development is probably from "to lop off" (as if by a sudden movement).
