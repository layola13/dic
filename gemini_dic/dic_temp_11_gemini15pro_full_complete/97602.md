inhabits:/ɪnˈhæbɪts/| v.|居住于，栖息于|lives in (a place) or occupies (a place).|例句：This rare bird inhabits the remote mountain forests. 这种稀有鸟类栖息于偏远的山地森林。|近义词：lives in, occupies, resides in, dwells in|反义词:vacates, leaves, deserts|词性:动词

inhabits:/ɪnˈhæbɪts/| v.|居住于，栖息于|occupies as a home or dwelling place; resides or exists in (a particular place or environment).|例句：Many species of fish inhabit coral reefs. 许多种鱼类栖息在珊瑚礁中。|近义词：populates, resides in, dwells in, occupies|反义词:abandons, evacuates|词性:动词
