non-purchase:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːtʃəs/ | n. | 非购买，未购买 |The act of not buying something, or the goods that are not bought. | Example:  The customer's non-purchase of the product was due to its high price.  顾客未购买该产品是因为价格太高。 | Example: The non-purchases were returned to the warehouse. 未购买的商品被退回了仓库。| Synonyms: non-acquisition, abstention from buying | Antonyms: purchase, acquisition | noun
