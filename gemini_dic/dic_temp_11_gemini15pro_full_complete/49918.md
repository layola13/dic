denationalises:/diːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使（产业）非国有化；从…手中收回（产业）的国有化
To transfer (an industry or asset) from public to private ownership.
The government denationalised the railway system. 政府将铁路系统私有化了。
denationalizes (US spelling)
privatizes, deregulates
nationalizes, socializes
v.
