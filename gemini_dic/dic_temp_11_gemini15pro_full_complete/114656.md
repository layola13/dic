malian: /ˈmɑːliən/ | adj. n. | 马里的；马里人 | Relating to Mali, a country in West Africa.  A person from Mali. |  例句：Malian music is known for its rich rhythms. 马里音乐以其丰富的节奏而闻名。

例句：She is a Malian citizen. 她是一位马里公民。| 近义词：(for n.) West African | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Mali + -an.  The name "Mali" itself derives from the name of the Mali Empire, which in turn is believed to derive from the Mandinka word "mali" meaning "hippopotamus".
