noncircuited:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːkɪtɪd/
adjective
未电路化的；未连接电路的
Not connected in a circuit; not forming part of an electrical circuit.
The noncircuited wires posed no fire hazard.  未接入电路的电线没有火灾隐患。
The device was shipped noncircuited for safety reasons.  出于安全考虑，该设备在运输过程中未连接电路。
Synonyms: unconnected, open-circuit,  disconnected
Antonyms: circuited, connected, closed-circuit
词性: 形容词
