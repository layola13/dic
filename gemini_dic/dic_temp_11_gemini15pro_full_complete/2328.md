adherence:/ədˈhɪrəns/| n.|坚持，遵守，依附|Steady or faithful attachment; devotion to a person, cause, or belief. |The doctor emphasized the importance of adherence to the prescribed medication regimen. 医生强调了坚持按照处方服药的重要性。| 近义词：compliance, obedience, conformity, fidelity|反义词:noncompliance, disobedience, defiance, violation|noun

adherence:/ədˈhɪrəns/| n.|粘附，粘附性|The quality or process of sticking to something. |The adherence of the glue was impressive.  这胶水的粘附力令人印象深刻。|近义词： adhesion, sticking, bonding|反义词:separation, detachment|noun
