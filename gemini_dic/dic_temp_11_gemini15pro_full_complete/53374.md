disaggregate:/dɪsˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 分解；分类；分散 | To separate something into its component parts; to break down a total into individual parts. | The data were disaggregated by gender and age. 数据按性别和年龄分类。|  The company disaggregated its financial results to show the performance of each division. 公司分解其财务结果以显示每个部门的业绩。 |  separate, break down, analyze, decompose, divide | aggregate, combine, consolidate, unite | verb

disaggregate:/dɪsˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/ | adj. | 分解的；分类的；分散的 |  Separated into component parts; not aggregated. | The report provided disaggregated data on income levels. 报告提供了关于收入水平的分类数据。| The disaggregated figures revealed significant regional variations. 分解的数字揭示了显著的区域差异。| separate, individual, categorized, broken down | aggregated, combined, consolidated, whole | adjective
