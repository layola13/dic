kraal:/krɑːl/| n.|畜栏，围栏；(南非)村庄|An enclosure for livestock, especially cattle or sheep, typically made of thorn bushes.  In Southern Africa, it can also refer to a traditional village of the Zulu people.|The farmer kept his cattle in a kraal.  农民把他的牛关在畜栏里。|The Zulu kraal was a bustling center of activity.  祖鲁村庄是一个熙熙攘攘的活动中心。|enclosure, pen, compound|open field|noun
