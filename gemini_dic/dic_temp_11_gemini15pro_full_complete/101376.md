ionospheric:/aɪˌɑːnəˈsfɪrɪk/ | adj. | 电离层的 | Relating to the ionosphere, the part of the Earth's atmosphere that contains a high concentration of ions and free electrons and is able to reflect radio waves. | The ionospheric disturbances can affect radio communications. 电离层扰动会影响无线电通信。 |  The ionospheric reflection makes long-distance radio communication possible. 电离层的反射使远距离无线电通信成为可能。 | synonyms: upper atmospheric,  | antonyms:  | adjective
