constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/| n. |成分; 选民; 构成要素|A constituent is a component part of something.  A constituent can also be a voting member of a community or organization and has the power to appoint or elect.|The constituents of the mixture were carefully measured. 这种混合物的成分经过仔细测量。|近义词:component, element, ingredient, voter, elector |反义词:whole, entirety|noun
constitutors:/kənˈstɪtʃuətərz/| n. | 组成者；构成者；立宪者（constituor 的复数） |Constitutors are those who establish or constitute something, often used in legal or political contexts to refer to those who create a constitution or other fundamental laws.|The constitutors of the new republic drafted a constitution that guaranteed basic rights. 新共和国的立宪者起草了一部保障基本权利的宪法。|近义词:founders, creators, establishers|反义词: destroyers, dismantlers |noun
