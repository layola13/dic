loughs:/lɒxs/ | n. | 湖泊；海湾（尤指爱尔兰或苏格兰的） | Lakes or inlets, especially in Ireland or Scotland. | The boat sailed serenely across the loughs of Killarney. 这艘船平静地航行穿过基拉尼湖泊。 | The loughs of Connemara are renowned for their beauty. 康尼马拉的湖泊以其美丽而闻名。 | synonyms: lakes, inlets, bays | antonyms: mountains, plains | noun
