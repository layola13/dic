nuclear-force:/ˈnjuːkliər fɔːrs/| n.|核力|The force that holds protons and neutrons together in the nucleus of an atom. |The nuclear force is much stronger than the electromagnetic force. 核力比电磁力强得多。|The strong nuclear force is responsible for the stability of atomic nuclei.  强核力负责原子核的稳定性。|近义词:strong force, strong interaction|反义词:weak nuclear force, weak interaction|词性:noun
