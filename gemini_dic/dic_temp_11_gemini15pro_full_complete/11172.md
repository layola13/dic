-archical:/haɪˈrɑːrkɪkəl/
adjective|等级的，分层的|arranged in order of rank or authority|The company has a hierarchical structure, with the CEO at the top.  这家公司有一个等级森严的结构，首席执行官位居顶层。| hierarchical society 等级森严的社会|例句：The military has a very hierarchical structure.  军队有一个非常等级森严的结构。|近义词：stratified, graded, ranked|反义词： egalitarian, democratic|词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek hierarkhia 'rule of a high priest', from hierarkhes 'high priest', from hieros 'sacred' + arkhein 'to rule'.
