grogshop:/ˈɡrɒɡʃɒp/ (US: /ˈɡrɑːɡʃɑːp/)| n. |酒馆，低级酒吧|A place where alcoholic drinks are sold, typically one of poor quality or reputation.  It often implies a place that is rough or disreputable.|The sailors spent their shore leave in the dimly lit grogshop.|Synonyms: tavern, pub, bar, saloon, alehouse|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps:  church, library, school)|noun
