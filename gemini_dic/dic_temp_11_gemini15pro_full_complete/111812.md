logarithmic:/ˌlɒɡərɪðmɪk/ /ˌlɑːɡərɪðmɪk/| adj.|对数的|Relating to or expressed in terms of logarithms.|The Richter scale is logarithmic. 里氏震级是对数的。|The growth of the bacteria was logarithmic.  细菌的生长是对数的。|近义词：exponential (in reverse sense)|反义词:linear|词性:形容词

logarithm:/ˈlɒɡərɪðm/ /ˈlɑːɡərɪðəm/| n.|对数|A quantity representing the power to which a fixed number (the base) must be raised to produce a given number.|The logarithm of 100 to the base 10 is 2.  以10为底100的对数是2。|We can use logarithms to simplify complex calculations. 我们可以使用对数来简化复杂的计算。|近义词：exponent (in a related sense)|反义词:  N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *logarithmus*, from Ancient Greek λόγος (lógos, “word, reason, ratio”) + ἀριθμός (arithmós, “number”).
