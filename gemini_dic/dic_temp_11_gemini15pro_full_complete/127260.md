naval:/ˈneɪvəl/| adj.|海军的，军舰的|Relating to a navy or warships.|例句：The country has a strong naval presence in the region.  这个国家在该地区拥有强大的海军力量。

例句：Naval battles were common during the war.  战争期间海战很常见。|近义词：maritime, nautical|反义词:None|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From Old French *naval, from Latin nāvālis (“of or belonging to ships”), from nāvis (“ship”).
