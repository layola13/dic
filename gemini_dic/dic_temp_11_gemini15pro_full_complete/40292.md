continuity:/ˌkɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/ /ˌkɑːntɪˈnuːɪti/| n.|连续性，持续性|The continuity of the project was ensured by careful planning.  这个项目的连续性是通过仔细的计划来保证的。|例句：The film lacked continuity; in one scene it was snowing, and in the next the sun was shining.  这部电影缺乏连贯性；在一个场景中下着雪，而在下一个场景中阳光普照。|近义词：continuation, persistence, sequence, flow|反义词:disruption, interruption, discontinuity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *continuitatem, from Latin continuus (“continuous”).
