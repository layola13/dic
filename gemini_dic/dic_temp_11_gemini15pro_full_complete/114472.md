makuru:/məˈkuːruː/ | n. | 马库鲁（澳大利亚土著语，指干旱季节）| The Makuru season is characterized by cold nights and clear days. |例句：The Makuru season is a time of ceremony and celebration for many Aboriginal groups.  马库鲁季节是许多原住民群体举行仪式和庆祝的时期。

例句：During Makuru, the Noongar people traditionally harvested root vegetables. 在马库鲁期间，努加尔人传统上收获根茎类蔬菜。|近义词：dry season, winter (in the context of Noongar calendar) |反义词：wet season |词性: 名词
