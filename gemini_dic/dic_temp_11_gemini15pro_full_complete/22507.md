boomerang:/ˈbuːməræŋ/ /ˈbuːmərɑːŋ/| n. vi. |回飞棒；自食其果的行为或事物|A curved wooden missile that can be thrown so as to return to the thrower.  A scheme, idea, or statement that injures its originator.|The politician's attack on his opponent boomeranged and ended up hurting his own campaign.  这个政客对对手的攻击适得其反，最终损害了他自己的竞选活动。|Synonyms: recoil, backfire, rebound|Antonyms:  straight shot, direct hit|noun, verb
