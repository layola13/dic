brittler:/ˈbrɪtlər/ | adj. | 更脆的，更易碎的 | Comparative form of brittle: more brittle.  Meaning more easily broken or cracked. | The old plastic became brittler over time and eventually cracked.  这旧塑料随着时间的推移变得更脆，最终破裂了。|The dry twig was brittler than the green one.  干树枝比绿树枝更脆。|近义词:fragiler, more delicate, more friable | 反义词: tougher, stronger, more flexible | 词性:形容词

brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/ | adj. | 脆的，易碎的 | Hard but liable to break or shatter easily. | Glass is a brittle material. 玻璃是一种易碎的材料。| The biscuits were dry and brittle.  饼干又干又脆。|近义词: fragile, friable, crisp | 反义词: tough, resilient, flexible | 词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *bryttian "to break," from Proto-Germanic *breutan (source also of Old Norse brjóta "to break," Dutch breken, German brechen). Related to break.
