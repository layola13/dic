例句：After weeks of elucubration, she finally solved the complex mathematical problem.  经过数周的苦思冥想，她终于解决了这个复杂的数学问题。|近义词：meditation, contemplation, deliberation, study, analysis|反义词:superficiality, thoughtlessness|词性:noun
