overcautious: /ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/ | adj. | 过分谨慎的，过于小心谨慎的 | Excessively cautious; too careful. Showing or characterized by excessive or undue caution. | He was overcautious in his investments, missing out on some great opportunities. 他在投资方面过于谨慎，错过了一些绝佳的机会。

She's overcautious about letting her children play outside. 她对让孩子们在外面玩耍过于谨慎。 | 近义词: careful, prudent, cautious, wary, circumspect | 反义词: reckless, careless, impulsive, rash, imprudent | 词性: adjective

词源: From over- + cautious.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," and "cautious" comes from the Latin word "cautus," meaning "wary" or "careful."
