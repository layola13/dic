elaphine:/ˈɛləfaɪn/| adj.| 鹿的，似鹿的；优雅的|Relating to or resembling an elaphine; graceful and elegant. |The elaphine grace of the dancer captivated the audience.  舞者如鹿般优雅的舞姿迷住了观众。|近义词：deer-like, cervine, graceful, elegant|反义词：clumsy, awkward|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *elaphinus, from Latin elaphus (“deer, stag”), from Ancient Greek ἔλαφος (élaphos, “deer, stag”).
