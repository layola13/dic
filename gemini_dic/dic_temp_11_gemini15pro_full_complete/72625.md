ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēogende (attested only as flēogende *fugelas "flying birds", literally "flying fowls"), present participle of flēogan "to fly".  From Proto-Germanic *fleuganą "to fly".  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pleuk- "to float, swim, fly".
