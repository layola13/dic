glyceride: /ˈɡlɪsərˌaɪd/ | n. | 甘油酯；甘油化物 | An ester formed from glycerol and one or more fatty acids. Glycerides are the principal constituents of animal fats and vegetable oils. | Vegetable oils are mostly composed of triglycerides.  植物油主要由甘油三酯组成。

| Triglycerides are a type of glyceride. 甘油三酯是一种甘油酯。 | synonym: acylglycerol,  fat, lipid | antonym:  N/A | noun
