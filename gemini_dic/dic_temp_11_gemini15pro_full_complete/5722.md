alveary: /ˈælviəri/ (US), /ˈælviəri/ (UK)| n. |蜂房；养蜂场|A place where bees are kept, especially a hive or a collection of hives.|The beekeeper checked the health of his alveary regularly. 养蜂人定期检查他蜂房的健康状况。|例句：The alveary was buzzing with activity. 蜂房里嗡嗡作响，一片繁忙。|例句：He built an alveary in his backyard to harvest honey. 他在后院建了一个蜂房来收集蜂蜜。|近义词：apiary, beehive, hive|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alvearium*, from *alveārius* (“of or belonging to a beehive”), from *alveus* ("cavity, hollow, beehive").
