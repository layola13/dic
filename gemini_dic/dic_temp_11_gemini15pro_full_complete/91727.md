hyperdelicacies:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkəsiz/
|n.|
|极品佳肴，珍馐美馔|
|A blend of "hyper" (meaning excessive or extreme) and "delicacies" (meaning choice or exquisite foods). It refers to extremely rare, luxurious, and often expensive food items.|
|The chef prepared a feast of hyperdelicacies for the visiting dignitaries.|主厨为来访的贵宾准备了一场极品盛宴。|
|The restaurant is known for its hyperdelicacies, including white truffles and caviar.|这家餐厅以其珍馐美馔而闻名，包括白松露和鱼子酱。|
|近义词: delicacies, gourmet food, luxury food|
|反义词: staple food, basic food|
|词性: noun|
