latest:/ˈleɪtɪst/| adj.|最新的；最近的|most recent; the most up-to-date|例句：This is the latest news from the front. 这是来自前线最新的消息。|近义词：newest, recent, current|反义词：oldest, earliest|词性:形容词

latest:/ˈleɪtɪst/| n.|最新事物；最新消息|the most recent development, information, or product|例句：Have you heard the latest about the election? 你听说过关于选举的最新消息吗？|近义词：newest thing, recent development|反义词：oldest thing, past event|词性:名词
