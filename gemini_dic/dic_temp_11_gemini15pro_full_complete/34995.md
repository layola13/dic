cloisterless:/ˈklɔɪstərlɪs/
adjective
无修道院的；不受约束的；非隐居的
Not confined to a cloister; not secluded or isolated.
She led a cloisterless life, engaging with the world and its many challenges. 她过着不受约束的生活，积极应对世界及其诸多挑战。
The artist, though introspective, chose a cloisterless existence, finding inspiration in the bustling city. 这位艺术家虽然喜欢内省，但却选择了一种非隐居的生活，在繁华的城市中寻找灵感。
近义词: uncloistered, worldly, secular, open, public
反义词: cloistered, secluded, isolated, monastic, withdrawn
词性: adjective

词源:
From cloister + -less.  "Cloister" comes from Old French "cloistre" and ultimately from Latin "claustrum" meaning "lock, bar, bolt, barrier," hence an enclosed space. The suffix "-less" indicates the absence of something.
