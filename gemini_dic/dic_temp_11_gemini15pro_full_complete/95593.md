incommensurable:/ɪnkəmɛnʃərəbl/| adj.|不可通衡的，不可公度的|The two systems of measurement are incommensurable. 这两种测量系统是不可通衡的。|His contribution to the project is incommensurable with anyone else's. 他对这个项目的贡献与任何其他人都是不可通衡的。|近义词：incompatible, disproportionate, incomparable|反义词：commensurable, comparable, compatible|词性:形容词
