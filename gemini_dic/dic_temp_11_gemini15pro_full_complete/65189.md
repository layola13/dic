ethopoetic:/ˌeθoʊpoʊˈetɪk/
adjective
人种诗学的；民族诗学的
Relating to or concerned with the representation of the character or ethos of a person, group, or culture, especially in literature.  Creating a sense of the unique spirit of a people or time.
例句：Her ethopoetic approach to novel writing vividly captures the spirit of the American South. 她人种诗学的小说写作方法生动地捕捉了美国南方的精神。

例句：The ethopoetic elements in the film effectively conveyed the cultural nuances of the indigenous community.  电影中的人种诗学元素有效地传达了土著社区的文化细微之处。


近义词：cultural, ethnographic, anthropological
反义词：ahistorical
