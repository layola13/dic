chert:/tʃɜːrt/ | n. | 燧石；角岩 | A hard, fine-grained sedimentary rock consisting of microcrystalline or cryptocrystalline quartz. It is very similar to flint and sometimes used interchangeably with it. |  The arrowheads were fashioned from chert. 箭头是用燧石做成的。| synonym: flint, jasper, chalcedony | antonym: (No direct antonym for a rock type. Could consider softer rocks like chalk or shale as contrasting examples.) | noun
