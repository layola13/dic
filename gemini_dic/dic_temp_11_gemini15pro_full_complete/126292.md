myocarditis:/ˌmaɪoʊkɑːrˈdaɪtɪs/| n.|心肌炎|Inflammation of the myocardium, the muscular tissue of the heart, often caused by viral infections.|He was diagnosed with myocarditis after experiencing chest pain and shortness of breath. 他在经历了胸痛和呼吸急促后被诊断出患有心肌炎。|近义词:carditis|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  myo-  +  -cardi-  +  -itis.  "myo-" comes from Greek "mys" meaning "muscle". "-cardi-" comes from Greek "kardia" meaning "heart". "-itis" is a suffix denoting inflammation.
