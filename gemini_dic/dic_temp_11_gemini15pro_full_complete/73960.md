fortification:/ˌfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 防御工事，强化，增强 |The city's fortifications were strong enough to withstand the siege. 这座城市的防御工事足以抵御围困。| The fortification of the levees prevented further flooding.  堤坝的加固防止了进一步的洪水泛滥。 |  Synonyms: reinforcement, strengthening, bulwark, rampart | Antonyms: weakening, undermining | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: fortificacioun, from Old French fortification, from Late Latin fortification-, fortificatio, from Latin fortificare ‘make strong’, from fortis ‘strong’.
