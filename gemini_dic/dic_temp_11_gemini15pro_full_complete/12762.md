assignat:/ˈæsɪɡnæt; ˌæsɪˈnjɑːt/| n. |指券（法国大革命时期发行的纸币）|A piece of paper money issued by the French revolutionary government (1789-96) on the security of confiscated lands.|The assignats, initially intended to solve the financial crisis, eventually led to hyperinflation.|Synonyms: paper money, currency, banknote|Antonyms: commodity money, gold standard|Noun
