apprentice:/əˈprɛntɪs/| n.  |学徒，徒弟|A person who is learning a trade or craft from a skilled employer, having agreed to work for a fixed period at low wages.|例句：The apprentice carpenter was eager to learn the trade.  这位木匠学徒渴望学习这门手艺。

例句：She worked as an apprentice for several years before opening her own bakery.  她在自己开面包店之前做了好几年的学徒。|近义词：trainee, learner, novice, beginner|反义词：expert, master, professional|词性:noun


apprentice:/əˈprɛntɪs/| v. |使当学徒，以学徒方式受雇|Employ (someone) as an apprentice.|例句：He was apprenticed to a local blacksmith. 他被安排在当地一位铁匠那里当学徒。

例句：The company apprentices young people to skilled trades.  这家公司让年轻人学习熟练的技艺。|近义词：train, instruct, teach|反义词：graduate, master|词性:verb
