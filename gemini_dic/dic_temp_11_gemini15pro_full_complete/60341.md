egomanias: /ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniəz/ | n. | 极度自负；极端利己主义 |  An obsessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own needs; excessive vanity or self-importance.  It's the plural form of "egomania." | His egomanias alienate even his closest friends. 他的极度自负甚至疏远了他最亲密的朋友。| Synonyms: narcissism, vanity, self-centeredness, conceit | Antonyms: humility, modesty, selflessness | noun
