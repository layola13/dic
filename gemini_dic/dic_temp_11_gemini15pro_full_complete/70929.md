firebreaks:/ˈfaɪərbreɪks/| n.|防火带，隔离带|A strip of land cleared of combustible material to prevent the spread of fire. |Firebreaks are crucial in preventing wildfires from spreading. 防火带对于防止野火蔓延至关重要。

The park rangers created firebreaks around the campground. 公园管理员在营地周围设置了防火带。|近义词：fire line, fire stop, fuel break|反义词:fuel, kindling|名词
