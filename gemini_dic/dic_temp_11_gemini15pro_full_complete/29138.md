casein:/ˈkeɪsiːn/ /ˈkeɪsiɪn/| n.|酪蛋白；干酪素|A milk protein that is used in making cheese, paints, glues, and plastics.|Casein is the main protein in cow's milk. 酪蛋白是牛奶中的主要蛋白质。

Casein-based glues are strong and water-resistant.  酪蛋白基胶水强度高且防水。|近义词：milk protein|反义词:|noun
