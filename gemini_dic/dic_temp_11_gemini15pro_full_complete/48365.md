decurion:/dɪˈkjʊəriən/| n. |十夫长；队长；市议员|A commander of a decuria, a unit of ten soldiers in the Roman army, or a member of a municipal council in Roman and Byzantine times.|例句：The decurion led his ten men into battle. 十夫长带领他的十个人投入战斗。

例句：The decurion was a respected member of the town council.  这位十夫长是市议会中受人尊敬的成员。|近义词：leader, commander, councilman|反义词:follower, subordinate|词性:noun
