apteria:/eɪˈptɪəriə/
n.
apteria (plural)
无羽区; 羽迹; 羽域
The apteria are the areas on a bird's skin that are not covered with feathers.
These birds have apteria on their undersides. 这些鸟的腹部有无羽区。
近义词: featherless tracts
反义词: pterylae (羽区)
词性: 名词
