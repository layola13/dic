epizoan:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊən/ | n. | 外寄生动物 | An animal that lives on the exterior of another living animal (the host). |  Example: The epizoan, a type of louse, was found clinging to the fish's gills.  这种外寄生动物，一种虱子，被发现紧紧 clinging 在鱼的鳃上。

Example:  Several epizoans were identified on the turtle's shell.  在乌龟壳上发现了几个外寄生动物。 | 近义词: ectoparasite | 反义词: endoparasite | 词性: noun
