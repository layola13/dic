batfish:/ˈbætˌfɪʃ/| n. |蝙蝠鱼|A fish of the family Ogcocephalidae, typically having a flattened, triangular body and a long, whiplike appendage on the head used to attract prey.  They are found in warm seas worldwide. |例句：The batfish uses its illicium to lure smaller fish close enough to eat. 蝙蝠鱼用它的吻触手引诱小鱼靠近以便捕食。

例句：Divers were amazed to see a batfish "walking" along the seabed. 潜水员惊奇地看到一只蝙蝠鱼在海底“行走”。|近义词：anglerfish, sea toad |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
