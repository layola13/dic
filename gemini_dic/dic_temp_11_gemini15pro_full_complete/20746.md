blanches:/blɑːnʃ/ /blænʃɪz/| v. | 漂白，使变白；(脸色)变苍白；使(蔬菜)变白；烫漂 | to become white or pale, or to make something white or pale, especially by using chemicals or by cooking it quickly in boiling water | The chef blanched the almonds before adding them to the cake.  厨师在把杏仁加入蛋糕之前先将它们烫漂。| synonyms: whiten, pale, bleach | antonyms: darken, color, flush | verb

blanche:/blɑːnʃ/ /blænʃ/| n. | 空白; 白色的东西 | a blank space or a white object |  I left the last line of the form blanche. 我把表格的最后一行留空了。| synonyms: blank, void, emptiness | antonyms: fullness, completeness | noun
