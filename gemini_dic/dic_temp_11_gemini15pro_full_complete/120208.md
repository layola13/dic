mineralogical:/ˌmɪnərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 矿物学的 | Relating to the scientific study of minerals, their composition, structure, properties, and occurrence. |  The museum boasts an impressive mineralogical collection.  博物馆拥有一系列令人叹为观止的矿物藏品。|  The geologist conducted a mineralogical analysis of the rocks. 地质学家对岩石进行了矿物学分析。 | 近义词: geological, petrological | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From mineral + -logical. "Mineral" comes from Medieval Latin "minerale," meaning "something mined," from "minera," meaning "mine." "-logical" comes from "-logic" + "-al," ultimately from Greek "logos," meaning "word," "reason," or "study."
