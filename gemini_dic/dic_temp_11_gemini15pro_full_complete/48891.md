defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/
verb
欺骗，诈骗
To illegally obtain money from someone by deception.
He was found guilty of defrauding investors of millions of dollars. 他被判犯有诈骗投资者数百万美元的罪行。
The company defrauded the government by submitting false claims. 这家公司通过提交虚假索赔来欺骗政府。
Synonyms: cheat, swindle, deceive, trick
Antonyms: compensate, repay, reimburse
词性: 动词
