canescent:/kəˈnɛsənt/| adj.|灰白的，老化的|Becoming gray or whitish; grayish.  Covered with short, fine, whitish hairs or down.|例句：The canescent leaves of the plant shimmered in the sunlight.  这种植物灰白的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。|例句：The old man's canescent beard reached down to his chest. 老人的灰白胡须垂到胸前。|近义词：hoary, grayish, silvery|反义词：dark, youthful, vibrant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *canescentem*, present participle of *canescō* (“to become gray or white”), from *canus* (“gray, white, hoary”).
