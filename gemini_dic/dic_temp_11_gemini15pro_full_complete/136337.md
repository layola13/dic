nonresident:/ˌnɒnˈrɛzɪdənt/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈrɛzɪdənt/ (UK)| n.|非居民|A person who does not live in a particular place or country.|例句：Nonresidents are not eligible to vote in local elections. 非居民没有资格在地方选举中投票。|近义词：foreigner, alien, outsider|反义词:resident, local|词性:名词

词源: non- (not) + resident (居住者)  From Latin *non resident- ‘not residing’, from the verb residēre.
