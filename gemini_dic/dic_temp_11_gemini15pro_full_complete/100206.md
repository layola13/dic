interrhymed:/ˌɪntərˈraɪmd/ | adj. | 内部押韵的 |  A term describing lines of poetry where rhymes occur within the lines, rather than just at the end.  | Example: "The cat sat back, the rat attacked." | 近义词: internal rhyming, leonine rhyme | 反义词: end-rhymed | 词性: adjective
