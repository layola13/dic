flathead:/ˈflætˌhɛd/ (US) /ˈflætˌhed/ (UK)| n. adj.|平头鱼，平头螺钉，愚笨的人|A fish with a flattened head, a type of screw with a flat top, or a derogatory term for a stupid person.|例句：He caught a large flathead in the river. 他在河里钓到了一条大平头鱼。|近义词：(fish) bullhead, catfish; (screw) countersunk screw; (person) idiot, fool|反义词：(person) genius, intelligent person|词性:名词，形容词

flathead:/ˈflætˌhɛd/ (US) /ˈflætˌhed/ (UK)| adj.|平头的，愚笨的|Having a flat head.|例句：The flathead screwdriver wouldn't fit. 平头螺丝刀拧不进去。|近义词：(shape) level, even; (person) stupid, unintelligent|反义词：(shape) rounded, curved; (person) smart, intelligent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *flat* + *head*.  The literal meaning of "having a flat head" led to both the fish and screw sense, and then pejoratively to the sense of "stupid person."
