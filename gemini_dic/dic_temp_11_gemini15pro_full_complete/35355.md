co-guardianship:/ˌkoʊˈɡɑːrdiənʃɪp/ |n.|共同监护|The sharing of guardianship responsibilities, typically between two adults, often parents who are separated or divorced.|例句：The divorced parents agreed on co-guardianship of their children. 这对离婚的父母同意共同监护他们的孩子。

例句：Co-guardianship can be a challenging but rewarding arrangement. 共同监护可能是一项具有挑战性但又有益的安排。

|近义词：joint custody, shared guardianship|反义词：sole custody|词性:名词
