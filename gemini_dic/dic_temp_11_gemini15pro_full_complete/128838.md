nightwards:/ˈnaɪtwədz/| adv.|向夜晚；夜间|Towards night; during the night.|The birds flew nightwards. 鸟儿朝着夜空飞去。|  He drifted nightwards, seeking a place to rest. 他向夜晚走去，寻找一个休息的地方。|近义词:towards night, after dark, at nightfall|反义词:daywards, dawnwards|词性:副词
Etymology: From "night" + "-wards" (a suffix indicating direction).  "Night" comes from Old English "niht," and "-wards" from Old English "-weardes."
