futureless:/ˈfjuːtʃərlɪs/| adj.|没有未来的，没有前途的|Without a future; having no prospects.|He felt futureless, trapped in a dead-end job. 他感觉没有未来，被困在一个没有前途的工作中。|The futureless youth wandered the streets aimlessly.  没有未来的年轻人漫无目的地在街上游荡。|近义词：hopeless, bleak, despairing, despondent|反义词:promising, hopeful, bright|词性:形容词

词源: future (n.) + -less (suffix)

future: From Old French futur, from Latin futurus, future participle of esse "to be."

-less: Old English -lēas "free from, without, devoid of," from Proto-Germanic *-lausaz (cognates: Old Saxon -los, Old Frisian -les, Dutch -loos, German -los, Gothic -laus), from PIE root *leudh- "to grow up, be strong."
