euthanise:/ˈjuːθənaɪz/ /ˈjuːθəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness. | The vet euthanised the dog to end its suffering. 兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死以结束它的痛苦。 |  The terminally ill patient was euthanised at his request.  身患绝症的病人在他的要求下被执行了安乐死。 | synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitate, revive | verb

euthanized:/ˈjuːθənaɪzd/ /ˈjuːθəˌnaɪzd/ | v. | 安乐死（euthanise的过去式和过去分词） | Past tense and past participle of euthanise. | The cat was euthanized after the accident.  猫在事故后被安乐死了。 | The suffering horse was euthanized humanely.  这匹受苦的马被 humane 地实施了安乐死。| synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitated, revived | verb
