internationalisms:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/| n.|国际主义，国际通用词汇|Words or expressions that are used in several languages with the same or similar meanings.|The use of internationalisms facilitates communication in a globalized world. 国际主义有助于在全球化的世界中进行交流。|Synonyms: globalisms, universalisms|Antonyms: localisms, colloquialisms|Noun
