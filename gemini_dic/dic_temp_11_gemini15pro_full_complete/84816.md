handyman:/ˈhændimæn/| n.|杂工，修理工|A person, often employed casually, who does small jobs involving manual work, especially repairs around the house.|例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个杂工来修理漏水的水龙头。

例句：He's a real handyman and can fix almost anything. 他是一个真正的杂工，几乎什么都能修。|近义词：fixer, repairman, jack-of-all-trades|反义词: specialist |词性:noun
