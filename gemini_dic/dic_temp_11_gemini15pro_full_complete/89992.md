horsly:/ˈhɔːrsli/| adj.|嘶哑的，粗哑的，像马一样的|Characterized by a rough, harsh, and grating voice, like that of a horse; suggestive of a horse.|例句：He spoke in a horsly whisper. 他用嘶哑的声音低语。|近义词：hoarse, raspy, gravelly, husky|反义词:clear, smooth, melodious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From horse + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The original meaning referred to the sound or appearance of a horse, and later extended to describe a rough, hoarse voice.
