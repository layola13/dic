anathematism:/əˈnæθəməˌtɪzəm/
|n.|诅咒，咒逐，革出教门|The act of anathematizing; a curse; a denunciation; excommunication.|The council pronounced anathematism against all who denied the doctrine of the Trinity.  议会对所有否认三位一体教义的人施以咒逐。|Example:  His anathematism of the corrupt government was met with cheers. 他对腐败政府的咒骂引来一片欢呼。|Synonyms: curse, denunciation, excommunication, condemnation, anathema|Antonyms: blessing, benediction, commendation, praise|noun
