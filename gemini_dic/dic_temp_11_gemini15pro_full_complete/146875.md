overinfluencing:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluənsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度影响 | Exerting too much influence; influencing to an excessive degree. | Social media influencers are sometimes accused of overinfluencing their young followers. 社交媒体影响者有时被指责过度影响他们的年轻粉丝。 | The constant barrage of advertising can overinfluence consumer choices.  不断的广告轰炸会过度影响消费者的选择。 |  近义词: dominating, manipulating, controlling | 反义词: underinfluencing, neglecting | 词性: 动词(present participle)

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar, "above, over") + "influence" (from Medieval Latin influentia, from Latin influere, "to flow into")
