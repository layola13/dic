originality:/əˌrɪdʒəˈnæləti/ | n. | 独创性，创造力 | The quality of being new and interesting in a way that is different from anything that has existed before. |  Her designs show a lot of originality. 她的设计展现了很强的独创性。| synonyms: creativity, innovation, novelty, freshness, uniqueness | antonyms: banality, commonness, staleness, imitation, unoriginality | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *originalité*, from Late Latin *originalitas*, from Latin *originalis* ("originating, primitive"), from *origo* ("origin").
