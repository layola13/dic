falsetto:/fɔːlˈsɛtoʊ/ (US), /fɔːlˈsɛtəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|假声；假嗓子|A method of voice production used by male singers, especially tenors, to sing notes higher than their normal range.  It is characterized by a breathy, flute-like quality.|He sang the high notes in falsetto. 他用假声唱高音。|近义词：head voice|反义词:chest voice|词性:名词, 形容词

falsetto:/fɔːlˈsɛtoʊ/ (US), /fɔːlˈsɛtəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|假声的；用假声唱的|Produced by or resembling falsetto.|His falsetto voice was surprisingly powerful.  他用假声唱的声音出奇地有力。|近义词：head voice|反义词:chest voice|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *falsetto*, diminutive of *falso* ("false"), from Latin *falsus*.
