globalizer:/ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪzər/| n. |全球化者；促使全球化的人或事物|A person or thing that promotes or causes globalization. |The internet is a powerful globalizer, connecting people and cultures worldwide. 互联网是一个强大的全球化推动者，连接着世界各地的人们和文化。

The company acted as a globalizer, spreading its products and influence across the globe.  这家公司扮演了全球化者的角色，将其产品和影响力传播到全球。|近义词：internationalist, cosmopolitan|反义词：nationalist, isolationist|词性:noun
