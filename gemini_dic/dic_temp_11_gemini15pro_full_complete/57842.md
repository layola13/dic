dressing:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ/ | n. |调味料；敷料；穿衣；打扮|A sauce for salads, meat, etc. / Material used to cover and protect a wound. / The act of putting on clothes. |
例句：The salad dressing is delicious.  沙拉酱很美味。

例句：The nurse changed the dressing on the wound. 护士更换了伤口上的敷料。

近义词：sauce, condiment, bandage, attire, clothing |
反义词：undressing |
词性：noun


dressing:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ/ | n. | 穿衣；打扮 | The act of putting on clothes. |
例句：She spent hours on her hair and dressing. 她花了几个小时做头发和打扮。

例句：He needs help with dressing in the morning. 他早上需要人帮忙穿衣服。

近义词：attire, clothing, apparel |
反义词：undressing |
词性：noun


词源: From Old French "dresser" (to arrange, prepare, straighten), from Frankish *dressojan, from Proto-Germanic *draustijaną (to make ready, prepare).
