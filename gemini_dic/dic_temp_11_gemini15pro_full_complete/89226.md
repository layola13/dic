homeynesses:/ˈhoʊminəsnɪsɪz/
n.
舒适感；亲切感；温馨感
The quality of being homey; coziness, comfort, and familiarity.
The room had a certain homeyness that made her feel relaxed.  这个房间有一种特别的温馨感，让她感到很放松。
homeyness, coziness, comfort, familiarity
unfamiliarity, coldness, discomfort, formality
noun


homey:/ˈhoʊmi/
adj.
舒适的；亲切的；温馨的
Cozy, comfortable, and familiar, like home.
The cabin was small but homey, with a crackling fire in the hearth.  小屋虽小，但很温馨，壁炉里的火噼啪作响。
cozy, comfortable, familiar, inviting
unfamiliar, cold, uncomfortable, formal
adjective
