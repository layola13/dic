assimilatory:/əˈsɪmələˌtɔːri/ |adj.|同化的，吸收的|Relating to or involving assimilation; causing assimilation.  The process of absorbing or integrating something into a larger whole.|The government implemented assimilatory policies towards immigrants. 政府对移民实施了同化政策。|Synonyms: integrative, incorporative, absorbing|Antonyms: segregative, divisive|adjective

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ |v.|同化，吸收|To absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture. To fully understand and incorporate information or ideas.|The company assimilated the new employees quickly. 公司很快吸收了新员工。|Synonyms: integrate, incorporate, absorb|Antonyms: segregate, separate|verb

assimilation:/əˌsɪməˈleɪʃn/ |n.|同化，吸收|The process of taking in and fully understanding information or ideas.|Cultural assimilation can be a complex process. 文化同化可能是一个复杂的过程。|Synonyms: integration, incorporation, absorption|Antonyms: segregation, separation|noun
