overcentralizes:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzɪz/
|v.|过度集中; 过分中央集权|To concentrate excessive power or control in a central authority or location.|The new CEO overcentralized decision-making, stifling innovation in the branch offices. 新任首席执行官过度集中决策权，扼杀了分支机构的创新。|centralizes, concentrates|decentralizes, distributes|verb
overcentralized:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzd/
|adj.|过度集中的; 过分中央集权的|Characterized by excessive concentration of power or control in a central authority or location.|The company's overcentralized structure hindered its ability to respond quickly to market changes. 该公司过度集中的结构阻碍了其快速响应市场变化的能力。|centralized, concentrated|decentralized, distributed|adjective
overcentralization:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|过度集中; 过分中央集权|The excessive concentration of power or control in a central authority or location.|The overcentralization of power in the government led to widespread discontent. 政府权力的过度集中导致了普遍的不满。|centralization, concentration|decentralization, distribution|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + centralize.  "Centralize" comes from the French "centraliser," from "central" ultimately derived from Latin "centralis."  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "across," or "excessive."
