deoxygenize:/diːˈɒksɪdʒənaɪz/
verb
脱氧；除去…的氧
To remove oxygen from something.
The blood is deoxygenized as it passes through the capillaries. 血液流经毛细血管时被脱氧。
deoxidize, deoxydize
oxygenate, oxidize
