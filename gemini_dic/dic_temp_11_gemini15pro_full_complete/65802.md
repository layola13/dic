evidencing:/ˈɛvɪdənˌsɪŋ/
verb
表明; 证明
serving as or providing evidence
The data obtained from the experiment is evidencing a new trend in consumer behavior.  从实验中获得的数据表明了消费者行为的新趋势。
show, demonstrate, prove, indicate, display
conceal, hide, obscure, mask
v.
