fiats:/ˈfiːæt/ /ˈfaɪæt/| n.|命令; 法令; 批准; (尤指政府或统治者的)法令，政令|An official order given by somebody in authority.  A decree. |The CEO issued a fiat that all employees must attend the meeting. 首席执行官发布命令，所有员工必须参加会议。|Synonyms: decree, edict, order, command, mandate|Antonyms: request, appeal, petition|noun
