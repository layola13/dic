mooningly:/ˈmuːnɪŋli/
adverb
痴痴地，呆呆地，出神地
In a dreamy, abstracted, or listless manner, as if lost in thought, often about something romantic.
She gazed mooningly at the photograph of her beloved. 她痴痴地凝视着爱人的照片。

She smiled mooningly, lost in her romantic daydreams.  她痴痴地笑着，沉浸在浪漫的白日梦里。

dreamily, abstractedly, wistfully, longingly
alertly, attentively, practically
adverb
