corroded:/kəˈroʊdɪd/
adjective
腐蚀的，生锈的
weakened or destroyed by chemical action
The metal was badly corroded by the salt water. 金属被盐水严重腐蚀了。
近义词:rusted, eroded, decayed, deteriorated
反义词:preserved, protected, intact, pristine
词性:adjective
