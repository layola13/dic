nonconclusions:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːʒənz/
noun
无结论；未得出的结论；悬而未决的事情
The investigation ended in nonconclusions, leaving many questions unanswered.  调查以无结论告终，留下许多未解之谜。
The report was full of nonconclusions and lacked any definitive findings.  这份报告充满了未得出的结论，缺乏任何明确的发现。
non- + conclusion
non-: prefix meaning "not"
conclusion: a judgement or decision reached by reasoning
Synonyms: indecision, ambiguity, uncertainty, unresolved matters, dead end
Antonyms: conclusions, findings, results, resolutions, closure
n.
