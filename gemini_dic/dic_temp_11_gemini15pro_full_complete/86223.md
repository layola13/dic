heathier:/ˈhelθiər/ (US), /ˈheθiər/ (UK)| adj.|更健康的|Comparative form of healthy.  Indicates a greater degree of health or well-being.|She's made a lot of changes and is much healthier now. 她做了很多改变，现在健康多了。|He chose the healthier option of grilled chicken instead of fried. 他选择了更健康的烤鸡而不是炸鸡。|近义词：fitter, stronger, well|反义词：sicker, unhealthier, less healthy|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *helthiyere, comparative of helthi (“healthy”), from Old English hǣlþig (“healthy”), from Proto-Germanic *hailiþiją (“healthy”), from Proto-Indo-European *kóil- (“whole, uninjured”).
