mutandis:/mjuːˈtændɪs/| adv.|相应地；比照办理；|With the necessary changes having been made; (used when comparing two or more cases or situations).|例句：The rule applies mutatis mutandis to all students. 这条规则比照适用于所有学生。

例句：The same logic applies, mutatis mutandis, to the case of the euro. 同样的逻辑，比照办理，也适用于欧元的情况。|近义词：correspondingly, similarly, accordingly|反义词: dissimilarly, differently|词性:副词
