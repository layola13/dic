coteau:/koʊˈtoʊ/ (US), /ˈkəʊtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |山坡；斜坡；小山|A hillside or slope, especially one covered with vineyards.  Specifically, a raised area of land bordering a river valley.|The coteau offered a stunning view of the valley below. 山坡提供了山谷底下令人惊叹的景色。|Synonyms: hillside, slope, bluff, escarpment|Antonyms: valley, plain|noun

coteaux:/koʊˈtoʊz/ (US), /ˈkəʊtəʊz/ (UK)| n. |山坡；斜坡；小山（coteau的复数形式）|Plural of coteau.  Hillsides or slopes, especially ones covered with vineyards.|The coteaux of the Loire Valley are famous for their wines.  卢瓦尔河谷的山坡以其葡萄酒而闻名。|Synonyms: hillsides, slopes, bluffs, escarpments|Antonyms: valleys, plains|noun
