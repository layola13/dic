collusion:/kəˈluːʒən/| n.|勾结，共谋，串通|Secret or illegal cooperation or conspiracy, especially in order to cheat or deceive others.|The investigation revealed collusion between several companies to fix prices. 调查揭露了几家公司之间串通操纵价格。|近义词：conspiracy, collaboration, connivance|反义词：honesty, integrity|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French collusion, from Latin collusio(n-) ‘fraudulent agreement’, from colludere ‘play together, conspire’, from col- ‘together’ + ludere ‘play’.
