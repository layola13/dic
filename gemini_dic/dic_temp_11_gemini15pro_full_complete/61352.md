embellishment:/ɪmˈbelɪʃmənt/| n.|装饰；修饰；润色|Something added to make something else more beautiful or interesting.  An addition of something to make a story more interesting.|The dress was simple, but the embellishments on the sleeves made it elegant. 这件裙子很简单，但袖子上的装饰使它显得优雅。|Synonyms: adornment, decoration, ornamentation, enhancement|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun
