blacksmithing: /ˈblækˌsmɪθɪŋ/ /ˈblækˌsmɪθɪŋ/
n.
铁匠活，锻造
The act, process, or trade of a blacksmith.
He learned blacksmithing from his father.  他从他父亲那里学习了锻造。
Near synonyms:  forging, smithing, metalworking
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider) idleness, inactivity
词性: noun


blacksmithings:  (Not commonly used as a plural noun, usually considered uncountable)
n. (rare)
多种铁匠制品或技术
Different types of blacksmith work or crafts.
(This usage is uncommon. It would more likely be phrased as "types of blacksmithing" or "blacksmithing techniques.")
The blacksmithings on display showed a variety of skills.  (Awkward, better phrased as: The blacksmithing work on display...)
Near synonyms:  (No direct synonyms in this rare usage)
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)
词性: noun (plural, rarely used)
