decameron:/ˌdekəˈmɛrən/ | n. |十日谈；十篇故事集| A collection of one hundred tales told over ten days by a group of seven young women and three young men sheltering in a villa outside Florence to escape the Black Death. |  Have you read Boccaccio's Decameron? 你读过薄伽丘的《十日谈》吗？ | synonyms: collection, anthology, compilation | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a collection of stories, potentially "single story," "monologue") | noun
