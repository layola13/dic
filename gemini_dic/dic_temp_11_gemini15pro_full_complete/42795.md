couples: /ˈkʌpəlz/ | n. | 夫妇，情侣；数个，几个 | Two or more people who are married or in a romantic relationship; several or a few of something. |  The restaurant was full of couples enjoying a romantic dinner.  这家餐厅坐满了享受浪漫晚餐的情侣。| synonyms: pairs, duos, twosomes | antonyms: singles, individuals | noun

couples: /ˈkʌpəlz/ | v. | 连接，结合 | To join or connect two or more things together. | The railroad company couples the carriages together.  铁路公司将车厢连接在一起。 | synonyms: joins, connects, links | antonyms: separates, disconnects, divides | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cople (Modern French couple), from Latin copula (“a link, tie, band”), from co- (“together”) + apere (“to fasten, attach”).
