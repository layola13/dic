blinding:/ˈblaɪndɪŋ/| adj.| 耀眼的，使人炫目的；使人眼花缭乱的；使人目眩的；（疼痛）剧烈的 |Causing temporary blindness or dazzlement because of excessive brightness; extremely bright.  Overwhelming; very great.  (of pain) extremely severe.| The blinding flash of the explosion temporarily disoriented everyone. 爆炸的强光使每个人暂时失去了方向感。| synonyms: dazzling, glaring, overwhelming, intense | antonyms: dim, faint, dull | adjective

blinding:/ˈblaɪndɪŋ/| n.| 使失明的东西；使眼花的东西|Something that blinds or dazzles.|The sun's glare was a blinding. 阳光很刺眼。| synonyms: glare, dazzle | antonyms: darkness, shade | noun
