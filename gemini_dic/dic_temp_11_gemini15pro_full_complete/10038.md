aplacentata:/əˌpleɪsɛnˈteɪtə/ | n. |无胎盘类|A subclass of mammals characterized by the absence of a placenta, including marsupials and monotremes.  These animals give birth to relatively undeveloped young. |例句：The aplacentata, such as kangaroos and platypuses, represent a fascinating branch of mammalian evolution.  无胎盘类动物，例如袋鼠和鸭嘴兽，代表了哺乳动物进化中一个引人入胜的分支。

例句：Studying the aplacentata provides valuable insights into the diverse reproductive strategies of mammals. 研究无胎盘类动物为了解哺乳动物多样化的繁殖策略提供了宝贵的见解。 |近义词：Metatheria (for marsupials), Prototheria (for monotremes)|反义词：Placentalia (Eutheria)|词性：noun
