delineate:/dɪˈlɪnieɪt/| v. |描绘，勾勒，描述|to describe or portray (something) precisely|The artist delineated the scene with delicate strokes. 艺术家用细腻的笔触描绘了这个场景。|近义词：describe, portray, depict, outline, sketch|反义词：obscure, confuse|词性:动词

delineated:/dɪˈlɪnieɪtɪd/| adj. |被描绘的，被勾勒的|clearly described or portrayed|The report provided a clearly delineated plan of action. 报告提供了一个清晰的行动计划。|近义词：described, portrayed, depicted, outlined, sketched|反义词：obscure, confused|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin  *dēlineātus*, past participle of  *dēlineāre* “to sketch out,” from  *dē-* "completely" +  *lineāre* "to make a line," from *līnea* "line."
