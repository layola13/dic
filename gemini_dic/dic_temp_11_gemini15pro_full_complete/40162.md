contending:/kənˈtɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 竞争的，争夺的 |  Competing, struggling, or vying, especially for a position or prize.  |  Many teams are contending for the championship this year.  今年许多队伍都在争夺冠军。|  competing, vying, struggling, battling | cooperating, yielding, submitting | adjective

contend:/kənˈtɛnd/ | v. | 竞争，争夺，声称，主张 | 1. To strive in rivalry or competition; to vie. 2. To assert something as a position in an argument. | 1.  He contended with his brother for the inheritance. 他与兄弟争夺遗产。 2.  I contend that this is the best course of action. 我认为这是最好的行动方案。|  compete, vie, argue, assert, maintain | yield, surrender, concede | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "contendere" (to stretch, strive, contend), from "com-" (together) + "tendere" (to stretch).
