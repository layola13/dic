coolnesses:/ˈkuːlnəsɪz/
noun
冷静；冷淡；凉爽
The state or quality of being cool.
例句：He handled the crisis with remarkable coolness. 他以非凡的冷静处理了危机。

例句：The coolness of the evening air was refreshing.  傍晚空气的凉爽令人神清气爽。

近义词：composure, calmness, equanimity, indifference, coldness
反义词：warmth, heat, excitement, agitation, passion
词性:n.
