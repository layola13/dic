contemplators:/ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtərz/ | n. | 沉思者；冥想者 | People who engage in contemplation; those who think deeply and thoughtfully about something. | The contemplators sat in silence, pondering the meaning of life. 沉思者们静静地坐着，思考着生命的意义。|  The monks were contemplators, dedicating their lives to prayer and reflection. 僧侣们是沉思者，将他们的一生奉献给祈祷和反思。 | synonyms: thinkers, meditators, ponderers, musers, reflectors | antonyms: doers, actors, activists | noun

contemplate:/ˈkɑːntəmpleɪt/ | v. | 沉思；冥想；思考 | To think profoundly and at length; to consider thoughtfully. | She contemplated the offer for a while before making a decision. 她仔细考虑了一段时间才做出决定。 | He contemplated the vastness of the universe. 他思考着宇宙的浩瀚。| synonyms: consider, ponder, meditate, muse, reflect | antonyms: ignore, disregard, neglect | verb
