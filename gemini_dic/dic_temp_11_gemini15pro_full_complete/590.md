abouts:/əˈbaʊts/ | prep. | 大约，关于，在...周围 | Concerning or related to something; approximately; in the vicinity of. |  He knew nothing about the meeting.  他对此次会议一无所知。

The book is about the history of China. 这本书是关于中国历史的。 | 近义词: concerning, regarding, respecting, around, near | 反义词: unrelated to, distant from | 词性: 介词(preposition)

词源:  From Middle English *aboutes, from Old English onbūtan (“around, about, outside, beyond”), from on (“on, upon, in, at”) + būtan (“outside, without, except”).  More at on, but.
