corollet: /kɒˈrɒlət/ | n. | 小花冠；花冠裂片 | A small corolla; one of the petals or petal-like parts of a corolla, especially when these are partly fused. | The delicate corollet of the wildflower trembled in the breeze. 野花的纤细花冠在微风中颤抖。| synonym: petal, segment | antonym: calyx | noun
