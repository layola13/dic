geodesic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛsɪk/| adj. n.|测地线的，测地线；短程线|Relating to or denoting the shortest line between two points on a curved surface.|例句：A geodesic dome is a structure built in an almost spherical shape. 测地线穹顶是一种近似球形的建筑结构。|近义词：shortest, direct|反义词: indirect, longest|词性:adjective

geodesic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛsɪk/| n.|测地线；短程线|The shortest line between two points on a curved surface.|例句：The airplane followed a geodesic route. 飞机沿着测地线路线飞行。|近义词：shortest path, shortest route|反义词: longest route, indirect path|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek "geodaisia" (γεωδαισία), meaning "division of the earth," from "geo-" (γῆ) "earth" + "daiesthai" (δαίεσθαι) "to divide."
