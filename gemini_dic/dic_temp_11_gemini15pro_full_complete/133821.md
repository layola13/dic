nonglossiest:/nɒnˈɡlɒsiɪst/
adjective
最不光滑的
Having the least gloss; most matte.  Lacking a shiny or polished surface.
The nonglossiest paper is best for charcoal drawings.  最不光滑的纸最适合画木炭画。
近义词:matte, dull, flat
反义词:glossy, shiny, polished
词性:adjective
