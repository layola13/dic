outgrowths:/ˈaʊtɡroʊθs/
n.
自然生长物；发展结果；产物
A natural consequence of development or growth.
These bureaucratic outgrowths have stifled innovation. 这些官僚主义的衍生物扼杀了创新。

War is an outgrowth of aggressive instincts. 战争是侵略性本能的产物。

synonyms: consequence, result, product, outcome, development, offshoot, ramification, byproduct, excrescence
antonyms:  cause, origin, source, root

n.
（从较大物体上）生长出来的东西
Something that grows out of a larger body.
The horns are bony outgrowths of the skull. 角是颅骨的骨质生长物。

The tumor was an outgrowth on his liver. 肿瘤是长在他肝脏上的一个赘生物。

synonyms: protuberance, projection, swelling, lump, bulge, protrusion, appendage
antonyms:  indentation, hollow, depression
