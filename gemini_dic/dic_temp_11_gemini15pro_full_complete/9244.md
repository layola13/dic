antipathic:/ˌæn.tɪˈpæ.θɪk/ | adj. | 反感的，厌恶的 | Having a strong aversion or dislike; opposed. |  He felt an antipathic reaction to the politician's speech.  他对这位政客的演讲感到反感。 | synonyms: averse, hostile, antagonistic, repugnant | antonyms: sympathetic, empathetic, attracted, fond | adjective

antipathic:/ˌæn.tɪˈpæ.θɪk/ | adj. | 不合的，不相容的 |  Mutually incompatible; unable to exist together in harmony. |  Their personalities were so antipathic that they couldn't work together.  他们的性格如此不合，以至于无法一起工作。 | synonyms: incompatible, conflicting, clashing, discordant | antonyms: compatible, harmonious, agreeable, concordant | adjective
