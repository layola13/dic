dressiness:/ˈdrɛsɪnɪs/
n.
正式程度; 穿戴讲究的程度
The quality of being dressed formally or elegantly.
The dressiness of the occasion called for a suit and tie.  这个场合的正式程度要求穿西装打领带。
formality, elegance, smartness
casualness, informality
noun

词源：dress + -iness

dress: From Old French *dreis(s)ier ‘to arrange, straighten’, (verb).  The noun, meaning 'clothing', arose from the verb.
-iness: a suffix forming nouns chiefly from adjectives, meaning ‘state or quality of being…’ (darkness; happiness;liness; readiness).
