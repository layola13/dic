break-even:/ˌbreɪk ˈiːvən/ | adjective, noun | 盈亏平衡的；盈亏平衡点 | The point at which revenue equals total costs, resulting in neither profit nor loss. | The company reached break-even point after two years of losses.  这家公司在亏损两年后达到了盈亏平衡点。| synonyms: cost-effective, self-supporting | antonyms: loss-making, unprofitable | adjective
break-even:/ˌbreɪk ˈiːvən/ | noun | 盈亏平衡点 | The point at which revenue equals total costs. | The break-even for the project is estimated at $1 million. 该项目的盈亏平衡点估计为 100 万美元。| synonyms: equilibrium point | antonyms: loss point | noun
