caviller:/ˈkævələr/| n.|吹毛求疵的人；挑剔者|A caviller is someone who raises trivial objections or makes petty criticisms.|He's a caviller who finds fault with everything. 他是个吹毛求疵的人，对任何事情都挑剔。|近义词：critic, nitpicker, faultfinder, carper|反义词:supporter, admirer|词性:noun
cavil:/ˈkævəl/| v.|吹毛求疵；挑剔；反对|To cavil is to raise trivial objections or make petty criticisms.|He cavilled at the wording of the contract. 他对合同的措辞吹毛求疵。|近义词：criticize, nitpick, carp, object|反义词:praise, approve|词性:verb
