hauled:/hɔːld/ | v. | 拖；拉；拽；费力地搬运；运输；用力拉；（尤指为改变方向）猛拉；使（车）停下；把…拖到（特定地方）；（将船）拖上岸；（尤指正式地）传唤；提审 |  To pull or drag something heavy with effort. |  The fishermen hauled the net onto the deck. 渔民们把网拖到甲板上。| synonyms: pull, drag, tug, heave, draw,  transport, convey | antonyms: push, shove | verb

hauled:/hɔːld/ | v. | (informal) criticize severely | To criticize someone severely. | The coach hauled the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。 | synonyms: criticize, reprimand, scold, berate | antonyms: praise, commend | verb
