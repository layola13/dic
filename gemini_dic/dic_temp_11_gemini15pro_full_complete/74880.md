freshed: /freʃt/
adjective
新鲜的；清新的；精神饱满的
refreshed, revived. Feeling or appearing renewed and invigorated.
例句：After a good night's sleep, I felt refreshed and ready to tackle the day.  睡了一夜好觉后，我感到精神焕发，准备迎接新的一天。

例句：A cool breeze freshed the air.  一阵凉风使空气清新。
近义词：renewed, revived, invigorated, refreshed
反义词：tired, exhausted, drained, stale
词性: adjective
