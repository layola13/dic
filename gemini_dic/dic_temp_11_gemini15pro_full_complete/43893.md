crete:/kriːt/
n.
concrete
混凝土
A building material made from a mixture of broken stone or gravel, sand, cement, and water, which can be spread or poured into molds and forms a mass resembling stone on hardening.
一种建筑材料，由碎石或砾石、沙子、水泥和水的混合物制成，可以铺展或浇注到模具中，硬化后形成类似石头的块状物。

例句：The building was made of reinforced concrete.  这座建筑是用钢筋混凝土建造的。

例句：The workers poured the concrete for the foundation.  工人们浇筑了地基的混凝土。

近义词：cement 水泥
