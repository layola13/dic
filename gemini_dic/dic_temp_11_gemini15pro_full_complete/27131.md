calisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n.|健美操，徒手体操|Calisthenics are exercises that you do using only your body weight, without special equipment.|She does calisthenics every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上做健美操来保持健康。|Calisthenics is a great way to build strength and flexibility. 健美操是增强力量和柔韧性的好方法。|近义词：bodyweight training, exercise, workout|反义词:inactivity, idleness|词性:noun

calisthenic:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪk/| adj.|健美操的，徒手体操的|Calisthenic exercises are good for building muscle. 健美操对增强肌肉有益。|He prefers calisthenic workouts to weightlifting. 他更喜欢徒手体操而不是举重。|近义词：gymnic, athletic|反义词:sedentary|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kalos* (beautiful) + *sthenos* (strength).
