instinctivity:/ɪnˌstɪŋkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |本能;天性|The quality or state of being instinctive; reliance on instinct.  The tendency to act on instinct. |例句：The cat's instinctivity led it to stalk the mouse. 猫的本能驱使它跟踪老鼠。

例句：Her instinctivity told her something was wrong. 她的直觉告诉她有些不对劲。|近义词：instinct, intuition, innate behavior |反义词: learned behavior, reason, rationality |词性:noun
