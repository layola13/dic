mosquito-net:/məˈskiːtəʊ net/ (UK) /məˈskiːtoʊ net/ (US)| n. |蚊帐|A net hung over a bed or other sleeping area, intended to keep out mosquitoes and other insects.|例句：We slept under mosquito nets to avoid being bitten. 我们睡在蚊帐里以免被叮咬。|近义词：bed net, insect net|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open air")|noun
