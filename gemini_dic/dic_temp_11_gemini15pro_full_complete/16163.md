bankable:/ˈbæŋkəbl/| adj.|可靠的，有赢利保障的，值得信赖的|Likely to make money; reliable and trustworthy.  Relating to a person or project considered likely to be financially successful.|例句：He's a bankable star, guaranteed to draw a crowd. 他是一位有票房保证的明星，肯定能吸引大批观众。

例句：The project is considered bankable because of its strong financial backing.  由于其强大的财政支持，该项目被认为是可靠的。|近义词：reliable, dependable, trustworthy, credible, marketable, lucrative|反义词：unreliable, risky, uncertain|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bank (n.) + -able.  Originally referred to something acceptable as security for a loan. The sense of "likely to be financially successful" developed later.
