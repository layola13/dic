aerodynamic:/ˌɛərəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 空气动力学的；流线型的 | Relating to the movement of air and the forces that act on objects moving through the air, or designed to reduce the effects of these forces. |  The car's aerodynamic design helps it achieve high speeds. 这辆车符合空气动力学的设计有助于它达到高速。| synonyms: streamlined, sleek | antonyms: clunky, boxy | adjective


aerodynamics:/ˌɛərəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 空气动力学 | The study of the movement of air and the forces that act on objects moving through the air. | The aerodynamics of a plane are crucial for its flight. 飞机的空气动力学对其飞行至关重要。| synonyms:  aeromechanics | antonyms: N/A | noun
