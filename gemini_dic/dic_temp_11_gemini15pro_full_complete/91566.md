hyperabsorptions:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈzɔːrpʃənz/
|n.|超吸收；过度吸收|The process of absorbing something to an excessive degree.  It refers to the capacity to absorb substances, typically liquids, to a greater extent than normal.|The soil exhibited hyperabsorption after the heavy rainfall. 土壤在暴雨后呈现出超强的吸收能力。|近义词：overabsorption, excessive absorption|反义词:  poor absorption, limited absorption|词性: Noun
Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, excessive") + "absorption" (Latin "absorbere," meaning "to swallow up")
