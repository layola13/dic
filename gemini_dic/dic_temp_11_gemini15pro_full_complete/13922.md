auletic:/ɔːˈlɛtɪk/
adjective
与吹管乐器有关的；管乐的
Relating to or involving the playing of a pipe or wind instrument, especially in ancient Greek competitions.
The auletic contests were a prominent part of the Panathenaic Games.  泛雅典运动会上，管乐比赛是重要的一部分。
The museum exhibited several ancient auletic instruments.  博物馆展出了几件古代的管乐器。
近义词:wind, piping
反义词:stringed, percussive
词性:adjective

auletic:/ɔːˈlɛtɪk/
noun
吹管乐器演奏者
A player of a pipe or wind instrument, especially in ancient Greek competitions.
The auletes competed for prizes in various musical categories.  吹管乐器演奏者在不同的音乐类别中争夺奖项。
The auletes played a mournful tune at the funeral.  在葬礼上，吹管乐器演奏者演奏了一曲哀乐。
近义词:piper, wind player
反义词:
词性:noun

词源: From Greek *auletikos*, from *aulos* "pipe, flute."
