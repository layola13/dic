cluckier:/ˈklʌkiər/ | adj. | 更想抱窝的，更母性的（用于母鸡）；更爱管闲事的，更爱唠叨的（用于人） |  Comparative form of "clucky": More inclined to brood (as a hen) or more fussily and overprotectively affectionate (as a person). | The hen became much cluckier after her chicks hatched.  那只母鸡在小鸡孵出来后变得更想抱窝了。|  She's getting cluckier as she gets older, always offering unsolicited advice.  她年纪越大越爱管闲事，总是主动提供不必要的建议。|  broodier, fussier, more motherly | less motherly, less fussy | adjective
