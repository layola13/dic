nonspiritedly: /ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtɪdli/ /ˌnɑːnˈspɪrɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 无生气地，缺乏热情地 |  In a way that shows a lack of enthusiasm or energy. | She played the piano nonspiritedly, clearly not enjoying the piece. 她弹钢琴弹得毫无生气，显然不喜欢这首曲子。|  listlessly, apathetically, halfheartedly | enthusiastically, energetically, spiritedly | adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non.
spirited:  From spirit + -ed.  Meaning "full of spirit."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
