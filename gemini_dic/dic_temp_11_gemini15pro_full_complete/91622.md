hyperbarbarous:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːrbərəs/
|adj.|极端野蛮的，极其残暴的|Excessively barbarous; extremely cruel and uncivilized.|
The hyperbarbarous acts of the warlord shocked the international community.|极端野蛮的军阀行为震惊了国际社会。|
The dictator's hyperbarbarous regime was responsible for countless atrocities.|独裁者的极端野蛮的政权造成了无数暴行。
|近义词: savage, brutal, ferocious, barbaric, inhuman, cruel|
|反义词: civilized, humane, compassionate, merciful|
|词性: adjective|


Etymology:
From hyper- + barbarous.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessively," and "barbarous" comes from the Late Latin "barbarus," ultimately from Greek "barbaros," meaning "foreign, strange, non-Greek," imitative of the unintelligible sounds of foreign speech.
