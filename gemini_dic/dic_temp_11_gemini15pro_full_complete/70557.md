filipendulous:/fɪlɪˈpɛndʒələs/ /fɪlɪˈpendʒʊləs/
adjective
悬垂的，下垂的
hanging by a thread and tending to droop; suspended so as to swing freely.
The willow tree's filipendulous branches swayed gently in the breeze. 柳树悬垂的枝条在微风中轻轻摇曳。
The chandelier's filipendulous crystals shimmered in the candlelight. 枝形吊灯下垂的水晶在烛光下闪闪发光。
近义词:pendulous, drooping, hanging
反义词:erect, upright
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *filipendulus, from filum ("thread") + pendulus ("hanging").
