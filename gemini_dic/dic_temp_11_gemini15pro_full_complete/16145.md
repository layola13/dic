banishers:/ˈbænɪʃərz/
noun
驱逐者；流放者
People who banish others; those who exile or expel.
The banishers drove the rebels from the kingdom. 驱逐者将叛乱分子赶出了王国。
The king sent his banishers to deal with the traitors. 国王派他的驱逐者去处理叛徒。
exiles, expellers
settlers, welcomers
n.
