mooed:/muːd/
|v.|哞哞叫|The cow mooed loudly.  母牛大声地哞哞叫。|The contented cows mooed softly in the pasture.  心满意足的奶牛在牧场上轻轻地哞哞叫。|近义词:lowed, bellowed|反义词:(没有明确的反义词)|动词

词源:
From Middle English *mouwen, *mowen, from Old English *mūgian (“to low, bellow”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Dutch moeien (“to moo”), German muhen (“to moo, low”), Swedish möa (“to moo”), Latin mūgīre (“to bellow, low”).
