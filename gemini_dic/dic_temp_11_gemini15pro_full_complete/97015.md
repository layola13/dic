infeasibility:/ˌɪnˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可行性，不可实现性|The infeasibility of the project became apparent after a thorough analysis. 该项目的不可行性经过彻底分析后变得显而易见。|例句：The infeasibility of the plan caused them to abandon it.  这个计划的不可行性导致他们放弃了它。|近义词：impracticability, impossibility, unworkability|反义词：feasibility, possibility, practicality|词性:名词

infeasible:/ɪnˈfiːzəbl/
adj.|不可行的，无法实现的|Building a bridge across the ocean is currently infeasible.  目前在海洋上建造一座桥梁是不可行的。|例句：The project was deemed infeasible due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被认为是不可行的。|近义词：impractical, impossible, unattainable|反义词：feasible, possible, attainable|词性:形容词
