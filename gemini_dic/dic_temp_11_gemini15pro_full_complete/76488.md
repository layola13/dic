galah:/ˈɡælə/ (AU), /ɡəˈlɑː/ (UK)| n.|凤头鹦鹉，傻瓜，笨蛋|A large, pink and grey Australian cockatoo, often used colloquially to refer to a foolish or eccentric person.|例句：I saw a flock of galahs in the tree. 我看到树上有一群凤头鹦鹉。

例句：He acted like a real galah, forgetting his own name. 他表现得像个傻瓜，连自己的名字都忘了。|近义词：fool, idiot, clown, cockatoo|反义词:genius, smart person|词性:noun
