antiloquist:/ænˈtɪləkwɪst/ | n. | 腹语术表演者 | A person who practices ventriloquism, the art of speaking without moving the lips, making it appear that the voice is coming from another source, typically a puppet. |  The antiloquist made the dummy seem alive with his skillful voice manipulation. 腹语术表演者用他娴熟的语音操控使假人看起来栩栩如生。

| Example: The antiloquist threw his voice, making it sound like the dummy was telling jokes.  腹语术表演者改变自己的声音，让它听起来像是假人在讲笑话。 | Synonyms: ventriloquist, dummy operator | Antonyms:  None (This is a specific skill, not a concept with an opposite.) | Noun
