aliferous:/əˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
产…的；有…的；多…的
Bearing, producing, or yielding something, typically specified by the initial element.
例句：
The valley is aliferous with flowers in the spring. 春天，山谷里开满了鲜花。

例句：
The region is known for its aliferous soil, perfect for growing grapes. 该地区以其肥沃的土壤而闻名，非常适合种植葡萄。

近义词：productive, fertile, fruitful
反义词：barren, sterile, unproductive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alifer, from *alēs (“bearing, carrying, nourishing”) + -fer (“bearing, carrying”).  *alēs is related to alō (“nourish, support, sustain”).
