multinational:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 跨国的；多国的；跨国公司 | Operating in many countries. |  Example:  Many multinational corporations have headquarters in the United States. 许多跨国公司总部设在美国。

Example:  She works for a multinational company. 她在一家跨国公司工作。

| 近义词: international, global, transnational, cosmopolitan | 反义词: domestic, local, national | 词性: 形容词, 名词


multinational:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | n. | 跨国公司 | A company operating in many countries. | Example: The multinational announced plans to expand into new markets. 这家跨国公司宣布了进军新市场的计划。

Example: Several multinationals are competing for the contract. 几家跨国公司正在竞争这份合同。

| 近义词: conglomerate, corporation, global company | 反义词: small business, local business | 词性: 名词
