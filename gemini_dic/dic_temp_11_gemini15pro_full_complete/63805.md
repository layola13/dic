epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/ | n. adj.| 流行病，流行性的 | A widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community at a particular time. |  The flu epidemic of 1918 was devastating. 1918年的流感疫情是毁灭性的。| 近义词: outbreak, plague, pandemic | 反义词: isolated case, sporadic | noun

epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/ | adj.| 流行性的，盛行的 | Affecting or tending to affect a disproportionately large number of individuals within a population, community, or region at the same time. |  There is an epidemic of obesity in the United States. 美国存在肥胖流行的现象。| 近义词: widespread, rampant, pervasive | 反义词: localized, contained, isolated | adjective
