blurters:/ˈblɜːrtərz/
| n. |脱口而出的人；冲动说话的人|People who blurt things out; those who speak impulsively. |He's one of those blurters who can't keep a secret. 他就是那种藏不住秘密，什么都往外说的人。|近义词：loudmouths, chatterboxes|反义词: reticent people, reserved individuals|词性:名词

blurt:/blɜːrt/
| v. |脱口而出；冲动地说|To say something suddenly and without thinking. |She blurted out the answer before I could even finish the question. 我还没问完问题，她就脱口而出答案了。|近义词：blurt out, exclaim, ejaculate|反义词: murmur, whisper|词性:动词
