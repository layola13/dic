misarrange:/ˌmɪsərˈeɪndʒ/ | v. | 弄乱，排列错误 | To arrange something badly or incorrectly. |  She misarranged the books on the shelf, putting the fiction next to the biographies. 她把书架上的书弄乱了，把小说放在了传记旁边。

The flowers were misarranged in the vase, looking cluttered and messy.  花瓶里的花摆放得很乱，看起来杂乱无章。 | 近义词: disarrange, disorder, jumble | 反义词: arrange, order, organize | 词性: 动词

词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + arrange (from Old French "arengier," meaning "to put in a row")
