artistry:/ˈɑːrtɪstri/
n.
艺术技巧；艺术才能
Skill and ability in art, especially in the visual arts.
Her artistry is evident in every brushstroke. 她的艺术技巧在每一笔中都显而易见。
skill, talent, craft, expertise, mastery
ineptitude, clumsiness, awkwardness
noun
