emotionlessly:/ɪˈmoʊʃənləsli/
adv.
没有感情地，冷漠地
Without emotion; in an emotionless manner.
She spoke emotionlessly, her voice flat and devoid of any warmth. 她说话毫无感情，声音平淡，没有任何温暖。
He stared emotionlessly at the scene unfolding before him. 他面无表情地盯着眼前发生的这一幕。
近义词: apathetically, indifferently, blankly, stoically
反义词: emotionally, passionately, expressively
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From emotionless + -ly.  Emotionless is formed from emotion + -less. Emotion comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin *ēmovēre "to move out, remove, agitate," from ē- "out" + movēre "to move."
