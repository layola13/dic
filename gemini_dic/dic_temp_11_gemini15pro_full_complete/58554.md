duke-it-out:/ˈduːk ɪt aʊt/
verb phrase
决一雌雄；一决胜负
To fight or argue fiercely until a winner is determined.  This phrase suggests a physical fight, but can also be used figuratively for a heated argument or competition.
The two rivals decided to duke it out in the boxing ring.  这两个对手决定在拳击台上决一雌雄。
The companies are duking it out for market share.  这些公司正在争夺市场份额。
近义词: fight it out, battle it out, slug it out, hash it out (for arguments)
反义词: compromise, negotiate, cooperate
词性: verb phrase
