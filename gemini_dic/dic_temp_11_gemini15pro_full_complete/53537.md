disavowal:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəl/ | n. |否认，拒绝承认，放弃|An act of denying responsibility for or knowledge of something. |The government issued a disavowal of the ambassador's remarks. 政府发表声明，否认该大使的言论。|Synonyms: denial, rejection, repudiation, renunciation |Antonyms: acknowledgment, admission, acceptance, confession |noun
