monocondylic:/ˌmɒnəʊkɒnˈdɪlɪk/ | adj. | 单髁的 | Having a single condyle, or rounded articular surface at the end of a bone.  |  The jaw of a mammal is monocondylic, allowing for more complex chewing movements. 哺乳动物的下巴是单髁的，允许更复杂的咀嚼运动。 |  monarthric |  bicondylic/dicondylic | adjective
