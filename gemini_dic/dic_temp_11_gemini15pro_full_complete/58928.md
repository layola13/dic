duskiest:/ˈdʌskiɪst/| adj.|最昏暗的，最黑暗的|Superlative form of dusky: most dusky.  Having very little light; dim; shadowy. |例句：The duskiest corners of the forest were home to nocturnal creatures. 森林中最黑暗的角落是夜行动物的家园。

例句：The photograph captured the duskiest hour just before dawn. 这张照片捕捉到了黎明前最黑暗的时刻。|近义词：darkest, dimmest, murkiest|反义词：brightest, lightest|词性:形容词
词源:From dusk + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *duskaz.
