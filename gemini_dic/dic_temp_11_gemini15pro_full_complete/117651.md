megazostrodon:/ˌmɛɡəˈzɒstrədɒn/
n.
巨嘴兽（一种已灭绝的早期哺乳动物）
An extinct, shrew-like mammal considered to be a transitional form between cynodonts and true mammals, known from fossils found in southern Africa.  It possessed characteristics of both groups, including differentiated teeth and a relatively large braincase.
Megazostrodon is an important fossil in understanding mammalian evolution. 巨嘴兽是了解哺乳动物进化的重要化石。
Megazostrodon lived during the Late Triassic and Early Jurassic periods. 巨嘴兽生活在三叠纪晚期和侏罗纪早期。
Synonym: none (highly specific scientific term)
Antonym: none (highly specific scientific term)
