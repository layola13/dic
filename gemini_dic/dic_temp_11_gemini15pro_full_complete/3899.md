agrees:/əˈɡriːz/
verb
同意，赞同，一致
This verb is the third-person singular present tense form of "agree." It indicates that someone shares the same opinion or view as another person, or that something is consistent with something else.
The doctor agrees that the patient needs more rest. 医生同意病人需要多休息。
His story agrees with hers. 他的说法和她的说法一致。
synonyms:concurs, assents, corresponds, matches
antonyms:disagrees, dissents, differs, clashes
词性:动词
