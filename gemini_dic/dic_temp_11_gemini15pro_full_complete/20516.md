bivvy: /ˈbɪvi/ (UK) /ˈbɪvi/ (US)| n. | 帐篷，露营袋 | A small, lightweight tent or shelter used for camping or sleeping outdoors. |  We spent the night in our bivvies under the stars. 我们在星空下睡在露营袋里。

| They bivvied out under the open sky. 他们在空旷的天空下露营。 | 近义词：camp, sleep out | 反义词: stay indoors | 词性: verb
