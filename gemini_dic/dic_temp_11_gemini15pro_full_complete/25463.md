bulled:/bʊld/ | v. | 欺负；恐吓；逼迫；(使)上涨 | to intimidate or persecute by superior strength; to cause (prices) to rise | The larger boys bulled the smaller ones into giving them their lunch money.  大男孩们恐吓小男孩，让他们交出午饭钱。| synonyms: intimidate, bully, cow, browbeat | antonyms: encourage, support | verb

bulled:/bʊld/ | adj. | (指市场)看涨的，上涨的 | characterized by rising prices |  The bulled market saw a surge in tech stocks. 看涨的市场见证了科技股的飙升。| synonyms: bullish, rising, upward | antonyms: bearish, falling, downward | adjective
