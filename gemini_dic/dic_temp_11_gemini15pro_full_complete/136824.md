nonsibilants:/ˌnɒnˈsɪbɪlənts/ | n. |非咝擦音|A speech sound that is not a sibilant; sounds which lack the characteristic high-pitched hissing quality of sibilants. |The sounds /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, /g/, /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/ are nonsibilants.  /p/、/b/、/t/、/d/、/k/、/g/、/m/、/n/和/ŋ/都是非咝擦音。|近义词:non-sibilants|反义词:sibilants|词性:名词
