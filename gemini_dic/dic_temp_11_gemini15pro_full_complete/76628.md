gallops:/ˈɡæləps/| n. v.| 疾驰，飞奔；飞速发展 |A fast gait of a horse or other quadruped in which all four feet are off the ground together in each stride.  (n.) The horse broke into a gallop.  马飞奔起来。  (v.)  He galloped his horse across the field. 他骑着马飞奔过田野。 | The horse gallops across the field. 马飞奔过田野。 The project galloped along and was completed ahead of schedule.  项目进展神速，提前完成。 | 近义词：sprint, dash, run, race, bound, bolt, career, charge, dart, hasten, hurry, rush, speed, tear | 反义词：crawl, creep, dawdle, plod, trudge |词性:名词,动词
