curlable:/ˈkɜːrləbl/ (US), /ˈkɜːləbl/ (UK)| adj.|可卷曲的，可蜷缩的|Something that is curlable can be curled or formed into curls.  It often refers to hair, but can also apply to other materials.|Her hair is long and very curlable. 她的头发很长，很容易卷曲。|The ribbon is curlable and can be used to make bows.  这丝带可以卷曲，可以用来做蝴蝶结。|近义词: curly, wavy, coiling|反义词: straight, uncurlable|词性: adjective

词源: From "curl" + "-able".  "Curl" comes from Middle English *curle*, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Middle Dutch *krul*.  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being".
