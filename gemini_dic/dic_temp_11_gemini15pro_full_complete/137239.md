nonsusceptibleness:/ˌnɒnsəˌsɛptɪˈbɪlnəs/
n.
无易感性；不受影响；抗性
The state or quality of not being susceptible; immunity or resistance.
His nonsusceptibleness to flattery made him a difficult man to manipulate. 他不易受奉承的影响，这使他很难被人操纵。
nonsusceptibility, immunity, resistance
susceptibility, vulnerability
