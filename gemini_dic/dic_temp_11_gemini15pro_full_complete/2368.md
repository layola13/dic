adieu:/əˈdjuː/
interjection|告别；再会|A formal expression of farewell.|例句：With a final adieu, she boarded the train. 她最后告别一声，登上了火车。|近义词：farewell, goodbye, so long|反义词:greeting, hello
noun|告别；辞别|A farewell.|例句：He bade her a fond adieu. 他和她深情告别。
