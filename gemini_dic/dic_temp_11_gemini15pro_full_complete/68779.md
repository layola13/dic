farsightedness:/ˌfɑːrˈsaɪtɪdnəs/ | n. |远视；目光长远|The condition of being able to see things clearly at a distance but not close up.  The ability to anticipate future needs or trends.|例句：She wears glasses to correct her farsightedness. 她戴眼镜来矫正她的远视。

例句：His farsightedness allowed him to invest wisely. 他的远见卓识让他能够明智地投资。|近义词：hyperopia, presbyopia, foresight, vision |反义词：nearsightedness, myopia, shortsightedness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From far + sighted + -ness.  "Sighted" meaning having sight or vision.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
