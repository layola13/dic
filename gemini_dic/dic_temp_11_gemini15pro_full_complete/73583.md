foretaste:/ˈfɔːrteɪst/ (US) /ˈfɔːteɪst/ (UK)| n. |预示；先兆；体验；一小部分|A sample or suggestion of something that is to come; an advance indication or warning.|例句：The appetizer was a foretaste of the delicious meal to come.  开胃菜预示着接下来的美味佳肴。

例句：The first few chapters of the book give you a foretaste of the exciting story. 这本书的前几章让你体验到扣人心弦的故事。|近义词：preview, sample, glimpse, inkling, presage, harbinger|反义词: aftertaste, hindsight|词性: noun
