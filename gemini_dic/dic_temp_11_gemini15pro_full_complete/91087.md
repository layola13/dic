huxter:/ˈhʌkstər/| n. |叫卖小贩；零售商(尤指在街上或市场上)；<贬>不择手段推销的人|A person who sells goods, especially food, from a stall or cart in the street or at a market.  Often used with a negative connotation implying aggressive or unethical sales tactics.|The huxter loudly advertised his wares.  那个小贩大声叫卖他的商品。|近义词：vendor, peddler, hawker, salesman, merchant|反义词:buyer, customer|noun
