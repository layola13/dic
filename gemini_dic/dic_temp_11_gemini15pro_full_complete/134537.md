nonlineal:/ˌnɒnˈlɪniə(r)/
adjective
非线性的
Not linear; not lying on the same straight line.  Relating to or denoting a system whose output is not directly proportional to its input.
Example: The relationship between stress and strain in some materials is nonlinear.  某些材料中的应力和应变之间的关系是非线性的。
Example:  Nonlinear equations are more difficult to solve than linear ones.  非线性方程比线性方程更难求解。
Synonym:  non-linear, curvilinear, crooked
Antonym:  linear, straight
词性: 形容词
