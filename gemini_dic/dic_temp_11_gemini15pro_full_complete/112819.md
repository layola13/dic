lucid:/ˈluːsɪd/| adj.|清晰的，明了的；清醒的；神志清楚的|Characterized by clarity of thought or expression; easily understood; bright or luminous;  (of a person) awake and fully aware of their surroundings, especially after a period of confusion or unconsciousness.|例句：She gave a lucid explanation of the complex problem. 她对这个复杂的问题作出了清晰的解释。|例句：He had a few lucid moments before slipping back into unconsciousness. 他在再次陷入昏迷之前有几个清醒的时刻。|近义词：clear, coherent, intelligible, transparent, rational, sane|反义词：obscure, confused, unintelligible, opaque, irrational, insane|词性:形容词
