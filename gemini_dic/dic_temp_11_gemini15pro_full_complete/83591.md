haematoid:/ˌhiːməˈtɔɪd/ | adj. | 血样的，似血液的 | Resembling blood; having the appearance of blood. | The bruise had a haematoid appearance.  淤青呈现出血液状。|  The substance secreted a haematoid fluid. 分泌物是一种类似血液的液体。 | 近义词: bloody, sanguineous, bloodlike | 反义词: bloodless | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *haîma* (blood) + *-oid* (resembling).
