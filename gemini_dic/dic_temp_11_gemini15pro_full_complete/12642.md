assayer:/əˈseɪər/ | n. |化验员；鉴定者；分析者| A person who assays ores or metals.  A person who examines and evaluates. | The assayer carefully tested the gold ore. 化验员仔细地测试了金矿石。| synonyms: analyst, examiner, evaluator, tester, inspector | antonyms:  N/A | noun

assayer:/əˈseɪ/ | v. |化验；鉴定；分析；尝试；考验| To examine and determine the content or quality of (a metal, ore, etc.). To put to a test; make trial of. |  They assayed the gold to determine its purity.  他们化验了黄金以确定其纯度。 He assayed a smile. 他勉强一笑。 | synonyms: analyze, examine, evaluate, test, try, attempt | antonyms: neglect, ignore | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *essaiier, from essai "trial, test," from Late Latin exagium "weighing, weight," from Latin exigere "to weigh, ascertain, drive out."
