accedence:/əkˈsiːdəns/ | n. | 同意；赞同；应允 | Agreement or acceptance.| He gave his accedence to the proposal. 他同意了这项提议。 | 近义词: acceptance, agreement, consent, assent | 反义词: refusal, rejection, dissent, disagreement | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *accedentia, from Latin accēdō (“I approach, assent”), from ad (“to”) + cēdō (“I go, yield”).
