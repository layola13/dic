ligulate:/ˈlɪɡjʊleɪt/ /ˈlɪɡjəleɪt/
adjective
舌状的；带舌片的；[植] 舌状花的
Having the shape of a strap; strap-shaped.  Relating to or resembling a ligule. In botany, specifically refers to flowers that have a prominent, strap-shaped corolla, as in many members of the Asteraceae (daisy) family.
The dandelion has ligulate flowers. 蒲公英有舌状花。
The plant's leaves were long and ligulate. 这种植物的叶子又长又呈舌状。
Synonym: strap-shaped, linear
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "ovate," "lanceolate," or describing the specific non-ligulate shape)
