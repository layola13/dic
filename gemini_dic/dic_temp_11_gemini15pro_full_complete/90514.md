hudibrastic:/ˌhjuːdɪˈbræstɪk/| adj.|滑稽模仿的，以讽刺诗体写的，诙谐打油诗的|Of, relating to, or resembling the style of Samuel Butler's Hudibras, a 17th-century mock-heroic poem characterized by doggerel verse, burlesque, and satire.|His hudibrastic poem poked fun at the pretensions of the local aristocracy. 他那首滑稽模仿的诗歌嘲弄了当地贵族的自命不凡。|Synonyms: burlesque, comic, mock-heroic, satirical|Antonyms: serious, solemn|adjective
