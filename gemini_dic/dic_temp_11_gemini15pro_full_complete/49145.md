dejected:/dɪˈdʒektɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
sad and depressed; disheartened.
She looked dejected when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后看起来很沮丧。
近义词:downcast, despondent, disheartened, discouraged
反义词:elated, cheerful, jubilant
词性:adjective

dejected:/dɪˈdʒektɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
From the Latin deicere "to throw down," past participle deiectus.  Figuratively, "cast down in spirits."
He walked home dejected after his team lost the championship. 输掉冠军后，他沮丧地走回家。
近义词:glum, miserable, downhearted
反义词:joyful, happy, exuberant
词性:adjective
