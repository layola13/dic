affix:/əˈfɪks/ (US); /əˈfɪks/ (UK)| n. vt.|词缀；附加物；贴上|A morpheme that is attached to a word stem to form a new word or word form.  An affix can be a prefix, an infix, or a suffix.|He added an affix to the root word to create a new meaning. 他在词根上添加了一个词缀以创造一个新的含义。|近义词：attachment, addition, suffix, prefix|反义词：root, stem|n.

affix:/əˈfɪks/ (US); /əˈfɪks/ (UK)| vt.|粘上；使附于|To attach, stick, or fasten something to something else.|She affixed a stamp to the envelope. 她把邮票贴在信封上。|近义词：attach, fasten, stick, adhere|反义词：detach, remove|vt.
