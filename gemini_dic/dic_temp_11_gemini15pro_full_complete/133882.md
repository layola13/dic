nongregariousness:/ˌnɑːnɡrɪˈɡɛəriəsnəs/
n.
不爱交际；不合群
The state or quality of being nongregarious; unsociability.
His nongregariousness made it difficult for him to make friends.  他的不爱交际让他很难交到朋友。
unsociability, introversion, aloofness
gregariousness, sociability
noun
