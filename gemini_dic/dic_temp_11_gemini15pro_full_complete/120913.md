miscarrying:/mɪsˈkæriɪŋ/
|v.|流产；失败；误送|Failing to achieve the intended purpose; (of a pregnancy) ending prematurely and spontaneously.|The project miscarried due to lack of funding.  该项目因缺乏资金而失败。|近义词：failing, aborting, misfiring|反义词:succeeding, thriving|verb

miscarry:/mɪsˈkæriɪŋ/
|n.|流产；失败|The spontaneous or induced expulsion of a fetus before it is viable.|She had a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她在妊娠的头三个月流产了。|近义词：abortion, failure|反义词:success, birth|noun
