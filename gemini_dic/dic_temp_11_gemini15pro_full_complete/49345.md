delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/
n.|描绘，勾画，描述|The architect's drawings provided a clear delineation of the proposed building.  建筑师的图纸清晰地勾勒出了拟建建筑的轮廓。|例句：The report offers a thorough delineation of the company's financial status. 这份报告对公司的财务状况进行了全面的描述。|近义词：description, portrayal, sketch, outline, definition|反义词：obscuration, confusion|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēlineātiō, from Latin dēlineātus, past participle of dēlineāre (“to sketch, delineate”), from dē (“down, completely”) + lineāre (“to make lines”), from līnea (“line”).
