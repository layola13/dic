combustion:/kəmˈbʌstʃən/ | n. |燃烧，氧化|the process of burning something|The combustion of fossil fuels releases harmful gases into the atmosphere. 化石燃料的燃烧将有害气体释放到大气中。|近义词：burning, ignition, oxidation|反义词:extinguishing|词性:名词

combustions:/kəmˈbʌstʃənz/ | n. |燃烧（复数）|multiple instances of the process of burning|The controlled combustions in the engine power the vehicle. 发动机内的受控燃烧为车辆提供动力。|近义词：burnings, ignitions, oxidations|反义词:extinguishing|词性:名词
