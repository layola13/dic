blacklists:/ˈblækˌlɪsts/| n. v.|黑名单；将…列入黑名单|A list of people or things that are disapproved of and should be avoided or penalized.|The company blacklisted him after he was caught stealing.  这家公司在他被抓到偷窃后将他列入了黑名单。|近义词：blocklist, denylist|反义词: whitelist, allowlist|词性:名词, 动词

blacklisting:/ˈblækˌlɪstɪŋ/| n.|将…列入黑名单的行为|The act of putting someone or something on a blacklist.|The blacklisting of the actor ruined his career.  将这位演员列入黑名单毁了他的职业生涯。|近义词：blocking, denying|反义词: whitelisting, allowing|词性:名词
