nonrevolutionary:/ˌnɒnˌrevəˈluːʃənəri/
adjective
非革命的；非革命性的
Not involving or promoting revolution; not revolutionary.
The changes were nonrevolutionary but significant. 这些变化不是革命性的，但意义重大。
Synonyms:  moderate, gradual, evolutionary, reformist, conservative
Antonyms: revolutionary, radical, rebellious, subversive, insurgent
词性: 形容词
