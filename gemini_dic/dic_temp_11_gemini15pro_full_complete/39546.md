conscience:/ˈkɒnʃəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃəns/ (US)|n.|良心，道德心|An inner feeling or voice viewed as acting as a guide to the rightness or wrongness of one's behavior.| I had a guilty conscience about not telling her the truth. 我没有告诉她真相，感到良心不安。|synonyms: morals, scruples, ethics|antonyms: immorality, unscrupulousness|noun
