ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of the Beretta family, founders of the firearms company, originally from Gardone Val Trompia, Italy.  The family's involvement in arms manufacturing can be traced back to the 1500s.  For the hat meaning, it's likely a reference to the shape resembling the flat top of some Beretta firearms.
