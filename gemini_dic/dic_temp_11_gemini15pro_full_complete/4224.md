airlifting: /ˈeərlɪftɪŋ/
n.
空运，空投
The act or process of transporting people or goods by aircraft, especially in an emergency.
例句：The airlifting of supplies to the refugees was crucial.  向难民空投物资至关重要。

例句：The airlifting of the injured climbers was a challenging operation.  空运受伤的登山者是一项具有挑战性的行动。

近义词：air transport, flying, aviation
反义词：shipping, trucking, land transport
词性：名词

airlift: /ˈeərlɪft/
v.
空运，空投
To transport people or goods by aircraft, especially in an emergency.
例句：They airlifted the survivors to safety.  他们用飞机把幸存者空运到安全的地方。

例句：The military airlifted food and medical supplies to the disaster zone. 军方空运了食物和医疗用品到灾区。

近义词：fly, transport by air
反义词：ship, transport by land/sea
词性：动词

词源: air + lift.  "air" 指的是 "空中", "lift" 指的是 "提升，搬运".  组合在一起表示通过空中运输。
