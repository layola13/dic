filoselle:/ˈfɪləˌsɛl/ /ˈfaɪləˌsɛl/
noun
绒丝，绢丝
A soft, floss silk used for embroidery.
She used filoselle to embroider delicate flowers onto the silk scarf. 她用绒丝在丝巾上绣上了精致的花朵。
Near Synonyms: floss silk, silk thread, embroidery silk
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider coarser threads like wool or cotton yarn)
词性: 名词
