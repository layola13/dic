battily:/ˈbætɪli/| adv.|疯狂地，古怪地|In a batty manner; crazily or eccentrically.  Characterized by erratic or irrational behavior.|She battily insisted on wearing a bathing suit in the snow. 她疯狂地坚持在雪地里穿泳衣。|近义词：crazily, eccentrically, insanely, foolishly, madly|反义词：sanely, rationally, sensibly|词性:副词
词源: From batty (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Batty itself is likely a shortened form of "bats in the belfry," a figurative expression for madness.
