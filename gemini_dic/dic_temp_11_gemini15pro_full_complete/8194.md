anti-dotal:/ˌæntiˈdōtl/ | adj. | 解毒的；抵消的；对抗的 |  Acting to counteract something unpleasant or harmful. |  The campaign was seen as an anti-dotal measure to the negative publicity. 这场运动被视为对抗负面宣传的解毒剂。 |  counteractive, remedial, corrective |  harmful, toxic | adjective
anti-dotal:/ˌæntiˈdōtl/ | n. | 解毒剂；矫正方法 | A medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison.  Something that counteracts or neutralizes an unpleasant feeling or situation. | Laughter is a good anti-dotal to stress. 笑是缓解压力的好方法。 | remedy, cure, antidote | poison, toxin | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antidotālis, from antidotum (“antidote”), from Ancient Greek ἀντίδοτον (antídoto, “given against”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + δίδωμι (dídōmi, “I give”).
