mip:/mɪp/| n.|移动网页加速项目 (Mobile Instant Pages)|A Google initiative to speed up the loading time of web pages on mobile devices. This is achieved by pre-loading content in a special format. |Example: This website uses MIP to ensure a fast loading experience on mobile.  这个网站使用MIP来确保在移动设备上的快速加载体验。|Example: MIP technology has significantly improved mobile browsing. MIP技术显著改善了移动浏览体验。|近义词: AMP (Accelerated Mobile Pages)|反义词: (None - a specific technology without a direct opposite)|词性: noun
