misproud:/mɪsˈpraʊd/
adjective
错误的自豪；不值得骄傲的；虚荣的
Having or showing pride in something unworthy or wrong.
He was misproud of his ill-gotten gains. 他对他不正当的所得感到不应有的骄傲。
近义词:vain, conceited, arrogant
反义词:humble, modest
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From mis- + proud.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English origin meaning "wrong," "badly," "wrongly," or conveying a sense of error or negativity. "Proud" comes from Old French prud, ultimately from Latin pro- (forth) + esse (to be).
