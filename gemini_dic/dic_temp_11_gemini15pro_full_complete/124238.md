mossi:/ˈmɒsi/ (UK), /ˈmoʊsi/ (US)| n. adj.| 摩西人；摩西语 |A member of a people inhabiting Burkina Faso and parts of Ghana. Also: the language of this people, belonging to the Gur group of the Niger-Congo family.| The Mossi people are known for their rich cultural heritage. 摩西人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。| The Mossi language is spoken by millions.  数百万人口讲摩西语。 | synonyms:  Mossi people, More, Mole | antonyms: N/A | noun, adjective
