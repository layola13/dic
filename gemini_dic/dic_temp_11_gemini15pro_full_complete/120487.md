mintiest:/ˈmɪntiɪst/| adj.|最薄荷味的，含有最多薄荷味的|Having the strongest or freshest mint flavor.  It's the superlative form of "minty."|The mintiest gum is my favorite.  我最喜欢薄荷味最浓的口香糖。|近义词：freshest, most minty|反义词:blandest, least minty|词性:形容词

词源:
From mint + -y + -est.  "Mint" comes from the Latin "menta" via Old French "minte." "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of," and "-est" is a suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives.
