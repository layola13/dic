cornflower-blues:/ˈkɔːrnˌflaʊər bluːz/| adj.|矢车菊蓝|A shade of blue reminiscent of the color of cornflower petals.|Her dress was a beautiful cornflower-blue, perfectly matching her eyes.|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky blue|反义词：crimson, scarlet|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"Cornflower" comes from the flower, Centaurea cyanus, also known as bachelor's button. "Blue" refers to the color. The term "cornflower-blue" simply describes a shade of blue that resembles the color of this flower.
