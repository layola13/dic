guisa:/ˈɡiːzə/ /ˈɡaɪzə/| n.|方式，样子，装束，伪装|manner, fashion, guise, semblance; external appearance, often with the implication of disguise or deception|He entered the room in the guisa of a repairman. 他以修理工的伪装进入了房间。|近义词：manner, fashion, semblance, appearance, pretense|反义词:reality, truth|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *gise, guise, from Old French guise, from Frankish *wīsa (“manner, fashion”). Cognate with Old High German wīsa (“manner, fashion, melody”). Doublet of wise.
