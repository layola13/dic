ludlamite:/ˈlʌdləˌmaɪt/ | n. | 块磷铁矿 | A rare, green iron phosphate mineral, Fe₃(PO₄)₂·4H₂O, occurring in small, monoclinic crystals. |  Ludlamite is a prized specimen for mineral collectors due to its vibrant green color. 块磷铁矿因其鲜艳的绿色而成为矿物收藏家的珍贵标本。|  Synonym: vivianite (similar composition but blue),  | Antonym: None (specific mineral, no direct opposite) | noun
