bulletproofing:/ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːfɪŋ/ |n.|防弹;防弹措施|The process of making something resistant to bullets.  This involves reinforcing materials like cars, vests, or glass with strong, protective layers.|The president's car underwent extensive bulletproofing. 总统的汽车进行了大量的防弹改装。|Synonyms: armoring, fortification|Antonyms: weakening, vulnerability|noun

bulletproof:/ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːf/ |adj.|防弹的;刀枪不入的|Impenetrable to bullets; also metaphorically used to describe something impervious to criticism or failure.|The police officers wore bulletproof vests. 警员们穿着防弹背心。|Synonyms: impenetrable, impervious, invincible|Antonyms: vulnerable, fragile, weak|adjective
