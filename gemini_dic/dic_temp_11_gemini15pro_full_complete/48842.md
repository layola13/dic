deforceor:/dɪˈfɔːrsər/| n.|非法剥夺者，强占者|A person who unlawfully withholds possession of land from the rightful owner.|The deforceor refused to vacate the property.|近义词：intruder, usurper, disseisor|反义词:owner, possessor|词性:名词
Etymology: From Anglo-French *deforceour, from deforcer "to dispossess by force," from de- "away, from" + force "force."|词源: 源于盎格鲁-法语 *deforceour，来自 deforcer “以武力剥夺”，来自 de- “离开，从” + force “武力”。
