bucket:/ˈbʌkɪt/| n.|桶; 一桶（的量）; 大量; （篮球运动的）投篮|A bucket is a round, open container with a handle, used for carrying liquids.  A bucket can also refer to the amount contained in such a container, or informally, to a large quantity of something.|She filled the bucket with water. 她把水桶装满了水。

He scored buckets of points in the game. 他在比赛中得了很多分。|近义词:pail, container, lots|反义词:(没有明确的反义词)|词性:名词

bucket:/ˈbʌkɪt/| v.|倾盆而下; 舀; （非正式）呕吐|To rain heavily. Also, to move or pour something quickly and forcefully, or to vomit.|It's bucketing down outside. 外面下着倾盆大雨。

He bucketed the water out of the boat. 他把船里的水舀了出去。|近义词:pour, rain heavily, throw up|反义词:(没有明确的反义词)|词性:动词
