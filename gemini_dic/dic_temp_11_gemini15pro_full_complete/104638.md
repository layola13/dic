karaoke:/ˌkæriˈoʊki/ /ˌkaraˈəʊki/| n.|卡拉OK|A form of entertainment, originating in Japan, in which people sing along to pre-recorded music, usually displayed on a screen with the lyrics.  The music typically omits the lead vocals, allowing the singer to take the lead vocal role.|We went to a karaoke bar last night and sang our hearts out. 我们昨晚去了卡拉OK吧，尽情歌唱。|Synonyms: singalong, karaoke singing|Antonyms: None|Noun
