fettering:/ˈfetərɪŋ/
n.|束缚，羁绊|The act of restraining or chaining.  The fettering of the press is a threat to democracy.|新闻自由受限是对民主的威胁。
fettering:/ˈfetərɪŋ/
adj.|束缚的，限制的|(Rarely used as an adjective)  A fettering regulation hindered economic growth.|一项限制性规定阻碍了经济增长。


近义词: restricting, confining, chaining, shackling, hindering
反义词: liberating, freeing, releasing, unshackling, empowering
词性:  Noun (primarily), Adjective (rarely)
词源: From the Old English "feter" (chain, shackle), from Proto-Germanic "*fetrō", from Proto-Indo-European "*ped-", meaning "foot". The original meaning referred to a shackle for the feet.
