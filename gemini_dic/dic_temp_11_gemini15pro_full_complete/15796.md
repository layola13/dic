balkanising:/ˈbɔːlkənaɪzɪŋ/ |v. |巴尔干化|To divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups.|The country was balkanized after the war. 战后，这个国家被分裂成几个相互敌对的小国。|近义词：fragmenting, dividing, partitioning, splitting|反义词：uniting, integrating, consolidating|词性: 动词

balkanization:/ˌbɔːlkənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n. |巴尔干化|The process of dividing a region or body into smaller mutually hostile states or groups.|The balkanization of the region led to years of conflict. 该地区的巴尔干化导致了多年的冲突。|近义词：fragmentation, division, partitioning|反义词：unification, integration, consolidation|词性: 名词

balkanized:/ˈbɔːlkənaɪzd/ |adj. |巴尔干化的|Divided into smaller mutually hostile states or groups.|The formerly unified country became increasingly balkanized. 这个曾经统一的国家变得越来越分裂。|近义词：fragmented, divided, partitioned|反义词：unified, integrated, consolidated|词性: 形容词


词源: Derived from the Balkans, a region in southeastern Europe that has historically been divided into many small, often warring, states.  源自巴尔干半岛，欧洲东南部的一个地区，历史上一直被划分为许多小的、经常交战的国家。
