conglaciate:/kənˈɡleɪʃiˌeɪt/ /kɒŋˈɡleɪʃɪˌeɪt/
verb
凝结，冻结
to change from a soft or fluid state to a rigid or solid state, as by freezing or cooling.
The water congealed into ice.  水凝结成了冰。
The blood had congealed around the wound.  血液在伤口周围凝结了。
近义词: freeze, solidify, harden
反义词: melt, liquefy, thaw
词性: verb

conglaciate:/kənˈɡleɪʃiˌeɪt/ /kɒŋˈɡleɪʃɪˌeɪt/
adjective
凝结的，冻结的
Being congealed; frozen.
The conglaciate lava formed bizarre shapes. 凝固的熔岩形成了奇异的形状。
The lake became conglaciate in the extreme cold.  在极度寒冷的天气下，湖水冻结了。
近义词: frozen, solidified, hardened
反义词: melted, liquid, thawed
词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *conglaciātus, past participle of *conglaciāre (“to freeze together, congeal”), from con- (“together”) + glaciēs (“ice”).
