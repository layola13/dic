dramatisable:/ˈdræmətʌɪzəbl/ /ˈdrɑːmətʌɪzəbl/
adjective
可戏剧化的；可改编成戏剧的
Suitable for being made into a play or presented dramatically.
Example: Her life story is highly dramatisable.  她的人生故事非常适合改编成戏剧。

Example: The novel is dramatisable and would make a great film. 这部小说可以戏剧化，会是一部很棒的电影。

Synonym: adaptable, dramatizeable
Antonym: undramatic, unstageable
词性: 形容词
