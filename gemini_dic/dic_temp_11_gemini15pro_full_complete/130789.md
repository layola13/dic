nonanimatingly:/ˌnɒnˈænɪmeɪtɪŋli/
adv.
无生气地，缺乏活力地
in a way that shows no life or energy, or in a way that makes something seem to have no life or energy
He spoke nonanimatingly, as if he didn't care about the topic. 他说话毫无生气，好像对这个话题漠不关心。
The performance was delivered nonanimatingly, leaving the audience bored.  这场演出表演得死气沉沉，让观众感到无聊。
近义词: lifelessly, dully, spiritlessly, apathetically
反义词: animatedly, lively, enthusiastically, energetically
词性: 副词


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  not; the opposite of
animate (verb): to give life or energy to
-ing (suffix): forms present participles and verbal nouns
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
