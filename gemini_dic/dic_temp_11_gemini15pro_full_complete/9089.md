antimodernness:/ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnɪs/ /ˌæntiˈmɑdərnəs/
|n.|反现代性，反现代主义|The state of being opposed to or rejecting modern trends, ideas, or values.  It often involves a nostalgia for the past and a skepticism towards progress.|
|例句：There's a strain of antimodernness running through his work. 他的作品中有一种反现代性的倾向。|

|例句：Her antimodernness led her to reject most new technologies. 她的反现代性导致她拒绝大多数新技术。|
|近义词：traditionalism, conservatism, reaction|
|反义词：modernism, modernity|
|词性：名词|


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- + modernness.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Modernness" is the state or quality of being modern, derived from the Old French word "moderne."
