murder:/ˈmɜːrdər/ (US) /ˈmɜːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|谋杀，凶杀|The unlawful premeditated killing of one human being by another.|例句：The murder remains unsolved. 这起谋杀案仍未破案。|例句：The detective investigated the murder.  侦探调查了这起谋杀。|近义词：homicide, killing, slaughter|反义词:  resurrection, revival|词性:名词, 动词
