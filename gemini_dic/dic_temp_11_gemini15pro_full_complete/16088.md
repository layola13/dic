bandrol:/ˈbændrɒl/ (British English), /ˈbændroʊl/ (American English)| n.| 旗帜，横幅，标语；(尤指)书衣上的纸带，印有价格或其他信息的纸带 |A small flag or streamer, especially a paper band around a book giving the price or other information.| The bandrol on the new novel announced it as a bestseller. 这本新小说的腰封宣称它是畅销书。| synonyms: banner, streamer, label, sticker | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider things like "cover" if referring to obscuring information) | noun
