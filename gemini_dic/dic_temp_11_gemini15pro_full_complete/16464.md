barest:/ˈbɛərɪst/| adj.|最少的，最小的，最基本的，仅仅的|The room was barest, with only a bed and a chair. 房间里空荡荡的，只有一张床和一把椅子。|The report gave only the barest details of the accident. 这份报告只提供了事故最基本的情况。|近义词：minimal, slightest, least,  |反义词: most, maximum, fullest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *barist, superlative of bar (“bare”).
