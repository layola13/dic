overqualifies:/ˌoʊvərˈkwɑːlɪfaɪz/
| v. | 使资历过高；使学历过高 | To have more qualifications, skills, or experience than are necessary for a particular job. | She overqualifies for this position, having a PhD when only a bachelor's is required. 她学历过高，不适合这个职位，这个职位只需要学士学位，而她拥有博士学位。 | Example 2: He felt he was overqualified for the entry-level role. 他觉得自己资历过高，不适合这个入门级职位。 |  overqualified | underqualified | verb
