crystalline:/ˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. |结晶的；水晶般的；清澈的|Resembling crystal; transparent and clear. Composed of crystals.|例句：The pond was frozen over with a crystalline layer of ice. 池塘被一层晶莹的冰覆盖着。|近义词：clear, transparent, glassy, lucid |反义词:opaque, cloudy, murky|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *crystallinus, from Latin crystallum "crystal" (from Greek krystallos "ice, crystal").
