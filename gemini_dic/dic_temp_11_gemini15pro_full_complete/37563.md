commote: /kəˈmoʊt/ | n. | 百户区，行政区 | A subdivision of a Welsh county, especially in ancient times, often containing about 100 households. |  The commote was the basic unit of local administration in medieval Wales.  中世纪威尔士的基层行政单位是百户区。 |  Each commote had its own court. 每个百户区都有自己的法庭。 | synonyms: district, territory, region | antonyms: N/A | noun
