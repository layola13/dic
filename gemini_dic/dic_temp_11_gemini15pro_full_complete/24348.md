brin:/brɪn/
noun
盐水，卤水
A strong solution of salt and water, used especially for pickling.
The olives had been steeped in brin. 这些橄榄已经用盐水浸泡过了。
近义词: brine, saltwater
反义词: freshwater

词源:
From Old English *bryne "burning," from Proto-Germanic *brunjo "a burning." The sense development from "burning" to "brine" is due to the smarting sensation it causes on a wound.  Related to burn.
