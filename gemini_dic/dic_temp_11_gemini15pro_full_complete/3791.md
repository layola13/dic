agminal:/ˈædʒmɪnəl/| adj.|成群的，聚集的|Of or relating to a cluster or group, especially of nerve fibers.|The agminal lesions were identified on the MRI scan.|近义词：clustered, aggregated, grouped|反义词:scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *agmen*, meaning "a marching column, a train, a flock, a swarm," from *agere* "to drive, lead, act, do."
