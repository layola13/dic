interministerial:/ˌɪntərˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/
adjective
部门间的；跨部委的
Involving or relating to more than one government ministry.  Existing or occurring between ministries.
The interministerial committee is working on a new trade agreement.  这个跨部委委员会正在制定新的贸易协定。
The government established an interministerial task force to address the issue. 政府成立了一个跨部门工作组来处理这个问题。
Synonyms: interdepartmental, cross-ministry, collaborative (between ministries)
Antonyms: intraministerial (within a single ministry), independent (ministries acting alone)
