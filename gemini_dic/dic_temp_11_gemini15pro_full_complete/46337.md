dacian:/ˈdeɪʃən/ | n. adj.|达契亚人，达契亚的|A member of an ancient people who inhabited Dacia (roughly modern Romania) before it was conquered by the Romans.  Also used as an adjective relating to Dacia or its people.|例句：The Dacians were known for their fierce resistance to Roman conquest. 达契亚人以其对罗马征服的强烈抵抗而闻名。

例句：Dacian gold artifacts are highly prized by collectors. 达契亚黄金制品受到收藏家的高度重视。|近义词：Getae (a related Thracian tribe)|反义词:Roman (in the context of the Roman-Dacian wars)|词性:noun, adjective
