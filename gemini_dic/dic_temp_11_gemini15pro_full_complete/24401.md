brioche:/ˈbriːɒʃ/, /briˈɔːʃ/| n. |奶油蛋卷，一种富含鸡蛋和黄油的甜面包|A rich, sweet yeast bread containing a high proportion of butter and eggs. |例句：The bakery is famous for its delicious brioches. 这家面包店以其美味的奶油蛋卷而闻名。

例句：She enjoyed a brioche and coffee for breakfast. 她早餐享用了奶油蛋卷和咖啡。

近义词：pastry, bun, sweet roll |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps plain bread) |词性:noun
