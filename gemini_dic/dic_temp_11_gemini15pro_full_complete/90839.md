hundredth-places:/ˈhʌndrədθ pleɪsɪz/ |n.| 百分位 |The hundredth-places represents the second digit to the right of the decimal point. 百分位代表小数点右边第二位数字。|
Example 1: Round 3.14159 to the hundredth-places. 将 3.14159 四舍五入到百分位。
Example 2:  The value in the hundredth-places is 4. 百分位上的值是 4。|Near synonyms: second decimal place, hundredths| Antonyms: N/A| Noun
