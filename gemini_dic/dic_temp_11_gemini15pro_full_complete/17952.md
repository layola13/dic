befitted:/bɪˈfɪtɪd/
verb
适合，相称
suited, appropriate, proper for (someone or something)
The elegant gown befitted the grand occasion.  这件优雅的礼服很适合这个盛大的场合。
suited, appropriate, becoming
unsuited, inappropriate, unbecoming
verb

Etymology:
From be- + fit, from Old English *fetian "to arrange, put in order," from Proto-Germanic *fegōjaną "to fit together, arrange" (source also of Old Saxon fegian, Old Norse fegja "to decorate, adorn," Old High German feggen "to sweep, cleanse").
