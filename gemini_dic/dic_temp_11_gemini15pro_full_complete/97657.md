inhibited:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd/ | adj. | 拘谨的，压抑的，抑制的 | unable to act or express yourself freely because you are nervous or shy;  slowed or prevented | Example: She felt inhibited by her shyness. 她因为害羞而感到拘谨。 | Example: The growth of the plant was inhibited by lack of sunlight. 植物的生长因缺乏阳光而受到抑制。 | Synonyms: shy, reserved, restrained, repressed, subdued | Antonyms: uninhibited, outgoing, extroverted, expressive, confident | adjective

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/ | v. | 抑制，约束 | to prevent someone from doing something, or to slow something down | Example:  Fear inhibited him from speaking freely. 恐惧使他不敢畅所欲言。 | Example: This drug inhibits the growth of cancer cells. 这种药物抑制癌细胞的生长。 | Synonyms: restrain, curb, check, suppress, repress | Antonyms: encourage, promote, stimulate, facilitate | verb
