impugnable:/ɪmˈpʌɡnəbl/ | adj. | 可质疑的，可责难的，可攻击的 |  Open to attack, criticism, or question; vulnerable. |  His theories were considered impugnable by many of his colleagues. 他的理论被很多同事认为是可质疑的。|  The castle's defenses were no longer impugnable after the invention of gunpowder. 火药发明后，城堡的防御工事不再坚不可摧。 |  challengeable, questionable, disputable, assailable, vulnerable |  unimpeachable, irrefutable, unassailable, invulnerable | adjective
