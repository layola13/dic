criticalnesses:/ˈkrɪtɪkəlnəsəz/
|n.|批判性，挑剔性|The state or quality of being critical; excessive criticism or fault-finding.|Her criticalnesses made it difficult for anyone to please her. 她的挑剔使得很难有人让她满意。|近义词：faultfinding, fastidiousness, captiousness|反义词：tolerance, leniency, acceptance|词性:名词

词源: criticalnesses 源自形容词 critical，其最终源自希腊语 kritikos，意思是“能够判断或分辨”。-ness 是一个名词后缀，用于表示状态或性质。因此，criticalnesses 表示“批判的状态或性质”。
