adductive:/əˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
附加的；引证的
bringing something forward as proof or evidence; inferring
例句：The lawyer presented adductive evidence to support his client's alibi. 律师提供了引证证据来支持其委托人的不在场证明。

例句：Her adductive reasoning convinced the jury of the defendant's innocence. 她引证的推理使陪审团相信了被告的清白。
近义词：supporting, corroborative, substantiating
反义词：refuting, contradictory
词性: adjective
