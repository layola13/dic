incompetently: /ɪnˈkɒmpɪtəntli/ /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtəntli/
| adv. | 无能地；不称职地 | in a way that shows a lack of skill or ability to do something successfully |  She handled the situation incompetently, making things worse. 她无能地处理了这个情况，使事情变得更糟。|Synonyms: ineptly, clumsily, awkwardly, inefficiently | Antonyms: competently, skillfully, adeptly, proficiently | 词性: 副词
