lententide: /ˈlɛntənˌtaɪd/ | n. | 四旬期，大斋期 | The period of Lent, the forty days of fasting and penitence before Easter observed by some Christians. |  During Lententide, many Christians abstain from certain foods.  四旬期期间，许多基督徒会戒除某些食物。 | 近义词: Lent | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially Eastertide as a contrasting period) | 词性: noun

lententide: /ˈlɛntənˌtaɪd/ | adj. | 四旬期的 | Relating to or characteristic of Lent. | Lententide services are often more somber. 四旬期礼拜通常比较严肃。| 近义词: Lenten | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective
