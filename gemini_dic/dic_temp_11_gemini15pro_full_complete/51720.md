devitalize:/diːˈvaɪtəlaɪz/
verb
使失去活力；使失去生命力
To deprive of vitality; to make lifeless or weak.
The long illness devitalized him.  这场久病使他元气大伤。
The city has been devitalized by the departure of so many young people.  许多年轻人的离开使这座城市失去了活力。
synonyms: weaken, enervate, sap, drain
antonyms: energize, invigorate, revitalize
词性: 动词
