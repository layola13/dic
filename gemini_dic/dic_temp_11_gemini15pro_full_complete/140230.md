officious:/əˈfɪʃəs/ | adj. | 爱管闲事的，多管闲事的 | Assertive, meddlesome, and interfering in a domineering way, especially with regard to matters that are not one's concern. | The officious waiter kept interrupting our conversation to offer unwanted advice.  那个多管闲事的服务员不断打断我们的谈话，提供我们不需要的建议。 | Synonyms: meddlesome, interfering, intrusive, pushy, overbearing, domineering, bossy | Antonyms: reserved, unobtrusive, discreet, helpful, respectful | adjective

officious:/əˈfɪʃəs/ | adj. | 非官方的，非正式的 (较少用) | Not officially authorized or appointed, but acting as if one were. |  He made officious pronouncements about the company's future, even though he had no authority to do so. 他对公司的未来发表了非官方的声明，尽管他没有这样做的权力。 | Synonyms: unauthorized, unofficial, informal | Antonyms: official, authorized, formal | adjective
