ill-founded:/ˌɪlˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
无根据的；站不住脚的
based on wrong ideas or information
例句：The accusations against her were completely ill-founded. 对她的指控完全是无中生有。

例句：His fears proved to be ill-founded. 他的恐惧最终证明是没有根据的。

近义词：baseless, groundless, unfounded, unjustified, spurious
反义词：well-founded, justified, valid, sound, reasonable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill + founded (past participle of found, meaning "to establish, base").  "Ill" here carries the sense of "badly" or "inadequately."
