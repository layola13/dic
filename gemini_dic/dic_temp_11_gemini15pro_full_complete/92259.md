hypervascularity:/ˌhaɪpərˌvæskjʊˈlærɪti/
noun
血管增生，血运丰富
The presence of an excessive number of blood vessels in a part of the body.
The tumor showed marked hypervascularity on the angiogram.  肿瘤在血管造影图上显示明显的血管增生。
hyperperfusion, neovascularization
hypovascularity
