harmoniousnesses:/hɑːrˈmoʊniəsnɪsɪz/ | n. |和谐，协调，一致|The state of being harmonious; concord.  A pleasing arrangement of parts.|The harmoniousnesses of the colors in the painting was striking.  这幅画中色彩的和谐令人印象深刻。|Synonyms:  harmony, concord, agreement, unity, consonance|Antonyms:  discord, disharmony, conflict, strife, dissonance|noun

Etymology:
From harmonious + -ness.  Ultimately from Greek *harmonia* (fitting together).
