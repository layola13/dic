nonfulminating:/ˌnɒnˈfʌlmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非暴发性的；非雷鸣般的
Not exploding or detonating suddenly or violently; not fulminating.
The doctor diagnosed a nonfulminating form of the disease. 医生诊断出这是一种非暴发性疾病。
