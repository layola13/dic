advertiser:/ˈædvərˌtaɪzər/| n.|广告商，广告客户|A person or company that pays for advertising.|The advertiser spent a lot of money on the new campaign. 广告商在新广告活动上花费了大量的资金。|synonyms: promoter, sponsor, backer|antonyms: audience, consumer, viewer|noun

etymology: From advertise + -er.  Ultimately from Latin advertere "to turn towards".
