megastome: /ˈmɛɡəˌstoʊm/ | n. |巨口动物；大口动物|An animal with an unusually large mouth opening. |例句：The megastome shark is known for its gaping maw. 巨口鲨以其巨大的嘴巴而闻名。

例句：Certain species of deep-sea fish are considered megastomes due to their disproportionately large mouths.  某些深海鱼类由于其不成比例的大嘴而被认为是巨口动物。

近义词：large-mouthed, gape-mouthed | 反义词: small-mouthed, microstome | 词性: noun
