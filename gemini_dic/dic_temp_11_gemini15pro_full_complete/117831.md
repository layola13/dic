mellifluously: /məˈlɪfluəsli/ /məˈlɪfluəsli/ | adv. |流畅地；甜美地；蜜のように|In a smooth, flowing manner, like honey.  Characterized by a smooth and sweet flow of sound.|Her voice flowed mellifluously through the hall. 她的声音甜美流畅地在整个大厅回荡。|The violin played mellifluously, filling the room with a warm, rich sound. 小提琴演奏流畅甜美，房间里充满了温暖而丰富的声音。|近义词: sweetly, smoothly, musically, lyrically|反义词: harshly, discordantly, roughly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Latin *mellifluus* (“flowing with honey”), from *mel* (“honey”) + *fluere* (“to flow”).
