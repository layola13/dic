counteracter:/ˈkaʊntərˌæktər/ | n. | 反作用物；对抗者；抵消者 |Something that counteracts something else. |  The drug acts as a counteracter to the poison. 这种药物可以作为毒药的解毒剂。 | Synonyms: neutralizer, antidote, remedy | Antonyms:  catalyst, stimulant, enhancer | noun

counteract:/ˌkaʊntərˈækt/ | v. | 抵消；中和；阻碍 | To act against something in order to reduce its force or neutralize it. |  The medicine counteracts the effects of the poison. 这种药可以抵消毒药的作用。| Synonyms: neutralize, offset, negate | Antonyms:  enhance, reinforce, amplify | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + act.  "Counter-" means "against" or "opposite," and "act" refers to taking action.  Therefore, "counteract" literally means to act against something.
