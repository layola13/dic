murdered:/ˈmɜːdəd/ (UK), /ˈmɝːdərd/ (US)| v. |被谋杀的|Killed unlawfully and with premeditation.  Past participle of "murder".|

例句：He was murdered in cold blood. 他被残忍地谋杀了。

例句：The murdered man was found in his apartment. 被谋杀的男子在他的公寓里被发现。


近义词：killed, slain, assassinated|反义词:rescued, saved|词性:Verb (past participle)|
