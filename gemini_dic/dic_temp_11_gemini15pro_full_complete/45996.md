cyathophylloid:/ˌsaɪəθoʊˈfɪlɔɪd/
adjective
杯状珊瑚的，似杯状珊瑚的
Resembling or characteristic of the extinct coral genus Cyathophyllum, typically having a horn-shaped or cylindrical skeleton with numerous radial partitions.  Characterized by or resembling the structure of a Cyathophyllum coral.
The fossil was identified as a cyathophylloid coral. 这块化石被鉴定为杯状珊瑚。
The limestone contained numerous cyathophylloid structures. 石灰岩中包含许多杯状珊瑚结构。
Near Synonyms:  coralloid,  rugose
Antonyms: None specifically for this technical term.
词性: adjective
