fungibles:/ˈfʌndʒɪblz/| n. |替代物；可互换物|Objects that are fungible are of such a nature or kind that one unit or item is equivalent to any other unit or item of the same nature or kind and may be exchanged or substituted therefor.|Example:  Bitcoin is considered by many to be a fungible asset, like a barrel of oil.|Synonyms: interchangeable, replaceable, equivalent, exchangeable|Antonyms: non-fungible, unique, irreplaceable|noun


fungible:/ˈfʌndʒɪbl/| adj. |可替代的；可互换的|Something that is fungible can be exchanged for something else of the same type and value.|Example:  Fungible goods are easily traded on commodity markets.|Synonyms: interchangeable, replaceable, equivalent, exchangeable|Antonyms: non-fungible, unique, irreplaceable|adjective
