bracketing: /ˈbrækɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 分组，括号化，包围 | The process of placing items or values within brackets, either literally (using parentheses, brackets, or braces) or figuratively (grouping things together conceptually). |  Scientists are bracketing the estimated age of the universe between 13 and 14 billion years. 科学家们将宇宙的估计年龄定在130亿到140亿年之间。| synonyms: grouping, classifying, categorizing | antonyms: separating, isolating, dividing | noun

bracketing: /ˈbrækɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  用括号括起来，将…归入同一类，包围  |  The survey bracketed people according to their income levels.  该调查根据人们的收入水平将他们分组。 | synonyms: enclosing, surrounding, encompassing | antonyms: excluding, separating, removing | verb
