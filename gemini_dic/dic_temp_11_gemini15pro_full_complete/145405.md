overcensored: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsərd/|  adj. | 过度审查的 |  Combined form of "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and "censored" (meaning subjected to censorship).  The word "censor" ultimately comes from the Latin "censere," meaning "to assess or judge."|The news report was overcensored, leaving out key details about the incident. 这篇新闻报道过度审查，遗漏了有关事件的关键细节。|Due to the overcensored media, the public was unaware of the true extent of the crisis. 由于媒体过度审查，公众没有意识到危机的真正程度。|近义词：heavily censored, excessively censored, overly redacted|反义词:uncensored, unedited, unexpurgated|词性:形容词
