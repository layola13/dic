madrigalian: /ˌmædrɪˈɡæliən/ | adj. n. | 牧歌式的，情歌体的；牧歌体歌曲，情歌 | Of or relating to a madrigal; in the style of a madrigal. |  The concert featured several madrigalian pieces from the Renaissance. 这场音乐会演奏了几首文艺复兴时期的牧歌作品。| The choir sang a beautiful madrigalian piece.  合唱团演唱了一首优美的牧歌作品。 | 近义词: lyrical, melodic, pastoral | 反义词: dissonant, cacophonous | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Italian *madrigale*, from *matrigale* "song in the mother tongue," perhaps from Late Latin *matricale* "(something) belonging to the matrix, womb," from Latin *matrix* "womb."
