irremediable:/ˌɪrɪˈmiːdiəbl/| adj.|不可挽回的，无法补救的|Not capable of being remedied or corrected; incurable.|例句：The damage caused by the flood was irremediable. 洪水造成的损害是不可挽回的。

例句：His mistake had irremediable consequences. 他的错误造成了无法挽回的后果。

近义词：incurable, irreparable, irreversible, uncorrectable|反义词:remediable, curable, fixable|词性:adjective

词源:Late Middle English: from Old French irremediable, from late Latin irremediabilis, from in- ‘not’ + remediabilis (from remedium ‘remedy’).
