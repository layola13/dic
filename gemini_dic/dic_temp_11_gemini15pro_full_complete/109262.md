lemmata's: /ˈlɛmətəz/ | n. | 词元，词目 | The dictionary lists the lemmata's of all the words. 这本词典列出了所有单词的词元。| Lemmata's are the base forms of words used in dictionaries. 词元是词典中使用的单词的基本形式。 | Example: The lemma of "running" is "run". 例如：“running”的词元是“run”。 | synonyms: headwords, dictionary entries | antonyms: inflected forms | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lemma, from Greek λῆμμα (lēmma, “something received, gain, profit, assumption”), from λαμβάνω (lambanō, “I take”).  The plural form in English can be either lemmata (following the Latin plural) or lemmas (a regular English plural).
