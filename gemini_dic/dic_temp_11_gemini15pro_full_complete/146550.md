overglutted: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌtɪd/
| adj. | 过饱和的，过剩的 | Excessively full; saturated; having a supply that far exceeds demand.  | The market is overglutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。 |  The art market is overglutted with mediocre paintings. 艺术市场充斥着平庸的画作。 | saturated, overflowing, glutted |  undersupplied, deficient, scarce | adjective
