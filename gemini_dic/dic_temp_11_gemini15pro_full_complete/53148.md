diplomatists:/dɪˈpləʊmætɪsts/
n.
外交官（复数）
Officials who represent their country in foreign countries and deal with international negotiations.
The diplomats worked tirelessly to secure a peace treaty.  外交官们不知疲倦地致力于达成和平条约。
The two countries recalled their diplomats. 这两个国家召回了各自的外交官。

近义词:envoys, ambassadors, negotiators
反义词:none

词性:noun (plural)
