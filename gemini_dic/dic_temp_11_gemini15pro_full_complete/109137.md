legislate: /ˈledʒɪsleɪt/ | v. | 立法；制定法律 | to make or enact laws | Congress has the power to legislate on national issues. 国会拥有就国家问题立法的权力。|  The government legislated against discrimination. 政府立法反对歧视。 | enact, decree, ordain | repeal, abolish | verb

legislated: /ˈledʒɪsleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  立法制定的；法定的 |  made or enacted by law |  The legislated minimum wage has increased. 法定的最低工资已经提高了。 | The newly legislated policy will take effect next month. 新立法的政策将于下个月生效。| statutory, legal, enacted | unregulated, illegal | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *legislatus, past participle of legislare "to propose laws," from lex (genitive legis) "law" + -latus, past participle suffix.
