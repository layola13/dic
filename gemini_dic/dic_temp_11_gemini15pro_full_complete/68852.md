fashioners:/ˈfæʃənərz/
n.
时装设计师；塑造者
People who create or design fashion, or those who shape or influence something.
The fashioners of this new trend are a group of young designers.  这个新潮流的缔造者是一群年轻的设计师。
The fashioners of public opinion have a great responsibility.  塑造公众舆论的人责任重大。
synonyms: designers, creators, makers, stylists
antonyms: destroyers, imitators
