encode:/ɪnˈkoʊd/ (US), /ɪnˈkəʊd/ (UK)| vt. |编码，加密| To convert information into a coded form.  |例句：The message was encoded to protect its secrecy. 这条消息经过加密以保护其机密性。

例句：The software encodes data for transmission over the internet.  该软件将数据编码以便通过互联网传输。

近义词：encrypt, cipher, code|反义词: decode, decrypt|词性:动词

encode:/ɪnˈkoʊd/ (US), /ɪnˈkəʊd/ (UK)| n. |编码，加密（名词形式较少用）| The act or process of encoding. |例句：The encode of the data took several hours.  数据的编码花了几个小时。（用法较少见）

例句：The security system uses a complex encode to protect user information.  该安全系统使用复杂的编码来保护用户信息。（用法较少见）


近义词：encryption, ciphering, coding|反义词: decoding, decryption|词性:名词
