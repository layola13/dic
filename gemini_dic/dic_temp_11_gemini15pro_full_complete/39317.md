conirostres:/ˌkɒnɪˈrɒstriːz/ /ˌkɑːnɪˈrɑːstriːz/
| n. | 雀形目锥嘴雀亚目|A suborder of passerine birds characterized by their cone-shaped bills, including finches, sparrows, and grosbeaks.|The conirostres are a diverse group of birds found worldwide. 锥嘴雀亚目是一个种类繁多的鸟类群体，分布于世界各地。|
