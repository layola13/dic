comfortress: /ˈkʌmfətrɪs/
noun
女安慰者; 安慰的女人
a woman who provides comfort
例句：She acted as a comfortress to her grieving friend. 她作为安慰者陪伴她悲伤的朋友。

例句：The queen was a comfortress to her people during the war.  在战争期间，女王是她子民的慰藉。

近义词：consoler, comforter, soother
反义词：tormentor, harasser

词性: n.
