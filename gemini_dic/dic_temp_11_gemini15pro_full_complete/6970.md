andis:/ˈændɪz/|conj.|和，与|Andis is a conjunction, a shortened form of "and is," often used in informal speech and writing.  It connects two clauses or phrases, indicating that both are true or relevant.|She's smart andis also very kind. 她很聪明，也很善良。|近义词:and|反义词:nor, but|词性:连词
