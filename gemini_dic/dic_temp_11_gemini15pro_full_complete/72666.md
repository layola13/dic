foam:/foʊm/ (US) /fəʊm/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|泡沫，起泡沫|A mass of small bubbles formed on or in a liquid, typically by agitation or fermentation.  A lightweight material, typically plastic or rubber, made by solidifying foam.|The beer had a thick head of foam. 啤酒上面有一层厚厚的泡沫。|
The waves crashed against the rocks, sending up sprays of white foam. 海浪拍打着岩石，激起白色的泡沫。|
He foamed the milk for his cappuccino. 他为他的卡布奇诺打了奶泡。|
The detergent foamed as I washed the dishes. 我洗碗时洗涤剂起了泡沫。|synonyms: froth, lather, bubbles, spume|antonyms: liquid, solid|noun, verb
