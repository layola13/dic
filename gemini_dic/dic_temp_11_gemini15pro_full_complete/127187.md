nature:/ˈneɪtʃər/ (US), /ˈneɪtʃə/ (UK)| n.| 自然；性质；本性；种类 |The beauty of nature is breathtaking.  大自然之美令人叹为观止。| essence, character, type, kind | artificial, unnatural | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *nature (Modern French nature), from Latin nātūra (“birth, origin, natural constitution or quality”), from nātus (“born”), perfect passive participle of nāscor (“be born”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵenh₁- (“to give birth, beget”).
