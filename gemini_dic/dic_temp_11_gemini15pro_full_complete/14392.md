autostability:/ˌɔːtəʊstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 自动稳定性 | The ability of a system, especially an aircraft, to maintain a stable flight path or equilibrium without constant input or adjustment. | The aircraft's autostability allowed the pilot to release the controls briefly.  这架飞机的自动稳定性允许飞行员短暂地放开控制杆。| Near synonyms: self-stability, inherent stability | Antonyms: instability, lability | noun
