ETYMOLOGY->
From Bern, the capital city of Switzerland, + -ese (suffix forming adjectives relating to a place).  The name "Bernese Mountain Dog" comes from its origin in the canton of Bern in Switzerland.  Originally used as farm dogs, they were known as Dürrbächler, named after the town and inn (Dürrbach) where they were commonly traded.
