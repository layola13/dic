impartibility:/ɪmˌpɑːrʃiˈælɪti/
n.
公平；公正；不偏不倚
The state of being impartial; fairness; lack of bias.
The judge demonstrated impartiality in his handling of the case.  法官在处理案件时表现出了公正。
Synonyms: fairness, neutrality, objectivity, disinterest, equitableness
Antonyms: bias, partiality, prejudice, unfairness
noun
