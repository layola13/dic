anti-malthusianism:/ˌænti mælˈθuːziənɪzəm/ | n. | 反马尔萨斯主义 | The belief that Malthus's prediction about population growth outstripping food supply is incorrect, often emphasizing the role of technological advancements and human ingenuity in increasing food production and resource management. | Example: Anti-Malthusianism argues that innovation can solve resource scarcity. 反马尔萨斯主义认为创新可以解决资源短缺问题。

Example:  The Green Revolution is often cited as evidence supporting anti-Malthusianism. 绿色革命经常被引用作为支持反马尔萨斯主义的证据。| 近义词: Cornucopianism | 反义词: Malthusianism | 词性: noun
