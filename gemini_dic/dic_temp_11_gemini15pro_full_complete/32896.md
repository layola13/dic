chondrostei: /kɒnˈdrɒsti.aɪ/
n.
软骨硬鳞鱼类
a group of primitive ray-finned fishes characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton, heterocercal tail, and ganoid scales.  They include sturgeons and paddlefishes.
The chondrostei are an ancient group of fish. 软骨硬鳞鱼类是一个古老的鱼类群体。
Sturgeons, members of the Chondrostei, are prized for their caviar.  鲟鱼，软骨硬鳞鱼类的一员，因其鱼子酱而备受珍视。

近义词:  Acipenseriformes (鲟形目 - a more modern taxonomic term)
反义词:  (No direct antonym as it's a taxonomic group. Could consider Teleostei - 真骨鱼类 - as a contrasting, more derived group)
词性: noun
