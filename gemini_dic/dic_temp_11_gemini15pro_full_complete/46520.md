dakoit:/dəˈkɔɪt/ | n. | 土匪，强盗 | A member of a band of armed robbers in India or Burma (Myanmar).  Originally, they were often mounted robbers. |  The dakoits terrorized the village, stealing livestock and valuables.  土匪恐吓了村庄，偷走了牲畜和贵重物品。| synonym: bandit, robber, outlaw, brigand | antonym: lawman, police officer | noun
