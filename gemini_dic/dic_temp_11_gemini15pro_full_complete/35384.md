co-operate:/koʊˈɑːpəreɪt/ (US), /kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ (UK)| v. |合作，协作|To work together towards a common goal or purpose.|Example: The two companies co-operated on the project.  两家公司合作开展这个项目。

Example: We need to co-operate if we want to succeed.  如果我们想成功，就需要合作。|近义词：collaborate, work together, join forces, team up|反义词: compete, oppose, obstruct|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin "co-" (together) + "operari" (to work).
