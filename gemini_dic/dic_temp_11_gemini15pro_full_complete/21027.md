blew:/bluː/
verb
吹；刮；吹奏；爆炸；输掉；挥霍
past simple of blow
The wind blew hard all night.  风刮了一整夜。
He blew out the candles. 他吹灭了蜡烛。


近义词: puffed, blasted, whistled, squandered (depending on the meaning)
反义词: inhaled, saved (depending on the meaning)
词性: verb (past tense of "blow")

词源:
From Middle English *blewen, from Old English blāwan (“to blow, breathe, puff, inflate, kindle, announce by blast of trumpet”), from Proto-Germanic *blēaną (“to blow”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰléh₁- (“to blow, swell, bloom”). Cognate with Scots blaw (“to blow”), West Frisian blaze (“to blow”), Dutch blazen (“to blow”), German blasen (“to blow”), Danish blæse (“to blow”), Swedish blåsa (“to blow”).
