irriguous:/ɪˈrɪɡjuəs/
adjective
灌溉的；水源丰富的
Supplied with water by artificial means; watered.  Characterized by abundant water.
例句：The irriguous land yielded a bountiful harvest. 这片灌溉的土地带来了丰收。

例句：The valley was irriguous and green, a stark contrast to the surrounding desert. 山谷水源丰富，绿意盎然，与周围的沙漠形成鲜明对比。
近义词：watered, irrigated, fertile
反义词：arid, dry, barren
词性:adjective
词源: From Latin irrigare ("to water, moisten, irrigate"),  from in- ("in, on") + rigare ("to water, irrigate").
