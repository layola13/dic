deplantation:/ˌdiːplænˈteɪʃən/
n.
移植；移栽；（器官或组织的）摘除
The surgical removal of an organ or tissue from its usual place.  The deplantation of a failing heart before transplantation.
The deplantation of the diseased liver was successful.  病变肝脏的摘除手术很成功。
近义词: removal, excision, extraction
反义词: implantation, transplantation
词性: 名词

词源:  From French *déplantation*, from déplanter (to transplant, uproot), from de- (expressing removal) + planter (to plant).  Ultimately from Latin planta (plant).
