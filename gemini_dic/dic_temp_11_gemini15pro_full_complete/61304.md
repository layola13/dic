embarkations:/ɛmˌbɑːrˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
登船，上船；开始，着手
The process of embarking, or an instance of this.  The act of going on board a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  The beginning of a course of action.
The embarkations were delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，登船被延误了。
The company's embarkation on a new marketing strategy proved successful. 该公司开始新的营销策略证明是成功的。

近义词: beginnings, commencements, departures, launchings
反义词: disembarkations, landings, arrivals

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From French embarquement, from embarquer "to embark," from em- "in" + barque "bark, boat."
