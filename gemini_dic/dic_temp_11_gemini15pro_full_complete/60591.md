elatrometer:/ɪˌlætˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 弹性计，弹力计 | An instrument for measuring the elasticity of a substance, especially of rubber or other elastic materials. | This elatrometer measures the rebound resilience of the rubber.  这台弹性计测量橡胶的反弹回弹性。

The laboratory uses a precision elatrometer to test the elasticity of different polymers.  实验室使用精密弹性计来测试不同聚合物的弹性。 | 近义词: elastometer, durometer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *elater* (driver, impeller) + *-meter* (measure).
