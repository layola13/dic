countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | v. | 撤销，取消（命令） | To revoke or cancel (a command or order). | The general countermanded the order to attack. 将军撤销了进攻的命令. | synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, annul, override, repeal, retract, withdraw, reverse, abrogate | antonyms: order, command, direct, instruct | verb


countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | n. | 取消命令，撤销命令 | An order that cancels or reverses a previous command. | The countermand arrived just in time to prevent the disaster. 取消命令及时到达，阻止了灾难的发生. | synonyms: revocation, cancellation, annulment | antonyms: order, command | noun
