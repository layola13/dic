mahone:/məˈhoʊn/| n. |我的朋友，哥们（主要用于爱尔兰英语）|A term of endearment or familiar address, similar to "my friend" or "pal," primarily used in Irish English.  It's derived from the Irish Gaelic "mo chara" meaning "my friend."|Example: "How are ya, mahone?" 你好呀，哥们？|Example: "Come on, mahone, let's go to the pub." 来吧，哥们，我们去酒吧。|Synonym: pal, buddy, friend, mate|Antonym: enemy, foe|Noun
