evenhanded:/ˈiːvənˈhændɪd/| adj.|公平的，公正的，不偏不倚的|Fair and impartial; treating all sides alike.|The judge delivered an evenhanded verdict, considering all the evidence presented.  法官做出了公正的判决，考虑了所有提出的证据。|例句：The mediator took an evenhanded approach to resolving the dispute. 调解员采取了不偏不倚的方式来解决争端。|近义词：impartial, unbiased, fair, just, equitable|反义词：biased, prejudiced, unfair, unjust, partial|词性:形容词
