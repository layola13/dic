emeriti:/ɪˈmɛrɪtaɪ/
noun
退休人员；荣誉退休人员
plural of emeritus; retired professors or ministers retaining their title as an honor.
Professor Green, one of the university's emeriti, gave a speech. 格林教授，大学的荣誉退休教授之一，发表了讲话。
retired professors, emeritus professors
current professors, active professors
n.
