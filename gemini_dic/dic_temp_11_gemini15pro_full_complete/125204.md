multicircuited:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪtɪd/
adjective
多电路的，多回路的
Having multiple circuits; equipped with many circuits.
The multicircuited system provided redundancy in case of failure.  这个多回路系统在出现故障时提供了冗余。
The engineer designed a multicircuited device to ensure uninterrupted power.  工程师设计了一个多电路设备以确保不间断供电。
近义词: multi-wired, complexly wired
反义词: single-circuited, simply wired
词性: adjective
词源: multi- (many) + circuit + -ed (forming adjectives)
