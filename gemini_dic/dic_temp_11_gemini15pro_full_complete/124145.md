To subdue (the body or its needs and desires) especially by abstinence or self-inflicted pain.|She was mortified when her mother yelled at her in front of her friends. 她妈妈当着她朋友的面吼她，让她感到很羞愧。|synonyms: humiliate, shame, embarrass, chagrin, abash|antonyms:  exalt, honor, praise, gratify|verb
