marvel:/ˈmɑːrvl/ (US) /ˈmɑːvəl/ (UK)| n. v.|奇迹；令人惊奇的事物；惊叹，感到惊奇|Something that causes wonder; be filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：The Grand Canyon is a natural marvel. 大峡谷是一个自然奇观。|近义词：wonder, miracle, phenomenon|反义词：disappointment, letdown|词性:名词

marvel:/ˈmɑːrvl/ (US) /ˈmɑːvəl/ (UK)| v.|惊叹，感到惊奇|Be filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：I marvel at her talent. 我惊叹于她的才华。|近义词：wonder, admire, be amazed|反义词：disregard, ignore|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *merveille*, from Latin *mirabilia* (“wonderful things”), neuter plural of *mirabilis* (“wonderful, marvelous”), from *mirari* (“to wonder at”).
