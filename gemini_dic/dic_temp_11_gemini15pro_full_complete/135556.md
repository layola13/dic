nonpolarizing:/ˌnɑːnˈpoʊləˌraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
非极化的
Not causing polarization, especially of light.  Reducing or eliminating glare and reflections.
This lens is coated with a nonpolarizing film.  这个镜片涂有一层非极化膜。
non-polarizing, non-polarising
polarizing, polarising
adjective
