overattention:/ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnʃən/ | n. | 过度关注，过分关注 | Excessive attention; paying more attention to something than is necessary or helpful. |  He suffered from overattention to detail, missing the bigger picture. 他过度关注细节，忽略了全局。| synonyms: hyperfocus, preoccupation, obsession | antonyms: inattention, neglect, disregard | noun

overattention:/ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnʃən/ | n. | 过度关注，过分关注 | The act of giving excessive attention; focusing too much on something. | The child's overattention to the video game caused him to neglect his homework.  这个孩子过度关注电子游戏，导致他忽略了家庭作业。| synonyms: fixation, absorption | antonyms: distraction, indifference | noun
