abhorrence:/əˈbɔːrəns/ /əbˈhɔːrəns/
近义词: hatred, loathing, detestation, aversion, repugnance, disgust
反义词: love, admiration, affection, liking
词性: noun

abhorrence:/əˈbɔːrəns/ /əbˈhɔːrəns/
词源: From Latin abhorrentia, from abhorrens, present participle of abhorrere "to shrink back from, shudder at," from ab- "away from" + horrere "to bristle, shudder."
词性: noun
