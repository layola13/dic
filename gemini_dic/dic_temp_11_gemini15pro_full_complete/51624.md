developmental:/dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntl/| n.|发育; 发展; 成长; 进化|The process of developing or being developed.|例句：Child development is a complex process. 儿童发展是一个复杂的过程。|近义词：evolution, growth, progress|反义词：stagnation, regression|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *developpen*, from Old French *desveloper* "to unwrap, unfold," from *des-* (expressing reversal) + *voloper* "to wrap."  The "-mental" suffix denotes "pertaining to."
