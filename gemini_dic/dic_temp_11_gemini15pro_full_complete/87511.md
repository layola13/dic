heterodactylae:/ˌhɛtərəˈdæktɪliː/
n.
异趾类
A group of birds characterized by having the first and second toes pointing backward and the third and fourth toes pointing forward, as in woodpeckers.
Woodpeckers, toucans, and cuckoos are examples of heterodactylae.  啄木鸟、巨嘴鸟和杜鹃是异趾类的例子。
synonym:  zygodactyl
antonym:  pamprodactyl
noun
