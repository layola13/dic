diligence:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəns/| n.|勤奋，勤勉，用功|Careful and persistent work or effort.|She achieved success through hard work and diligence. 她通过努力工作和勤奋取得了成功。|近义词：industry, assiduity, perseverance|反义词：laziness, negligence, indolence|Noun

词源:
From Middle French *diligence*, from Latin *diligentia*, from *diligens* (“careful, attentive”), present participle of *diligere* (“to value highly, esteem, love; to select, single out”), from *dis-* + *legere* (“to choose, gather, read”).
