digestiveness:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪvnəs/
noun
消化率；易消化性
The quality of being easy to digest.
Oatmeal is known for its high digestiveness. 燕麦以其高消化率而闻名。
digestibility, ease of digestion
indigestibility
