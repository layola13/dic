healingly:/ˈhiːlɪŋli/
adv.
治疗地，有疗效地
in a way that heals or promotes healing.
Her touch was gentle and healingly. 她的触摸轻柔而有疗效。
近义词: therapeutically, curatively, restoratively
反义词: harmfully, destructively
词性: 副词

词源:
From "healing" + "-ly."  "Healing" comes from Old English "hælan," meaning "to make whole, cure."  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs from adjectives.
