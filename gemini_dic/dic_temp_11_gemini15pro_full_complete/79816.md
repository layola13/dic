glycoluril:/ˌɡlaɪkəʊˈljʊərɪl/ | n. |乙内酰脲|A bicyclic organic compound with the formula C4H6N4O2. It is a white, crystalline solid that is poorly soluble in water.  It's used in the synthesis of various compounds, including polymers and pharmaceuticals. |Glycoluril is a key component in some slow-release fertilizers. 乙内酰脲是某些缓释肥料的关键成分。

Glycoluril derivatives are used in the manufacturing of certain resins. 乙内酰脲衍生物用于制造某些树脂。|近义词:tetrahydroimidazo[4,5-d]imidazole-2,5(1H,3H)-dione (IUPAC name)|反义词: N/A | noun
