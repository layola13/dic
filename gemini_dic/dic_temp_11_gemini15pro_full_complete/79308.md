gle:/ɡəl/
gle:/(no standard pronunciation - part of a word)/| suffix | 无独立中文含义，表示“闪耀、发光”|  A suffix used to form verbs and nouns often related to light or shining.  It's often combined with other elements to form words like "gleam," "glimmer," "glitter," and "glisten." |  No standalone example possible as it's a suffix.  See examples under related words. | 近义词: shine, sparkle, shimmer (for related words) | 反义词: dull, matte (for related words) | suffix


gleam:/ɡliːm/| n. vi. | 微光；闪光；闪烁| A faint or brief light, or a subdued or reflected light.  As a verb, it means to shine faintly or reflect light. | The moonlight gleamed on the water. 月光在水面上闪闪发光。| The polished surface gleamed under the lights.  抛光后的表面在灯光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: glimmer, shimmer, shine | 反义词: dullness | noun, verb


glimmer:/ˈɡlɪmər/| n. vi. | 微光；闪烁； glimmer of hope:一线希望 | A faint, unsteady light. As a verb, to shine faintly or unsteadily. |  A glimmer of hope remained. 还有一线希望。|  The candle glimmered in the darkness. 蜡烛在黑暗中闪烁。| 近义词: gleam, shimmer, flicker | 反义词: darkness | noun, verb


glitter:/ˈɡlɪtər/| n. vi. | 闪耀；闪光；灿烂|  To shine with many small, sparkling reflections.  As a noun, it refers to small, sparkling objects or the sparkling reflection itself. | The diamonds glittered brilliantly. 钻石闪耀着光芒。|  She wore a dress covered in glitter. 她穿着一件缀满亮片的连衣裙。| 近义词: sparkle, shimmer, flash | 反义词: dullness | noun, verb


glisten:/ˈɡlɪsən/| vi. | 闪耀；闪光（尤指潮湿或光滑的表面）|  To shine with a soft reflected light, especially of something wet or smooth. | Her eyes glistened with tears. 她的眼里闪着泪光。|  The wet pavement glistened in the rain.  湿漉漉的人行道在雨中闪闪发光。 | 近义词: gleam, glitter, shine | 反义词: dull | verb
