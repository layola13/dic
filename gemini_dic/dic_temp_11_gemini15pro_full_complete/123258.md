monodelphous:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfəs/ | adj. |单孔的，单阴道的| Having a single vagina or uterine cavity, as in most placental mammals.  It refers to the condition where the uteri are fused together, forming a single chamber. | Example: Humans are monodelphous. 人类是单孔的。

Example:  Most primates are monodelphous, with a single uterus.  大多数灵长类动物都是单孔的，只有一个子宫。 | 近义词: synuterine | 反义词: didelphous | 词性: adjective
