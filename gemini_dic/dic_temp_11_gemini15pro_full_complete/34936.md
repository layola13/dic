clobbered:/ˈklɒbəd/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbərd/ (US)| v. |痛打；狠狠批评；彻底击败|To hit someone or something very hard; to defeat someone completely.|例句：Our team got absolutely clobbered in the final. 我们的队伍在决赛中惨败。

例句：He clobbered his opponent in the second round. 他在第二轮比赛中击败了对手。|近义词：thrash, beat, defeat, trounce|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|词性：动词


clobbered:/ˈklɒbəd/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbərd/ (US)| adj. |喝醉的；筋疲力尽的|Drunk; exhausted.|例句：I was absolutely clobbered after that party. 那场派对之后我喝得烂醉。

例句：I felt completely clobbered after working all weekend.  整个周末工作后，我感到筋疲力尽。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, exhausted, worn out|反义词：sober, energetic|词性：形容词


词源：
Likely from the 19th-century British slang term "clobber," meaning "to hit hard," possibly related to "club."
