as-is:/ˌæzˈɪz/| adj. adv.|按现状，原样|In its current condition, without any changes or modifications.|We bought the house as-is, knowing it needed some repairs. 我们按原样买了这栋房子，知道它需要一些修理。|近义词：unaltered, unchanged|反义词:improved, renovated|词性:形容词, 副词
词源: From the phrase "as it is," meaning "in the state that it currently exists."  It became hyphenated for use as a modifier.
