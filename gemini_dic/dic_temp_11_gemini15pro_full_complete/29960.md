cavernously:/ˈkævərnəsli/| adv.|像洞穴般地，空洞地|Resembling a cavern, especially in being vast, dark, or hollow.|His voice echoed cavernously through the empty hall. 他的声音在空旷的大厅里回荡，像洞穴一样。|The wound gaped cavernously.  伤口像洞穴般张开着。|近义词：hollowly, emptily, resonantly|反义词：solidly, compactly|词性: 副词

cavernous:/ˈkævərnəs/| adj.|像洞穴的，巨大的，空旷的|Like a cavern; vast or hollow.|The cavernous warehouse was dimly lit.  巨大的仓库灯光昏暗。|He had cavernous eyes. 他有双深陷的眼睛。|近义词：huge, vast, immense, hollow|反义词：small, tiny, cramped|词性: 形容词
