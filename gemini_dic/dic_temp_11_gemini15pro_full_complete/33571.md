cilia: /ˈsɪliə/ (plural: cilia) /ˈsɪliaɪ/ (singular: cilium)
n.|纤毛|Slender, hair-like projections on the surface of some cells, used for movement or sensing.|The cilia in our respiratory system help to trap and remove dust and other particles. 我们的呼吸系统中的纤毛有助于捕获和清除灰尘和其他颗粒。|flagella, hairs| - |noun
cilia: /ˈsɪliə/ (plural: cilia) /ˈsɪliaɪ/ (singular: cilium)
n.|睫毛|Eyelashes.|Her long cilia framed her beautiful eyes. 她长长的睫毛衬托着她美丽的双眼。|eyelashes| - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cilium* "eyelid, eyelash," later generalized to any hair-like projection.
