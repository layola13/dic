accordable:/əˈkɔːrdəbl/
adjective
可给予的，可授予的，可调和的
able to be accorded; that can be granted or given
例句：The request for more funding was accordable.  更多资金的请求是可以批准的。

例句：The terms of the agreement were accordable to both parties.  协议条款双方都能接受。

近义词：grantable, bestowable, agreeable
反义词：unaccordable, unacceptable, non-negotiable
词性：adjective
