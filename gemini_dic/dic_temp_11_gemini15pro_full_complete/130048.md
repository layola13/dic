non-person:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːsn/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrsn/ (American)| n. |非人；不被认为是人的人；不受法律保护的人|A person who is considered to have no rights, status, or social significance, often due to political or social circumstances.  They are effectively erased or excluded from society.  |The regime declared political dissidents non-persons, stripping them of their citizenship and rights.  该政权宣布政治异议人士为非人，剥夺了他们的公民身份和权利。

The government's policy effectively rendered entire groups of people non-persons, denying them basic human rights. 政府的政策有效地使整群人成为非人，剥夺了他们的基本人权。|Synonyms: outcast, pariah, untouchable|Antonyms: citizen, individual, person|noun
