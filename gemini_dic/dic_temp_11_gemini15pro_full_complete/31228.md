chantant:/ʃɑ̃ˈtɑ̃/
adjective
歌唱的，吟唱的，旋律优美的
Characterized by a singing or songlike quality; melodious.
Her voice was soft and chantant, like a gentle lullaby.  她的声音轻柔而悠扬，像一首温柔的摇篮曲。
The chantant melody of the flute filled the air. 悠扬的笛声飘满了空中。
melodious, musical, lyrical, tuneful
discordant, harsh, grating
