dextroses: /ˈdɛkstroʊsɪz/
| n. |葡萄糖|A simple sugar that is an important energy source in living organisms and is a component of many carbohydrates.|Dextroses are often used in intravenous solutions to provide energy to patients. 葡萄糖经常用于静脉注射溶液，为患者提供能量。|Synonyms: glucose, simple sugar|Antonyms: complex carbohydrate|noun
