atheist: /ˈeɪθiɪst/ (US) /ˈeɪθiɪst/ (UK)| n. |无神论者|A person who disbelieves or lacks belief in the existence of God or gods.|例句：He is a devout atheist and doesn't believe in any supernatural beings. 他是一个虔诚的无神论者，不相信任何超自然的存在。

例句：She declared herself an atheist after years of questioning her faith.  经过多年的信仰质疑后，她宣布自己是无神论者。

近义词：nonbeliever, unbeliever, skeptic|反义词: theist, believer|词性: noun
