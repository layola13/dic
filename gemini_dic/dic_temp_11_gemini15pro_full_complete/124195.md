mosel:/ˈmoʊzəl/| n. |摩泽尔河；摩泽尔葡萄酒|A river that rises in France, flows through Luxembourg and Germany, and joins the Rhine at Koblenz. Also, a type of light dry white wine produced in the Mosel valley. |例句：The Mosel river is known for its scenic beauty. 摩泽尔河以其优美的景色而闻名。

例句：We enjoyed a bottle of crisp Mosel wine with our dinner. 我们晚餐时享用了一瓶清爽的摩泽尔葡萄酒。|近义词：Rhine (for the river, geographically), Riesling (for the wine, varietally)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
