isostemony:/ˌaɪsoʊˈstɛməni/
n.
等长雄蕊；等长雄蕊现象
The condition in which all the stamens in a flower are of the same length.
Isostemony is a characteristic feature of some plant families. 等长雄蕊是一些植物科的特征。
The botanist observed isostemony in the flower specimen.  植物学家观察到花的标本是等长雄蕊的。
近义词: homostemony
反义词: heterostemony
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- (Greek: equal) + stemon (Greek: warp thread, stamen)
