countermovements:/ˈkaʊntərˌmuːvmənts/| n. |反向运动，对抗性运动|An action or series of actions taken in opposition to another action or set of actions.|The government's economic policy triggered countermovements from various sectors of society. 政府的经济政策引发了社会各界的反向运动。|Synonyms: counteractions, reactions, responses|Antonyms: actions, initiatives|noun

countermovement:/ˈkaʊntərˌmuːvmənt/| n. |反运动，逆流|A movement that opposes a prevailing tendency or trend.|The countermovement against globalization gained momentum in recent years. 反全球化运动近年来势头强劲。|Synonyms: opposition, resistance, backlash|Antonyms: trend, mainstream|noun
