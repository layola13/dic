神经切断术医师
A surgeon who specializes in neurotomy (the surgical division of a nerve).
Example: The neurotomist performed a successful operation to relieve the patient's chronic pain.  神经切断术医师成功地进行了一次手术，缓解了病人的慢性疼痛。

Synonym: nerve surgeon
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps a neurologist who focuses on non-surgical treatments)
Etymology:  neuro- (Greek, "nerve") + -tomist (Greek, "-tomos," "cutting" + "-ist," "one who practices")
