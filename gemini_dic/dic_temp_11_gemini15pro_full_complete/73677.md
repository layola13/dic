forgetfulnesses:/fərˈɡetfəlnəsɪz/
n.
健忘；遗忘（forgetfulness的复数）
The state of being forgetful; instances of forgetting.
His forgetfulnesses were becoming more frequent. 他的健忘越来越频繁了。
近义词:oblivions, amnesias
反义词:remembrances, mindfulnesses
词性:名词

词源: From forgetful + -ness + -es.  Forgetful derives from Old English *forġietful (attested only in *forġietfulnes), from forġietan "to forget," of West Germanic origin; related to Dutch vergeten and German vergessen. The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state or quality. The suffix -es creates the plural form.
