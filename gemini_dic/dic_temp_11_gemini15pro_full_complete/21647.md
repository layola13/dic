blurting:/ˈblɜːrtɪŋ/
|n.|脱口而出；突然说出|The act of saying something suddenly and without thinking.|His blurting of the secret surprised everyone. 他脱口而出这个秘密，让所有人都很惊讶。|近义词：uttering, exclaiming, divulging|反义词：concealing, suppressing|词性:名词

blurt:/blɜːrt/
|vt.|脱口而出；突然说出|To say something suddenly and without thinking.|He blurted out the answer before I could even finish the question. 我还没问完问题，他就脱口说出了答案。|近义词：utter, exclaim, divulge|反义词：conceal, suppress|词性:动词
