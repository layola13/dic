biders:/ˈbɪdərz/
n.
投标人，竞标者
People or companies who offer a price for something, especially at an auction.
例句：The bidders competed fiercely for the antique vase. 投标者们为这件古董花瓶展开了激烈的竞争。

例句：Several bidders expressed interest in the property.  几位竞标者对这处房产表示了兴趣。

近义词：tenderers, offerers, applicants
反义词：sellers, vendors
词性：名词
