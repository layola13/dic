grunge:/ɡrʌndʒ/| n. adj.|垃圾，污垢；颓废摇滚|A style of rock music characterized by distorted electric guitars and lyrics expressing disillusionment and apathy.|The band's sound was a mix of punk and grunge. 这个乐队的音乐风格是朋克和垃圾摇滚的混合体。|近义词：dirt, grime, filth; alternative rock|反义词：cleanliness; pop|词性：名词，形容词

grunge:/ɡrʌndʒ/| adj.|邋遢的，破旧的|Dirty and untidy.|He wore a grunge outfit of ripped jeans and a faded t-shirt. 他穿着一身邋遢的衣服，破洞牛仔裤和褪色的T恤。|近义词：scruffy, messy, unkempt|反义词：neat, tidy, clean|词性：形容词
