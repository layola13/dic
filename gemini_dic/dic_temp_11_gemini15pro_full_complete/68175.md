fainter:/ˈfeɪntər (US) / ˈfeɪntə(r) (UK)| adj.|更微弱的；更模糊的；更昏厥的|Comparative form of faint: more faint.  Less strong, clear, or distinct.|The sound grew fainter as the car drove away. 汽车开走后，声音越来越微弱了。|Synonyms: weaker, dimmer, paler, lighter|Antonyms: stronger, brighter, clearer, louder|adjective

fainter:/ˈfeɪntər (US) / ˈfeɪntə(r) (UK)| n.|快要昏倒的人|A person who is about to faint.|She felt faint and like a fainter, she sat down quickly. 她感觉快要晕倒了，像一个快要昏倒的人一样，她迅速坐了下来。|Synonyms: weakling|Antonyms: strong person|noun
