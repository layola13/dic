boming:/ˈboʊmɪŋ/ | adj. | 繁荣的，兴旺的 | experiencing a period of great prosperity or rapid economic growth | The booming economy created many new jobs.  繁荣的经济创造了许多新的就业机会。|例句：The tourism industry is booming in this region.  该地区的旅游业蓬勃发展。| 近义词：flourishing, thriving, prosperous | 反义词：declining, failing, stagnant | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "boom" + "-ing" suffix, indicating a present participle or adjective.  "Boom" itself originates from a Middle Dutch word "boemen" meaning "to resound."
