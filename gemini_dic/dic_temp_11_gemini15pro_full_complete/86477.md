heifer:/ˈhɛfər/| n.|小母牛（未生育过的）|A young female cow that has not yet had a calf. |例句：The farmer bought a new heifer for his herd. 农民为他的牛群买了一头新的小母牛。

例句：The heifer grazed peacefully in the meadow. 小母牛在草地上平静地吃草。|近义词：cow, bovine|反义词:bull, steer|noun
