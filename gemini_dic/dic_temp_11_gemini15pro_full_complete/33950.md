cirrostratus:/ˌsɪroʊˈstrætəs/ /ˌsɪrəˈstreɪtəs/| n. |卷层云|A high-altitude, thin, sheetlike cloud, composed of ice crystals, that often covers the entire sky and sometimes produces a halo around the sun or moon.|例句：The cirrostratus clouds veiled the sun, creating a diffuse light. 卷层云遮蔽了太阳， creating a diffuse light.

例句：The appearance of cirrostratus clouds often signals an approaching warm front. 卷层云的出现通常预示着暖锋的到来。

|近义词：high cloud, veil cloud|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific cloud type)|词性:noun
