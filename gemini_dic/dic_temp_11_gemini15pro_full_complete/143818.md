outpassing:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːsɪŋ/
verb
超越传球，传球胜过
The act of passing the ball better or more frequently than an opponent.
Liverpool’s midfield outpassed Real Madrid in the first half. 利物浦的中场在上半场传球次数超过了皇家马德里。
outplay, surpass
be outpassed by
