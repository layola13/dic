lawrencium: /lɔːˈrɛnsiəm/ | n. |铹|A synthetic radioactive chemical element with the symbol Lr and atomic number 103. It is named after Ernest Lawrence, inventor of the cyclotron.|Lawrencium is a highly radioactive element and is not found in nature. | 近义词: (As it's an element, it doesn't have true synonyms.
