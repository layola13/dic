antiptosis:/ˌæntaɪˈtoʊsɪs/ | n. | 下垂矫正术；上睑下垂矫正术 | The surgical correction of ptosis (drooping) of an organ, especially the upper eyelid. |  She underwent antiptosis surgery to correct her drooping eyelid. 她接受了上睑下垂矫正手术来矫正她下垂的眼睑。|  Synonym:  blepharoplasty (for eyelid ptosis) | Antonym:  ptosis | noun
