misevaluations:/ˌmɪsɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃənz/| n. |错误评估，误判|An act or instance of misjudging the value or worth of something or someone. |The company's misevaluations of the market led to significant losses. 公司对市场的错误评估导致了重大损失。| The coach's misevaluation of the opposing team's strengths resulted in a crushing defeat. 教练对对方球队实力的误判导致了惨败。|近义词：misjudgments, miscalculations, misinterpretations|反义词: accurate assessments, correct evaluations|noun
