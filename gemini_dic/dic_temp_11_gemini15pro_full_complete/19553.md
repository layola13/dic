bifurcating:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |分叉的，分歧的|Dividing into two branches or forks.|The bifurcating river created two distinct waterways. 这条分叉的河流形成了两条截然不同的水道。|Synonyms: branching, forking, dividing, splitting|Antonyms: converging, uniting, merging|Adjective

bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪt/| v. |分叉，分歧|To divide into two branches or forks.|The road bifurcates at the top of the hill. 道路在山顶分叉。|Synonyms: branch, fork, divide, split|Antonyms: converge, unite, merge|Verb
