graphologies: /ɡræˈfɒlədʒiz/
n.
笔迹学，字迹分析
The study of handwriting, especially for the purpose of analyzing character and personality.
His graphologies revealed a meticulous and introverted personality. 他的笔迹分析揭示了他一丝不苟和内向的性格。
Synonyms: handwriting analysis, graphology
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider  "personality assessment (not based on handwriting)")
