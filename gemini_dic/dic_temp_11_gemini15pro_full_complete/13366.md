atmolyzation: /ˌæt.mə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/
n.
原子化;  雾化
The process of reducing something to atoms or a very fine spray.
The atmolyzation of the liquid created a fine mist.  这种液体的雾化产生了一种细小的薄雾。
