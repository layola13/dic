bipartisanism: /ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzənɪzəm/ | n. | 两党合作；跨党派合作 | Agreement or cooperation between two political parties that usually oppose each other's policies. |  Example 1:  Bipartisanism is essential for effective governance in a divided nation.  两党合作对于分裂国家中的有效治理至关重要。

Example 2: The bill passed with bipartisan support.  该法案在两党的支持下获得通过。 | Synonyms: cooperation, collaboration, unity, agreement, consensus | Antonyms: partisanship, division, discord, disagreement, polarization | noun
