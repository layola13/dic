intersystematically:/ˌɪntərˌsɪstəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 系统间地，跨系统地 |  In a manner that involves or relates to multiple systems, or occurs between different systems. |  Data is shared intersystematically across various departments.  数据在不同部门之间系统地共享。|  The company's network is designed to operate intersystematically with its partners' networks. 该公司的网络旨在与其合作伙伴的网络进行系统间的运作。 | synonym: cross-system, across systems | antonym: intrasystematically, within a system | 词性: 副词
