malkin:/ˈmɔːkɪn/ (US) /ˈmɔːlkɪn/ (UK)| n.|拖把；邋遢的女人；猫（古语）；稻草人|A mop or other cleaning implement; an untidy or dirty woman (often derogatory); a cat (archaic); a scarecrow|She used a malkin to wipe up the spilled milk. 她用拖把擦掉了洒出来的牛奶。|The old woman was described as a malkin by her cruel neighbours.  刻薄的邻居们把这位老妇人描述成一个邋遢的女人。|Synonyms: mop, slut, slattern (derogatory), scarecrow, cat (archaic)|Antonyms: tidy woman, neat person|noun
