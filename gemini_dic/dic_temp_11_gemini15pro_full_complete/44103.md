The crispated edges of the leaves added to the plant's unique appearance. 叶子卷曲的边缘增加了植物的独特外观。|近义词：frizzy, curled, wrinkled, crinkled, kinked|反义词：straight, smooth, flat|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *crispatus, past participle of crispare (“to curl, crisp”), from crispus (“curly, wavy”).
