mammoth:/ˈmæməθ/ (US) /ˈmæmɒθ/ (UK)| n. adj.|猛犸象；巨大的，庞大的|A large extinct elephant of the Pleistocene epoch, typically hairy with a sloping back and long curved tusks.  Also used to describe something huge or enormous.|例句：Scientists have discovered the frozen remains of a mammoth. 科学家们发现了猛犸象的冰冻遗骸。|近义词：enormous, gigantic, colossal, immense|反义词：tiny, small, minuscule|noun
