geometrize:/ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtraɪz/ | v. | 几何化; 用几何图形表示 | To represent or interpret in geometric terms; to give a geometric form or arrangement to. |  The artist geometrized the human form in his paintings.  这位艺术家在他的绘画中将人体几何化了。

The architect geometrized the building's facade with intricate patterns.  建筑师用复杂的图案将建筑物的正面几何化。 | synonyms:  formalize, schematize, systematize | antonyms: disorder, disorganize, randomize | verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *geometrizāre, from Latin geōmetria (“geometry”) + -izāre (suffix forming verbs).  Surface analysis:  geo- +‎ -metrize
