amphoras:/ˈæmfərəz/
n.
双耳陶罐；双耳瓶
A tall ancient Greek or Roman jar with two handles and a narrow neck.
The museum displayed a collection of Greek amphoras.  博物馆展出了一批希腊双耳陶罐。

The archaeologists unearthed several amphoras filled with ancient coins. 考古学家们挖掘出几个装满古代硬币的双耳陶罐。

近义词:jar, vase, urn
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a specific container type)
词性:Noun
