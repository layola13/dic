helenus:/ˈhɛlɪnəs/| n. |海伦纳斯|A Trojan prophet, son of Priam and Hecuba, and brother of Hector. After the Trojan War, he became the slave of Neoptolemus (Pyrrhus) and later married Andromache, Hector's widow.|Helenus prophesied that Aeneas would found a new Troy in Italy.|近义词: seer, soothsayer, prophet |反义词: None|Noun
