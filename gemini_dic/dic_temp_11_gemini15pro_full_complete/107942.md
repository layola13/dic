laryngoscopic:/ˌlærɪŋɡəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 喉镜的；用喉镜检查的 | Relating to or performed using a laryngoscope, an instrument used to examine the larynx (voice box). | The doctor performed a laryngoscopic examination to check for any abnormalities. 医生进行了喉镜检查以查找任何异常。|  The surgery required a laryngoscopic procedure.  手术需要喉镜检查程序。 | 近义词:  None (highly specific) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

laryngoscopy:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskəpi/ | n. | 喉镜检查 | Examination of the larynx using a laryngoscope. | The laryngoscopy revealed inflammation of the vocal cords. 喉镜检查显示声带发炎。|  The patient underwent a laryngoscopy to diagnose the cause of his hoarseness. 病人接受了喉镜检查以诊断他声音嘶哑的原因。 | 近义词:  None (highly specific) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


词源：
From Modern Latin *laryngoscopicus, from larynx + -scopic.  Larynx comes from Greek lárunx (throat). -scopic comes from Greek skopeîn (to look at).
