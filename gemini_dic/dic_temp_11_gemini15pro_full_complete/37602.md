communicating:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 交流；沟通；传达；感染（疾病）|The teacher is communicating with the students. 老师正在和学生们交流。|Synonyms: conveying, imparting, transmitting, interacting|Antonyms: withholding, concealing, isolating|Verb (present participle)

communicating:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 交际的；传达信息的；传染的 |  They have developed a communicating network of spies. 他们发展了一个间谍交流网络。|Synonyms: connective, communicative, contagious|Antonyms: isolated, non-communicative, non-contagious|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *communicaten*, from Latin communicāre (“to share, divide out; communicate, impart, inform; join, unite, connect”), from communis (“common”).
