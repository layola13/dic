chromophotography:/ˌkroʊmoʊfəˈtɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |彩色照相术，彩色摄影|The process of producing photographs in color.  It encompasses various early color photography methods predating modern color film.|Early chromophotography relied on complex processes involving multiple exposures. 早期的彩色照相术依赖于涉及多次曝光的复杂过程。| The museum showcased a fascinating exhibit on 19th-century chromophotography.  博物馆展出了一个关于19世纪彩色照相术的引人入胜的展览。 |synonyms: color photography, colour photography | antonyms: monochrome photography, black and white photography | noun
