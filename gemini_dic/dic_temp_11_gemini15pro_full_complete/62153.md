Something that encloses, such as a fence, wall, or container.
The enclosers kept the animals safe from predators. 围栏保护动物免受捕食者的侵害。
近义词: enclosures, containers, fences, barriers, walls
反义词: openings

词性: 名词 (Plural form of encloser)
词源: From enclose + -er.  Enclose comes from Old French enclos, past participle of enclore, from en- "in" + clore "to close."
