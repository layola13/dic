drumfish:/ˈdrʌmˌfɪʃ/| n. |鼓鱼|Any of various fishes that make a drumming or croaking sound, typically by vibrating their swim bladders.|例句：The drumfish are known for the distinctive sounds they produce. 鼓鱼以其发出的独特声音而闻名。

例句：We caught a large drumfish while fishing in the bay. 我们在海湾钓鱼时捕到了一条大鼓鱼。|近义词：croaker, grunt|反义词:  (No specific antonym for a type of fish)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *drum* + *fish*.  The "drum" part refers to the sound the fish makes.
