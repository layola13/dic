compotator:/kəmˈpɒteɪtər/ | n. | 饮酒作乐者; 酗酒者 | A person who drinks to excess; a heavy drinker. | He was known as a compotator and a carouser. 他被称为酒鬼和寻欢作乐的人。| The old compotator stumbled into the tavern, ready for his nightly dose of whiskey.  这个老酒鬼跌跌撞撞地走进酒馆，准备喝他每晚的威士忌。 | 近义词: drinker, imbiber, carouser, reveler, toper | 反义词: teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *compotator, from Latin compōtō (“I drink together, carouse”), from com- (“together”) + pōtō (“I drink”).
