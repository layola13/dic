babbitting:/ˈbæbɪtɪŋ/
n.
巴氏合金浇注; 巴氏合金轴承; 衬瓦;
The process of lining or repairing bearings with babbitt metal. Babbitt metal is a soft, white, nonferrous alloy (often tin-based) used to reduce friction in bearings.
The engineer oversaw the babbitting of the large engine's bearings. 工程师监督了大型发动机轴承的巴氏合金浇注。
The babbitting needed to be redone as the metal had worn down. 轴承衬瓦需要重做，因为金属已经磨损了。
近义词: bearing lining, white metalling
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bearing replacement" if the entire bearing is changed instead of just the lining)
词性: noun


babbit:/ˈbæbɪt/
n.
巴比特合金
A soft white alloy of tin, antimony, copper, and sometimes lead, used as an antifriction lining for bearings.
The babbitt used in these bearings is a tin-based alloy. 这些轴承中使用的巴比特合金是锡基合金。
They poured the molten babbitt into the bearing housing. 他们将熔化的巴比特合金倒入轴承座中。
近义词: white metal
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a specific harder bearing material like "bronze" or "steel")
词性: noun

词源: Named after Isaac Babbitt (1799-1889), the American inventor who patented the alloy in 1839.
