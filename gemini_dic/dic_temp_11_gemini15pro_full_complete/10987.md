arbor:/ˈɑːrbər/ (US), /ˈɑːbə/ (UK)| n.|凉亭，树荫处，藤架|A shady garden alcove with sides and a roof formed by trees or climbing plants trained over a framework.|例句：We sat in the arbor, enjoying the shade. 我们坐在凉亭里，享受着树荫。

例句：The wedding ceremony was held under a beautiful flower-covered arbor. 婚礼在一个美丽的鲜花覆盖的藤架下举行。

近义词：bower, pergola, gazebo|反义词:|词性:noun
