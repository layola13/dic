exilable:/ˈeksaɪləbl/| adj.|可放逐的，可流放的|Capable of being exiled; liable or subject to exile. |The dissident writer was declared exilable by the government. 这位持不同政见的作家被政府宣布可以被流放。|The former dictator was considered exilable after the coup. 政变后，前独裁者被认为可以被流放。|近义词：banishable, deportable|反义词: welcome, admissible|词性:形容词
词源: From exile + -able.  Ultimately from Latin exsilium "banishment".
