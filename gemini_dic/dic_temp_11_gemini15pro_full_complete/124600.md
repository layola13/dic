mousetail:/ˈmaʊsteɪl/| n. |鼠尾；鼠尾草；|A mousetail is the tail of a mouse.  It can also refer to a type of plant, specifically certain species in the genus Myosurus (mousetail).|The mouse scurried away, its mousetail twitching nervously.  老鼠匆匆逃窜，它的尾巴紧张地抽搐着。|The meadow was dotted with tiny mousetail plants. 草地上点缀着微小的鼠尾草。|近义词：(for the plant) Myosurus|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
