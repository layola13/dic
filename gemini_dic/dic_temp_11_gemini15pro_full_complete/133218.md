nonexcessive:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈsɛsɪv/
adjective
不过分的，适度的
Not exceeding reasonable limits; moderate.
The price was nonexcessive, considering the quality.  考虑到质量，价格并不算过分。
His demands were nonexcessive. 他的要求不过分。
Synonyms: moderate, reasonable, temperate, modest
Antonyms: excessive, immoderate, extreme, exorbitant
词性: 形容词
