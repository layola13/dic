foreshadowed:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊd/ (UK) /fɔːrˈʃædoʊd/ (US)| v. |预示，预兆|to be a sign of something that will happen in the future|The dark clouds foreshadowed a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。|The hero's tragic flaw foreshadowed his downfall. 主人公的悲剧性缺陷预示了他的垮台。| synonyms: presage, portend, augur, forebode| antonyms: conceal, hide, mask |verb (past tense)
