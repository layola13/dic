missionarize:/ˈmɪʃənəˌraɪz/ |v. |使基督教化；进行传教活动；积极劝人改变信仰|To try to persuade people to change their religious or political beliefs.|例句：They missionarized among the native tribes. 他们在土著部落中传教。

例句：The organization missionarized throughout the country. 这个组织在全国各地进行传教活动。|近义词：proselytize, evangelize|反义词：secularize|词性：动词
