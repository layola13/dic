nonmanagements:/ˌnɑːnˈmænɪdʒmənts/
|n.|非管理人员；非管理层；非管理部门|Those in nonmanagements positions often feel unheard.  那些非管理岗位的人员经常感到自己的意见被忽视。|The company is restructuring, impacting both management and nonmanagements. 公司正在重组，这将影响管理层和非管理层。|近义词: non-executive staff, rank and file, employees|反义词: management, executives|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "management" (from "manage," from Italian "maneggiare" - to handle, derived from Latin "manus" - hand).  "Nonmanagements" is a plural noun formed by adding "-s" to "nonmanagement," which itself is formed by combining the prefix "non-" with the noun "management."
