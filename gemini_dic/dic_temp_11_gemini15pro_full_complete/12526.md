asphyxiation:/əsˌfɪksiˈeɪʃən/| n.|窒息|The condition of being deprived of oxygen, which can lead to unconsciousness or death.|The victim died of asphyxiation due to smoke inhalation. 受害者因吸入烟雾窒息而死。|Near synonyms: suffocation, choking, smothering|Near antonyms: respiration, breathing|noun

asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksieɪt/| v.|使窒息；窒息而死|To cause someone to become unconscious or die due to lack of oxygen.|The fire asphyxiated several residents of the building. 大火使大楼内的几名居民窒息而死。|Near synonyms: suffocate, choke, smother|Near antonyms: resuscitate, revive|verb
