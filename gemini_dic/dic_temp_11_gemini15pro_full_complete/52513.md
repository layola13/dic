differentiatedness:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 区别性，差异性，分化性| The state or quality of being differentiated; the degree to which something is distinct or separate from other things.  | The differentiatedness of the product line allows the company to cater to a wider range of customers. 该产品线的差异性使公司能够迎合更广泛的客户。 | Synonyms: distinction,  dissimilarity,  diversity,  variance | Antonyms: similarity, uniformity, homogeneity | noun
