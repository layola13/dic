meadowy:/ˈmedəʊi/ (UK) /ˈmedəʊwi/ (US)| adj.|草地般的，像草地的|Characteristic of or resembling a meadow; abounding in meadows.|The air was sweet with the scent of wildflowers in the meadowy valley. 空气中弥漫着草地上野花的芬芳。|近义词：grassy, verdant, lush|反义词：barren, arid, desert|词性:形容词

词源: From meadow + -y.  "Meadow" comes from Old English *mǣdwe (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mēdwō, from Proto-Indo-European *mēdʰw-.
