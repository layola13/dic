literality:/ˌlɪtəˈrælɪti/
n.|字面性，字面意义|The state of being literal; adherence to the exact meaning of words or text without interpretation or embellishment.|He interpreted the poem with a strict literality, ignoring any metaphorical meanings. 他对这首诗进行了严格的字面解释，忽略了任何隐喻意义。|literalness, exactness, precision|figurativeness, symbolism|noun
