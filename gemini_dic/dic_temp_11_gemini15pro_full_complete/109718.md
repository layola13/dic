leveler:/ˈlɛvələr/ (US), /ˈlɛvlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|水平仪,矫正器;使平等的人(或物); 平均主义者|A tool or person that makes things level or equal.|The government introduced policies aimed at being a social leveler. 政府推出了旨在成为社会平衡器的政策。|近义词：equalizer, grader, smoother|反义词: aggravator, exacerbator|noun

leveler:/ˈlɛvələr/ (US), /ˈlɛvlə(r)/ (UK)| v.|使平; 夷平; 使平等; 瞄准; 对准|To make something level or equal.|The company leveled the playing field by offering the same benefits to all employees. 公司通过向所有员工提供相同的福利来创造公平的竞争环境。|近义词：equalize, flatten, even|反义词:  uneven, tilt, incline |verb
