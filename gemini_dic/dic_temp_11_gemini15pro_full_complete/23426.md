brackishnesses:/ˈbrækɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
咸度；微咸；半咸水
the state or quality of being slightly salty, as is the mixture of river water and seawater in estuaries.
The brackishnesses of the marsh made it unsuitable for drinking water.  沼泽的咸度使其不适合饮用。
近义词:saltiness, salinity
反义词:freshness
