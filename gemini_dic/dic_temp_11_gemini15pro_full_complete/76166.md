fusilli:/fuːˈsɪli/ | n. | 螺旋面，螺旋粉 | A type of pasta shaped like a short, thick spiral. |  I made a fusilli salad for lunch. 我午饭做了螺旋粉沙拉。

|  Fusilli is a popular pasta shape for holding sauces. 螺旋粉是一种很适合沾酱汁的意面形状。 | 近义词：rotini, spirali | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a pasta shape) | 词性: noun
