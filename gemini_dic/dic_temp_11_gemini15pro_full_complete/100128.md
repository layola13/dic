interprismatic:/ˌɪntərprɪzˈmætɪk/
adjective
棱柱间的；位于棱柱之间的
Situated between prisms.  Specifically referring to the space or substance between the prisms of a crystal.
The interprismatic faces of the quartz crystal were coated with a thin layer of iron oxide. 石英晶体的棱柱间面覆盖着一层薄薄的氧化铁。
The interprismatic cement in the rock sample was analyzed.  岩石样本中的棱柱间胶结物进行了分析。
近义词:intercristal
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective
词源:inter- (between) + prismatic (relating to a prism)
