inquiry:/ˈɪŋkwəri/ (US), /ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ (UK)| n.|询问，查询，调查|An act of asking for information.|例句：I made an inquiry about the price of the tickets. 我询问了票价。|例句：The police launched an inquiry into the robbery. 警方对这起抢劫案展开了调查。|近义词：question, query, investigation, research|反义词:answer, reply, solution|noun


inquiry:/ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v.|询问，查询|To ask for information.|例句：I inquired about the train schedule. 我询问了火车时刻表。 |例句：He inquired after her health. 他问候了她的健康状况。|近义词：ask, question, query|反义词:answer, reply|verb
