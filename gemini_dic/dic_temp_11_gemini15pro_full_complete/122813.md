moldy:/ˈmoʊldi/ (US) /ˈməʊldi/ (UK)| adj.|发霉的；陈腐的|Covered with or smelling of mold; stale or musty.|The bread was moldy and had to be thrown away. 面包发霉了，不得不扔掉。|近义词: musty, mildewy, stale, fusty|反义词: fresh, new|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldy*, from Old English *moldig*, from *mold* (“mold”) + *-ig* (adjective suffix). Cognate with German *moldig*.
