intransmutable:/ˌɪntrænzˈmjuːtəbl/
adjective
不可改变的；不能嬗变的
not capable of being transmuted or changed; unchangeable; immutable.
The laws of physics are considered intransmutable. 物理定律被认为是不可改变的。
Her love for her children was intransmutable. 她对孩子的爱是永恒不变的。
近义词: immutable, unchangeable, fixed, permanent, unalterable
反义词: mutable, changeable, variable, alterable, fluid
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + transmutable (capable of being changed).  Ultimately from Latin trans- (across, beyond) + mutare (to change).
