leafless:/ˈliːflɪs/| adj.|无叶的，光秃秃的|Without leaves; bare.|The trees stood leafless against the winter sky. 冬天光秃秃的树木映衬着天空。|近义词：bare, denuded, defoliated|反义词：leafy, verdant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From leaf + -less (suffix meaning "without").  The word "leaf" comes from Old English *lēaf, of Germanic origin.
