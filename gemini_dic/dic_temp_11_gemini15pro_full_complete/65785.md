eavesdrop:/ˈiːvzdrɒp/ (UK), /ˈiːvzdrɑːp/ (US)| v. | 偷听，窃听 | To secretly listen to a conversation | Example: She was eavesdropping on their conversation from behind the door. 她躲在门后偷听他们的谈话。

Example:  He was caught eavesdropping on his neighbors. 他被抓到偷听邻居的谈话。

| 近义词: listen in, overhear, snoop | 反义词: ignore, disregard |词性: 动词
