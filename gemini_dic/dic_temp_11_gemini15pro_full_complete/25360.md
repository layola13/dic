builtins:/ˈbɪltɪnz/| n.|内置函数/组件/模块|Built-in functions are functions that are readily available in a programming language without the need for importing any external modules or libraries.  They are essential parts of the language itself.|Example:  Python's `builtins` module provides functions like `print()`, `len()`, and `type()`. Python的`builtins`模块提供了像`print()`、`len()`和`type()`这样的函数。|近义词:integrated, inherent, intrinsic|反义词:external, add-on|noun
