lampoonry:/læmˈpuːnri/
n.
讽刺；嘲讽文章；讽刺诗
A harsh satire, often in verse, directed against an individual or institution.
The magazine was known for its merciless lampoonry of political figures. 这本杂志以其对政治人物无情的讽刺而闻名。
synonyms: satire, mockery, ridicule, caricature, parody
antonyms: praise, commendation, eulogy
noun
