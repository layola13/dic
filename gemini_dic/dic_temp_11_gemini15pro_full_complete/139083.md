ob:/ɒb/ | prefix | 反对，逆，朝向，在...之上，覆盖 |  A prefix occurring in loanwords from Latin, where it meant “toward,” “against,” “in the way,” “over,”  "completely," or "about," and often expressing opposition, concealment, or reversal. |  例句：object (反对); obstruct (阻碍) | 近义词:  (As a prefix, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but words formed with it have synonyms based on the specific meaning) | 反义词: (As a prefix, it doesn't have direct antonyms) | 词性: prefix

注意事项:
The prefix "ob-" often changes to "oc-" before "c," "f," and "p" (e.g., occur, offer, oppose), to "op-" before "p" (e.g., oppose), and to "os-" before "s" (e.g., ostensible).  前缀“ob-”在“c”、“f”和“p”之前经常变为“oc-”（例如，occur、offer、oppose），在“p”之前变为“op-”（例如，oppose），在“s”之前变为“os-”（例如，ostensible）。
