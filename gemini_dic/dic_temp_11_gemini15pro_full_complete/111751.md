lodde:/ˈlɒdɪ/ (British English)| n.|毛鳞鱼|A small fish (Mallotus villosus) found in Arctic waters, used as bait and for producing oil.|Fishermen use lodde as bait for cod. 渔民用毛鳞鱼作鳕鱼的诱饵。|近义词：caplan, sandlance|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Norwegian lodde, of uncertain origin.
