custom-built:/ˈkʌstəm bɪlt/| adj.|定制的，订做的|Designed and built to a particular customer's specifications.  Made to order rather than mass-produced.|We had a custom-built bookcase installed in the living room. 我们在客厅安装了一个定制的书柜。|bespoke, tailor-made, made-to-order|off-the-shelf, mass-produced|adjective
