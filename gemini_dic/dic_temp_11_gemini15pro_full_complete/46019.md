cybersecurity:/ˈsaɪbərˌsɪˈkjʊrəti/| n.|网络安全|The protection of computer systems and networks from information disclosure, theft of or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data, as well as from disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.|例句：Cybersecurity is a growing concern for businesses of all sizes.  网络安全是所有规模企业日益关注的问题。

例句：Investing in cybersecurity is crucial for protecting sensitive data.  投资网络安全对于保护敏感数据至关重要。|近义词：information security, computer security, IT security|反义词:cybercrime, cyberattack|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
cyber- + security

"cyber-"  is a combining form representing "cybernetics"  and by extension, computers, computer networks, and virtual reality.

"security" comes from  Old French "securite" and ultimately from Latin "securitas" meaning "freedom from care, safety."
