deafens:/ˈdɛfənz/ | v. | 使聋，震耳欲聋 | Makes someone temporarily or permanently unable to hear, or overwhelms with noise. | The explosion deafened him for several minutes. 爆炸声让他几分钟内什么也听不见。 | synonyms: stuns, muffles, drowns out | antonyms: clarifies, amplifies | verb

deafens:/ˈdɛfənz/ | v. | 使充耳不闻，使置若罔闻 | Makes someone unable or unwilling to hear or pay attention to something. | The constant complaints deafened him to their real problems.  不断的抱怨让他对他们的真正问题充耳不闻。 | synonyms:  ignores, disregards, overlooks | antonyms: heeds, listens to | verb
