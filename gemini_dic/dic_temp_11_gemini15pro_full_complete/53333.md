disacquaint:/ˌdɪsəˈkweɪnt/
verb
使不熟悉，使疏远
To make unfamiliar or unacquainted; to cause to lose familiarity with someone or something.
He became disacquainted with his former friends after moving to a new city.  搬到新城市后，他与以前的朋友变得不熟悉了。
The long illness disacquainted him with his work.  长期的疾病使他对工作生疏了。
近义词: estrange, alienate
反义词: familiarize, acquaint
词性: verb
