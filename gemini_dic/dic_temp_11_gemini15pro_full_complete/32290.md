chiefships:/ˈtʃiːfʃɪps/
n.
首领地位；酋长职位
the position or rank of a chief
The family held the hereditary chiefship of the clan.  这个家族世袭了宗族的首领地位。

近义词:leadership, headship, chieftaincy
反义词:subordination, subservience
词性:名词
