evolutionarily: /ˌɛvəˈluːʃəˈnɛrəli/ | adv. |进化地，从进化的角度| In terms of evolution; from an evolutionary perspective. | The human brain has evolutionarily developed to be highly adaptable. 人类的大脑进化发展出了高度的适应性。| Bats and dolphins have evolutionarily distinct origins.  蝙蝠和海豚有着进化上的不同起源。 | 近义词: phylogenetically, developmentally | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 副词

词源: From evolutionary + -ly.  "Evolutionary" comes from "evolution," which traces back to the Latin "evolutio," meaning "unrolling."
