inhumanly:/ɪnˈhjuːmənli/
adverb
非人地；残酷地；无情地
In a way that is not human; cruelly or without compassion.
He worked inhumanly long hours.  他非人地长时间工作。
The prisoners were inhumanly treated. 囚犯们受到了非人的待遇。
Synonyms:cruelly, barbarously, savagely, brutally, viciously, heartlessly, callously, mercilessly, ruthlessly, pitilessly, remorselessly, unfeelingly
Antonyms:humanely, kindly, compassionately
词性:副词
