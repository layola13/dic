brazen:/ˈbreɪzən/| adj.| 厚颜无耻的，肆无忌惮的；黄铜制的|  shameless or impudent; made of brass |  He exhibited a brazen disregard for the rules. 他公然蔑视规则。 |  bold, audacious, impudent, shameless, insolent, defiant |  shy, timid, modest, humble, reserved | adjective
brazens:/ˈbreɪzənz/| v. (third-person singular simple present brazens, present participle brazening, simple past and past participle brazened)| 使变得厚颜无耻；给…镀黄铜| to make brazen or bold; to cover with brass | She brazened it out, pretending she hadn't heard the criticism. 她厚着脸皮装作没听到批评。|  embolden, nerve, steel, fortify |  discourage, deter, cow | verb
