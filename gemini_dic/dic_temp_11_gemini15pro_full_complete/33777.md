circumambage:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbɪdʒ/ | n. | 迂回，绕圈子 |The act of going around something in a roundabout way, rather than taking a direct route; an indirect and wordy way of expressing something. | He told the story without circumlocution or circummambage. 他讲述这个故事没有拐弯抹角。| 近义词：circumlocution, periphrasis, indirectness | 反义词：directness, straightforwardness | noun

circumambulate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbjʊleɪt/ | v. | 绕行，环行 | To walk around something, especially a sacred object or place, as part of a ritual or ceremony. | Pilgrims circumambulate the temple. 朝圣者绕着寺庙走。| 近义词：circle, orbit, revolve | 反义词：N/A | verb
