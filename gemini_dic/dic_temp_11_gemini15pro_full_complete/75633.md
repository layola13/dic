fugitivity:/ˌfjuːdʒɪˈtɪvɪti/
n.
逃亡，逃亡状态；短暂，易逝
The state or quality of being a fugitive; the act of fleeing from captivity or danger.  Also, the quality of being fleeting or ephemeral.
The fugitivity of the moment made it all the more precious.  这一刻的短暂让它更加珍贵。
Synonyms: flight, escape, evanescence, transience
Antonyms: permanence, stability, durability
词性: 名词
