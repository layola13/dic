expeditiously:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəsli/
adverb
迅速地；敏捷地
With speed and efficiency.
The matter was handled expeditiously. 这件事处理得很迅速。
Synonyms: swiftly, promptly, quickly, efficiently
Antonyms: slowly, sluggishly, inefficiently
Part of Speech: adverb
