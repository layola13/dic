n.
易变性；不稳定性
The quality or state of being fluxile; instability.
The fluxility of the stock market makes it a risky investment. 股票市场的易变性使其成为一项风险投资。

The political situation is marked by great fluxility. 政局很不稳定。

instability, volatility, mutability, fluidity
stability, constancy, permanence
noun
