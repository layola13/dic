defensive:/dɪˈfɛnsɪv/ /dɪˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|防御的，防守的，保护的；戒备的，易怒的|Protecting or defending against attack; used or intended to defend or protect.|例句：The team adopted a defensive strategy.  该队采取了防守策略。|近义词：protective, guarded, shielding|反义词:aggressive, offensive|词性:形容词
defensive:/dɪˈfɛnsɪv/ /dɪˈfɛnsɪv/| n.|守势，防御|The position or attitude of someone who is protecting themselves from criticism or attack.|例句：He was immediately on the defensive when I questioned his motives. 当我质疑他的动机时，他立即采取了守势。|近义词：protection, guard|反义词:offense, attack|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *defensif, *defensyf, from Old French defensif, from Latin dēfēnsīvus (“serving to ward off”), from dēfēnsus, past participle of dēfendere (“to ward off, defend”).
