lightened: /ˈlaɪtənd/ | adj. |  (颜色或重量)变轻的，轻松的| made lighter in color or weight, or less serious | She wore a lightened version of the dress. 她穿了一件颜色更浅的裙子。| synonyms: paler, brighter, less burdened | antonyms: darker, heavier, more burdened | adjective
