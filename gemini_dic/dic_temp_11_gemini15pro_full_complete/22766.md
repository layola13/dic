borscht:/ˈbɔːrʃt/ (US), /ˈbɔːʃt/ (UK)| n. |罗宋汤|A beet soup of Ukrainian origin, commonly made with meat, cabbage, and other vegetables.|例句：My grandmother makes the best borscht. 我祖母做的罗宋汤最好吃。

例句：We had a hearty bowl of borscht for dinner. 我们晚饭喝了一碗丰盛的罗宋汤。|近义词：beet soup |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific food)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ukrainian борщ (boršč), from earlier бръщь (brščʹ), from Proto-Slavic *bъrščь, meaning "hogweed," a plant whose leaves were originally used in the soup. Over time, beets replaced hogweed as the main ingredient, but the name remained.
