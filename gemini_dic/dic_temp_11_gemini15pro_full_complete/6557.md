amyous: /eɪˈmaɪəs/
adj.
lacking muscle; weak
缺乏肌肉的；虚弱的
The amyous patient struggled to lift a glass of water.  这位肌肉萎缩的病人挣扎着举起一杯水。
The illness left him amyous and bedridden.  这场病使他肌肉无力，卧床不起。
近义词: weak, feeble, debilitated, atrophied
反义词: strong, muscular, robust
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *a- "without" + mys "muscle."  来自希腊语 *a- “没有” + mys “肌肉”。
