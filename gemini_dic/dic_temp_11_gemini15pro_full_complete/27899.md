cantata:/kænˈtɑːtə/| n.|康塔塔；清唱剧|A vocal composition, in several movements, for solo voices, chorus, and instrumental accompaniment, often based on a religious or dramatic text.|Bach's cantatas are masterpieces of the Baroque period. 巴赫的康塔塔是巴洛克时期的杰作。|The choir performed a beautiful cantata for Easter.  唱诗班为复活节表演了一首优美的康塔塔。|近义词：oratorio, choral work|反义词:|n.
