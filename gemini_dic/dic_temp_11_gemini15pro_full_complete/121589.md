misoccupying: /ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 占用错误；误用；滥用 |  The company was accused of misoccupying public land for its own purposes.  该公司被指控滥用公共土地以谋私利。 |  misusing, misapplying | correct use, proper use | verb
misoccupied: /ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪd/ | adj. | 被误用的；被滥用的 | The misoccupied building was eventually demolished. 这座被误用的建筑最终被拆除了。 | misused, misapplied | correctly used, properly used | adjective

Etymology:
"mis-" (Old English "mis-," from Proto-Germanic "*missa-," meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss") + "occupy" (from Old French "occuper," from Latin "occupare," meaning "to seize, take possession of").
