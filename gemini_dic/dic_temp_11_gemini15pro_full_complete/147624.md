overplot:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːt/ | v. n. | 过度绘制;  覆盖绘制; 过于复杂的情节 | To add a plot or subplot to an existing story, sometimes to the point of excessive complexity; an excessively complex or contrived plot. | The movie was overplotted and difficult to follow.  这部电影的情节过于复杂，难以理解。|  The author overplotted the novel with too many subplots.  作者在小说中加入了过多的支线情节，使之过于复杂。 | synonyms: overcrowd, overburden, complicate | antonyms: simplify, streamline | verb, noun
