nonliterarily:/ˌnɒnlɪtəˈrɛrɪli/ /ˌnɑːnlɪtəˈrɛrɪli/
adverb|非文学地，口语地|In a way that is not literary; informally.  Not according to the rules of literary style or expression.|He spoke nonliterarily, using slang and colloquialisms. 他说话非正式，用了俚语和口语。|Near Synonyms: informally, colloquially, conversationally, popularly|Antonyms: formally, literally, academically, scholarly
Etymology:  From non- + literary + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "literary" refers to literature and its conventions, and "-ly" forms adverbs.
