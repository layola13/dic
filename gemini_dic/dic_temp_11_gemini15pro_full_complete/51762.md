devotee:/ˌdevəˈtiː/ /ˌdɛvəˈtiː/
|n.|信徒，爱好者，热心者
|A devotee is a person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.|
|She is a devotee of classical music. 她是一位古典音乐爱好者。|

|近义词：fan, enthusiast, admirer, follower|
|反义词：critic, detractor, opponent|
|词性: noun|


devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/
|vt.|致力于，奉献|
|To devote means to give all or most of one's time, energy, or resources to a particular activity or cause.|
|He devoted his life to helping the poor. 他一生致力于帮助穷人。|

|近义词：dedicate, commit, allocate|
|反义词：neglect, ignore, forsake|
|词性: verb|
