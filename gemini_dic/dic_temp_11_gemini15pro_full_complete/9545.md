antiseparatist:/ˌæntiˈsɛpərətɪst/ | adj. n. | 反分裂主义的，反分裂主义者 | Opposed to separatism; a person who opposes separatism. |  The government implemented antiseparatist policies. 政府实施了反分裂政策。 | The antiseparatist movement gained momentum. 反分裂运动势头强劲。 | 近义词:unionist, centralist | 反义词:separatist, secessionist | 词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
separatist (noun/adjective): advocating or practicing separation from a larger group (from Late Latin separatus, past participle of separare "to separate")
