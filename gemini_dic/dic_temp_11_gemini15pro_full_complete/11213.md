architectures:/ˈɑːrkɪtɛktʃərz/
noun
建筑学; 建筑风格; 架构
The art or practice of designing and constructing buildings.  The complex or carefully designed structure of something.  The conceptual structure and logical organization of a computer or computer-based communication system.
The architecture of the new museum is stunning. 新博物馆的建筑风格令人惊叹。
The software has a modular architecture.  该软件采用模块化架构。
synonyms: structure, design, framework, construction, organization
antonyms: disorganization, chaos
词性: 名词(plural)
