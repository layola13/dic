motey:/ˈmoʊti/| adj.|多尘的，有尘埃的|Containing motes; dusty.  Full of small particles of dust or other matter.|The attic was motey and smelled of old wood. 阁楼里满是灰尘，散发着旧木头的味道。|近义词：dusty, dusty,  powdery|反义词：clean, spotless|词性:形容词

词源: From mote + -y.  From Middle English *motey, from mote + -y.
