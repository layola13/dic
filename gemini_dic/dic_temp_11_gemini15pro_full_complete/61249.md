emanators:/ˈɛməˌneɪtərz/
noun
散发器；发射器
A person or thing that emanates something.  Something that emits something, such as light, heat, or gas.
The sun is a powerful emanator of heat and light.  太阳是热和光的强大散发器。
These crystals are believed to be emanators of positive energy.  这些晶体被认为是正能量的散发器。
emitters, radiators, transmitters
absorbers, receivers
n.
