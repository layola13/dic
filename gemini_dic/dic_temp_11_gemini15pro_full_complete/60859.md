eleemosynary: /ˌɛlɪˈmɒsɪnəri/ /ˌɛliːˈmɑːsɪneri/ | adj. | 慈善的；施舍的；靠救济维持的 | Relating to or dependent on charity; charitable. |  The hospital was founded by an eleemosynary grant from a wealthy philanthropist. 这家医院是由一位富有的慈善家的慈善捐赠建立的。

The eleemosynary institution provided food and shelter for the homeless.  这家慈善机构为无家可归者提供食物和住所。| 近义词：charitable, philanthropic, benevolent | 反义词：selfish, greedy, stingy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin  *eleemosynarius, from Latin eleemosyna ‘alms’ (from Greek eleēmosunē ).
