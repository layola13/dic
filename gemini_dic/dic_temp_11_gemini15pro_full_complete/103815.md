jowliness:/ˈdʒaʊlinəs/| n.|丰满的下巴，双下巴|The state of having full or fleshy jowls.  This can be a sign of aging or weight gain.|His jowliness became more pronounced as he got older. 他的双下巴随着年龄的增长而变得更加明显。|synonyms: plumpness, chubbiness, fullness, fleshiness|antonyms: thinness, slenderness, leanness|noun
