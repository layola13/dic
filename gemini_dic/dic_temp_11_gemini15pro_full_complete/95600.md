incommoded:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdɪd/ |adj.| 不方便的，感到不便的 |  Put in a difficult or inconvenient position, especially by a restriction on space or time. | The cramped seating arrangements incommoded the tall passengers.  高大的乘客因为拥挤的座位安排而感到不便。|inconvenienced, disturbed, bothered | comfortable, convenient | adjective


incommode:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊd/ |v.|  打扰，妨碍，使不便 | To cause inconvenience or difficulty to. | The construction noise incommoded the residents of the neighborhood. 施工噪音给附近的居民带来了不便。| disturb, bother, inconvenience | assist, help | verb
