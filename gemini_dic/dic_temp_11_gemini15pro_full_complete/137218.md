nonsuppressive:/ˌnɑːnsəˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非抑制性的 | Not suppressing or tending to suppress; specifically : not tending to suppress the immune response. | The doctor prescribed nonsuppressive medication for her allergies. 医生给她开了非抑制性过敏药物。 |  The patient was given nonsuppressive therapy. 病人接受了非抑制性疗法。 | 近义词: permissive, tolerant | 反义词: suppressive, inhibitory | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: non- + suppressive. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "suppressive" comes from the verb "suppress," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "suppressus," the past participle of "supprimere," meaning "to press down" or "to restrain."
