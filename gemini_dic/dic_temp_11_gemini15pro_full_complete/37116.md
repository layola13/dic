combustibleness:/kəmˈbʌstɪb(ə)lnəs/
n.
可燃性，易燃性
The quality of being combustible; flammability.
The combustibleness of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干燥树叶的可燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。
flammability, inflammability
incombustibility, nonflammability
noun
