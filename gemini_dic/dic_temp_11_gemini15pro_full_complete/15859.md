balletic:/bæˈlɛtɪk/
adjective
芭蕾舞的；像芭蕾舞的
Resembling or characteristic of ballet.
Her movements were balletic, graceful and fluid. 她的动作像芭蕾舞一样，优雅流畅。
The dancer's balletic pose was captured perfectly in the photograph.  照片完美地捕捉到了舞者芭蕾舞般的姿势。
近义词: graceful, elegant, fluid, dancelike
反义词: clumsy, awkward, ungainly
词性: adjective

balletic:/bæˈlɛtɪk/
noun
芭蕾舞艺术; 芭蕾舞表演
The art or performance of ballet. (Less common usage)
