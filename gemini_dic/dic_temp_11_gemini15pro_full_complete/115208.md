manifested:/ˈmænɪfɛstɪd/
verb|显现，表明，证明|to show something clearly, through signs or actions|His illness first manifested itself in severe headaches.|近义词：displayed, revealed, demonstrated, exhibited, shown, expressed|反义词：concealed, hidden, masked|词性:v.
