high-end:/ˌhaɪ ˈend/ | adj. | 高端的，高级的 | Relating to or denoting the most expensive or sophisticated products in a particular range. |  She drives a high-end sports car. 她开着一辆高端跑车。

They stayed at a high-end hotel in Paris. 他们在巴黎住在一个高端酒店。


近义词:luxury, upscale, premium, top-of-the-line, exclusive | 反义词:low-end, budget, cheap, inexpensive | 词性:形容词


词源:
Compound formed from "high" and "end". "High" refers to a superior level or position, and "end" in this context implies the top or most desirable part of a scale or range.
