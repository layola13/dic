hazier:/ˈheɪzɪər (US)/ /ˈheɪziər (UK)/
adjective
朦胧的，模糊的
More hazy; less clear. Covered with or filled with haze.
The mountains in the distance were hazier than usual due to the wildfire smoke. 远处的山脉由于野火烟雾比平时更加朦胧。
The photograph is old and the image is rather hazy. 这张照片很旧，图像相当模糊。
近义词: misty, foggy, cloudy, obscure, vague
反义词: clear, bright, distinct, sharp
