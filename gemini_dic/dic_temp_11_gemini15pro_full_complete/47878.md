decelerators:/diːˈsɛləˌreɪtərz/
|n.|减速器；减速装置|A device used to slow down the motion of a vehicle or machine.|The car's decelerators helped it to stop safely on the icy road. 汽车的减速器帮助它在结冰的道路上安全停车。|Synonyms: brakes, retarders|Antonyms: accelerators, boosters
|noun|
