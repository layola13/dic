over-processes:/ˈoʊvərˈprɑːˌsesɪz/ |v. |过度处理|To subject something to excessive or unnecessary processing, often resulting in a less desirable outcome.|The photographer over-processed the image, making it look artificial.|近义词: overwork, overdo|反义词: underprocess, simplify|词性:动词


over-processed:/ˈoʊvərˈprɑːˌsɛst/ |adj. |过度处理的|Having been subjected to excessive or unnecessary processing.|The over-processed food lost much of its nutritional value.|近义词: overdone, overworked|反义词: natural, simple|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "process" (verb meaning "to treat or prepare by a particular process")
