examiningly:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnɪŋli/
adv.
审查地，检查地，审视地
in a way that shows you are looking at something or someone very carefully
He looked at her examiningly. 他审视地望着她。
近义词: scrutinizingly,  carefully, critically,  intently, searchingly
反义词: cursorily,  superficially,  inattentively
词性: adverb
