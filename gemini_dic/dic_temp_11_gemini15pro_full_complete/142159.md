oriyas:/ɔːˈriːəz/ | n. | 奥里亚人（复数） |  People from Odisha, a state in eastern India.  Formerly also referred to the language they speak (now called Odia). |  Many Oriyas migrated to other parts of India for work. 许多奥里亚人为了工作迁移到印度其他地区。 | Oriyas celebrate many unique festivals. 奥里亚人庆祝许多独特的节日。 |  Odias, people from Odisha |  (No clear antonym for a group of people) | Noun
