out-dodging:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒɪŋ/
verb
躲避，巧妙地避开
skillfully avoiding something, especially danger or an unpleasant situation
The fox was adept at out-dodging the hounds.  狐狸善于躲避猎犬。
近义词: evading, eluding, circumventing
反义词: confronting, facing
