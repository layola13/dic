arrears:/əˈrɪrz/ /əˈriərz/| n.|欠款，拖欠，积欠|Money that is owed and should have been paid earlier.  The state of being behind in the fulfillment of obligations.|例句：The company is in arrears with its rent.  这家公司拖欠租金。

例句：He is several months in arrears on his mortgage payments.  他的抵押贷款已拖欠数月。

近义词：debt, dues, unpaid balance, outstanding balance, default|反义词:prepayment, advance payment|词性:名词
