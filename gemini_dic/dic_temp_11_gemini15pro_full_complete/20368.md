bistipulate:/baɪˈstɪpjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 双托叶的 | Having two stipules. Stipules are small, leaf-like appendages typically found at the base of a leaf stalk. |  The bistipulate leaves of the plant were a key identifying feature.  这种植物的双托叶叶子是关键的识别特征。 | Near Synonyms: stipulate | Antonyms: exstipulate | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *bi- (two) + stipula (straw, stalk) + -ate (suffix forming adjectives).  The term "stipulate" refers to the presence of stipules, and "bi-" indicates two.
