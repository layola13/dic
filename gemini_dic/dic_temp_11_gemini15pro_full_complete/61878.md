emprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/| n. vt.|印记，铭刻；留下印记|An imprint is a mark made by pressing something onto a softer substance.  To imprint something means to fix it firmly in someone's mind.|The dog's paws left imprints in the wet cement. 狗的爪子在湿水泥上留下了印记。|The experience was deeply imprinted on her memory. 这段经历深深地印在了她的记忆里。|近义词：impression, mark, stamp|反义词:erase|词性:名词, 动词
