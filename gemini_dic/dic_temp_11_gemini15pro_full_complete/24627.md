brokenness:/ˈbroʊkənnəs/| n.|破碎，破损；（精神上的）崩溃，绝望|The state of being broken, either physically or emotionally.  It can refer to a physical object, a spirit, or a system.|The earthquake caused widespread brokenness and devastation. 地震造成了大范围的破坏和毁灭。  Her voice trembled with brokenness as she recounted the traumatic experience.  当她讲述那段痛苦的经历时，她的声音因绝望而颤抖。|近义词：fragility, vulnerability, ruin, devastation, despair, hopelessness|反义词：wholeness, strength, resilience, hope, completeness|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *brok(e)nesse, equivalent to broke +‎ -ness.  Broke is the past participle of break.
