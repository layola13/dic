chorography: /kəˈrɒɡrəfi/ /kɔːˈrɑːɡrəfi/
noun
地方志，区域描写
The systematic description of a particular region, country, or province, especially its physical features, climate, and inhabitants.
His book is a fascinating chorography of the English Lake District, detailing its history, geology and culture.  他的书是对英国湖区的引人入胜的地方志，详细描述了它的历史、地质和文化。
