inequitable:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbəl/
adjective
不公平的，不公正的
not fair; unjust
例句：The inequitable distribution of wealth is a major problem in many countries. 财富分配不公是许多国家的主要问题。

例句：The current healthcare system is inequitable and needs to be reformed.  目前的医疗保健体系不公平，需要改革。

近义词：unfair, unjust, biased, discriminatory, prejudiced
反义词：fair, just, equitable, impartial, unbiased
词性: adjective
