disqualified:/dɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪd/ | adj. |不合格的，被取消资格的|  Declared ineligible or unsuitable for something, typically due to a violation of rules or regulations or a lack of necessary qualities.  | The athlete was disqualified for using performance-enhancing drugs.  这位运动员因使用提高成绩的药物而被取消资格。| Synonyms: ineligible, unfit, unsuitable, debarred, ruled out | Antonyms: qualified, eligible, suitable, fit | adjective

disqualify:/dɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ | vt. |取消…的资格，使…不合格| To deprive someone of the right or privilege to participate in an activity or to hold a position, usually because they have broken a rule or are not suitable. | His criminal record disqualifies him from holding public office. 他的犯罪记录使他无法担任公职。| Synonyms: bar, preclude, prohibit, debar, rule out | Antonyms: qualify, enable, permit, allow | verb
