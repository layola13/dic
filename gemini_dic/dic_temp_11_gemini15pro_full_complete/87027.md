hepatics:/hɪˈpætɪks/
n.
苔类植物；肝脏药
liverworts; medicines for the liver
The hepatics thrive in damp, shady environments. 苔类植物在潮湿阴暗的环境中生长茂盛。
Hepatics are used in some traditional medicines to treat liver ailments.  一些传统药物使用苔类植物治疗肝病。
synonyms: liverworts, bryophytes
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as these are a specific type of plant)
