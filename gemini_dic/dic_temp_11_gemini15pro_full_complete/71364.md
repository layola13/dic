flagellant:/ˈflædʒələnt/| n. adj.|鞭笞者；苦行者；自虐者|A person who whips themselves, especially as a religious discipline.  Also used to describe something that involves self-punishment.|The monk was a flagellant, believing physical pain brought him closer to God.  这位僧侣是一个鞭笞者，相信肉体上的痛苦能让他更接近上帝。|synonyms: ascetic, self-punisher, masochist|antonyms: hedonist, sensualist|noun

flagellant:/ˈflædʒələnt/| adj.|鞭打的；苦行的；自虐的|Relating to flagellation or a flagellant.  Describing something that involves self-punishment.|He took a flagellant approach to his training, pushing himself to the point of exhaustion. 他采取了一种自虐式的训练方法，把自己逼到筋疲力尽的地步。|synonyms: ascetic, self-punishing, masochistic|antonyms: hedonistic, sensualistic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flagellantem (nominative flagellans), present participle of flagellare "to whip," from Latin flagellum "whip."
