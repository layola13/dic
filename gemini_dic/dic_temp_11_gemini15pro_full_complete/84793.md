handsomely:/ˈhændsəmli/| adv.|慷慨地，大量地；漂亮地，出色地|In a generous or liberal manner; in a way that is large in amount or impressive.  Also, in a good-looking or attractive manner; skillfully and effectively.|He rewarded them handsomely for their efforts. 他对他们的努力给予了丰厚的回报。|近义词：generously, liberally, amply, richly, well, beautifully, elegantly|反义词：meanly, poorly, stingily, inadequately, badly|词性:副词
