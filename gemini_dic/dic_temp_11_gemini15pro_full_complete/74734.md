freestanding:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/| adj.|独立式的，自立的|Existing or functioning independently; not attached to or supported by anything else.|例句：The refrigerator is freestanding, not built-in.  这台冰箱是独立式的，不是嵌入式的。|例句：They have a freestanding bathtub in their bathroom.  他们的浴室里有一个独立式浴缸。|近义词：independent, separate, detached, self-contained, self-supporting|反义词：attached, built-in, connected, dependent|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From free + standing.  "Free" meaning independent and "standing" referring to its physical position.
