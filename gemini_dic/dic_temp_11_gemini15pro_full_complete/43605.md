creamy:/ˈkriːmi/| adj.|奶油的，乳脂状的，柔和的|Smooth, rich, and thick like cream; having the consistency of cream.  Also, having a soft, pale colour.|The cake had a creamy vanilla frosting.  蛋糕上覆盖着奶油香草糖霜。|Synonyms: smooth, rich, velvety, buttery, luscious|Antonyms: thin, watery, grainy|adjective
