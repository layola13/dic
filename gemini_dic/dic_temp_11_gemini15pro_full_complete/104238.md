jurisdictive: /ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪktɪv/
| adj. |
司法权的；管辖的；
|Having the power to make legal judgments and decisions; relating to the administration of justice.|
|The court has jurisdictive authority over this matter. 法院对此事拥有管辖权。|
|The jurisdictive powers of the local government are limited. 地方政府的司法权是有限的。|
|judicial, jurisdictional|
|non-jurisdictive (rarely used)|
|adjective|
