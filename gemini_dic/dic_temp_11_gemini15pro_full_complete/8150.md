anti-conservationist:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 反环保主义者 | Someone who opposes environmental conservation efforts. |  例句：The anti-conservationist lobby argued against the new national park proposal.  反环保主义游说团体反对新的国家公园提案。

例句：Her stance on deforestation made her an anti-conservationist in the eyes of many. 她对森林砍伐的立场使她在许多人眼中成为反环保主义者。 | 近义词：environmental opponent,  opponent of conservation | 反义词：conservationist, environmentalist | 词性: noun


anti-conservationist:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/ | adj. | 反环保的 | Opposing or detrimental to environmental conservation. |  例句：The company's anti-conservationist policies led to protests. 该公司的反环保政策引发了抗议。

例句：His anti-conservationist views are shocking to many. 他的反环保观点令许多人感到震惊。| 近义词：environmentally destructive, anti-environmental | 反义词：pro-environment, conservationist | 词性: adjective
