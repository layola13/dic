leviathan:/ləˈvaɪəθən/ | n. | 巨兽，庞然大物；强大的机构或组织 | A sea monster mentioned in the Bible; something huge and powerful, like a large organization or ship. |例句：The company was a leviathan in the industry, dominating all competitors. 这家公司是行业的巨头，主宰着所有的竞争对手。

例句：The government bureaucracy is often described as a leviathan. 政府官僚机构常被描述为一个庞然大物。|近义词：monster, behemoth, giant |反义词: dwarf, pygmy | 词性: 名词
