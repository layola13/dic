gulags:/ˈɡuːlɑːɡz/
noun
古拉格,  (苏联)劳改营
A system of forced labor camps in the Soviet Union.
His grandfather perished in the gulags. 他的祖父死于古拉格。
synonyms:labor camps, concentration camps, prison camps
antonyms:freedom, liberty
