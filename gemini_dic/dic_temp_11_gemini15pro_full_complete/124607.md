mousier:/ˈmuːsiər/ | adj. | 更女性化的，更娇气的 | More effeminate; more delicate or refined in a way traditionally associated with women. | He had a rather mousier personality than his brother. 他比他哥哥的性格更女性化。 | 近义词: effeminate, unmanly, womanish | 反义词: masculine, manly | 词性: 形容词

词源: From mouse + -ier (comparative suffix).  Likely influenced by the perceived timid and delicate nature of mice.
