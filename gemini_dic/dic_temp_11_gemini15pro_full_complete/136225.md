nonremittable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmɪtəbl/
adjective
不可汇出的，不可汇付的
Not capable of being remitted or pardoned; unpardonable.
The court imposed a nonremittable fine. 法院处以不可减免的罚款。
Synonyms: unpardonable, unforgivable, inexcusable
Antonyms: remittable, pardonable, forgivable
词性: adjective
