numb:/nʌm/| adj.|麻木的；失去知觉的；迟钝的|Deprived of physical sensation or the ability to move a part of the body; lacking sensation or emotion.|My fingers were numb with cold. 我的手指冻麻了。|Synonyms: insensible, unfeeling, deadened, anesthetized|Antonyms: sensitive, responsive, aware|adjective

numb:/nʌm/| v.|使麻木；使失去知觉|To make numb; to deprive of feeling or responsiveness.|The cold wind numbed my face. 寒风使我的脸麻木了。|Synonyms: deaden, dull, desensitize|Antonyms: sensitize, stimulate, enliven|verb
