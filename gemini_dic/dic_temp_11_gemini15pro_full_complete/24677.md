brompicrin:/broʊmˈpɪkrɪn/ | n. |溴化苦，硝基甲烷三溴化物|A colorless, poisonous, crystalline solid, CBr3NO2, having a pungent odor, used as a soil fumigant, insecticide, and war gas. |Brompicrin was used as a chemical weapon in World War I. 溴化苦在一战中被用作化学武器。| The soil was fumigated with brompicrin to control nematodes.  用溴化苦熏蒸土壤以控制线虫。 |Synonyms:  tribromonitromethane|Antonyms: N/A| noun
