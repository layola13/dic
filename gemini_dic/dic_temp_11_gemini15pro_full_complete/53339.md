disadvantage:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/ | n. |不利条件，缺点，劣势|A disadvantage is a condition or situation that makes it more difficult for you to succeed than other people or things.|Living far from the city is a disadvantage when looking for work.  住在远离城市的地方在找工作时是一个劣势。|synonyms: drawback, handicap, detriment, weakness|antonyms: advantage, benefit, asset|noun

disadvantage:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/ | vt. |使处于不利地位|To disadvantage someone means to put them in a worse position than others.|His lack of experience disadvantaged him in the competition. 他缺乏经验使他在比赛中处于劣势。|synonyms: handicap, hinder, impair, undermine|antonyms: advantage, benefit, aid, assist|verb
