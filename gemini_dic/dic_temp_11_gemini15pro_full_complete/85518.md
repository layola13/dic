She gave a hasty glance at her watch and rushed out the door. 她匆匆看了一眼手表，就冲出了门。| 近义词：hurried, rushed, quick, speedy, precipitate | 反义词：deliberate, careful, slow, thoughtful, measured | 词性: 形容词
