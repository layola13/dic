effect's:/ɪˈfekts/| n.| 效果，影响，结果|The effect's of the medicine were immediate.  这药的效果立竿见影。|近义词：impact, consequence, outcome, result|反义词:cause, origin|noun
Etymology: From Middle English *effect*, from Old French *effeet*, from Latin *effectus* ("completion, execution, result"), from *efficere* ("to work out, accomplish"), from *ex-* ("out") + *facere* ("to do, to make").
