Igbo: /ˈiːboʊ/ (US), /ˈɪɡboʊ/ (UK) | n. adj.| 伊博人；伊博语 |A member of a people of southeastern Nigeria; the language of the Igbo people, belonging to the Kwa group of the Niger-Congo family. |  He is Igbo. 他是伊博人。| The Igbo language is tonal. 伊博语是有声调的。| synonyms: Ndi Igbo (more traditional)| antonyms: N/A | noun, adjective
