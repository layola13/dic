coelospermous:/siːləˈspɜːrməs/
adjective
中空种子的；腹缝线的
Having the seeds attached to the ventral suture of the pericarp, as in the peony.  The ovules and seeds are borne on the inner walls of the ovary or pod.
The peony is a coelospermous plant. 牡丹是一种中空种子的植物。
Near Synonyms:  ventrosepalous
Antonyms:  Not readily available.  Consider "axile" (referring to placentation where ovules attach to the central axis) if contrasting placentation types.
Etymology:  From Greek "koilos" (hollow) + "sperma" (seed)
