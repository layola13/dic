oilier:/ˈɔɪliər/ | adj. | 更油腻的; 更油滑的 | More oily or greasy; more unctuous. |  The mechanic's hands were oilier than usual after working on the engine. 技工修完发动机后，手比平时更油腻了。

The politician's smile was oilier than ever.  这个政客的笑容比以往任何时候都更加油滑。 | 近义词: greasier, slicker, smoother | 反义词: less oily, drier, rougher | 词性: 形容词

词源: From oil + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  来自 oil（油）+ -y（形容词后缀）+ -er（比较级后缀）。
