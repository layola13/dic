one-upsmanship:/ˌwʌnˈʌpmənˌʃɪp/ | n. | 胜人一筹；好胜心；争强好胜 | The practice of successively outdoing a competitor. | He’s always trying to one-up me, it’s exhausting! 他总是想胜过我，真让人精疲力尽！ |  She constantly boasts about her travels, it's pure one-upsmanship. 她经常吹嘘自己的旅行，这纯粹是为了胜过别人。 | synonyms: competitiveness, rivalry, oneupmanship | antonyms: cooperation, modesty, humility | noun
