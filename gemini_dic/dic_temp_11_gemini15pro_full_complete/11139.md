archencephala:/ɑːrkɛnˈsɛfələ/ | n. | 前脑 | The anterior of the three primary divisions of the developing vertebrate brain or the corresponding part of the adult brain.  It includes the telencephalon (cerebral hemispheres) and diencephalon (thalamus, hypothalamus, etc.). | The archencephala plays a crucial role in higher-level cognitive functions. 前脑在高级认知功能中起着至关重要的作用。| Synonym: prosencephalon | Antonym: N/A | noun
