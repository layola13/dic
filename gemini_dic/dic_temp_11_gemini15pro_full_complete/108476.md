lay oneself out:/leɪ wʌnˈsɛlf aʊt/
|phrasal verb|
|全力以赴；尽力；不遗余力|
|To exert oneself fully; to make a special effort.|
|He laid himself out to make the party a success. 他全力以赴，使聚会成功。|
|近义词: go all out, spare no effort, bend over backwards, strive, endeavor|
|反义词: hold back, do the bare minimum, slack off|
|词性: 动词短语
