overdigesting:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈdʒɛstɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度消化 |  The process of digesting food for too long or too thoroughly, often leading to discomfort.  This word is not commonly used in standard English and might be considered informal or non-standard. It describes a hypothetical state beyond normal digestion. |  "I think I'm overdigesting; my stomach feels uncomfortable." 我想我过度消化了；我的胃感觉不舒服。 | Near synonyms: indigestion, dyspepsia | Antonyms:  N/A (The opposite would imply insufficient digestion, for which there isn't a direct single word equivalent.) | Verb (derived from 'digest')
