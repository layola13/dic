missuses:/ˈmɪsɪzɪz/ | n. |太太们；女士们 (plural of Mrs., used to address or refer to more than one married woman) |  A formal and respectful way to address a group of married women. It is the plural of "Mrs." |例句：The missuses are meeting for tea this afternoon. 太太们今天下午要聚在一起喝茶。

例句：He addressed the letter to "The Missuses Smith." 他把信寄给了“史密斯太太们”。|近义词：wives, ladies |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: Noun
