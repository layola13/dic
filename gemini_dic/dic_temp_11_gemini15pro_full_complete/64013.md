epitaphed:/ˈɛpɪˌtæft/ /ˈɛpɪˌtɑːft/
| adj. |刻有墓志铭的；被写进墓志铭的|Inscribed with an epitaph; commemorated or summarized as if in an epitaph. |His life was epitaphed by his deeds of kindness.  他的一生因其善行而被铭记。| The fallen soldiers were epitaphed as heroes. 阵亡的士兵被誉为英雄，载入史册。| 近义词：commemorated, memorialized | 反义词: forgotten, unremembered |词性:形容词
