missel:/ˈmɪs(ə)l/| n. |导弹，火箭|A rocket-propelled weapon designed to be launched at a target.|The missel launched successfully and hit its target.|近义词：rocket, projectile, missile|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *missile (“that which is sent, thrown, or hurled”), from Latin missilis (“that may be thrown or hurled”), from missus, past participle of mittere (“to send, throw”).
