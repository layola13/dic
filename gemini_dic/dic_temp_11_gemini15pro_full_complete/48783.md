deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/| v. |转移，偏转，使转向|To cause something to change direction by hitting or touching it; to turn aside or make bend.|The bullet was deflected by the shield. 子弹被盾牌挡开了。|synonyms: divert, deviate, avert|antonyms: attract, focus, maintain|verb

deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/| v. |避开，挡开|To prevent something from reaching you by turning it away.|He deflected the blow with his arm. 他用手臂挡开了那一击。|synonyms: parry, block, ward off|antonyms: accept, receive, welcome|verb
