charisma: /kəˈrɪzmə/ | n. | 魅力，感召力 | A special personal quality or power that some people have to attract and impress other people |  She has a natural charisma that draws people to her. 她有一种天生的魅力，吸引着人们靠近她。

| Example:  The president's charisma made him popular with the voters.  这位总统的魅力使他深受选民欢迎。

| Example:  He lacks the charisma to be a successful leader. 他缺乏成为一个成功领导者的魅力。

| Synonym: charm, allure, magnetism, appeal | Antonym:  repulsiveness, unattractiveness | noun
