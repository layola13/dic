nonjudicially:/ˌnɑːnʤuˈdɪʃəli/
adverb
非司法地；在法庭外
without the involvement of a court or judge; outside of the legal system.
The matter was settled nonjudicially. 这件事是在庭外和解的。
Synonyms: informally, out of court, privately, extrajudicially
Antonyms: judicially, legally, officially
词性: 副词
