overdoubt:/ˌoʊvərˈdaʊt/| v. |过度怀疑；怀疑过度|To doubt excessively or to an unreasonable degree. | He overdoubted his abilities and missed the opportunity. 他过度怀疑自己的能力，错失了机会。|  I overdoubted my preparation for the exam and ended up doing better than I expected. 我过度怀疑自己对考试的准备，结果比预期的要好。 |近义词：distrust, suspect, question|反义词: trust, believe, have faith in |词性:动词
