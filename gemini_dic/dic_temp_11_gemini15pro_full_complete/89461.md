honesty:/ˈɑːnɪsti/ /ˈɒnɪsti/| n.|诚实，正直|The quality of being honest.|例句：Honesty is the best policy. 诚实是上策。

例句：He valued honesty above all else.  他最看重诚实。

近义词：integrity, truthfulness, sincerity, uprightness|反义词：dishonesty, deceit, deception|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *honeste*, from Old French *oneste*, *honneste* (“honorable, respectable, decent; honest, truthful”), from Latin *honestus* (“honorable, respectable”), from *honos* (“honor, reputation, dignity, office”).
