keelson: /ˈkiːlsən/ | n. |龙骨; 内龙骨|The main structural member of a ship's bottom, running lengthwise and supporting the whole frame.  Also, a similar structural member in other constructions, providing strength and rigidity.|The ship's keelson was damaged in the collision.  船的龙骨在碰撞中受损了。|Synonyms:  backbone, spine |Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
