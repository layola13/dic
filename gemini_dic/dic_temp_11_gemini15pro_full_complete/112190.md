looney:/ˈluːni/| adj. n.|疯狂的，古怪的；疯子|Crazy or eccentric.  A crazy person.|He had some pretty looney ideas about reforming the government. 他对政府改革有一些相当疯狂的想法。|She's a bit looney, but harmless. 她有点疯疯癫癫的，但没有恶意。|近义词：crazy, mad, insane, eccentric, daft|反义词：sane, rational, sensible|词性:形容词, 名词|词源:From *loony*, shortened from *lunatic*, from *luna* "moon" (see lunar).  The belief that changes of the moon caused intermittent insanity was widespread. 由*loony*缩写而来，*loony*又是*lunatic*的缩写，*lunatic*来自拉丁语*luna*，意为“月亮”。人们普遍认为月亮的变化会导致间歇性精神错乱。
