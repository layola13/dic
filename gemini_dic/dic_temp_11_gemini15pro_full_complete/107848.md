larders:/ˈlɑːrdərz/
n.
食品储藏室；食品储藏柜
a room or large cupboard for storing food
The larder was full of delicious homemade jams and preserves. 食品储藏室里装满了美味的自制果酱和蜜饯。

larders:/ˈlɑːrdərz/
n.
（尤指家庭的）食物供应，贮存的食物
(especially in a household) a supply of food; provisions
The family's larder was running low after the long winter.  漫长的冬天过后，这家的食物供应不足了。


Synonyms:
pantry, storeroom, cupboard, supply, provisions

Antonyms:
lack, shortage, scarcity

Etymology:
From Old French *lardier, from lard "bacon, fat," from Latin lardum. Originally a place to store bacon and other preserved meats.
