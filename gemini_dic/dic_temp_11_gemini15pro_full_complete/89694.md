hooting:/ˈhuːtɪŋ/ | n. | 喊叫；鸣叫；嘲笑 | The act of making a loud sound, like an owl, or expressing disapproval loudly. |  The hooting of the owls kept me awake all night. 猫头鹰的叫声让我整夜没睡。| synonyms: yelling, shouting, booing, clamor, ululation | antonyms: whispering, murmuring, silence | noun

hooting:/ˈhuːtɪŋ/ | adj. |  (informal) extremely funny | Hilarious, very amusing. | That joke was absolutely hooting!  那个笑话真是太好笑了！| synonyms: hilarious, funny, amusing, comical | antonyms: serious, boring, sad | adjective
