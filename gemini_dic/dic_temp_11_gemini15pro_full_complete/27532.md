campylotropous: /ˌkæmpɪˈlɒtrəpəs/
adjective
弯生胚珠的
Having the ovule curved in such a way that the micropyle is near the funiculus.
The campylotropous ovule is common in Leguminosae family. 豆科植物中常见的弯生胚珠。
Near Antonyms: orthotropous, anatropous
Word Origin:  From Greek *kampylos* "curved" + *tropos* "turn".
