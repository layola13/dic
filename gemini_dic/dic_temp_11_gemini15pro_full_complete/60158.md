effeminately:/ɪˈfɛmɪnɪtli/| adv.|女人味十足地；柔弱地；娇气地|In a way that is traditionally considered typical of a woman, especially in a delicate or overly refined way.|He gestured effeminately with his hands. 他女人味十足地挥舞着双手。|近义词：weakly, delicately, femininely|反义词：masculinely, strongly, robustly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *effeminatus* (from Latin *ex-* 'out of' + *femina* 'woman'), via *effeminate* + *-ly*.
