nonhabitability:/ˌnɒnhæbɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.| 不可居住性，不适宜居住|The nonhabitability of the planet makes it unsuitable for human colonization.  这个星球的不可居住性使其不适合人类殖民。|例句：The extreme temperatures contribute to the nonhabitability of the desert. 极端的温度导致沙漠不适宜居住。|近义词：uninhabitability, unlivable conditions|反义词：habitability, livability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + habitability.  Habitability comes from the Late Middle English habitable, from Old French habitable, from Late Latin habitabilis, from Latin habitare “to dwell.”  Non- is a prefix meaning “not.”
