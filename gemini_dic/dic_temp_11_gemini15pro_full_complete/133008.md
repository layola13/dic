nonenteric:/ˌnɑːnɛnˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
非肠道的
Not pertaining to, occurring in, or administered through the intestines.
Doctors administered the drug nonenterically. 医生们非肠道给药。
近义词: parenteral
反义词: enteric
词性: adjective
