clachan:/ˈklɑːxən/ /ˈklækən/
|n.|小村庄，小村落|A small village, especially a Highland village in Scotland, consisting of a cluster of houses.|The clachan nestled beside the loch. 这个小村庄依偎在湖边。|近义词: hamlet, village|反义词: city, metropolis|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *clachan*, meaning "stones," "village," or "collection of houses," related to Irish *cloch* "stone."
