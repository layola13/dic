inquisitorially:/ˌɪŋkwɪzɪˈtɔːriəli/
adverb
审问式地； inquisitively
in a manner suggesting that of an inquisitor, i.e., questioning closely, often in a harsh or severe way.
He stared inquisitorially at her, waiting for an explanation. 他审视着她，等待解释。
Synonyms:  questioningly, probingly, searchingly,  curiously, interrogatively
Antonyms:  indifferently, apathetically, uncuriously
词性: 副词
