imperfect:/ɪmˈpɜːrfɪkt/ /ɪmˈpɝːfɪkt/| adj. n.|不完美的，未完成的；未完成体|Not perfect; having faults or flaws; incomplete.  (n.) A verb tense indicating an action was ongoing or incomplete in the past.|例句：The imperfect vase was still beautiful despite its flaws.  这个不完美的花瓶尽管有瑕疵，但仍然很漂亮。

The potter left the imperfect clay sculpture to dry. 陶艺家把未完成的粘土雕塑晾干。|近义词：flawed, defective, incomplete, unfinished|反义词：perfect, flawless, complete, finished|词性:形容词,名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imperfectus, from Latin in- (“not”) + perfectus (“perfect”).
