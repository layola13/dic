annotator's:/ˈænəˌteɪtərz/ | n. |注释者的，注解者的 |  belonging to or associated with an annotator;  a person who annotates something, typically a text or book. | The annotator's notes were helpful in understanding the complex poem. 注释者的笔记有助于理解这首复杂的诗。 | 近义词: commentator's,  editor's | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "reader's" in some contexts) | 词性: possessive noun
