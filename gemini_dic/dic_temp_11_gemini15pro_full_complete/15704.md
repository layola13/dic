balaji:/bɑːləˈdʒiː/| n.|巴拉吉, 巴拉吉神|A Hindu deity, a form of Lord Vishnu.  Balaji is particularly popular in South India, and the Tirupati Venkateswara Temple dedicated to him is one of the richest and most visited religious sites in the world.|Example: Millions of pilgrims visit the Balaji temple every year.  每年有数百万朝圣者前往巴拉吉神庙。|Example: Balaji is revered as a benevolent and powerful deity. 巴拉吉神被尊为仁慈而强大的神。|Synonym: Venkateswara, Srinivasa|Antonym: N/A|Noun
