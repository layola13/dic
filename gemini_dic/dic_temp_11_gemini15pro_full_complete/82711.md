/ɡuːɪd/ /ɡwaɪd/ | n. | 全局唯一标识符;  指南;  向导; | A globally unique identifier (GUID) is a 128-bit integer used to identify resources.  A GUID is generally represented as a 32-character hexadecimal string. | Example: Each record in the database has a unique GUID. 数据库中的每条记录都有一个唯一的 GUID.| Synonyms: UUID, identifier | Antonyms: N/A | noun

/ɡuːɪd/ /ɡwaɪd/ | v. | 指导;  引导;  带领; | To show or tell someone how to get to a place or do something. | Example: The guide guided the tourists through the museum.  导游带领游客参观了博物馆. | Example: The signs guided us to the exit.  标志引导我们找到了出口. | Synonyms: lead, direct, escort, pilot, steer, usher | Antonyms: mislead, misdirect | verb
