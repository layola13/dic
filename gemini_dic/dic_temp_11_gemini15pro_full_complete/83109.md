gustatory:/ˈɡʌstətɔːri/ | adj. | 味觉的；品尝的 | Relating to the sense of taste. |  The chef created a gustatory masterpiece that tantalized the taste buds.  这位厨师创作了一道令人回味的味觉杰作。| synonyms: taste, flavorful, savory | antonyms: tasteless, bland, insipid | adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *gustatorius, from Latin gustator (“taster”), from gustare (“to taste”), from gustus (“taste”).
