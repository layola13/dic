glasnost:/ˈɡlæznɒst/ (US) /ˈɡlæznəst/ (UK)| n.|公开性，透明度|A policy of greater openness in government institutions and activities within the USSR, introduced by Mikhail Gorbachev in the late 1980s.|例句：Gorbachev's policy of glasnost led to increased freedom of speech. 戈尔巴乔夫的公开性政策导致言论自由的增加。

例句：The era of glasnost brought about significant changes in Soviet society.  公开化时代给苏联社会带来了重大的变化。


|近义词：openness, transparency, candor|反义词：secrecy, censorship, repression|词性:noun
