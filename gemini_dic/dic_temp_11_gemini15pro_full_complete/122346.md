miscible:/ˈmɪsɪbl/| adj.|可混合的|Capable of being mixed, especially of liquids, to form a homogeneous substance.|Oil and water are not miscible. 油和水不能混合。|Ethanol is miscible with water. 乙醇与水可混溶。|近义词：mixable, blendable, soluble (especially for solids in liquids)|反义词：immiscible, unmixable|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *miscibilis, from Latin miscēre "to mix".
