dipterocarpus:/ˌdɪptərəˈkɑːrpəs/| n. |龙脑香树，双翅果|A genus of tall, resinous trees of the family Dipterocarpaceae, found in Southeast Asia.  They are important timber trees and a source of resins, oils, and balsams.|The Dipterocarpus forest is a vital habitat for many endangered species. 龙脑香树林是许多濒危物种的重要栖息地。| Dipterocarpus turbinatus is a commercially important source of Gurjun balsam. 龙脑香树是重要的商业性古云香脂的来源。|synonyms: keruing, apitong, lauan (for specific species)|antonyms: N/A|noun
