omo:/ˈəʊmoʊ/| adj.|親密的，表示親近的 (Nigerian slang)|A term of endearment used in Nigerian Pidgin English, similar to "babe" or "honey."|例句： Omo, you look good today!  宝贝，你今天看起来真棒！|近义词：babe, honey, darling, dear|反义词: enemy, foe|词性:adjective

omo:/ˈəʊmoʊ/| interj.|表示惊讶或兴奋的感叹词 (Nigerian slang)|Used to express surprise, excitement, or other strong emotions. Similar to "wow" or "oh my god."|例句：Omo! I won the lottery! 天哪！我中彩票了！|近义词：wow, oh my god, OMG, gosh|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|词性:interjection
