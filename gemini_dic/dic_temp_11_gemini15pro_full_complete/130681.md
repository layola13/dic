nonaffective:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
非情感的；不涉及情感的
Relating to or denoting the aspects of mental processes other than emotions or feelings.
Her nonaffective response to the tragedy was disturbing. 她对这场悲剧的毫无感情的反应令人不安。
Synonyms: unemotional, impassive, apathetic, indifferent, detached
Antonyms: emotional, affective, passionate, sensitive
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + affective.  "Affective" comes from the Late Latin "affectīvus",  from "affectus" (past participle of "afficere" - to influence, act on), from "ad-" (to) + "facere" (to do, make).  Therefore, "nonaffective" literally means "not influencing or acting upon the emotions."
