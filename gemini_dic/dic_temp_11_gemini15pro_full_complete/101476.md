iridescence:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəns/ | n. |彩虹色，虹彩，闪光 |The quality of exhibiting a display of shimmering, rainbow-like colors.| The oil slick on the water showed a beautiful iridescence. 水面上的油污呈现出美丽的虹彩。 | 近义词: shimmer, sheen, luster, gleam | 反义词: dullness, flatness | 词性: 名词

iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/ | adj. |彩虹色的，闪光的 |Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles.| The iridescent feathers of the peacock are truly stunning.  孔雀闪光的羽毛真是令人惊叹。| 近义词: shimmering, opalescent, pearly, lustrous | 反义词: dull, matte | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin iris (rainbow) + -escent (becoming).  源自拉丁语 iris（彩虹）+ -escent（正在变成）。
