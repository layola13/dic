liberalising:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb, present participle
使自由化，放宽限制
The act of making something less strict or controlled, often referring to laws, regulations, or social customs.
The government is liberalising the laws on gambling. 政府正在放宽赌博方面的法律。
synonyms: freeing, loosening, relaxing, deregulating
antonyms: restricting, tightening, regulating
verb
