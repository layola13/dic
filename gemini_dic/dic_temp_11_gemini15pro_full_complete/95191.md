inaugurators:/ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪtərz/
n.
就职典礼的主持人；开创者；开幕者
Those performing the ceremony of inauguration; those who begin something.
The inaugurators of the new museum welcomed the first visitors.  新博物馆的开幕者欢迎第一批参观者。

The inaugurators of the new era were celebrated for their vision. 开创新时代的人们因其远见而受到赞扬。

近义词: initiators, founders, pioneers
反义词: followers, successors


词性: noun (plural)

词源: From the Latin "inaugurare," meaning "to take omens from birds," later "to consecrate, begin." The suffix "-or" denotes an agent or one who performs the action.
