inconvenienced:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənst/
adjective
感到不便的，感到麻烦的
caused trouble or difficulty for someone.
I was inconvenienced by the delayed flight.  我因航班延误而感到不便。
The road closure inconvenienced many commuters. 道路封闭给许多通勤者带来了不便。
近义词: bothered, troubled, disturbed, disrupted
反义词: accommodated, aided, assisted, helped
词性: adjective

inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/
noun
不便，麻烦
trouble or difficulty caused to one's personal requirements or comfort.
The power outage caused a great inconvenience.  停电造成了极大的不便。
I apologize for the inconvenience this may cause.  对于由此可能造成的不便，我深表歉意。
近义词: trouble, difficulty, bother, nuisance
反义词: convenience, ease, comfort, aid
词性: noun

inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/
verb
给…带来不便，使…感到麻烦
to cause trouble or difficulty for someone.
The construction work inconvenienced the residents.  施工给居民带来了不便。
I don't want to inconvenience you, but could you give me a ride? 我不想麻烦你，你能载我一程吗？
近义词: bother, trouble, disturb, disrupt
反义词: accommodate, aid, assist, help
词性: verb

词源: From in- (not) + convenience.  由in- (不) + convenience (方便) 组成。
