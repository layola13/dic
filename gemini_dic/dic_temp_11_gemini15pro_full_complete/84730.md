handiworks: /ˈhændɪwɜːrks/
n.
手工制品；手工艺品
Something made skillfully by hand.
The church was decorated with the handiworks of local artists.  教堂里装饰着当地艺术家的手工艺品。

The museum displayed exquisite handiworks from various cultures.  博物馆展出了来自不同文化的精美手工艺品。

近义词: crafts, creations, artifacts
反义词: factory-made goods

词性: 名词
