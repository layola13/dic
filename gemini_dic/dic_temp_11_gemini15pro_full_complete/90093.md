hostler:/ˈhɒstlə(r)/ (British) /ˈhɑːstlər/ (American)| n.|马夫；旅店老板；（大学）宿舍管理员|A person who takes care of horses, especially at an inn or stable; a person who manages a hostel or dormitory.|例句：The hostler led the horses to the stable. 马夫把马牵到马厩里。

例句：The hostler at the inn was very friendly. 旅店老板非常友善。

近义词：stableman, groom, innkeeper, manager|反义词:guest, boarder|词性:noun

hostlers:/ˈhɒstləz/ (British) /ˈhɑːstlərz/ (American)| n.|马夫们；旅店老板们；（大学）宿舍管理员们|Plural form of hostler.|例句：The hostlers were busy tending to the horses. 马夫们正忙着照料马匹。

例句：The hostlers at the university ensured the students' safety. 大学宿舍管理员们确保了学生们的安全。

近义词：stablemen, grooms, innkeepers, managers|反义词:guests, boarders|词性:noun
