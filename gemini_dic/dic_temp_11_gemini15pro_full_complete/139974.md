odorate:/ˈoʊdəreɪt/ |v. |使充满香气；使芳香|To perfume; to scent.|The baker odorated the shop with the sweet smell of cinnamon rolls. 面包师用肉桂卷的香味使整个商店充满香气。|例句：The flowers odorated the entire garden. 这些花使整个花园充满香气。|近义词：perfume, scent, aromatize|反义词：deodorize|词性：动词

odorate:/ˈoʊdəreɪt/ |adj. |有香味的；芳香的|Having a pleasant odor; fragrant.|The odorate blossoms filled the air with a delicate perfume. 芬芳的花朵使空气中充满了淡淡的香味。|例句：The odorate candles created a relaxing atmosphere. 香薰蜡烛营造了轻松的氛围。|近义词：fragrant, aromatic, perfumed|反义词：malodorous, fetid|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ōdōrātus*, past participle of *ōdōrō* (“to give forth a smell, perfume”), from *odor* (“smell, scent”).
