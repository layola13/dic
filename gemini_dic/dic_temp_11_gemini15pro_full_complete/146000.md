overdominated:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
过度支配的，被压倒的
being excessively influenced or controlled by something or someone.
The team's strategy was overdominated by the coach's rigid philosophy.  该团队的战略过度受制于教练僵化的理念。
His personality is overdominated by his insecurities. 他的性格被他缺乏安全感所支配。
近义词: controlled, dominated, governed, ruled
反义词: independent, autonomous, free

词性: adjective
