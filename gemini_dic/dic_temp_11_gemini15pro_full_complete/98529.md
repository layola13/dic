instigant:/ˈɪnstɪɡənt/| n.|煽动者；教唆犯；刺激物|Something that instigates or incites someone to do something, typically something bad.|The instigant behind the riot was never identified.|近义词：agitator, provoker, inciter, stimulator|反义词：pacifier, deterrent, inhibitor|名词

instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/| vt.|煽动；唆使；激起|Bring about or initiate (an action or event).|He instigated a rebellion against the king.|近义词：provoke, incite, foment, abet|反义词：prevent, deter, suppress, quell|动词
