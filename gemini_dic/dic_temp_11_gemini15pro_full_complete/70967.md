fireman:/ˈfaɪərmən/ (US), /ˈfaɪəmən/ (UK)| n. |消防员|A person whose job is to extinguish fires.|例句：The fireman bravely rushed into the burning building. 消防员勇敢地冲进燃烧的建筑物。

例句：Firemen are essential for the safety of our community. 消防员对我们社区的安全至关重要。|近义词：firefighter|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps arsonist as someone who *starts* fires)|词性: noun
