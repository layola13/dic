gato:/ˈɡatoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡætəʊ/ (UK)| n.|猫|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet or for catching mice.|The little girl loves her gato. 小女孩很爱她的猫。|近义词：cat, feline, kitty|反义词:dog (as a common contrasting pet)|noun
