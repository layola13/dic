laper:/ˈlɑːpər/
adjective
hungry (informal, chiefly British)
Feeling a desire or need for food.
例句：I'm feeling a bit laper, fancy grabbing a bite? 我有点饿了，想吃点东西吗？
例句：After hiking all day, we were absolutely laper. 爬了一天的山，我们饿极了。
近义词：peckish, hungry, starving, ravenous
反义词：full, satiated, satisfied
词性: adjective

词源：Likely a variant of "leper," influenced by "lap" (to drink or eat by licking).  The original meaning might have been related to the emaciated appearance of lepers, thus associating the word with hunger.
