alternating-current:/ˈɔːltərˌneɪtɪŋ ˈkɜːrənt/ | n. | 交流电 | An electric current that reverses its direction many times a second at regular intervals, typically used in power supplies. |  Most homes use alternating current for their electrical appliances. 大多数家庭的电器使用交流电。

Alternating current is more efficient for long-distance transmission than direct current. 交流电比直流电更适合远距离传输。 | synonym: AC | antonym: direct current (DC) | noun


alternating-currents:/ˈɔːltərˌneɪtɪŋ ˈkɜːrənts/ | n. | 交流电 |  Plural form of "alternating current". Refers to multiple instances or systems of alternating current. | The alternating currents in these circuits are out of phase. 这些电路中的交流电异相。


Modern power grids rely on complex systems of alternating currents. 现代电网依赖于复杂的交流电系统。 | synonym: ACs (informal) | antonym: direct currents (DCs) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "alternating" + "current".  "Alternating" comes from the Latin word "alternare" meaning "to change by turns". "Current" comes from the Old French word "corant" meaning "running".
