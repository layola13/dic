capillaceous:/ˌkæpɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
毛状的，毛细管的
Resembling a hair; fine and slender; hairlike.  Relating to capillaries.
The plant has capillaceous leaves.  这种植物有毛状的叶子。
synonyms: hairlike, filamentous, threadlike, capillary
antonyms: thick, coarse
adjective
