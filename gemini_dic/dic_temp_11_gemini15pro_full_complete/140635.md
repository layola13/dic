olympianism: /əˈlɪmpiənɪzəm/
n.
奥林匹克精神；奥林匹克主义
The set of principles and ideals, especially those of internationalism, fair play, and athletic excellence, associated with the Olympic Games.
He epitomized the spirit of Olympianism.  他体现了奥林匹克精神。
近义词: sportsmanship, fair play, athleticism
反义词:  dishonesty, cheating, unsportsmanlike conduct
词性: noun

Etymology: From Olympian + -ism. Olympian refers to the ancient Greek gods who resided on Mount Olympus, the site of the ancient Olympic Games. The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, belief, or practice.
