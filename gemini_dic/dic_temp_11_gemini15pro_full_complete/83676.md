hagberry:/ˈhæɡˌbɛri/ | n. | 黑果越橘;欧洲越橘 | A dark blue berry similar to a blueberry, the fruit of the hagberry shrub (Prunus padus). | The birds feasted on the ripe hagberries in the autumn. 秋天，鸟儿尽情享用成熟的黑果越橘。| 近义词: bird cherry, hackberry | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old English *haga-berie, from haga ("hedge, enclosure") + berie ("berry").  So literally, "hedge berry."
