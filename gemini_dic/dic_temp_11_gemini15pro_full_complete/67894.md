fac:/fæk/ | n. | 事实，真相 | something that is known to have happened or to exist | The fac is, I don't know him. 事实上，我不认识他。| synonym: truth, reality | antonym: lie, falsehood | noun


fac:/fæk/ | abbreviation | 事实上 (abbreviation of "in fact") | used to emphasize a statement or introduce a contrasting idea | Fac, I've never even met him. 事实上，我根本没见过他。| synonym: actually, in fact, in reality, as a matter of fact | antonym: supposedly, theoretically, hypothetically | abbreviation


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fac*, the neuter singular form of *factus*, the past participle of *facere* "to do, make". Related to English *fact*.
