abolishing:/əˈbɒlɪʃɪŋ/
verb
废除，取消
the act of formally ending or stopping something
例句：The government is considering abolishing the death penalty. 政府正在考虑废除死刑。

例句：They are abolishing some of the old rules. 他们正在废除一些旧规则。

近义词：eliminating, eradicating, terminating, ending, removing, stopping
反义词：establishing, instituting, creating, starting, commencing
词性: 动名词 (gerund) or present participle
