opponent: /əˈpəʊnənt/ (UK) /əˈpoʊnənt/ (US)| n.| 对手，反对者 |Someone who competes against or fights another in a contest, game, or argument; a rival or adversary.|  My opponent in the chess match was very skilled. 我在象棋比赛中的对手非常熟练。| synonym: adversary, rival, competitor, antagonist | antonym: ally, supporter, friend, partner | noun

opponent: /əˈpəʊnənt/ (UK) /əˈpoʊnənt/ (US)| adj.（不常用）| 对立的，对抗的|Acting against or in opposition to; opposing.|  He took the opponent view in the debate.  他在辩论中采取了相反的观点。 | synonym: opposing, contrary, adverse | antonym: supporting, favorable, allied | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *oponant, present participle of opposer ‘oppose’, from Latin opponere (see oppose).
