以下提供 what 的词语解析，因为推测可能是想问"what"的用法：

what: /wɒt/ (British), /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What do you want for dinner? 你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词: which, who | 反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What did she do yesterday? = 她昨天做了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing last night? = 你昨晚在做什么？
What was he thinking about? = 他在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for lunch? = 他们午饭吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? = 他们一直在讨论什么那么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will she wear to the party? = 她会穿什么去参加聚会？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by the deadline? = 他们在截止日期前会完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by graduation? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
