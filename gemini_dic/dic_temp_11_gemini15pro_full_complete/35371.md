co-obligant:/koʊˈɑːblɪɡənt/ /kəʊˈɒblɪɡənt/
n.|共同债务人；连带保证人|A person who is equally responsible with another person for repaying a debt.|例句：My husband is my co-obligant on the mortgage. 我的丈夫是这笔抵押贷款的共同债务人。|例句：The co-obligant was held responsible for the full amount of the debt when the primary borrower defaulted. 当主要借款人违约时，共同债务人要对全部债务负责。|近义词：joint debtor, guarantor, surety|反义词:|词性:名词
