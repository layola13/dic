go-aheadativeness:/ˌɡoʊ əˈhɛdətɪvnɪs/
n.|积极进取；主动性|The quality of being eager and willing to proceed or take action without hesitation.|Her go-aheadativeness impressed the interviewers, who were looking for someone proactive. 她的积极进取给面试官留下了深刻印象，他们正在寻找一个积极主动的人。|例句：His go-aheadativeness led him to start his own business. 他的进取心促使他创办了自己的企业。

例句：Her go-aheadativeness was a key factor in her promotion. 她的积极进取是她晋升的关键因素。|近义词：initiative, proactiveness, enterprise, dynamism|反义词：passivity, hesitancy, reluctance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "go-ahead" (adjective meaning enterprising and progressive) + "-ative" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  "Go-ahead" itself comes from the verb phrase "go ahead," meaning to proceed.
