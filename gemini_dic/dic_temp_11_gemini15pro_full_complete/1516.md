acetophenone:/ˌæsɪtoʊˈfiːnoʊn/ | n. |苯乙酮; 甲基苯基酮|A colorless liquid aromatic ketone C8H8O used as a solvent and in perfume.  It is the simplest aromatic ketone.| Acetophenone is used as a fragrance ingredient in soaps and perfumes. 苯乙酮用作肥皂和香水中的一种香料成分。| Acetophenone is a hypnotic agent.  苯乙酮是一种安眠药。 |Synonyms: methyl phenyl ketone, phenyl methyl ketone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
