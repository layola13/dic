hyperthermally:/ˌhaɪpərˈθɜːrməli/
adverb|高温地；超热地|Relating to or involving extremely high temperatures, typically those associated with hydrothermal vents or geothermal activity.|Hydrothermal vents are hyperthermally active areas on the ocean floor. 海底热液喷口是海底高温活跃的区域。|近义词：superheated, extremely hot|反义词:cold, frigid|词性:副词
Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hypér, "over, beyond") + "thermal" (from Greek θερμός, thermós, "hot") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
