acquainted:/əˈkweɪntɪd/
adjective
熟悉的，认识的
Having personal knowledge of someone or something.
例句：I am acquainted with his work. 我熟悉他的工作。

例句：Are you acquainted with this author? 你认识这位作家吗？

近义词：familiar, conversant, aware, informed
反义词：unfamiliar, ignorant, unacquainted
词性: adjective
