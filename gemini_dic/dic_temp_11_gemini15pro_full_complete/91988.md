hypernormalities:/ˌhaɪpərˌnɔːrˈmælɪtiz/ | n. | 超常态 |  A state of exaggerated or heightened normality, often referring to a situation where what is considered normal has become extreme or dysfunctional.  It can also refer to multiple overlapping normalities, creating a complex and potentially conflicting set of expectations. | The constant pressure to appear perfect on social media has created a hypernormality around body image. 社交媒体上持续不断的完美形象压力，在身体形象方面造成了一种超常态。

The hypernormalities of the corporate world, with its endless meetings and performance reviews, can be exhausting.  充斥着无休止的会议和绩效评估的企业界的超常态，可能会让人精疲力竭。| 近义词: excess, extreme, exaggeration | 反义词: abnormality, deviation, irregularity | 词性: 名词
