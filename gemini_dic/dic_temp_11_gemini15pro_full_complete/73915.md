forswearer:/fɔːrˈswɛərər/ | n. | 伪证者；发誓放弃者；背弃者 |  A person who renounces or gives up something under oath or solemnly; one who commits perjury. | He was labeled a forswearer after changing his testimony in court. 他在法庭上改变证词后，被贴上了伪证者的标签。|  perjurer, liar, renegade, apostate | loyalist, adherent | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *forswērere*, equivalent to forswear +‎ -er.  Ultimately from Old English *forswerian* (“to swear falsely, perjure oneself; abjure, renounce”).
