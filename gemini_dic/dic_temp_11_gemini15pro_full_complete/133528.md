nonfidelities: /ˌnɑːnfɪˈdɛlətiz/ | n. | 不忠，不贞行为 | Acts of being unfaithful, usually in a romantic relationship.  It refers to a breach of trust and commitment, often involving sexual or emotional intimacy with someone other than one's partner. |  Her repeated nonfidelities led to the breakdown of their marriage. 她的屡次不忠导致了他们婚姻的破裂。| Synonyms: infidelities, betrayals, unfaithfulness | Antonyms: loyalty, fidelity | Noun
