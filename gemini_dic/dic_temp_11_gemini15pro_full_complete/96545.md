indoor:/ˈɪndɔːr/ (US) /ˈɪndɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj.|室内的，户内的|Located, done, or used inside a building.|例句：Indoor sports are popular during the winter. 冬季室内运动很受欢迎。|例句：They have a beautiful indoor swimming pool. 他们有一个漂亮的室内游泳池。|近义词：inside, interior|反义词：outdoor, outside|词性:形容词

词源:
From in + door.  The word "in" traces back to Proto-Germanic *in, meaning "in, into," and ultimately to Proto-Indo-European *en, also meaning "in." "Door" comes from Old English dor, meaning "door, gate," from Proto-Germanic *dur-, also meaning "door."
