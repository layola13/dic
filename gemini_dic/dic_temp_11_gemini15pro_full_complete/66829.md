expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/
verb
详述，细说
To speak or write at length or in detail.
He expatiated on the benefits of vegetarianism.  他详细阐述了素食主义的好处。
She expatiated upon her travels in Europe. 她详细叙述了她的欧洲之旅。
Synonyms: elaborate, enlarge, expand, descant
Antonyms: condense, summarize, abbreviate
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin expatiatus, past participle of expatiari (“to wander, digress”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + spatiari (“to walk”), from spatium (“space”).
