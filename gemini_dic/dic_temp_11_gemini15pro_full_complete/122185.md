misunderstand:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/
|v.|误解，误会|To interpret (something) incorrectly; to not understand the correct meaning of something.|She completely misunderstood my intentions. 她完全误解了我的意图。|He misunderstood the directions and got lost. 他误解了方向，迷路了。|近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue|反义词：understand, comprehend|词性:动词

misunderstood:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstʊd/
|adj.|被误解的|Incorrectly interpreted or understood.|He felt misunderstood by his friends. 他觉得他的朋友误解了他。|Her silence was often misunderstood as arrogance. 她的沉默常被误解为傲慢。|近义词：misinterpreted, misconstrued|反义词：understood, comprehended|词性:形容词


词源:
From mis- + understand.  The prefix "mis-" means "wrongly" or "badly".  Therefore, "misunderstand" literally means to understand wrongly.
