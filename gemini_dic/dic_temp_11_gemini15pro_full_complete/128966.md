ninut: /ˈniːnʊt/ | n. | minute | a unit of time equal to 60 seconds or 1/60th of an hour |  I'll be back in a ninut. 我马上回来。 | synonyms: moment, instant, jiffy | antonyms: eternity, age | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *mynet*, from Old French *minute*, from Latin *minūta*, feminine of *minūtus* (“small, little”), past participle of *minuō* (“diminish”). Doublet of *minute*.
