benison:/ˈbɛnɪsən/ | n. |祝福，恩赐|A prayer or blessing.  A gift or benefit. |May God's benison be upon this house. 愿上帝的祝福降临在这座房子上。|The king bestowed his benison upon the newlyweds. 国王将他的祝福赐予了这对新婚夫妇。|近义词：blessing, boon, grace|反义词：curse, malediction|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *benisson, from Late Latin *benedictionem (nominative *benedictio), accusative singular of benedictio (“blessing”), from Latin benedicere (“to bless, speak well of”), from bene (“well”) + dicere (“to say”).  Doublet of benediction.
