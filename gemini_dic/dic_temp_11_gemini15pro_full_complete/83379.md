gyrostatic:/ˌdʒaɪrəˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 陀螺的，回转的 | Relating to or exhibiting the properties of a gyroscope, such as maintaining equilibrium or resisting change in direction. |  The gyrostatic effect of the spinning top kept it upright. 旋转陀螺的回转效应使其保持直立。| The gyrostatic compass is a vital navigational instrument. 回转罗盘是重要的导航仪器。 |  synonyms: gyroscopic, inertial | antonyms: unstable, non-rotating | adjective
