incaution:/ɪnˈkɔːʃən/
n.|不谨慎，轻率|Lack of caution; rashness.|His incaution led to a serious accident. 他的轻率导致了一场严重的事故。|近义词：recklessness, imprudence, carelessness, rashness|反义词:caution, prudence, carefulness, circumspection|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *incautiō, from in- "not" + cautiō "caution".
