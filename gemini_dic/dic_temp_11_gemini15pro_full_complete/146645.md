overhardy:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdi/
adjective
过于强壮的；过分耐寒的；鲁莽的
too hardy; excessively bold or rash.
The overhardy seedlings were killed by the late frost. 那些过于强壮的幼苗被晚霜冻死了。
近义词：reckless, rash, impetuous, foolhardy
反义词：cautious, prudent, timid
