coleseed:/ˈkoʊlˌsiːd/| n. |菜籽，油菜籽|The farmer sowed the field with coleseed. 农民在地里播种了油菜籽。|例句：Coleseed oil is a popular cooking oil. 油菜籽油是一种常用的食用油。

Coleseed is grown for its oil-rich seeds. 油菜籽因其富含油脂的种子而被种植。|近义词：rapeseed, canola (sometimes)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *cōl-sǣd ("cabbage seed"), from cōl ("cabbage") + sǣd ("seed"). Cognate with Dutch koolzaad, German Kohlsaat.
