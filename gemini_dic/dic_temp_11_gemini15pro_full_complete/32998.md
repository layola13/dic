choriambus:/ˌkɔːriˈæmbəs/ | n. | 长短格 | A metrical foot consisting of a long syllable followed by two short syllables and then another long syllable (– ᴗ ᴗ –). |  The poet skillfully employed choriambs in his verse. 这位诗人巧妙地在诗歌中运用了长短格。|  dactyl, amphibrach |  iamb, trochee | noun

choriambic:/ˌkɔːriˈæmbɪk/ | adj. | 长短格的 | Relating to or composed of choriambs. | The poem's choriambic rhythm created a lilting effect. 这首诗的长短格节奏营造了一种轻快的效果。| dactylic, amphibrachic | iambic, trochaic | adjective
