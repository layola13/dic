marital:/ˈmærɪt(ə)l/
adjective
婚姻的，夫妇间的
Of or relating to marriage or the relationship between husband and wife.
例句：They are having marital problems. 他们正经历婚姻问题。

例句：Marital bliss is not always easy to achieve. 婚姻幸福并非总是容易获得。


近义词：matrimonial, conjugal, spousal
反义词：single, unmarried, divorced
词性：adjective
