fascicular:/fəˈsɪkjʊlər/ |adj.|束状的；簇生的|arranged in or forming bundles or clusters; relating to a fascicle. |The fascicular cambium is the main growth tissue in stems and roots of many plants.  维管形成层是许多植物茎和根的主要生长组织。|The doctor pointed out the fascicular pattern of the muscle fibers. 医生指出了肌肉纤维的束状形态。|近义词:bundled, clustered, fasciculate|反义词:diffuse, scattered|词性:形容词

fascicle:/ˈfæsɪkəl/ |n.|束；分册；一期|a bundle or cluster of fibers, nerves, or other elongated structures; a section of a book or periodical published in installments. |This book is published in fascicles, with a new one coming out each month. 这本书是分册出版的，每个月出一本新书。|The nerve fascicle was damaged in the accident.  神经束在事故中受损了。|近义词:bundle, cluster, sheaf|反义词:single, individual|词性:名词

词源: From Latin fasciculus (diminutive of fascis "bundle")
