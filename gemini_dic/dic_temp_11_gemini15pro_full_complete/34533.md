cleansable:/ˈklɛnzəbl/| adj.|可清洗的，可洁净的|Able to be cleansed or cleaned.|This makeup is cleansable with just soap and water.  这种化妆品只需用肥皂和水就可以清洗干净。| The fabric is easily cleansable.  这种织物很容易清洗。|近义词：washable, cleanable|反义词：non-washable, uncleanable|词性:形容词
词源: cleanse + -able.  From cleanse (verb) + -able (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘able to be’). Ultimately from Latin *clēnsiāre, from clēns ‘clean’.
