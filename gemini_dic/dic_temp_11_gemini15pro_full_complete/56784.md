dopper:/ˈdɒpər/ (UK) /ˈdɑːpər/ (US)| n.|多普勒，多普勒效应|A device, often a radar or sonar device, that utilizes the Doppler effect to measure the speed and direction of moving objects (e.g., vehicles, weather systems).  It can also refer to a Doppler ultrasound, used in medicine.|The police officer used a doppler to track the speeding car. 警察用多普勒雷达追踪超速行驶的汽车。|Synonyms: Doppler radar, Doppler sonar, speed gun, velocity meter|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
