huggle:/ˈhʌɡəl/| v. n.|拥抱；依偎；紧抱|To hold (someone) close in one's arms, especially for affection or comfort; a close embrace.|She hugged her teddy bear tightly. 她紧紧地抱着她的泰迪熊。|近义词：cuddle, embrace, snuggle|反义词:repel, push away|词性:动词, 名词
Etymology:  Probably from dialectal *hug, frequentative of hug, of Scandinavian origin; related to Norwegian *hugga.
