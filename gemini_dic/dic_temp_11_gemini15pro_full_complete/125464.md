multiplicator:/ˈmʌltɪplɪkeɪtər/| n. |乘数，乘子|A quantity by which a given number (the multiplicand) is multiplied.|The multiplicator in the equation 5 x 3 = 15 is 3.|近义词：factor, coefficient|反义词:divisor, dividend|词性:noun
