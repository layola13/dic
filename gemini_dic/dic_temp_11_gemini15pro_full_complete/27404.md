camerade:/ˌkæməˈrɑːd/ /ˌkæməˈræd/ | n. |同志，朋友，伙伴|A comrade is a companion who shares one's activities or is a fellow member of an organization.  It often implies a sense of shared ideals or struggles.|例句：He fought alongside his comrades in the war.  他与他的同志们并肩作战。

例句：She valued the camaraderie among her comrades. 她珍视同志间的友情。|近义词：friend, companion, colleague, associate, partner|反义词：enemy, opponent, foe|词性: noun
