modiste:/moʊˈdist/| n. |女裁缝；女装设计师|A dressmaker or designer of women's fashions, especially one who creates fashionable hats.|例句：The famous modiste designed a stunning gown for the actress.  这位著名的女装设计师为这位女演员设计了一件惊艳的礼服。

例句：She visited the modiste to have a new dress made for the upcoming ball. 她去拜访女裁缝，为即将到来的舞会做一件新礼服。|近义词：dressmaker, couturier, designer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps customer)|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *modiste*, from *mode* ("fashion") + *-iste* (a suffix forming agent nouns). Ultimately from Latin *modus* ("measure, manner, fashion").
