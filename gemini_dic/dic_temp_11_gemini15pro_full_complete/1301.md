accountings:/əˈkaʊntɪŋz/
n.
会计；账目；决算
the process of keeping financial accounts; a record or statement of financial expenditure and receipts relating to a particular period or purpose.
The company's accountings are audited annually. 该公司的账目每年都会被审计。
synonyms: bookkeeping, reckoning, tallying
antonyms:  N/A (Accountings as a process doesn't have a direct antonym.)
noun
