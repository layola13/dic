disarranged:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒd/
adjective
混乱的，紊乱的
Being out of order; discomposed; jumbled.
The wind disarranged her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。
近义词: disordered, messy, chaotic, jumbled, rumpled
反义词: arranged, ordered, tidy, neat, organized
词性: adjective

disarrange:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒ/
verb
扰乱，弄乱
To disturb the arrangement or order of; disorder.
The earthquake disarranged the furniture in the house. 地震把屋里的家具都弄乱了。
近义词: disorder, disorganize, disturb, mess up, ruffle
反义词: arrange, organize, order, tidy, neaten
词性: verb

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + arrange (from Old French arengier, from a- (to) + rengier (to range, arrange)).
