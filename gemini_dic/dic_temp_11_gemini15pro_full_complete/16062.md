banding:/ˈbændɪŋ/| n. |条带，捆扎，结合，[无线]频带，[鸟]环志|The process of marking a bird with a band for identification.  鸟类环志，一种用于识别鸟类的标记方法。|例句：The banding of the birds allowed researchers to track their migration patterns. 鸟类环志使研究人员能够追踪它们的迁徙模式。

|近义词：strapping, binding, tying, taping, marking |反义词:unbinding, loosening|noun


banding:/ˈbændɪŋ/| v. | 用带子绑|To mark (a bird) with a band for identification.  用带子标记（鸟类）以便识别。|例句：Researchers are banding the birds to study their migration patterns. 研究人员正在给鸟类环志以研究它们的迁徙模式。|近义词：strapping, binding, tying, taping, marking|反义词:unbinding, loosening|verb
