overcopiously: /ˌoʊvərˈkoʊpiəsli/
adverb
过度丰富地，过分冗长地
Excessively, too abundantly; in an overly wordy or detailed manner.  It's formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive") to "copiously" (meaning "abundantly").
He documented his travels overcopiously, filling five notebooks with minute details. 他过度详细地记录了他的旅行，用五个笔记本记满了细枝末节。
The author overcopiously described the setting, losing the reader in unnecessary detail.  作者对场景的描写过于冗长，让读者迷失在不必要的细节中。
Synonym: excessively, profusely, redundantly, verbosely
Antonym: concisely, briefly, succinctly
词性: 副词
