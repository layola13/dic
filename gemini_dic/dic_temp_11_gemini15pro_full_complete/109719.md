levelest:/ˈlɛvəlɪst/ | adj. | 最水平的，最平坦的，最平静的 |  superlative form of level; most level, flat, or even. | The levelest ground we could find was still slightly sloped. 我们能找到的最平坦的地面仍然略有倾斜。 | The lake was levelest in the early morning, before the wind picked up.  清晨，在起风之前，湖面是最平静的。 | 近义词: flattest, smoothest, evenest | 反义词: steepest, roughest, most uneven | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *levelest, superlative of *level, from Old French livel, from Latin lībella (“level, plummet”), diminutive of lībra (“balance, scales”).
