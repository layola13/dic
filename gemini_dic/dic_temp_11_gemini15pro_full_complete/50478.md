depressingly:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地，压抑地
in a way that causes or is likely to cause depression
The weather has been depressingly wet all week.  这整个星期天气都潮湿得令人沮丧。
近义词:dishearteningly, sadly, gloomily
反义词:cheerfully, encouragingly, upliftingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From depress + -ing + -ly.  由depress（使沮丧）+ -ing（构成现在分词或形容词）+ -ly（构成副词）构成.
