overinforming:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 过度告知；提供过多信息 |  The act of providing too much information, often to the point of overwhelming or confusing the recipient. |  He overinformed his clients with unnecessary details, causing them to lose interest. 他向客户提供了过多的不必要的细节，导致他们失去了兴趣。 | synonyms: oversharing, inundating, overwhelming | antonyms: underinforming, withholding information | verb

overinforming:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | adj. | 提供过多信息的 | Characterized by providing excessive information. |  His overinforming email confused everyone. 他那封提供过多信息的邮件把大家都搞糊涂了。| synonyms: verbose, prolix, long-winded | antonyms: concise, succinct, brief | adjective
