ornaments:/ˈɔːrnəmənts/ (US) /ˈɔːnəmənts/ (UK)| n.|装饰品；装饰；点缀|Something that adds beauty or elegance; a decoration. |The Christmas tree was covered in beautiful ornaments.  圣诞树上挂满了漂亮的装饰品。

The bride wore simple but elegant ornaments.  新娘佩戴了简洁而雅致的饰品。|Synonyms: decorations, adornments, embellishments, trinkets|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|Noun
