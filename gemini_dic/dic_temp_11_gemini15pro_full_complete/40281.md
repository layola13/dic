continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ (US), /kənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| v.|继续，持续，延伸|To persist in an activity or process; to resume after interruption.|例句：The rain continued to fall all day. 雨持续下了一整天。|近义词：persist, proceed, resume, carry on, go on|反义词: stop, cease, halt, discontinue|verb

continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ (US), /kənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| n.|继续，持续|An uninterrupted flow or succession.|例句：The continue of the project is important.  项目的持续进行很重要。|近义词：continuation,  progression|反义词: interruption, cessation|noun
