diploidy:/ˈdɪplɔɪdi/| n.|二倍性|The state of having two sets of chromosomes, one from each parent.|Most animal cells are diploid. 大多数动物细胞是二倍体。|The diploid number of chromosomes in humans is 46.  人类的二倍体染色体数是46。|synonym: doubleness|antonym: haploidy|noun
