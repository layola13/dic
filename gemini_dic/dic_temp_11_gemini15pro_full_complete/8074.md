anthropomorphize: /ænˌθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪz/ | v. | 赋予人性，人格化 | To give human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object. |  She anthropomorphized her pet cat, believing it could understand her every word. 她把她心爱的宠物猫人格化了，认为它能听懂她说的每一句话。

Disney often anthropomorphizes animals in their movies. 迪士尼经常在他们的电影中赋予动物人性。

近义词: personify, humanize | 反义词: objectify, dehumanize | 词性: verb
