centric:/ˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 以...为中心的；中心化的 |  Relating to or situated in the center; having something as a central focus. |  Example 1:  This research project is very Eurocentric in its approach.  这项研究项目的方法非常以欧洲为中心。

Example 2:  The company adopted a customer-centric business model.  这家公司采用了一种以客户为中心的商业模式。 |  Synonyms: central, focused, concentrated | Antonyms: peripheral, marginal, decentralized |  adjective
