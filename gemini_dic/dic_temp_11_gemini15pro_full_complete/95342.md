incensurable:/ˌɪnsɛnˈʃʊrəbl/
adjective
无可指责的，无懈可击的
Not censurable; blameless; irreproachable.
His conduct throughout the ordeal was incensurable. 他在整个考验中的行为无可指责。
Synonyms: blameless, impeccable, irreproachable, faultless, unblemished, stainless
Antonyms: censurable, blameworthy, reprehensible, culpable
