apologists: /əˈpɒlədʒɪsts/ (US) /əˈpɒlədʒɪsts/ (UK)| n. |辩护者，辩解者；<贬>护教论者|A person who offers an argument in defense of something controversial.|He's a well-known apologist for the current regime. 他是现政权的著名辩护者。|synonyms: defender, advocate, supporter|antonyms: critic, opponent, detractor|noun

apologists: /əˈpɒlədʒɪsts/ (US) /əˈpɒlədʒɪsts/ (UK)| n. |<宗>护教论者|A person who defends Christianity against objections.|Many early Church Fathers were apologists who wrote extensively defending Christian beliefs. 许多早期的教父都是护教论者，他们写了大量的文章来捍卫基督教信仰。|synonyms: defender of the faith|antonyms: heretic, unbeliever|noun
