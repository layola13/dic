nonaromatic:/ˌnɒnˌærəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非芳香族的 | Not having the properties of an aromatic compound; lacking aromaticity.  An aromatic compound adheres to Hückel's rule, having 4n+2 pi electrons in a planar, cyclic, conjugated system. Nonaromatic compounds do not follow this rule. | Cyclohexane is a nonaromatic compound. 环己烷是一种非芳香族化合物。| Cyclooctatetraene, despite being cyclic and conjugated, is nonaromatic.  尽管环辛四烯是环状和共轭的，但它是非芳香族的。 |  aliphatic, non-aromatic | aromatic | adjective
