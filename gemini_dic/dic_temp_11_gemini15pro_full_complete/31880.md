cheerlessnesses:/ˈtʃɪrləsnəsɪz/
n.
阴郁，沉闷，不快乐
The state of being cheerless; lack of cheerfulness; gloominess.
The cheerlessnesses of the long winter nights were starting to get to her. 漫长冬夜的阴郁开始让她感到不适。
近义词: gloominess, dreariness, bleakness, despondency, melancholy
反义词: cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness
词性: 名词
