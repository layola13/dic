infant:/ˈɪnfənt/ (US), /ˈɪnfənt/ (UK)| n. |婴儿，幼儿|A very young child, especially one that has not yet learned to walk or talk.|The infant slept soundly in her crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|Synonyms: baby, newborn, toddler|Antonyms: adult, grown-up|noun

infant:/ˈɪnfənt/ (US), /ˈɪnfənt/ (UK)| adj. |婴儿的，幼稚的|Relating to or characteristic of infants; in an early stage of development.|The infant mortality rate has decreased significantly. 婴儿死亡率已显著下降。|Synonyms: babyish, immature, nascent|Antonyms: mature, developed, adult|adjective
