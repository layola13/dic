octosepalous:/ɒkˈtəʊˈsɛpələs/
adjective
八萼片的
Having eight sepals.
The botanist identified the rare flower as octosepalous due to its eight distinct sepals.  植物学家根据其八个明显的萼片，将这种稀有花朵鉴定为八萼片的。
Near Synonyms: eight-sepaled
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps five-sepaled, six-sepaled depending on the context)
词性: adjective
