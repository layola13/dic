fluttered:/ˈflʌtərd/ | v. | 飘动，挥动，颤动，(鸟)振翅，快速跳动 | To move lightly and quickly; to flap the wings rapidly. | The butterfly fluttered its wings. 蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。 | The flags fluttered in the breeze. 旗帜在微风中飘扬。 | synonyms: flap, wave, quiver, tremble | antonyms: still, steady | verb

fluttered:/ˈflʌtərd/ | adj. | 激动不安的，紧张的 | Feeling nervous and excited. | She felt fluttered by the attention.  她因受到关注而感到激动不安。| He had a fluttered feeling in his stomach before the exam. 考试前他感到胃里一阵紧张。 | synonyms: nervous, anxious, agitated | antonyms: calm, composed | adjective
