checked /tʃɛkt/ | adj. | 检查过的，选中的，格子的 | Having been examined or verified; marked with a check mark; controlled or restrained. | I packed my checked luggage. 我打包了我的托运行李。 | The doctor checked his pulse. 医生检查了他的脉搏。|近义词: examined, verified, inspected, controlled | 反义词: unchecked, uncontrolled, unrestrained | 词性: adjective

checked /tʃɛkt/ | v. | 检查，核对，制止 | To examine or verify something; to mark with a check mark; to control or restrain something. | I checked the door to make sure it was locked. 我检查了门，确保它锁上了。|  She checked her anger. 她抑制住了自己的愤怒。 | 近义词: examine, verify, inspect, control, restrain | 反义词: ignore, release | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chekken, from Anglo-Norman checker, from Old North French eschequier “to play chess, put in check”, from eschec “check” (at chess), from Vulgar Latin *scaccus, from Arabic *šaqq, from Persian šāh “king” (because checking the king is the object of chess).
