lemniscate: /lɛmˈnɪskət/ | n. | 双纽线，连珠线 | A plane curve generated by the locus of the point at which a tangent to a rectangular hyperbola intersects a perpendicular from the center to the tangent.  It resembles a figure eight. | The lemniscate is a fascinating mathematical curve. 双纽线是一条迷人的数学曲线。

The symbol for infinity (∞) is a rotated lemniscate.  无穷大符号 (∞) 是一个旋转的双纽线。| 近义词:  infinity symbol, figure eight | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a geometric shape) | 词性: noun
