incorporealist:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriːəlɪst/ | n. | 非物质论者；唯心论者 | A person who believes that material things have no real existence, only spiritual or mental entities are real. |  The incorporealist argued that the physical world was merely an illusion. 非物质论者认为物质世界只是一种幻觉。| synonym: idealist, spiritualist | antonym: materialist, physicalist | noun

incorporeal:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriːəl/ | adj. | 非物质的；无形的；精神上的 | Not consisting of matter; not having a physical body. | Ghosts are often described as incorporeal beings. 鬼魂通常被描述为非物质的存在。| synonym: intangible, immaterial, spiritual | antonym: corporeal, physical, tangible | adjective
