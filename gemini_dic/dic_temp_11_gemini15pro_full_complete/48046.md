deckings: /ˈdekɪŋz/
n.
甲板板材；装饰板；覆盖层
Planks or other materials used to make a deck or covering.
The new deckings were made of pressure-treated lumber. 新的甲板板材由经过加压处理的木材制成。
planking, flooring, covering
(通常不用反义词)
词性: 名词


deckings: /ˈdekɪŋz/
n.
（节日等的）装饰物
Decorations, especially festive ones.
The Christmas deckings were bright and cheerful.  圣诞装饰明亮而欢快。
decorations, ornaments, adornments
(通常不用反义词)
词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "to deck," meaning to cover or adorn, derived from the Middle Dutch "decken" and Old English "deccan."
