embroiders:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərz/
|v.|刺绣；渲染；润色|decorates with needlework; adds fictitious details to (a story, etc.)|She embroiders beautiful designs onto silk scarves. 她把美丽的图案绣在丝绸围巾上。| embellishes, decorates, adorns|plain, simple|verb
Etymology: From Old French *embroder, from en- (“in, on”) + broder (“to embroider”), from bord (“edge, border”).
