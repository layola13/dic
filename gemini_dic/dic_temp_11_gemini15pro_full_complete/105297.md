kibble: /ˈkɪbəl/ (US), /ˈkɪbəl/ (UK)| n.| 宠物食品；粗磨的谷物|Dry food for pets, typically in small, hard pellets. Also, coarsely ground grain. |The dog eagerly ate his kibble. 狗狼吞虎咽地吃着它的宠物食品。|She sprinkled kibble for the chickens. 她为鸡撒了些粗磨的谷物。|近义词：pellets, feed, chow (for pets), grain, meal (for coarsely ground grain)|反义词: None (no direct opposite)|词性: Noun

kibble: /ˈkɪbəl/ (US), /ˈkɪbəl/ (UK)| vt.| 将（谷物）粗磨|To grind coarsely. |The farmer kibbled the corn for the livestock. 农民将玉米粗磨给牲畜吃。|They kibbled the wheat to make a hearty porridge. 他们粗磨小麦来做营养丰富的粥。|近义词：grind, crush, mill|反义词:  pulverize, refine (implying finer grinding)|词性: Verb
