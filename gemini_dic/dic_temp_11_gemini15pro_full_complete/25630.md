bunched:/bʌntʃt/| adj.| 成束的，聚集在一起的|Gathered together in a close group; clustered.|The flowers were bunched together in a colorful bouquet.  这些花被扎成一束五彩缤纷的花束。|例句：The sheep were bunched together in the field. 羊群聚集在田野里。|近义词：clustered, grouped, gathered|反义词：scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From bunch + -ed.  Bunch originates from  the Old North French word “bonche” meaning a swelling, lump, or cluster.
