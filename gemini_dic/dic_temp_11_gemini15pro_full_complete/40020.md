consumer:/kənˈsuːmər/ (US) /kənˈsjuːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消费者，用户|A person who purchases goods and services for personal use.|例句：The consumer is always right. 顾客永远是对的。|近义词：customer, buyer, purchaser, shopper, client, user, end-user|反义词:producer, provider, supplier|noun

consumer:/kənˈsuːmər/ (US) /kənˈsjuːmə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|消费的，消费者的|Relating to consumers or consumption.|例句：Consumer spending is down this quarter. 本季度消费支出下降。|近义词：consumption-related, customer-related|反义词:production-related, supplier-related|adjective
