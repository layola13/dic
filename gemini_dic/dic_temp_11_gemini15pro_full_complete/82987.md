gunneries:/ˈɡʌn(ə)riz/
noun
炮术；炮兵学；炮兵部队
The science or art of managing guns; the emplacement of guns.  Also, a collection or body of gunners.
The coastal gunneries were reinforced during the war. 战争期间加强了海岸炮兵。
Synonyms: artillery, ordnance
Antonyms: disarmament, pacifism
词性: noun
