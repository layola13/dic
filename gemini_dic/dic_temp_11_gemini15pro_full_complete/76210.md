fustiness:/ˈfʌstɪnəs/
|n.|霉味，腐臭味|A stale, musty smell, often associated with dampness, lack of ventilation, and old age.|The fustiness of the basement made me cough.|近义词: mustiness, staleness, moldiness, dampness|反义词: freshness, airiness, sweetness|词性:名词

词源:
From the adjective "fusty," which comes from the Middle French "fusté," meaning "tasting of the cask."  Over time, the meaning shifted from the taste of wine stored too long in a cask to the smell of things that are old, damp, and not aired out.
