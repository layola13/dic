enterography:/ˌɛntəˈrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |小肠造影术|A technique for examining the small intestine by taking X-ray images after a contrast agent is swallowed or administered through a nasogastric tube. |Example 1: The doctor ordered an enterography to evaluate the patient's chronic abdominal pain. 医生为评估病人慢性腹痛而安排了小肠造影术。

Example 2: Enterography is a useful tool for diagnosing Crohn's disease. 小肠造影术是诊断克罗恩病的有用工具。| 近义词: small bowel imaging | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
