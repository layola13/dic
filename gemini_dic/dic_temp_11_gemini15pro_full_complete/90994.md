hurtling:/ˈhɜːrtlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɜːtlɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|猛冲，飞驰，快速移动|Moving with great speed, typically in a reckless or uncontrolled manner.  It implies a sense of force and often danger or destruction.|The car was hurtling down the hill. 汽车飞驰下山。|The meteor was hurtling towards Earth. 流星正朝着地球飞驰而来。|近义词：speeding, rushing, flying, careening, plummeting|反义词:crawling, creeping|词性:动词
