copeman:/ˈkəʊpmən/ (UK) /ˈkoʊpmən/ (US)| n.|商人，小贩；经销商|A trader, especially one who buys and sells goods. |例句：The copeman travelled from town to town selling his wares.  这个小贩走街串巷地卖他的货物。

例句：The copeman at the market had a wide variety of goods for sale.  市场上的小贩出售各种各样的商品。|近义词：merchant, trader, dealer, vendor, salesman|反义词: buyer, customer|词性:名词
