orbitar:/ɔrbiˈtar/ (Spanish), /ˈɔːrbɪtər/ (English if used as a noun for a spacecraft)| v.| 轨道运行，绕...运行|To revolve around a celestial body; to move in an orbit.  It comes from the Latin "orbita" meaning track or rut.|例句：El satélite orbita la Tierra.  The satellite orbits the Earth. 卫星绕地球运行。

例句：La Luna orbita alrededor de la Tierra. The moon orbits around the Earth. 月球绕地球运行。|近义词：girar, rotar, circle, revolve|反义词: N/A|词性: verb


/ˈɔːrbɪtər/ (English)| n.| 轨道飞行器，人造卫星 |A spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body. |例句：The Mars Orbiter captured stunning images of the red planet. 火星轨道飞行器捕捉到了这颗红色星球的惊人图像。

例句：The lunar orbiter will study the moon's surface. 月球轨道飞行器将研究月球表面。|近义词：satellite, spacecraft|反义词: N/A|词性: noun (only in English)
