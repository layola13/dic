membraneous:/ˌmɛmˈbreɪnəs/| adj.|膜的；膜状的|Relating to, consisting of, or resembling a membrane. |The illness caused a membraneous growth in his throat.|近义词：membranous, filmy, webby|反义词: nonmembranous|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *membranaceus, from Latin membrana (“membrane, parchment”) + -aceus.
