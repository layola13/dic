demolishments: /dɪˈmɒlɪʃmənts/ | n. |拆除，拆毁（通常指建筑物）|The act of demolishing something, or the state of being demolished.  It refers to the destruction of buildings or other structures.| The city council approved the demolitions of several derelict buildings. 市议会批准了拆除几栋废弃建筑。|synonyms: destructions, dismantling, wrecking| antonyms: constructions, building, establishment| noun

demolish: /dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/ | vt. |拆除，拆毁，推翻（论点等）| To completely destroy a building, especially in order to use the land for something else. It can also mean to thoroughly defeat someone in an argument or competition.| They demolished the old factory to make way for a new shopping mall. 他们拆除了旧工厂，以便为新的购物中心腾出空间。| synonyms: raze, destroy, level, flatten | antonyms: build, construct, erect | verb
