deera: /ˈdɪərə/ | n. |  雌鹿 (尤指赤鹿或黇鹿) | A female deer, especially a red deer or fallow deer. |  例句：The deera gracefully leaped over the fallen log.  雌鹿优雅地跳过了倒下的原木。|例句: The hunter spotted a deera and her fawn in the meadow. 猎人发现草地上有一只雌鹿和它的小鹿。 |近义词：doe, hind |反义词: buck, stag |词性: 名词

词源：
From Middle English *deere, dere, from Old English dēor (“animal, beast; deer”), from Proto-Germanic *deuzą (“animal”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰ₂éws- (“animal”). Cognate with Scots deir (“deer”), West Frisian dier (“animal”), Dutch dier (“animal”), German Tier (“animal”), Danish dyr (“animal”), Swedish djur (“animal”), Icelandic dýr (“animal”). More at deer.
