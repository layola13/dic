displayable:/dɪˈspleɪəbl/
adjective
可展示的，可显示的
Suitable or intended for being displayed.
The artwork is displayable in the gallery. 这件艺术品可以在画廊里展出。
This information is displayable on the screen.  这些信息可以显示在屏幕上。
近义词:showable, viewable, presentable,exhibitable
反义词:hidden, concealed, undisplayable
词性:adjective
