garniture:/ˈɡɑːrnɪtʃər/ /ˈɡɑːrnɪtər/| n.| 装饰品，配菜，(尤指肉食的)配菜蔬菜|Something added for ornament or embellishment; a trimming, garnish; specifically, vegetables or herbs served with a meat dish.|The roast chicken came with a simple garniture of roasted potatoes and carrots. 烤鸡配以简单的烤土豆和胡萝卜作为配菜。|近义词:garnish, trimming, decoration, embellishment|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:noun

garniture:/ˈɡɑːrnɪtʃər/ /ˈɡɑːrnɪtər/| n.| (家具的)全套配件，成套用品|A complete set of furnishings or accessories, especially for a room or table.|The antique garniture on the mantelpiece included a clock and two matching candelabra. 壁炉架上的古董装饰品包括一个钟和两个配套的烛台。|近义词:set, suite, collection, ensemble|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源：
