halogen:/ˈhælədʒən/ /ˈheɪlədʒən/ | n. |卤素|A nonmetallic element, such as fluorine, chlorine, bromine, iodine, and astatine, that forms a salt by direct union with a metal.|Fluorine is the most reactive halogen. 氟是最活泼的卤素。|近义词: nonmetal |反义词: metal |词性:noun

halogen:/ˈhælədʒən/ /ˈheɪlədʒən/ | adj. |卤素的|Of or relating to halogens.|Halogen lamps are very bright. 卤素灯很亮。|近义词: nonmetallic |反义词: metallic |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *halogène*, from Greek *hals* "salt" + *-gen* "producing."
