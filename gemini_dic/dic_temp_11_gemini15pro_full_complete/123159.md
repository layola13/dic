monkeyishness:/ˈmʌŋkiɪʃnəs/
noun
猴性；猴气；顽皮
The quality of resembling a monkey in behavior, particularly in being mischievous, playful, or imitative.
His monkeyishness got him into trouble again. 他的顽皮又让他惹上了麻烦。

近义词: mischievousness, playfulness, silliness, impishness, prankishness
反义词: seriousness, maturity, solemnity

词性: noun (名词)

词源: From monkey + -ishness.  "Monkey" comes from Middle Low German *moneke, perhaps influenced by the name Moneke given to the ape in Reynard the Fox. "-ishness" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "the state or quality of being," "action or practice of," "degree of resemblance to."
