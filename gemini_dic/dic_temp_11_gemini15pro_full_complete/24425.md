brisky:/ˈbrɪski/| adj.|活泼的，轻快的，兴隆的|Energetic, lively, and quick; marked by much activity.|The wind was brisk and cold. 风又冷又硬。|近义词：quick, lively, energetic, vigorous, active|反义词：sluggish, slow, languid|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *brisk, from Old French *brisque ("nimble, lively"), perhaps of Germanic origin, compare Old High German *brisk ("broken, brittle").
