connaturalness:/kəˈnætʃrəlnəs/
|n.|天然性；同质性；固有性|The state of being connatural; sharing the same nature.|His connaturalness with the land was evident in his deep respect for nature. 他对土地的天然亲和力体现在他对自然的深深敬重中。|
|近义词：innateness, inherentness, naturalness|反义词：artificiality, unnaturalness|词性: noun|
|词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin connaturalitas, from connaturalis (see connatural).|
