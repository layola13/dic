nonthinkings:/nɒnˈθɪŋkɪŋz/ | n. |  不动脑筋的人；缺乏思考的人 |  People who do not engage in critical thinking or reflection; those who accept information or ideas without questioning them.  |  The nonthinkings just follow the crowd. 不动脑筋的人只会随波逐流。 |  conformists, sheep, followers |  critical thinkers, intellectuals | noun

nonthinking:/nɒnˈθɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 缺乏思考的；不动脑筋的 | Lacking thought or consideration; unreflective. |  His nonthinking response angered the teacher. 他不动脑筋的回答激怒了老师。 |  unreflective, thoughtless, uncritical |  thoughtful, reflective, critical | adjective
