fakirism:/fəˈkɪərɪzəm/| n.|苦行主义；托钵僧的修行方式|The practice of a fakir; extreme asceticism, especially involving the endurance of physical hardship.|He chose a life of fakirism, renouncing material possessions and embracing poverty. 他选择了一种苦行僧式的生活，放弃物质财富，拥抱贫穷。|Near synonyms: asceticism, austerity, self-denial|Near antonyms: hedonism, indulgence, luxury|noun

fakir:/fəˈkɪər/| n.|托钵僧；苦行僧|A Muslim (or, loosely, a Hindu) religious ascetic who lives by begging.|The fakir sat cross-legged, meditating in the marketplace.  托钵僧盘腿坐在市场里冥想。|Near synonyms: ascetic, mendicant, monk|Near antonyms: materialist, hedonist|noun
