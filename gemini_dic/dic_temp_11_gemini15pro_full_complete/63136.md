ensnares:/ɪnˈsneərz/
verb
使…陷入圈套；使…落入陷阱
To catch or trap something, typically in a difficult or unpleasant situation.
The hunter ensnares rabbits with traps. 猎人用陷阱捕捉兔子。
The complexities of the legal system can ensnare unsuspecting individuals. 复杂的法律体系可能会使毫无戒心的人落入圈套。
近义词:trap, catch, tangle, entangle
反义词:release, free, liberate
词性:v.


ensnare:/ɪnˈsneər/
noun
陷阱,圈套 (罕用)
A trap or snare.
The spider's web is a deadly ensnare for flies. 蜘蛛网对苍蝇来说是致命的陷阱。 (这个用法比较文学化和少见)
近义词:trap, snare, pitfall
反义词:freedom, liberation
词性:n.
