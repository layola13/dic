metasomatism:/ˌmɛtəˈsoʊmətɪzəm/ | n. |交代作用；变质交代作用|The process of alteration of a rock by hydrothermal or other fluids that remove or introduce chemical constituents.  The original rock may retain its texture while its mineral composition is changed.| 例句1：Metasomatism played a significant role in the formation of this ore deposit. 交代作用对这个矿床的形成起了重要作用。

例句2：The skarn formed through metasomatism between the igneous intrusion and the limestone.  这个矽卡岩是火成岩侵入体和石灰岩之间通过交代作用形成的。|近义词：alteration, replacement |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
