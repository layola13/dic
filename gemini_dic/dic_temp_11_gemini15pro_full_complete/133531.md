nonfiduciary: /ˌnɑːn.fɪˈduː.ʃi.ɛr.i/ /ˌnɒn.fɪˈdʒuː.ʃər.i/ | adj. n. | 非信托的；非受信托人 |  Not holding, involving, or requiring trust, especially in a financial matter; not in a fiduciary capacity.  A person who is not a fiduciary. |  The investment advisor acted in a nonfiduciary capacity, so they weren't legally obligated to act in my best interest.  She hired a nonfiduciary financial planner. | 近义词: non-trustee | 反义词: fiduciary | 词性: adjective, noun
