long-sufferingly:/ˌlɔːŋˈsʌfərɪŋli/
| adv. | 长期受苦地，忍耐地 | In a manner that demonstrates prolonged patience and endurance, especially in the face of hardship or provocation.  | She long-sufferingly endured his constant complaints. 她长期忍受着他不断的抱怨。| 近义词: patiently, tolerantly, forbearingly | 反义词: impatiently, intolerantly | 词性: 副词

long-suffering:/ˌlɒŋˈsʌfərɪŋ/
| adj. | 长期受苦的，忍耐的 | Having or showing patience in spite of troubles or suffering. | He was a long-suffering husband with a nagging wife.  他是一位有个唠叨妻子的长期受苦的丈夫。| 近义词: patient, tolerant, forbearing | 反义词: impatient, intolerant | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From "long" + "suffering,"  describing someone who suffers for a long time.
