monkly: /ˈmʌŋkli/
adjective
像僧侣的；僧侣般的
Resembling or characteristic of a monk; monastic.
He led a monkly life of prayer and contemplation. 他过着祈祷和冥想的僧侣般的生活。
近义词: monastic, ascetic, cloistered
反义词: worldly, secular
词性: adjective

词源:  From monk + -ly.  "Monk" comes from Old English "munuc," from Late Latin "monachus," and ultimately from Greek "monakhos," meaning "solitary," from "monos," meaning "alone." The suffix "-ly" is an adverbial and adjectival suffix meaning "like" or "characteristic of."
