levitator:/ˈlevɪteɪtər/| n.|悬浮器；使悬浮的人或物|A device or person that causes something to levitate.  Something that lifts or causes something else to rise and float in the air, seemingly defying gravity.|The magician, a skilled levitator, astonished the audience by making his assistant float in mid-air.|近义词：suspender, floater|反义词:sinker|noun
