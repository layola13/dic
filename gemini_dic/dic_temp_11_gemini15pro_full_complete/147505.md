overpainful: /ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnfəl/ | adj. | 过于痛苦的，极度痛苦的 | Excessively painful; causing extreme pain.  Formed by the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively) and the adjective "painful". | The loss of her child was overpainful to bear. 失去孩子让她痛不欲生。| The surgery was overpainful, even with anesthesia. 即使打了麻药，手术还是非常痛苦。 | 近义词: excruciating, agonizing, unbearable | 反义词: painless, comfortable | 词性: 形容词
