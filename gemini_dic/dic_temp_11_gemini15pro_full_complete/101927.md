islanding:/ˈaɪləndɪŋ/
n.
孤岛效应，孤岛运行
The condition in which a distributed generation (DG) system continues to energize a portion of the electric power system after being disconnected from the main utility grid.
The safety system prevented islanding of the solar panels.  安全系统防止了太阳能电池板的孤岛效应。
isolation, separation
connection, integration
noun
