megalocyte: /ˈmeɡələˌsaɪt/ | n. | 巨红细胞 | An abnormally large red blood cell.  A megalocyte is characteristic of pernicious anemia and other disorders. | Example 1: The blood smear showed a number of megalocytes, suggesting a vitamin B12 deficiency.  血液涂片显示了许多巨红细胞，提示维生素B12缺乏。

Example 2: Megalocytes are often oval-shaped rather than the typical biconcave disc shape of normal red blood cells. 巨红细胞通常呈椭圆形，而不是正常红细胞典型的双凹圆盘状。 | Synonym: macrocyte | Antonym: microcyte | noun
