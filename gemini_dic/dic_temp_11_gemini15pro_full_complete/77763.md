genealogist:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒɪst/ | n. |系谱学家；家谱学者| A person who studies family history and ancestry, tracing lineages and relationships. |  例句：The genealogist helped me trace my family back to the 17th century.  系谱学家帮我追溯了我的家族史到17世纪。
