cancerate: /ˈkænsəˌreɪt/
verb
癌变; 溃烂

The mole began to cancerate after prolonged sun exposure.  这颗痣在长时间暴露在阳光下后开始癌变。
The wound on his leg began to cancerate due to infection. 他腿上的伤口由于感染开始溃烂。

近义词:  malignize,  ulcerate, fester
反义词: heal, recover
词性: v.
