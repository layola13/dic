lac:/læk/ | n. |虫漆; 紫胶;  |A resinous secretion of a scale insect, used to make shellac and varnish.| The antique table was finished with several coats of lac.  这古董桌子涂了好几层虫胶。 | synonyms: shellac, varnish | antonyms: none | noun

lac:/læk/ | v. |(诗/文) 缺乏；缺少；不足(尤指金钱) |To be without or deficient in something needed or desirable, especially money.|  He lacked the funds to start his own business. 他缺乏启动自己事业的资金。| synonyms: lack, want, need | antonyms: have, possess, abound | verb
