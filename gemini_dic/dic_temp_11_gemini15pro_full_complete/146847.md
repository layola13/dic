overindividualism:/ˌōvərˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm/
n.|过度个人主义|Excessive emphasis on individual rights and self-reliance, often at the expense of community or collective well-being.|The rise of overindividualism has led to a decline in social cohesion.|近义词:egoism, self-centeredness, narcissism|反义词:collectivism, altruism|词性:名词

Etymology:
over- (prefix) + individualism (noun)

"over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much"
"individualism" comes from "individual" (Latin individuus, meaning "indivisible") + "-ism" (suffix denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice).
