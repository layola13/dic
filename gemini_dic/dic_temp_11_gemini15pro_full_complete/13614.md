attesting:/əˈtɛstɪŋ/
|v. adj.|证明；证实；表明|Providing or serving as clear evidence of something.|The document, attesting to his birth in 1920, was found in the archives. 这份证明他1920年出生的文件在档案馆被找到。|Synonyms: certifying, confirming, corroborating, validating, verifying|Antonyms: denying, disproving, contradicting, refuting|Verb

attesting:/əˈtɛstɪŋ/
|adj.|证明的；证实的|Serving as evidence.|The attesting signature was smudged. 证明签字模糊不清了。|Synonyms: certifying, confirming, verifying|Antonyms: denying, refuting|Adjective
