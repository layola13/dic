deadener:/ˈdɛdnər/ | n. | 消音器，减震器，抑制剂 | Something that deadens or reduces sound, vibration, or intensity. |  The car manufacturer used new deadeners in the doors to reduce road noise. 汽车制造商在车门中使用了新的消音器来减少道路噪音。| synonyms: muffler, silencer, damper, suppressor, cushion, mute | antonyms: amplifier, intensifier | noun

deadener:/ˈdɛdnər/ | n. | 缓冲物，缓和剂 | Something that lessens the force or effect of something unpleasant. |  The thick carpet acted as a deadener for the sound of footsteps. 厚厚的地毯减轻了脚步声。| synonyms: buffer, cushion, mitigator, palliative | antonyms:  intensifier, aggravator | noun
