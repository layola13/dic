incogitant:/ɪnˈkɒdʒɪtənt/ | adj. | 缺乏思考的；不注意的；粗心大意的 | Not thinking; inconsiderate; heedless. | He made an incogitant remark that offended several people. 他说了一句欠考虑的话，冒犯了几个人。 |  The incogitant driver ran the red light. 粗心大意的司机闯了红灯。 | 近义词: thoughtless, careless, heedless, inattentive, unmindful | 反义词: thoughtful, considerate, mindful, attentive, careful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *incogitans*, present participle of *incogitare* (“not to think, not to consider”), from *in-* (“not”) + *cogitare* (“to think”).
