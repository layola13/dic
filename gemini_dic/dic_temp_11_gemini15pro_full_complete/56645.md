donnishness:/ˈdɒnɪʃnəs/ /ˈdɑːnɪʃnəs/| n.|学究气；书呆子气；迂腐|The quality of being donnish; pedantic or bookish behavior.  A display of excessive academic knowledge or a preoccupation with scholarly pursuits, often perceived as aloof or condescending. |He displayed a certain donnishness in his pronouncements on the subject. 他在这个问题上的言论显示出某种学究气。|近义词：pedantry, bookishness, academicism, intellectualism|反义词: worldliness, practicality, informality|词性: noun
