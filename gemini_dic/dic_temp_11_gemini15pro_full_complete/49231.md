delegator:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtər/| n.|委托人，授权者|A person who delegates or authorizes another to act as one's representative.|She acted as a delegator for her busy boss. 她作为她忙碌的老板的委托人行事。|近义词：representative, agent, proxy, envoy, commissioner|反义词：delegate, recipient|noun

delegator:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtər/| n.|代表，代表团成员|A person sent or authorized to represent others, in particular an elected representative sent to a conference.|He was a delegator at the national convention. 他是全国代表大会的代表。|近义词：representative, delegate, envoy, commissioner|反义词：constituent|noun
