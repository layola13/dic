misawarding:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrdɪŋ/
verb
错误授予，误判
Giving an award to the wrong person or for the wrong reason.
The committee was accused of misawarding the prize to a less deserving candidate. 委员会被指责将奖项错误地授予了一位不太合格的候选人。
misallocating, misjudging
rightfully awarding, correctly awarding
词性:动词
