lootings: /ˈluːtɪŋz/
n.
掠夺，抢劫(行为)
The act of stealing goods from a place, typically during a riot or war.
The lootings following the hurricane left many shops bare.  飓风过后发生的抢劫使得许多商店空空如也。
The museum was ransacked, and the lootings included priceless artifacts.  博物馆遭到洗劫，被掠夺的物品包括无价的文物。
plundering, pillaging, ransacking
restitution, restoration
noun
