expositional:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəl/
adjective
说明性的，解释性的
serving to expound or set forth; explanatory
The first chapter is expositional, laying out the basic principles of the theory.  第一章是说明性的，阐述了该理论的基本原理。
expositional writing is often found in textbooks. 教材中经常出现说明性的写作。
近义词:explanatory, descriptive, illustrative, expository
反义词:implicit, suggestive, allusive
