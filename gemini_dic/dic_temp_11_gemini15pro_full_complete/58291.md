drusus:/ˈdruːsəs/
n.
druse (晶簇)
A drusus is a crust of small crystals coating a rock cavity or surface.
Drusus formations are often found in geodes. 晶簇构造经常出现在晶洞中。
Drusus can be composed of various minerals, including quartz and amethyst. 晶簇可以由各种矿物组成，包括石英和紫水晶。
Near synonyms: druse, crystal cluster, crystal aggregation
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a geological formation)
