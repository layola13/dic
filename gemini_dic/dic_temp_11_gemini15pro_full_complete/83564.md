haemal:/ˈhiːməl/ | adj.| 血的，血管的，与血液循环有关的 | Relating to blood or the circulatory system, specifically the blood vessels. | The haemal arch of a fish vertebra encloses the caudal artery and vein. 鱼椎骨的血管弓包围着尾动脉和尾静脉。| The haemal system in some invertebrates is responsible for circulating nutrients and oxygen. 一些无脊椎动物的血管系统负责循环营养物质和氧气。 | synonyms: circulatory, vascular, sanguineous | antonyms: non-vascular, avascular | adjective
