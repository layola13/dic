incapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ |v. |封装；概括；压缩|To enclose something in a capsule or other protective covering; to summarize or condense something.|The artist incapsulated the dried flowers in resin. 艺术家用树脂封装了干花。|近义词：enclose, encapsule, summarize, condense, epitomize|反义词: release, expand, elaborate|词性:动词

incapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ |adj. |被封装的|Enclosed in a capsule or other protective covering.|The incapsulated documents were found inside the statue. 被封装的文件在雕像内被发现。|近义词：enclosed, encased|反义词: exposed, open|词性:形容词
