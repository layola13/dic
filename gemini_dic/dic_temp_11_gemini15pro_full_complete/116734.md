mauler:/ˈmɔːlər/| n.| 殴打者，痛打者 |A person who mauls or beats someone severely. | The boxer was a real mauler in the ring.  这个拳击手在拳台上是个十足的狠角色。| synonyms: batterer, thug, brute, assailant | antonyms: protector, defender | noun

maul:/mɔːl/| v.| 粗暴地对待，痛打；撕咬；严厉批评 | To handle roughly or clumsily; to beat or mangle; to criticize harshly. | The lion mauled its prey. 狮子撕咬它的猎物。

The critics mauled the new play.  评论家严厉批评了这出新戏。| synonyms: batter, beat, mangle, savage, criticize, lambaste | antonyms: protect, defend, praise, commend | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *mailer, probably from mal “badly” (from Latin male) + -er (agent suffix).  Related to the English word "maltreat."  The verb "maul" comes from the noun.
