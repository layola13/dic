alliterative:/əˈlɪtərətɪv/
adjective
押头的，头韵的
Characterized by alliteration; using alliteration.  Containing or using the same consonant or sound at the beginning of adjacent or closely connected words.
Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers is an alliterative tongue twister. 彼得·派珀摘了一小撮腌辣椒，这是一个押头的绕口令。
The author employed alliterative phrases to create a sense of rhythm. 作者使用押头短语来营造节奏感。
synonyms:  assonant, rhyming, poetic
antonyms:  prosaic, unrhymed
词性: adjective
