daglock:/ˈdæɡlɒk/| n.|缠结的羊毛，羊毛块；困境，僵局|Matted wool on a sheep, especially around the tail, that becomes soiled with dung; a difficult or awkward situation.|The sheep was struggling with a daglock caught on a bush. 这只羊的尾巴被一块缠结的羊毛挂在灌木丛上，它正在挣扎。|The negotiations reached a daglock, with neither side willing to compromise. 谈判陷入了僵局，双方都不愿妥协。|近义词：tangle, snarl, deadlock, impasse|反义词:solution, agreement|词性:名词

词源:  From 'dag' (meaning a piece of dung or excrement clinging to wool) + 'lock' (a portion of wool or hair).
