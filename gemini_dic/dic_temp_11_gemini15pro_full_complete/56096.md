doctorate: /ˈdɑːktərət/ (US) /ˈdɒktərət/ (UK) | n. | 博士学位 |  The highest degree awarded by a university.  A doctorate typically signifies that the recipient has completed original research and made a significant contribution to their field of study. |  She earned her doctorate in physics. 她获得了物理学博士学位。 |  She finally received her doctorate after years of research.  经过多年的研究，她终于获得了博士学位。 | 近义词: PhD, doctoral degree | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Bachelor's degree as a contrasting lower degree) | 词性: noun
