lakeweed:/ˈleɪkwiːd/
n.
湖草，水草
Aquatic plants that grow in lakes.
Lakeweed can clog waterways. 湖草会阻塞水道。

Lakeweed choked the shallows of the lake. 湖草阻塞了湖的浅滩。
近义词:waterweed, aquatic plant
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially terrestrial plant)
词性:noun
