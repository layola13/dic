outbud:/ˌaʊtˈbʌd/| v. |发芽早于；比...先发芽|To produce buds earlier than (another plant).|The rose outbudded all the other flowers in the garden. 这朵玫瑰比花园里所有其他的花都先发芽。|近义词：precede, outgrow|反义词: lag behind|词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "bud" (verb meaning "to put forth buds").
