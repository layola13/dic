badiaga:/bædɪˈɑːɡə/
n.|淡水海绵骨骼粉|A powder made from the dried skeleton of a freshwater sponge (Spongilla lacustris), used topically as a cosmetic and to treat bruises.|She used badiaga to reduce the swelling from her black eye.|Synonyms:  sponge spicules,  (for effect) arnica, comfrey |Antonyms:  None (lacks a direct opposite; could consider anti-inflammatory as a contrasting concept)|noun
