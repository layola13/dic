fictionalizers:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzərz/
n.
小说家；虚构者；小说作者
People who create fictional narratives or characters.  Those who transform real events or people into fiction.
The fictionalizers often draw inspiration from their own lives.  这些小说家经常从自己的生活中汲取灵感。
novelists, storytellers, writers, fabulists
realists, historians, biographers
noun
