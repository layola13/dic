hats:/hæts/ | n. | 帽子 | A covering for the head, often worn for protection from the elements, warmth, or as a fashion accessory. |  She wore a wide-brimmed hat to protect herself from the sun. 她戴了一顶宽檐帽来遮阳。

The store sells a variety of hats, from baseball caps to fedoras. 这家商店出售各种各样的帽子，从棒球帽到软呢帽。| synonyms: caps, headwear, bonnets | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object, could consider "bareheadedness" as a state) | noun
