intercomplexities:/ˌɪntərˌkəmˈpleksɪtiz/ | n. |相互的复杂性，错综复杂的关系 |The intercomplexities of the global economy make it difficult to predict future trends. 全球经济的错综复杂性使得难以预测未来趋势。|  The intercomplexities of human relationships are a source of both joy and frustration. 人际关系的复杂性既是快乐的源泉，也是沮丧的根源。| 近义词: complexities, intricacies | 反义词: simplicities | 词性: 名词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "complexity" (the state of being complex). "Complexity" comes from the Latin "complexus," meaning "embraced, woven together."
