larcenist:/ˈlɑːrsənɪst/ | n. |小偷，窃贼 |A person who commits larceny; a thief. | The larcenist was caught red-handed stealing jewelry. 小偷在偷珠宝时被当场抓获。|  The larcenist made off with a valuable painting. 小偷偷走了一幅珍贵的画。 |Synonyms: thief, robber, burglar, pilferer, shoplifter | Antonyms: law-abiding citizen, police officer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From larceny + -ist.  Larceny comes from Anglo-French *larcin, from Old French larrecin "theft, robbery," of uncertain origin. Possibly from Latin latrocinium "robbery, freebooting, highway robbery."
