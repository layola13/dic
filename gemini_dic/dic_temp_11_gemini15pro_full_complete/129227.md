noctambulist: /nɒkˈtæmbjʊlɪst/ /nɑːkˈtæmbjələst/
n.
梦游症患者；夜游者
A person who walks about in their sleep.
例句：The noctambulist had no recollection of his nightly wanderings.  梦游症患者不记得自己夜间的游荡。

例句：She was a known noctambulist and often woke up in strange places. 她是一个已知的梦游者，经常在陌生的地方醒来。

近义词：sleepwalker, somnambulist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


noctambulant: /nɒkˈtæmbjʊlənt/ /nɑːkˈtæmbjələnt/
adj.
梦游的；夜游的
Walking or moving about at night.
例句：Noctambulant animals are often more active during the night. 夜行动物在夜间通常更活跃。

例句：His noctambulant habits worried his family.  他梦游的习惯让他的家人很担心。

近义词：sleepwalking, somnambulant
反义词: diurnal
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *noctambulus*, from *nox* (night) + *ambulō* (I walk).
