caballer:/ˌkæbəˈlɪər/ | n. | 骑士；武士；彬彬有礼的绅士 | A horseman, especially a knight.  A courtly gentleman. | He behaved like a true caballer, opening doors and offering his arm. 他表现得像个真正的绅士，开门并搀扶着她。 | 近义词: knight, gentleman, cavalier | 反义词: boor, lout | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cavalier* (horseman), from Late Latin *caballarius* (horseman), from Latin *caballus* (horse). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *keb-*, meaning "to gallop."
