chimeras:/kaɪˈmɪərəz/ /kɪˈmɪərəz/| n.|嵌合体；幻想；虚幻的念头|A fire-breathing female monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail.  一个狮头、羊身、蛇尾的喷火女怪。|The artist's creations were strange chimeras, blending human and animal forms.  这位艺术家的创作是奇特的嵌合体，融合了人类和动物的形态。|近义词：illusion, fantasy, delusion|反义词：reality, truth|词性:noun

chimerical:/kaɪˈmɪrɪkəl/ /kɪˈmɪrɪkəl/| adj.|幻想的；虚幻的；荒诞不经的|His plans to become a millionaire were entirely chimerical. 他想成为百万富翁的计划完全是幻想。|The idea of a perpetual motion machine is chimerical. 永动机的想法是荒诞不经的。|近义词：illusory, fanciful, unreal|反义词：realistic, practical, real|词性:adjective
