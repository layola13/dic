forgetting:/fərˈɡetɪŋ/| n.|遗忘，忘记|the act of forgetting something or someone; the failure to remember something or someone|例句：Forgetting is a natural process. 遗忘是一个自然的过程。

例句：Her forgetting my birthday upset me. 她忘记我的生日让我很伤心。|近义词：oblivion, amnesia, lapse of memory|反义词：remembering, recalling|词性:名词 (Noun)
