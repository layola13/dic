outstorms:/ˈaʊt.stɔːrmz/
verb
超过风暴; 以更大的风暴胜过
To surpass or overcome in intensity, especially a storm or other natural phenomenon.
The hurricane outstormed all previous records for wind speed.  这场飓风的风速超过了以往所有记录。
After days of relentless rain, the sun finally outstormed the clouds.  经过连日不断的降雨，太阳终于战胜了乌云。
surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip
- (No clear antonym exists, consider "be outstormed by", "succumb to")
