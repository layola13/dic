forktail:/ˈfɔːkteɪl/ | n. | 叉尾鸟，燕尾 | A bird of the Old World flycatcher family (Muscicapidae) typically having a long forked tail. |  The forktail darted through the forest, catching insects in mid-air.  叉尾鸟在森林中飞来飞去，在半空中捕捉昆虫。

The grey-sided forktail is a common sight in the Himalayas. 灰胁叉尾鸟在喜马拉雅山脉很常见。| 近义词: fantail | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific bird type) | 词性: noun
