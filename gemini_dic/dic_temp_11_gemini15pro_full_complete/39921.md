constraint:/kənˈstreɪnt/ | n. | 限制；约束；强制 | Something that limits or restricts someone or something. |  Budget constraints forced them to cut staff. 预算限制迫使他们裁员。

The constraints of time and money affected the project. 时间和金钱的限制影响了这个项目。 | synonyms: restriction, limitation, restraint, curb, check | antonyms: freedom, liberty, license | noun
