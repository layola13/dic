horopter:/ˈhɔːrɑːptər/ | n. | 视界线，同视点 | The locus of points in space whose images fall on corresponding points on the two retinas of the eyes, and which are therefore seen as single. |  The object remained on the horopter as she moved her head. 当她移动头部时，物体保持在视界线上。|  The horopter allows us to perceive depth and distance. 视界线使我们能够感知深度和距离。| synonyms: visual plane, visual horizon | antonyms: N/A | noun
