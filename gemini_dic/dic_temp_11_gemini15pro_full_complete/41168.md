copycatism:/ˈkɒpiˌkætɪzəm/ | n. | 模仿主义，抄袭行为 | The act of copying or imitating someone else's work, behavior, or style, often without understanding or adding any original contribution. |  His latest design is just blatant copycatism. 他的最新设计只是公然的抄袭。 | Synonyms: imitation, mimicry, emulation, plagiarism (for written work) | Antonyms: originality, innovation, creativity | noun
