divulsive:/daɪˈvʌlsɪv/| adj.|分裂的，导致分裂的；抽搐的|Tending to pull apart; causing division or separation. Also, characterized by or causing convulsions.|The divulsive effects of the civil war tore the country apart. 内战的分裂作用使国家四分五裂。|近义词： disruptive, divisive, tearing, convulsive|反义词： unifying, cohesive, binding|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *divulsivus*, from divulsus, past participle of divellere "to tear apart," from di- "apart" + vellere "to pluck, tear."
