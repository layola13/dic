crop-out:/ˈkrɑːp aʊt/ (US) /ˈkrɒp aʊt/ (UK)
|n.|露出地面的岩层；露出地表的部分|A part of a rock formation or mineral deposit that appears at the surface of the earth.|The granite crop-out offered a stunning view of the valley. 花岗岩露头处可以欣赏到山谷的壮丽景色。|outcrop, showing|buried, concealed|noun
crop-out:/ˈkrɑːp aʊt/ (US) /ˈkrɒp aʊt/ (UK)
|v. (intr.)|露出地面；显露|To appear or become visible at the surface of the earth.|The vein of gold cropped out near the river. 金矿脉在河附近露出地面。|appear, emerge, surface|disappear, vanish|verb
