minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/| n.| 最小量; 一滴; 微量; (英国药量单位)一滴; (音乐)二分音符|A minim is a very small amount of liquid, equal to one drop. |The doctor prescribed a minim of the medicine. 医生开了极小剂量的药。|Synonyms: drop, bit, speck, iota|Antonyms: maximum, abundance|noun

minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/| n.| (音乐)二分音符 |In music, a minim is a note that has half the time value of a semibreve. |The piece was full of minims and crotchets. 这首曲子充满了二分音符和四分音符。|Synonyms: half note (US)|Antonyms: semibreve, whole note (US)|noun
