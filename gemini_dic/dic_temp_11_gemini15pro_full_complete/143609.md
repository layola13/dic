outinvented:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛntɪd/
verb
在发明创造方面胜过; 比...更具创造力
surpassed in inventiveness; invented something better than
Company A outinvented Company B with their new smartphone technology. A公司凭借其新的智能手机技术在发明创造方面胜过了B公司。
outmaneuver, surpass, outdo
