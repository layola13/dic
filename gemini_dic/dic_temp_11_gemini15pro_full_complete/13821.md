auditioners:/ɔːˈdɪʃənərz/
n.
试镜者，参加试演的人
People who try out for a role in a play, film, or other performance.
Many aspiring actors were among the auditioners for the new Broadway musical. 许多有抱负的演员都在百老汇新音乐剧的试镜者中。
candidates, applicants, hopefuls, aspirants, tryouts
rejectors, non-participants
noun
