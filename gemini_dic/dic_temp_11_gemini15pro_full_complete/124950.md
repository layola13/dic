muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/| v. n.|裹住；抑制；使声音降低|To wrap or cover something in order to keep it warm, protect it, or deaden a sound; to make a sound quieter or less distinct.|She muffled her throat with a scarf. 她用围巾裹住了她的喉咙。|近义词：stifle, suppress, mute, deaden|反义词：amplify, increase, enhance|词性:动词 & 名词
muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/| n.|围巾，包裹物（尤指厚的）|Something that muffles, especially a thick wrapping.|The thick mufflers on the exhaust pipes reduced the noise of the engine. 排气管上厚厚的消音器降低了发动机的噪音。|近义词：wrap, covering, scarf|反义词：-|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *mofler "to wrap up the face," of uncertain origin.
