nonurban:/ˌnɒnˈɜːrbən/
adjective
非城市的，乡村的
Not situated in a city or town; rural.
Many nonurban areas are losing basic services like post offices and shops. 许多非城市地区正在失去邮局和商店等基本服务。
The study focused on the healthcare challenges faced by nonurban populations. 这项研究的重点是非城市人口面临的医疗保健挑战。
近义词：rural, country, rustic
反义词：urban, city, metropolitan
词性：adjective

词源：
Prefix "non-" (not) + "urban" (of or relating to a city or town).  The prefix "non-" is derived from Latin.  "Urban" comes from Latin "urbanus."
