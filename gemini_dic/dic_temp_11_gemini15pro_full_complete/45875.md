cute: /kjut/ | adj. | 可爱的，漂亮的，聪明的 | Delightfully pretty or attractive.  | She has a cute little dog. 她有一只可爱的小狗。 |  The baby has such a cute smile. 这个婴儿的笑容真可爱。 | 近义词: adorable, charming, lovely, pretty, sweet | 反义词: ugly, unattractive, hideous | 词性:形容词

词源:
From earlier coot, from Middle Dutch *coet.  Related to Scots cuit (“ankle”), West Frisian koet (“ankle”), and possibly to Middle Low German kūt (“short; small; little”).
