overofficiously: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分殷勤地，过分正式地 | Excessively officious; too eager to offer unwanted help or advice, often in a way that is annoying or intrusive.  Characterized by excessive zeal and intrusiveness in offering one's services or performing duties. | He overofficiously corrected her grammar, making her feel uncomfortable. 他过分殷勤地纠正她的语法，让她感到不舒服。|  The hotel staff was overofficiously attentive, constantly interrupting our conversations. 酒店工作人员过分殷勤，不断打断我们的谈话。 | 近义词: officiously, meddlesomely, intrusively, fussily | 反义词: helpfully (when help is wanted), discreetly, unobtrusively | 词性: 副词
