non-sustainable:/ˌnɒn səˈsteɪnəbəl/ (US) /ˌnɒn səˈsteɪnəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可持续的|Not able to be maintained at the current rate or level.  Unable to continue over a period of time.|例句：The current rate of deforestation is non-sustainable.  目前的森林砍伐速度是不可持续的。

例句：Their lifestyle is non-sustainable in the long term. 他们的生活方式从长远来看是不可持续的。|近义词：unsustainable, non-viable, short-sighted|反义词:sustainable, viable, long-term|词性:adjective
