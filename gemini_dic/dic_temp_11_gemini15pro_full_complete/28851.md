carpet:/ˈkɑːrpɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːpɪt/ (UK)| n. v.|地毯，覆盖|A heavy woven fabric used for covering floors or stairs.  As a verb, it means to cover something with a carpet.|例句：The carpet in the living room is plush and soft. 客厅里的地毯毛茸茸的，很柔软。|近义词：rug, mat, floor covering|反义词: bare floor|noun
carpet:/ˈkɑːrpɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːpɪt/ (UK)|v.|铺以地毯|To cover something with a carpet.|例句：They carpeted the entire upstairs. 他们把整个楼上都铺上了地毯。|近义词：cover, floor|反义词: uncover, expose|verb
