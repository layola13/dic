edginesses:/ˈɛdʒɪnɪsɪz/
n.
锐利；尖锐；锋利;  紧张；急躁
Sharpness or pointedness of something, either literally or figuratively; A state of nervousness or irritability.
The edginesses of the cliffs made hiking dangerous. 悬崖的陡峭使得徒步旅行很危险。
There was a definite edginess in his voice. 他的声音里明显带着一丝急躁。
近义词: sharpness, pointedness, tension, nervousness, irritability
反义词: smoothness, bluntness, calmness, composure
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
