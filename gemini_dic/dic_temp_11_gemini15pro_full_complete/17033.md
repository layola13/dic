batfowler:/ˈbætˌfaʊlər/| n.|捕蝙蝠的人；捕鸟的人|A person who catches bats or birds, especially as a profession or for sport. |In the old days, a batfowler would use nets to capture bats in caves.  在过去，捕蝙蝠的人会用网在洞穴里捕捉蝙蝠。| The batfowler climbed the cliffs at dusk, hoping to catch some seabirds. 捕鸟人在黄昏时爬上悬崖，希望能抓到一些海鸟。|近义词：birdcatcher, fowler|反义词:conservationist|noun
