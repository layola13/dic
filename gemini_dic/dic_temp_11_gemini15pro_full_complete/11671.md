armoire:/ɑːrˈmwɑːr (US), ɑːˈmwɑː (UK)| n.|衣橱，衣柜|A large wardrobe or movable cupboard, typically one that is ornate or antique.  A tall piece of furniture, often having doors and shelves or drawers, used for storage, especially of clothing.|例句：The antique armoire held her grandmother's wedding dress.  这个古董衣橱里放着她祖母的结婚礼服。

例句：They purchased a beautiful armoire for their bedroom. 他们为他们的卧室买了一个漂亮的衣橱。|近义词：wardrobe, closet, cupboard|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *armoire*, from Old French *armaire*, from Latin *armarium* ("a chest, box for tools or arms"), neuter of *armarius* ("of or belonging to tools or arms"), from *arma* ("tools, arms").
