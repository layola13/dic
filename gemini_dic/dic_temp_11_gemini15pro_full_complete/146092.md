overeffort:/ˌoʊvərˈɛfərt/| n.|过度努力，用力过猛|An excessive expenditure of effort.  The act of trying too hard, often leading to negative consequences such as fatigue, stress, or injury.|He suffered from burnout due to overeffort at work. 他因为工作过度劳累而精疲力竭。|近义词：overexertion, strain, overwork|反义词:underexertion, relaxation|词性:名词

词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "effort" (exertion of physical or mental power).
