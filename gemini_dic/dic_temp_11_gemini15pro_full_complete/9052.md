antimedicine:/ˈæntiˌmedɪsɪn/| n. |反医学; 对医学的反对; 反对医学的观点或实践|Opposition to or distrust of conventional medicine, often accompanied by the promotion of alternative therapies.|Some people's antimedicine stance prevents them from seeking necessary medical care.  有些人对医学的反对立场阻止了他们寻求必要的医疗护理。|Example:  The rise of antimedicine sentiment is concerning for public health officials.  反医学情绪的兴起令公共卫生官员担忧。|近义词：alternative medicine, complementary medicine, holistic medicine, natural medicine|反义词:conventional medicine, orthodox medicine, mainstream medicine|词性:noun
