manifestations:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. | 表现；显示；示威活动 | An event, action, or object that clearly shows or embodies something, especially a theory or an abstract idea. | The fever is a manifestation of the underlying infection. 发烧是潜在感染的一种表现。

The political manifestations against the new law turned violent.  反对新法律的政治示威演变成了暴力事件。 | synonyms:  signs, symptoms, indications, demonstrations, displays, expressions, embodiments | antonyms:  concealments, suppressions,  hidden | noun
