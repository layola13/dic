noninfectious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛkʃəs/
adjective
非传染性的
Not capable of being transmitted through infection.
The doctor assured her that the rash was noninfectious. 医生向她保证皮疹不会传染。
non-contagious, noncommunicable
infectious, contagious
