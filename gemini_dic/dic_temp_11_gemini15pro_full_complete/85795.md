haywire:/ˈheɪwaɪər/| adj., adv.|混乱的，故障的；乱七八糟地|out of control; in a state of disorder|例句：The computer system went haywire after the power outage.  停电后，计算机系统变得混乱不堪。|近义词：chaotic, disordered, malfunctioning, awry|反义词：orderly, functional, working|词性:形容词, 副词

haywire:/ˈheɪwaɪər/| n.|(通常用复数)控制装置中的乱七八糟的金属丝|a jumble of wires, especially in a control apparatus|例句：The plane's controls were a tangle of haywire. 飞机的控制装置是一团乱麻般的金属丝。|近义词：tangle, mess|反义词：order|词性:名词
