ergs:/ɜːrɡz/
noun|尔格（能量单位）|A unit of work or energy equal to 10^-7 joules.|The energy required to lift a small weight a short distance can be measured in ergs.|近义词: joules|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek ergon, meaning "work."
