bloating:/ˈbloʊtɪŋ/| n.|腹胀，肿胀|A condition of being swollen with fluid or gas.|After eating too much, I experienced bloating. 吃得太多后，我感到腹胀。|近义词：swelling, distension|反义词: deflation, shrinking|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *bloten, from Old Norse blautr (“soft, wet”).
