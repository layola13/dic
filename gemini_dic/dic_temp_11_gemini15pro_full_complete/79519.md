globous:/ˈɡləʊbəs/ (US) /ˈɡloʊbəs/| adj.|球形的，球状的|Spherical in shape; globe-like.  Resembling a globe or sphere.|The planet appeared globous through the telescope. 通过望远镜，这颗行星看起来呈球形。|近义词：spherical, round, globular, orbicular|反义词：flat, planar|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin "globosus," from "globus" (globe).
