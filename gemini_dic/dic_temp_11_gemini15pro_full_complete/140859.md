on-the-house:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈhaʊs/ | adj. |免费的，店家赠送的 | Given free by a business, especially a bar or restaurant. | The drinks were on the house tonight. 今晚的饮料是免费的。| 近义词: complimentary, free, gratis | 反义词: paid, charged | adjective

on-the-house:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈhaʊs/ | adv. |免费地，店家赠送地 | In a way that is given free by a business, especially a bar or restaurant. | The appetizers were served on the house.  开胃菜是免费提供的。| 近义词: complimentarily, freely, gratis | 反义词: expensively | adverb
