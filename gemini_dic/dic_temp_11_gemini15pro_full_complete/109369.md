lensers:/ˈlɛnzərz/| n.|透镜效应；引力透镜效应|The phenomenon of light bending around massive objects, like galaxies, creating multiple images of the source object.  This effect is predicted by Einstein's theory of general relativity.|Astronomers observed multiple images of the distant quasar due to gravitational lensing by an intervening galaxy. 天文学家由于中间星系的引力透镜效应观测到了遥远类星体的多重图像。|Synonyms: gravitational lensing, lensing effect|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

lensers:/ˈlɛnzərz/| n.|戴眼镜的人；配戴隐形眼镜的人|People who wear eyeglasses or contact lenses to correct their vision.|Many lensers find that their vision improves significantly with corrective lenses. 许多戴眼镜的人发现他们的视力通过矫正镜片得到了显著改善。|Synonyms: eyeglass wearers, contact lens wearers, spectacle wearers|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
