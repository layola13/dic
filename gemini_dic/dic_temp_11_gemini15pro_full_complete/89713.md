hope-chest:/ˈhəʊp tʃɛst/| n.|希望之箱；嫁妆箱|A chest in which a young woman collects clothing, linens, etc., in anticipation of marriage.|Her grandmother had started a hope chest for her when she was just a little girl.|Synonyms: dowry chest, trousseau chest, bridal chest|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"hope" (Old English hopa) + "chest" (Old English cest, from Latin cista)
