overemployed: /ˌōvərɪmˈplɔɪd/ | adj. | 过度就业的；工作过量的 | Having more than one full-time job or working significantly more hours than a typical full-time position requires, often due to economic necessity or a desire for increased income.  It can lead to burnout and reduced productivity. |  Example: She's overemployed, juggling two full-time jobs to make ends meet. 她过度就业，同时做两份全职工作才能维持生计。

Example:  Feeling overemployed and stressed, he decided to quit one of his jobs.  感到过度就业和压力很大，他决定辞掉一份工作。 | 近义词: overworked, overburdened | 反义词: underemployed, unemployed | 词性: 形容词
