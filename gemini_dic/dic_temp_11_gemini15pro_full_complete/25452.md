bulldoggers:/ˈbʊldɔːɡərz/
n.
牛仔竞技者；牧牛者；摔牛者
A person who competes in bulldogging, a rodeo event in which a rider on horseback chases a steer, jumps from the horse onto the steer, grabs its horns, and wrestles it to the ground.
The bulldoggers were getting ready for their event at the rodeo. 牛仔竞技者们正在为牛仔竞技表演中的摔牛比赛做准备。
近义词:steer wrestlers, rodeo cowboys
反义词:None
词性:名词
