indo-british:/ˌɪndoʊˈbrɪtɪʃ/
adjective
印英的；英印的
Relating to both India and Britain, or to the relations between the two countries.  Specifically, often referring to the period of British rule in India or the cultural exchange between the two.
The Indo-British relationship has a complex history. 印英关系有着复杂的历史。
The museum houses a collection of Indo-British art.  博物馆收藏了印英艺术品。
Synonyms: Anglo-Indian
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider purely Indian or purely British)
词性: adjective
