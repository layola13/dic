acinose: /ˈæsɪnoʊs/ | adj. | 腺泡状的；似葡萄的 | Resembling a cluster of grapes; shaped like an acinus.  | The pancreas has an acinose structure. 胰腺具有腺泡状结构。|  The salivary glands are acinose glands. 唾液腺是腺泡状腺体。 |  grapelike, glandular | N/A | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *acinōsus, from acīnus (“grape, berry”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of, abounding in, like, of the nature of").
