mucking:/ˈmʌkɪŋ/
verb
清除；清理（尤指马厩）;瞎弄；胡搞;浪费时间
Cleaning out a stable or other area of animal waste; messing around; wasting time.
例句：He spent the morning mucking out the stables. 他上午一直在清理马厩。

例句：Stop mucking about and get on with your work! 别瞎混了，继续干你的活！

近义词：cleaning, clearing, messing, fiddling, dawdling
反义词：organizing, tidying

词性:v.

mucking:/ˈmʌkɪŋ/
noun
清除；清理（尤指马厩）;瞎弄；胡搞;浪费时间

例句：The mucking out of the stables is a daily chore. 清理马厩是每天的例行工作。

例句：All that mucking about achieved nothing.  所有这些瞎胡闹都一无所获。
