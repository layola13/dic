inert:/ɪˈnɜːrt/ (US) /ɪˈnɜːt/ (UK)| adj.|惰性的；迟钝的；无活动的|Lacking the ability or strength to move or act; chemically inactive.|例句：The inert gas argon is used in welding. 惰性气体氩气用于焊接。

例句：He lay inert on the floor. 他一动不动地躺在地板上。|近义词：inactive, passive, motionless, dormant|反义词：active, lively, dynamic, reactive|词性:形容词

inertness:/ɪˈnɜːrtnəs/ (US) /ɪˈnɜːtnəs/ (UK)| n.|惰性；迟钝|The state of being inert.|例句：The inertness of the noble gases makes them useful in many applications. 惰性气体的惰性使它们在许多应用中很有用。

例句：His inertness worried his friends. 他的迟钝让他的朋友们担心。|近义词：inactivity, passivity|反义词：activity, dynamism|词性:名词
