instills:/ɪnˈstɪlz/| v. |灌输；逐渐 instill 的第三人称单数形式|gradually establishes (an attitude, feeling, or idea) in someone's mind.|The coach instills a sense of discipline in his players. 教练向他的球员灌输纪律意识。|近义词：implant, inculcate, infuse, introduce|反义词：eradicate, remove, extract|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin instillare "to drip in," from in- "in" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "a drop."
