conjecturing:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərɪŋ/
| n. | 推测，猜想 | the formation or expression of an opinion or theory without sufficient evidence for proof. |  Scientists are still conjecturing about the origin of the universe. 科学家们仍在推测宇宙的起源。 | speculation, theorizing, hypothesizing | certainty, knowledge | noun
conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/
| v. | 推测，猜想 | form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information. | I can only conjecture about his motives. 我只能推测他的动机。 | surmise, guess, speculate | know, ascertain | verb
