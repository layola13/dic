coppled:/ˈkɒpəld/| adj.|倾覆的，倒塌的，摇摇欲坠的|Overturned; tumbled down.  Often used to describe something unstable or about to collapse.|The coppled wall looked like it would fall any minute.  摇摇欲坠的墙看起来随时都会倒塌。|The earthquake left many coppled buildings in its wake. 地震过后，许多建筑物倒塌了。| 近义词：collapsed, overturned, toppled, tumbled|反义词: upright, stable|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "copple," which is a variant of "topple."  Ultimately from a Germanic root related to "top."
