bloodwort:/ˈblʌdwɜːrt/| n.|血根草，红根草|A North American plant (Sanguinaria canadensis) with a red root and sap, white flowers, and toothed leaves.|The bloodwort is known for its medicinal properties. 血根草以其药用价值而闻名。|Synonyms: bloodroot, red puccoon, tetterwort|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bloodwort:/ˈblʌdwɜːrt/| n.|红门兰|A Eurasian orchid (Nigritella nigra) with dark red to almost black flowers.|The bloodwort orchid is a rare and protected species. 红门兰是一种稀有且受保护的物种。|Synonyms: vanilla orchid|Antonyms: N/A|noun
