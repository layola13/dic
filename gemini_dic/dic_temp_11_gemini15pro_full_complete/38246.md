computerless:/kəmˈpjuːtərləs/
adjective
无计算机的；没有计算机的
Without a computer; not using or having computers.
Many small businesses were computerless a few decades ago.  几十年前，许多小企业都没有计算机。
The library offers resources for computerless patrons.  图书馆为没有电脑的用户提供资源。
近义词:non-computerized, technologically backward, offline
反义词:computerized, technologically advanced, online
词性:adjective
