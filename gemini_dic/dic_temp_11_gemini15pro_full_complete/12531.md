asphyxia:/æsˈfɪksiə/
n.|窒息|a condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocation.|The victim died of asphyxia. 受害者死于窒息。|Synonyms: suffocation, choking, strangulation|Antonyms: respiration, breathing
asphyxias:/æsˈfɪksiəz/
n.|窒息（复数）|plural form of asphyxia, referring to multiple instances of the condition.|The report detailed several asphyxias caused by smoke inhalation. 报告详细描述了几个由吸入烟雾引起的窒息病例。|Synonyms: suffocations, chokings, strangulations|Antonyms: respirations, breathings
