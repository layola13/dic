infantes:/ɪnˈfæntiːz/
noun
婴儿（复数）,步兵(复数)
Children in the first year of life are called infantes.  生命的第一年的孩子被称为婴儿。
The Spanish infantes fought bravely. 西班牙步兵英勇作战。
Synonyms: babies, toddlers, infants (singular), foot soldiers
Antonyms: adults, elders, veterans, cavalry
