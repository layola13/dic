bloopy:/ˈbluːpi/| adj.|糊状的，粘稠的；不成形的|Resembling or having the consistency of bloop, a thick, semi-liquid substance.  Often used informally or humorously to describe something shapeless or amorphous.|The monster left a trail of bloopy slime.  怪物留下了一串粘糊糊的黏液。|近义词：gooey, slimy, viscous, gloppy|反义词:solid, firm, dry|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely a recently coined word, possibly onomatopoeic, evoking the sound or appearance of a thick liquid dropping or spreading.  No established etymology exists.
