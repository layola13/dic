/ˈmɒdɪfaɪəz/ (US) /ˈmɑːdɪfaɪərz/
| n. | 修饰语 | A modifier is a word, phrase, or clause that describes another word or group of words.  It provides more information or detail about the word it modifies. |  Adjectives, adverbs, and many phrases and clauses can act as modifiers. 例如：The *red* car is fast. （红色的车很快。）| Synonyms: qualifier, descriptor | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


/ˈmɑːdɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪfaɪ/
| vt. | 修改，修饰；更改，改变 | To make partial or minor changes to something, typically so as to improve it or to make it less extreme. |  He modified his car to make it faster. 他改装了他的车，让它跑得更快。| Synonyms: alter, change, adapt, adjust, amend | Antonyms: maintain, preserve, keep | Verb


/ˈmɑːdɪfaɪd/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪfaɪd/
| adj. | 修改的，修饰的 | Changed or altered, typically in a minor way. |  The recipe is a modified version of the original.  这个食谱是原版的修改版本。| Synonyms: altered, changed, adapted, adjusted, amended | Antonyms: original, unchanged, unaltered | Adjective
