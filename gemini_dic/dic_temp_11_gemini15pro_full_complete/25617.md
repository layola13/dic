bumpkinly:/ˈbʌŋkɪnli/| adj.|土气的，乡巴佬的|Of or relating to a bumpkin; rustic; unsophisticated.  Having the characteristics of a country bumpkin; awkward and unsophisticated.|His bumpkinly manners embarrassed her at the fancy restaurant.|近义词：rustic, unsophisticated, awkward, clumsy, boorish, countrified|反义词：sophisticated, refined, urbane, polished, elegant
