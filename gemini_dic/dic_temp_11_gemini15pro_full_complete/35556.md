coalgoose:/ˈkoʊlˌɡuːs/| n. |白额雁|A large goose (Branta leucopsis) with a black neck, breast, and back, and a white face and underparts. It breeds in the Arctic and winters in northwestern Europe.|例句：We saw a flock of coalgoose flying south for the winter. 我们看到一群白额雁向南飞去过冬。

例句：The coalgoose is a migratory bird. 白额雁是一种候鸟。|近义词：barnacle goose|反义词:|名词
