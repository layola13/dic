glossography:/ɡlɒˈsɒɡrəfi/ |ɡlɑːˈsɑːɡrəfi/| n. |词汇表；术语表；（某一学科或作者用语的）词汇汇编|A glossography is a specialized dictionary that explains terms used in a particular field of study or by a particular author. |This book includes a comprehensive glossography of medical terms. 这本书包含一份全面的医学术语词汇表。|
|Synonym: glossary, lexicon, vocabulary |Antonym: N/A |noun|

glossography:/ɡlɒˈsɒɡrəfi/ |ɡlɑːˈsɑːɡrəfi/| n. |注释；注解|Glossography can also refer to the practice of providing annotations or explanations for words or texts. |The scholar's glossography of the ancient manuscript provided valuable insights into its meaning.  这位学者对古代手稿的注释提供了对其意义的宝贵见解。|
|Synonym: annotation, commentary, explanation|Antonym: N/A|noun|
