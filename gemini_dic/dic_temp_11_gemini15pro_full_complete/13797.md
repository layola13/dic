audiometry:/ˌɔːdiˈɒmɪtri/ (US) /ˌɔːdiˈɑːmətri/ (UK)| n. |听力测验，听力计检查|The science of measuring hearing acuity.  It involves a series of tests using specialized equipment to determine the type and degree of hearing loss.|The doctor recommended audiometry to assess the extent of his hearing damage. 医生建议进行听力测验以评估他听力损伤的程度。|audiologic testing, hearing test|deafness, hearing impairment|noun
