bangers:/ˈbæŋərz/ | n. | 香肠；（非正式）好听的歌曲；精彩的事物 |  Sausages, especially good quality ones. (UK, informal) Excellent songs or things. |例句：We had bangers and mash for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了香肠和土豆泥。

例句：These new headphones are bangers! 这款新耳机太棒了！ | 近义词：sausages (for food), hits (for songs), great things | 反义词: duds (for songs/things) | 词性: 名词

banger:/ˈbæŋər/ | n. | 香肠；旧车；爆竹 | A sausage. An old car. A firecracker. | 例句：He threw a banger into the bonfire. 他把一个爆竹扔进了篝火。

例句：My old car is a real banger, but it still runs. 我的旧车真是辆破车，但还能开。 | 近义词：sausage, old car, firecracker | 反义词: new car | 词性: 名词
