hireling:/ˈhaɪərlɪŋ/ | n. | 雇佣兵；被雇佣者（尤指为钱而做不光彩或危险工作的人） | A person who is hired, especially to do unpleasant or dangerous work for money; a mercenary. |  He dismissed his staff as a bunch of lazy hirelings.  他把他的员工斥为一群懒惰的雇工。 | Synonyms: mercenary, soldier of fortune, condottiere, hack, yes-man | Antonyms: volunteer, philanthropist, altruist | noun

hireling:/ˈhaɪərlɪŋ/ | adj. | 雇佣的；唯利是图的 |  Motivated solely by money; mercenary. | The politician made a hireling speech, clearly pandering to his donors. 这位政客做了一个唯利是图的演讲，显然是在迎合他的捐赠者。 | Synonyms: mercenary, venal, corrupt | Antonyms: selfless, altruistic, principled | adjective
