gloppen:/ˈɡlɒpən/ | v. | 溅，泼，滴 | To make a sound like something thick and liquid being dropped or poured.  |  The mud gloppened onto the floor. 泥巴啪嗒啪嗒地掉在地板上。 | 近义词: plop, splash, drip | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "absorb") | 词性: 动词

词源:  Likely of imitative origin, echoing the sound it describes.  Similar to words like "glop" and "plop."
