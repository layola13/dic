hystricine:/ˈhɪstrɪsaɪn/ |n.| 豪猪箭毒碱 |A paralytic alkaloid obtained from certain South American arrow poisons.|  The indigenous people used hystricine, extracted from a local plant, to tip their hunting arrows. 当地土著使用从当地植物中提取的豪猪箭毒碱来涂抹他们的狩猎箭。|Synonyms: curare, toxin |Antonyms: antidote, remedy |noun
