noncommunal:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnl/
adjective
非公共的，非社区的
Not shared by or involving all members of a community; private.
例句：They lived in noncommunal apartments. 他们住在私人公寓里。
例句：He preferred a noncommunal workspace to avoid distractions. 他更喜欢一个独立的办公空间以避免分心。
近义词：private, individual, separate, independent
反义词：communal, shared, collective, public
词性: adjective
