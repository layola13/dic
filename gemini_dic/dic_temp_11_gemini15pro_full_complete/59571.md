ebon:/ˈɛbən/| adj.|乌木制的，黑檀木的；乌黑的，漆黑的|Made of ebony; dark black in color. |Her hair was ebon. 她的头发乌黑如墨。|近义词：black, dark, jet-black|反义词：white, light, pale|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *eben, from Old French ebene, from Latin ebenus, from Greek ebenos, of Semitic origin; akin to Hebrew ’ebony.
