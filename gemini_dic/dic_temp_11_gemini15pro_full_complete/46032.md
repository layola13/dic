cyborg: /ˈsaɪbɔːrɡ/ (US), /ˈsaɪbɔːɡ/ (UK)| n. | 半机械人；电子人 | A fictional or hypothetical person whose physical abilities are extended beyond normal human limitations by mechanical elements built into the body. | The cyborg in the movie had superhuman strength.  电影中的电子人拥有超人的力量。|  Synonyms: bionic human, android, robot | Antonyms: human, organic being | noun
