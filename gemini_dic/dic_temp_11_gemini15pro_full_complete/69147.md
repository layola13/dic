favouring:/ˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ /ˈfeɪvə rɪŋ/| adj.|偏袒的，有利的，良好的|showing or giving an advantage to someone or something, especially unfairly; conducive to something good or desirable | The current economic climate is favouring investors. 当前的经济环境对投资者有利。|近义词：advantageous, beneficial, preferential, biased, partial |反义词: unfavourable, disadvantageous, detrimental, impartial, unbiased
favouring:/ˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ /ˈfeɪvə rɪŋ/| v.|偏袒，有利于|(present participle of favour) to support or prefer one particular possibility |  The policy seemed to favour the richer families. 这项政策似乎偏袒富裕家庭。 |近义词：supporting, promoting, encouraging, preferring |反义词: opposing, hindering, discouraging, neglecting
