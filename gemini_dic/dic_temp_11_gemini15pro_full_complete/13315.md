athletism: /ˈæθləˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 运动能力，体育运动，竞技体育 | The quality of being athletic; physical strength, agility, and endurance. |  He possessed natural athleticism and excelled at various sports.  他拥有天生的运动能力，并且擅长各种体育运动。|  synonyms: athletic ability, sportsmanship, physical prowess | antonyms:  inactivity, lethargy, clumsiness | noun


Etymology:  From French *athlétisme*, from Ancient Greek  *ἆθλος* (âthlos, “contest, struggle, prize of a contest”) +  *-ισμός* (-ismós).
