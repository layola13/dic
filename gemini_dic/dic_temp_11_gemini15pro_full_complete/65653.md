evaporations:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃənz/
noun
蒸发；蒸发量
the process of a liquid changing to a gas, especially by heating
The evaporations from the lake create a humid atmosphere. 湖水的蒸发造成了潮湿的大气。
evaporation, vaporization
condensation, solidification
