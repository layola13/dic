disfavorably:/dɪsˈfeɪvərəbli/
adverb
不利地，不适宜地
in a way that is not favorable or positive; negatively.
The new policy was viewed disfavorably by the public. 这项新政策受到了公众的负面评价。
The economic downturn affected the company disfavorably. 经济衰退对公司产生了不利影响。

近义词:unfavorably, negatively, adversely, poorly
反义词:favorably, positively, well, advantageously
词性:adverb
