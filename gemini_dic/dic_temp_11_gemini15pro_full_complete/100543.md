interweave:/ˌɪntərˈwiːv/ | v. |交织，穿插，编织|blend or cause to blend together; intermingle. |The story interweaves elements of fantasy and reality.  这个故事交织着幻想和现实的元素。| The tapestry interweaves gold and silver threads. 这幅挂毯交织着金线和银线。 |近义词：intertwine, interlace, blend, merge|反义词：separate, disentangle, unravel|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *interweven*, from Old English *interwefan* (“to interweave”), equivalent to *inter-* + *weave*. Cognate with Dutch *interweven*, German *interweben*.
