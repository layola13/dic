enclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/ /ɛnˈkloʊʒər/| n.|围场，附件，封入之物|Something that is enclosed, such as a fence, wall, or other barrier, or the area enclosed by such a barrier.  Also, a document or object included with a letter or package. |The sheep were safe in the enclosure. 羊在围栏里很安全。|Synonyms: compound, paddock, fence, wall, barrier, insert, attachment|Antonyms: opening, exterior, freedom|noun

enclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/ /ɛnˈkloʊʒər/| n.|附件|A document or object included with a letter or package. |Please find the requested information in the enclosure.  请在附件中找到所需信息。|Synonyms: attachment, supplement, addendum|Antonyms: omission, exclusion|noun
