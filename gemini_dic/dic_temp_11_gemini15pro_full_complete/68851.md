fashioner:/ˈfæʃənər/| n.|塑造者，设计者，创造者|Someone who fashions or creates something, especially clothes or accessories.|The fashioner of this dress has a keen eye for detail. 这件衣服的设计者对细节有着敏锐的眼光。|近义词：designer, creator, maker, stylist|反义词：destroyer, demolisher|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "fashion" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Fashion" comes from Old French "façon" meaning "make, manner, shape," ultimately from Latin "factio"  meaning "a making, a doing."
