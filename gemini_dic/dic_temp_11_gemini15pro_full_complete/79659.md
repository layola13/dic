glossopharyngeal:/ˌɡlɒsəfəˈrɪndʒiəl/ /ˌɡlɑːsəfəˈrɪndʒiəl/| adj.|舌咽的；舌咽神经的|Relating to the tongue and pharynx.|The glossopharyngeal nerve controls the muscles of the throat and tongue. 舌咽神经控制喉咙和舌头的肌肉。|近义词:pharyngoesophageal|反义词:None|词性:adjective

glossopharyngeal:/ˌɡlɒsəfəˈrɪndʒiəl/ /ˌɡlɑːsəfəˈrɪndʒiəl/| n.|舌咽神经|The ninth cranial nerve, which supplies the tongue and pharynx.|Damage to the glossopharyngeal nerve can cause difficulty swallowing. 舌咽神经损伤会导致吞咽困难。|近义词:cranial nerve IX|反义词:None|词性:noun
