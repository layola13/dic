directee:/ˌdɪrɛkˈtiː/ /ˌdaɪrɛkˈtiː/
n.
被指导者，受指导者
a person who is given direction or guidance by someone in authority
The directee met with her mentor weekly to discuss her progress.  这位受指导者每周都会与她的导师见面讨论她的进展。
The program pairs experienced professionals with young directees. 该项目将经验丰富的专业人士与年轻的受指导者配对。
synonyms: trainee, mentee, protégé, pupil
antonyms: mentor, director, advisor, supervisor
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From direct + -ee (suffix forming nouns denoting one who receives or is affected by the action of the verb). "Direct" comes from the Latin word "dirigere," meaning "to guide" or "to steer."
