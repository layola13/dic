donate:/ˈdoʊneɪt/ (US), /dəʊˈneɪt/ (UK)| v. |捐赠，捐献|To give something, especially money, to a charity or other organization in order to help them.|He donated $100 to the Red Cross. 他向红十字会捐赠了100美元。|近义词：contribute, give, bestow|反义词: receive, take, accept|词性:动词

donation:/doʊˈneɪʃən/ (US), /dəʊˈneɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |捐赠，捐款，捐赠物|Something that is given to a charity or other organization, typically money.|The hospital received a large donation from an anonymous benefactor. 这家医院收到了一位匿名捐赠者的大笔捐款。|近义词：contribution, gift, offering|反义词: receipt, acquisition|词性:名词
