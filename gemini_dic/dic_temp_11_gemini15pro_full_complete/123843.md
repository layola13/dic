moralist:/ˈmɔːrəlɪst/| n.|道德家；卫道士|A person who teaches or promotes morality.|He's a bit of a moralist, always lecturing people on their behavior. 他有点像个道德家，总是教训别人该如何做人。|Synonyms: preacher, puritan, moralizer|Antonyms: immoralist, libertine|Noun
