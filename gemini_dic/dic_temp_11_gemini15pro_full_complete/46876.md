dargue:/ˈdɑːrɡjuː/ | v. | 争论，辩论 | To argue stubbornly or persistently, often about trivial matters. |  He spent the whole evening darguing with his wife about which TV show to watch. 他整晚都在和妻子争论看哪个电视节目。

He's always darguing about the smallest things. 他总是为鸡毛蒜皮的小事争论不休。| 近义词: argue, bicker, squabble, quibble | 反义词: agree, concur, concede | 词性: 动词
