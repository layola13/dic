nattier:/ˈnætiər/
adjective
更优雅的，更时髦的；更自然的
more stylish, elegant, or natural than something else
Her new dress is much nattier than her old one. 她的新裙子比旧的更时髦。
synonyms: smarter, more stylish, chicer, more elegant, fashionable
antonyms: dowdy, frumpy, drab, unfashionable
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From natty + -er (comparative suffix).  Natty itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from neat.
