kelpie:/ˈkɛlpi/ (UK) /ˈkelpi/ (US)| n. |水怪，凯尔派（苏格兰和爱尔兰民间传说中栖息在湖泊和河流中的变形水怪）|A kelpie is a shapeshifting water spirit inhabiting lochs and rivers in Scottish and Irish folklore, usually taking the form of a horse.|The kelpie lured the traveler into the river. 那匹水怪引诱旅行者进入了河里。|近义词：water horse, each-uisge, nixie|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源：From Scottish Gaelic *cailpeach* or *colpach*, meaning "colt" or "heifer".
