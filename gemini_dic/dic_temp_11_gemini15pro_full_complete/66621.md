exilic:/ˈɛksɪlɪk/ /ɛɡˈzɪlɪk/
adjective
流亡的；放逐的；与流放有关的
Relating to or characteristic of exile; banished.
The exilic community maintained their cultural traditions.  流亡群体保持着他们的文化传统。

The poet wrote poignant verses about his exilic experience.  这位诗人写了关于他流亡经历的凄美诗句。

synonym
banished, exiled, displaced, expatriated
antonym
native, indigenous, repatriated
词性
adjective
