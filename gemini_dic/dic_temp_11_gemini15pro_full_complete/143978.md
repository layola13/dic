outrages:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒɪz/
n.|暴行；骇人听闻的事件；侮辱；愤怒|An act of extreme violence or viciousness; a grave offense or insult.|The bombing was an outrage that shocked the world.  这起爆炸事件是一起骇人听闻的暴行，震惊了世界。|atrocities, offenses, insults|respect, kindness|noun
outrages:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒɪz/
v.|激怒；使震怒|To arouse intense anger, shock, or indignation in (someone).|The judge's decision outraged many people. 法官的判决激怒了很多人。|infuriates, enrages, angers|pleases, delights|verb
