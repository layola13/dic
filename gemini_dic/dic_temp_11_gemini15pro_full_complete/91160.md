hydra:/ˈhaɪdrə/ | n. |九头蛇; 水螅 | A many-headed serpent in Greek mythology that grew two heads for each one cut off. Also, a freshwater polyp with a tubular body and tentacles around the mouth. |  The hydra is a mythical beast known for its regenerative abilities.  九头蛇是一种神话中的野兽，以其再生能力而闻名。

Hercules slew the Lernaean Hydra. 海格力斯杀死了勒拿湖的九头蛇。| 近义词: monster, beast | 反义词: N/A | noun
