caor:/kɔːr/ | n. | peat; bog; fuel |A type of fuel similar to peat, often used in Ireland and Scotland.  Derived from decaying vegetation in bogs. | The islanders traditionally burned caor for warmth during the harsh winters. 岛民们传统上在严冬时燃烧泥炭来取暖。 | synonyms: peat, turf | antonyms: coal, wood | noun
