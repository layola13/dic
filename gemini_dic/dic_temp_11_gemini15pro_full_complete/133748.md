nongalactic:/ˌnɒnɡəˈlæktɪk/ | adj. | 银河系外的，河外星系的 | Located or originating outside the Milky Way galaxy. |  Example: Nongalactic astronomy studies celestial objects beyond our own galaxy.  河外星系天文学研究我们银河系以外的天体。

Example: The telescope detected nongalactic radiation. 望远镜探测到了河外辐射。 | Synonym: extragalactic | Antonym: galactic | Word type: adjective
