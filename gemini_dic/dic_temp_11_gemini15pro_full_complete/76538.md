gallanted:/ˈɡæləntɪd/| adj.|勇敢的，殷勤的，献殷勤的|Showing bravery or chivalry, especially toward women; courteous and attentive.|He gallanted her with flowers and compliments. 他用鲜花和赞美之词向她献殷勤。|近义词：chivalrous, courteous, attentive, polite|反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous|词性:形容词

词源: From French *galant*, present participle of *galer* "to rejoice, make merry," from Old French *galer* "to have fun, amuse oneself."
