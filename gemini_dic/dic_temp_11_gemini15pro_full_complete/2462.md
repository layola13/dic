adjustable:/əˈdʒʌstəbl/
adjective
可调节的，可调整的
Able to be changed to suit particular needs or conditions.
The height of the steering wheel is adjustable.  方向盘的高度是可以调节的。
The straps on the backpack are adjustable for comfort.  背包的带子可调节，以提高舒适度。
synonyms: adaptable, alterable, modifiable, variable
antonyms: fixed, inflexible, rigid, unchangeable
词性: adjective
