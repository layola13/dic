disputative: /dɪˈspjʊtətɪv/ /dɪˈspjuːtətɪv/
adjective
爱争论的，好辩的
inclined to dispute; argumentative
例句：He has a disputative nature and enjoys a good argument. 他天生爱争论，喜欢好好辩论一番。

例句：The disputative student challenged the professor's theory.  好辩的学生挑战了教授的理论。

近义词：argumentative, contentious, quarrelsome
反义词：agreeable, amiable, conciliatory
词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French disputatif, -ive or late Latin disputativus, from disputare ‘discuss, argue’.
