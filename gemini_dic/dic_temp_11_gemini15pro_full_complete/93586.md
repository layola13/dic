imbalance:/ɪmˈbæːləns/ | n. |不平衡，失衡 |lack of balance or equality between two things or sets of things. |例句：The imbalance between supply and demand caused prices to rise. 供需失衡导致价格上涨。

例句：There is a significant imbalance of power between the two countries. 这两个国家之间存在着显著的权力失衡。|近义词：disparity, inequality, asymmetry, disproportion |反义词：balance, equilibrium, symmetry, parity |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- "not" + balance (n.).
