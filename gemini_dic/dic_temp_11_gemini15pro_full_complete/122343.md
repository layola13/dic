mixer:/ˈmɪksər/ (US), /ˈmɪksə(r)/ (UK)| n.|搅拌机，混合器；调音师；社交场合|A device used for mixing substances, especially food ingredients, or for combining audio signals. Also, a person who mixes things, especially in social settings.|She used a mixer to blend the cake batter. 她用搅拌机搅拌蛋糕糊。|The sound mixer adjusted the levels for the band. 音响师调整了乐队的音量。|synonyms: blender, amalgamator, mingler, socializer|antonyms: separator, divider|noun

mixer:/ˈmɪksər/ (US), /ˈmɪksə(r)/ (UK)| n.|鸡尾酒|A drink made by mixing alcoholic beverages, usually with juice or soda.|He ordered a mixer from the bartender. 他向酒保点了一杯混合饮料。|She prefers mixers to straight liquor. 她喜欢混合饮料而不是纯酒。|synonyms: cocktail, mixed drink|antonyms: neat, straight|noun
