basto:/ˈbɑːstoʊ/
n.
棍棒，权杖，俱乐部(纸牌)
A club in card games, especially in Spain, Portugal, and Latin America. Also, a staff or cudgel.
The king held his basto as a symbol of power. 国王手持权杖作为权力的象征。
He played the basto of clubs. 他出梅花棍棒。
