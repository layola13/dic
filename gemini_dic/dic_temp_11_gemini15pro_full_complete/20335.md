bisilicate:/baɪˈsɪlɪkeɪt/ | n. | 双硅酸盐 | A salt containing two silicate tetrahedra, typically sharing an oxygen atom. | Example: Calcium bisilicate is a common component of cement.  硅酸钙是水泥的常见成分。| Example: The mineral wollastonite is a calcium bisilicate.  硅灰石是一种硅酸钙。 | Synonym: disilicate | Antonym: N/A | Noun
