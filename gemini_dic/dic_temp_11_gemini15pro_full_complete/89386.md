homoplasmy:/ˈhɒməʊplæzmi/ /ˈhoʊmoʊplæzmi/
noun
同质性，单一质型
The presence of only one type of mitochondrial DNA within a cell or individual.  This means all mitochondria in a cell or all cells in an organism contain the same mitochondrial DNA sequence.
Mitochondrial diseases are less severe in cases of homoplasmy compared to heteroplasmy.  线粒体疾病在同质性情况下比异质性情况下轻微。
Homoplasmy is the normal state for mitochondrial DNA.  同质性是线粒体DNA的正常状态。
近义词: uniformity, homogeneity
反义词: heteroplasmy
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From homo- (same, alike) + -plasmy (formation, substance)
