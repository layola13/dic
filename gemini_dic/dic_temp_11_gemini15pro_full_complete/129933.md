non-litigious:/ˌnɒn lɪˈtɪdʒəs/ | adj. | 非诉讼的，不爱打官司的 | Not inclined to engage in lawsuits; avoiding or settling disputes without court action. |  The company has a non-litigious approach to resolving disputes.  该公司采用非诉讼方式解决纠纷。| Example: They sought a non-litigious solution to the contract dispute. 他们寻求一种非诉讼的合同纠纷解决方案。| Synonyms: amicable, conciliatory, peaceable | Antonyms: litigious, contentious, quarrelsome | adjective
