myristate:/ˈmɪrɪsteɪt/| n. | 肉豆蔻酸盐；十四酸盐 |A salt or ester of myristic acid (tetradecanoic acid).  It is a saturated fatty acid with 14 carbon atoms. | Sodium myristate is a common ingredient in soap. 肉豆蔻酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。| Potassium myristate is also used in some cosmetics. 肉豆蔻酸钾也用于一些化妆品中。 |  palmitate, stearate |  | noun
