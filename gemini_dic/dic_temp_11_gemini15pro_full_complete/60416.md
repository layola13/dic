eighth:/eɪtθ/ | adj. n.| 第八的；八分之一 |  Number eight in a series; one of eight equal parts. |  She came in eighth in the race. 她比赛中获得了第八名。| synonyms: 8th | antonyms: first, last | adjective/noun

eighth:/eɪtθ/ | adj. n.| 八分之一的；八分之一 | One of eight equal parts of a whole. | He ate an eighth of the pizza. 他吃了披萨的八分之一。| synonyms: one-eighth | antonyms: whole, entire | adjective/noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *eahtoða (attested only as eahteoðan "eighth"), from eahta "eight". Cognate with Old High German ahtodo, Old Norse áttundi.
