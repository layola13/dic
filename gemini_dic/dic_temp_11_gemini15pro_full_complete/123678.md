moodish:/ˈmuːdɪʃ/| adj.|喜怒无常的；易怒的；闷闷不乐的|Subject to unpredictable changes of mood; temperamental.  Given to gloomy or sullen moods.|She's been very moodish lately, snapping at everyone for no reason. 她最近情绪很不稳定，无缘无故地对每个人都大发脾气。|Synonyms: temperamental, moody, capricious, fickle, erratic, mercurial|Antonyms: cheerful, good-humored, stable, even-tempered|adjective
