de-licensing:/diːˈlaɪs(ə)nsɪŋ/
n.
取消许可，撤销许可证
The act of removing or reducing government licensing requirements for a particular industry or activity.
The government is considering de-licensing the telecommunications industry. 政府正在考虑取消电信行业的许可证制度。
deregulation, liberalization
licensing, regulation
noun
