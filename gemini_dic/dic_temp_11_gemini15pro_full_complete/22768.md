borscht:/ˈbɔːrʃt/ (US), /ˈbɔːʃt/ (UK)| n. |罗宋汤|A beet soup of Russian and Ukrainian origin, usually served with sour cream.|例句：My grandmother makes the best borscht. 我祖母做的罗宋汤最好吃。

例句：We had a warming bowl of borscht on a cold winter evening.  在一个寒冷的冬夜，我们喝了一碗暖暖的罗宋汤。|近义词：beet soup|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific food)
