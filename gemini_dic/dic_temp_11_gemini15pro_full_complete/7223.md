anglicify: /ˈæŋɡlɪˌsaɪ/ /ˈæŋɡləˌsaɪ/
verb
使英语化;使英国化
To make English in form or character; to adapt to English usage.
Many immigrants anglicized their names to fit in better. 许多移民将他们的名字英语化，以便更好地融入社会。
The French word "rendezvous" has been completely anglicized. 法语单词“rendezvous”（约会）已经被完全英语化了。
synonyms: anglize, naturalize, adapt
antonyms: translate, foreignize
