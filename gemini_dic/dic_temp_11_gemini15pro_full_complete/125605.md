multivariate:/ˌmʌltɪˈveəriət/ | adj. | 多变量的; 多元的 | Involving multiple variables or factors.  Characterized by or involving more than one variate. | Multivariate analysis is used to study the relationships between multiple variables. 多变量分析用于研究多个变量之间的关系。|  multivariable, multiple | univariate, single-variable | adjective
