etiologists:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
病因学家；病原学家
Scientists who study the causes or origins of diseases.
The etiologists are working hard to identify the source of the new virus. 这些病原学家正在努力确定这种新病毒的来源。
The etiologists traced the outbreak back to contaminated water. 病因学家将疫情的爆发追溯到受污染的水。
synonyms:  pathologists, researchers, scientists
antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
词性: noun
