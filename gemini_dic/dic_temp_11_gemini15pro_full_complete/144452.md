outswirled:/aʊtˈswɜːrld/| v. |旋转而出，螺旋而出|To swirl outward; to move in a swirling motion outward.|The smoke outswirled from the chimney. 烟雾从烟囱里旋转而出。|The dancers outswirled across the stage. 舞者们旋转着穿过舞台。|近义词：spiral, whirl, twist, swirl out|反义词:spiral in, swirl in|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From out- + swirl.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating outward motion or direction. "Swirl" comes from Middle English *swerlen, of Scandinavian origin; akin to Old Norse *svirla “to whirl.”  First recorded use of "outswirl" is in the late 19th century.
