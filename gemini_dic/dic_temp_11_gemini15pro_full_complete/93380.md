illiberality:/ɪˌlɪbəˈrælɪti/
n.
吝啬；狭隘；缺乏雅量
lack of generosity; narrow-mindedness; intolerance
His illiberality was evident in his refusal to donate to the charity. 他的吝啬在他拒绝向慈善机构捐款中表现得很明显。
近义词: meanness, stinginess, bigotry, intolerance
反义词: generosity, liberality, tolerance, open-mindedness
词性: 名词


illiberal:/ɪˈlɪbərəl/
adj.
吝啬的；狭隘的；缺乏雅量的
not generous; narrow-minded; intolerant
She held some illiberal views on immigration. 她对移民持有一些狭隘的观点。
近义词: mean, stingy, bigoted, intolerant
反义词: generous, liberal, tolerant, open-minded
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *illiberalis, from in- "not" + liberalis "generous, noble".  Essentially the negative form of "liberal".
