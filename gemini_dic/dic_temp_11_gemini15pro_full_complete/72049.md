flirtatiousness:/ˌflɜːrˈteɪʃəsnəs/ /ˌflɝːˈteɪʃəsnəs/| n. |轻浮；调情；卖弄风情|The quality of being flirtatious; a tendency to flirt.  It suggests a playful and often superficial romantic or sexual interest in others.|Her flirtatiousness charmed everyone at the party. 她的轻浮迷住了派对上的每一个人。|Synonyms: coquetry, teasing, dalliance|Antonyms: seriousness, reserve, modesty|noun
