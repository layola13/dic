huzz:/hʌz/
interjection
欢呼声，喧闹声
A loud confused noise, typically of a crowd.
The fans made a tremendous huzz when the team scored. 球队得分时，球迷们发出了巨大的欢呼声。
近义词:cheer, clamor, uproar
反义词:silence, quiet
词性:感叹词
