combative:/kəmˈbætɪv/| adj.|好斗的，好战的|Ready or eager to fight or argue.|例句：He was in a combative mood after the meeting.  会议结束后，他情绪激动，好斗。

例句：She adopted a combative stance in the debate.  在辩论中，她采取了强硬的姿态。|近义词：aggressive, belligerent, pugnacious, antagonistic|反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, cooperative|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *combativus, from Latin combātus, past participle of combattere "to fight," from com- "together" + battuere "to beat."
