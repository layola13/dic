haggard:/ˈhæɡəd/| adj.|憔悴的，形容枯槁的|Thin, pale, and careworn as a result of worry, exhaustion, or illness; having a wild, worn look.|例句：He looked haggard after his long journey.  他长途旅行后看起来很憔悴。|例句：The refugees arrived looking tired and haggard.  难民们到达时看起来疲惫不堪，形容枯槁。|近义词：drawn, gaunt, exhausted, fatigued|反义词：healthy, robust, vibrant|词性:形容词

haggard:/ˈhæɡəd/| n.|野鹰，凶猛的鹰|A wild hawk; especially a female hawk used in falconry.|例句：The falconer released the haggard to hunt.  驯鹰人放出野鹰去捕猎。|例句：The haggard soared high above the cliffs. 野鹰在悬崖上空翱翔。|近义词：hawk, falcon|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑prey猎物)|词性:名词
