avisely:/əˈvaɪzɪdli/
adverb
明智地；审慎地
In a careful and thoughtful way, showing good judgment.
He avisely decided to wait until he had more information. 他明智地决定等到他有更多信息后再做决定。
She avisely invested her money in a diverse portfolio. 她明智地将资金投资于多元化的投资组合。
近义词: wisely, prudently, judiciously, sagaciously
反义词: foolishly, unwisely, imprudently, rashly
词性: adverb
