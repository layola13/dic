breakthroughs:/ˈbreɪkθruːz/
n.
突破，重大进展
A sudden, important discovery or development that helps to improve a situation or solve a problem.
Scientists have made a major breakthrough in the fight against cancer. 科学家们在抗癌斗争中取得了重大突破。
advancements, discoveries, progress
obstacles, setbacks
noun
