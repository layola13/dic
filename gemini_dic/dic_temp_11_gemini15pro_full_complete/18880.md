besiege:/bɪˈsiːdʒ/| vt.|围攻，包围；困扰|To surround a place, especially with an army, in order to capture it; to crowd around oppressively.|The city was besieged for months. 这座城市被围困了数月。|The actress was besieged by reporters after the awards ceremony.  颁奖典礼后，这位女演员被记者团团围住。|近义词：surround, encircle, beleaguer, beset|反义词：liberate, free|verb
