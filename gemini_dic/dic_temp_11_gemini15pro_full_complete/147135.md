overlogicalness:/ˌoʊvərˈlɒdʒɪkəlːnəs/ | n. | 过逻辑性；过度理性；过分讲逻辑 | The state of being excessively logical or relying too heavily on logic, sometimes to the detriment of common sense or practicality. |  He suffered from overlogicalness, unable to make a decision based on intuition or feeling. 他过于逻辑化，无法根据直觉或感觉做出决定。|  His overlogicalness prevented him from appreciating the beauty of the artwork. 他过分讲逻辑，使他无法欣赏艺术品的美丽。 |近义词:  over-rationality, hyper-rationality | 反义词: irrationality, illogicality | 词性: noun

overlogical:/ˌoʊvərˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于逻辑的；过分理性的 | Excessively logical or relying too heavily on logic. | He took an overlogical approach to the problem, ignoring the human element. 他对这个问题采取了过于逻辑化的方法，忽略了人为因素。|  Her overlogical analysis of the situation led her to a flawed conclusion. 她对情况的过度逻辑分析导致她得出了一个错误的结论。| 近义词: over-rational, hyper-rational | 反义词: irrational, illogical | 词性: adjective
