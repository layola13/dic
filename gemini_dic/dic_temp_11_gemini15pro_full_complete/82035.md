grisette:/ɡrɪˈzɛt/ | n. | 灰衣女工；轻佻女子；灰褐色小蛾 | A young working-class woman, especially a seamstress or shop girl, typically wearing a gray dress; also, a type of mushroom.  Historically, the term could carry connotations of flirtatiousness or easy virtue. |  In 19th-century Paris, grisettes were a common sight, flitting between workshops and cafes.  在19世纪的巴黎，灰衣女工是常见的景象，她们穿梭于工坊和咖啡馆之间。


She was a grisette, known for her quick wit and even quicker smile. 她是一位灰衣女工，以她机智的言语和更快的笑容而闻名。

| 近义词: working girl, shopgirl, seamstress, coquette (when referring to flirtatiousness) | 反义词:  lady, matron (implying respectability and higher social status) | 词性: noun
