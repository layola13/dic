intermontane:/ˌɪntərˈmɑːnteɪn/ /ˌɪntəˈmɒnteɪn/| adj.|山间的，位于山脉之间的|Situated between mountains; among mountains.|The intermontane basin is known for its unique ecosystem. 这个山间盆地以其独特的生态系统而闻名。|例句：The intermontane valley was fertile and well-populated. 山间的山谷肥沃，人口稠密。|近义词：mountainous, upland|反义词：lowland, coastal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "inter" (between) + "montanus" (mountainous), from "mons" (mountain).|词源: 来自拉丁语 "inter"（之间）+ "montanus"（多山的），来自 "mons"（山）。
