anticor:/ˈæntɪkɔːr/ |n.|解毒剂；抗体；对抗手段|A substance that acts against a poison, disease, or other harmful thing. |The researchers are developing an anticor to the virus.| 近义词:antidote, remedy, counteragent |反义词:poison, toxin, venom |词性:名词

词源: From French *anticorps*, from *anti-* "against" + *corps* "body".  Ultimately from Latin *anti-* "against" and *corpus* "body."
