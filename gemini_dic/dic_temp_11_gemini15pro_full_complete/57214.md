downchange:/ˈdaʊnˌtʃeɪndʒ/
noun
降档，向下改变
a change to a lower gear in a vehicle, or a reduction in size or importance.
The driver executed a smooth downchange as the car approached the hill.  司机在汽车接近山坡时平稳地降档。
The company's downchange from a large office to a smaller one saved them money. 公司从一个大办公室搬到一个小办公室，节省了开支。
downshift, reduction, decrease
upgrade, increase
