braids:/breɪdz/
| n. | 辫子；发辫 |A hairstyle formed by intertwining three or more strands of hair.  A complex structure or pattern formed by the interlacing of multiple strands or elements.|She wore her hair in braids. 她把头发扎成了辫子。|近义词：plaits, pigtails, cornrows|反义词:loose hair
| v. (braids, braiding, braided) | 编织；编辫子 | To intertwine three or more strands of hair or other material to form a braid.  To weave or combine elements into a complex whole.|She braided her daughter's hair. 她给她女儿编辫子。|近义词：plait, intertwine, weave|反义词:unbraid, untwist
