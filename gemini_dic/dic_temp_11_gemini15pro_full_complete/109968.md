liberetto:/lɪbəˈrɛtoʊ/ (US), /lɪbəˈrɛtəʊ/ (UK)
n.
歌剧脚本；歌词；剧本
the text of an opera or other long vocal work
The composer worked closely with the librettist on the libretto for his new opera.  作曲家与脚本作者密切合作，创作他的新歌剧的剧本。

The libretto of "The Marriage of Figaro" is by Lorenzo Da Ponte.  《费加罗的婚礼》的剧本是由洛伦佐·达·蓬特创作的。

synonym: script, text, book, words
antonym: score (specifically referring to the musical notation)
词性: noun
