indulgently:/ɪnˈdʌl.dʒənt.li/ | adv. |纵容地，放纵地，溺爱地 | in a way that allows someone to have or do what they want, even if this is not good for them | He smiled indulgently at his grandson. 他对孙子笑了笑，一脸宠溺。| 近义词: leniently, permissively, forgivingly | 反义词: strictly, severely, harshly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin *indulgentia* "kindness, tenderness, indulgence,"  from *indulgent-* (stem of *indulgens*) "yielding, kind, indulgent" present participle of *indulgere* "be kind or tender to, yield to, gratify," from *in-* "in" + *dulcis* "sweet."
