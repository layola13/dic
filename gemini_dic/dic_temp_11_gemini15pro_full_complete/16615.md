baronesses:/ˈbærənɪsɪz/
n.
女男爵（baroness的复数）
The plural form of baroness, referring to multiple female nobles holding the rank of baron or the wife or widow of a baron.
The baronesses attended the coronation ceremony. 女男爵们出席了加冕仪式。
The baronesses discussed their philanthropic endeavors. 女男爵们讨论了她们的慈善事业。
近义词:noblewomen, peeresses
反义词:commoners
词性:名词

baroness:/ˈbærənɪs/
n.
女男爵
A female noble holding the rank of baron in her own right, or the wife or widow of a baron.
The baroness donated generously to the local hospital. 女男爵慷慨地向当地医院捐款。
She became a baroness after inheriting her father's title. 她继承了父亲的头衔后成为了一名女男爵。
近义词:noblewoman, peeress
反义词:commoner
词性:名词
