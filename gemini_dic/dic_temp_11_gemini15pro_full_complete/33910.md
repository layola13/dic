circumventors:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntərz/ | n. |规避者；绕行者；逃避者| People who find a way around a rule, problem, or difficulty, often in a clever or dishonest way.  Those who cleverly outmaneuver or bypass obstacles or restrictions.|  The smugglers were adept circumventors of customs regulations. 这些走私者是规避海关规定的高手。| Synonyms: evaders, dodgers, outmaneuverers | Antonyms: conformists, followers | Noun

circumvent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛnt/ | v. |规避；绕行；逃避| To find a way around (an obstacle, problem, or law) in a clever or dishonest way.| The company circumvented the regulations by using a loophole. 这家公司利用漏洞规避了规定。| Synonyms: evade, dodge, bypass | Antonyms: confront, face, comply with | Verb
