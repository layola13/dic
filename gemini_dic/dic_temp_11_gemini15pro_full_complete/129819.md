non-habitable:/ˌnɒnˈhæbɪtəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈhæbɪtəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不适宜居住的；无法居住的|Not suitable for living in; unable to support human life.|例句：The planet is non-habitable due to its extreme temperatures.  这个星球由于极端温度而不适宜居住。|近义词：uninhabitable, unlivable|反义词：habitable, livable|词性:形容词

non-habitable:|Etymology:  From non- + habitable.  Habitable comes from the Old French word habitable,  from Late Latin habitabilis,  from Latin habitare "to inhabit," from habere "to have, hold."|词源: 来自non- + habitable。Habitable来自古法语单词habitable，来自晚期拉丁语habitabilis，来自拉丁语habitare“居住”，来自habere“拥有，持有”。
