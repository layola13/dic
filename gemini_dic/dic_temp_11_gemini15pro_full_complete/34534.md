cleanse:/klɛnz/| v.|清洗，净化，涤荡|To make something thoroughly clean or pure, often in a religious or ritual context.  To rid of impurities, foreign matter, or undesirable elements.|She cleansed her face with a special soap. 她用一种特殊的肥皂洗脸。|近义词：purify, purge, clean, wash|反义词:pollute, contaminate, soil|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *clǣnsian, from clǣne (“clean”). Cognate with Dutch klenzen (“to rinse”), German klenzen (“to rinse”).
