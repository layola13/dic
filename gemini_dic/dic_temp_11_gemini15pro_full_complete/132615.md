nondisabled:/ˌnɒndɪsˈeɪbld/ | adj. | 非残疾的 | Not having a disability; able-bodied. |  Example: The nondisabled often fail to understand the challenges faced by people with disabilities. 非残疾人士往往无法理解残疾人士所面临的挑战。

Example:  The architect designed a nondisabled-accessible building, neglecting the needs of wheelchair users.  这位建筑师设计了一座非残疾人士无障碍的建筑，忽略了轮椅使用者的需求。

| 近义词: able-bodied, non-disabled | 反义词: disabled, handicapped | 词性: adjective
