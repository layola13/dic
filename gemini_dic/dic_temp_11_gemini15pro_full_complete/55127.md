diss:/dɪs/| v. n.| 侮辱，轻视；贬低；批评 | To speak disrespectfully to or criticize someone or something. |  She dissed her former friend's new hairstyle. 她嘲笑了她以前朋友的新发型。| synonyms: disrespect, slight, put down, insult | antonyms: praise, compliment, respect | verb, noun

diss:/dɪs/| n.| 侮辱，轻视 | An expression of disrespect or criticism. |  That comment was a real diss. 那句话真的很侮辱人。| synonyms: insult, put-down, slight | antonyms: compliment, praise | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of disrespect. First appeared in African American Vernacular English (AAVE) in the 1980s.
