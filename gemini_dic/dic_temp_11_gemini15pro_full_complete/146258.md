overexuberance:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzjuːbərəns/| n.| 过度繁荣；过度热情；过于旺盛|Excessive enthusiasm or excitement. |The dot-com boom was fueled by overexuberance in the market.  互联网的繁荣是由市场过度繁荣推动的。

The team's overexuberance led to several unnecessary fouls.  该队的过度热情导致了几个不必要的犯规。|近义词：excessiveness, ebullience, exuberance|反义词：apathy, indifference, restraint|词性:noun

Etymology:
From over- + exuberance, from Latin exuberantia "abundance, fertility," from exuberantem (nominative exuberans) "overflowing, luxuriant, very rich," present participle of exuberare "be abundant, overflow," from ex "out" (see ex-) + uber "fertile, fruitful, copious" (see udder).
