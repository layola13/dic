overcommercialization:/ˌoʊvərˌkəˈmɜːrʃələˌzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度商业化 | The excessive focus on commerce and profit, often at the expense of other values. | The overcommercialization of Christmas has led to a decline in its true meaning.  圣诞节的过度商业化导致其真正意义的衰落。| overcommercialism, excessive commercialization | underselling, lack of commercialization | noun

overcommercialization:/ˌoʊvərˌkəˈmɜːrʃələˌzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度商业化 |  The act of commercializing something to an excessive degree, often resulting in a negative impact on its intrinsic value or quality. | The overcommercialization of the music industry prioritizes profit over artistic integrity. 音乐产业的过度商业化将利润置于艺术完整性之上。|  commercial saturation, overexposure |  underdevelopment, restraint | noun
