nephalism:/ˈnɛfəlɪzəm/
n.
裙带关系；任人唯亲；重用亲戚
The practice of showing favoritism toward one's relatives, especially in appointment to high office.
His rapid promotion was attributed to blatant nephalism. 他迅速升迁被认为是明显的裙带关系。

nephalism:/ˈnɛfəlɪzəm/
n.
裙带关系；任人唯亲；重用亲戚
Favoritism shown to relatives or close friends by those in power (as by giving them jobs).
The company's nephalism has led to many incompetent people being in positions of authority. 该公司的裙带关系导致许多不称职的人身居要职。

nephalism:/ˈnɛfəlɪzəm/
n.
裙带关系
Favoritism granted to relatives regardless of merit.
Nephalism is rampant in this organization, hindering truly talented individuals from advancing. 裙带关系在这个组织中猖獗，阻碍了真正有才华的人晋升。


近义词:cronyism, favoritism, patronage
反义词:meritocracy, impartiality
词性:noun
