crannog:/ˈkrænɒɡ/ (US) /ˈkrænɑːɡ/ (UK)| n.|人工岛，湖上住所|An artificial island or structure in a lake or other body of water, typically built of timber and stone, used as a dwelling. |例句：Archaeologists discovered a well-preserved crannog in the Scottish Highlands. 考古学家在苏格兰高地发现了一个保存完好的湖上住所。

例句：The crannog provided a safe refuge for the ancient inhabitants.  这个人工岛为古代居民提供了一个安全的避难所。|近义词：lake dwelling, artificial island|反义词: mainland|词性:noun
