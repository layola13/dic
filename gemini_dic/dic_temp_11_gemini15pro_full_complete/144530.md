outvanishing:/ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
超越消失的，消失得更快的
Surpassing or exceeding in vanishing; disappearing more quickly or completely than something else.  This word emphasizes a greater degree or speed of disappearance compared to a baseline.
The outvanishing mist revealed a breathtaking vista.  消散的薄雾展现出一幅令人惊叹的远景。
The magician's outvanishing act left the audience speechless. 魔术师的瞬间消失表演让观众哑口无言。
Synonyms:  outstripping, surpassing, exceeding, eclipsing
Antonyms:  appearing, emerging, materializing
词性: 形容词
