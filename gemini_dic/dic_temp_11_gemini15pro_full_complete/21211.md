blockily:/ˈblɑːkɪli/| adv.|笨拙地，粗糙地，块状地|In a blocky or clumsy manner; resembling blocks.  Characterized by a lack of smoothness or finesse.|He blockily carved the wood, resulting in a crude, unfinished look. 他笨拙地雕刻着木头，结果雕出来粗糙不堪，没有完成的样子。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, roughly|反义词:smoothly, gracefully, deftly|词性:副词
