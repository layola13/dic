barbacan: /ˈbɑːrbɪkən/ | n. | 城楼，外郭，城墙外的防御工事 | A fortified outpost or gateway, especially one protecting a bridge or castle.  A barbacan is an outer defense of a city or castle, or any outwork, such as an earthwork, built beyond the main line of fortification. |  The castle's barbacan offered a crucial first line of defense against attackers. 城堡的城楼提供了至关重要的第一道防线来抵御攻击者。

The barbacan was heavily damaged during the siege.  城楼在围攻期间遭到严重破坏。 | 近义词: outwork, fortification, gatehouse, portal | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inner keep" or "citadel") | 词性: noun
