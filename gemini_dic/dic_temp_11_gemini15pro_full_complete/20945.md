例句：The boxer was a known bleeder and his opponent targeted his nose.  这名拳击手以容易流鼻血著称，他的对手就瞄准了他的鼻子。|近义词：hemophiliac, sufferer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "healthy individual" in a specific context)|词性: Noun
