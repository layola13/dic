camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/ | adj. | 伪装的，被掩饰的 | disguised or concealed, especially to deceive an enemy or prey | The soldiers were camouflaged in the jungle. 士兵们在丛林中伪装了起来。| The sniper was so well camouflaged that we couldn't see him. 狙击手伪装得很好，我们根本看不见他。 | synonyms: disguised, concealed, hidden, masked, cloaked | antonyms: exposed, visible, revealed, uncovered | adjective

camouflage:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ/ | n. | 伪装，掩饰 | the disguising of military personnel, equipment, and installations by painting or covering them to make them blend in with their surroundings | The army uses camouflage to hide from the enemy. 军队使用伪装来躲避敌人。|  The chameleon's camouflage allows it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的伪装使其能够与周围环境融为一体。 | synonyms: disguise, concealment, masking | antonyms: exposure, revelation | noun

camouflage:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ/ | v. | 伪装，掩饰 | hide or disguise the presence of (a person, animal, or object) by means of camouflage |  They camouflaged the tanks with nets and branches. 他们用网和树枝伪装坦克。| She tried to camouflage her nervousness with a smile. 她试图用微笑掩饰她的紧张。| synonyms: disguise, conceal, hide, mask, cloak | antonyms: expose, reveal | verb
