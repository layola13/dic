fissilities:/fɪˈsɪlətiz/| n. |分裂性，裂变性；易裂变性|The fissilities of certain isotopes of uranium and plutonium make them suitable for nuclear weapons. 某些铀和钚同位素的裂变性使它们适合制造核武器。|例句：The treaty aims to limit the fissilities of nuclear materials. 该条约旨在限制核材料的裂变性。

例句：The fissilities of the material were a key factor in its selection.  这种材料的易裂变性是选择它的关键因素。

|近义词：divisibility, cleavability, splittability|反义词:inseparability, indivisibility|词性:noun


fissible:/ˈfɪsɪbl/| adj. |可裂变的，易分裂的|Uranium-235 is a fissible material. 铀-235是一种可裂变材料。|例句：Plutonium is a highly fissible element. 钚是一种高度易裂变的元素。

例句：The fissible nature of the material made it dangerous to handle. 这种材料的可裂变性使其处理起来很危险。

|近义词：cleavable, splittable|反义词:infissile|词性:adjective
