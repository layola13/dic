disembodiment:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɒdɪmənt/| n.|脱离肉体；抽象化；无实体化|The disembodiment of the spirit from the body is a common theme in many religions.  灵魂与肉体分离是许多宗教的共同主题。|  He felt a strange sense of disembodiment after the accident. 事故之后，他感到一种奇怪的灵魂出窍的感觉。|近义词：abstraction, incorporeality, immateriality|反义词：embodiment, incarnation, materialization|词性:名词

Etymology:
From dis- "not, opposite of" + embodiment.  "Embodiment" comes from the Old French word "embodier," meaning "to give a body to."  Ultimately from Latin "in-" (in) + "corpus" (body).
