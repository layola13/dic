mayfish: /ˈmeɪfɪʃ/ | n. | 五月鱼，白鱼 | A small freshwater fish of the dace family, common in European rivers and streams, that spawns in May.  It is also known as the bleak. |例句：Mayfish are often used as bait for larger fish. 五月鱼常被用作钓大鱼的诱饵。

例句：The mayfish swam in the clear river. 五月鱼在清澈的河里游动。
