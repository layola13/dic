leprosy: /ˈleprəsi/ (US), /ˈleprəsi/ (UK) | n. | 麻风病 | a chronic, progressive bacterial disease that affects the skin, mucous membranes, peripheral nerves, and eyes |  Leprosy is a curable disease. 麻风病是一种可治愈的疾病。|  Leprosy is a serious public health concern in some developing countries. 麻风病在一些发展中国家是一个严重的公共卫生问题。| synonyms: Hansen's disease | antonyms: health | noun
