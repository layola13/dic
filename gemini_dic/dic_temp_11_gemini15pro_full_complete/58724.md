duncishly:/ˈdʌnʃɪʃli/
adverb|愚蠢地，笨拙地|In a stupid or clumsy manner. |He duncishly tripped over the rug. 他笨手笨脚地被地毯绊倒了。|Synonyms: stupidly, clumsily, awkwardly, foolishly |Antonyms: cleverly, skillfully, gracefully, intelligently
