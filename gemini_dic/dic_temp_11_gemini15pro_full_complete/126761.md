nankeen:/nænˈkiːn/
noun
南京布，淡黄褐色
A durable buff-colored cotton cloth originally made in Nanjing, China. Also, the yellowish-brown color of this cloth.
The gentlemen wore nankeen trousers and white linen shirts.  绅士们穿着淡黄褐色的裤子和白色亚麻衬衫。
Synonyms: buff, khaki, beige
Antonyms: (For the color) black, dark brown, vibrant colors
