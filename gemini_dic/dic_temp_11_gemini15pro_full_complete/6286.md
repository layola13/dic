amoroso:/ˌɑːmɔːˈroʊsoʊ/ /ˌæmɔːˈroʊsoʊ/
adjective
充满爱的；柔情的；表示爱的
Showing or expressing love or affection.  Relating to or characteristic of love.  (Music) in a tender, loving style.
She hummed an amoroso melody. 她哼着一曲柔情的旋律。
The amoroso glance they exchanged spoke volumes. 他们充满爱意的眼神交流胜过千言万语。
近义词:loving, affectionate, tender, passionate
反义词:cold, unfeeling, indifferent
词性:adjective

词源:
From Italian *amoroso*, from *amore* "love," from Latin *amor*.
