familial:/fəˈmɪliəl/
adj.
家庭的；家族的；亲属的
Relating to or occurring within a family or its members.
Her illness is believed to have a familial link. 她的疾病被认为与家族遗传有关。

familial:/fəˈmɪliəl/
adj.
家族性的；遗传的
(of a disease or characteristic) tending to occur in more members of a family than can be readily accounted for by chance alone.
Familial cancers like breast and ovarian cancers often develop earlier in life. 家族性癌症，如乳腺癌和卵巢癌，通常在生命早期就发展出来。


近义词:genetic, hereditary, inherited
反义词:non-familial, acquired
词性:adjective
