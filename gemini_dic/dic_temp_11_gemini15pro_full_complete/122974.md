monadical: /məˈnædɪkəl/ | adj. | 单一的，独一的; 类似单子的 |  Relating to or resembling a monad; characterized by unity or singleness.  |  He had a monadical view of the world, seeing everything as interconnected. 他对世界持有单一的观点，认为一切都是相互联系的。 | Example: The artist's monadical focus on a single subject allowed for deep exploration. 这位艺术家对单一主题的专注使他能够进行深入的探索。 | Synonyms: singular, unique, solitary, individual | Antonyms: multiple, diverse, varied | Word type: Adjective


monadically: /məˈnædɪkli/ | adv. | 单一地，独一地 | In a monadical manner; singly, uniquely. | The philosopher approached the problem monadically, considering each aspect in isolation before synthesizing them. 这位哲学家以单一的方式处理这个问题，先孤立地考虑每个方面，然后再进行综合。 | Example:  He lived monadically, isolated from the rest of society. 他过着独居的生活，与社会隔绝。| Synonyms: singularly, uniquely, solitarily, individually | Antonyms: multiply, diversely, variedly | Word type: Adverb
