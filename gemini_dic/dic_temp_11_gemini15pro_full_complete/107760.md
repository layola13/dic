lapidaries:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛriːz/
n.
宝石工匠；宝石专家；宝石工艺；宝石加工
A person who cuts, polishes, or engraves precious stones.  One skilled in the art of working with precious stones. The art of cutting and polishing gems.
The lapidaries carefully examined the raw diamonds. 宝石工匠仔细检查了未加工的钻石。
Ancient Egyptian lapidaries were renowned for their intricate carvings. 古埃及宝石工匠以其复杂的雕刻而闻名。
Synonyms: gem-cutter, jeweler, lapidist
Antonyms: N/A
noun

lapidary:/ˈlæpɪˌdɛri/
adj.
宝石的；简洁的；精确的；（风格）典雅的
Relating to the engraving, cutting, or polishing of precious stones; suitable for inscription on stone; having the elegance and precision associated with inscriptions on stone.
The inscription on the tombstone was in elegant, lapidary style. 墓碑上的铭文是典雅的宝石风格。
Her lapidary prose was admired by critics.  评论家们钦佩她简洁的散文。
Synonyms: concise, precise, elegant, polished
Antonyms: verbose, rambling, clumsy, inelegant
adjective
