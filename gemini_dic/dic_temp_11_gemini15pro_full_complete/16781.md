basaltine:/ˈbæsəlˌtaɪn, ˈbæsəlˌtin/| adj.|玄武岩的，似玄武岩的|Relating to, resembling, or containing basalt.  Formed from or by basalt.|The basaltine rocks formed a dramatic cliff face.|近义词：basaltic, igneous|反义词:sedimentary|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *basaltin*, from *basalte* (basalt) + *-in* (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *basaltes*, from Greek *βασάλτης* (basaltēs), perhaps from Ethiopian.
