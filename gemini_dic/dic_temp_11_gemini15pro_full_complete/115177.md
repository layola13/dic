manichaean:/ˌmænɪˈkiːən/ | adj. n. | 摩尼教的；二元论的；善恶二元论者 | Of or relating to Manichaeism, a religion founded in the 3rd century AD by Mani, characterized by a dualistic cosmology of light and darkness, good and evil.  Also used more broadly to describe any system of thought that divides things into good and evil, light and dark, etc. |  The novel presents a Manichaean worldview, where characters are either purely good or irredeemably evil. 这部小说呈现了一种摩尼教的世界观，人物要么是纯善的，要么是不可救药的邪恶的。

The ancient philosopher was a staunch Manichaean.  这位古代哲学家是一位坚定的摩尼教徒。 | 近义词：dualistic, absolutist | 反义词： nuanced, relativistic | 词性: 形容词, 名词
