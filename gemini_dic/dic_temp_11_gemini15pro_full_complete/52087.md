diamagnetism:/ˌdaɪəˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n.|抗磁性|A property of substances that causes them to be repelled by a magnetic field.|Bismuth exhibits strong diamagnetism. 铋表现出很强的抗磁性。|近义词: non-magnetism (although not a perfect synonym)|反义词: paramagnetism, ferromagnetism|noun
