illusion:/ɪˈluːʒn/ (US), /ɪˈljuːʒn/ (UK)| n.|幻觉，错觉，幻想|something that deceives by producing a false or misleading impression of reality.|The magician created the illusion of a floating woman. 魔术师制造了一个女人漂浮的幻觉。|近义词：delusion, mirage, fantasy|反义词：reality, truth|noun
