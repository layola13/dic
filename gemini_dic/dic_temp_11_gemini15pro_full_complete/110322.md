lighteners:/ˈlaɪtnərz/
n.
增亮剂；淡化剂；打火机；使轻松愉快的人或物
Substances or products that make something lighter in color, weight, or mood.
She used hair lighteners to achieve a sun-kissed look. 她使用了头发增亮剂来达到阳光亲吻的效果。
The comedian's jokes were lighteners in a tense situation.  在这个紧张的局势下，喜剧演员的笑话让人轻松了不少。
Synonyms: brighteners, whiteners, bleaches (for color), relievers (for mood)
Antonyms: darkeners, intensifiers, aggravators
noun
