misk:/mɪsk/ | n. |麝香; 麝香鹿|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance secreted by a gland in the abdomen of the male musk deer, used in perfumery.|The perfume had a base of musk. 这款香水以麝香为基调。|Synonyms:  perfume, scent, fragrance|Antonyms: stench, odor|noun
