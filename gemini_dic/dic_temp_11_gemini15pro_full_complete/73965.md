fortifies:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪz/
v.
加强，增强，巩固
to make something stronger, especially in order to protect it against attack
The general fortified the city against the expected invasion. 将军加强了城市的防御以抵御预期的入侵。
strengthen, reinforce, bolster
weaken, undermine
verb
