looner:/ˈluːnər/| n.|疯子；古怪的人；加元|A person regarded as insane or eccentric; (Canadian informal) a Canadian dollar.|He's a bit of a looner, always talking to himself. 他有点疯疯癫癫的，总是自言自语。|近义词：lunatic, madman, eccentric, crank|反义词:sane person, rational person|词性:noun
Etymology: From *loon* (a foolish or crazy person) + *-er* (suffix forming agent nouns).  The sense referring to the Canadian dollar arises from the image of the common loon on the one-dollar coin.
