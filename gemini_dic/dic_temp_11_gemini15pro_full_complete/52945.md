ETYMOLOGY->
From *dine* (v.) + *-er* (suffix forming nouns denoting persons performing an action). *Dine* comes from Old French *disner* “to dine,” originally meaning "to break one's fast," from Vulgar Latin *disjunare* "to break one's fast," from Latin *dis- + *jejunare* "to fast"
