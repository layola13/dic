overmedications:/ˌōvərˌmedɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|过度用药|The practice of prescribing or taking more medications than is necessary or clinically indicated.  It can lead to adverse drug reactions and other health problems.|The elderly are particularly vulnerable to overmedication. 老年人特别容易过度用药。|polypharmacy, drug overuse|undermedication|noun
