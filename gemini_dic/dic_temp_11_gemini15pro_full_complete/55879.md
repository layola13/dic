divisionalization:/dɪˌvɪʒənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |部门化，分权化 |The act or process of dividing an organization into separate divisions, each responsible for a particular product, market, or function.  |  Example: Divisionalization allowed the company to respond more quickly to market changes.  部门化使公司能够更快地应对市场变化。

Example: The divisionalization of the company led to increased efficiency. 公司的部门化提高了效率。 |  Synonyms: decentralization, compartmentalization, segmentation | Antonyms: centralization, unification, consolidation | noun
