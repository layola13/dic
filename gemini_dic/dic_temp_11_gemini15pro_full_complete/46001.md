cyberbullying:/ˈsaɪbərˌbʊliɪŋ/| n.|网络欺凌|The act of harassing, intimidating, or threatening someone using electronic communication, such as social media, email, or text messages.|Cyberbullying can have devastating effects on victims. 网络欺凌会对受害者造成毁灭性的影响。|例句：
The school has a zero-tolerance policy on cyberbullying.  这所学校对网络欺凌采取零容忍政策。

She was the victim of relentless cyberbullying. 她是不间断网络欺凌的受害者。

|近义词：online harassment, online bullying, cyber harassment|反义词:cyber support, online encouragement|词性:noun
