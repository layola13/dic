overnationalizes:/ˌoʊvərˈnæʃnəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 超国家化，使超越国家界限 | To make something subject to the control of a supranational organization or authority, diminishing the power of individual nations. |  The treaty overnationalizes certain aspects of trade policy, giving the international body final say. 该条约将贸易政策的某些方面超国家化，赋予国际机构最终决定权。

The proposal to overnationalize banking regulations met with resistance from several member states.  将银行监管超国家化的提议遭到了几个成员国的抵制。 | 近义词: internationalizes, globalizes, federalizes | 反义词: nationalizes, localizes | 词性: 动词
