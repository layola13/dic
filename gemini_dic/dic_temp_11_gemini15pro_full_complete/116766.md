mausoleum:/ˌmɔːsəˈliːəm/ |/ˌmɑːsəˈliːəm/ | n. | 陵墓；壮丽的坟墓 | A large and impressive tomb. |  The emperor's remains were interred in a magnificent mausoleum. 皇帝的遗体安葬在一座宏伟的陵墓里。

The Taj Mahal is perhaps the most famous mausoleum in the world.  泰姬陵也许是世界上最著名的陵墓。

近义词: tomb, sepulcher, crypt | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mausōlēum*, from Greek *Μαυσωλεῖον (Mausōleîon)*,  "Mausoleum at Halicarnassus," referring to the elaborate tomb built for Mausolus, satrap in the Persian Empire (died c. 353 BC), by his sister-wife Artemisia II of Caria.  The name eventually became generalized to refer to any large, stately tomb.
