cruciality:/kruːʃiˈælɪti/
n.
关键性，重要性
The quality of being crucial; vital importance.
例句：The cruciality of this decision cannot be overstated.  这个决定的重要性怎么强调都不为过。

例句：He emphasized the cruciality of early intervention. 他强调了早期干预的重要性。


近义词：importance, significance, essentiality, vitalness, keyness
反义词：insignificance, unimportance, triviality
词性: noun


crucial:/ˈkruːʃəl/
adj.
至关重要的，关键的
Decisive or critical, especially in the success or failure of something.
例句：The next few weeks will be crucial. 接下来的几周将至关重要。

例句：He played a crucial role in the negotiations. 他在谈判中起了至关重要的作用。

近义词：critical, decisive, vital, essential, pivotal, key
反义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial
词性: adjective
