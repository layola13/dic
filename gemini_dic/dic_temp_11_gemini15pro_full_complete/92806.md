iconism:/ˈaɪkənɪzəm/
n.|图像主义，肖像崇拜，肖像画法|The use of icons or figures; the representation of an object by its image; a system of iconic representation.|The stained glass windows are a beautiful example of religious iconism. 彩色玻璃窗是宗教肖像画的一个美丽例子。|synonyms: idolatry, iconography, symbolism |antonyms: aniconism|noun
