bloody:/ˈblʌdi/| adj. adv.| 血腥的；该死的；非常的|A bloody nose.  一个流血的鼻子。|例句：It was a bloody awful film. 这部电影真是烂透了。|近义词：damned, cursed, extremely|反义词:blessed, clean|词性:形容词/副词
bloody:/ˈblʌdi/|Etymology: From Old English *blodig (bloody), from blod (blood) + -ig (suffix forming adjectives).  The intensive use developed in the 17th century and became very common by the mid-18th century.|词源: 来自古英语 *blodig (血腥的)，源自 blod (血) + -ig (构成形容词的后缀)。  强调用法出现在17世纪，并在18世纪中期变得非常普遍。
