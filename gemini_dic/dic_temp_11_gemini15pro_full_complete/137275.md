nonsympathizing:/ˌnɑːnˈsɪmpəθaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
不同情的，缺乏同情心的
Lacking sympathy or showing no sympathy.
She gave him a nonsympathizing look. 她冷漠地看了他一眼。

unsympathetic, uncaring, indifferent, callous, cold
sympathetic, compassionate, caring, understanding
词性: adjective
