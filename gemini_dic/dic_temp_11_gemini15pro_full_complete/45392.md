cupboards:/ˈkʌbərdz/
n.
橱柜，碗柜
A cupboard is a piece of furniture with doors and shelves used for storing things.
We keep the dishes in the cupboard above the sink. 我们把碗碟放在水槽上方的橱柜里。

The cat was hiding in the cupboard. 猫藏在橱柜里。
近义词:cabinet, closet
反义词:(none - a cupboard is a piece of furniture; there isn't a direct opposite)
词性:noun
