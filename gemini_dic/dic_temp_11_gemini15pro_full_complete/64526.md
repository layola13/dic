erp:/ˌiːɑːrˈpiː/ /ˌɜːrˈpiː/ | n. |企业资源计划(Enterprise Resource Planning)|A business process management software that allows an organization to use a system of integrated applications to manage the business and automate many back office functions related to technology, services and human resources. |例句：The company decided to implement a new ERP system to streamline its operations.  这家公司决定实施新的ERP系统以简化运营。

例句：The ERP system integrates all aspects of the business, from finance to human resources. ERP系统整合了业务的各个方面，从财务到人力资源。|近义词：business management software, enterprise software|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
