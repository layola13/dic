overdiligently: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪlɪdʒəntli/
adverb | 过于勤奋地，过分努力地 |  In a manner that is excessively diligent or hardworking. | He studied overdiligently, neglecting his health and social life. 他学习过于勤奋，忽略了健康和社交生活。 | Example: She cleaned the house overdiligently, scrubbing every surface until it shone. 她过度勤奋地打扫房子，擦洗每一个表面直到它闪闪发光。 | Synonyms: excessively, too much, overzealously | Antonyms: lazily, carelessly, negligently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "diligently" (adverb form of "diligent," from Latin "diligens," meaning "careful, attentive"). 由"over-"（表示“过度”的前缀）+ "diligently"（"diligent"的副词形式，源自拉丁语"diligens"，意为“仔细的，专心的”）组成。
