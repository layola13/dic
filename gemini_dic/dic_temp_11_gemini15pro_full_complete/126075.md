mustiest:/ˈmʌstiɪst/ | adj. | 发霉的；陈腐的；过时的 | Having a stale, moldy, or damp smell; outdated and uninteresting. | The attic was dark and mustiest, filled with forgotten relics. 阁楼又黑又霉，里面堆满了被人遗忘的旧物。

The library held a mustiest collection of old books.  图书馆里收藏了一批陈腐的旧书。 | 近义词：stale, moldy, fusty, musty | 反义词：fresh, airy, modern, new | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *musti, from Old French musti, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *muscidus (“moldy”), from Latin muscus (“moss”).
