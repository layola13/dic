awe:/ɔː/|n.|敬畏，惊叹|a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder.|Standing at the foot of the Himalayas, I felt a sense of awe. 站在喜马拉雅山脚下，我感到一种敬畏之情。|近义词：wonder, reverence, amazement|反义词：disrespect, contempt|名词

awes (third-person singular simple present awes, present participle awing, simple past awed, past participle awed) /ɔːz/|vt.|使敬畏，使惊叹|to inspire with awe.|The vastness of the universe awes me. 宇宙的浩瀚让我敬畏。|近义词：amaze, astonish, impress|反义词：bore, disappoint|动词
