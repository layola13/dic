misinfer:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɜːr/
verb
错误地推断，误解
To infer incorrectly; to draw a wrong conclusion.
He misinferred her silence as agreement. 他把她的沉默误解为同意。
misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrue
correctly infer, understand, interpret
