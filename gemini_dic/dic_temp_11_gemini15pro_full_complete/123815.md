mopsey:/ˈmɒpsi/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpsi/ (US)| n.|小家伙，宝贝，小甜心 (通常指小孩或宠物)|A term of endearment, often used for children or pets.  It suggests smallness and sweetness.|例句：She called her little kitten "Mopsey." 她叫她的小猫咪“小甜心”。

例句：He ruffled his son's hair and called him "Mopsey." 他抚摸着儿子的头发，叫他“小家伙”。

近义词：sweetie, darling, dear, pet, honey|反义词: (No direct antonym due to its nature as a term of endearment) |词性:noun
