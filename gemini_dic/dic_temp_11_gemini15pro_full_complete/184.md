abaya:/əˈbaɪə/ | n. | 阿巴亚 | A long, loose-fitting robe worn by some Muslim women, particularly in the Arabian Peninsula and some parts of North Africa. | She wore a beautiful black abaya. 她穿着一件漂亮的黑色阿巴亚。| The abaya flowed gracefully as she walked.  她走动时，阿巴亚优雅地飘动。 |Synonyms: jilbab, chador | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing) | noun
