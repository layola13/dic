christens:/ˈkrɪs(ə)nz/
Verb
为...施洗；命名
To give a first name to (someone) at baptism; to name (something) formally.
The ship was christened the 'Mayflower'. 这艘船被命名为“五月花号”。
Near Synonyms: baptize, name, dedicate
Antonyms: deconsecrate, profane
词性:动词

christens:/ˈkrɪs(ə)nz/
Noun (plural)
基督徒(指接受洗礼的人)
Christians, especially those newly baptized. (archaic)
The christens gathered in the church. 基督徒聚集在教堂里。（古语）
Near Synonyms: Christians, believers, converts
Antonyms: non-Christians, atheists
词性:名词
