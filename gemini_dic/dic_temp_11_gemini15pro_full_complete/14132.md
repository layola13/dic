authorise:/ˈɔːθəraɪz/ (British) /ˈɑːθəraɪz/ (American)| vt.|授权，批准|To give official permission for someone to do something.|I authorized my lawyer to act on my behalf. 我授权我的律师代表我行事。|近义词: commission, delegate, mandate|反义词: revoke, rescind, cancel|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *auctorisen*, from Old French *autoriser*, from Latin *auctorizare* "to authorize," from *auctor* "author, originator, creator."
