illusory:/ɪˈluːsəri/ |adj.|虚幻的；错觉的|Based on or producing illusion; deceptive.|例句：The oasis in the desert was illusory, a mirage caused by the heat. 沙漠中的绿洲是虚幻的，是由高温引起的幻景。

例句：His promises proved illusory, and she was left heartbroken. 他的承诺被证明是虚幻的，她伤心欲绝。|近义词：deceptive, misleading, false, unreal, imaginary, fanciful|反义词：real, actual, genuine, true, substantial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *illusorius, from Latin illusio (“mockery”), from illudere (“to mock”), from in- (“in, on”) + ludere (“to play”).
