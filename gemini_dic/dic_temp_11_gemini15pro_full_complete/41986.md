cost-out:/ˈkɔːst aʊt/
verb
降低成本，淘汰成本
The process of identifying and eliminating unnecessary costs in a product, process, or project.
The company implemented a cost-out initiative to improve profitability. 公司实施了一项降低成本的计划以提高盈利能力。
The project team is working to cost-out the new product design. 项目团队正在努力降低新产品设计的成本。
synonyms: cost reduction, cost saving, value engineering
antonyms: cost increase, cost escalation
v.
