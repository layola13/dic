liturgical: /lɪˈtɜːrdʒɪkəl/ (US) /lɪˈtɜːdʒɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|礼拜式的，仪式上的|Relating to the liturgy or public worship of a church.|例句：The liturgical calendar marks important dates in the Christian year.  基督教礼拜仪式日历标注了基督教年中重要的日期。

例句：The priest wore liturgical vestments during the service.  神父在仪式中穿着礼拜仪式法衣。|近义词：ceremonial, ritualistic|反义词：secular, informal|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *liturgicus*, from Greek λειτουργικός (leitourgikos, “of or pertaining to public service”), from λειτουργία (leitourgia, “public service, ministry”), from λειτος (leitos, “public”) + ἔργον (ergon, “work”).
