equipedal:/ˌiːkwɪˈpiːdəl/
adjective
等足的；同足的
Having legs of equal length or of the same length throughout.
例句：The equipedal robot moved smoothly across the terrain.  这个等足机器人在地形上平稳移动。

例句：Equipedal strides are characteristic of this species of bird.  等长的步伐是这种鸟类的特征。

近义词：equal-legged, isosceles (when referring to triangles)
反义词：unequal, uneven, disparate
词性：adjective
