boast:/boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)| v. n. |吹嘘，自夸；拥有（值得骄傲的事物）|To speak with exaggeration and excessive pride, especially about oneself.  To possess something to be proud of.| He boasted about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|synonyms: brag, crow, vaunt | antonyms: downplay, minimize, deprecate | verb

boast:/boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)| n. | 吹嘘，自夸；值得骄傲的事物 | An act of boasting; a proud statement. Something that deserves pride. | His main boast was that he had never missed a day of work.  他主要的吹嘘之处在于他从未缺勤过。 | synonyms: bragging, vaunting | antonyms: modesty, humility | noun
