amas:/ˈɑːmɑːz/ | n. |堆；堆积；大量| A large amount or collection of something, typically things that are considered desirable or valuable. |  She has amassed a fortune in real estate. 她积累了大量的房地产财富。 |  Synonyms: collection, accumulation, hoard, stockpile | Antonyms: lack, scarcity | noun

amas:/əˈmɑː/ | v. |  (法语) 爱；喜欢 (第一人称单数现在时) |  I love (in French). | J'amas la musique. 我喜欢音乐。 | Synonyms: adore, cherish (English equivalents, not French) | Antonyms: déteste, hais (French antonyms) | verb

注意事项:

"amas" as a French verb is archaic and rarely used.  It's much more common to see "aimer." The noun form, related to the English word "amass,"  is more commonly used.  Because of the rarity of the verb form, many examples above use "aimer" (to love) as a more accurate reflection of contemporary French.  The noun form examples reflect the meaning of "a large amount or collection."
