ministerialist:/ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəlɪst/
n.
部长主义者；唯部长是从者
A person who supports the authority and policies of a minister or ministers, especially without independent thought.
The prime minister relied heavily on his ministerialists for support. 首相非常依赖他的部长主义者们的支持。

The president faced opposition from within his own party, with several ministerialists voicing their concerns. 总统面临着来自党内的反对，几位部长主义者表达了他们的担忧。

supporter, follower, loyalist
critic, opponent, dissenter
noun
