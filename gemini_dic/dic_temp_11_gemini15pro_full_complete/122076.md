misthreaded:/ˌmɪsˈθrɛdɪd/
adjective
错误地穿线的；穿错线的
incorrectly threaded; having the thread passed through the wrong places.
The seamstress had misthreaded the machine, causing the stitches to be uneven.  那位女裁缝把机器的线穿错了，导致针脚不均匀。
Near Synonyms:  tangled, snarled, knotted, messed up
Antonyms: correctly threaded, untangled
词性: adjective
