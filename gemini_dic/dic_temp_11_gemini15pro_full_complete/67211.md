expressiveness:/ɪkˌsprɛsɪvnɪs/| n. |表现力；表达能力；富于表情|The quality of being expressive; the ability to convey thoughts or feelings effectively.|Her expressiveness on stage captivated the audience. 她在舞台上的表现力吸引了观众。|近义词：articulateness, eloquence, vividness|反义词：inarticulateness, inexpressiveness|词性:名词
Etymology: From expressive + -ness.  "expressive" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin expressivus, from exprimere ‘press out’.
