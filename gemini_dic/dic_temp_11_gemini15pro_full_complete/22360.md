n.

boob:/buːb/
vt.
犯愚蠢的错误；搞砸
To make a stupid mistake; to mess something up.
He boobed the exam and failed the course.  / 他考试考砸了，这门课不及格。
She really boobed by forgetting the client's name.  / 她忘记了客户的名字，真的搞砸了。
近义词：blunder, mess up, screw up
反义词: succeed, excel
词性: verb


词源:
Possibly imitative of a bulging shape.  First recorded in the early 18th century, initially meaning "a blunder," it later acquired the meaning of "breast" in the early 20th century.
