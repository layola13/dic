flotsam-and-jetsam:/ˈflɒtsəm ən ˈdʒetsəm/ (US) /ˈflɑːtsəm ən ˈdʒetsəm/ (UK)| n.|海上漂浮的残骸；废料；无价值的东西|Refers to the wreckage of a ship or its cargo found floating on the sea (flotsam) or washed ashore (jetsam); figuratively, it refers to useless or discarded objects or people.|The beach was covered in flotsam and jetsam after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上满是漂浮的残骸。|Synonyms: debris, wreckage, refuse, rubbish|Antonyms: valuables, treasure|noun
