mutedly:/ˈmjuːtɪdli/ | adv. | 静静地；低声地；沉闷地 | In a quiet and restrained manner, often due to sadness, shyness, or fear. | She spoke mutedly, her voice barely above a whisper. 她低声说话，声音几乎听不见。| Examples:  He mutedly agreed, not wanting to cause a scene. 他默默地同意了，不想引起一场争吵。 The celebration was mutedly joyful, overshadowed by recent events. 庆祝活动略显沉闷，因为最近发生的事情蒙上了一层阴影。 | Synonyms: softly, quietly, subduedly, faintly, dully | Antonyms: loudly, boisterously, brightly, clearly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mute (adj.) + -ed + -ly.  "Mute" comes from Old French "mut" meaning silent, from Latin "mutus."
