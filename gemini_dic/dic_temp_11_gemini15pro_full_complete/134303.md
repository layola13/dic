noninquiring:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
adj.
不打听的，不好奇的
Not asking questions; not inquisitive.
She had a noninquiring nature and rarely pried into other people's affairs. 她生性不爱打听，很少过问别人的事。
近义词: incurious, uninterested, indifferent
反义词: inquisitive, curious, inquiring
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + inquiring (asking questions).  "Inquiring" comes from the Latin "inquirere" (to seek into, investigate).
