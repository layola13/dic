dreamed:/driːmd/ /drɛmt/ | v. | 梦见，梦想；想像，设想 | to experience events and images in your mind while you are sleeping; to imagine something that you would like to happen | Last night I dreamed I was flying. 昨晚我梦见我在飞。| 近义词: envisioned, fantasized, imagined | 反义词:  | verb

dreamed:/driːmd/ /drɛmt/ | adj. | 梦想的，理想的 | Something that is dreamed is wished for, or is the best possible kind of. | My dreamed-of vacation would be a trip to Hawaii. 我梦寐以求的假期是去夏威夷旅行。| 近义词: ideal, desired, wished-for | 反义词:  | adjective
