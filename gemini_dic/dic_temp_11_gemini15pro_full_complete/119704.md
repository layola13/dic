midpoints:/ˈmɪdpɔɪnts/| n.|中点，中间点|A point that divides a line segment into two equal segments.  The midpoint is equidistant from the endpoints of the segment.|The midpoint of the line segment AB is C.|近义词：center, halfway point|反义词:endpoint|noun
