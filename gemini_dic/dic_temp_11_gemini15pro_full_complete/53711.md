disclosed:/dɪˈskloʊzd/
verb
已公开的，已透露的
made known; revealed
The company disclosed its financial results for the quarter.  该公司公布了本季度的财务业绩。
近义词: revealed, publicized, divulged, unveiled
反义词: concealed, hidden, suppressed, withheld
词性: Verb (past tense and past participle of disclose)


disclosed:/dɪˈskloʊzd/
adjective
已公开的，已透露的
made known; revealed
The disclosed information caused a scandal.  公开的信息引发了丑闻。
近义词: revealed, publicized, divulged, unveiled
反义词: concealed, hidden, suppressed, withheld
词性: Adjective (past participle of disclose used as an adjective)


词源: From Late Latin *disclausus, past participle of disclaudere "to open, reveal," from dis- "apart" + claudere "to close."  The meaning shifted from "opened" to "revealed."
