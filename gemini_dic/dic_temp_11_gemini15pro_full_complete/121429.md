misinforms:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmz/ | v. | 误报；提供错误信息 | To give someone wrong information about something. | The article misinforms readers about the true cost of the project. 这篇文章误导了读者，让他们对项目的真实成本产生了误解。

The government deliberately misinformed the public about the severity of the crisis. 政府故意向公众隐瞒了危机的严重性。 | misleads, deceives | informs, enlightens | verb
