jump-start:/ˈdʒʌmp stɑːrt/ | n. vt.| 启动，快速启动；激励，推动 |A jump-start is a way of starting a vehicle's engine when its battery is dead, by connecting it to another vehicle's battery with jumper cables. It can also refer to a boost or impetus given to something.|

例句：
He jump-started the car using jumper cables. 他用跨接电缆启动了汽车。

The government is trying to jump-start the economy. 政府正试图刺激经济复苏。


近义词：boost, revive, kick-start | 反义词: hinder, obstruct | 词性: noun, verb
