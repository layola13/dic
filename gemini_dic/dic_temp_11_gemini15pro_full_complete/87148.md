herbie:/ˈhɜːrbi/ | n. |赫比，小汽车的名字（通常指大众甲壳虫）| A name, often given to a Volkswagen Beetle, especially a racing or customized one.  It originated from the Disney movies "The Love Bug" (1968) and its sequels, featuring a sentient Beetle named Herbie. |例句：Herbie, the little car with a mind of its own, won the race.  赫比，那辆有自己想法的小汽车，赢得了比赛。

例句：My grandfather lovingly restored his old Herbie.  我祖父精心修复了他的旧赫比。|近义词：Beetle, Volkswagen|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
