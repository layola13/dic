harnessers:/ˈhɑːrnɪsər z/
n.
利用者；治理者；控制者
People who control and make use of something, typically a resource or power.
The harnessers of solar energy are making significant strides in renewable technology.  太阳能的利用者在可再生能源技术方面取得了重大进展。
Engineers are the harnessers of technology to solve real-world problems. 工程师是利用技术解决现实世界问题的控制者。
近义词：users, exploiters, controllers, developers
反义词：wasters, squanderers
