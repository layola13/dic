nonfreeman:/ˌnɒnˈfriːmən/ | n. | 非自由民；奴隶 |  A person who is not free; a slave or serf.  Historically, this term referred to individuals who lacked the rights and privileges of freemen, often due to their social status or being bound to a particular lord or land. |  In medieval England, nonfreemen were often tied to the land and could not leave without their lord's permission.  在中世纪的英格兰，非自由民通常被束缚在土地上，未经领主许可不得离开。|Synonyms: slave, serf, bondsman, villein | Antonyms: freeman, citizen, free person | noun
