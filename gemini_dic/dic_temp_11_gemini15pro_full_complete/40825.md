convulse:/kənˈvʌls/| v. |使剧烈震动，使激变|To cause a sudden, violent upheaval or change.|The country was convulsed by civil war. 这个国家因内战而动荡不安。|近义词：shake, disrupt, unsettle|反义词:stabilize, calm|动词
