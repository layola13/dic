furores:/fʊˈrɔːriːz/
n.
outbursts of excitement; crazes; rage; frenzy
A plural form of furor.  A sudden outburst of excitement or enthusiasm, especially over a person or thing; a craze.
The new video game caused a furore among teenagers.  这款新电子游戏在青少年中引起了轰动。
The politician's remarks caused a furore in the media.  这位政治家的言论引起了媒体的强烈反响。
frenzy, uproar, commotion, excitement, stir
calm, peace, tranquility
noun
