isobarisms:/ˈaɪsəʊˌbɑːrɪzəmz/ | n. |同压异构现象；同压异构体|The existence of two or more nuclides having the same number of nucleons but different numbers of protons and neutrons, and hence belonging to different elements, is known as isobarisms. 两种或两种以上核素具有相同数量的核子，但质子和中子的数量不同，因此属于不同的元素，这种现象称为同压异构现象。|Isobarisms are observed in several elements, for example, argon-40, potassium-40, and calcium-40 are isobars.  在几种元素中观察到同压异构现象，例如，氩-40、钾-40和钙-40是同压异构体。|近义词: nuclear isomerism (in a broader sense) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology:  From iso- (meaning "equal") + bar- (relating to barometric pressure or weight, here referring to atomic weight or mass number) + -ism (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, condition, or doctrine).
