dedimus: /ˈdɛdɪməs/ | n. | 授权令; 委托书 | A writ or warrant empowering one or more persons named to act as judges or commissioners. |  The court issued a dedimus potestatem to allow the witness to be deposed outside the jurisdiction. 法院发布了一项授权令，允许证人在管辖区外作证。| synonym: commission, warrant, authorization | antonym: prohibition, restriction | noun

dedimus potestatem: /ˈdɛdɪməs poʊˈtɛstɑːtɛm/ | n. | 授权令; 委托书 | A writ or warrant specifically empowering one or more persons named to act as judges or commissioners.  It is a more complete and traditional form of "dedimus." |  The dedimus potestatem authorized the appointed commissioners to take the witness's testimony. 授权令授权指定的委员获取证人证词。 | synonym: commission, warrant, authorization, dedimus | antonym: prohibition, restriction | noun
