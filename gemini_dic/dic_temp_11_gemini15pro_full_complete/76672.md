galvanizers:/ˈɡælvənaɪzərz/
n.
镀锌工人，镀锌器
People or companies that galvanize metal.
The galvanizers coated the steel beams with zinc to prevent rust. 镀锌工人给钢梁镀上了锌，以防生锈。

galvanizers:/ˈɡælvənaɪzərz/
n.
激励者，激励因素
Things or people that stimulate or excite someone to action.
The coach's words were galvanizers for the team, inspiring them to victory. 教练的话激励了球队，激励他们走向胜利。


Etymology->
From galvanize + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from the name of Italian scientist Luigi Galvani.
