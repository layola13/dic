love-ins:/ˈlʌvɪnz/
noun
愛之盛會；嬉皮士的聚會
A social gathering expressing love and peace, typically among hippies.
例句：The park hosted several love-ins during the summer of love.  那个公园在爱之夏期间举办了几场爱之盛会。

例句：The love-in attracted people from all walks of life.  这场爱之盛会吸引了各行各业的人。

近义词：gathering, festival, celebration
反义词：riot, protest
