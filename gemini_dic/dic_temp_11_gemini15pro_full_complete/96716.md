ineffervescence:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsəns/| n. |缺乏活力，沉闷，平淡|The ineffervescence of the meeting made it seem to drag on forever. |例句：The ineffervescence of the meeting made it seem to drag on forever. 会议的沉闷让它看起来像是永远地拖延下去。

The atmosphere of ineffervescence dampened the party spirits.  沉闷的气氛抑制了派对的兴致。|近义词：dullness, flatness, lifelessness, apathy, inertia|反义词: effervescence, liveliness, vibrancy, energy, enthusiasm|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + effervescence (bubbliness, liveliness).  Effervescence ultimately comes from the Latin ex- (out) + fervescere (to boil, to be hot).
