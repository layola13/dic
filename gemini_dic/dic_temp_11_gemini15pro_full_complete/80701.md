gorgons:/ˈɡɔːrɡənz/ | n. | 蛇发女妖；可怕的女人 |  Three monstrous sisters in Greek mythology, Stheno, Euryale, and Medusa, who had snakes for hair and whose look could turn people to stone. | Perseus slew Medusa, one of the Gorgons.  珀尔修斯杀死了美杜莎，她是蛇发女妖之一。| synonyms: monster, fiend, hag | antonyms: beauty, angel | noun
