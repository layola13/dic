decollete:/ˌdeɪkɒlˈteɪ/ /ˌdeɪkɒlˈtiː/
| adj. n.|低胸的，袒胸的；低胸衣，低胸领口|A dress with a low neckline, exposing the neck and shoulders.  A garment with such a neckline.|She wore a decollete gown to the gala. 她穿着一件低胸礼服参加了晚会。|The dress had a daring decollete. 这件衣服有一个大胆的低胸领口。|近义词：low-cut, revealing, plunging|反义词：high-necked, modest|词性:形容词, 名词
词源: From French *décolleter*, "to uncover the neck," from *dé-* (un-, off) + *collet* (collar, neck).
