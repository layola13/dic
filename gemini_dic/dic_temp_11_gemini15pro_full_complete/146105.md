overelliptical:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlɪptɪkəl/
adj.|过分省略的|Excessively elliptical; containing too much ellipsis, making the meaning obscure or difficult to understand.|The sentence was so overelliptical that it was almost unintelligible.|近义词:concise, terse, succinct, laconic, compressed|反义词:prolix, verbose, wordy, lengthy, rambling|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "elliptical" (from Greek "elleipein" meaning "to fall short, leave out").  Essentially, it describes something that has been shortened too much.
