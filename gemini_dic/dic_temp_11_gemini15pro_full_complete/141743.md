orangeism:/ˈɔːrɪndʒɪzəm/ |n.|橙衣主义，奥兰治主义|A Protestant political organization, primarily in Northern Ireland, that supports the continued union with Great Britain.  It is characterized by its strong anti-Catholic sentiment and its association with the Orange Order, a fraternal society.|The Troubles in Northern Ireland were exacerbated by sectarian violence, often fueled by orangeism. 北爱尔兰的动乱因宗派暴力而加剧，而橙衣主义经常是这种暴力的导火索。|近义词:Unionism, Loyalism|反义词:Nationalism, Republicanism|词性:名词
