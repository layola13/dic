nondefensiblenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪblənɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛnsɪblənɪsɪz/
|n.|不可辩护性；无力反驳|The state or quality of being nondefensible; inability to be defended or justified.|His nondefensibleness in the face of the accusations led to his resignation.  面对指控，他的无力反驳导致了他的辞职。|Synonyms: indefensibility, vulnerability, weakness|Antonyms: defensibility, strength, invulnerability|noun
