disgracers:/dɪsˈɡreɪsər z/
n.
玷辱者，丢脸的人
People who bring shame or dishonor upon themselves or others.
The disgracers were banished from the kingdom. 这些丢脸的人被驱逐出王国。
近义词：shamers, dishonorers, scandals
反义词：heroes, idols, role models
词性：名词
