confit:/ˈkɒnfiː/ (UK), /kɔ̃ˈfi/ (French)| n. |油封，蜜饯|A food that has been preserved and cooked slowly in fat or sugar.  Specifically, it refers to meat (typically goose, duck, or pork) cooked and preserved in its own fat. It can also refer to fruit or vegetables preserved in sugar.|例句：The duck confit was rich and flavorful. 这道油封鸭肉浓郁而美味。

例句：She made a delicious confit of oranges. 她做了一道美味的蜜饯橙子。|近义词：preserve, conserve|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh" in some contexts) |词性:noun
