backworm:/ˈbæk.wɜːrm/ | n. | 书呆子，书虫 | A person who spends a lot of time reading and studying, sometimes considered unsociable. | He was a real backworm in high school, always with his nose in a book. 他在高中时是个十足的书呆子，总是埋头苦读。 | bookworm, nerd | social butterfly, extrovert | noun

Etymology:
"Back" + "worm". "Back" likely refers to bending over books, and "worm" alludes to a creature that burrows or delves deeply into something.
