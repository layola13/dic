overbookish: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊkɪʃ/ | adj. | 书呆子气的；过爱读书的；迂腐的 | Excessively devoted to books or scholarship, often to the point of being impractical or out of touch with reality.  Characterized by a preoccupation with books and academic pursuits, sometimes to an excessive or impractical degree. | He was a bit overbookish and struggled to relate to his less academic peers. 他有点书呆子气，很难与不那么学术的同龄人相处。


She had an overbookish quality that made her seem aloof. 她有一种书呆子气，让她显得 aloof（冷漠）。 | 近义词: bookwormy, pedantic, scholarly, studious | 反义词: practical, down-to-earth, unscholarly | 词性: 形容词
