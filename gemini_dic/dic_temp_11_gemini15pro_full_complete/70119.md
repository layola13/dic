feyne:/feɪn/
adjective
美好的，精致的；高兴的，喜悦的
Excellent or fine; glad or joyful.  Primarily a Scots word, though found occasionally in older English texts.
例句：She wore a feyne gown to the ball. 她穿着一件精致的礼服去参加舞会。

例句：He felt feyne and blithe after hearing the good news. 听到好消息后，他感到高兴和愉快。

近义词：fine, excellent, beautiful, splendid, delightful, joyous, glad
反义词：poor, bad, awful, terrible, sad, sorrowful
词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English *fein, *feyn, from Old French fein, fin “fine, of good quality; elegant, refined; subtle, delicate; pure, unadulterated; faithful, loyal; true; perfect; finished, complete; small, thin,” from Latin fīnus.
