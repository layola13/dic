amiabilities:/ˌeɪmiəˈbɪlətiz/

亲切, 友好, 和蔼可亲
The quality of being amiable; pleasantness of disposition or manner.
Her amiabilities made her popular. 她的和蔼可亲使她很受欢迎。
Synonyms: affability, cordiality, geniality, kindness
Antonyms:  unfriendliness, hostility, coldness
