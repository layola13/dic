nonlitigiousness:/ˌnɒnlɪˈtɪdʒəsnəs/
n.
无诉讼倾向；非诉讼性
The state of being not litigious; avoidance of lawsuits or disputes.
The community was known for its nonlitigiousness, preferring to settle disagreements amicably.  这个社区以其非诉讼性而闻名，更喜欢友好地解决分歧。
Near Synonyms: amicability, peacefulness, harmony
Antonyms: litigiousness, contentiousness, quarrelsomeness
noun
