assassinating:/əˈsæsɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb
暗杀，行刺
The act of killing someone, especially a prominent person, for political or religious reasons.
He was charged with assassinating the president. 他被指控暗杀总统。
synonyms: murdering, killing, slaughtering, executing
antonyms: saving, rescuing, protecting
v.
