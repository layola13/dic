glowy:/ˈɡloʊi/| adj.|容光焕发的，光彩照人的，健康的|Having a warm, healthy glow; softly radiant or luminous.|Her skin looked healthy and glowy. 她的皮肤看起来健康而容光焕发。|近义词：radiant, luminous, glowing|反义词：dull, pale|词性:形容词

词源：From glow + -y.  The adjective "glow" meaning "to shine with intense heat" dates back to the 14th century. The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."  Therefore, "glowy" implies being full of or characterized by a glow.
