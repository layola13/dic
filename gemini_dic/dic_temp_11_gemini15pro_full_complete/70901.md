fire-eating:/ˈfaɪərˌiːtɪŋ/
n.|吞火；玩火|The act of performing tricks involving putting fire in one's mouth.|He amazed the crowd with his fire-eating skills. 他用吞火绝技使观众大为惊叹。| 近义词: fire performance, fire breathing| 反义词: None| noun

fire-eating:/ˈfaɪərˌiːtɪŋ/
adj.|玩火的；喷火的|Relating to the act of performing tricks involving fire, or metaphorically, aggressive and provocative.|The fire-eating performer swallowed flaming torches. 那位玩火的表演者吞下了燃烧的火把。| The fire-eating politician delivered a fiery speech. 这位言辞激烈的政治家发表了慷慨激昂的演说。|近义词: inflammatory, provocative, daring|反义词: cautious, timid, moderate|adjective
