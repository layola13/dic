fittingness:/ˈfɪtɪŋnɪs/| n.|恰当，合适，适宜|The fittingness of her remarks impressed everyone. 她的言辞恰当，给大家留下了深刻的印象。|例句：The fittingness of his attire for the occasion was impeccable.  他当天的着装非常得体。

例句：She questioned the fittingness of his behavior at the funeral. 她质疑他在葬礼上的行为是否恰当。|近义词：suitability, appropriateness, aptness, propriety|反义词：unsuitability, inappropriateness, impropriety|词性:名词

词源: From "fitting" + "-ness". "Fitting" comes from the Old English "fitt" (a division or section), later evolving to mean "suitable" or "appropriate".  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
