anacrotic:/ænəˈkrɒtɪk/
adjective
回升的，上升的 (pertaining to the upstroke of a pulse)
Relating to or characterized by anacrotism; having a pulse with a secondary elevation on the ascending limb.
An anacrotic pulse is often a sign of aortic stenosis.  回升脉搏通常是主动脉瓣狭窄的征兆。
The doctor noted the anacrotic quality of the patient's pulse. 医生注意到病人脉搏的回升性质。
近义词:rising, ascending
反义词:catacrotic, descending
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *ἀνάκροτος (anácrotos, “struck up, rebounding”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + κροτέω (croteō, “to strike, beat”).
