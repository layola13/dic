geostationary:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsteɪʃəneri/ | adj. | 地球静止的，对地静止的 |  Relating to a satellite that orbits the Earth at the same speed as the Earth rotates, so that it appears to remain in a fixed position above a particular point on the equator. |  A geostationary satellite can provide continuous coverage of a specific area.  地球静止卫星可以持续覆盖特定区域。 |  Synonyms: geosynchronous | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
