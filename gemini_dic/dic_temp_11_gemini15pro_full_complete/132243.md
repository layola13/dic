nondecisions:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪʒənz/
n.
未决定事项；未作决定
The report criticized the government's nondecisions on key environmental issues.  该报告批评了政府在关键环境问题上的不作为。
Failure to act can be a nondecision with significant consequences. 不采取行动可能是一种未作决定，会造成重大后果。
近义词：inaction, indecision, procrastination
反义词：decisions, actions, resolutions
词性：名词
