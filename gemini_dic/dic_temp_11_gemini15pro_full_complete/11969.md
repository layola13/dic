arterialize: /ɑːrˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ | v. | 使动脉化；使充血 | To make arterial; to oxygenate venous blood. To give something the qualities of an artery. | The surgeon arterialized the vein graft.  外科医生使静脉移植物动脉化。| The procedure arterialized the blood.  这个手术使血液充氧。| synonyms: oxygenate, vascularize | antonyms: deoxygenate | verb
