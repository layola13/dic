oversaturated:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度饱和的
Supplied beyond the point of saturation; excessively saturated.  Excessively filled or supplied.
The market is oversaturated with cheap clothing. 市场充斥着廉价服装。
oversaturated:/ˌəʊvəˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度饱和的
Supplied beyond the point of saturation; excessively saturated.  Excessively filled or supplied.
The colors in the photograph are oversaturated, making it look unnatural. 照片中的颜色过度饱和，看起来不自然。
近义词:saturated, glutted, inundated
反义词:undersaturated, depleted
词性:adjective

词源:
From over- + saturated, past participle of saturate.  Saturate comes from the Latin saturatus, past participle of saturare "to fill full, soak, sate," from satur "full."
