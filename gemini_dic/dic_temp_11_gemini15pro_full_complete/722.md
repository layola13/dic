absently:/ˈæbsəntli/ | adv. | 心不在焉地；茫然地 | In a way that shows that someone is not paying attention to what is happening or being said. | She smiled absently, her mind clearly elsewhere. 她心不在焉地笑了笑，显然心不在焉。| He absently stirred his coffee. 他心不在焉地搅动着咖啡。| 近义词: distractedly, inattentively, vaguely | 反义词: attentively, carefully, thoughtfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the adjective "absent" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Absent" comes from the Latin "absens," present participle of "abesse," meaning "to be away" (from "ab," meaning "away from," + "esse," meaning "to be").
