backchat:/ˈbækˌtʃæt/ | n. |顶嘴，回嘴，无礼的回答|Impudent or disrespectful replies, especially from a child to an adult. |"Don't give me any backchat!" she scolded. “别跟我顶嘴！”她责骂道。|例句：The teacher wouldn't tolerate any backchat from her students. 老师不允许她的学生有任何顶嘴的行为。

例句：He got detention for his constant backchat in class. 他因为在课堂上不断顶嘴而被留校。

|近义词：impudence, insolence, cheek, sass |反义词：respect, obedience |词性：名词
