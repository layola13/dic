mistletoe: /ˈmɪsltəʊ/ (UK), /ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ/ (US) | n. |槲寄生|A leathery-leaved parasitic plant that grows on apple, oak, and other broadleaf trees and bears white glutinous berries in winter.  It is traditionally hung at Christmas, and people kiss under it. |We kissed under the mistletoe at the Christmas party.| 近义词:  None (for the plant itself -  "kissing under the holly" is a similar tradition) |反义词: None | 词性: Noun
