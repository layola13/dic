luxuriates:/ˈlʌkʃəriˌeɪts/| v. |尽情享受；沉溺于；繁茂生长|To indulge in something that provides pleasure or comfort, often to excess; to grow profusely.|例句：She luxuriates in long, hot baths. 她尽情享受长时间的热水浴。

例句：The vegetation luxuriates in the humid climate.  这些植物在潮湿的气候中生长茂盛。|近义词：indulges, revels, basks, thrives, flourishes|反义词：abstains, suffers, withers|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *luxuriari*, present active infinitive of *luxuriō* (“be luxuriant, abound, be wanton, riot”), from *luxuria* (“luxury, abundance”).
