inviolacy:/ɪnˌvaɪəˈləsɪ/ | n. | 不可侵犯；神圣；安全 |The inviolacy of the home is a cherished principle. 住宅不可侵犯是一项珍视的原则。|例句：The inviolacy of human rights is a fundamental principle. 人权不可侵犯是一项基本原则。|例句：The constitution guarantees the inviolacy of private property. 宪法保障私有财产不可侵犯。|近义词：sanctity, sacredness, immunity |反义词：violation, infringement |词性：名词

词源: From Late Latin *inviolabilitas, from inviolatus (“uninjured, unharmed”) + -itas.  Inviolatus is from in- (“not”) + violatus, past participle of violare (“to violate, injure”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weyə- (“to bend, wind”).
