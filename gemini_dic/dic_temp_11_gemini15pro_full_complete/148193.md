overshadowy:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædəʊi/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈʃædəʊi/ (UK)| adj.|阴暗的，蒙上阴影的；使人忧郁的；（重要性或优越性上）超过，使逊色的，使黯然失色的|dominating to the point of seeming to obscure or eclipse; casting a shadow over something else|The team's victory was overshadowed by the injury to their star player.  这支队伍的胜利因他们的明星球员受伤而黯然失色。

The looming threat of war overshadowed the peace talks. 战争的迫在眉睫的威胁给和平谈判蒙上了阴影。|近义词：dark, gloomy, somber, depressing, eclipsing, outweighing|反义词：bright, sunny, cheerful, uplifting, insignificant|词性:形容词
