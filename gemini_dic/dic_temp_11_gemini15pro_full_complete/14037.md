auspicious:/ɔːˈspɪʃəs/| adj.|吉利的，吉祥的，幸运的|Suggesting a positive and successful future; indicating good fortune.|例句：It was an auspicious start to the new year. 这是新年的一个吉祥的开始。|例句：The project began on an auspicious day.  这个项目在一个吉利的日子开始了。|近义词：favorable, fortunate, promising, propitious|反义词：inauspicious, unfavorable, ominous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *auspicium* "divination by observing the flight of birds," from *avis* "bird" + *specere* "to look at."
