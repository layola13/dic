nonresponsibly:/ˌnɒnrɪˈspɒnsɪbli/ (US) /ˌnɑːnrɪˈspɑːnsɪbli/| adv.|不负责任地|In a manner that avoids or neglects responsibility.|He acted nonresponsibly by leaving the project unfinished.|近义词: irresponsibly, carelessly, recklessly|反义词: responsibly, carefully, conscientiously|词性:副词

词源: non- + responsibly.  "Responsibly" comes from "responsible," which traces back to the Latin "respondēre" meaning "to answer."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
