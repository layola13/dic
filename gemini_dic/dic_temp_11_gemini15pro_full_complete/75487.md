frustulent:/ˈfrʌstrələnt/
adjective
令人沮丧的; 令人泄气的
causing frustration; disappointing.
例句：The frustulent search for the missing document lasted for hours.  令人沮丧的寻找丢失文件的过程持续了数小时。

例句：The team experienced a frustulent loss in the championship game.  球队在冠军赛中经历了一场令人沮丧的失利。

近义词：disappointing, frustrating, discouraging, thwarting
反义词：satisfying, gratifying, rewarding
词性: adjective


词源：From Latin frustrātus, past participle of frustrārī (“to deceive, disappoint, frustrate”), from frustra (“in vain”).
