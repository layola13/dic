antinarcotic:/ˌænti.nɑːrˈkɑːtɪk/
antinarcotic: adj.|抗麻醉的，麻醉抑制的|Preventing or counteracting the effects of narcotic drugs. |The police conducted an antinarcotic raid. 警方进行了缉毒突击搜查。|Synonyms: antidrug, counter-narcotic|Antonyms: narcotic|adjective
antinarcotic:/ˌænti.nɑːrˈkɑːtɪk/
antinarcotic: n.|抗麻醉药，麻醉抑制剂|A drug that counteracts the effects of narcotics. |The doctor administered an antinarcotic to the patient. 医生给病人服用了抗麻醉药。|Synonyms: antidote, narcotic antagonist|Antonyms: narcotic|noun
