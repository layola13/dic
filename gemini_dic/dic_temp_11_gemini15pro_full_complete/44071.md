crinkliness: /ˈkrɪŋklɪnɪs/
noun
皱巴巴; 起皱
the quality of being crinkled or wrinkled.
The crinkliness of the old map made it difficult to read.  这幅旧地图皱巴巴的，很难阅读。
The crinkliness of the fabric added to its charm.  这种布料的褶皱增加了它的魅力。
synonyms: wrinkledness, rumpledness, crimpledness
antonyms: smoothness, flatness, evenness
