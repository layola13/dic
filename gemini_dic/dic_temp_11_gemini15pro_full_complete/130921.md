nonascertainably:/ˌnɒnæsərˈteɪnəbli/
adjective
无法确定的
Not able to be ascertained; impossible to determine with certainty.
The cause of the fire was nonascertainably due to faulty wiring, but further investigation is needed.  火灾原因无法确定是否由于线路故障，但需要进一步调查。
The damage was nonascertainably linked to the storm, though it seems likely.  损害与风暴的联系无法确定，尽管看起来很可能有关。
Synonyms: indeterminable, indefinable, unknowable, unprovable, unverifiable
Antonyms: ascertainable, determinable, definable, knowable, provable, verifiable
词性: adjective
