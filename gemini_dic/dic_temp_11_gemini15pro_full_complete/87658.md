hexadecapole:/ˌhɛksəˈdɛkəˌpoʊl/ | n. |十六极|A distribution of electric charge or magnetic strength which can be represented as a series of terms, the first of which is a dipole, the second a quadrupole, the third an octopole, the fourth a hexadecapole, and so on.|The hexadecapole moment contributes to the finer details of the electric field.| 近义词:multipole | 反义词: (No direct antonym exists, possibly monopole for the simplest case) | noun
