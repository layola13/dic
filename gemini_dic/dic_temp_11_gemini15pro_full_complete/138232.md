nosophobia:/ˌnoʊsəˈfoʊbiə/| n.|疾病恐惧症，恐病症|An abnormal and persistent fear of disease. It is a type of specific phobia, which involves an excessive and unreasonable fear of a particular object, situation, or activity.|She suffers from nosophobia and is constantly worried about getting sick.|近义词:pathophobia,  hypochondria|反义词:None|Noun
