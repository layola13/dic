concentual:/kənˈsɛptʃuəl/
adjective
概念上的，观念上的
Relating to or based on mental concepts.
例句：The architect presented a conceptual drawing of the building.  建筑师展示了该建筑的概念图。

例句：The project is still in its conceptual stage.  这个项目还处于概念阶段。

近义词：abstract, theoretical, notional
反义词：concrete, tangible, actual
词性: adjective
