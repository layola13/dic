contemplance:/ˈkɒntɛmpləns/ /kɑːnˈtɛmpləns/ | n. |沉思，凝视，盘算| The act of looking thoughtfully at something for a long time; deep reflective thought.|She sat in quiet contemplation of the painting. 她静静地坐着凝视着这幅画。| 近义词: meditation, reflection, deliberation, musing | 反义词: inattention, disregard, heedlessness | 词性: noun

contemplance:/ˈkɒntɛmpləns/ /kɑːnˈtɛmpləns/ | n. |沉思，凝视，盘算|The state of being lost in thought; the act of thinking deeply about something.|He was lost in contemplation of the universe. 他陷入了对宇宙的沉思。| 近义词: thoughtfulness, pensiveness | 反义词:  frivolity, carelessness| 词性: noun
