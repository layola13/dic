grimful: /ˈɡrɪmfʊl/
adjective
阴沉的，可怕的
Filled with or expressing grimness; gloomy and forbidding.
The grimful news of the impending war cast a shadow over the entire nation.  这即将到来的战争的可怕消息给整个国家蒙上了一层阴影。

The grimful expression on his face told me that the situation was serious. 他脸上阴沉的表情告诉我情况很严重。

近义词: grim, somber, gloomy, depressing, forbidding
反义词: cheerful, bright, happy, joyful, lighthearted
词性: adjective

词源:  A combination of "grim" (from Old English *grim(m), meaning "fierce, cruel, savage") and the suffix "-ful" (meaning "full of").
