archetypical:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtɪpɪkəl/ | adj. | 原型的，典型的 | Representing the most typical or perfect example of a quality or idea. |  The hero's journey is an archetypical story found in many cultures. 英雄之旅是在许多文化中都能找到的典型故事。|  His rugged individualism is archetypical of the American spirit. 他坚定的个人主义是美国精神的典型代表。 | 近义词: classic, quintessential, exemplary, prototypical | 反义词: atypical, unusual, aberrant | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From archetype + -al.  Archetype comes from Late Latin *archetypum, from Greek arkhetypon "original pattern," from arkhe- "primitive" + typos "impression, mold."
