falciform:/ˈfɔːlsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|镰状的，新月形的|Shaped like a sickle or crescent; curved and tapering to a point.|The doctor identified the falciform ligament during the surgery. 医生在手术过程中识别出了镰状韧带。|The peregrine falcon has long, falciform wings.  游隼有着长长的镰刀形翅膀。|crescent-shaped, sickle-shaped|straight|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *falciformis*, from falx (sickle) + -formis (having the form of). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)pelə- (to split, splinter).
