gemination:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|重复，双生，成对|the act of doubling a consonant or vowel sound; the resulting sound|The gemination of the consonant "p" in "apple" is a common feature of some dialects.|近义词：doubling, reduplication|反义词:singleton, simplification|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *geminātiō*, from *geminātus*, past participle of *gemināre* (“to double, repeat”), from *geminus* (“twin”).
