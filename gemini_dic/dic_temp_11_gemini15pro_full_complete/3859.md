agoras:/ˈæɡərəz/| n. |集市，广场；agora的复数形式|A public open space used for assemblies and markets, especially in ancient Greece.  古代希腊用于集会和市场的公共露天场所，尤指古希腊的广场。|The agoras of ancient Greece were centers of social and political life. 古希腊的广场是社会和政治生活的中心。|Agoras were important for trade and commerce in ancient cities.  广场在古代城市的贸易和商业中很重要。|近义词：marketplace, forum, plaza, square|反义词:None|名词
