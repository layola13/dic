non-methodistic:/nɒnˌmɛθəˈdɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非循道宗的，不拘泥于形式的 | Not following the doctrines or practices of Methodism, or more generally, not rigidly following any particular method or system. |  The artist's non-methodistic approach to painting allowed for greater creativity and spontaneity.  这位艺术家不拘泥于形式的绘画方法，使得他的作品更具创造性和自发性。 | Example: His non-methodistic approach to problem-solving was refreshing. 他不拘泥于形式的解决问题的方法令人耳目一新。| Synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, informal, flexible, adaptable | Antonyms: methodical, systematic, rigid, conventional, orthodox | Adjective
