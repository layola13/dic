nondeposition:/ˌnɒndɛpəˈzɪʃən/
n.
未沉积，未存放
The nondeposition of sediment in this area suggests a period of erosion.
该地区没有沉积物沉积表明存在一段时间的侵蚀。
nondeposition of evidence
未提交证据
The nondeposition of the crucial witness hampered the prosecution's case.
关键证人未出庭作证妨碍了控方的案件。
Near synonyms: nonaccumulation, absence of deposition
Antonyms: deposition, sedimentation, accumulation
noun
