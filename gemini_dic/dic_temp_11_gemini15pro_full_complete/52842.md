dimension:/daɪˈmɛnʃən/ /dɪˈmɛnʃən/ | n. |维度，尺寸，方面|a measurable extent of some kind, such as length, breadth, depth, or height.|The dimensions of the room are 10 feet by 12 feet.  房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。|synonyms: size, magnitude, extent, measurement|antonyms: insignificance, unimportance|noun

dimension:/daɪˈmɛnʃən/ /dɪˈmɛnʃən/ | n. |（事物的）方面，侧面|an aspect or feature of a situation, problem, or thing.|There is a political dimension to the problem. 这个问题有一个政治层面。|synonyms: aspect, feature, facet, element|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun
