nonperpetual:/ˌnɑːnpərˈpetʃuəl/
adjective
非永久的，非永恒的
Not perpetual; not continuing or enduring forever; temporary.
The lease agreement is nonperpetual and will expire in five years.  租约是非永久性的，五年后到期。
The nonperpetual nature of the contract allows for renegotiation. 合同的非永久性允许重新谈判。
Synonyms: temporary, finite, transient, impermanent
Antonyms: perpetual, eternal, everlasting, permanent
词性: 形容词
