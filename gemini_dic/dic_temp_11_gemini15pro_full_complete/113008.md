lumberjack: /ˈlʌmbərˌdʒæk/ (US), /ˈlʌmbədʒæk/ (UK)| n.|伐木工人|A person who fells trees and transports the logs to sawmills.|例句：The lumberjack swung his axe with practiced ease.  伐木工人熟练地挥舞着斧头。

例句：Lumberjacks work long hours in challenging conditions. 伐木工人在艰苦的条件下长时间工作。


近义词：logger, woodcutter|反义词: None (No direct opposite)|词性: Noun
