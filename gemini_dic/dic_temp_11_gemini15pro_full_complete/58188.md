drowsily:/ˈdraʊzɪli/
adverb
昏昏欲睡地，困倦地
in a sleepy way
She smiled drowsily and closed her eyes. 她困倦地笑了笑，闭上了眼睛。
He sat drowsily in the armchair. 他昏昏欲睡地坐在扶手椅上。
近义词: sleepily, lethargically
反义词: alertly, energetically
词性: adverb

词源:
From Middle English *drosli, from drosen +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drūsōną (“to be sluggish, troubled”).
