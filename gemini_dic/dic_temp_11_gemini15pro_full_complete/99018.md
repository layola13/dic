interaffiliation:/ˌɪntərəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/ | n. |相互联系，相互隶属，互相关联|A close relationship or association between different groups or organizations, often involving shared membership or goals.| The interaffiliation of the two universities allowed students to take courses at both institutions. 两所大学的相互联系允许学生在两所大学上课。| 近义词：interconnection, interrelationship, linkage, association | 反义词：separation, disconnection, isolation | 词性: noun

Etymology:
inter- (Latin) meaning "between, among" + affiliation (from Late Latin affiliatio, from affiliare "to adopt as a son," from ad- "to" + filius "son").
