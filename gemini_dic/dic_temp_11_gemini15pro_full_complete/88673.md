hoax:/hoʊks/| n. vt.|骗局，恶作剧；欺骗，愚弄|A deliberate deception or trickery.|The radio station apologized for the hoax call that caused widespread panic.  广播电台为造成 widespread panic 的恶作剧电话道歉。|He hoaxed the public with a false story. 他用一个假故事欺骗了公众。|近义词：trick, prank, deception, fraud, sham|反义词：truth, reality|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from *hocus*, itself of unknown origin, perhaps a variant of *hocus-pocus*.
