kibibyte:/ˈkɪbɪbaɪt/
n.|千字节（等于1024字节）|A kibibyte is a unit of digital information storage equal to 1024 bytes. It is often used in computer science and related fields.|My flash drive has a capacity of 8 kibibytes.|Synonym: kilobyte (in common usage, though technically different)|Antonym: N/A (Units of measurement don't typically have antonyms)|noun
