overmixing:/ˌōvərˈmɪksɪŋ/ | n. | 过度混合 | The act of mixing ingredients, especially in baking, for too long. This can result in tough or dense baked goods. | Overmixing the batter made the cake tough.  过度搅拌面糊使蛋糕变硬了。| Overbeating, overworking | Undermixing | noun
