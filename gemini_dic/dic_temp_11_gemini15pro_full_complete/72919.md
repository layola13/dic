fondant:/ˈfɑːndənt/ (US) /ˈfɒndənt/ (UK)| n. |翻糖；软糖；熔化的糖|A thick, creamy sugar paste used for icing cakes and making sweets.|The wedding cake was covered in smooth white fondant. 婚礼蛋糕上覆盖着光滑的白色翻糖。|近义词：icing, frosting|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ganache" as a contrasting icing)|名词

fondant:/ˈfɑːndənt/ (US) /ˈfɒndənt/ (UK)| adj. |熔化的；融化的|Melting; dissolving.|The fondant chocolate was rich and decadent.  融化的巧克力浓郁而美味。|近义词：melted, molten|反义词: solid, hard|形容词
