draftiness:/ˈdræftinɪs/
n.
通风；透风；有穿堂风
The state of being drafty; characterized by currents of air.
The draftiness of the old house made it difficult to heat.  这栋老房子的通风情况使得它很难被加热。
Synonyms: airiness, ventilation, breeziness
Antonyms: stuffiness, airlessness, airtightness
