blueprint:/ˈbluːprɪnt/| n.|蓝图，设计图，方案|A detailed plan or program of action.|The architects presented the blueprint for the new building. 建筑师们展示了新建筑的蓝图。|synonyms: plan, design, scheme, draft|antonyms: improvisation,  off-the-cuff|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From blue + print.  Originally, blueprints were white lines on a blue background, created by a contact print process.
