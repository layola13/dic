overpresumptuousness:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmpʧuːəsnəs/| n. | 过于自负，傲慢，放肆 |The state of being excessively or unwarrantedly presumptuous; overconfidence or arrogance.  An excessive belief in one's own importance or abilities. | His overpresumptuousness alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事们。 | Near Synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, conceit, presumption, audacity, superciliousness | Near Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence, self-effacement | noun
