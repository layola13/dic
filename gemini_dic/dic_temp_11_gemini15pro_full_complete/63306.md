entice:/ɪnˈtaɪs/ /ɛnˈtaɪs/| vt.|诱惑；诱使；怂恿|To attract someone to a particular activity or place by offering something pleasant or advantageous.|The store tried to entice customers with a special sale.  这家商店试图用特价促销来吸引顾客。

The smell of freshly baked bread enticed me into the bakery.  新鲜出炉面包的香味把我吸引进了面包店。|近义词：tempt, allure, seduce, attract|反义词：repel, deter, discourage|verb
