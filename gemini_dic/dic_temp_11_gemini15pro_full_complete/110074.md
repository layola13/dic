lichenivorous:/laɪˈkɛnɪvərəs/| adj.|食地衣的，以地衣为食的|Feeding on lichens.  An animal that subsists on lichens is described as lichenivorous.|Reindeer are lichenivorous animals, especially in winter. 驯鹿是食地衣的动物，尤其是在冬天。|The snail is lichenivorous and grazes on the rocks. 这种蜗牛以地衣为食，在岩石上吃草。|近义词: lichen-eating|反义词: None|词性: adjective

词源:
lichen- + -vorous.  From *lichen* (lichen) + *-vorous* (eating).  Ultimately from Greek *leikhēn* (lichen) and Latin *vorare* (to devour).
