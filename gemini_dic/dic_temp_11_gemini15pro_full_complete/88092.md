highs:/haɪz/
noun
高点；高潮；最高记录；兴旺时期
periods of euphoria or elation, often induced by drugs
The runner experienced highs and lows throughout the marathon. 这位跑步者在整个马拉松比赛中经历了高潮和低谷。
Synonyms: peaks, crests, summits
Antonyms: lows, depressions, nadirs
n.
