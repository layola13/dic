numismatist:/njuːˈmɪzmətɪst/ /nuːˈmɪzmətɪst/| n. |钱币学家；钱币收藏家|A person who studies or collects coins, medals, paper money, etc.|例句：The numismatist displayed his rare coin collection.  这位钱币学家展示了他珍稀的钱币收藏。

例句：She became a renowned numismatist after years of studying ancient currency.  经过多年的古代货币研究，她成为了一位著名的钱币学家。|近义词：coin collector, philatelist (for stamps)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French numismate, from Latin numisma, from Ancient Greek νόμισμα (nómisma, “current coin, usage, custom, law”), from νομίζω (nomízō, “I hold as a custom, use customarily, practice, observe a custom, hold as usual or current, deem, consider, suppose, imagine, opine”), from νόμος (nómos, “usage, custom, law”).
