inductile:/ɪnˈdʌktəl/ /ɪnˈdʌktaɪl/ | adj. | 非延展性的；不柔韧的；不易拉成丝的 | Not ductile; incapable of being drawn out into wire or threads. | The material proved to be inductile and unsuitable for wiring.  这种材料被证明是不易延展的，不适合用于布线。|The potter found the clay inductile and difficult to shape.  陶工发现这种粘土不易延展，难以塑形。| 近义词：brittle, fragile, inflexible, rigid, stiff | 反义词：ductile, malleable, pliable, flexible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + ductile (capable of being drawn into wire), from Latin ductilis, from ducere (to lead).
