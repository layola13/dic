monogramming: /ˈmɒnəɡræmɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəɡræmɪŋ/ (US)| n.|绣名字的首字母缩写; 刺绣字母组合|The act of personalizing an item by adding a monogram, typically initials.  The process involves embroidering, printing, or otherwise marking the item with these initials.|She requested monogramming on her new towels. 她要求在新毛巾上绣上名字的首字母。|近义词: personalization, customization, marking|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'plain' in the context of an unmarked item)|词性:noun
