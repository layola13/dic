nonlustrously:/ˌnɒnˈlʌstrəsli/ /ˌnɑːnˈlʌstrəsli/| adv. | 无光泽地，暗淡地 | In a way that lacks luster or shine; dully. | The moon shone nonlustrously through the thick clouds. 月亮透过厚厚的云层发出暗淡的光芒。|  The old silver plate sat nonlustrously on the shelf, neglected for years.  这枚旧的银盘暗淡无光地摆放在架子上，已经被忽视多年了。 | 近义词：dully, dimly, faintly | 反义词：brightly, shiningly, lustrously | 词性: 副词

词源:

non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
lustrous (adjective):  from Old French *lustros, from Latin *lustrosus, from luster "brightness"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
