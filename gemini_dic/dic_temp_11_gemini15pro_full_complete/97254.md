inflict:/ɪnˈflɪkt/| vt.|使遭受，使承受|To cause (something unpleasant or painful) to be suffered by someone or something.|例句：The sanctions inflicted severe economic hardship on the country. 制裁给该国带来了严重的经济困难。|例句：They inflicted a humiliating defeat on the home team. 他们给主队带来了屈辱的失败。|近义词：impose, cause, wreak, visit|反义词：alleviate, relieve, soothe|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin inflictus, past participle of infligere "to strike, dash against," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + fligere "to strike."
