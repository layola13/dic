fun's:/fʌnz/ | n. | 乐趣，玩笑; |  Non-standard and often considered incorrect form of "fun" used to indicate possession.  It attempts to treat "fun" as a noun that can take the possessive 's, like "cat's toys".  Generally, "fun" is considered an uncountable noun and doesn't take a possessive. |  "Where's the fun's in that?" (non-standard)  应该说 "Where's the fun in that?"  那有什么乐趣？ | 近义词: enjoyment, pleasure, amusement | 反义词: boredom, misery | 词性: Noun (non-standard usage)
