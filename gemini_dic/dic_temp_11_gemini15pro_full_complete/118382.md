merling:/ˈmɜːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 美人鱼；年轻的美人鱼 |A mythical creature with the head and upper body of a human and the tail of a fish, typically a young or small mermaid. |例句：The merling swam gracefully through the coral reef. 小美人鱼优雅地在珊瑚礁中游动。

例句：Legends tell of merlings luring sailors to their doom with enchanting songs. 传说美人鱼用迷人的歌声引诱水手走向毁灭。|近义词：mermaid, siren |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially "land-dweller") |词性:noun
