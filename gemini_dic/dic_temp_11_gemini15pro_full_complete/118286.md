mercilessly: /ˈmɜːrsɪləsli/ (US) /ˈmɜːsɪləsli/ (UK) | adv. | 无情地，残酷地 |  In a merciless manner; without pity or compassion.  Showing no mercy; cruel. | The soldiers mercilessly attacked the village. 士兵们无情地攻击了村庄。|  ruthlessly, cruelly, heartlessly, pitilessly, relentlessly |  mercifully, compassionately, kindly | adverb

Etymology:
From "merciless" + "-ly".  "Merciless" comes from Middle English "mercilees", from "merci" (mercy) + "-less" (without).  Ultimately from Latin "merces" (reward, wages, pity).
