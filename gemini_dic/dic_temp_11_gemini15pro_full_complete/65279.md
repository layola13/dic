euchology:/juːˈkɒlədʒi/ | n. |祷告书；祷告仪式；祷告学|A book of prayers, or the study of prayers and prayer forms.  It refers to the system of prayers used in a particular church or denomination, or the study of such systems.|例句：The priest consulted the euchology before the service.  神父在礼拜前查阅了祷告书。|例句：He is an expert in euchology and the history of liturgical prayer. 他是祷告学和礼拜仪式祈祷史方面的专家。|近义词：prayer book, liturgy, ritual|反义词: None|词性: Noun
