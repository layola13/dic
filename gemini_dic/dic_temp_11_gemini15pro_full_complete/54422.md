disinfectant: /ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt/ | n. adj. | 消毒剂；消毒的 | A chemical liquid that destroys bacteria. |

例句：
The disinfectant is used to clean the wound.
这种消毒剂用来清洗伤口。

The disinfectant smell permeated the hospital.
医院里弥漫着消毒剂的气味。


近义词：antiseptic, germicide, sterilizer | 反义词: contaminant | 词性: noun, adjective
