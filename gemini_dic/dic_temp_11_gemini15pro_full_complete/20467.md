bittily:/ˈbɪtɪli/| adv.|少量地，零星地，尖刻地|In a bitty, fragmented manner; sharply, sarcastically.|She spoke bittily about her former colleagues. 她尖刻地谈论她以前的同事。|近义词：piecemeal, fragmentarily, acrimoniously, sharply|反义词：generously, kindly, smoothly|词性: 副词

Etymology: From "bit" (small piece) + "-y" (forming adjectives) + "-ly" (forming adverbs).
