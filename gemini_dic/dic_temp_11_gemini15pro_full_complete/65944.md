exactingly:/ɪɡˈzæktɪŋli/
adverb|严格地，精确地|In a demanding and precise manner; with strict attention to detail.|The coach exactingly reviewed every play, looking for flaws. 教练严格地回顾了每一个动作，寻找 flaws。|例句：He exactingly followed the recipe. 他严格地按照食谱操作。|近义词：rigorously, meticulously, precisely, carefully|反义词：carelessly, loosely, approximately|词性: 副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From exact + -ing + -ly.  "Exact" comes from the Latin "exactus," past participle of "exigere," meaning "to drive out, demand, ascertain."
