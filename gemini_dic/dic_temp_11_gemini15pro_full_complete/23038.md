boulier:/bu.lje/ | n. | 算盘 | An abacus, a counting frame with beads sliding on rods, used for performing calculations. |  He used a boulier to quickly add up the long list of numbers. 他用算盘快速地把一长串数字加起来。 | Example: The shopkeeper used a boulier to calculate the total cost. 店主用算盘计算总价。| Synonym: abacus | Antonym: calculator | noun
