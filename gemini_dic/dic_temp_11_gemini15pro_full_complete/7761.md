announced: /əˈnaʊnst/
Verb
宣布，公布
to make something known publicly or officially.
The company announced a new product launch.  公司宣布了新产品的发布。
The government announced new tax policies. 政府公布了新的税收政策。
Synonyms: declared, proclaimed, publicized, revealed
Antonyms: concealed, hid, withheld
词性: 动词
