interscene:/ˈɪntəˌsiːn/| n.|幕间休息；幕间表演；幕间插曲|A short performance or entertainment given between the acts of a play or other performance.|The interscene featured a juggling act.|近义词：interlude, intermission, interval|反义词: main act, principal performance|词性:名词
Etymology: inter- (Latin, between) + scene (Latin scaena, from Greek skēnē, stage, scene)
