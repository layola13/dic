decategorized:/diːˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd/
|adj.|未分类的，取消分类的|Removed from a category; no longer categorized.  This often implies a deliberate act of removing something from a classification system.|The decategorized data made analysis more difficult.|近义词:unclassified, uncategorized|反义词:categorized, classified|词性:形容词
decategorize:/diːˈkætəɡəˌraɪz/
|v.|取消分类，从类别中移除|To remove something from a category or classification system.|The librarian decategorized the book, making it harder to find.|近义词:unclassify, uncategorize|反义词:categorize, classify|词性:动词
decategorization:/diːˌkætəɡərɪˈzeɪʃən/
