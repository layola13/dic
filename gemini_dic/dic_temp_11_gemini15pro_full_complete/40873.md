cookeries:/ˈkʊkəriːz/
n.
烹饪法; 烹调术; 菜肴;
The art or practice of cooking; culinary arts. Also, dishes prepared by cooking.
She learned about French cookeries during her trip to Paris. 她去巴黎旅行时学习了法国烹饪。
近义词:cuisine, cooking, gastronomy
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"raw food" 生食)
词性:名词(复数)

cookery:/ˈkʊkəri/
n.
烹饪法; 烹调术; 菜肴;
The art or practice of cooking; culinary arts. Also, dishes prepared by cooking.
She is taking a cookery class. 她正在上烹饪课。
近义词:cuisine, cooking, gastronomy
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"raw food" 生食)
词性:名词(单数)

词源：
From Middle English *cokerie, from Old French coquerie (“kitchen, cooking”), from coque (“cook”), from Latin coquus (“cook”), from coquere (“to cook”).
