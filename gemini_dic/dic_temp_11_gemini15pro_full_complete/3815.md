agnostic: /æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | n. adj. |不可知论者，不可知论的 | A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God.  Relating to agnosticism. |例句：He is an agnostic and doesn't subscribe to any particular religion. 他是一个不可知论者，不信仰任何特定的宗教。

|例句：She has an agnostic view on the existence of life after death. 她对死后生命的存在持不可知论的观点。 |近义词：skeptic, doubter, unbeliever |反义词：believer, devotee, faithful |词性: 名词, 形容词
