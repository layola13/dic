hypoxanthine:/ˌhaɪpɒˈzænthɪn/ | n. |次黄嘌呤|A purine derivative, C5H4N4O, closely related to xanthine and uric acid, found in plant and animal tissues and used in the biosynthesis of nucleic acids.  It's a naturally occurring purine base found in DNA and RNA, often as a result of adenine deamination.|Hypoxanthine can be used as a substrate to produce inosine monophosphate (IMP). 次黄嘌呤可以用作生产肌苷单磷酸 (IMP) 的底物。| Hypoxanthine is a product of adenine deamination. 次黄嘌呤是腺嘌呤脱氨基的产物。|Synonym: 6-Hydroxypurine, 6-oxypurine|Antonym: N/A|noun
