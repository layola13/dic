lepidopteran:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərən/ | adj. n. |鳞翅目的；鳞翅目昆虫|Relating to or denoting an insect of the order Lepidoptera, which comprises the butterflies and moths.  Characterized by two pairs of large, typically brightly coloured wings covered with minute overlapping scales.|Butterflies and moths are lepidopterans. 蝴蝶和飞蛾都是鳞翅目昆虫。|The museum has a vast collection of lepidopteran specimens.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的鳞翅目昆虫标本。|近义词: moth, butterfly |反义词: None |词性: adjective, noun
