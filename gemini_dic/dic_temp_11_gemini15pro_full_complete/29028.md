cartooned:/kɑːrˈtuːnd/ /kɑːrˈtuːnd/
adjective
卡通化的；画成卡通的
depicted or represented as in a cartoon.
The walls were cartooned with funny animals.  墙上画着卡通动物。
The president was cartooned in the newspaper as a pig. 报纸上把总统画成了一头猪。
近义词: animated, caricatured, illustrated
反义词: realistic, photographic
词性: adjective
