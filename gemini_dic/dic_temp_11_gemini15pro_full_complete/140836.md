on-sufferance:/ɒn ˈsʌf(ə)rəns/| n.|容忍；默许；勉强许可|A state of being tolerated or allowed to remain in a place or position, typically when one has no right to be there.  The permission is precarious and can be withdrawn at any time.|例句：They were living there on sufferance. 他们被默许住在那里。

例句：He only remained in the job on sufferance. 他只是被勉强允许继续留任。|近义词：toleration, forbearance, permission, allowance|反义词：prohibition, forbiddance|词性: noun
