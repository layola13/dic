bawson:/ˈbɔːsən/| n.| 舵工，水手长|A petty officer on a merchant ship who controls the work of other seamen, especially when they are mooring or anchoring the ship. |The bawson yelled orders to the deckhands. 舵工向甲板水手大声喊着命令。|近义词：boatswain, bosun|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "landlubber" as a humorous contrast) |noun
