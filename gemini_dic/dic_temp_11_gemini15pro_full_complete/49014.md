degrades:/dɪˈɡreɪdz/
verb
降低；退化；降解；使丢脸
to reduce someone to a lower rank; to make something worse; to break down (a substance)
Exposure to sunlight degrades the paint. 暴露在阳光下会使油漆褪色。
His actions degraded him in the eyes of his colleagues. 他的行为在他同事眼中降低了他的身份。
synonyms: deteriorate, worsen, decline, corrupt, demean, disgrace
antonyms: enhance, improve, upgrade, elevate, honor
词性:动词
