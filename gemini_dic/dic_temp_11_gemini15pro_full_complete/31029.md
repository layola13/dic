cham:/tʃæm/ | n. | 坎儿，裂缝，斜面 | A cham is a bevel or sloping edge. | The cham on the edge of the table prevented the tablecloth from slipping. 桌子边缘的斜面防止桌布滑落。 | The chamfer on the brick allowed for a tighter fit. 砖块上的斜角使其更紧密地贴合。 | 近义词：bevel, chamfer, slope | 反义词: edge, corner | 词性: noun

cham:/tʃæm/ | v. | 使成斜角，给…加斜边 | To cham means to bevel or create a sloping edge. |  He chammed the edges of the wood to make it safer. 他把木头的边缘削成斜角，使它更安全。| The machinist chammed the metal part to ensure a smooth fit.  机械师将金属零件倒角以确保其平滑贴合。| 近义词：bevel, chamfer | 反义词: square, flatten | 词性: verb
