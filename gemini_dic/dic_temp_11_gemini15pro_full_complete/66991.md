expertness:/ˈɛkspərtnɪs/| n. |专门技能，熟练，专长|The expertness of her playing was breathtaking. 她的演奏技巧令人叹为观止。|近义词：skill, proficiency, mastery, expertise|反义词：ineptitude, incompetence, clumsiness|词性:名词

词源: From expert + -ness.  Derived from the Latin *expertus*, past participle of *experiri* "to try, test".
