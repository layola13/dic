measurable:/ˈmeʒərəbl/ (US), /ˈmɛʒərəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可测量的，可衡量的|Able to be measured, quantifiable.|例句：The impact of the new policy is measurable. 新政策的影响是可以衡量的。|例句：The company's success is measurable in terms of profit.  这家公司的成功可以用利润来衡量。|近义词：quantifiable, assessable, calculable|反义词：immeasurable, incalculable|词性:形容词
