intershades:/ˈɪntərʃeɪdz/ | n. |中间色调；中间影调| The subtle intershades of the painting created a sense of depth. |例句：The photograph captured the delicate intershades of gray in the clouds. 照片捕捉到了云层中微妙的灰色调。

例句：The artist skillfully blended the intershades to achieve a realistic effect. 艺术家巧妙地混合了中间色调，以达到逼真的效果。|近义词：midtones, nuances, gradations |反义词: extremes, contrasts |词性:名词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "shades" (gradations of color or tone).  The prefix "inter-" indicates something that exists or occurs between other things. "Shades" refers to the variations in lightness or darkness of a color.
