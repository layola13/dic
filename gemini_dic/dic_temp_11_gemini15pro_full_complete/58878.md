durar:/duˈɾaɾ/ (Spanish) | v. | 持续，持久，耐久 | To last, endure, or remain in existence. | The battery should durar for about five hours.  电池应该能持续大约五个小时。

The pain only duró a few minutes. 疼痛只持续了几分钟。 | 近义词：last, persist, endure, remain | 反义词：cease, stop, end, perish | 词性：动词
