cropless:/ˈkrɑːplɪs/| adj.|无裁剪的，未裁剪的|Refers to images or videos that are displayed in their entirety, without any cropping or trimming.  Often used in the context of social media platforms.|She posted a cropless photo on Instagram. 她在Instagram上发布了一张未裁剪的照片。|近义词：full-frame, uncropped, complete|反义词:cropped, trimmed|词性:adjective
