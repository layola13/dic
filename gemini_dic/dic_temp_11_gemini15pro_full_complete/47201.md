dayfly:/ˈdeɪflaɪ/| n. |蜉蝣，朝生暮死之物|An insect that lives only for a very short time.  A short-lived thing or person.|例句：Like a dayfly, his fame was fleeting. 就像蜉蝣一样，他的名声转瞬即逝。|例句：The band was a dayfly, popular for only a summer.  这支乐队就像蜉蝣，只在一个夏天流行。|近义词：ephemeral, transient, short-lived|反义词:permanent, enduring|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dæġflēoge, from *dæġ (day) + *flēoge (fly).  Meaning "day fly" literally.
