half-convincing:/ˌhæf kənˈvɪnsɪŋ/
adjective
半信服的；不太令人信服的
Partially convincing; not entirely believable.
His explanation was half-convincing, but I still had some doubts. 他的解释半信服，但我仍然有些怀疑。
Synonyms: unconvincing, doubtful, dubious, questionable
Antonyms: convincing, persuasive, credible, believable
词性: 形容词


half-convincing:/ˌhæf kənˈvɪnsɪŋ/
词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "convincing" (from Latin convincere, "to conquer, overcome, prove wrong")
