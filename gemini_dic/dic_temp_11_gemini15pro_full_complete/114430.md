make-up-to:/ˈmeɪk ʌp tuː/
verb phrase
弥补；补偿；讨好
to try to be nice to someone because you have done something to upset them or make them angry
例句：He tried to make up to her with flowers after their argument.  他试图在争吵后送花来讨好她。

例句：He's been making up to the boss all week. 他整个星期都在拍老板马屁。
近义词：apologize to, compensate, flatter
反义词：offend, alienate
词性：短语动词
