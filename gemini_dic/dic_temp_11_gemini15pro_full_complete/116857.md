maximizers:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzərz/
n.
最大化者，力求完美者
People who always aim to make the best possible decision.  Those who  need to be assured that every purchase or decision was the best that could be made.
例句：Maximizers tend to feel less satisfied with their choices than satisficers. 最大化者往往比满意者对自己的选择更不满意。

例句：Maximizers often experience analysis paralysis when faced with many options.  当面临许多选择时，最大化者经常会经历分析瘫痪。

近义词：perfectionists, optimizers
反义词：satisficers, minimizers
词性: noun (plural)


maximizer:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzər/
n.
最大化者，力求完美者
A person who strives to make the best possible decision in every situation.
例句：He's a maximizer when it comes to buying electronics; he researches for weeks before making a purchase.  在购买电子产品方面，他是一个最大化者；他在购买前会研究几个星期。

例句：She’s a maximizer and always agonizes over even small decisions. 她是一个力求完美的人，即使是小决定也会苦苦思索。

近义词：perfectionist, optimizer
反义词：satisficer, minimizer
词性: noun (singular)


maximize:/ˈmæksɪmaɪz/
v.
最大化，充分利用
To increase something as much as possible; to make the best use of something.
例句：The company’s goal is to maximize profits.  公司的目标是最大化利润。

例句：She maximized her chances of getting the job by sending a personalized cover letter. 她通过发送个性化的求职信最大限度地提高了获得这份工作的机会。

近义词：increase, boost, amplify, enhance, optimize
反义词：minimize, reduce, decrease
词性: verb
