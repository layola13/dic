jehu:/ˈdʒiːhuː/| n.|耶胡；猛赶马车的人；狂奔的人|A driver of a coach or carriage, especially one who drives recklessly or at great speed.  Derived from Jehu, a king of Israel known for his furious chariot driving (2 Kings 9:20).|He drove like a jehu, barely avoiding the pedestrians. 他像耶胡一样驾车狂奔，险些撞到行人。|近义词：speedster, hot-rodder, daredevil|反义词:slowpoke, cautious driver|词性:noun
