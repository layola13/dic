cartoonish:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪʃ/
adjective
卡通化的；滑稽的
Resembling or characteristic of a cartoon, especially in being exaggerated, simplified, or unrealistic.
The villain's evil laugh was cartoonish.  反派的邪恶笑声很卡通化。

The special effects in the movie were rather cartoonish.  这部电影的特效相当卡通化。


similar: exaggerated, caricatured, farcical, unrealistic, absurd
opposite: realistic, lifelike, natural, authentic, believable
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From cartoon + -ish.
