madia:/ˈmeɪdiə/ | n. | 美洲麻黄 | A genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, native to the Americas.  They are commonly known as madias, or sometimes as tarweeds. | The madia plant produces oil-rich seeds. 美洲麻黄植物生产富含油脂的种子。| The madia flower is small and yellow. 美洲麻黄的花朵小而呈黄色。| synonyms: tarweed | antonyms: N/A | noun
