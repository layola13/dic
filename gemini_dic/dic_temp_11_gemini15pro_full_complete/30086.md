cease:/siːs/
v.
停止，终止
To stop happening or existing; to stop doing something.
The fighting ceased when the truce was called.  战斗在休战协议宣布后停止了。
The company ceased operations last year. 该公司去年停止了运营。

近义词: stop, halt, discontinue, terminate
反义词: start, begin, commence, continue

词性: 动词 (verb)
