PHRASE->
glance at: 瞥一眼  She glanced at her watch. 她瞥了一眼手表。
glance over: 浏览，粗略地看一遍  I'll glance over these documents later. 我稍后会浏览一下这些文件。
glance through: 快速浏览  He glanced through the newspaper. 他快速浏览了一下报纸。
cast a glance:  瞥一眼  She cast a glance at the stranger. 她瞥了一眼那个陌生人。
shoot a glance:  迅速地瞥一眼 He shot a glance at her and then looked away. 他迅速地瞥了她一眼，然后看向别处。
