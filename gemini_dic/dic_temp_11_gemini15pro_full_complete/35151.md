clovered:/ˈkloʊvərd/| adj.|覆盖着三叶草的，幸运的|Covered with clover. Can also imply luck, as clover, particularly four-leaf clover, is considered lucky.|The field was clovered and buzzing with bees. 田野上覆盖着三叶草，蜜蜂嗡嗡作响。|近义词：lush, verdant, lucky|反义词:barren, bare|词性:形容词

词源: From "clover" + "-ed."  "Clover" comes from Old English "clafre."
