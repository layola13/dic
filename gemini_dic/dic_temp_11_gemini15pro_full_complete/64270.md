equiradial:/ˌiːkwɪˈreɪdiəl/
adjective
等径的；辐射对称的
Having radii of equal length.  A circle or sphere is equiradial.  In biology, it refers to a body plan in which the organism can be divided into similar halves by passing a plane at any angle along a central axis, such as in jellyfish.
The starfish exhibits an equiradial body plan.  海星呈现出等径的身体构造。
Synonyms:  isoradial, radial, symmetrical
Antonyms:  asymmetrical, irregular
词性: adjective
