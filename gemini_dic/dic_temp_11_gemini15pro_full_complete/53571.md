disbelieving:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvɪŋ/
adjective
表示怀疑的，不相信的
showing that you do not believe something
He gave me a disbelieving look. 他给了我一个怀疑的眼神。
近义词:skeptical, doubtful, incredulous
反义词:believing, trusting, credulous
词性:adjective
词源:From dis- + believing, present participle of believe.
