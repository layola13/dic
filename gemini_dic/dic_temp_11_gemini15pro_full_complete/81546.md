grayed:/ɡreɪd/| adj.|灰色的，灰白的；（头发）变灰的；暗淡的，阴沉的|Having become gray; dull or drab; (Figuratively) made gloomy or depressing.|Her hair has grayed over the years. 她的头发这些年变白了。|近义词：grizzled, silver, hoary, ashen, faded, dull, drab|反义词：bright, colorful, vibrant|词性:形容词
词源：From gray + -ed.  The past participle of gray, used adjectivally.
