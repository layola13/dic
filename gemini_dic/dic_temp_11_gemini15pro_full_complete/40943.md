coontie:/ˈkuːnti/| n. |泽米苏铁，佛罗里达泽米|A cycad native to Florida and the Caribbean, with a short, thick stem and long, stiff, feather-like leaves.  It produces cones and its starchy root was historically used as a source of food by indigenous peoples.|The coontie is Florida's state plant.  泽米苏铁是佛罗里达的州植物。

The Seminole tribe traditionally made flour from the coontie root.  塞米诺尔部落传统上用泽米苏铁的根制作面粉。|近义词：arrowroot, sago palm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
