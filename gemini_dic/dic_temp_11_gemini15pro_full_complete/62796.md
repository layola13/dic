enguard:/ɛnˈɡɑːrd/| adj.| 警惕的，戒备的 |On guard; watchful; vigilant.  Prepared for attack or danger. | The soldiers stood enguard, ready to defend the castle. 士兵们保持警惕，准备保卫城堡。| 近义词: vigilant, watchful, alert, wary | 反义词: unaware, oblivious, unguarded, careless | 词性:形容词

词源: From en- + guard, from Old French garder "to guard, protect, keep," from Frankish *wardon "to watch," from Proto-Germanic *wardon "to watch," from PIE root *wer- (3) "perceive, watch out for."
