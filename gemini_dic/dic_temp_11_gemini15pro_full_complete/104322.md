jutelike: /ˈdʒuːtlaɪk/
adjective
像黄麻的；黄麻似的
Resembling jute; similar to jute in texture or appearance.
The bag was made of a coarse, jutelike material.  这个包是用一种粗糙的、像黄麻的材料制成的。
The curtains were made of a heavy, jutelike fabric.  窗帘是用一种厚重的、类似黄麻的织物制成的。
近义词:fibrous, coarse, rough, hemplike
反义词:smooth, silky, fine
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From jute + -like.  Jute comes from the Sanskrit word *jūṭa*, the name of the plant.
