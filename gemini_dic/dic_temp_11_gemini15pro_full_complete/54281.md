disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/ | n. vt. |伪装，掩饰；假扮 |A means of altering one's appearance or concealing one's identity.  | She wore a disguise to avoid being recognized. 她乔装打扮以避免被人认出。| synonyms: camouflage, mask, cloak, pretense | antonyms: reveal, expose | noun

disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/ | vt. |伪装，掩饰；假扮 | To change the appearance of something or someone so that they are not easily recognized. | He disguised his voice on the phone. 他在电话里伪装了声音。| synonyms: conceal, mask, camouflage | antonyms: reveal, expose | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "desguisier" meaning "to alter the guise or fashion of," from "des-" (expressing reversal) + "guise" (manner, fashion). Ultimately from Frankish "*wīsōn" (manner, fashion, wise).
