fatiferous:/fəˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
带来疲劳的；使人疲倦的
Causing fatigue or weariness.  Bearing or producing fatigue.
She had a fatiferous journey after a long day at work.  她工作了一整天后，经历了一段令人疲惫的旅程。
The fatiferous heat made it difficult to concentrate.  令人疲惫的酷热使人难以集中注意力。
近义词: tiring, exhausting, wearying, enervating
反义词: invigorating, energizing, refreshing
词性: adjective
词源: Latin *fatifer, from fatigare "to tire," from *fatis, ablative plural of fatum "fate, destiny" + -ferous "bearing, producing."
