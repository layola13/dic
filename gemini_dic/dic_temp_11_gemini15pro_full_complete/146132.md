overemploy:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪ/| v. |过度雇佣；雇佣过多员工|To employ more workers than are needed or can be used efficiently.|The company overemployed staff during the boom years and now has to make redundancies.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度雇佣了员工，现在不得不裁员。|The factory overemployed workers and now faces financial difficulties. 这家工厂过度雇佣了工人，现在面临财务困难。|近义词：overstaff, overman|反义词: understaff, underemploy|词性:动词
