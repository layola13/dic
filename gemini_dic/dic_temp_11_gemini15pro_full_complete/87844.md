hide-out:/ˈhaɪdaʊt/| n. | 藏身处；躲藏处 | A place where someone hides, especially from the police or from other people who are looking for them. | The robbers used an abandoned cabin as a hide-out.  强盗们用一个废弃的小屋作为藏身处。

The police raided the gang's hide-out. 警察突袭了该团伙的藏身处。 |  hideaway, refuge, shelter | exposure | noun
