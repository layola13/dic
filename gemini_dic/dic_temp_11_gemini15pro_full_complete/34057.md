civetlike:/ˈsɪvɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像灵猫的，灵猫似的
Resembling or characteristic of a civet.
The civetlike scent filled the air.  空气中弥漫着类似灵猫的香味。
The small mammal had a civetlike mask across its face.  这只小型哺乳动物的脸上有一个像灵猫一样的面具。
