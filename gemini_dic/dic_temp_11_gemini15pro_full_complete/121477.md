mislabeled:/ˌmɪsˈleɪb(ə)ld/
adjective
贴错标签的；错误标记的
incorrectly labeled
The package was mislabeled and sent to the wrong address.  这个包裹贴错了标签，被送到了错误的地址。
The specimens were mislabeled, leading to inaccurate results. 这些标本被错误标记，导致结果不准确。
Synonyms: wrongly labeled, incorrectly tagged, mismarked
Antonyms: correctly labeled, accurately labeled
词性: adjective
