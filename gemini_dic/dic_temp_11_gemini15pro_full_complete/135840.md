nonproprietaries:/ˌnɒnprəˈpraɪəˌtɛriz/ | n. |非专利药，非专有产品| Medicines or products that are not protected by patents or trademarks and can be manufactured and sold by multiple companies. |The pharmacy offers a range of nonproprietaries at competitive prices. 这家药店以具有竞争力的价格提供一系列非专利药。| Synonyms: generics, off-brand | Antonyms: proprietaries, brand-name | noun

nonproprietary:/ˌnɒnprəˈpraɪəˌtɛri/ | adj. | 非专利的，非专有的 | Not protected by patents or trademarks; available for general use. | The software is nonproprietary and can be modified by anyone.  这款软件是非专有的，任何人都可以修改。| Synonyms: generic, open-source | Antonyms: proprietary, patented | adjective
