crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/| v. |使结晶；使具体化；明确；|To cause to form crystals or assume crystalline form; to make something definite or clear.|The events of that day crystallized her determination to become a doctor. 那一天的事件使她决心成为一名医生。|solidify, concretize, coalesce|melt, dissolve|verb


crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/| n. | 结晶, 具体化 |The act or process of crystallizing.|The crystallization of his ideas into a coherent theory took years.  他的想法形成一个连贯的理论花了很多年。|solidification, concretion|melting, dissolution|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English cristalizen, from Medieval Latin crystallīzāre, from Latin crystallus (“crystal”) (from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos, “ice, crystal”)) + -izare (verbal suffix).
