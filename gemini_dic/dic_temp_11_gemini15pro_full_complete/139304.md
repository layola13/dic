oblongnesses:/ˌɒblɒŋnɪsɪz/ /ˌɑːblɔːŋnɪsɪz/
noun
椭圆形，长方形
the state or quality of being oblong
The oblongness of the table made it difficult to fit in the small room.  桌子的长方形形状使得它难以放进小房间里。
近义词:elongation, lengthiness
反义词:roundness, circularity
词性:n.
