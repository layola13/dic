enamorment:/ɪˈnæmərment/
n.|迷恋，倾心，热恋|The state of being enamored; a feeling of love or fascination.|She spoke of her enamorment with Italy.|近义词: love, infatuation, passion, captivation, enchantment|反义词: indifference, apathy, aversion, dislike, hatred|词性:名词

Etymology:
From enamour + -ment.  Enamour comes from Middle English *enamouren, from Anglo-Norman *enamourer, from en- + amour (“love”). Amour is from Old French amor, ultimately from Latin amor (“love”).
