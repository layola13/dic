commotion:/kəˈmoʊʃən/| n.|骚动，混乱，喧闹|a state of confused and noisy disturbance.|例句：There was a great commotion when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时，一片混乱。|近义词：disturbance, uproar, tumult, chaos, pandemonium|反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commotiōn- (stem of commotiō), from Latin commovēre "to move violently," from com- "together" + movēre "to move".
