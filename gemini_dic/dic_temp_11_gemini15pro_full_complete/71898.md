flexbox: /ˈflɛksbɒks/ | n. | 弹性盒子 | A layout model in CSS that provides a more efficient way to lay out, align and distribute space among items in a container, even when their size is unknown and/or dynamic. |  The website uses flexbox for its responsive design.  该网站使用flexbox进行响应式设计。 | synonyms: flexible box layout, flex layout | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fixed layout" or "table layout" as contrasting concepts) | noun
