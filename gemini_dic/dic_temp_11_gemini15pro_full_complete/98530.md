instigate: /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/| vt.|唆使，煽动，激起|To urge forward; provoke; stir up; foment.|The rebels instigated a riot. 叛乱分子煽动了一场暴乱。|Synonyms: incite, provoke, goad, abet, foment|Antonyms: discourage, deter, prevent, suppress|verb

Etymology->
From Late Latin instigatus, past participle of instigare "to urge, incite, stimulate," from in- "in, on" + stigare "to prick, goad."
