outspeeding:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdɪŋ/
verb
超越；比…更快
moving faster than something or someone else.
The newer model can outspeed the older one.  新型号比旧型号速度更快。
outspeed, outrun, surpass, overtake
lag behind, trail
v.
