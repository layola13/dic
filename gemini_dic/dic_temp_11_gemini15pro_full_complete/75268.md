frondose:/ˈfrɒndoʊs/| adj.|多叶的；枝叶繁茂的|Having many or large fronds or leaves; leafy. |例句：The frondose ferns created a lush, green carpet on the forest floor. 繁茂的蕨类植物在森林地面上形成了郁郁葱葱的绿色地毯。

例句：The tree was frondose, providing ample shade on a hot summer day.  这棵树枝繁叶茂，在炎热的夏日提供了充足的阴凉。|近义词：leafy, luxuriant, lush|反义词：bare, sparse, leafless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frondōsus*, from frōns (“leaf, foliage”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of', 'abounding in').  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰren- (“to sprout, grow”).
