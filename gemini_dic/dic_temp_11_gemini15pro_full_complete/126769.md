nannyishly:/ˈnæniɪʃli/
adverb
保姆式地；过度呵护地
In a manner resembling a nanny, often excessively protective or fussy.
She fussed over him nannyishly, tucking his napkin under his chin. 她像保姆一样对他大惊小怪，把餐巾塞到他的下巴底下。
He spoke to her nannyishly, as if she were a child. 他像对孩子一样对她说话，语气像保姆。
近义词: overprotectively, fussily, maternally (when referring to a woman)
反义词: independently, maturely
词性: adverb

词源:  From nanny + -ish (forming adjectives meaning "like" or "having the character of") + -ly (forming adverbs).
