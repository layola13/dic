moorpan:/ˈmɔːrpæn/| n. |硬盘层，铁盘层|A hardened layer of soil, cemented by iron and aluminum oxides, that is impermeable to water and roots.|The moorpan prevented the trees from growing deep roots.  硬盘层阻碍了树木深根生长。|例句：The farmer struggled to cultivate the land due to the presence of moorpan. 由于硬盘层的存在，农民难以耕种土地。

例句：The moorpan restricted drainage and caused waterlogging. 硬盘层限制了排水，导致了涝灾。|近义词：hardpan, ironpan|反义词:|noun
