distrusts:/dɪsˈtrʌsts/
| v. |不信任，怀疑|To lack trust or confidence in; to regard with suspicion.|She distrusts politicians and their promises. 她不信任政客和他们的承诺。|Synonyms: suspects, doubts, mistrusts|Antonyms: trusts, believes, confides|Verb (Third person singular present)

distrust:/dɪsˈtrʌst/
| n. |不信任，怀疑|Lack of trust or confidence; suspicion.|There is a deep distrust between the two countries. 这两个国家之间存在着深深的不信任。|Synonyms: suspicion, mistrust, doubt|Antonyms: trust, confidence, belief|Noun

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + trust.  The word "trust" ultimately comes from Old Norse "traust" meaning "confidence, protection."
