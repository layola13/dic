counterculturist:/ˈkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. |反主流文化者；反文化者|A person who opposes the dominant values and beliefs of their society and advocates for alternative ways of life.| The counterculturist rejected consumerism and embraced a simpler lifestyle.  反主流文化者拒绝消费主义，拥抱更简单的生活方式。|Synonyms: nonconformist, rebel, dissident, bohemian|Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist| Noun
