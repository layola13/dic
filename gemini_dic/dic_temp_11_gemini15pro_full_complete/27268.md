calquing:/ˈkɔːlkɪŋ/ /ˈkæl.kɪŋ/
n.
仿造; 复制; 描摹; 直译
Creating new words or expressions by literally translating them from another language.
The English word "skyscraper" was calqued from the German "Wolkenkratzer".  英语单词“skyscraper”（摩天大楼）是从德语单词“Wolkenkratzer”仿造而来。
Loan translation, loanword, borrowing
