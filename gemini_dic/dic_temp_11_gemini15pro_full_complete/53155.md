diplostemonous:/dɪpləˈstɛmənəs/| adj.|双雄蕊的，两轮雄蕊的|Having two whorls of stamens, with the outer whorl alternating with the petals and the inner whorl opposite the petals.  A flower is diplostemonous when it has twice as many stamens as petals, arranged in two whorls.|The flower of the mustard plant is diplostemonous.|近义词:bistemonous|反义词:haplostemonous|词性:adjective
