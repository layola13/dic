fosse:/fɒs/ (UK) /fɑːs/ (US)|n.|壕沟，舞池，(芭蕾舞的)体态|A long, narrow trench or ditch dug as a fortification or grave.  A pit or hollow area in a stage floor, used especially in ballet. A characteristic posture or style of a dancer, especially a ballet dancer.|The dancers moved gracefully in the fosse.|近义词：ditch, trench, pit, hollow|反义词: mound, ridge|词性:noun
