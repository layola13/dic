ideological: /ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌaɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
意识形态的
Relating to or concerned with ideology.  Based on or relating to a particular system of ideas and ideals, especially concerning economic or political theory and policy.
Her writings are highly ideological. 她的作品意识形态色彩很浓。
synonyms: doctrinaire, dogmatic, biased, partisan
antonyms: pragmatic, empirical, objective, neutral
词性: 形容词
