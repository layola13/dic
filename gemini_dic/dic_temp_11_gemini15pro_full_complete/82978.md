gunmetal:/ˈɡʌnˌmetl/| n. adj.|炮铜色，青铜色|A dark grey colour, like that of gunmetal.  A type of bronze formerly used for making cannons.|The car was painted a sleek gunmetal grey.|近义词:charcoal, slate, dark grey|反义词:bright, light, colourful|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From gun + metal, referring to the alloy of copper, tin, and zinc traditionally used for cannons.
