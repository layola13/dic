greyed:/ɡreɪd/ | adj. | 灰色；灰白；暗淡 |  Turned gray or grayish; dull or dimmed.  Having become gray.  |  Her hair has greyed with age. 她的头发因年老而变灰白了。 | The old photographs had greyed with time.  老照片随着时间的流逝而褪色了。 | 近义词: gray, grizzled, hoary | 反义词:  brightened, colorful | 词性: 形容词

词源: From gray + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grēwaz.
