misqualified:/ˌmɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪd/
adjective
不合格的，没有资格的
lacking the qualifications or skills required for something; declared ineligible
He was misqualified for the position due to lack of experience. 他因缺乏经验而不符合该职位的资格。
disqualified, ineligible, unfit, incompetent
qualified, eligible, suitable, competent
adjective
