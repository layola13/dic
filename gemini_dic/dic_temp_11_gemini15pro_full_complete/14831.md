axiomatic:/ˌæksiəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 公理的；不言自明的 | Self-evident or unquestionable. |  It is axiomatic that good health is vital to success.  良好的健康对成功至关重要，这是不言而喻的。 | Synonyms: self-evident, obvious, undeniable, unquestionable, taken for granted | Antonyms: questionable, debatable, disputable, controversial | adjective

axiomatically:/ˌæksiəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 公理地；不言而喻地 | In a self-evident or unquestionable manner. | Axiomatically, hard work leads to success.  不言而喻，努力工作会导致成功。| Synonyms: obviously, undeniably, self-evidently | Antonyms: questionably, debatably | adverb
