icu:/ˌaɪ siː ˈjuː/ | abbreviation | 重症监护室(Intensive Care Unit) | An abbreviation for Intensive Care Unit, a specialized hospital ward for patients requiring continuous monitoring and life support. | 例句：He was admitted to the ICU after the accident. 他在事故后被送进了重症监护室。| 例句：The ICU is equipped with advanced medical technology. 重症监护室配备了先进的医疗技术。 | 近义词：critical care unit, intensive therapy unit | 反义词: general ward | noun
