bleakish:/ˈbliːkɪʃ/| adj.|略显荒凉的，略显凄凉的|Somewhat bleak; slightly desolate or dreary.|The landscape was bleakish, with only a few stunted trees.  这片景色略显荒凉，只有几棵低矮的树。|The weather was bleakish and cold. 天气阴沉寒冷。|近义词：dreary, desolate, barren, stark|反义词：cheerful, bright, pleasant, vibrant|词性:形容词
词源: bleak + -ish.  From bleak (meaning exposed, cold, and cheerless) + -ish (a suffix forming adjectives and meaning "somewhat," "rather," or "having the quality of").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *blaikaz.
