interagent:/ˌɪntərˈeɪdʒənt/
adjective
代理人间的，代理人相互的
occurring between or involving interaction between agents.
The interagent communication protocol allowed the robots to coordinate their actions. 代理人间通信协议允许机器人协调它们的行动。
The interagent rivalry led to a decline in overall productivity. 代理人间的竞争导致整体生产力下降。
interagent
adjective
Synonyms:  inter-agent, between agents, among agents
Antonyms:  intra-agent (within a single agent)
