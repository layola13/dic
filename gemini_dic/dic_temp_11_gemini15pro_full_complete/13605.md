attestable:/əˈtɛstəbl/
adj.
可证实的，可作证的
Capable of being attested; verifiable.
The witness provided attestable evidence in court.  证人在法庭上提供了可证实的证据。
近义词: verifiable, confirmable, provable
反义词: unprovable, unverifiable, questionable
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *attestabilis, from attestari "to bear witness," from ad- "to" + testari "to bear witness," from testis "witness."
