comraderie:/ˌkɑːməˈrɑːdəri/ /ˌkɒməˈrɑːdəri/ | n. |同志情谊；友情；伙伴关系|  Mutual trust and friendship among people who spend a lot of time together. |The soldiers developed a close camaraderie during the war. 士兵们在战争期间建立了亲密的同志情谊。| synonyms: fellowship, companionship, fraternity, esprit de corps | antonyms: animosity, hostility, antagonism | noun
