noemics: /noʊˈɛmɪks/ | n. | 诺学，诺埃米克学 | The study of intellectual systems as structured wholes, focusing on their internal coherence and the relationships between their parts.  It emphasizes the understanding of systems of thought as complete entities rather than isolated elements. |  Noemics explores the underlying structure of belief systems. 诺学探索信仰体系的底层结构。|  His research in noemics focuses on the evolution of scientific paradigms. 他的诺学研究重点是科学范式的演变。 | synonyms:  noetics, epistemology | antonyms:  None (It's a specialized field of study, not readily lending itself to direct opposites.) | noun
