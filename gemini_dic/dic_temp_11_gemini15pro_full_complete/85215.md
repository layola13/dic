harkening:/ˈhɑːrkənɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɑːkənɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |倾听，留心；使人联想起|Evoking or suggesting something from the past; listening attentively.|The movie's soundtrack, harkening back to the 80s, was a hit with older audiences. 这部电影的原声音乐让人想起80年代，在老观众中很受欢迎。|近义词：hearkening, listening, recalling, reminiscent|反义词：ignoring, disregarding|词性: 动词(现在分词)

harken:/ˈhɑːrkən/ (US) /ˈhɑːkən/ (UK)| v. |倾听，留心；使人联想起|To listen attentively; to be reminiscent of something in the past.|Hark! I hear the sound of children playing. 听！我听到孩子们玩耍的声音了。|近义词：listen, heed, attend, recall, evoke|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *herknen, from Old English *heorcnian, from Proto-Germanic *harkōną (“to listen”). Cognate with Dutch harken (“to listen”), German horchen (“to listen”), Swedish and Danish harke (“to rake”).  The meaning "be reminiscent of" arose in the 19th century.
