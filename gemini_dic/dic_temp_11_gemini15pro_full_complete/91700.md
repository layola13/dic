hyperconscientiously:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/ | adv. | 极其认真地，过分谨慎地 |  In an excessively conscientious manner; with extreme care and attention to detail, often to an unnecessary or excessive degree.  It implies a level of conscientiousness that goes beyond what is normally considered diligent or thorough. | She hyperconscientiously proofread the document three times before submitting it. 她极其认真地校对了这份文件三次才提交。 | He hyperconscientiously followed every rule, even the ones that seemed unnecessary. 他过分谨慎地遵守每一条规则，即使是那些看起来不必要的规则。 | 近义词: meticulously, scrupulously, painstakingly | 反义词: carelessly, negligently, irresponsibly | 词性: 副词

词源:
"hyper-" (prefix): from Greek "hyper" (over, above, beyond)
"conscientious": from Latin "conscientia" (knowledge within oneself, conscience), from "con-" (with, together) + "scire" (to know)
"-ly" (suffix): forms adverbs
