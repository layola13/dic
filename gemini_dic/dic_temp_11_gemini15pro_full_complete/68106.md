faf:/fɑːf/| adj. | foolish; silly; frivolous | Foolish, silly, or frivolous; lacking seriousness or depth. | She dismissed his concerns with a faf wave of her hand. 她轻蔑地挥了挥手，对他的担忧不屑一顾。 | Example: Stop faffing about and get ready! 别磨磨蹭蹭的，快准备！| synonyms: frivolous, silly, foolish, trifling, superficial | antonyms: serious, sensible, practical | adjective


Etymology->
faf is likely a variant of "foff," which itself is of uncertain origin.  One theory suggests it's imitative of fussy or ineffectual movements. Another connects it to "fop," meaning a dandy or someone excessively concerned with their appearance.
