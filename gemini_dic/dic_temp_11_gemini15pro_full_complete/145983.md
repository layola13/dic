overdoers:/ˌoʊvərˈduːərz/
noun
过度者；做得过分的人
Those who overdo something, especially those who work or exercise too much.
The coach warned the athletes about becoming overdoers and risking injury. 教练警告运动员不要过度训练，以免受伤。
overachievers, fanatics
moderates, underachievers
n.
