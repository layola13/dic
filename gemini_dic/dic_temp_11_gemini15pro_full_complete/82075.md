grizelin: /ɡrɪˈzɛlɪn/ | n. | 灰白色, 灰褐色; 一种灰褐色的马 | A gray or grayish-brown color; a horse of this color. | The grizelin mare galloped across the field. 灰褐色的母马飞奔过田野。| 近义词: gray, greyish-brown, roan | 反义词:  bright, colorful | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *griselin, diminutive of gris "gray".
