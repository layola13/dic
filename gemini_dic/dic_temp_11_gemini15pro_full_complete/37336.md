commencer:/kəˈmɑːns/ (US), /kəˈmɛns/ (UK)| v. |开始，着手|To begin or start something.|例句：The ceremony will commence at noon. 典礼将于中午开始。|例句：They commenced building the new bridge last month.  他们上个月开始建造新桥。|近义词：begin, start, initiate, embark on|反义词：finish, end, conclude, cease|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *comencier*, from Late Latin *cominitiāre* "to begin," from Latin *com-* "together" + *initiāre* "to initiate," from *initium* "beginning."
