hyperlogically:/ˌhaɪpərˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌhaɪpərˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 超逻辑地 |  In a way that is excessively or extremely logical, often to the point of being impractical or unreasonable.  It emphasizes an overly strict adherence to logic, often disregarding practical considerations or common sense. | He approached the problem hyperlogically, ignoring the emotional needs of the people involved. 他超逻辑地处理这个问题，忽略了相关人员的情感需求。 |  He analyzed the situation hyperlogically, missing the subtle social cues. 他超逻辑地分析了情况，错过了微妙的社会线索。 | 近义词: over-logically, excessively logically, rigidly logically | 反义词: illogically, irrationally, emotionally | 词性: 副词
