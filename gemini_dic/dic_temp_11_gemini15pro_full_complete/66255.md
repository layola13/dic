exclusionary:/ɪkˈskluːʒənəri/
adjective
排斥的; 排他的; 限制性的
Tending to exclude or shut out.  Preventing people or groups from taking part in something or entering a place.
例句：The club had exclusionary membership policies.  这个俱乐部有排他的会员政策。

例句：Exclusionary zoning laws can make it difficult for low-income families to find housing. 排斥性的分区法使得低收入家庭很难找到住房。

近义词：restrictive, exclusive, discriminatory, prohibitive
反义词：inclusive, open, welcoming


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exclusionarius, from Latin exclusus (past participle of excludere "to shut out"), from ex- "out" + claudere "to shut".
