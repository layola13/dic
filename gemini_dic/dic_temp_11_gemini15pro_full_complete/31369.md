charbroiled:/ˈtʃɑːrbrɔɪld/| adj.|烤焦的，用炭火烤的|Cooked over or under a charcoal fire, often resulting in a slightly charred surface.|例句：We ordered charbroiled burgers. 我们点了炭烤汉堡。|例句：The charbroiled steak was delicious. 炭烤牛排很美味。|近义词：grilled, barbecued|反义词:boiled, steamed|词性:形容词
