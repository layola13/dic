eremite:/ˈɛrɪmaɪt/| n.|隐士，遁世者|A person who lives in solitude, especially for religious reasons.  A recluse.|例句：The eremite lived in a cave in the mountains.  这位隐士住在山里的一个洞穴里。

例句：He chose the life of an eremite to find spiritual peace. 他选择隐居生活以寻求心灵的平静。|近义词：hermit, recluse, anchorite|反义词：socialite, extrovert|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *eremite*, from Late Latin *eremita*, from Greek *erēmitēs* "desert dweller," from *erēmia* "desert," from *erēmos* "lonely, desolate."
