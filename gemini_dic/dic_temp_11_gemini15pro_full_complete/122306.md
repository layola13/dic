mitten: /ˈmɪtən/ (US), /ˈmɪtn/ (UK)| n. |手套，连指手套|A glove with a single compartment for all four fingers and a separate compartment for the thumb. |例句：I lost one of my mittens in the snow. 我在雪地里丢了一只手套。

例句：She knitted a pair of warm mittens for her grandson. 她为孙子织了一副暖和的手套。

近义词：glove|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 bare hands 赤手)|词性: noun
