dime-stores:/ˈdaɪm stɔːrz/ | n. |廉价商店；十美分商店|A retail store offering a variety of low-priced merchandise, originally priced at ten cents or less.  These stores were popular in the early to mid-20th century.|  My grandmother used to love browsing the dime-stores for trinkets. 我的祖母过去喜欢在廉价商店里寻找小饰品。| synonyms: five-and-dime, variety store, bargain store | antonyms: department store, boutique, luxury store | noun

dime-store:/ˈdaɪm stɔːr/ | adj. |廉价的；低劣的|Something that is cheap, mass-produced, and of low quality.|He criticized the movie's dime-store plot. 他批评这部电影的廉价剧情。| synonyms: cheap, tacky, shoddy | antonyms: high-quality, expensive, premium | adjective
