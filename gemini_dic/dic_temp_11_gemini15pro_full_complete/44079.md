crinoline: /ˈkrɪnəlɪn/ | n. | 衬裙；裙撑 | A stiffened petticoat or hooped underskirt worn to make a skirt stand out. |  She wore a crinoline under her wedding dress to give it a full, bell-shaped silhouette. 她穿着衬裙在婚纱里，让它呈现出饱满的钟形轮廓。

crinoline: /ˈkrɪnəlɪn/ | n. | 粗硬的马毛织物；用这种织物做的东西 | A stiff fabric made of horsehair and linen or cotton, used for petticoats, interlinings, etc. | The chair seats were made of a durable crinoline. 椅子坐垫是用耐用的马毛织物做的。


近义词: petticoat, underskirt, hoop skirt
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
