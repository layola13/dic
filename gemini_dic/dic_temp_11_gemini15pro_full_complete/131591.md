noncompetition:/ˌnɑːnkɑːmpəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 非竞争；不竞争；禁止竞争 | Absence of competition; an agreement not to compete. |  Example 1: The noncompetition agreement prevented her from working for a rival company. 她签署的竞业禁止协议使她无法为竞争对手公司工作。

Example 2:  The government is investigating the company for potential noncompetition practices. 政府正在调查该公司是否存在潜在的非竞争行为。 | Near Synonyms: non-compete,  exclusivity, monopoly | Antonyms: competition, rivalry, contest | noun


non-compete:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpiːt/ | n. | 竞业禁止协议 | A clause in an employment contract that prevents an employee from working for a competitor for a specific period after leaving the company.  | Example 1: He signed a non-compete as part of his employment contract. 他作为雇佣合同的一部分签署了竞业禁止协议。

Example 2: The non-compete is valid for two years after termination of employment.  竞业禁止协议在终止雇佣关系后两年内有效。 | Near Synonyms: noncompetition agreement, restrictive covenant | Antonyms: open competition | noun
