opinionatist: /əˈpɪnjənɪst/ | n. |评论员；持特定意见者；专栏作家| A person who expresses opinions, especially in a dogmatic or insistent way. | He's known as an opinionatist in the political sphere, never hesitant to share his views. 他在政界被称为评论员，从不犹豫分享他的观点。| The newspaper hired a new opinionatist to write editorials. 这家报纸聘请了一位新的专栏作家撰写社论。| synonyms: pundit, commentator, columnist, analyst | antonyms: listener, observer | noun
