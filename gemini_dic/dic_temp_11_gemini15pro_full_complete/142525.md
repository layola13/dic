ossify:/ˈɒsɪfaɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːsɪfaɪ/ (US)| v. |骨化；硬化；僵化|To change into bone; to become hardened or set in one's ways.|例句：The cartilage will eventually ossify into bone. 软骨最终会骨化成骨。

例句：His political views have ossified over the years. 他的政治观点多年来已经僵化。|近义词：harden, solidify, rigidify, fossilize|反义词:soften,liquefy|词性:动词


ossific:/ɒˈsɪfɪk/ (UK) /ɑːˈsɪfɪk/ (US)| adj. |骨化的；硬化的；僵化的|Causing or related to the formation of bone; tending to become hardened or inflexible.|例句：Ossific deposits were found in the joint. 在关节处发现了骨化沉积物。

例句：The ossific process begins in the womb. 骨化过程始于子宫内。|近义词：hardening, solidifying, rigidifying|反义词:softening, flexible|词性:形容词
