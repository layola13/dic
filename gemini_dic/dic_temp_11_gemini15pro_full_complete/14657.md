avowedly:/əˈvaʊɪdli/
adverb
公开地；公然地
openly and frankly acknowledged
He avowedly supported the new policy. 他公开支持这项新政策。
近义词: openly, frankly, explicitly, admittedly
反义词: secretly, covertly, implicitly
词性: adverb

词源: From avow + -ed + -ly.  Avow comes from Old French *avouer, from Latin advocare "to summon," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call."
