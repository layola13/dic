limpest:/ˈlɪmpɪst/| adj.|最无力的，最软弱的，最没精神的|lacking firmness or stiffness; flabby.  Superlative form of "limp".  It describes something extremely lacking in strength, energy, or stiffness.|The flag hung limpest on days without a breeze.  旗帜在无风的日子里垂得最无力。| The runner, exhausted, felt limpest after crossing the finish line.  跑者精疲力竭，越过终点线后感到浑身无力。|weak, feeble, flaccid|firm, strong, stiff|adjective
