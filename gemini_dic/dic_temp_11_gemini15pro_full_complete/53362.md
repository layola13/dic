disaffectionate:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛkʃənət/ | adj. | 不友好的，冷淡的，不亲热的 | Not displaying affection; cold or indifferent. |  She gave her father a disaffectionate glance. 她冷冷地瞥了她父亲一眼。 | He spoke in a disaffectionate tone. 他用冷淡的语气说话。 | 近义词: aloof, cold, distant, indifferent, unloving | 反义词: affectionate, loving, warm, caring | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from dis- (expressing reversal) + affectionate + -ate.
