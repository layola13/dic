began:/bɪˈɡæn/ (US), /bɪˈɡæn/ (UK)| v.|开始|The simple past tense of "begin". It indicates an action that started and finished in the past.|例句：The concert began at 8 pm.  音乐会晚上8点开始。

例句：She began to cry when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后开始哭泣。

近义词：started, commenced, initiated|反义词:ended, finished, ceased|词性:动词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I begin my day with a cup of coffee. 我每天以一杯咖啡开始新的一天。
He begins work at 9 am. 他早上9点开始工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am beginning to understand this concept. 我开始理解这个概念了。
She is beginning to feel better. 她开始感觉好些了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have begun a new exercise program. 我开始了一个新的锻炼计划。
He has begun writing a novel. 他开始写小说了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been beginning to suspect something. 我开始怀疑一些事情了。 (不太常用)
He has been beginning to learn Spanish. 他开始学习西班牙语了。(不太常用，一般用现在进行时)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The movie began at 7 pm. 电影晚上7点开始。
The rain began to fall. 雨开始下了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was beginning to get worried when he finally arrived. 他终于到了，我刚开始担心起来。
She was beginning to cook dinner when the phone rang. 电话铃响时她正开始做饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The play had begun before we arrived. 我们到达之前戏已经开始了。
The storm had begun before we left the house. 我们离开家之前暴风雨就开始了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been beginning to think he wouldn't come. 我开始以为他不会来了。 (不太常用)
She had been beginning to feel sleepy when the alarm went off. 闹钟响的时候，她刚开始感到困倦。(不太常用)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The class will begin next Monday. 课程将于下周一开课。
The meeting will begin at 2 pm. 会议将于下午2点开始。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be beginning my new job next week. 我下周将开始我的新工作。
She will be beginning her studies in the fall. 她将在秋季开始学习。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have begun my PhD program. 到明年，我将已经开始我的博士课程。
By June, she will have begun writing her dissertation. 到六月，她将已经开始写她的论文。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been beginning to work on this project for a year. 到下个月，我将已经开始做这个项目一年了。(不太常用)
By the time she graduates, she will have been beginning to learn this skill for five years. 到她毕业的时候，她将已经开始学习这项技能五年了。(不太常用)



PHRASE->
No particular phrases commonly use "began" directly.  Instead, phrases usually use "begin".

what begins as ... = 以...开始
begin with = 从...开始
begin to = 开始做...
begin again = 重新开始
begin anew = 重新开始


注意事项:
“began”是“begin”的过去式，没有进行时态。尽管语法上可以构成进行时态，但实际使用中非常罕见，通常可以用其他更自然的表达方式替代。
