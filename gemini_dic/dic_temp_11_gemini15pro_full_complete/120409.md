ministrative: /mɪˈnɪstrətɪv/
adjective|行政的，管理的|Relating to the administration or management of an organization or activity.|例句：She has excellent ministrative skills. 她有优秀的管理才能。|近义词：administrative, managerial, executive|反义词：operational, non-managerial|词性: adjective

词源:  Derived from the word "minister," ultimately from Latin *minister* "servant, agent."  The "-ative" suffix forms adjectives meaning "inclined to; tending to; serving to." The form "ministrative" is less common than "administrative" but shares the same root and essentially the same meaning.
