centigram: /ˈsɛntɪɡræm/ | n. | 厘克 | A unit of mass equal to one hundredth of a gram. |  A centigram is a small unit of measurement. 厘克是一个很小的计量单位。

The chemist carefully measured out a centigram of the powder. 化学家仔细称量出百分之一克的粉末。


|近义词: hundredth of a gram |反义词: kilogram |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French centigramme, from centi- (hundredth) + gramme (gram). Ultimately from Latin centum (hundred) and Greek gramma (small weight).
