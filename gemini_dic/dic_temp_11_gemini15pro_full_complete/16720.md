barrowist:/ˈbæroʊɪst/| n.| 手推车夫， Barrowman |A person who pushes or pulls a barrow (a small cart or wheeled frame).  This word is now largely archaic and rarely used.|He worked as a barrowist at the market, selling fresh produce.|近义词： costermonger, hawker, vendor, peddler |反义词: buyer, customer |词性: noun

Etymology:
From "barrow" (a small cart) + "-ist" (a suffix denoting a person who performs an action or works with something). The word "barrow" itself comes from Old English "bearwe," meaning "carrier."
