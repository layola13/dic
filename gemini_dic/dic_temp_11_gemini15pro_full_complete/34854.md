clinkery:/ˈklɪŋkəri/| adj.|似熔渣的；含熔渣的；烧结的|Resembling or containing clinker; burnt and fused together.|The clinkery residue from the fire was difficult to remove. 火烧后留下的熔渣状残留物很难清除。|Synonyms: slaggy, vitrified, fused|Antonyms: smooth, soft, powdery|adjective

clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/| n.|熔渣；矿渣|Hard, stony residue formed during the burning of coal or other substances.|The blacksmith swept the clinker from the forge. 铁匠把熔炉里的熔渣扫了出去。|Synonyms: slag, cinder, dross|Antonyms: ore, fuel|noun
