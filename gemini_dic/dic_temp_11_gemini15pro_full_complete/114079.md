magnet:/ˈmæɡnət/| n. |磁铁，磁石|A piece of iron or other material that has its component atoms so ordered that the material exhibits properties of magnetism, such as attracting other iron-containing objects or aligning itself in an external magnetic field.|The refrigerator magnets hold up the children's drawings. 冰箱上的磁铁吸附着孩子们的画。| 近义词: lodestone | 反义词: non-magnet | 词性: noun
