favourite (US: favorite): /ˈfeɪvərɪt/ (UK) /ˈfeɪvərɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|最喜欢的；最喜爱的人或物|Something or someone that is liked above all others.|My favourite colour is blue. 我最喜欢的颜色是蓝色.|She's my favourite teacher. 她是我最喜欢的老师.|Synonyms: preferred, dearest, beloved|Antonyms: disliked, hated|Adjective/Noun

favourite (US: favorite): /ˈfeɪvərɪt/ (UK) /ˈfeɪvərɪt/ (US)| n.|最喜爱的人或物|A person or thing that is preferred above all others.|Chocolate is my favourite. 巧克力是我的最爱.|He's a favourite with the fans. 他是粉丝们的最爱.|Synonyms: pet, darling|Antonyms:  least favorite|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English favorit, from Old French favorit, past participle of favorir (“to favor”), from Latin favor (“favor, goodwill”).
