deadbeats:/ˈdɛdˌbiːts/| n.|赖账的人；游手好闲的人；|A person who deliberately avoids paying debts or fulfilling responsibilities; a loafer.|He's a real deadbeat who never pays his rent on time. 他是个真正的赖账鬼，从不按时付房租。|Synonyms: loafer, sponger, freeloader, slacker|Antonyms: hard worker, responsible person|Noun
deadbeat:/ˈdɛdˌbiːts/| adj.|赖账的；游手好闲的；|Characterized by avoiding debts or responsibilities; lazy and unproductive.|She called her ex-husband a deadbeat dad. 她称她的前夫是个不负责任的父亲。|Synonyms: irresponsible, lazy, negligent|Antonyms: responsible, diligent, hardworking|Adjective
