gluttons:/ˈɡlʌtənz/
n.
贪吃的人；暴食者；贪婪的人
People who eat excessively or are greedy are called gluttons.
The buffet was raided by a horde of gluttons.  自助餐被一群贪吃的人洗劫一空。
He's a glutton for punishment. 他是个自讨苦吃的人。(比喻义，指喜欢接受挑战或艰苦任务)
近义词：gormandizer, gourmand, overeater, pig
反义词：ascetic, moderate eater
词性：名词(plural)
