homespun:/ˈhoʊmˌspʌn/ | adj. n. | 家织的；朴素的；简朴的 | Made at home, typically of cloth or yarn; simple and unsophisticated. |  She wore a homespun dress. 她穿着一件家织的连衣裙。| synonyms: homemade, rustic, simple, plain, unpretentious | antonyms: sophisticated, elaborate, refined, elegant | adjective

homespun:/ˈhoʊmˌspʌn/ | n. |  家织布；朴素的言辞 | Cloth made at home; simple and unsophisticated language or ideas. | The politician's homespun philosophy appealed to many voters.  这位政治家的质朴哲学吸引了许多选民。| synonyms: homespun cloth, homespun fabric| antonyms: sophisticated fabric, luxury fabric| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From home + spun, past participle of spin.  Meaning "made at home" is from the late 16th century, and the figurative sense of "simple, unsophisticated" is from the mid 18th century.
