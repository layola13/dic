bleak:/blik/ | adj. | 凄凉的，荒凉的；阴冷的；无望的，暗淡的 | lacking vegetation and exposed to the elements; cold and miserable; depressing; discouraging | The future looked bleak for the company. 这家公司的前途看起来很 bleak。| synonyms: desolate, barren, grim, dreary, depressing | antonyms: cheerful, bright, promising, hopeful | adjective

bleaks:/bliks/ | n. | 荒凉的地方；凄凉的景象（bleak 的复数形式）| desolate or exposed places; dreary scenes (plural of bleak) | The bleaks of the moors stretched before them.  荒凉的沼泽地展现在他们面前。 | synonyms: wastelands, barrens, wildernesses | antonyms: paradises, oases, havens | noun
