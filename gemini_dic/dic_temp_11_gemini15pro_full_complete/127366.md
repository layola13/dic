ndugu:/nˈduːɡuː/| n.|兄弟; 同志; 朋友 (主要用于东非)|A term of address for a male friend or comrade, primarily used in East Africa, often implying a close bond of brotherhood or solidarity.  It derives from Swahili.|Example 1:  He greeted his ndugu warmly. 他热情地问候了他的朋友。

Example 2:  The ndugu worked together to build the house.  朋友们一起建造房子。|近义词：brother, friend, comrade, pal, mate|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
