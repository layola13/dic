multicar:/ˈmʌltiˌkɑːr/ (US), /ˈmʌltiˌkɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.|多节车厢，多用途车|A vehicle consisting of multiple connected units, often used for transporting goods or passengers.  It can refer to articulated lorries, road trains, or specialized railway cars.|The multicar was loaded with coal and headed for the power plant. 这辆多节车厢装满了煤，正开往发电厂。|Near synonyms: road train, articulated lorry, modular vehicle|Antonym: single-unit vehicle|noun

multicar:/ˈmʌltiˌkɑːr/ (US), /ˈmʌltiˌkɑː(r)/ (UK)| adj.|多节车厢的，多用途的|Having or relating to multiple connected units, as in a vehicle.|The multicar design allowed for greater flexibility in transporting different types of cargo. 多节车厢的设计使得运输不同类型的货物更加灵活。|Near synonyms: modular, articulated|Antonym: single-unit|adjective
