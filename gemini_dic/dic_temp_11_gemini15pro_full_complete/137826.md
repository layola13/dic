nonvexatious:/ˌnɒnvɛkˈseɪʃəs/
|adj.|
无扰乱性的；不无理取闹的；不带来麻烦的|
Not causing annoyance or trouble; not frivolous or vexatious.|
例句：The lawyer assured his client that the lawsuit was nonvexatious and filed in good faith.  律师向他的客户保证，这起诉讼并非无理取闹，而是出于善意提出的。

例句：Her nonvexatious approach to the dispute helped to calm the situation. 她以不无理取闹的方式处理这场纠纷，有助于平息局势。
|近义词：reasonable, justifiable, legitimate, valid, unobjectionable|
|反义词：vexatious, frivolous, troublesome, annoying, harassing|
|词性: 形容词|
