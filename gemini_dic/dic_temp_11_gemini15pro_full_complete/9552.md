antisexism:/ˌæntiˈsɛksɪzəm/
n.
反对性别歧视；反性别歧视主义
Opposition to sexism; the belief that sexism is wrong and should be opposed.
Antisexism is a crucial part of achieving gender equality. 反性别歧视是实现性别平等的关键部分。
Many organizations are actively promoting antisexism in the workplace. 许多组织正在积极推动工作场所的反性别歧视。
近义词：feminism, gender equality, egalitarianism
反义词：sexism, misogyny, discrimination
