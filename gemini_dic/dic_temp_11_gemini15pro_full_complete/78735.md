giggly: /ˈɡɪɡli/ | adj. | 咯咯笑的，爱傻笑的 | Inclined to giggle; characterized by giggling. |  She was giggly and excited about the party. 她对派对感到兴奋，咯咯地笑。| 近义词： lighthearted, bubbly, merry, cheerful | 反义词： serious, somber, glum | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From giggle + -y. Giggle itself likely originates from a Scandinavian source, akin to Old Norse *gikkla.
