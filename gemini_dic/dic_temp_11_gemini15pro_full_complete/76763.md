gamenesses:/ˈɡeɪmnɪsɪz/
n.
勇敢；坚毅；斗志；体育精神
The quality of being brave and determined in a difficult situation, especially in sport.
He showed great gameness in continuing to play despite his injury.
他尽管受伤了，仍然坚持比赛，表现出了极大的毅力。
bravery, courage, pluck, determination, resilience, spirit, tenacity, fortitude
cowardice, timidity
