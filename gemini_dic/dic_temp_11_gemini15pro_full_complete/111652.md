一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The locative case identifies the location of the action. 位置格标识动作发生的地点。
He works at the library. 他在图书馆工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is studying in the library. 她在图书馆学习。
They are playing at the park. 他们在公园玩耍。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has lived in London for five years. 他在伦敦住了五年了。
She has worked at the museum since 2010. 她从2010年开始就在博物馆工作。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been living in Paris for a year. 他们已经在巴黎住了一年了。
She has been working at the hospital for six months. 她在医院工作了六个月了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He stayed at the hotel. 他住在酒店里。
She worked in the office. 她在办公室工作。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was working in the garden when I called. 我打电话的时候他正在花园里干活。
She was studying at the library when it closed. 图书馆关门的时候她正在学习。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had lived in Paris before moving to London. 在搬到伦敦之前，他曾在巴黎住过。
She had worked at the museum for ten years before retiring.  她在退休前在博物馆工作了十年。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been living in Japan for five years before returning home.  在回国之前，他们已经在日本住了五年了。
She had been working at the library for a year before changing jobs.  在换工作之前，她已经在图书馆工作了一年了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will work at the bank. 他将要去银行工作。
She will study in Rome. 她将要去罗马学习。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I were in Paris, I would visit the Louvre. 如果我在巴黎，我会去参观卢浮宫。
If she had studied at Oxford, she would have a different accent. 如果她在牛津大学学习过，她的口音就会不一样。

被动语态:
The meeting was held in the conference room. 会议在会议室举行。
The book was placed on the table. 书被放在桌子上。

疑问句:
Where do you live? 你住在哪里？
Where are you going? 你要去哪里？

否定句:
I don't live in London. 我不住在伦敦。
She isn't working at the library today. 她今天不在图书馆工作。
