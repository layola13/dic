nitwittery:/ˈnɪtwɪtəri/
noun
愚蠢的行为；傻事；无聊琐事
Silly or frivolous behavior or activities; trifling matters.
She wastes her time on nitwittery instead of focusing on important tasks. 她把时间浪费在无聊琐事上，而不是专注于重要任务。
近义词: foolishness, silliness, triviality, nonsense
反义词: seriousness, sense, importance
词性: noun

nitwittery:/ˈnɪtwɪtəri/
noun
中文翻译：愚蠢的行为；傻事；无聊琐事
英文解析：Silly or frivolous behavior or activities; trifling matters.
例句：He dismissed their concerns as mere nitwittery. 他把他们的担忧斥为纯粹的无聊琐事。
例句：The meeting was bogged down in nitwittery and achieved nothing of substance. 会议陷入了琐碎的讨论，没有取得任何实质性成果。
近义词: foolishness, silliness, triviality, nonsense, tomfoolery
反义词: seriousness, sense, importance, wisdom
词性: noun

词源:  From nit + wit + -ery.  "Nit" here refers to a louse egg, suggesting something small and insignificant.  "Wit" refers to intelligence, so "nitwit" is a foolish person.  "-ery" forms nouns denoting a state, condition, or behavior.
