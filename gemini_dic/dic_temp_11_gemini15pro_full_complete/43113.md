coxing: /ˈkɒksɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːksɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 掌舵；舵手；指挥（尤指赛艇）|The act of steering and directing a rowboat, especially in a race.  It also refers to the person who performs this role.|例句：The coxing was crucial to their victory in the race. 掌舵对他们赢得比赛至关重要。

例句：He took up coxing in college and has been doing it ever since. 他在大学里开始学习掌舵，从那以后就一直在做这件事。|近义词：steering, helmsmanship|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑rowing划船，如果强调舵手的缺失可以用crew without a coxswain 无舵手的船员)|词性: noun
