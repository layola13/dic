childrens:/ˈtʃɪldrənz/ | n. |孩子们的 (non-standard) | This word is a non-standard plural form of "children." "Children" is already the plural form of "child," so adding an "s" is grammatically incorrect.  | Example: Incorrect: The childrens' toys were scattered everywhere. Correct: The children's toys were scattered everywhere.| 近义词: kids' (informal) | 反义词: adults' | 词性: Noun (plural, non-standard)
