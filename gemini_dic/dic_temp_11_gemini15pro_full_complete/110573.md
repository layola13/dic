limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 限制；局限性；缺点；缺陷 | a restriction on the size or amount of something allowed or possible. |  The main limitation of this study is its small sample size. 这项研究的主要局限性是样本量小。|Synonyms: restriction, constraint, curb, restraint | Antonyms: freedom, liberty,  boundlessness| noun

limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 不足之处；弱点 | a weakness or failing. | He knows his limitations as a writer. 他知道自己作为一名作家的不足之处。 | Synonyms: shortcoming, weakness, failing, flaw, defect | Antonyms: strength, advantage,  virtue| noun
