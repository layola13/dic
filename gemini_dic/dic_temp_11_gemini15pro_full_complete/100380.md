interstriations:/ˌɪntərstraɪˈeɪʃənz/
| n. |肌间线，肌间纹|Fine lines or striations between larger ones, especially in muscle tissue.  These are typically seen under a microscope and help identify different types of muscle tissue.|The interstriations of cardiac muscle are less distinct than those of skeletal muscle.|近义词:striations, lines, markings|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly smooth)|noun
