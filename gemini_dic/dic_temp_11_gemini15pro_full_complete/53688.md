disciplined:/ˈdɪsɪplɪnd/| adj.|训练有素的，遵守纪律的，自律的|Showing a controlled form of behaviour or way of working.  Trained to obey rules and orders.|She is a disciplined worker who always meets deadlines. 她是一位自律的员工，总是能按时完成任务。|Synonyms: orderly, controlled, rigorous, strict, regimented|Antonyms: unruly, disorderly, chaotic, undisciplined|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French discipliner, from Latin disciplina ‘instruction, knowledge’, from discere ‘learn’.
