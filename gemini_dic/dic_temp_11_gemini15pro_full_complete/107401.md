laminated:/ˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|层压的，叠层的|covered with a thin layer of plastic or other material for protection or decoration; consisting of layers of material bonded together|例句：I have a laminated map of the city. 我有一张覆膜的城市地图。

例句：The laminated countertop is easy to clean. 层压的厨房台面很容易清洁。|近义词：coated, layered, overlaid|反义词:uncoated, single-layer|词性:形容词

laminate:/ˈlæmɪneɪt/| v. |层压，叠层|to cover or bond with a thin layer of plastic or other material; to construct from layers of material bonded together|例句：We need to laminate these documents to protect them. 我们需要把这些文件过塑以保护它们。

例句：The wood was laminated to create a stronger board. 这块木头被层压以制造更坚固的板材。|近义词：coat, overlay, veneer|反义词:separate, peel|词性:动词

laminate:/ˈlæmɪnət/| n. |层压材料，层压板|a material made by bonding together thin layers of material, typically plastic or wood|例句：The laminate flooring looks like real wood. 这层压地板看起来像真木头。

例句：This laminate is resistant to scratches and stains. 这种层压材料耐刮擦和污渍。|近义词：veneer, plywood, composite|反义词:solid wood, single-layer material|词性:名词


词源: From Latin *laminatus, past participle of *laminare "to make into thin plates," from lamina "thin plate, leaf, layer."
