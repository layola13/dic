oghamists:/ˈoʊɡəmɪsts/| n.|欧甘文书写者，欧甘文研究者|A person skilled in or studying Ogham, an early medieval alphabet used primarily to write the early Irish language.|The oghamists inscribed their messages on standing stones.|近义词：scholars of Ogham,  Ogham scribes|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "illiterate" in a broad sense, but this doesn't capture the specific skillset.)|Noun
