词源:
From Middle English *dashen, from Old French dachier, from Frankish *daskōn (“to strike, beat”), from Proto-Germanic *daskōną (“to strike, beat”). Cognate with Old High German tascōn (“to strike, beat”), Middle High German daschen (“to strike, beat”). Related to task. The meaning "to rush" arose in the 16th century.
