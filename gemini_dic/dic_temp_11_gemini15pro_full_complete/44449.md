cross-springer:/ˈkrɒsˌsprɪŋər/ | n. | 交叉跳跃者；交叉拱 | A rib that springs from a diagonal pier or responds to another rib crossing the vault diagonally.  In Gothic architecture, a rib that crosses the vault diagonally between the main transverse and diagonal ribs. | The cross-springers added complexity and beauty to the cathedral ceiling.  教堂天花板上的交叉拱增加了建筑的复杂性和美感。 | The intricate network of ribs and cross-springers supported the high vaulting. 错综复杂的肋骨和交叉拱网络支撑着高耸的拱顶。 | synonyms: cross-rib, tierceron | antonyms: N/A | noun
