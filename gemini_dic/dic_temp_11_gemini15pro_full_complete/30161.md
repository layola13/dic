celebraters: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪtərz/
n.
庆祝者，庆祝的人们
People who celebrate something.
例句：The celebraters gathered in the town square. 庆祝者聚集在城镇广场上。

例句：The celebraters were in high spirits after the victory.  胜利后庆祝的人们兴高采烈。

近义词：revelers, partygoers, celebrants
反义词：mourners
词性：名词

词源：From the verb "celebrate" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs an action).  "Celebrate" comes from Latin "celebrare" meaning "to frequent, honor, praise."
