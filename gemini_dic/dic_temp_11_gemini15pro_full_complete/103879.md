jubilee:/ˈdʒuːbɪliː/
n.
禧年，周年纪念，盛大的庆祝活动
a period of celebration or rejoicing, especially one marking a special anniversary
The Queen's Golden Jubilee celebrations were spectacular. 女王的金禧庆典非常壮观。
近义词：anniversary, celebration, festival
反义词：mourning, grief
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French jubile, from Late Latin jubilaeus, from Greek iōbēlaios, from Hebrew yōbēl, ram's horn (used as a trumpet), fiftieth year.
