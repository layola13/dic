offendedness:/əˈfɛndɪdnəs/
n.
冒犯；被得罪；恼怒
the state of being offended; resentment
Her offendedness at his remark was obvious.  她对他的话明显感到被冒犯了。

近义词:resentment, indignation, umbrage, pique
反义词:pleasure, delight, satisfaction
词性:noun
