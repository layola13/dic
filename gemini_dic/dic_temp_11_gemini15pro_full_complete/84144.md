half-normally:/ˌhæf ˈnɔːrməli/ | adv. | 半正常地；一半正常地 |  Partially or to some extent normally; in a way that is only partly typical or usual. | The factory is operating half-normally due to the ongoing strike. 工厂由于持续的罢工，目前只有一半产能正常运转。 | The system is working half-normally after the power outage.  断电后，系统半正常运行。 |  partially normally, semi-normally | abnormally, fully normally | adverb
