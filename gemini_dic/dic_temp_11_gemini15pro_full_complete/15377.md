backup:/ˈbækʌp/| n. v. adj.|备份，支持，后援，阻塞|a copy of computer data made in case the original is lost or damaged; support or help, if needed; jammed or blocked (of traffic)|例句：Remember to make a backup of your important files. 记得备份你的重要文件。|近义词：reserve, copy, support, substitute|反义词:original, primary|noun

backup:/ˈbækʌp/| v.|备份，支持，倒车|to make a copy of computer data; to support or help someone; to reverse a vehicle|例句：I backed up my files to an external hard drive. 我把文件备份到外部硬盘。|近义词：duplicate, second, reinforce, reverse|反义词:restore|verb

backup:/ˈbækʌp/| adj.|备用的，候补的|describing something that is kept in reserve; substitute|例句：We have a backup generator in case of power failure. 我们有一个备用发电机，以防断电。|近义词：reserve, spare, secondary, auxiliary|反义词:primary, main|adjective
