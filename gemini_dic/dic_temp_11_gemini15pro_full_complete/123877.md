moravianism:/ˌmɔːrəˈveɪniɪzəm/ | n. | 摩拉维亚教派；摩拉维亚主义 | A Protestant denomination originating in Bohemia and Moravia in the 15th century, emphasizing personal piety and missionary work. | Moravianism played a significant role in the early Protestant Reformation. 摩拉维亚教派在早期的新教改革中扮演了重要的角色。

The Moravianism community is known for its beautiful music and strong sense of fellowship. 摩拉维亚教派社区以其优美的音乐和强烈的友谊感而闻名。 | 近义词: Moravian Church,  Unitas Fratrum | 反义词: None (It's a specific religious denomination, not a concept with a direct opposite.) | 词性: noun
