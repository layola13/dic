onsen:/ˈɒnsɛn/ (UK), /ˈɑːnsɛn/ (US)| n.|温泉 (hot spring)|A Japanese hot spring; a bathing facility or resort offering hot-spring baths.|例句：We went to an onsen in Hakone. 我们去了箱根的一家温泉。

例句：The onsen was very relaxing.  这个温泉非常放松。

近义词：hot spring, spa, bathhouse|反义词:|词性:noun
