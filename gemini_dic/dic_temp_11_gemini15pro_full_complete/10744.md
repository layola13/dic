aptness:/ˈæptnɪs/| n.|恰当，贴切，适宜|The aptness of her remark surprised everyone. 她的评论的恰当令每个人都感到惊讶。|Appropriateness, suitability, fitness, relevance|Inappropriateness, unsuitability, irrelevance|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aptenesse, from Old English *æptnes, from *æpt (“apt”) + *-nes (“-ness”).  Ultimately from Latin *aptus (“fit, suitable”).
