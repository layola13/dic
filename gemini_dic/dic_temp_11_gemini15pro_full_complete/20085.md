bioscientific:/ˌbaɪoʊsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
生物科学的
Relating to the application of biological principles and techniques to scientific research, especially in medicine, agriculture, and industry.
Bioscientific research has led to breakthroughs in disease treatment. 生物科学研究已在疾病治疗方面取得突破。
The bioscientific community is working on developing new vaccines. 生物科学界正在致力于开发新的疫苗。
近义词:biological, life science
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

词源:
bio- (life) + scientific
"bio-" comes from Greek "bios" meaning "life."
"-scientific" comes from "science," ultimately from Latin "scientia" meaning "knowledge."
