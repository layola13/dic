materialised:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/ /məˈtɪriəlaɪzd/ | v. | 实现，出现，实体化 |To become real or actual; to appear or become visible. |  The ghost materialized in front of our eyes. 幽灵出现在我们眼前。

The promised funding never materialized. 承诺的资金从未兑现。

| 近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, materialize | 反义词：disappear, vanish, dematerialize |词性：动词

materialised:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/ /məˈtɪriəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 实体化的，实现了的 | Having become real or actual. |  His materialized fears kept him awake at night. 他实体化的恐惧让他夜不能寐。(不太常用, 更多用动词的过去分词形式做形容词)| 近义词：real, actual, tangible, concrete | 反义词：imaginary, intangible, unreal |词性：形容词
