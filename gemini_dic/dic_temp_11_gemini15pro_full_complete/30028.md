cayuga:/keɪˈjuːɡə/ /kəˈjuːɡə/
noun
卡尤加；卡尤加湖；卡尤加族人；卡尤加鸭
A member of an Iroquoian people formerly inhabiting the region around Cayuga Lake in central New York. Also: the Iroquoian language of this people. / A lake in central New York. / A variety of black duck of the eastern US, having a greenish-black head.
例句：The Cayuga were one of the six nations of the Iroquois Confederacy.  卡尤加人是易洛魁联盟的六个民族之一。

例句：Cayuga Lake is known for its beauty and diverse wildlife. 卡尤加湖以其美丽和多样的野生动物而闻名。

近义词：Iroquoian,  (for the duck) black duck
反义词：None
词性：n.

adjective
卡尤加的；卡尤加湖的；卡尤加族的
