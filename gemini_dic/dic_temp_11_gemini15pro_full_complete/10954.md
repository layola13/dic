arbiter: /ˈɑːrbɪtər/ (US) /ˈɑːbɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|权威人士，主宰者|A person or thing having power to judge or decide.|Fashion is the arbiter of taste. 时尚是品味的仲裁者。|synonyms: authority, controller, director, governor|antonyms: subject, follower|noun
