overcommunicated: /ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
过度沟通
Having communicated excessively, often to the point of redundancy or causing confusion.  The information has been relayed too many times or in too much detail.
The project manager overcommunicated the status updates, sending emails multiple times a day.  项目经理过度沟通了状态更新，每天多次发送电子邮件。

The marketing team overcommunicated the new product launch, leading to customer fatigue.  营销团队过度宣传了新产品发布，导致客户疲劳。

近义词: overshared, overexplained
反义词: undercommunicated
词性: verb (past participle)
