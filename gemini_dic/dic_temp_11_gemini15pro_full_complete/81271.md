granniest:/ˈɡræniɪst/| adj.|最像祖母的；最慈祥的；最古板的|Most resembling a grandmother, in terms of age, appearance, behavior, or qualities; most old-fashioned or traditional.|例句：She had the granniest smile I'd ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最慈祥的笑容。

例句：That's the granniest dress I've ever seen; where did you find it? 那是我见过最老式的裙子；你在哪里找到的？|近义词：oldest, most traditional, most old-fashioned|反义词：youngest, most modern, trendiest|词性:形容词

词源: "Granniest" is the superlative form of the adjective "granny," which is a colloquial term for "grandmother."  "Grandmother" comes from the Middle English "graundmoder," combining "graund" (grand) and "moder" (mother). The "-est" suffix is a common superlative ending in English.
