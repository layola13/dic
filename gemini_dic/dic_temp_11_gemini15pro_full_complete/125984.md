musketries:/ˈmʌskɪtriz/
n.
火枪射击，火枪齐射，枪声
The sound of musketries filled the air.  空中充满了火枪射击的声音。
The musketries of the enemy were incessant. 敌人的炮火不断。
Etymology: From musketry, from musket (a type of firearm) + -ry (a suffix forming nouns denoting a collection or art). Ultimately from Italian moschetto, diminutive of mosca "fly."
近义词: gunfire, volley, fusillade
反义词: silence, peace
