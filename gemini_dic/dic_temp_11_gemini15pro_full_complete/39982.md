consular: /ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsələr/ (US)
noun
领事
an official appointed by a government to live in a foreign city and protect and promote the government's interests and assist its citizens there
例句：
The consular advised her citizens to avoid the protest areas.
领事建议其公民避开抗议地区。

He contacted the consular for assistance with his lost passport.
他联系了领事寻求帮助找回丢失的护照。


近义词: consul
反义词: None
词性: noun
