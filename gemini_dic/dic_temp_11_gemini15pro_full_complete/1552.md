achievable:/əˈtʃiːvəbl/| adj.|可实现的；可达到的|Able to be achieved; attainable.|例句：Setting achievable goals is important for maintaining motivation.  设定可实现的目标对于保持动力很重要。

例句：With hard work, your dreams are achievable. 努力工作，你的梦想是可以实现的。|近义词：attainable, realistic, feasible, possible, viable|反义词：unattainable, impossible, unrealistic, impracticable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from achieve + -able.
