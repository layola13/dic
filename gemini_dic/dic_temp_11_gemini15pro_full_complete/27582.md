Canarians:/kəˈneəriənz/ | n. | 加那利群岛人；加那利群岛的鸟 | People from the Canary Islands, or birds native to the Canary Islands. | The Canarians are known for their warm hospitality. 加那利群岛人以热情好客而闻名。 | The Canarians sing beautifully. 加那利群岛的鸟儿歌唱得很动听。 | 近义词: islanders | 反义词: mainlanders | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Canary Islands + -an.  The Canary Islands are named after the Latin *canariae insulae* "islands of dogs", referring to the large dogs found there.  The birds, also native to the islands, took their name from the islands.
