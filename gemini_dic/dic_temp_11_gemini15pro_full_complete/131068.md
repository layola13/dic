nonbarbiturates:/ˌnɒnbɑːrˈbɪtʃərəts/ | n. | 非巴比妥类药物 | Drugs that are not barbiturates, a class of sedative and hypnotic drugs. nonbarbiturate:/ˌnɒnbɑːrˈbɪtʃərət/ | adj. | 非巴比妥类的 | Not belonging to the barbiturate class of drugs. | She was given a nonbarbiturate sedative. 她服用了非巴比妥类的镇静剂。| Synonyms: non-barbiturate | Antonyms: barbiturate | Adjective
