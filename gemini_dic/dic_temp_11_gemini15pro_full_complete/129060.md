nitrobenzene:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈbɛnˌziːn/ |n.|硝基苯|A pale yellow liquid with an almond-like odor, C6H5NO2, made by nitrating benzene and used in the manufacture of aniline and other organic chemicals.|Nitrobenzene is used in the production of aniline. 硝基苯用于苯胺的生产。|Nitrobenzene is a key precursor to aniline. 硝基苯是苯胺的关键前体。|Synonyms: oil of mirbane|Antonyms: None|noun
