inconvincible:/ˌɪnkənˈvɪnsəbl/
adjective
无法说服的，不信服的
not able to be convinced; not persuadable
His argument was weak and inconvincible. 他的论点很薄弱，无法令人信服。
近义词:unpersuadable, unconvincing, implausible
反义词:persuasive, convincing, credible
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + convincible (capable of being convinced), from Old French convincible, from Latin convincibilis, from convincere (to conquer, convict, convince).
