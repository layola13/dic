nondenominationalism:/ˌnɑːndɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |不分宗派的主义; 超宗派主义|The principle or practice of not being limited to or associated with a particular religious denomination. | Many couples now opt for nondenominational weddings. 许多夫妇现在选择不分宗派的婚礼。| The university has a nondenominational chapel. 这所大学有一个不分宗派的教堂。 | Synonym: interdenominationalism, ecumenism | Antonym: sectarianism | noun
