hairlessnesses:/ˈhɛərləsnəsɪz/
noun
无毛，秃头，脱毛
The state of lacking hair; baldness.
The Sphynx cat is known for its hairlessnesses.  斯芬克斯猫以其无毛而闻名。
The hairlessnesses of some breeds of dogs can be a result of selective breeding. 一些犬种的无毛是选择性育种的结果。
