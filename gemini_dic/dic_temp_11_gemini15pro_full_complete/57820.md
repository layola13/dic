drenchingly:/ˈdrɛntʃɪŋli/
adverb
湿透地，淋漓尽致地
so thoroughly wet as to soak or saturate
The rain was drenchingly heavy. 雨下得很大，把一切都淋透了。
Synonyms: soakingly, soppingly, thoroughly
Antonyms: dryly, slightly
词性: 副词
