orthodoxes:/ˈɔːrθədɒksiːz/
n.
正统观念，正统做法
orthodox beliefs or practices
The orthodoxes of the scientific community were challenged by the new findings. 科学界的正统观念受到了新发现的挑战。
近义词:  conventions, traditions, norms
反义词: heterodoxies, unorthodoxies
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


orthodox:/ˈɔːrθədɒks/
adj.
正统的；传统的；规范的；普遍接受的
following or conforming to traditionally accepted beliefs or practices
He is an orthodox Jew. 他是一个正统的犹太人。
近义词: conventional, traditional, established, accepted
反义词: unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox, radical
词性: 形容词
