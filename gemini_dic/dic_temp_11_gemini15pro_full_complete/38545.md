concludes:/kənˈkluːdz/
verb
结束，终止，断定
brings something to an end; arrives at a judgment or opinion
The meeting concludes at 5 pm. 会议下午五点结束。
The report concludes that the accident was caused by human error. 报告的结论是事故是由人为错误造成的。
synonyms: finishes, ends, terminates, closes, decides, infers, deduces
antonyms: begins, starts, commences, opens, initiates
