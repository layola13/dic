accompt:/əˈkaʊnt/| n.|账目，报告；解释，说明|A detailed record of money received and paid out, or a statement summarizing this.  A report or explanation of events.|He kept a meticulous accompt of his expenses. 他一丝不苟地记录他的开支。|Synonyms: account, reckoning, tally, record|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "disregard", "neglect" in the sense of failing to keep accounts)|noun
