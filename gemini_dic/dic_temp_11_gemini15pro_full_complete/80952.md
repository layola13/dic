graced:/ɡreɪst/| v. adj.|使优美，使增光；优雅的，优美的|To add grace or elegance to something or someone; having or showing grace or elegance. |The occasion was graced by the presence of the Queen. 女王的出席使这个场合增光添彩。|近义词：adorned, embellished, dignified, elegant|反义词:disgraced, marred, tarnished|词性:动词/形容词
词源:From Middle English *graced, *gracid, from Anglo-Norman grace, Old French grace, from Latin gratia (“grace, favor, thanks”).
