multiplicable:/ˌmʌltɪˈplɪkəbl/
adjective
可乘的
Capable of being multiplied.
Any even number is multiplicable by two. 任何偶数都可以乘以二。
近义词:divisible, factorable
反义词:indivisible, non-factorable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin multiplicabilis "capable of being multiplied," from Latin multiplicare "to multiply" (see multiply).
