extemporise: /ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz/
| v. | 即兴创作，即兴表演 | To compose, perform, or produce something without preparation; to improvise. |  She had to extemporise a speech when the scheduled speaker fell ill.  当预定的演讲者生病时，她不得不即兴演讲。 | improvise, ad-lib, wing it | plan, rehearse | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ex tempore "out of the time," from Latin ex "out of" + tempore, ablative of tempus "time."  The verb form emerged in the 17th century.
