entity:/ˈɛntɪti/ | n. | 实体；存在；本质 |Something that exists separately and has a clear identity of its own. |  Example:  He regarded the north of the country as a separate political entity. 他把这个国家的北部看作一个独立的政治实体。

Example: The mind and body are distinct entities. 精神和身体是截然不同的实体。 | Synonyms: being, existence, individual, organism, unit | Antonyms: nothingness, void | noun
