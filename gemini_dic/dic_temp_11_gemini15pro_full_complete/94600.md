imprecisions:/ˌɪmprɪˈsɪʒənz/| n. |不精确；不准确；误差|lack of exactness or accuracy|The report suffered from several imprecisions and omissions.|Synonyms: inaccuracies, errors, vagueness, ambiguities|Antonyms: precision, accuracy, exactness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *imprécision*, from *im-* (not) + *précision* (precision), from Latin *praecisio* (a cutting off), from *praecidere* (to cut off before), from *prae-* (before) + *caedere* (to cut).
