air-streams:/ˈɛər striːmz/ /ˈer striːmz/
noun
气流
a flow of air in a particular direction
例句：The air-streams from the Arctic bring cold weather.  来自北极的气流带来了寒冷的天气。

例句：Pilots need to be aware of air-streams at high altitudes. 飞行员需要了解高空气流的情况。

近义词：air currents, winds, drafts
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps stagnant air)
词性:n.
