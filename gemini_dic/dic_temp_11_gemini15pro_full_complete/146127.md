overemphasize: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪz/ | v. | 过分强调；过分重视 | To place excessive importance or emphasis on something; to stress too much. | He tends to overemphasize the importance of his work. 他倾向于过分强调他的工作的重要性。| overstate, exaggerate | understate, downplay | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + emphasize.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Emphasize" comes from the French "emphasiser," ultimately from the Greek "emphasis" meaning "significance" or "importance."
