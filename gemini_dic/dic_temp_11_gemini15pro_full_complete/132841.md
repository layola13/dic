noneducations:/ˌnɒnˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非教育，缺乏教育 |  Absence or lack of education; things that do not contribute to education or are considered uneducational. | The government's cuts in funding have led to many noneducations in poorer communities. 政府削减资金导致贫困社区出现许多缺乏教育的现象。|  The focus on standardized testing has resulted in a proliferation of noneducations, such as rote memorization without understanding. 对标准化考试的关注导致了非教育的泛滥，例如死记硬背而不理解。 |Near synonyms: ignorance, illiteracy, unenlightenment| Antonyms: education, learning, enlightenment | noun
