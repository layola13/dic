nation:/ˈneɪʃən/ (US), /ˈneɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |国家，民族|A large body of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory.|例句：The nation mourned the death of its leader.  这个国家哀悼其领导人的去世。|例句：The whole nation celebrated the victory. 整个国家都在庆祝胜利。|近义词：country, state, people|反义词：N/A|词性：noun

Etymology:
From Old French *nacion*  (Modern French *nation*) and directly from Latin *nationem* (nominative *natio*) "birth, race, stock, kind, species, nation," from *natus*, past participle of *nasci* "to be born."
