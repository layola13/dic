individual: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/ (US), /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒʊəl/ (UK)| n.|个人，个体|A single, separate being, distinguished from a group or class.|例句：The rights of the individual must be protected. 个人的权利必须得到保护。|近义词：person, being, human|反义词：group, society, community|词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin individualis, from individuus ‘indivisible’, from in- ‘not’ + dividuus ‘divisible’ (from dividere ‘to divide’).
