outquencher:/ˌaʊtˈkwɛntʃər/
n.
熄灭剂，抑制剂
A substance that inhibits or stops a process, especially a chemical or physical reaction.
The scientist added an outquencher to stop the luminescence reaction.  科学家添加了一种熄灭剂来停止发光反应。
Synonyms: inhibitor, suppressant, quencher
Antonyms: catalyst, accelerator
