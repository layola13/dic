censurers:/ˈsɛnʃərərz/
noun
批评者，责难者
People who express strong disapproval or criticism of someone or something.
The politician's controversial statement drew harsh rebukes from censurers across the political spectrum.  这位政客备受争议的言论引来了各政治派别批评者的严厉指责。
The play was praised by critics but condemned by some moral censurers.  这部戏剧受到了评论家的赞扬，但却遭到了一些道德卫士的谴责。
synonyms: critics, detractors, faultfinders
antonyms: supporters, admirers, defenders
n.

censure:/ˈsɛnʃər/
verb
责备，批评
To express strong disapproval of someone or something.
The Senate censured the senator for unethical conduct. 参议院谴责了这位参议员的不道德行为。
The film was censured for its violence and explicit content. 这部电影因其暴力和露骨的内容而受到谴责。
synonyms: criticize, condemn, rebuke
antonyms: praise, commend, applaud
v.
