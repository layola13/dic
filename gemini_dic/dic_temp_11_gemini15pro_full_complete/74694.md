freegan:/ˈfriːɡən/
n.
拾荒者；免费素食主义者
A person who rejects consumerism and seeks to help the environment by reducing waste, especially by retrieving and using discarded food and other goods.
Freegans often dumpster dive for food that is still edible but has been thrown away by supermarkets.  拾荒者经常在超市的垃圾箱里寻找仍然可以食用但已被丢弃的食物。

Freegans believe that consumerism is damaging to the environment. 免费素食主义者认为消费主义正在破坏环境。

近义词: dumpster diver, scavenger
反义词: consumerist
词性: noun
