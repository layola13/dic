heraud:/ˈhɛrɔːd/ | n. | 传令官；通报者 | A herald is an official messenger, especially one representing a monarch or a state, or a person or thing viewed as a sign that something is about to happen. |  The king sent a herald to announce the birth of his son. 国王派了一名传令官去宣布他儿子的出生。

The robin is a herald of spring. 知更鸟是春天的预兆。 | synonyms: messenger, envoy, announcer, harbinger, precursor | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "receiver," "audience") | noun
