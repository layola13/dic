intendent:/ɪnˈtɛndənt/ | n. |负责人；主管；指挥者|A person who is in charge of a particular place or thing, or who is responsible for managing certain activities. |The intendant of the park was responsible for its upkeep. 公园的负责人负责维护公园。|Synonyms: superintendent, manager, director, administrator, supervisor|Antonyms: subordinate, assistant|noun
