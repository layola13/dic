enforcingly:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪŋli/
adverb|强制地，强有力地|In a manner that compels observance or compliance.  Derived from "enforce" which means to compel observance of or compliance with (a law, rule, or obligation). The suffix "-ingly" transforms the verb into an adverb indicating the manner of action.|The rules were enforced enforcingly, leaving no room for dissent.|近义词：compulsorily, rigorously, strictly|反义词：leniently, permissively|词性: adverb
