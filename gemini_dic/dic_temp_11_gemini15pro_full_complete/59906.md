edemata:/ɪˈdiːmətə/
n.
水肿，浮肿
Swelling caused by excess fluid in the body tissues.
The patient presented with edemata in the lower extremities. 病人的下肢出现水肿。
近义词:swellings, oedema
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider normal tissue state)


Etymology:
From Modern Latin *edemata, plural of edema, from Greek oidēma "swelling," from oidein "to swell."
