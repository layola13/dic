ablaze:/əˈbleɪz/| adj.|燃烧的，着火的；闪耀的，激动的|Burning fiercely; brightly lit; filled with strong emotion.|例句：The building was ablaze after the explosion.  爆炸后，大楼燃起了熊熊大火。

例句：Her eyes were ablaze with anger. 她眼中燃烧着怒火。|近义词：aflame, alight, burning, fiery, ignited, radiant, shining|反义词：extinguished, dark, dull|词性:形容词

ablaze:/əˈbleɪz/| adv.|燃烧着，着火着；闪耀着|In a blaze; burning brightly.|例句：The forest was ablaze with the colours of autumn. 森林里秋色斑斓，一片 ablaze。

例句：The city was ablaze with lights for the festival. 为庆祝节日，城市灯火通明。|近义词：fiercely, brightly|反义词：dimly, darkly|词性:副词
