enterprises: /ˈɛntəpraɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈɛntərpraɪzɪz/ (US)| n.| 企业，事业；进取心，事业心 | Plural form of "enterprise." Refers to multiple businesses, companies, projects, or undertakings. | 例句：Several new enterprises have been launched in the tech sector. 科技领域涌现了几家新企业。|近义词：businesses, companies, ventures, undertakings|反义词: inactivity, idleness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *entreprise (“undertaking”), from entreprendre (“to undertake”), from entre- (“between, among”) + prendre (“to take”).
