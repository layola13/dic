intercolonizes:/ˌɪntərˈkɒlənaɪzɪz/
verb
相互殖民；在殖民地之间殖民
The act of establishing colonies within or between existing colonies, often by different colonizing powers.
European powers intercolonized within Africa, carving up territories amongst themselves. 欧洲列强在非洲内部相互殖民，瓜分领土。
The company intercolonized different regions within the established colony, exploiting resources and expanding their influence.  该公司在已建立的殖民地内的不同区域之间进行殖民，开发资源并扩大其影响力。
Synonyms: cross-colonize, inter-imperialize
Antonyms: decolonize, liberate
词性: 动词
