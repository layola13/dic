blastula:/ˈblæstʃʊlə/ (US), /ˈblæstjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |囊胚|An early stage of embryonic development in animals, consisting of a hollow, fluid-filled sphere of cells formed after the morula stage.  |The blastula is a crucial stage in development.  囊胚是发育的关键阶段。| The human blastula implants in the uterine wall about a week after fertilization. 人类囊胚在受精后大约一周植入子宫壁。|Synonyms: blastosphere|Antonyms:  N/A (This is a specific stage, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
