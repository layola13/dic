humidness:/ˈhjuːmɪdnəs/ /ˈhjuːmɪdnɪs/
n.
潮湿，湿润
The state or quality of being humid; dampness.
The humidness of the air made it difficult to breathe. 空气的潮湿使人难以呼吸。

近义词:humidity, dampness, moisture, mugginess
反义词:dryness, aridity

词性:noun
词源:From humid + -ness.  Humid comes from French humide, from Latin humidus “moist, damp,” from humere “to be moist.”
