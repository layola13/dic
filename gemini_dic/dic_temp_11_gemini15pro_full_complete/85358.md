harrow's:/ˈhæroʊz/ | n. | 耙； Harrow 的所有格；|A harrow is a farm implement used to break up and smooth out plowed land.  Harrow's can also indicate possession, such as "Harrow's School," a famous British school.| Example: The farmer used a harrow's to prepare the field for planting.  农民用耙来平整土地准备播种。 | Synonym: cultivator, tiller | Antonym: (For the tool itself, there isn't a direct antonym.  Perhaps "compactor" as it does the opposite.) | noun


harrow's:/ˈhæroʊz/ | n. |哈罗公学的|Harrow's refers to Harrow School, a prestigious independent school for boys in London, England.|Example: He attended Harrow's and went on to Oxford. 他就读于哈罗公学，后来去了牛津大学。|Synonym: Harrow School|Antonym: N/A|noun
