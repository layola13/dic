mismanagement: /ˌmɪsmˈænɪdʒmənt/
| n. |管理不善，经营不善|Act of mismanaging something; mismanagement implies not only failing to direct something well but also causing it to go wrong or fail. 对某事管理不善的行为；管理不善不仅意味着未能很好地指导某事，而且还意味着导致其出错或失败。|Derived from the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") and the verb "manage" (meaning "to control or direct"). 源自前缀“mis-”（意为“糟糕地”或“错误地”）和动词“manage”（意为“控制或指导”）。|例句：The mismanagement of the company led to its bankruptcy. 公司的管理不善导致其破产。|近义词：misconduct, mishandling, maladministration |反义词：good management, proper administration|词性:名词
