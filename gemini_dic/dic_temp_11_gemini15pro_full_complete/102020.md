isogonism: /aɪˈsɒɡənɪzəm/
n.
等角性，等角现象
The property of having equal angles; equality of angles.
Crystals exhibiting isogonism have identical angles between corresponding faces.  表现出等角性的晶体在相应的面之间具有相同的角度。
Isogonism is a crucial concept in crystallography. 等角性是晶体学中的一个关键概念。
近义词:  isoangularity
反义词:  anisogonism (不常用)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *isos* (equal) + *gonia* (angle) + *-ism* (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).
