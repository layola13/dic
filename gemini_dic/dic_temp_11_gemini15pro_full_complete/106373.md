konat:/ˈkɔːnæt/ /ˈkoʊnæt/| n.|锥体，球果|A konat is a cone-shaped structure, especially in plants, such as a pine cone.  一个konat是一个锥形结构，尤其是在植物中，例如松果。|例句：The pine tree was covered in konats. 松树上覆盖着球果。

例句：She collected several konats from the forest floor. 她从森林地面收集了几个球果。|近义词：cone, strobile|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


Etymology (词源):
From Modern Latin *cōnātus, from Latin cōnus (“cone”).  源自现代拉丁语 *cōnātus，来自拉丁语 cōnus（“锥体”）。
