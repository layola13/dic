Synonym: disappearance, vanishing, loss
Antonym: appearance, arrival, emergence
Word type: Noun

Etymology: From Old French *desaparaillier, from des- "un-" + aparaillier "to equip, array," from pareil "equal, like," from Latin pariculus "very small, tiny," diminutive of par "equal."  The original meaning was "to undress," later evolving to "vanish."
