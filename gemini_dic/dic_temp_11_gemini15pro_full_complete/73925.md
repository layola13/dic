forsythias:/fɔːrˈsɪθiəz/ | n. |连翘属植物，黄寿丹|Deciduous shrubs with bright yellow flowers in early spring, belonging to the olive family.|例句：The forsythias are blooming beautifully in the garden. 花园里的连翘开得真漂亮。

例句：Forsythias are a welcome sign of spring. 连翘是春天到来的一个受欢迎的标志。|近义词：golden bells|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑不开花的植物或冬季植物) winter shrubs|词性:noun
