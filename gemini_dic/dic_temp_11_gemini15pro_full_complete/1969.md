activators:/ˈæktɪveɪtərz/| n.|激活剂，活化剂|Substances or factors that initiate or promote a chemical or biological process.  They stimulate activity or cause something to start functioning.|Enzymes are biological activators that speed up chemical reactions in the body. 酶是生物激活剂，可加速体内化学反应。|synonyms: catalysts, stimulants, triggers|antonyms: inhibitors, suppressants|noun
