centiare:/ˌsɑːnˈtiɛər/ | n. | 公亩 | A unit of area equal to one square meter, or one hundredth of an are. |  A centiare is a small area, about the size of a large bath towel.  一块公亩是一小块区域，大约相当于一条大浴巾的大小。 | synonym: square meter | antonym: hectare | noun
