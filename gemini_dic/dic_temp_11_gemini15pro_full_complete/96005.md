incursion:/ɪnˈkɜːrʒən/ |/ɪnˈkɜːrʃən/|n.|入侵，侵犯；袭击|An invasion or attack, especially a sudden or brief one.|The rebels made an incursion into the south of the country. 叛军入侵了该国南部。|Synonyms: invasion, raid, attack, assault, foray, infiltration|Antonyms: retreat, withdrawal|noun

incursion:/ɪnˈkɜːrʒən/ |/ɪnˈkɜːrʃən/|n.|侵入；侵害|An act of entering another's territory, domain, or sphere of activity.|The company made an incursion into the European market.  这家公司打入了欧洲市场。|Synonyms: encroachment, intrusion, trespass|Antonyms: respect, observance|noun
