invalidator:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪtər/| n. |使无效者；宣布无效者|Someone or something that invalidates something else.  Rendering something null and void, or proving it to be wrong.  |The judge acted as the invalidator of the contract.|近义词：negator, nullifier, overruler, canceller|反义词：validator, confirmer, ratifier|noun
