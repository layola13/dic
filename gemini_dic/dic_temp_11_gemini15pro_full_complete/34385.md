classifiers:/ˈklæsɪfaɪərz/| n.|分类器，分类者|A person or thing that classifies or sorts something into categories.  A classifier can also be a word or morpheme that indicates the class or type of a noun, especially in some languages.|The library uses classifiers to organize its vast collection of books. 图书馆使用分类器来组织其庞大的藏书。|synonyms: categorizers, sorters, taxonomists|antonyms: aggregators, combiners|noun
