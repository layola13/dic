helminthic:/hɛlˈmɪnθɪk/| adj.|蠕虫的，驱虫的|Relating to or caused by parasitic worms (helminths).|例句：The doctor prescribed helminthic medication to treat the infection. 医生开了驱虫药来治疗感染。

例句：Helminthic infections are common in tropical regions. 蠕虫感染在热带地区很常见。|近义词：vermicular, vermifugal|反义词: None|词性: adjective


Etymology->
From Greek "helmins" (worm) + "-ic" (suffix forming adjectives).
