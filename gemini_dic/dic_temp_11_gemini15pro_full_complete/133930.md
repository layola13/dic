nonhandicaps:/ˌnɒnˈhændɪkæps/ | n. | 非障碍优势;优势;非残疾; |  Advantages or strengths, especially in a competitive situation, that are not related to disabilities.  It refers to inherent qualities, skills, resources, or circumstances that give an individual or group an edge or a better chance of success. It's the opposite of a handicap, which implies a disadvantage.  | The team's nonhandicaps include a strong coach and ample funding.  该队的优势包括强大的教练和充足的资金。|  The company's nonhandicaps in the market are its innovative technology and strong brand recognition.  该公司在市场上的优势在于其创新技术和强大的品牌认知度。 | Synonyms: advantages, strengths, assets, merits,  | Antonyms: handicaps, disadvantages, weaknesses, drawbacks | noun
