halfpennies: /ˈheɪpəniz/ (UK) /ˈhæfpɛniz/ (US)| n. | 半便士（旧英国货币，等于半便士）| A plural form of halfpenny, referring to multiple halfpenny coins.  Before decimalization in 1971, a halfpenny was equal to half of a penny. | I found a few halfpennies in my grandfather's old coin collection. 我在我祖父的旧硬币收藏中发现了几枚半便士。| Synonym: halfpence (more common in modern usage, also accepts singular or plural verbs) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps larger denominations like pennies, shillings, or pounds) | noun
