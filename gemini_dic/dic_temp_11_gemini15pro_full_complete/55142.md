dissectible:/dɪˈsɛktəbl/ /daɪˈsɛktəbl/| adj.|可解剖的，可剖析的|Able to be dissected; capable of being divided into separate parts for examination and study.|The frog is a commonly used dissectible animal in biology classes.  青蛙是在生物课上常用的可解剖动物。|The argument is complex but dissectible.  这个论点很复杂，但可以剖析。|近义词：analyzable, divisible|反义词：indivisible, inseparable|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *dissectibilis*, from dissectus (past participle of dissecāre ‘to dissect’), from dis- ‘apart’ + secāre ‘to cut’.
