interweaving:/ˌɪntərˈwiːvɪŋ/ | n. v. adj.|交织，交错，穿插|The act of weaving together; the state of being woven together.|The tapestry featured an intricate interweaving of silk and gold threads.  这幅挂毯的特点是丝绸和金线的复杂交织。|近义词：interlacing, intertwining, blending, merging|反义词：separating, disentangling|词性: Noun (primarily), Verb (present participle), Adjective (attributive)

interweave:/ˌɪntərˈwiːv/ | v. |交织，交错，穿插|To weave together; to blend or mix intricately.|The author skillfully interweaves historical facts with fictional narrative. 作者巧妙地将史实与虚构的叙事交织在一起。|近义词：interlace, intertwine, blend, merge|反义词：separate, disentangle|词性: Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *interweven*, from Old English *interwefan*, from *inter-* (between) + *wefan* (to weave).  The word has maintained its core meaning of weaving together throughout its history.
