counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/
n.|平衡重，抗衡力，抵消|The large concrete block acts as a counterweight for the drawbridge.  这块巨大的混凝土块充当吊桥的平衡重。|近义词:balance, offset, compensation|反义词:exacerbation, amplification|词性:名词
v.|使平衡，抵消|His calmness counterweights her anxiety. 他的冷静抵消了她的焦虑。|近义词:balance, offset, compensate|反义词:exacerbate, amplify|词性:动词


Etymology:
From "counter-" (meaning "against, opposite") + "weight" (meaning "a heavy object").  The concept is of a weight used to oppose or balance another weight. 源于 "counter-"（意为“反对，相反”）+ "weight"（意为“重物”）。这个概念是指用来对抗或平衡另一个重量的重量。
