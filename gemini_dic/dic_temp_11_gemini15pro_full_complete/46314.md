PHRASE->
a dab of:  少量，一点  (a dab of butter 一点黄油)
dab at: 轻拍，轻擦 (dab at the stain 轻擦污渍)
dab on: 涂抹 (dab on some lotion 涂抹一些乳液)
dab off:  擦掉 (dab off the excess paint 擦掉多余的油漆)
dab with: 用...轻拍/涂抹 (dab with a cloth 用布轻拍)
