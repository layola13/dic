evacuate: /ɪˈvækjueɪt/ (US), /ɪˈvækjuːeɪt/ (UK)
verb
撤离，疏散
To remove someone from a place of danger to a safer place.
The building was evacuated because of a bomb threat.  这栋楼因为炸弹威胁而被疏散。
Residents were evacuated from their homes due to the rising floodwaters. 由于不断上涨的洪水，居民们被从家中疏散。
近义词: clear, remove, withdraw
反义词: occupy, inhabit, populate
词性: verb
词源: From Latin evacuare "to empty out," from ex- "out" + vacuare "to empty."
