ballistically:/bəˈlɪstɪkli/| adv.|弹道式地，以射击方式|Relating to the flight of projectiles, especially bullets or missiles.  Driven by force, as if shot from a gun.  Informal: very quickly and forcefully.|The rocket propelled ballistically. 火箭以弹道方式推进。|Near Synonyms:  projectively, rapidly, forcefully|Antonyms: gradually, slowly|Adverb
