oligomerous:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɒmərəs/
adjective
少聚的，低聚的
Having a small number of parts, especially of floral parts.  Specifically, having fewer parts than the typical number for a related taxon.
The oligomerous flowers of this species distinguish it from its close relatives.  这种植物少聚的花朵使其区别于近亲。
