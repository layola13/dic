overnegligences:/ˌōvərˈneɡlədʒənsɪz/
noun
过度疏忽，极度疏漏
Extreme carelessness or a series of neglectful acts.
His overnegligences led to the downfall of the company. 他的极度疏忽导致了公司的倒闭。
Near synonyms: gross negligence, serious neglect, extreme carelessness
Antonyms: diligence, attentiveness, conscientiousness
词性: 名词
