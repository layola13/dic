disadventure:/ˌdɪsədˈvɛntʃər/ | n. |不幸的遭遇，灾难|An unlucky or unfortunate adventure; a mishap.|例句：Their camping trip turned into a disadventure when their tent was swept away by the flood. 他们的露营之旅变成了一场灾难，因为他们的帐篷被洪水冲走了。|例句：She recounted the disadventures of her travels through the jungle. 她讲述了她在丛林旅行中的种种不幸遭遇。|近义词：mishap, misfortune, misadventure, setback|反义词：fortune, success, good luck|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle French *desaventure*, from des- "away, from, without" + aventure "adventure." Ultimately from Latin adventūra "that which happens; a chance event," from adventus "arrival," past participle of advenīre "to arrive," from ad- "to" + venīre "to come."
