apetalous: /eɪˈpɛtələs/
adjective
无瓣的；无花瓣的
Lacking petals; having no petals.
The apetalous flowers of the wind-pollinated plant are inconspicuous.  这种风媒传粉植物的无瓣花并不显眼。
Synonyms: petal-less, non-petalous
Antonyms: petalous, petaliferous
词性: 形容词
