nonfighter:/ˈnɒnˈfaɪtər/ (US) /ˈnɒnˈfaɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |非战斗人员，非战斗机|A person who is not a fighter, especially a civilian in wartime or a member of the armed forces who does not engage in combat. |例句：The nonfighters were evacuated from the city. 非战斗人员已从城市撤离。|近义词：civilian, noncombatant|反义词：fighter, combatant, soldier|noun

nonfighter:/ˈnɒnˈfaɪtər/ (US) /ˈnɒnˈfaɪtə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |非战斗的|Not designed or used for fighting.  Relating to or characteristic of nonfighters. |例句：The plane was a nonfighter aircraft used for reconnaissance. 这架飞机是一架用于侦察的非战斗机。|近义词：noncombat, nonmilitary|反义词：fighter, combat, military|adjective
