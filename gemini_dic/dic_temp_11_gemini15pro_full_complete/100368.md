interstition:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃən/ | n. |空隙，缝隙|A small or narrow space between things or parts of things.|例句：Dust gathered in the interstices of the carved woodwork.  灰尘积聚在雕刻木制品缝隙中。

例句：The archaeologist discovered ancient coins in the interstices of the wall. 考古学家在墙缝中发现了古代硬币。|近义词：gap, crack, crevice, fissure, aperture, chink|反义词：closure, seal, juncture|词性：名词
