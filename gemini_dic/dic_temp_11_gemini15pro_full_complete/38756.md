condonation:/ˌkɒndəˈneɪʃən/ |n.|宽恕；赦免；默许|The condonation of violence is unacceptable. 对暴力的纵容是不可接受的。|  It implies not merely forgiveness but also an overlooking of the offense, as if it had not happened. 它不仅意味着宽恕，而且意味着对过错的忽视，就好像它没有发生过一样。|synonyms: forgiveness, pardon, remission, absolution|antonyms: condemnation, disapproval, censure|noun
