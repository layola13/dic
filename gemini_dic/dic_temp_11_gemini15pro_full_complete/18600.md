benitier:/ˌbɛnɪˈtɪər/| n.|圣水器，圣水钵|A receptacle for holy water, typically placed near the entrance of a church, used by worshippers for making the sign of the cross.|例句：The benitier at the entrance was made of carved marble. 入口处的圣水器是大理石雕刻的。|例句：She dipped her fingers in the benitier and crossed herself. 她把手指浸在圣水钵里划了个十字。|近义词：holy water font, stoup|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
