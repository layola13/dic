bolling:/ˈbɒlɪŋ/ | adj. |沸腾的; 奔涌的; 激昂的 |  Characterized by intense emotion, excitement, or activity, like something boiling.  | The crowd was bolling with excitement after the winning goal.  人群在进球后激动得沸腾起来。 | 近义词: seething, surging, exuberant, passionate | 反义词: calm, placid, apathetic | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Middle English *bollen, from Old English *bullan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bollen and German bollen.
