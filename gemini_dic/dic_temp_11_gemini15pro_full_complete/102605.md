jailership:/ˈdʒeɪlərʃɪp/
noun
狱卒的职位；监禁；在押
The state or period of being a jailer; imprisonment.
The corrupt official abused his jailership, treating inmates inhumanely.  这个腐败的官员滥用职权，不人道地对待囚犯。
He spent a year in jailership for his crimes. 他因犯罪被监禁了一年。
近义词：imprisonment, confinement, custody, incarceration
反义词：freedom, liberty, release
词性:n.
