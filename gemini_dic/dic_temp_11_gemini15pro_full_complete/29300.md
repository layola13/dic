castaways:/ˈkæstəˌweɪz/ | n. | 遇难者，漂流者，被抛弃者 | People who are shipwrecked and stranded on a deserted island or coast. | The castaways built a raft to escape the island.  遇难者们建造了一个木筏逃离小岛。 | 近义词: survivors, shipwreck victims | 反义词: rescuers | 词性: 名词

castaway:/ˈkæstəˌweɪ/ | adj. | 被抛弃的，遭难的 | Abandoned or adrift. | The castaway boat drifted aimlessly in the ocean.  被抛弃的船只在海上漫无目的地漂流。| 近义词: abandoned, adrift | 反义词: rescued | 词性: 形容词


词源: From "cast away," meaning to throw or discard something.  源自“cast away”，意为丢弃某物。
