demandant:/dɪˈmændənt/| n.|原告，请求者|A person who makes a demand, especially in a legal context; a plaintiff. |例句：The demandant in the case sought restitution for damages. 此案中的原告要求赔偿损失。

例句：The demandant filed a lawsuit against the company.  原告对该公司提起了诉讼。|近义词：plaintiff, claimant, petitioner|反义词：defendant, respondent|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demandant*, present participle of demander "to ask, demand," from Latin demandare "to entrust, commit, enjoin, order, demand," from de- "from" + mandare "to entrust, order."
