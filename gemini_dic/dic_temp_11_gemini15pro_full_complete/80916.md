gowned:/ɡaʊnd/
adjective
穿着长袍的，穿着礼服的
dressed in a gown, especially a formal or academic one
The gowned graduates processed into the hall. 穿着长袍的毕业生列队进入大厅。
gowned: robed, clad
gowned: naked, undressed
adjective
