defilading:/ˌdiːfɪˈleɪdɪŋ/
n.
掩护，遮蔽；散兵线
Protecting from enemy fire by using natural or artificial barriers.  The act of positioning troops or equipment so that they are protected from enemy fire, typically by using natural or artificial barriers.
The troops were positioned in a defilade to protect them from enemy fire. 部队被安置在一个掩蔽处以保护他们免受敌人的炮火。
The engineer corps began defilading the forward positions. 工兵部队开始掩护前方阵地。
protection, shielding, concealment
exposure
noun
