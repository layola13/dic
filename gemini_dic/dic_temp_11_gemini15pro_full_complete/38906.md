confessee:/kənˌfeˈsiː/
n.
忏悔者，告解者
the person to whom a confession is made
The priest listened patiently to the confessee's story.  神父耐心地听着忏悔者的故事。

The confessee felt a sense of relief after confessing.  忏悔者在忏悔后感到如释重负。

近义词: penitent, confessor (can be used for the one confessing in some contexts)
反义词: confessor (can be used for the one hearing the confession)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *confesse, from Old French confessé, past participle of confesser "to confess," from Latin confessus, past participle of confessari "to acknowledge, confess," from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge, confess."  The ending -ee denotes the recipient of the action.
