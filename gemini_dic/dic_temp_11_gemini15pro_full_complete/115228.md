manifoldnesses:/ˈmænɪˌfoʊldnɪsɪz/
n.
多方面性，多种多样，各种各样
The manifoldnesses of human experience are vast and complex. 人类经验的多样性是广阔而复杂的。
The poem explores the manifoldnesses of love. 这首诗探索了爱情的多种多样。
近义词:diversity, variety, multiplicity, plurality
反义词:uniformity, homogeneity, singularity
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From manifold + -ness.  Manifold itself comes from Middle English *manyfold, from Old English manigfeald, from manig (many) + feald (fold).
