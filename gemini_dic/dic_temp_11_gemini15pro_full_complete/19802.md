biltmore:/ˈbɪltmɔːr/
n.
比尔特莫庄园
Biltmore Estate, a large private estate and tourist attraction in Asheville, North Carolina.
We visited the Biltmore Estate last summer.  我们去年夏天参观了比尔特莫庄园。
The Biltmore is a popular destination for history buffs. 比尔特莫庄园是历史爱好者的热门目的地。
synonym:estate, mansion
antonym:hut, shack
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Biltmore" is a combination of "Built" and "More," coined by George Vanderbilt, the estate's owner. "Built" refers to the construction of the grand house, while "More" alludes to the surrounding vast estate and its extensive features.
