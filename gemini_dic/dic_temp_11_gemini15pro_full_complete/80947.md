grabed:/ɡræbd/ | v. | 抓住，攫取，赶紧做（非正式） | past tense of "grab" -  took or seized suddenly.  He grabbed the railing to steady himself. 他抓住栏杆稳住自己。| 近义词：seized, snatched, grasped, clutched | 反义词: released, dropped, let go | verb

Etymology:  From Proto-Germanic *grabōną (“to scratch, scrape”), of uncertain origin. Cognate with Dutch grabbelen (“to grabble”), German graben (“to dig”).
