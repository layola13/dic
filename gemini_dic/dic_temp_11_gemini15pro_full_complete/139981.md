odorosity:/ˌoʊdəˈrɑːsəti/
n.
气味；臭味；香味
The quality of having an odor, especially a strong or unpleasant one.
The odorosity of the durian fruit is notorious.  榴莲的气味是出了名的。
The odorosity of the roses filled the room. 玫瑰的香味充满了整个房间。
近义词:smell, aroma, scent, fragrance, stink, reek
反义词:inodorousness
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *odorositas, from Latin odorus (“odorous”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a quality or state).  Odōrus is from odor (“smell, scent”) (from Proto-Indo-European *h₃ed- (“to smell”)) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning “full of, prone to”).
