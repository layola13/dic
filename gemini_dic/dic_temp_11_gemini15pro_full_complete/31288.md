chaperoned:/ˈʃæpərəʊnd/ (UK) /ˈʃæpəroʊnd/ (US)| v. |陪伴；监督；护送|to accompany or supervise someone, especially a young person, on a social occasion to ensure proper behavior.|例句：The students were chaperoned on their school trip. 学生们在学校旅行中有老师陪伴。

例句：She chaperoned her younger sister to the dance. 她陪她妹妹去参加舞会。|近义词：supervised, escorted, accompanied, guided|反义词：unsupervised, unescorted|词性：动词
