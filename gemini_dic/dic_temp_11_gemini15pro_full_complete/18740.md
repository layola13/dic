bergschrund:/ˈbɛərɡʃrʊnd/ /ˈbɜːrɡʃrʊnd/
|n.|冰斗裂隙，冰裂缝|A crevasse or wide crack that separates flowing glacial ice from the stationary ice or firn above|The mountaineers had to navigate a treacherous bergschrund to reach the summit.  登山者必须穿过一条危险的冰斗裂隙才能到达顶峰。|Synonyms: crevasse, fissure, chasm|Antonyms: closure, seal|noun
