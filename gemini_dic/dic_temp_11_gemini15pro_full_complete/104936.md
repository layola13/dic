keloid:/ˈkiːlɔɪd/| n.|瘢痕疙瘩，疤痕瘤|A raised, thickened scar that forms after an injury or surgery, extending beyond the original wound boundaries.|例句：She developed a keloid after her ear piercing. 她穿耳洞后长了瘢痕疙瘩。

例句：Keloids can be itchy and painful. 瘢痕疙瘩会发痒和疼痛。|近义词：scar tissue, cicatrix|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "healthy skin")|词性:noun
