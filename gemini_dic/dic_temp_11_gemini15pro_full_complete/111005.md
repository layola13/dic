liquoring:/ˈlɪkərɪŋ/
n.
浸渍，润湿（尤指用液体处理皮革或纺织品）
The process of treating leather or textiles with a liquid solution.
The liquoring of the leather made it more supple.  皮革经过浸渍后变得更加柔软。
The fabric underwent a special liquoring process to enhance its durability.  这种织物经过特殊的浸渍处理以增强其耐用性。
saturation, wetting, soaking
drying
noun
