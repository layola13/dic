monosulphide:/ˌmɒnəˈsʌlfaɪd/ /ˌmɑːnəˈsʌlfaɪd/| n. |一硫化物|A binary compound of sulfur with another element or group, where sulfur has a valency of 2.  It contains one sulfur atom per molecule. |Iron monosulphide is a black solid. 硫化亚铁是一种黑色固体。| synonym: sulfide | antonym: None (lacks a direct chemical opposite) | noun
