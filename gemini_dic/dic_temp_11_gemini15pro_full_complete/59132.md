dynamited:/ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪtɪd/
adj.|被炸毁的；用炸药炸毁的|blown up with dynamite; destroyed by dynamite|The dynamited remains of the building were a stark reminder of the war.  被炸毁的建筑残骸是对战争的严酷提醒。|例句：They discovered a dynamited section of the railway track.  他们发现一段铁路被炸毁了。|近义词：blown up, exploded, demolished|反义词：intact, undamaged|词性:adj.


词源:

From dynamite + -ed.  Dynamite itself comes from the Greek dynamis "power" + -ite.
