machismo:/məˈtʃɪzməʊ/ (US) /məˈkɪzməʊ/ (UK)| n. |大男子主义，男子气概|Strong or aggressive masculine pride.  An exaggerated sense of manliness; an attitude of male superiority.  It can manifest as an overemphasis on traditional gender roles,  a tendency to dominate or control women, and a rejection of anything considered feminine.|He displayed a certain machismo by refusing to ask for directions. 他拒绝问路，表现出某种大男子主义。|Synonyms: masculinity, manliness, virility|Antonyms: femininity, weakness, submissiveness|noun
