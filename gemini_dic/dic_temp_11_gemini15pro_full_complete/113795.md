macrurous:/məˈkruːrəs/| adj.|长尾的，大尾的|Having a long tail, especially a long and prominent tail, typically in relation to crustaceans.  Relating to the Macrura, a former suborder of decapod crustaceans having a long tail, including lobsters, shrimps, and prawns.|Lobsters are macrurous crustaceans. 龙虾是长尾甲壳类动物。|The study focused on the behavior of macrurous decapods. 这项研究的重点是长尾十足目动物的行为。|long-tailed, caudate|brachyurous|adjective
