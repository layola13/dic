metrologists:/ˌmɛtrɒlədʒɪsts/
noun
计量学家
Experts in metrology, the science of measurement.
The metrologists calibrated the instruments to ensure accurate readings. 计量学家校准了仪器以确保读数准确。
The metrologists are responsible for maintaining the national standards of measurement. 计量学家负责维护国家计量标准。
Synonyms: measurement specialists, measurement scientists
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-experts in measurement")
