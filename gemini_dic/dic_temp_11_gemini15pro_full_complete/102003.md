isocyanic:/ˌaɪsoʊsaɪˈænɪk/ | adj. |异氰酸的；异氰酸酯的 | Relating to or containing the isocyanate group (-N=C=O).  Isocyanic acid is a colourless, volatile, and poisonous liquid. | Isocyanic acid reacts readily with water. 异氰酸很容易与水反应。 | Polyurethane foams are made using isocyanates. 聚氨酯泡沫是用异氰酸酯制成的。 | 近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) cyanate, isocyanate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- (Greek: "equal") + cyanic (from cyanide, ultimately from Greek kyanos, "dark blue").
