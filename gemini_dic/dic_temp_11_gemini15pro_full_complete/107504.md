lancinating:/ˈlænsɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 刺痛的，剧痛的 |  Characterized by sudden, sharp, and piercing pains, like a stab or cut. |  She felt a lancinating pain in her lower back. 她感到下背部一阵剧痛。 | synonyms: piercing, stabbing, sharp, acute, agonizing | antonyms: dull, throbbing, mild, soothing | adjective

lancinating:/ˈlænsɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  (of criticism, wit, etc.) incisive and hurtful. | His lancinating wit made him a feared opponent in debates. 他尖锐的机智使他成为辩论中令人畏惧的对手。| synonyms:  incisive, cutting, biting, trenchant, sardonic | antonyms: gentle, kind, mild, complimentary | adjective
