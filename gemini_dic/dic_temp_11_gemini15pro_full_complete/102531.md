jaded:/ˈdʒeɪdɪd/
|adj.|厌倦的，疲惫的，麻木的|Tired, bored, or lacking enthusiasm, typically after having had too much of something.|He was jaded by years of working in the same unfulfilling job.  多年来从事着同样一份没有成就感的工作，他感到厌倦了。|
|近义词:tired, bored, weary, worn out, cynical|
|反义词:enthusiastic, excited, passionate, interested, keen|
|词性:adjective|


Etymology: From the obsolete noun "jade," meaning a worn-out or worthless horse, possibly from an Old Norse word.  来自已废弃的名词 "jade"，意思是疲惫不堪或毫无价值的马，可能源自古挪威语。
