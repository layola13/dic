fifteener:/ˌfɪfˈtiːnər/
n.
十五行诗; 十五人组; 十五美元; 十五岁的人;
A poem of fifteen lines, typically having any form of rhyme scheme. Also, something consisting of fifteen parts, like a team, or a bill worth fifteen dollars. Can refer to a person who is fifteen years old.
She wrote a beautiful fifteener for the poetry competition. 她为诗歌比赛写了一首美丽的十五行诗。
The rugby fifteener took to the field.  这支十五人的橄榄球队上场了。
He paid a fifteener for the antique book. 他花了十五美元买了那本古董书。
The fifteener was excited about getting their driver's permit.  这个十五岁的孩子对拿到驾照感到兴奋。
近义词:  sonnet (for the poem form)
反义词:  (No direct antonym; depends on the meaning being used - e.g., five-dollar bill for the monetary meaning)
词性: noun
