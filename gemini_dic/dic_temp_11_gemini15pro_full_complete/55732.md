dive-bomber:/ˈdaɪv ˌbɑːmər/ (US) /ˈdaɪv ˌbɒmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|俯冲轰炸机|An aircraft designed to attack ground targets by diving steeply and releasing bombs.|The dive-bomber screamed down towards its target.|近义词：bomber, attack aircraft|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fighter aircraft" in some contexts, focusing on air-to-air combat rather than ground attack)|noun

dive-bomb:/ˈdaɪv bɑːm/ (US) /ˈdaɪv bɒm/ (UK)| v.|俯冲轰炸|To attack a ground target by diving steeply in an aircraft and releasing bombs.|The pilot dive-bombed the enemy position.|近义词：bomb, attack|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
