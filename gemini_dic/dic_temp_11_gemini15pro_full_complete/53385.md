disagreed:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːd/
verb
不同意，意见不合
failed to agree; had different opinions.
例句：They disagreed on the best course of action. 他们对最佳行动方案意见不一。

例句：He disagreed with her assessment of the situation. 他不同意她对情况的评估。

近义词：differed, clashed, argued, disputed, conflicted
反义词：agreed, concurred, consented
词性：v.
