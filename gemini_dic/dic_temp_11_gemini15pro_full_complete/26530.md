cabell:/kəˈbɛl/| n.|头发(尤指长发),马鬃,马尾|A thick, lustrous mass of hair, especially long hair; the hair of a horse's mane or tail.|Her long, dark cabell flowed down her back. 她乌黑的长发披在背上。|近义词：hair, tresses, locks|反义词:baldness|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *cabel, from Late Latin *capillus, from Latin capillus "hair," related to caput "head."
