frolicky: /ˈfrɒlɪki/ (UK) /ˈfrɑːlɪki/ (US)
adjective
嬉戏的，欢乐的
Playful and full of gaiety; merry and frisky.
The lambs were frolicky in the spring sunshine. 春天温暖的阳光下，小羊羔们欢快地嬉戏着。
The children were having a frolicky time at the park. 孩子们在公园里玩得很开心。
synonyms: playful, frisky, merry, cheerful, lively, spirited, exuberant, joyous, jolly
antonyms: serious, solemn, subdued, somber, gloomy, morose
词性: 形容词
