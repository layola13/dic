churchwise:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃwaɪz/
adv.
关于教会地，就教会而言
Relating to or concerning the church; in the context of church affairs or practices.
The matter was handled churchwise, with a meeting of the elders. 这件事是按照教会的方式处理的，长老们开会讨论了此事。
The issue was discussed churchwise.  这个问题在教会内部进行了讨论。
近义词: ecclesiastically
反义词: secularly
词性: 副词
