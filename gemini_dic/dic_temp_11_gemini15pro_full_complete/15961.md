balsamic: /bɔlˈsæmɪk/ | adj. | 香醋的，香脂的 | Relating to or containing balsam, a fragrant resinous substance.  |  The balsamic vinegar added a rich flavor to the salad.  香醋给沙拉增添了浓郁的风味。 |  fragrant, aromatic, resinous | acrid, pungent | adjective

balsamic: /bɔlˈsæmɪk/ | n. | 香醋 | A dark, intensely flavored vinegar aged in wooden barrels. | She drizzled balsamic over the strawberries. 她在草莓上淋了一些香醋。| vinegar, condiment | - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *balsamik*, from Latin *balsamicus*, from Ancient Greek *βαλσαμικός (balsamikós)*, from *βάλσαμον (bálsamon, “balsam”)*.
