conterranean:/ˌkɒntəˈreɪniən/ / ˌkɑːntəˈreɪniən/ | adj. | 地下的，隐藏的 | existing or occurring underground; hidden |  The conterranean network of tunnels was used by smugglers.  走私犯利用地下隧道网络。| subterranean, underground | aboveground | adjective


conterranean:/ˌkɒntəˈreɪniən/ / ˌkɑːntəˈreɪniən/ | n. | 地下居民 | an inhabitant of the underworld |  Legends speak of conterraneans who dwell in vast caverns.  传说中，地下居民居住在巨大的洞穴中。| subterranean dweller, underground inhabitant | surface dweller | noun
