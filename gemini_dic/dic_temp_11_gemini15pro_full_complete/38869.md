confederations: /kənˌfedəˈreɪʃənz/
n.
联盟，邦联
A group of states, nations, or other entities united for a common purpose.
The southern states formed a confederation during the Civil War.  南方各州在内战期间组成了一个邦联。
Switzerland is a confederation of 26 cantons. 瑞士是由26个州组成的联邦。
近义词: alliances, leagues, unions
反义词: divisions, separations
词性: 名词


confederation: /kənˌfedəˈreɪʃən/
n.
联盟，邦联
The act of confederating or uniting in a league.
The confederation of the tribes made them stronger.  部落的联盟使他们更加强大。
The articles of confederation were the first attempt at a unified government in the United States.  《邦联条例》是美国建立统一政府的第一次尝试。
近义词: alliance, league, union
反义词: division, separation
词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *confederatio, from Latin confoederatus past participle of confoederare "to unite in a league," from com- "together" + foederare "to make a treaty, to ally," from foedus "league, treaty."
