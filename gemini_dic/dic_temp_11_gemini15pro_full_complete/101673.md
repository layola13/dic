irrelievable:/ˌɪrɪˈliːvəbl/
adj.
无法缓解的，不可救药的
Not capable of being relieved or alleviated; incurable.
例句：He suffered from irrelievable pain. 他遭受着无法缓解的疼痛。
例句：The situation seemed irrelievable.  情况似乎不可救药。
近义词：incurable, unalleviated, relentless, unbearable
反义词：relievable, curable, alleviated
词性: 形容词

词源:  ir- (not) + relievable (capable of being relieved)  来自拉丁语 relevare "to lift up, lighten,"
