outlove:/ˌaʊtˈlʌv/
verb
超过…的爱；比…更爱
To love someone or something more than someone or something else loves them.
She outloved him by a mile. 她对他的爱比他对她的爱多得多。
outdo, surpass, excel
-
verb

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "to surpass") + "love" (verb meaning "to have strong affection for")
