nonfallaciously: /ˌnɒnfəˈleɪʃəsli/
| adv. | 无谬误地，正确地 |  In a way that is not based on a fallacy; validly and soundly. |  He argued nonfallaciously, presenting evidence to support each claim. 他进行了无谬误的论证，提供了证据来支持每一项主张。 | 近义词：validly, soundly, correctly, accurately, logically | 反义词：fallaciously, incorrectly, invalidly, unsoundly | 词性: 副词

nonfallaciously: /ˌnɒnfəˈleɪʃəsli/
| 词源 |  From "non-" (not) + "fallacious" (based on a fallacy) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Fallacious" ultimately derives from the Latin "fallere" (to deceive). | 词源: 拉丁语 | 词性: 副词
