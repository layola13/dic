negligible:/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbl/| adj.|微不足道的，可以忽略的|So small or unimportant as to be not worth considering; insignificant.|The difference in price was negligible. 价格差异微不足道。|近义词：insignificant, trivial, minor, slight, paltry|反义词：significant, substantial, considerable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *neglegibilis*, from negligere "to neglect," from nec "not" + legere "to gather, choose, read."
