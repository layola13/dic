/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒd/ | adj. | 语言的；措辞的 | Expressed in a specified style or manner of speaking or writing. | Her response was carefully languaged to avoid offense. 她的回答措辞谨慎，以免冒犯他人。 | 近义词: worded, phrased, expressed | 反义词:inarticulate, unsaid | adjective


/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒd/ | v. | 以某种风格或方式表达 | To express in a specified style or manner of speaking or writing.  | The lawyer carefully languaged the contract. 律师仔细地措辞合同。| 近义词: word, phrase, express | 反义词: misspeak, garble | verb


Etymology: From the noun "language" + "-ed" suffix.  The noun "language" comes from Old French "langage" (from Vulgar Latin *linguaticum), ultimately from Latin "lingua" (tongue).
