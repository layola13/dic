hypernyms:/ˈhaɪpərˌnɪmz/| n.|上位词，上义词|A hypernym is a word that is more general than another word. It refers to a broader category or class that includes the more specific word (hyponym). For example, "fruit" is a hypernym of "apple," "orange," and "banana."|"Fruit" is a hypernym of "apple". 水果是苹果的上位词。|Synonyms: superordinate, generic term|Antonyms: hyponym, subordinate|Noun
