derogate:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/ | v. | 贬低，诋毁 | to belittle or detract from; to disparage |  His actions derogated from his reputation as an honest man. 他的行为损害了他诚实的名声。

The article derogated the achievements of the previous government. 这篇文章贬低了前政府的成就。

| 近义词：belittle, disparage, denigrate, detract from | 反义词：praise, commend, extol | 词性：动词

derogated: /ˈdɛrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  贬低的，被诋毁的 | lessened in value, belittled |  He felt derogated by their constant criticism.  他觉得他们不断的批评贬低了他。

Her derogated status made it difficult for her to find work. 她被贬低的地位使她很难找到工作。

| 近义词：devalued, belittled, disparaged | 反义词：respected, esteemed, valued | 词性：形容词
