accreted:/əˈkriːtɪd/ | adj. |  增生的；积聚而成的 | formed by the gradual accumulation of matter | The accreted material formed a thick layer of rock. 积聚的物质形成了厚厚的岩层。| synonyms: accumulated, aggregated | antonyms: eroded, diminished | adjective
