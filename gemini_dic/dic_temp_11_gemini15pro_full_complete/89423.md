homozygotes:/ˌhəʊməˈzaɪɡoʊts/ | n. | 纯合子 | An individual having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes and so breeding true for the corresponding characteristic. |  Homozygotes for the sickle-cell gene produce abnormal hemoglobin. 纯合镰状细胞基因会产生异常血红蛋白。|  Homozygotes show less variation in their response to the drug. 纯合子对药物的反应变异较小。 | synonyms: homozygotic individual | antonyms: heterozygote | noun
