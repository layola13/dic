overpost:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊst/| v. |过度发布；发布过多内容|To post excessively, especially on social media.  To share too much information or too frequently.|She overposted on Instagram during her vacation, sharing dozens of photos every day. 她假期时在Instagram上过度发布，每天分享几十张照片。|He overposted about his new relationship and annoyed his followers. 他过度发布关于他的新恋情的内容，惹恼了他的粉丝。|近义词：overshare|反义词: underpost (not a common word)|词性:动词
