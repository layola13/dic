nonaccretion:/ˌnɒnəˈkriːʃən/
n.
无增值，无增加，无吸积
The process of not accumulating or adding to something, especially in the context of finance, astrophysics, or geology.  It refers to the absence of growth or accumulation.
The company experienced a period of nonaccretion in its earnings. 该公司经历了一段收益无增长的时期。
The nonaccretion of mass in the black hole puzzled scientists. 黑洞中质量的无增长使科学家们感到困惑。
近义词:stagnation, decline, decrease
反义词:accretion, growth, increase
词性:名词
