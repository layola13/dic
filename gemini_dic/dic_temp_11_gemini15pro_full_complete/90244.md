houseboaters:/ˈhaʊsˌboʊtərz/
n.
居住在船屋上的人；船屋居民
People who live on houseboats.
Many houseboaters enjoy the peaceful life on the water. 许多船屋居民享受在水上的宁静生活。
Houseboaters often form tight-knit communities. 船屋居民通常会形成紧密的社区。
residents, dwellers, inhabitants
