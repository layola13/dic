inscrutable:/ɪnˈskruːtəbl/| adj. | 不可测的，难以理解的，神秘莫测的 |His inscrutable face gave nothing away.  他那张波澜不惊的脸什么也看不出来。|例句：Her inscrutable smile left him wondering what she was thinking. 她神秘莫测的微笑让他想知道她在想什么。|近义词：enigmatic, mysterious, impenetrable, unfathomable|反义词：clear, obvious, transparent, perspicuous|词性:形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin inscrutabilis, from in- ‘not’ + scrutari ‘to search into’.
