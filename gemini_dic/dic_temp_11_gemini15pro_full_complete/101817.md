irritate:/ˈɪrɪteɪt/ (US), /ˈɪrɪteɪt/ (UK)| v. |激怒，使恼火；刺激（身体部位）|To make someone annoyed or a little angry; to cause a part of the body to become painful or uncomfortable.|The constant noise irritated me.  持续不断的噪音让我很烦。|  Her tight shoes irritated her feet.  她那双紧的鞋子磨脚。| synonyms: annoy, bother, vex, provoke, aggravate, exasperate | antonyms: soothe, calm, placate, mollify | verb
