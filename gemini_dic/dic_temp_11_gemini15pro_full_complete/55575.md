disturbers:/dɪˈstɜːrbərz/
n.
扰乱者，打扰者
People who disturb the peace or cause trouble.
The loud music from the neighbors are disturbers of the peace.
邻居家传来的 loud music are peace disturbers.

disruptors, troublemakers, agitators
pacifiers, appeasers
noun
