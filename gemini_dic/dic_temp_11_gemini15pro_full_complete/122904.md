molotov:/ˈmɒlətɒv/ (UK) /ˈmɑːlətɔːf/ (US)| n.|莫洛托夫鸡尾酒；燃烧瓶|A crude incendiary device typically consisting of a bottle filled with flammable liquid and with a means of ignition.  It's named after Vyacheslav Molotov, a Soviet politician.|Protesters hurled Molotov cocktails at the police. 抗议者向警察投掷燃烧瓶。|Synonyms: petrol bomb, firebomb|Antonyms: extinguisher, fire suppressant|noun
