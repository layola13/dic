epiphyllous:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 生于叶上的，叶生的 | Growing on, or attached to, the surface of a leaf. |  Example 1: Certain species of moss are epiphyllous, thriving on the moist surface of larger leaves in the rainforest.  某些种类的苔藓是叶生的，在雨林中较大叶片的潮湿表面上茁壮成长。

Example 2: The orchid is epiphyllous, anchoring itself to the broad leaves of the host tree.  这株兰花是叶生的，将自己固定在寄主树的宽阔叶片上。 | Near synonyms: epiphytic, foliicolous | Antonym: terrestrial | Word type: adjective
