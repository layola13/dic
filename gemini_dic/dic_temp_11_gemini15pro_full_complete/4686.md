alert:/əˈlɜːrt/ (US) /əˈlɜːt/ (UK)| adj. n. v.|警惕的，警报；使警觉|A watchdog is alert to strange noises. 一只看门狗对奇怪的声音很警觉。|近义词:watchful, vigilant, attentive, wary|反义词:inattentive, careless, negligent, oblivious|词性:形容词

alert:/əˈlɜːrt/ (US) /əˈlɜːt/ (UK)| n.|警报|The government issued an alert about the approaching hurricane. 政府发布了飓风逼近的警报。|近义词:warning, alarm, caution|反义词:all-clear|词性:名词

alert:/əˈlɜːrt/ (US) /əˈlɜːt/ (UK)| v.|使警觉，向…报警|The doctor alerted the patient to the risks of smoking. 医生提醒病人吸烟的风险。|近义词:warn, notify, inform, caution|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:动词

词源: From Italian *allerta* "on the watch," from all'erta "on the lookout," literally "on the height," from erta, feminine past participle of ergere "to erect," from Latin erigere "to raise up."
