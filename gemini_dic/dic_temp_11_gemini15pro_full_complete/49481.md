delusively:/dɪˈluːsɪvli/
adverb|欺骗性地；虚幻地|In a way that is based on or produces a delusion. |He was delusively optimistic about the future of the company. 他对公司的前途抱有不切实际的乐观态度。|
例句：She delusively believed she could win the lottery. 她痴心妄想地认为自己能中彩票。
例句：He clung delusively to the hope that she would return. 他痴迷地抱着她会回来的希望。
近义词：misleadingly, deceptively, falsely
反义词：realistically, truthfully, accurately
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From delusive + -ly.  Delusive comes from Late Latin dēlūsīvus (“deceitful”), from Latin dēlūdō (“deceive”). Ultimately from de- + lūdō (“play, mock”).
