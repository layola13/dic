overballasting:/ˌōvərˈbæləstɪŋ/
n.
过度压载，过量压载
The act of placing excessive ballast in a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle, resulting in reduced stability and performance.
The ship's poor performance was attributed to overballasting.  这艘船性能差的原因被归咎于过度压载。
The hot air balloon struggled to ascend due to overballasting.  由于过度压载，热气球难以升空。

近义词: overloading
反义词: underballasting
词性: noun
