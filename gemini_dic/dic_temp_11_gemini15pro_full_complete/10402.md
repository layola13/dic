apperil:/əˈpɛrəl/ | n. | 危难，危险 |A state of danger or exposure to harm. | The ship was in apperil of sinking. 这艘船处于沉没的危险之中。 | 近义词: danger, peril, jeopardy, hazard, risk | 反义词: safety, security | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *apereil, from Late Latin *appariculum, from Latin appariculum "preparation," diminutive of apparatus.  The sense shifted from "preparation" to "equipment, apparatus," then to "dangerous situation," possibly influenced by Old French peril "danger."
