loxodromism:/ˌlɒksəˈdrəʊmɪzəm/ /ˌlɑːksəˈdroʊmɪzəm/ | n. |斜航术，等角航线，斜航线|Navigation along a rhumb line; a course of constant bearing.  A loxodrome is a line on a sphere that crosses all meridians at the same angle.  It appears as a straight line on a Mercator projection.| Sailors used loxodromism to navigate across the ocean.  水手们使用斜航术来进行远洋航行。|近义词:rhumb line navigation|反义词:orthodromic navigation|词性:noun
