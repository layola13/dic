gentlemanlike:/ˈdʒɛntlmənlʌɪk/| adj.|绅士般的，有绅士风度的|Having or showing the qualities that are considered typical of a well-mannered, courteous, and considerate man.|He behaved in a very gentlemanlike manner, opening doors for her and offering his coat.|近义词：courteous, polite, chivalrous, refined, civil|反义词：rude, boorish, uncouth, vulgar|词性:形容词

词源: gentleman + like.  "Gentleman" comes from Old French "gentil homme," meaning "nobleman."  "Like" comes from Old English "lic," meaning "having the same form as; resembling."
