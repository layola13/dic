odalman:/ˈoʊdəlmən/| n.|奥达尔曼（土耳其封建时代的土地所有者）|An odalman was a freeholder, a landowning peasant, in the Ottoman feudal system.  They held their land conditionally in return for military service.|The odalman cultivated his own land and was obligated to provide military service when called upon.|近义词：freeholder, peasant, yeoman|反义词:serf|词性:noun
