class-conscious:/ˈklæs ˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /ˈklæs ˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.|阶级意识强的；具有阶级觉悟的|Aware of one's social class and its importance in society, often with a focus on the differences and inequalities between classes.|例句：The play is a class-conscious portrayal of Victorian society.  这出戏是对维多利亚时代社会阶级意识的刻画。

例句：He grew up in a very class-conscious environment. 他在一个阶级意识非常强的环境中长大。|近义词：class-aware, status-conscious|反义词：class-blind, unaware of class|词性: adjective
