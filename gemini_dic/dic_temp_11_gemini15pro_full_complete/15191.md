backbreaking:/ˈbækˌbreɪkɪŋ/| adj.| 令人筋疲力尽的，繁重的|  Physically demanding and exhausting; requiring strenuous labor.|例句： Harvesting sugarcane is backbreaking work.  收甘蔗是一项累断腰的活。|近义词： strenuous, arduous, grueling, exhausting, laborious |反义词: easy, effortless, simple |词性:形容词
Etymology:  "back" (referring to the spine) + "breaking" (causing to break or give way under pressure).  The word vividly suggests work so strenuous it feels like it could break one's back.
