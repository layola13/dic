council: /ˈkaʊnsl/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsɪl/ (UK) | n. | 委员会；理事会；地方议会 | A group of people elected or appointed to make decisions or give advice on a particular subject, or to run a local area. | The city council voted to approve the new budget. 市议会投票通过了新的预算。| The student council organized a fundraiser for the school. 学生会为学校组织了一次募捐活动。| synonyms: assembly, board, committee | antonyms: individual | noun
