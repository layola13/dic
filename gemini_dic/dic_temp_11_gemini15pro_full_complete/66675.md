exodic:/ɛkˈsɒdɪk/| adj.|外生的，向外的|Moving or directed outward; relating to or resembling an exodus.|The exodic growth of the fungus was visible under the microscope.  真菌的向外生长在显微镜下可见。|例句：The exodic migration of the population resulted in a decline in the city's economy. 人口的外迁导致了城市经济的衰退。|近义词：outward, external, efferent, centrifugal|反义词：endogenous, internal, afferent, centripetal|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *exodos*  "a going out," from *ex* "out" + *hodos* "way," related to *bainein* "to go."
