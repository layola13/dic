half-chanted:/ˌhɑːfˈtʃæntɪd/ | adj. | 半吟诵的，低声吟唱的 | Sung or spoken in a low, monotonous tone, as if chanting.  It implies a rhythmic and somewhat mystical quality to the speech or song, often suggestive of ritual or incantation. |  She offered a half-chanted prayer. 她低声吟诵了一段祈祷。| Near Synonyms: murmured, muttered, intoned, whispered, crooned | Antonyms: shouted, yelled, exclaimed | word property: adjective
