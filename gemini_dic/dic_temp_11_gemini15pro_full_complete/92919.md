identic:/aɪˈdɛntɪk/ | adj. | 相同的，同一的 | Identical means exactly the same, or very similar. | The twins were wearing  identic outfits. 这对双胞胎穿着相同的衣服。 |  The two signatures are virtually identic.  这两个签名几乎完全相同。 | synonyms: same, alike, indistinguishable, twin, duplicate | antonyms: different, dissimilar, distinct, unlike | adjective
