horticulturist: /ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. | 园艺家；园艺师 | A person who practices horticulture; a gardener, especially a professional one. |  The horticulturist grafted the apple tree with a new variety.  园艺师将苹果树嫁接了一个新品种。

The renowned horticulturist transformed the barren land into a vibrant garden.  这位著名的园艺师将贫瘠的土地变成了一个充满生机的花园。 |  gardener, landscaper, cultivator | antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From horticulture + -ist.  Horticulture comes from Latin hortus "garden" + cultura "cultivation."
