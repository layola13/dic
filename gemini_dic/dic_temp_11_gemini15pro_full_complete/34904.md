cliquish:/ˈkliːkɪʃ/ /ˈklɪkɪʃ/| adj.|小集团的，派系的|Tending to form or associate in small exclusive groups; snobbish.|例句：The school was very cliquish, and it was hard to break into any of the established groups.  这学校里小集团盛行，很难打入任何一个已经形成的团体。

例句：Her cliquish behavior alienated many of her coworkers. 她的小集团行为疏远了许多同事。|近义词：exclusive, clannish, insular|反义词：inclusive, open, welcoming|词性:adjective

cliquishness:/ˈkliːkɪʃnəs/ /ˈklɪkɪʃnɪs/| n.|小集团主义，排外性|The state or quality of being cliquish.|例句：The cliquishness of the club made it difficult for new members to feel welcome. 俱乐部的小集团主义让新成员很难感到受欢迎。

例句：There's a certain cliquishness among the upper management that makes it hard to get your ideas heard. 高层管理人员之间存在着某种小集团主义，这使得你的想法很难被听到。|近义词：exclusivity, insularity|反义词：inclusiveness, openness|词性:noun
