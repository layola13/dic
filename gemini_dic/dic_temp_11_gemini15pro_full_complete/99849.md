intermixture:/ˌɪntərˈmɪkstʃər/ | n. | 混合，混合物 | A substance made from blending different substances together. | The painter created a beautiful intermixture of colors on the canvas. 画家在画布上创造了美丽的色彩混合。 |  The rock is an intermixture of several minerals. 这块岩石是几种矿物的混合物。 | synonyms: mixture, blend, combination, amalgamation, composite, medley | antonyms: separation, segregation, division, purity | noun

intermix:/ˌɪntərˈmɪks/ | v. | 混合，掺杂 | To mix or blend together. | The different cultures intermix in this vibrant neighborhood.  不同的文化在这个充满活力的社区里融合在一起。 |  Oil and water don't readily intermix. 油和水不容易混合。 | synonyms: mix, blend, mingle, combine, merge, integrate | antonyms: separate, segregate, divide, sort | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intermixtūra, from Latin intermixtus (past participle of intermiscēre “to mix together, intermingle”), from inter- “between, among” + miscēre “to mix.”
