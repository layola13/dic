elocution:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃən/| n.| 演讲术; 口才; 雄辩;|The art of clear and expressive speech, especially of distinct pronunciation and articulation.|Her elocution was impeccable, captivating the audience with every word. 她的演讲技巧无可挑剔，每个字都吸引着观众。|近义词：articulation, pronunciation, delivery, oratory|反义词:inarticulacy, mumbled speech|noun

elocution:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃən/| n.|朗诵; 朗读;|The manner in which something is read aloud, especially with regard to clarity and expressiveness.|The teacher praised the student's clear elocution of the poem. 老师称赞了学生对这首诗清晰的朗诵。|近义词：recitation, reading, declamation|反义词:mumbling, stammering|noun
