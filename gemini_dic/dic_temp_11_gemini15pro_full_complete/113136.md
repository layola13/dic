lunistice: /ˈluːnɪstɪs/ | n. |月球静止; 月球停顿 (指月球在天空中看似停止运动的短暂时刻，尤其是在其轨道的最高点或最低点)|A lunistice is a brief moment when the Moon appears to pause in its motion across the sky, especially at the highest or lowest point in its orbit.  It's a less common term than "lunar standstill."|The astronomer observed the lunistice with great interest. 天文学家饶有兴趣地观察了月球静止的时刻。| The lunistice provided a unique opportunity for photographic documentation. 月球静止的时刻提供了一个独特的摄影记录机会。| 近义词: lunar standstill, standstill | 反义词: motion, movement | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Latin "luna" (moon) + "-stice" (standing still), akin to solstice. 源自拉丁语 "luna"（月亮）+ "-stice"（静止），类似于 solstice（至点）。
