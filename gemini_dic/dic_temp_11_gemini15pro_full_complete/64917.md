adjective
可建立的，可确立的
Capable of being established; possible to establish.
The scientist presented an establishable link between the two phenomena.  科学家提出了这两种现象之间可建立的联系。

反义词: unprovable,  undemonstrable,  refutable
词性: adjective
词源: From establish + -able.  Ultimately from Latin stabilis “firm”.
