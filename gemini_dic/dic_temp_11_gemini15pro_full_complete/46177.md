cypher:/ˈsaɪfər/ (US), /ˈsʌɪfə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|零，无价值的人或物|A person or thing of no value or importance.|例句：He felt like a cypher in the large corporation. 在这家大公司里，他觉得自己微不足道。|近义词：nobody, nonentity, zero|反义词:somebody, VIP|noun, verb
