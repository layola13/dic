dressiest:/ˈdrɛsiɪst/
adjective
最衣着的；最适合正式场合的
(superlative of 'dressy') Most suitable for formal occasions; most elegant or elaborate in style.
She wore her dressiest gown to the gala. 她穿着她最正式的礼服参加了晚会。
近义词: most formal, fanciest, most elegant
反义词: most casual, least formal, plainest
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From dressy + -est (superlative suffix).  Dressy comes from dress (n.) + -y (adjective suffix). Ultimately from Old French "dresser" meaning "to straighten, arrange."
