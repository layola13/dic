measure:/ˈmeʒər/ (US), /ˈmɛʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt. vi.|尺寸，测量，措施，程度，办法|A standard unit used for expressing a certain quantity.  An act of ascertaining the size, amount, or degree of something. measure:/ˈmeʒər/ (US), /ˈmɛʒə(r)/ (UK)| vt. vi.|测量，估量，权衡|To ascertain the size, amount, or degree of something by using an instrument or other means.
