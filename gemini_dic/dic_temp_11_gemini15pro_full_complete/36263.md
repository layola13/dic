cogware:/ˈkɒɡweə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːɡwer/ (UK)| n. |认知软件；认知计算软件；思维软件|Software designed to mimic or enhance human cognitive functions, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.|Cogware is becoming increasingly sophisticated, enabling machines to perform tasks previously thought impossible.| 近义词: cognitive computing software, AI software, thinking software | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional software" in a comparative context) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  A blend of "cognitive" and "software".  "Cognitive" comes from the Latin "cognoscere" meaning "to get to know, recognize, realize, become acquainted with".  "Software" is a combination of "soft" and "ware", with "ware" meaning "articles of merchandise".
