bakehouses:/ˈbeɪkˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
面包房，烘培坊
A place where bread and other baked goods are made and sold.
The smell of fresh bread wafted from the bakehouses.  新鲜面包的香味从面包房飘来。
The village bakehouse was a central gathering place.  村里的面包房是人们聚集的中心场所。
synonyms: bakery, bake shop
antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym; one might consider a grocery store as a loose antonym in terms of primary function)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bakhows*, from Old English *bæchus* (bakehouse), from *bacan* (to bake) + *hūs* (house).  The plural form simply adds -es.
