nonmanifestnesses:/ˌnɒnˈmænɪfɛstˌnɪsɪz/
n.
未显现的状态；未表明的状态
The state or quality of not being manifest; lack of manifestation.
His writings explored the nonmanifestnesses of the spiritual realm.  他的著作探索了精神领域的未显现状态。
Near synonyms: latency, dormancy, potentiality,  invisibility, impalpability
Antonyms:  manifestation,  visibility,  tangibility, obviousness
词性: noun
