nonsuspension:/ˌnɑːnsəˈspɛnʃən/ |n.| 不中止，不暂停 |The nonsuspension of the driver's license after the accident caused public outcry. 事故发生后驾驶执照未被吊销，引起了公众的强烈抗议。| The nonsuspension of the construction project despite the environmental concerns was controversial. 尽管存在环境问题，但建筑项目并未暂停，这引起了争议。 |近义词：continuation,  persistence|反义词：suspension, interruption|词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
suspension (noun): from Late Latin "suspensionem" meaning "a hanging up, delaying,"  from Latin "suspendere" meaning "to hang up, interrupt."
