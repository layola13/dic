gildings:/ˈɡɪldɪŋz/
n.
镀金；虚饰；金箔
A thin layer of gold applied to a surface for decoration.  The act of applying gold leaf or gold paint. Something that makes a situation seem better than it really is.
The ornate gildings on the picture frame added to its grandeur.  画框上华丽的镀金增添了它的庄严。
The politician's promises were just gildings, covering up his true intentions.  政客的承诺只是虚饰，掩盖了他的真实意图。
Synonyms: ornamentation, embellishment, decoration, veneer
Antonyms: plainness, simplicity, austerity
