necessarily:/ˌnesəˈserəli/ /ˌnesəˈsɛrəli/| adv.|必然地，不可避免地，必定地|Used to say that something is certainly true or is a certain way|例句：That's not necessarily true. 那未必是真的。

|近义词：inevitably, unavoidably, certainly, definitely|反义词:possibly, perhaps, maybe|词性:副词
