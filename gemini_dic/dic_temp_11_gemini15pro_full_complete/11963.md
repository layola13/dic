artemia:/ɑːrˈtiːmiə/| n. |卤虫；丰年虾|A small crustacean, also known as brine shrimp, that lives in saltwater lakes and is often used as food for fish and other aquatic animals. |例句：Artemia are a crucial food source for many fish larvae. 卤虫是许多鱼类幼体的关键食物来源。

例句：The researchers studied the effects of different salinity levels on artemia growth. 研究人员研究了不同盐度水平对卤虫生长的影响。|近义词：brine shrimp|反义词: None|词性: noun
