fussation:/fʌˈseɪʃən/
n.
小题大做；大惊小怪；忙乱
A state of excessive activity or concern, usually about something unimportant.
She made a great fussation about the misplaced keys. 她为钥匙放错了地方而大惊小怪。
近义词: fuss, commotion, ado, to-do
反义词: calmness, tranquility
词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "fuss" (from Middle English *fusen, of uncertain origin) + "-ation" (a suffix forming nouns of action or process).
