hobnob:/ˈhɑːbnɑːb/ (US), /ˈhɒbnɒb/ (UK)| vi.|亲切交谈；过从甚密；(与要人) 交往|To socialize, especially with someone important or famous. |例句：He hobnobbed with royalty at the garden party. 他在园游会上与皇室成员亲切交谈。

例句：She likes to hobnob with celebrities. 她喜欢与名人交往。

近义词：socialize, mingle, fraternize, associate|反义词：avoid, shun, isolate|词性：动词
