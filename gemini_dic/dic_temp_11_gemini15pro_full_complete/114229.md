mail-bombs:/ˈmeɪl bɒmz/ (US) /ˈmeɪl bɒmz/ (UK)| n.|邮件炸弹|The sending of a large volume of emails to a specific address, often with malicious intent, designed to overwhelm the recipient's email system or server.|The activist group was accused of using mail-bombs to disrupt the company's communications.  该激进组织被指控使用邮件炸弹扰乱公司的通讯。|近义词:email bomb, spam attack|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "legitimate email")|词性:名词
