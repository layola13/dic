dustier:/ˈdʌstiər/| adj.|更多灰尘的，更脏的|Covered with or full of dust, to a greater degree. |The attic was dustier than I remembered. 阁楼比我记忆中更脏了。|The old books on the shelf were dustier than ever. 书架上的旧书比以往任何时候都更脏。|近义词：dirtier, grimier, soilier|反义词：cleaner, purer|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dust + -y (suffix forming adjectives) + -er (suffix forming comparative adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English *dust.
