nonconvertibly:/ˌnɑːnkənˈvɜːrtəbli/ | adv. | 不可兑换地，非转换地 | Not capable of being converted, especially not exchangeable for another currency. | The bonds were issued nonconvertibly, meaning they couldn't be exchanged for stock.  这些债券是不可兑换发行的，意味着它们不能兑换成股票。|  The currency is nonconvertibly pegged to the US dollar. 该货币不可兑换地与美元挂钩。 | 近义词: irredeemably, unexchangeably | 反义词: convertibly, exchangeably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "non-" (not) + "convertible" (able to be converted) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Convertible" ultimately derives from the Latin "convertere," meaning "to turn around."
