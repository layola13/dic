meleagris: /ˌmɛliˈeɪɡrɪs/ | n. | 火鸡；吐绶鸡 | A genus of Galliformes, typically having wattles and, in the male, highly iridescent plumage.  It includes the wild turkey and the ocellated turkey. |  The Meleagris gallopavo is commonly known as the wild turkey.  野火鸡通常被称为吐绶鸡。| Meleagris ocellata is a more colourful species found in Central America. 眼斑吐绶鸡是一种颜色更鲜艳的物种，发现于中美洲。 | synonyms: turkey, fowl | antonyms:  N/A | noun
