fringilla:/frɪŋˈɡɪlə/ | n. | chaffinch（鸟）燕雀 | A small European finch (Fringilla coelebs) of the family Fringillidae, the male of which has a bluish-gray crown and reddish-brown breast. |例句：The fringilla perched on the branch, singing its cheerful song. 燕雀栖息在树枝上，唱着欢快的歌。

例句：The birdwatcher identified the small bird as a fringilla.  观鸟者将这只小鸟认定为燕雀。 | 近义词：finch, chaffinch | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: Latin *fringilla, from *frimīre ‘twitter, chirp’.
