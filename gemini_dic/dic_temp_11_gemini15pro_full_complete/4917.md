alignment:/əˈlaɪnmənt/| n.|排列，对准，校直；联盟，联合|An arrangement in a straight line, or in correct relative positions; a position of agreement or alliance.|The wheels need alignment.  车轮需要校准。|近义词：arrangement, positioning, adjustment, alliance, affiliation|反义词:misalignment, disagreement, opposition|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From align + -ment. Align comes from French aligner, from à and ligne "line."
