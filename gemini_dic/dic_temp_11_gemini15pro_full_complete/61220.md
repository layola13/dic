elytron:/ˈɛlɪtrɒn/ (US), /ˈɛlɪtrən/ (UK)| n.|鞘翅；覆翅|The hard, modified forewing of certain insects, especially beetles, that protects the hindwing.|The beetle raised its elytra and took flight. 甲虫抬起鞘翅，飞了起来。|Near synonyms: forewing, wing case|Antonyms: hindwing|noun

elytra:/ˈɛlɪtrə/ (US), /ˈɛlɪtriː/ (UK)| n.|鞘翅（复数）|The plural form of elytron, referring to the hard, protective forewings of beetles and certain other insects.|The elytra of the ladybug are bright red with black spots. 瓢虫的鞘翅是鲜红色的，带有黑点。|Near synonyms: forewings, wing cases|Antonyms: hindwings|noun
