nonproximities:/ˌnɒnprɒkˈsɪmɪtiz/ | n. |非邻近性，不相邻，距离; |lack of proximity; the state of being distant or separate. |The algorithm considers nonproximities between data points to identify clusters.  该算法考虑数据点之间的非邻近性来识别聚类。|  Nonproximities in the sensor readings indicated a fault in the system. 传感器读数的非邻近性表明系统存在故障。 | 近义词: distances, remoteness, separation | 反义词: proximities, closeness, nearness | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix): not; opposite of
proximity (noun): nearness in space, time, or relationship, from Latin proximitas, from proximus "nearest," superlative of prope "near."
-ity (suffix): forming nouns of quality or state
