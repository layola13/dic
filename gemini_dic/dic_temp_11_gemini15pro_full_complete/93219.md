iguanoid:/ɪˈɡwɑːnɔɪd/ | adj. n. |鬣蜥类的，像鬣蜥的；鬣蜥类动物| resembling or related to an iguana; a lizard of the iguanoid group |The iguanas and the agamas are both iguaniid lizards and the chameleons are also iguaniids, forming the iguaniid suborder Iguania. 鬣蜥和龙蜥都是鬣蜥科蜥蜴，变色龙也是鬣蜥科，构成了鬣蜥亚目。|The Galapagos land iguana is an iguaniid lizard that is endemic to the Galapagos Islands. 加拉帕戈斯陆鬣蜥是一种鬣蜥科蜥蜴，是加拉帕戈斯群岛的特有物种。|近义词: iguanian|反义词: (无明确的反义词) |词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology: From Latin Iguana (Iguana) + -oid (resembling)
