noncoincidence:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|不一致，不重合，不巧合|lack of agreement, correspondence, or coincidence.|The noncoincidence of the two events proved they were unrelated. 这两个事件的不一致证明它们之间没有关联。|近义词：discrepancy, disagreement, divergence|反义词：coincidence, agreement, conformity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + coincidence.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "coincidence" comes from the Latin "coincidere," meaning "to coincide" or "to happen at the same time."
