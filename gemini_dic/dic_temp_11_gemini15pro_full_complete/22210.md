bonair:/bəˈnɛər/| adj.|和蔼的；快活的；无忧无虑的|Having a cheerful, lively, and carefree manner; lighthearted; affable.|He had a bon air about him, always smiling and joking. 他总是面带微笑，爱开玩笑，一副无忧无虑的样子。|Synonyms: cheerful, jovial, lighthearted, carefree, debonair|Antonyms: gloomy, morose, sullen, dour|adjective

bonair:/bəˈnɛər/| n.|温和的微风|A gentle breeze.|The bon air off the ocean cooled the beachgoers.  来自海洋的微风使海滩上的人们感到凉爽。|Synonyms: breeze, zephyr|Antonyms: gale, storm|noun
