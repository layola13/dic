fungous:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/| adj.| 真菌的；似真菌的；海绵状的|Of, relating to, or resembling a fungus; spongy.|The fungous growth spread rapidly across the damp wall.  真菌在潮湿的墙上迅速蔓延。|Synonyms: fungal, mushroomy, spongy|Antonyms: nonfungal|adjective


fungus:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/| n.| 真菌；菌类；霉菌|Any of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter, including molds, yeast, mushrooms, and toadstools.|The tree was covered in a strange fungus.  这棵树上覆盖着一种奇怪的真菌。|Synonyms: mold, mildew, yeast, mushroom, toadstool|Antonyms: none|noun
