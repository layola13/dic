anise:/ˈænɪs;| n.|茴香，茴芹，八角茴香|A Mediterranean plant (Pimpinella anisum) of the parsley family, cultivated for its aromatic seedlike fruits.|I love the licorice-like flavor of anise. 我喜欢茴香的甘草味。|Synonyms: aniseed, fennel|Antonyms: None|noun

aniseed:/ˈænɪsiːd;| n.|茴香籽|The seed of the anise plant, used as a flavoring.|Aniseed is often used in baking. 茴香籽经常用于烘焙。|Synonyms: anise|Antonyms: None|noun
