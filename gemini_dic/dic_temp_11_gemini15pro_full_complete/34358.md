classicalisms:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəmz/
n.
古典主义；古典风格；古典语汇
A classical style or form of expression, especially in art, literature, or music.  A classical expression or construction.
His writing is full of classicalisms. 他的写作充满了古典主义风格。
The architect’s designs are notable for their classicalisms.  这位建筑师的设计以其古典主义风格而著称。
synonyms: classicism, traditionalism, formalism
antonyms: modernism, avant-garde, experimentalism
