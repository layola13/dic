formalizing:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzɪŋ/
v.
使正式化；正式规定；系统化
Making something official or definite, establishing something according to rules or procedures.
The two countries are formalizing their trade agreement. 这两个国家正在将其贸易协定正式化。
The company is formalizing its dress code policy. 公司正在将其着装规范政策正式化。
Synonyms: codifying, systematizing, regularizing, structuring
Antonyms: informalizing, relaxing, loosening
verb
