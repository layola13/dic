nonincarnated:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkɑːrneɪtɪd/
adjective
未化身的，非肉体的
Not having a physical body; not embodied in flesh.
The nonincarnated spirit is believed to wander the earth.  未化身的灵魂被认为在地球上游荡。
Angels are often depicted as nonincarnated beings. 天使通常被描绘成非肉体的存在。
synonym: disembodied, spiritual, ethereal, incorporeal
antonym: incarnate, embodied, physical, corporeal
词性: adjective
