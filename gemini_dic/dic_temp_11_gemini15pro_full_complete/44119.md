crisply:/ˈkrɪsplɪ/| adv.|干脆地，清晰地，爽快地|In a crisp and concise manner; quickly and efficiently without any hesitation or unnecessary detail.|She answered crisply and to the point. 她回答得干脆利落。|近义词：sharply, briskly, curtly, succinctly|反义词：vaguely, hesitantly, indistinctly|词性:副词
Etymology: From crisp + -ly.  Crisp itself comes from Old English "crisp" meaning "curly," likely of imitative origin.  The sense of "brittle" developed later, and the adverbial form reflects these qualities of quickness and sharpness.
