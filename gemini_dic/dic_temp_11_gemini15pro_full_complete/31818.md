checkup:/ˈtʃɛkʌp/ (US), /ˈtʃekʌp/ (UK)| n.|检查；体检|A thorough examination, especially a medical one to detect any problems.|I had my annual checkup last week. 我上周做了年度体检。|Synonyms: examination, inspection, review|Antonyms: neglect, disregard|noun

checkup:/ˈtʃɛkʌp/ (US), /ˈtʃekʌp/ (UK)| n.|核实；查证|An act of verifying or confirming something.|The police conducted a checkup on the suspect's alibi.  警方对嫌疑人的不在场证明进行了核实。|Synonyms: verification, confirmation, audit|Antonyms:  None (This meaning doesn't readily lend itself to an antonym)|noun
