optics:/ˈɒptɪks/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪks/ (US)| n.|光学|The scientific study of light and vision, including its physical properties, behavior, and interactions with matter.  It encompasses the study of how light is produced, transmitted, detected, and manipulated.|The new telescope uses advanced optics. 这台新的望远镜使用了先进的光学系统。|Synonyms: optical science, photology|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
