achier: /əˈtʃiːər/
noun
获得者，完成者，成功者
One who achieves something, especially a high level of success.
Example: She is a high achiever in her field. 她在她所在的领域是一位成就非凡的人。

Example: He was an achiever who always strived for excellence. 他是一个追求卓越的成功人士。


近义词: winner, victor, champion, successful person, high-flyer
反义词: loser, failure, underachiever
词性: n.
