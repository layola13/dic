hardballing:/ˈhɑːrdbɔːlɪŋ/
n.|强硬手段，强硬策略|Employing ruthless, uncompromising tactics in negotiations or business dealings.  Using aggressive and sometimes unethical methods to achieve a desired outcome.|The company president was known for his hardballing tactics during acquisitions.|近义词：aggressive negotiation, tough bargaining, no-holds-barred tactics|反义词：compromising, conciliatory, cooperative|词性:名词
