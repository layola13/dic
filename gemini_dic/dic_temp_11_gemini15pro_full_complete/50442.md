deprecator:/ˈdɛprəkeɪtər/| n.|反对者，弃用者|Someone who expresses disapproval of something or someone.  Someone who discourages the use of something (typically in software development, referring to a feature or practice that is no longer recommended).|The deprecators of the new policy voiced their concerns at the meeting. 那些反对新政策的人在会议上表达了他们的担忧。|The library included a deprecator to warn developers about using outdated functions.  这个库包含一个弃用器，用于警告开发人员不要使用过时的函数。|criticizer, objector, opponent|supporter, advocate|noun
