hyposulphuric:/ˌhaɪpoʊsʌlˈfjʊərɪk/
adjective
次硫酸的；连二硫酸的
relating to or containing hyposulphuric acid (also called dithionic acid), which has the formula H2S2O6.
Hyposulphuric acid is a strong acid formed by the oxidation of sulfur dioxide. 次硫酸是一种强酸，由二氧化硫氧化而成。
Dithionates are salts of hyposulphuric acid. 连二硫酸盐是次硫酸的盐。
Synonym: dithionic
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could consider sulfuric as a related but different acid)
词性: adjective
