omphalomesenteric:/ˌɒmfəloʊˌmɛzənˈtɛrɪk/ |adj.| 脐肠系的；卵黄肠系的 |Relating to the omphalomesenteric duct, a duct connecting the yolk sac to the midgut embryo in early development.| The omphalomesenteric duct usually disappears during fetal development. 卵黄肠管通常在胎儿发育过程中消失。 |Synonym: vitelline,  yolk stalk| Antonym: N/A |adjective

omphalomesenteric:/ˌɒmfəloʊˌmɛzənˈtɛrɪk/ |n.| 脐肠系 |The omphalomesenteric duct or structures derived from it. | An omphalomesenteric cyst is a rare type of cyst that develops from remnants of the omphalomesenteric duct. 脐肠系囊肿是一种罕见的囊肿，由脐肠系管残留物发育而来。|Synonym: vitelline duct, yolk stalk| Antonym: N/A |noun
