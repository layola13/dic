mouselike:/ˈmaʊslaɪk/| adj.|像老鼠的；胆小的，畏缩的|Resembling a mouse in appearance, behavior, or characteristics, often implying timidity or quietness.|The child had a mouselike, timid demeanor.  这孩子有一种像老鼠一样胆怯的举止。|Synonyms: timid, shy, meek, mousy, retiring|Antonyms: bold, brave, confident, assertive|adjective
