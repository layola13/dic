neutral:/ˈnjuːtrəl/ | adj. n. |中立的，中性的；中立者，空挡 |Not supporting or helping either side in a conflict, disagreement, etc.;  neither acid nor alkaline; having no colour | Switzerland remained neutral during World War II. 瑞士在二战期间保持中立。| Synonyms: impartial, unbiased, objective | Antonyms: biased, prejudiced, partial | adjective

neutral:/ˈnjuːtrəl/ | n. | 中立者，空挡 | A neutral person or thing, especially a country or person not taking part in a war; a position of gears in which no power is transmitted to the wheels | He put the car in neutral. 他把车挂到空挡。| Synonyms: noncombatant, bystander | Antonyms: combatant, participant | noun
