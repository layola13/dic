leaderboards:/ˈliːdərbɔːrdz/
n.
排行榜，积分榜
A list that shows the ranking of competitors in a game, competition, or other activity, based on their scores or performance.
He checked the leaderboards to see his position in the tournament. 他查看了排行榜，看看自己在锦标赛中的位置。
The team climbed to the top of the leaderboards after a string of victories.  在一系列胜利之后，该队攀升至排行榜榜首。
synonyms: ranking, standings, scoreboard, ladder
antonyms: N/A
noun
