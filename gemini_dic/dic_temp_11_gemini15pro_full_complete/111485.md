loadstar:/ˈloʊdstɑːr/ | n. | 指导原则；指路明灯；北极星 |  A star that is used to guide the course of a ship, especially Polaris. Something that serves as a guide or inspiration. |  The principles of equality and justice serve as our loadstar. 平等和正义的原则是我们的指路明灯。 |  guiding star, polestar, guiding light, beacon | antonym: Not applicable (lack of guidance doesn't have a single antonym) | noun
