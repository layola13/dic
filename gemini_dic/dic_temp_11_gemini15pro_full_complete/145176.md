overattentive: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 过于关心的，过分殷勤的 | Paying too much attention to someone or something; excessively attentive. |  He was so overattentive to his guests that they felt smothered. 他对客人过于殷勤，让客人感到窒息。 |  例句：The overattentive waiter kept interrupting our conversation.  过于殷勤的服务员不断打断我们的谈话。| 近义词：fussy, solicitous, officious | 反义词：inattentive, negligent, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "attentive" (from Latin "attendere" - to stretch toward, pay attention to)
