garget:/ˈɡɑːrɡɪt/ | n. | 牛炎症, (植物的)瘿, 刺激物 | An inflammation of the udder in cows or other mammals, especially after calving. Also, a growth or swelling on a plant, or something that irritates or provokes. | The cow was suffering from garget after giving birth. 这头母牛产犊后患上了乳房炎。 |  近义词: inflammation, swelling, tumor (for plant garget), irritant, provocation | 反义词:  soother, balm (for irritant meaning) | 词性: noun


garget:/ˈɡɑːrɡɪt/ | n. |  (废语) 装饰品, 小玩意儿 | (Archaic) A small ornament or gadget. | She wore a pretty garget on her hat. 她在帽子上戴了一个漂亮的小饰品。| 近义词: trinket, ornament, gadget | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
