handicraft: /ˈhændɪkrɑːft/ (US) /ˈhændɪkrɑːft/ (UK)| n.| 手工艺；手工艺品| An activity involving making something in a skillful way using your hands.  A decorative or useful object made by hand. |  She sells her handicrafts at the local market. 她在当地市场出售她的手工艺品。| synonyms: craft, handiwork, artwork, artisanry | antonyms: mass production, factory-made | noun
