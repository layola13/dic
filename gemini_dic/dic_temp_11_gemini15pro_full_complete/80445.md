goners:/ˈɡɒnəz/ (British), /ˈɡɑːnərz/ (American)| n. |注定要失败的人；注定要完蛋的人；死定了的人|People who are doomed to fail, die, or suffer some other unpleasant fate.  Usually used in the plural.|例句：With the factory closing down, we're all goners. 工厂倒闭了，我们都完蛋了。

例句：The wounded soldiers were left for goners. 受伤的士兵被遗弃等死。

近义词：doomed, finished, lost, dead men walking|反义词: safe, secure, thriving|词性:名词
