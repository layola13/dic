nonflagrantly:/ˌnɒnˈflæɡrəntli/
adverb
不明显地，不招摇地
in a way that is not flagrant or blatant.
The rules were nonflagrantly broken, so the punishment was minimal.  规则并没有被公然违反，所以惩罚很轻。
He nonflagrantly cheated on the test by using his phone under the table. 他偷偷摸摸地作弊，在桌子下面用手机。
Synonyms: subtly, discreetly, unobtrusively, covertly
Antonyms: flagrantly, blatantly, obviously, conspicuously
词性: 副词
