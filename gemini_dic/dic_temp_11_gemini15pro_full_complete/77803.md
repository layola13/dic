generalizes:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪz/
verb
概括，归纳，推广
Forms a general statement or concept based on particular instances.
This theory generalizes to all mammals.  这个理论可以推广到所有哺乳动物。
The findings of the study can be generalized to the wider population. 这项研究的结果可以推广到更广泛的人群。
generalise (British English)
specialize, particularize
v.
