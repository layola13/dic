emulsifiers:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪərz/ | n. | 乳化剂 | Substances that stabilize mixtures of liquids that would normally separate, like oil and water, by creating an emulsion. |  Mayonnaise contains emulsifiers to prevent the oil and vinegar from separating. 蛋黄酱含有乳化剂，以防止油和醋分离。| synonyms: stabilizers, surfactants | antonyms: demulsifiers | noun


emulsifier:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪər/ | n. | 乳化剂 | A substance that stabilizes mixtures of liquids that would normally separate, like oil and water, by creating an emulsion. | Lecithin is a common emulsifier used in food production. 卵磷脂是食品生产中常用的乳化剂。| synonyms: stabilizer, surfactant | antonyms: demulsifier | noun
