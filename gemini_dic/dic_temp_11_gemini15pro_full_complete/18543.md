beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/ /ˌbenəˈfɪʃieri/| n.|受益人，受惠者|A person who derives advantage from something, especially a trust, will, or life insurance policy.|The beneficiaries of the will were his wife and children. 遗嘱的受益人是他的妻子和孩子。|synonyms: recipient, heir, inheritor|antonyms: benefactor, donor|noun
