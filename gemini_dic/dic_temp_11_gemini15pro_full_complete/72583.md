fluxionary:/ˈflʌkʃəˌnɛri/
adjective
流动的；易变的；变化不定的
Subject to constant change or fluctuation.  Characterized by flux.
例句：The fluxionary nature of the stock market makes it a risky investment. 股票市场的波动性使其成为一项 risky 的投资。

例句：The political landscape is currently fluxionary, with alliances shifting constantly. 当前的政治格局变化不定，联盟不断变化。
近义词：changeable, volatile, unstable, fluid, dynamic
反义词：stable, constant, fixed, static
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *fluxioniarius, from Latin fluxio (“a flowing”), from fluere (“to flow”).
