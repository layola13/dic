niller:/ˈnɪlə(r)/ | n. | 无价值的人或物; 零分;  |A person or thing of no value or importance; a zero score. | He called the painting a niller, claiming it was worthless. 他称这幅画毫无价值，声称它一文不值。| 近义词: nobody, nonentity, zero, dud | 反义词: expert, professional, success, triumph | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely derived from "nil," meaning zero or nothing, with the suffix "-er" added to create a noun referring to a person or thing associated with nothingness or worthlessness.
