methodologically:/ˌmɛθədəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb|方法论地；方法上|In a way that relates to the system of methods used in a particular area of study or activity. |The research was methodologically flawed.  这项研究在方法论上存在缺陷。|Synonym: systematically, rigorously|Antonym: haphazardly, randomly
词性: 副词
