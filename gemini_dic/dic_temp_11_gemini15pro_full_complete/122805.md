ETYMOLOGY-> From Middle English *moldinge, from Anglo-Norman mulder, moulder (“to mold”), and Old French moillier, moldre, mouldre (“to grind, mill, mold”), from Latin molere (“to grind, mill”), from Proto-Italic *molēre, from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, crush”).
