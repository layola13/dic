gardant:/ˈɡɑːrdənt/
adjective
警戒的，注意的；（纹章）直立守卫状的
(especially of an animal in heraldry) represented full-faced, vigilant and watchful.  Also, watchful, alert.
The crest shows a lion gardant holding a sword.  徽章上是一头直立守卫状的狮子，握着一把剑。
近义词:watchful, vigilant, alert
反义词:inattentive, negligent, oblivious
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Old French *gardant (Modern French gardant), present participle of garder "to guard," from Frankish *wardōn, from Proto-Germanic *wardōną "to watch," from *warduz "guard."  Cognate with Old High German wartēn "to watch," Old English weardian "to watch, guard."
