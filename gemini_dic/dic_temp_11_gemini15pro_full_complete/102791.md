jar's:/dʒɑːrz/
noun
罐子的；广口瓶的
Possessive form of "jar," indicating ownership or belonging.  Relates to a jar or jars.
The jar's lid is rusty.  这个罐子的盖子生锈了。
My grandma's pantry is full of jars' of homemade jam. 我祖母的食品储藏室里装满了自制果酱的罐子。
Synonyms: container's, bottle's, vessel's
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a possessive form.  Could consider concepts like emptiness or lack of containment.)
词性: 名词所有格
