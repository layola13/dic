conveyed:/kənˈveɪd/ | v. |传达，表达，运送|  The letter conveyed her sadness. 这封信表达了她的悲伤. | Example: His speech conveyed a message of hope. 他的演讲传达了希望的信息。

Example: The train conveyed passengers from London to Paris. 火车将乘客从伦敦运送到巴黎。 |Synonyms:  transmit, communicate, transport, carry | Antonyms: withhold, suppress, retain, obstruct | verb

convey:/kənˈveɪ/ | v. |传达，表达，运送| To communicate or make known (ideas, feelings, etc.). To transport or carry from one place to another. | Etymology: From Old French *convoieier, from convoier "to escort, accompany," from Late Latin conviare "to accompany on the way," from Latin com- "with" + via "way."| verb
