gruff:/ɡrʌf/| adj.|粗暴的；生硬的；（声音）粗哑的|Having a rough or low voice, and seeming rather unfriendly or bad-tempered.|He answered me in a gruff voice. 他用粗哑的声音回答我。|Synonyms: abrupt, curt, surly, brusque, rough|Antonyms: gentle, kind, polite, friendly|adjective
