forks:/fɔːrks/ /fɔːrks/| n.|叉子；岔路；分叉；餐叉|A fork is a tool with two or more prongs used for lifting food to the mouth or for digging.  A fork can also refer to a place where a road or path divides into two or more branches.|She ate her salad with a fork. 她用叉子吃沙拉。|The road forks just ahead.  路就在前面分岔了。|synonyms: utensil, cutlery, prong, branch, divide|antonyms: join, merge|noun

forks:/fɔːrks/ /fɔːrks/| v.|分叉；叉起；使成叉状|To divide into two or more branches; to lift or pick up with a fork.|The river forks at this point. 河流在此处分叉。|He forked the spaghetti onto his plate. 他用叉子把意大利面倒在盘子里。|synonyms: branch, divide, spear|antonyms: join, merge|verb
