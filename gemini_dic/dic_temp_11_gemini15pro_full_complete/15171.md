back-yonder:/ˌbæk ˈjɑːndər/ | adj. | 遥远的，在那边的 |  Located far away, especially in a rural or remote area; over there (often used in Southern US dialects). |  He pointed towards the back-yonder hills. 他指向远处那边的小山。 | Example: My grandparents live way back yonder in the countryside. 我的祖父母住在很偏远的乡下。| Synonym: remote, distant, far-off, yonder | Antonym: near, nearby, close | Adjective
