outcaught: /ˌaʊtˈkɔːt/
verb
胜过，超过（在接球方面）
To surpass or excel someone in catching, especially in baseball or cricket.
The center fielder outcaught his teammate, making a spectacular diving catch.  中外野手接球胜过他的队友，完成了一次精彩的飞身接球。
The wicket-keeper outcaught the batsman with a brilliant one-handed catch.  守门员以精彩的单手接球胜过击球手。
近义词: outfielded, surpassed
