oriole:/ˈɔːriˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈɔːriɔːl/ (UK)| n.|黄鹂，金莺|A brightly colored songbird of the family Oriolidae, found in Europe, Asia, and Africa, or a similar but unrelated American blackbird.|例句：The oriole's bright orange plumage stood out against the green leaves.  黄鹂鲜艳的橙色羽毛在绿叶中格外醒目。

例句：We heard the melodious song of an oriole in the woods. 我们在树林里听到了黄鹂悦耳的歌声。|近义词：songbird, blackbird (for American orioles)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
