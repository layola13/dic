framing:/ˈfreɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 框架; 制定; 构成; 装裱 | the act of framing something; the way in which something is framed |  The framing of the picture made it look even more beautiful. 这幅画的装裱使它看起来更美了. | synonyms: construction, composition, formulation | antonyms: dismantling, destruction | noun

framing:/ˈfreɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 给…装框; 设计; 制定; 陷害; 表达 | to put a frame around something; to create or devise something; to express something in a particular way | The police were accused of framing an innocent man. 警察被指控陷害一个无辜的人。| synonyms: formulate, devise, compose, enclose, incriminate | antonyms: exonerate, free | verb
