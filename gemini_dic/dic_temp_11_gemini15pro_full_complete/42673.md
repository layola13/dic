counterstyle:/ˈkaʊntərsaɪl/| n.|对照风格，相反的风格|A style that contrasts with another, often deliberately so. |例句：The architect chose a minimalist counterstyle to the ornate Victorian architecture of the surrounding buildings.  建筑师选择了极简主义风格，与周围建筑华丽的维多利亚式建筑形成对比。

例句：Her casual counterstyle to his formal attire made them an interesting couple.  她休闲的风格与他正式的服装形成对比，使他们成为一对有趣的夫妇。|近义词：contrast, antithesis, opposite|反义词: conformity, similarity|词性:名词
