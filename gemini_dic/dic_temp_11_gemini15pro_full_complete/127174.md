naturalization:/ˌnætʃrəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 入籍，归化；同化；移植 | the process by which a foreign citizen becomes a citizen of a new country |  Example: She received her naturalization papers yesterday. 她昨天收到了她的入籍文件。

Example: The naturalization process can take several years.  归化过程可能需要几年时间。 | Synonyms: citizenship, incorporation, assimilation | Antonyms: expatriation, denationalization | noun
