heteronuclear:/ˌhɛtərəʊˈnjuːklɪər/ /ˌhɛtəroʊˈnuːkliər/| adj.|异核的|Containing atoms of different elements, especially applied to molecules or compounds.|例句：Heteronuclear diatomic molecules like HCl have a dipole moment.  异核双原子分子如 HCl 具有偶极矩。

例句：NMR spectroscopy can distinguish between homonuclear and heteronuclear coupling. 核磁共振波谱可以区分同核和异核耦合。|近义词：multinuclear|反义词：homonuclear|词性：形容词
