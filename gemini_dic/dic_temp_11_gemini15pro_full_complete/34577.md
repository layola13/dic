clearing-house:/ˈklɪərɪŋ haʊs/| n.|清算所；交换中心；信息交流中心|A central agency that settles accounts between banks or organizations; a place where information is gathered and distributed.|The clearing-house processed millions of transactions daily. 这家清算所每天处理数百万笔交易。|Synonyms: exchange, center, hub|Antonyms: N/A|noun

clearing-house:/ˈklɪərɪŋ haʊs/| n.|票据交换所|A central agency where banks exchange checks and drafts and settle balances.|The London clearing-house manages transactions between major banks. 伦敦票据交换所管理各大银行之间的交易。|Synonyms: exchange, clearing center|Antonyms: N/A|noun
