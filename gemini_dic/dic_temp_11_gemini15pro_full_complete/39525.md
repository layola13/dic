conquering:/ˈkɒŋkərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkərɪŋ/ (US)| v.|征服; 克服; 战胜|Present participle of conquer.  The act of overcoming and taking control of (a place or people) by military force. Also, the act of overcoming a feeling or condition.|Alexander the Great set about conquering the known world. 亚历山大大帝开始征服已知的世界。

He finally succeeded in conquering his fear of heights. 他终于克服了恐高症。|近义词：defeating, overcoming, subduing, vanquishing|反义词：surrendering, yielding, submitting|词性: verb (present participle)
