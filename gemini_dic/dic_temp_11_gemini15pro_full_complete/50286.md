depictors:/dɪˈpɪktərz/| n. |描绘者，描述者|People or things that depict something; those who represent or portray something visually or verbally.|The cave paintings are the work of skilled depictors of animals and daily life.  洞穴壁画是熟练的动物和日常生活描绘者的作品。|近义词：portrayers, illustrators, representers|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "depict" + "-or" (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or doer of the action).  "Depict" comes from the Latin "depingere" (to paint, portray), from "de-" (down, completely) + "pingere" (to paint).
