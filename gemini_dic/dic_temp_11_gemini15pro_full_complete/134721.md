nonmaturest:/ˌnɒn.məˈtʊr.ɪst/ | adj. | 最不成熟的 |  This is a non-standard word formed by adding the prefix "non-" to "maturest," the superlative form of "mature." It means "least mature" or "most immature."  While grammatically understandable, it's rarely used and considered awkward. "Most immature" is generally preferred. |  His nonmaturest behavior embarrassed everyone. 他极不成熟的行为让每个人都感到尴尬。|  The nonmaturest decision he made was to quit his job without notice. 他做的最不成熟的决定是不通知就辞职。| 近义词: most immature, least mature, childish, infantile | 反义词: maturest, most mature, adult, sophisticated | 词性: 形容词
