globelike:/ˈɡloʊblaɪk/
adjective
球状的；地球仪似的
Resembling a globe; spherical.
The artist created a globelike sculpture.  艺术家创作了一个球状的雕塑。
The hot air balloon was globelike in shape.  热气球的形状像球形。
近义词: spherical, globular, round, orb-like
反义词: flat, planar
