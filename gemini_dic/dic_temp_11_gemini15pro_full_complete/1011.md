acaulous:/ˈeɪkɔːləs/| adj.|无茎的，贴地生的|Lacking a visible stem above ground; stemless.  Appearing to rise directly from the ground or from a very short stem.|The acaulous dandelion hugged the earth, its bright yellow flower a stark contrast to the bare ground.  贴地生长的蒲公英紧贴着地面，其鲜黄色的花朵与光秃秃的地面形成鲜明对比。|The primrose displayed its delicate blooms on an acaulous stem, barely rising above the surrounding foliage. 报春花在无茎的茎上展示其娇嫩的花朵，几乎没有高出周围的叶子。|近义词：stemless, sessile|反义词：caulescent, stemmed|词性: adjective
