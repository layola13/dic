agar:/ˈeɪɡɑːr/ (US) /ˈeɪɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 琼脂，洋菜|A gelatinous substance obtained from certain red seaweeds and used in biological culture media and as a thickener in foods.|例句：The bacteria were cultured on agar plates. 细菌在琼脂平板上培养。|近义词：gelatin, agarose|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
