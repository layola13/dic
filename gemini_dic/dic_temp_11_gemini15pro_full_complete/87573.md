heterophyllous:/ˌhetərəˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 异形叶的 | Having different kinds of leaves on the same plant.  | Example 1:  Many aquatic plants are heterophyllous, with submerged leaves being different in shape from floating or aerial leaves. 许多水生植物是异形叶的，其沉水叶的形状与漂浮叶或气生叶不同。

Example 2: The heterophyllous nature of the ivy allows it to adapt to different light conditions. 常春藤的异形叶特性使其能够适应不同的光照条件。 | Synonym:  polymorphic, variable | Antonym: homophyllous, uniform | adjective
