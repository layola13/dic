hybridises:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
杂交，使杂交
to breed or cause to breed with a member of a different species or variety.
The gardener hybridised the two varieties of rose. 园丁将两种玫瑰杂交。
近义词:crossbreed, interbreed, cross-fertilize
反义词:inbreed
词性:v.
