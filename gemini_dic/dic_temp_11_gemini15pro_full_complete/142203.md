ornithichnite:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɪknaɪt/ | n. | 鸟迹，鸟足迹 | A fossilized footprint made by a bird. |  Example 1: The paleontologist discovered an ornithichnite in the sandstone. 古生物学家在砂岩中发现了一个鸟足迹。
