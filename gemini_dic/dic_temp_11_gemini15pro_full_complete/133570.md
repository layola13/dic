nonflagrancies: /ˌnɒnˈflæɡrənsiz/ | n. | 无罪行为；不显著的行为 |  The absence of flagrant offenses or crimes; instances of not being flagrant. It refers to a state or collection of instances where something is not openly outrageous or scandalous.  | The lawyer pointed to the nonflagrancies in the case as evidence of his client's innocence. 律师指出案件中没有明显的犯罪行为，以此作为其当事人无罪的证据。

The recent audit revealed several nonflagrancies in the company's accounting practices, suggesting a need for stricter oversight. 最近的审计揭示了公司会计实践中一些不明显的问题，表明需要更严格的监督。 | 近义词: innocence, blamelessness, unimpeachability | 反义词: flagrancies, offenses, crimes | 词性: noun
