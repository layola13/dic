gregariousnesses:/ˌɡrɛɡˈɛəriəsnəsɪz/
n.
群居性，合群性
The state or quality of being gregarious; sociability.
Her gregariousness made her a natural leader. 她的合群性使她成为天生的领导者。
gregariousness:/ˌɡrɛɡˈɛəriəsnəsɪz/
n.
群居性，合群性
The tendency to associate with others of one's kind.
The gregariousness of dolphins is well documented. 海豚的群居性是有据可查的。


近义词:sociability, conviviality, affability
反义词:unsociability, aloofness, introversion
词性:noun
