inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/| n.|抑制剂，抑制物|A substance that slows down or prevents a particular chemical reaction or other process.|The drug acts as an inhibitor, preventing the virus from replicating.  这种药物充当抑制剂，阻止病毒复制。|synonyms: suppressant, retarder, blocker|antonyms: catalyst, accelerator, stimulant|noun

inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/| n.|阻滞剂|A substance that reduces or suppresses the activity of another substance, such as an enzyme.|This inhibitor prevents the enzyme from breaking down the protein.  这种阻滞剂阻止酶分解蛋白质。|synonyms: repressor, antagonist|antonyms: activator, agonist|noun
