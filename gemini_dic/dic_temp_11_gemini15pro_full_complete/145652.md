overcontracting:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˌtræktɪŋ/
| n. |过度收缩；过度劳累|The act of contracting too much, either physically (as in muscles) or figuratively (as in taking on too many commitments).|Excessive shortening or tightening of a muscle or muscle group, often due to overuse or strain.  Figuratively, it can refer to taking on too many responsibilities or commitments.|
例句：
The overcontracting of his calf muscles led to painful cramps. 他的小腿肌肉过度收缩导致了痛苦的抽筋。

She was suffering from overcontracting after taking on three new projects at work.  她在工作中接手了三个新项目后，感到过度劳累。

|近义词：overexertion, overworking, straining, overcommitment|反义词:relaxation, undercommitment|词性:名词
