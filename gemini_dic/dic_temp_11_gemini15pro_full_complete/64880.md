essay:/ˈeseɪ/ (US), /ˈɛseɪ/ (UK)| n.|文章；短文；散文；尝试；企图|a short piece of writing on a particular subject.  A short literary composition on a single subject, usually presenting the personal view of the author.|She wrote an essay on the causes of the French Revolution. 她写了一篇关于法国大革命起因的文章。|synonym: article, composition, paper, thesis|antonym:  none (Essays don't have a direct opposite, but one might consider a "speech" or "presentation" as a contrasting form of expressing ideas.)|noun
