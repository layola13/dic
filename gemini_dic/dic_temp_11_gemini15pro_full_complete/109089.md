legatura:/leɡəˈtʊərə/ /leɡəˈtʃʊrə/
n.|连奏；连音；连字|A smooth, continuous transition between notes.  In printing, a group of two or more letters joined together as a single glyph.|The violinist played the passage with a beautiful legatura.|Synonyms: slur, tie, ligature|Antonyms: staccato|Noun

legatura:/leɡəˈtʊərə/ /leɡəˈtʃʊrə/
n.|装订；书籍装帧|The binding of a book, especially a fine or elaborate binding.|This antique book has a beautiful leather legatura.|Synonyms: binding, cover|Antonyms: unbound|Noun
