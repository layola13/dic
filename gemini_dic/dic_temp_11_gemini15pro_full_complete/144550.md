outvoyaging:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪɪdʒɪŋ/
|v.|超越航行，胜过航行|Surpassing or exceeding in voyaging; traveling farther or more extensively than.|例句： Magellan outvoyaged all other explorers of his time. 麦哲伦的航行超过了他那个时代的所有其他探险家。|例句：Their ambition is to outvoyaging Columbus. 他们的雄心壮志是超越哥伦布的航行。|近义词：outsailing, outtraveling, exploring further|反义词：staying home, remaining, lingering|词性：动词
