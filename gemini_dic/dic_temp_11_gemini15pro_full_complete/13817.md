audition:/ɔːˈdɪʃən/ | n. v. |试镜，试音，面试；进行试镜，试音|A short performance given by an actor, singer, musician, dancer, etc., so that a director or other person can decide if they are suitable to act in a play, sing in a concert, etc.|She had an audition for a part in the play. 她参加了这部戏的一个角色的试镜。|近义词:tryout, test, screening|反义词:None|Noun
