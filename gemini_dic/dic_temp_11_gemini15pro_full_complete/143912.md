outpreached:/ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃt/
verb
胜过…的讲道；讲道比…好
to surpass in preaching; to preach more effectively or eloquently than.
The young minister outpreached the older, more experienced one.  这位年轻的牧师的讲道胜过了那位年长、更有经验的牧师。
outdo, surpass, excel
be outdone by, be inferior to
v.
