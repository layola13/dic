Mississippi: /ˌmɪsɪˈsɪpi/ | n. | 密西西比州；密西西比河 |  A state in the southern United States; a major North American river. | Example: The Mississippi River is the longest river in the United States. 密西西比河是美国最长的河流。 | Example: Jackson is the capital of Mississippi. 杰克逊是密西西比州的首府。 | 近义词: (For the river) waterway, river  (For the state) state | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


Etymology: From the Ojibwe word "misi-ziibi" meaning "great river."
