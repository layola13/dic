indomable:/ɪnˈdɒməbəl/ /ɪnˈdɑːməbəl/| adj.|不屈不挠的，无法征服的|Untamable, unconquerable; not easily discouraged or subdued.  Resolute and unwavering.|例句：She showed an indomitable spirit in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中展现了不屈不挠的精神。|例句：The indomitable will of the people eventually led to their freedom. 人民不屈不挠的意志最终导致了他们的自由。|近义词：unyielding, resolute, invincible, tenacious, persistent|反义词：submissive, yielding, weak, docile|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indomabilis, from in- "not" + domabilis "tamable," from Latin domare "to tame, subdue."
