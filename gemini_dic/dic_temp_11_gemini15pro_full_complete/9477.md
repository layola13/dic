antirevolutionaries:/ˌæntiˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛriz/
| n. |反革命分子；反动派|People who oppose a revolution or try to reverse its results.  Those who actively fight against revolutionary movements or governments.|The antirevolutionaries were quickly rounded up and imprisoned. 反革命分子很快就被围捕并监禁。|Synonyms: reactionaries, counterrevolutionaries|Antonyms: revolutionaries, rebels|Noun

antirevolutionary:/ˌæntiˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/
| adj. |反革命的；反动的|Opposing or resisting revolution. Characterized by opposition to revolution.|The government implemented antirevolutionary policies to suppress dissent. 政府实施反革命政策以压制异议。|Synonyms: reactionary, counterrevolutionary|Antonyms: revolutionary, rebellious|Adjective
