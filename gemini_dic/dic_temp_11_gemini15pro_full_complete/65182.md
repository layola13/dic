ethnologists:/ɛθˈnɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
民族学家
Experts who study the origins, development, and characteristics of different cultures and societies.
Ethnologists often conduct fieldwork to observe and document cultural practices. 民族学家经常进行实地考察以观察和记录文化习俗。
The ethnologists studied the tribe's rituals and customs. 这些民族学家研究了该部落的仪式和习俗。
anthropologists, sociologists, cultural researchers
