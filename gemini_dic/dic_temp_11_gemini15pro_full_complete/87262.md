heritor:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtər/ | n. |继承人|A person who inherits property, a title, or a tradition.|例句：He is the sole heritor of his father's fortune. 他是他父亲财产的唯一继承人。

例句：She is the heritor of a rich musical tradition. 她是一位继承了丰富音乐传统的继承人。|近义词：heir, inheritor, successor|反义词:predecessor|词性:noun
