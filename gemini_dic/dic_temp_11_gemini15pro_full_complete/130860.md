nonarbitrariness:/ˌnɒnˌɑːbɪˈtrɛərɪnəs/ | n. | 非任意性 | The quality of not being arbitrary; the fact of being based on reason, logic, or inherent properties rather than on whim or chance. | The nonarbitrariness of the linguistic sign is a key principle in semiotics.  语言符号的非任意性是符号学中的一个关键原则。 |  systematicity, regularity, logicality | arbitrariness, randomness, capriciousness | noun
