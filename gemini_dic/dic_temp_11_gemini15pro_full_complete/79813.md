glycolic:/ɡlaɪˈkɒlɪk/
adjective
乙醇酸的；羟基乙酸的
Relating to or derived from glycol, especially glycollic acid.
Glycolic acid is often used in chemical peels.  乙醇酸常用于化学换肤。
Glycolic acid can improve skin texture. 乙醇酸可以改善皮肤纹理。
近义词: hydroxyacetic (羟乙酸的)
反义词: None.
