blunderbuss: /ˈblʌndərˌbʌs/ | n. |  滑膛枪；笨拙的人；鲁莽的行动 | A short-barreled firearm with a flared muzzle, used to scatter shot at close range.  Also refers to a clumsy person or a reckless action. | The pirate fired his blunderbuss at the approaching ship. 海盗向驶近的船开火了滑膛枪。| Synonyms: musket, shotgun, blunderer, fool | Antonyms: expert, professional, genius | noun

blunderbuss: /ˈblʌndərˌbʌs/ | adj. | 笨拙的，鲁莽的 | Clumsy or reckless. | His blunderbuss approach to diplomacy caused an international incident. 他笨手笨脚的外交方式引发了一场国际事件。| Synonyms: awkward, clumsy, reckless, rash | Antonyms: careful, cautious, skillful, adept | adjective
