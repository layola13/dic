onomatopoeically:/ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːɪkli/ | adv. | 象声词地，拟声地 | Formed from onomatopoeia + -ic + -ally.  It describes something done or expressed using onomatopoeia, words that imitate sounds. | The author described the cat's purr onomatopoeically as "murrrr."  作者象声词地将猫的呼噜声描述为“murrrr”。 | The engine sputtered and coughed, dying onomatopoeically with a final "chug." 引擎发出噼啪声和咳嗽声，最后发出“chug”的拟声词就熄火了。 | mimetically, imitatively | literally, descriptively | adverb
