meaninglessnesses:/ˈmiːnɪŋləsnəsɪz/
|n.|无意义；空虚；无价值|The meaninglessnesses of modern life can lead to feelings of alienation and despair. 现代生活的空虚会导致疏离感和绝望感。|例句：He reflected on the meaninglessnesses of his past actions.  他反思了过去行为的无意义。

The meaninglessnesses of war are evident in the countless lives lost. 战争的无意义体现在无数生命的丧失上。|近义词：futilities, absurdities, trivialities|反义词：significance, meaning, purpose|词性:名词

词源：meaninglessness + -es (plural suffix)
meaninglessness 来自 meaningless (无意义的) + -ness (名词后缀)
meaningless 来自 meaning (意义) + -less (形容词后缀，表示“没有”)
meaning 来自古英语 mænan (意思是)
