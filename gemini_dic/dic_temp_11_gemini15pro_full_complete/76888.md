gangsterish:/ˈɡæŋstərɪʃ/| adj.|像歹徒的，流氓气的|Characteristic of or resembling a gangster; thuggish.|He had a gangsterish swagger and a menacing glare. 他有着一副流氓式的趾高气扬和凶狠的目光。|The dimly lit bar had a gangsterish atmosphere.  昏暗的酒吧里有一种流氓般的气氛。|近义词：thuggish, villainous, criminal, hoodlum-like|反义词:law-abiding, respectable, gentlemanly|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From gangster + -ish.  Gangster itself comes from gang + -ster.  Ultimately from Old Norse *gangr "a going, a troop."
