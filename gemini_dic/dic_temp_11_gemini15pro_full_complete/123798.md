moper:/ˈmoʊpər/| n.| 闷闷不乐的人；忧郁的人|A person given to prolonged periods of low spirits; someone who mopes.|He's been a real moper ever since he lost his job.  自从他失业以来，他一直闷闷不乐。|近义词：brooder, sulker, pouter|反义词：optimist, enthusiast|词性:名词

词源:  From the verb "mope" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Mope" comes from the Middle English "mopen," meaning "to be silent or dull," likely of Scandinavian origin.
