gymnosperm:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌspɜːrm/| n. |裸子植物|A plant that has seeds unprotected by an ovary or fruit.  These seeds are usually borne in cones, such as conifers, cycads, and ginkgoes.|例句：Pine trees and spruces are common examples of gymnosperms. 松树和云杉是常见的裸子植物的例子。

例句：Gymnosperms were dominant in the Mesozoic era. 裸子植物在中生代占主导地位。|近义词：nonflowering plant|反义词：angiosperm|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek words *gumnos* (naked) and *sperma* (seed).
