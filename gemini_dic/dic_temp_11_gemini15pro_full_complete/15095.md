bacchanalianism:/ˌbækəˈneɪliənɪzəm/
n.
狂欢作乐；纵酒狂欢；酒神狂欢节
Unrestrained revelry and merrymaking, often involving excessive drinking and unrestrained behavior.
The bacchanalianism of the fraternity party resulted in several arrests.  兄弟会的狂欢作乐导致数人被捕。

The annual festival descended into bacchanalianism as the revelers indulged in excessive drinking and wild dancing.  一年一度的节日变成了纵酒狂欢，狂欢者们尽情地喝酒和疯狂地跳舞。

近义词:debauchery, revelry, carousing, orgy
反义词:asceticism, abstinence, sobriety
词性:noun

词源: From Latin Bacchanalia, plural of Bacchanalis ("of Bacchus"), from Bacchus, the Roman god of wine and revelry, equivalent to the Greek Dionysus.
