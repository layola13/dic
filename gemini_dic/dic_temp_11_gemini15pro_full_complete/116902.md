mayhem:/ˈmeɪhem/| n.|混乱，骚乱，浩劫|violent or damaging disorder; chaos|The hurricane caused mayhem in the city.  飓风给城市造成了巨大的混乱。|

The football hooligans caused mayhem in the streets after the match.  足球流氓在赛后造成了街道上的骚乱。|近义词：chaos, disorder, havoc, bedlam|反义词：order, peace, tranquility|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *mahaym, probably from an unattested Old French verb *mahaigner (“to maim, wound, injure”), from Old French mahaing (“maiming, wounding”),  from a Germanic source akin to Old English *maimian.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *maitōną (“to cut”). Related to maim.
