hungerless:/ˈhʌŋɡərləs/| adj.|无饥饿感的；不渴望的|Free from hunger; not feeling or showing hunger; not craving or desiring something.|"The hungerless child pushed the food away."  那个不饿的孩子把食物推开了。|近义词：satiated, full, satisfied|反义词：hungry, ravenous, starving|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From 'hunger' + '-less' (suffix indicating absence).  'Hunger' comes from Old English 'hungor', ultimately from Proto-Germanic '*hungraz'.
