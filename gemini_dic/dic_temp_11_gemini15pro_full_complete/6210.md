ammonitish: /ˌæməˈnaɪtɪʃ/ | adj. | 像菊石的；古生物学上的 | Resembling or characteristic of ammonites (extinct marine mollusks). | The intricate, spiral patterns on the pottery were distinctly ammonitish.  这陶器上复杂的螺旋图案明显像菊石。 | similar, spiral, convoluted | - | adjective

ammonitish: /ˌæməˈnaɪtɪʃ/ | n. | 菊石化石 |  A fossil of an ammonite. | He found a beautiful piece of ammonitish on the beach. 他在海滩上发现了一块美丽的菊石化石。| fossil, remains, relic | - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ammon + -itish.  "Ammon" refers to the Egyptian god Amun, whose ram's horns resembled the spiral shape of ammonite fossils. "-itish" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
