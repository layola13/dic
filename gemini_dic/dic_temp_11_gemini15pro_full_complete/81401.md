grasshoppers:/ˈɡræshɒpəz/ (UK), /ˈɡræshɑːpərz/ (US)| n. |蚱蜢，蝗虫|A plant-eating insect with long back legs that enable it to jump long distances.|Example 1: The grasshoppers were chirping loudly in the field. 蚱蜢在地里大声鸣叫。

Example 2:  A swarm of grasshoppers destroyed the crops. 一群蝗虫摧毁了庄稼。|近义词：locust|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
