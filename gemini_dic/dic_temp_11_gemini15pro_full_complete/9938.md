apetalousness:/ˌeɪˈpetələsnəs/
n.
无瓣性；无花瓣
The condition of being apetalous; lacking petals.
Wind-pollinated plants often exhibit apetalousness as petals are not necessary to attract pollinators. 风媒植物通常表现出无瓣性，因为花瓣对于吸引传粉者来说不是必需的。
The apetalousness of the flower made it difficult to identify.  这种花的无瓣性使得它难以识别。
近义词: apetaly
反义词: petaliferousness (having petals)
词性: noun
