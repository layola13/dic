at-every-turn:/æt ˈɛvri tɜːrn/
|adverbial phrase|
|处处；事事；时时；总是；每时每刻|
|Encountering obstacles or difficulties consistently and repeatedly.|
|She faced criticism at every turn.|她处处受到批评。
|He encountered problems at every turn in his new job.|他在新工作中处处遇到问题。
|constantly, repeatedly, incessantly, perpetually, everywhere|
|never, rarely, seldom|
