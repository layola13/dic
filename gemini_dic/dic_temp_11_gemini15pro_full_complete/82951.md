gundelet: /ˈɡʌndəlɛt/ | n. | 小艇，贡多拉 | A small gondola or narrow, flat-bottomed boat. |  Example 1:  We took a gundelet through the canals of Venice. 我们乘着小艇穿过威尼斯运河。

Example 2: The gundelet glided silently through the water. 小艇静静地滑过水面。 | 近义词：gondola, boat, skiff | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  Likely a diminutive of "gondola," from Venetian Italian *gondola, perhaps from Venetian *gondola "rocking motion," or from Late Latin *cunula "small boat."  The exact etymology is uncertain.
