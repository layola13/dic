mutina:/ˈmjuːtɪnə/| n.|兵变，叛乱，暴动|An open rebellion against constituted authority, especially by soldiers or sailors against their officers.|The mutina was quickly suppressed by the loyal troops.  兵变很快被忠诚的部队镇压了。|Synonyms: rebellion, revolt, uprising, insurrection|Antonyms: obedience, submission, compliance|noun
