ETYMOLOGY->
From French cristallin, from Latin crystallinus, from Latin crystallum “crystal,” from Greek krystallos “ice, crystal,” from kryos “frost, ice.”  The sense of “transparent” arose from the association with the clarity of ice or crystal.
