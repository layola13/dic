overliberalize: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəlaɪz/
verb
过度自由化
To liberalize to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences.
The government overliberalized the financial markets, leading to instability and a subsequent crash.  政府过度放开了金融市场，导致了不稳定和随后的崩溃。
overliberalized: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度自由化的
Characterized by excessive liberalization.
The overliberalized economy was vulnerable to external shocks. 过度自由化的经济容易受到外部冲击。


近义词: deregulate excessively, overderegulate
反义词: overregulate, overcontrol
词性: verb, adjective
