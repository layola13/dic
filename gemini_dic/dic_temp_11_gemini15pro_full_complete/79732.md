glugged:/ɡlʌɡd/
verb
发出汩汩声；咕噜咕噜地喝
make a sound like liquid being poured quickly from a bottle
The coffee glugged as it poured into the mug. 咖啡倒进杯子里时发出汩汩的声音。
近义词：gurgled, gulped
反义词：sipped
