blistered:/ˈblɪstərd/ | adj. |起泡的，起了水泡的；（油漆等）起泡的，鼓起的|Covered in blisters or having blisters.  Also, describes a surface like paint that has bubbled up.|例句：My feet were blistered after the long hike. 我的脚在长途跋涉后起了水泡。|例句：The blistered paint on the wall needs to be scraped off.  墙上起泡的油漆需要刮掉。|近义词：blistery, chafed, raw |反义词:smooth, unblemished |词性:adjective
blister:/ˈblɪstər/ | n. |水泡；（油漆等表面上的）气泡，疱|A small bubble on the skin filled with serum and caused by friction, burning, or other damage. Also, a bubble on a painted or varnished surface.|例句：I got a blister on my heel from my new shoes. 我的新鞋把我的脚后跟磨出了水泡。|例句：The heat caused blisters to form on the paintwork.  高温导致油漆表面形成了气泡。|近义词：vesicle, bleb |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
blister:/ˈblɪstər/ | v. |起泡；使起泡；（使）起疱|To cause blisters to form. To become covered in blisters.|例句：The hot sun blistered the paint on the car.  烈日晒得车上的油漆起了泡。|例句：My hands blistered from rowing the boat.  我的手因为划船起了水泡。|近义词：scald, burn |反义词: N/A |词性:verb
