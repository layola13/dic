manufactured:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərd/ | adj. | 制造的，人造的 |  Made in a factory, by hand, or by other means, especially on a large scale. |  These cars are manufactured in Germany. 这些汽车是在德国制造的。 | produced, fabricated, created | natural, handmade(in some contexts) | adjective
manufactured:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərd/ | v. (past tense and past participle of manufacture) |  (verb's past tense/past participle form, functions as part of a verb phrase) |  To make something, especially on a large scale using machinery.  | The company manufactured over 10,000 units last year.  这家公司去年生产了超过1万台设备。 | produced, fabricated, created | destroyed, dismantled | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *manufacture*, from French *manufacturer*, from Latin *manu factus* "made by hand," past participle of *manu facere* "to make by hand," from *manus* "hand" + *facere* "to make, do."
