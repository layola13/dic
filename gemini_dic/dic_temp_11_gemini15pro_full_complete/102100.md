isostasy:/aɪˈsɒstəsi/ | n. |地壳均衡；地壳均衡说|The state of gravitational equilibrium between Earth's crust and mantle such that the crust "floats" at an elevation that depends on its thickness and density.  地球的地壳和地幔之间的重力平衡状态，使得地壳“漂浮”在一个取决于其厚度和密度的海拔高度上。|The theory of isostasy explains how different topographic heights can exist on the Earth's surface. 地壳均衡理论解释了地球表面如何存在不同的地形高度。|Isostatic equilibrium is disrupted by tectonic uplift. 地壳均衡被构造抬升所扰乱。 |Synonyms: equilibrium, balance |Antonyms: imbalance, instability | noun
