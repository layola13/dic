carta:/ˈkɑːrtə/ (UK), /ˈkɑːrtə/ (US)| n.|地图，海图；（正式的）信，函|A map, especially a sea chart; a formal letter, especially one written in Latin or other foreign languages|例句：The captain studied the carta carefully before setting sail. 船长在起航前仔细研究了海图。

例句：He received a carta from the university confirming his acceptance. 他收到了大学的录取通知书。|近义词：map, chart, letter, epistle|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin carta "paper, papyrus, card, map, chart," from Greek khartēs "papyrus leaf, sheet of paper, page, letter," probably from a pre-Greek substrate word ultimately from Egyptian.
