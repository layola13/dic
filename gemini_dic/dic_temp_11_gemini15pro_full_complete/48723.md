defilade:/ˌdefɪˈleɪd/ /ˌdiːfɪˈleɪd/
n.|掩护；防御工事|Protection from hostile observation and fire, especially by using natural or artificial obstacles.|The troops were in defilade behind the hill. 部队在山后得到掩护。|近义词：cover, protection, shelter|反义词：exposure|noun
defilade:/ˌdefɪˈleɪd/ /ˌdiːfɪˈleɪd/
vt.|使…得到掩护；构筑防御工事|To protect (troops, a position, etc.) from enemy fire by screening, especially by a natural obstacle.|The engineers defiladed the command post. 工程兵掩护了指挥所。|近义词：protect, shield, screen|反义词：expose|verb
