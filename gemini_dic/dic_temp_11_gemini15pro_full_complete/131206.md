nonbusier:/nɒnˈbɪziər/ | adj. | 更不忙的，不更忙的 | Not busier; equally or less busy than a comparison point.  This word is not standard English and is grammatically incorrect.  The correct comparative form of "busy" is "less busy". | *Incorrect usage:* My schedule is nonbusier this week than last. | 近义词: less busy | 反义词: busier | 词性: 形容词 (incorrectly formed)
