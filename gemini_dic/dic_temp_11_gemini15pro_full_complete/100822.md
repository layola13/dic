introducer:/ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːsər/ | n. | 介绍人，引进者，引言 | someone who introduces another person or thing; something that introduces something else | The chairman acted as the introducer of the guest speaker. 主席担任了演讲嘉宾的介绍人。| synonyms: presenter, host, usher, facilitator | antonyms:  None (It's difficult to have a direct antonym for "introducer") | noun

introducer:/ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːsər/ | n. | 导论，绪论 | a short piece of writing at the beginning of a book or other piece of work that gives a general idea of its contents | The introducer to the textbook explained the author's approach to the subject.  教科书的绪论解释了作者研究这个主题的方法。| synonyms: preface, foreword, introduction, preamble | antonyms:  conclusion, epilogue | noun
