mascaraing:/mæˈskɑːrəɪŋ/
verb
涂睫毛膏
The act of applying mascara.
She was mascaraing her eyelashes. 她正在涂睫毛膏。
applying mascara, putting on mascara
removing mascara
v.
Etymology: From mascara + -ing.  "Mascara" itself comes from the Italian "maschera" meaning mask, ultimately from Arabic "maskharah" meaning buffoonery or mockery.
