kinglihood:/ˈkɪŋlɪhʊd/
n.
王位，王权，君权
The state, position, or dignity of a king.
He ascended to the kinglihood after his father's death. 他在父亲去世后继承了王位。

近义词: kingship, sovereignty, monarchy, reign
反义词: republic, democracy

词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:  From Middle English *kinglihede*, equivalent to kingly +‎ -hood.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kuningaz* (king) + *-haiduz* (state, condition).
