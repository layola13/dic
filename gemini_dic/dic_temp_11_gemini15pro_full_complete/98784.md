integrally:/ɪnˈteɡrəli/| adv.|完整地；不可分割地；作为整体|As a whole; completely; in a way that is essential to the nature of something. |例句：Safety is integrally linked to good design. 安全与良好的设计密不可分。|近义词：completely, totally, entirely, fully, wholly, thoroughly, comprehensively|反义词：partially, separately, independently|词性:副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *integralis*, from Latin integer "whole, complete".
