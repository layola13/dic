counter-evidence:/ˈkaʊntərˌevɪdəns/| n.|反证；相反的证据|Evidence that contradicts or opposes other evidence.|The defense lawyer presented counter-evidence that challenged the prosecution's case. 辩护律师提出了反驳控方案件的反证。|

The alibi provided counter-evidence to the accusation that he was at the scene of the crime.  不在场证明提供了反驳他在犯罪现场指控的反证。

|Synonyms: contradictory evidence, opposing evidence, disproof, rebuttal|Antonyms:  supporting evidence, corroborating evidence, proof, confirmation|noun|
