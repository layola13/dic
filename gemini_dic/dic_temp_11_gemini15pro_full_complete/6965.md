andesite:/ˈændɪsaɪt/ | n. |安山岩|A dark, fine-grained, volcanic rock, intermediate in composition between basalt and rhyolite.  It is typically gray or greenish-gray and is composed primarily of plagioclase feldspar and pyroxene or hornblende.|The Andes Mountains are largely composed of andesite. 安第斯山脉主要由安山岩构成。| Andesite is commonly used in construction. 安山岩常用于建筑。|Near synonyms:  trachyandesite,  latite|Antonyms: N/A (Rock types don't typically have opposites)|noun
