cohost:/ˈkoʊhoʊst/ (US) /ˈkəʊhəʊst/ (UK)| n. & v.|共同主持人，共同主办|A person who hosts something jointly with another or others. |例句：She cohosted the event with her colleague. 她和她的同事共同主持了这次活动。

例句：They will cohost a fundraising gala next month.  他们下个月将共同主办一场募款晚会。

近义词：joint host, co-presenter|反义词:|词性: noun, verb

cohost:/ˈkoʊhoʊst/ (US) /ˈkəʊhəʊst/ (UK)| v.|共同主持，共同主办|To host something jointly with another or others. |例句：We decided to cohost the party to share the workload. 我们决定共同举办派对以分担工作量。


例句：The two organizations will cohost a conference on climate change. 这两个组织将共同主办一个关于气候变化的会议。


近义词：jointly host, co-present|反义词:|词性: verb


词源: co- + host.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "jointly," "together," or "mutually."
