durableness:/ˈdjʊərəbəlnəs/ /ˈdʊrəbəlnəs/| n. |耐久性，耐用性|The quality of being durable; ability to withstand wear, decay, or damage.|The durableness of this fabric makes it ideal for outdoor use. 这种织物的耐久性使其非常适合户外使用。|近义词：durability, toughness, strength, sturdiness, firmness|反义词：fragility, weakness, flimsiness|词性:名词
Etymology: From durable + -ness. Durable comes from Old French durable (14c.) and directly from Late Latin durabilis "lasting," from Latin durare "to harden, last, endure," from durus "hard."
