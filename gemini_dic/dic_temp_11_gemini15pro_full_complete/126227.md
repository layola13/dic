mycelium:/maɪˈsiːliəm/| n.|菌丝体|The vegetative part of a fungus, consisting of a network of fine white filaments (hyphae).|The mushroom's mycelium spread throughout the soil.  蘑菇的菌丝体遍布土壤。|Synonyms: hyphae network, spawn|Antonyms: fruiting body (indirect)|noun
