figuratively:/ˈfɪɡjərətɪvli/ /ˈfɪɡjʊrətɪvli/
adverb
比喻地，象征性地
Departing from the literal use of words; metaphorically.
She was figuratively drowning in paperwork. 她简直被文书工作淹没了。
近义词:metaphorically, symbolically, poetically, allegorically
反义词:literally, actually, really
词性:adverb
