frappuccino:/ˌfræpəˈtʃiːnoʊ/ (American) /ˌfræpəˈtʃiːnəʊ/ (British)| n.|星冰乐；法布奇诺|A blended iced coffee drink, often flavored and topped with whipped cream.|I ordered a caramel frappuccino. 我点了一杯焦糖星冰乐。|近义词: iced coffee, blended coffee drink|反义词: hot coffee|noun

frappuccino:/ˌfræpəˈtʃiːnoʊ/ (American) /ˌfræpəˈtʃiːnəʊ/ (British)| adj.|星冰乐口味的；像星冰乐的 (较少用作形容词)|Resembling or characteristic of a frappuccino. |The cake had a distinct frappuccino flavor.  这个蛋糕有明显的星冰乐味道。|近义词: iced coffee flavored|反义词: N/A|adjective

词源:  "Frappuccino" is a portmanteau of "frappé," a New England milkshake thickened with ice cream, and "cappuccino," an Italian coffee drink.  The name and drink were developed, named, and sold by The Coffee Connection, which was later acquired by Starbucks.  星冰乐"是由“frappé”（一种用冰淇淋增稠的新英格兰奶昔）和“cappuccino”（一种意大利咖啡饮料）两个词组合而成的。这个名称和饮料是由The Coffee Connection开发、命名和销售的，该公司后来被星巴克收购。
