non-ionics:/ˌnɒn aɪˈɒnɪks/ |n.|非离子型表面活性剂|Substances that do not dissociate into ions in solution, often used as detergents or emulsifiers.|Non-ionics are commonly used in laundry detergents because they are less sensitive to water hardness.| 近义词: non-ionic surfactants|反义词: ionic surfactants|词性: 名词

non-ionic:/ˌnɒn aɪˈɒnɪk/ |adj.|非离子型的|Not ionizing; not dissociating into ions in solution.|Non-ionic detergents are effective in both hard and soft water.|近义词: non-ionized|反义词: ionic|词性: 形容词
