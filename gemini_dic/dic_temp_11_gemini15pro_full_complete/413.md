abjuratory:/ˌæbdʒʊˈreɪtəri/ |adj.  n.|放弃的，否认的；放弃誓言的地方|Relating to a formal renunciation or denial; a place where such a renunciation is made.|He signed an abjuratory document, renouncing his former beliefs. 他签署了一份放弃声明，放弃了他以前的信仰。|The abjuratory was a solemn place where heretics were forced to recant. 这个放弃誓言的地方是一个庄严的地方，异教徒被迫在这里放弃信仰。|Synonyms: renunciatory, recantatory, repudiatory|Antonyms: affirming, confirmatory, avowing|adjective, noun
