frequenter:/ˈfriːkwɛntər/ | n. |常客， frequent visitor|A person who visits a particular place often. | She is a frequenter of the local library. 她常去家附近的图书馆。 | He's a frequenter of that coffee shop. 他是那家咖啡店的常客。 |  近义词: regular, habitué, patron | 反义词: stranger, newcomer | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From the verb "frequent" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs the action of the verb).  "Frequent" comes from Old French "frequenter" and ultimately from Latin "frequentāre", meaning "to visit often," from "frequēns" meaning "crowded, numerous, often."
