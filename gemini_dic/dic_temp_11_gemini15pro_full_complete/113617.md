machete:/məˈʃɛti/ (US), /məˈtʃiːti/ (UK)| n. |弯刀，大砍刀|A large, heavy knife with a broad blade, used as a tool or weapon, especially in Latin America. |例句：The farmer used a machete to clear the brush. 农民用弯刀清理灌木丛。

例句：He carried a machete for protection in the jungle. 他在丛林中携带一把弯刀防身。|近义词：knife, blade, cutlass, sword|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "peace tool")|词性:noun


machetes:/məˈʃɛtiz/ (US), /məˈtʃiːtiz/ (UK)| n. |弯刀（复数）|Plural form of machete.  Large, heavy knives with broad blades, used as tools or weapons. |例句：The workers sharpened their machetes before harvesting the sugarcane. 工人们在收割甘蔗之前磨快了他们的弯刀。

例句：Machetes were used to clear the path through the dense forest. 弯刀被用来清理穿过茂密森林的小路。|近义词：knives, blades, cutlasses, swords|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun


词源: From Spanish *machete*, diminutive of *macho* "sledgehammer, male," from Latin *masculus* "male."
