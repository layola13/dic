organicities:/ɔːrˌɡænˈɪsɪtiz/
n.
有机性，有机物
The state or quality of being organic; organic matter.
The researchers studied the organicities present in the soil samples. 研究人员研究了土壤样本中存在的有机物。
近义词: organic matter, organic compounds
反义词: inorganic matter, inorganic compounds
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From organic + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + -es (plural suffix).  The root "organic" ultimately comes from the Greek word "organon" meaning "instrument" or "tool".
