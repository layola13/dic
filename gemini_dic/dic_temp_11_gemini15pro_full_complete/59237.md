e'er:/ɛər/ | adv. | ever的诗歌体 | A poetic contraction of "ever." |  E'er since that day, I've been a changed man. 从那天起，我就变成了一个不一样的人。| 近义词: always, ever | 反义词: never | 词性: 副词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):

If e'er I see him again, I'll tell him the truth. 如果我再见到他，我会告诉他真相。

She walks the beach e'er day. 她每天都去海滩散步。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

He is e'er striving for perfection. 他一直在追求完美。

She is e'er learning new things. 她一直在学习新事物。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

Have you e'er seen such beauty? 你见过如此美丽的事物吗？

He has e'er been a loyal friend. 他一直是一个忠诚的朋友。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

I have been e'er searching for the truth. 我一直在寻找真相。

She has been e'er working on her novel. 她一直在写她的小说。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):

He e'er visited me on Sundays. 他以前每个星期天都来看我。

She e'er walked to school. 她以前总是步行上学。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

I was e'er hoping for a miracle. 我一直盼望着奇迹出现。

He was e'er thinking of her. 他一直在想着她。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

I had e'er believed in his innocence. 我一直相信他是无辜的。

She had e'er dreamed of becoming a doctor. 她一直梦想成为一名医生。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

I had been e'er waiting for this moment. 我一直在等待这一刻。

They had been e'er arguing about the same thing. 他们一直在争论同一件事。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):

I will e'er remember this day. 我将永远记住这一天。

She will e'er be grateful for your help. 她将永远感激你的帮助。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

I will be e'er working on this project. 我将一直致力于这个项目。

He will be e'er traveling the world. 他将一直在世界各地旅行。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

By next year, I will have e'er finished my studies. 到明年，我将完成我的学业。

By then, she will have e'er achieved her goals. 到那时，她将实现她的目标。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

By then, she will have been e'er studying English for five years. 到那时，她将学习英语五年了。
