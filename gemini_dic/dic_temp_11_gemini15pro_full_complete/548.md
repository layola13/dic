aborigines:/ˌæbəˈrɪdʒəniz/ | n. |原住民，土著|The original inhabitants of a country or region, especially referring to indigenous peoples. |例句：The aborigines of Australia have a rich cultural heritage. 澳大利亚的原住民拥有丰富的文化遗产。

例句：The government has implemented policies to protect the rights of aborigines. 政府已经实施了保护原住民权利的政策。|近义词：indigenous people, native people, first nations|反义词:immigrants, settlers|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "ab origine" meaning "from the beginning."
