deforest:/ˌdiːˈfɔːrɪst/ /ˌdiːˈfɑːrɪst/ | vt. | 砍伐森林，清除树林 | To clear (an area) of forests or trees. |  They are deforesting the Amazon at an alarming rate. 他们正在以惊人的速度砍伐亚马逊森林。

The company was fined for illegally deforesting protected land.  该公司因非法砍伐受保护的土地而被罚款。


近义词:clear, log, denude | 反义词:reforest, afforest | 词性:动词


词源: de- (down, away) + forest (forest)
