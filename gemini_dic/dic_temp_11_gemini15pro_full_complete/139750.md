ockers:/ˈɑːkərz/
n.
储物柜
Lockers are small cabinets, typically with locks, used for storing personal belongings.
The students put their books and bags in their lockers. 学生们把书和包放在储物柜里。
cupboards, cabinets
safes

Etymology:
From lock + -er.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *luk-, meaning "to close, fasten."
