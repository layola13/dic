czars:/ˈzɑːrz/ /ˈtsɑːrz/| n. |沙皇；独裁者；专家|A person with great power and authority, especially in a particular area.  Originally, the title of the emperors of Russia.|The drug czar announced a new policy.  禁毒沙皇宣布了一项新政策。|synonyms: emperor, monarch, ruler, autocrat, dictator, leader, chief, boss, head, overlord, king, potentate, tycoon, magnate, mogul, lord, master  |antonyms: subject, subordinate, follower|noun
