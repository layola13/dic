autocarpian:/ˌɔːtəʊˈkɑːrpiən/
adjective|自花授粉的；自体受精的|Of or relating to autocarpous plants; self-fertilizing.|The autocarpian flowers of the pea plant allow it to self-pollinate. 豌豆植株的自花授粉花朵使其能够自花授粉。|self-fertilizing, self-pollinating|cross-fertilizing
noun|自花授粉植物|A plant that is autocarpous.|Autocarpians are often found in isolated environments. 自花授粉植物通常在孤立的环境中被发现。|self-fertilizer, self-pollinator|cross-fertilizer
