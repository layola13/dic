diapause:/ˈdaɪəpɔːz/| n. |滞育，休眠|A period of suspended development in an insect, other invertebrate, or mammal embryo, especially during unfavorable environmental conditions.|Many insects enter diapause to survive the winter. 许多昆虫进入滞育期以度过冬天。| The butterfly pupa entered diapause during the drought.  这只蝴蝶蛹在干旱期间进入了滞育期。|近义词：dormancy, quiescence, hibernation, aestivation|反义词: activity, growth|名词
