overborrow:/ˈoʊvərˈbɒroʊ/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˈbɒrəʊ/ (UK)| v. |过度借贷|To borrow excessively, beyond one's capacity to repay.|The company overborrowed during the boom years and now faces bankruptcy.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度借贷，现在面临破产。|近义词：overspend, overextend|反义词：save, underspend|词性：动词

overborrow:/ˈoʊvərˈbɒroʊ/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˈbɒrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |过度借贷（名词化）|The act of borrowing excessively.|The nation's overborrow is a serious economic concern.  该国的过度借贷是一个严重的经济问题。|近义词：excessive borrowing, overspending|反义词：saving, underspending|词性：名词
