mesolabe: /ˈmesəˌleɪb/ /ˈmiːzəˌleɪb/ | n. | 中比例求解器；中比例分割器 | An instrument used in ancient Greece for finding two mean proportionals between two given lines.  It was used in attempting to duplicate the cube. | The mathematician used a mesolabe to solve the complex geometrical problem.  这位数学家使用中比例求解器来解决复杂的几何问题。 |  近义词:  proportional compass, geometric instrument | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

mesolabe: /ˈmesəˌleɪb/ /ˈmiːzəˌleɪb/ | n. | 内插平均值；内插中项 |  An interpolated mean or intermediate value. (Less common usage) |  The mesolabe of the two data points was calculated to refine the model.  这两个数据点的内插平均值被计算出来以改进模型。 | 近义词:  mean, average, median | 反义词: extreme | 词性: noun
