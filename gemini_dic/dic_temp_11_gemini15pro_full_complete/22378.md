boodies:/ˈbuːdiz/ | n. | 朋友，伙伴；(尤指)女性朋友 |Close friends, especially female friends.  Often used informally and can sometimes be considered mildly vulgar. |"She went out with her boodies last night." 她昨晚和她的闺蜜们出去了。 | 近义词：friends, pals, mates, besties | 反义词：enemies, foes | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a playful variation of "bodies," emphasizing closeness and physical presence.  It may also be influenced by "boo," a term of endearment.
