commemorator:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪtər/| n. |纪念者，庆祝者|A person who commemorates someone or something.|例句：The commemorators gathered at the monument to honor the fallen soldiers. 纪念者聚集在纪念碑前，向阵亡的士兵致敬。

例句：The commemorators laid wreaths at the tomb of the unknown soldier. 纪念者们在无名战士墓前敬献花圈。

近义词：celebrant, observer, participant|反义词:detractor, critic|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemorator, from commemoratus, past participle of commemorare "to call to remembrance," from com- "together" + memorare "to mention, remind," from memor "mindful."
