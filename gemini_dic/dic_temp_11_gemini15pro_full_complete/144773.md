over-egg:/ˌoʊvərˈeɡ/
verb
过度烹饪；过分吹嘘；做的过火
To cook (an egg) excessively, especially by frying or poaching it for too long. To exaggerate or embellish something to an excessive degree.
He over-egged the pudding with too much brandy. 他在布丁里加了太多的白兰地，做过头了。
She over-egged her qualifications in her resume. 她在简历里过分夸大了自己的资历。
overcook, overdo, exaggerate
understate, downplay
v.
