biofuel:/ˈbaɪoʊˌfjuːəl/| n. |生物燃料|A fuel that is produced directly or indirectly from organic material – biomass – including plant materials and animal waste.|Brazil is the world’s largest producer of biofuel. 巴西是世界上最大的生物燃料生产国。

The government is encouraging the use of biofuels. 政府正在鼓励使用生物燃料。


|近义词：biodiesel, biogas, ethanol, green fuel|反义词:fossil fuel|词性:noun
