obsoletely:/ˌɒbsəˈliːtli/ /ˌɑːbsəˈliːtli/
adjective
过时地，陈旧地
in a way that is no longer used or needed because something newer and better exists.
The factory uses obsoletely designed machinery.  这家工厂使用设计过时的机器。
The information in the book is now completely obsoletely presented. 这本书里的信息现在完全过时了。
近义词: archaically, antiquatedly, outmodedly
反义词: modernly, contemporarily
词性: adverb

词源: From obsolete + -ly.  Obsolete comes from Latin obsoletus, past participle of obsolescere "to fall into disuse," from ob- "away" + solescere "to grow accustomed," related to solere "be accustomed."
