minimoto:/ˈmɪniˌmoʊtoʊ/| n.|迷你摩托车|A small motorcycle, typically with an engine displacement of 49cc or less, often used for recreational riding or racing by children and adults.  It's a portmanteau of "mini" and "motorcycle".|He bought his son a minimoto for his birthday. 他给他儿子买了一辆迷你摩托车作为生日礼物。|Synonyms: pocket bike, mini bike|Antonyms: motorcycle, motorbike|Noun
