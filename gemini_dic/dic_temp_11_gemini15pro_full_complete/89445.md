hone:/hoʊn/| v. |磨练，提高，使完美|To sharpen or refine something, especially a skill or talent.|She honed her writing skills by practicing every day. 她每天练习以磨练她的写作技巧。|He honed his debating skills at university. 他在大学里磨练了他的辩论技巧。|近义词：sharpen, refine, improve, perfect|反义词:blunt, dull|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old English *hān (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *hōniz (“whetstone”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kōi- (“to sharpen, whet”). Cognate with Old Saxon hōni (“whetstone”), Old Frisian hēn, hōne (“whetstone”), Middle Dutch hoen (“whetstone”), Dutch hoon (“scorn, mockery”), Old High German hōn (“whetstone”), German Hohn (“scorn, mockery”). Related to hen. More at hen.
