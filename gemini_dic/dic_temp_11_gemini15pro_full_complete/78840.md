gin:/dʒɪn/ | n. | 杜松子酒；轧棉机；陷阱；网 | A clear alcoholic spirit distilled from grain or malt and flavored with juniper berries. |  例句：She poured herself a gin and tonic. 她给自己倒了一杯杜松子酒加汤力水。

例句：The cotton gin revolutionized the textile industry.  轧棉机彻底改变了纺织工业。| 近义词：spirits, liquor (for alcoholic beverage); trap, snare (for other meanings) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on the meaning) | 词性: noun


gin:/dʒɪn/ | v. | 轧（棉花） | To separate the seeds from (cotton) with a gin. | 例句：The farmers ginned the cotton after the harvest.  农民们在收获后轧棉花。

例句：The machine can gin a large amount of cotton quickly. 这台机器可以快速轧大量的棉花。| 近义词：process, separate | 反义词: combine, unite | 词性: verb
