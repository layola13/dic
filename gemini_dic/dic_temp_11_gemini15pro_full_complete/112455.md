loudly:/ˈlaʊdli/ (US) /ˈlaʊdli/ (UK)| adv.|大声地，响亮地|In a loud manner; with much noise.|She laughed loudly at his joke. 她大声地笑了他的笑话。|近义词：noisily, clamorously, boisterously|反义词:quietly, softly, silently|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *lowdli, *ludeli, equivalent to loud +‎ -ly.
