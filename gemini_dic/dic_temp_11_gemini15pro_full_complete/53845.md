noun
不满；不满足
The possessive form of "discontent," referring to the dissatisfaction or unhappiness belonging to someone or something.
The discontent's source was the unfair treatment of the workers.  工人们的不满是源于不公平的待遇。
The government failed to address the people's discontents. 政府未能解决人民的不满。
Synonyms: dissatisfaction, unhappiness, displeasure, grievance
Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, happiness, pleasure
