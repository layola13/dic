derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/| n. adj.|衍生物，派生物；导出的，衍生的|Something that is derived from something else.  A derivative is something which has been developed or obtained from something else.|例句：This new drug is a derivative of morphine. 这种新药是从吗啡衍生出来的。|近义词：offshoot, by-product, descendant, spin-off|反义词：original, source, prototype|noun
derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/| adj.|衍生的，派生的|Based on or originating from something else.|例句：A derivative design. 一个衍生设计。|近义词：derived, secondary, unoriginal|反义词：original, primary|adjective
