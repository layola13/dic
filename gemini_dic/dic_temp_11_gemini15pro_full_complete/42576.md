counterpickets:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪts/
noun
对峙纠察队，反纠察队
A group of pickets acting in opposition to another group of pickets.
The union's counterpickets clashed with the company's pickets outside the factory.  工会的反纠察队与公司在工厂外的纠察队发生了冲突。
Counter-demonstrators formed counterpickets to oppose the anti-abortion protesters. 反堕胎抗议者的反对者组成了反纠察队来对抗他们。
synonyms: counter-demonstrators, counter-protesters
antonyms: pickets, demonstrators, protesters
n.
