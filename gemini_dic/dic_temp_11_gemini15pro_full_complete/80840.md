goulard:/ˈɡuːlɑːrd/
n.
高拉德水；醋酸铅溶液
A solution of lead acetate in water, formerly used as an astringent and antiseptic.
Goulard's extract was once a common remedy for burns.  高拉德提取液曾是一种常见的烧伤药。
Synonyms: lead acetate solution, lead subacetate
