abrenounce: /əˈbreˈnaʊns/| v. |放弃，宣布放弃；断绝关系|To formally reject or disavow a belief, claim, or course of action. |He abrenounced his claim to the throne. 他放弃了对王位的要求。|近义词：renounce, relinquish, forswear, repudiate|反义词：affirm, embrace, claim, uphold|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *abrenuntiare, from Latin ab- + renunciare “to renounce,” from re- + nuntiare “to announce.”
