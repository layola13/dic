aace:/eɪs/ | n. |扑克牌A; (某些运动中) 发球得分|A playing card with a single spot on it, ranked highest or lowest in most card games. Also, a point scored on a serve, as in tennis or volleyball.|He served an ace to win the match. 他发球得分赢得了比赛。|Synonyms: one, unit, single, champion |Antonyms: loser, failure | noun

aace:/eɪs/ | v. |(考试等) 得满分；轻松取胜|To excel; to achieve a perfect score or an outstanding result.|She aced her math exam. 她数学考试得了满分。|Synonyms: triumph, conquer, dominate, master |Antonyms: fail, flunk, lose | verb
