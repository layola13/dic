ill-gotten:/ˌɪlˈɡɒtn/ /ˌɪlˈɡɑːtn/
adjective
非法获得的；不义之财
obtained dishonestly or unjustly
例句：He deposited his ill-gotten gains in a Swiss bank account. 他把非法所得存入瑞士银行账户。

例句：The police seized the ill-gotten loot. 警察没收了非法掠夺的赃物。

近义词：stolen, pilfered, purloined, misbegotten
反义词：legitimate, honestly earned, lawfully acquired
词性: adjective
