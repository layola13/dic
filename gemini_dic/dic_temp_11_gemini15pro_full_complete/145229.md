overbias:/ˌoʊvərˈbaɪəs/| v. |过度偏置，过分偏袒|To introduce excessive bias into (a system, device, or attitude).  To influence unfairly in a particular direction.|The algorithm was overbiased towards certain demographics, leading to unfair outcomes.| 近义词: skew, distort, weight, prejudice | 反义词: balance, equalize, neutralize |词性:动词
