horsier:/ˈhɔːrsiər/ | adj. | 更像马的；马性更大的；粗俗的 | More like a horse; having more of the characteristics of a horse, especially in being large, strong, or coarse. |例句：He has a horsier face than his brother. 他的脸比他兄弟更像马脸。

例句：Her voice became horsier after shouting for so long.  她喊了很久后，嗓音变得沙哑了。 | 近义词：coarse, equine | 反义词：refined, delicate |词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From horse + -ier (comparative suffix).  The suffix "-ier" is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives, indicating a greater degree of the quality described by the base adjective.
