courtship: /ˈkɔːrtʃɪp/ (US) /ˈkɔːtʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|求爱，追求；求偶|The process of wooing or attracting a mate.  The period of time during which a couple develops a romantic relationship before marriage.|He conducted a long and arduous courtship before she finally agreed to marry him. 他经过长期而艰难的追求，她最终同意嫁给他。|近义词：wooing, pursuit, romance, courting|反义词: rejection, spurning|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *corteshipe, from Old French cortesie "courtesy." Originally referred to courtly behavior, later specialized to the behavior of a man seeking a woman's favor.
