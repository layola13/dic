indemnifies:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪz/| v. |赔偿；保障|compensates or protects someone against (a loss, penalty, or other financial burden).|The insurance policy indemnifies the homeowner against fire damage. 这份保险单保障房主免受火灾损失。|近义词：compensate, reimburse, protect, secure|反义词: penalize, charge|verb
