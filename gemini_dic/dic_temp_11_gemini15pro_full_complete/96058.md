indecisions:/ˌɪndɪˈsɪʒənz/| n.|犹豫不决，优柔寡断|The state of being unable to make a decision.|Her indecisions about her future career caused her much anxiety. 她对未来职业的犹豫不决使她非常焦虑。|近义词：hesitations, doubts, waverings|反义词:decisions, determination, resoluteness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + decision.  The suffix -s indicates the plural form.  Ultimately from Latin decidere (to decide).
