appurtenance:/əˈpɜːrtɪnəns/
n.
附属物，配件
something that belongs to something else; an accessory
The garage is an appurtenance to the house. 车库是房屋的附属建筑。

近义词: accessory, adjunct, attachment, appendage
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

appurtenance:/əˈpɜːrtɪnəns/
n.
(通常复数)附属物；设备
(usually plural) equipment needed for a particular activity
The knight wore full armor and all its appurtenances. 骑士身穿全套盔甲及其所有配件。

近义词: equipment, paraphernalia, gear, tackle
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


词源:
From Middle English *appurtenaunce, borrowed from Anglo-Norman apurtenance, a- + purtenance (“belonging”), from purtenir (“to pertain”).
