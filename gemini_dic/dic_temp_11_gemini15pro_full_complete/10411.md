appertinent:/ˌæpərˈteɪnənt/ | adj. |相关的，相配的|Relating to; belonging to; relevant.  Specifically, appertinent often implies a necessary or logical connection. |The documents appertinent to the case were presented in court.  案件的相关文件在法庭上出示了。|Synonyms: relevant, applicable, germane, pertinent|Antonyms: irrelevant, inapplicable, extraneous|adjective
