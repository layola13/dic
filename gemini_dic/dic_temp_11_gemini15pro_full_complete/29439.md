catallactics:/kætəˈlæktɪks/ | n. | 交集合理论 | The study of exchanges, or the science of mutually advantageous trade and cooperation.  It focuses on the analysis of market processes and the institutions that facilitate them. | Catallactics explains how prices are formed through voluntary interactions in the market.  交集合理论解释了价格是如何通过市场上的自愿互动形成的。

Catallactics is crucial to understanding how free markets allocate resources efficiently. 交集合理论对于理解自由市场如何有效地配置资源至关重要。 | Near synonyms:  praxeology, market theory | Antonyms:  central planning, command economy | noun
