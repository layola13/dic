longevous:/lɒnˈdʒiːvəs/ /lɑːnˈdʒiːvəs/
adjective
长寿的；长命的
Living to a great age; long-lived.
例句：The tortoise is a longevous animal. 龟是一种长寿的动物。

例句：Her longevous relatives all lived past ninety. 她长寿的亲戚都活过了九十岁。

近义词：long-lived, enduring
反义词：short-lived, ephemeral
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *longevus (“long-lived”), from longus (“long”) + aevum (“age, lifetime”).
