contrivers:/kənˈtraɪvərz/
n.
发明者，设计者，策划者
People who invent, design, or plan something, especially something clever or complicated.
The contrivers of this plan deserve praise for their ingenuity.  这个计划的设计者们因其独创性值得称赞。
inventors, designers, planners, schemers, architects
n/a
noun
