licensee:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiː/
n.
被许可人，获许可方
a person or company that has been granted a license
The licensee is permitted to sell alcohol on the premises.  该持证人被允许在场所内销售酒精。

licensee:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiː/
n.
被授权者
Someone who has official permission to do something.
The licensee is responsible for ensuring compliance with the terms of the license. 被授权人有责任确保遵守许可条款。


近义词:
permittee, grantee
反义词:

词性:
