lapis:/ˈlæpɪs/| n.|青金石，天青石|a bright blue metamorphic rock consisting largely of lazurite, used for decoration and as a pigment.|The ancient Egyptians prized lapis for its intense blue color. 古埃及人珍视青金石浓郁的蓝色。|Synonyms: lazurite, azure stone|Antonyms: N/A|noun

lapis lazuli:/ˌlæpɪs ˈlæzjʊˌlaɪ/| n.|青金石|a semiprecious stone of a rich azure blue color.|The necklace was adorned with lapis lazuli and gold. 这条项链用青金石和黄金装饰。|Synonyms: lazurite, azure stone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
