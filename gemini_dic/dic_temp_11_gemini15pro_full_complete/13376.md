atmospherically:/ˌætməsˈfɪrɪkli/ | adv. | 大气地，气氛上地 |  In a way that relates to the atmosphere of a place or situation, creating a particular mood or feeling. | The restaurant was atmospherically lit with candles.  这家餐厅用蜡烛照亮，营造了很好的氛围。| The film was atmospherically shot in black and white. 这部电影用黑白画面拍摄，很有气氛。 | synonym: evocatively, ambiently, moodily | antonym: plainly, blandly | 词性: 副词
