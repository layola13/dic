manipulative:/məˈnɪpjʊlətɪv/
adjective
控制的；操纵的；善于摆布人的
Exercising unscrupulous control or influence over a person or situation.
She was accused of being cold and manipulative. 她被指责冷漠且爱摆布人。

He's a manipulative bastard. 他是个控制欲极强的混蛋。

controlling, exploitative, scheming
honest, sincere, forthright
adjective
