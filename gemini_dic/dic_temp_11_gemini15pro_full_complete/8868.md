antifeudalists:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪsts/| n. |反封建主义者|People who oppose feudalism, a social system in which land ownership and power are based on a hierarchy of lords and vassals.|The antifeudalists advocated for land reform and greater rights for the peasantry.|Synonyms: reformers, radicals, revolutionaries |Antonyms: feudal lords, conservatives, traditionalists|Noun

antifeudal:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəl/| adj. |反封建的|Opposed to or against feudalism.|The antifeudal movement gained momentum in the 18th century. |Synonyms: anti-hierarchical, egalitarian |Antonyms: feudal, hierarchical|Adjective
