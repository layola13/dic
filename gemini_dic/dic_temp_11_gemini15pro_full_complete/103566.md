john-bullism:/ˈdʒɒn ˈbʊlɪzəm/ | n. | 约翰牛主义；英国式的爱国主义；英国人特有的气质 | John Bullism is a term used to describe the qualities and characteristics typically associated with the English, often including patriotism, bluntness, and a love of tradition.  It embodies the stereotypical image of a conservative, patriotic Englishman. |  John Bullism was evident in the enthusiastic celebrations following the national team's victory.  在国家队获胜后，约翰牛主义在热烈的庆祝活动中显而易见。|  patriotism, nationalism | cosmopolitanism | noun
