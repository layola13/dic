overboldly:/ˌoʊvərˈboʊldli/
adverb|过于大胆地；鲁莽地|In an excessively bold manner; recklessly.|He overboldly challenged the authority of the experienced leader. 他过于大胆地挑战了这位经验丰富的领导者的权威。|She overboldly approached the wild animal. 她鲁莽地靠近那只野生动物。|近义词：recklessly, rashly, audaciously, presumptuously|反义词：cautiously, timidly, hesitantly|词性: 副词
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "over, above") + "bold" (Old English beald, meaning "brave, confident") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
