bedazzles:/bɪˈdæzəlz/
verb
使眼花缭乱；使着迷
to impress or excite someone very much
The fireworks bedazzled the children. 焰火使孩子们眼花缭乱。
Synonyms: dazzle, fascinate, enthrall
Antonyms: bore, tire, disappoint
词性: 动词(verb)
