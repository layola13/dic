avolate:/ˈævəleɪt/| v. |飞走，逃走|To fly away; to escape by flight. |The startled birds avolated from the branches. 受惊的鸟儿从树枝上飞走了。|The thief avolated with the stolen jewels. 小偷带着偷来的珠宝逃走了。|近义词：flee, escape, abscond, depart, fly away|反义词：arrive, land, stay, remain|词性：动词
词源：From Latin *avolātus, past participle of *avolāre (“to fly away”), from ā (“away from”) + volāre (“to fly”).
