cuspated:/ˈkʌspeɪtɪd/| adj.|尖的，有尖头的|Having cusps or points; pointed.|The cuspated leaves of the holly tree are distinctive. 冬青树尖尖的叶子很独特。|近义词：pointed, sharp, acuminate, serrated|反义词:rounded, obtuse, smooth|adjective


cusp:/kʌsp/| n.|尖端，尖头；界限；分界线|A pointed end or apex, especially of a tooth or a crescent; a point of transition between two different states.|The cusp of the wave crashed against the shore.  波浪的尖端拍打着海岸。|近义词：point, apex, tip, vertex, peak, summit, climax, turning point, threshold|反义词:nadir, bottom, trough|noun
