biomorphic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈmɔːrfɪk/
adjective
生物形态的
Resembling or suggesting the forms of living organisms.
The artist is known for her biomorphic sculptures, which evoke the shapes of plants and animals.  这位艺术家以其生物形态的雕塑而闻名，这些雕塑让人联想到动植物的形状。
The building's biomorphic design mimics the curves of a seashell. 这座建筑的生物形态设计模仿了贝壳的曲线。
近义词:organic, lifelike
反义词:geometric, inorganic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From bio- (life) + -morphic (form)
