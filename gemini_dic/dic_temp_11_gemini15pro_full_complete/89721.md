hopeite:/ˈhoʊpaɪt/| n. |磷锌矿，希望石|A hydrous zinc phosphate mineral, Zn₃(PO₄)₂·4H₂O, occurring as colorless to white or light brown prismatic or tabular crystals. It is a secondary mineral formed in the oxidized zone of zinc deposits.|Hopeite is a relatively rare mineral found in oxidized zones of zinc deposits.|近义词:parahopeite (similar mineral)|反义词: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun
