half-barbarian:/ˌhæf bɑːrˈbeəriən/ | adj. n. | 半野蛮人；半开化的人 |  Someone who is partly civilized and partly uncivilized, often suggesting a mixture of refined and rough qualities.  Derived from "half" + "barbarian". | He was described as a half-barbarian, with a love for both fine art and brutal sports.  他被描述为一个半野蛮人，既热爱艺术又热爱野蛮的运动。| Near synonyms: semi-barbarian, semi-civilized | Antonyms: civilized, cultured | adjective, noun
