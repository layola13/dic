half-breed:/ˈhæfˌbriːd/| n.|混血儿；杂种|A person whose parents are of different races or ethnicities, especially when one parent is white or of European descent and the other is Native American or of another non-white group.|Historically, the term was often used in a derogatory way to refer to people of mixed Native American and European ancestry.  Now it is considered offensive and should be avoided. |The old westerns often portrayed half-breeds as outcasts.  (offensive usage) |近义词:mixed-race, biracial, multiracial (preferred terms)|反义词:purebred (in animal breeding contexts, not applicable to humans)|词性:noun

half-breeds:/ˈhæfˌbriːdz/| n.|混血儿；杂种 (复数)|Plural form of half-breed.|See the entry for "half-breed" for a full explanation. |The term "half-breeds" is now considered offensive and should not be used.|近义词:mixed-race individuals, biracial individuals, multiracial individuals (preferred terms)|反义词:purebreds (in animal breeding contexts, not applicable to humans)|词性:noun
