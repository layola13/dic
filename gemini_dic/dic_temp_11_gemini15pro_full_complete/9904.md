ape:/eɪp/| n. vt.|猿，类人猿；模仿|A large primate that lacks a tail, including the gorilla, chimpanzees, orangutan, and gibbons.  To imitate someone or something, often clumsily or ridiculously.|The zoo has several species of apes.  動物園裡有幾種猿。 He was aping the teacher's mannerisms. 他在模仿老師的舉止。|近义词：monkey, primate, mimic, imitate|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 human)|n. vt.
