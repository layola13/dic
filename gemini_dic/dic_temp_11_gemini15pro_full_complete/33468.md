chylaceous:/kaɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
乳糜的，乳糜状的
Resembling chyle; milky.  Of the nature of chyle.
The patient's peritoneal fluid was chylaceous in appearance.  病人的腹膜液呈乳糜状。
近义词: milky, chylous
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "clear" or "transparent" for contrasting appearance)
词性: adjective
