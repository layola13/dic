ambulacriform:/ˌæmbjʊˈleɪkrɪfɔːrm/
| adj. |步廊状的，走廊形的|Shaped like a cloister or covered walkway.|The ancient Roman villa featured an ambulacriform garden. 这座古罗马别墅有一个走廊式的花园。|Near Synonyms: cloister-like, arcade-like, gallery-like|Antonyms: open, exposed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambulacrum* (walk, walkway, promenade) + *-formis* (having the form of).
