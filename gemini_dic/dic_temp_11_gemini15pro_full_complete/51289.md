detachedly:/dɪˈtætʃɪdli/ | adv. | 冷淡地；超然地；客观地 |  In a way that shows a lack of emotional involvement; in a way that suggests separation or disconnection. |  She observed the argument detachedly, as if it had nothing to do with her. 她冷淡地旁观着这场争论，仿佛事不关己。 |  objectively, impartially, aloofly, indifferently, dispassionately |  emotionally, passionately, involvedly, subjectively | adverb

Etymology: From the adjective "detached" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Detached" comes from the French "détacher" meaning "to unfasten, detach," from "de-" (away from) + "attacher" (to attach).
