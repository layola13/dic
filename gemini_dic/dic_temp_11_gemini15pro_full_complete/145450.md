overchills:/ˈoʊvərˌtʃɪlz/ | n. |过度寒冷，过冷|A condition of excessive chilling or coldness, often accompanied by shivering.  It can be caused by prolonged exposure to cold temperatures, or as a side effect of certain medications.  Overchills is not a formally recognized medical term and is more commonly expressed as "feeling very cold" or "experiencing chills."|I spent too long in the icy water and suffered from overchills.|近义词: chills, shivering, coldness|反义词: overheating, feverishness|词性:名词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "chills" (noun meaning sensations of coldness).
