ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *multifarius* "manifold," from *multi-* "many" + *-farius* "times, -fold" (related to the root of *for, faris, fari* "to speak"). The suffix *-ness* turns the adjective into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being multifarious.
