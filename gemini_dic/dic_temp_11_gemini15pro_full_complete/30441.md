centre:/ˈsentər/ (US) /ˈsɛntə/ (UK)| n. v.|中心，中央，中心点；集中|A point or place that is equally distant from all points on the edge of a circle or sphere; the middle of something.|The town centre is always busy on Saturdays.  城镇中心在星期六总是很繁忙。|synonyms: middle, core, heart, hub|antonyms: edge, periphery, boundary|noun
centre:/ˈsentər/ (US) /ˈsɛntə/ (UK)| v.|集中，居中，以…为中心|To place something in the middle of something else; to focus attention or activity on something.|The discussion centred around the company's future.  讨论集中在公司的未来。|synonyms: focus, concentrate, converge|antonyms: disperse, scatter, spread|verb
