freewheeling:/ˌfriːˈwiːlɪŋ/
adjective
自由放任的；随心所欲的；无拘无束的
acting in a relaxed and uncontrolled way, without concern for rules or the consequences of your actions.
He has a freewheeling, casual style. 他有一种自由自在、随意的风格。

近义词:unconstrained, uninhibited, easygoing, relaxed, casual
反义词:constrained, restricted, inhibited, controlled, cautious
词性:adjective


freewheeling:/ˌfriːˈwiːlɪŋ/
noun
自由放任；随心所欲
the act of behaving in a freewheeling way.
The freewheeling of the 1960s gave way to the more conservative 1970s.  60年代的自由放任被更保守的70年代所取代。

近义词:unconstraint, uninhibition, ease, relaxation, casualness
反义词:constraint, restriction, inhibition, control, caution
词性:noun
