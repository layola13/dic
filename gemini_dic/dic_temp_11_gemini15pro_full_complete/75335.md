frostier:/ˈfrɔːstiər (US), ˈfrɒstiər (UK)| adj.|更冷的，更冷淡的|Comparative form of frosty: more frosty.  Characterized by a lower temperature, or exhibiting greater coldness in manner or demeanor.|例句：The reception was frostier than I expected.  招待会比我想象的要冷淡。

例句：It's getting frostier outside. 外面越来越冷了。|近义词：colder, icier, chillier, less friendly, more distant|反义词：warmer, friendlier, more welcoming|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *forstiġ, from frost + -iġ.  The comparative suffix -er derives from Proto-Germanic *-izô.
