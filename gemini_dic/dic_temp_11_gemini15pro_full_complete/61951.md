emulsifier's:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪərz/| n.|乳化剂的|The emulsifier's role is to bind oil and water together. 乳化剂的作用是将油和水结合在一起。|例句：The emulsifier's effectiveness depends on its chemical structure.  乳化剂的有效性取决于其化学结构。

例句：The quality of the emulsifier's performance affects the texture of the ice cream. 乳化剂性能的质量会影响冰淇淋的口感。|近义词：stabilizer, binder|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical function)|词性:Noun (Possessive)


Etymology:
From emulsify + -er + -'s.  Emulsify comes from  emulsion (from Late Latin *emulsionem, from emulgere "to milk out," from ex- "out" + mulgere "to milk") + -ify.
