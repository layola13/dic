cylindricity: /ˌsɪlɪnˈdrɪsɪti/
noun
圆柱度，圆柱性
The degree to which a surface resembles a true cylinder.  It is a measure of how close the cross-section of a cylindrical object is to a perfect circle.
The cylindricity of the piston is crucial for the engine's performance.  活塞的圆柱度对发动机的性能至关重要。

The manufacturer checks the cylindricity of the pipes before shipping. 制造商在发货前检查管道的圆柱度。

Near synonyms: circularity, roundness
Antonyms: non-cylindricity, irregularity
