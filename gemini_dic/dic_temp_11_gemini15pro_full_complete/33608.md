cinchona:/sɪŋˈkoʊnə/ |n.| 金鸡纳树；金鸡纳皮|A South American tree whose bark yields quinine, used to treat malaria.|The cinchona tree is native to the Andes Mountains. 金鸡纳树原产于安第斯山脉.|Synonym:  quinine tree|Antonym: N/A|noun
