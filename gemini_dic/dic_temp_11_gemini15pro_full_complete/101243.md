invitrifiable:/ɪnˌvɪtrɪˈfaɪəbl/
adjective
可玻璃化的；能变成玻璃的
Capable of being converted into glass or a glassy substance by heat and fusion.
This ceramic material is invitrifiable at high temperatures.  这种陶瓷材料在高温下可以玻璃化。
The glaze used on this pottery is invitrifiable, creating a smooth, waterproof surface.  这种陶器上使用的釉料是可玻璃化的，形成了光滑防水的表面。
近义词: vitrifiable, fusible
反义词: non-vitrifiable, infusible
词性: adjective
词源: in- (not) + vitri- (glass) + -fiable (capable of being)  来自拉丁语 vitrum "glass"
