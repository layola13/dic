microcontroller:/ˌmaɪkrəʊkənˈtrəʊlə(r)/ /ˌmaɪkroʊkənˈtroʊlər/| n. |微控制器|A small computer on a single integrated circuit containing a processor core, memory, and programmable input/output peripherals.  It's designed for embedded applications, controlling the functions of a specific device or system.|The microcontroller in this washing machine regulates the wash cycle. 这台洗衣机里的微控制器控制着洗涤周期。|近义词：microprocessor, embedded processor, chip|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "mainframe computer" as a contrasting concept)
词性:noun
