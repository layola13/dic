leister:/ˈliːstər/ | n. | 鱼叉，三叉鱼叉， (苏格兰) 用来烤干谷物或泥炭的装置 | A three-pronged spear for spearing fish, or (in Scotland) an implement for drying grain or peat over a fire. |  The fisherman used a leister to catch salmon. 渔夫用鱼叉捕鲑鱼。| synonyms: spear, trident | antonyms: net | noun

leister:/ˈliːstər/ | v. | 用鱼叉捕鱼，(苏格兰) 用火烤干 | To spear fish with a leister, or (in Scotland) to dry with fire. | He leistered three salmon that day. 那天他用鱼叉捕了三条鲑鱼。| synonyms: spear,  dry | antonyms:  wet | verb
