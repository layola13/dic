mitts:/mɪts/| n.|连指手套，棒球手套|Thick, warm gloves, often without separate fingers, or fingerless gloves with a place for the thumb.|She wore a pair of thick mitts to keep her hands warm in the snow. 她戴着一副厚厚的连指手套在雪地里保暖。|近义词：gloves, mittens|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mittes*, from Old English *mitte* (glove), from Proto-Germanic *mitō* (mitten, glove), from Proto-Indo-European *meyth- (to exchange, change).  The plural form likely arose by analogy with words like *feet* and *teeth*.
