correlatives:/kəˈrɛlətɪvz/ | n. |  关联词；相关物；相关成分 |  Words or phrases that express a mutual or reciprocal relation.  These often come in pairs. | Both *either* and *or* are correlatives, used together to offer a choice between two options. | 近义词: related terms, corresponding elements | 反义词: unrelated elements | 词性: noun

correlative:/kəˈrɛlətɪv/ | adj. |  相关的；关联的；相互依存的 | Having a mutual relationship; corresponding. | The study found a correlative relationship between smoking and lung cancer. | 近义词: related, corresponding, interdependent | 反义词: unrelated, independent | 词性: adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Both *neither* and *nor* are correlatives, used to express negation for two options. = *既不...也不...* 是相关联的词，用于表达对两个选项的否定。

*Whether* or not you decide to come is entirely up to you. = 你是否决定来完全取决于你自己。
