forepossessed:/ˌfɔːrpəˈzɛst/ | adj. |预先占有的；被先占的；(尤指)着迷的，心不在焉的 | preoccupied, obsessed; especially in a way that makes you forget other things | He was so forepossessed with his own thoughts that he didn't hear me call his name. 他太专注于自己的想法，以至于没听到我叫他的名字。 | 近义词: preoccupied, obsessed, absorbed, engrossed | 反义词: attentive, mindful | 词性:形容词

forepossessed:/ˌfɔːrpəˈzɛst/ | v. | (forepossess的过去式和过去分词) |  (archaic) To take possession of beforehand; to preoccupy. |  His mind was forepossessed with anxieties. 他的心中充满了焦虑。 | 近义词: preoccupied, obsessed, consumed | 反义词:  N/A (as a verb, it's archaic and lacks a direct antonym) | 词性:动词
