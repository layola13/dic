client's: /ˈklaɪənts/| n. |客户的，委托人的|The possessive form of "client," indicating ownership or belonging.|例句：The client's needs are our top priority. 客户的需求是我们的首要任务。|近义词：customer's, patron's|反义词: N/A |词性: possessive pronoun/adjective

Etymology: From the Old French "client" and ultimately from the Latin "cliēns," meaning "dependent." The possessive form is created by adding 's.
