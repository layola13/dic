impairable:/ɪmˈpɛrəbl/
adjective
可损害的，可削弱的
able to be impaired or weakened.
Her hearing was impairable due to the loud noise. 她的听力由于巨大的噪音可能受损。
The building's structural integrity was impairable after the earthquake.  地震后，建筑物的结构完整性可能受损。
近义词: damageable, vulnerable, susceptible
反义词: unimpairable, indestructible, invulnerable
词性: adjective
词源: From impair + -able.  Impair comes from Old French empeirer "make worse," from en- "in" + pejorer "make worse," from Latin pejorare "make worse," from pejor "worse."
