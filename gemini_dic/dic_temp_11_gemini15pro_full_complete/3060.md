aerographical:/ˌɛərəˈɡræfɪkəl/| adj.|航空摄影的，航空绘图的|Relating to or produced by aerography, which is the art or process of painting with an airbrush or producing pictures by spraying paint onto a surface using compressed air.  It can also relate to the study of the atmosphere.|The aerographical map showed the wind patterns over the Pacific. 航空地图显示了太平洋上空的风向。|近义词：aerial, atmospheric|反义词：terrestrial|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From aero- (relating to air) + -graphical (relating to writing or drawing).  "Aero-" ultimately comes from the Greek "aer" (air). "-graphical" comes from the Greek "graphikos" (of or for writing), from "graphein" (to write).
