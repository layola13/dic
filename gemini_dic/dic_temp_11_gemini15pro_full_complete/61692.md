emollience:/ɪˈmɒliəns/ /ɪˈmɑːliəns/| n. |柔软；润肤；缓和；(态度、举止的)温和|The lotion has excellent emollient properties that leave skin soft and smooth.  这款乳液具有极佳的润肤特性，使皮肤柔软光滑。|近义词：softness, smoothness, suppleness|反义词：roughness, hardness, stiffness|词性:名词

emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənt/| adj. |使柔软的；润肤的；缓和的|The cream contains several emollient ingredients to soothe dry skin.  这款面霜含有多种润肤成分，可以舒缓干燥的皮肤。|近义词：softening, soothing, moisturizing|反义词：irritating, drying, roughening|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin emollient- ‘making soft’, from the verb emollire, from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out, thoroughly’ + mollire ‘soften’, from mollis ‘soft’.
