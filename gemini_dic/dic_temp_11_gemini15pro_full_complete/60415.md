eightfold:/ˈeɪtfəʊld/| adj. adv.|八倍的，八重的；八方面地|Multiplied by eight; having eight parts.  Origin: Old English *eahtafeald, from eahta (eight) + -feald (fold).|The flowers had eightfold symmetry. 这些花有八重对称。| The profits increased eightfold this year. 今年利润增加了八倍。|近义词:octuple, eight times|反义词:(no direct antonym, consider fractions like one-eighth)|词性:adjective, adverb
