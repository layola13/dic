cringer:/ˈkrɪndʒər/| n.|畏缩的人，尴尬的人，令人尴尬的人或事|Someone who makes other people feel embarrassed or awkward, often by saying or doing something considered pathetic or cringeworthy.  A person who cringes a lot, especially out of embarrassment or awkwardness.  Something that makes someone cringe.|例句：He's such a cringer, always trying too hard to be funny. 他真是个令人尴尬的人，总是拼命想表现得滑稽。

例句：Watching that old video of myself singing karaoke is a total cringer. 看我以前唱卡拉OK的视频真是尴尬得要死。|近义词：embarrassment, awkward person, cringe-maker|反义词:  confident person, charmer|词性:noun
