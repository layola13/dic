filamentous:/ˌfɪləˈmentəs/| adj.|丝状的，纤维状的|Resembling a filament or filaments; threadlike.  Composed of long, thin strands or fibers. |例句：The fungus had a filamentous structure. 这种真菌具有丝状结构。|近义词：fibrous, threadlike, stringy, filiform|反义词:compact, solid|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *filamentum* (thread) + -ous (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *filum* (thread).
