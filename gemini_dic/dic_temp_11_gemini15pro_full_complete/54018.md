卧姿; 躺; 卧位; 在职期
the state of lying down

例句：The president's long discumbency allowed him to implement many policies.  总统的长期在职使他得以实施许多政策。
近义词：recumbency, prostration
反义词：standing, uprightness
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
