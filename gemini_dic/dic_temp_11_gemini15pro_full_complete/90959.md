hurrahs:/həˈrɑːz/ /hʊˈrɑːz/
|n.|欢呼声；喝彩声|A shout of joy or approval.|The crowd erupted in hurrahs as the team scored the winning goal. |队伍攻入制胜一球时，人群爆发出欢呼声。|近义词：cheers, applause, acclamations|反义词：boos, jeers|Noun


Etymology:
From hurrah, an exclamation of joy or approval.  The plural form simply adds an "s." The word "hurrah" itself has uncertain origins, possibly from a Slavic or Turkish source, or perhaps imitative.
