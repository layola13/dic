nougat: /ˈnuːɡɑːt/ (US), /ˈnuːɡæt/ (UK) | n. | 牛轧糖；乳白硬糖 | A confection made with sugar or honey, nuts, and often egg whites. |  I love the chewy texture of nougat in this chocolate bar. 我喜欢这块巧克力棒里牛轧糖的嚼劲。

The nougat was so sticky it got stuck to my teeth.  这牛轧糖太黏了，粘在我牙上了。

| 近义词: confection, candy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From French *nougat*, from Provençal *nogat*, from *noga*, "nut," from Latin *nux* ("nut").
