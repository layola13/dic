espresso:/ɛˈspresoʊ/ (US), /eˈspresəʊ/ (UK)| n. |浓缩咖啡，意大利特浓咖啡|A strong black coffee made by forcing steam through ground coffee beans.|I'll have a double espresso, please. 我要一杯双份浓缩咖啡。|Synonyms: short black, espresso coffee|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "weak coffee" or "Americano" which is diluted espresso)|noun
