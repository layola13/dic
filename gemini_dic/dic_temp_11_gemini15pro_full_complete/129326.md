noise:/nɔɪz/ | v. | 喧闹，大声地说 | to spread a report or rumour.  | He noised the news all over town. 他把这个消息传遍了全城。 | synonyms: publicize, broadcast,  rumor,  spread | antonyms: conceal, suppress, hide | verb
