desensationalizes:/ˌdiːsɛnsˈeɪʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使不耸人听闻；使不引起轰动
To deprive of sensational qualities; to make something seem less exciting or shocking.
The media often desensationalizes tragic events to avoid causing widespread panic. 媒体经常淡化悲剧事件以避免引起广泛恐慌。
The documentary desensationalizes the issue of climate change by presenting scientific facts.  这部纪录片通过呈现科学事实，使气候变化问题不再耸人听闻。
synonyms: downplay, minimize, understate, de-emphasize
antonyms: sensationalize, dramatize, exaggerate, hype
v.
