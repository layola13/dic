insolate:/ˈɪnsəleɪt/ /ˈɪnsoʊleɪt/ | vt.| 曝晒；使暴露于阳光下；|To expose to the rays of the sun.  | The researchers insolated the plants for different periods of time to study the effects of sunlight. 研究人员将植物曝晒在阳光下不同时间段，以研究阳光的影响。

insolate:/ˈɪnsəleɪt/ /ˈɪnsoʊleɪt/ | n.|日射，暴晒|The act of exposing to sunlight.|The prolonged insolate caused the paint to fade. 长时间的日晒导致油漆褪色。

|例句：The insolate on the beach was intense. 沙滩上的日晒很强烈。
|近义词：sunlight, sunshine, irradiation|反义词: shade, shadow|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insolatus*, past participle of *insolare* (“to expose to the sun”), from *in-* (“in, on”) + *sol* (“sun”).
