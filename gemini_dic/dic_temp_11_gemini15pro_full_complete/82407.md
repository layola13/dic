grudger:/ˈɡrʌdʒər/| n.|心怀不满的人，怨恨者|Someone who is unwilling to forgive or forget; someone who bears a grudge.|He's such a grudger; he still hasn't forgiven me for forgetting his birthday last year. 他真是个记仇的人，到现在还没原谅我去年忘了他的生日。|近义词：resentful person, malcontent, sorehead|反义词:forgiving person|词性:名词

grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/| n. vt.|怨恨，妒忌；吝惜，不愿给|n. a feeling of resentment or ill-will against someone or something.  vt. to be unwilling to give or allow something.|n. She held a grudge against him for years. 她对他怀恨在心多年。 vt. He grudged her every penny she earned. 他对她挣的每一分钱都舍不得。|近义词：(n.) resentment, animosity, rancor (vt.) begrudge, resent|反义词:(n.) forgiveness, goodwill (vt.) give freely|词性:名词,动词
