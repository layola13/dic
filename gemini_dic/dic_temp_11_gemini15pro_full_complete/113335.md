luxuriant:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/| adj. | 繁茂的，茂盛的；丰富的，奢华的 |  Her luxuriant hair cascaded down her back. 她茂密的头发像瀑布一样披散在背上。|近义词：abundant, profuse, lush | 反义词：sparse, meager |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *luxuriantia, from Latin luxurians, present participle of luxuriare (“to be luxuriant, to luxuriate”), from luxus (“luxury, excess”).
