| | | | The formal language felt out-of-place in the casual setting. 在这种轻松的场合下，正式的语言显得不太合适。 |  inappropriate, awkward, uncomfortable, unsuitable, misplaced | appropriate, suitable, fitting, proper, relevant | adjective
