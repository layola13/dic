insanity:/ɪnˈsænəti/ /ɪnˈsænɪti/| n. |疯狂，精神错乱，精神失常|The state of being seriously mentally ill; madness.|He suffered from bouts of insanity. 他患有阵发性精神错乱。|近义词：madness, lunacy, dementia, psychosis|反义词：sanity, rationality|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *insanitas, from Latin insanus (“insane”).  The Latin insanus is from in- (“not”) + sanus (“healthy, sane”).
