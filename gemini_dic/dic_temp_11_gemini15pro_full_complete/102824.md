jarosite:/ˈdʒærəˌsaɪt/ | n. | 黄钾铁矾 | A hydrous basic sulfate of potassium and iron, occurring as a yellowish or brownish earthy mineral. | Jarosite is a common mineral found in acid mine drainage. 黄钾铁矾是在酸性矿山排水系统中发现的常见矿物。| Jarosite forms in oxidizing environments rich in iron and sulfur. 黄钾铁矾形成于富含铁和硫的氧化环境中。 | synonyms:  natrojarosite,  | antonyms: (None, as it's a specific mineral) | noun
