malacatune: /ˌmæləˈkəˌtjuːn/ | n. | 馬拉卡通(菲律賓一種音樂類型) | A genre of Filipino folk music traditionally associated with serenading, often played on stringed instruments. |  Example: The band played a beautiful malacatune outside her window.  乐队在她窗外演奏了一首优美的马拉卡通。 | Example:  Malacatune is often heard during town fiestas in the Philippines.  在菲律宾的城镇节日里经常能听到马拉卡通。 | 近义词: harana, kundiman | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
