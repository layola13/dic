groupage:/ˈɡruːpɪdʒ/| n.|拼装货；集装箱拼箱货|The company specializes in groupage services for small shipments.  这家公司专门提供小批量货物的拼箱服务。| Groupage can be a cost-effective way to ship goods internationally. 拼箱货可以是一种经济高效的国际运输方式。|近义词：consolidation, LCL (Less than Container Load)|反义词：FCL (Full Container Load)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *groupage*, from *grouper* "to group," from groupe "group."
