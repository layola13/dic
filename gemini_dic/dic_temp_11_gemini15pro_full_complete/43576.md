creakily:/ˈkriːkɪli/| adv.|吱吱作响地；嘎吱嘎吱地|in a way that produces a creaking sound|The door opened creakily. 门吱呀一声打开了。|Synonym: squeakily, gratingly|Antonym: smoothly, silently|adverb

creak:/kriːk/| n. v.|吱吱作响；嘎吱嘎吱响|a harsh scraping or squeaking sound|The stairs creaked under his weight. 楼梯在他的重压下吱吱作响。|Synonym: squeak, groan|Antonym: silence|noun, verb
