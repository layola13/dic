clan:/klæn/
n.|家族，氏族，部落；(尤指苏格兰的)宗族；(志趣相投的)群体，帮派|A group of families, especially in Scotland, who claim to come from the same ancestor; a large group of people who share the same interests.|The Campbell clan has a long and rich history. 坎贝尔家族有着悠久而丰富的历史。|family, tribe, group|individual|noun
