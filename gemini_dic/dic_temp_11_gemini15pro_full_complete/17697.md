bedabble:/bɪˈdæbəl/
verb
溅湿，浸湿
To wet by splashing or dipping slightly.
The child bedabbled his clothes while playing in the puddles. 孩子在水坑里玩耍时溅湿了衣服。
近义词:splash, dabble, sprinkle
反义词:dry
词性:v.
