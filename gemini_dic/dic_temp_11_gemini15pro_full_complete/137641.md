nonubiquitously:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəsli/
adjective
非普遍地，并非无处不在
Not ubiquitously; not present or existing everywhere.
Specialized sensors are nonubiquitously deployed throughout the environment.  专门的传感器并非普遍部署在整个环境中。
The species is nonubiquitously distributed across the continent.  该物种并非遍布整个大陆。
近义词: rarely, infrequently, sporadically, locally
反义词: ubiquitously, everywhere, universally, globally
词性: adjective


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
ubiquitous (adjective):  From Latin ubique "everywhere," from ubi "where."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
