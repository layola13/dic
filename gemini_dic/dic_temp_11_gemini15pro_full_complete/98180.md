inscrutable:/ɪnˈskruːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可思议的，难以理解的，神秘莫测的 | Impossible to understand or interpret. |  Her face remained inscrutable, giving nothing away. 她的表情依然难以捉摸，什么也没有透露。

|  The motives behind the crime were inscrutable. 这起犯罪背后的动机令人费解。 | 近义词: enigmatic, mysterious, impenetrable, unfathomable | 反义词: transparent, obvious, clear, comprehensible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inscrutabilis, from in- "not" + scrutari "to search, examine," from scruta "trash, rags, lumber," of uncertain origin.  Essentially meaning "not able to be searched into."
