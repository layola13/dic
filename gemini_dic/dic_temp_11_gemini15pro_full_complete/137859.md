nonviral:/nɒnˈvaɪrəl/ | adj. | 非病毒性的 | Not caused by or related to a virus; not involving a virus. |  This is a nonviral infection.  这是一种非病毒性感染。| Synonyms: abiotic, noninfectious | Antonyms: viral | Adjective

Etymology:
From non- + viral. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "viral" refers to something related to or caused by a virus.
