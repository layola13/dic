enthusiasm:/ɪnˈθuziˌæzəm/ (US) /ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm/ (UK)| n.|热情，热忱|Intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval.|She approached the task with enthusiasm. 她满怀热情地投入到这项任务中。|近义词：passion, zeal, fervor, eagerness|反义词：apathy, indifference, lethargy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin enthusiasmus, from Greek enthousiasmos "divine inspiration,  enthusiastic frenzy," from entheos "inspired, possessed by a god,"  from en- "in" + theos "god."
