ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *klakken, frequentative of *klaken (“to crack, clap”), from Proto-Germanic *klakōną (“to clack, clatter”). Cognate with Dutch klakken (“to clack”), German klachen (“to clack, clap”), West Frisian klakje (“to clack, clatter”).
