gargoyle: /ˈɡɑːrˌɡɔɪl/ (US) /ˈɡɑːɡɔɪl/ (UK)| n.|滴水嘴兽，怪兽状的雕饰|A carved stone figure, often grotesque, projecting from a roof gutter to throw rainwater clear of a building.|The cathedral is adorned with numerous gargoyles. 这座大教堂装饰着许多滴水嘴兽。|Near synonyms: grotesque, chimera, monster|Antonyms: beauty, grace|noun
