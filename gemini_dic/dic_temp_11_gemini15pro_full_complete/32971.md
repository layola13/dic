chore:/tʃɔːr/ /tʃɔr/| n.|家务杂事；令人厌烦的任务|A routine task, especially a domestic one. |例句：Doing the laundry is my least favorite chore. 洗衣服是我最不喜欢的家务。

例句：Mowing the lawn is a weekly chore.  修剪草坪是每周的家务。


近义词：task, duty, job, assignment|反义词:fun, entertainment, pleasure|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chore*, from Old English *cerr*, *cierr* (“turn, time, occasion”), from Proto-Germanic *karją*, *kariją* (“care, worry, anxiety”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰer- (“to grasp, enclose”). Cognate with Scots *chare* (“turn of work”), Dutch *karwei* (“chore”). More at care.
