appendices:/əˈpɛndɪsiːz/
n.
附录，附属物
supplementary material at the end of a book, article, document, etc.
This book has two appendices containing tables of data. 这本书有两个附录，包含数据表。

appendices:/əˈpɛndɪsiːz/
n.
(解剖学)阑尾
a tube-shaped sac attached to and opening into the lower end of the large intestine in humans and some other mammals
Appendicitis is an inflammation of the appendix. 阑尾炎是阑尾的炎症。

近义词: supplements, additions, annexes, attachments
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "main body" or "core text" in specific contexts)
词性: noun (plural)
