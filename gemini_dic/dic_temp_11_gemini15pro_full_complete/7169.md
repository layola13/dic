angerless:/ˈæŋɡərlɪs/
adj.
无怒的，没有愤怒的
Without anger; not angry.
He maintained an angerless composure throughout the ordeal. 他在整个考验中都保持着一种没有愤怒的平静。
近义词：calm, peaceful, serene, tranquil
反义词：angry, furious, enraged, wrathful
词性: 形容词

词源:  anger (n.) + -less (suffix).  "Anger" comes from Old Norse "angr" meaning grief, pain, anguish. The suffix "-less" means "without" and is of Old English origin.
