booklets:/ˈbʊklɪts/
n.
小册子，手册
A small book, often with a paper cover, containing information or advertising.
The tourist office provided booklets about local attractions. 旅游局提供了当地景点的宣传册。
pamphlets, brochures, leaflets
books, volumes
noun
