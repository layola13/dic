hermitlike: /ˈhɜːmɪtlaɪk/ | adj. | 像隐士的；遁世的 | Resembling or characteristic of a hermit; solitary and reclusive. | He lived a hermitlike existence in the remote mountains. 他在偏远的山区过着隐居般的生活。 |  The old cabin had a hermitlike quality, tucked away deep in the woods.  这间古老的小屋有一种隐士般的气质，隐藏在树林深处。| 近义词: reclusive, solitary, secluded, withdrawn | 反义词: gregarious, sociable, outgoing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From hermit + -like.  "Hermit" comes from the Old French "ermite," ultimately from the Greek "erēmitēs," meaning "desert-dweller." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
