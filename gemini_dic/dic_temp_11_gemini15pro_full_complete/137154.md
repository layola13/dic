Etymology:
From non- + subtlety + -ness + -es (plural).  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Subtlety" comes from the Old French "sotilleté," ultimately from the Latin "subtilis," meaning "fine, thin, delicate." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state. "-es" marks the plural form.
