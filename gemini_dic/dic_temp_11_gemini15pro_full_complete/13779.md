audio-active:/ˈɔːdi.oʊˌæktɪv/| adj.|音频激活的，声音激活的|Activated by sound; reacting to sound.  A device that becomes operational when it detects sound.|The audio-active security system turned on when it detected the sound of breaking glass.|近义词：sound-activated, sound-sensitive|反义词：inactive, passive|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "audio-" comes from Latin "audire" (to hear). "-active" signifies "capable of action" or "in a state of action," deriving ultimately from Latin "actus" (an act).
