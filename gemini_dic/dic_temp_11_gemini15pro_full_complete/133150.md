nonethic:/nɒnˈɛθɪk/
adjective
非伦理的；与伦理无关的
Not pertaining to ethics; not involving questions of right and wrong; not moral.
Example: The scientist's nonethic experiments on animals caused public outcry.  这位科学家在动物身上进行的非伦理实验引起了公众的强烈抗议。

Example:  The company's nonethic business practices eventually led to its downfall.  该公司不道德的商业行为最终导致了它的垮台。
Synonym: unethical, amoral, immoral
Antonym: ethical, moral
词性: 形容词
