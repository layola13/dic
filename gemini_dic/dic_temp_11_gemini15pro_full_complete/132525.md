nondetrimentally:/ˌnɒndɛtrɪˈmɛntəli/
adv. |无害地，不妨碍地|Without causing harm or damage; in a way that does not have a negative impact. |例句：The project will be carried out nondetrimentally to the environment.  该项目将以不损害环境的方式进行。|例句：The company operates nondetrimentally to the interests of its employees.  该公司以不损害员工利益的方式运营。|近义词：harmlessly, safely, innocuously|反义词：detrimentally, harmfully|词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
detriment (noun): meaning "harm or damage" 来自拉丁语 detrimentum,  "loss, damage"
-al (suffix): forms adjectives from nouns
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
