anticommercial:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃl/
| adj. |反商业的，反对商业化的|Opposed to or hostile towards commercial principles or practices; not motivated by profit. | The artist's anticommercial stance meant they refused to sell their work through galleries.  这位艺术家的反商业立场意味着他们拒绝通过画廊出售他们的作品。|  The festival had a distinctly anticommercial vibe, prioritizing community and artistic expression over profit.  这个节日有一种明显的反商业氛围，优先考虑社区和艺术表达而不是利润。| synonyms: noncommercial, uncommercial, counter-commercial | antonyms: commercial, pro-business, mercenary | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + commercial.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Commercial" comes from the Late Latin "commercialis," meaning "relating to trade."
