fireable:/ˈfaɪərəbl/| adj.|可解雇的，可被开除的|Subject to dismissal from a job.  Able to be fired.|例句：His behavior was so egregious that he was immediately fireable. 他的行为如此恶劣，以至于他立即被解雇。

例句：The company's new policy makes it easier for employees to be fireable for minor infractions. 公司的新政策使得员工更容易因轻微违规而被解雇。|近义词：dismissible, terminable|反义词：indispensable, tenured|词性:形容词
