irony:/ˈaɪrəni/ | n. |反讽，讽刺；具有讽刺意味的事|a literary technique or event in which what appears, on the surface, to be the case, differs radically from what is actually the case.|She described the irony of the situation with a wry smile. 她苦笑着描述了情况的讽刺意味。|synonyms: sarcasm, satire, paradox|antonyms: sincerity, seriousness|noun

ironic:/aɪˈrɒnɪk/ | adj. |讽刺的，挖苦的|characterized by irony; conveying a meaning that is the opposite of its literal meaning.|It's ironic that he became a safety inspector after causing so many accidents.  具有讽刺意味的是，在他造成了这么多事故之后，他竟然成了安全检查员。|synonyms: sarcastic, satirical, sardonic|antonyms: sincere, earnest|adjective
