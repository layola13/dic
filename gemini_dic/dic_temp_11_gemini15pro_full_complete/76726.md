gambol:/ˈɡæmbəl/ (US), /ˈɡæmbɒl/ (UK)| vi. n.|雀跃；嬉戏；跳跃|To run or jump about playfully.|The lambs gamboled in the spring meadow. 羊羔在春天的草地上嬉戏。|The children's gambols in the park were a joy to watch. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍，令人赏心悦目。|近义词：frisk, frolic, cavort, play|反义词：trudge, plod|词性：动词(vi.), 名词(n.)
