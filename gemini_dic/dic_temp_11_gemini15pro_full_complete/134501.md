nonlegumes:/nɒnˈleɡjuːmz/ /nɑːnˈleɡjuːmz/
n.
非豆科植物
Plants that are not legumes, meaning they do not belong to the Fabaceae (or Leguminosae) family.  They do not form a symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria in their roots.
Farmers often rotate nonlegumes with legumes to maintain soil fertility.  农民经常将非豆科植物与豆科植物轮作以保持土壤肥力。
synonyms: nonleguminous plants
antonyms: legumes, leguminous plants
noun
