dropping:/ˈdrɒpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpɪŋ/ (US)| n. v. adj.|掉落，下降; 下垂物; 粪便|The act of falling or letting something fall. Can also refer to animal droppings (feces).  As an adjective, it can describe something that is falling or hanging down.|
例句：The dropping of the bomb marked a turning point in the war.  炸弹的投下标志着战争的转折点。

例句：The bird left its droppings on the car.  鸟在车上留下了粪便。

近义词：falling, descending, sinking, plummeting,  excrement, dung (for animal droppings)|
反义词：rising, ascending, climbing, soaring|词性:Noun, Verb (present participle), Adjective

例句：With a dropping heart, she realized she had lost the race. 她心灰意冷地意识到自己输掉了比赛。

例句：The dropping branches of the willow tree swayed in the breeze. 柳树低垂的枝条在微风中摇曳。

近义词：falling, descending, hanging, pendulous|
反义词：rising, ascending|词性:Adjective


词源: From Old English *droppian, frequentative of *dreopan "to drip, fall in drops"
