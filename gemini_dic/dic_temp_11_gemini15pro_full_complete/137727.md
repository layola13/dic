nonvagrant:/ˌnɒnˈveɪɡrənt/
adjective
非流浪的；定居的；不漂泊的
Not wandering; settled.
The nonvagrant population of the town has increased steadily over the past decade.  这个镇的定居人口在过去十年里稳步增长。
The study focused on the nonvagrant bird species of the region. 这项研究的重点是该地区的非迁徙鸟类。
synonym:settled, resident, established, permanent
antonym:vagrant, nomadic, migratory, wandering
词性:adjective
