mis-sell:/ˌmɪsˈsel/
verb
误售；不当销售
to sell something in a dishonest or misleading way, or to sell someone something unsuitable
例句：The bank mis-sold pensions to thousands of customers. 这家银行向成千上万的客户误售了养老金。

例句：They were accused of mis-selling financial products. 他们被指控不当销售金融产品。

近义词：deceive, defraud, swindle
反义词：honestly sell
词性：verb
