banalizing:/bəˈnælˌaɪzɪŋ/
verb|使...变得陈腐；使...变得平庸|The overuse of clichés can banalize even the most original ideas.  过度使用陈词滥调甚至会使最原始的想法变得陈腐。|The constant media exposure banalized the tragedy. 持续的媒体曝光使这场悲剧变得平庸。|trivializing, vulgarizing, commonizing|ennobling, elevating, dignifying|v.

Etymology:
From banal + -ize + -ing. Banal comes from French, ultimately from Old French banel "communal, pertaining to compulsory feudal service," hence "commonplace, trivial."
