ascospore: /ˈæskəˌspɔːr/ | n. | 子囊孢子 | A sexual spore formed within an ascus (a sac-like structure) in ascomycete fungi. | The ascospores are released from the ascus and dispersed by wind or water. 子囊孢子从子囊中释放出来，并通过风或水传播。 |  The scientist examined the ascospores under the microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查子囊孢子。 | synonym: spore | antonym: N/A | noun
