megaparsecs:/ˈmɛɡəˌpɑːrˌsɛks/ | n. | 百万秒差距 | A unit of distance equal to one million parsecs, used in astronomy.  (One parsec is approximately 3.26 light-years.) |  The Andromeda Galaxy is located about 2.537 megaparsecs from Earth. 仙女座星系距离地球约2.537百万秒差距。| synonyms:  (none - it's a specific unit) | antonyms:  (none - it's a unit of measurement) | noun
