debases:/dɪˈbeɪsɪz/
verb
降低…的价值；贬低；使堕落
to reduce the quality or value of something
例句：
The politician debased himself by accepting bribes.  这位政客因受贿而降低了自己的身份。

The counterfeit money debased the currency.  伪钞降低了货币的价值。


近义词：degrade, cheapen, corrupt, demean, lower, adulterate, deteriorate
反义词：enhance, elevate, improve, upgrade, enrich
词性：v.
