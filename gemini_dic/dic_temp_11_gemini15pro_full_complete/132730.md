nondivisive:/ˌnɒndɪˈvaɪsɪv/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈvaɪsɪv/
adjective
非分裂的；没有分歧的
Not tending to cause disagreement or dissension.
例句：The candidate’s nondivisive stance on the issue helped him gain broad support. 候选人在这个问题上的非分裂立场帮助他获得了广泛的支持。

例句：The community sought a nondivisive solution to the zoning dispute. 社区寻求一个不会引起分歧的解决方案来解决分区纠纷。

近义词：unifying, conciliatory, harmonious, uniting
反义词：divisive, polarizing, controversial, contentious
词性：adjective
