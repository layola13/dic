barb:/bɑːrb/ | n. | 倒钩，倒刺，尖刻的话 | A sharp projection pointing backward, as on a fishhook or arrow; a critical remark. | The fishhook has a barb to prevent the fish from escaping. 鱼钩上有倒刺，以防止鱼逃脱。| Synonyms: point, spike, prickle, sneer, gibe | Antonyms: compliment, praise | noun

barb:/bɑːrb/ | v. | 装倒钩，说尖刻的话 | To furnish with a barb or barbs; to speak sharply or critically to. | He barbed the wire with small pieces of metal. 他用小块金属给铁丝装上倒刺。| Synonyms: point, sharpen, criticize, taunt | Antonyms: blunt, soften, praise, commend | verb
