-arily:/ərɪli/ | adv. | -地，-式地  |  A suffix forming adverbs from adjectives ending in -ary. It indicates manner or characteristic. | She acted arbitrarily. 她行事武断。| 近义词: -ly | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 后缀

词源:
From Middle English -arily, from Old French -arielement, from Latin -āriē, ablative singular of -ārius.
