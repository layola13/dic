imposturage:/ɪmˈpɒstʃərɪdʒ/ | n. | 冒充；欺骗；赝品 | The act of pretending to be someone else in order to deceive; something that is not genuine. | He was eventually discovered, and his imposturage was revealed. 他最终被发现，他的欺骗行为被揭露了。| The painting was an obvious imposturage, a cheap copy of a masterpiece.  这幅画显然是赝品，一件名作的廉价复制品。| 近义词: deception, fraud, sham, counterfeit | 反义词: genuineness, authenticity | noun
