labials:/ˈleɪbiəlz/| n.| 唇音；唇音字母|Sounds and letters produced primarily using the lips.  These sounds are formed by obstructing airflow with the lips or by using the lips to modify airflow.|The sounds /p/, /b/, and /m/ are labials.|近义词:bilabial consonants|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider sounds produced with other parts of the mouth like "velars" or "dentals")|词性:noun
