cue-up:/kjuː ˈʌp/ | v. |准备播放; 安排;  | To prepare something, especially a piece of music or video, to be played or broadcast. To arrange for something to happen. | The DJ cued up the next song. DJ准备播放下一首歌。|  The director cued up the next scene. 导演安排了下一个场景。 |  prepare, set up, arrange |  delay, postpone | verb

cue-up:/kjuː ʌp/ | n. | (准备播放的)提示;  | A signal to begin something, especially a piece of music or video. The act of preparing something to be played or broadcast. | The band waited for their cue-up before starting to play. 乐队等待提示后再开始演奏。| The cue-up for the next commercial break was missed. 下一个广告时段的提示错过了。|  signal, prompt, cue | delay | noun
