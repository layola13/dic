insupportability:/ˌɪnsəˌpɔːrtəˈbɪləti/
n.
无法忍受；难以支撑；不能支持
The insupportability of the working conditions led to a strike. 工作条件无法忍受，导致了罢工。
The report detailed the insupportability of the current economic model.  报告详细说明了当前经济模式的不可持续性。
近义词:intolerableness, untenableness, unendurableness
反义词:supportability, tolerability, sustainability
词性:名词

insupportable:/ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbl/
adj.
无法忍受的；难以支撑的；不能支持的
The heat was insupportable, forcing us to stay indoors.  酷热难耐，迫使我们待在室内。
Her grief was insupportable after the loss of her child.  失去孩子后，她的悲痛难以承受。
近义词:intolerable, unbearable, unendurable
反义词:supportable, tolerable, bearable
词性:形容词
