liquefiable:/lɪkwɪˈfaɪəbl/
adjective
可液化的
Capable of being liquefied; convertible to a liquid state.
Natural gas is liquefiable under pressure at very low temperatures.  天然气在低温高压下可以液化。
The air is liquefiable at -190. At this temperature, it turns into a bluish liquid. 空气在-190℃时是可液化的。在这个温度下，它会变成一种蓝色液体。
synonyms:meltable, fusible
antonyms:non-liquefiable, solid, unmeltable
词性:adjective
