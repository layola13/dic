blacklight:/ˈblækˌlaɪt/| n. adj.|黑光灯；紫外线灯；|A lamp that emits ultraviolet light, causing fluorescent substances to glow.|例句：The nightclub was decorated with blacklights that made the white clothes glow.  夜总会里装饰着黑光灯，让白色的衣服发光。

例句：They used a blacklight to detect counterfeit money. 他们用黑光灯来检测假币。|近义词：ultraviolet light, UV light|反义词:normal light, visible light|词性:名词, 形容词

blacklight:/ˈblækˌlaɪt/|adj.|荧光的；|Appearing brightly fluorescent under ultraviolet light.|例句：The blacklight poster glowed in the dark room.  黑光海报在黑暗的房间里发光。
