felicitousnesses:/fɪˌlɪsɪˈtʌsnəsɪz/
|n.|恰当，贴切；幸福，快乐|The state or quality of being felicitous; appropriateness; aptness.  The plural form of felicitousness. |His felicitousnesses of phrase charmed the audience. 他措辞的贴切令观众着迷。|近义词：aptness, appropriateness, suitability, fitness|反义词：inaptness, inappropriateness, unsuitability|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From felicitous + -ness.  Felicitous comes from Late Latin fēlīcitōs (“happy, fortunate”), from Latin fēlīx (“happy, fruitful, lucky, fortunate”).
