loiterers:/ˈlɔɪtərərz/
noun
闲逛者；游荡者
People who linger or stand around idly without any apparent purpose.
The police moved the loiterers away from the shop front. 警察将闲逛者赶离了店面。
loafers, idlers, lingerers, dawdlers
workers, busybodies
n.
