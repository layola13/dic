-mosis:/ˈmoʊsɪs/| n.|渗透，渗透作用|A process of osmosis allows water to pass through the membrane. 渗透作用允许水穿过膜。|Osmosis is the movement of water molecules across a selectively permeable membrane. 渗透是水分子穿过选择性渗透膜的运动。|近义词：diffusion, permeation|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用阻碍渗透的词语，例如 blockage)|词性:名词
