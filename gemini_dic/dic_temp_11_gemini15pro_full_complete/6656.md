anaglyptograph:/ˌænəˈɡlɪptəɡræf/| n. |雕刻画，浮雕|A picture or design carved in low relief.  A process of producing stereoscopic effects by printing two slightly different images in two complementary colors (such as red and cyan) and viewing them through correspondingly colored filters.|The museum displayed an anaglyptograph of the Roman forum.|近义词：carving, relief, embossment, bas-relief|反义词:intaglio|noun
