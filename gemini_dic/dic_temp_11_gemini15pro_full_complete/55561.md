distrustless:/dɪsˈtrʌstləs/
adjective
无猜忌的; 信任的
free from distrust; trustful
例句：Children are naturally distrustless. 孩子们天生容易相信人。

例句：In a distrustless society, people feel safe and secure.  在一个没有猜忌的社会里，人们感到安全和有保障。

近义词：trusting, confiding, unsuspicious, naive, innocent
反义词：distrustful, suspicious, wary, cautious, skeptical
词性: adjective

词源:  "distrustless" comes from "distrust" + "-less".  "Distrust" originates from the Old French word "des-" (meaning "away" or "opposite") and "trust". The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."
