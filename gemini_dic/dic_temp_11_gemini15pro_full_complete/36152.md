coextension:/ˌkoʊɪkˈstɛnʃən/ | n. |共同外延，同延|The state of having the same extension (referring to concepts or predicates that apply to the same objects or individuals).  Two properties are coextensive if they apply to exactly the same class of objects.|The properties of being a "cordate" (heart-shaped) and being a "reniform" (kidney-shaped) leaf are coextensive in some plants.  某些植物的心形叶和肾形叶这两个特性是同延的。|Synonym: coextensiveness|Antonym: non-coextension|Noun
