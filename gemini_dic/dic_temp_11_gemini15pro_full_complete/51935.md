diabetic:/ˌdaɪəˈbetɪk/ | adj.  n. |糖尿病的，患糖尿病的；糖尿病患者|Relating to or having diabetes, a disease in which the body cannot properly control the amount of sugar in the blood.|例句：He is diabetic and has to watch his sugar intake. 他患有糖尿病，必须注意糖的摄入量。|例句：Diabetic complications can be serious. 糖尿病并发症可能很严重。|近义词：hyperglycemic|反义词：non-diabetic|词性:形容词

diabetic:/ˌdaɪəˈbetɪk/ | n. |糖尿病患者|A person who has diabetes.|例句：The clinic provides support for diabetics.  这家诊所为糖尿病患者提供支持。|例句：Diabetics need to monitor their blood sugar levels regularly. 糖尿病患者需要定期监测血糖水平。|近义词：sufferer of diabetes|反义词：N/A (Since it's a noun referring to a person, there isn't a direct antonym)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diabeticus*, from Greek *diabētēs* "diabetes," from *diabaínein* "to pass through," from *dia-* "through" + *baínein* "to go, walk."  The original sense referred to the excessive urination characteristic of the disease.
