latescence:/ləˈtɛsns/ |n.| 潜伏期; 隐伏期 |The latescence period of the virus is about two weeks. 这种病毒的潜伏期大约是两周。|The disease has a long latescence before symptoms appear. 这种疾病在症状出现之前有一个很长的潜伏期。|近义词：incubation, latency, dormancy|反义词：manifestation, expression|词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *latescentia, from latescere "to become hidden," from latere "to lie hidden, lurk," from PIE root *lādh- "to hide."
