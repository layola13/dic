ETYMOLOGY->
From German Krebs, meaning "cancer," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *krebit-, related to *krab- "to scratch, crawl."  The connection likely stems from the appearance of cancerous growths, which can resemble a crab with its legs extending outwards.
