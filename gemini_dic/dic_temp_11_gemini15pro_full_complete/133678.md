nonfraternity:/ˌnɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnəti/
adjective
非兄弟会的；不属于兄弟会的
Not belonging to or associated with a fraternity.
He chose to live in nonfraternity housing. 他选择住在非兄弟会宿舍。
The nonfraternity students organized their own social events. 非兄弟会的学生们组织了自己的社交活动。
Near Synonyms: independent, unaffiliated
Antonyms: fraternal, fraternity
词性: adjective
