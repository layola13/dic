disgustful:/dɪsˈɡʌstfəl/| adj.|令人厌恶的，令人作呕的|Causing disgust; extremely unpleasant.  |The smell of the rotting food was disgustful. 腐烂食物的气味令人作呕。|近义词：revolting, repulsive, sickening, nauseating, abhorrent, loathsome|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, delightful, appealing|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From disgust + -ful.  Disgust comes from the Old French desgouster, meaning "to distaste," ultimately from Latin gustus "taste."
