ford:/fɔːrd/ | n. | 浅滩，涉水处 | A shallow place in a river or stream that allows one to walk or drive across. | We crossed the river at a ford. 我们在浅滩处过河。| 近义词：crossing, drift | 反义词: deep | 名词

ford:/fɔːrd/ | vt. | 涉水，渡过 | To cross a river or stream at a shallow place. |  The pioneers forded the river with their wagons.  拓荒者们用马车渡过了河。| 近义词：traverse, wade | 反义词: bridge, avoid | 动词
