coediting:/koʊˈɛdɪtɪŋ/| n. |共同编辑; 协同编辑|The process of two or more people editing a document or project together.|Example: Coediting a document online allows for real-time collaboration. 在线共同编辑文档可以实现实时协作。|Example:  The coediting feature of this software is very useful for team projects. 这个软件的协同编辑功能对团队项目非常有用。|Synonyms: collaborative editing, joint editing|Antonyms: solo editing, independent editing|noun

coedit:/koʊˈɛdɪt/| v. |共同编辑; 协同编辑|To edit a document or project together with one or more other people.|Example: We coedited the article for the school newspaper. 我们共同编辑了校报的文章。|Example: They are coediting a book on the history of film. 他们正在合著一本关于电影史的书。|Synonyms: collaboratively edit, jointly edit|Antonyms: solely edit, independently edit|verb
