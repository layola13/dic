autogamous:/ɔːˈtɒɡəməs/| adj.|自花授粉的；自体受精的|Of or relating to autogamy; self-fertilizing.|例句：Autogamous plants can reproduce without external pollinators.  自花授粉植物可以在没有外部授粉媒介的情况下繁殖。

例句：The autogamous nature of this species allows it to thrive in isolated environments.  该物种的自花授粉特性使其能够在孤立的环境中繁衍生息。|近义词：self-fertilizing, self-pollinating|反义词：allogamous, cross-pollinating|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From auto- (self) + -gamous (marrying), from Greek gamos (marriage).
