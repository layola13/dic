outburst:/ˈaʊtbɜːrst/| n.|爆发，迸发；突发|A sudden, violent expression of feeling or activity.|例句：Her outburst of anger surprised everyone. 她的愤怒爆发让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：There was an outburst of cheering when the team scored. 当球队得分时，爆发出一阵欢呼声。|近义词：eruption, explosion, burst, fit|反义词：calm, restraint|词性:名词

词源:  From out- + burst.  "Burst" itself comes from the Old English *berstan and is related to the German "bersten." The prefix "out-" intensifies the meaning, indicating a bursting forth or outward.
