endoglossic:/ˌɛndoʊˈɡlɒsɪk/ | adj. | 内部的，内在的；(指语言)只在一个社群内使用的 |  Relating to a language or dialect used within a particular community or group, especially one without external contact. It describes a language situation where the primary language used within a community is distinct from the languages spoken in surrounding areas.  |  The isolated tribe developed an endoglossic language that was unintelligible to neighboring groups. 这个孤立的部落发展了一种内在的语言，邻近的群体都无法理解。 | Synonyms: internal, indigenous, native | Antonyms: exoglossic, external, foreign | adjective
