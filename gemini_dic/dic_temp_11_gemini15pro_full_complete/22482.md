bookselling: /ˈbʊkˌsɛlɪŋ/
n.
书籍销售；书商行业
The act or business of selling books.
Waterstones is a major player in UK bookselling.  华特斯通是英国图书销售行业的主要参与者。

The rise of online bookselling has impacted traditional bookstores. 在线图书销售的兴起影响了传统书店。
近义词：book trade, book distribution
反义词：book buying
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From book + selling (present participle of sell).  由 book + selling（sell 的现在分词）构成.
