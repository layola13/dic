outbow:/ˌaʊtˈbaʊ/
verb
超过…的弧度; 弧度大于
to surpass in bowing; to exceed in curvature
The new ship outbowed all others in the harbor. 这艘新船的弧度超过了港口里的所有其他船只。
outcurve, surpass
--- (no clear antonym, could be "be less curved than")
