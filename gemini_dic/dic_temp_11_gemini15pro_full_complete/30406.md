centipede:/ˈsɛntɪpiːd/ (US) /ˈsɛntɪpiːd/ (UK)| n. |蜈蚣|A predatory arthropod with a flattened, elongated body composed of numerous segments, each bearing one pair of legs. |例句：I saw a centipede crawling across the floor. 我看到一只蜈蚣在地板上爬行。

例句：Centipedes are beneficial in gardens as they eat other insects. 蜈蚣在花园里是有益的，因为它们吃其他昆虫。

近义词：chilopod|反义词: None (no direct opposite for an animal)|词性:noun
