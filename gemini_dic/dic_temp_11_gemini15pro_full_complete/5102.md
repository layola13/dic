allective:/əˈlɛktɪv/
adjective
引诱的，吸引人的
Having the power to attract or allure.
The allective power of her smile drew everyone in. 她迷人的微笑吸引了所有人。
近义词: attractive, alluring, enticing, captivating
反义词: repulsive, repellent, unappealing
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *allectivus, from allectus, past participle of allicere "to entice, allure," from ad- "to" + lacere "to entice, deceive."
