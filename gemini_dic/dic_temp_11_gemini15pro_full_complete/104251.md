jury:/ˈdʒʊəri/ (US), /ˈdʒʊːri/ (UK)| n.|陪审团；评判委员会|A group of people sworn to give a verdict in a legal case on the basis of evidence submitted to them in court.|The jury found the defendant guilty. 陪审团认定被告有罪。|synonyms: panel, committee, board|antonyms: defendant, plaintiff|noun

jury:/ˈdʒʊəri/ (US), /ˈdʒʊːri/ (UK)| n.|评判委员会；全体评委|A group of people who judge entries in a competition, etc.|The jury awarded the first prize to a young artist. 评委会将一等奖授予了一位年轻的艺术家。|synonyms: judges, panel, assessors|antonyms: contestant, competitor|noun
