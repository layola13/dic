nonlegato:/nɒn leˈɡɑːtoʊ/ | adj. | 非连奏的，断奏的 | Not legato; disconnected; staccato.  Characterized by the absence of legato, with notes detached from one another. | The pianist played the piece nonlegato, creating a crisp, percussive effect. 钢琴家以非连奏的方式演奏这首曲子，营造出清晰的打击乐效果。|  Synonyms: detached, staccato, disconnected | Antonyms: legato, connected, smooth | adjective

nonlegato:/nɒn leˈɡɑːtoʊ/ | adv. | 非连奏地，断奏地 | In a nonlegato manner; disconnectedly. | He played the melody nonlegato, emphasizing each individual note. 他以非连奏的方式演奏了旋律，强调了每个单独的音符。| Synonyms: detachedly, staccato, disconnectedly | Antonyms: legato, smoothly, connectedly | adverb
