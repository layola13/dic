manlike:/ˈmænlaɪk/| adj.|男子气的，像男人的|Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with men, such as strength, courage, or assertiveness.|He had a manlike grip on her arm. 他紧紧地抓住她的胳膊，很有男子气概。|近义词：masculine, manly, virile|反义词：feminine, womanly|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *manlich, equivalent to man +‎ -like.
