nonattributiveness:/ˌnɑːnəˈtrɪbjətɪvnəs/ | n. | 不可归属性 | The quality or state of not being attributable.  It refers to a word or phrase that cannot be used as an attributive adjective, i.e., it cannot modify a noun directly. | The nonattributiveness of the word "asleep" means you can't say "an asleep child," but rather "a child asleep." |近义词: unattributability |反义词: attributiveness | 词性: noun
