kofta:/ˈkɒftə/ (UK), /ˈkoʊftə/ (US)| n.|烤肉丸，肉饼|A spiced meatball or meatloaf, usually made with ground meat (often lamb or beef) and onions, and served on skewers or in a sauce.|例句：We had lamb koftas with a yogurt dip. 我们吃了蘸酸奶的羊肉烤肉丸。

例句：She made delicious vegetarian koftas using lentils and vegetables. 她用扁豆和蔬菜做了美味的素食烤肉丸。|近义词：meatball, kebab, patty|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
