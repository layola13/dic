dynamization:/ˌdaɪnəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |赋能，激活，动态化|The process of making something more dynamic or active; the act of injecting energy or vigor into something. |The dynamization of the classroom through interactive technology has significantly improved student engagement. 通过互动技术赋能课堂，极大地提高了学生的参与度。

The dynamization of the local economy is crucial for sustainable development.  激活地方经济对于可持续发展至关重要。 |近义词：activation, energization, invigoration|反义词：suppression, stagnation|词性: noun

Etymology: From French *dynamisation*, from *dynamiser* "to dynamize", from Ancient Greek *δύναμις* (dúnamis, “power”) + *-ίζειν* (-ízein, verbal suffix).
