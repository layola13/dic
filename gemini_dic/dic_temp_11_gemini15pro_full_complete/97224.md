inflation: /ɪnˈfleɪʃn/ (UK) /ɪnˈfleɪʃən/ (US)| n.| 通货膨胀|A general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money.|例句：The country is struggling with high inflation.  这个国家正与高通货膨胀作斗争。

例句：Inflation is eroding the value of savings. 通货膨胀正在侵蚀储蓄的价值。


近义词：increase, rise, expansion, escalation|反义词: deflation, devaluation |词性: noun
