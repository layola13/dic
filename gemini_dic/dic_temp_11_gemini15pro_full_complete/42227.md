count:/kaʊnt/| n. v.|计数；总数；伯爵；认为；重要|A count is the act of determining the total number of something. It can also refer to the total itself, a nobleman (like an earl), or the act of considering something in a particular way.  It can also mean to matter or be significant.|例句：The final count showed that we had 100 votes.  最终的计票结果显示我们获得了100票。|近义词：total, tally, enumerate, number, reckon, consider, matter|反义词:discount, disregard, neglect|词性:noun, verb
