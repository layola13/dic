disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ /dɪˈspɝːs/| v. |分散，驱散|To scatter or spread widely, or to cause something to scatter or spread widely. |The crowd dispersed after the concert.  音乐会结束后，人群散开了。|The police used tear gas to disperse the protesters. 警察用催泪瓦斯驱散抗议者。|近义词：scatter, dissipate, distribute|反义词：gather, collect, assemble|词性:动词
disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ /dɪˈspɝːs/| adj. |分散的|Spread over a wide area. |The company has a disperse network of offices across the country.  这家公司在全国各地都有分散的办事处。|The seeds were dispersed by the wind. 种子被风吹散了。|近义词：scattered, distributed, spread out|反义词：concentrated, clustered, gathered|词性:形容词
