be-toast:/biː tɔːst/
|adj. informal|完蛋了，注定要失败|Be ruined, doomed, or in serious trouble.|例句：If I don't get this project done on time, I'm toast. 如果我没有按时完成这个项目，我就完蛋了。|近义词：doomed, finished, done for|反义词：safe, secure|词性:形容词
be-toast:/biː tɔːst/
|n. informal|注定要失败的人或事物|Someone or something that is doomed to fail.|例句：The team is toast after losing their star player.  失去了他们的明星球员后，这支球队注定要失败。|近义词：goner, dead duck|反义词：winner, champion|词性:名词
