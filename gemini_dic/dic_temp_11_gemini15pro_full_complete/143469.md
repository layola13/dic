outgained:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnd/
verb
胜过，超过（在收益或产量方面）
To surpass in gains or production.
Our team outgained theirs by 100 yards. 我们的球队比他们的球队多进了100码。
The company outgained its competitors in sales last quarter.  该公司上个季度的销售额超过了竞争对手。
近义词:surpassed, exceeded, outstripped, outperformed
反义词:lost to, lagged behind
词性:v.

词源: out- + gain + -ed
"out-" prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing"
"gain" meaning "to acquire or obtain something desirable"
"-ed" past tense suffix
