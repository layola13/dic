miscue: /ˌmɪsˈkjuː/
noun
失误，错误
an action or utterance that is inappropriate or ill-timed.
The quarterback's miscue cost the team the game.  四分卫的失误使球队输掉了比赛。

verb
犯错，失误
make a mistake; perform badly.
He miscued on the final shot, handing his opponent the victory. 他最后一击失误，拱手让对手获胜。

近义词: error, mistake, blunder, slip-up, gaffe
反义词: success, triumph
词性: noun, verb
