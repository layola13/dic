nettlelike:/ˈnet(ə)lˌlaɪk/
adj.
像荨麻的; 类似荨麻的;
Resembling a nettle, especially in having stinging hairs or causing a stinging sensation.  Covered with stinging hairs like those of a nettle.
The plant has nettlelike leaves that can cause skin irritation.  这种植物有类似荨麻的叶子，会引起皮肤刺激。
The caterpillar has nettlelike spines. 这毛毛虫长着像荨麻一样的刺。

近义词:stinging, prickly, urticating
反义词:smooth, soft

词性:adjective
