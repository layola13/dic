dispersedly:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪdli/
adverb
分散地，散落地
In a dispersed manner; spread over a wide area.
The seeds were dispersedly scattered by the wind.  种子被风分散地吹散了。
The refugees were dispersedly located throughout the country.  难民分散地分布在全国各地。
近义词: widely, thinly, sparsely, scatteredly
反义词: densely, compactly, concentratedly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From disperse + -ed + -ly.  Disperse comes from Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart" + spargere "to strew."
