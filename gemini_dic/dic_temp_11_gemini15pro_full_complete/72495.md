fluorescent:/flʊˈrɛsənt/ /flɔːˈresnt/| adj. n.|荧光的；荧光灯；荧光物质|Emitting light during exposure to radiation, especially ultraviolet light; a fluorescent lamp.|The fluorescent lights buzzed overhead. 顶上的荧光灯发出嗡嗡声。|The fluorescent paint glowed under the black light.  荧光涂料在黑光下发光。|近义词：luminous, bright, radiant|反义词:dull, dark|词性:adjective, noun|词源:From Latin *fluorescēns, present participle of *fluōrescere (“to begin to shine, begin to blossom”), from fluō (“flow”).


fluorescents:/flʊˈrɛsənts/ /flɔːˈresnts/| n.|荧光灯|Plural of fluorescent (noun).|The fluorescents in the office flickered annoyingly. 办公室里的荧光灯闪烁不停，很烦人。|She replaced the old fluorescents with LED lights. 她把旧的荧光灯换成了LED灯。|近义词：fluorescent lamps, fluorescent tubes|反义词:incandescent lights|词性:noun|词源:From Latin *fluorescēns, present participle of *fluōrescere (“to begin to shine, begin to blossom”), from fluō (“flow”).
