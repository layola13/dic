hyperactions:/ˌhaɪpərˈækʃənz/ | n. |过度活跃，多动|Excessive actions or activity, often associated with a medical or psychological condition.  It refers to a state of heightened activity and restlessness. |The child's hyperactions disrupted the classroom.  这个孩子的过度活跃扰乱了课堂。|  His hyperactions made it difficult for him to focus on his work. 他的多动症使他难以集中精力工作。| 近义词: hyperactivity, restlessness, fidgeting | 反义词: inactivity, calmness, stillness | noun
