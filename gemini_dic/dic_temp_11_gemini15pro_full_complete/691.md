absconder:/æbˈskɒndə(r)/ (US: /æbˈskɑːndər/)| n.|潜逃者；逃亡者|A person who leaves hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an unlawful action such as theft.|The absconder was caught trying to cross the border. 潜逃者在试图越境时被抓获。|Synonyms: fugitive, runaway, escapee|Antonyms: returnee, resident|noun
