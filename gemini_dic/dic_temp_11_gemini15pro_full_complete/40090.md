containment:/kənˈteɪnmənt/
n.|遏制，控制，容纳|The containment of the virus is the top priority. 病毒的遏制是首要任务。|例句：The government implemented strict measures for the containment of the epidemic. 政府实施了严格的措施来控制疫情。|近义词：control, restraint, confinement|反义词：release, spread, liberation|词性:名词
Etymology: From contain + -ment.  "Contain" comes from the Old French "contenir" and ultimately from the Latin "continere," meaning "to hold together, enclose, or comprise."
