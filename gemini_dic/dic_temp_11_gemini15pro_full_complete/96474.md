individually:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəli/| adv.|个别地，单独地|One by one, separately.  Characterized by distinctness. |Each student was evaluated individually. 每个学生都单独进行评估。|Synonyms: separately, singly, one by one, respectively, severally|Antonyms: collectively, together, jointly|adverb

individually:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəli/| adj.|(不常用) 个体的，独特的|Existing as a separate entity. |Although part of a larger group, each piece was individually crafted. 虽然是更大群体的一部分，但每件作品都是单独制作的。|Synonyms: distinct, separate, unique|Antonyms: collective, shared, common|adjective
