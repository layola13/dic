constellations:/ˌkɑːns.təˈleɪ.ʃənz/ /ˌkɒn.stəˈleɪ.ʃənz/| n.|星座|A group of stars forming a recognizable pattern that is traditionally named after its apparent form or identified with a mythological figure.|She studied the constellations in the night sky. 她研究了夜空的星座。|The constellations guided ancient sailors. 星座指引着古代的水手。|synonyms: asterisms, star patterns|antonyms: (No direct antonym for a group of stars. constellation:/ˌkɑːns.təˈleɪ.ʃən/ /ˌkɒn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən/| n.|星座; 一群人或事物|A group of stars forming a recognizable pattern that is traditionally named after its apparent form or identified with a mythological figure.
