crutchlike: /ˈkrʌtʃˌlaɪk/
adj.
像拐杖一样的；依靠拐杖的；依赖性的
Resembling or suggestive of a crutch; dependent.
His crutchlike gait was a result of a childhood injury. 他像拐杖一样的步态是童年受伤的结果。
The company's crutchlike dependence on government subsidies is unsustainable.  这家公司对政府补贴的依赖性就像拐杖一样，是不可持续的。
近义词: supportive, dependent, reliant
反义词: independent, self-sufficient
词性: 形容词

词源:  crutch (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Crutch" comes from Old English "crycc" meaning staff or stick. "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English "-lic."
