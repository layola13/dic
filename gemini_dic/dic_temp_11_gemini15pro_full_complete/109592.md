lethargical: /ləˈθɑːrdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 昏睡的，无精打采的 | Affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic. |  The patient was lethargical and unresponsive. 病人昏睡不醒，没有反应。

The hot weather made everyone feel lethargical. 炎热的天气使每个人都感到无精打采。 | 近义词: sluggish, listless, apathetic, inert, drowsy | 反义词: energetic, active, vigorous, alert | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *lēthargicus, from Greek lēthargos 'forgetful' (from lēthē 'forgetfulness' + argos 'idle') + -icus.
