cuckoldom:/ˈkʌkəldəm/| n.|戴绿帽子，妻子通奸|The state of being a cuckold (a man whose wife has been unfaithful).|He feared cuckoldom more than anything.|近义词：infidelity, adultery, betrayal|反义词:fidelity, faithfulness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kukwoldom, from Old French cucuault (“cuckold”) + -dom.  Ultimately from cuckoo, referring to the cuckoo's habit of laying eggs in other birds' nests.
