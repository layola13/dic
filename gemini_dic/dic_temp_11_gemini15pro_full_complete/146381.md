overflour:/ˌoʊvərˈflaʊər/ | v. | 过度撒粉; 面粉放得过多 | To use too much flour in a recipe, resulting in a dry or tough finished product. |  I think I overfloured the cake batter; it's very stiff. 我想我在蛋糕糊里放太多面粉了；它很硬。| The biscuits were dry and crumbly because I overfloured them.  饼干又干又碎，因为我撒了太多面粉。 | over-mix, use too much flour | use too little flour | verb
