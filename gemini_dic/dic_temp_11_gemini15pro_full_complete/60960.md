elfin:/ˈɛlfɪn/ | adj. | 小精灵似的；精致的；优雅的 | Relating to or resembling an elf; small and delicate; having a magical quality. | She had an elfin grace, moving lightly and quickly. 她有一种小精灵般的优雅，轻盈而快速地移动着。| The child's elfin features were captivating.  这孩子精灵般的容貌令人着迷。 | 近义词：delicate, fairy-like, ethereal, graceful | 反义词：clumsy, awkward, coarse, rough | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From elf + -in.  "Elf" comes from Old English ælf, meaning "supernatural being." The suffix "-in" forms adjectives.
