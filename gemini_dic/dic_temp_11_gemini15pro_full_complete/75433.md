例句：The fruiteress at the market always has the freshest produce.  市场上的那个女水果贩总是卖最新鲜的农产品。


|近义词：fruit seller, greengrocer (if also selling vegetables)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fruit + -er (agent suffix) + -ess (feminine suffix).  The "-ess" suffix historically denotes a female agent.
