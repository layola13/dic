brachiopodous:/ˌbrækiˈɒpədəs/
adjective
腕足动物的；有腕足的
Relating to or characteristic of brachiopods, marine animals with two hinged shells.
Brachiopodous fossils were found in the ancient seabed.  在古代海床上发现了腕足类化石。
Synonyms:  brachiopod, shelled, bivalve (though not technically a bivalve)
Antonyms:  None specific. Could use terms like "vertebral" to contrast with invertebrate brachiopods.
