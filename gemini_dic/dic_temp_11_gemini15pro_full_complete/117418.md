medicos:/ˈmedɪkoʊz/
noun
医生，医务人员 (尤指复数)
Medical practitioners, especially used in the plural form.
The medicos worked tirelessly during the pandemic. 疫情期间，医务人员不知疲倦地工作。
近义词:doctors, physicians, surgeons
反义词:patients
词性:n.
