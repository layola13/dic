extricable:/ˈɛkstrɪkəbl/
adjective
可解救的，可摆脱的，可脱出的
Able to be freed or removed from a difficult situation or entanglement.
The dog's leash became entangled in the bushes, but thankfully it was extricable. 这只狗的皮带缠在了灌木丛里，幸好可以解开。
The government found itself in an extricable situation. 政府发现自己处于一个可以摆脱的困境。
近义词: rescuable, releasable, free, detachable
反义词: inextricable, trapped, stuck, fast
