fingerlike:/ˈfɪŋɡəlaɪk/
adj.
手指状的；指状的
Resembling a finger in shape; slender and tapering.
The plant has fingerlike leaves.  这种植物有指状的叶子。
近义词：digital, digitate
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From finger +‎ -like.
