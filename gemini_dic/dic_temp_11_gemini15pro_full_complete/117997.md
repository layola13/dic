memorializes:/məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪzɪz/
v.
纪念；使…永垂不朽
serves to keep alive the memory of someone or something, typically by creating a monument or work of art.
The statue memorializes the soldiers who died in the war. 这座雕像纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。
commemorates, remembers, celebrates
forgets, ignores
verb
