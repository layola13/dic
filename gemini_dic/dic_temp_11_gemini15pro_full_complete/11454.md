argument:/ˈɑːrɡjumənt/| n.|争论，论证，论点|A reason or set of reasons given with the aim of persuading others that an action or idea is right or wrong.|例句：They had a heated argument about politics. 他们就政治问题进行了激烈的争论。

例句：Her argument was based on solid evidence. 她的论点建立在确凿的证据之上。|近义词：dispute, debate, contention, quarrel|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord|词性:noun

argue:/ˈɑːrɡjuː/| v.|争论，辩论，主张|To give reasons or cite evidence in support of an idea, action, or theory, typically with the aim of persuading others to share one's view.|例句：They argued for hours about the best course of action. 他们就最佳行动方案争论了几个小时。

例句：He argued that the evidence was inconclusive. 他辩称证据不确定。|近义词：debate, dispute, contend, reason|反义词：agree, concede, concur|词性:verb
