gypsyesque:/ˌdʒɪpsiˈɛsk/
adj.
吉普赛式的，波西米亚风格的
Having a colorful, unconventional, or free-spirited quality reminiscent of the traditional image of Romani people (Gypsies).
Her clothing had a gypsyesque flair, with flowing skirts and vibrant scarves. 她穿着吉卜赛风格的飘逸长裙和鲜艳的围巾。
近义词:bohemian, nomadic, unconventional, romantic, free-spirited
反义词:conventional, traditional, conformist
词性:形容词
