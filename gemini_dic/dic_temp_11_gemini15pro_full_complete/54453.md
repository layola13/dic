disinherits:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪts/ | v. |剥夺继承权|To prevent (someone) from inheriting property, especially by making a will that excludes them. |Example 1: The wealthy businessman disinherited his son after a bitter feud.  这位富商在激烈的争吵后剥夺了他儿子的继承权。

Example 2: She was disinherited for marrying against her father's wishes. 她因为违背父亲的意愿结婚而被剥夺了继承权。|近义词：exclude, cut off |反义词: include, bequeath |词性:动词
