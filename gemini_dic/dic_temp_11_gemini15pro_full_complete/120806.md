misassertion:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrʃən/
|n.|
|错误断言；虚报；谎报|
|An untrue or misleading assertion.  A statement that is not true.|
|The politician's speech was full of misassertions about his opponent's record.|这位政客的演讲充满了对他对手记录的歪曲。
|
|The report contained several misassertions that needed to be corrected.|该报告包含几个需要更正的错误断言。

|
|Synonyms: misstatement, falsehood, untruth, lie, fabrication, inaccuracy|
|Antonyms: truth, fact, accuracy, correctness|
|noun|
