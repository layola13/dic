mallard:/ˈmælərd/| n.|野鸭（绿头鸭）|A common wild duck (Anas platyrhynchos) with a greenish head and white neck ring (male) or mottled brown plumage (female).|例句：We saw a mallard swimming on the lake. 我们看到一只野鸭在湖里游泳。

例句：The male mallard is easily recognizable by its iridescent green head. 雄性绿头鸭很容易通过其闪亮的绿色头部辨认出来。|近义词：duck, wild duck|反义词:|noun
