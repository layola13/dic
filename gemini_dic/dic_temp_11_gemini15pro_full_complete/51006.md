desistive:/dɪˈzɪstɪv/
adjective
停止的， desist的形容词形式
Relating to or characterized by desistance, the process of ceasing or abstaining from a behavior or activity.
The study examined the desistive process in former gang members.  这项研究调查了前帮派成员的停止犯罪过程。
The program aims to promote desistive behavior among young offenders.  该计划旨在促进年轻罪犯的停止犯罪行为。
近义词: ceasing, abstaining, stopping, quitting
反义词: continuing, persisting, persevering
词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "desist," from Old French "desister," from Latin "desistere" (to stand off, leave off).
