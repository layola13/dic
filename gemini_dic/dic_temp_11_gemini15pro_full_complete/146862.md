overindustrialized: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度工业化的
Having developed too much industry, to a point where it causes problems such as pollution or unemployment.
The region became overindustrialized and suffered from high levels of pollution.  这个地区过度工业化，遭受了严重污染。
Factories closed down in the overindustrialized city, leaving many unemployed. 在这个过度工业化的城市，工厂倒闭，导致许多人失业。
overdeveloped, excessively industrialized
underdeveloped, undeveloped
