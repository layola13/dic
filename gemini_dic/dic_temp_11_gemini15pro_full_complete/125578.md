multitracker:/ˈmʌltiˌtrækər/ | n. | 多轨录音机；多目标跟踪器 | A device or software that can track multiple objects or signals simultaneously.  It is often used in music recording and in fields like surveillance and radar. | The studio uses a sophisticated multitracker for recording albums. 这家工作室使用一台先进的多轨录音机录制专辑。 | The multitracker allowed the band to record each instrument separately. 这台多轨录音机允许乐队分别录制每种乐器。 | 近义词: multi-track recorder, tracker | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps single-track recorder) | 词性: noun
