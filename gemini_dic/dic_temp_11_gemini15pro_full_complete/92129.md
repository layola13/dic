hyperscrupulously:/ˌhaɪpərˈskruːpjələsli/| adv.|极其谨慎地，过分小心地|Characterized by excessive and meticulous attention to detail or propriety, often to an unnecessary degree.| He hyperscrupulously checked every detail of the contract before signing. 他在签合同之前极其谨慎地检查了每一个细节。|Example 2: She hyperscrupulously avoids any situation that might be considered remotely improper. 她过分小心地避免任何可能被认为稍微不合适的场合。|近义词: meticulously, painstakingly, excessively carefully|反义词: carelessly, negligently, recklessly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + scrupulously (from scrupulous, from Latin scrupulus "small sharp stone," figuratively "anxiety, uneasiness," diminutive of scrupus "sharp stone").
