impersonator:/ɪmˈpɜːrsəneɪtər/| n. |模仿者；假冒者|A person who impersonates someone else, often for entertainment or fraudulent purposes.  A person who pretends to be another person, especially for entertainment or criminal purposes.| The impersonator did a hilarious take on the president.  这位模仿者对总统进行了滑稽的模仿。|  The impersonator was arrested for trying to access the victim's bank account.  这名假冒者因试图访问受害者的银行账户而被捕。| synonyms: mimic, imitator,  actor,  pretender | antonyms:  original | noun
