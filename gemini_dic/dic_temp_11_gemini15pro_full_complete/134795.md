nonmetropolitans:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtənz/ | n. | 非都市居民；居住在非大都市地区的人们 | People who live outside large metropolitan areas.  |  Many nonmetropolitans commute to cities for work. 许多非都市居民通勤到城市工作。| residents of rural areas, country dwellers |  metropolitans, urbanites | noun


nonmetropolitan:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/ | adj. | 非都市的；非大都市地区的 | Relating to or characteristic of areas outside large metropolitan areas. | The government is investing in nonmetropolitan infrastructure. 政府正在投资非都市地区的基础设施。| rural, country, provincial | urban, metropolitan | adjective


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"metropolitan": from Greek "metropolis" meaning "mother city"
