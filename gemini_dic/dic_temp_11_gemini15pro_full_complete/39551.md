consciencelessly:/ˌkɒnʃənˈləsli/ /ˌkɑːnʃənˈləsli/
adverb
无情地，没良心;丧尽天良地
In a way that shows no concern about whether behavior is morally right or wrong.  Acting without conscience; cruelly or immorally.

He consciencelessly exploited his workers, paying them low wages and forcing them to work long hours.
他无情地剥削他的工人，付给他们低工资，强迫他们长时间工作。

She consciencelessly abandoned her family and moved to another country.
她狠心地抛弃了家人，搬到了另一个国家。


近义词:heartlessly, ruthlessly, cruelly, callously, remorselessly
反义词:conscientiously, scrupulously, compassionately, mercifully

词性:adverb
