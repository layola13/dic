luxe:/lʌks/ | n. adj. | 奢侈，奢华；豪华 |  A condition of great comfort, elegance, and extravagance.  Derived from the French word "luxe," ultimately from the Latin "luxus" meaning "excess." |  She lives a life of pure luxe, with a private jet and a yacht. 她过着纯粹奢华的生活，拥有私人飞机和游艇。|  The hotel offers luxe accommodations and personalized service. 这家酒店提供豪华的住宿和个性化的服务。| synonyms: luxury, opulence, lavishness, extravagance | antonyms: austerity, simplicity, poverty, modesty | noun/adjective
