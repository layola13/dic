curtest:/ˈkɜːtɪst/| adj.|最简短的，最简洁的|Shortest, most concise, often rudely so.|例句：He gave the curtest of replies. 他给了最简短的答复。|近义词：briefest, tersest, abruptest|反义词:longest, most verbose|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *curtest, superlative of *curt, from Old French *cort (Modern French court), from Latin curtus (“short”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“to cut”).
