eunuchisms:/ˈjuːnəkɪzəmz/ | n. | 阉割行为；阉人言行；柔弱的言行 | The eunuchisms of the courtiers were evident in their obsequious manner and high-pitched voices. 朝廷宦官的阉人言行表现在他们谄媚的态度和尖锐的嗓音上。| 例句：His writing style was criticized for its eunuchisms, lacking vigor and directness. 他的写作风格因其柔弱无力、缺乏直截了当而受到批评。 | 近义词：effeminacy, weakness, timidity | 反义词：masculinity, strength, boldness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *eunuchismus, from Greek eunouchismos, from eunouchos 'eunuch'.
