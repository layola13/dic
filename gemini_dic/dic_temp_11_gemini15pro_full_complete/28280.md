captivities:/kæpˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
囚禁；拘禁；俘虏；迷恋
the state of being held against one's will; the state of being kept as a prisoner or slave
例句：
He spent many years in captivity.  他被囚禁了很多年。

The zoo animals, though well cared for, lived in captivity.  虽然动物园的动物得到了很好的照顾，但它们仍生活在囚禁中。

近义词：
imprisonment, confinement, bondage, restraint, incarceration

反义词：
freedom, liberty, release

词性:
名词(plural noun)

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French captivité, from Latin captivitas, from captivus ‘captive’
