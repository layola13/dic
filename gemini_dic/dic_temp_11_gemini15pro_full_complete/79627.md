glossarist:/ˈɡlɒsərɪst/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːsərɪst/ (UK)| n.|词汇表编纂者；注释者|A person who compiles a glossary or makes glosses. |The glossarist meticulously researched the etymology of each term.  词汇表编纂者仔细研究了每个术语的词源。|Synonym: lexicographer, annotator|Antonym: None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *glossarius*, from glossarium "glossary" + -ist.  Ultimately from Greek glōssa "tongue, language."
