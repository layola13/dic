happened:/ˈhæpənd/| v.|发生，出现|Something unexpected happened. 发生了意想不到的事情。|例句：The accident happened so fast.  事故发生得太快了。|近义词：occurred, transpired, took place|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用prevented, averted)|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *happenen, from Old Norse happna (“to happen, turn out”), from happ (“chance, good luck”).  More at hap.
