footlicker:/ˈfʊtlɪkər/| n.|拍马屁的人，卑躬屈膝的人；奴颜婢膝者|A person who behaves obsequiously to someone in power, often to an excessive or demeaning degree.  It carries a strongly negative connotation.| He's such a footlicker, always praising the boss even when she's wrong.|Synonyms: sycophant, yes-man, brown-noser, toady, flatterer|Antonyms: rebel, nonconformist|noun
