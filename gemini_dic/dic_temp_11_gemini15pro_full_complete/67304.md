exsuction: /ɛkˈsʌkʃən/
n.
吸出；抽出；抽吸
the act of sucking something out of a place
例句：The exsuction of the fluid from the wound was necessary to prevent infection.  从伤口吸出液体对于防止感染是必要的。

例句：Exsuction of the venom from the snakebite was performed immediately.  立即对蛇咬伤进行了毒液抽吸。

近义词：aspiration, suction, withdrawal, extraction
反义词：injection, insertion, infusion
