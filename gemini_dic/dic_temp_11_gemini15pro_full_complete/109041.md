legal-size:/ˈliːɡl saɪz/
adjective
法定尺寸的
Of or relating to the size of paper commonly used for legal documents, typically 8.5 x 14 inches in the US.
The lawyer requested legal-size paper for the contract.  律师要求用法律尺寸的纸张来打印合同。
The printer can handle legal-size documents. 这台打印机可以处理法律尺寸的文件。
近义词:standard (for some regions)
反义词:letter-size, A4
