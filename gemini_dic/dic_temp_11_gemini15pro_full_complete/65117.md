etherize:/ˈiːθəraɪz/| vt.|麻醉，使失去知觉|To render unconscious with ether; to anesthetize.|The vet etherized the cat before the operation. 兽医在手术前给猫麻醉了。|近义词：anesthetize, narcotize|反义词: awaken, revive|词性:动词
Etymology: From ether + -ize.  Ether, referring to diethyl ether, a common anesthetic, comes from Late Latin aether, from Ancient Greek αἰθήρ (aithḗr, “upper air; bright, pure air; the sky”), from αἴθω (aíthō, “I burn, blaze”).
