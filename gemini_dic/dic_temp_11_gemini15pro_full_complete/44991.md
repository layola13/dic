crystallizes:/ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzɪz/
v.
结晶；使具体化；明确
The solution crystallizes as it cools.  溶液冷却时会结晶。
His vague ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 他模糊的想法具体化成了一个具体的计划。
crystallize (verb)
The sugar syrup began to crystallize. 糖浆开始结晶。
Her thoughts crystallized into a clear plan. 她的想法逐渐形成一个清晰的计划。
Synonyms: solidify, harden, congeal, coalesce, precipitate
Antonyms: melt, dissolve, liquefy
