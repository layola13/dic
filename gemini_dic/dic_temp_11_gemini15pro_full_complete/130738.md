nonambitiously:/ˌnɒnæmˈbɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 不求上进地，没有野心地 |  In a manner lacking ambition or drive; without a strong desire to achieve success or distinction. | He nonambitiously accepted the low-paying job, content with a simple life. 他不求名利地接受了这份低薪工作，满足于简单的生活。| Synonyms: unambitiously, half-heartedly, lackadaisically | Antonyms: ambitiously, zealously, eagerly | adverb
