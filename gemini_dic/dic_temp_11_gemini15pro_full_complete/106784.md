labels:/ˈleɪblz/| n. |标签，标记|A small piece of paper, fabric, plastic, or similar material attached to an object and giving information about it. |The label on the bottle says "milk". 瓶子上的标签写着“牛奶”。|synonyms: tag, sticker, badge, marker|antonyms: unmarked, unidentified|noun


labels:/ˈleɪblz/| v. |贴标签于，标记|Attach a label to something. |He labeled the boxes with the contents. 他在盒子上贴上了内容标签。|synonyms: tag, mark, identify, classify|antonyms: unlabel, remove tag|verb
