词源:
From French *fantasier, from Late Latin phantasiare, from Greek phantasioun, from phantasia "appearance, image, fancy," from phainesthai "to appear," from PIE root *bha- (1) "to shine."  The sense development in Greek is from "making visible" to "presenting to the mind."
