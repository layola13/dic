annotate:/ˈænəteɪt/ | v. | 注释，注解 | To add notes to a text or diagram, giving explanation or comment. | The historian annotated the ancient manuscript with detailed explanations of its origins. 历史学家为这份古代手稿添加了详细的注释，解释了它的来源。

The editor annotated the text with footnotes to clarify obscure references. 编辑用脚注注释了文本，以阐明晦涩的参考文献。

| 近义词：comment, explain, gloss, interpret | 反义词： | 词性：动词
