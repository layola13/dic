chromaticities: /ˌkroʊməˈtɪsətiz/ | n. | 色度 | The quality of a color as determined by its hue and saturation.  It refers to the measurable color characteristics of light or of an object that reflects or transmits light. | The chromaticities of the gemstones were stunning, ranging from deep reds to vibrant greens. 宝石的色度令人惊叹，从深红色到鲜艳的绿色都有。 | synonyms: colors, hues, tones, shades | antonyms: achromaticity, neutrality | noun
