instantiating:/ɪnˈstænʃiˌeɪtɪŋ/
| v.|实例化，创建实例|The process of creating a specific instance of a class or object.  Creating a real-world example based on a general concept.|The programmer is instantiating a new object of the 'Customer' class.|近义词：creating, initializing, materializing|反义词:destroying, deleting|动词

instantiation:/ɪnˌstænʃiˈeɪʃən/
| n.|实例化，实例|The act of instantiating; a specific instance of a class or object. A concrete realization of an abstract concept.|The instantiation of the new policy took several months.|近义词：example, instance, realization|反义词:abstraction, concept|名词
