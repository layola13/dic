booze:/buːz/
n.
酒精饮料，酒
alcoholic beverages
He often drinks too much booze. 他经常喝太多酒。
近义词: alcohol, liquor, spirits, drinks
反义词: sobriety, abstinence
词性: 名词

booze:/buːz/
v.
喝酒，豪饮
to drink alcohol, especially in large quantities
He boozed all night. 他整晚都在喝酒。
近义词: drink, imbibe, tipple
反义词: abstain
词性: 动词

词源：booze的词源可以追溯到中古荷兰语的 *būsen，意思是“喝大量”。
