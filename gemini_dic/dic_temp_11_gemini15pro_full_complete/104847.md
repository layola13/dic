keelhauling:/ˈkiːlhɔːlɪŋ/
noun
龙骨吊刑，一种严酷的惩罚
The act of punishing someone by dragging them under a ship's keel.
The captain threatened the mutineers with keelhauling. 船长威胁要对叛变者处以龙骨吊刑。
synonyms:  flogging, lashing, whipping, scourging, torture
antonyms: reward, praise, commendation,  accolade
n.
