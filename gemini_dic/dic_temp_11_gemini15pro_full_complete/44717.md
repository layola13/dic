cruelties: /ˈkruːəltiz/
n.
残暴行为；残酷行径
acts of extreme cruelty or violence.
The museum's exhibit detailed the cruelties of war.  博物馆的展览详细描述了战争的残酷。
The cruelties inflicted upon the prisoners of war were horrific.  战俘遭受的残酷行径令人发指。
近义词: atrocities, barbarities, brutalities
反义词: kindnesses, mercies, compassions
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crualtees*, from Old French *crualtez*, plural of *crualte* (“cruelty”), from Late Latin *crudelitas*, from Latin *crūdēlis* (“cruel, unfeeling”).
