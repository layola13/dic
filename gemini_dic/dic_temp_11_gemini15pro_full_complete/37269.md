comity: /ˈkɒmɪti/ (US) /ˈkɒmɪti/ (UK)| n.| 礼让；友谊；礼仪；团结|Courtesy and considerate behavior towards others.  A state of mutual harmony, friendship, and respect, especially between people of different groups or nations.|例句：The comity between the two nations allowed for peaceful trade.  两国之间的友好关系使得和平贸易成为可能。

例句：The meeting was marked by a spirit of comity and cooperation. 会议的特点是团结友爱和合作的精神。|近义词：civility, courtesy, amity, cordiality|反义词：enmity, hostility, animosity|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *comitete "courtesy, civility,"  from Latin comitas "friendliness, courtesy, kindness,"  from comis "courteous, kind," of unknown origin.
