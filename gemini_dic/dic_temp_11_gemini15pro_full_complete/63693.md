eperlan:/eɪˈpɜːrlən/ | n. | 欧洲白鱼,银鱼 |A small freshwater fish of the smelt family, found in Europe. It is considered a delicacy. |  例句：The eperlan is a popular fish for anglers in France.  欧洲白鱼是法国垂钓者喜爱的一种鱼。

例句：Eperlan are often served fried as a starter.  欧洲白鱼经常被油炸作为开胃菜。 | 近义词：smelt, whitebait | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *éperlan*, from Old French *esperlan*. Ultimately of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old High German *sparo* "sparrow," referring to its silvery color.
