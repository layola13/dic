mouthfuls:/ˈmaʊθfʊlz/ | n. | 满口，一口，少量 | A mouthful is the amount of food or drink that you can put in your mouth at one time. |  He ate the cake in two mouthfuls. 他两口就把蛋糕吃完了。| synonyms: bite, morsel | antonyms: none | noun

mouthfuls:/ˈmaʊθfʊlz/ | n. | (难读或难记的) 一串，长串(字词) | A mouthful is a long or complicated word or phrase that is difficult to say. |  His name is quite a mouthful. 他的名字很难念。| synonyms: tongue-twister | antonyms: none | noun
