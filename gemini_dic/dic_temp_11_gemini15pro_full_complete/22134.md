boltonite:/ˈboʊltəˌnaɪt/
noun
硅镁石
A mineral of the forsterite-fayalite series, (Mg,Fe)2SiO4, containing about 20 to 40 percent of the iron-rich member, fayalite.
Boltonite is a mineral found in igneous and metamorphic rocks. 硅镁石是一种在火成岩和变质岩中发现的矿物。
Near synonyms: forsterite, fayalite
