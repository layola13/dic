acarpous:/əˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
无果实的；不结果的
Not producing fruit; barren.
The acarpous strain of the plant was unable to produce any offspring.  这种无果实的植物品系无法繁殖后代。
The gardener was frustrated by the acarpous fig tree. 园丁对这棵不结果的无花果树感到沮丧。
近义词:barren, sterile, infertile, fruitless
反义词:fruitful, fertile, productive
词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *a- "not" + *karpos "fruit".
