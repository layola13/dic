affixation:/ˌæfɪkˈseɪʃən/| n.|词缀法；附加；粘着|The process of adding a prefix or suffix to a word to create a new word.  The word "unhappiness" is formed by affixation, with the prefix "un-" and the suffix "-ness" added to the root word "happy".|例句：The English language uses affixation extensively to create new words. 英语广泛使用词缀法来创造新词。

例句：Affixation is a common way to change the meaning or grammatical function of a word. 词缀法是改变词义或语法功能的常见方法。|近义词：derivation, word formation|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
