micronuclear:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈnjuːkliər/ | adj. |微核的| Relating to or involving micronuclei (small, extra nuclei in a cell, often a sign of damage). |  Micronuclear tests can detect chromosomal instability. 微核测试可以检测染色体不稳定性。|  Micronuclear analysis is used in toxicology. 微核分析用于毒理学。| 近义词: (No direct synonym, may use related terms like "genotoxic," "mutagenic") | 反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: adjective

micronucleus:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈnjuːkliəs/ | n. |微核| A small, extra nucleus in a cell, often a sign of chromosome damage. | The presence of micronuclei indicates DNA damage. 微核的存在表明DNA损伤。|  Micronuclei are formed during cell division. 微核在细胞分裂过程中形成。| 近义词: (No direct synonym) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
