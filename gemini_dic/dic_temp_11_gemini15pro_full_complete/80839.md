gouland:/ˈɡuːlənd/| n. |暴食者，贪吃的人；贪婪的人|A person who eats greedily or has a ravenous appetite; a glutton.|He was known as a gouland, always piling his plate high. 他是个出了名的贪吃鬼，总是把盘子堆得高高的。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, gormandizer|反义词:ascetic|词性：名词

词源: From French *gouland*, from *goule* "gullet, throat" (from Latin *gula* "throat") + *-and* (suffix forming nouns of agent).
