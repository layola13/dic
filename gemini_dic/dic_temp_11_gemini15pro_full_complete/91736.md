hyperdeliciousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˌdɪˈlɪʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|极其美味；超级美味|A state of being extremely delicious, beyond the ordinary level of deliciousness.|The chef's new creation was a symphony of hyperdeliciousnesses.  厨师的新作品是一曲极其美味的交响曲。|Synonyms: exquisite deliciousness, supreme deliciousness,  unparalleled deliciousness|Antonyms: unpalatableness, distastefulness|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"hyper-" (prefix): from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond, excessive)
"delicious": from Old French "delicieux" (delightful, delicious), ultimately from Latin "deliciosus" (delightful), from "deliciae" (pleasures, delights)
"-ness" (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
