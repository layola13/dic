avernian: /əˈvɜːrniən/
adjective
地狱的; 阴森的; 邪恶的
Relating to the underworld; infernal; hellish.  Also, relating to the Averni, an ancient Gallic tribe.
The poet described the avernian landscape of the battlefield. 诗人描述了战场上地狱般的景象。
近义词: infernal, hellish, chthonic, stygian
反义词: heavenly, celestial, ethereal
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *Avernus*, the name of a lake near Naples believed to be the entrance to the underworld.  Ultimately from Greek *Ἄορνος (Aornos)*, meaning "birdless,"  presumably because the volcanic fumes were thought to kill birds.
