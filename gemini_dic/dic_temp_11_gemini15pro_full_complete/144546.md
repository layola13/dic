outvoting: /ˌaʊtˈvoʊtɪŋ/
outvoting: v.
outvoting: 击败, 以票数胜过
outvoting: Defeating someone or something by having more votes.
outvoting: The proposal was outvoted by a large margin.  这项提议以很大的差距被否决了。
outvoting: The chairman was outvoted by the board members. 董事长被董事会成员投票否决了。
outvoting: defeat, overrule
outvoting: concede, lose
