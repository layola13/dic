anaglyptic:/ˌænəˈɡlɪptɪk/ | adj. | 浮雕的，雕刻的 | Relating to or produced by anaglyphs, a process that creates a three-dimensional image by superimposing two slightly different images, typically in contrasting colors, which are viewed through special glasses with colored filters. | The museum showcased an anaglyptic representation of the ancient city.  博物馆展示了这座古城的立体浮雕。| Synonym: embossed, carved, relief, three-dimensional | Antonym: flat, two-dimensional | adjective

anaglyph:/ˈænəɡlɪf/ | n. | 浮雕，立体照片 | A picture or image produced by anaglyphs, a process that creates a three-dimensional image by superimposing two slightly different images, typically in contrasting colors, which are viewed through special glasses with colored filters. |  We used red and blue glasses to view the anaglyph. 我们用红蓝眼镜来看立体照片。 | Synonym: stereogram, 3D image | Antonym: flat image, 2D image | noun
