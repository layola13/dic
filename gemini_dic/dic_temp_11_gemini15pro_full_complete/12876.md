assuasive:/əˈsweɪsɪv/| adj.|劝慰的；抚慰的；使人安心的|Having the power to soothe or calm; relieving or mitigating pain or distress.  Serving to assuage. |Her assuasive words calmed the frightened child. 她安慰的话语使受惊的孩子平静下来。|近义词：soothing, calming, comforting, tranquilizing, allaying, palliative, lenitive|反义词：agitating, distressing, upsetting|词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *assuasīvus, from Latin assuāsus, past participle of assuāre (“to soothe, soften, mitigate, calm, appease”), from ad- + suāvis (“sweet, agreeable, pleasant”).
