in-the-clear:/ˌɪn ðə ˈklɪr/
adjective
安全的；公开的；无罪的；解密的
safe from danger or suspicion; no longer in danger or trouble; understandable; (of a message) sent or received uncoded
例句：The hostages are now in the clear. 人质现在安全了。

例句：Once we get across the border we're in the clear. 一旦我们越过边境，我们就安全了。


近义词：safe, secure, clear, innocent, unencrypted
反义词：in danger, at risk, guilty, encrypted
词性：adjective
