cassiar:/ˈkæsiər/| n. |卡西亚|A region in northern British Columbia, Canada, known for its rugged mountains, pristine wilderness, and gold mining history.  It's also used to refer to a mountain range (Cassiar Mountains) and a highway (Cassiar Highway) in the same area.|The Cassiar Highway offers stunning views of the mountains. 卡西亚公路沿途山景壮丽。|近义词：northwestern BC, northern BC|反义词:None|词性:noun

cassiar:/ˈkæsiər/| adj. |卡西亚的|Relating to or characteristic of the Cassiar region of British Columbia. |The Cassiar Mountains are known for their challenging hiking trails. 卡西亚山脉以其富有挑战性的徒步旅行路线而闻名。|近义词：northwestern BC, northern BC|反义词:None|词性:adjective
