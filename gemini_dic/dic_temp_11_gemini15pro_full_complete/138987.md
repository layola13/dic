nymphean: /nɪmˈfiːən/ | adj. | 睡莲的，水仙的；清澈的，清新的 | Relating to or resembling a nymph; inhabiting or frequenting the water. |  The nymphean blossoms floated gracefully on the pond's surface. 睡莲的花朵优雅地漂浮在池塘的水面上。|  The pool had a nymphean quality, its clear water reflecting the sunlight.  这个水池有一种水仙般的气质，清澈的水面倒映着阳光。 | 近义词: aquatic, watery,  limpid, fresh | 反义词: murky, polluted, terrestrial | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *nymphaeus, from Greek νύμφη (nýmphē, “bride, young woman, nymph”).
