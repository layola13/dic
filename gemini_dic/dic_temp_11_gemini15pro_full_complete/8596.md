anticlimax:/ˌæntiˈklaɪmæks/
n.|虎头蛇尾；突降法；[修辞]反高潮|A disappointing end to an exciting or impressive series of events.|The movie built to a thrilling climax, only to end in a disappointing anticlimax.|Synonyms: letdown, disappointment, bathos|Antonyms: climax, culmination|noun

anticlimactic:/ˌæntiklaɪˈmæktɪk/
adj.|虎头蛇尾的；令人扫兴的|Disappointing or ineffective after a dramatic or exciting start.|The ending of the play was anticlimactic after the intense first act.|Synonyms: disappointing, underwhelming, flat|Antonyms: climactic, exciting, thrilling|adjective
