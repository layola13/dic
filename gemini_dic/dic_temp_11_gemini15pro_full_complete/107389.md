lametta:/ləˈmɛtə/
n.
tinsel, metallic strips used for decoration
Thin, glittering metallic strips used as Christmas decoration.
The tree shimmered with silver lametta.  这棵树闪耀着银色的金银丝。
The cat was playing with the lametta. 猫在玩金银丝。
synonyms: tinsel, glitter, metallic thread
antonyms: plainness, simplicity
