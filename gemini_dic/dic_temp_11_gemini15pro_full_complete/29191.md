cashing:/ˈkæʃɪŋ/
verb
兑现; 将…兑换成现金
the process of exchanging a check, money order, etc., for cash.
I'm going to the bank for cashing a check. 我要去银行兑现一张支票。
He was cashing his paycheck when the robbery occurred. 他正在兑现他的工资支票时，抢劫案发生了。
Synonyms: redeeming, exchanging, converting
Antonyms: depositing, saving
