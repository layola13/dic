athermanous:/ˌeɪθərˈmeɪnəs/ |adj.| 不透热的；不透红外线的 |Not transmitting heat; opaque to infrared radiation.  | This material is athermanous and thus ideal for heat shielding. 这种材料不透热，因此是隔热的理想选择。| The athermanous window prevented the greenhouse from overheating.  不透热的窗户防止了温室过热。 |  diathermic, heat-insulating |  diathermanous, heat-conducting |adjective
