ill:/ɪl/| adj. adv.|生病的，不健康的；坏的，邪恶的；不吉利的；敌意的；困难的|Not in good health; harmful, bad, or unkind. Also used adverbially to mean 'badly' or 'poorly'. From Proto-Germanic *ubilaz "evil," from PIE *upelo- "bad, evil."|She's been ill for a week. 她病了一个星期了。|近义词：sick, unwell, diseased, ailing, unhealthy, harmful, bad, evil, wicked, hostile|反义词:healthy, well, good, kind|adjective, adverb

ill:/ɪl/| n.|坏事，祸害|Something harmful or bad.  Ultimately from the same Proto-Germanic source as the adjective.|The root of all ill is greed.  所有祸害的根源都是贪婪。|近义词：harm, evil, misfortune, trouble|反义词:good, benefit, blessing|noun
