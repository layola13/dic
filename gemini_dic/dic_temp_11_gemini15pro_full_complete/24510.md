broad-way's: /ˈbrɔːdweɪz/
proper noun
百老汇的；百老汇剧院的
Referring to something belonging to or associated with Broadway, the famous theatre district in New York City.
Broadway's bright lights drew aspiring actors from across the country. 百老汇明亮的灯光吸引了来自全国各地的有抱负的演员。
The longest-running musical in Broadway's history closed last year. 百老汇历史上演出时间最长的音乐剧去年停演了。
近义词:  New York theater's, the Great White Way's
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with "off-Broadway's" or "off-off-Broadway's")
词性: proper noun
