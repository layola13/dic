benumbed:/bɪˈnʌmd/| adj.|麻木的；失去感觉的|Deprived of physical sensation or the power to feel or move normally.|My fingers were benumbed with cold. 我的手指冻僵了。|Synonyms: numb, insensible, deadened, anesthetized|Antonyms: sensitive, responsive, aware|adjective

benumbed:/bɪˈnʌmd/| v. (past participle of benumb)|使麻木；使失去感觉|To make numb or insensitive; to deprive of physical sensation or the power to feel or move normally.|The tragic news benumbed her.  这悲惨的消息使她麻木了。|Synonyms: numb, deaden, dull, stupefy|Antonyms: stimulate, enliven, awaken|verb
