alleviative:/əˈliːviˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
缓解的，减轻的
Making something less severe or intense; relieving.
The doctor prescribed alleviative medication for her back pain. 医生给她开了缓解背痛的药。
Synonyms: soothing, mitigating, palliative, relieving, assuaging
Antonyms: aggravating, exacerbating, intensifying
词性: adjective
