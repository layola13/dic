hand-me-downs: /ˈhænd miː daʊnz/ | n. | 旧衣服，别人穿过的衣服 | Secondhand clothes, typically passed down from an older sibling or other relative to a younger one. | My younger brother always had to wear my hand-me-downs. 我的弟弟总是不得不穿我的旧衣服。

My closet is full of hand-me-downs from my older sister. 我的衣橱里满是姐姐穿过的旧衣服。


| Synonym: secondhand clothes, used clothes | Antonym: new clothes | noun |
