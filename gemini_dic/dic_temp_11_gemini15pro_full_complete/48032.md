decivilizations:/diːˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|去文明化，野蛮化|The process of losing the qualities and characteristics of a civilization; a decline or deterioration in social and cultural values, morals, or behavior.  The decivilizations of war often lead to widespread suffering and the breakdown of social order.|  The prolonged conflict led to the decivilizations of the region, with widespread looting and violence.  长期的冲突导致该地区野蛮化，到处都是抢劫和暴力。| degeneration, barbarization|civilization, refinement|noun
