nonfealties: /nɒnˈfiːəltiz/
n.
不效忠；不忠诚；违背誓言（的行为）
Lack of fealty; disloyalty; breach of sworn allegiance.
The knight's nonfealties led to his expulsion from the order.  这位骑士的不忠导致他被逐出骑士团。
近义词: treachery, disloyalty, perfidy, infidelity
反义词: loyalty, fidelity, allegiance
词性: 名词 (plural)


Etymology:
From non- + fealty.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fealty" comes from Old French "fealté," ultimately from Latin "fidelitas," meaning "faithfulness."
