africanity:/ˌæfrɪˈkænɪti/
n.
非洲性，非洲特性

Her art is a celebration of Africanity. 她的艺术是对非洲性的颂扬。

Africanity is expressed through music, art, and storytelling. 非洲性通过音乐、艺术和讲故事来表达。

近义词:Africanness, African character, African identity
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Westernization or Eurocentrism depending on the context)
