miocene:/ˈmaɪ.əˌsiːn/ | adj. n.| 中新世的，中新世 |  Relating to or denoting the epoch of geological time following the Oligocene and preceding the Pliocene, lasting from about 23 to 5.3 million years ago.  The Miocene epoch. | The Miocene epoch saw the rise of grasslands and grazing mammals. 中新世见证了草原和食草哺乳动物的兴起。 |  Fossil evidence suggests that many modern mammal groups originated in the Miocene. 化石证据表明，许多现代哺乳动物群体起源于中新世。 | 近义词: Tertiary period (更广义的时期) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其前后相邻的地质时期，例如 Oligocene, Pliocene) | 词性:  adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *Miocène*, from Greek *meíōn* (less) + *kainós* (new) + *-ēnos* (adjectival suffix), referring to the relatively smaller proportion of modern mollusks found in Miocene strata compared to the Pliocene.
