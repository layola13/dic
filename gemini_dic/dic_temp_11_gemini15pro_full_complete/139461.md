obstinacy:/ˈɒbstɪnəsi/ (US) /ˈɑːbstɪnəsi/ (UK)| n. |固执，顽固，倔强 |The state of being stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or chosen course of action, despite attempts to persuade one to do so.| His obstinacy prevented a quick resolution to the problem. 他的固执阻碍了问题的快速解决。|  Synonyms: stubbornness, pertinacity, tenacity, inflexibility, dogmatism, intransigence | Antonyms: flexibility, compliance, acquiescence, docility, malleability | noun
