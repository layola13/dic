hydrotic:/haɪˈdrɒtɪk/ /haɪˈdrɑːtɪk/
adjective
促水合的; 发汗的
inducing sweating; pertaining to sweat
Example: The doctor prescribed a hydrotic medication to help reduce the patient's fever. 医生开了促水合的药物来帮助病人退烧。

Example:  Hydrotic drugs can be useful in certain medical situations. 发汗药物在某些医疗情况下是有用的。

近义词:sudorific, diaphoretic
反义词:anhidrotic, antihidrotic
词性:adjective


词源: From Greek *ὑδρōτικός (hudrōtikos) < *ὑδρῶτις (hudrōtis), "sweat" < *ὑδρῶτα (hudrōta), "sweat" ultimately from *ὕδωρ (hudōr) "water".
