indirectly:/ˌɪn.daɪˈrekt.li/ /ˌɪn.dəˈrekt.li/
adverb
间接地
not in a direct way; not directly
例句：He indirectly hinted that he wanted a raise. 他间接地暗示他想要加薪。

例句：The disease is indirectly caused by poor sanitation. 这种疾病是间接由卫生条件差引起的。

近义词：obliquely, circuitously, tacitly
反义词：directly, explicitly, straightforwardly
词性: adverb
