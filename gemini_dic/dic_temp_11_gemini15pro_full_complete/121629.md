misparaphrasing: /ˌmɪspærəˈfreɪzɪŋ/
noun
释义错误，歪曲原意
The act of paraphrasing something incorrectly, often by distorting the original meaning or leaving out important details.
He was accused of misparaphrasing the scientist's findings to support his own theory. 他被指责歪曲了科学家的发现来支持自己的理论。
近义词: misrepresenting, distorting, misinterpreting
反义词: accurately paraphrasing, correctly representing
词性: noun
