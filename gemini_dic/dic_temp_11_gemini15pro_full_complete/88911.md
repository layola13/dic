hold-ground:/ˈhoʊld ɡraʊnd/ |v. |坚守阵地，不退却；维持原状；保持立场| to maintain one's position; to refuse to yield or retreat; to remain firm in one's beliefs or convictions | The soldiers held their ground despite heavy enemy fire. 士兵们尽管受到猛烈的敌军炮火，仍然坚守阵地。|synonyms: stand firm, hold fast, stay put, maintain one's position | antonyms: retreat, withdraw, give way, back down | verb

hold-ground:/ˈhoʊld ɡraʊnd/ |n. |根据情况,可以翻译为据点，阵地，立场|the position that someone is defending| They lost hold-ground to the enemy. 他们失去了阵地/据点。|synonyms: position, standpoint, stance | antonyms: surrender, concession | noun
