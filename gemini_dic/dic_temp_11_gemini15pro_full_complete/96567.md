indrum:/ˈɪndrʌm/| n.| 敲击声，鼓声；消息，信息|A rhythmic beating, especially of a drum; news or information, often communicated quickly or in a secretive way.|The indrum of the distant drums signaled the approach of the warriors. 远处鼓声预示着战士们的到来。|近义词：beat, rhythm, drumming, news, report, tidings|反义词: silence|词性:名词

词源:  From Scottish Gaelic *`indream` "noise, clamour", related to Irish Gaelic `indream` "noise, uproar, murmur". Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *`en-drom-` "to run against".
