captain:/ˈkæptɪn/ (US), /ˈkæptən/ (UK)| n. v.|队长，船长，机长；指挥，率领|A captain is a leader, especially of a sports team or a ship/aircraft.  The captain steered the ship through the storm. 船长掌舵引领船只度过了风暴。|例句：The captain led his team to victory. 队长带领他的团队取得了胜利。|近义词：leader, chief, commander, skipper (for boats)|反义词：subordinate, crew member|词性:noun, verb

captain:/ˈkæptɪn/ (US), /ˈkæptən/ (UK)| v.|指挥，率领|To captain means to lead or command a team or ship. She captained the Olympic swim team. 她担任奥运游泳队的队长。|例句：He captained the ship for many years. 他指挥这艘船很多年了。|近义词：lead, command, head|反义词：follow|词性:verb
