inscrutablenesses:/ɪnˈskruːtəbəlˌnɛsɪz/
|n.| 不可测性，难以理解，神秘莫测 |The inscrutablenesses of his motives made it difficult to trust him. 他的动机难以捉摸，使人难以信任他。|例句：The inscrutablenesses of ancient hieroglyphs continue to fascinate scholars. 古代象形文字的神秘性继续吸引着学者们。

The inscrutablenesses of her expression made it impossible to guess her thoughts. 她表情的神秘莫测让人无法猜测她的想法。|近义词：enigma, mystery, impenetrability, unfathomability|反义词：clarity, transparency, obviousness|词性：名词

词源: From Late Middle English inscrutable + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin inscrūtābilis (“unsearchable, incomprehensible”) + -ness.
