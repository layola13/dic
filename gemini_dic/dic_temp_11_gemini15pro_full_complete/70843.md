finiteless:/ˈfaɪnaɪtlɪs/| adj.|无限的，无穷的|Without limits; endless.  Formed by adding the suffix "-less" (meaning "without") to "finite" (meaning "having limits").  It describes something that is not finite, that extends indefinitely.|The universe seems finiteless and full of mysteries. 宇宙似乎无边无际，充满了神秘。|The possibilities for human creativity are finiteless. 人类创造力的可能性是无限的。|近义词：infinite, boundless, limitless, unending|反义词：finite, limited, bounded|词性:形容词
