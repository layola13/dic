dynasty:/ˈdaɪnəsti/| n. |王朝；世家|A sequence of rulers of the same family or line.  A powerful group or family that maintains its position for a considerable time.|The Ming dynasty ruled China for centuries. 明朝统治了中国数百年。|synonyms: house, lineage, reign, empire|antonyms: anarchy, disorder|noun
