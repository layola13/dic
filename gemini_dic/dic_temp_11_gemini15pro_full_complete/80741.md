goshawk:/ˈɡɒsˌhɔːk/ (US) /ˈɡɒsˌhɔːk/ (UK)| n. |苍鹰|A large, powerful hawk with short, broad wings and a long tail, known for its swift flight and hunting prowess. |The goshawk plummeted from the sky, seizing a rabbit in its talons. 苍鹰从天而降，用爪子抓住了一只兔子。| The falconer trained the goshawk to hunt small game.  驯鹰师训练苍鹰捕猎小型猎物。 |近义词: hawk, raptor |反义词: prey |词性: noun
