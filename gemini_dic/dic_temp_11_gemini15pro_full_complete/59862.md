ectypography:/ɛkˈtaɪpɒɡrəfi/
n.
印刷术，排版术
The art or process of producing printed material, especially books or pamphlets.  It encompasses the selection of typefaces, paper, illustrations, and the arrangement of text and images on a page.
Gutenberg's invention of movable type revolutionized ectotypography.  古腾堡发明活字印刷术彻底改变了印刷术。
近义词: printing, typography
反义词: None
