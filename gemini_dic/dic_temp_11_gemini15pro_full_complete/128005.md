nephridium:/nɪˈfrɪdiəm/ | n. | 排泄器，肾管 |  A tubule open to the exterior that acts as an organ of excretion or osmoregulation. It typically has ciliated or flagellated cells and is found in many invertebrates, especially annelids, mollusks, and crustaceans. | Earthworms have nephridia to remove metabolic waste. 蚯蚓有肾管来排出代谢废物。 |  Synonym: excretory organ, renal tubule | Antonym: N/A | noun

nephridia:/nɪˈfrɪdiə/ | n. | 排泄器，肾管（复数） | Plural form of nephridium. | Many invertebrates possess nephridia for waste removal. 许多无脊椎动物拥有肾管用于废物清除。 | Synonym: excretory organs, renal tubules | Antonym: N/A | noun
