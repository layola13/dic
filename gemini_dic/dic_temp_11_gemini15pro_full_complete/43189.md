n.

The quality of being crabbed; bad temper; peevishness; sourness; difficulty of reading, as of manuscript.
His crabbedness's made it difficult for him to maintain friendships. 他的暴躁脾气让他难以维持友谊。

crabbed:/ˈkræbɪd/
adj.
暴躁的；易怒的；潦草的
(of handwriting) difficult to read; illegible.
Bad-tempered and irritable.
The old man's crabbed handwriting was almost impossible to decipher. 老人的潦草字迹几乎无法辨认。
近义词：grumpy, irritable, irascible, cantankerous, churlish
反义词：good-humored, affable, amiable, pleasant, genial
词性: 形容词

词源：crabbedness's 源自形容词 crabbed，意为“暴躁的，易怒的，潦草的”。crabbed 源自 crab（螃蟹），可能因为螃蟹横行霸道、脾气暴躁而得名。crabbed 加上后缀 -ness 构成名词 crabbedness，表示“暴躁，乖戾，字迹潦草”。最后加上所有格 's 表示“暴躁的……”或“潦草的……”。
