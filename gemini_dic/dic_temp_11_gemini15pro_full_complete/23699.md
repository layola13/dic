brasque:/bræsk/ | n. | 捣固, 捣实; 捣固材料, 衬层 | A lining of charcoal or other refractory material for a furnace or crucible, often mixed with clay or other binder. It is used to create a smooth, heat-resistant surface. | The crucible was lined with a brasque of charcoal and clay. 坩埚内衬是用木炭和粘土捣固而成的。| synonyms: lining, refractory coating | antonyms: unlined, bare | noun

brasque:/bræsk/ | v. | 捣固, 衬里 | To line (a furnace or crucible) with a brasque. |  The goldsmith brasqued the crucible before melting the gold. 金匠在熔化黄金之前先给坩埚做了衬里。 | synonyms: line, coat | antonyms: unline, strip | verb
