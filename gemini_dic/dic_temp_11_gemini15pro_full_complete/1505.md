acetimetry: /æsɪˈtɪmɪtri/ | n. | 醋酸测定法 | The measurement of acetic acid in a substance.  |  Acetimetry is used in the food industry to determine the acidity of vinegar. 醋酸测定法用于食品工业中测定醋的酸度。| 近义词: acidimetry (酸度测定法) | 反义词: alkalimetry (碱度测定法) | 词性: noun

词源:  From French *acétimétrie*, from *acétique* (“acetic”) + *-métrie* (“-metry”). Ultimately from Latin *acetum* (“vinegar”) + Ancient Greek *μέτρον* (*métron*, “measure”).
