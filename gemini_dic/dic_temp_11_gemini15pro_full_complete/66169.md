excesses:/ˈɛksɛsɪz/
noun
过度；过量；放肆；无节制
An instance of exceeding a proper, normal, or moderate limit.
The excesses of the Roman emperors are well documented. 罗马皇帝的放纵行为都有详细记载。

excesses:/ˈɛksɛsɪz/
noun
过分的行为；暴行
Extreme behavior; outrageous conduct.
The rebels committed terrible excesses during the war. 叛军在战争期间犯下了可怕的暴行。


excesses:/ˈɛksɛsɪz/
noun
(利润、收入等的)超出部分，盈余
The amount or degree by which one thing or quantity exceeds another.
The company's excesses in profit were returned to shareholders. 公司的超额利润返还给了股东。



Synonym:
overindulgence, extravagance, immoderation, surplus, superfluity, intemperance, dissipation

Antonym:
moderation, restraint, temperance, deficiency, lack, scarcity

词性: noun (名词)
