jaillike:/ˈdʒeɪlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像监狱的；监狱似的
Resembling or suggestive of a jail, especially in being confining or unpleasant.
The small, windowless room felt jaillike.  这间小而没有窗户的房间感觉像监狱一样。
The strict rules and regulations made the school feel jaillike. 严格的规章制度让学校感觉像监狱一样。
近义词:prisonlike, confining, restrictive
反义词:free, open, liberating
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From jail +‎ -like.  jail 源自古法语 jaiole，最终源自拉丁语 caveola “小笼子”。
