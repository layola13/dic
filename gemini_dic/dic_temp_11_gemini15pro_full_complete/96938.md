inextinguishable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
不能熄灭的；无法遏制的
Not able to be extinguished, quenched, or suppressed.

His inextinguishable enthusiasm inspired everyone around him.
他永不熄灭的热情鼓舞了周围的每一个人。

The memory of that day remains inextinguishable in my mind.
那一天的记忆在我心中永不磨灭。

Synonyms: unquenchable, enduring, persistent, ineradicable
Antonyms: extinguishable, quenchable, temporary, fleeting
词性: 形容词
