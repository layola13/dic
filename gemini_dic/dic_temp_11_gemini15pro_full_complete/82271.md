groundnuts:/ˈɡraʊndnʌts/| n. |花生|Peanuts; the seeds of the Arachis hypogaea plant, grown for their oil and edible seeds.|例句：The farmer harvested a large crop of groundnuts this year. 今年农民收获了大量的花生。

例句：Groundnuts are a good source of protein. 花生是蛋白质的良好来源。|近义词：peanuts, earthnuts|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其用途相反的食物，例如肉类 meats  作为蛋白质的另一种来源) |词性: 名词

词源: From ground + nut, literally "nut from the ground".
