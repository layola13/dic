biorhythmists:/ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðəmɪsts/
n.
生物节律学家
A person who believes in or studies biorhythms.  A biorhythmist might consult charts to predict a person's physical, emotional, and intellectual highs and lows.
Biorhythmists claim they can predict your good and bad days. 生物节律学家声称他们可以预测你的好日子和坏日子。
Biorhythmists believe that our lives are affected by physical, emotional and intellectual cycles. 生物节律学家认为，我们的生活受到生理、情感和智力周期的影响。

近义词:  cycle theorists, chronobiologists
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly skeptics)
词性: Noun
