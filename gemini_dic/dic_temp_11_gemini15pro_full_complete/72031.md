flippantly:/ˈflɪpəntli/| adv.|轻率地，轻浮地|In a way that shows a lack of seriousness or respect. |He flippantly dismissed their concerns. 他轻率地驳回了他们的担忧。|She flippantly remarked that it was no big deal. 她漫不经心地说没什么大不了的。|近义词：casually, frivolously, impertinently, disrespectfully|反义词：seriously, respectfully, earnestly|词性: 副词

flippant:/ˈflɪpənt/| adj.|轻率的，轻浮的|Not showing a serious or respectful attitude. |a flippant remark 轻率的评论|He gave a flippant answer to a serious question. 他对一个严肃的问题给出了轻率的回答。|近义词：glib, frivolous, cheeky, impertinent|反义词：serious, respectful, earnest|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From flip (v.) + -ant.  The verb "flip" originally meant "to flick" or "to snap," suggesting a quick, dismissive action. This sense evolved into the adjective "flippant," describing a similarly dismissive and disrespectful attitude.
