monomaniacs:/ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniæks/ | n. |偏执狂患者；独断专行者|A person who is excessively preoccupied with one idea or subject.|He's a bit of a monomaniac about keeping the house clean. 他有点洁癖，对保持房子干净过度执着。|
The CEO's monomaniacal focus on profits led him to neglect other important aspects of the business. 首席执行官对利润的偏执让他忽略了业务的其他重要方面。|
Synonyms: fanatic, obsessive, zealot|Antonyms: moderate, balanced|Noun
