gargler:/ˈɡɑːrɡlər/| n.|漱口者；含漱剂|A person who gargles or something used for gargling.  A gargler is someone who rinses their throat with a liquid kept in motion by exhaling through it.|He's a loud gargler in the morning. 他早上漱口声音很大。|Synonym: mouthwash user, throat rinser|Antonym: N/A|noun
