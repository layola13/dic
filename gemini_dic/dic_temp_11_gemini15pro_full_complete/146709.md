overhigh:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪ/| adj.|过高的，过度的|Excessively high; higher than is desirable or necessary.  It combines the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") with the adjective "high."|The price of the house was overhigh for the neighborhood.  这栋房子的价格对于这个社区来说太高了。| The overhigh temperature damaged the crops.  过高的温度损坏了农作物。|excessive, exorbitant, inflated, unreasonable|low, reasonable, moderate|adjective
