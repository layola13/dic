例句：His noninstinctual aversion to spiders stemmed from a childhood incident. 他对蜘蛛的厌恶并非本能，而是源于童年的一件事。|近义词：learned, acquired, conditioned, deliberate, reasoned|反义词：instinctive, innate, inherent, natural, intuitive|词性: adjective
