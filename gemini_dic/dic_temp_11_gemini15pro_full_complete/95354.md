inceptions:/ɪnˈsɛpʃənz/
n.
开始，开端，初期; 构思，设想
The inception of the project was in 2010.  这个项目的开始是在2010年。
The inception of the idea came from a dream. 这个想法的构思来源于一个梦。
Etymology:  From Latin *inceptionem, accusative singular of *inceptio (“a beginning”), from incipere (“to begin”), from in- + capere (“to take”).
Synonym: beginning, commencement, outset, origin, start
Antonym: conclusion, end, termination
noun
