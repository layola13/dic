almightinesses:/ɔːlˈmaɪtinɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
全能,无限权力,神性 (plural of almightiness)
The state or quality of being almighty; omnipotence.  Supreme power.
例句：He spoke of the almightinesses of the various gods. 他谈到了众神的全能。
例句：The ancient myths often exaggerated the almightinesses of their deities.  古代神话经常夸大他们神灵的全能。
近义词：omnipotence, supremacy, sovereignty
反义词：powerlessness, weakness, impotence
词性：名词

词源:
From Middle English *almyghtinesse, equivalent to almighty +‎ -ness.
