finch:/fɪntʃ/| n. |雀科鸣鸟|A small songbird of the Fringillidae family, characterized by its short, conical bill and often brightly colored plumage. |例句：A goldfinch perched on the branch. 一只金翅雀栖息在树枝上。

例句：The finch sang a cheerful melody.  那只雀唱了一支欢快的歌。|近义词：bird, songbird|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *finc, from Proto-Germanic *finkiz, from Proto-Indo-European *penkʷ- (“five; finch”). Cognate with Dutch vink (“finch”), German Fink (“finch”), Swedish fink (“finch”). The connection with "five" is uncertain, perhaps referring to the five primary feathers of the wing.
