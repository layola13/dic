hypothenuses:/haɪˈpɒtənˌjuːzɪz/
|n.|斜边（复数）
|The length of the hypotenuse can be calculated using the Pythagorean theorem.|可以使用勾股定理计算斜边的长度。
|The hypotenuses of these right triangles are all different lengths.|这些直角三角形的斜边长度各不相同。
|近义词:hypotenuse (单数)|
|反义词:无|
|词性:名词|


Etymology->
From Late Latin *hypotenusa, from Ancient Greek ὑποτείνουσα (hupoteínousa, “subtending line, hypotenuse”), present participle feminine of ὑποτείνω (hupoteínō, “I subtend, stretch under”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + τείνω (teínō, “I stretch, extend”).
