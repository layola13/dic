creationists:/kriˈeɪʃənɪsts/
n.
创造论者，神造论者
A person who believes that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.
Creationists believe that the Earth is only a few thousand years old. 创造论者相信地球只有几千年的历史。
The museum exhibit sparked debate between scientists and creationists.  博物馆的展览引发了科学家和神创论者之间的辩论。

近义词:
anti-evolutionists 反进化论者

反义词:
evolutionists 进化论者
词性: noun
