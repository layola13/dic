becquerel: /bɛkəˈrɛl/ | n. | 贝克勒尔 |A unit of radioactivity, corresponding to one nuclear disintegration per second.  It is named after Antoine Henri Becquerel, who discovered radioactivity. | The banana has a radioactivity of about 15 becquerels.  一根香蕉的放射性大约是15贝克勒尔。|  The sample emitted 100 becquerels of radiation.  该样品发射了100贝克勒尔的辐射。 | synonym:  unit of radioactivity | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement.  One might consider terms like "stability" or "inertness" in a broader context.) | noun
