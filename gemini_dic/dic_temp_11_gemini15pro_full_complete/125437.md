multiplant:/ˈmʌltiˌplænt/| adj.|多工厂的，多植株的|Having or involving multiple plants or factories; relating to a company operating several plants, especially in different locations.|The multiplant firm enjoys economies of scale. 这家多工厂公司享有规模经济效益。|近义词：multi-unit, multi-factory|反义词:single-plant, single-factory|词性:adjective
multiplant:/ˈmʌltiˌplænt/| n.|多植株|A group or system of multiple plants, often considered collectively.|The multiplant in the greenhouse was thriving. 温室里的多植株生长茂盛。|近义词: multiple plants|反义词: single plant|词性:noun
