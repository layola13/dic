episternum: /ɪˈpɪstɜːrnəm/ /ɪˈpɪstərnəm/ | n. | 胸骨前板;上胸骨|A plate of cartilage or bone in front of the sternum of many vertebrates, especially amphibians and reptiles.  It provides attachment for the clavicles and coracoids. |  The episternum of the frog provides an anchor point for the muscles involved in breathing. 青蛙的胸骨前板为参与呼吸的肌肉提供了一个附着点。 | The salamander's episternum is a cartilaginous structure. 蝾螈的胸骨前板是一种软骨结构。| synonyms: interclavicle, presternum | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific anatomical part) | noun
