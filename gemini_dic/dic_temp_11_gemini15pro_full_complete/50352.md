depolishes:/dɪˈpɒlɪʃɪz/
verb
擦掉…的光泽；使失去光泽
Removes the polish from something; makes something less shiny or elegant.
The harsh cleaner depolishes the wood.  这种强力清洁剂会使木头失去光泽。
The constant handling of the silverware depolishes it.  不断地触摸银器会使其失去光泽。
synonyms: dulls, tarnishes, mats
antonyms: polishes, shines, burnishes
词性: 动词
