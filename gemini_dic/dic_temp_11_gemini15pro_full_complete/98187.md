insculptured:/ɪnˈskʌlptʃərd/ | adj. | 雕刻的；铭刻的 | Carved into a surface. |  The words "Forever in our hearts" were insculptured on the tombstone. 墓碑上刻着“永远在我们心中”的字样。 | The intricate designs were insculptured into the golden bracelet.  这些复杂的设计被雕刻在金手镯上。| 近义词：carved, engraved, etched | 反义词: plain, smooth | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insculptus*, past participle of *insculpere*  ("to carve in, engrave"), from *in-* ("in, on") + *sculpere* ("to carve").
