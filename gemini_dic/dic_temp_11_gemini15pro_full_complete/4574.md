alcoholature: /ˈælkəhɒlətʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈælkəhɒlətʃər/ (UK)
n.
酊剂；药酒
A medicinal preparation made by extracting the medicinal properties of fresh plants with alcohol.
Alcoholatures are made from fresh plants, preserving their volatile constituents.  酊剂是由新鲜植物制成的，保留了它们的挥发性成分。
The herbalist recommended an alcoholature of echinacea for her cold.  草药师推荐用紫锥菊酊剂治疗她的感冒。
近义词: tincture, extract
反义词: None
词性: noun


alcoholature: /ˈælkəhɒlətʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈælkəhɒlətʃər/ (UK)
词源: From French *alcoolature*, from *alcool* (alcohol) + *-ature* (suffix forming nouns of action or result). Ultimately from Arabic *al-kuḥl* (kohl, a fine powder used as eye makeup).
词性: noun
