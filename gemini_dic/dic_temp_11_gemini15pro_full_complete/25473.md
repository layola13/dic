bulletining:/ˈbʊlɪtɪnɪŋ/
n.
发布公告；公布；用公告发布
The act of issuing or posting bulletins.
The company used bulletining to inform employees of the new policy. 公司用公告的方式通知员工新政策。
announcement, notification, posting
concealment, suppression
noun
