nominatives:/ˈnɑːmɪnətɪvz/| n.|主格|The case of a noun or pronoun when it is the subject of a verb.|The nominative case is used for the subject of a sentence. 主格用于句子的主语。|nominative case, subjective case|objective case|noun

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The nominative pronoun "he" performs the action.  他执行这个动作。
She sings beautifully. 她唱歌很优美。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is reading a book. 他正在读一本书。
She is playing the piano. 她正在弹钢琴。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has finished his homework. 他已经完成了他的作业。
She has traveled to many countries. 她去过很多国家旅行。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been studying English for five years. 他已经学习英语五年了。
She has been working on this project for months. 她已经在这个项目上工作了好几个月了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He visited his grandparents last week. 他上周去看望了他的祖父母。
She baked a cake yesterday. 她昨天烤了一个蛋糕。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was watching TV when I called. 我打电话时他正在看电视。
She was cooking dinner when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时她正在做晚饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had already eaten dinner before I arrived. 在我到达之前他已经吃过晚饭了。
She had finished her work before the deadline. 她在截止日期前完成了她的工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been waiting for an hour before the bus arrived. 在公共汽车到达之前，他已经等了一个小时。
She had been working on that project for months before it was finally completed. 在最终完成之前，她已经在这个项目上工作了几个月。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will go to the store tomorrow. 他明天会去商店。
She will graduate next year. 她明年将毕业。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He will be traveling to Europe next summer. 他明年夏天将去欧洲旅行。
She will be attending a conference next week. 她下周将参加一个会议。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
He will have finished his degree by next year. 到明年，他将完成他的学位。
She will have written her novel by the end of the year. 到年底，她将写完她的小说。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
He will have been living in London for five years by next June. 到明年六月，他将在伦敦生活五年了。
She will have been studying French for ten years by the time she graduates. 到她毕业的时候，她将学习法语十年了。


虚拟语气:
If I were he, I would accept the offer. 如果我是他，我会接受这个提议。
If she had more time, she would learn to play the guitar. 如果她有更多的时间，她会学习弹吉他。


被动语态:
The book was written by him. 这本书是他写的。
The song was sung by her. 这首歌是她唱的。


疑问句:
Was he at the party? 他在派对上吗？
Did she finish her homework? 她完成作业了吗？


否定句:
He is not a doctor. 他不是医生。
She does not like spicy food. 她不喜欢辛辣的食物。


PHRASE->
He is in the nominative case. 他是主格。
She is the subject of the verb. 她是动词的主语。
The nominative form of the pronoun is "he".  这个代词的主格形式是“他”。
The sentence uses "she" in the nominative.  这个句子用“她”作主格。
They, in the nominative case, performed the action. 他们，作为主格，执行了这个动作。
