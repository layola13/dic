gadzookery:/ˌɡædˈzuːkəri/
noun
古怪的言行；滑稽可笑的举动
An archaic, affected, or excessively ornamented style, especially of language or writing; whimsical or outlandish behavior.
His pronouncements were filled with such gadzookery that no one took him seriously. 他的声明充满了如此古怪的言辞，以至于没有人把他当回事。
Near Synonyms: affectation, pretension, mannerism, flourish, ornamentation
Antonyms: simplicity, naturalness, sincerity
