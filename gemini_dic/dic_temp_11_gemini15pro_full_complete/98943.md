inter-caste:/ˌɪntərˈkɑːst/ /ˌɪntərˈkæst/ | adj. |种姓间的，不同种姓之间的| Relating to or involving people of different castes. |  Inter-caste marriage is still a sensitive issue in some societies. 种姓间通婚在一些社会中仍然是一个敏感的问题。|  Inter-caste tensions sometimes lead to violence. 不同种姓之间的紧张关系有时会导致暴力。| 近义词: cross-caste | 反义词: intra-caste | 词性: adjective

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"caste": from Portuguese "casta" meaning "breed, race, lineage," ultimately from Latin "castus" meaning "pure, chaste."
