hypothecator:/haɪˈpɒθɪkeɪtər/ |n.| 抵押人，质押人 |A person who hypothecates something, especially property, as security for a loan.| The hypothecator retained possession of the ship, but the bank held the title as collateral. 抵押人保留了船只的拥有权，但银行持有所有权作为抵押品。|Synonym: pledger, mortgagor|Antonym: pledgee, mortgagee|noun
