bowerbird:/ˈbaʊərˌbɜːrd/| n.|园丁鸟|A bird of the family Ptilonorhynchidae, the males of which build elaborate bowers decorated with brightly coloured objects to attract females.|The bowerbird is known for its unique mating rituals.  园丁鸟以其独特的求偶仪式而闻名。|The male bowerbird constructs an elaborate bower to attract a mate. 雄性园丁鸟建造一个精致的凉亭来吸引配偶。|synonyms: decorator bird|antonyms: N/A|noun
