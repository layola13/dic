appliably:/əˈplaɪəbli/ | adv. | 可应用地，适用地 |  In a way that can be applied; practically or relevantly. | This theory is not appliably to the current situation. 这个理论不适用于当前的情况。| The new regulations are appliably to all businesses. 新规定适用于所有企业。 |近义词：practically, suitably, relevantly | 反义词:inappliably, irrelevantly | 词性:副词

Etymology:
From "applicable" + "-ly." "Applicable" comes from Late Latin "applicabilis," from "applicare" (to apply), from "ad-" (to) + "plicare" (to fold).
