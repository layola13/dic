creatorship:/ˈkriːeɪtərʃɪp/
n.
创造力，创造性；创造者身份
The state or quality of being a creator; the act of creating something.
His paintings demonstrate his remarkable creatorship. 他的绘画展现了他非凡的创造力。
creativity, innovation, inventiveness, originality
destruction, imitation
noun
