noninstructiveness:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
n.
无益; 没有启发性
lack of informative or educational value.
The noninstructiveness of the lecture left the students bored and confused.  这堂课毫无启发性，让学生们感到无聊和困惑。
Synonyms: unhelpfulness, uselessness, futility, pointlessness
Antonyms: helpfulness, usefulness, instructiveness, value
词性: 名词
