drossier:/ˈdrɒsiə(r)/ (US) /ˈdrɒsɪeɪ(r)/ (UK)| n. |档案，卷宗|A file or dossier is a collection of documents that contain information about a particular person or subject. |The FBI has a thick dossier on him.  联邦调查局有关于他的厚厚档案。|  The secret service keeps dossiers on all foreign dignitaries. 特勤局保留所有外国政要的档案。|synonyms: file, record, report, document, profile| antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "lack of information" or "nothing")| noun
