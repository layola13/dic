ballot:/ˈbælət/| n. v.|选票，投票；投票表决|A piece of paper used to cast a vote in an election or a survey.  A process of voting.|例句：Each member cast a secret ballot. 每位成员进行无记名投票。|近义词：vote, poll, election|反义词:None|Noun
ballot:/ˈbælət/| v.|投票表决|To vote or decide by using ballots.|例句：The union members balloted for strike action. 工会成员投票决定是否罢工。|近义词：vote, poll|反义词:None|Verb
