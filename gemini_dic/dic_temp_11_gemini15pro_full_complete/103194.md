jesuits:/ˈdʒɛzjuɪts/ | n. |耶稣会士；耶稣会成员 | Members of the Society of Jesus, a Roman Catholic religious order founded by Ignatius of Loyola. | The Jesuits have been influential in education and missionary work. 耶稣会在教育和传教工作方面很有影响力。| Example: The Jesuits established many universities around the world. 耶稣会在世界各地建立了许多大学。|  Synonyms: Society of Jesus members | Antonyms: None | Noun


jesuit:/ˈdʒɛzjuɪt/ | adj. |耶稣会的；耶稣会士的 | Relating to the Society of Jesus or its members. | He received a Jesuit education. 他接受了耶稣会的教育。| Example: Jesuit principles emphasize service to others. 耶稣会的原则强调服务他人。| Synonyms: Ignatian | Antonyms: None | Adjective
