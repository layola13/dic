noncognates:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːɡneɪts/| n.| 非同源词|Words in different languages that look similar but have different meanings, derived from different roots.|The words "bank" (financial institution) in English and "bank" (river bank) in Dutch are noncognates, despite their similar spelling.|近义词:false friends, false cognates|反义词:cognates|词性:noun
