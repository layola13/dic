aeroponics:/ˌeərəˈpɒnɪks/ /ˌɛrəˈpɑːnɪks/| n. |气栽法；雾培法|A technique of growing plants without soil in which the roots are suspended in air and sprayed with a nutrient solution.|例句：Aeroponics allows for faster growth and higher yields compared to traditional soil-based agriculture.  气栽法与传统的土壤农业相比，可以使植物生长更快，产量更高。

例句：The farmer decided to experiment with aeroponics in his greenhouse.  这位农民决定在他的温室里尝试气培法。

|近义词：hydroponics, soilless culture|反义词: geoponics|词性:noun
