colorizations:/ˌkʌlərɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|着色，彩色化|The process of adding color to a black and white image or film. |例句：The colorizations of old black and white movies have brought them to a new generation. 老黑白电影的彩色化将它们带给了一代新人。

例句：Modern technology allows for seamless colorizations, making it hard to distinguish from originally colored footage. 现代科技可以让彩色化天衣无缝，很难与原始彩色影片区分开来。|近义词：coloring, tinting|反义词:monochroming, desaturation|词性:noun
