desynchronized:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzd/
adjective
去同步的，不同步的
Out of synchronization; not occurring at the same time or rate.
The audio and video are desynchronized, making the movie difficult to watch. 音频和视频不同步，导致电影难以观看。
The dancers' movements became desynchronized, resulting in a chaotic performance. 舞蹈演员的动作变得不同步，导致演出混乱。
近义词: asynchronous, out of sync, misaligned, uncoordinated
反义词: synchronized, in sync, aligned, coordinated
词性: adjective

desynchronize:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪz/
verb
使不同步，去同步化
To cause to be out of synchronization.
The sudden power surge desynchronized the server from the network. 突然的电源浪涌导致服务器与网络不同步。
Rapid changes in temperature can desynchronize the biological clock.  温度的快速变化会使生物钟不同步。
近义词: uncouple, disengage, disrupt
反义词: synchronize, couple, engage
词性: verb

desynchronization:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
去同步化，不同步
The state of being desynchronized.
The desynchronization of the circadian rhythm can lead to sleep disorders.  昼夜节律的失调会导致睡眠障碍。
The network experienced a desynchronization, causing data loss. 网络经历了一次不同步，导致数据丢失。
近义词: asynchrony, misalignment, incoordination
反义词: synchronization, alignment, coordination
词性: noun


词源:  From de- + synchronize + -ized.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove," "synchronize" means to cause to occur at the same time, and "-ized" is a suffix that forms verbs and adjectives.
