amoebas:/əˈmiːbəz/ (US), /əˈmiːbiːz/ (UK)| n. |变形虫|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amoebas are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic.|Amoebas are fascinating organisms to observe under a microscope. 在显微镜下观察变形虫是很有趣的。|
|synonyms: protozoan, amoeba, rhizopod|antonyms: multicellular organism|noun|
