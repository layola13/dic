filings:/ˈfaɪlɪŋz/
n.
文件; 备案; 存档; (尤指提交给官方的) 申请文件，存档材料
Official documents or other papers submitted to a government office or other authority.
The company’s filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission revealed significant losses. 该公司提交给证券交易委员会的文件显示出巨额亏损。

近义词:documents, records, papers, submissions
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"withdrawals"表示撤回文件)


词性: 名词 (plural noun)


词源: filing (n.) + -s (plural suffix)
From file (v.) + -ing (suffix forming nouns from verbs).
Ultimately from Old French filer "to string documents on a thread or wire," from fil "thread," from Latin filum "thread."
