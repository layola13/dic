outdeliver:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈlɪ.vər/
verb
超越交付；在交付方面胜过
To deliver more or better than a competitor or expectation.
Our company consistently outdelivers its competitors in customer service. 我们的公司在客户服务方面一直优于竞争对手。
We aim to outdeliver on our promises. 我们的目标是超越我们的承诺。
近义词: outperform, surpass, exceed
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性: verb
