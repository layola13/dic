clanship: /ˈklænʃɪp/
n.
氏族关系；宗族观念
The state of belonging to a clan; the spirit or system of clannish association.
The Highlands of Scotland were long known for their strong clanship. 苏格兰高地长期以来以其强大的氏族关系而闻名。
The clanship in the village was so strong that outsiders were rarely welcomed.  村里的宗族观念很强，外来者很少受到欢迎。

近义词: kinship, tribalism
反义词: individualism, disunity
词性: noun
