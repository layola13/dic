lumen:/ˈluːmən/| n.|流明（光通量单位）|A lumen is a unit of measurement of luminous flux, which is the total amount of visible light emitted by a source per unit of time.  It measures how much light is being output.|The new LED bulb has a lumen output of 800.  这个新的LED灯泡的光通量输出为800流明。|Synonyms:  light unit, lux (when considering area), candlepower (when considering intensity) |Antonyms: darkness|noun
