debeige:/deɪˈbeɪʒ/
adjective
去米色的；还原到本色的
To remove the beige color from something; to restore something to its original, unbleached color.
The designer decided to debeige the living room by adding pops of vibrant colors.  设计师决定通过添加鲜艳的色彩来去除客厅的米黄色。
The old photographs were debeiged using digital software.  这些老照片使用数码软件去除了米黄色。
近义词:neutralize, restore
反义词:beige, tint
词性:adjective
