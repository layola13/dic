overexposure:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊʒər/| n.|过度曝光；过度暴露；过分宣传|Excessive exposure to something, such as sunlight, a chemical, or publicity.|The overexposure of the celebrity in the media led to a decline in his popularity.  这位名人在媒体上的过度曝光导致他的人气下降。|Synonyms: excess, surplus, overdose|Antonyms: underexposure, protection, concealment|noun

overexpose:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊz/| vt.|过度曝光；过度暴露；过分宣传|To expose something excessively, such as film to light or a person to publicity.|The photographer overexposed the film, resulting in a washed-out image. 摄影师过度曝光了胶卷，导致图像褪色。|Synonyms: overdo, saturate, bombard|Antonyms: underexpose, protect, conceal|verb
