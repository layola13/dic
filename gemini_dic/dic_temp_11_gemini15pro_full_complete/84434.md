hallucinatory:/həˈluːsɪnətɔːri/ /həˈluːsɪnəˌtɔːri/
adjective
幻觉的；引起幻觉的
Producing hallucinations; relating to or characterized by hallucinations.
The patient experienced hallucinatory episodes after the surgery.  病人术后出现了幻觉。
The drug can cause hallucinatory visions.  这种药物会导致幻觉景象。
近义词:  psychedelic, hallucinogenic, delusional, visionary
反义词: real, actual, factual, lucid
词性: adjective
