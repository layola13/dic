brothelly:/ˈbrʌðəlli/ | adj. | 妓院的；淫乱的；像妓院的 | Resembling or characteristic of a brothel; lewd. | The atmosphere in the club was decidedly brothelly.  这个俱乐部的氛围明显很淫乱。 | The room had a brothelly feel to it with its dim lighting and red velvet curtains. 房间里昏暗的灯光和红色天鹅绒窗帘给人一种妓院的感觉。| 近义词：sleazy, sordid, louche | 反义词：wholesome, respectable, decent | 词性:形容词 | 词源: From *brothel* + *-ly*.  *Brothel* comes from Old English *brōthel*, meaning "a worthless person," later evolving to mean "a house of prostitution."
