digestion:/daɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ /dɪˈdʒɛstʃən/| n.|消化|The process by which food is broken down in the body to be absorbed and used for energy, growth, and repair.|例句：Proper digestion is essential for good health. 好的消化对健康至关重要。|例句：He has problems with his digestion. 他的消化系统有问题。|近义词：absorption, assimilation|反义词：indigestion|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin digestio(n-), from the verb digerere (see digest).
