bacilluria:/ˌbæsɪˈljʊəriə/| n. |杆菌尿|The presence of bacilli (rod-shaped bacteria) in the urine.|Example 1: Bacilluria can be a sign of a urinary tract infection. 杆菌尿可能是尿路感染的迹象。

Example 2: The doctor ordered a urine culture to confirm the diagnosis of bacilluria. 医生要求进行尿培养以确认杆菌尿的诊断。|Synonym: bacteriuria (more general term)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, absence of bacteria in urine would be sterile urine)|noun
