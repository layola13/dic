noira:/ˈnwɑːrə/ | n. |黑色电影; 黑色; 黑色电影类型|A genre of crime film, often cynical and pessimistic, characterized by dark visuals, morally ambiguous characters, and a focus on urban corruption and violence. | Example: "The Maltese Falcon" is considered a classic example of film noir.  《马耳他之鹰》被认为是黑色电影的经典之作。| Example:  She loves the moody atmosphere of noir films. 她喜欢黑色电影忧郁的氛围。 | Synonym: darkness, blackness, dark,  black cinema | Antonym: lightness, whiteness, bright, color | noun
