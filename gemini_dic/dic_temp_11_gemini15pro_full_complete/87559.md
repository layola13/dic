heteronymic:/ˌhɛtərəˈnɪmɪk/ | adj. |  异名的，同形异义异音的 |  Having different pronunciation and meaning but the same spelling as another word.  | The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are heteronymic.  铅（金属）和领导（引导）这两个词是异名的。 | Synonyms: heteronymous | Antonyms: homonymic, homonymous | adjective

heteronym:/ˈhɛtərənɪm/ | n. | 异名词，同形异音异义词 | A word spelled the same as another word but having a different sound and meaning. |  "Lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are heteronyms. 铅（金属）和领导（引导）这两个词是异名词。 | Synonyms: heteronym | Antonyms: homonym | noun
