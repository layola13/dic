overillustrative:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlʌstrətɪv/
adjective
过分解释的；过度图解的
Providing or containing too many illustrations; excessively illustrated.
The textbook was overillustrative, making it difficult to focus on the text itself. 这本教科书插图过多，让人难以专注于文字本身。
Her presentation was overillustrative, with so many slides that the audience lost interest. 她的演讲插图过多，幻灯片太多，以至于观众失去了兴趣。
synonyms: overdone, excessive, superfluous
antonyms: concise, succinct, understated
word class: adjective
