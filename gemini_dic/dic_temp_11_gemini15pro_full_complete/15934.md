balmily:/ˈbɑːməli/ | adv. | 温和地；柔和地；芳香地 | In a balmy manner; gently and soothingly, often implying a pleasant fragrance. | The evening air was balmily scented with jasmine. 夜晚的空气弥漫着茉莉花的芳香。 | Near Synonyms: gently, mildly, soothingly, pleasantly | Near Antonyms: harshly, roughly, acridly, unpleasantly | adverb

Etymology:
From balmy + -ly.  Balmy comes from Middle English *bawmy, from Old English *bealme, from Late Latin balsamum, from Greek balsamon.
