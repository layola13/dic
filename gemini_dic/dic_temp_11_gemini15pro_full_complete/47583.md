debaser:/dɪˈbeɪsər/| n.|贬低者，降低者|Someone who debases something or someone; someone who lowers the quality, value, or character of something or someone.|The debauched emperor was a debasser of traditional Roman values. 这个放荡的皇帝降低了传统的罗马价值观。|Synonyms: degrader, corrupter, adulterator|Antonyms: elevator, enhancer, improver|noun

debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/| vt.|贬低，降低|To reduce the quality, value, or character of something or someone.|Debasing the currency by adding lead to the silver coins was a crime.  在银币中添加铅以降低货币价值是一种犯罪行为。|Synonyms: degrade, corrupt, cheapen, demean|Antonyms: elevate, enhance, improve, ennoble|verb
