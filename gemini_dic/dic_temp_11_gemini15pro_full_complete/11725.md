aroid:/ˈɛrɔɪd/| n.|天南星科植物；芋类植物|A plant of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and house plants such as philodendrons, caladiums, and dieffenbachias.  These plants are characterized by their spathe and spadix inflorescence.|The florist recommended an aroid for low-light conditions. 花店老板推荐了一种适合低光环境的天南星科植物。|Synonyms: Araceae,  aroid plant|Antonyms: None|Noun
