hatcheting:/ˈhætʃɪtɪŋ/| n. |用斧头砍，斧劈|The act of chopping or cutting with a hatchet. |The hatcheting of the trees was necessary to clear the land.  用斧头砍树对于清理土地是必要的。|近义词：chopping, hewing, hacking|反义词: constructing, building|Noun

hatcheting:/ˈhætʃɪtɪŋ/| v. |用斧头砍，斧劈|Present participle of *to hatchet*.|He is hatcheting the wood for the fire. 他正在用斧头劈柴生火。|近义词：chopping, hewing, hacking|反义词: constructing, building|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hatchet* + *-ing*. *Hatchet* comes from Old North French *hachete*, diminutive of *hache* ("axe"), from Frankish *hapja*  related to Old High German *hapia*.
