expectant:/ɪkˈspektənt/ /ɪkˈspɛktənt/| adj.|期待的，期望的|Having or showing an expectation of something, typically something good.|The expectant mother felt a kick. 怀孕的母亲感到胎动。|近义词：anticipating, hoping, waiting, pregnant (if referring to pregnancy)|反义词:uninterested, apathetic|adjective


expectant:/ɪkˈspektənt/ /ɪkˈspɛktənt/| n.|期待者，预备者|A person who is expecting something, especially a baby.|The expectants gathered in the waiting room. 准妈妈们聚集在候诊室。|近义词:candidate, prospect|反义词:incumbent|noun
