dumpling:/ˈdʌmplɪŋ/| n. |饺子；汤圆；团子|A small ball of dough (= flour and water mixed together) cooked by boiling or steaming, sometimes containing meat or vegetables.|例句：She made delicious pork dumplings for dinner. 她晚饭做了美味的猪肉饺子。|例句：I love eating dumplings with vinegar and chili oil. 我喜欢吃蘸醋和辣椒油的饺子。|近义词：potsticker, ravioli, gyoza|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soup" if considering a contrasting dish)|词性:noun
