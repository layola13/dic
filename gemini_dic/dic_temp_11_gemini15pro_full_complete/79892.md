gnathotheca:/ˌnæθəˈθiːkə/
noun
颚鞘；颚片；包住口器的鞘
A covering or sheath-like structure which encloses the mouthparts of some insects and other arthropods.
The gnatotheca of the grasshopper protects its delicate mouthparts. 蚱蜢的颚鞘保护着它脆弱的口器。
gnathoteca protects the mouthparts. 颚鞘保护口器。
covering, sheath
(No direct antonym)
