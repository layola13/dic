bouncebackability:/ˌbaʊnsbækəˈbɪləti/| n. |快速恢复的能力；适应力；韧性|The ability to recover quickly from setbacks or adversity.  It refers to a person's resilience and capacity to bounce back from difficult situations.|She showed remarkable bouncebackability after the accident. 她在那次事故后展现了惊人的恢复能力。|近义词：resilience, adaptability, toughness|反义词: fragility, vulnerability|词性:noun

词源: "Bounce back" + "ability".  "Bounce back" dates back to the late 1800s, meaning to rebound or recover.  "Ability" comes from the Old French "ablete" and Latin "habilitatem." The combined term "bouncebackability" is a more recent coinage, gaining popularity in the late 20th and early 21st centuries.
