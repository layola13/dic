chickenshit:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt/| adj. n.|胆小鬼的；懦弱的；胆小鬼；懦夫|Cowardly; lacking courage.  Can also refer to a cowardly person.  Considered vulgar slang.|例句：He was too chickenshit to ask her out. 他太胆小了，不敢约她出去。|近义词：cowardly, timid, fearful, gutless, spineless|反义词：brave, courageous, bold, daring|词性:形容词/名词
chickenshit:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt/| n.|琐碎的；不重要的|Trivial or insignificant matters. Considered vulgar slang.|例句：Don't bother me with that chickenshit. 别拿那些鸡毛蒜皮的小事烦我。|近义词：trivial, petty, insignificant, unimportant|反义词：significant, important, crucial|词性:名词
