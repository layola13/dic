overexpansiveness:/ˌōvərɪkˈspænsɪvnəs/| n.|过度扩张；过度膨胀|The state of being excessively expansive, especially in one's plans, ambitions, or spending.|His overexpansiveness led him into serious debt. 他的过度扩张导致他负债累累。|近义词：excessiveness, extravagance, profusion|反义词: restraint, moderation|词性:noun
overexpansive:/ˌōvərɪkˈspænsɪv/| adj.|过度扩张的；过度膨胀的|Characterized by excessive expansion, especially in one's plans, ambitions, or spending.|The company's overexpansive policies ultimately led to its downfall. 该公司过度扩张的政策最终导致其垮台。|近义词：excessive, extravagant, immoderate|反义词: restrained, moderate|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + expansive + -ness.  "Over-" signifies "too much" or "excessive". "Expansive" comes from the Latin "expansus," the past participle of "expandere," meaning "to spread out."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
