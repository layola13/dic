manmade:/ˌmænˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 人造的，人工的 | Made by humans rather than occurring naturally. |  This lake is manmade, not natural.  这个湖是人造的，不是天然的。 | The bridge is a manmade structure of great complexity. 这座桥是一座非常复杂的人造建筑。 | 近义词：artificial, synthetic, manufactured | 反义词：natural, organic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Compound of "man" and "made."  由“man”和“made”组合而成。
