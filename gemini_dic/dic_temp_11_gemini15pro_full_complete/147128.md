overloftiness:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːftinəs/ | n. | 过于高傲，傲慢，自命不凡 |  The state of being excessively lofty or haughty; extreme arrogance or pride. | His overloftiness alienated his colleagues, who found him difficult to work with. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事，他们发现他很难共事。|  arrogance, haughtiness, superciliousness, pomposity, conceit | humility, modesty, humbleness | noun

Etymology->
From over- + loftiness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much."  "Loftiness" derives from "lofty," meaning "high" or "noble," ultimately from Old Norse *lopt, meaning "air, sky."
