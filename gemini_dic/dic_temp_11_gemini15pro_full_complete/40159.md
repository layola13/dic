contendent:/kənˈtɛndənt/ | n. |竞争者，争夺者|A person who competes in a contest or struggle for something.|例句：He is a strong contender for the championship. 他是冠军的有力竞争者。|例句：Several contenders emerged for the vacant position.  几个竞争者出现在空缺的职位上。|近义词：competitor, rival, candidate, aspirant|反义词：non-contender, also-ran|Noun

contend:/kənˈtɛnd/ | v. |竞争，争夺；声称，主张|To compete or strive in contest or rivalry; to assert something as a position in an argument.|例句：Several companies are contending for the contract. 几家公司正在争夺这份合同。|例句：He contends that the theory is flawed. 他声称这个理论是有缺陷的。|近义词：compete, vie, argue, assert|反义词：yield, concede, surrender|Verb
