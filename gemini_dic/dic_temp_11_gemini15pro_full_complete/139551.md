obtund:/əbˈtʌnd/ /ɑːbˈtʌnd/| v. |使迟钝；使减弱|to reduce the intensity of (something, such as a sensation or feeling); to dull or blunt|The medication obtunded her pain. 药物减轻了她的疼痛。|synonym: dull, blunt, deaden, dampen, subdue|antonym: sharpen, heighten, intensify|verb
obtund:/əbˈtʌnd/ /ɑːbˈtʌnd/| adj. |迟钝的；减弱的|(of a sensation or feeling) reduced in intensity; dull or blunt|His senses were obtunded by the heavy blow. 重击使他的感觉变得迟钝。|synonym: dulled, blunted, deadened, dampened, subdued|antonym: sharpened, heightened, intensified|adjective
