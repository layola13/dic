nondisparately:/ˌnɒndɪˈspærɪtli/ | adv. | 无差别地；公平地 |  In a way that does not discriminate or show favoritism towards any particular group or individual.  Derived from "disparate" (meaning different or distinct) with the prefixes "non-" (meaning not) and "-ly" (forming an adverb). | The funds were distributed nondisparately among all eligible applicants.  资金在所有符合条件的申请人中公平分配。|  equally, evenly, fairly, uniformly | disparately, unequally, unfairly | adverb
