anti-stalinists:/ˈæntiˈstɑːlɪnɪsts/
noun
反斯大林主义者
People who opposed Joseph Stalin, his policies, and Stalinism.
Many anti-stalinists were persecuted during the Great Purge. 许多反斯大林主义者在大清洗中遭到迫害。
Critics of Stalin's regime often found themselves labeled as anti-stalinists. 批评斯大林政权的人经常被贴上反斯大林主义者的标签。
dissidents, opponents, critics
stalinists, supporters, loyalists
n.
