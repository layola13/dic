electro-oculography: /ɪˌlektroʊˌɒkjʊˈlɒɡrəfi/ (US), /ɪˌlektrəʊˌɒkjuˈlɒɡrəfi/ (UK)| n. |眼电图描记法|The technique of recording eye movements and position by measuring the corneo-retinal potential difference.|Electro-oculography is used to diagnose certain neurological and ophthalmological disorders. 眼电图描记法用于诊断某些神经和眼科疾病。|
Electro-oculography helped researchers understand rapid eye movement during sleep. 眼电图描记法帮助研究人员了解睡眠期间的快速眼动。|Synonyms: EOG|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
