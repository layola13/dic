noninjuriously:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʒʊəriəsli/ | adv. | 无害地，无损地 |  Without causing harm or injury.  | The procedure was performed noninjuriously.  该手术进行得无损伤。 | 近义词: harmlessly, safely, innocuously | 反义词: harmfully, injuriously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + injuriously (from injury, ultimately from Latin injuria "wrong, hurt")
