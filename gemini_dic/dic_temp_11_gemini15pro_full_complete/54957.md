disprison:/dɪsˈprɪzn/
verb
释放，解除监禁
To release from prison; to set free.
The king disprisoned the political rebels. 国王释放了政治犯。
近义词:release, liberate, free, discharge
反义词:imprison, jail, confine, incarcerate
词性:v.
