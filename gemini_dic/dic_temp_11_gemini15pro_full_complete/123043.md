moneral:/ˈmɒnərəl/ (US) /ˈmɒnərəl/ (UK)| n. |原核生物|A single-celled organism belonging to the kingdom Monera (a classification no longer used); prokaryotes, such as bacteria and blue-green algae. These organisms lack a membrane-bound nucleus and other organelles.|Example 1: In older biological classifications, bacteria were considered monerals. 在旧的生物分类中，细菌被认为是原核生物。

Example 2:  Monerals were once thought to be the simplest form of life. 原核生物曾被认为是最简单的生命形式。|Synonym: prokaryote|Antonym: eukaryote|Noun
