dwale:/dweɪl/| n.|昏迷；麻醉；致命的毒药；颠茄|A state of inactivity or unconsciousness, or a drug or plant that induces such a state, often referring to deadly nightshade.|He drank a potion of dwale and fell into a deep sleep. 他喝了一种颠茄药水，陷入了沉睡。|近义词：stupor, coma, narcotic, belladonna|反义词：consciousness, alertness|词性:名词

dwale:/dweɪl/| v.|使昏迷；使麻醉|To make unconscious or insensible.|The fumes from the fire dwaled the occupants of the house. 火产生的烟雾使房屋内的居民昏迷了。|近义词：stupefy, narcotize|反义词：awaken, arouse|词性:动词
