nap: /næp/ | n. vi.| 小睡，打盹 |A short sleep, especially during the day.|  I took a nap after lunch. 我午饭后小睡了一会儿。

nap: /næp/ | vi.| 小睡，打盹 |To sleep briefly, especially during the day.| The cat napped in a sunbeam. 猫在阳光下打盹。


Etymology: From Old English *hnappian, related to Dutch knappen "to snap, crack," German knappen "to gnaw, nibble." The sense evolution likely went from "to doze lightly" to "to take a short sleep."

Synonyms: doze, siesta, snooze

Antonyms: stay awake, be alert
