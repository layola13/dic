embroideress:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərɪs/
noun
女刺绣工，女绣工
a woman who embroiders
The embroideress skillfully stitched a delicate floral pattern onto the silk.  这位女刺绣工巧妙地将精致的花卉图案绣在丝绸上。
The embroideress created intricate designs with colorful threads. 女绣工用彩线绣出了复杂的图案。
synonyms: needlewoman, sewer, stitcher
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone who destroys or unravels embroidery)
词性: noun
