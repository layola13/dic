centauromachy:/ˌsɛntɔːrˈɒməki/ | n. | 人马之战，半人马之战 | A battle between centaurs and Lapiths, a mythical race of Thessaly.  It often represents the struggle between civilization and barbarism, or between the higher and lower natures of humanity. | The centauromachy depicted on the Parthenon metopes is a powerful symbol of the Greek victory over the Persians.  帕台农神庙墙面雕刻的人马之战象征着希腊战胜波斯。 |  Near synonyms: battle, struggle, conflict | Antonyms: peace, harmony, truce | noun
