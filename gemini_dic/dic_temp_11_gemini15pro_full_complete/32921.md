choosiest:/ˈtʃuːziɪst/
adjective
最挑剔的
Most selective or discriminating.
She's the choosiest eater I know; she only likes a few specific foods. 她是我认识的最挑剔的食客；她只喜欢几种特定的食物。
近义词:pickiest, fussiest, most selective, most discriminating
反义词:least selective, undemanding, easygoing
词性:adjective

词源:
From choose + -y + -est. "Choose" comes from Middle English *chosen, from Old English *cēosan "to choose, seek out, select, test, taste, decide," from Proto-Germanic *keusaną "to test, choose"
