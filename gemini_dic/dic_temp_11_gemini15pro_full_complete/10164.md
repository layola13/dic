apophlegmatic:/ˌæpəflɛɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 冷静的；不易激动的；沉着的 | Not easily excited to action or display of emotion; calm; composed. |  例句：His apophlegmatic demeanor made him seem uninterested, even bored. 他冷静的举止让他看起来很不感兴趣，甚至很无聊。

例句：Even when faced with a crisis, she remained remarkably apophlegmatic. 即使面对危机，她也保持着非凡的冷静。

近义词：calm, composed, imperturbable, phlegmatic, serene, tranquil | 反义词：excitable, agitated, nervous, passionate | 词性: adjective
