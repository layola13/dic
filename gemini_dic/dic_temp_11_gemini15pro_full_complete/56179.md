dodecaphonies:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈfoʊniz/
|n.|十二音音乐；十二音作品|A method of musical composition in which the twelve notes of the chromatic scale are organized into a series, called a tone row, and used as the basis of the composition.|Schoenberg's early dodecaphonies are often quite complex.勋伯格早期的十二音音乐通常相当复杂。|
|Synonyms: twelve-tone music, serial music|
|Antonyms: tonal music|
|noun|


dodecaphony:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈfoʊni/
|n.|十二音；十二音体系；十二音作曲法|A system of musical composition based on the twelve notes of the chromatic scale, arranged in a series called a tone row.|Dodecaphony revolutionized 20th-century music. 十二音法彻底改变了20世纪的音乐。|
|Synonyms: twelve-tone system, serialism|
|Antonyms: tonality|
|noun|
