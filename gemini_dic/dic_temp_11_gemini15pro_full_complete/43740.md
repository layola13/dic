creeling:/ˈkriːlɪŋ/
n.
装篓；用鱼篓捕鱼；篓中之物
The act of putting something into a creel, a wicker basket, especially for fish. Also refers to the contents of a creel.
The fisherman spent the morning creeling his catch. 渔夫早上都在把捕到的鱼装进鱼篓。
The creeling was good today, the basket was full. 今天的收获不错，鱼篓都满了。
近义词:basketful, catch
反义词:None
词性:名词

creeling:/ˈkriːlɪŋ/
v.
用鱼篓捕鱼；装篓
The act of catching fish using a creel or putting something into a creel.
He was creeling for trout in the river. 他在河里用鱼篓捕鳟鱼。
She was creeling the apples she picked from the orchard. 她把她从果园摘的苹果装进篓子里。
近义词:fishing, gathering
反义词:releasing
词性:动词
