onsetting:/ɒnˈsɛtɪŋ/ | adj. | 开始的，来临的 | Beginning, starting, commencing.  Describes something that is just starting to happen or develop. | The onsetting winter brought freezing temperatures. 来临的冬天带来了冰冻的温度。| The onsetting storm threatened to flood the coastal towns.  即将来临的暴风雨威胁着要淹没沿海城镇。 | 近义词：beginning, commencing, incipient, nascent | 反义词：ending, concluding, ceasing, waning | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "onset," which is formed from "on" + "set." "Set" in this context means "to begin a course or journey."
