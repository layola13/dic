caroche:/kəˈroʊʃ/ | n. | 四轮马车；豪华马车 | A large, ornate, horse-drawn carriage, often used by royalty or other important people. |  The queen arrived in a magnificent caroche. 女王乘坐一辆华丽的四轮马车抵达。| coach, carriage | car, bicycle | noun

Etymology:
From French *caroche*, from Italian *carroccio* "war chariot," from Late Latin *carrus* "wagon." Ultimately from Gaulish *karros* "wagon."
