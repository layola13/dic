axinite:/ˈæksɪnaɪt/| n.|斧石|A brown, violet, or reddish-brown mineral, a complex borosilicate of aluminium, calcium, iron, and manganese, occurring as triclinic crystals.|The geologist discovered a beautiful specimen of axinite.|近义词:mineral|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *axinite*, from Latin *axinis* (“axe”) + *-ite* (mineral suffix), referring to the characteristic wedge shape of the crystals. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂égʷsih₂* (“axe”).
