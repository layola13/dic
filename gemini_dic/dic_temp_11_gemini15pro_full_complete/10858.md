arabinose:/əˈræbɪnoʊs/| n.| 阿拉伯糖 |A five-carbon sugar (pentose) that is a component of some plant polysaccharides. It is an aldopentose, meaning it has five carbons and an aldehyde functional group.| Arabinose is a common component of hemicellulose found in plant cell walls. | synonyms: pectinose, gum sugar | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound. Could consider other monosaccharides like glucose or fructose as functional antonyms in certain contexts.) | noun
