importantly:/ɪmˈpɔːrtəntli/ | adv. | 重要地；显著地；尤其地 | Used to emphasize that something is particularly important or true.  It modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb, highlighting the significance of the information being conveyed. | Importantly, the deadline for submissions is tomorrow. 重要的是，提交截止日期是明天。| Importantly, everyone agreed on the plan.  重要的是，每个人都同意这个计划。| significantly, notably, chiefly, crucially | trivially, insignificantly | adverb
