clamped:/klæmpt/| v. adj.| 夹紧的，固定的 | to fasten (something) tightly so that it cannot move; held tightly in position | The wood was clamped to the workbench. 木头被夹在工作台上。|近义词：fastened, secured, fixed |反义词: loose, unfastened | verb, adjective

clamped:/klæmpt/| adj.| 夹紧的，固定的 | held tightly in position |  His jaw was clamped shut. 他的下巴紧紧地闭着。|近义词：fastened, secured, fixed |反义词: loose, unfastened | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "clamp," which comes from Middle Dutch or Low German "klampe" meaning "clamp, lump."
