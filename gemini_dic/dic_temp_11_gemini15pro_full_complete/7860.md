anteater:/ˈæntˌiːtər/ | n. |食蚁兽|A mammal that feeds on ants and termites, typically having a long snout, a long sticky tongue, and powerful claws.|The anteater used its long tongue to lap up the ants. 食蚁兽用它长长的舌头舔食蚂蚁。| The anteater flicked its tongue in and out of the anthill. 食蚁兽的舌头在蚁丘里进进出出。|Synonym: ant-eating animal, Myrmecophaga|Antonym: N/A|Noun
