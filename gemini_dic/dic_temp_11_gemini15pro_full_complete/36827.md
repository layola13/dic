colonies:/ˈkɒləniz/ (British) /ˈkɑːləniz/ (American)| n. |殖民地；群体；聚居地|A colony is a country or area under the full or partial political control of another country, typically a distant one, and occupied by settlers from that country.  A colony can also refer to a group of people of one nationality or ethnic group living in a foreign city or country or a group of animals or plants of the same kind living close together.|The British Empire had many colonies around the world.  英国在世界各地有很多殖民地。

The artist lived in an artists' colony in the mountains.  这位艺术家住在山里的一个艺术家聚居地。|近义词：settlements, territories, communities, outposts|反义词:homeland, mother country|Noun
