inspective:/ɪnˈspɛktɪv/| adj.|检查的；审视的；探究的|Given to examining one's own thoughts and feelings; introspective. Characterized by inspection; examining.|例句：He had an inspective look on his face. 他的脸上带着审视的目光。|例句：She cast an inspective glance around the room. 她审视地环顾了一下房间。|近义词：examining, scrutinizing, analytical, observant, introspective|反义词：unobservant, superficial, cursory|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *inspectīvus*, from *inspectus*, past participle of *inspicere* ("to look into, inspect"), from *in* ("in, into") + *specere* ("to look").
