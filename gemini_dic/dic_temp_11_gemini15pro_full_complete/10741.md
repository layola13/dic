aptitudinal: /ˌæptɪˈtjuːdɪnəl/ | adj. | 资质的，能力的 | Relating to aptitude; concerning natural ability or skill. |  Her aptitudinal test scores suggested a strong potential for a career in engineering. 她的能力倾向测试分数表明她在工程方面有很强的职业潜力。|  Example: The aptitudinal tests helped the company select the best candidates for the job. 这些能力倾向测试帮助公司挑选了最适合这份工作的候选人。 | Synonyms: inherent, innate, natural, intrinsic | Antonyms: learned, acquired, developed | adjective
