noninverted:/ˌnɒnɪnˈvɜːtɪd/ | adj. | 非倒置的，非反转的 | Not inverted; in the normal or usual order.  | The noninverted sentence structure is easier to understand.  非倒置的句子结构更容易理解。 | The image appeared noninverted on the screen. 图像在屏幕上显示为非倒置的。 | 近义词: upright, normal, standard | 反义词: inverted, reversed | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "inverted" (from Latin "invertere," meaning "to turn over").
