generalized:/ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 普遍的，广义的，概括的 | Made more general or applicable to a wider range; not specific or detailed. |  The study's findings were too generalized to be of much use.  这项研究的结果过于笼统，用处不大。|  Generalized anxiety disorder is a common mental health condition. 广泛性焦虑症是一种常见的精神健康疾病。 | general, broad, widespread, universal | specific, particular, localized | adjective
