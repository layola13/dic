anticeremoniously: /ˌæntisɛrɪˈməʊniəsli/ | adv. |  不拘礼节地，随便地 |  In a manner lacking ceremony; informally.  | He anticeremoniously dumped his coat on the floor. 他随便地把外套扔在地板上。 |  informally, casually, unceremoniously |  ceremoniously, formally | adverb

anticeremonious: /ˌæntisɛrɪˈməʊniəs/ | adj. | 不拘礼节的，随便的 |  Lacking ceremony; informal. |  It was an anticeremonious gathering of friends. 这是一个朋友间的非正式聚会。 | informal, casual, unceremonious | ceremonious, formal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against, opposite) + ceremonious (relating to ceremony) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "ceremonious" ultimately derives from Latin "caerimonia" (sacred rite).
