monticulate:/mɒnˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ /mɑːnˈtɪkjəleɪt/
adjective
多小丘的，有小突起的
covered with or resembling small mounds or prominences; having small projections or bumps.
The monticulate surface of the moon is fascinating to observe through a telescope. 通过望远镜观察月球那凹凸不平的表面令人着迷。
近义词: bumpy, hillocky, knobby, lumpy, uneven
反义词: smooth, flat, even
词性: adjective

monticulate:/mɒnˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ /mɑːnˈtɪkjəleɪt/
verb
使…成小丘状
to form into small mounds or prominences.
The artist monticulated the clay to create a miniature mountain range.  艺术家将黏土堆成小丘状，创造出一座微型山脉。
近义词: mound, heap, pile
反义词: flatten, level
词性: verb

词源: From Latin *monticulus (a little hill), diminutive of mons (mountain).
