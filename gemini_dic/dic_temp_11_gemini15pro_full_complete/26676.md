cadastral:/kəˈdæstrəl/
adjective
地籍的；土地登记的
Relating to an official register of the quantity, value, and ownership of land or property.
The cadastral survey showed the precise boundaries of the property. 地籍调查显示了该房产的确切边界。

The government is updating its cadastral maps. 政府正在更新其地籍图。

synonym:  land registry, property survey
antonym:  unregistered, undocumented
词性: adjective
