broish:/ˈbrɔɪʃ/ | adj. | broth-like; like broth; thin and watery |  Resembling or characteristic of broth; thin and watery, often used to describe a weak or tasteless soup or stew. | The soup was disappointingly broish and lacked flavor. 这汤淡而无味，令人失望。|  近义词: watery, thin, weak, bland | 反义词: thick, rich, flavorful, hearty | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a humorous or informal formation based on "broth" with the suffix "-ish" added to indicate resemblance.  No established etymological history exists for this word, suggesting it is a relatively recent coinage.
