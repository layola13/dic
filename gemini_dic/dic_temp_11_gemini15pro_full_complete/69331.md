fecund:/ˈfiːkənd/ /ˈfekənd/| adj.|多产的，肥沃的，富饶的|Producing or capable of producing abundant offspring or vegetation; fertile.|例句：The fecund soil produced a bountiful harvest. 肥沃的土壤带来了丰收。

例句：She is a fecund writer, publishing several novels a year. 她是一位多产的作家，每年出版几部小说。|近义词：fertile, productive, prolific|反义词：infertile, barren, sterile|词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *fecundus*, from *fe-* (probably related to *ferre* "to bear") + *-cundus* (akin to Greek *-gōnos* "producing").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\dʰeh₁-* "to put, place, set".
