hard-up:/ˌhɑːrd ˈʌp/ | adj. | 缺钱的，手头紧的 | short of money; in financial difficulty |  Example: They were so hard-up they couldn't afford to buy new shoes for their children. 他们手头太紧了，以至于无法给孩子们买新鞋。

Example:  He's been hard-up ever since he lost his job. 自从失业以来，他一直手头拮据。


近义词: broke, impoverished, penniless, strapped (for cash) | 反义词: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous | 词性: adjective
