celebrational:/ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃənəl/| adj.|庆祝的，节日的|Relating to or characteristic of a celebration.  Marked by celebration; suited to a celebration.|例句：The celebrational atmosphere filled the town square. 节日的气氛弥漫着整个城镇广场。

例句：They wore celebrational garments for the festival.  他们穿着节日的服装参加庆典。|近义词：festive, celebratory, commemorating, congratulatory|反义词：mournful, somber, grieving|词性:形容词
词源: From celebration + -al.  Ultimately from Latin celebratus, past participle of celebrare "to frequent, honor, practice, celebrate."
