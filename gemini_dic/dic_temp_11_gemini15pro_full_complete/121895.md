missamples:/ˌmɪsˈsɑːmpəlz/ | n. | 错误样本；不具代表性的样本 | Incorrect or atypical samples that do not accurately represent the population from which they are drawn.  These samples can skew results and lead to inaccurate conclusions. |  The study was flawed because of the missamples used in the analysis. 这项研究存在缺陷，因为分析中使用的样本有误。

The missamples collected from the contaminated area did not reflect the true environmental conditions. 从污染区域收集的错误样本并不能反映真实的的环境状况。 | 近义词：bad samples, unrepresentative samples, biased samples | 反义词：representative samples, accurate samples, valid samples | 词性: 名词


词源： "missamples" is a combination of the prefix "mis-" (meaning wrong or bad) and "samples" (a portion representative of a whole).
