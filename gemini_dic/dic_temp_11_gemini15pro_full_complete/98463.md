inspiringly:/ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋli/ | adv. | 鼓舞人心地，激励地 | In a way that inspires or motivates others. | Her speech was inspiringly delivered, moving many in the audience to tears.  她的演讲非常鼓舞人心，让许多观众感动落泪。| Synonyms: movingly, upliftingly, encouragingly | Antonyms: discouragingly, depressingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From inspire + -ing + -ly.  "Inspire" comes from the Latin "inspirare," meaning "to breathe or blow into," later taking on the metaphorical meaning of "to influence, excite, or animate."
