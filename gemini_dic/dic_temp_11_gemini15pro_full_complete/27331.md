camaraderie:/ˌkɑːməˈrɑːdəri/ /ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri/
n.
同志情谊；友情
mutual trust and friendship among people who spend a lot of time together
The soldiers developed a strong sense of camaraderie during the war.  这些士兵在战争期间建立了深厚的同志情谊。

例句：
There was great camaraderie among the team members.
队员之间有着极佳的同志情谊。

The camaraderie among the firefighters was evident.
消防队员之间的友情显而易见。


近义词：friendship, fellowship, companionship, esprit de corps
反义词：enmity, animosity, hostility
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *camaraderie*, from *camarade* "comrade," from Spanish *camarada* "roommate," from Late Latin *camera* "chamber, room." Ultimately from Greek *kamara* "vaulted chamber."
