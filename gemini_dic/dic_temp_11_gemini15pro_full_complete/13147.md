asymptomatic: /ˌeɪsɪmptəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 无症状的 | Showing no symptoms of a particular disease. |  Many carriers of the virus are asymptomatic, making it difficult to track the spread. 许多病毒携带者没有症状，这使得追踪病毒传播变得困难。|  He was asymptomatic for several weeks after contracting the disease.  感染疾病几周后，他一直没有出现症状。 | 近义词: subclinical, inapparent | 反义词: symptomatic | 词性: adjective

词源:  From a- (not) + symptomatic.  "Symptomatic" ultimately derives from Greek "symptoma" (accident, casualty, occurrence, symptom), from "syn-" (together) + "piptein" (to fall).
