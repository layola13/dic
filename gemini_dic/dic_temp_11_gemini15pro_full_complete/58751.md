dungiest:/ˈdʌndʒiɪst/
adjective
最肮脏的，最污秽的
Superlative form of dungy: most dungy.  Covered with or full of dung; extremely dirty and unpleasant.
He lived in the dingiest apartment in the whole city. 他住在这个城市里最肮脏的公寓里。
The team had to change in the dungiest locker room they had ever seen.  这个队伍不得不更换衣服在他们见过的最肮脏的更衣室里。
Synonyms: filthy, squalid, grimy, soiled, foul
Antonyms: clean, spotless, pristine, immaculate
词性: 形容词
