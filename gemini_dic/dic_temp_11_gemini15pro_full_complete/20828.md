blastiest:/ˈblæstiɪst/
adjective
最该死的；最讨厌的；最糟糕的
superlative adjective of blasty: most blasty.  Meaning extremely unpleasant or annoying.
That was the blastiest weather I've ever experienced. 那是我经历过的最糟糕的天气。
The traffic was blastiest, making me late for the meeting. 交通糟透了，害我开会迟到了。
近义词:awful, terrible, dreadful, horrible, rotten
反义词:wonderful, fantastic, great, excellent
词性:adjective
