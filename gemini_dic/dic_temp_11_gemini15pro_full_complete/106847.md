labour-saver:/ˈleɪbərˌseɪvər/| n.|节省劳力的工具/方法|A device, method, or product that reduces the amount of work needed to do something. |This new washing machine is a real labour-saver.  这台新的洗衣机真是省力。|Synonyms: timesaver, work-saver|Antonyms: labour-intensive|noun

Etymology:
From labour + saver.  Saver from save, from Old French sauver, from Latin salvare "to save, rescue, preserve," ultimately from salvus "safe, sound."
