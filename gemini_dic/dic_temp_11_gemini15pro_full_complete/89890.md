horrid:/ˈhɔːrɪd/ (US) /ˈhɒrɪd/ (UK)| adj.|可怕的，令人讨厌的|Causing horror; extremely unpleasant or disagreeable.|The food was absolutely horrid. 这食物简直糟透了.|近义词：awful, terrible, dreadful, frightful, ghastly|反义词：pleasant, delightful, wonderful|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *horridus, from horrēre (“to bristle, shudder”).
