lichens:/ˈlaɪkənz/ (US), /ˈlɪtʃɪnz/ (UK)| n. |地衣|A composite organism made up of a fungus and an alga (or a cyanobacterium) living in symbiotic association.|Lichens grow on rocks and trees. 地衣生长在岩石和树木上。|Synonyms: moss, liverwort|Antonyms: None|Noun

lichens:/ˈlaɪkənz/ (US), /ˈlɪtʃɪnz/ (UK)| n. |地衣|A composite organism that arises from algae or cyanobacteria living among filaments of multiple fungi species in a mutually beneficial relationship.|The reindeer feed on lichens in the winter. 驯鹿在冬天以地衣为食。|Synonyms:  None (precisely)|Antonyms: None|Noun
