capsule:/ˈkæpsjuːl/ (US), /ˈkæpsjuːl/ (UK)| n. | 胶囊；太空舱；小容器；浓缩；概要 |A small case or container, typically cylindrical, enclosing a dose of medicine or other substance. A sealed cabin in a spacecraft for astronauts or experimental animals.  A concise summary. |  She took two capsules before breakfast. 她早饭前吃了两粒胶囊。 The space capsule splashed down safely in the ocean. 太空舱安全地溅落在海洋中。 He gave us a capsule summary of the meeting. 他给了我们一个会议的简要概述。 | synonyms: tablet, pill, lozenge, spacecraft, pod, summary, synopsis | antonyms: expansion, enlargement | noun
