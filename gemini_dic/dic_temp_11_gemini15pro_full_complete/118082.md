mends:/mɛndz/
| v. |修理, 修补, 改善; 好转, 康复 (mend的第三人称单数)|repairs, improves, heals|He mends shoes for a living. 他以修鞋为生。|近义词：repairs, fixes, improves|反义词:damage, break|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *emendan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *amendjaną (“to improve, correct”), from Proto-Indo-European *mendʰ- (“defect, fault”). Cognate with Old Frisian emenda, Old Saxon amendon, Middle Dutch amenderen, Dutch amenderen (“to improve”), Old High German amendōn, German ändern (“to change”), Old Norse bæta (“to improve”), Gothic 𐌱𐍉𐍄𐌾𐌰𐌽 (bōtjan, “to help, repair”).  Related to amend.
