narceine: /ˈnɑːrsiːɪn/ | n. | 麻醉剂，那可汀 | A crystalline alkaloid, C23H27NO8, found in opium and having narcotic properties. | Narceine is a less potent opiate than morphine. 那可汀是一种比吗啡药效低的鸦片类药物。| Narceine was once used as a cough suppressant and analgesic. 那可汀曾经被用作镇咳药和镇痛药。 | synonyms: opiate, narcotic, analgesic | antonyms: stimulant | noun
