aquarelle:/ˌæk.wəˈrel/ | n. | 水彩画；水彩画法 | A painting done in watercolor.  |  The artist displayed a series of delicate aquarelles.  这位艺术家展出了一系列精致的水彩画。 | synonyms: watercolor, water-colour | antonyms: oil painting | noun

aquarelle:/ˌæk.wəˈrel/ | adj. | 水彩的；水彩画法的 | Relating to watercolor painting. | She prefers the aquarelle style to oils. 她喜欢水彩画风格胜过油画。 | synonyms: watercolor, water-colour | antonyms: oil-based | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aquarelle*, from Italian *acquarello*, diminutive of *acqua* ("water"). Ultimately from Latin *aqua* ("water").
