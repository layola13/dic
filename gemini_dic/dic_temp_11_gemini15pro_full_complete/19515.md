bierbalk:/ˈbɪərˌbɑːlk/ | n. | 梁，桁，大梁 | A large, horizontal beam, especially one of timber or steel, supporting a floor or roof. | The bierbalk in the old barn was starting to sag. 老谷仓里的横梁开始下垂了。|The cathedral's roof was supported by massive bierbalks.  大教堂的屋顶由巨大的横梁支撑。| synonym: beam, girder, joist | antonym: support (can be considered a functional opposite) | noun
