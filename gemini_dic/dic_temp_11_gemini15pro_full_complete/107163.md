lagan:/ˈlæɡən/| n. |货物，尤指沉入海底的货物（可被打捞）; 海藻，尤指被冲上岸的海藻|Goods, especially shipwrecked goods lying on the seabed; seaweed, especially that washed ashore.|The divers hoped to salvage some of the lagan from the wreck. 潜水员希望从沉船中打捞一些货物。|The beach was covered in lagan after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上覆盖着海藻。|近义词：jetsam, flotsam, wreckage (for shipwrecked goods); seaweed, kelp (for seaweed)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


lagan:/ˈlæɡən/| n. |(苏格兰法律)附着物，不动产| (Scots Law) An accessory or fixture to heritable property|The lagan of the house included the built-in cupboards. 这栋房子附带的物品包括嵌入式橱柜。|The lagan of the land included the fences and gates. 这片土地的附着物包括栅栏和大门。|近义词：fixture, appurtenance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
