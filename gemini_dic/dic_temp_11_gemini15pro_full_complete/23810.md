brazer:/ˈbreɪzər/| n.|铜焊工，钎焊工，焊接器|A person who brazes or a tool used for brazing.  A worker who joins metal parts together by melting and flowing a filler metal (solder) into the joint.|The brazer carefully joined the two pipes together.  铜焊工小心地将两根管子连接在一起。|近义词：welder, solderer|反义词:|noun
