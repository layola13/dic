hostelries: /ˈhɒstəlriz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːstəlriz/ (US)| n. |旅馆，客栈|A building that provides lodging and often food, drink, and entertainment for travelers; an inn or hotel. |例句：The weary travelers sought refuge in one of the many hostelries along the road.  疲惫的旅行者在沿途众多客栈中的一家寻求庇护。

例句：The medieval hostelries offered basic accommodations and a hearty meal.  中世纪的客栈提供基本的住宿和一顿丰盛的餐饭。|近义词：hotels, inns, lodgings, accommodations|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Old French *hostelrie (Modern French hôtellerie), from hostel + -erie. Ultimately from Latin hospitale "guest-house," neuter of hospitalis "of a guest, hospitable."
