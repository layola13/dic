comelier:/ˈkʌmliər/| adj.|更漂亮的，更清秀的|Comparative form of comely: more comely.  Meaning more pleasing in appearance; attractive. |例句：She was even comelier than her sister. 她甚至比她姐姐更漂亮。

comely:/ˈkʌmli/| adj.|漂亮的，清秀的|Pleasing in appearance; attractive. |例句：She was a comely young woman with a pleasant smile. 她是一位漂亮的年轻女子，面带愉悦的微笑。
