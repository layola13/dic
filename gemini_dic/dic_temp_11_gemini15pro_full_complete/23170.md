bow:/baʊ/ (US) /baʊ/ (UK)| v.|鞠躬，弯腰，屈服|To bend the head or upper body as a sign of respect or greeting. To bend or curve downwards.|The performers bowed to the audience. 表演者向观众鞠躬。|近义词：stoop, incline, yield|反义词：stand erect, straighten|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *boga (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *bōgon, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰogʰ-. Cognate with Old Norse bogi (“bow”), Dutch boog (“bow, arch”), German Bogen (“bow, arch”), Latin fornix (“arch, vault”).
