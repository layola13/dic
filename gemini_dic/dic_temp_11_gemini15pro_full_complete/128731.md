nigerianization:/naɪˌdʒɪəriənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|尼日利亚化|The process of making something Nigerian in character or bringing something under Nigerian control.  The adoption of Nigerian customs, laws, and practices.|例句：The nigerianization of the oil industry has been a long and complex process. 石油行业的尼日利亚化是一个漫长而复杂的过程。

例句：The government's policy of nigerianization led to the appointment of more Nigerians to senior positions. 政府的尼日利亚化政策导致更多尼日利亚人被任命为高级职位。|近义词：nationalization, localization, indigenization|反义词: internationalization, globalization|词性: noun
