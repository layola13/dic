half-reluctantly:/ˌhæf rɪˈlʌktəntli/
| adv. | 半勉强地，有点不情愿地 | Doing something with a feeling of slight unwillingness or hesitation, but not complete opposition. | She half-reluctantly agreed to go to the party. 她有点不情愿地同意去参加聚会。 | She half-reluctantly handed over the money. 她不太情愿地交出了钱。| 近义词: hesitantly, unwillingly, grudgingly | 反义词: eagerly, willingly, enthusiastically | 词性: 副词

reluctant: /rɪˈlʌktənt/
| adj. | 不情愿的，勉强的 | Unwilling and hesitant; disinclined. | He was reluctant to leave. 他不愿离开。 | She seemed reluctant to answer. 她似乎不愿回答。 | 近义词: unwilling, hesitant, disinclined | 反义词: willing, eager, enthusiastic | 词性: 形容词

-ly: /li/
