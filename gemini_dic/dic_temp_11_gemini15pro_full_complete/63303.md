enthymematical:/ˌɛnθaɪˈmɛmətɪkəl/
adjective
隐含的，省略前提的
Based on an enthymeme; containing an enthymeme.  An argument in which one premise is not explicitly stated.
例句：Her argument was enthymematical, relying on shared cultural assumptions. 她的论证是隐含的，依赖于共同的文化假设。

例句：The enthymematical nature of the advertisement made it more persuasive to the target audience.  这则广告的隐含性质使其对目标受众更具说服力。

近义词：implicit, tacit, unexpressed, allusive, suggestive
反义词：explicit, stated, overt

词性: adjective
