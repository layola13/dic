emcee:/ˌemˈsiː/| n. vt.|司仪，主持人|A master of ceremonies, especially at a nightclub, banquet, or variety show.  A person who acts as host at a formal event and introduces other speakers or performers.|The emcee introduced the next act with a flourish. 司仪以华丽的辞藻介绍了下一个节目。|The comedian emceed the awards ceremony.  这位喜剧演员主持了颁奖典礼。|近义词：host, presenter, moderator|反义词:audience, guest|词性:名词，动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From  M.C., an abbreviation of Master of Ceremonies.
