compresses:/kəmˈprɛsɪz/| v.|压缩，压紧|To press or squeeze something together so that it becomes smaller or more compact. |The nurse compresses the wound to stop the bleeding. 护士按压伤口以止血。

compresses:/ˈkɑːmˌprɛsɪz/| n.|敷布，压布|A pad of material pressed against a part of the body to relieve pain or reduce swelling. |Apply warm compresses to the injured area.  在受伤部位敷上温敷布。
