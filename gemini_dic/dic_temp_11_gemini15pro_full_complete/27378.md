came-forward:/keɪm ˈfɔːrwərd/| phrasal verb |挺身而出；自告奋勇；站出来|To volunteer oneself for a task or to give evidence or information.|例句：Several witnesses came forward after the police appeal.  警方呼吁后，几位目击者站了出来。|近义词：volunteer, step forward, speak up, come forth |反义词: hold back, stay silent|词性:动词短语

came-forward:/keɪm ˈfɔːrwərd/| phrasal verb |提出；提供|To present something for consideration.|例句：The company came forward with a new proposal. 公司提出了一个新的建议。|近义词：present, propose, offer, submit |反义词: withdraw, withhold|词性:动词短语


ETYMOLOGY->
"come" comes from Old English "cuman," meaning "to come, approach, arrive."  "Forward" comes from Old English "foreweard," meaning "onward, toward the front."
