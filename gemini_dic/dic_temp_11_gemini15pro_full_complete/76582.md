galliform:/ˈɡælɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. | 鸡形的；鹑鸡类的；鸡形目鸟 |Relating to or belonging to the Galliformes, an order of heavy-bodied ground-dwelling birds including turkeys, grouse, chickens, and pheasants.  | The turkey is a galliform bird. 火鸡是一种鸡形目鸟类。|  The farm raises various galliform species for meat and eggs.  这个农场饲养各种鸡形目鸟类以获取肉和蛋。 | 近义词: gallinaceous | 反义词: (无明确反义词) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *gallus* (“cock, rooster”) + *-formis* (“-shaped, -formed”).
