defrayals:/dɪˈfreɪəlz/| n. |支付，付款，费用支付|The defrayals of the travel expenses were handled by the company.  公司承担了差旅费的支付。|
Etymology:  From Old French *desfraier, from des- (expressing removal) + fraier ("to rub, break"). The original sense was "to break up," hence "to consume," "to expend," and then "to pay the expenses of."|
Synonyms: payments, reimbursements, expenditures, expenses|
Antonyms: income, earnings, revenue|
Noun
