half-healed:/ˌhæfˈhiːld/
adjective
半愈合的
partially healed; not fully recovered
例句：He walked with a slight limp, his broken leg still half-healed. 他走路有点跛，他的断腿还没有完全愈合。

例句：The half-healed wound still ached.  半愈合的伤口仍然疼痛。

近义词：partially healed, partly healed, incompletely healed
反义词：fully healed, completely healed, recovered
词性：adjective
