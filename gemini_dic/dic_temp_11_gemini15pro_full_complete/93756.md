immeasurable:/ɪˈmɛʒərəbəl/
adjective
不可估量的，无限的
too large, extensive, or extreme to measure
The damage caused by the hurricane was immeasurable. 飓风造成的损害不可估量。
近义词:incalculable, immense, vast, boundless, limitless
反义词:measurable, limited, finite
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + measurable.  Ultimately from Latin metiri (to measure).
