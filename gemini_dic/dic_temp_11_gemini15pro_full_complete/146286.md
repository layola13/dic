overfastidious:/ˌoʊvərˌfæˈstɪdiəs/| adj.|过分挑剔的，过于讲究的|Excessively attentive to details, especially regarding cleanliness or propriety; fussy.| She is so overfastidious about her appearance that it takes her hours to get ready. 她对自己的外表过于挑剔，以至于要花好几个小时才能准备好。|He's overfastidious about grammar, correcting even minor errors. 他对语法过于挑剔，即使是小错误也会纠正。|近义词：fussy, finicky, meticulous, picky, scrupulous, exacting|反义词：easygoing, undemanding, tolerant, casual, slovenly|词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *fastidium* "loathing, disgust," from *fastus* "pride, arrogance, disdain," related to *fari* "to speak" (via the notion of "speaking against"). The English prefix *over-* intensifies the meaning.
