annealers:/əˈniːlərz/
n.
退火炉操作员，退火装置
Persons or devices that anneal.
The annealers carefully monitored the temperature of the furnace. 退火炉操作员仔细监测炉子的温度。
The new annealers increased the efficiency of the process. 新的退火装置提高了工艺效率。
近义词:temperers
反义词:hardener
词性:名词
