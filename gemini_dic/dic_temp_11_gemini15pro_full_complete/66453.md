exempt:/ɪɡˈzempt/| adj. |免除的，豁免的|Free from an obligation or liability imposed on others.|Religious organizations are exempt from paying taxes. 宗教组织免缴税款。|近义词：immune, excused, free|反义词:liable, subject, responsible|词性:adjective
exempt:/ɪɡˈzempt/| vt. |免除，豁免|To free from an obligation or liability.|The government exempts low-income families from certain taxes. 政府免除低收入家庭的某些税款。|近义词：excuse, release, absolve|反义词:obligate, compel, subject|词性:verb

词源:
From Old French *exempt*  (Modern French *exempt*), from Latin *exemptus*, past participle of *eximere* "to take out, remove," from *ex* "out of" + *emere* "to take."
