ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French orizonte, from Late Latin horizontem, from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “limiting”), present participle of ὁρίζω (horízō, “I limit, define, separate”), from ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit, landmark”).
