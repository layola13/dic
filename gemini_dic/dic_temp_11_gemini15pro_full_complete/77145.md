gases:/ˈɡæsɪz/| n.|气体|The plural form of "gas," referring to substances in a state of matter without a fixed shape or volume.|Exhaust gases from cars contribute to air pollution. 汽车尾气导致空气污染。|近义词:vapors, fumes|反义词:solids, liquids|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from gas, perhaps from Greek khaos ‘chaos’.
