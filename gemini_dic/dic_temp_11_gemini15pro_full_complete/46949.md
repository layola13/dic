DARPA:/ˈdɑːrpə/ (US) /ˈdɑːpə/ (UK)| n. |美国国防高级研究计划局|Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency.  A US government agency responsible for the development of emerging technologies for use by the military.| The internet was originally a DARPA project. | 近义词: (loosely) ARPA, military research organization | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun (缩写)


Etymology:  Acronym derived from "Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency".  Originally named ARPA (Advanced Research Projects Agency) in 1958, it was renamed DARPA in 1972, then back to ARPA in 1993, and finally back to DARPA in 1996.
