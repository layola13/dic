built-in:/ˌbɪlt ˈɪn/| adj.|内置的，嵌入的|Something that is built-in is an integral part of something else, and cannot be easily removed.|This oven has a built-in timer. 这个烤箱有一个内置定时器。|integrated, incorporated|detachable, removable|adjective
built-in:/ˌbɪlt ˈɪn/| n.|内置设施|A built-in is a fixture, such as a cupboard or shelves, that is built as part of a house or room.|The house has several attractive built-ins. 这房子有几个吸引人的内置设施。|fixture, fitting|standalone, separate|noun
