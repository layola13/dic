indiscretion:/ˌɪndɪˈskreʃn/
n.
轻率；不谨慎；鲁莽；言行失检
Lack of discretion; imprudence.  An act or remark that shows a lack of good judgment.
例句：His youthful indiscretions were soon forgotten. 他年轻时的轻率行为很快就被遗忘了。

例句：She committed an indiscretion by revealing confidential information. 她泄露了机密信息，犯了一个错误。

近义词：imprudence, folly, recklessness, carelessness, slip, blunder, gaffe, faux pas
反义词：prudence, discretion, caution, circumspection
词性: 名词
