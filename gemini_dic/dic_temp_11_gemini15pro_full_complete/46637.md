damned:/dæmd/ | adj. | 讨厌的；该死的；十足的；非常的 | used to express anger with someone or something.  Also used for emphasis. |  This damned computer keeps crashing!  这该死的电脑一直崩溃！| synonyms: cursed, blasted, infernal | antonyms: blessed, sacred | adjective

damned:/dæmd/ | adv. | 非常；极其 | used to emphasize how great or bad something is. |  It's damned cold out there!  外面冷极了！| synonyms: extremely, very, awfully | antonyms: slightly, hardly | adverb
