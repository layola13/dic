abraders:/əˈbreɪdərz/| n. | 磨料，研磨剂 |Substances used for abrading or wearing away a surface. |  Industrial abraders are used to smooth metal surfaces. 工业磨料用于抛光金属表面。| synonyms: abrasives, grinding agents, polishing materials | antonyms: lubricants, polishes (in the sense of adding a shine rather than removing material) | noun
abrader:/əˈbreɪdər/| n. | 磨损物，磨蚀物 | Something that abrades or causes abrasion. |  Rough sandpaper can be an effective abrader. 粗糙的砂纸可以是一种有效的磨损物。 | synonyms: abrasive, irritant | antonyms: smoother, lubricant | noun
abrade:/əˈbreɪd/| v. | 磨损，擦伤 |To wear away the surface of something by friction. | The rocks abraded the skin on his knees. 石头擦伤了他膝盖上的皮肤。| synonyms:  scrape, scratch, erode, chafe, wear down | antonyms:  polish, smooth, protect | verb
