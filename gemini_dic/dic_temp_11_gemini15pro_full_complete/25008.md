brutelike:/ˈbruːtlaɪk/| adj.|野蛮的；残忍的；粗暴的|Resembling or characteristic of a brute; savage, cruel, or insensitive.|He displayed brutelike strength in lifting the heavy object. 他举起重物时展现出野蛮的力量。|The dictator ruled with brutelike force.  独裁者用野蛮的武力进行统治。|近义词：brutal, savage, ferocious, barbaric, cruel, inhuman, vicious|反义词：gentle, kind, humane, civilized, compassionate|词性:形容词
词源: brute (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Brute" comes from Latin "brutus" meaning "dull, senseless, irrational." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
