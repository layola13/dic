maltobioses:/ˌmɔːltəʊˈbaɪoʊsiːz/ | n. |麦芽二糖|A reducing disaccharide formed by the condensation of two glucose units linked by an α(1→4) bond; the repeating unit of starch and glycogen.|Maltobioses are important intermediates in the digestion of starch.|近义词: disaccharide|反义词: monosaccharide|词性:noun


Etymology:
malto- (from malt) + -bioses (plural of -biose, indicating a disaccharide)
