dazzlingly:/ˈdæzlɪŋli/
adverb
令人眼花缭乱地，耀眼地
in a way that is dazzling; brilliantly
The sun shone dazzlingly on the water.  太阳耀眼地照在水面上。
近义词：brightly, brilliantly, radiantly, glitteringly
反义词：dully, dimly
词性: adverb

词源: From *dazzle* (verb) + *-ingly* (adverbial suffix).  *Dazzle* ultimately comes from the Old Norse *dasa* "to become exhausted, dazed."
