cordlesses:/ˈkɔːrdləsɪz/| n.|无绳设备; 无绳电话（cordless的名词复数）|The cordlesses are all charged and ready to use. 所有的无绳电话都已充电完毕，可以使用。|近义词：wireless devices, portable devices|反义词：corded devices, wired devices|词性:名词
Etymology:  From "cordless" + "-es" (plural suffix). "Cordless" is formed from "cord" (a length of thick string or rope) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence). The word "cord" ultimately comes from the Latin "chorda" meaning "string of a musical instrument," from the Greek "khordē" meaning "gut, string."
