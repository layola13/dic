beguin:/ˈbeɪɡɪn/ (US), /ˈbiːɡɪn/ (UK)| n. |头巾，女用便帽；比喻虔诚女子，初学者|A close-fitting cap or hood worn by women, especially nuns or widows, in medieval times; figuratively, a devout woman, a novice.|例句：She wore a simple white beguin. 她戴着一顶简单的白色头巾。|例句：She entered the convent as a young beguin. 她年轻时作为初学者进了修道院。|近义词：hood, cap, coif, novice, beginner|反义词: expert, professional|词性:noun
