manoeuvring: /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ (British) /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ (American)
noun.
操控，调遣， maneuvering(美式拼写)
The complex manoeuvring of the spacecraft was a success.  宇宙飞船的复杂操控很成功。
synonyms: manipulation, handling, maneuvering, tactics
antonyms: inaction, stillness, idleness
n.

manoeuvring: /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ (British) /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ (American)
verb.
操控，调遣, maneuvering(美式拼写)
The captain skillfully manoeuvred the ship through the narrow channel. 船长熟练地驾驶着船穿过狭窄的航道。
synonyms: navigate, manipulate, steer, guide
antonyms: neglect, abandon, drift
v.


manoeuvring:/məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ (British) /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ (American)
adjective.
用于操控的, maneuvering(美式拼写)
The manoeuvring thrusters helped stabilize the satellite.  用于操控的推进器帮助稳定了卫星。
synonyms: tactical, strategic, operational
antonyms: static, fixed, immobile
adj.


Etymology: From French *manœuvrer*, from *manœuvre* "work done by hand," from Latin *manu operare* "to work by hand."
