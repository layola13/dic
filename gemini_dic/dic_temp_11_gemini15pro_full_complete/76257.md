fuzz:/fʌz/| n. v.|绒毛，细毛；模糊，失真|A mass of short, fine, light fibers or hairs; a blurred or indistinct image or sound.|The peach has a soft fuzz.  桃子有一层柔软的绒毛。|近义词：fluff, down, blur|反义词: clarity, sharpness|noun

fuzz:/fʌz/| v.|使模糊；起毛；变得模糊|To make or become blurred or indistinct.|The old photograph had fuzzed with age.  老照片因年代久远而模糊了。|近义词：blur, obscure|反义词: clarify, sharpen|verb
