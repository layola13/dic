intro:/ˈɪntroʊ/ (US) /ˈɪntrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |介绍，引言，序言|An introduction to something, such as a piece of music, a book, or a speech.|例句：The book has a short intro by the author. 这本书有作者写的简短介绍。|近义词：introduction, preface, foreword, prologue|反义词: conclusion, ending, epilogue|noun

intro:/ˈɪntroʊ/ (US) /ˈɪntrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |介绍性的，引言的|Serving as an introduction.|例句：This is an intro course to computer science. 这是计算机科学的入门课程。|近义词： introductory, preliminary|反义词: concluding, final|adjective
