lulls:/lʌlz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present lulls, present participle lulling, simple past and past participle lulled) | 使平静，使安静；缓和；哄骗 | to cause someone to feel calm or sleepy | The gentle rocking of the boat lulled me to sleep. 船的轻轻摇晃使我昏昏欲睡。 | synonyms: soothe, calm, quiet, pacify | antonyms: agitate, excite, rouse | verb

lulls:/lʌlz/ | n. (plural lulls) | 平静时期，间歇 | a temporary period of quiet or inactivity | There was a lull in the conversation.  谈话中出现了短暂的沉默。 | synonyms: pause, break, respite, intermission | antonyms: activity, bustle, commotion | noun
