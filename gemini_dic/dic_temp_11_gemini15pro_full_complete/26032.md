busheler's:/ˈbʊʃələr's/
n.
计量员的；量谷人的；以蒲式耳计的
belonging to or associated with a busheler (a person who measures or deals in bushels)
The busheler's scales were inaccurate.  计量员的秤不准。
近义词:measurer's
反义词: N/A
词性:名词所有格


Etymology:
From "busheler" + "'s" (possessive suffix).  "Busheler" comes from "bushel" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Bushel" originates from Old French "boissiel," meaning "small box," ultimately from Late Latin "buxillus," diminutive of "buxis," meaning "box" (of boxwood).
