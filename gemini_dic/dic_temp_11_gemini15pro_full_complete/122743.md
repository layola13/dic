moistens:/ˈmɔɪsənz/| v.|使湿润，弄湿|makes something slightly wet|The rain moistens the dry earth. 雨水滋润了干燥的土地。|dampens, wets|dries|verb
Etymology: From Middle English *moisten, from Old French moiste (“moist”), feminine of moist (“moist”), from Latin musteus (“new, fresh, young”), from mustum (“must, new wine”).
