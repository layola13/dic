goujere:/ɡuˈʒɛər/ /ɡuˈʒɛər/| n.|泡芙，奶酪泡芙；一种用奶酪做成的蓬松糕点|A small, light, savory pastry made of choux pastry mixed with cheese.|例句：The goujeres were warm and fluffy, perfect with a glass of wine.  奶酪泡芙温暖蓬松，搭配一杯葡萄酒堪称完美。

例句：She served a plate of delicious goujeres as an appetizer. 她上了一盘美味的奶酪泡芙作为开胃菜。|近义词：choux pastry, cheese puff, gignole|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of food)|词性:noun
