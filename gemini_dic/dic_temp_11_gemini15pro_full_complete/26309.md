buxomest:/ˈbʌksəməst/
adjective
最丰满的，最健壮的
superlative adjective of buxom; having a full, rounded figure; healthily plump
Example: Of all the women at the festival, she was the buxomest.  在节日的所有女性中，她是最丰满的。
Example: The buxomest barmaid served the thirsty patrons.  身材最丰满的女招待员为口渴的顾客服务。
Synonym:full-figured, plump, ample, curvy, voluptuous
Antonym:slim, thin, slender, skinny
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *buxsom, from Old English *būhsum (“obedient, pliant, submissive”), from *būgan (“to bow, bend”) + *-sum.  The semantic shift from "pliant" to "full-figured" likely occurred through an intermediate sense of "vigorous, lively."
