branchless:/ˈbrɑːntʃləs/| adj.|无分支的，不分叉的|Lacking branches; not divided into branches.  Referring to code execution without branches (conditional jumps), making it more efficient for some processors.|The company adopted a branchless programming approach to optimize performance.  这家公司采用了无分支编程方法来优化性能。|近义词：linear, straight, unbranched|反义词:branched, forked|词性:形容词
