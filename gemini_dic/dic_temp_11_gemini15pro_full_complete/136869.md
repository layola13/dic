nonslanderous:/ˌnɒnˈslændərəs/| adj.|非诽谤性的，不诽谤的|Not containing or constituting slander; not slanderous.|Her remarks were entirely nonslanderous, simply stating facts as she knew them. 她的言论完全没有诽谤之意，只是陈述了她所知道的事实。|The lawyer ensured the article was nonslanderous before publishing it. 律师在发表文章之前确保了文章不含诽谤内容。|近义词：nondefamatory, nonlibelous|反义词：slanderous, defamatory, libelous|词性：adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
slanderous (adjective): From slander (noun), from Old French esclandre "scandal, disgrace," ultimately from Late Latin scandalum "stumbling block, offense."
