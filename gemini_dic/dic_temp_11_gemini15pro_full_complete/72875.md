It often implies a lack of good sense or judgment and can range from minor mistakes to serious miscalculations.|The follies of youth are often forgiven.  青春的愚蠢行为通常会被原谅。|synonyms:  mistakes, blunders, indiscretions, absurdities, silliness, foolishness|antonyms: wisdom, prudence, sagacity|noun
