fiduciary: /fɪˈduːʃiˌɛri/ /fɪˈduːʃieri/ | adj. n. | 受托的，信托的；受托人，托管人 |  Involving trust, especially with regard to the relationship between a trustee and a beneficiary.  A person who holds a legal or ethical relationship of trust with one or more other parties (person or group of persons). Typically, a fiduciary prudently takes care of money or other assets for another person. |  As a fiduciary, the lawyer is bound to act in her client's best interests. 作为受托人，律师有义务为客户的最大利益行事。

 The company's fiduciary duty is to its shareholders.  公司的信托责任是对其股东的。 |  Synonym: trustee, guardian, custodian, representative  | Antonym: beneficiary, ward | adjective, noun
