golly:/ˈɡɒli/ (US) /ˈɡɔːli/ (UK)| int.|哎呀；我的天哪；|An exclamation of surprise or mild annoyance.|例句：Golly, that was close! 哎呀，那真惊险！

例句：Golly, I didn't expect to see you here. 我的天，没想到会在这里见到你。

近义词：gee, gosh, wow, my goodness|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|词性: Interjection
