flaxier:/ˈflæksiər/
adjective
更像亚麻的；更柔软的
more like flax; more flexible or yielding.
The flaxier material felt more comfortable against the skin. 这种更像亚麻的材料贴在皮肤上感觉更舒服。
Near Synonyms: softer, more pliable, more yielding, limper
Antonyms: stiffer, coarser, less flexible
Etymology: From flax + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flahsaz.
