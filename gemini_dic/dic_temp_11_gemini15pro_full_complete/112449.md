loudest:/ˈlaʊdɪst/| adj.|最响亮的，声音最大的|Superlative form of loud; having the greatest intensity of sound.|The loudest shout came from the back of the crowd.  人群中最大的喊叫声来自后面。|近义词：noisiest, strongest|反义词: quietest, softest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *loudest*, superlative of *loud*, from Old English *hlūd* (“loud”), from Proto-Germanic *hlūdaz* (“loud”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”). Cognate with Dutch *luid* (“loud”), German *laut* (“loud”), Swedish *ljud* (“sound”). The *-est* ending is a superlative suffix.
