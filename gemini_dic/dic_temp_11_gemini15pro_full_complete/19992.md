biographies:/baɪˈɒɡrəfiz/
noun
传记
An account of someone's life written by someone else.
I enjoy reading biographies of famous scientists. 我喜欢阅读著名科学家的传记。
synonyms:  life stories, memoirs, chronicles
antonyms: fictional stories, novels


Etymology:
From Late Latin *biographia, from Greek bios "life" + -graphia "writing".
