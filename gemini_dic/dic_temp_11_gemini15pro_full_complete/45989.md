cyanotype:/ˈsaɪənəˌtaɪp/ | n. |蓝晒；氰版照相法|A photographic process that produces a cyan-blue print. |例句：She learned how to create beautiful cyanotypes using sunlight and water. 她学会了如何用阳光和水制作美丽的蓝晒作品。

例句：The artist displayed a series of cyanotypes at the gallery.  这位艺术家在画廊展出了一系列蓝晒作品。|近义词：blueprint (in a metaphorical sense) |反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of other photographic processes like silver gelatin prints) |词性:noun

etymology->
From *cyan(o)-* "blue" + *-type* "impression, print."
