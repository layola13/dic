nonminorities:/ˌnɑːnmaɪˈnɔːrətiz/
noun
非少数群体，主流群体
People who are not part of a minority group.  This term is often used in contexts discussing diversity and inclusion, and can sometimes be perceived as insensitive.
The survey focused on the opinions of nonminorities regarding affirmative action policies.  这项调查重点关注非少数群体对平权行动政策的看法。
Nonminorities often have different experiences than minority groups in society.  非少数群体在社会中的经历通常与少数群体不同。
synonyms: majority, dominant group
antonyms: minorities, minority groups
