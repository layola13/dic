bitched:/bɪtʃt/ | v. | 抱怨；咒骂；狠狠批评 | To complain or criticize angrily and forcefully. | She bitched about her boss all night. 她整晚都在抱怨她的老板。 | He bitched me out for being late. 他因为我迟到而狠狠地骂了我一顿。 | 近义词：complained, grumbled, moaned, cursed, criticized | 反义词：praised, complimented | 词性：动词

bitched:/bɪtʃt/ | adj. |  糟糕的；讨厌的 (俚语，不常用) |  Very bad or unpleasant. (Slang, less common) | It was a bitched day at work. 今天工作很不顺。 |  The traffic was bitched this morning. 今早交通糟透了。 | 近义词：awful, terrible, horrible | 反义词：wonderful, great | 词性：形容词
