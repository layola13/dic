The action of performing an arabesque in ballet. | The ballerina held a graceful arabesquing pose.  芭蕾舞女演员保持着一个优美的蔓藤花纹姿势。| The ceiling was decorated with elaborate arabesquing. 天花板上装饰着精致的蔓藤花纹。 | synonyms:  flourishing, ornamentation, embellishment,  pose (in ballet) | antonyms:  simplicity, plainness | noun
