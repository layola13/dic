homogeneousnesses:/ˌhōməˌdʒēˈnēəsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 同质性，均匀性 | The state or quality of being homogeneous; uniformity. |  The homogeneousnesses of the samples allowed for accurate comparison. 样品的同质性使得能够进行精确的比较。| The homogeneousnesses of the population made it difficult to target specific demographics. 人口的同质性使得难以针对特定的人口统计数据。 | synonyms: uniformity, homogeneity, consistency, similarity | antonyms: heterogeneity, diversity, variety, difference | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From homogeneous + -ness. Homogeneous comes from the Greek "homogenes," meaning "of the same kind," from "homos" (same) + "genos" (kind, race, stock).
