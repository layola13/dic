managed:/ˈmænɪdʒd/ | v. |管理，经营；设法做到；控制；操纵| succeeded in achieving or producing (something difficult); controlled or handled (something or someone) |  I managed to finish the project on time. 我设法按时完成了项目。| synonyms: controlled, handled, directed, supervised, oversaw, administered, ran, operated, led, guided, conducted, coordinated | antonyms: failed, neglected, mishandled | verb (past tense)

managed:/ˈmænɪdʒd/ | adj. | 托管的；管理式的 |  describes something that is controlled or run by a management company or service |  We switched to a managed IT service. 我们改用托管IT服务。| synonyms: administered, controlled, supervised, run, operated | antonyms: unmanaged, independent, autonomous | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *maneger "to handle, wield, train (a horse)," from Italian maneggiare "to handle," especially "to control a horse," from mano "hand" (from Latin manus "hand"). The sense shifted from handling things skillfully to handling affairs to directing others.
