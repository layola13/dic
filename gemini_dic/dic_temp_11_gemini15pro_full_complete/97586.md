inhabitancy:/ɪnˈhæbɪtənsi/
n.
居住; 居住权；居住期间
The state or fact of inhabiting a place.
His inhabitancy in the town lasted for a decade. 他在该镇居住长达十年。
近义词: residence, habitation, occupation, dwelling
反义词: absence, vacancy
