jalapenos: /ˌhɑːləˈpeɪnjoʊz/ (US) /ˌhæləˈpeɪnəʊz/ (UK)| n. |墨西哥辣椒，哈拉皮尼奥辣椒|A medium-sized chili pepper pod of the species Capsicum annuum.  It is commonly used in Mexican cuisine.  Jalapeños range from mild to moderately hot, depending on factors like size and ripeness.|例句：I like jalapenos on my nachos. 我喜欢在玉米片上放墨西哥辣椒。

例句：These jalapenos are surprisingly mild. 这些墨西哥辣椒出奇的温和。|近义词：chili pepper, serrano pepper, chile|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific pepper, but perhaps "bell pepper" for its mildness) |词性:noun
