governmentalizes:/ˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使政府化，使官僚化
To make something governmental; to bring something under government control or influence.
The new regulations have governmentalized the healthcare industry. 新法规使医疗保健行业官僚化了。
The increasing regulations are governmentalizing the industry.  越来越多的法规使该行业变得官僚化。
Synonyms: nationalize, bureaucratize, regulate
Antonyms: privatize, deregulate
词性: 动词
