oversoaks: /ˌōvərˈsōks/
verb
浸透；过度浸泡
to soak or saturate excessively.
The heavy rain oversoaked the ground, turning the garden into a mud pit. 大雨浸透了地面，把花园变成了一个泥坑。
The towels were oversoaked and heavy with water. 毛巾被过度浸泡，充满了水。
近义词: drench, saturate, waterlog
反义词: dry, dehydrate
词性: v.
