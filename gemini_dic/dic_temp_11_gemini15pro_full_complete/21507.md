bludging:/ˈblʌdʒɪŋ/| n. vi.|偷懒，逃避工作；骗取；|The act of avoiding work or responsibility, often by pretending to be busy or incapacitated.  It can also refer to fraudulently obtaining something.|He was caught bludging on the dole. 他被发现骗取失业救济金。|近义词：slacking, shirking, dodging, malingering|反义词: working, toiling, grafting|noun
bludge:/blʌdʒ/| vi. vt.|偷懒，逃避工作；骗取；|To avoid work or responsibility. To fraudulently obtain something.|He bludged his way through school. 他在学校里一直偷懒。|近义词：slack, shirk, dodge, malinger|反义词: work, toil, graft|verb
