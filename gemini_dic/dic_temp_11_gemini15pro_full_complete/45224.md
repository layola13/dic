culminate:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/| v. |达到顶点；最终达到；以…告终|reach a climax or point of highest development.|例句：The celebration culminated in a spectacular fireworks display.  庆祝活动以一场壮观的烟火表演达到高潮。

例句：My years of research culminated in the publication of my book. 我多年的研究最终以我的书的出版而告终。|近义词：climax, peak, conclude, end|反义词:begin, start, commence|词性:动词

culmination:/ˌkʌlmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |顶点；最终；高潮|the highest or climactic point of something, especially as attained after a long time.|例句：The signing of the treaty was the culmination of years of negotiation.  签署该条约是多年谈判的最终结果。

例句：Winning the championship was the culmination of a lifetime of dedication. 赢得冠军是毕生奉献的顶点。|近义词：climax, peak, apex, summit|反义词:beginning, start, commencement|词性:名词
