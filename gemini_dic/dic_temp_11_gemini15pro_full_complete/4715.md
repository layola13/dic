alew:/ˈeɪluː/
n.
ale
一种啤酒，麦芽酒
Ale is a type of beer brewed from malted barley. 麦芽酒是一种用麦芽大麦酿造的啤酒。
They serve a variety of ales at the pub. 这家酒吧供应各种各样的麦芽酒。
近义词:beer, brew
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他类型的酒，例如wine)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *alu (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *aluth- (compare West Frisian oal, Dutch oud "old", Old High German alo, German Ale), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂elut- (compare Lithuanian alùs "beer", Russian álo "bottom; grounds"). The original sense was likely of a drink made from fermented honey.  最初的意思很可能是一种由发酵蜂蜜制成的饮料。
