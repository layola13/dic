constructiveness:/kənˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
noun|建设性，积极性|The quality of being helpful and intended to improve something.|Her criticism was always constructive, aimed at helping me improve. 她的批评总是建设性的，旨在帮助我改进。|
synonyms:positivity, helpfulness, usefulness, productiveness|
antonyms:destructiveness, negativity, unhelpfulness, counterproductiveness|
n.

constructive:/kənˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective|建设性的，积极的|Having a useful and helpful effect.|The meeting was very constructive, and we made a lot of progress. 这次会议非常有建设性，我们取得了很大进展。|
synonyms:helpful, useful, productive, beneficial|
antonyms:destructive, negative, unhelpful, detrimental|
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "constructive" + "-ness". "Constructive" comes from the Latin "constructus," the past participle of "construere"  (to build). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
