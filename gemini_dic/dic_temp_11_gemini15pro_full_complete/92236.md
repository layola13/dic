hypertragically:/ˌhaɪpərˈtrædʒɪkli/ | adv. | 极其悲惨地 |  In an extremely tragic manner; with excessive or exaggerated tragedy. |  He died hypertragically, leaving behind a young family and a grieving community. 他死得极其悲惨，留下了一个年轻的家庭和一个悲伤的社区。| The play ended hypertragically, with all the main characters dying. 这部戏以极其悲惨的方式结束，所有主要角色都死了。 | 近义词: very tragically, extremely sadly, devastatingly | 反义词: joyfully, happily | 词性: 副词

词源:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
tragic (adjective): from Greek "tragikos," related to tragedy
-ally (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
