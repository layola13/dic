aqueous:/ˈeɪkwiəs/| adj.|水的，水溶性的|Containing water; dissolved in water.  Relating to, or resembling water.|The chemist prepared an aqueous solution. 化学家配制了一种水溶液。|近义词：watery, hydrous|反义词：anhydrous|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *aquosus*, from aqua (“water”).
