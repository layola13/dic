lorette:/lɔːˈrɛt/ | n. |年轻女子，少女；(尤指)未婚女子|A young woman or girl, especially an unmarried one.|例句：The lorette giggled and blushed.  年轻女子咯咯地笑着，脸红了。

例句：He was courting a beautiful lorette. 他正在追求一位美丽的少女。|近义词：maiden, damsel, lass, girl|反义词：crone, hag|词性:noun


Etymology:
From French *lorette*, diminutive of *Lorette*, a place of pilgrimage near Paris, frequented by prostitutes.  The term originally referred to a prostitute or a woman of easy virtue.  Over time, the connotation softened to simply mean a young woman.
