glamourie:/ˈɡlæmərɪ/| n.| 魅力；魔力；迷人的地方|A quality of enchantment, mystery, and romance.|The actress exuded an aura of old Hollywood glamourie.  这位女演员散发着老式好莱坞的魅力。|近义词：glamour, magic, enchantment, allure, charisma|反义词：drabness, dullness, ordinariness|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Scots *glamourie, from glamour + -ie. Ultimately from grammar, via the notion of a magic spell.
