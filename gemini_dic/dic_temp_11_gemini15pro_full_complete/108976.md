leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/
n.|余地，余裕；自由活动的空间|An extra amount of time, space, materials, money, etc., that you can use if necessary. |The ship had 500 tons of leeway. 这艘船有500吨的载重余量。|Synonym: margin, latitude, flexibility, tolerance|Antonym: restriction, limitation
词性:名词

leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/
n.|（船）偏航；风压差|The sideways drift of a ship to leeward of the desired course.|The leeway caused the ship to drift off course. 风压差导致船偏离航线。|Synonym: drift, deviation|Antonym: course, track
词性:名词
