buckleless:/ˈbʌkəlləs/
adjective
无扣的；没有扣环的
Without a buckle or buckles.
The old saddle was buckleless and worn. 那旧马鞍没有扣环而且破旧不堪。
近义词:unbuckled, unfastened
反义词:buckled, fastened
词性:形容词

buckle:/ˈbʌkəl/
noun
扣环；带扣
A fastener for a belt or strap, consisting of a frame with a movable pin.
He fastened the buckle of his belt. 他系好了皮带扣。
verb
扣住；系牢
To fasten or be fastened with a buckle.
Buckle your seatbelt. 系好你的安全带。
词性:名词, 动词
