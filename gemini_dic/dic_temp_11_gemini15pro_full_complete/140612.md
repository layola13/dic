ollied:/ˈɒliːd/ /ˈɑːliːd/
adjective
醉醺醺的；烂醉如泥的
being happily drunk
例句：After a few ollies at the bar, he started singing loudly.  在酒吧喝了几杯后，他开始大声唱歌。

例句：She was completely ollied by the time the party ended. 派对结束时，她已经醉得一塌糊涂了。

近义词：drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy
反义词：sober
词性：adjective

词源:  Likely a slang term, possibly derived from "rolling" or "roly-poly," suggesting a swaying, unsteady movement associated with drunkenness. Its exact etymology is uncertain.
