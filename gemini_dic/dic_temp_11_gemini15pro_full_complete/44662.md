crucian:/ˈkruːʃən/ | n. |鲫鱼|A freshwater fish of the carp family, typically having a deep body and a long dorsal fin.|例句：The crucian carp is a popular fish for anglers. 鲫鱼是垂钓者喜爱的一种鱼。

例句：Crucian carp are known for their hardiness and ability to survive in low-oxygen environments. 鲫鱼以其耐寒性和在低氧环境中生存的能力而闻名。|近义词：carp, goldfish|反义词: None|词性:noun
