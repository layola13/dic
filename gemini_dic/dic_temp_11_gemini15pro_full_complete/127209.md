naughtest:/ˈnɔːtɪɪst/
adjective
最顽皮的，最淘气的
superlative adjective of naughty: most naughty
例句：He was the naughtest boy in the class. 他是班上最淘气的男孩。

例句：She gave him a stern look for being so naughtest.  她因为他这么淘气，狠狠地瞪了他一眼。


近义词：mischievous, disobedient, unruly
反义词：well-behaved, obedient, good
词性: adjective
