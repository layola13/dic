imitates:/ˈɪmɪteɪts/| v.|模仿，效仿|copies someone or something|He imitates his favorite singer perfectly. 他完美地模仿了他最喜欢的歌手。|近义词：mimics, copies, emulates|反义词：originates, creates|verb (third-person singular simple present)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imītāre, present active infinitive of imitō (“I imitate”), from Latin imitor (“I imitate”), from im- (“in, on, upon”) +‎ imitor (“I imitate, copy”).
