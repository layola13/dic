entogastric:/ˌɛntəˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
内脏的，胃内的
Relating to or involving both the intestines and the stomach.
The entogastric nervous system controls gut motility.  内脏神经系统控制肠道蠕动。
Entogastric reflexes regulate digestive functions. 内脏反射调节消化功能。
近义词:  intestinal, gastric, visceral
反义词:  (No specific antonym, perhaps extra-intestinal or extra-gastric could be considered in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
