de-polishing:/dɪˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/
verb
去光，磨光，抛光（的反义词）
The process of removing the polish or shine from a surface.
The jeweler specialized in de-polishing gemstones to achieve a matte finish.  珠宝商专门对宝石进行去光处理，以达到哑光效果。
The acid rain de-polished the car's paint. 酸雨腐蚀了汽车的油漆，使其失去光泽。
Synonyms: dulling, matting, roughening
Antonyms: polishing, buffing, shining
