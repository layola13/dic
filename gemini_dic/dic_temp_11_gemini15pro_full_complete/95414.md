incidental:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentl/ | adj. n. | 附带的，偶然的；附带事件 | happening as a result of something else, but not as the main reason; something that happens as a result of something else, but not as the main reason |  The discovery was incidental to their main research.  这个发现是他们主要研究的附带结果。| synonyms: accidental, casual, chance, secondary, subordinate | antonyms: essential, fundamental, important, intentional, primary | adjective

incidental:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentl/ | n. | 附带事件 | something that happens as a result of something else, but not as the main reason | There were a few minor incidentals on the journey. 旅途中发生了一些小插曲。 | synonyms: accident, circumstance, episode, event, happening | antonyms: plan, design | noun
