arson:/ˈɑːrsən/ (US), /ˈɑːsən/ (UK)| n.|纵火(罪)|The criminal act of deliberately setting fire to property.|例句：The police are investigating several recent arsons in the city. 警方正在调查该市最近发生的几起纵火案。

例句：He was charged with arson after burning down his neighbor's house.  他因烧毁邻居的房子而被控纵火罪。

近义词：incendiarism, firebombing|反义词:firefighting, fire prevention|词性:noun
