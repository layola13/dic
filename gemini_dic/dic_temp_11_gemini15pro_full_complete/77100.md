garrotting:/ɡəˈrɒtɪŋ/ /ɡəˈrɑːtɪŋ/
noun
绞杀，勒死
The act of strangling someone with a cord or wire.
He was charged with the garrotting of a wealthy businessman. 他被指控勒死了一名富商。
strangling, throttling, choking
releasing, freeing
