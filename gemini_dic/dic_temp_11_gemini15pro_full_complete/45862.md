cut-throat:/ˈkʌtθroʊt/| adj. n.|残酷的，激烈的；  凶手，刺客|

例句：The music industry is a cut-throat business.  音乐行业是一个残酷的行业。
例句：He was known as a cut-throat in the underworld. 他在地下世界被称为刺客。
近义词：ruthless, merciless, fierce, competitive, killer, assassin|

cut-throat:/ˈkʌtθrəʊt/| adj. n.|残酷的，激烈的；  凶手，刺客|

例句：The cut-throat competition for jobs left many graduates unemployed.  残酷的求职竞争导致许多毕业生失业。
例句：She faced cut-throat rivals in the tournament.  她在锦标赛中面临着残酷的对手。
近义词：ruthless, merciless, fierce, competitive, killer, assassin|
反义词：gentle, kind, compassionate, cooperative|
词性: adjective, noun


词源:  Originally referred to literally cutting someone's throat.  From "cut" + "throat".  Over time, the term evolved to describe any ruthless or merciless act, particularly in competitive situations.
