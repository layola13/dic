menstruate:/ˈmɛn.struː.eɪt/ (US), /ˈmɛn.streɪt/ (UK)| v.i.|月经来潮；行经|To discharge blood and other material from the lining of the uterus as part of the menstrual cycle.|例句：She started menstruating at age 12. 她12岁开始来月经。

例句：Some women experience pain when they menstruate. 一些女性来月经时会感到疼痛。

近义词：have one's period, have a period, be on one's period|反义词: N/A|词性:动词
