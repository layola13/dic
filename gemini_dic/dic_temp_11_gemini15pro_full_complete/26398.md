by-way-of: /baɪ weɪ əv/ | prep. | 经由；途经；通过…方式 |  Meaning "passing through," it indicates a route or method used to reach a destination or achieve something.  It can also introduce an explanation or additional information. | I flew to Tokyo by way of Seoul. 我经由首尔飞往东京。 | Synonyms: via, through, by means of | Antonyms: directly | preposition

by-way-of: /baɪ weɪ əv/ | adv. | 顺便提一下；附带说说 | Used to introduce a digression or additional remark, often parenthetical. | By way of introduction, let me tell you a little about myself.  顺便介绍一下，让我告诉你一些关于我自己的事情。| Synonyms: incidentally, by the way | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | adverb
