词源：
From Latin imperturbatus, from in- ‘not’ + perturbatus (past participle of perturbare ‘to disturb’), from per- ‘thoroughly’ + turbare ‘to disturb’.
来自拉丁语 imperturbatus，由 in-（不）+ perturbatus（perturbare 的过去分词，意为“打扰”）构成，perturbare 由 per-（彻底地）+ turbare（打扰）构成。
