dawe:/deɪv/| n.|朋友，伙伴 (Chiefly Scottish and Northern English)|A fellow, comrade; a friend, mate.  It's a term of endearment and familiarity, mostly used in Scotland and Northern England.|例句：Alright, dawe, how's it goin'?  好的，伙计，最近怎么样？|近义词：mate, pal, buddy, friend, chum|反义词:enemy, foe|名词
