co-organizer:/koʊˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzər/| n.|共同组织者，联合举办者|Someone who organizes something jointly with one or more other people.|例句：She was the co-organizer of the conference. 她曾是这次会议的联合组织者。

例句：The co-organizers worked tirelessly to make the event a success. 联合举办者们不知疲倦地工作，使这次活动取得了成功。

近义词：co-planner, joint organizer|反义词:participant, attendee|词性:noun
