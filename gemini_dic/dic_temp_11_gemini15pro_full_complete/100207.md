interrhymes:/ˈɪntərˌraɪmz/
n.|内韵，交韵|A rhyme between words within a line, rather than at the end of lines.|Example: The **rain** in **Spain** falls mainly on the plain (though not a perfect interrhyme, it illustrates the concept). 西班牙的雨主要下在平原上（虽然不是完美的内韵，但说明了这个概念）。|Example:  The cat sat back, relaxed and fat. 猫坐了回去，放松又肥胖。|近义词: internal rhyme|反义词: end rhyme|词性:noun
