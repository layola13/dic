hyperkinesis:/ˌhaɪpərkaɪˈniːsɪs/ | n. | 多动症，活动过度 | A condition characterized by excessive restlessness and movement.  It's often associated with ADHD. |  Example: The child's hyperkinesis made it difficult for him to sit still in class.  这个孩子的多动症使他难以在课堂上坐着不动。

Example:  Hyperkinesis can be a challenging condition for both children and their families. 多动症对儿童及其家庭来说都是一个具有挑战性的疾病。 | 近义词: hyperactivity, restlessness | 反义词: lethargy, inactivity | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Greek *hyper* (over, excessive) + *kinesis* (movement).
