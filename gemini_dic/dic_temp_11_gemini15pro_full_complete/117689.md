mela:/ˈmeɪlə/ | n. | 集市，庙会，节日 | A large gathering of people for religious worship, trade, or entertainment, especially in India. |  例句：The annual mela attracts thousands of pilgrims.  年度庙会吸引了成千上万的朝圣者。| 近义词：fair, festival, carnival | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "weekday" in some contexts) | 词性: noun

mela:/ˈmeɪlə/ | n. | (皮肤上的)黑斑，黑色素瘤 | A dark-pigmented patch of skin. (Medical) | 例句：The doctor examined the mela on her arm. 医生检查了她手臂上的黑斑。| 近义词：mole, blemish, spot | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
