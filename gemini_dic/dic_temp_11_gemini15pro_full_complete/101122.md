invest:/ɪnˈvɛst/ | v. | 投资，投入（时间、精力等） | To put (money) into financial schemes, shares, property, or a commercial venture with the expectation of achieving a profit.  To devote (time, effort, or energy) to a particular undertaking with the expectation of a worthwhile result. |  He invested heavily in the stock market. 他在股市投入了大量资金。

She invested a lot of time and energy in her children's education. 她为孩子的教育投入了大量时间和精力。 | synonyms:  expend, contribute, devote, allocate, commit | antonyms: divest, withdraw, liquidate | verb
