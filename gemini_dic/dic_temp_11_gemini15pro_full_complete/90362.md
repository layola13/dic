hovering:/ˈhʌvərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɒvərɪŋ/ (UK)| v. adj.|盘旋，徘徊；犹豫不决|remaining in one place in the air; remaining poised uncertainly|例句：A hawk was hovering over the field. 一只鹰在田野上盘旋。|近义词：drifting, floating, lingering, wavering|反义词: landing, settling, deciding|词性: verb (present participle of hover); adjective

hovering:/ˈhʌvərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɒvərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|徘徊的，犹豫不决的|being in a state of uncertainty|例句：He had a hovering sense of dread. 他有一种挥之不去的恐惧感。|近义词：lingering, uncertain, pending|反义词: decided, resolved, certain|词性: adjective
