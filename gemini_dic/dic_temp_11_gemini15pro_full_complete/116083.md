marty:/ˈmɑːrti/| n.|烈士;殉道者|A person who willingly suffers death rather than renounce his or her religion.  A person who is killed because of their religious or other beliefs.|Joan of Arc is a famous marty.  圣女贞德是一位著名的烈士。|Synonyms: sufferer, victim, sacrifice|Antonyms: perpetrator, oppressor|noun
