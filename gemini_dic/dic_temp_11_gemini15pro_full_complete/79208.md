glanders: /ˈɡlænərz/ | n. | 鼻疽 | An infectious disease of horses, donkeys, and mules, caused by the bacterium Burkholderia mallei. It is characterized by the formation of nodules and ulcers in the respiratory tract and other organs and can be fatal. | The horse was diagnosed with glanders and had to be euthanized. 这匹马被诊断出患有鼻疽，不得不被安乐死。|  Synonyms: farcy (a cutaneous form of glanders) | Antonyms: health | noun
