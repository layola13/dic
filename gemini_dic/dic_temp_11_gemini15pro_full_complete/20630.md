blacklister:/ˈblæklɪstər/| n.| 列入黑名单者；将…列入黑名单者 | A blacklister is someone who puts people or things on a blacklist. | The senator was a known blacklister of anyone who opposed his views.  这位参议员以把任何反对其观点的人都列入黑名单而闻名。| Synonyms: excluder, ostracizer | Antonyms: includer, welcomer | noun


blacklister:/ˈblæklɪstər/| vt.| 将…列入黑名单 | To blacklist someone or something means to put them on a blacklist, which is a list of people or things that are disapproved of and should be avoided. |  The company blacklisted several suppliers after a quality control scandal.  这家公司在质量控制丑闻之后将几家供应商列入了黑名单。| Synonyms: ban, bar, exclude | Antonyms: include, welcome, accept | verb
