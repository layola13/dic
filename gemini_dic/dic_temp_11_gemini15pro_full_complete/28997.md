carthamin:/ˈkɑːrθəmɪn/| n. |红花红; 红花素|A red crystalline pigment extracted from safflower blossoms.  A natural dye used in food and cosmetics.|Safflower petals yield a natural dye called carthamin. 红花花瓣会产生一种叫做红花素的天然染料。| The vibrant pink color of rouge was often derived from carthamin.  胭脂鲜艳的粉红色通常来自红花素。|Synonyms: safflower red, carthamic acid|Antonyms: None|Noun
