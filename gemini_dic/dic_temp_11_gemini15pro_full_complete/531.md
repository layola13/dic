abominates:/əˈbɒmɪneɪts/
| v. |憎恶；厌恶；痛恨|To have an intense dislike or hatred for something; to find something repugnant or loathsome.|She abominates cruelty to animals. 她痛恨虐待动物。|近义词：detests, loathes, abhors, despises|反义词：loves, adores, cherishes|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Latin abominari "to deprecate as an ill omen," from Latin ab "away from" + ominari "to forebode, prophesy," related to omen "sign, portent."  The original sense was thus "to shrink from as from an evil omen."
