imputes:/ɪmˈpjuːts/ | vt. | 归咎于，归罪于；认为…具有（某种性质）|  represents (something, especially something undesirable) as being done, caused, or possessed by someone; attribute. | The report imputes the accident to driver error. 报告将事故归咎于驾驶员失误。| synonyms: attribute, ascribe, assign, credit,  | antonyms: exculpate, absolve | verb

imputes:/ɪmˈpjuːts/ | n. |  (几乎不用作名词)归罪，归咎| An imputation. (Rarely used)|  He faced imputes of corruption. 他面临腐败的指控。(更常用的说法是 accusations of corruption)| synonyms: attribution, ascription | antonyms: exoneration | noun
