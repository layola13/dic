demicannon:/ˈdɛmiˌkænən/| n. |半加农炮|A type of cannon smaller than a regular cannon, typically firing a ball weighing about 30 pounds.|The demicannon roared, sending a shot across the battlefield. 半加农炮怒吼着，向战场另一边发射了一枚炮弹。|近义词：cannon, artillery, ordnance|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small arms" as a contrast in size and power) |词性:noun
