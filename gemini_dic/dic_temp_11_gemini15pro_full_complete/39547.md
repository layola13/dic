conscience's:/ˈkɒnʃənsɪz/ | n. | 良心，道德心 |  The possessive form of "conscience," referring to the moral sense of a particular person.  It indicates ownership or belonging related to one's conscience. | Her conscience's rebuke was loud and clear. 她的良心谴责非常强烈和清晰。| Synonyms: moral compass, inner voice, sense of right and wrong | Antonyms: immorality, amorality | Noun
