agoraphobic:/ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪk/| adj.| 广场恐惧症的，患旷野恐惧症的|  Relating to or afflicted with agoraphobia, an anxiety disorder characterized by a fear of places or situations where escape might be difficult or embarrassing, or where help might not be available in the event of a panic attack.| She became agoraphobic after a traumatic experience in a crowded shopping mall. 她在一间拥挤的购物中心经历了一次创伤性事件后患上了广场恐惧症。|Synonyms: anxious, fearful, panicked|Antonyms: calm, relaxed|adjective

agoraphobic:/ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪk/| n.| 广场恐惧症患者| A person who suffers from agoraphobia.| The agoraphobic rarely left their home.  这位广场恐惧症患者很少出门。|Synonyms: sufferer|Antonyms: None|noun
