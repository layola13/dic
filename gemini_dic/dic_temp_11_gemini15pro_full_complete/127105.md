nationalization:/ˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|国有化|The act of a government taking control of a privately owned business or industry.|The nationalization of the railways was a controversial move.  铁路国有化是一项有争议的举措。|近义词：state ownership, expropriation|反义词：privatization|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From nationalize + -ation.  Nationalize comes from national + -ize. National derives from Late Latin nationalis "pertaining to a nation," from Latin natio "nation, race, tribe," from natus, past participle of nasci "to be born."
