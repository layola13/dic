miszone: /mɪsˈzoʊn/
Verb
错误地划分区域; 划分错误的区域
To zone incorrectly; to assign to an incorrect zone.
The city council miszoned the land, leading to confusion among developers. 市议会错误地划分了这片土地的区域，导致开发商感到困惑。
The property was miszoned as residential when it should have been commercial.  这处房产被错误地划分为住宅区，而它本应该是商业区。
Misclassify, misallocate
Correctly zone
