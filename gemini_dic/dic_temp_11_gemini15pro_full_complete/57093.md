doughtiness:/ˈdaʊtinəs/
noun
勇敢；坚韧；刚毅
The quality of being doughty; valor; bravery; fortitude.
His doughtiness in battle was legendary. 他在战斗中的英勇是传奇。
Synonyms: bravery, valor, courage, fortitude, resilience, tenacity, boldness, fearlessness
Antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
n.

doughty:/ˈdaʊti/
adjective
勇敢的；坚强的；刚毅的
Brave and persistent; resolute.
A doughty warrior. 一位勇敢的战士。
Synonyms: brave, valiant, courageous, bold, fearless, intrepid, resolute, determined
Antonyms: cowardly, timid, fearful
adj.
