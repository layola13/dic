overcondenses:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˌdensɪz/
verb
过度压缩；过分简缩
reduces something excessively, especially a text or speech, making it too dense or concise.
The editor overcondensed the article, making it difficult to understand. 编辑过度压缩了文章，使其难以理解。
The author overcondenses complex ideas into short, confusing sentences.  作者将复杂的思想过度简缩成简短而令人困惑的句子。
近义词: abbreviates, compresses, shortens, summarizes
反义词: expands, elaborates, lengthens
