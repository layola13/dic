objectivate: /əbˈdʒɛktɪveɪt/ /əbˈdʒɛktɪvaɪt/
verb
使客观化；使具体化；物化
to treat a person as an object, especially a sexual object
She felt objectified by the way the men stared at her. 她觉得男人们盯着她的方式让她感觉自己被物化了。

verb
使客观化；使具体化；物化
to express something as an object
He struggled to objectivate his feelings. 他努力想把自己的感受客观化。

近义词: dehumanize, depersonalize, reify
反义词: personalize, humanize, subjectivize
词性: verb
