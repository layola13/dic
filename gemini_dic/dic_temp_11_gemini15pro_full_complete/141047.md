onlog: /ˈɒnlɒɡ/ (British) /ˈɑːnlɔːɡ/ (American) | n. | 在线日志；网络日志 | An online log, often shortened to "blog". It's a record of events, thoughts, or opinions maintained on a website, often updated frequently. | Example: She started an onlog to document her travels around the world. 她创建了一个在线日志来记录她的环球旅行。

Example:  He updates his onlog with new photos and stories every week. 他每周都会更新他的网络日志，添加新的照片和故事。

| 近义词: blog, weblog, online journal | 反义词: offline diary, paper journal | 词性: noun
