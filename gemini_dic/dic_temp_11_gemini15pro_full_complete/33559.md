cigarlike:/sɪˈɡɑːrlaɪk/| adj.|雪茄状的，像雪茄一样的|Resembling a cigar in shape; long and thin.|The aircraft had a cigarlike fuselage. 这架飞机有一个雪茄状的机身。|近义词：cigar-shaped, elongated, oblong|反义词:short, stubby|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cigar + -like. Cigar comes from Spanish cigarro, ultimately from Mayan sik'ar.  -like is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of".
