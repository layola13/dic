antagonistically:/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkli/| adv.|敌对地，对抗地|Showing or feeling active opposition or hostility towards someone or something.|The two groups debated the issue antagonistically. 这两个团体以敌对的态度辩论这个问题。|Example: He stared antagonistically at his opponent. 他敌视地盯着他的对手。

Example:  She antagonistically rejected all of his suggestions. 她以对抗的态度拒绝了他所有的建议。|Synonyms: hostilely, aggressively, belligerently, contentiously|Antonyms: cooperatively, amicably, supportively|词性: 副词
