noncoinages:/ˈnɑːnˌkɔɪnɪdʒɪz/
n.
非新造词; 未经创造的词语
Words that are not newly created or invented; existing words.
Many technical terms are noncoinages, borrowed from everyday language. 许多技术术语并非新造词，而是借用日常语言。
Synonyms: established words, existing vocabulary
Antonyms: neologisms, coinages
noun
