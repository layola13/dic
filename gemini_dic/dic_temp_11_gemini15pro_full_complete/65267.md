eucharis:/ˈjuːkərɪs/ /ˈuːkərɪs/| n. | Eucharis, 亚马逊百合 |A genus of bulbous plants native to the Amazon region, prized for their fragrant, white, trumpet-shaped flowers.  The name comes from the Greek word "eucharis," meaning "very graceful."| The eucharis filled the room with its sweet perfume. 亚马逊百合的香味弥漫了整个房间。|Synonyms: Amazon lily, Eucharis lily, Eucharis amazonica|Antonyms: N/A|noun
