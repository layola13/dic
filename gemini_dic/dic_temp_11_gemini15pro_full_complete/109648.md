leuc:/ljuːk/ | combining form | 白；白细胞 |  A combining form meaning “white,” “bright,” or “white blood cell,” used in the formation of compound words:  leukemia, leucocyte. |  Leukocyte counts are often elevated during infections. 白细胞计数在感染期间经常升高。 | 近义词: alb (although this refers more to general whiteness),  | 反义词: melan- (black, dark) | 词性: combining form
