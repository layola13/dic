dewrot:/ˈdjuːrɒt/ (US), /ˈdʒʊərɒt/ (UK)| n.|露 rot|A fungal disease affecting plants, characterized by soft, watery decay caused by excessive moisture, often associated with dew. |The dewrot ruined the entire strawberry crop this year. 今年露 rot 毁掉了所有的草莓作物。|近义词:blight, rot, decay|反义词:growth, health|词性:名词

词源: dew + rot.  "Dew" refers to the moisture from condensation, and "rot" refers to the decay process.  The word combines these two elements to describe the rot caused by excessive dew or moisture.
