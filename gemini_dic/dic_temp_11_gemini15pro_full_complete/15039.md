babushka:/bəˈbʊʃkə/ | n. | 老妇人；围巾；套娃 | An elderly woman, especially one perceived as a grandmotherly figure. Also refers to a headscarf tied under the chin, and a set of nested dolls. |  My grandmother, a true babushka, always has freshly baked cookies ready. 我的祖母，一位真正的俄罗斯老妇人，总是准备好刚烤好的饼干。

She wore a colorful babushka to keep her head warm. 她戴着一条色彩鲜艳的头巾保暖。 | Near synonyms: grandmother, granny, nana, headscarf, matron | Antonym:  (For person) young woman, girl  (For scarf)  None (For doll) None | noun
