heartener:/ˈhɑːrtnər/ (US), /ˈhɑːtənə(r)/ (UK)| n. |鼓舞人心的人或事；兴奋剂；强心剂|Something that cheers, encourages, or strengthens someone. |A hot cup of tea is a real heartener on a cold day.  一杯热茶在寒冷的日子里真让人感到温暖。|Synonyms: encouragement, boost, stimulant, tonic, pick-me-up|Antonyms: depressant, downer|noun
