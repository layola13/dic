descales:/diːˈskeɪlz/
verb
去除鳞片；除垢
removes scales or hard deposits, especially from metal surfaces.
The dentist descaled my teeth. 牙医给我洗了牙。
近义词: clean, remove, scrape
反义词: encrust, coat
词性:v.

descales:/diːˈskeɪlz/
noun
除垢剂；去鳞器
a substance or tool used to remove scales or deposits.
Use a descaler to remove limescale from your kettle. 用除垢剂去除水壶里的水垢。
近义词: cleaner, remover
反义词: N/A
词性:n.
