octopuses: /ˈɒktəpʊsɪz/ (UK), /ˈɑːktəpʊsɪz/ (US)| n. |章鱼|A sea creature with a soft body, eight arms, and a large head. It can change color and squirt ink.|例句：The octopuses camouflaged themselves against the coral. 章鱼在珊瑚中伪装自己。

例句：Octopuses are highly intelligent invertebrates. 章鱼是高智商的无脊椎动物。
