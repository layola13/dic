espied:/ɪˈspaɪd/| v.| 瞥见，发现| to see something or someone, usually after looking carefully or searching; to catch sight of, often unexpectedly. |例句：She espied a small boat in the distance. 她瞥见了远处的一艘小船。

例句：The detective espied the suspect entering the building. 侦探发现嫌疑人进入了大楼。


近义词：spotted, noticed, observed, detected, glimpsed|反义词:missed, overlooked|词性:verb
