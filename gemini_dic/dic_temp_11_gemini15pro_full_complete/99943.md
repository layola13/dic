internships:/ˈɪntɜːrnʃɪps/ (US) /ˈɪntɜːnʃɪps/ (UK)| n.|实习; 实习期|A period of work experience offered by an organization for a limited period of time, often to students or recent graduates.|She had several internships during her college years.  她在大学期间做过几次实习。|Synonyms: traineeship, apprenticeship, placement|Antonyms:  permanent employment|Noun
