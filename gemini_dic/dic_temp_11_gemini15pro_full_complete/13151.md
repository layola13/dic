asymptotically: /ˌæsɪmˈtɑːtɪkli/ | adv. | 渐近地 |  Approaching a value or curve arbitrarily closely but never reaching it.  | The graph of the function approaches the x-axis asymptotically.  函数的图像渐近于x轴。 | nearly, approximately | absolutely, exactly | adverb

asymptote: /ˈæsɪmptoʊt/ | n. | 渐近线 | A line that a curve approaches, as it heads towards infinity. | The x-axis is an asymptote to the curve y = 1/x. x轴是曲线y = 1/x 的渐近线。| limiting line | intersecting line | noun
