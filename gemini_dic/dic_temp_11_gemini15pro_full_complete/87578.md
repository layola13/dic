heteropodous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpədəs/ | adj. |异足的；具有不同类型的足的 | Having feet of different kinds. | Example: Certain crustaceans are heteropodous, with some legs adapted for walking and others for swimming.  某些甲壳类动物是异足的，一些腿适合行走，另一些适合游泳。| Example: The heteropodous nature of the creature allowed it to navigate both land and water with ease.  这种生物的异足性使它能够轻松地在陆地和水中航行。 | Synonym: anisoped | Antonym: homoped | Adjective
