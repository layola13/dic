disjointedness:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪdnɪs/ | n. | 不连贯性，脱节，支离破碎 | Lack of connection or coherence between parts or elements.  The state of being disjointed. | The disjointedness of the narrative made it difficult to follow.  叙述的不连贯性使它难以理解。 | incoherence, disconnectedness, fragmentation | coherence, unity, continuity | noun

disjoint:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/ | adj. |  不连贯的，脱节的，支离破碎的 |  Lacking connection or consistency; disconnected.  Separated at the joints. |  The plot felt disjointed and confusing.  情节感觉不连贯，令人困惑。| incoherent, disconnected, rambling | connected, coherent, unified | adjective
