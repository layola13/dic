commercialisms:/kəˈmɜːʃəlɪzəmz/
noun
商业主义；商业化；商业广告
the principles or methods of commerce, especially those regarded as excessive, debased, or exploitative.  The increasing commercialism of the holiday season.
Christmas has become increasingly commercialized. 圣诞节越来越商业化了。
synonyms:  merchandising, marketing, advertising, hucksterism, salesmanship, promotion
antonyms:  noncommercial, nonprofit
n.
