genes:/dʒiːnz/ | n. | 基因 |  The basic physical and functional unit of heredity, made up of DNA.  |  Genes determine characteristics such as eye color. 基因决定了诸如眼睛颜色之类的特征。

Genes play a crucial role in the development of diseases. 基因在疾病的发展中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: DNA, genome, chromosome, heredity | antonyms: N/A | noun
