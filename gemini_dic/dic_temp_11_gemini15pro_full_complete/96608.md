inducts:/ɪnˈdʌkts/| v. |正式引入，接纳；使就职|admits as a member; initiates or installs in office|The president inducted three new members into the society. 总统正式接纳了三位新成员加入协会。|The new CEO was inducted into the company last week. 新任首席执行官上周正式就职。|近义词：initiate, install, admit, introduce|反义词：expel, remove, discharge|词性：动词

词源: From Latin *inducere*, from *in* ("in, into") + *ducere* ("to lead").
