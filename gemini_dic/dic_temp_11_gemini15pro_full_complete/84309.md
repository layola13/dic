half-wildness:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪldnɪs/ | n. | 半野生状态; 半驯服状态 | The state of being partially wild or untamed; a mix of wildness and domestication. | The dog's half-wildness made him wary of strangers, yet he craved human affection. 这条狗半野生的状态使它对陌生人很警惕，但它又渴望人类的关爱。| Near synonyms: semi-wildness, partial domestication | Near antonyms: domesticity, tameness | noun

half-wildness:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪldnɪs/ | adj. | 半野生的; 半驯服的 | Partially wild or untamed; exhibiting characteristics of both wild and domesticated animals.  |  The half-wild horses roamed the plains, a magnificent sight. 这些半野生的马在平原上漫游，是一幅壮丽的景象。| Near synonyms: semi-wild, partially domesticated | Near antonyms: domesticated, tame | adjective
