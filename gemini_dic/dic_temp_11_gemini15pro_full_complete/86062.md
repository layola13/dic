Simple Present:
I hear the birds singing every morning. 我每天早上都能听到鸟儿唱歌。
He hears the news on the radio. 他通过收音机收听新闻。

Present Continuous:
I am hearing a strange noise. 我正在听到一个奇怪的声音。(较少用，通常用I can hear)
Are you hearing me clearly? 你能清楚地听到我说话吗？(较少用，通常用Can you hear)

Present Perfect:
I have heard that song before. 我以前听过那首歌。
She has heard the news about the accident. 她听说了关于事故的消息。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been hearing rumors about a new project. 我一直听到关于一个新项目的传闻。
They have been hearing complaints about the service. 他们一直在听到关于服务的投诉。

Simple Past:
I heard a knock at the door. 我听到敲门声。
Did you hear what he said? 你听到他说了什么吗？

Past Continuous:
I was hearing a strange noise when the phone rang.  电话响的时候我正听到一个奇怪的声音。
We were hearing the concert when the power went out.  停电时我们正在听音乐会。

Past Perfect:
I had heard the news before you told me. 在你告诉我之前我已经听说了这个消息。
She had heard the song many times before she learned to sing it. 在学会唱这首歌之前，她已经听过很多遍了。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been hearing rumors about his resignation for weeks. 我几周来一直听到关于他辞职的传闻。
They had been hearing strange noises coming from the basement for months. 几个月来，他们一直听到地下室传来奇怪的声音。


Simple Future:
I will hear the results of the test tomorrow. 我明天会听到测试结果。
You will hear from me soon. 你很快就会收到我的消息。

Future Continuous:
I will be hearing a presentation on the new product next week. 下周我将听取关于新产品的介绍。
They will be hearing arguments from both sides of the case. 他们将听取案件双方的辩论。

Future Perfect:
By the time you arrive, I will have heard the news. 等你到达的时候，我已经听说了这个消息。
By next year, she will have heard from all the universities she applied to. 到明年，她将会收到所有她申请的大学的回复。

Future Perfect Continuous:
By the end of the year, I will have been hearing complaints about this issue for six months. 到年底，我将已经听了六个月关于这个问题的投诉。
By the time he retires, he will have been hearing cases in court for over 30 years. 到他退休的时候，他将已经在法庭上审理案件超过30年了。

Subjunctive Mood:
If I heard any news, I would tell you. 如果我听到任何消息，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could hear what they are saying. 我希望我能听到他们在说什么。


Passive Voice:
The announcement was heard by everyone in the room. 房间里的每个人都听到了公告。
The case is being heard in court today. 此案今天正在法庭审理。


Interrogative Sentence:
Can you hear me now? 你现在能听到我说话吗？
What did you hear? 你听到了什么？

Negative Sentence:
I can't hear you. 我听不到你说话。
I didn't hear anything. 我什么也没听到。
