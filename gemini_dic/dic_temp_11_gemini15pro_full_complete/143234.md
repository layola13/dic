outdatedness:/ˌaʊtˈdeɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 过时；陈旧；落伍 |The outdatedness of the equipment hindered their research.  这些设备的过时阻碍了他们的研究。|例句：The outdatedness of their technology made them less competitive.  他们的技术过时使得他们竞争力下降。

例句：The outdatedness of the information rendered it useless. 信息的过时使其变得毫无用处。

近义词：obsolescence, antiquity, old-fashionedness | 反义词：modernity, currency, up-to-dateness | 词性：名词

词源:  From outdated + -ness.  Outdated comes from out- + date (verb).  Date (verb) means "to mark with a date," ultimately from Latin "datum" (given).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
