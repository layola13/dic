overdraws:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːz/ /ˌəʊvərˈdrɔːz/ | n. v. |透支；超支 | An overdraft occurs when money is withdrawn from a bank account exceeding the available balance. |  He had to pay charges on his overdraws. 他不得不支付透支费用。 | Synonyms: overdraft, excess, deficit | Antonyms: surplus, credit | noun
