hoggery:/ˈhɒɡəri/ (US) /ˈhɑːɡəri/ (UK)| n. |贪婪; 自私自利; 霸占; (尤指)对食物的贪婪| Excessive greed, selfishness, or the act of taking more than one's fair share, especially of food.|He displayed his hoggery by eating most of the cake. 他吃掉了大部分蛋糕，显露出了他的贪婪。|近义词：greed, gluttony, avarice, selfishness|反义词：generosity, altruism|词性:名词
Etymology: From "hog" (n.) + "-ery" (suffix forming nouns denoting a place or practice).  "Hog" itself comes from Old English "hogg," of uncertain origin.
