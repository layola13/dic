aimful:/ˈeɪmfʊl/
adjective
有目的的；目标明确的
Having a specific aim or purpose; purposeful.
She took aimful steps toward achieving her career goals. 她采取了有针对性的步骤来实现自己的职业目标。
The archer took aimful shots at the target.  弓箭手瞄准目标，射出了几箭。
近义词:purposeful, determined, focused, intentional
反义词:aimless, purposeless, random, undirected
词性:形容词

词源:
From aim + -ful.  "Aim" comes from Old French "aesmer" meaning "to estimate."  "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
