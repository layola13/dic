outwhirl:/aʊtˈhwɜːrl/
verb
旋转胜过; 旋转超过; (尤指通过旋转)超过
to surpass or exceed, especially by whirling or rotating
The new turbine design is expected to outwhirl all previous models in terms of efficiency.  这个新的涡轮机设计预计在效率方面将超过所有以前的型号。
The dancer's skirt outwhirled the others, captivating the audience.  舞者的裙子旋转超过了其他人，吸引了观众。
近义词: outspin, surpass, exceed, outdo
反义词: lag, fall behind
词性: v.
