heisler:/ˈhaɪzlər/ | n. | 海斯勒图; 海斯勒图表 | A chart used in heat transfer calculations, particularly for transient conduction in simple shapes like slabs, cylinders, and spheres, developed by M. P. Heisler.  It relates temperature to time and position within the object. |  Engineers use Heisler charts to determine the temperature distribution in a quenching process. 工程师使用海斯勒图来确定淬火过程中的温度分布。  The Heisler chart provided a quick solution for the transient heat conduction problem. 海斯勒图为此瞬态热传导问题提供了一个快速解决方案。 | 近义词: temperature chart, heat transfer chart | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for a specific chart) | noun
