例句：The intramercurial hypothesis was eventually disproven by Einstein's theory of general relativity. 水内行星假说最终被爱因斯坦的广义相对论所推翻。|近义词：submercurian|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps trans-Mercurian) |词性：adjective, noun
