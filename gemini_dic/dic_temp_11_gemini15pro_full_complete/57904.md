drifts:/drɪfts/| v.| 漂流，飘移；堆积；趋势，倾向 |The snow drifts piled up against the wall. 雪堆积在墙边。|例句：The conversation drifted from one topic to another.  谈话从一个话题转向另一个话题。|近义词：meanders, wanders, strays|反义词:focuses, directs|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *drifa, from Proto-Germanic *drifaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dhreybʰ- (“to drive, push, move”).
