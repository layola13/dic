Derived from "non-" (not) + "dyspeptic" (suffering from indigestion). |  He maintained a nondyspeptic outlook despite the stressful situation.  他尽管处境 stressful，仍然保持乐观的态度。 |  Example:  Her nondyspeptic nature made her a joy to be around. 她乐观的天性使人乐于与她相处。 | Synonyms:  healthy, cheerful, optimistic, contented | Antonyms:  dyspeptic, gloomy, pessimistic, irritable | Adjective
