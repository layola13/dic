immersing:/ɪˈmɜːsɪŋ/ | v. | 沉浸；浸入 | The process of becoming completely involved in something. |  She is immersing herself in her studies. 她正在专心致志地学习。

He was immersed in a good book. 他沉浸在一本好书中。

近义词: absorbing, engrossing, captivating | 反义词:  distracting, superficial | 词性: Verb (present participle)


immense:/ɪˈmens/ | adj. | 巨大的，广大的；无限的 | Extremely large or great, especially in scale or degree. | The universe is immense and awe-inspiring. 宇宙浩瀚无垠，令人敬畏。

They faced immense challenges during the expedition. 他们在探险过程中面临着巨大的挑战。

近义词: huge, vast, enormous, gigantic | 反义词: tiny, small, minute | 词性: Adjective


immersion:/ɪˈmɜːʃən/ | n. | 沉浸；浸入 | The action of immersing someone or something in a liquid or other substance. | Language immersion is a great way to learn. 语言沉浸是学习的好方法。

The immersion of the baptismal candidate symbolized a new beginning. 受洗者的浸礼象征着一个新的开始。


近义词: submersion, dipping | 反义词: emersion, removal | 词性: Noun
