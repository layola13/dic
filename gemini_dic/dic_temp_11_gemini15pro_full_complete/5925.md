ambitious:/æmˈbɪʃəs/| adj.|有雄心的；野心勃勃的；有抱负的|Having or showing a strong desire and determination to succeed.|She has ambitious plans for her future. 她对自己的未来有雄心勃勃的计划。|近义词:aspiring, determined, driven|反义词:unambitious, lazy, apathetic|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *ambicieux*, from Latin ambitiōsus (“going around canvassing for office, ambitious”), from ambītiō (“a going around, canvassing, ambition”), from ambīre (“to go around”), from ambi- (“around”) + īre (“to go”).
