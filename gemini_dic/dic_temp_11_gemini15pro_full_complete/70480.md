filacer:/ˈfɪləsər/| n.|档案管理员；卷宗保管员|An officer of a court of law, formerly in charge of filing writs and other documents.|The filacer handed the judge the necessary documents for the case.|近义词:clerk, registrar, archivist|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *filacer, from filer (“to file”) + -er (agent suffix). Ultimately from Latin fīlum (“thread”).
