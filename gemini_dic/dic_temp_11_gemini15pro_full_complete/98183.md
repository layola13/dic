inscrutably:/ɪnˈskruːtəbli/
adverb
难以捉摸地；神秘莫测地
in a way that is impossible to understand or interpret
He smiled inscrutably. 他神秘地笑了笑。
近义词: enigmatically, mysteriously, impenetrably
反义词: clearly, understandably, transparently
词性: adverb

inscrutable:/ɪnˈskruːtəbl/
adjective
难以捉摸的；神秘莫测的
impossible to understand or interpret
His face remained inscrutable. 他的表情依然难以捉摸。
近义词: enigmatic, mysterious, impenetrable
反义词: clear, understandable, transparent
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin inscrutabilis, from in- ‘not’ + scrutari ‘to search, examine’.
