budgeted:/ˈbʌdʒɪtɪd/
adjective
已编入预算的；按预算拨款的
Planned or allocated within a budget.
The project is fully budgeted. 该项目已全部编入预算。
The company has budgeted $1 million for marketing. 公司已将100万美元编入营销预算。
近义词:allocated, appropriated, earmarked
反义词:unbudgeted, unplanned
词性:adjective
词源:From budget (n.) + -ed. "Budget" originates from Old French "bougette" meaning a small bag or pouch, later coming to refer to a leather bag containing a sum of money or accounts, and eventually evolving to mean a financial plan.
