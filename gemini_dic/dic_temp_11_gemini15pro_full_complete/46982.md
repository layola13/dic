darwinistic:/dɑːrˈwɪnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 达尔文主义的；适者生存的 | Of or relating to Darwinism; based on the theory of natural selection. |  His darwinistic view of the business world meant only the strongest companies survived. 他对商业世界的达尔文主义观点意味着只有最强大的公司才能生存。

| Example: The company's darwinistic approach to hiring meant only the most qualified applicants were chosen.  这家公司达尔文式的招聘方法意味着只有最合格的申请者才能被选中。| Synonym: evolutionary, natural selection, survival of the fittest | Antonym: creationist, Lamarckian | 词性: 形容词
