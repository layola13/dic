milder:/ˈmaɪldər/ (US), /ˈmaɪldə/ (UK)| adj.|更温和的，更轻微的，更温暖的|comparative form of mild: less severe or strong; not extreme; gentle and not easily upset|The weather is milder today than it was yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更温和。|The symptoms were much milder this time.  这次症状轻微多了。|近义词：gentler, softer, warmer, calmer|反义词：harsher, stronger, colder, more severe|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *mylder, *mildre, comparative form of mild, from Old English mildre (“milder”), comparative form of milde (“mild”).
