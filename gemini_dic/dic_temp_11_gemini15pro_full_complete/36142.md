coexecutors: /koʊɪɡˈzɛkjətərz/
| n. |共同执行人；共同遗嘱执行人|Two or more people appointed to administer the estate of a deceased person.  Those authorized to act jointly in the execution of a will.|John and Mary were named coexecutors of their father's will. 约翰和玛丽被任命为他们父亲遗嘱的共同执行人。|synonyms: joint executors|antonyms: N/A|noun
