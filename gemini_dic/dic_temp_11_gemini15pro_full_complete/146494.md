overgarnished: /ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃt/
adjective
过度装饰的
Excessively decorated or embellished, especially with food garnishes.  Having too many garnishes, to the point of being cluttered or overwhelming.
The steak was perfectly cooked, but slightly overgarnished with parsley and fried onions. 这块牛排做得很好，但是香菜和炸洋葱的装饰有点过多了。
The salad was so overgarnished that I could barely see the lettuce underneath. 沙拉装饰得太多了，我几乎看不到下面的生菜了。
近义词: overdecorated, overdone, fussy
反义词: plain, simple, understated
词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "garnish" (from Old French "garnir" meaning "to furnish, equip, provide")
