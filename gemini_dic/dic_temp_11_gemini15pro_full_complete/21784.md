bobbing:/ˈbɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| v. n. adj.|上下摆动，轻拍，行屈膝礼|The act of moving up and down quickly and repeatedly, or making something do this.|
The apples were bobbing in the water. 苹果在水中上下浮动。
The children were bobbing for apples. 孩子们正在玩咬苹果游戏。

bobbing:/ˈbɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| v. n. adj.|剪短（头发）|Cutting hair short.|
She had a bobbing haircut. 她剪了一个短发。
He was bobbing his hair. 他在剪短他的头发。


bobbing:/ˈbɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| v. n. adj.|行屈膝礼|Making a curtsy.|
She bobbed a curtsy to the queen. 她向女王行了一个屈膝礼。
The girls were bobbing to the queen. 女孩们正在向女王行屈膝礼。


近义词：dipping, bouncing, nodding, ducking, curtseying|
反义词：rising, ascending, soaring|
词性：Verb (主要), Noun, Adjective (少用)|
词源：From Middle English *bobben*, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French *bober*.|
