bedrid:/ˈbedrɪd/| adj.|卧床不起的，长期卧床的|Confined to bed by illness or infirmity.|例句：He was bedrid for months after the accident. 事故发生后，他卧床不起数月。

例句：Her grandmother has been bedrid for years. 她祖母已经卧床多年了。|近义词：bedridden, confined to bed, housebound|反义词: active, mobile, healthy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *bedrida, composed of bed + rida "rider."  Originally meaning "one who constantly rides (stays in) bed."
