observator:/əbˈzɜːrvətər/| n.|观察者，观察员|A person who observes something or someone.  A person who is assigned to observe something or someone officially.|例句：The United Nations sent observators to monitor the elections. 联合国派观察员监督选举。

例句：He was a keen observator of human behavior. 他是一个敏锐的人类行为观察者。|近义词：watcher, onlooker, viewer, witness, monitor|反义词:participant, performer|noun
