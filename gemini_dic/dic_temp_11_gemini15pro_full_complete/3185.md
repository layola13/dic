affaire:/əˈfɛər/| n.|事情，事件；事务；私通|An affair is an event or sequence of events of a particular kind, or that is of public interest or importance.  It can also refer to a romantic or sexual relationship, typically one that is secret or illicit.|例句：The Watergate affair was a major political scandal. 水门事件是一起重大的政治丑闻。

例句：He was having an affair with a married woman. 他和一个已婚妇女有染。|近义词：matter, business, concern, issue, event, incident, romance, liaison|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
