mesmerizations:/ˌmɛzmərɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. | 迷惑，催眠，着迷 |The act of mesmerizing or the state of being mesmerized.  The process of captivating someone's attention completely.| The magician's mesmerizing performance left the audience spellbound. 魔术师令人着迷的表演使观众着迷。|  The mesmerizing beauty of the sunset captivated us all. 日落迷人的美景深深吸引了我们所有人。 | synonyms:  captivations, enchantments, fascinations, trances | antonyms:  disenchantments, repulsions | noun
