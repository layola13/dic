commendingly:/kəˈmɛndɪŋli/
adv.
赞赏地，称赞地
In a manner that expresses praise or approval.
He spoke commendingly of her efforts. 他赞赏地谈到她的努力。
近义词: approvingly, laudably, favorably
反义词: disapprovingly, critically, unfavorably
词性: 副词
