heedlessnesses:/ˈhiːdlɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
粗心大意；疏忽；轻率
lack of attention and care; thoughtlessness
His heedlessnesses led to several costly mistakes. 他的粗心大意导致了几个代价高昂的错误。
synonyms: carelessness, negligence, inattention, thoughtlessness, recklessness
antonyms: carefulness, caution, attention, mindfulness, prudence
