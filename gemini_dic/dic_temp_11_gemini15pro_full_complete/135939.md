nonpurchaser:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːrtʃəsər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈpɜːtʃəsə(r)/ (UK)| n. |非购买者，未购买者|A person who does not buy a particular product or service.|例句：The survey targeted both purchasers and nonpurchasers of the product. 这项调查针对产品的购买者和非购买者。|近义词：non-buyer|反义词: purchaser, buyer|词性: 名词

nonpurchaser:|Etymology: non- + purchaser.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "purchaser" comes from the Old French "purchaser" and ultimately from the Latin "procurrāre," meaning "to manage, procure."|词源：non- + purchaser。“Non-”是一个表示“不”的前缀，“purchaser”来自古法语“purchaser”，最终来自拉丁语“procurrāre”，意思是“管理、采购”。
