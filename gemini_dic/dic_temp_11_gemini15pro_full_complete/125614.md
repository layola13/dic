multivocal:/ˌmʌltɪˈvoʊkl/ /ˌmʌltiˈvəʊkəl/| adj.|多音的；多义的；有多种解释的|Having many voices or interpretations; capable of being understood in multiple ways.  Origin: from Latin *multus* "many" + *vocalis* "sounding, vocal."|The poem is multivocal, lending itself to various interpretations. 这首诗是多义的，可以有多种解读。|近义词:ambiguous, polysemous, multifaceted|反义词:univocal, monosemous, singular|词性:adjective
