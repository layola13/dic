obviable:/ˈɒbviəbl/ /ˈɑːviəbl/ | adj. | 可避免的，可消除的 | Capable of being obviated, avoided, or prevented. |  The accident was entirely obviable had the driver been paying attention.  如果司机当时注意了，事故是完全可以避免的。

The risks of infection are obviable through proper hygiene. 通过适当的卫生措施，感染风险是可以避免的。 | 近义词: avoidable, preventable, avertable | 反义词: unavoidable, inevitable, inescapable | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *obviabilis, from obviare (“to meet, to withstand, to obviate”) + -abilis.
