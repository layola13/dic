lotus:/ˈloʊtəs/ (US) /ˈləʊtəs/ (UK)| n.|莲花，荷花|A large-flowered water lily, especially the sacred lotus of India and Egypt.|例句：The lotus flower is a symbol of purity. 莲花是纯洁的象征。

例句：The lotus blossoms in muddy water.  莲花在泥水中绽放。

近义词：water lily|反义词:|n.

lotus:/ˈloʊtəs/ (US) /ˈləʊtəs/ (UK)| adj.|忘忧的，极乐世界的|Relating to or resembling the lotus flower, especially in its association with purity or spiritual enlightenment.|例句：He lived a lotus life, detached from worldly concerns. 他过着与世无争的极乐生活。

例句：She sought lotus-like serenity amidst the chaos.  在混乱中，她寻求莲花般的宁静。

近义词：peaceful, serene|反义词:turbulent, chaotic|adj.
