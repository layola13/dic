decodable:/dɪˈkoʊdəbl/ /diːˈkəʊdəbl/| adj.|可解码的；可译码的|Able to be decoded; capable of being deciphered or interpreted.|The message was decodable using a simple cipher. 这条消息可以使用简单的密码译出。|近义词：interpretable, decipherable, understandable|反义词：indecipherable, unreadable, incomprehensible|词性:形容词
decode:/diːˈkoʊd/ /diːˈkəʊd/| v.|解码；译码|To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language.|The spy decoded the secret message.  间谍解码了秘密信息。|近义词：decipher, interpret, translate|反义词：encode, encrypt|词性:动词
decoder:/diːˈkoʊdər/ /diːˈkəʊdə(r)/| n.|解码器；译码器|A person or thing that decodes.|The decoder converted the digital signal into an analog one. 解码器将数字信号转换成模拟信号。|近义词：deciphering device, interpreter|反义词：encoder|词性:名词
