ibero:/aɪˈbɪəroʊ/ /aɪˈbiːrəʊ/
adjective
伊比利亚的; 西班牙和葡萄牙的
Relating to the Iberian Peninsula, especially to Spain and Portugal, or to the languages and cultures of this region.
The Iberian lynx is native to Spain and Portugal.  伊比利亚猞猁原产于西班牙和葡萄牙。
The Iberian Peninsula is located in southwestern Europe. 伊比利亚半岛位于欧洲西南部。
synonym: Hispanic, Lusitanian
antonym: (No direct antonym, could use non-Iberian)
