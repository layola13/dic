loamless:/ˈloʊmlɪs/
adjective
无沃土的；缺乏肥沃土壤的
lacking loam; having no loam.
The loamless soil made it difficult to grow anything.  这片无沃土的土地使得种植任何东西都很困难。
The farmer struggled with the loamless terrain.  农民在这片贫瘠的土地上耕作很辛苦。
synonym:barren, infertile, sterile, unproductive
antonym:fertile, rich, loamy
词性:adjective
