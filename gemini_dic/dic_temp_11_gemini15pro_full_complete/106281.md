knuckle:/ˈnʌkəl/| n. v. |指关节； knuckle down:  认真工作，专心致志|A knuckle is a joint in a finger, especially one of the joints connecting the fingers to the hand.  To knuckle down means to apply oneself seriously to a task.|He rapped his knuckles on the door. 他用指关节敲了敲门。

It's time to knuckle down and finish this project. 是时候认真工作完成这个项目了。|近义词：(for joint) joint, finger joint (for knuckle down)  apply oneself, focus, concentrate|反义词: (for knuckle down) procrastinate, delay|词性:名词, 动词
