| The ancient Egyptians divinified the pharaohs. 古埃及人神化了法老。 | 近义词: deify, apotheosize, exalt, glorify | 反义词: secularize, profane, degrade | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divinificare, from Latin divinus (“divine”) + -ficare,  from facere (“to make”).  Essentially meaning "to make divine".
