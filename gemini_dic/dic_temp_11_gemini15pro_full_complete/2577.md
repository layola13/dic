admonish: /ədˈmɑːnɪʃ/ (US) /ədˈmɒnɪʃ/ (UK)| v.|劝告；警告；责备|To warn or reprimand someone firmly.|The teacher admonished the students for being late. 老师责备学生迟到。|例句：He was admonished for his carelessness. 他因粗心大意而受到警告。

例句：The judge admonished the jury to consider only the evidence presented in court. 法官告诫陪审团只考虑法庭上提供的证据。

近义词：reprimand, rebuke, caution, warn, scold|反义词: praise, commend, compliment|词性: verb
