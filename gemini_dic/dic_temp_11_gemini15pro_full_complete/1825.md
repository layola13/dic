acrobatical: /ˌækrəˈbætɪkl/ | adj. | 杂技的；特技的；杂技般的 | Relating to or characteristic of acrobats or acrobatics; involving or requiring agility and skill. |  The acrobatical feats of the performers were breathtaking.  表演者的杂技技艺令人叹为观止。 | 近义词： gymnastic, agile, nimble | 反义词： clumsy, awkward | 词性: 形容词
Etymology:  From acrobat + -ical. Acrobat is from French acrobate, from Greek akrobatēs "rope-dancer," from akrobatos "walking on tiptoe," from akron "extremity, summit" + bainein "to go, walk, step."
