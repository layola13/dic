obstructedly:/əbˈstrʌktɪdli/
adv.
阻碍地，妨碍地
in a way that blocks or hinders movement or progress.
The road was obstructedly narrow, making it difficult for two cars to pass. 道路狭窄阻塞，两辆车难以通行。
近义词: hinderingly, blockingly, impediently
反义词: clearly, openly, freely
词性: 副词

obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
v.
阻碍，阻塞，妨碍
block (an opening, path, road, etc.) so as to prevent or hinder passage or progress.
The fallen tree obstructed the road. 倒下的树阻塞了道路。
近义词: block, hinder, impede
反义词: clear, open, facilitate
词性: 动词

obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/
n.
阻碍，阻塞，障碍物
something that blocks or hinders movement or progress.
The protesters formed an obstruction in the street. 抗议者在街上形成了路障。
近义词: blockage, barrier, obstacle
反义词: clearance, opening, passage
词性: 名词
