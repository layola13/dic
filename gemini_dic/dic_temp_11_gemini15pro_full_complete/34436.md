claustrum:/ˈklɔːstrəm/ | n. |屏状核; 隔膜;  |A thin sheet of gray matter located within the white matter of the brain, lateral to the putamen and medial to the insular cortex, thought to be involved in consciousness and integrating information from different brain regions. |The claustrum's exact function remains a mystery, but research suggests it may play a role in integrating sensory information. 屏状核的确切功能仍然是一个谜，但研究表明它可能在整合感官信息方面发挥作用。

The claustrum is a relatively small structure, making it difficult to study. 屏状核是一个相对较小的结构，因此很难研究。 | 近义词:  (no direct anatomical synonym, functionally related to: insula, thalamus) | 反义词: (none) | 词性: noun

claustra:/ˈklɔːstrə/ | n. |屏状核（复数）; 隔膜（复数）|Plural form of claustrum. Refers to multiple claustrums (rarely used as the structure is singular in a single brain). |Studies on the claustra of different mammals may reveal evolutionary insights. 对不同哺乳动物屏状核的研究可能会揭示进化方面的见解。

Damage to the claustra could have widespread cognitive consequences. 对屏状核的损害可能会造成广泛的认知后果。 | 近义词: (no direct anatomical synonym) | 反义词: (none) | 词性: noun
