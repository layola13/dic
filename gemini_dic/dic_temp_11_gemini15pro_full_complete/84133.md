half-minded:/ˈhæfˈmaɪndɪd/
adjective
优柔寡断的；意志不坚定的；三心二意的
Having or showing a lack of determination or resolution; irresolute.
He was half-minded about accepting the job offer. 他对接受这份工作有些犹豫不决。

近义词:indecisive, hesitant, wavering, ambivalent, irresolute, uncertain, doubtful, vacillating
反义词:resolute, decisive, determined, committed

词性:adjective

词源:
