lipyl:/ˈlɪpɪl/ | n. | 脂酰 | A fatty acid radical.  Specifically, the radical of a fatty acid in which the hydroxyl group of the carboxyl has been replaced, as by an alkyl group. |  Lipyl groups are components of many lipids. 脂酰基是许多脂质的组成部分。|  synonym: acyl group, fatty acid residue | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a chemical component like lipyl) | noun
