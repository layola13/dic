extradites:/ˈɛkstrəˌdaɪts/
verb
引渡
hands over (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another country or state.
The US extradites criminals to other countries if they have committed crimes there. 美国将在他国犯下罪行的罪犯引渡到这些国家。
deports, hands over, delivers
protects, shelters, harbors
