excretes:/ɪkˈskriːts/ /ɛkˈskriːts/
verb
排泄，分泌
(of a living organism or cell) separates and expels waste matter from the body or from the cell.
The kidneys excrete waste products from the blood. 肾脏从血液中排出废物。
近义词:discharges, eliminates, expels, voids
反义词:absorbs, retains
词性:v.
