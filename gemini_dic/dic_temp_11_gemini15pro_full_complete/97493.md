ingerminate:/ɪnˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/
adjective
未萌发的;未发芽的
Not germinated; not having sprouted or developed.
The ingerminate seeds remained dormant in the dry soil.  未萌发的种子在干燥的土壤中保持休眠状态。
The farmer was concerned about the ingerminate corn kernels. 农民担心玉米粒没有发芽。
近义词:dormant, undeveloped, quiescent
反义词:germinated, sprouted, developed
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *in- "not" + germinatus, past participle of germinare "to sprout," from germen "bud, sprout."
