canvasers:/ˈkænvəsərz/
n.
拉选票者，游说者，推销员
People who solicit votes, support, or sales from the public.
The canvasers went door to door trying to persuade people to vote for their candidate.  拉票员挨家挨户地试图说服人们投票给他们的候选人。
The charity employed canvasers to raise funds for their cause.  该慈善机构雇佣了游说者为他们的事业筹款。
synonyms: solicitors, campaigners, canvassers
antonyms: customers, clients
