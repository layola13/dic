bonds: /bɑːndz/ (US) /bɒndz/ (UK)
| n. | 债券；纽带；联系；粘合剂 |  A financial security that represents a loan made by an investor to a borrower (typically corporate or governmental). Also refers to a connection or relationship between people or things, or a substance used for sticking things together. |  The company issued bonds to raise capital.  公司发行债券以筹集资金。 | synonyms: securities, debentures, ties, links, connections, adhesives, glue | antonyms: detachment, separation, freedom, disconnection | noun

bonds: /bɑːndz/ (US) /bɒndz/ (UK)
| v. |  结合；建立联系 | To connect or fasten securely; to establish a relationship. | The adhesive bonds the two surfaces together. 这款粘合剂把两个表面粘合在一起。 | synonyms: connect, link, join, unite, adhere | antonyms: separate, detach, disconnect, divide | verb
