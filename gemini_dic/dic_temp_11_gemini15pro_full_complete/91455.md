hygroscopicity:/ˌhaɪɡrəskəˈpɪsəti/ | n. | 吸湿性 | The ability of a substance to absorb moisture from the air. |  Table salt has high hygroscopicity. 食盐有很高的吸湿性。

| The hygroscopicity of the material makes it unsuitable for use in humid environments.  这种材料的吸湿性使其不适合在潮湿环境中使用。 |  absorbency, moisture absorption |  hydrophobicity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑγρός (hugrós, “wet, moist”) + -scopic (from scope, meaning "to view or examine") + -ity (noun suffix).  Essentially meaning "the tendency to observe or attract moisture."
