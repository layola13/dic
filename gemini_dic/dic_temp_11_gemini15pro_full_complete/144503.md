outtongues:/ˌaʊtˈtʌŋz/ | v. | 比…更雄辩；胜过；压倒 | to surpass in eloquence or persuasiveness; to outdo or overwhelm |  He outtongued his opponent in the debate and won the crowd's favor. 他在辩论中以雄辩胜过对手，赢得了观众的支持。 |  She outtongued him with her quick wit and sharp arguments. 她以机智和犀利的论证压倒了他。 | 近义词: outspeak, surpass, overcome, defeat | 反义词: lose to, be defeated by | 词性: 动词

outtongues:/ˌaʊtˈtʌŋz/ | n. | (罕见) 胜过；压倒 | (rare) the act of surpassing in eloquence or persuasiveness; an instance of outdoing or overwhelming |  His outtongues of his rivals secured his position as the leading orator.  他胜过对手的雄辩确保了他作为首席演说家的地位。(用法罕见) |  Her outtongues in the courtroom were legendary. 她在法庭上压倒对手的表现是传奇性的。(用法罕见) | 近义词: victory, triumph, mastery | 反义词: defeat, loss, failure | 词性: 名词
