immeshing:/ɪˈmɛʃɪŋ/
verb
啮合；使紧密结合
the process of becoming very involved with something, so that it is difficult to consider it separately
例句：The immeshing of gears ensures smooth transmission of power. 齿轮的啮合确保了动力的平稳传输。

例句：He found himself increasingly immeshed in the complexities of the legal system. 他发现自己越来越深陷于法律体系的复杂性之中。
近义词：engaging, involving, intertwining, integrating
反义词：disengaging, separating, disconnecting
词性：v.
