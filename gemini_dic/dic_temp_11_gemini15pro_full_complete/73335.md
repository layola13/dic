forefather:/ˈfɔːrˌfɑːðər/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌfɑːðə(r)/ (UK)| n. |祖先，祖宗|A person from whom one is descended; an ancestor, especially one more remote than a grandfather; a precursor.|Our forefathers fought for the freedom we enjoy today. 我们的祖先为我们今天享有的自由而战。|Synonyms: ancestor, progenitor, forebear|Antonyms: descendant, offspring|Noun
