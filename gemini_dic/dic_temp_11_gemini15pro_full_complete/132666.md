nondismissals: /ˌnɒndɪsˈmɪsəlz/ (British) /ˌnɑːndɪsˈmɪsəlz/ (American)| n.| 不解雇; 不开除; 保留职位 |The nondismissals of key personnel boosted morale.  | 例句：The company's nondismissals during the recession were surprising.  公司在经济衰退期间没有解雇员工，这令人惊讶。| 近义词：retentions, continuations | 反义词：dismissals, firings | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "dismissal" (from "dismiss," from Old French "desmettre," meaning "to send away").
