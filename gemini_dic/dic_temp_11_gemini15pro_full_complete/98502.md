instantaneous:/ˌɪnstənˈteɪniəs/| adj.|瞬间的，即刻的|occurring or done in an instant; immediate.|例句：The reaction was instantaneous. 反应是瞬间的。|近义词：immediate, instant, prompt, rapid|反义词：delayed, gradual, slow|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *instantaneus, from Latin instans (present participle of instare “to stand near, urge, press upon”).
