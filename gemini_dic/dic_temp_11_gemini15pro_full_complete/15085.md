baccara:/ˈbækərɑː/ (US), /ˈbækərɑː/ (UK)| n.|百家乐|A card game of Italian origin, popular in casinos. It is played between a "player" and a "banker," with each player and the banker receiving two or three cards. The goal is to get a hand as close to nine as possible.|例句：Baccara is a game of chance. 百家乐是一种靠运气的游戏。|例句：The casino offered several variations of baccara. 这家赌场提供了几种不同玩法的百家乐。|近义词：chemin de fer, punto banco|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
词源: From Italian *baccarà*, from *bacca* (“berry”), referring to the zero value of face cards and tens in the game.
