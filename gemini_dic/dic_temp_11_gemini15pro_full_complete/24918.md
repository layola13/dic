brunched:/brʌntʃt/
verb
早午餐过; 吃早午餐
have eaten brunch.  The act of eating a meal between breakfast and lunch.
We brunched at a local cafe. 我们在一家当地的咖啡馆吃了早午餐。
They brunched on pancakes and mimosas. 他们早午餐吃了煎饼和含羞草鸡尾酒。
近义词: had brunch, ate brunch
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) skipped brunch, didn't eat brunch
词性: verb (past tense of "brunch")
