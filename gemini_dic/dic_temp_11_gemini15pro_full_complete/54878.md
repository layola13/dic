displeasured:/dɪsˈplɛʒərd/| adj.|不高兴的，不满的| feeling or showing annoyance and disapproval| She gave him a displeased look. 她不满地看了他一眼。|近义词：annoyed, disgruntled, irritated, offended, cross|反义词：pleased, happy, delighted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + pleasured (from pleasure).
