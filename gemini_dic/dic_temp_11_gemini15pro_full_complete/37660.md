compactedly:/kəmˈpæktɪdli/
adverb
紧密地，简洁地
in a compact manner; densely.
The information was presented compactedly in the report.  这份报告里的信息被简洁地呈现出来。
The soil was compactedly packed. 土壤被压得很实。
Synonyms: densely, tightly, firmly, concisely, briefly
Antonyms: loosely, sparsely, diffusely, verbosely
词性: 副词
