boroughmongering:/ˈbʌrəˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ/
noun
操纵选区，买卖议席
The manipulation of borough elections by bribing or intimidating voters to secure the return of a particular candidate or party.
18th- and 19th-century Britain saw widespread boroughmongering, with wealthy individuals controlling the election of MPs. 18世纪和19世纪的英国，操纵选区现象十分普遍，富人控制着议员的选举。

The Reform Act of 1832 aimed to reduce boroughmongering and give more people the right to vote. 1832年的改革法案旨在减少操纵选区现象，并赋予更多人投票权。
synonym: political corruption, electoral manipulation, gerrymandering
antonym: fair elections, democratic process
