dunkable:/ˈdʌŋkəbl/| adj.|可灌篮的，易扣篮的|Something that is dunkable can be dunked (usually referring to a basketball).|例句：That basketball hoop is dunkable even for shorter players. 那个篮球框即使对矮个子球员来说也很容易灌篮。

例句：He baked a dunkable cookie, big and soft. 他烤了一个可以泡着吃的饼干，又大又软。|近义词：dipable, submersible|反义词: non-dunkable (non-standard)|词性: adjective
