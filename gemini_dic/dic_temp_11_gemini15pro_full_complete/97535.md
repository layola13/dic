ingrainedly:/ɪnˈɡreɪndɪdli/
adverb
根深蒂固地，牢固地
firmly fixed or established; deeply rooted
His distrust of authority was ingrainedly instilled in him from a young age. 他对权威的不信任从小就根深蒂固。
近义词: deeply, firmly, fixedly, inherently
反义词: superficially, shallowly
词性: 副词

词源:
From ingrained + -ly. Ingrained from in- + grained.  Grained from grain (n.) + -ed. Ultimately from Latin *granum.
