jujitsu: /dʒuːˈdʒɪtsuː/ | n. | 柔术 | A Japanese martial art and method of close combat using holds, throws, and grappling, without weapons, that maximizes the use of leverage against an opponent. | He's a black belt in jujitsu. 他是柔术黑带。|  Judo, aikido, wrestling | boxing, fencing | noun
