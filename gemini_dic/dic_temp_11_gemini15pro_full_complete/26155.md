busybodyness:/ˈbɪziˌbɒdinəs/| n. |爱管闲事, 多管闲事|The quality of being a busybody; meddling in other people's affairs.|Her busybodyness made her unpopular in the neighborhood. 她爱管闲事的性格让她在邻里不受欢迎。|近义词：meddlesomeness, intrusiveness, officiousness|反义词: indifference, apathy|词性:名词

Etymology: From busybody + -ness.  Busybody itself comes from busy + body, meaning someone who is overly occupied with the affairs of others.
