formers:/ˈfɔːrmərz/
n.
前者；形成者；塑造者
people or things that formed something, or the first-mentioned of two people or things.
The formers of the constitution were wise men. 宪法的制定者是智者。
synonyms: predecessors, creators, originators
antonyms: latter, successors
