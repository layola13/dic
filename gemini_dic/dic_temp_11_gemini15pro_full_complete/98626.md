instrumentals: /ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntlz/
noun
器乐曲；伴奏音乐
A piece of music consisting of musical instruments only, without vocals.
例句：The album features several catchy instrumentals. 这张专辑里有几首抓耳的器乐曲。

例句：He prefers listening to instrumentals while studying. 他学习时更喜欢听器乐曲。

近义词：instrumental music, background music
反义词：vocals, songs with lyrics
词性：n. (noun)
