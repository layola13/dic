intonate:/ˈɪntəneɪt/ (US), /ɪnˈtəʊneɪt/ (UK)| v. |吟诵；以某种语调说话；唱诵|to utter with a particular tone or modulation of voice, especially in singing or chanting. |The priest intoned the prayers.  神父吟诵着祈祷。

She intoned the words of the poem with great feeling. 她满怀深情地吟诵了这首诗。|近义词：chant, recite, sing, utter, vocalize|反义词：whisper, murmur|词性：verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin intonatus, past participle of intonare "to thunder, resound," from in- "in" + tonare "to thunder."
