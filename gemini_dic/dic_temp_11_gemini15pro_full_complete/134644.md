nonmalignancies: /ˌnɒnˌməˈlɪɡnənsiz/ | n. |非恶性肿瘤|Medical term referring to abnormal growths that are not cancerous and do not spread to other parts of the body.|Doctors will monitor the nonmalignancies to ensure they don't become cancerous. 医生会监测这些非恶性肿瘤，以确保它们不会癌变。| The biopsy revealed the growth was one of the nonmalignancies. 活检显示该增生物是良性的。 |Synonyms: benign tumors, benign growths|Antonyms: malignancies, cancers|Noun
