draintile:/ˈdreɪnˌtaɪl/ | n. |排水瓦，排水管|A pipe, typically made of clay or plastic, used to drain excess water from the soil.|The farmer installed draintiles in his field to prevent waterlogging.  农民在地里安装了排水瓦以防止涝灾。|Synonyms: drainage pipe, drain, agricultural drain|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "irrigation pipe" as it serves the opposite purpose) |noun
