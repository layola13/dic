lucent:/ˈluːsənt/ | adj. | 发光的，透明的，清晰的 | Shining, clear, or translucent. Allowing light to pass through diffusely. | The lucent moon illuminated the night sky.  发光的月亮照亮了夜空。| synonyms: luminous, bright, transparent, clear, pellucid | antonyms: opaque, dark, dim, dull | adjective

Etymology: From Latin "lucēns", present participle of "lucēre" (to shine).
