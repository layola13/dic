botnet:/ˈbɒtnɛt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtnɛt/ (US)| n. |僵尸网络|A network of private computers infected with malicious software and controlled as a group without the owners' knowledge, e.g., to send spam or launch attacks.|例句：The botnet was responsible for a massive denial-of-service attack. 这个僵尸网络对一次大规模的拒绝服务攻击负责。

例句：Criminals often use botnets to distribute malware. 犯罪分子经常使用僵尸网络来传播恶意软件。|近义词：zombie army, robot network|反义词: None|词性: noun
