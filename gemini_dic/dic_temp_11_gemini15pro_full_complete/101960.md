isobar:/ˈaɪsəʊbɑːr/ (US) /ˈaɪsəʊbɑː/ (UK)| n. |等压线; 同量异位素|A line on a map connecting points having the same atmospheric pressure at a given time or on average over a given period.  An atom having the same mass number as another but a different atomic number.|例句：Isobars are shown on weather maps. 等压线在地图上显示。

例句：Carbon-14 and nitrogen-14 are isobars. 碳-14和氮-14是同量异位素。|近义词：isopiestic (adj.)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *isos*  (equal) + *baros* (weight, pressure)
