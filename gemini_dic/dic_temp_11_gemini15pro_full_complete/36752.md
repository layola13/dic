colloquiums:/kəˈloʊkwiəmz/
n.
学术讨论会，研讨会
A meeting or conference for discussion of a particular subject, especially in an academic setting.
The department holds weekly colloquiums on various topics in physics.  该系每周都会举办关于物理学各个主题的学术讨论会。
The colloquiums attracted scholars from around the world. 这些研讨会吸引了来自世界各地的学者。
seminars, conferences, workshops, symposiums
lectures, speeches


Etymology:
From Latin colloquium "conversation, conference," from colloqui "to speak together, converse," from com- "together" (see com-) + loqui "to speak."
