drop-a-clanger:/ˌdrɒp ə ˈklæŋə(r)/ (UK) /ˌdrɑːp ə ˈklæŋər/ (US)| idiom | 出丑；说错话；犯尴尬的错误 | To make a blunder, especially a social gaffe or a tactless remark.  It often involves saying something inappropriate or embarrassing. |  He dropped a real clanger when he asked her about her ex-husband. 他问她前夫的情况时，真是说错了话。| Synonyms: make a gaffe, put one's foot in it, blunder, faux pas | Antonyms: behave impeccably, be tactful | idiom
