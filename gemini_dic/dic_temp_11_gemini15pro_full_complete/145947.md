overdiscriminating: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分歧视的，过度区分的 | Making excessively fine distinctions; discriminating too much.  Showing prejudice against a wider range of people or things than is usual or reasonable. |  Example 1:  The company was accused of being overdiscriminating in their hiring practices, rejecting candidates for minor flaws.  公司被指控在招聘过程中过度歧视，因为一些小瑕疵而拒绝候选人。

Example 2: He's become overdiscriminating about food, refusing to eat anything that isn't organic and locally sourced. 他对食物变得过于挑剔，拒绝吃任何非有机和非本地产的食物。 | 近义词: hypercritical, over-fastidious, too picky, excessively selective | 反义词: undiscriminating, uncritical, accepting | 词性: 形容词
