inholding:/ˈɪnhəʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɪnhəʊldɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
私有地;飞地;插花地
A piece of land entirely surrounded by land owned by another, especially a piece of public forest land retained by a private owner when the surrounding area became public.
The family maintained a small inholding of farmland within the national park.  这个家庭在国家公园内保留了一小块农田作为私有地。
synonym: enclave, exclave
antonym: surrounding area, outer territory
noun
