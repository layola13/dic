overbaiting:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪtɪŋ/
n.
过度投饵，过量投放诱饵
The act of using too much bait, especially in fishing or trapping, which can have negative consequences.
Overbaiting can scare fish away. 过度投饵会把鱼吓跑。
Overbaiting led to a decline in the local fish population. 过度投饵导致当地鱼类数量下降。
overfeeding, excessive baiting
underbaiting
noun
