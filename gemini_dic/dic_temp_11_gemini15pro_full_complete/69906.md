fertilizer:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/ | n. |肥料|A substance added to soil to enhance its fertility and promote plant growth. |The farmer used organic fertilizer on his crops. 农民在他的作物上使用了有机肥料。|
fertilizer:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/ | n. |化肥|A chemical or natural substance added to soil or land to increase its fertility. |Chemical fertilizers can harm the environment if used excessively. 化肥过量使用会破坏环境。|
fertilizer:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/ | n. |促进剂|Something that promotes growth or development.| Education is a fertilizer for the mind. 教育是心灵的养料。|

近义词: manure, compost, plant food, nutrient
反义词: pollutant

词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "fertilize,"  from Late Latin *fertilisare, from Latin fertilis "bearing in abundance, fertile, fruitful," from ferre "to bear, carry."
