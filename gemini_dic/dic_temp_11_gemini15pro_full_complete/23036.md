boulework:/ˈbuːlwɜːrk/
n.
壁垒，防御工事，保障
A defensive wall or rampart.  Something serving as a defense or safeguard.
例句：The castle's boulework protected the city from invaders. 城堡的壁垒保护城市免受侵略者的侵害。

例句：Free speech is a boulework against tyranny. 言论自由是抵御暴政的保障。
近义词：rampart, fortification, bulwark, bastion, defense, safeguard, protection
反义词：vulnerability, weakness, exposure
