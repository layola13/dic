cheongsam: /ˈtʃɔːŋsæm/ /ˈtʃiːɒŋsæm/ | n. | 旗袍 | A close-fitting dress with a high neck and a slit skirt, originating in China. |  She looked stunning in a red silk cheongsam. 她穿着一件红色的丝绸旗袍，看起来美极了。

The cheongsam is a symbol of Chinese elegance. 旗袍是中华优雅的象征。

近义词: qipao | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly Western dress) | 词性: noun
