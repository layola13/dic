noncensorious:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈsɔːriəs/
adjective
不加评判的；不吹毛求疵的；宽容的
Not critical or judgmental; tolerant and accepting.
She took a noncensorious approach to her friend's unconventional lifestyle. 她对朋友不落俗套的生活方式采取了不加评判的态度。
He listened with a noncensorious ear to her explanation. 他不带评判地倾听了她的解释。

近义词: tolerant, accepting, forgiving, understanding, open-minded, liberal, permissive
反义词: censorious, critical, judgmental, moralistic, puritanical, narrow-minded
词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + censorious, from censor + -ious.  Ultimately from Latin *censēre "to assess, judge."
