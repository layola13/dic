arboral:/ˈɑːrbərəl/ (US), /ɑːˈbɔːriəl/ (UK)| adj.|树栖的，树木的|Living in or among trees; of or relating to trees.|例句：Many arboreal animals, such as monkeys, have adapted to life in the trees. 许多树栖动物，例如猴子，已经适应了树上的生活。

例句：The arboreal habitat provides a rich source of food and shelter for various species.  树栖的栖息地为各种物种提供了丰富的食物和住所。

|近义词：arboreous, tree-dwelling, tree-living|反义词：terrestrial, ground-dwelling|词性：形容词
