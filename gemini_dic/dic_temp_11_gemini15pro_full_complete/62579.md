enervation:/ˌɛnərˈveɪʃən/ |n.| 衰弱；无力；疲惫 |The enervation caused by the extreme heat made it difficult to concentrate.  酷暑引起的疲惫使人难以集中精力。|例句：The long illness resulted in a state of enervation.  长期的疾病导致身体衰弱。

The enervation of the troops after the long march was evident. 长途跋涉后，部队的疲惫显而易见。|近义词：weakness, fatigue, exhaustion, debilitation |反义词：energy, vigor, strength| 词性: 名词


enervate:/ˈɛnərveɪt/ |vt.| 使衰弱；使无力；使失去活力 |The humid weather enervated everyone. 潮湿的天气使每个人都感到无力。|例句：The heat enervated her to the point of collapse.  高温让她虚弱得快要崩溃了。

The criticism from his boss completely enervated him. 老板的批评让他彻底失去了活力。|近义词：weaken, exhaust, drain, debilitate |反义词：invigorate, energize, strengthen| 词性: 动词
