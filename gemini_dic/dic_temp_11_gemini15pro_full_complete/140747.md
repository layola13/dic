omniscient:/ɒmˈnɪʃɪənt/ /ɑːmˈnɪʃɪənt/
adjective
无所不知的；全知的
Knowing everything.
例句：God is often described as omniscient and omnipotent. 上帝常被描述为无所不知、无所不能的。

例句：The narrator in the story is omniscient, revealing the thoughts and feelings of all the characters.  故事中的叙述者是无所不知的，揭示了所有角色的思想和感受。

近义词：all-knowing, all-seeing
反义词：ignorant, unknowing
词性: adjective
