dass:/dɑs/ | conj. | that | Subordinating conjunction introducing a subordinate clause.  It's the equivalent of "that" in English when introducing a statement or fact. |  Ich glaube, dass er recht hat. (I believe that he is right.) | Synonym:  (No direct synonym as a conjunction, meaning depends on context) | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a conjunction) | Conjunction


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle High German daz, from Old High German daz, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *þat.  Related to English "that," Dutch "dat," and Gothic "þata."


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Er sagt, dass er müde ist. = He says that he is tired.

Sie weiß, dass das stimmt. = She knows that that's true.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Ich sehe, dass es regnet. = I see that it is raining.

Wir hören, dass sie singt. = We hear that she is singing.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Er hat gesagt, dass er kommt. = He has said that he is coming.

Sie hat mir erzählt, dass sie dort war. = She has told me that she was there.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Ich habe bemerkt, dass er die ganze Zeit arbeitet. = I have noticed that he has been working the whole time.

Sie hat gesehen, dass die Kinder den ganzen Tag spielen. = She has seen that the children have been playing all day.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Er sagte, dass er müde war. = He said that he was tired.

Sie wusste, dass das stimmte. = She knew that that was true.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Ich sah, dass es regnete. = I saw that it was raining.

Wir hörten, dass sie sang. = We heard that she was singing.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Er hatte gesagt, dass er kommen würde. = He had said that he would come.

Sie hatte mir erzählt, dass sie dort gewesen war. = She had told me that she had been there.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Ich hatte bemerkt, dass er die ganze Zeit gearbeitet hatte. = I had noticed that he had been working the whole time.

Sie hatte gesehen, dass die Kinder den ganzen Tag gespielt hatten. = She had seen that the children had been playing all day.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Er wird sagen, dass er kommt. = He will say that he is coming.

Sie wird wissen, dass das stimmt. = She will know that that's true.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Ich werde sehen, dass es regnet. = I will see that it's raining. (This tense is less common with "dass" clauses)

Wir werden hören, dass sie singt. = We will hear that she is singing. (This tense is less common with "dass" clauses)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Er wird gesagt haben, dass er kommt. = He will have said that he is coming.

Sie wird gewusst haben, dass das stimmt. = She will have known that that is true.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "dass" clauses and sounds unnatural)


虚拟语气:
Ich wünschte, dass er hier wäre. = I wish that he were here.

Es wäre schön, dass sie käme. = It would be nice if she came.



被动语态:
Es wurde gesagt, dass er krank ist. = It was said that he is sick.

Es wird angenommen, dass sie recht hat. = It is assumed that she is right.


否定句:
Ich glaube nicht, dass er kommt. = I don't believe that he is coming.

Sie weiß nicht, dass das stimmt. = She doesn't know that that is true.


疑问句 (Dass is not used directly in questions, but within subordinate clauses in questions):
Glaubst du, dass er kommt? = Do you believe that he is coming?

Weißt du, dass das stimmt? = Do you know that that's true?



PHRASE->
(Dass doesn't form stand-alone phrases. It's a conjunction that connects clauses.)
