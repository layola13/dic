chymify:/ˈkɪmɪfaɪ/
verb
使成化学物质；用化学方法处理
To make something chemical or treat something by a chemical process.
The alchemist tried to chymify base metals into gold. 炼金术士试图将贱金属转化为黄金。
近义词:chemicalize, transmute
反义词:N/A
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Late Latin *chymificare, from chymicus (“chemical”) + -ficare (from Latin facere, “to make, do”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek χυμεία (khumeía, “alchemy”).
