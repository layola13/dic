hornswoggle:/hɔːrnˈswɒɡəl/| vt.|欺骗，哄骗|To deceive or trick someone, often in a playful or mischievous way.  It implies trickery and often involves elaborate schemes or stories.|He hornswoggled his little brother out of his candy. 他哄骗他弟弟把糖果给了他。|Synonyms: bamboozle, hoodwink, dupe, swindle, defraud|Antonyms: enlighten, inform, be honest|verb

hornswoggle:/hɔːrnˈswɒɡəl/| n.|欺骗，哄骗(较少用)|The act of deceiving or tricking someone.|The whole deal was a hornswoggle.  整个交易就是一场骗局。|Synonyms: trickery, deception, fraud, swindle|Antonyms: honesty, truthfulness|noun
