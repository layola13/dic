disannulment: /ˌdɪsəˈnʌlmənt/
n.
废除，取消（尤指婚姻或法律）
The act of officially declaring something, especially a marriage or law, to be invalid.
The Pope granted her a disannulment. 教皇批准了她婚姻的废除。
近义词： annulment, invalidation, revocation
反义词： validation, ratification, confirmation
词性：名词

词源：
Late Middle English: from Old French disannuller, based on Latin ad- ‘to’ + nullus ‘none’.
