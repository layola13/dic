misprovoking:/ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊkɪŋ/
adjective
令人恼火的；激怒人的
Tending or serving to provoke wrongly or amiss.  Causing anger or irritation through a misunderstanding or misinterpretation.
His misprovoking comment started an unnecessary argument. 他那句令人恼火的评论引发了一场不必要的争论。
Near Synonyms: irritating, annoying, vexing, exasperating, galling
Near Antonyms: soothing, calming, placating, mollifying
Word Origin:  'mis-' (wrongly) + 'provoking' (inciting anger or resentment).  From Middle English 'provoken', from Old French 'provoquer'.
