colloquy: /ˈkɒləkwi/ (US) /ˈkɒləkwi/ (UK)| n. |谈话，会谈，讨论| a formal conversation or discussion | Example: The professors engaged in a colloquy about the latest research findings. 教授们就最新的研究结果进行了会谈。

Example:  A colloquy between the two leaders helped ease tensions. 两位领导人之间的会谈缓和了紧张局势。

近义词: conversation, dialogue, discussion, conference, symposium

反义词: silence, quiet, monologue, argument (depending on the context)

词性: noun


Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French colloque, from Latin colloquium ‘conversation’, from colloqui ‘converse with’, from col- ‘together’ + loqui ‘speak’.
