demarcates:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪts/
verb
划定界限；区分
sets the boundaries or limits of something; distinguishes
The river demarcates the boundary between the two countries. 这条河流划定了两个国家之间的边界。
New signs clearly demarcate the pedestrian zone. 新的标志清楚地标明了步行区。
synonyms: separates, distinguishes, delimits, defines
antonyms: unites, merges, blends, combines
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *demarcare, from de- "off, away from" + marcare "to mark".
