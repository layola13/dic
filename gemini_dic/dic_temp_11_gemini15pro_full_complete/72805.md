foldaway:/ˈfəʊldəweɪ/ (UK) /ˈfoʊldəweɪ/ (US)| adj. n.|可折叠的；折叠式的；折叠床；折叠式家具|Something that is foldaway can be folded, usually so that it takes up less space.  It can be used as an adjective to describe something or a noun for the object itself.|The apartment has a foldaway bed for guests. 这间公寓有一张供客人使用的折叠床。|Synonyms: collapsible, folding, compact, portable|Antonyms: fixed, permanent, bulky, non-folding|adjective

foldaway:/ˈfəʊldəweɪ/ (UK) /ˈfoʊldəweɪ/ (US)| n.|折叠床；折叠式家具|A piece of furniture designed to be folded away for storage.|We bought a foldaway table for the patio. 我们买了一张可折叠的桌子放在露台上。|Synonyms: folding bed/table/chair, collapsible bed/table/chair|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form, but one might use terms like "standard furniture" or "non-folding furniture")|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "fold" + "away". "Fold" comes from Old English *fealdan, related to Old Frisian falda, Old Saxon faldan, Old High German faltan, and Gothic falþan. "Away" comes from Old English aweg.
