anito:/əˈniːtoʊ/ | n. | 祖先之灵; 神像; | A spirit of an ancestor, especially one worshipped as a guardian, or an image of such a spirit. |  The villagers prayed to the anito for a good harvest. 村民向祖灵祈祷丰收。|  She carved a small wooden anito to protect her home. 她雕刻了一个小的木制神像来保护她的家。| synonyms: deity, god, idol, spirit, divinity | antonyms: demon, devil | noun
