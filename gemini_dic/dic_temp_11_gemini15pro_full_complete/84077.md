half-hypnotized:/ˌhæf ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzd/
adjective
半催眠状态的，着迷的
Being in a state between fully awake and fully hypnotized; fascinated or captivated to the point of near-unconsciousness.
She sat staring at the fire, half-hypnotized by the flickering flames. 她坐在那里盯着火看，被闪烁的火焰迷住了。
The repetitive music had a half-hypnotizing effect on me.  重复的音乐对我产生了半催眠的效果。
Near synonyms: mesmerized, entranced, spellbound, captivated
Antonyms: alert, aware, conscious, attentive
词性: adjective
