decisional:/dɪˈsɪʒənəl/
adjective
决策的;关于作决定的
Relating to making decisions.
She plays a decisional role in the company. 她在公司扮演着决策的角色。
The CEO has the final decisional authority. 首席执行官拥有最终的决策权。
近义词: decisive, conclusive
反义词: indecisive, tentative
词性: adjective
