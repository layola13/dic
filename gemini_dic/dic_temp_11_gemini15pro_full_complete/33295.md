chubbedness:/ˈtʃʌbɪdnəs/| n. |圆胖，丰满|The state of being chubby; plumpness.  A childlike fullness or rosiness.|例句：The baby's chubbedness was adorable.  这个婴儿的胖乎乎的样子很可爱。

例句：She missed the chubbedness of her cheeks when she lost weight.  当她减肥后，她想念她脸颊上圆润的肉感。|近义词：plumpness, rotundity, chubbiness|反义词:thinness, slenderness|词性:noun

chubby:/ˈtʃʌbi/ | adj. |圆胖的，丰满的|Slightly fat in a pleasant way, typically of a child or young person.|例句：He had chubby cheeks and a bright smile.  他有着胖乎乎的脸颊和灿烂的笑容。

例句：The chubby toddler wobbled as he walked.  胖乎乎的小孩走路摇摇晃晃的。|近义词：plump, portly, stout|反义词:thin, slim, slender|词性:adjective
