cognizor:/ˈkɒɡnɪzər/ (US) /ˈkɑːɡnɪˌzər/| n. |认识者；认知者；感知者|One who cognizes; a thinker; a knower.  A being or system capable of cognition.|The philosopher argued that the cognizor shapes the world they perceive. 哲学家认为，认知者塑造了他们感知的世界。|Synonyms: knower, perceiver, thinker, observer, understander|Antonyms: ignoramus (although not a perfect antonym, it represents the opposite of a knower)|noun
