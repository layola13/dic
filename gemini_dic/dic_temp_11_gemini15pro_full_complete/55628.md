ditation:/dɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |  dictation,听写;口述;命令 | the action of saying words aloud to be typed, written down, or recorded. | Example: The students were given a spelling ditation. 学生们被要求进行一次拼写听写。

Example: He wrote a letter to his family at his father's ditation. 他按照父亲的口述给家人写了一封信。| 近义词：transcription, recital, decree | 反义词：N/A | 词性: noun
