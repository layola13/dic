endrudge: /ɪnˈdrʌdʒ/
verb
使辛劳工作；使做苦工
to make someone work hard and continuously at unpleasant or boring tasks
He endrudged himself with tedious tasks. 他让自己辛勤地做着繁琐的任务。
近义词: enslave, burden, overwork, tax
反义词: liberate, free, unburden
词性: verb

Etymology:
From en- + drudge.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be in" a particular state or condition.  "Drudge" comes from the Middle English "druggen," meaning "to do heavy, menial, or unpleasant work."
