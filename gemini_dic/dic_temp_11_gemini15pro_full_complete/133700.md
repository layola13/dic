nonfricatives:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɪkətɪvz/ | n. |非摩擦音|Consonant sounds produced without audible friction, such as nasals (m, n, ng) and approximants (l, r, w, j).|例句：The sounds /m/, /n/, and /l/ are nonfricatives.  /m/、/n/和/l/都是非摩擦音。

例句：Nonfricatives play an important role in the phonology of many languages. 非摩擦音在许多语言的音系中起着重要作用。|近义词：sonorants, approximates (sometimes) |反义词:fricatives|词性:noun
