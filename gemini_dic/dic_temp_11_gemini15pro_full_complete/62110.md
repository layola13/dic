encharge:/ɛnˈtʃɑːrdʒ/ (Often misspelled; the correct word is "in charge") | adj. | 负责，主管 |  Not a standard English word.  The correct form is "in charge," which means having control or responsibility over something or someone. |  (Incorrect usage) He was encharge of the project. (Correct usage) He was *in charge* of the project. 他负责这个项目。 | 近义词: responsible, controlling, managing | 反义词: subordinate, subservient | 词性: (incorrectly used as an adjective; "in charge" is a prepositional phrase)
