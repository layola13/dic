grimacier:/ɡrɪˈmeɪsiə(r)/
n.
扮鬼脸的人；做怪相的人
A person who makes grimaces; a person who distorts their face, typically to cause amusement or disgust.
He's a real grimacier, always pulling funny faces. 他真是个爱扮鬼脸的人，总是做各种滑稽的表情。
The grimacier entertained the children with his exaggerated expressions.  扮鬼脸的人用他夸张的表情逗孩子们开心。
synonyms: clown, buffoon, jester, mime, comic
antonyms: stoic, serious person

Etymology:
From French *grimacier*, from *grimace* "grimace," from *grimacher* "to make a grimace," of unknown origin.
