nonefficacious:/ˌnɒnˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
无效的，无用的
Not producing the desired effect; ineffective.
The nonefficacious treatment did nothing to alleviate his symptoms.  这种无效的治疗方法对缓解他的症状毫无作用。
The drug proved nonefficacious in treating the disease.  这种药物被证明对治疗这种疾病无效。
近义词:ineffective, useless, futile, unproductive
反义词:effective, efficacious, potent, useful
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + efficacious.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "efficacious" comes from Late Latin "efficax," meaning "effective." Ultimately from Latin "facere," "to do" or "to make."
