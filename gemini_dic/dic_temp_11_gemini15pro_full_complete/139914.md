odeon:/ˈəʊdiən/ (UK), /ˈoʊdiˌɑːn/ (US)| n.|音乐厅，剧院，电影院|A building or hall for concerts or performances, especially musical ones.  Originally, in ancient Greece, a roofed theater.|We went to see a play at the odeon.|Synonyms: theater, auditorium, concert hall, playhouse|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'open-air theatre')|noun
