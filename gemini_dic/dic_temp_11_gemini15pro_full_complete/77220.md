gastriloquous: /ˌɡæstrɪˈloʊkwəs/
adjective
用腹语术的；腹语的
Speaking from the belly; ventriloquial.  Relating to or characterized by ventriloquism.
The gastriloquous performer amazed the audience with his seemingly disembodied voice.  腹语表演者用他看似空灵的声音使观众惊叹。
The old man told stories in a gastriloquous voice, making the puppets seem alive. 老人用腹语讲述故事，使木偶看起来栩栩如生。

synonym: ventriloquial
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clear-voiced" or "articulate" in contrast)
词性: adjective
