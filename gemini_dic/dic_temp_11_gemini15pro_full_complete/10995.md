arboreous:/ɑːrˈbɔːriəs/ |adj.|树木的；栖于树木的；树木繁茂的|Relating to trees; living in trees; wooded.|例句：The monkey swung through the dense, arboreous canopy. 猴子在茂密的树冠中荡来荡去。|例句：Arboreous lichens are sensitive to air pollution.  树栖地衣对空气污染很敏感。|近义词：arboreal, woody, sylvan, forested|反义词：treeless, barren|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *arboreus*, from *arbor* "tree."
