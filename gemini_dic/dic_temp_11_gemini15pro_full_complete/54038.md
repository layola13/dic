discussing:/dɪˈskʌsɪŋ/
verb
讨论，谈论
the act of talking about something with another person or group of people
They were discussing the latest news. 他们在讨论最新的新闻。
近义词: debating, conversing, talking over
反义词: ignoring, avoiding
词性: present participle of discuss

discuss:/dɪˈskʌs/
verb
讨论，谈论
to talk about something with another person or group of people, especially in order to reach a decision
We need to discuss this further. 我们需要进一步讨论这个问题。
近义词: debate, converse, talk over
反义词: ignore, avoid
词性: verb

PHRASE->
discussing with
与…讨论
I am discussing with my colleagues about the new project. 我正在和我的同事讨论新项目。

discussing about (generally considered incorrect)
关于…的讨论 (通常被认为是不正确的)
Avoid using "discussing about." Use "discussing" followed directly by the topic.  避免使用 "discussing about"。直接在 "discussing" 后面加上主题。

under discussion
在讨论中
The proposal is currently under discussion. 该提案目前正在讨论中。

discuss something thoroughly
彻底讨论某事
We need to discuss this issue thoroughly before making a decision. 在做决定之前，我们需要彻底讨论这个问题。

discuss something further
进一步讨论某事
Let's discuss this further tomorrow.  我们明天再进一步讨论这个问题。


词源:
From Old French discusser, from Latin discutere "to dash to pieces, disperse, scatter," figuratively "investigate, examine," from dis- "apart" + quatere "to shake."
