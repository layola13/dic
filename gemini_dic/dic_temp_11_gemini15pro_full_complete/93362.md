illegitimacy:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪməsi/
n.
非法，不正当；私生
the state of being born to parents who are not married
例句：The stigma of illegitimacy has lessened in recent years.  私生子的耻辱近年来有所减轻。

例句：He was born into illegitimacy. 他是私生子。

近义词：bastardy, unlawfulness
反义词：legitimacy, legality
词性：noun
