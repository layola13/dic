heroology: /ˌhɪərəʊˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌhɪərəʊˈɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |英雄学；英雄人物研究|The study of heroes, their characteristics, and their impact on society.  It examines the qualities that define a hero, the different types of heroes that exist, and the role they play in culture and history.|He's writing a dissertation on heroology, focusing on the portrayal of female heroes in comic books. 他正在写一篇关于英雄学的论文，重点是漫画书中对女性英雄的刻画。|Near synonyms:  hero worship, study of heroism|Antonyms:  villainology (a less common and often humorous term)|Noun
