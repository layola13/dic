disobey:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/ /ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/ | v. |不服从，违抗|To refuse or fail to comply with rules, requests, or commands.|The soldier disobeyed a direct order.  这名士兵违抗了直接命令。

The child disobeyed his parents and stayed out late.  这个孩子不听父母的话，在外面待到很晚。


|近义词：defy, ignore, disregard, violate|反义词: obey, comply, conform|词性:动词
