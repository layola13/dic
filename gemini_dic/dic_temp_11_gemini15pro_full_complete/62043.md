encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/ | n. | 营地，宿营地 | A place where a group of people, especially soldiers or travellers, have set up tents or other temporary shelters. | The refugees set up an encampment near the border.  难民们在边境附近建立了一个营地。 | synonyms: camp, campsite, bivouac | antonyms: settlement, town, city | noun

encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/ | n. | 扎营，露营（的行为）| The act of setting up camp. | The encampment of the troops lasted for several weeks. 部队的扎营持续了几个星期。| synonyms: camping, bivouacking | antonyms: departure, moving on | noun
