half-fascinated:/ˌhɑːfˈfæsɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
半着迷的，有点儿感兴趣的
Partly fascinated; having a mixed feeling of fascination and disinterest or repulsion.
She was half-fascinated, half-repelled by the gruesome details of the crime. 她对这起犯罪的可怕细节既感到着迷，又感到反感。
Near synonyms: intrigued, captivated, curious, interested
Near antonyms: bored, disinterested, apathetic, indifferent
词性: 形容词
