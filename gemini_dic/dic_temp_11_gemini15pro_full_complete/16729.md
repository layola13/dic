barsky:/ˈbɑːrski/| adj.|巴斯基的；与巴斯基算法相关的|Relating to the Barsky algorithm, a line clipping algorithm used in computer graphics.  This algorithm is known for its efficiency and simplicity. It determines which portions of a line segment are visible within a rectangular clipping window.|Example: The Barsky line clipping algorithm is more efficient than the Cohen-Sutherland algorithm. 巴斯基线段裁剪算法比科恩-萨瑟兰算法更高效。|Example:  We used a Barsky-based method for rendering the scene. 我们使用基于巴斯基的方法来渲染场景。 |近义词: (No direct synonyms, could list other clipping algorithms like "Cohen-Sutherland", "Liang-Barsky")|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
