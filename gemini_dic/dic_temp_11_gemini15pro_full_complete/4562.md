alchemy: /ˈælkɪmi/ | n. | 炼金术; 神秘的变化; | A medieval chemical science and speculative philosophy aiming to transform base metals into gold, discover a universal cure for disease, and discover a means of indefinitely prolonging life.  A seemingly magical process of transformation, creation, or combination. |  The alchemist attempted to turn lead into gold. 炼金术士试图将铅变成金子。  The band's music is an alchemy of jazz, rock, and blues.  这个乐队的音乐是爵士乐、摇滚乐和蓝调的融合。 | synonyms: magic, sorcery, witchcraft, wizardry | antonyms: science, chemistry | noun
