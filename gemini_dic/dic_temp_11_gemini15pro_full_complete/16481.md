bargaining:/ˈbɑːrɡənɪŋ/| n.|讨价还价，谈判，讲价|The act of discussing prices, terms, or conditions in order to reach an agreement.|They spent hours bargaining with the street vendor. 他们花了几个小时与街头小贩讨价还价。|synonyms: negotiation, haggling, dealing|antonyms: accepting, conceding|noun

bargaining:/ˈbɑːrɡənɪŋ/| adj.|讨价还价的，谈判的|Relating to or involving the act of bargaining.|Bargaining power is crucial in business negotiations.  讨价还价的能力在商业谈判中至关重要。|synonyms: negotiating, haggling|antonyms: non-negotiable, fixed|adjective
