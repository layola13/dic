outrushed:/ˌaʊtˈrʌʃt/| v.|涌出，奔出|A wave of emotion outrushed from her heart. 一股情感从她心中涌出。|The children outrushed from the school gates at the end of the day.  放学时，孩子们从校门口涌出。|近义词：gushed, surged, streamed|反义词：trickled, seeped|词性:动词
词源: out- (prefix meaning "outward, beyond") + rush (verb meaning "to move with great speed")
