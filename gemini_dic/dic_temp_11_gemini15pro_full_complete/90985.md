hurtfully:/ˈhɜːrtfəli/ /ˈhɜːrtfʊli/| adv.| 伤害地，伤人地 |In a way that causes emotional pain or offense. | She spoke hurtfully about her ex-boyfriend. 她伤人地谈论她的前男友。|

He looked at her hurtfully when she refused to dance with him. 当她拒绝和他跳舞时，他受伤地看着她。| 近义词: cruelly, unkindly, offensively, maliciously | 反义词: kindly, gently, compassionately | 词性: 副词

词源: From 'hurtful' + '-ly'. 'Hurtful' comes from 'hurt' (from Old French 'hurter', meaning 'to knock against, strike, collide with'), with the suffix '-ful' indicating 'full of' or 'characterized by'. The suffix '-ly' transforms adjectives into adverbs.
