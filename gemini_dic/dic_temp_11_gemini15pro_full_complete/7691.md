anoints:/əˈnɔɪnts/ |v. |涂油，施以圣油；任命，指定|ceremonially applies oil to someone, often as part of a religious ritual; designates or appoints someone to a position|The priest anointed the new king.  牧师为新国王涂油。|Synonyms: consecrates, blesses, ordains, appoints, designates|Antonyms: desecrates, curses|verb

anoints:/əˈnɔɪnts/ |v. |涂油；任命|applies oil to (someone or something) as part of a religious ceremony; designates (someone) to a position or office|The bishop anointed her as a deacon. 主教任命她为执事。|Synonyms: consecrates, blesses, sanctifies (religious context); appoints, designates, nominates (secular context)|Antonyms:  desecrates, profanes (religious context); dismisses, removes (secular context)|verb
