intoxicatedly: /ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 醉醺醺地，陶醉地 | In a manner characterized by intoxication; in a drunken or euphoric way.  | He laughed intoxicatedly, clearly enjoying the party. 他醉醺醺地笑着，显然很享受派对。| She danced intoxicatedly to the music, lost in the rhythm. 她陶醉地在音乐中跳舞，沉浸在节奏里。 | 近义词： drunkenly, tipsily, blissfully | 反义词： soberly, clearly | 词性: 副词

词源: From intoxicated + -ly.  Intoxicated comes from Late Latin intoxicatus, past participle of intoxicare "to poison," from Latin in- "in" + toxicum "poison."
