citator:/ˈsaɪteɪtər/| n.|引证者，引注器|A citator is a tool used in legal research to update legal authorities by listing the subsequent cases and statutes that have cited them. It helps determine if a case or statute is still good law and how it has been interpreted or applied by later courts.|She used a citator to check the current status of the Supreme Court decision.|近义词:legal research tool, case finder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
