ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/ | adj. | 双手的；灵巧的；左右皆能的 | Able to use both hands equally well; very skillful; deceitful |  She's an ambidextrous tennis player, equally comfortable with a forehand or backhand. 她是一位双手皆能的网球运动员，正反手都运用自如。 |  versatile, adept, skillful | clumsy, awkward | adjective

ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/ | adj. | 双手的；灵巧的；左右皆能的 |  My brother is ambidextrous – he can write with either hand. 我弟弟双手都灵巧——他可以用任何一只手写字。 | versatile, adept, skillful | clumsy, awkward | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambidexter, from ambi- "both" + dexter "right-handed, skillful".  The original meaning was "double-dealing, deceitful," as someone who could "shake hands with both sides."
