hydrorhiza:/ˌhaɪdrəˈraɪzə/ | n. | 水螅根 |The base or rootlike structure by which a hydroid, such as a hydra, attaches to a substrate. | The hydrorhiza of this particular hydra species is remarkably strong, allowing it to withstand strong currents.  这种特殊水螅的水螅根非常强壮，使其能够承受强电流。| synonyms: rootlike structure, holdfast | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "shoot" in the context of plant growth) | noun
