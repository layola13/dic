词源：From "liberal" + "-ise". "Liberal" comes from Latin "liberalis" meaning "of freedom, pertaining to a freeman, worthy of a free man, noble, generous," from "liber" meaning "free."  "-ise" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or render" or "to become."
