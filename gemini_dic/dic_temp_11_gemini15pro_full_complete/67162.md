expositor:/ɪkˈspɑːzɪtər/
n.
讲解者，阐释者
A person who explains or interprets something, especially a text or doctrine.
The expositor skillfully explained the complex theories to the audience.  这位讲解员巧妙地向观众解释了复杂的理论。
explainer, interpreter, commentator
obscurer, confuser
noun
