conjectured: /kənˈdʒɛktʃərd/ | v. | 推测的，猜想的 |  Past participle of "conjecture," meaning something that has been guessed or inferred, but not proven. | It was conjectured that the painting was a forgery. 人们推测这幅画是赝品。| synonyms: supposed, assumed, presumed, hypothesized, theorized | antonyms: proven, demonstrated, confirmed | verb (past participle)

conjecture: /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | n. v. | 推测，猜想 | An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information.  To form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information. |  He conjectured that the company would soon go bankrupt. 他推测这家公司很快就会破产。| synonyms: guess, speculation, supposition, hypothesis, theory | antonyms: fact, proof, certainty | noun, verb
