nonpalatalization:/ˌnɑːnpælətəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非颚化 | The absence of palatalization; the process by which a sound does not become palatalized.  It refers to the articulation of a consonant without moving the tongue towards the hard palate.  |  The nonpalatalization of /k/ before front vowels is a characteristic of some dialects.  /k/在某些方言中前元音前不顎化是一个特征。| non-palatalisation (British English) | palatalization | noun
nonpalatalized:/ˌnɑːnpælətəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非颚化的 | Not palatalized; articulated without raising the tongue towards the hard palate. | The /k/ in "cat" is nonpalatalized. “cat”中的/k/是非颚化的。| non-palatalised (British English) | palatalized | adjective
