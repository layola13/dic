artefacts:/ˈɑːrtɪfækts/ (US) /ˈɑːtəˌfækts/ (UK)| n. |人工制品，手工艺品，史前古器物|An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest.|例句：The museum displays many ancient Egyptian artefacts.  博物馆展出许多古埃及文物。

例句：The archaeologists discovered some interesting artefacts at the dig site. 考古学家在挖掘现场发现了一些有趣的文物。|近义词：objects, relics, artifacts (alternative spelling), antiquities|反义词:natural objects
