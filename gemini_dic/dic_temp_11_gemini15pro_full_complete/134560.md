nonlitigations:/ˌnɒnlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃənz/
n.
非诉讼案件/事务
matters or disputes resolved outside of the court system, such as through negotiation, mediation, or arbitration.
The firm specializes in nonlitigations such as contract drafting and real estate transactions.  这家公司专门从事合同起草和房地产交易等非诉讼业务。
The lawyer handled several complex nonlitigations last month.  这位律师上个月处理了几起复杂的非诉讼案件。
synonyms: alternative dispute resolution, out-of-court settlements
antonyms: litigations, lawsuits
noun
