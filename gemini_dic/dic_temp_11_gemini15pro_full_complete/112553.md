例句：Her lovelessness made it difficult to form close relationships. 她的冷漠使她难以建立亲密关系。|近义词：coldness, indifference, apathy, heartlessness|反义词:love, affection, warmth, compassion|词性:noun

词源: love + -less + -ness.  From "love" (n.), meaning "an intense feeling of deep affection," plus the suffix "-less," indicating lack or absence of something, and the suffix "-ness," which forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
