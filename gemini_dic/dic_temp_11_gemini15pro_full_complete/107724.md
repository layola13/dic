laobach:/ˈlaʊˌbaʊtʃ/ (Approximation, no standard pronunciation)| n.| 老板；工头 |A transliteration of the Chinese word 老板 (lǎobǎn), meaning "boss" or "shop owner." It refers to a person in a position of authority, typically a business owner or manager, often used informally. | He asked the laobach for a raise. 他向老板要求加薪。|Synonyms: boss, manager, owner, proprietor |Antonyms: employee, worker, subordinate| noun
