felts:/fɛlts/
n.
毛毡; 毡制品; 毡状物
a thick, soft material made from matted wool or hair
The table was covered with green felt. 桌子上铺着绿色的毛毡。

近义词:baize, fabric, material
反义词:(No direct antonym for a material)
词性:noun


felts:/fɛlts/
v.
(feel的过去式和过去分词) 感觉; 摸; 触碰
experienced a particular feeling or emotion; touched
He felt a sharp pain in his chest. 他感到胸口一阵剧痛。

近义词:sensed, perceived, experienced
反义词:ignored, disregarded
词性:verb


词源:
From Middle English felt, from Old English felt, from Proto-Germanic *feltą (“felt”), from Proto-Indo-European *pēl- (“skin, hide; cloth; to spread, stretch”).
