divisor:/dɪˈvaɪzər/ | n. |除数|A number by which another number is divided.|Example 1: In the equation 10 / 2 = 5, 2 is the divisor.  在等式 10 / 2 = 5 中，2 是除数。

Example 2: The divisor of 20 can be 1, 2, 4, 5, 10, and 20. 20的除数可以是1、2、4、5、10和20。|近义词:factor|反义词:multiple, dividend|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *divysour*, from Anglo-Norman *devisour*, and Old French *deviseor*, from *deviser* (“to divide”), and ultimately from Latin *dīvīdere* (“to divide”).
