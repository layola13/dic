hommock:/ˈhɑːmək/ (US), /ˈhɒmək/ (UK)| n.|吊床; 小丘, 土墩|A hanging bed or couch made of canvas, rope, or other material, suspended between two points.|The explorers rested in their hommocks slung between two palm trees. 探险者们在吊在两棵棕榈树之间的吊床上休息。|近义词：hammock (Note: "hommock" is a less common variant)|反义词: bed, cot|noun
