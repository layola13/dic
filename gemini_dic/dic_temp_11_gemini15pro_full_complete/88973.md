holier-than-thou:/ˈhəʊliə(r) ðæn ˈðaʊ/ /ˈhoʊliər ðæn ˈðaʊ/
adjective
假装圣洁的，道貌岸然的
Characterized by an attitude of moral superiority; sanctimonious.
例句：He had a holier-than-thou attitude that made him unpopular. 他有一种道貌岸然的态度，使他不受欢迎。

例句：She delivered a holier-than-thou lecture on the evils of drinking. 她做了一场关于饮酒害处的道貌岸然的演讲。

近义词：sanctimonious, self-righteous, pious, moralistic
反义词：humble, modest, unpretentious
词性：adjective
