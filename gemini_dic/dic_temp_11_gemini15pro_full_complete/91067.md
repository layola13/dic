hut-like:/ˈhʌtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像小屋的，简陋的
Resembling a hut; small and simple.
The hermit lived in a hut-like dwelling in the woods.  这位隐士住在森林里一个像小屋一样的住所。
The refugees built hut-like shelters from salvaged materials.  难民们用回收的材料搭建了像小屋一样的住所。
Synonyms:  cabin-like, shack-like, primitive, rudimentary
Antonyms:  palatial, luxurious, grand, elaborate
词性: 形容词
