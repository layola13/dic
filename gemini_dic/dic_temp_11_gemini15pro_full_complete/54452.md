disinheriting:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtɪŋ/
verb
剥夺继承权
The act of preventing someone from inheriting property or money that they would normally be entitled to.
He is disinheriting his son for marrying against his wishes. 他因为儿子违背他的意愿结婚而剥夺了他的继承权。
disown, exclude
include, bequeath
