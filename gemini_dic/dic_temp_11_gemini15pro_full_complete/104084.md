jump-bail:/ˌdʒʌmp ˈbeɪl/
verb phrase
跳保；弃保潜逃
To fail to appear in court after being released on bail.  The act of absconding and forfeiting bail.
He jumped bail and fled the country. 他跳保逃到了国外。
abscond, flee, skip bail, forfeit bail
appear in court, surrender
