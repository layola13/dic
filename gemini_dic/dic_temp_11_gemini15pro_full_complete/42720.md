The workshop focused on counterviolence strategies, such as conflict resolution and de-escalation techniques. 研讨会重点讨论了反暴力策略，如冲突解决和降级技巧。 | 近义词: nonviolent resistance, peaceful resistance, civil disobedience | 反义词: violence, aggression | 词性: 名词
