drippily:/ˈdrɪpɪli/ | adv. |滴水地，湿漉漉地 | in a way that liquid drips steadily |  The faucet was leaking, and water dripped drippily onto the floor. 水龙头漏水了，水滴嗒滴嗒地滴在地板上。| The rain drippily fell from the eaves. 雨水滴滴答答地从屋檐落下。 | 近义词:  drop by drop, steadily dripping | 反义词: dryly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From drip (v.) + -ily (adv. suffix).  "Drip" comes from Old Norse *dryppa.  The suffix "-ily" forms adverbs from adjectives ending in "-y".
