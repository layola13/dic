dualer:/ˈduːələr/ | n. | 对决者，决斗者 |  Someone who fights a duel. A person involved in a contest or competition, especially one involving two opponents. | He challenged his rival to a duel, proclaiming himself the superior dualer. 他向对手提出决斗，宣称自己是更优秀的决斗者。| 近义词：duelist, contestant, competitor, rival, opponent | 反义词: ally, teammate | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Old French word "duel" (combat between two persons), ultimately from the Latin "duellum" (war, combat), related to "duo" (two).
