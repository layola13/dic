industrialization:/ɪnˌdʌstriələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |工业化|The process by which an economy is transformed from primarily agricultural to one based on the manufacturing of goods.  This involves the development of industries and factories, often accompanied by social and economic changes.| The Industrial Revolution marked a period of rapid industrialization in Europe. 欧洲的工业革命标志着欧洲快速工业化的时期。| industrialisation (British English), development, modernization | deindustrialization, stagnation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From industrialize + -ation.  Industrialize comes from industry + -ize.  Industry ultimately derives from the Latin *industria* meaning "diligence, activity."
