capitate:/ˈkæpɪteɪt/ | adj. | 头状的，有头状体的，人均拨款的 | Having a knob-like head, especially of a bone; relating to a method of payment for healthcare services in which a provider receives a fixed amount per patient. | The capitate bone of the wrist articulates with the third metacarpal. 腕骨的头状骨与第三掌骨连接。 |  The doctor is paid on a capitate basis. 医生按人头收费。 | 近义词：headed, knobbed, globular | 反义词：acaudal | 词性: 形容词
