neglects:/nɪˈɡlɛkts/| v.| 忽视，忽略，疏忽，不予重视| He neglects his studies. 他忽视学业。 |近义词：ignore, disregard, overlook, omit|反义词: cherish, nurture, attend to|verb (third-person singular simple present neglects, present participle neglecting, simple past and past participle neglected)

Etymology: From Old French *neglecte (modern French négliger), from Latin neglegere, from neg- (not) + legere (to gather, choose, read).
