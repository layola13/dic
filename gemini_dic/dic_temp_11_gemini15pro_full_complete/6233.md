amniocenteses:/ˌæmniəʊsɛnˈtiːsiːz/
|n.|羊膜穿刺术|A medical procedure in which a small amount of amniotic fluid is removed from the amniotic sac surrounding a developing fetus for testing. This procedure is used to diagnose chromosomal abnormalities and certain genetic disorders.|Amniocenteses is typically performed between 15 and 20 weeks of pregnancy. 羊膜穿刺术通常在怀孕15到20周之间进行。

The doctor recommended amniocenteses to determine the health of the fetus. 医生建议进行羊膜穿刺术以确定胎儿的健康状况。
|近义词: amniotic fluid test|反义词: None|词性: Noun
