besetments:/bɪˈsɛtmənts/
n.
困境，围攻，困扰
troubles or difficulties that surround someone or something
The company was beset by financial besetments.  这家公司被财政困境所困扰。
The small village was beset by bandits. 这个小村庄被土匪包围。

近义词: troubles, difficulties, problems, afflictions, tribulations, misfortunes, hardships, worries, anxieties, pressures
反义词: advantages, benefits, blessings, fortunes

词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源: From the verb "beset," which comes from the Old English word "besettan," meaning "to surround" or "to attack."
