interdetermination:/ˌɪntərdiˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |相互决定，互相决定|The concept of interdetermination emphasizes the reciprocal relationship between different factors, where each influences and is influenced by the others. 相互决定的概念强调不同因素之间的相互关系，其中每个因素都影响并受其他因素的影响。|The interdetermination of supply and demand determines market prices. 供求关系的相互作用决定了市场价格。| The interdetermination of social and economic factors shaped the development of the city. 社会和经济因素的相互作用塑造了城市的发展。|Synonyms: interaction, interdependence, reciprocity, interplay|Antonyms: independence, isolation|noun
