nematocalyx:/ˌnɛməˈtɒkəlɪks/
n.
线虫萼；(尤指)线虫囊
A nematocalyx is the nematocyst-bearing portion of the outer surface of a sea anemone or other cnidarian.
The nematocalyx of the sea anemone was brightly colored.  海葵的线虫萼颜色鲜艳。

The biologist studied the nematocalyx of the anemone under a microscope. 生物学家在显微镜下研究了海葵的线虫囊。
近义词:cnidocyst (刺细胞)
反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词)
词性:noun (名词)
