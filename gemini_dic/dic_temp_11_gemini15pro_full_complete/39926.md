constricted:/kənˈstrɪktɪd/
adjective
收缩的，狭窄的，受限制的
drawn together or tightened; made narrower, especially by encircling pressure.
His throat felt constricted, making it hard to breathe. 他的喉咙感觉很紧，呼吸困难。
近义词：narrowed, tightened, compressed, contracted
反义词：expanded, dilated, widened, enlarged
词性: adjective

constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/
verb
收缩，压缩，使狭窄
make narrower, especially by encircling pressure.
Fear constricted his chest.  恐惧使他的胸口发紧。
近义词：compress, contract, tighten, narrow
反义词：expand, dilate, widen, enlarge
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *constrictus*, past participle of *constringere* ("to draw together tightly"), from *com-* ("together") + *stringere* ("to draw tight").
