administrator:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/ | n. | 管理员，管理者 | A person who manages or directs affairs; a person legally appointed to manage the estate of another. | The school administrator announced new policies. 学校管理员宣布了新的政策。| synonyms: manager, director, executive, superintendent, supervisor | antonyms: subordinate, employee | noun

administrator:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/ | n. |  管理人员 |  Someone who manages a business, organization, or property. | The hospital administrators made a difficult decision. 医院管理人员做出了一个艰难的决定。 | synonyms: executive, director, manager, official | antonyms: employee, worker, subordinate | noun
