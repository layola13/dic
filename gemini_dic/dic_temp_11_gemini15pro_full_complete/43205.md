crabgrass:/ˈkræbɡræs/| n. |马唐，蟋蟀草|A weedy, fast-growing grass with creeping stems that root at the nodes, often considered a nuisance in lawns. |例句：The crabgrass is taking over the lawn. 马唐正在侵占草坪。|例句：We need to get rid of the crabgrass in the garden. 我们需要清除花园里的马唐。|近义词：weed, couchgrass|反义词: desirable grass, turfgrass | noun
