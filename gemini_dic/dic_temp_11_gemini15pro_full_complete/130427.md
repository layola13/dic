nonabjuratory:/ˌnɒnˌæbdʒʊˈreɪtəri/
adjective
不放弃的；不发誓放弃的
Not involving or relating to abjuration; refusing to abjure or renounce.
The king demanded a nonabjuratory oath of loyalty from his subjects. 国王要求臣民宣誓效忠，并且不得反悔。
Near Synonyms: unrenouncing, unretracting, steadfast, unwavering
Antonyms: abjuratory, renouncing, retracting
词性: adjective
