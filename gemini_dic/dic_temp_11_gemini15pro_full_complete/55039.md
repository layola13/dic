disquietive:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪv/
adjective
令人不安的，令人担忧的
Causing anxiety or uneasiness; disturbing.
The disquietive news of the approaching storm made everyone nervous.  令人不安的暴风雨即将来临的消息使每个人都感到紧张。
The silence after the explosion was disquietive. 爆炸后的寂静令人不安。
近义词: unsettling, disturbing, worrying, troubling, alarming, ominous
反义词: calming, soothing, reassuring, comforting, peaceful
词性: adjective

词源:
From disquiet (v.) + -ive.  Disquiet comes from Old French *desquiete, from des- "away from" + quiet.
