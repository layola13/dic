gung-ho:/ˌɡʌŋˈhoʊ/| adj.|热情洋溢的；极其热衷的；全力以赴的|extremely enthusiastic and eager to do something.|例句：The new recruits were gung-ho and ready for action. 新兵们热情高涨，随时准备行动。

例句：She's really gung-ho about her new job. 她对她的新工作充满热情。|近义词：enthusiastic, eager, keen, zealous, avid, ardent, passionate|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, half-hearted|词性:形容词
