mis-analyzing:/ˌmɪsˈænəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
错误分析
Analyzing something incorrectly or reaching a flawed conclusion based on faulty interpretation.
He was mis-analyzing the data, leading to incorrect conclusions. 他错误地分析了数据，导致结论不正确。
The report mis-analyzing the market trends resulted in poor investment decisions.  这份错误分析市场趋势的报告导致了糟糕的投资决策。
