atlantes:/ætˈlæntiːz/
noun
阿特拉斯神像；男像柱
Male figures used as supporting columns, especially in classical architecture.
The caryatids and atlantes of Greek architecture are examples of anthropomorphic columns.  希腊建筑中的女像柱和男像柱是拟人化柱子的例子。

近义词: telamones
反义词: caryatids
词性: noun
