garbled:/ˈɡɑːrbl̩d/ /ˈɡɑːrbəld/| adj.|混乱的，含糊不清的，失真的|mixed up or distorted, usually in such a way as to be unclear|The message was garbled and difficult to understand. 信息混乱且难以理解。|The radio signal was garbled, making it hard to hear what was being said. 无线电信号失真，难以听清所说的内容。|近义词：distorted, jumbled, scrambled, confused, unclear, muddled, incoherent|反义词：clear, distinct, coherent, intelligible|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From *garble, alteration of *garbell, from Old French *garbeler "to sift," from Arabic *ḡarbala "sieve." Originally referred to the removal of impurities from spices, later extended to the distortion of meaning.
