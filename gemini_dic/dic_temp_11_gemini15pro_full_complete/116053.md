martially:/ˈmɑːrʃəli/ | adv. | 以军事方式地，好战地 | In a military manner; aggressively. |  The country was martially ruled after the coup. 政变后，这个国家处于军事统治之下。 |  The protestors were martially dispersed by the police. 抗议者被警察以武力驱散。 | 近义词: militarily, воинственно (Russian), warring, combatively | 反义词: peacefully, pacifistically, diplomatically | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *martialiter, from Latin martialis (“of Mars, warlike”), from Mars (“Mars, the Roman god of war”).
