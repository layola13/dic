confederating:/kənˈfɛdəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
联合，结盟
The act of forming a confederation or alliance, especially of states or nations.
Several tribes were confederating against their common enemy.  几个部落联合起来对抗共同的敌人。
Synonyms:  uniting, allying, joining forces, coalescing
Antonyms:  separating, dividing, disuniting
词性: verb (动词)


confederate:/kənˈfɛdərət/
adjective
同盟的，联邦的
Belonging to a confederation or alliance.
The confederate states signed a treaty.  同盟国签署了一项条约。
Synonyms: allied, federated, united
Antonyms: independent, separate, disunited
词性: adjective (形容词)


confederate:/kənˈfɛdərət/
noun
同盟者，盟友
A person, state, or nation that belongs to a confederation or alliance.
He was a confederate in the plot.  他是该阴谋的同谋。
Synonyms: ally, partner, associate
Antonyms: enemy, opponent, adversary
词性: noun (名词)
