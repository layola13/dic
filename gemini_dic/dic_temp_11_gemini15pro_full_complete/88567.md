hitchhiking:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪkɪŋ/| n.|搭便车，免费搭车旅行|The act of traveling by getting free rides in passing vehicles.|He went hitchhiking across Europe last summer. 他去年夏天搭便车游遍了欧洲.|近义词：thumbing, backpacking|反义词：driving (自己开车), flying (坐飞机)|名词

hitchhike:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/| v.|搭便车，免费搭车旅行|To travel by getting free rides in passing vehicles.|She hitchhiked to California. 她搭便车去了加利福尼亚.|近义词：thumb, bum a ride|反义词：drive (自己开车), fly (坐飞机)|动词
