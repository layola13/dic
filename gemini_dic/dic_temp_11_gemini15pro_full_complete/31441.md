charity: /ˈtʃærɪti/ (US) /ˈtʃærəti/ (UK)| n.|慈善; 施舍; 慈善团体; 仁慈; 宽容|The act of giving money, food, or other kinds of help to people who are poor, sick, etc.; or money, food, or other help that is given to people.|例句：She gave money to charity. 她捐钱给慈善机构。|例句：The hospital is funded by charity. 这家医院是由慈善机构资助的。|近义词：benevolence, philanthropy, altruism|反义词：selfishness, greed, cruelty|词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French "charité"  (kindness, generosity), from Latin "caritas" (dearness, high price, esteem, affection), from "carus" (dear).
