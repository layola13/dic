deal-out:/diːl aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 分发，分配；发牌 | To distribute something, often cards, to a group of people. | The dealer dealt out five cards to each player. 荷官给每个玩家发了五张牌。 |  He dealt out the candy to the children. 他把糖果分发给孩子们。 | distribute, dispense, hand out | collect, gather | verb

deal-out:/diːl aʊt/ | noun | 分配；发牌 | The act of distributing something, especially cards. |  The deal-out was quick and efficient. 发牌迅速而有效率。| The deal-out of food parcels helped the refugees. 食品包裹的分发帮助了难民。 | distribution, allocation | collection | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "deal" (v.), from Old English *dǣlan "to divide, distribute," and "out" (adv.), from Old English ūt "outward, outside."
