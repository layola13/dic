filled-in:/fɪldˈɪn/ | adj. | 填写的，填满的 | Filled in refers to something that has had blank spaces completed with information.  It can also mean something that has been substituted for something else. | The application form was filled in correctly. 申请表已正确填写。

The substitute teacher filled in for the regular teacher. 代课老师代替了正式老师。 | completed, full, occupied | blank, empty | adjective
