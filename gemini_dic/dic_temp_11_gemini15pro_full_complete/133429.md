ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + farcical + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Farcical" comes from the Italian word "farsa" meaning "stuffing" (originally referring to the stuffing of liturgical plays with jokes and pranks).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
