layest:/ˈleɪɪst/| v.|你放置，你躺下 (古英语，第二人称单数)|This word is an archaic second-person singular present form of the verb "lay." It means "you lay" or "you place" something down.  It's rarely used in modern English. |Thou layest the foundation of the earth. 你奠定了地球的基础。|近义词：placest, puttest, settest|反义词：takest, liftest|词性:动词
