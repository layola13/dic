boom:/buːm/| n. vi.|繁荣，兴旺；发出隆隆声|A period of great economic prosperity. / To make a deep resonant sound.|例句：The housing market is experiencing a boom. 房地产市场正在蓬勃发展。

例句：The cannon boomed across the battlefield. 大炮在战场上隆隆作响。|近义词：prosperity, flourish, thrive, roar, rumble|反义词：slump, recession, decline, whisper|词性:名词，动词

booms:/buːmz/| n. vi. (boom的第三人称单数形式)|繁荣，兴旺；发出隆隆声|The third-person singular present tense form of "boom".|例句：The tech industry booms with innovation. 科技行业因创新而蓬勃发展。

例句：Thunder booms in the distance. 雷声在远处隆隆作响。|近义词：prospers, flourishes, thrives, roars, rumbles|反义词：slumps, declines|词性:动词
