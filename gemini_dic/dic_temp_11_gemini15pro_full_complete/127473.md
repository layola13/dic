necessitate:/nəˈsesɪteɪt/
verb
使成为必需，需要
to make something necessary
例句：Increased traffic necessitated widening the road. 交通流量的增加使得拓宽道路成为必要。
例句：His broken leg necessitated an operation. 他的断腿需要手术。
近义词：require, demand, call for
反义词：obviate, preclude
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin necessitat- stem of necessitas "necessity," from Latin necessarius "necessary" (see necessary). Related: Necessitated; necessitating.
