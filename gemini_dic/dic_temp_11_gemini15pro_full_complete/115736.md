mariculture: /ˈmærɪkʌltʃər/ | n. | 海水养殖；海产养殖 | The cultivation of marine organisms in their natural habitats, usually for commercial purposes. | Mariculture is an important industry in many coastal regions. 海水养殖是许多沿海地区的重要产业。|  Mariculture farms can produce a variety of seafood, including fish, shellfish, and seaweed. 海水养殖场可以生产各种各样的海鲜，包括鱼、贝类和海藻。| 近义词: aquaculture, fish farming | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps wild harvesting) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin "mare" (sea) + "cultura" (cultivation).
