claims:/kleɪmz/ /klɛɪmz/ | n. | 索赔；声明；所有权 | An assertion of a right or demand.  |  The insurance company is investigating the claims. 保险公司正在调查索赔。 | synonyms: demands, assertions, requests, applications | antonyms: waivers, concessions, denials | noun

claim:/kleɪm/ /klɛɪmz/ | v. | 要求；声称；断言；索取 | To state or assert that something is the case, typically without providing evidence or proof. | He claimed to have seen the accident. 他声称目睹了这起事故。| synonyms: assert, declare, profess, maintain | antonyms: deny, refute, reject | verb
