febrile:/ˈfiːbraɪl/| adj.|发热的，热性的|Marked or caused by fever; feverish.|例句：The patient presented with febrile symptoms, including a high temperature and chills. 病人出现发热症状，包括高烧和寒战。

例句：The febrile child was listless and irritable.  发烧的孩子无精打采且易怒。|近义词：feverish, pyretic|反义词:afebrile, apyretic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *febrilis*, from febris ("fever").
