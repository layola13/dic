macrotone:/ˈmækrəˌtoʊn/| n.|宏音; (诗歌)长音节; |A macrotone is a long syllable or vowel.  It can also refer to a stressed syllable in some contexts, particularly in prosody (the study of rhythm and intonation in language).|The poem utilized macrotones to create a sense of grandeur.|近义词：long syllable, stressed syllable|反义词:short syllable, unstressed syllable|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Ancient Greek  μακρός (makrós, “long”) + τόνος (tónos, “tone”).
