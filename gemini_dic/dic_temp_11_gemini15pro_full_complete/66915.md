expeller:/ɪkˈspɛlər/| n.|榨油机，排出器，驱逐者|A machine that presses or squeezes something to extract liquid or oil; someone or something that expels.|The expeller efficiently extracted oil from the seeds. 这台榨油机有效地从种子中榨取了油。|近义词：press, extractor, ejector|反义词: retainer, holder|noun

expeller:/ɪkˈspɛlər/| n.|驱逐者，排出者|Someone or something that forces something else to leave.|The new law acted as an expeller of illegal immigrants. 这项新法律起到了驱逐非法移民的作用。|近义词：ejector, outruster|反义词: welcomer, admitter|noun
