boree:/ˈbɔːriː/ /ˈbɔːreɪ/
n.
暴风雪，寒风
A sudden violent windstorm, especially one with snow or sleet.
The boree swept across the plains, bringing with it a biting cold.  暴风雪席卷了平原，带来了刺骨的寒冷。
blizzard, snowstorm, gale
calm, stillness
