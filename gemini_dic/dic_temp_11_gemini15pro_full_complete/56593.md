donate:/doʊˈneɪt/ (US), /dəʊˈneɪt/ (UK)| v. |捐赠，捐献|To give something, especially money, to a person or an organization in order to help them.|He donated $100 to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠了100美元。|近义词：contribute, give, bestow|反义词：receive, take|词性：动词

donate:/ˈdoʊneɪt/ (US), /ˈdəʊneɪt/ (UK)| n. |捐赠物|Something that is donated.|The library gratefully accepted the donate of books. 图书馆感激地接受了捐赠的书籍。|近义词：contribution, gift, offering|反义词：N/A|词性：名词 (较少使用)
