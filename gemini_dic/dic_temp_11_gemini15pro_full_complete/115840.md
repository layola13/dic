marketstead:/ˈmɑːrkɪtˌstɛd/| n.|市场地位; 市场份额|A company's marketstead refers to its position or share in a particular market. It often considers factors like sales, customer loyalty, and brand recognition.|Example: The company has a strong marketstead in the luxury car market.  这家公司在豪华汽车市场占有很强的市场地位。

Example:  They are working to improve their marketstead by launching a new marketing campaign.  他们正在通过发起新的营销活动来提升市场地位。|近义词：market share, market position, market standing|反义词: market weakness|词性:名词


词源:  Compound word formed from "market" and "stead".  "Market" refers to the place or sphere of trade, and "stead" means a place or position.
