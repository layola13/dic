ponderous:/ˈpɑːndərəs/
|adj.|沉重的；笨重的；（文章或言谈）呆板的，冗长的|The professor's lectures were ponderous and boring.  这位教授的讲座沉闷乏味。|例句：He carried a ponderous suitcase full of books. 他提着一个装满书的沉重手提箱。

例句：The ponderous novel was a challenge to finish. 这部长篇小说读起来很费劲。


|近义词：heavy, weighty, cumbersome, unwieldy, tedious|
|反义词：light, nimble, concise, engaging|
|词性：adjective|

词源：From Late Latin *ponderōsus*, from Latin pondus (“weight”).
