dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ (US) /daɪˈljuːt/ (UK)| adj. v. |稀释的，冲淡的；稀释，冲淡|To make a liquid thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent. To reduce the strength or concentration of something.|The artist diluted the paint with water. 艺术家用清水稀释了颜料。|synonyms: weaken, thin, water down|antonyms: concentrate, strengthen, thicken|verb, adjective

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ (US) /daɪˈljuːt/ (UK)| n. |稀释液|A diluted solution.|The chemist prepared a dilute for the experiment. 化学家为实验准备了稀释液。|synonyms: solution, mixture|antonyms: concentrate|noun
