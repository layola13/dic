culpability:/ˌkʌlpəˈbɪləti/
n.|有罪；有过失；应受谴责|The degree to which someone deserves blame for a crime or wrongdoing.  应受谴责的程度。|The jury found him guilty, but the judge questioned the degree of his culpability. 陪审团裁定他有罪，但法官质疑他的罪责程度。|Synonyms: guilt, blameworthiness, responsibility, liability|Antonyms: innocence, blamelessness|noun
