estimableness:/ˈɛstɪməblənəs/
n.
可估量性；值得尊敬
The quality of being worthy of esteem; respectability.
Her estimableness was evident in her charitable work and dedication to her family. 她的可敬之处体现在她的慈善工作和对家庭的奉献上。
近义词: respectability, worthiness, merit
反义词: disreputability, unworthiness
