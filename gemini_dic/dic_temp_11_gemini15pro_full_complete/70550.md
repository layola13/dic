filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/
n.|金银丝细工; 精致的装饰; (尤指文学或音乐的)华丽辞藻，精巧细致|Ornamental work of fine (typically gold or silver) wire formed into delicate tracery.|The brooch was a delicate piece of filigree. 这枚胸针是一件精致的金属丝细工。|近义词:tracery, ornamentation, decoration|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:noun

filigreed:/ˈfɪlɪɡrid/
adj.|饰以金银丝细工的; 非常精致的|Decorated with filigree.|The filigreed gates of the palace were impressive. 宫殿那扇饰有金银丝细工的大门令人印象深刻。|近义词:ornate, decorated, elaborate|反义词:plain, simple, undecorated|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *filigrana*, from Italian *filigrana* "filigree," from Latin *filum* "thread" + *granum* "grain."
