costumer:/ˈkɑːstəmər/ (US) /ˈkʌstəmər/ (UK)| n.|顾客，客户|A person who buys goods or services from a shop or business.|例句：The costumer was very satisfied with the service. 顾客对服务非常满意。

例句：The store offers a discount to regular costumers.  这家商店为常客提供折扣。|近义词：client, patron, shopper, buyer|反义词: seller, vendor|noun
