nonspiritually:/ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtʃuəli/
adverb
非精神上地，世俗地
Without regard to spiritual matters; in a worldly or mundane way.
She approached the problem nonspiritually, focusing on practical solutions rather than seeking divine guidance. 她以非精神的方式处理这个问题，专注于实际的解决方案，而不是寻求神圣的指引。
Synonyms: materially, secularly, worldly, mundanely
Antonyms: spiritually, piously, devoutly
词性: 副词
