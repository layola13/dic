mesmerize: /ˈmezməraɪz/ (US) /ˈmɛzməraɪz/ (UK)| v. | 迷住，催眠 | to hold the attention of (someone) entirely; to fascinate | The magician mesmerized the audience with his tricks.  魔术师用他的戏法迷住了观众。| synonyms:  captivate, enthrall, fascinate, hypnotize | antonyms: bore, repel, disgust | verb

mesmerizing: /ˈmezməraɪzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɛzməraɪzɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 令人着迷的，催眠般的 | having a strong, captivating effect | The sunset over the ocean was a mesmerizing sight.  海上日落是一个迷人的景象。| synonyms: captivating, enthralling, fascinating, hypnotic | antonyms: boring, repellent, disgusting | adjective
