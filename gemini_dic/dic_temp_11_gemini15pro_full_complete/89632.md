hookah:/ˈhʊkə/ (US), /ˈhʊkɑː/ (UK)| n. |水烟袋，水烟壶|A water pipe with a long flexible tube attached to a container where tobacco is burned and the smoke is drawn through water. |例句：
We smoked hookah at a cafe. 我们在咖啡馆抽了水烟。

He bought a new hookah. 他买了一个新的水烟袋。

近义词：water pipe, narghile, shisha|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi हुक्का (hukkä), from Arabic  hookah (“water pipe”). Ultimately from Arabic حق (ḥaqq) (“container, vessel”).
