megametre:/ˈmɛɡəˌmiːtər/ | n. |百万米；兆米|A unit of length equal to one million metres.|The distance between the two cities is about one megametre. 这两个城市之间的距离大约是一百万米。|近义词: million meters |反义词: (No direct opposite, possibly micrometer, nanometer for smaller units)|noun
