dosology:/doʊˈsɑːlədʒi/
n.
剂量学; (尤指)药物剂量学
The branch of medicine concerned with the determination of the proper dose of a medicine.
The dosology of this drug needs careful consideration.  这种药物的剂量需要仔细考虑。
近义词: posology
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but overdose could be considered a related opposite concept)
