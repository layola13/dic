deaden:/ˈdedn/| v. |使减弱，使降低，使失去活力|to make something less strong, loud, or bright|The thick curtains deadened the sound from the street. 厚厚的窗帘减弱了街上的声音。|The drug deadened the pain. 这种药物减轻了疼痛。|近义词：dull, dampen, mute, suppress, weaken|反义词：sharpen, intensify, heighten, amplify|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *dēadian "to make or become dead," from dēad "dead."
