ductile:/ˈdʌktaɪl/ (US), /ˈdʌktaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|延展的，易延展的，柔软的|Able to be drawn out into a thin wire or hammered into thin sheets without breaking; malleable; pliable.|例句：Copper is a ductile metal, easily drawn into wires. 铜是一种延展性金属，很容易拉成线。

例句：Gold is highly ductile and can be beaten into incredibly thin sheets.  金延展性极佳，可以被敲打成非常薄的薄片。

近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible, plastic|反义词：brittle, fragile, inflexible, rigid|词性：形容词
