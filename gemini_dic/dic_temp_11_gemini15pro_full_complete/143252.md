outdespatch:/ˌaʊtdɪˈspætʃ/ | vt. | 超越；胜过；派遣；发送 | To send something or someone quickly, especially on an important mission; to surpass or outperform. |  The king outdespatched a messenger with the urgent news. 国王派遣了一名信使送去紧急消息。

The new model car easily outdespatches its competitors in terms of fuel efficiency. 这款新型汽车在燃油效率方面轻松超越了竞争对手。 | 近义词: outstrip, surpass, excel, dispatch, send | 反义词: lag, fall behind | 词性: 动词
