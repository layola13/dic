administrants:/ədˈmɪnɪstrənts/
|n.|管理者，行政人员|People responsible for managing or administering something, typically an organization, business, or government entity.  Those who direct or control the affairs of something.|The administrants of the university made a controversial decision. 大学的管理人员做出了一个有争议的决定。|synonyms: administrators, managers, executives, directors, officials, supervisors|antonyms: subordinates, employees, staff|noun
