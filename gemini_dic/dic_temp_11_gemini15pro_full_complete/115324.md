manning:/ˈmænɪŋ/ | n. |人员配备；人手；操作人员|The act of providing people to do a job; the people who do a job; the number of people required to do a job.|The ship has a manning of 20. 这艘船有20名船员。|近义词：staffing, crew, workforce|反义词：understaffing, shortage|词性:名词

词源:  From the verb "man," meaning to supply with men, operate, or work. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mann- (man, person).
