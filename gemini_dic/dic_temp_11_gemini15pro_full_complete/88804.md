Hofbräuhaus:/ˈhoʊfbrɔɪhaʊs/ | n. |皇家啤酒馆；宫廷酿酒屋 | A German beer hall, especially the famous one in Munich. | Example 1:  We visited the Hofbräuhaus in Munich and had a great time. 我们去了慕尼黑的皇家啤酒馆，玩得很开心。

Example 2: The Hofbräuhaus is known for its lively atmosphere and traditional Bavarian food. 皇家啤酒馆以其热闹的氛围和传统的巴伐利亚美食而闻名。 | Synonym: beer hall, brewery, tavern | Antonym:  None (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
