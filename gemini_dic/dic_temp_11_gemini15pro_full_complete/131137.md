nonblinding:/ˌnɒnˈblaɪndɪŋ/ | adj. | 非盲法的，开放式的 |  Not blinding; referring to a study or trial in which both the researchers and participants are aware of the treatment being administered. | The nonblinding nature of the study introduced potential bias. 这项研究的非盲法性质引入了潜在的偏见。| The researchers opted for a nonblinding trial due to the practical difficulties of concealing the intervention.  由于隐藏干预措施的实际困难，研究人员选择了非盲法试验。| synonyms: open-label, unblinded | antonyms: blinded, double-blinded | adjective
