| n. |玛瑙|A variety of chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline form of quartz, characterized by its fineness of grain and intense color, particularly black and white.  It is often used in jewelry and decorative objects.|She wore a necklace of polished onyxes.|Synonyms: agate, chalcedony|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
