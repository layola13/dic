demolisher:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃər/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːlɪʃər/ (US)| n.|拆除者，破坏者|A person or thing that demolishes something, especially buildings.|The demolisher quickly reduced the old factory to rubble.  拆除者迅速将旧工厂夷为平地。|Synonyms: wrecker, destroyer, razer|Antonyms: builder, constructor, creator|noun

demolish:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːlɪʃ/ (US)| vt.|拆除，破坏，推翻，驳倒|To destroy (a building, structure, etc.) completely; tear down.  To ruin completely; destroy.|They demolished the old building to make way for a new shopping center. 他们拆除了旧建筑，以便为新的购物中心腾出空间。  The lawyer demolished the witness's testimony. 律师驳倒了证人的证词。|Synonyms: destroy, wreck, ruin, devastate, refute|Antonyms: build, construct, create, establish|verb
