chamomiles: /ˈkæməˌmaɪlz/ | n. | 洋甘菊；甘菊 | A plant with small white and yellow flowers, used to make tea and other products believed to have medicinal properties. |  例句：She made a cup of chamomile tea to help her sleep. 她泡了一杯甘菊茶来帮助睡眠。

例句：Chamomiles are often used in aromatherapy for their calming scent.  甘菊经常被用于芳香疗法，因为它们的气味能使人镇静。

近义词：camomile | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *camomille, from Late Latin chamomilla, from Latin chamaemēlum, from Greek χαμαίμηλον (khamainēlon, “earth-apple”), from χαμαί (khamai, “on the ground”) + μῆλον (mēlon, “apple”).  The word refers to the apple-like scent of the plant.
