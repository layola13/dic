abietin:/ˌeɪbiˈɛtɪn/| n.|松香脂，松香亭|A resinous substance obtained from the rosin of certain pine trees, specifically from the species *Abies pectinata*.  It is a mixture of isomeric abietic acids.|Pine trees exude abietin, which can be collected and processed. 松树会分泌松香脂，可以收集和加工。| The abietin was used in the production of varnish.  松香脂被用于制造清漆。 |近义词:rosin, colophony|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abies* ("fir tree") + -in (suffix forming names of chemical substances).
