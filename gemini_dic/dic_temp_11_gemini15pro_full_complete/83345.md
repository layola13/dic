n.
旋棘鲨（一种已灭绝的鱼类）
An extinct genus of fish, characterized by large, ornamented spines.
Gyracanthus fossils are commonly found in Carboniferous rocks.  旋棘鲨化石常见于石炭纪岩石中。
Gyracanthus is related to other early cartilaginous fishes. 旋棘鲨与其他早期软骨鱼类有亲缘关系。
Synonym:  (No direct synonym, but can be referred to as an "extinct cartilaginous fish" or "acanthodian")
Antonym:  (Not applicable for an extinct genus)
词性: noun
