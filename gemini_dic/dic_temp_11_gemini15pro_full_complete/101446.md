iratenesses: /ˌɪrəˈteɪtnɪsɪz/ | n. |  раздражительность (Russian - closest equivalent - irritability)| The state of being easily irritated; proneness to anger or annoyance. | Her constant irateness made it difficult to work with her. 她持续的易怒情绪让人很难和她一起工作。|  The irateness of the driver was evident in his aggressive driving. 司机的愤怒表现在他咄咄逼人的驾驶行为中。| 近义词: irritability, exasperation, petulance, crossness | 反义词: calmness, patience, tranquility | 词性: noun

Etymology: From irate (irritated, angry) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *iratus* (angered), past participle of *irare* (to be angry).
