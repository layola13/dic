nonfriend:/nɒnˈfrɛnd/ | n. | 非朋友；没有特别亲近的人；关系一般的人 |  A person whom one does not consider a friend, perhaps someone one actively dislikes or avoids, or simply someone with whom one has a neutral or distant relationship. |  例句：He's not an enemy, just a nonfriend. 他不是敌人，只是一个关系一般的人。

例句：She keeps her nonfriends at arm's length. 她与关系一般的人保持距离。 | 近义词：acquaintance, associate | 反义词：friend, close friend | 词性: 名词
