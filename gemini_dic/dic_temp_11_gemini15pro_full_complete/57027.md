doubles:/ˈdʌblz/| n.|双打；双份；两倍|A game between two pairs of players.|例句：They played doubles in the tennis tournament. 他们在网球锦标赛中打双打。|近义词：pairs, two-fold|反义词:singles|词性:名词

doubles:/ˈdʌblz/| v.|加倍；是…的两倍；兼任|To become twice as much or as many.|例句：He doubled his income in just two years. 他在短短两年内收入翻了一番。|近义词：increase, multiply|反义词:halve|词性:动词
