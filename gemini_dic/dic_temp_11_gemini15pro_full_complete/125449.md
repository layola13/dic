multiplexes:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksɪz/
n.|多路复用器；多厅电影院|A movie theater complex with multiple screens.  A device that combines multiple signals into one.|The new multiplex has 12 screens.  新的多厅电影院有12个屏幕。|synonyms: cinema complex, multiscreen cinema|antonyms: single-screen theater|noun

multiplex:/ˈmʌltɪpleks/
v.|多路传输，多路复用|To combine multiple signals into one.|The system multiplexes several data streams onto a single cable.  该系统将多个数据流多路复用到一根电缆上。|synonyms: combine, merge|antonyms: separate, divide|verb
