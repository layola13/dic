antiracist:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst/
n.|反种族主义者|A person who opposes racism and promotes racial tolerance and equality.|Example:  She is a vocal antiracist, always speaking out against injustice. 她是一位直言不讳的反种族主义者，总是公开反对不公正。|近义词: egalitarian, integrationist|反义词: racist, bigot|词性: 名词

antiracist:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst/
adj.|反种族主义的|Opposing racism; promoting racial tolerance and equality.|Example:  He supports antiracist policies that aim to dismantle systemic racism. 他支持旨在消除系统性种族主义的反种族主义政策。|近义词: anti-discrimination, nonracist|反义词: racist, discriminatory|词性: 形容词
