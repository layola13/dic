electrotechnician:/ɪˌlɛktroʊtɛkˈnɪʃən/| n. |电工技师，电气技师|A skilled worker who installs, maintains, and repairs electrical equipment and systems.|例句：The electrotechnician quickly diagnosed the problem with the wiring.  电工技师很快诊断出了线路问题。

例句：An experienced electrotechnician is needed to install the new machinery.  需要一位经验丰富的电工技师来安装新机器。|近义词：electrical technician, electrician|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
