decolonisation:/diːˌkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /diːˌkɑːlənɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.|去殖民化|The act of getting rid of colonization, or freeing a country from being dependent on another country.|The decolonisation of Africa occurred rapidly after World War II. 非洲的去殖民化在二战后迅速发生。|近义词：independence, liberation, emancipation|反义词：colonization, imperialism|词性:noun

decolonize:/diːˈkɒlənaɪz/ /diːˈkɑːlənɪz/
v.|去殖民化|To free a colony from dependent status.|Many African nations were decolonized in the mid-20th century. 许多非洲国家在20世纪中叶去殖民化。|近义词：liberate, free, emancipate|反义词：colonize, subjugate|词性:verb

decolonised:/diːˈkɒlənaɪzd/ /diːˈkɑːlənɪzd/
adj.|去殖民化的|Freed from colonial status.|India became a decolonised nation in 1947.  印度在1947年成为一个去殖民化的国家。|近义词：independent, liberated, free|反义词：colonized, dependent|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + colonization.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse," and "colonization" refers to the act of establishing a colony.
