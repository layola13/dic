maximised:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/
verb|最大化，使…最大限度|To increase something as much as possible.  To make the best use of something. |The company maximised its profits by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本实现了利润最大化。|Synonyms: optimized, boosted, amplified, enhanced|Antonyms: minimized, reduced, lessened|v.

maximized:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/
adjective|最大化的|Being in a state of having been increased as much as possible. |The maximized window filled the entire screen. 最大化的窗口占据了整个屏幕。|Synonyms: optimized, largest, fullest|Antonyms: minimized, smallest, least|adj.
