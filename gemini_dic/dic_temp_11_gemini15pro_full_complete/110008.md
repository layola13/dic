librarianship: /laɪˈbrɛəriənʃɪp/ | n. | 图书馆学；图书馆员的职业 | The study or practice of organizing and managing libraries. | She has a Master's degree in librarianship. 她拥有图书馆学硕士学位。|  She dedicated her life to librarianship. 她一生致力于图书馆事业。 | 近义词: library science, library work | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From librarian + -ship.  "Librarian" comes from Latin "librarius" (copyist of books), derived from "liber" (book). "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or skill.
