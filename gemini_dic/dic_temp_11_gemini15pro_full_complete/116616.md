matronal:/ˈmeɪtrənəl/ (US), /məˈtrəʊnl/ (UK)| adj.| matronly的；像主妇的；适于主妇的；（尤指年长的女人）稳重的，有威严的|Of, relating to, or characteristic of a matron; suitable for or characteristic of a married woman, especially one who is mature and dignified; having the qualities of a matron. |例句：She had a rather matronly air about her. 她身上有一种主妇的气质。

例句：Her matronal figure was comforting. 她那像主妇般丰满的身材让人感到安心。|近义词：motherly, stately, dignified, mature|反义词：immature, girlish, childish|词性：形容词
