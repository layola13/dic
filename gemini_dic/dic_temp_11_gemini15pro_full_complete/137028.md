nonstationaries:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪʃənɛriz/ | n. | 非平稳性；非平稳过程 |  A process that does not have constant statistical properties over time, particularly mean and variance.  Refers to time series data whose statistical properties change over time. |  The presence of nonstationaries in the data made it difficult to forecast future trends.  数据中非平稳性的存在使得预测未来趋势变得困难。|Synonyms: non-stationarity, volatility, instability | Antonyms: stationarity, stability | noun

nonstationary:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪʃənɛri/ | adj. | 非平稳的 | Not stationary; having statistical properties that change over time. | The economist identified a nonstationary time series in the housing market data.  这位经济学家在房地产市场数据中发现了一个非平稳时间序列。 | Synonyms: volatile, unstable, fluctuating | Antonyms: stationary, stable, constant | adjective
