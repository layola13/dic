budgerigar:/ˈbʌdʒərɪɡɑːr/ (US) /ˈbʌdʒərɪɡɑː/ (UK)| n. |虎皮鹦鹉；背黄青鹦鹉|A small, long-tailed parrot, usually light green with black and yellow markings, native to Australia and popular as a pet.|例句：My budgerigar can say "hello." 我的虎皮鹦鹉会说“你好”。

|例句：Budgerigars are social birds and enjoy the company of others. 虎皮鹦鹉是群居鸟类，喜欢和同伴相处。|近义词：parakeet, shell parakeet|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
