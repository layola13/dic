anticlassicist:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪst/
adjective
反古典主义的；反对古典主义的
Opposed to or rejecting classicism.
His anticlassicist stance made him unpopular with the traditional art critics. 他的反古典主义立场使他不受传统艺术评论家的欢迎。
The architect’s anticlassicist designs incorporated modern materials and geometric shapes.  这位建筑师的反古典主义设计融合了现代材料和几何形状。

近义词:modernist, iconoclastic
反义词:classicist, traditionalist
词性:adjective
