attune:/əˈtjuːn/
verb
使协调，使和谐；使合拍
to bring into harmony; to adjust or accustom
The musician attuned his instrument to the orchestra. 音乐家把他的乐器调到与管弦乐队和谐。
近义词：harmonize, adjust, adapt, acclimate
反义词：disharmonize, mismatch
词性：动词


attune:/əˈtjuːn/
verb
(attune oneself/something to something)  使适应；使合拍
to make someone or something able to understand or deal with a particular situation or person
I needed time to attune myself to the new job. 我需要时间来适应新工作。
近义词：adapt, adjust, acclimate
反义词：unadapt
词性：动词
