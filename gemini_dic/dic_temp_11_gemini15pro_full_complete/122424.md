moanful:/ˈmoʊnfəl/ (US) /ˈməʊnfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|悲叹的，哀伤的|Expressing sorrow or suffering through moans or complaints.  Characterized by a mournful sound.|The dog let out a moanful howl when its owner left.  这只狗在主人离开时发出一声悲鸣的嚎叫。|Synonyms: mournful, plaintive, doleful, sorrowful, lamenting|Antonyms: cheerful, joyful, happy, merry|adjective


moanful:/ˈmoʊnfəl/ (US) /ˈməʊnfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|悲哀的，令人悲伤的|Causing or tending to cause sadness or grief.|The moanful sound of the wind added to the desolate atmosphere. 悲凉的风声增添了凄凉的气氛。|Synonyms: saddening, depressing, heartbreaking|Antonyms: uplifting, inspiring, cheering|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
