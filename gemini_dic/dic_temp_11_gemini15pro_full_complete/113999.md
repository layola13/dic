magistrally:/məˈdʒɪstrəli/|/məˈdʒɪstrəli/| adv.|熟练地；巧妙地；权威地|In a masterly manner; skillfully and authoritatively.|She handled the crisis magistrally. 她熟练地处理了这场危机。|Synonyms: masterfully, skillfully, expertly, brilliantly, adeptly|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, poorly|adverb

magistrally:/məˈdʒɪstrəli/|/məˈdʒɪstrəli/| adv.|专横地；傲慢地|In an overbearing or domineering manner. (Less common usage)|He magistrally dismissed their concerns. 他专横地驳回了他们的担忧。|Synonyms: imperiously, dictatorially, autocratically|Antonyms: humbly, submissively, meekly|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magistralis*, from Latin magister ("master, teacher").
