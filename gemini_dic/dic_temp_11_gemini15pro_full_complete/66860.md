expectative:/ɪkˈspektətɪv/
adjective
期待的，预期的
Characterized by expectation; anticipating.
She had an expectant look on her face as she waited for the results. 她等待结果时脸上带着期盼的表情。
近义词:anticipatory, hopeful, expectant
反义词:pessimistic, hopeless, resigned
词性:adjective

expectative:/ɪkˈspektətɪv/
noun
期望，期待
Something expected; an expectation.
The expectatives of the shareholders were high for the next quarter. 股东对下一季度的期望很高。
近义词:expectation, anticipation, hope
反义词:disappointment, despair, pessimism
词性:noun


词源: From Late Latin *expectativus, from Latin exspectatus, past participle of exspectare (“to await, expect, look out for”).
