chemist:/ˈkemɪst/| n.|化学家；药剂师|A scientist who specializes in chemistry.  A person qualified to prepare and dispense drugs and medicines.|例句：The chemist analyzed the compound.  这位化学家分析了这种化合物。|例句：I went to the chemist to get my prescription filled. 我去药店配药了。|近义词：pharmacist, druggist, scientist|反义词:(No direct antonym)|noun
