inclusionary:/ɪnˈkluːʒənˌɛri/ | adj. | 包容性的，包含在内的 | Designed to include people of all groups, especially those who have been excluded in the past. |  The city council adopted an inclusionary zoning ordinance to create more affordable housing. 市议会通过了一项包容性分区条例，以建造更多经济适用房。

The school prides itself on its inclusionary practices, ensuring all students feel welcome and valued.  这所学校以其包容性做法为荣，确保所有学生都感到受欢迎和受到重视。 | 近义词：inclusive, integrating, comprehensive | 反义词：exclusionary, exclusive, restrictive | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "inclusion" + "-ary". "Inclusion" comes from Late Latin "inclusio," from Latin "includere" (to shut in, enclose), from "in-" (in) + "claudere" (to shut).
