guardable:/ˈɡɑːrdəbl/| adj.|可保护的，可守卫的|Capable of being guarded or protected.|The castle is guardable due to its high walls and strategic location.  这座城堡由于其高墙和战略位置易于防守。|The information is highly guardable and should be kept secure.  这些信息是高度机密的，应该妥善保管。|近义词:defensible, protectable, securable|反义词:vulnerable, unprotected, exposed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "guard" (verb) + "-able" (suffix). "Guard" comes from Old French "garder" meaning "to watch, protect, keep".  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being".
