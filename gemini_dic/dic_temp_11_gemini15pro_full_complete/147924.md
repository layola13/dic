overrelaxing:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlæksɪŋ/| adj.|过度放松的|Excessively relaxing; relaxing to an excessive degree.  The act of relaxing too much, often to the point of inactivity or lethargy.|Spending the entire weekend on the couch watching TV was overrelaxing.  整个周末都躺在沙发上看电视，真是太放松了。|Synonyms:  overly relaxed, excessively relaxed, too relaxed|Antonyms:  stressed, tense, anxious|adjective
