aggrieved:/əˈɡriːvd/| adj.| 委屈的，愤愤不平的| Feeling resentment at having been unfairly treated.|  例句：She felt aggrieved at not being invited to the party. 她因为没有被邀请参加聚会而感到委屈。| 近义词：resentful, indignant, offended, wronged| 反义词：content, satisfied, pleased| 词性: 形容词

aggrieved:/əˈɡriːvd/| adj.| 受委屈的| Having been subjected to unfair treatment.| 例句：The aggrieved party filed a lawsuit. 受委屈的一方提起了诉讼。| 近义词：wronged, injured, harmed| 反义词：privileged, favored|词性: 形容词
