inhumanity:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmænəti/
n.
lack of humanity; cruelty; barbarity
lack of kindness, pity, or compassion
The inhumanity of the war shocked the world. 战争的残酷震惊了世界。
The refugees suffered great inhumanity at the hands of their captors.  难民们遭受了俘虏他们的极大的不人道待遇。
近义词：cruelty, barbarity, brutality, savagery, wickedness
反义词：humanity, compassion, kindness, mercy, benevolence
词性：noun

inhuman:/ɪnˈhjuːmən/
adj.
lacking human qualities of kindness and pity; cruel
not human; not belonging to the human race
The prison guards displayed inhuman treatment towards the inmates. 监狱看守对囚犯表现出不人道的待遇。
Some people believe in inhuman creatures like aliens.  有些人相信外星人等非人类生物的存在。
近义词：cruel, brutal, barbaric, savage, merciless
反义词：human, humane, kind, compassionate, merciful
词性：adjective


词源：Late Middle English: from Old French inhumanité, from Latin inhumanitas, from inhumanus (see inhuman).
