morosis:/məˈroʊsɪs/ /mɔːˈrəʊsɪs/
noun
郁闷，闷闷不乐，忧郁
A state of gloominess or sullenness.
His morosis deepened as the rainy days continued.  他随着阴雨连绵的日子而变得更加郁闷。
The team's morosis after the loss was palpable. 球队输球后的郁闷之情显而易见。
近义词: gloom, dejection, melancholy, depression
反义词: cheerfulness, elation, joy
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *morōsis, from Latin morōsus (“peevish, fretful”), from mōs (“manner, custom, habit”) + -ōsus.
