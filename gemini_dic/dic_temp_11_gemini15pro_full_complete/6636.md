anaesthetic:/ˌænəsˈθetɪk/ /ˌænɪsˈθetɪk/| n. adj.|麻醉剂，麻醉的|A substance that causes loss of sensation, especially pain, either generally or locally.|The patient was given a general anaesthetic before the surgery. 病人在手术前进行了全身麻醉。|近义词：narcotic, analgesic|反义词: stimulant|词性: noun, adjective

anaesthetic:/ˌænəsˈθetɪk/ /ˌænɪsˈθetɪk/| adj.|麻醉的|Relating to or producing anaesthesia.|The anaesthetic properties of the drug made it useful for surgery.  这种药物的麻醉特性使其可用于外科手术。|近义词：numbing|反义词: stimulating|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *anaisthetos* "without sensation," from *an-* "without" + *aisthetos* "perceptible," from *aisthanesthai* "to perceive."
