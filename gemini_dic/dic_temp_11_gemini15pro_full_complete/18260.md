believing:/bɪˈliːvɪŋ/
verb|相信; 认为|Accepting something as true; holding something as an opinion.|I'm believing his story. 我相信他的故事。|
近义词:trusting, accepting, crediting
反义词:doubting, disbelieving
词性:present participle of believe
词源:From Middle English *bileven, *bleven, from Old English belīefan (“to believe, trust, allow, permit”), from Proto-Germanic *galauƀjaną (“to believe, hold dear, esteem”), equivalent to be- +‎ lief. Cognate with Dutch geloven (“to believe”), German glauben (“to believe”), Gothic (galaubjan, “to believe”). More at belief.
