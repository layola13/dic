codesign:/ˈkoʊdsaɪn/| v.|代码签名|The process of digitally signing software to guarantee its authenticity and integrity, allowing the operating system to verify its source and ensure it hasn't been tampered with.|Example:  You need to codesign your app before submitting it to the App Store. 你需要在将应用程序提交到应用商店之前对其进行代码签名。

Example:  The developer codesigned the application with their certificate. 开发人员使用他们的证书对应用程序进行了代码签名。|近义词:digitally sign|反义词:N/A|词性:动词
