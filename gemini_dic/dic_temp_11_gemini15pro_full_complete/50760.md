dervish:/ˈdɜːrvɪʃ/| n. |托钵僧，苦行僧|A member of a Muslim (especially Sufi) religious order who has taken vows of poverty and austerity.  They are known for their ascetic practices and sometimes for ecstatic dancing or whirling.|The dervish whirled around and around, seemingly lost in a trance. 托钵僧旋转了一圈又一圈，似乎陷入了恍惚状态。|The dervish wandered the countryside, begging for alms. 托钵僧在乡下游荡，乞讨施舍。|Synonyms: ascetic, mystic, fakir|Antonyms: materialist, hedonist|noun
