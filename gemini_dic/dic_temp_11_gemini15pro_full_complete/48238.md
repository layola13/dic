decontaminations:/diːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.| 去污，净化，消毒 |The process of removing contaminants.  The decontamination of the hospital wing took several days.| 例句：The decontaminations of the surgical instruments were carried out meticulously.  手术器械的消毒工作一丝不苟地进行。

decontamination:/diːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.| 去污，净化，消毒 |The process of removing contaminants.| 例句：The decontamination of the hospital wing took several days. 医院侧楼的去污工作花了几天时间。

例句：Thorough decontamination is essential after exposure to hazardous materials. 接触危险材料后必须彻底净化。|近义词：cleaning, purification, sanitization|反义词：contamination, pollution|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + contamination (from contaminate, from Latin contaminare "to pollute").
