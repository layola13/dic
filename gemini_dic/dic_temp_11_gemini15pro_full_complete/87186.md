herdsman:/ˈhɜːrdzmən/ (US) /ˈhɜːdzmən/ (UK)| n. |牧人；牧牛人|A person, especially a man, who takes care of a herd of livestock.|The herdsman led his cattle to the pasture. 牧人把他的牛赶到牧场。|Synonyms: shepherd, cowherd, stockman, drover|Antonyms: None|Noun
