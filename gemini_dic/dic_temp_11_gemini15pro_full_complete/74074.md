fossilization:/ˌfɑsəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌfɒsɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|化石化；僵化；固定化|The process by which organic matter becomes a fossil.  The fossilization of ancient trees formed petrified wood.|例句：The fossilization of the dinosaur bones took millions of years. 恐龙骨骼的化石化过程耗费了数百万年。

例句：The fossilization of old customs can hinder progress. 旧习俗的僵化会阻碍进步。|近义词：petrification, lithification|反义词:decomposition, decay|词性:noun
