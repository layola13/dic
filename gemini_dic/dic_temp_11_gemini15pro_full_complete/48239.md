decontaminative:/diːkənˈtæmɪnətɪv/
adjective
去污的；净化性的
Having the ability to decontaminate; relating to decontamination.
The company specializes in decontaminative solutions for hazardous waste.  这家公司专门提供危险废物的去污解决方案。
The decontaminative properties of this soap are remarkable.  这种肥皂的去污性能非常出色。
近义词: cleansing, purifying, sanitizing, disinfecting
反义词: contaminating, polluting

词性: adjective
