bad-off:/ˌbæd ˈɔːf/ | adj. | 糟糕的，境况不好的 | Poorly situated; in unfortunate circumstances. |  They were pretty bad-off after the fire destroyed their home.  他们家被火烧毁后境况很糟糕。| 近义词：poor, impoverished, destitute, badly off | 反义词: well-off, affluent, prosperous | 词性: 形容词
Etymology:  Compound of "bad" and "off," with "off" meaning "in a poor or disadvantaged condition."
