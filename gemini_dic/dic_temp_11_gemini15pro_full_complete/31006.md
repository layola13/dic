chalks:/tʃɔːks/| n.|粉笔|A soft white or coloured material (calcium carbonate) used for writing or drawing, especially on blackboards or pavement.|The teacher used colored chalks to draw a diagram on the blackboard. 老师用彩色粉笔在黑板上画了一个图表。|近义词：crayons, pastels|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑marker, pen)|词性:noun

chalks:/tʃɔːks/| v. (chalks, chalking, chalked)|用粉笔写或画|To write or draw with chalk.|She chalked the score on the board. 她在黑板上用粉笔写下了分数。|近义词：write, draw, sketch|反义词: erase, wipe|词性:verb
