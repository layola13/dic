ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Northern French *crate (compare Old French crete "wicker basket, hurdle," also "mud, clay"), from a Germanic source (compare Middle Dutch crate, Old High German chratto "basket, hamper"), from Proto-Germanic *kratto.
