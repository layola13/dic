choristic:/kɔːˈrɪstɪk/
adj.
分离的，地域性的
Relating to or characterized by spatial separation or distribution.  Specifically in biology, referring to species or populations that are geographically separated.
The choristic distribution of the two subspecies led to their eventual divergence. 这两个亚种的分离分布导致它们最终的分化。
近义词:allopatric, disjunct, separate
反义词:sympatric, contiguous
词性:形容词

choristic:/kɔːˈrɪstɪk/
n.
地域性
The state or condition of being geographically separated.
The choristic nature of the islands contributed to the development of unique species.  这些岛屿的地域性特征促成了独特物种的发展。
近义词:allopatry, separation
反义词:sympatry
词性:名词
