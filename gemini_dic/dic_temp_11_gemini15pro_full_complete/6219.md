ammunition:/ˌæmjuˈnɪʃn/ | n. | 弹药，军火 | Materials used in discharging firearms or other weapons, such as bullets, shells, cartridges, or bombs. | The soldiers were running low on ammunition. 士兵们的弹药快用完了。 | synonyms: ordnance, weaponry, munitions, arms | antonyms: peace, disarmament | noun

ammunitions: (Non-standard, usually considered incorrect)  While sometimes used informally, the word "ammunitions" is generally considered grammatically incorrect as "ammunition" is an uncountable noun. It is better to use "ammunition" in both singular and plural contexts. |  The soldiers checked their ammunitions. (Incorrect usage - should be "ammunition") 士兵们检查了他们的弹药。| synonyms: ordnance, weaponry, munitions, arms (all preferably used in singular form) | antonyms: peace, disarmament | noun
