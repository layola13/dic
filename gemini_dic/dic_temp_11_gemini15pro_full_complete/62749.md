engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/
engorge|v.|使充血；狼吞虎咽；贪婪地吃|to cause to swell with blood, or to eat greedily|The mosquito engorged itself with blood.  蚊子吸饱了血。|近义词：gorge, stuff, glut|反义词：starve, deplete|词性:动词
engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/
engorged|adj.|充血的；饱食的|swollen with blood or other fluid; having eaten to excess|The tick was engorged after feeding.  壁虱吃饱后胀起来了。|近义词：swollen, full, bloated|反义词：empty, deflated|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French engorger "to swell, choke up," from en- "in" + gorge "throat."
