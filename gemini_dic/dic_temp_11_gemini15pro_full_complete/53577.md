disbodied:/dɪsˈbɒdid/ | adj. | 脱离肉体的；无形的；非物质的 | Separated from or existing without the body.  |  Her disembodied voice echoed through the hall. 她那脱离肉体的声音在整个大厅回荡。 |  The spirit appeared as a disembodied light.  这灵魂以一种无形的光出现。 | 近义词: incorporeal, ethereal, spiritual, ghostly | 反义词: embodied, corporeal, physical | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From dis- + embodied, from embody + -ed.  "Dis-" prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," expressing negation, reversal, or depriving. "Embody" means to give a body to; to make concrete and perceptible.
