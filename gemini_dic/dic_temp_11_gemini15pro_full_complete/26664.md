cacoxenite:/kəˈkɒksɪnaɪt/
n.
针铁矿，纤铁矿
A hydrated iron phosphate mineral, typically occurring as yellowish-brown to brownish-yellow radial aggregates or crusts.
Cacoxenite is often found associated with other iron phosphate minerals like strengite. 针铁矿通常与其他磷酸铁矿物（如 strengite）伴生。
Synonym: cacoxene, lascellesite
No antonym found.
