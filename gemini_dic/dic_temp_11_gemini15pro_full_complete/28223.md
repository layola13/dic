capsicum:/ˈkæpsɪkəm/ | n. |辣椒，甜椒|A genus of plants in the nightshade family (Solanaceae), native to the Americas, cultivated worldwide for their edible fruits.  Many varieties of peppers, both sweet and hot, are derived from species of Capsicum. | I added some red capsicum to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一些红椒。 | synonym: pepper, chili, bell pepper | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps mild vegetables like cucumber or lettuce as a contrast in taste) | noun
