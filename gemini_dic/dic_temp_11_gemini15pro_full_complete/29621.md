catel:/ˈkætəl/ | n. |野猫; 猫; (尤指)家猫; (贬义)心怀恶意的人; |A small wild cat, especially a European wildcat (Felis silvestris).  A cat, especially a domestic one.  A spiteful or malicious woman.| Example: The catel stalked a mouse in the field. 野猫在田里跟踪一只老鼠。 Example: The black catel curled up on the rug. 黑猫蜷缩在地毯上。 | 近义词: cat, feline, wildcat | 反义词: dog, canine | 词性: noun

词源: From Old North French *catel, from Late Latin cat(t)ellus, diminutive of catus "cat".
