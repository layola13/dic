limeys:/ˈlaɪmiz/
noun
英国佬（贬义）
A derogatory slang term for a British person, especially a British sailor.  Originating from the British Navy's practice of giving sailors limes to prevent scurvy.
例句：He referred to the British tourists as "limeys." 他把那些英国游客称为“英国佬”。
例句：The old sailor resented being called a "limey." 那位老水手讨厌被称为“英国佬”。

近义词：Brits, Englishmen (if referring to men), Englishwomen (if referring to women)
反义词:  None (as it's a derogatory term, direct antonyms are inappropriate)
词性: noun
