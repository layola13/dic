mannitic:/mæˈnɪtɪk/ | n. | 甘露醇 | Mannitol, a sweet, crystalline alcohol, C6H14O6, derived from mannose and occurring in many plants, used as a diuretic and a sweetening agent. |  Mannitic is used as a sweetener in some sugar-free foods. 甘露醇在一些无糖食品中用作甜味剂。 |  The doctor prescribed mannitic to reduce swelling in the brain. 医生开了甘露醇来减轻脑部肿胀。 | 近义词: mannitol | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially other diuretics depending on context) | 词性: noun
