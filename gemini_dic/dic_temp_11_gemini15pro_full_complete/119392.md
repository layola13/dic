microeconomics: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪks/ /ˌmaɪkroʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. | 微观经济学 | The study of how households, firms, and individuals make decisions to allocate limited resources, given the prices of goods and services, resources, and labor. |  Microeconomics helps us understand how prices are determined in a market. 微观经济学帮助我们理解市场中价格是如何决定的。 |  The course covers topics such as supply and demand, market structures, and consumer behavior. 这门课程涵盖了供求关系、市场结构和消费者行为等主题。 |  近义词: none (as it's a specific field of study) | 反义词: macroeconomics | 词性: noun
