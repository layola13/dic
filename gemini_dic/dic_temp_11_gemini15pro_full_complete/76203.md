fustigate:/ˈfʌstɪɡeɪt/| v. |严厉批评；鞭打；抨击|To criticize severely; to beat with a stick as punishment. |The senator fustigated his opponent for his lack of experience.  参议员严厉批评他的对手缺乏经验。

The teacher fustigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师因学生考试作弊而严厉批评他。|近义词：criticize, chastise, lambaste, censure, reprimand, scold|反义词：praise, commend, laud|词性: verb

Etymology: From Latin *fustigāre* "to cudgel, beat," from *fustis* "stick, club."
