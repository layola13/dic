donot:/duː nɒt/ | aux. v. | 不要，别 | Do not is the contracted form of "do not," used to form negative sentences. It expresses prohibition or negation of an action. | Do not touch the hot stove. 不要碰热炉子。 |  Don't leave your valuables unattended.  不要把贵重物品放在无人看管的地方。 | synonyms: don't, refrain from, avoid | antonyms: do, allow, permit | verb
