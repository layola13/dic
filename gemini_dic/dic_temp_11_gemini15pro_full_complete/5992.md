ambushes:/ˈæmbʊʃɪz/
n.|埋伏，伏击|A surprise attack by people lying in wait in a concealed position.|The soldiers were killed in an ambush. 士兵们在一次伏击中丧生。|近义词：trap, snare, pitfall|反义词:open attack, fair fight|词性:名词(plural)
ambush:/ˈæmbʊʃ/
n.|埋伏，伏击|A surprise attack by people lying in wait in a concealed position.|The troops set up an ambush in the forest. 部队在森林里设置了埋伏。|近义词：trap, snare, pitfall|反义词:open attack, fair fight|词性:名词(singular)
ambush:/ˈæmbʊʃ/
v.|伏击，埋伏|Make a surprise attack on (someone) from a concealed position.|The rebels ambushed a convoy of government troops. 叛军伏击了政府军的一个车队。|近义词：waylay, entrap, surprise|反义词:confront, meet openly|词性:动词
