autogenetic:/ˌɔːtəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
自体发生的，自生的
Self-produced; self-generated; originating within the organism.
Autogenetic pressure within the rock caused it to fracture.  岩石内部的自生压力导致它断裂。
Synonyms: endogenous, innate, inherent
Antonyms: exogenous, acquired
词性: adjective
