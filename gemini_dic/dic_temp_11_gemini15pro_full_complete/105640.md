kingcraft:/ˈkɪŋkrɑːft/
noun
帝王术；治国之术；君主统治的技巧
The skill of ruling as a king; statesmanship.
King Henry VIII was a master of kingcraft, skillfully manipulating alliances to maintain his power.  亨利八世是帝王权术的大师，他巧妙地操纵联盟来维持自己的权力。
He learned the kingcraft from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了治国之术。
synonyms: statecraft, statesmanship, politics, diplomacy, governance
antonyms: misrule, tyranny, despotism
