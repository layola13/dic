gimmickery's:/ˈɡɪmɪkəriːz/| n.| 花招，噱头，诡计(的)| The possessive form of "gimmickery," referring to the tricks, gadgets, or devices used to attract attention or publicity, often in a deceptive or insincere way.  It implies ownership or association with these tactics.|The magician's gimmickery's success depended on misdirection.  魔术师的那些花招的成功取决于误导。|synonyms: tricks, shenanigans, deceptions, ruses, subterfuges|antonyms: honesty, sincerity, genuineness|noun
