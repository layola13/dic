budge:/bʌdʒ/
verb
移动; 让步
to move slightly; to change one's opinion
The heavy box wouldn't budge.  这个沉重的箱子一动不动。
He refused to budge on the price. 他拒绝在价格上让步。
近义词:shift, move, yield
反义词:resist, stay
词性:v.

budge:/bʌdʒ/
noun
移动; 轻微的变动
a slight movement; a small change of position or opinion
He hasn't made a budge toward finishing the project. 他在完成项目方面毫无进展。
After hours of negotiation, there wasn't a single budge from either side. 经过数小时的谈判，双方都没有丝毫让步。
近义词:shift, stir, concession
反义词:resistance, standstill
词性:n.
