nonretroactively: /ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊˈæktɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊˈæktɪvli/
| adv. | 不溯及既往地 |  Not applying to a period before a particular date or event; only applying from a particular date forward. | The new law will apply nonretroactively, meaning only offenses committed after its enactment will be punishable under it.  新法律将不溯及既往地适用，这意味着只有在其颁布后犯下的罪行才会受到惩罚。| synonym: prospectively, forward, henceforth | antonym: retroactively, retrospectively | adverb
