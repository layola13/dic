grievous:/ˈɡriːvəs/| adj.|严重的，令人悲伤的，极严重的|Causing or characterized by severe pain, suffering, or sorrow.  A grievous wound/loss/error.|例句：The earthquake caused grievous loss of life. 地震造成了严重的生命损失。

例句：She made a grievous error in judgment. 她犯了一个严重的判断错误。|近义词：severe, serious, grave, dreadful, terrible, awful, appalling, shocking|反义词：minor, slight, trivial, insignificant|词性:形容词
