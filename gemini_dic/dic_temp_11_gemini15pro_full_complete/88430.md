hispanicizes:/hɪˌspænɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使西班牙化，使具有西班牙文化特色
To make or become Hispanic in character.
Many cities in the Southwest have been hispanicized due to immigration. 西南部的许多城市由于移民而变得西班牙化。
