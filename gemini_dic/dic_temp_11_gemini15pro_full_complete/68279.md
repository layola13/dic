fakir:/fəˈkɪər/| n.|托钵僧，苦行僧|A Muslim or Hindu religious ascetic who lives solely on alms.|例句：The fakir sat cross-legged, meditating.  托钵僧盘腿坐着冥想。

例句：Fakirs are known for their feats of endurance. 苦行僧以其忍耐力而闻名。|近义词：ascetic, mendicant, holy man|反义词:materialist, hedonist|词性:名词
