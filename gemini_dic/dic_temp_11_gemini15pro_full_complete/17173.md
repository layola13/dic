battology:/bəˈtɒlədʒi/
noun
赘述，重复累赘
Needless repetition of words or ideas in speech or writing.
The politician's speech was filled with battology, repeating the same points over and over.  这位政治家的演讲充满了赘述，一遍又一遍地重复同样的观点。
The lengthy legal document was bogged down by battology. 这份冗长的法律文件因赘述而显得繁琐。
synonyms: redundancy, prolixity, verbosity, tautology, circumlocution
antonyms: conciseness, brevity, succinctness
