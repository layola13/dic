fronting:/ˈfrʌntɪŋ/ | n. |  前置; 置于句首 | The process of moving a constituent from its normal position to the beginning of a sentence. |  Fronting the adverbial phrase gives the sentence a more dramatic effect.  前置状语短语使句子更具戏剧性。 |  forefronting, topicalization |  postponement | noun
fronting:/ˈfrʌntɪŋ/ | adj. | 前面的；正面的 |  Situated at the front. | The fronting wall of the house was covered in ivy. 房子的正面墙上爬满了常春藤。| frontal, anterior, forward | rear, back, posterior | adjective
