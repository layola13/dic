knobbier:/ˈnɑːbiər/ /ˈnɒbiə(r)/ | adj. | 更有节的，疙瘩更多的 | Having more or larger knobs; more knobby. | The old tree had a knobbier trunk than the young one. 老树的树干比小树的树干更有节。 | 近义词：gnarled, bumpy, lumpy | 反义词：smooth, even | 词性: 形容词

词源: From knob + -y + -er.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *knubuz (“knob, lump”).
