boomkin:/ˈbuːmkɪn/| n. |夜精灵德鲁伊的平衡形态（魔兽世界）,蠢货，乡巴佬|A boomkin is a shapeshifting form for balance druids in the game World of Warcraft, often depicted as a large, owl-like creature. It can also be used as a derogatory term meaning a foolish or awkward person, particularly someone from a rural area. |例句：The boomkin swooped down and cast Starfall. 夜精灵德鲁伊俯冲下来施放了星落。

例句：He called him a boomkin, but he didn't mean it offensively. 他叫他乡巴佬，但他并没有恶意。|近义词：owlbear, yokel, simpleton|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:noun
