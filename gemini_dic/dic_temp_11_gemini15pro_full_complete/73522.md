foreshortened:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtnd/ /fɔːrˈʃɔːrtn̩d/
adjective
缩短的; (因透视关系)显得缩短的
appearing shorter than its actual length, as the result of being viewed at an angle
The artist skillfully foreshortened the outstretched arm in the painting.  这位艺术家巧妙地将画中伸出的手臂进行了透视缩短。
Her legs looked foreshortened in the photograph. 在照片中，她的腿看起来很短。
shortened, contracted, compressed
elongated, extended
adjective
