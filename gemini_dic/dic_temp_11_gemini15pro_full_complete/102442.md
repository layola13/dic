jacketed:/ˈdʒækɪtɪd/
adjective
穿夹克的；装在套子里的
covered or protected with a jacket or casing.
The scientist carefully examined the jacketed beaker. 科学家仔细检查了带套beaker。
The jacketed cable protected the wires inside from damage.  带护套的电缆保护里面的电线免受损坏。
近义词:covered, encased, sheathed
反义词:bare, exposed
词性:adjective

词源: From jacket + -ed, ultimately from Old French jaquet.
