hints:/hɪnts/ | n. |提示，线索，暗示|A hint is a subtle suggestion or clue, often indirect or implied, that is intended to guide someone towards understanding or discovering something.|She gave me a hint about the surprise party. 她给了我关于惊喜派对的提示。|synonyms: clue, suggestion, intimation, tip, indication|antonyms: explicit statement, direct statement|noun

/hɪnts/ | v. |暗示，示意|To hint is to suggest or indicate something indirectly or subtly.|He hinted that he might be leaving his job. 他暗示他可能会辞职。|synonyms: suggest, imply, intimate, allude, insinuate|antonyms: state, declare, announce|verb
