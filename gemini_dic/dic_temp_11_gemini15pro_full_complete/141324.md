noun
开场白; 开局; 开瓶器; (尤指板球、棒球比赛的)首发队员;
Something that opens or is used for opening, such as a device for opening tins or bottles, or a remark or act that begins a conversation or other interaction.
The comedian's openers were hilarious.  这位喜剧演员的开场白非常搞笑。
The team's openers scored a century in the first innings.  该队的首发队员在第一局就得分过百。
synonyms: beginnings, introductions, starts, initiations, leads, preludes, curtain-raisers, icebreakers
antonyms: closings, endings, conclusions, finales
