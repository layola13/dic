hydrophobic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdrəˈfɒbɪk/ (UK)| adj.|疏水的，憎水的|Tending to repel or fail to mix with water.|Oil is hydrophobic and does not mix with water. 油是疏水的，不会与水混合。|The lotus leaf's hydrophobic surface allows water to bead and roll off. 荷叶的疏水表面使水珠聚集并滚落。|近义词：water-repellent, nonpolar|反义词：hydrophilic, water-loving|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From hydro- (water) + -phobic (fearing), from Greek *hudrophobos* (fearing water), from *hudōr* (water) + *phobos* (fear).
