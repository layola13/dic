flatline:/ˈflætlaɪn/| n. v.|平线；死亡；毫无进展|A flat line on a medical monitor indicates no heartbeat or brain activity.  It can also refer to a period of no progress or activity in other contexts.|The patient's heart rate flatlined. 病人的心率平线了。|近义词：cease, stop, halt, stagnate|反义词:recover, improve, rise, surge|词性:名词, 动词

flatline:/ˈflætlaɪn/| v.|变得毫无进展；死亡；(使)停止跳动|(Of a business, project, etc.) to cease to make progress or become unsuccessful. (Of a person) to die. (Of a person's heart) to stop beating.|Sales have flatlined in recent months. 近几个月销售额毫无进展。|近义词：cease, stop, halt, stagnate|反义词:recover, improve, rise, surge|词性:动词

词源:  flatline 源自"flat"（平的）和"line"（线），指在心电图或其他医疗监测仪器上显示的平直线，表示没有生命体征。后来，这个词被引申用来指任何事物停止进展或毫无活动的状态。
