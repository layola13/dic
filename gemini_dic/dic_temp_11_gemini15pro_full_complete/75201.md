frockless:/ˈfrɒklɪs/
adjective
无礼服的，未穿礼服的
Not wearing a frock; without a frock.
The frockless children played in the garden.  无礼服的孩子们在花园里玩耍。
She felt a little awkward attending the formal event frockless. 她觉得没穿礼服参加正式活动有点尴尬。
近义词:dressless, undressed, informally dressed
反义词:frocked, dressed formally
词性:adjective
