caucused:/ˈkɔːkəst/| v.|参加（或举行）政党秘密会议|The Republicans caucused last night to select their candidate. 共和党人昨晚举行秘密会议，挑选他们的候选人。|近义词：confer, meet, convene|反义词:disperse, separate|verb

Etymology->
From caucus (n.), from Algonquian (probably Powhatan) *cau·cau·asu 'advisor, counselor'.
