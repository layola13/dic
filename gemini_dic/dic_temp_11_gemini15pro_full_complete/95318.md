例句：The incavation on the surface of the sculpture added depth and texture to the artwork.  雕塑表面的凹陷为艺术品增添了深度和质感。|近义词：depression, hollow, cavity, pit, indentation|反义词：protrusion, bulge, convexity|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incavatio, from Latin incavare (“to hollow out”), from in- (“in, into”) + cavare (“to hollow”).
