assurednesses:/əˈʃʊədɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
确信；把握；自信
The state of being assured; certainty; confidence.
His assurednesses about the future of the company inspired the team.  他对公司未来的信心鼓舞了团队。
Near Synonyms:  certainties, confidences, assurances, convictions
Antonyms:  doubts, uncertainties, hesitations
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From assuredness + -es (plural suffix).  Assuredness comes from assured + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Assured is the past participle of the verb assure, from Old French assurer, from Latin ad- (to) + securus (secure).
