eliquation:/ɪˌlɪˈkweɪʃən/ | n. | 液化；熔化；淘析 | The process of separating a solid from a liquid by heating the mixture until the solid melts and flows away, or by washing out a soluble constituent. |  The gold was extracted from the ore by eliquation.  金是通过淘析从矿石中提取出来的。| 近义词：melting, liquefaction, leaching | 反义词：solidification, freezing | noun
