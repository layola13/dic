foretasting:/ˈfɔːrteɪstɪŋ/ | n. | 预尝，品尝 | The act of tasting something before it is served or consumed, often to assess its quality or suitability. |  The chef offered a foretaste of the new menu.  主厨提供了新菜单的预尝。| synonyms: preview, sampling, tasting | antonyms: N/A | noun

foretasting:/fɔːrˈteɪstɪŋ/ | adj. | 预示的，预先体验的 | Serving as a preview or anticipation of something to come. | The appetizer was a foretasting of the delicious meal to follow.  这道开胃菜预示着接下来美味的正餐。| synonyms: premonitory, anticipatory, foreshadowing | antonyms: N/A | adjective
