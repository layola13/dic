indeeds:/ɪnˈdiːdz/| adv.|的确，确实|Used to emphasize a statement or response.  It often expresses agreement, confirmation, or acknowledgment.|He is, indeeds, a very talented musician. 他确实是一位非常有才华的音乐家。|近义词：certainly, truly, indeed, actually, really, very|反义词：doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
词源:From Middle English *in dede*, from Old English *in dǣde* (“in deed, in fact”), equivalent to *in* +‎ *deed*.
