hangars:/ˈhæŋərz/ /ˈhæŋɡɑːrz/
n.|飞机库；棚厂|A large building for housing aircraft.|The helicopters were stored in the hangars. 直升机存放在机库里。|近义词：sheds, depots|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 open space)|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *hangard, of Germanic origin.
