imposition:/ɪmpəˈzɪʃən/
| n. |强加；征收；负担；欺骗|An act of imposing something (e.g., a tax, a rule, a burden, or oneself) on someone or something else.  The imposition of new taxes angered the citizens. 新税的征收激怒了市民。|Synonyms: burden, levy, tax, demand, onus, intrusion, infliction|Antonyms: relief, liberation, assistance, boon|noun

imposition:/ɪmpəˈzɪʃən/
| n. |欺骗；强迫接受|An act of falsely presenting oneself or something as genuine or worthy. He considered their request an imposition on his time. 他认为他们的请求浪费了他的时间。|Synonyms: deception, fraud, trickery, imposition|Antonyms: honesty, truthfulness, fairness, justice|noun
