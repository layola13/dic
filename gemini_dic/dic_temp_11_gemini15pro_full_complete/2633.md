adopter:/əˈdɑːptər/ | n. | 收养人；采用者；接受者 | Someone who adopts something, such as a child, an idea, or a new technology. |  例句：The adopters of the new technology were pleased with its efficiency. 新技术的采用者对其效率感到满意。

例句：She and her husband are hoping to become adopters. 她和她的丈夫希望成为收养人。 | 近义词：taker, receiver, supporter, user | 反义词：opponent, rejector | 词性：noun
