capitalina:/ˌkɑːpiːtɑːˈliːnə/
adjective
首都的；来自首都的；(尤指)墨西哥首都的
Relating to or characteristic of a capital city, especially Mexico City.
She is a capitalina, born and raised in Mexico City. 她是一位首都人，在墨西哥城出生和长大。
The capitalina dialect has some unique characteristics. 首都方言有一些独特的特征。
近义词: metropolitan, city-dwelling
反义词: rural, provincial
词性: adjective

capitalina:/ˌkɑːpiːtɑːˈliːnə/
noun
首都人；(尤指)墨西哥城居民
A resident of a capital city, especially of Mexico City.
Many capitalinas enjoy the vibrant cultural life of the city. 很多首都居民享受着这座城市充满活力的文化生活。
As a capitalina, she is used to the hustle and bustle. 作为一名首都居民，她习惯了熙熙攘攘的都市生活。
近义词: citizen, resident, inhabitant
反义词: villager, countryman
词性: noun
