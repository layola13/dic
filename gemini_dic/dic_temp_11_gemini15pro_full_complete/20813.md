blassé:/blɑːˈzeɪ/
adjective
厌倦的，冷漠的，玩世不恭的
Unimpressed or indifferent to something because one has experienced or seen it so often before.
She was blassé about the dangers of the trip, having travelled extensively in the past. 她对旅行的危险漠不关心，因为她过去旅行经验丰富。
近义词: apathetic, indifferent, jaded, world-weary
反义词: enthusiastic, excited, passionate
词性: adjective

blassé:/blɑːˈzeɪ/
noun
玩世不恭的人
A person who is unimpressed or indifferent to something because one has experienced or seen it so often before.  (Less common usage)
He's become a bit of a blassé about everything after living in the city for so long.  在城市里生活了这么久，他对一切都变得有点玩世不恭了。 (Note: This usage is less grammatically standard but can be found informally.)
近义词: cynic, worldling
反义词: enthusiast, innocent
词性: noun (less common)
