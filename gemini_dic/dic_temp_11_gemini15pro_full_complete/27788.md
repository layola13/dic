cannily:/ˈkænɪli/| adv.|精明地；狡猾地；谨慎地|In a clever and shrewd way, often in a way that suggests great skill in achieving something. |She cannily negotiated a pay raise. 她精明地谈妥了加薪。|近义词：astutely, shrewdly, cleverly, craftily, artfully|反义词：foolishly, naively, clumsily|词性:副词

词源:
From "canny" + "-ly."  "Canny" comes from Scots, ultimately from Old English "cen(n)" meaning "knowledge, understanding, declaration."
