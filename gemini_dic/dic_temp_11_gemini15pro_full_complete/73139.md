foraminifer:/ˌfɔːrəˈmɪnɪfər/ |n.|有孔虫|A single-celled planktonic animal with a perforated shell through which pseudopods extend.  These shells form an important part of the ocean floor sediment.|Foraminifers are abundant in the fossil record. 有孔虫在化石记录中很丰富。|Synonyms: foraminiferan, foraminifera|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

foraminifera:/ˌfɔːrəˈmɪnɪfərə/ |n.|有孔虫类|A plural form of foraminifer, referring to the group of single-celled planktonic animals with perforated shells.|Foraminifera play a key role in marine ecosystems. 有孔虫类在海洋生态系统中起着关键作用。|Synonyms: foraminifers, foraminiferans|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

foraminiferan:/ˌfɔːrəˈmɪnɪfərən/ |n. |有孔虫|Another singular or plural form referring to a foraminifer. |The foraminiferan shells were analyzed for their chemical composition. 对有孔虫壳进行了化学成分分析。|Synonyms: foraminifer, foraminifera|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
