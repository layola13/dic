idylls: /ˈɪdɪlz/, /ˈaɪdɪlz/ | n. | 田园诗; 牧歌; 浪漫插曲 | A short poem or other piece of writing describing a peaceful and happy scene, usually in the countryside. |  The poet's idylls celebrated the beauty of nature. 这位诗人的田园诗歌颂了自然之美。

The novel includes several charming idylls of country life. 这部小说包含了几段迷人的乡村生活插曲。| 近义词: pastorals, bucolics, romances | 反义词: realities, tragedies | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *idylle*, from Late Latin *idylium*, from Greek *eidyllion*, diminutive of *eidos* "form, shape." Originally referring to a short, descriptive poem, often depicting rustic life.
