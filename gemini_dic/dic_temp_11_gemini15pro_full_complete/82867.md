gullible:/ˈɡʌləbl/| adj.|易受骗的；轻信的|Easily persuaded to believe something; credulous.|He was so gullible that he believed her every word. 他太容易受骗了，竟然相信她说的每一句话。|近义词：credulous, naive, trusting|反义词：suspicious, skeptical, cynical|词性: 形容词

词源:
From gull (verb) + -ible.  "Gull" in this sense means "to cheat or deceive," likely related to the bird's scavenging habits.
