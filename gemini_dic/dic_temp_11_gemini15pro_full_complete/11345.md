arenation: /ˌeɪrəˈneɪʃən/
n.
竞技；角斗；公开辩论
The act of competing or struggling, especially in public.
The candidates engaged in a fierce arenation during the debate. 候选人在辩论中进行了激烈的交锋。
近义词: competition, contest, rivalry, struggle
反义词: cooperation, collaboration, harmony, peace
词性: 名词

词源: From Latin "arena" (sand, place of combat) + "-ation" (suffix forming nouns of action or process).  源自拉丁语“arena”（沙子，战斗场所）+“-ation”（构成动作或过程名词的后缀）。
