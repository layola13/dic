karabiner:/ˌkærəˈbiːnər/| n.|登山扣，安全扣，快挂|A metal loop with a spring-loaded gate used for clipping ropes and other equipment together, especially in climbing and mountaineering.|The climber clipped his karabiner to the rope. 登山者把他的登山扣扣在绳子上。|近义词：clip, snap link, quickdraw (when used with a sling)|反义词: N/A|noun
karabiner:/ˌkærəˈbiːnər/| n.|卡宾枪|A type of carbine, a short-barreled rifle.|The soldier carried a karabiner. 士兵携带了一支卡宾枪。|近义词：carbine, rifle|反义词: N/A|noun
