autopilots:/ˈɔːtəˌpaɪləts/ | n. | 自动驾驶仪；自动舵 | A device that automatically steers ships, aircraft, and spacecraft. | The airplane was flying on autopilot. 飞机正以自动驾驶模式飞行。 | The ship's autopilot malfunctioned during the storm.  这艘船的自动驾驶仪在暴风雨中发生故障。 | synonyms: automatic pilot, self-steering gear | antonyms: manual control | noun
