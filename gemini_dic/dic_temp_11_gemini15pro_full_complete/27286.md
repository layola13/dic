calumnying: /kəˈlʌmniɪŋ/ | v. | 诽谤，中伤 | The act of making false and defamatory statements about someone. | He was calumnying his political rivals. 他在诽谤他的政治对手。 |  She was calumnying her ex-husband to anyone who would listen. 她逢人就诽谤她的前夫。 |近义词: slandering, defaming, libeling, vilifying | 反义词: praising, extolling, lauding | Verb (present participle)


calumny: /ˈkæləmni/ | n. | 诽谤，中伤 |  A false and defamatory statement made about someone. | The accusations against him were nothing but calumny. 对他的指控纯属诽谤。 |  He spread calumny about his colleagues to try and get promoted. 他散布关于同事的诽谤，试图获得晋升。 | 近义词: slander, defamation, libel, vilification | 反义词: praise, commendation, acclaim | Noun
