acquaints:/əˈkweɪnts/
verb
使认识；使了解；使熟悉
Makes someone aware of or familiar with something.
He acquainted himself with the rules of the game. 他熟悉了游戏规则。
familiarizes, informs, introduces
estranges, alienates
