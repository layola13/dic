bobbies:/ˈbɒbiz/| n.|警察（非正式，通常指英国警察，复数形式）|A colloquial, often humorous, term for police officers, especially in Britain.  It is the plural of "bobby".|例句：The bobbies were on patrol. 警察正在巡逻。

例句：Look out, the bobbies are coming! 小心，警察来了！

近义词：police officers, cops, constables|反义词:criminals, lawbreakers|词性:名词

词源：Bobby来源于Robert Peel, 他是19世纪英国的政治家，建立了伦敦警察厅。因此，"Bobby" 和 "Peeler" (另一个对英国警察的称呼) 都源于他的名字。
