choreography:/ˌkɔːriˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 编舞，舞蹈设计 | The art or practice of designing sequences of movements of physical bodies (or their depictions) in which motion, form, or both are specified. | The choreography of the ballet was breathtaking. 芭蕾舞的编舞令人叹为观止。| synonyms: dance composition, dance arrangement | antonyms: improvisation, extemporization | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chorégraphie*, from Ancient Greek *χορεία* (khoreía, “choral dance”) + *γράφειν* (gráphein, “to write”).
