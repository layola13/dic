neutrals:/ˈnuːtrəlz/| n.| 中立国；中立者；中性色；空挡|A neutral is a person or country that does not support or help either side in a war, disagreement, etc.  |Switzerland remained neutral during World War II. 瑞士在二战期间保持中立。|Synonyms: noncombatants, nonbelligerents|Antonyms: belligerents, combatants|noun

neutrals:/ˈnuːtrəlz/| n.| 中性色；素净色|Neutrals are colors that are not bright or strong. |She prefers to decorate with neutrals. 她更喜欢用中性色来装饰。|Synonyms: muted colors, subtle colors|Antonyms: bright colors, vibrant colors|noun

neutrals:/ˈnuːtrəlz/| n.| （汽车或其他机器的）空挡|Neutral is the position of the gears in a vehicle or other machine when they are not connected to the engine. |Put the car in neutral. 把汽车挂到空挡。|Synonyms: idle, disengaged|Antonyms: engaged, in gear|noun
