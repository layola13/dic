off-the-hook:/ˌɔːf ðə ˈhʊk/ | adj. | 脱钩的；摆脱困境的；免受责备的 | Free from blame, responsibility, or difficulty; no longer in trouble or danger. |  Example: He was off the hook for the crime after the police found the real culprit. 他在警方找到真凶后，摆脱了罪责。

Example:  I finally got off the hook for organizing the office party. 我终于不用再负责组织办公室派对了。

近义词: free, clear, exempt, released, absolved | 反义词: responsible, liable, guilty, trapped | 词性: 形容词
