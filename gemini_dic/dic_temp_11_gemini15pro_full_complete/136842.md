nonsimilitude:/ˌnɒnsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of resemblance; dissimilarity
The nonsimilitude between the twins surprised everyone. 双胞胎之间的不像让每个人都感到惊讶。
nonsimilitude between the two designs was striking. 两种设计之间的差异很明显。
Synonyms: difference, dissimilarity, disparity, divergence, unlikeness, variation
Antonyms: resemblance, similarity, likeness, similitude
