fraudulently:/ˈfrɔːdjələntli/
adv.
欺诈地
in a way that involves deception, dishonesty, or trickery.
He fraudulently obtained a visa. 他以欺骗手段获得了签证。
近义词：dishonestly, deceitfully, illegally
反义词：honestly, legally, truthfully
词性: 副词

词源:
From Latin *fraudulent-, from fraudulent-us ‘deceitful’, from fraud- ‘deceit’.
