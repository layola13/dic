noninflammably:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflæməbli/
adverb
不易燃地，非炎症地
in a way that does not easily catch fire; in a way that does not cause or relate to inflammation.
The material was treated to make it noninflammably.  这种材料经过处理，使其不易燃。
The wound healed noninflammably.  伤口无炎症地愈合了。
Near Synonyms: non-combustibly, fire-resistantly
Near Antonyms: inflammably, combustibly
词性: 副词
