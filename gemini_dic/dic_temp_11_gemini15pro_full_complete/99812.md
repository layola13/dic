interminabilities:/ɪnˌtɜːrmɪnəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
无限; 没完没了; 永无休止

The speaker droned on about the interminabilities of the bureaucratic process. 演讲者喋喋不休地谈论着官僚程序的无休止性。
近义词: endlessness, perpetuity, infinity
反义词: finiteness, brevity, transience
词性: 名词 (plural)

interminable:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/
adj.
无休止的; 没完没了的; 冗长的
Endless or apparently endless; too long.
The journey seemed interminable. 旅途似乎漫无止境。
近义词: endless, incessant, everlasting
反义词: finite, brief, short
词性: 形容词
