divisions: /dɪˈvɪʒənz/
n. | 分割，划分；部门；分歧；除法 | The act or process of dividing something into parts; a separate part of an organization or group; a disagreement or difference of opinion between people; the mathematical operation of finding how many times one number is contained within another. | The company has several divisions, each specializing in a different product.  这家公司有几个部门，每个部门专门生产不同的产品。 | synonyms: sections, departments, branches, segments, partitions, splits, disagreements, differences, schisms | antonyms: unions, mergers, agreements, accords | noun

divisions: /dɪˈvɪʒənz/
n. | 分界线 | A boundary line; a dividing line. | The river forms a natural division between the two countries. 这条河形成了两国之间的天然分界线。 | synonyms: boundaries, borders, demarcation lines | antonyms: connections, links | noun
