overfiercely: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪrsli/
adverb
过度激烈地；过于凶猛地
With excessive fierceness or ferocity.
The dog barked overfiercely at the stranger, causing the child to cry.  这只狗对着陌生人狂吠不止，把孩子都吓哭了。
The warrior fought overfiercely, exhausting himself quickly.  这名战士打得太猛，很快就精疲力竭了。
近义词: excessively fiercely, too fiercely, unduly fiercely
反义词: mildly, gently, calmly
词性: adverb
