misreprinting:/ˌmɪsˌriːˈprɪntɪŋ/
noun
误印，错印
the act of printing something incorrectly, or something that has been printed incorrectly
The book had to be recalled due to a misreprinting of several pages.  这本书由于几页的印刷错误而不得不被召回。
近义词: misprint, typographical error, erratum
反义词: correct printing
词性: n.


misprint:/ˌmɪsˈprɪnt/
verb
印错
to print something incorrectly
They misprinted the date on the invitation. 他们在请柬上印错了日期。
近义词: print incorrectly
反义词: print correctly
词性: v.
