countervair:/ˌkaʊntərˈvɛər/| n. adj.|反面貂皮，以貂皮装饰的;|A fur, especially the fur of a squirrel, used in heraldry and dressmaking, having patches of contrasting colors. In modern usage, also refers to any fabric or material with a similar variegated pattern.|The queen wore a robe of luxurious countervair.|近义词:variegated, mottled, pied|反义词:uniform, solid|noun, adjective
