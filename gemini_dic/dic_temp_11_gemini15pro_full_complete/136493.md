nonrival:/nɒnˈraɪvl/| adj.|非竞争性的，非对抗性的|Not subject to rivalry; not diminished by others' consumption or use.  Something that can be enjoyed or consumed by multiple individuals without reducing the availability or benefit for others.|例句：Clean air is a nonrival good.  干净的空气是一种非竞争性商品。

例句：Public parks are often considered nonrival, as one person's enjoyment doesn't diminish another's. 公共公园通常被认为是非竞争性的，因为一个人的享受不会减少另一个人的享受。|近义词：non-excludable, public, shared|反义词：rivalrous, private, excludable|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix) + rival (noun)

"non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not."
"rival" comes from Latin "rivalis," meaning "of or belonging to a riverbank," later evolving to mean "one using the same river (as another)," which led to the sense of competition.
