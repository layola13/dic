eyesights:/ˈaɪˌsaɪts/| n.|视力，视野|The ability to see; vision.  Multiple instances of the ability to see, often used in a literary or slightly archaic way.|He had remarkably keen eyesights, spotting the hawk high in the sky. 他视力极佳，发现了高空中的鹰。|近义词：vision, sight, eyesight (singular)|反义词: blindness|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *eyesight*, from *eye* + *sight*.  The plural form is less common than the singular *eyesight*.
