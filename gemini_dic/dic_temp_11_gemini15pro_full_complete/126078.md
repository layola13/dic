mustiness's:/ˈmʌstɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|霉味；霉烂；陈腐|The mustiness's of the old books filled the library.  图书馆里弥漫着旧书的霉味。|The mustiness's of the basement was overwhelming. 地下室的霉味令人难以忍受。|近义词:moldiness, fustiness, dampness|反义词:freshness, sweetness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From musty + -ness + -'s (possessive).  Musty comes from Middle English *musti, from Old French *musti, ultimately of uncertain origin.
