multiform:/ˈmʌltɪfɔːrm/
adjective
多种形式的；多样的
having many forms or appearances.
例句：The artist's multiform sculptures were a testament to her versatility. 这位艺术家的多形态雕塑证明了她的多才多艺。

例句：The protein exists in several multiform states. 这种蛋白质以几种不同的形式存在。

近义词：versatile, varied, diverse, multifaceted, polymorphic
反义词：uniform, homogeneous, consistent, singular
词性：adjective
