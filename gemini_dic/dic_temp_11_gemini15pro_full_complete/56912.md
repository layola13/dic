dotard:/ˈdoʊtərd/| n.| 老糊涂；年老昏聩的人|An old person, especially one who is weak and confused.|The king, once a wise ruler, had become a dotard in his old age. 国王曾经是一位英明的统治者，但到了老年却变成了一个老糊涂。|Synonyms: senile, decrepit, feebleminded|Antonyms: astute, sharp, sagacious|noun
