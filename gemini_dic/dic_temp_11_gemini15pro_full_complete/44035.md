crimsonness:/ˈkrɪmzənnɪs/| n.| 深红色，绯红色 |The crimsonness of the sunset was breathtaking.  夕阳的深红色令人叹为观止。|近义词：crimson, redness, scarlet |反义词: paleness, whiteness |词性:名词
Etymology: From crimson + -ness.  Crimson comes from the Old Spanish "cremesin," ultimately from the Arabic "qirmiz," referring to the kermes insect, from which a red dye was obtained.
