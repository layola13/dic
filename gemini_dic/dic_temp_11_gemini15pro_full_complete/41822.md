coruscant:/ˈkɔːrəskənt/ /ˌkɔːrəˈskænt/| adj.| 闪耀的，光辉灿烂的| Gleaming or flashing with intermittent brilliance.| The coruscant jewels glittered under the spotlights. 闪耀的珠宝在聚光灯下闪闪发光。|  The skyline was coruscant with lights. 天际线灯火辉煌。| synonyms: glittering, sparkling, gleaming, flashing, shimmering, radiant, luminous | antonyms: dull, dim, dark, lackluster | adjective
