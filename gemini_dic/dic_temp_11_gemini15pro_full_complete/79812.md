glycol:/ˈɡlaɪkɒl/ (US) /ˈɡlaɪkɔːl/ (UK)| n. |乙二醇，甘醇 |A viscous, sweet-tasting, colorless liquid, the simplest of the diols, used as an antifreeze and in the manufacture of polyester fibers and resins.| Ethylene glycol is commonly used as antifreeze in car radiators. 乙二醇通常用作汽车散热器中的防冻剂。| Ethylene glycol is used in the production of polyester fibers. 乙二醇用于生产聚酯纤维。| synonyms: 1,2-ethanediol,  dihydroxyethane | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound, but substances with opposite functions might be considered, such as anti-boiling agents) | noun
