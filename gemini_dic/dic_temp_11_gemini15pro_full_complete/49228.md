delegation:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|代表团，授权，委托|A group of people who are chosen to vote or act for someone else; the act of giving someone the authority to do something for you.|The delegation from China arrived in Washington D.C. yesterday.  中国代表团昨天抵达华盛顿特区。|The CEO gave the delegation to his vice president to negotiate the new contract. 首席执行官授权副总裁协商新合同。|synonyms: deputation, mission, assignment, commission|antonyms: retention, revocation, refusal|noun
