mispractice: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪs/ | n. |  渎职；玩忽职守；治疗失当 | Improper or negligent professional activity or treatment, especially by a medical practitioner, lawyer, or public official. | The doctor was sued for medical malpractice.  这位医生因医疗事故被起诉。|  The lawyer was accused of malpractice for failing to adequately represent his client.  这位律师因未能充分代表其客户而被指控玩忽职守。 | Synonyms: negligence, misconduct, dereliction of duty | Antonyms:  due diligence,  proper conduct | noun
