nonvenally:/ˌnɒnˈviːnəli/
adv. | 非静脉注射地；非通过贿赂地 | Not intravenously; not through bribery. | The medication was administered nonvenally.  这种药物是非静脉注射的。| The contract was awarded nonvenally, based purely on merit.  这份合同的授予并非通过贿赂，完全基于功绩。 | 近义词: ethically, morally, legitimately | 反义词: venally, corruptly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "venal" (capable of being bribed) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Venal" comes from Latin "venalis" (for sale), from "venus" (sale).
