domanial:/dəˈmeɪniəl/
adjective
领地的；土地所有制的；私有的
Relating to the private landed property of a sovereign; or relating to ownership of land.
The king retained domanial rights over the forest. 国王保留了对森林的土地所有权。
Synonyms:  proprietary, private, landed, territorial
Antonyms:  public, communal, shared
词性: adjective
