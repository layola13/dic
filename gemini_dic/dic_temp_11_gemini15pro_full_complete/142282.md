orsedew:/ˈɔːrsˌdjuː/ /ˈɔːrˌsuː/ | n. |马蹄露，马蹄草|A perennial plant (Alchemilla vulgaris) of the rose family, with yellowish-green flowers. Also called lady's mantle. The name comes from the belief that the plant could collect dew which had medicinal properties.|The orsedew glistened in the morning sun. 马蹄草在晨曦中闪闪发光。|近义词：lady's mantle|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *orsdewe, from Old English *ēarsþēaw, from ēar "ear" + þēaw "custom, habit," perhaps referring to the shape of the leaves.
