circumnavigate: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 环球航行，绕...航行 | To sail or travel completely around (something, especially the world). | Magellan was the first to circumnavigate the globe. 麦哲伦是第一个环球航行的人。

Ferdinand Magellan's expedition was the first to circumnavigate the Earth. 费迪南德·麦哲伦的探险队是第一个环球航行的。| 近义词: sail around, go around, circle | 反义词: - | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin circumnavigatus, past participle of circumnavigare (“to sail around”), from circum (“around”) + navigare (“to sail, navigate”).
