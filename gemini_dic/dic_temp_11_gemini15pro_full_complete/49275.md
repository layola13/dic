deliberating:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
仔细考虑，商议
engaging in long and careful consideration.
The jury is deliberating the verdict. 陪审团正在商议裁决。
The committee is deliberating on the proposal. 委员会正在仔细考虑这项提议。

近义词:considering, pondering, contemplating, discussing, debating
反义词:acting impulsively, rushing, deciding quickly
词性:Verb (present participle of deliberate)
