decriminalise: /ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/ | v. | 使合法化，非刑事化 |  To remove or reduce the criminal penalties associated with something. |  Many countries are considering decriminalising certain drug offences. 许多国家正在考虑将某些毒品犯罪非罪化。| legalize, authorize | criminalize | verb

Etymology:  de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + criminalize.  The word "criminalize" comes from the late 18th century, from "criminal" + "-ize." "Criminal" ultimately derives from the Latin word "criminālis."
