limitive:/ˈlɪmɪtɪv/
adjective
限制的，限定的
restricting or tending to restrict; confining.
The rules are too limitive; they don't allow for any creativity. 规则太限制性了，它们不允许任何创造力。
近义词: restrictive, confining, limiting
反义词: expansive, unlimited, unrestricted
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *līmītātīvus, from Latin līmītāre (to limit), from līmes (limit, boundary).
