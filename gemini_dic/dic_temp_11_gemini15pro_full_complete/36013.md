codified:/ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪd/| adj.|编纂的，成文的|arranged and recorded systematically|The rules of the game were codified in 1863.  这项游戏的规则在1863年被编纂成文。|Synonyms: systematized, organized, formulated|Antonyms: unorganized, unstructured, informal|adjective

codify:/ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪ/| vt.|编纂，整理|to arrange (laws, rules, etc.) into a systematic code|The convention codified the rules of war. 该公约编纂了战争规则。|Synonyms: systematize, organize, formulate|Antonyms: disorganize, disorder|verb
