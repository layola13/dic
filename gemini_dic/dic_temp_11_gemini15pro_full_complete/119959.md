millenarism:/ˌmɪləˈnɛərɪzəm/| n. |千禧年主义，千年至福说|The belief that Christ will reign on earth for a thousand years before the final judgment.  A doctrine or belief in a future golden age of peace, righteousness, and prosperity.|例句：Millenarism is a central tenet of some Christian denominations.  千禧年主义是一些基督教派的核心教义。

例句：The preacher's sermons often focused on themes of millenarism.  这位传教士的布道经常集中在千禧年主义的主题上。|近义词：chiliasm, millennialism|反义词: - |noun
