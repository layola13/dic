bedsores:/ˈbedˌsɔːrz/ (US), /ˈbedˌsɔːz/ (UK)| n. |褥疮; 压疮|Sores that develop on areas of the skin exposed to prolonged pressure, especially in bedridden or immobile individuals.  They are caused by restricted blood flow to the affected area.|The bedridden patient developed bedsores on his heels.  卧床不起的病人脚后跟长了褥疮。|Synonyms: pressure sores, decubitus ulcers|Antonyms: healthy skin|Noun
