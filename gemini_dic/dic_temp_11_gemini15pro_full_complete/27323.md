calyx:/ˈkeɪlɪks/ (US), /ˈkælɪks/ (UK)| n. |花萼|The calyx is the outer part of a flower, enclosing the petals before they open. 花萼是花的外部部分，在花瓣开放之前将其包裹。|The botanist carefully examined the calyx of the rare orchid.  植物学家仔细检查了珍稀兰花的花萼。|The calyx protected the delicate bud from the harsh weather. 花萼保护娇嫩的花蕾免受恶劣天气的侵害。|synonyms: sepals (collectively), outer perianth|antonyms: corolla|noun
