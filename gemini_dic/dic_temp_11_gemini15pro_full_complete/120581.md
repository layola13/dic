mirky:/ˈmɜːrki/ (US) /ˈmɜːki/ (UK)| adj.|黑暗的，阴暗的；浑浊的，模糊不清的|Dark and gloomy, or thick and unclear.|The streetlights cast a murky glow on the wet pavement. 街灯在潮湿的路面上投下昏暗的光芒。|近义词：dark, gloomy, obscure, dim, cloudy, muddy|反义词：bright, clear, transparent|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *mirke*, from Old English *myrce* (“dark, murky”), from Proto-Germanic *murkaz* (“dark”), from Proto-Indo-European *merk- (“dark”).  Related to Old Norse *myrkr* (“darkness”).
