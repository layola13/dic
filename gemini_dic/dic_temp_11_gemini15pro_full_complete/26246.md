buttery:/ˈbʌtəri/ (US), /ˈbʌtəri/ (UK)| adj.|黄油味的；像黄油的；含黄油多的；油腻的；奉承的；谄媚的|Resembling or containing butter; rich and smooth like butter; flattering or insincere.  It can refer to the taste, texture, or sometimes even the appearance of something. It can also describe someone who is excessively flattering, often in a way that is perceived as insincere. |The croissant had a light, buttery flavor. 这羊角面包有淡淡的黄油味。|Synonyms: rich, creamy, smooth, unctuous, oily, greasy, flattering, insincere|Antonyms: dry, lean, bland, sincere, genuine|Adjective
