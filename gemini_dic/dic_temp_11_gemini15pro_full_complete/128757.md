niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| v. n.|烦恼；挑剔；吹毛求疵；(使)隐隐作痛|To cause slight but persistent annoyance, discomfort, or anxiety. As a noun, it refers to a slight but persistent annoyance, discomfort, or anxiety.|The loose thread on my sweater kept niggling at me.  我的毛衣上松散的线一直困扰着我。|Synonyms: bother, irritate, annoy, worry, fret, pick at|Antonyms: soothe, comfort, please, delight|verb

niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| n.|小抱怨；小问题；(身体的)轻微不适|A slight but persistent annoyance, discomfort, or anxiety.|I had a niggle in my back all day. 我背部一整天都隐隐作痛。|Synonyms: worry, qualm, doubt, misgiving, reservation|Antonyms: assurance, certainty, confidence|noun
