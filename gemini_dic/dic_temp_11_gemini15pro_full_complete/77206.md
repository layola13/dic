gasteromycetes:/ˌɡæstərəˈmaɪsiːtiːz/ | n. | 腹菌纲 | A group of fungi, now considered an artificial assemblage, that don't actively discharge their spores, but instead form a fruiting body within which spores are produced, like puffballs and earthstars.  | Example: Gasteromycetes, such as puffballs, are fascinating fungi. 腹菌纲，例如马勃，是一种迷人的真菌。 | Example:  The stinkhorn mushroom is a type of gasteromycete known for its foul odor.  鬼笔鹅膏是一种以其恶臭而闻名的腹菌纲真菌。 | Synonym:  gastromycete (singular), stomach fungi | Antonym:  N/A | noun
