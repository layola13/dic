anorexia: /ˌænəˈrɛksiə/ (US), /ˌænəˈrɛksɪə/ (UK)| n. |厌食症；食欲减退|An emotional disorder characterized by an obsessive desire to lose weight by refusing to eat.|She was diagnosed with anorexia after losing a significant amount of weight.|Synonyms: anorexia nervosa, inanition|Antonyms: bulimia, gluttony|noun


anorexic: /ˌænəˈrɛksɪk/ (US), /ˌænəˈrɛksɪk/ (UK)| adj. |患厌食症的；食欲不振的|Suffering from or relating to anorexia.|She looked anorexic after months of dieting.|Synonyms: emaciated, gaunt, thin|Antonyms: obese, overweight|adjective
