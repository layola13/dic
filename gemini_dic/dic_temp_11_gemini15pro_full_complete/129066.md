nitroform:/ˈnaɪtrəfɔːrm/
noun
硝仿，三硝基甲烷
A colorless, oily, highly explosive liquid, CH(NO2)3, used as an oxidizing agent.
Nitroform is a powerful oxidizing agent. 硝仿是一种强氧化剂。
Nitroform can be used in the synthesis of explosives. 硝仿可用于炸药的合成。

synonyms: trinitromethane
