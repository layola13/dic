dispensatorily:/dɪspɛnˈsəˈtɔːrɪli/ | adv. | 免除地，豁免地 | In a way that exempts or releases someone from an obligation or requirement. |  He was dispensatorily excused from jury duty due to his illness. 他因病被免除陪审团的义务。| synonym: exemptly, preferentially | antonym: obligatorily, compulsorily | word type: adverb

dispensatory:/dɪˈspɛnsətɔːri/ | adj. |  豁免的，免除的| Relating to dispensing or distributing, or having the power to dispense or distribute. Can also mean related to a dispensary (a place where medicine or medical treatment is dispensed). | The king has dispensatory powers in matters of law. 国王在法律事务上有豁免权。  | synonym: exempting, releasing | antonym: obligatory, compulsory | word type: adjective
