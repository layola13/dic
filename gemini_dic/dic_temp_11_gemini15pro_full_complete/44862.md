crutching:/ˈkrʌtʃɪŋ/
n.|拄拐行走，支撑；依赖|The act of walking with crutches or relying on something for support.|He was crutching along the sidewalk after his accident. 他事故后拄着拐杖沿人行道行走。|Synonyms: limping, hobbling|Antonyms: walking, running
v.|拄着拐杖行走，支撑；依赖|Walking with crutches or relying on something for support.|She is still crutching after her knee surgery. 她膝盖手术后仍在拄拐杖行走。|Synonyms: leaning, depending|Antonyms: independent, self-sufficient
词性: 名词/动词

词源：来自名词 crutch (拐杖) + -ing (动名词/现在分词后缀)。 Crutch 源自古英语 crycc，与中古荷兰语 crucke 和古高地德语 krukka 同源，可能源自原始日耳曼语 *krukjo，模仿拐杖发出的声音。
