bordman:/ˈbɔːrdmən/ | n. | 膳食管理员，膳食承包商 | A person who provides or manages meals, typically in an institutional setting. |  The bordman at the school ensured all the children received a nutritious lunch. 学校的膳食管理员确保所有孩子都吃上了营养的午餐。| caterer, steward |  N/A | noun

Etymology:  "bord" (Old English) meaning "table" + "man" (Old English).  Originally referred to a person who attended to a table, evolving to someone who manages meals.
