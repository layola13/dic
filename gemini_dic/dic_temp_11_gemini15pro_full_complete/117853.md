mellownesses:/ˈmɛloʊnɪsɪz/
n.
成熟；柔和；圆润；醇厚
the quality of being mellow; ripeness, softness, smoothness, richness.
The mellownesses of the aged cheese delighted the connoisseur.  这块陈年奶酪的醇厚口感让鉴赏家欣喜不已。
近义词:ripeness, maturity, softness, smoothness, richness
反义词:harshness, acidity, sharpness, immaturity
词性:名词(plural noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mellow, from Old English mearu (“soft, tender, delicate, mellow”), from Proto-Germanic *marwaz (“soft, rotten”), from Proto-Indo-European *mṛh₂ús (“soft, fragile”). Cognate with Old Frisian mar (“tender, weak”), Dutch murw (“tender, mellow”), German mürbe (“tender, fragile”), Old Norse mjúkr (“soft”).  More at marrow. The suffix -ness forms abstract nouns from adjectives.
