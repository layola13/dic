hyperemotionally:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəli/
adverb
过度情绪化地; 过分感情用事地
in a way that shows or involves much stronger emotions than are usual or suitable.
She reacted hyperemotionally to the news. 她对这个消息反应过度情绪化。
Near Antonyms: calmly, impassively, placidly, serenely, tranquilly
Antonyms: unemotionally
