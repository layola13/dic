hyperstimulated:/ˌhaɪpərˈstɪmjuleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度刺激的
Excessively or abnormally stimulated.  This often refers to a physiological response, particularly in the context of medical treatments or hormonal changes.
The patient's ovaries were hyperstimulated from the fertility drugs.  病人的卵巢由于生育药物而过度刺激。

Her senses were hyperstimulated by the bright lights and loud music.  她的感官被明亮的灯光和嘈杂的音乐过度刺激了。

近义词:overstimulated, overexcited
反义词:understimulated, unresponsive
词性:adjective
词源:From hyper- "over, excessive" + stimulated, past participle of stimulate "to excite (a nerve, muscle, etc.) to its functional activity."
