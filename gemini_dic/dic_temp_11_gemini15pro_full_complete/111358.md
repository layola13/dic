livens:/ˈlaɪvənz/| v.|使活跃，使生动；使更有趣|Makes something more lively, interesting, or exciting.|The music livens up the party.  音乐使派对活跃起来。|Synonyms: animates, enlivens, brightens, invigorates|Antonyms: dulls, deadens, bores|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English liven, from Old English libban (“to live”), of Germanic origin. Related to German leben (“to live”). The suffix -en forms verbs from adjectives or nouns, meaning "to make or become".
