anticlassical:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəl/
adjective
反古典的，非古典的
Opposed to or rejecting the principles of classicism.
His anticlassical approach to composition often startled more traditional musicians. 他反古典的创作方法经常让更传统的音乐家感到吃惊。
近义词：modern, unconventional, nonconformist, iconoclastic
反义词：classical, traditional, conventional, orthodox
词性: adjective
