backtracks:/ˈbæktræks/ (US), /ˈbæktræks/ (UK)| v.|后退，折返，撤回，取消(原先说过的话); 倒退播放|To return along the same path or route one has just taken; to withdraw or reverse a statement or action.|例句：The hiker backtracked when he realized he was lost.  这名徒步旅行者意识到自己迷路后便折返了。

例句：The politician backtracked on his promise to raise taxes.  这位政客取消了他关于增税的承诺。|近义词：retreats, withdraws, reverses, retracts|反义词：advances, proceeds, continues|词性：动词

backtrack:/ˈbæktræk/ (US), /ˈbæktræk/ (UK)| n.|后退，折返的路线|The act of going back over the same route.|例句：The backtrack was difficult due to the dense undergrowth.  由于灌木丛生，折返的路线很艰难。

例句：We had to take a long backtrack to find the trail again. 我们不得不走很长一段回头路才能重新找到小路。|近义词：return, retreat|反义词：advance, progression|词性：名词
