outwrote:/ˌaʊtˈroʊt/
verb
比...写得多，写作胜过
to write more or better than someone else
Shakespeare outwrote all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的写作超过了他的所有同时代人。

近义词:surpassed, exceeded
反义词:was outwritten by
