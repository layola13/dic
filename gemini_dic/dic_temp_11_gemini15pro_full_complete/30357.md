centenaries: /senˈtiːnəriːz/ /senˈtenəriːz/
n.
一百周年纪念; 百年纪念
one hundredth anniversaries; celebrations of a hundredth anniversary.

The town celebrated its centennial with a parade and fireworks.  这个城镇以游行和烟花庆祝其百年诞辰。

The university marked its centenary with a series of lectures and events. 这所大学以一系列讲座和活动来纪念其百年校庆。

Synonym: centennials, anniversaries
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could consider beginnings, inceptions)
Noun
