aphanitic:/ˌæfəˈnɪtɪk/ | adj. | 隐晶质的；细密的 |  Relating to or denoting a rock texture in which the crystals are too small to be distinguished with the naked eye. | The aphanitic texture of the basalt indicated rapid cooling.  玄武岩的隐晶质结构表明其冷却速度很快。| fine-grained, microcrystalline | phaneritic, coarse-grained | adjective
