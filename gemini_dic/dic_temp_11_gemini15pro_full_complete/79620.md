词源:
From Middle English *glose, *glosse, from Old French glose, from Late Latin glossa (“a gloss, explanation of a hard word”), from Ancient Greek γλῶσσα (glôssa, “tongue, language, foreign language, obscure word needing explanation”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰengʷʰ- (“tongue”).
