arabber:/ˈærəbər/| n.|叫卖小贩，流动摊贩|A person who sells goods, typically produce, from a cart or wagon, often calling out to attract customers.  Originates from the historical practice of Arab traders selling goods in this manner.|The arabber's horse-drawn cart was piled high with fresh fruits and vegetables.|近义词：vendor, hawker, peddler, street vendor|反义词: customer, buyer|noun
