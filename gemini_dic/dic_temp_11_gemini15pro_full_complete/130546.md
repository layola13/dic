nonacoustical:/ˌnɒn əˈkuːstɪkəl/
adjective
非声学的
Not relating to sound or acoustics.
The architect focused on the nonacoustical properties of the materials, such as their fire resistance and durability.  建筑师专注于材料的非声学特性，例如它们的耐火性和耐久性。
The nonacoustical aspects of the theater's design, like the seating and lighting, were also carefully considered. 剧院设计的非声学方面，如座椅和灯光，也经过了仔细考虑。

近义词: non-acoustic
反义词: acoustical, sonic

词性: adjective
