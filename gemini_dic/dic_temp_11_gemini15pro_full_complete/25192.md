buddhistically:/ˌbʊdɪˈstɪkli/
adverb
佛教式地；从佛教徒的角度
In a manner consistent with the principles of Buddhism.
He approached the problem buddhistically, seeking enlightenment through meditation. 他以佛教的方式处理这个问题，通过冥想寻求启迪。
Synonym: philosophically, spiritually
Antonym: materialistically
词性: 副词
