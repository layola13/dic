lemon:/ˈlɛmən/ (US), /ˈlɛmən/ (UK)| n. adj.|柠檬，柠檬黄色的|A yellow citrus fruit with acidic juice, used as a flavoring and garnish.  Also, a pale yellow color.|例句：I like to add lemon to my tea. 我喜欢在茶里加柠檬。

例句：She wore a lemon-colored dress. 她穿了一件柠檬黄的连衣裙。|近义词：citron, lime (for the fruit)|反义词:sweet (for the taste)|n.

lemon:/ˈlɛmən/ (US), /ˈlɛmən/ (UK)| adj.|柠檬黄色的|Having a pale yellow color like a lemon.|例句：The walls were painted a soft lemon. 墙壁被漆成了柔和的柠檬黄色。

例句：He drove a lemon yellow car. 他开着一辆柠檬黄的车。|近义词：citrine, flaxen|反义词: (No single antonym for the color)|adj.
