hulls:/hʌlz/
n.
船体；外壳；皮；荚；(尤指水果或蔬菜的)外壳
The ship's hull was damaged in the storm.  这艘船的船体在风暴中受损了。
The hulls of the sunflower seeds were discarded.  葵花籽的外壳被丢弃了。

Etymology: From Middle English *hulle*, from Old Norse *hul* (covering, shell), from Proto-Germanic *hulją* (covering, husk, sheath), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kelH- (to cover, hide).  Cognate with Old English *hyll* (hill).

Synonyms: shell, casing, husk, covering, framework
Antonyms: interior, inside, core
