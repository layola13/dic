outreproduce:/ˌaʊtrɪprəˈdjuːs/
verb
过度繁殖，生育过多
To reproduce or multiply at a faster rate than.
The rabbits outreproduced the foxes, leading to an overpopulation problem. 兔子繁殖速度超过了狐狸，导致兔子数量过多。
outbreed, overproduce
underproduce
