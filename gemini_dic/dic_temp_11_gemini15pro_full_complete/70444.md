figurated:/ˈfɪɡjəˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
形象的；比喻的；(数字)由图形表示的
represented by a figure or shape; metaphorical; (of a number) capable of being represented by a geometric figure
例句：Figurated numbers, like triangular numbers, can be visually represented.  形象的数字，例如三角形数，可以用图形来表示。

例句：The poem uses highly figurated language to convey complex emotions.  这首诗使用高度比喻的语言来表达复杂的情感。
synonym:metaphorical, symbolic, emblematic, representative
antonym:literal, plain, unadorned
词性:adjective
