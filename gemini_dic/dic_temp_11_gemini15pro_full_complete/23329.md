boyishness:/ˈbɔɪɪʃnəs/| n. |男孩气；孩子气|The quality of behaving in a way that is typical of a boy, often in a playful or mischievous way.|He retained a certain boyishness even in middle age. 他即使到了中年，仍然保留着某种孩子气。|近义词：youthfulness, childishness, immaturity, playfulness|反义词：maturity, sophistication, seriousness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From boy + -ish + -ness.  "Boy" comes from Middle English *boi, of uncertain origin. "-ish" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "of, relating to, or resembling". "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being".
