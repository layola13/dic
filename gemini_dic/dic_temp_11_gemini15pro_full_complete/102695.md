jammy:/ˈdʒæmi/| adj.|幸运的；沾满果酱的|Covered with jam; lucky.  Originating from "jam" referring to something desirable, it evolved to mean fortunate or lucky, especially in British slang.|例句：He was jammy enough to win the lottery. 他真是走运中了彩票。|近义词：lucky, fortunate, charmed|反义词：unlucky, unfortunate|词性:形容词
