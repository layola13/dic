haematoporphyrin:/ˌhiːmətəˈpɔːrfɪrɪn/ /ˌhemətəˈpɔːrfɪrɪn/
noun
血卟啉
A dark red-brown porphyrin derived from hemoglobin, used in photodynamic therapy.
Haematoporphyrin derivative (HPD) is used in photodynamic therapy to treat certain types of cancer. 血卟啉衍生物 (HPD) 用于光动力疗法治疗某些类型的癌症。
