doglike:/ˈdɒɡˌlaɪk/| adj.|像狗的；犬类的|Resembling a dog in appearance, behavior, or characteristics.  Having qualities characteristic of a dog, such as loyalty, obedience, or a keen sense of smell.|He has a doglike devotion to his master. 他对他的主人有像狗一样的忠诚。|Synonyms: canine, doggy, houndlike|Antonyms: catlike, feline|adjective
