diglyph:/ˈdaɪɡlɪf/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A diglyph is a sequence of two letters that are pronounced as a single sound or phoneme.|The "ch" in "church" is a diglyph. “church”中的“ch”是一个双字母。|近义词:digraph (though often used interchangeably, digraph can also refer to two letters representing two sounds)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
