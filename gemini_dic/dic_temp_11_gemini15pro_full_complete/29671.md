Cathay: /kæˈθeɪ/
noun
契丹（古国名，即中国），中国（旧称）
An archaic name for China, derived from Khitan, a nomadic people who ruled parts of northern China.
例句：The ancient travelers spoke of the wonders of Cathay. 古代旅行者讲述了契丹的奇观。

例句：Marco Polo's travels took him to the far reaches of Cathay. 马可·波罗的旅行将他带到了契丹的远方。
