beelzebub:/biːˈɛlzɪbʌb/| n.|别西卜，魔王|A name for the devil; Satan.  Derived from a Philistine deity, originally Baal-zebub, "lord of the flies".  It later became associated with Satan in Christian theology.|He invoked Beelzebub in his dark rituals. 他在黑暗仪式中召唤别西卜。|近义词：Satan, Devil, Lucifer|反义词:God, Angel|名词
