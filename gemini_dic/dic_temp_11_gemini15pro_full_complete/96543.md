indonesians:/ɪnˌdoʊˈniːʒənz/
noun
印尼人; 印尼国民
People who live in or are from Indonesia.  Relating to or characteristic of Indonesia or its people.
Many Indonesians speak Javanese as their first language. 许多印尼人的母语是爪哇语。
synonyms: Indonesian people, citizens of Indonesia
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a nationality)
词性: 名词(plural)
