obdurateness:/ˌɒbdjʊrɪtnəs/ /ˌɑːbdʊrɪtnəs/
n.
固执,顽固,冷酷无情
the state of being unwilling to change your opinion or behavior; stubbornness
His obdurateness in the face of pleas for mercy was appalling. 他面对求饶仍冷酷无情，令人震惊。

近义词:stubbornness, inflexibility, intransigence, adamance
反义词:yielding, compliance, flexibility, acquiescence
词性:noun

obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ /ˈɑːbdʊrət/
adj.
固执的，顽固的，冷酷无情的
stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.
He remained obdurate, refusing to listen to our pleas. 他依然固执，不听我们的恳求。

近义词:stubborn, inflexible, intransigent, adamant
反义词:yielding, compliant, flexible, acquiescent
词性:adjective
