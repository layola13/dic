labeling:/ˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 贴标签的 | relating to or used for labeling |  Labeling machines are used in many industries. 贴标机用于许多行业。| tagging | removing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From label + -ing.  Label comes from Old French *label, diminutive of Old French *lambel "small strip," from Frankish *lamb "rag, shred."
