demedicate:/diːˈmɛdɪkeɪt/ | v. | 使停止服药；停药；取消药物治疗 | To discontinue medication for someone; to cease taking medication oneself. | The doctor decided to demedicate the patient after seeing positive improvements. 医生在看到病人情况好转后决定停止用药。

The patient chose to demedicate himself against medical advice. 病人不顾医嘱，自行停药。 | synonyms: discontinue medication, cease medication, withdraw medication, wean off medication | antonyms: medicate, administer medication, prescribe medication | verb
