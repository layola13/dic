adorative:/əˈdɔːrətɪv/ | adj. | 崇拜的，爱慕的 | Showing or expressing adoration; worshipping. | She gave him an adorative look. 她向他投以爱慕的目光。 |  He gazed at her with an adorative expression. 他带着崇拜的表情凝视着她。 | 近义词: worshipping, adoring, admiring, reverent, devoted | 反义词: critical, disapproving, scornful | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *adorativus, from adorare (see adore).
