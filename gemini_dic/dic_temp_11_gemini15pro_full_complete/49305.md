delegate:/ˈdɛlɪɡət/ /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/| n. vt.|代表，委托|A person sent or authorized to represent others, in particular an elected representative sent to a conference.|The company sent two delegates to the conference. 公司派了两名代表参加会议。|representative, envoy, commissioner|traitor, rebel|noun
delegate:/ˈdɛlɪɡət/ /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/| vt.|委派；授权|Entrust (a task or responsibility) to another person, typically one who is less senior than oneself.|He delegated the task to his assistant. 他把这项任务委托给了他的助手。|assign, entrust, commit|retain, keep, withhold|verb
