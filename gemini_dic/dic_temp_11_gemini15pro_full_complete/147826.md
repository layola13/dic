overpublicizing: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌblɪsaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度宣传；过分宣扬
Giving something too much publicity, often to the point of diminishing returns or causing negative consequences.
The studio overpublicized the movie, leading to inflated expectations and ultimately disappointing box office numbers. 这家电影制片厂对这部电影进行了过度宣传，导致期望值过高，最终票房令人失望。
The overpublicizing of the event led to a chaotic scene when thousands more people showed up than expected.  这次活动的过度宣传导致现场一片混乱，因为到场的人数比预期的多了数千人。
近义词: overhyping, overpromoting
反义词: underpromoting, downplaying
词性: verb (present participle)
