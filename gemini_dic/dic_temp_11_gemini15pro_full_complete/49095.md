deifiers:/ˈdiːɪfaɪərz/
n.
神化者，奉为神明者
People who treat others as gods.
The ancient Egyptians were deifiers of their pharaohs.  古埃及人把他们的法老神化了。
The cult's leader had many deifiers who would do anything he asked.  这个邪教的头目有很多崇拜者，他们会对他言听计从。
idolaters, worshippers
critics, skeptics
noun
