cactuslike: /ˈkæktəsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像仙人掌的；仙人掌状的
Resembling a cactus in appearance or characteristics.  Having spiny or fleshy stems and adapted to arid conditions.
The plant had cactuslike spines and a thick, fleshy stem.  这种植物有仙人掌般的刺和粗壮多肉的茎。
The landscape was barren and cactuslike.  这片土地荒芜，像仙人掌一样。
近义词: spiny, thorny, succulent, xerophytic
反义词: lush, leafy
词性: adjective
