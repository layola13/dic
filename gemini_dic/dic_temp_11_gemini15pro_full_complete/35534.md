coagulum:/kəʊˈæɡjʊləm/ | n. | 凝块；凝结物 | A soft, semi-solid mass formed from a liquid, especially blood, by coagulation. | The nurse removed the coagulum from the wound. 护士清除了伤口上的凝块。| The blood formed a coagulum in the test tube. 血液在试管中形成了凝块。 |  synonyms: clot, curd, jelly | antonyms: liquid, solution | noun

coagula:/kəʊˈæɡjʊlə/ | n. | 凝块；凝结物（复数） | Plural form of coagulum. | The coagula were examined under a microscope. 这些凝块在显微镜下被检查。 |  Several coagula formed in the beaker. 烧杯里形成了几个凝块。 | synonyms: clots, curds, jellies | antonyms: liquids, solutions | noun
