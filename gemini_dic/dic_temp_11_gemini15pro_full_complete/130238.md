non-smoker:/ˌnɒn ˈsməʊkə(r)/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn ˈsmoʊkər/ (US)| n. |不吸烟者|A person who does not smoke tobacco.|例句：The restaurant has a separate section for non-smokers.  这家餐厅有不吸烟者的单独区域。

例句：She's a lifelong non-smoker. 她一辈子都不吸烟。|近义词：non-user of tobacco|反义词:smoker|词性:名词
