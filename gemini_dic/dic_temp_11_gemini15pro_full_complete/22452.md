bookishness:/ˈbʊkɪʃnəs/
n.
书生气，书呆子气
The quality of being excessively devoted to reading and studying, often to the exclusion of social interaction and practical experience.
He was known for his bookishness and quiet demeanor. 他以书生气和安静的举止而闻名。
近义词:  studiousness, scholarly, pedantry, intellectualism
反义词: worldliness, practicality, sociability
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bookish + -ness.  "Bookish" dates back to the late 16th century, from "book" + "-ish".  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
