cruciformly:/kruːˈsɪfɔːmli/ | adv. | 十字形地，交叉形地 | In the shape of a cross. | The beams were arranged cruciformly.  横梁交叉排列。 | Example: The church was built cruciformly, with the nave and transepts forming a cross. 教堂呈十字形建造，中殿和耳堂形成一个十字形。 | Example: The petals of the flower were arranged cruciformly. 花的花瓣呈十字形排列。 |  Synonyms: crosswise, cross-shaped | Antonyms: linearly, straight | Word Type: Adverb

cruciform:/ˈkruːsɪfɔːm/ | adj. | 十字形的 | Having the shape of a cross. |  A cruciform church. 一个十字形的教堂。| Example: The building has a cruciform design. 这座建筑物是十字形的。| Example:  The stained glass window was cruciform. 彩色玻璃窗是十字形的。| Synonyms: cross-shaped, crosswise | Antonyms: linear, straight | Word Type: Adjective

Etymology: From Latin *cruciformis*, from *crux* ("cross") + *-formis* ("-shaped").
