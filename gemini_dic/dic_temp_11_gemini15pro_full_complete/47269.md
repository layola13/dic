de-anglicized:/diːˈæŋɡləsaɪzd/
adjective
去英语化的，非英语化的
Reduced or eliminated English influence or characteristics, especially in language or culture.
The government promoted a de-anglicized version of the language. 政府推广了该语言的非英语化版本。
The spelling of the town's name was de-anglicized.  这个城镇的名字拼写被去英语化了。
近义词:localized, vernacularized, adapted, indigenized
反义词:anglicized
