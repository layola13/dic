machs:/mɑːks/ /mæks/| n. |马赫数|A unit of speed equal to the speed of sound in the surrounding medium.  It is named after Ernst Mach, an Austrian physicist. |例句：The jet flew at Mach 2.  这架喷气式飞机以2马赫的速度飞行。

例句：The Concorde could travel at speeds exceeding Mach 2.  协和式飞机可以以超过2马赫的速度飞行。|近义词：speed of sound|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
