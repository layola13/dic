cataloging: /ˈkætəlɒɡɪŋ/ (British) /ˈkætəlɔːɡɪŋ/ (American)

cataloging: /ˈkætəlɒɡɪŋ/ (British) /ˈkætəlɔːɡɪŋ/ (American)
v.|编目，制作目录|The act of listing and describing items systematically.|She spent the afternoon cataloging her stamp collection. 她一下午都在为她的邮票收藏编目。|classifying, indexing|disorganizing|verb
