licked:/lɪkt/
verb
舔过；打败；解决
past tense and past participle of lick
The dog licked its paw. 狗舔了舔它的爪子。
He had licked the problem. 他已经解决了这个问题。
defeated, beaten, conquered
undefeated, unbeaten, unconquered

licked:/lɪkt/
adjective
被打败的; 筋疲力尽的
informal adjective meaning defeated or exhausted
