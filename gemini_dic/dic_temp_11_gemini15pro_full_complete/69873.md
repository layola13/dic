ferrule:/ˈferəl/ (US), /ˈferuːl/ (UK)| n. |套圈，金属箍，接管，（某些工具的）金属包头|A metal ring or cap placed around the end of a handle, stick, or tube to strengthen it or prevent splitting.|The ferrule on my walking stick came loose. 我的拐杖上的金属箍松了。|Synonyms: cap, ring, band, tip|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unprotected end")|noun

ferrule:/ˈferəl/ (US), /ˈferuːl/ (UK)| v. |用金属箍加固|To attach a ferrule to something.|He ferruled the end of the new axe handle. 他给新斧柄的末端加上了金属箍。|Synonyms: strengthen, reinforce, bind, cap|Antonyms: weaken, loosen|verb
