money-saving:/ˈmʌniˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
省钱的
Reducing or eliminating expenses; economical.
This website offers many money-saving tips for travelers.  这个网站为旅行者提供了许多省钱的技巧。
She's always looking for money-saving deals. 她总是在寻找省钱的交易。
近义词:economical, thrifty, frugal, budget-friendly
反义词:expensive, costly, extravagant, wasteful
词性:adjective
词源:Compound word formed from "money" and "saving" (present participle of "save").  "Money" comes from Old French "moneie," ultimately from Latin "moneta." "Save" comes from Old French "salver," ultimately from Latin "salvare."
