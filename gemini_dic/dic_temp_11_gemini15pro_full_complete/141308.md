open-pit:/ˈoʊpən pɪt/ |adj. n.|露天开采的，露天矿|An open-pit mine is a surface mining operation where minerals are extracted from an open pit. 露天矿是一种地表采矿作业，从露天矿中提取矿物。|The Bingham Canyon Mine is one of the largest open-pit mines in the world.  宾厄姆峡谷矿是世界上最大的露天矿之一。|The company operates an open-pit copper mine. 这家公司经营一个露天铜矿。|Synonyms: surface, opencast, open-cut|Antonyms: underground, subterranean|adjective, noun
