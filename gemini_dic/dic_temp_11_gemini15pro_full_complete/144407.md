outstrove:/ˌaʊtˈstrəʊv/
verb
超越，胜过
surpassed in effort or achievement; outdid.
He outstrove all his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过所有对手。
近义词: outdid, surpassed, excelled, exceeded, triumphed over
反义词: lost to, was defeated by, succumbed to
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + strive.  The prefix "out-" indicates exceeding or surpassing. "Strive" comes from Old French "estriver" meaning "to quarrel, dispute, struggle."
