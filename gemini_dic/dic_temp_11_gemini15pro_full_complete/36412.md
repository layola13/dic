coke:/koʊk/ (US) /kəʊk/ (UK)| n.|可乐；焦炭|A carbonated soft drink flavored with coca extract.  Originally containing cocaine, modern versions contain caffeine and other flavorings.|I'd like a coke, please. 我想要一杯可乐。|近义词：soda, pop (regional)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "water" or "juice" as alternatives)|noun

coke:/koʊk/ (US) /kəʊk/ (UK)| n.|焦炭|A solid fuel made by heating coal in the absence of air.|The steel mill uses coke in its blast furnaces.  这家钢铁厂在高炉中使用焦炭。|近义词：coal, fuel|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
