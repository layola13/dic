mobilised:/ˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ | adj. |动员的； mobilized /ˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/ | adj. |动员的|  Put into action or use. | The government mobilised the army for the war. 政府动员军队参战。| Synonyms: activated, deployed, marshaled | Antonyms: demobilised, deactivated | adjective

mobilise:/ˈməʊbəlaɪz/ | v. |动员；mobilize /ˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ | v. |动员| (1) Prepare and organize (troops) for active service. (2) Make something available for a particular purpose.| (1) The country mobilised its army. 该国动员了军队。(2)  They are mobilising resources to help the flood victims. 他们正在调动资源来帮助洪灾受害者。 | Synonyms: rally, assemble, marshal | Antonyms: demobilise, disband | verb

Etymology:
From French *mobiliser*, from *mobile* (“movable”) + *-iser* (from Latin *-izāre*).  Ultimately from Latin *mobilis* (“movable”).
