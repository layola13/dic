centromedian:/ˌsɛntrəˈmiːdiən/
| adj. |中心中位数的；|Relating to or situated at the centromedian nucleus of the thalamus.  位于或关于丘脑中心中位核的。|Centromedian thalamic deep brain stimulation has shown promise in treating epilepsy. 丘脑中心中位深部脑刺激已显示出治疗癫痫的希望。|近义词：midline thalamic (more general) |反义词:lateral thalamic|词性:形容词

centromedian:/ˌsɛntrəˈmiːdiən/
| n. |中心中位核|The centromedian nucleus is a part of the intralaminar nuclei of the thalamus. 中心中位核是丘脑板内核的一部分。|The centromedian nucleus plays a role in motor control and pain perception. 中心中位核在运动控制和疼痛感知中起作用。|近义词：CM nucleus (abbreviation) |反义词:N/A|词性:名词
