nonthrift:/ˈnɑːnθrɪft/
n.|挥霍；浪费；不节俭|A person who spends money wastefully or does not save.  A lack of thrift; extravagance.|He was known for his nonthrift and often found himself in debt. 他因挥霍无度而闻名，经常发现自己负债累累。|近义词：spendthrift, prodigal, wastrel|反义词：thrifty, frugal, economical
