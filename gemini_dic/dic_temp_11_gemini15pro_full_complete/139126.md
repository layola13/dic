obeisantly:/oʊˈbiːsəntli/ /əˈbiːsəntli/
|adv.|顺从地，恭顺地|In a manner showing obedience; submissively.  Showing dutiful compliance to authority.|She bowed her head obeisantly. 她顺从地低下了头。|Synonym: submissively, respectfully, dutifully, compliantly|Antonym: rebelliously, defiantly, disobediently|词性:副词
Etymology: From the adjective "obeisant," from Old French "obeissant," present participle of "obeir" (to obey), from Latin "oboedire," from "ob-" (towards) + "audire" (to hear).
