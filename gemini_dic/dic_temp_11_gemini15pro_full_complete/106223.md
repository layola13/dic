knottily:/ˈnɒtɪli/ (US) /ˈnɒtɪli/ (UK)| adv.| 纠结地，疙疙瘩瘩地|In a knotty or complex manner; intricately.  Characterized by difficulty or complexity.|The argument was presented knottily, making it hard to follow.  论证的表达很纠结，难以理解。|intricately, complexly, confusingly|simply, straightforwardly|adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From knotty + -ly.  Knotty comes from Old English cnottig, from cnotta "knot."
