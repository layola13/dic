ministered:/ˈmɪnɪstərd/
verb
服侍，照料；给予；执行牧师职务
attended to the needs of; provided care for; administered
The nurse ministered to the wounded soldiers.  护士照料受伤的士兵。
近义词: served, aided, attended, nursed
反义词: neglected, ignored
词性: verb

ministered:/ˈmɪnɪstərd/
verb
给予；提供
gave or supplied something beneficial
The government ministered aid to the flood victims. 政府向洪灾灾民提供了援助。
近义词: provided, furnished, supplied, offered
反义词: withheld, denied
词性: verb

ministered:/ˈmɪnɪstərd/
verb
执行牧师职务
performed the duties of a minister or priest
The pastor ministered to his congregation. 牧师为他的会众服务。
近义词: preached, officiated, served
反义词: N/A
词性: verb
