consciousness-raising: /ˈkɑːnʃəsnəs ˈreɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 意识提升; 觉悟提高 | The process of increasing awareness and understanding of a particular social or political issue, often with the aim of inspiring action or change.  | The feminist movement of the 1970s involved a lot of consciousness-raising about gender inequality.  20世纪70年代的女权运动中有很多关于性别不平等的意识提升活动。| synonyms: awareness-raising, education, enlightenment | antonyms: suppression, ignorance | noun
