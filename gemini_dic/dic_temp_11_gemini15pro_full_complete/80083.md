goatfish:/ˈɡoʊtfɪʃ/| n. | 羊鱼 | Any of numerous tropical marine fishes of the family Mullidae, typically having a pair of long barbels on the chin that are used to probe the sand for food. |  Goatfish are often seen foraging on the seabed. 羊鱼经常在海底觅食。| Goatfish are delicious when grilled.  烤羊鱼很好吃。 | synonyms: red mullet, surmullet | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) | noun
