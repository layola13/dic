bassiest:/ˈbeɪsiɪst/| adj.|最低沉的；最粗的（嗓音）|Superlative form of bassy: most bassy.  Having a deep, resonant sound like a bass instrument or voice. |例句：He had the bassiest voice I'd ever heard. 他拥有我听过最低沉的嗓音。

例句：The bassiest note on the piano rumbled through the room. 钢琴上最低沉的音符在房间里隆隆作响。|近义词：deepest, lowest, richest|反义词：highest, shrillest|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From bass + -y + -est.  "Bass" ultimately comes from the Italian "basso," meaning "low."  "-y" is a suffix used to form adjectives, often indicating having the quality of something. "-est" is the superlative suffix.
