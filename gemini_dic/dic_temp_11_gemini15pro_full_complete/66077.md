excandescent:/ˌɛksɪnˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 白炽的，发出白热光的；热情洋溢的，容光焕发的 | emitting light as a result of being heated; characterized by glowing or white heat; passionate and enthusiastic | The excandescent filament in the light bulb glowed brightly. 白炽灯泡里的灯丝发出明亮的光。| 近义词：glowing, incandescent, radiant, luminous, brilliant | 反义词：dull, dim, dark | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin "ex-" (out) + "candēscere" (to become white-hot)
