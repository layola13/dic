medieval:/ˌmediˈiːvəl/ /ˌmɪdˈiːvəl/| adj.|中世纪的，中古时代的|Relating to the Middle Ages (roughly AD 500 to AD 1500).|例句：Medieval castles are fascinating structures. 中世纪的城堡是令人着迷的建筑。|例句：They studied medieval history and literature. 他们研究中世纪的历史和文学。|近义词：ancient, archaic, old|反义词：modern, contemporary|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *meien* (modern *moyen*) "middle" (from Latin *medianus*) + *eieval*, from Latin *aevum* "age".  Literally "of the middle age".
