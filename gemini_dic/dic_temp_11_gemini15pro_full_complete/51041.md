desmoid:/ˈdɛzmɔɪd/| adj. n.|腱样的，纤维瘤|Resembling a tendon; a fibrous tumor.  A desmoid tumor is a growth that arises from connective tissue, most commonly in the abdomen, arms, or legs.  They are considered benign, but can be locally aggressive and difficult to remove completely.|She was diagnosed with a desmoid tumor in her shoulder. 她被诊断出肩膀患有纤维瘤。|近义词：fibromatous, fibrous|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

desmoid:/ˈdɛzmɔɪd/| n.|纤维瘤|A fibrous tumor.  Specifically refers to a desmoid-type fibromatosis, a locally aggressive benign tumor that arises from connective tissue.|The desmoid was causing pain and limiting her range of motion.  这个纤维瘤引起了疼痛并限制了她的活动范围。|近义词：fibromatosis|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
