kicksy:/ˈkɪksi/| adj.|时髦的，炫酷的，新潮的|Fashionable, stylish, trendy, especially in a way that is meant to attract attention.  It often implies a playful or slightly rebellious vibe.|Her kicksy new boots really made a statement. 她那双时髦的新靴子真的很抢眼。|近义词：stylish, trendy, fashionable, chic, cool, hip|反义词：dull, boring, unfashionable, frumpy|词性:形容词

词源:  "Kicksy" likely emerged in the late 20th century, possibly as a playful variation of "kicks," slang for shoes, combined with the suffix "-y" which often denotes a quality.  It suggests a connection between stylish footwear and a general sense of fashionable coolness.
