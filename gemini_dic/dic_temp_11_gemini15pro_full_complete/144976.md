overaccumulate:/ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjəleɪt/
verb
过度积累
to accumulate excessively
The company overaccumulated inventory due to inaccurate sales forecasts.  由于销售预测不准确，公司过度积累了库存。
Over the years, they had overaccumulated possessions, filling their house to the brim. 多年来，他们过度积累了财物，把房子装得满满当当。
synonyms: hoard, stockpile, amass
antonyms: deplete, expend, disperse
词性: 动词

overaccumulation:/ˌoʊvərəˌkjuːmjəˈleɪʃən/
noun
过度积累
excessive accumulation
The overaccumulation of capital can lead to economic stagnation. 资本的过度积累会导致经济停滞。
The warehouse was overflowing due to the overaccumulation of unsold products. 仓库因未售出产品的过度积累而爆满。
synonyms: surplus, glut, excess
antonyms: shortage, deficit, scarcity
词性: 名词
