awesomer:/ˈɔːsəmər/ | adj. | 更令人敬畏的，更棒的 |Comparative form of "awesome," meaning more inspiring awe or admiration.  Used informally to describe something extremely impressive or excellent. | That concert was even awesomer than the last one! 那场音乐会比上次更棒！| The new phone has an awesomer camera.  这款新手机的相机更棒了。|Synonyms:  more impressive, more amazing, better, cooler, greater | Antonyms: worse, less impressive, disappointing | Word Type: Adjective

awesome:/ˈɔːsəm/ | adj. | 令人敬畏的，很棒的 | Inspiring awe or admiration; extremely impressive or excellent. | The view from the mountaintop was awesome. 从山顶看到的景色令人叹为观止。|  She's an awesome singer. 她是一位很棒的歌手。| Synonyms: impressive, amazing, excellent, great | Antonyms: unimpressive, awful, terrible | Word Type: Adjective
