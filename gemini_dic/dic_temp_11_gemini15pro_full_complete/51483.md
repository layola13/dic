detoxifying:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
解毒的，排毒的
removing poisonous substances from something
Example:  A detoxifying diet can help cleanse your body.  一个排毒的饮食可以帮助清洁你的身体。

Example:  The spa offers detoxifying treatments.  这家水疗中心提供排毒疗法。
Synonyms: cleansing, purifying, purging
Antonyms: contaminating, polluting
Word type: adjective


detoxify:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪ/
verb
解毒，排毒
to remove poisonous substances from something
Example: This program helps to detoxify the body.  这个项目有助于身体排毒。

Example:  The liver detoxifies the blood. 肝脏能解毒血液。
Synonyms: cleanse, purify, purge
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute
Word type: verb


detoxification:/ˌdiːˌtɒksɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
noun
解毒，排毒
the process of removing poisonous substances from something
Example: The detoxification process can be challenging.  排毒过程可能会很有挑战性。

Example: He underwent detoxification for drug addiction. 他接受了戒毒治疗。
Synonyms: cleansing, purification, purging
Antonyms: contamination, pollution
Word type: noun
