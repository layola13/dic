headstall:/ˈhɛdstɔːl/ (US) /ˈhɛdstɔl/ (UK)| n. |马笼头；辔头|The part of a bridle that fits around a horse's head, to which the reins are attached. |例句：The headstall was too tight on the horse and he started to get agitated. 马笼头勒得太紧了，马开始烦躁起来。

例句：She adjusted the headstall to make sure it fit comfortably. 她调整了马笼头，以确保它戴起来舒服。|近义词：bridle, halter |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) |词性:noun
