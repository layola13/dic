homestead:/ˈhoʊmsted/ (US), /ˈhəʊmsted/ (UK)| n. | 宅基地，家园 |A house, especially a farmhouse, and the outbuildings and land around it.|They lived in a modest homestead on a small farm. 他们住在一个小农场上简朴的宅基地里。|例句：The family worked hard to improve their homestead. 这家人努力改善他们的家园。


例句：Their homestead was passed down through generations. 他们的家园世代相传。
