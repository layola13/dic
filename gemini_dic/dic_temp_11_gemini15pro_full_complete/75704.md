full-bloodedly:/ˌfʊlˈblʌdɪdli/
adv.
全心全意地，充满活力地
With unrestrained energy and enthusiasm; wholeheartedly.
He threw himself full-bloodedly into the project. 他全心全意地投入到这个项目中。
近义词: enthusiastically, wholeheartedly, passionately, vigorously
反义词: halfheartedly, apathetically, listlessly

词源:
From "full-blooded" + "-ly".  "Full-blooded" means having or showing a strong, healthy, and vigorous nature.  The suffix "-ly" turns it into an adverb.
