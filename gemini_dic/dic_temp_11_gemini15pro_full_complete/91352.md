hydroponists:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪsts/
noun
水培种植者；水耕栽培者
A person who grows plants in a nutrient solution instead of soil.
The hydroponists carefully monitored the pH levels of the water. 水培种植者仔细监测水的pH值。
Hydroponists can grow crops year-round, regardless of weather conditions.  水培种植者可以全年种植作物，不受天气条件的影响。
Synonyms:hydroculture grower, soilless cultivator
Antonyms:traditional farmer, soil-based gardener
