isotropic:/ˌaɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. |各向同性的|Having properties that are the same in all directions.|例句：The universe is assumed to be isotropic on large scales.  宇宙在大尺度上被假设是各向同性的。

例句：An isotropic material has the same strength in all directions. 各向同性材料在各个方向上的强度相同。|近义词：uniform, homogeneous|反义词：anisotropic|词性:adjective
