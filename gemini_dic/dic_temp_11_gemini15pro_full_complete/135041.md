nonnobility:/ˌnɒnnoʊˈbɪləti/
n.
平民；非贵族
the state of not being a member of the nobility
The French Revolution aimed to overthrow the nonnobility and establish a republic.  法国大革命旨在推翻贵族统治，建立共和国。

The rise of the merchant class challenged the traditional power of the nonnobility. 商人阶级的兴起挑战了传统贵族的权力。
近义词:commonalty, commoners
反义词:nobility, aristocracy
词性:名词


词源:
From non- + nobility.  "non-" 表示否定，"nobility" 指贵族。
