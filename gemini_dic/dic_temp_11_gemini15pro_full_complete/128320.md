neurotoxin:/ˈnjʊərəˌtɒksɪn/ (US) /ˈnjʊərəʊˌtɒksɪn/ (UK)| n. |神经毒素|A poisonous substance that acts on the nervous system, disrupting nerve function.|Example: Botulinum toxin is a potent neurotoxin. 肉毒杆菌毒素是一种强效的神经毒素。|Example:  Exposure to certain pesticides can result in neurotoxin buildup in the body. 接触某些杀虫剂会导致体内神经毒素的积累。|近义词：nerve poison, nerve agent|反义词: neurotrophic factor (promotes nerve growth)|词性:noun
