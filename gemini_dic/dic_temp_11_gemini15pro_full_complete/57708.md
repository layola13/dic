draying:/ˈdreɪɪŋ/
| n. |搬运; (用dray)运送;  |The draying of goods from the warehouse to the docks was a slow process.  从仓库到码头的货物搬运是一个缓慢的过程。|
| v. |(用dray)运送|They were draying beer to the local pubs.  他们用dray运啤酒到当地的酒吧。|
|词源:|From dray (a low, heavy cart) + -ing (present participle suffix).  来自dray（一种低矮的重型货车）+ -ing（现在分词后缀）。|
|近义词:|hauling, carting, transporting, conveying|
|反义词:|N/A|
|词性:|Noun, Verb (present participle)|
