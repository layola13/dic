crick:/krɪk/| n.| 脖子僵硬；肌肉抽筋；小溪|A stiff feeling in the neck or a muscle spasm. Also, a small stream. |I woke up with a crick in my neck. 我醒来时脖子僵硬。|近义词：spasm, twinge, stiffness, creek (for stream)|反义词:|noun

crick:/krɪk/| v.| 使…僵硬|To cause a crick in (a muscle or joint).|I cricked my neck while sleeping. 我睡觉时扭到了脖子。|近义词：strain, pull|反义词:|verb

词源:  From Middle English *crike, from Old English *cricc, a variant of crycc (“crutch, staff”). Related to Dutch krik (“jack”), German *Krick (“crack, crevice”).  The sense of "spasm" is probably related to the idea of something being crooked or bent.
