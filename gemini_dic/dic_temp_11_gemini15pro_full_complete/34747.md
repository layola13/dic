clienter:/ˈklaɪəntər/| n.|客户; 委托人; [计算机]客户端|A person or organization that uses the services of a lawyer, accountant, architect, etc.  Someone who receives services from a server on a network.|例句：The lawyer met with his clienter to discuss the case. 律师与他的客户见面讨论案情。

例句：The server sent the data to the clienter. 服务器将数据发送到客户端。|近义词：customer, patron, client|反义词:provider, server|词性:名词

词源: From client + -er.  "Client" itself comes from the Latin word "cliens," meaning "dependent."
