opalize:/ˈoʊpəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 使成蛋白石状；使具有乳白光 | To convert into opal; to make look like opal; to give an opalescent appearance to. | The silica solution slowly opalized, forming a beautiful gemstone.  二氧化硅溶液慢慢地变成了蛋白石，形成了一颗美丽的宝石。

The craftsman opalized the glass to give it a shimmering effect.  工匠将玻璃蛋白石化，使其具有闪光效果。| 近义词: iridize | 反义词:  None |词性: 动词
