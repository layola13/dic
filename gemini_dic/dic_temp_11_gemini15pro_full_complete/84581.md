hammock:/ˈhæmək/ (US), /ˈhæmək/ (UK)| n. |吊床|A hanging bed or couch made of canvas, rope, or netting, suspended between two points.|She relaxed in a hammock strung between two palm trees. 她在一张挂在两棵棕榈树之间的吊床上放松身心。|Synonyms: swing bed, hanging bed|Antonyms: bed, couch|noun
