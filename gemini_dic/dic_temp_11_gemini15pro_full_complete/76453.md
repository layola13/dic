gainsay:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/ /ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/| vt.|否认，反驳，否定|To deny or contradict; to dispute or oppose.|例句：No one could gainsay her brilliance. 没有人能否认她的才华。|例句：He attempted to gainsay the accusations against him. 他试图反驳对他的指控。|近义词：deny, dispute, contradict, oppose, challenge|反义词：confirm, admit, acknowledge, accept, concede|词性:动词
