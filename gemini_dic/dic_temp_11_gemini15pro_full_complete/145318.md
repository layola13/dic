overbroadened:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdnd/ | adj. | 过度扩展的，过度泛化的 | Extended or applied too broadly; encompassing more than is appropriate or accurate. |  The term "artificial intelligence" is often overbroadened to include any form of complex computation.  “人工智能”一词经常被过度泛化，包含了任何形式的复杂计算。 | 近义词: overgeneralized, overextended, too broad, excessively broad | 反义词: narrow, specific, precise, limited | 词性: adjective

overbroadened:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdnd/ | adj. | 过度扩展的，过度泛化的 |  The law was criticized for being overbroadened and infringing on free speech.  这项法律因过度扩展和侵犯言论自由而受到批评。 | 近义词: overinclusive | 反义词: restrictive | 词性: adjective
