bluestocking: /ˈbluːˌstɒkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbluːˌstɒkɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |女学者，女才子（尤指对学术过于热衷而忽略女性特质的女子）|A woman with strong scholarly or literary interests, especially one who is perceived as neglecting traditional feminine pursuits.|例句：She was known as a bluestocking, preferring books to balls. 她被称为女学者，喜欢读书胜过参加舞会。

例句：Her family worried that her bluestocking tendencies would make her unmarriageable.  她的家人担心她女学者的倾向会让她嫁不出去。


近义词：intellectual, academic, highbrow |反义词: socialite, philistine |词性: noun
