chattelism:/ˈtʃætəlɪzəm/
n.
奴隶制，动产制
The system of treating people as chattels, or personal property.
Slavery in the American South was a brutal system of chattelism.  美国南方的奴隶制是一个残酷的动产制。
Chattelism reduced human beings to mere commodities. 动产制将人类沦为单纯的商品。
近义词: slavery, bondage, enslavement
反义词: freedom, liberty
词性: noun

chattel:/ˈtʃæt(ə)l/
n.
动产，私人财产
A movable item of personal property.
The farmer's chattels included livestock and farm equipment.  农民的动产包括牲畜和农具。
The auctioneer sold the family's chattels after they lost their home.  在他们失去家园后，拍卖师卖掉了家里的动产。
近义词: property, possession, belongings
反义词: real estate
词性: noun
