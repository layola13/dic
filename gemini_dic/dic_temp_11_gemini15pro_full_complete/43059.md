cowling:/ˈkaʊlɪŋ/
n.
罩,盖,保护罩
A removable metal covering for an engine, especially of an aircraft or vehicle.
The engine cowling was damaged during the flight.  飞机引擎罩在飞行中受损。
cover, casing, housing
