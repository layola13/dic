oversoaking:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度浸泡
The act of soaking something for too long.
The oversoaking of the beans made them mushy.  过度浸泡豆子使它们变得糊状。
The wood was damaged by oversoaking in the rain. 木头因雨水浸泡过久而受损。

反义词: under-soaking, dehydrating
