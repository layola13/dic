disgustfulness:/dɪsˈɡʌstfʊlnəs/
n.
恶心; 令人厌恶的性质
The disgustfulness of the rotten food made me gag. 腐烂食物的恶心程度让我作呕。
Extreme disgustfulness characterized the scene.  极度的恶心充斥着整个场景。
近义词: repulsiveness, offensiveness, vileness
反义词: pleasantness, agreeableness, attractiveness
词性: 名词

disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/
n.
厌恶，恶心
He felt disgust at the sight of the rotting meat.  看到腐烂的肉，他感到恶心。
vt.
使厌恶，使作呕
The smell of the garbage disgusted her. 垃圾的气味让她感到恶心。
近义词: revulsion, nausea, aversion
反义词: attraction, liking, pleasure
词性: 名词, 动词

-ful:/fʊl/
suffix
充满...的; 有...性质的
A beautiful flower. 一朵美丽的花。
A helpful person. 一个乐于助人的人。
近义词: -ous, -ive, -y
反义词: -less
词性: 后缀

-ness:/nəs/
suffix
状态; 性质
Happiness is important. 快乐很重要。
Kindness is a virtue.  善良是一种美德。
近义词: -ity, -ship, -hood
反义词: 无
词性: 后缀


词源: disgust (from Old French desgouster, from des- "dis-" + gouster "to taste") + -ful + -ness
