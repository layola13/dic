faking:/ˈfeɪkɪŋ/| v.|假装，伪造，捏造|Pretending to have a particular feeling or to be a particular kind of person; making something so that it seems to be something else.|He was faking a headache to avoid going to school. 他假装头痛以逃避上学。|近义词：pretending, feigning, simulating, sham, counterfeit|反义词：genuine, authentic, real|verb (present participle)

faking:/ˈfeɪkɪŋ/| n.|伪造，假冒|The act of pretending or making something appear to be something it is not.|The painting was a clever faking of a Renaissance masterpiece. 这幅画是对文艺复兴时期杰作的巧妙伪造。|近义词：forgery, counterfeit, sham|反义词：authenticity, genuineness|noun (gerund)
