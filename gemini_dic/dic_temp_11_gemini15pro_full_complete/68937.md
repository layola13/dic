fatboy:/ˈfætˌbɔɪ/| n.|胖子；懒人；豆袋椅|A fatboy is a large, comfy beanbag chair. Fatboy也可以指胖子或懒人，但这种用法不太礼貌。|例句：I love relaxing on my fatboy after a long day.  我喜欢在漫长的一天后在我的豆袋椅上放松。

例句：He called him a fatboy, which was very rude. 他叫他胖子，这很粗鲁。|近义词：beanbag chair, lounger, oversized chair (for the furniture); overweight person, obese person (for the person - use with caution), couch potato (for the lazy person)|反义词:thin person, energetic person|词性:noun
