outsprinting:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪntɪŋ/
verb
超越，冲刺胜过
The act of running faster than someone else in a sprint.
Usain Bolt outsprinted his rivals to win the gold medal. 博尔特在短跑比赛中超越对手，赢得金牌。
outrunning, outpacing
being outsprinted, losing the sprint
