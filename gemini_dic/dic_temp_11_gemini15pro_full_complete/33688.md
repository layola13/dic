cinnamyl:/ˈsɪnəmɪl/ | adj.|肉桂基的；桂皮醛的|Relating to or containing cinnamic acid or cinnamaldehyde, which give cinnamon its characteristic flavor and aroma.|The cinnamyl group is a common component of fragrances and flavorings. 肉桂基是香精和调味剂的常见成分。|例句：Cinnamyl alcohol has a sweet, balsamic odor. 肉桂醇有一种甜的、香脂的气味。

例句：Cinnamyl compounds contribute to the spicy aroma of cinnamon. 肉桂基化合物构成了肉桂的辛辣香味。|近义词: cinnamonic|反义词: None|词性: adjective

cinnamyl:/ˈsɪnəmɪl/ | n.|肉桂基|The univalent radical derived from cinnamic alcohol by removing the hydroxyl group (C6H5CH=CHCH2–).|Cinnamyl radicals are important intermediates in organic synthesis. 肉桂基自由基是有机合成中的重要中间体。|例句：The cinnamyl group can be attached to various other molecules. 肉桂基可以与其他各种分子相连。

例句：Cinnamyl esters are used in perfumery. 肉桂酯用于香料制造。|近义词: None|反义词: None|词性: noun
