extortionary:/ɪkˈstɔːrʃəneri/| adj.|敲诈的；勒索的；过高的；高得离谱的|Characterized by extortion; exorbitant; unreasonably high.|The company was accused of using extortionary pricing tactics. 这家公司被指控使用敲诈性的定价策略。|近义词：exorbitant, excessive, outrageous, unreasonable|反义词：reasonable, fair, moderate|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French extortionnaire, from extorsion ‘extortion’, from Latin extorsio(n-), from extort- ‘extorted’, from the verb extorquere.
