fakeries:/ˈfeɪkəriz/
n.
赝品；伪造品；骗局
Something that is not genuine; a forgery.
The museum discovered several fakeries in its collection.  博物馆在其收藏中发现了几件赝品。
synonyms: forgeries, counterfeits, imitations, frauds, shams
antonyms: originals, authentics, genuines
noun
