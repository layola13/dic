amygdala: /əˈmɪɡdələ/ (US), /əˈmɪɡdələ/ (UK)| n. | 杏仁核；杏仁体 | A roughly almond-shaped mass of gray matter inside each cerebral hemisphere, involved with the experiencing of emotions. | The amygdala plays a crucial role in processing fear and other emotions. 杏仁核在处理恐惧和其他情绪方面起着至关重要的作用。|  The amygdala is activated during periods of high stress.  在高压力时期，杏仁核会被激活。 | synonyms:  tonsillar nucleus | antonyms: N/A | noun
