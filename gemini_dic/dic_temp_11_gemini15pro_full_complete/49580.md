demerge:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/
verb
拆分；分拆；分离
To separate (a part of a company) from the main company, creating a new, independent company.
The company plans to demerge its retail operations from its manufacturing business.  该公司计划将其零售业务从制造业务中分离出来。
The telecommunications giant is demerging its mobile phone division.  这家电信巨头正在拆分其手机部门。
spin off, divest, separate
merge, combine
v.
