head-hunter:/ˈhedˌhʌntər/| n.|猎头|A person who identifies and approaches suitable candidates employed elsewhere to fill business positions.|The head-hunter called me about a job opportunity at Google. 猎头打电话给我，说谷歌有一个工作机会。|近义词：recruiter, executive search consultant|反义词: applicant, job seeker|noun
