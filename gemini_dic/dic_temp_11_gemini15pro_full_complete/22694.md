bore-out:/ˈbɔːraʊt/| n.|倦怠; 厌倦|A state of extreme boredom and frustration experienced by employees in unchallenging or unstimulating jobs, leading to feelings of demotivation and disengagement.|Many employees experience bore-out due to repetitive tasks and lack of opportunities for growth. 许多员工由于重复性工作和缺乏成长机会而感到倦怠。|近义词: burnout, ennui, tedium, apathy|反义词: engagement, enthusiasm, motivation|词性: noun

bore-out:/ˈbɔːraʊt/| n.|职业倦怠; 工作厌倦|A state of boredom and frustration resulting from a lack of stimulation at work.|He suffered from bore-out because his job was too easy. 他的工作太简单了，所以他感到职业倦怠。|近义词: burnout, tedium|反义词: engagement, fulfillment|词性: noun
