murderesses:/ˈmɜːrdərɪsɪz/ (US) /ˈmɜːdəresɪz/ (UK)
n.
女杀人犯们
A woman or women who commit murder.
The murderesses were apprehended shortly after the crime.  女杀人犯们在犯罪后不久就被逮捕了。
The trial of the murderesses captivated the nation.  对这些女杀人犯的审判吸引了全国的注意。
近义词: killers, assassins, homicides
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑victim 受害者)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *murd(e)resse*, from Anglo-Norman *murderesse*, feminine of *murderer*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *murþrą* (see murder).
