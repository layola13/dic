kilojoule: /ˈkɪləˌdʒuːl/ | n. | 千焦耳 | A unit of energy equal to 1000 joules.  It's a metric unit used to measure energy or work. | A large apple contains about 420 kilojoules of energy.  一个大苹果含有大约420千焦耳的能量。 | The recommended daily intake is 8700 kilojoules for adult men. 成年男性建议的每日摄入量为8700千焦耳。|  calorie, joule | - | noun
