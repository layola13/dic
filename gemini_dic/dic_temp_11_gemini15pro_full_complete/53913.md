discouragement: /dɪˈskʌrɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 灰心，沮丧，泄气 | A feeling of loss of confidence or hope; a state of being discouraged. |  His constant criticism filled her with discouragement. 他不断的批评让她充满了沮丧。|  dejection, despondency, hopelessness | encouragement, hope, optimism | noun

discourage: /dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/ | vt. | 使灰心，使沮丧，劝阻 | To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to prevent (something) by expressing disapproval or raising objections. | The coach discouraged him from quitting the team. 教练劝他不要退出球队。| deter, dissuade, hinder | encourage, support, promote | verb
