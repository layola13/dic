emboss:/ɪmˈbɒs/ (UK), /ɪmˈbɑːs/ (US)| v. n.|浮雕，凸印；使…醒目|To carve, mold, or stamp a design on a surface so that it stands out in relief.  |The company logo was embossed on the leather cover of the notebook.  公司标志被压印在笔记本的皮革封面上。| 近义词: carve, mold, stamp, decorate, ornament | 反义词: flatten, smooth |词性:动词, 名词
