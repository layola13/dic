adhamant:/ˈædəmənt/| adj.|坚定不移的，坚决的|Refusing to be persuaded or to change one's mind.|例句：He was adamant that he had not seen the woman before. 他坚决表示以前从未见过那个女人。

例句：She remained adamant in her refusal to apologize. 她仍然坚决拒绝道歉。|近义词：unyielding, inflexible, resolute, firm, unwavering|反义词：yielding, flexible, compliant, amenable|词性：形容词
