impaler:/ɪmˈpeɪlər/| n.| 刺穿者；刺穿工具 |Someone or something that impales, especially a pointed stake or other sharp object used for impaling.  A person or device that pierces something with a sharp stake or point.|The impaler used in the ancient execution was a gruesome sight.  古代处决中使用的刺穿工具是一个可怕的景象。|Near synonyms:  piercer, spiker, stabber |Near antonyms: extractor, remover |noun
