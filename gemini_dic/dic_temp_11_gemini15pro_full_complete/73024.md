footfall:/ˈfʊtfɔːl/ (US) /ˈfʊtfɔːl/ (UK)| n.|脚步声；客流量；顾客人数|The sound of a person's foot hitting the ground as they walk; the number of people entering a shop or business.|例句：The shopkeeper noticed the increased footfall in his store during the holiday season.  店主注意到节日期间他店里的客流量增加了。

例句：The soft footfall of the cat went unnoticed.  猫轻柔的脚步声没有人注意到。|近义词：footstep, tread, traffic, customer count|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "absence," "desertion" for context of lack of people)|词性:名词
