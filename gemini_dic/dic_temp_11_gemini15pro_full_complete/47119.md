dauntingnesses:/ˈdɔːntɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
令人生畏的性质；令人怯步的特点
The quality or state of being daunting; the capacity to intimidate or discourage.
The sheer dauntingnesses of the task ahead made him hesitate.  摆在面前的任务之艰巨令他犹豫不决。
近义词: intimidation, discouragement, formidableness
反义词: easiness, simplicity, encouragement
词性: 名词


daunting:/ˈdɔːntɪŋ/
adj.
令人畏惧的；使人氣餒的
Seeming difficult to deal with in a way that discourages you.
Climbing Mount Everest is a daunting task. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项艰巨的任务。
近义词: intimidating, discouraging, formidable
反义词: easy, simple, encouraging
词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "daunt," which comes from Old French "danter" (to subdue), ultimately from Latin "domare" (to tame).
