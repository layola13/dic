anti-zionist:/ˌæntiˈzaɪənɪst/
adjective|反犹太复国主义的|Opposed to Zionism; relating to opposition to Zionism.  Zionism is a movement for (originally) the re-establishment and (now) the development and protection of a Jewish nation in what is now Israel.|Many anti-zionist activists argue that the movement is inherently discriminatory.|Synonyms: non-zionist, anti-Israel (often used, but not perfectly synonymous)|Antonyms: Zionist, pro-Israel|adj.

anti-zionist:/ˌæntiˈzaɪənɪst/
noun|反犹太复国主义者|A person who is opposed to Zionism.|Some anti-zionists believe that a two-state solution is not possible.|Synonyms: opponent of Zionism|Antonyms: Zionist, supporter of Israel|n.
