incask:/ɪnˈkæsk/| adj.|在桶中的；未装瓶的|In a cask; not bottled.  Referring to alcoholic beverages, especially beer or wine, that are stored and served directly from a cask, rather than being bottled or canned.|The pub offered a selection of incask ales. 这家酒吧提供各种桶装啤酒。|近义词：draught, on tap|反义词:bottled, canned|词性:形容词
