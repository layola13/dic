misstamped: /ˌmɪsˈstæmpt/ | adj. | 盖错邮戳的；印错的 | marked with the wrong stamp or impression | The letter was misstamped and returned to sender. 这封信盖错了邮戳，被退回给寄件人。| 近义词：incorrectly stamped, wrongly stamped | 反义词：correctly stamped | 词性：形容词

词源:  mis- + stamped. "mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly". "stamped" is the past participle of the verb "stamp", meaning to mark with a stamp.
