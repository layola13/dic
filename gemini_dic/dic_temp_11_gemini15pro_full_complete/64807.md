esoteric:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 秘传的，深奥的 | Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest. |  The professor's lectures were filled with esoteric references that only a few students could grasp. 教授的讲座充满了深奥的典故，只有少数学生能够理解。

|  The group's rituals were esoteric and shrouded in secrecy.  这个团体的仪式是秘传的，并且笼罩在神秘之中。 |  近义词: arcane, abstruse, recondite | 反义词: exoteric, common, simple | 词性: adjective
