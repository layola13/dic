gewgaw:/ˈɡuːɡɔː/ | n. | 小玩意儿；廉价而俗丽的饰品 | A showy trifle; a cheap ornament. | She wasted her money on cheap gewgaws. 她把钱浪费在廉价的小玩意儿上。 | The market stalls were laden with tawdry gewgaws. 市场摊位上摆满了俗丽的小玩意儿。 | 近义词: trinket, bauble, knick-knack, trifle | 反义词: treasure, valuable | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from Middle English *gewgaw, perhaps of imitative origin.
