enquirer:/ɪnˈkwaɪərər/ (UK), /ˈɪnkwɑɪrər/ (US)| n. |询问者，打听者|Someone who asks for information.| The enquirer wanted to know the price of the car. 询问者想知道这辆车的价格。|Example:  The journalist, acting as an enquirer, called the company for a statement.  这位记者作为一名询问者致电该公司要求发表声明。|Synonyms: inquirer, questioner, asker, investigator|Antonyms: respondent, answerer|noun

enquirer:/ɪnˈkwaɪərər/ (UK), /ˈɪnkwɑɪrər/ (US)| n. |探究者，研究者|Someone who investigates or researches something.|The enquirer delved into the historical archives. 探究者深入研究了历史档案。|Example: A curious enquirer by nature, she was always seeking new knowledge. 她天生就是一个好奇的探究者，总是寻求新的知识。|Synonyms:  researcher, investigator, explorer|Antonyms:  ignorant, apathetic|noun
