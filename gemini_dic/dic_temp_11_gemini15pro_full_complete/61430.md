embolismic:/ˌɛmbəˈlɪzmɪk/ | adj. | 闰的；插入的；补充的 | Relating to or inserted as an intercalation, especially of a day or month added to a calendar to harmonize it with the solar year. |  The embolismic month in the lunar calendar keeps it synchronized with the seasons. 农历中的闰月使其与季节同步。| synonymous: intercalary, inserted, added | antonymous: standard, regular | adjective

embolism:/ˈɛmbəlɪzəm/ | n. | 栓塞；障碍物 | Obstruction of a blood vessel by a clot of blood or foreign substance. | The doctor feared an embolism could occur after the surgery. 医生担心手术后可能会发生栓塞。| synonymous: blockage, obstruction, clot | antonymous: flow, clearance | noun
