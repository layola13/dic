electropermanent:/ɪˌlektroʊˈpɜːrmənənt/
adjective
电永磁的
Relating to a magnet that can be switched on or off by applying an electrical pulse.  A combination of "electro" and "permanent," signifying its dual nature of being electrically controlled and magnetically persistent.
Electropermanent magnets offer the advantage of strong holding force without the need for continuous power. 电永磁体提供了强大的保持力，而无需持续供电。
Electropermanent magnetic chucks are commonly used in machining operations. 电永磁吸盘常用于机械加工操作。
近义词: switchable magnetic, electrically controlled magnetic
反义词: permanent magnetic, electromagnetic
词性: adjective
