adware:/ˈædˌweər/ | n. | 广告软件 | Software that automatically displays or downloads advertising material (often unwanted) when a user is online. |  The free game I downloaded came bundled with adware that kept popping up annoying ads.  我下载的免费游戏捆绑了广告软件，不断弹出烦人的广告。| synonym: malware, spyware | antonym:  anti-adware, ad blocker | noun
