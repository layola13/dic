detestable:/dɪˈtestəbl/ /dɪˈtɛstəbl/ | adj. |可憎的，令人厌恶的|Deserving intense dislike; hateful.|例句：His behavior was so detestable that everyone avoided him. 他的行为令人厌恶，每个人都躲着他。|例句：The detestable smell of burning rubber filled the air. 燃烧橡胶的恶臭弥漫在空气中。|近义词：abhorrent, abominable, loathsome, repugnant, repulsive|反义词：admirable, lovable, pleasant|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dētestābilis, from Latin dētestārī (“to curse, denounce, abhor”), from dē- (“down, away”) + testārī (“to bear witness, testify”).
