derogation:/ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 减损，贬低， derogation |  The new rules represent a derogation from his authority. 新规定削弱了他的权威。 | synonyms: depreciation, detraction, lessening, impairment | antonyms:  enhancement, improvement, elevation | noun

derogatory:/dɪˈrɒɡət(ə)ri/ | adj. | 贬低的，减损的 |  He made some derogatory remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌发表了一些贬损的评论。 | synonyms: disparaging, belittling, denigrating | antonyms: complimentary, flattering, praising | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dērōgātiō, from Latin dērōgāre (to repeal partly, detract from), from dē- (from, away from) + rogāre (to ask, propose a law).
