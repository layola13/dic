agonism:/ˈæɡənɪzəm/| n.|对抗；竞争；斗争|The political agonism between the two parties has reached a fever pitch. 这两个政党之间的政治斗争已达到白热化。|例句：

The agonism between the two leading companies in the market is fierce.
市场上两家领先公司之间的竞争非常激烈。

Their relationship was marked by constant agonism, always vying for the upper hand.
他们的关系以不断的竞争为特征，总是争夺上风。|近义词：conflict, contention, rivalry, competition, struggle|反义词：cooperation, collaboration, harmony, agreement|词性:名词

词源: From Ancient Greek ἀγωνισμός (agōnismós, “contest”), from ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnízomai, “I contend for a prize”), from ἀγών (agṓn, “contest, struggle”).
