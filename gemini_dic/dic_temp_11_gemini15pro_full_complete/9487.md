antiromantic:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪk/
adj.
反浪漫的，反对浪漫主义的
Opposed to or lacking in romance; unromantic.
例句：
He has a rather antiromantic view of marriage.  他对婚姻持有一种相当反浪漫的观点。

Her antiromantic practicality made her a successful businesswoman. 她不浪漫的务实精神使她成为一名成功的商人。

近义词：unromantic, pragmatic, cynical, realistic
反义词：romantic, sentimental, idealistic
词性: adjective
