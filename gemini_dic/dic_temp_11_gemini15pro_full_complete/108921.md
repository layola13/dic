leechcraft:/ˈliːtʃkræft/ | n. | 敲诈勒索; 寄生虫学;  | The practice of exploiting others for personal gain, often through manipulation or extortion;  The study of leeches. | He accused the company of leechcraft, claiming they were overcharging customers for basic services. 他指责该公司敲诈勒索，声称他们向客户收取基本服务的过高费用。  The scientist dedicated his life to leechcraft, hoping to discover new medicinal uses for the creatures.  这位科学家毕生致力于寄生虫学研究，希望能发现这些生物新的药用价值。| 近义词：exploitation, extortion, parasitism | 反义词：generosity, altruism | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "leech" (meaning a bloodsucking worm, and figuratively, someone who exploits others) + "craft" (meaning skill, art, or trade).  The word originally referred to the practice of using leeches in medicine, but over time it acquired its negative connotation of exploitation.
