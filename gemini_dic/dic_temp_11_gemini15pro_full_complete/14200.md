autobracketing:/ˌɔːtəʊˈbrækɪtɪŋ/ | n. |自动包围曝光|The process of taking multiple photographs of the same scene at different exposure levels, usually in a sequence of underexposed, correctly exposed, and overexposed shots, which can then be combined or selected from to produce an image with optimal exposure, especially in high-contrast situations. | This camera supports autobracketing, so you don't have to worry about getting the perfect exposure every time.  这台相机支持自动包围曝光，所以你不用担心每次都要获得完美的曝光。| 近义词: exposure bracketing, bracketing | 反义词: single exposure |词性: noun
