diastyle: /ˈdaɪəˌstaɪl/ | n. | 间柱，列间 | The space between two columns.  The diastyle of the Parthenon is relatively wide. | 例句：The architect carefully considered the diastyle when designing the temple.  建筑师在设计寺庙时仔细考虑了列间距。

例句：The wider diastyle gave the building a sense of spaciousness.  较宽的列间距使建筑物显得宽敞。| 近义词：intercolumniation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *διάστυλος (diastylos)  "having columns two diameters apart," from διά (dia) "through, across" + στῦλος (stylos) "column, pillar."
