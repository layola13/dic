overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ | v. n. | 超载，过载；使超负荷；超负荷工作 | To load with too great a burden or cargo; overburden. To put excessive demands on (a person, system, etc.). | The circuit was overloaded and blew a fuse. 电路超载，烧断了保险丝。 | 近义词: overburden, overtax, overwhelm | 反义词: underload | 词性: 动词，名词


overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ | n. | 超载，过载；超负荷工作；过多信息 | An excessive load or amount. |  Information overload can make it difficult to focus. 信息过载会让人难以集中注意力。 | 近义词: excess, surfeit, glut | 反义词: shortage, deficit | 词性: 名词
