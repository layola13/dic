engrieve:/ɪnˈɡriːv/| vt.|使悲伤，使苦恼|To cause to feel grief or sorrow. |例句：The news of her death engrieves us all. 她去世的消息使我们都很悲伤。

例句：He was deeply grieved by the loss of his friend. 他因失去朋友而深感悲痛。|近义词：sadden, distress, upset, grieve, afflict|反义词：comfort, console, gladden|词性:动词
词源:From Old French *engrever, from en- (“in, on”) + grever (“to burden, aggrieve”), from Latin gravare, from gravis (“heavy”).
