gtc:/dʒiː tiː siː/ |abbr.| Good Till Cancelled (有效直至取消) | A stock market order that remains valid until it is executed or cancelled. | Example: I placed a GTC order to buy 100 shares of Apple. 我下了一个GTC订单购买100股苹果股票。 | Synonym: open order | Antonym: day order | abbreviation

gtc:/dʒiː tiː siː/ |abbr.| General Terms and Conditions (一般条款和条件) | The standard rules and regulations governing a contract or agreement. | Example: Please read the GTC carefully before signing the contract. 请在签订合同之前仔细阅读一般条款和条件。 | Synonym: terms of service, terms of use | Antonym: N/A | abbreviation
