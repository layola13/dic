multivalence:/ˌmʌltɪˈveɪləns/
n.|多价; 多重价值; 多重意义|The state or quality of having multiple meanings, interpretations, or values.|The poem’s multivalence allows for a variety of interpretations.|polysemy, plurality, multiplicity|singularity, univocality|noun
