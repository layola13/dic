basketlike:/ˈbæskɪtlaɪk/
adj.
像篮子一样的；篮状的
Resembling a basket in shape or form.
The bird built a basketlike nest of twigs and leaves.  这只鸟用小树枝和树叶筑了一个像篮子一样的巢。
The hot air balloon had a large, basketlike gondola.  热气球有一个巨大的，像篮子一样的吊篮。
近义词:basket-shaped, wicker
