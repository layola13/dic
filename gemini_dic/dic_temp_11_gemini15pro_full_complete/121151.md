misdescribing:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/
verb
错误地描述；歪曲；描述不当
Giving a wrong or inaccurate account of something.
He was misdescribing the situation to make himself look better. 他为了让自己看起来更好而歪曲了情况。
misrepresent, distort, falsify
accurately describe, portray truthfully
verb
