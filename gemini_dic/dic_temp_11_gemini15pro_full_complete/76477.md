galactically:/ɡəˈlæktɪkli/
adverb
银河地，宇宙地
Relating to or on the scale of a galaxy or galaxies.
The problem is galactically significant. 这个问题具有宇宙级的意义。
The distances involved are galactically vast.  所涉及的距离在宇宙尺度上是巨大的。
近义词: universally, cosmically
反义词: locally, terrestrially
词性: adverb
词源: From galaxy + -ic + -ally.  Ultimately from the Greek galaxias kyklos "milky circle."
