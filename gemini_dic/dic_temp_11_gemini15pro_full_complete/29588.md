catechismal: /ˌkætɪˈkɪzməl/ | adj. | 问答式的，教义问答的 | Relating to a catechism, which is a summary of religious doctrine in the form of questions and answers, often used for instruction. |  The priest's tone was catechismal, as if he expected rote responses. 牧师的语气像是教义问答式的，好像他期待着死记硬背的回答。 | The catechismal method of teaching can be effective for memorization. 教义问答式的教学方法对记忆很有效。 | 近义词: didactic, doctrinal, pedagogic | 反义词: n/a | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin "catechismus" (instruction, catechism), from Greek "katēkhein" (to teach orally, instruct), from "kata-" (down) + "ēkhein" (to sound, resound).
