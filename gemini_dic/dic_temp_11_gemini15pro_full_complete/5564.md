alsatian:/ælˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 阿尔萨斯狗，德国牧羊犬 | A breed of large, strong dog, originally from Alsace in France, often used as guard dogs or by the police or army.  Also known as German Shepherd. |  例句：The police officer and his Alsatian patrolled the park.  警察和他的阿尔萨斯犬在公园里巡逻。

例句：My neighbour has a well-trained Alsatian. 我邻居有一只训练有素的阿尔萨斯犬。| 近义词：German Shepherd, German Shepherd Dog | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑小型犬种 chihuahua 吉娃娃等) | 词性: noun
