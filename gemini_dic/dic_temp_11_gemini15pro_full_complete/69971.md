festlich:/ˈfɛstlɪç/| adj.|节日的，喜庆的，欢乐的|Relating to or suitable for a festival or celebration; festive.  Characterized by festivity and celebration. Conveying a sense of joy and merriment appropriate for a special occasion.|The festlich atmosphere filled the town square. 节日的气氛充满了城镇广场。|Synonyms: festive, celebratory, joyous, merry, jovial|Antonyms: somber, gloomy, mournful, austere|adjective
