Herbert J. Freudenberger, the psychologist who coined the term "burnout".

burnout: /ˈbɜːrnaʊt/
n.
倦怠，精疲力竭
A state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress.
He was suffering from burnout after years of working long hours. 他多年长时间工作后，身心俱疲。
She took a sabbatical to recover from burnout. 她休假以从倦怠中恢复过来。
Synonym: exhaustion, fatigue, overwork
Antonym:  energy, vigor, rejuvenation
词性: noun (名词)
