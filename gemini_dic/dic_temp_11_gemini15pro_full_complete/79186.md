glamourizes: 使...增添魅力，美化，渲染
glamourizes:  Makes something seem more attractive or exciting than it really is, often by ignoring or hiding its less desirable aspects.
glamourizes: The movie glamourizes violence, making it seem heroic.  这部电影美化了暴力，使其看起来像英雄行为。
glamourizes: romanticizes, idealizes, glorifies
glamourizes: downplays, de-emphasizes, vilifies
glamourizes: verb
