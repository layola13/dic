noncryptically:/ˌnɒnkrɪpˈtɪkli/ | adv. | 非密码地；明白地；直截了当地 | In a way that is not cryptic; clearly and straightforwardly. | He explained the situation noncryptically so everyone could understand.  他直截了当地解释了情况，以便每个人都能理解。| Synonyms: clearly, plainly, openly, straightforwardly, explicitly, directly, frankly, candidly | Antonyms: cryptically, obscurely, vaguely, ambiguously | Adverb

Etymology:  From non- + cryptically.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "cryptically" comes from "cryptic," meaning "having a hidden meaning."  Ultimately derived from Greek "kryptikos" meaning "fit for concealing."
