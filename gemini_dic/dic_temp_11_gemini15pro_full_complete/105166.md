ketzel:/ˈkɛtsəl/| n.|小猫，猫咪 (尤指犹太人对小孩的爱称)|A term of endearment for a child, especially among Yiddish speakers, meaning "kitten."|例句：Look at that cute little ketzel playing in the park. 看看那个在公园里玩耍的可爱的小猫咪。

例句：She called her grandson her little ketzel. 她称她的孙子为她的小猫咪。|近义词：kitten, child, kid, little one, sweetie, darling|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it's a term of endearment.)|词性:noun
