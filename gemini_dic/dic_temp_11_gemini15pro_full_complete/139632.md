occludent:/əˈkluːd(ə)nt/| n. adj.|闭塞物，闭合的|Something that occludes, or blocks; closed or blocked.  An occludent sound in phonetics is one made by completely blocking the passage of air from the lungs.|The dentist recommended an occludent to cover the exposed nerve. 牙医建议用一种闭塞物来覆盖暴露的神经。|近义词：obstruent, blockage, barrier|反义词:aperture, opening|词性:名词, 形容词
occlude:/əˈkluːd/| vt.| 闭塞，阻塞| To stop, close, or obstruct.|The thrombus occluded the artery. 血栓阻塞了动脉。|近义词：obstruct, block, close|反义词:open, unblock|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
