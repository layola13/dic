offloaders:/ˈɔːfˌləʊdərz/
n.
卸货机;卸货员;卸载程序
People or machines that remove loads from something, especially a ship or truck.
The offloaders worked quickly to get the cargo off the ship. 卸货员迅速地将货物从船上卸下。
Automated offloaders are used in many modern ports. 许多现代港口使用自动卸货机。
