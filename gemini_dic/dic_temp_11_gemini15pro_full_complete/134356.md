noninterchangeable:/ˌnɒnɪntətʃeɪndʒəbl/
| adj. | 不可互换的，不可替换的 | Not able to be exchanged or used in place of something else. | These parts are noninterchangeable, so make sure you order the correct one.  这些零件不可互换，所以确保你订购的是正确的。| The original documents are noninterchangeable. 原始文件是不可替换的。 | Synonyms: irreplaceable, nonsubstitutable, unique | Antonyms: interchangeable, replaceable, substitutable | adjective
