nonsibilances:/nɒnˈsɪbɪlənsɪz/
noun
非咝擦音; 非摩擦音
Sounds that are not produced by forcing air through a narrow channel in the mouth, such as /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, /g/, /m/, /n/, /ŋ/, /l/, /r/, /w/, and /j/.
The word "plot" is made up entirely of nonsibilances.  这个单词“plot”完全由非咝擦音构成。
Near Synonyms: non-sibilant sounds, non-fricative consonants (for some)
Antonyms: sibilants
