candidate: /ˈkændɪdət/ (US) /ˈkændɪdeɪt/ (UK)| n. |候选人；应试者|A person who applies for a job or is nominated for election.|例句：She is a strong candidate for the presidency. 她是一位强有力的总统候选人。|近义词：applicant, nominee, contender|反义词：incumbent (现任者)|词性：名词

candidate: /ˈkændɪdət/ (US) /ˈkændɪdeɪt/ (UK)| adj. (旧用法/罕见)|合适的；有资格的|Suitable or likely to be chosen.|例句：He was a candidate location for the new factory. 他是新工厂的候选地点。|近义词：suitable, eligible, likely|反义词：unsuitable, ineligible|词性：形容词 (旧用法/罕见)
