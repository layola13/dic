micromanipulate:/ˌmaɪkroʊməˈnɪpjuleɪt/| v. |微操，显微操作|To manipulate or control something very small and precise, typically using a microscope or specialized tools.|Scientists micromanipulated the DNA to insert a new gene. 科学家们对DNA进行了微操，以插入一个新的基因。|Example: Using a tiny needle, the surgeon micromanipulated the blood vessel to restore blood flow. 使用一根细小的针，外科医生对血管进行了微操，以恢复血流。|Synonyms: manipulate, control, adjust, fine-tune|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|verb

micromanipulation:/ˌmaɪkroʊməˌnɪpjʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |微操，显微操作|The act or process of micromanipulating something.|Micromanipulation is a key technique in genetic engineering. 显微操作是基因工程中的一项关键技术。|Example: The micromanipulation of cells allowed researchers to study their behavior in detail. 对细胞的显微操作使研究人员能够详细研究它们的行为。|Synonyms: manipulation, control|Antonyms: neglect, disregard|noun
