licensed: /ˈlaɪ.sənst/ (US) /ˈlaɪ.sənst/ (UK)| adj.|获得许可的，得到授权的|Having been granted a license, especially to do something professionally.|The restaurant is licensed to sell alcohol. 这家餐厅有售酒许可证。|近义词：authorized, permitted, certified|反义词：unlicensed, unauthorized, illegal|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "license," which comes from the Old French "licence" and ultimately from the Latin "licentia," meaning "freedom, permission."
