PHRASE->
(Since "gradino" is a very specific Italian word for "step", it doesn't have common English phrases associated with it.  However, here are some phrases using the English equivalent "step":)

step by step = 一步一步地
watch your step = 小心脚下
one step at a time = 一次一步; 循序渐进
a step in the right direction = 朝着正确方向迈出的一步
take a step back = 退一步


词源: Italian, from Latin *gradiminus, from gradus "step".
