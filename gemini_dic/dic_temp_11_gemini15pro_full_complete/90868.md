hunk:/hʌŋk/ | n. | 大块; 健壮英俊的男子; |A large piece of something, especially food.  A large, strong, and attractive man. | He took a large hunk of bread. 他拿了一大块面包。 She married a real hunk. 她嫁给了一个真正健壮英俊的男人。 | 近义词:chunk, piece, slab (for a large piece); stud, beefcake (for an attractive man) | 反义词:crumb, sliver (for a large piece) | 词性:名词

Etymology:  Likely from Middle Dutch *hunke "lump, hunch," related to hinken "to limp." The sense of "attractive man" is a later development, possibly influenced by the idea of a "well-formed piece."
