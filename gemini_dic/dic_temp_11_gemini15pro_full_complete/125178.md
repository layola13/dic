multicandidate:/ˌmʌltiˈkændɪdət/| adj.|多候选人的|Involving or having several candidates.  Describes a situation, typically an election, where more than two candidates are competing for the same position.|例句：The multicandidate election resulted in a surprising upset. 多候选人选举导致了令人惊讶的逆转。|近义词：multi-contender, multi-runner|反义词：two-candidate, single-candidate|词性:adjective

multicandidate:/ˌmʌltiˈkændɪdət/| n.|多候选人选举|An election in which several candidates are competing for the same position.|例句：Multicandidate elections often lead to coalition governments. 多候选人选举常常导致联合政府。|近义词：multi-contender election, multi-runner election|反义词：two-candidate election, single-candidate election|词性:noun
