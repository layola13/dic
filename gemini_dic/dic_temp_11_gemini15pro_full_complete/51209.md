destitute:/ˈdɛstɪˌtjut/ /ˈdɛstɪˌtuːt/| adj.|贫困的，赤贫的，缺乏的|Without the basic necessities of life; lacking something needed or desirable.|Many families were left destitute by the war. 战争使许多家庭一贫如洗。|Synonyms: impoverished, indigent, penniless, needy, deprived|Antonyms: wealthy, affluent, prosperous|adjective

destitute:/ˈdɛstɪˌtjut/ /ˈdɛstɪˌtuːt/| n.|赤贫者|A person without the basic necessities of life.|The government provides shelters for the destitute. 政府为赤贫者提供住所。|Synonyms: pauper, beggar|Antonyms: millionaire, tycoon|noun
