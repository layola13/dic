fubbery:/ˈfʌbəri/
noun
欺骗；胡说八道；愚弄
Deliberate deceit or trickery; nonsense.
His explanation was complete fubbery. 他的解释完全是胡说八道。
synonyms: nonsense, baloney, claptrap, humbug, hogwash, bunkum, hokum, drivel, twaddle, gibberish, gobbledegook, mumbo jumbo, piffle, tommyrot
antonyms: truth, honesty, fact
