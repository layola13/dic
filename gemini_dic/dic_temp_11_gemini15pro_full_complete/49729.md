democratizer:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzər/| n.|民主化者，民主推动者|A person or thing that promotes or enables democratization.  Someone or something that makes something more accessible to more people.|The internet has been a great democratizer of information, allowing access to knowledge previously held by only a select few. 互联网极大地促进了信息的民主化，让更多人能够接触到以前只有少数人掌握的知识。|Synonyms: reformer, liberalizer, advocate, champion|Antonyms: dictator, autocrat, oppressor|noun
