ideography:/ˌaɪdiˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌaɪdiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |表意文字；表意体系|A system of writing in which each symbol represents an idea or concept.|Chinese characters are an example of ideography. 汉字是表意文字的一个例子。|Synonym:  ideogram, logogram|Antonym: phonography|noun
