destock:/ˌdiːˈstɒk/ (US) /ˌdiːˈstɑːk/| v. |减少库存，清仓甩卖|To reduce the quantity of goods in stock, typically by selling them at a reduced price.|The company is destocking winter clothes to make way for the spring collection.  该公司正在清仓甩卖冬装，以便为春季系列腾出空间。|例句：They're destocking last season's fashions at half price. 他们正在半价清仓甩卖上一季的时装。

例句：The warehouse is destocking due to oversupply.  由于供应过剩，仓库正在清仓。|近义词：liquidate, clear out, sell off|反义词：stock up, accumulate|词性：动词
