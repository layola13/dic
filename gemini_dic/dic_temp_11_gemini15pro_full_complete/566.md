abortively:/əˈbɔːrtɪvli/
adverb
未成功地；流产地；夭折地
in a way that is unsuccessful; prematurely
The mission ended abortively due to bad weather.  由于恶劣天气，任务以失败告终。
The rebellion was abortively crushed.  叛乱被迅速镇压了。
近义词: unsuccessfully, prematurely, fruitlessly
反义词: successfully, completely, effectively
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *abortivus, from abortus (“miscarriage, abortion”), past participle of aboriri (“to miscarry, disappear”), from ab- (“away from”) + oriri (“to arise, be born”).
