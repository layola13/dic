amr:/ˈeɪmɑːr/ (US), /ˈæmɑːr/ (UK)| n.| 自動訊息閱讀器 (Automatic Message Reader)| An automatic message reader (AMR) is a software application that converts speech to text, allowing users to listen to their messages instead of reading them.  It is often used in voicemail systems and other messaging platforms. |Example: The company implemented an AMR system to improve accessibility for visually impaired employees.  公司实施了自动消息阅读器系统，以提高视障员工的可访问性。

Example:  The AMR transcribed the voicemail message accurately. 自动消息阅读器准确地转录了语音邮件消息。 |Synonym: voice-to-text, speech recognition software|Antonym: text-to-speech| Noun
