egs:/iːdʒiːˈɛs/ | n. | 例子；实例 | examples (plural)的缩写，用于非正式场合，表示“例如” | Egs: apples, bananas, and oranges. 例如：苹果、香蕉和橘子。 | 近义词: examples, instances, illustrations | 反义词:  (无直接的反义词，可以考虑 exceptions 例外) | abbreviation


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to improve his skills? = 他做了些什么来提高他的技能？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying for the past few months? = 过去几个月她一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? = 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后会做什么？
What will happen if we don't take action? = 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what he wanted, I would tell him. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我会告诉他。
I wish I knew what the future holds. = 我希望我知道未来会怎样。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:

She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
