oenanthylous:/iːˈnænθɪləs/
|adj.|
|有葡萄酒香味的；似葡萄酒气味的|
|Having a smell or flavor resembling that of wine, specifically reminiscent of oenanthol, a fragrant aldehyde found in wine.|
|The oenanthylous bouquet of the aged wine filled the cellar.  这瓶陈年葡萄酒的酒香弥漫了整个酒窖。|
|The cheese had a distinctly oenanthylous aroma.  这种奶酪有一种明显的葡萄酒香味。|
|近义词:vinous, winy|
|反义词:odorless|
|词性:形容词|
