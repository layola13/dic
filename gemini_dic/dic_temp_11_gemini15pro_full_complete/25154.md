buckram:/ˈbʌkrəm/| n. adj.|粗硬棉布，粗硬的|A coarse, stiff cloth made of cotton or linen, sized with glue and used for bookbindings, interlinings, and stiffening garments. Also, stiff and unyielding in manner.|例句：The book was bound in buckram. 这本书是用粗硬棉布装订的。

The curtains were made of a heavy buckram. 窗帘是用厚重的粗硬棉布做的。|近义词：stiff, rigid, inflexible|反义词：flexible, pliable, soft|词性:名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bouquerant* (modern French *boucassin*), from Italian *boquerano*, perhaps from *Bukhara*, a city in Uzbekistan.
