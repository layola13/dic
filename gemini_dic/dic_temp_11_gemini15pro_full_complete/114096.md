magnifier:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪər/| n.|扩大者，夸大者|Something that increases or exaggerates.|The media can be a magnifier of social problems. 媒体可以夸大社会问题。|Synonyms: amplifier, intensifier|Antonyms: diminisher, downplayer|noun
