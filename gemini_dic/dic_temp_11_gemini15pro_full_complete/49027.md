degreased:/diːˈɡriːst/| adj.|脱脂的，去油的|Removed grease or fat from something.  The process of removing grease or oil from something, typically a machine part or surface, often using a solvent or detergent.|The mechanic degreased the engine before repairing it. 机械师在修理发动机之前先对其进行了脱脂。|Synonyms: cleaned, cleansed, purified, scrubbed|Antonyms: greasy, oily|adjective

degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/| vt.|脱脂，去油|To remove grease or fat from something.|I need to degrease the oven before I can use it again. 我需要先把烤箱除油才能再次使用。|Synonyms: clean, cleanse, purify, scrub|Antonyms: grease, oil|verb
