inversion's:/ɪnˈvɜːrʒənz/
noun
反转，倒置，逆序
The process of reversing the normal order of something, or the reversed order.
Temperature inversion's can trap pollution close to the ground. 逆温会将污染物困在地表附近。
synonyms: reversal, turning, overturning, overthrow, upset, reversion
antonyms: order, arrangement


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French inversion, or from late Latin inversio(n-), from the verb invertere ‘turn upside down’ (see invert).
