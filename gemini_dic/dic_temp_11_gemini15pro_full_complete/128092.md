ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nesche*, from Old English *hnesce*, from Proto-Germanic *knaskaz*, from Proto-Indo-European *kneh₂- (“soft”). Cognate with Scots nesh (“soft, delicate”), West Frisian nêsk (“soft”), Dutch nesch (“delicate”), German nesch (“delicate, fastidious”). More distantly related to English nice.
