nonoxidizing:/ˌnɒnˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 非氧化性的 | Not causing or promoting oxidation; inert.  Relating to or denoting an atmosphere that does not readily support combustion. | Argon is a nonoxidizing gas and is often used in welding to protect the weld from atmospheric oxygen. 氩气是一种非氧化性气体，常用于焊接中保护焊缝免受大气氧气的影响。 | Inert, non-reactive | Oxidizing, corrosive | adjective
