griping:/ˈɡraɪpɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡraɪpɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|抱怨的，令人不满的；抱怨，牢骚|Complaining; expressing discontent. Also, the act of complaining.|例句：The constant griping about the food was getting tiresome.  不断抱怨食物让人厌烦。

例句：He couldn't stand her endless griping. 他受不了她没完没了的抱怨。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, whining, moaning|反义词：praising, commending, approving|词性：adjective, noun (gerund)

例句：Enough of your griping! Let's find a solution.  别再抱怨了！让我们找到一个解决方案。|近义词：complaints, grumbles, whines, moans|反义词：praise, commendation, approval|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "gripe," which comes from the Old English word "grīpan," meaning "to seize, grasp, grip." The meaning shifted over time to include "to afflict with pain" and eventually "to complain."
