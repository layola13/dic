aspermous:/əˈspɜːrməs/
| adj. |无种子的，无精子的|Lacking seeds; seedless.  Also, lacking sperm or male gametes.|The fern is an aspermous plant. 蕨类植物是无种子植物。|Near Synonyms: seedless, sterile|Antonyms: seed-bearing, fertile
