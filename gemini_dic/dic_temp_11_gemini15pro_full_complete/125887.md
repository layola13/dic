muscovado:/ˌmʌskəˈvɑːdoʊ/| n. |赤砂糖，黑砂糖|Unrefined or partially refined cane sugar with a strong molasses flavor and dark brown color. |例句：I used muscovado sugar in the cookies for a richer flavor. 我在饼干里用了赤砂糖，味道更浓郁。

例句：Muscovado sugar adds a lovely caramel note to the coffee. 赤砂糖给咖啡增添了可爱的焦糖味道。|近义词：brown sugar, jaggery, panela|反义词:white sugar, refined sugar|词性:noun
