nonapostolical:/ˌnɒnˌæpəˈstɒlɪkəl/
adjective
非使徒的；不符合使徒传统的
Not of or relating to the apostles or their teachings; not conforming to the doctrines or practices of the apostles.
The group held nonapostolical beliefs, rejecting the authority of the original apostles.  这个团体持有非使徒的信仰，拒绝原始使徒的权威。
The sect's nonapostolical practices led to their expulsion from the larger church.  该教派的非使徒做法导致他们被更大的教会驱逐。
Near Synonyms: unorthodox, heterodox, heretical, dissenting
Antonyms: apostolic, orthodox
