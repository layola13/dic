backless:/ˈbækləs/| adj.|无背的，无靠背的|Having no back or back support.|She wore a backless dress to the party. 她穿了一件露背的连衣裙去参加聚会。|Synonyms: open-backed, sleeveless (for dresses/tops)|Antonyms: backed, supported|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From back + -less.  "Back" comes from Old English "bæc," meaning "the back part of the human body."  The suffix "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas" meaning "without, free from."
