nontarnishable:/ˌnɒnˈtɑːnɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 不易失去光泽的，不锈的 | Not liable to tarnish; resistant to discoloration or corrosion. |  This silver is nontarnishable, so it will always look new.  这种银是不变色的，所以它看起来总是新的。| Example: She invested in nontarnishable silverware for her dining room. 她为餐厅购置了不会失去光泽的银餐具。| Synonyms: stainless, corrosion-resistant, untarnishable | Antonyms: tarnishable, corrodible | word type: Adjective
