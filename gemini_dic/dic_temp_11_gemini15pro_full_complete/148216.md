overshrinks:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋks/
verb
过度缩水
causes something to shrink excessively
The dry cleaner overshrunk my favorite sweater.  干洗店把我最喜欢的毛衣洗缩水了。
overshrink (past tense): overshrank /ˌoʊvərˈʃræŋk/
overshrink (past participle): overshrunk /ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋk/
overshrinking (present participle): overshrinking /ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ/
Synonyms: shrink excessively, contract too much
Antonyms: stretch, expand
词性: 动词
