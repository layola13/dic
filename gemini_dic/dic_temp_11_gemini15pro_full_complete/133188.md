Lack of evidence; information that does not constitute evidence.

词性:n.

Etymology:
From non- + evidence.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of." "Evidence" comes from Old French "evidence" and Latin "evidentia," meaning "clearness, distinctness, proof."
