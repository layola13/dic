gastronome:/ˈɡæstrənəʊm/ (US) /ˈɡæstrəˌnoʊm/| n. |美食家；讲究吃喝的人|A gastronome is someone who enjoys good food and drink and knows a lot about them. |例句：He is a true gastronome, always seeking out the finest restaurants. 他是一位真正的美食家，总是寻找最好的餐厅。

例句：The gastronome savored each bite of the exquisite meal.  这位美食家细细品味着这顿精致的佳肴。


近义词：gourmet, epicure, connoisseur|反义词:glutton (偏贬义 - 指贪吃的人)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gastronome*, from Greek *gastēr* (belly) + *nomos* (law, custom).  Literally, "laws of the stomach/belly".
