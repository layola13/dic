out-querying:/ˌaʊtˈkwɪəriɪŋ/
|v.|胜过查询；在查询方面超越|Surpassing or exceeding someone in the ability to query or retrieve information, especially through database searches or online research.|She was out-querying her colleagues, finding information they couldn't access. 她胜过她的同事，找到了他们无法访问的信息。|The new search algorithm is out-querying all the previous versions in terms of speed and accuracy.  新的搜索算法在速度和准确性方面都胜过所有以前的版本。|近义词: outperforming, surpassing, exceeding|反义词: underperforming, lagging|词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "querying" (present participle of "query," meaning to ask a question or request information).
