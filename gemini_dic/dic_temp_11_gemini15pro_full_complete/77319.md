gatherings:/ˈɡæðərɪŋz/
n.
集会，聚会
assemblies, meetings, congregations of people for social or other purposes.
We had family gatherings every Christmas.  我们每年圣诞节都会举行家庭聚会。
The town square was the site of many political gatherings.  城镇广场是许多政治集会的场所。
近义词：assemblies, meetings, congregations, get-togethers
反义词：dispersals, separations
词性：名词
