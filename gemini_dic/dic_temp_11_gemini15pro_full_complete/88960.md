holeyest:/ˈhoʊliɪst/| adj.|最神圣的，最虔诚的|Having the most holes.  Figuratively, most devout or pious, though often used ironically or humorously.  Derived from "holy" with the superlative suffix "-est," but playing on the literal meaning of "hole."|The holeyest pair of socks I own have more holes than sock material.  我最神圣的（满是洞的）袜子洞比袜子本身还多。|近义词：sacred, divine, pious (when used seriously)|反义词：profane, unholy, impious|词性:形容词
