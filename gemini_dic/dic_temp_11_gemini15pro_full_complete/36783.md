colobuses:/ˈkɒləbəsiz/
n.
疣猴（复数）
Old World monkeys of the subfamily Colobinae, typically having a long tail and living in trees.
Colobuses are known for their specialized digestive systems that allow them to eat leaves. 疣猴以其特殊的消化系统而闻名，这使得它们能够吃树叶。
Colobus monkeys are often hunted for their fur. 疣猴经常因为它们的皮毛而被猎杀。
近义词: monkeys, primates
词性: noun (plural)
