botryose:/ˈbɒtriəʊs/ (US) /ˈbɑːtrioʊs/| adj.|葡萄状的，似葡萄串的|Resembling a bunch of grapes; clustered like a bunch of grapes.  The mineral formed in botryose masses.|The sample exhibited a botryose structure, with small spherical crystals clustered together like grapes.|Synonyms: clustered, grapelike, racemose|Antonyms:  diffuse, scattered|adjective
