bed-chamber:/ˈbedˌtʃeɪmbər/ (US), /ˈbedˌtʃeɪmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 卧室；寝宫 | A room used primarily for sleeping. | The king retired to his bed-chamber for the night. 国王回到他的寝宫过夜。| 近义词: bedroom, chamber | 反义词: living room, dining room | 词性: 名词

bed-chamber: 词源来自古英语 *bedd-cambr, 由 bed (床) 和 chamber (房间) 组成。
