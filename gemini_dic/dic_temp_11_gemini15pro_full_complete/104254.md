juryless: /ˈdʒʊəri.ləs/
adjective
无陪审团的
conducted without a jury; (of a court or tribunal) not using a jury.
The defendant opted for a juryless trial.  被告选择了无陪审团审判。
The case was heard in a juryless court.  该案件在一个没有陪审团的法庭审理。
