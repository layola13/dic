butcher:/ˈbʊtʃər/ (US), /ˈbʊtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|屠夫，肉贩；屠宰；残杀|A person who slaughters animals for food or who sells meat.  To slaughter or dress (animals) for meat; to kill brutally or carelessly.|The butcher prepared the cuts of beef for the customers. 屠夫为顾客准备了牛肉块。|近义词: slaughterer, meat cutter, killer|反义词: healer, protector|词性: noun, verb
