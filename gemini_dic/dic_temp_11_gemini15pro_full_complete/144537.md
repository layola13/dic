outvie: /aʊtˈvaɪ/
verb
胜过，超越
surpass, excel, or defeat someone in competition or rivalry.
He outvied his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中胜过对手。
She outvied all the other applicants for the job. 她胜过了所有其他求职者。
surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip, beat, defeat
lose to, be inferior to
词性：动词
