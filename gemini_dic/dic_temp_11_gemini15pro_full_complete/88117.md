hijackers:/ˈhaɪˌdʒækərz/| n. |劫持者，劫机者|A person who illegally seizes an aircraft, ship, or vehicle in transit and forces it to go to a different destination or uses it for their own purposes.|The hijackers threatened to kill the passengers if their demands were not met. 劫机者威胁说如果他们的要求得不到满足就杀死乘客。|近义词：captors, abductors, kidnappers|反义词:rescuers, liberators|noun

hijacker:/ˈhaɪˌdʒækər/| n. |劫持者，劫机者|Someone who hijacks something, especially an aircraft.|The hijacker forced the pilot to change course. 劫持者强迫飞行员改变航线。|近义词：abductor, kidnapper|反义词: liberator|noun
