organogram: /ˈɔːɡənəɡræm/ | n. | 组织结构图；机构图 | A diagram that shows the structure of an organization, especially the relationships between departments and jobs. | The company's organogram clearly shows who reports to whom.  公司组织结构图清楚地显示了谁向谁汇报。|  The new CEO requested an updated organogram to understand the company's hierarchy. 新任首席执行官要求提供最新的组织结构图，以了解公司的层级结构。|Synonyms: organizational chart, organization chart, structure chart | Antonyms: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *organo-* + *-gram*.  *Organo-* refers to organization or organs (as in parts of a body or system). *-Gram* denotes something written or drawn, like a diagram.  So, an organogram is a drawing of an organization.
