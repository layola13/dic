nodule:/ˈnɒdʒuːl/ (British), /ˈnɑːdʒuːl/ (American)| n. |小结，小瘤，节结|A small rounded lump or swelling, especially an abnormal one in a tissue or organ. |例句：The doctor discovered a nodule on her thyroid gland. 医生在她的甲状腺上发现了一个小结。

|例句：The plant's roots were covered in nitrogen-fixing nodules.  这种植物的根部覆盖着固氮根瘤。
|近义词：lump, bump, swelling, growth, node|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "concavity" for the opposite shape)|词性:noun


nodule:/ˈnɒdʒuːl/ (British), /ˈnɑːdʒuːl/ (American)| n. |小块，小团|A small, irregularly shaped mass or piece of something. |例句：Nodules of clay were found in the soil. 在土壤中发现了小块的粘土。

|例句：He rolled the putty into small nodules. 他把油灰揉成小团。
|近义词：lump, nugget, chunk|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
