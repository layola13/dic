furring:/ˈfɜːrɪŋ/| v.| （使）结垢，（使）生苔；（使）长毛|The pipes are furring up with limescale.  这些管道因积聚石灰垢而堵塞。 |becoming coated with fur or a similar substance|近义词：encrusting, scaling|反义词: cleaning, clearing| verb
