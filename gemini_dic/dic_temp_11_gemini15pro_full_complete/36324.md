cohostesses:/ˌkoʊˈhoʊstɪsɪz/
n.
女共同主持人
A woman who jointly hosts an event or program with another person, typically a man.
The cohostesses welcomed the guests warmly.  两位女共同主持人热情地欢迎了来宾。
The awards ceremony was presented by two charming cohostesses. 颁奖典礼由两位迷人的女共同主持人主持。
近义词：co-hosts, joint hosts
反义词:guest, attendee
词性:noun
