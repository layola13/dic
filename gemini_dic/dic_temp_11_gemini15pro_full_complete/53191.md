dipstick:/ˈdɪpstɪk/| n.|量油尺；(测试用的)试纸；笨蛋|A dipstick is a graduated rod for measuring the depth of a liquid, especially oil in a vehicle's engine.  It can also refer to a strip of paper or plastic used for testing, or informally, a foolish person.|例句：The mechanic checked the oil level with the dipstick. 技工用量油尺检查了油位。

例句：He used a dipstick to test the pH of the soil. 他用试纸测试了土壤的pH值。

|近义词：gauge, indicator, rod (for measuring), test strip|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
