advance:/ədˈvæns/ /ədˈvɑːns/| n.  vt. vi. adj.|前进，进步；预付款；进展|An act of moving forward.  To make progress or move forward. To pay or lend (money) before it is due. Situated or occurring before.|
The army's advance was halted by the enemy. 部队的推进被敌人阻止了。

He advanced towards me with a smile. 他笑着向我走过来。

The company advanced him some money for the trip. 公司预支给他一些旅费。

There were advance warnings of the storm.  风暴来临前有预警。

近义词：progress, development, improvement, proceed, further, promote, prepayment, loan|
反义词：retreat, decline, hinder, delay, debt|
词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词
