hasn't:/ˈhæzənt/|aux. v.|没有|Contraction of "has not".  Used to indicate the negative present perfect tense of "have".|She hasn't finished her homework yet. 她还没有完成作业。|He hasn't eaten breakfast this morning. 他今天早上还没有吃早餐。|近义词：does not have, has not|反义词:has|词性:助动词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He doesn't have a car. 他没有车。
She doesn't speak French. 她不会说法语。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He isn't working today. 他今天不工作。
She isn't reading at the moment. 她现在没有在读书。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He hasn't been to Paris. 他没去过巴黎。
She hasn't seen that movie. 她没看过那部电影。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He hasn't been sleeping well lately. 他最近睡眠不好。
She hasn't been working on that project. 她最近没有在做那个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He didn't go to school yesterday. 他昨天没去上学。
She didn't finish her work. 她没有完成她的工作。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He wasn't working when I called. 我打电话时他没在工作。
She wasn't reading at that time. 她当时没在读书。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He hadn't finished his work before I arrived.  在我到达之前他还没有完成他的工作。
She hadn't eaten anything all day. 她一整天都没吃东西。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He hadn't been feeling well for a week. 他一周以来身体一直不好。
She hadn't been studying very hard. 她一直没有很努力学习。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He won't have time tomorrow. 他明天没时间。 (This refers to possession, not a completed action)
She won't be here next week. 她下周不会在这里。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He won't be working tomorrow. 他明天不工作。
She won't be traveling next month. 她下个月不会去旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
He won't have finished the project by next week. 他下周之前不会完成这个项目。
She won't have graduated by then. 到那时她将还没有毕业。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, he won't have been working here for very long. 到明年，他在这里工作的时间不会很长。(Negative usage is less common but still grammatical)
By the time she arrives, we won't have been waiting long.  在她到达之前，我们等待的时间不会很长。(Negative usage is less common but still grammatical)


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't helped me, I wouldn't have finished. 如果他没有帮助我，我就不会完成了。
I wish she hadn't said that. 我希望她没有说过那句话。


被动语态:
The project hasn't been completed yet.  项目还没有完成。
The email hasn't been sent.  邮件还没有发送。



疑问句:
Hasn't she finished her work yet? 她还没完成工作吗？
Hasn't he arrived yet? 他还没到吗？


否定句:
He hasn't left yet. 他还没有离开。
She hasn't called back. 她还没有回电话。



PHRASE->
He hasn't a clue. 他一点都不知道。
She hasn't the slightest idea. 她一点都不知道。
He hasn't any money. 他没有钱。
She hasn't much time. 她没有多少时间。
It hasn't been easy. 这不容易。


注意事项:
"Hasn't" is primarily used in American English.  British English more commonly uses "has not," especially in formal writing.  However, "hasn't" is acceptable in informal British English speech and writing.
