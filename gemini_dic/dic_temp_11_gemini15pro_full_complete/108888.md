lecturership: /ˈlɛktʃərərʃɪp/ (UK), /ˈlɛktʃərərʃɪp/ (US)
n.
大学讲师职位；讲师的职务
A position as a lecturer, especially at a university or college.
She was awarded a lecturership in the history department. 她获得了历史系的讲师职位。
He applied for a lecturership at several universities. 他申请了几个大学的讲师职位。
Synonyms:  lectureship, teaching post, faculty position
Antonyms:  studentship, scholarship (these are opposites in terms of role, not the word itself)
noun
