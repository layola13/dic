deposes:/dɪˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
罢免; 免职; 废黜; 宣誓作证
to remove someone important from a powerful position
例句：The king was deposed by his own son. 国王被他自己的儿子废黜了。

例句：The president was deposed in a coup d'état. 总统在一次政变中被罢免了。

to state something, especially in a law court, or to give a written statement
例句：She deposed that she had seen the accused leaving the scene of the crime. 她宣誓作证说她看到被告离开了犯罪现场。

例句：He deposed that he had no knowledge of the missing funds. 他作证说他对丢失的资金一无所知。

近义词：remove, oust, dethrone, testify, declare
反义词：appoint, install, reinstate
词性：v.
