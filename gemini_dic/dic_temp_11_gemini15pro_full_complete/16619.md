baronetcy:/ˈbærənɪtsi/
n.
男爵爵位；男爵身份
the rank or status of a baronet.
Sir Walter inherited the baronetcy from his father. 沃尔特爵士继承了他父亲的男爵爵位。
近义词:baronetage
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


baronetcy:/ˈbærənɪtsi/
n.
男爵爵位授予
the bestowing of the rank of baronet.
The baronetcy was created in 1603.  男爵爵位是1603年设立的。
近义词:N/A
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Middle French *baronete, from Old French *baronnet (diminutive of baron),  + -cy. Ultimately from Late Latin baro.
