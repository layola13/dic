modifiable:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪəbl/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪfaɪəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可修改的，可更改的|Something that is modifiable can be changed or altered. |The software is modifiable to suit individual needs.  这个软件可以修改以适应个人需求。

The terms of the contract are modifiable. 合同条款是可以修改的。|近义词: alterable, changeable, adaptable, adjustable|反义词: unmodifiable, fixed, permanent, immutable|词性: adjective
