co-organizers:/koʊˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzərz/| n.|共同组织者，联合举办者|People or groups who organize something together.  |Example: The co-organizers of the conference are planning the event schedule. 会议的共同组织者正在安排活动日程。

Example:  The co-organizers worked tirelessly to make the event a success.  共同组织者不知疲倦地工作，使活动取得了成功。|近义词：joint organizers, co-sponsors, collaborators|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) sole organizer|词性: Noun
