impel:/ɪmˈpel/| v. |驱使，迫使|to drive, force, or urge (someone) to do something|例句：A sense of duty impelled her to report the crime. 责任感迫使她去举报了这起犯罪。|近义词：compel, force, urge, drive, prompt|反义词：restrain, deter, discourage|词性:动词

impel:/ɪmˈpel/| v. |推动，促成|to urge or drive forward or onward|例句：The engine impels the car forward. 发动机推动汽车前进。|近义词：propel, push, drive|反义词：hinder, impede|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin impellere, from in- "into, towards" + pellere "to push, drive".
