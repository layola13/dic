fantasises:/ˈfæntəsaɪzɪz/
verb
幻想，想象，沉湎于幻想
indulges in fantasies or daydreams about something, especially something that is unlikely to happen.
She often fantasises about winning the lottery. 她经常幻想自己中了彩票。
近义词:daydreams, imagines, visualizes
反义词:none
词性:v
