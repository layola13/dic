blarneying:/ˈblɑːrniɪŋ/
verb
哄骗；用花言巧语骗取
persuading someone to do something, or give you something, by using flattery
He blarneyed his way into the exclusive club. 他用花言巧语骗取了进入高级俱乐部的资格。
近义词: cajoling, coaxing, wheedling
反义词:  coercing, intimidating
词性: 动词
