cult:/kʌlt/ | n. | 邪教；狂热崇拜；狂热；时尚 | a relatively small group of people having religious beliefs or practices regarded by others as strange or sinister; a system of religious veneration and devotion directed toward a particular figure or object; a misplaced or excessive admiration for a particular person or thing |例句：The singer has a cult following.  这位歌手有一群狂热的追随者。

例句：The film has become a cult classic. 这部电影已成为一部邪典经典。|近义词：sect, following, craze, fad |反义词: mainstream |词性: noun
