berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ | n. | 狂战士；暴怒的人 | A fierce warrior, especially one of the ancient Norse warriors, who fought with frenzied rage.  |  The berserkers charged into battle, heedless of danger. 狂战士们冲进战场，不顾危险。|  The football player went full berserker after a bad call by the referee.  在裁判做出错误判罚后，这名足球运动员彻底暴怒了。 | synonyms:  wild man, madman, fanatic, zealot | antonyms: pacifist, moderate | noun

berserker:/bərˈzɜːrkər/ | adj. | 狂暴的；狂怒的 | Resembling or characteristic of a berserker; frenzied. | His berserker rage terrified everyone around him. 他狂暴的愤怒吓坏了周围的每个人。 |  She launched into a berserker attack on the buffet table. 她对自助餐桌发动了猛烈的攻击。 | synonyms:  frenzied, furious, raging, wild | antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil | adjective
