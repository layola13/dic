evincing:/ɪˈvɪnsɪŋ/
verb present participle
表明; 显示; 表现出
revealing clearly; making evident
Her work evinces a strong sense of purpose. 她的作品展现出强烈的目标感。
The data evinced a clear upward trend. 数据显示出一个明显的上升趋势。
Synonyms: demonstrating, exhibiting, manifesting, revealing, showing
Antonyms: concealing, hiding, masking
verb
