analysts: /ˈænəlɪsts/ (US), /ˈænəlɪsts/ (UK)| n. |分析师，分析家|A person who conducts analysis, typically of financial or business data, or of a social or scientific subject.|The analysts predicted a strong economic recovery. 分析师预测经济将强劲复苏。|Synonyms: examiner, interpreter, researcher, evaluator|Antonyms: synthesizer|Noun
