colored: /ˈkʌlərd/ (US) /ˈkʌləd/ (UK)| adj.|有颜色的，彩色的；（美）指有色人种的 (现已不用，被认为带有歧视意味)|Having a color or colors; (dated, offensive) used to describe a person who is not white.|The artist used vibrant colored pencils for her drawing. 艺术家用鲜艳的彩色铅笔画画。|近义词：colourful, pigmented, tinted, dyed|反义词：colorless, uncolored, achromatic|词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "color" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Color" comes from Old French "color" (later "couleur"), and ultimately from Latin "color".
