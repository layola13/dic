ghastliness:/ˈɡæstlɪnəs/| n.|可怕；恐怖；惨状|The ghastliness of the accident scene was deeply disturbing.  事故现场的恐怖景象令人深感不安。|例句：The ghastliness of the war lingered in the memories of the survivors. 战争的恐怖在幸存者的记忆中挥之不去。

The ghastliness of the crime shocked the entire community. 这起罪行的可怕震惊了整个社区。

|近义词：horror, awfulness, dreadfulness, frightfulness|反义词：pleasantness, beauty, attractiveness|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ghastly + -ness.  Ghastly from Middle English gastlich, from Old English gæstlic (“spiritual, ghostly”), from gæst (“ghost, spirit”) + -lic (“-ly”).
