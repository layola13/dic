badian:/ˈbeɪdiən/ | n. | 八角，大茴香 | Star anise, a spice derived from the fruit of the Illicium verum plant, native to northeast Vietnam and southwest China. It is commonly used in Chinese, Vietnamese, Indian, and Malaysian cuisine. | Example:  Badian is a key ingredient in five-spice powder. 八角是五香粉的关键成分。

Example: The recipe calls for a whole badian.  这食谱需要一整颗八角。| 近义词: star anise, aniseed (flavor similarity) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *badiane*, from Arabic *bādiyān* (باديان), plural of *bādiyānah* (باديانة), ultimately from Persian *bādiyān* (باديان), also meaning "anise".
