academician: /əˌkædəˈmɪʃən/ | n. | 院士；学会会员 | A member of an academy, especially a learned society. | He is a respected academician in the field of physics. 他是物理学界受人尊敬的院士。 | Example:  She was elected an academician of the Royal Academy of Arts. 她被选为皇家艺术学院院士。

Example: The academician gave a lecture on the latest advances in his field.  这位院士就他所在领域的最新进展作了讲座。

|Synonyms: scholar, intellectual, expert, savant | Antonyms: amateur, novice, layman| noun
