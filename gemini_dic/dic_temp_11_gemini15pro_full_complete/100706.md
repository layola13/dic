intraday:/ˌɪntrəˈdeɪ/ | adj. | 日内的，交易日内的 | Occurring or done within the same day.  Relating to the changes in a financial market during a single day. | The intraday volatility of the stock was significant. 这支股票的日内波动很大。| Intraday trading can be risky. 日内交易可能会有风险。 |  short-term, same-day | long-term, overnight | adjective
