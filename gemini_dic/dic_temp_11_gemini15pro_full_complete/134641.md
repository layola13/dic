nonmaliciousnesses:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɪʃ.əs.nəs.ɪz/ | n. | 无恶意；善良 | The nonmaliciousnesses of his intentions were clear. 他的意图的无恶意是显而易见的。|  Absence of malice or harmful intent; kindness. |  He acted with complete nonmaliciousness, seeking only to help. 他完全出于善意，只是想帮忙。 | 近义词: benevolence, goodwill, kindness, harmlessness | 反义词: malice, malevolence, spite, hostility | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "malicious" (adjective meaning "characterized by malice or ill will") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives). Ultimately from Latin "malitia" (badness, wickedness).
