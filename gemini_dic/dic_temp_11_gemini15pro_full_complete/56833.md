dorn:/dɔːrn/| n.| 刺;荊棘|A sharp-pointed projection on a plant or animal, such as a thorn, spine, or prickle.  A short, blunt weapon.|The rose bush was covered in dorns. 玫瑰叢佈滿了刺。|Synonyms: thorn, spine, prickle, barb|Antonyms: smoothness, softness|noun


dorn:/dɔːrn/| v.|  (古语) 激怒;刺激|To irritate or provoke.|His constant criticism dorned her. 他不断的批评激怒了她。|Synonyms: irritate, provoke, annoy, vex|Antonyms: soothe, calm, placate|verb
