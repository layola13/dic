catchingnesses:/ˈkætʃɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
感染力；吸引力；易感染性；朗朗上口
The quality of being easily remembered or catchy, often referring to tunes or slogans.
The song's catchingness made it an instant hit. 这首歌朗朗上口，很快就流行起来。
近义词: memorability, appeal, infectiousness
反义词: forgettability, dullness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From catching + -ness. "Catching" comes from the Middle English "cacchen," from the Old North French "cachier" meaning "to chase, hunt, seize."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
