deuteropathy:/ˌdjuːtərˈɒpəθi/ | n. |继发性疾病|A disease or condition that is a consequence of another disease or condition. | Example: The deuteropathy, a persistent cough, was a result of his initial lung infection. 继发性疾病，持续性咳嗽，是他最初肺部感染的结果。

Example:  Her arthritis was a deuteropathy stemming from a childhood injury. 她的关节炎是源于童年时期受伤的继发性疾病。| 近义词: secondary disease, sequela, complication | 反义词: primary disease, underlying condition | 词性: noun
