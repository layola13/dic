harmonised:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzd/ (US) /ˈhɑːmənaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|协调的，统一的|Brought into agreement or made compatible.|例句：The regulations were harmonised across the European Union. 这些法规在整个欧盟范围内得到了协调。|例句：The different versions of the software have been harmonised. 不同版本的软件已经统一。|近义词：standardized, unified, coordinated, aligned|反义词：divergent, disparate, conflicting|词性:形容词

harmonise:/ˈhɑːmənaɪz/ (US) /ˈhɑːmənaɪz/ (UK)| v.|协调，统一|To bring into agreement or make compatible.|例句：The government is trying to harmonise regulations across different sectors. 政府正试图协调不同部门的法规。|例句：We need to harmonise our efforts to achieve the best results. 我们需要协调我们的努力以达到最佳效果。|近义词：standardize, unify, coordinate, align|反义词：separate, divide, conflict|词性:动词

词源：From French *harmoniser*, from *harmonie* (“harmony”), from Latin *harmonia*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονία* (*harmonía*, “joint, agreement, concord of sounds”).
