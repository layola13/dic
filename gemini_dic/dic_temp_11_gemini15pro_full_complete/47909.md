decentralist: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪst/
n.|去中心化主义者； 分散主义者|A person who advocates decentralization, especially of political or administrative power.|The decentralist argued for local control over education.|近义词: autonomist, federalist|反义词: centralist|词性: 名词

decentralist: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪst/
adj.|去中心化的；分散的|Relating to or supporting decentralization.|The decentralist movement gained momentum in the 1990s.|近义词: distributed, autonomous|反义词: centralized|词性: 形容词
