conclude:/kənˈkluːd/
verb
结束；推断出；缔结
to bring something to an end; to arrive at a judgment or opinion by reasoning; to formally settle or arrange (an agreement)
The meeting concluded at 5 p.m. 会议下午5点结束。
After careful consideration, we concluded that the project was not feasible. 经过仔细考虑，我们得出结论，该项目不可行。
The two countries concluded a peace treaty. 两国缔结了和平条约。
近义词:finish, end, deduce, infer, settle
反义词:begin, start, commence

词性:v.
