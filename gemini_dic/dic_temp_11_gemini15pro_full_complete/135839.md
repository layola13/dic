nonproportioned:/ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənd/ | adj. |不成比例的，不相称的|Not having the correct or pleasing relationship between size, shape, and position of different parts; disproportionate.| The figure in the painting was nonproportioned, with a head too large for its body. 这幅画中的人物比例失调，头部相对于身体来说太大了。| The building's nonproportioned design made it look awkward.  这栋建筑不成比例的设计使它看起来很别扭。| synonyms: disproportionate, unbalanced, asymmetrical, uneven, irregular, mismatched, ill-proportioned | antonyms: proportionate, balanced, symmetrical, even, regular, matched, well-proportioned | adjective
