disallowing:/dɪsəˈlaʊɪŋ/
| v. |禁止，不准，不允许|The rules are disallowing smoking in public places. 规则禁止在公共场所吸烟。|The school is disallowing students from bringing cell phones to class. 学校不允许学生带手机上课。| prohibiting, forbidding, banning|permitting, allowing|verb
Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + allowing (present participle of allow). Ultimately from Latin ad- (to) + laudare (to praise).
