coromandel:/ˌkɒrəˈmændl/ /ˌkɔːrəˈmændl/ | n. |科罗曼德；科罗曼德海岸（印度东南部）|A region on the southeastern coast of India, known for its scenic beauty and historical significance. |例句：The Coromandel Coast is known for its beautiful beaches. 科罗曼德海岸以其美丽的海滩而闻名。

|例句：They traveled along the Coromandel from Chennai to Pondicherry. 他们沿着科罗曼德海岸从钦奈旅行到本地治里。|近义词：coast, shoreline |反义词:inland|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Tamil word *Kara-mandala*, meaning "black region" or "realm of the Cholas" (an ancient South Indian dynasty).
