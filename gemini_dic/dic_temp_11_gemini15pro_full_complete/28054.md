capelle:/kəˈpɛl/| n. |小教堂，礼拜堂|A small chapel, especially one attached to a larger church or institution, or a private chapel.|The duke had a private capelle in his castle. 公爵在他的城堡里有一个私人小教堂。|Synonyms: chapel, oratory|Antonyms: cathedral, basilica|noun

capella:/kəˈpɛlə/| adv. |(音乐)无伴奏合唱|In the manner of singing without instrumental accompaniment.|The choir sang the hymn a capella. 合唱团无伴奏地演唱了这首赞美诗。|Synonyms: unaccompanied|Antonyms: accompanied, orchestrated|adverb
