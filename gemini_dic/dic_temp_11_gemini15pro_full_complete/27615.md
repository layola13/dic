cancellous:/ˈkænsələs/| adj.|海绵状的，多孔的，网状骨质的|Having a reticular or lattice-like structure, especially with regard to bone tissue; spongy.|The cancellous bone inside the skull is lighter than the compact bone on the outer surface.|近义词：spongy, porous, trabecular|反义词：compact, dense|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *cancellōsus, from Latin cancellus (“lattice, grating”).
