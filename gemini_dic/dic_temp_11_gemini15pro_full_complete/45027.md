ctenophore:/ˈtiːnəfɔːr/ | n. |栉水母|A marine invertebrate animal of the phylum Ctenophora, having a gelatinous, transparent body with eight rows of cilia and often bioluminescent. | Example: The ctenophore pulsed with a soft, blue light. 栉水母发出柔和的蓝光。

Example:  Researchers are studying the unique bioluminescence of ctenophores. 研究人员正在研究栉水母独特的生物发光现象。|Synonym: comb jelly | Antonym: N/A | noun
