bolshie:/ˈbɒlʃi/ (UK) /ˈboʊlʃi/ (US)| adj.|不服从的，反抗的，难对付的|Rebellious, argumentative, and uncooperative.  Often used to describe someone who is deliberately difficult.  Originates from the Bolsheviks, the revolutionary Marxist faction that seized power in Russia in 1917.|例句：The bolshie teenager refused to do his chores.  那个不服气的青少年拒绝做家务。

例句：He's been quite bolshie lately, always questioning authority. 他最近很不听话，总是质疑权威。|近义词：rebellious, defiant, awkward, difficult, uncooperative|反义词:obedient, compliant, cooperative, amenable|词性:形容词
