medic:/ˈmɛdɪk/ | n. | 医务人员，医生，救护人员 | A person who is trained to give medical treatment, especially in an emergency. | Example 1: The medic quickly bandaged the soldier's wound.  救护人员迅速包扎了士兵的伤口。

Example 2:  Call a medic! Someone's been hurt.  叫个医生！有人受伤了。| Synonym: doctor, physician, paramedic, surgeon | Antonym: patient | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin medicus, meaning "physician, surgeon," ultimately from medeor "I heal."
