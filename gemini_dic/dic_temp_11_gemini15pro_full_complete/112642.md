lowball:/ˈloʊbɔːl/
verb|低估；出价过低；|To offer a ridiculously low price or make an unreasonably low estimate.|They lowballed their offer on the house, hoping the sellers were desperate. 他们故意压低了对房子的报价，希望卖家走投无路。|近义词：underbid, undervalue, shortchange|反义词：overbid, overvalue|v.

lowballed:/ˌloʊˈbɔːld/
adjective|被低估的；被出价过低的|Having received a ridiculously low offer or estimate.|I felt lowballed by their initial salary offer. 我觉得他们一开始提供的薪水低得离谱。|近义词：underbid, undervalued, shortchanged|反义词：overbid, overvalued|adj.

lowballed:/ˌloʊˈbɔːld/
verb (past tense)|低估了；出价过低了|Past tense of "lowball".|They lowballed me on the trade-in value of my car. 他们在我的汽车折价上故意压低了价格。|近义词：underbid, undervalued, shortchanged|反义词：overbid, overvalued|v.
