intervisit:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪt/
n.
相互访问; 互相探望; 互访
A visit between or among individuals, groups, or institutions.  A reciprocal or mutual visit.
The two families scheduled an intervisit to strengthen their bond.  两个家庭安排了一次互访，以加强他们的联系。
The intervisit between the two schools was a great success. 两所学校之间的互访非常成功。
近义词: exchange visit, reciprocal visit, mutual visit
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) isolation, separation
