calcivorous: /kælˈsɪvərəs/
adjective
食钙的；以钙质为食的
Feeding on calcium or lime, especially of chalk or limestone.
Snails are calcivorous, obtaining calcium carbonate for their shells.  蜗牛是食钙的，它们从碳酸钙中获取构建壳所需的钙。
The larvae of some insects are calcivorous and bore into rocks.  一些昆虫的幼虫以钙为食，并在岩石中钻洞。
近义词: lime-eating
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly herbivorous, carnivorous, omnivorous depending on the context)
词性: adjective
