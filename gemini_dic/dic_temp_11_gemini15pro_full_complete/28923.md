carrier:/ˈkæriər/ (US), /ˈkæriə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 运输工具；运营商；带菌者；运输舰；搬运工；托架 |A person or thing that carries something. A company that provides telecommunications services.| The airline is a major carrier of passengers and cargo. 这家航空公司是主要的客货运输公司。| synonyms: transporter, conveyor, bearer, vehicle, operator | antonyms: receiver, recipient | noun

carrier:/ˈkæriər/ (US), /ˈkæriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 携带的；运输的 |Used to describe something that carries something else.|  He works for a carrier company. 他在一家运输公司工作。| synonyms: transporting, conveying | antonyms: receiving | adjective
