maladministration:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/| n. |管理不善，治理不当|Bad or dishonest management of something, typically by a government or organization.|The report criticized the maladministration of the hospital.|近义词：mismanagement, misconduct, corruption|反义词：good governance, proper management|词性:名词

Etymology:
From mal- (bad, wrong) + administration.  "Mal-" comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin "malus" (bad, evil).  "Administration" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French, from Latin "administratio," from "administrare" (to manage, direct).
