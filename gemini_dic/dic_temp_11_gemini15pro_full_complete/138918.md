nutritional:/njuˈtrɪʃənəl/| adj.|营养的；滋养的|Relating to the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.|例句：A nutritional diet is important for maintaining good health.  营养丰富的饮食对保持健康很重要。

例句：The nutritional value of this food is very high.  这种食物的营养价值很高。|近义词：nourishing, nutritive, dietary|反义词：unhealthy, detrimental|词性:形容词

词源: Late 18th century: from nutrition + -al.
