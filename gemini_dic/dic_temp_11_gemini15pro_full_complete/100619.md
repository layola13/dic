intimidatingly:/ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪŋli/| adv.|令人生畏地，吓人地|In a manner that causes fear or intimidation.  Derived from the verb "intimidate," meaning to frighten or overawe.  The suffix "-ly" transforms it into an adverb.|Her boss spoke intimidatingly, making her feel small and insignificant. 她老板说话的语气令人生畏，让她觉得自己渺小而无足轻重。| The dark, empty street felt intimidatingly quiet. 黑暗空旷的街道安静得吓人。|近义词: threateningly, menacingly, formidably|反义词: reassuringly, comfortingly, encouragingly|词性: adverb
