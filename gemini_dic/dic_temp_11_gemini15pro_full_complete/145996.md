overdomesticated:/ˌoʊvərdoʊˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度驯化的
Having been domesticated to an excessive degree, often resulting in undesirable traits or loss of original characteristics.
The overdomesticated dog breeds are prone to certain health issues.  这些过度驯化的犬种容易出现某些健康问题。
These overdomesticated tomatoes lack the flavor of heirloom varieties. 这些过度驯化的西红柿缺乏传统品种的风味。
Synonyms: overbred, excessively domesticated
Antonyms: wild, feral
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + domesticated.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Domesticated" comes from the Latin word "domesticus", meaning "belonging to the house".
