chokeable:/ˈtʃoʊkəbəl/| adj.|窒息的，易引起窒息的|Likely to cause choking; easily choked.  Something that is small enough to be lodged in the throat and block the airway.|Small toys are chokeable and dangerous for young children. 小玩具容易造成窒息，对幼儿很危险。|近义词：hazardous, dangerous, unsafe|反义词:safe, harmless|词性:形容词
