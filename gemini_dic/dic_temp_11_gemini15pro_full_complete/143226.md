outcuts: /ˈaʊtkʌts/
n. | 外景, 剪辑, 节选 | Pieces of film or video that have been removed during the editing process, often containing usable footage. | The director reviewed the outcuts to see if any of the deleted scenes could be reintegrated into the film. 导演检查了剪辑，看看是否有任何删除的场景可以重新整合到电影中。 | 近义词: clippings, offcuts, trims | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "final cut") | 词性: 名词
