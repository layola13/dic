evangelist:/ɪˈvændʒəlɪst/
n.
福音传道者；热心倡导者
a person who seeks to convert others to the Christian faith, especially by preaching. Also, a person who enthusiastically promotes or advocates a particular cause, policy, etc.
Billy Graham was a famous evangelist.  葛培理是一位著名的福音传道者。

The CEO is an evangelist for technological innovation.  这位CEO是技术创新的热心倡导者。

preacher, missionary, advocate, proponent, champion
opponent, antagonist, critic
noun
