coked:/koʊkt/| adj.|兴奋的，亢奋的；吸食可卡因后的| Describing someone who is highly energetic and possibly under the influence of cocaine.  It often implies a state of exhilaration or mania, sometimes stemming from drug use. |例句：He was completely coked up and couldn't sit still. 他完全兴奋过头，坐不住。|例句：The band played a coked-up performance, full of energy and barely contained chaos. 乐队表演时兴奋异常，充满活力，几乎失控。|近义词：high, wired, energized, hyped up|反义词：calm, relaxed, sedated
