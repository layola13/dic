pseudo-modern:/ˈsuːdoʊˈmɑːdɜːrn/|/ˌsjuːdəʊˈmɒdən/| adj.|伪现代的，仿现代的|Apparently modern but lacking the genuine qualities of modernism; often characterized by superficial imitation of modern styles.  It suggests a style that attempts to emulate modern design but falls short in terms of originality, innovation, or depth.|The building's architecture was described as pseudo-modern, a clumsy imitation of Bauhaus principles.|近义词：faux-modern, mock-modern, imitation-modern|反义词：genuinely modern, authentically modern
