penitency:/ˈpɛnɪtənsi/| n.|悔悟；忏悔|The feeling of regret for having done something wrong; remorse.  It often involves a desire to make amends for one's actions.|例句：He showed deep penitency for his crimes. 他对自己的罪行表示深深的悔恨。|例句：Her penitency was evident in her tears and apologies.  她的悔恨从她的眼泪和道歉中显而易见。|近义词：repentance, remorse, contrition|反义词：callousness, indifference, unconcern|名词
