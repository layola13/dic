haying:/ˈheɪɪŋ/| n.|割草；晒干牧草|The process of cutting and drying grass for hay.  Haying is a crucial part of farming in many regions.|例句：The farmer started haying early this year to avoid the summer rains.  今年农民早早地就开始割草晒干以避免夏季雨水。

例句：The haying season is usually in July and August in this area. 这个地区的割草季节通常在七、八月。


近义词：haymaking, harvesting hay|反义词：None directly applicable|词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer hays his fields every summer. = 这位农民每年夏天都在他的田地里割草。
They hay the pasture twice a year. = 他们一年两次割牧场的草。


一般过去时:
We hayed the entire field in one day. = 我们一天就把整个田地割完了草。
They hayed the meadows last week. = 他们上周割了草地上的草。


现在完成时:
They have already hayed most of the fields. = 他们已经割完了大部分田地的草。
The farmers have hayed all their crops this season. = 农民们在本季已经收割了所有的庄稼。


过去完成时:
They had hayed the north field before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经割完了北边的田地。
The farmers had hayed before the heavy rains started. = 大雨开始下之前，农民们就已经割完了草。


将来时:
We will hay the fields next week. = 我们下周将要去割草。
They will begin haying as soon as the weather improves. = 天气一旦好转，他们就开始割草。


虚拟语气:
If we had hayed earlier, we wouldn't have lost so much hay to the rain. = 如果我们早点割草，就不会损失那么多干草了。
I wish we had hayed the field before the storm. = 我真希望我们在暴风雨来临之前就割了草。


被动语态:
The field was hayed last week. = 这块地在上周被割了草。
The hay will be hayed before the weekend. = 草会在周末之前被割掉。


疑问句:

否定句:
