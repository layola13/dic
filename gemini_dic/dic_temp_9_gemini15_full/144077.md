outrunning: /ˌaʊtˈrʌnɪŋ/ | v. | 跑赢，超过，胜过 |  To run faster than (someone or something); to exceed (something) in speed or extent. |

例句：

The hare was outrunning the tortoise. = 兔子跑得比乌龟快。

He was outrunning his competitors in the race. = 他在比赛中超过了他的竞争对手。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outpacing, outstripping

反义词： lagging behind, falling short of


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outruns his brother in every race. = 他每次比赛都跑赢他哥哥。
The car outruns all its competitors. = 这辆车跑赢了所有竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outran her opponent in the marathon. = 她马拉松比赛中超过了对手。
The police outran the thief. = 警察追上了小偷。

现在完成时:
The company has outrun its initial projections. = 公司已经超过了最初的预期。
He has outrun all his rivals in the business. = 他在生意场上超过了所有竞争对手。

过去完成时:
They had outrun their supplies by the time they reached the summit. = 他们到达山顶时，物资已经消耗殆尽。
She had outrun all her expectations and achieved great success. = 她已经超过了她所有的预期并取得了巨大的成功。

将来时:
I think I will outrun him in the next game. = 我想我下一场比赛会超过他。
We believe our company will outrun the market in the next quarter. = 我们相信我们公司下个季度会跑赢市场。

虚拟语气:
If I were faster, I could outrun him. = 如果我更快，我就能超过他。
If she had trained harder, she would have outrun her opponent. = 如果她训练更努力，她就能超过她的对手。

被动语态:
The runner was outrun by a younger competitor. = 这位跑步运动员被一位年轻的竞争对手超过了。
Their plan was outrun by unexpected events. = 他们的计划被意外事件打乱了。

疑问句:
Can you outrun a cheetah? = 你能跑赢猎豹吗？
Did he outrun the deadline? = 他赶上截止日期了吗？

否定句:
I couldn't outrun the storm. = 我无法跑赢暴风雨。
They didn't outrun their competition this year. = 今年他们没有跑赢竞争对手。
