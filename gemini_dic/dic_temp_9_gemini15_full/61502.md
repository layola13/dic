embroil:/ɪmˈbrɔɪl/| vt.|使卷入；使陷入；使混乱|To involve someone or something in a difficult or unpleasant situation, typically a conflict or argument.  |例句：The scandal embroiled several leading politicians. 这场丑闻牵涉到几位政界要员。

例句：He became embroiled in a bitter dispute with his neighbours. 他和邻居卷入了一场激烈的争吵。


近义词：involve, entangle, implicate

反义词：exclude, disentangle, extricate

词性：动词
