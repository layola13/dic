eking-out:/ˈiːkɪŋ aʊt/| v.|勉强维持生计；设法补足；|to manage to live on a small amount of money; to supplement or add to something.|例句：They were eking out a meagre existence on a small farm. 他们在小农场上勉强维持着微薄的生活。

例句：She eked out her income by taking on extra work. 她通过多接一些工作来补贴收入。

近义词：making do, supplementing, stretching, scraping by

反义词：flourishing, thriving, prospering

词性:动词
