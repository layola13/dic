forayers:/fɔːˈreɪərz/| n. |远征者；侵略者；抢劫者 |People who make sudden attacks or raids into enemy territory;  those who venture into unfamiliar or hostile territory. The intrepid forayers ventured deep into the uncharted jungle.  勇敢的探险家们深入未开发的丛林。|近义词：raiders, invaders, explorers, adventurers |反义词：defenders, protectors, residents
