nickel-and-dimed:/ˈnɪkəl ænd daɪmd/| adj. | 勒索小钱的；因小钱而被剥削的 |  to exploit someone by charging them small amounts of money repeatedly for goods or services.  This phrase implies a pattern of small, seemingly insignificant charges that accumulate to a significant amount, often leaving the person feeling frustrated and exploited. | Example:  She felt nickel-and-dimed by the constant hidden fees associated with her airline ticket. 她觉得航空公司机票上不断出现的隐性费用让她感到被敲竹杠了。 |近义词：exploited, overcharged, ripped off | 反义词：generous, fair, reasonable |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They nickel-and-dime their customers with extra charges. = 他们通过额外收费来敲诈顾客的小钱。
The company nickel-and-dimes its employees with various deductions. = 公司通过各种扣除来剥削员工的小钱。


一般过去时:
The landlord nickel-and-dimed his tenants with unnecessary repairs. = 房东以不必要的修理为借口敲诈房客的小钱。
The hotel nickel-and-dimed us with resort fees and parking charges. = 酒店以度假费和停车费等名义敲诈我们的小钱。


现在完成时:
They have nickel-and-dimed me out of a significant amount of money. = 他们通过不断收取小额费用已经敲诈了我一大笔钱。
The contractor has nickel-and-dimed us throughout the entire project. = 承包商在整个项目过程中一直都在敲诈我们的小钱。


过去完成时:
The company had nickel-and-dimed the employees before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司就已经一直在敲诈员工的小钱了。
We had been nickel-and-dimed so much that we barely had any money left. = 我们已经被敲诈了这么多小钱，以至于几乎没剩下什么钱了。


将来时:
They will nickel-and-dime you with every opportunity. = 他们会抓住每一个机会敲诈你小钱。
The airline will likely nickel-and-dime passengers with baggage fees. = 航空公司很可能会通过行李费等来敲诈乘客的小钱。


虚拟语气:
If they nickel-and-dimed us any more, we would have complained. = 如果他们再敲诈我们小钱，我们会投诉的。
I wish they hadn't nickel-and-dimed me so much on that trip. = 我真希望在那次旅行中他们没有敲诈我这么多小钱。


被动语态:
Customers are often nickel-and-dimed by companies with hidden fees. = 顾客经常会被公司隐藏的费用敲诈小钱。
The tenants felt they were being nickel-and-dimed by their landlord. = 租客们觉得他们被房东敲诈了小钱。


疑问句:
Are they nickel-and-diming us with these extra charges? = 他们是不是在通过这些额外费用敲诈我们小钱？
Was the company nickel-and-diming its customers with the service charges? = 公司是不是在通过服务费敲诈顾客小钱？


否定句:
They didn't nickel-and-dime us on that project. = 他们在那个项目上没有敲诈我们的小钱。
The restaurant didn't nickel-and-dime us with extra charges for bread and water. = 那家餐厅没有因为面包和水收取额外的费用敲诈我们。



PHRASE->
to be nickel-and-dimed to death = 被小钱一点点地榨干
nickel-and-dimed by = 被...敲诈小钱
nickel and dime someone = 敲诈某人小钱
feeling nickel-and-dimed = 感觉被敲诈小钱
getting nickel-and-dimed = 正在被敲诈小钱

词源:
The phrase comes from the relatively low value of nickels (5 cents) and dimes (10 cents), emphasizing the small amounts involved in the exploitative practice.  The combination creates a sense of petty and cumulative financial exploitation.
