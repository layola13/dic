overreaching: /ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃɪŋ/ | adj., n. | 权限过大的；过分自信的；目标过高的；越权；力所不及 | Attempting to do more than one is capable of; exceeding one's authority or limits.  |例句：The company's overreaching ambitions led to its downfall. 公司的野心过大导致了它的垮台。  His overreaching attempt to control every aspect of the project resulted in chaos. 他试图控制项目的方方面面，结果导致了混乱。|近义词：excessive, immoderate, presumptuous, ambitious |反义词：moderate, restrained, humble, modest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His ambition is overreaching. = 他的野心过大。
The company's strategy is overreaching. = 公司的战略目标过高。

一般过去时:
Their attempts were overreaching. = 他们的尝试是力所不及的。
His demands were overreaching. = 他的要求太过分了。

现在完成时:
The government has been overreaching in its regulations. = 政府的规章制度已经越权了。
He has overreached himself in his attempts to please everyone. = 他为了取悦所有人而做得太过火了。

过去完成时:
They had overreached themselves financially before the crisis. = 危机爆发前，他们已经入不敷出了。
The company had overreached in its expansion plans. = 公司在扩张计划中目标过高。

将来时:
They will overreach themselves if they try to do too much. = 如果他们试图做太多事情，他们将会力不从心。
The project will likely overreach its budget. = 这个项目很可能会超出预算。

虚拟语气:
If they hadn't overreached, they might have succeeded. = 如果他们没有目标过高，他们也许会成功。
I wish he hadn't overreached in his criticism. = 我希望他没有在批评中太过分。

被动语态:

His authority was overreached by the new management. = 他的权力被新管理层越过了。

疑问句:
Is their plan overreaching? = 他们的计划是否目标过高？
Was the company's expansion overreaching? = 公司的扩张是否过分？

否定句:
Their actions were not overreaching. = 他们的行为没有越权。
The proposal was not overreaching. = 这个提案没有目标过高。

词源:
The word "overreaching" is formed from the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "reach," which in this context signifies "to extend" or "to attempt."  Therefore, "overreaching" literally means "reaching too far" or "exceeding limits."
