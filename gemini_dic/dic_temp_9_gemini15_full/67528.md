extradite:/ˈekstrədaɪt/| vt.|引渡，遣送|To surrender (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another state or country.|The government agreed to extradite the suspect to the United States to face trial.|近义词：deport, expel, handover|反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government extradites criminals to other countries. = 政府将罪犯引渡到其他国家。
They extradite suspects based on international treaties. = 他们根据国际条约引渡嫌疑人。

一般过去时:
The court ordered that he be extradited to France. = 法院下令将他引渡到法国。
The country extradited the fugitive to his home country. = 这个国家将逃犯引渡回他的祖国。

现在完成时:
Several criminals have been extradited this year. = 今年已经有几名罪犯被引渡。
The authorities have extradited him to stand trial. = 当局已将他引渡受审。

过去完成时:
The suspect had been extradited before the trial. = 在审判之前，嫌疑人已被引渡。
The evidence had been presented before he was extradited. = 在他被引渡之前，证据已经提交。

将来时:
The court will extradite the accused to Canada. = 法院将把被告引渡到加拿大。
They will extradite him as soon as the paperwork is complete. = 文件一旦办妥，他们就会将他引渡。

虚拟语气:
If they had evidence, they would extradite him immediately. = 如果他们有证据，他们会立即将他引渡。
It would be better if we could extradite him without delay. = 如果我们能立即将他引渡就好了。

被动语态:
The criminal was extradited to face justice. = 罪犯被引渡以接受审判。
He was extradited under a bilateral agreement. = 他根据双边协议被引渡。

疑问句:
Will the suspect be extradited? = 嫌疑人会被引渡吗？
When will he be extradited? = 他什么时候会被引渡？

否定句:
The court refused to extradite him. = 法院拒绝引渡他。
They did not extradite the suspect due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，他们没有引渡嫌疑人。


PHRASE->
extradite someone to a country = 将某人引渡到某个国家
be extradited to = 被引渡到
extradition treaty = 引渡条约
request extradition = 要求引渡
face extradition = 面临引渡
