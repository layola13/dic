helpless:/ˈhɛlpləs/| adj.|无助的，绝望的|Unable to do anything to help oneself or anyone else; powerless.|例句：The child felt helpless against the bully.  孩子面对欺凌者感到无助。|例句：She felt helpless in the face of such overwhelming grief. 面对如此巨大的悲伤，她感到无助。|近义词：powerless, defenseless, vulnerable|反义词：powerful, capable, resourceful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels helpless in this situation. = 他在这种情况下感到无助。
She is helpless to stop the argument. = 她无力阻止这场争吵。


一般过去时:
I felt helpless after the accident. = 事故发生后我感到无助。
They were helpless to prevent the disaster. = 他们无力阻止这场灾难。


现在完成时:
I have felt helpless many times in my life. = 我一生中多次感到无助。
She has been helpless to change her situation. = 她一直无力改变自己的处境。


过去完成时:
He had felt helpless before he found a solution. = 在找到解决办法之前，他感到过无助。
They had been helpless until the rescuers arrived. = 直到救援人员到达之前，他们一直处于无助状态。


将来时:
I will feel helpless if I lose my job. = 如果我失业了，我会感到无助。
She will be helpless without her medication. = 没有药物，她将会无助。


虚拟语气:
If I were helpless, I would need help. = 如果我无助，我就需要帮助。
I wish I weren't so helpless in this matter. = 我希望在这件事上不要如此无助。


被动语态:
The victims were left feeling helpless. = 受害者被留下来感到无助。
He was rendered helpless by the injury. = 他因受伤而变得无助。


疑问句:
Are you feeling helpless? = 你感到无助吗？
Does she feel helpless in her current role? = 她在她目前的职位上感到无助吗？


否定句:
I am not helpless; I can find a way. = 我并不无助；我能找到办法。
She is not helpless; she has resources to call upon. = 她并不无助；她有可以利用的资源。


PHRASE->
feel helpless = 感到无助

seem helpless = 显得无助
leave someone helpless = 使某人无助
helpless against = 对……无能为力
