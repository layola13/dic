federated:/ˈfɛdəreɪtɪd/ | adj. |联邦的；联合的|Combined into a federation or league; relating to or characteristic of a federation.|例句：The federated states of Germany were formed in 1871.  德国联邦各州于1871年组成。|近义词：united, combined, allied |反义词：separate, independent, disunited

This system is federated across multiple servers. = 这个系统跨多个服务器联合。

词源:
From Late Latin *fœderātus, past participle of fœderāre "to league, unite by treaty", from fœdus "treaty, agreement".  Ultimately related to words meaning "bind" or "trust".
