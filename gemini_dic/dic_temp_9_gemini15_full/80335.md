goldfinch: /ˈɡoʊldfɪntʃ/ | n. | 金翅雀 | A small, colorful songbird with a bright yellow and red plumage.  Goldfinches are known for their cheerful songs and acrobatic feeding habits. |  例句：We saw a goldfinch flitting amongst the sunflowers. 我们看到一只金翅雀在向日葵之间飞来飞去。  例句：The male goldfinch's bright plumage is a striking feature. 雄性金翅雀鲜艳的羽毛是一个显著的特点。| 近义词：canary, siskin | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with larger birds or birds of duller plumage.
