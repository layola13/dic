mouldering: /ˈmoʊldərɪŋ/ | adj. | 腐朽的；腐烂的 | Gradually decaying or rotting, especially due to age or neglect.  | The old house was in a state of mouldering decay. 这栋老房子正处于腐朽状态。  The mouldering timbers of the ship were barely visible beneath the sand. 船的腐朽的木头在沙子底下几乎看不见了。 | decaying, rotting, decomposing | sound, fresh, intact


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wood is mouldering. = 木头正在腐朽。
The leaves are mouldering on the ground. = 叶子在地面上腐烂。

一般过去时:
The fruit mouldered quickly in the heat. = 水果在高温下很快腐烂了。
The old building mouldered away over the years. = 这座旧建筑多年来逐渐腐朽了。

现在完成时:
The remains have mouldered into dust. = 遗骸已经腐朽成灰尘。
The ancient ruins have mouldered for centuries. = 古代遗址已经腐朽了几个世纪。

过去完成时:
The wood had mouldered before we could salvage it. = 在我们能够抢救之前，木材就已经腐朽了。
The documents had mouldered away in the damp cellar. = 文件在潮湿的地窖里已经腐烂了。

将来时:
The statue will moulder away if left unprotected. = 如果不加以保护，雕像将会腐朽。
The old books will moulder in the attic. = 旧书将会在阁楼上腐烂。

虚拟语气:
If the food had been refrigerated, it wouldn't have mouldered. = 如果食物被冷藏了，它就不会腐烂了。
I wish that these old photos hadn't mouldered. = 我真希望这些旧照片没有腐烂。

被动语态:
The wood was mouldered by the rain. = 木头被雨水腐蚀了。
The building is being mouldered by time. = 这栋建筑正在被时间侵蚀。

疑问句:
Is the wood mouldering? = 木头在腐烂吗？
Are the apples mouldering? = 苹果在腐烂吗？

否定句:
The wood isn't mouldering yet. = 木头还没腐烂。
The fruit didn't moulder because it was kept in the refrigerator. = 水果没有腐烂，因为它是放在冰箱里保存的。


PHRASE->
mouldering remains: 腐朽的遗骸
mouldering timbers: 腐朽的木材
mouldering ruins: 腐朽的废墟
mouldering bones: 腐朽的骨头
a mouldering corpse: 一具腐烂的尸体


词源:

From Middle English *mouldren, from Old English *muldrigan, from muld "dust, earth, soil."  The sense development is from "turning to dust" to "decaying."
