jamboree:/ˈdʒæmbəri/| n.|大型集会；狂欢；（尤指）童子军大露营|A jamboree is a large celebration or gathering, especially a large gathering of Boy Scouts or Girl Scouts.  It is often characterized by excitement, fun, and a sense of community.|The Boy Scouts held a jamboree in the national park.  童子军在国家公园举行了一次大型集会。 The festival was a real jamboree, with music, dancing, and food stalls.  这个节日真是一场盛大的狂欢，有音乐、舞蹈和食品摊位。|festival, celebration, rally|disorder, chaos, quietness|名词
