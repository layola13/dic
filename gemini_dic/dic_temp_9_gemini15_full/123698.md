mooncalf:/ˈmuːnˌkælf/| n. |月怪，傻瓜，笨蛋 |A mooncalf is a mythical creature, often depicted as a shapeless, pale, and foolish being, sometimes associated with the moon's influence.  It's often used figuratively to describe a foolish or stupid person. |Example: He acted like a complete mooncalf, tripping over his own feet. 他像个十足的傻瓜，自己绊倒自己。|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton |反义词：genius, intellect, sage |名词
