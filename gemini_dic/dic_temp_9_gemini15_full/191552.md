scholarly:/ˈskɑːrlɪ/| adj.|学术的；学问的；博学的 | relating to or characteristic of scholars or scholarship; learned; erudite.  |例句：His scholarly work on ancient civilizations is highly regarded. 他的古代文明学术著作备受推崇。|例句：The article presents a scholarly analysis of the poem. 这篇文章对这首诗作了学术性的分析。|近义词：academic, learned, erudite, intellectual |反义词：popular, unsophisticated, unscholarly
