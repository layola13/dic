jailbreak: /ˈdʒeɪlbreɪk/ | n. vt. |越狱；(对电子设备的)越狱；破解(软件) |

英文解析：The act of escaping from jail;  the act of removing software restrictions on a device, such as a smartphone or tablet, to allow the installation of unauthorized applications or operating systems.  As a verb, it refers to the process of performing this action.

例句：

The prisoner attempted a daring jailbreak. = 囚犯试图进行一次大胆的越狱。

He jailbroke his iPhone to install custom apps. = 他越狱了他的iPhone以安装自定义应用程序。


近义词：escape (n.), breakout (n.), circumvent (v.), hack (v.)

反义词：imprisonment (n.), incarceration (n.), confinement (n.)

词性：noun, verb
