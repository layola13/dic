/niˈændərˌθɔːlz/ | n. | 尼安德特人 | Neanderthals are an extinct species of archaic humans who lived in Eurasia until about 40,000 years ago.  They are known for their robust build and larger brains than modern humans. |  Recent studies have revealed much about Neanderthal culture and their interactions with early modern humans.  最近的研究揭示了尼安德特人文化及其与早期现代人的互动。|  Archaic humans, early humans |  Homo sapiens |  名词
