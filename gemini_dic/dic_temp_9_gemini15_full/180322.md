refuelled:/ˌriːˈfjuːəld/| v.| 加油；补充燃料 | To supply (a vehicle, aircraft, etc.) with more fuel.  |例句：The plane refuelled before continuing its journey.  飞机加油后继续飞行。|例句：We refuelled the car at a roadside station.我们在路边的加油站给汽车加油。|近义词：replenish, refill|反义词：deplete, exhaust


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane refuels regularly during long flights. = 飞机在长途飞行中定期加油。
He refuels his car every week. = 他每周给他的车加油。

一般过去时:
They refuelled the helicopter before the rescue mission. = 他们救援任务前给直升机加了油。
We refuelled the car at the gas station. = 我们在加油站给车加了油。

现在完成时:
The pilot has refuelled the aircraft. = 飞行员已经给飞机加了油。
I have refuelled my car twice this week. = 我本周已经给我的车加了两次油。

过去完成时:
The truck had refuelled before it set off. = 卡车出发前已经加过油了。
She had refuelled her motorbike before the long ride. = 她在长途骑行前给摩托车加过油了。

将来时:
We will refuel the plane in Dubai. = 我们将在迪拜给飞机加油。
They will refuel the spaceship before launch. = 他们将在发射前给宇宙飞船加油。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had refuelled my car yesterday. = 我真希望昨天给我的车加了油。
