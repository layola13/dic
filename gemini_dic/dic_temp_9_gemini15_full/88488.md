historicist:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | adj. |历史主义的；历史决定论的 | Relating to or characterized by historicism.  Historicism is an approach to the study of the past that emphasizes the historical context and the unique circumstances of each historical period. It rejects universal or timeless truths and focuses on the specific conditions that shaped past events and ideas. | The professor's approach to interpreting literature was decidedly historicist, emphasizing the social and political context of each work.  | 近义词：contextualist, relativist | 反义词：universalist, ahistorical
