countercoups:/ˈkaʊntərkʊz/| n.|反政变；反クーデター|A countercoup is a coup d'état that overthrows a government that itself came to power through a coup d'état.  It is essentially a coup against a coup.|例句：The military countercoup restored the previous president to power.  这次军事反政变使前任总统恢复了权力。 The countercoup was swift and decisive, leaving little room for resistance.  这次反政变迅速而果断，几乎没有留下抵抗的空间。|近义词：revolt, uprising, rebellion|反义词：coup d'état, takeover|名词
