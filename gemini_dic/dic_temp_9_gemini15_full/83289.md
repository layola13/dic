gynaecology:/ˌɡaɪnɪˈkɒlədʒi/| n. |妇科学，妇产科|The branch of medicine dealing with the diseases and conditions specific to women, particularly those affecting the reproductive system.  It encompasses various aspects of women's health throughout their lives, from puberty to menopause.|例句：She consulted a gynaecologist about her irregular periods. 她因为月经不调去看妇科医生了。|近义词：obstetrics (if focusing on childbirth), women's health|反义词：N/A
