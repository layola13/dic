perambulators:/pəˈræmbjʊleɪtərz/| n. |婴儿车，童车|A perambulator is a baby carriage, also known as a stroller or pram.  It's a wheeled vehicle used to transport infants and young children.|Example: My grandmother pushed me around in a perambulator when I was a baby. 我还是婴儿的时候，奶奶用婴儿车推着我到处走。  The park was full of parents pushing perambulators. 公园里到处都是推着婴儿车的父母。|近义词：pram, stroller, baby carriage|反义词：None

词源:
