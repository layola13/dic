eternal:/ɪˈtɜːrnəl/| adj.|永恒的，永久的|lasting or existing forever; without end.|例句：The eternal cycle of life and death is a fundamental concept in many religions.  许多宗教中，生与死的永恒循环是一个基本概念。

例句：Their love seemed eternal, a bond that would never break. 他们的爱似乎是永恒的，一种永远不会破裂的纽带。


近义词：everlasting, perpetual, immortal, endless|反义词：temporary, transient, fleeting, ephemeral|词性:形容词
