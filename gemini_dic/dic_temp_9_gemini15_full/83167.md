gutting:/ˈɡʌtɪŋ/| v.|掏空；去除内脏；破坏；使震惊；使沮丧|The act of removing the internal organs of an animal; the process of completely destroying or ruining something; a feeling of intense disappointment or despair.|例句：The fishermen were gutting the fish on the dock. 渔民们在码头上掏鱼的内脏。The news completely gutted him. 这个消息彻底让他崩溃了。|近义词：eviscerating, destroying, devastating, demoralizing|反义词：constructing, building, uplifting, encouraging|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is gutting the fish. = 他正在掏鱼的内脏。
They gut the chickens before cooking. = 他们在烹饪鸡肉之前先掏空鸡的内脏。

一般过去时:
She gutted the chicken yesterday. = 她昨天掏空了鸡的内脏。
The storm gutted the building. = 暴风雨摧毁了那栋建筑。

现在完成时:
They have gutted the entire apartment. = 他们已经把整个公寓都翻新了（拆空）。
The scandal has gutted his reputation. = 这场丑闻毁了他的名誉。

过去完成时:
He had gutted the fish before he cleaned it. = 他在清洗鱼之前已经掏空了鱼的内脏。
The company had gutted its marketing department before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经裁撤了其市场营销部门。

将来时:
They will gut the house next week. = 他们下周将要拆除这栋房子。
The fire will gut the whole forest. = 大火将烧毁整片森林。

虚拟语气:
If they gutted the building, it would be easier to rebuild. = 如果他们拆除了这栋建筑，重建会更容易。
I wish they hadn't gutted the old house. = 我真希望他们没有拆掉那座老房子。

被动语态:
The fish was gutted by the fisherman. = 这条鱼被渔民掏空了内脏。
The building was gutted by the fire. = 这栋建筑被大火烧毁了。

疑问句:
Has the house been gutted yet? = 房子已经拆空了吗？
Will the old factory be gutted? = 老工厂会被拆除吗？

否定句:
He didn't gut the fish properly. = 他没有 properly 掏空鱼的内脏。
They didn't gut the building completely. = 他们没有彻底拆除这栋建筑。
