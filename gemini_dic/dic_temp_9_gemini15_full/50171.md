deodorization:/ˌdiːoʊdərəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |除臭；去臭|The process of removing or neutralizing unpleasant odors.  It involves various techniques depending on the source and type of odor. |例句：The deodorization of the wastewater is crucial before it's released into the environment.  废水在排放到环境中之前，除臭至关重要。


The air freshener provided effective deodorization in the room. 空气清新剂有效地除去了房间里的异味。|近义词：odor removal, scent elimination, deodorization process |反义词：odorification,  (no direct antonym, but the opposite would be adding or intensifying odors)|词性：名词
