counteroffensive:/ˌkaʊntərəˈfɛnsɪv/| n. |反攻，反击|A military attack intended to repel an enemy offensive.  It is an organized and planned military operation launched to regain lost territory or to push back an enemy advance.|例句：The Allies launched a counteroffensive after the initial German invasion.  盟军在德军最初入侵后发起了反攻。

例句：The Ukrainian counteroffensive has made significant gains in recent weeks.  乌克兰的反攻在最近几周取得了重大进展。


近义词：counterattack, riposte, retaliation|反义词：offensive, attack
