crusade:/kruˈseɪd/ | n., v. | 十字军东征；运动；斗争 | A crusade is a religious war, especially one of the medieval expeditions to recover the Holy Land from the Muslims.  It can also refer to a vigorous campaign for political or social reform. |例句：The Children's Crusade was a tragic event in the Middle Ages.  儿童十字军东征是中世纪的一场悲剧。

The organization launched a crusade against poverty. 该组织发起了反对贫困的运动。|近义词：campaign, movement, struggle |反义词：inaction, apathy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He crusades for social justice. = 他为社会正义而奋斗。
She crusades against animal cruelty. = 她积极反对虐待动物。


一般过去时:
They crusaded for women's suffrage. = 他们为妇女参政权而斗争。
The knights crusaded to reclaim the Holy Land. = 骑士们远征以收复圣地。


现在完成时:
She has crusaded against corruption for many years. = 她多年来一直致力于反腐败斗争。
They have crusaded for environmental protection. = 他们一直在为环保而努力。


过去完成时:
He had crusaded against injustice before he became famous. = 他成名之前就一直在为反对不公正而奋斗。
They had crusaded for civil rights for decades. = 他们几十年来一直在为民权而斗争。


将来时:
She will crusade for better education. = 她将为改善教育而奋斗。
They will crusade against discrimination. = 他们将反对歧视。


虚拟语气:
If he were to crusade against this injustice, many would support him. = 如果他起来反对这种不公正，很多人都会支持他。
I wish I could crusade for a more just world. = 我希望我能为一个更公正的世界而奋斗。


被动语态:
The issue was crusaded for by many activists. = 许多活动家为这个问题而奋斗。
The cause was crusaded against by various groups. = 各种团体都反对这一事业。


疑问句:
Will they crusade for religious freedom? = 他们会为宗教自由而奋斗吗？
What cause will she crusade for next? = 她接下来会为哪个事业而奋斗？


否定句:
He did not crusade against the government's policies. = 他没有反对政府的政策。
They will not crusade for unpopular causes. = 他们不会为不受欢迎的事业而奋斗。


PHRASE->
a crusade against = 反对…的运动
launch a crusade = 发起一场运动
wage a crusade = 发动一场运动
a religious crusade = 一场宗教战争
a moral crusade = 一场道德运动
