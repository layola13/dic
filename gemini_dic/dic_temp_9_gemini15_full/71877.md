fleshy:/ˈfleʃi/| adj.|多肉的，肥胖的，肉质的 |Having much flesh; plump; full of flesh.  The adjective describes something having a lot of flesh, either in terms of being plump or having a fleshy texture.|例句：The peach is fleshy and juicy.  这桃子多肉多汁。 The baby's cheeks are quite fleshy.  婴儿的脸颊相当丰满。|近义词：plump, plumped, fleshy, full-bodied, succulent|反义词：lean, skinny, bony, thin
