例句：They mixed it up in the final game, resulting in an exciting and unpredictable finish. = 他们在决赛中大打出手，导致了令人兴奋且无法预测的结局。


近义词：vary, diversify, shake up, change, alter, jumble

反义词：maintain, keep the same, standardize,  unify
