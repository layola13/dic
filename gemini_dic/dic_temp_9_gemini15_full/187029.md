ritualize:/ˈrɪtʃuəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使…仪式化；使…具有仪式性 | To perform or observe as a ritual; to make something into a ritual.  |例句：They ritualized the process of making tea, each step carefully performed. 他们把泡茶的过程仪式化了，每个步骤都小心翼翼地进行。
They ritualized their morning routine to the point of absurdity. 他们把早上的例行公事仪式化到了荒谬的地步。|近义词：formalize, systematize | 反义词：casualize, informalize


USAGE->

They ritualize their greetings every morning. = 他们每天早上都按仪式进行问候。

过去完成时:
