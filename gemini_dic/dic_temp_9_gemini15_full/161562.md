potstone:/ˈpɒtˌstoʊn/| n. |滑石;陶工滑石|A metamorphic rock that is soft, easily carved, and often used for making pots, stoves, and other objects.  It's composed primarily of talc.| The sculptor used potstone to create a delicate carving. 雕塑家用滑石雕刻了一个精美的雕塑。  The ancient potters valued potstone for its heat resistance. 古代陶工看重滑石的耐热性。| soapstone, steatite|  (no direct antonym)|名词
