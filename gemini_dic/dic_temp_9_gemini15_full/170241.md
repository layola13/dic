protrudent: /prəˈtruːdənt/ | adj. | 突出的，凸出的 | projecting or thrusting outward; prominent.  | The protrudent chin was a family characteristic.  突出的下巴是家族特征。| prominent, projecting, jutting, sticking out | recessed, indented, concave |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rock protrudes from the ground. = 岩石从地面突出。
His eyes protrude slightly. = 他的眼睛略微突出。

一般过去时:
The shelf protruded into the room. = 架子突出到房间里。
His jaw protruded as he spoke. = 他说话时下巴突出。


现在完成时:
The tumor has protruded significantly. = 肿瘤已经明显突出。
His stomach has protruded after the big meal. = 大餐后他的肚子凸出来了。


过去完成时:
The branch had protruded into the road before the accident. = 事故发生前树枝已经伸到路上了。
The nail had protruded from the wood, causing a hazard. = 钉子已经从木头上突出，造成危险。


将来时:
The new building will protrude from the skyline. = 新建筑将从天际线突出。
The sun will protrude from behind the clouds soon. = 太阳很快就会从云层后面露出来。


虚拟语气:
If the bone protruded more, surgery would be necessary. = 如果骨头突出得更多，就需要手术。
I wish my muscles didn't protrude so much. = 我希望我的肌肉不要这么突出。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

His jaw didn't protrude while he was asleep. = 他睡觉时下巴没有突出。
