nonresurrections:/ˌnɒnˌrezəˈrɛkʃənz/| n. |非复活；永不复活|The concept of nonresurrections explores the finality of death in certain contexts, suggesting a state beyond the possibility of revival or rebirth.  This term is less common and often appears in theological or philosophical discussions.  It emphasizes the absolute nature of death in contrast to ideas of resurrection or reincarnation.|例句：The ancient texts described a realm of nonresurrections, where souls ceased to exist after death. 古代文本描述了一个非复活的领域，灵魂死后便不复存在。|近义词：nonrevival, extinction, annihilation|反义词：resurrection, rebirth, reincarnation|名词
