clutcher:/ˈklʌtʃər/| n.  | 紧紧抓住的人或物；离合器；|A clutcher is someone or something that clutches or grasps tightly. It can also refer to a mechanical device, like a clutch in a car, that connects and disconnects rotating shafts.|例句：He was a clutcher, never letting go of anything he considered valuable. 他是个很抓紧东西的人，从不放弃他认为有价值的东西。  The car's clutch was worn out. 这辆车的离合器磨损了。|近义词：grasp, grip, hold |反义词：releaser, loosener |名词
