nonimitativeness:/ˌnɒnɪmɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/| n. |非模仿性，不模仿|The lack of imitation or the quality of not being imitative.  It refers to the absence of copying or mimicking something else.  This can apply to various fields, such as art, behavior, or design. |例句：The artist's style is characterized by its nonimitativeness; he refuses to follow trends.|近义词：originality, uniqueness, unconventionality |反义词：imitativeness, mimicry
