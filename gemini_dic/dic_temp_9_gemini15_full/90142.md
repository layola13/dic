hotdog:/ˈhɒtˌdɒɡ/ /ˈhɑːtˌdɒɡ/| n.|热狗|A cooked sausage served in a long, soft roll, often with various toppings.|例句：I bought a hotdog from the street vendor. 我从街边小贩那里买了一个热狗。|例句：He enjoys a hotdog with mustard and ketchup. 他喜欢吃加芥末和番茄酱的热狗。|近义词：frankfurter, sausage|反义词：无
