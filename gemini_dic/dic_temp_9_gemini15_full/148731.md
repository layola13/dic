overtreating:/ˌoʊvərˈtriːtɪŋ/ | v. |过度治疗，治疗过度 |The act of giving more medical treatment than is necessary or appropriate.  This can lead to adverse effects and increased costs. |例句：Overtreating a common cold with antibiotics is ineffective and can contribute to antibiotic resistance. 过度使用抗生素治疗普通感冒是无效的，还会导致抗生素耐药性。

例句：The patient suffered from overtreating, leading to unnecessary side effects. 患者因过度治疗而遭受不必要的副作用。

近义词：overmedicating, overdosing (in the context of medication)

反义词：undertreating, neglecting (medical care)
