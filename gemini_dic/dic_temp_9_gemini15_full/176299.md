re-drafting:/ˌriːˈdræftɪŋ/| v. |重新起草；修改；修订 |The act of rewriting or revising a text, plan, or design.  It implies a more significant alteration than simply editing. |例句：The committee is re-drafting the proposal to address the concerns raised by the stakeholders. 委员会正在重新起草提案以解决利益相关者提出的担忧。

例句：After receiving feedback, she spent the weekend re-drafting her essay.  收到反馈后，她花了一个周末修改她的论文。


近义词：rewriting, revising, amending, redacting


反义词：finalizing, publishing
