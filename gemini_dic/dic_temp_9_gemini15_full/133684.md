nonfraudulent:/ˌnɒnˈfrɔːdʒələnt/| adj. |非欺诈性的，真实的|Not involving or obtained by fraud.  It describes something genuine and legitimate, free from deception or trickery.|例句：The company emphasized its nonfraudulent business practices. 这家公司强调其非欺诈性的商业行为。

例句：The audit confirmed the nonfraudulent nature of the transactions. 审计证实了交易的真实性。


近义词：legitimate, genuine, authentic, bona fide | 反义词：fraudulent, deceitful, deceptive, dishonest
