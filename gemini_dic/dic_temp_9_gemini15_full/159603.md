pollinates:/ˈpɒləneɪts/| /ˈpɑːləneɪts/| vt.|授粉；使受精|To transfer pollen from the anther of a flower to the stigma of the same or another flower, enabling fertilization.|例句：Bees pollinates flowers. 蜜蜂给花授粉。
The wind pollinates many grasses. 风使许多草受精。|近义词：fertilize, inseminate|反义词：deprive of pollen
