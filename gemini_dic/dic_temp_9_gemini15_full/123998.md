mornings:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋz/ | n. |早晨们，多个早晨|The plural form of "morning," referring to multiple instances of the morning.  It often implies a series of mornings or a habitual occurrence.|例句：The mornings have been cold lately. 最近的早晨都很冷。  I enjoy my quiet mornings with a cup of tea. 我喜欢在清晨安静地喝杯茶。|近义词：mornings (same word, plural form)|反义词：evenings, nights


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mornings are usually quiet. = 早晨通常很安静。
Mornings start early on the farm. = 农场上的早晨开始得很早。

一般过去时:
The mornings were sunny last week. = 上周的早晨阳光明媚。
The mornings were filled with bird song. = 早晨充满了鸟鸣声。

现在完成时:
The mornings have been colder than usual this year. = 今年的早晨比往年冷。
The mornings have become noticeably shorter. = 早晨明显变短了。

过去完成时:
The mornings had been rainy for weeks before the sun finally came out. = 在太阳最终出来之前，好几个星期的早晨都是阴雨连绵的。
By the time I woke up, the mornings had already grown light. = 我醒来的时候，早晨已经变亮了。

将来时:
The mornings will be warmer next month. = 下个月的早晨会更暖和。
The mornings will be filled with the sounds of children playing. = 早晨将会充满孩子们玩耍的声音。

虚拟语气:
If the mornings were always sunny, I would be happier. = 如果早晨总是阳光明媚，我会更快乐。
I wish the mornings were longer. = 我希望早晨更长一些。

被动语态:  (This word rarely uses passive voice directly)  The mornings are often described as... = 早晨经常被描述为……
The mornings are sometimes overlooked. = 早晨有时会被忽视。

疑问句:
Are the mornings usually this cold? = 早晨通常这么冷吗？
What do you do on cold mornings? = 冷天早晨你都做什么？

否定句:
The mornings aren't always pleasant. = 早晨并不总是令人愉快的。
The mornings weren't suitable for a picnic. = 早晨不适合野餐。
