overmobilizes: /ˌoʊvərˈmoʊbəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |过度动员；过度调动|  To mobilize excessively or to a degree that is unproductive or harmful.  The term implies an over-exertion of resources, efforts, or personnel, often leading to inefficiencies or negative consequences. |


例句：

The company overmobilized its resources for the new product launch, leading to burnout among employees and ultimately, a less successful product. = 公司为新产品的发布过度动用了资源，导致员工筋疲力尽，最终产品也不那么成功。

The government overmobilized its military in response to the minor border incident, creating unnecessary tension and escalating the situation. = 政府对轻微的边境事件做出了过度军事动员的反应，制造了不必要的紧张局势，并加剧了事态。


近义词：overextend, overstrain, overwork, exhaust

反义词：undermobilize, underutilize


词性：动词
