non-presbyterian:/ˌnɒnˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriən/| adj. |非长老会的；非长老宗的 |Not belonging to or connected with the Presbyterian Church.  This adjective describes people, organizations, or things that are not associated with Presbyterianism, a branch of Protestant Christianity. |Example:  The local school, despite its non-presbyterian origins, has a strong religious education program.  这所当地学校，尽管起源于非长老会，却有一个强大的宗教教育项目。|近义词：non-denominational, independent |反义词：Presbyterian
