manchuria:/mænˈtʃʊəriə/| n.|满洲；中国东北|Manchuria is a historical region in Northeast Asia, encompassing present-day Northeast China (including Liaoning, Jilin, and Heilongjiang provinces).  The term is now largely avoided in favor of more geographically precise designations due to its historical association with Japanese imperialism.|The Russo-Japanese War was fought largely in Manchuria.|近义词：Northeast China,  (Historically) Manchuria|反义词：无
