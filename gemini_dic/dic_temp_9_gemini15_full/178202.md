recommence:/ˌriːkəˈmɛns/ | v. |重新开始，继续 |To begin something again after it has been interrupted or stopped. |例句：The game recommenced after a short break.  比赛在短暂休息后重新开始。|例句：After the rain stopped, work recommenced on the building. 雨停后，建筑工程重新开始。|近义词：restart, resume, begin again |反义词：stop, cease, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting recommences at 2 pm. = 会议下午两点重新开始。
He recommences his studies next week. = 他下周重新开始学习。

一般过去时:
The concert recommenced after a power outage. = 停电后音乐会重新开始。
She recommenced her work after a long holiday. = 她经过漫长的假期后重新开始工作。

现在完成时:
The project has recommenced after a lengthy delay. = 项目在长时间延迟后重新开始了。
They have recommenced negotiations. = 他们已经重新开始谈判。

过去完成时:
The play had recommenced before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，戏剧已经重新开始了。
The investigation had recommenced after new evidence emerged. = 在出现新的证据后，调查已经重新开始了。

将来时:
The trial will recommence tomorrow. = 审判将于明天重新开始。
He will recommence his painting after his injury heals. = 他的伤痊愈后，他将重新开始绘画。

虚拟语气:
If he were well, he would recommence training. = 如果他身体好，他就会重新开始训练。
I wish she would recommence her music lessons. = 我希望她能重新开始她的音乐课。

被动语态:
The meeting was recommenced after a short delay. = 会议在短暂延迟后重新开始了。
The work will be recommenced as soon as the equipment arrives. = 设备到达后，工作将立即重新开始。

疑问句:
When will the match recommence? = 比赛什么时候重新开始？
Does the show recommence after the intermission? = 中场休息后节目还会重新开始吗？

否定句:
The game did not recommence due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，比赛没有重新开始。
He has not recommenced his writing career yet. = 他还没有重新开始他的写作生涯。
