peacock:/ˈpiːkɒk/ /ˈpiːkɑːk/| n.|孔雀|A large, brilliantly colored bird, the male of which has long tail feathers that it can spread into a fan.|例句：The peacock strutted proudly, displaying its magnificent tail feathers.  孔雀趾高气扬地走着，展示着它华丽的尾羽。

The peacocks in the zoo were a popular attraction. 动物园里的孔雀很受游客欢迎。|近义词：fowl, bird|反义词:None.
