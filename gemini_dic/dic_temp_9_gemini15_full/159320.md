pointrel:/ˈpɔɪntrel/| n. |点关联，点关系|A pointrel is a type of relationship between two points in a geometric or spatial context.  It can be used to describe the connection or association between different locations, objects, or data points.  The term is used more often in specialized fields like geographic information systems (GIS) and computer graphics. |例句：The software used pointrels to map the proximity of different buildings in the city.  该软件使用点关联来绘制城市中不同建筑物之间的距离。

The concept of a pointrel is less common in everyday language and more prevalent in technical contexts.|近义词：relationship, connection, link, association|反义词：disconnection, separation, independence|n.
