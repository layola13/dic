overmitigate: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/ | vt. |过度减轻；过度缓解 | To reduce or alleviate something to an excessive degree, often resulting in unintended negative consequences.  | The government's efforts to overmitigate the economic crisis led to unforeseen inflation.  政府过度缓解经济危机导致了无法预料的通货膨胀。 | mitigate, alleviate, lessen | exacerbate, worsen, aggravate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overmitigate the risks in their investment strategy. = 他们在投资策略中过度降低了风险。
The company overmitigates the environmental impact of its operations. = 这家公司过度减轻了其运营对环境的影响。


一般过去时:
The city overmitigated the effects of the storm, leading to infrastructure damage. = 该城市过度减轻了暴风雨的影响，导致基础设施受损。
They overmitigated the problem, resulting in a new set of challenges. = 他们过度缓解了问题，导致了一系列新的挑战。


现在完成时:
The insurance company has overmitigated its losses by over-reserving. = 保险公司通过过度准备金过度减轻了其损失。
We have overmitigated the potential dangers, creating a false sense of security. = 我们过度减轻了潜在的危险，造成了虚假的安全感。


过去完成时:
They had overmitigated the risks before the project even began. = 项目开始之前，他们就已经过度降低了风险。
The team had overmitigated the impact of the delays, leading to cost overruns. = 团队过度减轻了延误的影响，导致成本超支。


将来时:
The government will overmitigate the impact of the new regulations, causing market instability. = 政府将过度减轻新规定的影响，导致市场不稳定。
They will overmitigate the environmental damage, leading to long-term ecological problems. = 他们将过度减轻环境破坏，导致长期的生态问题。


虚拟语气:
If they had not overmitigated the risks, the project would have been more successful. = 如果他们没有过度降低风险，项目将会更加成功。
I wish they hadn't overmitigated the problem, as it created new issues. = 我真希望他们没有过度缓解这个问题，因为它造成了新的问题。


被动语态:
The risks were overmitigated by the management team. = 风险被管理团队过度降低了。
The environmental impact was overmitigated by using inefficient solutions. = 通过使用低效的解决方案，环境影响被过度减轻了。


疑问句:
Were the risks overmitigated in the initial assessment? = 在初步评估中，风险是否被过度降低了？
Have the potential dangers been overmitigated? = 潜在的危险是否被过度减轻了？


否定句:
They did not overmitigate the risks; instead, they took a calculated approach. = 他们并没有过度降低风险；相反，他们采取了一种深思熟虑的方法。
The company did not overmitigate its environmental responsibility; they made genuine improvements. = 公司并没有过度减轻其环境责任；他们做出了真正的改进。
