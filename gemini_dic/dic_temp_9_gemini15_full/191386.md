schematic:/skɪˈmætɪk/| adj. n.|示意图的；简图的；概要的 |A schematic diagram or representation shows the main features of something in a simplified way, often using symbols.  It's a visual representation that omits unnecessary detail. |例句：The engineer drew a schematic diagram of the circuit.工程师画了一张电路原理图。|例句：He gave us a schematic overview of the project.他给我们做了项目概要介绍。|近义词：diagrammatic, diagram, plan, outline |反义词：detailed, elaborate, comprehensive
