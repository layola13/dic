misaffirm:/ˌmɪsəˈfɜːrm/| vt.| 否认；不认可；误认；错误地肯定 |To state or assert incorrectly; to deny or contradict.  It implies a mistake in affirmation, not necessarily intentional deception. |例句：The witness misaffirmed the defendant's alibi. =目击证人否认了被告的不在场证明。The court misaffirmed the lower court's ruling. =法院错误地肯定了下级法院的裁决。|近义词：deny, contradict, disaffirm, gainsay |反义词：affirm, confirm, corroborate |动词
