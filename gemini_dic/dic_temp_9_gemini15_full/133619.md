nonforbearance:/ˌnɒnfɔːˈbɛəriəns/| n. |不宽容；不忍耐；坚持 |The absence of forbearance; unwillingness to be patient or tolerant.  It often implies a refusal to show leniency or mercy. |例句：His nonforbearance with his employees led to high turnover.  他的不宽容导致员工高离职率。

例句：The court's decision showed a shocking lack of nonforbearance towards the defendant. 法院的判决显示出对被告令人震惊的缺乏宽容。


近义词：intolerance, impatience, severity, strictness


反义词：forbearance, patience, tolerance, leniency, mercy


词性：名词
