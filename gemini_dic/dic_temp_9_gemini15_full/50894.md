desertifying: /dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 荒漠化；沙漠化 | The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.  | 例句：The desertifying process is irreversible without significant intervention.  沙漠化过程如果没有重大干预是不可逆转的。  

The ongoing desertification threatens the livelihood of millions.  持续的沙漠化威胁着数百万人的生计。


近义词：desertization


反义词：reforestation, afforestation, greening


词性：动词 (现在分词)


词源:  The word "desertifying" is derived from the noun "desert" and the suffix "-ify", which means "to make or become".  The "-ing" ending indicates the present participle form, suggesting an ongoing process.  The ultimate root lies in the Latin word "desertum," meaning "abandoned place."
