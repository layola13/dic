shortclothes:/ʃɔːrtˈkloʊðz/| n. |短衣;便服|Shortclothes refers to clothing that is short in length, typically used to describe informal or casual attire.  It's an older term and not frequently used in modern English.  The exact meaning can vary depending on context, but it generally implies a lack of formality.|例句：She changed into her shortclothes after work. 她下班后换上了便服。


例句：The children were playing in their shortclothes in the garden. 孩子们穿着便服在花园里玩耍。


近义词：casual clothes, informal wear, everyday clothes


反义词：formal wear, evening dress, full-dress
