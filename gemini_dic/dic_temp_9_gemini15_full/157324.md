pioneers:/paɪəˈnɪrz/| n. |先驱，开拓者 |People who are the first to explore or settle in a new area or who develop new methods or ideas.  They are often associated with innovation, risk-taking, and overcoming challenges to achieve progress.|例句：The pioneers of the American West faced many hardships.  美国西部的拓荒者们面临许多艰难困苦。

The pioneers of aviation revolutionized travel. 航空先驱们彻底改变了旅行方式。


近义词：trailblazers, innovators, forerunners, explorers, leaders


反义词：followers, imitators


词性:名词
