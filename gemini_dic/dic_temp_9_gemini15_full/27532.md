campylotropous: /ˌkæmpɪˈlɒtrəpəs/ | adj. |弯曲的，弓形的|  Referring to an ovule that is curved or bent, with the micropyle pointing towards the funiculus.  It describes the orientation of an ovule within the ovary of a flowering plant. | Example:  The campylotropous ovule showed a distinct curvature.  这枚弯生胚珠显示出明显的弯曲。| 近义词:  curved, bent,  incurved | 反义词: orthotropous, anatropous
