menthyl:/ˈmɛnθəl/| n. adj.|薄荷脑；薄荷基的|A cyclic terpene alcohol derived from peppermint oil, used in medicine and flavorings.  It has a characteristic strong minty odor and a cooling effect.|例句：Menthol is a common ingredient in cough drops and balms. 薄荷脑是止咳糖和药膏中常见的成分。|例句：The menthyl acetate in the perfume gave it a fresh, clean scent. 香水中的乙酸薄荷酯赋予它清新干净的气味。|近义词：mint,  (as an adjective) minty|反义词：N/A|名词，形容词
