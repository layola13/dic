hookah:/hʊˈkɑː/|/huːˈkɑː/| n. |水烟筒；水烟|A hookah, also known as a shisha, is a single- or multi-stemmed instrument for smoking tobacco or other substances, often flavored. The smoke is passed through a water basin before inhalation.|例句：He relaxed on the patio, enjoying his hookah. 他在露台上放松身心，享受着他的水烟。|例句：The hookah lounge offered a wide variety of flavored tobaccos. 这家水烟吧提供各种口味的烟草。|近义词：shisha, narghile, hubble-bubble|反义词：无
