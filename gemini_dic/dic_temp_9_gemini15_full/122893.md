mollycoddles:/ˈmɒlikɒdəlz/| v.|过分溺爱，娇惯|To treat (someone) in an excessively protective or indulgent way.|例句：She mollycoddled her children, and they grew up spoiled and unable to cope with adversity. 她过分溺爱她的孩子，导致他们长大后被宠坏，无法应对逆境。|近义词：pamper, spoil, coddle|反义词：neglect, discipline
