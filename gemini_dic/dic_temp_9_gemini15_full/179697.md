reenslavement:/ˌriːɪnˈsleɪvmənt/ | n. |重新奴役；再次奴役 | The act of enslaving someone again after they have been freed.  It implies a return to a previous state of enslavement.  This term is often used in discussions of historical injustices and ongoing struggles against oppression. | Example: The reenslavement of freed people after the Civil War was a devastating setback.  重新奴役内战后被解放的人民是毁灭性的挫折。 | 近义词: renewed enslavement, second enslavement | 反义词: liberation, emancipation |
