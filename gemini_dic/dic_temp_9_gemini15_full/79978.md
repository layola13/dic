go-carts:/ˈɡoʊkɑːrts/| n. |小型赛车，儿童赛车 |A go-kart is a small, open-wheeled motor vehicle designed for recreational use, especially racing. They are typically powered by small engines and are popular among children and adults.|例句：The kids had a blast racing their go-carts around the track.孩子们在赛道上开着他们的卡丁车玩得很开心。|近义词：kart, mini-car |反义词：large car, truck
