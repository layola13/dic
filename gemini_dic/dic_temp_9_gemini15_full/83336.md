gypsydoms:/ˈdʒɪpsidoʊmz/| n. |吉普赛人的住所，游牧民族的住所，临时住所 |A gypsydom refers to the dwellings or settlements of Romani people (often called Gypsies), typically characterized by their temporary and nomadic nature.  It can also be used more broadly to describe any temporary or makeshift dwelling, evoking the image of a transient lifestyle. | Example:  The traveling circus set up its gypsydom in the town square for the week.  这个巡回马戏团在本周在城镇广场上搭建了他们的住所。  The artist created a makeshift gypsydom in the abandoned barn.  艺术家在一个废弃的谷仓里搭建了一个临时的住所。 |近义词：encampment, settlement, camp, abode |反义词：permanent residence, fixed dwelling
