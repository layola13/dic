devised:/dɪˈvaɪzd/| vt.| 设计；发明；策划 | To plan or invent something, typically a complex scheme or system.  | 例句：They devised a plan to escape.  他们设计了一个逃跑计划。 | 例句：The engineers devised a new system for controlling traffic.工程师们设计了一个新的交通控制系统。| 近义词：invented, planned, conceived, formulated | 反义词：destroyed, ruined, dismantled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devises new marketing strategies every year. = 他每年都会设计新的营销策略。
They devise innovative solutions to complex problems. = 他们设计出解决复杂问题的创新方案。


一般过去时:
She devised a clever solution to the puzzle. = 她设计了一个巧妙的解谜方案。
The team devised a winning strategy for the competition. = 团队制定了一个赢得比赛的策略。


现在完成时:
Scientists have devised new methods for treating the disease. = 科学家们已经设计出了治疗这种疾病的新方法。
We have devised a system to track our progress. = 我们设计了一个系统来跟踪我们的进度。


过去完成时:
They had devised a backup plan in case the first one failed. = 他们已经设计了一个备用计划，以防第一个计划失败。
She had devised a complex plot for her new novel. = 她为她的新小说设计了一个复杂的情节。


将来时:
He will devise a more efficient system for data entry. = 他将设计一个更高效的数据录入系统。
They will devise a plan to raise money for the charity. = 他们将制定一个为慈善机构筹款的计划。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would devise a better solution. = 如果我有更多时间，我会设计一个更好的方案。
I wish I could devise a way to solve this problem. = 我希望我能想出一个解决这个问题的方法。


被动语态:
A new training program was devised by the HR department. = 人力资源部门设计了一个新的培训项目。
The escape route was cleverly devised. = 逃生路线设计得很巧妙。


疑问句:
What methods did they devise to achieve their goal? = 他们设计了什么方法来实现他们的目标？
Has a new strategy been devised to address the issue? = 是否已经制定了新的策略来解决这个问题？


否定句:
They did not devise a suitable plan for the project. = 他们没有为这个项目制定一个合适的计划。
He could not devise a solution to the problem. = 他无法找到解决这个问题的方案。


PHRASE->
devise a plan = 制定计划
devise a strategy = 制定策略
devise a method = 设计方法
devise a system = 设计系统
devise a solution = 想出解决方法
