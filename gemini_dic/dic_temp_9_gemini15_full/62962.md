ennobler:/ɪˈnoʊblər/ | n. v. | 使高尚的人；提升者；使…高贵；使…崇高 |  To elevate or improve the character or status of someone or something; to make more noble. |  例句：His selfless act ennobled him in the eyes of the community.  他的无私行为在他社区民众眼中提升了他的形象。 |近义词：elevate, exalt, refine, dignify | 反义词：debase, degrade, demean
