maximise:/ˈmæksɪmaɪz/| vt.|最大化；使最大限度地发挥作用|To increase or make the most of something.|The company is trying to maximise profits. 公司正试图最大化利润。|increase, optimize, amplify|minimize, reduce, lessen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We maximise our efficiency by using new software. = 我们通过使用新软件来最大化效率。
They maximise their profits through strategic investments. = 他们通过战略投资来最大化利润。


一般过去时:
The team maximised their performance in the final game. = 球队在决赛中最大限度地发挥了他们的实力。
She maximised her chances of success by studying hard. = 她通过努力学习来最大限度地提高了成功的几率。


现在完成时:
We have maximised our resources to meet the deadline. = 我们已经最大限度地利用了资源以满足最后期限。
They have maximised their market share through aggressive marketing. = 他们通过积极的营销策略最大限度地提高了市场份额。


过去完成时:
They had maximised their production capacity before the order arrived. = 订单到达之前，他们已经最大限度地提高了生产能力。
She had maximised her savings before investing in the stock market. = 她在投资股市之前已经最大限度地储蓄了资金。


将来时:
We will maximise our output with the new equipment. = 我们将用新设备最大限度地提高产量。
They will maximise their impact by focusing on key areas. = 他们将通过关注重点领域来最大限度地发挥影响。


虚拟语气:
If we maximised our efforts, we would achieve better results. = 如果我们最大限度地努力，我们将取得更好的结果。
I wish I had maximised my potential in college. = 我希望我在大学期间能最大限度地发挥我的潜力。


被动语态:
Profits are maximised by cutting costs. = 通过削减成本可以最大化利润。
Resources should be maximised to achieve the project's goals. = 应该最大限度地利用资源来实现项目的既定目标。


疑问句:
How can we maximise our return on investment? = 我们如何才能最大限度地提高投资回报率？
What strategies can maximise the effectiveness of the campaign? = 哪些策略可以最大限度地提高活动的有效性？


否定句:
They did not maximise their potential. = 他们没有最大限度地发挥他们的潜力。
The company failed to maximise its market share. = 公司未能最大限度地提高其市场份额。
