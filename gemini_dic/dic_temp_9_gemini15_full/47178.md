day-dream:/ˈdeɪdriːm/| n. v.| 白日梦；幻想 | A pleasant thought or fantasy that distracts one from what one is doing; to indulge in pleasant thoughts and fantasies, often while awake. |例句：She was daydreaming about her vacation. 她正在做关于假期的白日梦。

例句：He daydreamed during the boring lecture. 他在枯燥的演讲中做起了白日梦。

近义词：fantasy, reverie, vision |反义词：reality, focus


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He daydreams often. = 他经常做白日梦。
She daydreams about her future. = 她常常幻想她的未来。

一般过去时:
I daydreamed about my childhood. = 我回想起了我的童年。
They daydreamed during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间做白日梦。

现在完成时:
She has daydreamed about this for years. = 她多年来一直梦想着这个。
He has daydreamed of becoming a famous writer. = 他一直梦想着成为一名著名作家。

过去完成时:
I had daydreamed about that moment for so long. = 我已经对那一刻幻想了很久。
She had daydreamed of traveling the world before she got married. = 在结婚之前，她梦想着环游世界。

将来时:
I will daydream about my next adventure. = 我将要幻想我的下一个冒险。
He will daydream less once he starts his new job. = 一旦他开始他的新工作，他就会少做白日梦了。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would daydream more often. = 如果我有时间，我会更经常做白日梦。
I wish I could daydream without feeling guilty. = 我希望我能不做白日梦而不会感到内疚。

被动语态:
His daydreams were interrupted by the phone call. = 他的白日梦被电话打断了。
Her plans were often described as daydreams by her family. = 她家人都说她的计划是白日梦。

疑问句:
Do you often daydream? = 你经常做白日梦吗？
What do you usually daydream about? = 你通常做些什么白日梦？

否定句:
He doesn't daydream much anymore. = 他现在很少做白日梦了。
She didn't daydream during the exam. = 她考试期间没有做白日梦。


PHRASE->
daydream away (时间):  He daydreamed away the afternoon. 他整个下午都在做白日梦。
daydream about: She daydreamed about her wedding day. 她幻想她的婚礼。
lose oneself in daydreams: He lost himself in daydreams. 他沉醉于白日梦中。
a daydream of: It was a daydream of a life lived in luxury. 这是一个奢华生活的白日梦。
indulge in daydreams: She indulged in daydreams of success. 她沉迷于成功的幻想。
