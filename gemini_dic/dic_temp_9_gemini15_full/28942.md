carroting:/ˈkærətɪŋ/| n.|胡萝卜种植；使…变橙色；(头发)烫发|The process of growing or cultivating carrots, or the act of making something orange, or the process of perming hair.|例句：The farmer is busy with carroting this season.  这个季节农民忙着种植胡萝卜。 |近义词：cultivating (for carrots), dyeing (for orange), perming|反义词：None specifically applicable to all meanings.
