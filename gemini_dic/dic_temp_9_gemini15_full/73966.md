fortify:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪ/| vt.|加强，巩固，设防|to make something stronger or more secure, especially by adding something to it|The soldiers fortified the position with barbed wire. 士兵们用铁丝网加固了阵地。|strengthen, reinforce, secure|weaken, undermine, destabilize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fortify the city walls. = 他们加固城墙。
We fortify our immune system with vitamins. = 我们用维生素增强免疫系统。

一般过去时:
The army fortified the border. = 军队加固了边境。
She fortified herself with a cup of coffee. = 她喝了一杯咖啡提神。

现在完成时:
They have fortified the castle against attack. = 他们已经加固了城堡以抵御攻击。
He has fortified his position with new evidence. = 他用新的证据巩固了自己的立场。

过去完成时:
They had fortified the bridge before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，他们已经加固了桥梁。
She had fortified her argument with statistics. = 她用统计数据强化了自己的论点。

将来时:
They will fortify the defenses against a potential invasion. = 他们将加强防御工事以抵御潜在的入侵。
We will fortify the house against the storm. = 我们将加固房子以抵御风暴。

虚拟语气:
If they fortified the position, they would have a better chance of winning. = 如果他们加固了阵地，他们将有更大的获胜机会。
I wish they had fortified the building before the earthquake. = 我真希望他们在地震前加固了那栋建筑。

被动语态:
The walls were fortified by the soldiers. = 城墙被士兵们加固了。
The position is being fortified with additional troops. = 该阵地正在用增援部队加固。

疑问句:
Did they fortify the bridge? = 他们加固了桥梁吗？
Will they fortify the castle? = 他们会加固城堡吗？

否定句:
They did not fortify the town. = 他们没有加固城镇。
He will not fortify his position without more evidence. = 没有更多证据，他不会巩固自己的立场。
