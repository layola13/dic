savagely:/ˈsævɪdʒli/ | adj. |野蛮地；残酷地；凶猛地 |  In a fierce, violent, or brutal manner.  It suggests a lack of restraint and often implies a primitive or uncontrolled nature. |例句：The lion savagely attacked its prey.  狮子凶猛地袭击了它的猎物。

The storm savagely battered the coastline.  暴风雨猛烈地袭击了海岸线。


近义词：fiercely, brutally, violently, ferociously, cruelly

反义词：gently, kindly, mildly, tenderly, compassionately

词性：形容词
