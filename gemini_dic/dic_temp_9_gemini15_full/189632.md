salivated:/ˈsælɪveɪtɪd/| v.|流口水，垂涎欲滴|To secrete saliva; to drool.  The word implies an involuntary response, often associated with hunger, anticipation, or a strong desire.|例句：The dog salivated at the sight of the steak.  这只狗看到牛排就流口水了。

例句：The child salivated with excitement at the thought of the birthday party.  孩子想到生日派对就兴奋地流口水了。


近义词：drool, slobber


反义词：无


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salivates whenever he sees food. = 他每当看到食物就流口水。
The baby salivates constantly. = 婴儿不停地流口水。


一般过去时:
I salivated when I smelled the freshly baked bread. = 我闻到刚烤好的面包时流口水了。
The dog salivated excessively after the long walk. = 那只狗长时间散步后流口水过多。


现在完成时:
He has salivated all day because of the delicious aroma. = 因为香气扑鼻，他一整天都在流口水。
The child has salivated since seeing the candy. = 孩子自从看到糖果以来就一直在流口水。


过去完成时:
I had salivated for hours before the meal arrived. = 在饭菜送来之前我已经流口水好几个小时了。
The guests had salivated in anticipation of the feast. = 客人们在期待盛宴时早已垂涎欲滴。


将来时:
I think I will salivate when I taste the cake. = 我想当我尝到蛋糕的时候我会流口水。
The audience will salivate at the sight of the chef's creations. = 观众看到厨师的作品将会垂涎欲滴。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would salivate at the prospect of such a delicious meal. = 如果我是你，我会对如此美味的一餐垂涎欲滴。
I wish I hadn't salivated so much; it was embarrassing. = 我真希望我没有流那么多口水，真丢人。


被动语态:  (被动语态用得较少，因为 salivate 通常是自动词)
The steak was salivated over by the hungry dog. = 那块牛排让饥饿的狗垂涎欲滴。(较为不自然)


疑问句:
Does the baby salivate a lot? = 婴儿流口水多吗？
Will the audience salivate at the performance? = 观众会对表演垂涎欲滴吗？


否定句:
He didn't salivate at the sight of the vegetables. = 他看到蔬菜并没有流口水。
The cat didn't salivate at the sound of the can opener. = 猫听到开罐器的声音并没有流口水。


PHRASE->
salivate over sth: 垂涎某物
salivate with anticipation: 期待地流口水
salivate at the thought of: 一想到…就流口水
salivate uncontrollably: 不受控制地流口水
salivate excessively: 流口水过多
