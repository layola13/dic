overpunishments: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃmənts/ | n. | 过度惩罚 | Excessive or unduly harsh punishments.  The term implies that the punishment is disproportionate to the offense.  It often suggests a systemic problem rather than an isolated incident. |


例句：

The overpunishments inflicted on the prisoners led to widespread outrage. = 对囚犯施加的过度惩罚导致了广泛的愤怒。

The school's zero-tolerance policy resulted in a number of overpunishments for minor infractions. = 学校的零容忍政策导致对轻微违规行为进行了许多过度惩罚。


近义词：excessive punishments, unduly harsh penalties, disproportionate discipline

反义词：lenient punishments, mild penalties, forgiving discipline

词性：名词
