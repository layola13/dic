overdiscuss:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌs/| v.|过度讨论，反复讨论|To discuss something excessively or repeatedly; to talk about something too much.|例句：They overdiscussed the project details, delaying the deadline. 他们对项目细节进行了过度讨论，导致延误了最后期限。
例句：We overdiscussed the same issue multiple times, making everyone tired. 我们多次反复讨论同一个问题，让每个人都很疲惫。|近义词：rehash, belabor, dwell on|反义词：neglect, ignore

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overdiscuss the budget every meeting. = 他们每次会议都过度讨论预算。
We overdiscuss minor details, wasting valuable time. = 我们过度讨论细枝末节，浪费了宝贵的时间。

一般过去时:
They overdiscussed the marketing strategy last night. = 他们昨晚过度讨论了营销策略。
We overdiscussed the problem until everyone felt exhausted. = 我们反复讨论这个问题直到每个人都筋疲力尽。

现在完成时:
They have overdiscussed this topic for weeks. = 他们已经讨论这个话题几周了。
We have overdiscussed the contract terms, causing unnecessary delays. = 我们已经过度讨论了合同条款，造成了不必要的延误。

过去完成时:
They had overdiscussed the issue before the final decision was made. = 在最终决定做出之前，他们已经对这个问题进行了过度讨论。
We had overdiscussed the design multiple times before reaching a consensus. = 在达成共识之前，我们已经多次反复讨论了设计方案。

将来时:
They will overdiscuss the proposal tomorrow. = 他们明天将会过度讨论这个提案。
We will overdiscuss the plan unless we set a clear time limit. = 除非我们设定一个明确的时间限制，否则我们将过度讨论这个计划。

虚拟语气:
If they had not overdiscussed the details, they would have finished on time. = 如果他们没有过度讨论细节，他们就能按时完成。
I wish we hadn't overdiscussed the matter so much. = 我希望我们没有对这件事讨论那么多。

被动语态:

否定句:
