exculpation:/ˌɛkskʌlˈpeɪʃən/| n. |免罪；辩解；开脱|The act of clearing from blame or guilt; justification; vindication.|例句：The exculpation of the suspect came as a surprise to many.  嫌疑人的免罪令许多人感到意外。

例句：His detailed alibi provided a strong exculpation. 他详细的不在场证明提供了有力的辩解。


近义词：acquittal, absolution, vindication, justification|反义词：conviction, condemnation, incrimination|词性:名词
