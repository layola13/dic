/hɛrd/ | n. |  兔群；(猎物)一群；(尤指)一群野兔 | A herd is a large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live and feed together.  The term is most commonly associated with deer, cattle, and especially rabbits.  | Example: A herd of deer grazed peacefully in the meadow.  一群鹿在草地上平静地吃草。 |近义词：flock, drove, pack | 反义词：individual, single


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The herd grazes in the field. = 兔群在田野里吃草。
A large herd of elephants migrates across the savanna. = 一大群大象在稀树草原上迁徙。


一般过去时:
The herd moved slowly across the plain. = 兔群缓慢地穿过平原。
A massive herd of bison thundered across the prairie. = 一大群野牛隆隆地穿过草原。


现在完成时:
The herd has grazed in this area for centuries. = 这群动物在这个地区吃草已有数个世纪了。
The farmer has successfully herded his cattle into the barn. = 农民成功地将他的牛群赶进了谷仓。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
