realisms:/ˈriːəlɪzəmz/ | n. |现实主义;写实主义 |The philosophical position that emphasizes the importance of experience and reality.  Realism in art and literature focuses on portraying the world as it is, without idealization or romanticism. |例句：The painting is a stunning example of realism. 这幅画是现实主义的杰出例子。

例句：Her novels are known for their gritty realism. 她的小说以其粗犷的现实主义而闻名。


近义词：realism, naturalism, verisimilitude | 反义词：idealism, romanticism | 词性：名词
