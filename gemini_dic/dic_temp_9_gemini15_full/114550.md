malarious:/məˈleəriəs/ | adj. | 患疟疾的；疟疾盛行的 | Affected by or relating to malaria.  The adjective describes a place or person suffering from or associated with malaria. | 例句：The malarious swamp was avoided by all travelers.  所有旅行者都避开那片疟疾盛行的沼泽地。

The malarious region required prophylactic medication.  疟疾流行地区需要预防性药物。


近义词：feverish, infected (in the context of malaria), unhealthy

反义词：healthy, malaria-free


词性:形容词
