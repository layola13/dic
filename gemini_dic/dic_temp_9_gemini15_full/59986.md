edited:/ˈedɪtɪd/ | adj. | 编辑过的，修改过的 |  Having been corrected, revised, or improved.  The edited manuscript was much clearer than the original draft. | 近义词：revised, amended, corrected | 反义词：original, unedited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article is edited by a professional. = 这篇文章由专业人士编辑。
The editor edits the manuscript carefully. = 编辑仔细地编辑手稿。

一般过去时:
He edited the book last year. = 他去年编辑了这本书。
She edited the report before submitting it. = 她在提交报告之前对其进行了编辑。

现在完成时:
They have edited the video several times. = 他们已经编辑了几次视频。
The author has edited the novel for publication. = 作者已编辑小说以供出版。

过去完成时:
He had edited the document before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经编辑了该文件。
She had edited the essay twice before she submitted it. = 她提交论文之前已经修改了两次。

将来时:
We will edit the film tomorrow. = 我们明天将编辑这部电影。
They will have edited the footage by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末前编辑好素材。

虚拟语气:
If I had edited the paper more carefully, I wouldn't have made so many mistakes. = 如果我更仔细地编辑论文，我就不会犯这么多错误。
I wish I had edited the photos before posting them. = 我希望我在发布照片之前已经编辑过了它们。


被动语态:
The text was edited by a team of experts. = 这段文字由专家团队编辑。
The images are being edited for the website. = 图片正在被编辑以用于网站。

疑问句:
Has the document been edited? = 文件编辑了吗？
Will the video be edited soon? = 视频很快就会编辑好么？

否定句:
The manuscript was not edited properly. = 手稿没有被正确编辑。
The report hasn't been edited yet. = 报告还没有被编辑。
