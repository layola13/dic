grandees:/ɡrænˈdiːz/| n.|显要人物，要人|Important and influential people, especially in a particular field or society.  Grandees often hold high positions of power or authority and are respected for their status. |例句：The grandees of the court were summoned to the king's presence.  宫廷要员被召见国王。

The grandees of the business world attended the gala. 商业巨头们参加了这次盛会。|近义词：dignitaries, notables, magnates, elites|反义词：commoners, nobodies, unknowns|词性:名词
