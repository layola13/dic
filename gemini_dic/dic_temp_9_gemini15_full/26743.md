cadrans:/ˈkædrənz/| n.|象限，扇形区，钟面|A cadran is a quadrant or section of a circle, often used in the context of sundials or other measuring instruments.  It can also refer to a clock face.  The word is less common in modern English.|例句：The sundial's cadran was divided into four quadrants. 日晷的象限被分成四个象限。|The dial of the clock was beautifully decorated, each cadran clearly marked.  钟表的表盘装饰精美，每个扇形区都标示清晰。|近义词：quadrant, sector, division|反义词：whole, entirety
