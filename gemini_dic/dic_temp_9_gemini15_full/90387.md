hower: /ˈhaʊər/ | n. |阵雨；淋浴；大量倾泻| A shower is a brief period of rain, or a device used for showering.  |例句：We got caught in a sudden shower. 我们突然遇上了阵雨。
例句：I took a refreshing shower after my workout. 我锻炼后洗了个清爽的淋浴。|近义词：downpour, rainfall, deluge|反义词：drought, dry spell|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shower usually lasts for about 10 minutes. = 这阵雨通常持续大约10分钟。
We take showers every morning. = 我们每天早上都淋浴。

一般过去时:

将来时:
