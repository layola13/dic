delightfully:/dɪˈlaɪtfəli/| adj. |令人愉快地；使人高兴地 | Pleasantly; in a way that gives great pleasure.  It describes something that is very enjoyable and pleasing. |例句：The children played delightfully in the park.孩子们在公园里玩得很开心。

例句：The meal was delightfully prepared and presented. 这顿饭准备和摆盘都令人愉快。
