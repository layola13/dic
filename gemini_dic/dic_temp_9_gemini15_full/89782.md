horizontal:/ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntəl/ /ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəl/| adj.|水平的，横向的|Parallel to the horizon; at right angles to the vertical.  It refers to a line or plane that is parallel to the horizon.  |例句：The lines on the graph are horizontal. 图表上的线条是水平的。
The sun cast long horizontal shadows. 太阳投下长长的水平阴影。|近义词：level, flat, parallel|反义词：vertical, perpendicular

The table is horizontal. = 桌子是水平的。

一般过去时:

词源:
The word "horizontal" comes from the Latin word "horizon," meaning "bounding line,"  "limit," or "boundary."  The suffix "-al" is added to form the adjective "horizontal," meaning relating to or situated in a horizontal direction.
