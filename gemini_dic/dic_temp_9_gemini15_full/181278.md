reinsulate:/ˌriːˈɪnsjuleɪt/ | vt. | 重新隔热；再次隔音 | To insulate something again.  This often involves adding more insulation to an existing structure or replacing old, ineffective insulation. | 例句：We need to reinsulate the attic to improve energy efficiency. = 我们需要重新隔热阁楼以提高能源效率。

例句：The house was so cold, we decided to reinsulate the walls. = 房子太冷了，我们决定重新隔热墙壁。


近义词：re-insulate, add insulation, improve insulation | 反义词：de-insulate, remove insulation | 词性:动词
