locks:/lɒks/ /lɑːks/| n. v.|锁；卷发；锁住；固定|

英文解析:
Noun (n.):  A lock is a mechanism used to fasten something, preventing it from being opened or moved without a key or other means of release. It can also refer to a section of hair that is twisted or braided together.

Verb (v.): To lock means to fasten something with a lock.  It can also refer to the act of becoming fixed or stuck in a particular position or state.


例句(Noun):
The door locks are quite old and need replacing. = 门锁很旧了，需要更换。

He has beautiful locks of golden hair. = 他有一头漂亮的金发。


例句(Verb):
Please lock the door when you leave. = 请你离开时锁上门。

The car locks automatically when you drive away. = 汽车离开时会自动落锁。


近义词:
Noun: fastener, clasp, catch, tress (for hair)
Verb: fasten, secure, close, latch


反义词:
Noun: unlock, key
Verb: unlock, open, unfasten


词性:
Noun and Verb
