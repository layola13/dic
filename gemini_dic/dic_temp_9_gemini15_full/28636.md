cargos:/ˈkɑːrɡoʊz/ | n. |货物，船货 | Cargo refers to goods carried by a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  It encompasses a wide range of items transported commercially. | The ship's cargo included textiles, machinery, and food. 这艘船的货物包括纺织品、机械和食品。|  freight, shipment, load |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境用empty, unload等) | 名词

The ship carries a large cargo of grain. = 这艘船运载着大量的谷物。
Their company regularly transports various cargos to different countries. = 他们的公司定期向不同的国家运输各种货物。
