resubscription: /ˌriːsʌbˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |再次订阅；续订 | The act of subscribing again; renewing a subscription.  |例句：My resubscription to the streaming service automatically renewed. 我的流媒体服务订阅自动续订了。

例句：The magazine offered a discount for resubscription. 这本杂志为再次订阅提供了折扣。


近义词：renewal, re-enrollment, subscription renewal

反义词：cancellation, termination


词源:  re- (prefix meaning "again") + subscription (from subscribe)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resubscribes to the magazine every year. 他每年都重新订阅这本杂志。
She resubscribes to the streaming service after a month's break. 她在一个月的休息后重新订阅了流媒体服务。


一般过去时:
I resubscribed to the newspaper after a long break. 我在长时间休息后重新订阅了报纸。
They resubscribed to the software after the free trial ended. 免费试用期结束后，他们重新订阅了该软件。


现在完成时:
She has resubscribed to the fitness app this month. 她这个月重新订阅了健身应用程序。
He has resubscribed to the service since last year. 自从去年以来，他就重新订阅了这项服务。


过去完成时:
I had resubscribed to the magazine before I realized it was cheaper elsewhere. 在我意识到其他地方更便宜之前，我已经重新订阅了这本杂志。
They had resubscribed to the streaming service before the price increase. 在价格上涨之前，他们已经重新订阅了流媒体服务。


将来时:
I will resubscribe to the service next month. 我下个月将重新订阅这项服务。
They will resubscribe to the magazine once they have saved enough money. 他们攒够钱后就会重新订阅杂志。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would resubscribe to the gym. 如果我有更多钱，我会重新订阅健身房。
I wish I had resubscribed to the music streaming service earlier. 我真希望我早点重新订阅音乐流媒体服务。


被动语态:
The subscription was resubscribed automatically. 订阅已自动续订。
The service will be resubscribed by the customer next week. 客户下周将重新订阅这项服务。


疑问句:
Will you resubscribe to the magazine? 你会重新订阅这本杂志吗？
Have they resubscribed to the streaming service yet? 他们已经重新订阅流媒体服务了吗？


否定句:
I didn't resubscribe to the service this year. 我今年没有重新订阅这项服务。
He hasn't resubscribed to the magazine yet. 他还没重新订阅杂志。
