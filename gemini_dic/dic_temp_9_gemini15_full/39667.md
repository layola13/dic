conservators:/ˌkɒnsəˈveɪtərz/ /ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪtərz/| n. |保护者；馆长；保守派人士 |A person who preserves, protects, or restores something, especially works of art or historical objects.  They are also people who advocate for preserving traditional values and practices.|

例句：

The museum conservators carefully restored the ancient painting.  = 博物馆的馆长们小心翼翼地修复了这幅古代绘画。

Many art conservators work tirelessly to preserve cultural heritage. = 许多艺术保护者不知疲倦地致力于保护文化遗产。


近义词：preservers, curators, preservationists

反义词：destroyers, vandals, radicals (in the political sense)


词性：名词
