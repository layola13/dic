dreamland:/ˈdriːmlænd/| n. |梦境，幻想乡|A place or state of mind that is idyllic and unreal, often associated with sleep or imagination.  It can also refer to a place that seems perfect or wonderful, but is not actually real.|例句：The children were lost in dreamland.孩子们沉浸在梦境中。

例句：She described her perfect vacation as a tropical dreamland.她把完美的假期描述成一个热带的幻想乡。|近义词：fantasy land, wonderland, paradise|反义词：reality, harsh reality
