misquotations:/ˌmɪsˌkwoʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 误引用；曲解；断章取义 | Incorrect or inaccurate attributions of a statement or writing to someone; a false or misleading representation of what someone said or wrote.  | Example: The article was full of misquotations, twisting the speaker's words to fit a particular narrative. 这篇文章充满了误引用，歪曲了说话者的意思以符合特定的叙述。  |近义词：distortions, misrepresentations, paraphrases (inaccurate), misinterpretations | 反义词：accurate quotations, faithful representations | 名词
