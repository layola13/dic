semi-retirement: /ˈsɛmi rɪˈtaɪərmənt/ | n. | 半退休；半退役 |  A state of partial retirement, where one continues to work part-time or on a reduced scale.  It's a transition period between full-time employment and complete retirement.  |  例句：After 30 years in the corporate world, John opted for semi-retirement, consulting a few days a week.  三十年商界生涯后，约翰选择了半退休，每周咨询几天。  She's enjoying semi-retirement, teaching a couple of classes a week and spending more time with her grandchildren. 她享受着半退休生活，每周教几节课，并花更多时间陪伴孙子孙女。| 近义词：partial retirement, phased retirement | 反义词：full retirement, full-time employment | 名词
