noir:/nwɑːr/| adj.|黑色电影；黑色风格的| pertaining to or characteristic of a distinctive style of filmmaking characterized by cynicism, fatalism, and moral ambiguity; often featuring detectives, crime, and violence. |例句：The film is a classic example of film noir. 这部电影是黑色电影的经典之作。|近义词：dark, gloomy, shadowy|反义词：bright, cheerful, optimistic
