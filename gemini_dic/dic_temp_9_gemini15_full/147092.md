overlier:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪər/| n. |覆盖物，覆盖层|A covering layer or material placed over something else.  It can refer to a physical layer or a figurative one, such as a layer of paint or a layer of secrecy.|例句：The overlier protected the delicate seedlings from the harsh sunlight.  覆盖层保护了娇嫩的幼苗免受强烈的阳光照射。|The overlier of secrecy kept the truth hidden for years. 秘密的覆盖层让真相隐藏多年。|近义词：covering, overlay, layer|反义词：underlayer, base, foundation|名词
