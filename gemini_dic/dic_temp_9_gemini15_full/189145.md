sachet:/ˈsæʃeɪ/| n. |小袋；香料袋；小包 |A small bag or packet, typically containing a small quantity of a substance such as perfume, spices, tea, or medicine.  |例句：She added a sachet of herbs to the stew. 她往炖菜里加了一包香料。

例句：The hotel provided sachets of shampoo and conditioner.  酒店提供了洗发水和护发素的小包装。


近义词：packet, envelope, pouch |反义词：None.


词源:  From French *sachet*, diminutive of *sac* "sack".  Ultimately from Latin *saccus*.
