scolder:/ˈskoʊldər/| n. |责骂者，训斥者 |A person who scolds frequently.  Someone who often criticizes or rebukes others. |Example:  He's a terrible scolder; his children are always afraid of him. 他是个可怕的责骂者；他的孩子们总是害怕他。|近义词：rebuker, reprover, censurer |反义词：praiser, flatterer |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher scolds the noisy student. = 老师责备吵闹的学生。
He often scolds his employees for minor mistakes. = 他经常因为一些小错误而责备他的员工。


一般过去时:
My mother scolded me for breaking the vase. = 妈妈因为我打破花瓶而责备我。
The boss scolded the late employee yesterday. = 老板昨天责备了迟到的员工。


现在完成时:
She has scolded him several times this week. = 本周她已经责备他好几次了。
They have scolded the children for their bad behavior. = 他们因为孩子们的恶劣行为而责备了他们。


过去完成时:
He had scolded her before she even spoke. = 她还没开口他就已经责备她了。
The manager had scolded the team before the presentation. = 经理在演示之前已经责备了团队。


将来时:
I will scold him if he does it again. = 如果他再这样做，我会责备他。
She will scold her son if he doesn't clean his room. = 如果她儿子不打扫房间，她会责备他。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't scold him so harshly. = 如果我是你，我就不会那么严厉地责备他。
I wish I hadn't scolded her so severely. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地责备她。


被动语态:
He was scolded by his teacher for cheating. = 他因为作弊而被老师责备。
The employees were scolded for their inefficiency. = 员工们因为效率低下而受到责备。


疑问句:
Was he scolded for his mistake? = 他因为他的错误而受到责备了吗？
Will she be scolded by her parents? = 她会被父母责备吗？


否定句:
He didn't scold the children. = 他没有责备孩子们。
She doesn't usually scold her students. = 她通常不会责备她的学生。


PHRASE->
a harsh scolder = 一个严厉的责骂者
a constant scolder = 一个经常责骂的人
a public scolder = 一个公开责骂的人
to receive a scolding = 受到责备
to deserve a scolding = 应该受到责备
