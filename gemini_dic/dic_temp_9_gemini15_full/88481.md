historic: /hɪˈstɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 历史上著名的；具有历史意义的 | Relating to or connected with history; important in history.  |例句：The signing of the Declaration of Independence was a historic event.  签署《独立宣言》是一件具有历史意义的事件。


例句：This building is historic; it dates back to the 18th century. 这座建筑物是历史性的；它可以追溯到18世纪。


近义词：famous, momentous, significant, epoch-making | 反义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial | 词性：形容词

The museum showcases many historic artifacts. = 博物馆展出许多历史文物。
This historic site attracts many tourists every year. = 这个历史遗址每年吸引众多游客。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
Historians have studied this historic period extensively. = 历史学家们已经广泛研究了这段历史时期。
The city has preserved many of its historic buildings. = 这个城市保存了许多历史建筑。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
Is this a historic site? = 这是个历史遗迹吗？

否定句:
