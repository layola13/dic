progressivity:/ˌprɒɡrɛˈsɪvɪti/ /prɑːɡrɛˈsɪvɪti/ | n. |进步性；前进性；累进性 | The quality or state of being progressive; advancement or development.  It often refers to a system where the tax rate increases as the taxable amount increases.  |例句：The progressivity of the tax system ensures that higher earners contribute a larger share.  累进税制确保高收入者贡献更大比例。

The new government's policies show a strong commitment to social progressivity. 新政府的政策显示出对社会进步的坚定承诺。


近义词：advancement, development, progress, escalation (in the context of tax rates) |反义词：regression, retrogression, stagnation (in the context of progress); regressivity (in the context of tax rates)


词源：源自于英语单词 "progressive"，其词根为拉丁语 "progressus"，意思是“前进”。  "-ivity" 是一个名词后缀，表示“性质”或“状态”。
