come-unglued: /ˌkʌm ʌnˈɡluːd/ | v. | 崩溃；失控；情绪失控 | To lose control emotionally; to become distraught or hysterical; to fall apart.  |例句：She completely came unglued when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后完全崩溃了。|  例句：Under the pressure, the team came unglued and lost the game.  在压力下，球队崩溃了，输掉了比赛。|近义词：lose it, fall apart, crack up, freak out |反义词：remain calm, keep composure, stay cool |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes unglued when he's stressed. = 他压力大的时候就会崩溃。
She comes unglued easily. = 她很容易情绪失控。


一般过去时:
He came unglued after the accident. = 事故发生后，他崩溃了。
The audience came unglued during the concert. = 音乐会期间，观众们情绪激动失控了。


现在完成时:
She has come unglued several times this week. = 她本周已经好几次崩溃了。
He has never come unglued in public before. = 他以前从未在公共场合崩溃过。


过去完成时:
She had already come unglued before he even started to speak. = 他还没开始说话，她就先崩溃了。
The situation had come unglued long before anyone noticed. =  情况早就失控了，很久之后才有人注意到。


将来时:
I think he'll come unglued if he doesn't get the promotion. = 我认为如果他得不到晋升，他会崩溃的。
If you keep pushing her, she'll come unglued. = 如果你继续逼她，她会崩溃的。


虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I think I'd come unglued. = 如果我是她，我想我会崩溃的。
I wish I hadn't come unglued in front of everyone. = 我真希望我没有在大家面前崩溃。


被动语态:  (This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies active emotional breakdown.)


疑问句:
Did he come unglued during the presentation? = 他在演示期间崩溃了吗？
Will she come unglued when she hears the truth? = 她听到真相后会崩溃吗？


否定句:
He didn't come unglued, even though he was very upset. = 尽管他非常沮丧，但他并没有崩溃。
She remained calm and didn't come unglued. = 她保持冷静，没有崩溃。
