replaceable:/rɪˈpleɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可替换的，可更换的 | Able to be replaced; capable of being substituted.  This describes something that can be easily exchanged for another, similar item without significant loss of function or value. | Example: The broken part is replaceable; we can easily order a new one.  这个损坏的部件是可以更换的；我们可以很容易地订购一个新的。 The company uses replaceable components to simplify repairs. 公司使用可替换部件来简化维修。|近义词：substitutable, interchangeable, exchangeable |反义词：irreplaceable, essential, indispensable
