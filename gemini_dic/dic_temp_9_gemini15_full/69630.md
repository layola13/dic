feminists:/ˈfɛmənɪsts/| n. |女权主义者|Advocates for the rights and equality of women.  Feminists believe in the social, political, and economic equality of the sexes. They work to challenge gender stereotypes and discrimination. |例句：Many feminists are fighting for equal pay for equal work. 许多女权主义者正在争取同工同酬。

例句：The feminist movement has achieved significant progress in recent decades. 女权运动在近几十年取得了重大进展。


近义词：women's rights advocates, gender equality advocates |反义词：sexists, misogynists |词性:n.
