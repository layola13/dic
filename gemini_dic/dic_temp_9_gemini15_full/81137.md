grampus:/ˈɡræmpəs/| n. |海豚；杀人鲸|A grampus is a type of toothed whale, specifically referring to either the Risso's dolphin or, less commonly, the killer whale.  It is often used interchangeably with these names, though technically it's a broader term.|例句：The grampus breached the surface, showing its powerful fluke. 这头海豚跃出水面，露出它强壮的尾鳍。
The fisherman spotted a pod of grampus hunting near the coast.  渔民在海岸附近发现一群海豚在捕猎。|近义词：Risso's dolphin, killer whale, dolphin|反义词: N/A|名词
