provers:/ˈpruːvərz/ | n. |证明者；验证者 | A prover is a person or thing that proves something.  It can refer to someone who demonstrates the truth of a statement or a system or process used for verification. | Example: The scientists were the provers of the theory.  科学家们是这个理论的证明者。 |近义词：verifier, demonstrator, validator |反义词：disprover
