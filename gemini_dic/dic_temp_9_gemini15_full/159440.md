polemically:/pəˈlɛmɪkli/| adv.|论战地；好争论地；辩论性地 | In a manner involving or characteristic of polemic.  Characterized by controversy or argument; contentious. |例句：He wrote polemically against the government's policies. 他激烈地批评政府的政策。


例句：The article presented a polemical argument against the proposed legislation. 这篇文章提出了反对拟议立法的论战性论点。


近义词：controversially, argumentatively, contentiously


反义词：harmoniously, conciliatorily, amiably


词源：From polemic (n.) + -ally.  Polemic itself comes from Late Latin *polemicus, from Greek polemikos "warlike," from polemos "war."


用法说明：

polemically用作副词，修饰动词，形容词或整个句子，表示以论战的方式，带有争议或争论的意味。  它通常用于描述带有强烈意见或批判性的写作或言论。


例句：

He spoke polemically, eager to refute his opponent's claims. 他激烈地发言，渴望驳斥对手的主张。

The issue was discussed polemically in the media, with opposing viewpoints clashing.  这个问题在媒体上被辩论性地讨论，相互对立的观点相互冲突。
