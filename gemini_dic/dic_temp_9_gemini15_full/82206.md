grot:/ɡrɒt/| n.|肮脏的小洞穴；阴暗潮湿的地方 |A grot is a small, dirty cave or a dark, damp place.  It often implies something unpleasant or unattractive.  |例句：The children discovered a hidden grot behind the waterfall. 孩子们在瀑布后面发现了一个隐藏的岩洞。

The old cellar was a damp grot, full of cobwebs and discarded furniture.  那个旧地窖是一个潮湿肮脏的小洞穴，里面全是蜘蛛网和丢弃的家具。|近义词：cave, cavern, hole, den|反义词：palace, mansion, clean place
