longtime:/ˈlɒŋtaɪm/| adj.| 长期；老朋友 |  A longtime friend; a longtime resident.  长期朋友；长期居民。|  long + time | longtime friend, longtime supporter, longtime goal, longtime resident, longtime lover | short-term, recent, new | adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a longtime friend of mine. = 他是我的老朋友。
They are longtime supporters of the charity. = 他们是这个慈善机构的长期支持者。


一般过去时:
She was a longtime employee of the company. = 她曾是这家公司的长期雇员。
He was a longtime resident of the town. = 他曾是这个镇子的长期居民。


现在完成时:
We have been longtime friends since college. = 我们从大学时代就是老朋友了。
They have been longtime members of the club. = 他们一直是这个俱乐部的长期会员。


过去完成时:
She had been a longtime advocate for women's rights before her retirement. = 在退休之前，她一直是妇女权利的长期倡导者。
They had been longtime rivals before they finally collaborated. = 在他们最终合作之前，他们一直是长期的竞争对手。


将来时:
I will be a longtime customer of this shop. = 我将成为这家店的长期顾客。
They will be longtime partners in this venture. = 他们将成为这项事业的长期合作伙伴。


虚拟语气:
If I had a longtime friend, I would feel much better. = 如果我有一个老朋友，我会感觉好得多。
If she were a longtime employee, she would know better. = 如果她是长期雇员，她会更了解情况。


被动语态:
He was considered a longtime friend by everyone. = 他被大家认为是老朋友。
She was described as a longtime advocate for the cause. = 她被描述为该事业的长期倡导者。


疑问句:
Are you a longtime fan of this band? = 你是这个乐队的长期粉丝吗？
Has he been a longtime resident of this city? = 他是这个城市的长期居民吗？


否定句:
He is not a longtime friend, I just met him last week. = 他不是老朋友，我上周才认识他。
She was not a longtime supporter of the party. = 她并非该党的长期支持者。



PHRASE->
