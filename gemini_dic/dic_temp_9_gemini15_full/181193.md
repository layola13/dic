reinjures:/ˌriːˈɪndʒərz/| vt.|再次受伤；复发|To injure again; to cause an injury to occur again after a previous injury.  |例句：He reinjured his knee during the game. 他在比赛中再次伤到了膝盖。 |例句：The old back injury reinjured itself when she lifted the heavy box.  她搬动重箱子时，旧的背伤复发了。|近义词：re-injure, injure again, aggravate|反义词：heal, cure

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinjures his ankle frequently. = 他经常扭伤脚踝。
She reinjures her back lifting heavy objects. = 她搬重物时会再次伤到背部。

一般过去时:
The athlete reinjured his hamstring during the race. = 运动员在比赛中再次拉伤了腿筋。
She reinjured her wrist falling down the stairs. = 她从楼梯上摔下来，再次摔伤了手腕。

现在完成时:
He has reinjured his shoulder multiple times. = 他多次肩膀再次受伤。
She has reinjured her knee and is now undergoing physiotherapy. = 她膝盖再次受伤，现在正在接受物理治疗。

过去完成时:
He had reinjured his knee before the final game. = 在决赛之前，他再次伤到了膝盖。
She had reinjured her ankle, which prevented her from participating in the marathon. = 她脚踝再次受伤，这使她无法参加马拉松比赛。

将来时:
He will reinjure his back if he continues lifting weights improperly. = 如果他继续不正确地举重，他会再次伤到背部。
She fears she will reinjure her leg during the upcoming competition. = 她担心在即将到来的比赛中会再次伤到腿。

虚拟语气:
If he had warmed up properly, he wouldn't have reinjured his hamstring. = 如果他充分热身，就不会再次拉伤腿筋了。
I wish I hadn't reinjured my ankle; now I can't go hiking. = 我真希望我没有再次扭伤脚踝；现在我不能去远足了。

被动语态:

疑问句:
Did he reinjure his knee? = 他的膝盖再次受伤了吗？

否定句:
He didn't reinjure his shoulder during the match. = 他在比赛中没有再次伤到肩膀。
She hasn't reinjured her ankle since the last incident. = 自从上次事故以来，她没有再次扭伤脚踝。
