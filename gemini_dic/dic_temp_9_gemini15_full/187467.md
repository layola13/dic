roll-fed: /ˈroʊlˌfed/ | adj. | 卷筒送料的，卷纸送料的 |  Describes a system or machine where materials, typically paper or film, are fed from a roll.  This contrasts with sheet-fed systems, where individual sheets are used. | Example: The printer uses a roll-fed system for continuous printing.  卷筒式打印机用于连续打印。 The high-speed roll-fed packaging machine increased production efficiency.高速卷筒式包装机提高了生产效率。|近义词：roll-to-roll, reel-fed | 反义词：sheet-fed
