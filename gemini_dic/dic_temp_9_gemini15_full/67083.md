exploitatively: /ɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪvli/ | adv. | 剥削性地，利用性地 | In a manner that takes unfair advantage of someone or something.  |例句：The company was accused of treating its workers exploitatively.  这家公司被指控以剥削的方式对待其工人。

例句：The resources were exploitatively used, leading to environmental damage. 资源被过度利用，导致环境破坏。


近义词：predatoryly, unfairly, unjustly, oppressively | 反义词：fairly, justly, equitably


词源:  Derived from the verb "exploit," ultimately from Old French *exploiter (to achieve, accomplish, utilize), from Latin *ex- ("out") + *plorāre ("to weep, bewail"). The modern sense of "taking unfair advantage" developed later.  The suffix "-atively" forms an adverb, indicating manner.
