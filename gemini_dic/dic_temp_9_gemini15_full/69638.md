feminology:/ˌfɛmɪˈnɒlədʒi/| n. |女性学，女性研究|The study of women and their experiences, often encompassing social, cultural, and historical perspectives.  It examines gender roles, power dynamics, and societal influences on women's lives.  It's a multidisciplinary field drawing from sociology, history, psychology, and other disciplines.|Example:  Her research focused on feminology, specifically the impact of patriarchal structures on women's career advancement.  她的研究集中在女性学上，特别是父权结构对女性职业发展的影响。|近义词：women's studies, gender studies|反义词：andrology (the study of men)|名词
