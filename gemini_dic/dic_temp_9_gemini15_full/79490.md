globalized:/ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzd/| adj. |全球化的；全球一体化的|  形容词，指受全球化影响的，或参与全球化进程的。它描述了一种状态，事物或系统在全球范围内相互关联和依赖。This refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through the exchange of goods, services, information, and ideas.  |例句：The globalized economy has created both opportunities and challenges. 全球化经济既带来了机遇，也带来了挑战。

例句：The globalized world is increasingly interconnected through technology.  全球化的世界通过科技日益互联。


近义词：internationalized, worldwide, interconnected

反义词：localized, isolated, regionalized

词性：形容词
