cellarage:/ˈsɛləridʒ/| n.|地窖；酒窖储存量|The space or capacity for storage in a cellar, especially for wine.  It also refers to the act of storing something in a cellar.|例句：The winery boasts impressive cellarage for its aging wines. 这家酒庄拥有令人印象深刻的陈酿葡萄酒酒窖储存量。|例句：The cellarage of the old house was damp and musty. 老房子的地窖潮湿而霉味重。|近义词：cellar, storage, warehousing|反义词：loft, attic
