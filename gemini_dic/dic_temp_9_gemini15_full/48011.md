deciphering:/dɪˈsaɪfərɪŋ/ | v. |破译；解读；解释 | To decipher something means to succeed in understanding, interpreting, or reading it.  This often applies to something that is coded, encrypted, or otherwise difficult to understand. | 例句：The detective spent hours deciphering the cryptic message.  侦探花了数小时破译那条神秘的信息。


例句：She is deciphering the ancient hieroglyphs. 她正在破译古代象形文字。


近义词：decode, decrypt, interpret, unravel, solve


反义词：encrypt, encode


词性：动词
