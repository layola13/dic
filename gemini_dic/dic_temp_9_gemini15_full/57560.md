dramatics: /drəˈmætɪks/ | n. |戏剧；戏剧性；夸张的行为 |  The art or practice of dramatic writing or performance;  the quality of being dramatic.  It can refer to theatrical performances, the art of dramatic writing, or exaggerated behavior.


例句：

The school's dramatics club put on a wonderful production of Hamlet. 学校的戏剧社团上演了一场精彩的哈姆雷特。

Her dramatic exit from the room left everyone speechless. 她戏剧性地离开房间，让每个人都哑口无言。


近义词：theater,  thespianism, theatricality, melodrama

反义词：reality,  mundanity,  unremarkableness


词源:  From Middle French *dramatigue, from Late Latin dramaticus, from Greek dramatigós, from drama "action, deed," from dran "to do, act."
