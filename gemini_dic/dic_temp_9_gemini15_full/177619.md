rebukeful: /rɪˈbjuːkfəl/ | adj. | 责备的；斥责的；严厉批评的 | Expressing or characterized by rebuke; showing strong disapproval.  | The rebukeful tone of his voice made it clear he was displeased.  他的声音带着责备的语气，很明显他不高兴。| 近义词：reproachful, censorious, chiding, scolding | 反义词：approving, complimentary, praising
