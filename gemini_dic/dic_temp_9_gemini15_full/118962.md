meterage:/ˈmiːtərɪdʒ/| n. |米数；容积；计量|The measurement of something, especially length or area in meters.  It can also refer to the amount of something measured in meters, such as the meterage of cloth or the meterage of gas used.|例句：The meterage of the fabric was insufficient for the project. 这块布料的米数不足以完成这个项目。

The gas meter recorded a high meterage this month.  这个月的燃气表显示用气量很大。|近义词：measurement, quantity, amount|反义词：N/A
