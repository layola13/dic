railmen:/ˈreɪlmən/| n. |铁路工人，铁路职员|Railway workers, especially those involved in the operation and maintenance of trains.  The term often evokes a sense of camaraderie and shared experience among those who work on the railways.|例句：The railmen worked tirelessly to clear the tracks after the storm. 暴风雨过后，铁路工人夜以继日地清理轨道。|例句：Many railmen are proud of their contribution to the efficient running of the railway system.许多铁路工人为他们对铁路系统高效运行的贡献感到自豪。|近义词：railway workers, railroad workers, trainmen|反义词：passengers, commuters
