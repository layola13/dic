canine:/ˈkeɪnaɪn/| n. adj.|犬科动物；犬齿；犬的；|A canine is a member of the Canidae family, which includes dogs, wolves, foxes, and jackals.  The term can also refer to a pointed tooth. As an adjective, it describes things related to dogs or canids.|例句：Dogs are canines.  狗是犬科动物。

The canine tooth is sharp. 犬齿很锋利。|近义词：dog, wolf, fox (as nouns); doglike, canine-like (as adjectives)|反义词：feline (cat-like)
