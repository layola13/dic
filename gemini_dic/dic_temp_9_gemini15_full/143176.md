outclimbing: /ˌaʊtˈklaɪmɪŋ/ | v. |超越攀登；胜过攀登 | Climbing higher or better than.  The act of surpassing someone in climbing. |例句：He was outclimbing all the other climbers on the mountain. 他在攀登这座山上胜过所有其他登山者。

例句：

The experienced mountaineer outclimbed the novice with ease.  经验丰富的登山者轻松地超越了新手。


近义词：surpassing, excelling, outdoing


反义词：underclimbing (非标准词汇，表示攀登得比别人差)


词性：动词
