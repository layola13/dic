overapprehensiveness:/ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. |过度焦虑；过分担忧 | Excessive anxiety or worry; an inclination to be overly apprehensive.  It often manifests as anticipating potential threats or negative outcomes disproportionately to their actual likelihood. |例句：Her overapprehensiveness about her child's safety led her to be overly protective.  她的孩子安全问题让她过度保护。

例句：His overapprehensiveness prevented him from taking risks and achieving his full potential. 他的过度焦虑使他不敢冒险，无法充分发挥自己的潜力。


近义词：anxiety, worry, nervousness, apprehension, fearfulness, solicitude

反义词：calmness, composure, equanimity, nonchalance, unconcern
