instauration:/ˌɪnstɔːˈreɪʃən/ | n. |复兴；重建；确立；恢复|The act of establishing something again or for the first time; the process of restoring something to its former state.  It often implies the restoration of a system, institution, or practice that was previously lost or suppressed. |例句：The instauration of a new political system after the revolution was a long and difficult process.  革命后新政治制度的建立是一个漫长而艰难的过程。

The instauration of peace after the war was a priority for the government.  战后恢复和平是政府的当务之急。|近义词：reestablishment, restoration, revival, reinstatement |反义词：destruction, abolition, termination, dismantling |
