redefining:/ˌriːˈdiːfaɪnɪŋ/ | v. |重新定义，改写定义 |The act of redefining something involves changing its meaning, purpose, or boundaries.  It suggests a process of re-evaluation and a shift in understanding.  This can apply to concepts, roles, relationships, or even oneself. |例句：The company is redefining its brand image to attract a younger audience. 公司正在重新定义其品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。

The artist is redefining the boundaries of traditional painting.  这位艺术家正在重新定义传统绘画的界限。|近义词：revise, reinterpret, reshape, redefine, restructure |反义词：maintain, preserve, uphold
