cannulated:/ˈkænjuˌleɪtɪd/| adj.|插管的；装有套管的|Having a cannula inserted.  The term refers to a structure or body part that has a cannula (a small tube) inserted into it for the purpose of administering fluids, drawing fluids, or monitoring pressure. |例句：The patient underwent a cannulated surgery.|近义词：tubed, catheterized|反义词：uncathtered
