menstruated:/ˈmɛnstruˌeɪtɪd/| v.|月经来潮；行经|Experienced menstruation.  The act of having one's menstrual period. |例句：She menstruated regularly until she reached menopause. 她一直定期来月经，直到更年期。

例句：  The doctor asked if she had menstruated recently to determine the cause of her abdominal pain. 医生询问她最近是否来过月经以确定她腹部疼痛的原因。


近义词：had her period, bled (informal)
反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She menstruates regularly. = 她定期来月经。
He menstruates (in a transgender context).  = 他（在变性人的语境下）来月经。


一般过去时:
She menstruated last week. = 她上周来月经了。
I menstruated for the first time at age 12. = 我12岁时第一次来月经。


现在完成时:
She has menstruated since she was 13. = 她从13岁起就来月经了。
I haven't menstruated this month. = 这个月我没来月经。


过去完成时:
She had menstruated before she went on the trip. = 在她旅行之前，她来过月经。
They had menstruated for several years before seeking medical attention. = 在寻求医疗帮助之前，她们已经来月经好几年了。


将来时:
She will menstruate next week, as usual. = 她下周会像往常一样来月经。
I expect to menstruate soon. = 我预计很快就会来月经。


虚拟语气:
If she menstruated regularly, she wouldn't have these symptoms. = 如果她定期来月经，她就不会有这些症状。
I wish I didn't menstruate so heavily. = 我希望我的月经量不要这么大。


被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively)  It is rarely stated that someone *was menstruated*.


疑问句:
Did she menstruate last month? = 她上个月来月经了吗？
Has she ever menstruated? = 她来过月经吗？


否定句:
She didn't menstruate this month. = 她这个月没来月经。
She hasn't menstruated for several months. = 她好几个月没来月经了。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "menstruated".  The verb is generally used straightforwardly to describe the physiological process.

词源:
From Middle English *menstruacioun*, from Old French *menstruacion*, from Latin *menstruāre* "to menstruate," from *menstruus* "monthly," from *mensis* "month."
