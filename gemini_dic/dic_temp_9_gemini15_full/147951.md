overreprimanding: /ˌoʊvərˈrɛprɪmændɪŋ/ | v. | 过度训斥；严厉责备 |  The act of reprimanding someone excessively or unfairly. It implies that the punishment or criticism is too harsh, too frequent, or disproportionate to the offense. | 例句：His overreprimanding of the children left them feeling scared and resentful.  他的过度训斥让孩子们感到害怕和怨恨。

The constant overreprimanding of employees led to high turnover.  不断地过度训斥员工导致高离职率。| 近义词：over-scolding, excessively criticizing, unduly chastising | 反义词：under-correcting, leniently reprimanding |  词性：动词

He overreprimands his employees frequently. = 他经常过度训斥他的员工。
They overreprimand their children for minor mistakes. = 他们因为一些小错误而过度训斥他们的孩子。
