eludes:/ɪˈluːdz/ | vt. | 逃避；躲避；使困惑 | To escape from someone or something, or to avoid being caught or noticed.  It also means to be difficult to understand or remember. |例句：The criminal eluded the police for weeks.  罪犯数周之久躲避了警方。

The solution to the problem eludes me.  问题的解决方法让我困惑。

近义词：evade, avoid, escape, baffle, mystify | 反义词：be caught, be found, be understood, be clear

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thief eludes the police. = 小偷躲避警方。
The meaning of the poem eludes many readers. = 这首诗的意思让许多读者困惑。


一般过去时:
The suspect eluded capture. = 嫌疑犯逃脱了抓捕。
The answer eluded me for hours. = 我几个小时都想不出答案。


现在完成时:
The murderer has eluded justice for years. = 凶手多年来一直逍遥法外。
The significance of the event has eluded historians. = 事件的意义一直困扰着历史学家。


过去完成时:
The escaped prisoner had eluded capture for months. = 逃犯已经逃亡数月了。
The true cause of the accident had eluded investigators. = 事故的真正原因一直让调查人员困惑不解。


将来时:
The criminal will likely elude capture for a while longer. = 罪犯很可能会再躲藏一段时间。
The solution to the climate crisis may elude us for decades. = 气候危机的解决方法可能会困扰我们几十年。


虚拟语气:
If only the truth would not elude me. = 但愿真相不会逃离我。
I wish the answer didn't elude me. = 我希望答案不会让我困惑。


被动语态:
The escaped convict was eluded by the police for days. = 逃犯几天来一直躲避警方。
The solution to the puzzle was eluded by many participants. = 许多参与者都未能解开谜题。


疑问句:
Does the meaning of this passage elude you? = 这段话的意思让你困惑吗？
Why does the answer elude us? = 为什么我们找不到答案？


否定句:
The police did not elude the criminal. = 警方没有躲避罪犯。(This sentence implies the opposite: the police caught the criminal.)
The mystery did not elude the detective. = 这个谜团没有难倒侦探。
