outthrows: /aʊtθroʊz/ | vt. | 投掷；超过；胜过 | To throw something further than someone else; to surpass or exceed.  |例句：He outthrew all the other competitors in the javelin throw. 他在标枪投掷中超过了所有其他竞争者。

The team outthrew their opponents in passing yards. 这个队的传球码数超过了他们的对手。| 近义词：surpass, exceed, outdistance | 反义词：underthrow, fail  | 动词
