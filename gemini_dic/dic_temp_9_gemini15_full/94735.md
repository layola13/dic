improved:/ɪmˈpruːvd/ | adj. |改进的；提高的；改善的 | Improved means having been made better.  It describes something that is now better in quality, effectiveness, or condition than it was before. |  例句：The improved design resulted in a more efficient product. 改进的设计导致了更高效的产品。

例句：Her improved performance earned her a promotion.  她表现有所提高，获得了晋升。


近义词：better, enhanced, ameliorated, refined

反义词：worsened, deteriorated, declined


词源:  The word "improved" is derived from the verb "improve," which comes from the Old French word "améliorer," ultimately from the Latin "meliorare," meaning "to make better."
