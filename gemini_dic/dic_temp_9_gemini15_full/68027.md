factoring:/ˈfæktərɪŋ/| n. v.| 因素分解；融资；代销|The process of finding the factors of a number or expression.  It can also refer to a financial transaction where a business sells its accounts receivable to a third party at a discount to receive immediate cash.|例句：The factoring of the polynomial x^2 - 4 is (x+2)(x-2).  多项式x^2 - 4的因式分解是(x+2)(x-2)。|例句：The small business used factoring to improve its cash flow.这家小企业利用保理来改善现金流。|近义词：decomposition, analysis, financing|反义词：synthesis, combination|名词，动词
