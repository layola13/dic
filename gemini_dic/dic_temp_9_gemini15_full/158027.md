planters:/ˈplæntərz/| n.|种植者；花盆；种植园主|A person who plants, especially one who manages a plantation.  Also, a container for holding and growing plants.|例句：The planters worked tirelessly in the fields. 种植园工人辛勤地工作在田地里。

例句：She bought some beautiful ceramic planters for her balcony. 她为她的阳台买了一些漂亮的花盆。


近义词：cultivators, growers, gardeners, pot
反义词：harvesters (in the context of planting vs. harvesting)


词性:名词
