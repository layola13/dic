fallacious:/fəˈleɪʃəs/| adj.|谬误的；虚假的；欺骗性的|Based on a mistaken belief; illogical.  Something fallacious is based on a misconception or a false premise, leading to an incorrect conclusion.  It often involves flawed reasoning or deceptive arguments.|

例句：His argument was fallacious because it relied on an unproven assumption.  他的论点是谬误的，因为它依赖于一个未经证实的假设。

例句：The company's fallacious advertising claims led to numerous customer complaints.  该公司虚假的广告宣传导致了大量的客户投诉。


近义词：erroneous, false, misleading, specious, deceptive

反义词：sound, valid, true, accurate, correct

词性: adjective
