meetinger:/ˈmiːtɪŋər/| n. |开会者，参加会议的人|A person who attends meetings frequently or habitually.  The term is informal and somewhat uncommon.  It emphasizes the act of attending meetings. |He's a real meetinger; he's at every committee meeting.|近义词：attendee, participant|反义词：absentee
