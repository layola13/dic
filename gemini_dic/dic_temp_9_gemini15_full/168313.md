pro-Paraguay: /ˌproʊ ˈpærəɡwaɪ/ | adj. | 亲巴拉圭的；支持巴拉圭的 |  Supporting or favoring Paraguay.  This term signifies advocacy for or alignment with the interests of Paraguay.  It's used to describe individuals, policies, or organizations that are supportive of Paraguay's government, people, or causes. | 例句：The pro-Paraguay lobby in Congress successfully advocated for increased aid.  国会中的亲巴拉圭游说团体成功地争取到了更多的援助。


The pro-Paraguay media outlets consistently promote the nation's positive attributes. 亲巴拉圭的媒体不断宣传该国的积极方面。| 近义词：pro-Paraguayan,  Paraguayan-supporting | 反义词：anti-Paraguay, anti-Paraguayan |  adj.
