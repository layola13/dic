mestizos:/mesˈtiːzos/| n.|混血儿（尤指欧洲人和美洲原住民的混血儿）|A mestizo is a person of mixed European and Indigenous American ancestry.  The term is most commonly used in Latin America.  It historically carried social and political connotations, often reflecting a complex hierarchy of racial classifications.|The population of Mexico is largely mestizo.|近义词：mixed-race, hybrid|反义词：purebred
