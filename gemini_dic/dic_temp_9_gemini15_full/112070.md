longhouse:/ˈlɒŋhaʊs/| n. |长屋|A long, narrow house, typically one built of wood and used traditionally by various indigenous peoples, often communal.|例句：The Iroquois longhouse was a symbol of their communal lifestyle.  易洛魁人的长屋是他们集体生活方式的象征。


例句：Many Native American tribes lived in longhouses.  许多美洲原住民部落居住在长屋里。


近义词：communal dwelling, communal house|反义词：individual dwelling, detached house|词性：名词
