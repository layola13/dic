dandled:/ˈdændld/| vt.| 轻轻摇晃；宠爱；娇惯 | To move gently and rhythmically up and down, especially a baby.  To treat someone with excessive indulgence and affection. |例句：The mother dandled her baby to sleep. 妈妈轻轻地摇晃着婴儿哄他睡觉。

The teacher dandled the student, giving him special treatment.老师对这个学生特别优待，百般呵护。|近义词：rock, soothe, cosset, spoil |反义词：neglect, ignore, discipline
