oppa:/ˈɑːpɑː/ | n. | 哥哥；欧巴 |  A Korean term of endearment used by a female to refer to an older man, often implying a sense of admiration or affection. It can also be used informally among close friends.  | Example:  My oppa is so handsome! 我的欧巴好帅！ | 近义词: brother, older brother (in a formal context) | 反义词:  younger brother, sister (in the context of Korean kinship terms)
