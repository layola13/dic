deactivators:/ˈdiːæktɪveɪtərz/| n.|失活剂；钝化剂；抑制剂|Substances or agents that reduce or eliminate the activity of something, often a chemical or biological process.  They are used in various fields to control or prevent unwanted reactions or effects.|


例句：

Enzyme deactivators are used in food preservation to prevent spoilage.  酶抑制剂用于食品保鲜以防止腐败。

The researchers tested several chemical deactivators to neutralize the toxic substance. 研究人员测试了几种化学钝化剂来中和有毒物质。


近义词：inhibitors, suppressants, neutralizers

反义词：activators, stimulators


词性:名词
