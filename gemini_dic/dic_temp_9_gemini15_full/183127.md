repositioning:/ˌriːˈpɒzɪʃənɪŋ/ | /ˌriːˈpɒzɪʃənɪŋ/ | n. |重新定位，重新布局 |The act of changing the position, image, or marketing strategy of something.  It involves strategically adjusting a product, brand, or company to better appeal to a target audience or market. This can involve changes to the product itself, its branding, messaging, or distribution channels. | 例句：The company's repositioning strategy involved a complete overhaul of its branding and marketing. 公司的重新定位战略涉及对其品牌和营销的全面改革。

The successful repositioning of the product led to a significant increase in sales. 产品的成功重新定位导致销售额大幅增长。


近义词：rebranding, reimagining, restructuring, realignment | 反义词：stagnation, maintaining the status quo | 名词
