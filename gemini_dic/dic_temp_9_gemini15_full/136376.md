nonresponsibly:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒnsɪbli/ | adv. | 不负责任地 |  Acting in a way that shows a lack of care or concern for the consequences of one's actions.  | 例句：He acted nonresponsibly by leaving the children unsupervised. 他不负责任地留下孩子无人照看。

He handled the situation nonresponsibly, leading to further complications. 他处理事情不负责任，导致了进一步的复杂化。| 近义词：irresponsibly, carelessly, negligently | 反义词：responsibly, carefully, diligently |  无


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs or decline like nouns.  Its usage is determined by the verb it modifies.  Here are some example sentences demonstrating its use in different tenses:


一般现在时:
He acts nonresponsibly. = 他行为不负责任。
She consistently behaves nonresponsibly. = 她总是行为不负责任。


一般过去时:
He acted nonresponsibly last night. = 他昨晚行为不负责任。
They handled the project nonresponsibly. = 他们不负责任地处理了这个项目。


现在完成时:
He has acted nonresponsibly many times. = 他多次行为不负责任。
She has consistently behaved nonresponsibly throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中一直表现得非常不负责任。


过去完成时:
He had acted nonresponsibly before the accident. = 事故发生前，他就已经行为不负责任了。
They had consistently behaved nonresponsibly, leading to the company's downfall. = 他们一直行为不负责任，导致了公司的倒闭。


将来时:
He will act nonresponsibly again, I'm sure. = 我肯定他会再次行为不负责任。
They will likely handle the situation nonresponsibly. = 他们很可能会不负责任地处理这种情况。
