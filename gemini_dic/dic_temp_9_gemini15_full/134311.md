noninsistent:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɪstənt/ | adj. | 不坚持的；不固执的 | Not insistent; not persistent.  The adjective "noninsistent" describes someone or something that does not insist or press strongly on a point or course of action. It implies a lack of forceful persuasion or demand. | Example:  He was noninsistent about his request, preferring to let the matter rest. 他对自己的要求并不坚持，宁愿让事情就此结束。  She adopted a noninsistent approach to the negotiation. 她采取了不坚持的态度进行谈判。|近义词：unobtrusive, yielding, compliant, passive |反义词：insistent, persistent, demanding, assertive
