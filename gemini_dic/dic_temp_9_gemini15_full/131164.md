nonbrand:/ˈnɒnˌbrænd/ | adj. |非品牌的，无品牌的|Not associated with a particular brand name; generic.  This refers to products or services that are not marketed under a specific brand name, often cheaper than branded alternatives.|例句：The supermarket sells a range of nonbrand products at lower prices. 这家超市以较低的价格销售一系列非品牌产品。|例句：  He prefers nonbrand clothes as they are often just as good as designer labels but less expensive. 他更喜欢非品牌服装，因为它们通常和名牌一样好，但价格更便宜。|近义词：generic, unbranded, store brand|反义词：branded, name-brand
