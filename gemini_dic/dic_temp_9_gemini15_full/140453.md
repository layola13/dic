old-age: /ˈoʊld eɪdʒ/ | n. adj. |老年；老龄；衰老 | 英文解析:The period of life after middle age, typically considered to begin at around 65 years old.  It can refer to the state of being old or the process of growing old. As an adjective, it describes something related to old age. | 例句：He is facing the challenges of old age. 他正在面对老年带来的挑战。

The old-age pension system needs reform. 老年养老金制度需要改革。


近义词：senility, senescence, aging  | 反义词：youth, infancy, childhood | 词性：noun, adjective
