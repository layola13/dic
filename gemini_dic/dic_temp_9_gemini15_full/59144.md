dynamometric:/ˌdaɪnəmoʊˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. |测力(学)的；测功(率)的 | Relating to or using a dynamometer.  A dynamometer is an instrument for measuring force, torque, or power. | Example: The dynamometric test showed the engine's maximum power output.  测功仪测试显示发动机最大功率输出。|近义词：force-measuring, power-measuring |反义词：None readily available.
