ductor:/ˈdʌktər/ | n. |引导者，导体，传导体 | A person or thing that conducts or leads.  A substance that allows the flow of electricity or heat. |例句：The orchestra conductor led the musicians through the symphony. 乐队指挥带领乐手们演奏了交响乐。  Copper is a good conductor of electricity. 铜是良好的导电体。 |近义词：leader, guide, transmitter |反义词：insulator
