lamenting:/ləˈmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 哀悼；悲叹；抱怨 | Expressing grief or sorrow; bewailing; complaining.  |例句：She spent the evening lamenting her lost youth. 她整晚都在悲叹自己逝去的青春。 |例句：He was lamenting the lack of opportunities. 他在抱怨缺乏机会。 |近义词：mourning, grieving, bewailing, complaining |反义词：celebrating, rejoicing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is lamenting the loss of his job. = 他正在悲叹失去工作。
They are lamenting the poor weather. = 他们在抱怨糟糕的天气。

一般过去时:
She lamented the passing of her grandmother. = 她哀悼祖母的去世。
He lamented his mistakes. = 他后悔自己的错误。

现在完成时:
We have been lamenting the situation for weeks. = 我们已经抱怨这种情况几周了。
She has lamented her fate many times. = 她多次悲叹自己的命运。

过去完成时:
They had lamented the decision before the meeting. = 会议前他们就对这个决定表示过不满。
He had lamented his poor choices for years. = 他多年来一直后悔自己糟糕的选择。

将来时:
She will lament her actions later. = 她以后会后悔自己的行为。
They will be lamenting their decision soon enough. = 他们很快就会后悔自己的决定。

虚拟语气:
I would lament the situation if I were in their shoes. = 如果我站在他们的立场上，我也会感到惋惜。
She wished she hadn't lamented so much. = 她希望自己不要抱怨这么多。

被动语态:
The lost opportunity was lamented by many. = 许多人都为失去的机会感到惋惜。
His failures were lamented by his family. = 他的失败让家人感到悲痛。

疑问句:
Are you lamenting the current state of affairs? = 你在为目前的情况感到惋惜吗？
Was he lamenting the outcome of the game? = 他在为比赛的结果感到惋惜吗？

否定句:
He did not lament the results. = 他对结果并不感到惋惜。
She didn't lament her decision. = 她并不后悔自己的决定。


PHRASE->
lament over = 对…表示悲痛
lament the loss of = 为…的损失而悲伤
lament for = 为…哀悼
lament about = 抱怨…
lament the fact that = 悲叹…这一事实
