do honor to: /duː ˈɒnə(r) tuː/ | vt. | 给…以荣誉；对…表示敬意；使…增光 | To show respect or admiration for someone or something; to bring honor or credit to someone or something. | 例句：His achievements do honor to his family. 他的成就为他的家族增光。


His bravery did honor to the uniform he wore.  他的勇敢为他的军装增光。


近义词：glorify, exalt, celebrate, dignify | 反义词：disgrace, dishonor, shame, insult | 词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The awards ceremony does honor to the best athletes. =颁奖典礼是对最佳运动员的致敬。
This achievement does honor to our team's hard work. =这项成就为我们团队的辛勤工作增光。


一般过去时:
The soldiers did honor to their country by fighting bravely. =士兵们英勇作战，为国争光。
His speech did honor to the memory of his late father. =他的演讲是对已故父亲的缅怀。


现在完成时:
Her actions have done honor to her profession. =她的行为为她的职业增光。
The museum has done honor to the artist by holding a retrospective exhibition. =博物馆通过举办回顾展来纪念这位艺术家。


过去完成时:
They had done honor to their ancestors by preserving their traditions. =他们通过传承传统来缅怀他们的祖先。
The book had done honor to the historical events it described. =这本书对它所描述的历史事件进行了恰当的展现。


将来时:
The new building will do honor to the city's architectural heritage. =这座新建筑将为城市建筑遗产增光。
The upcoming ceremony will do honor to the recipients' achievements. =即将举行的仪式将是对获奖者成就的表彰。


虚拟语气:
If he had won the prize, it would have done honor to his years of hard work. =如果他获奖了，那将是对多年辛勤工作的肯定。
I wish that my actions would do honor to my family's reputation. =我希望我的行为能够维护家族的声誉。


被动语态:
The athlete was done honor to for his outstanding performance. =这位运动员因其杰出的表现而受到表彰。(虽然语法上可行，但这种被动语态用法较少见)
The project was done honor to by the critics for its innovative design. =该项目因其创新的设计而受到评论家的赞扬。(同样，这种被动语态用法较少见)


疑问句:
Does this action do honor to our values? =这个行为符合我们的价值观吗？(虽然不是直接问“do honor to”，但表达了类似意思)
Will this decision do honor to the company's image? =这个决定会提升公司的形象吗？(同样，不是直接问“do honor to”，但表达了类似意思)


否定句:
His behavior did not do honor to his position. =他的行为有损他的职位。
The scandal did not do honor to the organization's reputation. =这起丑闻损害了该组织的声誉。


PHRASE->
do honor to sb/sth = 向某人/某事表示敬意
do honor to the occasion = 符合场合
do honor to one's family name = 光宗耀祖
do honor to a guest = 隆重款待客人
bring honor to = 为...带来荣誉
