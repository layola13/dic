hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɒtɪk/ | adj. |催眠的，催眠似的；引人入胜的 | relating to or inducing hypnosis; tending to fascinate or hypnotize. | The hypnotic rhythm of the waves lulled her to sleep. 海浪催眠般的节奏使她入睡了。  | mesmerizing, captivating, enchanting |  unhypnotic | 形容词

This painting is hypnotic. = 这幅画很迷人。
