nonrecipient:/ˌnɒnˈrɪsɪpɪənt/| n. |非收件人，未收到的人|A person or entity that does not receive something, typically mail or a message.|The package was returned to sender because the nonrecipient had moved.|近义词：unintended recipient, non-receiver|反义词：recipient


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  The usage focuses on its role in a sentence.

Example 1:
The nonrecipient of the email was unaware of the meeting.  = 这封邮件的未收件人不知道会议的事。

Example 2:
The company apologized to the nonrecipient for the error in address. = 公司就地址错误向未收件人道歉。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "nonrecipient".  The word is used straightforwardly in sentences.
