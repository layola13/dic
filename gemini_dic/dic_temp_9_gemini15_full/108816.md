leave-off:/liːvˈɒf/| /livˈɔːf/| vt.  |停止；中断；留下；遗漏|To stop doing something; to discontinue; to leave something unfinished or unsaid; to omit. |例句：They left off work early. 他们提前下班了。|例句：He left off the last chapter of the book. 他遗漏了书的最后一章。|近义词：stop, cease, discontinue, omit,  terminate|反义词：continue, resume, begin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They leave off work at 5 pm every day. = 他们每天下午5点下班。
We leave off the meeting until tomorrow. = 我们把会议推迟到明天。


一般过去时:
The rain left off at noon. = 中午雨停了。
They left off the discussion abruptly. = 他们突然中断了讨论。


现在完成时:
The builders have left off work for the day. = 建筑工人今天已经收工了。
We have left off several details in this report. = 我们在这个报告中遗漏了一些细节。


过去完成时:
They had left off the important part of the project. = 他们已经遗漏了项目的重要部分。
The rain had left off before we reached home. = 我们到家之前雨已经停了。


将来时:
They will leave off the construction during the holidays. = 节日期间他们将停止施工。
We will leave off the argument until we both calm down. = 我们等双方都冷静下来再继续争论。


虚拟语气:
If they had left off arguing, they would have finished the task on time. = 如果他们当时停止争吵，他们就能按时完成任务。
I wish they had left off making so much noise. = 我真希望他们当时别那么吵。


被动语态:
Work was left off early due to the storm. = 由于暴风雨，工作提前结束了。
The last part of the presentation was left off due to time constraints. = 由于时间限制，演示的最后一部分被省略了。


疑问句:
When did you leave off writing your novel? = 你什么时候停止写作你的小说的？
What part of the plan did they leave off? = 他们遗漏了计划的哪一部分？


否定句:
They didn't leave off working until late at night. = 他们直到深夜才停止工作。
We didn't leave off any important information in the email. = 我们没有在邮件中遗漏任何重要信息。


PHRASE->
leave off work = 收工
leave off doing something = 停止做某事
leave something off = 遗漏某物
leave off where you left off = 从上次中断的地方继续
leave off a chapter = 删掉一章
