nondeductibles:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈdʌktɪblz/ | n. |不可扣除项目 | Expenses or losses that cannot be deducted from taxable income.  These are costs that aren't allowed as tax write-offs.  |Example:  Health insurance premiums are often nondeductibles for tax purposes in some countries.  健康保险费在一些国家通常是不可用于税收抵扣的。  |近义词：unallowable deductions, non-deductible expenses |反义词：deductibles
