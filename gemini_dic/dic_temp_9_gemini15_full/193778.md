self-deflations:/ˌsɛlfˌdiˈfleɪʃənz/| n.|自我贬低；自我通货紧缩|The act of diminishing one's own importance or accomplishments, often to an excessive degree.  It can also refer to an economic phenomenon where a country intentionally reduces its money supply to combat inflation.|例句：His constant self-deflations made it hard for others to believe in his abilities. 他不断自我贬低，使别人很难相信他的能力。

例句：The government implemented a series of self-deflations to curb runaway inflation. 政府实施了一系列自我通货紧缩政策来抑制失控的通货膨胀。|近义词：self-deprecation, downplaying, belittling (oneself)|反义词：self-aggrandizement, boasting, self-promotion|词性:名词
