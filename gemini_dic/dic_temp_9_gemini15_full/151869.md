passive:/ˈpæsɪv/| adj.|被动的；消极的；被动语态的|Relating to or denoting a grammatical voice used to indicate that the grammatical subject of a verb is the recipient (rather than the agent) of the action.  It often involves the use of a form of the verb "to be" plus the past participle.|例句：The ball was thrown by John.  球是被约翰扔的。The problem is being addressed. 这个问题正在被解决。|近义词：inactive, passive voice, receptive|反义词：active, aggressive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car is washed every week. = 这辆车每周都洗一次。
The letter is written in English. = 这封信是用英语写的。

一般过去时:
The house was built last year. = 这房子是去年建的。
The cake was eaten quickly. = 蛋糕很快就被吃掉了。


现在完成时:
The project has been completed. = 项目已经完成了。
The book has been read by many people. = 这本书已经被很多人读过了。


过去完成时:
The work had been finished before he arrived. = 他到达之前工作已经完成了。
The decision had been made before the meeting. = 会议之前决定已经做出了。


将来时:
The task will be completed tomorrow. = 任务将在明天完成。
The report will be submitted next week. = 报告将在下周提交。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were implemented, the results would be amazing. = 如果计划实施，结果将会令人惊叹。
It is important that the message be delivered promptly. = 及时传递信息很重要。


被动语态:
The window was broken. = 窗户被打碎了。
The thief was caught by the police. = 小偷被警察抓住了。


疑问句:
Was the letter sent? = 信寄出去了吗？
Is the work being done correctly? = 工作做得正确吗？


否定句:
The door wasn't locked. = 门没有锁。
The message wasn't received. = 信息没有收到。
