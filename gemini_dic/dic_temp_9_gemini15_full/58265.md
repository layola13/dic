drumstick:/ˈdrʌmstɪk/ | n. |鸡腿；鼓槌 | The drumstick is the fleshy lower leg of a bird, typically a chicken or turkey, eaten as food.  It is also the stick used to strike a drum. | 例句：I prefer the drumstick to the breast. = 我喜欢鸡腿胜过鸡胸肉。

The drummer hit the drums with his drumsticks. = 鼓手用鼓槌敲击鼓。|近义词：leg (of poultry), drumsticks, stick |反义词：无
