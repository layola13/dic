debtor:/ˈdɛtər/| n. |债务人，欠债者 |A debtor is a person or entity that owes money to another person or entity.  A debtor is legally obligated to repay the debt according to the terms of the agreement. |例句：The debtor failed to make the payments on time.  这个债务人没有按时付款。|例句：  He is a debtor to the bank for a large sum of money. 他欠银行一大笔钱。|近义词：borrower, obligor |反义词：creditor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The debtor pays back the loan regularly. = 债务人定期偿还贷款。
He is a debtor and owes money to several people. = 他是个债务人，欠很多人钱。

一般过去时:
The debtor defaulted on his loan last year. = 债务人去年无力偿还贷款。
She was a debtor in her youth, struggling to pay off her student loans. = 她年轻时是个债务人，努力偿还学生贷款。

现在完成时:
The debtor has finally paid off all his debts. = 债务人终于还清了所有债务。
He has been a debtor for many years, but now he is finally debt-free. = 他多年来一直是债务人，但现在他终于摆脱了债务。

过去完成时:
The debtor had already accumulated significant debt before he lost his job. = 债务人在失业前就已经积累了巨额债务。
Before the bankruptcy, the debtor had repeatedly failed to meet payment deadlines. = 破产前，债务人一再未能按时付款。

将来时:
The debtor will face legal action if he fails to repay the loan. = 如果债务人无力偿还贷款，将面临法律诉讼。
She will become a debtor if she takes out another loan. = 如果她再次贷款，她将成为债务人。


虚拟语气:
If he were a debtor, he would be more careful with his spending. = 如果他是债务人，他会更小心地花钱。
I wish I hadn't become a debtor. = 我真希望我没有成为债务人。


被动语态:
The debtor was declared bankrupt by the court. = 债务人被法院宣告破产。
The loan was secured by the debtor's property. = 这笔贷款由债务人的财产担保。


疑问句:
Is he a debtor? = 他是债务人吗？
Who is the debtor in this transaction? = 这笔交易中的债务人是谁？


否定句:
He is not a debtor; he is a creditor. = 他不是债务人，他是债权人。
She is not a debtor, because she always pays her bills on time. = 她不是债务人，因为她总是按时付账。


PHRASE->
debtor in possession (DIP) = 拥有财产的债务人
corporate debtor = 公司债务人
individual debtor = 个人债务人
insolvent debtor = 无力偿债的债务人
judgment debtor = 被判决的债务人

词源:
From Old French *debetor, from Latin debitor, from debēre "to owe".
