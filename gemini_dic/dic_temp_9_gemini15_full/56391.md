doled:/doʊld/| vt.|施舍；少量地给予；|to give out something, especially money or food, in small amounts|The government doled out emergency aid to the flood victims. 政府向洪灾受害者分发了紧急援助。|The charity doled out food to the homeless.  慈善机构向无家可归者分发食物。|give, distribute, allocate|withhold, keep, retain|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doles out advice freely. = 他随意地给予建议。
She doles out small portions of food. = 她少量地分发食物。

一般过去时:
They doled out the money to the needy. = 他们把钱分给了穷人。
The teacher doled out the tests. = 老师分发了试卷。

现在完成时:
The organization has doled out millions of dollars in aid. = 该组织已经分发了数百万美元的援助款。
She has doled out her time generously to charity. = 她慷慨地把时间奉献给了慈善事业。

过去完成时:
They had doled out all the supplies before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经分发了所有的物资。
The manager had doled out the bonuses before leaving for vacation. = 经理在休假前已经发放了奖金。

将来时:
The government will dole out more funds for infrastructure projects. = 政府将拨出更多资金用于基础设施项目。
They will dole out the prizes at the end of the competition. = 比赛结束后他们将颁发奖品。

虚拟语气:
If the company doled out more bonuses, morale would improve. = 如果公司发放更多奖金，士气就会提高。
I wish they would dole out the information more quickly. = 我希望他们能更快地提供信息。

被动语态:
The food was doled out to the refugees. = 食物分发给了难民。

疑问句:
