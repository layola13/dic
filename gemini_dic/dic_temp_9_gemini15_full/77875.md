genetrix:/dʒɪˈnɛtrɪks/| n.| 母系祖先；母亲；始祖母体| A female ancestor; a mother; a female progenitor.  The term often implies a significant or foundational role in lineage or creation.  It can also refer to a female entity that produces or originates something.| Example: In the mythology, the genetrix of the entire race was a powerful goddess.  在这个神话中，整个种族的母系祖先是一位强大的女神。|近义词：mother, ancestor, progenitor, matriarch|反义词：progenitor (in the male sense), offspring, descendant
