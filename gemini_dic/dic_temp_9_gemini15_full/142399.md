orthoxylene:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈzaɪliːn/ | n. |邻二甲苯|Ortho-xylene is a clear, colorless liquid aromatic hydrocarbon. It is one of the three isomers of dimethylbenzene.  It's used primarily in the production of phthalic anhydride, a key intermediate in the manufacture of various plastics and resins. |The chemical formula for orthoxylene is C₈H₁₀. It's often used as a solvent in various industrial processes. |近义词:1,2-dimethylbenzene |反义词:无
