reworking:/ˌriːˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | n. |重新制作；修改；改写 | The act of revising, improving, or changing something.  It often implies a significant alteration or a complete overhaul, rather than just minor edits. |例句：The reworking of the script improved the play considerably.  重新改写剧本大大提高了戏剧的质量。

The reworking of the engine increased its efficiency. 引擎的重新改造提高了它的效率。|近义词：revision, alteration, modification, revamp, overhaul |反义词：completion, finalization


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The team is reworking the budget. = 团队正在修改预算。
He reworks his paintings frequently. = 他经常修改他的画作。


一般过去时:
They reworked the design after receiving feedback. = 他们在收到反馈后修改了设计。
She reworked the essay to improve its clarity. = 她修改了论文以提高其清晰度。


现在完成时:
We have reworked the software several times. = 我们已经多次修改了软件。
They have reworked the contract to reflect the new agreement. = 他们已经修改了合同以反映新的协议。


过去完成时:
They had reworked the plan before presenting it to the board. = 他们在向董事会提交计划之前已经修改了计划。
She had reworked the manuscript several times before submitting it. = 在提交手稿之前，她已经修改过几次了。


将来时:
We will rework the website next month. = 我们下个月将重新设计网站。
He will rework the song before releasing it. = 他将在发行歌曲之前修改歌曲。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rework the presentation. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会修改演示文稿。
I wish I had reworked my application before submitting it. = 我希望我在提交申请之前已经修改过它。


被动语态:
The document is being reworked by the legal team. = 文件正在由法律团队修改。

疑问句:

否定句:
