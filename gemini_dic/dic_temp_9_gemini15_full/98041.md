inosite:/ˈɪnəsaɪt/| n. |肌醇|A group of stereoisomers of cyclohexanehexol, a cyclic alcohol with six hydroxyl groups.  Inositols are found in many plants and animals and have various biological roles. They are often involved in cell signaling and membrane structure.|Example:  Myo-inositol is a common type of inositol found in many foods.|近义词: cyclohexanehexol |反义词: None specifically.
