goblins:/ˈɡɑːblɪnz/ | n. |妖精，小鬼 | mischievous creatures of folklore, often depicted as small, ugly, and mischievous. They are often associated with darkness, caves, and the supernatural.  | Example:  The children told spooky stories about goblins living in the woods. 孩子们讲了关于住在森林里的妖精的可怕故事。

Example:  Many fantasy games feature goblins as antagonists. 许多幻想游戏都以哥布林为反派。


近义词: hobgoblins, imps, sprites, gremlins | 反义词: angels, fairies (depending on the context)


词性:名词
