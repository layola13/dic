printings:/ˈprɪntɪŋz/| n.|印刷品；版次|The plural form of "printing," referring to multiple instances of printed materials, such as books, newspapers, or other documents.  It can also refer to different editions or versions of a single work. |例句：The museum has a collection of rare printings of Shakespeare's plays.  博物馆收藏着莎士比亚戏剧的珍稀版本。

The first edition had several errors, so later printings corrected them.  第一版有一些错误，所以后来的版本都修正了这些错误。|近义词：editions, impressions, issues|反义词：manuscripts (handwritten originals)
