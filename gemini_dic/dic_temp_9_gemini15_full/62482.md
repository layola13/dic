endosperms:/ˈɛndoʊspɜːrmz/| n.|胚乳|The endosperm is the part of a seed that acts as a food store for the developing plant embryo, usually containing starch with protein and other nutrients.|例句：The endosperm of a corn kernel is a significant source of carbohydrates. 玉米粒的胚乳是碳水化合物的显著来源。|The endosperm provides nourishment to the growing seedling until it can photosynthesize on its own.|胚乳为正在生长的幼苗提供营养，直到它能够自己进行光合作用。|近义词：albumen, perisperm|反义词：embryo
