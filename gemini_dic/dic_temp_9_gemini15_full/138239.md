They tend to idealize the past and may actively seek out experiences or objects that evoke memories of former times. |Example: The exhibit was a magnet for nostalgists, drawing in crowds who fondly remembered the era of classic Hollywood.  许多怀旧者被这个展览吸引，成群结队地前来，深情地回忆着经典好莱坞的时代。|近义词：retrophiles, sentimentalists|反义词：futurists, modernists|名词
