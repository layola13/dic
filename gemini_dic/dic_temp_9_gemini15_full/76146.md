fusel:/ˈfjuːzəl/| n.|杂醇油，劣质酒；|A mixture of higher alcohols, especially amyl alcohol, produced during fermentation, often giving alcoholic drinks a harsh taste and smell. Fusel oil is a byproduct of fermentation and distillation. It can be toxic in large quantities.|The whiskey had a strong fusel oil taste.|近义词：higher alcohols, amyl alcohol|反义词：pure alcohol
