recoupments:/rɪˈkuːpmənts/| n. |补偿；收回；赔偿|The act of recouping something, typically money or losses.  It refers to the process of regaining something that has been lost or spent.  |例句：The company is hoping for quick recoupments on their investment. 公司希望投资能迅速获得回报。

例句：After the storm, the insurance company provided recoupments for the damaged property.  暴风雨过后，保险公司对受损财产进行了赔偿。


近义词：recoveries, reimbursements, compensations|反义词：losses, deficits


词性: 名词
