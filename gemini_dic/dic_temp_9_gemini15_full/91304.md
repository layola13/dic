hydrometallurgical:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌmɛtəˈlɜːrdʒɪkəl/ | adj. |湿法冶金的；水冶的 | Relating to or denoting the branch of metallurgy that uses aqueous solutions to extract metals from ores.  |例句：Hydrometallurgical processes are often used to extract copper from low-grade ores. 湿法冶金工艺常用于从低品位矿石中提取铜。

例句：The company is investing heavily in hydrometallurgical technology to improve its metal recovery rates.该公司正在大力投资湿法冶金技术，以提高其金属回收率。


近义词：aqueous metallurgical, solution mining


反义词：pyrometallurgical (火法冶金的)


词性：形容词
