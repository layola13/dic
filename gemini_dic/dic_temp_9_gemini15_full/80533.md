goodish:/ˈɡʊdɪʃ/| adj.|相当好的，尚可的|Fairly good; somewhat good.  It implies a degree of goodness that is not excellent but acceptable or satisfactory. |The weather was goodish, but a bit windy.  天气还不错，但有点风。|The food was goodish, nothing special. 食物还可以，没什么特别的。|good, decent, satisfactory|bad, awful, terrible, poor
