semireactionary:/ˌsɛmiˌriˈækʃənɛri/| adj.|半反动的；部分反动的|Partially or somewhat reactionary; exhibiting some characteristics of a reactionary but not fully embracing the ideology.  The term suggests a less extreme or committed form of reactionism, perhaps holding some progressive views alongside conservative ones.|例句：His views on social issues are considered semireactionary, a blend of traditional values and some modern perspectives. 他的社会问题观点被认为是半反动的，传统价值观和一些现代观点的混合体。


例句：The political party's platform, while generally conservative, contains some semireactionary elements that have drawn criticism.  该政党的纲领虽然总体上是保守的，但也包含一些半反动的因素，受到了批评。


近义词：moderately reactionary, partially conservative, somewhat traditionalist


反义词：progressive, radical, revolutionary


词性:形容词
