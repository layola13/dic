savings: /ˈseɪvɪŋz/ | n. |储蓄；节省；节约 | money that is saved, especially money saved in a bank account.  |例句：He has substantial savings in the bank. 他在银行有大量的存款。

例句：

We need to make savings in our budget. 我们需要节约预算。


近义词：economies, reserves, hoard


反义词：expenditure, spending, waste


词源:  Derived from the verb "save," ultimately from Old English *sæfian, meaning "to preserve, protect."  The noun form developed to signify the act of saving and the resulting accumulation of saved resources.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His savings account earns interest. = 他的储蓄账户赚取利息。
Their savings are growing steadily. = 他们的储蓄稳步增长。

一般过去时:
She made significant savings last year. = 她去年节省了大量的钱。
They used their savings to buy a house. = 他们用积蓄买了一套房子。

现在完成时:
We have saved a considerable amount of money. = 我们已经存了一大笔钱。
They have accumulated substantial savings over the years. = 他们多年来积累了大量的储蓄。

过去完成时:
By the time they retired, they had saved enough for a comfortable life. = 到他们退休的时候，他们已经存够了舒适生活的钱。
He had already used his savings before the emergency. = 在紧急情况发生之前，他已经用掉了积蓄。

将来时:
They will use their savings for their children's education. = 他们将用积蓄支付孩子的教育费用。
We will have enough savings for a down payment on a house next year. = 明年我们将有足够的储蓄支付房屋的首付款。

虚拟语气:
If I had more savings, I would travel the world. = 如果我有更多的积蓄，我就会周游世界。
I wish I had enough savings to buy that car. = 我真希望我有足够的积蓄买那辆车。

被动语态:
The savings were used to renovate the kitchen. = 储蓄被用来翻修厨房。
The interest earned on the savings was reinvested. = 储蓄赚取的利息被再次投资。

疑问句:
How much savings do you have? = 你有多少储蓄？
What are your savings goals? = 你的储蓄目标是什么？

否定句:
He doesn't have any savings. = 他没有任何积蓄。
They didn't have enough savings to cover the expenses. = 他们没有足够的储蓄来支付费用。
