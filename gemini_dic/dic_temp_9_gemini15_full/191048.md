scandalized: /ˈskændəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 愤慨的；震惊的；感到震惊的 |  Feeling or expressing strong disapproval of something considered immoral or shocking.  | 例句：She was scandalized by the politician's behaviour. 她对这位政治家的行为感到震惊。

例句：The public was scandalized by the revelations of corruption. 公众对腐败的揭露感到震惊。


近义词：shocked, appalled, horrified, outraged, disgusted

反义词：unmoved, unimpressed, indifferent
