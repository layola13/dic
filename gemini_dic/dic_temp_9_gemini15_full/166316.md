prereform:/ˌpriːˈrɛfɔːrm/| adj.|改革前的；在改革之前的|Existing or occurring before a reform or period of reforms.  The adjective describes something that existed or happened before a significant change or improvement was made.|例句：Prereform education systems often lacked resources and modern teaching methods. 改革前的教育体系往往缺乏资源和现代化的教学方法。
例句：The prereform economy was characterized by high levels of protectionism. 改革前的经济以高水平的保护主义为特征。|近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding|反义词：postreform, subsequent
