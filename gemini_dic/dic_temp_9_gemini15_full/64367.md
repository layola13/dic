It often refers to a dark, gloomy place or a state of darkness. | Example: The cave was filled with the erebus of deepest night.  这个山洞充满了夜晚最深处的黑暗。 The battle was fought in the erebus of early morning.  战斗发生在黎明前的黑暗中。 |近义词：darkness, gloom, shadow, abyss |反义词：light, brightness
