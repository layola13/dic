cases:/ˈkeɪsɪz/ | n. | 案例；情况；讼案；箱子 |  Cases refer to instances or examples of something, particularly in law or medicine.  It can also mean a container. |

例句：
The doctor reviewed several cases of the rare disease.  医生审查了几例这种罕见疾病的病例。

There are many cases of plagiarism in this assignment. 这项作业中有许多剽窃案例。
