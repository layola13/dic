cited:/ˈsaɪtɪd/| adj. |被引用的，被提及的|Mentioned or referred to as evidence or support, especially in a scholarly work.  |例句：The author cited several studies to support his theory. 作者引用了几项研究来支持他的理论。|近义词：quoted, mentioned, referenced |反义词：ignored, unmentioned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article cites several important studies. = 这篇文章引用了几项重要的研究。
This report cites numerous examples of successful initiatives. = 这份报告引用了许多成功的举措的例子。

一般过去时:
The professor cited a relevant case study during his lecture. = 教授在讲座中引用了一个相关的案例研究。
She cited her previous experience to justify her request. = 她引用了她之前的经验来证明她的请求是合理的。


现在完成时:
They have cited this research extensively in their publications. = 他们已经在他们的出版物中广泛引用了这项研究。
Scientists have cited the new findings as groundbreaking. = 科学家们已经将这一新发现称为具有突破性意义的发现。


过去完成时:
He had cited that article in his earlier work. = 他在他早期的作品中已经引用过那篇文章了。
The lawyer had cited precedent in her closing arguments. = 律师在结案陈词中引用了先例。


将来时:
They will cite this data in their upcoming report. = 他们将在即将发布的报告中引用这些数据。
The researcher will cite his sources meticulously. = 研究人员将一丝不苟地引用他的资料来源。


虚拟语气:
If I were to write that paper, I would cite at least ten sources. = 如果我要写那篇论文，我会至少引用十个资料来源。
I wish I had cited that important article in my thesis. = 我真希望在我的论文中引用过那篇重要的文章。


被动语态:
This study has been cited many times. = 这项研究已经被引用多次了。
The article was cited incorrectly in the review. = 这篇文章在评论中被错误地引用了。


疑问句:
Was this source properly cited? = 这个来源是否被正确引用？
Have all the necessary sources been cited? = 所有必要的来源都已引用了吗？


否定句:
The paper did not cite any primary sources. = 这篇论文没有引用任何主要来源。
He failed to cite the original author. = 他未能引用原作者。



PHRASE->
cited in = 被...引用
frequently cited = 经常被引用
highly cited = 高被引
widely cited = 广泛被引用
correctly cited = 正确引用
