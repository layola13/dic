epicondylar:/ˌɛpɪˈkɒndɪlər/ | adj. |  髁上(的)；肱骨髁上(的) | Relating to an epicondyle, a bony prominence on the distal end of a long bone, such as the humerus.  The term is often used in medical contexts to describe conditions affecting the area near the epicondyles. | Example: Epicondylitis is inflammation of the tendons surrounding the epicondyles of the elbow.  肱骨外上髁炎是肘部髁上肌腱的炎症。  |近义词: supracondylar (sometimes, depending on context) | 反义词: None readily available.
