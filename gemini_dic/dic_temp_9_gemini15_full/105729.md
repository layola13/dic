kiosk:/ˈkiːɑːsk/|/ˈkiːɒsk/| n. |售货亭；报刊亭；信息亭|A small structure, often outdoors, where goods or services are sold.  It can be a temporary or permanent structure. |例句：There's a kiosk selling ice cream near the beach. 海滩附近有个卖冰淇淋的售货亭。|例句：The airport has several kiosks where you can check in for your flight. 机场有几个自助值机亭。|近义词：stand, booth, stall |反义词:large building, store
