knotted:/ˈnɒtɪd/ /ˈnɑːtɪd/ | adj. | 打结的；纠结的；复杂的 |  Describes something that has knots, or is full of complications and difficulties.  | The rope was knotted and difficult to untie.  绳子打结了，很难解开。
The plot of the novel was knotted and confusing. 这部小说的情节复杂而混乱。 | tangled, entwined, complex | straight, simple, untied | 形容词
