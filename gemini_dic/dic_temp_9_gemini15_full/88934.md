holding-on: /ˈhoʊldɪŋ ˈɑːn/ | v. | 坚持；保持；紧紧抓住；依赖 |  The act of maintaining a grip or control;  persisting in a course of action or belief.  |

例句：

He was holding on to the railing for dear life. = 他紧紧抓住栏杆，拼命保命。

She's holding on to the hope that her son will recover. = 她仍然抱有希望，希望儿子能康复。


近义词：clinging, gripping, persevering, persisting

反义词：letting go, releasing, surrendering, giving up

词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is holding on to his beliefs. = 他坚持他的信仰。
They are holding on to the rope tightly. = 他们紧紧抓住绳子。

一般过去时:
She held on to her composure during the crisis. = 她在危机期间保持镇定。
He held on to the branch until help arrived. = 他抓住树枝直到有人来帮忙。

现在完成时:
I have been holding on to this secret for years. = 我保守这个秘密已经好几年了。
They have held on to their property despite the offers. = 尽管有人出价，他们仍然保留着他们的房产。

过去完成时:
He had held on to the hope of reconciliation until the very end. = 直到最后，他还抱有和解的希望。
She had held on to the old photograph for decades. = 她保存这张旧照片几十年了。

将来时:
I will hold on to this information for as long as possible. = 我会尽可能长时间地保留这些信息。
We will hold on to our position until a better offer comes along. = 我们将坚持我们的立场，直到有更好的提议出现。

虚拟语气:
If I held on to my anger, I would regret it. = 如果我继续生气，我会后悔的。
I wish I had held on to that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice, as it implies active clinging or maintaining)
The rope was being held on to by the climber. = 攀登者抓住绳子。 (不太自然)
The tradition was held on to by the community. = 这个传统被社区传承下来。(更自然的表达方式是The community held on to the tradition.)


疑问句:
Are you holding on to anything important? = 你有没有紧紧抓住什么重要的东西？
Will he hold on to his promise? = 他会信守承诺吗？

否定句:
He didn't hold on to the money for long. = 他没有长时间持有这笔钱。
She couldn't hold on to her emotions any longer. = 她再也控制不住自己的情绪了。


PHRASE->

hold on to the memories = 珍惜回忆
