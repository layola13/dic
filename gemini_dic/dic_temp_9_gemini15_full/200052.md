sketch:/skɛtʃ/ |/ˈsketʃ/| n., v.| 草图；素描；梗概；简略；勾画；速写 |

英文解析:A sketch is a quick, rough drawing that does not include many details. It can also be a short piece of writing, a plan, or an outline.  As a verb, it means to make a quick drawing or to plan something briefly.


例句:
He made a quick sketch of the landscape. = 他快速地勾勒了这幅风景画。

She sketched out a plan for the new project. = 她草拟了一个新项目的计划。


近义词:drawing, outline, draft, plan, diagram


反义词:masterpiece, detailed drawing, comprehensive plan


词性:Noun, Verb
