dorsiferous:/dɔːrsɪfərəs/| adj.| 背生的；背着生的 | Bearing fruit or other structures on the back or dorsal surface.  | Example:  Many ferns are dorsiferous, meaning their sporangia develop on the underside of the leaves. 许多蕨类植物是背生的，这意味着它们的孢子囊在叶子的下面发育。 | 近义词：dorsal, back-bearing | 反义词：ventral,  front-bearing
