rurally:/ˈrʊərəli/| adv.|乡村地；农村地；在农村|In a rural manner; in the countryside.|例句：They live rurally, far from any towns or cities.  他们住在农村，远离任何城镇或城市。
例句：The area is predominantly rurally populated. 这个地区主要居住在农村地区。|近义词：countryside, in the country|反义词：urbanly, in the city
