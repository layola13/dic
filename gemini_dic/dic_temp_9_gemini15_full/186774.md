rilles:/ˈrɪlz/| n.|月溪，沟壑|Narrow, sinuous channels or grooves on the surface of a celestial body, especially the Moon.  These features are often associated with volcanic or tectonic activity. |Example: The lunar rilles are fascinating geological formations. 月球上的月溪是令人着迷的地质构造。  The scientists studied the images of the Martian rilles to understand their origin. 科学家们研究了火星月溪的图像以了解它们的起源。|近义词：channels, grooves, furrows|反义词：mountains, highlands
