disestablisher: /ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃər/ | n. |废除者，取消者 | A person who disestablishes something, especially an established church.  Someone who actively works to end the official status or power of an institution, particularly a religious establishment. | The disestablisher of the Church of England would face significant opposition.  废除英国国教的人将会面临巨大的反对。|近义词：abolitionist, reformer |反义词：establisher, supporter
