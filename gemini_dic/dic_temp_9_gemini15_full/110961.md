liquefier:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪər/| n. |液化器；液态器 |A liquefier is a machine or device that converts a substance from a solid or gaseous state into a liquid state.  This is often achieved through processes like cooling, compression, or the application of solvents.|例句：The factory uses a sophisticated liquefier to process natural gas.  这家工厂使用先进的液化器来处理天然气。The liquefier in the lab is used for experiments involving low temperatures. 实验室里的液化器用于低温实验。|近义词：liquefactor|反义词：solidifier
