lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/ | vt. |润滑；使润滑 | To apply a lubricant to (a machine or its parts) to reduce friction.  |例句：We need to lubricate the engine before starting it. 我们需要在启动发动机之前润滑它。|例句：Regularly lubricating your bike chain will extend its lifespan. 定期润滑自行车链条可以延长其使用寿命。|近义词：oil, grease, smooth |反义词：dry, rough


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lubricates his car regularly. = 他定期给他的车润滑。
The mechanic lubricates the engine parts. = 机械师润滑发动机部件。


一般过去时:
I lubricated the hinges yesterday. = 我昨天润滑了铰链。
They lubricated the machine before operation. = 他们在操作前润滑了机器。


现在完成时:
She has lubricated the bicycle chain twice this month. = 这个月她已经润滑了自行车链条两次。
They have lubricated all the moving parts. = 他们已经润滑了所有活动部件。


过去完成时:

The technician had lubricated the equipment before the test. = 技术人员在测试前已经润滑了设备。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
