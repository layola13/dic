interspersal: /ˌɪntərˈspɜːrsəl/ | n. |  穿插；散布；点缀 | The act of interspersing; the state of being interspersed.  It refers to something scattered or placed at intervals among other things. | 例句：The teacher's lecture included the interspersal of jokes to keep the students engaged.  老师的讲座穿插了一些笑话，以保持学生的参与度。


The interspersal of wildflowers among the grass created a beautiful meadow. 野花散布在草丛中，创造了一个美丽的草地。| 近义词：intermingling, mixture, scattering | 反义词：concentration, aggregation, massing |  词性: 名词
