philanthropy: /fɪˈlænθrəpi/ | n. |慈善；慈善事业 | The act of giving money or other resources to help people or organizations in need;  charitable giving.  It encompasses both the donation of resources and the active engagement in charitable activities. | Example: Her philanthropy extended to supporting various educational initiatives.  她的慈善事业延伸到支持各种教育倡议。 |近义词：charity, beneficence, altruism | 反义词：selfishness, greed, avarice |名词
