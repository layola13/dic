inseminated: /ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪtɪd/ | v. | 受精；人工授精 |  To introduce semen into (a female animal or plant) for the purpose of fertilization.  | 例句：The farmer inseminated the cows to ensure a successful breeding season.  这个农民给母牛人工授精以确保繁殖季节成功。


例句：The bee inseminated the flower. 这只蜜蜂给花授粉。


近义词：fertilize, impregnate | 反义词：sterilize
