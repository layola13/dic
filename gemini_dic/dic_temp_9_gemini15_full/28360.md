caravanned:/ˈkærəvænd/ | v. | 旅行；以旅行车队形式旅行 | To travel in a caravan; to form a caravan.  |The family caravanned across the country, stopping at various campsites along the way.  家人驾着房车横穿全国，沿途在不同的营地停留。| traveled, journeyed | stayed put


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They caravan every summer. = 他们每年夏天都进行房车旅行。
We caravan to the coast each year. = 我们每年都驾车去海边。


一般过去时:
They caravanned across the desert last year. = 他们去年横穿沙漠旅行。
We caravanned through several states on our trip. = 我们在旅途中穿越了几个州。


现在完成时:
They have caravanned extensively throughout Europe. = 他们已在欧洲广泛地进行了房车旅行。
We have caravanned across the country three times. = 我们已经三次驾车横穿全国。


过去完成时:
They had caravanned for weeks before reaching their destination. = 他们到达目的地之前已经旅行了好几个星期。
We had caravanned for days before we found a suitable campsite. = 我们找了几天才找到合适的营地。


将来时:
They will caravan to the mountains next month. = 他们下个月将前往山区旅行。
We will caravan across the continent next year. = 我们明年将横穿大陆旅行。


虚拟语气:

I wish we had caravanned to the beach instead of staying home. = 我真希望我们去海边旅行而不是待在家里。
