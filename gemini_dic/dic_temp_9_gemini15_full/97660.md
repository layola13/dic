inhibiters:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtərz/ | n. |抑制剂|Substances that slow down or prevent a process, especially a chemical reaction or biological process.  They interfere with the activity of something. |Example: Enzyme inhibitors are used in medicine to treat various diseases. 酶抑制剂用于治疗各种疾病。

The development of new drug inhibitors is a major focus of pharmaceutical research. 新药抑制剂的研制是制药研究的主要方向。|近义词： suppressants, restrainers, dampeners |反义词：activators, stimulants, promoters
