outpour:/ˈaʊtpɔːr/|/ˈaʊtpɔːr/| |vt. n.|倾泻；大量涌出；倾诉；表达|To pour out; to flow or issue forth abundantly; to express one's feelings freely.|例句：The rain poured out from the dark clouds.  雨水从乌云中倾泻而下。|例句：She poured out her heart to her friend. 她向朋友倾诉衷肠。|近义词：gush, pour, deluge, torrent|反义词：drip, trickle, restrain, suppress|动词
