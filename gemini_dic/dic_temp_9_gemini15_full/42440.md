counterdemonstrates: /ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənstreɪts/ | vt. | 反驳；证明…是错误的 | To demonstrate the falsity or invalidity of (a claim, theory, etc.) by providing contrary evidence or argument.  |  The experiment counterdemonstrated the initial hypothesis.  实验反驳了最初的假设。 | refutes, disproves, contradicts | supports, confirms | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data counterdemonstrates the prevailing theory. = 数据反驳了流行的理论。
He counterdemonstrates his opponent's argument with facts. = 他用事实反驳了对手的论点。


一般过去时:
The scientist counterdemonstrated the previous findings. = 科学家反驳了之前的研究结果。
Her speech counterdemonstrated the claims made by the opposition. = 她的演讲反驳了反对派提出的主张。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have counterdemonstrated the effectiveness of that medication. = 最近的研究已经证明了该药物的无效性。
The new evidence has counterdemonstrated the original assumption. = 新证据反驳了最初的假设。


过去完成时:
They had counterdemonstrated the old paradigm before proposing a new one. = 在提出新的范例之前，他们已经反驳了旧的范例。
Researchers had counterdemonstrated the earlier conclusions through rigorous testing. = 研究人员通过严格的测试反驳了早期的结论。


将来时:
Further research will counterdemonstrate the current beliefs. = 进一步的研究将反驳目前的观点。
The upcoming experiment will likely counterdemonstrate the existing hypothesis. = 即将来临的实验可能会反驳现有的假设。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could counterdemonstrate their conclusion. = 如果我们有更多数据，我们就可以反驳他们的结论。
If the results were different, it would counterdemonstrate the existing model. = 如果结果不同，它将反驳现有的模型。


被动语态:
The theory was counterdemonstrated by subsequent research. = 该理论被随后的研究反驳了。
The initial findings were counterdemonstrated by a more comprehensive study. = 初步研究结果被一项更全面的研究反驳了。


疑问句:
Does this evidence counterdemonstrate the accepted wisdom? = 这证据是否反驳了公认的观点？
Can we counterdemonstrate this claim with concrete examples? = 我们能用具体的例子来反驳这一说法吗？


否定句:
The experiment did not counterdemonstrate the established principle. = 实验并没有反驳既定的原理。
The study failed to counterdemonstrate the hypothesis. = 该研究未能反驳该假设。


PHRASE->
counterdemonstrate the claim = 反驳这一说法
counterdemonstrate the validity = 反驳有效性
counterdemonstrate the argument = 反驳论点
counterdemonstrate the hypothesis = 反驳假设
counterdemonstrate the theory = 反驳理论
