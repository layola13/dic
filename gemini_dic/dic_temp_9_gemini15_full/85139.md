hardliners:/ˈhɑːrdˌlaɪnərz/ | n. |强硬派，强硬分子 |People who refuse to compromise, especially in politics.  They advocate for strict measures and are resistant to change or negotiation. |例句：The hardliners in the government blocked any attempt at compromise. 政府中的强硬派阻止了任何妥协的尝试。

例句：The peace talks broke down because of the hardliners on both sides. 和谈由于双方强硬派而破裂。


近义词：extremists, radicals, reactionaries, die-hards

反义词：moderates, centrists, compromisers, liberals


词性:名词
