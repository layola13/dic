nurses:/ˈnɜːrsɪz/ | n. |护士们，护理人员 |The nurses work tirelessly to care for their patients. 护士们不知疲倦地照顾病人。|近义词：medical staff, caregivers |反义词：patients


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The nurses care for the patients every day. = 护士们每天都照顾病人。
The nurses check the patients' vital signs regularly. = 护士们定期检查病人的生命体征。


一般过去时:
The nurses treated the injured man yesterday. = 护士们昨天治疗了那个受伤的男子。
The nurses administered medication to the patients last night. = 护士们昨晚给病人服药。


现在完成时:
The nurses have cared for hundreds of patients this year. = 今年护士们已经照顾了数百名病人。
The nurses have successfully completed their training program. = 护士们已经成功完成了他们的培训项目。


过去完成时:
The nurses had already prepared the operating room before the surgeon arrived. = 在外科医生到达之前，护士们已经准备好手术室了。
The nurses had administered the medication before the doctor's visit. = 在医生来访之前，护士们已经给病人服药了。


将来时:
The nurses will assist the doctor during the surgery tomorrow. = 护士们明天将在手术中协助医生。
The nurses will be working extra shifts next week due to the high patient load. = 下周由于病人数量多，护士们将加班。


虚拟语气:
If the nurses had more time, they would provide more individualized care. = 如果护士们有更多的时间，他们会提供更个性化的护理。

被动语态:
