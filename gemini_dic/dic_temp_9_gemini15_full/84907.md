hanking:/ˈhæŋkɪŋ/| n.|卷绕；缠绕；(尤指)成捆的纱线或绳索|The act of winding or coiling something, especially yarn or rope into hanks.  It often refers to the resulting bundle. |例句：The fisherman spent the afternoon hanking his nets.  渔夫花了一下午的时间把渔网卷起来。|例句：She bought a hank of wool to knit a sweater. 她买了一卷羊毛来织毛衣。|近义词：coiling, winding, reeling|反义词：unwinding, uncoiling|名词
