piranhas:/pɪˈrɑːnəz/| n.|食人鱼|Piranhas are small, sharp-toothed freshwater fish known for their aggressive feeding habits in schools.  They inhabit rivers and streams in South America.|例句：The piranhas attacked the carcass with ferocious speed. 食人鱼以凶猛的速度袭击了尸体。|例句：A school of piranhas quickly devoured the fallen animal.  一群食人鱼迅速吞噬了倒下的动物。|近义词：fish, carnivorous fish|反义词：herbivore
