knelt:/nelt/| v.|跪下，跪着|To go down on one's knees; to kneel.|例句：She knelt down to pray. 她跪下祈祷。  例句：He knelt beside the injured man. 他跪在受伤的人旁边。|近义词：kneel, bend, stoop|反义词：stand, rise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kneels in church every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都在教堂里跪着。
She kneels to tie her shoelaces. = 她跪下系鞋带。

一般过去时:
He knelt down and kissed the ground. = 他跪下亲吻了地面。
They knelt before the king. = 他们跪在国王面前。

现在完成时:
She has knelt for hours during the religious ceremony. = 她在宗教仪式上已经跪了几个小时了。
We have knelt and prayed for peace. = 我们已经跪下祈祷和平。

过去完成时:
He had knelt there for a long time before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经在那里跪了很久了。
They had knelt in prayer before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经跪下祈祷了。

将来时:
She will kneel and ask for forgiveness. = 她将跪下请求原谅。
He will kneel to propose to his girlfriend. = 他将跪下向他的女朋友求婚。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would kneel and beg for mercy. = 如果我是你，我会跪下求饶。
I wish I had knelt and apologized sooner. = 我希望我早点跪下道歉。

被动语态:
(The verb "kneel" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb.)


疑问句:
Did he kneel before the altar? = 他是否在祭坛前下跪？
Will she kneel during the wedding ceremony? = 婚礼仪式上她会下跪吗？


否定句:
He didn't kneel during the prayer. = 他祈祷时没有下跪。
She refused to kneel before her oppressor. = 她拒绝在她压迫者面前下跪。


PHRASE->
kneel down = 跪下
kneel before = 跪在...面前
kneel in prayer = 祈祷时下跪
fall to one's knees = 跪倒
kneel humbly = 谦卑地跪下
