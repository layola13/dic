fray:/freɪ/| n., v.|磨损；争吵；使磨损；使紧张|A fray is a noisy fight or argument.  It can also refer to the process of something becoming worn or unravelled.  As a verb, it means to wear away or unravel something, or to cause something to become worn or damaged. |例句：The fabric began to fray at the edges. 布的边缘开始磨损。

The soldiers engaged in a fierce fray. 士兵们参与了一场激烈的争吵。


近义词：fight, scuffle, brawl, unravel, wear, tatter|反义词：mend, repair, soothe, calm


词性: 名词和动词
