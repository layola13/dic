demulcent: /ˈdɛmjʊlsənt/ | adj. | 缓和的；润肤的；止痛的 | Soothing or softening, especially to the skin or mucous membranes.  A demulcent substance relieves irritation or inflammation. | 例句：The lotion has demulcent properties, soothing irritated skin. 这款乳液具有舒缓作用，可以舒缓受刺激的皮肤。


The nurse applied a demulcent ointment to the burn victim's wounds. 护士将一种缓和的药膏涂抹在烧伤患者的伤口上。


近义词：soothing, emollient, softening, alleviating | 反义词：irritating, harsh, abrasive | 词性：形容词


词源:  From Middle French *demulcent, from Latin demulcens, present participle of demulcere "to soothe, caress," from de- "away, from" + mulcere "to stroke, soothe."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *mel- "to grind, to soften."
