insinuative: /ɪnˈsɪnjuətɪv/ | adj. | 暗示的；含沙射影的；煽动的 |

英文解析：Suggesting something unpleasant or offensive in an indirect way; hinting.  Characterized by indirect or subtle suggestion.

例句：

His insinuative remarks about her honesty were deeply hurtful.  他的关于她诚实的含沙射影的言论非常伤人。

The insinuative tone of the article suggested corruption within the government. 这篇文章暗示性的语气暗示了政府内部的腐败。


近义词：suggestive, suggestive, implying, hinting, allusive, indirect, subtle

反义词：direct, explicit, forthright, straightforward


词性：形容词
