cowpoxes:/ˈkaʊpɒksɪz/| n.|牛痘|The plural form of cowpox, a mild viral disease affecting cattle, characterized by skin lesions.  Cowpox is related to smallpox and was historically used in the development of the smallpox vaccine.|The farmer noticed several cows with cowpoxes on their udders.  这位农民注意到几头奶牛的乳房上有牛痘。|  smallpox (in relation to vaccination), vaccinia|no direct antonym|名词
