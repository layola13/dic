pseudoroyally: /ˌsjuːdoʊˈrɔɪəli/ | adj. |伪王室地，假装王室般地 |  Affecting or suggesting royalty in a false or artificial way; pretending to be royal.  |例句：She decorated her home pseudoroyally, with velvet drapes and gilded furniture. 她以天鹅绒窗帘和镀金家具装饰她的家，营造出一种虚假的王室氛围。


例句：He behaved pseudoroyally, demanding special treatment and ignoring common courtesy. 他举止傲慢，要求特殊待遇，并且无视普通的礼仪。


近义词：kingly (in a superficial way), regally (pretentiously), imperially (affectedly)


反义词：commoner-like, humbly, modestly


词性：形容词
