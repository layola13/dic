reassociate: /ˌriːəˈsoʊʃieɪt/ | vt. |重新联想；重新联系；重新组合 | To connect or associate again; to form new connections or associations.  |例句：He tried to reassociate the smell with a pleasant memory. 他试图将这种气味与美好的回忆重新联系起来。

例句：After the accident, she had to reassociate her visual information with her sense of touch. 事故后，她不得不重新将视觉信息与触觉联系起来。


近义词：reconnect, recombine, link again | 反义词：dissociate, disconnect | 词性:动词
