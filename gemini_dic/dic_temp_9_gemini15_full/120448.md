The term can also be used figuratively to refer to someone or something insignificant or negligible. |例句：The Minotaur roamed the labyrinth, waiting for his next victim.  弥诺陶洛斯在迷宫里游荡，等待着下一个受害者。 The problem was a mere minotaur compared to the challenges ahead. 与未来的挑战相比，这个问题微不足道。|近义词：monster, beast, trifle, nothing|反义词：hero, giant, significant, important|名词
