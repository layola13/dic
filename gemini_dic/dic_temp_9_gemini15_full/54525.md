diskette:/dɪˈskɛt/| n. |软盘|A small, flat, usually rectangular magnetic storage device for computers, typically encased in a plastic or cardboard cover.|例句：I used a diskette to save my files before USB drives were common. 我在U盘普及之前用软盘保存文件。|近义词：floppy disk|反义词：hard drive, SSD


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I use a diskette to store data. = 我用软盘存储数据。
She saves her files on a diskette. = 她把文件保存在软盘上。


一般过去时:
He inserted the diskette into the drive. = 他把软盘插入驱动器。
We copied the files from the diskette. = 我们从软盘复制文件。


现在完成时:
I have used diskettes in the past. = 我过去用过软盘。
She has not used a diskette in years. = 她很多年没用过软盘了。


过去完成时:
He had already saved the file to the diskette before the computer crashed. = 电脑崩溃之前他已经把文件保存到软盘上了。
They had copied all the data onto the diskettes before leaving. = 离开之前他们已经把所有数据复制到软盘上了。


将来时:
