catboat:/ˈkætˌboʊt/ | n. |小帆船，单桅纵帆船 | A small sailboat with a single mast located well forward and a large sail extending almost the full length of the boat.  Catboats are known for their simple design and stability. | Example: He sailed his catboat across the bay. 他划着他的小帆船穿过海湾。  The old catboat bobbed gently on the waves. 那艘旧的小帆船轻轻地漂浮在波浪上。 | 近义词：sailboat, dinghy | 反义词： (无明确反义词，可根据语境考虑大型帆船等)
