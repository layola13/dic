orc:/ɔːrk/| n.|兽人，半兽人|A fictional humanoid creature, typically depicted as brutish, strong, and often evil, frequently found in fantasy literature and games.  Orcs are often portrayed as having a tribal or clan-based society and are frequently antagonists.|Example: The hero bravely fought the orc horde.  英雄勇敢地与兽人军队作战。 The orc chieftain roared his challenge. 兽人酋长咆哮着发起了挑战。|近义词：goblin, troll, ogre|反义词：elf, human (in a fantasy context)|名词
