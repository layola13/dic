disliked:/dɪsˈlaɪkt/| v. |不喜欢，厌恶 |To feel or express a strong aversion to something or someone.  |例句：I disliked the bitter taste of the medicine.  我讨厌药的苦味。

例句：Many people disliked his arrogant behavior. 许多人讨厌他傲慢的行为。


近义词：abhor, detest, loathe, hate | 反义词：love, adore, like, appreciate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dislikes spicy food. 他不喜欢辛辣的食物。
She dislikes going to crowded places. 她不喜欢去人多的地方。

一般过去时:
I disliked that movie. 我不喜欢那部电影。
They disliked the loud music. 他们不喜欢吵闹的音乐。

现在完成时:
I have disliked broccoli since I was a child. 从小我就讨厌西兰花。
She has disliked him ever since their argument. 自从他们争吵以来，她就一直不喜欢他。

过去完成时:
He had disliked her for years before he finally told her. 在他最终告诉她之前，他已经不喜欢她好几年了。
They had disliked the plan from the start. 从一开始他们就不喜欢这个计划。

将来时:
I will dislike this job if I have to work overtime every day. 如果我每天都要加班，我会讨厌这份工作的。
She will dislike the new rules if they are too strict. 如果新规太严格，她会讨厌的。

虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't dislike public speaking so much. 我希望我不那么讨厌公开演讲。
If I disliked him less, I would invite him to the party. 如果我不那么讨厌他，我会邀请他参加派对。

被动语态: (被动语态很少用于dislike)  被动语态表达不自然，几乎不用。


疑问句:
Do you dislike cats? 你讨厌猫吗？
Does she dislike her job? 她讨厌她的工作吗？

否定句:
I don't dislike coffee, I just prefer tea. 我不讨厌咖啡，我只是更喜欢茶。
He doesn't dislike her, he's just shy. 他不讨厌她，他只是害羞。


PHRASE->
be disliked by: 被...厌恶
strongly disliked: 强烈厌恶
generally disliked:  普遍不受欢迎
deeply disliked: 深恶痛绝
widely disliked:  广泛不受欢迎
