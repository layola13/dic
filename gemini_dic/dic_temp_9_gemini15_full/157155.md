pinelike:/ˈpaɪnlaɪk/| adj.|松树似的；像松树的|Resembling a pine tree in appearance or characteristics.  The word combines "pine" (a type of evergreen tree) and the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The tall, slender trees had a pinelike fragrance.  这些高瘦的树木散发着松树般的香味。The landscape was pinelike, with tall, dark evergreens dominating the view.  这片景观像松树林一样，高大的深绿色常青树占据了视野。|近义词：piney, coniferous|反义词：leafy, deciduous
