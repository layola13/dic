mesovarium:/ˌmezoʊˈveəriəm/| n. | 卵巢系膜 | The mesovarium is a fold of the peritoneum that connects the ovary to the broad ligament of the uterus.  It is a double layer of peritoneum that provides support and blood supply to the ovary. | The mesovarium plays a crucial role in supporting the ovary and providing it with essential blood vessels and nerves. |近义词： ovarian ligament |反义词：无
