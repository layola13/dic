crotaline:/ˈkroʊtəlaɪn/| adj.|响尾蛇的；响尾蛇属的|Relating to or characteristic of crotaline snakes, a subfamily of vipers that includes rattlesnakes, copperheads, and cottonmouths.  These snakes are known for their heat-sensing pits and, in many species, their rattles.|Example: The crotaline venom is highly toxic. 响尾蛇毒液剧毒。 The study focused on the crotaline subfamily's evolutionary adaptations. 这项研究关注响尾蛇亚科的进化适应性。|近义词：viperine (more general)|反义词：None specifically;  antonyms would describe characteristics opposite to those of crotaline snakes (e.g., non-venomous).
