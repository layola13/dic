USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dryness of the desert is extreme. = 沙漠的干燥程度非常高。
This bread lacks moisture; it's too dry. = 这面包缺水分，太干了。


一般过去时:
The dryness of the soil hindered plant growth. = 土壤的干燥阻碍了植物生长。
The extreme dryness last summer caused a drought. = 去年夏天极度的干旱导致了旱灾。


现在完成时:
The prolonged dryness has severely affected the crops. = 长期的干旱严重影响了庄稼。
Several years of dryness have led to water shortages. = 多年的干旱导致了缺水。


过去完成时:
The dryness had already damaged the plants before the rain came. = 下雨之前，干旱已经损坏了植物。
The prolonged dryness had depleted the water reservoir. = 长期的干旱已经耗尽了水库的水。


将来时:
The forecast predicts continued dryness for the next few weeks. = 预报预测未来几周将持续干旱。
We expect more dryness if we don't get enough rain. = 如果不下足够的雨，我们预计将会更加干燥。


虚拟语气:
If there were no dryness, the plants would thrive. = 如果没有干旱，植物就会茁壮成长。
I wish the dryness would end soon so the plants can recover. = 我希望干旱能尽快结束，这样植物就能恢复了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
