coat-tails:/ˈkoʊtˌteɪlz/| n.|衣襟后摆；(成功的)余波；|The trailing edges of a coat.  Figuratively, it refers to the benefits or advantages gained indirectly from the success of someone or something else. |例句：He rode on the coat-tails of his brother's success. 他沾了他哥哥成功的光。
例句：The smaller companies benefited from the coat-tails of the industry's growth. 小型公司得益于该行业增长的溢出效应。|近义词：aftermath, spillover,  repercussions|反义词：independent achievement, self-made success
