pygopod:/ˈpaɪɡəpɒd/| n. |无足蜥蜴|A legless lizard of the family Pygopodidae, characterized by its resemblance to a snake but possessing small, vestigial hind limbs.  These lizards are found in Australia and surrounding areas.|例句：The pygopod is a fascinating example of convergent evolution, resembling snakes despite being lizards.|近义词：legless lizard, snake-like lizard|反义词:  None specifically, but words implying legged lizards could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.
