hierologist:/ˌhaɪəˈrɒlədʒɪst/| n. |象形文字学家|A specialist in the study of hieroglyphs, the picture-writing system of ancient Egypt.  Hierologists decipher and interpret hieroglyphic texts, providing insights into ancient Egyptian culture, history, and religion. |例句：The hierologist spent years deciphering the ancient Egyptian inscription.  象形文字学家花了数年时间破译古埃及铭文。

例句：  The renowned hierologist presented her findings at the international conference. 著名的象形文字学家在国际会议上展示了她的研究成果。


近义词：Egyptologist (if specializing in Egyptian hieroglyphs)


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym)


词性：noun
