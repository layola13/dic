methodologies:/ˌmɛθəˈdɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 方法论；方法学 | The study of methods; a system of methods used in a particular area of study or activity.  It refers to the theoretical framework and practical approaches used to conduct research or solve problems.  | 例句：The researchers employed rigorous methodologies to ensure the validity of their findings.  研究人员采用了严格的方法论来确保其研究结果的有效性。

The company's methodologies for customer service are highly effective.  这家公司客户服务的流程方法非常有效。


近义词：methods, approaches, procedures, techniques | 反义词：haphazardness, randomness

词源:
The word "methodologies" is derived from the Greek word "methodos" (μέθοδος), meaning "way of doing something,"  and the suffix "-ologies," indicating a systematic study or theory of something.  Therefore, "methodologies" literally means "the study of methods."
