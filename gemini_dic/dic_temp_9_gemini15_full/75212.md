froggish:/ˈfrɒɡɪʃ/| adj.| 蛙似的；像青蛙的；粗声粗气的；脾气暴躁的 |Having the characteristics of a frog;  especially, grumpy, sullen, or croaking.  The word often implies a negative connotation, suggesting unpleasant behavior or a disagreeable disposition. |例句：His froggish voice grated on my nerves.  他的粗声粗气的嗓音让我很烦躁。

例句：He sat there, looking utterly froggish and unwilling to cooperate. 他坐在那里，看起来一脸不高兴，不愿意合作。


近义词：grumpy, surly, crotchety, sullen, grumpy


反义词：cheerful, amiable, pleasant, good-natured


词性:形容词


词源:源于古英语 *frogga，意思是“青蛙”。  The -ish suffix adds a qualitative or adjectival meaning, suggesting a resemblance to or a quality associated with frogs.
