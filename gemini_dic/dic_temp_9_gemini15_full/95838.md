incorrect:/ɪnˈkɔːrekt/| adj.|不正确的，错误的|Wrong; not accurate or true.  The information is incorrect. |The answer is incorrect.  你的答案是错误的。|近义词：wrong, erroneous, faulty, inaccurate|反义词：correct, accurate, right


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The information presented is incorrect. = 提供的信息不正确。
His calculations are incorrect. = 他的计算是错误的。


一般过去时:
The answer I gave was incorrect. = 我给出的答案是错误的。
His statement was incorrect. = 他的陈述是错误的。


现在完成时:
They have made several incorrect assumptions. = 他们做出了几个错误的假设。
I have realized that my previous answer was incorrect. = 我已经意识到我之前的回答是错误的。


过去完成时:
I had made an incorrect decision. = 我做了一个错误的决定。
She had given an incorrect address. = 她给了一个错误的地址。


将来时:
The forecast will likely be incorrect. = 预报可能会不准确。
His prediction will prove incorrect. = 他的预测将被证明是错误的。


虚拟语气:
If the information were incorrect, we would have to revise the report. = 如果信息不正确，我们就必须修改报告。
I wish I hadn't made that incorrect statement. = 我真希望我没有做出那个错误的陈述。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Is the information incorrect? = 信息不正确吗？

否定句:
