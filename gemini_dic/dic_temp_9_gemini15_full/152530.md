It's a method used by online publishers to monetize their content and limit free access.  |Example: The New York Times has a paywall, requiring a subscription to read most articles.  纽约时报设有付费墙，需要订阅才能阅读大部分文章。

Example: Many news websites use paywalls to generate revenue.许多新闻网站使用付费墙来创收。


近义词：subscription model, access restriction, content lock
