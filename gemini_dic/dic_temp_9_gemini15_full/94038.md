impartation: /ˌɪmpɑːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 传授；告知；给予 | The act of imparting something, especially knowledge, information, or a quality.  It refers to the process of sharing something with someone else, often something intangible like knowledge or a skill. |例句：The impartation of wisdom is a crucial part of a teacher's role. = 传授智慧是教师职责的重要组成部分。

The impartation of his experience benefited the younger generation. = 他经验的传授使年轻一代受益匪浅。


近义词：communication, transmission, bestowal, conveyance | 反义词：concealment, withholding, suppression | 词性：名词
