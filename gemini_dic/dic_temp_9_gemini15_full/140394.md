oilstone:/ˈɔɪlstəʊn/| n. |油石| A fine-grained stone, often of siliceous material, used with oil as an abrasive for sharpening tools.  It is typically rectangular and flat, with varying degrees of coarseness. |例句：He used an oilstone to sharpen his hunting knife. 他用油石磨刀。|例句：The carpenter carefully honed his chisel on the oilstone. 木匠小心地用油石磨他的凿子。|近义词：whetstone, hones|反义词:None
