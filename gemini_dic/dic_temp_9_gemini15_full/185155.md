retirement:/rɪˈtaɪərmənt/ | n. |退休；退役；隐退 | The state of no longer working, especially because of age; the act of ceasing work.  |He's looking forward to retirement and spending more time with his grandchildren. 他期待着退休，并能花更多时间和孙子们在一起。|近义词：resignation, withdrawal |反义词：employment, work


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His retirement plan includes traveling the world. = 他的退休计划包括环游世界。
Retirement is a major life change for many people. = 退休对许多人来说是一个重大的生活变化。


一般过去时:
His retirement from the company was announced last week. = 他公司退休的消息上周公布了。
She celebrated her retirement with a big party. = 她举办了一个盛大的派对来庆祝她的退休。


现在完成时:
He has enjoyed his retirement since last year. = 自从去年以来，他一直享受着退休生活。
She has already made plans for her retirement. = 她已经为退休做好了计划。


过去完成时:
Before his retirement, he had worked for the company for 30 years. = 在退休前，他在公司工作了30年。
They had discussed his retirement package before he left. = 在他离开之前，他们已经讨论了他的退休金问题。


将来时:
He will retire next year at the age of 65. = 他明年65岁退休。
She will enjoy a comfortable retirement thanks to her savings. = 多亏了她的积蓄，她将享受舒适的退休生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about early retirement options, I would have taken them. = 如果我知道提前退休的选择，我会选择它们的。
I wish I could afford an early retirement. = 我希望我能负担得起提前退休。


被动语态:
His retirement was celebrated by his colleagues. = 他的退休受到了同事们的庆祝。
The retirement policy is being reviewed by the company. = 公司正在审查退休政策。


疑问句:
What are your retirement plans? = 你的退休计划是什么？
When is he planning to retire? = 他计划什么时候退休？


否定句:
He is not yet ready for retirement. = 他还没准备好退休。
She doesn't plan to retire for another five years. = 她计划再过五年才退休。
