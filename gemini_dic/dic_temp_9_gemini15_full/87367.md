herolike: /ˈhɪəroʊlaɪk/ | adj. | 英雄般的，像英雄一样的 | Resembling a hero; having qualities or characteristics associated with heroes, such as courage, bravery, and nobility.  |例句：His herolike actions saved many lives. 他英雄般的行为挽救了许多人的生命。

例句：The firefighters showed herolike courage in the face of danger. 消防员面对危险表现出英雄般的勇气。


近义词：heroic, valiant, brave, courageous, noble

反义词：cowardly, craven, pusillanimous


词源:  The word "herolike" is a compound word formed from "hero" and the suffix "-like."  "Hero" originates from the Greek word "hērōs," meaning "hero," a legendary figure or a person of great courage and admired for their achievements. "-like" is a suffix that means "resembling" or "having the qualities of."
