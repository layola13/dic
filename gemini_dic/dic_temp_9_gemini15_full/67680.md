extuberation:/ˌɛkstʊbəˈreɪʃən/| n. |隆起消退；突出物消失|The gradual disappearance of a swelling or protrusion.  This term is primarily used in a medical context, referring to the reduction or disappearance of a swelling, protrusion, or abnormal growth. |Example: The extuberation of the tumor was observed after the chemotherapy treatment.  肿瘤在化疗后消退了。|近义词：retraction, subsidence, regression|反义词：protrusion, swelling, exacerbation|
