devour:/dɪˈvaʊər/| vt.|吞食；吞噬；狼吞虎咽；快速消耗|To eat (something) hungrily or quickly; to consume something eagerly or rapidly.  It can also refer to consuming something completely or destructively.|例句：The hungry lion devoured its prey.  饥饿的狮子吞食了它的猎物。

例句：She devoured the entire book in one sitting. 她一口气读完了整本书。


近义词：gobble, gulp, consume, inhale, engulf

反义词：starve, nibble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devours his food every day. 他每天狼吞虎咽地吃饭。
They devour information online. 他们在线快速吸收信息。


一般过去时:
The dog devoured the bone in seconds. 狗几秒钟就吞掉了骨头。
She devoured the news article with great interest. 她饶有兴致地快速阅读了新闻报道。


现在完成时:
He has devoured three whole pizzas. 他已经吃掉了三个比萨饼。
She has devoured countless books this year. 今年她已经读了无数本书。


过去完成时:
He had devoured all the cookies before his friends arrived. 在他的朋友们到达之前，他已经吃掉了所有的饼干。
She had devoured the entire manuscript by midnight. 到午夜时分，她已经读完了整篇手稿。


将来时:
He will devour that entire cake. 他将会吃掉整个蛋糕。
They will devour the new movie. 他们将很快看完这部新电影。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would devour that entire pie. 如果我饿了，我会把整个馅饼都吃掉。
I wish I could devour all the knowledge in the world. 我希望我能吞噬世界上所有的知识。


被动语态:
The entire meal was devoured quickly. 整顿饭很快就被吃完了。
The evidence was devoured by the authorities. 证据被当局吞噬了。


疑问句:
Did the bear devour the fish? 熊吃了那条鱼吗？
Will they devour all the food at the party?  他们会把派对上的所有食物都吃光吗？


否定句:
He did not devour the entire meal. 他没有吃完所有的食物。
She did not devour the book; she read it slowly. 她没有快速看完那本书；她慢慢地读。


PHRASE->
devour a book: 读完一本书
devour the landscape: 饱览风景
devour one's time: 消耗时间
devour resources: 消耗资源
devour the news: 迅速阅读新闻


词源:
From Old French *devorer, from Latin devorāre "to devour, swallow up," from de- "completely" + vorāre "to eat, swallow."
