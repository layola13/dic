laguna:/ləˈɡuːnə/| n.|泻湖；环礁湖|A lagoon is a shallow body of water separated from a larger body of water by a barrier reef, sandbar, or similar feature.  It's often brackish (a mix of fresh and saltwater).|例句：The turquoise lagoon was calm and inviting.  这片绿松石色的泻湖平静而诱人。|例句：We spent the afternoon swimming in the sheltered lagoon. 我们在受保护的泻湖里度过了下午。|近义词：inlet, bay, sound, estuary|反义词：ocean, sea
