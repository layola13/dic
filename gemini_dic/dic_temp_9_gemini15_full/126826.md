naphthalate:/ˈnæfθəleɪt/| n.|萘酸盐；萘酸酯|A salt or ester of naphthoic acid.  Naphthalates are used in various applications, including as intermediates in the synthesis of other chemicals and as components in some plastics and coatings.|例句：Sodium naphthalate is used as a dye intermediate. 萘酸钠用作染料中间体。|例句：The polymer contains naphthalate esters to enhance its flexibility.  该聚合物含有萘酸酯以增强其柔韧性。|近义词：naphthoate (less common)|反义词：无
