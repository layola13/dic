nonseasonally:/ˌnɒnˈsiːzənəli/| adj. |非季节性地，不按季节地|Not occurring or happening in a particular season or regularly throughout the year.  It refers to something that is not tied to a specific seasonal pattern.|例句：The company experienced nonseasonal growth in the third quarter.  这家公司在第三季度经历了非季节性增长。|例句：Nonseasonal fluctuations in the stock market are often caused by unexpected events. 股市的非季节性波动往往是由意外事件引起的。|近义词：irregularly, erratically, aperiodically|反义词：seasonally, regularly
