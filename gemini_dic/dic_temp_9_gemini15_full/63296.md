enthusiast:/ɪnˈθuːziæst/| n.| 热衷者，爱好者 | A person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject.  |  He's a keen enthusiast for classic cars. 他是一位狂热的经典汽车爱好者。| aficionado, devotee, buff | apathetic, indifferent | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an enthusiastic supporter of the team. = 他是这个队的狂热支持者。
She is an enthusiast for environmental protection. = 她是一位环保爱好者。


一般过去时:
He was an enthusiastic participant in the debate. = 他是这场辩论的积极参与者。
She was an enthusiast for vintage clothing. = 她是一位对复古服装很热衷的人。


现在完成时:
He has been an enthusiastic blogger for five years. = 他已经是一位热情的博主五年了。
She has remained an enthusiast for classical music throughout her life. = 她一生都对古典音乐保持着热情。


过去完成时:
He had been an enthusiastic fan of the band before they became famous. = 在乐队成名之前，他一直是他们的忠实粉丝。
She had been an enthusiast for photography since her childhood. = 从小她就对摄影很热衷。


将来时:
He will be an enthusiastic participant in the upcoming marathon. = 他将成为即将到来的马拉松比赛的积极参与者。
She will remain an enthusiast for astronomy for years to come. = 她未来几年仍将对天文学充满热情。


虚拟语气:
If he were an enthusiast, he would attend every meeting. = 如果他是一位热衷者，他会参加每次会议。
If she were an enthusiast for hiking, she would join us on this trip. = 如果她喜欢远足，她会加入我们的这次旅行。


被动语态:
The new project was enthusiastically supported by the team. = 这个新项目得到了团队的热烈支持。
The proposal was met with enthusiasm from the enthusiasts. =  这个提议受到了爱好者们的热烈欢迎。


疑问句:
Is he an enthusiast for jazz music? = 他是爵士乐爱好者吗？
Are you an enthusiast for historical novels? = 你是历史小说爱好者吗？


否定句:
He is not an enthusiast for competitive sports. = 他不是竞技体育爱好者。
She is not an enthusiast about modern art. = 她对现代艺术并不热衷。
