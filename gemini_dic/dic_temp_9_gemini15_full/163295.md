preconfer:/ˌpriːˈkɒnfər/| v.|预先商议，预先协商|To discuss or arrange something beforehand.  It implies a meeting or discussion that takes place before a main event or decision.|例句：The committee members preconferenced the agenda before the formal meeting.  委员会成员在正式会议前预先商议了议程。|近义词：prearrange, predetermine, plan, discuss beforehand|反义词：postpone, improvise


USAGE->

They preconference the project details every week. = 他们每周预先商议项目细节。
We preconference our strategies before each campaign. = 我们每次活动前都会预先商议策略。
