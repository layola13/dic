dispensaries:/dɪˈspɛnsəriːz/| n. |药房；分发处；施药处 |A dispensary is a place where medicines are dispensed, often as part of a hospital or other healthcare facility. It can also refer to a place where other goods are distributed or given out. |The local dispensary provides essential medicines to the community. 当地药房为社区提供必需药品。|pharmacy, drugstore |black market (in the context of illegal substances) |名词
