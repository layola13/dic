reweighing:/ˌriːˈweɪɪŋ/| v. |重新称重；再衡量|The act of weighing something again, often to ensure accuracy or to account for changes in weight.  It can also imply a reconsideration or reevaluation of something.|例句：The scientist is reweighing the sample to ensure the accuracy of the experiment. 科学家正在重新称量样品以确保实验的准确性。

例句：We need to reweigh the pros and cons before making a final decision. 在做出最终决定之前，我们需要重新权衡利弊。


近义词：reassess, reconsider, reevaluate |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be to ignore or neglect weighing. |动词
