lingua:/ˈlɪŋɡwə/| n.|语言|Language; a particular language.  The word often refers to a specific language,  but it can also be used more generally to refer to language as a system. | Example: Latin is a dead lingua. 拉丁语是一种死语言。The lingua of the indigenous people is quite complex.  当地人的语言非常复杂。|近义词：language, tongue, idiom|反义词：silence
