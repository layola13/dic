latifoliate:/ˌlætɪˈfoʊliˌeɪt/| adj. |宽叶的；阔叶的|Having broad leaves.  The term refers to plants with leaves that are relatively wide compared to their length. This characteristic is often associated with trees and shrubs that thrive in warmer, more humid climates.|Example: Many latifoliate trees lose their leaves in the autumn.  许多阔叶树在秋天落叶。The latifoliate forest was lush and green.  阔叶林郁郁葱葱。|近义词：broad-leaved, wide-leaved|反义词：narrow-leaved, acicular
