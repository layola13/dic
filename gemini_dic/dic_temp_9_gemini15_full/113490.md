lyricist:/ˈlɪrɪsɪst/| n. |作词家，填词人|A lyricist is a person who writes the lyrics for songs.  They are responsible for crafting the words and verses that convey the song's message and emotion.|Example:  Bob Dylan is a celebrated lyricist known for his poetic and insightful lyrics.  鲍勃·迪伦是一位以其诗意和富有洞察力的歌词而闻名的著名作词家。|近义词：song writer, poet (in some contexts)|反义词：composer (in the context of music creation)
