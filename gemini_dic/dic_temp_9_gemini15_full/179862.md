reexport:/ˌriːˈɛkspɔːrt/ | vt. | 再出口，转口 | To export (goods) that have been imported.  This involves importing goods from one country and then exporting them to another.  | Example: The company re-exported the textiles to several European countries after purchasing them from Asia.  这家公司在从亚洲购买纺织品后，将其再出口到几个欧洲国家。 | 近义词：re-ship, transship | 反义词：import


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-export goods to many countries. = 他们向许多国家再出口商品。
The company re-exports coffee beans from Brazil. = 这家公司再出口巴西咖啡豆。

一般过去时:
We re-exported the surplus materials last year. = 我们去年再出口了剩余的材料。
The merchant re-exported the goods to a third country. = 商人将货物再出口到第三国。

现在完成时:
They have re-exported a large quantity of tea this year. = 他们今年已经再出口了大量的茶叶。
The firm has re-exported its products to several Asian markets. = 该公司已将其产品再出口到几个亚洲市场。

过去完成时:
They had re-exported the products before the new regulations came into effect. = 新规生效前，他们已将产品再出口。
The trader had re-exported the goods before the price dropped significantly. = 价格大幅下跌前，商人已将货物再出口。

将来时:
We will re-export the remaining stock next month. = 我们下个月将再出口剩余的库存。
The government will likely re-export the surplus grain to needy nations. = 政府可能会将剩余的粮食再出口给贫困国家。

虚拟语气:
If they re-exported the goods, they would make a profit. = 如果他们再出口这些货物，他们将获利。
If the company had re-exported earlier, they would have avoided losses. = 如果公司早点再出口，他们就能避免损失。

被动语态:
The goods were re-exported to neighboring countries. = 货物被再出口到邻国。
The surplus produce is being re-exported to international markets. = 剩余产品正在被再出口到国际市场。

疑问句:
Were the goods re-exported legally? = 这些货物是否合法地再出口？
Will the products be re-exported to Africa? = 这些产品会被再出口到非洲吗？

否定句:
The company did not re-export the damaged goods. = 公司没有再出口损坏的货物。
They will not re-export the goods unless they get a permit. = 除非他们获得许可证，否则他们不会再出口这些货物。
