self-expanding:/ˌsɛlfɪkˈspændɪŋ/| adj.| 自膨胀的，自动扩张的 |  Describes something that expands or enlarges without external force or assistance.  It often refers to materials or structures that increase in size due to their inherent properties or a designed mechanism. | Example: The self-expanding stent was successfully deployed in the artery.  自膨胀支架成功地植入动脉。  The self-expanding foam filled the cavity completely. 自膨胀泡沫完全填满了空腔。|近义词：self-inflating, automatically expanding | 反义词：static, non-expanding
