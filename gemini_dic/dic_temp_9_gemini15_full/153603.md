pentaradial:/ˌpɛntəˈreɪdiəl/| adj.|五辐射的，五射线的|Having five rays or radiating parts; arranged in a pattern with five axes of symmetry.  This term is commonly used in biology to describe organisms with radial symmetry where the body parts are arranged around a central axis in fives or multiples of fives.|例句：Starfish exhibit pentaradial symmetry. 海星具有五辐射对称性。  The flower has a pentaradial arrangement of petals. 这朵花的花瓣呈五辐射状排列。|近义词：pentamerous|反义词：bilateral, biradial
