reaccreditations:/ˌriːæˌkrɛdɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|重新认证；再次认证|The process of being re-evaluated and granted accreditation again.  This typically involves a review of an organization's compliance with standards and requirements.  |例句：The university underwent rigorous reaccreditations to maintain its high standards. 大学经历了严格的重新认证以保持其高标准。

例句：The hospital's reaccreditations process involved extensive documentation and site visits. 医院的重新认证过程涉及大量的文件和现场考察。
