e-learning:/ˈiːlɜːrnɪŋ/| n. |电子学习，在线学习|Electronic learning; the learning of educational material using electronic devices such as computers and mobile devices.|例句：Many universities now offer e-learning courses. 许多大学现在都提供在线课程。|例句：E-learning provides flexibility and accessibility to education. 电子学习为教育提供了灵活性和便利性。|近义词：online learning, distance learning, virtual learning|反义词：traditional classroom learning, face-to-face learning
