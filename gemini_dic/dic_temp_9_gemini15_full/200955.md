slitted:/ˈslɪtɪd/| adj.|狭长的；裂开的；有缝隙的|Having a long, narrow cut or opening; characterized by slits.  The adjective form of the verb "slit."|例句：The slitted eyes of the cat watched me intently. 猫那狭长的眼睛紧紧地盯着我。

例句：The slitted fabric revealed glimpses of skin beneath.  裂开的布料露出了下面的肌肤。


近义词：split, cleft, fissured, incised

反义词：unbroken, whole, intact


词源:源自古英语 *slittan，意为“切割”。
