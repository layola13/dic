over-effort: /ˈoʊvərˈɛfərt/ | n. |过度努力，费力过猛 | Excessively strenuous exertion;  more effort than is necessary or reasonable.  It often implies inefficiency or a lack of skill leading to wasted energy. |例句：His over-effort on the project didn't improve the final result; it just made him exhausted.  他的项目过度努力并没有改善最终结果；反而只是让他筋疲力尽。  His over-effort to impress his boss backfired. 他为了给老板留下好印象而过度努力适得其反。|近义词：overexertion, strain, striving, excessive effort |反义词：underachievement, idleness, negligence |名词
