expires:/ɪkˈspaɪərz/| v.|过期，失效|To come to an end; to cease to be valid.|The milk expires tomorrow.  牛奶明天过期了。|My passport expires next year. 我的护照明年过期。|近义词：ends, lapses, terminates|反义词：begins, commences, starts|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This offer expires at the end of the month. = 这个优惠活动在这个月底到期。
The warranty on my car expires next year. = 我的汽车保修期明年到期。


一般过去时:
The coupon expired last week. = 优惠券上周过期了。
My driver's license expired two months ago. = 我的驾照两个月前过期了。


现在完成时:
The subscription has already expired. = 订阅已经过期了。
My membership has expired; I need to renew it. = 我的会员资格已经过期了；我需要续费。


过去完成时:
The contract had expired before they realized it. = 合同在他们意识到之前就已经过期了。
The deadline had expired before I could submit my application. = 截止日期在我提交申请之前就已经过期了。


将来时:
Your visa will expire in three months. = 你的签证三个月后过期。
The agreement will expire at the end of the year. = 协议将在年底到期。


虚拟语气:
If the medication had not expired, I would have taken it. = 如果药物没有过期，我就会服用它。
If my passport hadn't expired, I could have travelled. = 如果我的护照没有过期，我就可以旅行了。


被动语态:
The product is advertised as expiring soon. = 该产品被宣传为即将过期。
The agreement was declared expired by the court. = 该协议被法院宣布失效。


疑问句:
When does this license expire? = 这个执照什么时候过期？
Does your insurance policy expire soon? = 你的保险单快到期了吗？


否定句:
The guarantee does not expire for another year. = 保证还有一年才过期。
The contract will not expire until next year. = 合同直到明年才会过期。


PHRASE->
expire a license  = 使执照失效
expire a contract = 使合同失效
expire a visa = 使签证失效
expiration date = 到期日
before it expires = 在它过期之前
