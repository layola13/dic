falling-out: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ aʊt/ | n. | 争吵；不和；决裂 | A serious disagreement or quarrel, often resulting in a breakdown of a relationship.  |例句：Their falling-out was a sad end to a long friendship.  他们的争吵为一段长久的友谊画上了悲伤的句号。  |例句：The falling-out between the two brothers lasted for years.  两兄弟之间的不和持续了好几年。|近义词：quarrel, dispute, rift, breach, estrangement |反义词：reconciliation, agreement, harmony |名词
