diffusive:/dɪˈfjuːsɪv/ | adj. |弥散的；扩散的；漫射的 | Relating to or characterized by diffusion; tending to spread or become widely known.  |例句：The diffusive properties of the gas were studied in the experiment.  实验中研究了该气体的扩散特性。
例句：The diffusive nature of the news led to widespread panic.  新闻的扩散性质导致了广泛的恐慌。|近义词：dispersive, spreading, pervasive |反义词：concentrated, localized, restricted |词性:形容词


USAGE->

The gas is diffusive at high temperatures. = 高温下气体是弥散的。
The rumor is diffusive throughout the town. = 谣言在镇上广泛传播。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
