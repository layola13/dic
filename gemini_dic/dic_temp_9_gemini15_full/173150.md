quasi-anxious:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈæŋkʃəs/ | adj. |似焦虑的，近似焦虑的 |  Describes a state that resembles anxiety but may not be full-blown anxiety. It suggests a feeling of unease, apprehension, or nervousness, but less intense than true anxiety.  This word is often used to describe subtle or low-level anxiety, or a situation that evokes a feeling of being slightly anxious. |Example:  She felt a quasi-anxious feeling before the presentation, a mild nervousness rather than crippling fear.|近义词:apprehensive, uneasy, nervous, slightly anxious |反义词:calm, relaxed, tranquil, composed
