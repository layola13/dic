deleted:/dɪˈliːtɪd/ | adj. | 删除的，已删除的 |  Deleted refers to something that has been removed or erased, often from a digital file or system.  It can also refer to something omitted or expunged from a record. | Example: The deleted files were irretrievable.  已删除的文件无法找回。 The deleted scene was crucial to understanding the plot.  被删除的场景对于理解剧情至关重要。 |近义词：removed, erased, expunged, omitted | 反义词：added, inserted, included | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The message is deleted. = 这条信息已被删除。
The file is deleted from the system. = 文件已从系统中删除。

一般过去时:
I deleted the email. = 我删除了那封邮件。
She deleted all the photos. = 她删除了所有照片。

现在完成时:
He has deleted the entire document. = 他已经删除了整个文档。
They have deleted their social media accounts. = 他们已经删除了他们的社交媒体账号。

过去完成时:
I had deleted the file before I realized my mistake. = 我在意识到我的错误之前已经删除了文件。
She had deleted the message before he could read it. = 她在他能读到信息之前就删除了信息。

将来时:
I will delete the unwanted files tomorrow. = 我明天将删除不需要的文件。
He will delete the account after the trial period. = 试用期结束后，他将删除账户。

虚拟语气:
