/ɪmˈpæktərz/ | n. | 撞击物；影响因素 |  Things that impact or cause impact;  The agents or forces that produce a significant effect or consequence.  This word is often used in plural form. | 例句：The micrometeoroids are impacters that damage satellites.  微陨石是损坏卫星的撞击物。 The economic impacters of the pandemic are still being assessed.  大流行病的经济影响因素仍在评估中。 | 近义词：impactors, agents, influences, factors | 反义词：inert things, neutral forces
