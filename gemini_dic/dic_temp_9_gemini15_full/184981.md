retelephoning: /ˌriːˈtɛləfoʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 再打电话，复打 | The act of telephoning someone again, often to clarify or follow up on a previous conversation.  | Example:  He was retelephoning his client to confirm the meeting time. 他再次打电话给他的客户确认会议时间。 |近义词：calling back, phoning again |反义词：暂无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retelephones his mother every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都给他母亲打电话。
She retelephones her boss to clarify the instructions. = 她再次打电话给老板以澄清指示。


一般过去时:
I retelephoned the hotel to confirm my reservation. = 我再次打电话给酒店确认我的预订。
They retelephoned the supplier after receiving a faulty product. = 他们收到有缺陷的产品后再次打电话给供应商。


现在完成时:
He has retelephoned her several times this morning. = 他今天早上已经给她打了好几次电话了。
She has retelephoned the office to check on the progress. = 她已经再次打电话给办公室询问进度。


过去完成时:
I had retelephoned him twice before he finally answered. = 我在他最终接电话之前已经给他打过两次电话了。
They had retelephoned the client before sending the revised proposal. = 他们在发送修改后的提案之前已经再次打电话给客户。


将来时:
I will retelephone you later this afternoon. = 我今天下午晚些时候会再给你打电话。
She will retelephone the bank to inquire about the transfer. = 她会再次打电话给银行询问转账事宜。


虚拟语气:
If I had known his number, I would have retelephoned him. = 如果我知道他的号码，我就给他再打个电话了。
I wish I had retelephoned her before she left. = 我真希望在她离开之前再给她打个电话。


被动语态:
The client was retelephoned by the sales representative. = 客户被销售代表再次打电话联系了。
The information was retelephoned to the headquarters. = 信息被再次传送到总部。


疑问句:
Have you retelephoned the doctor yet? = 你给医生回电话了吗？
Will he retelephone her before the deadline? = 他会在截止日期前给她回电话吗？


否定句:
I didn't retelephone him because I was busy. = 我没有给他回电话，因为我很忙。
She didn't retelephone the company despite the problem. = 尽管出了问题，她也没有再次打电话给公司。


PHRASE->
retelephone a client = 给客户回电话
retelephone for clarification = 再打电话澄清
retelephone to confirm = 再打电话确认
retelephone immediately = 立即回电话
retelephone the details = 再打电话详细说明
