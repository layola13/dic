propulsion:/prəˈpʌlʃən/| n. |推进力；驱动力|The force that drives something forward.  It refers to the action of driving or pushing something forward.  This can be applied to various contexts, including vehicles, rockets, and even abstract concepts.|例句：The rocket's propulsion system malfunctioned. 火箭的推进系统出现了故障。

例句：The company's success is driven by strong propulsion in the market. 这家公司的成功源于其强大的市场推动力。|近义词：thrust, drive, impetus|反义词：resistance, impediment
