intermembrane: /ˌɪntərˈmembreɪn/ | adj. | 膜间的，膜间隙的 | Situated between two membranes.  The term typically refers to the space between two biological membranes, such as the space between the inner and outer mitochondrial membranes. |  Example: The intermembrane space of the mitochondrion is crucial for oxidative phosphorylation.  线粒体的膜间隙对于氧化磷酸化至关重要。|近义词: intermembranous | 反义词: intramembranous
