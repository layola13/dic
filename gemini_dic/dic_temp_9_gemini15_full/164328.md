preelected: /ˌpriːɪˈlɛktɪd/ | adj. | 预先选举的；预先选定的 |  Pre-elected refers to someone or something that has been chosen or selected before an official election or selection process. This often implies a process that lacks transparency or fairness, or one where the outcome is predetermined.  | Example: The preelected candidate faced little opposition in the official election.  预先选定的候选人在正式选举中几乎没有遇到任何反对。 | 近义词: preordained, predetermined, preselected | 反义词: elected (in a fair and open process), randomly selected | 词性: adjective
