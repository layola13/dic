skids:/skɪdz/| n. v.|侧滑；打滑；刹车失灵|The act of sliding sideways, typically uncontrollably.  It can refer to a vehicle losing traction, or to a person slipping on a surface.|例句：The car skidded on the icy road.  汽车在结冰的路上打滑了。The tires skidded, and the car spun out of control. 轮胎打滑，汽车失去控制。|近义词：slide, slip, swerve|反义词：grip, hold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car skids on wet roads. = 汽车在湿滑的道路上打滑。
He often skids when he brakes hard. = 他猛踩刹车时经常打滑。


一般过去时:
The bus skidded and nearly hit a tree. = 公共汽车打滑，差点撞到树上。
My bike skidded on the gravel. = 我的自行车在碎石上打滑了。


现在完成时:
The car has skidded several times this winter. = 这辆车今年冬天已经打滑好几次了。
He has never skidded on this road before. = 他以前从未在这条路上打滑过。


过去完成时:
The driver had skidded before he applied the brakes. = 司机在踩刹车之前就已经打滑了。
The car had skidded several times before it finally stopped. = 汽车在最终停下之前已经打滑了好几次。


将来时:
The car will skid if you brake too hard. = 如果你刹车太猛，汽车就会打滑。
I think the car will skid on this icy patch. = 我想汽车会在这一块结冰的路面上打滑。


虚拟语气:
If the road had been dry, the car wouldn't have skidded. = 如果路面是干的，汽车就不会打滑了。
I wish I hadn't skidded on the black ice. = 我真希望我没有在黑冰上打滑。


被动语态:
The car was skidded into the ditch. = 汽车打滑冲进了沟里。(侧重汽车是被动地打滑)
The skid marks were clearly visible. = 打滑的痕迹清晰可见。(侧重痕迹)


疑问句:
Did the car skid? = 汽车打滑了吗？
Why did the bike skid? = 为什么自行车会打滑？


否定句:
The car didn't skid. = 汽车没有打滑。
He didn't skid on the turn. = 他在转弯处没有打滑。
