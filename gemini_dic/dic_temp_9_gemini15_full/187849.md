rosewood:/ˈroʊzˌwʊd/ | n. | 玫瑰木，紫檀木 | A hard, reddish-brown wood from various tropical trees, highly valued for its beauty and durability, often used in furniture making.  | The antique table is made of exquisite rosewood. 这张古董桌子是用精美的玫瑰木制成的。 The piano's casing is crafted from polished rosewood. 这架钢琴的外壳是用抛光的玫瑰木制成的。| mahogany, ebony | (no direct antonym, but less valuable woods could be considered opposites in terms of value)| 名词
