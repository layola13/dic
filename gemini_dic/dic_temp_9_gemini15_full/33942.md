cirrocumular:/ˌsɪroʊˈkjuːmjʊlər/| adj.| 卷积云的 |  Descriptive of a cloud type characterized by small, white patches or flakes, often arranged in groups or lines.  These clouds are composed of ice crystals and are typically found at high altitudes. | Example: The cirrocumular clouds were a beautiful sight, resembling a scattering of tiny pearls across the sky.  卷积云非常美丽，如同散落在天空中的点点珍珠。|近义词：cirrus, floccus (referring to individual cloud elements)|反义词：nimbostratus (low, rain-producing cloud)
