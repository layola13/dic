sidings:/ˈsaɪdɪŋz/| n.|侧线；支线；（火车）道岔；外墙板|Side tracks or branches of a railway line.  Also, the outer covering of a building, often made of wood or metal. |例句：The train pulled into a siding to let the express train pass.  火车驶入侧线让特快列车通过。

The house was clad with weatherboard sidings. 这房子用木板外墙板覆盖。|近义词：branch line, spur, siding track, cladding|反义词：main line
