flapping:/ˈflæpɪŋ/| v.|拍打；拍动；飘动|The act of moving something, especially wings, up and down or back and forth.  It can also describe something moving loosely in the wind.|例句：The bird was flapping its wings frantically.  这只鸟疯狂地拍打着翅膀。

例句：The flag was flapping in the wind. 旗子在风中飘动。


近义词：fluttering, beating, waving|反义词：still, motionless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flaps its wings. = 鸟拍打着翅膀。
He is flapping his arms. = 他正在挥舞着手臂。

一般过去时:
The butterfly flapped its wings and flew away. = 蝴蝶拍打翅膀飞走了。
She flapped her hands in frustration. = 她沮丧地拍打着手。

现在完成时:
The wind has been flapping the curtains all day. =  风整天都在拍打窗帘。
The kite has been flapping wildly in the storm. = 风筝在暴风雨中剧烈地飘动。

过去完成时:
The flag had been flapping before the storm hit. =  暴风雨来临之前，旗子一直在飘动。
The leaves had been flapping in the breeze all afternoon. = 下午树叶一直在微风中飘动。

将来时:
The kite will be flapping in the wind. = 风筝会在风中飘动。
The birds will flap their wings and take flight. = 鸟儿会拍打翅膀起飞。

虚拟语气:
If the bird flapped its wings harder, it could fly higher. = 如果鸟儿更用力拍打翅膀，它就能飞得更高。
I wish the flag wasn't flapping so much in the wind. = 我希望旗子不要在风中飘动得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The laundry was flapping in the breeze. = 洗好的衣服在微风中飘动。
The wings were being flapped with great force. = 翅膀正被大力拍打着。

疑问句:

否定句:

The fabric wasn't flapping in the still air. =  在静止的空气中，织物没有飘动。
