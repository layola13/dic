preinfected:/ˌpriːɪnˈfɛktɪd/| adj.| 预先感染的，已感染的 |  Describes something or someone that has been infected before a particular point in time or event.  It implies a prior infection, often affecting subsequent events or analysis. | Example: The preinfected cells showed a different response to the treatment.  预先感染的细胞对治疗显示出不同的反应。 The study focused on the effects of the virus on preinfected patients.  这项研究集中在病毒对先前感染患者的影响。|近义词：previously infected, infected beforehand |反义词：uninfected,infection-free
