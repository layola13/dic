rediscussion:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌʃən/| n. |重新讨论，再次讨论 |A further discussion of a topic already discussed.  It often implies a more thorough or detailed examination than the initial discussion. |例句：After the initial meeting, a rediscussion of the budget was necessary.  重新讨论预算很有必要。|例句：The rediscussion of the contract terms led to a more favorable agreement.  对合同条款的重新讨论导致达成了一项更有利的协议。|近义词：reconsideration, review, re-examination |反义词：conclusion, resolution


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee rediscusses the proposal every week. = 委员会每周重新讨论该提案。
We rediscuss the project plan regularly. = 我们定期重新讨论项目计划。


一般过去时:
They rediscussed the issue after receiving new information. = 他们在收到新的信息后重新讨论了这个问题。
The team rediscussed the strategy following the setback. = 团队在遭遇挫折后重新讨论了战略。


现在完成时:
We have rediscussed this matter several times. = 我们已经多次重新讨论过这个问题。
They have rediscussed the terms of the contract. = 他们已经重新讨论了合同条款。


过去完成时:
They had rediscussed the plan before the final decision. = 他们在最终决定之前已经重新讨论了计划。
The board had rediscussed the merger proposal prior to the vote. = 董事会在投票之前已经重新讨论了合并提案。


将来时:
We will rediscuss the budget next month. = 我们下个月将重新讨论预算。
They will rediscuss the contract terms with the client. = 他们将与客户重新讨论合同条款。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rediscuss the proposal. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会重新讨论这个提案。
It would be beneficial if we rediscussed the project's objectives. = 如果我们重新讨论一下项目的总体目标，将会很有益处。


被动语态:
The budget was rediscussed by the committee. = 预算被委员会重新讨论了。
The issue will be rediscussed at the next meeting. = 这个问题将在下次会议上重新讨论。


疑问句:
Will the committee rediscuss the proposal? = 委员会会重新讨论该提案吗？
When will the team rediscuss the strategy? = 团队什么时候会重新讨论战略？


否定句:
They did not rediscuss the matter. = 他们没有重新讨论这个问题。
The board chose not to rediscuss the merger. = 董事会选择不重新讨论合并事宜。
