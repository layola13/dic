schreibersite:/ˈʃraɪbərˌsaɪt/| n. |陨铁磷铁矿|A nickel-iron phosphide mineral found in meteorites.  It is named after the German mineralogist Carl Friedrich Schreiber. Schreibersite is often associated with other minerals, such as kamacite and taenite, in iron meteorites. Its presence is significant in the study of meteorite formation and planetary science. | Example: The discovery of schreibersite in the meteorite provided valuable insights into the early solar system.  陨铁磷铁矿在陨石中的发现为了解早期太阳系提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：none (unique mineral)|反义词：none
