premodel:/ˈpriːmɒd(ə)l/| n.|预模型，原型|A premodel is a preliminary or initial model, often serving as a basis or template for further development or refinement. It's a simplified representation used for testing and exploration before a more complete model is created.|例句：The architects created a premodel of the building to visualize the design before starting construction. 建筑师们在开始施工前创建了一个建筑物的预模型来可视化设计。|近义词：prototype, archetype, template, draft|反义词：final model, finished product|名词
