pricier:/ˈpraɪsər/| adj.|较贵的，价格更高的|More expensive.  This word is used to describe something that costs more than something else or more than expected.|例句：That restaurant is pricier than I thought. 那家餐馆比我想象的要贵。|例句：Organic vegetables are often pricier than conventionally grown ones. 有机蔬菜通常比传统种植的蔬菜更贵。|近义词：dearer, costlier, more expensive|反义词：cheaper, less expensive
