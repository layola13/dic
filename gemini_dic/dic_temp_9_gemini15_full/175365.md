He strode rakishly into the room, a cigarette dangling from his lips. 他潇洒地走进房间，嘴唇上叼着一支香烟。


近义词：dashingly, jauntily, carelessly, nonchalantly | 反义词：primly, demurely, meticulously


词源:  Derived from "rakish," meaning "dashing" or "disreputable," ultimately from "rake," meaning a dissolute man.  The original sense emphasized a slightly immoral or unconventional flair, which is still present in some uses of the word.
