polyanion:/ˌpɒliˈænaɪən/| n. |多阴离子|A polyanion is a negatively charged ion that contains more than one atom.  It's a complex ion with multiple negative charges.  These ions often play crucial roles in various chemical and biological processes. For example, many biological molecules, like DNA and RNA, contain polyanions. |Example:  Many silicate minerals contain complex polyanions built from SiO4 tetrahedra.  许多硅酸盐矿物含有由SiO4四面体构成的复杂多阴离子。|近义词：multinuclear anion, complex anion |反义词：cation, monoanion
