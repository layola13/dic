khronos:/ˈkraɪnɒs/ | n. |克罗诺斯（时间之神）,时间|The word "khronos" is derived from the Ancient Greek word Χρόνος ( Khrónos), meaning "time". It is used in modern contexts, particularly in computing, to refer to time-related standards and technologies.  |Example: The Khronos Group develops open standards for 3D graphics, augmented reality, and other time-sensitive technologies.|近义词：time, epoch |反义词：eternity (in a philosophical sense)
