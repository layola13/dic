reverses:/rɪˈvɜːrsəz/| n. v.|颠倒；反转；逆转；撤销|The act of reversing something or the state of being reversed;  to change direction; to overturn or cancel something.|例句：The car reversed into the parking space.  车子倒车进入停车位。
The government reversed its decision. 政府撤销了它的决定。|近义词：invert, overturn, annul, repeal|反义词：affirm, confirm, uphold, advance


USAGE->
