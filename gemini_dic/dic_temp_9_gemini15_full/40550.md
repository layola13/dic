controverted:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːtɪd/| adj.|有争议的；引起争议的；被争论的 |  Disputed; called into question; subject to debate.  | The theory is highly controverted among experts.  该理论在专家中极具争议。 | disputed, debated, contested | undisputed, uncontested, agreed-upon | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The findings of the study are controverted by some researchers. =  一些研究人员对这项研究的结果提出了异议。
This issue is controverted in many academic circles. = 这个问题在许多学术界都存在争议。


一般过去时:
The decision was controverted by the opposition party. =  反对党对这一决定提出了质疑。
His claims were controverted by several witnesses. =  他的说法被几位证人驳斥。


现在完成时:
The interpretation of the ancient text has been controverted for centuries. =  对古代文本的解读几个世纪以来一直存在争议。
The results of the election have been controverted due to irregularities. =  由于违规行为，选举结果受到质疑。


过去完成时:
The theory had been controverted before the new evidence emerged. =  在新证据出现之前，该理论就已经受到了争议。
The scientist's findings had been controverted by a rival team. = 科学家的发现已被竞争对手团队质疑。


将来时:
The new law is likely to be controverted in the courts. = 新法律可能会在法庭上受到争议。
The proposed changes will undoubtedly be controverted by many people. =  拟议中的变更无疑会受到许多人的质疑。


虚拟语气:
If the results were controverted, the study would be retracted. = 如果结果存在争议，这项研究将会被撤回。
I wish the issue were not so controverted. =  我希望这个问题不要如此有争议。


被动语态:
The proposal was controverted by the board of directors. =  该提案遭到董事会的反对。
The author's arguments were controverted by critics. =  作者的论点受到了批评家的质疑。


疑问句:
Is the authenticity of the painting controverted? = 这幅画的真伪有争议吗？
Are the conclusions of this study controverted by other findings? = 这项研究的结论与其他发现相矛盾吗？


否定句:
The evidence was not controverted by the defense. =  证据并未受到辩方的质疑。
His explanation was not controverted by anyone present. = 在场没有人对他提出的解释表示异议。
