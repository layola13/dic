calcites:/ˈkælsaɪts/| n. |方解石(复数)|A crystalline form of calcium carbonate (CaCO3), a common mineral found in sedimentary rocks.  It is the main component of limestone and marble. |例句：The cave formations were composed primarily of calcites.  这些洞穴构造主要由方解石构成。


例句：The geologist identified several different types of calcites in the sample. 地质学家在样本中鉴定了几种不同类型的方解石。


近义词：calcium carbonate (化学名称)


反义词：无


词性:名词
