curlew:/ˈkɜːrluː/| n. |丘鹬；蛎鹬|A curlew is a wading bird with a long, down-curved bill. They are known for their haunting calls.  |例句：We heard the mournful cry of a curlew in the marshes.  我们在沼泽地里听到了丘鹬悲鸣的叫声。|例句：The curlew probed the mud with its long beak in search of food. 丘鹬用长长的喙探测泥土寻找食物。|近义词：snipe, sandpiper|反义词：无
