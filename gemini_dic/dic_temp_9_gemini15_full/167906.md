principalships:/ˈprɪnsɪpəlʃɪps/| n. |校长职位；主任职位；本金；资本|The plural of principalship, referring to the position or status of a principal (in a school, college, or other organization).  It can also, less commonly, refer to the sum of money forming a principal. |例句：The school is searching for suitable candidates for the principalship. 这所学校正在寻找合适的校长候选人。

例句：He invested a large sum of money, and the principalship grew significantly over the years. 他投资了一大笔钱，多年来本金增长显著。(less common usage)|近义词：headship, leadership, directorship |反义词：subordination, followership
