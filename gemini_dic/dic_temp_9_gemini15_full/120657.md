mis-understandingly:/ˌmɪsˌʌndərˈstændɪŋli/ | adv. |  误会地，由于误解而 |  In a manner showing misunderstanding or caused by misunderstanding.  It describes an action or state resulting from a failure to understand correctly. | He acted misunderstandingly, believing her silence meant agreement. 他由于误解而采取了行动，以为她的沉默意味着同意。  She spoke misunderstandingly, causing further confusion. 她说话含糊不清，导致进一步的混乱。 | understandably, mistakenly | clearly, correctly | 副词
