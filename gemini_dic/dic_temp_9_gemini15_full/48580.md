defeater:/dɪˈfiːtər/| n. |击败者，战胜者，挫败者 |A defeater is someone or something that defeats another.  It can refer to a person who wins a competition, a rival who is overcome, or an argument that refutes another.|例句：He was a formidable defeater in the chess tournament. 他是象棋锦标赛中一位强大的获胜者。  The new evidence proved to be a defeater of the defendant's alibi. 新证据证明被告的不在场证明是站不住脚的。|近义词：conqueror, victor, vanquisher |反义词：loser, defeated, vanquished |名词
