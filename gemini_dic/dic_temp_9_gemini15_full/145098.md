overappraises:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪz/ | vt. |过高评价；对…估价过高 | To estimate the value of something too highly.  |例句：The critics overappraised his early work, leading to unrealistic expectations for his later projects. 批评家们对他早期的作品评价过高，导致对他后期作品的不切实际的期望。

例句：He consistently overappraises his own abilities, leading to frequent disappointments. 他总是过高估计自己的能力，导致经常失望。


近义词：overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate the worth of, inflate the value of

反义词：underappraise, undervalue, underestimate
