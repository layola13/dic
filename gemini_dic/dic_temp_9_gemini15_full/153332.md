pendule:/ˈpɛndʒuːl/| n. |钟摆；摆锤|A pendule is a weight hung from a fixed point so that it can swing freely to and fro, especially one that regulates the movement of a clock.  It is a crucial component in mechanical clocks, rhythmically swinging to control the timing mechanism.|例句：The grandfather clock's pendule swung back and forth with a gentle tick-tock sound. 老爷钟的钟摆有节奏地来回摆动，发出轻柔的滴答声。|The pendulum of a clock moved rhythmically.  一个钟的摆锤有节奏地摆动。|近义词：pendulum|反义词：None.
