cirrus: /ˈsɪrəs/ | n. | 卷云 | A wispy, feathery cloud found high in the atmosphere, composed of ice crystals.  These clouds are typically white or light gray and appear as delicate strands or patches. |  Look at those beautiful cirrus clouds drifting across the sky.  看看那些漂浮在天空中美丽的卷云。 |  cumulus, stratus | nimbus  | 名词
