coronated:/ˈkɒrəneɪtɪd/|/ˈkɔːrənˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|加冕的；戴冠的| Crowned; invested with regal authority.  The word implies the act of coronation has already taken place. |例句：The newly coronated queen waved to the cheering crowds.  新加冕的女王向欢呼的人群挥手致意。

The coronated monarch began their reign. 加冕的君主开始了他们的统治。|近义词：crowned, enthroned|反义词：uncrowned
