impersonal:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənəl/| adj.|非个人的，客观的，没有人情味的 | Not relating to or involving particular people; objective.  Lacking personal warmth or concern. |例句：The impersonal tone of the letter made me feel unimportant.  这封信的冷漠语气让我感觉自己不重要。

例句：The report adopted an impersonal style, focusing on data rather than individual experiences.这份报告采用了客观的风格，侧重于数据而非个人的经历。


近义词：objective, detached, dispassionate, cold, unfeeling

反义词：personal, subjective, emotional, warm, intimate

词源：From Middle French *impersonnel, from Latin *impersonalis, from in- “not” + personalis “personal”.  The word ultimately derives from Latin persona, meaning "mask," "character," or "person."
