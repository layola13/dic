regret:/rɪˈɡrɛt/ | v. n. | 后悔，遗憾 |  To feel sorry about something that has happened or something that you have done.  |例句：I regret missing the concert. = 我后悔错过了音乐会。

例句： I deeply regret my hasty decision. = 我非常后悔我草率的决定。


近义词：rue, repent, be sorry for | 反义词：rejoice, celebrate | 词性:动词，名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I regret my actions. = 我后悔我的行为。
We regret to inform you that... = 我们很遗憾地通知您……

一般过去时:
He regretted telling her the truth. = 他后悔告诉她真相。
They regretted their decision later. = 他们后来后悔了他们的决定。

现在完成时:
I have regretted this decision ever since. = 从那以后我就一直后悔这个决定。
She has regretted her words many times. = 她后悔说过那些话很多次。

过去完成时:
He had regretted his impulsive behavior before he could apologize. = 在他道歉之前，他已经后悔自己冲动的行为。
They had regretted missing the deadline long before the consequences became apparent. = 早在后果显现之前，他们就后悔错过了最后期限。

将来时:
I will regret this if I don't try. = 如果我不尝试，我会后悔的。
She will regret not taking the opportunity. = 她会后悔没有抓住这个机会。

虚拟语气:
I would regret it if I didn't help. = 如果我不帮忙，我会后悔的。
She wished she hadn't said what she regretted saying. = 她希望自己没有说出那些让她后悔的话。

被动语态:
The mistake was deeply regretted by everyone involved. = 参与其中的每个人都对这个错误深感后悔。
His absence will be greatly regretted. = 他的缺席将令人非常遗憾。

疑问句:
Do you regret your choice? = 你后悔你的选择吗？
Does he regret what he did? = 他后悔自己所做的事情吗？

否定句:
I don't regret a single thing. = 我一点也不后悔。
He doesn't regret his decision. = 他不后悔他的决定。


PHRASE->
regret to inform you = 遗憾地通知您
regrettably = 遗憾地
have no regrets = 毫无遗憾
deep regret = 深深的遗憾
express regret = 表达遗憾
