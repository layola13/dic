glaceed:/ɡlæˈsiːd/| adj.|糖衣的，上釉的|Covered with a thin, shiny coating of sugar or icing.  The term often refers to fruits that have been preserved in sugar. |例句：The glaceed cherries added a touch of sweetness to the cake. 糖衣樱桃为蛋糕增添了一丝甜味。

例句：These glaceed apricots are a delicious treat. 这些糖衣杏子是一种美味佳肴。


近义词：candied, sugared, glazed|反义词：unglazed, plain|词性：形容词
