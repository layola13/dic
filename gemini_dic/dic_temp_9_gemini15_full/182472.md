renotifying:/ˌriːˈnoʊtɪfaɪɪŋ/| v.|重新通知，再次通知 |The act of notifying someone again; sending a notification a second time.|例句：The company is renotifying all customers about the product recall. 公司正在再次通知所有客户关于产品召回的事情。|近义词：re-notifying,  informing again, reminding|反义词：ignoring, neglecting|动名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are renotifying all employees about the policy change. = 他们正在再次通知所有员工关于政策变更的事情。
We renotify clients of upcoming deadlines regularly. = 我们定期再次通知客户即将到来的截止日期。


一般过去时:
The school renotified parents about the field trip cancellation. = 学校再次通知家长关于郊游取消的事情。
He renotified her of the meeting time. = 他再次通知她会议时间。


现在完成时:
They have renotified all stakeholders of the project's delay. = 他们已经再次通知所有项目利益相关者关于项目延误的事情。
She has renotified him several times about the payment. = 她已经几次再次通知他付款的事情。


过去完成时:
They had renotified the participants before the conference started. = 在会议开始之前，他们已经再次通知了与会者。
The manager had renotified the team about the urgent task. = 经理已经再次通知团队关于紧急任务的事情。


将来时:
They will renotify the customers once the product is available. = 产品可用后，他们将再次通知客户。
We will renotify you of any changes to the schedule. = 我们会将任何日程变更再次通知您。


虚拟语气:
If they had renotified me earlier, I would have been prepared. = 如果他们早点再次通知我，我就已经准备好了。
I wish they had renotified me about the change of plans. = 我希望他们已经再次通知我计划的改变。


被动语态:
All employees were renotified about the new dress code. = 所有员工都收到了关于新着装规范的再次通知。
The customers were renotified via email about the shipping delay. = 客户通过电子邮件收到了关于发货延误的再次通知。


疑问句:
Have they renotified everyone about the meeting? = 他们是否已经再次通知了所有人关于会议的事情？
Were the students renotified about the exam date? = 学生们是否收到了关于考试日期的再次通知？


否定句:
They did not renotify the customers about the price increase. = 他们没有再次通知客户关于价格上涨的事情。
She didn't renotify him about the deadline. = 她没有再次通知他截止日期。
