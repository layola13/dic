epilogation:/ˌɛpɪlɒˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |结语；尾声；补充说明 |The act of adding an epilogue; the state of being provided with an epilogue.  It refers to the process of adding a concluding section to a work, often summarizing or commenting on the main narrative.  |Example: The epilogation of the novel provided valuable insight into the characters' future.  小说结尾的补充说明提供了对人物未来宝贵的见解。|近义词：conclusion, postscript, addendum |反义词：prologue, preface
