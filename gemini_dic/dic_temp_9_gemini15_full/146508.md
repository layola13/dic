overgeneralize:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/ | vt. | 过度概括；以偏概全 | To draw a conclusion or make a statement that is too general and does not consider all the facts.  |  He overgeneralized about the capabilities of women. 他对女性的能力进行了过度概括。  | generalize, assume |  be specific, focus on details | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overgeneralizes about political issues. = 他对政治问题进行过度概括。
They often overgeneralize from limited experiences. = 他们经常根据有限的经验进行过度概括。


一般过去时:
She overgeneralized in her presentation. = 她在演讲中过度概括了。
The researcher overgeneralized his findings. = 研究人员过度概括了他的研究结果。


现在完成时:
Scientists have overgeneralized about the effects of climate change. = 科学家们对气候变化的影响进行了过度概括。
He has overgeneralized based on a small sample size. = 他根据小样本量进行了过度概括。


过去完成时:
They had overgeneralized about the cultural norms before traveling. = 他们在旅行之前对文化规范进行了过度概括。
She realized she had overgeneralized about her colleagues’ opinions. = 她意识到她对同事们的意见进行了过度概括。


将来时:
He will overgeneralize if he doesn't consider all the data. = 如果他不考虑所有数据，他将会过度概括。
They will likely overgeneralize the results of the study. = 他们很可能会过度概括研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If he were more careful, he wouldn't overgeneralize. = 如果他更仔细一点，他就不会过度概括了。
I wish I hadn't overgeneralized in my email. = 我真希望我没有在我的邮件中过度概括。


被动语态:
The results were overgeneralized in the report. = 报告中对结果进行了过度概括。
His conclusions were overgeneralized by the media. = 他的结论被媒体过度概括了。


疑问句:
Does he often overgeneralize? = 他经常过度概括吗？
Are they overgeneralizing about the situation? = 他们对情况进行过度概括了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't overgeneralize as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样经常过度概括了。
She tried not to overgeneralize about the group. = 她尽量避免对这个群体进行过度概括。


PHRASE->

词源:
