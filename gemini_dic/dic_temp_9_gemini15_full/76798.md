gammas:/ˈɡæməz/| n.|伽马射线|Gamma rays are a form of electromagnetic radiation. 伽马射线是一种电磁辐射。|近义词：gamma radiation|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Gamma rays are emitted by radioactive materials. = 伽马射线由放射性物质发出。
The sun emits gamma rays. = 太阳发出伽马射线。


一般过去时:
Scientists discovered gamma rays in 1900. = 科学家在1900年发现了伽马射线。(This is not entirely accurate historically, but serves as a grammatical example)
The experiment produced a burst of gamma rays. = 实验产生了一束伽马射线。


现在完成时:
Scientists have studied gamma rays for many years. = 科学家们已经研究伽马射线多年了。
We have detected several gamma ray bursts. = 我们已经探测到几次伽马射线暴。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, scientists had not understood gamma rays. = 在发现之前，科学家们并不了解伽马射线。
They had already measured the intensity of the gamma rays. = 他们已经测量了伽马射线的强度。


将来时:
Scientists will continue to study gamma rays. = 科学家们将继续研究伽马射线。
We will use gamma rays for medical imaging. = 我们将使用伽马射线进行医学成像。


虚拟语气:
If we had more powerful detectors, we would detect more gamma rays. = 如果我们有更强大的探测器，我们将探测到更多的伽马射线。
I wish I understood the physics of gamma rays better. = 我希望我更了解伽马射线的物理学。


被动语态:
Gamma rays are used in cancer treatment. = 伽马射线用于癌症治疗。
The gamma ray burst was observed by several telescopes. = 几个望远镜观测到了伽马射线暴。


疑问句:
What are the sources of gamma rays? = 伽马射线的来源是什么？
How are gamma rays produced? = 伽马射线是如何产生的？


否定句:
Gamma rays are not visible to the human eye. = 人眼看不到伽马射线。
The experiment did not produce the expected amount of gamma rays. = 实验没有产生预期的伽马射线量。
