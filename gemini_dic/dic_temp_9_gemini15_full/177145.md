rear-ended:/ˌrɪrˈɛndɪd/| adj. v.|追尾的；追尾事故；被追尾|Past participle of rear-end.  Describes a collision where a vehicle hits the rear of another vehicle.|例句：My car was rear-ended at a red light. 我的车在红灯时被追尾了。|例句：The accident report stated that one vehicle rear-ended another. 事故报告指出，一辆车追尾了另一辆车。|近义词：collided with (from behind), bumped, hit from behind|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be a head-on collision or side-swiped.|
