haematinometric:/ˌhiːmætɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|血红素测定法的|Relating to or employing haematinometry, a method for determining the amount of haematin (a form of hemoglobin) in a sample, often blood.  The technique typically involves colorimetric analysis, comparing the color intensity of the sample to a standard. |The haematinometric method provided a reliable assessment of the patient's hemoglobin levels.|近义词：hemoglobinometric (if referring specifically to hemoglobin)|反义词：None readily available, as it describes a specific method.
