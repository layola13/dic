overinsists:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪsts/| v. | 过分坚持；执意 |  To insist on something too much; to be excessively insistent.  | He overinsists on his opinion, even when presented with contradictory evidence. 他过分坚持自己的观点，即使面对相互矛盾的证据也是如此。 | insists, perseveres | yields, relents | 动词
