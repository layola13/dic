short-circuits:/ʃɔːrtˈsɜːrkɪts/| v.|短路；使短路；中断；阻碍|To create a short circuit; to bypass or interrupt a process.|The faulty wiring short-circuited the entire system.  故障的线路使整个系统短路了。|The negotiations short-circuited when the two sides couldn't agree.  当双方无法达成一致时，谈判陷入了僵局。| bypass, interrupt, circumvent |  function normally, operate smoothly | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The faulty wire short-circuits the system regularly. = 故障的电线经常使系统短路。
He short-circuits the process by skipping steps. = 他跳过步骤，从而缩短了流程。


一般过去时:
The power surge short-circuited the computer. = 电涌导致电脑短路。
The unexpected news short-circuited her plans. = 意外的消息打乱了她的计划。


现在完成时:
The repeated overloading has short-circuited the engine. = 反复超载导致发动机短路。
The project has been short-circuited by a lack of funding. = 项目由于缺乏资金而受阻。


过去完成时:
The system had short-circuited before the technician arrived. = 技术人员到达之前，系统就已经短路了。
They had short-circuited the discussion by jumping to conclusions. = 他们因为草率下结论而使讨论中断。


将来时:

If we don't address the issue, it will short-circuit the entire project. = 如果我们不解决这个问题，它将阻碍整个项目。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
