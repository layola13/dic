espouses:/ɪˈspaʊzɪz/| vt.|拥护，支持，主张|To espouse a cause or belief means to publicly support or advocate it.  It often implies a strong commitment and belief in the thing being supported.|例句：She espouses the cause of environmental protection. 她拥护环保事业。|例句：He espoused a new theory of physics. 他提倡一种新的物理理论。|近义词：advocate, champion, support, uphold|反义词：oppose, reject, denounce, condemn|动词
