empiricist:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪst/| n. |经验主义者|A person who supports empiricism; someone who believes that knowledge comes primarily or solely from sensory experience.|Empiricists often conduct experiments to test their hypotheses.|近义词：experimentalist, positivist|反义词：rationalist, idealist|名词
