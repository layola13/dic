id's:/ɪdz/ | pron. | 它的；他的；她的；它们的 |  The possessive form of "it," "he," "she," or "they." It shows ownership or relationship.  |例句：That's the dog's tail. 那是狗的尾巴。

例句：Those are the children's toys. 那些是孩子们 的玩具。


近义词：his, her, its, their | 反义词：无
