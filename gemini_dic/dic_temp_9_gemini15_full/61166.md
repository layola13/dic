elucidated:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪd/| v.|阐明；解释；说明|To make something clear; to explain something in a way that is easy to understand.|The professor elucidated the complex theory with simple examples.  教授用简单的例子阐明了复杂的理论。|The lawyer elucidated the points of the contract. 律师阐明了合同要点。|clarified, explained, illuminated|confused, obscured|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elucidates the problem clearly. 他清晰地阐述了这个问题。
She elucidates complex issues in simple terms. 她用简单的术语解释复杂的问题。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
