eyedropper:/ˈaɪˌdrɑːpər/ | n. |滴眼器，滴管 |A small device used to drop liquids, especially eye drops, into the eye or other small areas.  It typically consists of a small bulb or rubber squeeze part attached to a narrow tube. |例句：She used an eyedropper to apply the medicine to her eyes. 她用滴管把药水滴到眼睛里。|例句：The chemist used an eyedropper to measure the precise amount of liquid. 药剂师用滴管测量精确的液体量。|近义词：dropper, pipette (for more scientific applications)|反义词：None directly, but could consider a syringe for larger volumes.
