ex-servicemen:/ˌɛksˈsɜːrvɪsmən/ | n. | 退伍军人 | Former members of the armed forces.  The term often implies a period of service in the past, and the men may or may not be receiving benefits or pensions. | Example: Many ex-servicemen struggle to readjust to civilian life after returning from war. 许多退伍军人在从战场返回后努力适应平民生活。 |近义词：veteran, discharged soldier, former soldier | 反义词：serving soldier, active-duty personnel |  名词
