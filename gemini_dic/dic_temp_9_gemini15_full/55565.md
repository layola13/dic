disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/| vt. |打扰，扰乱，使不安 |to interrupt someone or something; to make someone feel worried or upset. |例句：Please don't disturb me while I'm working.  请在我工作的时候不要打扰我。

例句：The noise disturbed his sleep. 噪音扰乱了他的睡眠。

近义词：interrupt, bother, annoy, upset, trouble

反义词：calm, soothe, quiet, pacify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disturbs me every morning. = 他每天早上都打扰我。
The noise disturbs the peace. = 噪音扰乱了宁静。


一般过去时:
The phone call disturbed her concentration. = 电话打断了她的注意力。
The news disturbed him deeply. = 这个消息深深地打动了他的心。


现在完成时:
They have disturbed the natural environment. = 他们已经破坏了自然环境。
The scandal has disturbed many people. = 这起丑闻扰乱了许多人的生活。


过去完成时:
I had disturbed the meeting.  = 我已经扰乱了会议。
The cat had disturbed the sleeping baby. = 猫咪已经吵醒了正在睡觉的婴儿。


将来时:
I will disturb you later. = 我稍后会打扰你。
The storm will disturb the flight schedule. = 暴风雨会扰乱航班时刻表。


虚拟语气:
If you disturbed him, he would get angry. = 如果你打扰他，他会生气的。
I wish I hadn't disturbed her. = 我真希望我没有打扰她。


被动语态:
He was disturbed by the loud music. = 他被吵闹的音乐打扰了。
The meeting was disturbed by an unexpected visitor. = 会议被一位不速之客打断了。


疑问句:
Does the noise disturb you? = 噪音会打扰到你吗？
Will this disturb the process? = 这会扰乱这个流程吗？


否定句:
Please don't disturb me. = 请不要打扰我。
The quiet countryside didn't disturb her peace of mind. = 安静的乡村没有扰乱她的宁静。
