hoodoo:/ˈhuːduː/| n.|巫毒教；奇异的岩石构造；不可思议的事情 |A hoodoo is a tall, thin spire of rock that is formed by erosion.  It can also refer to voodoo magic or something mysterious and inexplicable. |例句：The hoodoos in Bryce Canyon National Park are a stunning sight.  布莱斯峡谷国家公园的奇石柱是令人惊叹的景象。  The old woman claimed the hoodoo had cursed her family. 老妇人声称巫毒诅咒了她的家人。|近义词：voodoo, hex, charm,  weirdness, mystery |反义词：normality, explanation, mundane
