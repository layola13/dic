pathologically:/ˌpæθəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 病态地；患病地；异常地 | In a way that shows the presence of disease or abnormality;  characterized by or suggestive of a disease.  | 例句：He was pathologically jealous of his brother. 他病态地嫉妒他的兄弟。

例句：Her behavior was pathologically selfish. 她的行为极其自私。


近义词：abnormally, morbidly, unhealthily, diseasedly

反义词：healthily, normally, sanely
