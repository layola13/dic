noria:/ˈnɔːriə/ | n. |努里亚（一种汲水装置）|A noria is a water-lifting device consisting of a large wheel with buckets attached to its rim, used to raise water from a river or well.  It is often powered by animals, water, or wind. |The ancient noria still worked, slowly turning to bring water to the parched fields.  古老的努里亚仍在工作，缓慢地转动，为干旱的田地带来水。|近义词：water wheel, sakia |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与现代抽水设备对比)|名词
