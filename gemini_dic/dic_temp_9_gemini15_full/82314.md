grousing:/ˈɡraʊzɪŋ/| v.|抱怨，牢骚，发牢骚|Complaining or grumbling, typically in a bad-tempered or discontented way.  |例句：He spent the whole afternoon grousing about his job. 他整个下午都在抱怨他的工作。
例句：The passengers were grousing about the delay.  乘客们都在抱怨延误。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, grumbling, whining, complaining|反义词：praising, complimenting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always grousing about the weather. = 他总是抱怨天气。
They grouse about their low salaries. = 他们抱怨他们的低工资。


一般过去时:
She groused about the long meeting. = 她抱怨冗长的会议。
He groused to his friend about his boss. = 他向朋友抱怨他的老板。


现在完成时:
They have been grousing all day. = 他们抱怨了一整天。
She has groused about this problem for weeks. = 她已经抱怨这个问题好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
He had groused about the unfair treatment before he resigned. = 他辞职前就抱怨过不公平的待遇。
They had groused repeatedly about the poor service. = 他们反复抱怨糟糕的服务。


将来时:
I know he will be grousing if we are late. = 我知道如果我们迟到他会抱怨。
They will grouse about the new rules. = 他们会抱怨新规定的。


虚拟语气:
If he knew how much work it was, he wouldn't be grousing. = 如果他知道有多少工作，他就不会抱怨了。
I wish he wouldn't grouse so much. = 我希望他不要抱怨那么多。


被动语态:
His complaints were often groused about by his colleagues.  = 他的抱怨经常被同事们议论。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with this verb)
The poor service was often groused about by customers. = 糟糕的服务经常受到顾客的抱怨。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with this verb, often rephrased)


疑问句:
Is he still grousing about the project? = 他还在抱怨那个项目吗？
Why are they grousing? = 他们为什么抱怨？


否定句:
He didn't grouse about the food. = 他没有抱怨食物。
They didn't grouse at all during the trip. = 他们在旅途中一点也没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
grouse about something = 抱怨某事
grouse to someone = 向某人抱怨
grouse and moan = 抱怨和呻吟
constantly grousing = 不停地抱怨
secretly grousing = 暗自抱怨
