obligatory:/ˈɒblɪɡətəri/ /ˈɑːblɪɡətɔːri/ | adj. | 义务的；强制的；必须的 |  Something that is obligatory is required by a rule or law, or considered essential. It must be done. | 例句：Attendance at the meeting is obligatory.  出席会议是必须的。

The wearing of seat belts is obligatory in most countries. 在大多数国家系安全带是强制性的。


近义词：compulsory, mandatory, required, necessary | 反义词：optional, voluntary
