declining:/dɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 下降的；衰退的；谢绝的 |  Describing something that is becoming smaller, weaker, or less important; also describing the act of refusing something politely. |

例句：
The company's profits are declining. = 公司的利润正在下降。

The population of the town is declining. = 这个城镇的人口正在减少。


近义词：decreasing, falling, dwindling, weakening, refusing, rejecting

反义词：increasing, rising, growing, strengthening, accepting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The number of students is declining. = 学生人数正在下降。
His health is declining steadily. = 他的健康状况正在稳步下降。

一般过去时:
The empire declined after the death of the emperor. = 皇帝死后，帝国衰落了。
He declined the offer of a job. = 他谢绝了这份工作。

现在完成时:
The value of the property has declined significantly. = 房产价值已大幅下降。
She has declined several invitations recently. = 她最近谢绝了几次邀请。

过去完成时:
The patient's condition had declined sharply before the surgery. = 手术前，病人的病情急剧恶化。
He had declined to comment on the allegations. = 他拒绝就这些指控发表评论。

将来时:
The economy is expected to decline next year. = 预计明年经济将下滑。
He will likely decline the invitation. = 他很可能会拒绝邀请。

虚拟语气:
If the trend continued to decline, we would face serious problems. = 如果趋势继续下降，我们将面临严重的问题。
I wish the noise level would decline. = 我希望噪音水平能降低。

被动语态:
The proposal was declined by the committee. = 该提案被委员会否决了。
His application for the scholarship was declined. = 他的奖学金申请被拒绝了。

疑问句:
Is the value of the stock declining? = 股票的价值是否在下跌？
Are you declining my offer? = 你在拒绝我的提议吗？

否定句:
The temperature is not declining; it's rising. = 气温没有下降，而是在上升。
He did not decline the opportunity to travel abroad. = 他没有拒绝出国旅行的机会。


PHRASE->
declining birth rate = 出生率下降

declining economy = 经济衰退
declining health = 健康状况恶化
declining population = 人口减少

词源:
From Old French *decliner, from Latin declinare "to bend, turn aside, deviate; decline," from de- "away, down" + clinare "to bend, incline."  The sense of "to refuse" developed later.
