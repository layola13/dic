deceived:/dɪˈsiːvd/| vt.|欺骗，蒙蔽|To deceive someone is to make them believe something that is not true, often in order to gain an advantage.  This involves misleading or tricking them.|例句：He deceived her into thinking he was rich. 他骗她相信自己很有钱。|例句：The magician deceived the audience with his skillful illusions. 魔术师用他娴熟的幻觉欺骗了观众。|近义词：trick, mislead, fool, cheat, defraud|反义词：reveal, enlighten, inform


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He deceives his clients regularly. = 他经常欺骗他的客户。
She deceives her parents about her whereabouts. = 她欺骗她的父母关于她的行踪。


一般过去时:
They deceived the police about their involvement. = 他们欺骗了警察关于他们的参与。
The salesman deceived many customers with false promises. = 销售员用虚假承诺欺骗了许多顾客。


现在完成时:
He has deceived us for years. = 他多年来一直欺骗我们。
She has deceived many people with her charm. = 她用她的魅力欺骗了许多人。


过去完成时:
I realised that I had been deceived from the start. = 我意识到从一开始就被欺骗了。
They discovered that they had been deceived by a fraudulent scheme. = 他们发现他们被一个欺诈计划欺骗了。


将来时:
He will deceive them if he gets the chance. = 如果他有机会，他会欺骗他们。
She will likely deceive her friends to protect her secret. = 她可能会欺骗她的朋友以保护她的秘密。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't deceived her, she wouldn't have lost her savings. = 如果他没有欺骗她，她就不会失去她的积蓄。
I wish I hadn't been deceived by his lies. = 我真希望我没有被他谎言欺骗。


被动语态:
The public were deceived by the false advertising. = 公众被虚假广告欺骗了。
Many investors were deceived by the investment scam. = 许多投资者被投资骗局欺骗了。


疑问句:
Were you deceived by his promises? = 你被他许下的诺言欺骗了吗？
Has anyone been deceived in this deal? = 在这笔交易中有人被欺骗了吗？


否定句:
He didn't deceive anyone; he was completely honest. = 他没有欺骗任何人；他是完全诚实的。
She wouldn't deceive her best friend. = 她不会欺骗她最好的朋友。



PHRASE->

deceive with = 用…欺骗
