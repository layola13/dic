phenolization:/ˌfɛnəʊlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |苯酚化；用苯酚处理|The process of treating something with phenol, typically to disinfect or preserve it.  This often involves the application of a phenol solution to kill microorganisms or prevent decay.|例句：The phenolization of the surgical instruments ensured sterility.  手术器械的苯酚处理确保了无菌性。

The phenolization of the wood helped preserve it from rot.  木材的苯酚处理有助于防止其腐烂。|近义词：disinfection, sterilization, preservation|反义词：contamination, decay
