macchiatos:/mɑːkiˈɑːtoʊz/| n.|玛奇朵（咖啡）|A macchiato is an espresso coffee drink that has a small amount of foamed milk added to it.  It is characterized by its distinct layers of espresso and milk foam. |Example: I ordered a caramel macchiato this morning. 今天早上我点了一杯焦糖玛奇朵。  I prefer macchiatos to lattes because they are less milky. 我更喜欢玛奇朵而不是拿铁，因为它们奶味没那么重。|近义词：espresso, latte (depending on the type of macchiato)|反义词：black coffee|名词
