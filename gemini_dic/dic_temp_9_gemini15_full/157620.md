pitier:/ˈpɪtiər/| n.|可怜的人，同情者|A pitier is someone who feels pity or compassion for others, often showing it through their actions or words.  They may be moved by the suffering of others and actively try to alleviate it. |例句：She was a pitier of the homeless, often donating food and clothing. 她是一个同情无家可归者的人，经常捐赠食物和衣服。|近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person|反义词：indifferent person, uncaring person
