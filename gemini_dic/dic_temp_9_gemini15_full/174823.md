races:/ˈreɪsɪz/| n.|种族；比赛；竞赛；速度；家族；血统|The word "races" refers to groups of people sharing common characteristics, typically inherited biological traits. It can also refer to competitions, often involving speed or skill.|例句：Many different races participated in the marathon.  许多不同的种族参加了马拉松比赛。


例句：The horse races were exciting to watch. 赛马观看起来很刺激。


近义词：ethnic groups, competitions, contests, speeds


反义词：None (the antonyms depend on the context.  For example, if referring to "races" as competitions, "cooperation" might be an antonym.)


词性:名词
