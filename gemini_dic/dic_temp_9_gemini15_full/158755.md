plowmen:/ˈplaʊmən/| n.|耕田人，犁地者|People who plow fields.  The word is the plural of plowman.|例句：The plowmen worked tirelessly from sunrise to sunset.  耕田人从日出到日落辛勤工作。

例句：Those strong plowmen could till the most stubborn soil. 那些强壮的耕田人能够耕种最顽固的土地。

近义词：farmers, tillers, agricultural workers
反义词：None directly applicable, but could consider words implying idleness or lack of agricultural work.
