reprioritizes:/ˌriːˌpraɪˈɔːrɪtaɪzɪz/| vt.|重新确定优先级，重新排序优先事项|To reconsider the priorities of (tasks, projects, etc.) and reorder them according to new criteria or circumstances.|The company had to reprioritize its projects after the market downturn. 公司在市场低迷后不得不重新安排项目的优先级。|近义词：re-evaluate, reorder, rearrange|反义词：maintain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reprioritizes his tasks daily. = 他每天都重新安排他的任务优先级。
She reprioritizes her workload based on urgency. = 她根据紧急程度重新安排工作量优先级。


一般过去时:
They reprioritized their goals after the merger. = 合并后，他们重新确定了目标的优先级。
The team reprioritized its projects following the feedback. = 团队根据反馈意见重新安排了项目的优先级。


现在完成时:
We have reprioritized our investments in renewable energy. = 我们已经重新调整了对可再生能源的投资优先级。
Management has reprioritized its focus on customer satisfaction. = 管理层已经重新将重点放在客户满意度上。


过去完成时:
They had reprioritized their budget before the crisis hit. = 在危机爆发之前，他们已经重新调整了预算的优先级。
She had reprioritized her research after receiving new data. = 在收到新的数据后，她已经重新安排了研究的优先级。


将来时:
We will reprioritize our spending next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度重新调整支出优先级。
They will reprioritize their product development strategy. = 他们将重新调整产品开发战略的优先级。


虚拟语气:
If the situation changed, we would reprioritize immediately. = 如果情况改变，我们将立即重新确定优先级。
I wish they had reprioritized their efforts sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新安排他们的工作重点。


被动语态:
The project was reprioritized due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，项目被重新确定了优先级。
The budget has been reprioritized to reflect the new strategy. = 预算已被重新调整以反映新战略。


疑问句:
Will the board reprioritize the funding? = 董事会会重新确定资金的优先级吗？
Has the team reprioritized its goals for this year? = 团队今年是否已重新确定目标的优先级？


否定句:
They did not reprioritize their commitments. = 他们没有重新安排他们的承诺优先级。
We will not reprioritize the project unless absolutely necessary. = 除非绝对必要，否则我们不会重新安排项目的优先级。
