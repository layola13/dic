flaggy:/ˈflæɡi/ | adj. | 柔软的；松软的；下垂的；无精打采的 |  Characterized by being weak, soft, or drooping; lacking firmness or stiffness.  It can also describe something that is excessively wet or damp. |

例句：

The flaggy plants drooped in the midday sun.  =  这些柔软的植物在中午的阳光下低垂着。

His flaggy muscles showed years of inactivity. = 他松弛的肌肉显示出多年的不活动。


近义词：limp, flabby, drooping, weak, soggy


反义词：firm, stiff, strong, rigid


词源：源自中古英语 *flaggy，来自古英语 *flacc，意思是“下垂的，松弛的”。  The word ultimately relates to the concept of "flagging" or becoming weaker.
