nabbing:/ˈnæbɪŋ/| n. v.|抓住；逮捕；夺取|The act of seizing or capturing someone or something.  It can refer to a quick, often informal, arrest or capture.  The word also implies a sense of stealth or suddenness.|例句：The police made a quick nabbing of the suspect near the bank.  警察在银行附近迅速抓获了嫌疑人。
The cat sat patiently, waiting for the perfect moment for nabbing the mouse. 猫耐心地坐着，等待抓住老鼠的最佳时机。|近义词：grabbing, seizing, capturing, arresting|反义词：releasing, freeing, letting go
