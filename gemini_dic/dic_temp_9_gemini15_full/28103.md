capitalisms:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪzəm/| n. |资本主义|An economic and political system in which a country's trade and industry are controlled by private owners for profit, rather than by the state.|例句：Many countries have embraced capitalism as a way to foster economic growth. 许多国家都采用资本主义来促进经济增长。|近义词：free enterprise, market economy|反义词：socialism, communism
