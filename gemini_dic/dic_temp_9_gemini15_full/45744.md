curvograph:/ˈkɜːrvəɡræf/ | n. | 曲线图，曲线描绘器 | A curvograph is a device or graph that visually represents curved lines or data, often used to show the relationship between two variables. It can also refer to a record or image produced by such a device.  | Example: The curvograph clearly showed the exponential growth of the company's profits.  曲线图清楚地显示了公司利润的指数增长。 |近义词: curve chart, graph, diagram | 反义词:  straight line graph (as a contrasting concept)
