buttonwood:/ˈbʌtənˌwʊd/ | n. | 棕榈树；美国梧桐树 | American sycamore; a North American tree (Platanus occidentalis) having a mottled bark that peels off in large plates.  The wood is used for various purposes including buttons and furniture. | The buttonwood tree's leaves turned vibrant shades of yellow and orange in the autumn.  这棵美国梧桐树的叶子在秋天变成了鲜艳的黄色和橙色。 | sycamore, plane tree |  | n.
