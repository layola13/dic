coop:/kʊp/| n. |鸡舍，畜栏；合作社|A coop is a small building or enclosure for keeping chickens or other poultry.  It can also refer to a cooperative organization.|Example: The chickens are safe in their coop at night.  鸡晚上在鸡舍里很安全。 The farmers formed a cooperative to sell their produce. 农民们组建了一个合作社来销售他们的农产品。|近义词：henhouse, pen, poultry house |反义词：N/A
