lentamente:/lenˈtɑːmenti/| adv.|缓慢地，慢慢地|Slowly, gradually, at a slow pace.  The adverb "lentamente" indicates a slow manner or speed. It is often used to describe actions, movements, or processes that occur at a deliberate and unhurried pace.|例句：He walked lentamente down the street. 他慢慢地走下街道。

例句：She spoke lentamente so everyone could understand. 她慢慢地说，以便每个人都能理解。

近义词：slowly, gradually, leisurely, softly

反义词：quickly, rapidly, swiftly, speedily

词性：副词
