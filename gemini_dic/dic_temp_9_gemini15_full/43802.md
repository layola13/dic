crenature:/ˈkriːneɪtʃər/| n. |波纹状，齿状纹理|A series of small, regular indentations or scallops along an edge or surface, resembling the teeth of a comb or saw.  It often refers to a decorative or naturally occurring pattern.|The fabric had a delicate crenature along its border. 这块布料的边缘有精细的波纹状纹理。The coastline featured a dramatic crenature of cliffs and bays. 海岸线呈现出戏剧性的悬崖和海湾的锯齿状纹理。|近义词：scalloping, fluting, undulation|反义词：smoothness, flatness|名词
