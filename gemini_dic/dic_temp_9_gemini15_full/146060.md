overdye:/ˈoʊvərdaɪ/| vt.|复染，加染|To dye (something) again, usually with a different color, to change or enhance the original color.|The fabric was overdyed to create a more vibrant hue.  织物经过复染，创造出更鲜艳的色调。|She overdyed her old jeans to give them a new look. 她复染了她旧牛仔裤，给它们一个新的面貌。|dye, re-dye|bleach, undye|动词


USAGE->

They overdye the fabric to create a unique pattern. = 他们复染织物以创造独特的图案。
The artist overdyes her silk scarves with natural dyes. = 艺术家用天然染料复染她的丝巾。

She overdyed her dress a darker shade of blue. = 她把她的裙子复染成深蓝色。
