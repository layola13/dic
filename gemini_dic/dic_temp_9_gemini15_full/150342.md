panicky: /ˈpænɪki/ | adj. | 惊慌的，恐慌的 | Feeling or showing sudden and uncontrolled fear.  | 例句：He became panicky when he realized he was lost. 他意识到自己迷路了，变得惊慌失措。 | 例句：The panicky crowd rushed for the exits. 惊慌的人群冲向出口。 | 近义词：frantic, hysterical, agitated, alarmed | 反义词：calm, composed, tranquil, serene
