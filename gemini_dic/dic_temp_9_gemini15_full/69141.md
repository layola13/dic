favourable: /ˈfeɪvərəbl/ | adj. |有利的；顺利的；赞成的；讨人喜欢的|

英文解析:  Giving a feeling of approval or support; positive.  Something favourable is advantageous or likely to produce a good result. It can also describe something that is pleasing or attractive.

例句:

The court gave a favourable ruling. = 法院做出了有利的裁决。

The weather forecast is favourable for our picnic tomorrow. = 明天的天气预报对我们的野餐有利。


近义词: advantageous, positive, supportive, propitious, auspicious, pleasing, agreeable

反义词: unfavourable, negative, adverse, unpropitious, inauspicious, displeasing, disagreeable


词源:  From Old French *favorable, from Latin *favorabilis, from favor "favor, kindness".
