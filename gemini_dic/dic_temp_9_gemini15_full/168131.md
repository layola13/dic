pro-Armenians: /ˌproʊ ˈɑːrmiːniənz/ | adj. n. | 支持亚美尼亚人的；亚美尼亚支持者 |  Supporting or favoring the Armenians.  This term can refer to individuals, groups, or policies that advocate for Armenian interests, often in the context of historical events like the Armenian Genocide or current political issues relating to Armenia and Artsakh. |

例句：

Many pro-Armenians organizations actively work to raise awareness about the Armenian Genocide.  许多支持亚美尼亚人的组织积极致力于提高人们对亚美尼亚种族灭绝的认识。


The pro-Armenians sentiment in the international community is strong. 国际社会对亚美尼亚人的同情情绪强烈。


近义词：Armenian sympathizers, Armenian supporters, pro-Armenian activists

反义词：pro-Azerbaijani, pro-Turkish (in the context of the Armenian Genocide and related conflicts)

词性：形容词，名词 (作名词时通常用复数形式)
