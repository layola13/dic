obloquies:/ˈɒbləkwiz/| n.|毁谤；恶言；辱骂|Public obloquies followed his scandalous behavior.  公开的辱骂紧随其不光彩的行为之后。|近义词：reproaches, condemnations, insults, aspersions|反义词：praise, commendation, compliments|名词

否定句:
