incubates:/ˈɪŋkjuˌbeɪts/| vt.|孵化；培养；酝酿|To incubate means to keep eggs warm until they hatch, or to provide the conditions for something to develop.  It can also refer to the development of an idea or plan. |例句：The hen incubates her eggs for three weeks. 母鸡孵蛋三周。

The scientist incubates the bacteria in a controlled environment. 科学家在受控环境中培养细菌。


近义词：hatch, brood, foster, nurture|反义词：destroy, hinder, stifle|动词
