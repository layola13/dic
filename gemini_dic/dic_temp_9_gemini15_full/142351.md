orthokinesis:/ˌɔːrθoʊkaɪˈniːsɪs/ | n. |正向运动，直动性|Movement in a straight line in response to a stimulus.  This is often seen in simple organisms.  It differs from klinokinesis, which involves changes in speed or turning rate.|Example: The amoeba exhibited orthokinesis, moving directly towards the light source.  变形虫表现出正向运动，直接朝向光源移动。|近义词：straight movement, direct movement|反义词：klinokinesis (转向运动)|名词
