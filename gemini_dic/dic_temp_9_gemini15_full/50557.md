deputising: /ˈdɛpjʊtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 代理；暂时替代 | Acting in the place of someone else, temporarily.  |  例句：He's deputising for the manager while she's on holiday. 他在经理休假期间代理经理职务。

例句：The junior doctor was deputising for the consultant.  这位年轻医生正在代理顾问医生的工作。


近义词：substituting, acting for, standing in for, filling in for

反义词：None directly opposite, but the antonym would be the concept of "not acting in place of someone".


词性：动词 (gerund/present participle)
