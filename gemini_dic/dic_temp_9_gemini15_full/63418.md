entrant:/ˈɛntrənt/| n.|参赛者，进入者|A person who enters a competition or a place.  |例句：She was a strong entrant in the race. 她是一位实力强大的参赛者。|例句：The entrant to the building needed a security pass. 进入大楼的人需要安全通行证。|近义词：competitor, participant, newcomer |反义词：spectator, observer


USAGE->

He is a regular entrant in the marathon. = 他是马拉松赛的常客。
