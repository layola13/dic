rocker:/ˈrɒkə(r)/|/ˈrɑːkər/| n. |摇滚乐手；摇摆者；摇动器；（椅子等的）摇臂 |A rocker is a person who plays rock music, especially in a band.  It can also refer to something that rocks or is used for rocking, such as a rocking chair or a rocking device. |例句：He's a famous rocker from the 1970s. 他是70年代一位著名的摇滚乐手。  The baby enjoyed sitting in the rocker. 婴儿喜欢坐在摇椅里。|近义词：musician, performer, oscillator |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rocks the baby to sleep every night. = 他每天晚上都摇着婴儿睡觉。
The chair rocks gently back and forth. = 椅子轻轻地前后摇晃。


一般过去时:
The band rocked the stadium last night. = 乐队昨晚在体育场进行了精彩的演出。
She rocked the baby until it fell asleep. = 她摇晃着婴儿直到它睡着。


现在完成时:
They have rocked the music world with their new album. = 他们凭借新专辑震撼了音乐界。
He has rocked that chair until it's broken. = 他把那把椅子摇坏了。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
