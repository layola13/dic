/ˌaʊtˈroʊɡd/ | adj. | 被超越的；被胜出的 |  Outrogued means to have been outmaneuvered or outsmarted in a strategic contest, typically involving cunning or deception. It implies a defeat due to superior tactics rather than superior force. |  The chess champion felt outrogued by his opponent's unexpected strategy.  国际象棋冠军感觉自己被对手出其不意的策略击败了。 |  outwitted, outmaneuvered, outplayed |  outperformed (in a less cunning context) |  adj.
