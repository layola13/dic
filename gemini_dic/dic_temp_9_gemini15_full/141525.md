oppilation:/ˌɒpɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |阻塞；闭塞；堵塞 |The obstruction or closure of a natural passage, especially in the body.  This term is largely obsolete in modern medicine, having been replaced by more specific terms depending on the location and cause of the obstruction.|例句：The physician suspected an oppilation of the bile duct causing the patient's jaundice.  医生怀疑胆管阻塞导致了病人的黄疸。|近义词：obstruction, blockage, occlusion, closure |反义词：opening, clearance
