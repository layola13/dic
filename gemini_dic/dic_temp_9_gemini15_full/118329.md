merged:/mɜːrdʒd/| adj. v. |合并的；融合的|Combined or united to form a single entity.  The merged company will be the largest in the industry. |近义词：combined, united, amalgamated |反义词：separated, divided, distinct |形容词，动词


英文解析:
As an adjective, "merged" describes something that has been combined or united to form a single entity.  As a verb, "merged" is the past tense and past participle of "merge," meaning to combine or blend together.  The context determines whether it's functioning as an adjective or a verb.


例句:
The two companies merged to create a global giant. = 两家公司合并，创造了一个全球巨头。

Their voices merged into a single, harmonious sound. = 他们的声音融合成了一种和谐的声音。


近义词：
combined, united, amalgamated, blended, consolidated, integrated


反义词：
separated, divided, distinct, segregated, unconnected


词性:
Adjective, Verb (past tense and past participle)
