single-handednesses:/ˈsɪŋɡəlˈhændɪdnəsɪz/| n. |单手操作能力；独自完成的能力|The ability to perform tasks or accomplish things using only one hand, or the quality of being able to do things alone.  This word is rarely used and often considered awkward.  It implies a focus on the ability, rather than the act itself.  A more common way to express this would be to describe the act of doing something single-handedly. |The surgeon's single-handednesses were evident in the complex operation.  这位外科医生的单手操作能力在复杂的手术中显而易见。

The remarkable single-handednesses of the artist allowed him to create intricate sculptures. 艺术家非凡的单手操作能力使他能够创作出精美的雕塑。|近义词：dexterity (with one hand), self-reliance, independence |反义词：dependence, reliance on others, teamwork
