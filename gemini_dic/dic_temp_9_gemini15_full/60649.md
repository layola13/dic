election:/ɪˈlɛkʃən/ | n. |选举，推选 |The process of choosing someone for a position, usually by voting.  It can refer to the act of voting itself, the campaign leading up to the vote, or the result of the vote. | Example: The presidential election is next month.  下个月是总统大选。|近义词：vote, poll, ballot |反义词：appointment (in some contexts)
