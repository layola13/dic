colostomies:/kəˈlɒstəmiːz/| n. |结肠造口术（复数）|The surgical creation of an artificial opening (stoma) in the colon, bringing one end to the surface of the abdomen to allow for the passage of feces.|例句：The patient required colostomies after extensive bowel resection.  病人需要进行结肠造口术，因为进行了大范围的肠切除手术。|例句：Multiple colostomies were performed to manage severe intestinal damage.  为了处理严重的肠道损伤，进行了多次结肠造口术。|近义词：colostomy (单数), bowel diversion|反义词：colostomy reversal (结肠造口术逆转)|名词
