deckhands:/ˈdɛkˌhændz/| n.|甲板工人，水手|A deckhand is a member of a ship's crew who works on deck.  Deckhands perform a variety of tasks, including assisting with mooring, loading and unloading cargo, maintaining the deck, and handling lines. |例句：The deckhands worked tirelessly to secure the ship during the storm. 甲板工人不辞辛劳地在风暴中固定船只。

例句：Experienced deckhands are in high demand in the shipping industry.  航运业对经验丰富的甲板工人需求很大。|近义词：sailor, seaman, hand|反义词：captain, officer
