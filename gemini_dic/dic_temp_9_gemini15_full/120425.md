minivans:/ˈmɪnɪvænz/| n. |小型货车|A minivan is a type of vehicle that is larger than a car but smaller than a van, typically having three rows of seats and sliding doors.  They are often used for families because of their spaciousness.|例句：Many families choose minivans for their practicality and ability to carry many passengers. 许多家庭选择小型货车是因为其实用性和载客能力。|例句：Our minivan is perfect for road trips because it has plenty of space for luggage and passengers. 我们的面包车非常适合公路旅行，因为它有足够的空间容纳行李和乘客。|近义词：minibus, MPV (multi-purpose vehicle)|反义词：sports car, convertible
