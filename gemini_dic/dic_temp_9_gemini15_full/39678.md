conserving:/kənˈsɜːrvɪŋ/| v.| 保护；保存；节约 | To protect something from harm or destruction; to keep something safe; to use something sparingly. | 例句：We need to conserve our natural resources. 我们需要保护我们的自然资源。| 例句：She is conserving energy by turning off the lights. 她正在通过关灯来节约能源。|近义词：preserving, protecting, saving |反义词：wasting, squandering, depleting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They conserve water by taking shorter showers. = 他们通过洗更短的淋浴来节约用水。
We conserve energy by using energy-efficient appliances. = 我们通过使用节能电器来节约能源。

一般过去时:
She conserved her strength for the final race. = 她为最后的比赛保存体力。
They conserved food by canning and preserving it. = 他们通过罐装和保存食物来保存食物。

现在完成时:
We have conserved a significant amount of money this year. = 今年我们已经节省了一大笔钱。
They have conserved the old building by restoring it. = 他们通过修复这座古老的建筑物来保护它。

过去完成时:
She had conserved all her energy for the marathon. = 她已经为马拉松赛保存了所有精力。
They had conserved the forest by preventing deforestation. = 他们通过防止森林砍伐来保护森林。

将来时:
We will conserve more water next year. = 明年我们将节约更多用水。
They will conserve energy by switching to renewable sources. = 他们将通过转向可再生能源来节约能源。

虚拟语气:
If we conserved more resources, we would have a better environment. = 如果我们节约更多的资源，我们将拥有更好的环境。
I wish we had conserved more energy in the past. = 我希望我们过去能节约更多的能源。

被动语态:
Natural resources are conserved through government regulations. = 自然资源通过政府法规得到保护。
Energy is conserved by using efficient technologies. = 通过使用高效技术来节约能源。

疑问句:
How can we conserve water effectively? = 我们如何才能有效地节约用水？
What measures are being taken to conserve biodiversity? = 正在采取什么措施来保护生物多样性？

否定句:
They did not conserve enough fuel for the journey. = 他们为旅程没有节约足够的燃料。
We haven't conserved enough money for our vacation. = 我们还没有为假期存够钱。
