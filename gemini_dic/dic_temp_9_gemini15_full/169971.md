protactic:/proʊˈtæktɪk/ | adj. | 并列的；并置的；（句法）左向递归的 |  Pertaining to a grammatical construction in which modifiers precede the words they modify, or to a sentence structure in which phrases or clauses are juxtaposed without subordination.  It often implies a lack of complex sentence structure. | The child's protactic sentence structure showed a lack of grammatical sophistication.  这个孩子的并列句式结构显示出语法水平的不足。| 近义词：paratactic, coordinate | 反义词：hypotactic
