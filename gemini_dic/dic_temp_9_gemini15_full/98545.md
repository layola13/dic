instillatory: /ɪnˈstɪlətəri/ | adj. | 灌输的；滴注的 | Relating to the instillation or dropping of liquids, especially into a body cavity.  It can also refer to the act or process of instilling ideas or principles. |

例句：

The doctor used an instillatory device to administer the medication.  医生使用滴注装置来施用药物。

His speeches were instillatory, aimed at shaping the public's opinion. 他的演讲具有灌输性，旨在塑造公众舆论。


近义词：infusional, implanting (for ideas), instilling


反义词：extracting (for liquids), removing (for ideas)


词性：形容词
