hexadecimals:/ˌhɛksəˈdɛsɪməlz/| n. |十六进制数|Hexadecimals are a base-16 number system, using sixteen distinct symbols (0-9 and A-F) to represent numbers.  They are frequently used in computer science and digital electronics because they provide a compact way to represent binary data.|Example: The hexadecimal color code #FF0000 represents red.  十六进制颜色代码 #FF0000 代表红色。|近义词：base-16 numbers|反义词：decimal, binary
