doublet:/ˈdʌblɪt/| n.|双联词，成对的事物，紧身衣|A doublet is a word that has the same origin as another word, but has developed a different meaning.  It can also refer to a pair of similar things or a close-fitting jacket worn by men in the 16th century.|例句：The words "nice" and "precise" are etymologically related, forming a doublet.  双词“nice”和“precise”在词源上相关，构成一对双联词。


例句：He wore a fine doublet to the ball. 他穿着考究的紧身衣去参加舞会。


近义词：pair, couple, duplicate|反义词：single, solitary

词性：名词
