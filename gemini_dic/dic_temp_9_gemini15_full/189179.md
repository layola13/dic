sacrament:/ˈsækrəmənt/| n.|圣礼，圣事|A sacred ritual or ceremony, especially one that is considered to be a means of grace, such as baptism or the Eucharist.  It often involves symbolic actions and words believed to convey spiritual blessings or effects.|例句：Baptism is a sacrament in many Christian churches.  洗礼在许多基督教教堂里是一个圣礼。
例句：The Eucharist is considered the most important sacrament in Catholicism. 圣餐被认为是天主教最重要的圣礼。|近义词：rite, ordinance, ceremony|反义词：profane, secular|名词
