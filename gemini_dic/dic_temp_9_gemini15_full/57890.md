driftage:/ˈdrɪftɪdʒ/| n.|漂流物；漂移；冲积物 |The debris carried by the current, including wood, seaweed, and other floating materials.| 例句：The driftage on the beach after the storm was extensive.  暴风雨过后海滩上的漂流物非常多。 The river's driftage included sand and small stones.  河里的冲积物包括沙子和细小的石头。|近义词：flotsam, jetsam, wreckage|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to fixed or stationary objects.|名词
