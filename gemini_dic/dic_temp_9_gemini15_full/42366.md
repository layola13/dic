counterassaults:/ˌkaʊntərəˈsɔːlts/| n.|反攻，反击|A counterassault is an attack made in response to an earlier attack.  It's a military or figurative action taken to retaliate against an offensive.|例句：The enemy launched a fierce counterassault after our initial advance.  敌人在我们最初推进后发起了猛烈的反攻。

例句：The company's counterassault against the negative publicity was swift and effective.  该公司对负面宣传的反击迅速而有效。


近义词：counterattack, retaliation, reprisal|反义词：surrender, retreat, capitulation|词性：noun
