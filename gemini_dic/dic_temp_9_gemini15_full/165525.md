premaniacal: /ˌpriːmænˈeɪəkəl/ | adj. | 近乎疯狂的，狂躁前的 |  Describing behavior or a state that suggests an imminent onset of mania or madness; exhibiting characteristics that precede a manic episode.  It implies a build-up to a state of intense excitement or frenzy. | 例句：His premaniacal laughter chilled everyone in the room.  他近乎疯狂的笑声让屋里每个人都感到不寒而栗。  The premaniacal gleam in his eyes suggested impending violence. 他眼中近乎疯狂的光芒预示着即将到来的暴力。| 近义词：unhinged, frenzied, frantic, deranged, unstable | 反义词：calm, composed, serene, tranquil, peaceful | 词性：形容词
