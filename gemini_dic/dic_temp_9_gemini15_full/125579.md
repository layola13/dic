multitracking:/ˈmʌltitrækiŋ/ | n. |多轨录音，多轨录制 |The process of recording multiple audio tracks separately and then combining them.  This allows for greater flexibility and control in the production of music or other audio.  |例句：Multitracking allows musicians to layer instruments and vocals for a richer sound. 多轨录音使音乐家能够分层乐器和人声以获得更丰富的声音。

例句：The producer used multitracking to create a complex and layered soundscape for the song. 制作人使用多轨录音为歌曲创造了一个复杂且分层的音景。

近义词：multi-track recording, layered recording |反义词：monotracking (单轨录音)


词性: 名词
