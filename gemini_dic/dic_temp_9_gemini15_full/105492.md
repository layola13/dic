kilned:/kɪld/| adj.|烧制过的；窑烧的|Having been subjected to the process of firing in a kiln.  It typically refers to clay products or other materials hardened by heat in a kiln.|例句：The kilned bricks were strong and durable. 烧制过的砖块坚固耐用。
The kilned pottery displayed intricate designs. 窑烧的陶器展现出复杂的图案。|近义词：fired, baked,  heat-treated|反义词：unfired, raw
