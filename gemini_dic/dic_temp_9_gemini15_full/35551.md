coalface:/ˈkoʊlfeɪs/| n. |煤矿工作面；最前线；最艰苦的工作岗位|The place in a mine where coal is being extracted; the point of direct contact with a difficult or challenging situation.  It often refers to the most demanding or difficult aspects of a job or situation.|例句：The miners worked tirelessly at the coalface. 矿工们在煤矿工作面辛勤工作。|例句：She's been at the coalface of the project for months, dealing with endless problems. 她几个月来一直奋战在项目的最前线，处理着无尽的问题。|近义词：front line, forefront, cutting edge|反义词：rear, background
