capriciously:/kəˈprɪʃəsli/| adv.|反复无常地；任性地；变化无常地 | acting in a capricious manner; unpredictably; erratically.  |例句：The weather changed capriciously throughout the day, shifting from sunshine to rain and back again.  天气在一天中反复无常地变化，一会儿阳光明媚，一会儿又下起雨来。

例句：He capriciously decided to quit his job and travel the world. 他反复无常地决定辞去工作，周游世界。


近义词：whimsically, erratically, unpredictably, arbitrarily, fitfully


反义词：consistently, predictably, steadily, reliably


词性:副词
