re-agitate:/ˌriːˈædʒɪteɪt/| vt.|再次激怒；重新煽动|To stir up again; to excite or disturb anew; to agitate again.|例句：The news re-agitated his already frayed nerves.  这条新闻再次激怒了他本已脆弱的神经。|例句：The politician tried to re-agitate the public's anger over the issue.  这位政治家试图再次煽动公众对这个问题的怒火。|近义词：re-excite, rekindle, inflame|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|动词
