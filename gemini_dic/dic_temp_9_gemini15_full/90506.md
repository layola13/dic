huddle:/ˈhʌd.əl/| n. v.|挤作一团；窃窃私语；(尤指因寒冷或害怕而) 挤在一起；混乱的一堆|To come close together in a group, especially because of cold or fear.  A small, close group of people or things.|The players huddled together to discuss their strategy. 球员们挤在一起讨论策略。  The sheep huddled together for warmth. 羊群挤在一起取暖。|gather, cluster, bunch|scatter, disperse|名词，动词
