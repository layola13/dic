postcivilization: /ˌpoʊstˌsɪvɪləˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 后文明时期；文明崩溃后的社会 |  A hypothetical societal condition following the collapse of a complex, technologically advanced civilization.  It often involves a reversion to simpler ways of life, potentially with a loss of technological capabilities and societal structures.  The exact nature of a post-civilization society is highly speculative and varies greatly depending on the context and the cause of the civilization's collapse. | Example:  The novel depicts a bleak postcivilization landscape, where remnants of the old world struggle to survive amidst ruins.  = 这部小说描绘了一幅凄凉的后文明景象，旧世界残余的人们在废墟中挣扎求生。


The study of postcivilization scenarios often explores potential societal changes, environmental impacts, and the long-term consequences of technological advancement and societal breakdown. = 对后文明情景的研究常常探索潜在的社会变化、环境影响以及技术进步和社会崩溃的长期后果。| 近义词：post-apocalyptic,  after-civilization | 反义词：civilization,  advanced society |  名词
