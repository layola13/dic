sabbatical:/sæˈbætɪkəl/ | n. |学术休假；安息年 | A sabbatical is a period of leave granted to a university teacher or other employee for study or research, typically one year.  It is often used to pursue advanced studies, conduct research, or work on a significant project. |She took a sabbatical to write her novel. 她休学术假去写小说。 He's on sabbatical this semester. 他这个学期正在休学术假。 | leave of absence, furlough | work, employment |
