orthogonal:/ɔːrˈθɑːɡənəl/| adj.|正交的；垂直的；相互独立的|Perpendicular; statistically unrelated.|The lines are orthogonal to each other.  这些线相互垂直。|The two research projects are orthogonal; they don't affect each other. 这两个研究项目是相互独立的；它们不会互相影响。|parallel, independent|oblique, dependent, correlated
