pater:/ˈpeɪtər/| n.|父亲；教父；（天主教）主祷文|The word "pater" is the Latin word for "father." It is the origin of many words in English and other Romance languages related to fatherhood.  It's also used in the Lord's Prayer. |Example:  He is a paterfamilias, the head of his household. 他是家长，家里的顶梁柱。|近义词：father, dad, papa, sire|反义词：son, child
