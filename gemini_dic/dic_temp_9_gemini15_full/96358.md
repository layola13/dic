indirectly:/ˌɪnˈdaɪrɛktli/| adv.|间接地，非直接地|In a way that is not direct or straightforward; not directly.  It implies a lack of directness or immediacy in the cause-effect relationship or in the communication itself.|例句：He indirectly influenced the decision by suggesting alternative solutions. 他通过建议替代方案间接地影响了这个决定。

例句：The sun indirectly heats the earth's atmosphere. 太阳间接地加热地球的大气层。


近义词：circuitously, indirectly, obliquely, roundaboutly, implicitly|反义词：directly, explicitly
