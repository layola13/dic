what:/wɒt/|ˈhwɑːt/| pron.|什么；多么；怎么|It is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something.  It can also be used to express the degree or extent of something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！


近义词：which, how


反义词：无


词性:代词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What time did the movie start? = 电影几点开始？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you came? = 你来之前吃了什么？
What had they already discussed? = 他们之前已经讨论了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会如何？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望知道该做什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想要什么。

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you were me? = 如果你是我，你会怎么做？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What will be done about this problem? = 这个问题将如何解决？
What was being discussed when I arrived? = 我到达时正在讨论什么？

疑问句:
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？

否定句:
I don't know what happened. = 我不知道发生了什么。
She didn't tell me what she thought. = 她没有告诉我她的想法。
