maligns:/məˈlaɪnz/| vt.|诽谤；中伤；污蔑 |To speak false and malicious statements about someone or something; to defame or slander.  |例句：He maligns his rivals in the press. 他在媒体上诽谤他的竞争对手。|例句：Don't malign her reputation; she's a good person. 不要诽谤她的名誉；她是个好人。|近义词：slander, defame, vilify, traduce |反义词：praise, commend, laud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maligns his opponents daily. = 他每天都诽谤他的对手。
She maligns her colleagues behind their backs. = 她背地里诽谤她的同事。


一般过去时:
The newspaper maliciously maligned the politician. = 这家报纸恶意诽谤了这位政治家。
He maligned her character in his testimony. = 他在他的证词中诽谤了她的品格。


现在完成时:
They have maligned the company's reputation for years. = 他们多年来一直在诽谤这家公司的声誉。
She has maligned her former friend in several interviews. = 她在几次采访中都诽谤了她的前朋友。


过去完成时:
He had maligned her for months before she finally confronted him. = 在她最终与他对质之前，他已经诽谤了她几个月了。
The article had maligned the scientist's work before the retraction was issued. = 在文章被撤回之前，它已经诽谤了这位科学家的工作。


将来时:
He will malign his rivals again in the upcoming election. = 在即将到来的选举中，他将再次诽谤他的对手。
She will likely malign her competitors to gain an advantage. = 她很可能会诽谤她的竞争对手以获得优势。


虚拟语气:
If he maligned her, he would be sued. = 如果他诽谤了她，他会被起诉。
I wish he hadn't maligned my family. = 我真希望他没有诽谤我的家人。


被动语态:
His name was maligned by the media. = 他的名字被媒体诽谤了。
The company's reputation was maligned by false accusations. = 该公司的声誉被虚假指控所玷污。


疑问句:
Did he malign her? = 他诽谤了她吗？
Who maligned the company's product? = 谁诽谤了这家公司的产品？


否定句:
He did not malign her; he defended her. = 他没有诽谤她；他为她辩护。
She wouldn't malign her friends, even if provoked. = 她不会诽谤她的朋友，即使被激怒了。
