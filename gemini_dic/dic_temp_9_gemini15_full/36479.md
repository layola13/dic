coleads:/ˈkoʊliːdz/ | n. | 共同领导，联合领导 |  Coleads refer to two or more individuals who share leadership responsibilities for a project, team, or organization.  They work collaboratively to guide and direct efforts towards a common goal.  This contrasts with a single leader structure. | Example: The marketing campaign had two coleads, each responsible for a different aspect of the strategy.  共同领导营销活动，每人负责战略的不同方面。|近义词：joint leaders, co-leaders, team leaders|反义词：sole leader, single leader|名词
