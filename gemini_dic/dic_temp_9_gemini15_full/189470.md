saintliness: /ˈseɪntlinəs/ | n. |圣洁；圣德；神圣 | The quality or state of being saintly; holiness; devoutness.  It refers to the exemplary virtue, piety, and moral excellence associated with saints.  |例句：Her saintliness was evident in her selfless dedication to helping others.  她的圣洁体现在她无私奉献于帮助他人。

例句：The saintliness of the monk inspired many to follow his path.  这位僧侣的圣洁感召了许多人追随他的道路。


近义词：holiness, piety, godliness, sanctity, righteousness

反义词：wickedness, sinfulness, depravity, ungodliness
