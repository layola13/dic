intombment:/ɪnˈtuːmbmənt/| n. |埋葬；入土；下葬 |The act of placing a dead body in a tomb or grave.  It often involves a ceremony and the final resting place of the deceased. |例句：The intombment of the king was a grand and solemn affair. 国王的安葬是一场盛大而庄严的仪式。

The intombment took place at dawn, attended only by close family.  下葬仪式在黎明时分举行，只有家人参加。|近义词：burial, interment, sepulture |反义词：exhumation
