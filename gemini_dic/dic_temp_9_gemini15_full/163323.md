preconfining: /ˌpriːkənˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先限制的，预先约束的 |  Describes something that restricts or confines something else before a particular event or process.  It implies a proactive limitation, often to control or manage a situation. | 例句：The preconfining of resources led to difficulties later in the project. 预先限制资源导致项目后期出现困难。

The preconfining regulations prevented any unauthorized access. 预先的规章制度阻止了任何未经授权的访问。


近义词：prelimiting, predetermining, preconstraining | 反义词：unrestricted, free, open


词性: adjective
