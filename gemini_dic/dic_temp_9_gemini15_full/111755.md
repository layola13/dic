lodes:/loʊdz/| n.|矿脉；引导；指引|A lode is a vein of metal ore in rock.|例句：The miners followed the lode deep into the mountain. 矿工们沿着矿脉深入山里。|近义词：vein, seam, guide, lead|反义词：N/A
