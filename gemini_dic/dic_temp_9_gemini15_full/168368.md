proa:/ˈproʊə/| n.|独木舟，双体船|A proa is a type of sailing vessel, particularly from the Pacific Islands, characterized by its asymmetrical hull and outrigger.|The ancient Polynesian proa was incredibly fast and efficient for navigating the open ocean.|近义词：outrigger canoe, sailing canoe|反义词：monohull
