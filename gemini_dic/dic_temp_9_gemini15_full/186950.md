rippled:/ˈrɪpəld/| adj. v.|起涟漪的；波动的；有波纹的|Having a slightly uneven surface with small waves or lines.  The word describes a surface texture or a movement. |例句：The pond rippled gently in the breeze.  池塘在微风中轻轻荡漾。


The silk scarf had a beautifully rippled texture. 这条丝巾有着美丽的波纹质感。

近义词：undulating, wavy, corrugated, wrinkled|反义词：smooth, flat, even|形容词，动词
