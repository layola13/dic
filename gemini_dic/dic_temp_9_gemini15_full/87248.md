hereupon:/ˌhɪərəˈpɒn/ | adv. |于是，因此，在此之后 |Upon this; as a consequence of this; immediately after this.  It indicates a consequence or an immediate action following a preceding event.  |例句：He received the news and thereupon immediately left. 他收到消息，于是立刻离开了。
The judge heard the evidence and thereupon delivered his verdict. 审判员听取了证据，于是宣判了判决。|近义词：therefore, consequently, subsequently, then, immediately |反义词：previously, beforehand, earlier


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives good news and thereupon smiles. = 他收到好消息，于是笑了。
She finishes her work and thereupon goes home. = 她完成工作，于是回家了。


一般过去时:
He received the letter and thereupon replied. = 他收到信后，立刻回复了。
They heard the alarm and thereupon evacuated the building. = 他们听到警报声后，于是疏散了大楼。

虚拟语气:
If he were to receive the promotion, he would thereupon resign. = 如果他获得晋升，他就会辞职。
If she were to win the competition, she would thereupon celebrate with her friends. = 如果她赢得比赛，她就会和朋友们一起庆祝。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not thereupon change his mind. = 他并没有因此改变主意。
She did not thereupon leave the country. = 她并没有因此离开国家。
