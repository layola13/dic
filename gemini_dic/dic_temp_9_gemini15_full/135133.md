nonoffensive:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛnsɪv/| adj. |不冒犯的，无伤大雅的|Not likely to cause offense or displeasure; inoffensive.  This word describes something that is unlikely to upset or annoy people.  It emphasizes the absence of anything that could be considered rude, disrespectful, or controversial.|

例句：

He tried to find a nonoffensive way to express his criticism. 他试图找到一种不冒犯人的方式来表达他的批评。


The speaker chose her words carefully to ensure her presentation was nonoffensive. 演讲者仔细斟酌词语，以确保她的演讲不冒犯人。


近义词：inoffensive, harmless, innocuous, polite, respectful

反义词：offensive, insulting, rude, abrasive, provocative

词性：形容词
