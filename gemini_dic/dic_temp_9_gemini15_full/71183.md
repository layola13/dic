fist:/fɪst/| n.|拳头，拳击|A clenched hand.  A fist is a hand with the fingers curled into the palm and the thumb on the outside.|He clenched his fist in anger. 他愤怒地握紧拳头。|He punched the wall with his fist. 他用拳头捶打墙壁。|近义词：knuckle, hand|反义词：open hand
