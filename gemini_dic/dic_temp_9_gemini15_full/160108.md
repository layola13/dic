pomiferous:/pɒmɪfərəs/| adj. |结果实的；多结果实的|Bearing pome fruit; of or relating to pome fruits.  A pomiferous tree is one that bears fruit with a core, such as apples or pears.|例句：The orchard is known for its pomiferous trees, producing abundant apples and pears every year. 这片果园以其多结果实的树木而闻名，每年都生产大量的苹果和梨。
例句： Many pomiferous species are cultivated for their edible fruit. 许多结果实的物种因其可食用的水果而被种植。|近义词：fruit-bearing, fruitful|反义词：barren, fruitless
