extravasation:/ˌɛkstrævəˈseɪʃən/| n. |渗出；溢出；血管外渗|The escape of fluid from a vessel into surrounding tissue.  This often refers to blood escaping from blood vessels, but can also apply to other fluids like lymph.  The condition can result in swelling, discoloration, and pain.|例句：The patient experienced extravasation at the IV insertion site, resulting in swelling and bruising. 病人静脉注射部位发生血管外渗，导致肿胀和瘀伤。|例句：Extravasation of the contrast medium caused significant discomfort during the procedure.造影剂的渗出在手术过程中引起了明显的 discomfort.|近义词：exudation, seepage, effusion|反义词：retention, containment|词性：名词
