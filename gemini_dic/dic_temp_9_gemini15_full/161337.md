postprocess:/ˈpoʊstˌprɑːses/ | vt. | 后处理；后期处理 | To perform operations on data or a product after the main processing or manufacturing stage has been completed. This often involves refining, enhancing, or correcting the results.  | Example: We need to postprocess the images to remove noise and enhance contrast. 我们需要对图像进行后期处理以去除噪点并增强对比度。


Example: The factory postprocesses the steel to improve its durability. 工厂对钢铁进行后处理以提高其耐用性。|近义词：refine, enhance, perfect |反义词：preprocess, pre-process |动词
