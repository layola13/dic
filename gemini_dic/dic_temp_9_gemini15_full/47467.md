deal:/diːl/| n. vt.|协议，交易；份量；待遇；打击；|英文解析：A deal can refer to an agreement or arrangement, often in business,  a quantity or amount of something, the way someone is treated, or a hard blow.|例句：We made a deal to buy the house. 我们达成协议买下这栋房子。  He dealt the cards. 他发牌。|近义词：agreement, transaction, amount, treatment, blow|反义词：disagreement, failure, mistreatment
