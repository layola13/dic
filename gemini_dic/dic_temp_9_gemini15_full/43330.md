cramp:/kræmp/| n., v.|抽筋；痉挛；限制；束缚 |A sudden, involuntary contraction of a muscle, causing pain.  Also, something that restricts or confines. |例句：I got a cramp in my leg during the marathon. 我在马拉松比赛中腿抽筋了。

The tight shoes cramped my feet. 这双紧鞋勒着我的脚。|近义词：spasm, spasm, convulsion, restriction, constraint |反义词：relaxation, freedom


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Muscle cramps often occur during exercise. = 肌肉痉挛经常发生在运动期间。
This job cramps my style. = 这份工作限制了我的发挥。


一般过去时:
He cramped his leg while swimming. = 他游泳时腿抽筋了。
The strict rules cramped their creativity. = 严格的规定限制了他们的创造力。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
