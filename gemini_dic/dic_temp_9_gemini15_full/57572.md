dramatization:/ˌdræmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |戏剧化；戏剧性表演；改编成戏剧|The act of representing something in a dramatic way; a dramatic representation of something.  It can refer to the process of adapting a story or event into a dramatic form, or to the resulting performance or piece of writing. |例句：The film offered a powerful dramatization of the war. 这部电影对战争进行了强有力的戏剧化演绎。

The play is a dramatization of a true story. 这部戏是根据真实故事改编的戏剧。|近义词：adaptation, staging, enactment, representation |反义词：factual account, plain narration
