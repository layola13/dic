self-contemplations:/ˌsɛlf ˌkɒntɛmˈpleɪʃənz/| n. |自我反省；沉思；冥想 |The act of thinking deeply and carefully about oneself, one's feelings, experiences, or actions.  It often involves introspection and self-reflection, aiming to gain a deeper understanding of oneself. |例句：Her self-contemplations led her to a new understanding of her own motivations.  她的自我反省让她对自己的动机有了新的理解。

例句：Hours of self-contemplations in the quiet of the mountains brought him peace. 在山中的宁静中进行数小时的自我反省给他带来了平静。


近义词：introspection, self-reflection, meditation, contemplation, soul-searching |反义词：recklessness, impulsiveness, thoughtlessness
