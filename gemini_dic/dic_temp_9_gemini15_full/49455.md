deltidium:/dɛlˈtɪdiəm/| n.|三角形邮票|A deltidium is a postcard or similar small piece of cardboard used for sending messages, often featuring a pictorial design.  It's a term primarily used in philately (stamp collecting) to refer to postcards used as philatelic items.  The term is derived from the Greek word "delta," referring to the triangular shape of some early postcards.|Example: The collector proudly displayed his rare deltidium featuring an early 20th-century image of Paris.|近义词：postcard, picture postcard|反义词：letter (as a longer format)|名词
