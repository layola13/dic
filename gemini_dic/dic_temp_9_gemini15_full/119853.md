mileages:/ˈmaɪlɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 英里数；里程；行驶里程；（尤指汽车的）耗油量 | The plural form of mileage, referring to the total number of miles traveled or the fuel efficiency of a vehicle.  It can also refer to the value or benefit derived from something. | 例句：The car's mileage is excellent. 这辆车的油耗很低。  The mileage on that trip was surprisingly high. 那次旅行的行驶里程出乎意料地高。 |近义词：distance, kilometerage |反义词: N/A
