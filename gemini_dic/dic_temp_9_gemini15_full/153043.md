peep:/piːp/| n./v.|偷看；窥视；吱吱叫声|To look quickly and secretly at something.  A short, high-pitched sound, such as that made by a bird or a small animal.|例句：I peeped through the keyhole. 我从钥匙孔里偷看。
The baby bird let out a tiny peep. 小鸟发出一声细微的吱吱叫。|近义词：glance, peek, squawk |反义词：stare, shout

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I peep at the neighbor's garden every morning. 我每天早上都偷看邻居家的花园。
Birds peep merrily in the early morning.  鸟儿在清晨快乐地鸣叫。


一般过去时:
She peeped into the room to see if anyone was there. 她偷偷地往房间里看了一眼，看看有没有人在。
The mouse peeped softly before running away. 老鼠轻轻地吱吱叫了一声，然后跑开了。


现在完成时:
I have peeped through that window many times. 我多次从那个窗户偷看过。
The baby chick has peeped many times since hatching. 小鸡孵化以来已经叫唤了很多次。


过去完成时:
I had peeped into the box before I opened it.  在我打开盒子之前，我已经偷偷看过它了。
The birds had peeped all night before I finally woke up. 鸟儿整夜都在叫，直到我终于醒来。


将来时:
I will peep into the letter later. 我晚点再偷偷看这封信。
The newborn kittens will peep for their mother in the night. 新生的小猫将在夜里发出叫声寻找它们的妈妈。


虚拟语气:
If I peeped through the window, I would see the inside. 如果我从窗户偷看，我就能看到里面。
I wish I had not peeped into their private conversation. 我真希望我没有偷听他们的私人谈话。


被动语态:
The house was peeped into by a stranger last night. 昨晚有人偷偷潜入了这所房子。
The baby bird's peeping was heard by its mother.  母鸟听到了小鸟的叫声。


疑问句:
Did you peep into the box? 你偷看盒子了吗？
Can you hear the little birds peeping? 你能听到小鸟的叫声吗？


否定句:
I did not peep at her. 我没有偷看她。
The chick did not peep today. 小鸡今天没有叫。
