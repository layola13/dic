complicant:/kəmˈplaɪənt/| adj. |顺从的；服从的；易处理的 |Willing to comply with the wishes, requests, or demands of others; obedient.  Someone who is compliant readily follows instructions or rules.  It can also describe something that is easily managed or handled. |例句：The compliant child readily obeyed his parents.  顺从的孩子很容易听从父母的话。

The software is compliant with all relevant industry standards. 该软件符合所有相关的行业标准。|近义词：obedient, submissive, docile, tractable |反义词：rebellious, defiant, intractable, disobedient |词性：adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is compliant with the rules. 他遵守规则。
The machine is compliant with safety regulations. 这台机器符合安全条例。

一般过去时:
She was compliant and followed all instructions. 她很顺从，并遵循所有指示。
The system was compliant with the new standards.  系统符合新标准。

现在完成时:
The company has been compliant with environmental regulations. 该公司一直遵守环境法规。
He has always been compliant with his doctor's orders. 他一直遵守医生的嘱咐。

过去完成时:
They had been compliant until the new manager arrived. 在新经理到来之前，他们一直很顺从。
The factory had been compliant with safety standards for years. 这家工厂多年来一直符合安全标准。

将来时:
She will be compliant with the new policy. 她将遵守新政策。
The company will remain compliant with all legal requirements.  公司将继续遵守所有法律要求。

虚拟语气:
If he were more compliant, things would be easier. 如果他更顺从，事情会更容易。
I wish the software were more compliant with our system. 我希望软件更符合我们的系统。

被动语态:
The rules must be complied with. 必须遵守规则。(Compliant is rarely used passively)
The system is designed to be compliant with industry standards. 该系统旨在符合行业标准。(Focuses on the system's design, not a passive state of being compliant)

疑问句:
Is he compliant with the company's policies? 他遵守公司政策吗？
Are the products compliant with the EU regulations? 产品符合欧盟法规吗？

否定句:
He is not compliant with the regulations. 他不遵守规章制度。
The factory was not compliant with the new safety standards.  工厂不符合新的安全标准。
