conventicler:/kənˈvɛntɪklər/| n. |秘密聚会；小派系；（贬义）秘密结社|A conventicle is a secret or private meeting, often of a religious or political nature, especially one held in defiance of authority.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting a clandestine and potentially subversive gathering. |Example: The dissenting members held secret conventicles to plot against the church leadership.  秘密反对派成员举行秘密聚会密谋反对教会领导。|近义词：secret meeting, clandestine gathering, cabal, clique |反义词：public meeting, open assembly
