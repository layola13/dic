debarrass:/ˌdiːbəˈræz/| vt.|使摆脱；使免除；清除|To free or relieve from something burdensome, troublesome, or embarrassing.|例句：He debarred himself from the pleasures of the world. 他使自己摆脱了世俗的享乐。
例句：The government is trying to debarrass the country of corruption. 政府正试图清除国家的腐败现象。|近义词：rid, free, relieve, clear|反义词：burden, encumber, oppress

词源:
The word "debarrass" comes from the Old French "desbaraisser," which is a compound of "de-" (from) and "barrasser" (to clear, rid).  The verb "barrasser" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to a Germanic root.
