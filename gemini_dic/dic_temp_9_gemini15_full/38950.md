confiders:/kənˈsɪdərz/ | v. | 认为；相信；考虑 | To believe something to be true; to think about something carefully. |  She confiders him to be a trustworthy friend. 她认为他是一个值得信赖的朋友。 He is considering his options carefully. 他正在仔细考虑他的选择。 | believes, thinks, contemplates | doubts, disbelieves, ignores | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confiders the plan to be feasible. 他认为这个计划是可行的。
They consider her a valuable asset to the team. 他们认为她是团队中宝贵的财富。


一般过去时:
She considered the consequences before acting.  她在行动前考虑了后果。
They considered all the possibilities. 他们考虑了所有可能性。


现在完成时:
He has considered several job offers. 他已经考虑了好几个工作机会。
We have considered your request carefully. 我们已经仔细考虑了你的请求。


过去完成时:
She had considered quitting her job before the promotion. 在升职之前，她曾考虑过辞职。
They had considered all aspects of the problem. 他们已经考虑了这个问题的各个方面。


将来时:
They will consider the proposal next week. 他们下周将考虑这项提案。
She will consider all the evidence before making a decision. 她会在做出决定之前考虑所有的证据。


虚拟语气:
If he considered the risks, he might have acted differently. 如果他考虑了风险，他可能会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had considered the long-term effects. 我真希望我当时考虑到了长期影响。


被动语态:
The proposal was considered by the committee.  该提案得到了委员会的考虑。
His ideas were carefully considered. 他的想法受到了仔细的考虑。


疑问句:
Do you consider this a fair offer? 你认为这是一个公平的提议吗？
Does she consider herself qualified for the position? 她认为自己有资格担任这个职位吗？


否定句:
He doesn't consider himself a genius. 他不认为自己是个天才。
They didn't consider the environmental impact. 他们没有考虑环境影响。


PHRASE->
consider doing sth: 考虑做某事
consider oneself: 自认为
take sth into consideration: 考虑到某事
give consideration to: 考虑
under consideration: 正在考虑中
