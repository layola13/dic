flexile:/ˈflɛksaɪl/ | adj. | 柔软的；易弯曲的；灵活的 | Easily bent; pliable; adaptable.  The flexile branches swayed in the breeze.  | 近义词：supple, pliable, flexible, adaptable | 反义词：rigid, inflexible, stiff | 形容词


例句：

The flexile willow branches bent easily in the wind.  = 柔软的柳枝在风中轻易地弯曲。

Her flexile mind allowed her to adapt quickly to new challenges. = 她灵活的头脑使她能够迅速适应新的挑战。


词源：

From Latin *flexilis, from flectere "to bend".  The suffix -ile is an adjectival suffix indicating "capable of" or "having the nature of".
