ecotopia:/ˌekoʊˈtoʊpiə/| n. |乌托邦式生态社会；生态乐土|An imaginary or fictional society where ecological harmony and social justice are achieved.|Example: Ernest Callenbach's novel "Ecotopia" depicts a utopian society in the Pacific Northwest that has successfully integrated environmental sustainability with social progress.  |近义词：utopia, paradise, Eden|反义词：dystopia, wasteland
