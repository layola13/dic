pro-Westerner: /ˌproʊˈwɛstərnər/ | n. | 亲西方人士 | A person who supports or favors Western culture, values, or policies.  Someone who is sympathetic to or actively promotes Western ideals and interests.  They often advocate for closer ties with Western nations and may criticize non-Western systems. | Example:  He was considered a pro-Westerner due to his outspoken support for democracy and human rights. 他因公开支持民主和人权而被认为是亲西方人士。


近义词:  Westernizer, pro-West,  Occidentalist (depending on context)

反义词: anti-Westerner, isolationist


词性: 名词
