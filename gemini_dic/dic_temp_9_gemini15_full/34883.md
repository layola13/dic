clipboards:/ˈklɪpˌbɔːrdz/| n.|剪贴板；文件板|A flat, thin board with a clip at the top, used for holding papers.  |例句：She carried a clipboard and a pen, checking off items on her list. 她拿着一块剪贴板和一支笔，在清单上勾选项目。

例句：The teacher used a clipboard to hold the students' assignments. 老师用剪贴板来存放学生的作业。


近义词：writing board, tablet (in some contexts)


反义词：无
