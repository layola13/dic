perverter:/pərˈvɜːrtər/| n. | 败坏者；歪曲者；堕落者 | One who perverts; one who corrupts or distorts something.  A perverter is someone who deliberately twists or misrepresents facts, morals, or principles. They can also be someone who engages in perverse or unnatural sexual acts. | Example: He was accused of being a perverter of justice. 他被指控为司法败坏者。 |近义词：corrupter, defiler, depraver | 反义词：reformer, purifier, uplifter |  名词
