prodemocrat: /ˌproʊˈdɛməkræt/ | adj. | 亲民主的，支持民主的 |  Supporting or advocating for democracy.  The term often describes individuals or groups who actively promote democratic principles and systems of government.  It can also be used to describe policies or actions that are consistent with democratic values. | 例句：The prodemocrat movement organized large-scale protests. 亲民主运动组织了大规模抗议活动。 The prodemocrat candidate won the election. 亲民主候选人赢得了选举。| 近义词：democratic, pro-democracy, liberal (in a political context) | 反义词：anti-democratic, authoritarian, totalitarian | 形容词
