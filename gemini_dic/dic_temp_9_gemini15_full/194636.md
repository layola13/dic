self-tortures:/ˈsɛlf ˈtɔːrtʃərz/| n.| 自我折磨；自我摧残 |The act of intentionally inflicting pain or suffering upon oneself.  This can manifest in various ways, including physical self-harm, emotional self-deprecation, or engaging in self-destructive behaviors.|例句：His self-tortures stemmed from a deep-seated feeling of guilt.  他的自我折磨源于深深的罪恶感。

His constant self-tortures over his past mistakes were affecting his mental health. 他不断地因过去的错误而自我折磨，影响了他的心理健康。|近义词：self-abuse, self-destruction, self-flagellation|反义词：self-care, self-love, self-compassion|名词
