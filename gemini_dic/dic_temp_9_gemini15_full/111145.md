lithargyrum:/ˈlɪθɑːrdʒərəm/| n. |迟钝药；麻醉药|A lethargyrum is an obsolete term for a medicine or drug that induces lethargy or drowsiness.  It was often used in historical contexts, particularly in the context of medieval medicine. The exact composition varied depending on the time period and the specific formulation.  The term is now largely archaic and rarely used in modern medical contexts.|例句：The physician prescribed a lethargyrum to ease the patient's pain and induce sleep.  医生开了迟钝药来缓解病人的疼痛并诱导睡眠。|近义词：soporific, narcotic, sedative|反义词：stimulant
