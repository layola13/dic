The incrassated walls of the artery indicated a build-up of plaque.  动脉增厚的壁表明斑块堆积。

近义词：thickened, swollen, enlarged, congested, corpulent (for fleshy application)

反义词：thinned, attenuated, reduced, emaciated


词源:

From Latin *incrassatus, past participle of incrassare "to thicken," from in- "in" + crassus "thick."
