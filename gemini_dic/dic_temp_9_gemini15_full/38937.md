confidences:/ˈkɒnfɪdənsɪz/ | n. |信任；信心；秘密；坦白 |The state of feeling or expressing trust in someone or something; self-assurance; a secret or piece of private information told to someone. |例句：He has complete confidence in his abilities. 他对自己的能力充满信心。

例句：She shared her deepest confidences with her diary. 她把最深的秘密告诉了她的日记。


近义词：trust, belief, assurance, secrets | 反义词：distrust, doubt, suspicion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confides in his friend. = 他向他的朋友倾诉。
She expresses her confidence in the project. = 她表达了她对这个项目的信心。


一般过去时:
They confided their secrets to each other. = 他们互相倾吐了秘密。
He confided in his therapist. = 他向他的治疗师倾诉。


现在完成时:
I have confided in him many times. = 我已经多次向他倾诉了。
She has always confided in her sister. = 她一直都向她姐姐倾诉。


过去完成时:
He had confided in her before the incident. = 在事件发生前，他已经向她倾诉了。
They had confided their plans to no one. = 他们没有向任何人透露他们的计划。


将来时:
I will confide in my best friend about this matter. = 我会向我最好的朋友倾诉这件事。
She will confide in her mother eventually. = 她最终会向她母亲倾诉。


虚拟语气:
If I had confided in him earlier, things might have been different. = 如果我早点向他倾诉，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish I had confided in someone sooner. = 我希望我早点向某人倾诉。


被动语态:
The secrets were confided to only a few people. = 这些秘密只被告诉了少数人。
His trust was betrayed; his confidences were violated. = 他的信任被背叛了；他的秘密被泄露了。


疑问句:
In whom do you confide? = 你向谁倾诉？
Can I confide in you? = 我可以向你倾诉吗？


否定句:
He didn't confide in anyone about his problems. = 他没有向任何人倾诉他的问题。
She wouldn't confide her worries to strangers. = 她不会向陌生人倾诉她的担忧。


PHRASE->
place confidence in = 对…有信心
in confidence = 秘密地
breach of confidence = 违反信任
lose confidence = 失去信心
build confidence = 建立信心
