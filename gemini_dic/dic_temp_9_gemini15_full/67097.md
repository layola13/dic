explorative:/ɪkˈsplɒrətɪv/| adj.|探索性的，探究性的| characterized by or engaged in exploration or investigation.  It suggests a systematic and thorough search for knowledge or understanding. |例句：The explorative research method involves collecting qualitative data to understand a phenomenon.  探索性研究方法包括收集定性数据以了解某种现象。

例句：His explorative essay delved into the hidden meanings of the poem. 他的探索性论文深入探讨了这首诗歌的隐含意义。


近义词：investigative, inquiring, exploratory, searching

反义词：superficial, cursory, perfunctory

词性：形容词
