nonobjections:/ˌnɒnəbˈdʒɛkʃənz/ | n. |无异议，没有反对 |The absence of objections; a situation where no one expresses opposition or disapproval.  This word is rarely used and is essentially a formal, somewhat cumbersome way of saying "no objections."| 例句：The proposal passed with nonobjections. = 这项提案在无人反对的情况下通过了。  The committee meeting concluded with a record of nonobjections. = 委员会会议在记录下无人反对的情况下结束。|近义词：acquiescence, consent, approval |反义词：objections, opposition, dissent |名词
