explorational:/ˌɛksploˈreɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 探测的；探险的；考察的 | Relating to or involving exploration.  The word suggests an active process of investigation and discovery, often in unfamiliar or challenging environments. | Example:  The explorational team mapped the previously uncharted cave system. 探险队绘制了以前未探明的洞穴系统地图。  The explorational phase of the project lasted six months.  该项目的探索阶段持续了六个月。|近义词：investigative, exploratory, research-oriented |反义词：conclusive, definitive
