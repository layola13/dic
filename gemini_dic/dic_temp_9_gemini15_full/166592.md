preseasonal: /ˌpriːˈsiːzənəl/ | adj. |季前赛的；季前期的| Relating to the period before a particular season, especially a sports season.  It often implies preparation or activities leading up to the main season. |例句：The preseasonal training camp was grueling but essential for the team's success. 季前训练营非常艰苦，但对球队的成功至关重要。

The team held several preseasonal friendlies to test their new tactics. 球队进行了几场季前友谊赛来测试他们的新战术。

近义词：ante-season, preparatory, preliminary

反义词：post-season, in-season


词源:  The word is a combination of "pre-" (meaning "before") and "seasonal" (relating to a season).  "Seasonal" itself derives from "season," ultimately from Old French *seison and Latin *satiōnē (a sowing).
