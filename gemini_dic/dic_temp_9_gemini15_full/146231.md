overexposed:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊzd/ | adj. | 过度曝光的；曝光过度的；过度显示的 |  Describes something that has been exposed to light (usually in photography or film) for too long, resulting in a loss of detail and a washed-out appearance.  It can also refer to something that has been excessively publicized or shown. | Example: The photograph was overexposed, losing all the detail in the highlights.  照片曝光过度，高光部分丢失了所有细节。 The celebrity felt overexposed after the constant media attention.  这位名人因持续不断的媒体关注而感到过度曝光。|近义词：overbright, washed-out | 反义词：underexposed, shadowed
