looked:/lʊkt/ | /lʊkd/ | v. | 看；显得 | To direct one's gaze towards something; to have the appearance of. |例句：He looked at the painting carefully. 他仔细地看了看那幅画。

例句：She looked tired after the long journey. 长途旅行后，她看起来很疲惫。|近义词：seemed, appeared, glanced, gazed | 反义词：ignored, overlooked | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He looks at the bird. = 他看着鸟。
She looks happy today. = 她今天看起来很开心。

一般过去时:
I looked at the sunset. = 我看了看日落。
They looked for their keys. = 他们寻找他们的钥匙。

现在完成时:
I have looked everywhere for my glasses. = 我到处都找过我的眼镜了。
She has looked much better since she started exercising. =自从她开始锻炼以来，她看起来好多了。

过去完成时:
I had looked at the contract before signing. = 我在签合同之前已经看过合同了。
He had looked for a job for months before he found one. = 他找工作找了几个月才找到一份。

将来时:
I will look at your proposal tomorrow. = 我明天会看看你的提案。
We will look into the matter further. = 我们会进一步调查此事。

虚拟语气:
If I had looked more carefully, I would have seen the mistake. = 如果我仔细点看，我就不会犯这个错误了。
I wish I had looked before I leaped. = 我真希望我在跳之前先看看。

被动语态:
The painting was looked at by many people. = 这幅画被很多人观赏过。
The problem is being looked into. = 这个问题正在被调查。

疑问句:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
Where did you look for your keys? = 你在哪里找你的钥匙？

否定句:
I didn't look at the map. = 我没有看地图。
She didn't look happy at all. = 她一点也不开心。
