get:/ɡɛt/ | /ɡet/ | v. | 得到，获得，理解 | to obtain or receive something; to understand something |  He got a new car. 他买了一辆新车。 | She finally got it. 她终于明白了。 | obtain, receive, acquire | lose, miss | 动词


goes:/ɡoʊz/ | /ɡəʊz/ | v. | 去，走，进行 | to move or travel; to proceed or continue | He goes to school every day. 他每天上学。 | The story goes like this. 故事是这样的。 |  travel, proceed, continue | stop, stay | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets a letter every week. = 他每周都收到一封信。
She goes to the gym three times a week. = 她一周去健身房三次。

一般过去时:
I got a new job last month. = 我上个月找到了一份新工作。
They went to the beach yesterday. = 他们昨天去了海滩。

现在完成时:
She has gotten much better. = 她好多了。
He has gone to London. = 他去了伦敦。(暗示人不在此处)

过去完成时:
I had gotten there before he arrived. = 我在他到达之前就到了那里。
They had gone home before the party started. = 派对开始之前他们就回家了。

将来时:
I will get a raise next year. = 明年我将加薪。
We will go to Paris next summer. = 我们明年夏天要去巴黎。

虚拟语气:
If I got a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我得到一百万美元，我会环游世界。
If she went to college, she would have more opportunities. = 如果她上大学，她将会有更多机会。

被动语态:
The problem was gotten rid of quickly. = 这个问题很快就被解决了。
The meeting was gone to by all the staff. = 全体员工都参加了会议。


疑问句:
Does he get enough sleep? = 他睡够了吗？
Where does she go every weekend? = 她每个周末去哪里？

否定句:
I didn't get the job. = 我没有得到那份工作。
He didn't go to the party. = 他没有去参加聚会。
