putamen:/ˈpjuːtəmən/ | n. |豆状核|The putamen is a component of the basal ganglia, a group of structures deep within the brain involved in motor control, learning, and reward.  It is a large, lenticular structure, and is primarily involved in motor function and procedural learning.  Damage to the putamen can lead to various movement disorders. |Example: Studies have shown the putamen's significant role in regulating movement.  研究表明豆状核在调节运动方面起着重要作用。|近义词：lenticular nucleus (部分重叠)|反义词：无
