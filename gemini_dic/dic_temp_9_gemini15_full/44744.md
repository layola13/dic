crumbing:/ˈkrʌmbɪŋ/| v.|碎裂；弄碎；崩溃；|The old building is crumbling.  这栋旧建筑正在倒塌。|The cake is crumbling. 蛋糕正在碎裂。|近义词：disintegrating, decaying, falling apart |反义词：solidifying, strengthening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old house is crumbling. = 这栋旧房子正在倒塌。
The plaster is crumbling from the ceiling. = 石膏正在从天花板上剥落。

一般过去时:
The empire crumbled after the war. = 战争之后，帝国崩溃了。
The bridge crumbled under the weight of the truck. = 桥梁在卡车的重量下坍塌了。

现在完成时:
The building has crumbled since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，这座建筑物已经倒塌了。
The relationship has crumbled beyond repair. = 这段关系已经破裂无法修复。


过去完成时:
The walls had crumbled before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，墙壁就已经倒塌了。
The support system had crumbled under the pressure. = 支撑系统在压力下已经崩溃了。


将来时:
The cliff will crumble into the sea eventually. = 这悬崖最终会崩塌到海里。
The economy will crumble if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，经济将会崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If the foundation were weaker, the building would crumble. = 如果地基更弱，建筑物就会倒塌。
I wish the government wouldn't let the healthcare system crumble. = 我希望政府不会让医疗体系崩溃。


被动语态:
The ancient ruins were crumbled by the passage of time. = 古代遗址被时间的流逝所摧毁。
The cake was crumbled into small pieces. = 蛋糕被弄碎成小块。


疑问句:
Is the building crumbling? = 这栋建筑物正在倒塌吗？
Are the relationships crumbling around her? = 她周围的人际关系正在破裂吗？


否定句:
The walls didn't crumble despite the storm. = 尽管暴风雨来临，墙壁并没有倒塌。
The empire did not crumble easily. = 帝国并没有轻易崩溃。
