cotemporaneous:/ˌkənˈtɛmpəreɪniəs/| adj.|同时期的；同时代的；同时发生的|Existing or occurring in the same period of time.  The term refers to events, people, or things that exist or happen at the same time.|例句：The two paintings are contemporaneous, both dating from the early 18th century. 这两幅画是同时期的，都创作于18世纪早期。

The discovery of the contemporaneous documents shed new light on the historical event.  对同时期文件的发现为这一历史事件带来了新的光亮。|近义词：simultaneous, concurrent, synchronic|反义词：diachronic, successive
