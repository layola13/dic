renewables:/rɪˈnuːəblz/| n. |可再生能源 |Renewable energy sources are those which are naturally replenished, such as solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal energy.  They are sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels. | Example:  Many countries are investing heavily in renewables to combat climate change. 许多国家正在大力投资可再生能源以应对气候变化。 The shift towards renewables is gaining momentum. 向可再生能源的转变正在加速。|近义词：sustainable energy, alternative energy |反义词：non-renewable energy, fossil fuels |名词
