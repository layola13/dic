portend:/pɔːˈtɛnd/| /poʊˈtɛnd/| vt.| 预示；预兆；兆示 | to be a sign or warning that something, especially something bad, will happen in the future.  | 例句：The dark clouds portended a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。

例句：The economist's report portended a recession. 经济学家的报告预示着经济衰退。


近义词：foreshadow, foretell, predict, augur, presage

反义词：assure, guarantee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dark clouds portent a storm. = 乌云预示着暴风雨。
These signs portent economic instability. = 这些迹象预示着经济不稳定。


一般过去时:
The soothsayer portended the king's death. = 预言家预言了国王的死亡。
The unusual silence portended trouble. = 不寻常的寂静预示着麻烦。


现在完成时:
The recent events have portended a major shift in power. = 最近发生的事件预示着权力的一次重大转移。
Recent economic indicators have portended a slowdown in growth. = 最近的经济指标预示着增长放缓。


过去完成时:
The old woman's whispers had portended the coming tragedy. = 老妇人的低语预示着即将到来的悲剧。
The strange noises had portended the arrival of uninvited guests.  = 奇怪的噪音预示着不速之客的到来。


将来时:
Experts portent that the next decade will see significant technological advances. = 专家们预言未来十年将出现重大的技术进步。
Analysts portent a rise in inflation next year. = 分析师预测明年通货膨胀将会上升。


虚拟语气:
If the signs had portended disaster, we would have acted differently. = 如果这些迹象预示着灾难，我们会采取不同的行动。
If these trends continued to portent trouble, we'd have to intervene. = 如果这些趋势继续预示着麻烦，我们将不得不进行干预。


被动语态:
A coming war was portended by the increased military activity. = 加强的军事活动预示着一场即将到来的战争。
The economic crisis was portended by several warning signs. = 经济危机是由几个预警信号预示的。


疑问句:
Does this unusual behavior portent anything serious? = 这种异常行为预示着什么严重的事情吗？
Could these events portent the end of the current political system? = 这些事件会预示着当前政治体制的终结吗？


否定句:
The initial reports did not portent any immediate danger. = 最初的报告并没有预示任何迫在眉睫的危险。
The calm before the storm did not portent the chaos that followed. = 风暴前的平静并没有预示随后而来的混乱。


PHRASE->
portent of doom = 厄运的预兆
portentous signs = 不祥之兆
a portentous event = 重大事件
portentous warnings = 警告
portent of change = 变革的预兆
