enhancement: /ɪnˈhɑːnsmənt/ | n. | 增强；改进；提高 | The improvement or augmentation of something.  It refers to the act of making something better or more attractive. |例句：The enhancement of the picture quality was remarkable. 图片质量的增强非常显著。

例句：The software update includes several performance enhancements. 软件更新包含若干性能改进。


近义词：improvement, betterment, upgrade, amelioration | 反义词：deterioration, worsening, decline


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The enhancement improves the user experience. = 增强功能改善了用户体验。
This project focuses on image enhancement. = 这个项目专注于图像增强。


一般过去时:
The team implemented several enhancements last year. = 团队去年实施了几个改进措施。
The upgrade brought significant enhancements to the system. = 升级为系统带来了显著的改进。


现在完成时:
We have made several enhancements to the website. = 我们已经对网站进行了一些改进。
They have significantly enhanced their marketing strategy. = 他们已经显著改进了他们的营销策略。


过去完成时:
They had already implemented the enhancements before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经实施了这些改进。
The company had made several enhancements to the product before its launch. = 公司在产品发布之前对其进行了一些改进。


将来时:
We will enhance the security system next month. = 我们下个月将增强安全系统。
The company will implement several enhancements in the coming year. = 公司将在来年实施几项改进措施。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could make further enhancements. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们可以进行进一步的改进。
I wish the software had more enhancements. = 我希望该软件有更多增强功能。


被动语态:
The image was enhanced using special software. = 图片是用特殊的软件增强的。
The system's performance is being enhanced through regular updates. = 系统的性能正在通过定期更新得到增强。


疑问句:
What enhancements are planned for the next version? = 下个版本计划有哪些改进？
Are there any enhancements planned for the software? = 软件有计划进行任何改进吗？


否定句:
The update didn't include any significant enhancements. = 更新没有包含任何显著的改进。
There were no enhancements made to the product design. = 产品设计没有任何改进。
