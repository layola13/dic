dodd:/dɒd/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜；(尤指)愚蠢的政客|A dodd is a foolish or stupid person, especially a foolish politician. The term often carries a connotation of incompetence or ineffectiveness.  |例句：He called the politician a dodd for his ineffective policies. 他称那位政客为笨蛋，因为他政策无效。|近义词：fool, idiot, buffoon, simpleton|反义词：genius, sage, mastermind|名词
