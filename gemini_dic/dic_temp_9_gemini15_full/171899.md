punting:/ˈpʌntɪŋ/| n. |撑篙；划船；(尤指)平底船赛艇；(赌博中的)押注 |The act of propelling a boat through water using a punting pole; also, the act of betting, especially in gambling.  |例句：He enjoys punting on the river Cam. 他喜欢在剑河上撑船。|例句：He lost a lot of money punting on horse races. 他在赛马赌博中输了很多钱。|近义词：rowing, paddling, betting, wagering |反义词：N/A
