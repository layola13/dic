enunciate:/ɪˈnʌnsieɪt/ | vt. |清楚地发音；明确表达；正式宣布|To pronounce words clearly; to state something clearly and formally; to announce something officially.| 例句：The teacher enunciated each word carefully so that the students could understand. 老师仔细地清楚地发出每个单词，以便学生们能够理解。

例句：The president enunciated his policy on immigration during his speech. 总统在演讲中明确阐述了他的移民政策。


近义词：pronounce, articulate, express, declare, proclaim| 反义词：mumble, slur, whisper
