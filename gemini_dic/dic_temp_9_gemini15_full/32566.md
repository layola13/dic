chiragra:/kaɪˈræɡrə/| n. |痛风, 手痛风 |A painful inflammation of the joints, especially in the hands and feet, caused by a buildup of uric acid crystals.  Chiragra specifically refers to gout in the hand. |Example:  He suffered from a severe case of chiragra in his right hand. 他右手患有严重的痛风。|近义词：gout (general term)|反义词：None specifically, but the absence of gout could be considered an antonym. |名词
