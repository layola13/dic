isometric:/ˌaɪsoʊˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|等距的，等大的；等轴测的 |  Relating to or employing isometric projection, a method of pictorial representation of a three-dimensional object in two dimensions in which the three principal axes are equally foreshortened.  It preserves the proportions of the object, but not necessarily the angles. | 例句：The isometric drawing clearly shows the dimensions of the building.  等轴测图清晰地显示了建筑物的尺寸。  |The isometric exercise helped strengthen my core muscles.  等长运动帮助增强了我的核心肌肉。|近义词：equal, isometrical|反义词：perspective, non-isometric
