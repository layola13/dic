perpetual:/pərˈpetʃuəl/ | adj. | 永久的；持续不断的；没完没了的 | Lasting or intended to last for ever.  | Example:  He felt a perpetual sense of unease. 他感到一种挥之不去的不安。 | 近义词：eternal, everlasting, constant, continuous | 反义词：temporary, transient, fleeting, ephemeral


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun perpetually shines in that desert. =  那片沙漠里阳光永不停歇地照耀着。
This machine perpetually runs. = 这台机器持续运转。


一般过去时:
The river perpetually flowed. =  这条河一直流淌着。
They perpetually complained. = 他们没完没了地抱怨。


现在完成时:
She has perpetually sought knowledge. = 她一直孜孜不倦地追求知识。
He has perpetually avoided responsibility. = 他一直逃避责任。


过去完成时:
They had perpetually ignored the warning signs. = 他们一直无视警告信号。
The machine had perpetually malfunctioned. = 这台机器一直出现故障。


将来时:
The machine will perpetually run. = 这台机器将持续运转。
The problem will perpetually exist. = 这个问题将持续存在。


虚拟语气:
If only I had perpetual youth! =  如果我能永远年轻就好了！
If it were perpetual summer, I’d be happy. = 如果是四季如夏，我会很开心。


被动语态:
The problem is perpetually being ignored. =  这个问题一直被忽视。
The project is perpetually being delayed. = 这个项目总是被延迟。


疑问句:
Is this a perpetual problem? = 这是一个永久性的问题吗？
Is this perpetual motion machine a hoax? = 这种永动机是个骗局吗？


否定句:
The happiness is not perpetual. = 幸福不是永恒的。
The warranty is not perpetual. = 保修期不是永久的。


PHRASE->
perpetual motion = 永动
perpetual calendar = 万年历
perpetual bond = 永久债券
perpetual care = 永久护理
perpetual succession = 永续经营

词源:
From Latin *perpetuus, from per- "through" + petere "to seek, strive for".  The original meaning implied something that continued without interruption.
