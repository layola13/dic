mush's:/mʌʃz/| n.|糊状物；烂泥；浆糊|A mush is a soft, pulpy mass.  It often refers to something that is overcooked or has become soft and indistinct.|例句：The overcooked potatoes were reduced to mush.  这些煮烂的土豆变成了糊状物。|近义词：mash, pulp, gruel|反义词：solid, firm


USAGE->

The baby eats mush for breakfast. = 婴儿早餐吃糊状食物。
This machine makes fruit mush. = 这台机器制作水果酱。
