molding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ | n. | 模制；造型；塑造；铸件 | The process of shaping a material into a specific form, typically by pouring it into a mold; also refers to the resulting shaped object.  |例句：The intricate molding on the ceiling was a masterpiece. 天花板上的复杂造型是一件杰作。

The artisan used a special molding technique to create the sculpture.  工匠使用特殊的造型技术来创作雕塑。|近义词：shaping, forming, casting, molding,  |反义词：destruction, demolition.


USAGE->

The factory molds plastic parts every day. = 工厂每天都模制塑料零件。
She is molding clay into a beautiful vase. = 她正在把粘土塑造成一个美丽的瓶子。
