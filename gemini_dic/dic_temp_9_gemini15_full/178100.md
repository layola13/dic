词源:
The word "reclusory" comes from the Late Latin word *reclusorium*, which is a derivative of the verb *recludere*, meaning "to shut up, enclose, or confine."  The suffix "-ory" indicates a place or thing associated with the action of the root verb.  Therefore, a "reclusory" is a place where one is shut up or secluded.
