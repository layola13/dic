peepul:/ˈpiːpəl/ | n. | 人们，人民 | people refers to human beings in general or a particular group of people.  | Example: The people of this country are very friendly.  这个国家的人民非常友好。 The people in the park were enjoying the sunshine. 公园里的人们正在享受阳光。 | 近义词：folk, persons, humanity | 反义词：none
