/rɪˈkʌvərər/ | n. | 找回者；恢复者；收回者 | A recoverer is a person or thing that recovers something or someone.  It can refer to someone who retrieves lost property, someone who recovers from an illness, or a system that recovers data. | Example: The mountain rescuers were experienced recoverers of lost hikers.  山地救援人员是经验丰富的迷路徒步旅行者找回者。  The data recoverer successfully retrieved the lost files. 数据恢复工具成功找回了丢失的文件。 | 近义词： retriever, restorer, recuperator | 反义词：loser, destroyer
