shushing:/ˈʃʌʃɪŋ/| n. |嘘声；轻声命令安静|The act of making a shushing sound to quiet someone or something.  It can refer to the sound itself or the action of making the sound.|例句：The librarian gave a shushing gesture. 图书管理员做了个噤声的手势。
例句：The shushing of the crowd indicated their anticipation.  人群的嘘声表明了他们的期待。|近义词：hushing, silencing|反义词：encouraging, exciting|名词
