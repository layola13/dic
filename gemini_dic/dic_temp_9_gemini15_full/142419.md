oryx:/ˈɔːrɪks/| n. | 羚羊 | A large, antelope-like mammal with long, straight horns, found in Africa and Arabia. Oryxes are known for their striking appearance and ability to survive in harsh desert environments.  |  The oryx is a magnificent creature, perfectly adapted to its arid habitat.  |  antelope, gazelle |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "domesticated animal") | 名词
