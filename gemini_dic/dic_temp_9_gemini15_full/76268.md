fuzzing:/ˈfʌzɪŋ/| n. v.|模糊测试，模糊处理|The process of testing software by feeding it with invalid, unexpected, or random data as input to discover flaws and vulnerabilities.|例句：Security researchers often use fuzzing to find vulnerabilities in software.  安全研究人员经常使用模糊测试来查找软件中的漏洞。

例句：We are conducting fuzzing tests on the new operating system. 我们正在对新的操作系统进行模糊测试。


近义词：software testing, vulnerability scanning|反义词：N/A
