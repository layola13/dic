sinister:/ˈsɪnɪstər/| adj.|险恶的；不祥的；阴险的|Something sinister is evil, wicked, or frightening.  It suggests a sense of impending danger or harm, often subtly or indirectly. It can refer to actions, intentions, or appearances.|例句：The dark, shadowy alleyway had a sinister atmosphere.  阴暗狭窄的小巷有一种险恶的气氛。

例句：His sinister smile made me uneasy.  他阴险的笑容让我不安。


近义词：evil, wicked, malicious, ominous, menacing, threatening, baleful|反义词：benign, benevolent, kind, harmless, auspicious


词源：源于拉丁语“sinister”，意为“左边”。在古代罗马文化中，左手被认为是不祥的，因此“sinister”逐渐演变成具有负面含义的词语。
