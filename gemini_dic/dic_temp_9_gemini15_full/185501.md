reuse:/ˌriːˈjuːz/ | vt. |再利用，重复使用 |To use something again.  |例句：We should reuse plastic bottles instead of throwing them away. 我们应该重复使用塑料瓶而不是扔掉它们。|近义词：recycle, repurpose, reemploy |反义词：discard, waste, dispose of |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reuse plastic bags for grocery shopping. = 他们重复使用塑料袋去买菜。
We reuse water bottles to reduce waste. = 我们重复使用水瓶以减少浪费。


一般过去时:
She reused the old jars to store spices. = 她重复使用了旧罐子来储存香料。
He reused the wrapping paper for another gift. = 他把包装纸重复利用来包装另一份礼物。


现在完成时:
They have reused many items this year, reducing their landfill contribution. = 他们今年已经重复使用了许多物品，减少了对垃圾填埋场的贡献。
We have reused this textbook for three semesters. = 我们这本教科书已经用了三个学期了。


过去完成时:
They had reused the containers before realizing they were contaminated. = 他们意识到容器被污染之前就已经重复使用了。
She had reused the towels several times before washing them. = 她洗毛巾之前已经重复使用了好多次。


将来时:
We will reuse the leftover food for tomorrow's lunch. = 我们将把剩下的食物留到明天的午餐食用。
They will reuse the cardboard boxes for shipping. = 他们将重复使用纸板箱进行运输。


虚拟语气:
If we reused more materials, we would reduce pollution. = 如果我们重复使用更多材料，我们将减少污染。
I wish I had reused that container instead of throwing it away. = 我真希望我没有扔掉那个容器而是重复使用了它。


被动语态:
The plastic bottles can be reused multiple times. = 塑料瓶可以重复使用多次。
These materials are being reused in a new project. = 这些材料正在一个新项目中被重复利用。


疑问句:
Can we reuse these containers? = 我们能重复使用这些容器吗？
Do you reuse shopping bags? = 你重复使用购物袋吗？


否定句:
We did not reuse the paper towels. = 我们没有重复使用纸巾。
They don't reuse plastic water bottles. = 他们不重复使用塑料水瓶。
