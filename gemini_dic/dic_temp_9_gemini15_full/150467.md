pantheology:/pænθiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |泛神论 |The philosophical belief that God is everything and everywhere, that the universe and God are identical.  This view contrasts with pantheism, which sees God as *immanent* in the world, but not identical with it. Pantheology often emphasizes the divine nature inherent in all things. |Example: Some interpretations of Eastern religions lean towards pantheology, seeing the divine essence permeating all existence.  一些对东方宗教的解释倾向于泛神论，认为神圣的本质弥漫于所有存在之中。|近义词: panentheism (泛神论的一种形式，认为神超越并内在于世界)|反义词: atheism (无神论), theism (有神论)
