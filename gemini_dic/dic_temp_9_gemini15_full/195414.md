seminaries:/ˈsɛməneriːz/| n. |神学院；修道院；预备学校|A seminary is an institution for the training of clergy or other religious personnel.  It can also refer to a preparatory school, especially one for girls.  |例句：He studied at a prestigious seminary before entering the priesthood. 他在进入神职人员之前在一所著名的神学院学习。

Many young women attended the seminary to prepare for their futures. 许多年轻女子就读于这所预备学校为她们的未来做准备。|近义词：theological college, divinity school, preparatory school|反义词：secular school, secular institution
