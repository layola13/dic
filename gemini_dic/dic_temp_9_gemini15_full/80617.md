goose-step:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛp/ | n. v. |鹅步，正步走 | A military march characterized by high knees and rigidly straight legs.  The term often carries negative connotations, associating it with militarism and authoritarian regimes. |例句：The soldiers goose-stepped down the street.  士兵们迈着正步走下街道。  The parade featured a goose-step, which many found unsettling.  阅兵式上出现了正步走，许多人觉得不安。|近义词：march, strut |反义词：shuffle, saunter |名词，动词
