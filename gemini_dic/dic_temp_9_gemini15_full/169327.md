promoter:/prəˈmoʊtər/ | n. | 推广者；启动子；促进者 | A promoter is a person or thing that promotes something, such as a product, event, or idea.  In genetics, a promoter is a region of DNA that initiates transcription of a particular gene. | Example 1: The marketing team hired a promoter to increase brand awareness.  (市场团队聘请了一名推广者来提高品牌知名度。) Example 2: The promoter region of the gene controls the rate of transcription. (基因的启动子区域控制转录速率。) |近义词：sponsor, advocate, activator |反义词：inhibitor, suppressor
