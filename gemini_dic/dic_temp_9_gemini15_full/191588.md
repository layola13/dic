schooldom:/ˈskuːldəm/| n.|学校制度；学校界；学校生活|The world of schools and education; the social environment of a school.  It often implies the system, rules, and overall atmosphere of a school or educational institution, rather than just the physical building.|例句：He's been immersed in the schooldom for years, and it shows in his understanding of educational policies. 他沉浸在学校制度多年，这在他对教育政策的理解中可见一斑。|例句：The rigid schooldom stifled creativity and independent thinking. 僵化的学校制度扼杀了创造力和独立思考。|近义词：school system, educational system, academic world|反义词：informal education, home schooling
