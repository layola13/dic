mechanizing: /ˈmɛkənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 机械化；使机械化 | To make (something) automatic or mechanical; to introduce machines into (a process or industry).  | 例句：The factory is mechanizing its production line to increase efficiency. 工厂正在对其生产线进行机械化改造以提高效率。


The company is mechanizing its accounting department, replacing manual tasks with software.  这家公司正在将其会计部门机械化，用软件取代手工任务。| 近义词：automate, robotize | 反义词：manualize, demechanize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mechanizing the entire process. = 他们正在使整个流程机械化。
We mechanize our workflow to save time. = 我们使我们的工作流程机械化以节省时间。

一般过去时:
They mechanized the farm using tractors. = 他们使用拖拉机使农场机械化。
The factory mechanized its operations last year. = 该工厂去年使其运营机械化。

现在完成时:
They have mechanized many aspects of their business. = 他们已经将其业务的许多方面机械化。
We have mechanized the assembly line to improve output. = 我们已经使装配线机械化以提高产量。

过去完成时:
They had mechanized the factory before the new regulations came into effect. = 新规生效前，他们已经使工厂机械化。
The company had mechanized its data entry system by the time the merger occurred. = 合并发生时，该公司已经使其数据录入系统机械化。

将来时:
They will mechanize the harvesting process next year. = 他们明年将使收割过程机械化。
The government will mechanize the postal service in the near future. = 政府将在不久的将来使邮政服务机械化。

虚拟语气:
If we mechanized the system, we would save money. = 如果我们使系统机械化，我们将节省资金。
I wish we had mechanized the process earlier. = 我希望我们早点使这个过程机械化。

被动语态:
The process is being mechanized by the engineers. = 该过程正在由工程师们机械化。
The factory was mechanized in the 1980s. = 该工厂在20世纪80年代被机械化。

疑问句:
Is the company mechanizing its operations? = 公司正在使其运营机械化吗？
Will they mechanize the entire production line? = 他们会使整个生产线机械化吗？

否定句:
They did not mechanize the factory. = 他们没有使工厂机械化。
The company is not mechanizing its operations at this time. = 公司目前没有使其运营机械化。

词源:
From "mechanism" (a system of parts working together), ultimately from Greek "mēchanē" (machine, contrivance).  The "-izing" suffix indicates the process of making something.
