inherited:/ɪnˈherɪtɪd/ | adj. | 继承的，遗传的 | Received or derived from an ancestor or predecessor.  Something inherited is passed down through generations or received as a legacy. | Example:  He inherited his father's love of gardening. 他继承了他父亲对园艺的热爱。
She inherited a significant fortune from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一笔可观的财富。|近义词：received, acquired, bequeathed | 反义词：earned, self-made, original


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting is inherited by his son. = 这幅画被他儿子继承了。
This tradition is inherited from our ancestors. = 这一传统是继承自我们的祖先。

一般过去时:
They inherited a large house from their aunt. = 他们从他们的姑妈那里继承了一座大房子。
He inherited his father's business. = 他继承了他父亲的事业。


现在完成时:
She has inherited a valuable collection of stamps. = 她继承了一套珍贵的邮票收藏。
We have inherited a responsibility to protect the environment. = 我们肩负着保护环境的责任。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had inherited a substantial sum of money. = 他死前继承了一大笔钱。
By the time she turned 21, she had inherited the family estate. = 等到她21岁的时候，她已经继承了家族产业。


将来时:
They will inherit their parents' house when they pass away. = 他们的父母去世后，他们将继承他们的房子。
She will inherit a large fortune from her uncle. = 她将从她的叔叔那里继承一大笔财产。


虚拟语气:
If I inherited a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我继承了一百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had inherited my grandfather's artistic talent. = 我真希望我继承了我祖父的艺术天赋。


被动语态:
The property was inherited by the eldest son. = 这处房产由长子继承。
The title was inherited from his father. = 这个头衔是从他父亲那里继承来的。


疑问句:
Did he inherit anything from his parents? = 他从父母那里继承了什么吗？
What will be inherited by the next generation? = 下一代将继承什么？


否定句:
She did not inherit anything from her family. = 她没有从她的家族那里继承任何东西。
They haven't inherited any land from their ancestors. = 他们没有从他们的祖先那里继承任何土地。
