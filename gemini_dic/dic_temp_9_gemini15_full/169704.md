proprietor:/prəˈpraɪətər/| n. |所有人；业主；经营者 |The owner or manager of a business, especially a small one.  This word emphasizes the ownership and management of a business. |例句：He is the proprietor of a small bakery. 他是一家小面包店的老板。|例句：The proprietor greeted each customer warmly. 业主热情地接待每一位顾客。|近义词：owner, manager, keeper |反义词：employee, tenant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The proprietor manages the store efficiently. = 业主有效地管理着这家商店。
She owns and operates the restaurant. = 她拥有并经营着这家餐馆。

一般过去时:
The proprietor closed the shop early yesterday. = 业主昨天早早关了店。
He successfully ran the business for ten years. = 他成功地经营了这家企业十年。

现在完成时:
The proprietor has expanded the business significantly. = 业主已经大幅扩展了业务。
She has owned this property for over twenty years. = 她拥有这处房产已超过二十年。

过去完成时:
The proprietor had already hired new staff before the expansion. = 在扩张之前，业主已经雇佣了新员工。
He had run the bookstore for five years before selling it. = 他经营这家书店五年后才卖掉它。


将来时:
The proprietor will open a new branch next year. = 业主明年将开设一个新分店。
She will retire and sell the business next month. = 她下个月将退休并出售这家企业。


虚拟语气:
If I were the proprietor, I would invest in new technology. = 如果我是业主，我会投资新技术。
I wish I were the proprietor of a successful cafe. = 我希望我是家成功咖啡馆的老板。


被动语态:
The business was successfully run by the proprietor. = 这家企业由业主成功经营。
The property is owned by a local proprietor. = 这处房产归一位当地业主所有。


疑问句:
Who is the proprietor of this building? = 这座建筑的业主是谁？
Is he still the proprietor of the restaurant? = 他还是这家餐馆的老板吗？


否定句:
He is not the proprietor of that shop. = 他不是那家店的老板。
She is no longer the proprietor of the hotel. = 她不再是这家酒店的老板了。
