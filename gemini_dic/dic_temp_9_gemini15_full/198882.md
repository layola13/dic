sidetracks:/ˈsaɪdtræks/| v.|使偏离正轨；岔开话题|To divert from a main course or topic.|The unexpected phone call sidetracked me from my work.  意外的电话使我无法专心工作。|The meeting was sidetracked by a lengthy discussion about office supplies. 会议被关于办公用品的冗长讨论岔开了话题。|divert, distract, deflect|focus, concentrate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sidetracks the conversation whenever it gets too serious. 他只要谈话变得严肃起来就岔开话题。
The detour sidetracks the traffic flow. 这条绕行路线使交通中断。


一般过去时:
The accident sidetracked the project for several weeks. 事故使项目延误了几周。
She sidetracked the discussion by bringing up an unrelated issue. 她提出了一个无关紧要的问题，从而岔开了话题。


现在完成时:
We have been sidetracked by unforeseen circumstances. 我们因不可预见的情况而偏离了正轨。
The unexpected rain has sidetracked our outdoor plans. 突如其来的雨水打乱了我们的户外计划。


过去完成时:
They had been sidetracked by numerous delays before they finally completed the project. 在他们最终完成项目之前，他们已经被无数的延误耽搁了。
I had already been sidetracked several times before I realized I was going in the wrong direction. 在我意识到自己走错路之前，我已经几次偏离了方向。


将来时:
The new regulations will sidetrack the company's expansion plans. 新的法规将使公司的扩张计划偏离轨道。
I'm afraid that this problem will sidetrack our efforts. 我担心这个问题会阻碍我们的努力。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been sidetracked, he would have finished the report on time. 如果他没有被岔开话题，他就能按时完成报告。
If the project hadn't been sidetracked by funding issues, it would have been completed sooner. 如果该项目没有因资金问题而受阻，它本可以更快完成。


被动语态:
The project was sidetracked by budget cuts. 项目因预算削减而搁浅。
The conversation was constantly sidetracked by irrelevant comments.  谈话不断被无关的评论打断。


疑问句:
Was the investigation sidetracked by political interference? 调查是否受到了政治干预而偏离了方向？
Are we being sidetracked from the main goal? 我们是不是偏离了主要目标？


否定句:
The team was determined not to be sidetracked by minor setbacks.  团队决心不被小的挫折所阻碍。
He refused to be sidetracked from his mission. 他拒绝偏离自己的使命。


PHRASE->

sidetrack from: 从...偏离
sidetrack into: 偏离到...
sidetrack someone: 使某人分心
get sidetracked:  偏离正轨
be sidetracked by: 被...所阻碍
