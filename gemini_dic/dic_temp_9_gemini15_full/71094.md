fish-out:/fɪʃˈaʊt/ | vt. | 费力地找出；搜寻出；查出 | To search for and find something, often with difficulty.  |例句：He fished out a crumpled photograph from his pocket. 他从口袋里掏出一张皱巴巴的照片。|例句：The police fished out the evidence from the suspect's car. 警方从嫌疑人的车里搜出了证据。|近义词：retrieve, recover, extract, unearth |反义词：lose, misplace


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fishes out his keys from his bag every morning. 他每天早上都从包里掏出钥匙。
She fishes out information from obscure websites. 她从不知名的网站上搜索信息。


一般过去时:
I fished out a twenty-dollar bill from my wallet. 我从钱包里掏出一张二十美元的钞票。
They fished out a sunken treasure chest from the ocean floor. 他们从海底打捞出一个沉没的宝箱。


现在完成时:
She has fished out all the relevant documents for the meeting. 她已经为会议找到了所有相关的文件。
He has fished out the solution to the problem from a complex algorithm. 他已经从一个复杂的算法中找到了问题的解决方案。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had fished out all his notes from his briefcase. 在演示文稿之前，他已从公文包里拿出所有笔记。
By the time the police arrived, the thief had fished out all the valuables from the safe. 当警察到达时，小偷已经从保险箱里偷走了所有贵重物品。


将来时:
I will fish out the recipe for that delicious cake from my old cookbook. 我会从我的旧食谱中找出那个美味蛋糕的食谱。
She will fish out the answer to that question from the textbook. 她会从教科书中找出那个问题的答案。


虚拟语气:
If I could fish out the truth, I would. 如果我能查出真相，我会的。
I wish I could fish out the lost earring from the drain. 我真希望我能从下水道里找到那只丢失的耳环。


被动语态:
The lost ring was fished out from the river by a fisherman.  一枚丢失的戒指被一位渔民从河里捞了出来。
The important documents were fished out from the damaged files. 重要的文件是从损坏的文件中找出来的。


疑问句:
Can you fish out my favorite sweater from the laundry? 你能从脏衣服里把我的最爱毛衣找出来吗？
Where did you fish out that antique vase? 你在哪儿找到那个古董花瓶的？


否定句:
I couldn't fish out my phone from my cluttered bag. 我找不到手机，我的包太乱了。
They failed to fish out the cause of the accident. 他们没能查出事故的原因。


PHRASE->

fish out of water:  像离开水的鱼一样；感到局促不安
fish for compliments:  讨好；寻求赞美
fish something out of somewhere: 从某地找出某物
fish a person out of a difficult situation:  帮助某人摆脱困境
fish around for something:  到处寻找某物
