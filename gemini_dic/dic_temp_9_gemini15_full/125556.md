multisystem: /ˌmʌltiˈsɪstəm/ | adj. |多系统的；多系统的；多系统疾病的 | Affecting or involving multiple systems of the body.  A multisystem disease affects several organ systems simultaneously. | 例句：The patient presented with a multisystem disorder, affecting her respiratory, cardiovascular, and neurological systems.  这位病人表现出一种多系统疾病，影响了她的呼吸系统、心血管系统和神经系统。

例句：The new software is designed for multisystem operation. 这个新软件设计用于多系统操作。


近义词：polysystemic, systemic (in the context of affecting multiple systems) | 反义词：single-system, localized (in the context of affecting only one system) | 词性：形容词


词源:  The word "multisystem" is a combination of "multi-" (meaning "many" or "multiple") and "system" (referring to an organized set of interacting parts or components).  Its usage emerged with the increasing understanding of complex interactions within biological and technological systems.
