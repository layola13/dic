needle:/ˈniːdl/| n.|针；针状物|A thin, pointed piece of metal with an eye, used for sewing or knitting; something resembling a needle in shape or function.|例句：She threaded the needle and began to sew. 她穿针引线开始缝纫。

例句：The doctor used a needle to inject the medicine. 医生用针注射药物。


近义词：pin, spire, stylus|反义词：无


词源:源自古英语 *nǣdl,  最终源自原始日耳曼语 *naþlō.
