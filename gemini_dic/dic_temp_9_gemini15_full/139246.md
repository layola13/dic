obligato:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/| /ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/| adj. n.|  义务的；必须的；(音乐)华彩乐段 |  (adjective)  Required; obligatory; (noun) A musical passage that is a necessary part of a composition, typically a solo passage that is not optional. | 例句：The singer's obligato was a stunning display of vocal skill.  这位歌手的华彩乐段展现了令人惊叹的演唱技巧。  The attendance at the meeting is obligato for all members. 所有成员必须参加会议。 |近义词：mandatory, required, necessary, compulsory|反义词：optional, voluntary
