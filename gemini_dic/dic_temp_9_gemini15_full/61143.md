eloquent:/ˈɛləkwənt/ | adj. |雄辩的；流畅的；有口才的 |  characterized by forceful and fluent expression; persuasive speaking or writing.  |  His eloquent speech moved the audience to tears. 他雄辩的演讲使听众感动得流泪。 |  fluent, articulate, persuasive | inarticulate, speechless, tongue-tied | 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is eloquent in his delivery. = 他演讲很流畅。
Her writing is always eloquent and engaging. = 她的写作总是流畅且引人入胜。


一般过去时:
The lawyer presented an eloquent closing argument. = 律师做了精彩的结案陈词。
His speech was eloquent and well-received. = 他的演讲雄辩且受到好评。


现在完成时:
She has always been eloquent, even as a child. = 她从小就口才很好。
He has eloquently defended his position on many occasions. = 他在许多场合都雄辩地捍卫了自己的立场。


过去完成时:
The speaker had eloquently explained the complex issue before the audience. = 演讲者在听众面前已雄辩地解释了这个复杂的问题。
They had already heard many eloquent speeches before this one. = 在这次演讲之前，他们已经听过许多雄辩的演讲。


将来时:
She will eloquently express her gratitude in her acceptance speech. = 她将在获奖感言中雄辩地表达她的感激之情。
He will deliver an eloquent address at the conference. = 他将在会议上发表一篇雄辩的演讲。


虚拟语气:
If he were more eloquent, he would be a better politician. = 如果他口才更好，他会成为一个更好的政治家。
I wish I could be as eloquent as Martin Luther King Jr. = 我希望我的口才像马丁·路德·金那样好。


被动语态:
His ideas were eloquently expressed in his latest book. = 他的想法在他的最新著作中得到了雄辩的表达。
The matter was eloquently debated by both sides. = 双方都雄辩地辩论了此事。


疑问句:
Is his writing style eloquent enough? = 他的写作风格足够流畅吗？
Was the speech eloquent and convincing? = 演讲雄辩且令人信服吗？


否定句:
His response wasn't eloquent, but it was honest. = 他的回应不够流畅，但很诚实。
The article was not eloquent, lacking both style and substance. = 这篇文章不流畅，既缺乏文采也缺乏内容。


PHRASE->
eloquent speech 雄辩的演讲
eloquent writing 流畅的写作
eloquent speaker  雄辩的演说家
eloquently expressed 雄辩地表达
eloquent defense 雄辩的辩护


词源:
From Middle English *eloquent, borrowed from Anglo-French eloquent, from Latin eloquens, present participle of eloqui “to speak out,” from e- “out” + loqui “to speak.”
