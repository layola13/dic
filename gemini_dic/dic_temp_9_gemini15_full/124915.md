mudhole:/ˈmʌdhoʊl/| n. |泥坑，污泥坑 |A mudhole is a hole or depression in the ground filled with mud. It is often wet and messy.  |例句：The children were playing in the mudhole after the rain. 雨后孩子们在泥坑里玩耍。

例句：The car got stuck in a deep mudhole.  汽车陷进了深深的泥坑里。


近义词：mire, quagmire, bog |反义词：dry land, solid ground
