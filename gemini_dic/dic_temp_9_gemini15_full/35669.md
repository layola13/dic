cobble:/ˈkɒbl/| /ˈkɑːbl/| n. v.|鹅卵石；粗糙的石块；（尤指）用鹅卵石铺成的路；拙劣地制作；拼凑；| A cobblestone is a small, rounded stone, typically used to pave roads.  To cobble something together is to make or repair something quickly and roughly. |例句：The path was made of smooth, grey cobbles. 那条小路是用光滑的灰色鹅卵石铺成的。


The old house was cobbled together from various salvaged materials. 这栋旧房子是用各种回收材料拼凑起来的。|近义词：pebble, stone, patch, mend |反义词：pave (smoothly), construct (carefully)
