catastrophically:/ˌkætəˈstrɒfɪkli/| adv.|灾难性地；彻底地|Describes something happening in a disastrous or devastating way, often implying a complete failure or a severe negative consequence.|例句：The project failed catastrophically, resulting in significant financial losses.  该项目彻底失败，导致了巨大的经济损失。|例句：The bridge collapsed catastrophically during the storm.  桥梁在暴风雨中灾难性地倒塌了。|近义词：disastrously, devastatingly, ruinously, terribly|反义词：triumphantly, successfully, wonderfully
