heave:/hiːv/| /hiv/| vt. & vi. |用力举起；投掷；呕吐；喘息；起伏|To lift or throw something heavy with great effort; to rise and fall rhythmically; to vomit.|例句：He heaved the heavy box onto the truck. 他费力地把沉重的箱子搬到卡车上。|例句：The ship heaved violently in the storm. 船在暴风雨中剧烈摇晃。|近义词：lift, hoist, toss, throw, vomit|反义词：lower, descend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heaves the sack of potatoes. = 他举起那袋土豆。
The ocean heaves gently. = 海洋轻轻地起伏。


一般过去时:
She heaved a sigh of relief. = 她舒了一口气。
The boat heaved and pitched in the rough seas. = 船在汹涌的海浪中颠簸起伏。


现在完成时:
He has heaved all the stones into the cart. = 他已将所有石头都搬到手推车里了。
The ground has heaved slightly after the earthquake. = 地震后地面略微隆起。


过去完成时:
They had heaved the anchor before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经抛锚了。
The ship had heaved violently before it capsized. = 船倾覆前剧烈摇晃过。


将来时:
He will heave the anchor at dawn. = 他将在黎明时分起锚。
The waves will heave against the shore. = 海浪将拍打着海岸。


虚拟语气:
If he had been stronger, he could have heaved the stone. = 如果他更强壮些，他就能搬动那块石头了。
I wish I hadn't heaved up my dinner. = 我真希望我没有把晚饭吐出来。


被动语态:
The heavy stone was heaved onto the wagon. = 那块沉重的石头被搬上了马车。
The ship was heaved about by the waves. = 船被海浪冲来冲去。


疑问句:
Can you heave that rock? = 你能搬动那块石头吗？
Is the ship heaving badly? = 船剧烈晃动吗？


否定句:
He couldn't heave the weight. = 他搬不动那个重量。
The boat didn't heave much in the calm waters. = 船在平静的水域中没有怎么晃动。


PHRASE->
heave a sigh  =  叹气
heave into view =  出现
heave to =  (船)停驶
heave ho! =  (用力举起时的呼喊)
heave-ho = 用力抛掷


词源:
The word "heave" comes from Old English *hebban, meaning "to lift, raise".  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
