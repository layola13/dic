nonemotional:/ˌnɒnɪˈmoʊʃənəl/| adj. |非情感的，冷静的 |Not showing or expressing emotion.  |例句：He remained nonemotional throughout the trial. 他在整个审判过程中都保持冷静。|例句：Her nonemotional response surprised everyone. 她冷静的反应让每个人都感到意外。|近义词：unemotional, apathetic, detached, impassive |反义词：emotional, passionate, feeling, sentimental

She appears nonemotional, but she cares deeply. = 她看起来很冷静，但她内心深处很关心。
