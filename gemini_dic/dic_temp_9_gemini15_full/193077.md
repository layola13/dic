sectarism:/ˈsɛktərɪzəm/| n.|宗派主义；派系主义|The belief that one's own sect or group is superior to others, often leading to prejudice and discrimination.  It involves excessive devotion to a particular sect, party, or cause, often to the exclusion of others.  This can manifest in various forms, from subtle biases to outright hostility and violence.|例句：Religious sectariansm has been a major cause of conflict throughout history.  宗教宗派主义是历史上冲突的主要原因之一。

例句：Political sectariansm can paralyze the government and prevent progress. 政治派系主义会使政府瘫痪并阻碍进步。


近义词：bigotry, factionalism, parochialism, tribalism|反义词：tolerance, inclusiveness, ecumenism


词源：源自于拉丁语 "secta"，意思是"派别"，"学派"。后缀 "-ism" 表示一种思想或体系。
