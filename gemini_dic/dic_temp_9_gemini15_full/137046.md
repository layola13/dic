nonstickiest: /ˈnɒnˈstɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 最不粘的 | Most not sticky; least adhesive.  This word is the superlative form of "nonsticky". It describes something that is the least likely to adhere to other surfaces. | Example:  The nonstickiest pan made cooking a breeze. 最不粘的平底锅让烹饪变得轻而易举。  | 近义词: least sticky, least adhesive | 反义词: stickiest, most adhesive
