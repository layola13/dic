nonlovers:/ˈnɑːnˌlʌvərz/ | n. |非恋人，不相爱的人 |People who are not in a romantic relationship.  This term often implies a lack of romantic feelings between individuals who may know each other or even be in close proximity.  It can refer to friends, family members, acquaintances, or even people who actively dislike each other.| Example:  They are just nonlovers who work together. 他们只是在同一个地方工作，并非恋人。|近义词：strangers, acquaintances, non-partners|反义词：lovers, partners
