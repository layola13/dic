concavo: /ˈkɒŋkeɪvoʊ/ | adj. | 凹的，凹面的 | Concave means curving inward like the inside of a bowl or sphere.  It describes a surface that is hollowed or curved inward. | Example: The concave lens dispersed the light.  凹透镜分散了光线。  The concave mirror reflected the image. 凹面镜反射了图像。 | 近义词:  hollowed, indented, curved inward | 反义词: convex, bulging, rounded outward
