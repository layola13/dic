raid:/reɪd/| n. vt.|袭击，突袭；搜查|A sudden attack on a place, especially by soldiers or police.  Also, a sudden and unauthorized search of a place.|例句：The police conducted a raid on the suspected drug dealer's house.  警方对涉嫌贩毒者的住所进行了突击搜查。

例句：The enemy launched a surprise raid on our position. 敌人对我们的阵地发起了突然袭击。|近义词：attack, assault, invasion, search|反义词：defense, protection|词性:n. vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police raid suspicious houses regularly. = 警方定期突击搜查可疑房屋。
He raids the fridge at night. = 他晚上经常偷偷摸摸地吃冰箱里的东西。


一般过去时:
The army raided the enemy camp last night. = 军队昨晚袭击了敌人的营地。
They raided the museum and stole valuable artifacts. = 他们洗劫了博物馆，偷走了珍贵的文物。


现在完成时:
The rebels have raided several villages this year. = 叛军今年已经袭击了几个村庄。
The police have raided the building three times. = 警方已经三次突击搜查了该建筑物。


过去完成时:
The thieves had raided the bank before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经抢劫了银行。
They had raided the store several times in the past. = 过去他们已经几次洗劫了这家商店。


将来时:
The military will raid the terrorist hideout tomorrow. = 军方将在明天突袭恐怖分子的藏匿处。
We will raid the pantry for snacks. = 我们将搜刮储藏室找零食。


虚拟语气:
If they raided the building tonight, they might find evidence. = 如果他们今晚突击搜查这栋楼，可能会找到证据。
I wish they hadn't raided my apartment. = 我真希望他们没有搜查我的公寓。


被动语态:
The house was raided by the police. = 这所房子被警方搜查了。
The village was raided by bandits last week. = 上周村庄遭到土匪袭击。


疑问句:
Did the police raid the suspect's apartment? = 警方是否搜查了嫌疑人的公寓？
Will they raid the building tonight? = 他们今晚会突击搜查那栋楼吗？


否定句:
The police didn't raid the building. = 警方没有搜查那栋楼。
We didn't raid the cookie jar. = 我们没有偷吃饼干。


PHRASE->
air raid: 空袭
police raid: 警方突击搜查
dawn raid: 黎明突袭
raid on: 对…的袭击
Treasury raid: 财政部突击检查
