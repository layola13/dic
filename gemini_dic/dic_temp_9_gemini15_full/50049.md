denounced:/dɪˈnaʊnst/| vt.|公开谴责；告发；声讨|To publicly condemn or criticize someone or something.  It often implies strong disapproval and may suggest the action is wrong or harmful.|例句：The politician denounced the government's new policies.  这位政治家谴责了政府的新政策。

例句：The human rights group denounced the violence against protesters. 人权组织谴责了针对抗议者的暴力行为。

近义词：condemn, criticize, censure, rebuke|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词
