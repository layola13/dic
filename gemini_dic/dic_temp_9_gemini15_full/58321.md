dryfoot:/ˈdraɪfʊt/| adj.|旱路的；徒步的；|Dryfoot refers to travel or a journey made on land, without using a boat or other watercraft. It emphasizes the absence of water travel.|例句：We made a dryfoot journey across the desert. 我们徒步穿越了沙漠。
例句：The explorers preferred a dryfoot route to avoid the river. 探险家们选择了一条旱路路线以避免河流。|近义词：landward, overland|反义词：sea-borne, waterborne
