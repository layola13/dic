pansy:/ˈpænzɪ/| n. |三色堇；（贬义）娘娘腔|A pansy is a type of flowering plant with colorful petals, often in shades of purple, yellow, and white.  The term can also be used as a derogatory slang term for a man who is considered effeminate. |例句：She planted pansies in her garden. 她在她花园里种了三色堇。|例句：He was called a pansy for being afraid to fight. 他因为不敢打架而被叫做娘娘腔。|近义词：violet (for the flower), sissy (derogatory)|反义词：masculine (opposite of the derogatory meaning)
