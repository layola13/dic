roulade:/ruːˈlɑːd/| n.|肉卷；卷状食品|A roulade is a dish of meat, often poultry or beef, that is thinly sliced, pounded flat, and then rolled around a filling.  It is then often roasted or braised.  The term can also refer to other rolled foods, such as sponge cakes or crêpes.|The chef prepared a delicious chicken roulade with mushroom duxelles.|近义词：roll, wrap|反义词：无
