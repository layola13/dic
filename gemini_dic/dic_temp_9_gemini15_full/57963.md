drippingly:/ˈdrɪpɪŋli/| adv.| 滴答地；湿淋淋地；夸张地；过分地 | Extremely or excessively; in a way that suggests something is excessive or overly sentimental.  |例句：He spoke drippingly of his love for her. 他夸张地表达了他对她爱情。
例句：The rain was coming down drippingly, soaking everything. 雨滴答滴答地下着，把一切都淋湿了。|近义词：excessively, overly, profusely, sentimentally |反义词：dryly, sparingly, minimally
