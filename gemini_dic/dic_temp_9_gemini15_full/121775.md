misreaders:/ˈmɪsˌriːdər/| n. |误读者，理解错误的人 |People who misunderstand or interpret something incorrectly.  They may lack the necessary knowledge, context, or critical thinking skills to understand the intended meaning.  Misreaders often draw inaccurate conclusions or form biased opinions based on their own preconceptions. |Example: The misreaders of the article focused solely on the headline and ignored the nuanced explanation within.  误读这篇文章的人只关注标题，忽略了文章中细致的解释。 |近义词：misinterpreters, wrong interpreters |反义词：experts, scholars
