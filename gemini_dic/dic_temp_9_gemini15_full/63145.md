enstatite:/ˈɛnstətaɪt/| n. |顽火辉石|Enstatite is a magnesium-rich pyroxene mineral.  It is a common mineral in mafic and ultramafic igneous rocks, and is also found in metamorphic rocks.  Its chemical formula is (Mg,Fe)SiO3.
