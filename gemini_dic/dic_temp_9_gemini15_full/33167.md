USAGE->
This is not applicable to a possessive form like "chrome's".  Possessive forms do not conjugate.  The verb conjugation would apply to verbs used *with* "chrome's", such as "Chrome's update *is* amazing."  The "is" would be conjugated, not "chrome's".
