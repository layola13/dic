monstrosities:/mɒnˈstrɒsətiz/| n.|畸形物；怪诞的事物；令人震惊的事物|Extremely ugly, deformed, or offensive things; things that are shocking or horrifying because of their size, appearance, or nature.|The abandoned factory was filled with rusty machinery and other monstrosities.  废弃的工厂里堆满了生锈的机器和其他畸形物。|The architect's design was considered a monstrosity by many. 许多人认为这位建筑师的设计是畸形的。|abominations, horrors, deformities|beauties, masterpieces|名词
