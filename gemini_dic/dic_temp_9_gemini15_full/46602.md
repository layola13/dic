damasks:/ˈdæməsks/| n.|达马斯革织锦，提花织物|A damask is a reversible fabric with a pattern woven into it, often in a satin or similar weave.  It's typically luxurious and used for tablecloths, curtains, and upholstery.|例句：The dining room table was adorned with a beautiful damask tablecloth. 餐厅的桌子铺着一条美丽的达马斯革桌布。

The curtains were made of heavy damask, adding elegance to the room. 窗帘是用厚重的达马斯革织成的，为房间增添了优雅。|近义词：brocade, tapestry|反义词：plain fabric
