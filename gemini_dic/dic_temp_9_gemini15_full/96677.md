indwell:/ɪnˈdwel/| v.|居住，存在于，萦绕于心头|To live in; to reside in. To exist within; to be inherent in.  To dwell mentally or emotionally upon; to be constantly occupied by a thought or feeling.|例句：Peace indwells in her heart.  宁静存在于她的心中。The spirit of innovation indwells this company.  创新的精神存在于这家公司。|近义词：reside, inhabit, dwell, pervade|反义词：leave, depart, vacate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The spirit indwells the community. =  这种精神存在于这个社区。
He indwells in the mountains. = 他住在山上。


一般过去时:
