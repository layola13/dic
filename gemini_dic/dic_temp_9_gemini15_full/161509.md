potentize:/ˈpoʊtənˌtaɪz/ | vt. |增强效力；赋予潜能；增强效力 |To increase the effectiveness or potency of something.  This often refers to enhancing the effects of a medicine, a substance, or even an idea.  |Example: The herbalist claimed to potentize the healing properties of the plant by using a special extraction method. 草药师声称通过一种特殊的提取方法增强了植物的药效。 |近义词：strengthen, amplify, enhance, boost |反义词：weaken, diminish, reduce, impair
