centre:/ˈsɛntər/ /ˈsɛntə(r)/| n. |中心，中央|The centre of the city is very busy. 城市中心非常繁忙。The earth is the centre of our solar system. 地球是我们太阳系的中心。|近义词：middle, heart, core |反义词：periphery, edge, outside |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The centre of the town is always crowded. = 城镇中心总是很拥挤。
The museum is located in the city centre. = 博物馆位于市中心。

一般过去时:
The meeting was held at the conference centre. = 会议在会议中心举行。
We visited the cultural centre last week. = 我们上周参观了文化中心。


现在完成时:
The city centre has undergone significant changes. = 市中心发生了巨大的变化。
They have moved their office to the new business centre. = 他们已将办公室搬到了新的商务中心。


过去完成时:
The project had already been completed before the centre opened. = 中心开放之前，项目就已经完成了。
We had never been to such a large shopping centre before. = 我们以前从未去过这么大的购物中心。


将来时:
They will build a new sports centre next year. = 他们明年将建造一个新的体育中心。
The concert will be held at the city centre. = 音乐会将在市中心举行。


虚拟语气:
If the centre were closer, I would go more often. = 如果中心更近一些，我会更经常去。
I wish the community centre were bigger. = 我希望社区中心更大一些。


被动语态:
The centre was damaged by the storm. = 中心被风暴破坏了。
The new building is being constructed in the centre of town. = 新建筑正在城镇中心建造。


疑问句:
Is the centre open today? = 中心今天开门吗？
Where is the centre of gravity? = 重心在哪里？


否定句:
The centre was not open yesterday. = 中心昨天没有开门。
The problem is not in the centre of the picture. = 问题不在图片中心。


PHRASE->
city centre: 市中心
town centre: 城镇中心
shopping centre: 购物中心
business centre: 商务中心
conference centre: 会议中心

词源:
源自古法语“centre”，最终源自拉丁语“centrum”，意为“中心点”。
