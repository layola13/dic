negligence:/ˈneɡlɪdʒəns/ | n. | 疏忽；过失；玩忽职守 | The state of being careless or inattentive to a person, thing, or duty. It often involves a failure to exercise the care that a reasonable person would exercise in a similar situation.  | Example:  The accident was caused by the negligence of the driver.  这次事故是由于司机的疏忽造成的。

The company's negligence led to a significant data breach. 公司的疏忽导致了重大的数据泄露。| carelessness, neglect, oversight, inattention | diligence, care, attentiveness | 名词
