sarculation:/ˌsɑːrkjuˈleɪʃən/| n. | 循环；流通；发行量 | The circulation of blood throughout the body is essential for life.  血液在全身的循环对生命至关重要。|近义词：circulation, distribution |反义词：stagnation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood circulates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。
The newspaper has a large circulation. = 这份报纸发行量很大。


一般过去时:
The rumour circulated quickly among the students. = 谣言迅速在学生中传播。
The company circulated a memo to all employees. = 公司向所有员工分发了备忘录。


现在完成时:
The book has circulated widely since its publication. = 自出版以来，这本书已广泛流传。
The news has circulated throughout the town. = 这个消息已经在镇上广为流传。


过去完成时:
The story had circulated for years before I heard it. = 我听到这个故事之前，它已经流传多年了。
The information had circulated widely before the meeting. = 会议之前，信息已经广泛传播。


将来时:
The new policy will circulate among the staff next week. = 新政策下周将在员工中传阅。
The article will circulate widely online. = 这篇文章将在网上广泛传播。


虚拟语气:
If the rumour had not circulated, the damage would have been less. = 如果谣言没有传播，损害就会小得多。
I wish the news hadn't circulated so quickly. = 我希望这个消息没有传播得这么快。


被动语态:
The memo was circulated to all departments. = 备忘录已分发给所有部门。
The information is being circulated to the relevant parties. = 信息正在传播给相关各方。


疑问句:
How many copies of the magazine are in circulation? = 这本杂志的发行量是多少？
Does the rumour circulate widely? = 这个谣言传播广泛吗？


否定句:
The blood didn't circulate properly due to the injury. = 由于受伤，血液循环不畅。
The document wasn't circulated to all employees. = 文件没有分发给所有员工。
