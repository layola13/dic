gill:/dʒɪl/| n. |鳃；(液体)吉尔；四分之一品脱|A gill is a respiratory organ of a fish or other aquatic animal, typically one of a pair situated behind the head.  It's also a unit of liquid measure equal to a quarter of a pint.|例句：The fish used its gills to breathe underwater. 这条鱼用鳃在水下呼吸。

The gills of the fish were bright red. 这条鱼的鳃是鲜红色的。


近义词：branchia (anatomy), quarter-pint (measurement)|反义词：None|词性: 名词
