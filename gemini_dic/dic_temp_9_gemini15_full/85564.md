hateful: /ˈheɪtfl/ | adj. | 可憎的；令人厌恶的 | Inspiring or deserving hatred; extremely unpleasant.  |例句：His hateful comments caused a lot of hurt.  他的可憎言论造成了许多伤害。

例句：The hateful weather made the trip unpleasant.  令人厌恶的天气使旅程不愉快。


近义词：odious, abhorrent, detestable, repugnant, loathsome | 反义词：lovely, delightful, charming, pleasant


词源:  The word "hateful" comes from the Old English word "hātful," which is a combination of "hāt" (meaning "hate") and the suffix "-ful" (meaning "full of").  Therefore, "hateful" literally means "full of hate."
