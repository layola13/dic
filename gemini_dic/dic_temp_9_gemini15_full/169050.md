program:/ˈproʊɡræm/ /ˈprəʊɡræm/| n. v.| 程序；节目；规划；项目；课程| A set of instructions or rules that a computer follows to perform a specific task; a plan or schedule of activities or events; a course of study.|例句：The computer program crashed.  电脑程序崩溃了。|例句：The television program started late. 电视节目开播晚了。|近义词：plan, scheme, schedule, course|反义词：chaos, disorder
