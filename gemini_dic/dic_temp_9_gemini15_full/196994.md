shakedowns:/ˈʃeɪkdaʊnz/ | n. |敲诈；勒索；彻底搜查；初步检验 | A shakedown is an act of extortion, typically involving threats or intimidation to obtain money or other valuables. It can also refer to a thorough search or inspection, or an initial test or trial.  | Example: The police conducted a shakedown of the suspect's apartment.  警方对嫌疑人的公寓进行了彻底搜查。  The mobster demanded a shakedown from the local businesses.  黑帮分子向当地商家勒索钱财。 |近义词：extortion, blackmail, robbery, search, inspection |反义词：donation, gift, leniency
