semiwildly: /ˈsɛmiˈwaɪldli/ | adv. | 半野生地，似野生地 |  Describes something that is partly wild or behaving in a way that is partly wild.  It suggests a state between fully domesticated and completely wild. | 例句：The horses lived semiwildly in the vast plains.  这些马在广阔的平原上过着半野生的生活。

The plants grew semiwildly along the riverbank. 植物沿着河岸半野生地生长。


近义词：partly wild, somewhat wild, untamed (partially) | 反义词：fully domesticated, completely tamed, cultivated


词性: 副词
