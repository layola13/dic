slang:/slæŋ/ | n. |俚语，行话|Informal language consisting of words and phrases that are regarded as very informal, are typically more common in speech than writing, and are used by a particular group of people.  Slang is often characterized by its novelty and its tendency to change rapidly.|例句：The teenagers were speaking in slang that I couldn't understand.  那些青少年说的是我听不懂的俚语。|近义词：colloquialism, jargon, vernacular|反义词：formal language, standard English
