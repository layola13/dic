devolutionaries:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənɛriːz/| n.|主张权力下放者；地方分权主义者|Supporters of devolution, the transfer of power from a central government to regional or local governments.  They advocate for greater autonomy and self-governance for subnational entities.|Example: The devolutionaries campaigned vigorously for more control over local resources and taxation.  主张权力下放者积极争取对当地资源和税收的更多控制权。|近义词： decentralizers, autonomists |反义词： centralizers, unitarists |名词
