semiquavers:/ˈsɛmɪˌkweɪvərz/| n.|十六分音符|A semiquaver is a musical note that is equal to 1/16 of a whole note or a 1/8 of a crotchet (quarter note). It is typically represented as a black note with a small flag.  |Example:  The fast passage included many semiquavers, making it challenging to play.  这段快速的乐句包含许多十六分音符，演奏起来很有挑战性。  |近义词：sixteenth note (美式)|反义词：whole note, half note, quarter note|名词
