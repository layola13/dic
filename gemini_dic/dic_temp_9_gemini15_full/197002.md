shakes: /ʃeɪks/ | v. | 摇晃；颤抖；震动 | To move quickly and slightly from side to side or up and down, or to make something do this.  |例句：The leaves were shaking in the wind. 叶子在风中摇晃。
The earthquake shook the building violently. 地震剧烈地摇晃了建筑物。|近义词：tremble, quiver, vibrate |反义词：still, steady


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shakes his head in disbelief. 他难以置信地摇摇头。
The tree shakes its leaves in the breeze.  树在微风中摇动树叶。


一般过去时:
She shook her head sadly. 她悲伤地摇了摇头。
The ground shook violently during the earthquake. 地震期间地面剧烈摇晃。


现在完成时:
The building has shaken after the explosion. 爆炸后，建筑物一直在摇晃。
He has shaken hands with the president. 他已经和总统握过手了。


过去完成时:
The old house had shaken in countless storms. 这座老房子经历过无数的风暴。
The glass had shaken so violently that it broke. 玻璃剧烈震动，碎了。


将来时:
The wind will shake the trees tomorrow. 明天风会摇动树木。
I think the building will shake during the concert. 我认为音乐会期间建筑物会震动。


虚拟语气:
If I shook that tree, all the apples would fall. 如果我摇晃那棵树，所有的苹果都会掉下来。
I wish the ground hadn't shaken so hard. 我希望地面没有那么剧烈地摇晃。


被动语态:
The house was shaken by the explosion. 房子被爆炸震动了。
The picture was shaken and fell off the wall.  画被震动掉了下来。


疑问句:
Does the building shake often? 这座建筑物经常摇晃吗？
Are you shaking? 你在发抖吗？


否定句:
The building doesn't shake easily. 这座建筑物不容易摇晃。
He didn't shake my hand. 他没有和我握手。
