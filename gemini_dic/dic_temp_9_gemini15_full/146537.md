overglamourized:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməraɪzd/| adj.|过度美化的，过于渲染的| Excessively glamorized; presented or depicted in a way that is more glamorous than is actually the case.  It often implies an unrealistic or artificial portrayal. |例句：The movie overglamourized the life of a rock star, ignoring his struggles with addiction.|近义词：idealized, romanticized, embellished,  exaggerated |反义词：understated, downplayed, realistic
