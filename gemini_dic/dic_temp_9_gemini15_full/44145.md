criterions:/kraɪˈtɪəriən/| n.|标准；准则；衡量尺度|A standard by which something can be judged or decided.  Plural of criterion.|例句：The main criterion for selection was experience. 选择的主要标准是经验。

例句：The criteria for admission to the university are very strict.  大学录取标准非常严格。


近义词：standard, norm, measure, benchmark, guideline

反义词：无明确反义词，可根据语境用lack of standards等表达。

词性：名词
