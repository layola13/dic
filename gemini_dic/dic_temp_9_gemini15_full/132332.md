nondeformed:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɔːrmd/| adj. |未变形的，未扭曲的 |Not deformed; remaining in its original shape or form.  The term often implies a contrast with a deformed state, suggesting that some external force or process could have caused deformation but did not. |例句：The bridge remained nondeformed after the earthquake.  地震后桥梁保持未变形。|The crystal structure showed a nondeformed lattice. 晶体结构显示出未变形的晶格。|近义词：undeformed, intact, unaltered, unchanged |反义词：deformed, distorted, warped, misshapen
