kitschy:/ˈkɪtʃi/| adj.|俗气的，庸俗的，矫揉造作的|Characterized by exaggerated sentimentality or superficial prettiness; gaudy.  It often refers to something that is considered to be in poor taste, but can also be appreciated ironically or for its nostalgic value.|例句：The restaurant's decor was rather kitschy, with bright pink walls and plastic flamingos.|近义词：tacky, gaudy, cheap, cheesy|反义词：tasteful, elegant, refined, sophisticated
