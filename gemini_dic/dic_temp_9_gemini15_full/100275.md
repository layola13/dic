intersect:/ˈɪntərˌsɛkt/ | v. | 交叉，相交；(道路、河流等)相交；(线段等)相交于一点；（观点、利益等）冲突 | To meet or cross at a point.  | The two roads intersect at the traffic lights.  两条路在交通灯处交叉。| The lines intersect at a right angle.  两条线成直角相交。 | cross, overlap, coincide | diverge, separate | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lines intersect at this point. = 这两条线在此点相交。
The two rivers intersect near the town. = 两条河流在小镇附近交汇。

一般过去时:
Their paths intersected unexpectedly in Paris. = 他们在巴黎意外地相遇了。
The train tracks intersected the highway. = 火车轨道与高速公路交叉。

现在完成时:
Their lives have intersected in many surprising ways. = 他们的生活以许多令人惊讶的方式交织在一起。
Our paths have intersected several times this year. = 今年我们的道路已经多次交汇。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, their lives had never intersected. = 事故发生前，他们的生活从未有过交集。
The two projects had intersected in several key areas. = 这两个项目在几个关键领域有所重叠。

将来时:
Their journeys will intersect again next year. = 他们的旅程明年将再次交汇。
The new road will intersect the existing highway. = 新路将与现有高速公路交叉。


虚拟语气:
If their paths had intersected earlier, things might have been different. = 如果他们的道路更早地交汇，情况可能会有所不同。
If the two lines intersected, it would form a right angle. = 如果两条线相交，将形成一个直角。

被动语态:
The highway is intersected by several smaller roads. = 高速公路与几条较小的道路交叉。
The two plans were found to intersect significantly. = 发现这两个计划有很大的重叠。


疑问句:
Where do the two streets intersect? = 这两条街在哪里交叉？
Do these two lines intersect? = 这两条线相交吗？


否定句:
The two circles do not intersect. = 这两个圆不相交。
Their interests did not intersect at all. = 他们的兴趣一点也不相同。


PHRASE->
intersect at a point = 在一点相交
intersect with = 与...相交
intersect each other = 互相交叉
intersect diagonally = 斜交
intersect at right angles = 成直角相交
