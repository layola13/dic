militaries:/ˈmɪlɪtɛriz/| n.|军队；军事力量|The plural of "military," referring to the armed forces of multiple countries or regions.  It often implies a comparison or collective consideration of different militaries.|例句：The global implications of the conflict involved the coordination of several different militaries.|近义词：armed forces, armies, navies, air forces|反义词：civilian populations
