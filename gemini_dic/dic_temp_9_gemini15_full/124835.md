mucking:/ˈmʌkɪŋ/| v.| 弄脏；乱搞；辛勤劳作 |To make dirty or messy; to work hard and diligently, often in unpleasant conditions. |例句：He spent the afternoon mucking out the stable. 他下午花了时间清理马厩。

例句：The children were mucking around in the mud. 孩子们在泥地里玩耍。

近义词：messing, soiling, toiling, slaving

反义词：cleaning, tidying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mucking around in the garden. = 他们在花园里乱玩。
He is mucking out the stables every morning. = 他每天早上都清理马厩。


一般过去时:
We mucked around on the beach all day. = 我们在沙滩上玩了一整天。
She mucked out the chicken coop before dinner. = 她在晚餐前清理了鸡舍。


现在完成时:
They have mucked up the whole project. = 他们把整个项目都搞砸了。
I have been mucking around trying to fix this computer. = 我一直在努力修理这台电脑，却一团糟。


过去完成时:
They had mucked up the kitchen before the party started. = 派对开始前，他们把厨房弄得乱七八糟。
He had mucked around for years before finally finding a job. = 他混迹多年后才最终找到工作。


将来时:
We will be mucking out the barn tomorrow. = 我们明天将清理谷仓。
They will muck up the deal if they are not careful. = 他们如果不小心，就会把交易搞砸。


虚拟语气:
If he had mucked in, the job would have been finished sooner. = 如果他帮忙的话，工作就会早点完成。
I wish I hadn't mucked around so much in my youth. = 我真希望我年轻的时候不要那么混日子。


被动语态:
The garden was mucked up by the storm. = 花园被暴风雨弄得乱七八糟。
The stable will be mucked out later today. = 马厩将在今天晚些时候清理。


疑问句:
Are they mucking about again? = 他们又在胡闹了吗？
Was the car mucked up in the accident? = 汽车在事故中被弄坏了吗？


否定句:
They didn't muck about when it was time to work. = 到该工作的时候，他们没有嬉戏玩耍。
She didn't want to muck out the pigsty. = 她不想清理猪圈。


PHRASE->
