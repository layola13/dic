precombines:/ˌpriːkəmˈbaɪnz/ | v. |预先组合；预先混合 | To combine or mix beforehand.  This word is rarely used and is more of a technical term. It implies combining elements before a larger process or application. |Example: The ingredients were precombined before being added to the main mixture.  预先混合的成分在加入主要混合物之前已经预先混合。|近义词：premix, blend beforehand, combine in advance | 反义词：combine later, mix afterwards
