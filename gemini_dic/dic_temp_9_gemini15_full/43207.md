crabs:/kræbz/| n.|螃蟹|A crustacean with ten legs, of which the front pair are usually modified as claws.  Crabs are found in various habitats, from shallow coastal waters to deep oceans.|例句：We caught several crabs in the rock pools. 我们在岩石池中抓到几只螃蟹。|例句：The crab scuttled sideways across the sand. 螃蟹侧着身子横穿沙滩。|近义词：shellfish, crustacean|反义词:None specifically.
