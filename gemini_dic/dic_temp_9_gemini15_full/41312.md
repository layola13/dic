corecipient:/ˌkoʊrɪˈsɪpiənt/| n. |共同接受者，共同获得者 |A corecipient is a person who receives something, such as an award, at the same time as someone else.  It implies a shared achievement or recognition.| Example:  "She was a corecipient of the Nobel Prize in Literature." = 她与他人共同获得了诺贝尔文学奖。|近义词：joint recipient, shared recipient |反义词：sole recipient, individual recipient |名词
