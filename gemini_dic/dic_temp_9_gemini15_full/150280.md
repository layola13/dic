panegyrist:/ˈpænədʒərɪst/| n. |颂扬者，赞美者 |A panegyrist is a person who delivers or writes a panegyric, a formal speech or writing praising someone or something.  They often focus on the positive aspects and achievements of the subject, sometimes to an excessive degree. |Example: The panegyrist eloquently praised the general's bravery and leadership during the war.  颂扬者雄辩地赞扬了将军在战争中的勇敢和领导才能。|近义词：eulogist,praiser,flatterer|反义词：critic,detractor
