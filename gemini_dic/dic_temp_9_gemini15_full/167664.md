pricket:/ˈprɪkɪt/| n.|小鹿角；尖钉；|A pricket is a male deer's horn in its second year, when it is still small and pointed. It can also refer to a small, sharp point or spike.|例句：The young stag grew a small pricket.  这头年轻的雄鹿长出了一对小鹿角。|例句：He stepped on a pricket and hurt his foot. 他踩到一个尖钉，伤了脚。|近义词：spike, point, antler (in the context of deer antlers)|反义词：large antler|名词
