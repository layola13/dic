hispanicizes:/ˌhɪspænɪˈsaɪzɪz/| vt.| 使具有西班牙裔特征；使西班牙化 | To cause (something) to become Hispanic or to adopt Hispanic characteristics.  The process involves cultural assimilation, often including language adoption and integration into Hispanic communities. | 例句：The restaurant hispanicizes its menu to appeal to a wider customer base.  这家餐馆使其菜单具有西班牙特色以吸引更广泛的顾客群。 The city's rapid growth hispanicized the previously homogenous population.  这座城市快速发展使之前同质的人口变得具有西班牙裔特征。| 近义词：Latinize, Hispanify | 反义词：de-Hispanicize
