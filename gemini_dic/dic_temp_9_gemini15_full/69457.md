feigning:/ˈfeɪnɪŋ/| v. |假装，伪装|The act of pretending; simulating something that is not true.  It implies a deliberate attempt to deceive or create a false impression. |例句：He was feigning sleep when his mother entered the room. 他母亲进房间时他假装睡着了。


例句：She was feigning illness to avoid going to school. 她假装生病以逃避上学。


近义词：pretending, simulating, affecting, sham


反义词：being genuine, being truthful, being honest


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feigns ignorance. = 他假装不知道。
She feigns interest in his work. = 她假装对他的工作感兴趣。

一般过去时:
He feigned surprise at the news. = 他假装对这个消息感到惊讶。
She feigned illness to get out of work. = 她假装生病以逃避工作。

现在完成时:
He has feigned innocence throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，他一直假装无辜。
She has feigned a headache to avoid the party. = 她一直假装头痛以避免参加聚会。

过去完成时:
He had feigned sleep until his parents left. = 他一直假装睡觉直到父母离开。
She had feigned interest in his hobby for years. = 她多年来一直假装对他的爱好感兴趣。

将来时:
He will feign illness to skip the meeting. = 他将假装生病以逃避会议。
She will feign ignorance when questioned. = 她被质问时将假装不知情。

虚拟语气:
If he feigned innocence, no one would believe him. = 如果他假装无辜，没有人会相信他。
I wish she hadn't feigned illness; we missed her. = 我真希望她没装病；我们想念她。

被动语态:
His illness was feigned. = 他的病是装的。
Her surprise was clearly feigned. = 她的惊讶显然是装出来的。

疑问句:
Was he feigning illness? = 他是在装病吗？
Is she feigning ignorance? = 她是在装傻吗？

否定句:
He didn't feign interest in the conversation. = 他对谈话并不假装感兴趣。
She didn't feign surprise; she was genuinely shocked. = 她并没有假装惊讶；她真的被吓到了。
