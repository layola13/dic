encumbrance: /ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/ | n. | 妨碍；累赘；负担；抵押 | A burden or impediment; something that hinders or obstructs; a legal claim or lien on a property.  |The large mortgage was a significant encumbrance on their finances.  大型抵押贷款对他们的财务造成了很大的负担。


The excessive paperwork proved to be a considerable encumbrance.  过多的文书工作证明是一个相当大的累赘。| impediment, hindrance, burden, liability, restriction | freedom, ease, relief |  n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mortgage is an encumbrance on the property. = 这笔抵押贷款是对该房产的妨碍。
This extra work is an encumbrance to my progress. = 这额外的工作妨碍了我的进度。


一般过去时:
The debt was a heavy encumbrance on his family. = 这笔债务沉重地压在他的家庭上。
Unexpected expenses were a major encumbrance last month. = 上个月意外的开支是一个很大的负担。


现在完成时:
The legal battle has been an encumbrance for years. = 这场法律战已经持续多年，成为一种负担。
Many regulations have encumbered the business. = 许多规章制度已经妨碍了这项业务。


过去完成时:
The inheritance had been encumbered by prior debts. = 遗产之前已经被债务抵押了。
They had been encumbered by the complicated procedures. = 他们已经被复杂的程序所拖累。


将来时:
The new regulations will encumber small businesses. = 新的规章制度将会妨碍小型企业。
Further delays will only encumber the project further. = 进一步的延误只会让项目更加复杂。


虚拟语气:
If the property weren't encumbered, we could sell it easily. = 如果房产没有抵押，我们就能轻松出售它。
I wish my life weren't so encumbered by responsibilities. = 我希望我的生活不会被责任所累。


被动语态:
The land was encumbered by a restrictive covenant. = 这块土地受到限制性契约的限制。
The project was encumbered by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目因不可预见的情况而受阻。


疑问句:
Is there any encumbrance on this title? = 这个产权有什么妨碍吗？
What encumbrances are there on the property? = 这处房产有什么负担？


否定句:
The property is free from encumbrance. = 这处房产没有抵押。
His business is not encumbered by debt. = 他的生意没有债务缠身。
