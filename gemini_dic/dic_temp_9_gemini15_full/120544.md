miracle:/ˈmɪrəkl/| n. |奇迹，奇事 |An event that is extraordinary and inexplicable by natural or scientific laws, attributed to a divine agency.  A miracle is something wonderful that is unexpected and difficult or impossible to explain. |例句：Her recovery was nothing short of a miracle. 她的康复简直就是一个奇迹。

例句：The doctor described the patient's survival as a medical miracle. 医生将病人的幸存描述为医学奇迹。


近义词：wonder, marvel, prodigy, phenomenon |反义词：impossibility, impossibility
