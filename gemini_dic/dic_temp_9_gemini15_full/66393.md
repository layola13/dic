executant:/ˈɛksɪkjuːtənt/| n. |执行者；演奏者|A person who executes or performs something, especially a musical work.  This term emphasizes the act of carrying out a piece of music or other performance. |The pianist was a brilliant executant, bringing a captivating energy to the concerto.  这位钢琴家是一位技艺精湛的演奏家，为协奏曲带来了引人入胜的活力。| performer, interpreter, artist |  incompetent, amateur | 名词
