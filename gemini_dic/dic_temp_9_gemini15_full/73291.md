forebrain:/ˈfɔːrˌbreɪn/ | n. |前脑 |The anterior part of the brain, containing the cerebrum, thalamus, and hypothalamus, responsible for higher-level cognitive functions.  |例句：The forebrain is the largest part of the human brain and is responsible for higher-level functions such as thinking and planning.  前脑是人脑中最大的一部分，负责高级功能，如思考和计划。|近义词：prosencephalon |反义词：hindbrain, midbrain |名词
