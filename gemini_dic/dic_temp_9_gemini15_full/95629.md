incompatibly:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbli/ | adv. | 不可协调地，不相容地 | In a way that is incompatible; in a way that cannot exist or be used together.  | The two systems are incompatibly designed and cannot be integrated.  这两个系统设计上不相容，无法整合。|  incongruously, discordantly | compatibly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" does.  The usage examples will show how to use it in different sentence structures.

They work incompatibly. = 他们工作方式格格不入。
The software and hardware operate incompatibly. = 这个软件和硬件不相容。

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases that include "incompatibly."  The word is used independently to modify a verb or describe a state of being.
