idled:/ˈaɪdld/| v.|闲置；无所事事；空转|to be inactive or unemployed; to run (an engine) without load|The car idled in the driveway.  汽车空转在车道上。|The factory idled for weeks after the strike.  罢工后工厂闲置了几周。|busy, occupied|working, active|动词


USAGE->

He idles away his time playing video games. = 他玩电子游戏消磨时间。
The engine idles smoothly. = 引擎平稳空转。

一般过去时:
She idled her time away watching TV. = 她看电视消磨时光。
The workers idled for days waiting for the pay. = 工人们等了几天工资才发。
