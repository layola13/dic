garnishes:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪz/ | n. |装饰物；配菜 | Things used to decorate or add flavor to a dish, typically placed on top.  |例句：The chef garnished the plate with fresh herbs.  厨师用新鲜香草装饰了盘子。


例句：The restaurant garnishes its cocktails with colorful fruit slices. 这家餐馆用五颜六色的水果片装饰它的鸡尾酒。


近义词：decorations, embellishments, toppings


反义词：N/A (No direct antonym, as it's about adding, not removing)


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef garnishes the dishes with parsley. = 厨师用欧芹装饰菜肴。
They garnish their pizzas with fresh basil. = 他们用新鲜罗勒装饰他们的披萨。


一般过去时:
She garnished the cake with chocolate shavings. = 她用巧克力屑装饰蛋糕。
He garnished the soup with a swirl of cream. = 他用一圈奶油装饰汤。


现在完成时:
I have garnished the salad with croutons. = 我已经用面包丁装饰了沙拉。
They have garnished the drinks with lemon wedges. = 他们已经用柠檬角装饰了饮料。


过去完成时:
She had garnished the platter with olives before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经用橄榄装饰了大盘子。
They had garnished the main course with roasted vegetables. = 他们已经用烤蔬菜装饰了主菜。


将来时:
We will garnish the desserts with whipped cream. = 我们将用鲜奶油装饰甜点。
He will garnish the tacos with salsa. = 他将用莎莎酱装饰玉米卷。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would garnish the dish more elaborately. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更精细地装饰这道菜。
I wish I had garnished the chicken with some lemon zest. = 我真希望我用一些柠檬皮装饰了鸡肉。


被动语态:
The dishes are garnished with fresh flowers. = 菜肴用鲜花装饰。
The cocktails were garnished with cherries. = 鸡尾酒用樱桃装饰。


疑问句:
How do you garnish this soup? = 你如何装饰这道汤？
What will you garnish the steak with? = 你要用什么装饰牛排？


否定句:
I didn't garnish the pasta with cheese. = 我没有用奶酪装饰意大利面。
They didn't garnish the drinks with anything. = 他们没有用任何东西装饰饮料。
