paroxysm: /ˈpærɒksɪzəm/ | /ˈpærəksɪzəm/ | n. |一阵（尤指狂暴的）；发作；突发 | A sudden attack or violent expression of a particular emotion or activity.  |例句：He suffered a paroxysm of coughing. 他一阵剧烈咳嗽。|  例句：A paroxysm of rage seized him. 一阵狂怒袭来。|近义词：fit, seizure, attack, outburst |反义词：calm, tranquility, peace
