hyper-nutrition:/ˌhaɪpərnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |过度营养，营养过剩 | Excessive intake of nutrients, leading to an imbalance and potential health problems.  This can manifest in various forms, including obesity and related metabolic disorders. | Example:  Hyper-nutrition in early childhood can lead to obesity later in life. = 儿童早期过度营养会导致成年后肥胖。  The study linked hyper-nutrition to an increased risk of heart disease. = 这项研究将过度营养与患心脏病的风险增加联系起来。 | 近义词: overnutrition, nutritional excess | 反义词: malnutrition, undernutrition |  词性:名词
