semijocular:/ˌsɛmiˈdʒɒkjələr/ | adj. | 半开玩笑的，略带玩笑的 |  Partly joking; not entirely serious.  It suggests a tone that is playful but not entirely without seriousness or sincerity.  The humor is often subtle or understated. |例句：He made a semijocular remark about the boss's new haircut. 他对老板的新发型做了一个半开玩笑的评论。

例句：Her semijocular suggestion to quit her job and travel the world was met with mixed reactions. 她半开玩笑地建议辞职去环游世界，引起了各种各样的反应。


近义词：playful, jocular, facetious, jocose, lighthearted | 反义词：serious, earnest, solemn, grave


词源：From "semi-" (half, partly) + "jocular" (given to or characterized by joking; merry).  "Jocular" itself comes from Latin "jocus" (joke).
