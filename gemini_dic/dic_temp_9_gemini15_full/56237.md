dog: /dɒɡ/ /dɔːɡ/ | n. |狗| A domesticated canine, typically kept as a pet or for work.  Dogs are known for their loyalty, intelligence, and diverse breeds.  |Example: My dog loves to play fetch. 我的狗喜欢玩捡球游戏。|近义词： canine, hound |反义词：cat (in a comparative sense)
