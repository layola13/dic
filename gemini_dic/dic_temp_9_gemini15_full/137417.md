nontestable: /ˌnɑːnˈtɛstəbl/ | adj. | 不可测试的，无法验证的 |  Unable to be tested or verified.  This adjective describes something that cannot be subjected to a test or experiment to determine its truth, validity, or effectiveness.  It often implies a lack of measurable or observable outcomes. |

例句：

The hypothesis is nontestable because it relies on unobservable phenomena.  = 这个假设是不可测试的，因为它依赖于不可观察的现象。

His claims are largely nontestable, making it difficult to assess their validity. = 他的说法大多是不可测试的，这使得难以评估其有效性。


近义词：unverifiable, unprovable, immeasurable, inassessable

反义词：testable, verifiable, provable, measurable

词性：形容词
