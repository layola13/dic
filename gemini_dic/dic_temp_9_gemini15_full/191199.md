scaring: /ˈskɛərɪŋ/ | v. | 恐吓；使害怕 |  The act of frightening someone or something; causing fear or alarm.  | 例句：The loud noise was scaring the birds.  大声的噪音吓跑了鸟儿。

The dark shadows were scaring the children.  黑暗的影子吓坏了孩子们。| 近义词：frightening, terrifying, intimidating | 反义词：soothing, comforting, reassuring |  动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music is scaring the neighbors. = 吵闹的音乐正在吓唬邻居们。
That dog is scaring the mailman. = 那条狗吓唬邮递员。


一般过去时:
The storm scaring everyone in the village. = 暴风雨吓坏了村里的每个人。
The sudden noise scared me. = 突然的噪音吓了我一跳。


现在完成时:
The news has scared me. = 这个消息吓到我了。
The movie has scared many people. = 这部电影吓到了很多人。


过去完成时:
I had been scared by the horror movie before I went to sleep. = 我在睡觉前被恐怖电影吓到了。
They had scared the thief away before the police arrived. = 他们在警察到达之前把小偷吓跑了。


将来时:
The thunder will scare the cat. = 雷声会吓到猫。
I think the test will scare many students. = 我认为考试会吓到很多学生。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would scare her, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道这会吓到她，我就不会做了。
I wish the loud noises wouldn't scare the baby. = 我希望吵闹的声音不会吓到婴儿。


被动语态:
The children were scared by the fireworks. = 孩子们被烟花吓到了。
He was scared by the unexpected visitor. = 他被意想不到的访客吓到了。


疑问句:
What is scaring you? = 什么东西吓到你了？
Is the dog scaring the children? = 那条狗吓到孩子们了吗？


否定句:
The sight did not scare me. = 这个景象并没有吓到我。
The movie wasn't scaring anyone. = 这部电影没有吓到任何人。
