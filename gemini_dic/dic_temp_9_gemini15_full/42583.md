counterploy:/ˈkaʊntərpɔɪ/| n. |反策略，对策|A strategic maneuver designed to counter or oppose an opponent's ploy.  It involves responding to an opponent's tactic with a strategic action of one's own.|例句：The general's counterploy successfully thwarted the enemy's flanking maneuver. 将军的反策略成功地挫败了敌人的侧翼机动。|例句：Her sudden resignation was a clever counterploy to avoid being fired. 她突然辞职是避免被解雇的巧妙对策。|近义词：countermeasure, riposte, retort|反义词：strategy, plan, tactic|名词
