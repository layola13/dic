misappraise:/ˌmɪsæˈpreɪz/ | vt. | 估价过低；误评；对…评价过低 | To judge or assess something incorrectly, especially to undervalue something. | The antique dealer misappraised the painting, thinking it was a modern copy. 古董商低估了这幅画的价值，以为它是现代复制品。  | undervalue, underestimate, misjudge | overvalue, overestimate, appreciate
