pushbike:/ˈpʊʃbaɪk/| n.|自行车，脚踏车|A pushbike is a bicycle, especially one that is simple and inexpensive.|例句：He rode his pushbike to school. 他骑着自行车去上学。|近义词：bicycle, bike, cycle|反义词:car, motorcycle
