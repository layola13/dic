circuited:/ˈsɜːkɪtɪd/| v.|电路连接；使短路|To connect (an electrical component) into a circuit; to cause a short circuit in (an electrical circuit).|例句：The technician carefully circuited the new components into the motherboard. 技师小心地将新组件连接到主板上。
例句：A faulty wire circuited the system, causing a power outage. 一根故障的电线使系统短路，导致停电。|近义词：wired, connected, shorted|反义词：disconnected, opened
