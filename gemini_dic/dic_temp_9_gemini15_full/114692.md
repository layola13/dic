malingerers:/məˈlɪŋɡərərz/| n.|装病者；装病逃避责任者 |Those who feign illness to avoid work or duty.  |例句：The company fired several malingerers who were constantly calling in sick.|近义词：shirkers, slackers, goldbrickers |反义词：hard workers, diligent employees |名词
