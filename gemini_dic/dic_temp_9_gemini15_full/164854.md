preformatting:/ˌpriːˈfɔːrmætɪŋ/ | n. |预格式化；预先格式化 | The process of applying formatting to a document or data before it is used or processed.  This often involves setting things like margins, fonts, and spacing.  | Example:  The preformatting of the report ensured consistent presentation across all versions. = 报告的预先格式化确保了所有版本的演示一致性。 |近义词：presetting,  initial formatting |反义词：post-formatting,  unformatted
