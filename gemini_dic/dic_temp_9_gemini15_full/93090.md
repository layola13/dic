idyll:/ˈɪdɪl/| n. |田园诗；田园生活；美好时光 |A short poem or piece of prose that describes a peaceful and happy scene or event, especially in the country.  It often depicts idealized rural life. |例句：The poet wrote an idyllic poem about the beauty of the countryside.  这位诗人写了一首关于乡村美丽的田园诗。
例句：She recalled her idyllic childhood summers spent by the lake. 她回忆了她童年在湖边度过的美好时光。|近义词：pastoral, bucolic, idyllic scene|反义词：chaos, turmoil, dystopia|名词
