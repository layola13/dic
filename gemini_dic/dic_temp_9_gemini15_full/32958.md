chorales:/kəˈrɑːlz/| n. |合唱曲；圣咏；赞美歌 | A chorale is a hymn tune, typically of a simple, harmonized nature, often used in Protestant church services.  It can also refer to a composition written in the style of a chorale. | Example: The choir sang a beautiful chorale during the service.  合唱团在仪式上唱了一首优美的圣咏。 The composer based the symphony on a well-known chorale melody.  这位作曲家以一首著名的圣咏旋律为基础创作了这首交响乐。|近义词：hymn, anthem, psalm |反义词：solo, monologue
