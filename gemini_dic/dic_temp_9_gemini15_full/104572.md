kang:/kæŋ/| n.|袋鼠|A marsupial mammal of Australia, having a powerful tail and large hind legs for hopping.|例句：The kangaroo is a symbol of Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚的象征。|近义词：wallaby|反义词:  无


USAGE->

The kangaroo eats grass. = 袋鼠吃草。
