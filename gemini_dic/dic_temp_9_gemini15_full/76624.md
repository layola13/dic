galloping:/ˈɡæləpɪŋ/| adj. v.|飞奔的；疾驰的；迅速的 |  Describes something moving or progressing at a very fast pace, like a horse's gallop.  It can also describe a rapid increase or development. |例句：The galloping horse thundered across the plain. 这匹飞奔的马在平原上飞驰而过。

The economy experienced a period of galloping inflation. 经济经历了一段飞速上涨的通货膨胀时期。 |近义词：rapid, swift, fast, furious,  |反义词：slow, sluggish, gradual, creeping

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse gallops across the field. = 马在田野上飞奔。
He gallops his way to success. = 他飞速走向成功。

一般过去时:
The rider galloped his horse through the forest. = 骑手骑着马飞驰穿过森林。
She galloped down the street. = 她飞快地跑下街道。

现在完成时:
The company has galloped ahead of its competitors. = 这家公司已经远远超过了竞争对手。
They have galloped to victory. = 他们已取得了胜利。

过去完成时:
He had galloped several miles before he stopped to rest. = 他飞奔了几英里才停下来休息。
The news had galloped across the country before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，消息已经在全国迅速传播。

将来时:
The soldiers will gallop towards the enemy. = 士兵们将向敌人冲锋。
She will gallop into the sunset. = 她将飞奔向夕阳。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would gallop across the countryside. = 如果我有一匹马，我就会在乡间飞驰。
If the economy continued to gallop, we would have a problem. = 如果经济持续高速增长，我们将面临问题。

被动语态:
The horse was galloped hard by the rider. = 这匹马被骑手猛烈地驱使着飞奔。
The news was galloped across the internet in minutes. = 消息在几分钟内就在互联网上迅速传播。

疑问句:
Does the horse gallop fast? = 这匹马跑得快吗？
Will they gallop to the finish line? = 他们会飞奔到终点线吗？


否定句:
The horse didn't gallop; it trotted. = 马没有飞奔；它是小跑的。
He didn't gallop to the meeting; he walked. = 他没有飞奔去开会；他是走过去的。
