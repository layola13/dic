planned:/plænd/| adj.| 计划的；预定的 | planned  means something that has been arranged or decided in advance. It implies intention and preparation. |例句：We have a planned trip to Europe next summer.  我们计划明年夏天去欧洲旅行。

例句：The planned expansion of the company will create new jobs. 公司计划的扩张将创造新的就业机会。


近义词：prearranged, scheduled, intended, designed, foreseen

反义词：unplanned, impromptu, spontaneous, unexpected


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is planned meticulously. = 这个项目计划得非常细致。
Our vacation is planned for next month. = 我们的假期计划在下个月。


一般过去时:
The meeting was planned weeks in advance. = 会议提前几周就计划好了。
The attack was carefully planned. = 这次袭击是经过精心策划的。


现在完成时:
The trip has been planned for months. = 这趟旅行已经计划了好几个月了。
Everything has been planned perfectly. = 一切都计划得完美无缺。


过去完成时:
The surprise party had been planned secretly. = 惊喜派对是秘密策划的。
They had planned to leave early, but the weather delayed them. = 他们原本计划早点离开，但天气耽搁了他们。


将来时:
The concert will be planned soon. = 音乐会很快就会安排好。
The event will be planned by a professional event planner. = 此活动将由专业的活动策划人策划。


虚拟语气:
If the project were better planned, it would be more successful. = 如果这个项目计划得更好，它会更成功。
I wish the vacation had been better planned. = 我希望假期能计划得更好。


被动语态:
The event is being planned by a committee. = 这个活动正在由一个委员会策划。
The details of the celebration are being carefully planned. = 庆祝活动的细节正在仔细规划中。


疑问句:
Is the meeting planned for tomorrow? = 会议安排在明天吗？
Has the party been planned yet? = 派对计划好了吗？


否定句:
The trip was not planned properly. = 这趟旅行计划得不妥当。
The project wasn't planned well enough. = 这个项目计划得不够好。


PHRASE->
planned economy: 计划经济
planned obsolescence: 计划报废
planned parenthood: 计划生育
well-planned: 计划周详的
long-planned:  长期计划的


词源:
The word "planned" is derived from the verb "plan," which comes from the Old French word "planer," meaning "to flatten, level, smooth."  The meaning evolved to encompass the sense of making a design or scheme, leading to its modern usage.
