forewrite:/ˈfɔːrˌraɪt/ | n. |前言，序言 | A short introductory section of a book or other piece of writing.  | Example: The foreword was written by a leading expert in the field.  前言是由该领域一位领先专家撰写的。 | 近义词：preface, introduction, prologue | 反义词：epilogue, afterword |  名词


USAGE->

The book has a foreword. = 这本书有一个前言。
The author writes a forewrite for every book. = 作者为每本书都写一个前言。

一般过去时:
She wrote a compelling foreword for his memoir. = 她为他的回忆录写了一个引人入胜的前言。
The editor added a forewrite to the collection of essays. = 编辑为这本论文集添加了一个前言。
