Punjabi refers to several related things:

1. **A language:** Punjabi is an Indo-Aryan language spoken primarily in the Punjab region of India and Pakistan.  It has numerous dialects.

2. **A people:** Punjabi refers to the people who speak the Punjabi language.  They have a rich cultural heritage.

3. **A region:** Historically,  "Punjab" refers to a geographical area encompassing parts of present-day India and Pakistan.
