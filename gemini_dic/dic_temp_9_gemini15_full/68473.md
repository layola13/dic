familiars:/fəˈmɪliərz/ | n. |熟人，熟悉的事物 | People or things that are familiar; those known to one.  Familiars can refer to trusted companions, known objects, or even spirits in some contexts. |例句：She felt comfortable among her familiars at the party.  她在派对上和她的熟人们在一起感到很自在。
The old house held many familiars from his childhood. 这栋老房子里有很多他童年时熟悉的东西。|近义词：acquaintances, friends, intimates |反义词：strangers, unknowns
