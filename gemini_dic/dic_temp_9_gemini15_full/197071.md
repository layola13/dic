shamblingly:/ˈʃæmblɪŋli/| adv. |蹒跚地，踉踉跄跄地 |Moving in a clumsy or unsteady way; shuffling.  It describes a manner of walking or moving that is awkward, unsteady, and lacking grace.  |例句：He shambled into the room, his clothes rumpled and his hair dishevelled. 他踉踉跄跄地走进房间，衣服皱巴巴的，头发蓬乱。

例句：The zombies shambled toward the town, their decaying limbs dragging on the ground. 僵尸们蹒跚地走向小镇，腐烂的四肢拖在地上。


近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, unsteadily, shufflingly


反义词：gracefully, elegantly, smoothly


词性:副词
