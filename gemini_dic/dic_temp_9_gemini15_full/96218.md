dexterity: /dekˈstɛrəti/ | n. |灵巧；熟练；敏捷 | Skill in performing tasks, especially with the hands.  Dexterity involves both physical coordination and mental quickness.  It often refers to the ability to handle objects skillfully and efficiently. |例句：The surgeon's dexterity was evident in the precision of the operation.  外科医生的灵巧在手术的精确性中显而易见。

The pianist's dexterity amazed the audience. 钢琴家的灵巧技艺让观众惊叹。|近义词：skill, agility, adroitness, deftness |反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptitude
