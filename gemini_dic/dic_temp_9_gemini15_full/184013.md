reshuffled:/ˌriːˈʃʌfəld/| v. adj.|重新洗牌；改组；重新安排|To rearrange (something) in a new order.  It implies a change in the arrangement or organization, often implying a significant alteration, but not necessarily a complete overhaul.|例句：The cabinet was reshuffled after the election.  选举后内阁进行了改组。

The team's lineup was reshuffled to improve performance. 球队的阵容进行了调整以提高表现。


近义词：reorganized, rearranged, reorganized, restructured|反义词：maintained, kept the same|动词
