outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|支架；平衡木；独木舟的平衡梁|A support or brace, typically a beam or spar projecting from the side of a boat or aircraft to provide stability.  It is often used in canoes and catamarans to prevent capsizing.|例句：The outrigger on the canoe kept it stable in the rough water.  这条独木舟上的平衡木使它在汹涌的水中保持稳定。

The outrigger plane helped to maintain its balance during flight. 这架飞机上的支架帮助它在飞行中保持平衡。|近义词：brace, support, strut|反义词：None specifically.
