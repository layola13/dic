cercal:/ˈsɜːrkl/| adj.|尾须的；尾部的|Relating to or resembling a cercus (a sensory appendage on the abdomen of some insects).  The term is often used in zoology and entomology to describe structures or features associated with the cerci.|例句：The insect's cercal filaments were remarkably sensitive to air currents.  这只昆虫的尾须细丝对气流非常敏感。

The cercal sensory organs detected the slightest movement. 尾须感觉器官探测到最轻微的运动。|近义词：caudal, posterior|反义词：anterior, cephalic
