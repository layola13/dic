ring:/rɪŋ/|n., v.|戒指；铃声；环；打电话|A ring is a piece of jewelry worn on a finger.  It can also be the sound made by a bell or telephone, or a circular shape.|例句：She received a beautiful diamond ring as a gift. 她收到一枚美丽的钻石戒指作为礼物。

例句：The alarm clock ringed at 6 am. 闹钟在早上6点响了。|近义词：band, circle, call|反义词: none|名词，动词
