revitalized:/ˌriːˈvaɪtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 使恢复活力的；使复兴的 | Made active or vigorous again; renewed.  The revitalized city center is now bustling with activity. | The revitalized economy shows signs of strong growth. |近义词：rejuvenated, renewed, refreshed, invigorated |反义词：depleted, exhausted, drained

一般现在时:
The new program revitalizes the community. = 新项目使社区焕发了活力。
This policy revitalizes the local economy. =这项政策使当地经济恢复了活力。

一般过去时:
The city was revitalized by the new park. = 新公园使这座城市焕发了活力。
The investment revitalized the struggling business. = 这项投资使这家苦苦挣扎的企业恢复了生机。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
