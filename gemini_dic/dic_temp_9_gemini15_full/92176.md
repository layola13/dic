USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have tense conjugations like verbs.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.

There are no common phrases directly using "hyperspeculatively".  The word itself is used to modify a verb within a phrase.  For example: "hyperspeculatively theorizing about the future".
