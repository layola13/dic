inflammations:/ˌɪnfləˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |炎症，发炎|The plural form of "inflammation," referring to multiple instances of swelling, redness, heat, and pain in a bodily area due to injury or infection.  Inflammations can occur in various parts of the body and are often a symptom of an underlying condition.|例句：The doctor diagnosed several inflammations in his joints, indicating a possible autoimmune disease. 医生诊断出他关节有几处炎症，这表明他可能患有自身免疫性疾病。

例句：Chronic inflammations in the gut can lead to digestive problems. 肠道慢性炎症会导致消化问题。


近义词：infections, swellings, irritations|反义词：healing, recovery|词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *inflammatioun, from Old French *enflammation, from Late Latin *inflammationem (nominative *inflammatiō), from Latin inflammare "to set on fire," from in- "in" + flamma "flame."  The root meaning reflects the visible signs of inflammation: redness and heat.
