separating: /ˈsɛpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 分离；分开；隔开 | The act of separating or the state of being separated.  This word is the present participle of the verb "separate". It describes an ongoing action of division or distinction. |例句：The fence is separating the two properties.  这道围栏将两处房产隔开。

The children are separating the toys into different boxes.孩子们正在把玩具分到不同的盒子里。


近义词：dividing, partitioning, splitting, detaching | 反义词：uniting, joining, combining, merging | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are separating the wheat from the chaff.  他们在分离麦子和糠。
We are separating the laundry into whites and colors. 我们正在把衣服分成白色和彩色的。


一般过去时:
She separated the eggs carefully. 她小心地把鸡蛋分开。
They separated after many years of marriage. 他们结婚多年后分开了。


现在完成时:
He has separated himself from the group. 他已经把自己从团队中分离出来了。
They have separated the recycling from the trash. 他们已经把可回收垃圾和普通垃圾分开了。


过去完成时:
The family had separated before the war broke out.  战争爆发前，这个家庭就已分开了。
She had separated the documents into different files before the meeting. 会议前，她已将文件分成了不同的文件夹。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If they separated now, it would be very painful. 如果他们现在分开，将会非常痛苦。

否定句:
