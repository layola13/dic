preadjustments:/ˌpriːəˈdʒʌstmənts/ | n. | 预先调整，预先设定 | Adjustments made in advance.  These are often settings or configurations prepared before a process begins.  | Example: The preadjustments to the telescope ensured accurate readings. 预先对望远镜进行调整确保了精确的读数。 |近义词：pre-settings, preliminary adjustments, prior calibrations |反义词：post-adjustments, fine-tuning (in the context of needing further adjustments after initial settings) | 名词
