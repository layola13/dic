multitracker:/ˈmʌltiˌtrækər/ | n. |多轨录音机；多轨录音系统 | A device or software that allows for the simultaneous recording and playback of multiple audio tracks.  It's commonly used in music production, allowing musicians to record different instruments or vocals separately and then combine them later. | Example: The band used a multitracker to record their new album, layering guitars, bass, and vocals.  乐队用多轨录音机录制了他们的新专辑，分层录制了吉他、贝斯和人声。 | 近义词：multitrack recorder, mixing console (partially) | 反义词：monotracker (rarely used)
