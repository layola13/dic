marmoretum: /ˌmɑːrməˈriːtəm/ | n. |大理石镶嵌图案；大理石装饰 | A type of floor or wall decoration made of inlaid marble pieces, creating intricate designs.  It was a popular decorative technique in ancient Roman architecture. | Example: The villa boasted a magnificent marmoretum adorning its floors, depicting scenes from mythology.  这座别墅以其装饰着神话场景的华丽大理石镶嵌图案地板而自豪。 | 近义词: mosaic, inlay | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of plain or undecorated surfaces)  |  词性：名词
