initial: /ɪˈnɪʃəl/ | adj. n. | 最初的；首字母 | 英文解析：Coming at the beginning; first.  As a noun, it refers to the first letter of a name. | 例句：The initial reaction was one of surprise.  最初的反应是惊讶。  His initials are J.R.R. 他的首字母是J.R.R。 | 近义词：beginning, primary, first, introductory | 反义词：final, last, concluding | 词性：adj., n.
