conceptualist:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst/| n. |概念主义者，概念艺术家|A conceptualist is an artist who prioritizes the idea or concept behind a work of art over its physical manifestation.  Their work often involves ideas, processes, and concepts rather than traditional artistic skills or aesthetics.  The meaning and impact of the work are central, and the physical form can be quite minimal or even absent.|Example:  Many conceptual artists use everyday objects to convey complex ideas.  许多概念艺术家使用日常物品来表达复杂的想法。|近义词：conceptual artist, idea artist |反义词：formalist, realist
