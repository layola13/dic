chance:/tʃɑːns/| n. /ˈtʃæns/| n. v.|机会；可能性；运气；偶然；冒险|A possibility of something happening; a lucky opportunity; a risk.|例句：There's a chance of rain later.  =  晚些时候可能下雨。

例句：She took a chance and invested all her money. = 她冒险把所有钱都投了进去。


近义词：opportunity, possibility, likelihood, probability|反义词：certainty, impossibility


词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is a chance of success. = 有成功的可能。
What are the chances of winning? = 赢的几率有多大？


一般过去时:
There was a chance to escape. = 有逃跑的机会。
I missed my chance. = 我错过了机会。


现在完成时:
I have had many chances in my life. = 我一生中有很多机会。
We have missed several chances to talk. = 我们错过了几次交谈的机会。


过去完成时:
I had already missed my chance before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经错过了机会。
They had several chances to win but failed. = 他们有好几次机会获胜，但都失败了。


将来时:
There will be a chance to discuss this later. = 以后会有机会讨论这个。
You will have another chance to try. = 你将会有另一次尝试的机会。


虚拟语气:
If I had had a chance, I would have done it. = 如果我有机会，我会做的。
I wish I had a chance to travel the world. = 我希望我有机会环游世界。


被动语态:
The chance was missed. = 机会错过了。
The chance was given to her. = 机会给了她。


疑问句:
Is there any chance of a promotion? = 有升职的可能性吗？
What are your chances of winning? = 你获胜的几率有多大？


否定句:
There is no chance of winning. = 没有获胜的可能。
I don't have a chance to win. = 我没有获胜的机会。
