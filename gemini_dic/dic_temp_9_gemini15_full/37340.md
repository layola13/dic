commend:/kəˈmɛnd/ | vt. | 推荐；称赞；委托；托付 | To praise or speak favorably of; to entrust to someone's care; to recommend. |例句：The teacher commended the student for his hard work. 老师称赞学生努力学习。  The captain commended the ship to God's care. 船长将船只托付给上帝的照顾。|近义词：praise, approve, entrust, recommend |反义词：criticize, condemn, blame


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commends his team for their efforts. 他称赞他的团队的努力。
She commends the new policy to the board. 她向董事会推荐这项新政策。


一般过去时:
The judge commended the lawyer for his skillful arguments.  法官称赞律师的技巧娴熟的辩论。
My boss commended me for completing the project ahead of schedule. 我的老板表扬我提前完成了项目。


现在完成时:
The committee has commended the project for its innovative design. 委员会已经赞扬了该项目的创新设计。
He has commended his daughter's achievements to the community. 他向社区推荐了他女儿的成就。


过去完成时:
They had commended the plan before the meeting started. 他们在会议开始之前已经推荐了这个计划。
She had commended her assistant's work to the CEO. 她之前已经向CEO推荐了她助理的工作。


将来时:
The CEO will commend the employees at the annual meeting.  首席执行官将在年度会议上表彰员工。
He will commend the book to his readers. 他将向他的读者推荐这本书。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would commend his honesty. 如果我是你，我会称赞他的诚实。
I wish I could commend her for her courage. 我希望我能赞扬她的勇气。


被动语态:
His work was commended by the judges. 他的作品受到评委的赞扬。
The proposal was commended to the committee.  该提案被推荐给委员会。


疑问句:
Will the manager commend the team's performance? 经理会表扬团队的表现吗？
Did the professor commend your research paper? 教授称赞你的研究论文了吗？


否定句:
The boss did not commend John's behavior. 老板没有称赞约翰的行为。
They did not commend the movie to their friends.  他们没有向朋友推荐这部电影。


PHRASE->
commend to:  推荐给...
commend oneself to: 使自己受...喜爱
commend itself to:  使自己为...所接受
commendable conduct: 值得称赞的行为
commendable effort: 值得称赞的努力
