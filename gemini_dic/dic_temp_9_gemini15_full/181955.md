remarried:/ˌriːˈmæriːd/| adj.|再婚的，再结婚的|Having married again after a previous marriage ended.|例句：She is remarried and very happy. 她再婚了，而且非常幸福。
例句：He remarried  a year after his divorce. 他离婚一年后又结婚了。|近义词：wedded again, married again|反义词：unmarried, single


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is remarried to a lovely woman. = 他和一位可爱的女士再婚了。
They are remarried and live happily. = 他们再婚了，生活得很幸福。


一般过去时:
She was remarried last year. = 她去年再婚了。
He was remarried shortly after his first wife passed away. = 他第一任妻子去世后不久就再婚了。


现在完成时:
They have been remarried for five years. = 他们再婚五年了。
She has remarried and seems much happier. = 她再婚了，看起来幸福多了。


过去完成时:
He had been remarried twice before he met her. = 在遇到她之前，他已经再婚两次了。
Before they met, she had been remarried once. = 在他们相遇之前，她再婚过一次。


将来时:
I think they will be remarried soon. = 我想他们很快就会再婚。
She said she will be remarried next year. = 她说她明年会再婚。


虚拟语气:
If he had remarried earlier, he might have been happier. = 如果他早点再婚，他可能会更快乐。
If she were remarried, she would have more support. = 如果她再婚了，她会得到更多的支持。


被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used in passive voice.  The verb "to remarry" is used instead.)  He was remarried to her. (This is grammatically correct but unnatural).  = 他和她再婚了。(这是语法上正确的，但不自然。)


疑问句:
Is he remarried? = 他再婚了吗？
Are they remarried? = 他们再婚了吗？


否定句:
He is not remarried. = 他没有再婚。
She is not yet remarried. = 她还没有再婚。
