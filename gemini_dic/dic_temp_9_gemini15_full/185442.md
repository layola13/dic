returnables:/rɪˈtɜːrnəblz/| n. |可退回物，可回收物 |Items that can be returned to a store or other place for a refund or reuse.  For example, bottles, containers, or crates that can be returned for a deposit. |例句：The store offers a deposit refund on all returnables.  这家商店对所有可退回物提供押金退款。

例句：We collected all the returnables after the party. 派对结束后我们收集了所有可退回的物品。


近义词：refundables, recyclables
反义词：non-returnables, disposables

词性:名词
