morning-glories:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ˈɡlɔːriz/|/ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˈɡlɔːriz/| n. |牵牛花；番薯；(常用复数)|Morning glories are a type of flowering plant known for their trumpet-shaped flowers that often open in the morning.  They are also sometimes called bindweeds.|例句：The garden was ablaze with colorful morning glories. 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的牵牛花。|例句：Many species of morning glories are cultivated as ornamental plants.|许多种类的牵牛花都被作为观赏植物栽培。|近义词：bindweed, convolvulus|反义词：无
