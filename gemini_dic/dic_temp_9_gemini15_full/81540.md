gravy:/ˈɡreɪvi/| n. |肉汁；美味佳肴|Thick sauce made from the juices of cooked meat, often thickened with flour.|The turkey was delicious, and the gravy was even better.  这火鸡很好吃，肉汁更好吃。|The roast beef was served with rich brown gravy. 烤牛肉配上浓郁的棕色肉汁。|近义词：sauce, jus|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gravy tastes delicious. = 肉汁尝起来很美味。
This gravy is made from chicken stock. = 这个肉汁是用鸡汤做的。

一般过去时:
The gravy thickened as it cooled. = 肉汁冷却后变稠了。
We poured the gravy over the mashed potatoes. = 我们把肉汁浇在土豆泥上。


现在完成时:
I have made the gravy for the roast. = 我已经为烤肉做了肉汁。
