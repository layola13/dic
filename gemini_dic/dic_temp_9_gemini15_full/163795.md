predemonstrate:/ˌpriːˈdɛmənstreɪt/| vt.|预先演示；预先展示|To demonstrate something beforehand; to give a preview demonstration.|例句：The professor predemonstrated the experiment before the students began their own work. 教授在学生开始自己动手做实验之前先进行了演示。
例句：The company predemonstrated the new software to potential clients. 公司向潜在客户预先演示了新软件。|近义词：preview, showcase, exhibit|反义词：conceal, hide|动词
