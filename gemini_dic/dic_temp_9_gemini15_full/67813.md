eyetooth:/ˈaɪtuːθ/ | n. |犬齿，尖牙 |A canine tooth, especially a prominent one.  Eyeteeth are the pointed teeth located at the corners of the mouth, one in each jaw. They are also known as canines. |例句：He has prominent eyeteeth. = 他有突出的犬齿。

例句：The dentist examined the child's eyetooth. = 牙医检查了孩子的犬齿。


近义词：canine, cusp, fang | 反义词：molar, incisor


词源：The word "eyetooth" comes from Old English *ēagtoþ, a compound of ēage "eye" and tōþ "tooth".  The connection between the eye and this particular tooth is not entirely clear, but several theories exist, including the belief that pain in these teeth might radiate towards the eye.
