floaters:/ˈfloʊtərz/ | n. |漂浮物，眼前闪光，游动的小点 |Small objects that appear to float in one's field of vision.  These are often caused by debris in the vitreous humor of the eye. |例句：I've been seeing more floaters in my vision lately.  最近我的视野里漂浮物越来越多。|例句：The lake was covered with fallen leaves and other floaters. 湖面上漂浮着落叶和其他漂浮物。|近义词：specks, spots, debris |反义词：N/A
