multiseries:/ˌmʌltiˈsɪəriːz/| n. |多系列的；多系列数据|A multiseries dataset contains multiple data series, each representing a different variable or category.  It's often used in charting and data visualization to compare multiple sets of data simultaneously. |例句：The chart displays a multiseries line graph showing sales figures for three different product lines.  图表显示了一个多系列线形图，显示了三条不同产品线的销售数据。|近义词：multivariate, polyserial|反义词：single-series, uniserial
