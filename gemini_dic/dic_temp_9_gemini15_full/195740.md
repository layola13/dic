semitheatrical:/ˌsɛmiθiˈætrɪkəl/| adj.|半戏剧性的；略带戏剧性的|Partially theatrical; having some of the characteristics of a theatrical performance; slightly dramatic or affected.  The performance, while enjoyable, had a somewhat semitheatrical quality.  |例句：His dramatic exit was quite semitheatrical. 他戏剧性的退场颇具半戏剧性。

例句：The speech, though sincere, had a semitheatrical quality to it.  尽管真诚，但这番演讲却略带戏剧性。


近义词：dramatic, theatrical, melodramatic, histrionic


反义词：realistic, understated, natural, unemotional
