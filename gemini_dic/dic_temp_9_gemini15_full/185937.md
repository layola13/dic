revue:/rɪˈvjuː/| n.|评论，评论文章；回顾；文藝演出|A revue is a type of theatrical entertainment consisting of a series of short, unrelated acts or sketches, often satirical or humorous.  It can also refer to a critical review of a book, play, movie, etc.  |例句：The magazine published a scathing revue of the new film. 这本杂志发表了一篇对这部新电影的严厉评论。

例句：The theatre group put on a lively revue featuring songs and dances from the 1920s.  这个剧团上演了一场热闹的歌舞表演，节目内容是20世纪20年代的歌曲和舞蹈。|近义词：review,  critique,  show, spectacle|反义词：original work (as opposed to a review of it)
