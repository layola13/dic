presignify: /ˌpriːˈsɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 预示；预兆；预先表示 | To indicate or suggest beforehand; to foreshadow.  The unusual weather patterns presignified a coming storm.  | 近义词：foreshadow, foretell, portend, augur | 反义词：obscure, conceal


USAGE->

His nervous behavior presignifies his anxiety about the test. = 他紧张的行为预示着他对考试的焦虑。
