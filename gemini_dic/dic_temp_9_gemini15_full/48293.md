decree:/dɪˈkriː/| n. v.|法令；命令；判决|An official order issued by a person in authority.  A decree can be a law, a judicial decision, or a command. |例句：The king issued a decree banning the sale of alcohol. 国王发布法令禁止出售酒精。

例句：The court decreed that the defendant was guilty. 法院判决被告有罪。|近义词：edict, ordinance, mandate, order |反义词：suggestion, request


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government decrees new tax laws annually. = 政府每年颁布新的税法。
The judge decrees the sentence. = 法官宣判刑罚。


一般过去时:
The emperor decreed the construction of a new palace. = 皇帝下令建造一座新宫殿。
The court decreed a settlement between the parties. = 法院判决双方和解。


现在完成时:
The council has decreed a new set of regulations. = 理事会已经颁布了一套新的规章制度。
The authorities have decreed a state of emergency. = 当局已宣布进入紧急状态。


过去完成时:
The king had decreed exile for his enemies before the rebellion. = 在叛乱之前，国王已经下令将他的敌人流放。
The court had decreed the property forfeited before the appeal. = 在上诉之前，法院已经判决没收该财产。


将来时:
The president will decree a national holiday. = 总统将宣布全国假日。
The committee will decree the winner of the competition. = 委员会将宣布比赛的获胜者。


虚拟语气:
If the king decreed it, it would be done. = 如果国王下令，它就会被执行。
I wish the judge had decreed a fairer sentence. = 我希望法官判决一个更公平的刑罚。


被动语态:
New restrictions were decreed by the local government. = 地方政府颁布了新的限制措施。
A new law was decreed to address the problem. = 颁布了一项新法律来解决这个问题。


疑问句:
What did the council decree regarding the building project? = 理事会关于建筑项目做了什么决定？
What will the court decree in this case? = 在此案中法院将如何判决？


否定句:
The governor did not decree any new measures. = 州长没有颁布任何新措施。
The judge did not decree a harsher penalty. = 法官没有判处更严厉的惩罚。
