nettlerash: /ˈnetəlˌræʃ/ | n. | 荨麻疹 | An itchy rash caused by contact with nettles or a similar allergic reaction.  It is characterized by raised, red welts. | Example:  The child developed a nettle rash after touching a stinging nettle.  孩子摸了荨麻后起了荨麻疹。 | 近义词: hives, urticaria | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but the absence of a rash could be considered the opposite.)
