pitch:/pɪtʃ/ |/pɪtʃ/| n./v.| 投球；音高；倾斜；推销；沥青 |  To throw or toss something, especially a ball in baseball or cricket; the highness or lowness of a sound; the degree to which something slopes; to try to persuade someone to buy something; a black, sticky substance used for roads and roofs. |例句：He pitched the ball perfectly. 他完美地投出了球。 The pitch of his voice was high. 他的声音很高。 The roof was made of pitch. 屋顶是用沥青做的。 He pitched his new product to investors. 他向投资者推销他的新产品。  |近义词：toss, throw, slant, incline, promote, sell, asphalt, tar |反义词：catch, receive, level, horizontal,  demote


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He pitches the ball well. = 他投球很好。
She pitches her ideas effectively. = 她有效地推销她的想法。


一般过去时:
He pitched the tent last night. = 他昨晚搭起了帐篷。
They pitched the proposal to the board. = 他们向董事会提交了提案。


现在完成时:
He has pitched several successful products. = 他已经成功推销了几种产品。
She has pitched the roof of her house. = 她已经修补了她房子的屋顶。


过去完成时:
They had pitched the tent before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经搭好了帐篷。
He had pitched his best game of the season. = 他已经打了他本赛季最好的一场比赛。


将来时:
He will pitch the ball to the batter. = 他将把球投给击球手。
She will pitch her ideas at the conference. = 她将在会议上宣传她的想法。


虚拟语气:
If he pitched better, he would win more games. = 如果他投球更好，他就能赢更多比赛。
If she had pitched the idea earlier, they might have accepted it. = 如果她早点提出这个想法，他们可能就接受了。


被动语态:
The ball was pitched perfectly. = 球投得完美无缺。
The new product is being pitched to potential customers. = 新产品正在向潜在客户推销。


疑问句:
How high is the pitch of that note? = 那音符的音高是多少？
What is the pitch of the roof? = 屋顶的坡度是多少？


否定句:
He didn't pitch the ball hard enough. = 他投球不够用力。
They didn't pitch their ideas convincingly. = 他们没有令人信服地推销他们的想法。
