pentandrous:/penˈtændrəs/| adj.|五雄蕊的；具有五个雄蕊的|Having five stamens.  The term refers to flowers with five stamens, a key characteristic used in plant classification.|Example:  The pentandrous flower was easily identified by its five prominent stamens.|近义词：pentamerous (if referring to overall floral structure)|反义词：无 (antonyms would describe a different stamen count, e.g., tetrandrous, hexandrous)|词性：形容词
