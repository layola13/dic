preeducates:/ˌpriːˈɛdʒʊkeɪts/ | vt. |预先教育；提前教育 | To educate or train beforehand; to prepare someone for future education or experiences.  |例句：The program preeducates students for the rigors of college life. 这个项目预先教育学生们应对大学生活的严峻挑战。  The teacher preeducated the class about the upcoming field trip.老师提前给同学们讲解了即将到来的实地考察。|近义词：prepare, train, prime, condition |反义词：neglect, ignore
