puddled:/ˈpʌdld/| adj. v.| 积水的；(使)积水 | Forming puddles; covered with puddles.  |例句：The rain had puddled the streets. 雨水使街道积满了水。 The children were playing in the puddled field. 孩子们在积水的田野里玩耍。|近义词：waterlogged, flooded, soggy |反义词：dry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain puddles the streets every morning. = 雨每天早上都会使街道积水。
The water puddles on the floor after I wash the dishes. = 洗完碗后，地板上积了水。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain puddled the roads last night. = 大雨昨晚使道路积水。
The spilled milk puddled on the kitchen counter. = 洒出的牛奶在厨房的柜台上积成了一滩。

The continuous rain has puddled the ground, making it difficult to walk. = 持续的降雨使地面积水，难以行走。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
