micromaniac: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 微观狂；对微小细节过于关注的人 | A person who is excessively concerned with minor details or insignificant matters; someone obsessed with minutiae.  |  He's a real micromaniac; he spends hours scrutinizing every comma in his reports. 他是个十足的微观狂，他花好几个小时仔细检查报告中的每一个逗号。 | 近义词：perfectionist, stickler, nitpicker | 反义词：sloppy, careless, indifferent |  名词
