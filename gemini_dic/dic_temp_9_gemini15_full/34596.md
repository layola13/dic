cleating:/ˈkliːtɪŋ/| n. |鞋钉；固定；卡紧|The act of securing something firmly, often using cleats or similar devices.  It can refer to the process of attaching cleats to shoes or boots, or securing something to a surface using cleats.|例句：The climber checked his cleating before ascending the icy slope.  攀登者在攀登冰坡之前检查了他的鞋钉。 The engineer ensured the proper cleating of the heavy machinery to the trailer. 工程师确保将重型机械牢固地固定到拖车上。|近义词：fastening, securing, fixing|反义词：loosening, detaching, unfastening
