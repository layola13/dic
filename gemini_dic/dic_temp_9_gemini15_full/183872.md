resemblance:/rɪˈzɛmbləns/ | n. |相似之处，类似，相像 |The resemblance between the two brothers is striking.  两兄弟之间的相似之处非常惊人。|近义词：similarity, likeness, similitude |反义词：difference, dissimilarity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting bears a striking resemblance to the original. = 这幅画与原作惊人地相似。
There is a clear resemblance between the two theories. = 这两个理论之间有明显的相似之处。


一般过去时:
The child showed a strong resemblance to his father. = 这个孩子长得非常像他的父亲。
The witness described a resemblance to the suspect. = 目击者描述了一个与嫌疑犯相似的特征。


现在完成时:
The twins have always borne a close resemblance to each other. = 这对双胞胎一直长得非常像。
Scientists have found a resemblance between the two species. = 科学家们发现了这两个物种之间的相似之处。


过去完成时:
Before the operation, he bore little resemblance to his former self. = 手术前，他与以前的自己几乎没有相似之处。
By the time the investigation concluded, they had discovered a surprising resemblance in the two cases. = 到调查结束时，他们发现了这两个案件中惊人的相似之处。


将来时:
The new model will show a strong resemblance to the previous version. = 新型号将与之前的版本非常相似。
Future studies will likely reveal further resemblances between these phenomena. = 未来的研究可能会揭示这些现象之间更多的相似之处。


虚拟语气:
If there were any resemblance, I would have noticed it. = 如果有任何相似之处，我会注意到的。
I wish there were more resemblance between theory and practice. = 我希望理论与实践之间有更多的相似之处。


被动语态:
The resemblance was noted by several experts. = 几位专家都注意到了这种相似之处。
A clear resemblance is often seen between siblings. = 兄弟姐妹之间经常可以看到明显的相似之处。


疑问句:
Is there any resemblance between these two paintings? = 这两幅画之间有什么相似之处吗？
What is the resemblance between these two ideas? = 这两个想法之间有什么相似之处？


否定句:
There is no resemblance between the two events. = 这两个事件之间没有任何相似之处。
The painting bears no resemblance to the original. = 这幅画与原作毫无相似之处。
