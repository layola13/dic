confiscated:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪtɪd/| v. |没收，充公|To take or seize (someone's property) especially by authority.|The police confiscated the drugs.|近义词：seized, appropriated, expropriated |反义词：returned, restored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government confiscates illegal weapons. = 政府没收非法武器。
Customs officials confiscate contraband goods. = 海关官员没收走私货物。


一般过去时:
The police confiscated his car. = 警察没收了他的车。
They confiscated all his illegal documents. = 他们没收了他所有非法的文件。


现在完成时:
The authorities have confiscated a large quantity of drugs. = 当局已经没收了大量的毒品。
They have confiscated his passport and other travel documents. = 他们没收了他的护照和其他旅行证件。


过去完成时:
The judge had already confiscated the property before the hearing. = 在听证会之前，法官已经没收了该财产。
The police had confiscated the evidence before the suspect arrived. = 嫌疑犯到达之前，警察已经没收了证据。


将来时:
The court will confiscate his assets if he is found guilty. = 如果他被判有罪，法院将没收他的资产。
They will confiscate the illegal products found in the warehouse. = 他们将没收在仓库中发现的非法产品。


被动语态:
His property was confiscated by the government. = 他的财产被政府没收了。
The illegal goods were confiscated at the border. = 走私货物在边境被没收了。


疑问句:
Were the stolen goods confiscated? = 被盗的货物被没收了吗？
Will the police confiscate the drugs they found? = 警察会没收他们发现的毒品吗？


否定句:
The police did not confiscate anything. = 警察什么也没收。
They did not confiscate the evidence. = 他们没有没收证据。
