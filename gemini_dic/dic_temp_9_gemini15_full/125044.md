mulched:/mʌlʧt/| v. |覆盖(土壤)以覆盖物|To cover (soil) with mulch.  Mulching helps retain soil moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature. |The gardener mulched the flower beds with wood chips. 园丁用木片覆盖了花坛。|近义词：cover, blanket |反义词：uncover, expose |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He mulches his garden every spring. = 他每年春天都给他的花园覆盖覆盖物。
They mulch the soil to retain moisture. = 他们用覆盖物覆盖土壤以保持水分。
