magnetician:/ˌmæɡnɪˈtɪʃən/| n. |磁学家；磁学专家|A magnetician is a scientist who studies magnetism and its effects.  They may work in various fields, including physics, engineering, and materials science, conducting research, developing applications, or teaching. |The leading magnetician gave a compelling lecture on the latest discoveries in magnetic fields.  顶尖的磁学家就磁场方面的最新发现做了一个引人入胜的演讲。|physicist, electrophysicist| (No direct antonym, but someone who denies the existence or importance of magnetism could be considered an indirect antonym)|名词
