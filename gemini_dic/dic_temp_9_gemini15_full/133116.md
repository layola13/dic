noneruptive:/ˌnɒnɪˈrʌptɪv/ | adj. |非爆发的，非喷发的|Not involving or characterized by eruption or explosion.  This term is typically used in geological contexts to describe volcanic activity or other geological processes that do not involve a sudden and violent release of energy or material.|The noneruptive phase of the volcano lasted several years before the catastrophic eruption.  非爆发期的火山持续了好几年，然后发生了灾难性的爆发。|近义词：quiet, dormant, passive |反义词：explosive, eruptive
