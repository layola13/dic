propensity:/prəˈpɛnsɪti/ | n. |倾向；癖性；习性 |a natural inclination or tendency |He has a propensity for getting into trouble. 他有惹麻烦的倾向。|近义词：inclination, tendency, predisposition |反义词：aversion, dislike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His propensity for risk-taking is well-known. = 他喜欢冒险是出了名的。
Her propensity for kindness is evident in her actions. = 她的善良体现在她的行为中。

一般过去时:
His propensity for procrastination hindered his progress. = 他拖延的倾向阻碍了他的进步。
Her propensity for overspending led to financial difficulties. = 她过度消费的倾向导致了经济困难。

现在完成时:
His propensity for exaggeration has become quite pronounced. = 他夸大的倾向已经变得相当明显。
Her propensity for secrecy has caused misunderstandings. = 她喜欢保密的倾向导致了误解。

过去完成时:
His propensity for impulsive decisions had already caused problems. = 他冲动做决定的倾向已经造成了问题。
Her propensity for neglecting her health had resulted in illness. = 她忽视健康的倾向导致了疾病。

将来时:
His propensity for laziness will likely impact his future career. = 他懒惰的倾向可能会影响他未来的职业生涯。
Her propensity for perfectionism might lead to burnout. = 她追求完美的倾向可能会导致精疲力竭。

虚拟语气:
If he didn't have such a propensity for gambling, he would be financially secure. = 如果他没有那么强的赌博倾向，他就会经济上稳定。
I wish she didn't have such a propensity for negativity. = 我希望她没有那么强的消极倾向。

被动语态:
His propensity for violence was noted by the police. = 他的暴力倾向被警方注意到了。
Her propensity for artistic expression was praised by her teachers. = 她艺术表达的倾向受到了老师的赞扬。

疑问句:
What is his propensity for arguing about? = 他总是争论什么？
What is the company's propensity for innovation? = 这家公司的创新倾向是什么？

否定句:
He doesn't have a propensity for violence. = 他没有暴力倾向。
She showed no propensity for dishonesty. = 她没有表现出不诚实的倾向。
