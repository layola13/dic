enamorment:/ɪˈnæmərment/| n. |迷恋；倾心；热爱 |A state of being completely captivated by someone or something;  a feeling of intense love or admiration.| Her enamorment with the actor was evident in her every action. 她对这位演员的迷恋在她的一举一动中都显而易见。|近义词：infatuation, fascination, enchantment|反义词：indifference, apathy, dislike|
