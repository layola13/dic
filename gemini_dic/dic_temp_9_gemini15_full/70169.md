fibon:/ˈfaɪbɒn/ | n. |斐波那契数列|The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, usually starting with 0 and 1.  It appears unexpectedly often in nature, such as in the arrangement of leaves on a stem, the spiral arrangement of florets in a sunflower, and the branching of trees.|The Fibonacci sequence is 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and so on.|近义词：Fibonacci numbers, Fibonacci series|反义词:无
