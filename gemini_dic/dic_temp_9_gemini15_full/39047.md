confocal:/ˈkɒnfəʊkəl/| adj. |共焦的，共焦扫描的 | Relating to or using a confocal microscope, which uses a pinhole to eliminate out-of-focus light, resulting in sharper images.  |Example: Confocal microscopy is a powerful technique for visualizing biological structures. 共焦显微镜是一种强大的生物结构可视化技术。|近义词：focused, sharp |反义词：blurred, unfocused |

词源:
The word "confocal" is derived from the combination of "con-" (meaning "together" or "with") and "focal" (relating to a focus or focal point).  It refers to the arrangement of lenses and mirrors in a confocal microscope where multiple points are in focus simultaneously.
