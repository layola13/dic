inventer:/ɪnˈvɛntər/ | n. |发明者，创造者 | A person who invents things.  Someone who creates something new. | 例句：Thomas Edison was a prolific inventor.  托马斯·爱迪生是一位多产的发明家。| 例句：The inventor of the telephone transformed communication. 电话的发明者改变了通讯方式。|近义词：creator, innovator, originator |反义词：destroyer, breaker


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inventor creates new devices. = 发明家创造新的设备。
He invents solutions to complex problems. = 他发明解决复杂问题的方案。


一般过去时:
She invented a new type of engine. = 她发明了一种新型发动机。
They invented a revolutionary method. = 他们发明了一种革命性的方法。


现在完成时:
He has invented several successful products. = 他已经发明了几种成功的产品。
Scientists have invented new medicines to cure diseases. = 科学家们发明了治疗疾病的新药。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, they had invented a similar tool. = 在发现之前，他们已经发明了一种类似的工具。
By 1900, they had invented the first airplane. = 到1900年，他们已经发明了第一架飞机。


将来时:
She will invent a groundbreaking technology. = 她将发明一项突破性的技术。
They will invent many new things in the future. = 他们将来会发明很多新东西。


虚拟语气:
If I were an inventor, I would invent a time machine. = 如果我是一个发明家，我会发明一台时光机。
I wish I had invented that device. = 我真希望是我发明了那个装置。


被动语态:
The new machine was invented by a young engineer. = 这台新机器是由一位年轻的工程师发明的。
Many useful tools have been invented throughout history. = 历史上发明了许多有用的工具。


疑问句:
Who invented the printing press? = 谁发明了印刷机？
When was the telephone invented? = 电话是什么时候发明的？


否定句:
He didn't invent the radio. = 他没有发明收音机。
They hadn't invented a cure for the disease yet. = 他们还没有发明出治疗这种疾病的方法。


PHRASE->
The inventor of... = ...的发明者
A prolific inventor = 多产的发明家
A brilliant inventor = 才华横溢的发明家
A renowned inventor = 著名的发明家
An amateur inventor = 业余发明家
