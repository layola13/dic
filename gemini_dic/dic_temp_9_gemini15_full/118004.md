memorise: /ˈmeməraɪz/ | vt. |记住，熟记 |To learn something by heart; to commit to memory.  |例句：I need to memorise this poem for tomorrow's class. 我需要为明天的课背诵这首诗。|近义词：learn by heart, commit to memory, retain |反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I memorise new vocabulary every day. = 我每天都记住新的词汇。
She memorises important dates easily. = 她很容易记住重要的日期。

一般过去时:
I memorised the entire speech last night. = 我昨晚记住了整篇演讲。
He memorised the phone number before he deleted it. = 他在删除电话号码之前记住了它。

现在完成时:
I have memorised all the capital cities of Europe. = 我已经记住了所有欧洲国家的首都。
She has memorised the lyrics to that song. = 她已经记住了那首歌的歌词。

过去完成时:
I had memorised the script before the audition. = 我在试镜前就已经记住了剧本。
They had memorised the instructions before starting the experiment. = 他们在开始实验之前就已经记住了说明。


将来时:
I will memorise the formula for the test. = 我将为考试记住这个公式。
She will memorise the presentation before the conference. = 她将在会议前记住演示文稿。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would memorise the whole book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把整本书都记住。
I wish I could memorise things more easily. = 我希望我能更容易记住东西。

被动语态:
The poem was memorised by many students. = 这首诗被许多学生背诵了。
The names of the planets are easily memorised. = 行星的名字很容易记住。


疑问句:
Do you memorise things easily? = 你很容易记住东西吗？
Can you memorise this list of words? = 你能记住这份单词列表吗？


否定句:
I cannot memorise long lists of numbers. = 我记不住很长的数字列表。
He didn't memorise the address correctly. = 他没有正确地记住地址。


PHRASE->
memorise a poem = 背诵一首诗
memorise a speech = 背诵一篇演讲
memorise a phone number = 记住一个电话号码
memorise a formula = 记住一个公式
memorise a script = 记住一个剧本
