labradorite:/ˈlæbrədɔːraɪt/| n. |拉长石|A mineral of the feldspar group, typically iridescent. It's known for its labradorescence, a shimmering display of colors.  Labradorite is used in jewelry and ornamental objects.|例句：The pendant was made from a beautiful piece of labradorite, showcasing its vibrant blue and green iridescence. 这枚吊坠是用一块美丽的拉长石制成的，展现了它鲜艳的蓝绿色虹彩。

The labradorite displayed a stunning play of colours in the sunlight.  这块拉长石在阳光下展现出令人惊叹的色彩变化。|近义词：feldspar (a broader category)|反义词：无
