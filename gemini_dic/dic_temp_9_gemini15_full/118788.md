metallurgist: /ˌmɛtəlɜːrdʒɪst/ | n. |冶金学家|A metallurgist is a scientist or engineer who studies the properties of metals and their alloys and who develops methods for extracting, refining, and processing metals.  They are experts in the science and technology of metals. |例句：The metallurgist analyzed the composition of the ancient artifact to determine its age and origin. 冶金学家分析了古代文物的成分以确定其年代和起源。  |例句：A skilled metallurgist is needed to improve the efficiency of the steel production process. 需要一位熟练的冶金学家来提高钢铁生产过程的效率。 |近义词：metalworker, materials scientist |反义词： (无明确反义词)
