colluded:/kəˈluːdɪd/ | v. |共谋；串通；勾结 | To secretly cooperate in a harmful way.  |  The companies were accused of colluding to fix prices.  几家公司被指控串通起来操纵价格。 | conspire, plot, intrigue | cooperate openly, act independently | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collude to manipulate the market. = 他们串通起来操纵市场。
The businesses collude to gain unfair advantage. = 这些企业串通起来获取不正当利益。

一般过去时:
The politicians colluded to cover up the scandal. = 这些政客串通起来掩盖丑闻。
The two companies colluded to drive out competitors. = 这两家公司串通起来打压竞争对手。

现在完成时:
They have colluded for years to avoid paying taxes. = 他们多年来一直串通起来逃税。
The evidence shows they have colluded to deceive the public. = 证据表明他们串通起来欺骗公众。

过去完成时:
It turned out that they had colluded before the deal was made. = 事实证明，他们在交易达成之前就已经串通好了。
The investigation revealed that they had colluded to defraud investors. = 调查显示他们串通起来诈骗投资者。

将来时:
They will collude to suppress the truth, I fear. = 我担心他们会串通起来压制真相。
We suspect they will collude to sabotage the project. = 我们怀疑他们会串通起来破坏这个项目。


虚拟语气:

I wish they hadn't colluded to ruin his reputation. = 我真希望他们没有串通起来毁掉他的名声。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
The investigation found no evidence that they colluded. = 调查未发现他们串通的证据。
They denied colluding with their competitors. = 他们否认与竞争对手串通。
