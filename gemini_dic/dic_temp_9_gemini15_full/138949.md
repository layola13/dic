nux:/nʌks/| n.|坚果；核仁|The word "nux" is the Latin word for "nut".  It's used as a combining form in many scientific and technical terms relating to nuts or nut-like structures.|Example:  Nux vomica is a highly poisonous seed used in some medicines.  马钱子是一种剧毒种子，用于某些药物。|近义词：kernel, seed|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "fruit" in some contexts.
