The word evokes a sense of deep-seated emotional pain.|例句：The poem depicts a deor of immense proportions. (这首诗描绘了巨大的悲伤。)|近义词：grief, sorrow, woe, misery, anguish|反义词：joy, happiness, bliss, gladness
