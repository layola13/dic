It's often used to describe competitions similar to, but not exactly the same as, a triathlon.  The specific events can vary. |Example: The company sponsored a corporate cathlon involving swimming, running, and cycling. 公司赞助了一场公司铁人三项赛，包括游泳、跑步和骑自行车。|近义词：multisport competition, triathlon (if it has three events)|反义词：single-sport competition |名词
