sagittal:/ˈsædʒɪtəl/| adj.|矢状的；矢状面的| Relating to the sagittal suture of the skull, or to a plane parallel to it.  The sagittal plane divides the body into right and left halves.  |例句：The sagittal suture joins the two parietal bones of the skull. 矢状缝连接颅骨的两个顶骨。

The image shows a sagittal view of the brain. 图片显示的是大脑的矢状面视图。|近义词：median, midsagittal|反义词：coronal, transverse
