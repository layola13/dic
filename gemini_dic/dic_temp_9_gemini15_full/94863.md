in a word:/ɪn ə wɜːrd/| adv. |总之，简而言之 |In short; briefly; to put it concisely.  It summarizes or encapsulates a longer statement or idea. |In a word, I disagree.  总之，我不同意。|近义词：briefly, in short, to sum up, in essence |反义词：in detail, elaborately
