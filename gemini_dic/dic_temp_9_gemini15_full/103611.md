jointer:/ˈdʒɔɪntər/ | n. |对接机，榫接机，拼缝机 | A jointer is a woodworking machine used to flatten and square the edges of boards.  It consists of a rotating cutter head and a table, allowing for precise planing. | Example: The carpenter used a jointer to prepare the boards for the table top.  木匠用对接机准备桌面的木板。|近义词：planer, surface planer |反义词：None that directly oppose its function.
