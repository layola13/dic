nonproportionate: /ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənət/| adj. |不成比例的，不相称的 |Not proportionate; out of proportion.  It refers to a lack of balance or equivalence between two or more things.  |例句：The punishment was nonproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。

The response to the crisis was nonproportionate to its actual severity. 对危机的反应与其实际严重程度不成比例。|近义词：disproportionate, unbalanced, unequal, uneven |反义词：proportionate, balanced, equal
