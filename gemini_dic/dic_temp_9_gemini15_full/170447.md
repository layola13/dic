prudency: /ˈpruːdənsɪ/ | n. | 谨慎；审慎；精明 | The quality of being prudent; cautiousness; discretion.  Prudency involves careful consideration of the potential consequences of actions before making decisions. It's about being wise and sensible, especially in the management of resources. |  例句：He showed great prudence in handling the delicate situation. 他在处理棘手局面时表现出极大的谨慎。

例句：Her financial prudence ensured a comfortable retirement.  她的财务谨慎确保了舒适的退休生活。


近义词：caution, carefulness, discretion, wisdom, foresight

反义词：recklessness, rashness, impulsiveness, folly

词源:

The word "prudence" comes from the Old French word "prudence," which in turn originates from the Latin word "prudentia," meaning "foresight," "wisdom," or "skill."  The Latin word is derived from "prudens," meaning "cautious" or "wise."
