organ-donor:/ˈɔːrɡən ˈdoʊnər/ | n. | 器官捐献者 | A person who donates an organ or organs for transplantation.  An organ donor can be a living person donating a part of an organ (such as a kidney) or a deceased person whose organs are harvested after death. | Example: He was a registered organ donor, and his heart saved three lives. 他是一位注册的器官捐献者，他的心脏挽救了三条生命。| 近义词: donor, giver | 反义词: organ recipient
