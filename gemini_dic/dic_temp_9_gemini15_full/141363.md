operationalize: /ˌɒpəreɪʃənəlaɪz/| /ˌɑːpəreɪʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使可操作化；具体化；将…付诸实施 | To put a concept, plan, or theory into practice; to define a concept in such a way that it can be measured.  | The researchers operationalized "stress" by measuring cortisol levels in saliva samples.  研究人员通过测量唾液样本中的皮质醇水平来使“压力”具体化。 | implement, realize, execute, apply |  abstract, theorize | 动词


USAGE->

They operationalize their research plan by collecting data. = 他们通过收集数据来实施他们的研究计划。
