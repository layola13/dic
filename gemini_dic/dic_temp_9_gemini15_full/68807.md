fascicle:/ˈfæsɪkəl/| n.|小册子；束；肌束 |A fascicle is a small bundle, especially of fibers or nerves.  It can also refer to a booklet or pamphlet. |例句：The journal was published in fascicles.  这本期刊分册出版。|例句：The muscle fibers were grouped into fascicles. 肌纤维成束排列。|近义词：leaflet, booklet, pamphlet, bundle |反义词：volume (in the sense of a complete work)
