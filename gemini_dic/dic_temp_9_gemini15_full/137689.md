nonurbanite:/ˌnɒnˈɜːbənaɪt/| n. |非城市居民，乡下人 |A person who lives outside of a city or town; a rural resident.  |Example:  The nonurbanite found the city's noise and crowds overwhelming.  这位非城市居民发现城市的喧嚣和人群令人难以承受。|近义词：rural resident, country dweller, villager |反义词：urbanite, city dweller
