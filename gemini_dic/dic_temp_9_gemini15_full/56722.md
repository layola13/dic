doorbells:/ˈdɔːrbɛlz/ | n. | 门铃 | A device that rings a bell when a button is pressed, typically used to signal arrival at a door.  | Example:  The doorbells rang, announcing the arrival of guests. 门铃响了，宣布客人到了。

The doorbells are broken and need replacing. 门铃坏了，需要更换。


近义词：door chime, bell, buzzer | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doorbell rings every time someone presses the button. 门铃每次有人按按钮都会响。
The doorbells announce visitors at the front door. 门铃会向我们宣布访客的到来。


一般过去时:
The doorbell rang loudly last night. 昨晚门铃响得很响。
The old doorbells stopped working years ago.  旧门铃几年前就停止工作了。


现在完成时:
The doorbell has rung several times this morning. 今早门铃响了好几次。
The neighbors have installed new doorbells.  邻居们安装了新的门铃。


过去完成时:
The doorbell had already rung before I answered the door. 我开门之前门铃已经响过了。
They had replaced the doorbells before we arrived.在我们到达之前，他们已经更换了门铃。


将来时:
The doorbell will ring when the delivery person arrives. 送货员到达时，门铃会响。
We will install new doorbells next week. 我们下周将安装新的门铃。


虚拟语气:
If the doorbell rang, I would answer it. 如果门铃响了，我会去开门。
I wish the doorbells worked properly. 我希望门铃能正常工作。


被动语态:
The doorbell was rung by a mischievous child. 门铃是被一个顽皮的孩子按响的。
The doorbells are being repaired this afternoon. 门铃正在今天下午维修。


疑问句:
Did the doorbell ring? 门铃响了吗？
Has the doorbell rung yet? 门铃响了吗？


否定句:
The doorbell didn't ring this morning. 今早门铃没响。
The doorbells haven't rung all day.  门铃整天都没响。
