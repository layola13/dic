niggardish:/ˈnɪɡərdɪʃ/| adj.|吝啬的；小气的；缺乏的|Stingy or miserly; characterized by or exhibiting niggardliness.  The word carries a strong negative connotation and is often considered offensive due to its resemblance to a racial slur.|例句：His niggardish attitude towards charity was disappointing. 他对慈善的吝啬态度令人失望。
He displayed a niggardish approach to spending on his family. 他对家庭开支采取了吝啬的方式。|近义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, mean, tightfisted|反义词：generous, munificent, lavish, bountiful
