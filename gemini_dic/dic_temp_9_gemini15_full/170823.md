词源：

* **pseudo-**:  From Greek *pseudes* meaning "false" or "lying."
* **evangelic-**: From Greek *euangelion* meaning "good news," referring to the Christian Gospel.
* **-ally**: An adverbial suffix indicating manner or way.


因此，"pseudoevangelically" 字面意思是“以虚假的福音的方式”。
