double-faulting:/ˈdʌbəlˌfɔːltɪŋ/ | v. | 双误，双误发球 | The act of committing two consecutive faults, typically in tennis, resulting in a point for the opponent.  | Example: He double-faulted on match point, costing him the game. 他在赛点时连续两次发球失误，输掉了比赛。|近义词：consecutive faults, two faults in a row |反义词：ace (in tennis)
