sitophobia:/ˌsɪtoʊˈfoʊbiə/| n.| 害怕食物或进食的恐惧症 |A persistent and excessive fear of food or eating, often stemming from traumatic experiences or other underlying psychological issues.  This fear can manifest in various ways, from avoiding specific foods to complete refusal to eat, leading to significant health problems. |He suffers from sitophobia and struggles to eat even small amounts of food. 他患有食物恐惧症，即使少量进食也感到困难。|food phobia, eating disorder | courage, bravery |名词
