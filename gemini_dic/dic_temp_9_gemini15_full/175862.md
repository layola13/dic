ratability:/ˌreɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可评价性，可估价性，等级评定性 | The ability to be rated or assessed; the suitability of something for rating.  This refers to how easily something can be evaluated or assigned a rank or score.  It often implies a system or criteria for rating exists or can be applied. | Example: The ratability of the new performance metric was tested through a pilot study.  新的绩效指标的可评价性通过试点研究进行了测试。|近义词：assessability, evaluability | 反义词：inassessability, unevaluability
