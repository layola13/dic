dizzinesses:/ˈdɪzinəsiz/| n. |头晕眼花（复数）|The plural form of dizziness, referring to multiple instances or types of dizziness.  It describes the sensation of feeling unsteady, faint, or lightheaded.|例句：He experienced several dizzinesses throughout the day, prompting a visit to the doctor. 他一天经历了几次头晕，促使他去看医生。  The patient reported a variety of dizzinesses, each triggered by different stimuli. 病人报告了各种各样的头晕，每次都是由不同的刺激引起的。|近义词：vertigo, lightheadedness, giddiness|反义词：steadiness, stability|名词
