jaggery:/ˈdʒæɡəri/| n.|粗糖，棕榈糖|A traditional unrefined sugar made from the concentrated sap of various palm trees, sugarcane, or date palms. It's often darker in color and has a strong, molasses-like flavor.|The cake was sweetened with jaggery instead of white sugar.|近义词：gur, palm sugar|反义词：refined sugar
