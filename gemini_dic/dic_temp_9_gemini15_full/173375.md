quasi-dividedly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ dɪˈvaɪdədli/ | adv. | 准分开地；似是而非地分开地 |  The word "quasi-dividedly" implies a state of being partially or seemingly divided, but not completely or definitively so. It suggests a condition of incompleteness or ambiguity in the division.  The prefix "quasi" indicates something that resembles or has the characteristics of something else, but is not truly that thing.  | The committee worked quasi-dividedly on the proposal, with some members leaning towards approval and others expressing reservations. = 委员会对该提案的意见似是而非地分歧，一些成员倾向于批准，另一些成员则表示保留。 The land was quasi-dividedly owned, with overlapping claims from different families. = 这块土地的拥有权似是而非地分割，不同家庭的索赔存在重叠。 |  partially divided, seemingly divided | fully united, completely separated | 副词
