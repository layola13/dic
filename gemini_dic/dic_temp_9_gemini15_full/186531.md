ricture:/ˈrɪktʃər/| n. |画面；影像；图画|A ricture is a representation of something, usually a person or scene, made using a camera or by drawing or painting.  It can also refer to a mental image or impression. |例句：The picture of the sunset was breathtaking.  日落的照片令人叹为观止。|例句：I have a vivid picture in my mind of that day. 我脑海中对那天有清晰的印象。|近义词：image, photograph, painting, illustration |反义词：nothingness, blankness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The picture hangs on the wall. = 图片挂在墙上。
She paints beautiful pictures. = 她画美丽的画。


一般过去时:
He took a picture of the mountain. = 他拍了一张山的照片。
They painted a picture of their family. = 他们画了一幅他们家庭的画。


现在完成时:
I have taken many pictures of my cat. = 我已经拍了很多我猫的照片。
She has painted several pictures this year. = 她今年已经画了几幅画。


过去完成时:
He had already taken the picture before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经拍好照片了。
They had painted the picture before the exhibition. = 展览前他们已经画好画了。


将来时:
I will take a picture of you tomorrow. = 我明天给你拍照。
She will paint a picture of the landscape. = 她将画一幅风景画。


虚拟语气:
If I had a camera, I would take a picture. = 如果我有相机，我会拍照。
If she had more time, she would paint a picture. = 如果她有更多时间，她会画一幅画。


被动语态:
The picture was taken by a professional photographer. = 这张照片是由一位专业摄影师拍摄的。
The painting was painted many years ago. = 这幅画是许多年前画的。


疑问句:
Did you take a picture? = 你拍照了吗？
What kind of picture is it? = 这是什么类型的图片？


否定句:
I didn't take a picture. = 我没有拍照。
She hasn't painted a picture yet. = 她还没画画。
