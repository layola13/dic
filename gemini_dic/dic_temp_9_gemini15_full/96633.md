indunas: /ɪnˈduːnəz/ | n. |印地安酋长(复数) |  A Zulu word referring to a group of advisors or counselors to a chief, often with considerable authority.  The term is often used interchangeably with "councillors" but carries a stronger connotation of influence and power.  | Example: The indunas advised the chief on matters of war and peace.  印地安酋长们就战争与和平的事宜向酋长进言。 |近义词: councillors, advisors, chiefs (in some contexts) |反义词: subjects, followers |名词
