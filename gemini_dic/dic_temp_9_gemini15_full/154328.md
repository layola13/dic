perjure:/ˈpɜːrdʒər/| vt.|伪证；作伪证|To willfully give false testimony under oath.  This is a serious crime that can result in significant penalties.|例句：He was accused of perjuring himself in court. 他被指控在法庭上作伪证。|例句：She perjured herself to protect her friend. 她为了保护朋友而作伪证。|近义词：forswear, lie under oath|反义词：testify truthfully, tell the truth|动词
