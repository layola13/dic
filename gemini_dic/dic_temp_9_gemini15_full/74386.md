framed:/freɪmd/| v. adj. n.| 框架；陷害；装裱；| to enclose (something) in a frame; to create a false impression about someone; a picture or photograph enclosed in a frame; |例句：He framed the photograph beautifully. 他把照片装裱得很漂亮。  He was framed for the crime. 他被陷害犯了罪。 |近义词：enclosed, incriminate, setting |反义词：free, exonerate, liberate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist frames the painting with a gold frame. = 艺术家用金色的画框装裱这幅画。
They frame every suspect in the case. = 他们把案件中的每个嫌疑人都陷害了。


一般过去时:
She framed the picture and hung it on the wall. = 她装裱了这幅画并把它挂在了墙上。
The media framed the story to suit their agenda. = 媒体歪曲了这个故事以符合他们的议程。


现在完成时:
He has framed many paintings in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中装裱过许多画作。
The police have framed an innocent man. = 警察陷害了一个无辜的人。


过去完成时:
They had framed the defendant before the trial. = 他们在审判前就已经陷害了被告。
She had framed the photo before giving it to her friend. = 在送给朋友之前，她已经装裱了照片。


将来时:
I will frame this diploma when I graduate. = 我毕业后会把这张文凭装裱起来。
The prosecutor will frame him with false evidence. = 检察官将用伪证陷害他。


虚拟语气:
If I had framed the picture differently, it would look better. = 如果我换一种方式装裱这幅画，它会看起来更好。
If they hadn't framed him, he would be a free man. = 如果他们没有陷害他，他就会是一个自由人。


被动语态:
The picture was framed by a professional. = 这幅画是由专业人士装裱的。
He was framed by a rival company. = 他被竞争对手公司陷害了。


疑问句:
Is the picture framed? = 这幅画装裱了吗？
Was he framed by the police? = 他是被警察陷害的吗？


否定句:
I didn't frame the photo. = 我没有装裱这张照片。
They didn't frame him for the murder. = 他们没有因为谋杀而陷害他。


PHRASE->
frame a question  = 提出一个问题
frame a story = 编造一个故事
frame of mind = 心情；心态
frame of reference = 参考系
out of frame = 脱离主题
