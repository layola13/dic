reincorporating: /ˌriːɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新合并；重新纳入；重新吸收 | The act of incorporating again; bringing something back into a whole or group.  This verb describes the process of reintegrating something that was previously separated or removed. |


例句：

The company is reincorporating its overseas branches. = 公司正在重新合并其海外分支机构。

The government is reincorporating traditional cultural practices into the school curriculum. = 政府正在将传统文化习俗重新纳入学校课程。


近义词：reintegrating, reuniting, consolidating, merging


反义词：separating, dividing, disintegrating, disbanding


词性：动词
