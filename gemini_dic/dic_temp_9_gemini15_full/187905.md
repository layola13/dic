rosys:/ˈroʊzi/| adj.|玫瑰色的，美好的，乐观的|Rose-colored; characterized by optimism and cheerfulness.  The word evokes a sense of pleasantness and hopefulness, often associated with a rosy complexion or a bright future.|例句：The rosy dawn painted the sky with vibrant colors.  玫瑰色的黎明用鲜艳的色彩描绘着天空。
The future looks rosy for the company. 这家公司的前景一片光明。|近义词：optimistic, cheerful, bright, promising|反义词：gloomy, pessimistic, bleak, discouraging|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The future looks rosy. = 未来一片光明。
Her cheeks are rosy. = 她的脸颊红润。

一般过去时:
The dawn was rosy. = 黎明是玫瑰色的。
Her outlook was rosy before the accident. = 事故发生前，她对未来很乐观。

现在完成时:
Things have become rosy since she got the promotion. = 自从她升职以来，情况好转了。
The situation has turned rosy unexpectedly. = 局势出乎意料地好转了。

过去完成时:
He had a rosy view of the world before experiencing hardship. = 在经历苦难之前，他对世界有着美好的憧憬。
They had a rosy picture of their future together. = 他们对他们共同的未来有着美好的憧憬。

将来时:
The prospects will be rosy if the deal goes through. = 如果这笔交易达成，前景将一片光明。
Everything will be rosy once the problem is solved. = 一旦问题解决，一切都会好起来。

虚拟语气:
If everything went as planned, the future would be rosy. = 如果一切按计划进行，未来将会一片光明。
I wish the future were rosy for everyone. = 我希望每个人的未来都一片光明。

被动语态:
Her cheeks were painted rosy by the artist. = 画家把她脸颊画成了玫瑰色。
The situation is being painted in a rosy light by the media. = 媒体正在将形势描绘成一片光明。


疑问句:
Is the outlook rosy? = 前景光明吗？
Are you feeling rosy? = 你感觉良好吗？（指气色好）


否定句:

Her complexion isn't rosy today. = 她今天脸色不好。
