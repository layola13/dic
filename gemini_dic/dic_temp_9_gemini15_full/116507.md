maternities:/məˈtɜːrnətiz/ | n. |分娩；产科医院；产妇时期 |The state or period of being a mother; motherhood.  It can also refer to a maternity hospital or ward. |例句：The hospital's maternity ward is always busy. 这家医院的产科病房总是很忙。

例句：She enjoyed her maternity leave. 她享受她的产假。


近义词：motherhood, childbirth, confinement (archaic) | 反义词：paternity


词源:  From Latin *maternitas, from mater "mother".  The suffix "-ity" indicates a state or condition.
