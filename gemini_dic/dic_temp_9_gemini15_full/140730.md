omnipotence:/ˈɒmnɪpəʊtəns/ /ˈɑːmnɪpəʊtəns/| n. |全能；万能；无限权力 |The state of being omnipotent; unlimited power.  The ability to do anything.  Often attributed to God.|例句：The belief in God's omnipotence is central to many religions. = 相信上帝的全能是许多宗教的核心。

The king believed in his own omnipotence, ignoring the warnings of his advisors. = 国王相信自己的万能，无视了顾问们的警告。


近义词：almightiness, supreme power, unlimited power |反义词：impotence, powerlessness, weakness |词性：名词
