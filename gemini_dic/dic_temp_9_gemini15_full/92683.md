ice-skating:/ˈaɪsˌskeɪtɪŋ/| n.|溜冰；滑冰|The activity of gliding on ice using skates.  It's a popular winter sport and recreational activity enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.|例句：Ice-skating is my favorite winter pastime. = 溜冰是我最喜欢的冬季消遣活动。

例句：She went ice-skating with her friends on the frozen lake. = 她和朋友们在冰冻的湖面上滑冰。


近义词：skating, ice-sliding|反义词：None|词性：名词
