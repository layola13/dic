overcoldly: /ˌoʊvərˈkoʊldli/ | adv. | 过分冷淡地，冷冰冰地 |  Describes an action or manner characterized by excessive coldness or lack of warmth in feeling or expression. It suggests a deliberate and perhaps unkind coldness. | 例句：He responded to her heartfelt confession overcoldly, which hurt her deeply. 他对她发自肺腑的告白反应过于冷淡，这深深地伤害了她。  She treated the newcomers overcoldly, creating an unfriendly atmosphere. 她对新来的人过于冷淡，营造了一种不友好的氛围。| 近义词：indifferently, coldly, distantly, unsympathetically | 反义词：warmly, affectionately, enthusiastically, cordially | 无


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc. It modifies verbs and describes how an action is performed.  Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable to "overcoldly".


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "overcoldly".  The word is used straightforwardly to describe a manner of acting or behaving.
