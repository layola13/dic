refortify:/ˌriːˈfɔːrtɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新强化；再设防|To strengthen or fortify again; to provide with new fortifications.|例句：The army refortified the border after the recent attack.  军队在最近的袭击之后重新加强了边境防御。

例句：The engineers refortified the dam to withstand the expected flood.  工程师们加固了大坝以抵御预期的洪水。


近义词：reinforce, strengthen, bolster|反义词：weaken, undermine, debilitate|词性:动词
