relink:/ˌriːˈlɪŋk/| vt.|重新链接，重新连接|To reconnect or establish a connection again.  It often refers to re-establishing a link that has been broken or severed. |例句：The technician relinked the severed network cables. 技术人员重新连接了断开的网络电缆。

例句：We need to relink the database to the new server. 我们需要将数据库重新链接到新服务器。


近义词：reconnect, rejoin, reattach, link up|反义词：disconnect, sever, unlink|动词
