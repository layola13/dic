prettified:/ˈprɛtɪfaɪd/| adj.| 美化的；打扮漂亮的；装饰漂亮的 | Made pretty or attractive; adorned.  The prettified version of the story omitted the unpleasant details. |例句：The prettified village looked nothing like the real thing.  这个经过美化的村庄和真实情况完全不一样。

例句：Her prettified appearance was a stark contrast to her usual tomboy style. 她打扮得漂漂亮亮的，和她平时假小子风格形成了鲜明对比。


近义词：beautified, embellished, adorned, decorated

反义词：uglified, disfigured, marred


词性：形容词
