silent: /ˈsaɪlənt/ | adj. |沉默的，无声的，寂静的 |  Making no sound; quiet.  | 例句：The library was silent. 图书馆里一片寂静。

例句：The room was silent except for the ticking clock. 除了滴答作响的钟表，房间里一片寂静。


近义词：quiet, hushed, still, speechless, mute | 反义词：noisy, loud, boisterous, vocal


词源：源自古法语 *silent,  来自拉丁语 *silēns, silēntis,  是silēre (保持沉默) 的现在分词。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The baby is silent. = 婴儿很安静。
The audience remains silent. = 观众保持沉默。


一般过去时:
She was silent for a moment. = 她沉默了一会儿。
He remained silent throughout the meeting. = 他在整个会议上都保持沉默。


现在完成时:
She has been silent since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，她就一直沉默不语。
They have remained silent about the scandal. = 关于那件丑闻，他们一直保持沉默。


过去完成时:
He had been silent for so long that I thought he was asleep. = 他沉默了这么久，我以为他睡着了。
The crowd had been silent, waiting for the announcement. = 人群一直沉默着，等待着宣布。


将来时:
She will be silent no longer. = 她将不再沉默。
He will remain silent about his involvement. = 他将对自己的参与保持沉默。


虚拟语气:
If she were silent, we could hear better. = 如果她保持安静，我们就能听得更清楚。
I wish he would be silent for once. = 我希望他这次能保持安静。


被动语态:
Her concerns were silenced by the authorities.  = 她对事情的担忧被当局压制了。
His protests were silenced quickly. = 他的抗议很快就被压制了。


疑问句:
Why are you so silent today? = 你今天为什么这么沉默？
Was everyone silent during the performance? = 表演期间大家都保持沉默吗？


否定句:
The children were not silent during the movie. = 孩子们在看电影的时候没有安静。
He wasn't silent about his feelings. = 他没有对自己的感受保持沉默。
