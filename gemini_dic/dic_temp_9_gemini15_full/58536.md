duffs:/dʌfs/| n.|笨蛋；无用的人；废物；(尤指)醉酒的人|A duff is a foolish or incompetent person; a worthless thing; specifically, a drunkard.  The word often carries a connotation of mild disapproval or amusement rather than harsh condemnation.|例句：He's a bit of a duff, always making silly mistakes. 他有点笨，总是犯傻错误。|例句：Don't be such a duff; clean up your room! 不要这么懒，收拾你的房间！|近义词：fool, idiot, incompetent, loser |反义词：genius, expert, competent
