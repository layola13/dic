finance-companies: /ˈfaɪnæns ˌkʌmpəniz/ | n. |金融公司 |Financial institutions that provide various financial services to individuals and businesses.  These services can include lending money, managing investments, providing insurance, and offering other financial products. They differ from banks in their focus and regulations. |Example: Many small businesses rely on finance companies for loans when banks are unwilling to lend. 许多小型企业在银行不愿贷款时依靠金融公司贷款。|近义词：financial institutions, lending institutions |反义词：N/A
