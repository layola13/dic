rearranging:/ˌriːəˈreɪndʒɪŋ/ | v. |重新安排；改组 |The act of changing the arrangement or order of something.  It implies a systematic or deliberate change, not just a random shuffling.  |例句：She spent the morning rearranging the furniture in the living room. = 她上午花时间重新布置客厅的家具。

例句：The company is rearranging its departments to improve efficiency. = 公司正在重组部门以提高效率。


近义词：reorganizing, reshuffling, reordering, readjusting | 反义词：leaving as is, keeping the same


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rearranging the books on the shelf. = 他们在重新摆放书架上的书。
He rearranges his schedule frequently. = 他经常重新安排他的日程表。


一般过去时:
I rearranged the flowers in the vase. = 我重新摆放了花瓶里的花。
They rearranged the seating plan for the conference. = 他们重新安排了会议的座位安排。


现在完成时:
She has rearranged the entire office. = 她已经重新布置了整个办公室。
We have rearranged our travel plans due to the weather. = 由于天气原因，我们已经重新安排了我们的旅行计划。


过去完成时:
He had rearranged the meeting before the chairman arrived. = 主席到达之前，他已经重新安排了会议。
They had rearranged the furniture several times before settling on the current layout. = 他们在最终确定目前的布局之前已经几次重新摆放了家具。


将来时:
I will rearrange the appointment for next week. = 我将把预约改到下周。
We will rearrange the items in the display case. = 我们将重新安排展示柜里的物品。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rearrange my priorities. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会重新安排我的优先级。
I wish I had rearranged my schedule earlier. = 我真希望我早点重新安排我的日程表。


被动语态:
The furniture was rearranged by the movers. = 家具被搬家公司重新摆放了。
The schedule is being rearranged due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，日程表正在重新安排。


疑问句:

Will they be rearranging the seating chart? = 他们会重新安排座位表吗？


否定句:
He did not rearrange his plans despite the problems. = 尽管出了问题，他也没有改变他的计划。
They haven't rearranged the exhibit yet. = 他们还没有重新布置展览。
