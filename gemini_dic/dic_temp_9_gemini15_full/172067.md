purloined:/pɜːrˈlɔɪnd/| v.| 偷窃；窃取 | To steal something in a secretive or sly way.  |例句：The thief purloined the jewels from the museum.  窃贼从博物馆偷走了珠宝。

例句：He purloined a cookie from the jar while no one was looking. 他趁没人注意的时候偷偷从罐子里拿了一块饼干。


近义词：steal, pilfer, filch, thieve |反义词：return, restore, donate


词源:  From Old French *purloigner, from pur "pure," and loigner "to lure away," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *logāre "to lure."  The original sense was to lure away, later evolving to imply stealing.

过去完成时:
