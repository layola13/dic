mambo:/ˈmæmboʊ/| n. v.|曼波舞；曼波乐曲|A lively dance of Cuban origin, characterized by a rhythmic beat and hip movements.  It can also refer to the music associated with this dance.|例句：They danced the mambo all night long.  他们整晚都在跳曼波舞。|例句：The band played a hot mambo number. 乐队演奏了一首热情的曼波舞曲。|近义词：rumba, cha-cha, salsa|反义词：无
