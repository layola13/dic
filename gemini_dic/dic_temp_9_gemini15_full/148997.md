ovoviviparous:/ˌoʊvoʊvɪˈvɪpərəs/| adj.|卵胎生的|Producing young by means of eggs that are hatched within the body, so the young are born alive.  This reproductive strategy combines aspects of oviparity (egg-laying) and viviparity (live birth).|例句：Some species of sharks are ovoviviparous, meaning the eggs develop inside the mother's body but hatch before birth.|近义词：viviparous (in some contexts)|反义词：oviparous
