remoulds:/ˌriːˈmoʊldz/| vt.|重塑；改造；重新塑造|To shape or form (something) again; to change the form or nature of something.|The sculptor remoulded the clay into a new shape.  雕塑家将粘土重新塑造成一个新的形状。|The company is remoulding its image to attract younger customers.  公司正在重塑其形象以吸引年轻客户。|reshape, reform, reconstruct|destroy, ruin, demolish|动词
