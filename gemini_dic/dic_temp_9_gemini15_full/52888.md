diminutiveness:/ˌdɪmɪˈnuːtɪvnəs/| n. |微小；细微；渺小；轻微|The state or quality of being diminutive; smallness; littleness.  It can refer to physical size, importance, or significance. |例句：The diminutiveness of the figurine made it easy to lose.  小雕像的微小使得它很容易丢失。

例句：The diminutiveness of their contribution was overlooked. 他们贡献的微不足道被忽视了。


近义词：smallness, littleness, insignificance, triviality, pettiness

反义词：largeness, enormity, significance, importance


词性: 名词
