现在完成时:

She has expiated her guilt by making amends to those she wronged. = 她通过向她伤害过的人赔礼道歉来赎清她的罪责。

过去完成时:

被动语态:

词源:
From Latin *expiāre "to appease," from ex- "out" + piāre "to appease, propitiate," related to pius "pious."  The sense evolved from appeasing a deity to atoning for wrongdoing.
