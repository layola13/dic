rebuffs:/rɪˈbʌfs/| v. n.|冷落；拒绝；斥退 |To reject or refuse someone or something abruptly or bluntly.  A blunt or abrupt rejection. |例句：He rebuffs all attempts at friendship. 他冷落所有想与他交朋友的人。

例句：Her proposal was met with a curt rebuffs. 她的提议遭到了冷酷的拒绝。


近义词：reject, refuse, snub, spurn |反义词：accept, welcome, embrace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rebuffs my advances. = 他拒绝我的示好。
She rebuffs any criticism. = 她拒绝任何批评。


一般过去时:
He rebuffed her offer of help. = 他拒绝了她提供的帮助。

现在完成时:
He has rebuffed every attempt to reconcile. = 他拒绝了所有和解的尝试。
She has consistently rebuffed his advances. = 她一直拒绝他的示好。


过去完成时:
