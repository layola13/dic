overjacket:/ˈoʊvərdʒækɪt/| n. |夹克衫，外套|A coat or jacket worn over other clothing.  It is typically heavier and more protective than a regular jacket.|Example: He put on his overjacket before venturing out into the cold. 他在冒险出去寒冷的外面之前穿上了他的夹克衫。|近义词：coat, jacket, topcoat|反义词：undergarment
