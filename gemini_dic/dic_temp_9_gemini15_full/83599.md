haematolysis:/ˌhiːmətˈɒlɪsɪs/ | n. |溶血|The destruction of red blood cells, resulting in the release of hemoglobin into the surrounding fluid.  This can be caused by various factors, including autoimmune diseases, infections, or certain medications.|Example: The patient presented with symptoms consistent with intravascular haematolysis, including hemoglobinuria.|近义词：hemolysis|反义词：erythropoiesis
