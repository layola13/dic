put-to-sea:/pʊt tuː siː/| v.|启航，出海|To set sail; to begin a voyage.|例句：The ship was put to sea in the early morning.  船在清晨启航了。|例句：The captain decided to put the ship to sea despite the storm. 船长决定尽管暴风雨来临也要出海。|近义词：set sail, depart, embark|反义词：return to port, come ashore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The captain puts the ship to sea every Monday. = 船长每周一都让船出海。
They put their small boat to sea at dawn. = 他们在黎明时分让他们的的小船出海。


一般过去时:
The sailors put the ship to sea before the storm hit. = 水手们在暴风雨来临之前让船出海了。
Columbus put his ships to sea in 1492. = 哥伦布在1492年让他的船出海了。


现在完成时:
They have put the fishing boat to sea three times this week. = 他们本周已经三次让渔船出海了。
We haven't put the yacht to sea yet this summer. = 我们今年夏天还没让游艇出海呢。


过去完成时:
They had put the ship to sea before the news of the storm arrived. = 在暴风雨的消息传来之前，他们已经让船出海了。
The fishermen had put their boats to sea before the fog rolled in. = 在雾到来之前，渔民们已经让他们的船出海了。


将来时:
We will put the ship to sea at first light tomorrow. = 我们明天拂晓时分将让船出海。
The expedition will put their research vessel to sea next month. = 探险队下个月将让他们的研究船出海。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, we would put the boat to sea. = 如果天气更好，我们会让船出海。
I wish we could put the ship to sea, but the storm is too strong. = 我希望我们可以让船出海，但暴风雨太强了。


被动语态:
The ship was put to sea despite the warnings. = 尽管有警告，船还是被放出海了。
The small boat was put to sea by inexperienced sailors. = 这艘小船是由缺乏经验的水手放出海的。


疑问句:
When will the ship be put to sea? = 船什么时候出海？
Have they put the fishing boats to sea yet? = 他们把渔船放出海了吗？


否定句:
They didn't put the ship to sea because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，他们没有让船出海。
We won't put the boat to sea unless the storm passes. = 除非暴风雨过去，否则我们不会让船出海。
