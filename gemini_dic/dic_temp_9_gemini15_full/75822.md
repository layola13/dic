fumigate:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪt/|/ˈfjuːməɡeɪt/| vt.|熏蒸；消毒|To expose (a building, room, etc.) to the fumes of a disinfectant or insecticide to destroy insects, bacteria, etc.|例句：We had to fumigate the house after the flood to prevent mold growth. 我们洪水过后不得不对房子进行熏蒸以防止霉菌滋生。
例句：The farmer fumigated his crops to kill pests. 农民对庄稼进行熏蒸以杀死害虫。|近义词：disinfect, sterilize|反义词：contaminate
