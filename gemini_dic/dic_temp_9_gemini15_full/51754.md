devour:/dɪˈvaʊər/| vt.|吞食；吞噬；毁灭；狼吞虎咽|to eat (something) eagerly and in large amounts; to destroy completely.|例句：The fire devoured the entire forest.  大火吞噬了整片森林。
例句：He devoured the entire pizza in ten minutes. 他十分钟内就狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整个披萨。|近义词：consume, gulp, gobble, engulf|反义词：starve, abstain, reject|动词
