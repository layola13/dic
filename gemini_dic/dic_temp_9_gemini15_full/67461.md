extolling:/ɪkˈstoʊlɪŋ/| v.|赞扬；颂扬；吹捧|To praise enthusiastically; to glorify.|例句：The speaker spent much of the speech extolling the virtues of hard work. 演讲者在演讲中大部分时间都在赞扬努力工作的优点。|例句：She extolled the benefits of regular exercise. 她赞扬了定期锻炼的好处。|近义词：praise, commend, laud, celebrate|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extols the virtues of patience. = 他赞扬耐心的美德。
They extol their company's achievements. = 他们赞扬他们公司的成就。

一般过去时:
The teacher extolled the students' hard work. = 老师赞扬了学生们的努力工作。
The author extolled the beauty of the natural world. = 作者赞扬了自然世界的美丽。

现在完成时:
She has extolled the benefits of meditation for years. = 她多年来一直赞扬冥想的好处。
They have extolled the virtues of their new product. = 他们一直赞扬他们新产品的优点。

过去完成时:
He had extolled the benefits of the program before the meeting. = 他在会议前就已经赞扬了该项目的好处。
The critics had extolled her previous novels. = 批评家们之前就赞扬过她的前几部小说。

将来时:
She will extol the virtues of kindness in her next speech. = 她将在下一次演讲中赞扬善良的美德。
The company will extol its new technology at the conference. = 公司将在会议上赞扬其新技术。

虚拟语气:
If I were to write a book, I would extol the importance of family. = 如果我要写一本书，我会赞扬家庭的重要性。
I wish they would extol the efforts of the volunteers. = 我希望他们会赞扬志愿者的努力。

被动语态:
The benefits of exercise are extolled by many health professionals. = 许多健康专业人士都赞扬了锻炼的好处。
His achievements were extolled in the local newspaper. = 他在当地报纸上受到赞扬。

疑问句:
Does the article extol the virtues of simplicity? = 这篇文章是否赞扬了简单的好处？
What qualities does the author extol in his hero? = 作者在他的英雄身上赞扬了哪些品质？

否定句:
He did not extol the virtues of laziness. = 他并没有赞扬懒惰的优点。
The review did not extol the film's originality. = 这篇评论并没有赞扬这部电影的原创性。


PHRASE->
extol the virtues of = 赞扬…的优点
extol someone's praises = 赞扬某人
extol the merits of = 赞扬…的优点
extol a person's achievements = 赞扬某人的成就
extol one's own virtues = 吹嘘自己的优点


词源:
From Old French *extoller, from Latin extollere, from ex- ("out, up, completely") + tollere ("to lift, raise").  The word ultimately reflects the idea of "lifting up" or "raising high" in praise.
