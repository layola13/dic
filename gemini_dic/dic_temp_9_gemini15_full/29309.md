castellation:/ˌkæs.təlˈeɪ.ʃən/| n.|城垛；齿状；锯齿状|The architectural feature of a castle's walls, characterized by regularly spaced projecting battlements.  It also refers to any structure or pattern resembling these battlements.|例句：The castle's castellations were clearly visible from the valley below. 城堡的城垛从山谷下清晰可见。

例句：The jagged edges of the mountain range resembled a line of castellations.  山脉的锯齿状边缘像一条城垛线。


近义词：battlement, crenellation, embattlement|反义词：smoothness, flatness|名词
