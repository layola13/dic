disseminates:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪts/ | vt. |传播；散布；播种|To spread or disperse something, especially information or ideas.|The government is trying to disseminate information about the virus. 政府正在努力传播有关病毒的信息。|近义词：spread, distribute, propagate, broadcast |反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university disseminates research findings to the public. = 大学向公众传播研究成果。
The wind disseminates seeds across the fields. = 风把种子散播到田野里。


一般过去时:
The news was disseminated quickly through social media. = 这个消息通过社交媒体迅速传播开来。
The farmer disseminated seeds in his field. = 农民在他的田里播种。


现在完成时:
The organization has disseminated countless articles on its website. = 该组织已在其网站上传播了无数文章。
The company has disseminated its new product information widely. = 公司已广泛传播其新产品信息。


过去完成时:
Before the conference, the organizers had disseminated all the necessary documents. = 会议之前，组织者已经分发了所有必要的文档。
The rumor had been disseminated throughout the town before anyone could stop it. = 在任何人能够阻止它之前，谣言已经在镇上广为流传。


将来时:
The company will disseminate the press release tomorrow. = 公司将于明天发布新闻稿。
The government will disseminate information about the upcoming election. = 政府将传播有关即将举行的选举的信息。


虚拟语气:
If they disseminated the information properly, there would be less confusion. = 如果他们能恰当地传播信息，就不会有那么多混淆了。
I wish they had disseminated the news more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地传播新闻。


被动语态:
The information was disseminated widely through various channels. = 信息通过各种渠道广泛传播。
The new policy is being disseminated to all employees. = 新政策正在向所有员工传播。


疑问句:
How is the company disseminating its marketing message? = 公司是如何传播其营销信息的？
How was the news disseminated so quickly? = 消息是如何传播得如此之快的？


否定句:
The government did not disseminate the information effectively. = 政府没有有效地传播信息。
They failed to disseminate the important research findings. = 他们未能传播重要的研究结果。
