preconfinement: /ˌpriːkənˈfaɪnmənt/ | n. | 预先限制；预先隔离 |  The state or period of being confined or restricted before a more formal or significant confinement.  It often refers to measures taken before a full lockdown or quarantine to prevent the spread of a disease or other harmful event.  | 例句：The preconfinement measures included enhanced sanitation and travel restrictions. = 预先限制措施包括加强卫生和出行限制。

The preconfinement period allowed authorities to prepare for a more extensive lockdown. = 预先隔离期使当局能够为更广泛的封锁做准备。


近义词：preliminary restriction, early containment, preparatory isolation


反义词：post-confinement, release, liberation

词性：名词
