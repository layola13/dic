overwear:/ˈoʊvərˌwɛr/| vt.|过度穿着；穿坏；磨损|To wear excessively or until worn out.  It implies wearing something so much that it becomes damaged or unusable.|例句：He overwears his shoes by wearing them every day. 他每天都穿那双鞋，把鞋穿坏了。|例句：Don't overwear that delicate silk blouse. 不要过度穿着那件精致的丝绸衬衫。|近义词：wear out, overuse, abuse|反义词：underwear, spare


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overwears his work boots. = 他把他的工作靴穿坏了。
She overwears her favorite sweater. = 她把最喜欢的毛衣穿坏了。

一般过去时:
They overwore their hiking boots on the trek. = 他们在徒步旅行中把登山靴穿坏了。

现在完成时:
I have overworn my running shoes. = 我把我的跑鞋穿坏了。
