missorting:/mɪsˈsɔːrtɪŋ/| n.|错误分类，错分|The act of sorting something incorrectly; the result of such an act.  It implies a failure to categorize or arrange items according to a set of rules or criteria.|例句：The missorting of the mail caused significant delays.邮件的错误分类导致了严重的延误。

The missorting of the data led to inaccurate conclusions. 数据的错误分类导致了不准确的结论。|近义词：misclassification, misarrangement, improper sorting|反义词：correct sorting, accurate classification|名词
