pigment:/ˈpɪɡmənt/| n.|色素；颜料|A pigment is a substance that is used to color something, such as paint, ink, or plastic.  It's a natural coloring matter of animal or plant tissue. |The artist mixed various pigments to create the desired shade of green.  艺术家混合了各种颜料来创造所需的绿色阴影。|dye, color, tint|colorless, transparent|名词
