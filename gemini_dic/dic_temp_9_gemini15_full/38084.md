composted:/kəmˈpoʊstɪd/| adj.|堆肥的；经过堆肥处理的|  Describes material that has undergone the composting process,  a method of decomposing organic matter to produce a nutrient-rich soil amendment. |The composted leaves enriched the soil. 堆肥的树叶使土壤肥沃。 The gardener used composted manure in his vegetable patch. 园丁在他的菜地里使用了堆肥的粪肥。|fertilized, enriched|unfertilized, barren|形容词


USAGE->

The farmer composts his vegetable scraps. = 农民将他的蔬菜残渣进行堆肥。
Composting reduces landfill waste. = 堆肥减少了垃圾填埋场的垃圾。
