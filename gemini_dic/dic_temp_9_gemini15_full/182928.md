rephased:/ˌriːˈfeɪzd/| v. |重新措辞；改写；重新表达 |To express something in a different way, often to make it clearer or more effective.  |Example:  I rephrased my answer to make it more understandable. 我重新措辞我的回答以使其更易理解。  The teacher rephrased the complex question to help the students understand.老师改写了复杂的问题以帮助学生理解。|近义词：reword, reformulate, paraphrase |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to leave the phrasing unchanged.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rephrases his sentences often. 他经常改写他的句子。
She rephrases her arguments to appeal to a wider audience. 她重新表达她的论点以吸引更广泛的受众。


一般过去时:
I rephrased my email before sending it. 我在发送邮件之前重新措辞了。
He rephrased the instructions so that everyone understood. 他重新表达了指示，以便每个人都理解。


现在完成时:
She has rephrased the paragraph several times. 她已经多次改写了这段文字。
They have rephrased the contract to clarify the terms. 他们已经重新措辞了合同以阐明条款。


过去完成时:
I had rephrased my speech before giving it. 我在发表演讲之前已经重新措辞了。
She had rephrased the letter several times before sending it. 她寄出信之前已经多次改写了信。

虚拟语气:
If I were to rephrase it, I would say this. 如果我要重新表达它，我会这么说。
I wish I had rephrased my comments more diplomatically. 我希望我当时能更委婉地重新表达我的评论。


被动语态:
The sentence was rephrased to improve its clarity. 句子被重新措辞以提高其清晰度。
The instructions were rephrased to make them easier to follow. 指示被重新表达以使其更容易遵循。


疑问句:

否定句:
