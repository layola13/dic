ripest:/ˈraɪpɪst/| adj.| 最成熟的；最完美的 |  Most mature or developed; perfect in its state.  It describes something that has reached its peak of ripeness or development. | 例句：The ripest mangoes were sweet and juicy. 最成熟的芒果又甜又多汁。

The ripest moment to harvest the grapes is just before they start to wrinkle. 收获葡萄最合适的时机是在它们开始起皱之前。

反义词：unripe, immature, undeveloped
