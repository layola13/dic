musks:/mʌskz/ | n. |麝香；分泌麝香的动物；强烈的气味 | Musk is a strong-smelling substance.  麝香是一种气味强烈的物质。The musks of the deer family are highly prized. 鹿科动物的麝香非常珍贵。|近义词：scent, fragrance, perfume |反义词：odorless

词源:
The word "musk" ultimately derives from the Persian word "mushk" (مشك), meaning "testicle" (referring to the source of the scent in the musk deer).  This Persian word entered Middle English via Old French and Arabic.
