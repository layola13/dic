embeddedness:/ɪmˈbedɪdnəs/ | n. | 嵌入性；融入性；嵌套性 | The state of being embedded; the degree to which something is integrated into a larger system or context.  It often refers to the interconnectedness of social structures and the influence of social networks on individual behavior.  | Example:  The study examined the embeddedness of small businesses within their local communities.  这项研究考察了小型企业与其当地社区的融合程度。 The high embeddedness of the culture made it difficult for outsiders to understand.  这种文化的深度融入使得外人难以理解。|近义词：integration, interconnectedness, involvement |反义词：isolation, detachment, independence | 名词
