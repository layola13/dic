crenelation:/ˌkrɛnəˈleɪʃən/| n. |垛口，齿状饰|The architectural feature of a battlement, characterized by a series of regularly spaced indentations or notches.  It refers to the pattern of alternating raised and lowered sections along a wall or parapet, providing defensive cover for archers or soldiers. |The castle walls were enhanced with intricate crenelations. 城墙上装饰着复杂的垛口。 The crenelations provided strategic cover during sieges. 垛口在围攻期间提供了战略掩护。| battlement, embattlement | smooth wall | 名词
