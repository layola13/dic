garage:/ɡəˈrɑːʒ/| n.|车库；修车厂|A garage is a building for storing a car or cars.  It can also refer to a place where cars are repaired.|例句：My car is in the garage. 我的车在车库里。

例句：He works at a garage. 他在修车厂工作。|近义词：carport, shed|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The garage houses two cars. = 车库里停着两辆车。
He often cleans the garage. = 他经常打扫车库。


一般过去时:
We parked the car in the garage yesterday. = 昨天我们把车停在了车库里。
The mechanic fixed my car at the garage last week. = 机械师上周在我的车库里修理了我的车。


现在完成时:
They have repainted the garage. = 他们已经重新粉刷了车库。
I have never seen such a big garage. = 我从未见过这么大的车库。


过去完成时:
The car had been in the garage for a week before we noticed the problem. = 我们发现问题之前，车已经在车库里放了一个星期了。
They had already fixed the flat tire before I arrived at the garage. = 我到达车库之前，他们已经修好了爆胎。


将来时:
We will clean out the garage tomorrow. = 我们明天将清理车库。
He will have his car serviced at the garage next week. = 他下周将在车库里保养他的车。


虚拟语气:
If I had a garage, I would store my bicycle there. = 如果我有车库，我会把我的自行车放在那里。
I wish I had a bigger garage. = 我希望我有一个更大的车库。


被动语态:
The garage is cleaned regularly. = 车库定期打扫。
The car was repaired in the garage. = 车子在车库里修理好了。


疑问句:
Is the car in the garage? = 车子在车库里吗？
Where is the nearest garage? = 最近的修车厂在哪里？


否定句:
The garage isn't big enough for two cars. = 车库不够大，容不下两辆车。
He didn't fix the car in the garage. = 他没有在车库里修理车子。
