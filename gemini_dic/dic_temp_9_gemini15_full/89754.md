hops:/hɒps/|ˈhɑːps/| n. v.|啤酒花；跳跃；迅速移动|The dried flowers of the hop plant, used in brewing beer to give it a bitter taste and aroma.  Also, to move quickly by jumping.|例句：The hops are harvested in the autumn.  啤酒花在秋季收获。

The rabbit hops across the field. 兔子跳过田野。|近义词：jump, leap, skip, bound|反义词：crawl, creep

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hops over the fence. 他跳过篱笆。
She hops on one foot. 她单脚跳。

一般过去时:
The frog hopped into the pond. 青蛙跳进了池塘。
He hopped off the bus quickly. 他迅速跳下公共汽车。

现在完成时:
The children have hopped all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在跳。
She has hopped on the bandwagon. 她已经加入了潮流。


过去完成时:
He had hopped on the train before it left. 火车离开前他已经跳上去了。
They had hopped over the wall before the guard saw them. 他们在警卫看到他们之前翻过了墙。


将来时:
He will hop on the next train. 他将搭乘下一班火车。
She will hop over to see her friend. 她将跳过去看望她的朋友。


虚拟语气:
If I were a kangaroo, I would hop everywhere. 如果我是一只袋鼠，我就会到处跳。
I wish I could hop as high as a grasshopper. 我希望我能像蚱蜢一样跳得高。


被动语态:
The ball was hopped over the net. 球被跳过网。
The fence was hopped over by several people. 篱笆被几个人翻过去了。


疑问句:
Does he hop over the fence? 他跳过篱笆吗？
Does she hop on one foot? 她单脚跳吗？


否定句:
He doesn't hop very high. 他跳不高。
She didn't hop on the train. 她没有跳上火车。
