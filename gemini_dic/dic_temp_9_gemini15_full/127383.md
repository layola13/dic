neanderthals:/niˈændərˌθɔːlz/| n. |尼安德特人|Extinct species of human beings who lived in Europe and western Asia until about 30,000 years ago.  They were characterized by a stocky build, prominent brow ridges, and a receding chin.  Modern humans share a small percentage of their DNA.|例句：Recent studies have shown that Neanderthals possessed sophisticated tools and social structures. 近期研究表明尼安德特人拥有复杂的工具和社会结构。

例句：The discovery of Neanderthal burial sites suggests a belief in an afterlife. 尼安德特人墓地的发现表明他们相信来世。


近义词：early humans, archaic humans|反义词：homo sapiens (modern humans)
