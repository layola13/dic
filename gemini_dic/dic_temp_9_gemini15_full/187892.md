rosters:/ˈrɒstərz/ /ˈroʊstərz/| n.|花名册，人员名单，值班表|A roster is a list of names, especially of people who work in a particular place or do a particular job, or who are members of a team.|The team roster includes several All-Stars.  这个队的花名册包括几位全明星球员。|The hospital keeps a detailed roster of all staff. 医院保存一份所有员工的详细花名册。|schedule, list, register|absence, absence list|名词
