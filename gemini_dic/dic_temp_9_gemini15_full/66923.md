expendables:/ɪkˈspɛndəbəlz/| n.|消耗品；可牺牲的人；消耗物资|Things that are used up or consumed, or people who are considered expendable (easily replaced or sacrificed).  The term often implies a lack of importance or value placed on the item or person.|例句：The army considered the soldiers in that unit expendables.  军队认为那个部队的士兵是可以牺牲的。

例句：Paper, ink, and toner are expendables for a printer. 纸张、墨水和墨粉是打印机的消耗品。


近义词：consumables, disposables, supplies

反义词：essentials, irreplaceables

词性:名词
