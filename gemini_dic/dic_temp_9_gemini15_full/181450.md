rejeopardizes:/ˌriːˈdʒɛpərdaɪzɪz/ | vt. |再次危及；重新使…处于危险境地 | To put something or someone in danger again after a previous period of safety or security.  |例句：The new policy rejeopardizes the fragile peace agreement. 新政策再次危及了脆弱的和平协议。


例句：His reckless driving rejeopardizes not only his own life but also the lives of others. 他的鲁莽驾驶不仅危及他自己的生命，也危及他人的生命。


近义词：re-endanger, imperil again, put at risk again


反义词：protect, safeguard, secure


词性：动词

词源:
