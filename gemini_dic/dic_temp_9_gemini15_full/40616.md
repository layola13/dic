convenes:/kənˈviːnz/| vt.|召集，召开；集合|To call people to a meeting; to assemble.  |The committee convenes once a month.  委员会每月召开一次会议。|The board convenes tomorrow to discuss the merger. 董事会明天召开会议讨论合并事宜。|assemble, summon, gather|disperse, adjourn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee convenes every Tuesday. = 委员会每周二召开会议。
The club convenes at 7pm sharp. = 俱乐部晚上7点准时集合。

一般过去时:
The court convened last week to hear the case. = 法院上周开庭审理此案。
The meeting convened promptly at 9:00 AM. = 会议在上午9点准时召开。

现在完成时:
The council has convened several times this year to discuss the budget. = 理事会今年已召开几次会议讨论预算。
The group has convened to address the urgent issue. = 该团体已集会处理紧急问题。

过去完成时:
The jury had convened before the judge entered the courtroom. = 陪审团在法官进入法庭前已就座。
After the alarm had convened the firefighters, they rushed to the scene. = 警报召集消防员后，他们迅速赶到现场。

将来时:
The parliament will convene next month to debate the new law. = 议会将于下个月召开会议讨论新法律。
The team will convene in the conference room at 2 PM. = 团队将于下午2点在会议室集合。

虚拟语气:
If the committee convened more often, progress would be faster. = 如果委员会更频繁地召开会议，进展会更快。
I wish the board convened more transparently. = 我希望董事会更透明地召开会议。

被动语态:

A special session was convened to discuss the crisis. = 召开了特别会议讨论危机。

疑问句:

否定句:
