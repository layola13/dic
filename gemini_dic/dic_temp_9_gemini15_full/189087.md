sac:/sæk/ | n. | 袋；囊；（植物的）果囊 | A sac is a flexible, membranous pouch or bag, typically containing a fluid or other substance.  It can refer to various anatomical structures or plant parts. | Example: The doctor examined the fluid-filled sac on the patient's knee. 医生检查了病人膝盖上的充满液体的囊肿。 |近义词：pouch, bag, cyst | 反义词：无
