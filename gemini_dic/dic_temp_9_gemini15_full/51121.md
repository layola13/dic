despiser:/dɪˈspaɪzər/| n. |蔑视者，轻视者 |One who despises; one who holds in contempt.  A despiser is someone who feels or expresses a strong feeling of contempt or dislike for someone or something. |例句：He was a despiser of all things modern. 他蔑视一切现代事物。

He is a despiser of hypocrisy. 他是一个厌恶虚伪的人。


近义词：contemner, scorner, contemnor |反义词：admirer, admirer,approver |词性:noun
