glazen:/ˈɡleɪzən/| v.|上釉；使光滑；|To cover (something) with a glaze or glossy coating.  It can also mean to give something a smooth, shiny surface.|例句：The potter glazed the ceramic vase. 陶工给陶瓷花瓶上了釉。

The chef glazed the ham before roasting it. 厨师在烤火腿之前给它上了糖浆。

近义词：coat, varnish, enamel|反义词：roughen, dull|词性:动词
