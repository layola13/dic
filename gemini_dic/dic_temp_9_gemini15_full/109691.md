leukemia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ | n. | 白血病 | A cancer of the blood-forming tissues, hindering the body's ability to fight infection.  It's characterized by an abnormal increase of immature white blood cells in the bone marrow. |  My grandmother passed away after a long battle with leukemia.  我的祖母在与白血病长期斗争后去世了。| lymphoma, myeloma |  health, wellness | noun
