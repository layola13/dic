liquifying: /ˈlɪkwɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 液化；使液化 |  The process of transforming a solid or gas into a liquid.  |例句：The heat is liquifying the ice. 热量正在使冰融化。
The process is liquifying the natural gas. 这个过程正在使天然气液化。|近义词：melting, fusing, liquefying |反义词：solidifying, freezing
