counterstrategist:/ˈkaʊntərstætədʒɪst/| n. |反策略专家，对策专家 |A counterstrategist is an expert in developing strategies to counteract or oppose the strategies of an opponent. They anticipate and respond to the actions of others, aiming to neutralize their advantages and exploit their weaknesses.  |The counterstrategist anticipated the enemy's flanking maneuver and devised a plan to trap them.  反策略专家预料到了敌人的侧翼机动，并设计了一个诱捕他们的计划。|strategist, tactician | (No direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be someone who blindly follows a strategy without considering countermeasures)
