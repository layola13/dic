/ˌmɒnəʊˈspɜːməl/ /ˌmɑːnoʊˈspɜːrməl/ | adj. | 单精细胞的；单精核的 | Having or relating to a single sperm cell or nucleus.  The term is primarily used in botany and describes a type of embryo sac development where only one megaspore develops into a functional embryo sac. | Example:  The *monospermal* embryo sac is characteristic of many angiosperms.  单精细胞胚囊是许多被子植物的特征。  | 近义词:  unisexual (in the context of having one functional gamete) | 反义词: polyspermal (having multiple sperm cells or nuclei) |  词性: adjective
