quartiles:/ˈkwɔːrtaɪlz/| n. |四分位数 |Four values that divide the sorted data set into four equal parts.  The first quartile (Q1) is the median of the lower half of the data, the second quartile (Q2) is the median of the entire data set, and the third quartile (Q3) is the median of the upper half of the data. Quartiles are used in descriptive statistics to summarize the distribution of data and to identify potential outliers.|The quartiles of the test scores were 60, 75, 85, and 95.  测试成绩的四分位数分别为 60、75、85 和 95。
