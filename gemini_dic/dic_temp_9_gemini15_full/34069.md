civilian:/səˈvɪljən/| n. |平民；老百姓|A civilian is a person who is not a member of the armed forces or the police.  Civilians are often contrasted with military personnel or law enforcement officers. They are ordinary people who live in a community and are not involved in military or police work.|Example:  The bomb exploded, injuring several civilians.  炸弹爆炸，炸伤了几名平民。
The soldiers were ordered to protect the civilians.士兵们奉命保护平民。|近义词：non-combatant,  layperson|反义词：military personnel, soldier, police officer|名词
