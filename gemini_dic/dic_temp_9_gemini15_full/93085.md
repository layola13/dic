idroid:/ˈaɪdrɔɪd/| n. |安卓机器人；类人机器人|A fictional humanoid robot, often depicted in science fiction.  The term is a blend of "I" (as in "I robot") and "android." It can also refer to the Android operating system developed by Google.|例句：The idroid in the movie had surprisingly realistic movements. 这部电影中的安卓机器人动作出奇的逼真。|近义词：android, humanoid robot |反义词:organic being, human
