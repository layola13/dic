fritzes:/ˈfrɪtsɪz/ | v. | 胡闹；捣乱；(非正式)笨拙地做某事 |  To act in a foolish or mischievous way; to do something clumsily.  |  例句：He fritzed around with the tools, and ended up breaking the machine. 他笨手笨脚地摆弄工具，结果把机器弄坏了。


例句：Stop fritzing around and get to work! 别再胡闹了，赶紧工作！


近义词：fool around, mess around, monkey around,  muck about


反义词：work diligently, be efficient, be careful


词性：动词
