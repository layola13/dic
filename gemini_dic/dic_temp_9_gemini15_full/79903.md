gnawer:/ˈnɔːwər/| n. |啃咬者，咬啮者 |A gnawer is an animal that gnaws, especially a rodent.  Gnawers have strong incisors designed for chewing hard materials. |Example: Mice are common gnawers that can cause significant damage to homes.  老鼠是常见的啃咬动物，会对房屋造成很大的破坏。The beaver, a large gnawer, uses its teeth to fell trees. 海狸，一种大型的啃咬动物，用牙齿砍倒树木。|近义词：rodent, chewer |反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting smoothness or lack of chewing action could be considered antonyms in context. |名词
