pseudo-Iranian:/ˌsuːdoʊaɪˈreɪniən/| adj.|伪伊朗的，假冒伊朗的 |  Appearing to be Iranian but not genuinely so; falsely claiming Iranian origin or characteristics.  |例句：The rug was marketed as a genuine Persian carpet, but it was actually a pseudo-Iranian imitation. 这块地毯被当作真正的波斯地毯销售，但实际上是伪造的伊朗仿制品。  |近义词：sham, spurious, counterfeit, imitation |反义词：genuine, authentic, real
