progressionally:/prəˈɡreʃənəli/| adv. |逐渐地；逐步地；循序渐进地 | Gradually or in stages.  It describes a process that happens over time, with each step building upon the previous one. |例句：The company's profits increased progressionally throughout the year.  公司的利润在全年逐步增长。

例句：His understanding of the subject matter improved progressionally with each lesson. 他对这个主题的理解随着每一节课的学习而逐步提高。


近义词：gradually, incrementally, progressively, stepwise


反义词：suddenly, abruptly, immediately


词性：副词
