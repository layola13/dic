railingly: /ˈreɪlɪŋli/ | adv. | 怒气冲冲地；激烈地；粗暴地 |  In a railing or ranting manner; angrily and vehemently.  |例句：He spoke railingly about the injustice of the situation. 他愤怒地谈论着这件事的不公正。

例句：She railingly accused him of lying. 她愤怒地指责他撒谎。


近义词：angrily, furiously, vehemently, indignantly, wrathfully

反义词：calmly, peacefully, serenely, gently


词源:  Derived from "rail," meaning to scold or rebuke vehemently, which itself comes from the Old French word "railler," meaning "to mock" or "to jeer."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the verb into an adverb.
