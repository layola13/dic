keyboard:/ˈkiːbɔːrd/ /ˈkiːbord/ | n. |键盘 |A keyboard is an input device used to enter data into a computer or other electronic device.  It typically has keys representing letters, numbers, and other symbols. | Example: I typed my essay on the keyboard. 我在键盘上打出了我的论文。|近义词：keypad, keyset |反义词：None
