rewarrant:/ˌriːˈwɒrənt/ | v. | 再担保；重新授权；再保证 | To warrant again; to give a new warrant or guarantee.  The term is less common and often implies a renewed or strengthened guarantee, rather than a simple repetition. |例句：The bank decided to rewarrant the loan after reviewing the improved financial statements.  银行在审查了改进后的财务报表后，决定再次担保贷款。

例句：The court rewarranted the arrest after new evidence emerged. 法院在出现新的证据后重新签发了逮捕令。


近义词：reassure, guarantee, reaffirm, endorse | 反义词：revoke, cancel, invalidate


词性:动词
