slalomist:/ˈslɑːləmɪst/| n. |回转滑雪者，激流回旋运动员|A slalomist is a person who participates in slalom racing, either skiing or canoeing/kayaking.  They are skilled at navigating a course marked by gates.|例句：The slalomist expertly maneuvered her kayak through the challenging course.  这位激流回旋运动员熟练地操控她的皮艇穿过充满挑战的赛道。

The experienced slalomist skied flawlessly down the mountain, clearing every gate.这位经验丰富的回转滑雪者在山上完美地滑雪，越过每一个旗门。|近义词：skier, kayaker, racer|反义词：spectator, observer
