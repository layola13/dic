palatic:/pælˈætɪk/| adj.|腭的，腭音的|Relating to the palate, the roof of the mouth.  The term is used in phonetics to describe sounds produced with the tongue against the hard palate.|The pronunciation of the /j/ sound in "yes" is palatal.  The /ʃ/ sound in "ship" is a palatal fricative.|近义词：palatal|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms like "velar" (referring to sounds produced at the back of the mouth).
