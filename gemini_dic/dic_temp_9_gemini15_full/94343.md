impingent:/ɪmˈpɪndʒənt/ | adj. | 侵犯的；撞击的；影响的 |  Having an effect or impact; encroaching.  | The impingent sound of the traffic made it hard to concentrate.  交通的喧嚣声让人难以集中精力。| encroaching, intrusive, impacting |  benign, harmless, inconsequential |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noise impinges on my concentration. = 噪音影响我的注意力。
His views impinge on mine. = 他的观点影响我的观点。


一般过去时:
The rock impinged on the ship's hull. = 岩石撞击了船体。
The crisis impinged heavily on the economy. = 危机严重影响了经济。


现在完成时:
The constant demands have impinged on my free time. = 持续不断的需求侵占了我的空闲时间。
Their actions have impinged on our rights. = 他们的行为侵犯了我们的权利。


过去完成时:
The rain had impinged on our picnic plans. = 雨水已经影响了我们的野餐计划。
Before the new law, the company's actions hadn't impinged on the public interest. = 在新法律颁布之前，公司的行为并没有损害公众利益。


将来时:
The new regulations will impinge on small businesses. = 新规将影响小型企业。
The expansion of the airport will impinge on the local wildlife. = 机场的扩张将影响当地野生动物。


虚拟语气:
If the project impinged on their privacy, they would protest. = 如果该项目侵犯了他们的隐私，他们就会抗议。
I wish the noise wouldn't impinge so much on my work. = 我希望噪音不要这么影响我的工作。


被动语态:
Their privacy was impinged upon by the government. = 他们的隐私受到了政府的侵犯。
Our freedom of speech is being impinged upon. = 我们的言论自由正受到侵犯。


疑问句:
Does the new law impinge on our rights? = 新法律是否侵犯了我们的权利？
How does this issue impinge on the overall strategy? = 这个问题如何影响总体战略？


否定句:
The decision did not impinge on their personal lives. = 该决定没有影响他们的个人生活。
The changes will not impinge negatively on the company's performance. = 这些变化不会对公司的业绩产生负面影响。


PHRASE->
impinge on/upon = 影响；侵犯
impinge against = 撞击；冲击
impinge with = 以...撞击
impinge severely = 严重影响
impinge directly = 直接影响
