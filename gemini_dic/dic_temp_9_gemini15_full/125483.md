multiprocessor:/ˌmʌltiˈprɑːsɛsər/| n. |多处理器|A computer system with more than one central processing unit (CPU).  Each CPU can execute instructions independently, allowing for parallel processing and increased computational power. |Example:  Modern servers often utilize multiprocessor architectures to handle heavy workloads.  现代服务器经常使用多处理器架构来处理繁重的工作负载。|近义词：multi-core processor, parallel processor |反义词：uniprocessor
