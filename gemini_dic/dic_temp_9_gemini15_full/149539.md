pageant:/ˈpædʒənt/| n.|盛会；选美大赛；壮丽的景象|A pageant is a public entertainment, typically one with historical or legendary themes, consisting of a procession of people in costume and often with floats.  It can also refer to a beauty contest.|例句：The town's annual summer pageant attracts thousands of visitors. 这个小镇一年一度的夏季盛会吸引了成千上万的游客。

例句：The Miss America pageant is a televised event watched by millions. 美国小姐选美大赛是一场由数百万观众收看的电视节目。|近义词：spectacle, show, celebration|反义词：insignificance, triviality|名词
