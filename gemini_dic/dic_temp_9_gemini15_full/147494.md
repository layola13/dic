overornamenting: /ˌoʊvərˈɔːrnəmentɪŋ/ | v. |过度装饰，装饰过度 | The act of excessively decorating something, often to the point of being gaudy or tasteless.  It implies that the decoration detracts from the object's inherent beauty or functionality. | Example:  The overornamenting of the cake made it difficult to appreciate the delicate flavour of the frosting.  = 蛋糕装饰过度，让人难以品味糖霜的细腻味道。 |近义词：overdecorating, embellishing excessively, overloading with ornament |反义词：underdecorating, simplifying, minimizing
