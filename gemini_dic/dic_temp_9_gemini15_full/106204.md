{know}

/noʊ/ | /nəʊ/ | v. |知道，了解，认识|To have knowledge or information about something; to be acquainted with; to be familiar with.|I know the answer. 我知道答案。| He knows her well. 他很了解她。|learn, understand|ignore, forget, misunderstand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I know him. = 我认识他。
She knows the answer. = 她知道答案。


一般过去时:
I knew him when I was young. = 我年轻的时候认识他。
She knew the answer yesterday. = 她昨天知道答案。


现在完成时:
I have known him for many years. = 我认识他很多年了。
She has known the truth all along. = 她一直都知道真相。


过去完成时:
I had known him before we met. = 在我们见面之前我就认识他了。
She had known the secret for a long time. = 她早就知道这个秘密了。


将来时:
I will know the result tomorrow. = 我明天会知道结果。
She will know the truth soon. = 她很快就会知道真相。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该做什么。


被动语态:
The answer is known to everyone. = 答案众所周知。
The truth will be known eventually. = 真相最终会大白于天下。


疑问句:
Do you know him? = 你认识他吗？
Does she know the answer? = 她知道答案吗？


否定句:
I don't know the answer. = 我不知道答案。
She doesn't know him. = 她不认识他。


PHRASE->
know-how  = 专业知识，技能
know-it-all = 万事通
in the know = 了解内情的人
let me know = 告诉我
you never know = 你永远不知道
