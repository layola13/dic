fattens:/ˈfætənz/| vt.|使肥胖；使丰满；使肥沃|To make or become fat or plump; to enrich or improve the quality of (soil, etc.).|例句：The rich diet fattens the pigs quickly.  富含营养的饮食使猪迅速肥胖。

例句：Regular fertilization fattens the soil and improves crop yields. 定期施肥使土壤肥沃，提高了作物产量。


近义词：enrich, plump up, fatten up|反义词：emaciate, thin out, impoverish|动词
