obligor:/ˈɒblɪɡɔːr/ | /ˈɑːblɪɡɔːr/| n. |债务人，义务人 |A person or entity legally bound to fulfill an obligation, typically to pay money or perform a service.  |Example: The obligor failed to make the mortgage payments on time.  债务人未能按时支付抵押贷款。  |近义词：debtor, guarantor, promisor |反义词：obligee
