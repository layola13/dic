colourants:/ˈkʌlə(r)ənts/ | n. |着色剂，颜料 | Substances used to impart color to other materials.  They are used in a wide variety of applications, from food and cosmetics to paints and plastics. | Example:  Food colourants are subject to strict regulations to ensure safety. 食品着色剂受到严格的规定以确保安全。The artist mixed various colourants to create the desired shade.  艺术家混合了各种着色剂来创造想要的色调。 |近义词：dyes, pigments, colorings |反义词：decolorants (脱色剂)
