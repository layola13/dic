corkscrew:/ˈkɔːrkskruː/| n. |瓶塞钻，螺旋起子|A tool used to remove corks from bottles. It typically has a spiral-shaped metal shaft that is twisted into the cork to pull it out.|例句：I need a corkscrew to open this wine bottle. 我需要一个开瓶器来打开这瓶酒。  He used a corkscrew to remove the cork from the champagne bottle. 他用螺旋起子从香槟酒瓶中拔出瓶塞。|近义词：bottle opener, wine opener|反义词：None
