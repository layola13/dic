drip-fed:/ˈdrɪpˌfed/| adj. | 逐渐提供的，少量多次提供的 | Gradually supplied in small amounts over a period of time.  | Example: The information was drip-fed to the public over several weeks.  信息在几周内逐渐向公众提供。|近义词：gradually supplied, incrementally provided, phased in |反义词：immediately given, all at once


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government drip-feeds information to the press. = 政府逐渐向媒体提供信息。
They drip-feed their employees new training materials. = 他们逐渐向员工提供新的培训材料。

一般过去时:
The company drip-fed its new product launch to the market. = 公司逐渐向市场推出其新产品。
She drip-fed her son small amounts of the medicine. = 她少量多次地给她儿子喂药。

现在完成时:
They have drip-fed the details of the scandal to the media. = 他们已经逐渐向媒体透露了丑闻的细节。
The professor has drip-fed the students information throughout the semester. = 教授整个学期都在逐渐向学生提供信息。

过去完成时:
The media had drip-fed the public with misleading information for months. = 媒体几个月来一直向公众灌输误导性信息。
They had drip-fed the details of the project over several weeks. = 他们在几周内逐渐透露了项目的细节。

将来时:
The company will drip-feed the new software updates to its users. = 公司将逐渐向用户提供新的软件更新。
We will drip-feed the students new concepts throughout the year. = 我们将全年逐渐向学生传授新的概念。

虚拟语气:
If they drip-fed the information more slowly, people would understand better. = 如果他们更缓慢地提供信息，人们就会更好地理解。
I wish they had drip-fed the news to us instead of releasing it all at once. = 我希望他们能把消息逐渐告诉我们，而不是一次性全部公布。

被动语态:
The information was drip-fed to the audience over several months. = 信息在几个月内逐渐提供给观众。
The students were drip-fed new vocabulary throughout the course. = 学生们在整个课程中逐渐学习新的词汇。

疑问句:
Was the information drip-fed to the media or released all at once? = 信息是逐渐提供给媒体还是一次性发布的？
Are they drip-feeding the new policy changes to the public? = 他们是否正在逐渐向公众公布新的政策变化？

否定句:
The company did not drip-feed its product information; it released it all at once. = 公司没有逐渐发布其产品信息；而是全部一次性发布。
They didn't drip-feed the news; they announced it abruptly. = 他们没有逐渐发布新闻；而是突然宣布。
