clear-cuts:/ˈklɪr kʌts/| n.|皆伐，皆伐林地|The practice of cutting down all trees in a specific area.  This is often done for timber harvesting or to prepare land for other uses.  Clear-cuts can have significant environmental impacts.|The logging company performed a clear-cut on the mountainside, leaving behind a barren landscape.|selective logging, selective harvesting|reforestation, afforestation, natural forest|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company clear-cuts large areas of forest each year. = 公司每年皆伐大片森林。
Clear-cuts often lead to soil erosion. = 皆伐通常会导致水土流失。

一般过去时:
They clear-cut the entire hillside last spring. = 他们去年春天将整个山坡都皆伐了。
The forest was clear-cut to make way for a new housing development. = 这片森林被皆伐以腾出空间建造新的住宅区。

现在完成时:
They have clear-cut thousands of acres of forest in the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，他们已经皆伐了数千英亩的森林。
Environmentalists have protested against the clear-cutting of old-growth forests. = 环境保护主义者抗议对原始森林的皆伐。

过去完成时:
Before the logging, the area had never been clear-cut. = 在伐木之前，该地区从未进行过皆伐。
The government had already approved the clear-cutting permits before the public outcry. = 在公众抗议之前，政府已经批准了皆伐许可证。

将来时:
They will clear-cut the remaining sections of the forest next year. = 他们明年将对森林的剩余部分进行皆伐。
The impact of the planned clear-cut will be assessed before the work begins. = 在工作开始之前，将评估计划中的皆伐的影响。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
