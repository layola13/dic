interdebated:/ˌɪntərˈdɛbeɪtɪd/| adj.|  相互辩论的，争论不休的 |  The word "interdebated" describes a situation where a debate or discussion involves multiple parties engaging in reciprocal arguments and counterarguments. It implies a back-and-forth exchange of ideas and opinions, often with no clear resolution.  | The issue of climate change has been intensely interdebated among scientists and policymakers for decades. | contentious, disputed, controversial |  unanimous, agreed-upon | 形容词
