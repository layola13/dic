This can manifest in various forms, including cutting, burning, hitting, or other destructive behaviors. |例句：The therapist helped the patient understand the root causes of her self-mutilation. 治疗师帮助病人理解她自残的根本原因。

例句：His self-mutilation was a desperate attempt to cope with unbearable grief. 他的自残行为是应对难以承受的悲伤的一种绝望尝试。
