cliche:/ˈkliːʃeɪ/| n. |陈词滥调，老生常谈 |A cliché is a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.  It is a stereotyped expression, a hackneyed phrase, or a commonplace idea. |例句：His description of the sunset was a tired cliché.  他的日落描写是老生常谈。

例句：The "happily ever after" ending is a common cliché in fairy tales.  “从此过着幸福的生活”的结局是童话故事中常见的陈词滥调。


近义词：platitude, stereotype, banality, truism, commonplace


反义词：originality, novelty, innovation


词性：名词

词源：法语 cliché，源自印刷术中的“刻板”，指重复使用的印刷版。
