moder:/ˈmɑːdə(r)/| adj.|适度的；温和的；中等的|Moderate means being within reasonable limits; not extreme or excessive. It can describe something that is average, temperate, or restrained.|例句：He advocates a moderate approach to economic reform. 他提倡采取温和的经济改革方法。|例句：The restaurant offers a moderate price range. 这家餐厅的价格适中。|近义词：temperate, mild, reasonable|反义词：extreme, excessive, radical
