overromanticized: /ˌoʊvərroʊˈmæn.tɪ.saɪzd/ | adj. | 过度浪漫化的；理想化的 |  To have presented something in an excessively romantic or idealized way, often to the point of being unrealistic or inaccurate.  |例句：The movie overromanticized the lives of the artists, ignoring their struggles and hardships.  这部电影过度浪漫化了艺术家们的生活，忽略了他们的挣扎和苦难。
The novel's depiction of Victorian England was overromanticized and lacked historical accuracy. 这部小说对维多利亚时代英国的描写过于浪漫化，缺乏历史准确性。|近义词：idealized, sentimentalized, romanticized |反义词：realistic, pragmatic, down-to-earth |形容词
