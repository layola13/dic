reverbing: /rɪˈvɜːrbɪŋ/ | v. | 回响；反复；持续回荡 |  The verb "reverbing" describes the action of sound continuing to be heard after the original sound has stopped, creating an echo-like effect.  It emphasizes the process of the sound's continuation and repetition.  |例句：The singer's voice was reverbing in the large cathedral. 歌手的歌声在大教堂里回荡。

The concert hall's acoustics were amazing; the music was beautifully reverbing.  音乐厅的音响效果很棒；音乐回荡得非常优美。| 近义词：echoing, resonating, reverberating | 反义词：silent, muted

词源:
From the verb "reverberate", ultimately from Latin *re- (back) + *verberare (to strike).  The meaning evolved from the physical striking of something to the repeated reflection of sound waves.
