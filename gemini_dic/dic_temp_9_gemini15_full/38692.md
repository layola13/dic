condenser:/kənˈdensər/| n.|冷凝器；电容器；浓缩器|A condenser is a device used to condense gases into liquids, or to store electrical charge.  It can also refer to a device that concentrates or compresses something.|例句：The condenser in the refrigerator cools the refrigerant.  冰箱里的冷凝器冷却制冷剂。|例句：The condenser in the radio circuit stores electrical energy. 收音机电路中的电容器储存电能。|近义词：capacitor (for electrical use), cooler, liquefier|反义词：evaporator
