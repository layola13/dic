overreacted:/ˌoʊvərɪˈæktɪd/ | adj. |反应过度的，过激的 |  Describes a reaction that is excessively strong or dramatic in response to a situation. It implies that the response was disproportionate to the stimulus. |例句：He overreacted to the minor criticism. 他对小小的批评反应过度了。|例句：She overreacted and started shouting. 她反应过度，开始大喊大叫。|近义词：exaggerated, overblown, excessive |反义词：underreacted, calm, composed |词性:形容词
