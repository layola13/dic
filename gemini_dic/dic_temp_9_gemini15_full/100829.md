introductive:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|介绍性的，导论的 |Serving as an introduction; introductory.  It is often used to describe something that serves as an introduction or explanation before a more detailed discussion.  |例句：The first chapter is introductive in nature, setting the stage for the rest of the book.  第一章具有介绍性，为本书的其余部分奠定了基础。|近义词：introductory, preliminary, prefatory |反义词：conclusive, final
