condolence:/kənˈdoʊləns/|/ˈkɒndlən(t)s/| n. |慰问；吊唁；哀悼 |Expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death.  |例句：Please accept my deepest condolences on the loss of your mother. 请接受我对您母亲去世的深切慰问。|例句：She sent a letter of condolence to the bereaved family. 她给丧家寄去了一封慰问信。|近义词：sympathy, commiseration, empathy |反义词：celebration, rejoicing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
My deepest condolences are with you. = 我向您致以最深切的慰问。
We offer our condolences to the family. = 我们向家属表示慰问。


一般过去时:
I offered my condolences to the grieving widow. = 我向悲痛的寡妇表示慰问。
They expressed their condolences at the funeral. = 他们在葬礼上表达了他们的慰问。


现在完成时:
I have sent my condolences to the family. = 我已经向家属表示慰问了。
Have you expressed your condolences yet? = 你已经表达你的慰问了吗？


过去完成时:
I had already expressed my condolences before the funeral began. = 在葬礼开始之前我已经表达了我的慰问。
They had sent a card of condolences before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经寄来慰问卡了。


将来时:
I will send a letter of condolence tomorrow. = 我明天将寄去一封慰问信。
We will express our condolences at the memorial service. = 我们将在追悼会上表达我们的慰问。


虚拟语气:
If I knew how to express my condolences better, I would. = 如果我知道如何更好地表达我的慰问，我会的。
I wish I could have expressed my condolences more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地表达我的慰问。


被动语态:
Condolences were offered by many people. = 许多人都表达了慰问。
The family received condolences from around the world. = 家属收到了来自世界各地的慰问。


疑问句:
Have you received my condolences yet? = 你收到我的慰问了吗？
What is the proper way to express condolences? = 表达慰问的正确方式是什么？


否定句:
I didn't know how to express my condolences. = 我不知道如何表达我的慰问。
