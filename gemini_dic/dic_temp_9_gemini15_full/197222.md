shareability: /ˌʃɛərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可分享性，易于分享 | The quality or state of being easily shared or spread; the extent to which something can be shared.  It often refers to the ease with which content, information, or experiences can be disseminated across various platforms and networks. | Example: The high shareability of the video led to it going viral.  这个视频的可分享性很高，导致它迅速走红。  The shareability of this article is quite low due to its length and complexity. 这篇文章的可分享性很低，因为它篇幅过长且复杂。 | 近义词: transmissibility, spreadability, communicability | 反义词: unshareability, insularity
