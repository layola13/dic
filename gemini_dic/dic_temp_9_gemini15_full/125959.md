musicological:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒɪkəl/| adj.|音乐学的，音乐理论的| Relating to musicology, the study of music as an academic discipline.  It involves the history, theory, and analysis of music. | Example:  His musicological research focused on the evolution of Baroque counterpoint.  他的音乐学研究集中在巴洛克时期对位法的演变上。|近义词：musical (less academic)|反义词：non-musical

词源:
The word "musicological" is derived from "musicology," which itself combines "music" (from Greek  μουσική, mousikē, meaning "art of the Muses") and "-ology" (from Greek -λογία, -logia, meaning "study of").  Therefore, "musicological" literally means "relating to the study of music."
