recriminalize:/ˌriːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/| vt.|重新定为犯罪|To make something that was previously legal or decriminalized illegal again.  This often involves reinstating criminal penalties for an act or behavior.|例句：The government is considering recriminalizing the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 政府正在考虑将少量持有大麻重新定为犯罪。|例句：After a period of decriminalization, the country decided to recriminalize drug use. 在一段时间的非罪化之后，该国决定将吸毒重新定为犯罪行为。|近义词：re-illegalize, outlaw, prohibit|反义词：decriminalize, legalize|动词
