mutineer:/ˌmjuːtɪˈnɪər/| n. |叛乱者，哗变者|A person who rebels against lawful authority, especially a member of the armed forces who takes part in a mutiny.|例句：The mutineers were imprisoned after their failed attempt to overthrow the captain.|近义词：rebel, insurgent, revolter |反义词：loyalist, follower
