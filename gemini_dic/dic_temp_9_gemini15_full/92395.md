hypodermatic:/ˌhaɪpəʊdərˈmætɪk/| adj.|皮下的，皮下注射的| Relating to or involving the hypodermis (the layer of skin beneath the dermis).  The term is often used in the context of injections administered beneath the skin. | Example:  The nurse administered a hypodermatic injection of insulin. 护士进行了皮下注射胰岛素。|近义词：subcutaneous, intradermal (depending on context)|反义词：epidermal, topical
