over-teaches:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃɪz/ | v. | 过度教学；教得过多 | To teach excessively or beyond the student's comprehension or ability level.  This often leads to student frustration and disengagement.  | The professor over-teaches, overwhelming students with too much information in each lecture. = 教授讲课内容过多，让学生不堪重负。  The teacher over-teaches the basic concepts, confusing the students instead of clarifying them. = 老师对基本概念讲解过多，反而让学生更加困惑。 | under-teaches |  (no direct antonym, but  "under-teaches" represents the opposite end of the spectrum) | 动词
