foam:/foʊm/ |/fəʊm/| n. v.|泡沫；泡沫塑料；起泡沫|A mass of small bubbles formed on the surface of a liquid.|The sea foam was white and frothy. 海上的泡沫又白又蓬松。The soap formed a thick foam. 肥皂形成了厚厚的泡沫。|froth, lather|solid, liquid|n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sea foams on the shore. = 海水在岸边起泡。
The soap foams well. = 这肥皂泡沫丰富。


一般过去时:
The beer foamed over the glass. =啤酒溢出了杯子。
The waves foamed against the rocks. = 海浪拍打着岩石，激起泡沫。


现在完成时:
The detergent has foamed up nicely. = 洗涤剂已经产生了很好的泡沫。
The river has foamed with pollution. = 河水因污染而泛起泡沫。


过去完成时:
The champagne had foamed before we could taste it. = 香槟酒在我们品尝之前就已经起泡了。
The machine had foamed the milk before adding the coffee. = 机器在加入咖啡之前已经把牛奶打成泡沫了。


将来时:
The beer will foam when you open it. = 你打开啤酒后，它就会起泡。
The detergent will foam better in hot water. = 洗涤剂在热水里会起更多泡沫。


虚拟语气:
If I added more soap, it would foam better. = 如果我多加点肥皂，泡沫会更多。
I wish the cappuccino would foam more. = 我希望卡布奇诺咖啡的泡沫更多一些。


被动语态:
The milk was foamed for the latte. = 牛奶被打成泡沫做拿铁咖啡。
The surface of the liquid was covered in foam. = 液体表面覆盖着泡沫。


疑问句:
Does this soap foam well? = 这肥皂泡沫丰富吗？
Does the beer foam easily? = 这啤酒容易起泡吗？


否定句:
The soap didn't foam at all. = 肥皂根本没起泡。
The mixture didn't foam as expected. = 混合物并没有像预期那样起泡。
