religion:/rɪˈlɪdʒən/ | n. |宗教，信仰| The belief in and worship of a superhuman controlling power, especially a personal God or gods.  Religion often involves a system of faith and worship. |例句：Many religions emphasize the importance of compassion.  许多宗教都强调慈悲的重要性。|例句：Her religion prohibits the consumption of alcohol. 她的宗教禁止饮酒。|近义词：faith, creed, belief, worship |反义词：atheism, secularism
