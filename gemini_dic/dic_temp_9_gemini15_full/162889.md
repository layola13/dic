precensure: /ˈpriːsɛnʃər/ | n. | 预先审查；预先批判 | The act of censoring something before it is published or released;  preemptive criticism or judgment.  | Example: The precensure of the book prevented its publication.  这本书的预先审查阻止了它的出版。 |近义词: pre-censorship, prior review, pre-judgment | 反义词: post-publication review, uncensored release | 名词
