maximizes:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪz/| v. |使最大化；最大限度地提高|To increase or make something as large or great as possible.|The company maximizes profits by cutting costs.|近义词：optimize, amplify, enhance|反义词：minimize, reduce, lessen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maximizes his productivity by working efficiently. = 他通过高效工作来最大限度地提高生产力。
The software maximizes the use of available resources. = 该软件最大限度地利用可用资源。


一般过去时:
She maximized her chances of success by studying hard. = 她通过努力学习来最大限度地提高了成功的几率。
The engineers maximized the engine's power output. = 工程师们最大限度地提高了发动机的功率输出。


现在完成时:
They have maximized their profits this quarter. = 他们本季度已经最大限度地提高了利润。
We have maximized the efficiency of the system. = 我们已经最大限度地提高了系统的效率。


过去完成时:
He had maximized his savings before retirement. = 他在退休前已经最大限度地储蓄了。
The company had maximized its market share before the new competitor entered. = 在新的竞争对手进入市场之前，该公司已经最大限度地提高了市场份额。


将来时:
We will maximize our investment returns by diversifying our portfolio. = 我们将通过多元化投资组合来最大限度地提高投资回报。
The project aims to maximize the impact of the aid. = 该项目旨在最大限度地提高援助的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would maximize our research efforts. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会最大限度地提高我们的研究工作。
I wish I could maximize my potential. = 我希望我能最大限度地发挥我的潜力。


被动语态:
Profits are maximized through efficient management. = 通过高效的管理来最大限度地提高利润。
Resources are maximized to ensure optimal performance. = 最大限度地利用资源以确保最佳性能。


疑问句:
How can we maximize our chances of winning? = 我们如何才能最大限度地提高获胜的机会？
Does this strategy maximize the company's value? = 这种策略是否最大限度地提高了公司的价值？


否定句:
The current system does not maximize efficiency. = 当前系统并没有最大限度地提高效率。
They failed to maximize their potential. = 他们未能最大限度地发挥自己的潜力。
