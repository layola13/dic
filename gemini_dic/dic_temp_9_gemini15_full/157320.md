pioneer:/paɪəˈnɪr/ | n., v. |先驱；开拓者；倡导者；开辟；倡导 |A pioneer is a person who is among the first to explore or settle a new area or to introduce new ideas, methods, or technologies.  |例句：Marie Curie was a pioneer in the field of radioactivity.居里夫人是放射性研究领域的先驱。


例句：He pioneered a new technique in heart surgery. 他开创了一种新的心脏手术技术。


近义词：leader, innovator, forerunner, trailblazer |反义词：follower, laggard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pioneers new methods in education. 他在教育领域开拓新方法。
She pioneers new ideas in sustainable living. 她倡导可持续生活的新理念。


一般过去时:
They pioneered the settlement of the West.  他们开拓了西部的定居点。
She pioneered the use of computers in classrooms. 她率先在课堂上使用电脑。


现在完成时:
Scientists have pioneered many life-saving treatments. 科学家们已经开创了许多挽救生命的疗法。
She has pioneered a new approach to conflict resolution. 她开创了一种解决冲突的新方法。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the internet, many people had pioneered the use of radio communication. 在互联网发明之前，许多人已经开创了无线电通信的使用。
They had pioneered a new system of irrigation before the drought hit. 干旱来临之前，他们已经开创了一种新的灌溉系统。


将来时:
Researchers will pioneer new ways to generate clean energy. 研究人员将开拓产生清洁能源的新途径。
We hope to pioneer a new era of peace and cooperation. 我们希望开创一个和平与合作的新时代。


虚拟语气:
If only they had pioneered this technology sooner.  要是他们早点开创这项技术就好了。
I wish I had pioneered a better solution to this problem. 我希望我找到一个更好的解决这个问题的方法。


被动语态:
New methods of farming were pioneered in the 19th century. 19 世纪开创了新的农业方法。
This revolutionary treatment is being pioneered by a team of doctors. 这种革命性的治疗方法正由一个医生团队开创。


疑问句:
Who pioneered this groundbreaking research? 谁开创了这项突破性的研究？
What company pioneered the use of this technology? 哪家公司率先使用了这项技术？


否定句:
They did not pioneer the use of that material. 他们并没有率先使用那种材料。
The company failed to pioneer any new products this year.  这家公司今年未能开创任何新产品。
