self-admired:/ˌsɛlf ədˈmaɪrd/| adj. |自负的，自我陶醉的|Self-admired describes someone who has an excessively high opinion of themselves and their abilities, often to the point of arrogance.  They are often preoccupied with their own perceived merits and accomplishments.|例句：He was a self-admired artist, constantly boasting about his talent.|近义词：narcissistic, conceited, vain, egotistical|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming
