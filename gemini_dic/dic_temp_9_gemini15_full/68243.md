fairylands:/ˈfɛəriˌlændz/| n. |仙境，梦幻之地 |A fairyland is a place of great beauty and enchantment, often imagined or described as being inhabited by fairies.  It evokes a sense of wonder and magic, typically characterized by idyllic landscapes and fantastical elements.  |例句：The garden was a fairyland of colorful flowers and singing birds.  花园是一个充满色彩鲜艳的花朵和鸟鸣的仙境。

The children's imaginations transformed the woods into a fairyland of mythical creatures and hidden treasures.孩子们丰富的想象力将树林变成了一个充满神话生物和隐藏宝藏的仙境。|近义词：wonderland, paradise, enchanted realm |反义词：nightmare, wasteland
