mousseline:/ˌmuːzˈliːn/| n. |薄纱；慕斯；轻柔的织物|A very fine, lightweight fabric, often made of silk or cotton.  It's also used to describe a light and airy dessert, typically made with whipped cream or egg whites.|The dress was made of delicate mousseline.  这件裙子是用轻薄的薄纱做的。|The chocolate mousseline was light and fluffy. 这款巧克力慕斯轻盈蓬松。| chiffon, voile | heavy fabric | noun
