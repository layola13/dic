reendorsement:/ˌriːɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 再背书；再次认可 | The act of endorsing something again; a second endorsement.  This often refers to a financial instrument like a check or bill of exchange being endorsed a second time, transferring ownership or liability.  It can also refer to a renewed approval or support. | Example: The reendorsement of the contract extended its validity for another year. = 合同的再次背书将其有效期延长了一年。 The bank refused the check due to the questionable reendorsement. = 由于背书可疑，银行拒收支票。 | 近义词：reconfirmation, reaffirmation, renewal | 反义词：rejection, disapproval


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reendorses the check every month. = 他每月都再次背书支票。
The company reendorses its commitment to sustainability. = 公司再次承诺致力于可持续发展。


一般过去时:
They reendorsed the agreement after reviewing the terms. = 他们审查条款后再次认可了协议。
She reendorsed the bill of lading to the new consignee. = 她将提单再次背书给新的收货人。


现在完成时:
The bank has reendorsed the loan application. = 银行已经再次背书了贷款申请。
He has reendorsed his support for the candidate. = 他再次表示支持该候选人。


过去完成时:
Before the final approval, they had reendorsed the project proposal. = 在最终批准之前，他们已经再次背书了项目提案。
The court had reendorsed the previous ruling. = 法院已经再次认可了之前的裁决。


将来时:
We will reendorse the policy next year. = 我们明年将再次认可该政策。
They will reendorse the candidate at the upcoming convention. = 他们将在即将召开的会议上再次认可该候选人。


虚拟语气:
If they reendorsed the contract, we would continue the project. = 如果他们再次背书合同，我们将继续该项目。
I wish the board had reendorsed my proposal. = 我希望董事会再次认可我的提议。


被动语态:
The document was reendorsed by the CEO. = 文件由首席执行官再次背书。
The application has been reendorsed by the committee. = 申请已被委员会再次认可。


疑问句:
Has the check been reendorsed? = 支票被再次背书了吗？
Will the treaty be reendorsed by all parties? = 所有各方都会再次认可条约吗？


否定句:
The bank did not reendorse the loan. = 银行没有再次背书贷款。
The committee refused to reendorse the resolution. = 委员会拒绝再次认可决议。
