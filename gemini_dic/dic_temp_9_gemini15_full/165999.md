prepayment:/ˈpriːpeɪmənt/| n. |预付，预付金额 |Payment made in advance.  Prepayments are common in various contexts, such as rent, insurance, and subscriptions.  |例句：The tenant made a prepayment of three months' rent.  = 租户预付了三个月的租金。

例句：The company requires a prepayment for large orders. = 公司要求大额订单预付。|近义词：advance payment, upfront payment |反义词：postpayment, arrears


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company accepts prepayments for all services. = 公司接受所有服务的预付款。
He makes prepayments on his mortgage every month. = 他每月预付抵押贷款。

一般过去时:
She made a prepayment for her holiday. = 她为假期预付了款项。
They prepayed their hotel bill before arrival. = 他们到达之前预付了酒店账单。

现在完成时:
We have already made a prepayment on the new house. = 我们已经为新房子预付了款项。
They have prepayed their subscription for the next year. = 他们已经预付了下一年的订阅费用。

过去完成时:
He had prepayed his flight tickets before the price increased. = 价格上涨前，他已经预付了机票。
She had made a prepayment for the concert tickets months ago. = 她几个月前就预付了演唱会门票。


将来时:
They will make a prepayment for the car next week. = 他们下周将为汽车预付定金。
We will prepay our insurance premium to get a discount. = 我们将预付保险费以获得折扣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would make a prepayment on my loan. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会预付我的贷款。
I wish I had made a prepayment for the conference. = 我希望我为会议预付了费用。

被动语态:
Prepayments are required for all international shipments. = 所有国际货运都需要预付。
The full amount will be prepayed by the customer. = 全款将由客户预付。

疑问句:
Is a prepayment required for this service? = 此项服务需要预付款吗？
Does the contract allow for prepayments? = 合同允许预付款吗？

否定句:
The company does not accept prepayments for small orders. = 公司不接受小额订单的预付款。
They did not make a prepayment for the repairs. = 他们没有预付修理费。


PHRASE->
prepayment penalty = 预付罚款

prepayment terms = 预付条款
partial prepayment = 部分预付款
prepayment schedule = 预付计划
