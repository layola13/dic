contagiousness:/kənˈteɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. | 传染性，感染性 | The ability of a disease or condition to spread from one person or organism to another.  It refers to the ease with which an infectious agent is transmitted. | Example: The contagiousness of the flu virus is a major concern during winter months.  流感的传染性在冬季月份是一个主要问题。  The high contagiousness of measles makes it a serious public health threat. 麻疹的高度传染性使其成为严重的公共卫生威胁。 | 近义词：infectivity, transmissibility | 反义词：non-contagiousness, immunity
