gobbling: /ˈɡɑːblɪŋ/ | v. | 狼吞虎咽地吃；发出咕咕声 |  To eat something quickly and noisily, or to make a sound like a turkey. | 例句：He was gobbling down his food. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着食物。  The turkey was gobbling loudly. 那只火鸡大声地咕咕叫着。 | 近义词：devouring, gulping, chomping | 反义词：nibbling, sipping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gobbles his food every day. = 他每天都狼吞虎咽地吃饭。
The turkey gobbles loudly in the morning. = 火鸡早上大声地咕咕叫。

一般过去时:
She gobbled her sandwich in a hurry. = 她匆匆忙忙地吃掉了三明治。
The children gobbled up all the cookies. = 孩子们狼吞虎咽地吃掉了所有的饼干。

现在完成时:
He has gobbled down three burgers already. = 他已经狼吞虎咽地吃了三个汉堡了。
The dog has gobbled up all the bones. = 狗已经把所有的骨头都吃掉了。

过去完成时:
They had gobbled up all the cake before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经把蛋糕吃光了。
The birds had gobbled the seeds before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，鸟儿们已经把种子吃掉了。


将来时:
She will gobble up that entire pizza. = 她会把整个披萨都吃掉。
I think he will gobble the food very quickly. = 我想他会很快地把食物吃掉。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would gobble it down. = 如果我饿了，我会狼吞虎咽地把它吃掉。
I wish I hadn't gobbled that entire pie. = 我真希望我没有把整个馅饼都吃掉。

被动语态:
The food was gobbled up quickly by the guests. = 食物被客人迅速吃掉了。
The cake was gobbled in a matter of minutes. = 蛋糕在几分钟内就被吃掉了。

疑问句:
Is he gobbling his food again? = 他又在狼吞虎咽地吃东西了吗？
Were they gobbling up the snacks? = 他们在狼吞虎咽地吃零食吗？

否定句:
He didn't gobble his food; he ate it slowly and carefully. = 他没有狼吞虎咽地吃东西；他吃得很慢很仔细。
She doesn't usually gobble her meals. = 她通常不吃得很快。
