crisscrossing:/ˈkrɪskrɒsɪŋ/| adj. v. | 交错的；交叉的 |  Describes lines, paths, or patterns that intersect or cross each other repeatedly in a complex way.  |例句：The city streets were a crisscrossing maze of traffic. 城市街道如同一个交通拥挤的交叉迷宫。
The hiker followed a crisscrossing trail through the forest.  徒步旅行者沿着一条蜿蜒穿过森林的小路行走。 |近义词：intersecting, intersecting, interwoven,  |反义词：parallel, straight, unintersected | 形容词，动词
