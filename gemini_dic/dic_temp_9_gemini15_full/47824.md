decategorization:/ˌdiːkætɪɡəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去范畴化；取消类别；去除分类|The process of removing something from a category or classification system;  the blurring of boundaries between categories. This often occurs in linguistics, where words lose their typical grammatical function or semantic meaning, or in cognitive psychology where rigid categories are relaxed. | Example: The decategorization of verbs in informal speech often leads to grammatical errors.  去范畴化在非正式讲话中经常导致语法错误。  The decategorization of traditional gender roles is a significant social change.  对传统性别角色的去范畴化是一项重大的社会变革。|近义词：declassification, de-categorization, generalization |反义词：categorization, classification
