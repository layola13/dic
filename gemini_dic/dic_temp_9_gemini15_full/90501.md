hucksterisms:/ˈhʌkstəˌrɪzəmz/| n. |江湖骗子的说辞；夸大其词的宣传；虚假广告|  Speech or writing that is characterized by exaggeration, deception, and insincerity, often used to promote something of little or no value.  It refers to the deceptive sales tactics and extravagant claims made by hucksters. |The politician's speech was full of hucksterisms, promising unrealistic changes.  这位政治家的演讲充满了江湖骗子的说辞，承诺了不切实际的改变。|近义词：hype, puffery, ballyhoo, sales pitch|反义词：truth, honesty, sincerity
