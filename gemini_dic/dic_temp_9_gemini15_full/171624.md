pulpit:/ˈpʊlpɪt/| n.|讲坛；布道坛|A raised platform or stand used by a preacher or speaker in a church or other place of worship.|The preacher delivered a powerful sermon from the pulpit.  牧师在讲台上发表了一篇强有力的布道。|The minister stood at the pulpit, Bible in hand.  牧师站在讲台上，手里拿着圣经。|lectern, rostrum|stage, floor|名词

The preacher uses the pulpit to deliver sermons. = 牧师用讲坛发表布道。
The speaker stands on the pulpit to address the audience. = 演讲者站在讲台上向观众讲话。
