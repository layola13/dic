nonformulation: /ˌnɒnˌfɔːrmjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 未经配制；未经制定；无药方 | The absence of a formulated or prepared substance or plan.  It refers to something that hasn't been put into a specific form or structure.  This could apply to various contexts, from medicine (lack of a prescription) to policy (absence of a defined plan). | Example: The nonformulation of a clear strategy led to confusion and delays.  未制定清晰的战略导致混乱和延误。 | 近义词：lack of formulation, absence of a plan, unpreparedness | 反义词：formulation, planning, preparation |
