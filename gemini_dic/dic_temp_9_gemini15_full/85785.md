hayrick:/ˈheɪrɪk/| n. |干草堆 |A large, conical pile of hay, typically outdoors.  Hayricks are often seen in rural areas and are used for storing hay for animal feed. They are usually built by hand, and their size and shape can vary depending on the amount of hay being stored and the local traditions.|例句：The farmer carefully built a large hayrick in the field.  这位农民在田里仔细地堆起了一个很大的干草堆。

The children played hide-and-seek amongst the hayricks. 孩子们在干草堆之间玩捉迷藏。|近义词：haystack|反义词：None directly applicable.  Perhaps "empty field" could be considered a contextual antonym.
