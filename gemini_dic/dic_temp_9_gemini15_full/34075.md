civilise: /ˈsɪvəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使文明；教化；开化 | To bring (a person or place) to a state of civilization; to refine or improve the manners or morals of. | 例句：The government invested heavily in education to civilise the population. 政府大力投资教育以使人民文明开化。| 例句： Centuries of contact have civilised some of the more remote tribes.几个世纪的接触使一些偏远的部落文明开化了。| 近义词：cultivate, refine, educate, enlighten | 反义词：barbarize, brutalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They civilise the rural areas through education. = 他们通过教育使农村地区文明起来。
The government civilises the people by promoting cultural activities. = 政府通过推广文化活动来教化人民。


一般过去时:
The Roman Empire civilised much of Europe. = 罗马帝国使欧洲大部分地区文明开化。
Missionaries attempted to civilise indigenous populations. = 传教士试图使当地居民文明开化。


现在完成时:
Education has civilised many societies throughout history. = 教育在历史上使许多社会文明开化。
Modern technology has civilised our way of life. = 现代科技使我们的生活方式更加文明。


过去完成时:
They had civilised the region before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，他们已经使该地区文明开化了。
The ancient Greeks had civilised a large part of the Mediterranean world. = 古希腊人已经使地中海世界的大部分地区文明开化了。


将来时:
We hope to civilise the world through international cooperation. = 我们希望通过国际合作使世界文明开化。
This new project will civilise the underdeveloped areas. = 这个新项目将使欠发达地区文明开化。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could civilise this region faster. = 如果我们有更多的资源，我们可以更快地使这个地区文明开化。
I wish we could civilise the hearts of those who commit violence. = 我希望我们可以教化那些实施暴力的人的心。


被动语态:
The remote villages were civilised by the introduction of modern infrastructure. = 偏远的村庄因引进了现代基础设施而变得文明。
The barbarians were civilised by the influence of the Roman Empire. = 野蛮人受到罗马帝国的影响而变得文明。


疑问句:
How can we civilise the next generation? = 我们如何使下一代文明开化？
Can education alone civilise a society? = 单靠教育就能使一个社会文明开化吗？


否定句:
They failed to civilise the unruly tribe. = 他们未能使那个难以管教的部落文明开化。
The harsh conditions prevented them from civilising the land. = 恶劣的环境阻止了他们使这片土地文明开化。
