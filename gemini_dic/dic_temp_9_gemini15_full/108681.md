leak:/liːk/| n., v.|泄漏；漏洞；泄露|To escape or allow to escape through a crack or hole; a hole or crack that allows something to escape.|The roof is leaking. 屋顶漏水了。 There's a leak in the pipe. 水管漏水了. |drip, seep, ooze,  percolate|plug, mend, repair|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The faucet leaks constantly. = 水龙头不断漏水。
Water leaks from the crack in the wall. = 水从墙上的裂缝里渗出来。

一般过去时:
The boat leaked during the storm. = 船在暴风雨中漏水了。
The news of the merger leaked to the press. = 并购的消息泄露给了媒体。


现在完成时:
The roof has leaked several times this year. = 今年屋顶已经漏过几次水了。
Information has leaked about the company's new product. = 关于公司新产品的资料已经泄露了。


过去完成时:
The pipe had leaked for weeks before we noticed it. = 水管漏了好几周我们才注意到。
The secret had leaked before the official announcement. = 秘密在正式宣布之前就已经泄露了。


将来时:
The dam will leak if the water pressure increases. = 如果水压增加，大坝将会漏水。
I'm afraid the secret will leak sooner or later. = 我担心秘密迟早会泄露。


虚拟语气:
If the pipe leaked, we would have to repair it. = 如果水管漏水，我们就得修理它。
If the news had not leaked, the surprise would have been complete. = 如果消息没有泄露，惊喜就会完整了。


被动语态:
The information was leaked to the competitor. = 信息被泄露给了竞争对手。
The problem was quickly leaked to the public. = 问题很快被泄露给了公众。


疑问句:
Is the roof leaking? = 屋顶漏水了吗？
Where is the leak? = 漏洞在哪里？


否定句:
The boat doesn't leak. = 船不漏水。
The secret hasn't leaked yet. = 秘密还没有泄露。


PHRASE->
memory leak: 内存泄漏
data leak: 数据泄漏
gas leak: 煤气泄漏
oil leak: 油泄漏
brain leak: 人才流失
