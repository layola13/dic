postcrises:/ˈpoʊstˈkraɪsɪz/| n.|危机后时期，危机过后|The period following a crisis, often characterized by recovery, rebuilding, and reflection on the events leading up to and during the crisis.  It can refer to economic, political, social, or environmental crises.|例句：Postcrises analysis is crucial for preventing future catastrophes. 危机后分析对于预防未来灾难至关重要。

例句：The postcrises reconstruction efforts focused on improving infrastructure. 危机后的重建工作侧重于改善基础设施。

反义词：precrisis, crisis period
