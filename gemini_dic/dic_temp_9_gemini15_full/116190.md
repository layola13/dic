mashings:/ˈmæʃɪŋz/| n.|捣碎；捣烂；糊状物 |The process of mashing something, typically food, into a soft pulp.  It can also refer to the resulting pulp itself.  The word is a plural form of "mashing," which is the gerund form of the verb "to mash."|例句：The mashings of potatoes were used to thicken the stew. 土豆泥用来使炖菜变稠。 The fruit mashings were sweet and delicious. 果泥又甜又好吃。|近义词：pureeing, crushing, pulverizing |反义词：solidifying, hardening
