eternalize:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪz/| vt.|使永存；使不朽；使永恒|To make something last forever or for a very long time; to immortalize.|例句：His heroic deeds eternalized his name in history.  他的英勇事迹使他的名字永载史册。

例句：The artist hoped to eternalize the beauty of the landscape in his painting.艺术家希望在他的画作中使这片风景的美丽永存。


近义词：immortalize, perpetuate, immortalize


反义词：destroy, obliterate, annihilate


词性:动词


USAGE->
