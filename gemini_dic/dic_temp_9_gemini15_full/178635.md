recovers:/rɪˈkʌvərz/| v.|恢复，痊愈，重新获得|To return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength after illness, injury, or loss.|例句：He is recovering well from his surgery. 他手术恢复得很好。|例句：The economy is slowly recovering from the recession. 经济正在缓慢地从衰退中复苏。|近义词：regain, recoup, retrieve|反义词：worsen, deteriorate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recovers quickly from illness. = 他很快就能从疾病中恢复过来。
She recovers her composure after a moment. = 她片刻后恢复了镇静。


一般过去时:
He recovered from his injury within a month. = 他在一个月内从伤病中恢复过来。
The company recovered its losses in the following year. = 公司在第二年收回了损失。


现在完成时:
She has recovered her stolen jewelry. = 她已经找回了被盗的珠宝。
He has recovered from the flu. = 他已经从流感中恢复过来了。


过去完成时:
He had recovered his strength before the race. = 他在比赛前已经恢复了体力。
They had recovered from the shock by the time I arrived. = 我到达时，他们已经从震惊中恢复过来了。


将来时:
The doctor hopes the patient will recover soon. = 医生希望病人很快康复。
The economy is expected to recover next year. = 预计经济将在明年复苏。


虚拟语气:
If he had recovered sooner, he could have played in the game. = 如果他早点恢复，他就能参加比赛了。
I wish I had recovered my lost keys. = 我真希望我能找到我丢失的钥匙。


被动语态:
The stolen painting was recovered by the police. = 失窃的画被警方找回。
The lost data was eventually recovered. = 丢失的数据最终被找回。


疑问句:
Has he recovered yet? = 他恢复了吗？
Will the market recover quickly? = 市场会迅速复苏吗？


否定句:
He didn't recover consciousness for hours. = 他几个小时都没恢复知觉。
The company has not yet recovered from the financial crisis. = 公司尚未从金融危机中恢复过来。


PHRASE->
recover from = 从…中恢复
recover oneself = 恢复镇静
recover one's losses = 挽回损失
recover one's health = 恢复健康
recover stolen goods = 追回赃物
