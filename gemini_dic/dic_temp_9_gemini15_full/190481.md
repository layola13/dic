sastra:/ˈʃɑːstrə/ | n. |圣典；经典；学问；文献|Sastra refers to sacred texts, scriptures, or canonical works, particularly within the context of Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism. It can also broadly encompass scholarly works and treatises on various subjects.  The term highlights the authoritative and learned nature of these writings.|例句：The ancient sastras offer profound insights into the nature of reality. 古代的圣典提供了对现实本质的深刻见解。|近义词：scripture, canon, treatise, literature |反义词：fiction, falsehood
