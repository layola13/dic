servile:/ˈsɜːrvaɪl/| adj.|卑躬屈膝的；奴性的；奴颜婢色的|Characterized by or exhibiting a submissive attitude; fawning.  It implies excessive deference and a lack of self-respect. |例句：His servile behavior towards his boss was embarrassing to watch.  他对他老板卑躬屈膝的样子令人尴尬。

例句：The servile attitude of the courtiers was sickening.  朝臣们卑躬屈膝的态度令人作呕。


近义词：submissive, obsequious, fawning, cringing, slavish, subservient

反义词：independent, assertive, defiant, rebellious, proud


词源：源自于古法语 servile，最终源于拉丁语 servus，“奴隶”。  This reflects the historical association of servility with the behavior of slaves.
