deflourer:/ˌdiːˈfluər/| vt.| 除去…的粉；使…失去光泽；玷污；糟蹋 |To remove the flour from (something); to deprive of flour; to spoil or tarnish the beauty or purity of something; to violate or defile (someone, especially a woman).|例句：The baker defloured the dough before adding the other ingredients. 面包师在加入其他配料之前去除了面团中的面粉。

The scandal defloured his reputation.  丑闻玷污了他的名誉。|近义词：disfigure, spoil, taint, violate |反义词：enhance, purify, restore
