incognito:/ɪnˈkɒɡnɪtoʊ/|/ɪnˈkɑːɡnɪtoʊ/| adj. n. adv.|匿名的；隐姓埋名的；|Incognito means disguised or concealed, often to avoid recognition.  It can refer to a person who is not known or identified, or to the act of keeping one's identity secret. |例句：The celebrity attended the event incognito, wearing a disguise.  这位名人在活动上乔装打扮，隐姓埋名地参加。

例句：She travelled incognito to avoid the paparazzi. 她为了躲避狗仔队而隐姓埋名地旅行。


近义词：anonymous, disguised, undercover, concealed


反义词：known, identified, revealed


词性：形容词，名词，副词
