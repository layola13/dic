endemical:/ɛnˈdɛmɪkəl/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的；（疾病）地方流行的 | Relating to or characteristic of a particular place or people.  Often used to describe plants or animals that are found only in a specific geographic region, or diseases that are prevalent in a particular area. | Example:  The koala is an endemic species of Australia.  考拉是澳大利亚的特有物种。  The disease is endemic in this region.  这种疾病在这个地区流行。|近义词：native, indigenous, local |反义词：exotic, foreign, widespread

过去完成时:
