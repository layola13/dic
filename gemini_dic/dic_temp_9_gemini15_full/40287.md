continuer:/kənˈtɪnjuːər/| n. |继续者，延续者 |A continuer is someone or something that continues something.  For example, the continuer of a family business is the person who keeps it running. |例句：He is the continuer of his father's legacy. 他是他父亲遗产的继承人。|例句：The company needs a strong continuer to navigate the economic downturn.  这家公司需要一位强有力的领导者来渡过经济低迷时期。|近义词：successor, follower |反义词：terminator, ender
