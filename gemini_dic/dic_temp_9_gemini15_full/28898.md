carrageen:/ˌkærəˈɡiːn/| n.|卡拉胶|A type of gelatinous substance extracted from certain species of red seaweed, used as a thickening or gelling agent in food and other products.  It's also known as Irish moss. |Example:  The chef used carrageen to thicken the soup.  厨师用卡拉胶来增稠汤。|近义词：agar-agar, Irish moss|反义词：N/A|名词
