dense:/dens/| adj.|密集的；浓密的；愚钝的；难懂的|  The English definition is "having the component parts closely compacted together; crowded closely together."  It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts.  | 例句：The forest was dense with trees and undergrowth. 这片森林树木和灌木丛生，十分茂密。


例句：The text was dense with technical jargon, making it difficult to understand. 这篇文章充满了专业术语，难以理解。


近义词：thick, compact, crowded, opaque, obtuse


反义词：sparse, thin, rarefied, clear, lucid


词性:形容词
