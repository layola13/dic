melt-away: /ˈmɛltəˈweɪ/ | adj. v. | 融化消失的；逐渐消失的 | Gradually disappearing; melting away. | The snow melted away in the spring sunshine. 春日的阳光下，积雪融化消失了。 The crowd melted away after the concert ended. 音乐会结束后，人群渐渐散去。 | 近义词：disappear, fade, vanish | 反义词：appear, materialize, emerge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snow melts away slowly. = 雪慢慢融化消失。
The worries melt away as the day progresses. = 随着一天的过去，忧虑逐渐消散。


一般过去时:
The ice cream melted away quickly in the heat. = 冰激凌在酷暑中很快就融化了。
His anger melted away after he talked to his friend. = 他和朋友谈话后，怒气消了。


现在完成时:
The evidence has melted away over time. = 证据随着时间的推移逐渐消失了。
My anxiety has melted away since I got the good news. = 自从我得到好消息后，我的焦虑就消失了。


过去完成时:
The problem had melted away by the time the meeting finished. = 会议结束时，问题已经解决了。
His fear had melted away once he saw his family. = 一旦看到家人，他的恐惧就消失了。


将来时:
The snow will melt away as the temperature rises. = 温度升高后，雪就会融化。
His doubts will melt away once he sees the results. = 一旦他看到结果，他的疑虑就会消除。


虚拟语气:
If the sun shone brighter, the snow would melt away faster. = 如果阳光更强烈，雪就会融化得更快。
If I had a better job, my stress would melt away. = 如果我有一份更好的工作，我的压力就会消失。


被动语态:

The evidence is being melted away by the passage of time. = 证据正在随着时间的推移而消失。


疑问句:

否定句:
