interpledge:/ˈɪntərˈplɛdʒ/| n. |互保；相互保证；共同承诺 |A mutual pledge or agreement, especially between two or more parties to guarantee something or support each other.  It often involves a shared responsibility or liability.|The two companies entered into an interpledge to share the costs of the project. 这两家公司达成了互保协议，共同承担项目成本。|近义词：mutual guarantee, reciprocal pledge, joint undertaking |反义词：separate undertaking, individual responsibility|名词
