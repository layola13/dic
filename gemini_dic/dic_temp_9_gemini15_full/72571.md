fluviometer:/ˈfluːviɒmɪtər/ | n. |河流流量计|A fluviometer is an instrument used to measure the flow rate of a river or stream.  It typically measures the velocity and cross-sectional area of the water to calculate the discharge.  |例句：The researchers used a fluviometer to monitor the river's flow rate during the flood. 研究人员用河流流量计监测洪水期间河流的流量。

The fluviometer readings showed a significant increase in discharge after the heavy rainfall. 流量计读数显示强降雨后流量显著增加。|近义词：flow meter, current meter |反义词：None.

词源:
The word "fluviometer" is a combination of "fluvio-" (from Latin *fluvius, meaning "river") and "-meter" (from Greek *metron, meaning "measure").  Therefore, it literally means "river measurer".
