nigerianization: /ˌnaɪdʒəriənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 尼日利亚化；使尼日利亚化 | The process of adopting Nigerian customs, practices, or characteristics.  It often refers to the adoption of Nigerian culture, language, or political systems in other contexts. The term can also describe the increasing influence of Nigerian culture globally. | Example: The increasing popularity of Nollywood films is a significant example of Nigerianization in the global film industry.  尼日利亚电影的日益流行是全球电影业尼日利亚化的一个重要例子。 | 近义词:  Africanization (in a broader context), indigenization | 反义词: westernization, globalization (in the sense of homogenization)
