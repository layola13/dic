evaporates:/ɪˈvæpəreɪts/| v.|蒸发；消失；逐渐减少 |To change from a liquid or solid state into a gas.  It can also refer to something gradually disappearing or fading away.| 例句：The water evaporates quickly in the hot sun.  阳光下水很快就蒸发了。

The tension in the room evaporated after the announcement.  公告之后房间里的紧张气氛消失了。

近义词：vaporizes, dries up, disappears | 反义词：condenses, precipitates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water evaporates slowly. = 水慢慢地蒸发。
The smell evaporates quickly in the wind. = 气味在风中迅速消散。

一般过去时:
The puddle evaporated after the rain stopped. = 雨停后水洼蒸发了。
His anger evaporated when he heard the good news. = 他听到好消息后怒气消散了。

现在完成时:
The lake has evaporated considerably this summer. = 今年夏天湖水已经蒸发了不少。
Most of the evidence has evaporated over time. = 大多数证据都随着时间的推移消失了。

过去完成时:
The snow had evaporated by the time we reached the summit. = 我们到达山顶时，雪已经融化了。
All his savings had evaporated before he could retire.  在他退休前，所有的积蓄都花光了。

将来时:
The dew will evaporate as the sun rises. = 太阳升起时，露水会蒸发。
His hopes will evaporate if he fails the exam. = 如果他考试不及格，他的希望就会破灭。

虚拟语气:
If the water evaporated more quickly, we could have finished sooner. = 如果水蒸发得更快一些，我们就能早点完成。
I wish the problem would evaporate as quickly as the water. = 我希望这个问题能像水一样迅速消失。

被动语态:
The water is evaporated by the heat. = 水被热蒸发了。
The evidence was evaporated by the passage of time. = 证据随着时间的推移消失了。

疑问句:
Does the water evaporate quickly in this climate? = 在这种气候下，水蒸发得快吗？
Does the paint evaporate easily? = 油漆容易挥发吗？

否定句:
The water doesn't evaporate easily in cold weather. = 天冷的时候水不容易蒸发。
The evidence didn't evaporate despite attempts to hide it. = 尽管试图隐瞒，但证据并没有消失。


PHRASE->
evaporate away = 完全消失
evaporate into thin air = 消失得无影无踪
rapid evaporation = 快速蒸发
slow evaporation = 缓慢蒸发
evaporation rate = 蒸发速率
