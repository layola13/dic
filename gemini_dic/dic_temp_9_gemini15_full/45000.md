USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What".  It describes *how* something is done, not an action itself.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable.


PHRASE->
There aren't common idiomatic phrases using "crystallographically".  Its use is primarily within scientific and technical writing.
