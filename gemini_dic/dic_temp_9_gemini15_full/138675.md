nugae:/ˈnuːɡeɪ/| n. |琐事；小事；废话 | Trifles; insignificant matters; nonsense.  The word often implies things of little importance or value, sometimes playfully or dismissively. |例句：His complaints were mere nugae. 他的抱怨只不过是些琐事。

例句：Don't bother me with your nugae; I have important matters to attend to. 不要拿你的琐事烦我；我有重要的事情要处理。


近义词：trifles, trivialities, nonsense, silliness, triviata

反义词：important matters, serious issues, significant events


词性:名词
