facelift:/ˈfeɪslɪft/ | n. | 整容手术；改观；翻新 | A facelift is a surgical procedure to improve the appearance of the face by tightening the skin and underlying tissues.  It can also refer to the act of renovating or modernizing something. |例句：She decided to get a facelift to reduce the appearance of wrinkles. 她决定做个拉皮手术来减少皱纹。

例句：The old building underwent a complete facelift. 这座旧建筑进行了彻底的翻新。|近义词：face-lift, rejuvenation, renovation, makeover |反义词：aging, deterioration
