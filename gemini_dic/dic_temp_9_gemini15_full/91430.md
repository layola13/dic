词源:
The word "hygienist" is derived from the word "hygiene," which comes from the Greek word "hygieia" (Ὑγίεια), meaning "health" or "healthiness," personified as the goddess of health.  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or specializes in a particular field.  Therefore, a hygienist is someone who practices hygiene, specifically focusing on promoting and maintaining health.
