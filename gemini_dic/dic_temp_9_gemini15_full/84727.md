handing-over: /ˈhændɪŋ ˈoʊvər/ | v. | 交接；移交；让与 | The act of transferring something, such as power, responsibility, or property, from one person or group to another.  |例句：The handing-over of the company to the new CEO was smooth and efficient. 公司移交给新任首席执行官的过程顺利而高效。

The handing-over ceremony was attended by many dignitaries.  移交仪式有许多显要人物出席。


近义词：transfer, handover, delivery, conveyance, cession


反义词：receiving, taking over, retention


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is handing over the documents to his successor. 他正在将他/她的文件移交给继任者。
They hand over the keys after the inspection. 他们在检查之后交出钥匙。


一般过去时:
She handed over the responsibility to her team. 她把责任移交给了她的团队。
The previous manager handed over all the files. 前任经理移交了所有文件。


现在完成时:
They have handed over the project to the new team. 他们已经将项目移交给新的团队。
He has handed over his resignation letter. 他已经递交了他的辞职信（引申为移交）。


过去完成时:
The old guard had handed over power before the revolution. 旧政权在革命前已经交出了权力。
She had handed over the evidence to the police before leaving the country.  她离开国家之前已经把证据交给了警察。


将来时:
The company will hand over the assets next month. 公司将于下个月移交资产。
He will hand over the reins of the department soon. 他很快就会交出部门的领导权。


虚拟语气:
If he handed over the evidence, the case would be solved. 如果他交出证据，案件就能结案。
I wish he had handed over the project earlier. 我希望他早点把项目移交了。


被动语态:
The documents were handed over to the authorities. 文件已被移交给当局。
The responsibility will be handed over to the new committee. 责任将移交给新的委员会。


疑问句:
When will the property be handed over?  房产什么时候移交？
Has the suspect handed over the stolen goods yet? 嫌疑人是否已经交出了赃物？


否定句:
He did not hand over the report on time. 他没有按时提交报告（引申为移交）。
They refused to hand over the control of the company. 他们拒绝交出公司的控制权。


PHRASE->
