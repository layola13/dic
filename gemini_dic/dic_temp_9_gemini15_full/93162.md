igniter:/ɪɡˈnaɪtər/ | n. |点火器，引燃器 | A device used to ignite something, such as a fuel or explosive.  It can be mechanical, electrical, or chemical. | Example: The igniter in the rocket malfunctioned, causing the launch to be aborted.  点火器在火箭中发生故障，导致发射中止。 The mechanic replaced the faulty igniter in the gas stove. 技工更换了燃气灶中故障的点火器。|近义词：lighter, kindler, spark plug |反义词：extinguisher
