nonthirsty: /ˌnɒnˈθɜːsti/ | adj. | 不渴的 | Not feeling thirsty; not needing to drink.  | 例句：After drinking a large glass of water, I felt nonthirsty. 喝了一大杯水后，我不觉得渴了。

The dog, having just finished a bowl of water, seemed nonthirsty. 那条狗刚喝完一碗水，看起来不渴。| 近义词：quenched, satiated (referring to thirst) | 反义词：thirsty, parched, dehydrated

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.  The passive voice would focus on the thirst being quenched, not the person's state.)

There are no common idiomatic phrases using "nonthirsty."  The word is relatively uncommon and usually replaced with simpler phrasing like "not thirsty."
