reinherited:/ˌriːɪnˈhɛrɪtɪd/| adj.| 重新继承的，再次继承的 |Inherited again; received a second time by inheritance.  |例句：The family reinherited the ancestral estate after a long legal battle.  这个家族在经过漫长的法律诉讼后再次继承了祖传的庄园。|例句：He reinherited his uncle's vast collection of rare books. 他再次继承了他叔叔收藏的大量珍本书籍。|近义词：reacquired, reobtained |反义词：disinherited, forfeited

一般现在时:
The family reinherits the property. = 这个家族继承了这处房产。
This generation reinherits the family business. = 这代人继承了家族企业。

一般过去时:
They reinherited the fortune after a long court case. = 经过漫长的诉讼后，他们再次继承了这笔财产。

现在完成时:
