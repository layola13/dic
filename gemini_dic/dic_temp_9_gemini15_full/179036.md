redemocratize:/ˌriːdɪˈmɒkrətaɪz/|/ˌriːdiˈmɑːkrətaɪz/| vt. |使重新民主化；恢复民主 |To restore democratic principles and practices to (a country or society).  The verb implies a process of reversing an authoritarian or undemocratic regime or system. |例句：The country is attempting to redemocratize after decades of military rule. = 这个国家在经历了数十年的军事统治后，正试图恢复民主。

例句：After the revolution, the people worked tirelessly to redemocratize their nation. = 革命后，人民努力工作以使他们的国家重新民主化。


近义词：democratize, liberalize

反义词：authoritarianize, totalitarize
