nonadaptation:/ˌnɒnˌædəpˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非适应性，不适应 | The lack of adaptation to the environment; a failure to adapt.  It refers to the inability of an organism, system, or process to adjust or modify itself successfully in response to environmental changes or challenges.  This can lead to decreased fitness, reduced performance, or even failure. | Example: The plant's nonadaptation to the arid climate resulted in its death.  这种植物不适应干旱气候导致其死亡。 The company's nonadaptation to the changing market led to its bankruptcy.  公司不适应变化的市场导致其破产。 | 近义词：maladaptation, unsuitability, inadaptability | 反义词：adaptation
