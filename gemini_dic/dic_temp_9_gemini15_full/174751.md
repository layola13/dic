rabbit:/ˈræbɪt/ | n. |兔子 |A small furry mammal with long ears, a short fluffy tail, and strong hind legs for hopping. Rabbits are herbivores and are known for their reproductive ability.  |The rabbit hopped across the field. 这只兔子跳过田野。  The hunter caught a rabbit. 猎人抓到了一只兔子。| hare, bunny | wolf, fox
