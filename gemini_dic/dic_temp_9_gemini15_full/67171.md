expostulator: /ɪkˈspɒstʃʊleɪtər/ | n. | 规劝者；抗议者；强烈反对者 | One who expostulates; one who remonstrates or protests earnestly.  |  The expostulator stood before the crowd, vehemently protesting the new law.  规劝者站在人群面前，强烈抗议这项新法律。 | remonstrator, protester, objector | supporter, approver | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The expostulator argues against the policy. = 规劝者反对这项政策。
He expostulates with his friend about his behavior. = 他规劝朋友改掉他的行为。


一般过去时:
The expostulator argued vehemently against the decision. = 规劝者强烈反对该决定。
She expostulated with her son about his poor grades. = 她因为儿子的坏成绩而规劝他。


现在完成时:
The expostulator has repeatedly voiced his concerns. = 规劝者已多次表达了他的担忧。
She has expostulated with him on numerous occasions. = 她已经多次规劝他。


过去完成时:
The expostulator had already warned them of the consequences. = 规劝者已经预先警告了他们后果。
He had expostulated with her before, but to no avail. = 他以前曾规劝过她，但没有用。


将来时:
The expostulator will continue to fight for justice. = 规劝者将继续为正义而斗争。
She will expostulate with the manager about the unfair treatment. = 她将向经理抗议不公平的待遇。


虚拟语气:
If he were an expostulator, he would voice his objections. = 如果他是规劝者，他会表达他的异议。
I wish the expostulator had been more persuasive. = 我希望规劝者更有说服力。


被动语态:
The policy was expostulated against by many. = 许多人反对这项政策。
His behavior was expostulated with by his parents. = 他的父母规劝了他的行为。


疑问句:
Is he an expostulator? = 他是规劝者吗？
Who are the expostulators in this debate? = 在这场辩论中，规劝者是谁？


否定句:
He is not an expostulator; he simply observes. = 他不是规劝者；他只是观察者。
She did not expostulate with him about his actions. = 她没有因为他的行为而规劝他。



PHRASE->
expostulate with someone = 向某人规劝
expostulate against something = 反对某事
vehement expostulation = 强烈的规劝
earnest expostulation = 恳切的规劝
fruitless expostulation = 无效的规劝

词源:
From Late Latin *expostulator, from expostulare "to demand, require urgently," from ex- "out, from" + postulare "to demand, request."  Ultimately derived from  Latin *posk- "to ask."
