ironed-out: /ˈaɪənd ˈaʊt/ | adj. | 解决的；已消除的 |  Describes a problem or difficulty that has been successfully resolved or dealt with.  |  例句：After hours of negotiation, they finally ironed out their differences.  经过数小时的谈判，他们最终解决了分歧。

例句：The wrinkles in the shirt were ironed out.  衬衫上的褶皱被熨平了。(This example uses a literal meaning, which is less common than the figurative meaning.)


近义词：resolved, settled, solved, smoothed out

反义词： unresolved, unsettled, unsolved, outstanding


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problems are ironed out smoothly. = 问题被顺利解决了。
The details are ironed out before the meeting. = 会议之前细节都已敲定。


一般过去时:
The conflict was ironed out quickly. = 冲突很快得到了解决。
They ironed out the misunderstanding between them. = 他们消除了彼此之间的误解。


现在完成时:
All the issues have been ironed out. = 所有问题都已解决。
The team has ironed out most of the bugs in the software. = 团队已经解决了软件中的大部分错误。


过去完成时:
The major disagreements had been ironed out before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，主要的意见分歧已经得到解决。
They had ironed out the details of the contract before signing. = 他们在签署合同之前已敲定细节。


将来时:
We will iron out the remaining details tomorrow. = 我们明天将解决剩余的细节问题。
The issues will be ironed out during the next meeting. = 这些问题将在下次会议上得到解决。


虚拟语气:
If only we could iron out our differences peacefully. = 如果我们能和平地解决分歧就好了。
I wish the problems had been ironed out earlier. = 我希望问题早点解决。


被动语态:
The misunderstandings were ironed out by the mediator. = 误解由调解人解决了。
The discrepancies will be ironed out by the accounting department. = 差异将由会计部门解决。


疑问句:
Have all the problems been ironed out? = 所有问题都解决了吗？
Were the differences ironed out successfully? = 分歧是否成功解决了？


否定句:
The issues haven't been ironed out yet. = 问题还没有解决。
They couldn't iron out the disagreement. = 他们无法解决分歧。



PHRASE->
iron out the wrinkles = 解决问题
iron out the details = 敲定细节
iron out a disagreement = 解决分歧
iron out a misunderstanding = 消除误解
iron out the kinks = 解决难题


词源:  The phrase comes from the literal meaning of "iron out," referring to the act of using an iron to smooth out wrinkles in fabric.  This sense is then extended metaphorically to problems and difficulties, suggesting that they are being smoothed out and made less complicated.
