ornamental:/ɔːrnəˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 装饰性的；用来装饰的 | Serving to adorn or decorate; decorative.  The word refers to something that is primarily intended for beauty or elegance rather than practical use.  | 例句：The ornamental fountain in the park was a beautiful sight. 公园里装饰性的喷泉景色优美。

The house has many ornamental features, such as carved moldings and stained glass windows. 这栋房子有很多装饰性特点，例如雕刻的线条和彩色玻璃窗。


近义词：decorative, embellishing, ornate,  adornative | 反义词：plain, functional, utilitarian


词源：源自于中古英语 *ornamental,  由动词 *ornament 派生而来，*ornament 最终源自于拉丁语 *ornamentum，意为“装饰；装饰品”。
