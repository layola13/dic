ephemeral:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. |短暂的，转瞬即逝的 |lasting for a very short time|The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。|近义词：transient, fleeting, short-lived |反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flower's beauty is ephemeral. = 这朵花的美丽是短暂的。
His fame is ephemeral. = 他的名声是短暂的。


一般过去时:
The feeling was ephemeral. = 那种感觉是短暂的。
