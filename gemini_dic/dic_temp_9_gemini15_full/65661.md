evasions:/ɪˈveɪʒənz/| n.|逃避；规避；推诿|The act of evading something, especially a responsibility, question, or law.  It often involves deception or trickery to avoid something unpleasant or difficult.|例句：His evasions during the interrogation were obvious. 他在审讯期间的闪烁其词是显而易见的。

例句：The company's evasions of tax laws led to a large fine.  该公司逃税的行为导致了巨额罚款。


近义词：avoidance, evasion, dodging, shirking, sidestepping |反义词：facing, confronting, honesty, openness |词性：名词
