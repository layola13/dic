on-wing:/ˌɒnˈwɪŋ/| adj.| 机翼上的；正在进行中的；飞行中的 | In the process of; underway; in flight.  It refers to something that is happening or being done currently, especially in the context of aircraft or projects. |例句：The on-wing maintenance was completed quickly. 机翼上的维修很快就完成了。 The on-wing repairs to the aircraft delayed the flight. 飞机机翼上的维修延误了航班。|近义词：in progress, underway, airborne, in flight |反义词：completed, finished, grounded
