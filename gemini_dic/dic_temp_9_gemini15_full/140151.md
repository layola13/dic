offencelessly:/əˈfɛnsləsli/ | adv. | 无冒犯地，无伤大雅地 |  Acting or speaking in a way that is not likely to cause offense.  It implies a lack of intention to offend, but doesn't necessarily guarantee the absence of offense. | 例句：He apologized profusely, hoping to explain his actions offencelessly. 他诚恳地道歉，希望能无冒犯地解释他的行为。

He spoke offencelessly, but his words still stung. 他说话很客气，但他的话还是让人难受。


近义词：inoffensively, harmlessly, innocently | 反义词：offensively, insultingly, rudely | 词性:副词
