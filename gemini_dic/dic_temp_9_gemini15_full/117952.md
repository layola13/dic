membranous: /ˈmembreɪnəs/ | adj. | 膜的；膜状的 |  Relating to or resembling a membrane; consisting of or covered with a membrane.  | 例句：The membranous wings of the dragonfly are incredibly delicate.  蜻蜓的膜状翅膀非常精致。

The membranous lining of the lungs facilitates gas exchange.  肺部的膜状内衬有助于气体交换。


近义词：filmlike, filmy, membranaceous | 反义词：thick, solid, rigid


词源:  From Middle English *membranous, from Old French membraneux, from Latin membranaceus, from membrana ("membrane").  Ultimately derived from Greek μεμβράνη (membránē, "skin, membrane").
