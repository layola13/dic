emption:/ɪmˈpʃən/| n. |免除；豁免；特免|The act of exempting or being exempt; freedom from an obligation, liability, or duty.|The company received an exemption from the new tax regulations. 这家公司获得了新的税收规定的豁免。|近义词：exemption, immunity, freedom|反义词：obligation, liability, duty


USAGE->

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
She has benefited from tax exemptions for years. 她多年来一直受益于税收减免。
The government has granted many exemptions to small businesses. 政府已向许多小型企业提供了许多豁免。
