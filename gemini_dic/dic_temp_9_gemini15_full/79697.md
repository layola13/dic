glowworm: /ˈɡloʊwˌwɜːrm/| n. |萤火虫|A small, soft-bodied insect, usually the larva of a beetle, that emits light.  Glowworms are known for their bioluminescence, which they use for attracting mates or prey.|例句：We saw several glowworms flickering in the dark. 我们看到几只萤火虫在黑暗中闪烁。
例句：The children were fascinated by the glowworms in the garden. 孩子们对花园里的萤火虫着迷了。|近义词：firefly (although technically distinct)|反义词:None.
