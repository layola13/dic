foresight:/ˈfɔːrsaɪt/| n. |预见；远见；先见之明 |The ability to predict or anticipate future events or trends and take appropriate action.  Foresight is a valuable asset in business and personal life, allowing one to plan effectively and avoid potential problems. |例句：Her foresight prevented a major crisis.  她的远见避免了一场重大危机。


例句：The company's success is due in part to its foresight in investing in new technologies. 这家公司的成功部分归功于其在投资新技术方面的远见。


近义词：foreknowledge, prescience, prevision, anticipation


反义词：shortsightedness, myopia, improvidence


词性:名词
