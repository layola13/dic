perineorrhaphy:/ˌpɛrɪniˈɔːræfi/| n. |会阴修补术|The surgical repair of a laceration or defect in the perineum.  This procedure is often performed after childbirth or due to trauma.|例句：The doctor recommended perineorrhaphy to repair the tear sustained during delivery.  医生建议进行会阴修补术以修复分娩时造成的撕裂。|近义词：perineoplasty, perineal repair|反义词：N/A
