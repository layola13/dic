infrastructural:/ˌɪnfrəˈstrʌkʃərəl/| adj.|基础设施的；基础结构的|relating to infrastructure.  It describes something that is part of or relates to the basic systems and services that are necessary for a society or organization to function.  This includes things like transportation, communication, power, water, and sanitation systems.|例句：The government is investing heavily in infrastructural improvements. 政府正在大力投资基础设施建设。

例句：Poor infrastructural development hindered economic growth in the region.  该地区基础设施发展不足阻碍了经济增长。


近义词：fundamental, basic, foundational

反义词：superficial, non-essential, secondary

词性：形容词
