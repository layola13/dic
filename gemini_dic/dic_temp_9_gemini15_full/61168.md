elucidating: /ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |阐明的；解释清楚的 | Making something clear; explaining something in a way that is easy to understand. | The professor's elucidating lecture helped the students grasp the complex concept. 教授清晰的讲座帮助学生理解了这个复杂的概念。 | clarifying, explaining, illuminating | confusing, obscuring
