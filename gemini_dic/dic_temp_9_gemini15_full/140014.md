oedematic:/ˌiːdəˈmætɪk/| adj.|水肿的；浮肿的|Affected by or characterized by edema (swelling caused by fluid retention).  The term describes a condition where there is an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the body tissues, leading to swelling.|例句：The patient presented with oedematic legs and ankles.  这位病人出现腿脚浮肿。

The oedematic tissue was a sign of congestive heart failure.  浮肿的组织是充血性心力衰竭的迹象。|近义词：swollen, puffy, edematous|反义词：dry, dehydrated
