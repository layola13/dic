firstperson: /ˈfɜːrstˈpɜːrsn/ | pron. |第一人称|  A grammatical person used to refer to the speaker or writer. It typically includes the pronouns "I," "me," "my," "mine," "we," "us," "our," and "ours".  | Example: I went to the store. 我去了商店。 We are going to the park. 我们要去公园。|近义词：speaker, narrator |反义词：third person, second person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am happy. = 我很快乐。
I eat breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上吃早餐。

一般过去时:
I went to school yesterday. = 我昨天上学了。
I saw a movie last night. = 我昨晚看了一部电影。

现在完成时:
I have finished my homework. = 我已经完成了我的作业。
I have lived here for five years. = 我在这里住了五年了。

过去完成时:
I had already eaten before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经吃过了。
I had never seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我从未见过如此美丽的日落。

将来时:
I will go to the beach tomorrow. = 我明天要去海滩。
I will call you later. = 我稍后会给你打电话。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would apologize. = 如果我是你，我会道歉。
I wish I could fly. = 我希望我会飞。

被动语态:
I was told to wait. = 我被告知要等待。
I am being followed. = 我正在被跟踪。

疑问句:
Am I right? = 我对吗？
What do I need to do? = 我需要做什么？

否定句:
I am not hungry. = 我不饿。
I did not go to the party. = 我没有去参加聚会。


PHRASE->
first-person narrative = 第一视角叙述
first-person singular = 第一单数
first-person plural = 第一复数
in the first person = 以第一人称
from my first-person perspective = 从我的第一人称视角
