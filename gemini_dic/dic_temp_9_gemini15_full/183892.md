resentenced: /ˌriːˈsɛntənst/ | v. | 再判刑，改判 | To sentence (someone) again, typically to a different sentence.  This usually happens after an appeal or a review of the original sentence. | 例句：After his appeal, the convicted murderer was resentenced to life imprisonment.  在提出上诉后，被判犯有谋杀罪的罪犯被改判无期徒刑。

例句：The judge resentenced him to a shorter prison term after considering new evidence.  法官在考虑新的证据后，将他改判为较短的监禁期限。


近义词：rejudge, retry, re-sentence


反义词：N/A (no direct opposite, but the opposite action would be upholding the original sentence)


词性：动词
