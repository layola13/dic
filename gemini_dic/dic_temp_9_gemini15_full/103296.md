jibed:/dʒaɪbd/ | v. | 嘲笑；讽刺；改变方向；(船)改变航向 | To speak to someone in a mocking or sarcastic way; to change direction (of a ship). | 例句：He jibed at her clumsy attempt. 他嘲笑她笨拙的尝试。The boat jibed sharply to avoid the rocks. 船急剧转向以避开岩石。 | 近义词：taunt, mock, sneer, veer | 反义词：praise, compliment
