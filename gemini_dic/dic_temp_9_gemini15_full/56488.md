dome:/doʊm/| n.|圆顶；穹顶|A dome is a rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure.  It's typically hemispherical or semi-spherical in shape.|The Pantheon in Rome is famous for its massive dome. 罗马万神殿以其巨大的圆顶而闻名。|The dome of the stadium was illuminated at night. 体育场的圆顶在晚上被照亮了。|arch, cupola, vault|flat roof|名词
