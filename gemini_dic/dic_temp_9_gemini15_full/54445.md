disinherison: /ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪsən/ | n. | 剥夺继承权；被剥夺继承权 | The act of depriving someone of their inheritance; the state of being deprived of an inheritance.  |  例句：The disinherison of his eldest son caused a family feud.  他的长子被剥夺继承权导致了家族纠纷。

例句：Her will resulted in the disinherison of her estranged daughter. 她的遗嘱导致她疏远的女儿被剥夺继承权。


近义词：disinheritance, deprivation of inheritance

反义词：inheritance, bequest

词性：名词
