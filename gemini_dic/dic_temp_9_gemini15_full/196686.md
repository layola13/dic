severance:/ˈsɛvərəns/ | n. |遣散费；解雇；切断；分离 | Severance refers to the act of separating something from something else, often in a forceful or decisive way.  It commonly refers to the termination of employment and the compensation provided to the employee. |例句：The company offered a generous severance package to employees who were laid off. 公司为被解雇的员工提供了丰厚的遣散费。

例句：The surgeon performed a clean severance of the tumor. 外科医生干净利落地切除了肿瘤。


近义词：termination, dismissal, separation, severance pay, compensation


反义词：retention, continuation, joining, unification


词性：名词
