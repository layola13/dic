lifelessly:/ˈlaɪfləslɪ/| adv.|无生气地，呆滞地，了无生气地|Without life or energy; in a dull, spiritless way.|例句：The flowers lay lifelessly on the ground. 花朵无精打采地躺在地上。

例句：He stared lifelessly ahead, lost in thought. 他呆滞地向前凝视着，陷入沉思。

近义词：dully, spiritlessly, apathetically, listlessly

反义词：vivaciously, energetically, enthusiastically, animatedly

词性: 副词
