mademoiselle:/ˌmædəmwəˈzɛl/| n. |小姐，女士|A French term of address for an unmarried woman.  It is a polite and formal way to address a young woman.  In modern French, it is less commonly used than "Madame" (for married women) or simply the person's name. However, it remains somewhat common in certain formal settings or in older generations.|Example: Mademoiselle, would you care for some coffee?  小姐，您想喝点咖啡吗？|近义词：Miss, young lady |反义词：Madame (for married women)
