half-convinced:/ˌhɑːf kənˈvɪnst/| adj.| 半信半疑的，不太确信的 |Partially convinced; not fully persuaded.  It implies a state of uncertainty, where one leans towards belief but hasn't completely committed. |例句：I'm half-convinced that he's telling the truth, but I need more evidence.  我半信半疑他说的真话，但我需要更多证据。

例句：She was half-convinced that the project would succeed, but a nagging doubt remained. 她不太确信这个项目会成功，但心中仍存疑虑。


近义词：unsure, doubtful, hesitant, dubious, unconvinced


反义词：certain, convinced, sure, persuaded
