rockwork:/ˈrɒkwɜːrk/| n.|石工制品；假山；堆石工程|Artificial rock formations, often used in gardens or landscaping.  It can refer to the work itself or the resulting structure.|例句：The garden featured impressive rockwork, complete with waterfalls and a small pond. 花园里有一处令人印象深刻的假山，配有瀑布和一个小池塘。|例句：The construction crew carefully placed each stone to create a convincing rockwork structure. 建筑队小心地放置每一块石头，以创造一个逼真的假山结构。|近义词：stonework, rockery, artificial rock|反义词：plain earth, flat ground
