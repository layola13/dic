priggishly:/ˈprɪɡɪʃli/| adv.| 伪善地；自以为是地；假道学地 | In a priggish manner; affectedly or ostentatiously moral.  |例句：He priggishly corrected her grammar. 他假道学地纠正了她的语法。

例句：She priggishly refused to join in the fun. 她假正经地拒绝加入到娱乐中。


近义词：self-righteously, sanctimoniously, smugly, holily


反义词：unpretentiously, humbly, modestly


词性: 副词
