poikilothermic:/ˌpɔɪkɪləˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.| 变温的；冷血的 |  Pertaining to organisms whose body temperature varies with the environmental temperature.  Poikilothermic animals, unlike homeothermic animals, cannot regulate their internal body temperature and rely on external sources of heat to maintain their body temperature.  |例句：Lizards are poikilothermic animals and their body temperature fluctuates depending on the ambient temperature.  蜥蜴是变温动物，它们的体温会根据环境温度而波动。

例句：Many fish are poikilothermic, meaning their internal temperature changes with the water temperature. 许多鱼类是变温动物，这意味着它们的内部温度会随着水温的变化而变化。


近义词：cold-blooded, ectothermic | 反义词：homeothermic, warm-blooded


词源:  From Greek *poikilos* ("varied, diverse, mottled") + *thermos* ("heat").  The term reflects the variation in body temperature in poikilothermic organisms.
