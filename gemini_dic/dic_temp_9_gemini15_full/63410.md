entrammel: /ɪnˈtræm(ə)l/ | vt. | 束缚；限制；阻碍 | To catch or entangle in or as if in a net; to hamper or impede the action or progress of.  |例句：The red tape entrammled the project.  繁文缛节阻碍了这个项目。

The vines entrammled the old house. 爬山虎缠绕着老房子。

近义词：restrict, hamper, impede, hinder, ensnare, shackle | 反义词：liberate, free, release, unfetter | 动词


词源:

Old English *in-tramlian, from *tram, "beam, bar," + -lian, a suffix forming verbs.  The original meaning referred to something being caught in a beam or bar, evolving to a more general sense of being trapped or entangled.  The word's usage declined significantly after the Middle Ages, experiencing a revival in the 19th century, primarily in literary contexts.
