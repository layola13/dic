overbeat:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːt/ | vt. |过度敲打；过分强调；(音乐)重音过强|To strike or beat something too hard or too often; to emphasize excessively;  in music, to accentuate a note or beat too strongly.|例句：He overbeat the eggs, making the cake too runny. 他把鸡蛋打过了，导致蛋糕太稀了。|例句：The politician overbeat the issue of immigration in his speech.  这位政治家在他的演讲中过分强调了移民问题。|近义词：overemphasize, overdo, overstress |反义词：underemphasize, understate
