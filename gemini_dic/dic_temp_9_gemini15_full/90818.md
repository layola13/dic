humuses:/ˈhjuːməsɪz/| n. |腐殖质（复数）|A dark brown or black organic substance formed by the decomposition of vegetable or animal matter and essential to the fertility of soil.  It is the product of the decomposition of organic matter in soil. |The rich humuses of the rainforest support a vibrant ecosystem.  热带雨林丰富的腐殖质支持着充满活力的生态系统。The farmer added compost to increase the humuses in the soil.  农民添加堆肥以增加土壤中的腐殖质。|compost, organic matter|sandy soil, barren land|名词
