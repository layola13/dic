overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsɛs/| vt.|过度加工，处理过度|To process something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative consequences.  This can refer to data, information, materials, or even ideas.|例句：The factory overprocessed the timber, resulting in significant waste.  工厂过度加工木材，导致大量浪费。

例句：Don't overprocess the images; natural imperfections add character. 不要过度处理图像；自然的瑕疵增添了特色。


近义词：overwork, overrefine, overmanipulate, excessively process


反义词：underprocess, simplify, streamline


词性：动词
