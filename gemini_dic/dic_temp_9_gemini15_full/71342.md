flabellum:/fləˈbɛləm/| n. |蒲扇；扇子|A flabellum is a large, often ornate fan, typically made of feathers or other light materials, used in religious ceremonies or for cooling.  It is often associated with liturgical practices, particularly in the Catholic Church.|Example: The priest used a flabellum to cool himself during the long ceremony.  主教在冗长的仪式中用蒲扇给自己降温。|近义词：fan, whisk|反义词：N/A
