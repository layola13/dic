misinstructing:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌktɪŋ/ | v. |错误指导，误导 |Giving someone incorrect or misleading instructions.  |例句：The manager was accused of misinstructing his employees, leading to significant errors in the project.  经理被指控错误指导其员工，导致项目出现重大错误。|近义词：misguiding, misleading, confusing |反义词：instructing correctly, guiding properly |动词
