sharking:/ˈʃɑːrkɪŋ/ | v. | 欺诈；敲诈；贪婪地索取 | The act of aggressively or unfairly exploiting a situation or person for personal gain, often involving deception or intimidation.  | Example:  He was accused of sharking investors out of their savings. 他被指控诈骗投资者，骗取他们的积蓄。  | 近义词：exploiting, swindling, defrauding, gouging | 反义词：helping, assisting, benefiting
