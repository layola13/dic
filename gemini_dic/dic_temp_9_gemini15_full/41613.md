corradiate:/kəˈreɪdieɪt/| v.|发光，辐射；焕发|To shine or emit rays of light; to radiate.  The word suggests a bright, intense, and perhaps even dazzling emission of light.|例句：The newly polished chrome corradiated under the spotlights.  镀铬的表面在聚光灯下闪闪发光。

例句：The sunset corradiated warmth and beauty across the landscape.  日落的光芒在风景中散发出温暖和美丽。


近义词：radiate, shine, gleam, glow|反义词：obscure, dim, dull

词性:动词
