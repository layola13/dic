repleader:/riːˈpliːdər/| n. |再诉; 补充答辩|A repleader is a pleading filed by a party to an action after a previous pleading has been deemed insufficient or defective.  It allows the party to correct the deficiencies and present their case more effectively. This is a legal term primarily used in common law jurisdictions. |Example: The judge ordered a repleader because the original complaint lacked sufficient detail.  法官命令重新答辩，因为原告状缺乏足够的细节。|近义词：amended pleading, supplemental pleading|反义词：final judgment, dismissal
