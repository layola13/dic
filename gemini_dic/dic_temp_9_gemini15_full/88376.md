hira:/ˈhɪrə/ | n. | 平假名 | Hiragana is a Japanese syllabary, one of the two phonetic scripts used in modern Japanese writing. It is characterized by its cursive style and is primarily used for writing grammatical particles, native Japanese words, and sometimes loanwords.  |  Japanese is written using a combination of hiragana, katakana, and kanji.  =  日语书写使用平假名、片假名和汉字的组合。| 近义词:kana (though broader)| 反义词:katakana, kanji | 名词
