one-woman: /ˌwʌnˈwʊmən/ | adj. | 独身女子经营的；由一个女人完成的 |  Describes a business, project, or performance that is run or completed by a single woman.  It emphasizes the solo nature of the undertaking. | 例句：She runs a successful one-woman law firm. 她经营着一家成功的独身女子律师事务所。


例句：The play was a one-woman show, captivating the audience with its powerful performance. 这部剧是一人独角戏，其精彩的表演迷住了观众。

近义词：solo, single-handed, independent

反义词：collaborative, team-based


词性：形容词
