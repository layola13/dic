metastasize:/məˈtæstəsaɪz/ | v. |转移(癌细胞);扩散|To spread to other sites in the body, especially of a cancerous tumor.|The cancer had metastasized to his lungs.  癌症已经转移到他的肺部。|spread, disseminate|localized, contained|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cancer metastasizes rapidly in some patients. = 在一些病人身上，癌症扩散得很快。
The tumor metastasizes through the bloodstream. = 肿瘤通过血液传播。


一般过去时:
The cancer metastasized quickly after the initial diagnosis. = 癌症在初步诊断后迅速转移。
The doctor found that the tumor had metastasized to the liver. = 医生发现肿瘤已经转移到肝脏。


现在完成时:
The cancer has already metastasized to other organs. = 癌症已经转移到其他器官。
Unfortunately, the disease has metastasized beyond treatment. = 不幸的是，疾病已经扩散到无法治疗的地步。


过去完成时:
By the time she was diagnosed, the cancer had already metastasized. = 当她被诊断出来时，癌症已经转移了。
The doctors realized the tumor had metastasized before the surgery. = 医生意识到肿瘤在手术前就已经转移了。


将来时:
The doctors fear the cancer will metastasize to the brain. = 医生担心癌症会转移到大脑。
Without treatment, the disease is likely to metastasize. = 没有治疗，疾病很可能会扩散。


虚拟语气:
If the cancer metastasized, the prognosis would be much worse. = 如果癌症转移，预后会更糟。
I wish the tumor hadn't metastasized so quickly. = 我真希望肿瘤没有这么快转移。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
