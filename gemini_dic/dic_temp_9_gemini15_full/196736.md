sexagenarian:/ˌsɛksədʒɪˈneəriən/| n. |六十岁的人|A person who is between the ages of 60 and 69.  This term refers to a person in their sixties.  It is often used in a more formal or descriptive context.|He is a vigorous sexagenarian who still enjoys hiking in the mountains. 他是一位精力充沛的六十多岁的人，仍然喜欢在山上远足。|octogenarian, septuagenarian|infant, toddler
