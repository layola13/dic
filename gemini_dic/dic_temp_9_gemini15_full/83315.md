gynophore:/ˈdʒaɪnəfɔːr/| n. |雌蕊柄；子房柄|A gynophore is a stalk-like extension of the receptacle of a flower, below the ovary, that elevates the ovary above the point of attachment of other floral parts.  It is an internode between the receptacle and the ovary.  Essentially, it's a structure that raises the ovary. |The Passiflora flower exhibits a prominent gynophore.|近义词：ovary stalk (less precise)|反义词：无|名词
