discoveries:/dɪˈskʌvəriːz/| n.|发现；发明|The plural form of "discovery," referring to multiple instances of finding something new or previously unknown.  It can refer to scientific breakthroughs, geographical explorations, or uncovering hidden truths.|例句：His discoveries revolutionized the field of medicine.他的发现彻底改变了医学领域。

例句：The archaeologists made several important discoveries at the dig site.考古学家在发掘现场取得了一些重要的发现。


近义词：findings, inventions, breakthroughs, revelations|反义词：losses, failures|词性:n.
