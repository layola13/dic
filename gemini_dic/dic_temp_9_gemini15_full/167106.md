presurvey:/ˈpriːˌsɜːrveɪ/| n. |预调查，前期调查|A preliminary survey conducted before a larger or more detailed study.  It often involves gathering preliminary data or information to inform the design and scope of the main survey.|例句：The presurvey helped us refine our questionnaire and target our sample population more effectively. 预调查帮助我们改进问卷并更有效地确定目标样本群体。

例句：We conducted a presurvey to assess the feasibility of the main study. 我们进行了一次预调查以评估主要研究的可行性。


近义词：pilot study, preliminary investigation, reconnaissance survey |反义词：post-survey, follow-up survey |词性:noun
