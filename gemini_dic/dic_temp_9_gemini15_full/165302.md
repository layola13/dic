preinterpret:/ˌpriːɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/| vt.|预先解释；预先阐释|To interpret (something) beforehand; to give a preliminary interpretation to.|The professor preinterpreted the complex data before presenting it to the class. 教授在向课堂展示复杂数据之前对其进行了预先解释。|近义词：pre-explain, anticipate, foreshadow|反义词：misinterpret, misunderstand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preinterprets the poem's meaning before teaching the class. 他在给班级上课之前预先解释了这首诗的意思。
She preinterprets the research findings to prepare for the presentation. 她预先解释了研究结果，为演示做准备。


一般过去时:
The critic preinterpreted the author's intentions before writing the review. 批评家在撰写评论之前预先解释了作者的意图。
They preinterpreted the ambiguous message to avoid confusion. 他们预先解释了含糊不清的信息，以避免混淆。


现在完成时:
She has preinterpreted the data and is ready to present her findings. 她已经预先解释了数据，并准备展示她的发现。

过去完成时:
