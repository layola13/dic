endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/ | n. |捐赠；天赋；基金；捐款 |A gift of money or property to a person or organization.  The natural talent or ability of a person. A fund of money that is given to an organization or institution to provide an income. |例句：The university received a large endowment from a wealthy alumnus.  大学从一位富有的校友那里收到了一笔巨额捐款。

The singer possesses a remarkable endowment of vocal talent.这位歌手拥有非凡的歌唱天赋。


近义词：donation, gift, grant, talent, aptitude | 反义词：deprivation, lack
