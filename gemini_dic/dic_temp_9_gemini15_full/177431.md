rebaptisms:/ˌriːˈbæptɪzəm/| n.|再洗礼，重新施洗|The act of baptizing someone again, often because of doubts about the validity of a previous baptism.  This practice is common in some religious denominations.|例句：The rebaptisms were performed by the dissenting minister. = 再洗礼是由持异议的牧师执行的。

例句：Some denominations require rebaptisms if a baptism was performed by an unauthorized person. = 一些教派要求如果洗礼是由未经授权的人员执行的，则需要重新洗礼。


近义词：re-baptism, second baptism|反义词：first baptism, initial baptism|名词
