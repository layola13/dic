lok:/lɒk/| n. v.|锁，锁住；固定；瞄准|a fastening device that is used to secure a door, lid, etc.  A lock is also the act of fastening something with a lock.  The verb "to lock" means to fasten something with a lock or to become fixed in a certain position. |例句：He locked the door before leaving. 他离开前锁上了门。|近义词：fasten, secure, latch |反义词：unlock, open


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He locks the door every night. 他每晚都锁门。
She locks her bike to the railing. 她把自行车锁在栏杆上。


一般过去时:
I locked myself out of my apartment. 我把自己锁在了公寓外面。
They locked the gate after dark. 天黑后他们锁上了大门。


现在完成时:
I have locked the car. 我已经锁好车了。
She has locked away all her valuables. 她已经把所有贵重物品锁起来了。


过去完成时:
I had locked the door before the earthquake hit. 地震来临之前我已经锁上门了。
They had locked the safe before leaving for the weekend. 他们周末离开前已经锁好了保险箱。


将来时:
I will lock the door when I leave. 我离开的时候会锁门的。
She will lock up the shop at closing time. 她会在关门的时候锁上商店。


虚拟语气:
If I had locked the car, it wouldn't have been stolen. 如果我锁了车，它就不会被偷了。
I wish I had locked the door last night. 我真希望昨晚锁了门。


被动语态:
The door was locked from the outside. 门是从外面锁上的。
The safe is locked securely. 保险箱被牢牢地锁着。


疑问句:
Is the door locked? 门锁了吗？
Have you locked the car? 你锁车了吗？


否定句:
He didn't lock the door. 他没有锁门。
She doesn't lock her bike. 她不锁她的自行车。
