cartogram:/ˈkɑːrtəɡræm/|/ˈkɑːrtəɡræm/| n. |统计地图；变形地图|A cartogram is a map in which some thematic variable, such as population density or gross national product, is substituted for land area or distance.  The geometry of the map is distorted to represent the thematic variable. |Example: This cartogram shows the relative population sizes of different countries, distorting their shapes to reflect their populations.  这个统计地图显示了不同国家的人口相对规模，通过扭曲形状来反映它们的人口数量。|近义词：value-by-area map, thematic map|反义词：geographic map, conventional map
