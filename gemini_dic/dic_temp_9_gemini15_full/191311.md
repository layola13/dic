sceneman:/ˈsiːnˌmæn/| n.|场景人员；场景主管；剧组人员|A sceneman is a member of a film crew responsible for managing the set and ensuring its readiness for filming.  They handle various aspects of the set, from props and equipment to the overall look and feel.|例句：The sceneman meticulously arranged the props before the director called "action". 场景人员在导演喊“开拍”之前一丝不苟地摆放道具。  The sceneman ensured the set was safe and clean for the actors. 场景人员确保布景对演员来说安全又干净。|近义词：set dresser, grip, set hand|反义词：actor, actress
