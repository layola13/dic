gout:/ɡaʊt/| n. |痛风|A form of acute arthritis characterized by sudden, severe attacks of pain, swelling, redness, and tenderness in one or more joints, typically in the big toe. It's caused by a buildup of uric acid crystals in the joints.|He suffered a severe attack of gout in his big toe.|近义词：arthritis|反义词：无
