hyperimmunity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. | 超免疫, 过度免疫 |  An immune response that is stronger or more prolonged than normal. This can be due to repeated exposure to an antigen, or to a particularly potent antigen. Hyperimmunity can be beneficial, such as in the case of acquired immunity after vaccination, or it can be harmful, leading to autoimmune diseases or hypersensitivity reactions. | Example:  The patient developed hyperimmunity to the drug after repeated courses of treatment.  这位病人经过反复疗程的治疗后，对该药物产生了超免疫反应。 |近义词：overimmunity, excessive immunity|反义词：immunodeficiency, immunocompromised
