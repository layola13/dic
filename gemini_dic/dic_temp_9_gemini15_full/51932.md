diabetes-mellitus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ˈmɛlɪtəs/| n. |糖尿病 |A chronic metabolic disorder characterized by elevated levels of glucose in the blood. This is due to the body's inability to produce or effectively use insulin.  There are various types, including type 1 and type 2 diabetes.  Symptoms can include increased thirst and urination, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, and blurred vision.|  She was diagnosed with diabetes-mellitus last year. 她去年被诊断出患有糖尿病。  He manages his diabetes-mellitus through diet and exercise. 他通过饮食和运动来控制他的糖尿病。|hyperglycemia, glycosuria|hypoglycemia|名词
