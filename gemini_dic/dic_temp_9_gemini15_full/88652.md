hoarding:/ˈhɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. v. |囤积；仓储；广告牌 |The act of accumulating and keeping a large quantity of something, often to an excessive degree; a large structure used for outdoor advertising.  |例句：The family's hoarding of food was concerning during the famine.  家庭在饥荒期间囤积食物令人担忧。  |近义词：accumulation, stockpiling, amassing, hoarding, hoarding (n.) billboard, poster, display |反义词：discarding, disposing, giving away


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hoards old newspapers. = 他囤积旧报纸。
They hoard money. = 他们囤积金钱。

一般过去时:
She hoarded candy as a child. = 她小时候囤积糖果。
The squirrels hoarded nuts for the winter. = 松鼠为冬天囤积坚果。

现在完成时:
He has hoarded so many books that his house is overflowing. = 他囤积了这么多书，以至于他的房子都快装不下了。
They have hoarded supplies in preparation for the hurricane. = 他们已经囤积了物资以应对飓风。

过去完成时:
She had hoarded all her savings before the economic crash. = 在经济崩溃之前，她已经囤积了所有的积蓄。
They had hoarded food for months before the siege began. = 在围攻开始前的几个月里，他们一直囤积食物。

将来时:
He will hoard his wealth and never spend it. = 他将囤积他的财富，永不花费。
They will hoard resources during the crisis. = 他们将在危机期间囤积资源。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't hoard so much. = 如果我是他，我不会囤积这么多东西。
I wish he hadn't hoarded all that money. = 我真希望他没有囤积那么多钱。

被动语态:
The old newspapers were hoarded in the attic. = 旧报纸被囤积在阁楼上。
The supplies are being hoarded by some people. = 一些人正在囤积物资。

疑问句:
Why are they hoarding food? = 他们为什么囤积食物？
Is he hoarding money again? = 他又在囤积钱了吗？

否定句:
He doesn't hoard stamps. = 他不囤积邮票。
She didn't hoard anything of value. = 她没有囤积任何值钱的东西。


PHRASE->
hoarding behavior: 囤积行为
compulsive hoarding: 强迫性囤积
hoarding disorder: 囤积障碍
hoarding of resources: 资源囤积
to hoard up: 积攒


词源:
The noun "hoard" comes from the Old English word *hord*, meaning "treasure, store."  The verb "to hoard" developed from this noun.  The modern meaning encompassing the negative connotation of excessive accumulation developed later.
