/ˈseɪtə/|abbr.|SATA|串行ATA|Serial ATA is a computer bus interface that connects host bus adapters to mass storage devices such as hard disk drives and optical drives.|例句：My computer uses SATA drives for faster data transfer. 我的电脑使用SATA硬盘以获得更快的數據傳輸速度。|近义词：PATA|反义词:None.
