gemmy:/ˈdʒɛmi/| adj.|宝石般的；闪闪发光的|Resembling a gem; sparkling; brilliantly clear and lustrous.  The word evokes the qualities of precious stones, suggesting brilliance, clarity, and often a vibrant color.|例句：The gemmy green of the emerald captivated everyone.  祖母绿宝石般的绿色迷住了所有人。

例句：Her gemmy eyes sparkled with mirth. 她闪闪发光的眼睛里闪烁着快乐。


近义词：sparkling, lustrous, brilliant, radiant, glittering

反义词：dull, opaque, matte, cloudy


词性:形容词
