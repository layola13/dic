dies:/daɪz/ | v. |死，死亡；凋谢；消亡 | to stop living; to cease to exist; to lose vitality or effectiveness | The old dog died peacefully in its sleep. 老狗安详地睡梦中去世了。  The flowers died from lack of water. 花儿因为缺水而枯萎了。|近义词：pass away, perish, expire | 反义词：live, survive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dies every day in his dreams. 他每天都在梦中死去。
The plant dies slowly without sunlight.  植物在没有阳光的情况下慢慢枯萎。


一般过去时:
My grandfather died last year. 我的祖父去年去世了。
The company died after a series of bad decisions.  公司在经历了一系列糟糕的决定后倒闭了。


现在完成时:
Many soldiers have died in this war.  这场战争中许多士兵阵亡。
The hopes of a peaceful solution have died.  和平解决的希望破灭了。


过去完成时:
The patient had died before the ambulance arrived.  救护车到达之前病人已经去世了。
The project had died before it even started.  项目甚至还没开始就夭折了。


将来时:
If he continues to drink like this, he will die young. 如果他继续这样喝酒，他会英年早逝。
The business will die without investment.  没有投资，这家企业将会倒闭。


虚拟语气:
If he had known the truth, he would have died a happier man. 如果他知道真相，他会过得更快乐一些。
I wish I had died before witnessing that. 我真希望在我看到那件事之前就死了。


被动语态:
The soldier was reported to have died in action. 据报道，这位士兵在行动中阵亡。
The flowers were found dead, presumably having died from frost.  发现花朵已经枯萎，可能是被冻死的。


疑问句:
Did he die a natural death? 他是自然死亡的吗？
When did the dinosaurs die out? 恐龙是什么时候灭绝的？


否定句:
He didn't die in vain. 他死得其所。
The rumor that he was dead proved to be false; he didn't die.  他死了的谣言被证明是假的；他没死。


PHRASE->
die down (逐渐减弱): The excitement eventually died down.  兴奋最终平息了。
die away (逐渐消失): The sound of the music died away in the distance.  音乐声逐渐消失在远处。
die for (渴望): I'm dying for a cup of coffee. 我非常想喝杯咖啡。
die of (死于): He died of a heart attack. 他死于心脏病。
die out (灭绝): Many species are dying out due to habitat loss.  许多物种由于栖息地丧失而灭绝。
