forebrace:/ˈfɔːrbreɪs/| n.|斜撑,支撑物|A forebrace is a supporting brace or strut, typically used in a ship or aircraft to strengthen a structure against forward stress. It's designed to resist forces pushing from the front.|例句：The ship's forebrace helped prevent the bow from collapsing under the pressure of the waves. 这艘船的斜撑有助于防止船头在海浪的压力下坍塌。|近义词：brace, strut, support|反义词：なし
