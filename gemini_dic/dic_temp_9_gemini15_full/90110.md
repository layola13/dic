hot-desking:/ˈhɒtˌdɛskɪŋ/| n. |轮换办公制，共享办公桌制度|A work arrangement where employees don't have assigned desks and instead choose a desk each day from a pool of available desks.  It's often used to reduce office space costs and promote collaboration. |Example:  Our company recently implemented hot-desking to save money on office rent. = 我们公司最近实施了轮换办公制以节省办公室租金。

Our team uses hot-desking, so I never know where my desk will be each day. = 我们的团队使用轮换办公制，所以我从来不知道我每天的办公桌会在哪里。|近义词：flexible working, desk sharing|反义词：assigned seating, dedicated desk|
