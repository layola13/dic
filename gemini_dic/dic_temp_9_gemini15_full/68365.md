fallibility:/ˌfæləˈbɪləti/| n. |易犯错；不可靠性；缺点|The inherent fallibility of human judgment makes it crucial to have checks and balances in place. 人类判断固有的易犯错性使得建立制衡机制至关重要。|  例句：His fallibility was evident in his repeated mistakes. 他的反复错误证明了他的易犯错性。  例句：The system's fallibility is a major concern.  该系统的不可靠性是一个主要问题。|近义词：imperfection, weakness, flaw |反义词：infallibility, perfection
