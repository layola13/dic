pluteus:/ˈpluːtiəs/| n. |伞菌属；小菇属|A genus of agaric fungi characterized by their typically small size, often brightly colored caps, and the absence of a ring on the stem.  Pluteus mushrooms are generally considered non-toxic, but some species may cause mild gastrointestinal upset.|Example:  Several species of *Pluteus* were found growing on the decaying log.  几株小菇属的真菌被发现生长在腐烂的木头上。|近义词:  (No direct synonym, as it refers to a specific genus)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|
