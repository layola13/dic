personation:/ˌpɜːrsəˈneɪʃən/ | n. |扮演；模仿；假冒；人格化 |The act of impersonating someone or something; the assumption of a character or role.  It can also refer to the attribution of human characteristics to something not human. |例句：Her personation of the famous actress was flawless.  她的著名女演员的扮演天衣无缝。

例句：The novel uses personification to bring the animals to life. 这部小说运用拟人手法使动物栩栩如生。


近义词：impersonation, portrayal, enactment, embodiment, representation

反义词：reality, truthfulness


词性：名词
