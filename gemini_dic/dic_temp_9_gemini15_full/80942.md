grabbed:/ɡræbd/ | v. |抓住；夺取；抢夺 |To take hold of something or someone suddenly and firmly.  |例句：He grabbed the bag and ran. 他抓起包就跑了。|例句：The thief grabbed the purse and disappeared. 小偷抢了钱包就消失了。|近义词：seize, snatch, clutch |反义词：release, let go
