outstretched:/ˌaʊtˈstrɛtʃt/| adj. |伸出的，伸开的，张开的| Extended to the full length; stretched out.  The adjective form of "outstretch". It describes something that is extended to its full length or breadth.|例句：He had his arms outstretched in welcome. 他张开双臂表示欢迎。

例句：The dog ran towards me with its legs outstretched.  那条狗伸着腿朝我跑来。


近义词：extended, stretched, spread, expanded

反义词：contracted, curled, bent, folded

词性:形容词
