letterpress:/ˈlɛtərˌprɛs/ | n. |凸版印刷；凸版印刷术 | A printing process where a raised surface (usually type or a printing plate) is inked and pressed onto paper or other material to create an impression.  The process is known for its tactile quality and unique aesthetic. | Example: The invitation was printed using letterpress, giving it a beautiful, textured feel.  这邀请函采用凸版印刷，质感精美。 |近义词：relief printing, typography (in context) |反义词：planographic printing (e.g., lithography)
