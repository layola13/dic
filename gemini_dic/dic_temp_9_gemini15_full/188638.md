rumkin:/ˈrʌmkɪn/| n. |小碗，小杯|A small drinking bowl or cup, typically made of wood.  The word is primarily found in dialectal or archaic usage.  |例句：He drank his ale from a sturdy oak rumkin. 他用结实的橡木小碗喝啤酒。  |近义词：bowl, cup, goblet|反义词：None specifically, but words implying large size could be considered antonyms (e.g., tankard)|名词
