peasant:/ˈpɛzənt/| n.|农民；乡下人|A peasant is a person who works on the land, especially one who has a low social status.  The term often implies poverty and a lack of education.|例句：The peasants worked tirelessly in the fields from sunrise to sunset.  农民们从日出到日落辛勤地耕作在田地里。
The feudal system relied heavily on the labor of peasants. 封建制度严重依赖农民的劳动。|近义词：farmer, rural dweller, rustic|反义词：noble, aristocrat, landowner
