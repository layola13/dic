laughing-at:/ˈlæfɪŋ æt/| v. |嘲笑；因…而笑|To find something amusing and express this through laughter.  It implies a sense of amusement, often at someone else's expense, but not necessarily malicious.|例句：They were laughing at his clumsy dance moves. 他们嘲笑他笨拙的舞姿。

例句：I couldn't help laughing at the funny joke. 我忍不住笑了，因为那个笑话太有趣了。


近义词：scoffing at, mocking, ridiculing, jeering at|反义词：sympathizing with, pitying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are laughing at the comedian. = 他们在嘲笑那个喜剧演员。
We laugh at silly movies. = 我们嘲笑傻乎乎的电影。


一般过去时:
She laughed at his attempt to sing. = 她嘲笑他唱歌的尝试。
They laughed at the clown's antics. = 他们嘲笑小丑的滑稽动作。


现在完成时:
I have laughed at that joke many times. = 我已经多次嘲笑过那个笑话了。
We have been laughing at his jokes all evening. = 我们整个晚上都在嘲笑他的笑话。


过去完成时:
They had laughed at him before, so he was prepared. = 他们以前就嘲笑过他，所以他有所准备。
She had already laughed at the same joke earlier. = 她之前已经笑过同一个笑话了。


将来时:
They will laugh at your silly idea. = 他们会嘲笑你愚蠢的主意。
We will be laughing at this in years to come. = 多年以后我们还会嘲笑这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I told that joke, they would laugh at me. = 如果我说那个笑话，他们会嘲笑我。
I wish they wouldn't laugh at my mistakes. = 我希望他们不要嘲笑我的错误。


被动语态:
His jokes were laughed at by everyone. = 他的笑话被每个人都嘲笑了。
The situation was being laughed at by the onlookers. = 旁观者正在嘲笑这种情况。


疑问句:
Are they laughing at me? = 他们在嘲笑我吗？
Why are you laughing at that? = 你为什么嘲笑那个？


否定句:
They didn't laugh at his serious speech. = 他们没有嘲笑他的严肃讲话。
I don't laugh at people's misfortunes. = 我不会嘲笑别人的不幸。


PHRASE->
laugh at someone's expense = 嘲笑某人
laugh at the top of one's lungs = 放声大笑
laugh something off = 对…一笑而过
laugh away one's worries = 抛开忧虑，开怀大笑
laugh in the face of danger = 面对危险大笑
