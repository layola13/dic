They can encompass various aspects, from slang and colloquialisms to unique grammatical structures or pronunciation patterns.  The term often implies a focus on features that might be challenging for non-native speakers to grasp fully. | Example: "The use of 'cheers' as a general thank you is a quintessential Englishism."  |近义词: idiom, colloquialism, peculiarity | 反义词:  standard English (in the sense of contrasting with non-standard usages) |名词
