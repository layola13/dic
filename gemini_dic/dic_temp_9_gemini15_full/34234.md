clannish: /ˈklænɪʃ/ | adj. | 排外的；宗族式的；家族似的 |  Characterized by or suggestive of clannishness; tending to be exclusive and unwelcoming to outsiders.  The word implies a strong sense of loyalty and unity within the group, but also a reluctance to interact with those outside it. |

例句：

The community was notoriously clannish, with newcomers finding it difficult to integrate.  这个社区以排外而闻名，新来的人很难融入。

His clannish behavior alienated many of his colleagues. 他排外的行为疏远了他的许多同事。


近义词：insular, exclusive, sectarian, inbred

反义词：inclusive, welcoming, open-minded, cosmopolitan


词性：形容词
