eru:/ˈɛruː/| n. |喷发；爆发|A sudden, violent eruption or outburst.|The volcano erupted with a deafening roar. 火山爆发发出震耳欲聋的轰鸣声。The crowd erupted in cheers. 人群爆发出一阵欢呼声。|近义词：explosion, outburst, eruption|反义词：calm, quiet|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The volcano erupts frequently. = 这座火山经常喷发。
He erupts in anger easily. = 他很容易发怒。


一般过去时:
The geyser erupted yesterday. = 间歇泉昨天喷发了。
She erupted into laughter. = 她突然大笑起来。


现在完成时:
The volcano has erupted several times this year. = 这座火山今年已经喷发了几次。
He has erupted in anger many times. = 他已经多次发怒了。


过去完成时:
The volcano had erupted before we arrived. = 我们到达之前火山已经喷发了。
She had erupted in tears before I could comfort her. = 在我安慰她之前，她已经哭了起来。


将来时:
The volcano will erupt again soon. = 这座火山很快就会再次喷发。
He will probably erupt if you criticize him. = 如果你批评他，他可能会勃然大怒。


虚拟语气:
If the volcano erupted, we would have to evacuate. = 如果火山爆发，我们就必须撤离。
If he erupted, I would remain calm.  = 如果他勃然大怒，我会保持冷静。


被动语态:
The city was erupted by the war. = 这个城市被战争摧毁了。(被动语态在此处略微牵强，更常用的表达是“The war erupted in the city”)
The news was received with an eruption of excitement. = 这个消息被激动地欢呼回应。(被动语态更侧重于反应)


疑问句:
When did the volcano last erupt? = 火山上次爆发是什么时候？
Will he erupt in anger at the meeting? = 他会在会议上发脾气吗？


否定句:
The volcano did not erupt yesterday. = 火山昨天没有喷发。
He didn't erupt in anger; he remained calm. = 他没有发脾气；他保持冷静。


PHRASE->
eruption of anger = 勃然大怒
eruption of violence = 暴力事件
volcanic eruption = 火山爆发
sudden eruption = 突然爆发
eruption of laughter = 突然大笑
