palingenesy:/ˌpæˌlɪnˈdʒɛnɪsi/| n.| 再生；轮回；重生 | The biological process of regeneration or rebirth, often referring to the cyclical renewal of life or the reappearance of extinct species.  It can also refer to a metaphorical rebirth or renewal.  | Example: The theory of palingenesy suggests that life forms are cyclical, repeatedly appearing and disappearing throughout history.  再生理论认为生命形式是循环的，在历史上反复出现和消失。 |近义词：regeneration, rebirth, resurrection, reincarnation |反义词：destruction, extinction, annihilation |名词
