morpheming:/ˈmɔːrfiːmɪŋ/| n. |词素分析，词素构成|The process of analyzing words into their constituent morphemes.  It involves identifying the smallest units of meaning in a language and understanding how they combine to form words.  Morpheming is a crucial aspect of linguistic analysis and morphology.|例句：Morpheming the word "unbreakable" reveals the morphemes "un-", "break", and "-able". 词素分析单词“unbreakable”揭示了词素“un-”、“break”和“-able”。|近义词：morphological analysis, morpheme segmentation|反义词：无
