dwelling: /ˈdwɛlɪŋ/ | n. |住所，住宅 | A place of residence; a house, apartment, or other building where people live.  |例句：They are looking for a new dwelling in the countryside. 他们正在乡下寻找新的住所。|例句：The old dwelling was in a state of disrepair.  这座旧房子破败不堪。|近义词：residence, abode, home, house, apartment |反义词： homelessness
