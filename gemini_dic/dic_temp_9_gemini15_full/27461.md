camp-sites:/ˈkæmpˌsaɪts/| n.|营地；野营地 |Places designated for camping, often providing basic amenities like toilets and sometimes water.  |例句：We reserved a camp-site near the lake. 我们预订了湖边的一个营地。
例句：The camp-sites were full, so we had to find another place to camp. 营地满了，所以我们不得不找另一个地方露营。|近义词：camping ground, campground |反义词：hotel, motel
