reforgeable: /ˌriːˈfɔːrdʒəbl/ | adj. | 可重铸的，可再锻造的 | Capable of being reforged or reshaped by heating and hammering.  This term typically applies to metals. |


例句：

The ancient sword, though damaged, was reforgeable.  古老的剑，虽然受损，但可以重铸。

The blacksmith proved the metal was reforgeable by shaping it into a new tool.  铁匠通过将金属塑造成新的工具来证明它是可重铸的。


近义词：reworkable, reshapeable

反义词：unforgeable, irreplaceable


词性: 形容词
