carnauba:/kɑːˈnɔːbə/| n. |巴西棕榈蜡|Carnauba wax is a wax obtained from the leaves of the carnauba palm tree (Copernicia prunifera) native to Brazil. It is the hardest natural wax and has a high melting point.  It's used in various products for its protective and glossy properties. |The carnauba wax coating on the car provided excellent protection from the elements. 巴西棕榈蜡涂层为汽车提供了极好的防风雨保护。|  |  |名词
