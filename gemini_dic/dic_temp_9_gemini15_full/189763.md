salty:/ˈsɔːlti/ /ˈsɔːlti/ | adj. | 咸的；含盐的；辛辣的；粗鲁的 | Having or containing salt; tasting of salt.  The word can also describe something unpleasant or harsh, like a salty remark. | 例句：The soup is too salty. 这汤太咸了。

The ocean water is salty. 海洋的水是咸的。


近义词：briny, saline, piquant (for the spicy sense),  sharp (for the harsh sense) | 反义词：fresh, sweet, bland


词源:  The word "salty" originates from the Old English word "sealt," meaning "salt."  This word, in turn, has roots in Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European languages, reflecting the ancient importance of salt as a preservative and flavoring agent.
