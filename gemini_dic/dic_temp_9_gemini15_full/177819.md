recense:/rɪˈsɛns/| v.|重新统计，重新调查，再审查 |To count or list again; to review or examine again.|例句：The government decided to recense the population after the natural disaster. 政府决定在自然灾害后重新统计人口。

例句：The committee will recense the evidence before making a final decision. 委员会将在做出最终决定之前重新审查证据。

近义词：recount, review, reassess, re-examine|反义词：None|词性:动词
