overindulge:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/| vt.|过度放纵，沉溺|To allow oneself to have more of something enjoyable than is good for one.  |例句：He overindulged in rich foods and desserts at the buffet. 他在自助餐上吃太多油腻的食物和甜点。|例句：She overindulged in alcohol at the party and regretted it the next day.她在派对上喝了太多酒，第二天后悔不已。|近义词：spoil, pamper, indulge excessively|反义词：restrain, moderate, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overindulges in sweets. = 他沉溺于甜食。
They overindulge in alcohol regularly. = 他们经常酗酒。

一般过去时:
I overindulged at the Thanksgiving feast. = 我在感恩节盛宴上吃太多了。
She overindulged in sleep after a long journey. = 长途旅行后，她睡得很久。

现在完成时:
I have overindulged recently and need to cut back. = 我最近放纵过度，需要节制一下。
She has overindulged in rich foods and gained weight. = 她吃了太多油腻的食物，体重增加了。

过去完成时:
He realized he had overindulged in the previous week. = 他意识到他上周放纵过度了。
We had overindulged in the celebratory dinner and felt sluggish the next day. = 我们在庆祝晚宴上吃喝太多了，第二天感觉很迟钝。

将来时:
I will overindulge if given the chance. = 如果有机会，我会放纵一下。
They will probably overindulge at the wedding reception. = 他们很可能会在婚礼招待会上放纵一下。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overindulged, I wouldn't feel so sick. = 如果我没有放纵过度，我就不会这么难受。
I wish I hadn't overindulged in that delicious cake. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多美味的蛋糕。

被动语态:  (Overindulge is rarely used in the passive voice.)
His body was overtaxed by his overindulgence. = 他的身体因他的放纵而超负荷运转。(此处被动语态更强调结果)
The children were overindulged by their grandparents. = 孩子们被祖父母过度溺爱。(此处更强调被动的接受)


疑问句:
Did you overindulge last night? = 你昨晚放纵过度了吗？
Does he frequently overindulge in fast food? = 他经常暴饮暴食吗？

否定句:
I didn't overindulge at the party. = 我在派对上没有放纵过度。
He tried not to overindulge during the holidays. = 他试图在假期里不要放纵过度。
