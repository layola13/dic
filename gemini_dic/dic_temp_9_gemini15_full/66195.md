excised:/ɪkˈsaɪzd/ | vt. | 切除；删去；去除 | To remove something, especially by cutting it out.  | The surgeon excised the tumor. 外科医生切除了肿瘤。 The editor excised several passages from the manuscript. 编辑从手稿中删去了几段文字。 | remove, delete, cut out, eliminate | retain, keep, insert, add | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The surgeon excises the tumor. = 外科医生切除肿瘤。
They excise offensive content from the website. = 他们从网站上删除冒犯性内容。


一般过去时:
The doctor excised the appendix. = 医生切除了阑尾。

现在完成时:
The editor has excised several paragraphs from the article. = 编辑已经从文章中删除了几段话。
They have excised the problematic sections of the code. = 他们已经删除了代码中有问题的部分。


过去完成时:
The author had excised the controversial chapter before publication. = 作者在出版前已经删除了有争议的章节。

将来时:
