replenishing: /rɪˈplɛnɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 再补充；重新装满；补充；使恢复；|The act of making something full or complete again.  It implies restoring something to its former state or level. |例句：The farmer is replenishing the soil with fertilizer.  农民正在用肥料补充土壤。

例句：We need to replenish our stock of coffee before the weekend rush. 我们需要在周末高峰期之前补充咖啡库存。


近义词：restocking, refilling, supplementing, renewing, restoring

反义词：depleting, exhausting, emptying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replenishes his water bottle after each run. 他每次跑步后都会补充水分。
She replenishes the bird feeder every morning. 她每天早上都会补充鸟食。

一般过去时:
The company replenished its inventory after the holiday sales. 公司在节假日销售后补充了库存。
They replenished the sugar bowl after dinner. 他们晚餐后补充了糖罐。

现在完成时:
We have replenished our supplies of paper and ink. 我们已经补充了纸张和墨水的供应。
She has replenished her savings account after paying off her debts. 她还清债务后已经补充了储蓄账户。

过去完成时:
They had replenished the fuel tanks before embarking on the long journey. 他们在开始长途旅行之前已经补充了油箱。
She had replenished the cookie jar before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经补充了饼干罐。

将来时:
We will replenish the stock next week. 我们下周将补充库存。
He will replenish his energy with a good night's sleep. 他将通过一夜好眠来恢复精力。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would replenish my wardrobe. 如果我有更多的钱，我会补充我的衣橱。
If they replenished their supplies, they would have been able to continue the project. 如果他们补充了物资，他们就能继续这个项目了。

被动语态:
The water tank is replenished automatically. 水箱会自动补充。
The shelves were replenished by the store staff. 货架由店员补充。

疑问句:
How often do you replenish your emergency fund? 你多久补充一次紧急基金？
When will the water supply be replenished? 水供应什么时候会补充？

否定句:
He did not replenish his coffee cup. 他没有补充咖啡杯。
They failed to replenish the stock on time. 他们未能按时补充库存。


PHRASE->

词源:
re- (again) + plenish (to fill)  源自古法语 *plenir (to fill),  最终源自拉丁语 *plenus (full).
