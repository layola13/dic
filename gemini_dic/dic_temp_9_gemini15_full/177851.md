receptionist:/rɪˈsɛpʃənɪst/| n. |接待员，接线员|A receptionist is a person who works in an office or other place of business, answering telephones, greeting visitors, and providing general administrative support.|例句：The receptionist greeted me warmly and directed me to the correct office.  接待员热情地接待了我，并把我带到了正确的办公室。|例句：She worked as a receptionist for a law firm for five years.  她在一家律师事务所做了五年接待员。|近义词：clerk, attendant, secretary|反义词：N/A
