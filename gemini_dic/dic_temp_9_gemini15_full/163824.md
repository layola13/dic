/ˌpriːdɪˈpɒzɪtəriːz/ | n. | 预先存放处；预先储存库 |  A predepository is a place where something is stored or deposited before its final destination.  It often implies a temporary storage location before further processing or distribution.  The term suggests a proactive and planned storage, rather than a random or accidental one. | Example: The company uses several predepositories to manage its inventory before shipping to retailers.  公司在向零售商发货前使用多个预先存放处来管理库存。


近义词:  staging area, interim storage, temporary warehouse, holding facility | 反义词: final destination, ultimate repository
