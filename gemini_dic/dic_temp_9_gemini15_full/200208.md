skinnies:/ˈskɪniz/| n. adj.|瘦子；紧身裤；纤细的|Extremely thin trousers, usually made of stretch fabric;  also describes someone who is very thin.|例句：She wore skinny jeans and a loose top. 她穿着紧身牛仔裤和一件宽松的上衣。|例句：He was a skinny boy with big eyes. 他是个瘦瘦的男孩，眼睛很大。|近义词：slim-fitting trousers, tights (for trousers), slender, lean, thin|反义词：wide-legged trousers, baggy, plump, stout|名词，形容词
