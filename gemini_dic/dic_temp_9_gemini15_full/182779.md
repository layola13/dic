repatriating: /ˌriˈpeɪtrieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |遣返；送回；归还 | The act of returning a person or thing to their own country or place of origin.  This often involves bringing someone back from a foreign country, but can also refer to returning something to its rightful place. | Example: The government is repatriating citizens from the war-torn country.  政府正在将公民从饱受战争蹂躏的国家遣返。

Example:  The museum is repatriating a stolen artifact to its country of origin. 博物馆正在将其被盗的文物送回原产国。


近义词: returning, sending back, bringing back

反义词: expatriating, deporting
