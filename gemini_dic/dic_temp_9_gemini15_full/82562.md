guaranteed:/ˌɡærənˈtiːd/|/ˈɡærənˌtiːd/| adj.|保证的；有保证的；确定的|Something that is guaranteed is certain to happen or be true.  It implies a promise or assurance of a particular outcome.|The hotel offers a guaranteed reservation.|近义词：assured, certain, promised|反义词：uncertain, doubtful, risky


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This product is guaranteed to last for five years. = 保证这款产品可以使用五年。
The success of the project is guaranteed. = 项目的成功是有保证的。


一般过去时:
The contract guaranteed him a job for life. = 合同保证他终身有工作。
The mechanic guaranteed the repair would fix the problem. = 机械师保证维修能解决问题。


现在完成时:
The company has guaranteed its customers' satisfaction for years. = 多年来，公司一直保证客户满意。
We have been guaranteed a fair trial. = 我们得到了公平审判的保证。


过去完成时:
They had guaranteed the delivery by Friday, but it was late. = 他们保证周五之前送达，但晚了。
He had guaranteed her safety, but she was still in danger. = 他保证了她的安全，但她仍然处于危险之中。


将来时:
The warranty guarantees that the product will be repaired or replaced. = 保修单保证产品将被修理或更换。
The contract will guarantee them a minimum wage. = 合同将保证他们获得最低工资。


虚拟语气:
If the project were guaranteed success, more investors would be involved. = 如果项目成功有保证，更多的投资者会参与。
I wish the outcome was guaranteed. = 我希望结果是有保证的。


被动语态:
The product is guaranteed against defects. = 产品保证无缺陷。
His position was guaranteed by the government. = 他的职位由政府保证。


疑问句:
Is the delivery date guaranteed? = 交货日期有保证吗？
Is this product guaranteed to work? = 这个产品保证有效吗？


否定句:
The outcome is not guaranteed. = 结果没有保证。
The company does not guarantee refunds. = 公司不保证退款。
