misreflects:/ˌmɪsˈrɪflekts/| v.|错误地反映，歪曲|To represent inaccurately or distort; to give a false or misleading impression of.|The article misreflects the views of the majority. 这篇文章错误地反映了大多数人的观点。|近义词：distort, misrepresent, falsify|反义词：accurately reflect, represent truthfully

The media misreflects public opinion on this issue.  媒体歪曲了公众对这个问题的看法。
This statistic misreflects the true level of unemployment.  这个数据错误地反映了真实的失业率。
