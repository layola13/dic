nonracial: /ˌnɒnˈreɪʃəl/ | adj. | 非种族的；不涉及种族的 | Not related to or based on race.  This term emphasizes the absence of racial considerations or discrimination. |例句：The company promotes a nonracial work environment. 这家公司提倡非种族的工作环境。

例句：The study used a nonracial approach to analyze socioeconomic factors. 这项研究采用非种族的方法来分析社会经济因素。


近义词：race-neutral, non-discriminatory, colorblind


反义词：racial, discriminatory, prejudiced

词性：形容词
