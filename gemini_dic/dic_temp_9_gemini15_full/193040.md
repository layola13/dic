secretariates:/ˌsɛkrəˈtɛəriəts/| n. |秘书处；办事处；总务部门 |A group of secretaries or administrative staff working together, often in a large organization.  It can also refer to the offices themselves. |The UN secretariats are responsible for implementing the decisions of the General Assembly.  联合国秘书处负责执行大会的决定。|offices, bureaus, departments |field offices, departments|
