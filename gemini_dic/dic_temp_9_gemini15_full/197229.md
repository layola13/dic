shared:/ʃeəd/| adj. | 共享的，共同的 |  Something that is shared is used or owned by more than one person. It implies a sense of community or joint ownership. |  例句：We shared a pizza for lunch.  我们午餐一起吃了一个披萨。

例句：They shared a common interest in music. 他们对音乐有着共同的兴趣。


近义词：common, joint, mutual, collective | 反义词：private, individual, sole, exclusive | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They share a car. = 他们合用一辆车。
We share a common goal. = 我们有着共同的目标。


一般过去时:
We shared a room during our trip. = 旅行期间我们合住一个房间。
They shared their experiences with us. = 他们和我们分享了他们的经历。


现在完成时:
We have shared many happy moments together. = 我们一起分享了许多快乐的时光。
They have shared their profits with their employees. = 他们已经把利润分给了员工。


过去完成时:
They had shared their concerns before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经分享了他们的担忧。
She had shared her secret with her best friend. = 她已经和她最好的朋友分享了她的秘密。


将来时:
We will share the costs equally. = 我们将平均分摊费用。
They will share their findings with the scientific community. = 他们将与科学界分享他们的发现。


虚拟语气:
If we shared our resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们共享资源，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish they had shared their ideas earlier. = 我希望他们早点分享他们的想法。


被动语态:
The profits were shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information was shared with the public. = 信息已与公众共享。


疑问句:
Do you share my opinion? = 你同意我的意见吗？
Will they share the responsibilities? = 他们会分担责任吗？


否定句:
They didn't share their food. = 他们没有分享他们的食物。
He didn't share his feelings with anyone. = 他没有和任何人分享他的感受。
