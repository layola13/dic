edward:/ˈɛdwərd/ | n. |爱德华 | A masculine given name of English origin, derived from the Old English elements "ēad" (wealth, prosperity) and "weard" (guardian, protector).  It signifies "wealthy protector" or "prosperous guardian". The name has been popular throughout history and continues to be used.  | Example: Edward is a handsome young man. 爱德华是一位英俊的年轻人。  |近义词：Edmund, Edwin |反义词：无
