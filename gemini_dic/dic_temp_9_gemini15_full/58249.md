drumfish:/ˈdrʌmfɪʃ/| n.|鼓鱼|A type of fish known for the drumming sound it produces by contracting its swim bladder.  They are typically found in warm, shallow waters and are often bottom-dwellers.  |例句：The drumfish's distinctive drumming sound can be heard from quite a distance. 鼓鱼独特的鼓声可以从相当远的地方听到。|例句：Several species of drumfish inhabit the coastal waters of the Gulf of Mexico. 几种鼓鱼栖息在墨西哥湾的沿海水域。|近义词：croaker (a similar fish that makes noise)|反义词:|
