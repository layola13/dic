far-fetched:/ˌfɑːrˈfetʃt/| adj.|牵强的，难以置信的|  Something that is far-fetched is unlikely to be true or is unconvincing because it is based on very little evidence. It suggests something is improbable or unrealistic.|例句：His explanation for his lateness was rather far-fetched. 他迟到的解释相当牵强。  例句：The plot of the novel is a bit far-fetched. 这部小说的情节有点牵强。|近义词：improbable, unbelievable, implausible, unconvincing|反义词：plausible, believable, credible, likely
