homocentric:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|同中心的，以自我为中心的|Having the same center;  centered on oneself; egocentric.  The term often describes a perspective or system where everything is viewed from a single, central point of reference, often the self.|例句：His homocentric worldview prevented him from understanding other cultures. 他以自我为中心的 worldview 妨碍了他理解其他文化。

例句：The homocentric design of the city made it difficult to navigate. 这个城市的同心圆设计使得它难以通行。


近义词：egocentric, self-centered, self-absorbed, centric


反义词：eccentric, objective, impartial, altruistic


词性：形容词
