reinaugurated:/ˌriːɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪtɪd/| v.|重新就职；再次启用|To inaugurate again; to install again in office or position.  The word implies a previous inauguration.|例句：The president was reinaugurated for a second term. 总统再次就职，开始了他的第二个任期。

例句：The museum was reinaugurated after extensive renovations.  博物馆经过大规模翻修后重新开放。


近义词：reinstall, reinstate, recommission


反义词：inaugurate (for the first time), decommission, close


词性:动词
