non-electronic: /ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɒnɪk/ | adj. |非电子的| Not involving or using electricity or electronic devices.  This adjective describes something that operates without electricity or electronic components.  It emphasizes the absence of electronic technology in the item or process being described.

例句：
The museum showcases many non-electronic devices from the early 20th century. = 博物馆展示了许多20世纪初期的非电子设备。

The library contains a significant collection of non-electronic books and journals. = 图书馆收藏了大量的非电子书籍和期刊。


近义词：mechanical, manual, analog

反义词：electronic, digital
