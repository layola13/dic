crooningly:/ˈkruːnɪŋli/| adv. |低吟地，哼唱地，柔情地 |Singing or speaking in a low, soft, and soothing voice.  It suggests a gentle, melodious quality, often associated with romance or sentimentality. |例句：He sang crooningly to the baby, lulling it to sleep. 他低声哼唱着哄婴儿睡觉。

例句：She spoke crooningly, her voice full of affection. 她柔声细语，声音充满了爱意。

近义词：softly, sweetly, tenderly, lovingly, murmuringly

反义词：harshly, loudly, roughly, aggressively

词性：副词
