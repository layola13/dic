rescindments:/rɪˈsɪndmənts/ | n. |撤销；废除；取消 |The plural form of "rescindment," referring to multiple acts of rescinding or repealing something.  It denotes the process or instances of withdrawing, annulling, or making void a contract, law, decision, or other action.  |例句：The numerous rescindments of the unpopular policy led to widespread confusion.|近义词：revocations, cancellations, annulments|反义词：affirmations, ratifications|名词
