favourites: /ˈfeɪvərɪts/ | n. | 最喜爱的事物；宠儿 |  Things that one likes best.  | My favourites are chocolate and ice cream. 我最喜欢巧克力和冰淇淋。  | favorites (美式拼写) |  preferences, darlings | dislikes, least favorites | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your favourites? = 你最喜欢什么？
What are his favourite foods? = 他最喜欢的食物是什么？

一般过去时:
What were your favourites as a child? = 你小时候最喜欢什么？
What were her favourite books? = 她最喜欢的书是什么？

现在完成时:
What have been your favourite moments this year? = 今年你最难忘的时刻是什么？
What have been his favourite activities lately? = 他最近最喜欢的活动是什么？

过去完成时:
What had been your favourite subjects before you changed schools? = 你转学前最喜欢的科目是什么？
What had been their favourite restaurant before it closed? = 那家餐馆关门前，他们最喜欢去哪家餐厅？


将来时:
What will be your favourite hobby next year? = 明年你最喜欢的爱好是什么？
What will be her favourite colour in the future? = 她将来最喜欢的颜色是什么？

虚拟语气:
If I had known this was his favourite, I would have bought it for him. = 如果我知道这是他最喜欢的，我就会给他买。
I wish my favourite restaurant hadn't closed down. = 我真希望我最喜欢的餐馆没有倒闭。

被动语态:
His painting was chosen as the favourite by the judges. = 他的画被评委选为最喜欢的。
This song is often cited as a favourite by many music lovers. = 这首歌经常被许多音乐爱好者评为最爱。

疑问句:
What are your favourite colours? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What is your favourite type of music? = 你最喜欢的音乐类型是什么？


否定句:
This isn't my favourite colour. = 这不是我最喜欢的颜色。
That wasn't her favourite restaurant. = 那不是她最喜欢的餐馆。


PHRASE->
