make the grade:/meɪk ðə ɡreɪd/ | v. |达到标准；及格；成功 |To succeed in meeting a required standard or expectation.  It implies achieving a level of competence or performance that is considered satisfactory or acceptable. |例句：He studied hard and finally made the grade in his exams. 他努力学习，最终考试及格了。

例句：Despite facing many challenges, she made the grade and secured a place in the prestigious university.  尽管面临许多挑战，她还是成功考上了那所著名大学。


近义词：succeed, pass, qualify, achieve, accomplish

反义词：fail, flunk, fall short, underperform
