mujahedeen:/ˌmuːdʒəˈhiːdiːn/| n. |圣战者；穆圣战士|A mujahedeen is a person who engages in a jihad, often referring to those who fought against the Soviet Union in Afghanistan during the 1980s.  The term often carries political and ideological connotations, and its use can be controversial.| 例句：The mujahedeen fought bravely against the Soviet occupation of Afghanistan.  圣战者英勇地对抗苏联对阿富汗的占领。  Many mujahedeen later joined extremist groups.  许多圣战者后来加入了极端组织。|近义词：fighter, guerrilla, insurgent, freedom fighter|反义词：collaborator, occupier
