nonsusceptible: /ˌnɒn.səˈsɛptɪbl/ | adj. |不易感染的；不受影响的；不敏感的 | Not susceptible; not easily affected or influenced.  | The new strain of influenza is nonsusceptible to the usual antiviral medications. 新型流感病毒不易被常用的抗病毒药物所抑制。|近义词：immune, resistant, impervious |反义词：susceptible, vulnerable
