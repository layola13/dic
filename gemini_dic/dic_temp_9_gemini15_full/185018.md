retesting:/ˌriːˈtɛstɪŋ/ | n. | 再次测试；复测 | The act of testing something again.  It often implies a previous test has been conducted, and this is a follow-up to verify results, identify errors, or improve accuracy. | Example:  The retesting of the software revealed a critical bug.  再次测试软件发现了严重的错误。 | 近义词：reexamination, second testing, verification | 反义词：initial testing, first test


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is retesting the product. = 公司正在对产品进行再次测试。
He retests the experiment regularly. = 他定期复测实验。


一般过去时:
They retested the sample yesterday. = 他们昨天对样品进行了复测。
We retested the hypothesis after the initial results were questionable. = 在最初的结果令人质疑之后，我们对假设进行了复测。


现在完成时:
She has retested the engine multiple times. = 她已经多次对发动机进行了复测。
They have retested the theory with new data. = 他们用新的数据对理论进行了复测。


过去完成时:
The scientists had retested the compound before publishing their findings. = 科学家们在发表研究结果之前已经对化合物进行了复测。
He had retested the equipment several times before the final experiment. = 在最终实验之前，他已经多次对设备进行了复测。


将来时:
We will retest the medication next week. = 我们下周将对药物进行复测。
They will retest the students after the break. = 他们将在休息后对学生进行复测。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would retest the results. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会对结果进行复测。
I wish we had retested the software before the launch. = 我希望我们在发布之前对软件进行了复测。


被动语态:
The sample was retested by the lab. = 样品由实验室进行了复测。
The hypothesis is being retested by the researchers. = 假设正在由研究人员进行复测。


疑问句:
Will the product be retested? = 产品会再次测试吗？
Has the experiment been retested? = 实验已经复测了吗？


否定句:
They did not retest the equipment. = 他们没有对设备进行复测。
The doctor didn't retest the patient. = 医生没有对病人进行复测。
