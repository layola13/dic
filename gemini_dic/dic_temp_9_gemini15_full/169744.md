propylene:/ˈprəʊpɪliːn/ | n. |丙烯|A colorless, flammable gas, C3H6, used in the manufacture of plastics, fibers, and other chemicals. It is an unsaturated hydrocarbon with a double bond between two carbon atoms. |例句：Propylene is a key component in the production of polypropylene plastics. 丙烯是生产聚丙烯塑料的关键成分。 |例句：The chemical plant produces large quantities of propylene for various industrial applications. 这家化工厂生产大量的丙烯用于各种工业用途。|近义词：propene|反义词：无


USAGE->

In a sentence with an adjective:
