rear-ends:/rɪrˈɛndz/ | n. | 车尾碰撞；追尾事故 | The act of one vehicle colliding with the rear of another vehicle.  It often implies a collision caused by the following vehicle failing to maintain a safe distance. | Example: The accident was a rear-end collision caused by distracted driving.  这起事故是由于分心驾驶造成的追尾事故。  |近义词：rear-end collision, fender bender (less severe) | 反义词：head-on collision |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car rear-ends another vehicle frequently. 这辆车经常追尾其他车辆。
He rear-ends other vehicles at intersections. 他在十字路口经常追尾其他车辆。


一般过去时:
A truck rear-ended my car yesterday. 昨天一辆卡车追尾了我的车。
She rear-ended the bus while texting. 她发短信时追尾了公共汽车。


现在完成时:
He has rear-ended three cars this year. 他今年已经追尾了三辆车。
I have never rear-ended anyone before. 我以前从未追尾过任何人。


过去完成时:
The driver had rear-ended another car before the accident. 司机在事故发生前已经追尾了另一辆车。
They had rear-ended several cars in the past week. 他们过去一周已经追尾了好几辆车。


将来时:
He will rear-end that car if he doesn't brake. 如果他不刹车，他就会追尾那辆车。
If he doesn't slow down, he will rear-end the vehicle in front. 如果他不减速，他就会追尾前面的车辆。


虚拟语气:
If he had seen the car, he wouldn't have rear-ended it. 如果他看到那辆车，他就不会追尾了。
I wish I hadn't rear-ended that expensive sports car. 我真希望我没有追尾那辆昂贵的跑车。


被动语态:
Two cars were rear-ended in the accident. 事故中有两辆车被追尾。
His car was rear-ended at a stoplight. 他的车在红绿灯处被追尾。


疑问句:
Was the car rear-ended? 那辆车是被追尾的吗？
Has anyone ever been rear-ended by a large truck? 有人曾经被大型卡车追尾吗？


否定句:
He didn't rear-end the car intentionally. 他不是故意追尾那辆车的。
I have never been rear-ended in my life. 我一生中从未被追尾过。
