cedes:/ˈsiːdz/| v.|割让，放弃；让步 |To yield or grant something, especially territory or rights, to another.  |例句：France ceded Alsace-Lorraine to Germany after the Franco-Prussian War. 法国在普法战争后将阿尔萨斯-洛林割让给了德国。|例句：He ceded the point to avoid an argument. 他为了避免争论而让步。|近义词：yield, relinquish, surrender, concede |反义词：retain, keep, hold, seize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cedes control of the company to his son. = 他把公司的控制权让给了他的儿子。
The government cedes land for a new park. = 政府划拨土地用于建设新的公园。


一般过去时:
Spain ceded Florida to the United States. = 西班牙将佛罗里达割让给了美国。
She ceded her position to a younger colleague. = 她把自己的职位让给了年轻的同事。


现在完成时:
They have ceded much of their market share to competitors. = 他们已经将大部分市场份额拱手让给了竞争对手。
The country has ceded its claim to the territory. = 这个国家已经放弃了对这片领土的主张。


过去完成时:
Before the treaty, they had ceded several islands to their neighbour. = 在签订条约之前，他们已经将几个岛屿割让给了他们的邻国。
He had ceded his right to the inheritance. = 他已经放弃了他继承权。


将来时:
The company will cede its manufacturing operations to a subsidiary. = 公司将把其制造业务转交给子公司。
She will cede the floor to the next speaker. = 她将把发言权让给下一位发言者。


虚拟语气:
If he ceded control, the company might survive. = 如果他放弃控制权，公司或许能够生存下去。
I wish he would cede his unreasonable demands. = 我希望他能放弃他无理的要求。


被动语态:
The land was ceded to the developers. = 这块土地被割让给了开发商。
The throne was ceded to his nephew. = 王位被让给了他的侄子。


疑问句:
Will they cede the disputed territory? = 他们会割让有争议的领土吗？
Did she cede her rights to the property? = 她放弃了她对该房产的权利吗？


否定句:
He would not cede an inch in the negotiations. = 他在谈判中寸步不让。
The government refused to cede any ground on the issue. = 政府拒绝在这个问题上让步。


PHRASE->
cede control = 放弃控制
cede ground = 让步
cede territory = 割让领土
cede power = 交出权力
cede rights = 放弃权利
