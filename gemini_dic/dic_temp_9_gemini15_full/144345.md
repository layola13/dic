outstartle:  /ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtl/ | v. | 使大吃一惊；使震惊 | To surprise or shock someone greatly. | 例句：The sudden noise outstartled the birds, causing them to fly away.  突然的噪音吓坏了鸟儿，使它们飞走了。  | 近义词：astonish, startle, shock, surprise | 反义词：calm, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud thunder outstartles the dog. = 响亮的雷声吓到了狗。
The unexpected news outstartles her. =  意外的消息让她大吃一惊。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
Nothing has outstartled me more than this. =  没有什么比这更让我震惊了。
