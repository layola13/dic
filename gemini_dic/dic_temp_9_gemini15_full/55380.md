distemperately:/dɪsˈtɛmpərətli/| adv.|脾气暴躁地；性情乖戾地；无节制地|In a manner characterized by a lack of self-control or moderation; irascibly; ill-temperedly.  It describes actions or behavior marked by irritability, anger, or uncontrolled passion.|例句：He reacted distemperately to the news, shouting and throwing things. 他对这个消息反应过度，又喊又叫，还扔东西。  She spoke distemperately, her words laced with anger and resentment. 她说话冲动，言语中充满了愤怒和怨恨。|近义词：irritably, irascibly, angrily, intemperately, furiously|反义词：calmly, serenely, patiently, temperately
