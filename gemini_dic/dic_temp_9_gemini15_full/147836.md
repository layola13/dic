overpunishment: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃmənt/ | n. |过度惩罚| Excessive punishment; punishment that is too severe or inappropriate for the offense.  It often leads to negative consequences, such as resentment, rebellion, or learned helplessness. |例句：The overpunishment of children can lead to behavioral problems later in life.  过度惩罚孩子可能会导致他们在以后生活中出现行为问题。

例句：His overpunishment for a minor infraction was deemed unfair by many. 他因轻微违规而受到的过度惩罚被许多人认为是不公平的。


近义词：excessive punishment, harsh punishment, undue punishment, cruel punishment| 反义词：leniency, forgiveness, understanding, tolerance | 词性: noun
