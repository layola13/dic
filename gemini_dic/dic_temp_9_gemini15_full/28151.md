capitulum:/kəˈpɪtʃʊləm/| n. | 小头；(植物的)头状花序；章节；小篇 | A capitulum is a type of inflorescence in which many small flowers are clustered together on a common receptacle, forming a dense head.  It is also used to refer to a small chapter or section of a book.  | Example: The daisy has a capitulum of many tiny florets.  雏菊有一个由许多小花组成的头状花序。 The final capitulum of the book summarized the key findings. 这本书的最后一章总结了主要发现。 |近义词：head, chapter, section |反义词：whole, entire
