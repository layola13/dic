pneumophora:/njuːməˈfɔːrə/| n. |气孔菌属|A genus of fungi characterized by the presence of pores or tubes on the underside of the fruiting body, similar to polypores.  They are often found on decaying wood and contribute to wood decomposition.|例句：The researcher identified several species of *pneumophora* growing on the fallen log. 研究人员在倒下的原木上发现了数种气孔菌属的菌类。|近义词：polypore (more general term)|反义词：N/A
