mass-product:/ˈmæs ˈprɒdʌkt/| n. |批量生产的产品；大规模生产的产品|Goods produced in large quantities using standardized methods.  Mass production is characterized by efficiency and lower costs per unit. |例句：The company's success is attributed to its ability to mass-produce high-quality products at low cost.  这家公司的成功归功于其低成本大规模生产高质量产品的能力。

例句：Many modern conveniences are the result of mass-product manufacturing techniques. 许多现代便利设施都是大规模生产制造技术的结果。|近义词：manufactured goods, factory-made goods |反义词：handmade goods, bespoke products |词性:noun
