heathendom:/ˈhiːðənˌdʌm/| n. |异教徒社会；异教；异教徒的土地|The state or condition of being a heathen; the territory or people of heathens; the practices or beliefs of heathens. Heathendom refers to the lands, people, or practices associated with paganism or non-Christian religions, often implying a lack of civilization or moral refinement from a Christian perspective.  |The missionary aimed to bring Christianity to the heathendom of the far north.|近义词：paganism, idolatry,  heathenry|反义词：Christianity, Christendom
