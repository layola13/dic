palatals:/ˈpælətəlz/| n. |硬颚音，腭音|Sounds produced with the tongue contacting the hard palate (the roof of the mouth).  They include sounds like /j/ (as in "yes"), /ʃ/ (as in "ship"), /ʒ/ (as in "treasure"), /tʃ/ (as in "church"), and /dʒ/ (as in "judge").|The pronunciation of palatals varies depending on the language and dialect.|近义词：palatal sounds|反义词：velar sounds
