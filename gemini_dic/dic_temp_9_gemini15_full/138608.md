nuciform:/ˈnuːsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|核形的，坚果形的|Resembling a nut in shape or form; nut-shaped.|The nuciform seeds were difficult to crack open.  核形的种子很难打开。The drupe has a nuciform kernel.  核果有一个核形的仁。|近义词：nut-shaped, gland-shaped|反义词：elongated, tubular
