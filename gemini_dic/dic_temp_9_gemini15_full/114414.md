make-one's-hair-stand-on-end: /meɪk wʌnz her stænd ɒn end/ | phrasal verb | 使人毛骨悚然；令人不寒而栗 | To cause someone to feel extremely frightened or shocked.  The phrase evokes the feeling of one's hair literally rising due to intense fear or surprise. |


例句：

The horror movie made my hair stand on end.  = 那部恐怖电影让我毛骨悚然。

The news report about the accident made her hair stand on end. = 关于事故的新闻报道让她不寒而栗。


近义词：terrify, horrify, frighten, scare, shock


反义词：calm, soothe, reassure, comfort


词性：phrasal verb
