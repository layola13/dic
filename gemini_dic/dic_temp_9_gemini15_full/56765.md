dopamine:/ˈdoʊpəmiːn/|/ˈdəʊpəmɪn/| n. |多巴胺|A neurotransmitter released into the synapse (the gap between nerve cells) to transmit impulses from one nerve to another.  It is involved in many functions of the brain and body, including mood, reward, motivation, and movement. |例句：Dopamine is a neurotransmitter that plays a crucial role in reward and motivation. 多巴胺是一种神经递质，在奖赏和动机中起着至关重要的作用。|例句：Low levels of dopamine are associated with Parkinson's disease. 多巴胺水平低与帕金森氏症有关。|近义词：neurotransmitter, catecholamine|反义词：N/A
