permeates:/ˈpɜːmieɪts/| vt.|弥漫；渗透；遍布|To spread throughout (something); to penetrate or pass into.|The aroma of freshly brewed coffee permeated the air.  新鲜煮好的咖啡香气弥漫在空气中。

The influence of social media permeates every aspect of modern life. 社交媒体的影响渗透到现代生活的方方面面。|pervade, penetrate, infuse|isolate, separate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma permeates the room.  香气弥漫房间。
Fear permeates the hearts of the villagers. 恐惧弥漫在村民的心中。

一般过去时:
The scent of lavender permeated the entire house.  薰衣草的香味弥漫了整个房子。
Doubt permeated his thoughts. 怀疑充满了他的思想。

现在完成时:
The idea has permeated the culture.  这个想法已经渗透到文化中。
Disappointment has permeated her being. 失望已经渗透到她的身心。

过去完成时:
The news had permeated the community before the official announcement. 官方宣布之前，消息已在社区中传开。
Anxiety had permeated her every action. 焦虑已渗透到她的每一个行动中。

将来时:
The new technology will permeate all aspects of our lives. 新技术将渗透到我们生活的方方面面。
This philosophy will permeate the company's operations.  这种理念将渗透到公司的运作中。

虚拟语气:
If the poison permeated his system, he would be in danger. 如果毒药渗透了他的身体系统，他就会有危险。
I wish this positive attitude permeated our workplace. 我希望这种积极的态度能渗透到我们的工作场所。

被动语态:
The fabric was permeated with water. 布料被水浸透了。
The society was permeated by a sense of unease. 社会弥漫着不安的情绪。

疑问句:
Does the scent permeate the entire building? 这气味是否弥漫了整栋大楼？
Will the new policy permeate all departments? 新政策会渗透到所有部门吗？

否定句:
The happiness didn't permeate the atmosphere. 幸福并没有弥漫在气氛中。
The message didn't permeate the entire organization.  信息并没有传遍整个组织。
