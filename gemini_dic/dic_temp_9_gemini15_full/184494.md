restarter:/riˈstɑːrtər/ | n. |重启器，重新启动者 |A person or thing that restarts something;  a device that restarts a machine or process.  |例句：The system's restarter automatically brought it back online after the power failure.  系统的重启器在电源故障后自动将其恢复在线。

例句：He's a restarter, always looking for new projects after finishing the old ones. 他是个重新开始者，总是在完成旧项目后寻找新项目。


近义词：rebooter, initiator, relauncher | 反义词：stopper, terminator


词性：名词
