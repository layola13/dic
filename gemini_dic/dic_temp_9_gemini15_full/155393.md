phased:/feɪzd/ | adj. | 分阶段的，逐步的 |  Consisting of or done in phases or stages; gradual.  | The project will be phased in over the next three years.  这个项目将在未来三年内分阶段实施。The withdrawal of troops was phased over several months. 撤军分几个阶段进行。 | gradual, step-by-step, staged | abrupt, sudden, immediate | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is phased in gradually. = 项目正在逐步分阶段实施。
This task is phased into several steps. =这项任务被分成几个步骤。


一般过去时:
The old system was phased out last year. = 旧系统去年逐步淘汰了。
They phased the new software in over a period of six months. = 他们在六个月的时间里逐步引入了新软件。


现在完成时:
We have phased out the use of harmful chemicals. = 我们已经逐步淘汰了有害化学品的用途。
The company has phased in a new management system. = 公司已经逐步引入了新的管理系统。


过去完成时:
They had phased out the old equipment before the new arrived. = 新设备到达之前，他们已经淘汰了旧设备。
The project had been phased in three stages before the final review. = 在最终审查之前，该项目已经分三个阶段实施。


将来时:
The new policy will be phased in next year. = 新政策将在明年分阶段实施。
We will phase out the outdated technology by the end of the year. = 我们将在年底前淘汰过时的技术。


虚拟语气:
If the project were phased in more slowly, it would be less disruptive. = 如果项目分阶段实施得更慢一些，破坏性就会更小。
I wish the changes had been phased in more gradually. = 我希望这些变化能够更逐步地实施。


被动语态:
The changes were phased in over a period of two years. = 这些变化在两年内分阶段实施。
The old machines are being phased out. = 旧机器正在逐步淘汰。


疑问句:
Is the new software being phased in yet? = 新软件正在逐步引入吗？
How will the transition be phased? = 这个过渡将如何分阶段进行？


否定句:
The changes were not phased in properly. = 这些变化没有得到妥善的分阶段实施。
The new system will not be phased in until next year. = 新系统要到明年才会分阶段实施。



PHRASE->

phased rollout = 分阶段推出
phased withdrawal = 分阶段撤出
phased implementation = 分阶段实施
phase out = 逐步淘汰
