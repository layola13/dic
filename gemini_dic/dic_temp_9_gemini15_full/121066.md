misconstruct:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkt/| vt.|误解，曲解；建造错误|To understand or interpret wrongly; to build incorrectly.  |例句：He misconstrued my silence as agreement. 他误解了我的沉默，认为我同意了。|例句：The bridge was misconstructed and collapsed after only a few years. 这座桥梁建造错误，几年后就坍塌了。|近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand, misconceive |反义词：understand, interpret correctly, construct correctly |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misconstruct my intentions. = 他们误解了我的意图。
He misconstructs the sentence. = 他曲解了这句话的意思。

一般过去时:
She misconstrued his remarks. = 她误解了他的话。
The builders misconstructed the wall. = 建造者把墙建错了。

现在完成时:
