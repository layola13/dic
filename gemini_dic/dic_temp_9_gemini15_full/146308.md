overfeasting: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːstɪŋ/ | n. |暴饮暴食；过量饮食 | The act of eating too much food at one time.  It often leads to discomfort and potential health problems. | Example: Overfeasting on Thanksgiving is a common occurrence.  感恩节暴饮暴食是很常见的事。  The overfeasting led to indigestion and a terrible stomach ache. 暴饮暴食导致消化不良和剧烈的胃痛。|近义词：gluttony, gorging, stuffing |反义词：fasting, dieting, starving | 名词

词源:

The word "overfeasting" is a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and "feasting" (the act of eating a large meal, especially a celebratory one).  "Feast" itself originates from Old French "feste" and ultimately from Latin "festa" (a festival or holiday).
