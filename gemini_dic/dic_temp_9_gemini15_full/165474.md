preliterate:/ˌpriːˈlɪtəreɪt/| adj.| 文盲的；文字产生前的；|Existing or occurring before the development of writing or literacy.  Describes societies or periods that lacked a writing system.|例句：Preliterate societies relied on oral traditions to pass down knowledge. 文盲社会依靠口头传统传承知识。The archaeological findings offer a glimpse into preliterate cultures. 考古发现让我们得以一瞥前文字文化。|近义词：illiterate, unlettered, nonliterate|反义词：literate
