USAGE->
This word is an interjection, and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations like other word types.  It doesn't follow typical grammatical tenses.


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases using "eww" in the same way that "what" has numerous phrases.  It's primarily used as a standalone expression.
