developmentary:/ˌdɛvəlˈɒpəntrɪ/| adj.|发展的；发育的；进化的|Relating to or characterized by development.  The word often implies a process of growth, change, or evolution.|例句：The developmental stage of the project is crucial.  这个项目的开发阶段至关重要。

例句：He showed a keen interest in the developmental biology of insects. 他对昆虫的发育生物学表现出浓厚的兴趣。


近义词：evolutionary, progressive, maturational

反义词：regressive, degenerative
