grunger:/ˈɡrʌndʒər/| n. | グラン吉风格的人；邋遢的人 | A person who embraces grunge culture, characterized by a deliberately unkempt appearance and a rejection of mainstream values.  They often wear ripped clothing, have untidy hair, and generally present a rebellious image. | Example: He was a grunger, sporting ripped jeans and a flannel shirt. 他是个不修边幅的グラン吉风格的人，穿着破洞牛仔裤和法兰绒衬衫。 |近义词：slacker, hipster (in some contexts) |反义词：preppy, neat freak
