pewters:/ˈpjuːtərz/| n.| 锡器；锡器制品|Pewters refers to objects made of pewter, a soft, malleable metal alloy consisting primarily of tin, often with additions of copper, antimony, and bismuth.  These objects can range from tableware (plates, mugs, tankards) to decorative items and even musical instruments.|The antique shop had a beautiful collection of pewters, including several ornate tankards.|近义词：tinware, metalware|反义词：无
