pyrolignous:/ˌpaɪroʊˈlɪɡnəs/| adj.|焦木的；木焦油的；|Derived from "pyro-" (fire) and "lignous" (woody), referring to substances produced by the destructive distillation of wood.|The pyrolignous acid obtained from the distillation of wood contains acetic acid and methanol.|近义词：acetous (referring to the acetic acid component)|反义词：None specifically, but terms suggesting clean, refined wood products could be considered opposite in connotation.
