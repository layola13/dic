palatopterygoid:/ˌpælətoʊˈtɛrɪɡɔɪd/| n. |腭翼骨|The palatopterygoid is a bone or cartilage in the skull of many vertebrates, particularly fishes and some amphibians. It is formed by the fusion of the palatine and pterygoid bones, contributing to the structure of the palate and jaw suspension.  The exact morphology varies widely across different species.|例句：The study examined the development and morphology of the palatopterygoid in various species of teleost fish.  这项研究检查了各种硬骨鱼类腭翼骨的发育和形态。|近义词：palatine (部分), pterygoid (部分) |反义词:|无
