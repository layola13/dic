prerecommends:/ˌpriːrɛkəˈmɛndz/ | v. |预先推荐；提前推荐 |To recommend something or someone before others have a chance to. This word is a relatively uncommon blend of "pre-" and "recommends."  It implies a proactive recommendation, often made in anticipation of a need or event. |The committee prerecommends the candidate for the position before the formal application process begins.  委员会在正式申请流程开始之前就预先推荐了该候选人。

The university prerecommends certain textbooks for incoming freshmen. 大学预先推荐了某些教科书给新生。


近义词:preselect, suggest in advance, propose beforehand
反义词:postpone recommending, delay recommending

词性:动词
