revivor:/rɪˈvaɪvər/| n.|复兴者；恢复活力的人或事物；恢复法令|A revivor is a person or thing that brings something back to life or activity, or a legal document that restores something to its former validity.|例句：The new CEO was seen as a revivor for the struggling company.  新的首席执行官被视为这家苦苦挣扎的公司的一位复兴者。

例句：The old law was revived by a revivor.  旧法律通过一项恢复法令而恢复了效力。|近义词：restorer, revitalizer, renewer|反义词：destroyer, terminator, annihilator|词性：名词
