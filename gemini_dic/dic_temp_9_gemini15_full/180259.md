refraining:/rɪˈfreɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 克制；避免；抑制 |  To refrain from doing something means to avoid doing it, usually because you think it's wrong or unwise.  | Example:  He refrained from criticizing his boss. 他避免批评他的老板。  |近义词: abstain, desist, forgo | 反义词: indulge, yield


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I refrain from eating sweets. = 我避免吃甜食。
They refrain from smoking in public. = 他们在公共场合避免吸烟。


一般过去时:
She refrained from commenting on the matter. = 她避免对这件事发表评论。
He refrained from drinking alcohol at the party. = 他在派对上避免喝酒。


现在完成时:
I have refrained from gossiping about my colleagues. = 我一直避免说同事的闲话。
We have refrained from making any hasty decisions. = 我们一直避免做出任何草率的决定。


过去完成时:
She had refrained from telling him the bad news. = 她一直避免告诉他坏消息。
They had refrained from interfering in the dispute. = 他们一直避免干涉这场争端。


将来时:
I will refrain from interrupting him again. = 我将避免再次打断他。
He will refrain from making any promises he can't keep. = 他将避免做出任何无法兑现的承诺。


虚拟语气:
I would refrain from drinking so much coffee if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会避免喝这么多咖啡。
It would be wise to refrain from making accusations without evidence. = 明智的做法是在没有证据的情况下避免指控。


被动语态:
Further action was refrained from pending further investigation. = 为了等待进一步调查，进一步的行动被避免了。
His criticism was refrained from out of respect for his seniority. = 出于尊重他的资历，对他的批评被避免了。


疑问句:
Will you refrain from using your phone during the meeting? = 你会在会议期间避免使用手机吗？
Does he always refrain from expressing his opinions? = 他总是避免表达自己的意见吗？


否定句:
He didn't refrain from expressing his anger. = 他没有克制住自己的怒火。
She couldn't refrain from laughing at the joke. = 她忍不住笑了。


PHRASE->
refrain from doing something = 避免做某事
refrain from comment = 避免评论
refrain from interfering = 避免干涉
refrain from speaking = 避免说话
refrain from drinking = 避免饮酒


词源:
The word "refrain" comes from Old French *refrener*, meaning "to bridle, restrain," from *frein*, meaning "bridle."  The suffix "-ing" forms the present participle, indicating an ongoing action.  Thus, "refraining" signifies the act of continuously holding back or restraining oneself.
