flashing:/ˈflæʃɪŋ/| adj. v.|闪光的；闪烁的；炫耀的|Quickly showing and disappearing; intermittent light.|The flashing lights of the police car warned drivers. 警车的闪烁灯警告司机。The child was flashing a bright smile. 孩子露出了灿烂的笑容。|近义词：blinking, shimmering, glittering|反义词：steady, constant, dull

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The light flashes intermittently. = 灯光间歇性闪烁。
The neon sign flashes brightly. = 霓虹灯闪烁明亮。

一般过去时:
The camera flashed and captured the moment. = 相机闪光并捕捉到了那一刻。
The lightning flashed across the sky. = 雷电划破天空。

现在完成时:
The lights have been flashing all night. = 灯光整夜都在闪烁。
The warning light has flashed several times. = 警告灯已经闪烁了几次。

过去完成时:
The light had flashed just before the power went out. = 灯光在停电前刚刚闪烁了一下。
The police car had flashed its lights to stop the speeding car. = 警车已经闪烁灯光来阻止超速行驶的汽车。

将来时:
The lights will flash when the system is activated. = 当系统启动时，灯光将会闪烁。
The fireworks will flash and boom in the night sky. = 烟花将在夜空中闪耀和轰鸣。

虚拟语气:
