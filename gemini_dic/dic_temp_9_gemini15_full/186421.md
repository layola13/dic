ribbings:/ˈrɪbɪŋz/| n.|细条，条纹，肋状物|Ribbings refer to narrow strips or bands of material, often used for reinforcement or decoration.  They can be found in various contexts, from the ribs of a fabric to the strengthening strips in a structure. | The fabric had decorative ribbings along the sleeves. 这块布料袖子上装饰着细条纹。 The concrete structure was reinforced with steel ribbings.  混凝土结构用钢筋加固。|stripes, bands, grooves|smooth, flat|名词
