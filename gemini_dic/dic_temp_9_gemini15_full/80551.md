goodreads:/ˈɡʊdˌriːdz/| n. |Goodreads |一个在线书籍评论和目录网站 |Goodreads is a social cataloging website that allows users to search its database of books, add books to their virtual shelves, and rate and review books.  Goodreads is a great resource for finding new books to read.|近义词：book review site, online book database |反义词：无
