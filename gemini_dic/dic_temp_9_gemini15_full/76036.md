furl:/fɜːrl/| v. |卷起，收拢；解雇|To roll or fold something up tightly; to dismiss someone from employment.  |例句：The sailors furled the sails. 水手们卷起了帆。|例句：The company furled several employees during the recession.  在经济衰退期间，公司解雇了几名员工。|近义词：roll up, wrap up, dismiss, discharge |反义词：unfurl, unfurl, employ, hire |动词


USAGE->
