overmodernized:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdərnaɪzd/| adj.| 过度现代化了的 | Excessively modernized; often implying a loss of traditional character or charm in the process.  The city, in its efforts to modernize, has become overmodernized, losing much of its historical aesthetic. |近义词：hypermodern, excessively modernized |反义词：traditional, antiquated
