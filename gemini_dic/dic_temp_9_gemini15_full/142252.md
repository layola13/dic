orphan:/ˈɔːrfən/| n.|孤儿|A child whose parents are dead.|例句：The orphan was adopted by a loving family.  这个孤儿被一个慈爱的家庭收养了。|近义词：waif, foundling|反义词：parent, guardian


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an orphan. = 他是一个孤儿。
She orphans many children in her charity work. = 在她的慈善工作中，她抚养了许多孤儿。(此处orphan作动词用)


一般过去时:
He was an orphan. = 他曾经是一个孤儿。
The parents died, leaving their children orphans. = 父母去世了，留下他们的孩子成了孤儿。


现在完成时:
Many children have been orphaned by the war. = 许多孩子在战争中成了孤儿。
She has orphaned many children. = 她抚养了许多孤儿。(此处orphan作动词用)


过去完成时:
He had been an orphan since he was five. = 从五岁起，他就是一个孤儿了。
Before the accident, they had never orphaned a child. = 事故发生前，他们从未抚养过孤儿。(此处orphan作动词用)


将来时:
He will be an orphan soon. = 他很快就会成为孤儿了。
If the parents die, the child will be orphaned. = 如果父母去世，孩子将成为孤儿。


虚拟语气:
If he were an orphan, he would be sad. = 如果他是孤儿，他会很伤心。
It is as if she has orphaned her own child. = 就好像她抛弃了自己的孩子一样。(此处orphan作动词用，表达一种类似孤儿状态的感觉)


被动语态:
The child was orphaned at a young age. = 这个孩子很小的时候就成了孤儿。
Many children are orphaned each year due to poverty. = 每年都有许多孩子因为贫困而成为孤儿。


疑问句:
Is he an orphan? = 他是孤儿吗？
Were they orphaned as children? = 他们小时候是孤儿吗？


否定句:
He is not an orphan. = 他不是孤儿。
The child was not orphaned. = 这个孩子没有成为孤儿。


PHRASE->
orphan train:孤儿列车

词源:
The word "orphan" comes from the Old French word "orfen," which in turn comes from the Latin word "orfanus."  The Latin word likely derives from the Greek word "orphanos," meaning "bereft of parents."
