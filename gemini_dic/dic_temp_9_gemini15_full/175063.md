radiometer:/reɪˈdiɑːmɪtər/ | n. |辐射计，辐射测定器 | An instrument for measuring the intensity of radiant energy, especially light, by the force it exerts on a surface.  It typically consists of vanes that rotate when exposed to radiation.  | Example: The scientist used a radiometer to measure the intensity of the infrared radiation emitted by the star. 科学家用辐射计测量恒星发出的红外辐射强度。 The radiometer showed a significant increase in solar radiation. 辐射计显示太阳辐射显著增加。 |近义词：actinometer, pyrometer |反义词：None |名词
