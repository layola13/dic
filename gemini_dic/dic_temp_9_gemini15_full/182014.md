remedial:/rɪˈmiːdiəl/| adj.|补救的；矫正的；治疗的 |intended to correct or improve something that is wrong or unsatisfactory|The remedial class helped students catch up on missed material.  补习班帮助学生赶上落下的课程。|近义词：corrective, restorative, curative |反义词：preventive, prophylactic
