clerkage:/ˈklɑːrkɪdʒ/| n. |书记费；办事费；职员工作|The fees paid to a clerk for services rendered, such as preparing documents or performing other administrative tasks.  It often refers to the cost associated with the official processing of documents or transactions.|The clerkage for filing the paperwork was surprisingly high.  书记费出奇的高。|The clerkage for registering the deed was included in the closing costs. 注册契据的书记费包含在结算费用中。|fee, charge, cost|
