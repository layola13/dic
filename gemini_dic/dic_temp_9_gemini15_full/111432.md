llamas:/ˈlɑːməz/| n.|羊驼|Llama is a domesticated South American camelid, valued for its soft, luxurious fiber. They are known for their gentle nature and are often kept as livestock.  |例句：The Andes mountains are home to many llamas. 安第斯山脉是许多羊驼的家园。

例句：We saw a herd of llamas grazing peacefully in the meadow. 我们看到一群羊驼正在草地上平静地吃草。


近义词：alpaca (羊驼的一种)
反义词：无


词性:名词
