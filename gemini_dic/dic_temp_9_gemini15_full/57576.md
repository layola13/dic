dramatizers:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzərz/| n. |戏剧化者；夸大其词者|People who dramatize or exaggerate events or situations. They tend to make things seem more exciting or important than they really are.  |The dramatizers in the office always added extra details to their stories, making them seem far more dramatic than they actually were.  办公室里的那些戏剧化者总是会在他们的故事中添加额外的细节，使它们看起来比实际情况要戏剧化得多。|exaggerators, sensationalists|minimalists, realists|名词
