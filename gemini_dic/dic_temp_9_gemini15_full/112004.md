lonesomeness: /ˈloʊnsəmnis/ | n. |孤寂，寂寞 | A feeling of being lonely and sad because you are alone or have no friends.  |例句：The lonesomeness of the long winter nights was almost unbearable.  漫长的冬夜的孤寂几乎令人难以忍受。

例句：Overcome with lonesomeness, she called her old friend.  被孤寂感淹没，她给她的老朋友打了电话。


近义词：loneliness, solitude, isolation | 反义词：companionship, sociability


词源:  From Middle English *loneliness, from lone + -ness. The word "lone" itself comes from Old English *lāna "alone," related to Old High German *lāni "lonely," and ultimately to the Proto-Germanic root *lānō-.
