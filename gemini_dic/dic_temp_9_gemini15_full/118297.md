mercurialize:/mɜːrˈkjuəriˌaɪz/ | vt. |使含汞；用汞处理|To treat (something) with mercury.  The process often involves coating or amalgamating a substance with mercury.|例句：The gold was mercurialized to improve its malleability.  这金子经过水银处理以提高其延展性。

例句：The dentist mercurialized the cavity before filling it. 牙医在填充龋洞之前用汞处理了它。


近义词：mercurate


反义词：无


词性：动词
