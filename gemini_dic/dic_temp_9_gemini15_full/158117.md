/ˈplætənɪst/| n. |白鳍豚|Platanist is a genus of freshwater dolphins, also known as the Indus river dolphin.  They are characterized by their long, slender beaks and poor eyesight. They are found in the Indus River system in Pakistan and India.  The species is critically endangered due to habitat loss and hunting.|The platanist is a fascinating creature, adapted to the murky waters of the Indus River.|近义词：Indus river dolphin|反义词：无
