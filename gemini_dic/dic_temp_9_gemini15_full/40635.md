conventionalism:/kənˈvɛnʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |约定俗成；习俗；传统主义|The belief that morality and knowledge are based on social convention rather than on objective reality.  It emphasizes the role of social agreement in shaping our beliefs and values.  For example, certain social norms, like table manners, are based on conventionalism rather than inherent rightness or wrongness.|The strict adherence to convention in art or literature often leads to predictable and uninspired works. 严格遵守艺术或文学中的惯例往往会导致作品平庸且缺乏灵感。|Traditionalism, formalism|Radicalism, innovation|名词
