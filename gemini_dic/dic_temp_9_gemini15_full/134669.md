nonmanufactures:/ˌnɑːnˌmænjuˈfæktʃərz/| n. |非制造业；非制造品|Industries and activities that are not involved in the production of goods.  This term often refers to the service sector, which includes things like finance, healthcare, education, and transportation.|The city's economy is increasingly reliant on nonmanufactures, with a growing service sector.  这座城市经济越来越依赖非制造业，服务业蓬勃发展。| 近义词：services, tertiary industries | 反义词：manufacturing, production
