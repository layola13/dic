preelectrical: /ˌpriːɪˈlɛktrɪkəl/ | adj. | 电气化之前的；前电气时代的 |  Relating to the period before the widespread use of electricity.  Describes the technology, practices, and social conditions that existed before electricity became commonly available. |

例句：
Preelectrical lighting relied heavily on candles and oil lamps. = 电气化之前的照明主要依靠蜡烛和油灯。

Preelectrical communication methods were significantly slower and less efficient. = 电气化之前的通讯方式要慢得多，效率也低得多。


近义词：pre-electric, antelectric (less common)

反义词：electrical, post-electrical


词性：形容词
