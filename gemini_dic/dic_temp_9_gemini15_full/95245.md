incameration: /ɪnˌkæməˈreɪʃən/ | n. |监禁；关押| The act of imprisoning or confining someone.  It refers to the state of being confined in a prison or other place of confinement. | Example: The incarceration of political prisoners sparked widespread protests.  政治犯的监禁引发了广泛的抗议。 The lengthy incarceration left him bitter and resentful. 漫长的监禁使他变得痛苦和怨恨。|近义词：imprisonment, confinement, detention |反义词：release, liberation, freedom |名词
