monogam:/ˈmɒnəɡæm/ /ˈmɑːnəɡæm/| n. adj.|一夫一妻制；一夫一妻的；专一的|The practice or state of having only one sexual partner at a time.  It can refer to a relationship, a lifestyle choice, or a biological phenomenon.|例句：He believes in monogamy and has been married to the same woman for 30 years. 他相信一夫一妻制，并且和同一个女人结婚30年了。

例句：The study examined monogamous relationships among certain bird species. 这项研究考察了某些鸟类物种中的一夫一妻制关系。


近义词：monogamous, fidelity, faithfulness|反义词：polygamy, infidelity


词性:名词，形容词
