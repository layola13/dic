disulphide:/ˈdɪsʌlfaɪd/| n. |二硫化物|A chemical compound containing two sulfur atoms bonded together.  Disulfides are found in various organic and inorganic compounds, and play important roles in biological systems, such as in proteins where they contribute to the tertiary structure. |Example:  The presence of disulfide bonds in proteins stabilizes their three-dimensional structure. 二硫键的存在稳定了蛋白质的三维结构。|近义词: disulfide bond |反义词:  N/A
