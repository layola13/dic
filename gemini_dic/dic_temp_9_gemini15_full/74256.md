例句：The foxiness of her smile captivated everyone in the room. 她迷人的笑容吸引了房间里所有的人。


近义词：cunning, shrewdness, slyness, charm, sex appeal, allure


反义词：innocence, naiveté, honesty, straightforwardness


词源:源自fox (狐狸)，体现了狐狸的狡猾和机灵特性。  The suffix "-iness" adds the meaning of "the quality or state of being."
