profanate:/ˈproʊfəneɪt/|/ˈprɒfəneɪt/| vt. |亵渎；玷污；污辱|To treat (something sacred) with contempt or disrespect.  To violate the sacredness of; desecrate.|例句：They profaned the sacred grounds by building a parking lot. 他们通过建造停车场亵渎了神圣的土地。

例句：It is considered a profanity to use God's name in vain.  滥用上帝的名字被认为是一种亵渎。


近义词：desecrate, defile, violate, pollute, abuse|反义词：revere, sanctify, consecrate|动词
