remora:/ˈrɛməɹə/| n.|印鱼，吸盘鱼|A remora is a fish that has a sucker on its head, which it uses to attach itself to larger marine animals, such as sharks or whales.  They are often found feeding on scraps of food left by their hosts.|The remora clung to the shark's belly, getting a free ride and some scraps of food.|近义词：suckerfish|反义词：无
