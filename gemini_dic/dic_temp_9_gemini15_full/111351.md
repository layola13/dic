liven:/ˈlaɪvən/ | vt. |使有生气；使活跃；使振奋|To make something more lively, interesting, or exciting.|The party livened up after he arrived.  派对在他到来后活跃起来。|The music livened the atmosphere. 音乐使气氛活跃起来。|近义词：animate, enliven, invigorate |反义词：depress, dampen, dull|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music livens the atmosphere. = 音乐使气氛活跃起来。
He livens up every party he attends. = 他参加的每个派对都被他活跃起来。

一般过去时:
The news livened her spirits. = 这个消息使她精神振奋。
The comedian's jokes livened up the show. = 喜剧演员的笑话使演出变得活跃起来。

现在完成时:
The addition of some bright colors has livened up the room. = 添加一些鲜艳的颜色使房间变得更活泼了。
Her speech has livened the debate. = 她的演讲使辩论变得活跃起来。


过去完成时:
The arrival of the band had livened up the previously dull party. = 乐队的到来使之前沉闷的派对活跃起来。
They had livened the room with colorful decorations. = 他们用色彩鲜艳的装饰使房间活跃起来。

将来时:

I think this new game will liven up the meeting. = 我认为这个新游戏会使会议活跃起来。

虚拟语气:
If you added some music, it would liven up the atmosphere. = 如果你加上一些音乐，气氛就会活跃起来。
I wish the party would liven up a bit. = 我希望派对能活跃一些。

被动语态:

否定句:
