outwrite:/ˈaʊtraɪt/| vt.|胜过(写作)|To surpass in writing; to write better than.|例句：He outwrote all his competitors in the essay contest. 他在论文竞赛中胜过所有竞争对手。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass|反义词：be outwritten by
