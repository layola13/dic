reassess:/ˌriːəˈses/| vt.|重新评估；再考虑|To assess something again, often in the light of new information or circumstances.  It involves a fresh examination and judgment of something previously evaluated. |例句：We need to reassess the risks involved in this project after the recent setbacks.  我们最近遭遇挫折后，需要重新评估这个项目中的风险。

例句：The company decided to reassess its marketing strategy after poor sales figures. 公司在销售业绩不佳后决定重新评估其营销策略。


近义词：reevaluate, reconsider, re-examine, review

反义词：accept, confirm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reassess the situation every week. = 他们每周都会重新评估形势。
We reassess our goals annually. = 我们每年都会重新评估我们的目标。

一般过去时:
The committee reassessed the budget last month. = 委员会上个月重新评估了预算。
She reassessed her career options after her promotion. = 她升职后重新评估了自己的职业选择。

现在完成时:
They have reassessed the damage caused by the storm. = 他们已经重新评估了风暴造成的损失。
We have reassessed our priorities since the pandemic. = 自疫情以来，我们已经重新评估了我们的优先事项。

过去完成时:
They had reassessed the plan before presenting it to the board. = 他们在向董事会提交计划之前已经重新评估了该计划。
She had reassessed her life choices before making a major decision. = 在做出重大决定之前，她已经重新评估了自己的生活选择。

将来时:
They will reassess the project's feasibility next week. = 他们下周将重新评估项目的可行性。
We will reassess the situation once we have more data. = 一旦我们有了更多数据，我们将重新评估形势。

虚拟语气:
If we had reassessed the situation earlier, we could have avoided the crisis. = 如果我们早点重新评估形势，就能避免这场危机。
I wish I had reassessed my options before accepting the job. = 我希望在接受这份工作之前，我已经重新评估了自己的选择。

被动语态:
The project's timeline was reassessed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，项目的期限被重新评估了。
The budget will be reassessed at the end of the quarter. = 预算将在本季度末重新评估。

疑问句:
Will they reassess the policy? = 他们会重新评估这项政策吗？
Should we reassess our marketing strategy? = 我们应该重新评估我们的营销策略吗？

否定句:
They did not reassess the risks. = 他们没有重新评估风险。
