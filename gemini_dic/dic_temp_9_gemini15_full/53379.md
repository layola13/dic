disagreeable:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbl/| adj.|令人不快的；讨厌的；不友好的|Unpleasant, offensive, or disliked.  It refers to something or someone that is not enjoyable or causes displeasure. This can be due to various factors, including personality, weather, or a situation.|例句：The weather was disagreeable, cold and rainy.  天气令人不快，又冷又下雨。

The meeting was disagreeable, full of arguments and shouting. 会议令人不快，充满了争吵和喊叫。|近义词：unpleasant, offensive, obnoxious, repellent|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, delightful, enjoyable|词性:形容词
