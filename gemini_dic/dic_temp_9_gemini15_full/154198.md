periosteum:/ˌpɛriˈɒstiəm/| n. |骨膜|The periosteum is a membrane that covers the outer surface of all bones, except at the joints of long bones and where tendons and ligaments attach. It contains blood vessels and nerves that supply the bone, and plays a crucial role in bone growth, repair, and nutrition.  |The periosteum is essential for bone health and healing. |近义词：bone membrane|反义词：None
