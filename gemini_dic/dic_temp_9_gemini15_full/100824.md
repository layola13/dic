introduces:/ˌɪntrəˈduːsɪz/| v. |介绍；引进；提出；使初步了解 | To make someone or something known to someone else; to bring something into use or existence. |例句：He introduced me to his parents. 他把我介绍给了他的父母。  |例句：The company introduced a new product last year.  去年公司推出了一款新产品。|近义词：presents, initiates, submits |反义词：conceals, hides, withdraws


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher introduces a new topic each week. = 老师每周介绍一个新话题。
This book introduces the basic principles of physics. = 这本书介绍了物理学的基本原理。


一般过去时:
She introduced her boyfriend to her friends. = 她把她男朋友介绍给了她的朋友们。
They introduced a new policy to improve efficiency. = 他们引入了一项新政策来提高效率。


现在完成时:
The company has introduced several new products this year. = 公司今年已经推出几款新产品了。
He has introduced himself to the audience. = 他已经向观众做了自我介绍。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had introduced herself to the organizers. = 在演示文稿之前，她已经向组织者做了自我介绍。
They had introduced the bill to the parliament before the debate. = 他们在辩论之前已经向议会提交了该法案。


将来时:
The speaker will introduce the main points of his speech. = 演讲者将介绍他演讲的主要内容。
The government will introduce new regulations next month. = 政府下个月将推出新的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If I were to introduce myself, I would say my name is John. = 如果我要自我介绍，我会说我叫约翰。
If he had introduced his ideas earlier, they might have been accepted. = 如果他早点提出他的想法，他们可能已经被接受了。


被动语态:
A new bill was introduced to the Congress. = 一项新法案被提交给了国会。
The new technology was introduced by a small startup company. = 这项新技术是由一家小型创业公司推出的。


疑问句:
Who introduced you to this job? = 谁把你介绍到这份工作的？
Will the CEO introduce the new strategy? = 首席执行官会介绍新的战略吗？


否定句:
He didn't introduce me to his wife. = 他没有把我介绍给他妻子。
The company hasn't introduced any new features this year. = 公司今年没有推出任何新功能。


PHRASE->
