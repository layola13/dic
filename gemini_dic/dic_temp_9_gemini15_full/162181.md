pre-manifest:/ˌpriːˈmænɪfɛst/| adj.| 预先清单的；预先申报的 |  Describing something that occurs or is submitted before a formal manifest.  It typically refers to documents or information provided in advance of a shipment or other formal declaration.  | 例句：The pre-manifest data allowed customs officials to expedite the processing of the cargo. = 预先清单数据使海关官员能够加快货物的处理速度。

The airline requires pre-manifest passenger information for security purposes. = 航空公司出于安全考虑需要预先申报乘客信息。


近义词：advance, preliminary, prior

反义词：post-manifest, subsequent


词性:形容词
