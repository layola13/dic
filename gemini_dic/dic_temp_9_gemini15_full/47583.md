debaser:/dɪˈbeɪsər/ | n. |贬低者；降低价值者 |One who debases something, especially currency or moral standards.  A person or thing that lowers the quality or value of something. |例句：The government's actions were seen as the work of a debaser of the currency.  政府的行为被视为是贬低货币的人所为。

例句：He is a debaser of artistic standards, churning out cheap imitations. 他是一个降低艺术标准的人，制作廉价的仿制品。


近义词：corrupter, degrader, depreciator, adulterator |反义词：enhancer, improver, refiner, purifier


词源:  From "debase" + "-er". "Debase" itself comes from Old French *debaser, from *de- (down) + *baser (to lower).  Ultimately related to the Latin word *bassus (low).
