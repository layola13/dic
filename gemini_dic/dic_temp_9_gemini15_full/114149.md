The adjective describes something related to Mahayana Buddhism. |例句：Many Mahayana Buddhists practice compassion and emptiness. 许多大乘佛教徒奉行慈悲和空性。  The Mahayana tradition emphasizes the Bodhisattva path. 大乘传统强调菩萨道。|近义词：Buddhist (general term) |反义词：Theravadin (referring to Theravada Buddhism)
