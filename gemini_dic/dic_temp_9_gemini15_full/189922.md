san:/sæn/|/n.|三，三个；圣人；圣徒；（旧金山）圣弗朗西斯科|The word "san" has multiple meanings and origins.  It can be a shortened form of "saint" (圣人), a numerical term meaning "three", or a part of place names such as San Francisco. |Example:  There are three apples. = 有三个苹果。

San Francisco is a beautiful city. = 旧金山是一个美丽的城市.  |近义词: three, trinity (for the numerical sense), holy, saintly (for the "saint" sense)|反义词: none (for the numerical sense), unholy, sinful (for the "saint" sense)|名词
