overreacts: /ˌoʊvərɪˈækts/ | v. |反应过度，过激反应 | To react in an excessive or disproportionate way to something. |例句：He overreacted to the minor criticism. 他对小小的批评反应过度了。|例句：She overreacted when she saw the spider, screaming and running from the room. 她看到蜘蛛时反应过度，尖叫着跑出了房间。|近义词：exaggerate, overrespond |反义词：underreact, remain calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overreacts to small problems. = 他对小问题反应过度。
They overreact to any kind of stress. = 他们对任何压力都反应过度。


一般过去时:
She overreacted to the news. = 她对这个消息反应过度。
He overreacted and shouted at his colleague. = 他反应过度，朝同事大喊。


现在完成时:
They have overreacted to the situation. = 他们对这种情况反应过度了。
She has overreacted several times this week. = 她本周已经好几次反应过度了。


过去完成时:
He had overreacted to the earlier incident. = 他对之前的事件反应过度了。
We realized that we had overreacted. = 我们意识到我们反应过度了。


将来时:
I think he will overreact if you tell him the truth. = 我想如果你告诉他真相，他会反应过度的。
She might overreact to the pressure of the deadline. = 她可能会对截止日期的压力反应过度。


虚拟语气:
If he were less sensitive, he wouldn't overreact to everything. = 如果他不那么敏感，他就不会对所有事情都反应过度。
I wish he wouldn't overreact so much. = 我希望他不要反应过度。


被动语态:
The situation was overreacted to by the media. = 媒体对这种情况反应过度了。(This usage is less common)
His response was seen as an overreaction. = 他的反应被认为是反应过度。


疑问句:
Did he overreact to the situation? = 他对这种情况反应过度了吗？
Is she prone to overreacting? = 她容易反应过度吗？


否定句:
He didn't overreact; he handled the situation calmly. = 他没有反应过度；他冷静地处理了这种情况。
She tried not to overreact to his words. = 她努力克制住对他说的话的过度反应。



PHRASE->
overreact to criticism  对批评过度反应
overreact to stress 对压力过度反应
overreact to a situation 对某种情况过度反应
overreact in a meeting 在会议上反应过度
prone to overreacting 容易反应过度
