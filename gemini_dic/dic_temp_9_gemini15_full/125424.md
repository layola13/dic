multiphased: /ˌmʌltiˈfeɪzd/ | adj. | 多阶段的，多阶段进行的 | Having or involving several phases or stages.  This adjective describes processes, projects, or systems that are composed of multiple distinct stages or phases. Each phase typically has its own set of characteristics, goals, and activities. |


例句：

The project is a multiphased undertaking, with each phase requiring careful planning and execution.  这个项目是一个多阶段的项目，每个阶段都需要仔细的计划和执行。

The multiphased approach to treatment allowed for gradual improvement and monitoring of the patient's progress. 多阶段的治疗方法允许逐渐改善和监测病人的病情进展。


近义词：staged, multi-stage, phased, sequential


反义词：single-phased, one-stage, monolithic


词源:

* **multi-**:  A prefix meaning "many" or "multiple".  源自拉丁语 "multus" (many).

* **phased**:  Derived from "phase,"  meaning a distinct stage in a process or development.  The word "phase" ultimately traces back to the Greek word "phasis," meaning "appearance" or "manifestation."
