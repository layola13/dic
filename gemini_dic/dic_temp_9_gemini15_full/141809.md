orbitally:/ˈɔːrbɪtəli/ | adj. | 轨道地；绕轨道地 | Relating to or characteristic of an orbit.  Describes something concerning the path of an object around another, often a celestial body.  | Example: The orbitally decaying satellite will soon re-enter the atmosphere.  这个轨道衰减的卫星很快就会重新进入大气层。  The planet's orbitally inclined axis is responsible for its seasons. 这颗行星轨道倾斜的轴线是造成其季节变化的原因。|近义词：rotationally, circumferentially | 反义词：linearly, directly.
