savannah:/səˈvænə/| n.|热带稀树草原|A large, flat area of land covered with grass and only a few trees.  It is characterized by its dry season and wet season, and supports a wide variety of wildlife.|The African savannah is home to lions, elephants, and giraffes.|近义词：grassland, plain|反义词：forest, jungle|名词
