sell:/sɛl/| /sel/| vt.|出售，销售；卖掉；推销；使…畅销|to exchange (something) for money; to persuade someone to buy something; to be sold easily |例句：They sell fresh fruit and vegetables. 他们出售新鲜水果和蔬菜。

They managed to sell the house quickly.他们设法很快卖掉了房子。


近义词：trade, market, peddle, vend|反义词：buy, purchase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sells handmade jewelry. = 她卖手工珠宝。
The store sells a wide variety of products. = 这家商店销售各种各样的产品。


一般过去时:
He sold his car last week. = 他上周卖掉了他的车。
We sold all our tickets. = 我们把所有的票都卖掉了。


现在完成时:
They have sold over 1000 copies of the book. = 他们已经卖出了1000多本这本书。
She has sold her house and moved to the countryside. = 她已经卖掉了房子，搬到了乡下。


过去完成时:
They had sold all the tickets before the concert started. = 音乐会开始前，他们已经把所有的票都卖掉了。
He had sold his business before he retired. = 他退休前已经卖掉了他的生意。


将来时:
I will sell my old bicycle tomorrow. = 我明天将卖掉我的旧自行车。
They will sell their house next year. = 他们明年将卖掉他们的房子。


虚拟语气:
If I sold my car, I would have enough money for a vacation. = 如果我卖掉我的车，我就有足够的钱去度假了。
I wish I could sell this painting. = 我希望我能卖掉这幅画。


被动语态:
Many products are sold online. = 许多产品都在网上销售。
The tickets were sold out quickly. = 票很快卖完了。


疑问句:
Do you sell coffee? = 你卖咖啡吗？
Where do they sell these books? = 他们在哪里卖这些书？


否定句:
We don't sell alcohol. = 我们不卖酒。
The shop didn't sell any bread this morning. = 这家店今天早上没卖任何面包。


PHRASE->
sell out = 卖光
sell off = 廉价出售
sell up = 变卖财产
sell short = 低估
sell oneself = 自卖自夸
