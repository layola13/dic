huskiest: /ˈhʌskɪəst/ | adj. | 最粗犷的；声音最沙哑的；毛皮最浓密的 |

英文解析:The superlative form of "husky," meaning having a deep, rough voice; strong and powerfully built; or having thick fur.  It implies the highest degree of these qualities.

例句:
He had the huskiest voice I'd ever heard. = 我听过的声音里，他的最沙哑。

That Siberian husky is the huskiest dog in the competition. = 那只西伯利亚雪橇犬是比赛中最强壮的狗。


近义词:  deepest (voice), strongest, thickest (fur), most powerful

反义词:  thinnest (fur), weakest, highest-pitched (voice), most delicate


词性: adjective (形容词)
