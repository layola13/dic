recessionary:/rɪˈseʃənəri/ | adj. | 经济衰退的；萧条的 | Relating to or characteristic of a recession.  The term often describes economic conditions marked by falling demand, production, and employment. |例句：The recessionary pressures are forcing many businesses to cut costs. 经济衰退的压力迫使许多企业削减成本。
例句：We are seeing recessionary trends in the housing market. 我们在房地产市场上看到了经济衰退的趋势。|近义词：depressive, deflationary |反义词：expansionary, inflationary
