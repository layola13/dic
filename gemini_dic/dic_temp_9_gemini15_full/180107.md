reflowers:/riːˈflaʊərz/| v.|再次开花；重新开花|To bloom again after a period of dormancy or after being cut back.|The roses reflowered after a late frost.  玫瑰在晚霜后再次开花。|The plants reflowered beautifully after being pruned.  这些植物修剪后又漂亮地开花了。|rebloom, blossom again|wither, die|动词
