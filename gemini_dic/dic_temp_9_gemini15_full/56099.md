doctorer:/ˈdɒktərə(r)/| vt.|篡改；伪造；窜改|To alter or falsify something, especially a document or record, in order to deceive.|例句：He was accused of doctoring the accounts to hide the company's losses. 他被指控篡改账目以掩盖公司的损失。  例句：They doctor the photos to make them look more attractive. 他们篡改照片使其看起来更吸引人。|近义词：falsify, tamper with, manipulate, adulterate|反义词：authenticate, verify


USAGE->

He doctorers the evidence to support his claim. = 他篡改证据以支持他的说法。
She doctorers the images before posting them online. = 她在网上发布图片之前会对其进行修改。
