competitiveness:/ˌkɒmpɛtɪtɪvnəs/ /ˌkɑːmpɛtɪtɪvnəs/ | n. |竞争力，竞争性 |The ability of a company, country, or individual to compete successfully.  This involves factors such as efficiency, innovation, and the quality of goods and services. |例句：The company's competitiveness has improved significantly in recent years.  这家公司的竞争力近年来显著提高。

例句：Increased global competitiveness requires continuous innovation. 全球竞争加剧需要持续创新。


近义词：competitiveness, rivalry, contention, competition | 反义词：uncompetitiveness, inferiority
