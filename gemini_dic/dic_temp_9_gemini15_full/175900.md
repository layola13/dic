ratifiable:/ˈrædɪfaɪəbl/| adj.| 可批准的；可认可的 | Capable of being ratified; able to be confirmed or approved formally.  | Example: The treaty is ratifiable by the Senate next week.  下周参议院可以批准该条约。The proposed amendment is ratifiable by a two-thirds vote.  该提议的修正案可以通过三分之二的投票获得批准。|近义词：approvable, confirmable |反义词：unratifiable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The treaty is ratifiable by the Senate. = 该条约可由参议院批准。
This agreement is ratifiable under current law. = 根据现行法律，这项协议是可以批准的。


一般过去时:
The contract was ratifiable under the previous administration. = 在上届政府执政期间，该合同是可以批准的。
The amendment was ratifiable until the deadline passed. = 在截止日期之前，该修正案是可以批准的。


现在完成时:
The changes have been ratifiable since the new policy was implemented. = 自实施新政策以来，这些变更一直是可以批准的。
The proposal has been ratifiable for months, but no action has been taken. = 这项提案已经可以批准好几个月了，但没有采取任何行动。


过去完成时:
The agreement had been ratifiable before the dispute arose. = 在争议出现之前，该协议是可以批准的。
The treaty had been ratifiable, but the circumstances changed. = 该条约是可以批准的，但情况发生了变化。


将来时:
The bill will be ratifiable once it passes the House. = 该法案一旦通过众议院，即可批准。
The decision will be ratifiable after further review. = 经过进一步审查后，该决定将是可以批准的。


虚拟语气:
If the treaty were ratifiable, we would have signed it already. = 如果该条约可以批准，我们早就签署了。
I wish the contract were ratifiable so we could proceed. = 我希望该合同是可以批准的，这样我们就可以继续进行下去了。


被动语态:
The amendment was found to be ratifiable by the legal team. = 法律团队认定该修正案是可以批准的。
The resolution is being examined to determine if it is ratifiable. = 正在审查该决议，以确定其是否可以批准。


疑问句:
Is the agreement ratifiable under international law? = 根据国际法，这项协议是可以批准的吗？
Is this amendment ratifiable without further debate? = 该修正案无需进一步辩论即可批准吗？


否定句:
The proposed changes are not ratifiable in their current form. = 拟议的变更以其目前的形式是无法批准的。
The contract is not ratifiable without the necessary signatures. = 没有必要的签字，合同是无法批准的。

词源:
From *ratify* + *-able*.  由 ratify（批准）+ -able（可…的）构成。  Ratify itself comes from Latin *ratificāre* "to confirm," from *rātus* "ratified, approved" + *facere* "to make, do."  Ratify 本身来自拉丁语 *ratificāre*，“确认”，来自 *rātus* “批准的，认可的” + *facere* “制作，做”。
