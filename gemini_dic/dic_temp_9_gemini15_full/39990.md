consult:/kənˈsʌlt/| vt. |咨询，请教；查阅；商量|To seek information or advice from (someone), especially an expert.|例句：I consulted a doctor about my headache. 我因为头痛去看医生了。|例句：She consulted the dictionary to check the spelling. 她查字典以核实拼写。|近义词：ask, seek advice from, refer to|反义词：ignore, disregard|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She consults her doctor regularly. = 她定期咨询她的医生。
We consult the manual before using the machine. = 我们在使用机器之前会查阅说明书。


一般过去时:
He consulted a lawyer about his legal problem. = 他就他的法律问题咨询了一位律师。
I consulted my notes before the exam. = 我考试前查阅了我的笔记。


现在完成时:
I have consulted several experts on this matter. = 我已经就此事咨询了几位专家。
They have consulted the map and found the correct route. = 他们查阅了地图，找到了正确的路线。


过去完成时:
She had consulted a therapist before making the decision. = 在做决定之前，她已经咨询过治疗师。
We had consulted the historical records and found the answer. = 我们查阅了历史记录，找到了答案。


将来时:
I will consult my financial advisor before investing. = 我会在投资前咨询我的理财顾问。
They will consult the weather forecast before planning the trip. = 他们会在计划旅行之前查询天气预报。


虚拟语气:
If I had consulted an expert, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我当时咨询了专家，我就不会犯那个错误。
It would be wise to consult your parents before making such a big decision. = 在做如此重大的决定之前，最好咨询一下你的父母。


被动语态:
The doctor was consulted by many patients. = 许多病人咨询了这位医生。
The manual should be consulted before operating the equipment. = 操作设备前应查阅说明书。


疑问句:
Should I consult a specialist? = 我应该咨询专家吗？
Have you consulted your family about this? = 你就此事咨询过你的家人了吗？


否定句:
He didn't consult anyone before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前没有咨询任何人。
She didn't consult the map, so she got lost. = 她没有查地图，所以迷路了。
