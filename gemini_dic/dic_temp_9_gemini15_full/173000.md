quantize:/ˈkwɒntaɪz/ /ˈkwɑːntaɪz/| vt.| 量化；量子化 | To represent (something, especially a continuous range of values) by a set of discrete values; to convert an analog signal to a digital signal.  | 例句：The software quantizes the image to reduce file size.  软件将图像量化以减小文件大小。| 例句：The audio signal was quantized before being stored digitally. 音频信号在数字存储之前被量化。|近义词：discretize, digitize|反义词：analogize
