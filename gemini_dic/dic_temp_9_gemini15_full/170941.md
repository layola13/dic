pseudonymity:/ˈsuːdəˈnɪmɪti/| n. |假名；匿名；化名 |The state or fact of using a pseudonym; the condition of being pseudonymous.  Pseudonymity allows authors to explore controversial topics or protect their privacy.  | 例句：The artist worked under a pseudonym to avoid public scrutiny.  这位艺术家化名工作以避免公众的审查。

The use of a pseudonym provides a degree of anonymity. 使用化名提供了一定程度的匿名性。|近义词：anonymity,  masking,  concealment |反义词：identity,  openness,  transparency
