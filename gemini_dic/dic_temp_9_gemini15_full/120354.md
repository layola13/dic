minimising: /ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 最小化；使减到最小 |  Reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  | 例句：The company is focusing on minimising its environmental impact. = 公司正专注于最大限度地减少其对环境的影响。


例句：We are working hard to minimise the risks involved. = 我们正在努力将所涉及的风险降到最低。


近义词：reduce, lessen, decrease, curtail, downsize


反义词：maximise, increase, enlarge, amplify


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They minimise their expenses. = 他们尽量减少开支。
We minimise the risk of error. = 我们最大限度地降低出错的风险。


一般过去时:
He minimised the damage. = 他将损失降到了最低。
She minimised her role in the incident. = 她将自己在事件中的作用降到了最低。


现在完成时:
They have minimised their carbon footprint. = 他们已经将碳足迹降到了最低。
We have minimised the disruption to services. = 我们已经将对服务的干扰降到了最低。


过去完成时:
They had minimised their losses before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经将损失降到了最低。
She had minimised the importance of the evidence. = 她之前已经淡化了证据的重要性。


将来时:
We will minimise the impact of the changes. = 我们将尽量减少这些变化的影响。
They will minimise their exposure to the risk. = 他们将尽量减少接触风险。


虚拟语气:
If we minimised our spending, we could save more money. = 如果我们减少开支，就能节省更多钱。
I wish they had minimised the noise. = 我希望他们当时能把噪音降到最低。


被动语态:
The risks were minimised by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险得到了最大程度的降低。
The damage could be minimised by quick action. = 通过迅速采取行动，可以将损失降到最低。


疑问句:
How can we minimise the costs? = 我们如何才能最大限度地降低成本？
Can we minimise the negative impact? = 我们能否最大限度地减少负面影响？


否定句:
They did not minimise the problem. = 他们没有将问题最小化。
We cannot minimise the importance of this issue. = 我们不能低估这个问题的重要性。


PHRASE->
minimise the risk = 最小化风险

minimise disruption = 减少干扰
minimise effort = 减少努力
