electroplaque:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈplæk/| n. |电板（鱼类器官）|A biological organ found in electric fish, consisting of modified muscle cells that generate an electric field.  These electric organs are used for hunting, defense, and communication. |Example: The electric eel uses its electroplaques to stun its prey. 电鳗用它的电板击晕猎物。|近义词：electrocyte (less common)|反义词：无
