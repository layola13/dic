puncher:/ˈpʌntʃər/| n. v.|冲压机；打孔器；穿孔者；拳击手；(非正式)攻击者|A person or thing that punches.  A tool or machine used for punching holes.  In informal use, someone who attacks or hits forcefully.|例句：The factory uses a powerful puncher to make holes in metal sheets.  这家工厂使用一台强大的冲压机在金属板上打孔。

He is a skilled puncher, known for his powerful left hook. 他是一位技术娴熟的拳击手，以其强大的左勾拳而闻名。|近义词：perforator, hole puncher, boxer, assailant|反义词：mender, repairer
