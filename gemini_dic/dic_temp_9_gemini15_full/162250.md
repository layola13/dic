pre-transaction: /ˌpriː trænzˈækʃən/ | adj. |交易前的，事前交易的 | Relating to the period or activities before a transaction takes place.  It often refers to processes, checks, or preparations made before a financial or commercial deal is finalized. | 例句：Pre-transaction due diligence is crucial to mitigate risks. 交易前的尽职调查对于降低风险至关重要。

例句：The pre-transaction analysis revealed several potential problems. 交易前的分析揭示了一些潜在的问题。


近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding, preliminary

反义词：post-transaction, subsequent


词性：形容词
