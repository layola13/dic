juneberry:/ˈdʒuːnˌbɛri/| n. |美洲泽米|A small, sweet, dark-purple or blue berry that ripens in June, native to North America.  The fruit is often used in jams, jellies, pies, and other desserts.|例句：The juneberries were ripe and ready for picking in early June.  六月初，美洲泽米已经成熟，可以采摘了。

例句：We made a delicious juneberry pie using the berries we picked from the woods. 我们用从树林里采摘的美洲泽米做了一个美味的派。


近义词：serviceberry (though not always a perfect synonym as different species exist under both names)


反义词：无


词性:名词
