emigration:/ˌɛmɪˈɡreɪʃən/| n. |移民（出国定居）|The act of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another;  the movement of people from one place to another.  It often involves a significant change in lifestyle and culture.|例句：Mass emigration from Ireland occurred during the potato famine. 爱尔兰在土豆饥荒期间发生了大规模移民。|例句：The emigration of skilled workers is a serious problem for the country. 熟练工人的外流对国家来说是一个严重的问题。|近义词：migration, exodus, expatriation |反义词：immigration
