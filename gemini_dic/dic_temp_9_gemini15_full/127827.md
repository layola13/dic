nematogene:/nɛˈmætədʒɛn/| n. |线虫发生物；线虫产生剂|A nematogene is an organism or substance that produces or causes the generation of nematodes.  It can refer to a host organism that supports nematode development, or a substance that stimulates nematode reproduction or growth.|Example: The rotting organic matter served as a nematogene, fostering the proliferation of nematodes in the soil.  腐烂的有机物充当了线虫发生物，促进了土壤中线虫的繁殖。|近义词：nematode producer, nematode stimulant|反义词：nematocide (线虫杀灭剂)|名词
