shaik:/ʃeɪk/| n.|谢赫，酋长|A shaik is a male leader of an Arab tribe or family.  The term is often used in the Middle East and refers to a person of high social standing and often significant wealth and influence.|例句：The shaik welcomed the delegation to his desert camp.  谢赫欢迎代表团到他的沙漠营地。|近义词：chief, sheikh, emir|反义词:subject, follower
