hogget:/ˈhɒɡɪt/| n.|小绵羊(一岁到两岁之间的绵羊)|A hogget is a sheep between one and two years old, after it has lost its first fleece but before it has become a mature sheep.  It's typically a term used in the UK and other parts of the world with a significant sheep farming industry.|例句：The farmer carefully selected the best hoggets from his flock for breeding.  这个农民仔细地从他的羊群中挑选最好的小绵羊进行繁殖。

The butcher displayed a row of prime hoggets ready for sale.  屠夫展示了一排准备出售的优质小绵羊。|近义词：sheep, lamb (younger), ewe (female), wether (castrated male)|反义词：ram (mature male sheep)
