lowery:/ˈlaʊəri/| adj.|阴沉的，阴雨的，低沉的|Dark and gloomy, especially due to rain clouds.  It suggests a feeling of depression or dreariness associated with overcast skies and impending or falling rain. |例句：The lowery sky threatened rain.  阴沉的天空预示着要下雨。

例句：The lowery atmosphere matched my mood.  阴沉的气氛与我的心情相符。

近义词：gloomy, overcast, cloudy, dismal, dreary

反义词：sunny, bright, cheerful, clear
