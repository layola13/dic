discountenance:/dɪsˈkaʊntənəns/| vt.|使丢脸；不赞成；反对；阻止|To refuse to accept or approve of something; to show disapproval of.|The headmaster discountenanced the students' unruly behavior. 校长不赞成学生们不守规矩的行为。|近义词：disapprove, condemn, forbid|反义词：approve, endorse, sanction

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He discountenances smoking in public. 他不赞成在公共场合吸烟。
The company discountenances unethical practices. 公司反对不道德的行为。

一般过去时:
The judge discountenanced the defendant's lies. 法官不相信被告的谎言。
My parents discountenanced my decision to quit my job. 我的父母不赞成我辞职的决定。

现在完成时:
The school has discountenanced bullying for years. 学校多年来一直反对欺凌。
Society has increasingly discountenanced discrimination. 社会越来越反对歧视。

过去完成时:
The court had discountenanced similar claims in the past. 法院过去曾驳回过类似的索赔。
She had discountenanced his advances for months. 她几个月来一直拒绝他的追求。


将来时:
The government will discountenance any attempt to undermine the law. 政府将阻止任何破坏法律的企图。
They will discountenance any form of corruption. 他们将反对任何形式的腐败。

虚拟语气:
If the teacher discountenanced cheating, students would study harder. 如果老师反对作弊，学生们就会更努力学习。
I wish the world would discountenance violence. 我希望世界能反对暴力。

被动语态:
His proposal was discountenanced by the committee. 他的提案被委员会否决了。
Such behavior is discountenanced by most people. 这种行为不被大多数人接受。

疑问句:
Does the company discountenance late arrivals? 公司是否反对迟到？
Would the judge discountenance perjury? 法官会不相信伪证吗？

否定句:
The principal did not discountenance the students' protest. 校长没有反对学生的抗议。
They did not discountenance his request. 他们没有拒绝他的请求。


PHRASE->
