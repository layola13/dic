prospected:/prɒˈspektɪd/| v.|勘探；勘察；寻找；预期 | To search for mineral deposits or other valuable resources; to explore a possibility or opportunity. |例句：The company prospected for gold in the mountains.  公司在山区勘探黄金。|例句：They prospected the possibility of a merger. 他们探讨了合并的可能性。|近义词：explore, investigate, survey, examine |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prospect for oil in the desert. = 他们在沙漠中勘探石油。
We prospect new markets for our products. = 我们为我们的产品寻找新的市场。


一般过去时:
The geologists prospected the area for minerals. = 地质学家勘探了该地区的矿产。
They prospected a new business opportunity. = 他们寻找了一个新的商业机会。
