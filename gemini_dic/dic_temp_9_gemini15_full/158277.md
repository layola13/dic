playability:/ˌpleɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可玩性，可操作性 | The ease with which something can be played or used, especially a game or piece of software.  It refers to how enjoyable and user-friendly something is.  | Example: The game's high playability made it a huge success.  这款游戏的极高可玩性使其获得了巨大成功。  The software's poor playability led to many user complaints.  该软件糟糕的可玩性导致许多用户抱怨。 | 近义词：usability, enjoyability, accessibility | 反义词：unplayability, difficulty, complexity | 名词
