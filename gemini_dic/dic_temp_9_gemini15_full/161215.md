postlabial:/ˌpoʊstˈleɪbiəl/| adj.|唇后的|Situated or occurring behind the lip.  This term is primarily used in anatomy and phonetics to describe structures or sounds related to the area behind the lips.| Example: Postlabial consonants are produced with the tongue positioned behind the lips. 唇后辅音是由舌头位于嘴唇后方发出的。|近义词:retrolabial|反义词:prelabial
