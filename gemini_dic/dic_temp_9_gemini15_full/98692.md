insultment:/ɪnˈsʌltmənt/| n. |侮辱；冒犯；伤害 | An insultment is a rude or offensive remark or action intended to cause hurt or anger.  It refers to the act of insulting someone, or the feeling of being insulted. | Example: His insulting remarks were an insultment to her dignity.  他的侮辱性言论是对她尊严的冒犯。  The insultment he suffered left him deeply hurt. 他遭受的侮辱让他深受伤害。|近义词：affront, offense, indignity, slight|反义词：compliment, praise, commendation|
