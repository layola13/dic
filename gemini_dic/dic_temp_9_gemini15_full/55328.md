dissuaded: /dɪˈsweɪdɪd/ | v. |劝退，劝阻|To persuade (someone) not to take a particular course of action.|例句：His friends dissuaded him from joining the army. 他的朋友劝他不要参军。|近义词：deter, discourage, prevent|反义词：encourage, persuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dissuade him from smoking. = 他们劝他戒烟。
We dissuade her from making hasty decisions. = 我们劝她不要草率决定。


一般过去时:
The doctor dissuaded him from surgery. = 医生劝他不要做手术。
My parents dissuaded me from going to that party. = 我父母劝我不要去参加那个派对。


现在完成时:
Nothing has dissuaded her from pursuing her dream. = 没有什么能阻止她追求梦想。
They have dissuaded many people from risky behavior. = 他们已经劝阻了许多人不要进行危险行为。


过去完成时:
She had already been dissuaded from the idea before the meeting. = 会议之前她就已被劝退了那个想法。
He had been dissuaded from investing in that company by his advisor. = 他的顾问已经劝他不要投资那家公司了。


将来时:
I will dissuade him from quitting his job. = 我会劝他不要辞职。
We will dissuade them from taking such a reckless risk. = 我们会劝他们不要冒如此鲁莽的风险。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would dissuade him from this foolish act. = 如果可以的话，我会劝他不要做这件蠢事。
I wish I had dissuaded her from marrying him. = 我真希望我当时劝她不要嫁给他。


被动语态:
He was dissuaded from entering the competition. = 他被劝退了比赛。
She was dissuaded by her friends from going alone. = 她被朋友劝阻独自前往。


疑问句:
Can anything dissuade him from this path? = 有什么能劝阻他走这条路吗？
What could dissuade him from his ambition? = 什么能劝退他放弃他的野心？


否定句:
Nothing could dissuade him from his goal. = 没有什么能阻止他实现目标。
They couldn't dissuade her from her determination. = 他们无法劝退她那份决心。


PHRASE->
dissuade someone from doing something = 劝某人不要做某事
be dissuaded from = 被劝阻做…
strongly dissuaded = 强烈劝阻
dissuade someone against = 劝某人反对…
have been dissuaded = 被劝退


词源:
dissuade 源自于拉丁语 dissuadere，由 dis- (否定前缀) + suadere (劝说，建议)构成。


词性:
动词
