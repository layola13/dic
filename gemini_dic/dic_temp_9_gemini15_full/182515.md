rent:/rent/| n. v.|租金；租用；出租|The noun "rent" refers to a sum of money paid for the use of something, typically property. The verb "rent" means to pay rent for something or to let something out for rent.  |例句：The rent is due on the first of the month.  这个月的房租在一号到期。

The landlord rents out apartments to students. 房东把公寓租给学生。|近义词：lease, hire, rental, charge|反义词：own, buy

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rents a small apartment near the university. 他在大学附近租了一套小公寓。
They rent out their spare room to tourists. 他们把空余的房间租给游客。

一般过去时:
I rented a car for my vacation. 我租了一辆车度假。
She rented a house by the beach last summer. 去年夏天她在海边租了一栋房子。

现在完成时:
We have rented this house for five years. 我们已经租了这栋房子五年了。
They have rented out all their properties. 他们已经把所有的房产都租出去了。

过去完成时:
He had rented the apartment before he moved in. 他在搬进去之前就已经租了那套公寓。
She had rented a car, but it broke down. 她租了一辆车，但是它坏了。

将来时:
I will rent a bigger apartment next year. 明年我将租一套更大的公寓。
They will rent out their property soon. 他们很快就会把他们的房产出租。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would rent a bigger house. 如果我有更多的钱，我会租一栋更大的房子。
I wish I could rent a car for the weekend. 我希望我能在周末租一辆车。

被动语态:
The apartment was rented quickly. 这套公寓很快就被租出去了。
The car is being rented by someone else. 这辆车正在被别人租用。

疑问句:
Do you rent your property? 你出租你的房产吗？
How much does it cost to rent a car? 租车要多少钱？

否定句:
We don't rent out our house. 我们不出租我们的房子。
He didn't rent a car because he couldn't afford it. 他没有租车，因为他负担不起。
