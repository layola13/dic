rankled:/ˈræŋkld/| v.| 使生气；使苦恼 | to make someone feel angry and resentful, especially over a period of time | The injustice of it all rankled him for years.  这其中的不公正让他多年来一直耿耿于怀。|近义词：irritate, annoy, exasperate, fester | 反义词：soothe, appease, pacify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His constant criticism rankles her. = 他不断的批评让她生气。
The unfair treatment rankles the employees. = 不公平的待遇让员工们感到愤愤不平。


一般过去时:
The delay rankled the passengers. = 延误让乘客们感到恼火。
Her words rankled him deeply. = 她的言语深深地刺痛了他。


现在完成时:
The insult has rankled with him ever since. = 从那以后，这个侮辱一直让他耿耿于怀。
His silence has rankled her for weeks. =  他的沉默让她生气了好几个星期。


过去完成时:
He had rankled over the perceived slight for months. = 他因为那次被认为是轻蔑的行为而生气了好几个月。
The injustice had rankled her since childhood. =  这种不公正让她从小就耿耿于怀。


将来时:
This decision will rankle with many people. = 这个决定将会让很多人感到不满。
His words will likely rankle with her for a long time. = 他的话很可能会让她生气很久。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been so slow, this wouldn't have rankled me so much. = 如果我不那么慢，这件事就不会让我这么生气了。
I wish that his constant interruptions hadn't rankled me so. = 我真希望他不断的打断没有让我这么生气。


被动语态:
He was rankled by their dismissive attitude. = 他被他们轻蔑的态度激怒了。
She was deeply rankled by the unfair accusation. = 她被不公平的指控深深地激怒了。


疑问句:
Does the injustice of the situation rankle you? =  这种情况的不公正是否让你感到生气？
Does his behavior rankle with anyone else? = 他的行为让其他人感到生气吗？


否定句:
The criticism didn't rankle him; he simply ignored it. = 批评并没有让他生气；他只是忽略了它。
The slight didn't rankle her as much as she thought it would. = 那点轻蔑并没有像她想象的那样让她生气。


PHRASE->
rankle deeply = 深深地激怒
rankle in one's mind = 萦绕心头
rankle with resentment = 充满怨恨
rankle with anger = 怒火中烧
rankle with injustice = 对不公正感到愤慨
