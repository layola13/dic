re-includes:/ˌriːɪnˈkluːdz/| v.|重新包含，再次纳入 |To include something again; to reintegrate something that was previously excluded.  |例句：The revised edition re-includes the chapters that were mistakenly omitted from the first print. 修订版重新收录了初版中错误遗漏的章节。

例句：The committee decided to re-include the controversial proposal on the agenda.  委员会决定将有争议的提案重新列入议程。


近义词：reincorporate, reintroduce, reinstate


反义词：exclude, omit, remove


词性：动词
