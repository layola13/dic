nonmanifestation:/ˌnɒnˌmænɪfɛsˈteɪʃən/| n. |非显现，未表现，不显现|The absence of a manifestation or symptom; the state of not being manifested or shown.  It often refers to a lack of outward expression or a hidden or latent condition.|例句：The nonmanifestation of symptoms made diagnosis difficult.  症状的不显现使得诊断变得困难。

The nonmanifestation of his anger surprised everyone. 他怒气的隐忍让所有人都感到惊讶。


近义词：latency, dormancy, concealment, suppression|反义词：manifestation, expression, revelation|名词
