debriefed:/diːˈbriːft/| v. | 汇报；详细询问；(任务完成后)向…了解情况 | To question someone about a completed mission or task in order to obtain information. |例句：The team was debriefed after their successful mission.  团队在成功完成任务后进行了汇报。

例句：The pilot was thoroughly debriefed after the emergency landing.  飞行员在紧急着陆后接受了彻底的询问。


近义词：interview, interrogate, question,  inform


反义词：brief (in the sense of giving instructions before a mission)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team debriefs after every operation. = 团队在每次行动后都会进行汇报。
He debriefs his staff weekly. = 他每周都会向他的员工了解情况。


一般过去时:
The spies were debriefed upon their return. = 间谍们回来后接受了汇报。
She debriefed the witnesses after the accident. = 事故发生后，她向目击者进行了询问。


现在完成时:
They have debriefed all the participants. = 他们已经向所有参与者了解了情况。
The police have debriefed the suspect. = 警方已经对嫌疑人进行了询问。


过去完成时:
The general had debriefed his officers before the battle. = 将军在战斗开始前已经向他的军官们进行了汇报。
She had debriefed the informant before he left. = 在线人离开之前，她已经向他了解了情况。


将来时:
We will debrief the team tomorrow. = 我们明天将对团队进行汇报。
The manager will debrief the employees next week. = 经理下周将对员工进行了解情况。


虚拟语气:
If we had debriefed the soldiers earlier, we would have avoided the mistake. = 如果我们早点向士兵们了解情况，我们就不会犯这个错误。
I wish I had debriefed him more thoroughly. = 我真希望我当时更彻底地向他了解情况。


被动语态:
The astronauts were debriefed extensively after their spacewalk. = 宇航员在太空行走后接受了广泛的汇报。
The witnesses are being debriefed by the police. = 目击者正在接受警方的询问。


疑问句:
Have you been debriefed yet? = 你已经汇报了吗？
Will the team be debriefed today? = 团队今天会进行汇报吗？


否定句:
They did not debrief the witnesses properly. = 他们没有对证人进行适当的询问。
The commander failed to debrief his troops. = 指挥官没有向他的部队进行汇报。


PHRASE->

formal debriefing = 正式汇报
