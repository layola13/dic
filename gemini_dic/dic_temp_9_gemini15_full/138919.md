nutrition:/njuːˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |营养，营养学 |The science or practice of nourishing or being nourished, especially the study of the relationship between food and health.  It encompasses the intake of food, assimilation, and utilization of nutrients by the body. |例句：Good nutrition is essential for good health. 良好的营养对健康至关重要。|例句：She's studying nutrition at university.  她在大学学习营养学。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, diet |反义词：malnutrition, starvation
