experimentative: /ɪkˌsperɪˈmɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 实验性的；尝试性的 |  Relating to or characterized by experimentation; willing to try new things.  It implies a process of trial and error, often involving innovation and risk. | 例句：He adopted an experimentative approach to his research, trying various methods until he found the most effective one. 他对他的研究采取了一种实验性的方法，尝试了各种方法，直到他找到最有效的方法。

例句：The company's experimentative marketing campaign utilized unconventional strategies to reach a wider audience.  这家公司的实验性营销活动利用非常规策略来接触更广泛的受众。


近义词：exploratory, tentative, innovative, pioneering, trial-and-error


反义词：conventional, traditional, established, proven


词性：形容词
