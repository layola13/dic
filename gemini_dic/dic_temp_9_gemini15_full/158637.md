plighter:/ˈplaɪtər/| n. |保证人；担保人；承诺者|A plighted person is someone who has made a solemn promise or pledge, often in the context of a betrothal or other serious commitment.  The word often carries a sense of formal commitment and responsibility.|例句：He was the plighter of their troth, witnessing their vows. 他是他们订婚的保证人，见证了他们的誓言。|近义词：guarantor, surety, sponsor, pledgor|反义词：breaker of promises, reneger
