polyhedrous: /ˌpɒliˈhiːdrəs/ | /ˌpɑːliˈhiːdrəs/ | adj. | 多面的；多体的 | Having many sides or faces.  The term typically refers to geometric solids. |例句：A diamond is a polyhedrous crystal.  钻石是一种多面晶体。

例句：The architect designed a polyhedrous building with many angles and facets. 建筑师设计了一座多面建筑，具有许多角度和刻面。


近义词： multifaceted, many-sided, multi-faceted


反义词： planar (in the context of geometric shapes)


词源:  From Greek πολύς (polús, "many") + ἕδρα (hédra, "seat, base").  The suffix "-ous" indicates adjectival form.  The word essentially means "having many bases" or "having many faces."
