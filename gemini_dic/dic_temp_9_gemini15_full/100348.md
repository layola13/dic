interstellar: /ˌɪntərˈstɛlər/ | adj. |星际的，星系间的 | Relating to the space between stars.  |例句：The interstellar probe sent back stunning images of distant nebulae.  星际探测器发回了令人惊叹的遥远星云图像。

例句：Scientists are searching for evidence of interstellar travel. 科学家们正在寻找星际旅行的证据。


近义词：intergalactic (more specifically referring to galaxies)


反义词：intra-stellar (within a star)


词性：形容词
