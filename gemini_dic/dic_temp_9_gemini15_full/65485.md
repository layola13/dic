PHRASE->

There are limited common phrases directly using "europeanise".  The word itself is relatively uncommon and usually used in a straightforward manner.  Instead, phrases would focus on the *process* of Europeanization:

*  Adopting European standards: 采用欧洲标准
*  Influenced by European culture: 受欧洲文化影响
*  The Europeanization of [subject]:  ……的欧洲化
*  Embracing European values: 拥抱欧洲价值观
*  Modernising with a European flair:  以欧洲风格进行现代化改造
