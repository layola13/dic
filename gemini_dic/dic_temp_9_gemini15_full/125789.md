muricated: /ˈmjʊrɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多刺的；具尖刺的 | Covered with short, sharp points or prickles.  The term often describes plants or shells. |例句：The muricated shell was difficult to handle without gloves.  = 戴着手套才能轻松地处理这个多刺的贝壳。

The fruit of the plant had a muricated surface, making it unpleasant to touch. =  这种植物的果实表面多刺，摸起来很不舒服。


近义词：spiny, thorny, prickly, bristly | 反义词：smooth, glabrous


词源：源自拉丁语 *muricātus，过去分词形式，源自 *mūrex，意思是“尖锐的物体，尖刺”。
