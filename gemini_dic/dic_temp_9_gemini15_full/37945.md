complexation:/ˌkɒmplɛkˈseɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːmplɛkˈseɪʃən/ | n. |络合，配合，复合 |The process of forming a complex; the formation of a coordination compound by the interaction of a metal ion with a ligand.  It involves the association of a central metal atom or ion with one or more ligands to form a coordination complex. |Example: The complexation of iron with cyanide ions forms a stable complex.  铁与氰离子络合形成稳定的配合物。The complexation reaction between metal ions and EDTA is widely used in analytical chemistry. 金属离子与EDTA的络合反应广泛应用于分析化学。|近义词：coordination, chelation|反义词：decomposition, dissociation|名词
