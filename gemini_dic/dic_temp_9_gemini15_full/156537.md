pick-over:/ˈpɪkˌoʊvər/ | vt. |仔细检查；挑拣；反复检查|To examine something carefully, often selecting only the best parts or removing unwanted parts.  It implies a thorough and often critical examination. |例句：She spent an hour picking over the vegetables, discarding any that were bruised. 她花了一个小时仔细挑选蔬菜，扔掉任何有伤痕的。

例句：The antiques dealer meticulously picked over the collection, searching for hidden treasures. 古董商仔细检查了收藏品，寻找隐藏的宝藏。


近义词：examine, inspect, scrutinize, sift through, cull


反义词：skim, glance over, overlook


词性:动词
