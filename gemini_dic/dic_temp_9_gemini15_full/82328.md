groveler's: /ˈɡrɑːvələrz/ | n. | 谄媚者（的） |  A groveler is someone who acts in an excessively humble and submissive way, often to gain favor or avoid punishment.  The term often implies insincerity and a lack of self-respect.  "Groveler's" is the possessive form, indicating ownership or relationship.  For example, "the groveler's pleas" refers to the pleas made by a groveler. |  例句：The groveler's apologies were unconvincing.  谄媚者的道歉令人难以置信。  The groveler's constant bowing and scraping irritated everyone. 谄媚者不停地点头哈腰，惹恼了所有人。| 近义词：flatterer's, sycophant's, toady's, fawner's | 反义词：proud person's, dignified person's | 名词
