词源:
Pornography comes from the Greek words "porne" (πόρνη), meaning "prostitute," and "graphein" (γράφειν), meaning "to write."  Therefore, literally, it means "writing about prostitutes."  Over time, the meaning broadened to encompass any material intended to arouse sexual excitement.
