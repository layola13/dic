profanity:/prəˈfænɪti/| n. |亵渎；粗话；污言秽语 |abusive or vulgar language.  It often involves words considered taboo or offensive in a particular culture or context.  The use of profanity can be considered rude, disrespectful, or even illegal depending on the setting and the words used.|例句：His speech was filled with profanity.他的演讲充满了脏话。

例句：The comedian's act included a lot of profanity, which some people found offensive.  这位喜剧演员的表演包含许多粗话，一些人觉得冒犯。|近义词：blasphemy, obscenity, vulgarity, curse words, foul language|反义词：decency, politeness, propriety
