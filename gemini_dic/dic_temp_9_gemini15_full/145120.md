overargues: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuːz/ | v. | 过分争论；反复争论 | To argue excessively or repeatedly.  | He overargues every point, making it difficult to reach a consensus. 他对每个观点都争论不休，很难达成共识。 | argue excessively, contend excessively, debate excessively | agree, concede, acquiesce | 动词
