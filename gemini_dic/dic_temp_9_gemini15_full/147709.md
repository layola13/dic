It often implies anxiety or a fear of failure. | 例句：His overpreparing for the exam led to burnout.  他的考试过度准备导致了精疲力竭。  She admitted to overpreparing for the presentation, which made her feel unnecessarily stressed. 她承认她为演示做了过度准备，这让她感到不必要的压力。 | 近义词：overdoing, excessive preparation,  exaggerated preparation | 反义词：underpreparing, insufficient preparation
