cursorary:/ˈkɜːrsəri/| adj.|粗略的；草率的；仓促的 | hasty and superficial; lacking thoroughness.  |例句：He gave the document a cursorary glance. 他粗略地看了一眼文件。
例句：Her understanding of the subject was cursorary at best.  她对这个主题的理解充其量只是粗略的。|近义词：superficial, hasty, cursory, perfunctory, brief, rapid |反义词：thorough, detailed, meticulous
