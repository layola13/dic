shareholders:/ˈʃɛərˌhoʊldərz/| n. |股东们|People who own shares in a company.  They are the owners of the company, and their ownership is proportional to the number of shares they own.|The shareholders voted to approve the merger.|近义词：stockholders, equity holders|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shareholders own a significant portion of the company. = 股东们拥有公司很大一部分股份。
Shareholders receive dividends based on company profits. = 股东根据公司利润获得股息。


一般过去时:
The shareholders approved the new CEO last year. = 股东们去年批准了新任首席执行官。
Shareholders expressed concerns about the company's financial performance. = 股东们对公司的财务业绩表示担忧。


现在完成时:
The shareholders have already approved the budget. = 股东们已经批准了预算。
Shareholders have consistently supported the company's long-term strategy. = 股东们一直支持公司的长期战略。


过去完成时:
The shareholders had already sold their shares before the stock price plummeted. = 在股价暴跌之前，股东们已经卖掉了他们的股票。
Before the meeting, the shareholders had reviewed the annual report. = 会议之前，股东们已经审查了年度报告。


将来时:
The shareholders will vote on the proposed acquisition next month. = 股东们将于下个月就拟议中的收购案进行投票。
Shareholders will likely receive a higher dividend next year. = 股东们明年可能会获得更高的股息。


虚拟语气:
If the shareholders were more involved, the company would be more successful. = 如果股东们更多地参与进来，公司将会更成功。
