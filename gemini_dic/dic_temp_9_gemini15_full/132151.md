noncurativeness:/ˌnɒnkjʊərətɪvnəs/ | n. |无效性，无疗效性 |The lack of ability to cure or heal; ineffectiveness in treatment.  This refers to a treatment or medicine that does not provide a cure.  |Example: The study highlighted the noncurativeness of the traditional herbal remedy for the disease.  这项研究强调了传统草药疗法对这种疾病的无效性。|近义词：ineffectiveness, inefficacy, futility |反义词：curativeness, efficacy, effectiveness
