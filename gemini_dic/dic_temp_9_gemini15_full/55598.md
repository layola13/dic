disuniting: /dɪsˈjuːnɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 使分裂；使不团结 |  The act of causing separation or lack of unity; to break apart or sever connections.  |例句：The political scandal is disuniting the country.  政治丑闻正在分裂这个国家。

例句：Their constant arguing is disuniting the family. 他们不断的争吵正在使这个家庭分裂。


近义词：dividing, separating, fragmenting, estranging


反义词：uniting, unifying, consolidating, harmonizing


词性：动词
