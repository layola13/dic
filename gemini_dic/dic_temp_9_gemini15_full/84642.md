hand-setting:/ˈhændˌsɛtɪŋ/| n. |手工排版，人工排字|The process of manually setting type, typically using individual type pieces.  This was the standard method of typesetting before the advent of computerized methods.|Example:  Hand-setting was a painstaking process requiring skill and precision.  手工排版是一个需要技巧和精度的费力过程。|近义词：manual typesetting, hand composition|反义词：machine typesetting, computerized typesetting|名词
