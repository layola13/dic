rebreathe:/ˌriːˈbriːð/| vt.|再次呼吸；重新呼吸|To breathe again; to breathe in again.|例句：After holding his breath underwater, he had to rebreathe quickly. 在水下屏住呼吸后，他不得不快速地重新呼吸。

例句：The diver rebreathed the recycled air from his scuba tank. 潜水员重新呼吸来自水肺潜水筒的循环空气。


近义词：inhale, breathe in again|反义词：exhale, breathe out|动词
