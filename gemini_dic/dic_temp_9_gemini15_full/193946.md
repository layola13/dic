self-essence:/selfˈɛsns/| n. | 本质，自我本质 | The self-essence refers to the fundamental nature or core being of a person or thing, often considered to be independent of external influences or perceptions. It is the intrinsic and defining characteristic that makes something what it is.  This concept is often explored in philosophy and psychology, focusing on the question of identity and what truly constitutes a self.  | Example:  He searched for his self-essence through meditation and introspection. 他通过冥想和反省来寻找他的自我本质。

The exploration of one's self-essence is a journey of self-discovery.  对自身本质的探索是一段自我发现的旅程。| 近义词：true nature, intrinsic nature, core being, identity, quintessence | 反义词：superficiality, illusion, facade, appearance | 名词
