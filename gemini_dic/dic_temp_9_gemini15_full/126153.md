mutoscope:/ˈmjuːtəskoʊp/| n. |幻影机，活动万花筒|A mutoscope is an early motion picture device that displayed a series of photographs on a revolving cylinder, creating the illusion of movement.  It was a popular form of entertainment in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, preceding the widespread adoption of film projectors.|Example: The arcade was filled with the whirring sound of mutoscopes, each displaying a different short, looping animation.  这个街机厅里充满了幻影机的嗡嗡声，每个幻影机都播放着不同的短片循环动画。|近义词：kinetoscope, zoetrope |反义词：None
