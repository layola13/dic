hyperpatriotically:/ˌhaɪpərˌpætriˈɑːtɪkli/| adv.|极端爱国地，过分爱国地|Extremely patriotic; excessively so.  It implies an excessive or unreasonable degree of patriotism.|例句：He reacted hyperpatriotically to any criticism of his country. 他对任何对其国家的批评都反应过度爱国。

例句：The politician's speech was considered hyperpatriotically charged by many. 许多人认为这位政治家的演讲充满了过分的爱国主义色彩。


近义词：ultra-patriotically, excessively patriotically, jingoistically


反义词：unpatriotically, disloyally


词性：副词
