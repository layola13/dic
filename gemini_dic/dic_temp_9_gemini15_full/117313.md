medallion:/məˈdæljən/| n.|奖章；大奖章；装饰章|A medallion is a large medal, often worn as jewelry, or a decorative object with a similar shape and design. It can be made of various materials, such as metal, stone, or plastic, and often features an engraved or embossed design.|The king awarded him a gold medallion for bravery. 国王授予他一枚金质奖章以表彰他的勇敢。She wore a beautiful silver medallion around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一枚漂亮的白银奖章。|badge, pendant, token| (no direct antonym)|名词
