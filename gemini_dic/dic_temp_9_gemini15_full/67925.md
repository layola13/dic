facer:/ˈfeɪsər/| n. |面对者，承受者，难题|A facer is a person or thing that confronts someone, especially a difficult or challenging situation or problem.  |例句：The new tax law is a real facer for small businesses. 这项新的税法对小型企业来说是一个真正的难题。|例句：The unexpected layoff was a tough facer for John.  意外的裁员对约翰来说是一个沉重的打击。|近义词：challenger, problem, difficulty, obstacle |反义词：solution, ease, relief |名词
