nonmetropolitan:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/| adj.|非都市的，非大都市的|Relating to or characteristic of areas outside of major cities or metropolitan areas.  It implies a less densely populated, often more rural, setting.|Example: The study focused on the healthcare disparities in nonmetropolitan counties. 这项研究关注的是非都市县的医疗保健差距。|近义词：rural, provincial, country, suburban (in some contexts)|反义词：metropolitan, urban
