poisonous:/ˈpɔɪzənəs/ | adj. | 有毒的；有害的 | Containing poison; capable of causing death or illness if ingested, inhaled, or absorbed through the skin.  | Example:  Many poisonous snakes live in the rainforest.  许多毒蛇生活在雨林中。

The venom of the snake is highly poisonous. 这条蛇的毒液剧毒。


近义词:toxic, venomous, noxious, harmful, deleterious | 反义词:harmless, innocuous


词源:  The word "poisonous" originates from the Old French word "poisonous," which itself derives from the Latin word "potio," meaning "a potion" or "drink."  Over time, the meaning evolved to encompass anything capable of causing harm or death through ingestion, inhalation, or absorption.
