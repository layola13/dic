mamboing:/ˈmæmboʊɪŋ/| v. |跳曼波舞；以曼波舞的节奏行动|Dancing the mambo; moving rhythmically in the style of the mambo. |例句：They were mamboing all night long. 他们整晚都在跳曼波舞。
例句：The crowd was mamboing to the infectious rhythm.  人群随着动感的节奏摇摆曼波舞。|近义词：dancing, swaying, grooving |反义词：standing still, being motionless
