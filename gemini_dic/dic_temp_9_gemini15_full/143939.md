output's: /ˈaʊtpʊts/ | n. | 输出，产量 | The output of the factory has increased this year.  今年工厂的产量增加了。 | 近义词：production, yield, result | 反义词：input, intake


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The factory's output is high. = 工厂的产量很高。
Their output meets the demand. = 他们的产量满足了需求。


一般过去时:
Last year's output was lower. = 去年的产量较低。
The project's output exceeded expectations. = 项目的输出超出了预期。


现在完成时:
The company has increased its output significantly. = 公司已经显著提高了产量。
We have achieved a higher output this quarter. = 本季度我们实现了更高的产量。


过去完成时:
Their output had already decreased before the new machine arrived. = 新机器到达之前，他们的产量已经下降了。
The factory's output had been declining for several years. = 该工厂的产量已经下降了几年了。


将来时:
The new technology will increase output. = 新技术将提高产量。
Next year's output will be higher. = 明年的产量将会更高。


虚拟语气:

I wish the output were higher. = 我希望产量更高。


被动语态:

否定句:
