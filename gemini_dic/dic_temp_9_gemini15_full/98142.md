insanity:/ɪnˈsænɪti/| n. |疯狂；精神错乱；荒谬 |The state of being severely mentally ill; madness.  It can also refer to extreme foolishness or unreasonableness.|例句：His actions bordered on insanity.  他的行为近乎疯狂。

例句：The court ruled him insane. 法院判定他精神错乱。


近义词：madness, lunacy, craziness, derangement, mania

反义词：sanity, rationality, reasonableness, sense
