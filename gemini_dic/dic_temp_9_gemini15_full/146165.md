overestimated:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪmeɪt/ | vt. | 高估，估计过高 | To estimate (something) too highly.  To place too high a value on something. |例句：He overestimated his abilities. 他高估了自己的能力。

例句：The project was overestimated, leading to budget overruns. 项目被高估了，导致预算超支。


近义词：overvalue, exaggerate, inflate


反义词：underestimate


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overestimate the difficulty of the task. = 他们高估了这项任务的难度。
We overestimate his influence on the market. = 我们高估了他对市场的影响力。


一般过去时:
I overestimated the time it would take to finish the project. = 我高估了完成项目所需的时间。
She overestimated the number of people who would attend the meeting. = 她高估了将要参加会议的人数。


现在完成时:
They have overestimated the cost of the materials. = 他们已经高估了材料的成本。
We have overestimated the potential risks involved. = 我们已经高估了潜在的风险。


过去完成时:

They had overestimated the support they would receive. = 他们高估了他们会得到的支持。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
