incubational:/ˌɪŋkjuˈbeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 孵化的；潜伏期的；培养的 | Relating to or characteristic of incubation;  the process of incubating or being incubated.  This term can refer to the period of development before something becomes fully apparent, often used in contexts like business, ideas, or diseases. | 例句：The incubational period for this virus is typically two to three days.  这个病毒的潜伏期通常为二到三天。

The company is in its incubational phase, still developing its core product. 这家公司正处于孵化阶段，仍在开发其核心产品。| 近义词：developmental, nascent, incipient | 反义词：mature, fully developed, manifest
