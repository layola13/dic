schoolmarming:/ˈskuːlˌmɑːrmɪŋ/| v.|教训，说教，严厉地教导|The act of behaving or speaking in a way that is reminiscent of a strict schoolteacher;  characterized by excessive strictness, pedantry, or moralizing.  It implies a condescending and often irritating manner.|例句：He was schoolmarming his colleagues about their sloppy work. 他在教训同事们工作马虎。

例句：Her constant schoolmarming annoyed everyone. 她不断的教训惹恼了所有人。


近义词：lecturing, preaching, moralizing, scolding, reproving


反义词：praising, encouraging, supporting, tolerating


词性：动词
