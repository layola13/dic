gluons:/ˈɡluːɒnz/| n. |胶子|  Fundamental particles that mediate the strong interaction between quarks. They are massless and carry color charge, which is responsible for binding quarks together to form hadrons such as protons and neutrons. Gluons are also responsible for the self-interaction of the strong force.  |例句：Gluons are the force carriers of the strong nuclear force, holding quarks together within protons and neutrons. 胶子是强核力的载体，将夸克结合在质子和中子内。|  例句：The study of gluons is crucial to understanding the structure of matter at the subatomic level.  胶子的研究对于理解亚原子水平物质的结构至关重要。| 近义词：None (unique particle)| 反义词：None (unique particle)| 名词
