judgey:/ˈdʒʌdʒi/| adj.|爱评判的，爱指指点点的，自以为是的|Having or showing an inclination to judge others critically; overly critical or judgmental.|例句：He's so judgey about everyone's life choices. 他对每个人的生活选择都非常挑剔。

例句：Her judgey comments made everyone uncomfortable. 她爱评判的言论让每个人都感到不舒服。


近义词：critical, judgmental, censorious, opinionated|反义词：tolerant, accepting, open-minded

词源:源自动词judge（判断）,加后缀-y(形容词后缀)，表示“具有…性质的”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is very judgey about other people's fashion sense. 他对别人的时尚品味非常挑剔。
She's always so judgey towards newcomers. 她总是对新人很挑剔。


一般过去时:
He was judgey about my choice of music. 他对我选择的音乐很挑剔。
She was being judgey and made everyone feel uncomfortable. 她当时很爱评判，让每个人都感到不舒服。


现在完成时:
He has been judgey all his life. 他一辈子都很爱评判。
She has become much less judgey since moving to a new city. 自从搬到新城市后，她变得不那么爱评判了。


过去完成时:
He had been judgey towards her for years. 他多年来一直对她很挑剔。
They had been judgey of her career choice before she became successful. 在她成功之前，他们一直对她职业选择很挑剔。


将来时:
He will be judgey about your new haircut. 他会对你新的发型很挑剔。
She will probably be judgey if you wear that dress. 如果你穿那件衣服，她可能会很挑剔。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so judgey, he'd have more friends. 如果他不那么爱评判，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she wasn't so judgey about people's personal lives. 我希望她不要那么爱评判别人的私生活。


被动语态:
His behavior was considered judgey by many. 许多人认为他的行为很爱评判。
Her comments were perceived as judgey and insensitive. 她的评论被认为是爱评判且不敏感的。


疑问句:
Is he always so judgey? 他总是那么爱评判吗？
Are you being judgey right now? 你现在是不是在评判？


否定句:
He isn't usually so judgey. 他通常不会那么爱评判。
She wasn't trying to be judgey, but her words came across that way. 她并非有意要评判，但她的措辞让人感觉如此。
