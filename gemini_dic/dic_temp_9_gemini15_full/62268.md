encyclopedia:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/| n. |百科全书|A book or set of books containing information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject, arranged alphabetically or systematically.  Encyclopedias aim to provide comprehensive and reliable information, often with illustrations, maps, and other visual aids. |Example:  I consulted the encyclopedia to learn more about the history of ancient Rome. 我查阅了百科全书以了解更多关于古罗马历史的知识。|近义词： compendium, dictionary (in some contexts)|反义词：None
