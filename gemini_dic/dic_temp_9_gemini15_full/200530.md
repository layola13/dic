slapped:/slæpt/ | vt. |掴，拍，拍打 | To strike with the open hand; to hit sharply with a flat object.  |例句：He slapped the table in anger. 他生气地拍了一下桌子。|例句：She slapped him across the face. 她掴了他一巴掌。|近义词：hit, strike, smack, cuff |反义词：caress, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slaps the mosquito. = 他拍打蚊子。
She slaps her knee in amusement. = 她好笑地拍了一下膝盖。

一般过去时:
He slapped the table angrily. = 他生气地拍了一下桌子。
She slapped her brother. = 她打了她弟弟一巴掌。

现在完成时:
He has slapped the dog several times. = 他已经打过那条狗好几次了。
She has never slapped anyone before. = 她以前从未打过任何人。

过去完成时:
He had slapped the boy before his father came. = 他父亲来之前已经打过那个男孩了。
She had slapped him for his bad behavior. = 因为他的恶劣行为，她已经打过他了。

将来时:
He will slap the insect. = 他将会拍打昆虫。
She will slap him if he does that again. = 如果他再这样做，她会打他。

虚拟语气:
If he slapped me, I would call the police. = 如果他打我，我会报警。
I wish I hadn't slapped him. = 我真希望我没有打他。

被动语态:
The table was slapped. = 桌子被拍了一下。
He was slapped across the face. = 他被人掴了一巴掌。

疑问句:
Did he slap the child? = 他打孩子了吗？
Has she ever slapped anyone? = 她曾经打过任何人吗？

否定句:
He didn't slap the dog. = 他没有打那条狗。
She didn't slap him out of anger. = 她不是出于愤怒才打他的。


PHRASE->
slapped together = 粗制滥造
slapped on = 草率地加上
slapped wrist = 轻微处罚
slapped down = 严厉批评
slapped with = 被判处
