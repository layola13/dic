overcloying: /ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分甜腻的；令人厌倦的 | excessively sweet or rich; causing disgust or distaste from an excess of something pleasant |


The dessert was overcloying; its sweetness was overwhelming and ultimately unpleasant.  = 甜点太甜腻了，甜味太浓，最终令人不快。

The constant praise became overcloying and made him uncomfortable. =  不断的赞美变得过分甜腻，让他感到不舒服。


近义词：excessive, overwhelming, sickening, nauseating


反义词：subtle, understated, bland, unsweetened


词性：形容词
