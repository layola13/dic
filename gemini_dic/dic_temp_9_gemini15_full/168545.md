procedure:/prəˈsiːdʒər/ | n. |程序；手续；步骤；方法 |A series of actions conducted in a certain order or manner.  It can refer to a set of instructions, a method of operation, or a formal process.  |例句：The procedure for applying for a visa is quite complicated.  申请签证的程序相当复杂。

例句：Following the correct procedure is crucial for safety. 遵循正确的程序对于安全至关重要。


近义词：method, process, technique, system, protocol |反义词：chaos, disorder, randomness |词性：名词
