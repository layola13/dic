controllable:/kənˈtroʊləbl/ | adj. | 可控的，可管理的 |  Able to be controlled or managed.  Something that is controllable can be influenced or directed. | The situation is largely controllable if we take the necessary precautions.  情况如果我们采取必要的预防措施，在很大程度上是可以控制的。|  Manageable, governable, regulable | Uncontrollable, unmanageable, uncontrollable.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature is controllable with this thermostat. = 这个恒温器可以控制温度。
The noise level is controllable with soundproofing. = 通过隔音可以控制噪音水平。


一般过去时:
The fire was controllable at first, but then it spread rapidly. = 起初大火是可以控制的，但后来迅速蔓延开来。
The spread of the disease was controllable through vaccination. = 通过疫苗接种可以控制疾病的传播。

过去完成时:
