sheep:/ʃiːp/| n.|绵羊，羊群|A woolly quadruped mammal, typically with a thick woolly coat and curved horns.  Sheep are raised for their wool and meat. |例句：The sheep grazed peacefully in the green pasture.  那些绵羊在绿色的牧场上安静地吃草。
例句：A flock of sheep crossed the road.  一群绵羊穿过马路。|近义词：lamb (young sheep), ewe (female sheep), ram (male sheep)|反义词：无
