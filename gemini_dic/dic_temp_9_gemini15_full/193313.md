seer:/ˈsiːər/| n. | 预言家，先知，有洞察力的人 | A seer is a person who is believed to be able to see the future or has exceptional insight.  They often possess supernatural abilities or a deep understanding of hidden truths. | The ancient seer predicted the downfall of the kingdom.  这位古代先知预言了王国的灭亡。
The artist was a seer, able to capture the essence of human emotion in her work.  这位艺术家是一位有洞察力的人，能够捕捉到作品中人类情感的本质。| prophet, visionary, soothsayer | skeptic, doubter | 名词
