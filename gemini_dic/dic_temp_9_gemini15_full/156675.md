picturizing: /ˈpɪktʃəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 描绘；想象；把…拍成电影 | The act of forming a mental image or picture of something; also, the act of making a movie or film.  |  例句：He was picturizing the scene in his mind. 他正在脑海中描绘那个场景。

例句：The director is picturizing the novel for the big screen.  导演正在把这部小说拍成电影。

近义词：visualizing, imagining, filming, portraying

反义词：abstracting, ignoring

He picturizes the events vividly in his mind. 他在他脑海里生动地描绘这些事件。
She picturizes her ideal future every day. 她每天都想象她理想的未来。

The artist picturized the landscape beautifully. 艺术家漂亮地描绘了这片风景。
