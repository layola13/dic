gurneys:/ˈɡɜːrniz/| n.|担架；手术床|A gurney is a wheeled cot or stretcher used to transport patients in hospitals and other medical facilities.  It typically consists of a flat surface on wheels, often with adjustable height and side rails for safety.|例句：The paramedics carefully placed the injured man on a gurney.  急救人员小心翼翼地把受伤的男子放在担架上。
The nurse wheeled the gurney into the operating room. 护士将担架推进了手术室。|近义词：stretcher, cot, trolley|反义词：无
