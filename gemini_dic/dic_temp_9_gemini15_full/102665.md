jambeux:/ʒɑ̃ˈbœ/| n. |腿甲，护腿|Leg armor, specifically articulated metal plates protecting the legs, commonly used in medieval times.  The term often refers to a pair of such plates.|Example: The knight donned his jambeux before the battle.  骑士在战斗前穿上了他的护腿。|近义词：leg armor, cuisses (thigh armor)|反义词：None
