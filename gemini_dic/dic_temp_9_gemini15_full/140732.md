omnipotent:/ɒmˈnɪpətənt/ | adj. | 全能的，万能的 | Having unlimited power; able to do anything.  |例句：Gods are often depicted as omnipotent beings.  神经常被描绘成全能的存在。

例句：The ruler believed himself to be omnipotent and above the law.  统治者认为自己全能且凌驾于法律之上。


近义词：almighty, all-powerful | 反义词：impotent, powerless


词源：来自拉丁语 *omnipotens，由 *omni- ("all") + *potens ("powerful") 构成。
