recessional:/rɪˈseʃənəl/| n.|退场进行曲；结束仪式；衰退|A recessional is a piece of music played or sung as people leave a ceremony, especially a religious service.  It can also refer to a period of decline or decrease. |例句：The recessional hymn was beautiful and moving. 退场赞美诗优美而感人。

The recessional marked the end of the graduation ceremony.  退场曲标志着毕业典礼的结束。


近义词：finale, closing hymn, decline, downturn |反义词：procession, advance, upswing
