plea-bargained: /ˈpliː bɑːrɡənd/ | adj. |认罪协商的，达成认罪协议的 |  Describes a situation where a defendant in a criminal case agrees to plead guilty to a lesser charge in exchange for a more lenient sentence. This is a legal agreement between the defendant and the prosecution, avoiding a trial. |例句：He plea-bargained with the prosecutor to reduce his sentence. 他与检察官达成认罪协议以减轻刑罚。

The defendant plea-bargained a reduced sentence in exchange for testimony against his accomplice.  被告以作证指控同伙为代价，通过认罪协议获得了减刑。


近义词：negotiated (in a legal context), compromised (in a legal context) | 反义词：tried (went to trial), unnegotiated (in a legal context) | 词性：形容词
