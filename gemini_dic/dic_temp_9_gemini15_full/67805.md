eyesores:/ˈaɪsɔːrz/| n.|眼中钉，碍眼的东西，讨厌的东西|Something that is unpleasant or offensive to look at.  It is typically something unsightly or out of place in its surroundings. |例句：The abandoned factory is a real eyesore.  那座废弃的工厂真是个碍眼的东西。

例句：Those graffiti are a terrible eyesore on the otherwise beautiful building. 那些涂鸦在这个原本美丽的建筑物上非常碍眼。


近义词：blight, nuisance, ugliness, monstrosity


反义词：beauty, delight, aesthetic pleasure


词性：名词
