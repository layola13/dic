seafront:/ˈsiːfrʌnt/ | n. |海滨；海滨区 |The area of land next to the sea.  It often includes beaches, promenades, and buildings. |例句：We spent the afternoon strolling along the seafront. 我们在海滨漫步了一下午。

例句：The new seafront development includes hotels, restaurants, and shops.新的海滨开发项目包括酒店、餐馆和商店。

近义词：coastline, waterfront, shore

反义词：inland
