overcame:/ˌoʊvərˈkeɪm/| v.|克服，战胜|To succeed in dealing with or controlling (a problem or difficulty).|例句：She overcame her fear of public speaking. 她克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。|例句：The team overcame many obstacles to win the championship.  这个团队克服了许多障碍最终赢得了冠军。|近义词：conquered, mastered, surmounted, defeated|反义词：succumbed, yielded, failed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overcomes challenges with determination. = 他以决心克服挑战。
She overcomes her shyness gradually. = 她逐渐克服了她的害羞。


一般过去时:
They overcame their differences and became friends. = 他们克服了分歧，成为了朋友。
He overcame a serious illness. = 他战胜了一场严重的疾病。


现在完成时:
She has overcome many hardships in her life. = 她一生中克服了许多苦难。
We have overcome the technical difficulties. = 我们已经克服了技术难题。


过去完成时:

将来时:
