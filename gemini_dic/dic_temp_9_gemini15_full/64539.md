errant:/ˈɛrənt/| adj.| 犯错误的；离轨的；迷路的 | Wandering or straying from the correct path or accepted standards; wrong; mistaken.  |例句：The errant knight was banished from the kingdom. 这名犯错的骑士被逐出王国。

例句：His errant behaviour led to his dismissal. 他行为不端导致被解雇。


近义词：straying, wayward, deviant, delinquent, erring |反义词：obedient, well-behaved, correct, proper


词源：源自于古法语“errant”，意思是“流浪的，游荡的”，最终源于拉丁语“errare”，意思是“犯错，迷路”。
