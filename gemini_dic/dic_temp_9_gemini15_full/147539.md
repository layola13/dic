overpaying: /ˌoʊvərˈpeɪɪŋ/ | v. | 付款过多，支付过高 | Paying more than the fair or agreed-upon price for something.  |例句：I'm worried about overpaying for the house. 我担心买房付的钱太多了。|例句：He regretted overpaying for the antique vase. 他后悔为那个古董花瓶付了过高的价钱。|近义词：overspending, exorbitant payment |反义词：underpaying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are overpaying for their new car. = 他们为他们的新车付出了过高的价钱。
We are constantly overpaying on our utility bills. = 我们不断地为我们的水电费支付过高的费用。


一般过去时:
She overpaid for the tickets. = 她为票付了过高的价钱。
They overpaid the contractor by $1000. = 他们多付了承包商1000美元。


现在完成时:
I have overpaid on my taxes this year. = 我今年的税交多了。
We have overpaid for this service several times. = 我们多次为这项服务支付了过高的费用。


过去完成时:
He realized he had overpaid for the painting after he'd bought it. = 他买下画后才意识到自己付的价钱太高了。
She discovered she had overpaid her rent for the last three months. = 她发现过去三个月的房租都交多了。


将来时:
I fear I will overpay for the repairs. = 我担心修理费用会付太多了。
They suspect they will overpay on the project. = 他们怀疑这个项目会超支。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the true value, I wouldn't have overpaid. = 如果我知道真实价值，我就不会付那么多了。
I wish I hadn't overpaid for that vacation package. = 我真希望我没有为那个度假套餐付那么多钱。


被动语态:
The customer was overpaid by the cashier. = 收银员多给了顾客钱。
The contractors were overpaid for the work they did. = 承包商为他们所做的工作收取了过高的费用。


疑问句:
Are we overpaying for this insurance? = 我们为这项保险付的钱是不是太多了？
Did they overpay for the land? = 他们买地是不是付的钱太多了？


否定句:
We didn't overpay for the house; it was a fair price. = 我们买房没有付过多的钱；价格很公道。
He made sure not to overpay for the groceries. = 他确保没有为杂货付过多的钱。


PHRASE->
overpaying for something = 为某事付了过多的钱
overpaying on a bill = 付账付多了
overpaying on taxes = 税交多了
overpaying for services = 为服务付了过高的费用
risk of overpaying = 支付过高的风险
