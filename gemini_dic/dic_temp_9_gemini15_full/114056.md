magnetisms:/ˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n. |磁性；磁力；吸引力；魅力|The property or phenomenon of attracting iron or steel; the force causing this attraction.  It also refers to a captivating or charming quality.|例句：The magnets demonstrated the principles of magnetism. 磁铁展示了磁性的原理。

例句：Her magnetism drew people to her. 她的魅力吸引了人们。|近义词：attraction, allure, charm, charisma |反义词：repulsion
