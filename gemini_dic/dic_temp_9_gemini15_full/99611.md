interjections:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkʃənz/ | n. |感叹词|Interjections are words or phrases used to express strong emotion or feeling. They are often grammatically independent of the sentence they appear in.  They can express a wide range of emotions, from surprise and joy to anger and pain. |Examples: Wow! That's amazing!  Oh no! I've broken it!|近义词：exclamations|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I exclaim "Wow!" when I see something amazing. = 我看到令人惊叹的东西时会惊呼“哇！”
She often says "Oops!" when she makes a mistake. = 她犯错时经常说“哎哟！”


一般过去时:
He shouted "Help!" when he fell into the river. = 他掉进河里时大喊“救命！”
She gasped "Oh my god!" at the sight of the accident. = 她看到事故现场惊呼“我的天哪！”

She has often muttered "Darn it!" under her breath. = 她经常低声抱怨“该死！”


过去完成时:
