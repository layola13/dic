nonoverlapping: /ˌnɒnˌoʊvərˈlæpɪŋ/ | adj. | 不重叠的 | Not overlapping; separate and distinct.  Describes things that do not share any common area or time period. |The nonoverlapping intervals of time allowed for focused work. = 不重叠的时间间隔允许专注的工作。

The research projects had nonoverlapping objectives. = 这些研究项目的目的是不重叠的。


近义词：separate, distinct, disjoint, independent

反义词：overlapping, intersecting, concurrent

词性:形容词
