inheritances: /ɪnˈhɛrɪtənsɪz/ | n. | 遗产；继承物 |  The process or right of inheriting something; something inherited.  |  例句：He received a large inheritance from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里继承了一大笔遗产。

例句：The family fought over the inheritance of their ancestral home.  这个家族为了他们祖传房子的继承权而争吵。


近义词：legacies, successions | 反义词：disinheritance


词性：名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inheritance includes a house and a large sum of money. = 遗产包括一栋房子和一大笔钱。
He discusses his inheritance with his lawyer. = 他和他的律师讨论他的遗产。


一般过去时:
She received a substantial inheritance last year. = 她去年收到了一笔可观的遗产。
They fought bitterly over their father's inheritance. = 他们为他们父亲的遗产而激烈争吵。


现在完成时:
She has finally settled her inheritance dispute. = 她终于解决了她的遗产纠纷。
He has inherited his father's business. = 他继承了他父亲的事业。


过去完成时:

They had already spent a significant portion of their inheritance. = 他们已经花掉了他们大部分的遗产。


将来时:
She will receive her inheritance next month. = 她下个月将收到她的遗产。
