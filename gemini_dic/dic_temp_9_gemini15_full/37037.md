comanaging:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. |共同管理；协同管理 |  The act of managing something jointly or in collaboration. It implies shared responsibility and decision-making.  | Example: The project was successfully comanaged by the two departments.  这个项目由两个部门成功地共同管理。 |近义词：co-managing, joint managing, collaboratively managing | 反义词：sole managing, independently managing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They comanage the marketing and sales efforts. = 他们共同管理营销和销售工作。
We comanage the family business. = 我们共同管理家族企业。


一般过去时:
The two partners comanaged the company for ten years. = 这两位合伙人共同管理公司十年了。
They comanaged the crisis effectively. = 他们有效地共同管理了这场危机。
