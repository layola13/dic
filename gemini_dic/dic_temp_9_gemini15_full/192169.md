scrapbooks:/ˈskræpˌbʊks/| n.|剪贴簿|A book with blank pages for pasting in pictures, newspaper cuttings, etc.  A scrapbook is a collection of mementos, typically photographs, ticket stubs, postcards, and other ephemera, arranged and preserved in a bound book. |She carefully placed the dried flower in her scrapbook.|近义词：memory book, keepsake album|反义词：None
