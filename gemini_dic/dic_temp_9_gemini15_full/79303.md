glaziers:/ˈɡleɪzərz/| n.|装玻璃工人，玻璃匠|A glazier is a person who cuts, installs, and repairs glass, typically in windows and doors.  They often work with a variety of glass types and may also install related hardware.|例句：The glaziers arrived early to replace the broken window. 装玻璃工人很早就到了，来更换碎了的窗户。

The glaziers are skilled in handling different types of glass.  这些装玻璃工人擅长处理不同类型的玻璃。|近义词：glass fitter, window fitter|反义词：无
