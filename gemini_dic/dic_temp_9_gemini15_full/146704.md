overhelpfully:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfʊli/ | adv. | 过度热心地，多此一举地 |  The adverb "overhelpfully" describes an action performed with excessive helpfulness, often to the point of being intrusive or unhelpful. It implies well-intentioned but ultimately unnecessary or counterproductive assistance. | 例句：He overhelpfully offered to carry all her bags, even though she had already managed them. 他过度热心地提出要帮她提所有包，尽管她已经自己弄好了。

He overhelpfully corrected my grammar in front of everyone, making me feel embarrassed. 他在大家面前过度热心地纠正我的语法，让我感到尴尬。| 近义词：excessively helpfully, intrusively helpfully | 反义词：unhelpfully, unhelpful
