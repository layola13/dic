pitted:/ˈpɪtɪd/| adj.| 有凹痕的；有斑点的；核桃仁去壳的 | Having small indentations or marks on the surface;  removed from the shell or pit.  |例句：The fruit was pitted and bruised.  这水果有凹痕和擦伤。

The olives were pitted before being bottled. 橄榄在装瓶前去核了。|近义词：dented, scarred, marked, pitted (as a verb means to remove pits) |反义词：smooth, unblemished, intact
