gummite:/ˈɡʌmaɪt/| n. |硅钙铀矿|A radioactive mineral consisting of hydrated calcium uranyl silicate.  It is usually found in association with other uranium minerals in hydrothermal veins.  The color varies, often yellowish or brownish, and it's generally found in granular or massive forms.|例句：Gummite is a secondary uranium mineral often found in weathered deposits. 硅钙铀矿是次生铀矿，通常存在于风化矿床中。|  The sample contained significant quantities of gummite. 样本含有大量的硅钙铀矿。|近义词：uraninite (in some contexts, as both are uranium minerals)|反义词：无|名词
