parallel:/ˈpærəlel/ | adj., n. | 平行的；类似的；类似物 |  Existing or happening at the same time; similar or analogous.  A line, plane, or surface that is parallel to another is everywhere equidistant from it. | 例句：The two lines are parallel. 这两条线是平行的。

The events unfolded in parallel in both countries. 这两国的事件同时发生。

近义词：simultaneous, corresponding, analogous | 反义词：intersecting, perpendicular, divergent

词源:

From Middle English *paralel, from Old French *paralel, from Late Latin *parallelus, from Greek παράλληλος (parállēlos), from παρά (pará) "beside" + ἀλλήλων (allēlōn) "one another".
