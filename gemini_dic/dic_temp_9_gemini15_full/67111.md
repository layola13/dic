explosively:/ɪkˈspləʊsɪvli/ | adv. | 爆炸地；猛烈地；突然地 |  In a way that is explosive; suddenly and violently.  It describes something happening with great force and suddenness, often in a destructive or dramatic manner. | 例句：The volcano erupted explosively, sending ash and debris miles into the air. = 火山猛烈喷发，将火山灰和碎片喷射到数英里高的空中。

The news of his resignation spread explosively across the internet. = 他辞职的消息在互联网上迅速传播开来。


近义词：violently, suddenly, forcefully, dramatically | 反义词：gradually, slowly, gently, calmly
