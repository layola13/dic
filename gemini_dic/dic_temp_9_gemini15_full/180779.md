regulatory: /ˈreɡjʊlətɔːri/ | adj. | 监管的；管理的；调节的 | Relating to the function of regulating or controlling something.  | The regulatory body issued new guidelines for food safety.  监管机构发布了新的食品安全指南。 | controlling, governing, supervisory | unregulated, uncontrolled | 形容词

This company strictly follows regulatory requirements. = 这家公司严格遵守监管规定。
