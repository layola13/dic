duality:/duˈæləti/ | n. |二元性，二重性 |The state or quality of having two parts or aspects.  It often refers to seemingly contradictory yet interdependent concepts or aspects of something. For example, the duality of human nature, encompassing both good and evil. |例句：The poem explores the duality of love and hate. 这首诗探讨了爱与恨的二元性。

例句：The physicist's work focused on the wave-particle duality of light. 物理学家的工作集中在光的波粒二象性上。


近义词：dichotomy, duality, doubleness,  twoness |反义词：unity, oneness, singularity


词源：From Middle French dualité, from Latin dualitas, from dualis "double," from duo "two."
