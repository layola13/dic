rebraces:/ˌriːˈbreɪsɪz/| n.|矫正器；牙套|Braces that are used again after a period of time, often because of relapse or shifting teeth.  They are essentially a second set of braces.  The term is not commonly used, and "re-treatment with braces" or simply "getting braces again" is preferred.|例句：After his teeth shifted, he needed rebraces. 他牙齿移位后，需要重新戴牙套。|近义词：re-treatment with braces, getting braces again|反义词：removal of braces
