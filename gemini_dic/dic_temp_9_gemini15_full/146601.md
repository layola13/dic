overgrieving:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvɪŋ/| v.|过度悲伤，悲痛过度 | Excessive grief or mourning, often beyond what is considered healthy or normal. It can manifest in various ways, including prolonged sadness, inability to function, and difficulty accepting the loss. |例句：She's been overgrieving since her husband died; she needs professional help.  她丈夫去世后一直过度悲伤，她需要专业帮助。
He's overgrieving the loss of his job and needs to find a new perspective. 他因失去工作而过度悲伤，需要找到新的视角。|近义词：excessive mourning, prolonged grief, unhealthy grieving |反义词：acceptance, resilience, moving on
