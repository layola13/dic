prophesize:/ˈprɒfɪsaɪz/ /ˈprɑːfəˌsaɪz/ | vt. | 预言；预示 | To predict or foretell a future event, especially one of a religious or supernatural nature.  | The oracle prophesied the king's downfall.  先知预言了国王的垮台。| The fortune teller prophesied great wealth for me.  算命先生预言我将拥有巨额财富。| predict, foretell, forecast | disprove, deny |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prophesizes the end of the world. = 他预言世界末日。
She prophesizes good fortune for us. = 她预言我们将会好运。

一般过去时:
The prophet prophesied a great famine. = 先知预言了一场严重的饥荒。
They prophesied the success of the new product. = 他们预言新产品会成功。

现在完成时:
The seer has prophesied many times. = 预言家已经预言过很多次了。
No one has prophesied such a dramatic change. = 没有人预言过如此剧烈的变化。

过去完成时:
They had prophesied the storm before it hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们就预言了它。
The ancient texts had prophesied the coming of a savior. = 古代文本预言了救世主的到来。

将来时:
Scientists will prophesize the effects of climate change. = 科学家将预言气候变化的影响。
She will prophesize the winner of the election. = 她将预言选举的获胜者。


虚拟语气:
If he prophesied correctly, we would be prepared. = 如果他预言正确，我们就会做好准备。
I wish the fortune teller had prophesied my future more accurately. = 我希望算命先生能更准确地预言我的未来。

被动语态:
The coming of the comet was prophesied centuries ago. = 彗星的到来几个世纪前就被预言了。
The disaster was prophesied by many experts. = 许多专家都预言了这场灾难。


疑问句:
Did the oracle prophesize anything about the future? = 神谕对未来预言了什么吗？
What did the ancient texts prophesize? = 古代文本预言了什么？

否定句:
The soothsayer did not prophesize a happy ending. = 预言家没有预言一个幸福的结局。
He refused to prophesize the outcome of the war. = 他拒绝预言战争的结果。
