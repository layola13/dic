semi-barbarous:/ˌsɛmiˈbɑːbərəs/| adj. |半野蛮的，半开化的|Partially or imperfectly civilized; showing some characteristics of barbarism.  It suggests a blend of civilized and uncivilized traits, often implying a lack of refinement or sophistication but not necessarily complete savagery.|例句：The semi-barbarous customs of the tribe included ritual sacrifices.  = 那个部落半野蛮的风俗包括祭祀仪式。

The semi-barbarous king ruled with a mixture of cruelty and generosity. = 半野蛮的国王既残暴又慷慨地统治着。|近义词：uncivilized, uncultured, primitive, uncouth|反义词：civilized, cultured, refined, sophisticated
