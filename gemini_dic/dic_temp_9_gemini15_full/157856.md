plagues:/pleɪɡz/| n.|瘟疫；灾难；大量；困扰 | a widespread disease that causes many deaths;  a large number of something undesirable; something that causes great trouble or suffering. |例句：The bubonic plague killed millions in the 14th century.  十四世纪鼠疫杀死了数百万人。 |例句：Plagues of locusts destroyed the crops.  蝗虫灾害毁坏了庄稼。 |近义词：epidemic, scourge, affliction, disaster |反义词：blessing, boon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drought plagues the farmers. = 干旱困扰着农民。
Many plagues affect the region. = 许多瘟疫影响着该地区。


一般过去时:
The city was plagued by crime. = 这个城市饱受犯罪困扰。
A plague of locusts devastated the fields. = 一场蝗灾摧毁了田地。


现在完成时:
The country has been plagued by corruption for years. = 这个国家多年来一直饱受腐败困扰。
This area has been plagued by floods recently. = 最近这个地区一直遭受洪水侵害。


过去完成时:
Before the cure was found, the plague had killed thousands. = 在找到治疗方法之前，瘟疫已经杀死了数千人。
The region had been plagued by civil war for decades before peace was achieved. = 在和平到来之前，该地区几十年来一直饱受内战的困扰。


将来时:
Experts fear that a new plague will sweep across the continent. = 专家担心一种新的瘟疫将席卷整个大陆。
Unless we take action, poverty will plague future generations. = 除非我们采取行动，否则贫困将困扰后代。


虚拟语气:
If only we had acted sooner, the plague might not have spread so widely. = 如果我们早点采取行动，瘟疫可能就不会传播得这么广泛。
If the government had taken preventative measures, the locust plague could have been avoided. = 如果政府采取预防措施，蝗灾本来是可以避免的。


被动语态:
The village was plagued by a mysterious illness. = 村庄受到一种神秘疾病的困扰。
The crops were plagued by insects. = 庄稼受到昆虫的侵害。


疑问句:
What plagues the region most? = 什么最困扰这个地区？
What plagues your mind these days? = 最近是什么在困扰你？


否定句:
The new policy doesn't plague the businesses. = 新政策并没有困扰企业。
The drought did not plague this area this year. = 今年干旱没有困扰这个地区。
