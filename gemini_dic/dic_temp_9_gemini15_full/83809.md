hairstreak: /ˈheəstreɪk/ | n. | 闪蝶；发绺 | A hairstreak is a type of butterfly characterized by a thin, often iridescent, streak of color along the trailing edge of its hindwings.  These butterflies are known for their delicate beauty and varied colors. | Example:  The hairstreak flitted among the wildflowers. 这只闪蝶在野花丛中飞舞。  I saw a beautiful purple hairstreak butterfly in the garden. 我在花园里看到一只美丽的紫色闪蝶。 | 近义词: butterfly (more general term) | 反义词: None specifically
