clot:/klɒt/|ˈklɑːt/| n., v.|凝块；血块；堵塞；凝结|A clot is a mass of something, especially blood, that has become thick and solid.  The verb refers to the process of forming a clot.|例句：A blood clot formed in his leg. 他腿上形成了血块。 The blood started to clot.  血液开始凝结。|近义词：lump, coagulum, thrombus|反义词：flow, liquefy
