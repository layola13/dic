endostyle:/ˈɛndoʊstaɪl/| n.|内柱，内褶|A ciliated groove in the pharynx of invertebrate chordates, such as lancelets and tunicates, that secretes mucus to trap food particles.  It is considered a homologous structure to the thyroid gland in vertebrates.| 例句：The endostyle in amphioxus plays a crucial role in filter feeding.|近义词：thyroid precursor (in evolutionary context)|反义词：N/A
