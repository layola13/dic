mensurate:/ˈmɛnʃəreɪt/| vt.|相称；与…相符；成比例|To be equal or similar in size, amount, or degree.|The punishment should commensurate with the crime.  惩罚应该与罪行相称。|The salary should commensurate with experience and skills. 薪水应该与经验和技能相符。|proportionate, corresponding, equivalent|disproportionate, unequal, inconsistent

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The reward commensurates with the effort. = 奖励与付出相称。
His actions commensurate with his words. = 他的行动与他的话语相符。


一般过去时:
The punishment commensurated with the severity of the crime. = 惩罚与罪行的严重程度相符。
The donation commensurated with his wealth. = 捐款与他的财富相符。


现在完成时:
The increase in salary has commensurated with the rise in inflation. = 工资的增长与通货膨胀的上升相符。
Her success has commensurated with her hard work. = 她的成功与她的努力相符。


过去完成时:
The damage had commensurated with the force of the impact. = 损害与撞击力相符。
The evidence had commensurated with the witnesses' testimony. = 证据与证人的证词相符。


将来时:
The project's cost will commensurate with its complexity. = 项目的成本将与其复杂程度相符。
Future rewards will commensurate with current efforts. = 将来的回报将与目前的努力相符。


虚拟语气:
If the sentence commensurated with the crime, justice would be served. = 如果判决与罪行相符，正义就会得到伸张。
I wish my efforts commensurated with my rewards. = 我希望我的努力与我的回报相符。


被动语态:
The penalties were commensurated with the offenses committed. = 处罚与所犯的罪行相符。
His contributions are commensurated with his dedication. = 他的贡献与其奉献精神相符。


疑问句:
Does the penalty commensurate with the offense? = 惩罚与罪行相符吗？
Does the reward commensurate with the effort put forth? = 奖励与付出的努力相符吗？


否定句:
The punishment did not commensurate with the crime. = 惩罚与罪行不相符。
The outcome did not commensurate with the expectations. = 结果与预期不相符。


PHRASE->
commensurate with = 与…相称
commensurate in size = 大小相等
commensurate to = 与…成比例
commensurate level = 相当的水平
commensurate scale = 相当的规模
