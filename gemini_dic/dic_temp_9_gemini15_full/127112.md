nationally:/ˈnæʃənəli/ | adv. | 全国地；在全国范围内 |  Nationally means throughout a whole nation or country. It refers to something that affects or applies to the entire country. | Example: The new law will be implemented nationally.  这条新法律将在全国范围内实施。  The program is nationally recognized for its excellence. 该项目因其卓越性而享誉全国。 | 近义词：nationwide, countrywide | 反义词：locally, regionally
