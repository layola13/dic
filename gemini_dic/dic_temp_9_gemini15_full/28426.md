carbonizers:/ˈkɑːrbənʌɪzərz/ | n. | 碳化剂；碳化器 | A substance or device that carbonizes something.  Carbonizers are used in various industrial processes to convert materials into carbon. | Example: The high temperatures in the furnace acted as efficient carbonizers, transforming the wood into charcoal.  高温炉内的温度起到了高效碳化剂的作用，将木材变成了木炭。 |近义词：carbonizing agents, charring agents | 反义词：decarbonizers
