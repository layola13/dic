ingredients:/ɪnˈɡriːdiənts/ | n. | 食材，成分 | The components of a mixture or compound.  Ingredients are the substances that are combined to make a particular dish or product.  | Example: The cake ingredients include flour, sugar, eggs, and butter.  蛋糕的配料包括面粉、糖、鸡蛋和黄油。


Example:  The recipe lists all the necessary ingredients.  食谱列出了所有必要的食材。
