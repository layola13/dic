candid:/ˈkændɪd/| adj.|坦率的；直言不讳的；公正的；真实的；偷拍的|frank, outspoken, honest, and sincere in expression; free from reservation, disguise, or subterfuge; impartial; unposed (as in a photograph)|例句：He gave a candid account of his experiences. 他坦率地讲述了他的经历。
例句：The photographer managed to capture a candid shot of the celebrity. 摄影师成功地拍到了一张名人的偷拍照。|近义词：frank, outspoken, honest, sincere, impartial|反义词：dishonest, insincere, biased, evasive, guarded
