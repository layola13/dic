guangong: /ɡwɑːŋɡʊŋ/ | n. |关公，关羽 | Guan Yu, a renowned general serving under the warlord Liu Bei during the late Eastern Han dynasty.  He is deified as a god of war, loyalty, righteousness, and brotherhood in Chinese culture. He is often depicted with a long beard, red face, and green attire.
