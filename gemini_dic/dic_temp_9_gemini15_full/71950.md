flight:/flaɪt/ | n. v. |飞行；航班；逃跑；飞翔 | A journey in an aircraft; the act of flying; a series of steps or actions taken to achieve something; a sudden escape or attempt to escape. |例句：The flight was delayed due to bad weather. 这趟航班因恶劣天气而延误。 |例句：The bird took flight from the tree.  鸟儿从树上飞了起来。|近义词：journey, voyage, escape, fleeing |反义词：arrival, landing

The bird flies high in the sky. = 鸟儿在高空中飞翔。
