newlyweds:/ˈnuːliˌwɛdz/| n. |新婚夫妇|Newlyweds are a couple who have recently married.  The word combines "newly" (recently) and "weds" (married couple). It refers to a couple in the initial stages of their marriage, often emphasizing the excitement and adjustment period. |The newlyweds honeymooned in Hawaii. 新婚夫妇在夏威夷度蜜月。

The newlyweds are settling into their new home. 这对新婚夫妇正在适应他们的新家。

近义词：newly married couple

反义词：divorced couple, long-married couple

词性：名词
