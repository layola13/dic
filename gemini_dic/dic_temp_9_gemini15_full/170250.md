protuberances:/proʊˈtuːbərənsɪz/| n. | 隆起物；凸出部分 |  A protuberance is a part of something that sticks out.  It can refer to a physical feature, a growth, or a metaphorical bump or irregularity. | The mountain's rocky protuberances were treacherous to climb.  这座山的岩石隆起物难以攀登。

The sun's surface was marked by several prominent protuberances. 太阳表面有几个明显的凸起。


| 近义词：bulge, projection, excrescence, outgrowth | 反义词：concavity, indentation, depression | 名词
