dropwort:/ˈdrɒpwɔːrt/| n.|水芹, 泽芹|A plant of the genus *Filipendula*, especially *Filipendula vulgaris*, having feathery leaves and small white or cream-colored flowers.  Several species are grown as ornamentals.|The dropwort blooms beautifully in the summer garden.  夏季花园里，水芹花开得非常漂亮。| 近义词: meadowsweet (某些种类)| 反义词:  无
