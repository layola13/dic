maleo:/məˈleɪoʊ/| n. |马来欧（一种鸟）|A maleo is a large megapode bird found in Indonesia.  Maleos are known for their unique breeding behavior, where they bury their eggs in volcanic soil or other hot areas to incubate them.|Example: The maleo's eggs are exceptionally large compared to the size of the bird itself. 马来欧的蛋与其体型相比非常大。|近义词：megapode (more general term)|反义词：无
