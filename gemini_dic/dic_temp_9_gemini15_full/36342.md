coilability:/ˌkɔɪləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 卷绕性，可卷绕性 | The ability of a material to be coiled or wound into a coil without breaking or cracking.  It is often used in the context of wires, cables, and other flexible materials. | Example: The high coilability of this wire makes it ideal for use in tight spaces.  这种电线的良好卷绕性使其非常适合在狭窄空间中使用。 | 近义词: flexibility, windability | 反义词: brittleness, rigidity
