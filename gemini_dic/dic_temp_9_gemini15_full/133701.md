nonfriction:/ˌnɒnˈfrɪkʃən/ | adj. |无摩擦的，无阻力的 | Not involving or causing friction.  Describes a surface or system that allows movement without significant resistance due to friction. |Example: The new bearings provide nonfriction movement for the machine.  新型轴承为机器提供了无摩擦的运动。The advanced material is designed for nonfriction applications.  这种先进材料的设计用于无摩擦应用。|近义词：frictionless, smooth |反义词：frictional, rough
