collectivising: /kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 集体化；公有化 | The process of transforming a system, typically an economic or social system, into one characterized by collective ownership and control.  This often involves the merging of individual holdings into larger, shared entities. | Example: The government implemented policies aimed at collectivising agriculture.  政府实施了旨在集体化农业的政策。|近义词：socialising, nationalising | 反义词：privatising, individualising
