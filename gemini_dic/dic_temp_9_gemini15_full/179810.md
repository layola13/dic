reexchanged:/ˌriːɪksˈtʃeɪndʒd/| v. |再交换，重新交换|The goods were reexchanged after being found defective. 货物因发现有缺陷而被重新交换。|近义词：exchanged again, re-traded |反义词：kept, retained |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexchange currencies regularly. = 他们定期兑换货币。
The company reexchanges faulty products. = 公司重新交换有缺陷的产品。


一般过去时:
We reexchanged the gifts because they were duplicates. = 我们重新交换了礼物，因为他们是重复的。
The store reexchanged the damaged item for a new one. = 商店将损坏的商品换成了新的。


现在完成时:
They have reexchanged the information several times. = 他们已经多次交换了信息。
The partners have reexchanged their shares in the company. = 合作伙伴已经重新交换了他们在公司中的股份。


过去完成时:
They had reexchanged the documents before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经重新交换了文件。
She had reexchanged her old phone for a newer model. = 她已经用旧手机换了一款更新的型号。


将来时:
We will reexchange the books next week. = 我们下周将重新交换书籍。

虚拟语气:
