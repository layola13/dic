puzzel:/ˈpʌzəl/| n.|难题；谜；拼图|A puzzle is something that is difficult to understand or solve.  It can be a game, a problem, or a mysterious situation.|例句：This crossword puzzle is really difficult. 这个纵横字谜真的很困难。

例句：The disappearance of the painting remains a puzzle.  这幅画的消失仍然是个谜。|近义词：enigma, riddle, mystery|反义词：solution, answer|名词
