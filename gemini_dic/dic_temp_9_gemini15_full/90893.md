hunted:/ˈhʌntɪd/| v. adj.|狩猎；追捕；搜寻；（被）追捕的 |To chase and kill (wild animals) for food or sport; to search for something persistently.  |例句：The hunters hunted deer in the forest. 猎人都在森林里打猎。
The police hunted the escaped prisoner. 警察追捕逃犯。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hunts deer every weekend. = 他每个周末都打猎。
She hunts for bargains in the shops. = 她在商店里寻找便宜货。


一般过去时:
They hunted foxes in the countryside. = 他们在乡下猎狐。
The detectives hunted for clues at the crime scene. = 侦探们在犯罪现场寻找线索。


现在完成时:
I have hunted in this forest many times. = 我在这个森林里打猎很多次了。
The police have hunted the suspect for months. = 警察已经搜捕嫌疑犯数月了。


过去完成时:
They had hunted for hours before they finally found their prey. = 他们在最终找到猎物之前已经搜寻了数小时。
The investigators had hunted through the archives before discovering the crucial document. = 调查人员在发现关键文件之前已经翻阅了档案。


将来时:
We will hunt ducks tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将去打鸭子。
They will hunt for a new apartment next month. = 他们下个月将寻找一套新公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I hunted more often, I would be fitter. = 如果我更经常打猎，我会更健康。
I wish I had hunted that opportunity when it arose. = 我真希望我在机会出现时抓住它。


被动语态:
The deer were hunted by the poachers. = 鹿被偷猎者猎杀。
The escaped criminal is being hunted by the authorities. = 逃犯正在被当局追捕。


疑问句:
Do you hunt? = 你打猎吗？
Where are they hunting? = 他们在哪里打猎？


否定句:
He doesn't hunt animals. = 他不猎杀动物。
They didn't hunt for long before giving up. = 他们没有搜寻很久就放弃了。


PHRASE->
hunt down = 追捕；追查
hunt for = 寻找
go hunting = 去打猎
on the hunt = 正在寻找
a treasure hunt =寻宝游戏


近义词: chase, pursue, track, seek, search
反义词:  release, free, liberate

词性: Verb (动词), Adjective (形容词)
