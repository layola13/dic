nonobservant:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvənt/| adj. |不遵守的；未注意到的；疏忽的 |Not observant; failing to notice or heed.  Someone who is nonobservant may not pay attention to details or religious practices. |例句：He was nonobservant of the speed limit. 他没有遵守限速规定。

例句：Her nonobservant nature often led to missed opportunities.  她不细心的性格经常导致她错过机会。


近义词：inattentive, heedless, unobserving, careless


反义词：observant, attentive, heedful, careful
