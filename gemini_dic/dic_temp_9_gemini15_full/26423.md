byliners:/ˈbaɪˌlaɪnərz/| n.|作者署名行，撰稿人姓名|Lines at the beginning of an article or other piece of writing that give the name(s) of the author(s).  It can also refer to the authors themselves.|Example: The byliners for this article are two well-known journalists. 这篇文章的作者署名是两位知名记者。 The byliners were invited to a press conference. 作者们被邀请参加新闻发布会。|近义词：author credits, author lines|反义词：anonymous, uncredited
