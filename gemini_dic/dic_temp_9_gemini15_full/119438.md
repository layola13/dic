microlevel:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌlevəl/ | n. |微观层次，微观水平 |  The study of microlevel interactions between individuals is crucial for understanding social dynamics. 微观层面个体间相互作用的研究对于理解社会动态至关重要。|近义词：microscopic level, granular level | 反义词：macrolevel, global level | 名词


英文解析：Referring to the smallest, most detailed level of analysis, often focusing on individual interactions or small groups, in contrast to macrolevel analysis which examines larger societal structures or trends.  It emphasizes the specific details and nuances of a particular situation or phenomenon.


例句：

The research employed a microlevel approach, analyzing individual decision-making processes within a specific organization.  这项研究采用了微观方法，分析了特定组织内个人的决策过程。

Researchers are interested in the microlevel factors contributing to this social phenomenon, such as individual beliefs and behaviors. 研究人员对导致这种社会现象的微观因素感兴趣，例如个体信念和行为。


词源：

* **micro-**:  From Greek *mikrós*, meaning "small."
* **-level**: From Old English *lāfel*, a word referring to a flat surface, and later extended to mean a stage or position in a hierarchy.
