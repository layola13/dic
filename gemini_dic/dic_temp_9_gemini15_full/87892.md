hierarch:/ˈhaɪərɑːrk/| n.|等级制度，层级结构|A system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority.|例句：The company has a strict hierarch of management. 这家公司有一个严格的管理层级制度。|例句：The social hierarch in that country is very rigid.  那个国家的社会等级制度非常严格。|近义词：hierarchy, ranking, stratification|反义词：equality, egalitarianism
