presharpened: /ˌpriːˈʃɑːrpənd/ | adj. | 预先磨尖的，预先削尖的 | Pre-sharpened describes something that has been sharpened before it is sold or used.  It typically refers to tools or implements that require a sharp edge, like pencils, knives, or scissors.  | Example:  The pre-sharpened pencils were convenient for the test.  预先磨尖的铅笔很方便考试使用。  The pre-sharpened knives were ready to use out of the box. 预先磨尖的刀子可以开箱即用。| 近义词：sharpened beforehand, pre-honed | 反义词：dull, unsharpened |  形容词
