pulling:/ˈpʊlɪŋ/| v.|拉，拖，拽|The act of pulling something or someone towards oneself.  It can also refer to the force or effort involved in this action, or to something that is being pulled.|例句：He was pulling the heavy cart. 他正在拉那辆重车。

例句：The children were pulling faces at each other. 孩子们互相做鬼脸。|近义词：dragging, hauling, tugging|反义词：pushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pulling the rope. = 他正在拉绳子。
They are pulling their weight. = 他们在尽力而为。


一般过去时:
She pulled the door open. = 她把门拉开了。
The horse pulled the cart. = 马拉车。


现在完成时:
I have pulled a muscle in my back. = 我拉伤了背部的肌肉。
They have been pulling pranks all day. = 他们整天都在恶作剧。


过去完成时:
He had pulled the lever before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经拉动了拉杆。
She had pulled all-nighters to finish the project. = 她为了完成项目熬了好几个通宵。


将来时:
We will pull the weeds tomorrow. = 我们明天将拔除杂草。
He will pull the trigger. = 他将扣动扳机。


虚拟语气:
If I pulled that lever, the machine would start. = 如果我拉动那个拉杆，机器就会启动。
I wish I hadn't pulled that prank. = 我真希望我没做过那个恶作剧。


被动语态:
The wagon was pulled by a horse. = 这辆马车是由马拉的。
The rope was being pulled taut. = 绳子正被拉紧。


疑问句:
Are you pulling my leg? = 你在开玩笑吗？
Who is pulling the strings here? = 这里谁在操纵？


否定句:
He didn't pull his weight on the project. = 他在这个项目上没有尽力。
She couldn't pull the heavy box. = 她拉不动那个重箱子。
