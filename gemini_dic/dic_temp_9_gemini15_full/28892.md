carports:/ˈkɑːrpɔːrts/| n.|  车棚，汽车棚 |A carport is an open-sided structure used to shelter a vehicle from the elements.  It typically has a roof but lacks walls. |例句：We built a carport to protect our cars from the sun and rain. 我们建了一个车棚来保护我们的车免受日晒雨淋。  The carport is attached to the house. 车棚与房子相连。|近义词：garage (but less enclosed), shelter |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be open exposure.
