investigators:/ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtərz/ | n. |调查员，研究人员 | Those who conduct investigations; people who systematically examine facts or circumstances to uncover information or reach conclusions.  |The investigators carefully examined the crime scene. 调查员仔细检查了犯罪现场。  The investigators presented their findings to the court. 调查员向法庭提交了调查结果。|researchers, examiners, detectives |  suspects, bystanders| 名词
