decrements:/ˈdekrimənts/| n. v.|递减，减少；减量|The process of decreasing gradually; reduction in amount, value, or degree.|例句：The decrements in the company's profits were alarming. 公司利润的减少令人担忧。The value of the currency experienced several decrements over the past year. 过去一年中，货币价值经历了几次下降。|近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, decline|反义词：increment, increase, growth|名词，动词

词源:
The word "decrement" comes from the Latin word "decrementum," which is a noun meaning "a lessening, decrease."  It combines the prefix "de-" (meaning "down" or "away") and "crescere" (meaning "to grow"). Thus, literally, it means "to grow down" or "to grow away."
