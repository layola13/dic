preapply:/ˌpriːəˈplaɪ/| v.|预先申请，提前申请|To apply beforehand; to make an application in advance.|例句：Applicants are encouraged to preapply for the scholarship.  申请者鼓励提前申请奖学金。
例句：She preapplied for the job opening before the official announcement.  她在正式公告前就预先申请了这个职位空缺。|近义词：apply in advance, submit early|反义词：postpone application, delay application


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He preapplies for every job he sees. = 他看到每个工作机会就提前申请。
They preapply for permits before starting construction. = 他们在开工前提前申请许可证。

一般过去时:
She preapplied for the visa last month. = 她上个月提前申请了签证。
They preapplied for the conference last year. = 他们去年提前申请了参加会议。

现在完成时:
He has preapplied for several internships. = 他已经提前申请了好几个实习职位。
She has preapplied for a new credit card. = 她已经提前申请了一张新的信用卡。

过去完成时:

将来时:
