go-native:/ɡoʊˈneɪtɪv/| adj. | 融入当地文化；本地化；变得像当地人一样 | To become fully integrated into and assimilated by the local culture of a place one is living in, adopting local customs, language and behaviour. | Example: After living in Japan for five years, he had truly gone native, speaking fluent Japanese and participating fully in local life. |近义词：assimilate, acculturate, integrate |反义词：remain foreign, stay aloof


USAGE->

He goes native every time he visits his grandparents' village. = 他每次去祖父母的村庄都会融入当地生活。
She goes native whenever she travels abroad. = 她只要出国旅行就会融入当地文化。

They went native during their year abroad. = 他们在国外的那一年里完全融入当地生活了。
