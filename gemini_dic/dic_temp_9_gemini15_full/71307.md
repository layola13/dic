fizz:/fɪz/| n. v.|嘶嘶声；泡沫；(使)发出嘶嘶声|(1) n.  A hissing sound, such as that made by escaping gas. (2) v. To make a hissing sound, like that of a carbonated drink when opened. |例句：The soda fizz when I opened the bottle.  当我打开瓶子时，苏打水发出嘶嘶声。

The champagne fizzled in the glass. 香槟酒在酒杯里发出嘶嘶声。|近义词：hiss, bubble, effervesce|反义词：silence, stillness|词性: n./v.
