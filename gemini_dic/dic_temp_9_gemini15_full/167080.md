presupported:/ˌpriːsʌˈpɔːtɪd/ | adj. | 预先支撑的，预先支持的 | Pre-supported refers to something that has been supported or provided with support beforehand.  This typically applies to physical structures or digital models, indicating that a base or framework is already in place. | Example:  The 3D-printed model arrived presupported, making assembly much easier.  =  3D打印模型预先支好了架，组装起来容易多了。|近义词：pre-built, pre-assembled, ready-made | 反义词：unsupported, unbraced
