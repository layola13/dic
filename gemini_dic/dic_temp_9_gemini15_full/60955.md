elevenths:/ɪˈlɛvənθs/| n.|十一分之一；第十一|The word "elevenths" refers to the plural form of "eleventh," which means one part out of eleven equal parts of a whole.  It can also refer to the ordinal number representing the eleventh position in a sequence.|Example: He divided the cake into elevenths, giving each person a fair share. 他把蛋糕分成十一份，每人一份。|近义词: parts, fractions |反义词: wholes
