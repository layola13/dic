scrutiny: /ˈskruːtəni/ | n. | 仔细审查；严格监察 |  The act of examining something closely and critically.  It implies a thorough and detailed investigation, often with the intent of finding flaws or inconsistencies. | 例句：The proposal came under intense scrutiny from the committee.  委员会对该提案进行了严格审查。


The company's financial records were subject to intense scrutiny. 公司的财务记录受到了严格审查。


近义词：inspection, examination, investigation, review, analysis | 反义词：neglect, oversight, disregard


词源:  From Middle English *scrutinen, from Old French escrutiner, from Late Latin scrutari "to search,"  from scrutārius "searcher," from scrūta "bags, wallets, receptacles," possibly ultimately of uncertain origin.
