menhaden:/ˈmɛnhædn/| n. |美洲鳀|A small, oily fish of the genus *Brevoortia*, found along the Atlantic coast of North America, important as a source of food for other fish and as a source of fish oil and meal.|例句：Menhaden are a keystone species in the Atlantic ecosystem.  美洲鳀是大西洋生态系统中的关键物种。|例句：The menhaden fishing industry is a significant part of the economy in some coastal areas.  在一些沿海地区，美洲鳀捕捞业是经济的重要组成部分。|近义词：herring, sardine, shad|反义词:|词性:名词
