cheapens:/ˈtʃiːpənz/| v.|使廉价；贬低；降低身价 | To reduce the price or value of something; to make something seem less important or valuable. |例句：Mass production cheapens the product. 大量生产降低了产品的价格。  The constant criticism cheapened his achievements.  不断的批评贬低了他的成就。|近义词：devalues, depreciates, degrades, diminishes|反义词：enhances, elevates, improves, values


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant advertising cheapens the product. =  不断的广告宣传使产品贬值。
This behavior cheapens his reputation. = 这种行为降低了他的声誉。

一般过去时:
The scandal cheapened the politician's image. = 丑闻损害了这位政治家的形象。
The overuse of the phrase cheapened its meaning. = 这个短语的过度使用降低了它的意义。

现在完成时:
Mass production has cheapened many goods. = 大规模生产已经使许多商品价格降低了。
His actions have cheapened the value of his work. = 他的行为降低了他作品的价值。

过去完成时:
The prior bad publicity had cheapened the company's stock. = 此前的负面宣传已经压低了公司的股价。
They had cheapened the experience by rushing through it. = 他们仓促行事，贬低了这次经历的价值。

将来时:
Inflation will cheapen the currency. = 通货膨胀将会使货币贬值。
This decision will cheapen the brand's image. = 这个决定将会损害品牌的形象。

虚拟语气:
If they lowered the prices, it would cheapen the product. = 如果他们降低价格，那就会使产品贬值。
I wish they hadn't cheapened the occasion with their behaviour. = 我真希望他们没有因为自己的行为而贬低了这次场合。

被动语态:
The product was cheapened by mass production. = 该产品因大规模生产而价格降低。
His reputation was cheapened by the false accusations. = 他的声誉因虚假指控而受损。

疑问句:
Does this discount cheapen the quality? = 这种折扣会降低质量吗？
Will this decision cheapen the brand's image? = 这个决定会损害品牌的形象吗？

否定句:
The high quality materials did not cheapen the product. = 高质量的材料并没有降低产品的价值。
The careful planning did not cheapen the event. = 细致的规划并没有贬低活动的意义。
