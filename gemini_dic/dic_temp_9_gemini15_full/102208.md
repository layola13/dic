italicizing: /ɪˈtælɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 斜体书写；强调 |  To write or print (words) in italics.  It is often used to emphasize a word or phrase, or to indicate a title of a work. | 例句：Please italicize the book titles in your bibliography. 请在你的参考文献中将书名斜体化。| 例句：He italicized the key words to draw attention to them. 他将关键词斜体化以引起注意。| 近义词：emphasize, highlight | 反义词：bold, capitalize
