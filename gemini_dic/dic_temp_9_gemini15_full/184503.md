restating:/ˌriːˈsteɪtɪŋ/| v. | 重述；重新声明 | To express something again or in a different way.  | Example:  The lawyer restated his client's innocence. 律师再次声明了他的当事人的清白。

The teacher restated the instructions more clearly. 老师更清晰地重述了指示。|近义词：rephrasing, reiterating, paraphrasing |反义词：misrepresenting, distorting |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restates his position every meeting. 他每次会议都重申自己的立场。
She restates the facts for clarity. 她为了清楚起见重述了事实。

一般过去时:
The witness restated his testimony. 证人重述了他的证词。
The politician restated his promise to the voters. 政治家向选民重申了他的承诺。

现在完成时:
She has restated her commitment to the project. 她已经重申了她对项目的承诺。
He has restated his views on several occasions. 他多次重申了他的观点。

过去完成时:
He had restated his concerns before the meeting adjourned.  他在会议休会前重申了他的担忧。
They had restated their objections to the plan. 他们已经重申了他们对该计划的反对意见。

将来时:
She will restate her case in court. 她将在法庭上重述她的案情。
He will restate the rules before the game begins. 他将在比赛开始前重述规则。

虚拟语气:
If he restated his argument more clearly, people would understand. 如果他更清楚地重述他的论点，人们就会理解。
I wish he had restated his position earlier. 我希望他早点重申他的立场。

被动语态:
The instructions were restated in simpler terms. 说明以更简单的术语重述了。
The policy has been restated several times. 政策已被多次重申。

疑问句:
Will he restate his position publicly? 他会在公开场合重申他的立场吗？
Did she restate the question for clarification? 她是否重述了问题以寻求澄清？

否定句:
He did not restate his objections. 他没有重申他的反对意见。
She did not restate the details of the agreement. 她没有重述协议的细节。
