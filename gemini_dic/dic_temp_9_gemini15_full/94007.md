impala:/ɪmˈpɑːlə/| n. |跳羚|A medium-sized antelope found in eastern and southern Africa, known for its elegant build and graceful movements.  Impalas are herbivores and are commonly seen in large herds. |  例句：The impala leaped effortlessly over the bush.  跳羚轻松地越过了灌木丛。
 The herd of impala grazed peacefully in the savanna.  一群跳羚在稀树草原上平静地吃草。|近义词：antelope, gazelle|反义词:None

词源:
The word "impala" comes from the Zulu word "impalala," which is the name for this antelope in the Zulu language of South Africa.  The word entered English through various colonial sources in the 19th century.
