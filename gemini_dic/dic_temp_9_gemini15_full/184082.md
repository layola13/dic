resignee:/ˌrɛzɪˈɡniː/| n. |辞职者，已辞职的人|A person who has resigned from a job or position.  |Example: The resignee left the company amicably.  这位辞职者友好地离开了公司。|近义词：former employee, ex-employee |反义词：employee, appointee |名词


USAGE->

The resignee is looking for a new job. = 这位辞职者正在寻找新工作。
He is a resignee from a large corporation. = 他是从一家大公司辞职的人。


一般过去时:
The resignee was offered a severance package. = 这位辞职者得到了遣散费。

The resignee has already found a new position. = 这位辞职者已经找到了新的职位。

虚拟语气:
