relinked:/ˌriːˈlɪŋkt/| v. |重新连接，重新关联 |To link or connect again.  This often implies a previous disconnection or a change in the connection.  |例句：The server was down, but we relinked it and restored service.  服务器宕机了，但我们重新连接了它并恢复了服务。

The system automatically relinked the files after the update. 系统在更新后自动重新链接了文件。|近义词：reconnect, rejoin, reattach |反义词：disconnect, unlink, sever | 动词
