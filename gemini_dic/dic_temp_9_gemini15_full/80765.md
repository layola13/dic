gossiped:/ˈɡɒsɪpt/ | v. | 嚼舌头；说闲话；传播小道消息 | To talk about other people's private lives, often in a way that is unkind or untrue. |例句：They gossiped about their neighbors all afternoon.  他们整个下午都在说邻居的闲话。


例句：She gossiped to her friends about the celebrity's divorce. 她向朋友们八卦了这位名人的离婚消息。


近义词：whispered, chatted, tattled, whispered, spread rumors


反义词：kept quiet, remained silent, kept a secret


词性:动词

USAGE->
