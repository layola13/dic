clothier:/ˈkloʊðɪər/ | n. |成衣商，服装商 | A clothier is a person or business that makes or sells clothes.  | Example:  He inherited his father's successful clothier business. 他继承了他父亲成功的成衣店生意。|近义词：tailor, draper, haberdasher | 反义词：无
