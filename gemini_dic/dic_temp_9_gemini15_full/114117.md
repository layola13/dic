magpies:/ˈmæɡpaɪz/| n.|喜鹊；饶舌的人|A magpie is a black-and-white bird known for its intelligence and habit of collecting shiny objects.  The term can also refer to a person who talks too much or gossips. |例句：The magpie swooped down and snatched a shiny button.  一只喜鹊俯冲下来，抓走了一颗闪亮的纽扣。

The children were chattering like magpies. 孩子们像喜鹊一样喋喋不休。|近义词：chatty person, gossip, chatterbox|反义词：quiet person, reserved person
