replicate:/ˈreplɪkeɪt/ | vt. & n. |复制，重复，模拟 | To make or do something again in exactly the same way.  To reproduce or copy something exactly. |例句：Scientists are trying to replicate the experiment to verify the results. 科学家们正在试图复制实验以验证结果。

The company replicated its success in the Asian market.  这家公司复制了其在亚洲市场的成功。


近义词：duplicate, copy, reproduce, imitate | 反义词：destroy, erase, obliterate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists replicate experiments to ensure accuracy. = 科学家复制实验以确保准确性。
The machine replicates data automatically. = 机器自动复制数据。


一般过去时:
He replicated the artist's style perfectly. = 他完美地复制了这位艺术家的风格。
The experiment was replicated by another team. = 另一个团队复制了这个实验。


现在完成时:
They have replicated the software successfully. = 他们已经成功地复制了软件。
The researchers have replicated the findings of previous studies. = 研究人员已经复制了先前研究的结果。


过去完成时:
She had replicated the design before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经复制了设计。
The team had replicated the procedure several times. = 该团队已经多次重复了该程序。


将来时:
We will replicate the process next week. = 我们下周将复制这个过程。
They will replicate the study using a larger sample size. = 他们将使用更大的样本量来复制这项研究。


虚拟语气:
If we could replicate the results, it would prove our hypothesis. = 如果我们能够复制结果，那将证明我们的假设。
I wish I could replicate his success. = 我希望我能复制他的成功。


被动语态:
The experiment was replicated by many researchers. = 许多研究人员都复制了这个实验。
The data was replicated across multiple servers. = 数据在多个服务器上被复制。


疑问句:
Can you replicate this image? = 你能复制这张图片吗？
Will they be able to replicate the experiment's results? = 他们能够复制实验的结果吗？


否定句:
They couldn't replicate the results. = 他们无法复制结果。
We did not replicate the study due to time constraints. = 由于时间限制，我们没有复制这项研究。


PHRASE->
replicate a study = 复制一项研究
replicate DNA = 复制DNA
replicate a process = 复制一个过程
replicate an experiment = 复制一个实验
Faithfully replicate = 忠实地复制


词源:
From Latin *replicare "to fold back, repeat," from re- "again" + plicare "to fold."  The meaning evolved from the physical act of folding something back to the concept of repeating or reproducing something.
