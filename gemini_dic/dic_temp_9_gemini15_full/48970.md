degenerating:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 衰退；退化；堕落 |  Becoming worse in quality or condition; declining.  |例句：The building is degenerating rapidly due to neglect. 这栋建筑由于疏于管理而迅速衰败。

The political situation is degenerating into chaos. 政治局势正在恶化成混乱。


近义词：deteriorating, declining, worsening, decaying | 反义词：improving, enhancing, advancing, flourishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is degenerating. 这栋建筑正在衰败。
His health is degenerating. 他的健康状况正在恶化。


一般过去时:
The empire degenerated into a series of warring factions. 这个帝国衰落成一系列互相争斗的派系。
His eyesight degenerated gradually over the years. 他的视力多年来逐渐衰退。


现在完成时:
The moral standards of the society have degenerated. 社会的道德标准已经堕落了。
The quality of the product has degenerated since the change in management. 自从管理层变动以来，产品的质量已经下降了。


过去完成时:
The situation had degenerated before the intervention. 在干预之前，情况已经恶化了。
By the time they arrived, the building had degenerated significantly. 当他们到达时，建筑物已经严重破败了。


将来时:
If we don't act now, the environment will degenerate further. 如果我们现在不采取行动，环境将进一步恶化。
Without proper treatment, his condition will degenerate rapidly. 没有适当的治疗，他的病情会迅速恶化。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't smoked so much, his lungs wouldn't have degenerated so quickly. 如果他不抽那么多烟，他的肺就不会这么快就衰退了。
I wish the situation hadn't degenerated to this point. 我希望情况没有恶化到这个地步。


被动语态:
The area has been allowed to degenerate.  这个地区被允许荒废了。
The bridge was allowed to degenerate into a state of disrepair. 这座桥被允许衰败到破损不堪的地步。


疑问句:
Is the situation degenerating? 情况正在恶化吗？
Are his mental faculties degenerating? 他的心智能力正在衰退吗？


否定句:
The quality of the food did not degenerate. 食物质量没有下降。
The situation did not degenerate further. 情况没有进一步恶化。
