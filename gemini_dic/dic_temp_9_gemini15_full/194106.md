self-important:/ˌsɛlfɪmˈpɔːrtnt/|/ˈsɛlfɪmˈpɔːrtənt/| adj. |自以为是的；自命不凡的 |Having or showing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or worth.  This adjective describes someone who believes they are more significant or capable than they actually are, often exhibiting arrogance and a lack of humility.  |例句：He was so self-important that he refused to listen to anyone else's ideas. 他如此自以为是，以至于拒绝听取任何其他人的意见。

He acted in a self-important manner, constantly interrupting others to boast about his accomplishments. 他表现得自以为是，不断打断别人来吹嘘自己的成就。


近义词：arrogant, conceited, vain, pompous, egotistical, supercilious |反义词：humble, modest, unassuming


词性：形容词
