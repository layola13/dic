eligibilities:/ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |资格；合格；符合条件|The plural form of "eligibility," referring to multiple instances or types of qualifications or suitability.  It signifies the state or quality of being eligible for something.  |Example:  The committee reviewed the eligibilities of all applicants before making a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前审查了所有申请人的资格。|近义词：qualifications, entitlements, suitabilities |反义词：ineligibilities, disqualifications
