capitalize:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪz/|/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪz/| vt.| 大写；利用；获利 |To write or print (a letter, word, or other element) in capital letters.  To take advantage of (something) to one's advantage. To convert (something) into capital.|例句：Capitalize the first letter of each sentence.  把每个句子的首字母大写。


例句：She capitalized on her opponent's mistake. 她利用了对手的失误。


近义词：upper case, utilize, profit from


反义词：lower case, neglect


词性:动词
