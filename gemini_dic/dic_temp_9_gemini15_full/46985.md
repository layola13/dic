darwinites:/ˈdɑːrwɪnaɪts/| n. |达尔文主义者，进化论者 |Supporters of Charles Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection.  They believe that species evolve over time through a process of natural selection.|Example: Many scientists are darwinites and accept the theory of evolution. 许多科学家都是达尔文主义者，并接受进化论。|近义词：evolutionists, naturalists|反义词：creationists
