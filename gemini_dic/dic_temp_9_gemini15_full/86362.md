hector:/ˈhɛktər/| v. n.| 欺侮；教训；（尤指）像赫克托尔那样地大声呵斥 | to bully or harass someone; to act in a bossy or bullying way, especially by using loud and forceful language. | 例句：He was always hectoring his employees about their performance. 他总是因为员工的表现而训斥他们。

例句：Don't hector me; I'm trying to concentrate. 不要训斥我；我正努力集中精力。


近义词：bully, browbeat, intimidate, domineer | 反义词：soothe, comfort, encourage


词源:  源自希腊神话中的特洛伊英雄赫克托尔(Hector)，他以其好斗和强势的性格而闻名。  因此，动词“hector”逐渐演变为指责备和粗暴地训斥他人的行为。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hectors his little brother daily. = 他每天都欺负他弟弟。
She constantly hectors her colleagues about their work. = 她不断地训斥她的同事们的工作。


一般过去时:
He hectored me into agreeing to his plan. = 他强迫我同意他的计划。
The manager hectored the staff about missed deadlines. = 经理因为错过截止日期而训斥员工。


现在完成时:
He has hectored his way to the top of the company. = 他凭借蛮横霸道的行为爬到了公司的高层。
She has hectored him into submission. = 她已经把他训服了。


过去完成时:
He had hectored her for weeks before she finally gave in. = 他在她最终屈服之前已经训斥了她几周。
They had hectored the witness into changing his testimony. = 他们已经恐吓证人改变证词。


将来时:
He will hector her until she agrees. = 他会一直训斥她直到她同意。
They will hector him into doing what they want. = 他们会强迫他做他们想让他做的事。


虚拟语气:
If he hectored me again, I would leave. = 如果他再次训斥我，我就离开。
I wish he wouldn't hector me so much. = 我希望他不那么训斥我。


被动语态:
He was hectored into signing the contract. = 他被逼着签了合同。
She was constantly hectored by her boss. = 她经常被老板训斥。


疑问句:
Does he often hector people? = 他经常训斥人吗？
Why does she hector her children so much? = 她为什么那么训斥她的孩子们？


否定句:
He didn't hector her; he simply explained the situation. = 他没有训斥她；他只是解释了情况。
She doesn't usually hector her employees. = 她通常不会训斥她的员工。


PHRASE->
hectoring tone: 训斥的语气
hectoring voice: 训斥的声音
hectoring manner: 训斥的态度
constantly hectoring: 不断地训斥
to be hectored into: 被逼着
