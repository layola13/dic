processing: /ˈprɑːsɛsɪŋ/ /ˈprəʊsesɪŋ/ | n.  |处理，加工，过程 |The act of processing something, such as information or data, or the series of actions involved in transforming raw materials into finished products.  |例句：The processing of applications takes several weeks.  申请处理需要几周时间。|例句：Food processing is a major industry. 食品加工是一个重要的产业。|近义词：handling, treatment, manipulation, preparation |反义词：creation (in some contexts), idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The computer is processing the data. = 电脑正在处理数据。
She processes orders every morning. = 她每天早上处理订单。

一般过去时:
They processed the film last night. = 他们昨晚冲洗了胶卷。
The factory processed tons of grain. = 工厂加工了数吨谷物。

现在完成时:
We have processed all the applications. = 我们已经处理了所有申请。
They have processed the data successfully. = 他们已经成功地处理了数据。

过去完成时:
The company had processed all the orders before the deadline. = 公司在截止日期前已经处理了所有订单。
He had processed the information before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经处理了信息。


将来时:
We will process your request as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快处理您的请求。
The system will automatically process the payment. = 系统将自动处理付款。

虚拟语气:
If they processed the data correctly, we would have accurate results. = 如果他们正确地处理数据，我们将得到准确的结果。
I wish I had processed the information more efficiently. = 我希望我更有效率地处理了信息。

被动语态:
The data is being processed by the computer. = 数据正在被电脑处理。
The application was processed quickly. = 申请很快就被处理了。

疑问句:
How is the data being processed? = 数据是如何被处理的？
Have you processed the order yet? = 你已经处理订单了吗？

否定句:
