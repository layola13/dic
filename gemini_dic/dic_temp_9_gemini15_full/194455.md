self-reproached:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈproʊtʃt/| adj.|自责的，自我谴责的|Feeling guilt or shame about one's own actions.|She felt self-reproached after snapping at her mother.|近义词：remorseful, repentant, contrite|反义词：unrepentant, complacent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-reproached about his mistakes. = 他为自己的错误感到自责。
She is always self-reproached after a fight. = 她每次吵架后都会自责。

一般过去时:
He was self-reproached after he lied. = 他撒谎后感到自责。
She felt self-reproached after hurting her friend. = 她伤害朋友后感到自责。

现在完成时:
He has been self-reproached since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，他一直很自责。
She has felt self-reproached for years about her past actions. = 多年来，她一直为过去的所作所为感到自责。

过去完成时:
He had been self-reproached for weeks before he finally confessed. = 在他最终坦白之前，他已经自责了好几个星期。
She had felt self-reproached about it long before she apologized. = 在她道歉很久以前，她就为此感到自责。

将来时:
He will be self-reproached if he doesn't apologize. = 如果他不道歉，他会感到自责。
She will likely be self-reproached if she continues to ignore the issue. = 如果她继续无视这个问题，她很可能会感到自责。

虚拟语气:
I would be self-reproached if I had hurt him. = 如果我伤害了他，我会感到自责。
If I had known better, I wouldn't have acted that way, and I wouldn't be so self-reproached. = 如果我早知道，我就不会那样做了，我也不会这么自责了。

被动语态: (This adjective doesn't typically use the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is he self-reproached about what happened? = 他对自己发生的事情感到自责吗？
Was she self-reproached after the argument? = 争吵后她自责了吗？


否定句:
He is not self-reproached for his actions. = 他对自己的行为并不感到自责。
She wasn't self-reproached at all; she just shrugged it off. = 她一点也不自责；她只是耸了耸肩就过去了。
