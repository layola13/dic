hanging-on: /ˈhæŋɪŋ ɒn/ | v. | 坚持；依靠；苟延残喘；紧紧抓住 |

英文解析:The phrasal verb "hanging on" implies a persistent clinging to something or someone, often despite difficulty or adversity. It can refer to physical clinging, emotional dependence, or persevering in a difficult situation.  The meaning is heavily context-dependent.

例句：

He was hanging on to the cliff edge for dear life.  = 他紧紧抓住悬崖边，拼命保命。

She's hanging on to the hope that he'll return. = 她仍然抱着一丝希望，希望他会回来。


近义词:clinging, persevering, persisting, enduring, holding on

反义词:letting go, giving up, abandoning, relinquishing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are hanging on to their old traditions. = 他们坚持着他们的古老传统。
He is hanging on every word she says. = 他仔细听着她说的每一句话。

一般过去时:
The company hung on despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，公司还是坚持了下来。
She hung on to the railing as the boat rocked violently. = 船剧烈摇晃时，她紧紧抓住栏杆。

现在完成时:
They have been hanging on for months, waiting for a miracle. = 他们已经坚持了几个月，等待着奇迹。
The small business has hung on through several tough years. = 这家小企业在经历了几年的艰难岁月后仍然坚持了下来。

过去完成时:
He had hung on to the hope of a reconciliation. = 他一直抱有和解的希望。
She had hung on to that old photograph for decades. = 她几十年来一直珍藏着那张旧照片。

将来时:
We will hang on until the rescue team arrives. = 我们将坚持到救援队到达。
I think they will hang on to their lead in the race. = 我认为他们将在比赛中保持领先地位。


虚拟语气:
If I had hung on a little longer, I might have succeeded. = 如果我再坚持一会儿，也许就能成功了。
I wish I had hung on to that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。

被动语态:  (Not commonly used in passive voice)


疑问句:
Are you hanging on okay? = 你还好吗？（坚持得住吗？）
Is the business still hanging on? = 公司还在坚持吗？

否定句:
He didn't hang on to his anger for long. = 他并没有长时间地生气。
They couldn't hang on any longer; they had to give up. = 他们再也坚持不住了，不得不放弃。
