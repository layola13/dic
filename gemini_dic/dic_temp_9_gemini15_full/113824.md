maddened:/ˈmædnd/| adj.|发狂的；愤怒的；激怒的|Driven to madness or fury; enraged.  The constant noise maddened him.|近义词：enraged, infuriated, enraged, frantic, wild|反义词：calm, composed, peaceful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music maddens me. = 吵闹的音乐让我抓狂。
His stubbornness maddens his wife. = 他的固执让他的妻子抓狂。


一般过去时:
The delay maddened the passengers. = 延误让乘客们抓狂。
The heat maddened the animals. = 炎热的天气让动物们发狂。


现在完成时:
The children's constant fighting has maddened their parents. = 孩子们不停的打架已经把他们的父母逼疯了。
His incompetence has maddened his colleagues. = 他的不称职已经激怒了他的同事们。


过去完成时:
He had been maddened by the injustice. = 他因为不公正而变得怒不可遏。
She had already been maddened by his behavior before he even spoke. =  在他开口之前，他的行为就已经让她怒火中烧了。


将来时:
The long wait will madden them. = 漫长的等待将会让他们抓狂。
Continuous interruptions will madden the speaker. =  不断的打断会让演讲者抓狂。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't do that; it would madden him. = 如果我是你，我不会那样做；那会让他发狂。
I wish that noise wouldn't madden me so. = 我希望那噪音不会让我如此抓狂。


被动语态:
He was maddened by the criticism. = 他被批评激怒了。
The crowd was maddened by the politician's lies. =  民众被政治家的谎言激怒了。


疑问句:
Is he maddened by the news? = 他被这个消息激怒了吗？
Are you maddened by the traffic? = 你被交通堵塞激怒了吗？


否定句:
The news didn't madden her. = 这个消息并没有让她生气。
He wasn't maddened by their insults. = 他并没有被他们的侮辱激怒。


PHRASE->
maddened by = 被...激怒
drive someone mad/madden = 使某人发疯/抓狂
become maddened = 变得愤怒
feel maddened = 感到愤怒
utterly maddened = 极其愤怒
