misclaiming:/mɪsˈkleɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 虚报；错误声称；误称 | The act of making a false or inaccurate statement, often intentionally.  It implies a deliberate attempt to mislead or deceive. |例句：The company was accused of misclaiming the effectiveness of its new drug. 公司被指控虚报其新药的疗效。

The politician was found guilty of misclaiming his expenses.  这位政治家被发现犯有虚报费用的罪行。


近义词：misrepresenting, falsifying, misrepresenting, distorting, exaggerating


反义词：truthfully stating, accurately reporting, verifying


词性:动词
