match:/mætʃ/| n. v.|比赛；匹配；火柴；相配|A contest in which people or teams compete against each other; to be similar or equal to something else; a small stick of wood or cardboard tipped with a flammable substance used for lighting fires; to find something that is similar or equal to something else.|例句：The football match was exciting. 足球比赛很精彩。  The color of the curtains matches the walls. 窗帘的颜色与墙壁相配。|近义词：game, contest, competition, fit, suit, coincide|反义词：mismatch, differ, contrast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teams match every week. = 球队每周比赛。
This dress matches my shoes. = 这件裙子与我的鞋子相配。

一般过去时:
They matched the suspect's description. = 他们与嫌疑人的描述相符。

现在完成时:
We have matched all the donations to the recipients. = 我们已经将所有捐款与接受者匹配。
The police have matched the fingerprints to a known criminal. = 警方已将指纹与已知罪犯进行匹配。

过去完成时:
She had matched the fabric before starting the project. = 她开始项目之前已经匹配好了面料。
They had matched the funds before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经匹配好了资金。

将来时:
The two companies will match their resources for the project. = 这两家公司将为该项目匹配资源。
We will match the grant with our own funding. = 我们将用我们自己的资金来匹配这笔赠款。

虚拟语气:
If the colours matched, the painting would be perfect. = 如果颜色匹配，这幅画就完美了。
I wish my shoes matched my dress better. = 我希望我的鞋子更配我的裙子。

被动语态:
The samples were matched for purity. = 样本的纯度得到了匹配。

疑问句:

否定句:
The accounts don't match. = 账户不匹配。
His story doesn't match the evidence. = 他的说法与证据不符。
