flotillas:/fləˈtɪləz/| n.|小舰队，小型船队 |A flotilla is a small fleet of ships or boats, often sailing together for a particular purpose.  It is usually composed of smaller vessels than a full-fledged fleet. |例句：A flotilla of fishing boats set out to sea at dawn.  一大群渔船在黎明时分出海了。

例句：The harbor was bustling with activity as a flotilla of yachts prepared for the regatta.  港口热闹非凡，一群游艇正准备参加帆船赛。


近义词：fleet, squadron, armada

反义词：None (no direct antonym, but "single ship" or "lone vessel" could be considered contrastive)

词性:noun
