skated:/ˈskeɪtɪd/| v.|滑冰；溜冰|To move on skates.  The word refers to the act of gliding on ice or other surfaces using skates.|例句：He skated gracefully across the ice. 他优雅地滑过冰面。
例句：The children skated happily in the park. 孩子们在公园里快乐地滑冰。|近义词：glided, slid|反义词：walked, ran|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skates every weekend. = 他每个周末都滑冰。
She skates with amazing grace. = 她滑冰姿态优雅迷人。

一般过去时:
I skated on the frozen lake yesterday. = 我昨天在冰冻的湖面上滑冰。
They skated for hours at the rink. = 他们在溜冰场滑了几个小时。

现在完成时:
I have skated many times in my life. = 我一生中滑过很多次冰。
She has never skated before. = 她以前从未滑过冰。

过去完成时:
He had skated before, so he felt confident. = 他以前滑过冰，所以他很有信心。
We had already skated when the rain started. = 下雨的时候我们已经滑完冰了。

将来时:
I will skate tomorrow if the weather is good. = 如果天气好，我明天就去滑冰。
They will skate at the Olympic Games. = 他们将在奥运会上滑冰。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would skate more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常滑冰。
I wish I could skate like a professional. = 我希望我能像专业人士一样滑冰。

被动语态:
The ice rink is often skated on by many people. = 许多人经常在这个溜冰场滑冰。（此处被动语态略显不自然，通常不这样表达）
The competition was skated with great skill. = 比赛以精湛的技巧进行。（此处被动语态侧重比赛过程）


疑问句:
Do you skate? = 你滑冰吗？
Have you ever skated before? = 你以前滑过冰吗？

否定句:
I don't skate very well. = 我滑冰不太好。
He didn't skate yesterday because it was raining. = 昨天因为下雨，他没有滑冰。


PHRASE->
skate over (something):  to avoid dealing with a problem or difficult topic  回避（问题）
