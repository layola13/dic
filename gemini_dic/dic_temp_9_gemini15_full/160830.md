possessions: /pəˈzɛʃənz/ | n. |所有物，财产 |Things that someone owns.  |例句：He carefully inventoried all his possessions before the move. 他在搬家前仔细清点了他所有的财产。

例句：Her possessions were few but precious.她的财产不多，但很珍贵。


近义词： belongings, effects, goods, property | 反义词：nothingness, lack


词源:  From Old French *possessioun, from Latin possessionem (nominative possessio), from possidere "to possess," from potiri "to be master of," from Proto-Indo-European *pot- "master, lord."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He possesses many valuable paintings. = 他拥有许多珍贵的画作。
What possessions do you have with you? = 你随身携带什么物品？


一般过去时:
She possessed a rare talent for music. = 她拥有罕见的音乐天赋。
They possessed a large house in the countryside. = 他们在乡下拥有一栋大房子。


现在完成时:
I have possessed this watch for over twenty years. = 我拥有这块手表二十多年了。
She has possessed that antique vase since her grandmother's death. = 自从她祖母去世后，她就拥有那个古董花瓶了。


过去完成时:
He had possessed the land for generations. = 他世世代代都拥有这块土地。
Before the fire, they had possessed a complete collection of stamps. = 在火灾之前，他们拥有完整的邮票收藏。


将来时:
She will possess a vast fortune after her father's death. = 她父亲死后将拥有巨额财富。
They will possess all the necessary documents by next week. = 下周他们将拥有所有必要的证件。


虚拟语气:
If I possessed a magic wand, I would grant everyone their wishes. = 如果我有一根魔杖，我会满足所有人的愿望。
I wish I possessed the skills to build a house. = 我希望我拥有建造房子的技能。


被动语态:
The land was possessed by the same family for centuries. = 这块土地几个世纪以来一直由同一个家族拥有。
The stolen goods were never possessed by their rightful owner again. = 被盗的货物再也没有回到其合法所有者手中。


疑问句:
What possessions do you value most? = 你最看重什么财产？
Does he possess the necessary qualifications for the job? = 他拥有这份工作所需的资格吗？


否定句:
He does not possess the required skills. = 他不具备所需的技能。
They did not possess enough money to buy a house. = 他们没有足够的钱买房子。
