concourse:/ˈkɒnkɔːrs/ /ˈkɑːnkɔːrs/| n. |大厅；广场；汇合点|A large open area, especially one inside a building such as a station or airport, where people can meet or wait.  |例句：The airport concourse was crowded with passengers waiting for their flights. 机场大厅挤满了等待航班的乘客。

例句：We met in the main concourse of the train station. 我们在火车站的主大厅相遇。


近义词：lobby, hall, plaza, atrium |反义词：None  |词性：名词


词源:

From Middle French *concours, from Latin concursus, from concurro "run together," from com- "together" + curro "run."  The sense evolved from "meeting" or "gathering" to the architectural space where such gatherings occur.
