nonlethally: /ˌnɒnˈliːθəli/ | adj. |非致命地；不致死地 |  In a way that does not cause death.  This adjective describes actions or methods that are designed to incapacitate or subdue without resulting in death. |例句：The police used nonlethal weapons to subdue the suspect.  警察使用非致命武器制服了嫌疑犯。


例句：The protesters were advocating for nonlethal crowd control methods.  抗议者们倡导使用非致命的控制人群的方法。


近义词：harmlessly, safely, peacefully


反义词：lethally, fatally, mortally


词性：形容词
