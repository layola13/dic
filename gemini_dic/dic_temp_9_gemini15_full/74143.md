founder:/ˈfaʊndər/| n. v.|创始人，奠基者；失败者；沉没|  A founder is a person who establishes or creates something, typically an organization or institution.  As a verb, it means to sink or become submerged; to fail or collapse; to stumble or falter. |例句：Steve Jobs was a founder of Apple. 史蒂夫·乔布斯是苹果公司的创始人。The ship founder in the storm. 船在风暴中沉没了。|近义词：(n.) originator, establisher, initiator; (v.) sink, collapse, fail |反义词：(n.) follower, destroyer; (v.) succeed, float, thrive |名词，动词
