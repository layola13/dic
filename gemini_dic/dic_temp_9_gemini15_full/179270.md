redistributes:/ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːts/ | vt. |重新分配，再分配 | To distribute or allocate (something) again or differently.  |例句：The government redistributes wealth through taxes and social programs. 政府通过税收和社会福利项目重新分配财富。|例句：The company redistributes its profits among its shareholders. 公司将其利润重新分配给股东。|近义词：reallocate, reallot, redistribute, apportion |反义词：concentrate, centralize
