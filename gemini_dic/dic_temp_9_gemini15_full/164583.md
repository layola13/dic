preexclude: /ˌpriːɪkˈskluːd/ | v. | 预先排除，预先阻止 | To exclude beforehand; to prevent beforehand.  This word is a relatively rare and often seen in formal contexts. It combines the prefix "pre-" (before) with "exclude". |例句：The company preexcluded candidates with insufficient experience from the interview process.  公司在面试过程中预先排除了经验不足的候选人。

The regulations preexcluded any possibility of appeal.  规章预先排除了任何上诉的可能性。


近义词：prevent, preclude, rule out, bar | 反义词：include, permit, allow | 词性: Verb
