clerk:/klɑːrk/| n. |职员；办事员；书记；店员 |A clerk is a person who works in an office, shop, or other business, performing administrative or clerical duties.  |例句：He works as a clerk in a bank. 他在银行做职员。
例句：The shop clerk helped me find the right size.  店员帮我找到了合适的尺寸。|近义词：employee, assistant, attendant |反义词：employer, boss, manager |名词
