collembola:/kəˈlɛmbəlɑː/| n. |弹尾目昆虫|Collembola is an order of small, wingless arthropods belonging to the class Collembola, commonly known as springtails. They are found in diverse habitats worldwide, from soil and leaf litter to aquatic environments.  They are characterized by a unique furcula (spring-like appendage) used for jumping. They play an important role in soil ecosystems as detritivores. |例句：Many species of collembola thrive in moist soil, contributing to nutrient cycling.  许多弹尾目昆虫种类在潮湿土壤中茁壮成长，有助于养分循环。|近义词：springtail (less formal)|反义词:|
