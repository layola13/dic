/ˈkwɒntɪfaɪər/ /ˈkwɑːntɪfaɪər/ | n. | 量词；限定词 | A quantifier is a word or phrase that indicates the quantity or amount of something.  It specifies how many or how much of something is being referred to.  They can be used with both countable and uncountable nouns. |  There are many books on the shelf. (many is a quantifier)  She drank some water. (some is a quantifier) |  many, some, few, several, all, any, each, every, no, much, little |  (none, zero - for a complete lack of something)| noun
