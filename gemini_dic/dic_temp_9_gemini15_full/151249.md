parented:/ˈpærəntɪd/| adj.| 受父母抚养的；有父母的 | Having parents; brought up by parents.  |例句：She was a parented child, unlike her siblings who were raised in foster care. 她是有父母抚养的孩子，不像她的兄弟姐妹是在寄养家庭中长大的。|近义词：raised, nurtured, fostered |反义词：orphaned, parentless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child is parented well. = 这个孩子被很好地抚养。
The plants are parented by experienced gardeners. = 这些植物由经验丰富的园丁照料。


一般过去时:
The dog was parented by a kind family. = 这只狗被一个友善的家庭抚养。
The project was parented by a well-known professor. = 这个项目是由一位著名的教授指导的。


现在完成时:
This project has been parented by the same team for five years. = 这个项目五年来一直由同一个团队负责。
The child has been parented with love and care. = 这个孩子一直被充满爱和关怀地抚养。


过去完成时:
The company had been parented by a larger corporation before it went public. = 这家公司上市前曾由一家更大的公司控股。
The plants had been parented meticulously before the competition. = 这些植物在比赛前都经过精心照料。


将来时:
The project will be parented by a new team next year. = 明年这个项目将由一个新的团队负责。
The child will be parented with the utmost care. = 这个孩子将得到最细致的照顾。


虚拟语气:
If the child were parented differently, she might be happier. = 如果这个孩子被不同的方式抚养，她可能会更快乐。
If the project had been better parented, it might have succeeded. = 如果这个项目得到更好的指导，它可能就成功了。


被动语态:
The children were parented responsibly. = 孩子们得到了负责任的抚养。
The project is being parented effectively. = 这个项目正在得到有效的指导。


疑问句:
Was the child properly parented? = 这个孩子是否得到了适当的抚养？
Is the project being parented effectively? = 这个项目是否正在得到有效的指导？


否定句:
The child was not parented well. = 这个孩子没有得到很好的抚养。
The project is not being parented sufficiently. = 这个项目没有得到足够的指导。


PHRASE->
parented by = 由...抚养/指导
