This adjective describes something that serves as an original model or first example of something. It suggests a foundational or exemplary nature. | Example:  The company unveiled its prototypal model of the new car.  这家公司展示了新款汽车的原型车。  The prototypal hero in many myths is often an underdog who overcomes great obstacles.  许多神话中的原型英雄通常都是克服巨大障碍的弱者。 |近义词：archetypal, exemplary, paradigmatic |反义词：derivative, copied, imitative
