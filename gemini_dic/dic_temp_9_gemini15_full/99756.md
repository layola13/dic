intermeddling:/ˌɪntərˈmɛdliŋ/ | v. | 干涉；干预；多管闲事 | The act of interfering in something that is not one's concern.  It often implies unwelcome or inappropriate involvement. |例句：His constant intermeddling in other people's affairs made him unpopular. 他不断干涉别人的事情，使他不得人心。

例句：Stop intermeddling in my business! 不要干涉我的事情！


近义词：interference, intervention, intrusion, meddling


反义词：noninterference, neutrality, abstention


词性：动词
