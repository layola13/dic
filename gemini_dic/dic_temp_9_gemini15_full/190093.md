sangria:/ˈsæŋɡriːə/| n. |桑格里亚酒|A refreshing wine punch, typically Spanish, made with red wine, fruit (such as oranges, lemons, and berries), and sometimes brandy or other liquors.  It is often served chilled.| 例句：We enjoyed a pitcher of sangria on the patio.  我们在露台上享用了一大壶桑格里亚酒。

例句：The restaurant's sangria was a perfect complement to the tapas. 这家餐馆的桑格里亚酒是塔帕斯菜的完美搭配。


近义词：wine punch, fruit punch


反义词：无


词性:名词
