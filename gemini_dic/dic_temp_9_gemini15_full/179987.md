refilmed:/ˌriːˈfɪlm/| v.|重新拍摄|To film something again, often with changes to the original.|例句：The director decided to refilm the scene because the lighting was poor.  导演决定重新拍摄这场戏，因为灯光不好。The entire movie was refilmed after the first cut proved unsatisfactory.  第一剪辑不满意后，整部电影都被重新拍摄了。|近义词：reshoot, re-record|反义词：film (for the first time)
