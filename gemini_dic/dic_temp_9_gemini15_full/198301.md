shouter:/ˈʃaʊtər/| n. |大声喊叫的人；叫喊者|A person who shouts.  A shouter can be someone who frequently shouts, or someone who is shouting at a particular moment.|例句：The shouter at the protest was eventually arrested.  抗议活动中的那个大声喊叫的人最终被捕了。|近义词：yeller,screamer|反义词：whisperer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shouts loudly at the game. 他在比赛中大声喊叫。
She shouts instructions to her team. 她向她的团队大声喊叫指示。


一般过去时:
He shouted at the top of his lungs. 他扯着嗓子喊叫。
She shouted her approval. 她大声表示赞同。


现在完成时:
He has shouted at me several times. 他已经对我大声喊叫好几次了。
She has shouted for help. 她已经大声呼救了。


过去完成时:
He had shouted warnings before the accident. 事故发生前他已经大声发出警告。
She had shouted herself hoarse. 她已经喊哑了嗓子。


将来时:
He will shout if he sees a bear. 如果他看到熊，他会大喊。
She will shout with joy. 她会高兴地大喊。


虚拟语气:
If he shouted louder, they might hear him. 如果他喊得更大声，他们可能会听到他。
I wish I hadn't shouted at him. 我真希望我没有对他大喊大叫。


被动语态:
The instructions were shouted across the field. 指示在田野上传达。
His name was shouted from the rooftops. 他的名字被大声喊叫着。


疑问句:
Who shouted? 谁在喊？
Why did she shout? 她为什么喊？


否定句:
He didn't shout. 他没有喊叫。
She didn't shout at anyone. 她没有对任何人喊叫。


PHRASE->
shout at  对着…大声喊叫
shout down  大声喝止；压制
shout out  大声喊出；突然高声说
shout with laughter  放声大笑
let out a shout 发出一声喊叫
