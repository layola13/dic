mohican:/ˈmoʊhɪkən/| n. adj.|莫希干人；莫希干式的|A member of a Native American people originally inhabiting the Hudson River valley.  The term is also used to describe a hairstyle. |例句：The Mohicans were a powerful tribe in the Hudson Valley. 莫希干人是哈德逊河谷的一个强大部落。

例句：He sports a Mohican hairstyle. 他留着莫希干发型。

近义词：Native American (for the people),  hawk (for the hairstyle)


反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the opposite of the hairstyle or the opposite of the tribe's cultural traits.


词性:Noun, Adjective
