nudicaul:/ˈnuːdɪkɔːl/| adj.|  无茎的；无叶的 | Having a stem without leaves or leaf-like structures, especially of plants.  The term is often used to describe plants whose flowers arise directly from the ground, seemingly without a stem.  | Example:  Many desert plants are nudicaul, adapting to harsh conditions.  许多沙漠植物是无茎的，以适应恶劣的环境。 The nudicaul flowers sprang directly from the root. 无茎的花直接从根部生长出来。|近义词：acaulescent, stemless|反义词：caulescent
