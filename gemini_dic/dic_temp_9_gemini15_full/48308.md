decrepitness:/dɪˈkrɛpɪtnəs/| n. |衰老；破旧；衰败 |The state of being decrepit; the condition of being worn out, weakened, or broken down by age or use.  It often refers to things or people showing signs of age and deterioration.  |例句：The decrepitness of the old house was evident in its crumbling walls and broken windows.  这栋老房子的破败从其坍塌的墙壁和破损的窗户中可见一斑。

The decrepitness of the elderly man was heartbreaking to witness.  目睹这位老人的衰老令人心碎。|近义词：decay, deterioration, ruin, dilapidation |反义词：youthfulness, vigor, strength, robustness
