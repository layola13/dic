idolum:/aɪˈdɒləm/| n. |偶像，幻象，虚像 | An idolum is a false image or idea, often one that is worshipped or revered.  It can refer to a mental image or a physical representation that is misleading or untrue. The term often carries a connotation of deception or illusion. |Example:  The cult leader fostered idolums of power and invincibility among his followers.  这个邪教领袖在其追随者中灌输了权力和无敌的幻象。|近义词：image, phantom, illusion, delusion |反义词：reality, truth |名词
