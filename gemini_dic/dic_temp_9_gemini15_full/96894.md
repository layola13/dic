inexpensively:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɛnsɪvli/| adv.|廉价地，便宜地|In an inexpensive manner; at a low cost.|例句：We furnished the apartment inexpensively. 我们以低廉的价钱布置了公寓。

例句：They managed to travel inexpensively by taking advantage of budget airlines and hostels. 他们通过利用廉价航空公司和青年旅社，成功地进行了经济实惠的旅行。


近义词：cheaply, affordably, economically

反义词：expensively, costlily


词性：副词
