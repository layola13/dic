phenoms:/ˈfɛnɑːmz/ | n. | 现象级人物；非凡的人才 |  Exceptional or outstanding people, typically in a particular field, who achieve great success at a young age.  They are often considered prodigies or wunderkinds.  |  The young phenoms of the tech world are revolutionizing the industry.  年轻的科技界奇才正在彻底改变这个行业。  |  prodigy, wunderkind, star, superstar |  nobody, nobody special | 名词
