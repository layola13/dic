byland:/ˈbaɪlænd/| n.|内陆地区，内陆；(诗歌中)陆地|An inland area, especially one far from the coast or a major river.  The term often evokes a sense of remoteness and perhaps even a slightly wild or untamed quality, especially in poetic contexts.|例句：The byland was sparsely populated, with small farms dotted across the rolling hills. = 这片内陆地区人口稀少，只有零星的农场散布在连绵起伏的山丘上。|例句：The poet described the byland as a place of mystery and ancient secrets. = 诗人将这片内陆地区描述成一个充满神秘和古老秘密的地方。|近义词：inland, hinterland, backcountry|反义词：coast, seaboard, littoral|名词
