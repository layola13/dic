monodramas:/ˈmɒnəˌdrɑːməz/|/ˈmɑːnəˌdrɑːməz/| n. |独角戏；一人剧 |A monodrama is a dramatic work written for a single performer, often involving multiple characters or voices.  It's a one-person show where the actor portrays different roles. |Example:  The actress gave a captivating performance in the monodrama, seamlessly transitioning between the various characters.  这位女演员在独角戏中奉献了一场引人入胜的表演，流畅地转换了不同的角色。|近义词：one-person show, solo performance |反义词：drama with multiple actors
