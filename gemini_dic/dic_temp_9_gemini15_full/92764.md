ichthyopterygia:/ˌɪkθiˈɒptərɪdʒiə/| n. |鱼龙类；鱼龙目|An extinct order of marine reptiles that resembled dolphins.  They were characterized by their streamlined bodies, paddle-like limbs, and large eyes.  Ichthyopterygia flourished during the Mesozoic Era. |The discovery of ichthyopterygia fossils sheds light on the evolution of marine reptiles. 鱼龙类化石的发现揭示了海洋爬行动物的进化过程。|近义词：暂无|反义词：暂无
