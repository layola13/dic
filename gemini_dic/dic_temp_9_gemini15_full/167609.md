prewritten:/ˈpriːˈrɪtn/| adj.|预先写好的；事先写好的|Already written before a particular time or event.|The prewritten speech was dull and lacked spontaneity.  预先写好的演讲枯燥乏味，缺乏 espontaneidad.|近义词：prepared, drafted, composed|反义词：improvised, spontaneous|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The script is prewritten. = 剧本是预先写好的。
This letter is prewritten. = 这封信是预先写好的。


一般过去时:
The contract was prewritten by the lawyer. = 合同是由律师预先写好的。
The essay was prewritten before the deadline. = 论文在截止日期前就预先写好了。


现在完成时:
The forms have been prewritten for your convenience. = 表格已预先写好，以方便您使用。
They have prewritten all the questions for the exam. = 他们已经预先写好了考试的所有问题。


过去完成时:
The instructions had been prewritten before the meeting. = 会议之前说明书就已经预先写好了。
The speech had been prewritten and carefully rehearsed. = 演讲稿已经预先写好并仔细排练过。

The invitations will be prewritten and sent out next week. = 请柬将在下周预先写好并发出。


虚拟语气:

I wish the answers were prewritten so I wouldn't have to think. = 我希望答案是预先写好的，这样我就不用思考了。

否定句:
