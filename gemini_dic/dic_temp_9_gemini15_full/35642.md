coauthors:/ˈkoʊɔːθər z/| n. |合著者，共同作者 |People who write a book, article, or other work together.  |例句：The coauthors of the study presented their findings at the conference.  合著者的研究结果在会议上发表了。
The novel was written by three coauthors. 这部小说是由三位合著者共同创作的。|近义词：collaborators, joint authors |反义词：sole author, single author |名词
