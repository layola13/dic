psychopathy: /saɪˈkɒpəθi/ | n. |精神变态；精神病态 | A mental disorder in which an individual manifests amoral and antisocial behavior, showing a lack of empathy and remorse.  It is characterized by a persistent disregard for and violation of the rights of others.  This can manifest in various ways, including manipulative behavior, a lack of guilt or remorse, and a superficial charm. | 例句：The psychologist suspected psychopathy in the patient due to their complete lack of remorse for their actions.  精神病学家怀疑病人患有精神变态，因为他们对自己的行为完全没有悔恨。

例句：His callous disregard for others' feelings suggested a possible case of psychopathy. 他对他人感受的冷漠漠视暗示了他可能患有精神变态。


近义词：sociopathy, antisocial personality disorder


反义词：empathy, remorse, conscience
