peppery:/ˈpepəri/| adj.|辛辣的；带胡椒味的|Having a strong, spicy flavor like that of pepper.  It can refer to the taste or smell. |例句：The soup had a peppery kick. 这汤有一股辛辣的味道。

例句：He prefers peppery steak. 他喜欢吃辛辣的牛排。


近义词：spicy, pungent, hot, piquant|反义词：bland, mild, tasteless
