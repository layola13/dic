classifier:/ˈklæsɪfaɪər/ | n. | 分类器，分类者 | A classifier is a person or thing that classifies, or a system used for classifying.  It can refer to a person who sorts items into categories, a machine that automatically categorizes data, or a grammatical element indicating the type of noun. |例句：The librarian acted as a classifier, organizing the new books by subject. 图书管理员充当分类员，按主题整理新书。

The software uses a sophisticated classifier to identify spam emails. 该软件使用复杂的分类器来识别垃圾邮件。

近义词：categorizer, sorter, categorist | 反义词：unifier (in some contexts) | 词性：名词
