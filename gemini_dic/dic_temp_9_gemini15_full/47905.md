decentralise:/ˌdiːˈsentrəlaɪz/ | vt. | 分散权力；权力下放 | To distribute or delegate power away from a central authority.  | The company is decentralising its operations to improve efficiency. 这家公司正在分散其业务以提高效率。 | centralize, devolve | centralize, concentrate | 动词


USAGE->

They decentralise decision-making to regional offices. = 他们将决策权下放给地区办事处。
The government decentralises power to local communities. = 政府将权力下放给地方社区。
