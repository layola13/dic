inflaming: /ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ/ | adj. | 煽动的；激怒的；炎症的 |  causing strong feelings of anger, excitement, or other strong emotions; relating to or causing inflammation. |

例句：
His inflaming speech ignited a riot.  他的煽动性演讲引发了暴乱。

The inflaming article caused widespread outrage. 这篇煽动性的文章引起了广泛的愤怒。


近义词：provocative, incendiary, inflammatory, irritating

反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying


词源:  From Middle English *inflamen, from Old French inflamer (“to set on fire, inflame”), from Latin inflammare (“to set on fire”), from in- (“in, into, on”) + flamma (“flame”).
