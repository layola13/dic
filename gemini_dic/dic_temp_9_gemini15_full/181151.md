reinforcement:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 加强；加固；增援；强化 | The act of strengthening or enforcing something.  It can refer to physical structures, ideas, behaviors, or even groups of people. |例句：The bridge needs reinforcement to withstand heavy traffic. 这座桥需要加固才能承受大量的交通流量。
The teacher's positive reinforcement helped the student improve their grades. 老师的积极强化帮助学生提高了成绩。|近义词：strengthening, support, bolstering | 反义词：weakening, undermining


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Positive reinforcement strengthens desired behaviors. = 积极强化增强了期望的行为。
The army receives reinforcement regularly. = 军队定期接受增援。


一般过去时:
The engineer reinforced the cracked foundation. = 工程师加固了开裂的地基。
The general requested reinforcement for his troops. = 将军要求为他的部队增援。


现在完成时:
They have reinforced the security measures after the incident. = 他们在此事件后加强了安全措施。
The company has reinforced its marketing campaign. = 公司已经加强了它的营销活动。


过去完成时:
The team had reinforced their defenses before the attack. = 团队在攻击前加强了他们的防御。
The government had reinforced its border controls before the crisis. = 政府在危机前加强了边境管控。


将来时:
We will reinforce the walls of the castle. = 我们将加固城堡的城墙。
They will reinforce their position in the market. = 他们将在市场上巩固自己的地位。


虚拟语气:
If we reinforced the structure, it would be more stable. = 如果我们加固结构，它会更稳定。
I wish they had reinforced the team earlier. = 我希望他们早点加强团队。


被动语态:
The bridge was reinforced last year. = 这座桥去年进行了加固。
The policy will be reinforced by the new legislation. = 该政策将由新法规来加强。


疑问句:
Will the company reinforce its commitment to sustainability? = 公司会加强其对可持续发展的承诺吗？
How can we best reinforce these positive behaviors? = 我们如何才能最好地强化这些积极的行为？


否定句:
The company did not reinforce its marketing strategy. = 公司没有加强其营销策略。
They failed to reinforce the bridge before the storm. = 他们在暴风雨前未能加固桥梁。

词源:

The word "reinforcement" comes from the Old French word "renforcement," which is a combination of the prefix "re-" (again) and "forcement" (strengthening).  "Forcement" itself derives from the Latin word "fortis" (strong).  Therefore, the word literally means "to make strong again."
