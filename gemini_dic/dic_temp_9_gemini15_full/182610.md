reoperated:/ˌriːˈɑːpəreɪtɪd/ | v. | 再手术，再次动手术 |  The verb "reoperated" is the past tense and past participle of "reoperate," meaning to operate on again; to perform surgery a second time.  It implies a previous surgical procedure. | Example: The surgeon reoperated on the patient due to complications.  医生因为并发症而再次为病人动手术。 The patient was reoperated on after the infection returned. 病人在感染复发后再次接受了手术。| 近义词: operated again, underwent a second operation | 反义词: N/A
