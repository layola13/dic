cocreate:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪt/ | vt. |共同创造，协同创作 |To create something together; to collaborate in the creation of something.  |例句：The artists decided to co-create a mural depicting their shared vision of the city.  艺术家们决定共同创作一幅壁画，描绘他们对这座城市的共同愿景。

例句：We are co-creating a new marketing strategy with our partners. 我们正在与合作伙伴共同制定新的营销策略。


近义词：collaborate, cooperate, work together | 反义词：destroy, dismantle, independently create | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We co-create innovative solutions for our clients. = 我们为客户共同创造创新的解决方案。
They co-create compelling content for social media. = 他们共同创作引人入胜的社交媒体内容。


一般过去时:
The team co-created a winning proposal for the project. = 团队共同制作了一个成功的项目提案。
We co-created a beautiful garden in our backyard. = 我们在后院共同创造了一个美丽的庭院。

We have co-created several successful products this year. = 今年我们已经共同创造了几款成功的产品。
They have co-created a strong brand identity for their company. = 他们已经为公司共同创造了一个强大的品牌形象。


过去完成时:

We had co-created a detailed presentation by the deadline. = 我们在截止日期前已经共同制作了一个详细的演示文稿。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If we co-created a better strategy, we would achieve greater success. = 如果我们共同制定更好的策略，我们将取得更大的成功。
I wish we had co-created that project earlier. = 我希望我们早点共同完成那个项目。


被动语态:
A new marketing campaign was co-created by the two companies. = 新的营销活动是由两家公司共同创建的。
The innovative design was co-created by a team of designers and engineers. = 创新的设计是由一个设计师和工程师团队共同创作的。


疑问句:

否定句:
