photocopies:/ˈfoʊtoʊkɑːpiːz/| n. |复印件，影印件 | Copies of documents or images made using a photocopier.  Photocopies are widely used in offices and schools for making quick duplicates of materials. |例句：I need to make some photocopies of my passport. = 我需要复印几份我的护照。

例句：She handed me a stack of photocopies. = 她递给我一叠复印件。


近义词：copies, reproductions

反义词：originals


词性:名词
