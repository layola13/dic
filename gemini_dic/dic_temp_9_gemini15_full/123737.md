moonshot:/ˈmuːnʃɒt/| n.|登月计划；极具雄心且可能难以实现的目标|An ambitious and innovative project, especially one with a low probability of success but with potentially high rewards.|例句：The company's new project is a real moonshot, aiming to revolutionize the industry. 这家公司的新项目是一个真正的登月计划，旨在彻底改变该行业。|近义词：ambitious project, long shot, high-risk venture|反义词：sure thing, easy task
