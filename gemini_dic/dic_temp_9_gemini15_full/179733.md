reequipment:/ˌriːɪˈkwɪpmənt/| n.|重新装备，再装备|The act of equipping again;  supplying with new equipment after loss or damage.|The factory underwent a major reequipment program to increase efficiency.  工厂进行了一次大规模的再装备计划以提高效率。|近义词：refitting, retooling|反义词：disarmament, decommissioning

一般现在时:
The company reequips its factories annually. = 公司每年都会重新装备其工厂。
They reequip the army with modern weapons. = 他们用现代武器重新装备军队。


一般过去时:
The army reequipped itself after the war. = 战争结束后，军队进行了重新装备。
