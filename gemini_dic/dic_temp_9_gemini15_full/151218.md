pardons:/ˈpɑːrdənz/| n. |宽恕；赦免；道歉|The act of forgiving or being forgiven for an offense or wrongdoing;  an act of excusing or overlooking a fault or mistake; an apology.|例句：He asked for pardons for his rude behavior. 他为他的粗鲁行为道歉。|例句：The governor granted him a pardon. 州长赦免了他。|近义词：forgiveness, apology, remission|反义词：condemnation, punishment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pardons his friend's mistakes. = 他原谅朋友的错误。
She pardons the offender. = 她赦免了罪犯。

一般过去时:
The king pardoned the traitor. = 国王赦免了叛徒。
He pardoned her for her lies. = 他原谅了她的谎言。

现在完成时:
The court has pardoned several prisoners. = 法院已经赦免了几名囚犯。
She has pardoned him for his past sins. = 她已经原谅了他过去的罪过。

过去完成时:
The judge had pardoned him before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，法官已经赦免了他。
He had pardoned her many times before. = 他以前已经原谅过她很多次了。

将来时:
The president will pardon the convicted criminal. = 总统将赦免被判刑的罪犯。
I believe he will pardon me eventually. = 我相信他最终会原谅我。


虚拟语气:
If he would only pardon me, I could move on. = 如果他肯原谅我，我就能继续生活了。
I wish he would pardon my intrusion. = 我希望他能原谅我的冒犯。

被动语态:
The prisoner was pardoned by the governor. = 囚犯被州长赦免了。
His offenses were pardoned after many years. = 他的罪行在许多年后被赦免了。

疑问句:
Will the court pardon him? = 法院会赦免他吗？
Will she pardon my lateness? = 她会原谅我的迟到吗？

否定句:
The judge did not pardon the defendant. = 法官没有赦免被告。
He will not pardon her easily. = 他不会轻易原谅她。


PHRASE->
ask for pardon = 请求原谅
beg pardon = 对不起
pardon my French = 对不起（用于说粗话后）
grant a pardon = 赦免
seek pardon = 寻求宽恕
