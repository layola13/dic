cross-beam:/ˈkrɒsbiːm/| n. |横梁|A horizontal beam that crosses another beam or structure, often used for support or reinforcement.|The cross-beam supported the weight of the roof.|近义词：transverse beam, crossbar|反义词：vertical beam


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cross-beam supports the roof. = 横梁支撑着屋顶。
That cross-beam connects the two walls. = 那根横梁连接着两面墙。


一般过去时:
The old cross-beam collapsed under the weight. = 那根旧横梁在重量下坍塌了。
The workers replaced the damaged cross-beam. = 工人们更换了损坏的横梁。


现在完成时:
They have installed a new cross-beam in the structure. = 他们在结构中安装了一根新的横梁。
The engineers have inspected all the cross-beams for damage. = 工程师们检查了所有横梁是否有损坏。


过去完成时:
The building had already been strengthened with additional cross-beams before the earthquake. = 地震前大楼已经用额外的横梁加固过了。
