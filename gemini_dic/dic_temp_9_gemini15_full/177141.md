reapproving:/ˌriːəˈpruːvɪŋ/| v.| 再批准，重新批准 |  The act of approving something again, often after a previous rejection or review.  It implies a second consideration and confirmation. | 例句：After further review, the committee is reapproving the project budget.  = 在进一步审查之后，委员会正在重新批准项目预算。

The board is reapproving the marketing plan with minor adjustments. = 董事会正在对营销计划进行小幅调整后重新批准。| 近义词：re-approving, reaffirming, ratifying | 反义词：rejecting, disapproving


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company reapproves the employee's expense report. = 公司重新批准了员工的费用报告。
They regularly reapprove the safety protocols. = 他们定期重新批准安全协议。


一般过去时:
The council reapproved the zoning change last week. = 委员会上周重新批准了分区变更。
The manager reapproved the leave request after further investigation. = 经理在进一步调查后重新批准了休假请求。


现在完成时:
The government has reapproved the infrastructure project. = 政府已经重新批准了基础设施项目。
The committee has reapproved the funding for the research. = 委员会已经重新批准了该研究的资金。


过去完成时:
They had reapproved the contract before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前重新批准了合同。
The board had reapproved the merger agreement after lengthy discussions. = 董事会在长时间讨论后重新批准了合并协议。


将来时:
The authority will reapprove the permit next month. = 当局将在下个月重新批准许可证。
We expect they will reapprove the application after the corrections. = 我们预计他们在更正后会重新批准申请。


虚拟语气:
If they reapproved the loan, we could start the project. = 如果他们重新批准贷款，我们就可以启动项目。
I wish they would reapprove my proposal. = 我希望他们能重新批准我的提案。


被动语态:
The proposal was reapproved by the committee. = 该提案得到了委员会的重新批准。
The budget was reapproved after some revisions. = 预算在一些修改后被重新批准。


疑问句:
Will the board reapprove the acquisition? = 董事会会重新批准收购吗？
Has the application been reapproved yet? = 申请已被重新批准了吗？


否定句:
The bank did not reapprove the loan application. = 银行没有重新批准贷款申请。
The committee refused to reapprove the resolution. = 委员会拒绝重新批准决议。

词源:

re- (prefix indicating repetition or again) + approve (from Old French *aprouver, from Latin approbare, "to approve," from ad- "to" + probare "to test, prove")
