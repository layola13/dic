republicanism:/rɪˈpʌblɪkənɪzəm/| n. |共和主义；共和国制度|The belief in or practice of a republican system of government.|The rise of republicanism in the 18th century led to many revolutions.|近义词：democracy, self-government|反义词：monarchism, autocracy|名词

一般现在时:
Republicanism emphasizes citizen participation in government. = 共和主义强调公民参与政府。
The principles of republicanism are enshrined in the constitution. = 共和主义的原则在宪法中得到体现。


一般过去时:
The American Revolution championed republican ideals. = 美国革命捍卫了共和理想。
Republicanism spread throughout Europe during the 19th century. = 共和主义在19世纪传播到整个欧洲。


现在完成时:
Republicanism has influenced many political systems worldwide. = 共和主义影响了世界许多政治制度。
Many countries have embraced republicanism in recent decades. = 近几十年来，许多国家都采用了共和主义。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, monarchism had dominated the political landscape.  = 在革命之前，君主制主导了政治格局。
The people had long yearned for the principles of republicanism. = 人民长期以来渴望共和主义的原则。
