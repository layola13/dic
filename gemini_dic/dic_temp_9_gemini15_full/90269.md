householders:/ˈhaʊsˌhoʊldərz/| n.|户主；家户；住户|People who own or occupy a house or dwelling.  Householders are responsible for maintaining their property and paying property taxes.|例句：The council sent a letter to all householders about the new recycling scheme. 市议会给所有住户寄了一封关于新回收计划的信。
The householders were angered by the constant noise from the nearby construction site.  住户们对附近建筑工地持续不断的噪音感到愤怒。|近义词：homeowners, residents, occupants|反义词：tenants (租户)
