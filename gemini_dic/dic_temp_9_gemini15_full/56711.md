doomsday:/ˈduːmzdeɪ/| n. |世界末日，审判日 |The day of final judgment, especially the end of the world.  It often refers to a catastrophic event leading to widespread destruction or the end of civilization.|例句：Many religious prophecies speak of a coming doomsday. 许多宗教预言都谈到了即将到来的世界末日。

例句：The movie depicts a post-doomsday world where humanity struggles to survive. 这部电影描绘了一个末日后的世界，人类在努力求生。


近义词：apocalypse, end of the world, the last day |反义词：beginning, genesis, creation|词性：名词
