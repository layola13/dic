employable: /ɪmˈplɔɪəbl/ | adj. | 可雇用的，适合工作的 | Having the skills, experience, and personal qualities that make someone suitable for employment.  Someone who is employable possesses the necessary qualifications and attributes to be hired for a job. |


例句：

He improved his employability by taking extra courses. = 他通过选修课程提高了自己的就业能力。

With her strong skills and experience, she is highly employable. =凭借她扎实的技能和经验，她非常容易找到工作。


近义词：suitable, qualified, eligible

反义词：unemployable


词源:

The word "employable" is derived from the verb "employ"  and the suffix "-able," which means "capable of being."  "Employ" itself comes from the Old French "emploier," ultimately from the Latin "in + plus + implicare," meaning "to fold in," "to involve," then evolving to mean "to use" or "to hire."
