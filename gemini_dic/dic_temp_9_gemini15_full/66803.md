expanding:/ɪkˈspændɪŋ/ | adj. | 扩张的；扩展的；膨胀的 |  Describes something that is becoming larger in size, scope, or extent.  It can refer to physical growth, the increase in something's influence, or the development of something new. | 例句：The expanding universe is a fascinating concept.  宇宙膨胀是一个引人入胜的概念。

The company is experiencing an expanding market share. 公司正在经历市场份额的扩张。


近义词：growing, enlarging, broadening, swelling, expanding upon


反义词：contracting, shrinking, diminishing, reducing


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The universe is expanding. = 宇宙正在膨胀。
The company is expanding its operations. = 公司正在扩大业务。


一般过去时:
The balloon expanded rapidly. = 气球迅速膨胀。
The empire expanded its territory through conquest. = 帝国通过征服扩张了领土。


现在完成时:
The city has expanded significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市已大幅扩张。
My knowledge of the subject has expanded considerably. = 我对这个主题的了解已经大大扩展。


过去完成时:
The ice had expanded before it cracked. = 冰在裂开之前已经膨胀了。
The company had expanded its product line before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经扩大了产品线。


将来时:
The city will expand further in the coming decades. = 未来几十年，这座城市将进一步扩张。
We will expand our research into this area. = 我们将把我们的研究扩展到这个领域。


虚拟语气:
If the economy expanded faster, we would see more jobs. = 如果经济增长更快，我们将看到更多就业机会。
I wish the company had expanded its marketing efforts sooner. = 我希望公司早点扩大营销力度。


被动语态:
The road is being expanded to accommodate more traffic. = 道路正在拓宽以容纳更多交通。
The business was expanded by acquiring a smaller competitor. = 通过收购一家较小的竞争对手，业务得到了扩展。


疑问句:
Is the universe still expanding? = 宇宙还在膨胀吗？
Is the company planning to expand into new markets? = 公司计划进军新市场吗？


否定句:
The metal did not expand when heated. = 金属加热时没有膨胀。
The business did not expand as rapidly as predicted. = 企业的扩张速度不如预期快。
