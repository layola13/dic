roastable:/ˈroʊstəbəl/| adj.| 可烤的，适于烤制的 |Suitable for roasting.  The word implies that the item in question can withstand the heat of roasting and will produce a palatable result.  It often refers to food items like meats, vegetables, or nuts. |例句：These potatoes are roastable. 这些土豆可以烤。

These peppers are perfectly roastable. 这些辣椒非常适合烤制。


近义词：roastable, suitable for roasting, fit for roasting |反义词：unroastable, unsuitable for roasting
