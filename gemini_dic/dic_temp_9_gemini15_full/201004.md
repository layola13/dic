slogged:/slɒɡd/ | v. | 努力工作；艰难地行走；| to work hard and continuously at a task that is difficult or unpleasant; to walk heavily or with difficulty |例句：I slogged away at my assignment all day. 我一整天都在努力完成我的作业。  
I slogged through the mud to reach the other side. 我艰难地趟过泥泞到达了对面。|近义词：toiled, laboured, trudged|反义词：idled, loafed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slogs every day to support his family. = 他每天努力工作养家糊口。
She slogs through her work, even when she's tired. = 她即使疲倦也努力工作。

一般过去时:
We slogged through the snow for hours. = 我们在雪地里艰难跋涉了好几个小时。
He slogged at the project until late into the night. = 他熬夜努力工作直到深夜。

现在完成时:
I have slogged all week to finish this report. = 我为了完成这份报告已经努力工作了一周。
She has slogged her way to the top of her profession. = 她通过努力工作达到了她职业的顶峰。

过去完成时:
They had slogged for weeks before they finally finished the project. = 他们在最终完成项目之前已经努力工作了好几个星期。
We had slogged through the rain for miles before finding shelter. = 我们在找到庇护所之前已经在雨中走了几英里。

将来时:
I will slog through this book, even if it's boring. = 我会坚持读完这本书，即使它很无聊。
They will slog away at their studies until the exams are over. = 他们会努力学习直到考试结束。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be so difficult, I wouldn't have slogged so hard. = 如果我知道这么难，我就不会这么努力了。
I wish I hadn't slogged so much; I'm exhausted. = 我真希望我没有那么努力，我精疲力尽了。

被动语态:
The work was slogged through by the team. = 这个工作被团队艰难地完成了。
The path was slogged through by many hikers before us. = 这条路在我们之前已经被许多徒步旅行者艰难地走过了。

疑问句:
Did you slog through the whole book? = 你把整本书都艰难地读完了吗？
How long did you slog to get here? = 你花了多长时间才艰难地走到这里？

否定句:
He didn't slog at his work today; he took a break. = 他今天没有努力工作；他休息了。
They didn't slog through the mud; they took a different route. = 他们没有趟过泥泞；他们走了另一条路。
