resistively:/rɪˈzɪstɪvli/| adv.| 电阻地，阻抗地 | In a manner characterized by resistance.  Describes something acting or happening with resistance to the flow of electricity or another form of energy. | The current flowed resistively through the damaged wire.  电流阻抗地通过受损的电线流动。|  |  |


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is primarily descriptive.

Example 1: The material behaves resistively at high temperatures.  这种材料在高温下表现出电阻性。
Example 2: The circuit performed resistively due to the faulty resistor.  由于电阻器故障，电路表现出电阻性。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "resistively."  Phrases involving resistance are more common, such as:

1. meet resistance: 遇到阻力
2. offer resistance: 提供阻力
3. overcome resistance: 克服阻力
4. put up resistance: 抵抗
5. line of resistance: 防御线
