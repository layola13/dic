coarctation:/ˌkoʊ.ɑːrkˈteɪ.ʃən/| n. | 狭窄；压迫；收缩 | The narrowing or constriction of a bodily passage or opening, especially of a blood vessel.  This often refers to a congenital heart defect. | Example: The surgeon successfully repaired the coarctation of the aorta.  外科医生成功地修复了主动脉狭窄。


The patient presented with symptoms indicative of aortic coarctation.  病人表现出主动脉缩窄的症状。


近义词：constriction, stenosis, narrowing | 反义词：dilation, widening | 词性：名词
