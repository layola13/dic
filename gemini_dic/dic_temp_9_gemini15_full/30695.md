certificates:/sərˈtɪfɪkəts/| n. |证书，证明 |A document that provides proof or evidence of something.  It can be issued by an organization or individual to verify a person's skills, qualifications, or the authenticity of something. |Examples: He received a certificate of completion after finishing the course. 他完成课程后获得了一张结业证书。  The antique dealer showed me a certificate of authenticity for the painting. 古董商给我看了一张画的真品证明。|近义词：diploma, license, warrant, credential |反义词：disproof, refutation
