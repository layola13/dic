non-assignment:/ˌnɒnəˈsaɪnmənt/| n. |非分配；未分配；免除分配 |The term "non-assignment" refers to the act or state of not assigning something, or the condition of something not being assigned.  It often appears in legal and contractual contexts, indicating that a right, obligation, or property cannot be transferred to another party. |Example: The contract contained a clause specifying the non-assignment of intellectual property rights.  这份合同包含一条关于知识产权不可转让的条款。  |近义词：non-transfer, non-delegation |反义词：assignment, transfer, delegation |名词
