ill-disposedly:/ˌɪl dɪˈspoʊzdli/ | adv. |怀有恶意的；不友善地；不乐意地 |  Acting in a manner showing ill will or bad feelings; unwillingly; reluctantly. |例句：He ill-disposedly ignored her pleas for help. 他恶意地无视了她求助的请求。

例句：She ill-disposedly refused to cooperate with the investigation. 她不情愿地拒绝配合调查。


近义词：maliciously, spitefully, unkindly, grudgingly, reluctantly

反义词：kindly, graciously, willingly, cheerfully, amicably

词性：副词
