objectifying:/ɒbˈdʒektɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 客体化；将…物化 | To treat someone as a thing or an object, rather than as a person with their own feelings and thoughts.  This often involves reducing a person to their physical attributes or sexual value, ignoring their personality, intellect, and individuality. | Example: The advertisement objectifies women by focusing solely on their bodies.  这则广告通过仅仅关注女性的身体来物化女性。 The film objectifies its female characters by reducing them to sexual objects. 这部电影通过将女性角色简化为性对象来物化她们。|近义词：dehumanize, depersonalize, instrumentalize | 反义词：humanize, personalize, respect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He objectifies women in his jokes. 他在笑话里物化女性。
She objectifies her employees, treating them as mere cogs in a machine. 她把她的员工物化了，把他们当作机器上的齿轮。

一般过去时:
