parring:/ˈpærɪŋ/| v.|配对；削皮；(尤指鱼)切片|Pairing refers to the act of matching two things together, often in a complementary way, or the act of peeling or slicing something, especially fish.  |例句：The chef expertly paired the wine with the cheese.  厨师巧妙地将葡萄酒与奶酪搭配在一起。

例句：She spent the morning paring apples for the pie. 她上午花了时间削苹果做馅饼。|近义词：matching, coupling, peeling, filleting|反义词：separating, uncoupling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pairs his socks carefully each morning. = 他每天早上都仔细地配对袜子。
The restaurant pairs its dishes with exquisite wines. = 这家餐厅将菜肴与精美的葡萄酒搭配。


一般过去时:
She paired the red and blue socks. = 她将红袜子和蓝袜子配对。
The chef paired the salmon with a lemon sauce. = 厨师将鲑鱼与柠檬酱搭配。


现在完成时:

He has paired the best wines with our meal for tonight. = 他已经为我们今晚的饭菜搭配了最好的葡萄酒。

虚拟语气:
