perigone:/ˈpɛrɪɡoʊn/| n. |花被;花萼和花冠的合称|The perigone is the collective term for the calyx and corolla of a flower, encompassing all the floral envelopes.  It's particularly used in describing flowers where the calyx and corolla are not easily distinguishable. | Example: The perigone of this tulip is a vibrant red. 这朵郁金香的花被是鲜红色的。|近义词：floral envelope, perianth|反义词：无
