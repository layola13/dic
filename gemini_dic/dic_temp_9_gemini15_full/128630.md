nickelite:/ˈnɪkəlaɪt/| n. |镍铁矿|Nickel arsenide, a mineral consisting of nickel and arsenic. It is a common ore of nickel.  Its formula is NiAs.  The name comes from the presence of nickel. |例句：Nickelite is an important source of nickel for metallurgical purposes.  镍铁矿是冶金用途的重要镍来源。

例句：Samples of nickelite were analyzed for their nickel content.  对镍铁矿样品进行了镍含量分析。


近义词：arsenical nickel (less common)


反义词：无


词性：名词
