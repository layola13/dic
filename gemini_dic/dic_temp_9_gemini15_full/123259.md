monodentate:/ˈmɒnəʊˈdɛnteɪt/|/ˈmɑːnoʊˈdɛnteɪt/| adj. |单齿的；一齿的 |  Describing a ligand that can form only one bond with a central metal atom.  A monodentate ligand binds to a metal center through only one atom.  | Example: Chloride ion (Cl⁻) is a monodentate ligand.  氯离子（Cl⁻）是单齿配体。


The complex [CoCl(NH₃)₅]²⁺ contains a monodentate chloride ligand.  络合物[CoCl(NH₃)₅]²⁺含有单齿氯配体。|近义词:unidentate |反义词:bidentate, tridentate, polydentate
