lava:/ˈlɑːvə/| n. |熔岩，火山岩|Hot molten or semi-molten rock erupted from a volcano.  Lava is typically characterized by its high temperature and viscosity, and it can flow significant distances before solidifying. |The lava flowed down the mountainside, destroying everything in its path.  熔岩顺着山坡流下，摧毁了沿途的一切。|molten rock, magma (after eruption)|solid rock|名词
