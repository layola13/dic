They are often members of a labor union and play a vital role in the shipping industry. |The longshoremen worked tirelessly to unload the cargo from the ship. 码头工人夜以继日地从船上卸货。  Many longshoremen were injured during the storm.  许多码头工人都在风暴中受伤了。|dockworker, stevedore| (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with office worker, etc.)|名词
