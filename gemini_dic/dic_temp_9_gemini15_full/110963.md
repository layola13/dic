liquefies:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪz/| v.|液化；使液化 |To change or become a liquid.  |例句：The gas liquefies under pressure.  气体在压力下液化。|例句：The heat liquefied the butter. 热量使黄油液化。|近义词：melt, fuse, dissolve|反义词：solidify, freeze
