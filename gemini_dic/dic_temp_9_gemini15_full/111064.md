listener:/ˈlɪsənər/| n. |听众，收听者|a person who listens, especially one who listens attentively or regularly to a particular radio or television program or podcast.|例句：The listener was captivated by the speaker's story.  听众被演讲者的故事迷住了。|近义词：hearer, audience|反义词：speaker, broadcaster


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a good listener. = 他是一位好的倾听者。
She listens attentively in class. = 她上课认真听讲。


一般过去时:
I listened carefully to his explanation. = 我仔细听了他的解释。
They listened to the music all night. = 他们整夜都在听音乐。


现在完成时:
I have listened to that song many times. = 我已经听过那首歌很多次了。
She has been a loyal listener to that podcast for years. = 她多年来一直是那个播客的忠实听众。


过去完成时:
I had listened to the entire album before I went to bed. = 我睡觉前已经听完了整张专辑。
They had listened to the news report before they left for work. = 他们上班前已经听过新闻报道了。


将来时:
I will listen to your advice. = 我会听取你的建议。
We will be listening to the concert tomorrow night. = 我们明天晚上将去听音乐会。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better listener, I would understand him better. = 如果我是一个更好的倾听者，我会更好地理解他。
I wish I had listened more carefully to her instructions. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地听她的指示。


被动语态:
The speaker's message was well-received by the listeners. = 演讲者的信息受到了听众的良好回应。
The music is being listened to by many people. = 许多人正在听这音乐。


疑问句:
Are you a good listener? = 你是一个好的倾听者吗？
Who is listening to the radio? = 谁在听收音机？


否定句:
He didn't listen to my warnings. = 他没有听我的警告。
She wasn't listening when I spoke. = 我说话的时候她没在听。


PHRASE->
active listener  积极的倾听者
attentive listener  专注的听众
critical listener  批判性听众
passive listener  被动的听众
ideal listener  理想的听众
