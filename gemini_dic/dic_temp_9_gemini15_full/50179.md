deoperculate:/ˌdiːˈɒpərkjuleɪt/| vt.|去除盖；除去盖子|To remove an operculum from (something), especially a mollusk shell or a snail egg.  The term often refers to removing the cap or lid from something.|例句：The researcher deoperculated the snail eggs before incubation. 研究人员在孵化前去除了蜗牛卵的盖子。

例句：The biologist carefully deoperculated the clam to study its internal anatomy. 生物学家小心地去除了蛤蜊的盖子来研究其内部结构。


近义词：uncapped, unlidded, opened


反义词：capped, covered, sealed


词性：动词
