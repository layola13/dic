nonadaptable: /ˌnɒnəˈdæptəbl/ | adj. | 不可适应的，不能适应的 |  Unable to adapt or adjust to different conditions or circumstances.  The system is inflexible and nonadaptable to changing market demands.  | 近义词：inadaptable, inflexible, unadaptable, rigid | 反义词：adaptable, flexible, adjustable


例句：

The inflexible software is nonadaptable to the new hardware.  = 僵化的软件无法适应新的硬件。

His nonadaptable personality made it difficult for him to adjust to new environments. = 他不适应的性格使他难以适应新的环境。
