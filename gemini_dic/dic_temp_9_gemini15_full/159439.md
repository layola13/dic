polemical:/pəˈlɛmɪkəl/| adj.|好争论的；论战的；引起争议的| relating to or involving strongly critical, controversial, or disputatious writing or speech.|The article presents a polemical attack on the government's policies. 这篇文章对政府的政策进行了尖锐的批评。|近义词：controversial, disputatious, contentious|反义词：uncontroversial, conciliatory
