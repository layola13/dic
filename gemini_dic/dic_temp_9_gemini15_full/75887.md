fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/ | adj. n. | 原教旨主义者；原教旨主义的 | Adhering to fundamental principles, especially of a religious or political doctrine.  Often implies a strict, literal interpretation of texts and a rejection of modern interpretations. |例句：He is a religious fundamentalist who believes in a strict interpretation of the Bible. 他是一个宗教原教旨主义者，相信圣经的严格解释。


例句：The political party is accused of being fundamentalist in its approach to social issues.  该政党被指责在处理社会问题上采取原教旨主义的方法。


近义词：traditionalist, orthodox, literalist | 反义词：liberal, modernist, progressive


词性: adjective, noun
