promiscuous:/prəˈmɪskjuəs/| adj.|滥交的；混杂的；杂乱的|Having or involving sexual relationships with a number of partners.  It can also describe something that is lacking in order or planning, or consisting of diverse elements. |例句：His promiscuous lifestyle led to several sexually transmitted diseases.他的滥交生活导致了几种性传播疾病。

例句：The garden was a promiscuous mixture of flowers and weeds. 花园里杂乱地混杂着花草。|近义词：unselective, indiscriminate, wanton, libertine|反义词：chaste, selective, restrained, monogamous
