postponed:/pəˈspoʊnd/| adj. |推迟的，延期的 |Delayed to a later time.  |例句：The meeting has been postponed until next week.  会议推迟到下周举行。|例句： Due to the storm, the game was postponed. 由于暴风雨，比赛被推迟了。|近义词：delayed, deferred, adjourned |反义词：advanced, expedited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting is postponed. = 会议被推迟了。
The event is postponed until further notice. = 活动推迟到另行通知。


一般过去时:
They postponed the trip. = 他们推迟了旅行。
The concert was postponed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，音乐会被推迟了。


现在完成时:
The project has been postponed several times. = 项目已经被推迟了好几次。
We have postponed our decision until we have more information. = 我们已经推迟了我们的决定，直到我们获得更多信息。


过去完成时:
They had already postponed the deadline before I even asked. = 我甚至还没开口问，他们就已经把截止日期推迟了。
The game had been postponed twice before it finally took place. = 比赛在最终举行之前已经被推迟了两次。


将来时:
They will postpone the launch until next quarter. = 他们将把发布推迟到下个季度。
The wedding will be postponed if the weather is bad. = 如果天气不好，婚礼将被推迟。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, we wouldn't have postponed the picnic. = 如果天气更好，我们就不会推迟野餐了。
I wish we hadn't postponed our vacation. = 我真希望我们没有推迟我们的假期。


被动语态:
The decision was postponed by the committee. = 决定被委员会推迟了。
The flight is being postponed due to technical difficulties. = 由于技术问题，航班正在被推迟。


疑问句:
Has the meeting been postponed? = 会议被推迟了吗？
Will the game be postponed? = 比赛会被推迟吗？


否定句:
The game was not postponed. = 比赛没有被推迟。
We did not postpone the meeting. = 我们没有推迟会议。


PHRASE->
postpone until = 推迟到...
postpone to = 推迟到...
postpone for = 推迟...时间
postpone indefinitely = 无限期推迟
postpone consideration of = 推迟考虑...
