cacolet:/ˈkækəlet/| n. |小驮鞍；便携式马鞍|A small saddle, especially one used for carrying burdens or a child.  It's typically smaller and lighter than a regular riding saddle.|例句：The child rode on the cacolet behind his mother on the donkey.  孩子坐在驴子后面母亲的驮鞍上。|例句：The pack mule carried supplies in a sturdy cacolet.驮骡用结实的驮鞍驮运物资。|近义词：pack saddle, pannier|反义词：无
