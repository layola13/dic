imprecisely:/ɪmˈpraɪsaɪsli/ | adv. | 不精确地，含糊地 |  Not exactly or accurately; vaguely. It suggests a lack of clarity or detail in a measurement, description, or statement. |例句：He imprecisely described the location of the accident. 他含糊地描述了事故发生的地点。
The measurements were imprecisely recorded.  测量记录得不精确。|近义词：inaccurately, vaguely, roughly, approximately |反义词：precisely, accurately, exactly
