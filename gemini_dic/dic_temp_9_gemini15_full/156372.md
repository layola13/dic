physiologist: /ˌfɪziˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |生理学家| A physiologist is a scientist who studies the functions and processes of living organisms.  They investigate how living things work at the cellular, tissue, organ, and system levels.  Their research may cover a wide range of topics, such as the nervous system, the circulatory system, or cellular metabolism. |  Dr. Lee is a renowned physiologist specializing in cardiovascular function.  李博士是一位著名的生理学家，专门研究心血管功能。

The team of physiologists conducted extensive experiments to understand the effects of exercise on the respiratory system.  生理学家团队进行了广泛的实验，以了解运动对呼吸系统的影响。| biologist, anatomist |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps someone who studies solely structure rather than function)
