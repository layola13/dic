return:/rɪˈtɜːrn/|/rɪˈtɝːn/| vt. vi. n. |返回；归还；回报；收益|To go or come back to a place or person. To give something back to someone.  The profit or advantage gained from something.|例句：He returned to his hometown after many years. 他多年后回到了家乡。


He returned the book to the library. 他把书还给了图书馆。


近义词：revert, comeback, restore, repay, yield |反义词：depart, leave, stay, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I return home every day. = 我每天回家。
They return the borrowed books to the library. = 他们把借来的书还给图书馆。


一般过去时:
She returned to her office after lunch. = 她午饭后回到了办公室。
He returned the call immediately. = 他立即回电了。


现在完成时:
I have returned the keys to him. = 我已经把钥匙还给他了。
Have you returned the survey yet? = 你已经寄回调查问卷了吗？


过去完成时:
They had returned home before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经回家了。
She had returned the favor before he asked. = 他还没开口，她就已经报答了他的好意。


将来时:
We will return to this topic later. = 我们稍后再讨论这个话题。
They will return the car tomorrow. = 他们明天会把车还回来。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would return to school. = 如果我有时间，我会回去上学。
I wish I could return to my childhood. = 我真希望我能回到童年。


被动语态:
The book was returned to the shelf. = 书被放回书架上了。
The call was returned promptly. = 电话很快就被回拨了。


疑问句:
When will you return? = 你什么时候回来？
Did he return the money? = 他还钱了吗？


否定句:
He did not return my call. = 他没有回我的电话。
They haven't returned from their trip yet. = 他们还没从旅行中回来。


PHRASE->
return a call = 回电话
return to normal = 恢复正常
return a favor = 帮个忙；报答
return ticket = 往返票
return address = 回邮地址
