decapitate:/dɪˈkæpɪteɪt/| vt.|斩首；使…失去首脑|To cut off the head of (a person or animal).  The verb is often used figuratively to describe the removal of a leader or the destruction of something important.|例句：The king was decapitated by his enemies. 国王被他的敌人斩首了。

The rebels decapitated the statue of the former dictator. 反叛者砍掉了前独裁者雕像的头。

近义词：behead, execute, sever (the head)
反义词：None (direct antonym doesn't exist, but actions like coronation or enthronement are opposite in a figurative sense)
