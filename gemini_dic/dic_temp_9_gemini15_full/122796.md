moldering:/ˈmoʊldərɪŋ/ | adj. | 腐朽的；腐烂的；陈旧的 | Decaying or rotting, especially from age or neglect; musty.  | The moldering remains of the old house were a testament to time's passage.  古老房屋腐朽的遗迹证明了时间的流逝。  | decaying, rotting, decomposing | fresh, new, pristine | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wood is moldering. = 木头正在腐烂。
The old books are moldering on the shelf. = 旧书在书架上发霉腐烂。


一般过去时:
The fruit moldered quickly in the heat. = 水果在高温下很快就腐烂了。
The old building moldered into ruin. = 老建筑腐朽成废墟。


现在完成时:
The fence has moldered away. = 栅栏已经腐朽掉了。
The ancient tomb has moldered over the centuries. = 古墓在几个世纪以来已经腐朽了。


过去完成时:
The wood had moldered before we could salvage it. = 我们还没来得及抢救，木头就已经腐烂了。
The documents had moldered away in the damp basement. = 文件在潮湿的地下室里已经腐烂了。


将来时:
The remains will molder away completely. = 遗骸将会完全腐烂。
The old house will likely molder away in the next few years. = 这栋老房子很可能会在未来几年内腐朽。


虚拟语气:
If the food were left out, it would molder. = 如果食物放在外面，它就会腐烂。
I wish that the old painting hadn't moldered. = 我真希望那幅旧画没有腐烂。


被动语态:
The building was moldering. = 建筑物正在腐朽。
The apples were moldering in the basket. = 苹果在篮子里腐烂了。


疑问句:
Is the wood moldering? = 木头在腐烂吗？
Are those old letters moldering away? = 那些旧信在腐烂吗？


否定句:
The building isn't moldering yet. = 建筑物还没有腐朽。
The apples weren't moldering when I bought them. = 我买苹果的时候它们还没腐烂。

词源:
From Middle English *moldren, from Old English *muldrien, from muld "dust, earth," akin to Old High German mulda "dust."  The sense evolved from "to crumble into dust" to "to decay."
