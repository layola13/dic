schlepp:/ʃlɛp/| vt. n.|拖，拉；费力地搬运；讨厌的人或事 |to carry or drag something heavy or awkward; to move something with difficulty and effort; also used informally to refer to a tedious or unpleasant person or thing. |例句：I had to schlepp all the groceries home. 我不得不费力地把所有杂货扛回家。


例句：That meeting was a real schlepp. 那次会议真是一场折磨。


近义词： lug, haul, drag, carry,  tote


反义词：  (无确切的反义词，取决于语境，可以考虑轻巧地移动等)


词性: 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He schlepps the heavy boxes every day. 他每天都拖着那些沉重的箱子。
She schlepps her luggage to the airport. 她拖着行李去机场。


一般过去时:
I schlepped the furniture upstairs by myself. 我自己把家具搬上了楼。
They schlepped the equipment across the field. 他们把设备拖过了田野。


现在完成时:
He has schlepped groceries for his family for years. 他多年来一直为家人搬运杂货。
She has schlepped through many difficult situations. 她经历过许多困境。


过去完成时:
I had schlepped all the way to the station before realizing I left my ticket at home. 我已经拖着行李走到车站了，才意识到我把车票落在家了。
They had schlepped the heavy equipment to the top of the hill before taking a break. 他们在休息之前把沉重的设备搬到了山顶。


将来时:
I will schlepp the bags to the car. 我会把包搬到车里。
She will schlepp the kids to school tomorrow. 她明天会把孩子们送到学校。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I wouldn't have to schlepp all these bags. 如果我有车，我就不用拖着这些包了。
I wish I didn't have to schlepp all these books. 我希望我不必搬运所有这些书。


被动语态:
The groceries were schlepped home by the delivery person. 杂货由送货员搬回家。
The heavy equipment is being schlepped to the construction site. 沉重的设备正在被运送到建筑工地。


疑问句:
Who schlepped the piano into the house? 谁把钢琴搬进了房子？
Did he schlepp the boxes all by himself? 他一个人把箱子都搬了吗？


否定句:
I didn't schlepp the luggage myself. 我没有自己搬运行李。
He didn't want to schlepp the heavy boxes across town. 他不想把沉重的箱子搬到城里。
