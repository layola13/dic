public-utility:/ˈpʌblɪk juːˈtɪləti/ | n. | 公用事业；公共事业单位 | A public utility is a company that provides essential services to the public, such as electricity, gas, water, or telecommunications. These services are typically regulated by the government to ensure fair pricing and reliable service. | Example:  The city's public utility company raised electricity rates last month.  这个城市的公用事业公司上个月提高了电价。


| 近义词：public service, essential service | 反义词：private enterprise | 词性:名词
