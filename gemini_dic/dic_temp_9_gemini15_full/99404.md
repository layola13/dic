interdiscipline:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsəplɪn/| n. |跨学科，多学科 |The study of interdiscipline involves drawing upon multiple academic fields to address complex problems. 跨学科研究涉及利用多个学术领域来解决复杂的问题。|近义词：multidisciplinarity, cross-disciplinarity |反义词：single discipline, specialization
