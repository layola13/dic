chasseur:/ʃæˈsɜːr/|/ʃəˈsɜːr/| n. |猎手；轻骑兵；（法餐）猎人酱汁|A chasseur is a type of light cavalry soldier, or a hunter, or a rich brown sauce in French cuisine.  The word's meaning stems from its French origins, relating to hunting and hunters.|例句：He was a skilled chasseur, known for his accuracy with a rifle. 他是一位熟练的猎手，以其精准的步枪射击而闻名。|例句：The restaurant's signature dish was the beef in chasseur sauce. 这家餐馆的招牌菜是猎人酱汁牛肉。|近义词：hunter, huntsman, cavalryman|反义词：prey, game
