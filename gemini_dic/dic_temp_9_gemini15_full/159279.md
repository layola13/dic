point:/pɔɪnt/ /pɔɪnt/| n., v.|点；要点；分数；尖端；目的；意义；指向；瞄准|

英文解析：
Noun:  A point can refer to a specific location in space, a dot or mark, a single item or detail in a discussion or argument, a score in a game, or the sharp end of something.  As a verb, it means to direct something towards a particular target or to draw attention to something.

例句：
The point of the story is to show the importance of kindness.  故事的要点是展现友善的重要性。

He scored a crucial point in the final seconds of the game.  他在比赛的最后几秒钟得到一个至关重要的分数。


近义词：
dot, spot, item, detail, purpose, aim, goal, direct, indicate, aim


反义词：
(for noun) area, expanse, generality, (for verb) misdirect, confuse

词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He points at the map. 他指着地图。
The needle points north. 指针指向北方。


一般过去时:
She pointed out my mistake. 她指出了我的错误。
He pointed to the exit. 他指着出口。


现在完成时:
Have you pointed out the problem to him? 你已经向他指出这个问题了吗？
I have pointed out the flaws in their argument. 我已经指出了他们论证中的缺陷。


过去完成时:
He had pointed out the danger before the accident. 事故发生前他已经指出了危险。
She had already pointed out the solution. 她已经指出了解决方法。


将来时:
I will point out the way. 我会指明方向。
He will point to the best option. 他会指出最佳方案。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the right point, I would point it out. 如果我知道正确的点，我会指出来。
I wish I had pointed out the error earlier. 我真希望我早点指出这个错误。


被动语态:
The problem was pointed out by the manager. 问题是由经理指出的。
The way was pointed out to us by a local. 当地人给我们指明了方向。


疑问句:
What point are you trying to make? 你想表达什么观点？
What is the point of this exercise?  这个练习的目的是什么？


否定句:
He didn't point out the error. 他没有指出错误。
I couldn't point out the specific cause. 我无法指出具体原因。
