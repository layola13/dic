The quality of the eatage significantly impacted the livestock's health. 放牧地的质量极大地影响了牲畜的健康。|近义词：pasture, grazing, forage|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "fallow land" (土地休耕) or "enclosure" (圈地) could be considered opposites in terms of access to grazing.
