monastically:/məˈnæstɪkli/| adv.|隐修地；禁欲地；僧侣般地|In a manner characteristic of monks or nuns; in a secluded or austere manner.  It implies a life of seclusion, devotion, and often self-denial.|例句：He lived monastically, devoting himself to prayer and study. 他过着隐修的生活，致力于祈祷和学习。

例句：She lived monastically in a remote cabin, far from the distractions of city life. 她在一个偏僻的小屋里过着隐修的生活，远离城市生活的喧嚣。


近义词：ascetically, austerely, reclusively, solitarily

反义词：worldly, luxuriously, lavishly


词性：副词
