pulverized: /ˈpʌlvəraɪzd/ | vt. adj. | 磨成粉末；使粉碎；彻底摧毁 | to reduce to powder or dust; to destroy completely | 近义词：crushed, ground, smashed, obliterated | 反义词：intact, whole, undamaged


英文解析:
The verb "pulverized" means to reduce something to a fine powder or dust by crushing or grinding.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something that has been completely destroyed or defeated.  As an adjective, it describes something that has been pulverized.


例句:
The machine pulverized the grain into flour. = 机器把谷物磨成面粉。

The earthquake pulverized the city. = 地震彻底摧毁了这座城市。

The boxer pulverized his opponent in the first round. = 拳击手在第一回合就彻底击败了他的对手。

The rocks were pulverized by the glacier. = 岩石被冰川磨成了粉末。
