gorges:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/ | n. |峡谷，峡；（尤指）美丽的峡谷 | A gorge is a deep ravine between cliffs often carved from the landscape by a river.  It is typically characterized by steep, rocky walls and a narrow bottom. Gorges can be breathtakingly beautiful, offering stunning views and opportunities for hiking and other outdoor activities. |例句：The hikers marveled at the stunning beauty of the river gorge.  徒步旅行者惊叹于河流峡谷的壮丽景色。

The Colorado River carved a spectacular gorge through the Arizona desert. 科罗拉多河在亚利桑那州的沙漠中雕刻出一个壮观的峡谷。


近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm | 反义词：plain, plateau
