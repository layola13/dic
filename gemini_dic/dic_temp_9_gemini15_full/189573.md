salesrooms:/ˈseɪlzruːmz/| n.|销售厅；展销室|Rooms where goods are displayed and sold, often in a wholesale or bulk context.  They are typically larger and more formal than retail shops.|例句：The new salesrooms are spacious and well-lit, showcasing the products effectively. 新的销售厅宽敞明亮，有效地展示了产品。

例句：Many antique dealers operate out of salesrooms at auction houses. 许多古董商在拍卖行的销售厅经营业务。|近义词：showrooms, showplaces, exhibition halls|反义词：warehouses (in the sense of storage rather than sales)|词性:名词
