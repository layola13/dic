reaccrediting:/ˌriːˈækrɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新认证，再次认可 | The process of granting accreditation again to an institution or program that has previously held it, often after a review or evaluation.  This typically involves demonstrating continued compliance with standards and requirements. | The university is undergoing reaccrediting this year. 这所大学今年正在进行重新认证。 The school's reaccrediting process was rigorous and thorough. 学校的重新认证过程严格而彻底。 | accredit, recertify, revalidate |  deaccredited, unaccredited | 动词
