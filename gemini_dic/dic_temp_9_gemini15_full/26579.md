cabooses:/kəˈbuːz/| n.|车尾；守车|A caboose is a car on a freight train that is usually placed at the end of the train and is used by the train crew for sleeping, eating, and storing supplies.  It can also be a small room at the end of a passenger car.|例句：The caboose brought up the rear of the long freight train.  这列长长的货运列车后面是守车。The conductor spent his nights in the caboose. 列车员在守车里过夜。|近义词：rear car, tail car|反义词：locomotive, engine
