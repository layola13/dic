fleeten:/ˈfliːtən/ | v. |使…变快；使…迅速消失；（时间）飞逝|To move swiftly; to pass quickly; to vanish rapidly.  |例句：The hours fleeted by. 时间飞逝而过。The magician fleeted the rabbit from the hat. 魔术师让兔子迅速消失在帽子中。|近义词：speed, hasten, vanish, disappear |反义词：linger, dawdle, remain, persist |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river fleeten through the valley. = 河水飞快地流过山谷。
The worries fleeten from his mind. = 他的忧虑迅速消失。


一般过去时:
The days fleeted past during the summer vacation. = 暑假期间，日子过得飞快。
The image fleeted before my eyes. = 那影像在我的眼前一闪而过。


现在完成时:
The years have fleeted by since we last met. = 自从我们上次见面以来，岁月已经飞逝。
The opportunity has fleeted. = 机会已经错过了。


过去完成时:
The vision had fleeted before he could grasp it. =  在他能够抓住它之前，幻象已经消失了。
The feeling of happiness had fleeted. = 幸福的感觉已经消失了。


将来时:
The moments will fleeten as we grow older. =  随着我们年龄的增长，时光将会飞逝。
The opportunity will fleeten if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不行动，机会将会消失。


虚拟语气:
If only the time would fleeten! = 但愿时间能过得快些！
I wish that my worries would fleeten. = 但愿我的忧虑能快点消失。


被动语态:
The opportunity was fleeted. = 机会错过了。(被动语态用得较少，因为fleeten本身就表示主动的动作)
The ship was fleeted away by the storm. = 船被风暴卷走了。(与fleeten含义略有不同)


疑问句:
Will the years fleeten quickly? = 岁月会飞逝吗？
Did the colors fleeten as the painting aged? = 画作随着时间的推移，颜色会褪去吗？


否定句:
The moment didn't fleeten. = 时刻并未流逝。
The pain didn't fleeten easily. = 疼痛并没有轻易消退。


PHRASE->
fleeten away: 消失；消逝
fleeten by: 飞逝；迅速过去
fleeten past: 迅速过去；一闪而过
fleeten from view: 从视野中消失
fleeten through: 快速穿过；飞速通过
