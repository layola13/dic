shipyard:/ˈʃɪpjɑːrd/| n. |船坞，造船厂 |A shipyard is a place where ships are built, repaired, and maintained.  It typically includes dry docks, wharves, and other facilities necessary for shipbuilding and ship repair. |例句：The shipyard is a bustling place, with workers welding, painting, and assembling ships.  造船厂是一个繁忙的地方，工人们在那里焊接、油漆和组装船只。
The old shipyard has been converted into a museum.  旧船坞已经被改造成博物馆了。|近义词：dockyard, shipbuilding facility |反义词:None
