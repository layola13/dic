ftped:/fɪpt/| v.|文件传输协议(FTP)传输|File Transfer Protocol (FTP)ed,  past tense of FTP (verb).  It indicates that a file or files have been transferred using the File Transfer Protocol. |Example: I ftped the files to the server. 我通过FTP将文件传输到服务器。|近义词：uploaded, transferred, transmitted|反义词：downloaded, received


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I ftp files to the server daily. = 我每天将文件传输到服务器。
She ftps her work to the cloud. = 她将她的工作通过FTP传输到云端。

一般过去时:
He ftped the document last night. = 他昨晚通过FTP传输了该文件。
They ftped the images to the client. = 他们将图像通过FTP传输给了客户。

现在完成时:
I have ftped all the necessary files. = 我已经通过FTP传输了所有必要的文件。
She has ftped the report to her boss. = 她已经通过FTP将报告传输给了她的老板。

过去完成时:
He had ftped the data before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经通过FTP传输了数据。
They had ftped the presentation before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经通过FTP传输了演示文稿。

将来时:
