presumptuous:/prɪˈzʌmptʃuəs/| adj. | 专横的；冒失的；放肆的 |  Assuming too much; taking liberties; impertinent.  It implies an overstepping of boundaries, often in a way that is arrogant or disrespectful. |


例句：

It was presumptuous of him to assume he could use my car without asking.  = 他未经允许就使用我的车，真是太放肆了。

Her presumptuous comments offended many people in the room. = 她那傲慢无礼的言论冒犯了房间里许多人。


近义词： arrogant, overconfident, impertinent, audacious, forward


反义词： humble, modest, respectful, deferential


词性：形容词
