constitutes:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːts/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtjuːts/ | vt. |构成，组成，等同于 |To make up or form something.  To be equivalent to or the same as something.|例句：Many factors constitute a successful business.  许多因素构成了成功的企业。

The evidence constitutes proof of his guilt.  证据足以证明他有罪。


近义词：compose, comprise, make up, form, represent

反义词：destroy, dismantle, deconstruct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These elements constitute a compound. = 这些元素构成一个化合物。
Hard work and dedication constitute success. = 辛勤工作和奉献构成成功。


一般过去时:
The committee constituted a special task force. = 委员会成立了一个特别工作组。
His actions constituted a breach of contract. = 他的行为构成违约。


现在完成时:
The new law has constituted a significant change. = 新法律已经构成了一项重大变化。
Years of neglect have constituted a serious problem. = 多年的忽视已经构成一个严重的问题。


过去完成时:
The evidence had constituted sufficient grounds for arrest. = 证据已经构成足够的逮捕理由。
Before the meeting, they had constituted a plan. = 会议之前，他们已经制定了一个计划。


将来时:
The new regulations will constitute a major challenge. = 新规将构成一项重大挑战。
The results of the election will constitute a mandate for change. = 选举结果将构成变革的授权。


虚拟语气:
If this action constituted a crime, he would be arrested. = 如果此行为构成犯罪，他将被逮捕。
I wish my efforts constituted a greater impact. = 我希望我的努力能产生更大的影响。


被动语态:
The team was constituted by members from different departments. = 该团队由来自不同部门的成员组成。
The new government was constituted after the election. = 新政府在选举后成立。


疑问句:
What constitutes a fair trial? = 什么构成公正的审判？
Does this behavior constitute harassment? = 这种行为构成骚扰吗？


否定句:
His words did not constitute a threat. = 他的话并不构成威胁。
The agreement did not constitute a binding contract. = 该协议并不构成具有约束力的合同。
