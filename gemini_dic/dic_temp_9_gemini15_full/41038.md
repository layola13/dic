copepod:/ˈkoʊpəpɒd/ /ˈkɒpəpɒd/| n. |桡足类动物|A small crustacean of the order Copepoda, typically having a segmented body with a pair of antennae and swimming by means of legs.| 例句：Many copepods are important members of the zooplankton community. 许多桡足类动物是浮游动物群落的重要成员。
Many fish feed on copepods as a primary food source.许多鱼类以桡足类动物为主要食物来源。|近义词：crustacean, zooplankton|反义词: 无
