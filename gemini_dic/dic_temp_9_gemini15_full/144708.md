ovariotomist:/ˌoʊvəriˈɑːtəmɪst/| n. |卵巢切除术医生；妇产科医生|A surgeon who performs ovariotomies (surgical removal of an ovary or ovaries).  The term is largely outdated and rarely used in modern medical contexts, as it's more common to refer to gynecologists or surgeons specializing in specific reproductive procedures.|例句：In the 19th century, ovariotomists were pioneers in surgical techniques.|近义词：gynecologist, surgeon, reproductive surgeon|反义词：N/A
