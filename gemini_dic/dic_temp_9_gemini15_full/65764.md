everybody: /ˈɛvriˌbɑːdi/ | pron. | 每个人，大家 |  Everybody refers to all people in a particular group or place.  It is used as a pronoun and is always singular in number, despite referring to multiple people. |

例句：Everybody enjoyed the party.  = 大家都很享受这次聚会。

例句：Everybody needs to be responsible for their actions. = 每个人都需要为自己的行为负责。


近义词：everyone, all, each person

反义词：nobody, no one


词性：代词
