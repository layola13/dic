goody:/ˈɡʊdi/| n.|好东西，甜食；乖孩子|A good thing, especially a sweet treat; a well-behaved child.|例句：The children were rewarded with goodies after their chores. 孩子们完成家务后得到了奖励——一些好吃的。|例句：She's such a goody-two-shoes; always following the rules. 她真是个循规蹈矩的好孩子；总是遵守规则。|近义词：treat, sweet, darling|反义词：baddie, naughty child
