console:/ˈkɒnsəʊl/ /ˈkɑːnsoʊl/| n. v.|控制台；安慰；操纵台；|A console is a device or part of a device that contains controls for operating another device or system.  It can also refer to the act of comforting someone. |例句：The game console is broken. = 游戏机坏了。
The mechanic consoled the worried driver. = 技师安慰了焦虑的司机。|近义词：control panel, dashboard, comfort, soothe|反义词：distress, upset, agitate|名词，动词
