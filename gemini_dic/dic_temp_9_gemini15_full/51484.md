detoxing:/ˌdiːˈtɒksɪŋ/|/ˈdiːtɑːksɪŋ/| v.|排毒；净化；戒毒|The process of removing toxins from the body or a system.  It can refer to physical detoxification, such as through a cleanse, or to mental or emotional detoxification, such as overcoming addiction.|例句：She's detoxing after a long period of heavy drinking. 她经过长时间的酗酒后正在戒酒排毒。

She is detoxing from social media to improve her mental health. 为了改善心理健康，她正在戒除社交媒体。|近义词：purifying, cleansing, cleansing, ridding|反义词：toxifying, polluting
