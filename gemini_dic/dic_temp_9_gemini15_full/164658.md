preexperiment:/ˌpriːɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/| n. |预实验，预备实验|A preliminary experiment conducted to test the feasibility of a research method or to refine a research design before the main experiment.  It helps to identify potential problems and improve the main study's design and execution. |Example:  The researchers conducted a preexperiment to determine the optimal dosage of the drug before starting the main clinical trial.  研究人员在开始主要的临床试验之前进行了一次预实验，以确定最佳药物剂量。|近义词：pilot study, preliminary trial, trial run|反义词：main experiment, final experiment|名词
