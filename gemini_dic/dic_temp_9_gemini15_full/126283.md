myna:/ˈmaɪnə/ | n. |八哥|A myna is a type of bird, typically from Asia, known for its ability to mimic sounds, including human speech.|例句：The myna perched on the branch and chattered incessantly. 那只八哥栖息在树枝上，喋喋不休地叫个不停。|  例句：We heard a myna mimicking a car alarm. 我们听到一只八哥在模仿汽车警报声。|近义词：starling (although not as close a relative)|反义词：无|名词
