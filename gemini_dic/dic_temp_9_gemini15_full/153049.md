peeping:/ˈpiːpɪŋ/| v. |偷窥；窥视|The act of secretly looking at something or someone, especially in a way that is considered improper or intrusive.|例句：He was caught peeping into his neighbor's window. 他被抓到偷窥邻居的窗户。|例句：The children were peeping through the keyhole. 孩子们正在透过钥匙孔偷看。|近义词：spying, spying on, watching secretly |反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peeping into the window. = 他正在偷窥窗户。
They peep at the birds. = 他们在偷看鸟儿。


一般过去时:
I peeped into the room. = 我偷偷地看了一眼房间。
She peeped through the curtains. = 她透过窗帘偷看。


现在完成时:
He has been peeping at his neighbor for weeks. = 他已经偷窥他的邻居好几个星期了。
