re-categorize:/ˌriːˈkætəɡəraɪz/| vt.|重新分类，重新归类 |To classify or categorize something again, often with a different system or into different groups.  |例句：We need to re-categorize the data to better analyze the trends. 我们需要重新分类数据以便更好地分析趋势。
例句：The librarian decided to re-categorize the books according to their subject matter. 图书管理员决定根据书籍主题重新分类。|近义词： reclassify, reorganize, regroup |反义词：classify (without re-), leave as is
