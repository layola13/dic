click:/klɪk/| /klɪk/| vt. vi. n.|点击；咔哒声|To make a short, sharp sound, or to cause something to make this sound.  Also, to press a button or icon on a computer screen or similar device.|例句：The door clicked shut. 门咔哒一声关上了。
例句：I clicked on the link to open the website. 我点击链接打开了网站。|近义词：tap, press, snap|反义词:  (无明确反义词，取决于语境)|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clicks the mouse. = 他点击鼠标。
She clicks the link every day. = 她每天都点击这个链接。

一般过去时:
I clicked the button. = 我点击了按钮。
He clicked his tongue in disapproval. = 他咂舌表示不满。

现在完成时:
She has clicked the “like” button on many posts. = 她已经点击了很多帖子的“点赞”按钮。
I have clicked on that ad several times. = 我已经多次点击了那个广告。

过去完成时:
He had clicked the wrong button before he realized his mistake. = 他意识到错误之前已经点击了错误的按钮。
She had clicked submit before she double-checked her answers. = 她在仔细检查答案之前就点击了提交。

将来时:
I will click the button when I'm ready. = 我准备好了就会点击按钮。
He will click on the link later. = 他稍后会点击链接。

虚拟语气:
If I clicked the button, the program would start. = 如果我点击按钮，程序就会启动。
I wish I had clicked the "save" button before the power went out. = 我希望断电之前我已经点击了“保存”按钮。

被动语态:
The button was clicked accidentally. = 按钮被意外点击了。
The link is clicked frequently. = 这个链接经常被点击。

疑问句:
Did you click the button? = 你点击了按钮吗？
Have you ever clicked on a suspicious link? = 你曾经点击过可疑链接吗？

否定句:
I didn't click the link. = 我没有点击链接。
He hasn't clicked the "submit" button yet. = 他还没有点击“提交”按钮。
