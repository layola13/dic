myrobalan:/ˌmaɪrəˈbælən/| n. |榅桲果, 乌桕子 |A myrobalan is the fruit of certain trees of the genus *Terminalia*, especially *Terminalia chebula*, used in tanning and medicine.  Myrobalans are astringent and have been used traditionally for various medicinal purposes. |Example:  The ancient Egyptians used myrobalans in their tanning processes.|近义词：Terminalia fruit |反义词：N/A
