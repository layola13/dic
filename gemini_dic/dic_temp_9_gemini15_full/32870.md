chomp:/tʃɑːmp/| vt.|用力咀嚼，大声咀嚼|To bite or chew with a forceful action, often making a loud noise.|例句：The dog chomped on the bone.  那条狗用力啃着骨头。|例句：She chomped her gum loudly. 她大声地嚼着口香糖。|近义词：chew, munch, gnaw|反义词：sip, nibble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chomps his food loudly. = 他大声咀嚼食物。
The cow chomps grass all day. = 母牛整天都在吃草。

一般过去时:
The child chomped on an apple. = 孩子用力咬着一个苹果。
She chomped her way through a whole pizza. = 她狼吞虎咽地吃掉了一整张披萨。

现在完成时:
He has chomped through three carrots already. = 他已经啃掉了三根胡萝卜。
The horse has chomped all the hay. = 马已经把所有的干草都吃光了。

过去完成时:
She had chomped all the cookies before I arrived. = 我到之前，她已经把所有的饼干都吃掉了。
The rabbit had chomped a hole in the garden fence. = 兔子已经啃坏了花园的栅栏。

将来时:
He will chomp on some popcorn during the movie. = 他将在看电影期间吃爆米花。
The squirrels will chomp the nuts in the autumn. = 松鼠将在秋天啃食坚果。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would chomp on that sandwich. = 如果我饿了，我会吃掉那个三明治。
I wish I could chomp on a juicy steak. = 我真希望我能吃一块多汁的牛排。

被动语态:
The apple was chomped by the child. = 苹果被孩子咬了。
The carrots were chomped by the rabbit. = 胡萝卜被兔子啃了。

疑问句:
Is he chomping on something? = 他在吃什么东西吗？
Does the dog usually chomp its food so loudly? = 那条狗通常吃东西那么大声吗？

否定句:
He didn't chomp on the apple. = 他没有咬苹果。
She doesn't chomp her food; she chews it delicately. = 她不吃东西那么用力，她吃得很优雅。
