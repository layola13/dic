memorises:/ˈmeməraɪzɪz/ | v. |记住；熟记；背诵 | To commit to memory; to learn by heart.  |He memorises long passages of poetry.| 近义词：learn, commit to memory, retain | 反义词：forget, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He memorises the vocabulary every day. = 他每天都记住词汇。
She memorises poems for her recitation. = 她为了朗诵而背诵诗歌。

一般过去时:
I memorised the entire speech for the presentation. = 我记住了整个演讲稿用于演示。
They memorised the lyrics before the concert. = 他们在音乐会之前记住了歌词。

现在完成时:
She has memorised all the capitals of the world. = 她已经记住了世界上所有的首都。
He has memorised the entire script for the play. = 他已经记住了整个戏剧的剧本。

过去完成时:
I had memorised the poem before the competition. = 我在比赛前已经背好了这首诗。
They had memorised the song for weeks before the performance. = 他们在演出前几周就记住了这首歌。

将来时:
I will memorise the phone number before I leave. = 我会在离开之前记住电话号码。
She will memorise the new vocabulary for the exam. = 她会记住考试的新词汇。

虚拟语气:
If I memorised the formula, I could solve the problem. = 如果我记住了公式，我就能解决这个问题。
I wish I had memorised the directions. = 我真希望我记住了路线。

被动语态:
The poem was memorised by many students. = 这首诗被许多学生背诵了。
The lines were memorised word for word. = 这些台词被逐字逐句地背诵了。

疑问句:
Have you memorised the speech yet? = 你背好演讲稿了吗？
Did he memorise the song for the karaoke? = 他为卡拉OK记住了这首歌吗？

否定句:
I could not memorise the long list of names. = 我记不住那长长的名字列表。
She didn't memorise the password. = 她没有记住密码。


PHRASE->
memorise something easily =  轻松记住某事
memorise something by heart =  死记硬背某事
memorise something word for word =  逐字逐句地记住某事
memorise a speech =  背诵演讲稿
try to memorise =  尝试记住


词源:
From Middle English *memorisen, from Old French memoriser, from Latin memorāre "to remind, mention", from memor "mindful".
