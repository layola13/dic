hero-worship:/ˈhɪəroʊˌwɜːrʃɪp/| n. |英雄崇拜；盲目崇拜 |The excessive admiration of a person.  It often involves the uncritical acceptance of their views and actions, sometimes to the point of idolatry. |例句：His hero-worship of the rock star led him to emulate every aspect of his life.  他的摇滚明星英雄崇拜使他模仿了他生活的方方面面。

例句：Blind hero-worship can be detrimental to personal growth. 盲目的英雄崇拜不利于个人成长。


近义词：idolatry, veneration, deification, adulation

反义词：criticism, contempt, disdain


词性:名词
