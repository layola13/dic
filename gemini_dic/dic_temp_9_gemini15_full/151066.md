paraplegias:/ˌpærəˈpliːdʒəz/ | n. | 瘫痪（复数） | Paraplegias refers to instances of paralysis affecting the lower half of the body, typically resulting from spinal cord injury.  It is the plural form of paraplegia. |  The study examined the prevalence of paraplegias in different age groups. 这项研究调查了不同年龄段瘫痪的患病率。  The accident resulted in several cases of paraplegias among the survivors. 事故导致几名幸存者瘫痪。 | paraplegia, paralysis |  full mobility, complete recovery |  名词
