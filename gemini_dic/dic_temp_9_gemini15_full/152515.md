payout:/ˈpeɪaʊt/| n. v.|支付，支出，收益|The act of paying out money; the amount of money paid out.|例句：The payout for winning the lottery was enormous. 彩票中奖的奖金非常丰厚。

The company announced a large payout to its shareholders. 公司宣布向股东支付巨额股息。|近义词：payment, disbursement, dividend, distribution|反义词：income, revenue, receipt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pays out bonuses annually. = 公司每年支付奖金。
He pays out a large sum of money every month. = 他每月支付一大笔钱。

一般过去时:
They paid out all their savings on the house. = 他们把所有的积蓄都花在了房子上。
The insurance company paid out a claim for the damaged car. = 保险公司支付了损坏汽车的赔偿金。

现在完成时:
We have paid out more than we expected this year. = 今年我们支付的款项超过了预期。
She has paid out a significant amount on her education. = 她为教育支付了大量的款项。

过去完成时:
They had paid out all their funds before the project was completed. = 项目完成之前，他们已经花光了所有的资金。
The government had paid out emergency relief to the victims. = 政府已经向受害者支付了紧急救济金。

将来时:
The company will pay out dividends next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度支付股息。
They will pay out the remaining balance next week. = 他们将在下周支付剩余的余额。

虚拟语气:
If the company paid out more, employees would be happier. = 如果公司支付更多，员工会更开心。
I wish they had paid out the money sooner. = 我希望他们早点支付这笔钱。

被动语态:
The bonuses were paid out last Friday. = 奖金在上周五发放了。
The winnings will be paid out within a week. = 奖金将在一个星期内支付。

疑问句:
When will the payouts be made? = 奖金什么时候支付？
How much will the company pay out in dividends? = 公司将支付多少股息？

否定句:
The company did not pay out any bonuses this year. = 公司今年没有发放任何奖金。
They haven't paid out the full amount yet. = 他们还没有支付全部金额。
