palpating:/ˈpælpeɪtɪŋ/| v.|触诊，摸诊|To examine (a part of the body) by touch, especially to detect abnormalities.|例句：The doctor palpated her abdomen to check for any tenderness. 医生触诊了她的腹部以检查是否有压痛。
例句：The nurse palpated the patient's pulse. 护士触诊了病人的脉搏。|近义词：feel, probe, examine|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor palpates the patient's chest regularly. = 医生定期触诊病人的胸部。
She palpates her own neck for swollen glands. = 她自己摸索自己的脖子是否有肿大的腺体。


一般过去时:
The physician palpated the lump carefully. = 医生仔细地触诊了肿块。
