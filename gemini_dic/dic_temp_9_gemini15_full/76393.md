gaffer:/ˈɡæfər/| n.| 照明师；老家伙；领班 | A gaffer is the chief electrician on a film or television set.  The term can also be used informally to refer to an older man, often as a term of endearment or respect. | Example: The gaffer expertly adjusted the lighting for the close-up shot. 照明师熟练地调整了特写镜头的灯光。  The old gaffer told wonderful stories of his youth.  这位老家伙讲述了他年轻时的精彩故事。|近义词：electrician (for the film/TV meaning), old man, boss|反义词：junior, youngster
