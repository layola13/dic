fieldsman:/ˈfiːldzmən/| n. |外场队员；田间工人|A person who works in the field, especially a player in a team sport who plays in the outfield.  The term often refers to a cricket player who plays in the outfield.|例句：The fieldsman dropped a crucial catch.  外场队员接了一个关键球。|例句：The fieldsman worked tirelessly in the hot sun. 田间工人顶着烈日辛勤工作。|近义词：outfielder, groundman|反义词：batsman (in cricket)
