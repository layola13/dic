epidermic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪk/|/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪk/| adj.|表皮的；皮肤上的| Relating to the epidermis, the outer layer of skin.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe something superficial or only affecting the surface.|例句：The cream is designed for epidermic application.  这种面霜设计用于外用。

例句：His understanding of the issue was purely epidermic; he lacked depth. 他对这个问题的理解仅仅停留在表面；缺乏深度。|近义词：cutaneous, superficial|反义词：deep, profound
