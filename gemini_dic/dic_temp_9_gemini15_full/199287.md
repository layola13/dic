silveriness:/ˈsɪlvərɪnəs/| n. |银色；银光；清亮；柔和|The quality or state of being silvery; a silvery appearance or sound.  It often implies a delicate or soft quality.|例句：The silveriness of the moonlit night was breathtaking. 月光之夜的银辉令人叹为观止。

The silveriness of her voice was captivating. 她声音的清亮动听极了。|近义词：silvery, gleam, sheen, luminosity |反义词：dullness, darkness, harshness
