entreatful: /ɪnˈtriːtfʊl/ | adj. | 恳求的；哀求的 |  Characterized by earnest entreaty; pleading; imploring.  It suggests a strong desire or need for something, often accompanied by humility and desperation. |  例句：Her entreatful eyes pleaded for mercy.  她的恳求的眼神乞求怜悯。

例句：His entreatful tone hinted at a desperate need for help. 他恳求的语气暗示了他迫切需要帮助。


近义词：supplicating, pleading, imploring, beseeching, importunate


反义词：demanding, imperious, commanding, arrogant, assertive


词性：形容词
