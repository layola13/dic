clipped:/klɪpt/| adj. v. |剪短的；简短的；删节的；(声音)急促的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "clipped" describes something that has been shortened or cut, either physically or figuratively.  It can refer to hair, speech, wings, or even a sentence.  As a verb, "clipped" is the past tense and past participle of "clip," meaning to cut something short.  It can also imply a quick, sharp action.

例句:
He had a clipped, military haircut. = 他留着短而整齐的军人发型。

Her speech was clipped and to the point. = 她的讲话简短扼要。


近义词:
short, brief, concise, abbreviated, curtailed, trimmed, snipped


反义词:
long, lengthy, extended, verbose, unedited


词性:
adjective, verb
