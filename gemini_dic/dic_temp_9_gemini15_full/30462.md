centrifugality: /ˌsɛntriˈfjuːɡælɪti/ | n. |离心性，向心力反作用| The tendency of a rotating body to move away from its center of rotation.  It describes the outward force experienced by objects in a rotating system. The opposite of centripetal force. |例句：The high speed of the centrifuge created a strong centrifugality, separating the blood components. 高速离心机产生了强大的离心力，分离了血液成分。

The centrifugality of the spinning top caused it to wobble. 旋转陀螺的离心力导致它摇晃。|近义词： centrifugal force, outward force |反义词：centripetality, inward force
