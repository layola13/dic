demiwolf:/ˈdɛmiˌwʊlf/| n.|半狼；狼人（神话生物）|A mythical creature, often depicted as a half-human, half-wolf being.  It can also refer to a wolf-like creature of indeterminate classification. |Example: The legends spoke of a demiwolf guarding the ancient forest.  传说中有一只半狼守护着古老的森林。 The creature, though wolf-like, was identified as a rare species of demiwolf.  虽然这种生物像狼，但被鉴定为一种稀有的半狼物种。|近义词：werewolf, lycanthrope|反义词：human, fully wolf
