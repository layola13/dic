constitutionless:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtʃuːʃənles/| adj.|无宪法的；缺乏宪法的 | Lacking a constitution; without a constitution.  A government or state without a formal constitution is described as constitutionless. The term often implies a lack of established legal frameworks and protection of rights. |例句：The country operated under a constitutionless regime for many years, leading to widespread human rights abuses.  这个国家多年来在无宪法制度下运作，导致普遍的人权侵犯。|例句：The newly independent nation struggled to establish a stable government in its constitutionless state. 新独立的国家在其无宪法状态下努力建立稳定的政府。|近义词：lawless, anarchic, unorganized |反义词：constitutional, governed
