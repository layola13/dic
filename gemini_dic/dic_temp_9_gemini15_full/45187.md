cuisinier:/kwɪzɪˈneɪər/| n. |厨师，主厨|A cuisinier is a professional cook, especially one who is highly skilled and works in a restaurant or hotel.  The term often implies a higher level of skill and experience than a simple "cook".|例句：The restaurant's renowned cuisinier created a breathtaking tasting menu.|近义词：chef, cook, chef de partie|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cuisinier prepares the meals. = 厨师准备饭菜。
He carefully seasons each dish. = 他仔细地给每道菜调味。


一般过去时:
The cuisinier cooked a delicious meal last night. = 厨师昨晚做了一顿美味的饭菜。
She presented the finished plates to the diners. = 她将做好的菜端给用餐者。
