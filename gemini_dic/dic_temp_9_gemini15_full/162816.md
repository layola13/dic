precalculation: /ˌpriːkæl.kjəˈleɪ.ʃən/ | n. |预先计算；预估| The process of calculating something in advance.  It involves estimating or determining a value before it is actually needed. This can save time and resources later on.  | Example: The precalculation of material needs allowed for efficient project management.  预先计算材料需求使项目管理更高效。  |近义词: estimation, prediction, forecasting, computation in advance|反义词: post-calculation, afterthought
