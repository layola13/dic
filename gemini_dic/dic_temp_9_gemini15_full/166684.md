presessions:/prɪˈseʃənz/ | n. |预备会议；预备会议记录 |  A presession is a meeting held before a main meeting or event to prepare for it, often involving planning or preliminary discussions.  It can also refer to the minutes or records from such a meeting. |例句：The presession for the conference focused on logistics and agenda setting.  预备会议的重点是后勤和议程制定。


The presession minutes detailed the decisions made regarding the guest speakers. 预备会议记录详细说明了关于客座演讲者的决定。|近义词：preliminary meeting, preparatory session, planning session |反义词：post-session, follow-up meeting
