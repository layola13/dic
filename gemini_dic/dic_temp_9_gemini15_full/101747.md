irresolution:/ˌɪrˌɛzəˈluːʃən/| n. |犹豫不决；优柔寡断；缺乏果断 |Lack of firmness of purpose; inability to make decisions.  It often stems from conflicting desires or fears about the consequences of a choice.|His irresolution led to missed opportunities.他的优柔寡断导致错失良机。|例句：Her irresolution prevented her from applying for the job.她的犹豫不决使她没能申请这份工作。|近义词：indecision, vacillation, wavering, hesitancy |反义词：determination, decisiveness, resolution
