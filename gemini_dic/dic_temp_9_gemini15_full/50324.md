deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/ | vt. | 谴责；对…深感遗憾 | To express strong disapproval of something; to feel or express great sorrow or regret for something. | 例句：We deplore the violence in the city.  我们谴责这座城市的暴力行为。


例句：The government deplores the recent rise in crime. 政府对近期犯罪率的上升深感遗憾。


近义词：condemn, denounce, lament, regret


反义词：approve, applaud, commend, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deplore the lack of funding for education. = 他们谴责教育经费不足。
We deplore the cruelty of the war. = 我们谴责战争的残酷。


一般过去时:
She deplored his rash decision. = 她对他的鲁莽决定表示谴责。
The committee deplored the company's unethical practices. = 委员会谴责了该公司的违反道德的行为。


现在完成时:
They have deplored the government's inaction for months. = 他们已经谴责政府的不作为数月了。
We have deplored the loss of so many innocent lives. = 我们对如此多无辜生命损失深感遗憾。


过去完成时:
He had deplored the decision before it was even announced. = 在决定宣布之前，他就已经表示谴责了。
They had deplored the lack of progress for years. = 他们多年来一直对缺乏进展深感遗憾。


将来时:
We will deplore the consequences of this action. = 我们将对这一行动的后果表示谴责。
They will deplore the loss of this valuable resource. = 他们将对这种宝贵资源的损失深感遗憾。


虚拟语气:
I would deplore such behaviour if I saw it. = 如果我看到这种行为，我会表示谴责。
We would deplore the outcome if the plan failed. = 如果计划失败，我们会对结果深感遗憾。


被动语态:
The policy was deplored by many citizens. = 许多公民谴责了这项政策。
The decision was deplored by environmental groups. = 环境保护组织谴责了这一决定。


疑问句:
Do you deplore the actions of the protesters? = 你谴责抗议者的行为吗？
Does she deplore the situation in the country? = 她对该国的局势深感遗憾吗？


否定句:
They do not deplore the use of force in this situation. = 他们并不谴责在这种情况下使用武力。
He did not deplore the outcome of the game. = 他并不对比赛的结果感到遗憾。



PHRASE->
deplore the loss of = 对…的损失深感遗憾
deplore the violence = 谴责暴力
deplore the lack of = 谴责…的缺乏
deeply deplore = 深深谴责
publicly deplore = 公开谴责
