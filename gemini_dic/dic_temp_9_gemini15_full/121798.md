misreferenced:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərənst/ | adj. | 引用错误的；参考错误的 | Incorrectly referenced; referring to the wrong source or item.  The data was misreferenced in the report, leading to incorrect conclusions. | 近义词：incorrectly cited, wrongly attributed, misquoted | 反义词：correctly referenced, accurately cited


例句：

The author misreferenced several sources in his bibliography.  作者在他的参考文献中错误地引用了几个来源。

The student's essay contained several misreferenced passages. 学生的论文包含几处引用错误的段落。
