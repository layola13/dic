jump over someone:/ˌdʒʌmp ˈoʊvər ˈsʌmwʌn/|/ˈdʒʌmp ˈəʊvə ˈsʌmwʌn/| phrasal verb|跨过某人|To leap or jump across a person.  This phrase often implies a playful or slightly aggressive action, depending on the context. It can also suggest avoiding or ignoring someone.|例句：The child jumped over his sleeping brother. 孩子跳过了他正在睡觉的哥哥。

例句：She jumped over the puddle to avoid getting her shoes wet, ignoring the man standing in it. 她跳过水坑以避免弄湿鞋子，无视站在水坑里的人。|近义词：step over, vault over, bypass|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jumps over his dog every morning. = 他每天早上都跳过他的狗。
They jump over the fence to get to the other side. = 他们跳过围栏去另一边。


一般过去时:
She jumped over the hurdle in the race. = 她在比赛中跳过了障碍物。
The cat jumped over the sleeping dog. = 猫跳过了睡着的狗。


现在完成时:
He has jumped over that wall many times. = 他已经跳过那堵墙很多次了。
The children have jumped over the stream countless times. = 孩子们无数次跳过了小溪。


过去完成时:
Before the race, she had jumped over smaller hurdles. = 比赛前，她已经跳过了较小的障碍物。
They had jumped over the fence before the guard saw them. = 警卫看到他们之前，他们已经跳过了栅栏。


将来时:
I will jump over the ditch tomorrow. = 我明天将跳过沟渠。
She will jump over the fence to escape. = 她将跳过栅栏逃跑。


虚拟语气:
If I were taller, I would jump over the car. = 如果我更高，我会跳过汽车。
If only I could jump over that building. = 但愿我能跳过那栋建筑。


被动语态:
The obstacle was jumped over by the athlete. = 运动员跳过了障碍物。（被动语态用在此处略显不自然，很少使用）
The fence was jumped over by the intruder. = 栅栏被入侵者跳过了。（被动语态用在此处略显不自然，很少使用）


疑问句:
Did he jump over the fence? = 他跳过栅栏了吗？
Will you jump over the puddle? = 你会跳过水坑吗？


否定句:
He didn't jump over the stream; he walked around it. = 他没有跳过小溪；他绕着它走了。
She couldn't jump over the high wall. = 她无法跳过高墙。
