gusting: /ˈɡʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 狂风的；一阵阵的；令人作呕的 |  Describes something unpleasant, especially a strong and unpleasant wind or a strong unpleasant smell or taste.  |例句：The gusting wind almost blew me over. 狂风差点把我吹倒了。The air was filled with a gusting smell of sewage. 空气中弥漫着令人作呕的下水道气味。|近义词：blustery, strong, foul, sickening |反义词：calm, gentle, pleasant, delightful |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind is gusting fiercely. = 风正猛烈地刮着。
The smell is gusting from the kitchen. = 厨房里飘来一阵阵气味。

一般过去时:
The wind gusted all night. = 风整夜都在刮。
A gust of wind gusted through the trees. = 一阵风吹过树林。

现在完成时:
The wind has gusted intermittently all day. = 风今天断断续续地刮了一整天。
The rain has gusted down heavily. = 雨已经下了好一阵子了。

过去完成时:
The wind had gusted strongly before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，风刮得很猛。
The smell had gusted unpleasantly all morning. = 一上午空气中一直弥漫着令人不快的味道。

将来时:
The wind will gust strongly tomorrow. = 明天风会很大。
The storm will gust and rain heavily. = 暴风雨会伴随着强风和暴雨。

虚拟语气:
If the wind gusted less, we could go for a walk. = 如果风小一些，我们就可以去散步了。
If the smell hadn't gusted so strongly, I would have stayed longer. = 如果气味没有那么浓烈，我会待久一点。

被动语态:
The ship was gusted by strong winds. = 船被强风吹着。
The air was gusted with the smell of burning wood. = 空气中弥漫着燃烧木材的气味。

疑问句:
Is the wind gusting? = 风在刮吗？
Does the smell gust unpleasantly? = 气味难闻吗？

否定句:
The wind wasn't gusting when I left. = 我离开时风没有刮。
The smell didn't gust strongly. = 气味不浓烈。
