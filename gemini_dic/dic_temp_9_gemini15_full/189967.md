sanctionist: /ˈsæŋkʃənɪst/ | n. | 制裁者；实施制裁者 | A person or group that supports or implements sanctions.  Someone who advocates for or enforces economic or political penalties against another country or entity. | 例句：The sanctionist government faced criticism for its harsh economic policies. 制裁政府因其严厉的经济政策而面临批评。


The sanctionist regime tightened its grip on the opposition. 实施制裁的政权加强了对反对派的控制。

近义词：punisher, enforcer, penalizer | 反义词：supporter (of the sanctioned), appeaser
