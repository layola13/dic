carefulness:/ˈkerfəl(n)əs/| n. |细心；谨慎；周到|The quality of being careful; paying close attention to detail and avoiding mistakes. It involves a deliberate and conscious effort to avoid errors or harm.  |例句：Her carefulness in preparing the presentation was evident. 她准备演示文稿的细心是显而易见的。

例句：The surgeon's carefulness saved the patient's life. 外科医生的细心挽救了病人的生命。|近义词：caution, diligence, meticulousness, precision, attentiveness |反义词：carelessness, negligence, recklessness, heedlessness|名词
