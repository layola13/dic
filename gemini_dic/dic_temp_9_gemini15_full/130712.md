nonaliens:/ˌnɒnˈeɪliənz/ | n. |非外星人，非外来物种 |Nonaliens refers to beings or things that are not aliens or extraterrestrial.  It emphasizes the absence of alien characteristics or origins.  The term is often used in science fiction contexts, but can also apply to biological or other contexts. | Example: The research focused on the nonalien flora and fauna of the island.  这项研究集中在岛屿上非外来的动植物。|近义词:earthlings, natives, terrestrials|反义词:aliens, extraterrestrials|名词
