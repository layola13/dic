indicators:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtərz/ | n. | 指标，指示器 | Indicators are signs or symptoms that point to a particular situation or condition.  They provide information about something.  For example, economic indicators such as inflation and unemployment can signal the health of an economy. |  The economic indicators suggest a recession is likely.  经济指标表明经济衰退的可能性很大。  The rising temperature is an indicator of a fever.  体温上升是发烧的征兆。 | signs, signals, symptoms, markers, measures |  (无明显反义词，可考虑用opposite, contrary等描述反义概念) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What indicators are being used to track the project's progress? = 使用哪些指标来跟踪项目的进度？
These indicators suggest a positive trend in the market. = 这些指标表明市场呈现积极趋势。


一般过去时:
What indicators did they use to assess the risk? = 他们用什么指标来评估风险？
The indicators last year showed a decline in sales. = 去年的指标显示销售额下降。


现在完成时:
What indicators have shown improvement in the patient's condition? = 哪些指标显示患者的病情有所好转？
The indicators have consistently pointed towards economic growth. = 指标始终指向经济增长。


过去完成时:
What indicators had already been identified before the study began? = 在研究开始之前已经确定了哪些指标？
The indicators had suggested a problem long before it became apparent. = 在问题变得明显之前，指标就已经暗示了这个问题。


将来时:
What indicators will be used to measure the effectiveness of the treatment? = 将使用哪些指标来衡量治疗的有效性？
The indicators will likely show an increase in productivity next quarter. = 下个季度指标可能会显示生产力提高。


虚拟语气:
If these indicators were more reliable, we would have made a different decision. = 如果这些指标更可靠，我们会做出不同的决定。
I wish the indicators were clearer about the future. = 我希望这些指标能更清楚地显示未来。


被动语态:
These indicators are being closely monitored by the government. = 这些指标正受到政府的密切监控。
The indicators were misinterpreted by the analysts. = 这些指标被分析师误解了。


疑问句:
What are the key indicators of success in this project? = 这个项目成功的关键指标是什么？
Are these indicators truly representative of the situation? = 这些指标是否真正代表了这种情况？


否定句:
There are no clear indicators of a change in policy. = 没有明确的迹象表明政策发生了变化。
The indicators did not support the initial hypothesis. = 指标不支持最初的假设。
