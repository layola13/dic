pro-Arabs: /ˌproʊˈærəbz/ | adj. | 亲阿拉伯的，支持阿拉伯人的 |  Supporting or favoring Arab people or Arab causes.  This term describes individuals, groups, or policies that express sympathy or support for Arab nations or their interests. It often carries a political connotation, suggesting alignment with Arab viewpoints or agendas in international relations.


例句：

The pro-Arab lobby exerted considerable influence on the government's foreign policy.  亲阿拉伯游说团体对政府的外交政策施加了相当大的影响。

His pro-Arab stance alienated many of his former supporters. 他亲阿拉伯的立场使他疏远了许多以前的支持者。


近义词：Arabophile (n.), pro-Arabian (adj.)


反义词：anti-Arab (adj.), pro-Israeli (adj.)


词性:形容词
