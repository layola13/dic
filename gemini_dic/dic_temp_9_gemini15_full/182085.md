remilitarized:/ˌriːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/| adj.|重新军事化；再次军事化|To make (a place or area) military again after a period of demilitarization.|The demilitarized zone was remilitarized after the outbreak of hostilities.  冲突爆发后，非军事区重新军事化了。|近义词：re-armed, fortified|反义词：demilitarized

一般现在时:
The country remilitarizes its borders every year. = 这个国家每年都会重新军事化其边界。
They are remilitarizing the island. = 他们正在使该岛屿重新军事化。


一般过去时:
The government remilitarized the region after the coup. = 政变后，政府使该地区重新军事化。
They quickly remilitarized the strategic port. = 他们迅速地使这个战略港口重新军事化。

过去完成时:
