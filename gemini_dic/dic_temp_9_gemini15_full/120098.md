mimes:/maɪmz/| n. v.|哑剧演员；哑剧；模仿|A mime is a person who performs mime, which is a form of acting without words, using gestures and facial expressions.  It can also refer to the act of mimicking or imitating someone or something.|例句：The mime artist captivated the audience with his skillful movements.  这位哑剧演员以他娴熟的动作吸引了观众。

The children were miming animals in the playground.孩子们在操场上模仿动物。|近义词：mimicker, imitator, impersonator|反义词：speaker, vocalist
