rite:/raɪt/| n.|仪式，典礼；习惯；宗教仪式|A rite is a religious or ceremonial act or practice.  It can also refer to a customary way of doing something.|例句：The ancient rite of sun worship was performed at dawn.  古老的太阳崇拜仪式在黎明时分举行。|例句：The daily rite of making coffee is a necessary part of her morning routine. 制作咖啡的日常习惯是她早晨例行工作中必要的一部分。|近义词：ceremony, ritual, custom|反义词：chaos, disorder
