chalcographist:/ˌkælkəˈɡræfɪst/ | n. |版画家，铜版画家 |A chalcographist is an artist who creates prints using a metal plate, typically copper or zinc,  through techniques like etching, engraving, or drypoint.  They are skilled in the process of preparing the plate, inking it, and printing the image onto paper. |The renowned chalcographist produced exquisite prints, each one a testament to his mastery of the intaglio process.|近义词：engraver, printmaker |反义词：无
