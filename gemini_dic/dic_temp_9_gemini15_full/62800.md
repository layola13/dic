engulfment:/ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt/ | n. |吞没；吞噬；淹没 |The act or process of being engulfed; overwhelming immersion or involvement.  It often implies a feeling of being overwhelmed or overpowered. |例句：The rapid engulfment of the small town by the flood waters left many homeless.  洪水迅速吞没了小镇，导致许多人无家可归。

The engulfment of his emotions prevented him from making a rational decision.  他的情绪吞噬了他，使他无法做出理性决定。


近义词：submersion, inundation, swallowing, absorption


反义词：emergence, liberation, release


词性：名词
