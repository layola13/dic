civets:/ˈsɪvɪts/| n. |麝猫；灵猫|A civet is a nocturnal mammal belonging to the family Viverridae, known for its musky scent glands.  They are found in Africa, Asia, and Europe.  The scent from these glands is used in perfumes and other products.|Example: The perfume contained civet musk, giving it a unique fragrance.  这款香水含有麝猫香，使其具有独特的香味。|近义词：musk deer (指麝香来源)|反义词：无
