pekinese:/ˌpɛkɪˈniːz/| n. |北京犬，京巴犬 | A small dog breed originating from China, characterized by its long, flowing coat, short muzzle, and brachycephalic (short-nosed) face.  They are known for their independent and sometimes stubborn personalities. |Example:  My aunt owns a beautiful Pekinese with a luxurious coat. 我阿姨养了一只毛色华丽的北京犬。


Example: The Pekinese is a popular companion dog. 北京犬是一种受欢迎的伴侣犬。


近义词：spaniel (广义上，指小型犬)


反义词：无


词性：名词
