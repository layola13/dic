repondered:/rɪˈpɒndəd/| v. |回应；答复|responded to something, typically in speech or writing.  It is the past tense of "reponder," a less common variant of "respond." |例句：He pondered the question for a moment before he responded. 他思考了一会儿才回答这个问题。  She pondered the implications of his words before she responded. 她仔细考虑了他话语的含义后才做出回应。|近义词：responded, answered, replied|反义词：ignored, disregarded|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to emails promptly. = 他迅速回复邮件。
She responds well to criticism. = 她能很好地接受批评。

一般过去时:
He responded to my question immediately. = 他立即回答了我的问题。
She responded with a smile. = 她笑着回应。

现在完成时:
He has responded to all the inquiries. = 他已经回复了所有询问。
She has responded positively to the treatment. = 她对治疗反应良好。

过去完成时:
He had responded before I could ask. = 我还没来得及问，他就已经回答了。
She had responded angrily to the accusations. = 她对那些指控愤怒地回应。

将来时:
He will respond to the letter tomorrow. = 他明天将回复这封信。
She will respond appropriately to the situation. = 她会根据情况做出适当的回应。

虚拟语气:
If he responded more quickly, we wouldn't have missed the deadline. = 如果他回应得更快一些，我们就不会错过截止日期。
I wish she had responded differently to my request. = 我希望她能对我的请求做出不同的回应。

被动语态:
His request was responded to promptly. = 他的请求得到了迅速的回应。
The proposal was responded to with enthusiasm. = 该提案受到了热情的回应。

疑问句:
How did he respond to your email? = 他如何回复你的邮件？
Did she respond to your invitation? = 她回复你的邀请了吗？

否定句:
He didn't respond to my call. = 他没有回复我的电话。
She didn't respond to my questions. = 她没有回答我的问题。
