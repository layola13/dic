jaconet:/ˈdʒækənɛt/| n. |雅卡纱布，细棉布 |A lightweight, plain-woven cotton fabric, often used for clothing and household items.  It's known for its softness and breathability.|例句：The dress was made from a delicate jaconet fabric. 这件裙子是用精致的雅卡纱布制作的。 The baby's blanket was soft jaconet. 婴儿的毯子是柔软的雅卡纱布。|近义词：muslin, cambric|反义词：None specifically, but heavier fabrics like denim could be considered antonyms in terms of weight and texture.|名词
