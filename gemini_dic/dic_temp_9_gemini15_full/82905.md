gumboot:/ˈɡʌmˌbuːt/| n. |胶鞋，高筒雨靴 |A gumboot is a tall waterproof boot, typically made of rubber or plastic, worn to protect the feet and legs from mud, water, or other elements.  |例句：I wore my gumboots to avoid getting my feet wet in the muddy field. 我穿上了我的胶鞋，以免在泥泞的田地里弄湿脚。

例句：The farmer always wears gumboots when working in the fields. 农民在田里工作时总是穿着胶鞋。


近义词：wellington boot, rubber boot |反义词：sandal, slipper |词性：noun
