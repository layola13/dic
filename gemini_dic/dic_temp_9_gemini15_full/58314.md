dryads:/ˈdraɪædz/| n. |树 нимфы,树精|Mythological creatures, typically depicted as beautiful female spirits inhabiting trees, particularly oak trees. They are associated with the natural world and often portrayed as guardians of forests and woodlands.  Dryads are closely linked to the life and health of their trees; if the tree dies, so does the dryad. |Example:  Legends tell of mischievous dryads who would lead travelers astray in the deep woods.  传说中调皮的树 нимфы会在深林里把旅行者带偏。|近义词：hamadryad, wood nymph, tree nymph|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
