rehabbed:/rɪˈhæbd/| v. |修复的；康复的；改造的 |Past participle of "rehabilitate".  It describes something or someone that has undergone rehabilitation.  This can refer to physical structures,  addiction recovery, or social reintegration. |例句：The old factory has been rehabbed into a stylish apartment complex.  旧工厂已被改造成时尚的公寓楼。 The rehabbed addict is now leading a productive life. 康复的瘾君子现在过着有成效的生活。|近义词：repaired, renovated, recovered, restored |反义词：damaged, destroyed, deteriorated
