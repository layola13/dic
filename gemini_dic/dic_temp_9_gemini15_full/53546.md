disbalance:/dɪsˈbæləns/ | n. | 不平衡，失衡 | A lack of balance or equilibrium; an uneven distribution.  It can refer to physical, mental, or emotional states, or to a system or situation. |例句：The disbalance in the company's budget led to financial difficulties. 公司预算失衡导致财政困难。

The disbalance of power between the two countries created tension.  两国之间的力量失衡造成了紧张局势。| 近义词：imbalance, disequilibrium, disproportion | 反义词：balance, equilibrium, harmony | 名词
