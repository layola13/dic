reorchestrated:/ˌriːˈɔːrkɪstreɪtɪd/| v. |重新编排（乐曲）；重新组织 | To arrange or organize (a piece of music, a system, etc.) again, often in a different way.  |例句：The conductor reorchestrated the symphony for a smaller ensemble. 指挥为小型乐团重新编排了这首交响乐。

The composer reorchestrated the opera to highlight the vocal parts. 作曲家重新编排了歌剧以突出声乐部分。


近义词：rearrange, reorganize, revise, adapt

反义词：leave unchanged, maintain the original form


词性：动词
