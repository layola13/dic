reelevation:/ˌriːˌɛləˈveɪʃən/| n. |重新抬升；重新提高；复兴|The reevaluation of the historical data led to a reevaluation of the conclusions.  重新评估历史数据导致了对结论的重新评估。|近义词：reassessment, reappraisal, revival|反义词：decline, degradation

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reevaluates its strategies annually. = 公司每年都会重新评估其战略。
This process reevaluates the risk factors involved. = 此过程会重新评估所涉及的风险因素。


一般过去时:
The government reevaluated its economic policies after the crisis. = 危机过后，政府重新评估了其经济政策。
They reevaluated the project's budget due to unforeseen costs. = 由于不可预见的成本，他们重新评估了项目的预算。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

They had reevaluated the situation and decided to retreat. = 他们已经重新评估了局势，并决定撤退。


将来时:
