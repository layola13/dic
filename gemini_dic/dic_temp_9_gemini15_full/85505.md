hastes:/heɪsts/| n.|匆忙，急速|Extreme speed or urgency of movement or action.|He finished his work in great haste. 他匆忙完成了他的工作。|近义词：hurry, rush, speed|反义词：leisure, slowness|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Haste makes waste. = 欲速则不达。
His haste is evident in his sloppy work. = 他工作的粗糙显露出了他的匆忙。


一般过去时:
They left in haste after the argument. = 争吵后他们匆忙离开了。
He regretted his haste in making the decision. = 他后悔当初做决定太匆忙了。


现在完成时:
He has acted in haste and now regrets it. = 他行事仓促，现在后悔了。
She has shown haste in completing the project.  = 她完成项目过于仓促。


过去完成时:
They had acted in haste, leading to regrettable consequences. = 他们之前行事仓促，导致了令人遗憾的后果。
She had rushed through her preparations, resulting in many mistakes. = 她仓促准备，导致很多错误。


将来时:
He will act in haste if he doesn't slow down. = 如果他不慢下来，他会行事仓促。
They will likely regret their haste later. = 他们以后可能会后悔自己的匆忙。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't acted in such haste, he wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他当时没有那么匆忙，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I hadn't acted in such haste. = 我真希望我当时没有那么匆忙。


被动语态:
The decision was made in haste. = 这个决定是匆忙做出的。
The project was completed in haste, resulting in errors. = 项目仓促完成，导致了错误。


疑问句:
Was there any haste in their departure? = 他们离开时是否匆忙？
Did his haste cause the accident? = 他的匆忙是否导致了事故？


否定句:
There was no haste in their actions. = 他们的行动并不匆忙。
He didn't act in haste; he carefully considered all options. = 他没有轻率行事；他仔细考虑了所有选择。


PHRASE->

词源:

The word "hastes" is the plural form of "haste," ultimately deriving from the Old French word "haste," meaning "speed," "hurry," or "swiftness."  The Old French word itself originates from the Latin word "haesta," which had a similar meaning.
