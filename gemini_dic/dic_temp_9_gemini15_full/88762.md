The children played hocus-pocus with the cards. 孩子们用纸牌玩起了戏法。 | 近义词：trickery, deception, illusion, sleight of hand, conjuring | 反义词：truth, reality, honesty | 名词

词源:
The origin of "hocus pocus" is uncertain, but it is believed to be a corruption of the Latin phrase "hoc est corpus," meaning "this is the body," a phrase used in the Catholic Mass.  The transformation from a sacred phrase to a term for trickery reflects a shift in its meaning over time.  The words themselves are not of Latin origin.
