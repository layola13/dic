This word is less common than its components "counter" and "distinct," and often implies an opposition or contradiction. | The counterdistinct approaches to the problem yielded surprisingly similar results.  截然不同的解决方法却产生了惊人的相似结果。 The two philosophies are counterdistinct in their fundamental assumptions. 这两种哲学在基本假设上截然不同。 | distinct, different, contrasting | similar, alike, identical |  adj.
