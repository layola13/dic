preformat:/ˈpriːfɔːrmæt/| vt.|预先格式化；预先设定格式|To prepare (data, a document, etc.) for formatting in advance, often using a specific program or system.|例句：The database administrator preformatted the hard drive before installing the new software. 数据库管理员在安装新软件之前预先格式化了硬盘。|例句：The author preformatted the manuscript according to the publisher's guidelines. 作者按照出版商的指南预先设定了稿件的格式。|近义词：format, prepare, arrange|反义词：postformat (无此词，仅作对比)|动词
