nonpercipiency:/ˌnɒnpəˈsɪpiənsi/| n. |非感知性，感觉迟钝|The inability to perceive or the state of not perceiving.  It often refers to a lack of awareness or understanding of something.  This can be due to various factors, including physical limitations, mental states, or deliberate avoidance.|Example: The patient's nonpercipiency of pain was a concern to the medical team. 患者对疼痛的无感让医疗团队担忧。|近义词: insensibility, unawareness, numbness|反义词: perceptiveness, awareness, sensitivity|名词
