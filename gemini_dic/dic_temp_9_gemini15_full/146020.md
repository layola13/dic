overdrained:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnd/ | adj. |过度排水；过于干燥的 |Excessively drained; lacking sufficient moisture.  This term often refers to soil or land that has had too much water removed, leading to dryness and potentially harming plant life or disrupting ecological balance. |The overdrained field yielded a poor harvest.  过度排水的田地收成很差。 The overdrained soil cracked under the summer sun.  夏季阳光下，过度排水的土壤开裂了。|well-drained, dry |waterlogged, saturated|形容词
