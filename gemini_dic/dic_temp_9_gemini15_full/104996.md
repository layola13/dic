kennels:/ˈkenlz/| n.|狗窝；犬舍；养狗场|A kennel is a structure for housing dogs, often outdoors.  It can range from a simple shelter to a complex facility for breeding or boarding dogs. |例句：The dogs were kept in clean, comfortable kennels.  那些狗被养在干净舒适的狗窝里。|例句：The local animal shelter has a large number of kennels. 当地动物收容所有很多狗舍。|近义词：dog house, dog shelter|反义词：N/A
