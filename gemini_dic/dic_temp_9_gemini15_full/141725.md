oral-histories: /ˈɔːrəl ˈhɪstəriz/ | n. | 口述历史 | Oral histories are firsthand accounts of past events, collected through interviews with people who experienced them. They provide valuable insights into the past, especially for marginalized or underrepresented groups whose stories might not be documented in traditional historical records.  | Example:  The museum is building an archive of oral histories from World War II veterans.  博物馆正在建立一个来自二战老兵的口述历史档案。

|近义词: personal accounts, firsthand accounts, life stories,  testimonial accounts | 反义词: written histories, official records | 词性: noun (plural)
