covenanting:/ˈkʌvənəntɪŋ/| v.|盟约；约定；立约|The act of making or entering into a covenant; the state of being bound by a covenant.  It implies a formal agreement, often with religious or legal implications, involving mutual promises or obligations.|例句：The two nations are covenanting to work together for peace.  两个国家正在缔结盟约，以共同致力于和平。

The ancient covenant between God and his people is still relevant today.  神与他子民之间的古老盟约至今仍具有现实意义。|近义词：agreeing, contracting, pledging|反义词：breaking, violating, reneging|动词
