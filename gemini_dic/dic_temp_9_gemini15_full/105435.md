kidult:/ˈkɪdʌlt/| n. |孩子气的成年人，成年儿童|An adult who retains the interests and behaviors of a child.  Kidults often enjoy activities and possessions typically associated with childhood, such as collecting toys, playing video games, watching cartoons, or engaging in other childlike hobbies. They are not necessarily immature or childish in all aspects of their lives; rather, they maintain a playful and youthful spirit. |例句：He's a kidult who still collects comic books and action figures. 他是一个孩子气的成年人，仍然收集漫画书和模型玩具。  She's a kidult at heart, always finding joy in simple pleasures. 她内心深处是个孩子气的成年人，总是从简单的快乐中找到乐趣。|近义词：childish adult, Peter Pan syndrome|反义词：mature adult, grown-up
