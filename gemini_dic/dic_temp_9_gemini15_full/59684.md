echoing:/ˈɛkoʊɪŋ/| adj.|回响的，重复的，空洞的 | Repeating or echoing a sound or idea; resembling an echo;  sounding hollow or resonant.  |例句：The canyon echoed with the sound of our voices.  峡谷回荡着我们的声音。

例句：His speech echoed the sentiments of many in the audience. 他的讲话反映了听众中许多人的观点。


近义词：resonating, reverberating, resounding, repeating | 反义词：silent, muted, unheard


词源：源自于动词echo,  最终源于希腊语“ἠχώ” (ēchō)，意思是“回声”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The empty hall is echoing with silence. = 空荡的大厅回响着寂静。
His words are echoing in my mind. = 他的话语在我的脑海中回荡。


一般过去时:
The stadium echoed with cheers after the winning goal. = 进球之后，体育场回响着欢呼声。
The announcement echoed throughout the building. = 整个大楼都回响着通知声。


现在完成时:
The news of the victory has been echoing across the country. = 胜利的消息已经在全国回荡。
Her laughter has echoed in my memory for years. = 她的笑声多年来一直回荡在我的记忆中。


过去完成时:
The thunder had echoed through the valley before the rain started. = 雷声在雨下之前就已经在山谷中回荡。
The gunshot had echoed in the quiet forest. = 枪声在寂静的森林中回荡。


将来时:
The applause will echo through the ages. = 掌声将永世流传。
The singer's voice will echo in the concert hall. = 歌手的歌声将在音乐厅回荡。


虚拟语气:
If the mountain echoed back my call, I would feel less alone. = 如果山谷回应我的呼唤，我会感觉不那么孤单。
I wish my words had echoed more deeply. = 我希望我的话语能更深刻地引起共鸣。


被动语态:
The message was echoed by many leaders. = 许多领导人都回应了这一信息。
The sentiment was echoed throughout the community. = 这种情绪在整个社区中得到共鸣。


疑问句:
Is the speaker's message echoing your own beliefs? = 演讲者的信息是否与你自己的信仰相呼应？
Does the empty room echo? = 这间空房间会回声吗？


否定句:
The message did not echo through the crowd. = 这条消息没有在人群中引起共鸣。
The hall did not echo with music. = 大厅里没有音乐的回声。


PHRASE->
echoing chamber:  回声室（比喻信息被反复强化的地方）
echoing silence:  寂静的回响
echoing laughter:  回荡的笑声
echoing emptiness:  空虚的回响
echoing concerns:  反复出现的担忧
