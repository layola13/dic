introduced:/ˌɪntrəˈdjuːst/ | v. | 介绍；引进；提出；采用 | To make (someone) known to another or others; to bring (something new) into use or existence. | 例句：He introduced me to his parents. 他把我介绍给了他的父母。 | 例句：The company introduced a new product last year.  这家公司去年推出了一款新产品。 | 近义词：present, acquaint, launch, implement | 反义词：withdraw, remove, abolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She introduces her friends to each other. = 她互相介绍她的朋友们。
He introduces new ideas into the discussion. = 他在讨论中引入了新的想法。

一般过去时:
They introduced a bill in Congress. = 他们在国会提出了一项法案。
The teacher introduced the new student to the class. = 老师把新生介绍给了全班同学。

现在完成时:
They have introduced many changes to the system. = 他们已经对系统做了许多改变。
She has introduced herself to the committee. = 她已经向委员会做了自我介绍。

过去完成时:
He had introduced himself before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经做了自我介绍。
The company had introduced the product before its competitors. = 在竞争对手之前，该公司已经推出了该产品。

将来时:
They will introduce a new policy next month. = 下个月他们将推出一个新政策。
She will introduce her fiancé to her family.  = 她将把未婚夫介绍给她的家人。

虚拟语气:
If they introduced a better system, the problem would be solved. = 如果他们引进一个更好的系统，问题就能解决。
I wish they had introduced the new technology sooner. = 我希望他们早点引进这项新技术。

被动语态:
The new policy was introduced last week. = 新政策是上周推出的。
The guest speaker was introduced by the chairman. = 主席介绍了客座演讲人。

疑问句:
Who introduced you to this company? = 谁把你介绍到这家公司的？
When was this technology introduced? =这项技术是什么时候引进的？

否定句:
They didn't introduce the new software on time. = 他们没有按时推出新软件。
He didn't introduce himself at the party. = 他在聚会上没有做自我介绍。


PHRASE->

introduce evidence = 提出证据
introduce a character = 介绍一个人物
