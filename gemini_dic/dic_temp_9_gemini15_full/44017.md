criminologists:/ˌkrɪmɪˈnɒlədʒɪsts/|/n.|犯罪学家|Experts who study crime, criminals, and criminal behavior. They analyze crime patterns, investigate causes, and develop prevention strategies.|例句：Criminologists are studying the root causes of the recent increase in violent crime. 犯罪学家正在研究近期暴力犯罪增加的根本原因。|例句：Many criminologists believe that poverty and lack of opportunity contribute to crime.  许多犯罪学家认为贫困和缺乏机会会导致犯罪。|近义词：criminal justice experts, crime analysts|反义词：无
