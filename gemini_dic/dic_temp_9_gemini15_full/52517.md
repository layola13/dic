differentiations:/ˌdɪfərenʃiˈeɪʃənz/| n. |区分；差异；分化 |The act of differentiating or the state of being differentiated; the process of making or becoming different.  It often refers to the process of distinguishing between things or people, or the differences that exist between them.  In biology, it refers to the process by which cells or tissues become specialized. |例句：The differentiations between the two species are subtle but significant.  两种物种之间的区别细微但意义重大。|例句：Cellular differentiations lead to the development of different organs and tissues. 细胞分化导致不同器官和组织的发育。|近义词： distinctions, differences, variations |反义词：similarities, uniformities |名词
