consistently: /kənˈsɪstəntli/ | adv. | 始终如一地；一致地；不断地 |  In a consistent manner; regularly; without variation.  | 例句：He consistently achieves high scores on his exams. 他考试总是取得高分。

例句：The company consistently exceeds its sales targets.  这家公司持续超过其销售目标。


近义词：regularly, uniformly, steadily, persistently, invariably

反义词：inconsistently, erratically, sporadically, intermittently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consistently arrives on time. = 他总是准时到达。
She consistently performs well under pressure. = 她总是在压力下表现出色。


一般过去时:
They consistently supported the charity. = 他们一直支持该慈善机构。
He consistently underestimated the challenges. = 他总是低估挑战。


现在完成时:
She has consistently maintained a positive attitude. = 她一直保持积极的态度。
We have consistently improved our processes. = 我们一直在改进我们的流程。


过去完成时:
They had consistently failed to meet their deadlines. = 他们一直没有按时完成期限。
He had consistently denied any involvement. = 他一直否认任何参与。


将来时:
She will consistently strive for excellence. = 她将始终追求卓越。
They will consistently update the software. = 他们将持续更新软件。


虚拟语气:
If he consistently practiced, he would be a better musician. = 如果他坚持练习，他会成为一个更好的音乐家。
I wish she consistently followed the instructions. = 我希望她一直按照指示去做。


被动语态:
The results were consistently misinterpreted. = 结果总是被误解。
The product is consistently rated highly by customers. = 该产品一直受到客户的高度评价。


疑问句:
Does he consistently follow the rules? = 他是否一直遵守规则？
Have they consistently maintained quality control? = 他们是否一直保持质量控制？


否定句:
He didn't consistently attend the meetings. = 他没有持续参加会议。
They haven't consistently delivered on their promises. = 他们一直没有信守承诺。
