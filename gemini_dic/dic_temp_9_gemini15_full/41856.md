coryphodont:/ˈkɒrɪfədɒnt/| n. |冠齿兽|A coryphodont is an extinct genus of early mammals belonging to the order Pantodonta.  They were large, herbivorous mammals that lived during the Paleocene and Eocene epochs. They are characterized by their large, robust teeth, particularly their prominent upper canines.  |Example: The fossil remains of the coryphodont were discovered in the Eocene deposits of Wyoming.  冠齿兽的化石遗骸在怀俄明州的始新世沉积物中被发现。|近义词:  (No direct synonyms as it's a specific extinct genus) |反义词: (Not applicable)|名词
