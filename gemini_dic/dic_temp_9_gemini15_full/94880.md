in-character:/ˌɪnˈkærəktər/| adj.|角色扮演的，符合角色的|Acting or behaving in a way that is consistent with the personality or role of a particular character.  |例句：The actor stayed completely in-character throughout the entire interview.  演员在整个采访过程中都完全保持角色状态。

例句：She played the role in-character, capturing the essence of the character perfectly. 她入戏很深，完美地捕捉到了角色的精髓。


近义词：in role, true to character, authentic

反义词：out of character, unconvincing, inconsistent


词性:形容词
