crescentade:/ˌkrɛsənˈteɪd/| n.|渐强乐段，渐强演奏|A gradual increase in loudness in music, often used as a musical figure.|The orchestra built to a powerful crescentade at the climax of the symphony. 乐队在交响乐的高潮部分逐渐增强音量。|近义词：crescendo, swell|反义词：decrescendo, diminuendo|名词
