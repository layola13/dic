conformations:/ˌkɒnfəˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |构象，构型 |The spatial arrangement of atoms in a molecule.  Different conformations arise from rotation around single bonds. Conformations are not distinct chemical species, but rather different arrangements of the same molecule.  |例句：The different conformations of cyclohexane affect its reactivity. 环己烷的不同构象影响其反应活性。

The study of protein conformations is crucial in understanding its function. 蛋白质构象的研究对于理解其功能至关重要。|近义词：configurations, arrangements |反义词：N/A
