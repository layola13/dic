protiston:/ˈproʊtɪstən/| n. |原生生物；单细胞生物|A protistan is a eukaryotic organism that is not an animal, plant, or fungus. Protists are a diverse group of organisms that live in a wide range of environments, including water, soil, and the bodies of other organisms. Many protists are single-celled, but some are multicellular.  | Example: Paramecium is a well-known example of a protistan.  纤毛虫是一个众所周知的原生生物的例子。 |近义词：single-celled organism, microorganism|反义词：multicellular organism
