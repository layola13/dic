iconic:/aɪˈkɒnɪk/ | /aɪˈkɑːnɪk/ | adj. |标志性的，偶像的，极负盛名的 | Widely recognized and representative of a particular place, thing, or person.  Something iconic is instantly recognizable and often associated with a particular culture, period, or movement. |  The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris.  巴黎铁塔是巴黎的标志性象征。

The Statue of Liberty is an iconic image of America. 自由女神像是美国的标志性形象。


近义词：emblematic, symbolic, representative, legendary, famous, renowned | 反义词：unremarkable, insignificant, obscure, unknown


词性：形容词

词源:
The word "iconic" comes from the Greek word "eikon," meaning "image" or "likeness."  It entered English in the 19th century and gained wider usage in the 20th century, particularly with the rise of mass media and popular culture. The suffix "-ic" denotes "relating to" or "characteristic of."
