kipskin:/ˈkɪpskɪn/| n. | 蹓跶皮，山羊皮| Kip skin is a type of leather made from the hide of a young goat or kid. It is known for its softness, suppleness, and often slightly pebbled surface.  It's frequently used in the manufacture of gloves, shoes, and other leather goods.| Example: The gloves were made of luxurious kipskin.| 近义词：kidskin, goatskin | 反义词：无
