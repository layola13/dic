nonfriends:/ˈnɒnfrɛndz/ | n. |非朋友，泛泛之交，不熟的人 |People who are acquaintances but not close friends.  They may interact superficially, but lack the intimacy and trust of a true friendship.  The term often implies a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a lack of warmth or genuine connection. | Example:  He considered many of his coworkers nonfriends; they were polite but not close. 他认为许多同事只是泛泛之交；他们很礼貌，但并不亲近。|近义词：acquaintances, casual friends |反义词：close friends, best friends
