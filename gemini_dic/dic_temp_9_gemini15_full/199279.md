silverer:/ˈsɪlvərər/| n. v.|镀银者；使…镀银的人或物|A person or thing that silvers something.  The word refers to someone who applies a silver coating to objects, or to something that produces a silver effect.|例句：The silversmith is a skilled silversmith, a true silversmith.  这位银匠技艺高超，是一位真正的银匠。


The antique mirror had a beautiful silvered finish, reflecting light brilliantly. 这面古董镜子有着美丽的镀银饰面，光线反射得非常明亮。|近义词：plater, coater|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., gilder if referring to gold plating).
