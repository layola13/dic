secretively:/sɪˈkriːtɪvli/ | adv. | 秘密地，偷偷地 |  in a secretive manner; in a way that is intended to be kept secret.  |例句：She whispered secretively to her friend. 她偷偷地对她朋友耳语。
例句：They met secretively in a back alley. 他们在后巷秘密会面。| 近义词：stealthily, clandestinely, furtively, privately | 反义词：openly, publicly, frankly

词源:
