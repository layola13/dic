resketch:/riːˈskɛtʃ/| vt.|重新描绘，重绘|To sketch something again; to make a new sketch of something.|例句：I need to resketch the design to incorporate the new feedback. 我需要重新绘制设计图以纳入新的反馈。|例句：The architect decided to resketch the blueprints after discovering a flaw. 建筑师在发现缺陷后决定重新绘制蓝图。|近义词：redraw, redraft, remake|反义词：finish, complete
