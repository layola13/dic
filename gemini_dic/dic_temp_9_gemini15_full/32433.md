chimer:/ˈkaɪmər/| n. |嵌合体；幻想；妄想 |A chimera is an organism that contains cells from two or more distinct lineages.  It can also refer to something that is wildly imaginative or fanciful, often unrealistic.  |例句：The scientist created a chimera by combining genes from different species. 科学家通过结合不同物种的基因创造了一个嵌合体。

The mythical creature was a chimera, a terrifying blend of lion, goat, and serpent.  那个神话生物是奇美拉，是狮、羊和蛇的可怕混合体。
