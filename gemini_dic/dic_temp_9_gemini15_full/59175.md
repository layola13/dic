dysfunctioning: /dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/ | adj. | 功能失调的；运作不良的 |  Describes something that is not working properly or effectively. It implies a failure in the normal function or operation of a system, organ, or process. | 例句：The dysfunctioning engine stalled repeatedly.  =  发动机功能失调，反复熄火。


The hospital's dysfunctioning air conditioning system made patients uncomfortable. = 医院空调系统失灵，让病人感到不舒服。| 近义词：malfunctioning, failing, broken, impaired | 反义词：functioning, working, operating properly
