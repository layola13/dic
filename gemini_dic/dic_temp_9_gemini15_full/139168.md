obituarial:/əˌbɪtʃuˈɛəriəl/| adj.|讣告的；悼词的|Relating to or characteristic of an obituary.  An obituarial notice is a formal announcement of a death, typically including a brief biography of the deceased.|例句：The newspaper published an obituarial notice for the renowned scientist.  报纸刊登了关于这位著名科学家的讣告。

The magazine included an obituarial column featuring several prominent figures who passed away that month. 这本杂志包含一个讣告专栏，其中介绍了该月去世的几位重要人物。|近义词： necrological, mortuary|反义词： celebratory, celebratory
