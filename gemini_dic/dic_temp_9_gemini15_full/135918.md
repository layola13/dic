nonpulmonary:/ˌnɒnˈpʌlmənəri/| adj. |非肺的；非肺部的|Not relating to or affecting the lungs.  The term describes conditions, processes, or structures that are not located in or associated with the lungs.|Example: The patient presented with nonpulmonary symptoms, indicating the problem wasn't in the lungs.  病人出现非肺部症状，表明问题不在肺部。|近义词：extrapulmonary|反义词：pulmonary
