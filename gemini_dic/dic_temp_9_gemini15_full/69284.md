feats:/fiːts/ | n. | 技艺；功绩；壮举 | Remarkable achievements or deeds; acts of skill, strength, or courage.  |  例句：Her feats of strength amazed the audience.  她的力量壮举让观众惊叹不已。

例句：The tightrope walker's feats were breathtaking.  走钢丝者的壮举令人叹为观止。


近义词：achievements, exploits, accomplishments, prowess | 反义词：failures, shortcomings, inadequacies | 词性：名词


词源:

The word "feats" originates from the Old English word "fæt," meaning "deed, act."  This Old English word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings. Over time, the spelling evolved to "feat," and the plural form "feats" emerged.
