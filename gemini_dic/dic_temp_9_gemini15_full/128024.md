neptunian:/nɛpˈtuːniən/| adj. |海王星的；类似海王星的|Relating to or characteristic of the planet Neptune.  The term can also refer to objects or phenomena resembling those found on Neptune, such as its atmosphere or icy composition.|Example:  The discovery of neptunian moons expanded our understanding of the solar system. 海王星卫星的发现拓宽了我们对太阳系的理解。The research paper detailed the neptunian atmospheric conditions. 研究论文详细介绍了海王星的大气条件。|近义词： Neptunian-like|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be applied depending on the context (e.g., terrestrial if contrasting with Earth-like characteristics).|
