confessor:/kənˈfɛsər/ | n. |忏悔者；告解神父| A person who confesses their sins, especially to a priest.  Also, a priest who hears confessions. |例句：The confessor wept openly as he recounted his past sins. 忏悔者在讲述他过去的罪孽时公开哭泣。

例句：The confessor offered words of comfort and absolution. 告解神父提供了安慰和赦免的话语。


近义词：penitent (忏悔者),  penitentiary (忏悔者, 监狱),  priest (神父,  仅在宗教语境下)


反义词：N/A (没有明显的反义词)


词性：名词
