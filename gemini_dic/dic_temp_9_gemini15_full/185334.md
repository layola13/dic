retrievement:/rɪˈtriːvmənt/| n. |检索；取回；恢复；找回 |The act of retrieving something; the process of getting something back.  It often refers to data, information, or objects. |例句：The retrieval of the lost data took several hours.  丢失数据的检索花费了数小时。

例句：The successful retrieval of the downed pilot was a testament to the rescue team's skill.  成功救回坠机的飞行员证明了救援队的技能。


近义词：recovery, reclamation, retrieval, restoration |反义词：loss, destruction
