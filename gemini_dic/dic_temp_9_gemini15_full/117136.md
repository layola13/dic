meanie:/ˈmiːni/| n.|坏人，刻薄的人|A meanie is someone who is unkind or unpleasant.|例句：Don't be such a meanie!  别这么刻薄！

例句：He's a real meanie to his little sister. 他对他妹妹非常刻薄。|近义词：bully, tyrant, tormentor|反义词：sweetheart, darling, angel
