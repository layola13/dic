preassurance: /ˌpriːəˈʃʊərəns/ | n. | 预先保证；事先保证 | A guarantee or assurance given in advance.  It suggests a confident expectation of a positive outcome before it actually occurs.  The word is relatively uncommon. | Example:  The preassurance of success fueled his ambition.  事先成功的保证激励了他的雄心壮志。  | 近义词: advance assurance, prior assurance, prior guarantee | 反义词: uncertainty, doubt
