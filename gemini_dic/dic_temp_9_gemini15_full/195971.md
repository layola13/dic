what:/wɒt/|ˈhwɑːt/| pron.|什么，什么东西，怎么，多么|  It is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something.  It can also be used to express surprise, disbelief, or other emotions.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which, how|反义词:nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished recently? = 她最近取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had they learned before the exam? = 考试前他们学到了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will we have for lunch? = 我们午餐吃什么？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What color is your car? = 你的车是什么颜色的？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事吗？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What are you doing? = 你在做什么？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？

what if = 如果...会怎么样
what for = 为了什么
no matter what = 无论如何
what's more = 而且；更重要的是

so what = 那又怎样
