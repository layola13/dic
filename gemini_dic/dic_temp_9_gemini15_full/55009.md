disputation:/ˌdɪspjuˈteɪʃən/| n. |争论；辩论；争吵 |The act of disputing; debate; controversy.  A discussion involving opposing arguments. |例句：The disputation between the two philosophers lasted for hours.  两名哲学家之间的辩论持续了数小时。

例句：The students engaged in a lively disputation about the meaning of the poem. 学生们就这首诗的含义进行了热烈的争论。


近义词：argument, debate, controversy, contention, discussion


反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, consensus


词性：名词


词源：源自拉丁语“disputare”，意为“争论，辩论”，由“dis-” (分开) + “putare” (思考，认为) 构成。
