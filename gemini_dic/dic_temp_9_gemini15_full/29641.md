caterpillars:/ˈkætərˌpɪlərz/| n.|毛毛虫，鳞翅目昆虫的幼虫|The larval stage of a butterfly or moth, characterized by its segmented body, numerous legs, and voracious appetite for leaves.  Caterpillars undergo metamorphosis to become butterflies or moths.|例句：The caterpillars were munching on the leaves of the cabbage. 毛毛虫正在啃食卷心菜的叶子。|例句：Many species of caterpillars are brightly colored to warn predators of their toxicity. 许多种类的毛毛虫颜色鲜艳，以警告捕食者它们的毒性。|近义词：larvae, grubs, inchworms|反义词:butterfly, moth (adult stage)
