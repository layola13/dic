predivider: /ˌpriːdɪˈvaɪdər/ | n. | 预分频器 | A predivider is an electronic circuit that reduces the frequency of an input signal before it is processed by another circuit, typically a frequency divider.  It's used to reduce the load on the main frequency divider, improve accuracy, and extend the range of frequencies that can be handled. |  The predivider lowered the frequency before it reached the main counter, improving measurement accuracy.  |近义词: frequency reducer, prescaler |反义词: frequency multiplier
