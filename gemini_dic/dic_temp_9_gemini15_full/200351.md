skutterudite:/ˈskʌtərˌdaɪt/| n. |方钴矿|A mineral consisting of arsenides of cobalt, nickel, and iron, often containing other elements such as sulfur, antimony, and bismuth. It is a potential thermoelectric material. | Skutterudite is a promising material for thermoelectric applications due to its unique crystal structure. 方钴矿由于其独特的晶体结构，是一种很有前景的热电材料。|近义词：cobaltite (部分情况下)|反义词：无
