padre:/ˈpɑːdreɪ/| n.|教父；神父；父亲|A padre is a priest, especially in the Roman Catholic Church, or a chaplain in the armed forces.  It can also be used informally to refer to a father.|例句：The padre gave a moving sermon.  神父做了一场感人的布道。


例句：The soldiers looked to their padre for comfort. 士兵们向他们的神父寻求安慰。


近义词：priest, chaplain, father, clergyman |反义词：N/A


词性：名词
