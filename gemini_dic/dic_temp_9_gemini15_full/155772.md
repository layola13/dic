phonograph:/ˈfɒnəɡrɑːf/ /ˈfɑːnəɡræf/| n. |留声机|A phonograph is a device for recording and reproducing sound using a stylus to cut grooves on a rotating cylinder or disc, and then playing those grooves back to produce sound.  It was a precursor to the gramophone and the modern record player.| The old phonograph in the attic still worked surprisingly well.  古老阁楼里的留声机居然还能出奇地好使。| gramophone, record player |  (无明确反义词，可考虑“digital audio player”等现代播放设备)| 名词
