farthingale:/ˈfɑːθɪŋɡeɪl/| n. | 衬裙；裙撑 | A farthingale is a support worn under a woman's skirt, typically in the 16th and 17th centuries, to give it a wide, full shape.  It's usually made of hoops or whalebone.  | Example:  The queen's farthingale was so large it nearly filled the doorway.  女王的衬裙很大，几乎占满了门口。|近义词：bustle, hoop skirt |反义词：none
