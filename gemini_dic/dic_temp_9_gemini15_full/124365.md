motivating: /ˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 激励的；鼓舞的 |  Something that is motivating inspires enthusiasm and a desire to act or achieve something.  It is characterized by its ability to stimulate interest, drive, and action.


例句：

The teacher's inspiring words were very motivating. = 老师鼓舞人心的话语非常激励人。

The new company policy is highly motivating for employees. = 新的公司政策对员工极具激励作用。


近义词：inspiring, encouraging, stimulating, inspiring, incentivizing

反义词：demotivating, discouraging, dispiriting


词性: 形容词
