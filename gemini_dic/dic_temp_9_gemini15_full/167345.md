pretrial:/ˈpriːtraɪəl/| adj.|审判前的；审理前的|Relating to the period before a trial in a court of law.  It refers to events, proceedings, or activities that occur before the actual trial begins.|例句：The pretrial hearing addressed the admissibility of the evidence. 审前听证会讨论了证据的可采性。|例句：The pretrial motions were denied by the judge.  法官驳回了审前动议。|近义词：antetrial, pre-trial|反义词：post-trial
