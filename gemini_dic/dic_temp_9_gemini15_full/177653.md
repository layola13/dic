recable: /riˈkeɪbl/ | vt. |重新布线|To replace or reinstall cables.  This verb implies a complete replacement or significant reworking of the existing cable system, often for improvement or repair.  It's not just about fixing a single broken wire, but rather a more extensive undertaking.|例句：The technician had to recable the entire server room after the flood.  技术人员必须在洪水过后重新布线整个服务器机房。

例句：We decided to recable the house to improve the internet speed. 我们决定重新布线房子以提高互联网速度。|近义词：rewire, rewire, replace cables |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be to leave the cabling as it is.  |词性：动词
