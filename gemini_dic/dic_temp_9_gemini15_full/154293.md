peristomium:/ˌpɛrɪˈstoʊmiəm/| n. |围口,口围|The peristomium is the first segment of the body of an annelid worm, surrounding the mouth. It is often modified for feeding or locomotion.  |例句：The peristomium of the earthworm is clearly visible, surrounding its mouth.  蚯蚓的围口清晰可见，环绕着它的嘴巴。

The peristomium of this polychaete worm is equipped with specialized appendages for capturing prey.  这种多毛类蠕虫的围口长有专门的附肢来捕获猎物。|近义词：oral region, mouth area|反义词:pygidium
