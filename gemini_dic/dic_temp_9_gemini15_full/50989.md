desireless: /dɪˈzaɪərləs/ | adj. | 无欲的，没有欲望的 | Lacking desire or ambition; apathetic.  |例句：He seemed desireless, almost indifferent to the praise. 他看起来无欲无求，对赞扬几乎漠不关心。


例句：After years of striving, she found a strange peace in a desireless existence.  多年奋斗之后，她在无欲无求的生活中找到了奇特的平静。


近义词：apathy, unconcerned, indifferent, passionless | 反义词：ambitious, desirous, aspirational, passionate | 词性：形容词
