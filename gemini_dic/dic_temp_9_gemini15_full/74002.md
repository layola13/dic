fortunates:/ˈfɔːrtʃənəts/| n.|幸运儿，幸运的人|People who are fortunate are lucky and have good things happen to them.  They often experience success, happiness, and good health. The word implies a positive outcome due to chance or favorable circumstances.|例句：The fortunates among us often take their good fortune for granted.  = 我们当中幸运的人往往把他们的好运视为理所当然。

The fortunates in the lottery won a million dollars. = 彩票中奖的幸运儿赢了一百万美元。|近义词：lucky ones, the privileged, the blessed|反义词：unfortunates, the unlucky, the deprived|名词
