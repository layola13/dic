ligate:/ˈlaɪɡeɪt/| vt.|结扎；扎缚；约束|To tie or bind (a vessel or duct) surgically.  To bind or tie off.  |例句：The surgeon carefully ligated the artery to stop the bleeding. 外科医生仔细地结扎了动脉以止血。

The nurse ligated the wound to prevent infection. 护士扎缚伤口以防止感染。|近义词：tie, bind, suture|反义词：untie, loosen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The surgeon ligates the artery. = 外科医生结扎动脉。
He ligates the wound. = 他扎缚伤口。


一般过去时:
The doctor ligated the vein yesterday. = 医生昨天结扎了静脉。
They ligated the bleeding vessel. = 他们结扎了出血的血管。


现在完成时:
The team has ligated all the bleeding points. = 团队已经结扎了所有出血点。
She has successfully ligated the duct. = 她已成功结扎了导管。


过去完成时:
The surgeon had ligated the artery before the hemorrhage worsened. = 在出血加剧之前，外科医生已经结扎了动脉。
They had ligated the wound to minimize infection. = 他们已经扎缚了伤口以最大限度地减少感染。

被动语态:

PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *ligāre "to bind," from the root *leig- "to bind."  The word has a long history in medical terminology, reflecting its consistent use in surgical procedures.
