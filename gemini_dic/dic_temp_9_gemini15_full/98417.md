insouciantly:/ɪnˈsuːsiəntli/ | adv. | 无忧无虑地；漫不经心地；漠不关心地 |  Characterized by a casual lack of concern; nonchalantly. It suggests a carefree attitude, often to the point of seeming indifferent or unconcerned. | 例句：She strolled insouciantly down the street, seemingly oblivious to the chaos around her. 她漫不经心地沿着街道漫步，似乎没有注意到周围的混乱。

He flipped through the pages of the book insouciantly. 他漫不经心地翻阅书页。


近义词：carefree, nonchalantly, casually, unconcernedly | 反义词：carefully, anxiously, worriedly, concernedly
