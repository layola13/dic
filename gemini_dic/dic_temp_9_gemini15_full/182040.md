remembered:/rɪˈmɛmbərd/| v. |记得，想起 |To have in one's memory; recall.  The past participle of "remember".| I remembered to lock the door. 我记得锁门了。|  recollect, recall | forget |动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
I remember my childhood. = 我记得我的童年。
She remembers the details of the accident. = 她记得事故的细节。

一般过去时:
I remembered his name. = 我想起了他的名字。
They remembered to bring gifts. = 他们记得带礼物了。

现在完成时:
I have remembered everything he told me. = 我记住了他告诉我的所有事情。
She has remembered to send the email. = 她记得发送邮件了。

过去完成时:
I had remembered to set my alarm clock. = 我记得我已经设置了闹钟。
They had remembered all the instructions. = 他们记住了所有的指示。


将来时:
I will remember this day for the rest of my life. = 我将永远记住这一天。
She will remember to call you. = 她会记得给你打电话。


虚拟语气:
If I remembered his number, I would call him. = 如果我记得他的号码，我会给他打电话。
I wish I remembered where I put my keys. = 我真希望我记得我把钥匙放在哪里了。


被动语态:
The event was remembered by many people. = 许多人都记得那件事。
His kindness was remembered by everyone. = 大家都记得他的好意。


疑问句:
Do you remember my name? = 你记得我的名字吗？
Did they remember the meeting? = 他们记得会议吗？


否定句:
I don't remember his face. = 我不记得他的脸。
She didn't remember to pay the bill. = 她忘记付账了。


PHRASE->
remember me to... = 代我向...问好
remember when... = 还记得...的时候吗
I'll always remember... = 我将永远记得...
remember to... = 记得去做...
as I remember... = 依我记得...
