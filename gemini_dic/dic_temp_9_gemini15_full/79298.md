glazer:/ˈɡleɪzər/| n. |上釉者；使光滑的人或物|A glazer is a person or thing that applies a glaze to something, typically pottery or other ceramics.  It can also refer to someone or something that makes something smooth or shiny. |例句：The glazer carefully applied the final coat of glaze to the pottery. 上釉工小心翼翼地将最后一层釉料涂在陶器上。|例句：The new car polish acts as a glazer, giving the paint a high shine. 新的汽车抛光剂就像一个上光剂，使油漆具有高光泽度。|近义词： polisher, smoother |反义词：roughener
