catcher:/ˈkætʃər/| n.|捕手；接球手；捕获器|A person who catches, especially one who catches balls in baseball or cricket.  It can also refer to a device or person that catches something. |例句：He's the best catcher in the league. 他是联盟里最好的捕手。

例句：The spider is a master catcher of insects.  蜘蛛是昆虫捕获高手。

近义词：receiver, trapper, interceptor

反义词：thrower, pitcher
