laddie:/ˈlædi/| n. |小伙子，小男孩|A young boy or man, often used affectionately or in a slightly condescending way.  It is a Scottish term. |例句：He's a fine laddie, that one. 他是个好小伙子。

例句：The laddie helped his mother with the chores.  那个小男孩帮他妈妈做家务。


近义词：boy, lad, young man, chap

反义词：old man


词性:名词

USAGE->

The laddie plays football every Saturday. = 这个小伙子每个星期六都踢足球。
That laddie always helps his neighbours. = 那个小伙子总是帮助他的邻居。
