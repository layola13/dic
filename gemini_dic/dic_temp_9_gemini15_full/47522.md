death-rattle: /ˈdɛθˌrætl/ | n. | 临死前的喘息；死亡的征兆 | The rattling sound made in the throat of a dying person, caused by the passage of air over accumulated mucus or fluid.  It is often a sign of impending death. |  例句：The old dog’s death-rattle was a heartbreaking sound.  这只老狗临死前的喘息声令人心碎。

例句：His final breath was a weak death-rattle. 他最后的气息是一阵微弱的临终喘息。


近义词：death throes, last gasp, final breath

反义词：None (as it describes a specific phenomenon related to death)

词性：名词
