landslide:/ˈlændˌslaɪd/ | n. | 山崩，滑坡；压倒性胜利 | A landslide is a large mass of earth and rock that falls down the side of a mountain or cliff.  It can also refer to a victory in an election by a very large margin. |例句：The heavy rains caused a massive landslide, blocking the highway.  大雨引发了大规模的山崩，阻塞了高速公路。

The election results were a landslide victory for the incumbent president.  选举结果是现任总统的压倒性胜利。|近义词：landslip, avalanche, rout |反义词：narrow victory, defeat
