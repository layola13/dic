liquefiers:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪərz/| n.|液化器；液化剂|A liquefier is a device or substance that converts a gas or solid into a liquid.  It can refer to equipment used in industrial processes or to chemical agents that facilitate liquefaction.|例句：The factory uses sophisticated liquefiers to process natural gas.  这家工厂使用先进的液化器来处理天然气。|例句：The addition of a liquefier to the mixture allowed for easier transport. 添加液化剂到混合物中使得运输更容易。|近义词：liquefying agent, liquefaction device |反义词：solidifier
