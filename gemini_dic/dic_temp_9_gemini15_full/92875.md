idealistic:/ˌaɪdiˈælɪstɪk/ | adj. |理想主义的；空想的| characterized by idealism; unrealistically aiming for perfection|  He's idealistic and believes everyone is inherently good. 他很理想主义，相信每个人本质上都是善良的。|近义词：utopian, visionary, romantic|反义词：realistic, pragmatic, cynical|

He is idealistic in his approach to social reform. = 他在社会改革方面采取理想主义的方法。
She remains idealistic despite facing many setbacks. = 尽管面临许多挫折，她仍然保持着理想主义。
