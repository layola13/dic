dropsied:/ˈdrɒpsid/ | adj. | 患水肿的；浮肿的 | Affected by dropsy (an obsolete term for edema or swelling caused by fluid retention).  The term is now largely archaic and considered insensitive.  | Example: The old woman was dropsied and barely able to walk.  这位老妇人患有水肿，几乎无法行走。 | 近义词：edematous, swollen, puffy | 反义词：dry, firm
