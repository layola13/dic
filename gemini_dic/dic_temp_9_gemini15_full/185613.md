revenging:/rɪˈvendʒɪŋ/| v. |复仇；报仇|The act of taking revenge; inflicting punishment in return for a wrong.  It implies a deliberate and often planned act of retribution.|例句：He spent years plotting his revenging on those who had wronged him. 他花了数年时间策划报复那些冤枉他的人。
例句：The revenging of the murder of his father consumed his life.  为他父亲的谋杀报仇占据了他的生活。|近义词：avenging, retaliating, exacting revenge|反义词：forgiving, pardoning|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revenges every slight. = 他报复每一次轻蔑。
They are revenging the injustices of the past. = 他们在报复过去的罪恶。

一般过去时:
She revenged herself on her enemies. = 她向她的敌人复仇了。
He revenged his brother's death. = 他为他兄弟的死报了仇。

现在完成时:
He has revenged himself for years of humiliation. = 他已经报复了他多年来的屈辱。
They have revenged the wrongs inflicted upon them. = 他们已经报复了加诸于他们身上的不公。

过去完成时:
She had revenged herself before he could escape. = 他还没来得及逃跑，她就报复了他。
They had revenged the insult before the meeting concluded. = 会议结束前，他们已经报复了这种侮辱。

将来时:
He will revenge his father's murder. = 他将为他父亲的谋杀报仇。
They will revenge the injustices suffered by their ancestors. = 他们将报复祖先遭受的不公正待遇。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would revenge this insult. = 如果我是他，我会报复这种侮辱。
I wish I could revenge the wrongs done to my family. = 我希望我能报复加诸于我家族的不公。

被动语态:
The insult will be revenged. = 这种侮辱将会得到报复。
The crime must be revenged. = 罪行必须得到报复。

疑问句:
Will you revenge this injury? = 你会报复这次伤害吗？
How will they revenge themselves? = 他们将如何报复？

否定句:
He did not revenge himself on his tormentors. = 他没有向折磨他的人报复。
She will not revenge her father's death passively. = 她不会消极地为父亲的死报仇。
