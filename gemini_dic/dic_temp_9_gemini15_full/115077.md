maneuverably:/məˈnuːvərəbli/ | adj. | 易于操纵的；灵活的 | Easily capable of being maneuvered; agile.  The small, nimble boat was highly maneuverable in tight spaces.  | 近义词：agile, nimble, flexible, easily handled | 反义词：unwieldy, clumsy, inflexible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is maneuverable in shallow waters. = 这艘船在浅水区易于操纵。
The drone is highly maneuverable, making it ideal for aerial photography. = 这架无人机非常灵活，非常适合航拍。


一般过去时:
The car was maneuverable despite its size. = 这辆车尽管很大，但仍然易于操纵。
The fighter jet was exceptionally maneuverable during the air combat. = 在空战中，战斗机表现出非凡的机动性。


现在完成时:
The new software has made the system more maneuverable. = 新软件使系统更易于操作。
The company has become more maneuverable in the competitive market. = 这家公司在竞争激烈的市场中变得更加灵活。


过去完成时:
The plane had been maneuverable before the engine failure. = 在发动机故障之前，飞机是可以操纵的。
The team had been maneuverable in their approach to the problem. = 该团队在解决问题的方法上非常灵活。


将来时:
The spacecraft will be maneuverable once it enters orbit. = 宇宙飞船一旦进入轨道，就能灵活地机动。
The new design will make the vehicle more maneuverable on rough terrain. = 新设计将使车辆在崎岖地形上更易于操纵。


虚拟语气:
If the vehicle were more maneuverable, we could reach the destination sooner. = 如果车辆更易于操纵，我们可以更快到达目的地。
I wish the helicopter were more maneuverable in this strong wind. = 我希望直升机在这种强风中更易于操纵。


被动语态:
The ship was maneuvered skillfully into the harbor. = 这艘船被熟练地操纵进港。
The aircraft was maneuvered expertly to avoid collision. = 飞机被熟练地操纵以避免碰撞。(注意这里是被动语态，maneuverable本身是形容词，不能用被动语态)


疑问句:
Is the vehicle maneuverable enough for this terrain? = 这辆车在这个地形上足够灵活吗？
Is the new software more maneuverable than the old one? = 新软件比旧软件更易于操作吗？


否定句:
The large truck was not very maneuverable in the narrow streets. = 大型卡车在狭窄的街道上不太好操纵。
The old system was not maneuverable enough to meet our requirements. = 旧系统不够灵活，无法满足我们的要求。


PHRASE->
highly maneuverable: 高度灵活的
relatively maneuverable: 相对灵活的
easily maneuverable: 易于操纵的
less maneuverable: 较难操纵的
poorly maneuverable: 操纵性差的

词源:
源自法语 *manœuvre (操纵，演习)，最终源自古法语 *manoeuvre，源自拉丁语 *manus (手) + *opera (工作)。  "-able" 是英语后缀，表示“可…的，能够…的”。
