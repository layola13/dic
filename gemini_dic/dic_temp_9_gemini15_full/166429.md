/priːrɪˈstrɪkʃən/ | n. | 事先限制；预先限制 |  A restriction imposed before something else happens or is done.  It often involves preventative measures or limitations put in place in anticipation of a potential problem or undesirable outcome.  | Example: The prerestriction on alcohol sales at the event prevented underage drinking. 事先对该活动酒精销售的限制防止了未成年人饮酒。 | 近义词：preemptive limitation, advance restriction, prior constraint | 反义词：post-restriction, subsequent limitation
