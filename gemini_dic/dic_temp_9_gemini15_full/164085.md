prediscuss:/ˌpriːdɪˈskʌs/| v.|预先讨论，预先商讨|To discuss something beforehand; to have a preliminary discussion.|例句：We prediscussed the agenda before the meeting. 我们在会议前预先讨论了议程。|例句：It's important to prediscuss the project's potential risks. 预先讨论项目潜在的风险很重要。|近义词：predetermine, prearrange, plan|反义词：postpone, delay
