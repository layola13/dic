kirkyard:/ˈkɪrkjɑːrd/| n. |教堂墓地|A kirkyard is a churchyard; a piece of ground surrounding a church, used for burying the dead.  It is primarily a Scottish term.|The old kirkyard was overgrown with ivy and moss.|近义词：churchyard, graveyard, cemetery|反义词：无
