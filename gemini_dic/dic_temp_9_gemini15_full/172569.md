pyrology:/paɪˈrɒlədʒi/| n. |火成岩学，火山学|The scientific study of fire and its effects, particularly in relation to geology and volcanic phenomena.  It encompasses the study of igneous rocks, volcanic eruptions, and related processes. |Example:  Pyrology helps us understand the formation of volcanic landscapes.  火成岩学帮助我们了解火山景观的形成。|近义词： volcanology |反义词：无
