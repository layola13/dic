recitation:/ˌresɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |背诵；朗诵；背诵表演|The act of reciting something, such as a poem or a speech, from memory or a prepared text.  It can also refer to the performance itself. |例句：The student gave a flawless recitation of the poem.  学生完美地背诵了这首诗。|例句：Her recitation of the Shakespearean sonnet was captivating. 她朗诵莎士比亚十四行诗的表演很迷人。|近义词：rehearsal, rendition, recital |反义词：improvisation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recites a poem every morning. = 他每天早上朗诵一首诗。
She recites her lines for the play. = 她背诵她的话剧台词。


一般过去时:
The children recited their poems for their parents. = 孩子们为父母朗诵了他们的诗歌。
He recited the Gettysburg Address flawlessly. = 他完美地背诵了葛底斯堡演说。


现在完成时:
She has recited this poem many times. = 她已经多次背诵这首诗了。
They have recited the Pledge of Allegiance. = 他们已经宣誓效忠了。


过去完成时:
He had recited the alphabet before he could read. = 他在会阅读之前就已经背诵了字母表。
She had recited the script many times before the performance. =  演出之前她已经排练过很多次剧本了。


将来时:
She will recite her poem at the school assembly. = 她将在学校集会上朗诵她的诗歌。
He will recite from memory at the competition. = 他将在比赛中进行背诵。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the poem, I would recite it. = 如果我知道这首诗，我会背诵它。
I wish I could recite Shakespeare as well as he can. = 我希望我能像他一样好地背诵莎士比亚的作品。


被动语态:
The poem was recited by many students. = 这首诗被许多学生背诵。
The speech will be recited at the ceremony. = 这篇演讲将在仪式上被朗诵。


疑问句:
Will you recite the poem for us? = 你会为我们朗诵这首诗吗？
Did he recite the entire play? = 他背诵了整出戏吗？


否定句:
He did not recite the poem correctly. = 他没有正确地背诵这首诗。
She cannot recite the lines without looking at her notes. = 她不看笔记就背不出台词。


PHRASE->
