reoccupation:/ˌriːˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃən/|/n.|重新占领，再占用|The act of occupying something again after it has been vacated or lost.  It often implies a forceful or assertive retake of control.|例句：The reoccupation of the city by the rebels sparked widespread unrest.  叛军重新占领该城市引发了广泛的骚乱。

例句：The reoccupation of the abandoned factory by squatters led to safety concerns.  无家可归者重新占据废弃工厂导致安全问题。


近义词：recapture, reconquest, retaking, repossession


反义词：evacuation, abandonment, surrender


词性:名词
