myotomic:/ˌmaɪəˈtɑːmɪk/| adj.|肌节的；肌节的；与肌节有关的|Relating to or characteristic of a myotome.  A myotome is a group of muscles innervated by a single spinal nerve root. Therefore, myotomic refers to the structure, function, or properties of these muscle groups.|The myotomic muscles of the back are responsible for posture and movement.  背部的肌节肌肉负责姿势和运动。|近义词：muscular, myomeric|反义词：None specifically contrasting.
