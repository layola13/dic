matross:/məˈtrɒs/| n. |水兵，海军陆战队士兵 |A matross was a type of sailor or soldier, typically a junior enlisted rank in the 17th and 18th centuries, often associated with artillery or other specialized duties.  The term has largely fallen out of use. |例句：The captain ordered the matrosses to prepare the cannons.  船长命令水兵准备火炮。  |近义词：sailor, soldier, gunner, seaman|反义词：officer, admiral
