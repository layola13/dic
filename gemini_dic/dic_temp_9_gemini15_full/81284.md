granter:/ˈɡræntər/| n. |授予者，给予者|A person or entity that grants something, such as a right, privilege, or permission.  This term often appears in legal contexts.|例句：The granter of the license is responsible for ensuring its validity.  授予许可证的人有责任确保其有效性。|例句：The university is the granter of scholarships to deserving students. 大学是向优秀学生提供奖学金的机构。|近义词：donor, provider, bestower |反义词：recipient, receiver
