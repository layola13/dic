excursion:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/ | n. |短途旅行，远足，游览 | A short journey or trip, especially one taken for pleasure.  |例句：We took a day excursion to the coast. 我们去海岸做了一日游。
例句：The school organized an excursion to the museum. 学校组织了一次去博物馆的郊游。|近义词：trip, outing, jaunt, tour |反义词：stay, residence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We take an excursion every summer. = 我们每年夏天都会进行一次短途旅行。
They often take excursions to the mountains. = 他们经常去山区远足。

一般过去时:
We took an excursion to the lake last weekend. = 上周末我们去湖边远足了。
They took a guided excursion through the castle. = 他们参加了一次城堡的导游游览。

现在完成时:
We have taken many excursions this year. = 今年我们已经进行了许多次短途旅行。
They have never taken an excursion abroad. = 他们从未出国旅行过。

过去完成时:
We had already taken several excursions before the summer ended. = 夏天结束前我们已经进行过几次短途旅行了。
They had never taken such a long excursion before. = 他们以前从未进行过这么长的旅行。

将来时:
We will take an excursion to the beach next month. = 下个月我们将去海滩远足。
They will be taking an excursion to the national park. = 他们将去国家公园游览。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would take an excursion to the countryside. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会去乡下远足。
I wish we could take an excursion to Europe next year. = 我希望我们明年能去欧洲旅行。

被动语态:
The excursion was cancelled due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，这次旅行被取消了。
The excursion is being organized by the school. = 学校正在组织这次旅行。

疑问句:
Will you take an excursion this weekend? = 这个周末你会去远足吗？
What kind of excursion are you planning? = 你计划什么类型的旅行？

否定句:
We didn't take an excursion last year. = 去年我们没有去远足。
They won't be taking an excursion this summer. = 今年夏天他们不会去远足。
