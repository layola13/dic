isn't:/ɪznt/| contraction| 不是 | It is a contraction of "is not". It's used as a negative form of the verb "to be" in the present tense, primarily in the third-person singular (he, she, it).  |He isn't coming to the party. 他不来参加派对。|is, are|is, are|
