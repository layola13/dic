pro-ethnic: /ˌproʊˈɛθnɪk/ | adj. | 亲民族的；支持民族主义的 |

英文解析:  The term "pro-ethnic" describes something or someone that supports or promotes the interests and values of a particular ethnic group. It often implies a positive view of ethnicity and a desire to preserve or enhance ethnic identity and culture. However, depending on the context, it can carry either positive or negative connotations.  In a positive light, it might refer to efforts to promote cultural preservation or community building. In a negative light, it might be associated with ethnocentrism, nationalism, or even exclusionary practices.

例句:

The pro-ethnic policies of the government aimed to strengthen national unity.  = 政府的亲民族政策旨在加强民族团结。

His pro-ethnic stance led to accusations of discrimination. = 他支持民族主义的立场导致了他受到歧视的指控。


近义词: nationalist, ethnocentric, pro-national

反义词: anti-ethnic, cosmopolitan, multiculturalist


词性: adjective (形容词)
