phototransformation:/ˌfoʊtoʊtrænzfərˈmeɪʃən/| n. |光转化；光致转变|The alteration of a molecule's structure or properties due to the absorption of light.  This process is crucial in many areas, including photography, photochemistry, and environmental science.|例句：The phototransformation of pollutants in the atmosphere is a complex process.  大气中污染物的光转化是一个复杂的过程。|近义词：photochemical reaction, light-induced transformation|反义词：There is no direct antonym, but the opposite could be described as a process unaffected by light.
