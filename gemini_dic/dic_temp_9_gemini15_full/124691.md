movin': /ˈmuːvɪn/ | adj. | 移动的；感人的；动人的 | Moving is an adjective describing something that is in motion or something that evokes strong emotion.  It can refer to physical movement or emotional impact. |例句：The moving van arrived late.  (移动货车迟到了。)

The movie had a very moving scene. (这部电影有一个非常感人的场景。)

近义词：stirring, touching, affecting, emotional, dynamic

反义词：static, stationary, unmoving, unemotional, impassive
