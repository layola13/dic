mode:/moʊd/|n.|模式，方式，样式|The way something is done or operates; a particular way of operating, doing something, or behaving.|The machine operates in two modes: automatic and manual. 这台机器有两种操作模式：自动和手动。  The car is in sports mode. 这辆车处于运动模式。|method, manner, style|chaos, disorder|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine is in sleep mode. = 机器处于睡眠模式。
We operate in a collaborative mode. = 我们以协作模式运作。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
