null-and-void: /nʌl ænd vɔɪd/ | adj. | 无效的，作废的 |  Meaningless or without legal force.  A contract or agreement that is null and void is legally unenforceable.  |  例句：The contract was declared null and void due to a lack of consideration.  合同因缺乏对价而被宣布无效。

The contract is null and void because it was signed under duress.  由于是在胁迫下签署的，该合同无效。


近义词：invalid, unenforceable, voidable, ineffective

反义词：valid, enforceable, legal, binding


词性: adjective
