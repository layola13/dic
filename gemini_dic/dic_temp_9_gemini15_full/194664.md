self-unloading:/ˌsɛlfˈʌnloʊdɪŋ/| adj.| 自动卸货的 |  Describing a vehicle or mechanism designed to unload its contents without external assistance.  This typically involves a system of conveyors, hydraulic rams, or other mechanisms built into the vehicle itself. | Example: The self-unloading barge efficiently transported and discharged the grain.  自动卸货驳船高效地运输和卸下了谷物。  The construction company used a self-unloading cement mixer to speed up the process. 建筑公司使用自动卸料水泥搅拌机来加快施工进度。|近义词：automatically unloading, self-discharging | 反义词：manually unloading, requiring external unloading |
