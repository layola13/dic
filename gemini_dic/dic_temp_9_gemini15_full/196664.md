sevensomes:/ˈsɛvənˌsʌm/| n. |七人一组；七个一组|A group of seven people or things.  The word is a blend of "seven" and "-some," a suffix indicating a group or collection.|例句：They formed a sevensome for the hike.他们组成了一个七人小组去远足。


例句：A sevensome of musicians played a lively tune.七个音乐家演奏了一支轻快的曲子。
