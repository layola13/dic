remould:/ˌriːˈmoʊld/ | vt. |重塑；改造；改变…的形状 |To change the shape or form of something, often by applying pressure or heat; to fundamentally alter or reshape something.|The sculptor remoulded the clay into a new form. 雕塑家将粘土重新塑造成了新的形态。|The company is attempting to remould its image. 公司正试图重塑其形象。|reshape, reform, reconstruct |destroy, maintain, preserve|动词


USAGE->

They remould the metal into different shapes. = 他们把金属重塑成不同的形状。
She remoulds her ideas based on new information. = 她根据新的信息重塑自己的想法。
