confirmable: /kənˈfɜːrməbl/ | adj. | 可证实的；可确认的 |  capable of being confirmed or verified; able to be proven true.  | The information is confirmable through independent sources.  这条信息可以通过独立的消息来源得到证实。 | verifiable, provable, demonstrable | unconfirmable, unproven, undemonstrable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The statement is confirmable. = 这份声明是可以证实的。
This information is confirmable through multiple sources. = 通过多个来源可以证实此信息。

一般过去时:
The theory was confirmable with the new evidence. = 用新的证据可以证实这个理论。
The alibi was confirmable by witnesses. = 目击证人可以证实不在场证明。

现在完成时:
The rumors have been confirmable since the investigation. = 自调查以来，谣言已被证实。
The results have been confirmable by repeated experiments. = 重复实验已证实了结果。

过去完成时:
The hypothesis had been confirmable before the experiment was completed. = 在实验完成之前，假设已被证实。
The identity of the suspect had been confirmable prior to the arrest.  嫌疑人的身份在逮捕之前就已经确定了。

将来时:
The prediction will be confirmable after the event. = 事件发生后将可以证实预测。
The findings will be confirmable through further research. = 通过进一步的研究，可以证实这些发现。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were confirmable, the case would be closed. = 如果证据可信，案件就会结案。
If the theory were confirmable, it would revolutionize the field. = 如果该理论可证实，它将彻底改变该领域。

被动语态:
The results are being confirmable by the scientists. = 科学家们正在证实结果。
The date of the meeting is being confirmable by the organizers. = 会议日期正在由组织者确认。

疑问句:
Is the claim confirmable? = 这一说法可信吗？
Is this information confirmable by independent sources? = 此信息可以通过独立的消息来源证实吗？

否定句:
The statement is not confirmable. = 这份声明无法证实。
The story is not confirmable by any credible evidence.  这个故事没有任何可信的证据可以证实。
