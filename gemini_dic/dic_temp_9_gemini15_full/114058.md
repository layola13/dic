magnetite:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪt/| n. |磁铁矿|A black, magnetic mineral consisting of iron(II,III) oxide (Fe3O4), the most important iron ore.| Magnetite is a common mineral that is often found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.  The strong magnetism of magnetite is responsible for the Earth's magnetic field.|近义词：loadstone|反义词：无
