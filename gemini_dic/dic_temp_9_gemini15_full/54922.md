disposers: /dɪˈspoʊzərz/ | n. | 垃圾处理器；处理者；清除者 |  A device that grinds up food waste and flushes it down the drain.  Also refers to those who dispose of something. |

例句：

Many modern kitchens are equipped with garbage disposers.  许多现代厨房都配备了垃圾处理器。

The city hired disposers to clean up the hazardous waste.  市政府雇佣了处理者来清理有害废物。


近义词：  waste disposal units, trash compactors (for some contexts), eliminators


反义词：  preservers, collectors (in the context of waste)


词源：dispose (v.) + -er (noun suffix)  dispose 源自法语 *desposer, 源于拉丁语 disponere "to arrange, set in order, put," from dis- "apart" + ponere "to put, place."  The "-er" suffix indicates an agent or doer of the action.
