self-revealed: /ˌsɛlf rɪˈviːld/ | adj. | 自揭的；自行暴露的 |  Describes something that has been revealed or made known by the thing itself, without external intervention.  It implies an inherent quality or characteristic that becomes apparent without needing to be explicitly stated or discovered by others. |

例句：The painting's self-revealed beauty captivated the audience.  这幅画的自显之美迷住了观众。

The criminal's self-revealed guilt was evident in his nervous demeanor. 罪犯的自白罪行在他紧张的神态中显而易见。


近义词：apparent, manifest, evident, obvious, unveiled

反义词：hidden, concealed, obscured, secret, covert


词性: adjective (形容词)
