fauves:/foʊvz/| n.|野兽派(画家)|The Fauves were a group of early 20th-century French artists whose works emphasized bold colors and expressive brushstrokes.  They rejected the academic conventions of their time and sought to express emotion and sensation directly through their art.|例句：The Fauves' vibrant canvases revolutionized the art world. 野兽派画家充满活力的画布彻底改变了艺术世界。|近义词：expressionists, modernists|反义词：classicists, realists
