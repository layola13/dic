laurinitis:/ˌlɔːrɪˈnaɪtɪs/| n. |劳伦氏菌性炎症| Inflammation of the lachrymal sac, caused by infection with bacteria of the genus Moraxella (formerly known as  Neisseria).  It is characterized by swelling, redness, and pain in the area of the tear sac, often accompanied by purulent discharge. |例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as laurinitis and prescribed antibiotics. 医生诊断她的病情为劳伦氏菌性炎症并开了抗生素。  The patient presented with symptoms consistent with laurinitis, including swelling and purulent discharge from the tear duct. 病人出现了劳伦氏菌性炎症的一致症状，包括泪管肿胀和脓性分泌物。|近义词：dacryocystitis (more general term for inflammation of the tear sac)|反义词：N/A|名词
