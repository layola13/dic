highwayman:/ˈhaɪweɪmən/| n. |公路强盗，拦路抢劫者 | A highwayman is a robber who attacks travelers on roads, especially on horseback.  They were a common feature of literature and folklore during the 17th and 18th centuries. | Example: The highwayman galloped away with the stolen jewels. 公路强盗骑马逃跑了，带着偷来的珠宝。


The highwayman, masked and armed, demanded the merchant's purse.  蒙面持械的公路强盗，向商人索要钱袋。|近义词：robber, bandit, brigand |反义词：policeman, lawman
