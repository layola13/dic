came about:/keɪm əˈbaʊt/ | v. |发生，产生|To happen or occur; to come into existence.|The accident came about because of the driver's carelessness.  事故是因为司机的粗心造成的。|The project came about after years of planning. 这个项目经过多年的规划后才得以实现。|occur, happen, arise|—|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It often comes about that... = 经常发生的是...
How does this come about? = 这是怎么回事？


一般过去时:
The change came about gradually. = 变化是逐渐发生的。
How did this situation come about? = 这种情况是怎么发生的？


现在完成时:
It has come about that we need more resources. = 我们需要更多资源的情况已经发生了。
How has this misunderstanding come about? = 这种误解是怎么产生的？


过去完成时:
It had come about that the company was in debt. = 公司已经负债的情况已经发生了。
By then, it had come about that he lost everything. 到那时，他已经一无所有了。


将来时:
It will come about that we will have to adapt. = 我们将不得不适应的情况将会发生。
How will this problem come about? = 这个问题将如何产生？


虚拟语气:
If it came about that I won the lottery, I would travel the world. = 如果我中了彩票，我会环游世界。
I wish it had come about differently. = 我希望事情的结果有所不同。


被动语态:
The agreement was brought about through negotiation. = 这项协议是通过谈判达成的。(虽然came about本身没有被动语态，但可以用被动语态的动词表达类似意思)
The solution was somehow brought about.  解决方案不知怎么就实现了。(虽然came about本身没有被动语态，但可以用被动语态的动词表达类似意思)


疑问句:
How did it come about? = 这是怎么回事？
How did the fire come about? = 火灾是怎么发生的？


否定句:
The plan did not come about as expected. = 计划没有按预期进行。
It didn't come about because of luck. 这不是因为运气才发生的。
