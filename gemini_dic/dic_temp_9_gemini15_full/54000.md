discriminatingly:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋli/| adv.|有鉴别力地；有选择地；挑剔地 |Showing good judgment and taste; able to recognize differences.  It implies a careful and discerning selection or judgment, often based on a high standard. |例句：She dressed discriminatingly, choosing only the finest fabrics. 她衣着讲究，只选择最好的面料。

例句：He selected his employees discriminatingly, looking for both skill and character. 他有选择性地挑选员工，既看技能也看品格。


近义词：selectively, discerningly, judiciously, tastefully

反义词：indiscriminately, randomly, carelessly


词源：源自动词discriminate，其词根为拉丁语的“discernere”，意为“to separate, distinguish”。  "-ingly" 是副词后缀。


用法说明：

discriminatingly 主要用作副词，修饰动词，形容词或整个句子，表示行为或判断具有鉴别力、选择性或挑剔性。 它通常带有积极的含义，强调选择的质量和精细。  与 indiscriminately （不加选择地）形成对比。
