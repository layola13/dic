delves:/delvz/| v.|深入研究；探究|To search deeply and thoroughly into something.|The historian delved into the archives to find evidence for his theory. 历史学家深入研究档案以寻找他理论的证据。|She delved into her memories of childhood. 她深入回忆起童年的往事。|investigate, explore, probe|superficially examine, skim.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He delves into ancient history. 他深入研究古代历史。
She delves into the mysteries of the universe. 她探究宇宙的奥秘。

一般过去时:
The detective delved into the case. 侦探深入调查了这个案子。
They delved into the meaning of the poem. 他们深入探究了这首诗的含义。

现在完成时:
She has delved into the subject for many years. 她研究这个课题多年了。
He has delved into the depths of despair. 他已陷入深深的绝望之中。

过去完成时:
They had delved into the matter before they reached a conclusion. 他们在得出结论之前已经深入研究过这个问题了。
She had delved into the books for hours before she found the answer. 她花了几个小时查阅书籍才找到答案。

将来时:
He will delve into the secrets of the past. 他将探究过去的秘密。
We will delve into this problem further. 我们将进一步研究这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would delve into the topic more thoroughly. 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地研究这个话题。
I wish I could delve into the mysteries of the human mind. 我希望我能深入研究人类思维的奥秘。

被动语态:
The topic was delved into by many scholars. 这个话题被许多学者深入研究过。
The mystery was being delved into by the police. 警方正在深入调查这个谜团。


疑问句:

否定句:
