inclusion:/ɪnˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 包含；内含物；包含行为 | The act of including someone or something; the state of being included.  It can also refer to something that is included within something else. |例句：The inclusion of women in the workforce has increased significantly.  妇女参与劳动力市场的比例显著增加。

The report highlighted the inclusion of diverse perspectives. 报告强调了包容不同观点的重要性。


近义词：inclusion, incorporation, embrace,  enrollment

反义词：exclusion, omission, rejection


词源:  From the Old French *inclusion, from Latin inclusio, from includere "to shut in," from in- "in" + claudere "to shut."

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
