privilege:/ˈprɪvəlɪdʒ/ | n. | 特权；荣幸；特免 | a special right, advantage, or immunity granted or available only to a particular person or group.  |例句：Having access to a good education is a privilege.  拥有良好的教育机会是一种特权。


例句：It was a privilege to meet such a renowned scientist.  能见到如此著名的科学家是一种荣幸。


近义词：advantage, benefit, right, immunity | 反义词：disadvantage, hardship, burden | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Old French *privilegie, from Latin privilegium ("a private law, special right"), from privus ("private, personal") + lex, legis ("law").
