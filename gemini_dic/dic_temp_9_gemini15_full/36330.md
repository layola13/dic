coif:/kwɑːf/| n. |发型；头巾；（尤指妇女的）装饰性头饰|A coif is a hairstyle, especially a woman's, or a type of head covering.  It can also refer to a close-fitting cap worn by judges or lawyers. |例句：She wore a beautiful coif for the wedding. 她婚礼上戴了一个漂亮的发型。|例句：The judge wore a traditional coif in court. 法官在法庭上戴着传统的头巾。|近义词：hairstyle, headdress, head covering|反义词：baldness
