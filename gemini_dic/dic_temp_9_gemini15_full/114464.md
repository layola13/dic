making-off:/ˈmeɪkɪŋ ɒf/| v. |潜逃；逃之夭夭；匆忙离开 |Leaving hurriedly and secretly, especially to avoid arrest or detection.  |例句：The thieves were making off with the stolen jewels. 小偷们带着偷来的珠宝逃跑了。|例句：He made off after the accident, leaving his car behind. 事故发生后，他弃车逃跑了。|近义词：fleeing, escaping, absconding |反义词：staying, remaining, surrendering


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are making off with the company's funds. = 他们正携款潜逃。
The birds are making off into the sunset. = 鸟儿们正飞向夕阳。


一般过去时:
The robbers made off in a getaway car. = 抢劫犯们驾着逃逸车辆逃跑了。
She made off before anyone could stop her. = 还没等任何人阻止她，她就逃跑了。


现在完成时:
The suspects have made off, and the police are searching for them. = 嫌疑人已经潜逃了，警方正在搜捕他们。
They have made off with a significant amount of money. = 他们已经卷走了一大笔钱。


过去完成时:
The thief had made off before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
They had made off with the evidence before we could find it. = 我们找到证据之前，他们就已经把它带走了。


将来时:
The criminals will make off under the cover of darkness. = 罪犯们将在夜色的掩护下潜逃。
He will make off as soon as he has the chance. = 一有机会，他就会逃走。


虚拟语气:
If he had not made off, he would have been arrested. = 如果他没有潜逃，就会被捕了。
I wish I had not made off with that cake. = 我真希望我没有偷吃那块蛋糕。


被动语态:
The stolen goods were made off with quickly. = 被盗的货物很快就被卷走了。
The money was made off with by an unknown person. = 钱被一个身份不明的人卷走了。


疑问句:
Where did the thief make off to? = 小偷逃到哪里去了？
Did they make off with anything valuable? = 他们带走了什么值钱的东西吗？


否定句:
They did not make off with any of the money. = 他们没有带走任何钱。
He didn't make off, he surrendered to the police. = 他没有逃跑，而是向警方自首了。
