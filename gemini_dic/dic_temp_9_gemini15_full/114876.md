mammonize:/ˈmæmənˌaɪz/| vt.| 崇拜金钱；贪图钱财 |To be excessively preoccupied with or devoted to the acquisition of wealth.|The company's policies seemed to mammonize its employees, encouraging a relentless pursuit of profit above all else.  这家公司的政策似乎让其员工拜金，鼓励不顾一切地追求利润。|近义词：materialize, commercialize |反义词：spiritualize, altruize

They mammonize their values. = 他们把价值观都扭曲成了对金钱的追求。
