hyperbolize:/haɪˈpɜːrbəlaɪz/| vt.|夸张，夸大； |To exaggerate; to use hyperbole.|例句：He hyperbolized his achievements to impress the interviewer. 他夸大了自己的成就以给面试官留下深刻印象。|近义词：exaggerate, overstate, magnify|反义词：understate, downplay|动词


USAGE->

She often hyperbolizes when she talks about her problems. = 她谈论自己的问题时经常夸大其词。
