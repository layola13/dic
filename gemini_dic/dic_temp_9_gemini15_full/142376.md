orthopterous:/ɔːrˈθɒptərəs/ | adj. | 直翅目的；具有直翅目特征的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Orthoptera, an order of insects that includes grasshoppers, crickets, and locusts.  These insects are typically characterized by their straight, leathery forewings and powerful hind legs for jumping. | Example: The orthopterous insects were abundant in the field.  直翅目的昆虫在田野里很丰富。 The orthopterous sound filled the night.  直翅目的鸣叫声充满了夜晚。| 近义词：saltatorial (referring to jumping ability) | 反义词：None directly opposite, but terms describing insects from other orders could be considered contrasting.
