outwin:/aʊtˈwɪn/| vt.|胜过，超过|To defeat or surpass someone or something in a competition or contest.  |例句：Our team outwon their team by a score of 3-1.  我们的团队以3比1的比分击败了他们的团队。|例句：His latest novel outwins all his previous works in terms of sales. 他的最新小说在销量上超过了他以前的所有作品。|近义词：defeat, surpass, outdo, excel|反义词：lose, be defeated by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outwins his opponents in most matches. = 他在大多数比赛中都战胜了他的对手。
The company outwins its competitors in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上胜过了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outwon her rival in the election. = 她在选举中击败了她的对手。
The team outwon the opposing team in the final game. = 这支队伍在决赛中击败了对方队伍。

现在完成时:
He has outwon every challenger this season. = 他这个赛季战胜了每一个挑战者。
Our company has outwon the competition in customer satisfaction. = 我们公司在客户满意度方面胜过了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
They had outwon all their previous opponents before reaching the finals. = 在进入决赛之前，他们已经战胜了所有之前的对手。
The author had outwon many other writers in terms of critical acclaim. = 这名作者在评论界的赞誉方面已经胜过了许多其他作家。

将来时:
She will outwin her opponent if she plays her best game. = 如果她发挥出最佳水平，她将会战胜她的对手。
We believe our product will outwin the competition in the marketplace. = 我们相信我们的产品将在市场上胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outwon his opponent. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他也许就能战胜对手了。
I wish I had outwon my fears and taken the opportunity. = 我真希望我战胜了我的恐惧并抓住了那个机会。

被动语态:
The champion was outwon by a newcomer. = 冠军被一位新人击败了。
The old design was outwon by the new model. = 旧设计被新模型超越了。

疑问句:
Did the blue team outwin the red team? = 蓝队战胜了红队吗？
Will the new technology outwin the current standard? = 新技术会超越目前的标准吗？

否定句:
He didn't outwin his opponent in the first round. = 他在第一轮没有战胜他的对手。
The company failed to outwin its competitors in the new market. = 这家公司未能在这个新市场上胜过竞争对手。
