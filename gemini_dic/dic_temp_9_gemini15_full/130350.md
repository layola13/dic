non-truth:/ˌnɑːnˈtruːθ/| n. |虚假；不真实；谬误 |A statement or belief that is not true; a falsehood.  It often implies a deliberate attempt to deceive, unlike a simple mistake. |例句：The politician's statement was a blatant non-truth designed to mislead the public.  这位政治家的声明是蓄意误导公众的公然谎言。

例句：His account of events contained several non-truths. 他的事件描述包含了几处不实之处。


近义词：falsehood, lie, fabrication, deception, untruth, misrepresentation


反义词：truth, fact, reality, veracity


词性:名词
