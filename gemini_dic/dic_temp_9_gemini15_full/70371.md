fiestas:/fjɛsˈtɑːz/ | n. |节日；庆典 | Festivals; celebrations.  The word refers to festive occasions, often involving merrymaking, parades, and traditional customs.  It's commonly used in reference to Spanish-speaking cultures. |例句：The town is preparing for its annual fiestas.  这个小镇正在准备一年一度的节日。

例句：We enjoyed the vibrant fiestas during our trip to Mexico. 我们在墨西哥旅行期间享受了热烈的节日。


近义词：celebrations, festivals, festivities | 反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context, perhaps something like "mourning" or "sadness."


词性：名词
