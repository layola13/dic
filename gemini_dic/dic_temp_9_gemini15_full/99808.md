intermigrated:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪtɪd/| v. | 互相迁移；相互迁徙 |  To move between different regions or countries, often implying a reciprocal or mutual movement of populations.  This term suggests a two-way flow of migration, unlike unidirectional migration. |例句：The two countries experienced significant intermigration of their populations across their shared border.  两国在其共享边界经历了大量人口的互相迁移。

例句：Over the past decade, there has been a noticeable increase in the intermigration of skilled workers between the two nations.  在过去的十年中，这两个国家之间熟练工人的互相迁移显著增加。


近义词：mutually migrated, reciprocally migrated, cross-migrated | 反义词：emigrated (one-way migration), immigrated (one-way migration) | 词性：动词
