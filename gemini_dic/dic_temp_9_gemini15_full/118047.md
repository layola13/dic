menagogue:/ˈmɛnəɡoʊɡ/| n.| 促经药；催经药 | A menagogue is a substance or medicine that stimulates menstruation.  It's often used to induce or restart menstruation in women who have missed their period. | Example:  The doctor prescribed a menagogue to help regulate her menstrual cycle.  医生开了促经药来帮助调节她的月经周期。| 近义词：emmenagogue | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与抑制月经相关的词语，但并非完全反义)| 名词
