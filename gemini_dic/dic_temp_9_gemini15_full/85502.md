hasteners:/ˈheɪsnərz/| n.|催促者；促进物；加速器|Something or someone that hastens or accelerates a process.  It can refer to people who urge something to happen faster, or things that speed up a process.|例句：The new software is a real hastener for the workflow. 新软件极大地加快了工作流程。|例句：The constant reminders from his boss acted as hasteners to his completion of the project.老板不断的提醒促使他更快地完成了项目。|近义词：accelerators, catalysts, spurs|反义词：retardants, inhibitors, delays|名词
