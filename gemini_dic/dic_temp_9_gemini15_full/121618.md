mispackage:/mɪsˈpækɪdʒ/| v.|错误包装，包装错误|To pack (something) incorrectly or improperly.  This usually implies that the contents are damaged or misplaced due to the poor packaging.|例句：The company had to recall the product after several customers reported that it had been mispackaged.  公司在几名顾客报告产品包装错误后不得不召回产品。


例句：They mispackaged the fragile items, resulting in breakage during shipping.  他们错误地包装了易碎物品，导致运输途中损坏。


近义词：pack incorrectly, improperly package, wrap badly |反义词：pack correctly, properly package


词性：动词
