scoriaceous:/ˌskɔːriˈeɪʃəs/| adj.|多渣的；似浮渣的；充满火山渣的|Containing or resembling scoria;  characterized by a rough, porous, and often glassy surface texture, similar to that of volcanic scoria.  The term often describes rocks or volcanic formations.|The scoriaceous lava flowed down the mountainside, leaving behind a field of rough, porous rock.|近义词：vesicular, pumiceous, slaggy|反义词：compact, dense, smooth
