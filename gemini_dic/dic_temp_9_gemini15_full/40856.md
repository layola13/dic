cooey:/ˈkuːiː/| int.| 喂；哎；(招呼声)| A call used to attract attention, similar to a shout or a whistle.  It's often used in a rural or informal setting. |例句：He gave a loud cooey to attract the attention of the farmer. 他大声叫了一声“喂”来引起那个农民的注意。


例句：She cooeyed from across the field. 她从田那边喊了一声“喂”。


近义词：yodel, shout, yell, holler


反义词：无


词性: int. (感叹词)
