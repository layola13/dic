miscommunicate:/ˌmɪskəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/| vt.| 误传；沟通不良 | To fail to communicate effectively; to convey a message incorrectly or unclearly.  |例句：He miscommunicated his intentions, leading to a major misunderstanding. 他误传了他的意图，导致了重大的误解。|例句：The company miscommunicated the new policy to its employees, causing widespread confusion.  公司向员工误传了新政策，造成了普遍的混乱。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, confuse |反义词：communicate clearly, articulate


USAGE->

He miscommunicates his ideas to his team. = 他向他的团队误传了他的想法。
