focussed:/ˈfoʊkəst/ /ˈfəʊkəst/| adj.| 集中的；专注的；清晰的 | Focused or concentrated; having a clear purpose or direction.  It implies a deliberate act of concentrating one's attention or efforts. | 例句：He remained focussed on his goal despite the distractions. 他尽管分心，仍然专注于自己的目标。

例句：The photograph is beautifully focussed. 这张照片非常清晰。


近义词：concentrated, centered, fixed, attentive | 反义词：distracted, unfocused, scattered, diffuse | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is focussed on his studies. = 他专注于学业。
She is focussed on her career. = 她专注于她的事业。


一般过去时:
He was focussed on the task at hand. = 他专注于手头的任务。
She was focussed on the speaker. = 她专注地听着演讲者。


现在完成时:
I have been focussed on this project for weeks. = 我几周来一直专注于这个项目。
They have remained focussed on their objectives. = 他们一直专注于自己的目标。


过去完成时:
He had been focussed on his work all day. = 他整天都专注于工作。
She had been focussed on the problem for hours. = 她几个小时都专注于这个问题。


将来时:
I will be focussed on my presentation tomorrow. = 我明天将专注于我的演示。
He will remain focussed on his training. = 他将继续专注于他的训练。


虚拟语气:
If I were focussed, I would finish the work faster. = 如果我专注一些，我会更快完成工作。
I wish I had been more focussed during the exam. = 我希望考试时我能更专注一些。


被动语态:
His attention was focussed on the details. = 他的注意力集中在细节上。
The camera lens needs to be focussed properly. = 相机的镜头需要正确聚焦。


疑问句:
Is he focussed on his work? = 他专注于他的工作吗？
Are you focussed on what I'm saying? = 你专注于我在说的话吗？


否定句:
He wasn't focussed during the meeting. = 他在会议上没有集中注意力。
She is not focussed enough to complete the task. = 她不够专注，无法完成任务。
