prophane:/ˈproʊfeɪn/| adj.|亵渎的；不神圣的；粗俗的|  To show disrespect or contempt for something considered sacred or holy. It can also refer to language or behavior that is vulgar or indecent. |例句：It is profane to use God's name in vain.  随意使用上帝的名字是亵渎的。

例句：His profane language offended many people. 他粗俗的语言冒犯了许多人。


近义词：blasphemous, irreverent, sacrilegious, vulgar, obscene

反义词：sacred, holy, reverent, pious


词源：From Middle English *profane, from Old French *profane, from Latin *profanus, from pro- ("before," "forth," "in front of") + fanum ("temple," "shrine").  The original meaning referred to what was "outside the temple," hence, not sacred.
