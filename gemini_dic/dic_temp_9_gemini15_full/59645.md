echelon:/ˈɛʃəlɒn/| n.|梯队；阶层；等级；行列 |A level or rank in an organization, a society, or a system.  It often refers to a group of people at a similar level within a hierarchical structure. |例句：The senior echelon of the company made the final decision. 公司的高层做出了最终决定。
The new recruits were placed in the lowest echelon. 新兵被安排在最低的梯队。


近义词：level, rank, tier, stratum |反义词：base, bottom

词源:
From the French word *échelon*, meaning "rung of a ladder," ultimately from the Old French word *escalon*.  The military use, referring to a formation of troops arranged in steps or echelons, predates the more general use describing levels of power or hierarchy.
