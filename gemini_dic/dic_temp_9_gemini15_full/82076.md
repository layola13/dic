grizzle:/ˈɡrɪzəl/| v. n.| 愠怒；抱怨；(头发)灰白；|To complain or grumble in a low, unhappy voice; to become grey or grizzled.  |例句：The children grizzled all the way home. 孩子们一路上都在抱怨。  The old man's beard was grizzled with age. 老人的胡子因年老而花白。|近义词：grumble, grump, mutter, whine|反义词：be happy, be cheerful|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grizzles about his work every day. 他每天都抱怨他的工作。
She often grizzles when she's tired. 她累了的时候经常抱怨。


一般过去时:
The child grizzled until his mother gave him a candy. 孩子一直哭闹直到他妈妈给了他糖果。
He grizzled about the unfairness of the situation. 他抱怨情况的不公平。


现在完成时:
He has grizzled about the same problem for weeks. 他几周来一直在抱怨同一个问题。
She has grizzled to the point of exhaustion. 她抱怨到筋疲力尽。


过去完成时:
She had grizzled for hours before finally falling asleep.  她在最终睡着前抱怨了几个小时。
He had grizzled all day, making everyone around him miserable. 他抱怨了一整天，让周围的人都感到痛苦。


将来时:

She will probably grizzle if she doesn't get what she wants. 如果她得不到想要的东西，她可能会抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If he grizzled less, people would like him more. 如果他少抱怨一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish he wouldn't grizzle so much. 我希望他不那么抱怨。


被动语态:

The issue was grizzled about for months before a solution was found.  这个问题在找到解决方案之前被抱怨了几个月。


疑问句:

否定句:
