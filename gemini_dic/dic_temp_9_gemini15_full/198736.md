siccing:/ˈsɪkɪŋ/| v.|唆使；煽动；放狗追逐|To urge or incite (someone) to attack or harass someone else; to set a dog on (someone or something).|例句：He was siccing the dogs on the trespassers. 他唆使狗去攻击那些闯入者。|例句：The bully was siccing his friends on the smaller boy.  那个恶霸唆使他的朋友们去欺负那个个子小的男孩。|近义词：incite, instigate, urge on|反义词：protect, defend, restrain
