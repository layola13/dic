rapscallion:/ˈræpskæliən/| n. | 淘气鬼；顽童；无赖 | A rapscallion is a mischievous or playfully naughty person, often a child but sometimes an adult behaving in a childish way.  They are typically characterized by their playful disregard for rules and conventions. | Example: That rapscallion climbed the tree and stole all the apples!  那个淘气鬼爬上树偷走了所有的苹果！  The old rapscallion finally got caught trying to pull another one of his pranks.  那个老顽童最终因为试图再次恶作剧而被抓住了。| 近义词：rascal, scamp, rogue, imp, urchin | 反义词：saint, angel, goody-goody
