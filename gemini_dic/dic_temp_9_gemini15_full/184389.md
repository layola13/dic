respondence:/rɪˈspɒndəns/| n. |回应；反应；通信；书信往来 |The act of responding or answering; a reply; communication by letters.  |例句：There was a lively correspondence between the two scholars. 两位学者之间进行了热烈的通信交流。 |例句：His quick response to the emergency showed good presence of mind. 他对紧急情况的迅速反应显示出良好的临场反应。|近义词：reply, answer, communication, correspondence, reaction |反义词：silence, inaction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company responds to customer inquiries promptly. = 公司迅速回应客户的询问。
He responds well to treatment. = 他对治疗反应良好。


一般过去时:
She responded to my email yesterday. = 她昨天回复了我的邮件。
The government responded quickly to the crisis. = 政府迅速回应了危机。


现在完成时:
They have responded positively to our suggestions. = 他们对我们的建议做出了积极的回应。
She has not yet responded to my invitation. = 她还没有回复我的邀请。


过去完成时:
He had already responded before I could ask the question. = 我还没来得及提问，他已经作出了回应。
The team had responded effectively to previous challenges. = 该团队已经有效地应对了过去的挑战。


将来时:
We will respond to your concerns as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快回应您的担忧。
They will respond to the complaint by next week. = 他们将在下周回复投诉。


虚拟语气:
If he responded honestly, it would be better. = 如果他诚实地回应，那就更好了。
I wish they had responded to my request sooner. = 我希望他们早点回应我的请求。


被动语态:
Customer inquiries are responded to promptly. = 客户询问得到及时回复。
The proposal was responded to with enthusiasm. = 该提案受到了热情的回应。


疑问句:
How did he respond to the news? = 他对这个消息作何反应？
Has she responded to your letter yet? = 她回复你的信了吗？


否定句:
He didn't respond to my questions. = 他没有回答我的问题。
The company hasn't responded to the complaint. = 公司尚未回应投诉。


PHRASE->
in correspondence with = 与…通信
enter into correspondence = 开始通信
a flurry of correspondence = 一连串的信件往来
open a correspondence = 开始通信
maintain correspondence = 保持联系
