distribute:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/| vt. |分发，分配，散布|to divide and share (something) among several people or places.|The teacher distributed the papers to the students.老师把试卷分发给学生们。|allocate, dispense, share|concentrate, gather, collect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company distributes its products worldwide.  = 这家公司在全球范围内分销其产品。
She distributes flyers in the neighborhood. = 她在家附近分发传单。

一般过去时:
They distributed food and water to the victims. = 他们向受害者分发了食物和水。
He distributed the workload evenly among the team members. = 他将工作量平均分配给团队成员。

现在完成时:
We have distributed all the invitations. = 我们已经分发了所有的请柬。
The company has distributed its profits to shareholders. = 公司已将利润分配给股东。

过去完成时:
They had distributed the questionnaires before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经分发了问卷。
She had distributed the gifts before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经分发了礼物。

将来时:
We will distribute the new software next week. = 我们下周将发布新软件。
The company will distribute its products to new markets. = 公司将把产品分销到新的市场。

虚拟语气:
If the company distributed more effectively, they would increase sales. = 如果公司更有效率地分销产品，他们的销售额就会增加。
It would be better if we distributed the tasks more fairly. = 如果我们更公平地分配任务，那就更好了。

被动语态:
The leaflets were distributed throughout the city. = 传单在全城分发。
The profits will be distributed among the employees. = 利润将分配给员工。

疑问句:
How will the resources be distributed? = 资源将如何分配？
Who distributed the information? = 谁分发了这些信息？

否定句:
They did not distribute the funds fairly. = 他们没有公平地分配资金。
The company hasn't yet distributed the bonuses. = 公司尚未发放奖金。


PHRASE->
distribute evenly = 平均分配
distribute widely = 广泛分发
distribute among = 在...之间分配
distribute throughout = 在...各地分发
distribute to = 分发给


词源:
来自古法语 *distribuer，来自拉丁语 distribuere，由 dis- (“apart”) + tribuere (“to give, allot”)构成。
