resided:/rɪˈzaɪdɪd/| v.|居住，定居|To live in a particular place.|例句：The family resided in a small village.  这个家庭居住在一个小村庄里。|例句：They resided at the same address for over twenty years. 他们在这个地址住了二十多年。|近义词：lived, dwelt, inhabited|反义词：departed, left


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resides in London. = 他住在伦敦。
Where do they reside? = 他们住在哪里？

一般过去时:
She resided in Paris for five years. = 她曾在巴黎住了五年。
We resided in that house before moving to the suburbs. = 我们搬到郊区之前住在那个房子里。

现在完成时:
They have resided in this city since 2000. = 他们自2000年以来一直居住在这个城市。
How long have you resided at your current address? = 你住在你现在的地址多久了？

过去完成时:
He had resided in New York before he moved to California. = 他搬到加利福尼亚之前住在纽约。
She had resided there for many years before she decided to move. = 她在那里住了很多年才决定搬家。

将来时:
They will reside in a new apartment next year. = 他们明年将住在新公寓里。
Where will you reside after graduation? = 毕业后你将住在哪里？

虚拟语气:
If I resided in a warmer climate, I would be much happier. = 如果我住在气候更温暖的地方，我会更快乐。
I wish I resided closer to my family. = 我希望我住得离我的家人更近。

被动语态:
This property has been resided in for generations. = 这处房产已经住了几代人了。 (被动语态在此处用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Do you reside in this building? = 你住在这栋楼里吗？
Where does he reside? = 他住在哪里？


否定句:
They do not reside in the same city. = 他们不住在同一个城市。
He did not reside there for long. = 他没在那里住多久。


PHRASE->
resided in = 住在...
resided at = 住在（某个地址）
resided with = 和...同住
resided permanently = 永久居住
resided temporarily = 临时居住
