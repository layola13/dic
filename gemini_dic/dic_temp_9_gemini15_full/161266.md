postmodernisms:/ˌpoʊstˈmɒdərnɪzəm/| n.| 后现代主义 | Postmodernism is a late-20th-century movement in the arts, architecture, and criticism that was a departure from modernism.  It is characterized by skepticism, irony, and the rejection of grand narratives and objective truth.  Postmodern works often incorporate elements of pastiche, parody, and intertextuality. | Example: The building's design is a striking example of postmodernism, blending classical and futuristic elements.  这座建筑的设计是后现代主义的突出例子，融合了古典和未来主义元素。|近义词：postmodernity, poststructuralism |反义词：modernism, classicism
