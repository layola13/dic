meiji:/ˈmeɪdʒiː/| n.|明治(时代)|The Meiji period (明治時代, Meiji jidai) was a period in Japanese history from 1868 to 1912.  It was characterized by significant political, economic, and social reforms that transformed Japan into a modern, industrialized nation.|例句：The Meiji Restoration marked a turning point in Japanese history. 明治维新标志着日本历史的转折点。|近义词：Meiji era, Meiji restoration (in the context of the restoration itself)|反义词：无|名词
