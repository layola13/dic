inhabits:/ɪnˈhæbɪts/| v.|居住，栖息|To live in or occupy a place or environment.|例句：Many species of birds inhabit this forest.  许多鸟类栖息在这片森林里。|例句：A large family inhabits that old house.  一个大家庭居住在那栋老房子里。|近义词：dwells, occupies, resides|反义词：vacates, leaves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The family inhabits a small cottage. = 这个家庭居住在一个小别墅里。
Many birds inhabit the rainforest. = 许多鸟类栖息在雨林中。

一般过去时:
The nomads inhabited the desert for centuries. = 游牧民族几个世纪以来居住在沙漠中。
Dinosaurs inhabited the Earth millions of years ago. = 恐龙几百万年前居住在地球上。

现在完成时:
They have inhabited this island for generations. = 他们世世代代居住在这个岛屿上。
The species has inhabited this area since the Ice Age. = 这个物种自冰河时代以来就一直栖息在这个地区。

过去完成时:
Before the flood, the village had inhabited the riverbank for 500 years. = 在洪水泛滥之前，村庄在河岸居住500年。
The settlers had inhabited the land before the war broke out. = 定居者在战争爆发前就已居住在这片土地上。

将来时:
They will inhabit the new apartment building next month. = 他们下个月将入住新的公寓楼。
The birds will inhabit the newly planted trees in the spring. = 春天，鸟儿们将栖息在新种植的树木上。

虚拟语气:
If they inhabited a larger house, they would have more space. = 如果他们住在一所更大的房子里，他们就会有更多空间。
I wish I inhabited a tropical island. = 我真希望我住在一个热带岛屿上。

被动语态:
The cave was inhabited by a family of bears. = 山洞里住着一家人熊。
The area is inhabited by various species of wildlife. = 这个地区栖息着各种野生动物。

疑问句:
Does anyone inhabit that abandoned building? = 有人居住在那座废弃的建筑物里吗？
What kinds of animals inhabit the Arctic? = 北极栖息着哪些动物？

否定句:
No one inhabits this remote island. = 没有人居住在这个偏远的岛屿上。
That building is not inhabited anymore. = 那栋建筑物不再有人居住了。
