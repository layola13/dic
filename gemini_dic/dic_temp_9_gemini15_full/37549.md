It refers to the ability to make sound judgments based on experience and common knowledge.|He showed a surprising lack of commonsense in handling the situation. 他在处理这件事时，表现出令人惊讶的缺乏常识。|She used her commonsense to solve the problem. 她用常识解决了这个问题。|wisdom, good judgment, practicality|folly, foolishness, unreasonableness|名词
