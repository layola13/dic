carpophore:/ˈkɑːrpəfɔːr/|/ˈkɑːrpəˌfɔːr/| n. |果柄；花托|The carpophore is the stalk that supports the fruit or gynoecium in some plants, often acting as an extension of the receptacle.  It's a specialized structure that helps with fruit dispersal and development.|Example: The umbel of the plant had a prominent carpophore elevating the fruits.|近义词: fruit stalk, receptacle stalk|反义词: N/A
