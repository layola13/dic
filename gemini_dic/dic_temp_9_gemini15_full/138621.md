nuclei:/ˈnuːkleɪaɪ/| n. |细胞核；核；原子核|The plural of nucleus.  Nuclei are the central and most important part of a biological cell, containing the genetic material. In physics, nuclei are the central part of an atom, containing protons and neutrons.|例句：The nuclei of the cells were damaged by radiation. 细胞核被辐射破坏了。|The cell's nucleus contains its DNA. 细胞核包含它的DNA。|近义词：core, center|反义词：periphery, exterior|名词
