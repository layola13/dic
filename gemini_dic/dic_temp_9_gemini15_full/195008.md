semiactiveness:/ˌsɛmiˈæktɪvnəs/| n. |半主动性，部分主动性 |The state of being partly active; a condition that is neither fully active nor fully passive.  It often describes systems or processes that exhibit a blend of automatic and manual control. |例句：The system's semiactiveness allows for both automated responses and manual overrides.  该系统的半主动性允许自动响应和手动覆盖。

The software's semiactiveness means it only updates when triggered, conserving resources. 该软件的半主动性意味着它只在被触发时更新，从而节省资源。|近义词：partial activity, conditional activity |反义词：passiveness, inactivity, fully active
