mahatmas:/məˈhɑːtmɑːz/ | n. |圣雄，伟人，尊者 |  A mahatma is a person who is revered for their spiritual wisdom and influence.  The term is often associated with Mahatma Gandhi. |例句：Mahatma Gandhi was a mahatma who inspired millions with his philosophy of nonviolent resistance.  圣雄甘地是一位以其非暴力抵抗哲学激励了数百万人的伟人。|近义词：sage, saint, guru, leader |反义词：villain, tyrant, evil-doer
