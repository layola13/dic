detests:/dɪˈtɛsts/| vt.|痛恨，厌恶|To dislike intensely; to hate.|She detests liars and cheats. 她痛恨说谎者和骗子。 He detests the smell of fish. 他讨厌鱼腥味。|近义词：abhor, loathe, despise|反义词：adore, love


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He detests loud music. 他讨厌吵闹的音乐。
She detests being interrupted. 她讨厌被打扰。


一般过去时:
They detested the movie. 他们讨厌这部电影。
He detested his job. 他讨厌他的工作。


现在完成时:
I have detested spinach my whole life. 我一辈子都讨厌菠菜。
She has detested him since childhood. 她从小就讨厌他。


过去完成时:
He had detested her even before they met.  他甚至在他们见面之前就讨厌她了。
They had detested the situation for years. 他们多年来一直讨厌这种情况。


将来时:
I will detest this task forever. 我将永远讨厌这项任务。
She will likely detest the new rules. 她很可能会讨厌新的规定。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't detest public speaking so much. 我真希望我不那么讨厌公开演讲。
If I detested her less, I would be more willing to cooperate. 如果我不那么讨厌她，我会更愿意合作。


被动语态:
Loud noises are detested by many people. 许多人讨厌大声的噪音。(很少用被动语态)
His behaviour was detested by everyone.  他的行为遭到所有人的厌恶。(很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does he detest broccoli? 他讨厌西兰花吗？
Do you detest the cold weather? 你讨厌寒冷的天气吗？


否定句:
I don't detest all vegetables. 我并不是讨厌所有的蔬菜。
She doesn't detest cats, but she's not fond of them either. 她不讨厌猫，但她也不喜欢猫。


PHRASE->
detest doing something: 讨厌做某事
detest the idea of: 讨厌...的想法
detest someone intensely: 强烈地厌恶某人
detest with a passion:  充满激情地厌恶
detest the thought of: 讨厌想到...
