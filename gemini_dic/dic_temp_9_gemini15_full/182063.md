remercie:/ʁəmɛʁsjɛ/ | v. |感谢；道谢 |To express gratitude or thanks.  It is typically used in French. |例句：Je vous remercie de votre aide. = Thank you for your help.  |近义词：thank, acknowledge, appreciate |反义词：blame, criticize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Je te remercie. = I thank you.
Nous vous remercions. = We thank you.


一般过去时:
Je vous ai remercié. = I thanked you.
Ils m'ont remercié. = They thanked me.


现在完成时:
Je l'ai remercié. = I have thanked him/her.
Nous les avons remerciés. = We have thanked them.


过去完成时:
J'avais remercié mon professeur. = I had thanked my professor.
Elles avaient remercié leurs parents. = They had thanked their parents.


将来时:
Je te remercierai. = I will thank you.
Nous vous remercierons. = We will thank you.


虚拟语气:
Je vous remercierais si vous m'aidiez. = I would thank you if you helped me.
Nous vous remercierions de votre coopération. = We would thank you for your cooperation.


被动语态:
Il a été remercié pour son travail. = He was thanked for his work.
Elles ont été remerciées de leur aide. = They were thanked for their help.


疑问句:
Me remercierez-vous? = Will you thank me?
Vous remercieront-ils? = Will they thank you?


否定句:
Je ne vous remercie pas. = I don't thank you. (This is less common;  "Merci" is typically used instead)
Ils ne m'ont pas remercié. = They didn't thank me.


PHRASE->
Je vous remercie infiniment. = Thank you very much.
Je vous remercie de votre attention. = Thank you for your attention.
Je vous remercie pour votre aide. = Thank you for your help.
Merci beaucoup. = Thank you very much. (This is a more common and informal phrase)
Je vous remercie sincèrement. = I sincerely thank you.
