rheumatic:/ruːˈmætɪk/| adj.|风湿性的，患风湿病的 | Relating to or characteristic of rheumatism.  The word often describes conditions involving inflammation of the joints and connective tissues. |例句：She suffers from rheumatic pain in her joints. = 她患有关节风湿痛。

例句：He has rheumatic fever. = 他患有风湿热。


近义词：arthritic, inflammatory |反义词：healthy, sound


词源:  From Late Latin *rheumaticus, from Greek rheuma ("a flow, discharge, current"), from rhein ("to flow").  The original sense referred to a flow or discharge of humors believed to cause the disease.
