nondevotional: /ˌnɒnˌdɪˈvoʊʃənəl/ | adj. | 非宗教的，世俗的 | Not relating to or connected with religious worship or devotion.  It describes something that is not dedicated to or characterized by religious piety or practice.  | 例句：The museum's collection included both devotional and nondevotional art.  博物馆的藏品包括宗教和非宗教艺术。

The concert featured a mix of devotional and nondevotional music. 音乐会既有宗教音乐，也有世俗音乐。


近义词：secular, worldly, profane, nonreligious | 反义词：religious, devotional, sacred


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".
* **devotional**:  Relating to or characterized by devotion, especially religious devotion.  This word derives from "devotion," ultimately from the Old French "devotion" and Latin "devotio," meaning "a vow, dedication."


用法说明:

This word is primarily used as an adjective to modify nouns. It is commonly used in contexts where a distinction is made between activities, practices, or art forms that have a religious purpose and those that do not.  It can be applied to a wide range of things, from music and art to literature and architecture.  The emphasis is on the lack of religious intent or association.

需要注意的是，"nondevotional"这个词本身并没有语法上的特殊变化，因为它是一个形容词。其用法与其他形容词相同，可以修饰名词，构成名词短语。
