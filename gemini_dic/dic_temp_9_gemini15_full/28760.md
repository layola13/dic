carnival:/ˈkɑːrnɪvəl/| n. |狂欢节，嘉年华，节日游行|A period of revelry, typically associated with Shrove Tuesday (Mardi Gras) and involving public festivities, parades, and merrymaking.  It can also refer to a traveling amusement park with rides and games.|例句：The Rio Carnival is famous worldwide.  里约热内卢狂欢节闻名世界。

例句：The children enjoyed the carnival rides and games. 孩子们喜欢嘉年华的游乐设施和游戏。


近义词：festival, celebration, fete, fair


反义词：None directly, but antonyms could be applied to the opposite of the revelry aspect, such as solemnity, austerity.


词性:名词
