saddlebow:/ˈsædlˌboʊ/| n. |鞍弓|The curved part of a saddle that forms the pommel and cantle.  It provides support and stability for the rider.  The saddlebow is typically made of wood or metal and is covered in leather.|例句：The saddlebow was beautifully carved and inlaid with silver. 鞍弓雕刻精美，并镶嵌着银子。


The worn saddlebow creaked under his weight. 磨损的鞍弓在他体重下发出嘎吱声。|近义词：pommel, cantle (parts of the saddlebow)|反义词：无|词性:名词
