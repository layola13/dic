ligamentous:/ˈlɪɡəməntəs/| adj. |韧带的；像韧带的| Relating to or of the nature of a ligament.  A ligament is a tough band of fibrous tissue connecting bones or cartilages.  The term can also describe something resembling a ligament in its strength and flexibility.|The ligamentous structure of the knee joint provides stability.|近义词：fibrous, tough, strong|反义词：weak, fragile
