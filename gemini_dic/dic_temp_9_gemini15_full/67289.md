exserted:/ɪɡˈzɜːrtɪd/| v. |用力的；尽力的；施加（压力等）|To make a great effort; to exert oneself.  To apply force; to exert pressure. |例句：He exerted all his strength to lift the heavy box. 他使出全身力气搬动那个沉重的箱子。


例句：The government exerted pressure on the company to change its policies. 政府向该公司施压，要求其改变政策。


近义词：applied, employed, strained, put forth


反义词：relaxed, neglected, ignored


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exerts himself in his studies. = 他在学习上很努力。
She exerts considerable influence over her employees. = 她对她的员工有相当大的影响力。


一般过去时:
He exerted all his energy to finish the project. = 他用尽全力完成项目。
The pressure exerted on the gas caused it to expand. = 对气体的压力导致它膨胀。


现在完成时:
She has exerted herself to achieve her goals. = 她为了实现目标而努力奋斗。
The company has exerted considerable effort in marketing. = 公司在市场营销方面付出了巨大的努力。


过去完成时:
He had exerted himself all day and was exhausted. = 他整天都在努力，筋疲力尽了。
The team had exerted great pressure to meet the deadline. = 团队为了赶上截止日期施加了巨大的压力。


将来时:
They will exert their influence to support the candidate. = 他们将运用他们的影响力来支持候选人。
The new policy will exert a significant impact on the economy. = 新政策将对经济产生重大影响。


虚拟语气:
If he exerted himself more, he would succeed. = 如果他更加努力，他就会成功。
I wish I had exerted more effort in my studies. = 我真希望我在学习上更加努力。


被动语态:
Pressure was exerted on the government to reform. = 政府承受着改革的压力。
Great effort was exerted to save the endangered species. = 为了拯救濒危物种付出了巨大的努力。


疑问句:
How much effort will be exerted in this project? = 这个项目将投入多少精力？
What pressure is being exerted on the witness? = 目击者承受着什么压力？


否定句:
He didn't exert himself enough to win the race. = 他没有付出足够的努力赢得比赛。
The government did not exert sufficient control over the situation. = 政府没有对局势进行充分的控制。



PHRASE->
exert influence =施加影响
exert pressure =施加压力
exert oneself =努力，尽力
exert control =控制，支配
exert power =运用权力

词源:
源自拉丁语“exertus”，是“ex-”（向外）和“tendere”（伸展，拉伸）的组合。  最初的意思是“伸出，伸展”，后来引申为“运用（力量、能力等）”。
