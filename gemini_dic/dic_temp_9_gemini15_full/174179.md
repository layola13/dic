queenslander:/ˈkwiːnzlændər/| n. |昆士兰人；昆士兰式房屋|A Queenslander is a person who lives in Queensland, Australia, or a type of house traditionally built in Queensland.  The houses are characterized by their elevated position on stumps, wide verandas, and timber construction. |例句：Many Queenslanders enjoy the state's beautiful beaches. 许多昆士兰人都喜欢该州美丽的沙滩。|例句：Their new home is a beautiful Queenslander with a wrap-around veranda. 他们的新家是一栋带有环绕式阳台的漂亮昆士兰式房屋。|近义词：Queenslander (referring to the person) might be replaced with "resident of Queensland" or "Queenslander" (referring to the house style) has no perfect synonym but could be described as a "raised house" or a "timber house with a veranda".|反义词：无|名词
