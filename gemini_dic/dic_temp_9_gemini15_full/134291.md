noninherited: /ˌnɒnɪnˈherɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非遗传的，非继承的 | Not inherited or passed down genetically or through inheritance.  It refers to traits, characteristics, or properties that are not acquired through genetic transmission from parents to offspring or through legal inheritance. |

例句：

The researcher found that the disease was noninherited, caused instead by environmental factors.  研究人员发现这种疾病是非遗传性的，而是由环境因素引起的。

His wealth was entirely self-made; it was noninherited. 他的财富完全是白手起家的；它不是继承来的。


近义词：acquired, nongenetic, nonhereditary

反义词：inherited, hereditary, genetic


词性：形容词
