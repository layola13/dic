retelling:/ˌriːˈtɛlɪŋ/| n.|复述；改编本；讲述|The act of telling a story again, often with changes or additions.  It can refer to a new version of a story, or simply recounting an event. |例句：Her retelling of the incident differed significantly from the police report.  她的事件叙述与警方的报告大相径庭。

例句：The film is a loose retelling of a classic fairy tale. 这部电影是对经典童话故事的松散改编。

近义词：recount, narration, version, adaptation

反义词：original (in the sense of the first telling)
