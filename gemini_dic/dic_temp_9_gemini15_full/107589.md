landscape:/ˈlændskeɪp/| n.|风景，景色；地貌；风景画|A view or picture of a natural scene, especially one showing the countryside.  It can also refer to the overall appearance of a place, or a particular type of scenery.  Additionally, it can be used metaphorically.|例句：The landscape was breathtaking, with rolling hills and sparkling rivers.  这片风景美得令人惊叹，有连绵起伏的丘陵和波光粼粼的河流。


The artist painted a beautiful landscape of the Scottish Highlands.  这位艺术家画了一幅美丽的苏格兰高地风景画。|scenery, vista, view|urban, cityscape|名词
