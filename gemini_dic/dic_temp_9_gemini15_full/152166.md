/ˈpɑːtrə/| n. | पत्र (印地语)| leaf, letter, document, writing,  script; (in some contexts) a sacred text, a scroll, or a record.  The word has diverse meanings depending on the context, often relating to written or printed material.  It derives from Sanskrit and is used in various South Asian languages.|例句：The ancient patra contained prophecies of the future.  古代的帕特拉包含着对未来的预言。|例句：He received a patra from the king. 他收到了国王的一封信。|近义词：document, letter, scroll, manuscript|反义词：orality (as an opposing concept), blank page|名词
