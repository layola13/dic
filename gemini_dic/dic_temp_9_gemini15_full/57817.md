drencher:/ˈdrɛntʃər/| n. | 湿透的人或物；大雨；浸湿的东西 | A drencher is something that makes something or someone very wet, or the state of being very wet.  It can refer to a heavy rainfall, or to something that soaks something else. |例句：The sudden downpour was a real drencher. 突如其来的倾盆大雨真是让人湿透了。
The thunderstorm was a complete drencher.  这场雷雨把一切都淋透了。|近义词：soaker, downpour, soaking |反义词：drought, dryness
