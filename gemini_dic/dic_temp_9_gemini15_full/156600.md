picnicked:/ˈpɪkˌnɪkt/| v.|野餐；进行野餐|To have a picnic.  This word describes the act of having a picnic.|例句：We picnicked by the lake. 我们在湖边野餐。
例句：They picnicked in the park after the game. 比赛结束后他们在公园里野餐。|近义词：had a picnic, dined outdoors|反义词：N/A|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We picnic in the park every Sunday. = 我们每周日都在公园野餐。
They picnic by the river during summer. = 他们在夏天在河边野餐。

一般过去时:
We picnicked by the lake yesterday. = 我们昨天在湖边野餐了。
They picnicked in the woods last weekend. = 他们上周末在树林里野餐了。

现在完成时:
We have picnicked here many times. = 我们在这里野餐过很多次了。
They have picnicked at that beach before. = 他们以前在那片海滩野餐过。

过去完成时:
We had picnicked before the rain started. = 下雨之前我们已经野餐过了。
They had picnicked several times that month. = 他们那个月已经野餐过好几次了。

将来时:
We will picnic on the mountain tomorrow. = 我们明天将在山上野餐。
They will picnic by the sea next week. = 他们下周将在海边野餐。

虚拟语气:
If the weather was nice, we would picnic. = 如果天气好的话，我们会去野餐。
I wish we had picnicked earlier. = 我真希望我们早点野餐。

疑问句:
