gutless:/ˈɡʌtlɪs/| adj.|懦弱的；胆小的；无勇气的|Lacking courage or determination; cowardly.|He made a gutless decision to avoid conflict. 他做了一个懦弱的决定以避免冲突。|近义词：cowardly, craven, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, bold|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gutless in the face of danger. 他面对危险时很懦弱。
She seems gutless when it comes to standing up for herself.  说到为自己挺身而出，她似乎很胆小。


一般过去时:
He acted gutlessly during the crisis.  他在危机期间表现得很懦弱。
She was gutless in confronting her boss about the unfair treatment. 她面对老板的不公平待遇时很胆小。


现在完成时:
He has been gutless throughout this whole ordeal. 在整个磨难中，他一直很懦弱。
She has shown herself to be gutless time and again. 她一次又一次地表现出自己的胆小。


过去完成时:
He had been gutless for so long that no one expected him to act bravely. 他懦弱了这么久，以至于没有人期望他勇敢地行动。
She had been gutless in the past, but this time she surprised everyone. 她过去很胆小，但这次她让每个人都大吃一惊。


将来时:
He will be gutless and fail to defend his position. 他将会懦弱，无法捍卫自己的立场。
She will likely be gutless and not speak up about the injustice. 她很可能会懦弱，不敢说出不公正的事情。


虚拟语气:
If he were not gutless, he would have stood up for his beliefs. 如果他不懦弱，他会为自己的信仰挺身而出。
If she had not been gutless, she would have reported the incident. 如果她不懦弱，她就会报告这件事。


被动语态:
His actions were seen as gutless by many. 许多人认为他的行为很懦弱。
Her decision was considered gutless by her colleagues. 她的决定被她的同事们认为是懦弱的。


疑问句:
Is he truly gutless, or just afraid? 他真的是懦弱，还是仅仅害怕？
Was her response gutless, or simply cautious? 她的回应是懦弱，还是仅仅谨慎？


否定句:
He was not gutless; he simply chose a different approach. 他并不懦弱；他只是选择了一种不同的方法。
Her actions were not gutless; they were strategic. 她的行为并非懦弱；而是策略性的。
