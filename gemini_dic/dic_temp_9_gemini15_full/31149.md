chander:/ˈtʃændər/| n.|杂货店；小商店；(尤指)旧货店 | A chandler is a shop that sells a variety of goods, especially groceries and household supplies, often in a small or informal setting.  It often implies a slightly old-fashioned or quaint establishment.  The term is also used to refer to a supplier of goods to ships. | 例句：The village chandler stocked everything from tinned goods to fishing nets.  村里的杂货店什么都卖，从罐头食品到渔网。

The old chandler by the harbor was a treasure trove of nautical supplies.  港口旁的老杂货店是航海用品的宝藏。| 近义词：shop, store, general store, purveyor | 反义词：None specifically.
