mucked:/mʌkt/| v.|弄脏，弄乱，搞砸|To make something dirty or messy; to spoil or ruin something.|例句：The kids mucked up the living room. 孩子们把客厅弄乱了。例句：He mucked up the job interview. 他把求职面试搞砸了。|近义词：mess up, dirty, spoil, ruin|反义词：clean, tidy, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mucks up his room every day. = 他每天都把他的房间弄乱。
They muck about in the garden. = 他们在花园里胡闹。


一般过去时:
I mucked up my presentation. = 我把我的演示文稿搞砸了。
She mucked the car. = 她把车弄脏了。


现在完成时:
He has mucked up his chances of promotion. = 他已经搞砸了他晋升的机会。
They have mucked the kitchen. = 他们把厨房弄脏了。


过去完成时:
I had mucked up the recipe before I realized it. = 我在意识到之前已经把菜谱搞砸了。
She had mucked her dress before the party. = 派对前她把她的裙子弄脏了。


将来时:
He will muck up the project if he's not careful. = 如果他不小心，他会把项目搞砸。
They will muck the floor later. = 他们稍后会把地板弄脏。


虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he wouldn't have mucked up the exam. = 如果他更细心一点，他就不会把考试搞砸了。
I wish I hadn't mucked up the interview. = 我真希望我没有把面试搞砸。


被动语态:
The garden was mucked up by the storm. = 花园被暴风雨弄乱了。
The car has been mucked by the mud. = 车子被泥巴弄脏了。


疑问句:
Did you muck up the assignment? = 你把作业搞砸了吗？
Has he mucked the carpet? = 他把地毯弄脏了吗？


否定句:
He didn't muck up the experiment. = 他没有把实验搞砸。
She didn't muck her shoes. = 她没有弄脏她的鞋子。


PHRASE->
muck about/around: 胡闹，浪费时间
muck in: 帮忙，参与
muck out: 清理（马厩等）
muck up: 弄糟，搞砸
muck and brass: 粗俗的，下流的
