choreographs:/kɔːriˈɑːɡræfs/| vt.|编排（舞蹈、戏剧等）|To plan the movements, steps, and arrangement of a dance, play, or other performance.|The choreographer meticulously choreographed the ballet's final scene.  编舞仔细地编排了芭蕾舞剧的最后场景。|plan, arrange, devise|disorganize, improvise|动词
