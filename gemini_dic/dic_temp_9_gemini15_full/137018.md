nonstaining: /ˌnɒnˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不染色；不沾污的 | Not causing stains or discoloration.  The term describes materials or substances that do not leave marks or blemishes on surfaces. | 例句：The nonstaining sealant protected the countertop from coffee spills.  不沾污的密封剂保护了台面免受咖啡溢出的影响。  The artist used nonstaining paints to create a delicate watercolor.艺术家使用不染色颜料创作了一幅精美的水彩画。 | 近义词：stain-resistant, colorfast, unblemished | 反义词：staining, staining, soiling
