cleanability:/ˌkliːnəˈbɪləti/| n. |易清洁性，可清洁度|The ease with which something can be cleaned.  It refers to the material's resistance to staining, its ability to be easily wiped or washed, and the effectiveness of cleaning agents on it.|例句：The cleanability of the surface is a major selling point. 表面的易清洁性是一个主要的卖点。

The high cleanability of stainless steel makes it ideal for kitchens. 不锈钢的高清洁度使其成为厨房的理想选择。|近义词：cleanliness, washability |反义词: difficulty to clean, unhygienic
