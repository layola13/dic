drifted:/ˈdrɪftɪd/ | v. |漂流；漂移；逐渐变化；漫无目的地闲逛 | to be carried along by a current of air or water; to move or change gradually; to wander aimlessly |例句：The boat drifted aimlessly out to sea.  船漫无目的地漂流到海上。

The conversation drifted to the topic of politics.  谈话逐渐转向政治话题。


近义词：wandered, floated, drifted off, meandered | 反义词：stayed, remained, anchored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaves drift on the wind. = 叶子随风飘动。
He drifts between jobs. = 他在不同的工作之间漂泊。


一般过去时:
The raft drifted downstream. = 木筏顺流而下。
She drifted off to sleep. = 她慢慢睡着了。


现在完成时:
The boat has drifted miles from the shore. = 船已漂离海岸数英里。
My thoughts have drifted to the past. = 我的思绪飘回了过去。


过去完成时:
The ship had drifted for days before being rescued. = 这艘船漂流了好几天才获救。
We had drifted apart over the years. = 这些年来，我们疏远了。


将来时:
The ice floes will drift southwards. = 浮冰将向南漂移。
He will drift into a state of unconsciousness. = 他将陷入昏迷状态。


虚拟语气:
If the boat had not drifted, we would have reached the island. = 如果船没有漂流，我们就能到达岛屿了。
I wish I hadn't drifted so far from my goals. = 我真希望自己没有偏离目标那么远。


被动语态:
The boat was drifted by the strong current. = 船被强劲的水流冲走了。(被动语态少用，因为drift本身就包含被动含义)
The balloon was drifted across the sky. = 气球飘过天空。(被动语态少用，因为drift本身就包含被动含义)


疑问句:
Where did the boat drift to? = 船漂到哪里去了？
Where are we drifting now? = 我们现在漂向哪里？


否定句:
The boat didn't drift far from the shore. = 船没有漂离海岸太远。
He didn't drift into a depression. = 他并没有陷入抑郁。


PHRASE->
drift apart = 疏远
drift off to sleep = 慢慢睡着
drift into (something) = 逐渐陷入某种状态
drift snow = 积雪
driftwood = 浮木

词源:
Old English *drifen, from Proto-Germanic *driban ("to drive, push").  The sense of "to be driven by wind or water" developed from the original meaning of "to drive".
