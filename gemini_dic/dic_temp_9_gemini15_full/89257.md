hominini:/ˌhɒmɪˈnaɪni/| n. |人族|The hominini are a tribe of primates that includes modern humans and extinct human ancestors.  This tribe is characterized by bipedalism, large brains, and the use of tools.  The study of hominini helps us understand human evolution and our place in the primate family.  |Example:  The discovery of new hominin fossils in Africa is constantly reshaping our understanding of human evolution.  非洲发现新的原始人化石不断重塑我们对人类进化的理解。  |近义词:hominid (broader term)|反义词:none|名词
