remarked:/rɪˈmɑːrkt/| vt.| 评论；谈论；说起；注意到 |to say something as a comment or observation; to notice or observe something|例句：He remarked that the weather was fine. 他评论说天气很好。

例句：I remarked on her beautiful dress. 我注意到她美丽的裙子。


近义词：observed, noted, commented, mentioned


反义词：ignored, overlooked


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remarks that the meeting is important. = 他评论说会议很重要。
She remarks on the beauty of the sunset every evening. = 她每天晚上都评论日落的美丽。


一般过去时:
He remarked that he was tired. = 他说他累了。
She remarked on the delicious food. = 她评论了美味的食物。


现在完成时:
He has remarked several times on the poor service.  = 他已经多次评论了糟糕的服务。
She has remarked that she is feeling better. = 她已经说过她感觉好多了。


过去完成时:
He had remarked that he felt unwell before he left. = 他离开前说过他感觉不舒服。
She had remarked on the strange noise earlier. = 她早些时候已经注意到奇怪的声音了。


将来时:
He will remark on the project's progress tomorrow. = 他明天将评论项目的进展。
She will remark on the quality of the product later. = 她稍后会评论产品的质量。


虚拟语气:
If he had remarked sooner, we could have avoided the problem. = 如果他早点评论，我们就可以避免这个问题了。
I wish she had remarked about the potential danger. = 我希望她能提到潜在的危险。


被动语态:
His intelligence was remarked upon by everyone. = 他的聪明才智受到了所有人的称赞。
The beauty of the scenery was remarked on in the travel guide. = 旅行指南中提到了风景的美丽。


疑问句:
Did he remark anything about the incident? = 他对这件事有什么评论吗？
What did she remark about the movie? = 她对这部电影有什么评价？


否定句:
He didn't remark on the matter. = 他没有评论这件事。
She didn't remark anything about the new policy. = 她对这项新政策没有任何评论。


PHRASE->
remark on/upon sth: 对某事发表评论
remarkable: 显著的，非凡的
make a remark:  发表评论
pass a remark:  顺便提一句
without remark:  默默地，不作评价
