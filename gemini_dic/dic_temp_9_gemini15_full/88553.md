hitched:/hɪtʃt/| v. adj.|系上；结婚；搭便车|To fasten or connect something; to get married; to get a ride in a vehicle|例句：He hitched the horse to the cart. 他把马系在车上。  He hitched a ride to the city. 他搭便车去了城市。|近义词：fastened, married, connected|反义词：unhitched, unmarried, disconnected|动词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hitches his horse to the fence every day. = 他每天把马拴在栅栏上。
She hitches her skirt up when she walks through the mud. = 她穿过泥地时会把裙子撩起来。


一般过去时:
They hitched their wagon to a star. = 他们把他们的梦想寄托在了一个目标上。
We hitched a ride from a friendly stranger. = 我们搭了一个友善陌生人的便车。


现在完成时:
She has hitched her hopes to this project. = 她把希望寄托在这个项目上了。
They have hitched their fortunes to the rising star of the company. = 他们把自己的命运与公司冉冉升起的新星联系在一起了。


过去完成时:
He had hitched his horse before the storm broke. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他已经把马拴好了。
Before the wedding, they had hitched their lives together. = 婚礼之前，他们已经把生活联系在一起了。


将来时:
We will hitch our tent to a sturdy tree. = 我们将把帐篷系在一棵结实的树上。
They will hitch a ride to the airport tomorrow. = 他们明天将搭车去机场。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would hitch a trailer to it. = 如果我有一辆车，我会在上面挂一个拖车。
If she hadn't hitched her career to that company, she would be richer now. = 如果她没有把自己的事业与那家公司联系在一起，她现在会更富有。


被动语态:
The trailer was hitched to the truck. = 拖车被系在卡车上。
The horse was hitched to the post. = 马被拴在了柱子上。


疑问句:
Have you ever hitched a ride before? = 你以前搭过便车吗？
Is the boat hitched to the dock securely? = 船牢固地系在码头上吗？


否定句:
He didn't hitch the boat properly. = 他没有把船系好。
She didn't hitch her hopes on a single project. = 她没有把希望寄托在一个项目上。
