outcut:/aʊtˈkʌt/| vt.|胜过，超过，裁剪|To surpass or exceed in cutting or trimming something.|The experienced tailor outcut the apprentice in speed and precision.|近义词：outshine, surpass, excel|反义词：undercut


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tailor outcuts the apprentice in every task. = 裁缝在每项任务中都胜过学徒。
Our company outcuts its competitors in terms of efficiency. = 我们公司在效率方面胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year, our team outcut the opponents in the debate. = 去年，我们的团队在辩论中胜过了对手。
She outcut the others in the wood carving competition. = 木雕比赛中，她技压群芳。


现在完成时:
He has outcut all his rivals in this field. = 在这个领域，他已经胜过了所有竞争对手。
The new software has outcut the old one in performance. = 新软件在性能方面已经超越了旧软件。


过去完成时:
By the time the competition ended, she had outcut everyone. = 比赛结束时，她已经胜过所有人了。
They had outcut their rivals in the market before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经在市场上胜过了竞争对手。


将来时:
We believe our new design will outcut the existing products. = 我们相信我们的新设计将超越现有产品。
The new machine is expected to outcut the old one in production speed. = 预计新机器的生产速度将超过旧机器。


虚拟语气:
If we had better tools, we could outcut them easily. = 如果我们有更好的工具，我们就能轻松地胜过他们。
I wish I had outcut my opponent in the last game. = 我真希望在上一场比赛中能胜过我的对手。


被动语态:
The old design was outcut by the new one. = 旧设计被新设计超越了。
The rival team was outcut by our superior strategy. = 竞争对手团队被我们更胜一筹的策略超越了。


疑问句:
Can our team outcut the opposing team? = 我们的团队能胜过对方团队吗？
Does this design outcut the previous version? = 这个设计比之前的版本好吗？


否定句:
Our efforts did not outcut the challenges we faced. = 我们的努力并没有克服我们面临的挑战。(outcut在此处指克服，而非单纯的胜过)
The company failed to outcut its rivals in the market. = 公司未能超越其在市场上的竞争对手。


PHRASE->
outcut the competition = 超越竞争对手
outcut the market = 占据市场领先地位
outcut rivals = 超越竞争对手
outcut in efficiency = 在效率上胜过
outcut in quality = 在质量上胜过
