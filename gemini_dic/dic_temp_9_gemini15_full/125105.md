mulls:/mʌlz/| v.|仔细考虑，沉思|To think about something carefully and for a long time.  |例句：He mulled over the problem for days before finding a solution. 他考虑这个问题好几天才找到解决方法。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, consider, meditate |反义词：decide quickly, rush into


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mulls over his options every day. = 他每天都在仔细考虑他的选择。
She mulls the possibilities before making a decision. = 她会在做决定之前仔细考虑各种可能性。


一般过去时:
They mulled over the contract for hours. = 他们仔细考虑了几个小时的合同。
I mulled the idea for a long time. = 我仔细考虑了很久这个主意。


现在完成时:
He has mulled over the proposal for weeks. = 他已经考虑这个提案好几个星期了。
She has mulled the implications of her actions. = 她一直在思考她行为的含义。


过去完成时:
They had mulled the situation before they acted. = 他们在采取行动之前已经仔细考虑了情况。
I had mulled the consequences before I spoke. = 我在说话之前已经考虑过后果。


将来时:
She will mull over the offer before accepting it. = 她会在接受报价之前仔细考虑。
He will mull the decision for a few days. = 他会考虑几天这个决定。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mull over the details further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步仔细考虑细节。
I wish I had mulled the consequences more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑过后果。


被动语态:
The problem was mulled over by the committee. = 这个问题由委员会仔细考虑。
The decision is being mulled over by the board. = 董事会正在仔细考虑这个决定。


疑问句:
Are you mulling over the job offer? = 你在考虑这份工作吗？
Is she still mulling over her options? = 她还在考虑她的选择吗？


否定句:
He didn't mull over the options before he chose one. = 他在选择之前没有仔细考虑选择。
They did not mull over the consequences. = 他们没有仔细考虑后果。


PHRASE->
mull over: 仔细考虑
mull something through: 彻底思考
mull it around: 反复考虑
mull something out: 仔细斟酌
mull and worry: 忧心忡忡地反复思量
