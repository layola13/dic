electropainting:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈpeɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 电泳涂漆，电镀涂漆 | The process of applying a coating to a metal object using an electric current.  This method results in an even, high-quality finish. | Example: Electropainting is a common method for painting car bodies. 电泳涂漆是汽车车身喷漆的常用方法。 The factory uses electropainting to protect its metal components from corrosion.  这家工厂使用电泳涂漆来保护其金属部件免受腐蚀。| 近义词：electrocoating, electrodeposition | 反义词：hand painting, conventional painting | 名词
