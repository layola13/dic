impaired:/ɪmˈpɛərd/ | adj. | 受损的；受影响的；有障碍的 |  Damaged, weakened, or otherwise not functioning properly.  It describes a condition where something is not working as well as it should. |

例句：His impaired vision made driving dangerous.  他的视力受损，开车很危险。

例句：The storm caused impaired communication across the region.  暴风雨导致该地区通讯中断。


近义词：damaged, weakened, compromised, deficient, disabled, handicapped


反义词：enhanced, improved, strengthened, unimpaired, perfect


词性：形容词
