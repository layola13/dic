erudition:/ˌɛrʊˈdɪʃən/| n. |博学；渊博的学识 |Extensive knowledge acquired chiefly from books.  Erudition is often associated with scholarly pursuits and a deep understanding of a particular subject or field. |His erudition in ancient history was impressive.  他的古代史知识渊博令人印象深刻。| scholarship, learning, knowledge |ignorance, illiteracy
