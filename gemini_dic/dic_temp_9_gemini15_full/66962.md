experimentalists: /ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəlɪsts/ | n. |实验者；实验主义者 |People who conduct experiments, especially in science.  They emphasize empirical evidence and practical application over theoretical speculation. |例句：The experimentalists in the lab meticulously documented their findings.  实验室里的实验人员一丝不苟地记录了他们的发现。

The experimentalists' work led to a groundbreaking discovery in physics.  实验人员的工作导致了物理学上的突破性发现。

近义词：scientists, researchers, investigators |反义词：theoreticians, theorists |词性：名词
