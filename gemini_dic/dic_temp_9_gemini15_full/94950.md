in regard to: /ɪn rɪˈɡɑːrd tuː/ | prep. | 关于；至于 |  This prepositional phrase means "concerning" or "with respect to." It introduces a topic or subject that is being discussed or considered.  | 例句：In regard to your question about the deadline, it has been extended. 关于你关于截止日期的问题，它已经延期了。


例句：In regard to your application, we will contact you soon. 关于你的申请，我们会尽快联系你。


近义词：regarding, concerning, respecting, as to, about


反义词：irrespective of, regardless of


词性：介词短语
