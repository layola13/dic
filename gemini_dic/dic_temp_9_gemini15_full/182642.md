reorganization:/ˌriːˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən/| n. |改组；重组；改编|The act or process of reorganizing something, such as a company, structure, or system.  It involves restructuring, rearranging, or reforming to improve efficiency, effectiveness, or to adapt to changing circumstances. |例句：The company underwent a major reorganization last year.  去年公司进行了大规模的改组。

The reorganization of the department resulted in improved workflow. 部门的改组提高了工作效率。


近义词：restructuring, restructuring, reformulation, overhaul, revamp |反义词：disorganization, chaos, disorder |词性：名词
