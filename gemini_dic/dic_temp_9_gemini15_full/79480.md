globalised:/ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzd/| adj.|全球化的；全球一体化的 |  The process by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence or start operating on an international scale.  It refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through the exchange of goods, services, information, and ideas.  |例句：The globalised economy has created both opportunities and challenges. 全球化经济既带来了机遇，也带来了挑战。


例句：The globalised nature of modern business requires companies to adapt to diverse cultures and markets. 现代商业的全球化性质要求公司适应不同的文化和市场。


近义词：internationalized, worldwide, global


反义词：localized, regionalized, isolated


词性：形容词
