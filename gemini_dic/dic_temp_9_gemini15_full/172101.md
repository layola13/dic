purpose-like: /ˈpɜːrpəsˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 有目的的，像有目的的 | Resembling or suggestive of purpose; having or showing a clear aim or intention.  The term is often used informally and can imply a degree of intentionality that isn't explicitly stated. |


例句：

He had a purpose-like gait, suggesting he was on an important mission. = 他步伐坚定，像是执行一项重要任务。

Her actions, though seemingly random, felt purpose-like in retrospect. = 她当时的行动看似随意，事后回想却感觉是有目的的。



近义词：intentional, deliberate, determined, goal-oriented, purposeful


反义词：aimless, random, haphazard, unintentional, accidental


词性:形容词
