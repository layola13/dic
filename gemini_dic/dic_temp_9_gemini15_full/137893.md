nonvitally: /ˌnɒnˈvaɪtəli/ | adv. |非致命地，非重要地 |  Not vitally; in a way that does not affect the essential nature or functioning of something.  | 例句：The wound was nonvitally located.  = 伤口不在致命部位。


The injury was nonvitally important; it was a minor scrape. = 伤势不重要；只是轻微擦伤。


近义词：unimportantly, insignificantly, marginally, minimally


反义词：vitally, critically, essentially


词性：副词
