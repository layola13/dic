/ɡʌs/| n.|一阵风，一阵强风|A sudden and strong gust of wind.|例句：A gust of wind blew my hat off.一阵风吹掉了我的帽子。|近义词：blast, flurry, puff|反义词：calm, stillness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind gusts frequently in this area. = 这个地区经常刮大风。
A strong gust blows the leaves. = 一阵强风吹落树叶。


一般过去时:
A sudden gust of wind knocked over the tree. = 一阵突然的强风吹倒了那棵树。
The strong gust nearly blew the boat away. = 那阵强风差点把船吹走。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
