cosigning:/koʊˈsaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 共同签署；连带签字 | To sign a document together with another person, thereby sharing responsibility for the agreement or debt.  | Example: I'm cosigning a loan for my brother. 我正在为我兄弟的贷款担保。 | 近义词：endorsing, guaranteeing | 反义词：refusing, rejecting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cosigns loans for his friends. = 他为他的朋友们担保贷款。
They are cosigning the lease agreement. = 他们在共同签署租赁协议。


一般过去时:
She cosigned her daughter's car loan. = 她为女儿的汽车贷款担保。
We cosigned the mortgage last year. = 我们去年共同签署了抵押贷款。


现在完成时:
I have cosigned several loans in the past. = 我过去已经为几笔贷款担保过。
They have cosigned a business partnership agreement. = 他们已经共同签署了一份商业伙伴关系协议。


过去完成时:
He had cosigned the contract before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经共同签署了合同。
She had already cosigned the document when I arrived. = 我到达时，她已经签署了文件。


将来时:
I will cosign the application tomorrow. = 我明天将共同签署申请。
We will be cosigning the lease next week. = 我们下周将共同签署租赁协议。


虚拟语气:
If I were to cosign, I would need to review the terms carefully. = 如果我要共同签署，我需要仔细审查条款。
I wish I hadn't cosigned that loan. = 我真希望我没有担保那笔贷款。


被动语态:
The loan was cosigned by her parents. = 这笔贷款由她的父母共同担保。
The agreement will be cosigned by both parties. = 这份协议将由双方共同签署。


疑问句:
Will you be cosigning the mortgage? = 你会共同签署抵押贷款吗？
Are they cosigning the loan application? = 他们是否正在共同签署贷款申请？


否定句:
I will not cosign any more loans. = 我不会再为任何贷款担保了。
He did not cosign the contract. = 他没有签署合同。


PHRASE->
cosign a loan = 共同签署贷款
cosign a mortgage = 共同签署抵押贷款
cosign a lease = 共同签署租赁协议
cosign a contract = 共同签署合同
cosign a document = 共同签署文件
