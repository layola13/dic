magician:/məˈdʒɪʃən/| n. |魔术师，变戏法的人|A magician is a person who performs magic tricks, illusions, and other feats of deception.  Magicians often use sleight of hand, misdirection, and elaborate stagecraft to create a sense of wonder and amazement. |Example: The magician pulled a rabbit out of a hat.  魔术师从帽子中变出一只兔子。 The magician's amazing illusions left the audience speechless. 魔术师令人惊叹的幻象让观众说不出话来。|近义词：illusionist, conjurer, sorcerer |反义词：N/A
