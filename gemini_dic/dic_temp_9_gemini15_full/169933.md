prosthetic:/prɒsˈθɛtɪk/| /prɑːsˈθɛtɪk/| adj., n.| 假体的；义肢的；修复的 | Artificial body part, such as a limb, tooth, or eye.  |例句：She received a prosthetic leg after the accident. 她事故后得到了一条假肢。|例句：The dentist fitted him with a prosthetic tooth. 牙医给他装了一颗假牙。|近义词：artificial, substitute, replacement |反义词：natural, real, organic
