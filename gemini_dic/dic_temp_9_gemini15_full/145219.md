overbearingness: /ˌoʊvərˈbɛrɪŋnəs/ | n. | 专横；盛气凌人；过度；压制 | The quality or state of being overbearing; domineering or oppressive behavior.  It implies an excessive display of authority or power, often in a way that is insensitive to the needs or feelings of others.  | Example:  Her overbearingness made it difficult to work with her.  她的专横使得和她一起工作很困难。  The overbearingness of the manager created a hostile work environment. 经理的盛气凌人创造了一个充满敌意的工作环境。| 近义词：domineeringness, bossiness, tyranny, oppression | 反义词：submissiveness, meekness, gentleness
