hungering:/ˈhʌŋɡərɪŋ/| adj.|渴望的，饥饿的|Feeling a strong desire or need for something, often food.|例句：The children were hungering for food after a long day of hiking.  孩子们在徒步旅行了一整天后渴望食物。|例句：He was hungering for knowledge and spent hours in the library. 他渴望知识，在图书馆里待了几个小时。|近义词：craving, yearning, longing|反义词：satisfied, satiated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hungering for success. = 他渴望成功。
They are hungering for a better life. = 他们渴望更好的生活。

一般过去时:
She was hungering for affection. = 她渴望得到爱。
We were hungering for news from home. = 我们渴望收到家里的消息。

现在完成时:
I have been hungering for adventure my whole life. = 我一生都在渴望冒险。
They have been hungering for justice for years. = 他们多年来一直渴望正义。

过去完成时:
He had been hungering for a change for a long time. = 他渴望改变很久了。
She had been hungering for recognition for her work. = 她一直渴望得到对她工作的认可。

将来时:
They will be hungering for rest after the marathon. = 马拉松结束后，他们会渴望休息。
He will be hungering for knowledge in the coming years. = 未来几年，他会渴望知识。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungering for power, I would do things differently. = 如果我渴望权力，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I were not hungering for something I cannot have. = 我真希望我不渴望我无法得到的东西。

被动语态:  (The adjective "hungering" is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:

否定句:
