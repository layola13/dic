overplant:/ˌoʊvərˈplænt/ | vt. |过度种植，栽种过多 |To plant too many plants in a given area.  This can lead to overcrowding, competition for resources, and reduced yields. |例句：They overplanted the tomatoes, resulting in small, weak fruit.  他们种的西红柿太多了，导致果实又小又弱。

例句：The farmer overplanted his field with corn, leading to a poor harvest.  这位农民玉米种植过多，导致收成不好。


近义词：overcrowd, overcrowd with plants


反义词：underplant


词性:动词
