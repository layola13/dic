monothalmic:/ˌmɒnəˈθælmɪk/| adj.|单眼(的);只有一个眼室的|Having only one eye chamber or eye;  referring to a shell with a single eye.  The term is primarily used in the context of  foraminifera (single-celled protists) and their shell structures. |例句：The foraminifera specimen exhibited a monothalmic test, indicating a simple, single-chambered shell.  该有孔虫标本显示出一个单眼室的壳，表明它是一个简单的单室壳。|近义词：unicameral (in the context of chambers)|反义词：polythalmic (多眼室的)
