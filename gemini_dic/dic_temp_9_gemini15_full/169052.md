programables: /proʊˈɡræməblz/ | adj. | 可编程的 | Capable of being programmed; able to have its functions or operations determined or altered by a program.  This adjective describes something that can be controlled or instructed by a set of instructions or a program. | Example:  Many modern devices are programable, allowing users to customize their functionality.  许多现代设备都是可编程的，允许用户自定义其功能。


近义词: programmable, configurable, adjustable, customizable | 反义词: fixed, inflexible, unchangeable

词性: adjective
