noctule:/ˈnɒktjuːl/| n. |夜蝠, 大棕蝠 |A noctule is a bat of the genus Nyctalus, known for its large size and nocturnal activity.  They are characterized by their dark brown fur and relatively large ears. Noctules are insectivores, feeding primarily on insects caught in flight. | Example: The researchers observed the flight patterns of the common noctule.  研究人员观察了普通夜蝠的飞行模式。|近义词：bat (general term)|反义词：无
