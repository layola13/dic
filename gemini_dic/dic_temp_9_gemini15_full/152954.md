pedicule:/ˈpɛdɪkjuːl/| n.|小梗；蒂；柄|A small stalk or stem, especially one by which an organism or organ is attached.|The flower had a long, slender pedicule. 这朵花有一个细长的小梗。The surgeon carefully dissected the tumor from its pedicule. 外科医生小心地将肿瘤从其蒂部分离。|近义词：stalk, stem, peduncle|反义词：无
