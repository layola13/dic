play-around: /pleɪ əˈraʊnd/ | v. | 玩耍；胡闹；随意尝试 | To engage in informal or casual activities, often without a specific purpose or goal; to experiment or try things out casually.  |例句：The children were playing around in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍。

例句：Let's play around with different design ideas before we finalize the project.  让我们在最终确定项目之前尝试不同的设计理念。


近义词：fool around, mess around, fiddle around, experiment, tinker | 反义词：work diligently, be serious, be meticulous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play around in the garden every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在花园里玩耍。
We play around with the new software to understand its features. = 我们玩弄新的软件来了解它的功能。


一般过去时:
The kids played around in the sand for hours. = 孩子们在沙滩上玩了好几个小时。
I played around with the settings until I found the perfect image. = 我摆弄设置，直到找到完美的图像。


现在完成时:
They have played around with the idea for months. = 他们已经玩弄这个主意好几个月了。
She has played around with several different career paths before settling on this one. = 在选择这条职业道路之前，她尝试过几种不同的职业道路。


过去完成时:
They had played around with the equipment before the demonstration. = 他们在演示之前已经玩弄过设备了。
We had played around with the recipe before finally making it perfectly. = 我们在最终完美制作之前已经尝试过这个食谱。


将来时:
We will play around with the new toys tomorrow. = 我们明天将玩弄新的玩具。
They will play around with the marketing strategies before launching the product. = 他们将在推出产品之前尝试不同的营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would play around with photography more. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更多地尝试摄影。
I wish I could play around with that new technology. = 我真希望我能玩弄那项新技术。


被动语态:  (This verb doesn't readily lend itself to passive voice.  A more suitable phrasing would be needed to express the passive concept.)


疑问句:
Are they playing around again? = 他们又在玩耍了吗？
Will you play around with that software before giving your feedback? = 你在给出反馈之前会尝试使用那个软件吗？


否定句:
They don't play around with dangerous chemicals. = 他们不会玩弄危险的化学品。
I didn't play around with the settings; I followed the instructions carefully. = 我没有乱动设置；我仔细地按照说明操作。




PHRASE->
play around with (something) = 随意尝试(某事物)
play around with (someone) = 与(某人)调情
play around with ideas = 尝试不同的想法
play around at home = 在家玩耍
play around with numbers = 随意玩弄数字
