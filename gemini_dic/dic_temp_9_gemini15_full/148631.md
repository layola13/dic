overthinks:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋks/ | v. |过度思考，想得太多 | To think too much about something, often to the point of anxiety or worry.  It implies excessive analysis and dwelling on possibilities, often leading to negative outcomes. |例句：He overthinks every decision, which often leads to inaction. 他对每一个决定都过度思考，这常常导致无所作为。

例句：She overthinks social interactions, worrying about what others think of her. 她过度思考社交互动，担心别人怎么看她。|近义词：ruminate, ponder excessively, worry excessively |反义词：act impulsively, disregard, ignore
