caustic:/ˈkɔːstɪk/| adj.|腐蚀性的；刻薄的；尖酸的|Something caustic is capable of burning, destroying, or eating away by chemical action. It can also describe a person's remarks as being sarcastic, bitter, or severely critical.|例句：The acid is caustic and will burn your skin.  这种酸具有腐蚀性，会灼伤你的皮肤。


例句：Her caustic remarks hurt his feelings. 她尖酸刻薄的话语伤了他的感情。|近义词：corrosive, biting, sarcastic, acerbic|反义词：mild, gentle, soothing, bland|词性：形容词
