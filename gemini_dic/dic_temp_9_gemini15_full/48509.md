deeri:/ˈdɪəri/ | n. |鹿(复数形式)|Deer is a collective noun referring to a group of deer. It is also used as the plural form of "deer".  The singular and plural forms are the same. | 例句：I saw a herd of deer in the forest. = 我在森林里看到一群鹿。  There are many deer in this park. = 这个公园有很多鹿。|近义词：cervid, hart, hind|反义词：无
