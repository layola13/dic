peritoneum:/ˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm/| n. |腹膜|The peritoneum is a thin membrane that lines the abdominal cavity and covers most of the abdominal organs. It protects the organs, reduces friction during movement, and helps to keep them in place.|例句：The surgeon carefully examined the peritoneum during the laparoscopy.  外科医生在腹腔镜检查中仔细检查了腹膜。|例句：Inflammation of the peritoneum, known as peritonitis, can be a life-threatening condition. 腹膜炎，即腹膜炎症，可能是一种危及生命的疾病。|近义词：mesentery (部分腹膜)|反义词：无
