occupied:/ˈɑːkjupaɪd/| adj.|被占领的；忙碌的；从事…的|The word "occupied" means being filled or taken up, whether it's a space, time, or mind. As an adjective, it can describe something that is in use or possession, or someone who is busy or engaged in an activity.  |例句：The enemy occupied the city. 敌人占领了这座城市。 The room is occupied. 这个房间有人住了。|近义词：busy, engaged, filled, used, taken |反义词：free, vacant, unoccupied, available
