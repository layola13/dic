ferrule:/ˈfɛrəl/| n.|金属套管；保护套；笔套|A ferrule is a metal ring or cap fitted around the end of something, such as a tool handle or a cable, to protect it or to strengthen it.  It can also refer to a small metal tube used to hold the bristles of a brush.|例句：The ferrule on my paintbrush is loose and needs replacing. 我的画笔笔套松了，需要更换。|例句：The cable's ferrule prevented damage from bending. 电缆的金属套管防止了弯曲造成的损坏。|近义词：sleeve, cap, ring, bushing|反义词：None
