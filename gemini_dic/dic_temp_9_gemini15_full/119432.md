microgyria:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈdʒɪəriə/| n. |大脑微回旋畸形|A condition characterized by abnormally small and simplified gyri (the ridges or convolutions) in the brain.  This results in a smoother brain surface than normal. Microgyria is often associated with neurological developmental disorders and epilepsy.|例句：The MRI scan revealed microgyria in the patient's cerebral cortex.  脑部核磁共振扫描显示患者大脑皮层存在大脑微回旋畸形。
The infant's seizures were suspected to be a consequence of microgyria. 婴儿的癫痫发作被怀疑是大脑微回旋畸形的结果。|近义词：pachygyria (although pachygyria involves abnormally thick gyri, while microgyria involves abnormally small gyri)|反义词：lissencephaly (smooth brain, absence of gyri and sulci)
