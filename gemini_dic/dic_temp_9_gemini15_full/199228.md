sillies:/ˈsɪliz/| n.|傻瓜；笨蛋；傻事|The plural of silly.  It refers to foolish or silly people or things.  It is often used in a playful or affectionate way.|例句：Don't be such sillies! 别这么傻！
The children were acting like such sillies. 孩子们表现得像傻瓜一样。|近义词：fools, idiots, goofballs|反义词：geniuses, intellectuals, wise people|名词
