novelistic:/ˌnɒvəˈlɪstɪk/| adj. | 小说似的；具有小说特色的 | Relating to or characteristic of novels; having the qualities of a novel.  The novelistic style of the biography made it a captivating read. 这本传记的小说风格使其成为引人入胜的读物。 | 近义词：fictional, narrative, story-like | 反义词：factual, realistic, documentary
