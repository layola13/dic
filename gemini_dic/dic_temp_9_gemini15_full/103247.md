jewel:/ˈdʒuːəl/| n. |宝石，珍宝|A jewel is a precious stone, such as a diamond, ruby, or emerald, that is often used in jewelry.  It can also refer to something highly valued or precious.|That necklace is adorned with beautiful jewels.  这条项链装饰着美丽的宝石。| gem, treasure, gem stone| worthless, common|名词
