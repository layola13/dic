nonterritorial: /ˌnɒnˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 非领土的；不占领地盘的 | Not associated with or limited to a particular territory or area; not exhibiting territorial behavior.  | 例句：Many bird species are nonterritorial, roaming widely in search of food.  许多鸟类是非领土性的，它们四处游荡寻找食物。

例句：The nomadic lifestyle of these people makes them nonterritorial by nature. 这些人的游牧生活方式使他们天生就不占领地盘。


近义词：unclaimed, itinerant, nomadic, wandering


反义词：territorial, possessive, sedentary


词性: adjective
