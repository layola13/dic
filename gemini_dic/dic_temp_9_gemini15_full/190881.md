saying:/ˈseɪɪŋ/ | n. |谚语；格言；说法 | A saying is a well-known phrase that expresses a common truth or belief.  It can also refer to something someone says. | 例句： "A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush" is a common saying.  这句谚语“手里的一只鸟胜过树上的两只鸟”很常见。

| 例句： What was your saying again? 你刚才说了什么？ | 近义词：proverb, maxim, adage, expression | 反义词：silence,  (no direct antonym, but the opposite would be an untrue statement) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The saying goes that... = 这句话的意思是……
This saying is often used by... = 这句话经常被...使用

一般过去时:
He said a saying about hard work. = 他说了句关于努力工作的谚语。
They repeated an old saying. = 他们重复了一条古老的谚语。


现在完成时:
People have used this saying for centuries. = 人们几个世纪以来一直在使用这句话。
She has heard that saying many times. = 她听过很多次那句话了。


过去完成时:
They had already heard the saying before the meeting. = 他们之前就已经听过那句话了。
I had never heard that saying before. = 我以前从未听过那句话。


将来时:
They will use this saying in their speech. = 他们将在演讲中使用这句话。
The saying will be remembered for years to come. = 这句话将在未来几年被人们记住。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the meaning of that saying, I would tell you. = 如果我知道那句话的意思，我会告诉你的。
I wish I had understood that saying earlier. = 我真希望我早点明白那句话的意思。


被动语态:
This saying is often misquoted. = 这句话经常被误引用。
The saying was written by a famous philosopher. = 这句话是由一位著名的哲学家写的。


疑问句:
What is the meaning of this saying? = 这句话是什么意思？
What saying did he use to describe the situation? = 他用什么谚语来形容这种情况？


否定句:
That saying isn't true in all cases. = 那句话并非在所有情况下都适用。
I don't understand the meaning of that saying. = 我不明白那句话的意思。
