overwind:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪnd/| vt.|使过度运转；使过度兴奋；过度上弦|To wind (something, especially a clock or watch) too tightly or for too long.  It can also refer to making someone overly excited or stressed.|例句：Don't overwind the clock, or it might break.  别把钟上得太紧，否则它可能会坏掉。

He was overwound after his stressful day at work. 他在工作压力大的一天后过度兴奋。|近义词：overtighten, strain, overexcite|反义词：underwind, relax
