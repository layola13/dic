reinheriting: /ˌriːɪnˈhɛrɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新继承；再次继承 |  The act of inheriting something again, either from a different source or after a previous inheritance.  This word is less common and often implies a second or subsequent inheritance. | Example:  The family is reinheriting the ancestral estate after a long legal battle.  = 在一场旷日持久的法律诉讼之后，这个家族正在重新继承祖传的庄园。


近义词: inheriting again, reacquiring (in the context of inheritance)


反义词: disinheriting, forfeiting (losing an inheritance)


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reinheriting his uncle's collection of rare stamps. = 他正在重新继承他叔叔的珍稀邮票收藏。
They are reinheriting the family business after a period of mismanagement. = 在一段时间的管理不善之后，他们正在重新继承家族企业。


一般过去时:
She reinherited her grandmother's jewelry after her aunt's death. = 她在她姑姑去世后重新继承了她祖母的珠宝。
The company reinherited its original patent after a successful lawsuit. = 在一起成功的诉讼之后，该公司重新获得了其最初的专利。


现在完成时:
They have reinherited the property after years of legal disputes. = 多年法律纠纷后，他们已经重新继承了这处房产。
He has reinherited the title and responsibilities of the family estate. = 他已经重新继承了家族产业的头衔和责任。


过去完成时:
Before the will was contested, they had reinherited the fortune. = 在遗嘱受到质疑之前，他们已经重新继承了这笔财富。
She had reinherited the farm after her brother's unexpected passing.  = 在她哥哥意外去世后，她已经重新继承了农场。


将来时:
The heir will reinherit the throne after the current monarch's death. = 在现任君主去世后，继承人将重新继承王位。
He will reinherit the business once he completes his training. = 一旦他完成培训，他将重新继承这家企业。


虚拟语气:
If the court ruled in their favor, they would reinherit the land. = 如果法院判决对他们有利，他们将重新继承这片土地。
I wish I could reinherit my grandfather's legacy of philanthropy. = 我希望我能重新继承我祖父的慈善事业遗产。


被动语态:
The estate was reinherited by the rightful heir. = 这处房产被合法继承人重新继承。
The family business was reinherited after a period of bankruptcy. = 家族企业在破产一段时间后被重新继承。


疑问句:
Will they reinherit the family home? = 他们会重新继承家里的房子吗？
Who will reinherit the fortune after his death? = 他死后谁将重新继承这笔财富？


否定句:
He did not reinherit the property due to legal complications. = 由于法律纠纷，他没有重新继承这处房产。
They were unable to reinherit the family business due to financial difficulties. = 由于财务困难，他们无法重新继承家族企业。
