overgrieves: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvz/ | v. |过度悲伤，极度悲痛 | To grieve excessively or to an unhealthy degree.  It implies a sorrow that is overwhelming and potentially debilitating. |例句：She overgrieves the loss of her pet, refusing to leave her room for days. 她对失去宠物过度悲伤，几天都不愿离开房间。
She overgrieves the death of her father, neglecting her health and responsibilities. 她对她父亲的去世过度悲伤，忽视了自己的健康和责任。|近义词：excessively mourn, deeply lament, excessively sorrow |反义词：celebrate, rejoice
