co-operates:/koʊˈɒpəreɪts/ | v. |合作，协作 |To work together for a common purpose or goal.  |The team co-operates effectively to achieve project goals.  团队有效合作以实现项目目标。|collaborates, works together |competes, opposes |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team co-operates well. = 团队合作良好。
They co-operate on most projects. = 他们在大多数项目上都合作。

一般过去时:
The two companies co-operated on the new product. = 两家公司在开发新产品方面进行了合作。
We co-operated with the police in the investigation. = 我们在调查中与警方合作。

现在完成时:
We have co-operated on several projects this year. = 今年我们已经合作过几个项目了。
They have co-operated successfully in the past. = 他们过去合作得很成功。

过去完成时:
They had co-operated before on similar projects. = 他们之前在类似项目上合作过。
The company had co-operated with its competitors before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司曾与竞争对手合作过。

将来时:
We will co-operate fully with the investigation. = 我们将全力配合调查。
The two nations will co-operate on environmental protection. = 两国将在环境保护方面进行合作。

虚拟语气:
If they co-operated, the project would be finished sooner. = 如果他们合作，项目就能更快完成。
I wish they would co-operate more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地合作。

被动语态:
The plan was co-operated on by several departments. = 该计划得到了几个部门的合作。
The investigation is being co-operated with by many agencies. = 许多机构正在配合调查。

疑问句:
Does everyone co-operate on this project? = 每个人都在这个项目上合作吗？
Will they co-operate with our request? = 他们会配合我们的请求吗？

否定句:
They did not co-operate with the authorities. = 他们没有与当局合作。
The two sides refused to co-operate. = 双方拒绝合作。


PHRASE->
co-operate with = 与...合作
co-operate fully = 全力配合
co-operate effectively = 有效合作
co-operate on a project = 合作一个项目
co-operate in a task = 合作完成一项任务

词源:
The word "co-operates" comes from the prefix "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") and the verb "operate" (meaning "to work," "to function," or "to be active").  The combination signifies working together.
