orientability: /ˌɔːriənˈtæbɪləti/ | n. | 可定向性 | The property of a surface that can be consistently oriented; a surface is orientable if it's possible to define a consistent "up" direction at every point on the surface.  A surface is orientable if one can consistently choose a normal vector at each point such that the normal vector varies continuously over the surface.  | Example: A sphere is orientable, but a Möbius strip is not.  球面是可定向的，但莫比乌斯带不是。|近义词：定向性 |反义词：non-orientability  不可定向性
