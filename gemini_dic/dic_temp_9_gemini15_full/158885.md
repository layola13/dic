plumula:/ˈpluːmjʊlə/ | n. | 羽状体；胚芽；绒毛 | The plumule is the part of a seed that develops into the shoot of a plant.  It is the embryonic shoot of a plant, consisting of the epicotyl (the stem above the cotyledons), the plumular bud (the first bud), and the first few leaves. |  The plumule of the bean seed is very small and delicate.  豆芽的胚芽非常小而娇嫩。The plumule emerges from the seed first, followed by the radicle. 胚芽先从种子中长出，然后是胚根。|  germ, sprout, shoot |  radicle |  名词
