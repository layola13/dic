exorcises:/ˈɛksɔːrsɪzɪz/ | n. |驱魔仪式；练习；排遣|The act of driving out evil spirits;  a practice or exercise to develop or improve a skill; the act of relieving or getting rid of something unpleasant. |例句：The priest performed an exorcism to rid the house of evil spirits.  牧师进行了驱魔仪式以驱除房子里的邪灵。 The daily exercises helped improve her fitness.  每天的练习帮助她提高了体能。|近义词：expulsion, practice, workout, relief |反义词：possession, infestation
