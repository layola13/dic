franca:/ˈfræŋkə/| n.|法兰克；法郎；法兰克福；自由港|A franca is a currency unit, historically used in several countries. It can also refer to a free port or a place of free trade.  The word is also used as a proper noun, referring to cities like Franca (Brazil) and Francavilla al Mare (Italy).|例句：The Swiss franc is a strong currency. 瑞士法郎是一种强势货币。

The city of Franca is known for its shoe industry. 法兰卡市以其制鞋业闻名。

近义词：currency (as a unit of money), free port, free trade zone|反义词：restricted port, controlled trade zone|名词
