emus:/ˈiːmjuːz/| n. |鸸鹋|A large flightless bird native to Australia, similar to an ostrich but smaller and with brown plumage.| 例句：Emus are the second-largest living birds in the world.  鸸鹋是世界上第二大的鸟类。| 例句：The emu's powerful legs allow it to run at high speeds. 鸸鹋强壮的腿可以让它以高速奔跑。| 近义词：ostrich (though smaller), rhea | 反义词：None
