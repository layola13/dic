pollination: /ˌpɒləˈneɪʃən/ /ˌpɑːləˈneɪʃən/ | n. |授粉；花粉传播| The transfer of pollen from the anther to the stigma of a flower, enabling fertilization.  This process is crucial for the reproduction of flowering plants.  Many plants rely on insects, birds, or wind for pollination. | Bees play a vital role in the pollination of many crops. 蜜蜂在许多农作物的授粉中起着至关重要的作用。

The wind facilitated the pollination of the tall grasses. 风促进了高草的授粉。


近义词：fertilization (in the context of plant reproduction)
反义词：无  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与植物不育相关的词语，但不是完全反义)
