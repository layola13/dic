prudentiality: /ˌpruːdənʃiˈæləti/ | n. |谨慎；审慎；精明 |The quality or state of being prudent; cautiousness; discretion.  It emphasizes carefulness and forethought in decision-making, avoiding risks and potential harm. |例句：The company's success is due in part to its prudentiality in financial matters.  公司的成功部分归功于其在财务上的谨慎。


例句：The prudentiality of his investment strategy protected him from significant losses during the market downturn. 他投资策略的谨慎性保护他在市场低迷时期免受重大损失。


近义词：caution, circumspection, discretion, forethought, providence


反义词：recklessness, imprudence, carelessness, rashness


词性：名词
