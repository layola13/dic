reflective: /rɪˈflɛktɪv/ | adj. | 反射的；深思熟虑的；反映的 |

英文解析:  Having the property of reflecting light, heat, or sound.  Also, given to or characterized by reflection or contemplation; thoughtful.

例句:

The reflective surface of the lake mirrored the mountains.  湖面的反射面映照着山峦。

She spent the evening in reflective silence. 她花了整个晚上在沉思默想中。


近义词: thoughtful, contemplative, ruminative, specular (for reflecting surfaces)

反义词: thoughtless, impulsive, unreflective


词源:  From Middle English *reflectif, from Old French reflectif, from Latin reflectere "to bend back," from re- "back" + flectere "to bend."
