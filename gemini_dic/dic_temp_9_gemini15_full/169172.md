proletarianly:/ˌproʊlɛˈtɛəriənli/| adv.| 无产阶级地，以无产阶级的方式 | In a manner characteristic of the proletariat; in a way that reflects the values, attitudes, or conditions of the working class. | 例句：He lived proletarianly, sharing a cramped apartment with several other workers. 他过着无产阶级的生活，与其他几个工人挤在一间狭小的公寓里。
The workers acted proletarianly, demanding better wages and working conditions. 工人们以无产阶级的方式行动，要求更高的工资和更好的工作条件。| 近义词：working-classly, communally | 反义词：bourgeoisly, aristocratically
