nonpersuasiveness:/ˌnɒn.pəˈsweɪ.sɪv.nəs/| n. |不具有说服力；缺乏说服力|The lack of persuasive power; inability to convince or influence.  It refers to the quality or state of being not persuasive. This can be due to various factors, including weak arguments, poor delivery, lack of credibility, or an uninterested audience.|The nonpersuasiveness of his arguments led to the rejection of his proposal.他的论点缺乏说服力，导致他的提案被否决。


The presentation lacked nonpersuasiveness and was ultimately successful.  这次演示缺乏说服力，最终获得成功。(This sentence is incorrect, should be: The presentation lacked *persuasiveness* and was ultimately unsuccessful.)|近义词：unpersuasiveness, unconvincingness,ineffectiveness|反义词：persuasiveness, persuasiveness, effectiveness|名词
