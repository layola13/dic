myricin:/ˈmɪrɪsɪn/| n. |杨梅素|A waxy, crystalline substance found in the leaves and berries of certain plants, notably Myrica species (bayberry). It is a mixture of fatty acids and their esters.  It has some medicinal uses and is used in candles and cosmetics.|例句：Myricin is extracted from bayberry wax and used in the production of candles.|近义词：bayberry wax|反义词：无
