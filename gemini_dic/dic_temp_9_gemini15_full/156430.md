phytophagic:/ˌfaɪtəˈfædʒɪk/| adj.|植食性的；食草的|Relating to or denoting organisms that feed on plants.|例句：Many insects are phytophagic, relying on plant matter for sustenance.  许多昆虫是植食性的，依靠植物物质维持生计。|例句： Phytophagic insects can cause significant damage to crops. 植食性昆虫会对农作物造成重大损害。|近义词：herbivorous, plant-eating|反义词：carnivorous, zoophagous
