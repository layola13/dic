mountaintops: /ˈmaʊntɪntɑːps/ | n. |山顶；山峰之顶 | The highest points of mountains.  The plural form of "mountaintop".  |例句：The mountaintops were covered in snow. 山顶覆盖着积雪。

The climbers finally reached the mountaintops after days of arduous climbing. 登山者经过数日艰苦攀登后终于到达了山顶。|近义词：peaks, summits, crests |反义词：valleys, bottoms

词源:
mountain (n.) + tops (n. pl.)  "Mountain" derives from Old French "montaigne," ultimately from Latin "mont," meaning "mountain." "Tops" is the plural of "top," from Old English "topp," meaning "summit, top." Therefore, "mountaintops" literally means "the tops of mountains".
