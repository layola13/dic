forbearer's: /fɔːrˈbeərərz/ | n. | 祖先；前辈 |  A forbearer is an ancestor; a person from whom one is descended. The term often implies a more distant ancestor than a parent or grandparent.  |  例句：Many of his forbearers were farmers. 他的许多祖先都是农民。

例句：She researched her family tree to learn more about her forbearers. 她研究家谱以了解更多关于她祖先的信息。


近义词：ancestor, predecessor, forefather

反义词：descendant, successor


词性：名词
