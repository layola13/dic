nonsentences: /ˈnɑːnsɛntənsɪz/ | n. |非句子；不成句子的词语|A sequence of words that does not form a grammatically complete sentence.  They often lack a subject, verb, or predicate, or violate standard sentence structure.  They can be used for various stylistic or expressive purposes.|例句：The child's utterance, "More juice!", was a nonsentence.  孩子的表达，“再来点果汁！”，是一个非句子。|近义词：fragments, phrases, incomplete sentences|反义词：sentences, complete sentences
