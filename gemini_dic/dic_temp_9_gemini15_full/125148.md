multi-user:/ˈmʌltiˈjuːzər/| adj.|多用户的|Having the capacity to be used by multiple users simultaneously or concurrently.  It describes systems, software, or resources designed to support interaction from several individuals at the same time.  |例句：This database system is multi-user, allowing many employees to access and update information simultaneously. 这个数据库系统是多用户系统，允许许多员工同时访问和更新信息。


例句：The new software has a multi-user interface that facilitates collaborative work. 这款新软件具有多用户界面，便于协同工作。


近义词：multi-access, shared, collaborative


反义词：single-user, individual


词性：形容词
