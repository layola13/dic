prescind:/prɪˈsɪnd/| vt.| 抽象；撇开；忽视；不考虑 | To separate or detach (something) from something else; to disregard or ignore (something) while considering something else.  | Example: Let's prescind from the question of cost for now and focus on the design.  让我们暂时撇开成本问题，专注于设计。|近义词：abstract, disregard, ignore, set aside, detach|反义词：consider, include, regard, account for|动词
