recarpet:/ˌriːˈkɑːrpɪt/| vt.|重新铺地毯|To cover (a floor, etc.) with a new carpet.  This verb implies replacing an existing carpet.|例句：We need to recarpet the living room; the old carpet is worn out. 我们需要重新铺设客厅的地毯；旧地毯已经磨损了。

例句：They decided to recarpet the entire house after the flood.  洪水过后，他们决定重新铺设整个房子的地毯。


近义词：re-carpet, refit (in the context of flooring), replace (carpet)
反义词：remove carpet, uncover (floor)
