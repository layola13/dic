dedicate:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 奉献；致力于；题献 | To dedicate something to someone or something means to formally set it apart for a specific purpose or person. It often involves a commitment of time, effort, or resources.  | 例句：He dedicated his life to helping the poor. 他毕生致力于帮助穷人。

He dedicated his new book to his wife. 他将新书献给他妻子。


近义词：devote, consecrate, commit | 反义词：neglect, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She dedicates her weekends to volunteering. = 她把周末时间都用来做志愿者工作。
He dedicates his time to his family. = 他把时间都奉献给了家人。


一般过去时:
They dedicated the park to the memory of the fallen soldiers. = 他们把公园献给阵亡士兵的纪念。
She dedicated her award-winning novel to her mentor. = 她把获奖小说献给了她的导师。


现在完成时:
He has dedicated his career to fighting for social justice. = 他毕生致力于争取社会正义。
She has dedicated much of her life to charitable causes. = 她把大部分生命都奉献给了慈善事业。


过去完成时:
They had dedicated years to researching the disease before finding a cure. = 在找到治疗方法之前，他们已经花了数年时间研究这种疾病。
He had dedicated his youth to his musical ambitions. = 他把青春都奉献给了他的音乐理想。


将来时:
She will dedicate her next book to her children. = 她将把她的下一本书献给她的孩子们。
He will dedicate his efforts to completing the project on time. = 他将全力以赴按时完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dedicate it to learning a new language. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把它用来学习一门外语。
I wish I could dedicate more time to my hobbies. = 我希望我能有更多时间投入我的爱好。


被动语态:
The monument was dedicated to the victims of the war. = 这座纪念碑是献给战争受害者的。
The new hospital wing was dedicated last week. = 新的医院病房区上周启用。


疑问句:
To whom will you dedicate your next project? = 你将把你的下一个项目献给谁？
What cause will you dedicate your life to? = 你将把你的生命奉献给什么事业？


否定句:
He didn't dedicate enough time to his studies. = 他没有足够的时间投入学习。
She did not dedicate herself fully to the task. = 她没有全身心地投入这项工作。
