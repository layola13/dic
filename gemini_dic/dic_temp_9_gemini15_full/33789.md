circumcision:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈsɪʒən/| n. |割礼；包皮环切术 |The surgical removal of the foreskin from the penis.  This practice is common in some cultures and religions for religious, hygienic, or medical reasons. |Example: Male circumcision is a common practice in many parts of the world.  男性包皮环切术在世界许多地方是一种普遍的做法。|近义词：foreskin removal |反义词：None.
