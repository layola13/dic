clearance:/ˈklɪərəns/| n.| 清除；许可；间隙；余地；特价商品|The act of clearing or the state of being cleared; permission or authorization; the space between two objects; room or space for movement or operation; goods sold at a reduced price.|例句：The clearance of the debris after the storm took several days.  暴风雨过后的残骸清理花了几天时间。
例句：He received security clearance to access classified documents. 他获得了安全许可，可以访问机密文件。


近义词：authorization, permission, space, gap, allowance, sale|反义词：obstruction, blockage, restriction, congestion

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store offers clearance sales every season. = 这家店每季都会进行清仓大甩卖。
We need more clearance between the two buildings. = 我们需要这两栋建筑物之间有更大的间隙。

一般过去时:
The customs officer gave the package clearance. = 海关官员批准了包裹通关。
They cleared the snow from the road. = 他们清除了路上的积雪。

现在完成时:
The company has completed the clearance of its old inventory. = 公司已经完成了旧库存的清理。
They have obtained security clearance for the project. = 他们已经获得了该项目的安保许可。


过去完成时:
The workers had completed the clearance before the deadline. = 工人们在截止日期前完成了清理工作。
They had received clearance to proceed with the plan. = 他们已经获得了执行计划的许可。

将来时:
The airport will have a long clearance process for luggage. = 机场将会有一个漫长的行李检查流程。
They will clear the field for the construction. = 他们将清理场地以便施工。

虚拟语气:
If we had more clearance, we could fit the furniture. = 如果我们有更大的空间，我们就能放下家具。
I wish I had received clearance earlier. = 我希望我早点获得批准。

被动语态:
The documents were cleared by the security personnel. = 文件由安保人员进行了审核。
The area was cleared of all obstacles. = 该区域已清除所有障碍物。

疑问句:
Do you have clearance to enter this area? = 你有进入这个区域的许可吗？
Is there sufficient clearance for the truck to pass? = 卡车通过是否有足够的间隙？

否定句:
The project lacked necessary clearance. = 该项目缺乏必要的许可。
The road was not cleared of snow. = 道路没有清除积雪。


PHRASE->
security clearance 安全许可
ground clearance 地面间隙
clearance sale 清仓大甩卖
customs clearance 海关清关
air clearance 空中净空

词源:
源自古法语 *clairiere,  意为“空地，林间空地”，进一步源自拉丁语 clarus, 意为“清晰，明亮”。  其含义逐渐扩展，涵盖了“清除障碍物”、“获得许可”等方面。
