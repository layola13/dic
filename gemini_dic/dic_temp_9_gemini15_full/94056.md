impass:/ˈɪmpæs/| n.|僵局；死胡同；困境|A situation in which no progress is possible, especially because of disagreement; a deadlock.  |例句：Negotiations reached an impasse when neither side would compromise.  谈判陷入僵局，因为双方都不肯妥协。

例句：The two countries are at an impasse over trade negotiations.  这两个国家在贸易谈判上陷入僵局。


近义词：deadlock, stalemate, standstill, gridlock|反义词：breakthrough, resolution, solution|名词
