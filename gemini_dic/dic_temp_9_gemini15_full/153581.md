pentamery:/penˈtæməri/| n. |五聚体|Pentamery refers to a structure composed of five units or parts, often arranged in a cyclical or radial pattern.  It's commonly used in biological contexts to describe structures like the five-part radial symmetry of some flowers or the five-membered rings in some chemical compounds. |Example: The flower exhibits clear pentamery in its petal arrangement. 这朵花的花瓣排列显示出明显的五辐射对称。|近义词:  quinquepartite, five-membered|反义词:  monomeric (for chemical context),  dimeric (for chemical context)


USAGE->
(由于pentamery主要用于描述特定结构，故其用法不包含时态变化等。)
