charcoal:/ˈtʃɑːrkoʊl/| n. |木炭；炭笔|A black substance made by burning wood or other organic matter in a limited supply of air.  It is used as fuel, a drawing material, and in various other applications.|例句：We used charcoal to grill the burgers. 我们用木炭烤汉堡。

例句：The artist sketched the portrait with charcoal.艺术家用炭笔速写了这幅肖像。

近义词：coal, coke |反义词：None specifically, but terms like "bright," "colorful," or "vivid" could be considered contextual opposites depending on the usage. |词性：名词
