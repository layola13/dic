conclusively:/kənˈkluːsɪvli/ | adv. |最终地；决定性地；无可辩驳地 |  Conclusively means in a way that leaves no doubt; decisively. It emphasizes the finality and irrefutability of a conclusion. |The evidence conclusively proved his guilt.  证据确凿地证明了他的罪行。| decisively, definitively, undeniably | inconclusively, tentatively, ambiguously
