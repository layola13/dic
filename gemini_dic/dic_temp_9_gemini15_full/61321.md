embassade:/ˌæmˈbæsədi/| n. |大使馆|The embassy is responsible for representing its country's interests abroad.  大使馆负责代表其国家在国外的利益。|近义词：diplomatic mission|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The embassy organizes many cultural events. = 大使馆组织许多文化活动。
The embassy protects its citizens abroad. = 大使馆保护其在国外的公民。
