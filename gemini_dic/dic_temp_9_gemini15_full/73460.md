forensics:/fəˈrɛnzɪks/| n.|法医学；鉴定学|The scientific study and analysis of evidence used in a court of law.  It encompasses various disciplines like ballistics, toxicology, and DNA analysis to investigate crimes.|例句：The forensics team collected crucial evidence at the crime scene.  法医小组在犯罪现场收集了关键证据。|近义词：criminalistics, forensic science|反义词：无
