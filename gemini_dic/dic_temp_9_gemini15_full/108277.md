laundries:/ˈlɔːndriz/| n.|洗衣店；洗衣房；待洗的衣物|Places that do laundry or the process of washing clothes.|The laundries were overflowing with dirty clothes after the festival.  节日过后，洗衣店里堆满了脏衣服。|Many people use self-service laundries to save money. 许多人使用自助洗衣店来省钱。|dry cleaners, wash houses|dry cleaning shops


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The laundry cleans clothes every day. = 洗衣店每天都洗衣服。
She does her laundry on weekends. = 她周末洗衣服。


一般过去时:
We did the laundry yesterday. = 我们昨天洗了衣服。
The laundry closed early last night. = 洗衣店昨晚提前关门了。


现在完成时:
I have already done the laundry. = 我已经洗完衣服了。
She has never used a coin-operated laundry. = 她从未使用过投币式洗衣店。


过去完成时:
They had finished the laundry before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经洗完衣服了。
I had never seen so much laundry before. = 我以前从未见过这么多待洗的衣服。


将来时:
I will do the laundry tomorrow. = 我明天会洗衣服。
The laundry will be open until 10pm tonight. = 洗衣店今晚营业到晚上十点。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would do the laundry. = 如果我有时间，我会洗衣服。
I wish I had done the laundry earlier. = 我真希望我早点洗了衣服。


被动语态:
The laundry is cleaned every week. = 洗衣房每周都打扫一次。（此处指洗衣房本身的清洁）
The clothes were laundered carefully. = 衣服被仔细地洗涤了。


疑问句:
Where is the nearest laundry? = 最近的洗衣店在哪里？
Do you have time to do the laundry today? = 你今天有时间洗衣服吗？


否定句:
I didn't do the laundry this week. = 我这周没洗衣服。
They don't offer dry cleaning services at that laundry. = 那家洗衣店不提供干洗服务。


PHRASE->
do the laundry = 洗衣服
