oculomotor: /ˌɒkjʊləˈməʊtə(r)/, /ˌɑːkjʊləˈmoʊtər/ | adj. |动眼神经的；控制眼球运动的 | Relating to or controlling eye movement. The oculomotor nerve is the third cranial nerve and is responsible for most of the eye's movement.  | Example: The oculomotor nerve controls the muscles that move the eyeball.  动眼神经控制移动眼球的肌肉。|近义词: oculokinetic |反义词:  None.
