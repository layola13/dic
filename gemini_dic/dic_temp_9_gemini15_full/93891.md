The philosophical study of immortality and strategies for achieving it. |  例句：The pursuit of immortalism has driven scientific advancements in fields like genetics and regenerative medicine.  追求不朽主义推动了基因学和再生医学等领域的科学进步。


例句：Some proponents of immortalism believe technological advancements will eventually allow humans to overcome biological aging. 一些不朽主义的支持者认为，科技进步最终将使人类克服生物衰老。


近义词：longevity, life extension | 反义词：mortality, death
