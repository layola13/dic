capo:/ˈkæpoʊ/| n.|卡布，吉他调音夹|A capo is a device used on stringed instruments, such as guitars and ukuleles, to change the pitch of the strings by shortening the length of the strings. It clamps onto the fretboard, effectively raising the pitch of all the strings by the same interval.|例句：I used a capo on the second fret to play the song in a higher key. 我在第二品上使用卡布来演奏这首歌，使其音调更高。  例句：The guitarist put a capo on his guitar to change the key. 吉他手在吉他上装上卡布来改变调。|近义词：clamp, tuner (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically.
