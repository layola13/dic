recancellation:/ˌriːkænsəˈleɪʃən/| n. |重新取消；再次取消|The act of canceling something that has already been canceled.  This often involves a reversal of a previous cancellation, reinstating something that was previously cancelled.  It implies a second level of cancellation or a change of mind regarding a prior cancellation.|例句：The recancellation of the flight caused widespread inconvenience.  航班的再次取消造成了广泛的不便。 The recancellation of the order was due to a clerical error. 订单的重新取消是由于文书错误造成的。|近义词：revocation, reversal, annulment|反义词：confirmation, ratification, implementation
