caponize:/ˈkæpənaɪz/| vt.|阉割（家禽）|To surgically remove the testicles of a male bird (usually a chicken, rooster, or turkey) to prevent it from crowing, becoming aggressive, or to improve meat quality.  The term can also be used figuratively to imply neutering or emasculating something.|例句：The farmer caponized the roosters to make them calmer and improve their meat.|近义词：emasculate, geld, neuter|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be to leave the bird intact.|动词
