cynical:/ˈsɪnɪkəl/ | adj. | 愤世嫉俗的；怀疑的；讽刺的 |  Characterized by or showing cynicism; believing the worst of human nature and motives.  It often involves a distrust of others' sincerity or goodness.  | 例句：He had a cynical view of human nature. 他对人性持一种愤世嫉俗的观点。

He gave a cynical laugh at the politician's promises. 他对政治家的承诺报以嘲讽的冷笑。


近义词：skeptical, distrustful, pessimistic, sarcastic, sardonic | 反义词：optimistic, trusting, naive, idealistic
