plastically:/ˈplæstɪkli/| adv.|  柔韧地；可塑地；人工地；做作地 | In a plastic manner; artificially or superficially.  |例句：The clay was molded plastically into various shapes.  黏土被塑造成各种形状。


例句：He smiled plastically, a forced and unnatural expression. 他虚假地笑了笑，表情生硬而不自然。


近义词：artificially, synthetically, superficially


反义词：naturally, genuinely, authentically


词性：副词
