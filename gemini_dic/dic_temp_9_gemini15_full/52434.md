didymous:/ˈdɪdɪməs/| adj.| 双体的；成对的 | Having two similar parts or lobes; paired.  The term often refers to paired organs or structures in biology. | Example: The stamens of the flower are didymous, meaning they occur in pairs.  这朵花的花丝是成对的，意味着它们成对出现。|近义词：paired, twinned, double | 反义词：single, solitary
