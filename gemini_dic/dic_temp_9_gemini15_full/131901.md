noncontending: /ˌnɑːn kənˈtɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 不竞争的，不相争的 | Not competing; not striving for victory or dominance.  The term often implies a passive or inactive stance rather than an active choice to avoid competition. | 例句：The noncontending team accepted their loss gracefully.  不参与竞争的球队优雅地接受了他们的失败。

The noncontending players simply watched the others compete. 不参与竞争的球员只是看着其他人比赛。


近义词：uncompetitive, passive, inactive, noncompetitive

反义词：competitive, contending, aggressive, ambitious


词性: adjective
