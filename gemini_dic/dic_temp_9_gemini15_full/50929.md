desiccator:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtər/ | n. |干燥器，脱水器 | A desiccator is an airtight container used to keep things dry by absorbing moisture. It typically contains a desiccant, such as silica gel, to remove water vapor from the air inside.  | Example: The samples were stored in a desiccator to prevent moisture absorption.  样品储存在干燥器中以防止吸湿。 | 近义词：drying chamber, exsiccator | 反义词：humidifier

词源:

The word "desiccator" comes from the Latin word "desiccare," meaning "to dry up" or "to make dry."  The suffix "-ator" is an agent noun suffix, indicating something that performs the action of drying.
