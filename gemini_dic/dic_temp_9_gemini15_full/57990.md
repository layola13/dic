drivelled:/ˈdrɪvəld/| v.|喋喋不休地说话；胡说八道；|to talk foolishly or incoherently; to let saliva dribble from the mouth|例句：He drivelled on about his childhood for hours. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的童年好几个小时。|例句：The old man drivelled incoherently.  老人语无伦次地胡言乱语。|近义词：babble, ramble, prate, gibber|反义词：speak clearly, articulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drivels incessantly. = 他没完没了地唠叨。
She drivels nonsense. = 她胡说八道。

一般过去时:
He drivelled on about politics. = 他没完没了地谈论政治。
She drivelled incoherently after the accident. = 事故后她语无伦次地胡言乱语。

现在完成时:
He has drivelled for hours. = 他已经唠叨了几个小时了。
She has drivelled such nonsense before. = 她以前也说过这种胡话。

过去完成时:
He had drivelled about his problems all day. = 他整天都在抱怨他的问题。
She had drivelled until she was exhausted. = 她一直唠叨到筋疲力尽。

将来时:
He will drivel about the same old topics. = 他还会唠叨那些老掉牙的话题。
She will probably drivel on for another hour. = 她可能会再唠叨一个小时。

虚拟语气:
If he drivelled less, people would listen more. = 如果他不那么唠叨，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't drivel so much. = 我希望他不那么唠叨。

被动语态: (Drivel is rarely used in the passive voice)
His words were drivelled (implying nonsense was spoken). = 他的话是胡说八道（暗示说了废话）。
The speech was characterized by drivelled pronouncements. = 这篇讲话的特点是充满了废话。


疑问句:
Does he always drivel like this? = 他总是这样唠叨吗？
Why does she drivel so much? = 她为什么说这么多废话？


否定句:
He didn't drivel; he spoke clearly and concisely. = 他没有胡说八道；他说话清晰简洁。
She didn't drivel on about her problems. = 她没有没完没了地抱怨她的问题。


PHRASE->
senile drivel = 痴呆的胡言乱语
meaningless drivel = 无意义的废话
pointless drivel = 无聊的废话
utter drivel = 完全是废话
a stream of drivel = 一连串的废话

词源:
Drivel ultimately derives from the Old English word *drivelan*, meaning "to slaver" or "to let saliva dribble."  The sense of foolish talk developed later, likely due to the association of drooling with mental incapacitation.
