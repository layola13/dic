phosphates:/ˈfɒsfeɪts/ | n. |磷酸盐 | Phosphates are salts or esters of phosphoric acid. They are essential nutrients for plants and animals, playing a vital role in various biological processes.  They are also used in various industrial applications. | Example:  Plants require phosphates for healthy growth.  植物需要磷酸盐才能健康生长。  Fertilizers often contain phosphates to boost crop yields.  肥料通常含有磷酸盐以提高作物产量。|近义词：phosphoric acid salts |反义词：无
