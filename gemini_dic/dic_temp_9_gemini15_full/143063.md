outbounds:/ˈaʊtbaʊndz/| n.|出站航班；出局；出界|Outbound flights refer to flights departing from a particular location.  It can also refer to something going out of bounds in a game or exceeding a limit.|例句：The outbound flight was delayed due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，出站航班延误了。

例句：His shot went out of bounds. = 他的球出界了。|近义词：departures, outgoing, off-limits|反义词：inbounds, inbound
