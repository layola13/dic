loots:/luːts/| n.|战利品，赃物|Stolen goods or property, especially those taken by force.|The soldiers divided the loots among themselves after the battle.  士兵们在战斗结束后瓜分了战利品。| plunder, spoils, booty |  - |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army loots the village.  军队洗劫了村庄。
He often loots small shops. 他经常抢劫小商店。


一般过去时:
The pirates looted the ship. 海盗洗劫了那艘船。
They looted the museum of its valuable artifacts. 他们洗劫了博物馆的珍贵文物。


现在完成时:
The rebels have looted many towns. 叛军已经洗劫了许多城镇。
They have looted the bank of millions of dollars. 他们已经从银行抢劫了数百万美元。


过去完成时:
The thieves had looted the house before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经洗劫了房子。
The army had looted the city before the civilians could escape.  平民逃脱之前，军队已经洗劫了这座城市。


将来时:
The bandits will loot the caravan.  强盗将洗劫商队。
They will loot the town under the cover of darkness. 他们将在夜幕的掩护下洗劫城镇。


虚拟语气:
If the robbers had not been caught, they would have looted more houses. 如果强盗没有被抓住，他们还会洗劫更多的房屋。
If they looted the bank, they would be rich. 如果他们抢劫了银行，他们就会富有。


被动语态:
The village was looted by the invading army. 村庄被入侵的军队洗劫了。
The bank was looted last night. 银行昨晚被抢劫了。


疑问句:
Were the houses looted? 房屋被洗劫了吗？
Has the museum been looted? 博物馆被洗劫了吗？


否定句:
The soldiers did not loot the town. 士兵们没有洗劫城镇。
The thieves did not loot the houses. 小偷没有洗劫房屋。
