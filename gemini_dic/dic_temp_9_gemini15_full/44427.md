cross-posted:/ˈkrɒsˌpoʊstɪd/| adj. |交叉发布的，同时发布的 |  Posted on multiple online platforms or forums simultaneously.  | Example:  The announcement was cross-posted on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. 这则公告同时发布在Facebook、Twitter和Instagram上。 | 近义词：simultaneously posted, reposted across platforms | 反义词：exclusively posted, single-platform posted |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article is cross-posted on several websites. = 这篇文章在多个网站上交叉发布。
They cross-post their blog entries to different social media platforms. = 他们将博客文章交叉发布到不同的社交媒体平台。


一般过去时:
The company cross-posted the job opening on LinkedIn and Indeed. = 公司在领英和Indeed上交叉发布了职位空缺。
She cross-posted her artwork on DeviantArt and ArtStation. = 她将她的艺术作品交叉发布在DeviantArt和ArtStation上。
