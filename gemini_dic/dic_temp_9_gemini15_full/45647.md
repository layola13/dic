cursory:/ˈkɜːrsəri/| adj. |粗略的，草率的 | hasty and not thorough | He gave the document a cursory glance before signing it. 他在签署文件之前草率地浏览了一下。 |近义词：superficial, perfunctory, hasty |反义词：thorough, detailed, meticulous

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor gives cursory lectures. = 教授的讲座很粗略。
His examination of the evidence was cursory. = 他对证据的审查很草率。

一般过去时:
She gave the problem a cursory examination. = 她草率地检查了这个问题。
They made a cursory inspection of the building. = 他们粗略地检查了这座建筑物。

现在完成时:
He has given many cursory explanations. = 他给出过许多粗略的解释。
She has made a cursory review of the report. = 她草率地审阅了报告。

过去完成时:
They had made a cursory assessment of the situation. = 他们事先对情况进行了粗略的评估。
He had given the matter a cursory thought. = 他对这件事只粗略地想过一下。


将来时:
She will give the project a cursory overview. = 她将对项目进行粗略的概述。
They will conduct a cursory search for the missing item. = 他们将粗略地搜索丢失的物品。


虚拟语气:
If he had given it more than a cursory glance, he might have noticed the error. = 如果他不只是粗略地看了一眼，他可能会注意到错误。
I wish I hadn't given such a cursory response. = 我真希望我没有给出如此草率的回答。


被动语态:
The proposal was given a cursory review. = 该提案受到了粗略的审查。
The evidence was examined cursorily. = 证据受到了草率的审查。


疑问句:
Was your review of the document cursory? = 你对文件的审查是粗略的吗？
Is a cursory inspection sufficient for this task? = 粗略的检查足以完成这项任务吗？


否定句:
His analysis wasn't cursory; it was quite thorough. = 他的分析并非粗略；而是相当彻底的。
The investigation was not cursory; it was very detailed. = 调查并非草率；而是非常详细的。

词源:
The word "cursory" comes from the Latin word "cursorius," meaning "running," "running quickly," or "hasty."  This reflects the idea of a quick, superficial action.
