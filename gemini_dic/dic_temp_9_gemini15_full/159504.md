polishes:/ˈpɒlɪʃɪz/ | /ˈpɑːlɪʃɪz/ | n. v. |擦亮；抛光；润色；改进|The act of making something smooth and shiny by rubbing it.  It can also refer to the process of improving or refining something.|例句：She polishes her shoes every morning. = 她每天早上都擦亮她的鞋子。

She polishes her writing until it's perfect. = 她润色她的写作直到完美。

近义词：burnish, shine, refine, improve | 反义词：roughen, dull, worsen | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He polishes his car every weekend. = 他每个周末都擦他的车。
She polishes the silver before every dinner party. = 她在每次晚宴前都擦拭银器。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
She has polished all the silverware. = 她已经擦亮了所有的银器。
He has polished his presentation for tomorrow's meeting. = 他已经润色了他明天会议的演示文稿。

过去完成时:

She had polished her essay several times before submitting it. = 在提交之前，她已经润色了她的论文好几次。

将来时:
I will polish my shoes before the interview. = 我将在面试前擦亮我的鞋子。
