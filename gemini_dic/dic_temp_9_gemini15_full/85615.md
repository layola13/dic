haul:/hɔːl/| /hɔːl/| n., vt.|拖，拉；大量；捕获物| to pull or drag with effort; a large quantity of something; the amount of fish caught in one fishing trip|例句：The farmer hauled the heavy wagon across the field. 农民费力地把重重的马车拉过田地。|例句：The police made a large haul of drugs. 警方缴获了大量毒品。|近义词：drag, pull, tow, transport, loot, booty, catch|反义词：push, leave, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hauls the heavy boxes every day. = 他每天都搬运沉重的箱子。
She hauls the groceries home from the store. = 她从商店把杂货拉回家。

一般过去时:
They hauled the boat onto the shore. = 他们把船拖到岸上。
The fishermen hauled in a big catch. = 渔民们捕获了一大批鱼。

现在完成时:
We have hauled all the furniture upstairs. = 我们已经把所有的家具都搬到楼上了。
The company has hauled millions of tons of cargo this year. = 今年该公司已经运输了数百万吨的货物。

过去完成时:
They had hauled the logs to the sawmill before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已将原木运送到锯木厂。
The workers had hauled away all the debris before the inspection. = 检查人员到来之前，工人们已经清除了所有碎石瓦砾。

将来时:
We will haul the timber to the construction site tomorrow. = 我们明天将把木材运送到建筑工地。
The contractor will haul away the waste materials next week. = 承包商下周将清除废料。

虚拟语气:
If I had a truck, I would haul the furniture myself. = 如果我有一辆卡车，我会自己搬家具。
If they hauled the equipment earlier, the project would have finished sooner. = 如果他们早点把设备运来，项目早就完成了。

被动语态:
The heavy equipment was hauled by a crane. = 重型设备是由起重机吊运的。
The stolen goods were hauled away by the police. = 赃物被警方运走了。

疑问句:
How much did they haul in from the auction? = 他们从拍卖会上获得了多少收益？
What kind of cargo are you hauling today? = 你今天运送的是什么货物？

否定句:
They didn't haul the materials on time. = 他们没有按时运送材料。
She couldn't haul the suitcase up the stairs. = 她无法把行李箱搬上楼。
