praxis:/ˈpræksɪs/| n.|实践，实践活动，实际运用 |The practical application of a theory,  a set of practices, or a method.  It often refers to the application of knowledge or skills in a real-world context.  |例句：The teacher emphasized the importance of praxis in learning a new language.  老师强调了在学习一门外语中实践的重要性。


例句：Her artistic praxis involved a combination of painting and performance art.她的艺术实践结合了绘画和行为艺术。


近义词：practice, implementation, application |反义词：theory, speculation, abstraction|词性：名词
