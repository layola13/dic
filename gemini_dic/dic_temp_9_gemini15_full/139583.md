obviation:/ˌɒbviˈeɪʃən/| n. |消除；避免；排除；使无必要|The act of obviating something; the removal of a difficulty, objection, or the like.  Obviation refers to the process of preventing something from happening or removing an obstacle.  It implies a proactive approach to problem-solving, rather than simply reacting to a problem after it arises.|例句：The new system aims for the obviation of errors. 新系统旨在消除错误。

The improved design led to the obviation of many previous problems. 改进的设计避免了许多以前的问题。|近义词：avoidance, prevention, elimination, removal|反义词：creation, causation, aggravation
