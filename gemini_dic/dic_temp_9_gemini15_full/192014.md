scorodite:/ˈskɔːrədaɪt/| n. | 毒砂 | A hydrated iron arsenate mineral, FeAsO₄·2H₂O, often found in hydrothermal veins and as a weathering product of arsenopyrite.  It is usually green to greenish-yellow, but can also be brown or colorless. Scorodite is a secondary mineral, meaning it forms from the alteration of other minerals. It is an important ore of arsenic. | Example: Scorodite crystals were discovered in the abandoned mine.  毒砂晶体在废弃的矿井中被发现。 | 近义词: arsenate mineral | 反义词: N/A
