payability: /ˌpeɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可支付性，偿付能力 | The ability to be paid or the state of being payable;  the ability of a business to meet its financial obligations as they become due.  | 例句：The payability of the invoice is questionable due to the client's recent financial difficulties.  发票的可支付性由于客户最近的财务困难而令人质疑。  The company's high payability rating made it attractive to investors.  该公司较高的偿付能力评级使其对投资者具有吸引力。 | 近义词：solvency, liquidity | 反义词：insolvency, illiquidity |  名词
