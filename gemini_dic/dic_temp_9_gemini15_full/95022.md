inaccessibilities: /ˌɪnæksesəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 难达性；不可接近性；难以进入 | The inaccessibilities of the remote mountain region made rescue efforts extremely challenging.  偏远山区的难以到达使得救援工作极具挑战性。 |近义词:unapproachability, impassability, remoteness | 反义词: accessibility, approachability


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable.  Instead, we will illustrate its usage in different sentence structures.


单数句:
The inaccessibility of the summit deterred many climbers. = 山顶的难以到达阻止了许多登山者。

复数句:
The many inaccessibilities of the island slowed down exploration. = 岛屿的诸多难以到达之处减缓了探险的进度。


所有格:
The inaccessibilities' impact on the economy was significant. = 难以到达对经济的影响是巨大的。(Note:  This usage is less common and can sound awkward.)


介词短语:
Because of the inaccessibilities, the project was delayed. = 由于难以到达，项目被延迟了。


定语从句:
The challenges, which were largely due to the inaccessibilities, proved insurmountable. = 这些挑战，很大程度上是由于难以到达造成的，证明是无法克服的。


感叹句:
The inaccessibilities of the area! = 这地区的难以到达！


PHRASE->
