jibe:/dʒaɪb/ | v. n. | 嘲笑；符合；转向；(船)突然改变方向 | To criticize someone, often in a teasing way; to be in agreement; to change direction suddenly; (of a ship) to change direction suddenly.  |例句：He jibed at her attempts to cook. 他嘲笑她做饭的尝试。|近义词：taunt, gibe, agree, correspond |反义词：praise, compliment, disagree
