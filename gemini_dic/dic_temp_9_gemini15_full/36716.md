collocate:/ˈkɒləkeɪt/ /ˈkɑːləkeɪt/| v.|搭配；共现|To exist or occur together; to place or arrange together.  The verb typically refers to the way words frequently appear together in a language.  It emphasizes the natural co-occurrence of words in specific contexts.|例句：The words "strong" and "coffee" often collocate.  强咖啡这两个词经常搭配使用。

例句：The artist carefully collocated the colours to create a harmonious effect. 艺术家仔细地搭配颜色以创造和谐的效果。


近义词：combine, associate, juxtapose|反义词：separate, dissociate|词性：verb
