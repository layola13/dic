mastercard:/ˈmæstərkɑːrd/| n. |万事达卡|A major international payment technology company and its associated credit and debit cards.  Mastercard operates a global payments network and provides processing services for financial institutions.  It is one of the world's largest payment processing companies, rivaling Visa. |例句：I paid for the hotel with my Mastercard. 我用我的万事达卡付了酒店的钱。|例句：Many businesses accept Mastercard as a form of payment. 许多商家接受万事达卡付款。|近义词：Visa, American Express, Discover|反义词：cash, check
