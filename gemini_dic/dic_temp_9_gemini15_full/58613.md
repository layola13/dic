dullsville:/ˈdʌlzvɪl/| n. |沉闷乏味的地方；无聊透顶的地方 |A place or situation that is extremely boring and unexciting.  It is often used informally and hyperbolically to express extreme boredom or lack of excitement. |例句：That party was total dullsville.  = 那次派对无聊透顶。

例句：Life in this town is dullsville. = 这个小镇的生活枯燥乏味。


近义词：boredom, tedium, monotony, drag, snoozefest

反义词：excitement, thrill, fun, exhilaration


词性：名词
