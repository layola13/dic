self-helping:/ˌselfˈhɛlpiŋ/ | adj. |自助的；自我帮助的 | Relating to or denoting methods or techniques for improving one's own life, typically without professional help.  | 例句：She found self-helping books helpful in overcoming her anxiety. 她发现自助书籍对克服焦虑很有帮助。 | 例句：The self-helping group provided a supportive environment for members to share their experiences. 这个自助小组为成员分享经验提供了支持性的环境。| 近义词：self-reliant, independent,自助的 | 反义词：dependent, reliant on others, 需要他人帮助的 |  形容词
