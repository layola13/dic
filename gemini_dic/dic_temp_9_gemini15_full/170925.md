pseudomorphism:/ˌsuːdəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |假象同晶; 假晶|The imitation of the external form of a crystal by a mineral of different chemical composition.|The calcite pseudomorph after fluorite shows the original crystal habit of the fluorite. 方解石假晶体模仿萤石的外部形状。|近义词：paramorphism, metamorphism|反义词：isomorphism
