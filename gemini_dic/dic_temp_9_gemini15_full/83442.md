habitation:/ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |居住；栖息；住所 |The act of inhabiting or the state of being inhabited; residence; dwelling.  It can also refer to the place where someone lives or a particular type of dwelling. |例句：The habitation of the island was sparse.  岛上的居住人口稀少。 |例句：The quality of their habitation improved significantly after the renovations.  装修后，他们的住所质量有了显著提高。 |近义词：dwelling, residence, occupancy, settlement |反义词：desertion, abandonment
