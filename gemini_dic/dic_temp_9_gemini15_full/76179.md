fusses:/ˈfʌsɪz/| v.| 烦扰；小题大做；忙乱；(尤指对琐碎事)过分操心 |To worry or be concerned excessively about unimportant things; to make a fuss.  |例句：She fusses over her children all day. 她整天为她的孩子操心。|例句：Don't fuss, everything will be alright. 不要着急，一切都会好起来的。|近义词：worry, fret, bother, dither|反义词：calm, relax, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fusses over every detail. = 他对每一个细节都过分操心。
She fusses about the house all day. = 她整天在家里忙乱。


一般过去时:
He fussed over his appearance before the interview. = 面试前他过分在意自己的外表。
She fussed and fretted until he arrived. = 她一直焦急不安地等待着他到来。


现在完成时:
She has fussed over this project for weeks. = 她为这个项目操心了好几个星期了。
He has fussed too much about nothing. = 他为一些无关紧要的事情过于操心了。


过去完成时:
She had fussed over the cake for hours before the party. = 派对前，她为蛋糕忙活了几个小时。
He had fussed and worried until he received the news. = 直到收到消息，他才停止焦虑不安。


将来时:
I know she will fuss if I’m late. = 我知道如果我迟到了她会着急的。
He will fuss over the arrangements for the wedding. = 他会为婚礼的安排操心。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't fuss so much, I'd be less stressed. = 如果我不那么操心，我的压力就会小一些。
I wish I hadn't fussed so much about the small details. = 我真希望我没有为那些小细节过于操心。


被动语态:
The matter was fussed over by the committee. = 这件事被委员会过度关注了。
The cake was fussed over until it was perfect. = 蛋糕被精心制作直到完美。


疑问句:
Does she fuss about everything? = 她是不是事事都操心？
Why is he fussing so much? = 他为什么这么着急？


否定句:
He didn't fuss about the minor problems. = 他没有为那些小问题而烦恼。
She didn't fuss when her plans changed. = 当她的计划改变时，她并没有感到不安。


PHRASE->
fuss over: 过分关注
fuss about: 为...忙乱
make a fuss: 小题大做
fuss and bother: 烦扰
don't fuss: 不要着急
