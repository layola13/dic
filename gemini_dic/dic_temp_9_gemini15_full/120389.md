minish: /ˈmɪnɪʃ/ | vt. | 缩小；减少；减轻 | To make or become less or smaller.  | The rain diminished the crowd. 雨水减少了人群。 The doctor's treatment diminished his pain. 医生的治疗减轻了他的痛苦。 | lessen, reduce, decrease | increase, enlarge | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He diminishes his workload every week. = 他每周都减少他的工作量。
The sun diminishes in size as it sets. = 太阳落山时体积变小。


一般过去时:
The intensity of the storm diminished quickly. = 风暴的强度迅速减弱。
The value of the property diminished over time. = 房地产价值随着时间的推移而下降。


现在完成时:
The company has diminished its environmental impact. = 公司已经减少了其对环境的影响。
My enthusiasm has diminished since the project started. = 自从项目开始以来，我的热情减退了。


过去完成时:
The floodwaters had diminished considerably by morning. = 到早上洪水已经大大消退了。
His confidence had diminished after the failure. = 他在失败后信心下降了。


将来时:
The effect of the medicine will diminish over time. = 药物的效果会随着时间的推移而减弱。
The population of the town is expected to diminish in the coming years. = 预计该镇人口在未来几年将会减少。


虚拟语气:
If the government diminished taxes, the economy might improve. = 如果政府降低税收，经济可能会改善。
I wish the noise would diminish. = 我希望噪音能减小。


被动语态:
The problem was diminished by the new solution. = 新的解决方案减少了这个问题。
His influence has been diminished by recent events. = 他最近受到的事件影响降低了他的影响力。


疑问句:
Does this action diminish our chances of success? = 这个行动是否会降低我们成功的几率？
Will the new policy diminish the number of accidents? = 新政策会减少事故数量吗？


否定句:
The new policy did not diminish the problem. = 新政策并没有减少这个问题。
The rain did not diminish the enthusiasm of the crowd. = 雨水并没有减少人群的热情。


PHRASE->
diminish in size = 缩小
diminish in number = 数量减少
diminish in importance = 重要性降低
diminish in value = 贬值
diminish the risk = 降低风险
