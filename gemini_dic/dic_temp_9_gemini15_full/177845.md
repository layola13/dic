receptaculum:/rɛsɛpˈtækjʊləm/| n. |容器，接受器，贮藏器 |A receptaculum is a container or receptacle, often used in a scientific or technical context to refer to a structure that receives or holds something.  It can also refer to a structure in plants that receives pollen. |Example: The pollen receptaculum of the flower is sticky. 花粉接受器的花是粘的。The lab used a special receptaculum to store the radioactive material. 实验室使用特殊的容器来储存放射性材料。|近义词：container, vessel, receptacle |反义词：source, origin
