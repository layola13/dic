cavities:/ˈkævətiz/ | n. | 腔；空洞；龋齿 | Cavities are hollow spaces within a solid object or body.  In dentistry, cavities refer to tooth decay. | Example: The dentist found several cavities in her teeth. 牙医在她牙齿上发现了几个龋洞。

The ancient Roman aqueducts were built with cavities to carry water. 古罗马的渡槽是用有空腔的结构建造的，用来输送水。|近义词：holes, hollows, spaces | 反义词：solidity, fullness
