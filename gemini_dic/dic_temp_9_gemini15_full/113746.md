macrology:/mæˈkrɒlədʒi/| n. |宏观学，宏观生物学 |The study of large-scale structures and processes.  It often refers to the study of large organisms or ecological systems.  |Example:  Macrology is a field of biology that examines ecosystems and their interactions. = 宏观生物学是生物学的一个分支，研究生态系统及其相互作用。|近义词：macroscopic biology, large-scale ecology|反义词：micrology, microscopy
