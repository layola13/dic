seaport:/ˈsiːpɔːrt/| n. |海港，港口 |A seaport is a town or city with a harbor where ships can load and unload cargo.  It's a significant center for maritime trade and commerce.|例句：Shanghai is a bustling seaport known for its extensive trade activities. 上海是一个以其广泛的贸易活动而闻名的繁华海港。|例句：The ancient seaport of Tyre was once a major center of the Phoenician civilization. 古代的推罗港曾经是腓尼基文明的主要中心。|近义词：harbor, port |反义词：inland
