reacclimates:/ˌriːˈækləmeɪts/| v. |重新适应；使重新适应 |To become accustomed to a new climate or environment again after a period of time away.  It implies a previous period of adaptation that has been disrupted.|例句：After spending a month in the tropics, it took him a week to reacclimate to the cold weather.  在热带地区待了一个月后，他花了整整一周时间才重新适应寒冷的天气。

例句：The soldiers quickly reacclimated to the harsh desert conditions after their leave.  士兵们休假后很快重新适应了恶劣的沙漠环境。


近义词：adjust, adapt, acclimatize | 反义词：maladapt
