polished:/ˈpɒlɪʃt/ /ˈpɑːlɪʃt/| adj.|抛光的；完美的；优雅的；圆滑的|  having a smooth, shiny surface as a result of being polished; refined and elegant in manner or style; skillfully done or presented. |例句：The polished table gleamed under the lights.  =  桌子在灯光下闪闪发光。

例句：Her polished presentation impressed the audience. = 她完美的陈述给观众留下了深刻的印象。


近义词：refined, sophisticated, elegant, smooth, perfect

反义词：rough, unrefined, crude, imperfect, coarse

词性：形容词
