loathing: /ˈloʊðɪŋ/| n. |厌恶；憎恨 |a feeling of intense dislike or disgust.  |例句：His loathing of hypocrisy was well known.  他的虚伪令人厌恶是众所周知的。

例句：I felt a deep loathing for the man who had betrayed me. 我对背叛我的人感到深深的厌恶。


近义词：abhorrence, aversion, disgust, hatred, contempt |反义词：love, affection, fondness, adoration
