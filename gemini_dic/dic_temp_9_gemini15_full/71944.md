flied:/flaɪd/| v.|飞过；飞行；过去式|flew的过去分词形式，表示飞行的完成动作。|The bird flied over the mountains.  鸟飞过了山。The kite flied high in the sky. 风筝飞上了高空。|flew, flown|grounded|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flies a kite every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都放风筝。
The birds fly south for the winter. = 鸟儿冬天飞到南方去。

一般过去时:
The plane flew to London yesterday. = 飞机昨天飞往伦敦。
He flew a kite in the park. = 他在公园里放风筝。

现在完成时:
The bird has flown away. = 鸟飞走了。
I have flown to many countries. = 我去过很多国家。

过去完成时:
The bird had flown away before I arrived. = 我到达之前鸟已经飞走了。
They had flown to Paris before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经飞到巴黎了。

将来时:
The plane will fly to New York tomorrow. = 飞机明天将飞往纽约。
We will fly to Hawaii for our vacation. = 我们将飞往夏威夷度假。

虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly. = 如果我有翅膀，我会飞。
I wish I could fly. = 我希望我能飞。

被动语态:
The kite was flown by a child. = 风筝是一个孩子放飞的。
The plane was flown safely. = 飞机安全地飞行了。

疑问句:
Did the bird fly away? = 鸟飞走了吗？
Has the plane flown yet? = 飞机起飞了吗？

否定句:
The bird didn't fly away. = 鸟没有飞走。
The plane hasn't flown yet. = 飞机还没有起飞。
