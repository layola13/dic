marvellingly:/ˈmɑːvəlɪŋli/ | adv. |令人惊叹地，奇迹般地 |In a way that causes great wonder or astonishment. The sunset was marvellously beautiful, painting the sky with vibrant colours. 日落美得令人惊叹，天空被染上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：wonderfully, amazingly, astonishingly, spectacularly |反义词：dully, unexceptionally, ordinarily
