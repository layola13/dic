nonmortally: /ˌnɒnˈmɔːrtəli/ | adv. |非致命地，非致死地 | Not causing death; not fatally.  |The soldier was nonmortally wounded in the battle.  士兵在战斗中受了非致命伤。|近义词：slightly, mildly, superficially |反义词：fatally, lethally
