chieftain:/ˈtʃiːftən/| n. |酋长，族长 |A chieftain is the leader of a tribe or clan.  Chieftains often hold considerable power and influence within their communities. They may be chosen through heredity, election, or other means depending on the specific customs of the group. |Example: The chieftain led his tribe in a fierce battle against their enemies.  酋长带领他的部落与敌人进行了激烈的战斗。 The chieftain addressed his people with words of wisdom and encouragement. 酋长用充满智慧和鼓励的话语向他的子民讲话。|近义词：leader, headman, tribal leader |反义词：follower, subject
