rentage:/ˈrɛntɪdʒ/| n.|租金；租赁；租期|The sum of money paid for the use of something, typically property or land.  It also refers to the period of time for which something is rented.|例句：The rentage for the apartment is quite high. 这套公寓的租金相当高。

例句：The rentage of the farm includes the use of all equipment.  这农场租金包含所有设备的使用。


近义词：rental, lease, hire, charge |反义词：ownership
