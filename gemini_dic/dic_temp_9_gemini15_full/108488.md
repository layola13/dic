layaways: /ˈleɪəweɪz/ | n. |分期付款购买；预购；分期付款商品 | A system of purchasing goods where the buyer makes a series of payments over time, and takes possession of the goods only after the final payment is made.  | Example:  She bought the television on layaway. = 她分期付款买了电视。

The store offers a layaway plan for large purchases. = 这家商店为大件商品提供分期付款计划。


近义词：installment plan, payment plan

反义词：cash purchase, immediate purchase


词性：名词
