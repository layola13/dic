genomics:/dʒɪˈnoʊmɪks/ | n. |基因组学|The study of genomes, including the structure, function, evolution, and mapping of genomes.  Genomics involves analyzing the complete set of DNA within a single cell of an organism. It encompasses various techniques like DNA sequencing, bioinformatics, and molecular biology to understand the genetic makeup of organisms and their interactions.| Example:  Advances in genomics have led to breakthroughs in disease diagnosis and treatment. 基因组学方面的进步导致了疾病诊断和治疗的突破。|近义词：genetal analysis, genome research |反义词：N/A
