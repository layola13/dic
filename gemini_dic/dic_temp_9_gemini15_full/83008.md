gunreach:/ˈɡʌnriːtʃ/| n.|枪的射程，枪械有效射程|The effective range of a firearm; the distance a gun can accurately and effectively hit a target.|例句：The gunreach of this rifle is 500 meters. 这支步枪的射程是500米。

例句：We need to stay outside the gunreach of the enemy. 我们需要待在敌人的射程之外。


近义词：firing range, effective range, range


反义词：close range (相对词，并非完全反义)


词性:名词
