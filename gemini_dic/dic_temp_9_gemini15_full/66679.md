exoduses:/ˈɛksədəsɪz/| n. |出埃及记；大批离去；大规模迁移|The plural of exodus, referring to multiple instances of a mass departure or emigration.  It often implies a large-scale movement of people, often driven by hardship or oppression. |例句：The recent political upheaval led to several exoduses from the country. 最近的政治动荡导致该国发生了多次大规模外逃。

例句：The history books detail the various exoduses of refugees throughout the centuries. 历史书籍详细记载了几个世纪以来难民的多次大规模迁移。

近义词：migrations, departures, emigrations, mass movements

反义词：inmigrations, arrivals, returns
