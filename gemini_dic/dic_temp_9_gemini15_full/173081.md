quartet:/kwɔːˈtɛt/| n. |四重奏；四人一组；四部曲 |A quartet is a group of four people or things, often musical performers playing together.  It can also refer to a musical composition written for four instruments or voices. |例句：The string quartet played a beautiful piece by Beethoven.  弦乐四重奏演奏了贝多芬的一首美丽的乐曲。

The famous author finished his literary quartet. 著名的作家完成了他的四部曲。

|近义词：quadruplet, group of four |反义词：solo, duet, trio |名词
