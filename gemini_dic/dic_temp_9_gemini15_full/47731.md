decagons:/ˈdekəɡɑːnz/| n. |十边形 |A decagon is a polygon with ten sides and ten angles.  Decagons can be regular, meaning all sides and angles are equal, or irregular, with unequal sides and angles.  The sum of the interior angles of a decagon is 1440 degrees. |例句：The architect designed a building with a decagonal base. 建筑师设计了一座底座为十边形的建筑物。

The child drew a picture of a regular decagon using a ruler and compass.  孩子用尺子和圆规画了一幅正十边形的图画。|近义词：polygon |反义词：triangle, quadrilateral, pentagon (referring to polygons with fewer sides)
