silliest: /ˈsɪliəst/ | adj. | 最愚蠢的，最傻的 |  The superlative form of "silly," indicating the highest degree of silliness. It describes something or someone exhibiting extreme foolishness or lack of intelligence. | 例句：That's the silliest thing I've ever heard!  那是听过最蠢的事情了！


That was the silliest joke I ever heard.  那是听过最傻的笑话了。

近义词：foolishest, most absurd, most ridiculous, most inane | 反义词：wisest, most intelligent, most sensible, most clever | 词性：形容词
