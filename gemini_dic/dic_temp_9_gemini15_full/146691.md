overheartiness:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrtiːnəs/ | n. |过分热情；过度热心；过分殷勤 | Excessive warmth or enthusiasm; overly effusive friendliness.  It often implies a degree of insincerity or inappropriateness. |  His overheartiness in welcoming the new employees felt forced and unnatural.  他的过度热情欢迎新员工感觉很做作，不自然。|近义词：effusiveness, gushiness, exuberance | 反义词：aloofness, coldness, reserve | 名词
