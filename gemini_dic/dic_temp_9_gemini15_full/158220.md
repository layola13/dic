platypus:/ˈplætɪpʌs/| n. |鸭嘴兽|A semi-aquatic, egg-laying mammal native to eastern Australia, with a duck-like bill, beaver-like tail, and otter-like body.|例句：The platypus is a unique and fascinating creature. 鸭嘴兽是一种独特而迷人的生物。|例句：The platypus's venomous spur is a remarkable defense mechanism. 鸭嘴兽的毒刺是一种非凡的防御机制。|近义词：monotreme (a more scientific term)|反义词：None.
