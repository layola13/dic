osculum:/ˈɒskjʊləm/| n. |吻;小口; (海绵动物的)出水孔|A small opening, especially the mouth of a sponge or a small opening in a structure.|The osculum is the main exhalant opening of a sponge, through which water exits after passing through the sponge's body.|近义词：pore, aperture, opening|反义词：inlet, orifice (in the sense of entrance)
