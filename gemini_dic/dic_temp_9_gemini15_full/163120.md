precolor:/ˈpriːkʌlər/| adj.| 预先着色；预先染色 |  Describes something that has been colored or dyed before another process.  It often refers to materials like fabrics or yarns that have been pre-treated with color before being used in manufacturing or further processing. | Example: The precolored yarn made the knitting process much faster. 预先染色的纱线使编织过程快得多。|近义词：pre-dyed, dyed beforehand|反义词：uncolored, undyed
