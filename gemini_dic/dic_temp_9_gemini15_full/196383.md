sermonized:/ˈsɜːrmənaɪzd/| v.|说教；教训；冗长地训斥|To deliver a sermon or preach; to lecture someone in a long, tedious, and moralistic way.  |例句：The preacher sermonized on the evils of greed.  牧师冗长地训斥贪婪的罪恶。
The teacher sermonized the students about the importance of hard work. 老师对学生们说教，强调勤奋的重要性。|近义词：lectured, preached, harangued, scolded|反义词：praised, complimented

USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sermonizes every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都布道。
She sermonizes to her children about the importance of good manners. = 她向孩子们说教，强调良好礼貌的重要性。


一般过去时:
The priest sermonized on forgiveness. = 牧师就宽恕这个话题布道。
My mother sermonized me about my messy room. = 我妈妈就我的凌乱房间训斥了我。


现在完成时:
He has sermonized on this topic many times. = 他已经就这个话题布道很多次了。
She has sermonized to me about my spending habits. = 她已经就我的消费习惯训斥过我了。


过去完成时:
He had sermonized for an hour before the congregation grew restless. = 他已经布道了一个小时，会众才开始变得不安。
She had sermonized to him repeatedly about the dangers of smoking. = 她已经反复地就吸烟的危害训斥过他了。


将来时:
The pastor will sermonize on the parable of the Good Samaritan. = 牧师将就好撒玛利亚人的比喻布道。
He will sermonize to his employees about punctuality. = 他将就准时这个问题训斥他的员工。


虚拟语气:
If he sermonized less, people might listen more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he hadn't sermonized so much at the dinner party. = 我真希望他在晚宴上不要说教那么多。


被动语态:
The congregation was sermonized to about the importance of charity. = 会众被教导要重视慈善。
The students were sermonized on the dangers of drugs. = 学生们被训斥吸毒的危害。


疑问句:
Did he sermonize at the meeting? = 他在会议上说教了吗？
Will she sermonize at the wedding? = 她会在婚礼上说教吗？


否定句:
He didn't sermonize during the service. = 他在仪式期间没有说教。
She didn't sermonize to her children about their bad grades. = 她没有因为孩子们的坏成绩而训斥他们。


PHRASE->
sermonize endlessly = 没完没了地说教
sermonize on a subject = 就某个话题说教
sermonize against something = 反对某事说教
sermonize with someone = 对某人说教
sermonize from the pulpit = 从讲台上布道
