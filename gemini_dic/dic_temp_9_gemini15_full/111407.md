livor:/ˈlaɪvər/ | n. |淤血；青紫；死后尸斑 |  Livor refers to the discoloration of the skin due to the settling of blood after death (postmortem lividity) or from a bruise (a contusion). It is characterized by a purplish-red hue.  | Example: The livor mortis helped determine the time of death. 死后尸斑有助于确定死亡时间。The livor on his arm indicated a recent injury. 他胳膊上的淤血表明最近受过伤。 |近义词：bruise, discoloration, contusion |反义词：pale, healthy skin
