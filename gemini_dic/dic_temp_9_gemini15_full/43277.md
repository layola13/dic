craftsman:/ˈkræftsmən/ | n. |工匠，技工 |A skilled worker who makes things by hand.  A craftsman is someone who is highly skilled in their craft, often producing high-quality, unique items.  |例句：He is a craftsman who specializes in creating exquisite wooden furniture. 他是一位专门制作精美木制家具的工匠。

例句：The old craftsman patiently showed his apprentice the secrets of his trade.  这位老工匠耐心地向他的学徒传授技艺的秘密。


近义词：artisan, artificer, skilled worker | 反义词：amateur, novice, unskilled worker


词源:  The word "craftsman" comes from the Old English words "cræft" (meaning "craft, skill, strength") and "mann" (meaning "man").  It's a compound word reflecting the skilled work done by men historically.  The term is now gender-neutral.
