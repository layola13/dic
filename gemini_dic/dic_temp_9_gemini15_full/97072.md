infernally:/ɪnˈfɜːrnəli/| adv. |极度地；非常；地狱般地 | Extremely; to an excessive degree; in a hellish manner.  |例句：The heat was infernally hot.  这热得要命。


例句：The mosquitoes were infernally bothersome.  蚊子烦人极了。


近义词：extremely, intensely, terribly, awfully, devilishly


反义词：mildly, slightly, gently


词源：源自"infernal" (地狱的；极坏的)，加上"-ly"构成副词。  "Infernal" itself comes from Latin "infernus" (hell).
