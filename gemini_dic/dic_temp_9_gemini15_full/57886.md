dries:/draɪz/| v.| 干燥；使干燥 |To become dry or to make something dry.|The laundry dries quickly in the sun.  衣服在阳光下很快就会干。|The hot air dried the paint. 热空气使油漆变干。|近义词：dehydrate, desiccate|反义词：wet, moisten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun dries the clothes. = 太阳晒干了衣服。
The paint dries slowly in damp weather. = 潮湿的天气下油漆干得很慢。


一般过去时:
The clothes dried overnight. = 衣服一夜之间就干了。
He dried the dishes after dinner. = 他晚饭后擦干了盘子。


现在完成时:
The rain has dried the ground. = 雨水已经把地面弄干了。
The paint has dried, so we can continue. = 油漆已经干了，所以我们可以继续了。


过去完成时:
The clothes had dried before we left. = 我们离开之前衣服已经干了。
The ground had dried out after the long drought. = 经过长时间的干旱后，地面已经干涸了。


将来时:
The clothes will dry quickly in this wind. = 在这种风力下，衣服很快就会干。
The laundry will be dry by tomorrow morning. = 到明天早上，衣服就会干了。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were warmer, the clothes would dry faster. = 如果天气暖和一点，衣服就会干得更快。
I wish the paint would dry quickly. = 我希望油漆能快点干。


被动语态:
The clothes are dried in the sun. = 衣服在阳光下晒干。
The dishes were dried by the machine. = 盘子是用机器擦干的。


疑问句:
Does the laundry dry easily? = 洗衣物容易干吗？
Have the flowers dried out yet? = 花儿干了吗？


否定句:
The clothes didn't dry because of the rain. = 因为下雨，衣服没干。
The paint won't dry if it's too humid. = 如果湿度太高，油漆就不会干。


PHRASE->
