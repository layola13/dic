readmits:/ˌriːædˈmɪts/| vt.|重新接纳；再次允许进入|To admit or allow someone or something to enter or join again.  |例句：The university readmits students who have been suspended for good behavior.|近义词：reaccept, reinstate, readmit|反义词：expel, dismiss, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school readmits students who demonstrate academic improvement. = 学校重新接纳那些在学业上有所进步的学生。
They readmit members who have paid their dues. = 他们重新接纳那些已经缴纳会费的会员。


一般过去时:
The club readmitted him after he apologized. =  俱乐部在他道歉后重新接纳了他。
The hospital readmitted the patient for further observation. = 医院为了进一步观察而再次收治了这位病人。


现在完成时:
The company has readmitted several former employees. = 公司已经重新接纳了几名前雇员。
The university has readmitted her after a year's suspension. =  大学在她停学一年后重新接纳了她。


过去完成时:
She had been readmitted to the program before the deadline. =  她在截止日期前已经被重新接纳到这个项目中了。
The judge had readmitted the evidence after reviewing it. =  法官在复审后重新接受了证据。


将来时:
The committee will readmit him if he meets the requirements. = 如果他满足要求，委员会将会重新接纳他。
They will readmit the students once the investigation is complete. = 一旦调查结束，他们就会重新接纳这些学生。


虚拟语气:
If he apologized, they would readmit him. = 如果他道歉，他们就会重新接纳他。
I wish the school would readmit me. = 我希望学校能重新接纳我。


被动语态:
He was readmitted to the hospital last night. = 他昨晚被重新送进了医院。
The case was readmitted to the court for further consideration. = 此案被重新提交法院进一步审理。


疑问句:
Will they readmit her to the team? = 他们会把她重新接纳回队伍吗？
Was he readmitted to the college after his suspension? = 他停学后被重新接纳回大学了吗？


否定句:
They did not readmit him to the group. = 他们没有把他重新接纳回小组。
The judge refused to readmit the evidence. = 法官拒绝重新接受证据。


PHRASE->
readmit to a program = 重新接纳到某个项目
readmit to a hospital = 重新送进医院
readmit to a club = 重新加入俱乐部
readmit to membership = 重新恢复会员资格
readmit to the school = 重新回到学校
