degassing:/diˈɡæsɪŋ/| n. |脱气；除气；排气 |The process of removing dissolved gases from a liquid or solid.  This is often done to improve the quality or properties of the material. For example, degassing is important in the production of beer to remove carbon dioxide and prevent excessive foaming.  |例句：The molten metal underwent a degassing process to remove impurities.  熔融金属经过了脱气处理以去除杂质。

The beer was degassed to reduce its carbonation. 这啤酒经过脱气处理以减少其碳酸化程度。

|近义词：outgassing, venting |反义词：gassing, infusing (with gas) |名词
