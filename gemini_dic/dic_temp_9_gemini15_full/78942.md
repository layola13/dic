girders:/ˈɡɜːrdərz/| n. |大梁，钢梁 |A girder is a large, horizontal beam used in construction to support a structure.  Girders are typically made of steel or reinforced concrete and are essential for the stability of buildings and bridges. |例句：The building's weight rests on a network of steel girders. 这座建筑的重量依靠钢梁网络支撑。

例句：The bridge was reinforced with additional girders to increase its load-bearing capacity. 桥梁加固了额外的钢梁以提高其承重能力。


近义词：beam, joist, rafter |反义词：None (no direct opposite) |词性：名词
