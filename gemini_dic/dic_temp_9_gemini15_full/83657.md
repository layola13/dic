haemoscope:/ˈhiːməskoʊp/| n. |血液镜，血细胞计数器 |A haemoscope is an instrument used to examine blood, particularly for counting blood cells.  It typically involves a microscope and specialized counting chambers. |Example: The doctor used a haemoscope to analyze the patient's blood sample. 医生用血液镜检查病人的血样。|近义词：haematocytometer, blood cell counter |反义词：无
