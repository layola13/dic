pewter:/ˈpjuːtər/| n. | 锡合金；青铜色 | A metal alloy consisting primarily of tin, often with additions of other metals such as copper, antimony, and bismuth.  Pewter is known for its malleability and its ability to be cast into various shapes. It has a dull grayish-white color. |The old church possessed beautiful pewter candlesticks. 这座古老的教堂拥有精美的锡制烛台。  The antique pewter tankard was a family heirloom. 这只古董锡酒杯是家族传家宝。|  bronze, tin |  gold, silver |
