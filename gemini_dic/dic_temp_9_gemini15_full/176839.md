readwrite: /ˈriːdˌraɪt/ | adj. | 读写 |  Able to both read from and write to a storage device or medium.  This term is commonly used in the context of computer memory, storage devices (like hard drives or SSDs), and file systems.  A "read-write" device or memory allows for both data retrieval and data modification. | Example: The new flash drive is read-write, allowing you to both save and access files. 新的闪存盘是读写型的，允许你保存和访问文件。|近义词: read/write-capable, bidirectional | 反义词: read-only, write-protected | 词性: adjective
