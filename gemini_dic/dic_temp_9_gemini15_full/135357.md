nonperceptional: /ˌnɒnˌpɜːˈsɛpʃənəl/ | adj. | 非知觉的，非感知的 | Not relating to or involving perception; lacking the ability to perceive.  This adjective describes something that does not involve the senses or the process of understanding through the senses.  | 例句：The machine's operation is largely nonperceptional, relying on automated processes rather than human observation. 这台机器的操作很大程度上是非知觉的，依赖于自动化流程而不是人工观察。

The study used nonperceptional data to analyze the phenomenon. 这项研究使用非感知数据来分析这种现象。


近义词：unperceived, insensible, unaware, non-sensory | 反义词：perceptual, sensory, perceptible
