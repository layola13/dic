shoplot:/ˈʃɒplɒt/| n.|商铺，店屋|A shoplot is a commercial property, typically a building or a unit within a building, designed for retail or business purposes.  It often combines a shopfront with living quarters above.  These are common in Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Singapore.|例句：He owns a shoplot in the city center where he runs his bookstore. 他在市中心拥有一家店屋，在那里经营他的书店。|近义词：commercial property, retail unit, storefront|反义词：residential property
