crew-necked: /ˈkruːnekt/ | adj. | 圆领的 | Having a round neckline.  A crew neck is a round neckline that sits close to the neck, typically without a collar.  It's a simple and classic neckline design often found in t-shirts, sweaters, and other garments. | Example: He wore a simple crew-necked sweater. 他穿着一件简单的圆领毛衣。  She preferred crew-necked tops to V-necks. 她更喜欢圆领上衣而不是V领上衣。| 近义词：round-necked | 反义词：V-necked, turtleneck
