realizer: /ˈriːəlaɪzər/ | n. | 实现者；认知者；感知器 | A person or thing that realizes something, often a specific goal or plan; in linguistics, a component of a grammar that converts underlying structures into surface structures.  | Example: The project manager was the key realizer of the company's new software. 项目经理是公司新软件开发的关键人物。 The parser is a realizer that transforms the logical form into a surface structure. 解析器是一个将逻辑形式转换为表面结构的实现器。 |近义词: implementer, achiever, interpreter |反义词: obstructer, saboteur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team realizes its goals every year. = 团队每年都实现其目标。
He realizes the importance of education. = 他意识到教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
They realized their dream of owning a home. = 他们实现了拥有房子的梦想。
She realized she had made a mistake. = 她意识到自己犯了一个错误。


现在完成时:
We have realized significant progress in the project. = 我们在这个项目中取得了显著进展。
He has realized his potential. = 他已经发挥了他的潜力。


过去完成时:
I had realized my error before submitting the report. = 我在提交报告之前已经意识到了我的错误。
They had realized the danger before it was too late. = 他们在为时已晚之前意识到了危险。


将来时:
She will realize her ambition to become a doctor. = 她将实现她成为医生的雄心壮志。
We will realize the benefits of this plan soon. = 我们很快就会体会到这个计划的好处。


虚拟语气:
If he had realized the consequences, he wouldn't have done it. = 如果他意识到了后果，他不会那样做的。
I wish I had realized that earlier. = 我希望我早点意识到这一点。


被动语态:
The potential of the technology has not yet been fully realized. = 这项技术的潜力尚未完全发挥出来。
The goals were realized through teamwork. = 这些目标是通过团队合作实现的。


疑问句:
Do you realize the gravity of the situation? = 你意识到情况的严重性吗？
Does he realize what he has done? = 他意识到自己做了什么吗？


否定句:
They did not realize the extent of the damage. = 他们没有意识到损害的程度。
I didn't realize how late it was. = 我没有意识到已经这么晚了。
