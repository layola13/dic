conditionate:/kənˈdɪʃənət/ | vt. | 使适应；使服从；以…为条件 | To make (something) dependent on a condition; to subject to certain conditions; to adapt or accustom. | The company conditioned its employees to work long hours.  这家公司让员工习惯于长时间工作。  The experiment conditioned the rats to press a lever for food.  实验让老鼠学会按杠杆获取食物。 | accustom, adapt, subject | unconditonal, absolute | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conditions his dog to sit on command. = 他训练他的狗按指令坐下。
The teacher conditions the students to study hard. = 老师让学生养成努力学习的习惯。

一般过去时:
They conditioned the air in the building. = 他们调节了建筑物内的空气。
She conditioned her hair with a special product. = 她用一种特殊的护发产品护理头发。


现在完成时:
They have conditioned the athletes for the competition. = 他们已经让运动员为比赛做好了准备。
The therapist has conditioned the patient to cope with their fear. = 治疗师已经让病人适应了他们的恐惧。


过去完成时:
The manager had conditioned the staff to meet tight deadlines. = 经理已经让员工习惯于按时完成工作。
The trainer had conditioned the horses for the race. = 训练员已经让马匹为比赛做好了准备。


将来时:
They will condition the new recruits for the challenging assignment. = 他们将让新兵适应具有挑战性的任务。
The scientist will condition the mice for the experiment. = 科学家将为实验训练老鼠。


虚拟语气:
If they conditioned the soil properly, the plants would grow better. = 如果他们正确地处理土壤，植物就会长得更好。
If she had conditioned her muscles beforehand, she wouldn't have injured herself. = 如果她事先锻炼了肌肉，她就不会受伤了。


被动语态:
The room was conditioned to a comfortable temperature. = 房间的温度被调节到舒适的程度。
The animals were conditioned to respond to specific stimuli. = 动物被训练对特定刺激做出反应。


疑问句:
Is the air conditioned in this building? = 这栋建筑物有空调吗？
How long did it take to condition the athletes for the marathon? = 让运动员为马拉松做好准备花了多长时间？


否定句:
The company did not condition its employees to work overtime.  公司没有让员工习惯加班。
He didn't condition his car properly before the long drive. 他没有在长途驾驶前正确保养他的车。


PHRASE->
air conditioning = 空调
conditioned reflex = 条件反射
conditioned response = 条件反应
stimulus conditioning = 刺激条件反射
classical conditioning = 经典条件反射
