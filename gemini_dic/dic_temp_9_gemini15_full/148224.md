oversilent: /ˌoʊvərˈsaɪlənt/ | adj. | 过于沉默的，过于安静的 | Excessively silent; unusually quiet.  This word is not commonly used and is considered a less formal, more emphatic alternative to simply "silent" or "quiet".  It emphasizes the unusual or excessive nature of the silence. | 例句：The room was oversilent, even eerie.  房间过于安静，甚至有点怪异。The oversilent child worried her parents.  孩子过于沉默让父母担心。 | 近义词：unusually quiet, excessively still, eerily quiet | 反义词：noisy, boisterous, clamorous | 词性：形容词
