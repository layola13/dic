deconsecration:/ˌdiːkɒnˈseɪkreɪʃən/ | n. | 亵渎；废弃圣职；取消奉献 | The act of removing something from consecrated status; the process of depriving something of its sacred character.  It often refers to the removal of the sacred status of a building or object, such as a church or religious artifact. | Example: The deconsecration of the old church was a sad event for the community.  旧教堂的废弃对社区来说是一个悲伤的事件。 The deconsecration of the idol angered the villagers.  偶像的亵渎激怒了村民。 | 近义词：desecration, profanation, pollution | 反义词：consecration, sanctification
