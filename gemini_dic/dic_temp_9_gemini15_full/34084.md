civilizational:/ˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃənəl/| adj. |文明的；文化的；与文明相关的|Relating to or characteristic of civilization or a particular civilization.  The word emphasizes the processes and structures of a civilization rather than simply its material aspects.|例句：The lecture focused on the civilizational differences between East and West.  讲座重点关注东西方文明的差异。

例句：The exhibition explored the civilizational impact of the Silk Road.  展览探讨了丝绸之路对文明的影响。
