overpromoted:/ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 被过度提拔的；职位过高的 |  Someone who is overpromoted has been given a job that is too senior for their abilities or experience.  This often leads to inefficiency and dissatisfaction.  | Example: He was overpromoted and struggled to handle the responsibilities of his new position. 他被过度提拔，难以胜任新职位的职责。 | 近义词:  too high, elevated beyond ability, inappropriately promoted | 反义词: underpromoted, appropriately promoted
