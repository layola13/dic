precandidatures:/ˌpriːˈkændɪdeɪtʃərz/| n. |预备候选人资格；预选提名|The process of declaring one's intention to run for office before the official candidacy period.  It often involves gathering support and assessing viability.  |例句：The precandidatures for the mayoral election were announced last week.  = 上周宣布了市长选举的预备候选人资格。


The numerous precandidatures highlight the intense competition for the nomination. =  众多的预备候选人资格突显了提名竞争的激烈程度。|近义词：preliminary nominations, early declarations|反义词：final candidatures, official nominations|名词
