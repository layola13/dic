dactyli: /ˈdæktɪlaɪ/ | n. | 指节，音步 | Plural of dactyl; a metrical foot consisting of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables.  | Example:  The poem's rhythm is based on a series of dactyls. 这首诗的节奏基于一系列的抑扬格。 | 近义词:  trochee (opposite stress pattern) | 反义词: spondee (two stressed syllables)


USAGE->
There is no verb or adjective form of "dactyli."  It is only used as a plural noun and therefore does not conjugate through tenses or voice.  Its usage is limited to discussions of meter in poetry and related fields.


Example sentences demonstrating usage:

* The line contains three dactyls and a spondee.  这行诗包含三个抑扬格和一个长音步。

*  Scanning the verse reveals a predominance of dactyls.  扫描诗句揭示了抑扬格的优势。
