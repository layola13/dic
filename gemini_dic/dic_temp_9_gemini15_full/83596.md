haematologist:/ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. |血液病学家|A haematologist is a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the blood, bone marrow, and lymphatic system.  They deal with conditions such as anemia, leukemia, lymphoma, and blood clotting disorders.|例句：My grandmother is seeing a haematologist for her anemia. 我祖母去看血液病学家治疗她的贫血。|近义词：hematologist|反义词: N/A
