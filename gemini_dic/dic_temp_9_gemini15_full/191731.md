sciences:/ˈsaɪənsɪz/| n. |科学（学科）|The systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment.  It encompasses a vast range of disciplines, from physics and chemistry to biology and astronomy.  |例句：The sciences are crucial for technological advancement. 科学对于技术进步至关重要。

例句：She dedicated her life to the pursuit of sciences. 她毕生致力于科学事业。


近义词：scientific disciplines, branches of knowledge, fields of study | 反义词：None (It's a broad category, not something with a direct opposite) | 词性：n.
