cottaged:/ˈkɒtɪdʒd/ | v. |建造小屋；住在小屋里 |To build or live in a cottage.  The verb "cottaged" implies a past action of either building a cottage or residing in one. |They cottaged in the countryside for the summer.  他们在乡下的小屋里住了整个夏天。|  lived in a small house, resided in a cottage |  lived in a mansion, resided in a palace
