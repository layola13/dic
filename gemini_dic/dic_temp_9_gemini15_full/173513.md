quasi-fraternally:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ fræˈtɜːrnəli/ | adj. |近似兄弟般地，如同兄弟般地 |  Describes a relationship or behavior that resembles that of brothers, but not exactly. It suggests a bond of camaraderie, loyalty, and possibly informal closeness, but without the formal ties of brotherhood.  | Example: They treated each other quasi-fraternally, sharing jokes and inside information. 他们互相以近似兄弟般的方式相处，分享笑话和内部信息。 |近义词：brotherly, comradely, friendly |反义词：hostile, unfriendly, antagonistic |  形容词
