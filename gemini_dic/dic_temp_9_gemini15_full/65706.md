eventful:/ɪˈvɛntfəl/| adj.|多事的；充满事件的；重要的|Full of events, especially important or exciting ones.  It implies a significant number of happenings, often with a sense of activity or excitement.|例句：It was an eventful year, marked by both triumphs and setbacks.  那是一个多事之年，既有胜利也有挫折。

例句：Her trip to Europe was eventful, filled with unexpected adventures. 她的欧洲之旅多姿多彩，充满了意想不到的冒险。|近义词：busy, active, momentous, significant|反义词：uneventful, quiet, calm, monotonous
