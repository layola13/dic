skreen:/skriːn/| n. vt.|屏幕；遮蔽物；筛选；掩盖|A screen is a flat surface, often made of glass, plastic, or metal, that displays images.  It can also refer to a barrier or a protective device. As a verb, it means to shield or protect something.|例句：The movie was projected onto a large screen. 电影投射到一块大屏幕上。
The bushes screened the house from view. 灌木丛遮住了房子的视线。|近义词：display, shield, filter, cover|反义词：reveal, uncover
