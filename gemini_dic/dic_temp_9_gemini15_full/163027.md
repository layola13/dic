preclaims:/prɪˈkleɪmz/| v.|预先声明，预先声称|To state or declare something beforehand; to make a claim in advance.|例句：The company preclaimed its commitment to environmental sustainability. 公司预先声明其致力于环境可持续性。例句：She preclaimed her intention to run for office. 她预先声明她打算竞选公职。|近义词：announce beforehand, declare in advance, assert beforehand|反义词：deny, retract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preclaims his innocence. = 他预先声明自己是无辜的。
The company preclaims its new product line. = 公司预先声明其新的产品线。

一般过去时:
They preclaimed their support for the candidate. = 他们预先声明支持该候选人。
The scientist preclaimed the results of his experiment. = 科学家预先声明了他的实验结果。

现在完成时:
She has preclaimed her retirement from politics. = 她已经预先声明她将退出政坛。
The government has preclaimed its intention to lower taxes. = 政府已经预先声明其打算降低税收。

过去完成时:
He had preclaimed his opposition to the proposal. = 他此前已预先声明反对该提案。
They had preclaimed their arrival for the meeting. = 他们之前已预先声明他们将参加会议。

将来时:
They will preclaim the winner of the competition. = 他们将预先宣布比赛的获胜者。
The company will preclaim its earnings for the quarter. = 公司将预先公布本季度的收益。

虚拟语气:
If he preclaimed his intentions, it would have saved a lot of trouble. = 如果他预先声明了他的意图，那就省去了很多麻烦。
I wish he had preclaimed his visit. = 我希望他事先声明过他的来访。

被动语态:
His innocence was preclaimed by his lawyer. = 他的清白由他的律师预先声明。
The new policy was preclaimed by the administration. = 新政策由政府预先声明。

疑问句:
Did they preclaim their strategy? = 他们是否预先声明了他们的策略？
Has the company preclaimed its new product launch date? = 公司是否预先声明了其新产品发布日期？

否定句:
He did not preclaim his arrival. = 他没有预先声明他的到来。
The company did not preclaim any changes to its policy. = 公司没有预先声明其政策的任何变化。
