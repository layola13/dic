multivariant:/ˌmʌltiˈveriənt/| adj.|多元变量的，多变量的|Having or involving a number of independent variables.|Multivariate analysis is a statistical technique used to analyze data with more than one dependent variable.|近义词：multivariate|反义词：univariate


USAGE->
This word is an adjective,  so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It describes a characteristic of data or analysis.  Example sentences will demonstrate its use.

Example sentences demonstrating its usage in different sentence structures:

Declarative: The study employed a multivariate statistical model.  = 这项研究采用了多元统计模型。

Interrogative: Is this a multivariate problem requiring specialized analysis? = 这是一个需要专门分析的多元问题吗？

Exclamatory: What a complex multivariate dataset this is! = 这真是一个复杂的多元数据集！
