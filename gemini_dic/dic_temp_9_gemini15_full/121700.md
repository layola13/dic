mispraise:/ˈmɪsˌpreɪz/| vt.|误赞，错赞，贬低|To praise wrongly or inadequately; to give undeserved praise to; to disparage.|例句：He mispraised her efforts, implying they were insignificant. 他误赞了她的努力，暗示它们微不足道。|例句：The critic mispraised the film, focusing only on minor aspects while ignoring its overall brilliance. 批评家误赞了这部电影，只关注一些次要方面，而忽略了它的整体杰出之处。|近义词：underpraise, belittle, slight, deprecate|反义词：praise, commend, extol
