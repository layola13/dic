disbelieved:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvd/| v.|不相信；不信；怀疑|To refuse to believe something; to doubt the truth of something. |例句：He disbelieved the story about the monster. 他不相信关于那个怪物的故事。|例句：I disbelieved her claims of innocence. 我不相信她无辜的说法。|近义词：doubted, questioned, mistrusted|反义词：believed, accepted, trusted|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disbelieves everything he reads online. = 他不相信他在网上读到的任何东西。
They disbelieve the rumors spreading around the town. = 他们不相信在城里流传的谣言。

一般过去时:
She disbelieved his explanation for being late. = 她不相信他迟到的解释。
The jury disbelieved the witness's testimony. = 陪审团不相信证人的证词。

现在完成时:
I have disbelieved everything he's told me so far. = 到目前为止，我不相信他告诉我的任何事情。
We have disbelieved his promises for years. = 多年来，我们一直不相信他的承诺。

过去完成时:
They had disbelieved his warnings before the accident happened. = 事故发生前，他们不相信他的警告。
She had disbelieved the forecast, resulting in her getting caught in the storm. = 她不相信预报，结果被困在暴风雨中。

将来时:
He will disbelieve any evidence presented without proper verification. = 他会不相信任何未经适当验证的证据。
They will disbelieve her until she provides concrete proof. = 在她提供确凿证据之前，他们不会相信她。

虚拟语气:
If I had disbelieved him earlier, things would have been different. = 如果我早点不相信他，情况就会不一样了。
I wish I had disbelieved his lies. = 我真希望我不相信他的谎言。

被动语态:
His story was disbelieved by everyone. = 他的故事不被任何人相信。
The claims were disbelieved by the scientific community. = 科学界不相信这些说法。

疑问句:
Did you disbelieve what he said? = 你不相信他说的话吗？
Would you disbelieve me if I told you the truth? = 如果你告诉我实话，你会不相信我吗？

否定句:
I didn't disbelieve her at first. = 我一开始并不怀疑她。
They didn't disbelieve the evidence presented. = 他们没有不相信提出的证据。


PHRASE->
disbelieve in something = 不相信某事
disbelieve someone's words = 不相信某人的话
disbelieve a story/report = 不相信一个故事/报告
disbelieve wholeheartedly = 全盘否定
disbelieve with good reason = 有充分理由不相信
