processing: /ˈprɑːsɛsɪŋ/ /ˈprəʊsesɪŋ/ | n. v. | 加工；处理；处理过程 | The act of systematically handling or dealing with information or materials.  |例句：The processing of data takes a significant amount of time. 数据处理需要大量时间。

The factory is capable of processing 100 tons of grain per day. 这个工厂每天可以加工100吨粮食。|近义词：handling, treatment, preparation, manipulation |反义词：neglect, ignoring
