exhaust:/ɪɡˈzɔːst/ /ɪɡˈzɔːst/| n. vt. adj.|排气；废气；精疲力竭；使精疲力竭；详尽无遗的|英文解析：As a noun, it refers to the waste gases expelled from an engine. As a verb, it means to tire someone or something out completely. As an adjective, it means thorough and complete.|例句：The car's exhaust fumes were polluting the air.  汽车的废气正在污染空气。
The long journey exhausted me. 漫长的旅程让我筋疲力尽。

近义词：tire, deplete, weary, drain, spent;  (n.) emission, fumes
反义词：energize, invigorate, refresh, revitalize; (adj.) incomplete, superficial

词性：n. vt. adj.
