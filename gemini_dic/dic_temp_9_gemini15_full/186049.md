reworks:/ˌriːˈwɜːrks/ | n. | 重制版；重新制作；修改后的作品 |  The band released a reworked version of their hit song. 乐队发布了他们热门歌曲的重制版。 | 近义词：remake, revision, adaptation | 反义词：original


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reworks his paintings frequently. 他经常修改他的画作。
The artist reworks the clay until it is perfect. 艺术家反复揉捏陶土直到完美。


一般过去时:
She reworked the entire chapter. 她重写了整章。

现在完成时:
The company has reworked its marketing strategy. 公司已经修改了其营销策略。
He has reworked the manuscript several times. 他已经修改了手稿好几次。


过去完成时:

将来时:
