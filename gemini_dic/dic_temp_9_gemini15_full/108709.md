leaned-on: /liːndˈɑːn/ | adj. | 依靠的，依赖的 |  Describes something or someone that is relied upon for support, either physically or emotionally.  |  The leaned-on chair finally broke.  那把常被依靠的椅子最终断了。  The leaned-on employee was promoted.  那个被依赖的员工被提升了。 |  dependent, relied-upon, trusted | independent, self-reliant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leans on his cane for support. = 他依靠拐杖来支撑自己。
She leans on her friends for emotional support. = 她依靠朋友来获得情感支持。


一般过去时:
He leaned on his brother for help. = 他依靠他哥哥寻求帮助。
They leaned on each other during difficult times. = 他们在困难时期互相依靠。


现在完成时:
She has leaned on her faith throughout her struggles. = 在她奋斗的整个过程中，她一直依靠自己的信仰。
He has leaned on his family for financial support. = 他一直依靠家人来获得经济上的支持。


过去完成时:
She had leaned on her parents for advice before making the decision. = 在做出决定之前，她已经向父母寻求过建议。
They had leaned on each other for years before the business partnership ended. = 在商业伙伴关系结束之前，他们已经互相依靠了很多年。


将来时:
He will lean on his experience to guide him. = 他将依靠自己的经验来指导自己。
She will lean on her team for assistance with the project. = 她将依靠她的团队来协助完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If he leaned on his friends more, he wouldn't feel so alone. = 如果他更依赖朋友，他就不会感到那么孤单。
I wish I had leaned on my mentors earlier in my career. = 我希望在我的职业生涯早期就更多地依靠我的导师。


被动语态:
The company is often leaned on by its subsidiaries. = 这家公司经常被它的子公司依赖。
The system is not designed to be leaned on so heavily. = 这个系统的设计并非要承受如此大的依赖。


疑问句:
Who do you lean on when you're feeling down? = 当你情绪低落时，你依靠谁？
What resources do they lean on to complete the task? = 他们依靠什么资源来完成任务？


否定句:
He doesn't lean on anyone for help. = 他不依靠任何人来寻求帮助。
She doesn't want to lean on her family for financial assistance. = 她不想依靠家人来获得经济援助。
