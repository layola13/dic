quasi-suppressed:/ˈkwɑːziˌsʌpɹɛst/| adj. | 准抑制的，近乎被抑制的 |  Describes something that is almost but not completely suppressed; partially or incompletely inhibited or restrained.  It implies a degree of control or limitation, but not a total elimination. | The quasi-suppressed immune response allowed for a mild infection.  准抑制的免疫反应导致轻微感染。The quasi-suppressed rebellion simmered beneath the surface of society. 近乎被压制叛乱在社会表面下暗流涌动。|近义词：partially suppressed, muted, subdued, dampened |反义词：fully expressed, uninhibited, unrestrained |
