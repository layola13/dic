/ˌiː.ɑːrˈpiː/|/ˈɑːr.piː/| n. |企业资源计划；企业资源规划|Enterprise Resource Planning; a system used by businesses to manage and integrate all facets of their operations, including product planning, development, manufacturing, sales, and marketing.|Our company recently implemented a new ERP system to streamline our operations.  = 我们公司最近实施了一个新的ERP系统来简化我们的运营。|Supply chain management system, business management software|No direct antonym, but inefficient systems could be considered an opposite concept.|名词
