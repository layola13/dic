musicomania: /ˌmjuːzɪkəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 音乐狂热；对音乐的痴迷 | An excessive enthusiasm or obsession with music.  It's a blend of "music" and "-mania," indicating an extreme passion or craze.  The term suggests a level of obsession beyond simply enjoying music; it implies a potentially overwhelming or disruptive preoccupation. | Example:  Her musicomania led her to spend all her savings on concert tickets and rare vinyl records.  她的音乐狂热让她把所有的积蓄都花在了演唱会门票和稀有黑胶唱片上。| 近义词:  music obsession, audiophilia (if focused on high-fidelity sound), melomania (though this is less common and sometimes implies a specific type of musical obsession)| 反义词:  apathetic to music, musical indifference |  名词
