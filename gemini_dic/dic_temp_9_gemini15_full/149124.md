Oxidants are often used in bleaching, disinfecting, and other chemical processes.|Example:  Hydrogen peroxide is a common oxidant used in many household cleaning products.  过氧化氢是许多家用清洁产品中常用的氧化剂。|近义词：oxidizing agent, oxidizing substance|反义词：reductant, reducing agent
