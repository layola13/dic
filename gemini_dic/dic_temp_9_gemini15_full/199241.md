silo:/ˈsaɪloʊ/| n. |筒仓；信息孤岛；|A silo is a tall cylindrical structure used for storing grain or other bulk materials.  It can also refer to a situation where information or activities are kept separate and not shared, hindering communication and collaboration. |例句：The farmer stored his corn in a large silo.  农民把玉米储存在一个大型筒仓里。

The marketing department operates in a silo, rarely sharing information with sales. 市场部独立运作，很少与销售部共享信息。|近义词：granary, bin, storehouse |反义词：openness, collaboration, communication
