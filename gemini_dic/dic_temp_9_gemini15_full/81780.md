/ɡreɪt/ | adj. | 极好的，伟大的 | excellent; of great size, extent, or importance |例句：That's a great idea! 这是一个好主意！
The Great Wall is a wonder of the world. 长城是世界奇迹。|近义词：wonderful, excellent, magnificent |反义词：terrible, awful, poor |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake is great. = 蛋糕很好吃。
The view from the mountain is great. = 山上的景色很棒。

一般过去时:
The party was great. = 派对很棒。
The movie was great. = 电影很棒。

现在完成时:
We've had a great time. = 我们玩得很开心。
She's done a great job. = 她做得很好。

过去完成时:
They had already had a great journey before arriving home. = 他们在回家之前已经度过了一段美好的旅程。
I had never seen such a great performance before. = 我以前从未见过如此精彩的演出。

将来时:
The trip will be great. = 这趟旅行会很棒。
The concert will be great. = 音乐会将会很棒。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would have a great time. = 如果我有更多钱，我会玩得很开心。
It would be great if you could come to my party. = 如果你能来我的派对就好了。

被动语态:
The project was considered great. = 这个项目被认为很棒。
The painting is widely regarded as great. = 这幅画被广泛认为是伟大的。

疑问句:
Is the food great? = 食物好吃吗？
Is this a great opportunity? = 这是一个好机会吗？

否定句:
The food isn't great. = 食物不好吃。
The weather isn't great today. = 今天天气不好。


PHRASE->
