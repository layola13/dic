cinquecento:/ˌtʃɪŋkwɛˈtʃɛntoʊ/| n. |意大利文艺复兴时期（约1500-1600年）|The Cinquecento, meaning "five hundred" in Italian, refers to the 16th century in Italy, a period of immense artistic and cultural flourishing during the High Renaissance. This era witnessed the masterpieces of artists like Michelangelo, Raphael, and Leonardo da Vinci.  |The Cinquecento saw the peak of the High Renaissance in Italy. |High Renaissance, 16th century|Middle Ages, Baroque
