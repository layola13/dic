conjugal:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡəl/| adj. |婚姻的；夫妻的|Relating to marriage or the relationship between a married couple.  The adjective conjugal is used to describe things related to marriage or the marital relationship. It often focuses on the aspects of intimacy and companionship between spouses.|例句：They enjoyed a happy conjugal life.  他们享受着幸福的婚姻生活。

例句：Their conjugal bliss was evident to everyone. 他们的夫妻恩爱显而易见。

近义词：marital, matrimonial, spousal

反义词：unmarried, single


词性:形容词
