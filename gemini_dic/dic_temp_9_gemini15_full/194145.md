self-inspected:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈspɛktɪd/| adj.| 自检的；自我检查的 |  Describes something that has been examined or checked by the person or entity responsible for it, rather than by an external party.  This often implies a lack of independent verification. | Example: The self-inspected report showed no major safety violations, but an independent audit is still recommended.  自检报告显示没有重大安全违规，但仍建议进行独立审计。| 近义词：self-examined, self-evaluated | 反义词：externally inspected, independently audited | 词性:形容词

The factory is self-inspected monthly. = 工厂每月进行自检。
He self-inspects his work before submitting it. = 他在提交工作之前会自我检查。
