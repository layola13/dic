mournfully:/ˈmɔːrnfəli/| adj.|悲伤地；忧郁地 |Expressing sorrow; sorrowful.  It describes something done or said in a way that shows sadness and grief. |例句：She spoke mournfully of her lost childhood. 她悲伤地谈起她失去的童年。

例句：He looked mournfully at the ruined house. 他悲伤地看着那座毁坏的房子。


近义词：sadly, sorrowfully, dolefully, dejectedly

反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully

词性：形容词
