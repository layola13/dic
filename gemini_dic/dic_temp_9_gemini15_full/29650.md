/ˈkeɪtərɪŋ/| n. |餐饮服务；承办酒席；供应饮食|The provision of food and drink, especially at a social event or for a particular purpose.  It often involves planning, preparing, and serving food and beverages.| Example: The catering for the wedding was excellent. 婚礼的餐饮服务很棒。  The company provides catering services for corporate events. 这家公司为企业活动提供餐饮服务。| 近义词：catering service, food service, banqueting| 反义词：self-catering (自己做饭)| 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hotel caters for large events. = 这家酒店承办大型活动。
Our company provides excellent catering. = 我们公司提供优质的餐饮服务。


一般过去时:
They catered the party last night. = 他们昨晚承办了派对。
The restaurant catered a large wedding reception. = 这家餐厅承办了一场大型婚礼酒席。


现在完成时:
We have catered many weddings this year. = 今年我们已经承办了许多婚礼。
They have catered for hundreds of guests. = 他们已经为数百位宾客提供了餐饮服务。


过去完成时:
They had catered the event before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经准备好了活动所需的餐饮。
The chef had catered many similar events in the past. = 厨师过去曾承办过许多类似的活动。


将来时:
We will cater the conference next week. = 我们下周将承办这次会议的餐饮。
They will cater the party at the beach. = 他们将在海滩上承办派对的餐饮。


虚拟语气:
If we had better catering, the party would have been better. = 如果我们有更好的餐饮服务，派对就会更好。
I wish they had catered the event more efficiently. = 我希望他们能更有效率地承办这次活动的餐饮。


被动语态:
The event was catered by a professional company. = 这场活动由一家专业公司承办餐饮。
The food was catered to the guests' dietary needs. = 食物根据客人的饮食需求进行准备。


疑问句:
Does this restaurant cater for vegetarians? = 这家餐厅有素食吗？
Who will cater the company picnic? = 谁将承办公司野餐的餐饮？


否定句:
We don't cater for large groups. = 我们不承办大型团队的餐饮。
They didn't cater the event themselves. = 他们没有自己承办这次活动的餐饮。
