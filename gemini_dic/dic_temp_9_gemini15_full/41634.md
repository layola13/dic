correcting:/kəˈrektɪŋ/ | v. | 校正；纠正；改正 | The act of making something right or accurate;  removing errors or mistakes.  | Example:  He spent the afternoon correcting his essay. 他花了整个下午修改他的论文。|近义词：amending, rectifying, improving |反义词：worsening, exacerbating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is correcting the grammar mistakes in the document. = 她正在修改文档中的语法错误。
They are correcting the errors in the data. = 他们正在纠正数据中的错误。


一般过去时:
He corrected the spelling errors in his report. = 他更正了他报告中的拼写错误。
The teacher corrected the student's homework. = 老师批改了学生的作业。


现在完成时:
She has corrected all the typos in the manuscript. = 她已经更正了稿件中的所有错字。
They have corrected the faulty wiring. = 他们已经纠正了错误的线路连接。


过去完成时:
He had corrected the mistake before anyone noticed it. = 他在任何人注意到之前就已经纠正了错误。
They had corrected the errors in the database before the report was generated. = 他们在生成报告之前就已经纠正了数据库中的错误。


将来时:
She will correct the mistakes in the next draft. = 她将在下一稿中更正错误。
They will correct the problem as soon as possible. = 他们会尽快解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the answer, I would have corrected the mistake. = 如果我知道答案，我就会纠正错误。
I wish I had corrected the error earlier. = 我真希望我早点纠正了这个错误。


被动语态:
The mistakes were corrected by the editor. = 错误由编辑更正。
The problem will be corrected shortly. = 问题很快就会得到解决。


疑问句:
Are you correcting the figures? = 你正在校正数据吗？
Has the error been corrected yet? = 错误已经更正了吗？


否定句:
He did not correct the errors in the report. = 他没有更正报告中的错误。
They haven't corrected the problem yet. = 他们还没有解决这个问题。
