词源:
The word "furry" originates from the Old English word "fyr," meaning "fur".  The suffix "-y" is an adjective-forming suffix, indicating a quality or characteristic.  Therefore, "furry" literally means "having fur."  The modern meaning has expanded to include the subculture.
