mislabeled:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbəld/ | adj. | 错误标记的，贴错标签的 |  Something that is mislabeled has been given the wrong label or name.  It is often used to describe items that are incorrectly identified or categorized. | Example: The package was mislabeled, and it was sent to the wrong address.  包裹贴错了标签，被送到了错误的地址。


The medication was mislabeled, leading to a dangerous dosage error.  药物贴错了标签，导致了危险的剂量错误。|近义词：incorrectly labeled, wrongly identified, misnamed |反义词：correctly labeled, accurately identified
