emotional: /ɪˈmoʊʃənəl/ | adj. | 感情的；情绪的；易动感情的 | Relating to or characterized by emotion; easily moved emotionally. |例句：He became emotional when talking about his childhood. 他谈到童年时变得情绪激动。
例句：She gave an emotional speech at the funeral. 她在葬礼上发表了一篇感人的演讲。

近义词：sentimental, passionate, feeling, sensitive | 反义词：unemotional, apathetic, indifferent, stoic

词源:  The word "emotional" derives from the late Middle English word "emotioun," which itself comes from the Old French word "emocioun" and ultimately from the Latin word "emotio," meaning "a stirring up, excitement."  The suffix "-al"  indicates an adjective form.
