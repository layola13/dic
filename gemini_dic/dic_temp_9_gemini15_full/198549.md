shrubberies:/ˈʃrʌbəriːz/| n.|灌木丛；灌木林|The plural form of "shrubbery," referring to a collection of shrubs or a planted area containing shrubs.  Shrubberies can range from small, neatly trimmed collections of plants to extensive, wild areas filled with various shrubs.|例句：The gardener meticulously pruned the shrubberies in the formal gardens. 园丁一丝不苟地修剪着正式花园里的灌木丛。|例句：Wild shrubberies covered the hillside, teeming with birds and insects.  野生的灌木丛覆盖着山坡，鸟类和昆虫成群结队。|近义词：bushes, thickets, undergrowth|反义词：open field, grassland
