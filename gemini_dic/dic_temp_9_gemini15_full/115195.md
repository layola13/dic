manicures:/ˈmænɪkjʊərz/| n. |修指甲；美甲|The process of caring for fingernails and toenails, typically involving cleaning, shaping, polishing, and sometimes applying nail art.  Manicures are often performed in salons or spas, but can also be done at home. |例句：She gets manicures every two weeks. 她每两周做一次美甲。

例句：I gave myself a manicure last night. 我昨晚自己做了美甲。


近义词：nail care, nail treatment

反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable to this noun)

词性：名词
