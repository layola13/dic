counterstock:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɑːk/| n. | 对抗性股票；抵押品；反向股票| A counterstock is a type of security that is used to offset or hedge against the risk of a decline in the value of another security.  It can also refer to goods pledged as security for a loan.| Example: The investor used counterstock to mitigate the risk associated with their volatile investments.  投资者使用对抗性股票来减轻其波动性投资相关的风险。|近义词：collateral, security, hedge|反义词：N/A|名词
