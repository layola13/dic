reshoot:/ˌriːˈʃuːt/| vt.|重拍，重新拍摄|To film or photograph something again.|例句：We had to reshoot the scene because of the bad weather.  我们不得不因为糟糕的天气而重拍这场戏。|例句：The director decided to reshoot several key scenes. 导演决定重拍几个关键场景。|近义词：refilm, re-record|反义词：shoot (for the first time)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reshoot the commercial every year. = 他们每年都重拍广告。
The director reshoots difficult scenes multiple times. = 导演多次重拍困难的场景。

一般过去时:
We reshot the entire sequence last week. = 我们上周重拍了整个片段。
The studio reshot the ending after negative feedback. = 制片厂在收到负面反馈后重拍了结局。

现在完成时:
They have reshot the film three times. = 他们已经重拍了这部电影三次。
The production team has reshot several scenes to improve the quality. = 制作团队已经重拍了几个场景以提高质量。

过去完成时:
They had reshot the scene before the director was satisfied. = 在导演满意之前，他们已经重拍了那个场景。
The actors had reshot their lines several times before the take was approved. = 在镜头被批准之前，演员们已经多次重拍了他们的台词。

将来时:
We will reshoot the problematic scenes tomorrow. = 我们明天将重拍有问题的场景。
The crew will reshoot the entire sequence if necessary. = 如果需要，剧组将重拍整个片段。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reshoot the entire movie. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会重拍整部电影。
I wish we had reshot that scene; it looks terrible. = 我希望我们重拍了那个场景；它看起来很糟糕。

被动语态:
The scene was reshot due to technical difficulties. = 由于技术困难，该场景被重拍。
The entire movie will be reshot if the studio decides to. = 如果制片厂决定的话，整部电影将会被重拍。

疑问句:
Will they reshoot the controversial scene? = 他们会重拍有争议的场景吗？
Have they reshot any scenes yet? = 他们重拍任何场景了吗？

否定句:
We didn't reshoot the scene; we just edited it. = 我们没有重拍那个场景；我们只是剪辑了它。
They wouldn't reshoot the movie despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，他们也不愿意重拍电影。
