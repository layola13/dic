canular:/kəˈnjuːlər/ | n. | 骗局；谣言；恶作剧 | A canular is a hoax or a fabricated story intended to deceive.  It often involves a playful or mischievous intent, unlike more serious forms of deception. | Example: The story about the Loch Ness Monster is considered by many to be a canular.  这关于尼斯湖水怪的故事被许多人认为是一个骗局。 | 近义词：hoax, fabrication, sham, ruse, trick | 反义词：truth, reality, fact | 名词
