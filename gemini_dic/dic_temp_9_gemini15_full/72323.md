flouting:/ˈflaʊtɪŋ/| v.|蔑视；违反；嘲笑；炫耀 | To treat with contempt; to disregard openly; to show contempt for a rule, law, or convention.  |例句：They were flouting the law by speeding. 他们超速行驶，公然违反交通法规。

例句：He was flouting convention by wearing jeans to the wedding. 他穿着牛仔裤参加婚礼，蔑视传统习俗。


近义词：defying, ignoring, disregarding, scorning, mocking

反义词：obeying, respecting, conforming to

词性：动词
