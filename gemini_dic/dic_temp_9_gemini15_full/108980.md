left's: /lɛfts/ | n. | 左边；左派；左翼 |  The possessive form of "left," indicating something belonging to the left side or the left-wing.  It is primarily used in informal contexts. |  例句：That's the left's argument.  那就是左派的论点。  |  近义词：leftist's, left-wing's | 反义词：right's


USAGE->
This word is primarily a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, typical tense examples are not applicable.  The usage focuses on showing possession.


例句1:  The left's victory was unexpected. 左派的胜利出人意料。
例句2: We need to consider the left's perspective. 我们需要考虑左派的观点。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "left's".  The word itself is relatively uncommon in formal writing.  Phrases would usually involve "left" without the possessive 's.  Examples would include: "on the left", "turn left", "left wing".
