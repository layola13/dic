/ˈrætlɪŋz/| n.| 嘎嘎声，咯咯声，喋喋不休 |  A rattling sound is a series of short, sharp knocking or clicking sounds.  It can also refer to rapid, indistinct speech. |例句：The old car made a rattling sound as it went down the road. 这辆旧车行驶在路上发出嘎嘎声。

The old woman's rattling speech was difficult to understand. 老太太喋喋不休的讲话很难理解。|近义词：clatter, clattering, rattle,  jangle|反义词：silence, hush
