charioteers:/ˌtʃæriəˈtɪrz/| n.|御者；驾车者|People who drive chariots.  Charioteers were skilled drivers who needed strength, agility, and courage to control their powerful horses and chariots in races or battles.|Example: The skilled charioteers guided their teams through the treacherous course.|近义词：chariot driver, driver|反义词：passenger
