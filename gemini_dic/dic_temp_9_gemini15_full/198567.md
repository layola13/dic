shrunken:/ˈʃrʌŋkən/| adj.| 萎缩的；缩小的；皱缩的 | Having become smaller or less in size, amount, or value.  |例句：The sweater had shrunken in the wash.  这件毛衣洗后缩水了。

例句：His shrunken face showed the effects of years of hardship. 他萎缩的脸庞显示出多年艰辛的痕迹。


近义词：reduced, diminished, contracted, withered, dwarfed

反义词：enlarged, expanded, inflated, swollen

词性:形容词
