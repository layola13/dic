louchest:/ˈluːtʃɪst/| adj. | 最吵闹的；最喧嚣的；最粗俗的 |  This word is a superlative form, meaning "most louche."  It describes something or someone exhibiting a decadent or disreputable quality to the highest degree. It suggests a combination of stylishness and moral laxity, often with a hint of recklessness or amorality.  The word carries a strong connotation of being deliberately provocative and unconventional. |例句：He threw the louchest party the town had ever seen.  这个城镇从未见过他举办的如此喧闹的派对。|近义词：raunchiest, wildest, most decadent, most dissolute |反义词：most virtuous, most proper, most respectable
