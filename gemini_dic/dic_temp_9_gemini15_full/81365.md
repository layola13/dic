grapple's:/ˈɡræpəl/| n.| 抓住；扭打；铁抓；难题 |The grappling hook caught on the rock.  铁抓钩住了岩石。|近义词：struggle, wrestle, fight |反义词：release, let go


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grapples with the problem. = 他正在努力解决这个问题。
The team grapples with the difficult task. = 团队正在努力完成这项艰巨的任务。

一般过去时:
She grappled with her opponent. = 她和对手扭打在一起。
The firefighters grappled with the burning building. = 消防员与燃烧的建筑物搏斗。

现在完成时:
They have grappled with this issue for years. = 他们多年来一直努力解决这个问题。
The company has grappled with declining sales. = 公司一直在努力应对销售额下降的问题。

过去完成时:
He had grappled with the decision for days before he finally made up his mind. = 他在最终做出决定之前已经纠结了几天。
The climbers had grappled with the icy slope before reaching the summit. = 登山者在到达山顶之前与冰坡搏斗。

将来时:
We will grapple with the consequences of our actions. = 我们将努力应对我们行为的后果。
The government will grapple with the economic crisis. = 政府将努力应对经济危机。

虚拟语气:
If I grappled with the problem, I might find a solution. = 如果我努力解决这个问题，我可能会找到一个解决方案。
I wish I had grappled with the issue sooner. = 我希望我早点处理这个问题。

被动语态:
The problem was grappled with by the experts. = 这个问题由专家们努力解决。
The situation is being grappled with by the authorities. = 当局正在努力解决这种情况。

疑问句:
How does he grapple with such difficult challenges? = 他是如何应对如此艰难的挑战的？
Will the team be able to grapple with this new obstacle? = 团队能否克服这个新的障碍？

否定句:
