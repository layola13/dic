cooks:/kʊks/| v. n. |厨师；烹调；煮|to prepare food by heating it|The chef cooks delicious meals.|近义词：prepares, bakes, fries|反义词：eats, consumes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cooks dinner every night. = 她每晚都做晚饭。
He cooks a mean steak. = 他煎牛排很有一套。

一般过去时:
I cooked pasta for lunch yesterday. = 我昨天午餐做了意大利面。
They cooked a feast for the celebration. = 他们为庆祝活动做了一顿盛宴。

现在完成时:
She has cooked us a wonderful meal. = 她为我们做了一顿美餐。
We have cooked several times this week. = 我们这周已经做了几次饭了。

过去完成时:
He had cooked the chicken before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他已经把鸡肉煮好了。
They had cooked all day for the party. = 为了聚会，他们已经做了一整天的饭。

将来时:
I will cook a big breakfast tomorrow. = 我明天要做一顿丰盛的早餐。
She will cook dinner for us next week. = 她下周将为我们做晚饭。

虚拟语气:
If I could cook, I would make a cake. = 如果我会做饭，我会做个蛋糕。
I wish I could cook like my grandmother. = 我希望我能像我奶奶一样会做饭。

被动语态:
Dinner is cooked by my mother. = 晚饭是我妈妈做的。
The food was cooked perfectly. = 食物做得非常完美。

疑问句:
Do you cook often? = 你经常做饭吗？
Who cooks in your family? = 你家谁做饭？

否定句:
I don't cook very well. = 我不太会做饭。
He didn't cook anything today. = 他今天什么都没做。


PHRASE->
cook up = 编造；策划

pressure cook = 高压锅烹饪
