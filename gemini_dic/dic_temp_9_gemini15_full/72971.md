fool-away: /ˌfuːl əˈweɪ/ | v. | 虚度；浪费；消磨（时间） | To spend time idly or unproductively; to waste.  |例句：He fooled away his entire inheritance. 他挥霍了他的全部遗产。

He fooled away the afternoon playing video games. 他下午玩电子游戏，虚度了时光。| 近义词：waste, fritter away, squander | 反义词：utilize, utilize efficiently, make good use of | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fools away his time watching TV. = 他把时间浪费在看电视上。
They fool away their money on useless things. = 他们把钱花在无用的东西上。

一般过去时:
She fooled away the entire summer. = 她虚度了整个夏天。
We fooled away our childhood playing in the streets. = 我们在街上玩耍，虚度了童年。


现在完成时:
I have fooled away too much time on this project. = 我在这个项目上浪费了太多时间。
He has fooled away his savings on gambling. = 他把积蓄都赌掉了。


过去完成时:
They had fooled away their chances before realizing their mistake. = 他们意识到错误之前就已经错过了机会。
She had fooled away years before finding her true passion. = 她花了数年时间才找到自己的真正热情。


将来时:
I will not fool away my future. = 我不会虚度我的未来。
He will fool away another day if he is not careful. = 如果他不小心，他还会浪费一天时间。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't fooled away my youth, I would be in a better position now. = 如果我没有虚度青春，我现在会处于更好的境地。
I wish I hadn't fooled away my college years. = 我真希望我没有虚度我的大学时光。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in passive voice because it describes an active wasting of time.)  The time was fooled away. 时间被浪费了。(不自然)
His money was fooled away on frivolous pursuits. 他的钱被花在了无意义的事情上。(不自然，更好表达为His money was wasted on frivolous pursuits)


疑问句:
How much time do you fool away each day? = 你每天浪费多少时间？
Are you fooling away your potential? = 你在浪费你的潜力吗？


否定句:
I will not fool away my opportunity. = 我不会浪费我的机会。
She doesn't fool away her time on trivial matters. = 她不会把时间浪费在琐事上。
