management's: /ˈmænɪdʒmənts/ | n. | 管理层的；管理部门的 |  The possessive form of "management," indicating something belonging to or associated with management. It refers to the actions, decisions, or possessions of a management team or group. |

例句：

Management's decision was met with resistance from the employees.  = 管理层的决定遭到了员工的抵制。

Management's new strategy aims to increase profitability. = 管理层的新的策略旨在提高盈利能力。


近义词：the management's,  executive's,  directors',  officials'

反义词：N/A (This is a possessive form, not a descriptive adjective with an inherent opposite)

词性:名词性物主代词


USAGE->  (This word itself doesn't conjugate as it's a possessive form. The verb "to manage" does conjugate.)
