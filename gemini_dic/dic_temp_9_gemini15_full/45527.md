curial:/ˈkjʊəriəl/| adj.| 重要的；关键的；枢纽的 |Crucial means extremely important or vital.  It refers to something that is essential for success or achieving a particular outcome.  The word often emphasizes the critical nature of something, highlighting its indispensable role. |例句：The timing of the announcement was crucial to its success.  这次宣布的时机对其成功至关重要。

The judge's decision was crucial in determining the outcome of the case.  法官的决定对案件的结果至关重要。


近义词：essential, vital, critical, important, indispensable, pivotal

反义词：unimportant, insignificant, trivial, minor
