rosy:/ˈroʊzi/ /ˈrəʊzi/ | adj. | 玫瑰色的；美好的；乐观的 |  Rose-colored; optimistic; characterized by or suggesting optimism or hope.  | The rosy cheeks of the child indicated good health.  孩子红润的脸颊表明身体健康。|  pink, flushed, optimistic | gloomy, pessimistic, dismal | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The future looks rosy. = 未来看起来一片光明。
Her cheeks are rosy from the cold. = 她因为寒冷而脸颊红润。

一般过去时:
The prospects were rosy before the crisis. = 危机爆发前前景一片光明。
Her complexion was rosy after a long walk. = 长时间散步后，她的肤色红润。

现在完成时:
Things have become rosy since they started working together. = 自从他们开始一起工作以来，情况已经变得一片光明。
Her health has been rosy ever since she started exercising. = 自从她开始锻炼以来，她的健康状况一直很好。

过去完成时:
The situation had looked rosy until the unexpected news arrived. = 直到收到意外的消息之前，情况一直看起来很乐观。
The report had painted a rosy picture of the company's financial health before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，报告对公司的财务状况描绘了一幅美好的图景。


将来时:
The forecast predicts a rosy economic outlook for next year. = 预测明年经济前景一片光明。
If everything goes well, the future will be rosy for them. = 如果一切顺利，他们的未来将一片光明。


虚拟语气:
If the economy were rosy, unemployment would be lower. = 如果经济形势一片大好，失业率就会降低。
I wish the future were as rosy as they predict. = 我希望未来能像他们预测的那样光明。


被动语态:
The situation was painted as rosy by the government. = 政府将形势描绘成一片光明。
The future is often seen as rosy by those who are unaware of potential problems. = 对那些没有意识到潜在问题的人来说，未来往往被视为一片光明。


疑问句:
Is the outlook really so rosy? = 前景真的那么光明吗？
Are her cheeks always so rosy? = 她的脸颊总是那么红润吗？


否定句:
The economic forecast is not rosy. = 经济预测并不乐观。
Her prospects are not rosy at the moment. = 她目前的前景并不乐观。
