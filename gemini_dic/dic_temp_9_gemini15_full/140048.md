oenophilist:/ˈoʊənəfɪlɪst/| n. |爱酒之人，品酒家|A person who loves and is knowledgeable about wine.  |He's a true oenophilist; his cellar is legendary.|近义词：wine lover, connoisseur, wine buff |反义词：None specifically, but someone who dislikes wine could be considered an antonym.


USAGE->

She enjoys tasting different vintages. = 她喜欢品尝不同年份的葡萄酒。
