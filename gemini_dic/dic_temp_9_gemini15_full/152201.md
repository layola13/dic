patricides:/ˈpætrisaɪdz/| n.|杀父罪；杀父者|The killing of one's father;  a person who kills their father.  The term can also refer to the act of killing a father figure. |例句：The ancient Greeks considered patricides a heinous crime. 古希腊人认为杀父是令人发指的罪行。

例句：He was accused of patricides after his father's mysterious death. 他父亲神秘死亡后，他被指控犯有杀父罪。


近义词：filicide (killing of a child), homicide (killing of a human being), murder


反义词：filial piety (respect for parents)


词性:名词
