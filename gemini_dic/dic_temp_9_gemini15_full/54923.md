disposes:/dɪˈspoʊz/ | vt. |处理；处置；安排；使倾向于 | To get rid of something; to arrange or settle something; to make someone more likely to do something. |例句：The council disposes of waste responsibly.  市政当局负责地处理垃圾。|例句：She disposed her troops strategically. 她战略性地部署了她的军队。 |近义词：dispose of, get rid of, arrange, settle, incline |反义词：retain, keep, accumulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disposes of his rubbish every week. = 他每周都处理垃圾。
The company disposes of its waste responsibly. = 这家公司负责任地处理废物。

一般过去时:
They disposed of the old furniture last month. = 他们上个月处理掉了旧家具。
She disposed of her inheritance wisely. = 她明智地处理了她的遗产。

现在完成时:
We have disposed of all the evidence. = 我们已经处理掉了所有证据。
The government has disposed of the problem effectively. = 政府已经有效地解决了这个问题。

过去完成时:
They had disposed of the body before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经处理掉了尸体。（此例句因涉及敏感内容，仅供语法示例，请勿模仿）
She had disposed of her old clothes before moving. = 她搬家之前已经处理掉了旧衣服。

将来时:
We will dispose of the waste according to regulations. = 我们将按照规定处理废物。
The company will dispose of its toxic materials safely. = 公司将安全地处理其有毒材料。

虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I would dispose of less things. = 如果我有更多空间，我就会扔掉更少的东西。
If we disposed of our waste properly, the environment would be cleaner. = 如果我们妥善处理垃圾，环境就会更清洁。

被动语态:
The waste is disposed of daily. = 废物每天都被处理。
The old building will be disposed of soon. = 这座旧建筑很快就会被处理掉。

疑问句:
How do you dispose of your electronic waste? = 你如何处理你的电子垃圾？
Where will the hazardous materials be disposed of? = 危险材料将在哪里处理？

否定句:
He didn't dispose of the evidence properly. = 他没有妥善处理证据。
They did not dispose of the waste according to the regulations. = 他们没有按照规定处理废物。
