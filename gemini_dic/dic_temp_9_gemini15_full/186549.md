riddles:/ˈrɪdlz/| n.|谜语，难题|A riddle is a question or statement intentionally phrased so as to require ingenuity in ascertaining its answer or meaning.  It often involves wordplay, metaphors, or other forms of figurative language.|Example:  "What has an eye but cannot see?" is a classic riddle.  这是一个经典的谜语。“什么东西有眼睛却看不见？”|近义词：enigma, puzzle, conundrum|反义词：answer, solution
