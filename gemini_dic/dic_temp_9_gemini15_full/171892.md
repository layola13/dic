punted:/ˈpʌntɪd/| v.|轻踢；尝试；把…押给；(橄榄球)踢球|To kick (something, especially a ball) in a particular way; to try something risky;  to bet on something; in American football, to kick the ball. |例句：He punted the ball downfield. 他把球踢向球场下端。 |例句：They punted on a new marketing strategy. 他们尝试了一种新的营销策略。|近义词：kick, try, gamble, wager |反义词：hold back, refrain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He punts the ball every game. = 他每场比赛都踢球。
She punts on risky investments. = 她避免进行高风险投资。


一般过去时:
The quarterback punted the ball. = 四分卫踢了球。
He punted on the deal after careful consideration. = 他仔细考虑后放弃了这笔交易。


现在完成时:
The team has punted several times this quarter. = 这支球队在本节比赛中已经几次踢球。
She has punted on her original plan and adopted a new one. = 她放弃了最初的计划，采纳了一个新的计划。


过去完成时:

He had punted on the idea before realizing its potential. = 他在意识到其潜力之前已经放弃了这个想法。


将来时:

They will punt on the project until next year. = 他们将把项目推迟到明年。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
