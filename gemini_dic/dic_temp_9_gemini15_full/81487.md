graveller:/ˈɡrævələr/| n. |碎石路面铺设者；砾石；（尤指）铺设碎石路面的工人|A person who lays gravel, especially on roads.  This word is less common and often refers to a person who works with gravel, particularly in road construction. It can also refer to the gravel itself, though this usage is less frequent.|例句：The graveller worked tirelessly to complete the driveway.  碎石路面铺设者辛勤工作以完成车道铺设。  The road was made of rough graveller. 这条路是用粗糙的碎石铺成的。|近义词：graveler, road worker,  paver (in the context of paving with gravel)|反义词：paver (in the context of paving with asphalt or concrete)
