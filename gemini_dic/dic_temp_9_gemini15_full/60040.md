education: /ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 教育；培养；训导 | The process of receiving or giving systematic instruction, especially at a school or university.  It encompasses the development of knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits. | 例句：He received a good education at a private school. 他在一所私立学校接受了良好的教育。 | 例句：The education of young children is crucial for their future development.  幼儿教育对他们未来的发展至关重要。 | 近义词：training, instruction, upbringing, schooling | 反义词：ignorance, illiteracy
