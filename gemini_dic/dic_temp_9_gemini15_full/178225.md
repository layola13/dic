recompact:/ˌriːkəmˈpækt/| vt.|重新压实；重新压缩|To compact again; to compress or consolidate again.|例句：The soil was re-compacted after the construction.  施工后土壤被重新压实。

例句：The recycling plant re-compacts the plastic bottles before shipping.  回收厂在运输前会重新压缩塑料瓶。


近义词：compress, consolidate, condense|反义词：expand, loosen, decompress|动词
