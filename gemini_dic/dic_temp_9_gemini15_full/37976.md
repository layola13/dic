complicate:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/ /ˈkɑːmplɪkeɪt/| vt.|使复杂化；使难懂|To make something more difficult or complex.|The new rules have complicated the process considerably. 新规使流程变得相当复杂。|  The situation was complicated by the arrival of the police.  警方的到来使情况更加复杂。| simplify|simplify|

一般现在时:
He complicates things unnecessarily. 他不必要地使事情复杂化。
They complicate the issue with their conflicting statements.  他们相互矛盾的陈述使问题复杂化。


一般过去时:
The unexpected rain complicated our travel plans.  突如其来的雨水打乱了我们的旅行计划。
