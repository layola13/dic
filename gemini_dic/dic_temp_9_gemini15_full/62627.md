enforcements:/ɪnˈfɔːrsmənts/ | n. | 执行；实施；强制 | The enforcements of the new traffic laws have reduced accidents.  新的交通法规的实施减少了事故。 | 近义词：implementations, executions | 反义词：neglects, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government enforces strict environmental regulations. = 政府执行严格的环境法规。
These rules are enforced consistently across all departments. = 这些规则在所有部门都得到了一致执行。


一般过去时:
The police enforced the court order. = 警方执行了法院的命令。
The company enforced a new dress code last year. = 公司去年实施了新的着装规定。


现在完成时:
They have enforced the ban on smoking in public places. = 他们已经强制执行了在公共场所禁烟的规定。
The authorities have enforced stricter penalties for drunk driving. = 当局已经对酒后驾车实施了更严厉的处罚。


过去完成时:
The judge had enforced the previous ruling before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，法官已经执行了之前的判决。
Before the new policy was implemented, they had enforced a stricter approach. = 在新政策实施之前，他们采取了更严格的措施。


将来时:
The government will enforce stricter penalties for tax evasion. = 政府将对逃税行为实施更严厉的处罚。
The new laws will be enforced starting next month. = 新法律将于下个月开始实施。


虚拟语气:
If the law were enforced properly, crime rates would decrease. = 如果法律得到适当执行，犯罪率就会下降。
I wish the regulations were enforced more effectively. = 我希望这些规定能得到更有效的执行。
