bye-election: /baɪɪˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 补选 | A bye-election is an election held to fill a political office that has become vacant between general elections.  This can be due to the death, resignation, or expulsion of the incumbent. |  The bye-election for the local council seat attracted a surprisingly high turnout.  这次地方议会席位的补选吸引了惊人数量的投票者。


The local MP's sudden death necessitated a bye-election.  当地国会议员的突然去世使得补选成为必要。| 近义词：special election, supplementary election | 反义词：general election
