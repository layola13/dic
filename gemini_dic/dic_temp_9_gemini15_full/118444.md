mesaticephalic:/ˌmɛzætɪˈsɛfəlɪk/| adj. |中头型的；中等头型的 |Having a cephalic index between 75 and 80.  This refers to the ratio of the maximum breadth of the head to its maximum length, expressed as a percentage.  A mesaticephalic head is neither particularly long and narrow (dolichocephalic) nor particularly broad and short (brachycephalic).| The study showed that the population was predominantly mesaticephalic.|近义词：medium-headed|反义词：dolichocephalic, brachycephalic


词源:

* **meso-:**  From Greek *mesos*, meaning "middle," "intermediate."
* **-cephalic:** From Greek *kephalē*, meaning "head."


用法说明:

mesaticephalic主要用作形容词，描述头型指数介于75到80之间的头型。  它是一个比较专业的术语，主要用于人类学、解剖学和医学等领域。  在日常生活中很少用到。


例句：

The anthropologist measured the skull's breadth and length to determine if it was dolichocephalic, brachycephalic, or mesaticephalic.
人类学家测量了头骨的宽度和长度，以确定它是长头型、短头型还是中头型。


The study categorized the participants based on their cranial indices, identifying three distinct groups: dolichocephalic, mesaticephalic, and brachycephalic.
该研究根据参与者的颅骨指数对参与者进行分类，确定了三个不同的群体：长头型、中头型和短头型。


近义词：

medium-headed (less formal)


反义词：

dolichocephalic (long-headed)
brachycephalic (short-headed)
