self-awareness:/ˈsɛlf əˈwɛərnəs/| n. |自我意识，自我认知 |The understanding and recognition of one's own personality, emotions, motivations, strengths, and weaknesses.  It involves being aware of how your actions and feelings affect yourself and others. |例句：Developing self-awareness is crucial for personal growth.  培养自我意识对个人成长至关重要。

例句：With increased self-awareness, she was able to better manage her stress levels. 通过提高自我意识，她能够更好地管理自己的压力水平。


近义词：self-knowledge, introspection, self-perception |反义词：self-ignorance, unawareness |词性：名词
