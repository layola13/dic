fractionated:/ˈfrækʃəneɪtɪd/| adj.|分馏的；分级的；分开的|Divided or separated into parts or fractions.  The process of separating a mixture into its components, especially by distillation.|例句：The crude oil was fractionated into various petroleum products. 原油被分馏成各种石油产品。
The student's presentation was fractionated into several smaller segments. 学生的演讲被分成几个较小的部分。|近义词：separated, divided, segregated|反义词：unified, combined, whole
