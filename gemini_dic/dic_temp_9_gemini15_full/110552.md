limescale:/ˈlaɪmˌskeɪl/| n. |水垢，水碱|A hard, chalky deposit of calcium carbonate that forms on the inside of kettles, pipes, and other surfaces in contact with hard water.|The limescale buildup in our kettle is terrible; we need to descale it soon.  我们的水壶里水垢堆积得很厉害，我们需要尽快除垢。|scale, deposit|clean water, soft water|
