eutychian: /juːˈtɪkiən/ | adj. |优提其的；属于优提其的 (指公元5世纪的基督教神学家优提其的学说)| Relating to Eutyches, a 5th-century monk and theologian whose teachings, known as Eutychianism, asserted that Christ had only one nature, a divine one, after the Incarnation, denying the distinct natures of Christ’s divinity and humanity.  | The Eutychian heresy caused considerable upheaval in the early Church.  优提其的异端在早期教会中引起了相当大的动荡。|  Monophysite, Monothelitic | Nestorian | adj.
