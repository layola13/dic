derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ | /dəˈraɪv/ | vt.  | 获得；取得；推断；源于 | To obtain something from a source. To deduce something. To trace the origin or development of something. | 例句：He derived great pleasure from his work. 他从工作中获得了极大的乐趣。  |  例句：We can derive the formula from these equations. 我们可以从这些方程式中推导出公式。 | obtain, deduce, infer |  deprive, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I derive satisfaction from helping others. = 我从帮助他人中获得满足感。
She derives her income from investments. = 她从投资中获得收入。

一般过去时:
He derived much knowledge from his studies. = 他从学习中获得了许多知识。
They derived their conclusions from the data. = 他们从数据中得出结论。

现在完成时:
Scientists have derived many benefits from this research. = 科学家们从这项研究中获得了许多益处。
We have derived a new method for solving the problem. = 我们已经找到了一种解决这个问题的新方法。

过去完成时:
He had derived considerable profit from his business before he retired. = 他退休前从他的生意中获得了可观的利润。
They had derived their inspiration from ancient mythology. = 他们从古代神话中汲取了灵感。

将来时:
We will derive more efficient methods from the project. = 我们将从该项目中获得更有效的方法。
They will derive significant benefits from the new policy. = 他们将从新政策中获得重大益处。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have derived greater benefits. = 如果我学习更努力，我会获得更大的好处。
I wish I could derive more joy from my work. = 我希望我能够从工作中获得更多快乐。

被动语态:
The results were derived from a large sample size. = 结果来自一个大的样本量。
The formula can be derived using simple algebra. = 这个公式可以用简单的代数推导出来。

疑问句:
Where do you derive your inspiration from? = 你的灵感来自哪里？
What benefits can be derived from this technology? = 从这项技术中可以获得什么好处？

否定句:
He didn't derive any satisfaction from his job. = 他从他的工作中没有得到任何满足感。
They could not derive a meaningful conclusion from the data. = 他们无法从数据中得出有意义的结论。
