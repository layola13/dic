inuit:/ˈɪnuɪt/| n. |因纽特人；因纽特语 |The Inuit are an indigenous people inhabiting the Arctic regions of Canada, Greenland, Alaska, and Russia.  因纽特人是居住在加拿大、格陵兰、阿拉斯加和俄罗斯北极地区的土著居民。|近义词：Eskimo (considered outdated and potentially offensive)|反义词：None|名词
