lapboard:/ˈlæpˌbɔːrd/ | n. |写字板；膝上板 | A flat board, often with a slightly raised edge, designed to rest on one's lap to provide a writing surface. It is often used for writing or drawing while seated, especially in situations where a table is not available.  | Example: She used a lapboard to write her notes on the train. 她用膝上板在火车上写笔记。|近义词：writing board, portable desk |反义词：None |
