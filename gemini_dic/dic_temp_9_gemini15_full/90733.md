humiliatory: /hjuːˈmɪlɪəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 羞辱性的，丢脸的 | Causing or characterized by humiliation; mortifying.  The experience was deeply humiliating, leaving her feeling small and insignificant. | 近义词：degrading, mortifying, shameful, embarrassing | 反义词：honorable, uplifting, glorious


例句：

The public humiliation was a deeply humiliating experience.  公开的羞辱是一次非常丢脸的经历。

His humiliating defeat left him feeling dejected. 他惨败的经历让他感到沮丧。
