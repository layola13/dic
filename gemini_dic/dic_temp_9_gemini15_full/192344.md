scrimmaging:/ˈskrɪmɪdʒɪŋ/| n. |练习赛；混战；争夺 |The act of engaging in a scrimmage; a practice game or a rough and disorganized fight or struggle.  |例句：The football team engaged in a vigorous scrimmaging session before the game.  橄榄球队在比赛前进行了一次剧烈的练习赛。
The two teams were locked in a fierce scrimmaging for possession of the ball. 两队为了争夺球权而陷入激烈的混战。|近义词：practice, rehearsal, tussle, scuffle |反义词：formal game, orderly competition
