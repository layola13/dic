componental:/ˌkɒmpəˈnɛntəl/| adj.|成分的；组成的|Relating to or denoting components; of, relating to, or characteristic of components.  The word emphasizes the aspect of being a constituent part of a larger whole. |例句：The componental analysis of the sentence revealed its underlying structure.  句子成分分析揭示了其底层结构。

例句：The componental approach to teaching focuses on breaking down complex skills into smaller, manageable units.  这种教学的成分方法侧重于将复杂技能分解成更小、更容易管理的单元。


近义词：constituent, component, elemental

反义词：integral (in the sense of not being separable), holistic

词性：形容词
