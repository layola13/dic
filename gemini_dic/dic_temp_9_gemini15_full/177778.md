recede:/rɪˈsiːd/ | vi. | 退去；减弱；衰退 | To move back or further away from a previous position.  |例句：The floodwaters slowly receded. 洪水慢慢退去。
The pain gradually receded. 疼痛逐渐减轻。|近义词：withdraw, retreat, subside, ebb |反义词：advance, proceed, progress, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide recedes twice a day. = 潮汐一天退去两次。
His hairline is receding. = 他的发际线正在后移。

一般过去时:
The army receded from the border. = 军队从边境撤军。
The symptoms receded after a few days. = 几天后症状消退了。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have receded, leaving behind a trail of mud. = 洪水已经退去，留下一条泥泞的小路。
His influence has receded in recent years. = 近年来他的影响力下降了。

过去完成时:
The glacier had receded significantly before the researchers arrived. = 在研究人员到达之前，冰川已经明显退缩了。
The pain had receded by the time he reached the hospital. = 等他到达医院时，疼痛已经减轻了。

将来时:
The storm is expected to recede by tomorrow. = 预计风暴将在明天减弱。
The economic crisis will eventually recede. = 经济危机最终会过去。

虚拟语气:
If the waters receded, we could cross the river. = 如果水退了，我们就能过河。
I wish my fear would recede. = 我希望我的恐惧能够消失。

被动语态:
(Recede is an intransitive verb and cannot be used in the passive voice.)  退去这个动作本身就是不及物的，不能用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Is the tide receding? = 潮水正在退去吗？
Will the symptoms recede soon? = 症状很快就会消退吗？


否定句:
The floodwaters did not recede quickly enough. = 洪水退去的速度不够快。
His influence has not receded despite the scandal. = 尽管发生了丑闻，他的影响力并没有减弱。


PHRASE->
recede into the background = 退居幕后
recede from view = 从视野中消失
recede from memory = 从记忆中消失
recede into the distance = 消失在远处
recede into oblivion = 销声匿迹
