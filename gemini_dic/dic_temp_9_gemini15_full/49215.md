delectation:/ˌdɛlɪkˈteɪʃən/| n. |快乐；乐趣；享受|The act of giving great pleasure or delight; enjoyment; satisfaction.  It often implies a refined or intellectual pleasure.|例句：He found delectation in tending his garden. 他从侍弄花园中获得乐趣。  The delectation of reading a good book is unparalleled. 阅读好书的乐趣无与伦比。|近义词：pleasure, delight, enjoyment, gratification |反义词：displeasure, misery, suffering, anguish |名词
