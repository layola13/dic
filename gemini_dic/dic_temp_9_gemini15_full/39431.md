connectedness:/kəˌnɛktɪdnɪs/ | n. |关联性；连接性；联系；相互关联 |The state of being connected or interconnected; the quality of being related or linked.  It refers to the relationships and interactions between things, people, or systems. |例句：The connectedness of the global economy is undeniable. 全球经济的关联性是不可否认的。

例句：The feeling of connectedness with nature is essential for well-being. 与自然的联系感对幸福至关重要。


近义词：interconnectedness, relationship, linkage, association, cohesion

反义词：isolation, separation, disconnectedness, fragmentation
