deforce:/dɪˈfɔːrs/ | vt. |剥夺；压制；驱逐；(尤指)非法剥夺土地所有权 |To wrongfully dispossess someone of land or property, especially by withholding legal possession.  The term often implies a subtle or indirect action rather than outright force. |Example: The landlord attempted to deforce the tenant from the property without proper legal proceedings.  房东试图在没有适当法律程序的情况下将房客驱逐出房产。

|Example: The king's enemies tried to deforce him of his rightful throne. 国王的敌人试图剥夺他应有的王位。


|近义词：dispossess, oust, evict, deprive |反义词：grant, bestow, restore, reinstate |动词
