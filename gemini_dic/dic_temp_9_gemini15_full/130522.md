nonaccountable: /ˌnɒnəˈkaʊntəbəl/ | adj. | 不负责任的；无法解释的；无法追究责任的 | Not subject to being held responsible or accountable; unable to be explained or justified. |


例句：

The company's nonaccountable spending habits led to its financial ruin.  公司的无法追究责任的消费习惯导致了它的财务破产。

His actions were nonaccountable, leaving everyone baffled.  他的行为无法解释，让每个人都感到困惑。


近义词：unaccountable, irresponsible, inexplicable, unanswerable

反义词：accountable, responsible, explainable, justifiable


词源：

The word "nonaccountable" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "accountable".  "Accountable" ultimately derives from the verb "account," which comes from the Old French word "aconter" (to narrate, recount), itself from the Latin "ad" (to) + "computare" (to compute, reckon). Thus, "accountable" signifies being able to give a satisfactory explanation or justification for one's actions.
