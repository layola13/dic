dogfighters:/ˈdɒɡˌfaɪtərz/| n.|狗斗飞行员；进行空战的飞机|A dogfighter is a pilot skilled in close-range aerial combat, or a type of aircraft designed for such combat.  The term often evokes images of World War II air battles.|例句：The dogfighters engaged in a furious aerial ballet of twists and turns.  狗斗飞行员们进行了激烈的空中缠斗。  The museum displays a rare WWII dogfighter. 博物馆展出了一架罕见的二战狗斗飞机。|近义词：fighter pilot, aerial combat pilot|反义词：n/a
