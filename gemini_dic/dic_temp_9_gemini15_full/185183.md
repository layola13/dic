retortion:/rɪˈtɔːrʃən/| n. |报复；反击；反驳|The act of retaliating or returning like for like; counteraction; reprisal.  It often implies a forceful or harsh response. |例句：The government's retortion to the economic sanctions was swift and decisive. 政府对经济制裁的反击迅速而果断。

例句：His sharp retortion silenced his critics. 他强烈的反驳使批评者哑口无言。


近义词：reprisal, retaliation, revenge, counterattack |反义词：forgiveness, reconciliation, appeasement |词性：名词

词源:
The word "retortion" comes from the Old French word "retorsion," which itself is derived from the Latin word "retorsionem," meaning "a twisting back,"  reflecting the idea of turning something back against its source.
