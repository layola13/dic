laboring:/ˈleɪbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 辛勤工作的；费力的；艰难的 | Engaged in strenuous work; characterized by hard work or effort; difficult or slow.  |例句：He was laboring under a heavy burden. 他背负着沉重的负担。  |例句：The laboring oarsmen struggled against the strong current.  辛勤划桨的水手们奋力对抗强劲的洋流。|近义词：toiling, struggling, striving, strenuous |反义词：idle, effortless, easy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is laboring in the fields. = 他在田里辛勤劳作。
They are laboring to finish the project on time. = 他们正在努力按时完成项目。

一般过去时:
She labored tirelessly for many years. = 她辛勤工作了很多年。
The workers labored through the night to repair the bridge. = 工人们通宵达旦地工作来修理桥梁。


现在完成时:
He has been laboring under a misconception. = 他一直误解了。
They have labored long and hard to achieve their goals. = 他们为了实现目标辛勤工作了很久。


过去完成时:
They had labored for days before they finally completed the task. = 他们在最终完成任务之前已经辛勤工作了好几天。
She had labored to make ends meet for her family. = 她为了养家糊口而辛勤工作。


将来时:
He will be laboring away at his desk all day. = 他将一整天都在办公桌前辛勤工作。
They will be laboring to meet the deadline. = 他们将努力赶上截止日期。


虚拟语气:
If he were laboring under less pressure, he would be more productive. = 如果他承受的压力小一些，他的效率就会更高。
I wish I weren't laboring under so many misconceptions. = 我希望我没有那么多误解。


被动语态:
The land was labored extensively by generations of farmers. = 这片土地被几代农民辛勤耕耘。
The point was labored excessively in his presentation. = 他在陈述中对这一点过于强调。


疑问句:
Is he laboring under the illusion that he can win? = 他是不是抱有能赢的幻想？
Are they laboring under any difficulties? = 他们是否遇到任何困难？


否定句:
He is not laboring under any illusions. = 他没有抱有任何幻想。
They are not laboring in vain. = 他们的努力不是徒劳的。


PHRASE->

labor under a delusion/misconception: 误以为；抱有错觉
labor in vain: 白费力气；徒劳无功
laboriously: 费力地；辛苦地
labor market: 劳动力市场
labor relations: 劳资关系
