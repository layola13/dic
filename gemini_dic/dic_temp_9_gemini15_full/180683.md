regresses:/rɪˈɡrɛs/| v.|倒退；退化；回归；回复|To return to a former or less developed state.|The patient regressed to a childlike state after the trauma.  病人遭受创伤后退化到孩童般的状态。|The economy has regressed significantly in the past year.  经济在过去一年中大幅倒退。|progress, advance|progress, advance|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regresses under pressure. = 他在压力下退缩。
The company regresses in its sales every year. = 这家公司的销售额每年都在下降。

一般过去时:
She regressed to her childhood habits. = 她退回到她童年的习惯。
The project regressed due to funding cuts. = 由于资金削减，项目进展倒退。

现在完成时:
The country has regressed economically since the war. = 自战争以来，这个国家在经济上已经倒退了。
The patient has regressed significantly in the past week. = 病人在过去一周中明显退化了。

过去完成时:
The team had regressed before the new coach arrived. = 新教练到来之前，球队已经退步了。
The economy had regressed considerably before the reforms were implemented. = 在改革实施之前，经济已经相当衰退了。

将来时:
The government fears the country will regress without further investment. = 政府担心如果不进一步投资，国家将会倒退。
If we don't act now, the environment will regress further. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，环境将会进一步恶化。

虚拟语气:
If he were to regress further, we would need to intervene. = 如果他进一步退化，我们就需要介入。
I wish the situation hadn't regressed so dramatically. = 我希望情况没有如此剧烈地恶化。

被动语态:
The patient was regressed into a hypnotic state. = 病人被催眠进入一种催眠状态。（此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
The discussion was regressed to earlier points. = 讨论又回到了之前的论点。（此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）

疑问句:
Does the child regress under stress? = 孩子在压力下会退缩吗？
Has the technology regressed in recent years? = 近年来这项技术倒退了吗？

否定句:
The company did not regress despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，公司并没有倒退。
The situation has not regressed significantly. = 情况并没有显著恶化。
