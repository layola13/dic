endangered:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərd/ | adj. | 濒危的，面临危险的 |  Threatened with extinction; in danger of becoming extinct.  | Example: The giant panda is an endangered species.  大熊猫是一种濒危物种。 The endangered bird is protected by law. 这种濒危鸟类受法律保护。|近义词：threatened, imperiled, jeopardized | 反义词：safe, secure, flourishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tiger is endangered in many parts of Asia. = 老虎在亚洲许多地区都面临灭绝的危险。
This plant species is endangered due to habitat loss. = 由于栖息地丧失，这种植物物种正濒临灭绝。


一般过去时:
The dodo bird was endangered before it went extinct. = 渡渡鸟在灭绝之前就已经濒临灭绝了。
The population of this fish was endangered by pollution. = 这种鱼类的数量因污染而减少。


现在完成时:
Many species have become endangered due to human activity. = 许多物种由于人类活动而濒临灭绝。
The government has implemented measures to protect endangered animals. = 政府已实施措施以保护濒危动物。


过去完成时:
The species had already been endangered before the conservation efforts began. = 在保护工作开始之前，该物种就已经濒临灭绝了。
The habitat destruction had endangered the local wildlife. = 栖息地的破坏使当地野生动物处于危险之中。


将来时:
If we don't act now, many more species will be endangered. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，将会有更多物种濒临灭绝。
This species will likely be endangered within the next decade. = 这种物种很可能在未来十年内濒临灭绝。


虚拟语气:
If the pollution were stopped, the species wouldn't be endangered. = 如果污染能够停止，该物种就不会濒临灭绝了。
It is vital that endangered species be protected. = 保护濒危物种至关重要。


被动语态:
Many habitats have been damaged, and many species are being endangered. = 许多栖息地已被破坏，许多物种正面临灭绝的危险。
The ecosystem was endangered by the industrial waste. = 生态系统因工业废料而受到威胁。


疑问句:
Are these animals endangered? = 这些动物濒危吗？
Is the current protection plan effective for endangered species? = 目前的保护计划对濒危物种有效吗？


否定句:
The species is not endangered yet, but it is threatened. = 该物种目前尚未濒危，但已受到威胁。
That bird is not endangered, it's quite common. = 那只鸟并不濒危，它很常见。
