coheir:/ˈkoʊhɛr/| n. |共同继承人|A co-heir is one of two or more people who inherit property or title from the same person.  Coheirs inherit equally unless otherwise specified in a will.|The two brothers were coheirs to their father's estate. = 这两兄弟是他们父亲遗产的共同继承人。|The sisters are coheirs to their mother's vast fortune. = 这几个姐妹是她们母亲巨额财富的共同继承人。|heir, joint heir|sole heir
