forever: /fɔːˈɛvər/ | adv. | 永远，永久 |  Forever means for all time in the future; eternally. It can also be used figuratively to express a very long time. |例句：I will love you forever. 我会永远爱你。

例句：Their love will last forever. 他们的爱会永远持续下去。


近义词：eternally, perpetually, always, incessantly | 反义词：never, temporarily, briefly


词性：副词
