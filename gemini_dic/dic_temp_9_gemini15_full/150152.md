pan-frying:/ˈpænˌfraɪɪŋ/| n. |煎锅煎|The act of cooking food in a pan with a small amount of fat or oil over moderate heat. This method involves browning the food and then cooking it through by turning it frequently.|例句：Pan-frying is a great way to cook chicken breasts. 煎锅煎是烹制鸡胸肉的好方法。
Pan-frying the vegetables until they are tender-crisp. 将蔬菜煎至嫩脆。|近义词：sautéing, stir-frying|反义词：boiling, steaming, baking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pan-fry my eggs every morning. = 我每天早上都煎鸡蛋。
She pan-fries the tofu until golden brown. = 她把豆腐煎至金黄色。
