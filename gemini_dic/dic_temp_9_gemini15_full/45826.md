customhouse:/ˈkʌstəməsˌhaʊs/| n. |海关；海关大楼|A customhouse is a building where customs duties are collected and where goods are inspected.  It's the office of customs officials.|例句：The ship docked at the customhouse for inspection. 这艘船停靠在海关进行检查。|例句：We had to clear customs at the customhouse before leaving the country. 我们必须在离开国家之前在海关办理通关手续。|近义词：customs office, customs house, port of entry|反义词：None specifically.
