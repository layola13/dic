The word often suggests a deliberate and conscious choice to remain pure. |例句：Her chastest writings are still considered provocative by modern standards. 她最纯洁的著作即使以现代标准来看也依然被认为具有煽动性。

The painting, in its chastest form, focused on simple lines and colors. 这幅画以其最朴素的形式，专注于简单的线条和色彩。


近义词：purest, purest, most innocent, most virtuous |反义词：lewdest, most licentious, most impure


词源:  The word "chastest" is the superlative form of the adjective "chaste," deriving from the Old French "chast," meaning "pure, virtuous, continent."  This Old French word ultimately traces back to the Latin "castus," sharing the same meaning.
