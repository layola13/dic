词源:
The word "parsed" comes from the verb "parse," which originates from the Latin word "pars," meaning "part."  In linguistics, parsing refers to the process of breaking down a sentence into its constituent parts to understand its grammatical structure.  The use extended to computer science to describe the analysis of data structures like code or XML.
