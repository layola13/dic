preinfections:/ˌpriːɪnˈfɛkʃənz/| n. | 预感染；先期感染 | Infections that occur before a primary infection.  These infections can sometimes influence the course or outcome of the subsequent primary infection. For example, prior exposure to certain viruses might offer some level of protection against a later, more severe infection.  | Example:  Studies are investigating the impact of preinfections on the severity of COVID-19.  研究正在调查预感染对COVID-19严重程度的影响。 |近义词: prior infections, antecedent infections | 反义词: postinfections |名词
