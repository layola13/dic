offprinting: /ˈɒfˌprɪntɪŋ/ | n. |抽印本，单行本|The process of producing separate copies of an article or chapter from a larger publication, such as a journal or book.|例句：The author requested offprints of his article for distribution to colleagues. 作者要求为他的文章制作抽印本分发给同事。|近义词：reprint, separate print|反义词：N/A

The journal provides authors with offprints of their articles.  该期刊为作者提供其文章的抽印本。
