pastries:/ˈpeɪstriːz/| n.|糕点，点心|Baked goods made from dough, often sweet and containing fillings such as fruit, cream, or chocolate.  Pastries can be made from various types of dough, including puff pastry, shortcrust pastry, and choux pastry.|例句：The bakery offers a wide variety of pastries, from croissants to éclairs. 这家面包店提供各种各样的糕点，从羊角面包到泡芙。|例句：For dessert, we had a selection of delicious pastries with coffee. 甜点，我们喝着咖啡，吃着各种美味的糕点。|近义词：baked goods, desserts, sweets|反义词：savory dishes
