hypercoagulability:/ˌhaɪpərˌkoʊæɡjʊləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 高凝状态，血液高凝性 |  An increased tendency of the blood to clot. This condition can lead to various health problems, including thrombosis (blood clot formation in a blood vessel), embolism (blood clot traveling through the bloodstream), and stroke.  | Example:  The patient's hypercoagulability was a major concern during surgery.  这位病人的高凝状态在手术期间是一个主要问题。

|近义词:thrombophilia, hypercoagulable state |反义词:hypocoagulability |词性:noun
