conf: /kɒnf/ /kɑːnf/ | n. |  会议；秘密；信任；宗教仪式 | A formal meeting for a particular purpose;  a secret; trust or confidence; a religious rite or ceremony. |  例句：The conference was held in Geneva.  会议在日内瓦举行。  The company is in complete confidence about its future. 公司对其未来充满信心。 | 近义词：meeting, gathering, secret, trust, faith | 反义词：distrust, suspicion, deceit


USAGE->

The conference begins tomorrow. = 会议明天开始。
He attends many conferences each year. = 他每年参加许多会议。

一般过去时:
The conference ended yesterday. = 会议昨天结束了。

现在完成时:
