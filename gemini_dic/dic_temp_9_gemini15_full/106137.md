knitting:/ˈnɪtɪŋ/| n. |编织；针织品|The act or process of making fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with needles; also, the fabric produced in this way.  Knitting is a relaxing hobby for many people.|例句：She enjoys knitting scarves in the winter. 她喜欢在冬天织围巾。|例句：That sweater is a beautiful example of hand-knitting. 那件毛衣是手工编织的漂亮例子。|近义词：crocheting, weaving|反义词：unraveling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She knits every evening. = 她每天晚上都织毛衣。
He knits sweaters for his grandchildren. = 他为他的孙子孙女织毛衣。

一般过去时:
I knitted a hat last week. = 我上周织了一顶帽子。
My grandmother knitted me a blanket. = 我祖母为我织了一条毯子。

现在完成时:
She has knitted several blankets this year. = 她今年已经织了好几条毯子了。
He has knitted since he was a child. = 他从小就一直在织东西。

过去完成时:
She had knitted the entire sweater before the party. = 派对之前她已经织好了整件毛衣。
They had knitted all the socks before the winter arrived. = 冬天到来之前他们已经织好了所有的袜子。

将来时:
I will knit a new scarf this weekend. = 我这个周末会织一条新围巾。
She will have knitted the baby blanket by next month. = 下个月她就会把婴儿毯子织好了。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would knit a quilt. = 如果我有更多时间，我会织一床被子。
I wish I could knit as well as my grandmother. = 我希望我能像我祖母一样擅长织毛衣。

被动语态:
The sweater was knitted by my aunt. = 这件毛衣是我姑姑织的。
Many blankets are knitted by volunteers for charity. = 许多毯子由志愿者为慈善机构编织。

疑问句:
Do you knit? = 你会织毛衣吗？
What are you knitting? = 你在织什么？

否定句:
He doesn't knit very often. = 他不经常织毛衣。
She didn't knit the sweater herself. = 这件毛衣不是她自己织的。
