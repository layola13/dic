colorer: /ˈkʌlə(r)ər/ | n. |着色者；染色者；着色剂 | A person or thing that colors something.  It can refer to a person who applies color, a substance used for coloring, or a software program that alters the color of images. | Example: The colorer meticulously applied the dye to the fabric. 着色者一丝不苟地将染料涂抹在织物上。  The digital colorer enhanced the photograph's vibrancy. 数字着色器增强了照片的鲜艳度。 |近义词：dyer, tinter, colorist |反义词：bleacher (in the context of removing color) | 名词
