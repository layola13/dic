entertake:/ˈɛntərteɪk/| vt.|承担，从事，进行|to undertake something means to start working on something that is likely to be difficult or time-consuming.  It implies commitment and responsibility.|He undertook the challenging task of climbing Mount Everest. 他承担了攀登珠穆朗玛峰的艰巨任务。|近义词：undertake, commence, begin |反义词: abandon, forsake, relinquish


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He undertakes many projects every year. 他每年承担许多项目。
She undertakes the responsibility of caring for her aging parents. 她承担起照顾年迈父母的责任。

一般过去时:
They undertook a detailed investigation into the accident. 他们对事故进行了详细调查。
He undertook to finish the report by Friday. 他答应在星期五之前完成报告。

现在完成时:
She has undertaken several ambitious research projects. 她已经承担了几项雄心勃勃的研究项目。
We have undertaken to provide financial assistance. 我们已经承诺提供财政援助。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had undertaken to review all the proposals. 会议之前，他们已经承诺审查所有提案。
She had undertaken a lot of work before she went on vacation. 她休假前已经做了很多工作。

将来时:
They will undertake a new marketing campaign next quarter. 下个季度他们将开展新的营销活动。
He will undertake the surgery next week. 他将在下周进行手术。


虚拟语气:
If he undertook the job, he would be successful. 如果他承担这项工作，他会成功的。
I wish I had undertaken that opportunity. 我希望我当时抓住了那个机会。

被动语态:
The project was undertaken by a team of experts. 这个项目是由一个专家团队承担的。
The repairs will be undertaken as soon as possible.  维修工作将尽快进行。

疑问句:
Will you undertake this difficult assignment? 你会承担这项困难的任务吗？
Who undertook the responsibility of organizing the event? 谁承担了组织这次活动的责任？

否定句:
He did not undertake the responsibility. 他没有承担责任。
She refused to undertake the task. 她拒绝承担这项任务。
