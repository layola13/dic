lattice-points:/ˈlætɪs pɔɪnts/ | n. | 格点；晶格点 | Lattice points are points in a lattice structure, often used in mathematics and crystallography to represent the positions of atoms or other elements within a crystal.  They are points with integer coordinates in a coordinate system. | Example:  The number of lattice points within a given region is a topic studied in geometry.  格点在给定区域内的数量是几何学中研究的一个课题。 The calculation of lattice points in a high-dimensional space can be computationally intensive. 高维空间中格点的计算可能需要大量的计算。 | 近义词：grid points, nodal points | 反义词：无  | 名词
