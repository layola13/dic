mentioned:/ˈmenʃənd/| v. |提及，提到|To speak of or refer to (something or someone) briefly or incidentally.|例句：He mentioned his trip to Paris. 他提到了他去巴黎的旅行。|近义词：referred to, alluded to, noted|反义词：ignored, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mentions his work often. 他经常提到他的工作。
She mentions the problem in her report. 她在她的报告中提到了这个问题。

一般过去时:
They mentioned the meeting yesterday. 他们昨天提到了会议。
He mentioned that he was tired. 他提到他累了。

现在完成时:
She has mentioned the project several times. 她已经多次提到这个项目了。
Have you mentioned the deadline to him? 你有没有跟他提到截止日期？


过去完成时:
He had mentioned the issue before the meeting. 他在会议之前就提到了这个问题。
They had mentioned it several times already. 他们之前已经多次提到了。

将来时:
He will mention the plan at the meeting. 他将在会议上提及这个计划。
She will mention her concerns to the manager. 她会向经理提及她的担忧。


虚拟语气:
If he mentioned it, I would have known. 如果他提到了，我就知道了。
I wish he had mentioned the difficulty earlier. 我希望他早点提到这个困难。

被动语态:
The problem was mentioned in the report. 这个问题在报告中被提到了。
The issue has been mentioned repeatedly.  这个问题已被反复提及。

疑问句:
Did he mention anything important? 他提到什么重要的事情了吗？
What did she mention about the project? 她关于这个项目提到了什么？


否定句:
He didn't mention anything about the accident. 他没有提及任何关于事故的事情。
She didn't mention her concerns. 她没有提到她的担忧。
