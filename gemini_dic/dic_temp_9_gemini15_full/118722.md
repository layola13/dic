/ˈmɛtədʒ/| n. |元数据，元信息|Data that provides information about other data.  It describes, explains, locates, or otherwise makes it easier to retrieve, use, or manage data.  Metage is often used in digital libraries, databases, and other information systems to organize and search large amounts of information.|Example:  The metage for this image includes the date it was taken, the location, and the photographer's name.  这个图像的元数据包括拍摄日期、地点和摄影师姓名。|近义词：metadata, descriptive data|反义词：data (in the sense of the information itself, not its description)|名词
