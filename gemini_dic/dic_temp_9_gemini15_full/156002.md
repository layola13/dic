photojournalist:/ˈfoʊtoʊˈdʒɜːrnəlɪst/|/ˈfəʊtəʊˈdʒɜːnəlɪst/| n. |摄影记者|A photojournalist is a journalist who uses photography as their primary means of reporting news and telling stories. They combine journalistic skills with photographic expertise to capture compelling images that inform, persuade, and move viewers.|Example: The photojournalist captured a powerful image of the aftermath of the earthquake.|近义词：press photographer, news photographer|反义词：None
