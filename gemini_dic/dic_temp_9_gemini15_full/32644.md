chisels:/ˈtʃɪzəl/| n.|凿子|A chisel is a tool with a sharp, flat edge used for shaping wood, stone, or metal by cutting or chipping away small pieces.|例句：He used a chisel to carve the intricate details into the wood. 他用凿子在木头上雕刻出复杂的细节。|近义词：gouge, adze|反义词:None

He chisels away at the stone. = 他用凿子凿石头。
