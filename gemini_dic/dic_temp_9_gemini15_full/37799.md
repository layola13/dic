compelling: /kəmˈpɛlɪŋ/ | adj. | 引人入胜的；令人信服的；强迫性的 |

英文解析:  Something compelling is so interesting or exciting that it holds your attention completely. It can also mean something so persuasive that it makes you believe it or do something.  In a third sense, it can refer to something that forces you to do something.

例句:

The compelling story kept me glued to my seat. = 这引人入胜的故事让我一直坐在座位上。

His compelling argument convinced the jury of his innocence. = 他令人信服的论点使陪审团相信了他的清白。


近义词: persuasive, convincing, captivating, fascinating, irresistible, forceful

反义词: unconvincing, unpersuasive, boring, dull, tedious
