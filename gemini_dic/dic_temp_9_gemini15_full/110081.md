lichenographist: /ˌlaɪkənˈɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. |地衣学家|A lichenographist is a scientist who studies lichens.  They are experts in the taxonomy, ecology, physiology, and biochemistry of lichens. |例句：The lichenographist meticulously documented the diverse lichen species found in the rainforest.  地衣学家一丝不苟地记录了雨林中发现的各种地衣物种。  |近义词：lichenologist |反义词：无
