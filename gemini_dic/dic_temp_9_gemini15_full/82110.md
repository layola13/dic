groggiest:/ˈɡrɑːɡiɪst/| adj.|极度疲惫的，昏昏沉沉的|Extremely tired and weak, feeling mentally confused.  The word implies a state of sluggishness and disorientation, often caused by illness, lack of sleep, or intoxication.|例句：After the marathon, I felt groggiest than ever. 马拉松之后，我感到前所未有的疲惫。 He was groggiest from the medication. 他因为药物而昏昏沉沉。|近义词：dazed, drowsy, lethargic, sleepy, exhausted|反义词：alert, energetic, refreshed
