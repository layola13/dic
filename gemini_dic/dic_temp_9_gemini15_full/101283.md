involve:/ɪnˈvɒlv/ | /ɪnˈvɑːlv/ | vt. | 包含；涉及；使卷入；牵涉 | To include or contain as a part or component; to have as a necessary consequence; to bring into participation or connection; to engage deeply. |例句：The job involves a lot of travel. 这份工作需要大量的出差。

The accident involved three cars. 这起事故涉及三辆车。

近义词：include, encompass, implicate, engage | 反义词：exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project involves extensive research. = 这个项目涉及大量的研究。
Her job involves working long hours. = 她的工作需要长时间工作。

一般过去时:
The scandal involved several high-ranking officials. = 这场丑闻牵涉到几位高级官员。
The investigation involved interviewing dozens of witnesses. = 调查涉及采访数十名证人。

现在完成时:
The company has involved itself in several charitable causes. = 这家公司已经参与了几项慈善事业。
The dispute has involved numerous legal battles. = 这场纠纷已经引发了多次法律诉讼。

过去完成时:
Before the trial began, the lawyer had involved himself in the case thoroughly. = 在审判开始之前，律师已经彻底参与了此案。
It had involved a long and complex negotiation process. = 这其中涉及一个漫长而复杂的谈判过程。


将来时:
The new policy will involve significant changes to the system. = 新政策将对系统带来重大变化。
The upcoming summit will involve representatives from several countries. = 即将来临的峰会将邀请来自多个国家的代表参加。


虚拟语气:
If the plan involved more risk, we would not proceed. = 如果这个计划风险更大，我们不会继续进行。
I wish my job didn't involve so much paperwork. = 我希望我的工作不需要处理那么多文书工作。


被动语态:
Many people were involved in the accident. = 许多人卷入了这场事故。
The decision was involved in a series of political compromises. = 这个决定是经过一系列政治妥协后做出的。


疑问句:
What exactly does the job involve? = 这份工作到底包括什么？
Are you involved in this project? = 你参与这个项目吗？


否定句:
The incident did not involve any violence. = 这起事件没有涉及任何暴力行为。
He was not involved in the decision-making process. = 他没有参与决策过程。


PHRASE->
be involved in = 参与...
get involved = 卷入
involved in = 参与...
deeply involved = 深度参与
become involved = 卷入
