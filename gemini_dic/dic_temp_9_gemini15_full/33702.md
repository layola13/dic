ciphered:/ˈsaɪfərd/ | adj. | 加密的，用密码写的 |  Converted into or written in cipher; encoded.  | Example: The message was ciphered to protect its secrecy.  这条消息被加密以保护其机密性。 |近义词：encoded, encrypted, coded | 反义词：decoded, decrypted, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government ciphers sensitive information. = 政府对敏感信息进行加密。
She ciphers her diary entries for privacy. = 她用密码写日记以保护隐私。


一般过去时:
They ciphered the message before sending it. = 他们在发送消息之前对其进行了加密。
He ciphered the code successfully. = 他成功地破译了密码。


现在完成时:
She has ciphered all the important documents. = 她已经将所有重要的文件都加密了。
We have ciphered the data to prevent unauthorized access. = 我们已经对数据进行了加密以防止未经授权的访问。


过去完成时:
They had ciphered the message before the enemy intercepted it. = 敌人截获消息之前，他们已经对其进行了加密。
She had ciphered the letter before posting it. = 她寄信之前已经将信加密了。


将来时:
We will cipher the data before transmitting it. = 我们将在传输数据之前对其进行加密。

虚拟语气:
If I knew the cipher, I would decipher the message. = 如果我知道密码，我就会破译这条消息。
It would be safer if we ciphered all communications. = 如果我们对所有通信进行加密，将会更安全。

否定句:
