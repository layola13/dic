latitudes:/ˈlætɪˌtuːdz/| n. |纬度；范围；自由 |The latitudes refer to the angular distance of a place north or south of the Earth's equator.  Latitudes are expressed in degrees, minutes, and seconds.  The term can also refer to a scope or range of things, or a degree of freedom. |例句：The city is located at a high latitude. 这座城市位于高纬度地区。

例句：They enjoyed the latitudes of their summer vacation. 他们享受着暑假带来的自由。

近义词：degrees, ranges, scopes, freedoms

反义词：longitudes (经度), restrictions (限制)
