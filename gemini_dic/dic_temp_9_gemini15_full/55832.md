dividends:/ˈdɪvɪdɛndz/ | n. |股息，红利 |Profits of a company that are paid to the shareholders.  Dividends are usually paid out quarterly.  |例句：The company announced a substantial dividend payout to its shareholders. 这家公司宣布向股东支付巨额股息。|例句：Investors are eager to receive high dividends from their investments. 投资者渴望从投资中获得高额股息。|近义词：profits, returns, payout |反义词：losses


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company pays dividends to its shareholders every quarter. = 公司每季度向股东支付股息。
She receives dividends from her stock investments. = 她从股票投资中获得股息。


一般过去时:
The company paid a large dividend last year. = 公司去年支付了巨额股息。
He received his dividends in the mail. = 他通过邮件收到了他的股息。


现在完成时:
The company has paid out substantial dividends this year. = 公司今年已支付了巨额股息。
She has received her dividends for the past three years. = 她过去三年都收到了股息。


过去完成时:
They had already received their dividends before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，他们已经收到了股息。
The company had paid out all its available dividends. = 公司已经支付了所有可用的股息。


将来时:
The company will pay a dividend next quarter. = 公司下个季度将支付股息。
Investors expect to receive higher dividends in the future. = 投资者期望将来获得更高的股息。


虚拟语气:
If the company were profitable, it would pay dividends. = 如果公司盈利，它就会支付股息。
I wish I had received higher dividends on my investment. = 我希望我的投资能获得更高的股息。


被动语态:
Dividends are paid out quarterly by the company. = 股息由公司每季度支付。
High dividends were expected by the investors. = 投资者预期高额股息。


疑问句:
What are the dividends for this quarter? = 本季度的股息是多少？
Will the company pay dividends this year? = 公司今年会支付股息吗？


否定句:
The company did not pay dividends last year. = 公司去年没有支付股息。
She hasn't received her dividends yet. = 她还没有收到股息。


PHRASE->
dividend yield: 股息收益率
dividend payout: 股息支付
dividend policy: 股息政策
cash dividend: 现金股息
stock dividend: 股票股息


词源:

The word "dividend" comes from the Latin word "dividendum," the gerundive form of "dividere," meaning "to divide" or "to share."  It reflects the nature of dividends as a portion of profits divided among shareholders.
