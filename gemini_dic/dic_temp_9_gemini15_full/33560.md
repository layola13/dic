cigars:/sɪˈɡɑːrz/| n.|雪茄|A cigar is a roll of finely cut, dried tobacco leaves, typically wrapped in a leaf of a different type of tobacco.  They are smoked for pleasure or social reasons.|例句：He lit a cigar and settled into his armchair. 他点燃一支雪茄，然后舒服地坐在扶手椅上。|例句： My grandfather always enjoyed a good cigar after dinner. 我爷爷总是喜欢在晚餐后抽支好雪茄。|近义词：cigarettes, stogies|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with things like "pipe tobacco" or "cigarettes" depending on the context.|名词
