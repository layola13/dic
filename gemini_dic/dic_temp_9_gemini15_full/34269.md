/klæk/| n.| 应声虫；雇佣的鼓掌者；(对演出等的) 虚假赞扬| A claque is a group of people hired to applaud a performer or performance, often to create a false impression of popularity or success.  It can also refer to the insincere or artificial praise given by such a group. | The singer's performance was met with enthusiastic applause, but some suspected it was a paid claque.  这位歌手的表演受到了热烈的掌声，但一些人怀疑这是雇来的应声虫。|近义词： sycophants, flatterers |反义词： genuine critics, honest audience
