例句：His poor dentition contributed to his digestive problems. 他糟糕的牙齿状况导致了他的消化问题。


近义词：teeth, set of teeth |反义词：无


词源:  From Middle French *denticion, from Latin dentitio, from dens, dentis "tooth".
