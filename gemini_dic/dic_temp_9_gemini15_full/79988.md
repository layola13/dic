go-getter:/ˈɡoʊˌɡɛtər/ | n. |有进取心的人，事业心强的人 | A go-getter is a highly motivated and ambitious person who is proactive and determined to achieve their goals. They are often self-starters who are not afraid of hard work and challenges.  | Example:  She's a real go-getter; she climbed the corporate ladder in record time. 她真是个有进取心的人；她以创纪录的速度升职加薪了。 |近义词：achiever, self-starter, high-achiever, hustler, dynamo |反义词：slacker, loafer, idler, layabout


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a go-getter. = 他是一个有进取心的人。
She is always a go-getter in her work. = 她工作中总是充满进取心。


一般过去时:
He was a go-getter in his youth. = 他年轻时很有进取心。
She was a go-getter when she started her business. = 她创业时非常有干劲。


现在完成时:
He has been a go-getter throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都很有进取心。
She has always been a go-getter, achieving great success. = 她一直很有进取心，取得了巨大的成功。


过去完成时:
