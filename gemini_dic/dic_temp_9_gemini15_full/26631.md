cacklers:/ˈkæklərz/| n.|咯咯叫的人；（尤指）母鸡|People or things that cackle, especially hens.  The word refers to the characteristic sound made by hens, and by extension, to people who make a similar sound, often associated with laughter.|例句：The farmer's cacklers were disturbing the peace at dawn.  农夫家的母鸡黎明时分扰乱了宁静。


The old women, a group of merry cacklers, filled the room with their laughter.  一群快乐的咯咯笑的老妇人，她们的笑声充满了整个房间。|近义词：gigglers, laughers|反义词：silent ones
