pumice:/ˈpʌmɪs/| n. |浮石|A light, porous volcanic rock formed when lava containing a large amount of gas cools rapidly.  It is often used as an abrasive. |例句：The gardener used pumice to improve the drainage of the soil. 园丁用浮石改善土壤排水。|例句：She exfoliated her skin with a pumice stone. 她用浮石磨砂去角质。|近义词：volcanic rock, scoria|反义词：dense rock
