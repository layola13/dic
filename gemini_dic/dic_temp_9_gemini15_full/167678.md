prickling: /ˈprɪklɪŋ/ | adj. | 刺痛的；发痒的；兴奋的 | A tingling or stinging sensation; a feeling of slight irritation or excitement.  |例句：I felt a prickling sensation on my skin. 我感觉皮肤上有刺痛感。

例句：The suspense was building, creating a prickling excitement in the room. 悬念越来越大，房间里弥漫着兴奋的刺痛感。


近义词：tingling, stinging, itching, thrilling

反义词：numb, calm, unexciting


词源:  From Middle English *prikelyn, from Old English *prician "to prick, pierce". The -ling suffix denotes a diminutive or continuous action.
