on-the-part-of: /ˌɒn ðə ˈpɑːt əv/ | prep. | 就…而言；来自…方面；由于… |  Indicates the source or origin of an action, feeling, or quality. It shows whose perspective or responsibility something is. |

例句：The mistake was entirely on the part of the secretary.  错误完全是秘书造成的。

例句：There was a great deal of enthusiasm on the part of the students. 学生们表现出了极大的热情。


近义词：from, by, owing to

反义词：None (it's a preposition, not easily replaced by an antonym)

词性：介词
