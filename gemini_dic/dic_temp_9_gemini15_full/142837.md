out-designs:/ˌaʊtˈdɪzaɪnz/| vt.|胜过设计，设计得比…好|To surpass in design; to design better than.|例句：Our new product out-designs all competitors. 我们的新产品设计胜过所有竞争对手。
例句：The architect out-designed his previous work with this innovative building.  这位建筑师用这座创新建筑超越了他以前的作品。|近义词：surpass, excel, outperform|反义词：underdesign, poorly design|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He out-designs his competitors consistently. = 他总是设计得比竞争对手好。
The company out-designs its rivals in terms of innovation. = 在创新方面，这家公司设计得比竞争对手好。

一般过去时:
The team out-designed the previous model with superior features. = 团队通过优越的功能设计超越了之前的型号。
She out-designed her opponents by incorporating sustainable materials. = 她通过采用可持续材料的设计，胜过了她的对手。

现在完成时:

The firm has out-designed its rivals in the market for several years. = 这家公司在市场上已经多年设计得比竞争对手好了。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
