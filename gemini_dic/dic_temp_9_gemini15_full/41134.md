copsing:/ˈkɒpsɪŋ/ | n. | 砍伐树木以促进再生；矮林的生长 | The practice of cutting trees to encourage regrowth, typically applied to coppice woodland.  This method promotes the growth of multiple stems from the base of a tree, creating a dense thicket. | The woodland is managed by copsing, ensuring a sustainable supply of timber. = 这片林地通过砍伐树木以促进再生进行管理，确保木材的可持续供应。 The ancient practice of copsing has been revived in many areas. = 许多地区已经恢复了古老的砍伐树木以促进再生的做法。| coppicing, pollarding |  (无明显反义词，反义词可以理解为不砍伐树木，即 letting trees grow naturally) | 名词
