reenumerate:/ˌriːɪˈnuːməreɪt/ | vt. |重新编号；重新列举 | To number or list again.  This often implies a correction or a more detailed listing than before. |例句：The committee reenumerated the priorities, placing greater emphasis on community outreach. 委员会重新列出了优先事项，更加重视社区外展工作。

例句：The librarian reenumerated the books after a section was added to the library. 图书管理员在图书馆增加了一个部分后重新编号了图书。


近义词：renumber, list again, recount, reiterate


反义词：None directly opposite, but  "omit" or "ignore" could be considered contextual opposites.


词性：动词
