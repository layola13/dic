dar:/dɑːr/| v.|亲爱的，敬爱的|To feel or show great affection for someone.|例句：I dar my family very much. 我非常爱我的家人。|近义词：love, adore, cherish|反义词:hate, despise.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dar my dog. = 我爱我的狗。
They dar their children. = 他们爱他们的孩子。

一般过去时:
I dared my friend. = 我爱我的朋友。
She dared her parents. = 她爱她的父母。

现在完成时:
I have dared my family since I was a child. = 从小我就爱我的家人。
They have dared each other for many years. = 他们彼此相爱多年了。

过去完成时:
I had dared her before I met him. = 在我遇到他之前，我就爱她了。
They had dared their country before the war. = 战争爆发前，他们就爱着自己的国家了。

将来时:
I will dar her forever. = 我将永远爱她。
We will dar our friends always. = 我们将永远爱我们的朋友。

虚拟语气:
If I dared her, I would marry her. = 如果我爱她，我会娶她。
If they dared each other, they would get married. = 如果他们彼此相爱，他们就会结婚。

被动语态:
She is dared by her husband. = 她被丈夫深爱着。
He is dared by his family. = 他被家人深爱着。

疑问句:
Do you dar your family? = 你爱你的家人吗？
Does she dar her cat? = 她爱她的猫吗？

否定句:
I don't dar him. = 我不爱他。
She doesn't dar him anymore. = 她不再爱他了。
