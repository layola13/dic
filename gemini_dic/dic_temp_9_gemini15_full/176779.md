reading:/ˈriːdɪŋ/ | n. |阅读；朗读；阅读材料|The act of reading, the process of understanding written or printed material. It can also refer to a specific piece of writing or a public reading of a text.|例句：I enjoy reading novels before bed. 我喜欢睡前读小说。
例句：The teacher assigned us a reading for homework. 老师布置了阅读作业给我们。|近义词：study, perusal, recitation |反义词：writing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I read a book every week. = 我每周读一本书。
She reads newspapers every morning. = 她每天早上读报纸。


一般过去时:
I read a good book last night. = 我昨晚读了一本好书。
He read the letter aloud to his family. = 他大声朗读这封信给他的家人听。


现在完成时:
I have read that book twice. = 我已经读过那本书两次了。
She has read many novels this year. = 她今年已经读了很多小说了。


过去完成时:
I had read the article before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经读过那篇文章了。
They had read the instructions carefully. = 他们已经仔细阅读了说明。


将来时:
I will read that new novel next week. = 我下周会读那本新小说。
She will read a story to the children tonight. = 她今晚将给孩子们读故事。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would read more books. = 如果我有更多时间，我会读更多书。
I wish I had read that book sooner. = 我希望我早点读那本书。


被动语态:
The book was read by many people. = 这本书被很多人读过。
The story is being read to the children. = 故事正在被读给孩子们听。


疑问句:
Do you enjoy reading? = 你喜欢阅读吗？
What are you reading at the moment? = 你现在正在读什么？


否定句:
I don't read much these days. = 我最近读的书不多。
She didn't read the email. = 她没有读那封邮件。


PHRASE->
reading comprehension = 阅读理解
reading list = 阅读书单
speed reading = 快速阅读
silent reading = 默读
extensive reading = 泛读
