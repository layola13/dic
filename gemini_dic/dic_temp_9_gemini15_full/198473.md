shrikes:/ʃraɪks/| n.|  掠鸟，伯劳鸟 | A shrike is a predatory passerine bird of the family Laniidae, characterised by its habit of impaling its prey on thorns or other sharp objects.  | Example: The shrike perched on the barbed wire fence, a lizard impaled on a thorn nearby.  这只伯劳鸟栖息在带刺的铁丝网上，旁边一根刺上串着一只蜥蜴。  | 近义词：butcherbird | 反义词：None specifically;  the antonym would relate to the opposite of its predatory nature, such as a songbird or hummingbird. | 名词
