RSVP:/ˌɑːrsviːˈpeɪ/|abbr.|请回复|Respondez s'il vous plaît, a French phrase meaning "Please reply".  It's used as an abbreviation on invitations to request a response from the recipient indicating whether or not they will attend. |例句：Please RSVP by Friday.  请在周五之前回复。|近义词：reply, respond|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The invitation requests that guests RSVP by next week. = 请柬要求宾客在下周之前回复。
We always RSVP to invitations promptly. = 我们总是迅速回复邀请函。


一般过去时:
They RSVP'd to the wedding invitation last night. = 他们昨晚回复了婚礼邀请函。
She RSVP'd yes to the party. = 她回复说参加派对。


现在完成时:
Have you RSVP'd to the event yet? = 你已经回复参加活动了吗？
I have already RSVP'd, confirming my attendance. = 我已经回复了，确认参加。


过去完成时:
By the time the deadline arrived, everyone had RSVP'd. = 到截止日期之前，每个人都回复了。
We had already RSVP'd before the reminder email was sent. = 在提醒邮件发送之前，我们已经回复了。


将来时:
I will RSVP to the conference invitation tomorrow. = 我明天将回复会议邀请函。
We will RSVP as soon as we decide. = 我们决定后就会回复。


虚拟语气:
If I had known earlier, I would have RSVP'd sooner. = 如果我早点知道，我会早点回复。
It would be helpful if everyone would RSVP promptly. = 如果每个人都能迅速回复，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The RSVP deadline has been extended. = 回复截止日期已延长。
The invitations were sent with a request to RSVP. = 邀请函附有回复请求。


疑问句:
Have you RSVP'd yet? = 你已经回复了吗？
When is the RSVP deadline? = 回复截止日期是什么时候？


否定句:
I haven't RSVP'd yet. = 我还没回复。
They didn't RSVP on time. = 他们没有按时回复。
