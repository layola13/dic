recriminating: /rɪˈkrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |互相指责的；反责的 | Accusatory; expressing mutual accusations.  The atmosphere became tense, filled with recriminating glances and hushed whispers.  |近义词：accusatory, reproachful, self-accusing |反义词：forgiving, conciliatory


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children are constantly recriminating each other. = 孩子们不断地互相指责。
He is always recriminating his colleagues for their mistakes. = 他总是指责同事们的错误。


一般过去时:
They spent the evening recriminating over who was to blame. = 他们整个晚上都在互相指责谁该负责。
She recriminated her brother for breaking her favorite vase. = 她指责她哥哥打破了她最喜欢的花瓶。


现在完成时:
They have been recriminating each other since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，他们一直在互相指责。
He has recriminated himself for not acting sooner. = 他一直责备自己没有早点行动。


过去完成时:
They had been recriminating for hours before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们已经互相指责了几个小时。
She had recriminated him countless times before finally apologizing. = 在最终道歉之前，她已经无数次地责备他了。


将来时:
They will likely be recriminating each other for years to come. = 他们很可能会在未来的几年里互相指责。
He will recriminate her if she fails to meet the deadline. = 如果她未能按时完成，他会责备她。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't argued, they wouldn't be recriminating each other now. = 如果他们没有争吵，他们现在就不会互相指责了。
I wish I hadn't recriminated him so harshly. = 我希望我没有那么严厉地责备他。


被动语态:
The couple were constantly being recriminated by their families. = 这对夫妇不断受到他们家人的指责。
His actions were recriminated by the public. = 他的行为受到了公众的指责。


疑问句:
Are they still recriminating each other? = 他们还在互相指责吗？
Was he recriminating her for the lost documents? = 他是否因为丢失的文件而责备她？


否定句:
They were not recriminating; they were simply discussing the problem. = 他们不是在互相指责；他们只是在讨论问题。
He did not recriminate her for the mistake, understanding it was an accident. = 他并没有因为这个错误而责备她，因为他明白这是一个意外。


PHRASE->
mutually recriminating = 互相指责的
recriminating tone = 指责的语气
recriminating accusations = 互相指责的控诉
engaged in recrimination = 参与互相指责
a period of recrimination = 一段互相指责的时期

词源:
From Middle French *récriminer, from Latin recriminārī "to cry out against," from re- "back" + criminārī "to accuse."
