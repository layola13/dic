preshow: /ˈpriːʃoʊ/ | n. | 预演，演出前的准备 | A rehearsal or preparation before a main performance or event.  It often involves checking equipment, sound, lighting, and the performers' readiness. | 例句：The preshow for the concert included a sound check and a run-through of the lighting cues.  演出前的准备包括音响检查和灯光提示的彩排。 |近义词：rehearsal, preparation, preview | 反义词：aftershow, post-show | 名词
