shintoism:/ˈʃɪntoʊɪzəm/| n. |神道教|Shintoism is the indigenous religion of Japan, characterized by the worship of kami (spirits) and a reverence for nature. It lacks a single founder, a central deity, or a codified doctrine.  |例句：Many Japanese people practice Shintoism alongside Buddhism.  许多日本人同时信仰神道教和佛教。|近义词：kami-no-michi (神道, a Japanese term)|反义词：Christianity, Islam, Buddhism (as contrasting religions)|名词
