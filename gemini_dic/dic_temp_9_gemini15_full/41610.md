corpuses:/ˈkɔːrpəsiːz/| n.|语料库；语料集合|A corpus is a large and structured set of texts used for linguistic research.  It can be used to study grammar, vocabulary, and other aspects of language.  The plural form of "corpus".|例句：Researchers used a large corpus of English texts to analyze the frequency of different words.|近义词：collection, compilation, database|反义词：N/A
