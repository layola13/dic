frat:/fræt/| n.|兄弟会，联谊会|A fraternity; a social organization for male students at a college or university.|例句：He pledged to join the frat his older brother was in. 他发誓要加入他哥哥所在的兄弟会。|近义词：fraternity, brotherhood|反义词：sorority


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is in a frat. = 他在一个兄弟会里。
The frat throws a party every weekend. = 兄弟会每个周末都举办派对。

一般过去时:
He joined a frat last year. = 他去年加入了一个兄弟会。
The frat hosted a charity event. = 兄弟会举办了一场慈善活动。

现在完成时:
He has been in a frat for three years. = 他加入兄弟会三年了。
The frat has raised a lot of money for charity. = 兄弟会为慈善机构筹集了很多钱。

过去完成时:
He had already pledged to the frat before I met him. = 我认识他之前，他已经加入了兄弟会。
The frat had planned the event for months. = 兄弟会已经筹划这个活动好几个月了。

将来时:
He will join a frat next semester. = 他下学期将加入一个兄弟会。
The frat will hold a recruitment event next week. = 兄弟会下周将举行招募活动。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't join that frat. = 如果我是他，我不会加入那个兄弟会。
I wish I had joined a frat in college. = 我希望我在大学时加入了一个兄弟会。

被动语态:

否定句:
