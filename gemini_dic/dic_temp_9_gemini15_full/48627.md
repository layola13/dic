defences:/dɪˈfɛnsɪz/| n.|防御措施；防卫工事；辩护|Means of protection against attack or harm;  the act of defending against attack or criticism;  arguments used in defense.|例句：The country strengthened its defences against potential invaders.  这个国家加强了防御工事以抵御潜在的侵略者。

The lawyer presented a strong defence for her client.  律师为她的当事人进行了强有力的辩护。


近义词：protections, safeguards, defenses, arguments|反义词：attacks, offenses, assaults|名词
