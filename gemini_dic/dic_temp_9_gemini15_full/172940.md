qualifiers:/ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪərz/ | n. |限定词，修饰语 |Words or phrases that limit or modify the meaning of another word or phrase.  They specify or restrict the application of a noun, verb, adjective, or adverb.  |例句：In the sentence "The big red car," "big" and "red" are qualifiers.  这个句子中，“大的”和“红色的”是限定词。

例句：The adjective "small" acts as a qualifier, describing the size of the object.  形容词“小的”充当限定词，描述物体的尺寸。


近义词：modifiers, specifiers, descriptors, restrictives |反义词：generalizers, broadenings


词性:名词
