nemertean:/ˈnɛmərtiːn/| n. |纽形动物；[动物] 纽虫|A nemertean is a worm-like marine invertebrate belonging to the phylum Nemertea. They are known for their long, ribbon-like bodies and a proboscis used for capturing prey.  |Example:  The researchers discovered a new species of nemertean in the deep ocean.  研究人员在深海发现了新的纽形动物物种。|近义词: ribbon worm (带状蠕虫)|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与其他无脊椎动物对比)|名词
