pacu:/ˈpækuː/| n.|帕库鱼|A pacu is a type of freshwater fish that belongs to the Serrasalmidae family, which also includes piranhas.  Pacus are known for their human-like teeth, which are used for cracking nuts and seeds. They are generally herbivores, though some species may be omnivorous.|例句：The pacu's unusual teeth make it easily distinguishable from piranhas.|近义词：fish, serrasalmid|反义词：None specifically.
