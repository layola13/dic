meanderers:/miˈændərərz/| n. |蜿蜒而行者，漫游者 |People or things that meander; those who wander aimlessly or follow a winding course.  The term emphasizes a lack of directness or a leisurely, indirect path. |例句：The meanderers strolled along the riverbank, enjoying the scenery.  蜿蜒而行者沿着河岸漫步，欣赏风景。 The tourists, a group of happy meanderers, explored the charming streets of the old town.  游客们，一群快乐的漫游者，探索着古镇迷人的街道。|近义词：wanderers, ramblers, roamers, drifters |反义词：direct travelers, purposeful navigators
