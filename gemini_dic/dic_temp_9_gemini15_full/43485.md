crater:/ˈkreɪtər/ | n. | 火山口，陨石坑 | A crater is a large, bowl-shaped cavity in the ground or on a celestial body, typically formed by an impact or volcanic activity.  | Example: The meteor impact created a huge crater in the desert.  陨石撞击在沙漠中形成一个巨大的陨石坑。The volcano's crater was filled with bubbling lava. 火山的火山口充满了沸腾的熔岩。 |近义词：cavity, pit, hole | 反义词：peak, summit |
