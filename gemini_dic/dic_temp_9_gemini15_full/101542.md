ironical:/aɪˈrɒnɪkəl/ | adj. | 讽刺的；反语的 | Expressing meaning indirectly by using language that normally signifies the opposite, typically for humorous or emphatic effect.  It suggests a contrast between expectation and reality. | Example: It's ironical that the fire station burned down.  这真讽刺，消防站烧掉了。|  近义词：sarcastic, satirical, paradoxical, incongruous | 反义词：literal, straightforward
