counterpower: /ˈkaʊntərpaʊər/ | n. | 反制力量，对抗力量 | The ability of a group or individual to resist or oppose the power of another group or individual.  It often involves using strategies and tactics to challenge dominant power structures.  |  例句：The workers organized a counterpower movement to challenge the management's unfair labor practices. 工人们组织了一个反制力量运动来挑战管理层的不公平劳动行为。

例句：The marginalized community used its collective counterpower to advocate for better resources.边缘化社区利用其集体反制力量来争取更好的资源。


近义词：opposition, resistance, counterforce, defiance | 反义词：subjugation, submission, compliance | 词性：名词
