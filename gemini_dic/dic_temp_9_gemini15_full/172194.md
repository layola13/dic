purveyors:/pərˈveɪərz/| n.|供应商，提供者|Those who supply or provide something, especially goods or services.  They often imply a sense of providing something on a larger scale or to a specific market. |例句：Many small businesses are purveyors of local produce. 许多小型企业都是当地农产品的供应商。

例句：The company is a major purveyor of software to financial institutions. 这家公司是向金融机构提供软件的主要供应商。


近义词：suppliers, providers, vendors, dealers

反义词：consumers, recipients


词源:  From the verb "purvey," meaning "to supply or provide," which ultimately derives from the Old French word "pourveir," meaning "to provide for."  The suffix "-ors" indicates the agent or doer of the action.
