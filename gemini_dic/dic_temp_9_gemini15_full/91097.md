hyacine:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθ/| n. |风信子|Hyacinth is a genus of bulbous flowering plants in the family Asparagaceae, subfamily Scilloideae.  They are fragrant spring-blooming plants with bell-shaped flowers.  |例句：The hyacinths in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的风信子盛开了。|例句：She received a pot of hyacinths as a birthday gift. 她收到了一盆风信子作为生日礼物。|近义词：bluebell (某些种类)|反义词：无|名词
