eartha:/ˈɜːrθə/| n. |地球(诗歌或文学用语)|A poetic or literary term for the Earth.  It emphasizes the planet's physical nature and can evoke a sense of awe or reverence.  The word is less common in everyday speech.|例句：The eartha groaned under the weight of the storm.  地球在暴风雨的重量下呻吟着。|近义词：Earth, globe, planet, world|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "heaven," "sky," or "cosmos".|
