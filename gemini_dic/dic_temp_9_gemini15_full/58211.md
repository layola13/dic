drug's: /drʌɡz/ | n. | 药物的；毒品的 |  The possessive form of "drug," indicating ownership or belonging.  It refers to something that belongs to or is associated with a drug. | 例句：The drug's effects were immediate. (药物的作用是立即的。)  The drug's side effects were severe. (药物的副作用很严重。) | 近义词：medicament's,  narcotic's | 反义词：无
