edifice:/ˈedɪfɪs/| n.|大厦；大建筑；宏伟建筑|A large and imposing building.  The word often implies grandeur, complexity, or significance. |例句：The cathedral is a magnificent edifice of Gothic architecture. 这座大教堂是一座宏伟的哥特式建筑。|例句：The new government edifice was a symbol of national pride. 新政府大厦是民族自豪感的象征。|近义词：building, structure, construction, monument |反义词：hut, shack, cabin
