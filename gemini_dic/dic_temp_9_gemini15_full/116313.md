master:/ˈmɑːstər/ /ˈmæstər/| n., v.|主人；大师；掌握；精通|A person who is skilled in a particular art, craft, or technique; to become skilled in or completely understand something.|例句：He is a master of his craft. 他是技艺大师。 He mastered the difficult task. 他掌握了这项艰难的任务。|近义词：expert, proficient, skilled, conquer|反义词：novice, beginner, unskilled


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He masters the guitar. = 他精通吉他。
She masters the art of negotiation. = 她掌握了谈判的艺术。


一般过去时:
He mastered the language in a year. = 他一年内掌握了这门外语。
She mastered the skill after years of practice. = 她经过多年的练习才掌握了这项技能。


现在完成时:
He has mastered several foreign languages. = 他已经掌握了几门外语。
She has mastered the art of persuasion. = 她已经掌握了劝说的技巧。


过去完成时:
He had mastered the technique before the competition. = 他在比赛前已经掌握了这项技术。
She had mastered the subject before the exam. = 考试前她已经掌握了这门学科。


将来时:
He will master the skill with enough practice. = 他通过足够的练习将会掌握这项技能。
She will master the art of cooking in time. = 她最终会掌握烹饪的技巧。


虚拟语气:
If he mastered the skill, he would be promoted. = 如果他掌握了这项技能，他就会被提升。
If she had mastered the subject, she would have passed the exam. = 如果她掌握了这门学科，她就会通过考试。


被动语态:
The technique was mastered by many students. = 这项技术被许多学生掌握了。
The skill is being mastered by the young apprentice. = 年轻的学徒正在掌握这项技能。


疑问句:
Has he mastered the technique yet? = 他已经掌握这项技术了吗？
Have you mastered the material for the exam? = 你已经掌握考试的材料了吗？


否定句:
He did not master the skill in time. = 他没有及时掌握这项技能。
She hasn't mastered the art of public speaking yet. = 她还没有掌握公开演讲的艺术。
