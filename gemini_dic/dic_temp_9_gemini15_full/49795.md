demonocracy: /ˌdɛməˈnɒkrəsi/ | n. | 妖魔统治；暴民统治；恶魔统治 | A system of government in which the power is held by a small group of people who are often characterized by their cruelty, violence, or disregard for the rights of others.  It's a blend of "demon" and "cracy," suggesting a rule by evil or destructive forces. The term is often used to describe a government that is oppressive, tyrannical, and lacks legitimate authority.  It implies a chaotic and destructive form of governance. |  The recent political upheaval has led some to describe the current regime as a demonocracy.  | 近义词: tyranny, dictatorship, autocracy | 反义词: democracy, republic
