disliker:/dɪsˈlaɪkər/ | n. |厌恶者，不喜欢的人 | A person who dislikes something or someone.  |He's a disliker of all things modern. 他是一个厌恶所有现代事物的人。|近义词：detester, opponent, critic |反义词：admirer, enthusiast, fan | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a disliker of loud music. = 他是一个不喜欢大声音乐的人。
She is a disliker of early mornings. = 她是一个不喜欢早起的人。


一般过去时:
He was a disliker of broccoli as a child. = 他小时候不喜欢西兰花。
She was a disliker of her job until she got a promotion. = 在升职之前，她不喜欢她的工作。


现在完成时:
He has been a disliker of reality TV for years. = 多年来，他一直不喜欢真人秀节目。
She has remained a disliker of spicy food her whole life. = 她一生都讨厌辛辣的食物。


过去完成时:
He had been a disliker of cats before he met his current pet. = 在他遇到他现在养的宠物之前，他一直不喜欢猫。
She had been a disliker of classical music until she attended that concert. = 在她参加那场音乐会之前，她一直不喜欢古典音乐。


将来时:
He will likely remain a disliker of crowds. = 他很可能仍然不喜欢人群。
She will probably always be a disliker of vegetables. = 她可能永远都不喜欢蔬菜。


虚拟语气:
If he were a disliker of chocolate, he wouldn't have eaten the whole bar. = 如果他不喜欢巧克力，他就不会吃掉整块巧克力了。
I wish I weren't a disliker of public speaking. = 我真希望我不那么讨厌公开演讲。


被动语态: (This word doesn't typically take a passive voice)  This is because the word itself describes the person who does the disliking, thus rendering a passive construction grammatically awkward.


疑问句:
Is he a disliker of opera? = 他不喜欢歌剧吗？
Are you a disliker of that particular author? = 你不喜欢那个特定的作家吗？


否定句:
He is not a disliker of dogs. = 他并不讨厌狗。
She is not a disliker of modern art. = 她并不讨厌现代艺术。
