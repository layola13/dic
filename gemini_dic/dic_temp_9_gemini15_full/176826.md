readvertised:/ˌriːˈædvərtaɪzd/| v.|再次刊登广告，重新做广告|To advertise something again.|The job was readvertised after they failed to find a suitable candidate.  这份工作因为他们没有找到合适的候选人而被再次刊登广告。|近义词：re-advertised, advertised again|反义词：withdrew advertisement
