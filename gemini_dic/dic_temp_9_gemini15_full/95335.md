incenser:/ˈɪnsɛnsər/| n. |香炉；熏香器 |A container for burning incense, often made of metal, ceramic, or wood.  |例句：The delicate porcelain incenser filled the temple with a sweet fragrance.  精致的瓷制香炉使寺庙充满甜美的香气。

例句：He lit the incense in the ornate silver incenser. 他在华丽的银香炉里点燃了香。


近义词：censer, burner |反义词：None.


词源:  From Middle English *incensere, from Old French encensier, from Late Latin incensarium, from Latin incendere "to burn incense."  The word ultimately derives from the Latin prefix *in- ("in, into") and *candēre ("to be white, to glow, to burn").
