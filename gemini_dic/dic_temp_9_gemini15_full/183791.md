resculpting:/ˌriːˈskʌlptɪŋ/| v.|重新塑造；再塑；重塑造型|The act of reshaping or remodeling something, often implying a significant change or improvement.  It often refers to artistic processes, but can also apply to other contexts like physical therapy or business strategies.|例句：The sculptor is resculpting the clay figure, adding finer details.  雕塑家正在重新塑造粘土雕像，添加更精细的细节。

The architect is resculpting the building's facade to improve its aesthetic appeal. 建筑师正在重新塑造建筑物的立面以提高其美感。|近义词：remodel, reshape, reform, refashion|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin|动词
