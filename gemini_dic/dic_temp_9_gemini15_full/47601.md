debauch:/dɪˈbɔːtʃ/| n. v.|放荡，沉溺；堕落；使沉溺于；|英文解析：to engage in excessive drinking and debauchery; to corrupt morally; to seduce.  The noun refers to an act of excessive indulgence in pleasure, especially drinking. |例句：The king's debauch led to his downfall. 国王的放荡导致了他的垮台。

The party turned into a wild debauch.  派对变成了狂欢。

近义词：revelry, dissipation, excess, corruption, depravity |反义词：temperance, abstinence, purity, virtue
