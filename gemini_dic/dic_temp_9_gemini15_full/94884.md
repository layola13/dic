in-chorus: /ɪn ˈkɔːrəs/ | adv. |齐声地，合唱地|  In unison; all together at the same time, typically singing. | 例句：The children sang the song in chorus. 孩子们齐声演唱这首歌。| 例句：The crowd chanted in chorus, demanding justice. 人群齐声高喊，要求正义。| 近义词：together, simultaneously, unison,  in harmony | 反义词：individually, separately, alone
