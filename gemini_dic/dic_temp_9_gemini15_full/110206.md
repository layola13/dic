life-giving: /ˈlaɪfˌɡɪvɪŋ/ | adj. |赋予生命的；生命攸关的 |  giving or sustaining life; essential for life.  |例句：The sun is life-giving. 太阳是生命之源。

The rain brought life-giving moisture to the parched land.  雨水为干旱的土地带来了生命之水。


近义词：vital, essential, vivifying, invigorating | 反义词：deadly, lethal, fatal

词性：形容词
