holism:/ˈhəʊlɪzəm/| n. |整体论；整体主义|The philosophical theory that the properties of a whole system cannot be determined solely from the properties of its parts.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and interdependence of things within a system.  Holism is applied across many fields, including medicine, psychology, and ecology. | Example:  A holistic approach to healthcare considers the patient's physical, mental, and emotional well-being, not just the symptoms of a disease.  整体医疗方法考虑病人的身心健康，而不仅仅是疾病的症状。 |近义词：wholeness, integration, unity|反义词：reductionism, atomism
