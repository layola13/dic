dichotomize:/daɪˈkɒtəmaɪz/| /ˈdaɪkətəmaɪz/| vt.| 二分法；分成两类 |To divide into two mutually exclusive groups or categories.  |例句：The researcher dichotomized the participants into two groups based on their age.  研究人员根据参与者的年龄将其分成两组。

例句：The problem can be dichotomized into two main aspects: cost and efficiency.  这个问题可以分为两个主要方面：成本和效率。


近义词：bifurcate, divide, split, categorize


反义词：unify, integrate, combine


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist dichotomizes the data into two sets. = 科学家将数据分成两组。
We dichotomize the responses into "yes" and "no". = 我们将回复分成“是”和“否”两类。


一般过去时:
She dichotomized the sample into experimental and control groups. = 她将样本分成实验组和对照组。
They dichotomized the issue into two opposing viewpoints. = 他们将问题分成两种对立的观点。


现在完成时:
Researchers have dichotomized the population into urban and rural dwellers. = 研究人员已将人口分为城市居民和农村居民。
The study has dichotomized the results into positive and negative outcomes. = 这项研究已将结果分为正面和负面两种结果。


过去完成时:
He had dichotomized the data before running the analysis. = 他在进行分析之前已经将数据二分了。
They had dichotomized the participants into high and low risk categories. = 他们已将参与者分为高风险和低风险类别。


将来时:
We will dichotomize the responses according to gender. = 我们将根据性别对回复进行分类。
The experiment will dichotomize the subjects into treatment and placebo groups. = 实验将把受试者分成治疗组和安慰剂组。


虚拟语气:
If we dichotomized the sample, the results would be more easily interpreted. = 如果我们将样本分成两类，结果将更容易解释。
I wish we had dichotomized the data before beginning the analysis. = 我希望我们在开始分析之前已经将数据分成两类。


被动语态:
The data were dichotomized by the software. = 数据由软件进行二分。
The participants were dichotomized into two groups based on their scores. = 参与者根据他们的分数被分成两组。


疑问句:
How did the researchers dichotomize the variables? = 研究人员是如何对变量进行二分法的？
Should we dichotomize the dependent variable? = 我们应该将因变量二分吗？


否定句:
The study did not dichotomize the participants into distinct groups. = 这项研究没有将参与者分成不同的组。
We did not dichotomize the data, instead we used a continuous scale. = 我们没有对数据进行二分，而是使用了连续量表。


PHRASE->
dichotomize a variable = 将变量二分

dichotomize the population = 将人口二分
dichotomize the sample = 将样本二分
dichotomize the results = 将结果二分


词源:
From Greek  *dichotomia*  (a cutting in two), from  *dicha*  ("in two") +  *temnein*  ("to cut").
