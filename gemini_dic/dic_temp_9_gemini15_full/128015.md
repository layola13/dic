nepo:/ˈnɛpoʊ/| n.|外甥；侄子|Nephew refers to a son of one's brother or sister.  It's a term of kinship. |例句：My nephew is coming to visit next week.  下周我的外甥要来拜访。|例句：He left his entire estate to his nephew. 他把全部遗产留给了他的外甥。|近义词：grandson (if referring to a son of one's sibling's son), kinsman|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is my nephew. = 他是我的外甥。
My nephew plays the piano. = 我的外甥弹钢琴。

一般过去时:
My nephew visited last year. = 我的外甥去年来访过。
He was my favorite nephew. = 他是我最喜欢的外甥。

现在完成时:
I have just seen my nephew. = 我刚见过我的外甥。
My nephew has grown so tall. = 我的外甥长这么高了。

过去完成时:
I had already spoken to my nephew. = 我已经和我的外甥谈过了。
Before he arrived, I had prepared a gift for my nephew. = 他到达之前，我已经为我的外甥准备了一份礼物。

将来时:
I will visit my nephew next month. = 我下个月将去看望我的外甥。
My nephew will graduate from college soon. = 我的外甥很快就要大学毕业了。

虚拟语气:
If he were my nephew, I would help him. = 如果他是我的外甥，我会帮助他。
I wish my nephew were here. = 我希望我的外甥在这里。

被动语态:
He was called my nephew. = 他被称为我的外甥。（较为不自然）
The inheritance was left to his nephew. = 遗产留给了他的外甥。

疑问句:
Is he your nephew? = 他是你的外甥吗？
Whose nephew is he? = 他是谁的外甥？

否定句:
He is not my nephew. = 他不是我的外甥。
That child is not my nephew. = 那孩子不是我的外甥。
