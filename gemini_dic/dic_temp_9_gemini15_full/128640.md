nickered:/ˈnɪkərd/| v.|发出嘶嘶声；低声抱怨 | to make a short, dry cough or a whinnying sound, especially of a horse; to speak in a low, complaining tone.|例句：The horse nickered softly to its foal.  那匹马轻轻地对着它的马驹嘶鸣。


The old woman nickered about the price of groceries. 老妇人低声抱怨杂货的价格。|近义词：whinny, snort, murmur, grumble |反义词：roar, shout
