rozzer:/ˈrɒzə(r)/| n.|警察，巡警|A British slang term for a police officer.  It's often used informally and sometimes carries a slightly negative or humorous connotation.|例句：The rozzer pulled me over for speeding.  警察因为超速把我拦下了。|例句：I saw a rozzer on patrol this morning. 我今天早上看到一个巡警在巡逻。|近义词：copper, bobby, cop, police officer|反义词：criminal, offender
