enseint:/ɑ̃ˈsɛ̃t/| adj.|怀孕的，怀胎的|Pregnant; expecting a child.  The word is primarily used in a formal or literary context.  |例句：The queen was enseint with her first child.  女王怀上了她的第一个孩子。|例句：She was enseint and radiant with happiness. 她怀孕了，脸上洋溢着幸福。|近义词：pregnant,expectant, with child|反义词：barren, infertile
