fleecer: /ˈfliːsər/ | vt. |欺诈者；剥削者| To defraud someone, especially by charging excessive prices.  To shear (a sheep).| 例句：The company was accused of fleecing its customers. 那家公司被指控欺骗顾客。


The landlord tried to fleece his tenants with exorbitant rent increases.  房东试图通过大幅提高租金来剥削他的房客。


近义词：cheat, swindle, defraud, rob, exploit | 反义词：benefit, help, assist


词性:动词
