intuits:/ɪnˈtuːɪts/ | vt. |凭直觉知道；直觉地理解 | To understand something instinctively, without the need for conscious reasoning.  |She intuited his displeasure from his silence. 她从他的沉默中直觉地察觉到他不高兴。|  perceive, sense, feel | ignore, disregard | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intuits the market trends. = 他凭直觉知道市场趋势。
She intuits her clients' needs. = 她直觉地理解客户的需求。

一般过去时:
I intuited that something was wrong. = 我直觉地感觉到有什么不对劲。
They intuited the solution to the problem. = 他们凭直觉找到了问题的解决方法。

现在完成时:
She has intuited the answer to the riddle. = 她已经凭直觉猜到了谜底。
