defending: /dɪˈfɛndɪŋ/ | v. | 保卫；辩护；为…辩护 | Protecting someone or something from attack or harm; arguing in favor of someone or something. |例句：The soldiers were defending their country. 士兵们正在保卫他们的国家。

The lawyer is defending her client against the charges. 律师正在为她的当事人辩护，以对抗指控。|近义词：protecting, shielding, upholding, advocating |反义词：attacking, assaulting, opposing, condemning |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defends his beliefs fiercely. 他坚决捍卫自己的信仰。
She defends the rights of the oppressed. 她捍卫受压迫者的权利。

一般过去时:
The knights defended the castle bravely.  骑士们英勇地保卫了城堡。
He defended his actions in court. 他在法庭上为自己辩护。


现在完成时:
They have defended their champion throughout the tournament. 他们在整个锦标赛中一直为他们的冠军辩护。
She has defended her thesis successfully. 她成功地为她的论文进行了辩护。


过去完成时:
The army had defended the city for months before surrendering.  军队在投降前已经保卫这座城市数月了。
He had defended his innocence throughout the trial. 他在整个审判过程中一直为自己申辩。


将来时:
We will defend our interests vigorously. 我们将坚决捍卫我们的利益。
The government will defend its policies in parliament. 政府将在议会中为其政策进行辩护。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would defend us. 如果他在这里，他会为我们辩护。
I wish I could defend myself better. 我希望我能更好地为自己辩护。


被动语态:
The city was defended by brave soldiers.  这座城市由勇敢的士兵保卫。
His actions were defended by his lawyer. 他的行为得到了律师的辩护。


疑问句:
How will you defend yourself against these accusations? 你将如何为自己反驳这些指控？
Who defended the city during the siege?  围城期间是谁保卫了这座城市？


否定句:
He didn't defend his friend. 他没有为他的朋友辩护。
They couldn't defend their position. 他们无法捍卫自己的立场。


PHRASE->
