eddoes:/ˈɛdoʊz/| n. |芋头；芋头植物|A starchy tuber of a tropical plant (Colocasia esculenta) of the arum family, eaten as a vegetable.  It is similar to taro. |Example:  We had eddoes and saltfish for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了芋头和咸鱼。


Example: The eddoes were roasted to perfection.  这些芋头烤得恰到好处。


近义词：taro, dasheen


反义词：无


词性:名词
