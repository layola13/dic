re-attributing: /ˌriːəˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新归属，重新分配 |  The act of assigning something (such as authorship, ownership, or responsibility) again or differently.  It implies a previous attribution that is being revised or changed. |

例句：

The museum is re-attributing several paintings to a previously unknown artist. = 博物馆正在将几幅画重新归于一位以前不为人知的艺术家。

The company is re-attributing tasks to improve workflow efficiency. = 公司正在重新分配任务以提高工作流程效率。


近义词：reassigning, reallocating, redistributing


反义词：attributing (without the re-), claiming


词性：动词
