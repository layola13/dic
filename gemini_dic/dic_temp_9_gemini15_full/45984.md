/saɪənəʊfɪl/ | n. |蓝藻素，藻青素|A blue pigment found in cyanobacteria (blue-green algae).  It is responsible for the characteristic blue-green color of these organisms.  Cyanophyll is a type of phycobilin, a class of light-harvesting pigments.|Example: The researchers studied the absorption spectrum of cyanophyll to understand its role in photosynthesis.  研究人员研究了藻青素的吸收光谱，以了解其在光合作用中的作用。|近义词：phycocyanin (藻蓝蛋白), phycobilin (藻胆素)|反义词：N/A
