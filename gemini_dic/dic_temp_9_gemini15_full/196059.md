separately:/ˈsɛpərətli/| adv. |分别地，各自地|The tasks should be completed separately.  任务应该分别完成。|together, jointly|together, jointly|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They eat separately. = 他们分开吃饭。
We work separately on the project. = 我们分别进行项目工作。


一般过去时:
They lived separately before their marriage. = 结婚前他们分开居住。
We studied separately for the exam. = 我们分别为考试复习。


现在完成时:
They have lived separately for five years. = 他们分开生活五年了。
We have worked separately on this project until now. = 到目前为止，我们一直分别从事这个项目的工作。


过去完成时:
They had lived separately before they decided to get married. = 他们在决定结婚之前就分开住了。
We had prepared separately for the presentation before the meeting. = 会议前我们分别为演示做了准备。


将来时:
They will live separately after the divorce. = 离婚后他们将分开居住。
We will work separately on different aspects of the project. = 我们将分别负责项目的不同方面。


虚拟语气:
If they lived separately, they might be happier. = 如果他们分开住，也许会更快乐。
I wish we had worked separately on the report. = 我希望我们当时分别完成报告。


被动语态:
The tasks were completed separately. = 任务是分别完成的。
The tests should be graded separately. = 考试应该分别评分。


疑问句:
Should we work separately or together? = 我们应该分开工作还是一起工作？
Do they prefer to live separately? = 他们更喜欢分开住吗？


否定句:
They don't live separately; they share a house. = 他们不住在分开的地方；他们合住一栋房子。
We didn't work separately on that project; we collaborated closely. = 我们没有分别进行那个项目的工作；我们密切合作了。
