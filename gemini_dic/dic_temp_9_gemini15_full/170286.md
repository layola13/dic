proveditor:/prəˈvaɪdə(r)/| n. | 供应者；管理者；（尤指）军需官 | A proveditor is a person responsible for supplying provisions, especially to an army or other organization.  The term often carries connotations of managing resources and logistics.  | The army's proveditor ensured that the troops were adequately supplied with food and ammunition.  军队军需官确保军队有足够的粮食和弹药供应。| purveyor, supplier, commissary |  consumer, receiver | 名词
