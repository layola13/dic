gratifications:/ˌɡrædəfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 满足；喜悦；令人满意的事物 |  The feeling of satisfaction or pleasure derived from something. Gratifications can be either physical or emotional, and they often stem from fulfilling a need or desire.  | Example: The artist felt immense gratification from completing her masterpiece.  艺术家从完成她的杰作中获得了巨大的满足感。  |近义词：satisfaction, pleasure, delight, fulfillment | 反义词：disappointment, dissatisfaction, frustration | 名词
