overaccumulate: /ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjʊleɪt/ | vt. |过度积累；积聚过多 |To accumulate to an excessive degree.  This implies a negative consequence from having too much of something.  It suggests that the accumulation is unwanted or problematic. |例句：The company overaccumulated inventory, leading to significant losses. 公司库存积压过多，导致重大损失。
The patient's body overaccumulated fluid, causing edema. 病人体内积聚过多水分，导致水肿。|近义词：accumulate excessively, amass excessively, hoard |反义词： deplete, dissipate, exhaust
