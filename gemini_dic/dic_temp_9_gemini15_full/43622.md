created:/kriˈeɪtɪd/ | v. |创造的，被创造的 | Past participle of "create."  It describes something that has been brought into existence. |例句：The universe was created billions of years ago.  宇宙诞生于数十亿年前。

例句：The painting was created by a famous artist. 这幅画是一位著名艺术家创作的。


近义词：made, formed, produced, generated | 反义词：destroyed, ruined, demolished | 词性：过去分词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is not used in simple present tense as it is a past participle.


一般过去时:
The artist created a masterpiece.  艺术家创作了一幅杰作。
They created a new company last year. 他们去年创建了一家新公司。


现在完成时:
She has created many beautiful sculptures. 她创作了许多美丽的雕塑。
We have created a new system for managing data. 我们创建了一个新的数据管理系统。


过去完成时:
They had created the prototype before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前已经创建了原型。
The team had created a detailed plan before starting the project.  团队在开始项目之前制定了一个详细的计划。


将来时:
They will have created a working model by next week. 下周他们将创建一个可行的模型。
We will create a new marketing strategy next month.  下个月我们将制定一个新的营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If they had created a better plan, the project would have been successful. 如果他们制定了一个更好的计划，项目就会成功。
I wish I had created that amazing painting. 我真希望我创作了那幅令人惊叹的画作。


被动语态:
The website was created by a team of experts.  该网站是由一个专家团队创建的。
The new rules were created to improve safety. 新规是为了提高安全而制定的。


疑问句:
Was the universe created by a divine being? 宇宙是由神创造的吗？
When was the company created?  公司是什么时候成立的？


否定句:
The project was not created on time. 项目没有按时完成。
They did not create a successful product.  他们没有创造出一个成功的产品。


PHRASE->
newly created 新创建的
specially created 特别制作的
user-created 用户创建的
computer-created 电脑生成的
divine created 神创造的
