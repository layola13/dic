giantish:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪʃ/| adj.|巨大的，巨人般的|Exaggeratedly large; resembling a giant.  The word is informal and often used hyperbolically.|例句：He had a giantish appetite, consuming three whole pizzas in one sitting. 他有一个巨人般的胃口，一顿饭吃了三个比萨饼。|近义词：gigantic, enormous, colossal, immense|反义词：tiny, minuscule, diminutive
