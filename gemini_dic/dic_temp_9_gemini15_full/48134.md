decollate:/dɪˈkɒleɪt/| vt.| 切除…的顶端；摘除…的叶片；修整（书页）|To remove the top or leaves of something; to trim (pages of a book).|例句：The printer decollated the sheets of paper before binding them. 印刷工在装订纸张之前先切除了纸张的顶端。

例句：The gardener decollated the dead leaves from the rose bushes. 园丁从玫瑰花丛中摘除了枯叶。|近义词：sever, remove, trim|反义词：attach, join, add
