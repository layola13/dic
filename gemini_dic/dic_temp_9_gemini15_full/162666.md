preasserting: /ˌpriːəˈsɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先声明；预先断言 |  To state or assert something beforehand; to make a claim or statement in advance.  | The company preasserted its commitment to environmental sustainability. 公司预先声明了其对环境可持续性的承诺。 | assert, declare, affirm |  deny, refute, contradict | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preasserts his innocence. = 他预先声明自己是无辜的。
She preasserts her right to privacy. = 她预先声明她有权享有隐私。


一般过去时:
The lawyer preasserted his client's alibi. = 律师预先声明了其当事人的不在场证明。
They preasserted their intention to appeal. = 他们预先声明了他们打算上诉。


现在完成时:
He has preasserted his position on the matter several times. = 他已经多次预先声明了他对这件事的立场。
She has preasserted her belief in the project's success. = 她已经预先声明了她相信这个项目的成功。
