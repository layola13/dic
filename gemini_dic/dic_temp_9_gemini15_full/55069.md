disregardful: /ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdfəl/ | adj. | 漠视的；不顾的；忽略的 | Showing a lack of regard or consideration; inattentive.  |例句：His disregardful attitude towards safety regulations led to the accident. 他对安全规章的漠视态度导致了事故。

例句：Her disregardful comments hurt his feelings deeply. 她不顾及他人感受的评论深深地伤害了他的感情。


近义词：neglectful, careless, inconsiderate, thoughtless, unmindful


反义词：considerate, mindful, attentive, careful, heedful


词性：形容词
