juggle:/ˈdʒʌɡəl/| vt. n.|玩杂耍；同时应付几件事|to throw and catch several objects continuously to entertain or as a skill; to manage many things at the same time|He juggled three balls in the air. 他抛接三个球。|She juggles a job, a family, and college courses. 她同时兼顾工作、家庭和大学课程。|balance, manage, handle|fail, drop|动词
