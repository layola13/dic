inlander:/ˈɪnlændər/ | n. |内陆居民；本地人 | A person who lives in the inland area of a country, especially one who is considered unsophisticated or provincial compared to city dwellers.  | Example:  The inlander was surprised by the hustle and bustle of the city.  内陆居民对城市的喧嚣感到惊讶。  |近义词：native, local, provincial | 反义词：outsider, foreigner
