cortexes:/ˈkɔːrtɛksɪz/ | n. |皮质（复数）|The outer layer of an organ, especially the brain, is called the cortex.  Cortexes are involved in various higher-level functions such as thinking, perceiving, and remembering.  The plural form of "cortex". |例句：Studies of the brain's cortexes have revealed much about human cognition.  大脑皮质的研究揭示了关于人类认知的许多信息。
