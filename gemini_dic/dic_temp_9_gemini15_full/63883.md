epilogued:/ˈɛpɪlɒɡd/ | v. | 附上结语；为…写结语 | To add an epilogue to something, such as a book, play, or event.  | The author epilogued the novel with a reflection on the characters' fates.  作者在小说结尾处写了一段关于人物命运的反思。 |  conclude, summarize |  begin, preface | 动词
