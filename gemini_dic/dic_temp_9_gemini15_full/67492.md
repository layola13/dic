词源：
The word "extra-terrestrial" is a combination of "extra-" (meaning "outside" or "beyond") and "terrestrial" (meaning "of or relating to the earth").  "Terrestrial" comes from the Latin word "terrestris," from "terra," meaning "earth."  Therefore, extra-terrestrial literally means "outside of the earth."
