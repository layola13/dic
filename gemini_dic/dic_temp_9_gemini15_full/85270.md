harmonies:/ˈhɑːrməniz/| n.|和谐；协调；和声；融洽|The state of being in agreement or accord; a pleasing combination of sounds; a congruity of parts.|例句：The harmonies in the choir's performance were exquisite.  合唱团表演中的和声非常优美。|例句：There's a need for greater harmony between management and workers. 管理层和工人之间需要更大的和谐。|近义词：accord, concord, agreement, unity|反义词：discord, conflict, dissonance
