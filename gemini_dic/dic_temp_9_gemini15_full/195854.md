USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense and other verb conjugations are not applicable.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.

例句一: He spoke senilely, barely remembering where he was. 他说话前言不搭后语，几乎不记得自己在哪儿了。
例句二: She moved senilely, her steps slow and uncertain. 她行动迟缓而迟疑，步履蹒跚。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "senilely".  The word is primarily used to describe a manner of speaking or acting.
