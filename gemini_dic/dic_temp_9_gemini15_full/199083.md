signposts:/ˈsaɪnpostz/| n.|路标；标志；征兆|Signs or objects that indicate direction or location, often used figuratively to refer to clues or indications.  |Example: The signposts along the trail were helpful in guiding us to the summit.  山径上的路标帮助我们找到了山顶。|近义词：indicators, markers, clues, signals|反义词：misdirections, obstructions

The signposts clearly mark the path. = 路标清晰地标明了路径。
These signposts indicate the way to the beach. = 这些路标指示着去海滩的路。
