seldom:/ˈsɛldəm/| adv.|很少，难得|Rarely; not often.|He seldom goes to the cinema. 他很少去看电影。|  She seldom visits her parents. 她很少去看望她的父母。|often, frequently, usually|always, frequently, often


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seldom visits his grandparents. = 他很少去看望他的祖父母。
They seldom go out on weekdays. = 他们很少在工作日外出。


一般过去时:
She seldom ate meat before becoming a vegetarian. = 她成为素食者之前很少吃肉。
We seldom traveled abroad when we were younger. = 我们年轻的时候很少出国旅行。


现在完成时:
I have seldom seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我很少看到如此美丽的日落。
They have seldom had the opportunity to travel. = 他们很少有机会旅行。


过去完成时:
He had seldom felt so happy before that moment. = 在那一刻之前，他很少感到如此快乐。
We had seldom encountered such a difficult problem. = 我们很少遇到如此困难的问题。


将来时:
I will seldom have time for hobbies next year. = 明年我很少有时间从事爱好。
They will seldom be able to attend the meetings. = 他们很少能够参加会议。


虚拟语气:
I wish I seldom had to work overtime. = 我希望我很少需要加班。
If I seldom watched TV, I would have more free time. = 如果我很少看电视，我会有很多空闲时间。


被动语态:
This museum is seldom visited by tourists. = 这个博物馆很少被游客参观。
These rules are seldom broken. = 这些规则很少被打破。


疑问句:
Do you seldom eat breakfast? = 你很少吃早餐吗？
Does she seldom go to the gym? = 她很少去健身房吗？


否定句:
He doesn't seldom go to the park. (This is grammatically incorrect.  Use "He often goes to the park" instead.) = 他经常去公园。(这在语法上是不正确的。应该用“他经常去公园”。)
It is not seldom that they argue. = 他们吵架并非罕见。


PHRASE->
seldom if ever = 几乎从不
seldom or never = 几乎从不
seldom seen = 难得一见
seldom heard = 难得一闻
seldom used = 很少使用
