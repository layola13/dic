preconstructing: /ˌpriːkənˈstrʌktɪŋ/ | v. | 预先构建；预先建造 |  The act of constructing something beforehand, typically involving planning and preparation before the main construction phase.  It implies a proactive and organized approach to building or creating something. | 例句：The team is preconstructing the project's framework before starting the detailed design.  团队在开始详细设计之前正在预先构建项目的框架。


例句：The city is preconstructing a new transportation system to alleviate traffic congestion.  城市正在预先构建一个新的交通系统以缓解交通拥堵。


近义词：preparing, planning, designing, prefabricating


反义词：deconstructing, demolishing, dismantling


词性:动词
