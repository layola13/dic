polychromy:/pɑːlɪˈkroʊmi/| n. |多色性；多色装饰；彩绘|The use of many colors in art, especially painting.  Polychromy is a technique used to create works of art with a variety of colors.  It's often seen in ancient sculptures and pottery. |例句：The ancient Greek statues were originally painted with vibrant polychromy.  古希腊雕像最初是用鲜艳的色彩绘制的。

The polychromy of the cathedral's stained-glass windows is breathtaking. 大教堂彩色玻璃窗的多彩令人叹为观止。|近义词：multicoloredness, variegation |反义词：monochromy
