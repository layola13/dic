cellared:/ˈsɛlərd/| adj.|储藏在地窖里的；陈酿的|Stored in a cellar; aged in a cellar, especially of wine.|例句：The cellared wine had a rich, earthy aroma.  这瓶在地窖里储藏的葡萄酒有着浓郁的泥土香气。

例句：We tasted several cellared Cabernet Sauvignons. 我们品尝了几款陈酿赤霞珠葡萄酒。


近义词：stored, aged, vintaged|反义词：fresh, new


词源:源自cellar(地窖) + -ed (形容词后缀), 表示“在地窖里的”，引申为“陈酿的”。  The word "cellar" itself comes from the Old French "cellier," ultimately from the Latin "cellarium," a diminutive of "cella," meaning "small room."
