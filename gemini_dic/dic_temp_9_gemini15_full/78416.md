get-off:/ɡɛt ˈɔːf/|/ɡet ˈɒf/| v.|下车；离开；脱下；逃脱；出发；开始；停止；完成|To leave a vehicle, to depart from a place, to remove something from something else, to escape punishment or a difficult situation, to begin a journey, to start a task or activity, to cease an activity, to finish a task or activity.|例句：I got off the bus at the wrong stop. 我在错误的车站下了车。
He got off work early today. 他今天早下班了。|近义词：depart, leave, alight, escape, disembark|反义词：get on, arrive, board, stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get off the bus at 5 pm every day. 我每天下午5点下车。
He gets off work early on Fridays. 他星期五早下班。

一般过去时:
I got off the train at the last stop. 我在最后一站下了火车。
She got off the phone quickly. 她很快挂了电话。


现在完成时:
I have gotten off the plane safely. 我已经安全下飞机了。
We have gotten off to a good start. 我们已经有一个好的开始。


过去完成时:
I had gotten off the bus before he arrived. 我在他到达之前已经下车了。
They had gotten off to a rough start. 他们已经有一个艰难的开始。


将来时:
I will get off the plane at 10 am tomorrow. 我明天上午10点下飞机。
He will get off work early this Friday. 他本周五会早下班。


虚拟语气:
If I got off work earlier, I would go to the gym. 如果我早点下班，我会去健身房。
I wish I could get off this project sooner. 我希望我能早点完成这个项目。


被动语态:
The passengers were gotten off the bus safely. 乘客们安全地下车了。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达其他意思)
The meeting was gotten off to a good start. 会议有一个好的开始。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达其他意思)


疑问句:
Where do you get off the bus? 你在哪里下车？
When does he get off work? 他几点下班？


否定句:
I didn't get off the bus at the right stop. 我没有在正确的车站下车。
He doesn't get off work until 6 pm. 他直到下午6点才下班。


PHRASE->
get off easy:  侥幸逃脱
get off lightly:  逃过轻处罚
get off scot-free:  全身而退
get off one's chest:  倾诉衷肠
get off the ground:  启动；开始运作
