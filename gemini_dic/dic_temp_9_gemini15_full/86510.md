heirlooms:/ˈɛrlʊmz/| n.|传家宝，祖传遗物|Objects of historical or sentimental value that have been passed down through generations within a family.|The antique clock is a treasured heirloom passed down from my great-grandmother.  这块古董钟是一件从我的曾祖母那里传下来的珍贵的传家宝。| antiques, relics, keepsakes |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：newly acquired items)|名词
