non-national: /ˌnɒnˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. | 非本国的；外国的 | Not belonging to or connected with a particular nation.  This adjective describes individuals, entities, or things that are not native or indigenous to a specific country.  It often implies a lack of citizenship or a different national origin. |

例句：

He is a non-national resident in this country. = 他是这个国家的非本国居民。

The company has a large number of non-national employees. = 这家公司有大量的外国雇员。


近义词：foreign, alien, international


反义词：national, domestic, native


词性: adjective
