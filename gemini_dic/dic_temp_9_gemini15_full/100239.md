interrupt:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/ | vt. n. |打断；中断；插嘴 |To break the continuity of (a process, activity, or event); to stop (someone) from continuing what they are saying or doing. |例句：Please don't interrupt me while I'm speaking.  请不要在我说话的时候打断我。|例句：The meeting was interrupted by a sudden power outage. 会议因突然停电而中断。|近义词：disrupt, disturb, hinder |反义词：continue, resume


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interrupts frequently during meetings. = 他在会议期间经常插嘴。
She interrupts her work to answer the phone. = 她中断工作接电话。


一般过去时:
The loud noise interrupted my sleep. = 巨大的噪音打断了我的睡眠。
He interrupted the speaker in the middle of his sentence. = 他打断了演讲者的话。


现在完成时:
They have interrupted the project several times. = 他们已经几次中断这个项目了。
She has been interrupted constantly throughout the presentation. = 在整个演示过程中，她不断被打扰。


过去完成时:
The storm had interrupted our journey before we reached our destination. = 我们到达目的地之前，暴风雨打断了我们的旅程。
The phone had interrupted them before they could finish their discussion. = 电话在他们完成讨论之前打断了他们。


将来时:
I'm afraid the heavy rain will interrupt our picnic. = 我担心大雨会打断我们的野餐。
They will interrupt the program to broadcast an important news bulletin. = 他们将中断节目播放重要新闻简报。


虚拟语气:
If you hadn't interrupted me, I would have finished my speech. = 如果你没有打断我，我就已经完成了我的演讲。
I wish he hadn't interrupted the meeting with his irrelevant questions. = 我真希望他没有用他无关紧要的问题打断会议。


被动语态:
The concert was interrupted by a fire alarm. = 音乐会因火警而中断。
Our conversation was interrupted by a phone call. = 我们的谈话被电话打断了。


疑问句:
Did the phone call interrupt your work? = 电话是否打断了你的工作？
Will the bad weather interrupt the flight? = 恶劣天气会中断航班吗？


否定句:
Please don't interrupt. = 请不要打断。
The meeting proceeded without interruption. = 会议没有被打扰地继续进行。


PHRASE->
interrupt briefly = 简单打断
interrupt rudely = 粗鲁地打断
interrupt constantly = 不断打断
interrupt unintentionally = 无意打断
interrupt frequently = 经常打断
