demency:/ˈdiːmənsi/| n. |宽大；慈悲；赦免；减刑|Leniency or mercy shown toward someone, especially by someone in authority.  It often refers to a reduction in punishment or a pardon.|The judge showed clemency, reducing the sentence from life imprisonment to 20 years.|mercy, leniency, pardon, forgiveness|severity, harshness, cruelty, strictness|名词
