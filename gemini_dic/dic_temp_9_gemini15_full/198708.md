sibilate:/ˈsɪbɪleɪt/| v.|发出嘶嘶声|To pronounce with a hissing sound, typically of /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /tʃ/, or /dʒ/.  It often refers to the articulation of sounds involving friction of the breath against the teeth or tongue.|例句：The snake sibilated menacingly.  那条蛇发出威胁的嘶嘶声。

例句：She sibilated her disapproval. 她用嘶嘶声表示不满。


近义词：hiss, whisper, susurrate|反义词：boom, bellow
