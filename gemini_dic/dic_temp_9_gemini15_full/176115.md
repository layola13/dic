raytrace:/ˈreɪtreɪs/| vt. |光线追踪，光线跟踪|To calculate the path of light rays in a scene to generate a realistic image.  This involves simulating how light bounces off surfaces and interacts with the environment. |例句：The software raytraces the scene to create photorealistic images.  该软件通过光线追踪来创建逼真的图像。

The game uses raytracing to render reflections and shadows accurately. 该游戏使用光线追踪来精确地渲染反射和阴影。|近义词：render, simulate |反义词：rasterize (in some contexts)|动词
