rechanneling:/ˌriːˈtʃænəlɪŋ/| v.|重新引导；重新分配；改变方向|To redirect or guide something (such as energy, resources, or attention) into a new channel or course.|例句：The company is rechanneling its resources into research and development. 公司正在将其资源重新分配到研发领域。|例句：We need to rechannel our efforts to achieve our goals. 我们需要调整我们的努力以实现我们的目标。|近义词：redirect, reroute, reallocate|反义词：disperse, scatter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They rechannel funds to support new projects. = 他们重新分配资金以支持新项目。
The government rechannels resources to improve infrastructure. = 政府重新分配资源以改善基础设施。


一般过去时:
The river was rechanneled to prevent flooding. = 河流被重新疏导以防止洪水。
She rechanneled her anger into creative writing. = 她将愤怒转化为创造性写作。


现在完成时:
They have rechanneled their efforts towards sustainability. = 他们已经将他们的努力重新导向可持续发展。
The company has rechanneled its marketing budget to digital platforms. = 公司已经将其营销预算重新分配到数字平台。


过去完成时:

She had rechanneled her grief into helping others. = 她已经将她的悲伤转化为帮助他人。


将来时:

The project will rechannel investment into renewable energy. = 该项目将把投资重新导向可再生能源。


虚拟语气:
