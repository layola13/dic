nonslanderous: /ˌnɒnˈslændərəs/ | adj. | 不诽谤的，不中伤的 | Not containing or involving slander; not defamatory.  | 例句：He made a nonslanderous statement about his competitor's product. 他对竞争对手的产品发表了不诽谤的声明。


例句：The journalist ensured his report was nonslanderous before publication.  记者在发表报道前确保其内容不构成诽谤。


近义词：inoffensive, harmless, innocent, truthful


反义词：slanderous, defamatory, libelous, calumnious


词性：形容词
