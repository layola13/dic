questionable:/ˈkwɛʃənəbl/| adj. | 可疑的；有疑问的；值得怀疑的 | open to doubt; not entirely believable or satisfactory.  |例句：His explanation of the incident was questionable. 他对事件的解释值得怀疑。

例句：The company's accounting practices are questionable. 这家公司的会计做法令人质疑。


近义词：doubtful, suspicious, dubious, suspect, uncertain | 反义词：certain, definite, unquestionable, reliable


词源:  From "question" + "-able," a suffix indicating capability or susceptibility.  The word's origins trace back to the Old French word "questioner" (to question) and ultimately to the Latin "quaestio" (inquiry, question).


用法说明:

questionable通常用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某事或某人值得怀疑或令人质疑。它可以指证据不足、动机不明确或行为不妥当的情况。

例句：

1.His alibi was questionable, so the police continued their investigation.  他的不在场证明令人怀疑，所以警方继续调查。

2. The ethics of the experiment are highly questionable.  这个实验的伦理道德非常值得怀疑。

3.The restaurant's hygiene was questionable, leading to several cases of food poisoning. 餐厅的卫生状况令人担忧，导致了几起食物中毒事件。

4.Her motives for donating such a large sum of money are questionable. 她捐赠如此巨额款项的动机令人怀疑。

5.The data used in the study is questionable; further research is needed. 研究中使用的数据值得怀疑；需要进一步的研究。
