gabler:/ˈɡæblər/| n. |喋喋不休的人；饶舌者 |A gabler is a person who talks excessively and often about unimportant things. They are characterized by their incessant talking and often rambling speech.  |例句：He's a terrible gabler; I could barely get a word in edgewise. 他是个喋喋不休的人；我几乎插不上话。|例句：She's a notorious gabler, always filling the room with her chatter. 她是个出了名的饶舌者，总是用她的喋喋不休充满整个房间。|近义词：chatterbox, blabbermouth, talkative person |反义词：silent person, quiet person |名词
