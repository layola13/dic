hirelings:/ˈhaɪərliŋz/| n.|雇佣兵，受雇者|People employed for wages, especially mercenary soldiers.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of loyalty or commitment.|例句：The king relied heavily on his hirelings to win the battle. 国王严重依赖他的雇佣兵来赢得战斗。

例句：He felt like a mere hireling, easily replaced by anyone. 他觉得自己只是一个廉价的雇工，很容易被任何人取代。


近义词：mercenaries, employees, lackeys, mercenaries

反义词：loyalists, volunteers, regulars


词性:名词
