overenergetic:/ˌoʊvərˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/| adj. |精力过剩的，过于活跃的 | excessively energetic; having too much energy.  |例句：The overenergetic child bounced off the walls.  这个精力过剩的孩子在墙上蹦来蹦去。

例句：Her overenergetic puppy kept her awake all night.  她精力过剩的小狗让她整夜睡不着觉。


近义词：hyperactive, excessively energetic, overly active, tireless, dynamic


反义词：lethargic, sluggish, apathetic, inactive, languid


词源：over- (prefix indicating excess) + energetic (having or showing energy)  前缀over-表示过度，energetic表示精力充沛的。
