morbidity:/mɔːˈbɪdəti/| n. | 病态；发病率；死亡率 | The morbidity of the disease is high in this region.  这个地区该疾病的发病率很高。|近义词：sickliness, illness, death rate |反义词：health, wellness


英文解析:Morbidity refers to the state of being diseased or unhealthy, often in a way that is unpleasant or disturbing.  It can also refer to the rate of disease or illness in a population.

例句:

The high morbidity rate among the elderly is a cause for concern.老年人的高发病率令人担忧。

His morbid fascination with death was unsettling to others.他对死亡的病态迷恋令他人不安。


词源:

The word "morbidity" is derived from the Latin word "morbidus," meaning "diseased" or "sick."  It entered English in the 16th century.
