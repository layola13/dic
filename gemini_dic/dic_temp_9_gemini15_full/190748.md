savant:/ˈsævɑːnt/| n. |学者；天才；专家|A savant is a person with a very high level of ability or knowledge in a particular area, often despite having limited overall intellectual capacity.  They often exhibit exceptional skills in specific domains, such as memorization, calculation, or artistic creation. |Example: The autistic savant could instantly calculate complex mathematical problems.  这位自闭症天才可以瞬间计算复杂的数学问题。  The child is a savant in music, demonstrating extraordinary talent at a young age. 这个孩子是音乐天才，从小就展现出非凡的天赋。|近义词：genius, prodigy, expert, specialist|反义词：ignoramus, dunce, incompetent|名词
