perthite:/ˈpɜːrθaɪt/| n. |透长石|A feldspar mineral consisting of an intimate intergrowth of two feldspars, usually albite and orthoclase.  It exhibits a characteristic play of colors, often pearly or iridescent. Perthite is a relatively common mineral found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|Example: The geologist identified the pearly sheen in the rock sample as indicative of perthite.|近义词:antiperthite|反义词:None
