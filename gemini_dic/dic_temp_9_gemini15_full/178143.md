recognize:/ˈrekəɡnaɪz/ | vt. |认出，识别，承认|To identify someone or something previously known.  To acknowledge the existence, validity, or legality of something.|例句：I recognized him from his photograph. 我从照片上认出了他。|例句：The government recognized the new republic. 政府承认了新的共和国。|近义词：identify, acknowledge, perceive|反义词：misidentify, ignore, deny|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recognize her face. = 我认出她的脸了。
We recognize the importance of education. = 我们认识到教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
He recognized his mistake. = 他认识到了自己的错误。
She recognized the melody instantly. = 她立刻认出了这首曲调。


现在完成时:
I have recognized that pattern before. = 我以前见过这种模式。
Scientists have recognized the link between smoking and cancer. = 科学家们已经认识到吸烟和癌症之间的联系。


过去完成时:
I had recognized him from a previous meeting. = 我在之前的会议上认出了他。
They had recognized the danger before it was too late. = 他们在为时已晚之前就意识到了危险。


将来时:
I will recognize him when I see him. = 我看到他时就会认出他来。
We will recognize the need for change soon. = 我们很快就会意识到需要改变。


虚拟语气:
If I recognized him, I would say hello. = 如果我认出他，我会和他打招呼。
I wish I had recognized the signs sooner. = 我真希望我早点发现这些迹象。


被动语态:
His talent was recognized by the critics. = 他的天赋受到了评论家的认可。
The problem is widely recognized. = 这个问题得到了广泛的认可。


疑问句:
Do you recognize this song? = 你认得这首歌吗？
Does she recognize the significance of the event? = 她认识到这次事件的重要性吗？


否定句:
I didn't recognize him at first. = 我一开始没认出他。
They didn't recognize the seriousness of the situation. = 他们没有意识到情况的严重性。
