mantle: /ˈmæntl/ | n. | 地幔；斗篷；覆盖物；职责；继承权 |

英文解析:A mantle can refer to the Earth's mantle (the layer between the crust and the core), a loose cloak or shawl, a covering or layer, a significant role or responsibility, or the right or privilege of succession.

例句:
The Earth's mantle is incredibly hot and under immense pressure.  = 地球的地幔非常热，并且承受着巨大的压力。

He draped his mantle over the chair. = 他把斗篷披在椅子上。

A mantle of snow covered the landscape. = 一层积雪覆盖了整个景色。

She inherited the mantle of leadership from her father. = 她从父亲那里继承了领导权。


近义词: cloak, shawl, covering, robe, responsibility, inheritance, legacy

反义词:  (Depending on the context)  bareness, nudity, abandonment, relinquishment
