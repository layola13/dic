craze:/kreɪz/| n. v.|狂热；风尚；使狂热；使发狂|A craze is a very strong enthusiasm or interest in something that is shared by many people, especially for a short period.  It can also mean to make someone excited or enthusiastic about something.|例句：The latest craze is fidget spinners.  最近的时尚是手指陀螺。|近义词：fad, mania, passion, enthusiasm|反义词：apathy, indifference|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new dance craze sweeps the nation. = 新的舞蹈热潮席卷全国。
This new technology is creating a craze. = 这项新技术正在引起狂热。


一般过去时:
The Pokemon craze gripped the world in the late 1990s. = 精灵宝可梦热潮在20世纪90年代末席卷全球。
That fashion craze quickly faded. = 那股时尚热潮迅速消退了。


现在完成时:
The craze for online shopping has transformed retail. = 网购热潮改变了零售业。
This craze has lasted longer than many predicted. = 这股热潮持续的时间比许多人预测的要长。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the beanie baby craze had already peaked. = 当我到达时，豆豆娃热潮已经达到顶峰。
Before the concert, the craze for tickets had driven prices up. = 音乐会之前，对门票的狂热已经导致价格上涨。


将来时:
Experts predict a new fitness craze will emerge next year. = 专家预测明年将会出现一种新的健身热潮。
The next big craze will likely be related to virtual reality. = 下一个大的热潮可能与虚拟现实有关。


虚拟语气:
If this were a craze, it would be everywhere. = 如果这是种时尚，它会到处都是。
I wish I hadn't missed out on the initial craze. = 我真希望我没有错过最初的热潮。


被动语态:
The city was gripped by a craze for historical reenactments. = 这座城市被历史重演的狂热所席卷。
The market is being driven by the latest craze. = 市场正被最新的时尚所驱动。


疑问句:
Is this just a passing craze? = 这只是一时的狂热吗？
What is the current craze among young people? = 年轻人现在的时尚是什么？


否定句:
That wasn't a genuine craze; it was just hype. = 那不是真正的狂热；那只是炒作。
The new product failed to generate a craze. = 新产品未能引起狂热。
