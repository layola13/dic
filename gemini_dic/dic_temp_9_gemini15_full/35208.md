cluck:/klʌk/| v. n.|咯咯叫；啧啧声|To make a short, repeated sound like a hen.  A short, repeated sound, especially one made by a hen.|The hen clucked contentedly as she sat on her eggs. 母鸡满意地咯咯叫着，坐在蛋上。 The old woman clucked her tongue in disapproval. 老妇人啧啧地表示不满。|chatter, cluck,peep|silent, quiet|动词，名词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hen clucks all day long. = 母鸡整天咯咯叫。
He often clucks his tongue when he is annoyed. = 他生气时经常啧啧舌头。

一般过去时:
The hen clucked loudly after laying an egg. = 母鸡下蛋后大声咯咯叫。
She clucked her tongue at the messy room. = 她对凌乱的房间啧啧舌头。

现在完成时:
The hen has clucked all morning. = 母鸡叫了一上午了。
He has clucked his disapproval at the plan several times. = 他多次啧啧表示对这个计划的不赞成。

过去完成时:
The hen had clucked before I even woke up. = 我醒来之前母鸡就已经咯咯叫了。
She had clucked her tongue in disappointment before she spoke. = 她说话之前就失望地啧啧了舌头。

将来时:
The hen will cluck when she sees her chicks. = 母鸡看到小鸡就会咯咯叫。
He will probably cluck his tongue if he sees the results. = 如果他看到结果，他可能会啧啧舌头。

虚拟语气:
If the hen clucked louder, the chicks would come running. = 如果母鸡叫得更大声，小鸡就会跑过来。
If she had clucked her disapproval earlier, things might have been different. = 如果她早点表示反对，事情可能会有所不同。

被动语态:
(The sound of clucking was) heard from the henhouse. = 从鸡舍里听到咯咯叫的声音。(被动语态中，cluck 通常不用被动)
(Disapproval was) expressed by a cluck of the tongue. = 用啧啧舌头来表达了不满。（被动语态中，cluck 通常不用被动）

疑问句:
Does the hen cluck often? = 母鸡经常咯咯叫吗？
Did you hear the hen cluck? = 你听到母鸡咯咯叫了吗？

否定句:
The hen didn't cluck this morning. = 母鸡今天早上没叫。
He didn't cluck his tongue, he just sighed. = 他没有啧啧舌头，只是叹了口气。


PHRASE->
cluck of disapproval: 啧啧表示不满
cluck of satisfaction: 满意地咯咯叫
cluck around: 闲逛，浪费时间 (informal)
cluck something out:  （用手指）快速敲打出（信息） (informal)
to cluck and fuss:  唠叨抱怨，焦虑不安


词源:
The word "cluck" is an onomatopoeia, meaning it imitates the sound it describes.  Its origins are likely Proto-Germanic, though precise tracing is difficult due to its imitative nature.
