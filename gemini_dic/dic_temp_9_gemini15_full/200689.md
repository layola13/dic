sleepful: /ˈsliːpfʊl/ | adj. | 困倦的，想睡的 |  Sleepy; inclined to sleep.  The word is rarely used in modern English and is considered somewhat archaic or literary.  It emphasizes a state of being full of sleepiness. | 例句：The sleepful child drifted off to slumber.  这个困倦的孩子渐渐睡着了。|近义词：sleepy, drowsy, somnolent | 反义词：wakeful, alert
