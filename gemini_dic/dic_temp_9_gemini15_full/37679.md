compadres:/kɒmˈpædreɪz/ | n. |哥们儿，朋友，老伙计 |  A compadre is a close friend, especially a male friend. The term often implies a strong bond of friendship, often with shared experiences or a sense of camaraderie. It's particularly common in Hispanic cultures. | Example:  He and his compadres went fishing every weekend. 他和他的哥们儿每个周末都去钓鱼。  |近义词：buddy, pal, friend, mate | 反义词：enemy, foe, stranger
