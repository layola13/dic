louse:/laʊs/| n.|虱子|A small, wingless insect that lives on the bodies of humans and animals, feeding on blood.  Lice are parasitic and can cause itching and irritation.|例句：The child had a bad case of head lice.  这个孩子得了严重的头部虱子。|例句：We need to treat the infestation of lice on the chickens. 我们需要治疗鸡身上的虱子感染。|近义词：nit (louse egg)|反义词：None
