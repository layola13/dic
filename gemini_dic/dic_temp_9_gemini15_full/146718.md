/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmədʒənaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度均质化的 | Excessively homogenized; lacking diversity or individuality.  This term typically refers to situations where a process intended to create uniformity has gone too far, resulting in a lack of variety and potentially negative consequences.  For example, it can describe a cultural landscape that has lost its unique regional characteristics, or a food product that has been processed to the point of lacking flavor and texture. | The overhomogenized music industry produces too many songs that sound alike. = 过度均质化的音乐产业制作了太多听起来一样的歌曲。

The overhomogenized educational system fails to cater to diverse learning styles. = 过度均质化的教育体系未能满足不同的学习风格。|近义词：uniform, standardized, monotonous, bland |反义词：diverse, varied, heterogeneous, distinctive
