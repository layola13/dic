scrapies: /ˈskreɪpiːz/ | n. |羊搔痒病 | A fatal, degenerative, neurological disease of sheep and goats, caused by a prion.  It is characterized by intense itching, progressive neurological dysfunction, and ultimately death.  The disease is transmissible to other animals, including humans (causing Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease). | Example: Scrapie is a serious concern for sheep farmers, leading to significant economic losses.  羊搔痒病是养羊户严重关心的问题，导致了巨大的经济损失。  The symptoms of scrapie, such as intense itching and behavioral changes, are often subtle in the early stages. 羊搔痒病的症状，例如剧烈瘙痒和行为改变，在早期通常是微妙的。|近义词:  transmissible spongiform encephalopathy (TSE) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it refers to a specific disease)| 词性:名词
