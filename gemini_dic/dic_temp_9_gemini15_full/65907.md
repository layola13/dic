ewers:/ˈjuːərz/| n.| 水罐；大水壶 |A large jug, typically with a wide mouth and handle, used for holding and pouring liquids.  |Example: The old ewer sat on the mantelpiece, gleaming in the firelight.  古老的水罐放在壁炉台上，在火光中闪闪发光。  The server carried a large ewer of water to the table. 服务员端着一大壶水走向餐桌。|近义词：jug, pitcher, carafe|反义词：None specifically, but small container could be considered opposite.|名词
