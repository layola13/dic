repossession:/ˌriːpəˈzeʃən/| n. |收回；收回财产；赎回|The act of taking back something (especially property) because of a failure to make payments.  It often refers to the legal process by which a lender takes back property after a borrower defaults on a loan. |例句：The bank initiated repossession of his car after he missed several payments.  银行在他拖欠几期贷款后启动了汽车收缴程序。

例句：After failing to meet the mortgage payments, the family faced repossession of their home.  由于未能按时支付抵押贷款，这家人面临着房屋被收回的风险。


近义词：recovery, seizure, reclamation|反义词：release, relinquishment
