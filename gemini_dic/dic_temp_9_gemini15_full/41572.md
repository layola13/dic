corporation:/ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/| n. |公司，法人|A large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity (legally a person) and recognized as such in law.|例句：He works for a large multinational corporation. 他在一个大型跨国公司工作。|例句：The corporation is facing a lawsuit. 这家公司正面临诉讼。|近义词：company, firm, enterprise|反义词：sole proprietorship, partnership
