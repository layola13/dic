quenching:/ˈkwɛntʃɪŋ/| v.| 淬火；熄灭；抑制；解渴 | To cool (metal) suddenly after heating, to suppress or extinguish something, to satisfy thirst. |例句：The blacksmith quenched the hot iron in water.  铁匠把烧红的铁淬在水里。
例句：He quenched his thirst with a glass of water. 他喝了一杯水解渴。


近义词：extinguishing, suppressing, satisfying, cooling


反义词：igniting, inflaming, stimulating
