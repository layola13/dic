decker:/ˈdɛkər/| n. |层楼；甲板；(尤指双层或多层)公共汽车；|A decker is a structure with multiple levels, often referring to a building with multiple floors or a bus with two or more decks.  The term often implies height and layered structure.|例句：The hotel has ten deckers.|十层楼的酒店。The double-decker bus is full.|双层巴士满了。|近义词：floor, level, storey|反义词：single-story, ground floor
