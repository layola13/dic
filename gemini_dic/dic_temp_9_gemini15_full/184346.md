respice:/ˈrɛspaɪs/ | v. |回顾，反省，缅怀|To look back; to review past events or experiences, often with a sense of reflection or contemplation.  It implies a thoughtful consideration of the past, often to gain insight or learn from it.|例句：Let us respice the lessons learned from past mistakes. =让我们回顾一下从过去的错误中吸取的教训。

例句：Respice, and you will find the wisdom needed to proceed. =回顾一下，你就会找到继续前进所需的智慧。


近义词：review, reflect, contemplate, consider, remember

反义词：ignore, neglect, forget, overlook

词性：动词
