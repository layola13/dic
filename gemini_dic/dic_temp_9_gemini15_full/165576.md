premedication: /ˌpriːˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 预先用药，术前用药 | Medication given before a procedure or treatment to reduce anxiety, pain, or other undesirable effects.  | 例句：The patient received premedication to reduce anxiety before surgery.  病人术前接受了预先用药以减轻焦虑。| 例句：Premedication with antiemetics is common before chemotherapy.  化疗前常用抗呕吐药进行预先用药。| 近义词：prophylactic medication, pre-operative medication | 反义词：postmedication
