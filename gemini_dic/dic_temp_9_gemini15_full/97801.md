injuring: /ɪnˈdʒɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 伤害；损害 | The act of causing physical harm or damage.  |例句：The accident resulted in several people being injured.  事故导致多人受伤。|例句：His careless words injured her feelings. 他粗心的话语伤害了她的感情。|近义词：harming, wounding, hurting |反义词：healing, curing, repairing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is injuring his knee by overtraining. = 他过度训练，伤到了膝盖。
The company's actions are injuring its reputation. = 公司的行为正在损害其声誉。

一般过去时:
The fall injured his ankle. = 那次摔倒伤了他的脚踝。
She injured her pride by failing the exam. = 考试不及格伤害了她的自尊心。

现在完成时:
He has injured his back lifting heavy boxes. = 他搬重箱子伤了背。
The scandal has injured the company's image. = 丑闻损害了公司的形象。

过去完成时:
He had injured his arm before the game even started. = 比赛还没开始他就已经伤了胳膊。
They had injured their relationship beyond repair. = 他们已经伤及感情，无法弥补。

将来时:
He will injure himself if he continues to work so hard. = 如果他继续这么努力工作，他会伤到自己的。
This new policy will injure small businesses. = 这项新政策将会损害小型企业。

虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he wouldn't have injured himself. = 如果他更小心一点，他就不会受伤了。
I wish I hadn't injured his feelings. = 我真希望我没有伤害他的感情。


被动语态:
Many people were injured in the earthquake. = 许多人在地震中受伤。
His reputation was injured by the false accusations. = 他名誉因为虚假指控而受损。

疑问句:
Were any people injured in the accident? = 事故中有没有人受伤？
Was his reputation injured by the scandal? = 他的声誉因丑闻受损了吗？

否定句:
He didn't injure himself during the match. = 他在比赛中没有受伤。
The incident didn't injure anyone seriously. = 这起事件没有造成严重的伤亡。


PHRASE->
seriously injuring = 严重受伤

injuring property = 损坏财产

causing injury = 造成伤害
