nonconnivances:/ˌnɒnkənˈaɪvənsɪz/ | n. | 不纵容；不勾结；不串通 | The absence of connivance or collusion; a lack of secret cooperation for an illegal or harmful purpose.  Nonconnivance implies a refusal to participate in or facilitate wrongdoing. | Example: The investigation revealed a climate of nonconnivance among the officials, suggesting they were not involved in the corruption scandal.  调查显示官员之间存在一种不纵容的气氛，这表明他们没有参与腐败丑闻。|近义词: non-collaboration, non-complicity,  disassociation | 反义词: connivance, collusion, complicity
