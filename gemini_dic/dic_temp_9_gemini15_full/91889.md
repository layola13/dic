hyperirritable: /ˌhaɪpərˈɪrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 极度易怒的，过敏的 | Extremely irritable; easily provoked to anger or irritation.  This word describes a state of being significantly more irritable than usual, often indicating an underlying medical condition. |  The patient presented with hyperirritable symptoms, including extreme sensitivity to light and sound.  病人表现出过敏的症状，包括对光和声音极其敏感。  |近义词：irritable, oversensitive, hypersensitive, choleric |反义词：calm, placid, serene, unflappable
