fissipara:/fɪˈsɪpərə/| adj.|裂殖的；裂殖生的| Relating to or characterized by fission, a type of asexual reproduction in which a cell or organism divides into two or more parts, each of which develops into a separate individual.  |Example: The amoeba reproduces by fissiparous division.  变形虫通过裂殖繁殖。|近义词：fission, splitting, dividing|反义词：sexual reproduction, budding (in some contexts)
