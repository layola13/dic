non-treaty: /ˌnɑːnˈtriːti/ | adj. | 非条约的，不受条约约束的 |  Not governed or established by a treaty; outside the scope of a treaty.  | 例句：The non-treaty obligations were still considered binding.  非条约义务仍被认为具有约束力。

The non-treaty dispute led to increased tensions between the two countries.  非条约纠纷导致两国关系紧张加剧。


近义词：unconventional, extralegal, informal


反义词：treaty-based, contractual
