exaction:/ɪkˈsækʃən/| n. |敲诈；强索；苛刻的要求；(尤指税款的)征收 |The act of demanding and obtaining something, especially money, by force or authority;  the imposition of something onerous.  It often implies unfairness or excessive demands. |例句：The exaction of heavy taxes led to widespread discontent.  沉重的赋税征收导致了民众广泛的不满。  The exaction of promises from the hostages was a cruel act.  强迫人质许下承诺是残酷的行为。|近义词：extortion, levy, demand, imposition |反义词：leniency, generosity, concession |名词
