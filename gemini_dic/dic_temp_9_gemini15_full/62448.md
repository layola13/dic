endorse:/ɛnˈdɔːrs/|/ɪnˈdɔːrs/| vt.|认可；背书；支持；赞同|To give one's public approval or support to.|The athlete endorsed a new brand of sports drinks.  这位运动员为一个新的运动饮料品牌代言。|She endorsed the candidate's platform. 她支持该候选人的政纲。|support, approve, sanction, advocate|oppose, reject, condemn, denounce|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endorses many products. 他为许多产品代言。
They endorse the new policy. 他们支持这项新政策。

一般过去时:
She endorsed the check. 她在这张支票上背书。
The company endorsed his proposal. 公司批准了他的提案。

现在完成时:
He has endorsed several charities. 他已经为几家慈善机构捐款并支持。
They have endorsed the agreement. 他们已经认可了这项协议。

过去完成时:
The board had endorsed the plan before the meeting. 董事会在会议之前已经批准了这个计划。
She had endorsed the book before its publication.  她在书出版之前就推荐过这本书。

将来时:
The celebrity will endorse the new perfume.  这位名流将为这款新香水代言。
I believe they will endorse the bill. 我相信他们会通过这项法案。

虚拟语气:
If he endorsed the project, we would have more funding. 如果他支持这个项目，我们将会有更多的资金。
I wish the government would endorse this initiative. 我希望政府能支持这项倡议。

被动语态:
The product was endorsed by a famous actor.  该产品由一位著名演员代言。

疑问句:
Does he endorse this political party? 他支持这个政党吗？

否定句:

The bank refused to endorse the cheque. 银行拒绝在支票上背书。
