desire:/dɪˈzaɪər/| n. v.|渴望；欲望；愿望 |To want or wish for something strongly.  A strong feeling of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen.|例句：She has a strong desire to succeed. 她有强烈的成功欲望。

例句：I desire nothing more than peace and quiet. 我只渴望平静。


近义词：wish, longing, yearning, craving |反义词：aversion, dislike, indifference|词性:Noun & Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I desire a peaceful life. = 我渴望平静的生活。
She desires to travel the world. = 她渴望环游世界。


一般过去时:
He desired a promotion. = 他渴望升职。
They desired to see the new movie. = 他们渴望去看这部新电影。


现在完成时:
I have desired this for years. = 我多年来一直渴望这个。
She has desired to become a doctor since childhood. = 她从小就渴望成为一名医生。


过去完成时:
He had desired a better job before he found this one. = 在找到这份工作之前，他渴望一份更好的工作。
They had desired to buy a house but couldn't afford it. = 他们渴望买一套房子，但买不起。


将来时:
I will desire even more success in the future. = 未来我将渴望获得更大的成功。
She will desire a quiet retirement. = 她将渴望一个平静的退休生活。


虚拟语气:
I wish I desired more courage. = 我希望我更有勇气。(渴望某种特质)
If I desired it more strongly, I would have achieved it. = 如果我渴望得更强烈一些，我就会实现它了。


被动语态:
The position is desired by many candidates. = 许多候选人都渴望这个职位。
Peace is desired by all. =  和平是人人所渴望的。


疑问句:
What do you desire most in life? = 你生命中最渴望什么？
Does she desire anything else? = 她还渴望其他什么吗？


否定句:
I do not desire conflict. = 我不渴望冲突。
He did not desire to participate. = 他不想参与。


PHRASE->
have a desire for = 渴望…
express a desire = 表达愿望
fulfill a desire = 实现愿望
object of desire = 渴望的对象
beyond desire = 超出愿望


词源:
desire源自古法语desirer，最终源于拉丁语desiderare，意为“to long for, to wish for”。  The Latin word is composed of de- ("from, away") and siderare ("to wish, to desire").  The sense has remained largely consistent throughout its evolution.
