facetting:/ˈfæsɪtɪŋ/| v.|刻面，赋予多面性|To cut (a gem) into facets.  To give something multiple aspects or interpretations.|例句：The jeweler expertly facetted the diamond, bringing out its brilliance. 珠宝匠熟练地为钻石刻面，使其光彩夺目。 The political issue was facetted with economic and social considerations.  这个政治问题包含了经济和社会方面的考虑。|近义词：cutting, polishing, multi-faceted|反义词:smoothing, simplifying
