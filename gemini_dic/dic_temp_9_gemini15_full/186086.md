rezones:/ˌriːˈzoʊnz/| v.|重新分区，重新划区|To divide (an area) into new zones, often for different purposes such as residential, commercial, or industrial use. This often involves changing zoning laws and regulations.|The city council rezones the downtown area to encourage more residential development.|近义词：rezone, redistrict|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to maintain existing zoning.
