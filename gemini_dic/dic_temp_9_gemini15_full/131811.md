/ˌnɒnkɒnsɪkwɛns/ | adj. | 不重要的；无关紧要的；无足轻重的 | Not important or significant; inconsequential.  | The minor spelling error was a nonconsequence in the overall context of the essay.  这篇论文中细微的拼写错误在整体语境中无关紧要。 | unimportant, insignificant, trivial | important, significant, consequential | 形容词


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  The usage focuses on its application within sentences.

Example 1: His complaints were deemed nonconsequences by his manager.  他的抱怨被他的经理认为无关紧要。

Example 2:  The delay, though frustrating, was a nonconsequence in the grand scheme of things. 尽管令人沮丧，但在全局看来，延误是无关紧要的。


PHRASE->
This word doesn't readily form common phrases in the same way as "what".  Its usage is primarily as a standalone adjective.  Examples of phrases using words with similar meanings:

of little consequence = 不重要的
a matter of little consequence = 无关紧要的事情
of no consequence = 无足轻重的
without consequence = 无结果地
a consequence of = ...的结果
