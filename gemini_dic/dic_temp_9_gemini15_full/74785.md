freighted:/ˈfreɪtɪd/| adj.|充满…的；装载…的；忧虑的；焦虑的|burdened; filled; weighed down|The air was freighted with tension.  空气中充满了紧张气氛。|近义词：laden, burdened, charged|反义词：unburdened, light, carefree


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is freighted with valuable cargo. = 这艘船装载着贵重的货物。
His voice is freighted with emotion. = 他的声音充满了情感。


一般过去时:
