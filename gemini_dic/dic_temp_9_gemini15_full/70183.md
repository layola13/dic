fibrillation:/ˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |纤维颤动；心房颤动 |Rapid, irregular contractions of the heart muscle. This can be life-threatening if it affects the ventricles.  |例句：The patient experienced atrial fibrillation after the surgery.  手术后病人出现了心房颤动。

例句：His irregular heartbeat was diagnosed as ventricular fibrillation. 他的心律不齐被诊断为心室颤动。


近义词：flutter, tremor |反义词：normal heart rhythm


词源:  From Latin *fibrilla (diminutive of fibra "fibre"), + -ation.
