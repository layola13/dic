词源:
The word "jutelike" is a compound word.  It combines "jute," referring to the natural fiber obtained from the jute plant (Corchorus capsularis and Corchorus olitorius), with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.  Therefore, "jutelike" means "resembling jute."
