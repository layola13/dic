patent:/ˈpeɪtənt/| n. v.|专利；专利权；特许；公开；显而易见的 | a document granting someone the sole right to make, use, or sell an invention |  The inventor applied for a patent on his new device.  发明者申请了他新设备的专利。 | copyright, license | secret, hidden |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He holds several patents for his inventions. = 他拥有他几项发明的专利。
The company patents new technologies every year. = 这家公司每年都会申请新的技术专利。


一般过去时:
They patented their new software last year. = 他们去年为他们的新软件申请了专利。
Edison patented many inventions during his lifetime. = 爱迪生在他的一生中申请了许多发明专利。


现在完成时:
She has patented three innovative designs so far. = 到目前为止，她已经获得了三项创新设计的专利。
The researchers have patented their groundbreaking discovery. = 研究人员已经为他们的突破性发现申请了专利。


过去完成时:
Before launching the product, they had patented the core technology. = 在推出产品之前，他们已经为核心技术申请了专利。
He had patented the process years before his competitors. = 他在竞争对手之前几年就为这个过程申请了专利。


将来时:
They will patent their new drug next year. = 他们明年将为他们的新药申请专利。
We will have patented all our key technologies by the end of the year. = 到年底，我们将为我们所有的关键技术申请专利。


虚拟语气:
If they patented the invention earlier, they would have made more money. = 如果他们早点申请这项发明的专利，他们就会赚更多钱。
I wish I had patented my idea before someone else did. = 我真希望我能在别人之前为我的想法申请专利。


被动语态:
The invention was patented in 2023. = 这项发明于2023年获得专利。
Many innovative ideas are patented every year. = 每年都有许多创新想法获得专利。


疑问句:
Has the product been patented? = 这个产品获得专利了吗？
Is it possible to patent this design? = 这项设计可以申请专利吗？


否定句:
The design hasn't been patented yet. = 这项设计尚未获得专利。
They didn't patent their invention. = 他们没有为他们的发明申请专利。
