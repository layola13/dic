lion:/ˈlaɪən/| n.|狮子|A large, powerful cat with a tawny mane (in the male). Lions are social animals, living in prides.|例句：The lion roared loudly. 狮子大声咆哮。|例句：We saw a pride of lions in the African savanna. 我们在非洲大草原上看到一群狮子。|近义词：big cat, feline|反义词：lamb, rabbit
