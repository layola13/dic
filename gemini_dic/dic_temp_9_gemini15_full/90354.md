hovels:/ˈhɒvəl/| n.| 贫民窟; 破旧小屋 | A hovel is a small, poorly built and maintained dwelling, typically one that is dirty and overcrowded. It is often associated with poverty and lack of sanitation. |例句：They lived in a dilapidated hovel on the outskirts of town. 他们住在城郊一间破败不堪的小屋里。

例句：The refugees were forced to live in makeshift hovels after the earthquake. 地震后，难民被迫住在临时搭建的棚屋里。


近义词：shack, shanty, hut, cabin


反义词：mansion, palace, villa


词性:名词
