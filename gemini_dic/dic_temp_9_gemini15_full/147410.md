overnormalize:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməlaɪz/| v. |过度标准化；过度规范化 |To normalize excessively; to go beyond the appropriate level of normalization.  This often implies a loss of nuance, individuality, or vital information in the process. |Example: The company's attempt to overnormalize its processes led to decreased creativity and innovation. 公司试图过度规范化其流程，导致创造力和创新能力下降。|近义词: overstandardize, overregulate |反义词: undernormalize, individualize
