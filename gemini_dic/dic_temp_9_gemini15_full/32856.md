cholesterols:/kəˈlɛstərɔːlz/| n. |胆固醇（复数）|Cholesterols are a type of fat-like substance that is essential for the structure of cell membranes and the production of certain hormones. However, high levels of cholesterol in the blood can increase the risk of heart disease.  |例句：High levels of cholesterols in the blood can lead to atherosclerosis.  高胆固醇血症会导致动脉粥样硬化。

例句：Doctors often test for cholesterols as part of a routine health checkup. 医生经常在常规健康检查中检测胆固醇。


近义词：lipids (脂类)


反义词：无
