doddles:/ˈdɒdəlz/ | v. | 笨手笨脚地做事；随意涂鸦；闲逛 | To do something clumsily or aimlessly; to doodle; to wander idly. | Example: He was just *doddling* around the house, not really doing anything productive. 他只是在家里闲逛，并没有做任何有成效的事情。|近义词：fidget, trifle, dawdle | 反义词：work diligently, focus


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doddles around the garden. = 他在花园里闲逛。
She doddles aimlessly with a pen. = 她漫无目的地用笔涂鸦。


一般过去时:
He doddled about all afternoon. = 他整个下午都在闲逛。
She doddled a meaningless picture. = 她随意涂鸦了一幅毫无意义的画。


现在完成时:
He has been dooddling all morning. = 他整个上午都在涂鸦。
She has doddled her way through the whole project. = 她整个项目都是漫不经心地完成的。


过去完成时:
He had been dooddling for hours before he finally started working. = 他在开始工作之前已经涂鸦了好几个小时。
She had doddled away most of the day before realizing her deadline. = 她在她意识到截止日期之前已经虚度了大部分时间。


将来时:
He will be dooddling during the meeting. = 他将在会议期间涂鸦。
She will doodle all through the lecture. = 她将在整个讲座中涂鸦。


虚拟语气:
If he had more time, he would doodle more often. = 如果他有更多时间，他会更频繁地涂鸦。
If she were not so busy, she would not have doddled away so much time. = 如果她不那么忙，她就不会浪费那么多时间。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used passively)  A picture was doddled on the margin of the book.  = 一幅画被随意涂鸦在书的页边上。
(This is less common and sounds unnatural)


疑问句:
What are you dooddling? = 你在涂鸦什么？
Does he often doodle during lectures? = 他经常在课堂上涂鸦吗？


否定句:
He doesn't doodle very often. = 他不经常涂鸦。
She didn't doodle anything on the paper. = 她没有在纸上涂鸦任何东西。


PHRASE->
doodle around = 随意涂鸦；闲逛
doodle something = 涂鸦某物
idle doodle = 无聊的涂鸦
mindless doodle = 无意识的涂鸦
aimless doodle = 漫无目的的涂鸦
