gherao: /ɡəˈraʊ/ | v. |包围，围困 |  Hindi word meaning to surround or encircle, often used in the context of a political protest or demonstration where a building or person is surrounded by protesters.  It often implies a non-violent, but forceful, protest tactic. | 例句：The factory was gheraoed by striking workers. 工厂被罢工工人包围了。 The politician was gheraoed by angry constituents.  这位政治家被愤怒的选民包围了。| 近义词：besiege, surround, encircle, blockade | 反义词：free, liberate, release |  动词
