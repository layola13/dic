irascibility:/ˌɪræsɪˈbɪləti/| n. |易怒；暴躁；性情急躁 |The quality or state of being easily provoked to anger; irritability.  It refers to a person's tendency to become angry or annoyed quickly and easily. |His irascibility was legendary; he'd fly off the handle at the slightest provocation.  他的暴躁是出了名的；他会因为一点小事就勃然大怒。|irritability, short temper, hot temper, choler |calmness, patience, equanimity, placidity |名词
