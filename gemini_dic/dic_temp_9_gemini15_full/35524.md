coagulant: /koʊˈæɡjələnt/ | n. | 凝结剂，凝固剂 | A substance that causes a liquid to become thick or solid.  It is used in various processes to promote clotting or thickening. For example, in water treatment, coagulants help to clump together small particles so they can be removed.  In medicine, coagulants may be used to stop bleeding. |  例句：Aluminum sulfate is a common coagulant used in water purification.  硫酸铝是水净化中常用的凝结剂。

The addition of a coagulant helped to solidify the mixture. 添加凝结剂有助于使混合物凝固。


近义词：flocculant, thickener, clotting agent | 反义词：anticoagulant
