scooped:/skuːpt/| v.| 舀，挖，抢先获得 | To lift or take out (something) with a scoop or as if with a scoop; to obtain something before others. |例句：She scooped ice cream into a bowl. 她用勺子往碗里舀冰淇淋。

例句：The journalist scooped the story before any other news agency. 这名记者抢先报道了这条新闻。


近义词：ladled, dipped, excavated, preempted

反义词：poured (in the sense of pouring something back in)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scoops ice cream every day. 他每天都舀冰淇淋。
She scoops the dirt out of the garden. 她把土从花园里挖出来。

一般过去时:
I scooped the winning lottery ticket. 我买中了彩票大奖。
They scooped the news from a competitor. 他们从竞争对手那里抢到了新闻。

现在完成时:
She has scooped the most awards this year. 她今年获得的奖项最多。
They have scooped all the best apples. 他们把最好的苹果都摘了。

过去完成时:
He had scooped the ice cream before the party started. 派对开始前，他已经把冰淇淋舀好了。
She had scooped up all the toys before bedtime. 睡觉前她已经把所有的玩具都收拾好了。

将来时:
I will scoop the snow from the driveway. 我将铲除车道上的积雪。
They will scoop the information from the database. 他们将从数据库中提取信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had scooped the news earlier, I would have been famous. 如果我早点抢到这个新闻，我就会出名了。
I wish I had scooped that delicious pudding. 我真希望我舀到了那个美味的布丁。

被动语态:
The ice cream was scooped into cones. 冰淇淋被舀进蛋卷筒里。
The information was scooped by a rival company. 信息被竞争对手公司抢走了。

疑问句:
Did you scoop the ice cream? 你舀冰淇淋了吗？
Who scooped the award? 谁获得了奖项？

否定句:
I didn't scoop the ice cream. 我没有舀冰淇淋。
She didn't scoop the news. 她没有抢到新闻。


PHRASE->
scoop up: 迅速收集，抱起
scoop out: 挖出，掏空
scoop in: 舀入
scoop the pool: 独占鳌头，获得所有奖项
scoop someone: 抢先于某人
