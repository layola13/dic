dynamic: /daɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 动态的；有活力的；有影响力的 |  Possessing or characterized by energy or effective action; forceful; energetic.  |例句：The dynamic leader inspired her team to achieve great things.  这位充满活力的领导人激励她的团队取得了巨大的成就。|例句：The company is known for its dynamic marketing campaigns. 这家公司以其富有活力的营销活动而闻名。 |近义词：energetic, powerful, forceful, active |反义词：static, passive, inactive, inert |


USAGE->
