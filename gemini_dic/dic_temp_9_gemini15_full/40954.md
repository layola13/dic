cooperating:/koʊˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |合作；协作|The act of working together towards a common goal.  It implies mutual effort and shared responsibility. |例句：The team is cooperating on a new project. 团队正在合作一个新项目。
例句：The two countries are cooperating to fight terrorism. 这两个国家正在合作打击恐怖主义。|近义词：collaborating, working together, uniting |反义词：competing, opposing, hindering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are cooperating on the research. = 他们在合作研究。
We cooperate to achieve our goals. = 我们合作以实现我们的目标。


一般过去时:
The two companies cooperated on a joint venture. = 两家公司在合资企业上进行了合作。
They cooperated fully with the police investigation. = 他们全力配合警方调查。


现在完成时:
We have cooperated on many projects over the years. = 多年来，我们在许多项目上进行了合作。
They have cooperated effectively to solve the crisis. = 他们有效地合作解决了危机。


过去完成时:
They had cooperated before on similar projects. = 他们之前在类似项目上合作过。
The team had cooperated well, leading to success. = 团队合作良好，最终取得了成功。


将来时:
We will cooperate closely on the upcoming campaign. = 我们将在即将到来的活动中密切合作。
They will cooperate to resolve the conflict peacefully. = 他们将合作和平解决冲突。


虚拟语气:
If they cooperated, the project would be completed on time. = 如果他们合作，项目就能按时完成。
I wish they would cooperate more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地合作。


被动语态:
The project was cooperated on by several teams. = 该项目由几个团队合作完成。
The plan was cooperated on by all stakeholders. = 该计划得到了所有利益相关者的合作。


疑问句:
Are they cooperating on this project? = 他们在这个项目上合作吗？
Will they cooperate with our team? = 他们会与我们的团队合作吗？


否定句:
They are not cooperating with the authorities. = 他们没有与当局合作。
The two parties refused to cooperate. = 双方拒绝合作。


PHRASE->
cooperate with = 与...合作
cooperate on = 合作进行
fully cooperate = 全力合作
refuse to cooperate = 拒绝合作
international cooperation = 国际合作
