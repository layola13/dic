lutulent:/ˈluːtʃələnt/| adj.|浑浊的；污秽的；晦涩难懂的|Muddy or opaque; obscure or difficult to understand.  The meaning is often extended to imply something morally questionable or corrupt.|例句：The lutulent water of the swamp reflected the murky sky.  沼泽里浑浊的水倒映着阴暗的天空。


The lutulent prose of the academic paper made it nearly impossible to follow. 这篇学术论文晦涩难懂的文字使人几乎无法理解。|近义词：murky, turbid, opaque, obscure, muddy, ambiguous|反义词：clear, transparent, lucid, pellucid, unambiguous

词源:  From Late Latin *lutulentus, from lutum "mud."  The word ultimately derives from Proto-Italic *louto-, related to Latin lavo "I wash," suggesting a connection to something washed out or muddy.
