sine:/saɪn/| n.|正弦；正弦函数|The sine is a trigonometric function that relates the angle of a right-angled triangle to the ratio of the length of the side opposite the angle to the length of the hypotenuse.  It is widely used in mathematics, physics, and engineering.|例句：The sine of 30 degrees is 0.5.  30度的正弦值为0.5。|近义词：trigonometric function|反义词：无
