seriation:/ˌsɪəriˈeɪʃən/| n. |排序；序列；系列化 |The arrangement of things in a series or sequence.  It often refers to the ordering of events in time or the arrangement of objects in space. Seriation is a crucial technique in archaeology for dating artifacts and understanding cultural development.|例句：The seriation of the pottery shards helped archaeologists establish a chronological sequence of the site's occupation.  陶器碎片的排序帮助考古学家建立了遗址居住的年代顺序。

例句：The children learned seriation by arranging blocks from smallest to largest. 孩子们通过将积木从小到大排列来学习排序。


近义词：ordering, sequencing, arrangement, classification |反义词：randomness, disorder, chaos |词性：名词
