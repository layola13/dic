priggishnesses:/ˈprɪɡɪʃnəsɪz/| n. |假道学，自以为是的行为；装腔作势|The plural of priggishness, referring to multiple instances of affected propriety or self-righteousness.  It denotes a display of excessive or affected virtue, often accompanied by a condescending attitude. |例句：His priggishnesses were constantly on display, irritating everyone around him.  他的假道学行为总是显露无遗，惹恼了周围所有人。
His constant priggishnesses made him unpopular with his colleagues. 他总是装腔作势，同事们都不喜欢他。|近义词：pretentiousness, sanctimoniousness, self-righteousness |反义词：humility, modesty, unpretentiousness
