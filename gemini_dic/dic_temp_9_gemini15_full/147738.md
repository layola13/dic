overprocrastination: /ˌoʊvərˌproʊkræˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |过度拖延；拖延症加重 |The act of procrastinating to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences.  It goes beyond simple procrastination and involves a significant impairment in one's ability to manage time and tasks effectively. |例句：His overprocrastination led to him missing the deadline and losing the contract.  他的过度拖延导致他错过最后期限并失去了合同。

His overprocrastination on the project resulted in a significant loss of productivity and a decline in his mental well-being. 他在项目上的过度拖延导致生产力大幅下降，精神健康状况也恶化。|近义词：extreme procrastination, chronic procrastination, excessive delay |反义词：promptness, timeliness, diligence
