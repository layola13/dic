lugubriously:/luːˈɡruːbriəsli/| adv.|悲哀地；忧郁地；凄凉地|In a mournful, gloomy, or dismal manner; sorrowfully.  The adjective "lugubrious" describes something that expresses sorrow or grief in an exaggerated or affected way. It often suggests a theatrical display of sadness. |例句：He spoke lugubriously about the death of his cat. 他悲哀地谈论着他猫的死。

例句：She lugubriously recounted the events of that terrible day. 她凄凉地叙述了那可怕的一天发生的事情。


近义词：mournfully, sorrowfully, dolefully, gloomily, dejectedly

反义词：joyfully, cheerfully, merrily, happily
