emendatory: /ɪˈmɛndətɔːri/ | adj. | 修正的；订正的；改正的 | Relating to or characterized by emendation (the correction of a text).  It suggests the process of improving or perfecting something, typically a text, by making alterations. |

The emendatory notes in the scholarly edition clarified several obscure passages.  | 修正的学术版本中的注释阐明了几个晦涩的段落。

The professor offered several emendatory suggestions for the student's essay. | 教授为学生的论文提供了一些修改建议。


近义词：corrective, corrective, amendatory, revisory, rectifying


反义词：uncorrected, unaltered, unchanged


词性：形容词
