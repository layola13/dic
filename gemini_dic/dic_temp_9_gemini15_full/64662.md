escalates:/ˈɛskəleɪts/| v.| 升级；恶化；加剧 | To become or make something become more intense or serious.  |例句：The situation escalated quickly and violence erupted.局势迅速恶化，暴力爆发了。
例句：The conflict escalated into a full-blown war. 冲突升级为全面战争。|近义词：intensify, worsen, aggravate, heighten |反义词：de-escalate, lessen, mitigate, reduce |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tension escalates daily. = 紧张局势每天都在升级。
The price of gas escalates steadily. = 油价稳步上涨。

一般过去时:
The argument escalated into a fight. = 争吵升级为打斗。
The fire escalated rapidly. = 大火迅速蔓延。

现在完成时:
The conflict has escalated significantly. = 冲突已显著升级。
The situation has escalated beyond our control. = 局势已失控。

过去完成时:
The problem had escalated before we intervened. = 我们介入之前，问题已经恶化了。
Tensions had escalated significantly before the summit. = 峰会之前，紧张局势已显著升级。

将来时:
The situation will escalate if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会恶化。
The cost is likely to escalate in the coming months. = 未来几个月成本可能会上升。

虚拟语气:
If we don't address the issue, the problem will escalate. = 如果我们不解决这个问题，问题将会恶化。
I wish the conflict hadn't escalated so quickly. = 我希望冲突没有这么快恶化。

被动语态:
The conflict was escalated by the actions of both sides. = 冲突因双方的行为而升级。
The situation was escalated unnecessarily. = 局势被不必要地升级了。

疑问句:
Will the situation escalate further? = 局势还会进一步恶化吗？
Has the conflict escalated to a dangerous level? = 冲突是否已升级到危险的程度？

否定句:
The situation did not escalate as we feared. = 情况没有像我们担心的那样恶化。
We managed to prevent the situation from escalating. = 我们设法阻止了局势的恶化。


PHRASE->
escalate a conflict = 升级冲突
escalate a crisis = 升级危机
rapidly escalate = 迅速升级
gradually escalate = 逐渐升级
escalate tensions = 加剧紧张局势
