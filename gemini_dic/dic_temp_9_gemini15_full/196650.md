setup:/ˈsɛtʌp/| n. vt.|装置；准备；设置；安排；结构；体制|To arrange or prepare something; the way something is arranged or organized; a system or structure.|例句：The setup for the experiment was complex.  实验的装置很复杂。

例句：We need to setup a meeting to discuss this project. 我们需要安排一次会议来讨论这个项目。


近义词：arrangement, configuration, installation, system, organization|反义词：teardown, dismantling, destruction|词性:n. vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sets up the equipment every morning. = 他每天早上都安装设备。
They set up a new company last year. = 他们去年成立了一家新公司。


一般过去时:
She set up a beautiful display in the window. = 她把橱窗布置得很漂亮。
We set up camp by the river. = 我们在河边扎营。


现在完成时:
They have set up a new website for their business. = 他们已经为他们的业务建立了一个新的网站。
I have set up a meeting with the client. = 我已经与客户安排了一次会议。


过去完成时:

They had set up the stage before the concert began. = 音乐会开始前，他们已经搭建好了舞台。


将来时:

I wish I had set up a retirement fund earlier. = 我真希望我早点建立退休基金。

否定句:

词源:
The word "setup" originates from the verb "set," which has Old English roots, and the preposition "up."  The meaning evolved from the literal sense of placing something upright or in position to its broader usage of arrangement and preparation.
