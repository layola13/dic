erythema:/ˌɛrɪˈθiːmə/| n. |红斑，潮红 | Redness of the skin caused by dilation of superficial blood vessels, often a symptom of inflammation or infection.  Erythema can manifest in various ways, depending on the underlying cause. It can be localized or widespread, and may be accompanied by other symptoms such as swelling, pain, or itching. | Example: The sunburn caused significant erythema across his shoulders.  这次晒伤导致他肩膀上出现了明显的红斑。  The rash presented as widespread erythema with small, raised bumps.皮疹表现为弥漫性红斑，伴有小的隆起。|近义词：redness, flushing, inflammation |反义词：pallor
