resanctify: /ˌriːˈsæŋktɪfaɪ/ | v. |重新神圣化；重新奉献 | To make sacred or holy again; to consecrate anew.  The act of resanctifying involves restoring something to a state of holiness or sacredness after it has been profaned or neglected. |例句：The church was resanctified after extensive renovations. 教堂经过大规模翻修后重新被奉献。

The ancient ritual was resanctified by the community after years of neglect.  古老的仪式在多年被忽视后被社区重新神圣化。


近义词：reconsecrate, rededicate, sanctify (again) | 反义词：desecrate, profane
