scrutinise: /ˈskruːtənaɪz/ | vt. |仔细检查；细察；严格审查 | To examine or inspect closely and thoroughly. |例句：The police scrutinised his passport carefully.  警察仔细检查了他的护照。

例句：The judge scrutinised the evidence before making a decision.  法官在做出决定之前仔细审查了证据。


近义词：examine, inspect, investigate, review, analyze | 反义词：glance over, skim


词源：源自中古英语 *scrutiner,源自古法语 escrutiner,源自拉丁语 scrutari (to search, examine)
