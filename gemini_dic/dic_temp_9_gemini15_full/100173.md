interregionally: /ˌɪntərˈriːdʒənəli/ | adv. | 区域之间地；跨区域地 |  Relating to or occurring between different regions.  |例句：The company operates interregionally, with offices in several states.  该公司跨区域运营，在多个州设有办事处。

例句：Interregional trade agreements are crucial for economic growth.  区域间的贸易协定对经济增长至关重要。


近义词：transregionally, across regions, between regions


反义词：locally, regionally (in a single region)


词性：副词
