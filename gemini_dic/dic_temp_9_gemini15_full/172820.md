quadriplegias: /kwɑːdrɪˈpliːdʒəz/ | n. | 四肢瘫痪 | Paralysis affecting all four limbs.  It refers to a condition where a person loses the ability to move or feel in their arms and legs due to injury or illness.  | Example:  The accident resulted in quadriplegias, leaving him completely dependent on others.  事故导致他四肢瘫痪，完全依赖他人。 | 近义词: tetraplegia, total paralysis | 反义词:  full mobility, unimpaired movement |
