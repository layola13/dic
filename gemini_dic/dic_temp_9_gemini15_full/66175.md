exchange:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | n., vt. | 交换；交流；兑换；交易 |  The act of giving something and receiving something else in return;  a reciprocal giving and receiving.  |  例句：They exchanged gifts at Christmas.  他们在圣诞节交换礼物。

例句： We had a lively exchange of ideas during the meeting.  我们在会议上进行了热烈的思想交流。


近义词：swap, trade, barter, interchange | 反义词：retain, keep


词源：源自古法语 *eschangier，由 *es- (加强前缀) + *changier (改变) 构成。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They exchange emails regularly. = 他们定期交换邮件。
I exchange greetings with my neighbors every morning. = 我每天早上都和邻居们打招呼。

一般过去时:
She exchanged her old car for a new one. = 她用旧车换了一辆新车。
They exchanged pleasantries before getting down to business. = 他们寒暄一番后才开始谈正事。


现在完成时:
We have exchanged many letters over the years. = 多年来我们互通了很多信件。
They have exchanged ideas on the project. = 他们就该项目交换了意见。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had exchanged views on the topic. = 会议之前，他们就这个话题交换了意见。
She had already exchanged her currency before arriving in the country. = 她在到达该国之前已经兑换了货币。


将来时:
We will exchange contact information after the event. = 活动结束后我们将交换联系方式。
They will exchange gifts at the party. = 他们将在派对上交换礼物。


虚拟语气:
If I had a dollar, I would exchange it for a euro. = 如果我有一美元，我会把它兑换成欧元。
I wish I could exchange my current job for a better one. = 我希望我能换一份更好的工作。


被动语态:
The currency was exchanged at the bank. = 货币是在银行兑换的。
The views were exchanged politely during the debate. = 在辩论中，观点被礼貌地交换。


疑问句:
Can we exchange seats? = 我们能交换座位吗？
Do you want to exchange numbers? = 你想交换号码吗？


否定句:
I did not exchange words with him. = 我没有和他说话。
They didn't exchange gifts this year. = 他们今年没有交换礼物。
