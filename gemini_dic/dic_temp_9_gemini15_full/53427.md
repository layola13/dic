disappearing: /ˌdɪsəˈpɪəriŋ/ | adj. | 消失的；逐渐减少的 |  Describes something that is in the process of vanishing or becoming less noticeable.  | Example: The disappearing act of the magician amazed the audience. 魔术师的消失表演让观众惊叹不已。 The disappearing glaciers are a clear sign of climate change. 消失的冰川是气候变化的明显迹象。| 近义词：vanishing, fading, diminishing | 反义词：appearing, emerging, increasing | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ice is disappearing rapidly. = 冰正在迅速消失。
His confidence is disappearing. = 他的信心正在消失。

一般过去时:
The ship disappeared into the fog. = 船消失在雾中。
The symptoms disappeared after a few days. = 几天后症状消失了。

现在完成时:
Many species have disappeared from the wild. = 许多物种已从野外消失。
The problem has disappeared since we implemented the solution. = 自从我们实施了解决方案以来，问题已经消失了。

过去完成时:
The evidence had disappeared before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前证据已经消失了。
He realized that his wallet had disappeared. = 他意识到他的钱包不见了。

将来时:
The old town will disappear soon if the project goes ahead. = 如果项目继续进行，这个老城镇很快就会消失。
The symptoms will disappear gradually with time. = 随着时间的推移，症状会逐渐消失。

虚拟语气:
If the ice cap disappeared, sea levels would rise drastically. = 如果冰盖消失，海平面将急剧上升。
I wish my worries would disappear. = 我希望我的担忧能消失。

被动语态:
The clues were gradually disappearing. = 线索逐渐消失。
The forest is being disappearing at an alarming rate. = 森林正以惊人的速度消失。

疑问句:
Is the pain disappearing? = 疼痛正在消失吗？
Are the problems disappearing? = 问题正在消失吗？

否定句:
The evidence didn't disappear. = 证据没有消失。
The symptoms haven't disappeared yet. = 症状还没有消失。
