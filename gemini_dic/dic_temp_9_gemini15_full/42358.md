counterarguing:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuɪŋ/| v.| 反驳；辩驳 |  The act of presenting arguments against a previously stated position or claim.  It involves offering reasons or evidence to refute or challenge the opposing viewpoint. |  例句：The lawyer spent considerable time counterarguing the prosecution's case.  律师花了相当多的时间反驳检方的论点。
The team's strategy involved counterarguing every point made by their opponents.  该团队的策略包括反驳对手提出的每一个论点。|近义词：refuting, contradicting, disputing, rebutting |反义词：agreeing, conceding, supporting, confirming |动词
