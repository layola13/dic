resentments:/rɪˈzɛntmənts/| n.|怨恨；不满|Feelings of anger or displeasure caused by a perceived wrong or injustice.|例句：He harbored deep resentments towards his former employer. 他对他以前的雇主怀有深深的怨恨。

例句：Her resentments stemmed from years of unfair treatment. 她的怨恨源于多年的不公平待遇。


近义词：anger, bitterness, indignation, animosity, displeasure|反义词：acceptance, forgiveness, gratitude, appreciation|词性:n.
