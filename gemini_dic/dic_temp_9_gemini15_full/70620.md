films:/fɪlmz/ | n. |电影；胶片 |Motion pictures; a series of related images projected onto a screen to create the illusion of movement.  Films can be fictional stories, documentaries, or experimental works. |例句：I love watching action films. 我喜欢看动作片。

例句：We watched a classic film last night. 昨晚我们看了一部经典电影。


近义词：movies, motion pictures | 反义词：无
