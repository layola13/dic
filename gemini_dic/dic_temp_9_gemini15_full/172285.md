put-downs:/ˈpʊtdaʊnz/| n.|贬低；打击；讽刺的话|Insulting or belittling remarks intended to humiliate or diminish someone.|例句：He endured a series of put-downs from his colleagues. 他忍受了一系列来自同事的贬低。|例句：Her constant put-downs made him feel inadequate. 她不断的贬低让他感觉自己力不从心。|近义词：insults, criticisms, sneers, jabs, slurs|反义词：compliments, praise, encouragement
