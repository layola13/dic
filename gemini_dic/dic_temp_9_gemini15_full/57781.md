dreariness:/ˈdrɪrɪnəs/| n. |沉闷；阴郁；乏味 |A state or feeling of being dreary; dullness; gloominess.  It often refers to a lack of cheerfulness or excitement, and can describe weather, atmosphere, or a person's mood. |例句：The dreariness of the long winter evenings weighed heavily on her.  漫长的冬夜的沉闷让她感到非常压抑。

The dreariness of the landscape was emphasized by the grey sky.  灰色的天空更突显了景色的沉闷。

近义词：gloom, dullness, monotony, bleakness, despondency |反义词：cheerfulness, brightness, gaiety, liveliness
