complementer:/ˈkɒmplɪmentə(r)/, /ˈkɑːmplɪmentər/ | n. | 补充者；互补物；（基因）互补因子 | A complementer is something that completes or makes something else whole.  In genetics, it refers to a gene that can compensate for the loss of function of another gene. | Example: The new software acts as a complementer to the existing system, enhancing its capabilities.  新的软件作为现有系统的补充，增强了其功能。  |近义词：supplementary, enhancer,  complement |反义词：inhibitor, antagonist |名词
