paronomastic: /ˌpærənəˈmæstɪk/ | adj. | 双关语的；谐音的 |  Employing or characterized by paronomasia (the use of words alike in sound but different in meaning).  It involves a play on words that sound alike but have different meanings, often for humorous or rhetorical effect.


例句：

The comedian's act was full of paronomastic jokes.  = 这个喜剧演员的表演充满了双关语笑话。

His speech was filled with clever paronomastic puns. = 他的演讲充满了巧妙的谐音双关。


近义词：punny,  alliterative (if the similarity is in the initial sounds)


反义词：literal, unambiguous


词性：形容词
