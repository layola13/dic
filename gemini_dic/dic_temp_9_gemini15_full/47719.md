decadency:/ˈdɛkədənsi/| n. |颓废；衰落；堕落 |The state of being decadent; moral or cultural decline.  It often refers to a period of decline in art, literature, or society marked by excessive luxury and moral corruption. |例句：The decadence of the Roman Empire led to its eventual downfall. 罗马帝国的颓废导致了它最终的灭亡。
