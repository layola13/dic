compressible:/kəmˈprɛsəbl/ | adj. | 可压缩的，可压的 | Capable of being compressed; readily yielding to pressure.  | Example: Gases are generally more compressible than solids. 气体通常比固体更容易压缩。  The sponge is highly compressible. 这块海绵极易压缩。|近义词：compactible, squeezable |反义词：incompressible, rigid
