pelagic:/pəˈlændʒɪk/| adj.|远洋的；深海的；远海的 |Relating to the open ocean, far from the shore.  Pelagic organisms are those that live in the water column of the open ocean, not near the bottom or shore. | 例句：Many pelagic fish migrate long distances. 许多远洋鱼类迁徙很远的距离。

例句：The study focused on the pelagic ecosystem of the Pacific Ocean. 这项研究集中在太平洋的远洋生态系统上。


近义词：oceanic, offshore
反义词：neritic, littoral, coastal


词性：形容词
