overdiscourages: /ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒɪz/ | v. |过度劝退，极力劝阻 | To discourage excessively or to a degree that is counterproductive.  It implies a level of discouragement that goes beyond what is necessary or helpful, potentially leading to unintended negative consequences. | Example: The excessively harsh penalties overdiscouraged students from participating in the debate.  过度严厉的惩罚让学生们不敢参加辩论。 | 近义词:  deter strongly, excessively dissuade,  intimidate | 反义词: encourage, inspire, motivate
