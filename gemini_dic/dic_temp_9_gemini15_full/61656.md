emissives:/ɪˈmɪsɪvz/| adj.| 发射的；辐射的 |  Relating to or characterized by the emission of something, especially light, heat, or radiation.  The term often describes substances or processes that release energy or particles. | Example:  The emissive properties of the material were tested under various conditions.  该材料的发射特性在各种条件下进行了测试。The sun is a highly emissive body. 太阳是一个高辐射体。|近义词：radiant, emitting, radiative | 反义词：absorptive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat. = 太阳发出光和热。
This lamp emits a warm glow. = 这盏灯发出温暖的光芒。


一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large plume of ash. = 火山喷发出大量的火山灰。
The factory emitted harmful pollutants into the air. = 工厂向空气中排放有害污染物。


现在完成时:
The star has emitted a powerful burst of energy. = 这颗恒星已经发出强大的能量爆发。
The device has emitted a series of signals. = 该设备已经发出了一系列信号。


过去完成时:
The reactor had emitted dangerous levels of radiation before the shutdown. = 在关闭之前，反应堆已经释放出危险级别的辐射。
The experiment had emitted unexpected results. = 实验产生了意想不到的结果。


将来时:
The rocket will emit a trail of fire upon launch. = 火箭发射时会留下火焰轨迹。
The sensor will emit a warning signal if the temperature rises too high. = 如果温度过高，传感器将发出警告信号。


虚拟语气:
If the object emitted more light, we could see it better. = 如果物体发出更多光，我们就能看得更清楚。
I wish the machine emitted less noise. = 我希望机器发出更少的噪音。


被动语态:
A significant amount of heat was emitted by the engine. = 发动机释放出大量的热量。
A warning signal was emitted by the security system. = 安全系统发出了警告信号。


疑问句:
What kind of radiation is emitted by this substance? =这种物质会发射什么类型的辐射？
Does this material emit any harmful substances? =这种材料会释放任何有害物质吗？


否定句:
The device did not emit any detectable signals. = 该设备没有发出任何可检测的信号。
The star does not emit visible light. = 这颗恒星不发出可见光。
