preremitted:/ˌpriːrɪˈmɪtɪd/| adj.| 预先汇款的；预付的 |Pre-remitted refers to something that has been sent or paid in advance.  It usually applies to funds or payments sent before a specific event or transaction.  |例句：The supplier confirmed that the preremitted funds were received. 供应商确认已收到预先汇款。

The preremitted payment allowed us to start the project immediately. 预付的款项使我们能够立即启动项目。|近义词：prepaid, advanced, sent in advance |反义词：postpaid, unpaid, outstanding

词源:
The word "preremitted" is formed from the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the verb "remit" (meaning "to send or transmit (money)").  The past participle "-ed" indicates a completed action.  Therefore, "preremitted" means "sent or transmitted (money) before."
