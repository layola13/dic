In theological contexts, it often relates to the debate about whether humans are inherently sinful or capable of sin. | Example: The theologian argued that human peccability is a fundamental aspect of our nature.  神学家认为人的易犯错性是人性的基本方面。|近义词：fallibility, imperfection, sinfulness |反义词：infallibility, impeccability
