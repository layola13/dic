flowering:/ˈflaʊərɪŋ/ | adj. |开花的；繁荣的 |  Describes something that is in the process of producing flowers, or refers to a period of great success or prosperity.  | Example: The flowering cherry trees are a beautiful sight.  盛开的樱花树是一道美丽的风景。  The flowering of the Renaissance brought about great advancements in art and science. 文艺复兴的繁荣带来了艺术和科学的巨大进步。 |近义词：blooming, blossoming, flourishing | 反义词：wilting, decaying


USAGE->

The garden is flowering beautifully this year. = 今年花园里的花开得非常漂亮。
The business is flowering, thanks to the new marketing strategy. = 多亏了新的营销策略，生意兴隆。
