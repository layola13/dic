expatiates:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪts/ | v. |详述；详细解释；冗长地谈论 | To speak or write at length or in detail.  |例句：He expatiated on the virtues of his new invention. 他详细阐述了他新发明的优点。


例句：The professor expatiated on the intricacies of quantum physics for an hour. 教授花了整整一个小时详细讲解量子物理的复杂性。


近义词：elaborate, enlarge upon, dwell on, discourse on | 反义词：summarize, abridge, condense


词性：动词
