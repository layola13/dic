retestifies:/ˌriːˈtɛstɪfaɪz/| v.|再次作证，重新作证 |To testify again; to give evidence again in a court of law.  |例句：The witness retestified to clarify some inconsistencies in his earlier statement. 目击者再次作证以澄清其先前证词中的一些不一致之处。  The defendant retestified after new evidence emerged.  在出现新的证据后，被告再次作证。|近义词：testify again, re-testify|反义词：None specifically, but implies ceasing to testify.
