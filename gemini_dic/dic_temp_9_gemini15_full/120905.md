miscaptioning:/ˌmɪskæpʃənɪŋ/ | v. |  误加标题；错误地加字幕 | The act of providing an incorrect or inappropriate caption for something, such as an image, video, or other media. This could involve using inaccurate information, misleading text, or a caption that is simply irrelevant.  | Example: The miscaptioning of the photograph led to widespread confusion.  误加标题的照片导致了广泛的混乱。  | 近义词: mislabeling, mistitling, wrongly captioning | 反义词: accurately captioning, correctly labeling
