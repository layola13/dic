overbuy:/ˌoʊvərˈbaɪ/ | vt. |过度购买，买得过多 | To buy more of something than is needed or can be used.  |例句：He overbought on groceries this week and now has way too much food.  他这周买菜买多了，现在食物太多了。  |例句：The company overbought inventory last quarter, leading to significant losses. 上个季度公司库存积压，导致重大损失。|近义词：overstock, hoard,  accumulate excessively |反义词：underbuy, economize
