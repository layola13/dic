knop:/nɒp/| n.|旋钮；球形把手；花蕾|A knob is a round handle or button that you turn to operate a machine or piece of equipment.  It can also refer to a rounded projection or a bud on a plant.|例句：He turned the knob to adjust the volume. 他转动旋钮来调节音量。

The gardener carefully pruned the rose knobs. 园丁仔细修剪了玫瑰花蕾。|近义词：handle, button, boss, bud|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., if referring to a handle, the opposite might be a lever).
