concavity's:/kɒnˈkævɪtiz/| n. |凹度；凹面；凹性|The state or quality of being concave; a concave surface or part.  It refers to the inward curve of a surface.  The possessive 's indicates that it is the concavity belonging to something. |例句：The lens's concavity focuses the light.  透镜的凹度聚焦光线。 The bowl's concavity held the water. 碗的凹面盛着水。|近义词：curvature, hollowness |反义词：convexity
