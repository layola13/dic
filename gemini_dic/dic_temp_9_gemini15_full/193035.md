secret: /ˈsiːkrɪt/ | n. adj. | 秘密；机密的；隐秘的 | Something kept private or unknown to others; a piece of information that is intended to be kept private.  |例句：She confided her secret to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友吐露了她的秘密。

例句：The magician kept his secret techniques hidden from the audience. 魔术师将他的秘密技巧隐藏在观众面前。


近义词：mystery, enigma, confidential, private | 反义词：open, public, revealed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keeps a secret diary. = 他写秘密日记。
The company protects its secret formula. = 公司保护它的秘密配方。


一般过去时:
She kept her secret for many years. = 她保守秘密多年。
They shared a secret between them. = 他们之间分享了一个秘密。


现在完成时:
I have kept this secret for a long time. = 我保守这个秘密很久了。
She has revealed her secret to everyone. = 她已经向所有人透露了她的秘密。


过去完成时:
He had kept the secret until the day he died. = 他一直保守这个秘密直到他去世的那一天。
They had shared a secret before they became friends. = 他们成为朋友之前就分享过一个秘密。


将来时:
I will keep your secret safe. = 我会保守你的秘密。
She will reveal the secret tomorrow. = 她明天将揭露这个秘密。


虚拟语气:
If I knew her secret, I wouldn't tell anyone. = 如果我知道她的秘密，我不会告诉任何人。
I wish I knew the secret to his success. = 我真希望知道他成功的秘诀。


被动语态:
The secret was revealed by a journalist. = 这个秘密被一名记者揭露了。
The secret is being kept from the public. = 这个秘密正对公众保密。


疑问句:
What's your secret to success? = 你成功的秘诀是什么？
Is it a secret? = 这是秘密吗？


否定句:
It's not a secret anymore. = 这不再是秘密了。
I don't have any secrets from you. = 我对你没有任何秘密。
