draughtsboards:/ˈdrɑːftsbɔːrdz/ | n. |跳棋盘|A draughtsboard is a board used to play the game of draughts (also known as checkers). It is typically a checkered board with 64 squares, arranged in an 8x8 grid, alternating in color (usually black and white or dark and light).| The children were engrossed in a game of draughts on the draughtsboard.孩子们全神贯注地在一个跳棋盘上玩跳棋。|checkerboard, game board|  | n.
