remediates:/rɪˈmiːdieɪts/| vt.|改进；补救；矫正|To remedy or improve a bad situation, problem, etc.|例句：The government is trying to remediate the environmental damage caused by the factory. 政府正在努力补救工厂造成的环境破坏。

例句：The company is working to remediate the issues raised in the audit report. 公司正在努力解决审计报告中提出的问题。


近义词：rectify, correct, ameliorate, improve | 反义词：worsen, exacerbate, aggravate | 词性:动词
