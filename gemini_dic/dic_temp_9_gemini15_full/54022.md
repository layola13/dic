discursion:/dɪˈskɜːʒən/| n. |离题；漫谈；讨论；偏离正题|A digression or departure from the main subject.  A discursive discussion or essay may wander from topic to topic, lacking a clear focus. |例句：His lecture was full of fascinating discursions on unrelated topics.  他的演讲充满了关于不相干话题的引人入胜的离题讨论。

例句：The writer's discursion into the history of the region added depth to the narrative.  作者对该地区历史的离题讨论为叙事增添了深度。


近义词：digression, excursion, rambling, deviation |反义词：conciseness, focus, directness |词性：名词


词源：源自拉丁语 *discursus，意为“跑来跑去；讨论”。  It's related to the verb "discursive," meaning tending to depart from the main point.
