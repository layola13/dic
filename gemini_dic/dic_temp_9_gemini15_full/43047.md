cowing:/ˈkaʊɪŋ/| v.|使胆怯；威吓；使屈服|To make (someone) timid or fearful; to subdue or intimidate.  |例句：The bully's threats cowed the younger children.  恃强凌弱的威胁吓坏了那些小孩子。

例句：His aggressive manner cowed his opponents into silence. 他咄咄逼人的态度使对手吓得不敢吭声。


近义词：intimidate, bully, subdue, daunt, frighten


反义词：embolden, encourage, inspire


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud noises cow the dog. = 响亮的声音吓到了狗。
Her stern gaze cows the witness. = 她严厉的眼神震慑住了证人。


一般过去时:
The storm cowed the sailors. = 暴风雨使水手们胆怯了。
His threats cowed me into submission. = 他的威胁使我屈服了。


现在完成时:
The constant criticism has cowed her. = 持续不断的批评已经让她胆怯了。
Years of bullying have cowed the child. = 多年的欺凌已经使这个孩子胆怯了。


过去完成时:
She had been cowed by her previous experiences. = 她之前的经历使她胆怯了。
The army had been cowed by the enemy's superior firepower. = 这支军队被敌人的强大火力吓破了胆。


将来时:
The new regulations will cow many small businesses. = 新规将吓退许多小型企业。
I fear that his anger will cow her into silence. = 我担心他的愤怒会让她吓得不敢说话。


虚拟语气:
If only he hadn't been cowed by the crowd, he would have spoken his mind. = 如果他没有被人群吓倒，他就会说出自己的想法。
I wish I hadn't been cowed by his threats. = 我真希望我没有被他威胁吓倒。


被动语态:
The villagers were cowed into obedience. = 村民们被吓唬得服从了。
The witness was cowed by the defendant's aggressive lawyer. = 证人被被告咄咄逼人的律师吓住了。


疑问句:
Was he cowed by the challenge? = 他被挑战吓倒了吗？
Were the workers cowed by the management's threats? = 工人们被管理层的威胁吓倒了吗？


否定句:
The soldiers were not cowed by the enemy's attack. = 士兵们没有被敌人的攻击吓倒。
She refused to be cowed by his intimidation.  她拒绝被他恐吓吓倒。


PHRASE->

cowed expression = 胆怯的表情
to cow someone into doing something = 威胁某人做某事
