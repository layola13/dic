misgrounded:/ˌmɪsˈɡraʊndɪd/| adj.|缺乏根据的；不切实际的；基础薄弱的 | lacking a sound basis; unrealistic; poorly founded.  | The theory is misgrounded in fact.  这个理论缺乏事实依据。|His ambitions are wildly misgrounded. 他的雄心壮志很不切实际。|近义词：unfounded, baseless, unrealistic, ill-founded |反义词：well-grounded, sound, realistic, justified


USAGE->
