mesmerizer: /ˈmɛzməraɪzər/ | n. | 催眠者，迷住者 | A person or thing that mesmerizes; someone or something that fascinates or hypnotizes.  | Example: The magician was a skilled mesmerizer, captivating the audience with his illusions. 催眠师是一位技艺高超的催眠大师，他的幻术迷住了观众。 | 近义词：hypnotizer, fascinator, enchanter | 反义词：disillusioner
