half-marked:/ˈhɑːf mɑːrkt/| adj.| 半标记的，部分标记的 |Partially marked; indicating something is only partly complete or correct.  It often implies incompleteness or a need for further action.|例句：The assignment was half-marked, so I knew I needed to complete the remaining sections.  作业只批改了一半，所以我意识到需要完成剩下的部分。


The document was half-marked with red pen, suggesting corrections were needed. 文件用红笔做了部分标记，表明需要修改。


近义词：partially marked, incompletely marked, unfinished|反义词：fully marked, completely marked
