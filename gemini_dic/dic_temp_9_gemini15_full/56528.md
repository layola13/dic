domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/| n.|住所，户籍所在地，居住地|The place where a person has their permanent home;  a person's legal residence.|例句：His domicile is in London, but he spends much of his time in Paris.他的住所是伦敦，但他大部分时间都在巴黎度过。|近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, home|反义词：nomadic life, transient stay


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His domicile is in the countryside. = 他的住所位于乡下。
Where is your official domicile registered? = 你的官方户籍登记在哪里？


一般过去时:
Her domicile was in a small village. = 她以前住在一个小村庄里。
What was his domicile before he moved here? = 他搬来这里之前住在哪里？


现在完成时:
He has changed his domicile several times. = 他已经换过几次住所了。
She has maintained her domicile in this city for over twenty years. = 她在这个城市居住已超过二十年了。


过去完成时:
They had established their domicile in the city before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，他们已在城市里安家落户。
Where had he been domiciled before his emigration? = 他移民之前住在哪里？


将来时:
He will establish his domicile in New York next year. = 他明年将在纽约定居。
Where will they domicile after their retirement? = 他们退休后将住在哪里？


虚拟语气:
If he had a stable domicile, he would feel more secure. = 如果他有一个稳定的住所，他会感觉更安全。
I wish I had a more permanent domicile. = 我希望我有一个更永久的住所。


被动语态:
Their domicile was determined by the court. = 他们的住所由法院确定。
His domicile is being challenged in the legal proceedings. = 他的住所正在法律程序中受到质疑。


疑问句:
What is your domicile? = 你的住所在哪里？
Where is the defendant's domicile located? = 被告的住所在哪里？


否定句:
He does not have a fixed domicile. = 他没有固定的住所。
She didn't declare her domicile correctly on the form. = 她没有在表格上正确声明她的住所。
