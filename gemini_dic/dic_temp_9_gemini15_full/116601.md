matrilineal: /ˌmætɹɪˈlɪniəl/ | adj. | 母系的；沿母系继承的 | Relating to or based on kinship with the mother or the female line.  Matrilineal societies trace descent and inheritance through the female line. | Example: In many matrilineal societies, property and titles are inherited through the mother's line.  在许多母系社会中，财产和头衔都是通过母系继承的。|近义词：uterine, female-line | 反义词：patrilineal
