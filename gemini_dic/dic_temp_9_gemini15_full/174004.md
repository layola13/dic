quasi-technically: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˈtɛknɪkəli/ | adj. | 准技术上地；非严格技术意义上地 |  Approximating a technical explanation or description;  not strictly or fully technical but based on a technical understanding.  It implies a simplification or generalization of a technical concept to make it more accessible or less precise. |


例句：

He quasi-technically explained the problem, omitting some of the finer details. = 他用一种近似技术性的方式解释了这个问题，省略了一些细节。

She described the process quasi-technically, focusing on the overall workflow rather than individual steps. = 她用一种准技术性的方式描述了这个过程，重点是整体工作流程而不是单个步骤。


近义词：  semi-technically, informally technically, roughly technically


反义词： technically (strictly), precisely, accurately


词性：形容词
