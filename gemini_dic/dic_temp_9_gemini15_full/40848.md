cooccurring:/ˌkoʊəˈkɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. |同时发生的；共生的|Occurring at the same time; existing together.|The study examined the cooccurring effects of stress and depression on academic performance. 这项研究考察了压力和抑郁对学业成绩的同时影响。|synonym: concurrent, simultaneous, concomitant |antonym: separate, distinct |形容词
