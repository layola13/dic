glacially:/ˈɡleɪʃəli/| adj.|冰川的；缓慢的；冰冷的|extremely slowly; in a manner resembling a glacier's movement; coldly and unfeelingly.  |例句：The glacier moved glacially across the valley. 冰川缓慢地穿过山谷。

例句：He responded to her request glacially, offering no warmth or enthusiasm. 他冷漠地回应了她的请求，没有表现出任何热情。


近义词：slowly, frigidly, coldly, icily

反义词：quickly, rapidly, warmly, enthusiastically

词性：形容词
