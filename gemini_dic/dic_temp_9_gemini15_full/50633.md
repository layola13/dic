derider:/daɪˈraɪdər/| n. |嘲笑者，讥讽者 |One who derides; a mocker.  A derider often expresses contempt or ridicule through words or actions.  |例句：The comedian's jokes were met with laughter from some and derision from others, the deriders shaking their heads in disapproval.  一些人对这位喜剧演员的笑话报以笑声，而另一些人则报以嘲笑，嘲笑者们不赞成地摇了摇头。|近义词：mocker, scoffer, jeerer, critic |反义词：supporter, admirer, praiser
