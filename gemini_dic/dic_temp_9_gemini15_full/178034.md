recladding:/ˌriːˈklædɪŋ/| n.|重新包层，翻新外墙|The process of replacing the exterior cladding of a building.  It often involves removing old materials and installing new ones to improve aesthetics, energy efficiency, or structural integrity.|例句：The building is undergoing extensive recladding to improve its energy efficiency. 这座建筑正在进行大规模的重新包层以提高其能源效率。

The old hospital needed recladding to address water damage. 这家旧医院需要重新包层以解决漏水问题。|近义词：refacing, resurfacing, renovation|反义词：None specifically.  The opposite would be to leave the cladding as it is.|名词
