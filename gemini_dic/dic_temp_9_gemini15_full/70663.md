fimbria:/ˈfɪmbrɪə/| n.| 缘毛；穗；（解剖学） fimbriae (复数)| A fringe, border, or edge, often hair-like or thread-like.  In anatomy, it refers to a fringe-like structure, particularly those found in the fallopian tubes and the brain.| 例句：The fimbriae of the fallopian tube catch the released egg.  输卵管的缘毛抓住释放的卵子。

The brain's fimbria is a white matter tract connecting the hippocampus to other brain regions.  大脑的缘毛是一束白质纤维束，连接着海马体和其他大脑区域。| 近义词：fringe, border, tassel, filament| 反义词：smooth, plain
