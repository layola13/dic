lexicographist: /ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːɡrəfɪst/ | n. | 词典编纂者 | A lexicographist is a person who compiles dictionaries. They are responsible for researching, selecting, defining, and organizing words for inclusion in a dictionary.  Lexicographers also determine pronunciation, etymology, and usage examples for each entry.  Their work often involves collaboration with other specialists, including linguists and editors. | The chief lexicographist spent years compiling the new dictionary.  首席词典编纂者花了数年时间编纂这部新词典。|  compiler, editor |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 anti-lexicographer, 但该词不存在) | 名词
