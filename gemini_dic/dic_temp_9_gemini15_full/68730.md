farmable:/ˈfɑːrməbəl/| adj.|可耕种的，适宜耕作的|Suitable for farming or cultivation.  The land is farmable after the irrigation project.|例句：This land is farmable, with rich soil and ample water. 这块地适合耕种，土壤肥沃，水源充足。

例句：The farmable area has been significantly expanded due to improved irrigation techniques.  由于灌溉技术的改进，可耕种面积已大幅扩大。

近义词：cultivable, arable, tillable

反义词：uncultivable, barren, infertile

词性：形容词
