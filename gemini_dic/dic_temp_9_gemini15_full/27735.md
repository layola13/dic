canines:/ˈkeɪnaɪnz/| n. |犬科动物；犬齿|Canines are mammals belonging to the family Canidae, which includes dogs, wolves, foxes, and jackals.  They are characterized by their sharp teeth and often pack hunting behavior.|例句：Dogs, wolves, and foxes are all examples of canines.  犬类、狼和狐狸都是犬科动物的例子。  Many canines have a keen sense of smell. 许多犬科动物具有敏锐的嗅觉。|近义词：dogs (in informal contexts), doglike animals|反义词：felines (cats)
