quit:/kwɪt/| v. |停止；离开；辞职；放弃|To stop doing something; to leave a job or place; to give up.|He quit smoking last year. 他去年戒了烟。|She quit her job to travel the world. 她辞掉工作去环游世界。|近义词：stop, cease, resign, abandon|反义词：continue, start, begin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quits his job every few months. 他每隔几个月就辞职。
I quit smoking three years ago. 我三年前戒烟了。

一般过去时:
She quit her job unexpectedly. 她意外地辞掉了工作。
He quit the team after the losing streak.  在经历了一系列失败后，他退出了队伍。

现在完成时:
I have quit drinking. 我已经戒酒了。
She has quit several jobs in the past year. 过去一年她已经辞了几份工作了。

过去完成时:
He had quit his job before we met.在我们见面之前他已经辞职了。
She had quit smoking before she got pregnant. 她怀孕前就已经戒烟了。

将来时:
I will quit my job next month. 我下个月将辞职。
He will quit smoking after his birthday. 他生日过后将戒烟。

虚拟语气:
If I quit my job, I would travel the world. 如果我辞职，我会环游世界。
I wish I had quit smoking years ago. 我真希望几年前就戒烟了。

被动语态: (quit本身很少用被动语态)
The project was quit due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，项目被终止了。(这里quit指项目被终止，而非主动放弃)
The meeting was quit early. 会议提前结束了。(这里quit指会议被提前结束，而非主动放弃)


疑问句:
Will you quit your job? 你会辞职吗？
Did he quit the team? 他退队了吗？

否定句:
I won't quit my job. 我不会辞职。
He didn't quit smoking. 他没有戒烟。


PHRASE->
quit smoking: 戒烟
quit drinking: 戒酒
quit your job: 辞职
quit cold turkey:  突然完全戒掉（习惯或瘾）
quit playing games: 不要再玩游戏了
