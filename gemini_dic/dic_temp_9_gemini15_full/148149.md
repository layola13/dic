oversentimental: /ˌoʊvərˌsentɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 过于感性的；多愁善感的 |

英文解析:  excessively sentimental; characterized by or showing excessive sentimentality.  It implies an overabundance of emotion, often to the point of being unrealistic or excessive.

例句:

Her oversentimental reaction to the movie surprised everyone.  她的过度感性的电影反应让每个人都感到惊讶。

He wrote an oversentimental poem about his lost love. 他写了一首关于他失去的爱人的过于感伤的诗。


近义词: mawkish, sentimental, mushy, emotional, weepy

反义词: unemotional, apathetic, stoic, hard-hearted, indifferent


词源:  The word comes from the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively) and "sentimental," which itself derives from the French word "sentimental" (meaning feeling, sensitive), ultimately from the Latin "sentire" (to feel).
