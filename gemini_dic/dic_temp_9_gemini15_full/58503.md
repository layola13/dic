duelled:/ˈduːəld/ | v. |决斗；争论 | To fight a duel; to engage in a prolonged and intense argument or conflict. |例句：The two men duelled at dawn.  = 两个男人在黎明时分决斗。

The rivals duelled verbally for hours. = 竞争对手们进行了数小时的言语争斗。

近义词：fought, battled, contended, argued, clashed | 反义词：agreed, cooperated, compromised
