sarcomatous:/ˌsɑːrkəˈmeɪtəs/| adj.| 肉瘤的，肉瘤性的 | Relating to or characteristic of a sarcoma.  Sarcomas are cancers that arise from connective tissue, such as bone, cartilage, fat, muscle, and blood vessels. | The patient presented with a sarcomatous mass in the leg.  这位病人腿部出现了一个肉瘤肿块。| cancerous, malignant | benign
