probation-officers:/proʊˈbeɪʃən ˈɑːfɪsər/| n. |缓刑官，假释官|A probation officer is a law enforcement official who supervises individuals who have been placed on probation or parole.  They work to ensure that these individuals comply with the terms of their release and to help them reintegrate into society.| 例句：The probation officer regularly checks in with the offender to monitor their progress. 缓刑官定期与罪犯联系以监督他们的进展。  The probation officer helped the young man find a job and a place to live. 缓刑官帮助这个年轻人找到工作和住所。|近义词：parole officer, probation supervisor|反义词：judge (in the context of final sentencing)|词性:名词
