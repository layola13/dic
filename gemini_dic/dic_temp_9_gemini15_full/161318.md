postponence:/pəˈspoʊnmənt/ | n. | 推迟；延期 | The act of postponing something to a later time; delay.  Postponence of the meeting was necessary due to unforeseen circumstances.  会议因不可预见的情况而不得不推迟。 | 近义词：delay, deferment, adjournment | 反义词：acceleration, hastening | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The postponement of the game is causing a lot of disappointment. = 比赛的推迟引起了很多失望。
Postponence of the project is under consideration. = 项目的推迟正在考虑中。


一般过去时:
The postponement of the wedding was announced yesterday. = 婚礼的推迟昨天宣布了。
The sudden postponement of the trip surprised everyone. = 旅行的突然推迟让每个人都感到惊讶。


现在完成时:
There have been several postponements of the concert. = 音乐会已经推迟了好几次了。
The repeated postponements have frustrated the fans. = 反复的推迟让粉丝们感到沮丧。


过去完成时:
The announcement of the postponement had already been made before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，推迟的通知已经发布了。
They had anticipated the postponement, so they weren't surprised. = 他们预料到了推迟，所以并不感到惊讶。


将来时:
Further postponement of the deadline is unlikely. = 截止日期进一步推迟的可能性不大。
The company will announce any postponement of the product launch. = 公司将宣布产品发布的任何推迟。


虚拟语气:
If there were a postponement, we would be notified. = 如果有推迟，我们会得到通知。
I wish there hadn't been a postponement of the festival. = 我希望节日没有推迟。


被动语态:
The meeting was postponed due to the weather. = 会议由于天气原因被推迟了。
The decision to postpone the launch was made by the board. = 推迟发布的决定是由董事会做出的。


疑问句:
Is there any chance of a postponement? = 有可能推迟吗？
What is the reason for the postponement? = 推迟的原因是什么？


否定句:
There will be no postponement of the exam. = 考试不会推迟。
The company did not announce any postponement of its plans. = 公司没有宣布其计划有任何推迟。
