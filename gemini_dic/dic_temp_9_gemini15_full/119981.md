millesimal:/ˈmɪlɪsɪməl/| adj. |千分之一的；极微小的| Relating to or denoting a thousandth part.  The term is often used in contexts involving very small quantities or proportions, particularly in measurements or calculations. |Example:  A millesimal error in the calculation led to a significant discrepancy in the final result.  千分之一的计算误差导致最终结果出现重大差异。|近义词：minute, infinitesimal, trifling |反义词：massive, enormous, significant
