gemarist:/ˈdʒɛmərist/ | n. |宝石学家，鉴定师 | A gemarist is an expert in gemology, the science of gems. They study, identify, and evaluate precious stones.  |例句：The gemarist carefully examined the diamond under a microscope. 宝石学家在显微镜下仔细检查了钻石。|近义词：gemologist, gem expert |反义词：无
