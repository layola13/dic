pilgriming:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪŋ/ | n. |朝圣；朝圣之旅 |The act of making a pilgrimage; a journey to a sacred place.  |例句：Their pilgrimage to Mecca was a deeply spiritual experience. 他们的麦加朝圣是一次非常精神上的体验。|例句：The annual pilgrimage to the shrine attracts thousands of visitors.  每年到神社的朝圣吸引了成千上万的游客。|近义词：pilgrimage, journey, travel |反义词：staying home, remaining stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pilgriming to Santiago de Compostela. = 他正在去圣地亚哥德孔波斯特拉朝圣。
They are pilgriming across the country. = 他们在全国各地朝圣。


一般过去时:
She pilgrimed to Lourdes last year. = 她去年去卢尔德朝圣。
We pilgrimed to the holy mountain. = 我们去圣山朝圣了。


现在完成时:
He has pilgrimed to many sacred sites. = 他去过许多圣地朝圣。
They have been pilgriming for months. = 他们朝圣了好几个月了。


过去完成时:
She had pilgrimed to Jerusalem before. = 她以前去过耶路撒冷朝圣。
They had completed their pilgrimage before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经完成了朝圣之旅。


将来时:
We will be pilgriming to Rome next spring. = 我们明年春天将去罗马朝圣。
She will pilgrimage to the Ganges River. = 她将去恒河朝圣。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would pilgrimage to Tibet. = 如果我有时间，我会去西藏朝圣。
I wish I could pilgrimage to the Himalayas. = 我真希望我能去喜马拉雅山朝圣。


被动语态:
The pilgrimage was completed successfully. = 朝圣之旅圆满完成。
The route of the pilgrimage is well-marked. = 朝圣路线标示清晰。


疑问句:
Are you pilgriming this year? = 你今年要去朝圣吗？
Where are they pilgriming to? = 他们去哪里朝圣？


否定句:
He is not pilgriming this year. = 他今年不去朝圣。
They did not pilgrimage last year. = 他们去年没有去朝圣。


PHRASE->
go on a pilgrimage = 进行朝圣
make a pilgrimage = 进行朝圣
pilgrimage to = 朝圣到...
religious pilgrimage = 宗教朝圣
a pilgrimage of faith = 信仰之旅
