serang:/səˈræŋ/| n.|船长；领班；头目|A serang is a senior crew member on a ship, especially a Malay or Indonesian ship, who is in charge of a group of sailors or a particular task.  The term often implies a position of leadership and responsibility, though not necessarily the highest rank on the vessel.|例句：The serang skillfully navigated the small fishing boat through the treacherous reefs.  船长熟练地驾驶着这条小渔船驶过危险的暗礁。|近义词：foreman, captain, supervisor, boss|反义词：subordinate, crewman
