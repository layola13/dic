culled:/kʌld/| vt.|挑选；剔除；精选|To select from a large quantity; to eliminate undesirable elements.|The editor culled the best photos for the magazine. 编辑为杂志挑选了最好的照片。  The farmer culled the weakest sheep from the flock. 农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的羊。|select, choose, pick, eliminate, weed out|retain, keep, preserve|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He culls the best apples from the harvest. 他从收成中挑选最好的苹果。
She culls information for her research paper. 她为她的研究论文收集信息。


一般过去时:
They culled the damaged crops from the field. 他们从田里剔除了受损的庄稼。
The museum culled its collection of outdated artifacts. 博物馆淘汰了其过时的文物收藏。


现在完成时:
The gardener has culled the weeds from the flower bed. 园丁已经从花坛里除掉了杂草。
The company has culled its workforce to reduce costs. 公司已经裁员以降低成本。


过去完成时:
The editor had culled the articles before submitting the manuscript. 编辑在提交稿件之前已经挑选了文章。
The farmer had culled the diseased chickens before the outbreak spread. 农民在疫情蔓延之前已经剔除了患病的鸡。


将来时:
The librarian will cull outdated books from the shelves. 图书管理员将从书架上剔除过时的书籍。
They will cull the applicants based on their qualifications. 他们将根据申请人的资格进行筛选。


虚拟语气:
If we culled the weak links, the team would perform better. 如果我们剔除薄弱环节，团队的表现就会更好。
I wish they had culled the irrelevant details from the report. 我希望他们从报告中剔除无关紧要的细节。


被动语态:
The weak plants were culled from the greenhouse. 弱小的植物从温室里被剔除了。
The outdated information was culled from the database. 过时的信息已从数据库中删除。


疑问句:
Will the company cull any more employees this year? 今年公司还会裁员吗？
Have they culled the inferior products from the market? 他们是否已将劣质产品从市场上剔除？


否定句:
They did not cull the unhealthy animals from the herd. 他们没有从兽群中剔除不健康的动物。
The editor did not cull any of the submitted articles. 编辑没有剔除任何提交的文章。


PHRASE->
