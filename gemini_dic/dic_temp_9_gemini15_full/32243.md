chickens:/ˈtʃɪkɪnz/| n.|小鸡；鸡肉|Young domestic fowls, especially when young; the meat of these fowls.|例句：The chickens are pecking at the grain.  小鸡在啄食谷物。

例句：We had roast chicken for dinner.  我们晚餐吃烤鸡。


近义词：poultry, fowl|反义词：None|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chickens lay eggs every day.  鸡每天都下蛋。
The farmer feeds the chickens twice a day.  农民每天喂鸡两次。


一般过去时:
The chickens escaped from the coop last night.  鸡昨晚从鸡舍逃跑了。
We ate fried chickens yesterday.  我们昨天吃了炸鸡。


现在完成时:
The chickens have laid a lot of eggs this month.  这个月鸡下了很多蛋。
We have eaten chicken many times this week. 我们这周已经吃了很多次鸡肉了。


过去完成时:
The chickens had already laid their eggs before the fox arrived.  狐狸来之前，鸡已经下蛋了。
We had never eaten such delicious chicken before. 我们以前从未吃过这么好吃的鸡肉。


将来时:
The chickens will be ready to eat next week.  鸡肉下周就可以吃了。
We will buy some chickens for the barbecue. 我们将买一些鸡肉来烧烤。


虚拟语气:
If we had more chickens, we could sell eggs. 如果我们有更多的鸡，我们就可以卖鸡蛋了。
I wish I had more time to care for the chickens. 我希望我有更多的时间来照顾鸡。


被动语态:
The chickens are fed by the farmer. 鸡由农民喂养。
The chicken was cooked perfectly. 鸡肉做得非常完美。


疑问句:
How many chickens do you have? 你有多少只鸡？
Are the chickens healthy? 鸡健康吗？


否定句:
The chickens didn't lay any eggs today. 鸡今天没下蛋。
We don't eat chicken very often. 我们不经常吃鸡肉。
