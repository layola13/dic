prairie:/ˈprɛəri/| n. |大草原，牧场|A large, flat area of grassland with few or no trees.  Prairies are characterized by tall grasses and wildflowers. |例句：We drove across the vast prairie, seeing nothing but grass and sky. 我们驱车穿过广阔的大草原，只看到草地和天空。|例句：The prairie was ablaze with wildflowers in the spring. 春天，草原上鲜花盛开。|近义词：grassland, plain, savanna|反义词：forest, jungle

The prairie stretches for miles. = 大草原绵延数英里。
Wildflowers bloom on the prairie in the summer. = 夏季，草原上鲜花盛开。

The prairie has changed dramatically over the years. = 多年来，草原发生了巨大的变化。

过去完成时:

将来时:
