retracks:/riːˈtræk/| vt.|重走，追溯，重新追踪|To travel back along a route previously followed; to trace back the course or origin of something. |例句：The hikers retraced their steps to find their lost compass.  徒步旅行者们沿原路返回寻找丢失的指南针。

例句：Police retraced the suspect's movements before the crime. 警方追溯了犯罪嫌疑人在犯罪之前的行动轨迹。


近义词：refollow, retrace, backtrack|反义词：advance, proceed, forge ahead|动词
