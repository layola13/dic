lump-together:/lʌmp təˈɡɛðər/| v. |合并；混为一谈|To group or combine things together without much thought or distinction.  This often implies a lack of careful consideration or categorization.|例句：They lumped all the complaints together and dealt with them as a single issue.他们把所有投诉都放在一起，作为单一问题处理。

例句：Don't lump all teenagers together; they're individuals with different personalities. 不要把所有青少年都混为一谈；他们是具有不同个性的个体。


近义词：combine, amalgamate, consolidate, aggregate, group, blend

反义词：separate, differentiate, distinguish, categorize, classify

词性:动词
