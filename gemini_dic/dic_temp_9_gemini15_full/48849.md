deforester:/ˌdiːfɒˈrɛstə(r)/| n. |毁林者；滥伐森林者|A person who destroys forests, often through illegal logging or unsustainable practices.  Deforesters are often driven by profit, leading to widespread environmental damage.|例句：The deforesters were caught red-handed, their trucks laden with illegally harvested timber.  毁林者被当场抓住，他们的卡车装满了非法采伐的木材。|近义词：logger (if illegal), tree-cutter (if illegal and indiscriminate)|反义词：reforester, conservationist|名词
