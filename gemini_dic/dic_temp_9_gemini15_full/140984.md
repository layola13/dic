oneish: /ˈwʌnɪʃ/ | adj. |大约一；接近一；差不多一| Approximately one; close to one; roughly one.  It's an informal way of expressing a number that's slightly more or less than one. |例句：I'll be there in oneish hours. 我大约一小时后到。  It cost me oneish dollars.  它花了我大约一美元。|近义词：about one, around one, roughly one |反义词：precisely one, exactly one
