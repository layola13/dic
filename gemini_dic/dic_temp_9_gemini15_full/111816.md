logbook:/ˈlɒɡbʊk/ /ˈlɔːɡbʊk/| n. |航海日志；记录本；飞行日志|A logbook is a book in which events, details, or observations are recorded regularly.  It's often used in fields like shipping, aviation, and trucking to document important information. |The captain meticulously kept a logbook detailing every aspect of the voyage. 船长一丝不苟地记录航海日志，详细记录航程的方方面面。

The pilot updated the flight logbook after each successful landing. 飞行员每次成功着陆后都会更新飞行日志。|  diary, journal, record | (no direct antonym)
