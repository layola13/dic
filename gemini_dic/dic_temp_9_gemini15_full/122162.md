mistrusting: /ˌmɪsˈtrʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 不信任的；怀疑的 |  Characterized by or showing a lack of trust; suspicious.  | 例句：He gave her a mistrusting glance. 他给了她一个怀疑的目光。


例句：Her mistrusting nature made it difficult to form close relationships.  她不信任的性格使得她很难建立亲密的关系。


近义词：suspicious, skeptical, doubtful, wary, incredulous | 反义词：trusting, believing, confident


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is mistrusting of strangers. = 他不信任陌生人。
She is mistrusting of his motives. = 她怀疑他的动机。


一般过去时:
He was mistrusting of her promises. = 他不相信她的承诺。
They were mistrusting of the government's claims. = 他们不相信政府的说法。


现在完成时:
I have been mistrusting him ever since the incident. = 从那件事发生以来，我一直不信任他。
She has been mistrusting of his intentions for a long time. = 她很久以来就对他的意图表示怀疑。


过去完成时:
They had been mistrusting of his abilities before the project began. = 在项目开始之前，他们就不相信他的能力。
He had been mistrusting of her honesty for years. = 多年来，他对她的诚实一直持怀疑态度。


将来时:
I will be mistrusting of his advice in the future. = 将来我不会相信他的建议。
They will be mistrusting of any promises he makes. = 他们不会相信他做出的任何承诺。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would be mistrusting of that deal. = 如果我是你，我会对那笔交易持怀疑态度。
I wish I hadn't been so mistrusting of her. = 我真希望我不要那么不信任她。


被动语态:  (此词作形容词，一般不用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Are you mistrusting of my judgment? = 你不相信我的判断吗？
Is she mistrusting of his word? = 她不相信他说的话吗？


否定句:
I am not mistrusting of his intentions. = 我不怀疑他的意图。
They are not mistrusting of her capabilities. = 他们不怀疑她的能力。


PHRASE->
a mistrusting look  怀疑的眼神
a mistrusting attitude 不信任的态度
feel mistrusting 感到怀疑
be mistrusting of 对...不信任
have a mistrusting heart  有一颗不信任的心
