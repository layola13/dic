polyps:/ˈpɒlɪps/| n. |息肉；珊瑚虫 |A polyp is a small, soft-bodied animal that usually lives attached to a surface, often in colonies.  Many polyps are aquatic invertebrates, including corals and sea anemones.  They have a simple body structure with a single opening that serves as both mouth and anus. |Example: The doctor removed a polyp from his colon during the colonoscopy. 医生在结肠镜检查中切除了他结肠中的一个息肉。


Example:  Coral reefs are built by colonies of polyps secreting calcium carbonate.珊瑚礁是由分泌碳酸钙的珊瑚虫群落建造的。


近义词：tumor, growth, mass


反义词：无


词性：名词
