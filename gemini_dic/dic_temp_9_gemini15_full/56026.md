doability:/ˌduːəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可行性，可操作性 | The feasibility or practicality of something.  It refers to the degree to which something can be done or accomplished successfully. |  The doability of the project depends on securing sufficient funding.  这个项目的可行性取决于能否获得足够的资金。 The doability of this plan is questionable given the limited resources.  鉴于资源有限，这项计划的可行性值得怀疑。 | feasibility, practicality, workability | impossibility, impracticality, infeasibility | 名词
