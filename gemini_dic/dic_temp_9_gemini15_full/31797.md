checklists:/ˈtʃɛklɪsts/| n. |清单，核对表 | A checklist is a list of items to be checked or tasks to be performed. It is used to ensure that all necessary steps are taken or all required items are accounted for.  | Example:  I made a checklist for packing my suitcase to avoid forgetting anything important. 我做了一个打包行李的清单，以免忘记重要的东西。  |近义词：list, inventory | 反义词：无
