gasoline:/ˈɡæsəliːn/ (US) /ˈɡæsəliːn/ (UK)| n. |汽油|A volatile, flammable liquid made from petroleum, used as fuel in internal combustion engines.|例句：The price of gasoline has increased significantly this year.  今年汽油价格大幅上涨。|例句：My car needs gasoline; it's almost empty. 我的车需要加油了，油箱快空了。|近义词：petrol (UK), gas (US informal)|反义词：None|名词
