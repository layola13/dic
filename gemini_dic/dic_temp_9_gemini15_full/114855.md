mammilliform:/mæˈmɪlɪfɔːrm/| adj. |乳头状的，乳头形的|Having the form or shape of a nipple or small, rounded protuberance.  It refers to a structure resembling a nipple in shape and size, often with a rounded, slightly elevated surface. |Example: The surface of the fungus displayed a distinct mammilliform texture.|近义词：papillary, nipple-shaped, rounded|反义词：flat, concave
