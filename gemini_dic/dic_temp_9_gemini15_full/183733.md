resawing:/ˌriːˈsɔːɪŋ/ | v. | 再锯，重锯 | The act of sawing again, often to correct a previous cut or to further process wood.  | Example:  The carpenter was resawing the board to make it thinner. 木匠正在再次锯木板，使其更薄。  |近义词：resawing, re-sawing | 反义词：N/A
