libels:/ˈlaɪbəlz/| n.|诽谤；中伤；(对某人的)不实之词|The act of publishing a false statement that damages someone's reputation.  It is a serious offense that can lead to legal consequences. |例句：He was sued for libel after writing a false article about his competitor. 他因撰写一篇关于其竞争对手的不实文章而被起诉诽谤。|例句：The magazine was found guilty of libel for publishing false information about the celebrity. 这家杂志因刊登关于这位名人的不实信息而被判犯有诽谤罪。|近义词：slander, defamation, calumny|反义词：praise, commendation, compliment|名词
