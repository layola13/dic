passages:/ˈpæsədʒɪz/ | n. | 段落；章节；航程；通过 | A continuous piece of writing, music, or speech;  a journey or voyage; the act of passing through or across something. |例句：The passages in the book were long and complex. 这本书里的段落又长又复杂。
The ship made a safe passage through the storm. 船安全地度过了暴风雨。|近义词：section, excerpt, journey, transit |反义词：whole, entirety
