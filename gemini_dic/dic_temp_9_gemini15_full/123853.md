moralizer:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 道德说教者，说教的人 | A person who constantly moralizes or lectures others on morality.  They often focus on pointing out the flaws in others' behavior without offering constructive solutions. | Example: He became a tiresome moralizer, always lecturing everyone on their shortcomings. 他变成了一个令人厌烦的说教者，总是教训每个人他们的缺点。|近义词：preacher,lecturer,propagandist |反义词：agnostic, nihilist
