reposes:/rɪˈpoʊzɪz/ | n. | 静止；休息；安息；存放 | The act of resting or being at rest; a state of rest, tranquility, or quiet.  It can also refer to the act of placing something somewhere for safekeeping. |例句：After a long day's work, she found repose in her quiet garden.  长日工作之后，她在宁静的花园里找到了安宁。

例句：The museum carefully reposes its valuable artifacts in climate-controlled vaults.  博物馆小心地将珍贵的文物存放在气候控制的地下室中。|近义词：rest, sleep, peace, tranquility |反义词：activity, agitation, unrest

词源:
From Old French *repos, from repos, from Latin reponere "to replace, put back," from re- "back, again" + ponere "to put, place."  The noun sense developed from the verb meaning "to place oneself at rest."
