by-passing:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːsɪŋ/ | v. |绕过；避开；忽略 | To avoid or circumvent something, often a process or rule.  |例句：The hikers by-passed the dangerous mountain pass.  徒步旅行者绕过了危险的山路。

例句：They by-passed the usual approval process. 他们绕过了通常的审批流程。


近义词：circumventing, avoiding, evading, sidestepping | 反义词：confronting, addressing, facing


词源:  The word "bypass" is a combination of "by" (meaning "past" or "near") and "pass" (meaning "to go").  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle or gerund form.  Its origins trace back to Old English and reflect the simple act of going past something.
