nonfossils: /ˌnɒnˈfɒsɪlz/ | n. | 非化石 | Things that are not fossils;  materials or objects that have not been formed through the fossilization process.  They may be living organisms, recently deceased organisms, or other geological materials. | Example: The researchers found a mixture of fossils and nonfossils in the sedimentary layer. 研究人员在沉积层中发现化石和非化石的混合物。 | 近义词: non-fossiliferous materials, recent materials | 反义词: fossils |  词性：名词
