pseudoaristocratic:/ˌsuːdoʊˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/| adj.|伪贵族气的，冒充贵族的|Affecting or suggesting an aristocratic manner or style, but lacking genuine aristocratic qualities.  It often implies a pretentious or artificial display of refinement and superiority.|例句：His pseudoaristocratic accent and mannerisms were clearly a facade. 他伪装的贵族口音和举止显然是虚张声势。

例句：She adopted a pseudoaristocratic demeanor, hoping to impress her new acquaintances. 她装出一副贵族小姐的姿态，希望能给新认识的人留下好印象。


近义词：affected, pretentious, highfalutin, snobbish

反义词：genuine, authentic, unpretentious, humble


词源:

* **pseudo-**:  From Greek *pseudēs*, meaning "false," "lying," or "pretended."

* **aristocratic**: From Greek *aristokratia*, meaning "rule by the best," composed of *aristos* ("best") and *kratos* ("power," "rule").


用法说明：

pseudoaristocratic主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某种行为、举止、风格或人具有贵族气派但实际上并非如此，通常带有虚伪或做作的意味。  它可以用来形容人的行为举止、口音、穿着、甚至是整个生活方式。
