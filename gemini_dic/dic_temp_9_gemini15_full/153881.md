percoid:/ˈpɜːrkɔɪd/| n. |鲈形鱼；鲈形目鱼类|A percoid is a fish belonging to the order Perciformes, a very diverse group characterized by spiny fins and a generally fusiform body shape.  Many commercially important fish are percoids, including sea bass, groupers, and snappers.| Example:  Many popular game fish, such as the yellowtail and the snapper, are percoids.  许多受欢迎的游钓鱼类，例如黄尾鱼和笛鲷，都是鲈形目鱼类。|近义词：perciform (adj.)|反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with fish from other orders.|名词
