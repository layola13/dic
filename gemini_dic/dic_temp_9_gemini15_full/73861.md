formulation:/ˌfɔːrmjuˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 制定；配方；表达方式 | The act of creating or devising a plan, system, or product; a particular way of expressing something.  | The formulation of a new policy requires careful consideration.  新政策的制定需要仔细考虑。The new drug's formulation is a closely guarded secret.  这种新药的配方是一个严密保守的秘密。| preparation, development, expression | chaos, disorder | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is constantly working on new product formulations. = 公司不断研发新产品配方。
His formulation of the problem is quite clear. = 他对这个问题的表达非常清晰。


一般过去时:
They formulated a plan to increase sales. = 他们制定了一个增加销售额的计划。
She carefully formulated her response to the question. = 她仔细地组织了她对这个问题的回答。


现在完成时:
Scientists have formulated a new theory to explain the phenomenon. = 科学家们已经制定了一个新的理论来解释这种现象。
They have formulated a detailed strategy for the project. = 他们已经为这个项目制定了一个详细的策略。


过去完成时:
They had formulated a preliminary plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经制定了一个初步计划。
She had formulated her arguments carefully before the debate. = 在辩论之前，她已经仔细地构思好了自己的论点。


将来时:
They will formulate a new marketing strategy next year. = 他们明年将制定一项新的市场营销策略。
The team will formulate a response to the customer's complaint. = 团队将对客户的投诉做出回应。


虚拟语气:
If they had formulated a better plan, they would have succeeded. = 如果他们制定了一个更好的计划，他们就会成功了。
I wish they had formulated a clearer explanation. = 我希望他们能给出更清晰的解释。


被动语态:
A new policy was formulated by the government. = 新政策是由政府制定的。
The drug's formulation was carefully tested. = 该药物的配方经过了仔细的测试。


疑问句:
What is the formulation of this new product? = 这种新产品的配方是什么？
How was the plan formulated? = 这个计划是如何制定的？


否定句:
They did not formulate a clear strategy. = 他们没有制定一个清晰的策略。
The company has not yet formulated a response. = 公司尚未制定回应。
