scrupler:/ˈskruːplər/ | n. | 顾忌者；有顾虑的人 | A person who is reluctant to do something that seems wrong or dishonest.  Someone with scruples hesitates before doing something that might be morally questionable. |  He was a scrupulous man, always considering the ethical implications of his actions. 他是一个谨慎的人，总是考虑他行为的伦理意义。|近义词：conscientious objector, moralist |反义词：ruthless person, unprincipled person


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scruples over every decision. = 他对每一个决定都犹豫不决。
She scruples about telling lies. = 她对说谎很犹豫。


一般过去时:
He scruples about his actions. = 他对自己的行为有所顾忌。
