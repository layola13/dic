disected:/daɪˈsɛktɪd/ | adj. | 解剖的；被仔细分析的 | Dissected refers to something that has been cut apart, typically for examination or analysis.  It can be used literally (as in a dissected frog) or figuratively (as in a dissected argument). | Example: The dissected frog lay on the table.  解剖的青蛙躺在桌子上。The professor presented a dissected analysis of the poem. 教授对这首诗进行了细致的分析。 | 近义词:analyzed, examined, cut open | 反义词:intact, whole, unanalyzed |
