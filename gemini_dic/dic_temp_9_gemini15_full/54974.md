disproportionally:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəli/ | adv. |不成比例地，不相称地 |  To an extent that is not in proportion; unevenly or unfairly.  It describes a situation where something affects one group or aspect more significantly than others, relative to its size or importance. |

例句：

The benefits of the new policy disproportionately favored the wealthy.  新的政策的好处不成比例地偏向富人。

Minorities are disproportionately represented in prison. 少数族裔在监狱中的比例不成比例地高。


近义词：unevenly, unfairly, disproportionately, inequitably


反义词：proportionally, equitably, evenly


词性：副词
