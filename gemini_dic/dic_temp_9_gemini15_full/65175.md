ethnolinguisticses:/ˌeθnoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪksɪz/| n. |民族语言学; 民族语言学研究|The study of the relationship between language and culture.  It examines how language reflects and shapes cultural values, beliefs, and social structures. Ethnolinguistics explores topics such as language variation within and across cultures, the impact of language contact and language change on culture, and the role of language in maintaining or challenging social hierarchies.|例句：Ethnolinguisticses explores the connection between language and identity.  民族语言学探讨了语言与身份之间的联系。|例句：Research in ethnolinguisticses often involves fieldwork and ethnographic methods.  民族语言学的研究通常涉及田野工作和人种志方法。|近义词：sociolinguistics, linguistic anthropology|反义词: N/A |名词
