entablature:/ɪnˈtæblətʃər/| n.|  额枋，檐口|The horizontal, continuous structural member supported by columns or walls in classical architecture, consisting of architrave, frieze, and cornice.  It forms the upper part of a classical order.|The entablature of the Parthenon is a masterpiece of classical architecture.  帕特农神庙的额枋是古典建筑的杰作。|  lintel, cornice |  foundation, base | 名词
