phylactolema:/ˌfaɪlækˈtoʊliːmə/| n. |群体营，群体营动物|A freshwater bryozoan colony, characterized by a protective sheath or case (the phylactotheca) enclosing the zooids.  Phylactolemata are a class within the phylum Bryozoa.  They are distinguished by their statoblasts (resting buds) and their unique lophophore structure.|例句：The researchers studied the morphology of the phylactolema colony to understand its adaptation to the aquatic environment. 研究人员研究了群体营群体的形态，以了解其对水生环境的适应性。|近义词：bryozoan colony|反义词：无|名词
