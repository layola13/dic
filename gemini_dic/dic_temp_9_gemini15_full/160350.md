pootling:/ˈpuːtlɪŋ/ | v. | 慢吞吞地移动；闲逛；慢慢地做 |  To move slowly and leisurely, often in a pleasant way; to potter about.  |例句：We spent the afternoon pootling around the countryside in our old car. 我们下午开着旧车在乡下慢慢地兜风。|近义词：pottering, dawdling, ambling, meandering |反义词：rushing, hurrying, speeding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are pootling along the coast. = 他们在沿海慢悠悠地行驶。
He pootles around the garden every afternoon. = 他每天下午都在花园里四处闲逛。


一般过去时:
We pootled through the town. = 我们慢吞吞地穿过小镇。
She pootled along the riverbank. = 她沿着河岸慢慢地走着。


现在完成时:
They have been pootling around all day. = 他们一整天都在四处闲逛。
I have pootled through several chapters of the book. = 我已经慢慢地读了几章书。


过去完成时:
They had pootled along for hours before reaching their destination. = 他们在到达目的地之前已经慢悠悠地走了好几个小时。
She had pootled around the shops before finding what she needed. = 在找到她需要的东西之前，她已经在商店里闲逛了一阵子。


将来时:
We will pootle down to the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将慢悠悠地去海滩。
They will pootle through the countryside on their bikes. = 他们将骑着自行车在乡间慢慢地骑行。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would pootle around the lake. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们就会在湖边闲逛。
I wish I could pootle through the countryside today. = 我真希望今天能去乡下慢慢地走走。


被动语态:  (Pootling is rarely used passively)  The boat was pootled gently along the canal.  = 小船缓缓地沿着运河行驶。 (This is unusual and sounds awkward).
The car was being pootled along the quiet road.  = 汽车正慢慢地沿着安静的道路行驶。(This is also unusual and sounds awkward).


疑问句:
Are they pootling along the road? = 他们正在沿着路慢悠悠地走吗？
Will you be pootling around the market later? = 你晚点会在市场上闲逛吗？


否定句:
They didn't pootle around the city much. = 他们没有在城市里四处闲逛太多。
We won't pootle around today; we have too much to do. = 我们今天不会闲逛了，我们有很多事情要做。
