replow:/rɪˈplaʊ/ | vt. |重新耕犁；翻耕 |To plow (land) again.  This often implies a more thorough plowing than the initial plowing, or plowing after a period of time.  It can also refer to revisiting or re-examining something in detail. |例句：The farmer decided to replow the field after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，农民决定重新耕犁田地。

The team decided to replow the data to ensure accuracy. = 团队决定重新分析数据以确保准确性。|近义词：retill, rework, reexamine |反义词：harvest, leave untouched
