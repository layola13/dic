fuzzes:/ˈfʌzɪz/ | n. |绒毛；模糊不清的东西；(无线电干扰)|  The word refers to soft, fluffy fibers, a state of indistinctness or confusion, or radio interference. It often implies a lack of clarity or precision. |例句：The sweater has little fuzzes on its surface. 这件毛衣表面有小绒毛。


例句：The photo is all fuzzes; I can't make out the details. 这张照片很模糊；我无法分辨细节。


近义词：fluff, lint, fuzz, indistinctness |反义词：clarity, precision, sharpness |词性：名词
