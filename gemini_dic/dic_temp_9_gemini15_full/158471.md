pledget:/ˈplɛdʒɪt/| n. |小块绷带；少量保证金；誓言|A small piece of material, typically gauze, used to cover a wound or to absorb blood or other fluids.  It can also refer to a small sum of money given as a pledge or guarantee. |例句：The nurse applied a pledget to the bleeding wound. 护士用一块小纱布敷在流血的伤口上。
The investor gave a small pledget as a show of good faith. 投资者提供了一小笔保证金以示诚意。|近义词：compress, pad, guarantee|反义词：N/A
