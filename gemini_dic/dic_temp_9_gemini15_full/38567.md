concoctor:/kənˈkɑːktər/ | n. |调制者；捏造者；发明者 | A person who concocts something, especially a drink or a plan.  Someone who invents or creates something, often in a clever or skillful way. It can also have a negative connotation, implying deception or fabrication. |例句：He was a skilled concoctor of potent cocktails. 他是一位调制烈性鸡尾酒的行家。

例句：The journalist was accused of being a concoctor of false stories.  这位记者被指控捏造虚假新闻。


近义词：mixer, inventor, creator, deviser, fabricator

反义词：deconstructor, destroyer, dismantler


词性：名词
