musicality: /ˌmjuːzɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 音乐性；音乐天赋 | The quality of being musical; the skill and artistry of musical expression.  It encompasses elements like rhythm, melody, harmony, phrasing, and overall aesthetic appeal. | 例句：The pianist's musicality was evident in every note.  这位钢琴家的音乐天赋在每一个音符中都显而易见。

The singer's performance lacked musicality.  这位歌手的表演缺乏音乐性。


近义词：melodiousness, artistry, expressiveness | 反义词：tonelessness, inharmoniousness


词性: 名词
