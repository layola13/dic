dozes:/ˈdoʊzɪz/| v.|打盹儿，瞌睡|To sleep lightly for a short time.|The old man dozed in his armchair. 老人在扶手椅上打起了盹。  He dozed off during the lecture. 他在讲座期间打起了瞌睡。|sleep, nap, slumber|wake, be awake|动词

He often dozes off after lunch. 他午饭后经常打盹。
She dozes while watching TV. 她看电视时会打瞌睡。
