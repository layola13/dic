lacquer:/ˈlækər/ | n., v. |清漆；漆器；(用漆)涂饰 | a shiny coating made from the sap of a tree or other substance, used to protect or decorate surfaces.  | The table is covered with a thick layer of lacquer. 这张桌子涂了一层厚厚的漆。  | varnish, enamel, shellac | unlacquered | 名词，动词
