silicoses:/ˌsɪlɪˈkoʊsɪz/| n. |矽肺病（复数）|A group of lung diseases caused by inhaling silica dust.  Silicosis is a progressive disease, meaning it gets worse over time.  It's characterized by inflammation and scarring of the lungs, leading to difficulty breathing and other respiratory problems.  There is no cure for silicosis, but treatment can help manage symptoms.|The miners suffered from silicoses after years of working in the mines.|pneumoconiosis, lung fibrosis|healthy lungs
