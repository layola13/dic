downturns:/ˈdaʊntɜːrnz/| n. |经济衰退；低迷时期；下降趋势 | A downturn is a period of decline in economic activity.  It's characterized by things like reduced consumer spending, decreased business investment, and rising unemployment.  The severity and duration of downturns can vary widely. | Example: The recent global downturn has led to increased job losses.  最近的全球经济低迷导致失业人数增加。  The company experienced a downturn in sales after the launch of a competing product.  该公司的销售额在竞争产品推出后出现下滑。 |近义词：recession, slump, decline, depression |反义词：upswing, boom, recovery, expansion |名词
