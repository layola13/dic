hyphening:/ˈhaɪfənɪŋ/ | n. |  连字符使用；用连字符连接 | The process of joining words or parts of words with a hyphen.  It clarifies meaning, improves readability, and sometimes indicates a compound word. | Example: The hyphening in "self-esteem" is crucial for understanding the word's meaning.  连字符在“self-esteem”（自尊）中的使用对于理解该词的含义至关重要。|近义词：hyphenation | 反义词：无
