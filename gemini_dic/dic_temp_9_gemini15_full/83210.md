guzzles:/ˈɡʌzəlz/| v.| 狂饮，狼吞虎咽 | To drink large quantities of liquid quickly and noisily.  |例句：He guzzled down the entire bottle of water in seconds. 他几秒钟就喝光了整瓶水。
例句：The thirsty dog guzzled the water from the bowl.  这条口渴的狗狼吞虎咽地喝着碗里的水。|近义词：gulp, chug, quaff |反义词：sip, savor

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He guzzles his coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都狼吞虎咽地喝咖啡。
They guzzle beer at the party. = 他们在派对上狂饮啤酒。

一般过去时:
She guzzled the soda quickly. = 她很快地喝掉了苏打水。
We guzzled down the leftover pizza. = 我们狼吞虎咽地吃掉了剩下的披萨。(Note: This example uses "guzzle" figuratively with food)

现在完成时:
He has guzzled three liters of water today. = 他今天已经喝了三升水。
She has guzzled her way through a whole bottle of wine. = 她已经喝掉了一整瓶酒。


过去完成时:
He had guzzled all the beer before his friends arrived. = 在朋友们到达之前，他已经喝掉了所有的啤酒。
They had guzzled most of the snacks before the movie started. = 电影开始前，他们已经吃掉了大部分零食。(Note: This example uses "guzzle" figuratively with food)


将来时:

They will guzzle their drinks before the show begins. = 演出开始前，他们会喝掉他们的饮料。


虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would guzzle that water. = 如果我渴了，我会痛快地喝掉那水。
I wish I hadn't guzzled so much coffee last night. = 我真希望昨晚不要喝那么多咖啡。


被动语态:
(The verb "guzzle" is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies a deliberate action.)  The soda was quickly drunk. = 苏打水很快就被喝掉了。 (Alternative phrasing)
The entire bottle was guzzled in minutes. = 整瓶酒在几分钟内就被喝光了。(Alternative phrasing and less common)


疑问句:
Does he often guzzle his food? = 他经常狼吞虎咽地吃饭吗？
Will they guzzle all the punch? = 他们会把所有的果汁都喝掉吗？


否定句:
He doesn't guzzle his food; he eats slowly and deliberately. = 他不吃得很快；他吃得很慢很仔细。
They didn't guzzle the drinks; they sipped them slowly. = 他们没有狂饮；他们慢慢地啜饮。


PHRASE->
guzzle down:  狼吞虎咽地喝下
guzzle up: 狂饮；大吃大喝
guzzle away:  不断地喝；不断地吃
guzzle through:  喝光；吃光
