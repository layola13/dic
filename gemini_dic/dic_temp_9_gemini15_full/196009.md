sentimentalizer: /ˌsɛntɪmənˈtælʌɪzər/ | n. |煽情者；多愁善感的人 | One who sentimentalizes; one who excessively indulges in sentimentality.  | He's a sentimentalizer, always dwelling on the past. 他是个煽情的人，总是沉湎于过去。 |近义词：romanticist, daydreamer |反义词：cynic, realist |
