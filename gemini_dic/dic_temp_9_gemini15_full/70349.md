fiendish:/ˈfiːnɪʃ/| adj.| 恶魔似的；极坏的；狡猾的；极难对付的 |extremely evil or wicked; diabolically clever or ingenious.  |例句：He devised a fiendish plan to get revenge. 他设计了一个恶毒的计划来报复。

例句：The puzzle was fiendishly difficult to solve. 这个谜题难到令人发指。


近义词：devilish, diabolical, wicked, malicious, cruel, cunning, tricky,  infernal

反义词：angelic, benevolent, kind, harmless, simple, easy
