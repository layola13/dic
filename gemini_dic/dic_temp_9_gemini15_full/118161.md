ment:/ment/| n.|行为；方式；结果|The suffix "-ment" is added to verbs to form nouns that denote the act, process, or result of the verb.  It often signifies a state, condition, or product. For example, "development" is the act of developing. |The development of the project took longer than expected.  这个项目的开发比预期的要长。The government's management of the crisis was poor. 政府处理危机的措施很糟糕。|development, achievement, improvement|deterioration, decline, worsening|名词
