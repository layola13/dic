devoutful: /dəˈvaʊtfəl/ | adj. |虔诚的，恭敬的 |Characterized by or showing deep religious devotion or piety; pious.  The word suggests a more intense and heartfelt devotion than simply "devout".|例句：She showed a devoutful reverence for the ancient texts. 她对古代文本表现出虔诚的敬意。  His devoutful prayers were answered. 他的虔诚祈祷得到了回应。|近义词：pious, devout, reverent, religious |反义词：irreverent, impious, profane
