liberetto:/ˌlɪbəˈrɛtoʊ/ | n. | 歌剧脚本；剧本 | A libretto is the text of an opera or other musical work.  It is usually written in verse and is intended to be sung. | Example: The libretto for this opera is quite complex. 这部歌剧的剧本相当复杂。 | 近义词：script, text, book | 反义词：score (in the context of musical notation)
