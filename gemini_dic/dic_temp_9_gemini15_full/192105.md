scouting:/ˈskaʊtɪŋ/| n.  |侦察；童子军活动；物色；考察|The act of scouting involves searching for information or exploring a new area.  It also refers to the activities of the Boy Scouts or Girl Scouts.|例句：The army did some scouting before the attack.  军队在进攻前进行了一些侦察。|例句：She's been scouting for a new apartment. 她一直在物色新公寓。|近义词：reconnaissance, exploration, surveying, searching |反义词：hiding, concealing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is scouting for new talent. = 他正在物色新人材。
She scouts the area for potential risks. = 她侦察该地区潜在的风险。


一般过去时:
They scouted the terrain before the battle. = 他们在战斗前侦察了地形。
He scouted the woods for lost hikers. = 他在树林里搜寻迷路的徒步旅行者。


现在完成时:
We have been scouting locations for our new store. = 我们一直在为我们的新店寻找地点。
The team has scouted several promising candidates. = 该团队已经物色了几位很有希望的候选人。


过去完成时:
They had scouted the route before the long hike. = 他们在长途徒步旅行之前已经侦察过路线了。
She had scouted the market before launching her product. = 她在推出产品之前已经考察过市场了。


将来时:
We will be scouting new areas for expansion. = 我们将要侦察新的地区以进行扩张。
They will scout the competition before making a decision. = 他们将在做出决定之前考察竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had scouted the area earlier, we would have found a better campsite. = 如果我们早点侦察这个地区，我们就能找到更好的营地。
I wish I had scouted the market before investing. = 我希望在投资之前我已经考察过市场了。


被动语态:
The area was scouted by the military before the operation. = 在行动之前，该地区已被军方侦察过。
The new candidates are being scouted by the hiring manager. = 新的候选人正在被招聘经理物色。


疑问句:
Have you scouted any suitable locations yet? = 你已经物色到任何合适的地点了吗？
Are they scouting for new members? = 他们正在招募新成员吗？


否定句:
They did not scout the area thoroughly enough. = 他们没有充分侦察该地区。
We haven't scouted all the possibilities yet. = 我们还没有考察所有可能性。


PHRASE->
scout around = 到处寻找

scout out = 侦察；寻找
go scouting = 去侦察；去参加童子军活动
Boy Scouting/Girl Scouting = 童子军活动/女童子军活动
