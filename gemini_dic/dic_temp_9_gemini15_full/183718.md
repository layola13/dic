resalute:/riˈsæluːt/| vt.|再次敬礼；重新致敬|To salute again; to salute anew.  The soldier resaluted the officer after receiving his instructions.|近义词：resalute, salute again|反义词：None.

The guard resalutes passing dignitaries. = 警卫向经过的贵宾敬礼。
