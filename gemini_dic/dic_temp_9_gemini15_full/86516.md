heist:/haɪst/| n. v.| 偷窃；抢劫 | A heist is a robbery, typically a daring and well-planned one, often involving a large sum of money or valuable goods.  It often implies skill and planning beyond a simple robbery. |例句：The gang pulled off a daring heist at the museum, stealing priceless artifacts.  这伙匪帮在博物馆策划了一场大胆的抢劫案，偷走了无价的文物。
The police are investigating a large-scale heist at the bank.警方正在调查一起发生在银行的大规模抢劫案。|近义词：robbery, burglary, theft |反义词：donation, gift
