karabiner:/ˈkærəbaɪnər/ | n. | 登山扣；保险扣；快挂 | A carabiner is a metal loop with a spring-loaded gate, used for connecting climbing ropes, equipment, or other items.  It's a crucial piece of safety equipment in mountaineering, rock climbing, and other outdoor activities. | Example: He clipped the carabiner to his harness before descending the cliff. 他在下降悬崖之前将登山扣扣到他的安全带上。 | 近义词：snap hook, quick link | 反义词：无
