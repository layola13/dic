degrades:/dɪˈɡreɪdz/| v.| 降低；恶化；贬低 | To lower in quality, value, or status; to become worse or less effective. |例句：The quality of the air degrades during rush hour.  高峰期空气质量下降。|例句：His behavior degrades him in the eyes of others. 他的行为在他人眼中贬低了他。|近义词： deteriorates, worsens, declines, diminishes |反义词： improves, enhances, elevates, betters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The environment degrades slowly but surely. = 环境缓慢但肯定地恶化着。
His reputation degrades with each scandal. = 他的名声随着每一次丑闻而受损。

一般过去时:
The building degraded over the years. = 这座建筑物多年来逐渐破败。
The relationship degraded after the argument. = 争吵之后，关系恶化了。

现在完成时:
The quality of life has degraded significantly. = 生活质量已经显著下降。
The bridge has degraded due to lack of maintenance. = 由于缺乏维护，桥梁已经老化。

过去完成时:
The situation had degraded considerably before they intervened. = 在他们干预之前，情况已经大大恶化。
His health had degraded rapidly before he sought medical attention. = 在他寻求医疗救助之前，他的健康状况迅速恶化。

将来时:
The ecosystem will degrade further if pollution continues. = 如果污染继续下去，生态系统将进一步恶化。
The value of the currency will degrade if inflation rises. = 如果通货膨胀上升，货币价值将会贬值。

虚拟语气:
If we continued to pollute, the environment would degrade even faster. = 如果我们继续污染，环境将恶化得更快。
I wish the situation hadn't degraded so rapidly. = 我希望情况没有恶化得如此迅速。

被动语态:
The environment is being degraded by pollution. = 环境正在被污染所破坏。
The building was degraded by years of neglect. = 这座建筑物由于多年的忽视而破败。

疑问句:
Does stress degrade cognitive function? = 压力会降低认知功能吗？
How much has the value of the property degraded? = 房地产价值贬值了多少？

否定句:
The machine didn't degrade despite heavy use. = 尽管使用频繁，机器并没有损坏。
The quality of the product didn't degrade over time. = 产品质量没有随着时间的推移而下降。
