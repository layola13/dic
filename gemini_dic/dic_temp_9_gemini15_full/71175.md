fissural:/ˈfɪʒərəl/| adj.|裂缝的；裂隙的 | Relating to or resembling a fissure; characterized by fissures.  A fissure is a narrow opening or crack, especially in rock or the earth.  |例句：The geologist examined the fissural structure of the rock.  地质学家检查了岩石的裂隙结构。

The patient presented with fissural ulcers on their lips. 病人嘴唇上有裂缝性溃疡。

近义词：fissured, cracked, cleft, split |反义词：unbroken, seamless, intact


词性：形容词
