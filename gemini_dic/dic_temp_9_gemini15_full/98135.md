insanity:/ɪnˈsænɪti/| n.|疯狂；精神错乱；荒谬|The state of being mentally ill; madness.  It can also refer to extreme foolishness or unreasonableness.|例句：His actions bordered on insanity. 他的行为近乎疯狂。

例句：The idea of building a house on a fault line is sheer insanity. 在断层线上建房子的想法简直是荒谬绝伦。


近义词：madness, lunacy, craziness, derangement, mania

反义词：sanity, reasonableness, rationality


词性：名词


词源：源自中古英语 *insanitie，来自古法语 insanie，最终源自拉丁语 insanitas，意为“不健康的状态”。  insanitas 由 in- (否定前缀) + sanitas (健康)构成。
