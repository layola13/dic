redrying:/ˌriːˈdraɪɪŋ/| v.| 再干燥；重新干燥 | The process of drying something again, often after it has become damp or wet.  | Example:  The redrying process is crucial to maintain the quality of the product.  重新干燥的过程对于保持产品的质量至关重要。 The farmer is redrying the hay after unexpected rain. 农民在意外下雨后正在重新晾晒干草。|近义词：re-drying, drying again|反义词：wetting, dampening
