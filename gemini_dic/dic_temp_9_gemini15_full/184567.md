restock:/riːˈstɒk/| vt.|重新进货，补充库存|To replenish a supply of goods that have been sold or used.  |例句：We need to restock the shelves before the weekend rush. 我们需要在周末人潮来临之前重新进货。|例句：The supermarket restocked its shelves with fresh produce. 超市用新鲜农产品补充了货架上的存货。|近义词：replenish, refill, replenish supplies |反义词：deplete, exhaust, empty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store restocks its inventory daily. = 这家商店每天都补充库存。
He restocks the shelves after closing time. = 他在关店后补充货架上的商品。

一般过去时:
They restocked the shelves with new books. = 他们用新书补充了货架上的书籍。
The company restocked its warehouse last week. = 公司上周补充了仓库的库存。

现在完成时:
We have restocked the office supplies. = 我们已经补充了办公用品。
She has restocked the medicine cabinet. = 她已经补充了医药箱里的药品。

过去完成时:
They had restocked the store before the sale started. = 他们在促销开始之前就已经补充了商店的货品。
The restaurant had restocked its ingredients before the dinner rush. = 餐厅在晚餐高峰期之前已经补充了食材。

将来时:
We will restock the shelves tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将补充货架上的商品。
They will restock the warehouse next week. = 他们下周将补充仓库的库存。

虚拟语气:
If we had restocked earlier, we wouldn't have run out of products. = 如果我们早点补充库存，就不会缺货了。
I wish I had restocked the printer ink. = 我真希望我已经补充了打印机墨水。

被动语态:
The shelves were restocked overnight. = 货架在夜间得到了补充。
The warehouse is being restocked as we speak. = 仓库正在补充库存。

疑问句:
Have they restocked the items yet? = 他们已经补充那些商品了吗？
Will the shop be restocked before the holiday? = 假期前商店会补充货品吗？

否定句:
They didn't restock the shelves in time. = 他们没有及时补充货架上的商品。
We haven't restocked the coffee yet. = 我们还没补充咖啡。
