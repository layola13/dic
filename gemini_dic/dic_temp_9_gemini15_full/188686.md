run-around: /ˈrʌnəraʊnd/ | n. | 瞎忙；奔波；欺骗；躲避；拖延 | A lot of pointless activity that achieves little or nothing; a frustrating and time-consuming experience; deception or evasion. |例句：The whole project turned into a complete run-around.  整个项目变成了一场毫无意义的瞎忙。  |近义词：fuss, hassle, chase, evasion, deception |反义词：efficiency, straightforwardness, honesty


USAGE->

The paperwork is a real run-around. = 这堆文书工作真是让人烦不胜烦。
This job is just a run-around. = 这份工作只是在瞎忙。


一般过去时:
The meeting was a complete run-around. = 那次会议完全是在浪费时间。
I experienced a run-around trying to get my refund. = 我为了退款费了好大劲。
