ecotourism:/ˈekoʊˌtʊərɪzəm/| n. |生态旅游|Environmentally responsible travel to natural areas, conserving the environment, and improving the well-being of local people.  Ecotourism focuses on minimizing the negative impact on the environment and maximizing the positive impact on local communities.|Example:  Many countries are promoting ecotourism to attract visitors while protecting their natural resources.  许多国家正在推广生态旅游，以吸引游客，同时保护其自然资源。

Many tourists are now choosing ecotourism over traditional tourism. 现在许多游客选择生态旅游而不是传统旅游。|近义词：sustainable tourism, responsible travel |反义词：mass tourism, irresponsible tourism
