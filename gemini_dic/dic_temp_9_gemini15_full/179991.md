refiltered:/ˌriːˈfɪltərd/ | adj. | 再过滤的，重新过滤的 |  Having been filtered again.  It implies a process of filtering something that has already been filtered once. | Example: The refiltered water was now perfectly clear and safe to drink.  再过滤后的水现在非常清澈，可以安全饮用。 The refiltered coffee tasted much smoother than the first brew.  再过滤的咖啡比第一次冲泡的味道更顺滑。 | 近义词：reprocessed, purified, refined | 反义词：unfiltered, raw, impure |  形容词
