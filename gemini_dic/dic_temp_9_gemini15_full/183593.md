requisition:/ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n., vt. | 申请；征用；要求；索取 |  The act of formally requesting something, especially supplies or equipment;  to officially demand or take something, especially for military or official use. |例句：The school submitted a requisition for new computers. 学校提交了一份购买新电脑的申请单。|例句：The army requisitioned all available vehicles.  军队征用了所有可用的车辆。|近义词：request, demand, order, requisition form |反义词：release, surrender, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He requisitions supplies every month. 他每月都申请供应品。
The government requisitions land for the new highway. 政府征用土地修建新高速公路。


一般过去时:
They requisitioned additional troops. 他们增调了部队。
The company requisitioned new equipment last year. 公司去年申请了新的设备。


现在完成时:
The department has requisitioned all the necessary materials. 部门已经申请了所有必要的材料。
The military has requisitioned several buildings for use as hospitals.  军队已经征用了几座建筑物作为医院。


过去完成时:
They had requisitioned the hall for the party before the other group even applied. 在另一组人申请之前，他们就已经为派对征用了大厅。
The general had requisitioned additional ammunition before the battle. 将军在战斗前已经申请了额外的弹药。


将来时:
We will requisition more funds for the project next month. 我们下个月将为该项目申请更多资金。
The company will requisition new software for all employees. 公司将为所有员工申请新的软件。


虚拟语气:
If they requisitioned more resources, they could finish the project earlier. 如果他们申请更多资源，他们可以更早完成项目。
I wish they had requisitioned the equipment sooner. 我希望他们早点申请设备。


被动语态:
Additional funds were requisitioned for the project. 该项目申请了额外的资金。
The building was requisitioned by the army during the war.  那栋建筑在战争期间被军队征用。


疑问句:
Have you requisitioned the necessary equipment? 你已经申请了必要的设备了吗？
Will the materials be requisitioned in time? 材料能及时申请到吗？


否定句:
They did not requisition enough supplies. 他们申请的物资不够。
The company hasn't requisitioned the software yet. 公司还没有申请软件。
