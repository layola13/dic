rechristened: /ˌriːˈkrɪs(ə)nd/ | vt. |重新命名，改名 | To give a new name to something or someone.  |例句：The company was rechristened "InnovateTech" after its merger.  公司在合并后被重新命名为“InnovateTech”。|近义词：rename, retitle, redesignate |反义词：retain original name
