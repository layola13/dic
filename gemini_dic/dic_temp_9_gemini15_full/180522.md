regerminating: /ˌriːdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再生，重新发芽 | The act of a seed or plant starting to grow again after a period of dormancy.  It refers to the process of germination occurring a second or subsequent time. | Example:  The seeds, after being stored for months, showed signs of regerminating in the spring. 春天的时候，那些储存了几个月的种子显示出重新发芽的迹象。

|近义词:sprouting, sprouting anew, re-sprouting|反义词:dying, withering, decaying|词性:动词
