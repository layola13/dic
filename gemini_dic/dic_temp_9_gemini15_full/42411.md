countercomplaints:/ˈkaʊntər kəmˈpleɪnts/| n. |反诉，反控告 |A countercomplaint is a complaint made in response to a previous complaint, especially in a legal context.  It alleges wrongdoing by the party who initially filed the complaint. | Example: After being sued for breach of contract, the company filed a countercomplaint alleging fraud by the plaintiff.  该公司在被控违约后，提出反诉，指控原告欺诈。 |近义词：counterclaim, rebuttal, rejoinder |反义词：original complaint, initial claim
