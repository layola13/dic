re-pondering:/ˌriːˈpɒndərɪŋ/| v.|重新考虑，反复思考 |再次思考；重新权衡 |She spent the evening re-pondering her decision. 她花了整个晚上重新考虑她的决定。|近义词：reconsidering, reviewing, reflecting on |反义词：deciding, resolving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is re-pondering his options. 他正在重新考虑他的选择。
They are re-pondering the implications of the new policy. 他们正在重新权衡新政策的影响。


一般过去时:
She re-pondered her response to his proposal. 她重新考虑了她对他提议的回应。
We re-pondered the evidence before reaching a verdict. 我们在做出判决前重新审视了证据。


现在完成时:
I have been re-pondering this problem all day. 我整天都在反复思考这个问题。
They have re-pondered their strategy and decided to change course. 他们重新考虑了他们的策略，并决定改变方向。


过去完成时:
He had re-pondered his decision several times before finally making up his mind. 他在最终下定决心之前已经反复考虑过几次他的决定。
After she had re-pondered the situation, she realized her mistake. 在她重新思考了情况之后，她意识到了自己的错误。


将来时:
I will re-ponder my decision tomorrow. 我明天会重新考虑我的决定。
They will re-ponder the matter after they have gathered more information. 他们收集到更多信息后会重新考虑此事。


虚拟语气:
If I had re-pondered the situation, I might have made a different choice. 如果我重新考虑过这种情况，我可能会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had re-pondered my options before accepting the job. 我希望在接受这份工作之前，我重新考虑过我的选择。


被动语态:
The proposal is being re-pondered by the committee. 该提案正在由委员会重新考虑。
The decision was re-pondered by the judge after new evidence was presented. 在新的证据提交后，法官重新考虑了判决。


疑问句:
Are you re-pondering your decision? 你在重新考虑你的决定吗？
Have they been re-pondering the plan? 他们一直在重新考虑计划吗？


否定句:
He is not re-pondering his decision. 他没有重新考虑他的决定。
They did not re-ponder their options before making a choice. 他们在做出选择之前没有重新考虑他们的选择。
