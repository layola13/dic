limbing:/ˈlɪmbɪŋ/| v. |攀登；攀爬|The act of climbing, especially using one's limbs.|例句：The monkeys were limbing effortlessly through the trees. 猴子们轻松地攀爬过树木。

例句：He was limbing up the steep cliff. 他正在攀登陡峭的悬崖。


近义词：climbing, scrambling, scaling|反义词：descending, falling
