overdogs:/ˈoʊvərˌdɒɡz/ | n. |社会优胜者，掌权者 |People or groups who hold significant power and influence in a society, often at the expense of others.  They are often associated with privilege and dominance.  The term implies a critique of social hierarchies and power imbalances. | Example: The overdogs in this city control the media and the political system.  这个城市的掌权者控制着媒体和政治体系。|近义词：elite, ruling class, power brokers, establishment |反义词：underdogs, marginalized, oppressed
