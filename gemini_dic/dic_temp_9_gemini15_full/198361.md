showered:/ˈʃaʊərd/ | v. | 淋浴；大量给予；倾泻 | To bathe or wash oneself with a shower; to give or receive something in abundance; to fall or pour down heavily. |例句：I showered before going to work.  我上班前洗了个澡。 |例句：The team showered the winning player with gifts.  球队给获胜的球员送了很多礼物。|近义词：bathed, deluged, inundated |反义词：drenched, soaked (these are more intense and imply complete saturation)
