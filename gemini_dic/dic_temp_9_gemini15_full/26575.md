cabochon:/ˈkæbəʃɒn/| n. |凸圆形宝石|A cabochon is a gemstone that has been polished smooth but not faceted.  It has a rounded or dome-like shape, rather than the flat, multifaceted surfaces of a faceted gemstone. The cabochon cut emphasizes the stone's color and translucence, rather than its brilliance. |Example: The ring featured a beautiful cabochon ruby. 这枚戒指上镶嵌着一颗美丽的凸圆形红宝石。|近义词：polished stone, uncut gem|反义词：faceted gem

词源:
