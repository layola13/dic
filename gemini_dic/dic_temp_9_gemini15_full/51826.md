dewing:/ˈdjuːɪŋ/| n.|结露，露水|The process of depositing dew; the formation of dew.  Dewing is common in the early morning hours.|例句：The early morning dewing on the grass created a beautiful scene.清晨草地上的露水创造了一幅美丽的景象。

The heavy dewing last night left the plants glistening.  昨晚的大量露水使植物闪闪发光。|近义词：condensation, precipitation|反义词：evaporation, drying
