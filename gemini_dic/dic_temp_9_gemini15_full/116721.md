maudlinisms:/ˈmɔːdlɪnɪzəm/| n. |伤感的言行；多愁善感；(尤指)矫揉造作的伤感 |Sentimental or overly emotional expressions or behavior, often characterized by excessive self-pity or exaggerated displays of grief.  It often implies a lack of sincerity or authenticity. |例句：His maudlinisms at the funeral were embarrassing to everyone.  他的葬礼上的矫揉造作令所有人尴尬。

例句：Her constant maudlinisms about her lost love grew tiresome. 她对失去的爱人不断地伤春悲秋，让人厌烦。


近义词：sentimentality, mushiness, gushiness, melodrama, pathos (when excessive)


反义词：stoicism, cynicism, apathy, indifference


词性：名词


词源：源自圣经人物玛利亚·抹大拉（Mary Magdalene），传统上被描绘成一个悔过自新的罪人，其形象在艺术作品中经常被赋予过度悲伤和悔恨的表情，从而演变为“伤感”、“多愁善感”的含义。  The word's association with excessive sentimentality stems from artistic depictions of Mary Magdalene, often portrayed with an exaggeratedly sorrowful expression.
