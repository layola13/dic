dust-jacket:/ˈdʌstˌdʒækɪt/ | n. |护封，书套 |A protective covering of paper, cardboard, or other material placed over the cover of a book to protect it.  |Example: The dust jacket of the book was torn and faded. 这本书的护封撕破了，而且褪色了。|近义词：book jacket, cover |反义词：N/A
