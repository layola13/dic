pounded:/ˈpaʊndɪd/| v.| 重击；猛击；捣碎；连续猛击 | to strike or beat repeatedly with force; to crush or grind into powder or fragments |例句：The waves pounded the shore. 海浪拍打着海岸。|例句：She pounded the dough with her fist. 她用拳头捶打面团。|近义词：beat, strike, crush, smash |反义词：gently tap, caress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pounds the table in anger. = 他生气地猛拍桌子。
The machine pounds the grain into flour. = 机器把谷物碾成面粉。

一般过去时:
The rain pounded the roof all night. = 雨整夜猛烈地敲打着屋顶。
