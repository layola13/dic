preoutline: /ˌpriːˈaʊtlaɪn/ | n. |初步大纲，预先概要 | A preoutline is a preliminary outline created before a detailed outline is made.  It helps organize thoughts and ideas before writing a longer piece.  It typically includes main points and sub-points, but less detail than a full outline. |Example: I created a preoutline before I started writing my essay to get my ideas in order. 我在开始写论文之前创建了一个初步大纲，以便整理我的想法。|近义词：draft outline, preliminary sketch, initial plan |反义词：final outline, finished draft
