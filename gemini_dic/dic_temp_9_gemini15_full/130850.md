nonapproachable: /ˌnɒnəˈproʊtʃəbl/ | adj. |难以接近的，不易亲近的 |  Difficult to approach or get to know; unfriendly or unwelcoming.  The word combines "non-" (not) and "approachable" (easy to approach). | 例句：He seemed nonapproachable, his face set in a permanent frown. 他看起来难以接近，脸上总是皱着眉头。

例句：The CEO maintained a nonapproachable demeanor, rarely interacting with employees.  首席执行官保持着难以接近的态度，很少与员工互动。


近义词：unapproachable, aloof, distant, reserved, unfriendly, forbidding, intimidating


反义词：approachable, friendly, welcoming, warm, open, accessible


词性：形容词
