grassier:/ˈɡræsiər/| adj.|草多的，青草茂盛的|More grassy; covered with more grass.|The grassier fields were perfect for a picnic.  草多的田野非常适合野餐。The park became grassier after the recent rain. 最近雨后，公园的草更茂盛了。|grassy, verdant|barren, desolate|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The field is grassier this year. = 今年这块地草更多了。
The lawn is always grassier after a good rain. = 大雨过后，草坪总是更绿更茂盛。

一般过去时:
The meadow was grassier last spring. = 去年春天这片草地草更茂盛。
The hillside was much grassier before the drought. = 干旱之前，山坡上的草更茂盛。

现在完成时:
The pasture has become grassier since we started fertilizing it. = 自从我们开始施肥以来，牧场上的草越来越多了。
The park has gotten grassier over the years. = 多年来，公园的草越来越茂盛了。

过去完成时:
The field had been grassier before the sheep grazed it. = 在羊吃草之前，这块地上的草更茂盛。
The lawn had been much grassier before the disease affected it. = 在疾病影响之前，草坪上的草更茂盛。

将来时:
The meadow will be grassier next year. = 明年这片草地上的草会更茂盛。
The hillside will likely be grassier after the spring rains. = 春雨过后，山坡上的草可能会更茂盛。

虚拟语气:
If the lawn were grassier, it would look better. = 如果草坪上的草更茂盛，它看起来会更好。
I wish the pasture were grassier. = 我希望牧场上的草更茂盛。

被动语态:
The field is considered to be grassier than last year. = 这块地被认为比去年草更多。
The area was described as being grassier in the old photographs. = 在旧照片中，该地区被描述为草更多。

疑问句:
Is this side of the hill grassier than that side? = 这边山坡比那边山坡草更多吗？
Isn't the lawn getting grassier? = 草坪不是越来越茂盛了吗？


否定句:
The desert is not grassier than the plains. = 沙漠的草不像平原那么多。
The area behind the house isn't very grassier. = 房子后面的区域草并不茂盛。
