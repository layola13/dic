monometric:/ˌmɒnəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj. |单音节的；单音的；单度量衡的| Relating to or characterized by a single meter or measurement.  It typically refers to poetry written in a single metrical pattern or to systems of measurement using a single unit.|The poem is monometric, using only iambic pentameter throughout. 这首诗是单音节的，贯穿始终都只使用抑扬五音步。|近义词：uniform, single-metered |反义词：polymetric, varied-metered
