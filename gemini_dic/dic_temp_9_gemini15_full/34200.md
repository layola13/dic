clamshell-phone:/ˈklæmʃɛl foʊn/| n. |翻盖手机|A clamshell phone is a mobile phone that has two parts that fold over each other, like a clamshell.  This design usually protects the screen when the phone is closed.  It is a style of mobile phone popular before the rise of touchscreen smartphones.|Example: My grandmother still uses a clamshell phone because she finds it easy to use.|近义词：flip phone|反义词：smartphone, touchscreen phone
