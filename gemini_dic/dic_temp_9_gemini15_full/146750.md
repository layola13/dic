overhumanizing: /ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度拟人化 | To attribute human qualities or characteristics to something that is not human, to an excessive or inappropriate degree.  This often leads to inaccurate or misleading interpretations. | 例句：Overhumanizing AI can lead to unrealistic expectations and ethical concerns.  过度拟人化人工智能会导致不切实际的期望和伦理问题。


例句：The marketing campaign overhumanized the brand's mascot, making it seem more like a real person than a fictional character.  市场营销活动过度拟人化了品牌的吉祥物，使其看起来更像一个真实的人，而不是一个虚构的角色。


近义词：anthropomorphizing, personifying


反义词：dehumanizing, depersonalizing


词性：动词
