shorten:/ˈʃɔːtn̩/| /ˈʃɔːrtən/ | vt. |缩短；缩写；简化 |To make or become shorter or less in length, duration, or extent.  |例句：The editor shortened the article to fit the space constraints. 编辑缩短了文章以适应空间限制。

例句：We shortened our vacation due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，我们缩短了假期。


近义词：abbreviate, curtail, reduce, condense | 反义词：lengthen, extend, prolong | 词性：动词


USAGE->

He shortens his speeches to avoid boring the audience. 他缩短了他的演讲以避免让听众感到厌烦。
She shortens her skirt every year. 她每年都会把裙子改短。
