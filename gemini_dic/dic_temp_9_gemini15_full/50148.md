denunciate:/dɪˈnʌnsieɪt/ | vt. | 公开谴责；告发 | To publicly condemn or criticize (someone or something).  | 例句：The politician denounced the government's handling of the crisis.  这位政治家谴责了政府处理危机的方案。

例句：The witness denounced the criminal to the police. 目击证人向警方告发了罪犯。


近义词：condemn, criticize, censure, denounce, reprobate | 反义词：praise, commend, applaud


词源:  From Latin *denuntiāre, from de- + nuntiāre "to announce," from nuntius "messenger."  The original meaning was to announce formally, which evolved to include the sense of condemning.

过去完成时:
