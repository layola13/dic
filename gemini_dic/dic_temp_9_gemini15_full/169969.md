prosyndicalists: /ˌproʊˌsɪndɪkəlɪsts/ | n. |工团主义者（复数）| Supporters of prosynicalism, a political movement advocating for workers' control of industry through syndicates or unions.  They believe in direct action and worker solidarity to achieve social and economic justice. | Example: The prosynicalists organized a general strike to demand better working conditions. 工团主义者组织了一次大罢工以争取更好的工作条件。|近义词：syndicalists, labor activists |反义词：capitalists, anti-unionists
