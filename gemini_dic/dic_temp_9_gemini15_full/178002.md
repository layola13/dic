recite:/rɪˈsaɪt/| vt.|背诵；朗诵；列举|To repeat aloud from memory, or to read aloud.  It can also mean to list or enumerate something.|例句：The student recited the poem flawlessly.  学生流利地背诵了这首诗。|例句：She recited the names of all the attendees. 她列举了所有与会者的名字。|近义词：repeat, rehearse, enumerate, recount|反义词：forget, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recites the alphabet every morning. = 他每天早上都背诵字母表。
She recites Shakespeare at the theatre. = 她在剧院朗诵莎士比亚的作品。

一般过去时:
I recited the poem for my class. = 我为我的班级背诵了这首诗。
He recited the pledge of allegiance.  = 他宣读了效忠誓言。


现在完成时:
She has recited that speech many times. = 她已经多次朗诵过那个演讲。
He has recited the entire book of Genesis. = 他已经背诵了创世纪全书。


过去完成时:
They had recited the lines before the performance. = 他们在演出前就已经背诵了台词。
She had recited the same prayer every night before bed. = 她每天晚上睡觉前都背诵同样的祈祷文。


将来时:
I will recite my speech at the graduation ceremony. = 我将在毕业典礼上朗诵我的演讲。
She will recite the poem at the competition. = 她将在比赛中朗诵这首诗。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have recited the poem perfectly. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能完美地背诵这首诗了。
I wish I could recite the entire Iliad. = 我希望我能背诵整部《伊利亚特》。


被动语态:
The poem was recited by many students. = 这首诗被许多学生朗诵。
The list of names was recited slowly and clearly.  = 名单被缓慢而清晰地念了出来。


疑问句:
Will you recite the poem for us? = 你会为我们背诵这首诗吗？
Did he recite the passage correctly? = 他正确地朗诵了这段话吗？


否定句:
I cannot recite the whole poem. = 我不能背诵整首诗。
She did not recite the answer to the question. = 她没有背诵出问题的答案。


PHRASE->
recite a poem = 背诵一首诗
recite from memory = 凭记忆背诵
recite a list = 背诵一个清单
recite the facts = 陈述事实
recite one's lines = 背诵台词
