gambled:/ˈɡæmbəld/ | v. | 赌博；冒险；孤注一掷 | To bet money on a game of chance or on a future event; to risk something valuable on the outcome of something uncertain.  | 例句：He gambled away all his savings. 他把所有的积蓄都赌掉了。 | 例句：She gambled on the success of her new business. 她孤注一掷，押宝在她新开的生意上。 | 近义词：bet, wager, risk, speculate | 反义词：save, conserve, secure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gambles every weekend. = 他每个周末都赌博。
They gamble on the stock market. = 他们在股市上投机。

一般过去时:
I gambled on the horse race yesterday. = 我昨天赌马了。
She gambled and lost everything. = 她赌博输掉了所有东西。

现在完成时:
He has gambled away his inheritance. = 他把继承的遗产都赌掉了。
They have gambled their future on this project. = 他们把未来押在这个项目上了。

过去完成时:
He had gambled his entire fortune before he realized his mistake. = 他在意识到自己的错误之前已经赌光了他的全部财产。
She had gambled and lost several times before she quit. = 在她放弃之前，她已经赌博输了好几次了。

将来时:
I will gamble on the next race. = 我会在下一场比赛中赌博。
They will gamble their savings on a new house. = 他们将用积蓄去赌一把买新房子。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would gamble. = 如果我有更多钱，我会去赌博。
I wish I hadn't gambled away my money. = 我真希望我没有把钱赌掉。

被动语态:
A large sum of money was gambled away at the casino. = 大笔的钱在赌场被赌掉了。
The future of the company is being gambled on a risky investment. = 公司的未来正押在一项高风险的投资上。

疑问句:
Did you gamble last night? = 你昨晚赌博了吗？
Have they ever gambled before? = 他们以前赌博过吗？

否定句:
He doesn't gamble anymore. = 他不再赌博了。
She didn't gamble on that risky venture. = 她没有在那项冒险的投资上赌博。


PHRASE->
gamble away = 赌掉
gamble on = 赌…；冒险…
high-stakes gamble = 高风险赌博
to gamble with = 拿…冒险
a calculated gamble = 精心策划的冒险
