/ɔːk/ | n. | 后果，结果 | The consequences of his actions were severe.  他的行为造成了严重的后果。|近义词：result, effect, outcome |反义词：cause, origin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the consequence of this decision? = 这个决定的后果是什么？
What are the consequences of global warming? = 全球变暖的后果是什么？

一般过去时:
What were the consequences of the war? = 那场战争的后果是什么？
What was the consequence of his mistake? = 他犯错的后果是什么？

现在完成时:
What have been the consequences of his actions? = 他行为的后果是什么？
What consequences have resulted from the policy change? = 政策改变带来了什么后果？


过去完成时:
What had been the consequences before the intervention? = 干预之前有什么后果？
What consequences had already occurred before the report was released? = 在报告发布之前已经发生了什么后果？

将来时:
What will be the consequences of this project? = 这个项目的 后果将会是什么？
What consequences will we face if we fail to act? = 如果我们不采取行动，我们将面临什么后果？

虚拟语气:
If we ignore the problem, the consequences could be disastrous. = 如果我们忽视这个问题，后果可能是灾难性的。
I wish I had known the consequences before I acted. = 我希望在我行动之前就知道后果。

被动语态:
The consequences were carefully considered by the committee. = 委员会仔细考虑了后果。
The consequences are being assessed by experts. = 专家们正在评估后果。

疑问句:
What are the consequences? = 后果是什么？
What will the long-term consequences be? = 长期后果将会是什么？

否定句:
There were no serious consequences. = 没有严重的后果。
The consequences were not as bad as we feared. = 后果没有我们担心的那么糟糕。


PHRASE->
in consequence = 因此
as a consequence = 因此
consequence of = ...的结果
with the consequence that = 结果是...
face the consequences = 面对后果
