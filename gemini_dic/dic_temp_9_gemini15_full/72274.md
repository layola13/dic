flossy:/ˈflɒsi/| adj.|华丽的，时髦的，蓬松的|Something that is flossy is showy, stylish, and often considered excessively luxurious or elegant.  It can also refer to something fluffy or soft.|例句：Her flossy dress was the highlight of the party.  她的华丽礼服是派对的亮点。

例句：The cat's flossy tail looked incredibly soft.  那只猫蓬松的尾巴看起来非常柔软。


近义词：stylish, showy, luxurious, plush, fluffy

反义词：plain, simple, unadorned, drab


词性:形容词
