monembryony:/ˌmɒnɪˈmbrɪəni/| n. |单胚胎发生|The development of a single embryo from a single fertilized ovum.  This is the typical mode of development in most animals.  It contrasts with polyembryony, where multiple embryos develop from a single zygote.|Example:  Monembryony is the usual reproductive process in humans, resulting in one offspring per pregnancy.|近义词:single embryo development|反义词:polyembryony
