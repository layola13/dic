rarity:/ˈrɛərɪti/| n. |稀有；罕见；珍品 |Something that is rare; an uncommon thing.  It can also refer to the state or quality of being rare.|The discovery of a new species of bird is a rarity.  发现一个新的鸟类物种是一件罕见的事。 The stamp collection contains several rarities. 这套邮票收藏包含几件珍品。|近义词：scarcity, infrequency, exception |反义词：abundance, commonness, prevalence
