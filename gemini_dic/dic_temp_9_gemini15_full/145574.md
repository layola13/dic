overcomplicate:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmplɪkeɪt/ | vt. |使过于复杂；使复杂化 | To make something more complicated than necessary.  | 例句：Don't overcomplicate the instructions; keep them simple and clear. 不要把说明弄得太复杂；保持简单明了。 | 例句：He overcomplicated the solution to a simple problem. 他把一个简单问题的解决方案搞得太复杂了。|近义词： complicate,  convolute,  embroil |反义词： simplify, clarify
