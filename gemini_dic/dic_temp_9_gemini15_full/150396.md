panoramic:/ˌpænəˈræmɪk/| adj.| 全景的；全景式的 |  Giving a wide view; encompassing a wide range of vision.  Describes a view that is wide and impressive, encompassing a large area. | Example: The panoramic view from the mountaintop was breathtaking.  山顶的全景令人叹为观止。  The hotel boasts panoramic ocean views. 这家酒店拥有全景海景。|近义词：wide-ranging, sweeping, comprehensive | 反义词：limited, restricted, narrow
