implosion:/ɪmˈploʊʒən/| n. |内爆；崩溃；瓦解 | The sudden and violent collapsing of something inwards, especially a building or structure.  It often refers to a dramatic inward collapse, unlike an explosion which is outward.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe the rapid decline or failure of something. |例句：The old building suffered an implosion after a controlled demolition.  旧楼在受控爆破后发生了内爆。

The company experienced a rapid implosion following the scandal. 这家公司在丑闻后经历了迅速的崩溃。|近义词：collapse, disintegration, downfall |反义词：explosion, expansion
