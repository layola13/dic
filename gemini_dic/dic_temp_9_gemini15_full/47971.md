decile:/ˈdesʌɪl/| n. |十分位数，十分位 |A decile is any of the nine values that divide the sorted data into ten equal parts, such that each part represents 10% of the sample or population.  Deciles are often used in statistics to summarize and describe the distribution of data. For example, the second decile represents the value below which 20% of the data falls. | The second decile of test scores was 75.  测试成绩的第二十分位数是75分。 The data set's ninth decile was exceptionally high.  该数据集的第九十分位数异常高。 | percentile, quartile |  | noun
