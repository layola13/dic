retelegraphing:/ˌriːˈtɛləɡræfɪŋ/| v.| 再电报；重复发电报 | Sending a telegram again, typically to ensure its receipt or to correct an error.  |例句：Due to the poor connection, they had to retelegraph the urgent message. 由于连接不良，他们不得不再次发送紧急电报。

例句：The company decided to retelegraph the order to clarify the quantity. 公司决定重新发电报以澄清数量。


近义词：resending, retransmitting, relaying


反义词：deleting, cancelling


词性：动词
