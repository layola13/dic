repatroled:/ˌriːpəˈtroʊld/| v.|再次巡逻|To patrol again or repeatedly.|The police repatroled the area after the incident.|近义词：re-patrolled, patrolled again |反义词：abandoned, neglected


USAGE->

He patrols the building regularly. = 他定期巡逻大楼。
