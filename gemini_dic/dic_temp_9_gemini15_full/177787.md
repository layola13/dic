receipted:/rɪˈsiːptɪd/ | adj. | 已开收据的；已付清款项的 | Having a receipt; for which a receipt has been given.  | The receipted invoice shows the payment was made on time.  已开收据的发票显示款项已按时支付。| paid, acknowledged | unpaid, unacknowledged | 形容词


USAGE->

The invoice is receipted. = 发票已开具收据。
This item is receipted. = 这件商品已付清款项。
