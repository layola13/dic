shrinkingly:/ˈʃrɪŋkɪŋli/| adv.| 畏缩地；逐渐减小地；越来越小地 | In a way that shows fear, shyness, or unwillingness; gradually becoming smaller. |例句：He answered shrinkingly, avoiding eye contact. 他畏缩地回答，避免眼神接触。

She looked shrinkingly at the large dog. 她畏缩地望着那条大狗。


近义词：timidly, hesitantly, faintly, gradually | 反义词：boldly, confidently, assertively | 词性：副词
