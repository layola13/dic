epistemically:/ˌɛpɪstəˈmɪkli/| adv.|认知上地，认识论地|Relating to or involving epistemology; concerning knowledge or justified belief.|例句：Epistemically, the claim is questionable.  认知上，这个说法值得怀疑。  The argument is unsound epistemically.  论证在认识论上站不住脚。|近义词：cognitively, intellectually|反义词：empirically, practically
