/ˌpriːˈtɛstɪmoʊniz/ | n. | 预先证词；预审证词 |  Pretestimonies refers to statements or evidence given before a formal testimony, often during a preliminary investigation or pre-trial hearing.  These statements might be used to prepare for a later, more formal testimony, or to help determine the direction of the investigation or legal proceedings.  The term is not commonly used and is relatively rare.  It's a combination of "pre-" (before) and "testimonies." |  The police reviewed the pretestimonies before questioning the suspect.  警方在讯问嫌疑人之前审查了预先证词。


近义词: preliminary statements, prior statements, depositions  | 反义词:  final testimony, official testimony |  词性:名词
