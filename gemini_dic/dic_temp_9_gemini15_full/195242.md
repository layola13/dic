semiexternally: /ˌsemiɪkˈstɜːrnəli/ | adv. | 半外部地，部分外部地 |  Partially or to some extent externally; not completely internal or within.  It describes something that is partly outside or on the surface, but not entirely so. | The tumor was semiexternally located, making surgical removal more complex.  肿瘤部分位于外部，使得手术切除更加复杂。| partially, outwardly | internally, completely internally | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense changes are not applicable.  The usage is dependent on the context of the sentence.


Example 1:
The device is semiexternally powered, drawing some energy from an internal battery and some from an external source.  该设备由半外部供电，部分能量来自内部电池，部分来自外部电源。

Example 2:
The pressure was applied semiexternally to the vessel, minimizing internal damage.  压力部分施加在容器外部，最大限度地减少内部损伤。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "semiexternally."  Its usage is primarily technical or scientific.
