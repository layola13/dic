ineffectual: /ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəl/ | adj. | 无效的；不起作用的；软弱无力的 | Not producing any significant or desired effect.  |例句：His attempts to help were ineffectual. 他的帮助尝试是无效的。
His leadership proved ineffectual in preventing the company's decline. 他的领导力在阻止公司衰落方面证明是无效的。 | 近义词：useless, ineffective, fruitless, futile, vain | 反义词：effective, successful, potent, powerful | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine is ineffectual against this virus. 这药对这种病毒无效。
His efforts are ineffectual in solving the problem. 他的努力在解决这个问题上是无效的。

一般过去时:
Her pleas were ineffectual; he didn't listen. 她的恳求是无效的；他没听。
The previous attempts to fix the leak were ineffectual.  之前堵漏的尝试都是无效的。

现在完成时:
All his efforts have been ineffectual so far. 到目前为止，他所有的努力都无效。
The negotiations have proved ineffectual in reaching an agreement.  谈判未能达成协议，证明是无效的。

过去完成时:

His earlier attempts to contact her had been ineffectual. 他先前联系她的尝试都失败了。

将来时:
Their protests will likely be ineffectual. 他们的抗议很可能无效。
Further attempts to persuade him will be ineffectual.  进一步说服他的尝试将是无效的。

虚拟语气:
If his strategy had been more effective, it wouldn't have been ineffectual. 如果他的策略更有效，就不会无效。
I wish my complaints hadn't been so ineffectual. 我真希望我的抱怨没有那么无效。

被动语态:
The new policy was deemed ineffectual by many. 许多人认为这项新政策无效。
His arguments were considered ineffectual by the judge.  法官认为他的论点无效。

疑问句:
Is this approach ineffectual? 这种方法无效吗？

否定句:
