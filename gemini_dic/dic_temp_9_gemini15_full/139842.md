octopoid:/ˈɒktəpɔɪd/|adj.|章鱼似的，八腕类的|Resembling an octopus; relating to or characteristic of octopuses.  The term often refers to cephalopods with eight arms, although not all such creatures are true octopuses.|例句：The octopoid creature had a remarkable ability to camouflage itself.  这个章鱼似的生物具有非凡的伪装能力。


例句：The researcher described the newly discovered species as octopoid in nature. 研究人员将新发现的物种描述为章鱼似的。


近义词：octopus-like, eight-armed


反义词：无


词性:形容词
