pipistrelle:/ˌpɪpɪˈstrɛl/ | n. |蝙蝠（一种小型蝙蝠）|A pipistrelle is a small bat belonging to the genus Pipistrellus.  They are common in many parts of the world and are characterized by their relatively small size and high-pitched calls. |Example:  I saw a pipistrelle flitting around the garden last night.  昨晚我看到一只蝙蝠在花园里飞舞。|近义词：bat (general term)|反义词：无
