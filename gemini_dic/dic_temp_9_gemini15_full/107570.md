landlubbers:/ˈlændˌlʌbərz/| n. |陆地人；不谙海事的人|People unfamiliar with the sea or ships; those who spend their lives on land.  They are often depicted as clumsy or inexperienced at sea. |例句：The sailors laughed at the landlubbers struggling to tie a knot. 水手们嘲笑那些笨手笨脚地系绳结的陆地人。

例句：The seasoned captain easily spotted the landlubbers among the crew. 经验丰富的船长很容易就能从船员中认出陆地人。


近义词：landsman, greenhorn, novice, amateur


反义词：sailor, seaman, mariner, seafarer
