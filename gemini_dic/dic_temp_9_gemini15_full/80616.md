goose:/ɡuːs/| n.|鹅|A goose is a large water bird with a long neck, short legs, and webbed feet.  Geese are known for their honking calls and often live in flocks.|例句：The geese flew south for the winter.  鹅们飞往南方过冬了。
The farmer raised geese for their eggs and meat. 农民养鹅是为了它们的蛋和肉。|近义词：gander (雄鹅)|反义词：None.

词源:
The word "goose" comes from the Old English word "gōs," which is ultimately of Proto-Germanic origin.  It's related to words in other Germanic languages such as German "Gans" and Dutch "gans."
