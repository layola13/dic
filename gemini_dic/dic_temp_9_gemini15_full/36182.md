/kɔːfr/ | n. |保险箱；行李箱；(汽车的)引擎盖|A strong box or container for valuables, typically one that is fireproof and requires a key or combination to open.  It can also refer to a trunk of a car or a compartment for storing things. |例句：He kept his important documents in the coffre. 他把重要的文件放在保险箱里。

例句：The coffre of the car was full of luggage.  汽车的行李箱里装满了行李。

近义词：strongbox, safe, trunk, boot (UK), hood (US - car engine compartment) |反义词：open container, unprotected storage.  |词性: 名词

词源:
The word "coffre" is derived from the Old French word "cofre," meaning "chest" or "trunk."  This ultimately comes from the Latin word "coffer," also meaning "chest" or "box."
