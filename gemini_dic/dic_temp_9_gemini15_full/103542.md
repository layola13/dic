joggler:/ˈdʒɑːɡlər/ | n. | 耍弄杂耍的人，杂耍演员 | A person who juggles.  A juggler performs tricks by throwing and catching several objects at once. They often use balls, clubs, rings, or other items.  | Example: The circus juggler amazed the audience with his skill. 马戏团的杂耍演员以其精湛的技艺令观众惊叹。 | 近义词：juggler, equilibrist | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
