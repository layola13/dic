mucific:/mjuːˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. | 粘液性的，产生粘液的 | Producing or containing mucus.  The term refers to substances or tissues that secrete or are composed of mucus, a viscous fluid secreted by mucous membranes. | Example: The mucific glands in the respiratory tract help to trap inhaled particles.  呼吸道中的粘液腺有助于捕获吸入的颗粒物。|近义词：mucous, slimy, viscid | 反义词：dry
