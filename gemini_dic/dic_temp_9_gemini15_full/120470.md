mins:/mɪnz/|abbr.|分钟|minutes的缩写，表示时间单位|例句：The meeting will last 15 mins.  会议将持续15分钟。|近义词：minutes|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting lasts 30 mins. = 会议持续30分钟。
It takes 10 mins to walk there. = 走过去需要10分钟。


一般过去时:
The film lasted 90 mins. = 电影持续了90分钟。
It took us 20 mins to find a parking space. = 我们花了20分钟才找到一个停车位。


现在完成时:
The meeting has lasted for over 60 mins. = 会议已经持续了60多分钟。
We have waited for over 30 mins. = 我们已经等了30多分钟了。


过去完成时:
The movie had already started 15 mins before we arrived. = 我们到达之前电影已经开始了15分钟。
They had waited for an hour before the bus finally arrived. = 公交车最终到达之前，他们已经等了一个小时。


将来时:
The meeting will last approximately 45 mins. = 会议将持续大约45分钟。
It will take about 15 mins to get to the airport. = 到达机场大约需要15分钟。


虚拟语气:
If the meeting lasted only 10 mins, we could have finished earlier. = 如果会议只持续10分钟，我们就能早点结束。
I wish the journey had taken less than 30 mins. = 我希望旅程不到30分钟。


被动语态:
The task was completed in 20 mins. = 任务在20分钟内完成。
The concert was shortened to 60 mins. = 音乐会缩短到60分钟。


疑问句:
How many mins are left? = 还剩几分钟？
How long will this process take in mins? = 这个过程需要几分钟？


否定句:
The meeting did not last more than 30 mins. = 会议没有持续超过30分钟。
It didn't take me long, just a few mins. = 我没花很长时间，仅仅几分钟。


PHRASE->
5 mins break = 5分钟的休息
10 mins walk = 10分钟的路程
several mins ago = 几分钟前
a few mins later = 几分钟后
in mins = 分钟内
