nonradiating: /ˌnɒnˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非辐射的；不辐射的 | Not emitting radiation.  This term describes something that does not produce or emit electromagnetic radiation.  | Example: The nonradiating antenna design minimizes interference.  非辐射天线设计最大限度地减少了干扰。


近义词: radiationless, non-emissive


反义词: radiating, emissive


词性: adjective
