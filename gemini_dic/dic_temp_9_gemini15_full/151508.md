parrotize:/ˈpærətaɪz/| vt.|鹦鹉学舌；模仿；|to repeat something someone else has said or written without understanding it:|He parrotized the teacher's words without really understanding them. 他鹦鹉学舌般地重复老师的话，并没有真正理解。|近义词：mimic, imitate, echo|反义词：originate, innovate|动词
