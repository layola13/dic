ridger:/ˈrɪdʒər/| n.|山脊；山梁；脊状物|A ridger is a long, narrow, raised strip of land, like the top of a hill or mountain. It can also refer to something that resembles a ridge in shape or function.  |例句：The farmer used a ridger to create furrows in the field.  这个农民用犁沟器在田里开沟。|近义词：crest, ridge, spine|反义词：valley, depression
