preinfection: /ˌpriːɪnˈfɛkʃən/ | n. |感染前，感染期前 |The period or state before infection occurs; the conditions existing before infection.  It often refers to the host's state or the environment before a pathogen establishes itself. |例句：Researchers studied the preinfection immune status of the patients. 研究人员研究了患者感染前的免疫状况。
例句：The preinfection environment played a significant role in the severity of the outbreak. 感染前的环境对疫情的严重程度起到了重要作用。|近义词：antecedent, precursor |反义词：postinfection
