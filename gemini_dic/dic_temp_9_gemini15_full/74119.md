foulard:/fuːˈlɑːr/| n. |丝巾；方巾；印花绸|A foulard is a lightweight silk scarf or square of fabric, often with a printed pattern.  It can also refer to the fabric itself.  The term is derived from the French word for "silk cravat".|She wore a beautiful silk foulard around her neck.|scarf, headscarf|—|
