interobserver:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvər/| n. |观察者间|Referring to the agreement or consistency between multiple observers making independent judgments or observations of the same phenomenon or event.  It often relates to the reliability and validity of research data, especially in qualitative studies.|例句：High interobserver reliability is crucial for ensuring the validity of the study.  观察者之间高度的一致性对于确保研究的有效性至关重要。


例句：The study demonstrated excellent interobserver agreement on the coding of behavioral responses. 这项研究表明，对行为反应的编码具有极好的一致性。


近义词：observer agreement, inter-rater reliability


反义词：interobserver disagreement, low reliability


词性:名词
