overgenerous: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəs/ | adj. | 过分慷慨的，过于大方的 | Excessively generous; giving or offering more than is necessary or reasonable.  |  例句：His overgenerous donation depleted his savings. 他过于慷慨的捐款耗尽了他的积蓄。  | 近义词：extravagant, prodigal, munificent, lavish | 反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overgenerous with his time. = 他对时间过于慷慨。
She is always overgenerous to her friends. = 她对朋友总是过于大方。


一般过去时:
He was overgenerous in his assessment of the situation. = 他对局势的评估过于乐观。
They were overgenerous with the holiday gifts. = 他们送的节日礼物过于大方。


现在完成时:
He has been overgenerous in his spending lately. = 他最近花钱过于大方。
She has been overgenerous with her praise. = 她赞扬得过于溢美。


过去完成时:
They had been overgenerous with their promises. = 他们许诺得过于大方。
He had been overgenerous with his time, neglecting his own work. = 他对时间过于慷慨，忽略了自己的工作。


将来时:
He will be overgenerous with the rewards. = 他将给予过多的奖励。
She will be overgenerous in her hospitality. = 她将过度热情好客。


虚拟语气:
If he were not overgenerous, he would have more money. = 如果他不那么慷慨，他就会有更多的钱。
I wish he were not so overgenerous with his money. = 我希望他不那么挥霍无度。


被动语态:
His generosity was considered overgenerous by some. = 有些人认为他的慷慨过头了。
The offer was deemed overgenerous by the recipient. = 收到的人认为这个提议过于慷慨。


疑问句:
Isn't he being overgenerous with his compliments? = 他是不是夸奖得太过分了？
Was she overgenerous with her assessment of the project? = 她对项目的评估是不是过于乐观了？


否定句:
He wasn't overgenerous with his criticism. = 他批评得并不算过分。
She wasn't overgenerous with her help; she only did what was necessary. = 她并没有过度帮忙，只做了必要的事情。
