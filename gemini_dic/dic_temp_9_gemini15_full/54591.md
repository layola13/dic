dismantlement:/dɪsˈmæntlmənt/ | n. | 拆卸；废除；瓦解 | The process of taking something apart, especially a machine or structure, or of gradually destroying a system or organization.  | 例句：The dismantling of the old factory began last week. 上周旧工厂的拆除工作开始了。

The dismantling of the Soviet Union was a significant historical event. 苏联的解体是一个重大的历史事件。

近义词：disassembly, demolition, destruction, breakdown | 反义词：assembly, construction, establishment
