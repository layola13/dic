retake:/ˌriːˈteɪk/| vt. n.|重做；重拍；重考|To take or do something again.|例句：I have to retake the math exam. 我必须重考数学考试。
例句：The director decided to retake the scene. 导演决定重拍这场戏。|近义词：redo, repeat, revise|反义词：finish, complete|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retakes the test every week. = 他每周都重考。
They retake the photos for the brochure. = 他们为宣传册重拍照片。


一般过去时:
She retook the exam and passed. = 她重考并通过了。
We retook the pictures because of the bad lighting. = 因为灯光不好，我们重拍了照片。


现在完成时:
He has retaken the driving test three times. = 他已经重考驾照三次了。
The photographer has retaken the portrait several times. = 摄影师已经重拍了这幅肖像好几次了。


过去完成时:
She had retaken the course before she finally understood it. = 在她最终理解之前，她已经重修了这门课。
They had retaken the footage, hoping to improve the quality. = 他们已经重拍了镜头，希望能提高质量。


将来时:
I will retake the exam next week. = 我下周将重考。
They will retake the photos tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上将重拍照片。


虚拟语气:
If I failed, I would retake the course. = 如果我考试不及格，我会重修这门课。
I wish I had retaken that photography class. = 我真希望我重修了那门摄影课。


被动语态:
The exam will be retaken by many students. = 许多学生将重考。
The scene was retaken due to a technical error. = 由于技术错误，场景被重拍。


疑问句:
Will you retake the exam? = 你会重考吗？
Did they retake the scene? = 他们重拍了那个场景吗？


否定句:
He did not retake the test. = 他没有重考。
They will not retake the photos. = 他们不会重拍照片。
