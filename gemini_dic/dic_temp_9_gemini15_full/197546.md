shelled:/ʃeld/ | adj. v. |去壳的；炮击的；受炮击的 |  Having had its shell removed; subjected to shelling. |例句：The shelled peanuts were delicious.  剥了壳的花生真好吃。

The city was shelled heavily during the war.  战争期间，这座城市遭到猛烈的炮击。 |近义词：husked, shelled out(付钱), bombarded |反义词：unshelled, intact


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shells the peas for dinner. 他在为晚餐剥豌豆。
She shells corn every Sunday. 她每个星期天都剥玉米。


一般过去时:
I shelled the nuts last night. 我昨晚剥了坚果。
They shelled the beach with artillery. 他们用炮火轰击了海滩。


现在完成时:
She has shelled all the corn. 她已经剥完了所有的玉米。
The army has shelled the enemy positions.  军队已经炮击了敌人的阵地。


过去完成时:
He had shelled the peanuts before the party started. 派对开始前他已经剥好了花生。
The town had been shelled repeatedly before the ceasefire. 停火前小镇已经被多次炮击。


将来时:
I will shell the beans tomorrow. 我明天会剥豆子。
They will shell the city if negotiations fail. 如果谈判失败，他们将炮击这座城市。


虚拟语气:
If I had shelled the peas earlier, dinner would be ready. 如果我早点剥豌豆，晚餐就准备好了。
If they had not shelled the village, many lives would have been saved. 如果他们没有炮击村庄，许多生命就能得救。


被动语态:
The peanuts were shelled by hand. 花生是手工剥壳的。

疑问句:
