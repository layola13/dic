monogrammous:/mɒnəˈɡræməs/ | adj. | 单字母的；使用单字母标志的 | Having or using a monogram; characterized by a monogram.  The adjective describes something that features or uses a single letter, typically an initial, as a symbol or logo. | Example:  Her stationery was monogrammous, featuring a large, elegant "M".  她的文具上印有醒目的优雅的“M”字母，是单字母的。 |近义词：initialed, monogrammed |反义词：polygrammous (this word is not commonly used)
