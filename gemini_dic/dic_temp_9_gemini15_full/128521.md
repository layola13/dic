newts:/nuːts/| n. |蝾螈|A newt is a type of amphibian that resembles a lizard but has smooth, moist skin and typically lives in or near water.  They are characterized by their slender bodies, long tails, and four limbs.|例句：The children found a newt in the garden pond.孩子们在花园池塘里发现了一只蝾螈。|近义词：eft, salamander|反义词:  None specifically.
