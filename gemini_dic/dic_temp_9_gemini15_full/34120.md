clacks:/klæks/| v.|咔哒咔哒作响，发出啪嗒声|The sound of repeated, sharp, clicking noises.|The old wooden chair clacked as he sat down.  老木椅在他坐下时发出咔哒声。The typewriter clacked away, producing a steady rhythm. 打字机咔哒咔哒地响着，发出稳定的节奏。|click, rattle, snap|silent, quiet|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine clacks constantly. 这台机器一直在咔哒作响。
The shoes clack on the pavement. 鞋子在人行道上发出咔哒声。

一般过去时:
The door clacked shut. 门啪嗒一声关上了。
The hammer clacked against the metal. 锤子敲击金属发出啪嗒声。

现在完成时:
The machine has clacked all day. 这台机器咔哒作响了一整天。
The old woman has clacked her knitting needles for hours. 老妇人已经咔哒咔哒地织针织活好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
The clock had clacked twelve times before I fell asleep. 我睡着之前，钟已经敲了十二下。
The birds had clacked their beaks before flying away. 鸟儿们在飞走之前已经敲击它们的喙。

将来时:
The train will clack along the tracks. 火车将沿着铁轨咔哒咔哒地行驶。
The machine will clack loudly when it starts. 机器启动时会发出很大的咔哒声。

虚拟语气:
If the machine clacked louder, I would notice it. 如果机器咔哒声更大，我会注意到。
I wish the machine didn't clack so much. 我希望机器不要发出这么大的咔哒声。


被动语态:
The sound was clacked out by the machine.  声音是由机器发出的咔哒声。
The rhythm was clacked out on the old typewriter. 节奏是用旧打字机发出的咔哒声敲出来的。


疑问句:
Does the machine always clack like that? 这台机器总是这样咔哒作响吗？

否定句:
