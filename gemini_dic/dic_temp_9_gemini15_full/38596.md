concorporation:/kɒnˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/| n. |合并，联合，公司合并|The act of incorporating or uniting into one body; the state of being incorporated; also, the body so formed.  It specifically refers to the merging of two or more corporations into a single entity.|Example: The concorporation of the two companies resulted in a stronger market presence.  这家公司的合并使其在市场上拥有更强的竞争力。|近义词：merger, amalgamation, consolidation |反义词：separation, division, dissolution|名词
