flatteries:/ˈflætəriːz/| n. |奉承；谄媚；恭维|  Flatteries are insincere expressions of praise intended to win favor or gain something.  They are often excessive and exaggerated. |例句：He showered her with flatteries in an attempt to win her over. 他用甜言蜜语试图讨好她。|例句：His flatteries were transparent and easily seen through. 他的奉承很虚伪，很容易识破。|近义词：praise, compliments, adulation, blandishments|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure|名词
