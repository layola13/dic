democrats:/ˈdɛməkræts/| n. |民主主义者；民主党人 |Members of the Democratic Party, a major political party in the United States.  The term can also refer to those who advocate for democratic principles and systems of government.| 例句：Many Democrats support policies aimed at social justice.  许多民主党人支持旨在实现社会正义的政策。

The Democrats won the majority in the House of Representatives. 民主党赢得了众议院的多数席位。


近义词：Democrats (as a group):  the Democratic Party, progressives (often associated, but not always synonymous)


反义词：Republicans (in the US context)


词性: 名词
