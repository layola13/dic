czarists:/ˈzɑːrɪsts/ | adj. |沙皇的；沙皇制度的 | Relating to or characteristic of the czars or their rule.  The word refers to the period of Russian history when the country was ruled by czars, typically associated with autocratic rule and often implying oppression. | Example: The czarist regime was overthrown in the Russian Revolution. 沙皇政权在俄国革命中被推翻了。 |近义词：imperial, autocratic, tyrannical |反义词：democratic, republican
