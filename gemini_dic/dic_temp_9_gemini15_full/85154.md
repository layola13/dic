hardtail:/ˈhɑːrteɪl/| n.|硬尾（自行车）|A hardtail bicycle is a mountain bike with a rigid rear triangle (the part of the frame connecting the rear wheel to the seat tube).  Unlike full-suspension bikes, it lacks rear suspension. This makes it lighter and simpler, but less comfortable on rough terrain.|Example:  He prefers a hardtail mountain bike for its efficiency on climbs.|近义词：rigid bike|反义词：full-suspension bike|名词
