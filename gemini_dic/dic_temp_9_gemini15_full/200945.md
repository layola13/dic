slit:/slɪt/| n./v.|狭缝；裂缝；切开；撕开|A narrow opening or cut; to make a narrow opening or cut in something.|例句：There was a slit in the curtain. 窗帘上有一条缝。 He slit open the envelope. 他割开了信封。|近义词：crack, gap, tear, cut|反义词：mend, repair|名词/动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slits the envelope carefully. 他小心地划开信封。
The slit in the fabric is barely visible. 布料上的裂缝几乎看不出来。


一般过去时:
She slit the bag open to check the contents. 她割开袋子检查里面的东西。
The old man slit his wrists. 老人割腕了。


现在完成时:
The wind has slit the sail. 风把帆撕裂了。
They have slit open the package. 他们已经拆开了包裹。


过去完成时:
The thief had slit the lock before entering the room. 窃贼在进房间前已经撬开了锁。（此处 slit 用法略有不同，但可理解为“切开”）
She had slit the fabric before realizing her mistake.  在她意识到错误之前已经剪坏了布料。


将来时:
He will slit the throat of the chicken. 他将割鸡的喉咙。
The surgeon will slit the skin to perform the operation. 外科医生将切开皮肤进行手术。


虚拟语气:
If I slit the bag, I could get the keys. 如果我把包割开，我就能拿到钥匙。
I wish I hadn't slit the painting.  我希望我没有划破那幅画。


被动语态:
The envelope was slit open by the postman. 信封被邮递员划开了。
The fabric was slit by a sharp object. 布料被尖锐的物体割破了。


疑问句:
Has the package been slit open? 包裹被划开了吗？
Did he slit the rope? 他割断绳子了吗？


否定句:
He didn't slit the paper. 他没有撕开纸。
She wouldn't slit her own throat. 她不会自杀的。


PHRASE->
eye slit: 眼裂
a slit skirt: 开衩裙
a slit lamp: 裂隙灯
vent slit: 通风口
drain slit: 排水口
