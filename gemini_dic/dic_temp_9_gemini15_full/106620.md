kulturkampf:/ˈkʊltʊɐˌkampf/| n. |文化斗争，文化冲突 |A cultural struggle or conflict, especially one involving religion and politics.  It typically refers to a conflict between secular and religious forces over the role of religion in public life.|例句：The Kulturkampf in 19th-century Germany saw fierce clashes between the Catholic Church and the state.  十九世纪德国的文化斗争见证了天主教会与国家之间激烈的冲突。|近义词：cultural conflict, ideological struggle, religious conflict |反义词：cultural harmony, religious tolerance
