overpained:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnd/| adj. |过度痛苦的，极度痛苦的|extremely pained; suffering intensely from grief or distress.|例句：She was overpained by the loss of her beloved pet. 她因失去心爱的宠物而悲痛欲绝。
例句：The overpained expression on his face revealed the depth of his sorrow. 他脸上痛苦的表情显露出了他悲伤的程度。|近义词：agonized, heartbroken, distressed|反义词：joyful, elated, cheerful
