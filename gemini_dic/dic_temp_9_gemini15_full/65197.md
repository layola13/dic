ethylene:/ˈɛθɪliːn/ | n. |乙烯 |A colorless flammable gas, the simplest alkene, used extensively in the chemical industry to produce plastics and other organic compounds. It is also a plant hormone that regulates various aspects of plant growth and development.  |Example: Ethylene is used to ripen fruits artificially. 乙烯被用于人工催熟水果。|近义词：ethene |反义词：无
