mesquite:/məˈskiːt/| n. |牧豆树；墨斯基特树|A mesquite is a thorny shrub or small tree of the legume family, common in arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and Mexico.  It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh conditions and its edible pods. |Example: The desert landscape was dotted with mesquite trees.  沙漠景观点缀着牧豆树。 The rancher used mesquite wood for his fence posts.  农场主用牧豆树木做篱笆柱。|近义词：acacia (some species)|反义词：无|名词
