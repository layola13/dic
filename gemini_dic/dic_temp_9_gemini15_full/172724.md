quacks:/kwæk/| n. v.|江湖郎中；骗子；鸭叫声；发出嘎嘎声 |A quack is someone who pretends to have medical skills or knowledge but does not.  It can also refer to the sound a duck makes.|例句：He was exposed as a quack doctor after several patients died under his care.  他被揭露为江湖郎中，在他诊治下几名病人死亡。|近义词：charlatan, imposter, fraud|反义词：expert, professional, legitimate|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ducks quack loudly in the morning. = 鸭子早上大声嘎嘎叫。
He often quacks about his medical expertise. = 他经常吹嘘他的医疗专业知识。

一般过去时:
