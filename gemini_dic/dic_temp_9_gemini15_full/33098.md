christeners:/ˈkrɪsənərz/| n.|施洗者；为…举行洗礼的人|People who christen someone or something.|The christeners of the new ship threw a bottle of champagne against its hull.|近义词：baptizers|反义词：N/A

一般现在时:
The priest christens the baby. = 牧师为婴儿施洗。
They christen their new boat "Sea Serpent". = 他们为他们的新船命名为“海蛇”。


一般过去时:
My parents christened me John. = 我的父母给我取名约翰。
The captain christened the ship with a bottle of champagne. = 船长用香槟酒为船举行洗礼仪式。


现在完成时:
They have christened their twins this morning. = 他们今天早上为双胞胎举行了洗礼仪式。
We have christened our new software "Project X". = 我们已经将我们的新软件命名为“X 项目”。


过去完成时:
Before the ceremony, they had christened the baby in a private ceremony. = 在仪式之前，他们已经私下为婴儿举行了洗礼。
The ship had been christened weeks before its maiden voyage. = 这艘船在首航几周前就已经举行过洗礼仪式了。


将来时:
They will christen their daughter next Sunday. = 他们将在下周日为女儿举行洗礼仪式。
The company will christen its new product at the trade show. = 公司将在贸易展上推出其新产品。


虚拟语气:
If they christened the baby today, we could celebrate. = 如果他们今天为婴儿施洗，我们就可以庆祝了。
I wish they had christened the ship before the storm. = 我希望他们在暴风雨前就为船举行了洗礼仪式。


被动语态:
