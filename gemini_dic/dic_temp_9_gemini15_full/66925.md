expended:/ɪkˈspɛndɪd/ | adj. | 花费的；用尽的；扩展的 |  Past participle of "expend".  Describes something that has been used up, spent, or extended.  It can refer to resources, energy, or time. | 例句：He expended all his energy on the project. 他把所有的精力都花在了这个项目上。

例句：The company expended considerable resources on the new marketing campaign.  这家公司在新的营销活动上花费了大量的资源。


近义词：spent, used up, depleted, disbursed, expanded


反义词：conserved, saved, retained, unexpended


词性：形容词 (past participle used adjectivally)


词源:

From Middle English *expendyd, from Old French *expendit, past participle of *expendre, from Latin expendere "to weigh out," from ex- "out" + pendere "to weigh, to hang."  The modern sense of "to spend" developed later.
