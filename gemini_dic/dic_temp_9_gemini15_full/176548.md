re-segregate:/ˌriːˈseɡrɪɡeɪt/| vt. |重新隔离，再次种族隔离 |To separate again into groups, especially by race.  This term usually refers to the act of reinstating racial segregation after a period of integration. |例句：After decades of progress, some fear that society might re-segregate.几十年进步之后，一些人担心社会可能会重新隔离。

例句：The new housing policy risks re-segregating the community. 新的住房政策有使社区再次种族隔离的风险。

近义词：re-separate, isolate, divide (by race)

反义词：integrate, desegregate, unify
