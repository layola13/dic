chickenshits: /ˈtʃɪkənʃɪts/ | n. adj. |胆小鬼；懦夫；胆小的|

英文解析:A chickenshit is a cowardly person, someone who lacks courage or is easily intimidated.  The term is highly informal and considered vulgar or offensive.  It combines "chicken," a slang term for a coward, with "shit," a vulgar term of contempt.

例句:
He's such a chickenshit, he wouldn't even stand up to his boss. 他真是个胆小鬼，连老板都不敢顶撞。

He acted like a total chickenshit when faced with the challenge. 面对挑战时，他表现得像个十足的懦夫。


近义词: coward, wimp, sissy, poltroon

反义词: brave, courageous, valiant, bold

词性: noun, adjective
