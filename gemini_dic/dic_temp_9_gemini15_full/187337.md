rockish:/ˈrɒkɪʃ/| adj.|略带摇滚乐风格的；有点像岩石的|Slightly resembling rock music; somewhat like rock.|例句：His style is a bit rockish, with ripped jeans and a leather jacket. 他的风格有点摇滚，穿着破洞牛仔裤和皮夹克。  例句：The terrain was rockish and difficult to traverse. 地形崎岖难行。|近义词：rocky, rock-like, slightly rebellious|反义词：smooth, refined, polished
