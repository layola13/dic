lathed:/leɪðd/| v. |车削；旋车| To shape (a piece of wood or metal) on a lathe.  |例句：He lathed the wood into a smooth, elegant curve. 他用车床把木头车削成一个光滑优雅的曲线。

例句：The mechanic lathed the metal part to the precise specifications. 机械师将金属部件车削到精确的规格。


近义词：turned, shaped, machined |反义词：None directly, but words implying opposite actions like "hammered" or "cast" could be considered contextual opposites. | 动词
