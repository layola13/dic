sensitiveness:/ˈsɛnsɪtɪvnəs/| n. |敏感性；敏感；易激动|The quality or state of being sensitive; the ability to be affected by external stimuli or influences.  It can refer to physical sensations, emotions, or perceptions.  |例句：Her sensitiveness to criticism made her withdraw from social interactions.  她的批评敏感性使她退出了社交互动。

例句：The high sensitiveness of the equipment requires careful handling.  该设备的高灵敏度需要小心操作。

近义词：sensitivity, susceptibility, responsiveness, delicacy|反义词：insensitivity, callousness, numbness
