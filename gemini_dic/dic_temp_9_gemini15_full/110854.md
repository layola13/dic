lintz:/lɪnts/| n. |棉绒；细绒毛；|  Lint is a fluffy substance that is produced by wearing fabrics.  It is often found in clothes dryers and washing machines. |例句：The dryer was full of lint. = 干衣机里全是棉绒。

例句：I removed the lint from my clothes. = 我把衣服上的棉绒清理掉了。

近义词：fluff, down, fuzz | 反义词：无
