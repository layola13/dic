nosebanded: /ˈnoʊzˌbændɪd/ | adj. | 鼻带的；有鼻带的 | Having a band or mark across the nose.  This term is primarily used to describe birds, but can be applied to other animals with similar markings. | Example: The nosebanded parrot is easily identified by the distinctive dark band across its beak.  这条鼻带鹦鹉很容易通过其喙上的独特深色条纹来识别。| 近义词: banded, marked | 反义词: unbanded
