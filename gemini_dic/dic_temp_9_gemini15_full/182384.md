renegades:/ˈrenɪɡeɪdz/| n.|叛徒，背叛者，逃亡者|A renegade is a person who deserts a cause, group, or principle.  They are often considered traitors or outlaws.  The term implies a deliberate and often dramatic break from established norms or allegiances.|例句：The renegade soldier fled to the enemy lines.  这个叛变的士兵逃到了敌人的阵线。

例句：Several members of the gang turned renegades and helped the police.  该帮派中的几名成员变成了叛徒，并帮助了警方。|近义词：traitor, deserter, defector, outlaw|反义词：loyalist, conformist|名词
