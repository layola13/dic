It can also refer to the act of binding or fastening something with the hand.  |例句：They celebrated their handfast with a small gathering of friends and family. = 他们与一些朋友和家人一起庆祝他们的订婚仪式。

They handfasted the two pieces of wood together. = 他们用手把两块木头捆绑在一起。|近义词：betrothal, marriage, engagement, union|反义词：separation, divorce
