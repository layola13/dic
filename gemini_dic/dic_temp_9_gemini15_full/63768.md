epicondyle:/ˈɛpɪkɒndaɪl/| n.|肱骨髁上突；踝上突|A rounded prominence at the end of a bone, located above the condyle.  It serves as an attachment point for muscles and ligaments.|例句：The tennis player suffered an injury to his lateral epicondyle, commonly known as tennis elbow.|近义词：condyle, process, eminence|反义词：None specifically.
