high-security:/ˈhaɪ sɪˈkjʊrɪti/ | adj. | 高安全性，高度安全 |  Describes something with a very high level of security measures in place to protect against unauthorized access, theft, or damage.  It implies a robust system designed to minimize risks. | Example: The high-security prison held some of the country's most dangerous criminals.  这个戒备森严的监狱关押着该国一些最危险的罪犯。  | 近义词：tightly guarded, heavily secured, well-protected | 反义词：insecure, unprotected, vulnerable | 形容词
