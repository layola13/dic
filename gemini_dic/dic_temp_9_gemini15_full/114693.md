malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| v.|装病；装病逃避责任| The act of pretending to be ill or injured in order to avoid work or duty.  It often involves exaggerating symptoms or inventing illnesses altogether. |例句：He was accused of malingering to avoid deployment to the war zone. 他被指控装病以逃避前往战区的部署。|例句：The doctor suspected the patient of malingering after finding no evidence of illness.  医生在找不到任何疾病证据后怀疑病人装病。|近义词：feigning illness, shirking,  skulking|反义词：diligence, industriousness
