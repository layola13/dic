reswelled:/riːˈswɛld/| v.|再次膨胀；重新肿胀|To swell again; to become swollen again.|例句：The injured knee reswelled after the strenuous activity. 受伤的膝盖在剧烈运动后又肿了起来。
The river reswelled after the heavy rainfall.  大雨过后，河水再次上涨。|近义词：swell again, re-inflate|反义词：deflate, shrink
