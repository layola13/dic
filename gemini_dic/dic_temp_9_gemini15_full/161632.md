pounce's:/paʊns/| n.|猛扑；突然袭击|The act of pouncing, a sudden spring or attack.|The cat pounced on the mouse. 猫猛扑过去抓老鼠。|近义词：leap, spring, swoop|反义词：retreat, withdraw, back off


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat pounces on the mouse. = 猫扑向老鼠。
He pounces on every opportunity. = 他抓住每一个机会。


一般过去时:
The cat pounced on the mouse. = 猫扑向老鼠。
She pounced on the mistake in his report. = 她发现了报告中的错误。


现在完成时:
The cat has pounced on several mice today. = 猫今天已经抓了好几只老鼠。
He has pounced on the chance to travel abroad. = 他抓住了出国旅行的机会。


过去完成时:
The cat had pounced on the mouse before I could intervene. = 我还没来得及干预，猫就已经扑向老鼠了。
She had pounced on the opportunity before anyone else. = 在其他人之前，她抓住了这个机会。


将来时:
The cat will pounce on the mouse when it moves. = 老鼠一动，猫就会扑上去。
He will pounce on any weakness in his opponent's argument. = 他会抓住对手论证中的任何弱点。


虚拟语气:
If the cat saw a mouse, it would pounce. = 如果猫看到老鼠，它就会扑过去。
If I had known about the opportunity, I would have pounced. = 如果我知道这个机会，我就会抓住它。


被动语态:
The mouse was pounced upon by the cat. = 老鼠被猫扑住了。
The opportunity was quickly pounced upon by the ambitious young man. = 那个雄心勃勃的年轻人迅速抓住了这个机会。


疑问句:
Will the cat pounce? = 猫会扑过去吗？
Did he pounce on the chance? = 他抓住机会了吗？


否定句:
The cat did not pounce. = 猫没有扑过去。
He didn't pounce on the opportunity. = 他没有抓住机会。
