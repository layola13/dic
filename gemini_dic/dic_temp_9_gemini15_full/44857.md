crusty:/ˈkrʌsti/| adj.|粗糙的；硬邦邦的；脾气暴躁的；（食物）烤得外皮酥脆的|Covered with or resembling a crust; hard, rough, or brittle.  Also refers to a person who is grumpy or bad-tempered.|例句：The bread was crusty and delicious. 这面包又脆又好吃。

The old man was crusty and unwilling to help.  这位老人脾气暴躁，不愿意帮忙。|近义词：rough, hard, brittle, grumpy, surly, cantankerous|反义词：smooth, soft, pliable, pleasant, amiable
