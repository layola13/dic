haberdashers:/ˈhæbədæʃərz/ | n. |男装用品店；帽饰品店；经营男装用品的商人 | A haberdasher is a shop that sells men's clothing and accessories, or a person who owns or works in such a shop.  The term is now somewhat archaic, but it is used to evoke a sense of old-fashioned charm and traditional craftsmanship. |例句：He bought new buttons at the haberdasher's. 他在男装用品店买了新的纽扣。|例句：My grandfather was a haberdasher, known for his fine suits. 我的祖父是一位男装用品店的店主，以他精良的西装而闻名。|近义词：clothier, tailor, outfitter |反义词：无
