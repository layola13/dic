gracefully:/ˈɡreɪsfʊli/| adj. |优雅地，优美地 |In a graceful manner; elegantly.  The dancer moved gracefully across the stage.  |近义词：elegantly, smoothly, gracefully, effortlessly |反义词：awkwardly, clumsily, ungracefully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She gracefully accepts compliments. = 她优雅地接受赞美。
He gracefully handles difficult situations. = 他优雅地处理棘手的情况。

一般过去时:
The ballerina gracefully performed the pirouette. = 芭蕾舞演员优雅地完成了旋转动作。
He gracefully bowed to the audience. = 他优雅地向观众鞠躬。

现在完成时:
She has gracefully aged over the years. = 她这些年来优雅地老去。
He has gracefully retired from his position. = 他优雅地从他的职位上退休了。

过去完成时:
She had gracefully conceded defeat before the final results were announced. = 在最终结果公布之前，她优雅地承认了失败。
He had gracefully accepted the award with a humble speech. = 他以谦逊的演讲优雅地接受了奖项。

将来时:
She will gracefully navigate the challenges ahead. = 她将优雅地应对未来的挑战。
He will gracefully transition into retirement. = 他将优雅地过渡到退休生活。

虚拟语气:
If she were more graceful, she would dance more beautifully. = 如果她更优雅，她就会跳得更漂亮。
I wish I could move as gracefully as a swan. = 我希望我能像天鹅一样优雅地移动。

被动语态:
The problem was gracefully solved by the team. = 这个问题被团队优雅地解决了。
The difficult situation was gracefully handled by the mediator. = 棘手的情况被调解人优雅地处理了。

疑问句:
Did she gracefully accept the criticism? = 她优雅地接受了批评吗？
Can he gracefully navigate social situations? = 他能优雅地应对社交场合吗？

否定句:
He didn't gracefully handle the rejection. = 他没有优雅地处理拒绝。
She didn't gracefully exit the room. = 她没有优雅地离开房间。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Old French *gracieusement, from gracieux (graceful), from Latin gratia ("grace, favor, thanks").  The "-ly" suffix is an adverbial ending indicating manner.
