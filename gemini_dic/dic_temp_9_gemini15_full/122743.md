moistens: /ˈmɔɪsnz/ | vt. | 使湿润；使潮湿 | To make something slightly wet.  |例句：The rain moistens the dry earth. 雨水滋润着干旱的土地。

The gentle mist moistens the leaves. 轻柔的薄雾使树叶湿润。|近义词：dampen, wet, humidify |反义词：dry, desiccate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain moistens the ground. = 雨水使地面湿润。
She moistens her lips before speaking. = 她说话前润了润嘴唇。


一般过去时:
He moistened the sponge with water. = 他用水把海绵弄湿。
The gardener moistened the newly planted seedlings. = 园丁给新种的幼苗浇了水。


现在完成时:
The prolonged drought has not moistened the parched land. = 持续的干旱并没有使干涸的土地湿润。
She has moistened the tea leaves before brewing. = 她泡茶前先把茶叶润湿了。


过去完成时:
He had moistened the brush before applying the paint. = 他在涂漆之前已经把刷子弄湿了。
They had moistened the soil to make it easier to plant. = 他们已经把土壤弄湿，以便更容易种植。


将来时:
The approaching storm will moisten the fields. = 即将来临的风暴将会使田地湿润。
I will moisten the cake with syrup. = 我要用糖浆把蛋糕弄湿。


虚拟语气:
If it rained, it would moisten the parched land. = 如果下雨，它会使干旱的土地湿润。
I wish the rain would moisten my wilting plants. = 我希望雨能滋润我枯萎的植物。


被动语态:
The soil was moistened by the recent rainfall. = 土壤被最近的降雨湿润了。
The cake was moistened with a sweet liqueur. = 蛋糕用甜酒浸湿了。


疑问句:
Does the humidifier moisten the air enough? = 加湿器能使空气足够湿润吗？
Will this lotion moisten my dry skin? = 这款乳液能滋润我的干燥肌肤吗？


否定句:
The dry air did not moisten the plants. = 干燥的空气没有使植物湿润。
The desert sun did not moisten the cracked earth. = 沙漠的阳光并没有使龟裂的土地湿润。

词源:
The word "moistens" comes from the Old French word "moisten," which in turn is derived from the Latin word "mollis," meaning "soft."  The suffix "-en" is a verb-forming suffix.
