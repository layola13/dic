redisbursed:/ˌriːdɪsˈbɜːrsd/| v.|重新分配，再分配|To distribute again or anew; to pay out again.  It implies a previous distribution that is being altered or repeated. |例句：The funds were redisbursed to different departments based on revised needs.  资金根据修订后的需求重新分配给了不同的部门。

例句：The company redisbursed the bonuses after a calculation error was discovered. 公司在发现计算错误后重新分配了奖金。


近义词：redistribute, reallocate, reallot|反义词：concentrate, centralize
