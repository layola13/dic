pentecostalisms:/ˌpɛntɪˈkɒstəlɪzəm/|/n.|五旬节教派；五旬节主义|The collective body of Pentecostal churches and denominations worldwide.  It refers to a diverse range of Christian movements characterized by a belief in spiritual gifts, such as speaking in tongues (glossolalia), divine healing, and prophecy.  |例句：The growth of Pentecostalisms in Africa is remarkable.  非洲五旬节教派的增长令人瞩目。|近义词：Pentecostalism (singular), charismatic movement|反义词：mainstream Christianity (in some contexts)
