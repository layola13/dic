cassoulet:/ˈkæsʊleɪ/| n. |法国香肠炖菜|A rich, slow-cooked French stew typically containing various meats (such as duck, sausage, pork), beans, and herbs.  It's a hearty and flavorful dish, often served in a large earthenware pot. |This cassoulet is incredibly flavorful and tender. 这道法国香肠炖菜非常美味可口。|  stew, ragout|  (None readily available)|名词
