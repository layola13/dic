rebuttal:/rɪˈbʌtəl/| n. |反驳；驳斥 |A refutation or contradiction.  It is a strong and forceful response to an argument or accusation, aiming to prove it wrong.|  例句：His rebuttal of the accusations was convincing. 他的反驳令人信服。

例句：The lawyer presented a powerful rebuttal to the prosecution's case.  律师对检方的陈述提出了强有力的反驳。


近义词：refutation, counterargument, denial, rejoinder |反义词：confirmation, corroboration, affirmation |词性：名词
