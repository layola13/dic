reconsidered:/ˌriːkənˈsɪdəd/| v.|重新考虑，再考虑|To think again about a decision, plan, or opinion.|例句：After reconsidering the offer, she decided to accept it.  重新考虑了这个提议后，她决定接受它。|例句：He reconsidered his hasty decision to quit his job. 他重新考虑了他仓促辞职的决定。|近义词：re-evaluate, review, rethink|反义词：finalize, confirm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconsiders his options regularly. = 他定期重新考虑他的选择。
She reconsiders her position on the matter. = 她重新考虑她在这个问题上的立场。

一般过去时:
They reconsidered their travel plans. = 他们重新考虑了他们的旅行计划。
I reconsidered my initial assessment. = 我重新考虑了我最初的评估。

现在完成时:
She has reconsidered her decision. = 她已经重新考虑了她的决定。
He has reconsidered the proposal several times. = 他已经多次重新考虑了这个提案。

过去完成时:
They had reconsidered the contract before signing. = 他们签字之前已经重新考虑了合同。
I had reconsidered my options before making a choice. = 我在做出选择之前已经重新考虑了我的选择。

将来时:
She will reconsider her application. = 她将重新考虑她的申请。
We will reconsider the matter at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上重新考虑此事。

虚拟语气:
If I had reconsidered earlier, I would have made a better decision. = 如果我早点重新考虑，我会做出更好的决定。
I wish I had reconsidered the project before starting. = 我希望我在开始之前就重新考虑过这个项目。

被动语态:
The proposal was reconsidered by the committee. = 这个提案被委员会重新考虑了。
The matter will be reconsidered at a later date. = 此事将在稍后日期重新考虑。

疑问句:
Did you reconsider your offer? = 你重新考虑你的提议了吗？
Will they reconsider the plan? = 他们会重新考虑这个计划吗？

否定句:
He did not reconsider his stance on the issue. = 他没有重新考虑他在这个问题上的立场。
They did not reconsider their decision, despite the new evidence. = 尽管有新的证据，他们也没有重新考虑他们的决定。
