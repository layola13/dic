incautious:/ɪnˈkɔːʃəs/| adj. |不谨慎的；轻率的；鲁莽的 |lacking caution; careless; rash.  It suggests a lack of forethought and consideration of potential consequences. |例句：His incautious remarks offended many people. 他不谨慎的言论冒犯了许多人。

例句：Her incautious handling of the situation led to a major crisis. 她处理事情不谨慎，导致了严重的危机。


近义词：rash, reckless, careless, imprudent, heedless, thoughtless


反义词：cautious, careful, prudent, wary, circumspect


词源：in- (否定前缀) + cautious (谨慎的)  The word derives from the Latin word "cautus," meaning "careful" or "wary," with the prefix "in-" adding the sense of negation.
