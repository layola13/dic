furlers:/ˈfɜːrlərz/ | n. |卷帆器，卷篷器 | A device used to furl (roll up) a sail or awning.  Furlers are commonly used on sailboats to make handling sails easier and faster. They typically consist of a drum or cylinder around which the sail is wrapped, and a mechanism for winding the sail onto the drum. | Example: The boat was equipped with a headsail furler for efficient sail handling.  船上配备了头帆卷帆器，以便高效地操纵帆。  The awning furler made it easy to protect the patio furniture from rain.  遮阳篷卷篷器使保护庭院家具免受雨淋变得很容易。 | 近义词：sail furling system, sail wrapping mechanism | 反义词：unfurler (a device for unfurling)
