darkening:/ˈdɑːrkənɪŋ/| v. |变暗；使变暗 |The process of becoming darker or causing something to become darker.  It can refer to a gradual decrease in light or brightness, or the act of making something darker. |例句：The sky is darkening. 天色渐暗。

The room was darkening as the sun set.  日落时，房间逐渐暗了下来。


近义词：dimming, shading, obscuring |反义词：brightening, lightening |动词
