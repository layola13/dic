inventious: /ɪnˈvɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 富有创造力的；有发明才能的；爱发明创造的 |  Characterized by inventiveness; showing originality and imagination.  |  His inventious mind constantly churned out new ideas.  他的富有创造力的头脑不断产生新的想法。  She is known for her inventious approach to problem-solving. 她以其富有创造性的解决问题的方法而闻名。 | creative, innovative, imaginative, resourceful | unimaginative, uncreative, conventional


近义词：creative, innovative, imaginative, resourceful
反义词：unimaginative, uncreative, conventional

词源:  Derived from the word "invention," ultimately from the Latin "inventio," meaning "discovery" or "finding." The suffix "-ious" adds an adjectival quality, signifying "full of" or "characterized by."
