reconcilement:/rɛkənˈsaɪlmənt/ | n. |和解；调和；一致 |The act of reconciling; the state of being reconciled.  It implies the restoration of friendly relations after a quarrel or disagreement, or the bringing of conflicting elements into harmony. |例句：After years of estrangement, there was a heartfelt reconciliation between the brothers.  多年疏远后，兄弟俩终于衷心和解了。

例句：The reconciliation of their differing viewpoints proved to be a challenging task. 调和他们不同的观点被证明是一项具有挑战性的任务。

近义词：reconciliation, appeasement, settlement, agreement |反义词：estrangement, discord, conflict, disagreement |词性：名词
