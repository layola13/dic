double-deckers:/ˈdʌbəl ˈdɛkərz/ | n. |双层巴士 | A double-decker is a bus or other vehicle with two levels or decks for passengers.  They are commonly used in cities for public transportation. | Example:  London is famous for its red double-decker buses.  伦敦以其红色的双层巴士而闻名。  I saw a double-decker bus carrying many tourists. 我看到一辆双层巴士载着许多游客。 | 近义词: double-decker bus, two-story bus | 反义词: single-decker bus
