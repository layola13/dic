nonsocieties: /ˌnɒn.səˈsaɪ.ə.tiz/ | n. |非社会群体；非社会组织|Groups or organizations that exist outside of or in opposition to established societal norms and structures.  They often lack formal organization and may be characterized by unconventional behavior or beliefs.  The term emphasizes the absence of integration into mainstream society, not necessarily a negative connotation. | Example:  Countercultural movements can be considered nonsocieties, operating outside the dominant culture.  反文化运动可以被认为是非社会群体，它们存在于主流文化之外。|近义词:subcultures, countercultures, fringe groups |反义词: mainstream societies, established institutions |名词
