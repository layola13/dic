mispense:/mɪsˈpɛns/| v.|分发错误，误发；错误分配|The act of dispensing something incorrectly or inappropriately, often referring to medication.  It implies an error in the process of distribution, leading to an incorrect dose, wrong medication, or to the wrong patient.|例句：The pharmacist admitted to a mispense of the patient's medication. 药剂师承认给病人开了错药。  The hospital investigated a series of mispenses in the oncology ward. 医院调查了肿瘤科的一系列用药错误。|近义词：misadministration, misapplication, error|反义词：correct dispensing, accurate distribution|动词
