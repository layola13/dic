rushiest:/ˈrʌʃiɪst/| adj.|最匆忙的；最急促的；最繁忙的|Most hurried or rushed; characterized by great haste or urgency.  The word is the superlative form of "rushy."|例句：The rushiest time of day at the airport is always just before boarding.|The rushiest period for the post office is usually during the Christmas season.|近义词：most hurried, most frantic, most hectic|反义词：most leisurely, most relaxed, most calm
