graph's: /ɡræfs/ | n. | 图表的；图形的 |  The possessive form of "graph," indicating belonging to or relating to a graph.  It refers to something associated with or characteristic of a graph, such as its properties, data, or representation. | Example:  The graph's axes clearly showed the trend.  图表坐标轴清晰地显示了趋势。 |近义词: chart's, diagram's | 反义词: N/A


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It's used to show possession or relationship.  Therefore, standard verb tenses don't apply.
