copyists:/ˈkɑːpiɪsts/| n. |抄写员，复制者|People who make copies of documents, manuscripts, or other written materials.  They are often skilled in calligraphy or other forms of precise writing.|The ancient copyists meticulously reproduced religious texts.|近义词：scribes, transcribers,  imitators|反义词：originators, authors
