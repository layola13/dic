manikins:/ˈmænɪkɪnz/| n. |人体模型；小木偶|A manikin is a small model of a human being, often used for display in clothing stores or for medical or artistic purposes.  It can also refer to a small puppet.|例句：The department store used manikins to display its new line of winter coats.  这家百货公司用人体模型展示其新款冬衣。|The artist used a manikin to study the human anatomy.这位艺术家用人体模型来研究人体解剖学。|近义词：dummy, model, figure|反义词:无
