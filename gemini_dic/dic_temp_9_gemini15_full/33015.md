choroid:/ˈkɔːrɔɪd/ | n. |脉络膜 |The choroid is the vascular layer of the eye, located between the retina and the sclera. It provides oxygen and nutrients to the outer layers of the retina.  |例句：Damage to the choroid can result in vision loss. 脉络膜损伤会导致视力下降。|例句：The choroid's rich blood supply is crucial for retinal function. 脉络膜丰富的血液供应对视网膜功能至关重要。|近义词：choriocapillaris |反义词：无
