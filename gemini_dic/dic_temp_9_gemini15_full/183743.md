reschedules:/ˌriːˈskedʒuːlz/| v. |重新安排时间 |To change the scheduled time of (something).  It implies a pre-existing schedule that is being altered. |例句：They reschedules the meeting for next week. = 他们将会议重新安排到下周。

例句：We had to reschedule our trip due to the storm. = 由于暴风雨，我们不得不重新安排行程。


近义词：replan, rearrange, postpone, defer

反义词：confirm, keep, maintain


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reschedules appointments frequently. = 他经常重新安排约会。
She reschedules her flights whenever necessary. = 她根据需要重新安排她的航班。

一般过去时:
They rescheduled the concert because of the rain. = 由于下雨，他们重新安排了音乐会。
The company rescheduled the launch of the product. = 公司重新安排了产品的发布。

现在完成时:
We have rescheduled the meeting three times. = 我们已经三次重新安排了会议。
She has rescheduled her vacation plans. = 她已经重新安排了她的假期计划。

过去完成时:
They had rescheduled the trip before the hurricane hit. = 在飓风来临之前，他们已经重新安排了行程。
He had rescheduled the meeting before realizing the conflict. = 他在意识到冲突之前已经重新安排了会议。

将来时:
We will reschedule the game if it rains. = 如果下雨，我们将重新安排比赛。
They will reschedule the interview for a later date. = 他们将把面试重新安排到以后的日期。

虚拟语气:
If I had known earlier, I would have rescheduled the appointment. = 如果我早知道，我就已经重新安排约会了。
I wish I had rescheduled my flight. = 我希望我已经重新安排了我的航班。

被动语态:
The meeting was rescheduled due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，会议被重新安排了。
The flight was rescheduled because of mechanical problems. = 由于机械故障，航班被重新安排了。

疑问句:
Will the meeting be rescheduled? = 会议会被重新安排吗？
Should we reschedule the presentation? = 我们应该重新安排演示吗？

否定句:
They did not reschedule the meeting. = 他们没有重新安排会议。
We cannot reschedule the event at this late stage. = 在这个后期阶段，我们无法重新安排活动。


PHRASE->
reschedule a meeting = 重新安排会议
reschedule a flight = 重新安排航班

reschedule a trip = 重新安排旅行
reschedule a deadline = 重新安排截止日期

词源:
re- (prefix indicating again) + schedule (from Old French *escedule, from Medieval Latin *schedula, diminutive of Latin scheda "leaf of paper," from Greek skhédē)
