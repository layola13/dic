rearguments:/ˌriːˈɑːrɡjumənts/| n. | 再辩论，再次论证 | Repeated arguments or discussions, often after a previous decision or judgment has been made.  It implies a renewed consideration of a previously discussed topic. | Example: The court granted rearguments in the case due to newly discovered evidence.  法院由于发现了新的证据而允许再次辩论此案。|近义词：reconsideration, review, rehearing | 反义词：final decision, conclusion
