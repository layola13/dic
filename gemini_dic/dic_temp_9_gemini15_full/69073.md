faults:/fɔːlts/| /fɔːlts/ | n. |缺点；故障；错误；过失 |Flaws or imperfections in a person, thing, or system.  Faults can refer to minor imperfections or serious flaws depending on the context. |例句：The car has several mechanical faults. 这辆车有几个机械故障。

例句：He admitted his faults and apologized. 他承认了他的错误并道歉了。


近义词：defects, flaws, imperfections, shortcomings, blemishes |反义词：perfections, strengths, virtues |名词
