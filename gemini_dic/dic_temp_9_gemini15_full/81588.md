greases:/ˈɡriːsiːz/| n. |润滑脂，油脂 |A thick, oily substance used to lubricate machinery.  |例句：The mechanic used grease to lubricate the engine. 机械师用润滑脂润滑发动机。|例句：The gears were coated in thick grease. 齿轮上涂满了厚厚的润滑脂。|近义词：lubricant, oil, fat |反义词：dryness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine requires regular greasing. = 机器需要定期润滑。
He greases his car's engine every 3,000 miles. = 他每行驶3000英里就给汽车发动机润滑。

一般过去时:
I greased the hinges to make them move more smoothly. = 我给铰链涂了润滑脂，使它们转动更顺畅。
She greased the wheels of her bike before the race. = 比赛前她给自行车的车轮上了油脂。

现在完成时:
We have greased all the bearings. = 我们已经给所有轴承上了润滑脂。
They have greased the moving parts of the machine. = 他们已经给机器的活动部件上了润滑脂。

过去完成时:
He had greased the bicycle chain before the long ride. = 长途骑行前，他已经给自行车链条上了油脂。
The mechanic had greased the engine before starting it. = 机械师启动发动机之前已经给它上了润滑脂。

将来时:
I will grease the car's chassis tomorrow. = 我明天将给汽车底盘上油脂。
They will grease the equipment before the next operation. = 他们将在下次操作前给设备上油脂。

虚拟语气:
If I had greased the door hinges, they wouldn't squeak. = 如果我给门铰链上了润滑脂，它们就不会吱吱作响。
I wish I had greased the wheels before the journey. = 我真希望在出行前给车轮上过油脂。

被动语态:
The engine was greased by the mechanic. = 发动机是由机械师润滑的。
The moving parts need to be greased regularly. = 活动部件需要定期润滑。

疑问句:
Have you greased the bicycle chain? = 你给自行车链条上油脂了吗？
Does the machine require frequent greasing? = 这台机器需要经常润滑吗？

否定句:
He didn't grease the gears properly. = 他没有正确地给齿轮上润滑脂。
I haven't greased the hinges yet. = 我还没给铰链上油脂。

词源:
The word "grease" comes from the Old French word "graisse," meaning "fat" or "grease."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "crassus," meaning "thick" or "fat."
