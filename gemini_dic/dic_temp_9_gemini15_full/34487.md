claymore:/ˈkleɪmɔːr/| n.|克莱莫尔剑，大砍刀|A claymore is a type of large, two-handed sword, historically associated with Scotland.  It's characterized by its length and broad blade, often with a fuller (a groove running the length of the blade to reduce weight).|The Highland warrior wielded his claymore with deadly skill.|近义词：greatsword, broadsword|反义词：dagger, short sword
