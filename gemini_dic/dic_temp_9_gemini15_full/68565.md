fangless:/ˈfæŋləs/| adj.|无牙的，温和的，无效的|Lacking fangs; lacking the power to harm or intimidate.  Describes something that is harmless or ineffective due to a lack of potency or force.|例句：The snake was fangless and therefore posed no threat. 这条蛇没有毒牙，因此构不成威胁。

例句：His argument was fangless and unconvincing. 他的论点无力且缺乏说服力。


近义词：toothless, harmless, impotent, ineffective|反义词：fanged, venomous, potent, effective|词性：形容词
