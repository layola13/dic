heathier:/ˈhɛlθiər/| adj. |更健康的 |More healthy; possessing or exhibiting better health.  This word implies a comparative state, suggesting an improvement in health compared to a previous state or to someone else.|

例句：
He feels much healthier after quitting smoking.  戒烟后，他感觉健康多了。

She adopted a healthier diet and lifestyle. 她采用了更健康的饮食和生活方式。


近义词：healthier, more robust, more vigorous

反义词：unhealthier, sicker, weaker

词性：形容词
