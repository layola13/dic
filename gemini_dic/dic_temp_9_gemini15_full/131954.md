noncontributory:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/| adj. |不缴纳的；无贡献的；非供款的| Not contributing; not making a contribution.  This term often refers to a pension or retirement plan where the employer does not contribute funds.  |例句：This is a noncontributory pension plan, meaning the company pays the entire cost. 这是一项非供款养老金计划，这意味着公司支付全部费用。

例句：The study found that the noncontributory nature of the program led to lower participation rates. 研究发现，该计划的非供款性质导致参与率降低。


近义词：unfunded, gratuitous, free


反义词：contributory, funded
