congealers: /kənˈdʒiːlərz/ | n. |凝固剂；胶凝剂 |  A substance that causes a liquid to solidify or thicken.  Congealers are used in various industries, including food processing and cosmetics, to create desired textures and consistencies. | Example:  The chef added a congealer to the sauce to give it a thicker consistency.  厨师在酱汁中加入了凝固剂，使其浓稠。|近义词：thickener, solidifier, coagulant |反义词：thinners, liquefiers
