cuckoos:/ˈkʊkuːz/| n.|杜鹃鸟；（尤指）布谷鸟|The word "cuckoos" refers to birds of the family Cuculidae, known for their distinctive call and brood parasitism.  Many cuckoo species lay their eggs in the nests of other birds, leaving the host birds to raise their young. |例句：We heard the distinctive call of the cuckoos in the woods.我们在树林里听到了杜鹃鸟独特的叫声。

例句：Several cuckoo eggs were found in the robin's nest.在知更鸟的巢里发现了几个杜鹃蛋。


近义词：cuckoo (singular)|反义词：无
