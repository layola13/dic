prothorax: /ˈproʊθɔːræks/ | n. | 前胸 |

英文解析:The prothorax is the first segment of the thorax of an insect, located in front of the mesothorax and metathorax.  It typically bears the first pair of legs and may also bear wings in some insects.  Its structure varies greatly depending on the insect species and its lifestyle.

例句:
The prothorax of the beetle was heavily sclerotized, providing protection. = 甲虫的前胸高度硬化，提供了保护。

The researchers studied the prothorax's articulation with the head to understand insect locomotion. = 研究人员研究了前胸与头部之间的连接，以了解昆虫的运动方式。


近义词:  (There aren't direct synonyms, as it's a specific anatomical term.  Related terms could include:)  thorax segment, anterior thorax


反义词: mesothorax, metathorax (These are the other thoracic segments)


词性: noun
