rumaging: /ˈrʊmeɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 翻找；搜寻 |  To search unsystematically and thoroughly through something.  | 例句：She was rummaging in her handbag for her keys. 她正在翻找她的手提包找钥匙。  | 例句：He spent the morning rummaging through old boxes in the attic. 他上午花时间在阁楼上翻找旧箱子。 | 近义词：searching, hunting, ferreting | 反义词：organizing, tidying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rummages through his pockets every morning. = 他每天早上都在翻找他的口袋。
She rummages in her closet for a dress. = 她在衣橱里翻找一件衣服。

一般过去时:
I rummaged through the drawers looking for my passport. = 我翻遍了抽屉寻找我的护照。
They rummaged in the attic for old toys. = 他们在阁楼上翻找旧玩具。

现在完成时:
She has rummaged through all the boxes but still hasn't found it. = 她已经翻遍了所有的箱子，但仍然没有找到它。
He has rummaged in every cupboard, but the cake is missing. = 他翻遍了每个橱柜，但蛋糕不见了。

过去完成时:
I had rummaged through the entire house before I realized it was in my pocket. = 我翻遍了整个房子才意识到它在我的口袋里。
They had rummaged through the suitcase but found nothing valuable. = 他们翻遍了手提箱，但什么值钱的东西都没找到。

将来时:
I will rummage through the attic tomorrow to find some old photos. = 我明天将翻找阁楼寻找一些旧照片。
She will rummage through the boxes when she has time. = 她有时间的时候会翻找箱子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rummage through the library archives. = 如果我有更多时间，我会翻找图书馆档案馆。
I wish I could rummage through that antique shop. = 我真希望我能翻找那家古董店。

被动语态:
The drawers were rummaged through by the police. = 抽屉被警察翻找过了。
The attic hasn't been rummaged through for years. = 阁楼多年没有被翻找过了。

疑问句:
Are you rummaging in the trash? = 你在垃圾桶里翻找东西吗？
Was she rummaging in her purse when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候，她在钱包里翻找东西吗？

否定句:
He didn't rummage through my belongings without my permission. = 他没有未经我的允许翻找我的东西。
She doesn't usually rummage through other people's things. = 她通常不会翻找别人的东西。
