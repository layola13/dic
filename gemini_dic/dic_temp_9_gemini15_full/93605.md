imbeciles:/ˈɪmbəsɪlz/| n.|弱智者，傻瓜|A person who is considered to be stupid or foolish.|例句：He called them imbeciles for their foolish actions. 他称他们为傻瓜，因为他们的愚蠢行为。|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, simpleton|反义词：genius, intellectual, sage
