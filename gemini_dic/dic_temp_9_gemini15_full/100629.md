entirely:/ɪnˈtaɪəli/ | adv. | 完全地；彻底地；全部地 | Completely, totally, wholly; in every respect.  |  例句：I entirely agree with you. = 我完全同意你。

例句：The project was entirely successful. = 这个项目完全成功了。


近义词：completely, totally, wholly, fully, absolutely, perfectly


反义词：partially, incompletely, imperfectly


词性: 副词
