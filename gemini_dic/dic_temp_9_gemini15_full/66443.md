exemplars:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɑːrz/ | n. |典范；榜样；范例 |  Examples that are considered typical of a class or group.  They serve as models or illustrations of a particular quality or type. | The paintings of the Renaissance masters are exemplars of artistic skill.  文艺复兴时期大师的绘画是艺术技巧的典范。  | models, prototypes, paradigms, archetypes |  exceptions, opposites | 名词
