check-on:/tʃɛk ˈɑːn/ | vt. | 检查；查看；探望 | To make a brief inspection or examination of something or someone; to visit someone briefly to see how they are doing. | 例句：I'll check on the kids before I go to bed. 我睡前会去看看孩子们。  | 例句：The doctor will check on the patient later. 医生稍后会去查看病人。| 近义词：inspect, examine, visit, monitor | 反义词：ignore, neglect |  动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I check on my plants every morning. = 我每天早上都会检查我的植物。
She checks on her elderly mother regularly. = 她定期去看望她年迈的母亲。

一般过去时:
He checked on the car engine before driving. = 他开车前检查了汽车引擎。
They checked on their neighbors after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们去查看了邻居的情况。

现在完成时:
I have checked on the baby several times tonight. = 我今晚已经好几次去看婴儿了。
She has checked on the progress of the project. = 她已经检查了项目的进度。

过去完成时:
They had checked on the equipment before starting the experiment. = 他们在开始实验前已经检查过设备了。
He had checked on the patient's vital signs before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经检查了病人的生命体征。

将来时:
I will check on the situation tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会查看情况。
She will check on her friend after work. = 她下班后会去看望她的朋友。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would check on the children more often. = 如果我有时间，我会更经常地去看孩子们。
I wish I could check on him right now. = 我真希望我现在就能去看他。

被动语态:
The machine is checked on daily. = 这台机器每天都会被检查。
The patients are checked on regularly by the nurses. = 病人会被护士定期检查。

疑问句:
Will you check on the oven while I'm out? = 我不在的时候你会检查烤箱吗？
Have you checked on the order status? = 你检查过订单状态了吗？

否定句:
I didn't check on the mail today. = 我今天没有检查邮件。
They haven't checked on the results yet. = 他们还没有检查结果。
