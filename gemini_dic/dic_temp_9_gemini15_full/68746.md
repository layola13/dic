farmland:/ˈfɑːrmlænd/ | n. |农田，耕地 | Land used for farming, especially the land used for growing crops.  |例句：The farmland stretches as far as the eye can see. 这片农田一眼望不到边。 |例句：They bought a large tract of farmland to expand their business. 他们买下了一大片农田来扩大他们的业务。|近义词：cultivated land, cropland, arable land |反义词：wilderness, wasteland
