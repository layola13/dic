grassers:/ˈɡræsərz/| n. |食草动物|Animals that feed on grass and other plants.|例句：Cows and sheep are grazers.  奶牛和绵羊是食草动物。|例句：The African savanna is home to many large grazers. 非洲大草原是许多大型食草动物的家园。|近义词：herbivores, browsers|反义词：carnivores


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cows graze peacefully in the field. = 牛儿们在田野里安静地吃草。
Many animals graze on the plains. = 许多动物在平原上吃草。

一般过去时:
The deer grazed near the river yesterday. = 鹿昨天在河边吃草。
The sheep grazed all day long. = 绵羊整天都在吃草。

现在完成时:
The cattle have grazed this pasture all summer. = 牛群整个夏天都在这个牧场上吃草。
The goats have grazed all the grass. = 山羊已经把所有的草都吃光了。

过去完成时:
The horses had grazed in the meadow before the storm. = 马群在暴风雨来临之前已经在草地上吃草了。
The sheep had grazed most of the grass before the farmer arrived. = 在农民到来之前，羊群已经吃掉了大部分的草。

将来时:
The herd will graze in the valley tomorrow. = 牛群明天将在山谷里吃草。
The sheep will graze on the hillside next week. = 羊群下周将在山坡上吃草。

虚拟语气:
If the animals grazed more evenly, the pasture would be healthier. = 如果动物更均匀地吃草，牧场会更健康。
I wish the goats had grazed less near the house. = 我希望山羊在房子附近少吃点草。

被动语态:
The pasture was grazed heavily last month. = 上个月牧场被过度放牧了。
The field is being grazed by the sheep. = 田地正在被绵羊吃草。

疑问句:
Do the horses graze in this field? = 马匹在这个田野里吃草吗？
Where do the cattle graze? = 牛群在哪里吃草？

否定句:
The sheep didn't graze in the field yesterday. = 羊昨天没在这个田野里吃草。
The deer don't graze near the road anymore. = 鹿不再在路边吃草了。
