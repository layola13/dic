roof-rack:/ˈruːfræk/| n. |车顶行李架|A roof rack is a structure attached to the roof of a vehicle to carry luggage or other items.  It's typically made of metal bars or similar materials and secured to the roof using clamps or other fastening mechanisms. |例句：We need a roof rack to transport our camping gear. 我们需要一个车顶行李架来运输我们的露营装备。

例句：He secured the kayaks to the roof rack before driving to the lake. 他在开车去湖边之前把皮划艇固定在车顶行李架上。


近义词：roof carrier, luggage rack, car top carrier|反义词：无

词源:
The word "roof-rack" is a compound word.  "Roof" comes from Old English *hrūf, meaning "covering," and "rack" comes from Old Norse *rekkr, meaning a framework or structure.  Therefore, "roof-rack" literally means a structure on the roof.
