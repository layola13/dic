laggards:/ˈlæɡɑːrdz/| n. |落后者，慢郎中，行动迟缓者 |Someone who makes slow progress and falls behind others.  Laggards often struggle to keep up with the pace of change or demands of a particular situation. They may be reluctant to adapt or innovate, preferring familiar methods even when they are less efficient.  |The laggards in the race were quickly overtaken.  落后的人很快就被人超过了。The company is struggling to compete because of its laggards. 公司由于落后而难以竞争。| latecomers, stragglers, slowpokes | leaders, innovators, pioneers | 名词
