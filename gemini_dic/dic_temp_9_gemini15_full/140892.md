on-your-uppers:/ˌɒn jɔːr ˈʌpəz/ | adj. | 赤贫的；身无分文的 |  Completely without money; penniless.  This idiom describes someone who is extremely poor and has no resources.  | 例句：After losing his job, he was on his uppers and struggled to make ends meet.  = 丢了工作后，他穷困潦倒，难以维持生计。

He found himself on his uppers after a string of bad investments. =  一系列糟糕的投资后，他发现自己身无分文。


近义词：penniless, broke, destitute, impecunious, impoverished


反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
