mention:/ˈmenʃən/| vt. |提及，说起|To speak of; refer to briefly.|He mentioned that he was leaving soon. 他提到他很快就要离开了。|refer to, allude to, cite|ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mentions his trip to Paris every time we meet. 他每次见到我们都提到他去巴黎的旅行。
She mentions the importance of hard work in her speeches.  她在演讲中提到努力工作的重要性。


一般过去时:
He mentioned his new job during the conversation. 他在谈话中提到了他的新工作。
She mentioned seeing him at the store yesterday. 她提到昨天在商店看到他了。


现在完成时:
He has mentioned that problem several times. 他已经几次提到过那个问题。
She has mentioned her plans to travel to Japan. 她已经提到了她去日本旅行的计划。


过去完成时:
He had mentioned the deadline before the meeting. 他在会议前就已经提到了截止日期。
She had mentioned her concerns earlier that day.  她那天早些时候就提到了她的担忧。


将来时:
He will mention the issue in his next report. 他将在他的下一份报告中提到这个问题。
She will mention the project during the presentation. 她将在演示期间提到这个项目。


虚拟语气:
I wish he had mentioned the difficulties earlier. 我希望他早点提到这些困难。
If she had mentioned the problem sooner, we could have avoided it. 如果她早点提到这个问题，我们就可以避免它了。


被动语态:
The incident was mentioned briefly in the news.  新闻简要提到了这一事件。
His contribution was mentioned in the final report.  他的贡献在最终报告中被提及。


疑问句:
Did he mention anything about the meeting? 他有没有提到关于会议的事情？
Has she mentioned her new boyfriend? 她有没有提到她的新男友？


否定句:
He didn't mention anything about his family. 他没有提到任何关于他家人的事情。
She didn't mention seeing anyone suspicious. 她没有提到看到任何可疑的人。


PHRASE->
make mention of:  提及
worthy of mention: 值得一提
at the mention of: 一提到...
without mention of: 没有提及
a passing mention: 一笔带过
