castanets:/kæˈstænɛts/| n.|响板|A pair of small, shell-like percussion instruments, typically made of wood or plastic, that are clicked together in the hands to produce a rhythmic sound. They are often used in Spanish and other flamenco music.|The dancer clicked her castanets in time with the music.  舞者随着音乐的节奏敲响她的响板。|近义词：percussion instrument, clappers|反义词:None
