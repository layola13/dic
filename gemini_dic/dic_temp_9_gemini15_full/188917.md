rutate:/ruːˈteɪt/|v.|旋转；轮流；循环|To turn or cause to turn around a central point or axis.|例句：The Earth rotates on its axis. 地球绕其轴自转。|例句：The crops are rotated to maintain soil fertility. 作物轮作以保持土壤肥力。|近义词：spin, revolve, cycle|反义词：remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Earth rotates on its axis. = 地球绕其轴自转。
He rotates his crops every year. = 他每年轮换种植作物。


一般过去时:
The wheel rotated smoothly. = 轮子旋转得很平稳。
They rotated the responsibilities among the team members. = 他们将责任在团队成员之间轮换。


现在完成时:
The planet has rotated many times since its creation. = 自形成以来，这颗行星已经旋转了许多次。
The company has rotated its top executives frequently in recent years. = 近年来，该公司频繁轮换高层管理人员。


过去完成时:
The machine had rotated for hours before it broke down. = 机器运转了数小时后才发生故障。
They had rotated the tires before the long road trip. = 长途旅行之前，他们已经换过轮胎了。


将来时:
The moon will rotate around the Earth. = 月球将绕地球旋转。
We will rotate the shifts next week. = 我们下周将轮换班次。


虚拟语气:
If the Earth didn't rotate, we would have no day and night. = 如果地球不旋转，我们就不会有白天和黑夜。
I wish I could rotate my work schedule more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地轮换我的工作时间表。


被动语态:
The tire was rotated by the mechanic. = 轮胎被技工换了。
The responsibilities are rotated among the team members. = 责任在团队成员之间轮换。


疑问句:

否定句:
