frangipanis: /ˌfrænˈdʒɪpəniːz/ | n. |鸡蛋花，缅栀子 | Frangipanis are tropical flowering plants known for their intensely fragrant flowers.  They are often used in perfumes and other beauty products due to their sweet scent. The name comes from the French botanist Charles Plumier, who named the plant after a friend. | The frangipanis bloomed profusely, filling the air with their intoxicating aroma. 鸡蛋花盛开，空气中弥漫着令人陶醉的香气。

The frangipani tree in our garden is laden with beautiful flowers.  我们花园里的鸡蛋花树上开满了美丽的花朵。

近义词：plumeria (学名)
反义词：无
