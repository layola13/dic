probation:/proʊˈbeɪʃən/| n. |缓刑；试用期；考察期 | A period of time when someone who has committed a crime is allowed to stay out of prison if they behave well and meet certain conditions.  Also, a period of time when someone new is tested or observed to see if they are suitable for a job or other position.  |例句：He was given two years' probation. 他被判处两年缓刑。

例句：She's on probation at her new job.  她在新工作中处于试用期。


近义词：parole, trial period, testing period | 反义词：imprisonment, conviction, permanent employment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is on probation for drunk driving. = 他因酒后驾车正在缓刑。
The new employee is currently on probation. = 新员工目前处于试用期。


一般过去时:
He was on probation for a year. = 他缓刑一年。
She completed her probation successfully. = 她成功地完成了试用期。


现在完成时:
He has been on probation for six months. = 他缓刑六个月了。
She has successfully completed her probationary period. = 她已成功完成了试用期。


过去完成时:
He had already served his probation before the incident. = 在那件事发生之前，他已经完成了缓刑。
She had been on probation for a year before receiving a permanent position. = 在获得正式职位之前，她试用期已有一年了。


将来时:
He will be on probation for another six months. = 他还要缓刑六个月。
She will start her probation next week. = 她下周开始试用期。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't violated his probation, he wouldn't be back in jail. = 如果他没有违反缓刑规定，他就不会再次入狱。
If she had failed her probation, she would have lost the job. = 如果她试用期失败了，她就会失去这份工作。


被动语态:
He was put on probation after his conviction. = 他在定罪后被判缓刑。
The new hire was placed on probation for three months. = 新员工被安排了三个月的试用期。


疑问句:
Is he still on probation? = 他还在缓刑吗？
Is she still in her probationary period? = 她还在试用期吗？


否定句:
He was not on probation. = 他没有缓刑。
She was not placed on probation. = 她没有被安排试用期。



PHRASE->
