rehouses: /ˌriːˈhaʊzɪz/ | vt. |重新安置；为…提供住房 |To provide new housing for (someone who has lost their home).  |例句：The government is rehousing families affected by the flood. 政府正在为受洪水影响的家庭重新安置住房。

例句：The council is working hard to rehouse all the homeless people in the city.  市议会正在努力为城市中所有无家可归的人提供住房。


近义词：relocate, resettle, accommodate

反义词：evict, displace


词性: 动词
