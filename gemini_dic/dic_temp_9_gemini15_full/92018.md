hyperoxygenation:/ˌhaɪpərɒkˈsɪdʒəneɪʃən/ | n. |高氧合；过度氧合 |The process of increasing the oxygen concentration in the blood or tissues beyond normal levels.  It can be a therapeutic intervention or a consequence of certain medical conditions. |Example: Hyperoxygenation therapy is sometimes used to treat carbon monoxide poisoning. 高氧治疗有时用于治疗一氧化碳中毒。  The patient presented with symptoms of hyperoxygenation, including dizziness and nausea. 病人出现高氧合症状，包括头晕和恶心。|近义词：oversaturation, superoxygenation |反义词：hypoxia, anoxia |词性：名词
