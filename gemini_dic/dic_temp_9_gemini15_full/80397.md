golly:/ˈɡɑːli/| int.|哎呀，我的天哪|An exclamation expressing surprise, astonishment, or dismay.  It is considered a mild interjection.|例句：Golly, that's a big spider!  哎呀，那是一只大蜘蛛！

例句：Golly, I didn't expect that!  哎呀，我没想到！


近义词：Goodness, gee, wow, my goodness, oh my

反义词:None (interjections don't typically have antonyms)

词性:感叹词


USAGE->
Golly is an interjection, and as such, it doesn't conjugate like verbs or change form like nouns or adjectives.  It's used as a standalone expression.  Therefore, grammatical tenses, voice, or negation don't apply.

PHRASE->
Golly is rarely used in phrases beyond its basic exclamation form.  It doesn't commonly combine with other words to create set expressions.
