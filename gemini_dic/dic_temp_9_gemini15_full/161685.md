pouted:/paʊt/| v.|撅嘴，生气地不说话|To push out one's lips in a expression of annoyance, displeasure, or sulkiness.  |例句：The child pouted when he didn't get the toy he wanted.  孩子没得到想要的玩具，便撅起了嘴。|例句：She pouted for hours after her boyfriend broke up with her.  她和男朋友分手后，生气地撅着嘴好几个小时。|近义词：sulked, sulk, grumbled|反义词：smiled, grinned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pouts when he is unhappy. = 他不开心的时候会撅嘴。
She often pouts to get her way. = 她经常撅嘴来达到自己的目的。

一般过去时:
The baby pouted after his nap. = 婴儿小睡后撅起了嘴。
He pouted all afternoon after the argument. = 争吵后，他整个下午都撅着嘴。

现在完成时:
She has pouted all day because of the bad grade. = 因为考试不及格，她整天都撅着嘴。
He has pouted ever since he lost the game. = 自从输了比赛后，他就一直撅着嘴。

过去完成时:
He had pouted for hours before he finally agreed. = 他撅着嘴好几个小时后才最终同意。
She had pouted all morning before her mom gave in. = 她撅着嘴整个上午，她妈妈才让步。

将来时:
I think she will pout if she doesn't get the present. = 我想如果她没得到礼物，她会撅嘴。
He will probably pout if we don't invite him. = 如果我们不邀请他，他可能会撅嘴。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't pout so much. = 如果我是你，我就不会撅这么久的嘴。
I wish he wouldn't pout all the time. = 我希望他不要总是撅着嘴。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His unhappiness was shown by his pouting. = 他的不快乐表现在他撅嘴上。(不太自然)
The pout was clearly visible on her face. = 她脸上明显地撅着嘴。(更自然的被动表达)

疑问句:
Is she pouting again? = 她又在撅嘴吗？
Why is he pouting? = 他为什么撅着嘴？

否定句:
He didn't pout when he lost the race. = 他输了比赛并没有撅嘴。
She didn't pout; she just cried. = 她没有撅嘴，她只是哭了。


PHRASE->
pout one's lips = 撅起嘴唇
pout angrily = 生气地撅嘴
pout silently = 默默地撅嘴
pout and sulk = 撅嘴和生气
pout incessantly = 不停地撅嘴
