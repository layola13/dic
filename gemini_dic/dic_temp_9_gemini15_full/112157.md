look-in-to:/lʊkˈɪntuː/|/ˈlʊkɪntuː/| v. |调查；审查；探究|To investigate or examine something carefully.  It often implies a more thorough investigation than a simple "look at."|例句：The police are looking into the circumstances of the accident. 警方正在调查事故的经过。

例句： We need to look into the possibility of expanding our business. 我们需要研究一下扩大业务的可能性。


近义词：investigate, examine, inquire into, explore, probe

反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook

词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We look into the matter every week. = 我们每周都会调查此事。
They look into customer complaints promptly. = 他们迅速调查客户投诉。


一般过去时:
The committee looked into the issue last month. = 委员会上个月调查了这个问题。
She looked into the problem and found a solution. = 她调查了这个问题并找到了解决办法。


现在完成时:
Have you looked into the new software yet? = 你已经调查过新的软件了吗？
They have looked into the reasons for the delay. = 他们已经调查了延误的原因。


过去完成时:
They had looked into the problem before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经调查过这个问题了。
We had looked into all the options before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前已经研究过所有方案了。


将来时:
We will look into your complaint as soon as possible. = 我们会尽快调查你的投诉。
The manager will look into the matter tomorrow. = 经理明天将调查此事。


虚拟语气:
If they looked into the issue, they would find a solution. = 如果他们调查这个问题，他们会找到解决办法。
I wish they had looked into the matter earlier. = 我希望他们早点调查此事。


被动语态:
The matter is being looked into by the authorities. = 此事正在由当局调查。
The situation will be looked into by the management team. = 情况将由管理团队调查。


疑问句:
Are you looking into this problem? = 你正在调查这个问题吗？
Will they look into the allegations? = 他们会调查这些指控吗？


否定句:
They didn't look into the details thoroughly. = 他们没有彻底调查细节。
We haven't looked into that possibility yet. = 我们还没有调查那种可能性。


PHRASE->
look into the matter = 调查此事
look into the cause = 调查原因
look into the details = 调查细节
look into the future = 展望未来
look into someone's eyes = 注视某人的眼睛
