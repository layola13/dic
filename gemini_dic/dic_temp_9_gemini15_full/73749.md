forlore:/fɔːˈlɔːr/| adj.|凄凉的，孤独的，被遗弃的|sad and lonely, especially because one is abandoned or alone|The forlorn child sat alone on the park bench.|近义词：desolate, lonely, abandoned, forsaken|反义词：happy, cheerful, joyful, accompanied
