consulted:/kənˈsʌltɪd/| v. |咨询，请教，查阅 |To seek information or advice from (someone), especially an expert.  |例句：I consulted a doctor about my headache. 我因为头痛去看医生了。|例句：She consulted a dictionary to check the spelling. 她查字典来核对拼写。|近义词：advised, sought, referred to|反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consults a lawyer regularly. = 他定期咨询律师。
She consults her diary every morning. = 她每天早上查阅日记。


一般过去时:
I consulted my doctor before taking the medication. = 在服用药物之前，我咨询了我的医生。
They consulted a map to find their way. = 他们查阅地图来寻找路线。


现在完成时:
I have consulted several experts on this matter. = 我已经就此事咨询了几位专家。
She has consulted her notes several times. = 她已经多次查阅了她的笔记。


过去完成时:
He had consulted the manual before attempting the repair. = 在尝试修理之前，他已经查阅了手册。
They had consulted with their colleagues before making a decision. = 他们做出决定之前已经与同事们进行了协商。


将来时:
I will consult my accountant about my taxes. = 我将就我的税务咨询我的会计师。
She will consult the relevant documents before responding. = 她将在回复之前查阅相关文件。


虚拟语气:
If I had consulted an expert, I might have avoided this problem. = 如果我咨询了专家，我可能会避免这个问题。
I wish I had consulted my parents before making that decision. = 我真希望在做那个决定之前咨询了我的父母。


被动语态:
The doctor was consulted about the patient's condition. = 医生被咨询了病人的病情。
The dictionary was consulted frequently by the students. = 学生们经常查阅词典。


疑问句:
Did you consult anyone before making the decision? = 你在做决定之前咨询过任何人吗？
Have you consulted the manual? = 你查阅过手册了吗？


否定句:
I didn't consult anyone about my plans. = 我没有向任何人咨询我的计划。
She did not consult the instructions before assembling the furniture. = 她组装家具之前没有查阅说明书。


PHRASE->
consult with = 与...协商
consult a doctor = 看医生
consult a lawyer = 咨询律师
consult a map = 查地图
consult the archives = 查阅档案
