overabsorbing: /ˌoʊvərəbˈzɔːrbɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度吸收的；过度专注的 | Excessively absorbing or engaging; tending to absorb or engage more than is desirable or healthy.  |  例句：The overabsorbing nature of her work left her with little time for her family.  她工作的过度专注让她几乎没有时间陪伴家人。

例句：His overabsorbing hobby of collecting stamps left him with little money. 他过度沉迷于集邮的爱好，导致他几乎没有钱。

近义词：engrossing, absorbing, captivating, all-consuming

反义词：uninteresting, boring, tedious, disengaging

词性：形容词
