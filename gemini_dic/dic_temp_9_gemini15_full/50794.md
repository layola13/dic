descendibility: /ˌdɛsɛnˈdɪbɪləti/ | n. | 可继承性，可遗传性 | The capacity or state of being descendible; capable of being inherited or passed down.  It often refers to property or titles that can be passed from one generation to the next.  | Example: The descendibility of the estate was challenged in court.  该遗产的可继承性在法庭上受到了质疑。| 近义词：heritability, transmissibility | 反义词：uninheritability, inalienability |
