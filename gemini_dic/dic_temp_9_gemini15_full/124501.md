moulted:/ˈmoʊltɪd/ | adj. |蜕皮的；换毛的 | Shed or cast off (a covering, such as skin, feathers, or horns).  Describes something that has undergone the process of molting. | Example: The snake had moulted its skin, revealing bright new scales.  这条蛇蜕了皮，露出了鲜艳的新鳞片。The bird moulted its feathers in the autumn. 这只鸟在秋天换了羽毛。|近义词：shed, cast off |反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The snake moults its skin regularly. = 蛇定期蜕皮。
The bird moults its feathers every year. = 鸟每年换一次羽毛。

一般过去时:
The caterpillar moulted several times before becoming a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前蜕皮好几次。
The snake moulted its skin last night. = 蛇昨晚蜕皮了。

现在完成时:
The bird has moulted its feathers. = 这只鸟已经换了羽毛。
The snake has moulted twice this year. = 这条蛇今年已经蜕皮两次了。

过去完成时:
The bird had moulted its feathers before winter arrived. = 冬天到来之前，这只鸟已经换了羽毛。
The snake had moulted its skin before it went into hibernation. = 冬眠之前，蛇已经蜕了皮。

将来时:
The snake will moult its skin again soon. = 蛇很快会再次蜕皮。
The bird will moult its feathers in the fall. = 这只鸟将在秋天换毛。

虚拟语气:
If the snake moulted its skin, it would be easier to catch. = 如果蛇蜕皮了，就更容易抓到。
If the bird had moulted sooner, it would have been better prepared for winter. = 如果鸟早点换毛，它就能更好地为冬天做准备。

被动语态:
The skin was moulted by the snake. = 蛇蜕了皮。（被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
The feathers were moulted by the bird. = 鸟换了羽毛。（被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）

疑问句:
Has the bird moulted yet? = 鸟换毛了吗？
Did the snake moult its skin last night? = 蛇昨晚蜕皮了吗？

否定句:
The snake hasn't moulted its skin yet. = 蛇还没蜕皮。
The bird didn't moult its feathers this year. = 这只鸟今年没有换羽毛。
