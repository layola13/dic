词源:
The word "chemist" ultimately derives from the word "alchemy," which itself comes from the Arabic word "al-kīmiyā'," meaning "the art of transmutation."  Over time, "alchemy" evolved into "chemistry," and "chemist" became the term for someone who practices chemistry.
