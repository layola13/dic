misestimate:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/| vt.| 估计错误，低估或高估 | To make a wrong judgment about the amount, size, or value of something.  |例句：I completely misestimate the time it would take to finish the project. 我完全低估了完成项目所需的时间。|例句：They misestimate the market demand and produced too many goods. 他们高估了市场需求，生产了过多的商品。|近义词：misjudge, miscalculate, undervalue, overvalue |反义词：accurately estimate, correctly assess
