repudiators: /rɪˈpjuːdieɪtərz/ | n. | 否认者；拒绝者；抛弃者 | Those who repudiate or reject something, such as a claim, agreement, or relationship.  They actively disavow or refuse to acknowledge it. |  例句：The repudiators of the treaty argued that it was unfair.  否认该条约的人认为它不公平。

例句：The bank's repudiators faced legal consequences for their actions.  银行的否认者因其行为面临法律后果。


近义词：deniers, rejectors, disavowers, disclaimants


反义词：affirmers, acceptors, approvers


词性：名词
