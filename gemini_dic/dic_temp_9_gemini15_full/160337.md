poorhouses:/ˈpʊrˌhaʊzɪz/| n. |济贫院，贫民院 |A poorhouse is a house or institution, usually publicly funded, providing care and shelter for the poor, especially the elderly and infirm.  Historically, poorhouses were often associated with harsh conditions and limited resources. |例句：Many people in the 19th century relied on poorhouses for basic necessities. 19世纪许多人都依靠济贫院来满足基本需求。|例句：The conditions in the poorhouse were deplorable. 济贫院的条件很糟糕。|近义词：workhouse, almshouse, asylum(in this context)|反义词：mansion, palace
