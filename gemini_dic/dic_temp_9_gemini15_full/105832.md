kitbash:/ˈkɪtbæʃ/| v.|拼装模型；混合拼装|To combine parts from different kits or models to create a new, unique model.  This often involves modifying and customizing the parts to fit together.|例句：He kitbashed a futuristic spaceship using parts from several different model kits. 他用几个不同模型套件的零件拼装了一艘未来派宇宙飞船。
例句：The artist kitbashed various sculptures to create a new, abstract artwork.艺术家将各种雕塑混合拼装，创造了一件新的抽象艺术品。|近义词：combine, assemble, modify, customize|反义词：disassemble, separate
