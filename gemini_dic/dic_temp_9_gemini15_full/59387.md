ears:/ɪərz/ | n. |耳朵 |The organs of hearing and balance, located on either side of the head.  They are used to perceive sound and help maintain equilibrium.  |例句：He cupped his ears to hear better. 他捂着耳朵以便听得更清楚。|例句：The doctor examined his ears for infection. 医生检查了他的耳朵是否有感染。|近义词：hearing, auditory organs |反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑与“deafness”或“lack of hearing”相关联) |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My ears ring after the concert. = 音乐会后我的耳朵嗡嗡作响。
The dog pricks its ears when it hears a sound. = 狗听到声音时竖起耳朵。

一般过去时:
I covered my ears during the fireworks. = 在放烟花的时候我捂住了耳朵。
The child pulled at his ears in pain. = 孩子疼得扯他的耳朵。

现在完成时:
I have had ear infections many times. = 我多次患过耳部感染。
She has never had her ears pierced. = 她从未穿过耳洞。

过去完成时:
I had already covered my ears before the explosion. = 爆炸发生前我已经捂住了耳朵。
They had cleaned their ears before going to bed. = 他们睡觉前已经洗过耳朵了。

将来时:
I will protect my ears from loud noises. = 我会保护我的耳朵免受噪音的侵害。
He will have his ears checked by a doctor. = 他将去看医生检查耳朵。

虚拟语气:
If I had better hearing, I would appreciate music more. = 如果我的听力更好，我会更欣赏音乐。
I wish my ears weren't so sensitive to loud sounds. = 我真希望我的耳朵对大声的噪音不那么敏感。

被动语态:
His ears were examined by the doctor. = 他的耳朵被医生检查了。
Her ears were pierced when she was a teenager. = 她十几岁的时候打了耳洞。

疑问句:
Do your ears hurt? = 你的耳朵疼吗？
Are your ears sensitive to cold? = 你的耳朵对寒冷敏感吗？

否定句:
I didn't hear you; my ears were blocked. = 我没听见你；我的耳朵堵住了。
The dog didn't prick its ears at the usual sound. = 狗没有像平时那样听到声音时竖起耳朵。


PHRASE->

have a word in someone's ear:  私下对某人说 (to speak privately to someone)
