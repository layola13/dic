contemplate:/ˈkɒntɛmˌpleɪt/ /ˈkɑːntəmˌpleɪt/ | vt.  |沉思；冥想； contemplate doing sth. 考虑做某事| To think deeply and carefully about something.|

例句：I spent hours contemplating the meaning of life. 我花了数小时思考人生的意义。

例句：She is contemplating a career change.  她在考虑换一份工作。


近义词：consider, meditate, ponder, reflect

反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I contemplate the vastness of the universe. 我沉思宇宙的浩瀚。
He contemplates his future every night. 他每晚都在思考他的未来。

一般过去时:
We contemplated the options carefully. 我们仔细考虑了各种选择。
She contemplated the beauty of the sunset. 她沉思着日落的美丽。

现在完成时:
I have contemplated this problem for days. 我已经思考这个问题好几天了。
They have contemplated moving to the countryside. 他们一直在考虑搬到乡下。

过去完成时:
He had contemplated quitting his job for months. 他已经考虑辞职好几个月了。
She had contemplated the consequences before acting.  她在行动之前已经考虑过后果。

将来时:
I will contemplate my next move. 我将仔细考虑我的下一步行动。
They will contemplate the offer before making a decision. 他们会在做出决定之前仔细考虑这个提议。

虚拟语气:
If I had contemplated the risks, I wouldn't have done it. 如果我当时考虑过风险，我就不会那样做了。
I wish I had contemplated my options more carefully. 我真希望我当时更仔细地考虑过我的选择。

被动语态:
The problem was contemplated by the committee. 这个问题被委员会仔细考虑了。
The proposal was being contemplated by the board.  这个提案正在由董事会考虑。

疑问句:
What are you contemplating? 你在思考什么？
Have you contemplated the possibility of failure? 你考虑过失败的可能性吗？

否定句:
He did not contemplate the long-term effects. 他没有考虑长远的影响。
She didn't contemplate the consequences of her actions. 她没有考虑她行为的后果。
