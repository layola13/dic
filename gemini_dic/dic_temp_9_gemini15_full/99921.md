/ˌɪntərˈnekʃən/ | n. |相互作用，相互影响；交往，交流 |The interplay between two or more objects, agents, or systems; an instance of this.  It often implies a reciprocal effect, where each participant influences the other. |例句：The interaction between light and matter is a fundamental concept in physics. 光与物质的相互作用是物理学中的一个基本概念。
The interaction between the two countries has improved recently. 两国之间的互动最近有所改善。|近义词：interplay, reciprocity, interplay, communication, exchange |反义词：isolation, separation, independence
