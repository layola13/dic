coffee-beans:/ˈkɔːfi biːnz/| n. |咖啡豆|The seeds of the coffee plant, from which coffee is made.  Coffee beans are roasted before being ground and brewed.|例句：We bought a bag of freshly roasted coffee beans. 我们买了一袋新鲜烘焙的咖啡豆。|例句：The aroma of freshly ground coffee beans filled the air. 新磨咖啡豆的香味充满了空气。|近义词：coffee seeds|反义词: N/A
