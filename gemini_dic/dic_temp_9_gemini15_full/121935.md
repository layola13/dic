misshipping:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpɪŋ/| n.|错运，错寄|The act of shipping something to the wrong address or recipient.  It often results from errors in labeling, addressing, or handling during the shipping process.|例句：The misshipping of the package resulted in a delay of the project.  包裹错寄导致项目延期。


例句：The company apologized for the misshipping and promised to resend the order immediately. 公司为错寄道歉并承诺立即重新发送订单。


近义词：misdelivery, misdirection, wrong delivery


反义词：correct shipping, proper delivery


词性:名词
