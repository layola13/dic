coders:/ˈkoʊdərz/ | n. |程序员，编码员 | People who write computer code.  They create software, websites, and applications by writing instructions in programming languages. | Example:  The coders worked late into the night to fix the bug. 程序员们工作到深夜才修复了这个错误。|近义词: programmers, developers, software engineers |反义词:  None specifically.


USAGE->
