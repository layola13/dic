sassolin:/ˌsæsəˈliːn/| n.|硼砂|A mineral consisting of hydrated boric acid, found naturally in volcanic regions and used in various applications, including as a flux in soldering and as a component in some fertilizers and insecticides.|例句：Sassolin is a valuable mineral source of boric acid. 硼砂是硼酸的重要矿物来源。|近义词：borax (though not identical chemically)|反义词:|无
