entry-level: /ˈɛntri ˌlɛvəl/ | adj. |入门级的，初级的|  Relating to a job or position that is suitable for someone with little or no previous experience.  It describes the lowest level in a hierarchy or career progression. | 例句：This job is entry-level, so don't expect a high salary. 这份工作是入门级的，所以不要指望高薪。


This internship is an entry-level position. 这个实习是一个入门级职位。


近义词：beginner, junior, introductory, initial | 反义词：senior, advanced, experienced, expert | 词性：形容词
