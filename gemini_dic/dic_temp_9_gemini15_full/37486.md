committed:/kəˈmɪtɪd/ | adj., v. | 承诺的；坚定的；投入的；犯罪的 |  To be pledged or bound to a certain course of action; to perpetrate a crime or other wrongdoing.  | 例句：He is committed to his work. 他致力于他的工作。  She committed a serious crime. 她犯了重罪。 | 近义词：dedicated, devoted, involved, engaged, guilty | 反义词：uncommitted, detached, indifferent, innocent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is committed to finishing the project. = 他致力于完成这个项目。
They are committed to helping the poor. = 他们致力于帮助穷人。

一般过去时:
She committed herself to the cause. = 她献身于这个事业。
He committed a robbery last night. = 他昨晚犯了抢劫罪。

现在完成时:
We have committed to donating 10% of our profits to charity. = 我们已经承诺将10%的利润捐给慈善机构。
They have committed many mistakes in the past. = 他们过去犯过很多错误。

过去完成时:
She had already committed to the plan before she learned the new information. = 在她得知新信息之前，她就已经答应了这个计划。
They had committed themselves to the project long before it started. = 在项目开始很久以前，他们就已经全身心地投入到这个项目中了。

将来时:
I will commit to learning a new language next year. = 我明年将致力于学习一门外语。
They will commit themselves to finding a solution. = 他们将致力于寻找解决方案。

虚拟语气:
If I were committed to this goal, I would work harder. = 如果我致力于这个目标，我会更加努力。
I wish I had committed to this opportunity earlier. = 我希望我早点抓住这个机会。

被动语态:
The company was committed to the deal. = 公司已承诺参与这笔交易。
The funds were committed to the project. = 资金已投入到该项目中。

疑问句:
Are you committed to this relationship? = 你对这段关系认真吗？
Is he committed to his studies? = 他对他的学业认真吗？

否定句:
He is not committed to any political party. = 他不属于任何政党。
She was not committed to the idea. = 她并不认同这个想法。


PHRASE->
be committed to = 致力于；献身于
commit oneself to = 承诺；保证
commit murder = 犯谋杀罪
commit suicide = 自杀
commit a mistake = 犯错误
