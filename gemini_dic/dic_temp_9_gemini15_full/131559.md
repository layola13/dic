noncommunally:/ˌnɑːn kəˈmjuːnəli/ | adj. | 非公有的；非公社的 | Not in common; not shared; individually.  This term emphasizes the lack of shared ownership or use. It often contrasts with communal living or resource management. | Example: The land was used noncommunally, with each family owning its own plot.  这块土地是非公有的，每家都拥有自己的地块。| 近义词：individually, privately, separately | 反义词：communally, collectively, jointly
