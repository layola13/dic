dipsticks:/ˈdɪpstɪks/| n. |油尺；试纸；测量棒|A dipstick is a device used to measure the level of a liquid in a container, typically the oil level in an engine.  It can also refer to a strip of absorbent material used to test substances such as urine. |例句：The mechanic checked the engine oil level with a dipstick. (机械师用油尺检查了发动机油位。)  The doctor used a dipstick to test the patient's urine. (医生用试纸测试了病人的尿液。) |近义词：gauge, probe, indicator |反义词: N/A
