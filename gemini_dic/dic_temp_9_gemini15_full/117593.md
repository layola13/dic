megalops:/ˈmɛɡələps/| n. |巨幼体，大眼幼体 |A megalops is the larval stage of certain crustaceans, especially brachyuran crabs, characterized by large eyes and a well-developed carapace.  They are planktonic and undergo significant metamorphosis before reaching adulthood.| 例句：The megalops stage of the blue crab is crucial for its survival and dispersal.  蓝蟹的巨幼体阶段对其生存和扩散至关重要。

The megalops of the Dungeness crab can be identified by its distinctive swimming legs.  帝王蟹的巨幼体可以通过其独特的游泳腿来识别。|近义词：larva, zoea (referring to earlier larval stages)|反义词：adult
