ceases:/siːzɪz/| v.|停止；终止；结束 |To stop; to come to an end.  |例句：The rain ceased and the sun came out. 雨停了，太阳出来了。|例句：His heart ceased to beat. 他的心跳停止了。|近义词：stop, end, finish, terminate|反义词：begin, start, commence|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine ceases to function. = 机器停止运转。
He ceases his complaining. = 他停止了抱怨。


一般过去时:
The music ceased abruptly. = 音乐突然停止了。
The fighting ceased after a week. = 战斗一周后停止了。


现在完成时:
The pain has ceased. = 疼痛已经停止了。
The bleeding has ceased. = 出血已经停止了。


过去完成时:
The rain had ceased before we arrived. = 我们到达之前雨已经停了。
The arguments had ceased before the meeting. = 会议开始前争论已经停止了。


将来时:
The show will cease next month. = 节目下个月将结束。
The project will cease soon. = 项目很快就会结束。


虚拟语气:
If the noise ceased, we could concentrate. = 如果噪音停止，我们就能集中注意力。
I wish the arguing would cease. = 我希望争吵能停止。


被动语态:
The production was ceased due to the accident. = 由于事故，生产被停止了。
Further investigation has been ceased. = 进一步的调查已经停止了。


疑问句:
Does the pain cease when you take the medicine? = 你吃药后疼痛会停止吗？
When will the construction cease? = 建设什么时候停止？


否定句:
The rain did not cease all night. = 雨整夜都没有停。
He did not cease his efforts. = 他没有停止他的努力。
