extrachromosomal: /ˌɛkstrəˈkroʊməˌzoʊməl/ | adj. | 染色体外的 | Existing or occurring outside a chromosome.  It refers to genetic material that is not part of a cell's main chromosomes, typically found in plasmids or other independent structures.  This material can replicate independently of the chromosomal DNA.

例句：

Extrachromosomal DNA elements, such as plasmids, often carry genes for antibiotic resistance.  = 染色体外的DNA元件，例如质粒，通常携带抗生素抗性基因。

The study investigated the role of extrachromosomal circular DNA in cancer development. = 这项研究调查了染色体外环状DNA在癌症发展中的作用。


近义词：extragenomic, extranuclear (in some contexts)

反义词：chromosomal, genomic (in some contexts)

词性：形容词
