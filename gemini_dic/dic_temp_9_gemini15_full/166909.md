presterilizes: /ˌpriːˈstɛrəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 预先消毒 | To sterilize beforehand or in advance.  This word is formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "sterilizes" (the act of sterilizing).  It implies the sterilization process occurs *before* some other action or event. | Example: The surgical instruments were presterilized before the operation began.  手术器械在手术开始前已经预先消毒。  The hospital presterilizes all its equipment to prevent infections. 医院预先消毒所有设备以预防感染。| 近义词: sterilize in advance, pre-sanitize | 反义词: contaminate, infect | 动词
