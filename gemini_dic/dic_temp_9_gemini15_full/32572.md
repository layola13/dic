chirk:/tʃɜːrk/| v. |高兴地叫；（使）变得欢乐； |to make a cheerful sound; to become cheerful or lively; to brighten or cheer up |例句：The birds chirked merrily in the morning sun.  清晨的阳光下，鸟儿快乐地啁啾着。
The news chirked her up considerably. 这个消息让她高兴了不少。|近义词：chirp, tweet, twitter, brighten, cheer |反义词：depress, sadden, gloom
