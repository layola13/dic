photographed:/ˈfoʊtəɡræft/ | v. | 拍照，拍摄 | To take a photograph of someone or something. | 例句：He photographed the sunset. 他拍摄了日落。  He was photographed leaving the building. 他被拍到离开大楼。| 近义词：shot, pictured, captured | 反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I photograph nature scenes. = 我拍摄自然景观。
She photographs wildlife for a living. = 她靠拍摄野生动物为生。

一般过去时:
He photographed the accident. = 他拍摄了事故现场。
They photographed the family reunion. = 他们拍摄了家庭聚会。

现在完成时:
I have photographed many famous landmarks. = 我已经拍摄了许多著名的地标建筑。
She has photographed the same subject many times. = 她已经多次拍摄同一个主题。

过去完成时:
He had photographed the bird before it flew away. = 那只鸟飞走之前，他已经拍下了照片。
They had photographed the scene before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经拍下了现场照片。

将来时:
I will photograph the wedding next week. = 下周我将拍摄婚礼。
They will photograph the event for the newspaper. = 他们将为报纸拍摄该活动的图片。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would photograph more often. = 如果我有一台更好的相机，我会更频繁地拍照。
I wish I had photographed the eclipse. = 我真希望我拍下了日食的照片。

被动语态:
The event was photographed by several journalists. = 该事件被多名记者拍摄。
The painting was photographed for the catalogue. = 这幅画被拍摄下来用于画册。

疑问句:
Have you ever photographed a celebrity? = 你曾经拍过名人的照片吗？
Did he photograph the protest? = 他拍摄了抗议活动吗？

否定句:
I didn't photograph the meeting. = 我没有拍摄会议。
They didn't photograph the scenery because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，他们没有拍摄风景。


PHRASE->
be photographed = 被拍摄
get photographed = 被拍照
photographed evidence = 照片证据
well-photographed = 拍摄得好的
poorly photographed = 拍摄得很差的
