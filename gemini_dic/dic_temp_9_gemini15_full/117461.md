Mediterranean: /ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən/ | adj. | 地中海的；地中海地区的 | Of or relating to the Mediterranean Sea or the countries bordering it.  The adjective describes something connected to the Mediterranean Sea, its surrounding lands, or its climate.  |  例句：The Mediterranean climate is characterized by hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters.  地中海气候的特点是夏季炎热干燥，冬季温和潮湿。

例句：We took a cruise on the Mediterranean Sea. 我们在地中海乘船游览。


近义词：Southern European (in some contexts)

反义词：None directly applicable;  antonyms would depend on the specific context.


词源：From Latin *Mediterraneus, from medius ("middle") + terra ("earth").  It literally means "in the middle of the earth," referring to the sea's position within the lands surrounding it.
