nunciature:/ˈnʌnʃɪətʃər/| n. |使馆；公署|The official residence or office of a papal nuncio.  A nunciature is the diplomatic mission of the Holy See to a foreign country, headed by a papal nuncio.  It's similar to an embassy but represents the Vatican.|The nunciature in London hosted a reception for visiting dignitaries.|近义词：embassy, legation|反义词：无
