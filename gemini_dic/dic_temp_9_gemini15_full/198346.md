showance:/ˈʃoʊəns/| n. |展览会，演出|A showance is a public display or performance, often involving entertainment or artistic expression.  It's a blend of "show" and "performance," suggesting a more elaborate or significant event than a simple show. |例句：The town square hosted a spectacular showance of local talent.  小镇广场举办了一场当地人才的精彩演出。

The annual showance of the art students' work was a huge success.  艺术系学生的年度作品展非常成功。|近义词：exhibition, performance, spectacle, display |反义词：privacy, secrecy
