molecules:/ˈmɒlɪkjuːlz/ /ˈmɑːlɪkjuːlz/ | n. |分子|A molecule is the smallest particle of a substance that has all the chemical properties of that substance.  It consists of two or more atoms held together by chemical bonds.|  Water molecules are made of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom. 水分子由两个氢原子和一个氧原子构成。| The air we breathe is a mixture of different molecules. 我们呼吸的空气是由不同的分子组成的。| atoms, particles |  (None directly opposite, but macroscopic  counterpart could be a substance)|名词
