heedful: /ˈhiːdfəl/ | adj. | 注意的；留心的；谨慎的 | Paying close attention to something; mindful; cautious.  |例句：He was heedful of the dangers of the mountain climb. 他注意到了登山的危险。


例句：She was heedful of her words, choosing them carefully. 她谨慎地选择自己的话语。


近义词：attentive, mindful, cautious, wary, observant | 反义词：negligent, careless, reckless, inattentive


词源:  The word "heedful" comes from the Old English word "hēdan," meaning "to pay attention to," "to heed."  The suffix "-ful" adds the sense of "full of" or "characterized by."  Therefore, "heedful" literally means "full of heed" or "characterized by paying attention."
