/ˈaɪərnər/| n.|熨斗工，熨衣工|A person whose job is ironing clothes.|She works as an ironer at a dry cleaner's. 她在一间干洗店做熨衣工。|近义词：ironist, presser|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ironer irons clothes every day. = 熨衣工每天熨衣服。
He carefully ironers the shirts. = 他仔细地熨烫衬衫。


一般过去时:
The ironer ironed all the shirts yesterday. = 熨衣工昨天熨了所有的衬衫。
