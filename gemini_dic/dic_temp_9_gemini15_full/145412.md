overcentralizations: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntrəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度集中化；权力过度集中 | The excessive concentration of power or authority in a central location or authority.  This can lead to inefficiency, lack of responsiveness to local needs, and suppression of dissent. | Example: The overcentralizations of the company's decision-making process led to delays and hampered innovation.  公司决策过程的过度集中导致了延误并阻碍了创新。  | 近义词: centralization, concentration of power | 反义词: decentralization, devolution | 名词
