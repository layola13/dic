metastasized:/məˈtæstəsaɪzd/ | v. |转移（指癌细胞）|  The cancer has metastasized to other organs. 癌症已经转移到其他器官。|近义词：spread, disseminated|反义词：localized, contained|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cancer metastasizes rapidly in some patients. = 在一些病人身上，癌症转移得很快。
The tumor rarely metastasizes to the brain. = 肿瘤很少转移到大脑。


一般过去时:
The disease metastasized quickly after the initial diagnosis. = 在初步诊断后，疾病迅速转移。
The cancer had already metastasized by the time he sought treatment. = 当他寻求治疗时，癌症已经转移了。


现在完成时:
The cancer has metastasized to her lungs. = 癌症已经转移到她的肺部。
The doctor confirmed that the tumor has metastasized. = 医生确认肿瘤已经转移。


过去完成时:
The doctors realized the cancer had metastasized before surgery. = 医生在手术前意识到癌症已经转移。
By the time she was diagnosed, the cancer had already metastasized to the lymph nodes. = 当她被诊断出来时，癌症已经转移到淋巴结了。


将来时:
Doctors fear the cancer will metastasize to the bone. = 医生担心癌症会转移到骨头上。
If left untreated, the tumor will likely metastasize. = 如果不治疗，肿瘤很可能会转移。


虚拟语气:
If the cancer had not metastasized, the prognosis would have been much better. = 如果癌症没有转移，预后会好得多。
I wish the tumor hadn't metastasized so quickly. = 我希望肿瘤没有转移得这么快。


被动语态:
It was discovered that the cancer had metastasized. = 发现癌症已经转移了。
The extent to which the cancer has metastasized is still being assessed. = 癌症转移的程度仍在评估中。


疑问句:
Has the cancer metastasized to other organs? = 癌症是否转移到其他器官？
To what extent has the tumor metastasized? = 肿瘤转移到什么程度？


否定句:
The cancer has not yet metastasized. = 癌症尚未转移。
Thankfully, the tumor did not metastasize. = 谢天谢地，肿瘤没有转移。


PHRASE->
cancer metastasized = 癌症转移
metastasized to the lungs = 转移到肺部
metastasized to the bone = 转移到骨骼
metastasized to the brain = 转移到脑部
metastasized to the lymph nodes = 转移到淋巴结


词源:
From Late Latin *metastasīsus, from Greek metastasis (μετάστασις), from meta- (μετά-) "after, beyond" + stasis (στάσις) "a standing, position".  The word ultimately refers to a change of position or location.
