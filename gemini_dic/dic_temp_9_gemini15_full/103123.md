jerking:/ˈdʒɜːrkɪŋ/| n. v.|抽搐；猛拉；突然移动|The act of making a sudden, sharp movement; a sudden, sharp movement.  Also, the act of pulling or moving something suddenly and forcefully.|例句：The dog gave a sudden jerking of its head.  这只狗猛地一甩头。


The car made a jerking stop.  汽车猛地停了下来。|近义词：twitch, spasm, jolt, snatch|反义词：smooth, gradual, steady|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jerks his head in agreement. = 他猛地点头表示同意。
She often jerks the door open. = 她经常猛地拉开门。


一般过去时:
The horse jerked its reins. = 马猛地拉了一下缰绳。
He jerked the rope violently. = 他猛烈地拉了一下绳子。


现在完成时:
The car has jerked several times. = 这辆车已经猛地停了好几次了。
Have you ever jerked your knee? = 你曾经膝跳反射过吗？


过去完成时:
The train had jerked to a halt before the accident. = 事故发生前火车已经猛地停了下来。
He had jerked the steering wheel to avoid a collision. = 他猛地转动方向盘以避免碰撞。


将来时:
The engine will jerk when it starts. = 引擎启动时会猛烈抖动。
He will jerk the lever to start the machine. = 他将拉动操纵杆启动机器。


虚拟语气:
If he jerked the rope harder, it might break. = 如果他更用力地拉绳子，它可能会断。
I wish I hadn't jerked my neck so quickly. = 我真希望我没有那么快地扭动我的脖子。


被动语态:
The puppet was jerked around by the puppeteer. = 木偶被木偶表演者操纵着四处移动。
The car was jerked to a sudden stop. = 汽车猛地停了下来。


疑问句:
Did the car jerk when you braked? = 你刹车的时候车抖动了吗？
Why did he jerk his hand away? = 他为什么猛地把手缩回？


否定句:
The machine didn't jerk at all. = 机器根本没有抖动。
He didn't jerk the handle, he turned it smoothly. = 他没有猛拉把手，而是平稳地转动它。



PHRASE->

jerk off:  自慰 (粗俗用语)
a jerk:  傻瓜；笨蛋 (粗俗用语)
