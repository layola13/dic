disassenter:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛntər/| n. |持异议者，反对者 |A person who disagrees with an official opinion, especially one expressed by the government or a religious institution.  |Example:  The dissenter refused to sign the petition.  这位持异议者拒绝签署请愿书。|近义词：objector, protester, opponent |反义词：conformist, supporter
