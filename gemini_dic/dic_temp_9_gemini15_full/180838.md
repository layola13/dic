rehear:/ˌriːˈhɪr/| vt.|复述；重听；再审理|To hear again; to listen to again; to hear a case again in court.|例句：The judge decided to rehear the case.  法官决定重新审理此案。

例句：Let's rehear that beautiful song.  让我们再听一遍那首美丽的歌曲。

近义词：relisten, review, reconsider|反义词：ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rehears the story every day. 他每天都复述这个故事。
They rehear the evidence carefully. 他们仔细地重听证据。


一般过去时:
She rehearsed the speech last night. 她昨晚排练了演讲。
The court reheard the appeal. 法院重新审理了上诉。


现在完成时:
I have reheard the recording several times. 我已经多次重听录音了。
They have reheard the witnesses' testimonies. 他们已经重新听取了证人的证词。


过去完成时:
He had reheard the argument before making his decision. 他在做出决定之前已经重新听取了论点。
The committee had reheard the complaints from the residents. 委员会已经重新听取了居民的投诉。


将来时:
We will rehear the case next week. 我们下周将重新审理此案。
The jury will rehear the testimony. 陪审团将重新听取证词。


虚拟语气:
If the judge were to rehear the case, the outcome might be different. 如果法官要重新审理此案，结果可能会不同。
I wish I had reheard the lecture. 我希望我当时重听了讲座。


被动语态:
The case was reheard by the appellate court. 此案由上诉法院重新审理。

疑问句:

否定句:
