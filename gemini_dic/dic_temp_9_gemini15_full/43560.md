crazily:/ˈkreɪzɪli/ | adv. | 疯狂地；狂热地；荒唐地 |  In a crazy manner; wildly; frantically.  It describes an action performed in a way that is excessively enthusiastic, chaotic, or irrational. | 例句：He laughed crazily. 他疯狂地大笑。  She danced crazily to the music. 她随着音乐疯狂地跳舞。 | 近义词：madly, wildly, frantically, insanely, feverishly | 反义词：calmly, serenely, rationally, sanely
