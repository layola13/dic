scragged:/skræg/| v.| 勒死；处决；粗暴对待；彻底击败 | To kill (someone) by hanging, strangling, or otherwise constricting their neck.  It can also mean to severely defeat or reprimand someone.  The term often carries a strong connotation of violence or brutality. | 例句：The outlaw was scragged by the posse.  = 这个亡命之徒被追捕队勒死了。  He totally scragged his opponent in the debate. = 他在辩论中彻底击败了他的对手。| 近义词：hang, strangle, throttle, defeat, beat, trounce | 反义词：save, rescue, spare
