cuddling: /ˈkʌd(ə)lɪŋ/ | v. | 拥抱；依偎 | To hold close and affectionately.  |例句：The couple were cuddling on the sofa.  这对情侣依偎在沙发上。|近义词：hugging, embracing, snuggling |反义词：pushing away, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cuddle every night before bed. = 他们每晚睡前都会拥抱。
She cuddles her baby gently. = 她轻轻地抱着她的婴儿。

一般过去时:
We cuddled by the fireplace last night. = 我们昨晚在壁炉旁依偎着。
The children cuddled together for warmth. = 孩子们依偎在一起取暖。

现在完成时:
They have cuddled their cat all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在抱着他们的猫。
I have never cuddled a koala bear before. = 我以前从未抱过考拉。


过去完成时:
They had cuddled for hours before falling asleep. = 他们在睡着前已经拥抱了好几个小时。
She had cuddled the teddy bear since she was a child. = 她从小就抱着这个泰迪熊。

将来时:
