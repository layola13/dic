ciders:/ˈsaɪdərz/| n.|苹果酒|A cider is an alcoholic beverage made from fermented apple juice.  It can vary widely in sweetness and alcohol content depending on the type of apples used and the fermentation process.|例句：We enjoyed a bottle of dry cider with our dinner. 我们晚餐时享用了一瓶干苹果酒。|例句：The local orchard produces award-winning ciders. 当地果园生产屡获殊荣的苹果酒。|近义词：apple wine, alcoholic apple juice|反义词：无
