high-tops:/ˈhaɪ tɑːps/| n. |高帮鞋|High-tops are shoes with high uppers that extend above the ankles. They are often associated with basketball and hip-hop culture.  |例句：He wore his favorite high-tops to the basketball game. 他穿着他最喜欢的篮球鞋去打篮球。|例句：Many sneakerheads collect rare high-tops. 许多球鞋爱好者收藏稀有的高帮鞋。|近义词：high-cut sneakers, ankle-high shoes|反义词：low-tops, slip-ons
