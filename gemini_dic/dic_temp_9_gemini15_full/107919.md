larviform:/ˈlɑːrvɪfɔːrm/| adj.|幼虫状的，似幼虫的|Resembling a larva in form or structure.  The term is used to describe something that has the shape or characteristics of a larva, the immature, often worm-like stage in the life cycle of many insects and other invertebrates.|例句：The parasitic worm exhibited a larviform body, elongated and segmented.|近义词：larval, worm-like|反义词：adult, mature
