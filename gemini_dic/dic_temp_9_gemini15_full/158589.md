The term often refers to the body shape characterized by bilateral asymmetry and a flattened body adapted for bottom dwelling.|例句：The fossil shows pleuronectoid characteristics, suggesting it was a bottom-dwelling fish.  化石显示出侧扁鱼状的特征，表明它是一种底栖鱼类。|近义词：flatfish-like, laterally compressed|反义词：cylindrical, elongated
