dementias:/dɪˈmɛnʃəz/ | n. |痴呆症（复数） |Dementias are a group of neurological disorders characterized by a decline in cognitive abilities, such as memory, thinking, and judgment.  They are not a single disease but a collection of symptoms caused by various underlying conditions.  The most common type is Alzheimer's disease. |例句：The early symptoms of dementias can be subtle and easily overlooked.  痴呆症的早期症状可能很细微，很容易被忽视。

The increasing prevalence of dementias is placing a significant strain on healthcare systems worldwide.  痴呆症发病率的上升给全球医疗系统带来了巨大的压力。


近义词：neurocognitive disorders, cognitive impairments | 反义词：cognitive health, mental acuity
