reoperation:/ˌriːˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 再手术，二次手术 | The act of performing a surgical operation again, often on the same area or for the same condition.  It often implies a previous surgery that was unsuccessful or resulted in complications requiring further intervention. | Example: The patient required a reoperation due to complications from the initial surgery.  病人由于初次手术的并发症需要再次手术。  The surgeon scheduled a reoperation to address the persistent infection. 外科医生安排了再次手术来处理持续的感染。|近义词：second operation, repeat surgery |反义词：initial operation, first surgery |名词
