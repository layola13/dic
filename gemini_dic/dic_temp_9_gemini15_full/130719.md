nonallegiance: /ˌnɒnəˈliːdʒəns/ | n. | 不效忠；不忠诚 | The state or condition of not being loyal or faithful to a government, sovereign, or cause.  It implies a refusal or rejection of allegiance. |  His nonallegiance to the crown led to his imprisonment. 他的不效忠于王室导致了他被监禁。  The rebels declared their nonallegiance to the oppressive regime. 反叛者宣布他们不效忠于压迫性政权。 | disloyalty, disaffection, defection | allegiance, loyalty, fidelity | 名词
