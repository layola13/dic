postmarks:/ˈpoʊstˌmɑːrks/|/ˈpəʊstˌmɑːks/| n. |邮戳；邮票印记 |The marks made on a letter by a post office to show the date and place it was posted.  Postmarks are often collected by philatelists. |The letter bore a clear postmark from London. 这封信上清晰地盖着伦敦的邮戳。  I found an old letter with a fascinating postmark. 我找到一封带有迷人邮戳的旧信。|  mailmarks, stamps (in the sense of the impression) |  (无明显反义词)|名词
