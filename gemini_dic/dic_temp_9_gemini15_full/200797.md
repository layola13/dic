slickly:/ˈslɪkli/| adv.|流畅地；圆滑地；油滑地 |In a smooth and effortless way; skillfully and cleverly, often in a way that is deceptive or insincere.  |例句：He slickly avoided answering the question. 他巧妙地避开了这个问题。|例句：She slickly maneuvered her way through the crowded room. 她轻巧地穿梭于拥挤的房间。|近义词：smoothly, smoothly, adeptly, cleverly, smoothly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
