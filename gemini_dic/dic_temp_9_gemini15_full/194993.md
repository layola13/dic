semi-tropics:/ˈsɛmiˈtrɒpɪks/ | adj. |亚热带的 |  Relating to or characteristic of the semi-tropical regions, which lie between the tropics and the temperate zones, characterized by warm temperatures and often high humidity.  | The semi-tropics boast a rich biodiversity. 亚热带地区拥有丰富的生物多样性。  The climate of the semi-tropics is generally warm and humid. 亚热带气候通常温暖潮湿。| tropical, subtropical | arctic, antarctic |  adj.
