relater:/rɪˈleɪtə(r)/| n. |关联者，叙述者，讲述者|A person or thing that relates or connects two or more things.  Someone who tells a story or recounts events. |例句：The professor served as a relater between the two conflicting theories. 教授充当了两种相互冲突理论之间的联系者。|例句：The relater spun a captivating yarn about a lost treasure.  讲述者讲述了一个关于失落宝藏的引人入胜的故事。|近义词：connector, narrator, storyteller, reporter |反义词：separator, divider
