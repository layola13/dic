even-out: /ˈiːvən aʊt/ | vt. | 使均匀；使平衡；使相等 | To make something smooth, level, or uniform; to balance or equalize something. | 例句：The contractor evened out the foundation before building the house.  承包商在建房之前将地基找平了。


例句：We need to even out the workload among the team members. 我们需要在团队成员之间平均分配工作量。


近义词：level, smooth, balance, equalize


反义词：disrupt, imbalance, unequalize


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine evens out the surface of the wood. = 机器使木材表面平整。
He evens out his work schedule each week. = 他每周都调整工作时间表，使其平衡。


一般过去时:
She evened out the wrinkles in her dress. = 她把衣服上的褶皱熨平了。
The rain evened out the temperature differences across the region. = 雨水使该地区各地的温差趋于一致。


现在完成时:
They have evened out the differences between the two groups. = 他们已经消除了两组之间的差异。
The team has evened out their performance this season. = 这个赛季球队表现更加稳定。


过去完成时:
He had evened out the accounts before the audit. = 他在审计之前已经核对过账目了。
The farmer had evened out the field before planting the crops. = 农民在种植庄稼之前已经平整了田地。


将来时:
We will even out the playing field for all participants. = 我们将为所有参与者创造公平的竞争环境。
The government will even out the distribution of resources. = 政府将使资源分配更加均衡。


虚拟语气:
If we evened out the workload, we would finish earlier. = 如果我们平均分配工作量，我们会早点完成。
I wish they had evened out the playing field for the competition. = 我希望他们在比赛中创造公平的竞争环境。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
