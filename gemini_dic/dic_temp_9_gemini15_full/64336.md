eradication:/ɪˌrædəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 根除；消灭；消除 | The complete destruction of something, such as a disease, a harmful practice, or a group of people.  | Example: The eradication of polio is a significant achievement.  根除小儿麻痹症是一项重大成就。 |近义词：extermination, elimination, annihilation | 反义词：propagation, proliferation, fostering |  名词
