clash:/klæʃ/| /klæʃ/| n., v.|冲突；碰撞；不协调|

英文解析:
As a noun, "clash" refers to a violent confrontation or conflict; a strong disagreement. As a verb, it means to collide with a forceful impact; to be in conflict or disagreement.  It can also describe a discordant combination of sounds or colors.

例句:
There was a violent clash between the two armies. = 两军之间爆发了激烈的冲突。

The two designs clashed horribly. = 这两种设计极其不协调。


近义词:
conflict, collision, disagreement, discord, fight, contention

反义词:
harmony, agreement, accord, concord


词性:
Noun, Verb
