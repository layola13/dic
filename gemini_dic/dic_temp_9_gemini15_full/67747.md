eyebar:/ˈaɪbɑːr/ | n. |眼梁；（眼镜的）眼桥 |A metal bar connecting the lenses of eyeglasses or sunglasses.  It's also used in other contexts to refer to a bar-like structure resembling an eye. |例句：The eyebar on my glasses broke. = 我的眼镜的眼梁断了。
