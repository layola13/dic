high-fives:/haɪˈfaɪvz/| n. |击掌庆祝|A high-five is a celebratory gesture where two people simultaneously slap each other's upraised hands together, usually expressing excitement, congratulations, or agreement.  It's a casual and informal way to show enthusiasm.|Example: The team gave each other high-fives after winning the game.  球队在赢得比赛后互相击掌庆祝。|近义词：hand slap, celebratory gesture|反义词：none
