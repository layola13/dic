figurate:/ˈfɪɡjəreɪt/| adj.|比喻的；象征的；有形的|Figurative language uses figures of speech, such as metaphors and similes, to create vivid imagery and convey meaning indirectly.  It is not literal.|Example:  The poet used figurative language to express his grief.  这位诗人用比喻的语言来表达他的悲伤。|近义词：metaphorical, symbolic, representative|反义词：literal, literal-minded
