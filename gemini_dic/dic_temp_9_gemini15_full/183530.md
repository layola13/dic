repurpose:/ˌriːˈpɜːrpəs/| vt. |重新利用；赋予新用途|To find a new purpose or use for something.|We repurposed the old jars as storage containers. 我们把旧罐子重新利用作储物容器。|reuse, refashion, recycle|discard, waste|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They repurpose old clothes into new designs. = 他们将旧衣服改造成新的设计。
We repurpose glass bottles into vases. = 我们将玻璃瓶改造成花瓶。

一般过去时:
She repurposed the wooden pallets into a garden bench. = 她把木托盘改造成花园长凳。
The company repurposed its waste materials. = 公司对废弃物进行了再利用。

现在完成时:
They have repurposed many items in their home. = 他们已经在家中重新利用了许多物品。
We have repurposed the old tires into planters. = 我们已经把旧轮胎改造成花盆。

过去完成时:
They had repurposed the building before the renovation. = 他们在翻新之前已经对建筑物进行了重新利用。
She had repurposed the fabric scraps into a quilt. = 她已经把布头做成了被子。

将来时:
We will repurpose the leftover food into a new meal. = 我们将把剩余的食物重新做成一顿新饭。
They will repurpose the old furniture for the new office. = 他们将把旧家具重新用于新办公室。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would repurpose the old boxes. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新利用旧箱子。
I wish I could repurpose this broken chair. = 我真希望我能重新利用这把坏椅子。

被动语态:
The old tires were repurposed into playground equipment. = 旧轮胎被改造成操场设备。
The unused materials are being repurposed for another project. = 未使用的材料正在被重新用于另一个项目。

疑问句:
How can we repurpose these plastic bottles? = 我们怎样才能重新利用这些塑料瓶？
Will the company repurpose its waste products? = 公司会重新利用它的废弃产品吗？

否定句:
They did not repurpose the old furniture. = 他们没有重新利用旧家具。
We cannot repurpose this damaged equipment. = 我们不能重新利用这台损坏的设备。
