hail:/heɪl/| n., v.|冰雹；向…欢呼；招呼；|  Hail is frozen precipitation in the form of ice pellets.  To hail someone is to greet them enthusiastically. |例句：The storm brought hail the size of golf balls.  暴风雨带来了高尔夫球大小的冰雹。

例句：The crowd hailed the winning team.  人群欢呼胜利的队伍。|近义词： (n.)  sleet, pellets; (v.) greet, salute, acclaim |反义词：(n.)  sunshine, rain; (v.)  ignore, snub |词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hails from a small town. = 他来自一个小镇。
They hail the hero every day. = 他们每天都向英雄致敬。

一般过去时:
The farmers hailed the much-needed rain. = 农民们欣喜地迎接了这场急需的雨水。
We hailed a taxi in the street. = 我们在街上拦了一辆出租车。

现在完成时:
The team has hailed their victory. = 球队庆祝了他们的胜利。
She has hailed me as a genius. = 她把我誉为天才。

过去完成时:
They had hailed the arrival of the new year with fireworks. = 他们用烟花庆祝新年的到来。
Before the meeting, they had hailed the new leader. = 会议之前，他们已经向新领导人表示祝贺。

将来时:
We will hail the new ship when it arrives. = 新船到达时，我们将向它致敬。
They will hail a cab to the airport. = 他们将叫出租车去机场。

虚拟语气:
If it hailed tomorrow, the crops would be ruined. = 如果明天下冰雹，庄稼就会毁了。
I wish they had hailed me before entering the room. = 我希望他们在进房间之前先打个招呼。

被动语态:
The new law was hailed as a great success. = 新法律被誉为巨大的成功。
He was hailed as a visionary. = 他被誉为有远见的人。

疑问句:
Do you hail from this city? = 你是这个城市的人吗？
Will they hail the train? = 他们会向火车致敬吗？

否定句:
The crowd didn't hail the politician. = 人群没有向这位政治家欢呼。
They didn't hail a cab; they walked. = 他们没有叫出租车；他们步行。
