mishap:/ˈmɪsˌhæp/| n. |不幸事故，意外事件 |An unfortunate accident or unlucky event.  |例句：The flight was delayed due to a mishap with the engine.  航班由于发动机出现故障而延误。|例句：A series of mishaps led to the project's failure. 一系列的意外导致了项目的失败。|近义词：accident, incident, misfortune |反义词：success, good fortune

USAGE->
一般现在时:
A mishap often occurs during rush hour. = 意外事故经常发生在高峰时段。
Mishaps are a common occurrence in this industry. = 意外事故在这个行业很常见。

一般过去时:
A serious mishap happened on the highway yesterday. = 昨天高速公路上发生了一起严重事故。
We had a minor mishap with the car, but nothing too serious. = 我们车出了点小故障，但没什么大不了的。

现在完成时:
There have been several mishaps at the factory this year. = 今年工厂发生了几起事故。
No serious mishaps have occurred during the construction. = 在施工过程中没有发生严重事故。

过去完成时:
Before the final launch, several mishaps had occurred during testing. = 在最终发射前，测试过程中发生了几起事故。
By the time they arrived, several mishaps had already taken place. = 当他们到达时，已经发生了几起事故。


将来时:
We hope no mishaps will occur during the trip. = 我们希望旅途中不会发生任何事故。
If we are not careful, a mishap might occur. = 如果我们不小心，可能会发生事故。


虚拟语气:
If there had been no mishap, we would have finished on time. = 如果没有发生意外，我们就能按时完成。
I wish there hadn't been a mishap with the delivery. = 我希望送货过程中没有发生意外。


被动语态:
The project was delayed due to a mishap that was not foreseen. = 项目由于不可预见的事故而延误。
Many accidents were caused by mishaps on the road. = 许多事故都是由路上的意外事故造成的。


疑问句:
Was there a mishap during the performance? = 演出过程中发生了意外吗？
What kind of mishap occurred? = 发生了什么类型的意外？


否定句:
There was no mishap during the operation. = 手术过程中没有发生意外。
Fortunately, no mishap occurred during the flight. = 幸运的是，飞行过程中没有发生意外。
