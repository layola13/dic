interflowed:/ˌɪntərˈfloʊd/| v. | 交错流动；互相融合 | To flow or move in and out or among each other; to blend or merge together.  |例句：The two rivers interflowed at the confluence, creating a wider waterway. 两条河流在汇合处交汇，形成了一条更宽的水道。

The ideas of the two thinkers interflowed throughout the debate, making it difficult to distinguish their individual contributions. 两个思想家的观点在整个辩论中交织在一起，很难区分他们的个人贡献。|近义词：intermingle, intertwine, merge, blend |反义词：separate, diverge, isolate
