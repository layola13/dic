outshouts:/ˌaʊtˈʃaʊts/| vt.|大声喊过；超过…的音量|To shout louder than someone or something else.  It implies a competition or struggle to be heard.|例句：He outshouted the crowd to make his point. 他大声喊叫盖过人群的声音来表达他的观点。

例句：The singer outshouted the noisy band. 歌手的声音盖过了嘈杂的乐队。


近义词：outcry, overpower (in terms of sound), drown out

反义词：whisper, murmur, mutter


词性:动词
