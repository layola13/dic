knit:/nɪt/ | v. | 编织；缝合；紧密结合 | To interloop yarn or thread to make cloth or other material.  | She knits sweaters for her grandchildren. 她为她的孙子孙女织毛衣。He knitted his brows in concentration. 他皱着眉头全神贯注。| weave, crochet, stitch | unravel, separate | 动词


USAGE->

He knits a scarf for his wife. = 他为他妻子织一条围巾。
