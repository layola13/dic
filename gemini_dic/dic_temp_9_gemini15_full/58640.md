dumbfoundered:/ˌdʌmˈfaʊndərd/| adj.| 惊呆的；目瞪口呆的；哑口无言的 | Extremely surprised and speechless; astonished.  |例句：He was dumbfounded by the news of his sudden promotion. 他对突然晋升的消息感到非常震惊。  |例句：I was completely dumbfounded when she told me she was leaving. 当她告诉我她要离开时，我完全惊呆了。|近义词：astonished, amazed, astounded, flabbergasted, speechless |反义词：unimpressed, unimpressed, calm, composed
