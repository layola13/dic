emboil:/ɪmˈbɔɪl/| vt.| 使沸腾；使激动；使充满| to cause (a liquid) to boil; to cause (a person or their emotions) to be agitated or excited; to fill or permeate (something) thoroughly|例句：The heat emboiled the water. 热量使水沸腾。 The news emboiled the crowd with anger.  这个消息激怒了人群。|近义词：boil, seethe, excite, agitate|反义词：calm, soothe, quiet
