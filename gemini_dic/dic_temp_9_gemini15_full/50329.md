deplorer:/dɪˈplɔːrər/| n. |悲叹者，谴责者 |One who expresses strong disapproval of something.  A person who deplores something. |例句：The protesters were deplorers of the new policy. 抗议者们强烈谴责这项新政策。

例句：He was a vocal deplorer of the war. 他是战争的强烈反对者。


近义词：condemner, critic, objector |反义词：supporter,approver, advocate |词性：名词
