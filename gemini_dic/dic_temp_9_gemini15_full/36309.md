cohesiveness:/ˌkoʊhiːˈsɪvnəs/| n. |凝聚力；内聚性；一致性 |The quality or state of being cohesive; the tendency to stick together.  It refers to the degree to which members of a group or team are attracted to each other and work together effectively.  This can apply to a variety of contexts, from the molecules in a substance to the members of a social group. |例句：The cohesiveness of the team was evident in their synchronized movements.  团队的凝聚力体现在他们同步的动作中。

The high cohesiveness of the glue made it ideal for the project.  胶水的粘合度很高，非常适合这个项目。|近义词：unity, solidarity, coherence, integration |反义词：disunity, fragmentation, discord, disintegration
