horsehide:/ˈhɔːrshaɪd/| n. |马皮；马皮革|Horsehide is a strong, durable leather made from horse skin.  It's often used for making saddles, gloves, and other items requiring resilience and toughness.|例句：The baseball glove was made of high-quality horsehide. 这只棒球手套是用优质马皮制成的。|例句：The craftsman carefully stitched the horsehide to create a sturdy saddle. 工匠仔细缝制马皮，制作了一个结实的马鞍。|近义词：leather, hide|反义词：无
