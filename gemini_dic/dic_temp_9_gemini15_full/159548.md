politically:/pəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 政治上地；从政治角度来看 |  In a way that relates to politics; in a manner showing political skill or wisdom.  |  The situation was politically explosive.  (局势在政治上非常爆炸性。)  He handled the delicate situation politically. (他巧妙地处理了这个棘手的情况。) | diplomatically, tactfully | undiplomatically, tactlessly | 副词
