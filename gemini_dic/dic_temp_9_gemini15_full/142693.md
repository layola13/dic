other side of the coin: /ðiː ˈʌðər saɪd əv ðə kɔɪn/ | n. phr. | 事物的另一方面；反面；另一层意思 | This expression refers to a different perspective or a contrasting aspect of a situation. It implies that there's more to the story than what's immediately apparent, and that considering the opposite viewpoint is important for a complete understanding. |  例句：He's always complaining about his job, but there's another side to the coin; he's actually quite successful. 他总是抱怨他的工作，但事情还有另一方面；事实上他相当成功。


例句：While the new policy has some benefits, we need to look at the other side of the coin and consider the potential drawbacks.  虽然这项新政策有一些好处，但我们需要看看事情的另一方面，并考虑潜在的缺点。


近义词：converse, opposite, reverse, flip side


反义词：same side, single perspective


词性：名词短语(noun phrase)
