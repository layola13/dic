curtains:/ˈkɜːrtənz/| n.|窗帘，幕布|A curtain is a piece of heavy cloth that is hung to cover a window or doorway, or as a decoration.  They can be made from various materials and come in many styles and colors. | The curtains were drawn, creating a dimly lit room. 窗帘拉上了，房间里光线昏暗。  She hung beautiful velvet curtains in her bedroom. 她在卧室里挂上了美丽的丝绒窗帘。| drapes, blinds, screens | open windows, bare windows |名词
