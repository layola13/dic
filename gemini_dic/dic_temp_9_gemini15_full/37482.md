commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | vt. | 承诺；委托；犯（罪）；提交 | To pledge or bind oneself to a course of action; to entrust or consign to someone's care; to perpetrate (a crime, error, etc.); to send or give (something) for a particular purpose. | 例句：He committed himself to finishing the project by Friday. 他承诺在周五之前完成项目。| 例句：She committed the manuscript to her editor. 她把稿件交给了她的编辑。 | 近义词：pledge, entrust, dedicate | 反义词：neglect, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commits to his work every day. = 他每天都全身心地投入工作。
She commits a significant portion of her income to charity. = 她把很大一部分收入捐给了慈善机构。

一般过去时:
They committed a serious error in their calculations. = 他们在计算中犯了一个严重的错误。
He committed himself to the cause of social justice. = 他致力于社会正义事业。


现在完成时:
She has committed herself to learning a new language. = 她已经投入学习一门外语。
They have committed many resources to this project. = 他们已经投入了大量的资源到这个项目中。


过去完成时:
He had committed himself to the plan before he realized its flaws. = 他在意识到计划的缺陷之前就已经承诺了。
They had committed the crime before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们就已经犯了罪。


将来时:
She will commit to the new strategy next week. = 她下周将致力于新的策略。
He will commit his time and energy to the project. = 他将把时间和精力投入到这个项目中。


虚拟语气:
If he committed to the project, he would succeed. = 如果他致力于这个项目，他就会成功。
I wish I had committed to my studies earlier. = 我希望我早些投入到学习中。


被动语态:
The funds were committed to the research project. = 资金已投入到研究项目中。
The case was committed to the court. = 此案已提交法院。


疑问句:
Will you commit to this plan? = 你会承诺这个计划吗？
Has she committed any mistakes? = 她犯了什么错误吗？


否定句:
He did not commit himself to any specific course of action. = 他没有承诺任何具体的行动方案。
She didn't commit the crime. = 她没有犯罪。


PHRASE->
commit suicide = 自杀
commit a crime = 犯罪
commit oneself = 承诺；保证
commit to memory = 记住；熟记
commit adultery = 通奸
