ignited:/ɪɡˈnaɪtɪd/ | v. |点燃；激起；引发 | To set something on fire; to arouse or stimulate strong feelings or reactions. |例句：The match ignited the dry leaves. 火柴点燃了干树叶。

The news ignited a firestorm of controversy. 这则新闻引发了一场激烈的争议。|近义词：kindled, inflamed, sparked |反义词：extinguished, quenched


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pilot light ignites the gas. = 点火器点燃了燃气。
He ignites a passion in her. = 他在她心中点燃了激情。


一般过去时:
The spark ignited the gunpowder. = 火花点燃了火药。
Her speech ignited the crowd. = 她的演讲激起了人群的热情。


现在完成时:
The fire has ignited the forest. = 大火已经点燃了森林。
His words have ignited a debate. = 他的话引发了一场辩论。


过去完成时:
The wick had ignited before the match was struck. = 还没划火柴之前，灯芯就已经点燃了。
The rumour had ignited public anger before the truth was revealed. = 在真相大白之前，谣言已经激起了公众的愤怒。


将来时:
The fuse will ignite in five seconds. = 引信将在五秒钟后点燃。
His actions will ignite a conflict. = 他的行为将引发冲突。


虚拟语气:
If the pilot light had ignited, the gas would have been working. = 如果点火器点燃了，燃气就会正常工作。
If only his words hadn't ignited such strong emotions! = 但愿他的话没有激起如此强烈的感情！


被动语态:
The gas was ignited by a spark. = 燃气被火花点燃。
The crowd was ignited by his inspiring speech. = 人群被他鼓舞人心的演讲所激励。


疑问句:
Did the match ignite the bonfire? = 火柴点燃了篝火吗？
What ignited the conflict between them? = 是什么引发了他们之间的冲突？


否定句:
The lighter failed to ignite the candle. = 打火机未能点燃蜡烛。
His speech did not ignite any passion in the audience. = 他的演讲没有激起观众的任何热情。
