mustards:/ˈmʌstərdz/| n. |芥末；芥末酱|A condiment made from mustard seeds.  It comes in various forms, from mild to very hot.| 例句：I like to put mustard on my hot dogs. 我喜欢在我的热狗上放芥末。

例句：The chef used Dijon mustard in the sauce.  厨师在酱汁里用了第戎芥末。
