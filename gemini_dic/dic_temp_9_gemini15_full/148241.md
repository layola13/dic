oversized:/ˌoʊvərˈsaɪzd/| adj.|过大的，超大的|Excessively large; larger than usual or necessary.|例句：He wore an oversized sweater. 他穿着一件超大的毛衣。|例句：The oversized furniture dwarfed the small room.  超大的家具使小房间显得更小了。|近义词：bulky, large, immense, enormous|反义词：undersized, small, tiny
