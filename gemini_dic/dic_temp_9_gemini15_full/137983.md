nonzonally:/ˌnɒnˈzəʊnəli/ | adj. |非纬向的，非带状的 | Not relating to or aligned with zones or latitudinal bands, especially in the context of atmospheric or oceanic circulation.  It refers to patterns or processes that are not confined to specific latitude lines but are more irregular or three-dimensional. | Example: The nonzonal distribution of precipitation resulted in regional droughts.  非纬向的降水分布导致了区域性干旱。 |近义词：irregular, asymmetric, three-dimensional | 反义词：zonal, latitudinal
