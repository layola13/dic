mismanagement:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒmənt/| n. |管理不善；错误管理；经营不当|The failure of the company was attributed to mismanagement.  公司的失败归咎于管理不善。|近义词：maladministration, misrule, bad management |反义词：good management, efficient administration

一般现在时:
The company's mismanagement causes significant losses. = 公司的管理不善导致了巨大的损失。
Poor mismanagement leads to decreased productivity. = 管理不善导致生产力下降。


一般过去时:
Mismanagement of the project resulted in delays. = 项目管理不善导致了延误。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
Continued mismanagement will lead to further problems. = 持续的管理不善将导致更多的问题。
Without improvement, mismanagement will likely result in failure. = 如果没有改进，管理不善很可能会导致失败。


虚拟语气:
