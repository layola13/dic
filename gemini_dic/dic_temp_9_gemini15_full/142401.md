ortolan:/ˈɔːtələn/| n. |欧歌莺（一种小型鸣禽）|A small, migratory Old World songbird (Emberiza hortulana) prized as a culinary delicacy, especially when fattened.  It's known for its distinctive song.|The ortolan bunting is a highly sought-after delicacy in some parts of France.|近义词：Emberiza hortulana (scientific name)|反义词：无
