retributions:/ˌrɛtrɪˈbjuːʃənz/ | n. |报应；惩罚；报酬 | The act of giving someone what they deserve, especially punishment.  This can be a deserved punishment for wrongdoing, or it can refer to the consequences of one's actions.  The word often implies a sense of justice, whether divine or human. |例句：His actions brought about swift retribution.  他的行为招致了迅速的报应。

例句：The company faced retribution for its unethical practices. 这家公司因其不道德行为而受到了惩罚。


近义词：punishment, penalty, vengeance, recompense, payback |反义词：reward, blessing, forgiveness


词性：名词
