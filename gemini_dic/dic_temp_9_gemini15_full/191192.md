scarifiers: /ˈskærɪfaɪərz/ | n. | 疏松机；起皱机；刻痕器 | Tools or machines used to scarify the surface of the ground or other materials.  They break up compacted soil, create grooves, or roughen surfaces.  |例句：The farmer used a scarifier to break up the hardpan before planting.  农民在播种前用疏松机翻耕坚硬的土壤。

The gardener used a scarifier to create texture on the garden path. 园丁用刻痕器在花园小路上制造纹理。


近义词：cultivator, aerator, roughener |反义词：smoother, compactor |名词
