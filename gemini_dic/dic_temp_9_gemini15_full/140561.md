oligarch:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːrk/ /ˈɑːləɡɑːrk/| n. |寡头政治统治者；寡头；巨富| A member of a small group of powerful people who control a country or organization.  An extremely wealthy person, especially one who gained their wealth through business. |例句：The country is ruled by a small group of oligarchs. 这个国家由一小撮寡头统治。

The rise of oligarchs in Russia has led to increased social inequality.  俄罗斯寡头的崛起导致社会不平等加剧。|近义词：tycoon, magnate, plutocrat|反义词：democrat, egalitarian
