polygyny:/pəˈlɪdʒɪni/| n. |一夫多妻制|The practice or custom of a man having more than one wife at the same time.  Polygyny is a form of marriage in which one man is married to multiple women.|例句：Polygyny is practiced in some cultures around the world. 一夫多妻制在世界某些文化中被实行。

例句：The anthropologist studied the social implications of polygyny in the tribe. 人类学家研究了该部落一夫多妻制的社会影响。

近义词：polygamy (更广泛的术语，包括一妻多夫制)
反义词：monogamy (一夫一妻制)

词性：名词
