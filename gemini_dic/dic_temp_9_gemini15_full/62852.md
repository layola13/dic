enjoins:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪnz/| vt.| 命令；吩咐；劝告；强制 | To urge or command (someone) to do something.  To prohibit or forbid (something).|例句：The law enjoins us to respect the rights of others. = 法律命令我们尊重他人的权利。The rules enjoin silence during the examination. = 考试期间规定要保持安静。|近义词：command, order, instruct, urge, mandate|反义词：permit, allow, tolerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor enjoins the patient to rest. = 医生命令病人休息。
The school enjoins students to wear uniforms. = 学校规定学生要穿校服。

一般过去时:
The judge enjoined the defendant from contacting the witness. = 法官禁止被告联系证人。
My mother enjoined me to be careful. = 我母亲告诫我要小心。

现在完成时:
The court has enjoined the company from polluting the river. = 法院已禁止该公司污染河流。
The teacher has enjoined the students to study hard for the exam. = 老师已告诫学生要努力学习考试。

过去完成时:
The general had enjoined his troops to maintain discipline. = 将军曾命令他的部队保持纪律。
She had enjoined her son to be honest, but he lied anyway. = 她曾告诫儿子要诚实，但他还是撒谎了。

将来时:
The government will enjoin the factories to reduce emissions. = 政府将命令工厂减少排放。
He will enjoin his children to be respectful to their elders. = 他将告诫他的孩子们要尊敬长辈。

虚拟语气:
If the court enjoined the construction, the project would be delayed. = 如果法院禁止施工，项目将会延误。
I wish the teacher had enjoined us to bring our textbooks. = 我希望老师当时告诫我们带上课本。

被动语态:
The workers were enjoined from striking. = 工人们被禁止罢工。
Silence was enjoined upon the audience. = 观众被要求保持安静。

疑问句:
Does the law enjoin us to pay taxes? = 法律是否命令我们纳税？
Does the company enjoin its employees to work overtime? = 公司是否命令其员工加班？

否定句:
The contract does not enjoin the parties from negotiating. = 合同没有禁止双方谈判。
The regulation does not enjoin smoking in this area. = 该规定没有禁止在这个区域吸烟。


PHRASE->
enjoin upon = 郑重告诫；强烈要求
enjoin silence = 命令保持安静
enjoin obedience = 命令服从
enjoin restraint = 命令克制
enjoin conformity = 命令遵守
