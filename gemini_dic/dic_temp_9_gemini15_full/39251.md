congregating:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |聚集，集合 |The act of coming together in a crowd or group.  It implies a gathering of people or things, often for a specific purpose or shared interest. |例句：People were congregating in the town square to celebrate the holiday. 人们聚集在市中心广场庆祝节日。

例句：The birds were congregating on the power lines. 鸟儿们聚集在电线上。


近义词：assembling, gathering, collecting, meeting, clustering | 反义词：dispersing, scattering, separating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds congregate near the lake every evening. = 鸟儿们每天晚上都聚集在湖边。
People congregate in the city center during lunchtime. = 人们在午餐时间聚集在市中心。


一般过去时:
The crowd congregated outside the stadium before the game. = 在比赛开始前，人群聚集在体育场外。
They congregated in the church for the service. = 他们聚集在教堂参加礼拜。


现在完成时:
A large crowd has congregated to protest the new law. = 一大群人聚集起来抗议新法律。
Many people have congregated in the streets to celebrate the victory. = 许多人聚集在街上庆祝胜利。


过去完成时:
The protesters had congregated before the mayor arrived. = 市长到达之前，抗议者们已经聚集了。
The students had congregated in the library before the announcement. = 在宣布消息之前，学生们已经聚集在图书馆了。


将来时:
The fans will congregate outside the stadium tomorrow. = 粉丝们明天将在体育场外聚集。
People will congregate in the park for the picnic. = 人们将在公园里聚餐。


虚拟语气:
If they congregated earlier, they would have better seats. = 如果他们早点聚集，他们就能有更好的座位。
I wish the audience had congregated more quietly. = 我希望观众们能更安静地聚集。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Where do the birds congregate? = 鸟儿们在哪里聚集？
Why are people congregating here? = 人们为什么聚集在这里？


否定句:
The animals did not congregate near the water. = 动物们没有聚集在水边。
They didn't congregate in the usual spot. = 他们没有聚集在通常的地方。


PHRASE->
congregate together = 聚集在一起
congregate around = 聚集在...周围
congregate for = 为...而聚集
congregate in = 聚集在...
congregate to = 为...而聚集


词源:
From the Latin word "congregare," meaning "to gather together."  The prefix "con-" means "together," and "gregare" is related to the word "gregarious," meaning sociable or fond of company.
