regelate:/ˈriːɡjəleɪt/ | vt. |重新规定；重新管理；重新控制 | To regulate again; to control or govern anew.  This word is rarely used in modern English. It implies a re-establishment of rules or control after a period of deregulation or disorder. | Example: The government attempted to regelate the chaotic financial market after the crisis.  政府试图在危机后重新规范混乱的金融市场。 |近义词：reorganize, restructure, redefine |反义词：deregulate, loosen, liberalize |动词
