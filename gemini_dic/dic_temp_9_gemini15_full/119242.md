mica:/ˈmaɪkə/| n.|云母|A mineral consisting of hydrous aluminum phyllosilicates, occurring as easily cleavable transparent or translucent crystals, often forming thin flexible sheets. It is used in various applications, including electrical insulation, lubricants, and cosmetics.|例句：The artist used thin sheets of mica to create a shimmering effect in her painting.  艺术家用薄薄的云母片在她的画中创造出闪闪发光的效果。|例句： Mica is a common ingredient in many makeup products. 云母是许多化妆品中的常见成分。|近义词：muscovite, phlogopite|反义词:|
