obscure: /əbˈskjʊr/ | adj. |模糊的；晦涩的；无名的；偏僻的 | adj.  not discovered or known about; hidden; difficult to understand; not clear or distinct |


英文解析：The word "obscure" can describe something that is not easily seen, heard, or understood.  It can refer to things that are hidden, unknown, or difficult to comprehend.  It can also describe something that is not famous or well-known.


例句：

The meaning of the poem is obscure. = 这首诗的意思很晦涩。

The path led to an obscure village in the mountains. = 这条路通向山区一个偏僻的小村庄。


近义词：unclear, vague, ambiguous, indistinct, unknown, hidden, unnoticed, dim, faint, cryptic


反义词：clear, obvious, plain, transparent, famous, well-known, renowned, celebrated, bright, distinct


词性:形容词
