humidification:/ˌhjuːmɪdɪˈfeɪʃən/ | n. | 加湿；增湿作用 | The process of adding moisture to the air.  Humidification is crucial in dry climates to improve comfort and prevent respiratory problems. |  例句：The air conditioning system includes a humidification function. 空调系统包含一个加湿功能。

例句：The hospital uses humidification to prevent patients from suffering from dry air. 医院使用加湿器来防止病人遭受干燥空气的困扰。


近义词：moisturization, hydration | 反义词：dehumidification, drying
