expert-at: /ˈɛkspɜːrt æt/ | adj. | 熟练的；精通的 |  skilled or proficient in a particular area or activity.  | He's an expert at chess. 他是个西洋棋高手。She is expert at handling difficult customers. 她擅长处理难缠的顾客。 |近义词：proficient, skilled, adept, masterful | 反义词：inexperienced, unskilled, amateur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is expert at playing the piano. = 他擅长弹钢琴。
She is expert at solving complex problems. = 她擅长解决复杂的问题。


一般过去时:
He was expert at carpentry in his youth. = 他年轻时擅长木工活。
She was expert at negotiating deals. = 她擅长谈判交易。


现在完成时:
He has been expert at coding for over ten years. = 他精通编程已有十多年了。
She has been expert at public speaking since college. = 从大学时代起她就擅长公开演讲。


过去完成时:
He had been expert at painting before he took up sculpting. = 在他开始雕塑之前，他就擅长绘画了。
She had been expert at financial analysis for many years before joining the firm. = 加入公司之前，她已经在金融分析方面积累了多年的经验。


将来时:
He will be expert at data science after completing his degree. = 完成学位后，他将成为数据科学专家。
She will be expert at project management by next year. = 到明年她将精通项目管理。


虚拟语气:
If he were expert at marketing, he would have a better chance of success. = 如果他精通市场营销，他的成功机会就会更大。
If she were expert at coding, she could have developed the app herself. = 如果她精通编程，她就可以自己开发这个应用程序。

否定句:
