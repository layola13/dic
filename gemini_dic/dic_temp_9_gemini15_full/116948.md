mazes:/ˈmeɪzɪz/| n.|迷宫；令人困惑的事物|A maze is a network of paths and hedges designed to puzzle and confuse people.  Mazes can also refer to something complex and confusing.|例句：The children loved getting lost in the corn maze.孩子们喜欢在玉米迷宫里迷路。

The legal proceedings were a complete maze of confusing rules and procedures.法律程序是一系列令人困惑的规则和程序的复杂迷宫。|近义词：labyrinths, tangles, puzzles|反义词：straight path, clear path, simple solution|名词
