seduced:/sɪˈduːst/| v.| 诱惑；引诱；勾引 | To persuade or induce (someone) to do something, typically something morally wrong, by means of charm, flattery, etc.  |例句：He was seduced by the promise of quick riches. 他被一夜暴富的承诺诱惑了。

例句：The beautiful siren seduced the sailors to their deaths.  美丽的塞壬用歌声诱惑水手走向死亡。


近义词：tempt, entice, allure, charm, attract

反义词：repulse, deter, discourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seduces women with his charm. = 他用魅力诱惑女人。
She seduces her audience with her powerful speeches. = 她用她有力的演讲吸引听众。


一般过去时:
The salesman seduced the customer into buying the expensive car. = 销售员诱导顾客购买昂贵的汽车。
He was seduced by the idea of easy money. = 他被轻松赚钱的想法诱惑了。


现在完成时:
He has seduced many women throughout his life. = 他一生中诱惑过许多女人。
The advertising campaign has seduced consumers into buying the product. = 广告活动诱导消费者购买该产品。


过去完成时:
She had been seduced by his promises before she realized his true intentions. = 在她意识到他的真实意图之前，她已经被他的承诺所诱惑。
They had seduced the public into believing their lies. = 他们已经欺骗公众相信他们的谎言。


将来时:
He will seduce her with his romantic gestures. = 他将用浪漫的姿态来诱惑她。
The new marketing strategy will seduce a wider audience. = 新的营销策略将吸引更广泛的受众。


虚拟语气:
If he had not been seduced by greed, he would not have lost everything. = 如果他没有被贪婪所诱惑，他就不会失去一切。
I wish I had not been seduced into that bad deal. = 我真希望我没有被诱入那笔糟糕的交易。


被动语态:
Many people have been seduced by the promises of get-rich-quick schemes. = 许多人被快速致富计划的承诺所诱惑。
The audience was seduced by the performer's charisma. = 观众被表演者的魅力所吸引。


疑问句:
Was she seduced by his flattery? = 她是被他的奉承所诱惑吗？
Have you ever been seduced by a tempting offer? = 你曾经被诱人的提议所诱惑过吗？


否定句:
He did not seduce her; she willingly participated. = 他没有诱惑她；她是自愿参与的。
The company's marketing strategy did not seduce the target demographic. = 该公司的营销策略没有吸引目标人群。


PHRASE->
be seduced by  被…诱惑
seduce someone into doing something  诱使某人做某事
seduce someone away from  把某人从…诱惑走
seduce someone with  用…诱惑某人
the seductive power of…  …的诱惑力


词源:
来自古法语 *seduire，源于拉丁语 *ducere "to lead" ，与拉丁语 *se- "aside" 组合，引申为“引诱偏离正道”。
