geoponics:/ˌdʒioʊˈpɑːnɪks/| n.|土壤科学；地力学|The science or practice of cultivating the soil; soil science.  It encompasses the study of soil properties, their improvement, and their management for optimal plant growth.  Geoponics focuses on the physical, chemical, and biological aspects of soil, aiming to maximize crop yields and improve soil health. |例句：Modern geoponics emphasizes sustainable practices to protect soil fertility. 现代土壤科学强调可持续措施以保护土壤肥力。

例句：Research in geoponics is crucial for ensuring food security in a growing population. 土壤科学研究对于确保人口增长中的粮食安全至关重要。
