madhouse: /ˈmædˌhaʊs/ | n. |疯人院；混乱不堪的地方 | A place of great confusion and disorder; an insane asylum.  | The office was a madhouse during the holiday rush.  | 近义词：bedlam, chaos, pandemonium | 反义词：order, calm, tranquility
