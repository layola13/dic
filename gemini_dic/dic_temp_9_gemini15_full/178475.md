reconstructs:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌkts/| v.|重建；复原；重新构建|To build or create something again, often after it has been damaged or destroyed.  It involves putting together the parts or elements to form a whole.  The process can be physical, as in rebuilding a house, or abstract, as in reconstructing a historical event.|例句：The archaeologists are reconstructing the ancient city from the fragments they found. 考古学家正在根据他们发现的碎片重建古代城市。

例句：The police are trying to reconstruct the events leading up to the accident. 警方正在努力重建导致事故发生的事件。


近义词：rebuild, reconstruct, restore, reform, remake


反义词：destroy, demolish, raze, dismantle


词性：动词


USAGE->

He reconstructs old furniture. = 他修复旧家具。
