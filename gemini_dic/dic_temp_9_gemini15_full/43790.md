crematory:/ˈkrɛməˌtɔːri/| n. |火葬场；焚尸炉 |A crematory is a building or place equipped for cremating bodies.  It often includes furnaces and related equipment for the incineration of human remains. |例句：The crematory was a modern facility, designed for efficiency and respect.  这家火葬场是一座现代化的设施，旨在提高效率和尊重死者。

The family chose to have their loved one cremated at a local crematory.  这家人选择在当地一家火葬场火化他们的亲人。|近义词：incinerator, funeral home (sometimes)|反义词：burial ground, cemetery|词性 n.
