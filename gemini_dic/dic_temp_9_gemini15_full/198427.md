shredder:/ʃrɛdər/ | n. |碎纸机；撕碎机 | A machine that cuts paper or other material into small pieces.  |Example:  He used a shredder to destroy the confidential documents. 他用碎纸机销毁了机密文件。 |近义词：disintegrator, pulverizer |反义词：combiner, assembler

词源:
The word "shredder" comes from the verb "shred," meaning to cut or tear into small pieces.  "Shred" itself has Old English roots, likely derived from words meaning "to cut" or "to tear."  The "-er" suffix is a common agentive suffix in English, indicating a person or thing that performs the action of the verb.
