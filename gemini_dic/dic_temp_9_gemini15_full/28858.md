carpetbag:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡ/ | n. | 旅行袋；（美国南北战争后）南方败军政客 | A large, soft bag made of carpet material, traditionally used for carrying luggage.  After the American Civil War, the term was used derisively to refer to Northerners who went South to seek political office, often seen as exploiting the situation for personal gain. | Example: He packed his belongings into his old carpetbag. 他把他的行李装进旧旅行袋里。  The carpetbaggers dominated the Southern political landscape for a time.  地毯袋政客一度主导着南方的政治局面。 |近义词：satchel, bag, suitcase | 反义词：N/A
