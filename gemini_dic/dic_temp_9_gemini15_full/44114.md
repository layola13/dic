crispin:/ˈkrɪspɪn/| n. |克里斯平（男子名）,圣克里斯平节|A male given name; also, a saint's day celebrated on October 25th.|Crispin is a popular name in many countries. 克里斯平是一个在许多国家都很流行的名字。|The feast day of Saint Crispin is October 25th. 圣克里斯平节是在10月25日。|近义词:Christopher,  Crispian|反义词:  无
