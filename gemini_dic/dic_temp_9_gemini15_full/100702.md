intractably: /ɪnˈtræktəbli/ | adj. | 难以驾驭的；倔强的；棘手的 |  Difficult or impossible to manage or control; stubbornly resistant to persuasion or treatment. |

The problem proved intractable, resisting all attempts at a solution.  这个问题证明是棘手的，所有解决尝试都失败了。

The child's intractable temper made it difficult for her parents.  孩子倔强的脾气让她的父母很困难。


近义词：unmanageable, unyielding, obstinate, stubborn, recalcitrant

反义词：tractable, manageable, docile, compliant


词源：来自拉丁语 *intractare (无法处理)，由 *in- (不) + *tractare (处理) 组成。
