concretize: /ˈkɒŋkriˌtaɪz/| /ˈkɑːŋkriˌtaɪz/ | vt. | 使具体化；使具体表现出来 | To make (something abstract) concrete or real.  To give definite form to a plan, idea, etc.; to make real. |

例句：

The architect concretized his ideas into detailed blueprints.  建筑师将他的想法具体化为详细的蓝图。

They need to concretize their vague plans before they can start the project. 他们需要在开始项目之前将他们模糊的计划具体化。


近义词：realize, actualize, materialize, specify, define

反义词：abstract, generalize, idealize
