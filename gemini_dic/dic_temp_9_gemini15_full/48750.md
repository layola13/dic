definites:/ˈdɛfɪnɪts/ | adj. | 明确的；确定的；肯定的 | Certain or sure; leaving no doubt.  | Example:  The definites answer is yes.  明确的答案是肯定的。  |近义词：certain, sure, positive, unequivocal | 反义词：uncertain, doubtful, ambiguous, vague |
