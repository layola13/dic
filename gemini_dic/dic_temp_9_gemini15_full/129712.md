non-egocentric: /ˌnɒnˌeɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 非以自我为中心的；不自私的 | Not self-centered; unselfish; considering the needs and feelings of others.  | 例句：He showed a non-egocentric approach to the problem, considering the team's needs before his own. 他以一种非以自我为中心的的方式处理这个问题，先考虑团队的需要而不是他自己的。

例句：Her non-egocentric nature made her a popular and respected member of the team.  她不自私的天性使她成为团队中受欢迎且受人尊敬的一员。


近义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish, empathetic, considerate, objective

反义词：egocentric, selfish, self-centered, narcissistic, inconsiderate


词性: adjective
