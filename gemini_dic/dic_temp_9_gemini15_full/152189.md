patrician:/pəˈtrɪʃən/| n.|贵族；上流社会人士|A patrician is a person of noble birth or high social rank.  They are often associated with wealth, power, and influence within their society. The term often implies a sense of superiority and privilege.|例句：The patricians of Rome held considerable power and influence.  罗马的贵族拥有相当大的权力和影响力。|近义词：aristocrat, noble, elite|反义词：plebeian, commoner
