lose out:/luːz aʊt/| phrasal verb| 失去机会；错失良机；处于不利地位 | To fail to get something good or to miss an opportunity.  |例句：He lost out on the promotion because he didn't apply in time. 他因为没有及时申请而错过了晋升机会。|例句：Many small businesses lost out during the recession. 许多小型企业在经济衰退期间遭受损失。|近义词：miss out, forfeit, fail to get |反义词：gain, win, succeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lose out on many opportunities because of their lack of experience. = 他们因为缺乏经验而错失了许多机会。
We lose out if we don't invest in new technology. = 如果我们不投资新技术，我们会吃亏。


一般过去时:
She lost out on the scholarship because of her low GPA. = 她因为GPA低而失去了奖学金。
I lost out on a great job offer because I hesitated. = 我因为犹豫而错失了一份好工作。


现在完成时:
They have lost out on several potential clients due to poor marketing. = 由于营销不力，他们已经失去了几个潜在客户。
We have lost out on significant profits this quarter. = 本季度我们损失了大量的利润。


过去完成时:
He had lost out on many chances before he finally found success. = 他在最终获得成功之前错过了许多机会。
The company had lost out on a major contract because of a bidding error. = 公司因为投标错误而失去了一个重要的合同。


将来时:
If we don't act quickly, we will lose out to our competitors. = 如果我们不迅速行动，我们将输给竞争对手。
They will lose out on the benefits of early adoption. = 他们将失去早期采用的好处。


虚拟语气:
If I had applied earlier, I wouldn't have lost out on the job. = 如果我早点申请，我就不会失去这份工作。
She would have won the competition if she hadn't lost out on the crucial last round. = 如果她没有在关键的最后一轮比赛中失利，她就会赢得比赛。


被动语态:
Many people were lost out on the housing market crash. = 许多人在房地产市场崩盘中遭受损失。
The small businesses were lost out by the new regulations. = 小型企业因新的法规而遭受损失。


疑问句:
Will we lose out if we don't expand our product line? = 如果我们不扩大产品线，我们会吃亏吗？
Did they lose out on the deal because of a pricing error? = 他们是因为定价错误而失去交易的吗？


否定句:
We didn't lose out on the contract, we actually won it. = 我们没有失去合同，我们实际上赢得了它。
They didn't lose out; they were actually chosen for the project. = 他们没有失败；他们实际上被选中参与该项目。


PHRASE->
lose out on opportunities = 失去机会
lose out to the competition = 输给竞争对手
lose out on a deal = 失去交易
lose out on a promotion = 失去晋升机会
lose out big time = 遭受重大损失
