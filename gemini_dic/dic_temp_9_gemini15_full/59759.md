economies:/ɪˈkɑːnəmiːz/| n.|经济；节约；经济制度|The plural form of "economy," referring to multiple economic systems or the practice of saving in multiple contexts.  It can also refer to the efficient use of resources in various areas.|例句：The economies of East Asia are growing rapidly.  东亚各国经济正在快速增长。

例句：She employs several economies in her household management.她在家庭管理中采用了几种节约措施。|近义词：systems, savings, efficiencies|反义词：wastefulness, extravagance, profligacy|名词
