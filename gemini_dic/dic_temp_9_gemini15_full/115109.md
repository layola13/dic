managed:/ˈmænɪdʒd/| v.|管理；经营；设法做到|To successfully bring something to completion, often despite difficulties.  It implies skillful handling and overcoming obstacles.|例句：She managed to finish the project on time despite the setbacks. = 尽管遇到挫折，她还是设法按时完成了项目。|例句：He managed the company for over twenty years. = 他管理这家公司超过二十年了。|近义词：directed, controlled, handled, succeeded, accomplished|反义词：failed, neglected, abandoned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manages the team effectively. = 他有效地管理着团队。
She manages a large portfolio of clients. = 她管理着大量的客户。

一般过去时:
They managed to escape the burning building. = 他们设法逃出了燃烧的建筑物。
I managed to find a solution to the problem. = 我设法找到了解决这个问题的方法。

现在完成时:
She has managed to overcome many challenges in her life. = 她一生中克服了许多挑战。
He has managed the company successfully for many years. = 他多年来成功地经营着这家公司。

过去完成时:
They had managed to reach the summit before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经成功地到达了山顶。
She had managed to secure funding for her research project. = 她已设法为她的研究项目获得资金。

将来时:
I will manage to finish the report by Friday. = 我将在星期五之前完成报告。
They will manage the transition to the new system smoothly. = 他们将顺利地管理向新系统的过渡。

虚拟语气:
If I had managed to get there earlier, I would have seen him. = 如果我能早点到那里，我就会看到他了。
I wish I had managed to convince her. = 我真希望我能说服她。

被动语态:
The company is managed by a highly experienced team. = 这家公司由一支经验丰富的团队管理。
The project was managed efficiently. = 该项目管理效率很高。

疑问句:
How did you manage to do that? = 你是怎么做到的？
Will they manage to meet the deadline? = 他们能按时完成吗？

否定句:
I couldn't manage to open the door. = 我无法打开门。
They didn't manage to win the game. = 他们没有赢得比赛。


PHRASE->
manage to do something = 设法做某事
manage without something = 没有...也能应付
manage one's time = 管理时间
manage expectations = 管理预期
manage a smile = 强作欢颜
