miscaption:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃən/| n. |误标题；错误的标题；误解|A miscaption is an incorrect or misleading caption accompanying an image or other media.  It can be due to a simple mistake, intentional misrepresentation, or a lack of understanding of the context.  |Example: The photo of the cat sleeping peacefully had a miscaption claiming it was about to attack.|近义词：incorrect caption, wrong caption, misleading label|反义词：accurate caption, correct caption, truthful description|名词
