lade:/leɪd/| n.|梯子，阶梯|A ladder is a structure consisting of two long pieces of wood, metal, or rope with steps or rungs between them, used for climbing up or down.|例句：He climbed the ladder to reach the roof. 他爬上梯子到达屋顶。|近义词：stairs, steps|反义词:ground, floor|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ladder leans against the wall. = 梯子靠在墙上。
He uses a ladder to clean the windows. = 他用梯子擦窗户。


一般过去时:
The painter climbed the ladder yesterday. = 画家昨天爬了梯子。
She fell off the ladder. = 她从梯子上摔下来了。


现在完成时:
They have repaired the ladder. = 他们已经修理好了梯子。
He has used that ladder many times. = 他已经多次使用过那个梯子。


过去完成时:
She had climbed the ladder before the accident. = 事故发生前她已经爬上梯子了。
The workers had already removed the ladder. = 工人们已经移走了梯子。


将来时:
