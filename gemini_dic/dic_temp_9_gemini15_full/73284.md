forebodingly:/fɔːrˈboʊdɪŋli/| adv.|不祥地，预兆地，凶兆地 |In a way that suggests something bad will happen; ominously.  It implies a feeling of apprehension or dread about the future. |例句：The wind howled forebodingly, raising goosebumps on her arms.  = 风不祥地呼啸着，让她起了鸡皮疙瘩。 The dark clouds gathered forebodingly above the castle. =  乌云不祥地聚集在城堡上空。|近义词：ominously, menacingly, threateningly, uncannily|反义词：reassuringly, comfortingly, hopefully
