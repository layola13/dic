miasmata:/maɪˈæzmətə/| n.|瘴气，恶臭空气|A miasma is a noxious atmosphere or vapor, formerly believed to cause disease.|例句：The miasma theory of disease held that illnesses were caused by bad air. 瘴气致病理论认为疾病是由污浊的空气引起的。|近义词：vapors, fumes, effluvia|反义词：fresh air, pure air
