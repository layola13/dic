likening:/ˈlaɪkənɪŋ/ | n. |比拟；比喻；类比 | The act of comparing one thing to another; a resemblance or similarity.  | Example:  His likening of the political situation to a powder keg was quite apt.  他的把政治局势比作火药桶的比喻非常贴切。 |近义词:comparison, analogy, metaphor, simile |反义词:dissimilarity, contrast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor is likening the cell to a tiny city. = 教授正在把细胞比作一座小城市。
He likens his work to climbing a mountain. = 他把他的工作比作攀登一座山。


一般过去时:
She likened her experience to a rollercoaster ride. = 她把她经历比作过山车。
The author likened the character to a wolf in sheep's clothing. = 作者把这个人物比作披着羊皮的狼。


现在完成时:
He has likened the economic crisis to a perfect storm. = 他已经把经济危机比作一场完美的风暴。
Scientists have likened the brain to a complex network. = 科学家们已经把大脑比作一个复杂的网络。


过去完成时:
Before the speech, he had likened the upcoming election to a battle. = 在演讲之前，他已经把即将到来的选举比作一场战斗。
She had likened the situation to a ticking time bomb. = 她已经把这种情况比作一颗定时炸弹。


将来时:
The speaker will liken the challenges to a marathon race. = 演讲者将把这些挑战比作马拉松比赛。
They will liken the process to building a house brick by brick. = 他们将会把这个过程比作一块一块地盖房子。


虚拟语气:
If he likened the project to a jigsaw puzzle, it might be easier to understand. = 如果他把这个项目比作一个拼图游戏，可能更容易理解。
I wish he hadn't likened my work to a child's drawing. = 我真希望他没有把我的作品比作孩子的涂鸦。


被动语态:
The poem was likened to a beautiful tapestry. = 这首诗被比作一幅美丽的挂毯。
The new technology is being likened to a game changer. = 这项新技术正被比作一个改变游戏规则的东西。


疑问句:
How can you liken these two completely different things? = 你怎么能把这两件完全不同的事情相提并论呢？
To what would you liken the feeling of grief? = 你会把悲伤的感觉比作什么呢？


否定句:
He didn't liken the situation to a disaster. = 他没有把这种情况比作灾难。
She wouldn't liken her feelings to anything so simple. = 她不会把她自己的感觉比作任何如此简单的东西。


PHRASE->
likening something to something else = 把某物比作其他事物
a striking likening = 一个惊人的比喻
a forced likening = 一个牵强的比喻
a poetic likening = 一个诗意的比喻
by likening...to... = 通过把...比作...
