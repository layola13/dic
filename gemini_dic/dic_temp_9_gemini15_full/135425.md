nonperseverance: /ˌnɒnpɜːsɪˈvɪərəns/ | n. | 不坚持，缺乏毅力 | Lack of perseverance; the failure to persist in a course of action.  |  His nonperseverance led to his failure in the project. 他的缺乏毅力导致了他在项目中的失败。 | perseverance, persistence |  determination, resolve | 名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "hello".  We can examine how it's used in different sentence structures.


Simple sentences:
His nonperseverance was evident.  = 他的缺乏毅力是显而易见的。
The project failed due to nonperseverance. = 项目由于缺乏毅力而失败。


Complex sentences:
Because of his nonperseverance, he gave up easily. = 因为他缺乏毅力，他很容易放弃。
The team's ultimate success was in spite of some initial nonperseverance. = 团队最终的成功尽管一开始缺乏毅力。


Negative sentences:
It wasn't nonperseverance that caused the problem, but poor planning. = 导致问题的原因不是缺乏毅力，而是糟糕的计划。
There was no sign of nonperseverance in her approach. = 在她的方法中没有缺乏毅力的迹象。



PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "nonperseverance".  However, we can show phrases expressing the *idea* of nonperseverance:


Giving up easily:  = 轻易放弃
Lack of commitment: = 缺乏承诺
Quitting early: = 早早放弃
Half-hearted effort: = 半心半意的努力
Failure to follow through: = 无法坚持到底
