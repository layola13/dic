/pɪlz/ | n. |药丸；丸剂|A small, solid dose of medicine.  Pills are often swallowed whole. |例句：He took two pills for his headache. 他服了两片药丸治头痛。|例句：The doctor prescribed a course of pills. 医生开了个疗程的药丸。|近义词：tablet, capsule |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可根据语境用liquid medicine 液体药等表达)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes his pills every day. = 他每天都吃药。
She always takes her pills with water. = 她总是用水送服药丸。


一般过去时:
I took two pills before bed last night. = 昨晚睡觉前我吃了两粒药。
He took the pills as prescribed by the doctor. = 他按照医生的处方服药。


现在完成时:
I have taken all my pills this week. = 我这周已经把药都吃完了。
She has taken her medication regularly. = 她一直有规律地服药。


过去完成时:
He had taken his pills before the pain started. = 在疼痛开始之前，他已经服药了。
She had taken all the pills in the bottle. = 她已经把瓶子里所有的药都吃完了。


将来时:
I will take my pills after dinner. = 我晚饭后会吃药。
She will take the pills as directed on the label. = 她会按照标签上的说明服药。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken the pills earlier, I wouldn't be feeling so bad now. = 如果我早点吃药，现在就不会感觉这么难受了。
I wish I had taken the pills before the headache started. = 我真希望头痛开始前我就吃药了。


被动语态:
The pills were taken by the patient. = 药丸被病人服用了。
The pills are being developed by a pharmaceutical company. = 这些药丸正在由一家制药公司研发。


疑问句:
Have you taken your pills yet? = 你吃药了吗？
Did he take his pills on time? = 他按时吃药了吗？


否定句:
I did not take my pills today. = 我今天没吃药。
She hasn't taken her pills yet. = 她还没吃药。
