pursing:/ˈpɜːrsɪŋ/|/ˈpɝːsɪŋ/| vt.| 紧闭；追赶；追求；从事；皱着（脸）|To pursue something is to try to achieve it over a period of time.  To purse one's lips is to press them together, often showing displeasure or concentration. |例句：She is pursuing a career in medicine. 她正在追求医学事业。

例句：He was pursing his lips in thought. 他紧闭嘴唇思考着。


近义词：following, chasing, seeking, pursuing, achieving| 反义词：neglecting, ignoring, abandoning


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purses his lips when he's concentrating. = 他专注时会紧闭嘴唇。
She is pursuing her dream of becoming a writer. = 她正在追求成为作家的梦想。


一般过去时:
He pursued his studies diligently. = 他勤奋地学习。
She pursed her lips in disapproval. = 她不满地紧闭嘴唇。


现在完成时:
He has been pursuing this goal for years. = 他多年来一直在追求这个目标。
She has pursued many different career paths. = 她追求过许多不同的职业道路。


过去完成时:
He had been pursuing her for months before she finally agreed to a date. = 在她最终答应约会之前，他已经追求了她几个月。
She had pursued her education relentlessly before landing her dream job. = 在获得梦想的工作之前，她一直不懈地追求她的教育。


将来时:
He will pursue his legal claim. = 他将继续他的法律诉讼。
She will be pursuing a master's degree next year. = 她明年将攻读硕士学位。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pursue my passions. = 如果我是你，我会追求我的激情。
I wish I had pursued my dreams more aggressively. = 我真希望我以前更积极地追求我的梦想。


被动语态:
The matter is being pursued by the police. = 警方正在调查此事。
His career was pursued with great determination. = 他的事业是凭借极大的决心追求而来的。


疑问句:
Are you pursuing any hobbies at the moment? = 你现在有什么爱好吗？
What are you pursuing in life? = 你在生活中追求什么？


否定句:
He didn't pursue the opportunity. = 他没有抓住这个机会。
She wasn't pursuing a relationship with him. = 她并没有和他发展感情。


PHRASE->
pursue a goal = 追求目标
pursue an interest = 追求兴趣
pursue a career = 追求事业
pursue justice = 追求正义
pursue happiness = 追求幸福
