devastates:/ˈdɛvəsteɪts/| vt.|毁坏；使震惊；使极度悲伤|To damage something very badly or completely destroy it; to shock and upset someone very much.|例句：The hurricane devastated the coastal town.  飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。

例句：The news of her death devastated her family.  她去世的消息使她的家人悲痛欲绝。

近义词：destroy, wreck, ruin, shatter, overwhelm

反义词：build, construct, repair, restore, comfort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earthquake devastates the city every year. = 地震每年都摧毁这座城市。
He devastates his opponents in debates. = 他在辩论中击败了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The fire devastated the entire forest. = 大火摧毁了整片森林。
The news devastated her. = 这个消息使她震惊。


现在完成时:
The flood has devastated the farmland. = 洪水已经摧毁了农田。
The scandal has devastated his reputation. = 丑闻毁了他的名誉。


过去完成时:
The war had devastated the country before the peace treaty was signed. = 在和平条约签订之前，战争已经摧毁了这个国家。
They had devastated the enemy's defenses before the main attack. = 在主要进攻之前，他们已经摧毁了敌人的防御工事。


将来时:
The storm is expected to devastate the region. = 预计风暴将摧毁该地区。
If he continues like this, he will devastate his health. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会毁了自己的健康。


虚拟语气:
If the storm had hit, it would have devastated the town. = 如果风暴袭击了，它就会摧毁这个城镇。
I wish the news hadn't devastated her so much. = 我希望这个消息没有让她如此悲痛。


被动语态:
The city was devastated by the bombing. = 这个城市被轰炸摧毁了。
His reputation was devastated by the allegations. = 他的名誉被指控毁掉了。


疑问句:
Will the coming typhoon devastate the crops? = 即将来临的台风会摧毁庄稼吗？
Has the fire devastated the entire building? = 大火是否摧毁了整栋建筑物？


否定句:
The storm didn't devastate the coastal areas as much as expected. = 风暴没有像预期的那样严重摧毁沿海地区。
The earthquake did not devastate the city completely. = 地震并没有完全摧毁这座城市。
