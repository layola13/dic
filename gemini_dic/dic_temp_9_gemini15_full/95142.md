inapproachable: /ˌɪnəˈproʊtʃəbəl/ | adj. |难以接近的，高不可攀的，冷漠的 |  Unfriendly and unwilling to talk to people; seeming distant and superior.  |例句：The CEO seemed inapproachable, always surrounded by bodyguards and assistants.  首席执行官看起来难以接近，总是被保镖和助理包围着。

例句：Her aloof demeanor made her seem inapproachable to her colleagues. 她冷漠的态度让她在同事眼中显得难以接近。


近义词：unapproachable, distant, aloof, remote, reserved, cold, standoffish, forbidding


反义词：approachable, friendly, welcoming, warm, open, accessible
