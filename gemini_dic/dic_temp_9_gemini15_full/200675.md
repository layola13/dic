sleeked:/sliːkt/| v.|使光滑；使整洁；打扮整齐|To make something smooth and shiny, or to make oneself or something look neat and stylish.|She sleeked her hair back before the interview. 她面试前把头发梳理得光滑整齐。|近义词：smoothed, polished, groomed|反义词：roughened, disheveled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sleekes his hair every morning. = 他每天早上都梳理头发。
She sleekes her dog's fur regularly. = 她定期为她的狗梳理毛发。


一般过去时:
I sleeked my shoes before the party. = 我在派对前擦亮了我的鞋子。
He sleeked down his rebellious hair. = 他把他不羁的头发梳理服帖。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
