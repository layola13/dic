recreational:/ˌrɛkreˈeɪʃənəl/| adj.|娱乐的；消遣的；休闲的| Relating to or denoting activity done for enjoyment when one is not working.  It often implies leisure activities. |例句：He enjoys recreational activities such as fishing and hiking. 他喜欢钓鱼和远足等娱乐活动。

例句：The park offers a variety of recreational facilities, including tennis courts and a swimming pool.  公园提供各种各样的休闲设施，包括网球场和游泳池。


近义词：leisurely, avocational, sportive


反义词：work-related, professional, occupational


词性：形容词
