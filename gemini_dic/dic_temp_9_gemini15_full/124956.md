mufflers:/ˈmʌflər/| n. |消音器；围巾；护耳罩 |A muffler is a device fitted to the exhaust pipe of an internal combustion engine to reduce the noise.  It can also refer to a scarf worn around the neck for warmth or a type of earmuff. |例句：The car's muffler was broken and made a loud noise.  这辆车的消音器坏了，发出很大的噪音。

例句：She wore a wool muffler to keep warm in the cold weather. 她戴着羊毛围巾御寒。


近义词：silencer (for exhaust), scarf, ear warmer |反义词：amplifier (opposite function of muffler as a silencer)
