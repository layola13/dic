resentiment:/ˌrezɪnˈtɪmənt/| n. |怨恨；愤恨；不满|A feeling of deep and bitter anger and ill-will.  It often involves a sense of being wronged and a desire for revenge or retribution.  Resentment is usually directed towards a specific person or group, and can fester over time, impacting mental and physical health.|He harbors a deep resentiment towards his former employer for the unfair dismissal.|近义词：indignation, bitterness, animosity, anger |反义词：forgiveness, reconciliation, acceptance
