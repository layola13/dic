couping:/ˈkuːpɪŋ/| n.|政变；突然袭击|A coup is a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government.  It often involves the military overthrowing the existing leadership.|例句：The military coup ousted the democratically elected president.  这场军事政变推翻了民选总统。|例句：The attempted coup failed due to lack of public support.  由于缺乏公众支持，政变企图失败了。|近义词：overthrow, seizure, rebellion |反义词：peaceful transition, democratic election|名词
