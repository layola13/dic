frugal:/ˈfruːɡəl/ | adj. |节俭的；朴素的 |  Thrifty and economical; avoiding waste.  Frugal people are careful about how they spend their money and resources. They don't spend money unnecessarily. |例句：She lived a frugal life, even though she was wealthy.  她过着节俭的生活，即使她很富有。

She manages to feed her family on a frugal budget. 她设法用微薄的预算养活她的家人。


近义词：economical, thrifty, sparing, saving, modest | 反义词：extravagant, wasteful, lavish, prodigal, spendthrift | 词性:形容词
