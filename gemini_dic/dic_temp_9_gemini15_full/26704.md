cadence:/ˈkeɪdəns/|/ˈkeɪdns/| n. |节奏；韵律；抑扬顿挫；节奏感 |The rhythmic flow of a sequence of sounds or words.  It can refer to the beat or measure of music, the rise and fall of the voice in speaking, or the pattern of stressed and unstressed syllables in poetry. |The cadence of the poem was mesmerizing. 这首诗的节奏令人着迷。 The speaker's cadence was slow and deliberate.  说话者的节奏缓慢而谨慎。|rhythm, tempo, beat, meter |dissonance, cacophony|名词
