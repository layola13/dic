renown:/rɪˈnaʊn/ | n. | 名声，声誉 | Fame and widespread acknowledgement of ability or achievement.  Renown is often associated with something remarkable or extraordinary. |  Shakespeare's renown as a playwright is unparalleled.  莎士比亚作为剧作家的名声是无与伦比的。|  fame, reputation, prestige | obscurity, anonymity | 名词
