nozzles:/ˈnɒzəl/| n. |喷嘴；喷头 |A nozzle is a tube or pipe that is attached to the end of a hose or pipe to control the flow of a liquid or gas.  It often narrows the opening to increase the speed and pressure of the outflow. |例句：The firefighter adjusted the nozzle to increase the water pressure. 消防员调整喷嘴以增加水压。

例句：The garden hose has a variety of interchangeable nozzles for different watering needs. 花园软管有多种可互换的喷嘴，以满足不同的浇水需求。


近义词：spout, orifice, jet


反义词：None directly applicable, but the opposite concept would be a wide opening.


词性：名词
