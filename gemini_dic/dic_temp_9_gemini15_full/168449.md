probably: /ˈprɒbəbli/ /ˈprɑːbəbli/ | adv. |很可能，大概 |  Probably means in all likelihood; very likely. It expresses a degree of certainty that something will happen or is true, but not absolute certainty.  |例句：He will probably be late. 他很可能要迟到了。|例句：It will probably rain tomorrow. 明天可能会下雨。|近义词：likely, possibly, perhaps |反义词：certainly (in the context of expressing certainty), impossibly
