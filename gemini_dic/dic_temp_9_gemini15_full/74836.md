frenzied:/ˈfrɛnziːd/| adj.|狂乱的；疯狂的；极度兴奋的 | Extremely agitated; wild or uncontrolled. Characterized by or exhibiting frenzy. |例句：The frenzied crowd surged forward. 狂暴的人群蜂拥而上。

The dog, frenzied with fear, barked incessantly.  害怕得发狂的狗不停地叫着。

近义词：frantic, frantic, wild, hectic, agitated, manic, delirious

反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, composed
