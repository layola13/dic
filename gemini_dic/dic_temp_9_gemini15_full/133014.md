nonenthusiastically:/ˌnɒnɛnˌθjuːziˈæstɪkli/| adv. |不热心地，缺乏热情地 |Describes an action or behavior performed without much enthusiasm or interest.  It implies a lack of energy, passion, or excitement.|He answered the question nonenthusiastically. 他不情愿地回答了这个问题。|enthusiastically, eagerly, passionately | unenthusiastically, apathetically, indifferently
