overresisted:/ˌoʊvər.rɪˈzɪs.tɪd/ | adj. | 过度抵抗的，顽抗的 | Excessively resisted; stubbornly opposed.  This word describes a situation where resistance has gone beyond what is considered normal or reasonable. It implies a strong and prolonged opposition. | The overresisted reforms ultimately failed due to public outcry.  过度抵抗的改革最终由于公众的强烈抗议而失败了。 | stubbornly opposed, fiercely resisted | readily accepted, willingly complied
