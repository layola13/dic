pharmacopeias:/ˌfɑːrməˈkoʊpiəz/| n. |药典（复数）|A pharmacopeia is an official book containing a list of medicinal drugs with their effects and directions for their use.  It's a comprehensive guide for pharmaceutical preparations, including standards for quality, purity, and strength.  Pharmacopeias are usually published by national or international governmental or professional organizations. |The United States Pharmacopeia (USP) sets standards for the identity, strength, quality, and purity of medicines, food ingredients, and dietary supplements.  美国药典（USP）制定药品、食品成分和膳食补充剂的特性、效力、质量和纯度的标准。|近义词：formularies, dispensatories |反义词：None
