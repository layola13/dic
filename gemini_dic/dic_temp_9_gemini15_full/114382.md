make:/meɪk/| /meɪk/| vt. & n.|制作；使；造成；获得|to create or produce something; to cause something to happen or exist; to earn or achieve something|例句：She made a beautiful cake. 她做了一个美丽的蛋糕。

They made a lot of noise. 他们制造了很多噪音。|近义词：create, produce, build, form|反义词：break, destroy, ruin|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I make breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上做早餐。
She makes beautiful paintings. = 她画美丽的画。

一般过去时:
He made a mistake. = 他犯了一个错误。
They made a decision. = 他们做出了一个决定。

现在完成时:
We have made great progress. = 我们取得了很大的进步。
She has made many friends. = 她交了很多朋友。

过去完成时:
I had made the arrangements before they arrived. = 在他们到达之前，我已经做好了安排。
They had made a plan to escape. = 他们已经制定了一个逃跑计划。

将来时:
I will make a reservation for dinner. = 我将预订晚餐。
They will make a decision tomorrow. = 他们明天将做出决定。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would make a bigger cake. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会做一个更大的蛋糕。
I wish I had made a better choice. = 我希望我做出了更好的选择。

被动语态:
The cake was made by my mother. = 这个蛋糕是我妈妈做的。
The decision was made quickly. = 决定很快就被做出了。

疑问句:
What are you making? = 你在做什么？
Does she make her own clothes? = 她自己做衣服吗？

否定句:
I didn't make a sound. = 我没有发出声音。
He doesn't make much money. = 他赚的钱不多。
