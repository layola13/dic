resecrete: /ˌriːˈskriːt/ | vt. | 再分泌；重新分泌 | To secrete again or anew.  The process involves the renewed release of a substance that has already been secreted. | 例句：The glands resecrete the hormones after a period of inactivity. 腺体在一段时间的休眠后重新分泌激素。

The salivary glands resecrete saliva after a meal.  唾液腺在饭后重新分泌唾液。


近义词：re-excrete, re-emit


反义词：absorb,  reabsorb (depending on context)


词性：动词
