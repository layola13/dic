cams:/kæmz/| n.|凸轮；凸轮轴；摄像机|A cam is a rotating or sliding piece in a mechanical device which transforms rotary motion into linear motion, or vice versa.  It's also a colloquial term for cameras.|例句：The engine's camshaft was worn out.  发动机的凸轮轴磨损了。The security cameras captured the thief. 安全摄像头拍到了小偷。|近义词：lobe (in the context of a camshaft),  gear (in the context of mechanical function)|反义词：N/A
