nonveterinaries:/ˌnɑːnˈvɛtərɪnɛriːz/| n. |非兽医人员|People who are not veterinarians.  This term refers to individuals who do not hold veterinary degrees or licenses and are not professionally involved in veterinary medicine.| Example:  The new regulations impact both veterinarians and nonveterinaries who work at the animal shelter. 新规定影响在动物收容所工作的兽医和非兽医人员。|近义词：laypeople, nonprofessionals, civilians (in the context of veterinary medicine)|反义词：veterinarians
