They can cause a variety of diseases depending on the species and the host. |Example:  The veterinarian diagnosed the dog with a severe entozoon infestation.  兽医诊断出这只狗感染了严重的内寄生虫。|近义词:internal parasite, endoparasite |反义词:ectoparasite
