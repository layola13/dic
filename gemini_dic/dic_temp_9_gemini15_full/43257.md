cradled:/ˈkreɪdəld/| vt.| 轻轻地抱；怀抱；呵护；支撑；|To hold something or someone gently and carefully, often in the arms or hands; to support or protect something gently.|例句：The mother cradled her baby in her arms. 母亲轻轻地抱着她的婴儿。|例句：The valley was cradled by mountains. 山谷被群山环抱。|近义词：held, supported, nestled, embraced|反义词：abandoned, neglected, cast aside|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cradles her baby every night. = 她每晚都抱着她的婴儿。
He carefully cradles the fragile vase. = 他小心翼翼地抱着易碎的花瓶。


一般过去时:
