nonkosher:/ˌnɒnˈkoʊʃər/| adj.| 不符合犹太教饮食规定的；非犹太教洁净的 |Not conforming to the dietary laws of Judaism.  This term refers to food or practices that are forbidden according to Jewish dietary laws (kashrut).|  例句：That restaurant serves only kosher food; they don't have any nonkosher options. 那家餐馆只供应符合犹太教饮食规定的食物；他们没有任何不符合规定的食物选择。

例句：The ingredients list showed that the sausage was nonkosher, so I didn't buy it. 配料表显示香肠不符合犹太教饮食规定，所以我没有买。

近义词：unclean (in a religious context), forbidden, prohibited

反义词：kosher, permissible, allowed
