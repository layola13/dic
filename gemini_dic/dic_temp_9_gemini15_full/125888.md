muscovite:/ˈmʌskəvaɪt/| n. |白云母|A common mineral, a hydrous potassium aluminum silicate, that is a major constituent of many igneous and metamorphic rocks.  It is characterized by its perfect basal cleavage, resulting in thin, flexible sheets. |例句：Muscovite is often used in electronics as an insulator. 白云母常被用作电子元件中的绝缘体。|例句：The geologist identified the sample as muscovite based on its pearly luster and perfect cleavage. 地质学家根据其珍珠光泽和完美的解理将样品鉴定为白云母。|近义词：mica (a broader term)|反义词：N/A
