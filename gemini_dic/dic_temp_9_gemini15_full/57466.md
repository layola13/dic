draftier:/ˈdræftiər/| adj.|通风的，漏风的，简陋的 | More drafty; less well-insulated.  A draftier room is one that lets in more cold air.  The old house was draftier than we expected. |例句：The old house was draftier than we expected. 这栋老房子比我们预期的更漏风。

The wind howled through the draftier parts of the building. 风从建筑物漏风的部位呼啸而过。


近义词：airy, breezy, windy, exposed


反义词： airtight, insulated, sealed


词源：源自动词draft，意思是“吹气”，引申出“通风”的意思。后缀“-ier”表示比较级，表示“更通风的”。
