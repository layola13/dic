gire:/dʒaɪər/| n.|旋转，回旋；(尤指)轻快的旋转|A swift, graceful whirling or circling movement, especially one that is light and quick.|例句：The dancer executed a graceful gire across the stage.  舞者在舞台上优雅地旋转了一圈。|例句：The leaves performed a slow gire in the autumn breeze. 秋风中树叶缓缓旋转。|近义词：twirl, whirl, spin, swirl|反义词：straight line, standstill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The top continues to gire. = 陀螺继续旋转。
The dancer gires gracefully. = 舞者优雅地旋转。

一般过去时:
The ballerina gired across the stage. = 芭蕾舞演员在舞台上旋转。
The wind gired the leaves. = 风把树叶吹得旋转。

现在完成时:
The dancer has gired many times. = 舞者已经旋转了很多次。
The leaves have gired in the wind. = 树叶在风中旋转过。

过去完成时:
The top had gired for several minutes before it stopped. = 陀螺旋转了几分钟才停下来。
The dancer had already gired before the music started. = 音乐开始之前，舞者已经旋转过了。

将来时:
She will gire across the stage. = 她将在舞台上旋转。
The leaves will gire in the wind. = 树叶将在风中旋转。

虚拟语气:
If she could gire, she would. = 如果她能旋转，她就会旋转。
I wish I could gire like her. = 我希望我能像她一样旋转。

被动语态:
The leaves were gired by the wind. = 树叶被风吹得旋转。
(Note:  "Gire" is less commonly used passively.)


疑问句:
Does the top gire quickly? = 陀螺旋转得快吗？
Will the dancer gire again? = 舞者还会旋转吗？

否定句:
The top doesn't gire anymore. = 陀螺不再旋转了。
The dancer didn't gire during the performance. = 舞者在表演中没有旋转。
