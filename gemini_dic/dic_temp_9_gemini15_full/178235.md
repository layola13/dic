recompensates:/ˌrekəmˈpɛnseɪts/| vt.| 补偿；报答；酬谢 | To give someone something good in return for something they have done, suffered, or given up.  It implies making amends or providing equivalent value for a loss or service. |例句：The company compensates its employees generously. 公司慷慨地补偿其员工。

The government will recompense farmers for their losses due to the drought. 政府将赔偿农民因干旱造成的损失。


近义词：compensate, repay, reward, remunerate | 反义词：punish, penalize

USAGE->

The company recompenses its employees for their hard work. = 公司因员工的辛勤工作而给予补偿。
