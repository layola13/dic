prep:/prɛp/| pron.| 准备；预备；预习| A preparation, especially one made in advance.  It can also refer to something prepared beforehand or ready for use.|例句：The prep work for the party took all day.  派对的准备工作花了一整天。|例句：I need to do some prep before the exam. 我需要在考试前做一些准备。|近义词：preparation, readiness, groundwork|反义词：neglect, unpreparedness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do my prep every night. = 我每天晚上都预习功课。
She preps her speeches meticulously. = 她一丝不苟地准备她的演讲。

一般过去时:
He prepped the car for the race. = 他为比赛准备了汽车。
They prepped the ingredients before cooking. = 他们在烹饪前准备了配料。

现在完成时:
I have prepped for the presentation all week. = 我已经准备了一周的演示文稿。
She has prepped the documents for the meeting. = 她已经为会议准备好了文件。

过去完成时:
He had prepped the materials before the class started. = 在课程开始之前，他已经准备好了材料。
They had prepped the food earlier in the day. = 他们当天早些时候已经准备好了食物。

将来时:
I will prep the vegetables tomorrow. = 我明天将准备蔬菜。
She will prep the classroom before the students arrive. = 学生到达之前，她将准备教室。

虚拟语气:
If I had prepped better, I would have done better on the test. = 如果我准备得更好，考试成绩就会更好。
I wish I had prepped more for the interview. = 我希望我为面试做了更多准备。

被动语态:
The ingredients were prepped by the chef. = 配料是由厨师准备的。
The room was prepped for the conference. = 会议室已为会议做好了准备。

疑问句:
Have you prepped for the exam? = 你为考试做好了准备吗？
Is she prepped for the interview? = 她为面试做好了准备吗？

否定句:
I didn't prep for the test. = 我没有为考试做准备。
They hadn't prepped the equipment properly. = 他们没有正确地准备设备。


PHRASE->

prep time: 准备时间
get prepped: 做好准备
battle prep: 战前准备
