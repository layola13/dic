sennet:/ˈsɛnɪt/| n.|宣告乐曲；号角声；|A short musical flourish, typically played on trumpets or other brass instruments, often used to signal an entrance or exit, or to mark a significant event in a play or ceremony.|例句：The sennet announced the king's arrival.  宣告乐曲宣告了国王的到来。

例句：A loud sennet signaled the beginning of the tournament.  一声响亮的号角声标志着比赛的开始。|近义词：fanfare, flourish, call|反义词:silence, hush
