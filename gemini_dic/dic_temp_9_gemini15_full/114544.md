malapterurus:/ˌmælæpˈtɛrəs/| n. | 电鲇属 |  Malapterurus is a genus of electric catfish found in Africa.  They are characterized by their ability to generate strong electric discharges for defense and hunting.  The genus includes several species, each adapted to different habitats within Africa's freshwater systems. | Example: Several species of *Malapterurus*, including the electric catfish, are found in the Nile River.  几类电鲇属的鱼，包括电鲇，都生活在尼罗河中。| 近义词: electric catfish (though this refers to the common name, not the genus) | 反义词: N/A
