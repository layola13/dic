fettering: /ˈfɛtərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 束缚；限制 |  The act of restraining or hindering;  chains or shackles.  |  例句：The fettering of free speech is a serious concern. 言论自由受到限制令人担忧。
The heavy chains fettered the prisoner.  沉重的锁链束缚着囚犯。| 近义词：restraining, binding, shackling, confining | 反义词：liberating, freeing, unbinding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is fettering the press. = 政府正在限制新闻界。
He fetters his creativity with self-doubt. = 他用自我怀疑束缚了自己的创造力。

一般过去时:
They fettered the horse to a post. = 他们把马拴在柱子上。
The old laws fettered economic growth. = 旧的法律限制了经济增长。

现在完成时:
The regulations have fettered innovation for years. = 多年来，这些规章制度阻碍了创新。
She has fettered herself with unrealistic expectations. = 她给自己施加了不切实际的期望。

过去完成时:
They had fettered the suspect before questioning him. = 他们在审问嫌犯之前已经将其铐住了。
The company had fettered itself with debt before it collapsed. = 这家公司在倒闭前已经被债务缠身。

将来时:
The new policies will fetter small businesses. = 新政策将束缚小型企业。
They will fetter the criminal and bring him to justice. = 他们将逮捕罪犯并将其绳之以法。

虚拟语气:
If they fettered the dog, it wouldn't run away. = 如果他们拴住狗，它就不会跑掉了。
I wish they hadn't fettered my creativity. = 我希望他们没有束缚我的创造力。

被动语态:
The bird was fettered by a strong net. = 这只鸟被一个结实的网困住了。
His movements were fettered by the heavy coat. = 他厚重的外套限制了他的行动。

疑问句:
What fetters your progress? = 什么在阻碍你的进步？
Are the regulations fettering free trade? = 这些规章制度是否在限制自由贸易？

否定句:
The new law does not fetter individual liberties. = 新法律并没有束缚个人自由。
They did not fetter the suspect without a warrant. = 他们没有在没有搜查令的情况下逮捕嫌犯。
