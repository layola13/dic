indicant:/ˈɪndɪkənt/| adj.|指示的；表明的；象征的|Something that is indicant serves as a sign or indication of something else.  It suggests or points towards a particular condition, fact, or trend.|例句：The high fever is indicant of a serious infection. 高烧预示着严重的感染。|例句：Her pale face was indicant of her illness. 她苍白的脸色表明她生病了。|近义词：indicative, suggestive, symptomatic|反义词：unindicative, inconsequential
