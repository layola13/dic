mammoths:/ˈmæməθs/| n.|猛犸象|Extinct elephant-like mammals with hairy coats and long curved tusks.  They were widespread during the Pleistocene epoch.|例句：Scientists have found well-preserved mammoth remains in the Siberian permafrost. 科学家们在西伯利亚的永久冻土层中发现了保存完好的猛犸象遗骸。
例句：The woolly mammoth is an iconic symbol of the Ice Age.  长毛猛犸象是冰河时代的一个标志性象征。|近义词：mastodon (though technically a different genus)|反义词：None.
