ounded:/ˈaʊndɪd/| adj.|受伤的，受损的| wounded refers to an injury, typically a physical one caused by a weapon or accident,  It can also refer to emotional hurt.  The word implies a degree of seriousness.|例句：The soldier was wounded in battle.  这名士兵在战斗中受伤了。

The child's feelings were wounded by the harsh criticism.  孩子的感情受到了严厉批评的伤害。|近义词：injured, hurt, damaged|反义词：unharmed, unscathed, healthy
