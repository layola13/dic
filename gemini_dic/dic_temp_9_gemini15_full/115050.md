mandrakes:/ˈmændreɪks/| n. |曼德拉草 |A mythical plant, mentioned in the Bible, believed to have magical properties and shriek when uprooted.  Its root often resembles a human figure.  In some cultures, it was believed to have aphrodisiac properties or to bring good luck.  |例句：The witches brewed a potion using mandrakes and other herbs. 女巫用曼德拉草和其他草药酿造了一种药剂。

例句：According to legend, pulling a mandrake from the ground would cause a shriek that could kill a person. 传说中，从地上拔出曼德拉草会发出足以杀死人的尖叫声。


近义词：magic root (非正式),  herb (广义) |反义词：无
