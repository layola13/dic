defrauds:/dɪˈfrɔːdz/| vt.|欺诈；骗取； |To deprive (someone) of something, especially money, by deceit.  It implies a deliberate and dishonest act to gain something at another's expense.| 例句：He defrauded the company of thousands of dollars. 他欺骗公司骗取了数千美元。

The government accused him of defrauding taxpayers. 政府指控他欺骗纳税人。


近义词：cheat, swindle, deceive, trick, rip off | 反义词：help, assist, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defrauds his clients regularly. = 他经常欺骗他的客户。
The company defrauds investors through misleading advertising. = 该公司通过误导性广告欺骗投资者。


一般过去时:
They defrauded the bank of a large sum of money. = 他们诈骗银行一大笔钱。
The con artist defrauded many unsuspecting victims. = 骗子欺骗了许多毫无戒心的受害者。


现在完成时:
He has defrauded numerous people over the years. = 多年来，他已经欺骗了许多人。
The company has been defrauding customers for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在欺骗顾客。


过去完成时:
The investigators discovered that he had defrauded the insurance company before. = 调查人员发现他以前曾欺骗过保险公司。
It turned out that she had defrauded her own family. = 原来她欺骗了自己的家人。


将来时:
They will defraud more people if they are not caught. = 如果他们不被抓住，他们将欺骗更多的人。
He plans to defraud the system using this loophole. = 他计划利用这个漏洞来欺骗系统。


虚拟语气:
If he defrauded them, he would be in big trouble. = 如果他欺骗他们，他会有大麻烦。
I wish he hadn't defrauded the charity. = 我真希望他没有欺骗慈善机构。


被动语态:
Many investors were defrauded by the Ponzi scheme. = 许多投资者被庞氏骗局欺骗了。
The company was defrauded out of millions of dollars. = 公司被骗取了数百万美元。


疑问句:
Did he defraud the bank? = 他是否欺骗了银行？
Were they defrauded by the salesman? = 他们是被推销员欺骗了吗？


否定句:
He did not defraud anyone. = 他没有欺骗任何人。
The company denies that it defrauded its customers. = 该公司否认它欺骗了它的客户。


PHRASE->
defraud someone of something = 欺骗某人某物
defraud the public = 欺骗公众
defraud the government = 欺骗政府
be defrauded out of = 被骗取…
charged with defrauding = 被指控欺诈


词源:
From Old French *defrauder, from des- (from Latin dis-) + frauder ("to deceive, cheat").  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but possibly related to Old French frauder, from a Germanic root.
