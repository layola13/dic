memorialist: /məˈmɔːriəlɪst/ | n. |回忆录作者，纪念碑撰写者 | A person who writes memoirs; someone who writes or creates a memorial.  | Example:  The memorialist recounted her childhood in vivid detail in her bestselling memoir. 回忆录作者在她畅销的回忆录中生动地讲述了她的童年。 | 近义词：memoirist, biographer (if the memoir focuses on another person) | 反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "critic" if the memorialist's work is being critiqued.
