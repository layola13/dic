fraternizers:/ˈfrætərˌnaɪzərz/| n.|结交者；亲善者|People who fraternize, especially soldiers interacting socially with the enemy.  This word often carries a negative connotation, suggesting inappropriate familiarity or collaboration.|例句：The fraternizers were court-martialed for their actions. 那些与敌军交往的人因他们的行为而被军事法庭审判。|近义词：socializers,  associates (in a negative context)|反义词：enemies, antagonists|名词
