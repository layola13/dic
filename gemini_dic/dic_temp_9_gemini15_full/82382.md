grub:/ɡrʌb/| n. v.|幼虫；蛆；食物；挖掘；寻觅；吃；|英文解析：As a noun, "grub" refers to the larva of an insect, especially a beetle. It can also mean food, especially simple or informal food. As a verb, it means to dig in the earth, or to search for something persistently. |例句：The birds were digging for grubs in the soil.  = 鸟儿们正在泥土里寻找昆虫幼虫。
The hikers were hungry and decided to grub for some food. = 徒步旅行者们饿了，决定去寻找一些食物。|近义词：larva, worm, food, forage, search|反义词：adult(insect), feast(for food)
