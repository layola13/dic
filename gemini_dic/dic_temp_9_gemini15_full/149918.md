pallbearer:/ˈpɔːlˌbɛrər/ | n. | 抬棺者 | A person who helps carry a coffin at a funeral.  | The pallbearers carried the coffin slowly and solemnly. 抬棺者缓慢而庄严地抬着棺材。 | mourner, bearer |  (无明显的反义词) | 名词

They are pallbearers for the fallen soldier. 他们是阵亡士兵的抬棺者。

词源:
From "pall" (a cloth covering a coffin) + "bearer" (a person who carries something).  因此，"pallbearer"字面意思是“携带覆盖棺材的布的人”。
