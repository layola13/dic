phyle:/ˈfaɪliː/| n. | 门(生物分类)| A phylum is the highest taxonomic rank in the biological classification of organisms, below kingdom and above class.  It groups together organisms that share fundamental characteristics.  For example, Chordata is the phylum that includes all vertebrates. | Example: The phylum Arthropoda includes insects, spiders, and crustaceans.  节肢动物门包括昆虫、蜘蛛和甲壳类动物。|近义词：division (in botany)|反义词：None
