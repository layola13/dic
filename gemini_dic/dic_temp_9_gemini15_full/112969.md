lukewarmness:/ˈluːkwɔːrmnəs/| n. |不热心；冷淡；温吞；不热情|A lack of enthusiasm or interest; mildness or indifference.  It describes a state of being unenthusiastic, apathetic, or indifferent.  This can apply to feelings, actions, or responses.|例句：His lukewarmness towards the project was evident in his lack of participation.  他对这个项目的冷淡态度在他缺乏参与中显而易见。

The lukewarmness of the response to their campaign was disappointing.  他们活动的反响不热烈，令人失望。|近义词：apathy, indifference, coolness, unenthusiasm|反义词：enthusiasm, passion, zeal, fervor|词性：名词
