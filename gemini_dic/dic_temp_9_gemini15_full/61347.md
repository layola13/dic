embellished:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃt/ | adj. | 装饰的；润色的；美化的 |  To make something more attractive by adding decorative details or features.  It suggests an improvement in appearance or attractiveness, often through the addition of ornamentation or detail. | 例句：The cake was embellished with intricate icing flowers. = 蛋糕装饰着复杂的糖霜花。


The story was embellished with fantastical details. = 故事中添加了奇幻的细节。


近义词：adorned, decorated, ornamented, enhanced, beautified | 反义词：stripped, plain, undecorated, bare


词源:  Comes from the Old French *embellir*  (to make beautiful), which itself is from *bel* (beautiful).  The prefix "em-" is an intensifying prefix.
