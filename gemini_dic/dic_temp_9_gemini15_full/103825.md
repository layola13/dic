The word "joye" is an archaic spelling of "joy".  Therefore, a separate analysis of "joye" is not necessary.  Instead, the following analysis is for the modern spelling "joy".


joy:/dʒɔɪ/ | n. |快乐，喜悦 | a feeling of great pleasure and happiness |例句：The birth of her child filled her with joy. 她孩子的出生使她充满了喜悦。|例句：She expressed her joy by singing. 她唱歌表达了她的喜悦。|近义词：happiness, delight, gladness, pleasure |反义词：sadness, sorrow, grief, misery |名词
