riches:/ˈrɪtʃɪz/| n.|财富，财产|great wealth; abundant possessions.|例句：His riches allowed him to travel the world.  他的财富使他能够周游世界。|例句：She inherited considerable riches from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了相当多的财富。|近义词：wealth, fortune, affluence|反义词：poverty, destitution, indigence
