inquired:/ɪnˈkwaɪrd/| v.|询问，打听|To ask for information.  |例句：I inquired about the price of the tickets. 我询问了票价。|近义词：asked, questioned, investigated|反义词：answered, responded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inquires about the weather forecast daily. = 他每天都询问天气预报。
She inquires politely before borrowing anything. = 她借东西之前都会礼貌地询问。


一般过去时:
I inquired about the lost luggage at the airport. = 我在机场询问了丢失的行李。
They inquired into the cause of the accident. = 他们调查了事故的原因。


现在完成时:
She has inquired about the job opening several times. = 她已经多次询问过关于这个职位空缺的事情。
He has inquired after your health. = 他问过你的健康状况。


过去完成时:
I had inquired about the train schedule before arriving at the station. = 我到达车站之前已经询问过火车时刻表了。
They had inquired about the possibility of a refund. = 他们已经询问过退款的可能性。


将来时:
I will inquire about the availability of the room tomorrow. = 我明天将询问房间的可用性。
She will inquire further into the matter. = 她将进一步调查此事。


虚拟语气:
If I had inquired earlier, I would have known the result. = 如果我早点询问，我就知道结果了。
I wish I had inquired about the deadline. = 我真希望我早点问过截止日期。


被动语态:
The matter is being inquired into by the police. = 警方正在调查此事。
The price was inquired about by many customers. = 许多顾客都询问过价格。


疑问句:
Did you inquire about the delivery time? = 你询问过送货时间了吗？
Has she inquired about the new policy? = 她询问过新政策了吗？


否定句:
I didn't inquire about the details. = 我没有询问细节。
They did not inquire into the matter thoroughly. = 他们没有彻底调查此事。


PHRASE->
inquire about = 询问关于...
inquire after = 询问(某人的健康状况)
inquire into = 调查，探究
make inquiries = 进行询问
inquire of = 向…询问
