masks:/mæskz/ | n. |面具；口罩；伪装 |A mask is something worn to cover the face, often for disguise, protection, or performance.  Masks can be made of various materials and have different purposes.  |例句：She wore a mask to protect herself from the dust. 她戴着口罩以防尘土。

例句：The actor wore a scary mask for the play. 演员在剧中戴了一个可怕的面具。


近义词：disguise, cover, veil |反义词：exposure, unveiling |词性:名词
