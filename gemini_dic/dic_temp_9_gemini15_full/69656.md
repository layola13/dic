fenced:/fɛnst/| adj.|围起来的，用篱笆围住的| surrounded by a fence; enclosed |The garden was fenced to keep the animals out. 花园被围起来以防止动物进入。| enclosed, surrounded, confined |open, unfenced


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The field is fenced. = 田地被围起来了。
The farmer fences his land every year. = 农民每年都为他的土地围栏。


一般过去时:
They fenced the yard yesterday. = 他们昨天给院子围上了篱笆。
The horses were fenced in. = 马被围起来了。


现在完成时:
He has fenced the entire property. = 他已经为整个房产围上了篱笆。
The land has been fenced for years. = 这块地已经围栏多年了。


过去完成时:
They had fenced the area before the event. = 他们在活动前已经将该区域围起来了。
The animals had been fenced in securely. = 动物们已经被安全地围起来了。


将来时:
We will fence the garden next week. = 我们下周将为花园围栏。
The construction company will have fenced the site by Friday. = 建设公司将在周五前为工地围栏。


虚拟语气:
If they fenced the garden, the dog couldn't escape. = 如果他们给花园围上篱笆，狗就不能逃跑了。
I wish they had fenced the pool area. = 我希望他们已经给游泳池区域围上了栏杆。


被动语态:
The area was fenced off for safety reasons. = 该区域出于安全原因被封闭了。
The field is being fenced at the moment. = 田地正在被围起来。


疑问句:
Is the area fenced? = 这个区域被围起来了吗？
Was the garden fenced before? = 花园以前被围起来过吗？


否定句:
The garden isn't fenced. = 花园没有被围起来。
They didn't fence the property. = 他们没有给房产围栏。


PHRASE->
