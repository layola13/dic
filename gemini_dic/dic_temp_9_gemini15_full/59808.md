ecstasy:/ˈɛk.stə.si/| n. |狂喜；欣快；摇头丸 | An overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement.  |例句：The birth of her child filled her with ecstasy. 她孩子的出生使她充满了狂喜。

例句：The music induced a state of ecstasy. 音乐使人进入狂喜的状态。


近义词：rapture, euphoria, bliss, delight |反义词：misery, despair, agony, anguish


词源:  From Middle French *extase, from Late Latin extasis, from Greek ἔκστασις (ékstasis), from ἐκστασις (ekstasis), from ἐκ- (ek-) "out" + στῆναι (stênai) "to stand". Literally "a standing outside oneself," referring to a state of being outside oneself, often in a religious or spiritual context.
