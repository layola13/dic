dominants:/ˈdɒmɪnənts/ /ˈdɑːmɪnənts/ | n. adj. |支配的；主要的；显性的|  The word "dominant" refers to something that is most important, powerful, or influential. As a noun, it typically refers to a gene that determines the expression of a particular trait. As an adjective, it describes something that exerts control or influence. |

例句：

His dominant personality influenced the whole team.  他的强势个性影响了整个团队。

The dominant allele for eye color is brown.  眼睛颜色的显性等位基因是棕色。


近义词：predominant, prevalent, leading, chief, governing, controlling

反义词：subordinate, recessive, minor, insignificant


词性：Noun, Adjective
