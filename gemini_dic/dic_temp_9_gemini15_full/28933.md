carroll:/ˈkærəl/| n.  |卡罗尔（人名）|Carroll is a surname of Irish origin, meaning "free man". It is also a given name, often used for both males and females.  The name is associated with Lewis Carroll, the author of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland.|例句：My friend's name is Carroll. 我的朋友名叫卡罗尔。|近义词:  (as a name)  Charles, Carol|反义词: N/A
