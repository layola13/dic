doubleclick:/ˈdʌblklɪk/| v.|双击|To click a computer mouse button twice in quick succession.  This action typically selects an item or performs a specific function within a software application.|例句：Double-click the icon to open the program. 双击该图标以打开程序。

例句：I double-clicked the file, and it opened immediately. 我双击了文件，它立即打开了。|近义词：click twice, tap twice|反义词：single-click


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He double-clicks the mouse to open the file. = 他双击鼠标打开文件。
She double-clicks the icon to start the game. = 她双击图标开始游戏。

I double-clicked the link, but nothing happened. = 我双击了链接，但没有任何反应。
