clinostat:/ˈklɪnəstæt/| n. | 临床旋转器；植物旋转器 | A clinostat is a device that slowly rotates a plant or other organism to simulate the effects of weightlessness or microgravity.  This is often used in experiments to study the effects of gravity on growth and development. | Example: Researchers used a clinostat to study how plants grow in simulated microgravity conditions.  研究人员使用临床旋转器来研究植物如何在模拟微重力条件下生长。 |近义词：rotator (in a specific context)|反义词：None specifically
