demilitarized:/ˌdiːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/| adj.|非军事化的；解除武装的|  Free from military forces or weapons; not used for military purposes.  The word implies a deliberate action to remove military presence or capabilities.| 例句：The demilitarized zone between the two countries is heavily patrolled.  两国之间的非军事区受到严密的巡逻。

The demilitarized island is now a popular tourist destination.  这个非军事化的岛屿现在成了一个受欢迎的旅游胜地。


近义词：neutralized, unarmed, pacifistic|反义词：militarized, armed
