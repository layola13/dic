monstrance:/ˈmɒnstrəns/ | n. |圣体匣；展示；炫耀 |A monstrance is a vessel, usually made of precious metals and adorned with jewels, used in the Catholic Church to display the consecrated Eucharist for veneration.  It is often ornately decorated and designed to highlight the sacred host. |例句：The priest held up the monstrance, allowing the congregation to adore the Eucharist.  = 牧师举起圣体匣，让会众敬拜圣体。

The monstrance was a masterpiece of craftsmanship, glittering with gold and precious stones. = 这座圣体匣是工艺的杰作，金光闪闪，镶嵌着宝石。


近义词：reliquary (圣物匣), display, exhibition |反义词：concealment, hiding
