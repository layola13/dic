self-destroyed: /ˌsɛlf dəˈstrɔɪd/ | v. | 自毁，自我毁灭 |  To destroy oneself or itself; to cause one's own destruction.  | The building self-destructed after the explosion.  爆炸后，建筑物自行坍塌。 The project self-destructed due to poor management.  由于管理不善，项目自行瓦解。|  immolate, annihilate | survive, thrive |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system self-destructs if the password is entered incorrectly three times. = 如果密码输入三次错误，系统将自毁。
The bomb self-destructs after five minutes. = 炸弹五分钟后自毁。


一般过去时:
The spaceship self-destructed to avoid capture. = 宇宙飞船为了避免被俘获而自毁。
The faulty device self-destructed during the test. = 故障设备在测试过程中自毁。


现在完成时:
The evidence has self-destructed, making it impossible to solve the crime. = 证据已经自毁，使破案成为不可能。
The company has self-destructed due to years of mismanagement. = 公司由于多年的管理不善而自我毁灭。


过去完成时:

The reputation of the politician had self-destructed after the scandal broke. = 丑闻爆发后，这位政治家的名誉已经自我毁灭了。


将来时:
The missile will self-destruct if it deviates from its course. = 如果导弹偏离航向，它将自毁。
The program is designed to self-destruct after a certain period of time. = 该程序设计在一段时间后自毁。


虚拟语气:
If the system had a self-destruct mechanism, we could have prevented the data breach. = 如果系统有自毁机制，我们就可以防止数据泄露。
I wish the virus had self-destructed before it infected my computer. = 我希望病毒在我电脑感染之前就自毁了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
