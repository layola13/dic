overdoctrinaire: /ˌoʊvərˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛər/ | adj. | 过于教条的；墨守成规的 | Excessively dogmatic or rigid in adherence to doctrines or principles.  It suggests an inflexibility and unwillingness to consider alternative viewpoints. |

例句：

His overdoctrinaire approach to education stifled creativity in the classroom.  他的教育方法过于教条，扼杀了课堂上的创造力。

The overdoctrinaire policies of the government led to widespread discontent. 政府墨守成规的政策导致了民众的广泛不满。


近义词：dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire, authoritarian, uncompromising, stiff-necked


反义词：flexible, open-minded, tolerant, compromising, adaptable
