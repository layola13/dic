pickery:/ˈpɪkəri/| n.|挑剔；吹毛求疵；(尤指对食物的)挑食|The act of being picky or choosy, especially about food.  It often implies excessive or unreasonable criticism.|例句：Her pickery at the buffet was embarrassing.  她的自助餐挑剔行为令人尴尬。

例句：The child's pickery made mealtimes a battleground.  孩子的挑食让用餐时间变成了战场。


近义词：fastidiousness, fussiness, squeamishness |反义词：uncritical, indiscriminate, undemanding


词性: 名词
