pseudopsychological:/ˌsuːdoʊsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 似是而非的心理学的；伪心理学 |  Relating to or characteristic of pseudopsychology;  appearing to be psychological but lacking scientific basis.  It often involves unsubstantiated claims, techniques, or theories presented as scientifically valid. | Example:  The seminar offered pseudopsychological techniques that promised to unlock hidden potential, but lacked any empirical evidence.  这个研讨会提供了一些似是而非的心理学技巧，声称可以释放隐藏的潜力，但却没有任何经验证据。 | 近义词：unscientific, unfounded, bogus, quack | 反义词：scientific, evidence-based, valid
