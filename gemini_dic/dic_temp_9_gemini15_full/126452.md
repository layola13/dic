mystique:/mɪˈstiːk/| n. |神秘感，魅力，迷惑力 |An aura of mystery and fascination; something mysterious, fascinating, or alluring.  It often refers to a quality that is difficult to understand or explain, often associated with power or allure. |例句：The mystique surrounding the ancient temple drew many visitors. 古庙周围的神秘气氛吸引了许多游客。

例句：The band cultivated a mystique of rebellion and danger.  乐队营造了一种叛逆和危险的神秘感。

近义词：charm, allure, fascination, enigma, magic

反义词：clarity, transparency, ordinariness, banality

词性: 名词
