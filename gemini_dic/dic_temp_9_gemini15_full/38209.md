compunctions:/kəmˈpʌŋkʃənz/| n.|良心不安；悔恨；内疚|Feelings of guilt or moral scruple that prevent or follow the doing of something bad.|She felt no compunctions about lying to the police. 她对向警察撒谎毫无内疚感。|近义词：remorse, regret, guilt, qualms|反义词：remorselessness, unconcern
