coursebook:/ˈkɔːrsbʊk/| n. |课程教材，教科书 |A coursebook is a textbook used in a course of study.  It typically contains lessons, exercises, and other materials designed to help students learn the subject matter. |Example: The students were assigned to read Chapter 3 in the coursebook. 学生被要求阅读教材的第三章。|近义词：textbook, manual |反义词：None
