schoolma'am:/ˈskuːlˌmæm/| n. |女教师，女校长|A female schoolteacher, especially one in a relatively elementary school.  The term often carries a slightly informal or even slightly condescending connotation, suggesting a strict or old-fashioned teacher.|例句：The schoolma'am was known for her strict discipline.  那位女教师以其严格的纪律而闻名。|例句：She remembered her old schoolma'am fondly, even though she was quite strict. 她 fondly 回忆起她以前严格的老师。|近义词：schoolteacher, teacher, instructress|反义词：student, pupil
