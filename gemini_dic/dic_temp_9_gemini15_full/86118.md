heartburned: /ˈhɑːrtbɜːrnd/ | adj. | 心烧的，胃灼热的 | Feeling a burning sensation in the chest or upper abdomen, typically caused by acid reflux.  | Example:  I woke up with a terrible heartburned this morning.  今早我醒来时胃灼热得厉害。 | Near synonyms: acid indigestion, pyrosis, esophageal reflux | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but the absence of heartburn could be described as) comfortable digestion,  no acid reflux.
