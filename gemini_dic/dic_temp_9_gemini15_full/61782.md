emperor:/ˈɛmpərər/| n. |皇帝，君主|A male sovereign ruler of an empire.|The Roman emperor ruled a vast territory.|近义词：monarch, sovereign, king |反义词：subject, citizen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The emperor rules the empire. = 皇帝统治着帝国。
He is the emperor of a powerful nation. = 他是一个强大国家的皇帝。


一般过去时:
The emperor died last year. = 皇帝去年去世了。
The Roman emperor conquered many lands. = 罗马皇帝征服了许多土地。


现在完成时:
The emperor has reigned for many years. = 皇帝已经统治了很多年了。
He has been emperor since 2000. = 他从2000年起就一直是皇帝。


过去完成时:
The emperor had already left before the rebellion began. = 在叛乱开始之前，皇帝已经离开了。
Before the war, the emperor had consolidated his power. = 在战争之前，皇帝巩固了他的权力。


将来时:
The emperor will visit the provinces next month. = 皇帝下个月将访问各省。
They believe the emperor will soon abdicate. = 他们认为皇帝很快就会退位。


虚拟语气:
If he were emperor, he would rule justly. = 如果他是皇帝，他会公正地统治。
I wish I were emperor for a day. = 我希望有一天我能当皇帝。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
