redismissed:/ˌriːdɪsˈmɪst/| v.|再次解雇，重新解雇|To dismiss someone again after they have already been dismissed once.|The employee was redismissed after violating company policy again.  这位员工再次违反公司政策后被重新解雇。|The company redismissed the worker for insubordination. 公司因不服从而再次解雇了该工人。|dismiss, fire, sack|reinstate, rehire|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redismiss employees who violate company rules. = 他们会再次解雇违反公司规章制度的员工。
The company redismisses underperforming staff regularly. = 公司定期再次解雇表现不佳的员工。


一般过去时:
He was redismissed from his job last week. = 他上周被再次解雇了。
She was redismissed for her repeated tardiness. = 她因一再迟到而被再次解雇。


现在完成时:
They have redismissed several employees this year. = 今年他们已经再次解雇了几名员工。
The company has redismissed him twice for misconduct. = 公司因行为不端而两次再次解雇了他。


过去完成时:
He had been redismissed before the new CEO arrived. = 新任首席执行官到来之前，他已经再次被解雇了。
They had redismissed her after she failed to meet the deadline again. = 她再次未能按时完成任务后，他们再次解雇了她。


将来时:
They will redismiss him if he continues to be late. = 如果他继续迟到，他们将会再次解雇他。
The manager said he would redismiss anyone who broke the rules. = 经理说，任何违反规章制度的人都会被再次解雇。


虚拟语气:
If he had followed the rules, he would not have been redismissed. = 如果他遵守了规则，他就不会被再次解雇了。
I wish they hadn't redismissed her; she was a valuable employee. = 我希望他们没有再次解雇她；她是一位宝贵的员工。


被动语态:
He was redismissed without warning. = 他被毫无预警地再次解雇了。
Several employees were redismissed due to budget cuts. = 由于预算削减，几名员工被再次解雇了。


疑问句:
Was he redismissed for a serious offense? = 他是因为严重违规而被再次解雇的吗？
Have any employees been redismissed recently? = 最近有没有员工被再次解雇？


否定句:
He wasn't redismissed; he resigned. = 他没有被再次解雇；他是辞职的。
She wasn't redismissed despite her mistakes. = 尽管她犯了错误，但她并没有被再次解雇。


PHRASE->

redismissed without cause = 无故再次解雇
redismissed for misconduct = 因行为不端再次解雇
redismissed unfairly = 不公平地再次解雇
