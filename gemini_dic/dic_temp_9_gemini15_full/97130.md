infiltrate:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪt/ | vt.  |渗透；潜入；渗透进入 |To pass into or through a substance gradually; to enter or become established gradually and often secretly in a place or group.|The enemy soldiers infiltrated our lines during the night.  敌人士兵在夜间渗透了我们的防线。|The new ideas gradually infiltrated the organization. 新思想逐渐渗透到这个组织中。| penetrate, permeate, seep | obstruct, block |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water infiltrates the soil slowly. = 水缓慢地渗透到土壤中。
The enemy attempts to infiltrate our defenses. = 敌人试图渗透我们的防御。


一般过去时:
The spies infiltrated the enemy camp. = 间谍潜入了敌营。
Rainwater infiltrated the basement. = 雨水渗入了地下室。


现在完成时:
The pollutants have infiltrated the groundwater. = 污染物已经渗透到地下水中。
Terrorist groups have infiltrated the country. = 恐怖组织已经渗透到这个国家。


过去完成时:
The virus had infiltrated the computer system before we detected it. = 在我们发现病毒之前，它就已经渗透到计算机系统中了。
The rebels had infiltrated the city before the attack. = 在袭击之前，叛乱分子已经渗透到城市中。


将来时:
The army will infiltrate the enemy territory. = 军队将渗透到敌方领土。
The new technology will infiltrate every aspect of our lives. = 新技术将渗透到我们生活的方方面面。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy infiltrated our base, we would be in serious trouble. = 如果敌人渗透到我们的基地，我们将陷入严重的困境。
I wish the rumors hadn't infiltrated our ranks. = 我真希望谣言没有渗透到我们的队伍中。


被动语态:
Our defenses were infiltrated by the enemy. = 我们的防御被敌人渗透了。
The city was infiltrated by the rebels. = 城市被叛乱分子渗透了。


疑问句:
Did the enemy infiltrate our lines? = 敌人是否渗透了我们的防线？
Has the new policy infiltrated the organization yet? = 新政策已经渗透到这个组织了吗？


否定句:
The enemy did not infiltrate our defenses. = 敌人没有渗透我们的防御。
The water did not infiltrate the dam. = 水没有渗透大坝。


PHRASE->
infiltrate into: 渗透到……
infiltrate with: 渗透……
infiltrate through: 渗透穿过……
deep infiltration: 深度渗透
infiltrate the ranks: 渗透到队伍中
