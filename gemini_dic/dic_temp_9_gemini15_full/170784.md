pseudocultivated: /ˌsuːdoʊˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 似栽培的；貌似栽培的 | Apparently cultivated; having the appearance of being cultivated but not genuinely so.  This term often refers to plants or ecosystems that resemble cultivated ones but have arisen through natural processes or human intervention that falls short of true cultivation.  |  The field appeared pseudocultivated, with some wild plants interspersed among the crops.  这片田地看起来像是人工种植的，但杂草丛生，混杂着农作物。|  cultivated, domesticated | wild, uncultivated
