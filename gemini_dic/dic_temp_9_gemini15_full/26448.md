byss:/bɪs/| n.|亚麻纤维，细麻布|Fine, strong fiber from the flax plant, used in ancient times for making fine linen.  It refers to a type of fine linen fabric, often associated with luxury and high quality.  The word also has a more archaic usage referring to the flax plant itself.|例句：The ancient Egyptians used byss to create their finest garments.  古埃及人用亚麻纤维制作他们最精美的衣物。

The shimmering byss of the priest's robe was breathtaking. 祭司长袍闪闪发光的亚麻布令人叹为观止。|近义词：flax, linen|反义词：coarse fabric, rough material
