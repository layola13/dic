outbuilding:/ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋ/| n. |附属建筑，外屋|A secondary building on a property, separate from the main house.  It is often used for storage, workshops, or guest accommodation.|例句：The old outbuilding was used to store garden tools.  旧的附属建筑用来存放园艺工具。|例句：They converted the outbuilding into a charming guest house. 他们把附属建筑改造成了一间迷人的宾馆。|近义词：shed, annex, dependency|反义词：main house, principal building
