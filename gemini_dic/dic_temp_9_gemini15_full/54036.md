discussers:/dɪˈskʌsərz/| n. |讨论者，参与讨论的人|People involved in a discussion or debate.  The discussers reached a consensus after a lengthy debate.|The panel of discussers engaged in a lively exchange of ideas.|近义词：participants, debaters, participants in a discussion|反义词：listeners (in the context of not participating)|名词
