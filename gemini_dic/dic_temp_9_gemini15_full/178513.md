recontracting:/ˌriːkənˈtræktɪŋ/ | v. | 再签订合同；重新签订合同 | The act of entering into a new contract to replace or modify an existing one. This often happens when there are changes in circumstances, or when the original contract is found to be inadequate or flawed.  | Example: After the initial contract proved unsatisfactory, the two parties engaged in recontracting to address the shortcomings.  = 最初的合同被证明不令人满意后，双方进行了重新签订合同以解决不足之处。 | 近义词： renegotiation, re-negotiating, amending, modifying | 反义词：terminating, canceling
