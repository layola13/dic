harquebus:/ˈhɑːrkɪbʌs/| n. |火绳枪|A harquebus is a type of firearm, a matchlock gun, that was used in the 15th and 16th centuries. It was heavier than a handgun, and usually fired from a rest.  |Example: The soldiers lined up, their harquebuses resting on their shoulders.  士兵们排成一列，他们的火绳枪架在肩膀上。  The battle was decided by a volley of harquebus fire.  战斗被一阵火绳枪齐射决定了。|近义词：arquebus, matchlock|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with modern firearms.
