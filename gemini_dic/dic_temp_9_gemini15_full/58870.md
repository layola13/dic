duramen:/dʊˈreɪmən/| n. |心材，木质部|The duramen is the hard, central wood of a tree, usually darker in color than the surrounding sapwood.  It is formed from dead cells and provides structural support. |例句：The duramen of the oak tree is very dense and resistant to decay.  橡树的心材非常致密且耐腐烂。|例句：The furniture was made from the duramen of a rare tropical hardwood. 这件家具是用一种稀有的热带硬木的心材制成的。|近义词：heartwood |反义词：sapwood |名词
