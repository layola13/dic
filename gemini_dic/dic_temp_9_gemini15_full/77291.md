gatecrashing:/ˈɡeɪtkræʃɪŋ/| v.|闯入，不请自来 |The act of attending a party or event without an invitation.  |例句：They gatecrashed the party and caused a lot of trouble. 他们不请自来参加派对，并制造了很多麻烦。

例句：Several uninvited guests gatecrashed the wedding reception.  好几个不速之客闯入了婚礼招待会。


近义词： crashing, intruding, trespassing, crashing a party


反义词：being invited, receiving an invitation


词性：动词

词源:
The word "gatecrashing" is a compound word.  "Gate" refers to the entrance to a place, often a secured or controlled entrance. "Crash" in this context means to enter forcefully or uninvited.  The combination suggests forcefully entering a place without permission, typically a party or event.  The term originated in the early 20th century.
