cityless: /ˈsɪtiliːs/ | adj. | 无城市的；远离城市的 |  Lacking cities; without cities; far from cities.  This word describes places or situations devoid of urban characteristics or influence. It emphasizes the absence of cities and the prevalence of rural or undeveloped areas.  |

例句：The vast, cityless plains stretched as far as the eye could see. =  广阔无垠的平原一眼望不到边。

The cityless landscape was both beautiful and isolating. =  无城市的景观既美丽又孤立。


近义词：rural, unurbanized, undeveloped, countrified

反义词：urban, metropolitan, city-based


词性:形容词
