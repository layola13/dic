lithodomus:/lɪˈθɒdəməs/| n. |凿石蛤，石蛏|A marine bivalve mollusk of the family Mytilidae, that bores into rocks and stones.  They are often found in intertidal zones and are known for their ability to create burrows in hard substrates. |Example: Lithodomus species are often found in the Mediterranean Sea.  地中海经常能发现凿石蛤的种类。|近义词：rock borer, date mussel|反义词：N/A
