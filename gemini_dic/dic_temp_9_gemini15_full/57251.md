downlo:/ˈdaʊnloʊ/| vt.|下载|To copy (a file, program, etc.) from a computer network to a local computer.|例句：I downloaded the latest software update. 我下载了最新的软件更新。|近义词：transfer, obtain, acquire |反义词：upload


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I download music every day. = 我每天都下载音乐。
They download files from the server. = 他们从服务器下载文件。

一般过去时:
I downloaded a movie last night. = 我昨晚下载了一部电影。
She downloaded all the necessary documents. = 她下载了所有必要的文档。

现在完成时:
I have downloaded all the pictures. = 我已经下载了所有的图片。
He has downloaded many apps to his phone. = 他已经下载了很多应用程序到他的手机上。

过去完成时:
I had downloaded the game before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经下载好了游戏。
They had downloaded the report before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经下载好了报告。

将来时:
I will download the new album tomorrow. = 我明天会下载这张新专辑。
She will download the files after the meeting. = 会议结束后她会下载这些文件。


被动语态:
The software was downloaded automatically. = 软件自动下载了。
The files are being downloaded now. = 文件正在下载中。


疑问句:
Can I download this video? = 我可以下载这个视频吗？
Have you downloaded the latest driver? = 你下载了最新的驱动程序了吗？


否定句:
I didn't download the file. = 我没有下载那个文件。
He couldn't download the game because of the slow internet. = 因为网络慢，他无法下载游戏。
