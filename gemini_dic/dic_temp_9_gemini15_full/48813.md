defluous:/ˈdɛfluəs/| adj.|多余的；不必要的；累赘的 | Superfluous means exceeding what is sufficient or necessary.  It implies an excess that is unnecessary or even burdensome.  The word often describes things that are extravagant, lavish, or wasteful. |例句：The lengthy introduction was defluous to the main point of the essay.冗长的引言与文章的主题无关。
The extra decorations were defluous and detracted from the overall simplicity of the design.额外的装饰是多余的，削弱了设计的整体简洁性。|近义词：superfluous, redundant, unnecessary, excessive, surplus |反义词：necessary, essential, required, vital
