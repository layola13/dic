melliphagous:/ˈmɛlɪfəɡəs/| adj.|食蜜的，食花蜜的|Relating to or characteristic of animals that feed on honey or nectar.  The term often refers to birds, particularly honeyeaters.|Example:  Many melliphagous birds have long, slender beaks adapted for probing flowers. 许多食蜜鸟类都长着细长的喙，适合探入花朵采蜜。|近义词： nectarivorous |反义词： carnivorous, insectivorous
