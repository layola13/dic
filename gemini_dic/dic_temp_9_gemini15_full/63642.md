{eos}: /ˌiː.oʊˈɛs/ |abbr.|  结束符，文件结束标记 | End Of Stream.  It is a special character or sequence of characters that signals the end of a data stream, often used in computer programming and data transmission.  It indicates that no more data is expected from the source. | 例句：The program reads data until it encounters the {eos} character. = 程序读取数据，直到遇到{eos}字符。


The {eos} marker was reached prematurely. = {eos}标记过早到达。


近义词：EOF (End Of File), EOT (End Of Transmission) | 反义词：无
