merriest:/ˈmɛriɪst/| adj.|最快乐的，最愉快的|Most joyful or cheerful.  It is the superlative form of "merry".|例句：The children were having the merriest time at the party.孩子们在派对上玩得最开心。

例句：It was the merriest Christmas I'd ever had.  那是我度过的最快乐的圣诞节。

近义词： happiest, jolliest, gayest, cheeriest
反义词：saddest, most miserable, most gloomy
