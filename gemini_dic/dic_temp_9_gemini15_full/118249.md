merchandisable: /ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可销售的；适销的 | Suitable for sale; able to be sold profitably.  This describes goods that are attractive to consumers and likely to sell well.  It implies not just saleability but also a degree of desirability and profitability.


例句：

The new line of clothing is highly merchandisable, with its stylish designs and affordable prices. = 这条新的服装系列非常适销对路，款式时尚，价格合理。

The damaged goods were deemed unmerchandisable and were therefore discarded. =  损坏的货物被认为不可销售，因此被丢弃了。


近义词：saleable, marketable, sellable, commercially viable


反义词：unsaleable, unmarketable, unsellable


词性：形容词
