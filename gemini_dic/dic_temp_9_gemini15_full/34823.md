clines:/klaɪnz/ | n. | 渐变；连续谱；cline | A cline is a gradual change in a character or feature along a geographical transect.  It often refers to a continuous gradation in a phenotypic character across a geographic area, often correlated with an environmental gradient.  For example, a cline might describe the gradual change in skin color from the equator towards the poles. | Example:  The researchers documented a clear cline in beak size across the island chain.  研究人员记录了该岛链上喙大小的明显渐变。  |近义词: gradient, continuum, gradation |反义词: discontinuity, abrupt change | 名词
