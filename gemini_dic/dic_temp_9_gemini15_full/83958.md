half-consumed:/ˌhæf kənˈsuːmd/| adj.| 半消耗的；半吃完的 |Partially eaten or used.  Something that is half-consumed is only partly used or eaten. |例句：The half-consumed bottle of wine sat on the table.  桌子上放着一瓶喝了一半的酒。

例句：The half-consumed cake was still delicious.  这块吃了一半的蛋糕仍然很好吃。

近义词：partially eaten, partly used, unfinished

反义词：fully consumed, completely eaten, finished

词性:形容词
