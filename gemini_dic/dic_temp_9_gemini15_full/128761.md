niggles:/ˈnɪɡəlz/| n. v.| 小麻烦；琐碎抱怨；微痛；反复唠叨 | A niggle is a minor complaint or worry; a small, persistent pain or irritation.  It can also refer to the act of repeatedly complaining about small things. |例句：I have a niggling doubt about the project's success. 我对项目的成功有点儿隐隐的担忧。
The constant niggling pain in my knee is driving me mad. 我膝盖持续的隐隐作痛快把我逼疯了. |近义词：minor complaint, quibble, grumble, gripe, ache |反义词：major problem, significant issue, serious pain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He constantly niggles about the smallest things. = 他总是为一些鸡毛蒜皮的小事抱怨。
These small details niggle at my mind. = 这些小细节让我心里烦躁。


一般过去时:
She niggled at him until he finally agreed. = 她一直对他唠叨，直到他最终同意。
The small problem niggled at me all day. = 这个小问题让我烦了一整天。


现在完成时:
He has been niggling at me about my work all week. = 他整个星期都在为我的工作唠叨我。
That nagging doubt has niggled at me for days. = 那挥之不去的疑虑已经困扰我好多天了。


过去完成时:
She had niggled at him for weeks before he finally gave in. = 在他最终让步之前，她已经对他唠叨了好几个星期了。
I had niggled over the details for hours before I found the solution. = 我花了几个小时才解决掉这些细节问题。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he niggled at me any more, I would explode. = 如果他再对我唠叨，我会爆发。
I wish he wouldn't niggle at me so much. = 我希望他不要总是对我唠叨。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
