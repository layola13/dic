handbells:/ˈhændbɛlz/ | n. |手铃 | Handbells are small, tuned bells that are played by hand, often in groups or ensembles to create musical performances. They are typically made of metal and are held individually by the player.  | The church choir performed a beautiful piece with handbells. 教堂唱诗班用小型手铃演奏了一首优美的乐曲。|  chimes, bells |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
