expel:/ɪkˈspɛl/| vt.|驱逐，开除，排出|to force someone to leave a place, especially a school or organization; to force something out.|The student was expelled from school for cheating.  学生因作弊被学校开除。

The volcano expelled a cloud of ash. 火山喷出云状的火山灰。|oust, dismiss, eject|admit, welcome, retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school expels students who cheat. = 学校开除作弊的学生。
The factory expels harmful chemicals into the air. = 工厂向空气中排放有害化学物质。

一般过去时:
They expelled him from the club. = 他们把他逐出了俱乐部。
The pump expelled all the water from the tank. = 水泵把水箱里的水都排空了。

现在完成时:
The government has expelled several diplomats. = 政府已经驱逐了几名外交官。
The pressure has expelled all the air from the container. = 压力已将容器中的所有空气排出。

过去完成时:
He had expelled the intruder before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经把入侵者赶走了。
The machine had expelled all the waste before it shut down. = 机器在关闭之前已经排出了所有废物。

将来时:
They will expel him if he continues to misbehave. = 如果他继续行为不端，他们将会开除他。
The rocket will expel its fuel as it ascends. = 火箭上升时会排出燃料。

虚拟语气:
If they expelled him, it would be a great loss. = 如果他们开除他，那将是一个巨大的损失。
I wish they hadn't expelled him from the team. = 我真希望他们没有把他开除出队伍。

被动语态:
He was expelled for his disruptive behavior. = 他因扰乱秩序的行为而被开除。
The toxins were expelled from the body naturally. = 毒素自然地从体内排出。

疑问句:
Will they expel him from the university? = 他们会把他从大学开除吗？
Does the machine expel any harmful substances? = 这台机器会排出任何有害物质吗？

否定句:
The school didn't expel him despite his bad grades. = 尽管他的成绩很差，学校也没有开除他。
The pump didn't expel all the water. = 水泵没有排出所有的水。
