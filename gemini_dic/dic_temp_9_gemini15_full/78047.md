genuineness:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnəs/| n. | 真实性；真诚；纯正 | The genuineness of his apology was questionable.  他的道歉的真实性令人怀疑。 | authenticity, sincerity, truthfulness | artificiality, falseness, hypocrisy | 名词


The genuineness of her emotions was evident in her tearful eyes. 她真挚的情感从她泪汪汪的眼睛里显而易见。


近义词: authenticity, sincerity, truthfulness, reality


反义词: artificiality, falseness, hypocrisy, sham, pretense


词源:  From genuine + -ness.  "Genuine" ultimately derives from the Latin *genuinus, meaning "of noble birth," "native," or "true."  The suffix "-ness" indicates a state or quality.
