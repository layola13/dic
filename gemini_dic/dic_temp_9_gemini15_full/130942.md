nonassertive:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːrtɪv/| adj.|非独断的，不武断的，顺从的|Not assertive; lacking in self-assurance or forcefulness.  Characterized by a lack of decisiveness or firmness. |例句：He was nonassertive in the meeting, failing to express his opinions. 他在会议上表现得很顺从，没有表达自己的意见。
例句：Her nonassertive nature often led to her being taken advantage of. 她顺从的性格经常导致她被别人利用。|近义词：submissive, passive, compliant, unassuming|反义词：assertive, forceful, aggressive, dominant|词性:形容词
