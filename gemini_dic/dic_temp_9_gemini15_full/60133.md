effectives:/ɪˈfektɪvz/ | adj. |有效的，生效的 | Effective strategies are crucial for achieving goals.  有效的策略对于实现目标至关重要。  |近义词：efficient, productive, successful |反义词：ineffective, useless, fruitless |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy is effective immediately. = 新政策立即生效。
These methods are effective in treating the disease. = 这些方法对治疗这种疾病有效。


一般过去时:
The medicine was effective in relieving his pain. = 这药有效地缓解了他的疼痛。
Her efforts were effective in solving the problem. = 她的努力有效地解决了问题。


现在完成时:
The changes have been effective in improving productivity. = 这些变化有效地提高了生产力。
Our marketing campaign has been effective in increasing sales. = 我们的营销活动有效地增加了销售额。


过去完成时:
The treatment had been effective before the relapse. = 在复发之前，治疗是有效的。
The strategy had been effective until the market changed. = 直到市场变化之前，该策略一直有效。


将来时:
The new software will be effective in streamlining our workflow. = 新软件将有效简化我们的工作流程。
The project will be effective in improving community relations. = 该项目将有效改善社区关系。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were effective, we would see results by now. = 如果计划有效，我们现在应该看到结果了。
I wish the medication were more effective. = 我希望这种药物更有效。


被动语态:
The changes were effectively implemented. = 这些变化得到了有效的实施。
The problem was effectively addressed by the team. = 该问题由团队有效地解决了。


疑问句:
Is this treatment effective for all patients? = 这种治疗对所有患者都有效吗？
Are these measures effective in preventing further damage? = 这些措施能有效防止进一步的损害吗？


否定句:
The drug was not effective in treating the infection. = 该药物对治疗感染无效。
His attempts to resolve the conflict were ineffective. = 他试图解决冲突的尝试无效。



PHRASE->
effective immediately = 立即生效
cost-effective = 经济有效的
effective date = 生效日期
effective communication = 有效沟通
effective management = 有效管理
