nonseizures:/ˌnɑːnˈsiːʒərz/| n. |非癫痫发作|Periods or episodes that mimic seizures but are not caused by abnormal electrical activity in the brain.  These can be caused by various factors such as psychogenic nonepileptic seizures (PNES), syncope (fainting), migraine, or other neurological conditions.|Example: The doctor determined that the patient's episodes were nonseizures, ruling out epilepsy.|近义词：pseudo-seizures, nonepileptic events|反义词：seizures
