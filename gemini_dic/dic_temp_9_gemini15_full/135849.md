nonprosecutable: /ˌnɒnˈprɒsɪkjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可起诉的 |  Not subject to prosecution; unable to be prosecuted.  This term typically refers to a case where there is insufficient evidence, the statute of limitations has expired, or there are other legal reasons why prosecution is impossible or inappropriate. |  The judge ruled the case nonprosecutable due to lack of evidence.  法官裁定此案因证据不足而不可起诉。 | 近义词：unprosecutable,  untriable | 反义词：prosecutable
