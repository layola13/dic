howlet: /ˈhaʊlɪt/ | n. |猫头鹰(尤指角鸮)|A howlet is a type of owl, especially a small owl such as a scops owl.  The term is now largely archaic or poetic. |例句：The howlet hooted mournfully in the dark woods.  夜里猫头鹰在幽暗的树林里凄厉地叫着。|  The howlet's call echoed through the valley. 猫头鹰的叫声在山谷中回荡。|近义词：owl, screech owl, little owl |反义词：None specifically.
