proxy:/ˈprɒksi/|ˈprɑːksi/| n. v.| 代理；代理人；代表；[计算机] 代理服务器 | A proxy is a person or thing that acts on behalf of another.  It can also refer to a server that acts as an intermediary for requests from clients seeking resources from other servers. |例句：He acted as a proxy for his sick brother. 他代替生病的兄弟行事。

例句：Our company uses a proxy server to enhance security and filter web traffic.  我们的公司使用代理服务器来增强安全性并过滤网络流量。


近义词：agent, representative, substitute, stand-in | 反义词：principal, original


词性：名词，动词
