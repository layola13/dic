英文解析:The mesothorax is the middle segment of the thorax of an insect, located between the prothorax and the metathorax.  It typically bears the second pair of legs and the forewings (in winged insects).

The mesothorax of the dragonfly is particularly strong, supporting its powerful flight muscles.  = 蜻蜓的中胸特别强壮，支撑着它强大的飞行肌肉。
