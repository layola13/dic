exclusivist:/ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪst/| adj. |排外的；唯我独尊的；排他主义的 |Someone who is an exclusivist believes that only a select group of people should be allowed to participate in something or benefit from something.  They often promote the idea that their group is superior or more deserving. |The exclusivist policies of the club kept many people from joining.  这个俱乐部的排外政策阻止了许多人加入。|近义词：elitist, sectarian, bigoted |反义词：inclusive, egalitarian, open-minded
