rutheniate:/ruːˈθiːnieɪt/| n. |钌酸盐；钌化物 |A compound containing ruthenium in a high oxidation state, typically as a part of an anion.  Rutheniates are often used in materials science and catalysis. |Example:  Scientists are investigating the catalytic properties of various rutheniates.  科学家们正在研究各种钌酸盐的催化性能。|近义词：ruthenium compound |反义词：无
