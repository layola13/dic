definitives:/dɪˈfɪnətɪvz/| n.|决定性的事物；权威著作；最终决定|Something that is conclusive or decisive; an authoritative work; a final decision. |The judge's decision was definitive, ending the long legal battle.  法官的判决是最终决定性的，结束了这场旷日持久的法律斗争。|definitive evidence, conclusive proof |ambiguous, uncertain|名词


例句:

The book is considered a definitive guide to the subject. 这本书被认为是该主题的权威指南。

His testimony provided definitive proof of the defendant's guilt. 他的证词提供了被告有罪的确凿证据。
