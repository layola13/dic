loveed:/lʌvd/| v.|爱过，喜爱过|The past tense of "love". It indicates a love that existed in the past but may or may not continue in the present.|例句：I loveed her deeply, but we are no longer together. 我曾经深深地爱着她，但我们不再在一起了。|近义词：cherished, adored, cared for|反义词:hated, despised.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I love pizza. = 我爱披萨。
She loves to dance. = 她喜欢跳舞。

一般过去时:
I loved that movie. = 我喜欢那部电影。
They loved to play together. = 他们喜欢一起玩。

现在完成时:
I have loved you since I first met you. = 从我第一次见到你我就爱上你了。
She has loved animals all her life. = 她一生都热爱动物。

过去完成时:
I had loved him for many years before we got married. = 在我们结婚之前，我已经爱了他很多年了。
She had loved painting since childhood. = 她从小就热爱绘画。

将来时:
I will always love you. = 我会永远爱你。
They will love their new home. = 他们会喜欢他们的新家。

虚拟语气:
If I loved her, I would marry her. = 如果我爱她，我会娶她。
I wish I loved to sing. = 我希望我喜欢唱歌。

被动语态:
(The verb "love" is rarely used in the passive voice, as it typically requires a direct object representing the recipient of love.)  A more suitable example would use a similar verb: He was adored by his fans. 他受到粉丝们的崇拜。
She was cherished by her family. 她受到家人的珍爱。


疑问句:
Did you love the present? = 你喜欢这个礼物吗？
Do you love your job? = 你喜欢你的工作吗？

否定句:
I did not love him anymore. = 我不再爱他了。
She doesn't love spicy food. = 她不喜欢辣的食物。


PHRASE->
