physics's: /ˈfɪzɪksɪz/ | n. | 物理学的；物理的 |  The possessive form of "physics," indicating belonging or possession.  It refers to something related to or owned by the field of physics.  This form is less common than using "of physics" or a similar phrasing. | Example:  Physics's laws govern the universe. = 物理学的定律支配着宇宙。


The study of physics's principles is crucial for understanding the world around us. = 学习物理学的原理对于理解我们周围的世界至关重要。

近义词: of physics, pertaining to physics, relating to physics

反义词: N/A (Not applicable;  it's not a word with an antonym in the same way that adjectives might have opposites)

词性: Noun (possessive)
