contextualised:/kənˈtekstʃʊəlaɪzd/| adj.|语境化的，情境化的|Placed or considered within its context; interpreted or understood in relation to its context.  This means the word or idea is understood based on the situation or circumstances in which it is used.|例句：The data was contextualised within the broader historical trends. 数据在更广泛的历史趋势中得到了语境化解释。|例句：His remarks, when contextualised, were not as offensive as they initially appeared.  他的话语，在语境化之后，并没有一开始看起来那么冒犯。|近义词：situated, framed, interpreted|反义词：decontextualized, isolated
