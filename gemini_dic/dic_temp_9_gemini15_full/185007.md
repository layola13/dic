retentor: /rɪˈtɛntər/ | n. | 保持器；抑制器；记忆体；滞留物 | A device or substance that retains something.  It can refer to a mechanical device that holds something in place, a chemical substance that prevents something from escaping, or a part of the brain associated with memory. | Example: The dam acts as a retentor for water.  水坝充当水的保持器。  The new medication is a potent retentor of moisture in the skin. 新药是皮肤中水分的有效保持剂。 | 近义词：retainer, holder, reservoir | 反义词：releaser, expeller
