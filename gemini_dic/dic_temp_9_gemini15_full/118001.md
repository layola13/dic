memoriam:/məˈmɔːriəm/| n. |纪念，追悼|In memory of; a tribute to someone who has died.  It often refers to a dedication or inscription in memory of someone.  The word is often used in the phrase "in memoriam."|例句：The book was published in memoriam of her late husband. 这本书是为纪念她已故的丈夫而出版的。

例句：A plaque in memoriam was placed on the wall.  一块纪念碑被放置在墙上。


近义词：remembrance, commemoration, tribute|反义词：oblivion, forgetfulness|词性:n.
