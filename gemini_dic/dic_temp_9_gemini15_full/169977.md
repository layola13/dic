protagonist:/proʊˈtæɡənɪst/|/ˈprɒtæɡənɪst/| n. |主角，主人公 |The main character in a story, play, or movie; the leading actor or actress.  A person who is the main participant or leader in an event or situation.| 例句：The protagonist of the novel is a young woman who travels to a distant land. 这部小说的主人公是一位前往遥远国度旅行的年轻女子。

例句：The protagonist’s journey was filled with unexpected challenges.  主人公的旅程充满了意想不到的挑战。


近义词：hero, heroine, lead, main character | 反义词：antagonist, villain
