precompliances:/ˌpriːkəmˈplaɪənsɪz/| n. |预先符合；预先遵守；提前符合规定 |The term "precompliances" refers to actions or measures taken in advance to ensure compliance with future regulations or requirements.  It suggests proactive steps to meet standards before they are officially mandated.  This is often used in industries with stringent regulations, such as pharmaceuticals or finance, where anticipating and addressing potential non-compliance is crucial. |例句：The company implemented several precompliances to avoid potential penalties from the upcoming environmental regulations. 公司实施了几项预先符合规定措施，以避免即将实施的环境法规带来的潜在处罚。


The pharmaceutical company's precompliances ensured a smooth transition to the new safety standards.这家制药公司的预先合规性确保了向新的安全标准的顺利过渡。|近义词：proactive compliance, anticipatory compliance, preventative measures |反义词：non-compliance, violation, breach
