nonretraceable: /ˌnɒn rɪˈtreɪsəbl/ | adj. |不可追溯的，无法追查的 |  Incapable of being traced or retraced; impossible to follow back to an origin or previous stage.  |例句：The money came from a nonretraceable source, making it difficult to investigate. 这笔钱来自一个不可追溯的来源，这使得调查变得困难。

The trail was nonretraceable due to the heavy snow.  由于大雪，这条路迹无法追溯。| 近义词：untraceable, unidentifiable, inexplicable | 反义词：traceable, identifiable
