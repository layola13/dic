词源:
The name Clifford is of English origin. It is composed of the elements "Cliff" (meaning "near a cliff, by the cliff") and "ford" (meaning "a shallow place in a river for crossing"). Therefore, the name literally translates to "near a cliff ford" or "ford by the cliff."  The name's popularity has fluctuated throughout history.
