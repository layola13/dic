concretization: /ˌkɒŋkrɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | /ˌkɑːŋkrɪtɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |具体化；实体化；凝固|The process of making something concrete or real; the act of giving definite form to an idea or concept.  It involves transforming abstract ideas or concepts into tangible or observable realities.  This can be a physical process, such as solidifying a liquid, or an intellectual one, such as formulating a detailed plan.|


例句：

The concretization of their plans involved securing funding and hiring staff.  他们的计划具体化包括获得资金和雇用员工。

The artist's concretization of his vision resulted in a stunning sculpture.  艺术家对其愿景的具体化创作出一尊令人惊叹的雕塑。


近义词：materialization, realization, embodiment, implementation, specification


反义词：abstraction, idealization, generalization


词性：名词
