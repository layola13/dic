precriticized: /ˌpriːˈkrɪtɪsaɪzd/ | v. | 预先批评；事先批评 |  To criticize beforehand or in advance.  This is not a commonly used word and is essentially a combination of "pre-" and "criticized". It implies criticism delivered before the event or action being criticized has taken place. |  The project was precriticized by the board before it even began.  这个项目甚至在开始之前就被董事会预先批评了。  |近义词: pre-judge, prejudge, anticipate criticism |反义词: praise beforehand, endorse in advance | 动词
