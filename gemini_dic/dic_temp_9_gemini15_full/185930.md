revotes:/riːˈvoʊts/| n. |再次投票，重新投票|The act of voting again on a matter that has already been voted on.  It often occurs when a previous vote is deemed invalid or when there's a need for reconsideration. |例句：The council held revotes on the controversial budget proposal. = 理事会就备受争议的预算提案进行了再次投票。

例句：After discovering irregularities, the election officials ordered revotes in several precincts. = 在发现违规行为后，选举官员下令在几个选区进行重新投票。

近义词：reballots, re-elections, second votes
反义词：initial votes, first votes
词性：名词
