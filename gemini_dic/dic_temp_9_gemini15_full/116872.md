May's: /meɪz/ | n. |梅(五月)的；梅的；梅氏家族的 |  May's is a possessive form indicating belonging or ownership related to the month of May or a person named May. It can be used as an adjective or a noun depending on the context.  | 例句：May's flowers are in bloom.  五月的花盛开了。

May's birthday is next week.  梅的生日是下个星期。


近义词：of May (for the month),  [No direct equivalent for the possessive of a person named May] | 反义词：[No direct antonym] | 词性: Noun (possessive)
