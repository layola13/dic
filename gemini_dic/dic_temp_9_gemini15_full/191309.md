/siːn/ | n. |场景，场面，景象 |A scene is a view or picture, especially one that is visually striking or memorable; a sequence of events that happen in a particular place.  |例句：The sunset over the ocean was a breathtaking scene.  日落时的海洋景色令人叹为观止。

例句：The first scene of the movie showed a bustling city street. 电影的第一个场景展现了一条熙熙攘攘的城市街道。


近义词：view, sight, spectacle, event, episode |反义词：nothingness, void


词源:  From Old French *scène, from Latin scaena, from Greek skēnē, literally "tent," "hut," referring to the stage building in a Greek theatre.
