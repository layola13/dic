exsufflation:/ˌɛksʌfleɪʃən/| n. |呼气；排出；吹出|The forceful expulsion of air or other substance from a cavity.  It is often used in a medical context, referring to the removal of air or fluid from a body cavity. |例句：The physician performed an exsufflation to remove the excess air from the patient's lungs. 医生进行了呼气术以排出病人肺部的多余空气。
The procedure involved exsufflation of the pleural cavity. 该手术包括胸腔的空气排出。|近义词：expulsion, ejection, evacuation|反义词：insufflation
