redhibition:/ˌrɛdɪˈbɪʃən/| n. |撤销买卖；解除契约；卖方对瑕疵品的责任|The legal right to rescind a contract for the sale of goods due to a defect.  This usually applies when a significant defect renders the goods unfit for their intended purpose.  Redhibition allows the buyer to return the goods and receive a refund or other compensation.|The buyer sought redhibition after discovering a major engine fault in the newly purchased car. 买家在发现新购汽车的发动机存在重大故障后，寻求撤销买卖。|rescission, cancellation, avoidance|affirmation, validation|名词
