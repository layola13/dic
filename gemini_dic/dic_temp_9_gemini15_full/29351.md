castor:/ˈkæstər/| n.|蓖麻，海狸鼠，（家具的）脚轮|A castor is a type of plant that produces castor oil, a thick oil used in various industrial applications and sometimes as a laxative. It can also refer to a type of rodent, the beaver, or a small wheel used on furniture.  |例句：Castor oil is a powerful laxative. 蓖麻油是一种强力的泻药。

The antique chair had beautifully carved castors. 那把古董椅有雕刻精美的脚轮。|近义词：ricinus (plant),  beaver (animal), swivel (wheel)|反义词：无
