resuscitates:/ˌrɛzəˈsɪteɪts/| v.| 使复活；使复苏；使恢复生机 | To revive someone or something from apparent death or unconsciousness; to restore to life, consciousness, or vigor. |例句：The paramedics resuscitated the accident victim.  急救人员使车祸受害者复活了。

例句：The doctor's efforts resuscitated the failing business. 医生们的努力使快要倒闭的企业恢复了生机。


近义词：revive, revitalize, revive, restore, reanimate| 反义词：kill, destroy, extinguish

词源：
