hil:/hɪl/| int.|嘿，你好|An informal greeting, similar to "hi".|Hi, how are you doing? 嘿，你好吗？|hi, hey|bye, goodbye|感叹词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He says hi to everyone. 他跟每个人打招呼。
She often says hi to her neighbors. 她经常跟邻居们打招呼。


一般过去时:
He said hi to me yesterday. 他昨天跟我打招呼了。
She hilariously said hi to the cat. 她滑稽地跟猫打招呼。


现在完成时:
I have said hi to him twice today. 我今天已经跟他打招呼两次了。
She has never said hi to me before. 她以前从未跟我打招呼过。


过去完成时:
I had already said hi before he arrived.  在他到达之前我已经打过招呼了。
They had said hi to each other many times. 他们之前已经互相打过很多次招呼了。


将来时:

She will say hi when she sees him. 她看到他时会打招呼。
