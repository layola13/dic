post-ictal: /ˌpoʊstˈɪktəl/ | adj. | 痫发作后的；发作后的 | Relating to the period following a seizure or convulsive episode.  The term refers to the symptoms, behaviors, and physiological changes that occur after the cessation of seizure activity. |

例句：

The patient experienced post-ictal confusion and amnesia.  = 病人经历了痫发作后的意识混乱和失忆。

He remained drowsy and disoriented in the post-ictal period. = 痫发作后的一段时间内，他仍然昏昏沉沉且迷失方向。


近义词：post-seizure, post-convulsive

反义词：pre-ictal (痫发作前的)

词性：形容词


USAGE->  (由于post-ictal是形容词，没有动词、名词等变化的用法，因此不需要列出不同时态的例句)


PHRASE->

post-ictal confusion: 痫发作后意识混乱
post-ictal depression: 痫发作后抑郁
post-ictal headache: 痫发作后头痛
post-ictal paralysis: 痫发作后瘫痪
post-ictal aphasia: 痫发作后失语症
