outcatching: /ˌaʊtˈkætʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 醒目的，引人注目的 |  Attracting attention; striking or noticeable.  This word is less common and often considered informal. It emphasizes the act of catching attention from a distance or unexpectedly. | 例句：The outcatching colors of the parrot immediately caught my eye.  鹦鹉醒目的颜色立刻吸引了我的目光。

The outcatching headline grabbed everyone's attention.  醒目的标题吸引了所有人的注意。


近义词：eye-catching, striking, conspicuous, noticeable, prominent | 反义词：unnoticeable, inconspicuous, subtle, understated
