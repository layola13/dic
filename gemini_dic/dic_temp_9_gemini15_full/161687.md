poutful: /ˈpaʊtfəl/ | adj. | 噘嘴的；愠怒的；表现不满的 |  Showing or characterized by a pout; sulky; sullen.  |例句：Her poutful expression showed her displeasure. 她噘嘴的表情显示了她不高兴。

例句：The poutful child refused to eat his vegetables.  那个噘嘴的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。


近义词：sullen, sulky, grumpy, displeased, disgruntled

反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful, pleased, delighted

词性：形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is poutful because he didn't get the toy. = 他因为没有得到玩具而噘嘴。
She is always poutful when she doesn't get her way. = 她总是得不到想要的东西时就噘嘴。


一般过去时:
He was poutful after his friend left. = 他的朋友离开后，他很生气。
She was poutful yesterday because of the bad grade. = 她昨天因为坏成绩而生气。


现在完成时:
He has been poutful all day. = 他整天都闷闷不乐。
She has been poutful since she lost her phone. = 自从她弄丢手机后，她就一直闷闷不乐。


过去完成时:
He had been poutful for hours before he finally spoke. = 他闷闷不乐了好几个小时才终于开口说话。
She had been poutful all morning before she finally apologized. = 她整个上午都闷闷不乐，然后才终于道歉。


将来时:
He will be poutful if he doesn't win the game. = 如果他输了比赛，他会很生气。
She will be poutful if you don't invite her to the party. = 如果你不邀请她参加聚会，她会很生气。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so poutful, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么爱生气，就会有更多朋友。
If she weren't so poutful, people would like her more. = 如果她不那么爱生气，人们会更喜欢她。


被动语态: (不太适用poutful作被动语态)
His poutful expression was noticed by everyone. = 大家都注意到了他那生气的样子。（此处是被动，但poutful本身并非被动）
Her poutful demeanor was misinterpreted as arrogance. = 她的生气态度被误解为傲慢。（此处是被动，但poutful本身并非被动）


疑问句:
Is he still poutful about the situation? = 他对这件事还生气吗？
Was she poutful when you told her the news? = 你告诉她这个消息的时候，她生气了吗？


否定句:
He wasn't poutful at all; he was just tired. = 他一点也不生气，他只是累了。
She wasn't poutful after she received the gift. = 她收到礼物后并没有生气。


PHRASE->
poutful lips 噘嘴
poutful face 生气的脸
poutful expression 生气的表情
a poutful silence 生气的沉默
poutful child 生气的孩子


词源:  Derived from "pout" (to thrust out one's lips in a sulky or displeased expression) + "-ful" (a suffix indicating full of or characterized by).  The word essentially means "full of pouting."
