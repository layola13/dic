dirge:/ˈdɜːrdʒ/| n. |挽歌，哀歌 | A dirge is a mournful song or piece of music, especially one for a funeral.  It often expresses sorrow or grief. |Example: The funeral procession was accompanied by a somber dirge.  葬礼队伍伴随着一首忧郁的挽歌。The choir sang a beautiful dirge for the departed soul. 合唱团为逝去的灵魂唱了一首美丽的挽歌。|近义词：lament, elegy, threnody |反义词：celebration, anthem
