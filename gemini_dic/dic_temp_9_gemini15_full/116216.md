masonry:/ˈmeɪsənri/ | n. | 石工；砌体；石造建筑 | The art or process of constructing buildings or other structures from stone, brick, or concrete blocks.  It involves the skillful arrangement and bonding of these materials to create strong and durable structures.  | The ancient Roman aqueducts are impressive examples of masonry.  罗马古代的渡槽是砌体建筑的令人印象深刻的例子。The walls of the castle were built of solid masonry. 城堡的墙壁是用坚固的石砌成的。| bricklaying, stonework | wood construction | n.
