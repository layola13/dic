hydrotherapist:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈθerəpɪst/| n. | 水疗治疗师；理疗师 | A hydrotherapist is a healthcare professional who uses water to treat patients. They may work in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, or private practices.  | Example: The hydrotherapist recommended a series of underwater exercises to improve my range of motion. 水疗治疗师建议我进行一系列水下运动以改善我的活动范围。| 近义词：physiotherapist, physical therapist, therapist | 反义词：无
