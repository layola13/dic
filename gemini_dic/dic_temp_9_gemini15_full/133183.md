nonevasively: /ˌnɒnɪˈveɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 不回避地；不躲闪地；直截了当地 |  In a way that does not avoid or evade something; directly and openly. |


例句：

He answered nonevasively, confirming the rumors. 他直截了当地回答，证实了那些谣言。

She addressed the difficult questions nonevasively, earning the audience's respect. 她不回避地回答了那些棘手的问题，赢得了观众的尊重。


近义词：directly, frankly, openly, straightforwardly

反义词：evassively, indirectly, ambiguously, evasively


词性: 副词
