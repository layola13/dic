emission:/ɪˈmɪʃən/| n. |排放；散发；发射|The release of something, such as a gas or radiation.  It can refer to the act of emitting or the thing emitted.  The context usually clarifies the meaning.|例句：The emission of greenhouse gases is a major concern.  温室气体的排放是一个主要问题。|例句：The car's emission levels were tested. 这辆车的排放水平经过了测试。|近义词：release, discharge, emanation, radiation|反义词：absorption, intake|名词
