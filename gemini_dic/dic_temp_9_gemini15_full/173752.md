quasi-normally:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˈnɔːrməli/ | adj. | 准正常地；近似正常地 |  Approximating the normal; almost normal, but not exactly so. It implies a deviation from true normality, but not a significant or drastic one.  |  The system operated quasi-normally despite the minor malfunction. 系统尽管出现小故障，但运行状况近似正常。The data showed a quasi-normal distribution, with a slight skew to the right. 数据显示近似正态分布，略微右偏。| 近义词：nearly normally, approximately normally | 反义词：abnormally, exceptionally
