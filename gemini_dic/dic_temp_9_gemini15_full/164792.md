prefeudalisms:/ˌpriːˈfjuːdəlɪzəm/| n. |前封建社会，封建社会之前的社会制度|A hypothetical social system existing before fully developed feudalism, characterized by elements that foreshadow feudal structures but lack their full complexity.  The term is often used speculatively and is not a universally accepted historical category.  Scholarly debate exists regarding its actual existence and characteristics.|The study of prefeudalisms attempts to understand the transitional period between earlier social structures and the emergence of fully-fledged feudalism.|近义词：proto-feudalism, early medieval society |反义词：feudalism
