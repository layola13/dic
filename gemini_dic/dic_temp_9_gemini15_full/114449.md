makes up:/meɪks ʌp/| v.|组成，构成，编造，化妆，弥补|to form something; to invent something; to apply cosmetics; to compensate for something.|例句：The team is made up of five players.  团队由五名队员组成。|例句：She made up a story about a lost dog. 她编造了一个关于丢失的狗的故事。|近义词：constitutes, comprises, invents, fabricates, compensates|反义词：takes apart, destroys, reveals, truth


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She makes up stories for her kids every night. = 她每天晚上都为孩子们编故事。
The team makes up a diverse group of people. = 这个团队由一群来自不同背景的人组成。


一般过去时:
He made up an excuse for being late. = 他编了个迟到的借口。
They made up the difference in cost. = 他们弥补了价差。


现在完成时:
She has made up her mind to quit her job. = 她已经下定决心辞职。
The committee has made up its report. = 委员会已经完成了报告。


过去完成时:
They had made up their minds before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就已经做好了决定。
She had made up a whole new identity for herself. = 她为自己编造了一个全新的身份。


将来时:
He will make up for the lost time. = 他将弥补失去的时间。
They will make up a new plan. = 他们将制定一个新的计划。


虚拟语气:
If she made up her mind, she would be happier. = 如果她下定决心，她会更快乐。
I wish I had not made up that excuse. = 我真希望我没有编造那个借口。


被动语态:
The story was made up by a famous writer. = 这个故事是由一位著名作家编造的。
The team was made up of talented individuals. = 这个团队由有才华的人组成。


疑问句:
What makes up the majority of the population? = 人口中的大多数是由什么组成的？
Does she make up her own stories? = 她自己编故事吗？


否定句:
He didn't make up the facts. = 他没有编造事实。
The team wasn't made up of experienced players. = 这个团队并非由经验丰富的队员组成。
