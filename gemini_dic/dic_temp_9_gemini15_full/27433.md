camlet:/ˈkæmlət/| n.|一种轻薄的驼毛织物|A lightweight fabric, typically made of camel hair, or a blend of camel hair and other fibers.  It is known for its softness, warmth, and often a slightly fuzzy texture.  |例句：The coat was made of a luxurious camlet.  这件外套是用奢华的驼毛织物制成的。

例句：She wore a camlet shawl on the chilly evening.  她在寒冷的夜晚披着一条驼毛披肩。


近义词：camel's hair cloth, cashmere


反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以根据语境选择与之对比的粗糙厚重面料)  heavy wool, thick fabric
