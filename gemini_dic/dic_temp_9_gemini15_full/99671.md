interlinears: /ˌɪntərˈlɪniərz/ | n. | 逐行注释；词间注释 |  Interlinears are translations or explanations inserted between the lines of a text. They are often used in language learning materials to help students understand the meaning of difficult words or phrases.  | Example: The textbook included helpful interlinears for each vocabulary word.  这条教科书为每个词汇提供了有用的逐行注释。|近义词：glosses, annotations | 反义词：None.
