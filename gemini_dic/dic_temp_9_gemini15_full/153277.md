penalty:/ˈpɛnəlti/ | n. |处罚，罚款，不利因素 | a punishment imposed for breaking a law, rule, or contract.  |例句：He received a penalty for dangerous driving. 他因危险驾驶受到了处罚。|例句：The penalty for cheating is expulsion from school. 作弊的惩罚是开除学籍。|近义词：punishment, sanction, fine |反义词：reward, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The penalty for speeding is a fine. = 超速的处罚是罚款。
What penalty do they face for this crime? = 他们犯下这罪行将面临什么惩罚？


一般过去时:
The team received a penalty for a foul. = 球队因犯规受到了处罚。
He paid a penalty for his mistakes. = 他为自己的错误付出了代价。


现在完成时:
They have paid the penalty for their actions. = 他们已经为他们的行为付出了代价。
Has she ever faced a penalty before? = 她以前是否曾面临过处罚？


过去完成时:
They had already paid the penalty before the trial. = 审判前他们就已经付出了代价。
The judge had considered the mitigating circumstances before deciding the penalty. = 法官在决定处罚前已经考虑了减轻处罚的情况。


将来时:
He will face a heavy penalty if found guilty. = 如果罪名成立，他将面临严厉的惩罚。
What penalty will be imposed for this infraction? = 违反规定将受到什么处罚？


虚拟语气:
If he had followed the rules, he wouldn't have incurred a penalty. = 如果他遵守规则，他就不会受到处罚。
I wish I hadn't received that penalty. = 我真希望我没有受到那样的处罚。


被动语态:
A penalty was imposed on the company for polluting the river. = 公司因污染河流而受到了处罚。
The penalty is being considered by the judge. = 法官正在考虑处罚。


疑问句:
What is the penalty for parking here? = 在这里停车的处罚是什么？
What penalty will apply in this situation? = 在这种情况下将适用什么处罚？


否定句:
He didn't receive a penalty for his minor infraction. = 他因轻微违规没有受到处罚。
There was no penalty for being late. = 迟到没有处罚。
