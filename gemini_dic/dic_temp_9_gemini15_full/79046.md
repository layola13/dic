giveables:/ˈɡɪvəbəlz/ | n. | 可赠送之物，礼物 | Things that can be given as gifts.  The term often implies small, inexpensive items suitable for giving away casually. | Example:  The company handed out giveables at the trade show, such as pens and keychains.  公司在贸易展上分发了赠品，例如钢笔和钥匙链。| 近义词：gifts, giveaways, freebies | 反义词：keepsakes, valuables
