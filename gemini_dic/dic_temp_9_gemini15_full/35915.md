cocreation:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪʃən/| n. |共同创造，协同创作 |The act of creating something together, typically involving collaboration between a company and its customers or users.  It emphasizes shared ownership and input in the creative process. |Example: The company embraced cocreation, inviting customers to participate in the design of its new product line.  这家公司采用了共同创造的方法，邀请顾客参与其新产品线的設計。|近义词：collaboration, joint creation, partnership |反义词：sole creation, individual creation
