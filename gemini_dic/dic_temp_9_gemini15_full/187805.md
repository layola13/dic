rosary:/ˈroʊzəri/| n.|念珠；玫瑰经|A string of beads used in the recitation of prayers, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.  It typically consists of 5 decades of 10 beads each, with larger beads marking the beginning of each decade.|例句：She clutched her rosary beads nervously. 她紧张地攥着念珠。|例句：The priest led the congregation in reciting the rosary.  牧师带领会众诵念玫瑰经。|近义词：chaplet, beads|反义词：无
