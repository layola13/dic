redefined:/ˌriːˈdɪfaɪnd/| vt.|重新定义，改定|To define again or differently; to give a new meaning or interpretation to.|例句：The company redefined its marketing strategy after a period of decline. 公司在经历一段时间的衰退后重新定义了其营销策略。  例句：The discovery of new evidence redefined our understanding of the event. 新证据的发现重新定义了我们对这一事件的理解。|近义词：revise, reinterpret, reformulate|反义词：define (in the original way), clarify (without changing meaning)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redefine their goals every year. = 他们每年都会重新定义自己的目标。
The scientist redefines the experiment's parameters. = 科学家重新定义了实验的参数。

一般过去时:
The artist redefined the boundaries of his art. = 艺术家重新定义了他的艺术界限。
They redefined the rules of the game. = 他们重新定义了游戏的规则。


现在完成时:
The company has redefined its brand image. = 公司已经重新定义了自己的品牌形象。
We have redefined our priorities. = 我们已经重新定义了我们的优先级。


过去完成时:
After the revolution, they had redefined the social order. = 革命之后，他们重新定义了社会秩序。
The court had redefined the law. = 法院已经重新定义了法律。


将来时:
They will redefine the rules next year. = 他们明年将重新定义规则。
The project will redefine the industry standards. = 该项目将重新定义行业标准。


虚拟语气:
If they redefined their approach, they might succeed. = 如果他们重新定义了他们的方法，他们可能会成功。
I wish they had redefined the terms earlier. = 我希望他们早些重新定义这些术语。


被动语态:
The concept was redefined by the new research. = 这个概念被新的研究重新定义了。
The rules have been redefined by the committee. = 规则已经被委员会重新定义了。


疑问句:
How will the new technology redefine the industry? = 新技术将如何重新定义这个行业？
Have the terms been redefined? = 术语已经被重新定义了吗？


否定句:
They did not redefine their strategy. = 他们没有重新定义他们的策略。
The committee has not yet redefined the rules. = 委员会尚未重新定义规则。
