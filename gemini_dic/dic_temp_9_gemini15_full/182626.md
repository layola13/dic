reorchestrating: /ˌriːˈɔːrkɪstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新编排（乐曲）；重新安排 |  The act of arranging or rearranging a musical composition, often to adapt it for a different instrumentation or context. It can also refer to the act of reorganizing or restructuring something in a more effective or efficient way. | 例句：The conductor is reorchestrating the symphony for a smaller ensemble. 指挥正在为小型乐团重新编排交响乐。

The composer is reorchestrating the opera to highlight the vocal parts. 作曲家正在重新编排歌剧以突出声乐部分。


近义词：rearrange, reorganize, restructure | 反义词：leave unchanged, maintain the status quo | 词性:动词
