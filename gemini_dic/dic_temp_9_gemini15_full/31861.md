cheeriest:/ˈtʃɪəriɪst/| adj. |最快乐的；最愉快的|Most cheerful; full of cheer.  The cheeriest person in the room always brightens everyone's day.|近义词：most joyful, most merry, happiest|反义词：gloomiest, saddest, most depressed|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun shines cheeriest in the summer. = 夏天阳光最灿烂。
His cheeriest smile always makes me happy. = 他最快乐的笑容总是让我开心。


一般过去时:
Yesterday was the cheeriest day of the week. = 昨天是一周中最快乐的一天。
Her cheeriest greeting lifted my spirits. = 她最愉快的问候提振了我的精神。


现在完成时:
The children have been the cheeriest they've ever been. = 孩子们一直是他们有史以来最快乐的。
This has been the cheeriest Christmas we've had in years. = 这是我们多年来最快乐的圣诞节。


过去完成时:
It had been the cheeriest party I had ever attended. = 那是我参加过的最快乐的派对。
She had been the cheeriest person I knew before the accident. = 事故发生前，她是我认识的最快乐的人。


将来时:
The weather forecast predicts the cheeriest days are yet to come. = 天气预报预测最快乐的日子还在后头。
I hope tomorrow will be the cheeriest day of my life. = 我希望明天将成为我生命中最快乐的一天。


虚拟语气:
If only every day could be as cheeriest as this one! = 但愿每一天都能像今天一样快乐！
I wish I could be as cheeriest as he is. = 我希望我能像他一样快乐。


被动语态:
The cheeriest moment was described by the reporter. = 最快乐的时刻被记者描述了。
The cheeriest song was chosen for the wedding. = 最快乐的歌曲被选为婚礼歌曲。


疑问句:
What's the cheeriest thing that happened today? = 今天发生的最快乐的事情是什么？
Whose the cheeriest person you know? = 你认识的最快乐的人是谁？


否定句:
It wasn't the cheeriest of situations. = 这不是最快乐的处境。
Today wasn't the cheeriest day for me. = 今天对我来说不是最快乐的一天。


PHRASE->

词源:
源自cheerful的最高级形式，cheerful  来自古英语 *cēorful，由 *cēo "欢欣鼓舞" + *ful "充满..."构成。
