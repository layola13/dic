dramatising: /ˈdræməˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使戏剧化；夸大；强调 | To make something seem more dramatic, exciting, or important than it really is, often by exaggerating or embellishing details.  |  例句：He was dramatising the situation to gain sympathy. 他夸大了情况以博取同情。


例句：The media often dramatises events to increase viewership. 媒体经常夸大事件以提高收视率。


近义词：exaggerate, sensationalize, heighten, emphasize


反义词：downplay, understate, minimize


词性：动词
