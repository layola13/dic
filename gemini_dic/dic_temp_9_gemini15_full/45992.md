cyanuret:/ˈsaɪənjʊreɪt/| n.|氰化物|A salt or ester of hydrocyanic acid; a highly poisonous compound containing the cyanide group (CN).  Cyanurets are extremely toxic and can cause death if ingested or inhaled.|例句：The victim died from cyanide poisoning, indicating the presence of a cyanuret. 受害者死于氰化物中毒，表明存在氰化物。|近义词：cyanide|反义词：无|名词
