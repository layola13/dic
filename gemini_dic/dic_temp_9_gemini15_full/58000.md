driverless:/ˈdraɪvərləs/| adj.| 无人驾驶的；自动驾驶的 | Without a driver; operating automatically.  | Example:  The company is developing driverless cars. 这家公司正在研发无人驾驶汽车。

Example:  The future of transportation may include more driverless vehicles on the roads. 交通的未来可能包括道路上更多无人驾驶车辆。


近义词：self-driving, autonomous

反义词：manned, human-driven


词源：driver (司机) + less (否定后缀)  The word is a compound of "driver" and the suffix "-less," meaning "without."  It's a relatively modern word, reflecting the recent advancements in autonomous vehicle technology.
