re-probing:/ˌriːˈproʊbɪŋ/ | v. | 再次探查；重新调查 | To investigate or examine something again.  This often implies a more thorough or focused investigation than the initial one.  |例句：The police are re-probing the murder case after new evidence emerged.  警方在出现新的证据后正在重新调查谋杀案。

例句：After the initial tests proved inconclusive, the scientists decided to re-probe the subject matter.  在初步测试证明不确定后，科学家们决定重新探究主题。


近义词：re-examine, reinvestigate, re-evaluate, scrutinize


反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook


词性：动词
