natural-born: /ˈnætʃrəlˈbɔːrn/ | adj. | 天生的，生来就有的 |  Describes someone who is born with a particular quality or characteristic, especially a right or citizenship.  It emphasizes the inherent nature of the quality, rather than acquired through effort or circumstance. | Example: He's a natural-born leader. 他是一位天生的领导者。  She is a natural-born citizen of the United States. 她是美国天生的公民。 | 近义词: innate, inborn, congenital | 反义词: acquired, learned
