disenchant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃɑːnt/| vt.|使醒悟；使不再抱幻想；使幻灭|To free from enchantment or illusion; to disillusion.  The experience disenchanted him with politics.|近义词：disillusion, disillusionize, disenthrall|反义词：enchant, bewitch, enthrall|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news disenchants many people about the future. = 这个消息让许多人对未来感到幻灭。
He disenchants his followers with his lies. = 他用谎言让追随者对他失去幻想。


一般过去时:
The magician disenchanted the princess. = 魔术师解除了公主的魔法。
Her betrayal disenchanted him with love. = 她的背叛让他对爱情感到幻灭。


现在完成时:
His experiences have disenchanted him with the legal profession. = 他的经历让他对法律职业感到失望。
The recent scandals have disenchanted many voters. = 最近的丑闻让许多选民感到幻灭。


过去完成时:
She had been disenchanted with her job for years before she finally quit. = 在她最终辞职之前，她对她的工作已经失望多年了。
He had already been disenchanted with the promises of the government. = 他早已对政府的承诺感到失望。


将来时:
This revelation will disenchant many people who supported him. = 这一揭露将让许多支持他的人感到幻灭。
The upcoming election might disenchant young voters. = 即将到来的选举可能会让年轻选民感到幻灭。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, I wouldn't have been disenchanted. = 如果我知道真相，我就不会感到幻灭了。
If he hadn't lied, she wouldn't have been disenchanted with him. = 如果他没有撒谎，她就不会对他感到失望了。


被动语态:
Many were disenchanted by the politician's broken promises. = 许多人因这位政治家的空头支票而感到幻灭。
The public was disenchanted by the lack of transparency. = 公众对缺乏透明度感到幻灭。


疑问句:
Was he disenchanted by the outcome? = 他对结果感到失望吗？
Have you been disenchanted by your experiences? = 你的经历让你感到幻灭了吗？


否定句:
The ending of the story didn't disenchant me. = 故事的结局并没有让我失望。
The success of the project didn't disenchant the team. = 项目的成功并没有让团队感到幻灭。
