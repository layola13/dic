It can also refer to a slight difference or distinction.  | Example: The composer used a diesis to create a subtle dissonance.  作曲家使用了升音符创造了一个微妙的不和谐音。| 近义词：sharp, semitone (in musical context); nuance, difference (in general context) | 反义词：None directly, but could be considered opposite to a flat (♭) in a musical context.  |
