loved:/lʌvd/| v. |爱过的；被爱的| Past tense and past participle of "love".  It describes an action of love that has already happened.  It can also be used as an adjective describing something that is cherished or greatly appreciated. |例句：I loved that movie. 我爱看那部电影。

She was loved by everyone. 她受到大家的爱戴。|近义词：cherished, adored, cherished, appreciated |反义词:hated, despised


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I love you. = 我爱你。
He loves his dog. = 他爱他的狗。


一般过去时:
I loved her deeply. = 我深深地爱着她。
They loved living in the countryside. = 他们喜欢住在乡下。


现在完成时:
I have loved you since I first met you. = 从我第一次见到你我就爱上你了。
She has loved painting all her life. = 她一生都热爱绘画。


过去完成时:
I had loved him for many years before we got married. = 在我们结婚之前，我已经爱了他很多年了。
They had loved each other since childhood. = 他们从小就彼此相爱。


将来时:
I will always love you. = 我将永远爱你。
He will love his children unconditionally. = 他将无条件地爱他的孩子们。


虚拟语气:
If I loved her, I would have married her. = 如果我爱她，我就已经娶她了。
I wish I loved traveling more. = 我希望我更喜欢旅行。


被动语态:
He was loved and respected by his colleagues. = 他受到同事的爱戴和尊敬。
The painting is loved by art critics. = 这幅画受到艺术评论家的喜爱。


疑问句:
Did you love the present? = 你喜欢这个礼物吗？
Have you ever loved someone so much? = 你曾经如此爱过某人吗？


否定句:
I didn't love him anymore. = 我不再爱他了。
She never loved anyone as much as she loved him. = 她从未像爱他那样爱过任何人。


PHRASE->
well-loved = 深受喜爱的
deeply loved = 深深地被爱
newly loved = 新近被爱的
long-loved = 长期被爱的
much loved = 深受喜爱的
