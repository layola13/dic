exculpating: /ˈɛkskʌlpeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 开脱罪责的；证明无罪的 |  Justifying or clearing from blame or guilt.  The act of showing or declaring someone to be not guilty. |

例句：

The new evidence was exculpating, freeing the suspect from all charges.  = 新证据证明了被告无罪，使其免于所有指控。

The lawyer worked tirelessly to present exculpating evidence to the jury. = 律师不遗余力地向陪审团呈递无罪证据。


近义词：absolving, clearing, exonerating, vindicating

反义词：incriminating, accusing, condemning, convicting

词性：动词


USAGE->

The evidence exculpates him. = 证据证明他无罪。
They are trying to exculpate themselves. = 他们试图开脱自己的罪责。


一般过去时:

The court exculpated the defendant. = 法院宣告被告无罪。

现在完成时:

The new evidence has exculpated the suspect. = 新证据已证明了嫌疑人的清白。
They have exculpated themselves from any wrongdoing.  = 他们已经为自己开脱了任何不当行为。


过去完成时:

They had exculpated the witness before the trial. = 他们在审判前已经为证人开脱了。


将来时:

被动语态:

attempt to exculpate =  试图开脱罪责
