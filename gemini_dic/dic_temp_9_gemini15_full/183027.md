replicating:/ˈrɛplɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.|复制，重复，模拟|Making an exact copy of something.  The process of creating a duplicate or copies.  It can refer to biological processes (like DNA replication) or artificial processes (like copying a file).|例句：The experiment aimed at replicating the results of previous studies.  该实验旨在复制先前研究的结果。
The software can replicate the functionality of the older system. 该软件可以复制旧系统的功能。|近义词：duplicate, reproduce, copy, imitate|反义词：destroy, erase, delete|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists are replicating the experiment to verify the results. 科学家们正在重复实验以验证结果。
She replicates her mother's cooking style. 她模仿她母亲的烹饪风格。


一般过去时:
The researchers replicated the study using a different sample size. 研究人员使用不同的样本量复制了这项研究。
He replicated the design flawlessly. 他完美地复制了设计。


现在完成时:
They have replicated the experiment several times with consistent results. 他们已经多次重复了实验，结果一致。
She has successfully replicated the famous painting. 她成功地复制了那幅名画。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had replicated all the necessary charts. 在演示之前，他已经复制了所有必要的图表。
The team had replicated the error in their previous analysis.  团队在之前的分析中重复了错误。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
