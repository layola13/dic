cottonwood:/ˈkɑːtnwʊd/ | /ˈkɒtənˌwʊd/| n. |杨木，棉白杨|A cottonwood is a type of poplar tree known for its fluffy seeds that resemble cotton. These trees are common in North America and are fast-growing and tolerant of various soil conditions.  They are often found near rivers and streams.|The cottonwood trees along the riverbank were shedding their seeds.|近义词：poplar,  aspen|反义词：无
