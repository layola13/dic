counterstating:/ˌkaʊntərˈsteɪtɪŋ/| v.|反驳，反述，提出相反的论点|The act of stating something in opposition to a previous statement; presenting a counter-argument.  It involves presenting a different perspective or contradicting an existing claim.|例句：He counterstated the witness's testimony, providing evidence that contradicted it. 他反驳了证人的证词，提供了相互矛盾的证据。

例句：The lawyer counterstated the prosecution's theory, offering an alternative explanation. 律师反驳了检方的理论，提出了另一种解释。


近义词：contradicting, refuting, opposing, rebutting|反义词：confirming, corroborating, supporting, agreeing|词性:动词
