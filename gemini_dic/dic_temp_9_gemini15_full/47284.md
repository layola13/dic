de-deputize:/ˌdiːˈdɛpjʊtaɪz/| vt.|撤销…的代理权；解除…的副职|To remove someone from a position of deputy or authority.|The sheriff decided to de-deputize the officer after the incident.  警长决定在事件发生后撤销该警官的代理权。|近义词：revoke, rescind, dismiss|反义词：deputize, appoint
