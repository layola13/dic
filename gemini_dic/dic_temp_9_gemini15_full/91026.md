hushing:/ˈhʌʃɪŋ/| v.| 哄；使安静；轻微的沙沙声 |The act of making a quiet sound, or the sound itself; the act of making someone be quiet.  |例句：The teacher hushed the noisy children.老师使吵闹的孩子安静下来。

The wind hushed through the trees. 风在树林中发出轻微的沙沙声。|近义词：quieting, silencing, shushing |反义词：rousing, exciting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hushing the baby. = 他正在哄婴儿睡觉。
She hushes the noisy crowd. = 她使喧闹的人群安静下来。

一般过去时:
He hushed the baby to sleep. = 他哄婴儿睡觉。
The librarian hushed the students. = 图书管理员让学生们安静下来。

现在完成时:
She has hushed the children many times. = 她已经多次让孩子们安静下来。
They have hushed the argument. = 他们已经平息了争论。

过去完成时:
He had hushed the dog before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经让狗安静下来了。
She had hushed the rumour before it spread. = 在谣言传播之前，她已经把它压下去了。

将来时:

被动语态:
