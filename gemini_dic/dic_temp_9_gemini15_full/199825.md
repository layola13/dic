sit-downs:/ˈsɪtdaʊnz/| n.|静坐；谈判；非正式会谈|A sit-down is a protest where people sit down in a public place to block it or to show their disapproval. It can also refer to a formal or informal meeting or negotiation.|例句：The workers staged a sit-down strike to protest their low wages. 工人们举行静坐罢工以抗议低工资。  The two leaders held a sit-down to discuss the treaty. 两位领导人举行会谈以讨论条约。|近义词：protest, demonstration, meeting, negotiation|反义词：riot, violence
