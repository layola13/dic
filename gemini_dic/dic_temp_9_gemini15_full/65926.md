exacerbate:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪt/ | vt. | 加剧；恶化 | to make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse | The drought exacerbated the food shortage. 干旱加剧了粮食短缺。 | aggravate, worsen, intensify | alleviate, mitigate, improve | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exacerbates the problem by his careless behavior. = 他粗心的行为加剧了这个问题。
The hot weather exacerbates my allergies. = 炎热的天气加剧了我的过敏。

一般过去时:
The rain exacerbated the already difficult driving conditions. = 雨水加剧了本来就已经很困难的驾驶条件。
His comments exacerbated the tension in the room. = 他的评论加剧了房间里的紧张气氛。

现在完成时:
The conflict has exacerbated tensions between the two countries. = 冲突加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。
Years of drought have exacerbated the water shortage. = 多年的干旱加剧了缺水问题。

过去完成时:
The previous administration's policies had exacerbated the economic crisis. = 前一届政府的政策加剧了经济危机。
Years of mismanagement had exacerbated the company's financial problems. = 多年的管理不善加剧了公司的财务问题。

将来时:
Further cuts in funding will exacerbate the situation. = 进一步削减资金将加剧局势。
The coming winter will exacerbate the energy crisis. = 即将来临的冬天将加剧能源危机。

虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the situation will only exacerbate. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况只会恶化。
I wish my anxiety wouldn't exacerbate my symptoms. = 我希望我的焦虑不会加剧我的症状。

被动语态:
The situation was exacerbated by the recent political unrest. = 最近的政治动荡加剧了局势。
The problem is being exacerbated by a lack of funding. = 缺乏资金正在加剧这个问题。

疑问句:
Will this decision exacerbate the existing problems? = 这个决定会加剧现有的问题吗？
Does stress exacerbate your health conditions? = 压力会加剧你的健康状况吗？

否定句:
The new policy did not exacerbate the situation. = 新政策并没有加剧局势。
The medication did not exacerbate his allergies. = 这种药物并没有加剧他的过敏。
