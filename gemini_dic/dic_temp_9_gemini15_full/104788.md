kazillion:/kəˈzɪljən/| n. |极大数量，无数|An informal term for an extremely large, unspecified number.  It is not a real number; it's used for humorous or emphatic effect. |例句：I've told you a kazillion times to clean your room! 我已经告诉了你无数遍了，把你的房间打扫干净！  例句：There are a kazillion stars in the sky. 天上有无数颗星星。|近义词：millions, billions, trillions, countless, innumerable |反义词：few, little, none
