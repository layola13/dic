espousers:/ɪˈspaʊzərz/| n. |拥护者，支持者 |Those who advocate or support a particular cause, belief, or system.  |例句：The espousers of this new technology believe it will revolutionize the industry.  这些新技术的拥护者相信它将彻底改变这个行业。

例句：Many espousers of free market capitalism believe in minimal government intervention. 许多自由市场资本主义的支持者相信政府应该尽量少干预。


近义词：proponents, advocates, supporters, champions

反义词：opponents, adversaries, critics, detractors

词性:名词
