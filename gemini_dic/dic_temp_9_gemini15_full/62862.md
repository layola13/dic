enjoyment: /ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt/ | n. | 享受；乐趣 | The state of experiencing pleasure or satisfaction.  It refers to the feeling of happiness and pleasure derived from an activity or experience. |例句：The enjoyment of a good book is immeasurable.  一本好书带来的乐趣是无法衡量的。

例句：Her enjoyment of the concert was evident in her enthusiastic applause. 她对音乐会的享受从她热烈的掌声中显而易见。


近义词：pleasure, delight, satisfaction, gratification | 反义词：misery, suffering, displeasure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children derive great enjoyment from playing outdoors. = 孩子们从户外玩耍中获得极大的乐趣。
My enjoyment of this job is increasing every day. = 我对这份工作的享受日益增加。


一般过去时:
We had immense enjoyment at the party last night. = 我们昨晚在派对上玩得很开心。
I experienced pure enjoyment during my holiday in Bali. = 我在巴厘岛度假期间体验到了纯粹的乐趣。


现在完成时:
I have had much enjoyment from learning a new language. = 我从学习一门外语中获得了许多乐趣。
She has derived immense enjoyment from her volunteer work. = 她从志愿工作中获得了巨大的满足感。


过去完成时:
By the end of the movie, I had already experienced a great deal of enjoyment. = 到电影结束时，我已经体验到了很大的乐趣。
They had never before experienced such enjoyment from a simple game. = 他们以前从未从一个简单的游戏中获得过如此多的乐趣。


将来时:
I anticipate great enjoyment from our upcoming trip to Italy. = 我期待着我们即将到意大利的旅行带来极大的乐趣。
We will undoubtedly derive much enjoyment from this new project. = 我们无疑会从这个新项目中获得很多乐趣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more free time, I would have more enjoyment in life. = 如果我有更多空闲时间，我的生活会更有乐趣。
I wish I could experience the enjoyment of traveling the world. = 我希望我能体验环游世界的乐趣。


被动语态:
Great enjoyment was derived from the beautiful scenery. = 美丽的景色带来了极大的享受。
The enjoyment of the concert was enhanced by the excellent acoustics. = 音乐会的享受因优异的音响效果而增强。


疑问句:
What is the source of your enjoyment? = 你快乐的源泉是什么？

否定句:
He found no enjoyment in his work. = 他在他的工作中找不到乐趣。
She derived little enjoyment from the tedious task. = 她从那项乏味的任务中获得的乐趣很少。
