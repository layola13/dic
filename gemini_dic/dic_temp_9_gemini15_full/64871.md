espy:/ɪˈspaɪ/| vt.| 瞥见；发现；侦察|To catch sight of something or someone, especially unexpectedly or from a distance.  It often implies a sense of discovery or careful observation.|例句：From the hilltop, we espied a herd of deer grazing in the valley.  从山顶上，我们瞥见了一群鹿在山谷里吃草。

例句：The detective espied a clue hidden in plain sight.  侦探发现了一个隐藏在显眼处的线索。


近义词：catch sight of, glimpse, discern, detect, observe|反义词：overlook, miss
