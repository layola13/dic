mended:/ˈmɛndɪd/ | v. | 修补；修理；改正；弥合 | To repair something that is broken or damaged.  |例句：She mended the tear in her jeans. 她缝补了她牛仔裤上的破洞。|例句：He mended the broken fence. 他修理了坏掉的篱笆。|近义词：repair, fix, patch, restore |反义词：break, damage, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mends his clothes regularly. = 他定期缝补他的衣服。
She mends her ways after making a mistake. = 她犯错后改过自新。


一般过去时:
The tailor mended my jacket. = 裁缝修补了我的夹克。
They mended their relationship after a long argument. = 他们经过长时间的争吵后修复了关系。


现在完成时:
I have mended all the broken toys. = 我已经修好了所有坏掉的玩具。
She has mended her broken heart after the breakup. = 分手后，她已经治愈了受伤的心。


过去完成时:
He had mended the roof before the storm arrived. = 他在暴风雨来临之前已经修好了屋顶。
They had mended their differences before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前解决彼此的分歧。


将来时:
I will mend the hole in my sock tomorrow. = 我明天将缝补我袜子上的洞。
The mechanic will mend the car engine next week. = 机械师下周将修理汽车引擎。


虚拟语气:
If I had a sewing machine, I would mend this dress. = 如果我有一台缝纫机，我会缝补这件衣服。
I wish I had mended my relationship with him sooner. = 我希望我早点修复与他的关系。


被动语态:
The broken vase was mended by a skilled artisan. = 破碎的花瓶被一位技艺精湛的工匠修补好了。
The bridge is being mended after the recent storm. = 近期风暴后，桥梁正在维修中。


疑问句:
Can you mend this rip in my shirt? = 你能缝补我衬衫上的这个裂口吗？
Will the company mend its flawed business practices? = 公司会纠正其有缺陷的经营行为吗？


否定句:
He didn't mend the broken chair. = 他没有修理坏掉的椅子。
She couldn't mend the tear in the fabric. = 她无法缝补织物上的破洞。
