massaging:/ˈmæsɑːʒɪŋ/ | v. | 按摩；揉捏；篡改 | The act of rubbing, kneading, or pressing a part of the body to relieve pain or promote relaxation.  It can also refer to manipulating data or information subtly to achieve a desired effect. | 例句：She's massaging his shoulders to relieve tension. 她正在按摩他的肩膀以缓解紧张。  He's massaging the numbers to make the results look better. 他正在篡改数字以使结果看起来更好。 | 近义词：rubbing, kneading, manipulating | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting
