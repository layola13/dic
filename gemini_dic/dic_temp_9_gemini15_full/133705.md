nonfrightening: /ˌnɒnˈfraɪtənɪŋ/ | adj. | 不吓人的，使人安心的 | Not causing fear or alarm; reassuring. |

例句：

The nonfrightening atmosphere of the museum made the children feel at ease.  博物馆里轻松的氛围让孩子们感到自在。

The teacher used nonfrightening methods to help the shy student overcome their fear of public speaking. 老师使用不吓人的方法帮助害羞的学生克服公开演讲的恐惧。


近义词：reassuring, calming, comforting, soothing, peaceful

反义词：frightening, terrifying, scary, alarming, daunting


词性：形容词
