reoperate:/ˌriːˈɒpəreɪt/| vt.|再次手术，重新操作|To perform a surgical operation again on (a patient or part of the body); to operate on something again.|例句：The surgeon had to reoperate because of complications.  外科医生不得不再次手术，因为出现了并发症。|例句：The technician had to reoperate the machine after the power surge. 技术人员在电源浪涌后不得不重新操作机器。|近义词：operate again, redo, repeat|反义词：heal, recover
