pomarium:/pəˈmɛəriəm/| n.|果园，果树园|A pomarium is an orchard or garden where fruit trees are grown.  It specifically refers to a garden or enclosure planted with fruit trees, often associated with Roman villas and estates.|Example: The Roman villa boasted a magnificent pomarium, providing a bounty of fruit for the household.|近义词：orchard, fruit garden|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a vegetable garden or flower garden.|名词
