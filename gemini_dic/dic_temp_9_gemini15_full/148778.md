overuses:/ˈoʊvərjuːzɪz/| n.|过度使用，滥用|The overuse of antibiotics is contributing to antibiotic resistance.  抗生素的过度使用正在导致抗生素耐药性。|近义词：misuse, abuse, exploitation|反义词：underuse, sparing use|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overuses his phone. = 他过度使用手机。
The company overuses its resources. = 公司过度使用其资源。

一般过去时:
She overused the painkillers. = 她过度使用了止痛药。
They overused the equipment. = 他们过度使用了设备。

现在完成时:
I have overused my voice. = 我用嗓过度了。

过去完成时:
He had overused his credit card. = 他之前过度使用了信用卡。

将来时:
She will overuse her computer if she's not careful. = 如果她不小心，她会过度使用电脑。
