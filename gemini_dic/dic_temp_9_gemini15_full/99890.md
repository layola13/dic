internalizes:/ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使内化；吸收；将…纳入| To make (something) part of one's own being or beliefs; to incorporate (something) into one's personality or way of thinking. | 例句：He internalized his father's values. 他将父亲的价值观内化了。| 例句：The company internalized its losses. 公司将损失计入账内。| 近义词：assimilate, absorb, integrate | 反义词：externalize, reject, ignore | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She internalizes her feelings. = 她将自己的感受内化。
He internalizes new information quickly. = 他很快就能吸收新信息。

一般过去时:
I internalized the lesson after many mistakes. = 在犯了很多错误之后，我吸收了教训。
They internalized the company culture. = 他们将公司文化内化了。

现在完成时:
She has internalized the principles of non-violence. = 她已经将非暴力的原则内化了。
We have internalized many cultural norms without realizing it. = 我们在不知不觉中已经内化了许多文化规范。

过去完成时:
He had internalized his grief before he spoke to us. = 在跟我们说话之前，他已经将悲伤内化了。
They had internalized the rules of the game before the match started. = 比赛开始前，他们已经将比赛规则内化了。

将来时:
She will internalize the new training methods soon. = 她很快就会将新的训练方法内化。
They will internalize the lessons learned from this experience. = 他们将会将从这次经验中学到的教训内化。

虚拟语气:
If I were to internalize this information, I could understand it better. = 如果我能将这些信息内化，我就能更好地理解它。
I wish I had internalized the importance of planning earlier. = 我希望我早点就将计划的重要性内化。

被动语态:
The lesson was internalized by the students. = 学生们将教训内化了。
The company's values are being internalized by new employees. = 新员工正在将公司的价值观内化。

疑问句:
Does he easily internalize new concepts? = 他容易将新概念内化吗？
Have they internalized the new safety regulations? = 他们是否已经将新的安全规章内化了？

否定句:
He didn't internalize the feedback he received. = 他没有将收到的反馈内化。
She doesn't internalize criticism well. = 她不太擅长将批评内化。
