obstacles: /ˈɑːbstəklz/ | n. |障碍，阻碍 |Things that make it difficult to do something.  Obstacles can be physical, like a wall, or abstract, like a problem. |例句：The biggest obstacle to peace is the lack of trust between the two sides.  最大的和平障碍是双方之间缺乏信任。

例句：Many obstacles stood in their way, but they persevered and eventually succeeded.  许多障碍挡在他们面前，但他们坚持不懈，最终取得了成功。


近义词：hindrance, impediment, obstruction, barrier |反义词：assistance, help, aid


词源:  From Old French *obstacle, from Latin obstaculum, from obstare "to stand in the way," from ob- "in the way of" + stare "to stand."
