fixed:/fɪkst/| adj. v.|固定的；修理；解决；确定；不变的 |  Something that is fixed is not likely to change.  It can also refer to something that has been repaired or made secure. |例句：The price is fixed. 价格是固定的。

The mechanic fixed my car. 技工修好了我的车。|近义词：secured, repaired, settled, constant|反义词：broken, loose, unstable, changeable|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price is fixed. = 价格是固定的。
The table is fixed to the wall. = 桌子固定在墙上。

一般过去时:
He fixed the broken chair. = 他修好了坏掉的椅子。
She fixed the problem quickly. = 她迅速解决了这个问题。

现在完成时:
They have fixed the leak in the roof. = 他们已经修好了屋顶的漏水。
We have fixed a date for the meeting. = 我们已经确定了会议日期。

过去完成时:
She had fixed the dinner before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经准备好了晚餐。
The mechanic had fixed the car before I picked it up. = 我取车之前，技工已经修好了车。

将来时:
They will fix the fence tomorrow. = 他们明天会修理围栏。
We will fix a time to talk later. = 我们稍后会约定一个时间谈谈。

虚拟语气:
If I had fixed the problem earlier, I would have avoided the trouble. = 如果我早点解决这个问题，我就能避免麻烦了。
I wish I had fixed the bike before the race. = 我真希望比赛前我已经修好了自行车。

被动语态:
The problem was fixed by the technician. = 这个问题由技术员解决了。
The date for the meeting has been fixed. = 会议日期已经确定了。

疑问句:
Is the appointment fixed? = 会面时间确定了吗？
Has the broken window been fixed yet? = 破窗已经修好了吗？

否定句:
The problem is not fixed yet. = 问题还没解决。
The appointment wasn't fixed until yesterday. = 直到昨天约会才确定下来。
