monochromy's:/mɒˈnɒkrəmiːz/| n. | 单色；单色画法 | The state or quality of having only one color.  It refers to a work of art using only one color or hue, or variations in the lightness and darkness of that single color.  The possessive 's' indicates possession or belonging. For example, "the monochromy's impact" refers to the impact of a monochromatic artwork. | The artist's monochromy's impact was profound.  这位艺术家的单色画作影响深远。|  monochromaticism, single-color painting | polychromy, multicolor painting | 名词
