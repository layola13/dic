foregather:/fɔːˈɡæðə(r)/| v.|聚集，集合|To come together, especially in a large group.  This verb often implies an informal or spontaneous gathering.|例句：The family foregathered at the old house for a reunion.  家人聚集在老房子里举行团聚。|例句：After the game, the fans foregathered in the pub to celebrate.  比赛结束后，球迷们聚集在酒吧庆祝。|近义词：assemble, congregate, gather, meet|反义词：disperse, scatter, separate|动词
