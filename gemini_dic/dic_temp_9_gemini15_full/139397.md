observation-cars: /ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃən kɑːrz/ | n. | 观景车 | Passenger cars on a train designed for sightseeing, often with large windows and comfortable seating.  These cars are typically found on scenic routes or tourist trains, offering passengers panoramic views. | Example: The observation cars on the Rocky Mountaineer train provide breathtaking views of the Canadian Rockies.  观景列车上的观光车厢提供了加拿大落基山脉的壮丽景色。|近义词：sightseeing cars, panoramic cars | 反义词：无
