deadlined: /ˈdedlaɪnd/ | adj. | 有最后期限的；受到最后期限限制的 | Having a deadline; subject to a deadline. | 例句：The project is deadlined for next Friday.  这个项目最迟下周五截止。

The report is deadlined; we must submit it by tomorrow. 报告有截止日期；我们必须在明天之前提交。


近义词：time-limited, time-bound, due


反义词：open-ended, without deadline


词性:形容词
