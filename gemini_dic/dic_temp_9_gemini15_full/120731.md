misallotting: /ˌmɪsæˈlɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 错配，分配错误 | The act of allocating something incorrectly or unfairly.  It implies a mistake in the distribution or assignment of resources, responsibilities, or tasks. |例句：The company's misallotting of funds led to a significant budget shortfall. 公司资金分配错误导致预算严重短缺。

The misallotting of resources resulted in project delays. 资源分配错误导致项目延误。


近义词：maldistribution, misallocation, misapplication | 反义词：proper allocation, equitable distribution | 词性：动词
