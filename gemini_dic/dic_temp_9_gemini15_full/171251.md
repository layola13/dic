pteranodon:/ˈtɛrənədɑːn/| n. |无齿翼龙|A genus of large, extinct flying reptiles from the Late Cretaceous period, characterized by their large wingspans and lack of teeth.|例句：The *Pteranodon* soared through the skies of the Late Cretaceous. 无齿翼龙翱翔于晚白垩世的空中。|近义词：pterodactyl (though not a perfect synonym, as Pteranodon is a specific genus)|反义词：无|词性：名词
