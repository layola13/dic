penfriend: /ˈpenˌfrɛnd/ | n. |笔友| A person with whom one regularly exchanges letters, especially one in a different country.  |例句：I've had a penfriend in Japan for five years. 我有一个在日本的笔友，已经有五年了。|近义词：correspondent, email pal |反义词：N/A

She sends postcards to her penfriend. = 她给她的笔友寄明信片。
