preauthorized:/ˌpriːˈɔːθəraɪzd/|/ˈpriːɔːθəraɪzd/| adj.|预先授权的，预先批准的|Already authorized in advance; approved beforehand.  This term typically refers to a transaction or action that has been given permission to proceed before it actually happens.|例句：The payment was preauthorized, so the funds were transferred automatically.  付款已预先授权，因此资金已自动转账。

例句：We have a preauthorized payment plan for our monthly utility bills. 我们有一个预先授权的每月水电费支付计划。


近义词：approved, sanctioned, authorized, permitted


反义词：unauthorized, unapproved, forbidden


词性：形容词
