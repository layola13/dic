noncranking:/ˌnɒnˈkræŋkɪŋ/| adj.|发动机无法启动的；起动失败的|Describes a condition where an engine fails to start or crank.  This usually refers to a vehicle's engine but can apply to other engines as well.|例句：The car experienced noncranking issues after the recent storm.  最近的风暴后，汽车出现了无法启动的问题。 The technician diagnosed the noncranking condition as a faulty starter motor. 技师诊断出无法启动的原因是启动马达故障。|近义词：unstartable, not starting, failed to start|反义词：cranking, starting, operational
