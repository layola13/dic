irritably:/ˈɪrɪtəbli/| adj. |易怒地；烦躁地| in an irritable manner; showing irritation.  |例句：He answered irritably, "I don't know!" 他烦躁地回答道：“我不知道！”|例句：She slammed the door irritably. 她生气地砰地关上了门。|近义词：testily, crossly, snappishly, grumpily |反义词：calmly, patiently, pleasantly

词源:
irritably derives from the adjective "irritable," which comes from the verb "irritate" (to annoy or provoke).  The suffix "-ably" indicates "in a manner that is."  The root ultimately traces back to Latin "irritare" (to provoke).
