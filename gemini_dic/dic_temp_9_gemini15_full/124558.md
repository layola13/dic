mountingly:/ˈmaʊntɪŋli/ | adv. |日益，越来越 | Increasingly; to a growing extent.  The word describes something that is growing or increasing in intensity, size, or importance. |例句：The evidence against him is mountingly persuasive.  越来越多的证据表明他是有罪的。  The pressure on the government is mountingly intense. 政府面临的压力越来越大。|近义词：increasingly, progressively, gradually, escalatingly |反义词：decreasingly, diminishingly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tension is mounting increasingly. = 紧张局势日益加剧。
His anger is mounting steadily. = 他的怒气日益增长。


一般过去时:
The problem mounted steadily last year. = 这个问题去年持续加剧。
Her anxiety mounted throughout the day. = 她一整天的焦虑感与日俱增。


现在完成时:
The evidence has mounted significantly against the suspect. = 对嫌疑人的指控证据已经显著增加。
My frustration has mounted over the past few weeks. = 我过去几周的挫败感越来越大。


过去完成时:
The debt had mounted alarmingly before the intervention. = 在干预之前，债务已经达到了令人担忧的程度。
The tension had mounted considerably before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，紧张气氛已经相当高涨。


将来时:
The costs will mount significantly in the coming year. = 未来一年的成本将大幅增加。
The difficulties will mount as the project progresses. = 随着项目的进展，困难将会增多。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure mounted further, the system might collapse. = 如果压力进一步增大，系统可能会崩溃。
I wish the workload hadn't mounted so quickly. = 我希望工作量没有增长得这么快。


被动语态:
The situation is being mounted by the media. = 媒体正在夸大其词地报道这一情况。(Note: This usage is less common for "mountingly")
The pressure was being mounted on the company to change its policies. = 公司正承受着越来越大的压力来改变其政策。


疑问句:
Is the tension mounting noticeably? = 紧张局势是否明显加剧？
Are the difficulties mounting rapidly? = 困难是否迅速增加？


否定句:
The support for the proposal isn't mounting. = 对该提案的支持并没有增加。
The evidence isn't mounting against them. = 没有越来越多的证据表明他们有罪。


PHRASE->
mountingly clear = 越来越清楚
mountingly obvious = 越来越明显
mountingly urgent = 越来越紧急
mountingly difficult = 越来越困难
mountingly complex = 越来越复杂
