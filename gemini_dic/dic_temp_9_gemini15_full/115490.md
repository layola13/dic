manure: /məˈnʊr/ | n. |肥料，粪肥 |  A natural substance used to make soil more fertile, typically consisting of the dung and urine of animals.  | Example: The farmer spread manure on the fields to improve the crop yield.  农民在田里施肥以提高作物产量。|  fertilizer, dung |  (无明确反义词，可考虑贫瘠的土壤  infertile soil) | 名词
