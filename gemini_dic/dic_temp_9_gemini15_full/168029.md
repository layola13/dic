printing:/ˈprɪntɪŋ/| n. |印刷术；印刷；印刷品|The process of producing text and images on paper or other materials using a printing press or other method.  It encompasses various techniques, from letterpress to digital printing. |例句：The printing of the book was delayed due to unforeseen circumstances. 这本书的印刷因不可预见的情况而被推迟。|例句：The quality of the printing is excellent.  印刷质量非常好。|近义词：publication, impression, typography|反义词：handwriting, manuscript|名词
