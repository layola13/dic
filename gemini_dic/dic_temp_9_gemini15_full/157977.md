planisphere:/ˈplænɪsfɪər/| n. |星图，天球仪 |A planisphere is a star chart projected onto a flat surface, typically on a circular card or disc, that can be rotated to show the stars visible at any given time and location.  It's a useful tool for amateur astronomers. |Example: He used a planisphere to identify the constellations visible tonight. 他用星图来辨认今晚可见的星座。|近义词：star chart, celestial map |反义词：globe (in the sense of a 3D representation)
