self-worshiping: /ˈsɛlf ˈwɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我崇拜的，自恋的 |  Engaging in or characterized by excessive admiration of oneself; narcissistic.  | 例句：His self-worshiping behavior alienated his friends.  他的自我崇拜行为疏远了他的朋友们。

例句：The self-worshiping artist refused to acknowledge any criticism of his work.  这位自我崇拜的艺术家拒绝承认对他作品的任何批评。


近义词：narcissistic, egotistical, self-centered, vain, conceited


反义词：humble, modest, self-effacing


词性：形容词
