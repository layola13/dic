non-hesitant: /ˌnɒnˈhezɪtənt/ | adj. | 不犹豫的，毫不迟疑的 |  Describes someone who acts or speaks without delay or doubt; readily and promptly.  | Example: She gave a non-hesitant "yes" to the proposal. 她毫不犹豫地对这个提议说了“是的”。  |近义词: ready, willing, prompt, immediate, decisive | 反义词: hesitant, reluctant, wavering, irresolute, undecided | 词性: adjective
