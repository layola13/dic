nonabridgable: /ˌnɒnəˈbrɪdʒəbəl/ | adj. |不可删节的，不可缩减的 | Incapable of being abridged; not allowing shortening or condensation.  This word describes something that cannot be made shorter without losing essential meaning or impact.  | 例句：The author insisted that his novel was nonabridgable, refusing any cuts or alterations. 作者坚持他的小说不可删节，拒绝任何删减或改动。


The editor found the manuscript nonabridgable and decided to publish it in its entirety. 编辑发现手稿不可删节，决定全文发表。 | 近义词：unabridged, unexpurgated, complete, integral | 反义词：abridged, shortened, condensed, summarized | 词性：形容词
