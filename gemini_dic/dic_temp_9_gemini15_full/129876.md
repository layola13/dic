non-intelligent: /ˌnɒnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt/ | adj. |非智能的，不聪明的 | Not intelligent; lacking intelligence.  |The non-intelligent design of the software led to many errors.|近义词：unintelligent, stupid, dull, unthinking |反义词：intelligent, smart, clever, bright

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is non-intelligent in matters of finance. = 他在财务方面不够聪明。
The system is non-intelligent and requires human intervention. = 这个系统是非智能的，需要人工干预。


一般过去时:
Her approach to the problem was non-intelligent. = 她解决问题的方法不够聪明。
The previous version of the software was non-intelligent and inefficient. = 软件的先前版本是非智能且低效的。


现在完成时:
They have been non-intelligent in their investment strategies. = 他们的投资策略一直不够明智。
The company has made several non-intelligent decisions lately. = 公司最近做出了一些不明智的决定。


过去完成时:
He had been non-intelligent about his health, leading to serious consequences. = 他以前对自己的健康不够重视，导致了严重的后果。
The team had made a series of non-intelligent choices before realizing their mistake. = 团队在意识到错误之前做了一系列不明智的选择。


将来时:
They will be non-intelligent in their approach to this project. = 他们处理这个项目的方法将会不够聪明。
The new software will not be non-intelligent; it will incorporate AI. = 新软件不会是非智能的；它将整合人工智能。


虚拟语气:
If he were non-intelligent, he wouldn't have achieved so much. = 如果他不聪明，他就不会取得这么大的成就。(注意：此句使用虚拟语气，表达与事实相反的情况)
I wish I hadn't been so non-intelligent in my youth. = 我真希望我年轻的时候不要那么愚蠢。


被动语态:
His actions were considered non-intelligent by many. = 许多人认为他的行为不够聪明。
The design was deemed non-intelligent and needed revision. = 该设计被认为不够聪明，需要修改。


疑问句:
Is this approach non-intelligent? = 这种方法不够聪明吗？
Was his decision truly non-intelligent, or just unfortunate? = 他的决定真的是不明智的，还是仅仅是不幸的？


否定句:
The argument wasn't non-intelligent; it was simply misguided. = 论点并非不聪明；它只是被误导了。
The plan isn't necessarily non-intelligent; it just requires more data. = 该计划不一定是愚蠢的；它只是需要更多数据。
