importer:/ɪmˈpɔːrtər/ | n. | 进口商，进口者 | A person or company that imports goods.  | Example:  He is an importer of fine wines from France. 他是一位法国优质葡萄酒进口商。 |近义词：buyer, dealer, distributor | 反义词：exporter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The importer sells goods throughout the country. = 这位进口商在全国各地销售商品。
This company imports a wide variety of products. = 这家公司进口各种各样的产品。


一般过去时:
The importer received a large shipment last week. = 进口商上周收到了一大批货物。
They imported textiles from India in the past. = 他们过去从印度进口纺织品。


现在完成时:
The importer has built a strong reputation. = 这位进口商已经建立了良好的声誉。
The company has imported over 10,000 units this year. = 今年该公司已经进口了超过10000件产品。


过去完成时:
Before the trade war, the importer had already secured a large order. = 在贸易战之前，进口商已经获得了大订单。
They had imported similar goods before, so they knew the market well. = 他们以前进口过类似的商品，所以他们很了解市场。


将来时:
The importer will expand its business next year. = 进口商明年将扩大其业务。
They will import more goods from Southeast Asia in the future. = 他们将来会从东南亚进口更多商品。


虚拟语气:
If the importer were more efficient, they would make more profit. = 如果进口商效率更高，他们就会赚更多钱。
I wish the importer had better communication with suppliers. = 我希望进口商与供应商有更好的沟通。


被动语态:
The goods were imported from China. = 这些商品是从中国进口的。
A large quantity of tea is being imported this month. = 这个月正在进口大量的茶叶。


疑问句:
Who is the importer of this product? = 这种产品的进口商是谁？
Where did they import the coffee beans from? = 他们从哪里进口咖啡豆？


否定句:
He is not an importer of electronics. = 他不是电子产品的进口商。
The company did not import any goods last month. = 上个月该公司没有进口任何商品。
