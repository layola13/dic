floorcloth:/ˈflɔːrklɒθ/| n. |地毯；擦地板的布|A floorcloth is a heavy piece of cloth, typically oilcloth or similar material, used to cover a floor.  It's often used as a protective covering or a type of inexpensive floor covering. |例句：The old house had a worn floorcloth in the kitchen.  这老房子厨房里铺着一块磨损的地毯。

例句：She cleaned the spilled milk with a damp floorcloth. 她用一块湿地板布擦掉了洒出的牛奶。


近义词：floor covering, mat, rug (depending on context) |反义词：None directly, but concepts like "bare floor" are opposite in function. |词性:名词
