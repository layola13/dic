puffling:/ˈpʌflɪŋ/| n. |小海雀|A young puffin, especially one that is still downy.  Pufflings are adorable, flightless young birds known for their fluffy down and comical appearance.| Example: The pufflings huddled together for warmth on the cliff edge.  小海雀们在悬崖边挤在一起取暖。|近义词：young puffin, chick (general term)|反义词：adult puffin
