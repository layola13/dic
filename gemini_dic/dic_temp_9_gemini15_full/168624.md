proclive:/prəˈklaɪv/| adj.|倾向于…的；有…倾向的；喜爱的|Having a natural inclination or tendency; disposed or prone.  It implies a strong natural leaning or preference.|He was proclive to exaggeration.|近义词：inclined, prone, disposed, apt, given|反义词：averse, reluctant, resistant


例句：

He was proclive to fits of anger.  他容易发脾气。

She is proclive to making hasty decisions. 她倾向于草率决策。

She is proclive to helping others. 她乐于助人。

被动语态:
