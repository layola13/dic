refractor:/rɪˈfræktər/ | n. | 折射望远镜；折射器 | A refractor is a type of telescope that uses lenses to gather and focus light.  It works by refracting (bending) the light as it passes through the lenses.  | Example:  The observatory boasts a powerful refractor telescope capable of observing distant galaxies.  天文台拥有一个强大的折射望远镜，能够观测遥远的星系。 | 近义词：refracting telescope | 反义词：reflector (反射望远镜) | 名词
