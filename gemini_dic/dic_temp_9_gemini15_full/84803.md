handsprung: /ˈhændˌsprʌŋ/ | adj. | 手工制作的，手工弹簧的 |  A handsprung watch is one in which the mainspring is wound by hand, rather than automatically. This term primarily applies to antique or vintage watches.  It implies a higher level of craftsmanship and often a more delicate mechanism. | Example: My grandfather's handsprung pocket watch is a family heirloom. 我的祖父的手动上弦怀表是传家宝。 | 近义词: hand-wound, manually wound | 反义词: automatic, self-winding |  词性: 形容词
