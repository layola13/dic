glistening:/ˈɡlɪsənɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，光亮的|Shining with a soft light; reflecting light in a sparkling way.  The word evokes a sense of smoothness and wetness, often associated with things that are freshly cleaned or wet with dew.|例句：The glistening dew drops on the grass sparkled in the morning sun.  早晨阳光下，草叶上闪闪发光的露珠晶莹剔透。

The glistening surface of the polished table reflected the candlelight.  抛光桌子的光亮表面反射着烛光。|近义词：shining, sparkling, shimmering, lustrous|反义词：dull, matte, opaque
