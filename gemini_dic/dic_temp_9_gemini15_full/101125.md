If you meant "investment", here's the analysis:


investment: /ɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 投资；投入 | An investment is the act of committing money or capital to an endeavor with the expectation of obtaining an additional income or profit.  It can also refer to the money or capital itself that is invested.  Investments can be in various forms, such as stocks, bonds, real estate, or a business. | Example:  He made a wise investment in the stock market. 他在股市上做了一笔明智的投资。

Example: The company's investment in new technology paid off handsomely. 公司对新技术的投资获得了丰厚的回报。


近义词:  expenditure, outlay, placement, stake

反义词:  withdrawal, divestment
