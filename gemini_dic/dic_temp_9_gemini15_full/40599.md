convallarin: /kɒnˈvælərɪn/ | n. |铃兰毒苷|A glycoside found in lily of the valley (Convallaria majalis) that has cardiotonic properties. It is a potent cardiac stimulant, but also highly toxic.  It's used in some medications, but its use is restricted due to its toxicity.|The convallarin in lily of the valley can be dangerous if ingested.|近义词: convallatoxin,  (other cardiac glycosides)|反义词:  (no direct antonym, but substances with opposite effects on the heart could be considered, such as certain beta blockers)|名词
