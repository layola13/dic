nymphish:/ˈnɪmfɪʃ/| adj.|若虫似的；轻盈的；少女般的|  Suggestive of a nymph; graceful and delicate; often used to describe a young woman with a delicate and charming appearance. The term often carries a slightly suggestive connotation of youthful sexuality.|例句：Her nymphish beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她若虫般的美貌迷住了房间里的每一个人。

例句：The dancer’s movements were incredibly nymphish, light and airy.  舞者的动作轻盈飘逸，宛如若虫。


近义词：gracile, sylphlike, ethereal, girlish


反义词：coarse, heavy, matronly, mannish
