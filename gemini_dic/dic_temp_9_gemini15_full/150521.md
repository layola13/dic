pantophagy:/pænˈtɒpədʒi/| n. |杂食性；泛食性|The practice or characteristic of eating all kinds of food, both animal and plant.  It refers to the dietary habit of an organism that consumes both plants and animals. |Example:  The raccoon's pantophagy allows it to thrive in diverse environments.  浣熊的杂食性使它能够在不同的环境中繁荣发展。|近义词：omnivory, polyphagy |反义词：monophagy, herbivory, carnivory
