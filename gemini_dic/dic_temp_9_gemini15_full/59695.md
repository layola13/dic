echos:/ˈɛkoʊz/ | n. |回声，回响；重复；模仿 | The sound of the echo was faint but clear. 回声微弱但清晰。|近义词：repetition, reverberation |反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The canyon echoes with the sound of birds. =峡谷回荡着鸟鸣声。
His words echo the sentiments of many people. =他的话语反映了许多人的心声。


一般过去时:
The stadium echoed with cheers. =体育场回荡着欢呼声。
Her laughter echoed in the empty room. =她的笑声在空荡的房间里回响。


现在完成时:
The news has echoed around the world. = 这则新闻已经在全世界引起反响。
The impact of his discovery has echoed through the scientific community. =他的发现的影响在科学界引起了广泛的反响。


过去完成时:
The story had echoed through generations. =这个故事代代相传。
The events of that day had echoed in his mind for years. =那一天的事件多年来一直萦绕在他的脑海里。


将来时:
The announcement will echo throughout the company. =这项声明将在公司内部引起反响。
The concert will echo with the sounds of music. =音乐会将回荡着音乐声。


虚拟语气:
If only my voice could echo across the ages. =但愿我的声音能穿越时空。
I wish my words could echo more deeply in their hearts. =我希望我的话语能更深刻地触动他们的心。


被动语态:
His ideas were echoed by many scholars. =他的想法得到了许多学者的认同。
The message was echoed across all communication channels. =这条信息通过所有沟通渠道传播开来。


疑问句:
Does your voice echo in this cavern? =你的声音在这个洞穴里会回响吗？
What echoes from the past? =过去留下了什么回响？


否定句:
The hall did not echo with sound. =大厅里没有声音回响。
My words did not echo his sentiments. =我的话语没有表达他的情感。
