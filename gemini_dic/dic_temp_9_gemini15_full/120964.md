mischaracterizing:/ˌmɪskærəktəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |歪曲，误解，曲解 | To represent (someone or something) in a way that is inaccurate or unfair.  |例句：He accused the media of mischaracterizing his remarks. 他指责媒体歪曲了他的言论。
例句：The article mischaracterized the scientist's findings, leading to public confusion. 这篇文章曲解了这位科学家的研究结果，导致公众困惑。 |近义词：distort, misrepresent, misinterpret |反义词：accurately represent, correctly portray
