cultivated:/ˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. |  耕种的；培养的；有教养的；精耕细作的 |  Improved and refined by education or training; showing good taste and manners.  |例句：She has a cultivated taste in music. 她对音乐品味高雅。

例句：The cultivated fields stretched as far as the eye could see.  一眼望去，田野一望无垠。


近义词：refined, cultured, polished, sophisticated | 反义词：uncultivated, unrefined, crude, rustic

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer cultivates his land every year. = 农民每年耕种他的土地。
She cultivates a positive attitude. = 她培养积极的态度。
