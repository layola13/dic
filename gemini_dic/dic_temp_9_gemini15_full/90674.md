humblest: /ˈhʌmbləst/ | adj. | 最谦逊的，最卑微的 |  Most humble; showing the greatest humility.  It is the superlative form of "humble".  | 例句：He offered his humblest apologies for his mistake. 他为他犯的错误表示最诚挚的歉意。

He showed the humblest of hearts in his service to the poor. 他在为穷人服务时展现出最谦卑的心。


近义词：most modest, most meek, most unassuming | 反义词：most arrogant, most proud, most haughty | 词性：形容词
