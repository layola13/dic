fibsters:/ˈfɪbstərz/| n. |说谎者，骗子 |A fibster is someone who tells lies, especially small, insignificant ones.  They are not necessarily malicious liars, but their habit is to embellish the truth or avoid telling the whole truth. |例句：He's a bit of a fibster, always exaggerating his accomplishments. 他有点爱说谎，总是夸大自己的成就。

例句：Don't trust him; he's a notorious fibster.  别相信他；他是个臭名昭著的说谎者。


近义词：liar, prevaricator, storyteller (in a negative context), deceiver

反义词：truth-teller, honest person


词性：名词
