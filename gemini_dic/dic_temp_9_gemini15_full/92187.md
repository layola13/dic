hyperstimulates: /ˌhaɪpərˈstɪmjuleɪts/ | vt. |过度刺激，过度兴奋 | To stimulate excessively; to overstimulate.  This verb implies an excessive or potentially harmful level of stimulation. |例句：The bright lights hyperstimulated the baby, causing it to cry.  明亮的灯光过度刺激了婴儿，导致它哭了起来。
The constant noise hyperstimulated his nervous system.  持续的噪音过度刺激了他的神经系统。|近义词：overstimulate, overexcite, excite excessively |反义词：under-stimulate, soothe, calm
