re-case:/ˌriːˈkeɪs/| vt.|重新装箱；重新考虑；重新安排|To repackage or rearrange something; to reconsider or re-evaluate something.|例句：We need to re-case the goods before shipping them. 我们需要在发货前重新包装货物。|例句：Let's re-case the plan and see if we can improve it. 让我们重新考虑一下计划，看看能否改进。|近义词：repackage, reconsider, rearrange |反义词：leave as is, maintain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-case the products every week. = 他们每周都重新包装产品。
He re-cases his arguments regularly. = 他定期重新考虑他的论点。


一般过去时:
She re-cased the jewelry before putting it in the safe. = 她把珠宝重新装箱后才放进保险箱。
They re-cased the evidence to present it more effectively. = 他们重新整理证据以便更有效地呈递。


现在完成时:
We have re-cased all the damaged goods. = 我们已经重新包装了所有损坏的货物。
She has re-cased her position on the issue. = 她已经重新考虑了她在这个问题上的立场。


过去完成时:
He had re-cased the boxes before the movers arrived. = 在搬运工到达之前，他已经重新装箱了。
They had re-cased their strategy after the first attempt failed. = 第一次尝试失败后，他们已经重新调整了策略。


将来时:
We will re-case the exhibit for the next show. = 我们将为下一次展览重新布置展品。
She will re-case her arguments for the appeal. = 她将为上诉重新整理她的论点。


虚拟语气:
If we re-cased the data, we might find a different result. = 如果我们重新整理数据，我们可能会得到不同的结果。
I wish I had re-cased my decision before submitting it. = 我真希望在提交之前重新考虑我的决定。


被动语态:
