nonnationalisms: /ˌnɒnˌnæʃənəlɪzəmz/ | n. |非民族主义；反民族主义|The rejection or opposition to nationalism, often encompassing diverse ideologies and practices that challenge the nation-state and its assumptions.|The rise of nonnationalisms in the late 20th century reflects a growing awareness of global interconnectedness and the limitations of nationalist ideologies.|近义词：antinationalism, transnationalism, cosmopolitanism|反义词：nationalism, patriotism
