osteoarthritic:/ˌɒstiːoʊɑːrˈθraɪtɪk/| adj.|骨关节炎的；患骨关节炎的|Relating to or characteristic of osteoarthritis.  Osteoarthritis is a degenerative joint disease characterized by the breakdown of cartilage and the resulting inflammation.|例句：She suffers from osteoarthritic pain in her knees. 她膝盖患有骨关节炎疼痛。|例句：He underwent surgery to alleviate his osteoarthritic symptoms. 他接受了手术来减轻他的骨关节炎症状。|近义词：arthritic, degenerative|反义词：healthy (in relation to joints)
