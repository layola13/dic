preilluminating: /ˌpriːɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先照明的；预先阐明的 |  Describing something that illuminates or clarifies something else before the main event or explanation.  It suggests a preparatory or anticipatory illumination, providing context or background understanding. |


例句：

The preilluminating lecture gave us a strong foundation for understanding the complex research paper.  = 预先的讲座为我们理解复杂的论文打下了坚实的基础。


The artist's preilluminating sketches hinted at the masterpiece to come. = 艺术家的预先草图暗示了即将问世的杰作。


近义词： introductory, preparatory, preliminary, prefatory


反义词： concluding, final, subsequent


词性：形容词
