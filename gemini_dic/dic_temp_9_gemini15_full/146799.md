overimaginativeness: /ˌoʊvərɪˌmædʒɪnətɪvnəs/ | n. |过度幻想；想入非非 |  Excessive imagination; the tendency to imagine things that are unlikely or impossible.  It often leads to unrealistic expectations or anxieties. | The child's overimaginativeness led him to believe monsters lived under his bed.  孩子的过度幻想让他相信床底下住着怪物。|近义词：fantasticalness, flights of fancy | 反义词：realism, practicality
