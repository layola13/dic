cocounselling: /ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ | n. |  共同咨询，互助咨询 |  A form of peer support where two people take turns sharing their problems and offering each other support and advice.  It is not a professional therapy but a form of mutual help. | Example:  They found cocounselling to be a helpful way to work through their emotional difficulties.  他们发现共同咨询是解决他们情绪问题的有效方法。 | 近义词：peer support, mutual support, self-help | 反义词：professional therapy (in the sense that it's not professionally led)
