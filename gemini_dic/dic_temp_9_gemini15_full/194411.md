self-realizations: /ˌsɛlfˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |自我实现，顿悟 |The process of understanding oneself and one's potential, and fulfilling that potential.  It often involves a significant personal growth and transformation. |例句：Her self-realizations led her to quit her job and pursue her passion for art.  她的顿悟使她辞去工作，追求她对艺术的热情。


例句：Through meditation and introspection, he experienced profound self-realizations about his life's purpose. 通过冥想和反省，他对自己人生目标有了深刻的顿悟。


近义词：self-discovery, enlightenment, awakening, epiphany | 反义词：self-deception, delusion, ignorance | 词性: n.
