endorhiza: /ˌɛnˈdɔːraɪzə/ | n. | 内生菌根 | A type of mycorrhiza in which the fungal hyphae penetrate the cortical cells of the plant root.  This symbiotic relationship provides the plant with increased access to nutrients and water, while the fungus receives carbohydrates from the plant.  |  Example:  Arbuscular mycorrhizae are a common type of endorhiza found in many plant species.  内生菌根是一种常见的菌根类型，存在于许多植物物种中。| 近义词：mycorrhiza (although a broader term)| 反义词：ectotrophic mycorrhiza (外生菌根) |  名词
