costarring: /ˌkoʊˈstɑːrɪŋ/ | v. | 配演，联合主演 |  The act of starring in a film or play alongside another actor or actors. It implies a shared prominence, but usually less than the main star.  |例句：The movie features Brad Pitt costarring with Angelina Jolie. 这部电影由布拉德·皮特和安吉丽娜·朱莉联合主演。

The film is costarring  Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone. 这部电影由瑞恩·高斯林和艾玛·斯通联合主演。


近义词：co-starring, appearing with, sharing the screen with | 反义词：starring alone, solo performance | 词性：动名词
