cyanide:/ˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. |氰化物 | A poisonous compound containing the cyanide group (CN), especially potassium cyanide.  Cyanide is extremely toxic and can be fatal if ingested, inhaled, or absorbed through the skin. It interferes with cellular respiration, preventing cells from using oxygen. | Example:  The victim died from cyanide poisoning. 氰化物中毒导致受害者死亡。  The detective found traces of cyanide at the crime scene.  侦探在犯罪现场发现了氰化物的痕迹。|近义词：poison, toxin |反义词：antidote (解毒剂)
