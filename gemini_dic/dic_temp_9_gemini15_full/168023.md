pristine:/ˈprɪstiːn/| adj.|原始的；纯净的；崭新的|Free from dirt or pollution; completely clean, pure, or uncorrupted.|The pristine beach was a welcome sight after days of travel.|近义词：unspoiled, immaculate, untouched, perfect|反义词：dirty, polluted, contaminated, spoiled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lake remains pristine. = 湖泊依旧保持着原始状态。
This pristine environment needs protection. = 这个纯净的环境需要保护。

一般过去时:
The car was pristine when it left the factory. = 这辆车出厂时崭新如初。
The air was pristine before the industrial revolution. = 工业革命前空气非常纯净。

现在完成时:
The area has remained pristine for centuries. = 这个地区几个世纪以来一直保持着原始状态。
The painting has remained pristine despite its age. = 这幅画尽管年代久远，却依然完好无损。

过去完成时:
The forest had remained pristine before the logging began. = 在伐木开始之前，森林一直保持着原始状态。
The artifact had been kept pristine in a museum vault. = 这件文物一直被保存在博物馆的保险库中，保持着完好无损的状态。

将来时:
We hope the beach will remain pristine for future generations. = 我们希望海滩能够为子孙后代保留原始状态。
The government plans to keep the park pristine. = 政府计划保持公园的原始状态。

虚拟语气:
If only the city were pristine! = 但愿城市能保持干净整洁！
I wish the air were as pristine as it once was. = 我希望空气能像以前一样纯净。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
The river is no longer pristine. = 这条河不再纯净了。
The once pristine forest is now heavily polluted. = 曾经原始纯净的森林现在严重污染了。
