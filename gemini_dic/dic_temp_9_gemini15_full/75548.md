fuchsine:/ˈfuːksɪn/| n. |品红，碱性品红|A synthetic dye, typically magenta or reddish-purple, derived from aniline. It is used in various applications, including textile dyeing, biological staining, and as a food coloring (though less common now due to safety concerns).|The dress was dyed a vibrant fuchsine pink.|近义词：magenta, crimson, rose, carmine|反义词：无明显反义词
