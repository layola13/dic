half-closing: /ˈhæfˌkloʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 半关闭的；半闭合的 | Partially closed; in a state between fully open and fully closed.  This term is often used to describe a state of transition or incomplete closure. |


例句：

The valve was in a half-closing state, causing a slight leak. = 阀门处于半关闭状态，导致轻微泄漏。

The door remained half-closing, stuck halfway. = 门半掩着，卡在半路上了。


近义词：partially closed, semi-closed, ajar


反义词：fully open, fully closed, wide open


词性：形容词
