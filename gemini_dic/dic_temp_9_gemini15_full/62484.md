endosporous: /ˌɛndoʊˈspɔːrəs/ | adj. | 内生孢子的；产生内生孢子的 |  Relating to or characteristic of endospores, the dormant, resistant form of certain bacteria.  Endosporous bacteria are capable of forming endospores. |

例句：

Bacillus species are known for their endosporous nature.  | 芽孢杆菌属以其产生内生孢子的特性而闻名。

The endosporous nature of the bacteria allowed it to survive harsh conditions. | 细菌的内生孢子特性使其能够在恶劣条件下生存。


近义词：spore-forming (成孢子的)


反义词：asporous (无孢子的)


词性：形容词
