deeply:/ˈdiːpli/ | adv. | 深深地；强烈地 |  to a great extent or degree; intensely.  | 例句：I was deeply moved by her story. = 我被她的故事深深打动了。 | 例句：He deeply regretted his actions. = 他深深地后悔自己的行为。 | 近义词：intensely, profoundly, thoroughly | 反义词：superficially, slightly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deeply loves his wife. = 他深深地爱着他的妻子。
She deeply cares about her friends. = 她非常关心她的朋友们。


一般过去时:
They deeply regretted their decision. = 他们深深地后悔他们的决定。
I deeply appreciated your help. = 我非常感谢你的帮助。


现在完成时:
I have been deeply affected by this news. = 我被这个消息深深地影响了。
She has deeply considered the consequences. = 她已经深刻地考虑了后果。


过去完成时:
He had deeply loved her for many years. = 他已经深深地爱她多年了。
They had deeply regretted their past mistakes. = 他们已经深深地后悔他们过去的错误。


将来时:
We will deeply miss him when he's gone. = 他走了我们会非常想念他。

虚拟语气:
If I knew the truth, I would be deeply saddened. = 如果我知道真相，我会非常悲伤。
I wish I had deeply understood the situation earlier. = 我希望我早点深入了解情况。


被动语态:
His words were deeply felt by the audience. = 他的话语深深地触动了观众。
The impact of the event was deeply felt throughout the country. = 这件事的影响在全国范围内都感受到了。


疑问句:

否定句:
