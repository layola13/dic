haggis:/ˈhɑːɡɪs/| n. |羊杂碎|A savoury pudding containing sheep's pluck (heart, liver, and lungs), minced with onion, oatmeal, suet, spices, and traditionally enclosed in a sheep's stomach before boiling; a traditional Scottish dish.| Example:  Haggis is a national dish of Scotland.  羊杂碎是苏格兰的国菜。|近义词:  (No direct synonyms, as it's a unique dish)|反义词: (No direct antonyms)
