hand-down:/hændˈdaʊn/| vt.| 传下，传递；判决；|to pass something down from one generation to the next; to give a legal decision|例句：He handed down the family heirloom to his son. 他把传家宝传给了儿子。 |例句：The judge handed down a sentence of five years. 法官判处五年徒刑。|近义词：pass down, bequeath, transmit|反义词：inherit, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hands down the wisdom to his children. = 他将智慧传给他的孩子们。
She hands down judgments fairly. = 她公平地做出判决。


一般过去时:
My grandmother handed down this recipe to me. = 我祖母把这个食谱传给了我。
The court handed down a guilty verdict. = 法庭作出了有罪判决。


现在完成时:
They have handed down this tradition for centuries. = 他们几个世纪以来一直传承着这一传统。
The judge has handed down the sentence. = 法官已经宣判了。


过去完成时:
The family had handed down the secret for generations. = 这个家族世世代代保守着这个秘密。
Before the trial ended, the judge had handed down several rulings. = 在审判结束之前，法官已经下达了几项裁决。


将来时:
He will hand down his business to his son. = 他将把他的生意传给他的儿子。
The court will hand down its decision next week. = 法院将在下周做出裁决。


虚拟语气:
If he had handed down the inheritance, his son would be wealthy. = 如果他传下了遗产，他的儿子就会富有。
I wish the judge had handed down a lighter sentence. = 我希望法官能判个轻一点的刑罚。


被动语态:
The decision was handed down by the supreme court. = 该决定由最高法院作出。
The family recipe has been handed down through many generations. = 这个家传食谱已经传承了好几代。


疑问句:
Will the judge hand down the verdict today? = 法官今天会宣判吗？
Has the tradition been handed down to the next generation? = 这个传统已经传给下一代了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hand down the family business. = 他没有把家族企业传承下去。
The judge did not hand down a decision immediately. = 法官没有立即做出裁决。


PHRASE->
hand down a decision = 作出裁决
hand down a judgment = 作出判决
hand down a ruling = 作出裁决
hand down an order = 下达命令
hand down a sentence = 宣判
