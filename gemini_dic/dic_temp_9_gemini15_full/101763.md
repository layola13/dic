irresponsive:/ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsɪv/| adj.| 无反应的；无动于衷的 | Not responding to something; unresponsive.  | Example:  The unresponsive patient showed no signs of improvement.  这个毫无反应的病人没有显示出任何好转的迹象。

The government's unresponsive stance on the issue led to widespread protests. 政府对该问题的无动于衷态度导致了大规模抗议。 | 近义词：unresponsive, unreactive, insensitive | 反义词：responsive, reactive, sensitive |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is unresponsive to my questions. 他对我的问题没有反应。
The machine is unresponsive. 这台机器没有反应。

一般过去时:
She was unresponsive to his advances. 她对他示好没有反应。
The system was unresponsive yesterday. 系统昨天没有反应。

现在完成时:
The patient has been unresponsive for hours. 病人几个小时都没有反应了。
The market has been unresponsive to recent economic changes. 市场对最近的经济变化没有反应。

过去完成时:
He had been unresponsive to treatment for weeks. 他几周来对治疗都没有反应。
The company had been unresponsive to customer complaints. 公司对客户投诉一直没有反应。

将来时:
The system will be unresponsive if overloaded. 系统如果超载将无法响应。
I fear he will be unresponsive to my pleas. 我担心他不会理会我的请求。

虚拟语气:
If he were more responsive, we would have avoided this. 如果他反应更灵敏，我们就不会遇到这种情况了。
I wish the government were more responsive to the needs of its citizens. 我希望政府能更积极地回应公民的需求。

被动语态:
His concerns were ignored; his pleas were left unanswered and unresponsive. 他的担忧被忽视了；他的请求没有得到回应，无人理会。
The proposal was deemed unresponsive to the current challenges. 该提案被认为无法应对当前的挑战。

疑问句:
Is the computer unresponsive? 电脑没有反应吗？
Why is he so unresponsive to my emails? 他为什么对我的邮件没有反应？

否定句:
He is not unresponsive to criticism; he just needs time to reflect. 他并非对批评毫无反应；他只是需要时间反思。
The company wasn't unresponsive; they simply needed more information. 公司并非没有反应；他们只是需要更多信息。


PHRASE->
be unresponsive to 对…没有反应
remain unresponsive 保持无反应
become unresponsive 变得无反应
prove unresponsive 证明没有反应
show an unresponsive attitude 表现出冷漠的态度
