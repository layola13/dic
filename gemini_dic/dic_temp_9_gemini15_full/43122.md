coyer:/ˈkɔɪər/| adj.| 羞怯的；含羞的；做作的；扭捏的 |  Shy, coy, and affected; pretending to be shy or modest in order to attract attention.  It often implies a playful or flirtatious shyness. | 例句：She gave him a coy smile. 她对他嫣然一笑。

She cast a coy glance over her shoulder. 她羞涩地回头看了一眼。


近义词：shy, bashful, demure, reserved | 反义词：bold, forward, brazen, immodest | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kitten is coy with strangers. = 小猫对陌生人很羞怯。
She is coy about her age. = 她对自己的年龄很含糊其辞。


一般过去时:
He was coy about revealing his plans. = 他对透露他的计划很含糊。
She acted coy when asked about her boyfriend. = 当被问到她的男朋友时，她装作很羞涩。


现在完成时:
She has been coy about her feelings for him. = 她一直对她对他的感情含糊其辞。
He has always been coy in public. = 他一直以来在公共场合都很羞怯。


过去完成时:
She had been coy all evening, but finally confessed her love. = 她整个晚上都很含羞，但最终还是承认了她的爱意。
They had been coy about their relationship until the wedding. = 在婚礼之前，他们对他们的关系一直很保密。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If she were less coy, he might ask her out. = 如果她不那么羞怯，他可能会约她出去。
I wish she weren't so coy about her ambitions. = 我希望她对她的抱负不要那么含糊其辞。


被动语态:
Her feelings were coyly expressed in her poems. = 她的感情在她诗歌中含蓄地表达出来。
His intentions were coyly masked by his actions. = 他的意图被他含糊其辞的行为掩盖了。


疑问句:

Why is he so coy about this matter? = 他为什么对这件事如此含糊其辞？


否定句:
She wasn't coy at all; she was simply shy. = 她一点也不做作；她只是害羞。
He wasn't coy about his feelings; he openly expressed his love. = 他对自己的感情并不含糊其辞；他公开表达了他的爱意。
