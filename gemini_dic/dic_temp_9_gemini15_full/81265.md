granitoids:/ˈɡrænaɪtɔɪdz/ | n. | 花岗质岩 | Granitoids are intrusive igneous rocks that are rich in feldspar and quartz. They are often light-colored and coarse-grained.  | Example: The mountain range is primarily composed of granitoids. 这座山脉主要由花岗质岩组成。 |近义词：plutonic rocks, felsic rocks |反义词：volcanic rocks, mafic rocks
