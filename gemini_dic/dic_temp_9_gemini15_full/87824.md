hickories:/ˈhɪkəriːz/ | n. |山核桃树；山核桃木；山核桃 |The word "hickories" is the plural form of "hickory," referring to various types of hardwood trees in the genus Carya, known for their strong, durable wood and edible nuts.  The wood is used in furniture making, tool handles, and smoking meats.
