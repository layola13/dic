polyconic:/ˌpɒliˈkɒnɪk/| adj.|多圆锥的；多锥体的|Relating to or denoting a map projection in which the meridians are represented by straight lines converging at a common point, and the parallels of latitude are represented by arcs of circles, the centers of which lie on a line that is perpendicular to the central meridian.  This projection minimizes distortion in areas near the central meridian.|The polyconic projection is used for topographic maps.|近义词：multiconic|反义词：无
