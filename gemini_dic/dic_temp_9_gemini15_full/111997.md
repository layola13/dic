lonely:/ˈloʊnli/ /ˈləʊnli/ | adj. | 孤独的；寂寞的；偏僻的 | Feeling sad because one has no friends or company.  Characterized by a lack of people or activity. | 例句：She felt lonely after her husband died.  她丈夫死后感到很孤独。

例句：The house stands lonely on the hill. 这房子孤零零地矗立在山上。


近义词：solitary, isolated, desolate, alone | 反义词：companionable, sociable, crowded


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He feels lonely every night. 他每晚都感到孤独。
She is lonely because she has no friends. 她很孤独，因为她没有朋友。


一般过去时:
I felt lonely when I first moved to the city. 我初到这个城市时感到很孤独。
They were lonely during the long winter. 他们在漫长的冬天里感到孤独。


现在完成时:
I have felt lonely since he left. 自从他离开后，我一直感到孤独。
She has been lonely for many years. 她已经孤独了很多年了。


过去完成时:
I had felt lonely before I met my best friend. 在我遇到我的好朋友之前，我一直感到孤独。
They had been lonely for a long time before they found each other. 在他们找到彼此之前，他们已经孤独了很久。


将来时:
I will feel lonely if you leave. 如果你离开，我会感到孤独。
She will be lonely without her family.  没有家人陪伴，她会很孤独。


虚拟语气:
I wish I weren't so lonely. 我希望我不那么孤独。
If I had friends, I wouldn't be so lonely. 如果我有朋友，我就不会这么孤独了。


被动语态: (形容词lonely本身不能用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Are you lonely? 你孤独吗？
Does he feel lonely? 他感到孤独吗？


否定句:
I am not lonely. 我不孤独。
She doesn't feel lonely. 她不感到孤独。
