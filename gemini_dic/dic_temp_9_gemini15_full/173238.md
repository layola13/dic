quasi-comprehensively:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪvli/ | adv. | 几乎全面地；近乎完整地 |  Almost completely; nearly comprehensively.  It suggests a thoroughness that falls just short of being truly comprehensive.  The prefix "quasi" means "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent." | The study quasi-comprehensively covered the topic, leaving only minor details unexplored.  = 这项研究几乎全面地涵盖了该主题，只留下了一些小的细节未探索。  The report attempted a quasi-comprehensively analysis of the market, but some niche segments were missed. = 这份报告试图对市场进行几乎全面的分析，但错过了一些利基细分市场。| comprehensively, thoroughly | superficially, partially | adv.


USAGE->
