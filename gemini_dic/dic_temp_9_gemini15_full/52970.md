dingo:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊ/| n. |澳洲野犬|A wild dog native to Australia, typically reddish-brown in color.  Dingos are known for their intelligence and adaptability. They are often considered a pest due to their predation on livestock.|Example:  The farmer lost several sheep to a pack of dingos.  农夫损失了几只羊，因为一群澳洲野犬。

The dingo population is threatened in some areas.  在某些地区，澳洲野犬的数量受到了威胁。|近义词：wild dog, native dog |反义词：domestic dog
