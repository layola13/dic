reshuffles:/ˌriːˈʃʌfəl/| vt.|重新洗牌；改组；重新安排 |To change the order or arrangement of something, especially the members of a group or team.  |例句：The company reshuffled its management team after a period of poor performance. 公司业绩不佳后，对管理团队进行了改组。

例句：The teacher reshuffled the desks to create more space.老师重新安排课桌以腾出更多空间。


近义词：reorganize, rearrange, reshuffle, reorganise, re-arrange


反义词：leave unchanged, maintain the status quo


词性：动词
