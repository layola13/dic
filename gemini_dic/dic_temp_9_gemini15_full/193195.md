sediments:/ˈsɛdəmənts/| n.|沉积物|Material that has been deposited by some natural process, as by the action of water, ice, or wind.  Sediments typically consist of particles of rock, minerals, and organic matter.|例句：The river carries sediments downstream and deposits them in the delta. 这条河携带沉积物向下游，并将它们沉积在三角洲。

例句：Geologists study sediments to understand past climates and environments. 地质学家研究沉积物以了解过去的气候和环境。


近义词：deposits, accumulations, precipitates|反义词：None directly, but antonyms could be related to the processes that oppose sedimentation, such as erosion or uplift.|词性:名词
