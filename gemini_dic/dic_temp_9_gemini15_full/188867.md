russophobist:/ˈrʌsɵfoʊbɪst/| n. | 患有恐俄症的人 | A person who has an irrational fear or hatred of Russia, its people, or its culture.  Russophobists often exhibit prejudice and negative stereotypes against anything Russian. | Example: Some historians argue that the Cold War fueled russophobia in the West.  一些历史学家认为冷战加剧了西方对俄罗斯的恐惧。|近义词：anti-Russian, Russia-hater |反义词：Russophile
