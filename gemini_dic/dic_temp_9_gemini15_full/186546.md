riddled:/ˈrɪdld/| adj.|充满…的；布满…的；充满漏洞的|Full of; permeated with; containing many flaws or inconsistencies.|例句：The garden was riddled with weeds.  花园里长满了杂草。
The argument was riddled with inconsistencies. 论点充满了自相矛盾之处。|近义词：filled with, permeated with, full of holes, flawed|反义词：perfect, flawless, immaculate


USAGE->

The document is riddled with errors. = 这份文件充满了错误。
His speech is riddled with clichés. = 他的演讲充满了陈词滥调。
