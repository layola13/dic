ciggie's:/ˈsɪɡiz/| n. |香烟（复数所有格）|Ciggie's is a colloquial and informal plural possessive form of "cigarette," referring to multiple cigarettes belonging to someone.  It's a shortened, informal version of "cigarettes."|He left his ciggie's on the table. 他把他的香烟留在桌上了。|cigarettes, smokes|n/a


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  The possessive form already indicates ownership.
