ill-treat:/ɪlˈtriːt/| vt.|虐待，粗暴对待|To treat someone badly or cruelly.|The animals were ill-treated in the factory farm.  这些动物在工厂化农场里受到虐待。|He ill-treated his wife for years. 他多年来虐待他的妻子。|abuse, mistreat, maltreat|treat kindly, care for|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ill-treat the animals. = 他们虐待动物。
She ill-treats her employees. = 她虐待她的员工。

一般过去时:
He ill-treated his dog. = 他虐待了他的狗。
They ill-treated the prisoners. = 他们虐待了囚犯。

现在完成时:
They have ill-treated the workers for years. = 他们多年来一直虐待工人。
She has ill-treated her children. = 她虐待了她的孩子们。

过去完成时:
The farmer had ill-treated his animals before the inspection. = 在检查之前，这个农民虐待了他的动物。
They had ill-treated the refugees for months. = 几个月来他们一直虐待难民。

将来时:
They will ill-treat the animals if they are not stopped. = 如果不阻止他们，他们将会虐待动物。
He will ill-treat his staff if they don't meet his demands. = 如果他的员工不满足他的要求，他会虐待他们。

虚拟语气:
If I were in their shoes, I wouldn't ill-treat them. = 如果我处于他们的位置，我不会虐待他们。
I wish he hadn't ill-treated that poor animal. = 我真希望他没有虐待那可怜的动物。

被动语态:
The animals were ill-treated. = 动物受到了虐待。
The children were ill-treated by their parents. = 孩子们受到了父母的虐待。

疑问句:
Were the animals ill-treated? = 动物受到了虐待吗？
Have the workers been ill-treated? = 工人们受到了虐待吗？

否定句:
They did not ill-treat the animals. = 他们没有虐待动物。
She would never ill-treat a child. = 她决不会虐待孩子。


PHRASE->

词源:
ill- (否定前缀) + treat (对待)
