haemoplastic:/ˈhiːməʊˈplæstɪk/ | adj. |促凝的，止血的 | Relating to or promoting the formation of blood clots.  Haemoplastic agents are used to stop bleeding. |Example: The surgeon applied a haemoplastic dressing to the wound to promote clotting and stem the bleeding.  外科医生在伤口上敷上了促凝敷料以促进凝血和止血。|近义词：coagulant, hemostatic |反义词：anticoagulant
