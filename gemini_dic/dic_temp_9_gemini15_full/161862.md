praecommissure:/ˌpriːkəˈmɪʃər/| n. |前连合|A small bundle of nerve fibers connecting the two hemispheres of the brain anterior to the anterior commissure.  It is involved in various functions, including visual processing and emotional regulation.|The praecommissure plays a crucial role in the brain's intricate network of communication. |近义词：anterior commissure (though functionally distinct)|反义词：无
