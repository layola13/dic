insolation:/ˌɪn.səˈleɪʃən/| n. |日照；日射；太阳辐射 |The amount of solar radiation reaching a particular surface area.  It's a crucial factor in various processes, including weather patterns, plant growth, and solar energy production. |例句：The high insolation in the desert leads to extreme temperatures.  沙漠地区的高日照导致极端温度。

例句：The amount of insolation varies depending on latitude and season. 日照量取决于纬度和季节。


近义词：solar radiation, sun radiation, insolation rate |反义词：shade, shadow |词性: 名词
