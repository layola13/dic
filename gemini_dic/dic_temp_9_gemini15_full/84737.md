handle: /ˈhændəl/ | n., vt. |  把手；柄；控制；处理；应付 |  A handle is a part of an object designed to be grasped by the hand.  To handle something is to deal with or manage it. | 近义词：grip, control, manage, deal with | 反义词：neglect, ignore


n.  把手；柄；控制；管理

例句：

The door handle is broken.  = 门把手坏了。

He has a good handle on the situation. = 他对局势掌控良好。


vt. 处理；应付；操作；管理

例句：

She handled the crisis calmly and efficiently. = 她冷静有效地处理了这场危机。

Can you handle the pressure of this job? = 你能应付这份工作带来的压力吗？


词源：

Old English *handlian, from hand + -ian.  The meaning evolved from "to grasp with the hand" to the more abstract sense of "to manage" or "to deal with."
