perpetrable: /ˈpɜːrpɪtrəbl/ | adj. | 可做到的；可实施的 | Capable of being perpetrated or committed; able to be done.  Something that is perpetrable is something that can be done, often implying a negative or harmful action. | The crime was perpetrable only because of lax security.  这起罪行之所以能够发生，仅仅是因为安全措施松懈。  The plan, while ambitious, seemed barely perpetrable given the resources available.  尽管这个计划雄心勃勃，但考虑到现有资源，它似乎几乎无法实施。 | feasible, practicable, possible | impossible, unfeasible, impracticable
