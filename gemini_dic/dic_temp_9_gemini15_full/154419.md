permissiveness:/pərˈmɪsɪvnəs/| n. |宽容；许可；放纵 |The state or quality of being permissive; readiness to allow or tolerate something.  It often implies a lack of strict control or discipline.  |例句：The permissive atmosphere of the school allowed students considerable freedom. 学校宽松的氛围让学生们享有相当大的自由。

例句：The parents' permissiveness led to their child's unruly behavior. 父母的放纵导致了孩子的不良行为。


近义词：tolerance, leniency, indulgence, liberalism |反义词：strictness, severity, authoritarianism, discipline
