churchmanship:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmənʃɪp/| n. |教会精神；教会管理；教会成员身份 |The quality of being a good and faithful member of a church;  the skill and practice of managing a church.  It encompasses theological understanding,  devotion, leadership, and administration within a church context.|Example: His strong churchmanship was evident in his dedication to the parish and his commitment to its growth.  他的坚定教会精神体现在他对教区的奉献和对其发展的承诺上。|近义词：ecclesiastical administration,  religious devotion, piety |反义词：irreverence,  godlessness, secularism
