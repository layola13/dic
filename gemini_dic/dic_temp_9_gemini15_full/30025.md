caymen:/ˈkeɪmən/| n.|凯门鳄|A cayman is a crocodilian reptile found in Central and South America.  They are similar in appearance to alligators but are generally smaller and have a narrower snout.|例句：The tourists saw a cayman basking in the sun near the river. 游客们在河边看到一只凯门鳄在晒太阳。|近义词：crocodile, alligator|反义词:None.
