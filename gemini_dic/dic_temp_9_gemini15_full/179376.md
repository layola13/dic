redressing:/rɛˈdrɛsɪŋ/| v.|改正，纠正；补偿；穿衣|To correct or improve a wrong or bad situation; to compensate for a wrong or injury; to put on clothes.|例句：The company is redressing the grievances of its employees. 公司正在纠正员工的冤屈。|例句：The government is redressing the balance of power. 政府正在纠正权力失衡。|近义词：rectifying, rectifying, compensating, amending|反义词：worsening, exacerbating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redress grievances promptly. = 他们迅速地纠正了委屈。
The government redresses the imbalance in education. = 政府正在纠正教育中的不平衡。


一般过去时:
