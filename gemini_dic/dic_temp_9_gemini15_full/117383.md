mediatrix: /ˈmiːdiˌeɪtrɪks/ | n. | 女调解人，女仲裁人 | A woman who mediates or arbitrates disputes.  A female mediator. |例句：The mediatrix patiently listened to both sides before suggesting a compromise. 女调解人耐心地倾听了双方的意见，然后提出了一项妥协方案。

例句：She acted as a mediatrix in the family feud, helping to bridge the gap between her estranged parents. 她作为家庭纠纷的调解人，帮助弥合了她疏远的父母之间的裂痕。


近义词：mediator, arbitrator, conciliator, intercessor | 反义词：aggressor, antagonist


词性：名词
