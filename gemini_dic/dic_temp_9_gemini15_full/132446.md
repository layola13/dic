nondepositor:/ˌnɒndɪˈpɒzɪtə(r)/| n. |非存款人|A person who does not have a deposit account with a particular bank or financial institution.  Someone who doesn't have money deposited in a bank.|Example: The nondepositor was surprised by the high interest rates offered to account holders.  这位非存款人对账户持有人享有的高利率感到惊讶。|近义词：non-customer, outsider |反义词：depositor
