houdini:/ˈhuːdɪni/ | n. | 逃脱术大师；逃脱魔术；不可思议的逃脱 |  A person who performs escapes from seemingly impossible situations, especially a magician specializing in such acts; an act of escape from a seemingly impossible situation; a skillful and surprising escape.  | Example: Harry Houdini was a legendary escape artist.  哈里·胡迪尼是一位传奇的逃脱艺术家。 He managed a Houdini-like escape from the burning building. 他从燃烧的建筑物中逃脱，如同胡迪尼般不可思议。 | 近义词：illusionist, escapologist, magician | 反义词：captive, prisoner
