furly:/ˈfɜːrli/| adj.|狂暴的；暴怒的；凶猛的|  Describes something or someone that is violent, ferocious, or furious.  It suggests a wild, untamed quality, often associated with animals but can also be applied to people.|The furly bear attacked the hiker. = 那只狂暴的熊袭击了徒步旅行者。|The storm brought furly winds and heavy rain. = 暴风雨带来了狂暴的狂风和暴雨。|近义词：fierce, ferocious, violent, raging|反义词：calm, gentle, peaceful
