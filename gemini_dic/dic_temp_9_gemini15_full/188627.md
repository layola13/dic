ruminate:/ˈruːmɪneɪt/| vt. vi. |沉思；反刍；反复思考 |To think deeply and at length; to ponder.  It can also refer to the process of chewing cud (for animals).|例句：He sat alone, ruminating on the events of the day. 他独自一人坐着，反复思考当天发生的事情。

The sheep were ruminating peacefully in the field. 羊儿们在田野里安详地反刍。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, reflect |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I ruminate on my mistakes daily. = 我每天都反复思考我的错误。
She ruminates on the meaning of life. = 她沉思生命的意义。


一般过去时:
He ruminated on his future career path. = 他反复思考他未来的职业道路。
They ruminated over the implications of the new policy. = 他们仔细考虑这项新政策的影响。


现在完成时:
I have ruminated on this problem for weeks. = 我已经思考这个问题好几个星期了。
She has ruminated over the decision for days. = 她已经考虑这个决定好几天了。


过去完成时:
He had ruminated for hours before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前思考了好几个小时。
They had ruminated on the subject extensively before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经广泛地思考过这个主题。


将来时:
I will ruminate on this proposal before responding. = 我会在回复之前仔细考虑这个提案。
She will ruminate on the options available to her. = 她将会仔细考虑她可选择的方案。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ruminate more on the subject. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地思考这个问题。
I wish I could ruminate more peacefully. = 我希望我能更平静地思考。


被动语态:
The problem was ruminated upon by the committee. = 委员会反复思考了这个问题。(较为正式，少用)
The issue has been ruminated over extensively. =  这个问题已经被广泛地反复思考了。(较为正式，少用)


疑问句:
Do you often ruminate on your past? = 你经常回顾过去吗？
Does she ruminate before making important decisions? = 她做重要决定之前会反复思考吗？


否定句:
I don't ruminate on trivial matters. = 我不会去思考琐碎的事情。
He didn't ruminate on the implications of his actions. = 他没有仔细考虑他行为的影响。


PHRASE->
ruminate on/upon:  反复思考
ruminate over: 仔细考虑
ruminate deeply: 深思熟虑
ruminate endlessly: 无休止地思考
ruminate silently:  默默地沉思


词源:
From Latin *rumināre "to chew the cud," from rumen "throat, gullet" (source also of English rumen). The sense "to muse, ponder" developed in the 16th century.
