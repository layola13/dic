preceptor:/prɪˈsɛptər/| n. |导师，指导者，教导者 |A preceptor is a teacher, tutor, or instructor, especially one who provides guidance and instruction in a particular field or skill.  A preceptor often has a more personal and mentoring role than a typical teacher. |例句：He served as a preceptor for many young doctors. 他担任许多年轻医生的导师。

例句：The experienced artist acted as a preceptor, guiding the aspiring painters.  这位经验丰富的艺术家担任导师，指导有抱负的画家们。


近义词：mentor, tutor, instructor, guide, teacher

反义词：student, pupil, learner


词性:名词
