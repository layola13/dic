maxim:/ˈmæksɪm/| n. |格言；座右铭；准则 |A maxim is a short, memorable statement expressing a general truth or rule of conduct.  It is often used to provide guidance or advice.  |Example:  "Honesty is the best policy" is a well-known maxim.  这句“诚实为上策”是一句众所周知的格言。|近义词：proverb, saying, motto, adage |反义词：none specifically, but antonyms might be found within the maxim itself (depending on its content).|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's maxim is "quality first". = 这家公司的座右铭是“质量第一”。
His maxim for life is to always be kind. = 他的人生格言是永远要善良。

一般过去时:
Their maxim was "work hard, play hard". = 他们的座右铭是“努力工作，尽情玩乐”。
The old maxim guided their decisions. = 那句古老的格言指导了他们的决定。

现在完成时:
This maxim has guided countless people throughout history. = 这句格言在历史上指导了无数人。
The company's maxim has remained unchanged for decades. = 这家公司的座右铭几十年来一直没有改变。

过去完成时:
They had adopted the maxim before the project started. = 他们在项目开始之前就采用了这条格言。
The maxim had been passed down through generations.  = 这句格言代代相传。


将来时:
Their new maxim will be "innovation and sustainability". = 他们的新座右铭将是“创新和可持续性”。
This maxim will likely influence future generations. = 这句格言可能会影响后代。


虚拟语气:
If only I had followed that maxim earlier! = 早知道我早点遵循那句格言就好了！
I wish I had known this maxim before making that decision. = 我真希望在做那个决定之前就知道这句格言。


被动语态:
The maxim was widely quoted in the media. = 这句格言在媒体上被广泛引用。
The maxim is often misinterpreted. = 这句格言经常被误解。


疑问句:
What is the company's guiding maxim? = 公司的指导格言是什么？
What maxim should we follow in this situation? = 在这种情况下我们应该遵循什么格言？


否定句:
That isn't a maxim I agree with. = 那不是我认同的格言。
He didn't live by that maxim. = 他没有按照那句格言生活。


PHRASE->
a guiding maxim = 指导性格言
a central maxim = 中心格言
a fundamental maxim = 基本原则
a timeless maxim = 隽永的格言
live by a maxim = 奉行格言


词源:
The word "maxim" comes from the Latin word "maxima," the feminine form of "maximus," meaning "greatest" or "largest."  Over time, it evolved to refer to a statement of the greatest importance or truth, hence a maxim.
