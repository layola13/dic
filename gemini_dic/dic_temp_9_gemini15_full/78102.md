geographies:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfiz/ | n. |地理学；地理环境 |The study of the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere, and of human activity as it affects and is affected by these, including the distribution of populations and resources.  |例句：The course covers physical and human geographies. 这门课程涵盖了自然地理和人文地理。

例句：He's fascinated by the geographies of different countries. 他对不同国家的地理环境很着迷。


近义词：topographies, cartography | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
