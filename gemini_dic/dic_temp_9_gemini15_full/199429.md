simulacres:/ˈsɪmjʊleɪkr/| n. |仿真物；虚像；模拟物 | A simulacrum is a copy or imitation of something, often lacking the substance or authenticity of the original.  It can also refer to something that merely represents or simulates reality, often in a deceptive way.  The term often carries a sense of artificiality or superficiality. | The theme park was a simulacrum of a historical town, lacking the genuine feel of the real thing.  | copy, imitation, representation, likeness, facsimile | reality, authenticity, genuine article | 名词
