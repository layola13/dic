inextinguishable: /ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəl/ | adj. | 不可熄灭的；无法扑灭的；永久的 |  Incapable of being extinguished or put out; unquenchable.  It refers to something that cannot be stopped or eliminated, often used to describe fires, emotions, or ideas. |

例句：

The inextinguishable fire raged through the forest.  =  熊熊大火席卷了整个森林。

Her inextinguishable spirit helped her overcome many obstacles. = 她坚韧不拔的精神帮助她克服了许多困难。


近义词：unquenchable, unstoppable, everlasting, permanent

反义词：extinguishable, quenchable, suppressible


词性：形容词
