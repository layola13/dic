energizes:/ˈɛnərˌdʒaɪzɪz/| vt.|使充满活力；使活跃；激励|To give energy to; invigorate; stimulate.|例句：The music energizes the dancers.  音乐使舞者充满活力。|例句：The good news energized the whole team.  好消息激励了整个团队。|近义词：invigorates, stimulates, revitalizes|反义词：depletes, exhausts, drains|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music energizes the dancers. = 音乐使舞者充满活力。
Regular exercise energizes the body. = 定期锻炼使身体充满活力。

一般过去时:
