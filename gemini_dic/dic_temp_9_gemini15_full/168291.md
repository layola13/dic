/proʊməˈrɒkən/ | adj. | 亲摩洛哥的，支持摩洛哥的 |  Pro-Moroccan refers to something or someone that supports or favors Morocco or its people, policies, or interests.  It indicates a positive stance towards Morocco. | Example:  The pro-Moroccan sentiment was strong during the World Cup.  亲摩洛哥的情绪在世界杯期间非常强烈。  | 近义词: pro-Moroccan, Moroccan-supporting | 反义词: anti-Moroccan | 形容词
