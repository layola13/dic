overcompensatingly: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmpənseɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 过度补偿地；矫枉过正地 |  In a manner that excessively compensates for something perceived as a deficiency or weakness.  It implies an overreaction or an attempt to make up for something in an excessive way. | 例句：He overcompensatingly praised her work, clearly uncomfortable with his earlier criticism. 他过分地赞扬了她的工作，显然对他之前的批评感到不安。

He tried to overcompensatingly make up for his lateness with an extravagant gift. 他试图用一份奢华的礼物来过度弥补他的迟到。


近义词：excessively, immoderately, disproportionately, overzealously | 反义词：undercompensatingly, insufficiently, moderately


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have different tense forms like verbs.  Its usage depends on the verb it modifies.  Here are examples demonstrating its use in different sentence structures:


肯定句:
He behaved overcompensatingly to impress his new boss. 他行为过度补偿以讨好他的新老板。
She smiled overcompensatingly, trying to hide her nervousness. 她过度地微笑，试图掩饰她的紧张。


否定句:
He did not act overcompensatingly; his apology was sincere and appropriate. 他并没有过度补偿；他的道歉真诚而恰当。
She didn’t overcompensatingly praise his efforts; she gave honest feedback. 她并没有过分赞扬他的努力；她给了诚实的反馈。


疑问句:
Was he overcompensatingly kind to her?  他对她是过度友善了吗？
Did she react overcompensatingly to his criticism? 她对他的批评反应过度了吗？

词源:

The word is formed from the following elements:

* **over-**: a prefix indicating excess or to a great extent.
* **compensate**:  from Latin *compensare*, "to weigh equally, make amends for".
* **-ing**: present participle suffix, forming an adjective.
* **-ly**: adverbial suffix.

Therefore, "overcompensatingly" literally means "in a manner of excessively compensating."
