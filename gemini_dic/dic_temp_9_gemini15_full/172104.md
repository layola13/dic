purposeful:/ˈpɜːrpəsfəl/ | adj. | 有目的的；故意的；有计划的 | Having or showing a definite purpose; intentional.  It suggests a clear aim and deliberate action. |例句：He made a purposeful stride toward the door. 他朝着门走去，步伐坚定有力。

例句：Her purposeful actions showed her determination.  她的行动果断，显示出她的决心。


近义词：intentional, deliberate, designed, calculated, premeditated | 反义词：accidental, unintentional, haphazard, random, chance


词源:  The word "purposeful" comes from the Old French word "purposer," meaning "to propose" or "intend."  The "-ful" suffix adds the meaning of "full of" or "characterized by."  Therefore, "purposeful" literally means "full of purpose."
