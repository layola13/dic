intrepid:/ˈɪntrepɪd/| adj.|勇敢的，无畏的|fearless and adventurous (often despite danger)|The intrepid explorer journeyed into the uncharted Amazon.  勇敢的探险家深入未经探测的亚马逊地区。|近义词：bold, courageous, valiant, dauntless|反义词：timid, cowardly, fearful
