interreflected:/ˌɪntərɪˈflɛktɪd/ | adj. |相互反射的，相互影响的 |  Describes a situation where two or more things reflect or influence each other reciprocally.  The effect is mutual and cyclical. | Example: The ideas of the two philosophers were interreflected, each shaping and reshaping the other.  相互影响的思想，彼此塑造和重塑对方。 |近义词:mutually influencing, reciprocally affecting | 反义词: independent, unrelated | 词性:形容词
