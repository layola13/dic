marmoset:/ˈmɑːməzɛt/|/ˈmɑːrməzɛt/| n. |狨猴|A small monkey with a long tail, found in Central and South America.  Marmosets are known for their small size and distinctive features, such as their large eyes and tufts of fur.  They are also notable for their social behavior and vocalizations.|Example: The zoo has a new exhibit featuring pygmy marmosets.|近义词：monkey, small monkey|反义词：gorilla, orangutan
