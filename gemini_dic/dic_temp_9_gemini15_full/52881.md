diminute:/ˈdɪmɪnjuːt/| adj.|极小的；微不足道的|extremely small; insignificant.|例句：The dimunitive figure of the child was barely visible in the crowd.  那个孩子的娇小身影在人群中几乎看不见。|例句：His contribution to the project was dimunitive, almost negligible. 他对项目的贡献微不足道，几乎可以忽略不计。|近义词：tiny, minuscule, insignificant, trifling|反义词：huge, enormous, immense, significant|形容词

词源:
