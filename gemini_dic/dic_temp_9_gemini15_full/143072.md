outbragging:/ˌaʊtˈbræɡɪŋ/| v.|吹牛超过；夸耀胜过 |The act of surpassing someone else in boasting or bragging.|例句：He was outbragged by his own son at the family reunion.  他在家族聚会上被自己的儿子吹牛吹过了。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass|反义词：understate, downplay
