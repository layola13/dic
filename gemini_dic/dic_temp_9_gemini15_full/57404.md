dping:/dɪpɪŋ/| n. |蘸，浸，涂抹|The act of dipping something into a liquid or substance.|例句：The dipping of the bread into the soup was satisfying.  把面包蘸进汤里很令人满足。|例句：The artist used a dipping technique to apply the paint.艺术家用蘸染技巧来涂抹颜料。|近义词：immersion, submersion, coating|反义词：lifting, raising


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is dipping his fries into ketchup. = 他正在把薯条蘸番茄酱。
She dips her cookies into milk. = 她把饼干蘸牛奶吃。

一般过去时:
They dipped their toes into the cool water. = 他们把脚趾头浸在凉水中。
He dipped the brush into the paint. = 他把画笔蘸进颜料里。

现在完成时:
I have dipped my hand in the icy water. = 我把手浸在冰冷的水里了。
She has dipped the candle in wax many times. = 她已经多次把蜡烛浸在蜡里了。

过去完成时:
Before painting, he had dipped the brush in thinner. = 在作画之前，他已经把画笔浸在稀释剂里了。
They had dipped the chicken in batter before frying. = 炸鸡之前，他们已经把鸡肉裹上面糊了。


将来时:
We will dip the strawberries in chocolate. = 我们将把草莓蘸上巧克力。
She will dip her paintbrush in water to clean it. = 她将用清水清洗她的画笔。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would dip the vegetables in olive oil. = 如果我是你，我会把蔬菜蘸橄榄油。
I wish I had dipped the cake in chocolate before serving. = 我真希望在端上蛋糕之前把它蘸上巧克力。

被动语态:
The bread was dipped in garlic butter. = 面包蘸了蒜香黄油。
The fabric is being dipped in dye. = 布料正在染色。

疑问句:
Are you dipping your chips in salsa? = 你在把薯片蘸萨尔萨酱吗？
Is she dipping the brush into the paint? = 她正在把画笔蘸进颜料里吗？

否定句:
He did not dip his biscuit in his tea. = 他没有把饼干蘸茶喝。
She doesn't dip her cookies in coffee. = 她不把饼干蘸咖啡吃。


PHRASE->

dip into:  略微涉猎；少量地使用

dip a toe in the water: 试探性地参与
take a dip: 游泳；洗个澡
French dip: 法式牛肉三明治
