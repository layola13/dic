predicatory:/ˈprɛdɪkətɔːri/| adj.|述谓的，断言的| Relating to or of the nature of a predicate; expressing or containing a predicate.  A predicatory adjective is one that functions as the predicate of a sentence, typically linked to the subject by a linking verb. |例句：The predicatory adjective "happy" in the sentence "She is happy" describes the subject's state of being.  这句话中“快乐的”这个述谓形容词描述了主语的状态。|近义词：affirmative, assertive, declarative|反义词：negative, denying, refutative
