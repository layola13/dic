dry:/draɪ/| adj., v.| 干的；干燥的；枯燥的；使干燥|  lacking moisture; uninteresting or dull. |例句：The weather is dry today. 今天天气干燥。  The lecture was incredibly dry.  这次讲座非常枯燥。|近义词：arid, parched, dull, tedious|反义词：wet, moist, damp, interesting, exciting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The desert is dry. = 沙漠很干燥。
This paint dries quickly. = 这颜料干得很快。

一般过去时:
The flowers dried up in the sun. = 花在阳光下枯萎了。
He dried the dishes after dinner. = 他晚饭后擦干了碗碟。

现在完成时:
The clothes have dried. = 衣服已经干了。
She has dried her hair. = 她已经吹干了头发。

过去完成时:
The paint had dried before we could move the furniture. = 我们能移动家具之前，油漆已经干了。
The river had dried up completely by summer. = 到夏天的时候，河水已经完全干涸了。

将来时:
The clothes will dry overnight. = 衣服会在夜里晾干。
I will dry the car after washing it. = 我洗完车后会擦干它。

虚拟语气:
If the weather were dry, we could have a picnic. = 如果天气干燥，我们就可以野餐。
I wish the paint would dry faster. = 我希望油漆能干得快一点。

被动语态:
The clothes are dried in the sun. = 衣服在太阳下晒干。
The dishes were dried by the machine. = 碗碟是用机器擦干的。

疑问句:
Is the laundry dry yet? = 洗的衣服干了吗？
Does this shampoo dry your hair quickly? = 这个洗发水能让你的头发很快干吗？

否定句:
The ground isn't dry enough to plant. = 地面不够干燥，不能种植。
He didn't dry the dishes properly. = 他没有把碗碟擦干净。
