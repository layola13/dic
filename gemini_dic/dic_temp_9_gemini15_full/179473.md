redwood:/ˈrɛdˌwʊd/| n. |红杉，红木|A redwood is a large evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Sequoia, especially Sequoia sempervirens (coast redwood) or Sequoiadendron giganteum (giant sequoia).  Redwoods are known for their immense size, longevity, and reddish-brown wood.|例句：The redwood forests of California are breathtaking. 加州的红杉林令人叹为观止。

例句：We walked among the towering redwoods, feeling dwarfed by their size. 我们走在高耸的红杉树之间，感觉自己渺小无比。


近义词：sequoia (指红杉属植物) |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|词性：名词
