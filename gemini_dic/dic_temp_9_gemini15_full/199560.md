singleness: /ˈsɪŋɡlənəs/ | n. | 单身；单一；唯一性；单纯 | The state of being single, unmarried, or not in a relationship.  It can also refer to the quality of being single, unique, or undivided. |

例句：

Her singleness was a source of both freedom and loneliness.  她的单身既是自由的源泉，也是孤独的根源。

The singleness of purpose was evident in their unwavering dedication.  他们坚定不移的奉献体现了他们目标的唯一性。


近义词：solitude, celibacy, unity, oneness

反义词：marriage, partnership, multiplicity, plurality


词源:

The word "singleness" derives from the Old English word "singol," meaning "single," "alone," or "unmarried."  This word further evolved through Middle English into the modern "single," and the suffix "-ness" was added to create the noun form "singleness," indicating a state or quality.
