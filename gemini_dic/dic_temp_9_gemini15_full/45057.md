cubed:/kjuːbd/ | adj. | 立方的；三次方的 |  Cubed means raised to the power of three; multiplied by itself twice.  It is also used to describe something shaped like a cube. | Example:  The volume of a cube is calculated by cubing the length of one side.  立方体的体积是通过将一边的长度立方来计算的。 The ice cubes were melting quickly. 冰块很快就融化了。 |近义词：cubic | 反义词：None  |  adj.
