concatenating:/ˌkɒŋˈkætɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 连接，串联 | Joining or linking together (things, especially data or computer files).  The process often involves combining multiple items into a single, continuous whole. | Example: The program is concatenating the database records into a single file.  程序正在将数据库记录连接到单个文件中。


The software is concatenating several smaller images to create a panorama.  该软件正在将几个较小的图像连接起来以创建全景图。


近义词：linking, joining, uniting, combining, merging

反义词：separating, disconnecting, disjoining, dividing


词源:  From the Latin word "concatenare," meaning "to chain together," which is composed of "con-" (together) and "catena" (chain).
