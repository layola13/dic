foolscap:/ˈfuːlzˌkæp/| n. |大号纸张，象皮纸|A large size of paper, originally about 13 by 16 inches (33 by 41 cm), now often 13 by 17 inches (33 by 43 cm).  The name is believed to have originated from the fact that the paper was formerly watermarked with a fool's cap.|The artist used foolscap paper for his drawings.|近义词：large paper, writing paper|反义词：small paper
