pinnatiped:/ˈpɪnətɪpɛd/ | n. |鳍足类动物 | A pinniped is a fin-footed mammal, belonging to the order Carnivora.  They are characterized by their flippers, which are modified limbs used for swimming.  Common examples include seals, sea lions, and walruses. | Example:  Seals, sea lions, and walruses are all examples of pinnipeds.  鳍海豹、海狮和海象都是鳍足类的例子。 |近义词：seal, sea lion, walrus (depending on the specific pinniped) |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terrestrial mammals.
