pro-Iraqi:/ˌproʊ ɪˈræki/| adj. |亲伊拉克的，支持伊拉克的 |Supporting or favoring Iraq or its government or people.  The term often carries political connotations and can refer to individuals, groups, or policies. |例句：The pro-Iraqi demonstrations were met with a strong police presence. 亲伊拉克的示威游行受到了大批警力的严密监控。
The pro-Iraqi sentiment in the region was palpable. 该地区对伊拉克的支持情绪是显而易见的。|近义词：pro-Iraq, Iraq-supporting, Iraqi-sympathetic |反义词：anti-Iraqi
