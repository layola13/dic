reprimanded:/ˈrɛprɪmændɪd/| v.|训斥；谴责；申斥|To reprimand someone is to rebuke or scold them formally for something they have done wrong.  It implies a more serious and official rebuke than a simple scolding.|例句：The teacher reprimanded the student for talking in class. 老师因学生在课堂上讲话而训斥了他。|例句：The manager reprimanded the employee for their repeated lateness.  经理因员工反复迟到而训斥了他。|近义词：rebuke, scold, chastise, admonish|反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher reprimands students who misbehave. = 老师训斥那些行为不端的学生。
He often reprimands his employees for their mistakes. = 他经常因为员工的错误而训斥他们。

一般过去时:
My boss reprimanded me for being late. = 我的老板因为我迟到而训斥了我。
The principal reprimanded the students for their disruptive behavior. = 校长因为学生扰乱秩序的行为而训斥了他们。

现在完成时:
She has been reprimanded several times for her poor work. = 她因为工作表现差已经被训斥过好几次了。
The company has reprimanded the manager for his negligence. = 公司因为经理的疏忽而训斥了他。

过去完成时:
He had been reprimanded before for the same offense. = 他之前就因为同样的罪过被训斥过。
The employee had already been reprimanded for similar behavior. = 员工因为类似的行为已经被训斥过了。


将来时:
The manager will reprimand the employee if the problem persists. = 如果问题持续存在，经理将会训斥这位员工。
I know I will be reprimanded if I don't finish the project on time. = 我知道如果我不按时完成项目就会被训斥。


被动语态:
The student was reprimanded for cheating on the exam. = 学生因为考试作弊而被训斥。
He was reprimanded publicly for his misconduct. = 他因为不当行为而被公开训斥。


疑问句:
Was the employee reprimanded for their mistake? = 员工因为他们的错误而被训斥了吗？
Have you ever been reprimanded at work? = 你在工作中有没有被训斥过？


否定句:
The teacher did not reprimand the student this time. = 老师这次没有训斥这个学生。
My boss didn't reprimand me, but he was clearly displeased. = 我的老板没有训斥我，但他显然很不高兴。



PHRASE->
severely reprimanded  严厉训斥
publicly reprimanded 公开谴责
formally reprimanded 正式训斥
officially reprimanded 官方谴责

词源:
The word "reprimand" comes from the Old French word "reprimander," which itself is derived from the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "primander," meaning "to demand," "to order," or "to command."  The sense shifted over time from a simple command to a formal rebuke.
