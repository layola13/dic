enigma's:/ɪˈnɪɡməz/| n.|谜，谜团，难以理解的事物|Something that is mysterious, puzzling, or difficult to understand.  It often refers to a person, thing, or situation whose nature is difficult to understand.|例句：His disappearance remains an enigma.  他的失踪仍然是个谜。|例句：The painting is an enigma to art historians. 这幅画对艺术史学家来说是个谜。|近义词：mystery, puzzle, riddle, conundrum|反义词：clarity, solution, answer
