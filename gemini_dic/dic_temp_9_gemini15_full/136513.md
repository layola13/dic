nonroyalties:/ˌnɒnˈrɔɪəltiz/| n. |非版税；非特许权使用费|Payments or income that are not subject to royalty payments.  These might include salaries, bonuses, or profits from activities not covered by a royalty agreement.|例句：The company's profits this year come from nonroyalties, primarily sales of its own products. 今年公司的利润来自非版税收入，主要是自产产品的销售。  The author received a substantial nonroyalties payment for the film adaptation of their book. 作者因其书籍的电影改编获得了可观的非版税收入。|近义词：non-license fees, non-copyright income|反义词：royalties|名词
