ninnies:/ˈnɪniz/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋 | A ninny is a foolish or silly person.  The word often implies a lack of intelligence or common sense, and can be used in a playful or affectionate way, but is generally considered informal. |例句：Don't be such a ninny! Just try it. 别那么傻！试一下吧。

例句：He acted like a ninny in front of everyone. 他当着所有人的面表现得像个傻瓜。


近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, dunce, twit

反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind, sage


词性: 名词
