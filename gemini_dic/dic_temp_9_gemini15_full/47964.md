deciduous:/dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/| adj.|落叶的；脱落的；短暂的|Falling off, as of leaves of trees; not permanent.|Many deciduous trees lose their leaves in the autumn.  许多落叶树在秋天落叶。

The leaves of deciduous trees change color before they fall. 落叶树的叶子在落下之前会变色。


近义词：shedding, ephemeral, temporary|反义词：evergreen, perennial

词性：形容词
