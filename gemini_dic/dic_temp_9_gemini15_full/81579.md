greaseball:/ˈɡrisˌbɔːl/| n. |油腻的家伙，脏兮兮的人(贬义)|A derogatory term for a person of Italian or other Mediterranean descent, often used to emphasize perceived negative stereotypes of being oily, dirty, or unsophisticated.|例句：He called the waiter a greaseball, which was deeply offensive. 他称服务员为“油腻的家伙”，这非常冒犯人。  The term "greaseball" is considered a slur and should not be used.  “油腻的家伙”这个词被认为是侮辱性词语，不应该使用。|近义词：wop,guido(也具有贬义)|反义词:无|名词
