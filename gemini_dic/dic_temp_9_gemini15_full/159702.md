polybromide:/ˌpɒliˈbroʊmaɪd/ /ˌpɑːliˈbroʊmaɪd/| n. |多溴化物|A polybromide is a chemical compound containing multiple bromine atoms.  These compounds often involve bromine anions (Br⁻) bound to a central cation or molecule.  They can vary significantly in their structure and properties depending on the specific cation or molecule involved. |Example:  Some polybromides are used as flame retardants, but their environmental impact is a concern.  一些多溴化物被用作阻燃剂，但其环境影响令人担忧。|近义词：bromine compound,  polyhalide|反义词：N/A
