postmidnight: /ˌpoʊstˈmɪdnaɪt/ | adj. |午夜后 |  Occurring or happening after midnight.  It describes events, actions, or periods of time that take place after 12:00 AM. |

例句：

The postmidnight phone call startled her. = 午夜后的电话把她吓了一跳。

He felt most creative during the postmidnight hours. = 他觉得在午夜后的时间里最有创造力。


近义词：after midnight, past midnight

反义词：premidnight (before midnight)

词性：形容词
