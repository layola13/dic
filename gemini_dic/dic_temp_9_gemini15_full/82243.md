groundable:/ˈɡraʊndəbəl/ | adj. | 可接地的；可证实的；可解释的；有根据的 | Capable of being grounded or justified; capable of being explained or accounted for.  |例句：His theory is groundable in empirical evidence. 他的理论是有经验事实根据的。

例句：The new design is groundable, meaning it can be easily connected to the earth. 新的设计是可接地的，这意味着它可以很容易地连接到地球。


近义词：justifiable, defensible, explainable, verifiable | 反义词：unjustifiable, indefensible, inexplicable, baseless | 词性：形容词
