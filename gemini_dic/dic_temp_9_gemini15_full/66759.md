exosmose:/ˌɛksoʊˈsmoʊz/| n. |渗出；外渗|The outward passage of a fluid through a semipermeable membrane, as in the passage of water from a cell into a surrounding solution.  This process is driven by a difference in solute concentration or pressure across the membrane.|例句：Exosmose of water from the plant cells resulted in wilting. 植物细胞的水分渗出导致萎蔫。|例句：The researchers observed exosmose of the dye from the dialysis bag. 研究人员观察到染料从透析袋中渗出。|近义词：efflux, exudation|反义词：endosmosis
