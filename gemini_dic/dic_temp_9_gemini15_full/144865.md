over-processes: /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsɛsɪz/ | v. |过度处理，流程冗余 |To subject (something) to excessive or unnecessary processing.  This often implies inefficiency and a waste of resources.  It can refer to data, information, or even tasks. |例句：The company over-processes its paperwork, leading to delays and extra costs. 公司过度处理文书工作，导致延误和额外成本。

The marketing team over-processes the data, making it harder to identify key trends. 市场团队过度处理数据，使得难以识别关键趋势。


近义词：overwork, overcomplicate, overrefine, overanalyze |反义词：streamline, simplify, optimize |动词
