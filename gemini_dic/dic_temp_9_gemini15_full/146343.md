It's a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively) and "fiercely" (meaning intensely or violently).  It describes an action or behavior that is excessively intense or aggressive. | 例句：He reacted overfiercely to the criticism, shouting and storming out of the room. 他对批评反应过度激烈，大喊大叫着冲出了房间。
