earmarking:/ˈɪərmɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 预先拨款；指定用途；预留 | The process of setting aside funds for a specific purpose in a budget.  It often involves directing money to particular projects or programs, sometimes bypassing the normal budgetary process. | The government announced earmarking of funds for infrastructure development. 政府宣布拨款用于基础设施建设。|The committee voted to earmark $1 million for the new library. 委员会投票决定为新图书馆拨款100万。| allocation, appropriation |  general funding, unrestricted funding |  名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government earmarks funds for education annually. = 政府每年都为教育拨款。
They earmark a portion of their profits for charity. = 他们将部分利润捐献给慈善机构。

一般过去时:
Congress earmarked millions of dollars for the project last year. = 国会在去年为该项目拨款数百万美元。
The company earmarked a specific budget for marketing. = 公司为营销预留了一笔专门的预算。

现在完成时:
The city has earmarked several plots of land for affordable housing. = 该城市已预留几块地用于建造经济适用房。
They have earmarked a significant sum for research and development. = 他们已为研发拨出巨款。

过去完成时:
Before the project started, they had earmarked sufficient resources. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经预留了足够的资源。
The committee had earmarked the funds months before the deadline. = 委员会在截止日期前几个月就已预留了资金。

将来时:
The council will earmark funds for the renovation of the park next year. = 理事会将在明年为公园的翻新拨款。
They will earmark a substantial amount for employee training programs. = 他们将为员工培训项目拨出大量资金。


虚拟语气:
If they earmarked more funds, the project would be completed sooner. = 如果他们拨出更多资金，项目就能更快完成。
I wish they had earmarked more money for marketing. = 我希望他们为营销预留更多资金。

被动语态:
Millions of dollars were earmarked for disaster relief. = 数百万美元被指定用于救灾。
The funds were earmarked for specific research purposes. = 这些资金被指定用于具体的科研目的。


疑问句:
Has the government earmarked any funds for this initiative? = 政府是否为这项倡议拨款？
What amount has been earmarked for the project? = 为该项目预留了多少资金？


否定句:
The company did not earmark any funds for advertising this year. = 公司今年没有为广告拨款。
They haven't earmarked enough money for the trip. = 他们为这次旅行预留的钱不够。

earmark funds for = 为…拨款

earmark resources for = 为…分配资源
earmark a budget for = 为…预留预算
earmarked for a specific purpose = 指定用途
