disbodied:/dɪsˈbɒdid/| adj.|脱离肉体的；无形的；无实体的| Separated from the body; disembodied.  The term often implies a spiritual or supernatural context, suggesting a soul or spirit existing independently of a physical form.|例句：The disembodied voice echoed through the hall.  空灵的声音在大厅回荡。  The disembodied spirit appeared before him.  无形的灵魂出现在他面前。|近义词：intangible, incorporeal, spectral, ethereal|反义词：embodied, incarnate, physical
