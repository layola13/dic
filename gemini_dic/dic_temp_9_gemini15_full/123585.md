montaignism: /mɒnˈteɪnɪzəm/ | n. |蒙田主义|The philosophical system or set of beliefs attributed to Michel de Montaigne, characterized by skepticism, relativism, and a focus on personal experience and introspection.  It emphasizes self-knowledge, tolerance, and the limitations of human understanding. | Montaigne's essays exemplify montaignism through their exploration of personal experience and reflections on human nature. 蒙田的散文通过对个人经历和对人性的反思来体现蒙田主义。 | 近义词：humanism, skepticism, relativism | 反义词：dogmatism, absolutism
