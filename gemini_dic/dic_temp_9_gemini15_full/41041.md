copes:/koʊps/| v.|应对，克服；足够；|to deal successfully with a difficult situation|例句：She coped well with the pressure of the job. 她很好地应对了工作压力。|例句：The life raft can cope with six people. 救生艇能容纳六个人。|近义词：manage, handle, deal with|反义词：fail, succumb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He copes with stress effectively. = 他有效地应对压力。
The bridge copes with heavy traffic. = 这座桥能够承受大量的交通流量。


一般过去时:
She coped admirably during the crisis. = 她在危机期间表现得令人钦佩。
The old building coped with the storm surprisingly well. = 这座旧建筑出乎意料地经受住了暴风雨的考验。


现在完成时:
We have coped with many challenges over the years. = 多年来，我们克服了许多挑战。
The company has coped successfully with the economic downturn. = 公司成功地应对了经济衰退。


过去完成时:
They had coped with similar problems before. = 他们以前曾处理过类似的问题。
The city had coped with floods in the past. = 该城市过去曾应对过洪水。


将来时:
We will cope with whatever comes our way. = 我们将应对任何可能发生的事情。
The new system will cope with increased demand. = 新系统将能够应对不断增长的需求。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cope better. = 如果我有更多时间，我会处理得更好。
I wish I could cope with this situation more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地应对这种情况。


被动语态:
The problem was coped with efficiently. = 这个问题得到了有效的解决。
The increased workload can be coped with by hiring more staff. = 通过雇佣更多员工可以应对增加的工作量。


疑问句:
How do you cope with difficult customers? = 你如何应对难缠的顾客？
Can the system cope with a large volume of data? = 系统能够处理大量的资料吗？


否定句:
He couldn't cope with the workload. = 他无法应付工作量。
She didn't cope well under pressure. = 她承受不住压力。
