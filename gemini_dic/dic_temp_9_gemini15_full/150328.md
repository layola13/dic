panhellenic:/ˌpæn.hɛˈlɛnɪk/| adj.|泛希腊的，希腊全境的|Relating to or including all the Greeks or all of Greece.  The term often refers to events, organizations, or movements that involve or unite different parts of the Greek world.|例句：The Panhellenic Games were an important event in ancient Greece. 泛希腊运动会是古希腊的一项重要赛事。

例句：The Panhellenic Union of Students advocates for student rights across Greece. 泛希腊学生联盟倡导希腊各地学生的权利。


近义词：all-Greek, Greece-wide


反义词：local, regional


词性:形容词
