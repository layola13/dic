quota:/ˈkwəʊtə/| n.|配额，限额|A quota is a limited quantity of something that is officially allowed.|The company reached its sales quota for the quarter.  这家公司达到了本季度的销售配额。|The government imposed a quota on the import of textiles. 政府对纺织品进口实行配额限制。|limit, allocation, allowance|surplus, excess|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory has a production quota of 1000 units per day. = 这家工厂每天的生产配额为1000件。
What is the quota for this project? = 这个项目的配额是多少？


一般过去时:
Last year, we exceeded our import quota. = 去年，我们超过了进口配额。
They didn't meet their sales quota for the month. = 他们没有达到当月的销售配额。


现在完成时:
We have already reached our quota for this year. = 我们今年已经达到了配额。
They have not yet met their quota for the quarter. = 他们还没有达到本季度的配额。


过去完成时:
By the end of the month, we had already surpassed our quota. = 到月底，我们已经超过了配额。
They had not met their quota before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前没有达到配额。


将来时:
We will likely exceed our quota next quarter. = 下个季度我们可能会超过配额。
What will the quota be for next year's production? = 明年的生产配额是多少？


虚拟语气:
If we had a higher quota, we could produce more. = 如果我们的配额更高，我们可以生产更多。
I wish we had a larger quota for this project. = 我希望我们这个项目的配额更大些。


被动语态:
The quota was set by the government. = 配额由政府设定。
The quota is being reviewed by the management. = 配额正在由管理层审查。


疑问句:
What is the quota for this product? = 这个产品的配额是多少？
What is the import quota for this year? = 今年的进口配额是多少？


否定句:
We did not reach our sales quota. = 我们没有达到销售配额。
There is no quota for this particular item. = 对于这个特定项目没有配额限制。


PHRASE->
import quota = 进口配额
production quota = 生产配额
export quota = 出口配额
sales quota = 销售配额
fishing quota = 捕捞配额


词源:
The word "quota" comes from the Latin word "quota," meaning "what share," which is the past participle of the verb "quo," meaning "how much."
