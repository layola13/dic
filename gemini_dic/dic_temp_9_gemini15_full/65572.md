evacuate:/ɪˈvækjuˌeɪt/ | vt. | 撤离；疏散；排空 | To remove (people) from a place of danger to a safer place.  To empty (a place or container) completely. | 例句：The residents were evacuated from their homes before the hurricane hit.  飓风来临前，居民们被疏散离开了家园。

The doctor evacuated the pus from the wound. 医生排出了伤口里的脓液。|近义词：remove, clear, empty | 反义词：populate, fill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government evacuates citizens during natural disasters. = 政府在自然灾害期间疏散公民。
They evacuate the building every fire drill. = 他们每次消防演习都疏散大楼。


一般过去时:
The police evacuated the building after the bomb threat. = 警方在收到炸弹威胁后疏散了大楼。
We evacuated our homes due to the wildfire. = 由于野火，我们撤离了家园。


现在完成时:
The authorities have evacuated thousands of people from the flood-affected area. = 当局已将数千名居民从受洪水影响的地区疏散。
They have evacuated the hospital due to a potential biohazard. = 由于潜在的生物危害，他们已经疏散了医院。


过去完成时:
The villagers had evacuated their homes before the tsunami hit. = 海啸来袭前，村民们已经撤离了家园。
The firefighters had already evacuated the building by the time the fire spread. = 大火蔓延时，消防员已经疏散了大楼。


将来时:
The city will evacuate its residents if the volcano erupts. = 如果火山爆发，这座城市将疏散其居民。
We will evacuate the school if there is a threat. = 如果有威胁，我们将疏散学校。


虚拟语气:
If the warning had been issued earlier, more people could have been evacuated. = 如果警告发布得早一些，就可以疏散更多的人。
I wish we had evacuated sooner. = 我希望我们早点撤离。


被动语态:
The village was evacuated overnight. = 村庄在一夜之间被疏散了。
The building is being evacuated now. = 大楼现在正在被疏散。


疑问句:
Will the residents be evacuated? = 居民会被疏散吗？
Have they evacuated the school yet? = 他们已经疏散学校了吗？


否定句:
They did not evacuate the building until it was too late. = 直到太晚他们才疏散大楼。
The government failed to evacuate the vulnerable population in time. = 政府未能及时疏散弱势群体。
