conquistadores:/ˌkɒŋkɪsteɪˈdɔːrɛz/| n. |征服者（尤指16世纪西班牙征服美洲的探险者）|Spanish explorers, especially those who conquered the Americas in the 16th century.  They were driven by a mixture of motives, including the desire for gold, land, and glory, as well as religious zeal to spread Christianity.|The conquistadores brought devastating diseases to the Americas, decimating the indigenous populations.|近义词：conquerors, explorers, adventurers|反义词：conquered, colonized
