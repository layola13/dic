pseudoarcheological:/ˌsuːdoʊˌɑːrkiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. |伪考古学的；假考古的 | Relating to or characteristic of pseudoarcheology, which is the study of the past using methods and approaches that are not scientifically valid or rigorous.  Pseudoarcheology often involves interpretations that are based on speculation, bias, or a lack of evidence. | Example: The documentary presented a pseudoarcheological interpretation of the ancient ruins, ignoring established scientific findings.  伪考古学纪录片对古代遗址作出了解读，忽略了既定的科学发现。|近义词：unproven, unsubstantiated, speculative, fantastical |反义词：scientific, factual, proven, legitimate
