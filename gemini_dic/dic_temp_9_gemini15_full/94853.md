in a bad light: /ɪn ə bæd laɪt/ | adj. phrase | 不好的方面；负面形象；以负面形象 | Presented in an unfavorable or negative manner; showing someone or something in a negative way.  |例句：The media portrayed the politician in a bad light.  媒体以负面形象报道了这位政治家。

例句：The incident cast his actions in a bad light.  这事件使他的行为蒙上了阴影。(使他的行为显得不好)


近义词：negatively, unfavorably, poorly, unflatteringly

反义词：in a good light, favorably, positively


词性：形容词短语 (Adjective Phrase)
