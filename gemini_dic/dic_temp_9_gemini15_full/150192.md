panchayat:/pʌnˈtʃaɪjæt/ | n. | 村委会；村政；（印度的）村社自治机构 | A panchayat is a local council in villages, especially in India and some other parts of South Asia, that acts as a form of self-governance.  It typically deals with local issues and disputes.  |  The village panchayat resolved the land dispute peacefully.  这个村委会和平地解决了土地纠纷。 | council, village council, local government |  central government, national government | 名词
