hyperaesthete:/ˌhaɪpərˈiːsθiːt/| n. |感觉过敏者，感觉异常敏感的人 |A person abnormally sensitive to sensory stimuli.  Hyperaesthetes experience heightened sensations, often finding everyday stimuli overwhelmingly intense.  They might be overly sensitive to light, sound, touch, or taste. | Example:  She's a hyperaesthete; even the faintest sound can be incredibly distressing to her. 她是一个感觉过敏者；即使是最微弱的声音也会让她非常痛苦。 |近义词：oversensitive person, highly sensitive individual |反义词：hyposensitive person, insensitive person |名词
