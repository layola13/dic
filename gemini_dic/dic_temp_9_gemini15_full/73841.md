formly:/ˈfɔːrmli/| adv.|正式地，正规地|In a formal manner; officially or ceremonially.|例句：He was formally charged with theft. 他被正式指控犯有盗窃罪。|近义词：officially, ceremonially, ritually|反义词：informally, casually
