cochampion:/koʊˈtʃæmpiən/| n.|联合冠军，并列冠军|A co-champion is one of two or more people or teams who share a championship title because they finished equal in a competition.|例句：The two teams were declared co-champions after a tie in the final game.  两队在决赛中打成平局后被宣布为联合冠军。

例句：Both John and Mary were co-champions of the chess tournament.  约翰和玛丽都是这次象棋比赛的联合冠军。


近义词：joint champion, shared champion|反义词：sole champion, undisputed champion|词性:名词
