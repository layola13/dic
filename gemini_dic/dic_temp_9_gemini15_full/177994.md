recitals:/rɪˈsaɪtəlz/ | n. |独奏会；朗诵会；叙述；背诵；列举 | A recital is a performance of music or poetry, especially one given by a single performer.  It can also refer to a formal statement of facts or a detailed account. |例句：The pianist gave a breathtaking recital of Chopin's nocturnes.  这位钢琴家演奏了肖邦的夜曲，令人叹为观止。

The contract included a recital of the parties' previous agreements. 合同中载明了双方之前的协议。


近义词：performance, presentation, account, enumeration, statement |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms might be implied depending on context (e.g., silence instead of a recital) |词性:Noun
