ectasis:/ɛkˈteɪsɪs/| n. |扩张，扩张症 |A dilation or expansion of a hollow organ or part.  It often refers to a pathological widening or stretching.  |Example:  Bronchiectasis is a chronic condition characterized by the abnormal widening of the bronchi. 支气管扩张症是一种以支气管异常扩张为特征的慢性疾病。|近义词：dilation, distension, expansion |反义词：constriction, contraction |名词
