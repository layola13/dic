self-dedicated:/ˌsɛlfˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd/| adj. |  自我奉献的，献身于自己的事业的 |  Devoted entirely to one's own work or aims; showing great dedication to one's own goals and pursuits.  |  He was a self-dedicated scholar, spending countless hours in his study. 他是一位自我奉献的学者，在书房里花费了无数的时间。

She is a self-dedicated artist, always striving for perfection in her work. 她是一位自我奉献的艺术家，总是努力追求作品的完美。


近义词：devoted, committed, dedicated, single-minded, assiduous | 反义词：lazy, indolent, apathetic, uncommitted
