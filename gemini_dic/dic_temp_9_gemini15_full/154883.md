pervestigation:/ˌpɜːrvestɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |彻底调查，详尽调查|A thorough and detailed investigation;  a process of examining something closely and systematically.  It implies a more exhaustive and rigorous inquiry than a simple investigation.|例句：The pervestigation into the company's accounting practices revealed widespread fraud. = 对该公司会计行为的彻底调查揭露了普遍存在的欺诈行为。  The police conducted a pervestigation of the crime scene, leaving no stone unturned. = 警方对犯罪现场进行了彻底调查，不放过任何细节。|近义词：investigation, inquiry, scrutiny, examination, inquisition|反义词：superficial examination, cursory glance
