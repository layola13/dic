enlarges:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒɪz/ | vt. |扩大；放大；详述 | To make or become larger; to increase in size or extent; to expand upon or elaborate on something. |例句：The company enlarges its market share. 公司扩大了市场份额。  |例句：The photograph was enlarged to poster size. 照片被放大到海报尺寸。|近义词：expand, magnify, amplify, extend|反义词：reduce, diminish, shrink


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun enlarges as it sets. = 太阳在落下时显得更大。
He enlarges his business every year. = 他每年都扩大他的生意。


一般过去时:
They enlarged the garden last spring. = 他们去年春天扩大了花园。
She enlarged on the details of her trip. = 她详细讲述了她旅行的细节。


现在完成时:
The company has enlarged its operations significantly. = 公司已经大幅扩大了业务。
We have enlarged the picture for better viewing. = 我们已经放大了图片以便更好地观看。


过去完成时:

将来时:

否定句:
