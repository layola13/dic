interreign:/ˈɪntərreɪn/| n. |(君主)空位期；过渡时期；摄政时期 |The period between two reigns, especially when there is no ruler.  The time between the death or deposition of a monarch and the accession of their successor.  It often implies a period of instability or uncertainty. |Example: The country experienced a period of interregnum following the king's death.  这个国家在国王去世后经历了一段空位期。


Example: The interregnum after the revolution was marked by widespread chaos.  革命后的过渡时期充满了混乱。

近义词：interim, gap, interval, hiatus

反义词：reign, rule, governance


词性:名词
