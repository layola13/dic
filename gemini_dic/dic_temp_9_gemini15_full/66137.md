exceptionality:/ˌɛkˌsɛpʃəˈnæləti/ | n. |杰出；异常；特殊性 |The state or quality of being exceptional; the fact of being different from others.  It refers to significant departures from the norm, whether positive (e.g., exceptional talent) or negative (e.g., exceptional difficulty).|例句：His musical talent is a matter of exceptionality.  他的音乐天赋异于常人。


The school provides support services for students with exceptionalities. 这所学校为有特殊需要的学生提供支持服务。|近义词：unusualness, peculiarity, distinctiveness, rarity |反义词：ordinariness, normality, typicality |词性：名词
