schizoids:/ˈskɪtsɔɪdz/| n. |精神分裂症患者；具有精神分裂症特质的人|People with schizoid personality disorder are characterized by a detachment from social relationships and a restricted range of emotional expression.  精神分裂症人格障碍患者的特点是脱离社会关系和情感表达范围有限。|例句：Some studies suggest a correlation between childhood trauma and the development of schizoid personality disorder in adulthood.一些研究表明，儿童时期的创伤与成年后患精神分裂症人格障碍之间存在关联。
例句：He exhibited classic schizoid traits, preferring solitude and avoiding close relationships. 他表现出典型的精神分裂症特征，喜欢独处并避免亲密关系。|近义词：schizophrenic (in some contexts), asocial, withdrawn |反义词：extroverted, sociable, gregarious |名词
