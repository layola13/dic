endochondral:/ˌɛndoʊˈkɑːndrəl/| adj. |软骨内生的；软骨内成骨的 | Relating to or denoting bone formation within cartilage.  Endochondral ossification is the process by which most bones of the skeleton are formed.  |例句：Endochondral ossification is a crucial process in skeletal development.  软骨内成骨是骨骼发育中的一个关键过程。|例句：The long bones of the limbs develop through endochondral ossification.  四肢的长骨通过软骨内成骨发育。|近义词：intracartilaginous|反义词：intramembranous


词源:

* **endo-**:  From Greek *endon*, meaning "within" or "inside."
* **chondr-**: From Greek *chondros*, meaning "cartilage."
* **-al**:  A suffix forming adjectives, meaning "relating to" or "of the nature of."

因此，endochondral字面意思是"在软骨内的"。
