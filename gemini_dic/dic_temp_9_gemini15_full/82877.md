gullying:/ˈɡʌliŋ/| n. |沟壑形成；冲沟侵蚀|The process of forming gullies by erosion.  This often involves the action of water, carving channels into the earth. |例句：The heavy rains caused significant gullying on the hillside.  暴雨导致山坡上出现了严重的沟壑。

例句：Years of unchecked farming led to severe gullying and soil erosion in the region.  多年不受控制的耕作导致该地区严重的沟壑和水土流失。


近义词：erosion, channel cutting, rilling (for smaller channels) |反义词：land reclamation, soil conservation


词性:名词
