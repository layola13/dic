distractedness:/dɪˈstræktɪdnəs/ | n. |注意力不集中；分心；心不在焉 | The state of being distracted; lack of concentration or attention.  It often stems from preoccupation with something else or an inability to focus. |例句：Her distractedness during the meeting was noticeable.  她的会议期间心不在焉很明显。

例句：His distractedness led to a series of careless mistakes.  他的心不在焉导致了一系列的粗心错误。


近义词：inattention, preoccupation, absentmindedness, woolgathering

反义词：focus, concentration, attentiveness


词性: 名词
