overprivileged:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd/| adj. |享有特权的，过分优越的 |Having or showing the advantages associated with excessive privilege.  It implies an unfair advantage or benefit due to birth, wealth, or social status, often to the detriment of others. |例句：He comes from an overprivileged background and has never had to work for anything. 他出身于一个过分优越的家庭，从未为任何事情努力过。

例句：The overprivileged elite often fail to understand the struggles faced by the less fortunate. 过分优越的精英阶层往往无法理解弱势群体所面临的困境。


近义词：privileged, advantaged, favored, entitled


反义词：disadvantaged, underprivileged, deprived
