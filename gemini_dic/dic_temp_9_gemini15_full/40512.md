controllers:/kənˈtroʊlərz/ | n. |控制器，控制装置 |A controller is a device or system that manages, directs, or regulates the operation of another device or system.  For example, a game controller, a flight controller, or a traffic controller. |例句：The pilot used the flight controllers to navigate the plane.  飞行员使用飞行控制器来操纵飞机。|例句：The factory uses computer controllers to monitor the production line. 工厂使用计算机控制器来监控生产线。|近义词：regulator, operator, governor |反义词：uncontrolled system
