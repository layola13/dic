reformatting:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmætɪŋ/ | v. |重新格式化 |The process of changing the structure or layout of something, typically data or text, to a new or different format.  It often involves changing the appearance or organization of information to improve readability, efficiency, or compatibility. | Example:  The software automatically reformats the document to fit the new printer settings.  软件会自动重新格式化文档以适应新的打印机设置。 |近义词：restructuring, rearranging, reformatting|反义词：disorganizing, scrambling
