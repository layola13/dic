prestrengthen:/ˌpriːˈstrɛŋθən/| vt.|预先增强，事先加强|To strengthen something beforehand or in advance.  It implies taking action to make something stronger before it is needed or before a potential problem arises.|例句：The engineers prestrengthened the bridge supports before the hurricane season.  工程师们在飓风季节来临之前预先加固了桥梁支撑结构。|例句：The company prestrengthened its financial position by securing a large loan. 公司通过获得一笔巨额贷款来预先增强其财务状况。|近义词：reinforce, fortify, bolster|反义词：weaken, undermine|动词
