mancala:/mænˈkɑːlə/| n. |马恩卡拉（一种棋类游戏）|A strategy board game played with pits and stones, originating in Africa.  It involves sowing seeds into pits and capturing opponent's seeds.  Variations exist across many African cultures.| Example: My grandfather taught me how to play mancala when I was a child.  我的祖父在我小时候教我玩马恩卡拉。|近义词：oware (a specific type of mancala)|反义词：无
