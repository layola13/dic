indicting: /ɪnˈdaɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 控告；起诉；表明；暗示 | Accusing someone formally of a crime;  suggesting or implying something. |


例句：

The grand jury is indicting the CEO for fraud. = 大陪审团正在以欺诈罪起诉首席执行官。

The evidence indicts the suspect. = 证据表明了嫌疑人的罪行。


近义词： accusing, charging, prosecuting, arraigning, implying, suggesting

反义词： acquitting, exonerating, clearing


词性: 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The prosecutor is indicting the defendant. = 检察官正在起诉被告。

They are indicting him for theft. = 他们正在指控他盗窃。


一般过去时:

The court indicted the man for murder. = 法院以谋杀罪起诉了该男子。

She indicted him for his lies. = 她指责他撒谎。


现在完成时:

The grand jury has indicted the politician on corruption charges. = 大陪审团已就腐败指控起诉该政治家。

They have indicted him for tax evasion. = 他们已就逃税指控起诉他。


过去完成时:

The judge had already indicted the criminal before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，法官已经起诉了罪犯。

The police had indicted the suspect before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，警方已经起诉了嫌疑人。


将来时:

The authorities will indict him next week. = 当局将在下周起诉他。

The committee will be indicting several members for misconduct. = 委员会将起诉数名成员的不当行为。


虚拟语气:

If they had more evidence, they would indict him immediately. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们会立即起诉他。

I wish the court would indict the responsible party. = 我希望法院能起诉责任方。


被动语态:

He was indicted on charges of conspiracy. = 他被指控犯有共谋罪。

The case was indicted yesterday. = 该案昨天被起诉。


疑问句:

Will they indict the mayor? = 他们会起诉市长吗？

Has the suspect been indicted yet? = 嫌疑人已被起诉了吗？


否定句:

The judge did not indict the defendant. = 法官没有起诉被告。

The jury refused to indict him. = 陪审团拒绝起诉他。


PHRASE->

indict for:  以…罪名起诉
indict on:  就…提出起诉
true bill of indictment:  正式起诉书
to indict oneself:  自证其罪
indictment against: 对…的起诉
