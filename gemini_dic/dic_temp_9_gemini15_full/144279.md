outspan:/ˈaʊtspæ̃n/| v.|解开牲口（尤指牛或马）, 让牲口在旷野吃草|To unyoke or unharness a team of oxen or horses, especially to allow them to graze.  |例句：The farmers outspanned their oxen at the end of the day. 农民在一天结束时解开了他们的牛。

例句：After a long journey, the teamsters outspanned their weary horses to rest and graze. 长途跋涉后，车夫们解开了疲惫的马匹让它们休息和吃草。


近义词：unyoke, unharness, turn out to pasture

反义词：inspan


词性:动词
