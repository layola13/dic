puppylike:/ˈpʌpiˌlaɪk/| adj. |像小狗一样的；天真烂漫的；幼稚的|Resembling a puppy; characterized by playful innocence, naiveté, or dependence.  It often implies a charming but somewhat immature quality.|例句：Her puppylike enthusiasm was infectious.  她的天真烂漫的热情极具感染力。

例句：He had a puppylike dependence on his mother. 他像小狗一样依赖他的母亲。


近义词：childlike, naive, innocent, playful, dependent

反义词：mature, sophisticated, worldly, independent


词性:形容词
