extender:/ɪkˈstɛndər/ | n. | 延长器，延伸器，扩充器 | An extender is a device or substance that increases the length, range, or capacity of something.  It can refer to physical objects that add length, like a cable extender, or to more abstract concepts, like a software extender that adds functionality. | Example:  He used a USB extender to connect his printer to the computer. 他用USB延长线将打印机连接到电脑上。
The software extender added new features to the application.  该软件扩展程序为应用程序添加了新功能。|近义词：extension, prolongation, supplement |反义词：reducer, shortener |名词
