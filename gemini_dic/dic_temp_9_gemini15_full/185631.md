reverberated:/rɪˈvɜːrbəreɪtɪd/ | v. |回荡；反响；震动 | to continue in a series of echoes; to be repeated many times | The sound of the explosion reverberated through the mountains. 爆炸声在山谷中回荡。 The news of the scandal reverberated throughout the country. 丑闻的消息在全国引起强烈反响。 | resonate, echo, vibrate | silence, quiet | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sound reverberates through the hall. = 声音在大厅里回荡。
The news reverberates across the internet. = 这则新闻在互联网上传播。

一般过去时:
The thunder reverberated across the valley. = 雷声在山谷中回荡。
His words reverberated in my mind. = 他的话在我脑海中回响。

现在完成时:
The impact of the event has reverberated through society. = 此事件的影响已在社会上产生反响。
The effects of the policy have reverberated for years. = 该政策的影响已经持续多年。

过去完成时:
The implications of the decision had reverberated throughout the organization before the announcement. = 在宣布决定之前，其影响已经在整个组织中产生了反响。
The rumors had reverberated for months before they were confirmed. = 谣言在得到证实之前已经流传了几个月。

将来时:
The consequences of this action will reverberate for generations to come. = 此举的后果将影响后代。
The impact of the technology will reverberate across various industries. = 这项技术的影响将波及各个行业。

虚拟语气:
If the news were to reverberate as widely as expected, it would cause a crisis. = 如果消息像预期的那样广泛传播，将会引发危机。
I wish my voice didn't reverberate so much in this empty room. = 我希望我的声音在这个空房间里不要回响得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The news was reverberated across social media. = 这条新闻在社交媒体上传播。
The message was reverberated throughout the company. = 这条信息在公司内部广泛传播。

疑问句:
Does the sound reverberate well in this room? = 这个房间的音响效果好吗？
Will the impact of this decision reverberate for long? = 这个决定的影响会持续很久吗？

否定句:
The sound did not reverberate as much as expected. = 声音的回响不如预期的大。
The news did not reverberate widely. = 这条新闻没有广泛传播。
