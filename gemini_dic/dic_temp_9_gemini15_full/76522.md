gales:/ɡeɪlz/| n.|大风，强风|A gale is a very strong wind.  It's a type of windstorm, often associated with stormy weather.|例句：The gales howled through the night.  狂风呼啸了一整夜。

例句：We had to postpone our trip due to the strong gales. 由于强风，我们不得不推迟旅行。|近义词：storm, tempest, squall|反义词：calm, breeze, stillness|名词
