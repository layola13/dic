divorced:/dɪˈvɔːrst/| adj. v. |离婚的；使离婚|

英文解析:  Past participle of divorce.  Used as an adjective to describe someone who is no longer married, or as a verb in the past participle form. It implies the legal termination of a marriage.

例句:

They are divorced. = 他们离婚了。

She was divorced last year. = 她去年离婚了。


近义词:separated, unmarried

反义词:married


词性: Adjective, Verb (past participle)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is divorced. = 他离婚了。
They are divorced. = 他们离婚了。


一般过去时:
They divorced last year. = 他们去年离婚了。
My parents divorced when I was young. = 我小时候父母就离婚了。


现在完成时:
She has been divorced for five years. = 她离婚五年了。

过去完成时:
They had been divorced for a decade before he remarried. = 他们离婚十年后，他才再婚。
She had already been divorced when they met. = 他们相遇时，她已经离婚了。


将来时:
They will be divorced soon. = 他们很快就会离婚。
I think they will divorce eventually. = 我认为他们最终会离婚。


虚拟语气:

否定句:
