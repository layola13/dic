oxymoronic:/ˌɒksɪˈmɔːrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 矛盾的；似是而非的 |  Describing a statement, expression, or situation that contains apparently contradictory elements.  It refers to something that is self-contradictory, combining contradictory or incongruous terms. | Example:  "The phrase 'military intelligence' is often cited as an oxymoronic expression."  "军事情报"这个词组常常被认为是矛盾的表达。| 近义词：paradoxical, contradictory, incongruous | 反义词：consistent, harmonious, logical |  词性:形容词
