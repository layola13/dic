megaflora:/ˈmɛɡəˌflɔːrə/| n. |巨型植物群，大型植物区系|A megaflora is a very large and diverse flora, often characterized by its size and abundance of plant life.  It typically refers to a collection of plants from a specific region or time period that is unusually large or significant. |Example: The Amazon rainforest boasts a megaflora of unparalleled richness and diversity. 亚马逊雨林拥有无与伦比的丰富多样的巨型植物群。|近义词:rich flora, abundant vegetation, extensive plant life|反义词:sparse flora, barren landscape|名词
