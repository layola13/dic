fistula:/ˈfɪstʃʊlə/| n.|廔管；瘘；窦道|An abnormal passage between two internal organs or between an internal organ and the body surface.|The surgeon successfully closed the fistula after the operation. 手术后，外科医生成功地关闭了瘘管。|近义词：sinus, tract, channel|反义词：None


USAGE->

A fistula often causes discomfort. 瘘管通常会引起不适。
The fistula drains pus. 瘘管排出脓液。

The doctor diagnosed a fistula. 医生诊断出是瘘管。

The patient has undergone surgery to repair the fistula. 病人已经接受了手术来修复瘘管。
