exceptions:/ɪkˈsɛpʃənz/ | n. |例外，除外；异议 | Instances or things that do not conform to a rule, pattern, or generalization.  |例句：There are exceptions to every rule.  每个规则都有例外。
There are some exceptions to this policy.  这项政策有一些例外。|近义词：exemptions, deviations, anomalies |反义词：rules, norms, standards |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are exceptions to this rule. = 这条规则有例外。
Exceptions are made for special cases. = 特殊情况会破例。

一般过去时:
There were exceptions to the policy last year. = 去年这项政策有例外。
