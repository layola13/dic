nonrelative: /ˌnɒnˈrelətɪv/ | adj. | 非亲属的；无关的 | Not related by blood or marriage.  Something that is not related or connected. |


例句：

He is a nonrelative friend. = 他是一位非亲属的朋友。

The two concepts are nonrelative. = 这两个概念是无关的。


近义词： unrelated, unconnected, extraneous

反义词：related, kin, affiliated


词源:

The word "nonrelative" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "relative."  "Relative" itself comes from the Old French "relatif," ultimately deriving from the Latin "relativus," meaning "related."


用法说明:


这个单词主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示与某人没有血缘关系或婚姻关系，或者指事物之间没有关联。它通常用于正式场合。
