filters:/ˈfɪltərz/| n.|过滤器，滤波器|A device or substance that removes unwanted material from a liquid or gas.|例句：The water filter removes impurities from the water.  水过滤器去除水中的杂质。|例句：The air filter in my car needs replacing. 我的车里的空气过滤器需要更换。|近义词：sieve, strainer, screen|反义词：None specifically
