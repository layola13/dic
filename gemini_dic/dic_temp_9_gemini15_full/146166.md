overestimates:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪmeɪt/| vt.|高估，过高估计|To estimate something to be larger, better, or more important than it really is.|He overestimates his abilities. 他高估了自己的能力。| underestimate|underestimate|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overestimate the market demand. = 他们高估了市场需求。
I often overestimate how much time I have. = 我经常高估自己有多少时间。


一般过去时:
The company overestimated the project costs. = 公司高估了项目成本。
He overestimated his strength and got injured. = 他高估了自己的实力，结果受伤了。


现在完成时:
We have overestimated the difficulty of the task. = 我们高估了这项任务的难度。
She has overestimated her own capabilities on numerous occasions. = 她多次高估了自己的能力。


过去完成时:
They had overestimated the resources needed for the expedition. = 他们已经高估了探险所需的资源。
The investors had overestimated the potential return on investment. = 投资者已经高估了投资的潜在回报。


将来时:
They will overestimate the impact of the new policy. = 他们将高估新政策的影响。
I think they will overestimate the number of attendees. = 我认为他们会高估与会人数。


虚拟语气:
If I had not overestimated my abilities, I wouldn't have failed. = 如果我没有高估自己的能力，我就不会失败了。
I wish I hadn't overestimated the time it would take. = 我真希望我没有高估所需时间。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
