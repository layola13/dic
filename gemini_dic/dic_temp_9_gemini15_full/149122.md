oxidable:/ˈɒksɪdəbl/ /ˈɑːksɪdəbəl/| adj.| 可氧化的；易氧化的 | Capable of being oxidized.  A substance that is oxidable readily combines with oxygen. | Example: Iron is an oxidable metal.  铁是一种易氧化的金属。Many organic compounds are oxidable. 许多有机化合物是易氧化的。|近义词：reactive, susceptible to oxidation |反义词：non-oxidizable, resistant to oxidation
