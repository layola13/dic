deciding:/dɪˈsaɪdɪŋ/| v. |决定；判决；裁决 |The act of making a choice or judgment.  It involves weighing options and coming to a conclusion.|例句：He is deciding whether to accept the job offer. 他正在决定是否接受这份工作。|例句：The judge is deciding the case. 法官正在审理此案。|近义词：determining, resolving, settling |反义词：hesitating, wavering, procrastinating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am deciding what to wear. = 我正在决定穿什么。
She is deciding on her college major. = 她正在决定她的大学专业。


一般过去时:
We decided to go to the beach. = 我们决定去海滩。
He decided against buying the car. = 他决定不买那辆车。


现在完成时:
I have decided to move to a new city. = 我已经决定搬到一个新的城市。
She has decided on a career path. = 她已经决定了职业道路。


过去完成时:
They had decided to leave before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经决定离开了。
I had decided against going, but then changed my mind. = 我本来决定不去，但后来改变了主意。


将来时:
I will decide later. = 我以后再决定。
They will decide on the budget next week. = 他们下周将决定预算。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would decide quickly. = 如果我是你，我会迅速决定。
I wish I had decided to go to the party. = 我真希望我决定去参加派对。


被动语态:
The date of the meeting has been decided. = 会议日期已经确定。
The winner will be decided by a panel of judges. = 获胜者将由评审团决定。


疑问句:
Have you decided yet? = 你决定了吗？
What are you deciding? = 你在决定什么？


否定句:
I haven't decided what to do. = 我还没决定做什么。
They didn't decide to go. = 他们决定不去。


PHRASE->
decide on = 决定…
decide against = 决定不做…
decide to = 决定做…
make up one's mind to decide =下定决心
come to a decision = 做出决定
