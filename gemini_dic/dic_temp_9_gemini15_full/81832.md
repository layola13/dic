greyest: /ˈɡreɪɪst/ | adj. | 最灰的，最暗淡的 |  The superlative form of "grey," indicating the highest degree of grayness.  It describes something that is the most gray or the most lacking in color or brightness. | 例句：The sky was the greyest I had ever seen.  = 我见过的天空是最灰暗的。  The old building, with its greyest walls, seemed to sigh in the wind. = 那座古老的建筑，有着最灰暗的墙壁，似乎在风中叹息。 | 近义词：darkest, dullest, most somber | 反义词：brightest, whitest, most vibrant |  形容词
