misunderstandingly: /ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændɪŋli/ | adv. |由于误解地，因误解而 |  Because of a misunderstanding; in a way that shows misunderstanding. |The instructions were worded misunderstandingly, leading to several errors. 指示的措辞含糊不清，导致了好几个错误。| unclearly, confusingly | clearly, understandably | 副词


USAGE->

This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  It describes how an action is performed.


例句1: He reacted misunderstandingly to the news, thinking it was about him. 他对这个消息反应过激，以为是关于他的。

例句2: She spoke misunderstandingly, and her message was lost. 她说话含糊不清，信息没能传达出去。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly using "misunderstandingly."  However, phrases related to misunderstanding include:

1. by misunderstanding: 通过误解
2. due to a misunderstanding: 由于误解
3. through a misunderstanding: 通过误解
4. because of a misunderstanding: 因为误解
5. owing to a misunderstanding: 由于误解


词源:

The word is derived from the adjective "misunderstanding" + the adverbial suffix "-ly".  "Misunderstanding" itself comes from the verb "to misunderstand," which is formed from the prefix "mis-" (wrongly, badly) and the verb "to understand."
