fluoroscope:/ˈflʊərəˌskoʊp/ | n. |荧光屏；透视仪 | A fluoroscope is a medical imaging device that uses X-rays to obtain real-time moving images of the internal structures of a patient.  It allows doctors to view the movement of internal organs and bones. | Example: The doctor used a fluoroscope to examine the patient's swallowing mechanism.  医生用荧光屏检查病人的吞咽机制。| 近义词：X-ray machine, image intensifier | 反义词：无

词源:
The word "fluoroscope" is a combination of "fluoro-" (referring to fluorescence, the emission of light by a substance after absorbing radiation) and "-scope" (meaning an instrument for viewing).  It reflects the device's use of fluorescent screens to visualize X-ray images.
