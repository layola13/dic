frocked:/frɒkt/| /frɑːkt/ | adj. |穿着长袍的；穿法衣的|Wearing a frock or robe, especially a long robe worn by clergy.  The word often implies a formal or religious context.|例句：The priest, frocked in his finest vestments, delivered a powerful sermon.  穿着最精美法衣的牧师发表了一篇精彩的布道。


The newly frocked nuns took their vows.  新受戒的修女们宣读了誓言。|近义词：robed, vested, gowned|反义词：unfrocked (disrobed), secular
