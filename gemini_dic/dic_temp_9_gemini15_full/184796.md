resurrection:/ˌrezəˈrɛkʃən/| n. |复活；复兴；重生 |The act of rising from the dead; a revival or restoration.  The resurrection of Jesus Christ is a central tenet of Christianity.  The economic resurrection of the town was attributed to the new factory. |近义词：revival, rebirth, regeneration |反义词：death, destruction, demise

例句：

The resurrection of Jesus is celebrated each Easter.  = 耶稣的复活在每个复活节庆祝。

The company's resurrection from bankruptcy was a remarkable feat. = 公司从破产中复活是一项了不起的壮举。
