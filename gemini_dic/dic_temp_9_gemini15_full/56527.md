domicils:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪlz/|/ˈdɑːməˌsaɪlz/| n. |住所；户籍；居住地 |A person's domicil is the place where they have their permanent home.  It is often used in legal contexts to determine jurisdiction or tax residency.  The term emphasizes the place of permanent residence, rather than a temporary address.|例句：Establishing your domicil in a new country can be a complex legal process.  在新的国家建立你的住所可能是一个复杂的法律程序。

例句：For tax purposes, his domicil remained in the UK despite living abroad for several years. 出于税收目的，尽管他多年居住在国外，但他户籍仍然在英国。


近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, home |反义词：nomadic existence, transient state


词性：名词
