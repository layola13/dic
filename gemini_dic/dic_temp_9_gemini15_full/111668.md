lock-down:/ˈlɒkdaʊn/| n. v.|封锁；停工；关闭；限制|The act of securing a place to prevent entry or exit;  a period of enforced confinement or restriction of movement.|例句：The city was under lockdown due to the riot.  城市因为暴乱而处于封锁状态。|例句：The school implemented a lockdown after a threat was received.  学校在收到威胁后实施了封锁。|近义词：confinement, quarantine, shutdown, curfew|反义词：opening, freedom, release|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government locks down the city every night. = 政府每天晚上都封锁这座城市。
We lockdown the building during storms. = 我们在暴风雨期间封锁大楼。

一般过去时:
They locked down the school after the alarm. = 警报响起后，他们封锁了学校。
The police locked down the area. = 警察封锁了该区域。

现在完成时:
The authorities have locked down the entire region. = 当局已经封锁了整个地区。
They have locked down the suspect's house. = 他们已经封锁了嫌疑人的房子。

过去完成时:
They had locked down the perimeter before the bomb squad arrived. = 爆破小组到达之前，他们已经封锁了周边地区。
The city had been locked down for days before the news broke.  新闻传出前，这座城市已经封锁多日了。

将来时:
The mayor will lock down the city if the protests continue. = 如果抗议持续下去，市长将封锁这座城市。
We will lock down the server if there is a security breach. = 如果发生安全漏洞，我们将关闭服务器。

虚拟语气:
If the virus spread, they would lock down the country. = 如果病毒蔓延，他们将封锁全国。
It would be wise to lock down the system if there is any suspicion of a virus. = 如果怀疑有病毒，最好封锁系统。

被动语态:
The city was locked down due to the severe weather. = 由于恶劣天气，城市被封锁了。
The suspect's house is being locked down by the police. = 嫌疑人的房子正在被警察封锁。

疑问句:
Will the city be locked down tonight? = 今晚城市会被封锁吗？
Is the building currently locked down? = 这栋楼现在被封锁了吗？

否定句:
They did not lock down the area quickly enough. = 他们没有及时封锁该区域。
The school was not locked down properly. = 学校没有被妥善封锁。
