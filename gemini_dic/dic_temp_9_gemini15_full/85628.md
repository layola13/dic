hault:/hɔːlt/ | vt. | 突然停止；使停止；制止 | To bring or come to a sudden stop.  | The train halted abruptly. 火车突然停了下来。  The police halted the suspect. 警察拦住了嫌疑犯。 | stop, cease, arrest | continue, proceed | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He halts his car at the red light. = 他在红灯处停车。
The engine halts unexpectedly. = 发动机意外地停了。


一般过去时:
The runner halted to catch his breath. = 跑步者停下来喘口气。
She halted her work to answer the phone. = 她停下工作接电话。


现在完成时:
The project has halted due to funding issues. = 项目因资金问题而暂停。
The government has halted the construction. = 政府已叫停了这项建设。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had halted their advance before the enemy fire. = 士兵们在敌军炮火前停止了前进。
They had halted their discussion when the boss came in. = 老板进来时，他们停止了讨论。


将来时:
The meeting will halt at 5 pm. = 会议将在下午5点结束。
The train will halt at the next station. = 火车将在下一站停车。


虚拟语气:
If he had halted the car earlier, the accident could have been avoided. = 如果他早点停车，事故就可以避免了。
I wish he had halted the project before it was too late. = 我真希望他在太晚之前就停止了那个项目。


被动语态:
The procession was halted by the police. = 游行队伍被警方阻止了。
The construction was halted due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，施工被暂停了。


疑问句:
Will the negotiations halt? = 谈判会中断吗？
Did the game halt because of rain? = 比赛因为下雨而暂停了吗？


否定句:
The car didn't halt at the crossing. = 车没有在十字路口停车。
He didn't halt his criticism despite the protests. = 尽管遭到抗议，他也没有停止批评。
