flatlined:/ˈflætˌlaɪnd/| v.|心跳骤停；停止进展；彻底失败|To stop showing any activity or progress; to become completely flat.|The patient flatlined after the accident. 事故后，病人心跳骤停了。|The project flatlined after the funding was cut.  资金被削减后，项目进展停滞了。| ceased, stagnated, crashed|improved, progressed, succeeded|动词
