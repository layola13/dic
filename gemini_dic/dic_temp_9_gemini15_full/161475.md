potato:/pəˈteɪtoʊ/|n.|土豆，马铃薯|A starchy tuber of the nightshade family, eaten as a vegetable.|例句：I love mashed potatoes. 我喜欢土豆泥。
例句：We planted potatoes in the garden. 我们在花园里种了土豆。|近义词：tuber, spud|反义词：无|
