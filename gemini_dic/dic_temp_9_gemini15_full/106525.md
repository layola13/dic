krills:/krɪlz/| n.|磷虾|Small, shrimp-like crustaceans that live in large swarms in the ocean.  They are a vital part of the marine food web, serving as a primary food source for many larger animals, including whales, seals, and birds. |例句：Krill are a major food source for many marine animals. 磷虾是许多海洋动物的主要食物来源。|例句：The abundance of krill in the Antarctic waters supports a rich ecosystem. 南极水域丰富的磷虾支撑着丰富的生态系统。|近义词：shrimp (though larger than most shrimp), euphausiids|反义词：N/A|名词
