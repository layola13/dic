perturbable:/pərˈtɜːrbəbl/| adj.|易受干扰的；可扰乱的|easily upset or disturbed; capable of being perturbed.  The experiment requires a highly stable, non-perturbable environment to ensure accurate results.|近义词：unsettled, agitated, susceptible|反义词：calm, imperturbable, steady


例句：

The sensitive equipment is highly perturbable by even minor vibrations.  即使是很小的震动，也会严重干扰这种灵敏的设备。

His normally calm demeanor became perturbable after receiving the bad news.  收到坏消息后，他平时平静的举止变得烦躁不安。


词源:

From perturb + -able.  Perturb comes from the Latin *perturbare, from *per- ("completely") + *turbare ("to disturb").


用法说明：

perturbable 主要用作形容词，描述容易被扰乱或干扰的事物或人。它强调的是一种状态或倾向，而不是一次性的事件。


注意：没有语法时态变化，因为它是形容词。
