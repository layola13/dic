overcrediting: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度记入贷方；给予过多信用 | The act of giving someone more credit than they deserve, often financially.  It can also refer to overestimating someone's abilities or contributions. | 例句：The bank overcredited his account, resulting in an unexpected surplus.  银行将其账户记入过多贷方，导致意外盈余。

The accounting department overcredited the project by $10,000. 会计部门将该项目的金额多记了1万美元。


近义词：overestimate, exaggerate, inflate, overvalue | 反义词：undercredit, undervalue, underestimate


词性:动词
