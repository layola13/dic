crinoid:/ˈkrɪnɔɪd/| n. |海百合 |A crinoid is a marine animal that looks like a plant, belonging to the class Crinoidea of the phylum Echinodermata.  They have feathery arms that they use to filter feed.  Many are attached to the seafloor by a stalk, but some are free-swimming. |The fossil crinoids are abundant in the Paleozoic rocks.|近义词：sea lily (though not strictly a synonym, it's the common name for many crinoids)|反义词：N/A|名词
