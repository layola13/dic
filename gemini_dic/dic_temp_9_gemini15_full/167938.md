printest: /ˈprɪntɛst/ | n. | 打印测试；印刷测试 | A printest is a test run of a printing process to check for errors or quality issues before mass production.  It involves printing a small sample to evaluate the color accuracy, sharpness, and overall quality of the print. | 例句：We ran a printest before committing to the large print run to ensure the colors were accurate. 我们在大批量印刷前进行了一次印刷测试，以确保颜色准确。


例句：The printest revealed a slight misalignment in the printing plates. 印刷测试显示印刷版材略微错位。

近义词：proof, test print, trial run
