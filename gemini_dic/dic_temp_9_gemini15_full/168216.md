/ˈproʊˌfaɪlɪŋz/ | n. | 预先备案；预先登记；初步文件 |  The act of recording or registering something in advance, often referring to documents or information submitted before a formal process.  This often implies a preliminary or incomplete record. | 例句：The pro-filings for the new building project were submitted to the city council. = 新建筑项目的预先备案已提交给市议会。

The initial pro-filings contained only basic information about the applicants. = 初始的预先登记只包含申请人的基本信息。


| 近义词：pre-registration, preliminary filings, initial submissions | 反义词：final submission, complete documentation |  名词
