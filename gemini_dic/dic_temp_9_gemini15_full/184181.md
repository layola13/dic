reslating:  /rɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 关联；叙述；认同 | relating refers to the act of connecting things or ideas, telling a story, or feeling a sense of connection or understanding.  | Examples:  The two events are closely relating. 这两个事件密切相关。 He was relating the story of his travels. 他正在讲述他的旅行故事。 |近义词：connecting, associating, narrating, describing | 反义词：disconnecting, separating, ignoring



USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is relating his experience in Africa. 他正在讲述他在非洲的经历。
They are relating the two concepts in their research. 他们在研究中将这两个概念联系起来。


一般过去时:
She related the story to her friends. 她向朋友们讲述了这个故事。
He related his childhood memories to his therapist. 他向治疗师讲述了他童年的记忆。


现在完成时:
I have related all the necessary information. 我已经提供了所有必要的信息。
She has related her feelings to her partner. 她已经向她的伴侣表达了她的感受。


过去完成时:
He had related the incident to the police before they arrived. 在警察到达之前，他已经向警方报告了这起事件。
They had related their experiences to each other before the meeting. 会议前，他们已经互相交流了各自的经验。


将来时:
She will relate her findings at the conference. 她将在会议上报告她的发现。
He will relate the details of the accident to the insurance company. 他将把事故的细节告诉保险公司。


虚拟语气:
If I could relate my feelings better, I would be happier. 如果我能更好地表达我的感受，我会更快乐。
I wish I could relate to his experiences more easily. 我希望我能更容易地理解他的经历。


被动语态:
The incident was related to the media. 这件事被报道给了媒体。
The two ideas are closely related in the report. 这两个想法在报告中密切相关。


疑问句:
How are these two events relating? 这两个事件是如何关联的？
Can you relate what happened yesterday? 你能说说昨天发生的事情吗？


否定句:
He didn't relate the information correctly. 他没有正确地提供信息。
She couldn't relate to the characters in the book. 她无法与书中的人物产生共鸣。


PHRASE->

In relation to: 关于；涉及
Relating factors: 相关因素
Closely relating: 密切相关
Relating experience:  分享经历
