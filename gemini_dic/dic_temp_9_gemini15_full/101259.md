invoked:/ɪnˈvoʊkt/| vt.| 祈求；恳求；援引；调用；使生效 | To invoke something is to call upon it, especially a higher power or some authority.  It can also mean to cite something as support or justification, or to activate or bring something into effect. |例句：The priest invoked the name of God during the ceremony.  牧师在仪式上祈求上帝的名号。

例句：She invoked the law to protect her rights. 她援引法律来保护她的权利。


近义词：evoke, conjure, cite, implement, activate |反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect

一般过去时:
