polycarbonates:/ˌpɒliˈkɑːrbənˌeɪts/| n. |聚碳酸酯|A group of thermoplastic polymers containing carbonate groups in their chemical structure. They are strong, transparent, and resistant to heat and impact.  Polycarbonates are used in a wide range of applications, including safety glasses, lenses, and construction materials.|例句：The safety helmet is made of polycarbonate, making it highly impact-resistant.  安全帽由聚碳酸酯制成，因此具有很高的抗冲击性。|The lenses of my sunglasses are made from a high-quality polycarbonate. 我的太阳镜镜片是由高质量的聚碳酸酯制成的。|近义词：thermoplastic polymers, plastics|反义词：N/A
