readjustments:/ˌriːəˈdʒʌstmənts/ | n. |重新调整；再调整 |The readjustments to the schedule were necessary due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，必须对日程安排进行重新调整。|近义词：realignments, modifications, alterations |反义词：stabilizations, maintenances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes readjustments to its pricing strategy every quarter. = 公司每个季度都会对其定价策略进行调整。
Readjustments are vital for maintaining market competitiveness. = 重新调整对于保持市场竞争力至关重要。


一般过去时:
They made several readjustments to the plan after the initial feedback. = 在收到初步反馈后，他们对计划做了一些调整。
The government made significant readjustments to its economic policies. = 政府对其经济政策进行了重大调整。


现在完成时:
The team has made several readjustments to improve efficiency. = 团队已经进行了多次调整以提高效率。
We have made readjustments to the software based on user feedback. = 我们根据用户反馈对软件进行了调整。


过去完成时:
They had made several readjustments before the project launch. = 在项目启动之前，他们已经进行了一些调整。
The company had made readjustments to its marketing strategy before the crisis. = 在危机之前，公司已经调整了其营销策略。


将来时:
The company will make readjustments to its product line next year. = 公司明年将调整其产品线。
We will make necessary readjustments as the situation develops. = 随着情况的发展，我们将进行必要的调整。


虚拟语气:
