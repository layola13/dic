ladify:/ˈleɪdifaɪ/| vt.|使变得女性化；使变得优雅；使变得淑女化|To make or become more ladylike or refined.  It often implies a superficial or artificial attempt to adopt feminine characteristics.|例句：She tried to ladify her behavior for the interview. 她试图在面试中使自己的行为举止更像个淑女。

例句：He ladified his speech to impress his date. 他为了给约会对象留下好印象而使自己的谈吐变得文雅。


近义词：feminize, refine, polish, grace|反义词：roughen, masculinize, coarsen|词性：动词
