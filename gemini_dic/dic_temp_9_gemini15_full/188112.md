rounds:/raʊndz/ | n. |回合，轮次；巡回；路线；（一）轮；（一）圈；大量；(复数)弹药；|英文解析：A round is a period of time during which something happens, or a single occurrence of something. It can also refer to a route or course taken, or a quantity of something. |例句：We played three rounds of golf. 我们打了三轮高尔夫球。

The police made several rounds of the neighborhood. 警察在附近巡逻了好几圈。


近义词：turns, cycles, series | 反义词：single, one-time
