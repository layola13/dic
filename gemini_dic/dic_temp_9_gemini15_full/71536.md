flaps:/flæps/|/n. v.|拍打声；襟翼；拍动；（鸟类的）翅膀；|a piece of material, usually fabric, that is attached along one edge and hangs loose from the other; a hinged part of an aircraft's wing that can be moved to provide lift or control.|The bird flapped its wings and took flight.  这只鸟拍打着翅膀飞了起来。The plane's flaps were deployed for landing.  飞机的襟翼已放下以准备着陆。|wings, movements |stiff, rigid|名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He flaps his arms when he's excited. = 他兴奋时会拍打胳膊。
The bird flaps its wings regularly. = 鸟儿有规律地拍打着翅膀。


一般过去时:
The flag flapped in the wind. = 旗子在风中飘扬。
She nervously flapped her hands. = 她紧张地拍打着双手。


现在完成时:
The wind has flapped the curtains all day. = 风整天都在拍打窗帘。
The bird has flapped its wings countless times. = 这只鸟已经拍打过无数次翅膀了。


过去完成时:
The kite had flapped wildly before it crashed. = 风筝坠毁前剧烈地飘动过。
The engine had flapped violently before it stopped.  发动机剧烈地拍打后才停止。


将来时:
The sails will flap in the strong wind. = 船帆将在强风中飘动。
The bird will flap its wings to escape. = 鸟儿将拍打翅膀逃脱。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, the flag would flap more. = 如果风再大一些，旗子就会飘得更厉害。
I wish the curtains wouldn't flap so much in the draft. = 我希望窗帘不会在气流中飘动得那么厉害。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Does the flag flap in the breeze? = 旗子在微风中飘动吗？

否定句:
The bird didn't flap its wings. = 鸟没有拍打翅膀。
The fabric doesn't flap in the still air. = 在静止的空气中，织物不会飘动。
