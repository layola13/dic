interorganizational: /ˌɪntərˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 组织间的，跨组织的 | Relating to or involving more than one organization.  It describes activities, relationships, or processes that occur between different organizations. | Example: The project required significant interorganizational cooperation.  这个项目需要大量的组织间合作。 |近义词: cross-organizational, multi-organizational | 反义词: intraorganizational, internal  | 词性: adjective
