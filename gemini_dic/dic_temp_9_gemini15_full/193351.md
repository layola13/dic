segmenting:/ˈsɛɡmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 分割；划分；切分 | The act of dividing something into segments or sections.  This often involves breaking down a larger entity into smaller, more manageable parts for analysis or processing.  It's used across various fields, from linguistics to image processing. | Example:  The software is segmenting the image into different regions based on color.  软件正在根据颜色将图像分割成不同的区域。


The company is segmenting its market to target specific customer groups. 公司正在细分市场以针对特定客户群体。


近义词：dividing, partitioning, splitting, subdividing | 反义词：uniting, combining, merging, integrating | 动词
