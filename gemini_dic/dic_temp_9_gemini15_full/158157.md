plating:/ˈpleɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|电镀；镀层；摆盘|The process of covering a metal object with a thin layer of another metal, usually for protection or decoration; the act of arranging food on plates.|例句：The plating on the jewelry is starting to wear off.  珠宝上的镀层开始磨损了。  The chef carefully considered the plating of the dishes.  厨师仔细考虑了菜肴的摆盘。|近义词：coating, electroplating,  gilding|反义词：unplating (去除镀层)
