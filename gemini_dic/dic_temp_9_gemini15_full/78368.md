gesticulating: /ˈdʒɛstɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 手势表达；比划；做手势 |  The act of making gestures, especially while speaking.  It often conveys emotion or emphasizes a point. |

例句：
He was gesticulating wildly, trying to explain his point.  他挥舞着手臂，试图解释他的观点。

The speaker gesticulated emphatically as he delivered his speech. 演讲者在发表演讲时激动地做着各种手势。


近义词：posturing, gesturing, signaling, motioning

反义词：remaining still, being silent


词源:
From Late Latin *gesticulārī, from Latin gestus ("gesture, posture, action") + -āre ("to do").
