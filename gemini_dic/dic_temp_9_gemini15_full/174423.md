quill:/kwɪl/| n.| 羽毛笔；箭羽；刺；豪猪刺 | A quill is a pen made from a bird's feather, or a sharp spine of a porcupine or similar animal.  | Example: The writer carefully dipped his quill into the ink.  这位作家小心翼翼地将羽毛笔蘸进墨水里。  The porcupine's quills were sharp and dangerous.  豪猪的刺又尖又危险。 |近义词：pen (as a writing instrument), spine |反义词：None specifically, but "blunt" could be considered an antonym in the context of sharpness.
