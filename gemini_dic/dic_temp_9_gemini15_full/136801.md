nonshareholder: /ˈnɑːnʃɛərˌhoʊldər/ | n. | 非股东 | A person or entity that does not own shares in a company.  This term refers to individuals or organizations that do not have an ownership stake in a particular corporation or other type of entity that issues shares.  | Example:  The nonshareholders were invited to the annual meeting as observers.  非股东被邀请作为观察员参加年度会议。  The company's policies are designed to protect both shareholders and nonshareholders. 该公司的政策旨在保护股东和非股东。 | 近义词: outsider, non-owner | 反义词: shareholder, stockholder | 名词
