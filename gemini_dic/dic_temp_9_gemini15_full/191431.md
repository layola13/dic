schiff:/ʃɪf/| n. |席夫碱；席夫试剂|A Schiff base is a compound with a carbon-nitrogen double bond with the nitrogen atom connected to an aryl or alkyl group.  They are formed by a condensation reaction between a primary amine and a carbonyl compound (aldehyde or ketone).  Schiff bases are important intermediates in organic synthesis and have various applications. |Example:  Many Schiff bases exhibit interesting optical and electronic properties, making them useful in materials science.  许多席夫碱表现出有趣的的光学和电子特性，使其在材料科学中得到应用。|近义词：imine (a more general term)|反义词：无|名词
