leap:/liːp/| n., v.|跳跃；飞跃；骤变|A sudden jump or movement; a significant change or advance.|例句：The cat leaped onto the fence. 猫跳上了栅栏。 He made a leap of faith and started his own business. 他凭着信心冒险，自己创业了。|近义词：jump, spring, bound, vault |反义词：crawl, creep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leaps over the hurdle. 他跳过障碍物。
The frog leaps into the pond. 青蛙跳进池塘。

一般过去时:
She leaped for joy. 她高兴地跳了起来。
The horse leaped the fence. 马跳过了栅栏。

现在完成时:
I have leaped over many obstacles in my life. 我一生中克服了许多障碍。
She has leaped to the top of her field. 她已跃居她所在领域的顶峰。

过去完成时:
He had leaped to a conclusion before hearing all the facts. 他在听到所有事实之前就仓促下结论了。
The thief had leaped from the building before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，小偷已经从大楼跳下逃跑了。

将来时:
The athlete will leap high in the competition. 运动员将在比赛中跳得很高。
I will leap at the opportunity. 我将抓住这个机会。

虚拟语气:
If I could leap tall buildings, I would. 如果我能跳过高楼，我会的。
I wish I could leap across the ocean. 我希望我能跳过海洋。

被动语态:
The gap was leaped by the brave athlete. 勇敢的运动员跳过了差距。
The conclusion was leaped to prematurely.  结论是过早得出的。

疑问句:
Can he leap that high? 他能跳那么高吗？
Will she leap into action? 她会立即行动吗？

否定句:
He did not leap to any conclusions. 他没有贸然下结论。
The dog didn't leap the fence. 狗没有跳过栅栏。

take a leap: 冒险一试
