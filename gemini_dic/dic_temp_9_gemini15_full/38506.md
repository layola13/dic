conciliated:/kənˈsɪlieɪtɪd/| v.| 安抚；调解；抚慰 | To appease or pacify, especially by concessions; to reconcile.  To bring into agreement or harmony. |例句：The mediator conciliated the two warring factions. 调解员调解了两个交战的派系。

The company conciliated its employees by offering a bonus. 公司通过提供奖金来安抚员工。


近义词：appease, pacify, reconcile, placate, mollify | 反义词：antagonize, alienate, exacerbate, inflame
