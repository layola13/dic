linger:/ˈlɪŋɡər/| v.|逗留；徘徊；持续；迟迟不消退|to remain or stay longer than necessary or expected; to continue to exist or persist|The aroma of freshly baked bread lingered in the air.  新鲜烘焙面包的香味在空气中久久不散。

He lingered over his coffee, reluctant to leave. 他慢慢地喝着咖啡，不愿离开。


近义词：remain, stay, persist, tarry|反义词：depart, leave, vanish, disappear|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lingers in the garden every evening. 他每天晚上都在花园里逗留。
They linger over their meals. 他们慢慢地享用他们的食物。


一般过去时:
She lingered in the bookstore for hours. 她在书店里逗留了好几个小时。
The smell of smoke lingered long after the fire was out.  即使火已经熄灭很久了，烟味仍然残留。


现在完成时:
The pain has lingered for days. 疼痛已经持续了好几天了。
The sweet scent of roses has lingered in the air. 玫瑰的甜香仍然飘荡在空气中。


过去完成时:
He had lingered too long at the party and missed the last bus. 他在派对上逗留太久，错过了末班车。
The effects of the medicine had lingered for several weeks. 药效持续了好几个星期。


将来时:
I expect the cold weather will linger into next week. 我预计寒冷天气会持续到下周。
She will linger at the beach until sunset. 她会在海滩上待到日落。


虚拟语气:
If he had not lingered, he would have caught the train. 如果他没有逗留，他就能赶上火车了。
I wish the summer would linger a little longer. 我希望夏天能再持续久一点。


被动语态:
The old memories were lingered over by the old man. 老人反复回味着旧日的回忆。
The problem is lingered over by the committee.  委员会迟迟未能解决这个问题。


疑问句:
Does the smell linger? 气味会持续多久吗？
Why do you linger here? 你为什么在这里逗留？


否定句:
The pain didn't linger. 疼痛没有持续很久。
He didn't linger at the party. 他没有在派对上逗留。



PHRASE->

linger over: 慢慢地享用；仔细考虑
linger in:  继续留在...
linger about:  徘徊；闲逛
linger around: 徘徊；闲逛
