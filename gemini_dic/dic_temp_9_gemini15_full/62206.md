encouraged:/ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒd/ | v. | 鼓励，激励 | To give support, confidence, or hope to someone.  |例句：Her teacher encouraged her to apply for the scholarship.她的老师鼓励她申请奖学金。

例句：The positive feedback encouraged him to continue his work.积极的反馈鼓励他继续他的工作。|近义词：inspired, motivated, spurred |反义词：discouraged, disheartened, depressed |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encourages his students to think critically. 他鼓励他的学生批判性思考。
She encourages her team to work collaboratively. 她鼓励她的团队协同工作。


一般过去时:
My parents encouraged me to pursue my dreams. 我的父母鼓励我去追求我的梦想。
The coach encouraged the team after their loss. 教练在他们输球后鼓励了球队。


现在完成时:
She has encouraged many young people to enter science. 她已经鼓励许多年轻人进入科学领域。
He has encouraged me throughout my career. 他在我的职业生涯中一直鼓励我。


过去完成时:
They had encouraged her to apply, but she missed the deadline. 他们曾鼓励她申请，但她错过了截止日期。
I had encouraged him to try again, but he gave up. 我曾鼓励他再试一次，但他放弃了。


将来时:
The government will encourage investment in renewable energy. 政府将鼓励对可再生能源的投资。
I will encourage my child to learn a musical instrument. 我将鼓励我的孩子学习乐器。


虚拟语气:
If she encouraged him more, he might have succeeded. 如果她多鼓励他一些，他可能会成功。
I wish he had encouraged me when I was struggling. 我希望在我挣扎的时候他能鼓励我。


被动语态:
The students were encouraged by their teacher's support. 学生们受到了老师的支持鼓励。
The team was encouraged by the positive results.  团队受到了积极结果的鼓舞。


疑问句:
Does your family encourage your ambitions? 你的家人是否鼓励你的抱负？
What encouraged you to make that decision?是什么鼓励你做出那个决定？


否定句:
They didn't encourage his risky behavior. 他们没有鼓励他的冒险行为。
She didn't encourage him to quit his job. 她没有鼓励他辞职。


PHRASE->
encourage sb to do sth: 鼓励某人做某事
be encouraged by: 被…所鼓励
strongly encouraged: 强烈鼓励
mutually encouraged: 相互鼓励
feel encouraged: 感到受鼓舞
