dlc:/ˌdiːelˈsiː/ | n. | 可下载内容 | Downloadable content; additional content for a video game or other software that can be downloaded separately.  It often includes new levels, characters, weapons, or other features. | Example: The new DLC for the game adds three new playable characters.  这个游戏的新的DLC增加了三个新的可玩角色。|近义词：add-on, expansion, update |反义词：base game
