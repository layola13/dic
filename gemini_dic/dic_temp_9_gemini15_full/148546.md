oversweetening: /ˌoʊvərˈswiːtnɪŋ/ | n. | 过度甜味；甜味过重 | The act of adding too much sugar or sweetener to something, resulting in an excessively sweet taste.  It can refer to food, drinks, or even metaphorical situations. |例句：The cake was ruined by oversweetening; it was far too sugary.  蛋糕因为加糖过多而被毁了；太甜了。

The oversweetening of the political discourse made it difficult to find common ground.  政治话语的过度甜化使得难以找到共同点。|近义词：excess sweetness, excessive sugar, over-sugaring |反义词：undersweetening, lack of sweetness
