emitting:/ɪˈmɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 发射；散发；发出 |  The act of sending out or giving off something, such as light, heat, sound, or gas.  |例句：The sun is emitting light and heat.  太阳正在发射光和热。

The factory was emitting harmful pollutants into the air. 工厂正在向空气中排放有害污染物。|近义词：radiating, releasing, discharging |反义词：absorbing, receiving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is emitting light. = 太阳正在发光。
The car is emitting exhaust fumes. = 汽车正在排放废气。

一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large cloud of ash. = 火山喷发出大量的火山灰。
The speaker emitted a loud sigh. = 演讲者发出一声长叹。

现在完成时:
The factory has emitted tons of pollutants this year. = 今年这家工厂已经排放了数吨污染物。
The device has been emitting a strange noise all day. = 这个装置整天都在发出奇怪的噪音。

过去完成时:
The star had emitted a powerful burst of energy before it collapsed. = 这颗恒星在坍缩之前曾发出过一股强大的能量爆发。
They had emitted a warning signal before the accident. = 事故发生前，他们已经发出警告信号。

将来时:
The rocket will emit a trail of smoke as it ascends. = 火箭上升时会喷出一道烟雾。
The machine will emit a high-pitched sound when it starts. = 机器启动时会发出高亢的声音。

虚拟语气:
If the device were emitting radiation, we would have detected it. = 如果该装置正在发射辐射，我们就会探测到它。
I wish the factory weren't emitting so much pollution. = 我希望工厂不要排放这么多污染物。

被动语态:
Radiation is emitted by the sun. = 辐射是由太阳发射的。
Harmful gases were emitted by the factory. = 有害气体是由工厂排放的。

疑问句:
Is the lamp emitting enough light? = 这盏灯的光线够亮吗？
What is the machine emitting? = 机器正在排放什么？

否定句:
The device is not emitting any signals. = 该装置没有发出任何信号。
The plant does not emit harmful chemicals. = 该工厂不排放有害化学物质。
