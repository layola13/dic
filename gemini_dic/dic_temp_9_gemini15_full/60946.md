eleven-plus:/ɪˈlɛvənˈplʌs/| n.|十一岁以上；十一加考试|The term "eleven-plus" refers to an examination taken by children in the United Kingdom at the age of eleven, which was used to determine their suitability for grammar schools.  The exam is largely obsolete now, but the term remains to signify this type of selective educational system.|Example:  Many children felt immense pressure from the eleven-plus exam.  许多孩子都感到十一加考试带来的巨大压力。  The eleven-plus system has been largely phased out. 十一加制度已被逐步淘汰。|近义词：eleven plus examination, selective examination|反义词：comprehensive education (a system without selective exams)|名词
