The term is less common in modern usage, often replaced by "person with rickets."|Example: The study examined the prevalence of rachitome in the impoverished community.  这项研究调查了贫困社区中佝偻病患者的患病率。|近义词:person with rickets, child with rickets|反义词:None specifically, but implies the opposite of someone with healthy bones.|名词
