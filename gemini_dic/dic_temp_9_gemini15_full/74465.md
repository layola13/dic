frankensteinian:/ˌfræŋkənˈstaɪniən/| adj.|弗兰肯斯坦式的；骇人的；拼凑的；不自然的|  relating to or suggestive of Frankenstein's monster;  used to describe something that is monstrous or unnatural, especially something that is made up of different parts or has been created in a way that is considered unnatural or disturbing.  It often implies a sense of horror or unease. | The scientist's frankensteinian creation was a horrifying spectacle.  这位科学家的弗兰肯斯坦式的创造是一个可怕的景象。| monstrous, unnatural, grotesque, macabre | natural, beautiful, harmonious, benign |
