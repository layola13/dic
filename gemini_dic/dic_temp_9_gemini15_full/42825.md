courgettes:/kʊrˈʒɛt/| n. |西葫芦|A courgette is a type of summer squash, typically dark green, with a mild flavor.  It's popular in many cuisines and is often used in stir-fries, gratins, and other dishes. | Example: I added some courgettes to the pasta sauce. 我在意大利面酱里加了一些西葫芦。  |近义词：zucchini, summer squash |反义词：无
