glamoured:/ˈɡlæmərd/| v.|使着迷；使魅力四射；使显得迷人 |To make someone or something appear glamorous or charming.  It often implies a magical or enchanting effect.|例句：The city lights glamoured the night sky. 城市的灯光使夜空显得迷人。

The magician glamoured the audience with his amazing tricks. 魔术师以他令人惊叹的魔术迷住了观众。|近义词：enchanted, charmed, captivated, bewitched|反义词：disenchanted, repulsed
