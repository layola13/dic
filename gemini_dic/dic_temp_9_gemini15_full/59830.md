ectocyst: /ˈɛktoʊsɪst/ | n. | 外膜，外被 | The outer membrane or covering of a cell or organism, especially a cyst.  It's primarily used in biological contexts to describe the outer layer of a cyst or similar structure.  | Example: The ectocyst of the parasite provided protection from the host's immune system.  外寄生虫的外膜保护它免受宿主的免疫系统攻击。 | 近义词: outer membrane,  covering,  exoskeleton (in some contexts) | 反义词: endocyst (inner membrane)
