disinflame:/dɪsɪnˈfleɪm/| vt.|消炎，减轻炎症|To reduce inflammation; to lessen the inflammation of (a part of the body).|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to disinflame the injured area.医生开了药来消炎受伤的部位。


例句：The cream helped to disinflame the irritated skin.  这种药膏帮助消除了皮肤的炎症。


近义词：reduce inflammation, alleviate inflammation, anti-inflammatory


反义词：inflame, aggravate inflammation


词性：动词
