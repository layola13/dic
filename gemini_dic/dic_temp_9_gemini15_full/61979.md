enabling:/ɪˈneɪblɪŋ/| adj. |使能够的；使可能的|Making something possible; giving someone the power or means to do something.|The enabling legislation was passed last year.  这项授权立法于去年通过。|近义词：facilitating, empowering, authorizing|反义词：disabling, hindering, preventing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Enabling technologies are transforming industries. = 使能技术正在改变产业。
This software enables efficient data processing. = 此软件支持高效的数据处理。


一般过去时:
The new policy enabled greater participation. = 新政策促进了更大的参与。
The funding enabled the project to proceed. = 这笔资金使项目得以继续进行。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have enabled unprecedented connectivity. = 技术进步带来了前所未有的互联互通。
This innovation has enabled significant improvements in efficiency. = 这一创新带来了效率的显著提高。


过去完成时:
Previous research had enabled a better understanding of the problem. = 先前的研究使人们对这个问题有了更好的理解。
The earlier investment had enabled the company's expansion. = 早期投资使公司得以扩张。


将来时:
Future developments will enable even more sophisticated applications. = 未来的发展将使更复杂的应用程序成为可能。
This technology will enable faster communication speeds. = 这项技术将使通信速度更快。


虚拟语气:
If we had the right tools, we could enable faster progress. = 如果我们有合适的工具，就能加快进度。
It is vital that we enable access for everyone. = 我们必须确保人人都有机会使用。


被动语态:
The project was enabled by a generous grant. = 该项目得到了慷慨的资助。
Access to information is enabled by the internet. = 因特网使人们能够获取信息。


疑问句:
What factors enabled their success? = 哪些因素促成了他们的成功？
Does this software enable remote access? = 这个软件支持远程访问吗？


否定句:
The lack of resources disabled the project. = 资源匮乏导致项目失败。
The system did not enable the desired functionality. = 系统没有实现所需的功能。
