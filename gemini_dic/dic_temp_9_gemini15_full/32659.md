chits:/tʃɪts/| n.|小票；借条；便条；小纸片|Small pieces of paper used as tokens or vouchers, often for small amounts of money or as reminders.  They can also be informal notes or messages.|例句：He gave me a chit for a free drink. 他给了我一张免费饮料的小票。

例句：She left a chit on the table explaining her absence. 她在桌上留了一张便条解释她缺席的原因。


近义词：voucher, coupon, note, slip, memo|反义词：None specifically|词性：名词
