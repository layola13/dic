grind-out:/ɡraɪnd aʊt/ | vt. | 艰难地完成；勉强完成；辛辛苦苦地做；磨损；压榨出 | To produce something with great effort and difficulty, often implying a tedious or unpleasant task.  |例句：He ground out another article before the deadline. 他在截止日期前艰难地完成了另一篇文章。

例句：She ground out a living working long hours at a low-paying job. 她靠一份低薪、长时间工作的苦差事勉强维持生计。


近义词：produce, churn out, force out,  laboriously create,  eke out


反义词：easily produce, effortlessly create


词性：动词
