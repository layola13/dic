commences:/kəˈmɛnsɪz/| v. |开始，着手 |To begin or start something.  |The meeting commences at 2 p.m.  会议在下午两点开始。|The concert commenced with a fanfare. 音乐会以一阵号声开始。|begin, start, initiate |end, finish, conclude |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program commences at 7 pm. = 节目在晚上7点开始。
Work commences at 8 am every day.=每天早上8点开始工作。


一般过去时:
The meeting commenced yesterday. = 会议昨天开始了。
The ceremony commenced promptly at noon. = 典礼准时在中午开始。


现在完成时:
The project has commenced. = 项目已经开始了。
The construction has commenced already. = 建筑工程已经开始了。


过去完成时:
The play had commenced before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，戏剧已经开始了。
The rain had commenced before the game began. =  比赛开始前雨就已经下了。


将来时:
The concert will commence at 8 pm. = 音乐会将在晚上8点开始。
The trial will commence next week. = 审判将在下周开始。


虚拟语气:
If the rain commenced, we would have to postpone the picnic. = 如果下雨了，我们就得推迟野餐。
I wish the meeting had commenced on time. = 我希望会议准时开始。


被动语态:
The meeting was commenced by the chairman. = 会议由主席开始。
The project was commenced last month. = 项目是上个月开始的。


疑问句:
When does the class commence? = 课程什么时候开始？
Does the performance commence at 7:30? = 演出7:30开始吗？


否定句:
The game did not commence until 3 p.m. =  比赛直到下午3点才开始。
The meeting did not commence on time. = 会议没有准时开始。
