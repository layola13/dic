hit-and-run:/ˌhɪt ænd ˈrʌn/| n. adj.| 肇事逃逸；打带跑； | A hit-and-run accident is a traffic accident in which a driver hits a person or another vehicle and then leaves the scene without stopping to provide help or information.  It is a crime in most jurisdictions.  As an adjective, it describes something done quickly and without regard for consequences. |例句：The police are investigating a hit-and-run accident that occurred last night. 警方正在调查昨晚发生的一起肇事逃逸事故。

The suspect was involved in a hit-and-run incident. 嫌疑人卷入了一起肇事逃逸事件。


近义词：肇事逃逸；逃逸；逃离现场 |反义词：承担责任；留下帮助；自首
