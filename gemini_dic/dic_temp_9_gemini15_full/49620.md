demicannon:/ˈdɛmɪˌkænən/| n. |轻型火炮，轻型加农炮|A demicannon was a type of small cannon used in the 15th and 16th centuries.  It was smaller and lighter than a full-sized cannon, making it more portable and easier to maneuver.  They were often used on ships and in siege warfare.|Example: The pirates used their demicannons to bombard the coastal fort.  海盗们用他们的轻型火炮轰击沿海堡垒。 The smaller demicannon proved more effective in the narrow streets.  较小的轻型火炮在狭窄的街道上证明更有效。|近义词：small cannon, culverin (depending on size)|反义词：heavy cannon, bombard
