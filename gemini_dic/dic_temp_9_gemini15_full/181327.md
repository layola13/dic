reinterviewed:/ˌriːˈɪntərvjuːd/| v. |再次面试；重新采访|The candidate was reinterviewed after the initial interview raised some concerns.  候选人在初步面试引起一些担忧后再次接受了面试。|近义词：interviewed again, questioned again|反义词：initially interviewed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinterview candidates who are particularly impressive. = 他们会再次面试那些特别令人印象深刻的候选人。
The journalist reinterviews key witnesses to get more information. = 记者再次采访关键证人以获取更多信息。


一般过去时:
The police reinterviewed the suspect after finding new evidence. = 警方在发现新的证据后再次讯问了嫌疑人。
She was reinterviewed for the promotion after her initial interview was deemed inconclusive. = 在她最初的面试被认为没有定论之后，她再次接受了晋升面试。


现在完成时:
The company has reinterviewed several applicants for the position. = 公司已经为该职位再次面试了几名申请人。
He has reinterviewed all the witnesses in the case. = 他已经再次采访了此案中的所有证人。


过去完成时:
Before the final decision, the committee had reinterviewed the top three candidates. = 在做出最终决定之前，委员会已经再次面试了前三名候选人。
They had reinterviewed the witness several times before they obtained a consistent statement. = 在他们获得一致的证词之前，他们已经多次再次采访了证人。


将来时:
The manager will reinterview the staff next week. = 经理下周将再次面试员工。
They will reinterview the witnesses before the trial. = 他们在审判前将再次采访证人。


虚拟语气:
If they had reinterviewed the applicant, they might have hired a better candidate. = 如果他们再次面试了申请人，他们可能会聘用一个更好的候选人。
I wish they had reinterviewed him; it might have clarified things. = 我希望他们再次采访了他；这或许能澄清一些事情。


被动语态:
The witness was reinterviewed by the detective. = 证人被侦探再次采访。
Several applicants were reinterviewed for the open position. = 几个申请人再次接受了空缺职位的面试。


疑问句:
Was the suspect reinterviewed by the police? = 嫌疑人被警方再次讯问了吗？
Will the candidates be reinterviewed before the final decision? = 在最终决定之前，候选人会被再次面试吗？


否定句:
The candidate was not reinterviewed. = 候选人没有再次接受面试。
They did not reinterview the witness because their initial statement was considered sufficient. = 他们没有再次采访证人，因为他们的初步陈述被认为足够充分。
