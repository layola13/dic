reembellishing: /ˌriːɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 再装饰；重新装饰 |  The act of embellishing something again; adding further ornamentation or decoration.  |例句：She spent the weekend reembellishing her already ornate living room. 她整个周末都在重新装饰她那已经很华丽的客厅。


例句：The artist was reembellishing the painting, adding more intricate details.  艺术家正在重新装饰这幅画，添加更多复杂的细节。

近义词：redecorating, refurnishing, adorning, enhancing

反义词：simplifying, stripping, bareing
