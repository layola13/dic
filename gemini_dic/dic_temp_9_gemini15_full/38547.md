concluding:/kənˈkluːdɪŋ/| adj. | 总结的；结论性的 | Serving to conclude or bring to an end; forming a conclusion.  |例句：The concluding chapter summarized the main points.  总结性章节概括了要点。

例句：His concluding remarks were brief and to the point. 他最后的讲话简短而切题。


近义词：final, last, ultimate, terminal | 反义词：initial, opening, beginning | 词性：形容词
