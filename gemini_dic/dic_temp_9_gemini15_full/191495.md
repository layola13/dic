schlockest:/ˈʃlɒkɪst/| adj.|最劣质的，最俗气的 |  The superlative form of "schlocky," meaning of the lowest quality; trashy; kitsch. It suggests something extremely cheap, gaudy, and lacking in taste or artistic merit. |例句：That movie was the schlockiest thing I've ever seen. 那部电影是我看过的最烂的电影。 The schlockiest souvenirs were displayed prominently in the gift shop. 礼品店里最劣质的纪念品被摆放在显眼的位置。|近义词：cheesiest, tackiest, crappiest,  most awful |反义词：best, finest, most exquisite, superb
