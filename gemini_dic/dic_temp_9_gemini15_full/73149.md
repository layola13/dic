forayer:/fɔːˈreɪər/| n.| 初次尝试者；先驱；探险者 | A person who makes a foray into a new field or activity.  A foray is a sudden attack or raid, often into enemy territory.  The word "forayer" emphasizes the exploratory and pioneering aspect of this activity, suggesting a venture into uncharted territory. | Example:  She was a forayer into the world of independent filmmaking, bravely tackling challenging projects. 她是一位独立电影界的先驱，勇敢地承担具有挑战性的项目。|近义词：pioneer, innovator, explorer, trailblazer |反义词：follower, imitator
