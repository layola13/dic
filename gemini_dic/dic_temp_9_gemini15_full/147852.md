overran:/ˌoʊvərˈræn/| vt.|超过；溢出；侵略；蔓延|To extend beyond the limits of; exceed.  To spread or grow rapidly and uncontrolled.|例句：The weeds overran the garden.  杂草蔓延到整个花园。 The army overran the enemy's territory.  军队占领了敌人的领土。|近义词：invaded, flooded, overwhelmed, surpassed|反义词：underran, retreated, withdrew|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water overruns the banks of the river. = 河水漫过河岸。
The project overruns its budget. = 项目预算超支。


一般过去时:
The flood overran the village. = 洪水淹没了村庄。
The rebels overran the city. = 叛军占领了这座城市。


现在完成时:
The weeds have overran the path. = 杂草已经蔓延到小路上。
The rumors have overran the internet. = 谣言已经遍布互联网。


过去完成时:
The army had overran the country before the peace treaty was signed. = 和平条约签署之前，军队已经占领了该国。
The fire had overran the forest before the firefighters arrived. = 消防员到达之前，大火已经蔓延到了森林里。


将来时:
The river will overrun its banks if it rains heavily. = 如果下大雨，河水将会漫过河岸。
The project will overrun its deadline if we don't work faster. = 如果我们不加快进度，项目将无法按时完成。


虚拟语气:
If the army had not overran the city, many lives would have been saved. = 如果军队没有占领城市，许多生命就能得救。
I wish the weeds hadn't overran my garden. = 我真希望杂草没有蔓延到我的花园里。


被动语态:
The village was overran by the flood. = 村庄被洪水淹没了。
The country was overran by the enemy forces. = 该国被敌军占领了。


疑问句:
Did the water overrun the dam? = 水是否漫过了水坝？
Has the virus overrun the hospital? = 病毒是否已经蔓延到医院？


否定句:
The army did not overrun the enemy's defenses. = 军队没有突破敌人的防线。
The project did not overrun its budget. = 项目没有超支。


PHRASE->
overrun with = 充满...；被...淹没
overrun by = 被...淹没；被...占领
overrun schedule = 超出进度
overrun budget = 超出预算
overrun capacity = 超出容量
