proudest:/praʊdəst/| adj.| 最骄傲的，最自豪的 | Most proud; feeling or showing the greatest pride.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "proud".|例句：She was proudest of her children's achievements. 她最引以为豪的是她孩子的成就。

例句：He felt proudest when he received the award for his lifetime's work. 他在获得终身成就奖时感到最自豪。


近义词：most self-satisfied, most vainglorious, most self-important
反义词：most ashamed, most humble, most modest

词性：形容词
