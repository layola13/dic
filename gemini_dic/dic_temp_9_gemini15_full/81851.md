griddlecake:/ˈɡrɪdlkeɪk/| n. |煎饼|A griddlecake is a type of pancake, typically thicker and flatter than a crêpe, cooked on a griddle or flat frying pan.  Griddlecakes are often leavened with baking powder or baking soda and can be made with various ingredients, such as buttermilk, flour, eggs, and sugar. |Example:  I made a stack of delicious griddlecakes for breakfast.  我做了好几张美味的煎饼当早餐。

|近义词: pancake, hotcake|反义词:|None|词性:名词
