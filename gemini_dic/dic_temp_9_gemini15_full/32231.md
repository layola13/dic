chicka:/ˈtʃɪkə/| n. |小鸡；年轻女子；（非正式）女孩|A chicka is a young chicken or a young woman, often used informally.  The term carries a slightly playful or even flirtatious connotation depending on context. |例句：The chicka ran around the farmyard.  那只小鸡在农场院子里跑来跑去。


例句：She's a real chicka, always up for a good time. 她是个真正的辣妹，总是乐于享受美好时光。


近义词：chick, hen (for the bird); girl, lass, dame (for the woman)  |反义词：old woman (for the woman); rooster (for the bird)|名词
