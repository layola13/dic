equipollent:/ɪˈkwɪpələnt/| adj.|等效的；同值的；同等的|Equivalent in force, value, or meaning.  Something equipollent has the same effect or power as something else.|The two arguments are equipollent; they both lead to the same conclusion. 这两个论点是等效的；它们都得出相同的结论。|近义词：equivalent, equal, commensurate|反义词：unequal, dissimilar, different
