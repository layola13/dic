congregations:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənz/|/ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n. |会众；聚集；集合 | A congregation is a group of people who have gathered together, especially for religious worship.  It can also refer to the act of gathering together. | Example: The church congregation sang hymns during the service. 教堂会众在仪式上唱赞美诗。 The congregation of birds was a spectacular sight.  鸟群聚集的景象十分壮观。|近义词：assembly, gathering, assemblage |反义词：dispersion, scattering |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The church congregation sings hymns every Sunday. = 教堂会众每周日都唱赞美诗。
A large congregation gathers for the annual festival. = 许多人聚集参加一年一度的节日。


一般过去时:
The congregation listened attentively to the sermon. = 会众专心致志地听讲道。
A small congregation gathered for the quiet prayer meeting. = 少数会众聚集参加安静的祈祷会。


现在完成时:
The congregation has grown significantly in recent years. = 近年来，会众人数显著增长。
The choir has prepared beautifully for the congregation's arrival. = 合唱团为迎接会众做了精美的准备。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
