refashion:/ˌriːˈfæʃən/| vt.|改造，重新设计；改变…的风格|To make or do something again in a different way; to change the appearance or style of something.|例句：She refashioned the old dress into a stylish skirt. 她把旧衣服改成了时髦的裙子。

例句：The designer refashioned the classic handbag with modern materials.  设计师用现代材料重新设计了这款经典手提包。


近义词：remodel, redesign, remake, restyle|反义词：destroy, ruin|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She refashions old clothes into new designs. = 她把旧衣服改造成新的设计。
They refashion their marketing strategy every year. = 他们每年都会调整他们的营销策略。


一般过去时:
He refashioned the old chair with new upholstery. = 他用新的软垫重新装饰了旧椅子。
The company refashioned its image after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，公司重塑了形象。


现在完成时:
She has refashioned several vintage dresses this year. = 今年她已经改造了几件复古连衣裙。
They have refashioned their business model to adapt to the market changes. = 他们已经改造了他们的商业模式以适应市场变化。


过去完成时:
She had refashioned the entire room before the party. = 聚会前她已经重新装修了整个房间。
The artist had refashioned his painting several times before he was satisfied. = 艺术家在满意之前已经修改了他的画作好几次。


将来时:
She will refashion the old coat into a jacket. = 她会把旧外套改成夹克。
The architect will refashion the building's facade to improve its appearance. = 建筑师将改造建筑物的立面以改善其外观。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would refashion my website. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新设计我的网站。
I wish I could refashion my past mistakes. = 我希望我能纠正我过去的错误。


被动语态:
The old building is being refashioned into a modern hotel. = 这座旧建筑正在被改造成一家现代化的酒店。
The outdated curriculum will be refashioned to meet the needs of students. = 过时的课程将被改编以满足学生的需要。


疑问句:
Will you refashion the broken vase? = 你会修理这个破花瓶吗？
How did they refashion the old furniture? = 他们是怎么改造旧家具的？


否定句:
I cannot refashion this broken chair. = 我无法修理这把坏椅子。
She did not refashion the dress as she originally planned. = 她没有按照最初的计划修改这件衣服。
