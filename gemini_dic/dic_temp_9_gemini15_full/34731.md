clickstream:/ˈklɪkˌstriːm/| n. | 点击流 | A record of a user's clicks on a website or app, showing the sequence of pages or items viewed.  It provides valuable data for understanding user behavior and improving website design. | Example: Analyzing the clickstream data revealed that users were abandoning the checkout process at the payment page.  分析点击流数据显示用户在支付页面放弃了结账流程。|近义词：user path, navigation trail |反义词：无
