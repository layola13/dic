regainment:/rɪˈɡeɪnmənt/| n. |恢复；重新获得；复得|The regainment of his health was a slow process.  他的健康恢复是一个缓慢的过程。|近义词：recovery, restoration, retrieval|反义词：loss, deprivation


例句：

After a long illness, the regainment of his strength was a slow but steady process.  一场大病之后，他力量的恢复是一个缓慢而稳定的过程。

The regainment of control over the company's finances was a major victory.  重新掌控公司财务是一场重要的胜利。
