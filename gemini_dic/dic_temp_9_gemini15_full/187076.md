riverhead:/ˈrɪvərhed/| n. |河口；河的源头；河名|A riverhead is the place where a river meets the sea or a larger body of water, or the source of a river.  It can also refer to a specific place name.|Example: The town of Riverhead is located near the mouth of the Peconic River.  河口镇位于佩科尼克河河口附近。|近义词：river mouth, source, estuary|反义词：river's end (in the sense of the end of a river's journey)|名词
