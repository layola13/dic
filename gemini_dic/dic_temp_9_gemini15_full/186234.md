rhinoceros:/raɪˈnɒsərəs/| n. |犀牛|A large, thick-skinned, herbivorous mammal with one or two horns on its snout.  Rhinoceroses are known for their powerful build and are found in Africa and Asia.|例句：The rhinoceros charged at the jeep. 这只犀牛向吉普车冲了过去。|例句：Poachers are threatening the survival of the rhinoceros. 偷猎者正在威胁犀牛的生存。|近义词：rhino|反义词：无
