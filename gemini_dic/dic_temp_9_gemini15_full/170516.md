psalmody:/ˈsɑːlmədi/| n. |圣诗歌咏；赞美诗歌颂|The singing of psalms; the chanting or singing of sacred songs, especially in a liturgical context.  It often involves a repetitive or melodic structure, and is used in religious worship.|例句：The monks engaged in psalmody throughout the night.  僧侣们整夜都在吟唱圣诗。|例句：The beauty of the psalmody filled the cathedral. 圣诗歌咏的优美旋律充满了整个大教堂。|近义词：chanting, singing, hymnody |反义词：silence, profanity
