sheikh:/ʃeɪk/ | n. |酋长，谢赫 |A male leader of an Arab tribe or family, often with significant religious or political authority.  The term is also used as a title of respect. |The sheikh welcomed the visitors to his village.  这位酋长欢迎游客来到他的村庄。| emir, chief, leader | subject, follower |名词
