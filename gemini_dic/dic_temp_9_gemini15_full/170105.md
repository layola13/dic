Linguists have pieced together Proto-Hittite through the comparative method, analyzing similarities and differences between Hittite and other Indo-European languages.|The reconstruction of Proto-Hittite is still ongoing, and many aspects of the language remain uncertain. |近义词:  Pre-Hittite (though less precise)|反义词:  None directly applicable|词性:名词
