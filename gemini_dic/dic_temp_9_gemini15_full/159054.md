pneumometry:/njuːˈmɒmɪtri/ | n. |肺量测定法；肺活量测定 |The measurement of the air capacity of the lungs.  Pneumometry involves various techniques to assess lung volumes and capacities, providing valuable information about respiratory function. |Example:  The doctor ordered a pneumometry test to evaluate the patient's lung capacity. = 医生为了评估病人的肺活量，开了肺量测定检查。  The results of the pneumometry showed a significant reduction in lung volume. = 肺量测定结果显示肺容量显著减少。|近义词：spirometry (although spirometry is more commonly used and measures airflow)|反义词：无 |名词
