/ˈkædjʊkəs/| adj.|早落的；脱落的；短暂的|Falling off or shed at an early stage of development, typically before maturity; ephemeral.  This term is often used to describe plant parts, such as leaves or petals, that fall off prematurely.|The leaves of this deciduous tree are caducous.  这棵落叶树的叶子是早落的。|The petals of the poppy are caducous.  罂粟的花瓣是早落的。|deciduous, ephemeral, transient|persistent, enduring
