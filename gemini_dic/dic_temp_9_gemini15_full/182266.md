removable:/rɪˈmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 可移动的，可拆卸的 | Capable of being removed.  Something that is removable can be detached or taken away easily. | Example: The wheels on this stroller are removable for easy cleaning.  这个婴儿车的轮子是可拆卸的，方便清洁。 The hard drive is removable, allowing for easy data transfer. 这个硬盘是可移动的，方便数据传输。|近义词：detachable, detachable, extractable | 反义词：permanent, fixed, integral
