refolds:/riːˈfoʊldz/ | v. |重新折叠；再折叠 | To fold again or repeatedly.  The act of folding something again, often after it has been unfolded or partially unfolded. | 例句：She refolded the laundry after her son had rumpled it. = 她儿子把衣服弄皱了，她又重新叠好。

例句：He refolded the map, smoothing out the creases. = 他重新折叠地图，抚平褶皱。


近义词：refold, re-crease


反义词：unfold, straighten

词性:动词
