hydrologies:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒiz/ | n. |水文学（复数）|The scientific study of the movement, distribution, and quality of water on Earth, including its occurrence, circulation, and distribution in the atmosphere, on the surface of the earth, and underground.|例句：The hydrologies of the region are complex, influenced by both monsoonal rains and glacial melt.|近义词：hydrological sciences|反义词：无
