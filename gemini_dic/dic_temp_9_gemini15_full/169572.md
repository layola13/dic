prophesy:/ˈprɒfəsi/ /ˈprɑːfəsi/| v. n.|预言；预示|To predict or foretell a future event, typically one that is considered to be divinely inspired.|The oracle prophesied the king's downfall.  先知预言了国王的垮台。| He prophesied great things for the future. 他预言未来将会发生伟大的事情。|predict, foretell, forecast|fulfill (a prophesy)|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prophesies the end of the world. 他预言世界末日。
She prophesies good fortune for us. 她预言我们会有好运。


一般过去时:
The prophet prophesied the coming storm. 先知预言了即将到来的风暴。
They prophesied a great victory. 他们预言了一场伟大的胜利。


现在完成时:
The fortune teller has prophesied my future. 算命先生已经预言了我的未来。
No one has prophesied such a dramatic change. 没有人预言过如此剧烈的变化。


过去完成时:
The ancient texts had prophesied the return of the king. 古代文本预言了国王的回归。
They had prophesied a famine, and it came to pass. 他们曾预言过饥荒，而且它真的发生了。


将来时:
The seer will prophesy the fate of the nation. 预言家将预言国家的命运。
Scientists will prophesy the effects of climate change. 科学家们将预言气候变化的影响。


虚拟语气:
If only I had prophesied the market crash! 要是我能预言市场崩盘就好了！
If the prophet had prophesied correctly, we would have been prepared. 如果先知预言正确，我们就会做好准备。


被动语态:
The coming disaster was prophesied by many. 即将到来的灾难被许多人预言。
The future was prophesied to be full of challenges. 未来被预言充满了挑战。


疑问句:
Did the Sibyl prophesy the fall of Rome?  西比尔预言了罗马的灭亡吗？
What did the oracle prophesy about the future?  神谕对未来作了何种预言？


否定句:
The soothsayer did not prophesy any harm.  占卜者没有预言任何危害。
They did not prophesy the earthquake.  他们没有预言地震。

prophecy of failure 失败的预言
