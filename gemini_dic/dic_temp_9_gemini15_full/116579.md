matriarchalisms: /ˌmeɪtriˈɑːrkəlɪzəm/ | n. | 母系制度；母权社会习俗 | The systems and practices of matriarchal societies.  The study or analysis of matriarchal societies and their structures. |  例句：The anthropologist studied the matriarchalisms of the indigenous tribe, focusing on land ownership and inheritance patterns. 人类学家研究了土著部落的母系制度，重点是土地所有权和继承模式。


例句：Some scholars argue that certain aspects of matriarchalisms can be found in various historical and contemporary societies. 一些学者认为，在各种历史和当代社会中都可以发现母系制度的某些方面。
