outproduces:/ˌaʊtprəˈduːsɪz/| vt.|超过产量，产量高于|To produce more than; surpass in output.|例句：This factory outproduces all others in the region.这家工厂的产量超过该地区所有其他工厂。|例句：Our new system outproduces the old one by 20%. 我们的新系统比旧系统产量高20%。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：underproduce, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This farm outproduces its neighbour in terms of wheat yield. = 这个农场的小麦产量超过了它的邻居。
The new machine outproduces the old one significantly. = 新机器的产量大大超过旧机器。


一般过去时:
Last year, the company outproduced its competitors. = 去年，这家公司超过了其竞争对手的产量。
The team outproduced their rivals in the final match. = 在决赛中，这支队伍的得分超过了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
This company has outproduced all expectations this quarter. = 本季度这家公司已经超出了所有预期产量。
The farmer has outproduced his previous best yield by 10%. = 这位农民的产量比他之前的最高产量提高了10%。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had outproduced their target by a significant margin. = 到年底，他们的产量已大大超过目标。
The factory had outproduced its previous record before the maintenance shutdown. = 在维护停机之前，工厂的产量已经超过了之前的记录。


将来时:
We expect the new technology will outproduce the current methods. = 我们预计新技术将超过目前的产量。
Analysts predict that this company will outproduce its rivals next year. = 分析师预测这家公司明年将超过其竞争对手的产量。


虚拟语气:
If we invested more, we could outproduce our competitors. = 如果我们投入更多资金，我们就能超过竞争对手的产量。
I wish our team had outproduced the others in the competition. = 我希望我们的团队在比赛中产量超过其他团队。


被动语态:
Our production was outproduced by a smaller competitor. = 我们的产量被一个规模较小的竞争对手超过了。
The target was outproduced by a significant margin last year. = 去年目标产量被大大超过了。


疑问句:
Does this factory outproduce its rivals? = 这家工厂的产量超过竞争对手吗？
Will this new technology outproduce the old one? = 这项新技术会超过旧技术的产量吗？


否定句:
Our factory did not outproduce its competitors this year. = 今年我们的工厂产量没有超过竞争对手。
This year's harvest did not outproduce last year's. = 今年的收成没有超过去年的收成。
