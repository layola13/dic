noncivilian:/ˌnɒnˈsɪvɪliən/| adj. |非民间的；非平民的|Not belonging to or connected with the armed forces or the police.  This word describes individuals or things that are not part of the civilian population; rather, they are associated with the military, police, or other non-civilian entities. |例句：The noncivilian casualties were surprisingly high. 非民间的伤亡人数出奇地高。

例句：The base was open only to noncivilian personnel.  该基地只对非民用人员开放。


近义词：military, armed forces, police, governmental


反义词：civilian


词性：形容词
