overoffensiveness: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. | 过度冒犯；过分得罪 | The state or quality of being excessively offensive; going beyond the bounds of acceptable behavior or language.  It implies a degree of offense that is more than simply rude or impolite; it suggests a deliberate or careless disregard for the feelings of others. | 例句：His overoffensiveness alienated many potential allies. 他的过度冒犯疏远了许多潜在的盟友。  The overoffensiveness of the comedian's jokes led to numerous complaints.  这位喜剧演员笑话的过度冒犯导致了许多投诉。 | 近义词：insultingness, offensiveness, rudeness, discourtesy, impertinence | 反义词：politeness, courtesy, respectfulness | 名词
