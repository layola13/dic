prediploma: /ˌpriːdɪˈpləʊmə/ | n. | 预科证书；预科文凭 | A prediploma is a qualification obtained before a full diploma or degree is awarded, often representing the completion of a preparatory program or a significant portion of the requirements for a full diploma.  It might be a stepping stone towards a higher qualification. | Example:  After completing the prediploma program, she was well-prepared to enter the university's engineering program.  | 近义词：preparatory certificate, preliminary qualification | 反义词：diploma, degree
