residing:/rɪˈzaɪdɪŋ/| v.|居住，定居|The act of dwelling or living in a particular place.|例句：The family is residing in a small town.  这个家庭居住在一个小镇上。|例句：He is currently residing at a hotel near the airport. 他目前住在机场附近的一家酒店。|近义词：living, dwelling, inhabiting, occupying|反义词：leaving, departing, vacating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resides in London. = 他住在伦敦。
They reside at a countryside villa. = 他们居住在乡下的别墅里。

一般过去时:
She resided in Paris for many years. = 她曾在巴黎住了许多年。
My grandparents resided in this house their whole lives. = 我的祖父母一生都住在这所房子里。

现在完成时:
They have resided in this city since 2000. = 自2000年以来，他们一直居住在这个城市。
We have resided here for over a decade. = 我们在这里住了十多年了。

过去完成时:
Before moving to New York, she had resided in California. = 在搬到纽约之前，她住在加利福尼亚。
He had resided in that apartment for five years before it was renovated. = 那套公寓装修之前，他已经在那里住了五年。

将来时:
We will reside in a new apartment next month. = 下个月我们将住进一套新公寓。
They will reside in the suburbs after retirement. = 退休后，他们将住在郊区。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would reside in a bigger house. = 如果我有钱，我会住在一所更大的房子里。
I wish I could reside in a tropical country. = 我希望我能住在热带国家。

被动语态:
The house is resided in by a large family. = 这所房子住着一大家子人。(此用法不常见，主动语态更常用)
The property is currently resided in by tenants. = 这处房产目前由租户居住。(此用法不常见，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Where do you reside? = 你住在哪里？
Does he reside in the city or countryside? = 他住在城市还是农村？

否定句:
She does not reside in that building anymore. = 她不再住在那栋楼里了。
They did not reside in the same city. = 他们不住在同一个城市。


PHRASE->
reside in = 住在…
reside with = 与…同住
reside at = 住在（特定地址）
place of residence = 住址
legal residence = 法定住所
