on-side:/ˌɒnˈsaɪd/| adj.| 符合规则的；在允许的位置的；支持的 |In football (soccer), a player is onside if they are not in an offside position.  In other contexts, it means being in agreement or support of something. |例句：The referee ruled the goal was valid because the player was onside.  裁判裁定进球有效，因为球员处于允许的位置。

The team was onside with the new policy.  该团队支持这项新政策。|近义词：legal, permissible, supportive, in agreement |反义词：offside, illegal, against, opposed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The player is onside. = 球员处于允许的位置。
He is onside with our plans. = 他支持我们的计划。

一般过去时:
The goal was disallowed because the striker was offside. = 进球被判无效，因为前锋越位了。
She was onside with the proposal. = 她支持该提案。

现在完成时:
They have been onside throughout the negotiations. = 在整个谈判过程中，他们都持支持态度。
The team has always been onside with the manager's decisions. = 球队一直支持教练的决定。

过去完成时:
The pass was deemed offside because the receiver had been onside before the pass. = 传球被判越位，因为接球者在传球前处于越位位置。(This example is tricky with 'onside' but illustrates the tense.)
They had been onside with the project from the start. = 从一开始他们就支持这个项目。

将来时:
The player will be onside if he stays behind the last defender. = 如果球员保持在最后一名后卫身后，他将处于允许的位置。
We expect they will be onside with our new strategy. = 我们预计他们会支持我们的新战略。

虚拟语气:
If he were onside, the goal would have counted. = 如果他处于允许的位置，进球将算数。
If they were onside with the initiative, it would have a better chance of success. = 如果他们支持这项倡议，它将有更大的成功机会。

被动语态:
The position was deemed onside by the linesman. = 边裁认为该位置符合规则。
The proposal was not considered to be onside by the committee. = 委员会认为该提案不可行/不支持。

疑问句:
Is the player onside? = 球员处于允许的位置吗？
Are you onside with this decision? = 你支持这个决定吗？


否定句:
The player was offside. = 球员越位了。
He was not onside with the changes. = 他不支持这些改变。


PHRASE->
to be onside with: 支持，赞同
to get onside: 获得支持
to stay onside: 保持符合规则/保持支持
to keep someone onside: 保持某人的支持
offside (as a phrase): 越位 (this is an antonymic phrase rather than a phrase with "onside")
