hybridizers:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzərz/ | n. |杂交育种者，杂交者 | People who create hybrids, especially of plants or animals.  They are involved in the process of crossbreeding different species or varieties to produce offspring with desirable traits. | Example:  The hybridizers carefully selected parent plants with complementary characteristics. 杂交育种者仔细挑选了具有互补特性的亲本植物。|近义词：breeders, crossbreeders |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "purebred breeders" focusing on maintaining a single breed.
