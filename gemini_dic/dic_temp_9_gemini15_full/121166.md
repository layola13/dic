misdial:/mɪsˈdaɪəl/ | vt. |拨错号码，拨错电话 |To dial the wrong number when using a telephone.  |例句：I misdialed and ended up talking to a stranger. 我拨错号码了，结果和一个陌生人聊了起来。|近义词：wrong number, dial incorrectly |反义词：dial correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misdials frequently. = 他经常拨错号码。
She misdials numbers quite often. = 她经常拨错号码。


一般过去时:
I misdialed the number yesterday. = 我昨天拨错了号码。
They misdialed and got a wrong connection. = 他们拨错号码，接通了错误的线路。


现在完成时:
I have misdialed several times today. = 我今天已经拨错好几次号码了。
She has misdialed that number before. = 她以前拨错过那个号码。


过去完成时:
I realized I had misdialed after the call ended. = 通话结束后，我才意识到我拨错了号码。
He found out he had misdialed when the person answered in a foreign language. = 当对方用外语回答时，他发现自己拨错了号码。


将来时:
I think I will misdial again if I don't write down the number. = 如果我不把号码记下来，我想我会再次拨错号码。
He will probably misdial if he's in a hurry. = 如果他很匆忙，他可能会拨错号码。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misdialed, I would have reached my friend. = 如果我没有拨错号码，我就联系到我的朋友了。
I wish I hadn't misdialed; I missed an important call. = 我真希望我没有拨错号码；我错过了一个重要的电话。


被动语态:
The number was misdialed. = 号码拨错了。
The wrong number was misdialed by mistake. = 错误的号码被误拨了。


疑问句:
Did you misdial the number? = 你拨错号码了吗？
Have you ever misdialed an emergency number? = 你曾经拨错过紧急号码吗？


否定句:
I didn't misdial; I'm sure I pressed the right buttons. = 我没有拨错号码；我确定我按对了按钮。
She didn't misdial, it was a wrong connection. = 她没有拨错号码，是线路连接错误。
