formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjuleɪt/ | vt. | 制定；构想；表达 | To create or develop (a plan, system, etc.) | The team formulated a new marketing strategy.  团队制定了一个新的营销策略。|  devise, develop, create |  dismantle, destroy | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They formulate a plan every year. = 他们每年都制定一个计划。
She formulates her thoughts carefully before speaking. = 她说话前会仔细组织好自己的思绪。


一般过去时:
The scientists formulated a hypothesis to explain the phenomenon. = 科学家们制定了一个假设来解释这种现象。
He formulated his response in a clear and concise manner. = 他以清晰简洁的方式表达了他的回应。


现在完成时:
They have formulated a comprehensive response to the crisis. = 他们已经制定了一个全面的应对危机的方案。
She has formulated her ideas into a well-structured essay. = 她已经把她的想法写成一篇结构良好的论文。


过去完成时:
They had formulated a plan before the meeting began. = 会议开始前他们已经制定了一个计划。
She had formulated her argument before presenting it to the committee. = 在向委员会陈述她的论点之前，她已经将其构思好了。


将来时:
They will formulate a new policy next month. = 他们下个月将制定一项新政策。
She will formulate her speech carefully before the presentation. = 她将在演讲前仔细准备她的发言。


虚拟语气:
If they formulated a better plan, they would succeed. = 如果他们制定一个更好的计划，他们就会成功。
I wish I had formulated my thoughts more clearly. = 我希望我当时能更清晰地表达我的想法。


被动语态:
A new strategy was formulated by the management team. = 新策略是由管理团队制定的。
The plan was formulated after careful consideration. = 该计划是在仔细考虑之后制定的。


疑问句:
How did they formulate their conclusions? = 他们是如何得出结论的？
What factors did she consider when formulating her argument? = 制定论点时，她考虑了哪些因素？


否定句:
They did not formulate a clear action plan. = 他们没有制定明确的行动计划。
She did not formulate her ideas effectively. = 她没有有效地表达她的想法。
