/dɪˈskraɪb/| vt.|描述，形容|To give a detailed account of; to depict in words.|例句：Please describe the suspect to the police. 请向警方描述嫌疑人的样子。|例句：Can you describe what happened? 你能描述一下发生了什么吗？|近义词：portray, depict, delineate|反义词：misrepresent, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He describes the painting beautifully. = 他优美地描述了这幅画。
She describes her childhood vividly. = 她生动地描述了自己的童年。

一般过去时:
The witness described the accident clearly. = 目击者清晰地描述了事故经过。
He described his feelings in detail. = 他详细地描述了他的感受。

现在完成时:
She has described the process several times. = 她已经多次描述了这个过程。
They have described the new product in the brochure. = 他们在宣传册中描述了新产品。

过去完成时:
He had described the scene before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经描述了现场情况。
She had described her experience to her therapist. = 她已经向她的治疗师描述了她的经历。

将来时:
He will describe his trip to the conference. = 他将描述他去参加会议的旅程。
She will describe the research findings in her presentation. = 她将在她的报告中描述研究结果。

虚拟语气:
If he described the situation honestly, people would understand. = 如果他诚实地描述情况，人们就会理解。
I wish she had described the problem more clearly. = 我希望她能更清楚地描述这个问题。

被动语态:
The event was described in the newspaper. = 这件事在报纸上被报道了。
The painting is described as a masterpiece. = 这幅画被描述为杰作。

疑问句:
How would you describe your personality? = 你如何形容你的性格？
Can you describe the problem more specifically? = 你能更具体地描述一下问题吗？

否定句:
He didn't describe the situation accurately. = 他没有准确地描述情况。
She couldn't describe the man's face clearly. = 她无法清晰地描述那个男人的脸。


PHRASE->
describe in detail = 详细描述
describe as = 将…描述为
describe oneself as = 自称是
describe the scene = 描述场景
vividly describe = 生动地描述
