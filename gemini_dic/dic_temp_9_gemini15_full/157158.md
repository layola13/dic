pines:/paɪnz/| n.|松树；松林| Pines are evergreen coniferous resinous trees.  They are known for their needle-like leaves and cones. Many species of pines exist worldwide, varying in size and characteristics. | 例句：The wind whispered through the pines. 风在松林中低语。

The majestic pines towered over the valley.  雄伟的松树耸立在山谷之上。|近义词：conifers, evergreens|反义词：deciduous trees
