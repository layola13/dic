reseller:/riːˈsɛlə(r)/| n. |经销商，转售商 |A reseller is a person or company that buys products from a manufacturer or wholesaler and sells them to consumers or other businesses. They typically don't manufacture the products themselves.  |Example:  Many online retailers are resellers of products manufactured by others. 许多在线零售商都是其他厂商生产的产品的经销商。

Many small businesses act as resellers, buying wholesale and selling retail.许多小型企业充当经销商，以批发价购买，以零售价出售。|近义词：distributor, dealer, vendor |反义词：manufacturer, producer
