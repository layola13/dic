hypodermics:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪks/| n.|皮下注射器；皮下注射法|relating to or denoting the injection of substances beneath the skin.  The term often refers to syringes used for such injections.|例句：The nurse prepared the hypodermics for the injection. 护士准备了皮下注射器进行注射。

例句：Hypodermics are used to administer medication directly into the subcutaneous tissue. 皮下注射器用于将药物直接注射到皮下组织中。


近义词：subcutaneous injection, injection, syringe


反义词：oral administration (口服给药)


词性:noun
