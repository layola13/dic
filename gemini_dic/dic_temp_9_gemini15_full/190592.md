satoshi:/ˈsɑːtɒʃi/ | n. |聪; 聪(比特币最小单位)|A satoshi is the smallest unit of Bitcoin.  It is one hundred millionth of a Bitcoin (0.00000001 BTC).  The name is a tribute to Satoshi Nakamoto, the pseudonymous creator of Bitcoin.|例句：One Bitcoin is equivalent to 100 million satoshis. 一个比特币等于1亿聪。

例句：Sending small amounts of Bitcoin is easier when using satoshis. 使用聪发送少量比特币更容易。
