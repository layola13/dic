lazes:/ˈleɪzɪz/| v.|懒散；消磨时间|to spend time in a lazy way, doing very little|例句：He lazes away his afternoons watching television. 他下午懒洋洋地看电视消磨时光。 He lazes around the house all day. 他整天懒洋洋地待在家里。|近义词：loaf, idle, relax|反义词：hustle, work, strive
