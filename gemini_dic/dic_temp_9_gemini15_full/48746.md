definite: /ˈdɛfɪnət/ | adj. | 明确的；确定的；肯定的 |  Having exact limits; clearly defined or determined.  It implies a lack of ambiguity or vagueness.  | 例句：The evidence is definite proof of his guilt.  证据确凿证明他有罪。

例句：We need a definite answer by tomorrow. 我们需要在明天之前得到明确的答复。


近义词：certain, sure, positive, explicit, unambiguous | 反义词：uncertain, vague, indefinite, ambiguous


词源:  Comes from the Latin word "definītus," the past participle of "definīre," meaning "to define," "to limit," or "to bound."  The Latin root is "fīnīre," meaning "to end" or "to limit."
