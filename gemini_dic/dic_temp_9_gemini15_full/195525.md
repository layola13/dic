semipeace:/ˈsemiˌpiːs/| adj.|半和平状态；相对和平|A state of relative peace, often characterized by the absence of major conflict but with underlying tensions or unresolved issues remaining.  It implies a fragile or incomplete peace.|例句：The ceasefire brought a fragile semipeace to the region, but underlying tensions remained.  停火为该地区带来了脆弱的半和平状态，但潜在的紧张局势依然存在。


例句：Despite the peace treaty, a sense of semipeace lingered, with sporadic skirmishes continuing. 尽管签署了和平条约，但零星冲突仍在继续，一种半和平的状态依然存在。


近义词：truce, armistice, quiet, calm|反义词：war, conflict, turmoil, chaos


词性:形容词
