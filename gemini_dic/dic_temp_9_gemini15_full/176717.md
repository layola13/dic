reactive: /riˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 反應性的；敏感的；反應遲鈍的 | readily responding to stimulation; characterized by reaction to stimuli |  The reactive nature of the chemical made it dangerous to handle.  这种化学物质的反应性使其处理起来很危险。|近义词：responsive, sensitive, responsive | 反义词：proactive, unresponsive, inert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is reactive to changes in temperature. = 系统对温度变化有反应。
He is reactive to criticism. = 他对批评很敏感。


一般过去时:
The market was reactive to the news. = 市场对这个消息做出了反应。
She was reactive to the medication. = 她对药物有反应。


现在完成时:
The team has been reactive to the crisis. = 团队对危机做出了反应。
The patient has become reactive to light. = 病人对光线变得敏感。


过去完成时:
The economy had been reactive to the policy changes. = 经济已经对政策变化做出了反应。
He had become reactive to certain foods. = 他已经对某些食物过敏了。


将来时:
The software will be reactive to user input. = 该软件将对用户输入做出反应。
They will be reactive to any change in the market. = 他们将对市场上的任何变化做出反应。


虚拟语气:
If the system were more reactive, it would perform better. = 如果系统反应更快，它的性能就会更好。
If he were less reactive, he would be less stressed. = 如果他不那么敏感，就不会那么有压力。


被动语态:
The situation was reacted to quickly. = 情况得到了迅速的处理。
The proposal was reacted to negatively by some members. = 一些成员对该提案反应消极。


疑问句:
Is the material reactive to water? = 这种材料遇水会反应吗？
Is he reactive to stress? = 他容易承受压力吗？


否定句:
The material is not reactive to acids. = 这种材料不会与酸发生反应。
He is not reactive to most things. = 他对大多数事情都不敏感。


PHRASE->
reactive oxygen species (ROS) = 活性氧
reactive power = 无功功率
reactive depression = 反应性抑郁症
highly reactive = 高度活泼的
reactive arthritis = 反应性关节炎

词源:
The word "reactive" originates from the verb "react," which comes from the Latin prefix "re-" (back, again) and the verb "agere" (to do, to act).  Therefore, "reactive" literally means "acting back" or "responding to a stimulus."
