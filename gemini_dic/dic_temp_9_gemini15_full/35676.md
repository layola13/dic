cobbling:/ˈkɒblɪŋ/| n. v.|粗制滥造；修补；用鹅卵石铺路|The act of repairing something in a clumsy or makeshift way;  the process of paving with cobblestones.|例句：His attempt at fixing the chair was a bit of cobbling. 他的椅子修理尝试有点粗制滥造。The old street was being recobbled. 这条老街正在重新用鹅卵石铺路。|近义词：repairing, patching, mending, paving|反义词：perfecting, refining, constructing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is cobbling together a new plan. = 他正在拼凑一个新的计划。
She cobbles shoes for a living. = 她靠修鞋为生。

一般过去时:
They cobbled the path with stones. = 他们用石头铺了小路。
He cobbled together a meal from leftovers. = 他用剩菜剩饭拼凑了一顿饭。

现在完成时:
I have cobbled together a presentation at the last minute. = 我在最后一刻仓促准备了一个演示文稿。
She has been cobbling shoes for over twenty years. = 她修鞋已经超过二十年了。

过去完成时:
They had cobbled together a shelter before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经搭建了一个简易的庇护所。
He had cobbled together a story to explain his lateness. = 他编造了一个故事来解释他的迟到。

将来时:
We will cobble together a solution to the problem. = 我们将会想出一个解决问题的办法。
She will be cobbling shoes at the market tomorrow. = 她明天将在市场上修鞋。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cobble the garden wall properly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把花园墙好好地修补一下。
I wish I had cobbled the report more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地整理报告。

被动语态:

疑问句:
