intercutting:/ˌɪntərˈkʌtɪŋ/| v. | 交叉剪辑；穿插剪辑 | The technique of alternating two or more scenes, often to create suspense or contrast.  | Example: The film used intercutting to show the parallel storylines of the two characters. 这部电影运用交叉剪辑来展现两个角色平行的故事情节。 |近义词：crosscutting, parallel editing |反义词：sequential editing
