deviser:/dɪˈvaɪzər/| n. |设计者；发明者；策划者 | A deviser is someone who creates or invents something, often a plan or scheme.  They are skilled in conceiving and developing new ideas or systems. |  The deviser of the plan was a brilliant strategist.  这位计划的设计者是一位杰出的战略家。 | inventor, creator, planner, architect | destroyer, dismantler,  | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team devises a new marketing strategy. = 团队正在制定一项新的营销策略。
He devises innovative solutions to complex problems. = 他正在设计出解决复杂问题的创新方案。


一般过去时:
They devised a clever plan to escape. = 他们设计了一个巧妙的逃跑计划。
She devised a new method for teaching mathematics. = 她设计了一种新的数学教学方法。


现在完成时:
We have devised a system to track our progress. = 我们已经设计了一个系统来跟踪我们的进度。
They have devised several ways to improve efficiency. = 他们已经设计了几种提高效率的方法。


过去完成时:
He had devised a plan before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，他已经制定了一个计划。
They had devised a strategy to counter the opponent's moves. = 他们已经制定了一个策略来应对对手的行动。


将来时:
We will devise a new game for the children. = 我们将为孩子们设计一个新的游戏。
She will devise a solution to the current crisis. = 她将想出一个解决当前危机的方案。


虚拟语气:
If I were to devise a solution, this is what I would do. = 如果我要设计一个解决方案，我会这样做。
I wish I had devised a better plan. = 我希望我当时制定了一个更好的计划。


被动语态:
A new system was devised to improve efficiency. = 一个新的系统被设计出来以提高效率。
The plan was devised by a team of experts. = 这个计划是由一个专家团队制定的。


疑问句:
Who devised this ingenious system? = 谁设计了这个巧妙的系统？
What methods did they devise to solve the problem? = 他们设计了什么方法来解决这个问题？


否定句:
They did not devise a successful plan. = 他们没有制定一个成功的计划。
We could not devise a solution to the problem. = 我们无法找到解决这个问题的方案。
