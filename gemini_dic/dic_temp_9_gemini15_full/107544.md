landleaper:/ˈlændˌliːpər/| n. |跳跃土地的人，土地跳跃者|A landleaper is a person or creature that leaps or jumps across land, often implying a significant distance or height.  The term is often used figuratively or in fantasy contexts.|例句：The mythical landleaper could cross vast distances with a single bound. 神话中的土地跳跃者能够一跃跨越广阔的距离。The young deer, a miniature landleaper, bounded across the meadow.  那只年轻的鹿，一个微型的土地跳跃者，在草地上跳跃着。|近义词：jumper, leaper, springer|反义词：crawler, creeper|名词
