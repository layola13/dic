concocter:/kənˈkɒktə(r)/| vt.|调制；捏造；虚构|to prepare or make something, especially food or drink, by mixing different things together; to invent or create something, especially a story or explanation|例句：She concocted a delicious stew from leftover vegetables. 她用剩下的蔬菜调制了一道美味的炖菜。

例句：He concocted a story about being robbed to explain his lateness. 他编造了一个被抢的故事来解释他的迟到。

近义词：prepare, mix, invent, fabricate, devise

反义词：destroy, dismantle, deconstruct


USAGE->
