scuffles:/ˈskʌfəlz/| n. v.|小争吵；冲突；拖着脚走|a short, confused fight or struggle.|The students got into a scuffle in the hallway. 学生们在走廊上发生了一场小争吵。|近义词：brawl, skirmish, tussle|反义词：peace, harmony|

They often scuffle in the playground. = 他们在操场上经常打闹。
