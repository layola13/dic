client's:/ˈklaɪənts/| n.|客户的，委托人的|  Belonging to or relating to a client.  It is the possessive form of the noun "client".|例句：The client's request was unreasonable.  客户的要求不合理。

例句：We need to review the client's contract before proceeding. 我们需要在继续之前审查客户的合同。


近义词：customer's, patron's
反义词：None (it's a possessive form, not a descriptive adjective with direct opposites)
词性：形容词性物主代词
