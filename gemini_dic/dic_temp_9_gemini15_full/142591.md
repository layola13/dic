osteogeny:/ˌɒstiˈɒdʒəni/| n. |成骨作用；骨发生|The formation of bone; the process by which bone tissue is formed.  This involves the differentiation of osteoblasts from mesenchymal stem cells and their subsequent deposition of bone matrix.|例句：The study focused on the osteogeny of the fractured bone. 这项研究的重点是骨折骨的成骨作用。

例句：Understanding osteogeny is crucial for treating bone diseases. 理解成骨作用对于治疗骨骼疾病至关重要。


近义词：bone formation, ossification


反义词：bone resorption, osteolysis


词性：名词
