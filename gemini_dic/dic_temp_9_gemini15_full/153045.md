peeped:/piːpt/| v.|偷看，窥视|To look quickly and secretly at something.  |例句：I peeped through the keyhole. 我从钥匙孔里偷看。

例句：She peeped at the birthday present before the party. 她在派对前偷看了生日礼物。|近义词：glance, peek, spy|反义词：stare openly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I peep at the birds in the garden every morning. = 我每天早上都偷看花园里的鸟儿。
He peeks into the room before entering. = 他在进房间之前先偷看一眼。


一般过去时:
I peeped through the keyhole and saw them kissing. = 我从钥匙孔里偷看，看到他们在接吻。
She peeped into the box to see what was inside. = 她偷偷地看了一眼盒子，看看里面有什么。


现在完成时:
I have peeped into her diary several times, but I have never read anything significant. = 我偷看过几次她的日记，但我从未读到过什么重要的东西。
We have peeped at the neighbor’s house many times, but we never saw anything interesting. = 我们多次窥探邻居的房子，但从未看到任何有趣的东西。


过去完成时:
I had peeped into the room before he called me. = 他叫我之前，我已经偷偷地看过房间了。
They had peeped several times at the house that night, before deciding to call the police. = 那天晚上，他们在决定报警之前已经多次窥探了那所房子。


将来时:
I will peep at the baby while the mother is away. = 妈妈不在的时候，我会偷偷看一眼婴儿。
He will peep into the envelope before opening it. = 他会在打开信封之前先偷偷看一眼。


虚拟语气:
If I had peeped earlier, I would have seen them leave. = 如果我早点偷看，我就会看到他们离开了。
I wish I hadn't peeped at my friend’s exam paper. = 我真希望我没有偷看我朋友的试卷。


被动语态:
The room was peeped into by the police. = 警察偷偷地看过那个房间。
The letter was peeped at before it was opened. = 这封信在打开之前被偷看过。


疑问句:
Did you peep at the answer sheet? = 你偷看了答案吗？
Have you ever peeped into your neighbor's window? = 你有没有偷看过你邻居的窗户？


否定句:
I did not peep at her. = 我没有偷看她。
They didn't peep into the room. = 他们没有偷看房间。
