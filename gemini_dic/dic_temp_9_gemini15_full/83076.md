gurry:/ˈɡʌri/| n.|糊状物；烂泥；污秽物|A thick, messy, often unpleasant mixture of substances.  It often refers to food that is overcooked or poorly prepared, resulting in a slimy or unappetizing consistency.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe something messy or confusing.|例句：The stew was a complete gurry; I couldn't even bring myself to taste it. 这道炖菜完全是一团糟，我甚至无法尝试品尝它。|近义词：slurry, mush, mess, slop|反义词：neat, clean, refined|名词
