monarchism:/ˈmɒnəkɪzəm/ | n. |君主制；君主政治 |The belief in or advocacy of monarchical government.  A system of government in which supreme power is vested in a monarch. |Example:  Many European countries have a long history of monarchism. 许多欧洲国家都有悠久的君主制历史。|近义词：royalism, kingship |反义词：republicanism, democracy
