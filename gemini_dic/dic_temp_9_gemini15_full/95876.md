increase:/ɪnˈkriːs/ | v., n. | 增加，增长 | To become or make greater in amount, number, or size.  |例句：The company's profits increased by 10% last year.  公司去年的利润增长了10%。

The demand for electric cars has increased significantly. 对电动汽车的需求显著增加。|近义词：augment, enlarge, expand, grow, heighten |反义词：decrease, diminish, reduce, lessen |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Prices increase steadily. = 价格稳步上涨。
Demand for the product increases during the holiday season. = 节日期间对该产品的需求增加。


一般过去时:
The population increased dramatically last decade. = 人口在上个十年急剧增加。
My salary increased after I got promoted. = 我升职后工资增加了。


现在完成时:
Sales have increased significantly this year. = 今年销量大幅增长。
The number of students has increased since last year. = 自去年以来学生人数增加了。


过去完成时:
The company's profits had increased before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司的利润已经增加了。
The temperature had increased before the rain started. = 下雨前气温已经升高了。


将来时:
Interest rates will increase next month. = 利率下个月将上涨。
The company expects its revenue to increase next year. = 公司预计明年收入将增加。


虚拟语气:
If the temperature increased further, the ice would melt. = 如果气温进一步升高，冰就会融化。
I wish my income increased more quickly. = 我希望我的收入增长更快。


被动语态:
The price was increased by 15%. = 价格上涨了15%。
The production level is being increased to meet the demand. = 正在提高产量以满足需求。


疑问句:
Has the number of participants increased? = 参加人数增加了多少？
Will the cost of living increase next year? = 明年的生活成本会增加吗？


否定句:
The number of visitors didn't increase this year. = 今年游客人数没有增加。
Production hasn't increased despite the investment. = 尽管进行了投资，产量也没有增加。


PHRASE->
increase in =  增加
increase by = 增加到
a sharp increase = 急剧增加
a gradual increase = 逐渐增加
a significant increase = 重大增长
