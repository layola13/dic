reating: /ˈkriˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 创造；创作；引起 |  The act of creating something new.  This word is the present participle of the verb "to create". It describes an ongoing action of creation. | 例句：She is creating a beautiful painting. = 她正在创作一幅美丽的画作。


例句：The chef is creating a new dish for the menu. = 厨师正在为菜单创作一道新菜。


近义词：making, producing, inventing, designing, originating


反义词：destroying, ruining, obliterating


词性：动词
