flees:/fliːz/ | v. |逃跑，逃离 | to run away from a place or situation, especially because you are afraid or in danger. |例句：The thief flees the scene of the crime. 小偷逃离犯罪现场。|例句：The family flees their war-torn country.  这个家庭逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。|近义词：escape, run away, bolt |反义词：stay, remain, approach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flees every time he sees a police officer. 他每次看到警察就逃跑。
The bird flees from the cat. 鸟儿从猫那里逃走。

一般过去时:
She fled the burning building. 她逃离了燃烧的建筑物。
They fled the country during the revolution. 他们在革命期间逃离了这个国家。

现在完成时:
He has fled the country and is now living in exile. 他已经逃离了这个国家，现在流亡海外。
The criminals have fled the scene. 罪犯们已经逃离了现场。

过去完成时:
She had fled before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，她已经逃跑了。
They had fled their homes before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，他们已经逃离了家园。

将来时:
He will flee the city if he loses his job. 如果他丢了工作，他会逃离这座城市。
They will flee to the mountains if war breaks out. 如果战争爆发，他们将逃往山区。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would flee. 如果我是你，我会逃跑。
I wish I could flee this oppressive regime. 我真希望我能逃离这个压迫性政权。

被动语态: (flees本身很少用被动语态，因为它是及物动词，需要宾语，而被动语态通常用于及物动词)  可以考虑用“be driven away”代替。
The villagers were driven away by the invading army. 村民们被入侵的军队赶走了。
The animals were driven away from their habitat. 动物们被赶出了它们的栖息地。

疑问句:
Will he flee the country? 他会逃离这个国家吗？
Did she flee when she saw the police? 她看到警察时逃跑了没有？

否定句:
He did not flee; he stayed and fought. 他没有逃跑；他留下来战斗了。
She did not flee from the problem; she faced it head-on. 她没有逃避问题；她正面面对它。


PHRASE->

flee for one's life  拼命逃跑
put to flight 使…溃逃
take flight  起飞；逃走
