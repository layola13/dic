inlet: /ˈɪnlet/ | n. | 入口；水湾；小河 | A narrow passage or bay where the water enters a larger body of water.  An inlet can also refer to a small opening or passage for admitting something. | 例句：The boat sailed into the narrow inlet.  这艘船驶入狭窄的水湾。  The air inlet on the machine was clogged with dust. 机器上的进气口被灰尘堵住了。 | 近义词：entrance, bay, cove, estuary | 反义词：outlet, exit
