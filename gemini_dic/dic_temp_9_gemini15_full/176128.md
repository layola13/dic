razorback:/ˈreɪzərˌbæk/| n. adj.|野猪；（尤指）背部隆起的猪；背部有脊状突起的|A razorback is a wild pig, especially one with a ridge along its back.  The term can also describe anything with a prominent ridge or spine along its back.|例句：The hunters tracked the razorback through the dense forest. 猎人追踪着野猪穿过茂密的森林。The old house had a razorback roof. 这栋老房子有个脊状屋顶。|近义词：wild pig, hog, boar|反义词：暂无
