sapphire:/ˈsæfaɪər/| n. |蓝宝石|A precious stone, a variety of corundum, that is typically blue but may also be other colors such as yellow, pink, or white.  It is highly valued for its beauty and hardness. |例句：Her engagement ring featured a dazzling sapphire.  她的订婚戒指上镶嵌着一颗耀眼的蓝宝石。

例句：The jeweler carefully examined the sapphire for imperfections. 珠宝商仔细检查蓝宝石是否有瑕疵。
