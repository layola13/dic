leafletted:/ˈliːflɛtɪd/ | v. |散发传单；分发传单 | To distribute leaflets.  |The activists leafletted the neighborhood to promote their cause.  积极分子们在社区散发传单来宣传他们的事业。| distribute, hand out | collect, withhold | 动词


USAGE->

They leaflet the town every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都在镇上散发传单。
We leaflet our new product in the shopping mall. = 我们在购物中心散发我们新产品的传单。
