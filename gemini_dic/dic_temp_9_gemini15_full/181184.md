reinitiate:/ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪt/| vt.|重新开始；再启动；|To begin something again after it has been stopped or interrupted.|The company reinitiated the project after a period of inactivity. 公司在一段时间的停滞后重新启动了该项目。|近义词：restart, recommence, resume|反义词：terminate, end, cease|动词


USAGE->

He reinitiates the conversation every time we meet. 他每次见到我们都会重新开始谈话。
They reinitiate the process annually. 他们每年都会重新启动这个流程。

She reinitiated contact after several months of silence. 她沉默了几个月后重新取得了联系。
The government reinitiated negotiations last week. 政府上周重新开始了谈判。

过去完成时:
