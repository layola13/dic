gynarchy:/ˈɡaɪnɑːrki/| n. |女性统治；女权政治|A system of government ruled by women.  The term often implies a matriarchal society where women hold primary power and authority.|Example: Some feminist theorists have proposed gynarchy as an alternative to patriarchal systems.  一些女权主义理论家已经提出将女性统治作为父权制度的替代方案。|近义词：matriarchy, female rule|反义词：patriarchy, male rule|名词
