osteophytic:/ˌɒstiəˈfɪtɪk/| adj.|骨赘的，骨刺的| Relating to or characterized by osteophytes (bony projections that develop along the edges of bones).  Osteophytic growths are common in osteoarthritis.|例句：The X-ray showed significant osteophytic lipping along the cervical spine. X光显示颈椎有明显的骨赘。 The patient presented with osteophytic spurs causing nerve impingement. 病人出现骨刺导致神经受压。|近义词：bony, spondylitic|反义词：smooth, unblemished
