grapelet:/ˈɡreɪplət/| n. |小葡萄；小串葡萄 |A small grape; a small cluster of grapes.  |Example: The vine produced a bountiful harvest of grapelets. 这藤蔓结出了丰收的小葡萄。|近义词：small grape, little grape|反义词：bunch of grapes (相对词，并非反义词)|名词
