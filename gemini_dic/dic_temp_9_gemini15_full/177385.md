reauthorized:/ˌriːˈɔːθəraɪzd/| v. |重新授权，再次授权 | To authorize again; to grant authority or power to someone or something once more.  |The committee reauthorized the project after reviewing the new data. 委员会在审查了新的数据后，再次批准了该项目。|authorize, empower|revoke, disapprove|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reauthorize the program annually. = 他们每年都会重新授权该项目。
The government reauthorizes the bill every five years. = 政府每五年重新授权该法案一次。

一般过去时:
The president reauthorized the military operation. = 总统再次批准了军事行动。
Congress reauthorized the funding for the research. = 国会再次批准了该项研究的资金。

现在完成时:
They have reauthorized the contract for another year. = 他们已经将合同续签了一年。
The board has reauthorized the company's expansion plans. = 董事会已经再次批准了公司的扩张计划。

过去完成时:
They had reauthorized the spending before the crisis hit. = 危机发生前，他们已经重新批准了支出。
The court had reauthorized the search warrant before the police acted. = 在警方采取行动之前，法院已经重新批准了搜查令。

将来时:
They will reauthorize the funding next year. = 他们明年将重新批准拨款。
The legislature will reauthorize the law after reviewing public comments. = 立法机关将在审查公众意见后重新授权该法律。

虚拟语气:
If they reauthorized the project, we could continue the work. = 如果他们重新批准了该项目，我们就可以继续工作了。
I wish they had reauthorized the grant earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新批准拨款。

被动语态:
The bill was reauthorized by the Senate. = 该法案已由参议院重新授权。
The project was reauthorized after a lengthy debate. = 该项目在长时间的辩论后获得了重新授权。

疑问句:
Has the committee reauthorized the funds? = 委员会是否已经重新授权了资金？
Will the program be reauthorized next year? = 该项目明年会被重新授权吗？

否定句:
They did not reauthorize the spending. = 他们没有重新批准支出。
The government has not yet reauthorized the emergency powers. = 政府尚未重新授权紧急权力。
