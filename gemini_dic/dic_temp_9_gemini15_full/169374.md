pronationalistic: /ˌproʊˌnæʃənəlˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 拥护民族主义的；民族主义的 |  Characterized by or advocating pronationalism; exhibiting or characterized by strong feelings of national pride and/or a belief in the superiority of one's own nation.  This term often implies an excessive or aggressive form of nationalism, potentially leading to exclusionary or hostile attitudes towards other nations.


例句：

The pronationalistic rhetoric of the political party alienated many voters.  拥护民族主义的政党言论疏远了许多选民。

His pronationalistic views led him to support protectionist trade policies.  他拥护民族主义的观点导致他支持贸易保护主义政策。


近义词：nationalistic, jingoistic, chauvinistic, ultranationalistic


反义词：internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist


词性：形容词
