doubler:/ˈdʌblər/| n. v.|加倍者；使加倍的人或物；倍增器|One that doubles or causes to double; a device that doubles something.|例句：The doubler amplified the signal.  倍增器放大了信号。|例句：He acted as a doubler for the actor in the dangerous scenes. 他在危险的场景中担任替身演员。|近义词：multiplier, amplifier|反义词：halver, divider|名词、动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine doubles the output. = 这台机器使产量翻倍。
He doubles as a teacher and a writer. = 他同时担任教师和作家。


一般过去时:
They doubled the recipe to feed everyone. = 他们把食谱加倍以喂饱所有人。
The company doubled its profits last year. = 公司去年的利润翻了一番。


现在完成时:
She has doubled her income in the last five years. = 在过去的五年里，她的收入翻了一番。
The population has doubled since the last census. = 自上次人口普查以来，人口翻了一番。


过去完成时:
They had doubled their efforts before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前加倍努力。
The value of the property had doubled before they sold it. = 他们在出售房产之前，房产价值翻了一番。


将来时:
We will double our production next year. = 我们明年将把产量翻一番。
The price of oil is expected to double in the next decade. = 预计石油价格在未来十年内将翻一番。


虚拟语气:
If we doubled our marketing budget, we might see better results. = 如果我们把营销预算翻一番，我们可能会看到更好的结果。
I wish I had doubled my investment earlier. = 我真希望我早点把投资翻一番。


被动语态:
The workforce was doubled to meet the increased demand. = 为了满足日益增长的需求，员工人数增加了一倍。
The image was doubled to create a mirrored effect. = 图像被复制以创建镜像效果。


疑问句:
Does this machine double the speed? = 这台机器能使速度翻倍吗？
Will doubling the workforce solve the problem? = 增加一倍的员工人数能解决问题吗？


否定句:
The company did not double its sales this year. = 公司今年的销售额并没有翻一番。
They didn't double check their work before submitting it. = 他们在提交工作之前没有仔细检查。
