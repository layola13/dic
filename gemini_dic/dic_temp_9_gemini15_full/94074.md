impassive:/ɪmˈpæsɪv/| adj.|冷漠的；无表情的；不动声色的|Not feeling or showing emotion; apathetic.  Impassive people do not show their feelings, even when something dramatic is happening. |例句：Her face remained impassive throughout the trial. 她在整个审判过程中都面无表情。

例句：He listened to the bad news with an impassive expression. 他面无表情地听着坏消息。


近义词：unemotional, apathetic, indifferent, stoic, emotionless

反义词：emotional, expressive, passionate, demonstrative


词源:
From Latin *impassīvus, from in- "not" + passīvus "suffering," from passus, past participle of pati "to suffer."  The original sense was "unfeeling," later evolving to the more common meaning of showing no emotion.
