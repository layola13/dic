oenometer:/oʊˈnɑːmɪtər/ | n. | 酒精计 | An oenometer is a type of hydrometer used to measure the alcoholic content of wine.  It works by measuring the density of the wine, which is related to its alcohol content. | The oenometer showed the wine had reached its desired alcohol percentage.  酒精计显示葡萄酒已达到其所需的酒精含量。 | hydrometer, alcoholometer |  (No direct antonym, as it measures a property, not a quality with a direct opposite.) | 名词
