cambric:/ˈkeɪmbrɪk/ | n. |细棉布，细布|A fine, thin, plain-woven cotton fabric, originally from Cambrai, France.  It is known for its softness, smoothness, and often sheerness.  It's used for various purposes, including clothing, bedding, and embroidery.|例句：The delicate cambric handkerchief was a treasured heirloom. 这块精致的细棉布手帕是传家宝。

例句：She chose a cambric dress for the summer wedding. 她为夏日的婚礼选择了一件细布连衣裙。

近义词：batiste, muslin, lawn | 反义词：canvas, denim
