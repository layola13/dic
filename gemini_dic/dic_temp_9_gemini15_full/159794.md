polyglacial: /ˌpɒliˈɡleɪʃəl/| adj. | 多冰期的；多冰川作用的 | Relating to or denoting a period of time during which there were several glacial periods or advances and retreats of glaciers.  |例句：The landscape shows clear evidence of polyglacial activity. 这片景观清晰地展现了多冰期活动的证据。

The polyglacial deposits are complex and difficult to interpret. 多冰期沉积物复杂且难以解释。


近义词：multiglacial | 反义词：interglacial
