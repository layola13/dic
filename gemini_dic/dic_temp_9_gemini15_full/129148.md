no-gooder:/ˈnoʊɡʊdər/| n. |无用的人；坏蛋；不中用的人 |A no-gooder is someone who is considered worthless or bad.  This term is informal and often carries a strong negative connotation. It implies a lack of moral character or competence. |He's a complete no-gooder; he never keeps his promises. 他是个十足的无赖；他从来不遵守诺言。| scoundrel, good-for-nothing, rascal |worthy person, upstanding citizen |名词
