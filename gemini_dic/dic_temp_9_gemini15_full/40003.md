consultatively:/kənˈsʌltətɪvli/| adv.|商议地；咨询地|in a way that involves or seeks consultation; in a manner suggesting consultation or seeking advice.  This adverb describes an action performed in a way that implies discussion, seeking advice, or collaboration.|例句：They discussed the matter consultatively before making a decision.  他们商议之后才做决定。

例句：The team worked consultatively, ensuring everyone's input was considered. 团队协作，确保每个人的意见都被考虑在内。


近义词：deliberatively, collaboratively, cooperatively


反义词：unilaterally, dictatorially, arbitrarily


词性:副词
