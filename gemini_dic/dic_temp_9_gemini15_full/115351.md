manometrical:/ˌmænəˈmɛtrɪkəl/| adj.|测压的；气压计的|Relating to or measured by a manometer.  A manometer is a scientific instrument used to measure pressure.  The term often refers to the measurement of gas pressure.|Example: The manometrical reading indicated a significant pressure drop in the system.  测压读数表明系统压力显著下降。|近义词：pressure-measuring, barometrical (if referring to atmospheric pressure)|反义词：None directly applicable.
