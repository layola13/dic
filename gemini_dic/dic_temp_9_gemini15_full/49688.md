demiurges:/ˈdɛmiɜːrdʒɪz/| n.|造物主，创世主|A demiurge is a being responsible for creating the universe or a significant part of it, often depicted as less powerful than a supreme god.|例句：In some Gnostic traditions, the demiurge is a flawed creator, responsible for the material world's imperfections.  在某些诺斯替教派中，造物主是一个有缺陷的创造者，对物质世界的缺陷负有责任。|近义词：creator, architect, artificer|反义词：destroyer, annihilator|名词
