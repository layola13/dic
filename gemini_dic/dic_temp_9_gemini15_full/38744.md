condolences:/kənˈdoʊlənsɪz/| n. |慰问；吊唁 |Expressions of sympathy and sorrow, especially on the occasion of a death.  Condolences are typically offered to the bereaved family or friends. |例句：Please accept our deepest condolences on the loss of your father. 请接受我们对您父亲去世的最深切慰问。

例句：She offered her condolences to the grieving widow. 她向悲伤的寡妇表示慰问。


近义词：sympathy, commiseration, empathy

反义词：celebration, rejoicing

词性：名词
