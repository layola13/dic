kan:/kæn/| n. vt.|看，看见；审视；查看；理解；照顾| to perceive with the eyes; to examine visually; to understand; to take care of|例句：I can see the mountains from my window. 我能从我的窗户看到山。|例句：The doctor examined the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。|近义词：see, view, observe, understand, attend to|反义词：ignore, neglect, misunderstand
