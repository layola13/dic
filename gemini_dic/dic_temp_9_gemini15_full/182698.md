repackages:/ˌriːˈpækɪdʒɪz/| v. |重新包装；重新组合；重新发布 |To repackage something means to change its appearance or presentation, often to make it more appealing or to target a different market.  This can involve changing the packaging, the marketing materials, or the content itself. |

例句：The company repackaged its product with a new logo and brighter colors. 公司以新的标识和更鲜艳的颜色重新包装了其产品。

例句：The old software was repackaged as a user-friendly mobile app. 旧软件被重新包装成一个用户友好的移动应用程序。


近义词：rebrand, revamp, reformulate, restyle

反义词：leave as is, keep unchanged


词性: 动词
