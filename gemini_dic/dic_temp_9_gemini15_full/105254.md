khans:/kæn/| n.|汗家族；可汗；可汗王朝|The word "khans" refers to a family or group of khans, often used in the context of Central Asian history, particularly referring to the Mongol Khans or other Turkic rulers.  It can also refer to a dynasty or empire ruled by khans.|Example: The Khans ruled a vast empire across Central Asia. 汗家族统治着横跨中亚的广阔帝国。|近义词:chiefs, leaders, rulers |反义词:subjects, ruled
