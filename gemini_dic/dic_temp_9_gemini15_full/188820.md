rushbuckler:/ˈrʌʃˌbʌklər/| n. |轻率的冒险家；挥舞短剑的人|A person who engages in reckless adventures; originally, a swordsman who used a buckler (a small, round shield).  The term evokes a sense of impetuousness and bravado.|Example:  He was a true rushbuckler, always seeking out danger and excitement. 他是个不折不扣的轻率冒险家，总是寻找危险和刺激。|近义词：daredevil, adventurer, swashbuckler|反义词：coward, cautious person|名词
