cant's:/kænt/| v.|不能，不会|The word "cant's" is not a standard English word. It's likely a misspelling or informal contraction of "cannot's," which itself is also generally considered non-standard.  The correct form is "cannot" or the contraction "can't."  The word expresses inability or lack of permission. | He can't come to the party. = 他不能来参加聚会。  They can't finish the project on time. = 他们不能按时完成项目。|could, may, might|can, able|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I can't believe it. = 我不敢相信。
He can't swim. = 他不会游泳。

一般过去时:
I couldn't go to school yesterday. = 我昨天没去上学。
She couldn't find her keys. = 她找不到她的钥匙了。

现在完成时:
I haven't been able to contact him. = 我一直联系不上他。(This uses "haven't been able to" which is a more common and grammatically correct alternative to a hypothetical "can't have" in this context)
She hasn't been able to sleep well recently. = 她最近一直睡不好觉。(Similar to above)


过去完成时:
I couldn't have finished the work without your help. = 没有你的帮助我无法完成这项工作。
They couldn't have known what would happen. = 他们不可能知道会发生什么。


将来时:
I won't be able to attend the meeting. = 我将无法参加会议。
He won't be able to make it on time. = 他将无法准时到达。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would help you. = 如果我能，我会帮助你。
I wish I could go. = 我真希望我能去。


被动语态:

The problem can't be solved easily. = 这个问题不容易解决。


疑问句:

Can she speak French? = 她会说法语吗？


否定句:

They cannot understand. = 他们无法理解。
