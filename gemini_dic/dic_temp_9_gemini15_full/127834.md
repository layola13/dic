nemean:/ˈniːmiən/| adj.|尼米亚的；尼米亚的（尤指与尼米亚狮有关的）|relating to Nemea, a region in ancient Greece famous for the Nemean Games and the Nemean lion.  The adjective is often used in reference to the legendary Nemean lion, slain by Heracles (Hercules) as one of his twelve labors.|The Nemean Games were held in Nemea every two years.|近义词：Nemean lion (尼米亚狮)|反义词：无
