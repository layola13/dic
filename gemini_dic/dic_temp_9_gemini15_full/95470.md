inciter:/ɪnˈsaɪtər/| n. |煽动者；激励者|A person who incites or encourages others to do something, especially something violent or unlawful.  Someone who stirs up trouble or motivates others to action.|例句：The inciter of the riot was finally caught and arrested.  煽动暴乱的人最终被抓捕归案了。


The police are searching for the inciter of the violence. 警方正在搜寻暴力事件的煽动者。|近义词：agitator, instigator, provocateur |反义词：pacifier, peacemaker |名词

词源:
The word "inciter" derives from the verb "incite," which comes from the Old French word "inciter," meaning "to stir up, provoke."  The Old French word itself has roots in the Latin word "incitare," meaning "to urge on, stimulate."
