half-fertileness:/hɑːfˈfɜːrtlənəs/| n. |半育性，部分生育力 |Partial fertility; the ability to reproduce only partially or with difficulty.  The term often implies reduced reproductive capacity compared to a fully fertile individual or species. |例句：The study showed a correlation between environmental toxins and half-fertileness in the local frog population.  这项研究表明，当地的青蛙种群的半育性与环境毒素之间存在关联。


The observed half-fertileness in the hybrid plants was attributed to genetic incompatibility.  杂交植物的半育性归因于基因不相容。|近义词：subfertility, reduced fertility |反义词：full fertility, complete fertility
