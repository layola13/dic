own-brand: /ˈoʊn brænd/ | adj. | 自有品牌，自主品牌 |  Relating to goods sold by a retailer or other business under its own name, rather than a manufacturer's brand.  It refers to products that a company produces and sells under its own name, rather than selling products made by other companies. | Example:  The supermarket's own-brand coffee is surprisingly good. 这家超市的自有品牌咖啡出奇的好。  The clothing store has expanded its range of own-brand clothing. 这家服装店扩大了自有品牌的服装系列。 | 近义词：private label, house brand | 反义词：name-brand, manufacturer's brand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The supermarket stocks many own-brand products. = 这家超市储存许多自有品牌产品。
Our company promotes its own-brand products heavily. = 我们公司大力推广自有品牌产品。


一般过去时:
They launched a new range of own-brand goods last year. = 他们去年推出了一个新的自有品牌商品系列。
The store previously only sold name-brand products, but now features own-brand items as well. = 这家商店以前只销售名牌产品，但现在也提供自有品牌商品。


现在完成时:
The company has successfully built a strong reputation for its own-brand products. = 这家公司已经成功地为其自有品牌产品建立了良好的声誉。
We have expanded our own-brand offerings significantly in recent years. = 近年来，我们大大扩展了自有品牌产品。


过去完成时:
Before the expansion, the company had only offered a limited range of own-brand items. = 在扩张之前，该公司只提供有限范围的自有品牌商品。
They had already established a strong presence in the market with their own-brand line before launching the new product. = 在推出新产品之前，他们已经凭借自有品牌系列在市场上确立了强大的地位。


将来时:
