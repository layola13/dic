/daʊt/ | v. n. |怀疑，疑惑 |To feel uncertain about something; to question the truth or validity of something.  |I doubt his honesty. 我怀疑他的诚实。|He doubted whether she would come. 他怀疑她是否会来。|believe, suspect |certainty, assurance |动词, 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I doubt his intentions. = 我怀疑他的意图。
They doubt the accuracy of the report. = 他们怀疑报告的准确性。

一般过去时:
I doubted his word. = 我不相信他的话。
She doubted her ability to succeed. = 她怀疑自己能否成功。

现在完成时:
I have doubted his motives for a long time. = 我很久以来就怀疑他的动机。
They have doubted the wisdom of the decision. = 他们一直怀疑这个决定的明智性。

过去完成时:
I had doubted his story from the beginning. = 我从一开始就怀疑他的说法。
She had doubted her chances of winning before the race. = 比赛前她就怀疑自己获胜的机会。

将来时:
I will doubt his claims until I see proof. = 在我看到证据之前，我会怀疑他的说法。
They will doubt the results unless further testing is done. = 除非进行进一步的测试，否则他们会怀疑结果。

虚拟语气:
I would doubt his sincerity if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会怀疑他的真诚。
If I hadn't doubted myself, I would have succeeded. = 如果我没有怀疑自己，我就会成功了。

被动语态:
His honesty is doubted by many. = 很多人怀疑他的诚实。
The validity of the study is being doubted by experts. = 专家们正在质疑这项研究的有效性。

疑问句:
Do you doubt my word? = 你怀疑我说的话吗？
Does she doubt his commitment? = 她怀疑他的承诺吗？

否定句:
I don't doubt his capabilities. = 我不怀疑他的能力。
She doesn't doubt his love for her. = 她不怀疑他对她的爱。


PHRASE->
