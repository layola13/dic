houseparties:/ˈhaʊspɑːrtiːz/| n.|家庭聚会，小型派对|Informal gatherings of people, typically at someone's home, often involving socializing, music, and sometimes dancing.|例句：We're having a houseparty this weekend.  我们这个周末要举办一个家庭聚会。

例句：The houseparty got a little out of hand. 家庭聚会有点失控了。|近义词：gathering, party, get-together|反义词：formal event, solo activity
