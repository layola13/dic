rosa:/ˈroʊzə/| /ˈrəʊzə/ | n. | 罗莎；蔷薇；玫瑰色 | A rosa is a type of flower, typically with fragrant petals and a thorny stem.  The word is also used as a color term, referring to a pale pink or reddish-pink hue.  | Example:  She received a beautiful red rosa for her birthday. 她收到了一个美丽的红色玫瑰作为生日礼物。
The walls were painted a delicate rosa. 墙壁漆成了淡粉色。|近义词：rose,  pink (as a color) |反义词： (as a color)  dark,  deep
