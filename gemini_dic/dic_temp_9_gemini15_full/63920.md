epiphyte:/ˈɛpɪfaɪt/| n. |附生植物|A plant that grows on another plant (such as a tree) but is not parasitic.  Epiphytes obtain their nutrients from the air and rain, not from the plant they grow on.|Example: Orchids are a common type of epiphyte.  兰花是一种常见的附生植物。 Many bromeliads are epiphytes in tropical rainforests. 许多凤梨科植物是热带雨林中的附生植物。|近义词：air plant|反义词：parasite
