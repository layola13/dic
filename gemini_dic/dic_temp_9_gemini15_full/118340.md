meridian:/məˈrɪdiən/| n. |子午线；顶点；全盛时期 |A meridian is an imaginary line on the Earth's surface that runs from the North Pole to the South Pole, passing through a particular place.  It also refers to the highest point or culmination of something. |例句：The sun crossed the meridian at noon.  太阳在中午穿过子午线。|例句：Her career reached its meridian in the 1990s. 她的事业在20世纪90年代达到顶峰。|近义词： zenith, peak, apex, culmination |反义词： nadir, low point, bottom
