hydrophytology:/ˌhaɪdroʊfaɪˈtɒlədʒi/| n. |水生植物学|The study of aquatic plants, including their morphology, physiology, ecology, and taxonomy.  It encompasses the investigation of how these plants adapt to and thrive in various aquatic environments.|Example:  Hydrophytology is a crucial field for understanding wetland ecosystems and their conservation.|近义词：aquatic botany|反义词：None
