convolvuluses: /kɒnvɒlvjuːləsiːz/ | n. |旋花属植物（复数）|  The plural of convolvulus, referring to plants of the genus Convolvulus, characterized by twining stems and trumpet-shaped flowers.  These plants are commonly known as bindweeds or morning glories, though not all species belong to the morning glory family.  | Example: The field was ablaze with a profusion of convolvuluses in various shades of pink and purple.  田野里盛开着各种粉色和紫色的旋花，美不胜收。|近义词: bindweeds, morning glories (some species)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it refers to a group of plants)|名词
