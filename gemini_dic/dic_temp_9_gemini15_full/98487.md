installments:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənts/ | n. |分期付款；分期连载 |  Payments made at regular intervals to discharge a debt or purchase an item, or parts of a serial publication issued periodically.  | Example:  He bought the car in installments over three years. 他分三年付清了汽车款。

Example: The novel was published in installments. 这部小说分期连载出版。


近义词: payments, series, parts


反义词: lump sum, single payment


词性:名词
