limelighted:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtɪd/| adj.|聚光灯下的；备受瞩目的|Being the focus of attention; in the public eye.|例句：The actress felt limelighted after her successful Broadway debut.  这位女演员在百老汇首演成功后备受瞩目。|例句：The newly elected president was constantly limelighted by the media. 新当选的总统不断受到媒体的关注。|近义词：famous, celebrated, prominent, renowned|反义词：obscure, unknown, unnoticed
