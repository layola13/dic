salvational:/ˌsælˈveɪʃənəl/| adj.| 救世的；带来救赎的 | Relating to salvation or the act of saving someone or something from destruction or danger.  It often implies a spiritual or religious context, but can also be used in a more secular sense. |例句：The church offered salvational messages of hope and redemption. 教堂提供了带来救赎的希望和救赎的信息。

The new policy was seen as a salvational measure to prevent economic collapse.  这项新政策被视为防止经济崩溃的救世措施。|近义词：redemptive, saving, restorative |反义词：damning, destructive, ruinous
