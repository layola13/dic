proach:/prəʊtʃ/|v.|接近；处理；着手；|to come near or nearer to something or someone in space or time.  To begin to deal with a problem or task.|例句：He approached the house cautiously. 他谨慎地走近房子。

He approached the problem systematically. 他系统地处理这个问题。|近义词：near, address, tackle|反义词：avoid, evade, shun|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He approaches the task with enthusiasm. 他热情地处理这项任务。
She approaches her work methodically. 她有条不紊地工作。


一般过去时:
The army approached the city.  军队逼近了城市。
He approached the problem from a different angle. 他从不同的角度来解决这个问题。


现在完成时:
She has approached the issue several times. 她已经多次尝试解决这个问题。
We have approached the deadline. 我们快到截止日期了。


过去完成时:
They had approached the summit when the storm hit. 他们快到达山顶时暴风雨来了。
She had approached the problem unsuccessfully before. 她之前尝试解决这个问题但未成功。


将来时:
He will approach the subject carefully. 他会谨慎地谈论这个话题。
We will approach the building from the west side. 我们将从西侧接近那栋建筑物。


虚拟语气:
If I approached the problem differently, I might succeed. 如果我换个方法处理这个问题，我可能会成功。
I wish I had approached the situation more calmly. 我真希望当时我能更冷静地处理这种情况。


被动语态:
The problem was approached with caution.  这个问题被谨慎地处理。
The project is being approached systematically. 这个项目正在系统地进行中。


疑问句:
How did you approach the challenge? 你是如何应对这个挑战的？
How should we approach this task? 我们应该如何处理这项任务？


否定句:
He did not approach the subject directly. 他没有直接谈论这个话题。
They did not approach the problem effectively. 他们没有有效地解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
approach to：方法，途径
make approaches to：尝试接近
a new approach：新方法
a different approach：不同的方法
a scientific approach：科学的方法
