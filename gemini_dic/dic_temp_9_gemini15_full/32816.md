chokeable: /ˈtʃoʊkəbl/ | adj. |  令人窒息的；哽咽的；难以呼吸的 | Capable of being choked; causing a choking sensation.  Something that is chokeable can cause someone to choke or have difficulty breathing. | 例句：The thick smoke was chokeable.  浓烟令人窒息。

The air in the room was so thick with dust, it felt chokeable.  房间里的空气充满了灰尘，令人窒息。


近义词：suffocating, stifling, strangling

反义词：breathable, refreshing


词源:  Derived from the verb "choke," ultimately from Old English *ceocan, meaning "to gasp, choke".  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
