dwindles:/ˈdwɪndl/ | v. | 逐渐减少；衰落 | To gradually decrease in size, amount, or strength. |例句：His savings dwindled to almost nothing. 他的积蓄几乎减少到零了。 |例句：The number of students dwindled after the school closed. 学校关闭后，学生人数减少了。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, fade |反义词：increase, grow, expand, augment |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His hope dwindles day by day. = 他的希望日渐消逝。
The supply of water dwindles during the drought. = 干旱期间，水供应减少了。

一般过去时:
The population of the village dwindled over the years. = 多年来，村庄的人口减少了。
Her energy dwindled after a long day's work. = 长时间工作后，她的精力减少了。

现在完成时:
Our food supplies have dwindled considerably. = 我们的食物供应已大大减少。
His influence has dwindled since he lost his job. = 自从他失业后，他的影响力就下降了。

过去完成时:
By the time they reached the summit, their supplies had dwindled to almost nothing. = 当他们到达山顶时，他们的补给几乎消耗殆尽。
The company's profits had dwindled significantly before the new CEO took over. = 在新任首席执行官上任之前，公司的利润已大幅减少。

将来时:
The ice caps will dwindle further with global warming. = 随着全球变暖，冰盖将会进一步减少。
His enthusiasm will dwindle if he doesn't see any results. = 如果他看不到任何结果，他的热情就会减退。

虚拟语气:
If the funding dwindled, the project would be cancelled. = 如果资金减少，项目将被取消。
I wish my patience wouldn't dwindle so quickly. = 我希望我的耐心不会这么快就消磨殆尽。

被动语态:
The army was dwindled by disease and desertion. = 军队因疾病和逃亡而减少。
Resources are being dwindled by overconsumption. = 资源因过度消费而减少。

疑问句:
Is the number of tourists dwindling? = 游客数量是否正在减少？
Are your funds dwindling rapidly? = 你的资金正在迅速减少吗？

否定句:
The company's profits did not dwindle last year. = 该公司去年的利润没有减少。
His determination did not dwindle despite the setbacks. = 尽管遇到挫折，他的决心并没有减弱。


PHRASE->

dwindle to nothing:减少到零
dwindle in number:数量减少
dwindle to a trickle:减少到涓涓细流
