/dɪˈkriːərz/ | n. | 下令者；裁决者 | Those who issue decrees or make authoritative decisions.  They are often in positions of power and authority, responsible for establishing rules and regulations.  | Example: The decreers of the ancient empire enforced strict laws.  古代帝国的法令发布者执行着严格的法律。 |近义词：deciders, rulers, judges, legislators | 反义词：obeyers, followers, subjects
