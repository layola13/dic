词源:
The word "precedentless" is formed from the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before"), "cedent" (related to the Latin verb "cedere," meaning "to yield, go, proceed"), and the suffix "-less" (meaning "without").  Therefore, it literally means "without a preceding example."
