observants:/əbˈzɜːrvənts/ | adj. | 观察仔细的；善于观察的 | Carefully watching and noticing things; perceptive.  | The observant detective noticed a crucial detail that others missed.  这位细心的侦探注意到其他人错过的关键细节。 | perceptive, watchful, attentive | unobservant, inattentive, oblivious | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is observant of his surroundings. 他仔细观察周围环境。
She is observant and notices even small details. 她很细心，甚至注意到很小的细节。


一般过去时:
The teacher was observant of the students' behavior. 老师仔细观察着学生的行为。
He was observant during the meeting, taking notes on everything. 他在会议期间很细心，把一切都记了下来。


现在完成时:
She has been observant throughout her career, leading to many breakthroughs.  她职业生涯始终保持着敏锐的观察力，这带来了许多突破。
They have been observant of the market trends for years. 他们多年来一直密切关注市场趋势。


过去完成时:
He had been observant of her behavior for weeks before he confronted her. 在与她对质之前，他已经观察了她几周的行为。
They had been observant of the situation for months before taking action. 在采取行动之前，他们已经观察了几个月的情况。


将来时:
She will be observant of the children's reactions to the new toy. 她将会仔细观察孩子们对新玩具的反应。
He will be observant of the changing weather patterns. 他将密切关注不断变化的天气模式。


虚拟语气:
If he were more observant, he would have noticed the error. 如果他更细心的话，他就会注意到这个错误。
I wish I had been more observant during the lecture. 我希望我在讲座期间更细心一些。


被动语态:
The details were observed by the observant scientist.  细心的科学家观察到了这些细节。(被动语态更强调“细节”被观察)
His behavior was not observed by anyone observant enough.  没有人足够细心观察到他的行为。(被动语态强调“行为”没有被观察到)


疑问句:
Was he observant enough to catch the thief? 他是否足够细心以抓住小偷？
Are you observant enough to spot the mistake in the painting? 你是否足够细心能发现绘画中的错误？


否定句:
He was not observant enough to notice the subtle change. 他不够细心，没有注意到细微的变化。
She wasn't observant of the danger signs. 她没有注意到危险信号。
