reillustrating:/ˌriːˈɪləstreɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新举例说明；再次阐明|To illustrate again or anew; to explain or clarify something by providing further examples or details.|例句：The professor reillustrated the concept using a different analogy. 教授用不同的比喻再次阐明了这个概念。

例句：The author reillustrated the point with a compelling anecdote. 作者用一个引人入胜的轶事再次说明了这一点。


近义词：reexplaining, clarifying, elucidating, demonstrating

反义词：obscuring, confusing, muddling

词性：动词
