rehabilitated:/ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪtɪd/| v. | 康复；修复；恢复名誉 | To restore someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness; to restore something to good condition. |例句：The prison system aims to rehabilitate offenders and help them reintegrate into society.  监狱系统旨在让罪犯改过自新，并帮助他们重新融入社会。


The injured athlete underwent intensive physical therapy to be rehabilitated. 受伤的运动员接受了强化体能治疗以康复。

近义词：restore, recover, reclaim, reform |反义词：destroy, damage, ruin
