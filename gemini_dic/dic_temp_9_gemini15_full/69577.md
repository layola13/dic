felsic:/ˈfɛlsɪk/| adj.|富硅铝的，长英质的|Descriptive of igneous rocks that are rich in feldspar and silica.  These rocks are typically light in color and have a lower density than mafic rocks.  The term is derived from "feldspar" and "silica".|例句：Granite is a felsic igneous rock. 花岗岩是一种富硅铝的火成岩。|例句：The felsic minerals in this sample are easily identifiable.  这个样本中的长英质矿物很容易识别。|近义词：silicic|反义词：mafic
