repatrol:/ˌriːpəˈtroʊl/| v.|再次巡逻；重新巡逻|To patrol again or anew.  It implies a return to a previously patrolled area or a resumption of patrolling after an interruption.|例句：The police had to repatrol the area after the disturbance. = 骚乱之后，警察不得不再次巡逻该地区。
例句：The soldiers repatrolled the border after the ceasefire. = 停火后，士兵们重新巡逻了边境。|近义词：revisit, patrol again|反义词：cease patrolling, withdraw
