pastes:/peɪsts/ | n. |浆糊；糊状物；粘贴；(数据等的)粘贴|A paste is a thick, sticky substance, often used for adhering things together.  It can also refer to the act of pasting something. |例句：He used wallpaper paste to hang the new wallpaper. 他用墙纸胶粘贴新的墙纸。

例句：She pasted the photo into her scrapbook. 她把照片粘贴到她的剪贴簿里。


近义词：glue, adhesive, cement | 反义词：N/A


词源:  From Old French *paster (to paste), from paster (to spread).  Ultimately derived from Latin *pastum (something spread), from pastus (fed, pastured). The connection lies in the idea of spreading a substance like food (pasture) or glue.
