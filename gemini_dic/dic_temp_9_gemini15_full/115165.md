maniac:/ˈmeɪniæk/| n. adj.| 狂人；狂热者；精神病患者 | A maniac is a person who is uncontrollably enthusiastic or passionate about something, or someone who is mentally ill and potentially violent.  The word can be used figuratively to describe someone exhibiting extreme behavior. |例句：He's a fitness maniac, spending hours at the gym every day. 他是个健身狂，每天在健身房花上数小时。

例句：The driver was described as a maniac after causing the accident. 事故发生后，司机被形容为一个疯子。


近义词：lunatic, fanatic, enthusiast, psychopath |反义词：sane, rational, calm, composed |词性: noun, adjective


USAGE->

She is a work maniac. = 她是个工作狂。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
