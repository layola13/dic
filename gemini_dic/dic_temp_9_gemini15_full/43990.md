criminal:/ˈkrɪmɪnəl/| adj., n.|罪犯；刑事的；犯罪的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "criminal" describes something related to crime or having the characteristics of a crime. As a noun, it refers to a person who has committed a crime.

例句:
The criminal justice system needs reform. = 刑事司法制度需要改革。

He was convicted as a criminal. = 他被判为罪犯。


近义词:
culprit, offender, felon, delinquent

反义词:
innocent, law-abiding


词性:
adjective, noun
