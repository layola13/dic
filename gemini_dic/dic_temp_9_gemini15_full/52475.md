dieters:/ˈdaɪəɾz/| n. |节食者，减肥者 |People who are following a diet to lose weight or for health reasons. They often restrict their food intake or make specific choices about what they eat.|例句：Many dieters find it difficult to stick to their plans. 许多节食者发现很难坚持他们的计划。|例句：The magazine featured interviews with several successful dieters. 这本杂志刊登了几位成功节食者的采访。|近义词：weight-losers, slimming individuals|反义词：glutton, gourmand
