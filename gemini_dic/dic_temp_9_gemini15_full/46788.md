dangling:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ/| adj.|悬垂的；未完成的；不确定的|  Hanging loosely or unsupported; not fixed or attached securely; left unresolved or incomplete.|The dangling participle is a common grammatical error.  悬垂分词是一个常见的语法错误。|近义词：hanging, loose, unattached, unresolved|反义词：secure, fixed, attached, resolved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chandelier is dangling precariously. = 吊灯摇摇欲坠地悬挂着。
That loose thread is dangling from your shirt. = 你衬衫上有一根线松垂着。


一般过去时:
The sign dangled from the broken post. = 标志摇摇晃晃地挂在断掉的柱子上。
He dangled his feet over the edge of the boat. = 他把脚悬在船边。


现在完成时:
The earring has been dangling from its hook all day. = 耳环整天都挂在钩子上。
The question has been dangling unanswered for weeks. = 这个问题已经悬而未决数周了。


过去完成时:
The painting had been dangling by a single thread before it fell. = 画之前是用一根线悬挂着的，然后掉了下来。
The matter had been dangling unresolved for months. = 这件事情已经悬而未决好几个月了。


将来时:
The decorations will be dangling from the ceiling. = 装饰品将悬挂在天花板上。
The decision will be dangling in the balance until next week. = 决定将悬而未决直到下周。


虚拟语气:
If the rope were dangling, I would not cross the bridge. = 如果绳子悬挂着，我不会过桥。
If the problem had been dangling, we would have addressed it sooner. = 如果问题悬而未决，我们会更早地解决它。


被动语态:
The ornament was dangled before the child. = 装饰品在孩子面前晃动。
The possibility was dangled before the investors. = 机会摆在投资者面前。


疑问句:
Is that picture still dangling crookedly? = 那张画还歪歪扭扭地悬挂着吗？
Is the matter still dangling unresolved? = 这件事仍然悬而未决吗？


否定句:
The lights were not dangling dangerously. = 灯光没有危险地摇晃。
The decision was not dangling, it was clear. = 决定不是悬而未决的，它是明确的。
