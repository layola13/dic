decimalization:/ˌdesɪməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 十进制化；改用十进制 | The process of converting a system of numbers or currency to a decimal system.  This often involves changing the base of the number system from something else (like a duodecimal system, base-12) to base-10.  It can also refer to the adoption of decimal currency. | Example: The decimalization of the British currency in 1971 led to the phasing out of pounds, shillings, and pence.  英国货币在1971年的十进制化导致英镑、先令和便士的逐步淘汰。|近义词：decimation (in the sense of widespread destruction, this is not a perfect synonym)|反义词：N/A (no direct opposite)|
