expresses:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪz/ | v. |表达；表示；快递|To convey a thought, feeling, or idea; to send something by courier.|例句：His painting expresses his deep emotions.他的画表达了他深沉的情感。

例句：The company expresses packages overnight.这家公司提供隔夜快递服务。

|近义词：conveys, communicates, transmits,  ships|反义词：suppresses, conceals, hides|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expresses his opinions freely. = 他自由地表达自己的意见。
She expresses her gratitude regularly. = 她定期表达她的感激之情。


一般过去时:
He expressed his concerns to the manager. = 他向经理表达了他的担忧。

现在完成时:

She has expressed her apologies for the mistake. = 她已经为这个错误道歉了。


过去完成时:

She had expressed her worries to her parents. = 她已经向她的父母表达了她的担忧。


将来时:
