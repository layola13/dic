prehardened:/ˌpriːˈhɑːrdənd/ | adj. | 预硬化的；预先硬化的 |  Describes a material, especially metal, that has undergone a preliminary hardening process before further processing or use. This reduces the need for additional hardening later and improves dimensional stability.  | Example: The prehardened steel was ideal for the precision machining required. 预先硬化的钢材非常适合所需的精密加工。 |近义词：tempered, hardened, pre-stressed | 反义词：annealed, soft
