selected:/sɪˈlɛktɪd/ | adj. | 被选择的，精选的 | chosen, picked out from a group; considered to be the best or most suitable.  |例句：The selected candidates will be notified by email.  被选中的候选人将通过电子邮件收到通知。|例句：This is a selection of our best-selling products. 这是我们畅销产品的精选。|近义词：chosen, preferred, picked |反义词：rejected, unwanted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The selected items are on display. = 被选择的商品正在展出。
The selected candidate possesses strong leadership skills. = 被选中的候选人拥有强大的领导能力。


一般过去时:
The committee selected three finalists. = 委员会选出了三位决赛选手。
He selected a red car from the showroom. = 他从展厅里选了一辆红色的车。


现在完成时:
We have selected a new CEO. = 我们已经选定了一位新的首席执行官。
She has selected her wedding dress. = 她已经选好了她的婚纱。


过去完成时:
They had selected the location before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经选定了地点。
The artist had selected his palette before he began painting. = 艺术家在开始作画之前就已经选好了他的调色板。


将来时:
The jury will select the winner tomorrow. = 评审团将在明天选出获胜者。
The school will select students for the exchange program. = 学校将为交换项目挑选学生。


虚拟语气:
If I were selected, I would be very happy. = 如果我被选中，我会非常高兴。
I wish I had been selected for the team. = 我真希望自己被选进这个团队。


被动语态:
The winner was selected by a panel of judges. = 获胜者是由评委小组选出的。
The books were selected for their educational value. = 这些书因其教育价值而被选中。


疑问句:
Which candidate was selected? = 选中的是哪位候选人？
Have the finalists been selected yet? = 决赛选手已经选出来了吗？


否定句:
He was not selected for the job. = 他没有被选中担任这份工作。
The proposal was not selected for funding. = 该提案没有被选中获得资金。
