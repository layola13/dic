gullet:/ˈɡʌlət/| n.|食道；咽喉；管状物|The gullet is the part of the alimentary canal that connects the pharynx to the stomach. It is also known as the esophagus.  It's a muscular tube that transports food from the mouth to the stomach through peristalsis.|例句：The food went down his gullet with surprising ease.食物很容易就滑进了他的食道。

The fish was swallowed whole, disappearing down the bird's gullet. 鱼被整条吞下，消失在鸟的食道里。|近义词：esophagus, throat|反义词：None specifically.
