self-provision:/ˌsɛlf prəˈvɪʒən/| n.|自我供应，自行配置|The act of supplying or providing something for oneself; the process of configuring and managing one's own resources or services, especially in a computing context.  |例句：The company encourages self-provisioning of IT resources to empower employees. 公司鼓励员工自行配置IT资源以增强其能力。
The server allows for self-provisioning of virtual machines. 这台服务器允许自行配置虚拟机。|近义词：self-catering, self-sufficiency |反义词：dependence, reliance
