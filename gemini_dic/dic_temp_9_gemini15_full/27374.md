came around:/keɪm əˈraʊnd/ | v. | 改变主意；苏醒；恢复健康；拜访；流行起来 | To change one's opinion; to regain consciousness; to recover from an illness; to visit; to become popular. |


例句：

He initially opposed the plan, but he eventually came around.  = 他起初反对这个计划，但最终改变了主意。

After the accident, it took him a while to come around. = 事故之后，他花了一段时间才苏醒过来。


近义词：changed one's mind, recovered, visited, became popular


反义词：remained unconvinced, stayed unconscious, persisted


词性:动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He comes around every Tuesday. = 他每周二都来。
She comes around to my point of view eventually. = 她最终认同我的观点。

一般过去时:
They came around to see us last week. = 他们上周来看我们了。
He came around after a few minutes. = 他几分钟后苏醒了。

现在完成时:
She has come around to supporting the new policy. = 她已经转而支持这项新政策了。
I have come around to seeing things differently. = 我已经开始用不同的眼光看待事情了。

过去完成时:
He had come around to the idea before the meeting. = 会议之前他已经接受了这个想法。
They had come around to the conclusion that it was unsafe. = 他们已经得出结论，那是不安全的。

将来时:
They will come around to visit us soon. = 他们很快就会来拜访我们。
I think he will come around to our way of thinking. = 我认为他会认同我们的想法。

虚拟语气:
If he came around, I would tell him the truth. = 如果他来了，我会告诉他真相。
I wish she would come around to my way of thinking. = 我希望她能认同我的想法。

被动语态: (不适用被动语态，因为come around是主动行为)


疑问句:
Will they come around to our house tonight? = 他们今晚会来我家吗？
Did he come around to your opinion? = 他认同你的意见了吗？


否定句:
He didn't come around to the meeting. = 他没有来参加会议。
She hasn't come around to seeing my perspective. = 她还没能理解我的观点。


PHRASE->

come around to (something):  改变对某事的看法
come around to (someone's opinion):  认同某人的观点
come around again: 再来一次
come around later: 晚点再来
come around to (a place): 到达某个地方
