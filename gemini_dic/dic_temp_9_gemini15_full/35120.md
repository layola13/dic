cloudflare:/ˈklaʊdˌfleər/| n. |Cloudflare公司；Cloudflare网络|Cloudflare is a global network that provides content delivery network (CDN), domain name system (DNS) services, and DDoS mitigation services. It improves website performance and security.|例句：We use Cloudflare to protect our website from DDoS attacks. 我们使用Cloudflare来保护我们的网站免受DDoS攻击。|近义词：CDN provider, DDoS mitigation service|反义词：无
