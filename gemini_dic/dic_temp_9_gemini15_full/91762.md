hyperemotiveness:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənɪvnəs/ | n. |过度情绪化，情感过激|The tendency to experience and express emotions intensely and frequently, often to an excessive or overwhelming degree.  This can manifest in various ways, including frequent crying, outbursts of anger, or difficulty regulating emotional responses.  It can be a symptom of underlying mental health conditions.|例句：Her hyperemotiveness made it difficult for her to maintain stable relationships.  她的过度情绪化让她难以维持稳定的关系。

例句：His hyperemotiveness was a significant barrier to his professional success. 他的过度情绪化严重阻碍了他的职业发展。


近义词：emotional instability, oversensitivity, emotional dysregulation


反义词：emotional stability, stoicism, emotional control


词性: 名词
