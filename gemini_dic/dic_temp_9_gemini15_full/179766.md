reestimating:/ˌriːˈɛstɪmeɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新估算，重新估计 |The act of estimating again; reassessing a previous estimate.  It involves reviewing the initial assessment and making adjustments based on new information or circumstances. |例句：The project manager is reestimating the costs after discovering unforeseen delays. 项目经理在发现不可预见延误后正在重新估算成本。

例句：We are reestimating the budget due to increased material prices. 由于材料价格上涨，我们正在重新评估预算。


近义词：recalculating, reassessing, reevaluating

反义词：accepting (the initial estimate)


USAGE->

They are reestimating the project's timeline. = 他们正在重新评估项目的进度。
We reestimate the value of the property each year. = 我们每年都会重新评估房产的价值。
