exchanging: /ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | v. | 交换；交易；兑换 | The act of giving something and receiving something else in return.  It often involves mutual benefit or agreement. | 例句：They are exchanging gifts. 他们正在交换礼物。

They are exchanging currencies at the bank. 他们在银行兑换货币。


近义词：swapping, trading, bartering | 反义词：keeping, retaining | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are exchanging ideas. = 他们在交换想法。
We exchange greetings every morning. = 我们每天早上互相问候。

一般过去时:
They exchanged numbers after the meeting. = 会议后他们交换了电话号码。
I exchanged my old car for a new one. = 我用旧车换了一辆新车。

现在完成时:
We have exchanged emails several times. = 我们已经通过邮件交流了好几次。
She has exchanged her winter coat for a spring jacket. = 她已将冬衣换成了春装。

过去完成时:
They had exchanged vows before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经交换了誓言。
Before the trip, we had exchanged currency. = 旅行前，我们已经兑换了货币。

将来时:
We will exchange gifts during the holiday season. = 我们将在假期期间互赠礼物。
They will exchange information with their counterparts. = 他们将与同行交换信息。

虚拟语气:
If we exchanged roles, I think we'd understand each other better. = 如果我们交换角色，我想我们会更好地理解彼此。
I wish we could exchange ideas more freely. = 我希望我们能够更自由地交换想法。

被动语态:
The information was exchanged between the two companies. = 这两家公司之间交换了信息。
The old car was exchanged for a newer model. = 旧车被换成了一款更新的车型。

疑问句:
Are you exchanging currencies? = 你在兑换货币吗？
Will they be exchanging gifts at the party? = 他们会在派对上互赠礼物吗？

否定句:
They didn't exchange contact information. = 他们没有交换联系方式。
We didn't exchange gifts this year. = 今年我们没有互赠礼物。
