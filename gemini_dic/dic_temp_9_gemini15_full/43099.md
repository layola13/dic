cox:/kɒks/| n. v.|舵手；掌舵；(船的)舵柄；|英文解析：A coxswain is the person who steers a boat, especially a racing boat.  The cox can also be the steering mechanism itself. As a verb, it means to steer or guide a boat.|例句：The cox steered the boat expertly through the rough waters.  舵手熟练地驾驶小船穿过波涛汹涌的水域。He coxed the boat to victory. 他掌舵使船获胜。|近义词：steersman, pilot (for boats)|反义词：passenger|词性：n. v.
