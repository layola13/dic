flabby:/ˈflæbi/| adj.|松弛的，软弱的，无力的|Lacking firmness; soft and loose.  Often used to describe muscles that are not toned.  Can also describe something lacking in strength or effectiveness.|例句：His arms were flabby from lack of exercise.  他的手臂因缺乏锻炼而松弛无力。

例句：The government's response to the crisis was flabby and ineffective. 政府对危机的回应软弱无力且无效。


近义词：soft, loose, weak, flaccid, sagging

反义词：firm, toned, strong, muscular, robust
