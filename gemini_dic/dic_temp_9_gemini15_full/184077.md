resigned:/rɪˈzaɪnd/ | adj., v. | 辞职的；顺从的；认命的 |

英文解析:As an adjective, "resigned" describes someone who has accepted a difficult or unpleasant situation without complaining. As a verb, "resigned" means to give up a job or position.

例句:

(adj.) He looked resigned to his fate. = 他看起来对自己的命运认命了。

(adj.)  She accepted the bad news with a resigned sigh. = 她带着无奈的叹息接受了这个坏消息。


(v.) He resigned from his job last week. = 他上周辞职了。

(v.)  She resigned her position after a disagreement with her boss. = 她与老板意见不合后辞职了。


近义词: (adj.) accepting, submissive, passive, acquiescent; (v.) quit, stepped down, left

反义词: (adj.) rebellious, defiant, resistant, protesting; (v.) started, began, joined


词性: adjective, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resigned to his fate. = 他认命了。
She is resigned to working long hours. = 她甘愿加班。

一般过去时:
He resigned from his job last year. = 他去年辞职了。
She resigned from the committee. = 她辞去了委员会的职务。

现在完成时:
He has resigned from his position. = 他已经辞职了。
She has resigned herself to the inevitable. = 她已经认命了。

过去完成时:
He had resigned before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前他已经辞职了。
She had resigned herself to failure. = 她已经认命自己会失败了。

将来时:
He will resign next month. = 他下个月将辞职。
She will resign if she doesn't get a raise. = 如果她没有加薪，她将会辞职。

虚拟语气:
If he were resigned to his fate, he would be happier. = 如果他认命，他会更快乐。
I wish I were more resigned to the situation. = 我希望我对这种情况更能接受。

被动语态:
The position was resigned by him. = 这个职位被他辞去了。（较少用）
His resignation was accepted. = 他的辞职申请被接受了。

疑问句:
Has he resigned yet? = 他辞职了吗？
Will she resign if she's offered a better job? = 如果给她更好的工作机会，她会辞职吗？

否定句:
He hasn't resigned yet. = 他还没辞职。
She didn't resign from her job. = 她没有辞职。
