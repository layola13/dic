mulled:/mʌld/| v.|仔细考虑，深思熟虑；温(酒)|to think about something carefully for a long time; to heat (wine or other drinks) with spices.|例句：I mulled over the implications of his proposal. 我仔细考虑了他提议的含义。|例句：We mulled some wine to warm up on a cold evening.在一个寒冷的晚上，我们温了一些酒来暖和身子。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, consider, brew|反义词：ignore, disregard
