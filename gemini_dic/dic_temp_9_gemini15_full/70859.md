finlands:/ˈfɪnlændz/| n.|芬兰（复数）|The plural form of "Finland," referring to multiple instances or aspects of the country.| 例句：The research compared the education systems of several finlands. 这项研究比较了几个芬兰的教育体系。|近义词: Finlands (alternative spelling) |反义词: None.


USAGE->
This word is a plural noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense changes don't apply.  The usage is simply to refer to multiple instances related to Finland.


例句：
The discussions involved representatives from various finlands.  讨论涉及来自不同芬兰地区的代表。

Several finlands participated in the international trade agreement.  几个芬兰参与了国际贸易协定。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "finlands" as a plural noun.  Phrases would relate to Finland itself, not the plural form.  For example:


"Made in Finland"  =  “芬兰制造”
"Visit Finland"  =  “游览芬兰”
"The people of Finland" = "芬兰人民"
"Politics of Finland" = "芬兰政治"
"Economy of Finland" = "芬兰经济"
