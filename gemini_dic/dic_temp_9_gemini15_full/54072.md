diseases:/dɪˈziːzɪz/| n.|疾病，病症|The plural form of "disease," referring to multiple illnesses or ailments.  It encompasses a wide range of conditions affecting living organisms, from minor infections to chronic and life-threatening illnesses. |例句：Many diseases are preventable through vaccination. 许多疾病可以通过接种疫苗来预防。
例句：Heart disease is a leading cause of death in many countries. 心脏病是许多国家的主要死因。|近义词：ailments, illnesses, sicknesses, disorders|反义词：health, wellness
