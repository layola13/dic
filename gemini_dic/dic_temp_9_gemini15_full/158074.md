plasterer:/ˈplæstərər/| n. |泥瓦匠，灰泥匠 |A plasterer is a person whose job is to apply plaster to walls and ceilings.  They work with various types of plaster to create smooth, even surfaces. |The plasterer skillfully applied the finishing coat to the walls.|近义词：renderer, stucco worker |反义词：无
