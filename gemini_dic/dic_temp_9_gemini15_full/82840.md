gulfed:/ɡʌlft/| vt.|吞没；吞噬；|to swallow up or engulf; to be overwhelmed by (something, typically something negative) |例句：The tsunami gulfed the coastal villages. 海啸吞噬了沿海村庄。

The darkness gulfed his hope. 黑暗吞噬了他的希望。


近义词：engulf, swallow, overwhelm, submerge|反义词：save, rescue, liberate


USAGE->

The ocean gulfes the coastline. = 海洋吞噬着海岸线。
He gulfes his sorrows in alcohol. 他借酒消愁。


一般过去时:
The quicksand gulfed the traveler. = 流沙吞没了旅行者。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
