matricidal:/ˌmætrɪˈsaɪdəl/ | adj. |杀母的；弑母的 | Relating to or characterized by the killing of one's mother.  The term often implies a deliberate and premeditated act, rather than accidental killing. |例句：The novel explores the matricidal impulses of the protagonist. 这部小说探讨了主人公杀母的冲动。

例句：The police investigated the possibility of a matricidal crime. 警方调查了可能是杀母罪的可能性。


近义词：mother-murdering, parricidal (if the parent is a mother)


反义词：filial (showing respect and affection to parents)


词性：形容词
