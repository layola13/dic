saver:/ˈseɪvər/| n.|救星，节省者，节约物|a person or thing that saves someone or something from harm or destruction; something that saves money or resources|例句：He was a saver of lives during the war. 他在战争中挽救了许多人的生命。  The new energy-saving light bulbs are real savers.  新的节能灯泡是真正的节约物。|近义词：rescuer, preserver, economizer |反义词：waster, squanderer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He saves money every month. = 他每个月都存钱。
She saves the children from danger. = 她把孩子们从危险中救了出来。


一般过去时:
He saved a drowning child. = 他救了一个溺水儿童。
They saved a lot of money on their vacation. = 他们在假期省了很多钱。


现在完成时:
She has saved enough money for a new car. = 她已经攒够了买新车的钱。
They have saved countless lives through their charity work. = 通过慈善工作，他们已经挽救了无数人的生命。


过去完成时:
He had saved the document before the computer crashed. = 电脑崩溃之前他已经保存了文件。
They had saved enough money to buy a house before the interest rates rose. = 在利率上升之前，他们已经攒够了买房子的钱。


将来时:
She will save the world one day. = 她有一天会拯救世界。
They will save money by buying in bulk. = 他们会通过批量购买来省钱。


虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a house. = 如果我存了更多的钱，我就能买房子了。
If he had saved her, she would still be alive. = 如果他救了她，她现在还活着。


被动语态:
Many lives were saved by the firefighters. = 消防员救了很多人的命。
The money was saved for a rainy day. = 这笔钱是为以备不时之需而存的。


疑问句:
Did you save the file? = 你保存文件了吗？
How much money do you save each month? = 你每个月存多少钱？


否定句:
He didn't save any money last month. = 他上个月没有存任何钱。
She didn't save the cat from the tree. = 她没有把猫从树上救下来。
