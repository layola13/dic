duodecimo:/ˌduːoʊˈdɛsɪmoʊ/| n. |十二开本的书；十二开本|A duodecimo is a book or pamphlet printed on sheets of paper folded so as to form twelve leaves.  It is often abbreviated as 12mo. The term refers to the size and format of the book, not its content.|Example:  The rare book was a beautifully preserved duodecimo edition of Shakespeare's sonnets.|近义词:octavo (八开本), quarto (四开本)|反义词:folio (对开本)
