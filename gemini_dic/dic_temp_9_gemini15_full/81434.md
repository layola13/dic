/ˈɡreɪtərz/| n.|擦菜板；磨碎器|A grater is a kitchen utensil used for grating food, such as cheese, vegetables, or nuts.  It typically has a surface with many small holes or sharp edges.|She used a grater to shred the carrots for the salad. 她用擦菜板把胡萝卜擦丝做沙拉。|shredder, rasp|smooth, whole


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The chef uses a grater to prepare the ginger. = 厨师用擦菜板准备生姜。
We grate cheese onto our pasta. = 我们把奶酪磨碎到意大利面上。


一般过去时:
I grated the potatoes for the hash browns. = 我把土豆磨碎做土豆饼。
She grated the Parmesan cheese over the pizza. = 她把帕尔马干酪磨碎撒在比萨饼上。


现在完成时:
He has grated all the carrots for the soup. = 他已经把所有胡萝卜都磨碎做汤了。
We have grated enough cheese for the lasagna. = 我们已经磨碎了足够的奶酪做千层面了。


过去完成时:
They had grated the nutmeg before adding it to the sauce. = 他们在加入肉豆蔻到酱汁之前已经磨碎了它。
She had grated the vegetables before she started cooking. = 她开始做饭之前已经把蔬菜磨碎了。


将来时:
I will grate the ginger for the stir-fry tomorrow. = 我明天将磨碎生姜做炒菜。
She will grate the zucchini for the bread. = 她将把西葫芦磨碎做面包。


虚拟语气:
If I had a grater, I would grate the cheese myself. = 如果我有一个擦菜板，我会自己磨碎奶酪。
I wish I had grated the lemon zest before it dried out. = 我希望我早点磨碎柠檬皮，在它变干之前。

疑问句:

否定句:

She hadn't grated the nutmeg before she added the spices. = 她加香料之前没有磨碎肉豆蔻。
