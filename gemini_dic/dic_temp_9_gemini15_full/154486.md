peroxides:/ˈpɛrɒksaɪdz/| n. |过氧化物|A peroxide is a compound containing the peroxide anion, which has the chemical formula O₂²⁻.  It's characterized by an oxygen-oxygen single bond. Peroxides are often used as bleaching agents, disinfectants, and oxidizers.  They are generally reactive and can be harmful if mishandled.|Example: Hydrogen peroxide (H₂O₂) is a common peroxide used as a disinfectant and antiseptic. 	过氧化氢（H₂O₂）是一种常用的过氧化物，用作消毒剂和防腐剂。|近义词：oxidant, bleaching agent|反义词：reductant
