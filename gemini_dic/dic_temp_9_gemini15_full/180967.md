reimburser:/ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrsər/ | n. | 报销者；偿还者 | A person or entity that reimburses expenses.  Someone who pays back money that has been spent. | The company is a reliable reimburser of travel expenses. 这家公司是可靠的差旅费报销者。  The reimburser promptly sent the check. 报销者迅速寄来了支票。 |近义词：payer, refunder | 反义词：debtor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reimburses employees for business travel. = 公司报销员工的商务旅行费用。
He regularly reimburses his assistant for parking fees. = 他定期报销他助理的停车费。

一般过去时:
They reimbursed me for the conference fees. = 他们报销了我的会议费。
The insurance company reimbursed her for the medical bills. = 保险公司报销了她的医疗费用。

现在完成时:
My employer has reimbursed me for all my expenses. = 我的雇主已经报销了我所有的费用。
She has been reimbursed for the damage to her car. = 她的汽车损坏已经得到了赔偿。

过去完成时:
They had reimbursed him before he even submitted the claim. = 甚至在他提交索赔之前，他们就已经报销了他的费用。
The company had reimbursed her for the lost luggage before she arrived home. = 她回家之前，公司已经报销了她丢失的行李费用。

将来时:
The government will reimburse farmers for their crop losses. = 政府将报销农民的作物损失。
They will reimburse you once the invoice is received. = 收到发票后，他们将报销您的费用。

虚拟语气:
If they reimbursed me, I would be able to afford the trip. = 如果他们报销我的费用，我就能负担得起这次旅行。
I wish the company would reimburse me for the extra costs. = 我希望公司能报销我的额外费用。

被动语态:
Expenses are reimbursed monthly by the department. = 部门每月报销费用。
The damages will be reimbursed by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司赔偿。

疑问句:

Does your employer reimburse you for commuting expenses? = 你的雇主会报销你的通勤费用吗？

否定句:

They will not reimburse expenses without proper documentation. = 没有适当的凭证，他们将不会报销费用。
