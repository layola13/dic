laryngologist:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |喉科医生|A laryngologist is a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and disorders of the larynx (voice box), pharynx (throat), and related structures.  They often deal with conditions affecting voice, swallowing, and breathing.|例句：I have an appointment with a laryngologist next week to address my persistent hoarseness.  下周我预约了喉科医生，解决我持续的沙哑问题。|近义词：ENT specialist, otolaryngologist |反义词：N/A
