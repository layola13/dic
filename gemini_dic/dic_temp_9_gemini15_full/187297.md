rock-climb:/ˈrɒkˌklaɪm/|/ˈrɑːkˌklaɪm/| vt. & n.|攀岩|To ascend a rock face or cliff using specialized equipment and techniques.  Rock climbing is a physically and mentally demanding sport that requires strength, endurance, and skill. |例句：He enjoys rock climbing in Yosemite National Park. 他喜欢在约塞米蒂国家公园攀岩。

例句：She is training to rock climb El Capitan.  她在训练攀登酋长岩。


近义词：mount, scale, ascend |反义词：descend
