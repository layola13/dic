gawp:/ɡɔːp/| v.|呆瞪；傻看；张着嘴傻看|To stare with one's mouth open, typically in a state of amazement or stupidity.|例句：He stood gawping at the accident. 他张着嘴呆呆地看着事故现场。|例句：The tourists gawped at the magnificent scenery.  游客们目瞪口呆地看着壮丽的景色。|近义词：stare, gape, ogle|反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gawps at the television all evening. = 他整个晚上都呆呆地看着电视。
They gawp at the strange sight. = 他们张着嘴看着奇怪的景象。


一般过去时:
She gawped at the painting in astonishment. = 她惊讶地盯着那幅画。
The children gawped at the clown's performance. = 孩子们目瞪口呆地看着小丑的表演。

过去完成时:
