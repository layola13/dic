minimal:/ˈmɪnɪməl/| adj. | 最小的；最低限度的；极简主义的 |  Minimal means the least possible; the smallest amount. It can refer to size, extent, or importance.  The adjective also describes a style characterized by simplicity and absence of ornamentation, often seen in art and design. |

例句：
The minimal damage to the car was surprising given the severity of the accident.  | 事故严重，但汽车损坏程度最小，这令人惊讶。

The project requires minimal effort. | 这个项目只需要最少的努力。


近义词：least, smallest, bare minimum, rudimentary

反义词：maximal, maximum, extensive, abundant
