词源:  The word is a compound of "club" (referring to a heavy, blunt weapon) and "fisted" (referring to a fist, suggesting a forceful, unrefined grip or action).  It evokes the image of someone using a club with a clumsy, heavy-handed manner.  The implication is not just physical clumsiness but also a lack of finesse and sensitivity in social interactions.
