shellac:/ˈʃɛlək/ | n. v. |虫胶；清漆；用虫胶涂饰 | A resinous substance secreted by certain insects, used to make varnish and other products.  Also, the varnish itself. |例句：The antique furniture was coated with shellac. 这件古董家具涂上了虫胶。

The artist used shellac to seal the painting. 艺术家用虫胶来封固画作。


近义词：varnish, lacquer, polish | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying the absence of a shellac coating could be considered antonyms (e.g., unvarnished, untreated).

词源:
