postdoctoral:/ˌpoʊstˈdɑːktərəl/| adj. |博士后的；博士后阶段的| relating to the period of study and research after receiving a doctorate.  A postdoctoral researcher conducts independent research, often under the supervision of a more senior faculty member. |例句：She secured a postdoctoral fellowship at Harvard University. 她获得了哈佛大学的博士后奖学金。


例句：He is currently pursuing postdoctoral research in bioengineering. 他目前正在从事生物工程方面的博士后研究。


近义词：postgraduate (if referring to the period of study), post-doctoral


反义词：undergraduate (opposite stage of education)


词性:形容词
