drillable:/ˈdrɪləbl/ | adj. | 可钻孔的，适于钻孔的 | Capable of being drilled; suitable for drilling.  This term typically refers to materials or surfaces that can be penetrated by a drill bit without significant difficulty or damage.  | Example: The rock formation was found to be drillable, allowing for the extraction of oil.  这块岩层被发现是可钻孔的，允许开采石油。


近义词：perforable, penetrable | 反义词：undrillable, impenetrable
