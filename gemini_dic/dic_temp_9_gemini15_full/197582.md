shemite:/ˈʃiːmaɪt/| n. |闪米特人；闪族人 |A member of any of the Semitic peoples, whose languages form the Semitic branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family.  Semitic peoples historically inhabited and still inhabit parts of the Middle East and North Africa.  The term can be considered controversial due to its potential for misuse and association with racist ideologies. |例句：The ancient civilizations of the Near East were largely populated by Shemites.  古代近东文明主要由闪米特人构成。|近义词：Semite |反义词：无直接反义词，但可根据语境用非闪米特人等词语表达对立含义|名词
