modernising: /ˈmɒdənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |使现代化；现代化改造 |  The process of making something more modern or up-to-date.  It involves updating systems, processes, or technologies to improve efficiency, effectiveness, or relevance. | 例句：The company is modernising its production facilities to increase output. = 公司正在对其生产设施进行现代化改造以提高产量。

The government is modernising its infrastructure to improve transportation. = 政府正在对其基础设施进行现代化改造以改善交通。


近义词：updating, renovating, revitalizing, reforming


反义词：antiquating, archaizing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are modernising their website. = 他们正在对他们的网站进行现代化改造。
The factory is modernising its equipment. = 工厂正在对其设备进行现代化改造。


一般过去时:
They modernised their office last year. = 他们去年对他们的办公室进行了现代化改造。
The company modernised its marketing strategy. = 公司对其营销策略进行了现代化改造。


现在完成时:
They have modernised their entire system. = 他们已经将他们的整个系统现代化了。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

词源:
Modernising comes from the word "modern," ultimately deriving from the Latin "modernus," meaning "of the present time," or "recent." The suffix "-ising" is a present participle suffix indicating the act of making something modern.
