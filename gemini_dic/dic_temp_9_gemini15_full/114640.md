malevolent: /məˈlevələnt/ | adj. | 恶意的；怀有恶意的；邪恶的 | Having or showing a wish to do evil to others.  It implies a desire to inflict harm or suffering, often in a subtle or insidious way. |

例句：

He gave me a malevolent glare. 他给了我一个恶狠狠的眼神。

The villain in the story had malevolent intentions. 故事中的反派怀有恶意。


近义词：malicious, wicked, evil, spiteful, vindictive

反义词：benevolent, kind, good, benign, amiable

词性：形容词
