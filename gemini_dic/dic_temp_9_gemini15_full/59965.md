edifices:/ˈedɪfɪsɪz/| n. |建筑物，大厦|Large and impressive buildings.  The word often implies grandeur, permanence, and perhaps even a sense of historical weight. |例句：The ancient edifices of Rome stand as testaments to the empire's power. 罗马的古代建筑是帝国力量的证明。|例句：Many modern edifices now overshadow the older buildings in the city. 许多现代建筑现在都遮蔽了城市里较旧的建筑。|近义词：structures, buildings, constructions|反义词：ruins, shacks, huts|名词
