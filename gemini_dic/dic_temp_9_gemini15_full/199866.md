situational:/ˌsɪtʃuˈeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 情境的；视情况而定的；取决于具体情况的 | Relating to or dependent on a specific situation, circumstances, or context.  It emphasizes the influence of the current environment or context on something. | Example:  The situational awareness training helped the soldiers react appropriately in various scenarios.  情境意识训练帮助士兵在各种情况下做出适当的反应。  The company adopted a situational leadership style, adapting their approach to individual employees' needs. 公司采用了一种情境领导风格，根据员工的个人需求调整他们的方法。|近义词：conditional, contingent, contextual | 反义词：absolute, unconditional, invariant
