reconsented:/ˌriːkənˈsɛntɪd/ | v. | 再同意，重新同意 | To consent again; to give consent a second time.  It implies a previous consent that was perhaps withdrawn or questioned, and is now reaffirmed. |例句：After initially refusing, she reconsented to the interview.  她起初拒绝，后来又同意接受采访了。| 近义词：reagreed, reaffirmed, consented again | 反义词：withdrew consent, refused, objected
