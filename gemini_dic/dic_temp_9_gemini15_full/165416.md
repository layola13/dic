prelatial: /prɪˈleɪʃəl/ | adj. |主教的；大主教的；与主教相关的| Relating to a prelate or prelacy; characteristic of a prelate.  The word suggests authority, high rank, and often, a degree of formality and ceremony associated with the church hierarchy. |The prelatial robes were magnificent.  主教的礼袍十分华丽。| Episcopal, archiepiscopal |Lay, secular|
