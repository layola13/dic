indomitable: /ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbl/ | adj. |不屈不挠的，顽强的|unyielding; incapable of being subdued|The indomitable spirit of the human race has allowed us to overcome countless obstacles. 人类的不屈不挠的精神使我们克服了无数的障碍。|近义词：unyielding, unconquerable, invincible |反义词：yielding, submissive, weak

词源:
The word "indomitable" comes from the Latin word "indomitābilis,"  which is a combination of "in-" (not) and "domitābilis" (tamable, subdue-able).  Therefore, it literally means "untamable."
