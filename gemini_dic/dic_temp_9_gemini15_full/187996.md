rouche:/ruːʃ/| n.|褶皱，装饰褶边|A rouche is a gathered or pleated frill or trimming of fabric, often used as a decorative element on clothing or other textiles.  It is characterized by its gathered or pleated appearance, creating a textured and visually interesting effect.|例句：The dress featured a delicate lace rouche at the neckline. 这件裙子在领口处饰有精致的蕾丝褶边。|近义词：frill, ruffle, flounce|反义词：plain, smooth
