overstay:/ˈoʊvərˈsteɪ/ | v. |逾期逗留；滞留 |To remain beyond the permitted or expected time.  |例句：He overstayed his visa by three months. 他逾期居留三个月了。|例句：They overstayed their welcome at the party. 他们在派对上待的时间过长，不受欢迎了。|近义词：outstay, remain beyond the allotted time |反义词：depart on time, leave promptly
