extremity:/ɪkˈstrɛmɪti/ | n. |极端；末端；肢体 | The furthest point, part, or limit of something.  It can refer to a physical extreme point of something, or a figurative extreme of a situation or feeling.  |例句：The climbers faced the extremity of the harsh mountain weather. 登山者面临着恶劣山区天气的极端考验。

The extremity of his anger was frightening. 他愤怒到了令人害怕的程度。


近义词：end, tip, limit, edge, farthest point |反义词：center, middle, core
