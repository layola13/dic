jejuna:/dʒəˈdʒuːnə/| n. |空肠|The jejunum is the middle part of the small intestine, between the duodenum and the ileum. It's characterized by its relatively smooth inner surface and its role in nutrient absorption.  |例句：The surgeon carefully examined the jejunum during the laparoscopic procedure. 外科医生在腹腔镜手术中仔细检查了空肠。

The jejunum plays a crucial role in absorbing nutrients from digested food. 空肠在吸收消化食物中的营养物质方面起着至关重要的作用。


近义词：midgut (less specific)|反义词：duodenum, ileum (adjacent sections of small intestine)


词性:名词


词源:From Latin *ieiūnum, neuter of ieiūnus "fasting, hungry," from ieiūnāre "to fast."  The name reflects the relatively empty state of this part of the intestine during a post-mortem examination.
