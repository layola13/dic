pyrography:/paɪˈrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |焦笔画;火笔画|The art of decorating wood or other materials with burn marks made with a heated tool.  It involves using a heated stylus to create designs by burning into the surface.|The artist created a beautiful pyrography piece depicting a majestic eagle.|近义词：wood burning, brandishing|反义词：painting (in the sense of adding rather than subtracting material)|名词
