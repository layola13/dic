lanceolate:/ˈlænsɪəleɪt/| adj.|披针形的；狭长的|Shaped like a lance head; tapering to a point from a broad base.  The leaves are characteristically lanceolate.|例句：The willow tree has lanceolate leaves. 柳树的叶子是披针形的。

The leaves of the iris are lanceolate.  鸢尾的叶子是披针形的。


词源:From Medieval Latin *lanceolatus, from lancea ("lance").

近义词：lance-shaped, ensiform


反义词：broad, ovate, rounded


词性:形容词
