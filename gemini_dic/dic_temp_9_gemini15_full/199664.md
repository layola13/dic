sinuously:/ˈsɪnjuəsli/ | adj. |蜿蜒的，蛇形的；曲线优美的 | Moving in a sinuous manner; characterized by a series of curves and bends.  It describes a smooth, curving movement, often resembling a snake or wave. |例句：The river flowed sinuously through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。

The road sinuously climbed the mountain. 这条路蜿蜒曲折地爬上了山。 |近义词：winding, curving, meandering, serpentine, undulating |反义词：straight, direct, linear
