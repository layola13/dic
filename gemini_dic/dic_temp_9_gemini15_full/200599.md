slaveholder: /ˈsleɪvˌhoʊldər/ | n. |奴隶主 | A person who owns slaves.  The term typically refers to the ownership of enslaved people, often within a system of chattel slavery.  The practice is now widely condemned as a gross violation of human rights. | Example:  The slaveholder's cruelty was legendary throughout the region.  奴隶主的残酷在整个地区都是出了名的。  The wealthy plantation owner was a ruthless slaveholder.  那个富有的种植园主是一个残酷的奴隶主。|近义词：slave owner, enslaver |反义词：abolitionist (in relation to the practice), freedom fighter (in relation to the struggle against it)
