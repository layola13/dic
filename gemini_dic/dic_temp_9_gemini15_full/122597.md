modernistically: /ˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪkli/ | /ˌmɑːdəˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 现代主义地；现代派地 |  In a manner characteristic of modernism;  with the style or principles of modernism.  | 例句：The building was designed modernistically, with clean lines and a minimalist aesthetic. 这座建筑的设计风格是现代主义的，线条简洁，极简主义的美学。

The novel was written modernistically, employing stream-of-consciousness techniques. 这部小说采用现代主义的写作手法，运用意识流技巧。


近义词：modernly, avant-gardistly, contemporarily

反义词：traditionally, conventionally, classically


词性: 副词
