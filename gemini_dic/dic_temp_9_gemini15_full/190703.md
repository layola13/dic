sauropsida:/sɔːˈrɒpsɪdə/| n. |蜥形纲|Sauropsida is a clade of amniotes that includes reptiles and birds.  It is characterized by a number of shared derived traits, including a diapsid skull (in most members), scales or feathers, and a unique pattern of embryonic development.| Example:  The evolution of sauropsids is a fascinating area of study.  蜥形纲的进化是一个引人入胜的研究领域。|近义词:reptiles (不完全同义，因为蜥形纲包含鸟类)|反义词: synapsida (哺乳动物类群)|名词
