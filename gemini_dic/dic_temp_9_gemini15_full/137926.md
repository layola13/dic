It refers to a state or instances of not being voluble (fluent and readily talkative). | Example: Her nonvolubilities during the interview suggested a lack of confidence.  她的面试中沉默寡言暗示她缺乏自信。  |近义词: taciturnity, reticence, silence, inarticulateness | 反义词: volubility, fluency, loquacity | 名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense-based examples are not applicable.  The usage centers on describing a state or instances of being uncommunicative.


Example 1: His nonvolubilities were misinterpreted as rudeness. 他的沉默寡言被误解为粗鲁。

Example 2: The meeting was marked by periods of uncomfortable nonvolubilities. 会议期间出现了令人不安的沉默。
