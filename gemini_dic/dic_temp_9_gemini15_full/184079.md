resignedness: /rɪˈzaɪndnəs/ | n. | 顺从；逆来顺受；认命；甘心 |  A feeling of acceptance of something undesirable that cannot be avoided.  It suggests a quiet acceptance, often stemming from weariness or a lack of alternative. |


例句：

There was a quiet resignedness in her voice as she described the situation.  = 她描述情况时，声音中带着一种平静的顺从。

He accepted his fate with a weary resignedness. = 他带着疲惫的认命接受了他的命运。


近义词：acceptance, submission, resignation, acquiescence


反义词：resistance, defiance, rebellion, protest


词性：名词
