conspicuously:/kənˈspɪkjuəsli/ | adv. |显著地；显眼地；引人注目地 |Clearly visible; attracting attention.  |例句：The red car was conspicuously parked in the middle of the street.  那辆红色的汽车显眼地停在街道中央。

例句：He conspicuously ignored her question. 他故意无视她的问题。


近义词：noticeably, prominently, strikingly, remarkably, obviously

反义词：inconspicuously, subtly, unobtrusively


词源:

* **con-:**  prefix meaning "together" or "with"
* **spic-:** from Latin "specere," meaning "to look" or "to see" (related to "spectacle," "species," "inspect")
* **-uous:** adjective suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by"
* **-ly:** adverb suffix


用法说明:

conspicuously主要用作副词，修饰动词或整个句子，表示某种行为或事物非常显眼、引人注目，甚至可能故意为之。它强调的是可见性和明显的特征，常常带有某种程度的负面含义，例如，暗示行为不当或试图引起注意。
