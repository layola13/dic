cuspidal: /ˈkʌspɪdəl/ | adj. |尖顶的；尖状的；尖端形的|

英文解析:  Relating to or resembling a cusp; having a pointed or sharp end.  Often used to describe a pointed shape or structure, especially in geometry or dentistry.

例句:
The mountain peak had a cuspidal shape. = 山峰呈尖顶状。

The dentist examined the cuspidal teeth. = 牙医检查了尖牙。


近义词: pointed, sharp, peaked, acuminate

反义词: blunt, rounded, obtuse


词源: From Latin *cuspidātus, past participle of *cuspidāre "to make pointed," from *cuspis "point, spear point."
