self-description:/ˈsɛlf dɪˈskrɪpʃən/| n. |自我描述，自述|A self-description is a statement or account that someone gives about themselves, their characteristics, or their experiences.  It can be written or spoken. |Example:  Her self-description in the online dating profile was quite intriguing.  她的在线交友资料中的自我描述非常吸引人。|近义词：autobiography (for longer accounts), account of oneself, portrayal of oneself |反义词：N/A
