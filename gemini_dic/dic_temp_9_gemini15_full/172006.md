purgatories:/ˈpɜːrɡətɔːriz/| n.|炼狱；痛苦的经历|A state or place of suffering or temporary punishment, often conceived as an intermediate state between death and heaven or hell, where souls undergo purification before entering heaven.  It can also refer to any difficult or painful experience.|例句：Some religions believe in purgatories where souls atone for their sins before entering heaven.  一些宗教相信存在炼狱，灵魂在那里为罪过赎罪后再进入天堂。|例句：Her life felt like a purgatory of endless paperwork and meetings.她的生活感觉就像一个充满无休止文书工作和会议的炼狱。|近义词：purgatory, ordeal, tribulation|反义词：heaven, paradise
