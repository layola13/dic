ease-out:/ˌiːzˈaʊt/ | n. adj. |缓和，平滑过渡；逐渐消失 |  A gradual decrease in speed or intensity; a smooth transition to a stop.  It often describes the ending of an animation or transition, where the movement slows down smoothly before coming to a complete halt.  | Example: The animation had a satisfying ease-out effect, making the transition feel natural.  | 近义词：fade-out, deceleration, tapering off | 反义词：ease-in, abrupt stop |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The animation eases out smoothly. = 动画平滑地缓和结束。
The music eases out at the end of the song. = 音乐在歌曲结尾逐渐淡出。


一般过去时:
The car eased out of the parking lot. = 车子缓缓驶出停车场。
The pain eased out gradually. = 疼痛逐渐减轻。


现在完成时:
The tension has eased out considerably. = 紧张感已大大缓解。
The knot has eased out after I applied some pressure. = 我施加了一些压力后，结解开了。


过去完成时:
The crowd had eased out before the rain started. = 下雨前人群已经散去。
The problem had eased out before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前问题已经解决了。


将来时:
The traffic will ease out after rush hour. = 交通高峰期过后交通会缓和。
The symptoms will ease out with the medication. = 服药后症状会逐渐缓解。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure eased out, the situation would improve. = 如果压力缓解，情况就会好转。
I wish the pain would ease out quickly. = 我希望疼痛能迅速缓解。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
