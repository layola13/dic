exercised:/ˈɛksərsaɪzd/ | v. | 运动；运用；行使；使不安 | to use a skill, right, or quality | He exercised his right to remain silent. 他行使了他保持沉默的权利。 | employed, used, utilized | neglected, ignored | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exercises every morning. = 他每天早上都锻炼。
She exercises her authority wisely. = 她明智地运用她的权力。


一般过去时:
I exercised yesterday. = 我昨天锻炼了。
The company exercised its option to buy the land. = 公司行使了购买土地的选择权。


现在完成时:
I have exercised regularly for years. = 我多年来一直定期锻炼。
They have exercised their right to vote. = 他们已经行使了投票权。


过去完成时:
He had exercised before he went to work. = 他上班前已经锻炼过了。
She had exercised her influence to secure the contract. = 她曾运用她的影响力来确保合同的签订。


将来时:
I will exercise tomorrow. = 我明天会锻炼。
The government will exercise its power to control inflation. = 政府将运用它的权力来控制通货膨胀。


虚拟语气:
If I exercised more, I would be healthier. = 如果我多锻炼，我会更健康。
I wish I had exercised more last year. = 我希望去年我能多锻炼一些。


被动语态:
The right was exercised by the people. = 这一权利由人民行使。
His authority was exercised unjustly.  = 他的权力被不公正地运用。


疑问句:
Do you exercise regularly? = 你定期锻炼吗？
Was the power exercised fairly? = 权力是否被公平地运用？


否定句:
He didn't exercise at all last week. = 上周他根本没有锻炼。
They didn't exercise their options. = 他们没有行使他们的选择权。


PHRASE->
exercise caution = 小心谨慎
exercise restraint = 约束克制
exercise control = 行使控制
exercise judgment = 行使判断力
exercise one's rights = 行使某人的权利

词源:
源自古法语 *exerciser 和拉丁语 *exercitium，意思是“练习，训练”。
