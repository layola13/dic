psyched:/saɪkt/| adj.|兴奋的，激动万分的|Extremely excited; enthusiastic.|例句：I'm so psyched for the concert tonight!  今晚的音乐会我太兴奋了！
例句：She was totally psyched about her new job. 她对她的新工作非常兴奋。|近义词：excited, thrilled, stoked, exhilarated|反义词：depressed, uninterested, apathetic|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am psyched about the trip. = 我对这次旅行感到兴奋。
They are psyched to see their friends. = 他们很兴奋能见到他们的朋友。


一般过去时:
I was psyched when I heard the news. = 当我听到这个消息时，我非常兴奋。
We were psyched to win the game. = 我们很高兴赢得了比赛。


现在完成时:
I have been psyched all day. = 我一整天都很兴奋。
She has been psyched since she got the promotion. = 自从她升职以来，她就一直很兴奋。


过去完成时:
I had been psyched for weeks before the event. = 在活动开始前的几周，我一直都很兴奋。
They had been psyched to finally meet. = 他们一直很兴奋终于能见面了。


将来时:
I will be psyched when I finally graduate. = 当我最终毕业时，我会非常兴奋。
We will be psyched to celebrate with you. = 我们很高兴能和你一起庆祝。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be psyched. = 如果我中了彩票，我会很兴奋。
I wish I were psyched about my work. = 我希望我对我的工作感到兴奋。


被动语态:  (被动语态不常用在"psyched"上)


疑问句:
Are you psyched about the party? = 你对派对感到兴奋吗？
Is he psyched for the challenge? = 他对这个挑战感到兴奋吗？


否定句:
I'm not psyched about going to work today. = 我今天不想上班。
She wasn't psyched to hear the bad news. = 她听到这个坏消息并不兴奋。


PHRASE->
get psyched up =  使自己兴奋起来
psyched out =  被吓倒，被搞垮
psyched to do something = 兴奋地去做某事
be psyched about something = 对某事感到兴奋
psyched and ready = 兴奋并已准备好
