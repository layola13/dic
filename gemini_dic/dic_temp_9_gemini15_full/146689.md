overhears: /ˌoʊvərˈhɪrz/ | vt. | 无意中听到；偷听到 | To hear (something) without the speaker's knowledge or intention.  | 例句：I overheard them talking about their vacation plans. 我无意中听到他们在谈论他们的假期计划。

例句：She overheard her colleagues gossiping in the break room. 她无意中听到同事们在休息室里闲聊。


近义词：eavesdrop, overhear,  catch (a word or phrase)


反义词：intend to hear, deliberately listen to


词性：动词

词源：
The word "overhear" is a combination of "over" and "hear."  "Over" here implies the sense of something happening incidentally or unintentionally, while "hear" retains its standard meaning.  The word developed its current meaning over time.
