rethawed:/ˌriːˈθɔːd/| adj.|解冻的，再次解冻的|thawed again;  having been frozen and then thawed, possibly more than once.|The rethawed meat was slightly mushy.  再次解冻的肉有点软烂。|近义词：defrosted, thawed|反义词：frozen, icy
