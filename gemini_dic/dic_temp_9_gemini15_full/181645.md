relayed:/rɪˈleɪd/| v. |转播；传递；转达；重复；重新铺设|To communicate or send something, especially news or information, to someone else; to repeat something that you have heard from someone else; to lay something again.|例句：The news was relayed to the President immediately.  新闻立即被转达给了总统。|例句：He relayed the message to his boss. 他把消息转达给了老板。|近义词：transmit, convey, repeat, retell|反义词：retain, keep secret


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relays the information to his team daily. 他每天都向他的团队转达信息。
She relays the news from the press conference. 她转播新闻发布会上的新闻。


一般过去时:
The journalist relayed the breaking news to the public.  记者向公众转达了突发新闻。
The runner relayed the baton to his teammate. 赛跑运动员将接力棒传递给了队友。


现在完成时:
They have relayed the message to all relevant parties. 他们已经将消息转达给了所有相关方。
The station has relayed the concert live.  电台已经现场转播了这场音乐会。


过去完成时:
The messenger had relayed the urgent message before the meeting started. 信使在会议开始前已经转达了紧急信息。
She had relayed the gossip to everyone in the office before lunch. 她在午餐前已经把八卦告诉了办公室里的每个人。


将来时:
We will relay the results of the experiment to the committee. 我们将把实验结果转达给委员会。
The network will relay the football game live tonight.  今晚网络将现场转播足球比赛。


虚拟语气:
If he had relayed the message earlier, the accident might have been avoided. 如果他早点转达信息，事故也许就能避免了。
I wish I had relayed the important information to my supervisor sooner. 我希望我早点把重要信息转达给我的主管。


被动语态:

The live broadcast will be relayed to millions of viewers. 现场直播将转播给数百万观众。


疑问句:

否定句:
