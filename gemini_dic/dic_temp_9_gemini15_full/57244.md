downing: /ˈdaʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 推翻；击落；使沮丧；吞下 | To bring down or cause to fall; to swallow; to depress or subdue.  | The fighter pilot successfully downed the enemy plane.  战斗机飞行员成功击落了敌机。|  The government was downed by a coup.  政府被政变推翻了。 | bring down, shoot down, overthrow | uplift, encourage |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He downs a glass of water every morning. = 他每天早上喝一杯水。
They are downing tools in protest. = 他们在抗议中放下工具。


一般过去时:
The pilot downed his plane safely. = 飞行员安全地迫降了他的飞机。
She downed a whole pizza in one sitting. = 她一口气吃掉了一个披萨。


现在完成时:
The rebels have downed several government helicopters. = 反叛军已经击落了几架政府直升机。
He has downed three cups of coffee already. = 他已经喝了三杯咖啡了。


过去完成时:
The bird had downed a large worm before I saw it. = 我看到它之前，那只鸟已经吞下了一条大虫子。
They had downed their weapons before surrendering. = 他们在投降前已经放下了武器。


将来时:
The protestors will down tools if their demands are not met. = 如果他们的要求得不到满足，抗议者将放下工具。
She will down that entire bottle of soda in no time. = 她很快就会把整瓶苏打水喝完。


虚拟语气:
If he downed that drink, he might feel better. = 如果他喝了那杯酒，他可能会感觉好些。
I wish I hadn't downed that whole cake. = 我真希望我没有吃掉整个蛋糕。


被动语态:
The plane was downed by enemy fire. = 飞机被敌人的炮火击落了。
The entire shipment of goods was downed by a storm. = 整批货物都被暴风雨摧毁了。(这里downed表示“摧毁”，并非直接的“击落”)


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't down the whole bottle. = 他没有喝完整瓶酒。
They didn't down their weapons easily. = 他们没有轻易放下武器。
