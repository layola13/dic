eyewear:/ˈaɪwɛr/| n. |眼镜，眼部防护用具|Articles worn to protect or correct vision, including glasses, goggles, and sunglasses.  Eyewear is an essential accessory for many people, providing vision correction or protection from the sun and other elements.|例句：She chose a stylish pair of eyewear to complement her outfit. 她选择了一副时尚的眼镜来搭配她的服装。

例句：He needs to wear protective eyewear when working with chemicals. 他在使用化学品时需要佩戴防护眼镜。


近义词：spectacles, glasses, goggles, sunglasses|反义词：none|词性:名词
