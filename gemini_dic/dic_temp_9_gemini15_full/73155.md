forbear:/fɔːrˈbeər/ | /fɔːrˈbɛər/ | v. | 克制；忍耐；容忍；避免；祖先 | To restrain oneself from doing something; to refrain from; to abstain from; to tolerate; to endure.  |  近义词：refrain, abstain, restrain, tolerate, endure | 反义词：indulge, give in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forbears to comment on the matter. = 他克制自己不去评论此事。
She forbears criticism even when it's deserved. = 她即使在应受批评时也忍着不批评。


一般过去时:
They forbore to interfere in the dispute. = 他们避免干涉这场争端。
I forbore from mentioning his mistake. = 我避免提及他的错误。


现在完成时:
She has forborne to express her anger. = 她一直忍着没有表达她的愤怒。
He has forborne all temptation. = 他克制住了所有的诱惑。


过去完成时:
Having forborne for so long, he finally exploded. = 忍耐了这么久之后，他终于爆发了。
They had forborne to speak until the right moment. = 他们一直忍住不说话，直到合适的时机。


将来时:
I will forbear from making any judgments until I have all the facts. = 在我掌握所有事实之前，我将避免做出任何判断。
She will forbear to answer his rude questions. = 她将忍住不回答他粗鲁的问题。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would forbear from interfering. = 如果我是你，我会避免干涉。
I wish he would forbear to gossip. = 我希望他能忍住不八卦。


被动语态:
His bad behavior should not be forborne. = 他的恶劣行为不应该被容忍。
Criticism should not be forborne when deserved. = 应得的批评不应该被忍让。


疑问句:
Will you forbear to interrupt? = 你会忍住不打断吗？
Should we forbear from taking action? = 我们应该避免采取行动吗？


否定句:
He did not forbear to express his opinion. = 他没有忍住表达了自己的意见。
She could not forbear laughing at the joke. = 她忍不住笑了。



PHRASE->
forbearance of others = 对别人的容忍
forbearance of patience = 耐心的克制
forbearance and tolerance = 忍让和宽容
to forbear from action = 避免采取行动
act with forbearance = 以克制的方式行事


词源:
From Old English *forberan, from for- ("away, completely") + beran ("to bear").  The original meaning related to "bearing away" or "carrying away," evolving to the sense of restraint or patience.
