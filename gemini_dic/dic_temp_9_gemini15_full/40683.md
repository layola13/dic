conversations: /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənz/ | n. |谈话，会话 |A conversation is an oral exchange of thoughts, ideas, or feelings between two or more people.  It involves a back-and-forth exchange of communication, often involving verbal language but also potentially nonverbal cues like body language and tone of voice.  Conversations can be formal or informal, depending on the context and relationship between the participants. |例句：We had a long conversation about our plans for the future. 我们就未来的计划进行了长时间的谈话。

例句：The conversations at the party were lively and engaging. 派对上的谈话热闹而引人入胜。


近义词：talks, dialogues, discussions, chats


反义词：silence, solitude


词性: 名词
