cremator:/krɪˈmeɪtər/| n.|火葬者；火葬场；焚化炉|A cremator is a person or machine that cremates bodies.  It can also refer to the building where cremation takes place.|例句：The cremator carefully placed the body in the furnace. 火葬员小心翼翼地将遗体放入焚化炉。  The new cremator was state-of-the-art. 新的火葬场设备先进。|近义词：incinerator, burner (for machines)|反义词：burial ground, cemetery (for places)|名词
