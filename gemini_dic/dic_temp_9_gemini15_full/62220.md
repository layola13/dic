encrinital:/ɛnˈkraɪnɪtəl/| adj.|海百合的；由海百合化石组成的|Pertaining to, composed of, or resembling encrinites (fossil crinoids).  The term refers to rocks or sediments containing a significant amount of crinoid remains.|The limestone is encrinital, indicating a shallow-marine depositional environment. 这块石灰岩是海百合的，表明这是一个浅海沉积环境。 The encrinital debris formed a significant part of the ancient seabed. 海百合碎片构成了古代海底的重要组成部分。|crinoidal, crinoid-bearing|non-encrinital
