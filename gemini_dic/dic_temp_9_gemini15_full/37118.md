combustibles:/kəmˈbʌstəblz/| n.|易燃物，可燃物|Materials that are easily set on fire.  Combustibles are often found in homes and workplaces and need to be handled with care to prevent fires.|例句：Gasoline and propane are examples of combustibles.汽油和丙烷是易燃物的例子。

The storage of combustibles near open flames is strictly prohibited. 在明火附近储存易燃物是严格禁止的。|近义词：inflammables, flammables|反义词：non-combustibles, fire-resistant materials|名词
