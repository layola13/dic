maniacal:/məˈneɪəkəl/| adj.|疯狂的；狂热的；极度兴奋的|Characterized by or suggestive of mania; wildly excited or uncontrolled.  It often implies a dangerous or destructive element to the excitement.|例句：His maniacal laughter echoed through the empty halls. 他的疯狂笑声回荡在空荡的大厅里。
例句：The dictator's maniacal obsession with power led to widespread suffering. 独裁者对权力的疯狂迷恋导致了广泛的苦难。|近义词：insane, frenzied, frantic, raving, deranged|反义词：calm, composed, rational, sane, tranquil
