galvanoplasty:/ɡælˈvænəplæsti/| n.| 电镀，电铸 | The process of electroplating or electroforming metal objects.  It involves using an electric current to deposit a layer of metal onto a conductive surface. | Example:  Galvanoplasty is used to create highly detailed replicas of objects. = 电镀用于制作高度精细的物体复制品。

The use of galvanoplasty is common in jewelry making. = 电镀在珠宝制作中很常见。| 近义词：electroplating, electroforming | 反义词：None directly applicable, but processes that remove metal coatings could be considered opposites. | 名词
