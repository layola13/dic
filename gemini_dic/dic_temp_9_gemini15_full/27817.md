cannulae:/ˈkænjuːleɪ/| n. |套管(复数) |Thin tubes, typically used in medicine for inserting into a body cavity or vessel.  They are often used for drainage, injection, or other medical procedures. |Example: The surgeon inserted cannulae into the patient's veins to administer fluids.  外科医生将套管插入病人的静脉中以输送液体。

The doctor used several cannulae to drain the excess fluid. 医生使用了几个套管来排出多余的液体。|近义词：tubes, catheters |反义词：N/A
