dissolvingly:/dɪˈzɒlvɪŋli/| adv.|溶解地；逐渐消失地；逐渐减弱地 |In a manner that dissolves or causes to dissolve; gradually fading away; weakening gradually.  It describes an action or process of gradual disappearance or weakening.  |例句：The tension in the room dissolvedly as the announcement was made.  房间里的紧张气氛随着宣布的作出而逐渐消散。

The snow dissolvedly into the spring air.  雪逐渐融化在春天的空气中。


近义词：gradually, slowly, subtly, imperceptibly |反义词：suddenly, abruptly, rapidly


词源:  Derived from the verb "dissolve," which comes from the Late Latin *dissolvere, a combination of the prefix dis- (meaning "apart") and solvere (meaning "to loosen, to release").  The -ly suffix transforms the verb into an adverb, indicating the manner in which something happens.
