heteropoda:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpədə/ | n. |异足类动物|A suborder of spiders characterized by their long, slender legs and often flattened bodies, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. They are known for their hunting prowess and some species are large and strikingly colored.  | Example:  Many species of *Heteropoda* are found in Southeast Asia.  许多种类的异足类动物都产于东南亚。
