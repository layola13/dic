makers:/ˈmeɪkərz/| n.|制造商，制作者，创造者|People or companies that produce goods.  Those who create something.|例句：Many makers are exhibiting their products at the trade show. 许多制造商都在贸易展上展出他们的产品。|例句：The makers of this software are constantly updating it. 这个软件的制作方一直在不断更新它。|近义词：manufacturers, producers, creators|反义词：consumers, users
