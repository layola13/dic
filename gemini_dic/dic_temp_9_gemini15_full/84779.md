handsaws:/ˈhændsɔːz/| n. |手锯|A handsaw is a saw with a relatively short blade designed to be used with one or both hands.  They are typically used for cutting wood, but some are designed for metal or other materials.  Handsaws come in various types, depending on the intended use and the material to be cut. |Example: He used a handsaw to cut the lumber for the new bookshelf. 他用一把手锯切割新书架的木材。  The carpenter carefully selected a handsaw suitable for the delicate woodwork. 木匠仔细选择了一把适合精细木工的手锯。|近义词：saw, hand saw|反义词：power saw
