fates:/feɪts/| n. |命运；天数；结局|The inevitable and often unwelcome events that will happen to a person.|例句：The fates were against them, and they lost the battle.  命运与他们作对，他们输掉了战斗。
例句：She accepted her fate with quiet dignity. 她平静而尊严地接受了自己的命运。|近义词：destiny, lot, doom|反义词：choice, free will|
