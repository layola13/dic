quince:/kwɪns/| n. |木瓜； quinceañera（女孩15岁生日）|A quince is a fruit similar to an apple or pear, but larger and firmer, with a slightly acidic taste.  It is often used in jams, jellies, and other desserts.  The term also refers to a quinceañera, a celebration of a girl's fifteenth birthday in some Hispanic cultures.|例句：We made quince paste for the pie. 我们做了木瓜酱做馅饼。

例句：Her quinceañera was a lavish affair. 她的十五岁生日宴非常奢华。


近义词：apple, pear (in terms of fruit)|反义词：N/A|词性：名词


词源：The word "quince" comes from the Old French "coinse," which in turn derives from the Latin "cȳdonia," referring to the city of Cydonia in Crete, where the fruit was originally cultivated.
