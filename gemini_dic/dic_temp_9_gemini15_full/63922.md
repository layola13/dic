epiphytical:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪkəl/| adj.|附生的，附生植物的|Growing upon another plant but not parasitic.  Epiphytical plants derive moisture and nutrients from the air, rain, and debris that collects around them.|例句：Many orchids are epiphytical, growing on the branches of trees.  许多兰花是附生的，生长在树枝上。|例句：The epiphytical ferns thrived in the humid rainforest canopy. 附生的蕨类植物在潮湿的雨林树冠中茁壮成长。|近义词：parasitic (if slightly parasitic), air plant|反义词：terrestrial, subterranean
