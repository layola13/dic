mono-substituted:/ˌmɒnəʊˈsʌbsɪtjuːtɪd/| adj.| 单取代的 | Having only one substituent group attached to a molecule or compound.  A mono-substituted benzene ring, for example, has only one atom or group of atoms replacing a hydrogen atom on the ring. | Example: Chlorobenzene is a mono-substituted benzene derivative.  氯苯是一种单取代苯衍生物。  The reaction yielded a mono-substituted product.  反应产生了一种单取代产物。|近义词: singly substituted |反义词: polysubstituted, disubstituted
