footnoting: /ˈfʊtˌnoʊtɪŋ/ | v. |脚注；加脚注 |The act of adding footnotes to a text.  It involves providing supplementary information, explanations, or citations at the bottom of a page or section.  |例句：The author meticulously footnoted every claim in the research paper. 作者一丝不苟地在研究论文中为每一个论点都加了脚注。


例句：She spent hours footnoting her dissertation. 她花了数小时为她的论文加脚注。


近义词：annotating, referencing, citing
