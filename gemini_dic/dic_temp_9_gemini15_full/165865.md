preofficially: /ˌpriːəˈfɪʃəli/ | adv. | 预先正式地，在正式宣布之前 |  Before something is officially announced or made public.  It implies an informal or unofficial announcement or action that precedes the formal one. |

例句：

The news was preofficially leaked to the press before the official announcement. =  消息在正式宣布前被预先泄露给了媒体。

The company preofficially unveiled its new product at a private event. = 公司在一个私人活动中预先非正式地展示了它的新产品。


近义词：unofficially, informally, tentatively

反义词：officially


词性: 副词
