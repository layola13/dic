phenomenon:/fəˈnɑːmɪnən/| n.|现象，奇观|A fact or situation that is observed to exist or happen, especially one whose cause or explanation is in question.  It can also refer to a remarkable or exceptional person, thing, or event.|例句：Gravity is a natural phenomenon. 重力是一种自然现象。

例句：The Northern Lights are a spectacular phenomenon. 北极光是壮丽的奇观。


近义词：occurrence, event, incident, marvel, wonder|反义词：normality, ordinariness


词源：From Late Latin phaenomenon, from Greek phainómenon 'that which appears', neuter of phainómenos 'appearing', present participle of phaínein 'to appear, show'.
