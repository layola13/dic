explainer:/ɪkˈspleɪnər/ | n. |解释者；讲解者；说明书；解释性文章 | A person or thing that explains something.  An explainer video or article provides a clear and concise explanation of a complex topic. | Example:  The museum provided an explainer for the ancient artifacts.  博物馆为古代文物提供了解说。 The website's explainer video helped me understand the software.  网站的解说视频帮助我理解了该软件。 | 近义词：interpreter, commentator, guide, tutorial | 反义词：obscurer, confuser
