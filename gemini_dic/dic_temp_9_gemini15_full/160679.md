porting:/ˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. |移植；搬运；携带|The act of transferring something from one place, system, or environment to another.  It often implies adaptation or modification to make the transfer successful.  |例句：The company is porting its software to the new operating system.  公司正在将其软件移植到新的操作系统。|例句：He was porting heavy boxes from the truck to the warehouse. 他正在把沉重的箱子从卡车搬运到仓库。|近义词：transferring, transplanting, moving, conveying |反义词：removing, deleting


USAGE->

They are porting the game to mobile devices. = 他们正在将游戏移植到移动设备上。
The developers are porting the application to a new platform. = 开发人员正在将应用程序移植到新的平台上。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
