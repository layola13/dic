sandals:/ˈsændəlz/| n.|凉鞋|Footwear consisting of a sole held to the foot by straps.  Sandals are typically worn in warm weather.|例句：She wore sandals to the beach. 她穿着凉鞋去了海滩。

例句：My new sandals are very comfortable.我的新凉鞋很舒服。|近义词：flip-flops, thongs, slides|反义词：boots, shoes (in the sense of fully enclosed footwear)

词源:
The word "sandals" originates from the Old French "sandale," ultimately derived from the Late Latin "sandalium," which itself comes from the Greek "sandálion" (σανδάλιον).  The Greek word referred to a type of open-toed footwear.
