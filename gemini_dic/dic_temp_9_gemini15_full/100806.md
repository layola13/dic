intrigue:/ɪnˈtriːɡ/ | vt.  | 激起…的好奇心；密谋；阴谋 | To arouse the curiosity or interest of someone; to make secret plans to do something, especially something bad. | The mystery of the missing painting intrigued everyone.  = 失踪的画作之谜激起了每个人的好奇心。 |He was involved in an intrigue to overthrow the government. = 他卷入了一场推翻政府的阴谋。 | 近义词：fascinate, captivate, plot, conspire | 反义词：bore, disenchant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The novel intrigues me. = 这部小说激起了我的兴趣。
Her strange behavior intrigues the police. = 她奇怪的行为引起了警方的兴趣。


一般过去时:
The old castle intrigued the tourists. = 古堡激起了游客的好奇心。
The spy's secret mission intrigued his colleagues. = 间谍的秘密任务引起了同事们的兴趣。


现在完成时:
The unsolved case has intrigued investigators for years. = 这个悬而未决的案件多年来一直让调查人员着迷。
The plot of the movie has intrigued audiences worldwide. = 这部电影的情节吸引了全世界的观众。


过去完成时:
The ancient ruins had intrigued explorers for centuries. = 古代遗迹几个世纪以来一直吸引着探险家。
The mysterious message had intrigued the detective for days. = 神秘的信息几天来一直让侦探着迷。


将来时:
The new scientific discovery will intrigue many researchers. = 新的科学发现将引起许多研究人员的兴趣。
The upcoming exhibition will intrigue art lovers. = 即将来临的展览将吸引艺术爱好者。


虚拟语气:
If the story intrigued me more, I would finish reading it. = 如果这个故事更吸引我，我会读完它。
I wish the mystery would intrigue me more. = 我希望这个谜能更吸引我。


被动语态:
The audience was intrigued by the magician's performance. = 观众被魔术师的表演迷住了。
The detectives were intrigued by the new evidence. = 侦探们被新的证据所吸引。


疑问句:
Does the idea of space travel intrigue you? = 太空旅行的想法是否吸引你？
What intrigues you about this painting? = 这幅画的什么地方吸引了你？


否定句:
The story didn't intrigue me at all. = 这个故事一点也没吸引我。
The simple explanation didn't intrigue the scientists. = 简单的解释并没有引起科学家的兴趣。


PHRASE->

intrigue with = 与…密谋
political intrigue = 政治阴谋
court intrigue = 宫廷阴谋
romantic intrigue = 浪漫的阴谋
an element of intrigue = 一丝神秘感
