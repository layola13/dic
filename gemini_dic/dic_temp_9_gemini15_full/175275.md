raindrop:/ˈreɪndrop/| n. |雨滴|A single drop of rain.  Rain drops are formed when water vapor in the atmosphere condenses and becomes heavy enough to fall to the earth.|例句：The raindrops splattered against the windowpane. 雨滴溅在窗玻璃上。

例句：I felt a cool raindrop on my cheek. 我感觉脸颊上有一滴凉凉的雨水。


近义词：drop of rain, water droplet|反义词：无


词性:名词
