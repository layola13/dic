indaba:/ɪnˈdɑːbə/| n. |印巴语会议；大型集会；商讨|A large meeting or assembly, especially one held by the Zulu people of South Africa to discuss important matters.  It often involves discussion, debate, and decision-making within a community. |例句：The tribal leaders held an indaba to discuss the future of their people. 部落首领举行了一次印巴语会议来讨论他们人民的未来。

The community held an indaba to address the ongoing water shortage. 社区举行了一次印巴语会议来解决持续的缺水问题。|近义词：meeting, conference, assembly, gathering |反义词：None specifically applicable.
