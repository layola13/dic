serotonins:/ˌsɛrəˈtoʊnɪnz/| n. |血清素（复数）|Serotonins are a group of neurotransmitters that play a vital role in various bodily functions, including mood regulation, sleep, appetite, and digestion. They are produced in the brain and intestines.|例句：Low levels of serotonins are often associated with depression and anxiety. 低水平的血清素通常与抑郁症和焦虑症有关。|近义词：neurotransmitters (神经递质)|反义词：无|名词
