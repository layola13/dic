词源:
From Middle English *knihtlich, from Old English *cnihtlīċ, from *cniht (“boy, servant, knight”) + -līċ (“-like”).  The Old English word *cniht is of Germanic origin, related to Old High German *kneht (“boy, servant”).  The meaning evolved from a young servant to a warrior of noble rank.
