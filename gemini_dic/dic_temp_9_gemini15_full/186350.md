rhymed:/raɪmd/| adj.|押韵的；用韵文的|Having or ending with similar sounds, especially at the end of lines in poems or songs.|The poem's final two lines rhymed perfectly.|近义词：rhythmical, metrical|反义词：unrhymed, prosaic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The poem's lines rhyme beautifully. = 这首诗的诗行押韵优美。
These words don't rhyme. = 这些词不押韵。


一般过去时:
The children rhymed words during the game. = 孩子们在游戏中押韵。
The poet rhymed the verses skillfully. = 诗人巧妙地押韵。


现在完成时:
She has rhymed many poems in her career. = 她在她职业生涯中写了很多押韵的诗歌。
He has just rhymed a couplet. = 他刚刚写了一对韵文。


过去完成时:
