culls:/kʌlz/| v. n.|挑选；剔除；精简|To select from a group; to remove or eliminate undesirable individuals or things from a group.|The farmer culled the weakest lambs from the flock.  农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的羔羊。|The editor culled the unnecessary words from the article. 编辑从文章中删去了不必要的词语。|select, weed out, eliminate|keep, retain, preserve|动词，名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He culls the weak plants from his garden. = 他从花园里剔除弱小的植物。
She culls information from various sources. = 她从各种来源收集信息。

一般过去时:
They culled the damaged apples from the harvest. = 他们从收成中剔除了损坏的苹果。
The museum culled its collection of artifacts. = 博物馆精简了其文物收藏。

现在完成时:
The company has culled its workforce to reduce costs. = 公司已经裁员以降低成本。
She has culled many interesting facts for her presentation. = 她为她的演示文稿收集了许多有趣的事实。

过去完成时:
The editor had culled the manuscript before submitting it. = 编辑在提交稿件之前已经仔细修改过。
They had culled the herd before the winter arrived. = 冬天来临之前，他们已经淘汰了部分牲畜。

将来时:
The farmer will cull the diseased sheep next week. = 农民下周将淘汰患病的羊。
The company will cull its product line to focus on key items. = 公司将精简其产品线，专注于主要产品。

虚拟语气:
If they culled the weaker animals, the herd would be healthier. = 如果他们淘汰较弱的动物，羊群就会更健康。
I wish they had culled the damaged goods before shipping them. = 我希望他们在发货前就把损坏的货物淘汰掉。

被动语态:
The weak plants were culled from the garden. = 弱小的植物从花园里被剔除了。
The unnecessary information was culled from the report. = 报告中不必要的信息已被删除。

疑问句:
Will the farmer cull the sick animals? = 农民会淘汰患病的动物吗？
Did they cull the damaged products? = 他们淘汰了损坏的产品吗？

否定句:
They did not cull the entire flock. = 他们没有淘汰整个羊群。
The editor did not cull any sentences from the article. = 编辑没有从文章中删除任何句子。
