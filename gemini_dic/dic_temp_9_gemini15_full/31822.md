chedda:/ˈtʃɛdə/| n.|切达干酪|A type of firm, pale yellow cheese originally from the village of Cheddar in Somerset, England.  It has a sharp, slightly salty taste and is often used in cooking and sandwiches.|例句：I love the sharp taste of mature cheddar cheese. 我喜欢成熟切达干酪的浓郁味道。

例句：We used cheddar cheese in the macaroni and cheese. 我们在通心粉和奶酪中使用了切达干酪。
