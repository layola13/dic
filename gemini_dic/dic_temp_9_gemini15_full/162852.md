precatory:/ˈprɛkətɔːri/| adj.|恳求的；祈求的；祈使的| Expressing or conveying a request or entreaty;  having the nature of a prayer or supplication.  It often implies a lack of authority or power to enforce the request.|例句：The letter contained a precatory suggestion, not a formal demand. 这封信中包含的是恳求性的建议，而不是正式的要求。

例句：His precatory remarks were ignored by the board. 董事会无视了他的恳求。


近义词：supplicatory, entreating, imploring, pleading


反义词：mandatory, imperative, compulsory, obligatory


词性:形容词
