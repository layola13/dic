slighting:/ˈslaɪtɪŋ/| adj. |轻视的；怠慢的；微不足道的|Treating someone or something with a lack of respect or attention;  insignificant or unimportant.  |例句：Her slighting remark about his work deeply hurt him.  她对他工作的轻蔑言论深深地伤害了他。

例句：The slighting of his contributions to the project was unfair.  对他在项目中贡献的轻视是不公平的。


近义词：disrespectful, insulting, minimizing, belittling, unimportant, trivial

反义词：respectful, honoring, important, significant, substantial

词性:形容词
