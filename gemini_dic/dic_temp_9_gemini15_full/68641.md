far:/fɑːr/|adj./adv.|远的，遥远的；非常，很|Far describes something that is a great distance away.  It can be used as an adjective or an adverb.|例句：The school is far from my house. 学校离我家很远。|例句：He threw the ball far. 他把球扔得很远。|近义词：distant, remote, long way|反义词：near, close, nearby


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store is far from here. = 这家店离这里很远。
That idea seems far-fetched. = 那个主意似乎很牵强。


一般过去时:
The journey was far longer than we expected. = 这趟旅程比我们预期的要长得多。
They lived far away from their families. = 他们住得离家人很远。


现在完成时:
We have come a long way, but we still have far to go. = 我们已经走了很长一段路，但我们还有很长的路要走。
She has traveled far and wide. = 她走遍了世界各地。


过去完成时:
They had traveled far before they reached their destination. = 他们到达目的地之前已经走了很远的路。
I had gone far beyond my limits. = 我已经远远超出了我的极限。


将来时:
We will travel far in the future. = 我们将来会走得很远。
The project will go far beyond the initial budget. = 这个项目将远远超出最初的预算。


虚拟语气:
If I were far away, I would be peaceful. = 如果我在很远的地方，我会很平静。
I wish I were far from this chaos. = 我希望我远离这场混乱。


被动语态:
The destination was considered far away. = 目的地被认为很远。
The journey was seen as far too long. = 这段旅程被认为太长了。


疑问句:
How far is it to the airport? = 到机场有多远？
Is it far to walk to the station? = 走路到车站远吗？


否定句:
The destination is not far away. = 目的地不远。
The house isn't far from here. = 这房子离这儿不远。



PHRASE->
far and away: 绝对地，无疑地
far from: 远非，决非
so far: 到目前为止
as far as: 就...而言；直到...
far cry from: 大相径庭
