paedogenesis:/ˌpiːdəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |幼体生殖|The reproduction of an organism in its larval or immature stage.  This process bypasses the typical adult stage of development.  It is often seen in certain insects and amphibians. |Example: Paedogenesis is a fascinating example of how organisms can adapt their reproductive strategies to their environment. 幼体生殖是生物如何适应其环境的繁殖策略的一个引人入胜的例子。|近义词：neoteny (in some contexts)|反义词：None directly applicable.
