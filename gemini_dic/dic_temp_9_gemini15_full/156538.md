pick-your-own: /pɪk jɔːr ˈoʊn/ | adj. | 自选的；自己挑选的 |  Describes a system or place where customers can select items themselves, often from a field or display, rather than having them pre-selected.  | Example: We went to a pick-your-own strawberry farm. 我们去了一个自摘草莓的农场。 |近义词：self-select, self-service | 反义词：pre-selected, pre-picked |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We pick our own apples every fall. = 我们每年秋天都自己摘苹果。
They pick their own flowers at the market. = 他们在市场上自己挑选鲜花。

一般过去时:
We picked our own pumpkins last Halloween. = 我们去年万圣节自己摘了南瓜。
She picked her own bouquet for the wedding. = 她自己挑选了婚礼用的花束。

现在完成时:
I have picked my own vegetables all summer. = 我整个夏天都在自己种菜。
They have picked their own berries for the pie. = 他们自己采摘了做馅饼的浆果。

过去完成时:
We had picked our own tomatoes before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前，我们已经自己摘了西红柿。
She had picked her own herbs for the dinner party. = 她为晚宴自己采摘了香草。

将来时:
We will pick our own cherries next weekend. = 我们下周末将自己采摘樱桃。
They will pick their own sunflowers for the display. = 他们将为展览自己采摘向日葵。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would pick our own fruit. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会自己采摘水果。
I wish I could pick my own grapes for wine-making. = 我希望我能自己采摘葡萄酿酒。

被动语态:
The strawberries are picked by customers themselves. = 草莓由顾客自己采摘。
The flowers were picked by the bride herself. = 花是由新娘自己挑选的。

疑问句:
Do you pick your own vegetables? = 你自己种菜吗？
Will they pick their own apples this year? = 他们今年会自己摘苹果吗？

否定句:
We don't pick our own oranges; we buy them at the store. = 我们不自己摘橙子；我们在商店买。
They didn't pick their own flowers for the prom; they bought them. = 他们没有自己挑选舞会的鲜花；他们买了。
