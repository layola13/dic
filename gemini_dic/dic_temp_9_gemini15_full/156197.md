词源:
From Greek *phren-, *phrēn "diaphragm, midriff, mind," from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreh₂- "to burn, glow," referring to the warmth felt in the diaphragm.  The connection to "mind" reflects ancient beliefs about the diaphragm's role in emotions and thought.
