drumlike:/ˈdrʌmlaɪk/| adj.|像鼓的；鼓状的|Resembling a drum in shape or sound.  The word combines "drum" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The low, drumlike sound echoed through the cavern.  低沉的、像鼓一样的声音在洞穴中回荡。The large, drumlike container held the grain.  那个大大的、像鼓一样的容器装着谷物。|近义词：drum-shaped, cylindrical, resonant|反义词：flat, shallow, quiet
