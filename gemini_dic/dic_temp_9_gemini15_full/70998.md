firewood:/ˈfaɪərwʊd/| n. |柴火，木柴|Wood used for fuel, especially in a fireplace or wood-burning stove.|例句：We gathered firewood for the campfire. 我们收集柴火生营火。|例句：The fireplace needs more firewood to keep the room warm.壁炉需要更多柴火来保持房间温暖。|近义词：fuel, kindling, logs|反义词：None specifically.
