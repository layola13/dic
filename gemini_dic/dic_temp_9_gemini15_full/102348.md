/hwɑːt/ | pron.|什么，什么东西|What is used to ask for information about something, to introduce a clause expressing a consequence, or to indicate something unexpected or surprising.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which, who |反义词:nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to protect the environment? = 正在采取什么措施来保护环境？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She didn't say what she wanted. = 她没说她想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么样？/你好吗？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What if...? = 如果...怎么办？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
No matter what = 无论如何
