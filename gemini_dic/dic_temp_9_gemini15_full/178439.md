reconsigns:/ˌriːˈkɒnsaɪnz/| vt.|重新托运；再寄送|To send (goods) again by a different carrier or route after they have already been shipped.|The company reconsigned the damaged goods via a faster shipping route.|近义词：reship, forward, re-route|反义词：receive, collect
