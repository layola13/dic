dialler: /ˈdaɪələr/ | n. |拨号器，电话拨号盘| A device used to dial a telephone number, either manually or automatically.  It can refer to a physical rotary dial or a keypad, or the software component in a computer or mobile phone that allows you to make calls. |例句：The old phone had a rotary dialler.  这个旧电话有一个旋转拨号盘。

例句：My smartphone uses a touch-screen dialler. 我的智能手机使用触摸屏拨号器。


近义词：keypad, dial pad, caller


反义词：无


词性: 名词
