nonmatriculated:/ˌnɒnˈmætrɪkjuleɪtɪd/| adj.|未注册的；非注册学生的；未入学的 |Not officially enrolled as a student at a college or university.  This term typically refers to students who are taking courses but haven't yet been formally admitted to a degree program. |例句：The nonmatriculated students were allowed to audit the course.  未注册的学生被允许旁听这门课程。

The nonmatriculated student attended lectures but couldn't receive credit.  这位未注册的学生参加了讲座，但不能获得学分。|近义词：unenrolled, unregistered, unaffiliated |反义词：matriculated, enrolled, registered
