nonruminant:/ˌnɒnˈruːmɪnənt/| adj. |非反刍动物的|An animal that does not have a rumen (a part of the stomach used for digesting plant matter).  Nonruminants typically have a simpler digestive system than ruminants. They cannot efficiently digest cellulose, the main component of plant cell walls.|Example:  Horses and pigs are nonruminant mammals.  马和猪是非反刍动物。|近义词: monogastric|反义词: ruminant
