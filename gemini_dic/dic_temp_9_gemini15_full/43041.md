cowhide:/ˈkaʊhaɪd/| n. |牛皮，黄牛皮|Leather made from the hide of a cow. It is often used in making leather goods such as belts, shoes, and bags. |例句：The craftsman used cowhide to make a sturdy belt.  工匠用牛皮制作了一条结实的皮带。

例句：This handbag is made of high-quality cowhide. 这个手提包是用优质牛皮制成的。|近义词：leather, hide |反义词:无
