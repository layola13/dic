in a sense:/ɪn ə sɛns/| adv.|在某种意义上；从某种角度来说 |In a sense, he was right, although his methods were questionable.  在某种意义上，他是对的，尽管他的方法值得怀疑。|近义词：to some extent, in a way, sort of|反义词：absolutely, completely, entirely
