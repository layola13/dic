commodore:/ˈkɒmədɔːr/| n. |准将；海军上校；（某些公司或俱乐部的）会长；（旧时）大型商船船长 |A commodore is a naval officer of high rank, typically above a captain but below a rear admiral.  The term also historically referred to the master of a large merchant ship. In some organizations, a commodore is a senior officer or leader. |例句：He was promoted to commodore after many years of distinguished service. 他在多年的杰出服役后被晋升为准将。


The company's commodore oversaw all aspects of the business. 公司的会长负责业务的各个方面。|近义词：captain, admiral, president |反义词：private, ensign
