sarcobasis:/ˌsɑːrkəˈbeɪsɪs/ | n. | 肉基；肉质基部 |  Sarcobasis refers to a fleshy or succulent base, often of a plant or fungus.  It describes the thickened, often fleshy part at the base of a structure, providing support and sometimes storage. The term is primarily used in botanical and mycological contexts. | Example: The orchid exhibited a prominent sarcobasis, providing a robust anchor for its aerial growth.  这种兰花展现出明显的肉质基部，为其空中生长提供了坚实的支撑。| 近义词: fleshy base, succulent base | 反义词:  woody base, fibrous base | 名词
