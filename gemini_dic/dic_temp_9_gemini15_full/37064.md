combating: /kəmˈbeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 对抗，与…作斗争 |  The act of fighting against something, especially a difficult problem or opponent.  | 例句：The government is combating inflation with new economic policies. 政府正在用新的经济政策来对抗通货膨胀。

The doctor is combating the disease with new medication. 医生正在用新药对抗这种疾病。

近义词：fighting, struggling, battling, opposing | 反义词：supporting, aiding, assisting | 动词
