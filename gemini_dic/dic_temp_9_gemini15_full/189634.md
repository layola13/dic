salivating: /ˈsælɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |垂涎欲滴的，渴望的 |  Describes someone or something that is excessively eager or desirous, often in relation to food but also applicable to other things.  It implies an intense craving or anticipation. | 例句：The dog was salivating at the sight of the steak.  = 那条狗看到牛排就垂涎欲滴。

The children were salivating over the prospect of a trip to Disneyland. = 孩子们对去迪斯尼乐园的计划非常渴望。| 近义词：craving, longing, yearning, eager | 反义词：indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is salivating over the new car. = 他对这辆新车垂涎欲滴。
The cat is salivating at the smell of tuna. = 猫闻到金枪鱼的味道就流口水了。


一般过去时:
She salivated at the mention of chocolate cake. = 她一提到巧克力蛋糕就垂涎欲滴。
They salivated over the winning lottery numbers. = 他们对中奖彩票号码非常渴望。


现在完成时:
He has been salivating over that promotion for months. = 他几个月中都对那次晋升垂涎欲滴。
The team has been salivating at the chance to play in the finals. = 这支球队一直渴望参加决赛。


过去完成时:
I had salivated over the menu all afternoon. = 我整个下午都在垂涎菜单上的菜肴。
She had salivated over the idea of owning a yacht. = 她一直渴望拥有一艘游艇。


将来时:
They will be salivating when they see the buffet. = 他们看到自助餐时会垂涎欲滴。
We will be salivating over the results of the election. = 我们将对选举结果翘首以盼。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen that dessert, I would have been salivating. = 如果我看到那个甜点，我会垂涎欲滴。
If I were to win the lottery, I would be salivating at the possibilities. = 如果我中了彩票，我会对各种可能性垂涎欲滴。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The food was described as being so delicious that everyone was salivating. = 食物被描述得如此美味，以至于每个人都垂涎欲滴。


疑问句:
Are you salivating over the new phone? = 你对这款新手机垂涎欲滴吗？
Was he salivating over the opportunity? = 他对这个机会垂涎欲滴吗？


否定句:
He wasn't salivating at all; he was simply hungry. = 他一点也不垂涎欲滴；他只是饿了。
She didn't salivate at the thought of public speaking. = 她想到公开演讲并不感到兴奋。
