heartsick: /ˈhɑːrtsɪk/ | adj. | 心碎的；悲伤的；忧郁的 | Extremely sad and unhappy, often because of loss or disappointment.  |例句：She was heartsick after the death of her pet dog. 她宠物狗死后非常伤心。|例句：The news left him heartsick and despairing. 这个消息让他心碎绝望。|近义词：devastated, heartbroken, grief-stricken, despondent |反义词：joyful, elated, cheerful, happy |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is heartsick over the loss of his job. 他对失去工作感到心碎。
She is heartsick because her best friend moved away. 因为她最好的朋友搬走了，她感到心碎。


一般过去时:
He was heartsick after the breakup. 分手后他很伤心。
She was heartsick when she failed the exam. 她考试不及格时非常难过。


现在完成时:
I have been heartsick ever since my grandmother passed away.  自从祖母去世后，我一直很伤心。
They have been heartsick waiting for news. 他们一直在焦急地等待消息，心情很沉重。


过去完成时:
He had been heartsick for months before he finally sought help. 他在最终寻求帮助之前已经伤心了好几个月。
She had been heartsick all day before she heard the good news.  在她听到好消息之前，她一整天都很伤心。


将来时:
I will be heartsick if my team loses the game. 如果我的球队输了比赛，我会非常伤心。
He will be heartsick when he learns the truth. 当他得知真相时，他会非常伤心。


虚拟语气:
I would be heartsick if anything happened to my child. 如果我的孩子发生什么事，我会非常伤心。
If I were in his shoes, I would be heartsick too. 如果我处在他的位置，我也会很伤心。


被动语态:  (Heartsick is rarely used passively)
*The passive voice is not natural for this adjective.*


疑问句:
Is she heartsick about the situation? 她对这种情况感到伤心吗？
Are you heartsick after hearing the bad news? 听到坏消息后，你感到伤心吗？


否定句:
He wasn't heartsick, just a little disappointed. 他并不伤心，只是有点失望。
She wasn't heartsick at all; she was relieved. 她一点也不伤心；她松了一口气。
