countertenors: /ˈkaʊntəˌtɛnərz/ | n. | 男高音 | Male singers with a vocal range that extends into the high register typically associated with female voices.  Countertenors achieve this high range through a technique called falsetto, although some possess a naturally high voice. They often sing roles traditionally written for female voices in Baroque and Renaissance music. | The countertenors in the choir delivered a breathtaking performance.  |  alto, tenor (in some contexts) | bass |
