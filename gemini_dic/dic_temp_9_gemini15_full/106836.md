laborious:/ləˈbɔːriəs/ | adj. | 费力的；艰难的；辛苦的 | Requiring considerable effort, time, and energy.  The task was laborious and took several weeks to complete. |  arduous, strenuous, painstaking, difficult | easy, simple, effortless |


例句：

The climb up the mountain was laborious. = 爬上这座山非常费力。

She completed the laborious task of sorting through hundreds of documents. = 她完成了费力的工作，整理了数百份文件。


近义词：arduous, strenuous, painstaking, toilsome, taxing


反义词：easy, effortless, simple, straightforward
