outleapt:/ˌaʊtˈliːpt/| v.|跳出，超越|To leap or jump out; to surpass or exceed.|The rabbit outleapt the fox's grasp.  兔子跳出了狐狸的抓捕范围。|The new technology outleapt all previous attempts. 新技术超越了以往所有尝试。|surpass, exceed, transcend|remain behind, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outleaps the hurdle. 他跳过了障碍。
The athlete outleaps his competitor.  运动员跳过了他的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
The horse outleapt the fence. 马跳过了栅栏。
She outleapt her expectations. 她超出了自己的预期。


现在完成时:
He has outleapt all his previous personal bests. 他已经超越了他以往所有的个人最好成绩。
The company has outleapt its competitors in the market. 这家公司在市场上超越了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
The runner had outleapt his previous record before the final race.  在决赛之前，这位跑步运动员已经超越了他之前的记录。
The project had outleapt the initial budget before completion.  项目在完成之前就超出了最初的预算。


将来时:
The gymnast will outleap the bar. 体操运动员将跳过横杆。
The stock price will outleap the forecast. 股价将会超过预期。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outleapt the others. 如果他训练更努力，他就会超过其他人。
If she had more experience, she could have outleapt her limitations. 如果她更有经验，她就能克服她的局限性。


被动语态:
The record was outleapt by a young athlete. 这项纪录被一位年轻运动员打破了。
The expectations were outleapt by the phenomenal success.  预期被非凡的成功所超越。


疑问句:
Did he outleap the fence? 他跳过了栅栏吗？
Will the company outleap its rivals? 这家公司会超越竞争对手吗？


否定句:
He did not outleap the wall. 他没有跳过墙。
The plan did not outleap the challenges. 计划没有克服挑战。
