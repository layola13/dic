rolley:/ˈrɒli/| n. |手推车，行李车|A trolley is a small cart or carriage, typically on wheels, used for carrying things.  It can refer to various types of carts, from those used in supermarkets to those used for luggage at airports. |例句：The airport worker used a trolley to transport the passengers' luggage. 机场工作人员用一辆手推车运送乘客的行李。

例句：She pushed a trolley full of groceries down the supermarket aisle. 她推着一辆装满杂货的手推车走过超市的过道。


近义词：cart, handcart, truck (in some contexts) |反义词：无
