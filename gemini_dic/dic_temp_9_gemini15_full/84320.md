half-written:/ˈhɑːfˈrɪtn/| adj.| 半写成的；未写完的 |Partially written; incomplete.  |例句：The report is half-written, and I need more time to finish it. 报告只写了一半，我还需要更多时间来完成它。  |近义词：unfinished, incomplete, partial |反义词：completed, finished, whole


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  The usage depends on the sentence structure.  Here are examples showing its use in different sentence structures:


Affirmative Sentence:
The half-written letter lay on the desk. =  那封未写完的信放在桌子上。
His half-written novel needs more work. = 他那本未写完的小说还需要修改。


Negative Sentence:
The document wasn't half-written; it was completely finished. = 文件并非半写成的；它是完全写完了的。
It wasn't a half-written story; it was a complete draft. = 它不是个半写成的故事；而是一个完整的草稿。


Interrogative Sentence:
Is the essay half-written? = 这篇论文写了一半了吗？
Was the assignment half-written when you submitted it? = 你提交作业的时候，作业写了一半吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "half-written".  However, you could use it in phrases like:

a half-written story = 一个未写完的故事
a half-written song = 一首未写完的歌
a half-written letter = 一封未写完的信
a half-written report = 一份未写完的报告
