equalizer:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzər/ | n. |均衡器，平衡器 | A device or system that makes things equal or balances them.  It can refer to audio equipment that adjusts the volume of different frequencies, or a system that balances different factors. | Example:  The sound engineer used an equalizer to boost the bass in the recording.  声音工程师用均衡器来增强录音中的低音。 The government implemented an equalizer to distribute wealth more fairly. 政府实施了均衡器以更公平地分配财富。|近义词：balancer, leveler |反义词：distorter, unbalancer
