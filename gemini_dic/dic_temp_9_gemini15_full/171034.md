pseudostoma:/ˌsuːdoʊˈstoʊmə/| n. |假口, 假口器|A pseudostoma is a false mouth or an opening that resembles a mouth but does not function as one.  It may be a morphological feature found in certain organisms or a descriptive term for an artificial structure. |Example: The parasitic organism developed a pseudostoma to attach itself to its host.  寄生生物进化出一个假口来附着在宿主身上。|近义词: false mouth, imitation mouth |反义词: true mouth, stoma
