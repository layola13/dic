rifling:/ˈraɪflɪŋ/| n.|膛线；螺旋槽|The grooves cut into the barrel of a firearm to make the bullet spin, thereby increasing accuracy and range.|例句：The rifling in the gun barrel was worn, affecting its accuracy.  枪管的膛线磨损了，影响了它的精度。

例句：The precise rifling of the new rifle significantly improved its long-range performance. 新步枪精确的膛线大大提高了它的远距离性能。


近义词：grooving (in a firearm barrel)


反义词：smooth bore (lack of rifling)


词性：名词
