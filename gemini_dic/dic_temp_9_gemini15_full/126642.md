naïve:/naɪˈiːv/| adj.|天真的；幼稚的；轻信的|Lacking worldly experience and understanding; showing a lack of judgment or experience.  It often implies a charming innocence, but can also suggest foolishness or gullibility.|例句：She was so naïve that she believed everything he said. 她太天真了，以至于相信了他说的所有话。|例句：His naïve approach to business cost him a lot of money. 他幼稚的商业手法让他损失了很多钱。|近义词：innocent, unsophisticated, ingenuous, credulous|反义词：sophisticated, worldly, cynical, shrewd|
