idioms:/ˈɪdiəm/| n. |习语，成语 |A phrase or expression whose meaning cannot be understood from the meanings of the individual words. Idioms are often culturally specific and figurative. |例句： "Kick the bucket" is an idiom meaning "to die".  "踢桶子"是一个习语，意思是"死了"。

|例句：  The idiom "break a leg" is used to wish someone good luck.  习语“break a leg” 用于祝愿某人好运。|近义词：proverb, saying, expression |反义词：literal meaning
