sciurine:/ˈsaɪəraɪn/| n. |松鼠类动物|A sciurine is a member of the Sciurinae subfamily of squirrels, characterized by their bushy tails and often arboreal lifestyle.  They are diverse and found across various habitats globally.|Example:  Many sciurine species are highly adaptable and can thrive in both forests and urban environments.|近义词：squirrel (general term)|反义词：None (no direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic grouping)|名词
