rudimental:/ˌruːdɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 基本的；初步的；未发展的 | rudimentary refers to something being in its earliest stages of development or being basic and undeveloped. It suggests a lack of complexity or sophistication.  | The child showed only a rudimentary understanding of mathematics.  这个孩子只显示出对数学的基本理解。 | basic, elementary, fundamental, primitive | advanced, sophisticated, complex, developed

The building is in a rudimentary state of construction. = 这座建筑物处于建设的初步阶段。
