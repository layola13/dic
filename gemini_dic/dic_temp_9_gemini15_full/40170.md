contented:/kənˈtɛntɪd/ | adj. | 满足的；心满意足的 | feeling or showing satisfaction with one's possessions or situation.  |例句：She felt contented with her life. 她对自己的生活感到满足。

例句：He sat contentedly by the fire, reading a book. 他满意地坐在火炉旁看书。


近义词：satisfied, pleased, happy, fulfilled, gratified | 反义词：dissatisfied, unhappy, discontent, miserable


词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is contented with his simple life. 他对简朴的生活感到满足。
She is always contented, no matter what happens. 无论发生什么，她总是心满意足的。

一般过去时:
He was contented after finishing his work. 完成工作后，他感到很满足。
They were contented with the results of the meeting. 他们对会议的结果很满意。

现在完成时:
She has been contented since she got married. 她结婚后一直很幸福。
He has remained contented despite many challenges. 尽管面临许多挑战，他仍然保持着满足感。

过去完成时:
I had been contented until I lost my job. 在我失业之前，我一直很满足。
They had been contented with their small apartment until their family grew.  直到他们家人口增加，他们才对他们的狭小公寓感到不满。

将来时:
I will be contented if I can achieve my goals. 如果我能实现我的目标，我会感到满足。
They will be contented once they finish the project.  一旦他们完成项目，他们就会感到满意。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would be contented. 如果我有更多钱，我会感到满足。
I wish I were contented with my current situation. 我希望我对目前的处境感到满意。

被动语态:  (Contented is rarely used passively)  N/A

疑问句:
Is he contented with his job? 他对他的工作满意吗？
Are you contented with your life right now? 你现在对你的生活满意吗？

否定句:
He is not contented with his current salary. 他对目前的薪水不满意。
She was not contented with the service she received. 她对收到的服务不满意。
