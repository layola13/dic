extense:/ɪkˈstɛns/| adj.|广阔的；广泛的；大量的|Extensive means covering a large area or a wide range. It suggests something is large in scope or amount.|例句：The museum boasts an extensive collection of ancient artifacts.  这个博物馆拥有大量的古代文物收藏。|例句：Her knowledge of literature is extensive.  她对文学的知识非常渊博。|近义词：comprehensive, vast, wide-ranging, broad|反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, small
