darners:/ˈdɑːrnərz/| n.| 蜻蜓；缝补者 |  The word "darners" is the plural form of "darner," referring primarily to dragonflies, especially larger ones.  It can also, less commonly, refer to someone who darns (mends) clothes. | 例句：The children chased the colorful darners flitting near the pond.孩子们追逐着在池塘边飞舞的五彩缤纷的蜻蜓。  The old woman was a skilled darner, mending clothes for the whole village.那位老妇人是一位熟练的缝补者，为全村的人缝补衣服。 | 近义词：dragonflies, needlewomen (for the mending sense) | 反义词：None directly applicable.
