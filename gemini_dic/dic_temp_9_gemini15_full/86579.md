heliocentrical:/ˌhiːliəʊˈsɛntrɪkəl/| adj.|日心说的；以太阳为中心的| Relating to or denoting the astronomical model in which the earth and planets revolve around the sun.|The heliocentrical model replaced the geocentric model.|近义词：sun-centered|反义词：geocentric
