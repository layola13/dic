self-levelling: /ˈsɛlf ˈlɛvəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 自流平的，自动调平的 | Automatically leveling itself; requiring no manual leveling.  This adjective describes something that automatically adjusts to a level surface.  For example, a self-levelling floor automatically becomes flat, even if the underlying surface is uneven. | 例句：The self-levelling compound smoothed out the uneven floor.  自流平化合物使不平整的地面变得平整。

The new self-levelling cement requires minimal effort to achieve a perfectly flat surface. 新的自流平水泥只需最少的努力就能获得完美的平整表面。


近义词：self-aligning, automatic-levelling | 反义词：unlevelled, uneven


词性: adjective
