paretic:/pəˈretɪk/ | adj. | 瘫痪的；麻痹的 | Affected by paresis; partially paralyzed.  The condition is characterized by muscle weakness and impaired motor function due to nerve damage or disease. | Example: The stroke left him with a paretic arm. 中风使他的手臂瘫痪。  The patient exhibited paretic symptoms in their lower extremities. 病人下肢出现瘫痪症状。|近义词：paralyzed, palsied, weakened | 反义词：strong, healthy
