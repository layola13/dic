kidnapper:/ˈkɪdnæpər/| n. |绑架者|A person who kidnaps someone.  A kidnapper is someone who unlawfully takes a person captive, usually for ransom or other gain.  The act itself is a serious crime with severe consequences.|例句：The police are searching for the kidnapper who took the child.  警察正在搜寻绑架了孩子的绑架犯。

The kidnapper demanded a large sum of money for the victim's release. 绑匪要求支付巨额赎金才能释放受害者。|近义词：abductor, captor|反义词：victim, rescuer
