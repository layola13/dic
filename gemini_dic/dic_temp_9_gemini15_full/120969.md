mischief:/ˈmɪstʃɪf/| n. |恶作剧；淘气；调皮；损害 |  Mischief refers to playful misbehavior or troublemaking, often causing minor harm or inconvenience. It can also refer to harm or damage caused, often playfully. |例句：The children were up to mischief all afternoon.  孩子们整个下午都在搞恶作剧。

The storm caused considerable mischief to the crops. 暴风雨对庄稼造成了相当大的损害。|近义词：prank, naughtiness, trouble, harm |反义词：good behavior, obedience, order
