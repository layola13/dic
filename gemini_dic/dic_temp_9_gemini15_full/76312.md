/ˈɡæbər/| n.|喋喋不休的人，饶舌者|A gabber is a person who talks incessantly and often about unimportant matters.  They are characterized by their excessive and often rambling speech.|He's a real gabber; I could barely get a word in edgewise. 他是个真正的饶舌者；我几乎插不上话。| chatterbox, blabbermouth, talkative person| quiet person, reserved person|名词

一般现在时:
He gabbers incessantly. = 他喋喋不休。
She gabbers about her trip all day. = 她整天都在喋喋不休地谈论她的旅行。

一般过去时:
He gabbered throughout the meeting. = 他在整个会议上都在喋喋不休。
She gabbered until everyone left. = 她一直喋喋不休地说，直到每个人都离开。

She has gabbered so much that I have a headache. = 她说了太多，我头都痛了。
