It indicates a situation where something is not entirely exclusive but possesses some degree of exclusivity.  This word is rarely used and might appear in highly specialized contexts. | Example: The research findings showed semiexclusivenesses in the distribution of resources among the different social groups.  研究结果表明，在不同社会群体之间的资源分配中存在部分排他性。 | 近义词: partial exclusivity, limited exclusivity | 反义词: inclusiveness, complete accessibility |  名词
