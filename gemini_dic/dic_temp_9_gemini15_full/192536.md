scud:/skʌd/| v. n.|疾驰，飞奔；快速移动；碎云；|To move swiftly, especially in a hurried or furtive manner; a small, fleeting cloud.|The clouds scudded across the sky. 云快速地飘过天空。|The ship scudded before the wind. 船在风中飞驰。|近义词：dash, rush, flit, scurry|反义词：crawl, creep, meander


USAGE->

The clouds scud across the sky. = 云快速地飘过天空。
He scuds through the streets. = 他在街上飞奔。

The ship scudded before the strong wind. = 船在强风中飞驰。
The rain scudded against the window. = 雨点拍打着窗户。

The storm has scudded away. = 风暴已经过去了。
The clouds have scudded across the moon. = 云彩已经飘过了月亮。
