replating:/riˈpleɪtɪŋ/| n. |再镀；重新电镀；再铺设|The process of applying a new coating to a surface, typically a metal, to restore its appearance or protect it from corrosion.  It is often used in manufacturing and electronics to repair or refurbish components. |例句：The factory performs replating of worn-out engine parts.  工厂对磨损的发动机零件进行重新电镀。

The circuit boards require careful replating to ensure conductivity. 电路板需要仔细地重新电镀以确保导电性。|近义词：recoating, refinishing |反义词：stripping (removing the coating)
