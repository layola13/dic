mimicking: /ˈmɪmɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 模仿，模拟 | Imitating someone or something's behaviour or speech.  |例句：The child was mimicking his father's walk.  孩子在模仿他父亲的走路姿势。|近义词：imitating, copying, aping, mocking | 反义词：originating, innovating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is mimicking my accent. = 他正在模仿我的口音。
The parrot is mimicking human speech. = 这只鹦鹉正在模仿人类的说话。


一般过去时:
She mimicked her teacher's voice. = 她模仿了她老师的声音。
The comedian mimicked several famous politicians. = 喜剧演员模仿了几位著名的政治家。


现在完成时:
The students have been mimicking each other all day. = 学生们一整天都在互相模仿。
He has mimicked that style for years. = 他模仿那种风格已经很多年了。


过去完成时:
She had mimicked the famous actress's performance before the audition. = 在试镜之前，她已经模仿过这位著名女演员的表演。
They had mimicked the bird's song perfectly. = 他们已经完美地模仿了鸟的歌声。


将来时:
They will be mimicking the dance moves in the performance. = 他们将在表演中模仿舞蹈动作。
The robot will mimic human expressions. = 机器人将模仿人类的表情。


虚拟语气:
If he mimicked her better, she might laugh. = 如果他模仿得更好，她可能会笑。
I wish I could mimic her singing voice. = 我真希望我能模仿她的歌声。


被动语态:
His accent was mimicked by many students. = 他的口音被许多学生模仿。
The comedian's performance was mimicked online. = 喜剧演员的表演在网上被模仿。


疑问句:
Is he mimicking anyone? = 他在模仿任何人吗？
Are they mimicking the animals' sounds? = 他们正在模仿动物的声音吗？


否定句:
He didn't mimic her style. = 他没有模仿她的风格。
They were not mimicking the original song. = 他们没有模仿原版歌曲。
