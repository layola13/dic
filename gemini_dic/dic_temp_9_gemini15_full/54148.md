disenjoy:/dɪsɪnˈdʒɔɪ/| vt.|不享受，厌恶|To experience something unpleasant or undesirable; to not enjoy something.|例句：I disenjoyed the movie; it was too long and boring. = 我不喜欢这部电影；它太长太无聊了。 He disenjoyed his time at the party. = 他不喜欢在派对上度过的时间。|近义词：dislike, hate, detest|反义词：enjoy, relish, savor|动词

I disenjoy this music. = 我不喜欢这音乐。
She disenjoys her job. = 她不喜欢她的工作。
