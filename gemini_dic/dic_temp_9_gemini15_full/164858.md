preformulating: /ˌpriːˈfɔːrmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先制剂；预先配方 |  The act of preparing a formulation in advance, often involving the selection and testing of ingredients and processes before large-scale production.  It's a crucial step in pharmaceutical development and other industries dealing with complex mixtures. | Example:  The scientists were preformulating the drug to optimize its stability and bioavailability. 科学家们正在预先配制药物以优化其稳定性和生物利用度。|近义词: premixing, preparing, formulating beforehand |反义词: improvising, ad-libbing
