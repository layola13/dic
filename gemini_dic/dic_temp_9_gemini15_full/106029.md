kneads:/nidz/| vt.|揉捏；按摩；搓揉|To work and press (dough or clay) with the hands to make it smooth and elastic.  It can also refer to manipulating something with pressure and force.|例句：She kneaded the dough until it was smooth and elastic. 她揉面团直到它光滑有弹性。
例句：The therapist kneaded the knots out of his back.  理疗师揉开了他背上的结。|近义词：massage, work, mold|反义词：stretch (in some contexts)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She kneads the dough every morning. = 她每天早上都揉面团。
He kneads his shoulders after a long day. = 他长途跋涉后会按摩肩膀。

一般过去时:
I kneaded the clay until it was soft. = 我揉捏粘土直到它变软。
The baker kneaded the bread dough for ten minutes. = 面包师揉面团揉了十分钟。

现在完成时:
She has kneaded the dough for the bread. = 她已经揉好了面包的面团。
He has kneaded his muscles to relieve the soreness. = 他已经按摩肌肉来缓解酸痛。

过去完成时:
I had kneaded the clay before I started sculpting. = 我在开始雕塑之前已经揉捏过粘土了。
She had kneaded the dough for hours before the bread was ready. = 面包做好之前，她已经揉面团好几个小时了。

将来时:
I will knead the dough for the pizza tonight. = 今晚我将揉比萨饼的面团。
She will knead her aching muscles after the workout. = 锻炼后她会按摩酸痛的肌肉。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would knead the dough more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地揉面团。
I wish I had kneaded the clay longer; it’s too brittle. = 我希望我揉粘土的时间更长一些；它太脆了。

被动语态:
The dough was kneaded until it was smooth. = 面团被揉捏直到光滑。
His muscles were kneaded by a professional masseuse. = 他的肌肉被专业的按摩师按摩了。

疑问句:
Do you knead the dough by hand or with a machine? = 你用手还是用机器揉面团？
Does he regularly knead his neck and shoulders? = 他经常按摩脖子和肩膀吗？

否定句:
She doesn't knead the dough very often. = 她不经常揉面团。
He didn't knead the clay enough; it cracked during firing. = 他没有充分揉捏粘土；在烧制过程中它裂开了。


PHRASE->
knead the dough = 揉面团
knead the clay = 揉粘土
knead the muscles = 按摩肌肉
deep knead = 深度按摩
knead into = 揉入 (例如：knead the yeast into the flour 将酵母揉进面粉里)
