rechoreographed:/ˌriːkɔːriːəˈɡræft/| v. |重新编排舞蹈（或音乐）|To choreograph something again, often with changes or improvements.  It implies a previous choreography existed and is being revised. |The ballet was rechoreographed by a renowned dancer.  芭蕾舞由一位著名的舞蹈家重新编排。
The conductor rechoreographed the symphony to highlight the percussion section. 指挥重新编排了交响乐，以突出打击乐部分。|re-choreograph, rearrange|unchoreographed
