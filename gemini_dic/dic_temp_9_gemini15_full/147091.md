overlied:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪd/ | vt. |覆盖；压倒；躺在…上面|To lie on top of something or someone.  It implies a covering or a state of being positioned above.|例句：The blanket overlied the sleeping child.  毯子盖在熟睡的孩子身上。
The heavy snow overlied the landscape. 厚厚的积雪覆盖了整个景色。|近义词：cover, overlay, suppress|反义词：uncover, reveal|动词
