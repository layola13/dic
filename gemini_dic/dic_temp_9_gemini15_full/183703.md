reruns:/ˈriːrʌnz/| n.|重播，重演|Repeated broadcasts of television programs or films.  The word implies that these are not original broadcasts but rather a later showing of previously aired material.|例句：Many people enjoy watching reruns of their favorite sitcoms. 很多人喜欢看他们最喜欢的喜剧的重播。

例句：The local channel is showing reruns of old movies tonight. 当地频道今晚正在播放老电影的重播。


近义词：repeats, rebroadcasts|反义词：original broadcasts, premieres|词性:名词
