pro-Jordan:/ˌproʊˈdʒɔːrdn/| adj.|亲约旦的，支持约旦的|Pro-Jordan refers to something or someone that supports or favors Jordan, its government, or its people.  It often implies a political or ideological stance.|例句：The pro-Jordan demonstrations were peaceful and well-organized. 亲约旦示威游行和平且组织良好。|例句：His pro-Jordan views are well-known in the region. 他亲约旦的观点在该地区广为人知。|近义词：pro-Jordanian, supportive of Jordan|反义词：anti-Jordan, opposed to Jordan
