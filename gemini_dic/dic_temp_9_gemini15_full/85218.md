harking:/ˈhɑːrkiŋ/| v.|追溯；回忆；暗示；专注于|To mention or refer to something; to recall something from the past; to suggest something indirectly; to pay close attention to something.|例句：He kept harking back to his childhood. 他一直在追忆他的童年。


例句：The speaker harked on the importance of education. 演讲者强调了教育的重要性。


近义词：recalling, remembering, mentioning, suggesting, focusing on


反义词：forgetting, ignoring, overlooking


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is harking back to his college days. 他正在回忆他的大学时光。
She constantly harks on the same old problems. 她总是喋喋不休地谈论那些老问题。


一般过去时:
They harked back to the golden age of Hollywood. 他们追溯到好莱坞的黄金时代。
The lawyer harked on the inconsistencies in the witness's testimony. 律师着重指出证人证词中的矛盾之处。


现在完成时:
He has been harking back to his youth for years. 他多年来一直在追忆他的青春。
She has constantly harked on the need for reform. 她一直强调改革的必要性。


过去完成时:
Before starting the project, they had harked back to their previous experiences. 在开始项目之前，他们回顾了之前的经验。
She had harked on the dangers of smoking for years, but no one listened.  她多年来一直强调吸烟的危害，但没有人听。


将来时:
He will hark back to this moment in his memoirs. 他将在他的回忆录中追溯到这一刻。
They will hark on the environmental consequences of the decision. 他们将强调该决定的环境后果。


虚拟语气:
If he harked back to his roots, he might find peace. 如果他追溯到他的根源，他可能会找到平静。
I wish she wouldn't hark on about her problems all the time. 我希望她不要总是喋喋不休地谈论她的问题。


被动语态:
The past is often harked back to in times of difficulty. 在困难时期，人们常常会追忆过去。
The importance of teamwork was constantly harked on by the manager. 经理不断地强调团队合作的重要性。


疑问句:
Is he still harking on about politics? 他还在喋喋不休地谈论政治吗？
Does she often hark back to her childhood memories? 她经常回忆起童年的记忆吗？


否定句:
He didn't hark back to his difficult childhood. 他没有追忆他艰难的童年。
She didn't hark on the details of the accident. 她没有详细讲述事故的细节。
