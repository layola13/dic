grouty:/ˈɡraʊti/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的；易怒的；粗鲁的 |  Characterized by a bad temper; surly; grumpy.  The word suggests a persistent, ingrained irritability, rather than a fleeting bad mood. | 例句：He was feeling grouty after a long day at work. 他在工作了一整天后感到脾气暴躁。

The grouty old man refused to help the lost child.  那个脾气暴躁的老人拒绝帮助迷路的孩子。| 近义词：grumpy, irritable, surly, crotchety, cantankerous | 反义词：good-natured, amiable, pleasant, cheerful
