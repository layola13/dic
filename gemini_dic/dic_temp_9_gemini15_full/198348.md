showboated:/ˈʃoʊboʊtɪd/| v.|炫耀；卖弄；哗众取宠|To show off one's skills or abilities in a flamboyant or ostentatious manner, often to impress others.  This often implies a lack of genuine skill or an overemphasis on style over substance.|例句：He showboated after scoring the winning goal, much to the chagrin of the opposing team. 他在攻入制胜球后卖弄了一番，这让对方球队非常恼火。
例句：The singer showboated throughout the concert, more focused on her appearance than her singing.  这位歌手在整个演唱会期间都在炫耀自己，更关注的是她的外表而不是她的歌声。|近义词：ostentate, flaunt, grandstand, swagger|反义词：humble, modest, understated|动词
