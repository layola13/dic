languidness:/ˈlæŋɡwɪdnəs/| n. |倦怠；懒散；无力；疲软|A state of being languid; lack of energy; weakness.  It often suggests a pleasant, relaxed state, but can also imply weakness or apathy.|Example: The languidness of the afternoon sun induced a feeling of drowsiness.  午后阳光的慵懒让人昏昏欲睡。 The languidness in his movements betrayed his illness. 他动作的无力暴露了他的病情。|近义词：lethargy, lassitude, listlessness, torpor|反义词：energy, vigor, vitality, animation
