inclinable: /ɪnˈklaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可倾斜的；易受影响的；愿意的 |

英文解析:  Likely or tending to incline; having a disposition or tendency to lean or bend.  Also, easily persuaded or influenced; susceptible.

例句:

The table is inclinable, so be careful not to knock it over.  桌子是可倾斜的，所以小心别把它撞倒。

He is inclinable to believe what people tell him, even if it's not true. 他容易相信别人告诉他的话，即使那是假的。


近义词:  inclined, prone, susceptible, amenable, predisposed

反义词:  unyielding, inflexible, resistant, impervious


词性: adjective
