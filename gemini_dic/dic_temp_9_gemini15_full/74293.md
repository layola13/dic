fractional:/ˈfrækʃənəl/| adj.|小数的；部分的；零碎的|Relating to or denoting a fraction.  It can also describe something that is only a small part of a whole.|例句：The increase in sales was only fractional. 销售额的增长只是微不足道的。

例句：He only completed a fractional portion of the assignment. 他只完成了作业的一小部分。


近义词：partial, minor, insignificant, minute|反义词：whole, entire, complete|词性:形容词
