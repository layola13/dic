He lambastes his employees for minor mistakes. 他因一些小错误而痛斥他的员工。

The policy was lambasted by the public. 该政策受到了公众的痛斥。


疑问句:

She didn't lambaste him; instead, she offered constructive criticism. 她没有痛斥他；相反，她提出了建设性的批评。
