digitigrade: /ˈdɪdʒɪtɪɡreɪd/ | adj. | 趾行的；用脚趾行走的 |  Describing animals that walk on their toes, with their heels raised off the ground.  This is in contrast to plantigrade (walking on the soles of the feet) and unguligrade (walking on hooves).  Many birds, cats, dogs, and other mammals are digitigrade. |例句：Cats are digitigrade animals, meaning they walk on their toes. 猫是趾行动物，这意味着它们用脚趾走路。

例句：Many running animals, such as cheetahs, are digitigrade to increase speed. 许多奔跑的动物，例如猎豹，都是趾行动物，以提高速度。


近义词：toe-walking (informal)


反义词：plantigrade, unguligrade


词性:形容词
