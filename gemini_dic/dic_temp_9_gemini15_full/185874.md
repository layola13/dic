revokement: /rɪˈvoʊkmənt/ | n. |撤销；废除；取消|The act of revoking something; the state of being revoked.  It refers to the formal withdrawal or cancellation of something previously granted, authorized, or enacted. |例句：The revokement of his license was a severe punishment.  他的执照被吊销是对他严厉的惩罚。

The revokement of the contract left the company in a difficult position. 合同的取消使公司陷入困境。


近义词：cancellation, annulment, repeal, rescission

反义词：granting, authorization, confirmation

词性：名词


词源:  Derived from the verb "revoke" + "-ment" (a suffix forming nouns of action or state). "Revoke" itself comes from the Old French "revoquer," ultimately from the Latin "revocare" meaning "to call back."
