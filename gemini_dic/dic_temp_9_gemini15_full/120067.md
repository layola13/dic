milometers:/ˈmɪləˌmiːtərz/| n.|毫米|The word "milometers" appears to be a misspelling of "millimeters".  Millimeters are units of length in the metric system, equal to one thousandth of a meter.  |例句：The thickness of the paper is about 0.1 millimeters. 这张纸的厚度大约是0.1毫米。
The insect measured 5 millimeters in length.  这只昆虫长5毫米。|近义词：millimetres|反义词：kilometers, meters
