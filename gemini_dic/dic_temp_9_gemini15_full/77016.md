garget:/ˈɡɑːrɡɪt/| n.| 溃疡；脓肿；咽喉痛；|A garget is a type of sore throat or inflammation of the throat,  often associated with pus or swelling.  It can also refer to a festering sore or abscess. The term is now largely archaic or dialectal.|例句：The child suffered from a painful garget in his throat.  这个孩子喉咙里长了一个疼痛的溃疡。|近义词：abscess, sore throat, ulcer, inflammation |反义词：health, healing
