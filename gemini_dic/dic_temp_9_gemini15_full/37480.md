commitments:/kəˈmɪtmənts/| n.|承诺；承担的义务；约定|A commitment is a promise or an agreement to do something, or the fact of being obliged to do something. It can refer to a pledge, a responsibility, or a dedication.|例句：She has many commitments, including family and work. 她有很多承诺，包括家庭和工作。

例句：He made a commitment to donate to charity. 他承诺向慈善机构捐款。


近义词：promises, pledges, obligations, responsibilities|反义词：freedom, liberty

My commitments take up most of my time. = 我的承诺占据了我大部分时间。
