orometer:/əˈrɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |高程计；测高仪|An orometer is an instrument used to measure altitude or height, typically above sea level.  It can be a simple aneroid barometer or a more sophisticated device integrated into other equipment, such as aircraft or surveying tools.|Example: The climbers used an orometer to track their ascent up the mountain. 登山者使用高程计追踪他们攀登山峰的过程。|近义词：altimeter, height gauge|反义词：None|名词
