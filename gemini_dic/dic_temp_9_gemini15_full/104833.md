keel-over:/kiːlˈoʊvər/|/ˌkiːlˈəʊvə(r)/| vt. vi.|倾覆；突然倒下；突然死去|To turn over; to capsize; to fall over suddenly; to die suddenly.  |例句：The boat keeled over in the strong wind.  船在强风中倾覆了。The old man keeled over from a heart attack. 老人因心脏病突然去世了。|近义词：capsize, overturn, collapse, succumb|反义词：right itself, recover, survive|动词
