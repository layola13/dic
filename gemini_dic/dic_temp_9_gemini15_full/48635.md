defender:/dɪˈfɛndər/| n. |辩护者；防御者；卫冕者|A person or thing that defends someone or something.  A defender protects something or someone from attack or harm.  It can also refer to a player in sports whose role is to defend their team's goal or territory.|例句：The lawyer acted as the defendant's defender.  律师充当被告的辩护人。

The city's strong defenses made it a formidable defender against attack. 城市坚固的防御工事使其成为难以攻克的防御目标。|近义词：protector, advocate, champion|反义词：attacker, assailant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defends his client vigorously. 他积极地为他的客户辩护。
She defends her beliefs passionately. 她热情地捍卫自己的信仰。

一般过去时:
The knight defended the castle bravely.  骑士英勇地保卫了城堡。
They defended their territory fiercely. 他们激烈地保卫了自己的领土。

现在完成时:
He has defended the innocent throughout his career. 他在整个职业生涯中一直为无辜者辩护。
She has defended her country with great courage. 她以极大的勇气保卫了自己的国家。

过去完成时:
The army had defended the city for months before surrendering.  军队在投降前已经保卫这座城市数月了。
He had defended his thesis successfully before the committee. 他已成功地在委员会面前答辩了他的论文。

将来时:
They will defend their rights in court. 他们将在法庭上捍卫自己的权利。
She will defend her title at the next championship. 她将在下一届锦标赛上卫冕冠军。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would defend myself more forcefully. 如果我处在他的位置，我会更强有力地为自己辩护。
I wish I had defended my ideas more clearly. 我希望我当初更清晰地表达我的观点。

被动语态:
The city was defended by a strong army. 这座城市由一支强大的军队保卫。
His actions were defended by his supporters. 他的行为得到了支持者的辩护。

疑问句:

否定句:
