impurities:/ɪmˈpjʊərətiz/ | n. |杂质，不纯物 |  Substances that are present in a material but are not wanted because they reduce its quality or effectiveness. Impurities can be found in many things, from chemicals to food to water.  |例句：The water sample contained harmful impurities.  这个水样含有有害杂质。

The gold ore needs to be refined to remove impurities.  金矿石需要精炼以去除杂质。|近义词：contaminants, adulterants, foreign substances |反义词：purities
