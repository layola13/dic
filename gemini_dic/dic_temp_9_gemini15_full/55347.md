distad:/ˈdɪstæd/| adv.|远离中心；远离近端|Away from the center; away from the proximal end.  This term is primarily used in anatomy and describes the location of something relative to a central point or a point of reference.  For example, in describing the location of a structure on a limb, "distal" would indicate further away from the body.  The opposite is "proximal".|The wound was located distad to the elbow joint.|近义词：distal|反义词：proximal|副词
