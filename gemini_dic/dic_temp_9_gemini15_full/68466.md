familiarizer: /fəˈmɪljərizaɪzər/ | n. | 使熟悉的人或事物 | A person or thing that makes something more familiar.  It can also refer to a process or technique that aids in familiarization. |  例句：The new employee orientation served as a familiarizer for company policies. 新员工培训为公司政策熟悉化起到了作用。  The interactive tutorial acted as a familiarizer for the software.  交互式教程充当了软件熟悉化的工具。 | 近义词：introducer, guide, tutorial, primer | 反义词：obfuscator, confusing element
