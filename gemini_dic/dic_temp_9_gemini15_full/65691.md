evenings:/ˈiːvnɪŋz/| n. |夜晚，傍晚 |The evenings are getting longer.  傍晚越来越长了。|  nights, twilights | mornings, days |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evenings are cool in autumn. 秋天的傍晚很凉爽。
We often watch TV in the evenings. 我们经常在晚上看电视。

一般过去时:
The evenings were long and dark in winter. 冬天的夜晚又长又黑。
We spent many evenings playing games. 我们度过了许多夜晚玩游戏。

现在完成时:
The evenings have been getting colder recently. 最近傍晚越来越冷了。
We have enjoyed many beautiful evenings this summer. 这个夏天我们享受了许多美好的夜晚。

过去完成时:
The evenings had already turned cold before the snow fell.  下雪之前傍晚就已经变冷了。
We had spent many pleasant evenings together before they moved away. 他们搬走之前，我们一起度过了许多愉快的夜晚。

将来时:
The evenings will be warmer next month. 下个月傍晚会更暖和。
We will have a party in the evenings next week. 我们下周晚上会举办一个派对。

虚拟语气:
If the evenings were longer, we could spend more time outdoors. 如果傍晚更长一些，我们就可以在户外多待一会儿。
I wish the evenings were always as warm as this. 我希望傍晚总是像现在这样温暖。

被动语态:
The evenings are often filled with entertainment. 傍晚经常充满娱乐活动。(此处被动语态略显不自然，更常用的表达是主动语态)
The evenings were lit up by the city lights. 城市的灯光照亮了夜晚。(此处被动语态更自然)


疑问句:
What do you usually do in the evenings? 你晚上通常做什么？
Are the evenings longer in summer? 夏天的夜晚更长吗？

否定句:
The evenings aren't as long as they used to be. 傍晚不像以前那么长了。
We didn't go out much in the evenings last week. 我们上周晚上很少出去。
