gainsayer:/ˈɡeɪnˌseɪər/| n. |反对者；否认者|A gainsayer is a person who contradicts or denies something.  They often express disagreement or opposition to a particular statement, belief, or action. |Example:  Despite the overwhelming evidence, there were still gainsayers who refused to believe the defendant was guilty.  尽管有压倒性的证据，仍然有一些反对者拒绝相信被告有罪。|近义词：opponent, doubter, critic, denier |反义词：supporter, believer, advocate
