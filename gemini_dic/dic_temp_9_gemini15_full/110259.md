lifetimes:/ˈlaɪftaɪmz/| n.|一生，寿命；终身；漫长的时期|The period of time during which something or someone is alive or exists.  It can refer to the entire duration of a person's life, or to a very long period.|例句：He lived a long and happy lifetime. 他过着漫长而幸福的一生。

例句：The project will take several lifetimes to complete. 这个项目需要几代人的时间才能完成。


近义词：lifespans, ages, durations|反义词：death, demise|词性:名词
