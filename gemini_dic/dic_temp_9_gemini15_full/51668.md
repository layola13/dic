devices:/dɪˈvaɪsɪz/ | n. | 设备，装置 |  A device is an object, machine, or piece of equipment designed to serve a particular purpose or perform a particular function.  | Example:  Modern smartphones are remarkable devices. 现代智能手机是了不起的设备。
          |       The inventor patented his new device. 发明家为他的新装置申请了专利。|近义词：apparatus, gadget, instrument, tool, mechanism | 反义词：无 |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The device works perfectly. = 这个设备运行完美。
These devices are used in many industries. = 这些设备被用于许多行业。

一般过去时:
The device malfunctioned yesterday. = 这个设备昨天出故障了。

现在完成时:

They have developed several innovative devices this year. = 他们今年开发了几种创新设备。

过去完成时:
The device had already failed before we could fix it. =在我们能修理之前，设备就已经坏了。
They had used that device for years before replacing it. = 他们在更换之前已经使用了该设备多年。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
