invertebrate:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrət/ | n. | 无脊椎动物 | An animal lacking a backbone or spinal column.  Invertebrates make up the vast majority of animal species on Earth. They include insects, mollusks, crustaceans, arachnids, and many others. | Example:  Spiders are invertebrates.  蜘蛛是无脊椎动物。  Many invertebrates live in the ocean. 许多无脊椎动物生活在海洋中。|近义词： invertebrate animal | 反义词：vertebrate
