overlooking: /ˌoʊvərˈlʊkɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 忽略；俯瞰；眺望 | To fail to notice something; to have a view from above. |


例句：

He was overlooking a crucial detail in the report. 他忽略了报告中一个至关重要的细节。

From the mountain top, we had a breathtaking overlooking of the valley. 从山顶上，我们可以俯瞰令人叹为观止的山谷。


近义词：ignoring, neglecting, disregarding, overlooking, commanding

反义词： noticing, heeding, attending to


词性: 动词，形容词


USAGE->
