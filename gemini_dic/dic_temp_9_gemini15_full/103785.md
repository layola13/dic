USAGE->
一般现在时:
My journey to work takes about an hour. = 我上班的路程大约需要一个小时。
Their journeys across the country are often challenging. = 他们穿越全国的旅程通常充满挑战。


一般过去时:
We journeyed through the mountains last summer. = 我们去年夏天穿越了山区。
He journeyed to a faraway land in search of adventure. = 他为了寻求冒险而前往遥远的国度。


现在完成时:
