prestimuli: /ˌpriːˈstɪmjʊlaɪ/ | n. | 预刺激；前刺激 | Stimuli that precede a target stimulus in an experiment.  They are used to prepare the participant for the target stimulus or to manipulate their state before the target stimulus is presented.  | Example:  The researchers used a series of prestimuli to increase the participants' arousal level before presenting the target images.  研究人员在呈现目标图像之前使用了一系列预刺激来提高参与者的唤醒水平。 | 近义词: antecedent stimuli, preceding stimuli | 反义词: poststimuli | 名词
