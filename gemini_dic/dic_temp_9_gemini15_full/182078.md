remigrated:/ˈriːmaɪɡreɪtɪd/ | v. | 重新移民；再移民 | To migrate again; to return to a country one has previously emigrated from.  |  After living abroad for ten years, she remigrated to her homeland.  十年后，她在国外生活了十年后，又回到了祖国。 | emigrate, repatriate | immigrate  | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They remigrate to warmer climates every winter. = 他们每年冬天都迁移到气候较温暖的地方。
Birds remigrate north in the spring. = 鸟类在春季向北迁徙。


一般过去时:
My grandparents remigrated to Italy after the war. = 我的祖父母在战争结束后回到了意大利。
He remigrated to his native village after many years in the city. = 他在城市生活多年后，又回到了他的家乡。


现在完成时:
Many families have remigrated to the countryside after the pandemic. = 大流行之后，许多家庭已经搬回了农村。
She has remigrated to Canada twice. = 她已经两次移民加拿大。


过去完成时:
They had remigrated before the new law came into effect. = 新法律生效之前，他们已经重新移民了。
Before the revolution, many nobles had remigrated to other countries. = 革命前，许多贵族已经移居其他国家。


将来时:
We will remigrate to the coast once we retire. = 我们退休后将搬到海边。
They plan to remigrate to their ancestral home next year. = 他们计划明年回到祖先的家园。


虚拟语气:
If they had the money, they would remigrate to their homeland. = 如果他们有钱，他们就会回国。
I wish I could remigrate to a warmer country. = 我希望我能移居到一个气候温暖的国家。


被动语态:
Many people were remigrated forcefully during the war. = 战争期间，许多人被迫重新移民。
The policy was designed to encourage people to remigrate to rural areas. = 这一政策旨在鼓励人们重新移民到农村地区。


疑问句:
Have they remigrated yet? = 他们已经重新移民了吗？
Will you remigrate if you get the opportunity? = 如果你有机会，你会重新移民吗？


否定句:
They did not remigrate after all. = 他们最终没有重新移民。
She decided not to remigrate despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，她还是决定不重新移民。
