miscarriage:/ˈmɪskærɪdʒ/| n.|流产，失败|The spontaneous expulsion of a fetus from the uterus before it is viable.  It can also refer to the failure of a plan or project.|例句：She suffered a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她第一次怀孕时流产了。|例句：The peace negotiations ended in a complete miscarriage. 和平谈判彻底失败了。|近义词：abortion (induced miscarriage), stillbirth (fetal death), failure|反义词：successful birth, successful completion|名词
