loans:/loʊnz/| n.|贷款，借款|Money borrowed, usually with interest.|例句：He took out a loan to buy a car. 他贷款买了一辆车。|The bank approved his loan application. 银行批准了他的贷款申请。|近义词：debt, credit, advance|反义词：repayment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes out a loan every year. 他每年都贷款。
She often borrows money from her family. 她经常向家人借钱。


一般过去时:
They took out a large loan to buy a house. 他们贷款买了一栋房子。
I borrowed a small sum of money from a friend. 我向朋友借了一小笔钱。


现在完成时:
We have paid off our loan. 我们已经还清了贷款。
She has borrowed money several times this month. 这个月她已经借过几次钱了。


过去完成时:
They had already repaid their loan before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前已经还清了贷款。
He had borrowed money from his parents before he started his business. 在他创业之前，他已经向父母借过钱了。


将来时:
I will apply for a loan next month. 我下个月将申请贷款。
She will borrow money to pay for her tuition. 她将借钱支付学费。


虚拟语气:
If I had a loan, I would buy a new house. 如果我有贷款，我会买一栋新房子。
I wish I hadn't borrowed so much money. 我真希望我没有借这么多钱。


被动语态:
The loan was approved quickly. 贷款很快就被批准了。
The money was borrowed from a private lender. 钱是从私人贷款人那里借来的。


疑问句:
How much did you borrow? 你借了多少钱？
Can I get a loan from the bank? 我可以从银行贷款吗？


否定句:
He didn't take out a loan. 他没有贷款。
She didn't borrow any money. 她没有借钱。
