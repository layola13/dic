childbearing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌbɛərɪŋ/| n.|生育，分娩，怀孕|The act or process of bearing or giving birth to children.  It also refers to the period of pregnancy and the related physical and emotional changes.|例句：The joys of childbearing are often overshadowed by the hardships. 生育的喜悦常常被其中的艰辛所掩盖。|例句：She experienced complications during childbearing.她在分娩过程中经历了一些并发症。|近义词：pregnancy, maternity, childbirth|反义词：childlessness
