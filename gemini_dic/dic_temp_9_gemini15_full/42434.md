countercycle:/ˈkaʊntərsaɪkl/| adj.| 逆周期的；反周期的 |Acting in opposition to the prevailing economic cycle; designed to mitigate the effects of economic fluctuations.| 例句：The government implemented countercyclical fiscal policies to stimulate the economy during the recession. 政府在经济衰退期间实施了反周期财政政策以刺激经济。

例句：Countercyclical investments aim to profit from market downturns. 反周期投资旨在从市场低迷中获利。


近义词：anticyclical, contracyclical | 反义词：procyclical,  cyclical | 词性：形容词

词源:
The word "countercyclical" is a combination of "counter," meaning "against" or "opposite," and "cyclical," referring to cyclical economic patterns.  It emerged as economists sought to describe policies and actions designed to counteract the ups and downs of the business cycle.
