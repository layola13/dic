retex:/ˈriːteks/| n. |重新编排；重写；重印|The act of re-editing or revising a text, often to improve its quality, clarity, or accuracy.  It can also refer to the reprinting of a text with corrections.|例句：The author did a retex of his manuscript before submitting it to the publisher. 作者在向出版商提交手稿之前对其进行了重新编排。|近义词：rewrite, revise, re-edit|反义词：original draft, first edition
