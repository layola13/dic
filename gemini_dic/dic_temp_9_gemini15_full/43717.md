credulence: /ˈkrɛdjələns/ | n. |轻信；易信；相信|  Belief in something, especially without proof; gullibility.  It implies a readiness to believe, often without sufficient evidence or critical thinking. |The child's credulence made him an easy target for the con man.  孩子的轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。|近义词：gullibility, naiveté, trustfulness |反义词：skepticism, cynicism, incredulity
