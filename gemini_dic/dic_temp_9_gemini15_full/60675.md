electric:/ɪˈlɛktrɪk/ | adj. | 电的；电动的；令人兴奋的 | Relating to or operated by electricity; thrilling or exciting. | 例句：The electric car is very popular now.  现在电动汽车非常流行。

The concert was electric; the audience was captivated.  音乐会非常精彩；观众被迷住了。|近义词：electrical, electronic, thrilling, exciting | 反义词：manual, mechanical


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fan is electric. = 这个风扇是电动的。
This train is electric. = 这列火车是电动的。

一般过去时:
The lights were electric. = 灯是电的。
The car was electric. = 那辆车是电动的。


现在完成时:
We have installed an electric fence. = 我们已经安装了电围栏。
They have chosen an electric kettle. = 他们选择了一个电热水壶。


过去完成时:
The house had already been wired for electric. = 这房子已经布好电线了。
He had driven an electric car before. = 他以前开过电动汽车。


将来时:
They will install electric heating. = 他们将安装电热。
We will buy an electric scooter. = 我们将买一辆电动滑板车。


虚拟语气:
If it were electric, it would be more efficient. = 如果它是电动的，它会更高效。
I wish my car were electric. = 我希望我的车是电动的。


被动语态:
The room is electrically heated. = 这个房间是电热的。
The car is electrically powered. = 这辆车是电力驱动的。


疑问句:
Is the car electric? = 这车是电动的吗？
Is this device electric? = 这个装置是电动的吗？


否定句:
The toy is not electric. = 这个玩具不是电动的。
The system isn't electric. = 这个系统不是电动的。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Middle English *electrik, from Late Latin electricus, from Greek ἤλεκτρον (ēlektron) "amber," so called because of its ability to attract light objects after being rubbed.  The suffix "-ic" denotes "relating to".
