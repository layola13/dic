nondisclosures: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskləʊ.ʒərz/ | n. | 不公开；保密协议 | Agreements or actions that prevent information from being revealed.  Nondisclosures are often used in business to protect sensitive information, such as trade secrets or confidential business plans. They can also be used in personal contexts to protect private information. | Example:  The company signed a nondisclosure agreement to protect its intellectual property.  这家公司签署了一份保密协议以保护其知识产权。


The company implemented strict nondisclosures to safeguard its new product launch strategy. 公司实施了严格的保密措施来保护其新产品的发布策略。


近义词：secrecy, confidentiality, non-revelation | 反义词：disclosure, revelation | 词性: 名词
