redistills:/ˌriːdɪˈstɪlz/| vt.|重新蒸馏；重新提炼；再次仔细考虑|To distill again; to subject to a second distillation.  It can also mean to reconsider something carefully and thoroughly.|例句：The chemist redistilled the solution to remove impurities. = 化学家重新蒸馏溶液以去除杂质。

The perfumer redistilled the essential oils to enhance their fragrance. = 调香师重新蒸馏精油以增强其香味。

近义词：refine, purify, reprocess|反义词：None specifically, but  "to leave as is" could be considered an antonym in the context of refining.|动词
