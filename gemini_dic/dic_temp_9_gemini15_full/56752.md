doorstop:/ˈdɔːrstɑːp/| n. |门挡；阻碍物 |A doorstop is a wedge-shaped object placed at the bottom of a door to keep it open or closed.  It can also refer to something that hinders progress or stops something from happening.|例句：I used a heavy book as a doorstop. 我用一本厚书当门挡。

The new regulations are a doorstop to economic growth. 新规是经济增长的阻碍。|近义词：stopper, wedge, obstacle |反义词：accelerator, facilitator
