exabytes:/ˈɛksəbaɪts/| n. |艾字节，艾位|A unit of information equal to 10<sup>18</sup> bytes (a billion billion bytes).  It's a very large unit of data storage used to measure massive datasets.  | Example: The data center stores exabytes of information daily. 数据中心每天存储艾字节的信息。 |近义词：  (no direct synonyms, but related units): petabytes, zettabytes |反义词： (no direct antonyms, but related units representing smaller amounts of data): kilobytes, megabytes
