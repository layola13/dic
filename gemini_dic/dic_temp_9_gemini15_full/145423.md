overcharged:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒd/| v. adj.|过高收费的；超载的 |To charge (someone) too much money for something.  |例句：I was overcharged for the repair.  = 我修理费被多收了。

The restaurant overcharged us for the wine. = 餐厅的酒价向我们收取过高。


近义词：overpriced, exorbitant, excessive

反义词：undercharged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overcharge customers for their services. = 他们向顾客收取过高的服务费。
The hotel routinely overcharges guests for mini-bar items. = 这家酒店经常向客人收取过高的迷你吧商品费用。


一般过去时:
The mechanic overcharged me for the simple repair. = 修理工因为简单的修理而向我收取了过高的费用。
The taxi driver overcharged us for the short ride. = 出租车司机因为短途车程而向我们收取了过高的费用。


现在完成时:
They have overcharged me on several occasions. = 他们已经多次向我收取了过高的费用。
The company has been overcharging its clients for years. = 这家公司多年来一直向客户收取过高的费用。


过去完成时:
I realized they had overcharged me after I left the store. = 我离开商店后才意识到他们向我收取了过高的费用。
We discovered that the contractor had overcharged us for the materials. = 我们发现承包商在材料方面向我们收取了过高的费用。


将来时:
They will likely overcharge you if you don't check the bill carefully. = 如果你不仔细检查账单，他们很可能会向你收取过高的费用。
I fear they will overcharge us for the additional work. = 我担心他们会因为额外的工作而向我们收取过高的费用。


被动语态:
Customers are often overcharged by unscrupulous businesses. = 顾客经常被不择手段的商家收取过高的费用。
I was overcharged for the phone call. = 我被收取了过高的电话费。


疑问句:
Were we overcharged for the drinks? = 我们酒水费用被收取过高了吗？
Do you think we were overcharged at that restaurant? = 你认为我们在那家餐厅被收取过高的费用了吗？


否定句:
They did not overcharge us for the service. = 他们并没有因为这项服务而向我们收取过高的费用。
The shop assistant assured me that they would not overcharge me. = 店员向我保证他们不会向我收取过高的费用。


PHRASE->
be overcharged for = 因...而被收取过高费用
overcharge on = 在...方面收取过高费用
overcharge someone = 向某人收取过高费用
overcharge a customer = 向顾客收取过高费用
get overcharged = 被收取过高费用


词源:
over- (prefix indicating excess) + charge (verb meaning to demand payment)


单词属性:
verb (transitive) and adjective
