lurp:/lɜːrp/| v.|呕吐，倒胃地发出声音|To make a sound like someone vomiting or retching, often in disgust or with a sudden, involuntary expulsion of air or liquid.|例句：The baby lurped up some milk.  婴儿吐出一些奶。  The smell made me want to lurp. 这气味让我恶心想吐。|近义词：retch, gag, vomit|反义词: swallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lurps when he's nauseous. = 他恶心的时候会发出呕吐声。
She lurps every morning before breakfast. = 她每天早上早餐前都会发出呕吐声。

一般过去时:
The child lurped after eating too much candy. = 孩子吃了太多糖后呕吐了。
He lurped with disgust at the sight of the rotting food. = 他看到腐烂的食物后厌恶地发出呕吐声。

现在完成时:
The cat has lurped up a hairball. = 猫吐出了毛球。
She has lurped several times since getting sick. = 她自从生病以来已经呕吐了好几次。

过去完成时:
He had lurped before the doctor even arrived. = 医生还没到，他就已经吐了。
She had lurped all night before finally falling asleep. = 在最终睡着之前，她整晚都在呕吐。

将来时:
I think I will lurp if I eat any more of that. = 我想如果再吃那个的话我会吐的。
He will probably lurp if he drinks that much alcohol. = 如果他喝那么多酒，他可能会吐。


虚拟语气:
If I ate that, I think I would lurp. = 如果我吃了那个，我想我会吐的。
I wish I hadn't eaten so much; I might lurp. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多；我可能会吐。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)  该动词很少用于被动语态

疑问句:
Did he lurp? = 他吐了吗？
Will she lurp after the ride? = 她坐完过山车后会吐吗？

否定句:
He didn't lurp. = 他没有吐。
She didn't lurp, despite feeling sick. = 尽管感觉不舒服，她也没有吐。


PHRASE->
(This verb doesn't commonly appear in phrases.  Its usage is primarily as a single verb.) 该动词通常不出现在短语中，主要作为单个动词使用。
