indignancy:/ɪnˈdɪɡnənsi/| n. |义愤，愤慨 | a feeling of anger and annoyance caused by something considered unfair or wrong.  |例句：His indignation at the injustice of it all was palpable. 他对这一切的不公正感到义愤填膺，显而易见。

例句：The blatant disregard for safety regulations fueled the workers' indignation. 对安全规章的公然漠视激起了工人们的愤怒。


近义词：anger, resentment, outrage, fury, wrath |反义词：acceptance, complacency, indifference


词性:名词
