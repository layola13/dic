drizzles:/ˈdrɪzəl/| v. n.|毛毛雨；下毛毛雨|Light rain falling in very small drops.  It is a gentle and relatively light form of rain.|例句：The rain drizzled all day. = 雨下了一整天毛毛雨。

The drizzle made the roads slippery. = 毛毛雨使道路湿滑。|近义词：mist, sprinkle, shower|反义词： downpour, deluge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It drizzles lightly today. = 今天下着毛毛雨。
The rain drizzles constantly. = 雨一直下着毛毛雨。

一般过去时:
It drizzled throughout the night. = 整夜下着毛毛雨。
A light drizzle covered the city yesterday. = 昨天毛毛雨覆盖了这座城市。

现在完成时:
It has drizzled intermittently all afternoon. = 整个下午断断续续地下着毛毛雨。
It has drizzled so much that the ground is soaked. = 下了这么多毛毛雨，地面都湿透了。

过去完成时:
It had drizzled for hours before the sun came out. = 太阳出来之前已经下了好几个小时的毛毛雨了。
They had drizzled the cake with chocolate sauce. = 他们已经在蛋糕上淋上了巧克力酱（用作动词的比喻用法）。

将来时:
It will drizzle tomorrow morning. = 明天早上会下毛毛雨。
They predict it will drizzle throughout the weekend. = 他们预测整个周末都会下毛毛雨。

虚拟语气:
If it drizzled tomorrow, we would postpone the picnic. = 如果明天下毛毛雨，我们就推迟野餐。
I wish it hadn't drizzled during the parade. = 我真希望游行期间没有下毛毛雨。

被动语态:
The streets were drizzled with a fine mist. = 街道上蒙上了一层薄雾（被动语态，强调街道被雾覆盖）。
The cake was drizzled with a delicious caramel sauce. = 蛋糕上淋上了美味的焦糖酱。（被动语态，强调蛋糕被淋上酱汁）

疑问句:
Is it drizzling outside? = 外面下毛毛雨了吗？
Was it drizzling when you left? = 你离开的时候下毛毛雨了吗？

否定句:
It didn't drizzle yesterday. = 昨天没有下毛毛雨。
It isn't drizzling right now. = 现在没有下毛毛雨。


PHRASE->

a drizzle of rain = 一阵毛毛雨
