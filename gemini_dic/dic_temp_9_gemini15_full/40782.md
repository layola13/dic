convivial:/kənˈvɪviəl/| adj.|欢乐的；友好的；热闹的 |Friendly and lively; enjoying company.  It describes a social atmosphere or a person's disposition.  The word suggests a cheerful and sociable environment where people are happy to be together. |例句：The party had a convivial atmosphere.  派对气氛热烈友好。

The restaurant is known for its convivial atmosphere. 这家餐厅以其热闹友好的氛围而闻名。


近义词：sociable, friendly, jovial, merry, festive |反义词：unsociable, unfriendly, gloomy, sullen, reserved


词性：形容词
