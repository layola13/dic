profess:/prəˈfɛs/| vt. n.|公开宣称；声称；教授；信奉|to declare or affirm openly; to claim; to teach (a subject) as a professor; to avow one's belief in|例句：He professed his innocence. 他宣称自己是无辜的。|例句：She professed a deep love for her country. 她公开表达了她对祖国的深爱。|近义词：declare, affirm, avow, claim, teach|反义词：deny, disclaim, conceal|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He professes his love for music. = 他公开表达他对音乐的热爱。
She professes to know everything. = 她自称什么都知道。


一般过去时:
He professed his ignorance of the matter. = 他声称自己对此事一无所知。
She professed a strong belief in God. = 她表示自己坚信上帝。


现在完成时:
He has professed his commitment to the cause. = 他已经公开表示了他对这项事业的承诺。
She has professed her love for him many times. = 她已经多次表达了她对他的爱意。


过去完成时:
He had professed his innocence before the trial. = 在审判之前，他已经宣称自己是无辜的。
She had professed her faith in him for years. = 她多年来一直信赖他。


将来时:
He will profess his love to her tomorrow. = 他明天将向她表白。
She will profess her loyalty to the company. = 她将公开表达她对公司的忠诚。


虚拟语气:
If he professed his love, she would be happy. = 如果他表白，她会很高兴。
I wish he professed his feelings more openly. = 我希望他能更公开地表达他的感情。


被动语态:
His innocence was professed by him repeatedly. = 他反复宣称自己是无辜的。（被动语态略显不自然，此句更常用主动语态）
The theory was professed by many scientists. = 许多科学家都宣称这一理论。（被动语态更常用）


疑问句:
Does he profess any religious beliefs? = 他信奉什么宗教信仰吗？
Does she profess to understand quantum physics?  = 她自称理解量子物理学吗？


否定句:
He did not profess to be an expert. = 他并没有自称是专家。
She didn't profess any interest in the project. = 她对这个项目没有表现出任何兴趣。


PHRASE->
profess ignorance = 宣称无知
profess one's faith = 表达信仰
profess to be = 自称是
profess love = 表达爱意
profess one's allegiance = 表达效忠
