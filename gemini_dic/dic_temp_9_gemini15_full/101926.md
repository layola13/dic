islanders:/ˈaɪˌlændərz/| n.|岛民，岛屿居民|People who live on an island or islands.  The term can refer to a specific group of people with a shared culture and history, or simply to inhabitants of islands in general.|例句：The islanders welcomed the tourists with open arms. 岛民们张开双臂欢迎游客。|例句：The islanders have a unique culture, shaped by their isolated environment.  岛民们拥有独特的文化，这种文化是由他们与世隔绝的环境塑造的。|近义词：islanders, islanders, island dwellers|反义词：mainlanders, continentals
