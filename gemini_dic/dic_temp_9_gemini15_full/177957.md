recipience:/rɪˈsɪpiəns/ | n. |接受；领受；接收能力 | The act or capacity of receiving.  It refers to the state of being receptive to something, whether it's information, a gift, or an experience.  | Example: The student showed great recipience to new ideas.  学生对新思想表现出极大的接受能力。 The hospital's high recipience of patients demonstrated its effectiveness. 医院高效率的病人接收能力证明了它的有效性。|近义词：reception, acceptance, receptivity |反义词：rejection, refusal
