misproposal:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzəl/| n. |错误的建议，不当的提议|An incorrect or inappropriate proposal.  It refers to a suggestion or plan that is flawed, unsuitable, or likely to have negative consequences.|Example: The misproposal to build the dam in the flood plain was quickly rejected.|近义词：bad suggestion, flawed proposal, ill-advised plan|反义词：sound proposal, excellent suggestion, well-considered plan|名词
