freeing:/ˈfriːɪŋ/| v.|解放，释放，使自由|The act of making something free or releasing something from confinement or restriction.  It emphasizes the process of liberating or setting free.|例句：Freeing the hostages was a top priority for the police. 解放人质是警方最优先的任务。

例句：Freeing up time in your schedule allows you to pursue hobbies.  腾出时间表中的时间可以让你追求爱好。


近义词：liberating, releasing, emancipating|反义词：imprisoning, confining, enslaving|动词
