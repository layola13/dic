pre-agricultural: /ˌpriːˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 前农业的，农业化以前的 | Relating to the period of time before the development of agriculture.  This typically refers to the Paleolithic and Mesolithic periods, characterized by hunter-gatherer societies. |


例句：

Pre-agricultural societies relied on hunting and gathering for sustenance. = 前农业社会依靠狩猎和采集来维持生计。

The archaeological evidence suggests a pre-agricultural settlement in this region. = 考古证据表明该地区存在前农业时期的定居点。


近义词：prehistoric, pre-farming, hunter-gatherer (as a descriptor, not a direct synonym)


反义词：post-agricultural, agricultural, agrarian


词性: 形容词
