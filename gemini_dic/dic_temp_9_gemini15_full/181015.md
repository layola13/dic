reimprison:/ˌriːɪmˈprɪzən/| vt.|再次监禁；重新关押|To imprison someone again after they have been released from prison.|例句：He was reimprisoned for violating his parole. 他因为违反假释而再次被监禁。

例句：After a brief period of freedom, the escaped convict was reimprisoned.  短暂的自由后，逃犯再次被监禁。


近义词：reincarcerate, reconfine|反义词：release, liberate|动词
