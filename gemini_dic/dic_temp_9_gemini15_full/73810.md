formatting:/ˈfɔːrmætɪŋ/|/ˈfɔːrmætiŋ/| n. |格式化，排版|The process of arranging data or information according to a specific structure or layout.  This includes the organization of text, images, and other elements to create a visually appealing and easily understandable presentation. For example, formatting a document might involve adjusting font size, adding headings, and creating numbered lists.|Example: The formatting of the report was impeccable.  这份报告的排版非常完美。The formatting of the website needs improvement.  这个网站的格式需要改进。|styling, arrangement, layout|disarray, chaos|名词
