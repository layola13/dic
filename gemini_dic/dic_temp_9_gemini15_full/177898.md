recharacterize:/ˌriːˈkærəktəraɪz/| vt.|重新描述；重新解释；重新刻画|To describe or interpret something in a new or different way.  It involves changing the existing understanding or portrayal of something.|例句：The company decided to recharacterize its image as more environmentally friendly. 公司决定将其形象重新定位为更环保。
例句： Historians have recently recharacterized the events of that era, shedding new light on the causes of the war. 历史学家们最近重新诠释了那个时代的事件，为战争的起因提供了新的视角。|近义词：redefine, reinterpret, reframe|反义词：maintain, uphold
