gamboling:/ˈɡæmbəlɪŋ/| v.|嬉戏，跳跃，雀跃|To playfully skip or leap about; to frolic.  It often implies a lighthearted and joyful movement.|例句：The children were gamboling in the meadow.孩子们在草地上嬉戏跳跃。The lambs were gamboling in the green pasture.  小羊们在绿色的牧场上嬉戏跳跃。|近义词：frolicking, skipping, leaping, playing|反义词：sitting still, being inactive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lambs gambol in the field. = 小羊在田野里嬉戏。
They often gambol on the beach. = 他们经常在海滩上嬉戏。

一般过去时:
The children gamboled happily in the park. = 孩子们在公园里快乐地嬉戏。
The puppies gamboled playfully around their mother. = 小狗们在妈妈周围嬉戏玩耍。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice, as it implies active, playful movement.)
