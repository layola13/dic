ecotourists:/ˈekoʊˌtʊərɪsts/ | n. |生态旅游者 | People who travel to natural areas, conserving the environment and respecting the local culture. Ecotourism focuses on sustainable travel that minimizes environmental impact and benefits local communities. | Example: The ecotourists spent a week hiking through the rainforest, learning about the local flora and fauna and supporting local businesses.  生态旅游者花了一周时间徒步穿越雨林，了解当地的动植物，并支持当地企业。|近义词：environmental tourists, nature tourists, responsible travellers|反义词：mass tourists, irresponsible travellers
