functionally:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəli/ | adv. |  功能上地；实际上地 |  In a functional manner; in effect; practically.  It describes something that works in practice, even if it doesn't meet ideal standards. | 例句：Functionally, the system works, although it's not elegant. = 功能上来说，系统有效运行，虽然不够优雅。


The software is functionally complete, but we need to improve the user interface. = 软件功能上已经完成了，但是我们需要改进用户界面。


近义词：effectively, practically, in effect, operationally | 反义词：dysfunctionally, ineffectively, theoretically
