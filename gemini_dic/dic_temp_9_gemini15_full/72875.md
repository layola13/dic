follies:/ˈfɒliz/ /ˈfɑːliz/| n.|愚蠢的行为；荒唐事；轻率的言行；(尤指)大型娱乐场所；华丽的建筑；|英文解析:Folly can refer to a lack of good sense; foolishness, or a costly and impractical building or undertaking.  It can also refer to a theatrical entertainment or a type of entertainment venue.|例句：His actions were sheer folly. 他的行为纯粹是愚蠢。

The building was a folly built by a wealthy eccentric. 这座建筑是一座由一位有钱的怪人建造的毫无用处的建筑。


近义词：silliness, foolishness, absurdity, extravagance, nonsense|反义词：wisdom, prudence, sense, practicality
