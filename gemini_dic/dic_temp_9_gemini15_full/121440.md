misintends:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndz/| vt.|误解，曲解|To understand wrongly; to interpret incorrectly.|例句：I misintended his silence as disapproval. 我误解了他的沉默为反对。 He misintended my good intentions. 他误解了我的好意。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|反义词：understand, comprehend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misintends my actions every time. 他每次都误解我的行为。
They misintend the meaning of the poem. 他们误解了这首诗的意思。


一般过去时:
She misintended his instructions and failed the test. 她误解了他的指示，考试不及格。
I misintended his message and replied inappropriately. 我误解了他的信息，回复得不恰当。


现在完成时:
I have misintended his words on several occasions. 我多次误解了他的话。
They have misintended the contract's clauses and are now facing legal issues. 他们误解了合同条款，现在面临法律问题。


过去完成时:
He realized he had misintended her earlier comments. 他意识到他之前误解了她的评论。
We discovered that we had misintended the map and were lost. 我们发现我们误解了地图，迷路了。


将来时:
I fear he will misintend my email. 我担心他会误解我的邮件。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:

They did not misintend the instructions; they simply made a mistake. 他们没有误解指示；他们只是犯了一个错误。
