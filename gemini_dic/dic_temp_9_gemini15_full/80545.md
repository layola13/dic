goodman:/ˈɡʊdmæn/| n. |好人；好家伙；（姓氏）古德曼|A goodman is a kind, honest, and trustworthy person.  A goodman can also be used as a term of address, similar to "sir" or "mister", though less formal. It can also be a surname. |例句：He was a goodman, always willing to lend a hand. 他是个好人，总是乐于助人。


He's a real goodman, that one. 他真是个好家伙。


近义词：good fellow, honest man, gentleman|反义词：villain, scoundrel, bad person|词性:名词
