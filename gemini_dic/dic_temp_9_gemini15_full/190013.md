sandbox:/ˈsændbɒks/ | n. |沙箱；安全测试环境 | A sandbox is a controlled environment, often used for testing software or experimenting with code without risking damage to the main system.  It's a place where you can safely try things out without worrying about the consequences. | Example:  The developers tested the new software in a sandbox before releasing it to the public.  开发人员在向公众发布新软件之前在沙箱中对其进行了测试。 |近义词：test environment, isolated environment, virtual environment |反义词：production environment
