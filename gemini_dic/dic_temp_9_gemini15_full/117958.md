memento:/mɪˈmɛntoʊ/ | n. |纪念品，遗物 |A memento is an object kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event.  It is often a small item that has sentimental value. |例句：She kept a seashell as a memento of her trip to the beach. 她保留了一个贝壳作为她海滩旅行的纪念品。|例句：He gave her a watch as a memento of their time together. 他送给她一块手表作为他们在一起的纪念品。|近义词：souvenir, keepsake, reminder |反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion

词源:
