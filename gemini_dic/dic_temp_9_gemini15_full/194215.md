self-lover: /ˈsɛlf ˈlʌvər/ | n. | 自恋者；自我陶醉者 | A person who excessively admires themselves and their qualities; someone with a high degree of self-regard, often to the detriment of others.  This term often carries a negative connotation, implying narcissism or egotism.  | Example: He's a self-lover, always boasting about his achievements and ignoring others' feelings. 他是个自恋狂，总是吹嘘自己的成就，而忽略别人的感受。 | 近义词：narcissist, egotist, self-admirer | 反义词：self-deprecator, humble person | 名词
