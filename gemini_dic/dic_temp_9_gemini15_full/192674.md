The plan was scuttled by a sudden change in policy. 计划由于政策的突然变化而被搁置。|近义词：dash, scuttle, hurry, frustrate, wreck |反义词：promote, advance, support

词源:
The word "scuttling" comes from the Middle English word "scut," meaning "the tail of an animal," particularly a hare or rabbit.  This likely alludes to the quick, darting movements of these animals. The nautical meaning of "scuttling" (to sink a ship) developed later.
