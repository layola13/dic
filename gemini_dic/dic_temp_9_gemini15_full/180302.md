refrigerator:/rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtər/| n. |冰箱|An appliance that uses electricity to keep food and drinks cold.|例句：We need a new refrigerator; this one is too old. 我们需要一台新冰箱；这台太旧了。|近义词：icebox, cooler|反义词：oven, heater|名词
