entree:/ˈɑːntreɪ/| n. |主菜；入门费；进入权 |A main course of a meal.  It can also refer to the right to enter a place or a fee for entry. |例句：The entree was a delicious roast chicken. 主菜是一道美味的烤鸡。 The museum charges a small entree fee. 博物馆收取少量入场费。|近义词：main course, dish, admission |反义词：appetizer, dessert
