rewarning:/ˌriˈwɔːrnɪŋ/ |adj.| 再警告；再次警告 | A rewarning is a second or repeated warning, often emphasizing the seriousness of a prior warning that was ignored or not fully heeded.  It implies a heightened sense of urgency or danger. |例句：The police issued a rewarning about the dangers of driving under the influence of alcohol.  警方再次警告酒后驾车的危险性。|近义词：second warning, renewed warning, fresh warning |反义词：no warning, reassurance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sign rewarns drivers about the sharp curve ahead. = 告示牌再次提醒司机前方有急转弯。
This rewarning emphasizes the potential risks. = 再次警告强调了潜在的风险。
