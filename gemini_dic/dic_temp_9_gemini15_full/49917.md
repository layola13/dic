denationalised:/ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪzd/| v.|使脱离国有；使私有化 |To remove something from national ownership or control; to privatize.  |The government denationalised the railway system, leading to increased fares and job losses. 政府将铁路系统私有化，导致票价上涨和失业。|privatize, de-nationalize |nationalize |动词
