prepractising:/priːˈpræktɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 预先练习；预先实践 |  The act of practicing something beforehand.  This word is a combination of "pre-" (before) and "practising" (the act of practicing). It implies preparation or rehearsal before the main event or activity. | 例句：The musicians spent hours prepractising before their concert.  音乐家们在音乐会前花了数小时预先练习。


The team engaged in prepractising their strategy before the crucial game.  球队在关键比赛前进行了策略预演。| 近义词：rehearsing, preparing, rehearsing, drilling | 反义词：improvising, winging it | 动词
