pulverizator:/ˌpʌlvəraɪˈzeɪtər/| n. |粉碎机；研磨机 |A pulverizator is a machine used to pulverize or grind materials into a fine powder.  It's often used in industrial processes for various materials like grains, minerals, or chemicals.|例句：The farmer used a pulverizator to prepare the soil before planting.  农民在播种前用粉碎机准备土壤。  The factory uses a large pulverizator to grind limestone into powder. 工厂用大型粉碎机将石灰石磨成粉末。|近义词：grinder, mill, crusher |反义词：agglomerator, consolidator
