mitzvah:/ˈmɪtsvə/ | n. |善行；功德；诫命；（犹太教的）善行；宗教仪式 | A mitzvah is a commandment or good deed in Judaism.  It can refer to a religious obligation or a charitable act.  The plural is mitzvot (mitzvos). | 例句：Performing acts of charity is a mitzvah. 行善是善行。  Attending synagogue is a mitzvah for many observant Jews. 对许多虔诚的犹太人来说，参加犹太教堂的仪式是一项善行。| 近义词：good deed, act of kindness, commandment, obligation | 反义词：sin, transgression, evil deed | 名词
