juvenile:/ˈdʒuːvənʌɪl/|/ˈdʒuːvənaɪl/| adj., n.|幼稚的；少年的；少年读物 |Young; characteristic of youth; relating to young people.  A juvenile is also a young person, especially one who is not yet legally an adult. |例句：His juvenile behavior got him into trouble. 他的幼稚行为让他惹上了麻烦。

例句：The library has a large selection of juvenile fiction. 图书馆有大量的少年小说。


近义词：childish, immature, puerile, adolescent (for adj.) youth, minor, youngster (for n.)

反义词：mature, adult, grown-up


词源：From Latin *iuvenilis, from iuvenis "young man".
