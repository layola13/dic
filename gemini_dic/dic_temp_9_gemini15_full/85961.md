headquartered:/ˌhedˈkwɔːrtərd/ | adj. |总部设在…的；以…为总部的 | Having its headquarters located in a specified place.  The adjective form of "headquarter." It describes an organization or company, indicating the location of its main administrative offices. |例句：The company is headquartered in New York. 这家公司的总部设在纽约。


例句：Google is headquartered in Mountain View, California. 谷歌总部位于加利福尼亚州山景城。


近义词：based in, located in, situated in


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑使用表示分散或没有总部的词语，例如 decentralized, distributed


词性：形容词
