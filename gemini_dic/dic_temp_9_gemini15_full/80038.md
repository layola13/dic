go-west:/ˌɡoʊˈwɛst/| phr. v.|向西走，西进|To travel or move westward; often used to refer to the westward expansion of the United States in the 19th century.|例句：Many pioneers went west in search of land and opportunity.  许多拓荒者向西寻找土地和机会。|例句：The family decided to go west and settle in California. 这家人决定向西迁移，定居在加利福尼亚州。|近义词：move westward, head west|反义词：go east


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go west every summer. = 他们每年夏天都去西部。
We go west to escape the heat. = 我们向西去躲避炎热。

一般过去时:
She went west to find a new life. = 她向西去寻找新的生活。
They went west across the plains. = 他们横穿平原向西而去。

现在完成时:
He has gone west to visit his family. = 他已经西行去看望他的家人了。
They have gone west many times. = 他们已经多次去过西部了。

过去完成时:
Before the gold rush, many people had already gone west. = 在淘金热之前，许多人已经向西去了。
She had gone west before I met her. = 我认识她之前，她已经向西去了。

将来时:
We will go west next year. = 我们明年将向西去。
They will go west to start a new business. = 他们将向西去开创一个新事业。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would go west. = 如果我有钱，我会向西去。
I wish I could go west and see the Grand Canyon. = 我希望我能向西去看看大峡谷。

被动语态:
(This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an active action.)

疑问句:
Will you go west with us? = 你会和我们一起去西部吗？
Are they going west this summer? = 他们今年夏天要去西部吗？

否定句:
They did not go west; they went south. = 他们没有去西部；他们去了南部。
We will not go west this year. = 我们今年不会去西部。


PHRASE->
go west young man = 年轻人，去西部吧！(鼓励年轻人去西部寻找机会)
to go west = 死去 (Informal)
go west of the Mississippi = 去密西西比河以西
to go west quickly = 快速地衰败，迅速地失败
go west, young man = 去西边发展吧！（鼓励年轻人去西部发展）
