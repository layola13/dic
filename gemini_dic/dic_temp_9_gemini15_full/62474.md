endosmometer: /ˌɛndoʊˈzmɑːmɪtər/ | n. |渗透压计| An instrument used to measure osmotic pressure.  It typically consists of a semipermeable membrane separating two solutions of different concentrations, allowing the measurement of the pressure required to prevent osmosis.| The endosmometer showed a significant difference in osmotic pressure between the two solutions.  渗透压计显示两种溶液之间的渗透压存在显著差异。|osmometer, tonometer|  (no direct antonym)| 名词
