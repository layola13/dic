relate:/rɪˈleɪt/ | vt. | 叙述；讲述；关联；认同 | To tell or describe something; to connect or show a relationship between things; to feel sympathy or understanding towards someone. | 例句：He related the story of his childhood. 他讲述了他童年的故事。  He related the two events. 他将这两件事联系起来。 | 近义词：tell, narrate, connect, link, associate | 反义词：disconnect, separate, dissociate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relates his experiences to his friends. 他向朋友们讲述他的经历。
She relates the symptoms to the underlying disease. 她将症状与潜在疾病联系起来。

一般过去时:
She related the incident to the police. 她向警方报告了此事。
He related how he had overcome the challenge. 他讲述了他如何克服挑战。

现在完成时:
I have related all the important facts. 我已经叙述了所有重要的事实。
She has related her feelings to her therapist. 她已经向她的治疗师倾诉了她的感受。

过去完成时:
He had related the news before I arrived.  在我到达之前他已经说了这个消息。
They had related the two concepts before the discussion. 在讨论之前他们已经将这两个概念联系起来。

将来时:
She will relate her journey to the class tomorrow. 她明天将向班级讲述她的旅程。
He will relate the findings to the broader context. 他将把这些发现与更广泛的背景联系起来。

虚拟语气:
If he related the truth, they would believe him. 如果他讲实话，他们就会相信他。
I wish I could relate to her struggles. 我希望我能理解她的困境。

被动语态:
The story was related in great detail.  这个故事被详细地讲述了。
The two ideas were related by the professor. 这两个概念由教授联系起来。

疑问句:
How does she relate to her colleagues? 她和同事们相处得如何？
Does this event relate to the current investigation? 这件事与目前的调查有关吗？

否定句:
He didn't relate the events accurately. 他没有准确地叙述这些事件。
The two concepts don't relate to each other. 这两个概念之间没有关联。


PHRASE->
relate to:  与…相关；理解；同情
relate to someone: 与某人有关系
in relation to: 关于；至于
with relation to: 关于；至于
in this relation:  在这方面
