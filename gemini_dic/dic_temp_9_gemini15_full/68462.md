familiarization:/fəˌmɪljəriˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |熟悉化；熟悉过程 | The process of becoming familiar with something or someone.  It involves gaining knowledge, understanding, and experience related to a particular subject, environment, or person. | Example:  The familiarization program for new employees lasted a week. 新员工的熟悉工作流程计划持续了一周。 |近义词：acquaintance, introduction, acclimation | 反义词：estrangement, alienation
