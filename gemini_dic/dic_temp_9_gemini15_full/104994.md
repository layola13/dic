kennelled:/ˈkenld/| v. |关进狗窝；把…关在狗窝里 |To confine (an animal) in a kennel.  The verb is the past tense and past participle of "kennel".|例句：The dog was kennelled for the night.  这只狗晚上被关在狗窝里了。
The farmer kennelled all his hunting dogs after the hunt. 猎人在打猎之后把所有的猎犬都关进了狗窝。|近义词：confined, housed, penned |反义词：released, freed

He kennels his dog every night. 他每天晚上都把狗关进狗窝。
She kennels her dogs during the day while she is at work. 她白天上班的时候把狗关在狗窝里。
