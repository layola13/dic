layerable: /ˈleɪərəbl/ | adj. | 可分层的，可层叠的 |  Able to be layered or arranged in layers.  This term often refers to materials or software that can be stacked or arranged in multiple layers without interfering with each other's functionality. | 例句：This software has layerable graphics, allowing for complex designs. = 这个软件具有可分层的图形，允许创建复杂的图案。

The new fabric is layerable, perfect for creating a warm winter outfit. = 这款新型面料可分层，非常适合制作保暖的冬季服装。


近义词：stackable, superposable | 反义词：unlayered, monolithic


词性:形容词
