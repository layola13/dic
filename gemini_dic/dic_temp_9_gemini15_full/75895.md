funded:/ˈfʌndɪd/ | adj. | 已资助的；有资金支持的 | Provided with money for a particular purpose.  | Example: The project is fully funded by the government. 这个项目完全由政府资助。 |近义词：financed, supported, bankrolled | 反义词：unfunded, underfunded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is funded by a private company. = 这个项目由一家私营公司资助。
This research is funded by a grant. = 这项研究由拨款资助。


一般过去时:
The museum was funded by donations. = 博物馆是由捐款资助的。
The startup was funded by venture capitalists. = 这家初创公司由风险投资家资助。


现在完成时:
The organization has been funded by several charities. = 该组织已得到几家慈善机构的资助。
The project has been fully funded since its inception. = 该项目自启动以来已获得完全资助。


过去完成时:
The experiment had been funded before the researchers began their work. = 在研究人员开始工作之前，实验就已经获得了资助。
The company had been funded by angel investors for several years. = 这家公司多年来一直得到天使投资人的资助。


将来时:
The initiative will be funded by the local council next year. = 该倡议明年将由地方议会资助。
The new school will be funded by government bonds. = 新学校将由政府债券资助。


虚拟语气:
If the project were funded, it would be a success. = 如果该项目获得资助，它将取得成功。
I wish the research were funded so we could continue our work. = 我希望这项研究能够获得资助，以便我们能够继续我们的工作。


被动语态:
The scholarship is funded by the university. = 这笔奖学金由大学资助。
The construction of the bridge was funded by the state government. = 桥梁的建设由州政府资助。


疑问句:
Is the project fully funded? = 这个项目资金充足吗？
How is the research funded? = 这项研究是如何获得资助的？


否定句:
The program is not fully funded. = 该项目资金并不充足。
The proposal was not funded because of budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，该提案未获得资助。


PHRASE->
fully funded: 全额资助
partially funded: 部分资助
privately funded: 私人资助
government funded: 政府资助
externally funded: 外部资助
