glossed:/ɡlɒst/| /ɡlɑːst/| adj.|注释的；带注释的；|Provided with explanatory notes or translations.  Gloss refers to a brief explanation or translation accompanying a word or text.|The ancient text was glossed in the margins. 古文在页边加了注释。|近义词：annotated, commented, explained|反义词：unannotated, uncommented
