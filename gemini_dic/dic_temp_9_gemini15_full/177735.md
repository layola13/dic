recapturing: /ˌriːˈkæptʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 重新获得；夺回；重温；再现 |  The act of regaining or retaking something that was lost or taken away. It can also refer to the act of reliving or recreating a past experience or feeling. |

例句：

The army is attempting to recapture the lost territory.  军队正试图夺回失去的领土。

She spent the evening recapturing her childhood memories. 她花了整个晚上重温童年记忆。


近义词：retaking, regaining, recovering, reviving, recreating

反义词：losing, surrendering, relinquishing


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are recapturing the market share. 他们正在夺回市场份额。
We recapture lost memories through photos. 我们通过照片重温失去的记忆。

一般过去时:
The police recaptured the escaped prisoner. 警方抓获了逃犯。
He recaptured the feeling of joy he had as a child. 他重拾了儿时那种快乐的感觉。

现在完成时:
They have recaptured the stolen jewels. 他们已经夺回了被盗的珠宝。
She has recaptured the spirit of the original novel in her adaptation.  她在改编中再现了原著的精神。


过去完成时:
The team had recaptured their winning form before the final game.  在决赛之前，球队已经恢复了获胜的状态。
By the time he finished the book, he had recaptured the author's intention. 他读完书后，已经领会了作者的意图。


将来时:
We will recapture the lost city. 我们将夺回失落的城市。
The company hopes to recapture its former glory. 公司希望重现昔日的辉煌。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could recapture the lost ground. 如果我们更努力工作，就能夺回失去的地盘。
I wish I could recapture the magic of that summer night. 我真希望能够重温那个夏夜的魔力。

被动语态:
The lost painting was recaptured by the police. 失窃的画被警方追回。
The atmosphere of the old town was beautifully recaptured in the film.  这部电影漂亮地再现了古镇的氛围。

疑问句:
Can we recapture our past success? 我们能重现过去的成功吗？
How can we recapture the public's attention? 我们如何才能重新吸引公众的注意力？

否定句:
They failed to recapture the city. 他们未能夺回这座城市。
He couldn't recapture the exact wording of the conversation. 他无法准确地回忆起谈话的内容。
