riverflow:/ˈrɪvərfloʊ/| n.|河流的流动，河水奔流|The continuous movement of water in a river.  It often refers to the visual and auditory aspects of a river's current.|例句：The riverflow was strong and swift after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水奔流湍急。|例句：The peaceful riverflow reflected the tranquil scenery. 平静的河水倒映着宁静的景色。|近义词：current, stream, flow|反义词：stagnation, stillness
