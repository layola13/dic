nonsympathetic:/ˌnɒnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk/| adj. |无同情心的，冷漠的 |Lacking sympathy or compassion; unfeeling.  It describes someone who is indifferent to or uncaring about the suffering or distress of others.|例句：Her nonsympathetic response to my plight only added to my misery.  她对我的困境漠不关心，这更增加了我的痛苦。


例句：The manager's nonsympathetic attitude towards his employees led to high turnover. 经理对员工缺乏同情心，导致员工流动率高。


近义词：unsympathetic, apathetic, callous, indifferent, heartless, unfeeling


反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, empathetic, caring, kind, tender
