goats:/ɡoʊts/ | n. | 山羊 | A goat is a ruminant mammal belonging to the genus Capra. They are known for their climbing ability and often kept for their milk, meat, and hair. Goats are found in various parts of the world and come in many breeds. | Example: The farmer milked his goats every morning.  这个农民每天早上都挤山羊奶。  The goats grazed peacefully on the hillside.  山羊平静地吃着山坡上的草。| 近义词：ibex,  chamois | 反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其他家畜例如牛，羊进行对比)| 名词
