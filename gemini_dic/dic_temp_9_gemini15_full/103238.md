jetty:/ˈdʒɛti/ | n. | 栈桥；防波堤 | A jetty is a structure that projects into the water, typically to protect a harbor or provide a landing stage for boats.  It can also be a pier or a mole. | Example: The fishermen repaired the damaged jetty after the storm.  渔民在暴风雨过后修理了损坏的栈桥。  The cruise ship docked at the long jetty.  游轮停靠在长长的栈桥上。 | 近义词：pier, wharf, dock, mole | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept might be open coastline.
