nebular:/ˈnɛbjələr/| adj. |星云的；星云状的|Of or relating to a nebula or nebulae; resembling a nebula.|The nebular hypothesis suggests that the solar system formed from a rotating cloud of gas and dust.|近义词：nebulous, cloudy|反义词：clear, definite
