overinsist:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪst/| v.| 过分坚持；强调过度 | To insist on something too much or too forcefully.  It implies an excessive or unreasonable degree of insistence. |例句：He overinsisted on his opinion, ignoring all counterarguments. 他过分坚持自己的意见，无视所有反驳。

例句：Don't overinsist on that detail; it's not crucial to the overall plan. 不要过分强调那个细节；它对整体计划并不重要。


近义词：persist, insist excessively, press hard, urge strongly,  hammer away at

反义词：yield, concede, compromise, acquiesce


词性:动词
