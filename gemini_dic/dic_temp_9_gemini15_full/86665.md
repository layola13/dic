hellenistically:/ˌhɛləˈnɪstɪkli/ | adj. | 希腊化的；受希腊文化影响的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Hellenistic period (roughly 323-31 BC), or its culture and influence.  The term often refers to the spread of Greek culture and language throughout the Mediterranean world following Alexander the Great's conquests. | The Hellenistic kingdoms saw a flourishing of art and learning.  希腊化王国见证了艺术和学习的繁荣。  The city's architecture displays strong Hellenistic influences. 这个城市的建筑展现出强烈的希腊化影响。 |  Greco-, classical |  un-Hellenistic
