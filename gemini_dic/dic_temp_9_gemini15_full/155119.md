petrosiliceous:/ˌpɛtroʊsɪˈlɪʃəs/| adj.|含硅质的，硅质岩石的|Containing or composed of silica and rock.  The term refers to rocks or materials that are rich in silica (SiO2), often in the form of quartz or other silicate minerals. These materials are typically hard and resistant to weathering.|The petrosiliceous rock formation proved difficult to excavate.  含硅质的岩石构造被证明很难挖掘。|siliceous, siliceous rock|non-petrosiliceous
