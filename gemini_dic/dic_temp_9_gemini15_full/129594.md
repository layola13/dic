non-communicating: /ˌnɒn kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不交流的，不沟通的 | Not communicating or interacting; failing to communicate.  This adjective describes a situation, person, or system that is unable or unwilling to exchange information or ideas.  It implies a lack of interaction or feedback. |

例句：

The two groups remained non-communicating throughout the negotiations.  = 在整个谈判过程中，这两个群体保持着不沟通的状态。

The patient became non-communicating after the accident. = 事故后，病人停止了交流。


近义词：uncommunicative, incommunicative, unresponsive, silent, isolated

反义词：communicative, interactive, responsive


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  It's derived from Old French *non* and Latin *nōn*.

* **communicating**:  The present participle of the verb "to communicate", meaning to convey information or feelings.  This comes from Latin *communicare*, meaning "to share", "to make common", from *communis* ("common").


USAGE->  (Since "non-communicating" is an adjective, it doesn't have verb conjugations like "to communicate".)  It's used to describe a state or condition.

一般现在时: The patient is non-communicating. = 病人没有交流能力。
The two teams remain non-communicating. = 两队之间没有沟通。

一般过去时: The suspect was non-communicating during interrogation. = 嫌犯在审讯期间没有交流。
The child remained non-communicating for several hours. = 孩子几个小时都没有交流。

现在完成时:  The patient has been non-communicating since the surgery. = 手术后，病人一直没有交流。
He has remained stubbornly non-communicating. = 他一直固执地保持沉默不语。


过去完成时: The witnesses had been non-communicating before the trial. = 在审判之前，证人们一直没有交流。
She had become non-communicating after the trauma. = 经历创伤后，她变得沉默寡言。


将来时: The doctor fears he will be non-communicating for several weeks.  = 医生担心他会持续几周无法交流。
They expect the negotiations to remain non-communicating. = 他们预计谈判将继续处于无沟通状态。


被动语态:  (Non-communicating itself is not typically used in the passive voice, as it describes a state.)

疑问句: Is the patient non-communicating? = 病人是不是无法交流？
Are the parties involved non-communicating? = 相关各方是否没有沟通？


否定句: The suspect is not non-communicating; he's just being uncooperative. = 嫌犯并非不交流，他只是不合作。
The children are not non-communicating; they're just shy. = 孩子们并非不交流，他们只是害羞。


PHRASE->

There aren't common phrases directly *with* "non-communicating,"  as it's primarily used as a descriptive adjective.  However, it often appears in phrases like:

* non-communicating patient =  无法交流的病人
* non-communicating individual =  不与人交流的个人
* remain non-communicating = 保持不交流状态
* become non-communicating = 变得不交流
* a non-communicating system = 一个不沟通的系统
