confabbing:/kənˈfæbɪŋ/| v.|闲聊；密谈；商讨|Engaging in informal conversation; chatting; usually implies a casual or secret discussion.|例句：They were confabbing in the corner, their voices hushed.  他们在角落里低声私语。|例句：The politicians were confabbing late into the night, trying to reach a compromise. 政治家们深夜还在商谈，试图达成妥协。|近义词：chatting, conversing, whispering, plotting|反义词：arguing, fighting, debating
