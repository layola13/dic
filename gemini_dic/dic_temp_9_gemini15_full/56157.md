dodder:/ˈdɒdə(r)/| v. n.| 蹒跚；衰弱；菟丝子 | To move or behave in a feeble or unsteady way;  a weak and feeble plant, specifically a parasitic plant that twines around other plants. |例句：The old woman doddered along the path. 老妇人沿着小路蹒跚而行。The dodder was strangling the rose bush. 菟丝子正在扼杀玫瑰花丛。|近义词：stumble, wobble, totter, wither |反义词：flourish, thrive, robust


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dodders around the house. = 他在房子里蹒跚而行。
The plant dodders across the fence. = 植物缠绕着篱笆生长。


一般过去时:
She doddered down the stairs. = 她蹒跚地下了楼梯。
The vine doddered around the tree trunk. = 藤蔓缠绕着树干生长。


现在完成时:
He has doddered his way through life. = 他以蹒跚的方式度过了一生。
The parasite has doddered across the entire field. = 寄生植物已经蔓延到整个田野。


过去完成时:
She had doddered for years before she finally fell. = 她蹒跚了好几年才最终摔倒。
The plant had doddered extensively before it was discovered. = 植物在被发现之前已经广泛蔓延。


将来时:
He will dodder more as he gets older. = 他年纪越大，就会越蹒跚。
The vine will likely dodder onto the neighboring plant. = 藤蔓可能会缠绕到附近的植物上。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't dodder so much, he wouldn't fall. = 如果他不那么蹒跚，他就不会摔倒。
If the plant hadn't doddered so wildly, it wouldn't have covered the entire garden. = 如果植物没有如此疯狂地蔓延，它就不会覆盖整个花园。


被动语态:
(Not typically used in passive voice for the verb meaning "to walk unsteadily."  The noun usage is below.)
The rose bush was being strangled by the dodder. = 玫瑰花丛正被菟丝子缠绕窒息。


疑问句:
Does he dodder when he walks? = 他走路时是否蹒跚？
Is that plant a dodder? = 那株植物是菟丝子吗？


否定句:
He doesn't dodder anymore. = 他不再蹒跚了。
That's not a dodder, it's a different kind of vine. = 那不是菟丝子，是另一种藤蔓。
