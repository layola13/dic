overindulges:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒɪz/| v.|过度放纵，沉溺|To allow oneself to have more than is needed or wanted of something enjoyable, often to the point of harming oneself. |例句：He overindulges in sweets and now has a toothache. 他吃糖太多，现在牙疼了。|例句：She overindulges in alcohol at parties. 她在派对上酗酒。|近义词：spoil, pamper, indulge excessively|反义词：restrain, moderate, discipline


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overindulges in fast food. = 他沉溺于快餐。
She overindulges in shopping. = 她沉迷于购物。

一般过去时:
I overindulged in cake at the party. = 我在派对上吃太多蛋糕了。
They overindulged in wine last night. = 他们昨晚喝了太多酒。

现在完成时:
He has overindulged in rich foods lately. = 他最近吃太多油腻的食物了。
She has overindulged in sunbathing and got sunburned. = 她过度晒太阳，结果晒伤了。

过去完成时:
I had overindulged in coffee, so I couldn't sleep. = 我喝了太多咖啡，所以睡不着。
They had overindulged in sweets as children, so they have dental problems now. = 他们小时候吃太多糖，所以现在有牙齿问题。

将来时:
I will overindulge if I go to the buffet. = 如果我去自助餐，我会吃太多。
She thinks she will overindulge at the wedding reception. = 她认为她会在婚礼招待会上吃喝太多。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overindulged last night, I wouldn't feel so sick today. = 如果我昨晚没有吃喝太多，今天就不会感觉这么难受了。
I wish I hadn't overindulged in the chocolate. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多巧克力。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies a voluntary action.)  While grammatically possible, it sounds unnatural.  A better way to express a passive idea would be to focus on the object being overindulged in.  For example:

The children were allowed to overindulge in sweets. = 孩子们被允许吃太多糖果。(Focuses on the sweets)
His sweet tooth was overindulged. = 他的嗜甜被过度满足了。(Focuses on the sweet tooth)


疑问句:
Does he overindulge in alcohol? = 他酗酒吗？
Do you ever overindulge in desserts? = 你会沉溺于甜点吗？

否定句:
I don't overindulge in unhealthy foods. = 我不吃太多不健康的食物。
She doesn't overindulge; she's very disciplined. = 她不会过度放纵；她很有自律性。
