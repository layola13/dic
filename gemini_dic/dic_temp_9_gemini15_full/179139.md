redialing: /ˌriːˈdaɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 重新拨号 | The act of dialing a telephone number again, usually after a failed attempt.  | Example:  He kept redialing the number, hoping to finally reach her. 他不停地重新拨打号码，希望能最终联系到她。 |近义词：recalling, trying again | 反义词：disconnecting, hanging up | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redials the number every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就重新拨打号码。
They are redialing the important client. = 他们正在重新拨打重要客户的电话。

一般过去时:
I redialed the number after the line went dead. = 电话断了之后，我又重新拨打了号码。
She redialed the wrong number several times. = 她几次重新拨打了错误的号码。

现在完成时:
He has redialed that number countless times. = 他已经无数次重新拨打那个号码了。
I have redialed the number, but still can't reach him. = 我已经重新拨打了号码，但仍然联系不上他。

过去完成时:
She had redialed the number before realizing it was incorrect. = 她意识到号码错误之前已经重新拨打过号码了。
They had redialed the number several times, but there was still no answer. = 他们已经重新拨打了好几次号码，但仍然没有人接听。

将来时:

They will redial the number tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上会重新拨打这个号码。

虚拟语气:
