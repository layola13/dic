crazy-quilts:/ˈkreɪzi kwɪlts/| n.|疯人被; 百衲被|A crazy quilt is a type of quilt made from irregularly shaped pieces of fabric sewn together in a patchwork design.  The pieces are often of different colors, patterns, and textures, and may include embellishments such as embroidery, beads, or sequins.|例句：My grandmother made a beautiful crazy quilt from scraps of old clothing.  我的祖母用旧衣服碎片做了一床漂亮的百衲被。|近义词：patchwork quilt, crazy patchwork|反义词：plain quilt
