nondangerous:/ˌnɒnˈdeɪndʒərəs/| adj. |无危险的，安全的|Not dangerous; safe.  The activity is designed to be nondangerous and fun for children of all ages.|近义词：safe, harmless, innocuous|反义词：dangerous, perilous, hazardous

例句：

The nondangerous chemicals are used in the experiment. =  实验中使用了无害的化学物质。

The doctor assured us that the procedure was nondangerous. = 医生向我们保证这个手术是安全的。


词源:

non- + dangerous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "dangerous" means presenting a risk of harm.


用法说明:


nondangerous主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某事物不危险，安全。 它可以用于描述各种事物，例如活动、物质、环境等等。
