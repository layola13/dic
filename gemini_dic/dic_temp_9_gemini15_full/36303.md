cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ | n. |内聚性；凝聚力；结合；团结 |The state of sticking together; the act or state of cohering.  It refers to the act or state of uniting or sticking together.  In various contexts, it can describe the unity of a group, the structural integrity of a material, or the logical connection in a piece of writing. | Example: The team's cohesion was evident in their synchronized performance.  团队的凝聚力在他们同步的表演中显而易见。

The cohesion of the clay allowed the sculptor to create intricate details. 粘土的结合力使雕塑家能够创造出复杂的细节。|近义词：unity, solidarity, coherence, adhesion |反义词：disunity, fragmentation, discord, separation |
