hornsnake: /ˈhɔːrnˌsneɪk/ | n. |角蛇 |A type of non-venomous snake characterized by its horn-like projections on its head.  They are often slender and found in various habitats. | Example: The hornsnake slithered through the tall grass. 角蛇在高高的草丛中蜿蜒而行。|近义词： none specifically, but could be described as a type of snake,  colubrid (if specifying family). |反义词： none
