exorcisory:/ˈɛksɔːrsɪsɔːri/| adj.|驱魔的，逐魔的 | Relating to or used in exorcism.  The exorcisory rite was performed by the priest.  | 近义词：expelling, banishing | 反义词：inviting, welcoming


例句：

The exorcisory ritual involved chanting and the burning of incense.  驱魔仪式包括吟唱和焚香。

He claimed to possess exorcisory powers, able to banish evil spirits. 他声称拥有驱魔能力，能够驱逐邪灵。


词源:

From exorcise + -ory.  "Exorcise" itself comes from Late Latin *exorcismus, from Greek  exorízein "to conjure," from ex- "out" + horkos "oath."  The suffix "-ory" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "serving for."


用法说明:


由于exorcisory是形容词，它主要用于修饰名词，例如：

* **exorcisory rites/rituals:** 驱魔仪式
* **exorcisory prayers:** 驱魔祈祷
* **exorcisory power/abilities:** 驱魔能力
* **exorcisory symbols:** 驱魔符号
* **an exorcisory incantation:** 一种驱魔咒语


需要注意的是，"exorcisory" 通常用于描述与驱魔相关的宗教或超自然活动，而非科学或世俗的驱魔方法。
