permacultural:/ˌpɜːməˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.| 永续农业的；永久性农业的 | Relating to or characteristic of permaculture, a system of agricultural and social design principles centered on mimicking the patterns and relationships found in natural ecosystems.  | Example:  The permacultural design incorporated natural water retention systems.  这个永久农业的设计结合了自然蓄水系统。  |近义词:sustainable, ecological, holistic |反义词: unsustainable, destructive
