disload:  /dɪsˈloʊd/ | v. |卸货，卸载 |To unload (goods, cargo, etc.) from a vehicle or vessel.  |例句：The workers disloaded the truck quickly and efficiently. 工人们快速有效地卸下了卡车上的货物。  |例句：The ship disloaded its cargo at the port. 船只在港口卸下了货物。 |近义词：unload, discharge, offload |反义词：load, embark


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disload the containers every day. = 他们每天都卸货柜。
The port disloads hundreds of ships annually. = 这个港口每年卸货数百艘船。

一般过去时:
The crew disloaded the cargo in the afternoon. = 船员在下午卸了货。
We disloaded the furniture from the van. = 我们把家具从货车上卸了下来。

现在完成时:
They have disloaded all the boxes. = 他们已经卸完了所有的箱子。
The team has disloaded the equipment successfully. = 团队已经成功卸下了设备。

过去完成时:
The workers had disloaded the goods before the rain started. = 下雨前工人们已经卸完了货物。
Before the inspection, they had disloaded all the hazardous materials. = 检查前，他们已经卸下了所有危险材料。

将来时:
They will disload the shipment tomorrow. = 他们明天将卸货。
The crane will disload the heavy machinery. = 起重机将卸载重型机械。

虚拟语气:
If they had disloaded the cargo sooner, they would have finished earlier. = 如果他们早点卸货，他们就能早点完成。
I wish they had disloaded the containers more carefully. = 我希望他们能更仔细地卸货柜。

被动语态:
The goods were disloaded at the warehouse. = 货物在仓库卸下。
The containers are being disloaded now. = 集装箱现在正在卸货。

疑问句:
When will the ship disload its cargo? = 船什么时候卸货？
Have they disloaded the truck yet? = 他们已经卸货了吗？

否定句:
They didn't disload the truck on time. = 他们没有按时卸货。
The crane could not disload the heavy object. = 起重机无法卸下重物。
