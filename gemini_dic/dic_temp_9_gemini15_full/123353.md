monologist:/ˈmɒnəlɒdʒɪst/ /ˈmɑːnəlɒdʒɪst/| n. |独白表演者；独角戏演员| A monologist is a performer who delivers a monologue, a long speech by one person.  They often perform in stand-up comedy, theater, or other performance art forms.  |Example: The monologist captivated the audience with his witty and insightful observations. 独白表演者以其诙谐而富有洞察力的见解吸引了观众。The famous monologist received a standing ovation after his performance. 著名的独白表演者演出结束后获得了全场起立鼓掌。|近义词：soloist, performer, speaker |反义词：interlocutor, conversationalist
