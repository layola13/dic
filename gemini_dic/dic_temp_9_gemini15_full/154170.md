periodic:/ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪk/ /ˌpɪəriˈɑːdɪk/| adj.| 周期的；定期的；间歇的 | occurring or appearing at regular intervals | The periodic table organizes elements by their atomic number.  元素周期表按元素的原子序数排列。|近义词：regular, recurring, cyclical |反义词：irregular, sporadic, continuous |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant blooms periodically. = 这种植物定期开花。
He periodically checks his email. = 他定期检查他的电子邮件。


一般过去时:
The lights flickered periodically throughout the night. = 整个晚上灯都间歇性地闪烁。
She periodically visited her grandmother. = 她定期去看望她的祖母。


现在完成时:
The machine has periodically malfunctioned. = 这台机器时不时地出现故障。
He has periodically updated the software. = 他定期更新了软件。


过去完成时:
They had periodically warned us about the risks. = 他们曾多次警告我们这些风险。
The system had periodically failed before the upgrade. = 系统升级前曾多次出现故障。


将来时:
The comet will periodically return to our solar system. = 这颗彗星将定期返回我们的太阳系。
She will periodically review the project's progress. = 她将定期审查项目的进展。


虚拟语气:
If the payments were periodic, it would be easier to budget. = 如果付款是定期的，预算就会更容易。
I wish the train schedule were more periodic. = 我希望火车时刻表更规律一些。


被动语态:
The reports are periodically reviewed by the supervisor. = 这些报告由主管定期审查。
The system is periodically backed up to prevent data loss. = 系统定期备份以防止数据丢失。


疑问句:
Is the payment periodic or one-time? = 付款是定期的还是一次性的？
Are the inspections performed periodically? = 检查是定期进行的吗？


否定句:
The updates are not periodic; they are irregular. = 更新不是定期的；它们是不规律的。
His visits were not periodic; he came when he felt like it. = 他的来访不是定期的；他高兴的时候才来。
