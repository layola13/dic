mezzo-soprano:/ˈmɛtsoʊˌsoʊprɑːnoʊ/| n. |女中音|A mezzo-soprano is a female singer with a vocal range between a soprano and an alto.  Their voice is typically richer and warmer than a soprano's, and possesses a greater power and depth than an alto's.  Mezzo-sopranos often sing roles that require both lyrical beauty and dramatic intensity.|The opera featured a magnificent mezzo-soprano whose voice filled the hall.|soprano, alto|bass, baritone|名词
