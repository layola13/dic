polecat:/ˈpoʊlkæt/| n.| 鼬鼠；臭鼬 | A polecat is a small, carnivorous mammal belonging to the weasel family, known for its musky odor.  They are often found in Europe and Asia. | Example: The farmer saw a polecat raiding his henhouse.  那个农民看到一只鼬鼠在袭击他的鸡舍。 |近义词：ferret, weasel |反义词：无
