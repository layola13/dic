horseshoe:/ˈhɔːrsʃuː/ | n. | 马蹄铁 | A horseshoe is a U-shaped piece of metal nailed to the bottom of a horse's hoof to protect it.  It's also considered a lucky charm. | Example: The blacksmith carefully shaped the horseshoe before nailing it onto the horse's hoof.  铁匠在将马蹄铁钉在马蹄上之前仔细地把它塑形。 | 近义词:horseshoes (复数)| 反义词: None.
