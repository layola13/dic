preomitting:/ˌpriːoʊˈmɪtɪŋ/| v. |预先省略，提前忽略|The act of omitting something beforehand; neglecting or leaving something out in advance.  This is typically done to simplify or streamline a process, or to avoid unnecessary detail.|例句：Preomitting certain steps in the instructions made the assembly process much faster. 预先省略说明书中的某些步骤使组装过程快得多。


例句：The software preomitted the error checking phase, resulting in unexpected crashes.  软件预先省略了错误检查阶段，导致意外崩溃。


近义词：preclude, omit, exclude, disregard


反义词：include, retain, incorporate


词性:动词
