overwatered:/ˌoʊvərˈwɔːtərd/| adj.|浇水过多的；水浸的|Given too much water; excessively watered.  The plants are wilting because they've been overwatered.|近义词：waterlogged, sodden, drenched|反义词：underwatered, parched


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plants are overwatered. = 植物浇水过多。
The gardener overwaters the flowers every day. = 园丁每天都给花浇水过多。

一般过去时:
The farmer overwatered his crops last week. = 农民上周给庄稼浇水过多。
I overwatered my seedlings, and they died. = 我给幼苗浇水过多，结果它们死了。

现在完成时:
He has overwatered his garden several times this month. = 这个月他已经几次给花园浇水过多。
The plants have been overwatered and are now rotting. = 植物浇水过多，现在正在腐烂。

过去完成时:
The flowers had been overwatered before the competition. = 比赛前花已经浇水过多。
Because the plants had been overwatered, they developed root rot. = 因为植物浇水过多，所以产生了根腐病。

将来时:
I will overwater the plants if I'm not careful. = 如果我不小心，我会给植物浇水过多。
We will not overwater the seedlings next time. = 下次我们不会给幼苗浇水过多。


被动语态:
The lawn was overwatered by the automatic sprinkler system. = 草坪被自动洒水系统浇水过多。
The plants were overwatered, resulting in their wilting. = 植物浇水过多，导致它们枯萎。

疑问句:
Were the plants overwatered? = 植物浇水过多吗？
Have you ever overwatered your houseplants? = 你曾经给你的室内植物浇水过多吗？

否定句:
I did not overwater my plants this morning. = 我今天早上没有给我的植物浇水过多。
The farmer didn't overwater his crops this season. = 农民本季没有给他的庄稼浇水过多。


PHRASE->
overwatered plants = 浇水过多的植物
overwatered soil = 浸水的土壤
overwatered lawn = 浇水过多的草坪
overwatered seedlings = 浇水过多的幼苗
overwatered flowers = 浇水过多的花朵

词源:
over- (prefix indicating excess) + watered (past participle of water)
