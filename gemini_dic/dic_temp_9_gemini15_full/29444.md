catalogs:/ˈkætəlɒɡz/| n. |目录，商品目录 |A catalog is a complete list of items, typically with descriptions and prices, often used for selling goods or providing information.  It can be a printed book or an online database.|例句：The store's catalog featured a wide selection of furniture. 这家店的商品目录展示了各种各样的家具。|例句：We ordered several items from the online catalog. 我们从网上目录订购了几件商品。|近义词：list, inventory, index |反义词：无  |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store catalogs its products online. = 这家商店在线编制其产品目录。
This company catalogs its inventory annually. = 这家公司每年都会编制库存目录。


一般过去时:
