lay-out:/ˈleɪaʊt/ | n. | 布局；设计；安排 | The arrangement of elements in a space or on a page.  It refers to the overall plan or organization of something, especially a printed page, a website, or a building. | Example: The magazine's layout was modern and appealing.  这本杂志的版式现代而吸引人。  The architect presented several different layouts for the new house. 建筑师展示了几种不同的新房布局。 | 近义词：arrangement, design, plan, scheme | 反义词：disorder, chaos
