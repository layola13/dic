shrug:/ʃrʌɡ/| vt. vi. n.|耸肩，不屑一顾|To raise and lower your shoulders quickly, usually to show that you do not know or care about something.  |例句：He shrugged his shoulders and walked away. 他耸了耸肩，走开了。|例句：I shrugged off his criticism. 我对他的批评不屑一顾。|近义词：shrugging, dismissing|反义词:caring, attending


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shrugs his shoulders when he doesn't understand. = 他不明白的时候会耸耸肩。
She shrugs off minor inconveniences. = 她对小的不便不屑一顾。

一般过去时:
He shrugged his shoulders in response to the question. = 他对这个问题耸了耸肩作为回应。
She shrugged off the insult. = 她对侮辱不屑一顾。

现在完成时:
He has shrugged off numerous setbacks in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中克服了许多挫折。
She has shrugged off the pressure. = 她已经摆脱了压力。

过去完成时:
He had shrugged off the warning signs. = 他之前已经对警告信号不屑一顾。
She had shrugged off many similar offers before accepting this one. = 在接受这个之前，她已经拒绝了许多类似的提议。

将来时:
He will shrug off the criticism. = 他会对批评不屑一顾。
She will shrug her shoulders if she doesn't know the answer. = 如果她不知道答案，她会耸耸肩。

虚拟语气:
If he knew the answer, he wouldn't shrug his shoulders. = 如果他知道答案，他就不会耸肩了。
I wish I could shrug off my worries. = 我希望我能摆脱我的担忧。

被动语态:
His concerns were shrugged off by his boss. = 他的担忧被他的老板忽视了。
The problem was shrugged off as insignificant. = 这个问题被轻描淡写地认为微不足道。

疑问句:
Did he shrug his shoulders when you asked him? = 当你问他的时候，他耸了耸肩吗？
Why did she shrug off the opportunity? = 她为什么放弃这个机会？

否定句:
He didn't shrug off the responsibility. = 他没有推卸责任。
She didn't shrug her shoulders; she answered directly. = 她没有耸肩；她直接回答了。


PHRASE->
shrug off = 忽略；摆脱；对…不屑一顾
shrug something aside = 摒弃；漠视
give a shrug = 耸肩表示
a shrug of the shoulders = 耸肩的动作
shrug one's shoulders = 耸肩
