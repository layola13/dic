eastbound:/ˈiːstbɑʊnd/| adj. |向东的，东行的|Going or directed toward the east.  The adjective describes something moving or oriented in an easterly direction.|例句：The eastbound train was delayed by a landslide.  东行的火车因山体滑坡而延误。|例句：We took the eastbound highway to reach the coast. 我们走东部高速公路到达海岸。|近义词：easterly, eastward |反义词：westbound

USAGE->

The plane is eastbound. = 这架飞机是东行的。
The traffic is eastbound on this road. = 这条路上的交通是东向的。
