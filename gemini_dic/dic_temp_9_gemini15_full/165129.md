preincreasing: /ˌpriːɪnˈkriːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先增加的；先增的 |  Describing something that increases before another event or process.  It often implies a preparatory or anticipatory increase.  The word is less common and often found in technical contexts. |


例句：

The preincreasing of the water level in the reservoir prevented flooding downstream.  = 水库水位的预先增加防止了下游的洪水。

The preincreasing of troops in the border region signaled an impending conflict. = 边境地区军队预先增加预示着即将爆发的冲突。


近义词：advance, preliminary, prior


反义词：decreasing, post-increasing


词性：形容词
