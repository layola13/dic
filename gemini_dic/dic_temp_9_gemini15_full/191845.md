sclerogen:/ˈsklɛrədʒən/| n. |硬蛋白，巩膜蛋白|A protein substance forming the supporting tissues of certain invertebrates, notably the horny parts of sponges and corals.  It is a tough, fibrous protein similar to collagen but differing in certain chemical properties.|Example: The sclerogen in the coral skeleton provides structural support and protection.  珊瑚骨骼中的硬蛋白提供结构支撑和保护。|近义词：spongin (for sponge sclerogen)|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying softness or flexibility could be considered opposites.|名词
