precovering:/ˌpriːˈkʌvərɪŋ/ | v. | 正在恢复中，开始恢复 |  Describes a process or state of recovering,  implying the recovery is still in progress and not yet complete.  It suggests an early stage of recovery. | 例句：The economy is precovering after the recent recession. = 最近经济衰退后，经济正在复苏。 The patient is precovering from surgery, showing some signs of improvement. = 病人正在术后恢复中，并显示出一些好转的迹象。 | 近义词：recovering, improving, convalescing | 反义词：worsening, deteriorating | 动词
