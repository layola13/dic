euphorbin:/juːˈfɔːrbɪn/ | n. |大戟醇，大戟毒素 | A toxic, milky substance found in plants of the genus Euphorbia, containing diterpene esters.  It is irritant to the skin and mucous membranes. | Example:  The sap of the euphorbia plant contains euphorbin, a potent irritant. 大戟属植物的汁液含有大戟醇，一种强效刺激物。|近义词: euphorbium (less common) |反义词: (no direct antonym, but substances with soothing properties could be considered opposite in effect)|名词
