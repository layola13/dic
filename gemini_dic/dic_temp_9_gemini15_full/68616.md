fantasticism:/fænˈtæstɪsɪzəm/| n. |狂想；幻想癖；奇想；怪诞；幻想性|The belief in or pursuit of fantastic things; extravagant or unrealistic ideas or behavior.  It often implies an excessive or unhealthy preoccupation with fantasy, sometimes bordering on delusion.|例句：His fantasticism led him to believe he could fly. 他的幻想使他相信自己能够飞行。

例句：The artist's work is characterized by a whimsical fantasticism.这位艺术家的作品以奇特的幻想为特色。


近义词：fantasy, whimsy, daydreaming, flights of fancy, surrealism

反义词：realism, practicality, pragmatism


词性：名词
