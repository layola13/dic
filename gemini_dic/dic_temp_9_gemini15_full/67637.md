extricate:/ˈɛkstrɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 解救；使解脱；摆脱 | To free (someone or something) from a constraint or difficulty.  | 例句：The rescuers worked tirelessly to extricate the survivors from the wreckage.  救援人员不懈努力地将幸存者从残骸中解救出来。

例句：He struggled to extricate himself from the complicated legal dispute. 他努力摆脱复杂的法律纠纷。


近义词：free, liberate, release, disentangle, disengage | 反义词：entangle, involve, trap, ensnare | 词性:动词
