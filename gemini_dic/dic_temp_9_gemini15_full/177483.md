rebids:/ˌriːˈbɪdz/| v.|再投标，重新报价|To submit a bid again, often with a revised or improved offer.|例句：The contractor rebid the project after receiving feedback on their initial proposal.  承包商在收到对其初步提案的反馈后重新投标了该项目。
例句：After the initial auction failed, the seller decided to rebid the item at a lower price. 首次拍卖失败后，卖家决定以较低的价格重新拍卖该商品。|近义词：re-offer, re-tender|反义词：withdraw a bid|动词
