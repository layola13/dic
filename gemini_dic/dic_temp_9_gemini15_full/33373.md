chunk:/tʃʌŋk/ | n. | 块；部分；大量 | A chunk of something is a fairly large piece of it.  |Example: He ate a large chunk of cheese. 他吃了一大块奶酪。|近义词：piece, portion, lump |反义词：fragment, bit, speck


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A large chunk of the budget is allocated to research. = 大部分预算都拨给了研究。
This chunk of code is responsible for user authentication. = 这段代码负责用户身份验证。

一般过去时:
A significant chunk of the forest was destroyed by the fire. = 大部分森林被大火烧毁了。
He carved a chunk of wood into a bird. = 他把一块木头雕刻成一只鸟。

现在完成时:
A large chunk of the work has been completed. = 大部分工作已经完成了。
We have already eaten a chunk of the cake. = 我们已经吃掉了一大块蛋糕。

过去完成时:
A sizable chunk of the funds had already been spent before the project even started. = 项目开始前，相当一部分资金已经被花掉了。
They had already finished a chunk of the assignment by the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了大部分作业。

将来时:
A large chunk of the city will be renovated next year. = 明年将会有很大一部分城市被翻新。
We will cut a chunk of the meat for dinner. = 我们将切下一块肉做晚餐。

虚拟语气:
If we had a larger chunk of time, we could finish the project. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们就能完成这个项目。
I wish I had a bigger chunk of land to build a house. = 我希望我有一块更大的地来建房子。

被动语态:
A significant chunk of the profits was distributed to shareholders. = 一大部分利润被分发给了股东。
A chunk of the road was damaged by the recent storm. = 一段路在最近的风暴中受损。

疑问句:
What chunk of the report are you working on? = 你正在处理报告的哪一部分？
Which chunk of the data is most important? = 哪一部分数据最重要？

否定句:
That chunk of the argument doesn't make sense. = 那部分论点说不通。
He didn't understand that chunk of the lecture. = 他不明白讲座的那一部分。
