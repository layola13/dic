shrieked:/ʃriːkt/| v.|尖声叫喊；发出刺耳的声音 | to utter a loud, sharp, piercing cry  |例句：The woman shrieked in terror when she saw the snake.  女人看到蛇后吓得尖叫起来。

The children shrieked with laughter. 孩子们高兴地尖叫起来。


近义词：scream, yell, screech, howl | 反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She shrieks when she's scared. = 她害怕的时候会尖叫。
The brakes shriek as the car stops suddenly. = 车子突然刹车时，刹车发出尖锐的声音。


一般过去时:
He shrieked in pain. = 他疼得尖叫。
The audience shrieked with delight. = 观众们高兴地尖叫。


现在完成时:
She has shrieked so loudly that everyone could hear. = 她尖叫的声音很大，每个人都能听到。
The baby has shrieked all night. = 婴儿整夜都在尖叫。


过去完成时:
She had shrieked before anyone could react. = 在任何人能反应过来之前，她就尖叫了。
The dog had shrieked before running away. = 狗在逃跑前尖叫了一声。


将来时:
He will shriek if he sees a spider. = 如果他看到蜘蛛，他会尖叫。
The crowd will shriek with excitement. = 人群将兴奋地尖叫。


虚拟语气:
If she saw a mouse, she would shriek. = 如果她看到老鼠，她会尖叫。
I wish I hadn't shrieked so loudly. = 我真希望我刚才没有尖叫那么大声。


被动语态:
A shriek was heard from the next room. = 从隔壁房间传来一声尖叫。
No shriek was heard from the crowd. = 人群中没有听到尖叫声。


疑问句:
Did she shriek when she saw the accident? = 她看到事故时尖叫了吗？
Why did he shriek at the top of his lungs? = 他为什么扯着嗓子尖叫？


否定句:
She didn't shriek, she just gasped. = 她没有尖叫，只是倒吸了一口气。
He didn't shriek at all. = 他根本没尖叫。


PHRASE->

shriek in pain = 痛苦地尖叫
let out a shriek = 发出一阵尖叫
