emaceration:/ɪˌmæsəˈreɪʃən/| n. |衰弱；消瘦；浸渍；软化|The process of making something soft or weak by soaking it in liquid, or the state of being softened or weakened in this way.  It can also refer to the emaciated condition of a person or animal. |例句：The prolonged drought led to the emaceration of the crops.  长期干旱导致庄稼枯萎。|例句：His emaceration was a clear sign of his illness. 他的消瘦是疾病的明显迹象。|近义词：debilitation, weakening, atrophy, maceration|反义词：strengthening, invigorating, nourishing
