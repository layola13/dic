hedgerows:/ˈhɛdʒroʊz/| n. |树篱；灌木丛|Lines of shrubs or trees planted close together, especially as a boundary or fence. Hedgerows are important habitats for wildlife and can be found in many rural areas.|Example: The old farm was surrounded by tall, dense hedgerows.|近义词：fences, hedges, windbreaks|反义词：open fields
