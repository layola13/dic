kilos:/ˈkɪloʊz/| n.|公斤，千克|The plural form of "kilo," a unit of mass equal to 1000 grams.|例句：I bought five kilos of potatoes. 我买了五公斤土豆。|近义词：kilograms|反义词:grams


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He weighs 70 kilos. = 他重70公斤。
The package contains 2 kilos of rice. = 这个包裹包含2公斤大米。


一般过去时:
She carried 10 kilos of books. = 她搬了10公斤的书。
The truck transported 500 kilos of goods. = 卡车运送了500公斤货物。


现在完成时:
I have lifted more than 100 kilos today. = 我今天已经举起了超过100公斤的重量。
They have already consumed 5 kilos of apples. = 他们已经吃了5公斤苹果。


过去完成时:
She had already lost 5 kilos before the wedding. = 婚礼前她已经减掉了5公斤。
We had carried 20 kilos of luggage before we got tired. = 我们在感到疲倦之前已经搬运了20公斤的行李。


将来时:
I will buy 3 kilos of oranges tomorrow. = 我明天将买3公斤橙子。
They will need 1000 kilos of cement for the construction. = 他们将需要1000公斤水泥用于建筑。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I could lift 100 kilos. = 如果我更强壮，我就能举起100公斤。
I wish I had only bought 2 kilos of flour. = 我真希望我只买了2公斤面粉。


被动语态:
Two kilos of sugar were used in the cake. = 蛋糕用了两公斤糖。
Five kilos of apples were delivered to the store. = 五公斤苹果被送到了商店。


疑问句:
How many kilos of flour do we need? = 我们需要多少公斤面粉？
How many kilos does the package weigh? = 这个包裹重多少公斤？


否定句:
The bag didn't weigh more than 2 kilos. = 这个包的重量不超过2公斤。
He hasn't lost any kilos yet. = 他还没有减掉任何公斤。
