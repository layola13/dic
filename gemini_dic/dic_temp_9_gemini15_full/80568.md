goodyship:/ˈɡʊdʃɪp/| n.|友谊；船只；船舶|A now largely obsolete term referring to friendship or camaraderie, particularly among sailors; also historically used to refer to a ship or vessel.|例句：The goodyship between the two men was evident in their easy conversation.  两个男人之间的友谊在他们轻松的谈话中显而易见。|近义词：friendship, camaraderie, fellowship, vessel, ship|反义词：enmity, hostility, antagonism
