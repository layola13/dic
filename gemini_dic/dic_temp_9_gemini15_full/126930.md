narking:/ˈnɑːkɪŋ/| v.|告密；打小报告；抱怨；批评|To inform on someone or something; to complain or grumble; to criticize.|例句：He was narking on his classmates to the teacher. 他向老师告发了他的同学们。

例句：She spent the entire afternoon narking about the weather. 她整个下午都在抱怨天气。


近义词：informing, snitching, complaining, criticizing,  whining

反义词：praising, complimenting, ignoring
