reabridged:/rɪˈbrɪdʒd/| adj.|删节本的；缩略的；简略的|Shortened or condensed, especially a book or text.|The abridged version of the novel is easier to read.  删节版的这本小说更容易阅读。|近义词：shortened, condensed, summarized, abbreviated|反义词： unabridged, expanded, full-length|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor is abridging the manuscript. = 编辑正在删节手稿。
This version of the dictionary is abridged. = 这个词典版本是删节的。

一般过去时:
They abridged the play for a shorter performance. = 他们为了缩短演出时间而删节了戏剧。
He abridged his report to meet the deadline. = 他为了赶上截止日期而缩短了他的报告。

现在完成时:
She has abridged several books for young readers. = 她已经为年轻读者删节了几本书。
We have abridged the original text considerably. = 我们已经大大缩短了原文。


过去完成时:
They had abridged the novel before publishing it. = 他们出版小说之前已经删节了它。
The author had abridged his manuscript many times before submitting it. = 作者在提交手稿之前已经多次删节了它。

将来时:
The publisher will abridge the encyclopedia for a new edition. = 出版社将为新版百科全书进行删节。
He will abridge his speech to fit the allotted time. = 他将缩短他的演讲以符合规定的时间。

虚拟语气:
If they abridged the book further, it would be too short. = 如果他们进一步删节这本书，它就会太短了。
I wish they had abridged the lengthy report. = 我希望他们已经删节了冗长的报告。

被动语态:
The text was abridged for clarity. = 文本为了清晰起见而被删节。
The movie was abridged to fit the time slot. = 电影被删节以适应时间段。

疑问句:
Was the article abridged before publication? = 这篇文章在发表前是否被删节了？
Has the book been abridged for children? = 这本书是否为儿童删节过？

否定句:
The original manuscript was not abridged. = 原稿没有删节。
The director did not abridge the film. = 导演没有删节电影。
