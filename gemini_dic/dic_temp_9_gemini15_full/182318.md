renaissancist:/ˌrɛnəˈsɑːnsɪst/| n. |文艺复兴时期的人；文艺复兴主义者|A person who lived during or who is associated with the Renaissance.  Someone who embodies the ideals and characteristics of the Renaissance. |例句：Many renaissancists were patrons of the arts, commissioning works from talented artists. 许多文艺复兴时期的人都是艺术赞助人，他们委托有才华的艺术家创作作品。|  例句：He studied the works of several prominent renaissancists to understand their philosophies. 他研究了几位杰出文艺复兴时期人物的作品以了解他们的哲学思想。|近义词：Renaissance man, humanist, revivalist |反义词：medievalist (in the context of opposing artistic and intellectual styles)
