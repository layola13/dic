responsiblest:/rɪˈspɑːnsəbl/| adj.|负责任的，尽责的|Responsible describes someone who is reliable, dependable, and takes ownership of their actions and obligations.  It implies a sense of duty and accountability. |He is a responsible employee and always completes his tasks on time. 他是一位负责任的员工，总是按时完成他的任务。She is responsible for managing the team. 她负责管理团队。|reliable, dependable, accountable, trustworthy|irresponsible, negligent, careless, reckless|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is responsible for the project. = 他负责这个项目。
She is responsible for her actions. = 她要为自己的行为负责。

一般过去时:
He was responsible for the accident. = 他对这次事故负有责任。
She was responsible for managing the team last year. = 去年她负责管理团队。

现在完成时:
He has been responsible for many successful projects. = 他已经负责过许多成功的项目。
She has been responsible for the department for five years. = 她已经负责这个部门五年了。

过去完成时:
He had been responsible for the company before the merger. = 合并之前，他一直负责这家公司。
She had been responsible for training new employees. = 她一直负责培训新员工。

将来时:
He will be responsible for the new marketing campaign. = 他将负责新的营销活动。
She will be responsible for overseeing the project. = 她将负责监督这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If he were responsible, he would have apologized. = 如果他负责任的话，他会道歉的。
If she had been responsible, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果她负责任的话，这件事就不会发生了。

被动语态:
The team is responsible for the results. = 团队对结果负责。
The success of the project was attributed to the responsible team. = 项目的成功归功于负责任的团队。

疑问句:
Who is responsible for this mess? = 谁应该为这烂摊子负责？
Is he responsible enough for the job? = 他胜任这份工作吗？

否定句:
He is not responsible for the damage. = 他对损坏不负责任。
She wasn't responsible for the delay. = 她对延误不负责任。


PHRASE->

be held responsible for = 对...承担责任
a responsible citizen = 一个负责任的公民
a responsible decision = 一个负责任的决定
