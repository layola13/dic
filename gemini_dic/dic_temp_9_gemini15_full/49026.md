degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/| vt.|去油，脱脂|To remove grease or oil from something.|例句：We need to degrease the engine before we rebuild it. 我们需要在重建发动机之前对其进行脱脂。|近义词：clean, defat, de-oil|反义词: grease, oil.
