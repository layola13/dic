hypopharynx:/ˌhaɪpəˈfærɪŋks/| n. |下咽|The hypopharynx is the part of the pharynx located below the level of the epiglottis, extending from the upper esophageal sphincter to the laryngeal inlet. It is a crucial part of the digestive and respiratory systems, playing a key role in swallowing and breathing.  It's a muscular tube lined with mucous membrane, and its function is to conduct food and air.  |The patient's hypopharynx was examined for any signs of cancer during the endoscopy.  患者的下咽在内窥镜检查中被检查是否有癌症迹象。|  oropharynx, laryngopharynx |esophagus (in terms of function)|
