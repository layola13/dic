grindery:/ˈɡraɪndəri/| n.|磨坊；研磨厂；磨具；研磨用具|A grindery is a place where things are ground or a collection of tools used for grinding.  It can refer to a mill or factory where grain is ground into flour, or it can refer to a workshop with tools for sharpening and grinding.|例句：The old grindery was powered by a water wheel.  古老的磨坊由水轮驱动。|例句：He keeps his grindery neatly organized in his workshop. 他把他的研磨工具整齐地放在他的工作室里。|近义词：mill, workshop, factory|反义词：N/A
