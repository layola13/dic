refiling:/ˌriːˈfaɪlɪŋ/| n.|重新提交；再次提交|The act of submitting something again, typically a document or application.  It implies a previous submission that was perhaps incomplete, rejected, or needs updating.|例句：The refiling of the lawsuit was necessary due to a procedural error. = 由于程序错误，有必要重新提交诉讼。

例句：The company's refiling of its patent application avoided potential legal issues. = 公司重新提交专利申请避免了潜在的法律问题。


近义词：resubmission, reapplication, resubmittal|反义词：withdrawal, rejection


词性：名词
