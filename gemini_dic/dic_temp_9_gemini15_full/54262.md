disgracing: /dɪsˈɡreɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 败坏；使丢脸；玷污 |  The act of causing shame or dishonor; bringing into disrepute.  |


例句：

His actions were disgracing to his family. = 他的行为败坏了他的家庭名声。

The scandal disgraced the entire organization. = 这场丑闻使整个组织蒙羞。


近义词：shaming, dishonoring, discrediting, defaming

反义词：honoring, glorifying, exalting


词源:

dis- (否定前缀) + grace (优雅，恩典) + -ing (现在分词后缀)


用法说明:

disgracing 是动词 disgrace 的现在分词形式，通常用作现在分词或形容词。


现在分词：

The disgracing behavior of the politician was widely reported. = 这位政治家的不光彩行为被广泛报道。

His continuous lying is disgracing his reputation. = 他持续不断的谎言正在败坏他的名誉。


形容词：

The disgracing incident shocked the whole town. =  这起丢脸的事件震惊了整个城镇。

Her disgracing conduct led to her dismissal. = 她不光彩的行为导致她被解雇。


其他时态的用法，由于disgracing本身是动名词，需结合其动词原形disgrace来解释：


一般现在时:

He disgraces himself with his bad behavior. = 他因不良行为而自取其辱。

They disgrace their families by their actions. = 他们因自己的行为而使家人蒙羞。


一般过去时:

He disgraced himself at the party last night. = 他昨晚在派对上丢尽了脸。

The athlete disgraced his country by cheating. =  这位运动员因作弊而使国家蒙羞。


现在完成时:

He has disgraced himself many times. = 他多次自取其辱。

She has disgraced her family name. = 她败坏了家族的名声。


过去完成时:

He had disgraced himself before the competition even started. =  比赛甚至还没开始，他就已经丢脸了。

They had disgraced the team by their poor performance. =  他们因糟糕的表现而使球队蒙羞。


将来时:

He will disgrace himself if he continues to act this way. = 如果他继续这样行为，他会自取其辱。

Their actions will disgrace the company. = 他们的行为将会使公司蒙羞。


虚拟语气:

If he had acted differently, he wouldn't have disgraced himself. = 如果他行为有所不同，他就不会自取其辱。

I wish he hadn't disgraced the family name. = 我真希望他没有败坏家族的名声。


被动语态:

He was disgraced by his actions. = 他因自己的行为而蒙羞。

The team was disgraced by the scandal. =  该团队因丑闻而蒙羞。


疑问句:

Did his actions disgrace him? = 他的行为使他蒙羞了吗？

Will this decision disgrace the company? = 这个决定会使公司蒙羞吗？


否定句:

His actions did not disgrace him. = 他的行为没有使他蒙羞。

The company will not be disgraced by this incident. = 公司不会因为这起事件而蒙羞。


PHRASE->

bring disgrace on/upon = 使...蒙羞

fall from grace = 失宠；名誉扫地

live in disgrace = 蒙受耻辱地生活

a disgrace to =  是...的耻辱

the disgrace of = ...的耻辱
