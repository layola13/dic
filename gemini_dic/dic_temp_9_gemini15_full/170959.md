pseudophallic: /ˌsuːdoʊˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 假阳具的；类似阳具的 |  Relating to or resembling a phallus;  a term used to describe something that superficially resembles a penis but lacks its true function or structure.  This term is often used in the context of botany, zoology, and psychology. |

例句：

The plant's unusual growth was described as pseudophallic due to its elongated, phallic-like shape. = 植物异常的生长由于其细长、类似阳具的形状而被描述为假阳具的。

The psychologist noted the patient's pseudophallic anxieties, stemming from childhood experiences. = 心理学家注意到患者的假阳具焦虑，这源于童年经历。


近义词：phallic-like, penis-shaped

反义词：non-phallic


词源:

* **pseudo-:**  From Greek *pseudēs*, meaning "false," "lying," or "spurious."

* **phallic:** From Greek *phallikos*, relating to the phallus (penis).
