fogging:/ˈfɒɡɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːɡɪŋ/| v.|喷雾；使模糊；(使)起雾|To cover or obscure with fog or a similar substance; to apply a liquid in a fine spray.|例句：The farmer was fogging his fields to protect the crops from frost.  农民正在给田地喷雾以保护庄稼免受霜冻。

例句：The heavy fog was fogging the windows.  浓雾使窗户模糊不清。|近义词：spraying, misting, obscuring|反义词：clearing, brightening|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fogs his orchids regularly. = 他定期给他的兰花喷雾。
They fog the streets to reduce dust. = 他们向街道喷雾以减少灰尘。

一般过去时:
