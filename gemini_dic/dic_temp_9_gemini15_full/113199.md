lurked:/lɜːkt/| v.|潜伏；潜藏；隐匿|To remain hidden or concealed, often in a sinister or secretive way.  It implies a sense of waiting or watching, often with ill intent.|例句：The thief lurked in the shadows, waiting for the right moment to strike. 小偷潜伏在阴影中，等待合适的时机下手。

例句：A dangerous feeling lurked in the back of my mind.  我心中潜藏着一种危险的感觉。


近义词：hid, concealed, shadowed, waited,  prowled


反义词：revealed, exposed, unveiled


USAGE->

He lurks in the alleyway. = 他潜伏在小巷里。
The danger lurks beneath the surface. = 危险潜伏在表面之下。
