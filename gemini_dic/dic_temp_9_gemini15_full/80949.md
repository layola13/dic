grabs:/ɡræbz/| vt.|抓住；夺取；吸引；引起注意 |to take hold of something or someone suddenly and firmly|例句：He grabbed the rope and pulled himself up. 他抓住绳子把自己拉了上去。|例句：The news grabbed the attention of the whole nation. 这条新闻引起了全国的关注。|近义词：seize, snatch, catch, grip|反义词：release, let go


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grabs the phone every morning. = 他每天早上都抓起电话。
She grabs a quick lunch before work. = 她在上班前快速吃午餐。


一般过去时:
I grabbed my bag and ran to the bus stop. = 我抓起我的包跑到公交车站。
He grabbed the opportunity to speak. = 他抓住机会发言了。


现在完成时:
She has grabbed the lead in the race. = 她已经占据了比赛的领先地位。
He has grabbed a few minutes to relax. = 他已经抽出几分钟来放松一下。


过去完成时:
I had grabbed my coat before leaving the house. = 我离开家之前已经拿好外套了。
They had grabbed the chance to escape before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们已经抓住机会逃跑了。


将来时:
I will grab a coffee on my way to work. = 我上班途中会买杯咖啡。
She will grab the chance to travel abroad. = 她将抓住机会出国旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I could grab that job, I would be so happy. = 如果我能得到那份工作，我会非常高兴。
I wish I could have grabbed that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时能抓住那个机会。


被动语态:
The thief was grabbed by the police. = 小偷被警察抓住了。
The opportunity was quickly grabbed by a competitor. = 机会很快被竞争对手抓住了。


疑问句:
Did he grab the money? = 他拿了钱吗？
Will she grab the microphone? = 她会抓住麦克风吗？


否定句:
He didn't grab the chance to apologize. = 他没有抓住机会道歉。
I couldn't grab the ball in time. = 我没能及时抓住球。
