hippocras:/ˈhɪpəkˌræs/| n. |希波克拉底酒，一种加香料的葡萄酒|A spiced, sweetened wine popular in the Middle Ages. It was often served medicinally and was named after Hippocrates, the ancient Greek physician, although there's no evidence he actually made or drank it.  |Example: The medieval feast featured a variety of dishes, and hippocras was served to aid digestion. 中世纪的盛宴上有各种各样的菜肴，还有希波克拉底酒助消化。|近义词： spiced wine, mulled wine |反义词： plain wine
