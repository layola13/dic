crowdedness:/ˈkraʊdɪdnəs/| n. |拥挤，人群众多|The state or quality of being crowded; a large number of people or things occupying a limited space.  It often implies a feeling of discomfort or constraint.|例句：The crowdedness of the subway made it difficult to breathe.  地铁的拥挤让人难以呼吸。

例句：The crowdedness of the concert venue made it hard to enjoy the performance.  音乐会的场地过于拥挤，难以欣赏演出。


近义词：congestion, overpopulation, density, compression

反义词：spaciousness, emptiness, roominess


词性：名词
