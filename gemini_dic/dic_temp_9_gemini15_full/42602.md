counterprotester: /ˈkaʊntərˈproʊˌtɛstər/ | n. | 反抗议者 | A person who participates in a protest against another protest.  Someone who actively opposes or demonstrates against a protest or demonstration. | Example: The counterprotesters outnumbered the original protesters, leading to a tense standoff.  反对抗议者的人数超过了最初的抗议者，导致了紧张的僵局。 |近义词: opponent, adversary, antagonist | 反义词: protester, demonstrator | 名词
