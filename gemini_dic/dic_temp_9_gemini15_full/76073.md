furnus:/ˈfɜːrnəs/| n.|炉子，熔炉|A furnus is an enclosed structure in which intense heat is produced for cooking, melting metals, or other industrial processes.  It is often made of fire-resistant materials and designed to contain and control the heat.|例句：The ancient Romans used furnuses to heat their bathhouses. 古罗马人用炉子给他们的浴室加热。|例句：The steel is melted in a large furnus. 钢铁在一个大型熔炉中熔化。|近义词：oven, furnace, kiln|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  The usage depends on the context and the preposition used with it.
