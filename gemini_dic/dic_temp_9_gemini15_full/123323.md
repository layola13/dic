monographical:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 专题的；论著的；专论的 | Relating to or characteristic of a monograph; treating a single subject in detail.  A monographical study focuses on a specific aspect or topic in depth. | 例句：He wrote a monographical study on the life of Leonardo da Vinci. 他写了一篇关于达芬奇生平的专题研究。

例句：The professor delivered a monographical lecture on ancient Greek pottery. 教授做了一个关于古希腊陶器的专题讲座。


近义词：specialized, detailed, exhaustive, comprehensive


反义词：general, broad, superficial, cursory


词性：形容词
