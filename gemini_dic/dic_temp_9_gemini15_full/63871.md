epileptical:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪkəl/| adj.|癫痫的，癫痫发作的|Relating to or characteristic of epilepsy; suffering from epilepsy.  The word describes something connected to or indicative of epilepsy, a neurological disorder causing seizures.|例句：He experienced an epileptical seizure during the meeting. 他在会议期间经历了一次癫痫发作。|例句：The doctor confirmed the epileptical nature of her fits. 医生证实了她发作的癫痫性质。|近义词：convulsive, spasmodic|反义词：None specifically antonymous, but could use terms implying neurological normalcy.|词性：形容词
