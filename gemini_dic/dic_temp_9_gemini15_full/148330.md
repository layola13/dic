oversow: /ˈoʊvərsoʊ/ | vt. | 过度播种；再播种 | To sow seed on (land) in addition to or after the first sowing.  It often implies sowing more densely than usual or sowing a different type of seed. | 例句：We oversowed the field with rye to prevent soil erosion. = 我们在田里过量播种黑麦以防止水土流失。

例句：The farmer oversowed the barley with clover to improve the soil. = 农民在小麦上再播种三叶草以改良土壤。


近义词：resow, sow again, plant additionally | 反义词：harvest, reap
