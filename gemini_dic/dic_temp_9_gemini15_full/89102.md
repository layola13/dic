homage:/ˈhɒmɪdʒ/ /ˈɑːmɪdʒ/| n. |敬意，崇敬，致敬 |The act of showing respect or admiration for someone or something.  It can be expressed through various actions or offerings.  |例句：The film is a homage to classic Hollywood musicals. 这部电影是对经典好莱坞歌舞片的致敬。

例句：They paid homage to the fallen soldiers. 他们向阵亡士兵致敬。


近义词：tribute, reverence, respect, admiration

反义词：disrespect, scorn, contempt

词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pays homage to his heroes. = 他向他的英雄致敬。
The artist's work pays homage to nature. = 艺术家的作品是对自然的致敬。


一般过去时:
She paid homage to her mentor. = 她向她的导师致敬。
The band paid homage to their musical influences. = 乐队向他们的音乐影响致敬。


现在完成时:

The museum has paid homage to the artist's life and work. = 博物馆已经向这位艺术家的生平和作品致敬。


过去完成时:

The sculptor had paid homage to classical forms in his early work. = 雕塑家在其早期作品中向古典形式致敬。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I were to write a novel, I would pay homage to my favorite authors. = 如果我要写小说，我会向我最喜欢的作家致敬。
I wish I could pay homage to the great artists of the Renaissance. = 我希望我能向文艺复兴时期的大艺术家们致敬。


被动语态:
Homage was paid to the deceased by his family and friends. = 他的家人和朋友向死者致敬。
The artist's work was seen as a homage to surrealism. = 艺术家的作品被视为对超现实主义的致敬。


疑问句:

否定句:
