mips:/mɪps/| n.| 每秒百万条指令 | Millions of Instructions Per Second, a measure of a computer's processing speed, representing the number of millions of instructions a central processing unit (CPU) can execute per second.  | Example:  The new processor boasts a MIPS rating of 5000. 新的处理器拥有5000 MIPS的额定值。|近义词：FLOPS (floating-point operations per second),  CPI (cycles per instruction)|反义词:None.
