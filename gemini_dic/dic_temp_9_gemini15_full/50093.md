denticulated:/dɛnˈtɪkjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有细齿的；锯齿状的 | Having small teeth or tooth-like projections; resembling a saw in having a series of small, pointed projections.  | Example: The leaf margin was denticulated, giving it a finely serrated appearance.  叶缘有细齿，呈现出精细的锯齿状外观。  |近义词：serrated, toothed, dentate | 反义词：smooth, entire
