riverboat:/ˈrɪvərboʊt/| n.|河船，内河船|A boat designed for travel on rivers.  Riverboats are typically flat-bottomed to navigate shallow waters and often have features suitable for passenger transport or freight hauling.|例句：The Mississippi riverboat was a majestic sight, steaming down the river.密西西比河上的汽船景象雄伟壮观，沿着河流冒着蒸汽缓缓驶下。|例句：We took a scenic riverboat cruise through the canals of Amsterdam. 我们乘坐游船在阿姆斯特丹的运河上进行了风景优美的游览。|近义词：river steamer, barge (if for freight)|反义词：ocean liner, seagoing vessel
