emblazonment:/ɪmˈbleɪzənment/| n. |装饰；炫耀；显著标志|The act of emblazoning; the state of being emblazoned; a decoration or ornament, especially one that is prominent or showy.|The emblazonment of the family crest on the shield was a symbol of their heritage. 家族纹章在盾牌上的装饰象征着他们的传统。The emblazonment of their victory across the headlines was a testament to their hard work. 他们胜利的消息占据各大头条，证明了他们的辛勤工作。|adornment, ornamentation, decoration|obscurity, concealment|名词


USAGE->

This company emblazons its logo on all its products. = 这家公司在其所有产品上都印有其标志。
