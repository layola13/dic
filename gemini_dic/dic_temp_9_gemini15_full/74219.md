four-year: /ˌfɔːrˈjɪr/ | adj. | 四年的；为期四年的 |  Four-year refers to a period of four years or something lasting four years. It is a compound adjective, formed by combining "four" and "year" with a hyphen.  | Example:  He completed a four-year degree. 他完成了一个为期四年的学位。  She's been working at the company for four years. 她在这家公司工作了四年。|近义词: quadriennial (if referring to a period or event) | 反义词:  None directly, but one could use terms like "one-year," "two-year," etc. as contrasting terms. | adjective
