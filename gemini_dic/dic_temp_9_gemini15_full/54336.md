The reasons for disherison can vary widely, from family disputes to the heir's misconduct.|例句：The son's rebellious behavior led to his disherison.  =儿子的叛逆行为导致他被剥夺继承权。

The farmer's will resulted in the disherison of his estranged daughter. = 农民的遗嘱导致他疏远的女儿被剥夺继承权。|近义词：disinheritance, dispossession|反义词：inheritance, bequest|名词
