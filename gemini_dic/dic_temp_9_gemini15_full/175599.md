range:/reɪndʒ/| /reɪnʒ/| n./v.|范围，幅度；排列；变化；延伸；放牧|to extend over a particular area or range of values; a set of similar things; a series of mountains or hills|The price range is from $100 to $500. 价格范围在100美元到500美元之间。  The mountains range across the horizon. 山脉绵延到地平线。|scope, extent, span, gamut|limit, restrict|名词/动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The temperature ranges from 20 to 30 degrees. = 温度范围在20到30度之间。
His interests range from music to sports. = 他的兴趣从音乐到体育。

一般过去时:
The survey ranged across several different demographics. = 调查涵盖了几个不同的统计群体。
The cattle ranged freely on the plains. = 牛群在平原上自由地吃草。

现在完成时:
The project has ranged over many years. = 这个项目已经持续了很多年。
The effects of the medicine have ranged from mild to severe. = 这种药的效果从轻微到严重不等。

过去完成时:
The discussion had ranged widely before a conclusion was reached. = 在得出结论之前，讨论已经广泛展开。
The search had ranged across multiple states before finding the missing person. = 在找到失踪人员之前，搜寻范围已遍及多个州。

将来时:
The company's influence will range across several continents. = 该公司影响力将遍及几大洲。
The debate will range over several key issues. = 辩论将涵盖几个关键问题。

虚拟语气:
If the price range were lower, I would buy it. = 如果价格范围低一些，我就买了。
I wish the range of options were wider. = 我希望选择范围更广一些。

被动语态:
The products are ranged on the shelves by price. = 产品按价格排列在货架上。
The area is ranged by herds of wild horses. = 这片地区是野马群的放牧地。

疑问句:
What is the range of your product line? = 你的产品线范围是什么？
What does the range of mountains encompass? = 这片山脉包括什么？

否定句:
The data doesn't range far beyond the average. = 数据并没有超出平均值太多。
The benefits of the program don't range widely. = 该计划的好处范围不广。

in the range of = 大约在……范围内
beyond the range of = 超出……的范围
out of range = 超出范围
