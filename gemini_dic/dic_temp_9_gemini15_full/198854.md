siderolite:/ˈsaɪdərəlaɪt/| n. |陨石(一种)|A siderolite is a stony-iron meteorite, a type of meteorite that is composed of roughly equal amounts of silicate minerals and nickel-iron metal.  They are relatively rare compared to other types of meteorites. |The siderolite was found in the Arizona desert. 这块陨石是在亚利桑那沙漠发现的。| stony meteorite, stony-iron meteorite |  n/a | n.
