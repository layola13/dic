hermitary:/ˈhɜːrmɪtəri/ | n. |隐居处，隐士住所 | A hermitary is a dwelling place of a hermit; a secluded place.  | Example: The hermit lived a solitary life in his secluded hermitary, far from the bustling city.  隐士在他僻静的隐居处过着孤独的生活，远离喧嚣的城市。 |近义词：hermitage, retreat, seclusion |反义词：metropolis, city
