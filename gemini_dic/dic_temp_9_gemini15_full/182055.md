remend:/rɪˈmɛnd/| vt.|修补，弥补，补偿|To repair something that is damaged or broken; to make amends for a fault or mistake.|例句：The tailor remended the tear in my trousers.  裁缝修补了我裤子上的破洞。|例句：He tried to remend his relationship with his brother. 他试图弥补和兄弟的关系。|近义词：repair, mend, fix, patch|反义词：break, damage, destroy|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remands the broken vase. = 他修补了破花瓶。
She remands her mistakes by apologizing. = 她通过道歉来弥补她的错误。

一般过去时:
They remended the fence after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们修补了栅栏。
He remended his broken promise with a gift. = 他用一份礼物弥补了他失信的承诺。

现在完成时:
She has remended all the holes in her sweater. = 她已经修补了她毛衣上的所有洞。
He has remended his relationship with his colleagues. = 他已经修复了他和同事的关系。

过去完成时:
They had remended the roof before the rain started. = 在雨开始下之前，他们已经修补好了屋顶。
She had remended her error before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，她已经弥补了她的错误。

将来时:
He will remend the torn shirt tomorrow. = 他明天将修补那件破衬衫。
They will remend the damage caused by the accident. = 他们将弥补事故造成的损失。

虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would remend the chair. = 如果我有工具，我会修补椅子。
I wish I had remended the relationship sooner. = 我真希望我早点弥补了这段关系。

被动语态:
The broken window was remended by the glazier. = 破窗户被玻璃匠修好了。
The damage will be remended by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司赔偿。

疑问句:
Can you remend this tear in my dress? = 你能修补我衣服上的这个裂口吗？
Will they remend the bridge before next month? = 他们会在下个月之前修好桥吗？

否定句:
He couldn't remend the antique clock. = 他无法修补这台古董钟。
She didn't remend the broken vase. = 她没有修补破花瓶。
