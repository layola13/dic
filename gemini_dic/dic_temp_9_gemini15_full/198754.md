sicken:/ˈsɪkən/| vt. vi.|使厌恶；使恶心；使生病；使腐败|To cause (someone) to feel disgust or nausea; to make (someone) ill; to cause (something) to become rotten or corrupt.|例句：The smell of rotting fish sickened me.  腐烂鱼的臭味让我恶心。

The news sickened her. 这个消息让她恶心。


近义词：disgust, nauseate, revolt,  offend|反义词：heal, cure, delight|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell of garbage sickens me. = 垃圾的气味让我恶心。
The repetitive work sickens him. = 重复性的工作让他厌烦。


一般过去时:
