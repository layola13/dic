impeachment:/ɪmˈpiːtʃmənt/| n. |弹劾；控告；指控|The formal process of accusing a public official of misconduct in office.  This process often leads to a trial and potential removal from office.  It's a crucial part of checks and balances in many governmental systems. |例句：The impeachment proceedings against the president were highly controversial.  总统的弹劾程序极具争议性。|例句：The articles of impeachment detailed numerous instances of alleged wrongdoing. 弹劾条款详细列举了多起所谓的违法行为。|近义词：accusation, indictment, charge|反义词：acquittal, exoneration
