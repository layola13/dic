consultations:/ˌkɒnsəlˈteɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːnsəlˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 咨询；会诊；商讨 |  The act of consulting; a meeting for consultation.  It refers to a formal discussion between people to seek advice, information, or to make a decision. | 例句：The doctor recommended further consultations with a specialist. 医生建议进一步咨询专家。

例句：The team held several consultations before making a final decision.  团队在做出最终决定之前进行了多次协商。


近义词：discussions, meetings, conferences, deliberations

反义词：decisions (in the sense of a lack of consultation)


词性：名词
