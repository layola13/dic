gab:/ɡæb/| v. n.|闲聊，喋喋不休；空话 |To talk casually and continuously, often about unimportant matters; idle talk. |例句：They were gabbing away about their holiday. 他们一直在闲聊他们的假期。

例句：The old women were gabbing on the park bench. 老妇人们在公园的长椅上喋喋不休。


近义词：chatter, prate, blab, gossip |反义词：silence, quiet


词性:动词，名词
