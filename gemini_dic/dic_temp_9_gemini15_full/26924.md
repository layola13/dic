calamitous:/kəˈlæmɪtəs/| adj. |灾难性的，不幸的| causing great and often sudden damage or suffering; disastrous.| The calamitous earthquake devastated the city.  这场灾难性的地震摧毁了这座城市。|近义词：disastrous, catastrophic, ruinous|反义词：fortunate, auspicious, beneficial|
