holophote:/ˈhɒləfoʊt/ | n. | 全向灯；泛光灯 | A holophote is a type of lamp or light fitting designed to distribute light evenly in all directions,  typically using a reflector to achieve this.  It's often used for general illumination. | Example: The museum installed holophote lighting to evenly illuminate the exhibits.  博物馆安装了全向灯，以均匀地照亮展品。|近义词：floodlight, spotlight (in some contexts)|反义词：directional light, focused light
