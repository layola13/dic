intercommunity: /ˌɪntərˈkəmjuːnɪti/ | adj. n. | 社区间的；社区间的合作 | Relating to or involving more than one community.  The term often implies collaboration or interaction between different communities. |

例句：

The intercommunity project aimed to improve transportation between the neighboring towns.  社区间项目旨在改善邻近城镇之间的交通运输。

There is significant intercommunity cooperation on environmental protection initiatives.  在环境保护倡议方面存在大量的社区间合作。


近义词：inter-community, cross-community, multi-community

反义词：intracommunity (within a single community)


词性：形容词，名词
