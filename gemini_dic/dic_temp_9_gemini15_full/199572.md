singsong:/ˈsɪŋsɒŋ/| adj.|吟唱似的，轻快活泼的|Having a light, cheerful, and often slightly monotonous tone; characterized by a repetitive, melodic quality.  It often describes a speech pattern or a musical quality.|例句：She spoke in a singsong voice. 她用轻快活泼的嗓音说话。

例句：The children's laughter was a singsong sound. 孩子们的笑声像轻快的歌声。


近义词：melodious, lilting, chanting, rhythmic, musical

反义词：monotonous (in the sense of lacking melodic quality), harsh, gruff


词源:The word is a compound of "sing" and "song," reflecting its descriptive nature.  The "song" element emphasizes the melodic or repetitive quality, while "sing" suggests the act of producing such a sound.
