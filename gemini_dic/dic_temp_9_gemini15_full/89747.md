hopping:/ˈhɑːpɪŋ/|/ˈhɒpɪŋ/| v.|跳跃；蹦跳；快速移动|To move by hopping; to jump on one foot repeatedly; to move quickly or excitedly.|例句：The rabbit was hopping across the field. 兔子正在田野里跳跃。

The children were hopping with excitement. 孩子们兴奋地蹦跳着。


近义词：jumping, leaping, skipping|反义词：crawling, walking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hops on one leg. = 他单腿跳。
The kangaroo hops across the plains. = 袋鼠在平原上跳跃。


一般过去时:
She hopped over the fence. = 她跳过篱笆。
The frog hopped into the pond. = 青蛙跳进了池塘。


现在完成时:
I have hopped on the bandwagon. = 我已经加入了潮流。
They have hopped on the train. = 他们已经上了火车。


过去完成时:
He had hopped off the bus before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他已经下了公共汽车。
She had hopped over the wall before the guard saw her. = 警卫看到她之前，她已经翻过了墙。


将来时:
I will hop on the next bus. = 我将搭乘下一班车。
He will hop over to see his friend. = 他将跳过去看望他的朋友。


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would hop from branch to branch. = 如果我是一只鸟，我会在树枝间跳跃。
I wish I could hop as high as that kangaroo. = 我希望我能像那只袋鼠一样跳得那么高。


被动语态:
The ball was hopped over the net. = 球被高高地抛过网。
The message was hopped across the network. = 消息在网络上传播。


疑问句:
Is the rabbit hopping? = 兔子在跳吗？
Why are you hopping around? = 你为什么蹦蹦跳跳的？


否定句:
He didn't hop on the train. = 他没有上火车。
The children didn't hop into the pool. = 孩子们没有跳进游泳池。
