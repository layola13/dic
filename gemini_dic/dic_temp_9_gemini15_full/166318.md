prerefusal: /ˌpriːrɪˈfjuːzəl/ | n. | 预先拒绝；事先拒绝 | A prior refusal; a rejection made before a formal request or offer is made.  It often implies an anticipation of a request and a proactive rejection to avoid it. | Example:  The company issued a prerefusal to the merger proposal before it was even formally submitted. 公司在合并提案正式提交之前就发出了预先拒绝。


近义词：preemptive rejection, anticipatory refusal, preemptive denial

反义词：acceptance, approval


词性:名词


USAGE->
无，因为prerefusal本身是名词，不具备动词的时态变化。


PHRASE->
无，prerefusal本身就是一个完整的词，一般不与其他词构成常用短语。
