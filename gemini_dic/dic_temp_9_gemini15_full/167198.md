pretend:/prɪˈtɛnd/ | v. |假装，伪装 | To behave as if something is true when it is not, in order to deceive someone or for fun. |例句：He pretended to be asleep. 他假装睡着了。|例句：She pretended she didn't see me. 她假装没看见我。|近义词：feign, simulate, affect |反义词：be honest, be genuine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pretends he doesn't care. = 他假装不在乎。
They pretend to be rich. = 他们假装有钱。

一般过去时:
She pretended to be ill yesterday. = 她昨天假装生病了。
He pretended he hadn't seen the letter. = 他假装没看见那封信。

现在完成时:
I have pretended to be brave my whole life. = 我一辈子都在假装勇敢。
They have pretended not to notice us. = 他们假装没注意到我们。

过去完成时:
She had pretended to be happy before the news came. = 在消息传来之前，她一直假装快乐。
He had pretended not to know me for years. = 他多年来一直假装不认识我。

将来时:
I will pretend I didn't hear you. = 我会假装没听见你说话。
They will pretend to be busy. = 他们会假装很忙。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pretend to be sick. = 如果我是你，我会假装生病。
I wish I could pretend to be someone else. = 我真希望我能假装成别人。

被动语态:
His illness was pretended.  = 他的病是装出来的。(This is less common, as "pretend" is usually active.)
It was pretended that nothing was wrong. = 人们假装一切正常。(This is a more common passive use.)


疑问句:
Are you pretending to be deaf? = 你是在假装听不见吗？
Is she pretending to be interested? = 她是在假装感兴趣吗？

否定句:
He didn't pretend to know the answer. = 他没有假装知道答案。
She doesn't pretend to be perfect. = 她不假装完美。


PHRASE->
pretend to be = 假装是...
pretend to do something = 假装做某事
pretend that = 假装...
make-believe pretend = 假装游戏
pretend sleep = 假装睡觉


词源:
From Old French *pre-tendre, from pre- "before" + tendre "to stretch, extend."  The modern sense developed from the idea of "stretching forth" a false appearance.
