scholar:/ˈskɑːlər/|/ˈskɒlə(r)/| n. |学者；奖学金获得者 |A scholar is a person who studies a subject in great detail, especially at an advanced level, often at a university.  They are typically experts in their field and possess extensive knowledge. |例句：He is a renowned scholar of ancient history. 他是一位著名的古代史学者。|例句：She received a scholarship to study abroad. 她获得奖学金去国外留学。|近义词：student, academic, expert, intellectual |反义词：ignoramus, dunce

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He studies ancient literature. = 他研究古代文学。
She researches the impact of climate change. = 她研究气候变化的影响。

一般过去时:
They studied medieval art history. = 他们研究过中世纪艺术史。
He researched the causes of the war. = 他研究了战争的原因。

现在完成时:
She has published several books on philosophy. = 她已经出版了几本关于哲学的书。
He has completed his doctoral dissertation.  = 他已经完成了他的博士论文。

过去完成时:
They had already learned Latin before starting their graduate program. = 在他们开始研究生课程之前，他们已经学过拉丁语了。
She had researched the topic extensively before writing her paper. = 在写论文之前，她已经广泛地研究了这个主题。

将来时:
He will study abroad next year. = 他明年将出国留学。
She will present her research findings at the conference. = 她将在会议上展示她的研究成果。

虚拟语气:
If I were a scholar, I would dedicate my life to research. = 如果我是一位学者，我会把我的生命奉献给研究。
I wish I had the time to pursue my scholarly interests. = 我希望我有时间去追求我的学术兴趣。

被动语态:
The manuscript was reviewed by several scholars. = 这篇手稿被几位学者审阅了。
His theories are widely studied in universities. = 他的理论在大学里被广泛研究。

疑问句:
Is he a scholar of linguistics? = 他是语言学学者吗？
What are the main findings of her scholarly work? = 她学术工作的的主要发现是什么？

否定句:
He is not a scholar of mathematics. = 他不是数学学者。
She did not publish her research findings. = 她没有发表她的研究成果。
