inchoatives: /ɪnˈkoʊeɪtɪvz/ | n. | 起始的；初步的；未完成的 | Inchoative verbs describe the beginning of an action or state.  They often express the commencement of a process or the transition into a new state.  These verbs are often formed by adding prefixes or suffixes to existing verbs or by using specific verbs that inherently indicate the beginning of an action. |


例句：

The snow began to fall. = 雪开始下起来了。

The wound started to heal. = 伤口开始愈合了。


近义词：incipient, nascent, initial, commencing


反义词：completed, finished, concluded, ultimate


词性：名词
