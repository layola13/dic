disking:/ˈdɪskɪŋ/| n. |磁盘化；犁地；|The act of using a disk harrow to prepare land for planting.  This can also refer to the process of creating or using computer disks. |例句：The farmer spent the morning disking the field.  农民花了整个上午犁地。|近义词：plowing, harrowing, tilling |反义词：None specifically; antonyms depend on the context (e.g., leaving land untouched).
