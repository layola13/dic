fearer:/ˈfɪərər/| n. |害怕者，恐惧者 |A person who experiences or expresses fear.  Someone who is easily frightened or intimidated. |例句：He was a known fearer of public speaking. 他是一个已知害怕公开演讲的人。|例句：The little girl, a fearer of the dark, clung to her mother's hand. 小女孩害怕黑暗，紧紧抓住她母亲的手。|近义词：coward, scaredy-cat, phobic |反义词：brave, courageous, fearless
