die-cast: /ˈdaɪkæst/ | adj. |压铸的，模压铸造的 |  Relating to or denoting a method of manufacturing metal objects by pouring molten metal into a mold under pressure.  Die-cast objects are known for their precise details and durability. | 例句：He collects die-cast model cars. 他收集压铸模型车。

He showed me his impressive collection of die-cast airplanes. 他给我展示了他令人印象深刻的压铸飞机收藏。


近义词：molded, cast  | 反义词：hand-made, hand-crafted | 词性：形容词
