richer:/ˈrɪtʃər/| adj.|更富有的；更丰富的；更浓厚的 | More wealthy; possessing greater abundance or richness; more intense or concentrated. |例句：He became richer after inheriting his uncle's fortune. 他继承了他叔叔的财产后变得更富有。|例句：The richer the soil, the better the crops. 土壤越肥沃，庄稼越好。|近义词：wealthier, more affluent, more opulent |反义词：poorer, less affluent, impecunious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The richer person often donates more to charity. =  较富有的人通常会向慈善机构捐款更多。
This wine is richer in flavor than that one. = 这款酒比那款酒味道更浓郁。

一般过去时:
She became much richer after selling her business. = 她卖掉她的生意后变得富有得多。
The soup tasted richer after adding cream. = 加了奶油后，汤的味道更浓郁了。

现在完成时:
He has become much richer since he started his own company. = 自从他创办了自己的公司以来，他已经变得富有得多。
The color has become richer after several coats of paint. = 涂了几层漆后，颜色更深沉了。

过去完成时:
They had become richer than anyone expected. = 他们变得比任何人都预料的还要富有。
The cake had become richer and more moist after sitting overnight. = 蛋糕放置一夜后变得更松软湿润了。

将来时:
She will be richer if she invests wisely. = 如果她投资明智，她将会更富有。
The painting will look richer with a new frame. = 这幅画配上新的画框会显得更精美。

虚拟语气:
If I were richer, I would travel the world. = 如果我更富有，我会周游世界。
I wish the colors were richer in this photograph. = 我希望这张照片的颜色更鲜艳。

被动语态:
The broth is made richer by the addition of herbs. = 加入香料后，肉汤变得更鲜美了。
The soil is made richer through composting. = 通过堆肥使土壤变得更肥沃。

疑问句:
Is the chocolate cake richer than the vanilla cake? = 巧克力蛋糕比香草蛋糕更浓郁吗？
Which painting is richer in color? = 哪幅画的颜色更丰富？

否定句:
The fabric is not as rich or as expensive. = 这种面料不如那种面料丰富或昂贵。
The soup isn't richer after adding water. = 加水后汤没有更浓郁。
