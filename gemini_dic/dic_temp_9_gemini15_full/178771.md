recultivated:/ˌriːˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/| adj. | 再耕作的；重新耕种的 |  Having been cultivated again after a period of neglect or abandonment.  It implies the restoration of land to a productive state. | Example: The recultivated fields yielded a bountiful harvest.  再耕作的田地获得了丰收。|近义词：reclaimed, restored, rehabilitated | 反义词：degraded, depleted, ruined |
