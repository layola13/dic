hypersensitivities:/ˌhaɪpərsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/| n. |过敏反应（复数）|An exaggerated or excessive immune response to an antigen (allergen).  Hypersensitivities are classified into four types based on the mechanism of the reaction. They range from mild reactions like hay fever to severe, life-threatening conditions like anaphylaxis.|Example:  Many people suffer from various hypersensitivities, such as allergies to pollen or peanuts. 许多人患有各种过敏反应，例如对花粉或花生过敏。


Example:  The patient's hypersensitivities were triggered by exposure to cat dander. 病人的过敏反应是由接触猫毛引起的。|近义词：allergies, sensitivities, immune responses |反义词：hyposensitivities (less common and often implies a decreased or absent immune response) |词性:noun
