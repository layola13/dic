pooed:/puːd/| v.| 过去式，拉屎；排泄粪便 | To defecate; to excrete feces.  |The dog pooed on the carpet.  狗在毯子上拉屎了。|  |  | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He poos regularly. = 他定期排便。
The baby poos several times a day. = 婴儿一天拉好几次屎。


一般过去时:
The dog pooed in the garden. = 狗在花园里拉屎了。
She pooed on the hiking trail. = 她在远足小路上拉屎了。


现在完成时:
The dog has pooed on the rug again. = 狗又在地毯上拉屎了。
He has pooed three times today. = 他今天已经拉了三次屎。


过去完成时:
The child had pooed before we got home. = 我们回家之前，孩子已经拉屎了。
They had pooed before the walk. = 散步之前他们已经拉过屎了。


将来时:
The baby will poo soon. = 婴儿很快就要拉屎了。
The dog will poo before we leave. = 我们走之前，狗会拉屎。


虚拟语气:
If the dog pooed inside, I would be angry. = 如果狗在屋里拉屎，我会很生气。
I wish the cat hadn't pooed on the bed. = 我真希望猫没在床上拉屎。


被动语态:
(Pooed is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed.)


疑问句:
Has the dog pooed? = 狗拉屎了吗？
Did the baby poo this morning? = 宝宝今天早上拉屎了吗？


否定句:
The dog didn't poo all day. = 狗一整天都没拉屎。
She hasn't pooed since yesterday. = 她从昨天起就没拉屎了。
