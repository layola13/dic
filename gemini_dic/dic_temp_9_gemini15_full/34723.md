clicked:/klɪkt/| v.|点击，发出咔嗒声|To press a button or link on a computer screen or other device; to make a short, sharp sound like that of a small object hitting another.  |例句：I clicked the "submit" button. 我点击了“提交”按钮。

例句：The old door clicked shut.  旧门咔嗒一声关上了。|近义词：pressed, tapped, snapped|反义词：ignored, overlooked|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I click the link every day. = 我每天都点击这个链接。
He clicks the mouse repeatedly. = 他反复点击鼠标。

一般过去时:
I clicked the button and the page loaded. = 我点击了按钮，页面加载了。
She clicked on the advertisement. = 她点击了广告。

现在完成时:
I have clicked the link several times. = 我已经点击了这个链接好几次了。
He has clicked on every image on the page. = 他已经点击了页面上的每一张图片。

过去完成时:
I had clicked the link before the page crashed. = 在页面崩溃之前，我已经点击了链接。
She had clicked the wrong button. = 她点击了错误的按钮。

将来时:
I will click the button when I'm ready. = 我准备好后就会点击按钮。
He will click on the link tomorrow. = 他明天将点击链接。

虚拟语气:
If I clicked the button, it would work. = 如果我点击按钮，它就会工作。
I wish I had clicked the link earlier. = 我希望我早点点击链接。

被动语态:
The button was clicked accidentally. = 按钮被意外点击了。
The link is clicked frequently. = 这个链接经常被点击。

疑问句:
Did you click the button? = 你点击了按钮吗？
Has he clicked the link yet? = 他已经点击链接了吗？

否定句:
I didn't click the button. = 我没有点击按钮。
She hasn't clicked the link yet. = 她还没有点击链接。
