musculocutaneous:/ˌmʌsˌkjuːloʊkjuˈteɪniəs/| adj.|肌皮的|Pertaining to both muscle and skin; relating to or affecting both muscle and skin.  This term is typically used in anatomical contexts to describe nerves or structures that innervate both muscle and skin.|The musculocutaneous nerve supplies the biceps brachii, brachialis, and coracobrachialis muscles, as well as the skin of the lateral forearm.|近义词：musculcutaneous (alternative spelling)|反义词：N/A
