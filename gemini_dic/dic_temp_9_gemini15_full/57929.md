drink-driving: /ˈdrɪŋkˌdraɪvɪŋ/ | n. |酒后驾车| Driving a motor vehicle after consuming alcoholic beverages.  This is a serious offense in many countries, often resulting in significant penalties including fines, license suspension, or imprisonment.  The term highlights the dangerous combination of alcohol consumption and operating a vehicle. | Example: Drink-driving is illegal and incredibly dangerous. 酒后驾车是违法的，而且非常危险。  He was arrested for drink-driving after causing an accident. 他因酒后驾车引发事故而被捕。| 近义词：driving under the influence (DUI), driving while intoxicated (DWI) | 反义词：sober driving | 词性: 名词
