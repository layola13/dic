optimised:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzd/ /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzd/| adj. |优化的，最佳化的|  Improved in efficiency or performance.  The process of making something as functional and effective as possible. |例句：The software has been optimised for speed.  软件已经过速度优化。

例句：The algorithm has been optimised to minimize energy consumption. 算法已优化以最大限度地减少能耗。


近义词：improved, enhanced, refined, perfected

反义词：unoptimized, suboptimal, inefficient


词性：形容词

USAGE->

The system is optimised for maximum efficiency. = 系统经过优化以达到最大效率。
The code is optimised for readability. = 代码经过优化以提高可读性。


一般过去时:

词源:
The word "optimised" comes from the verb "optimise," which itself is derived from the adjective "optimal," ultimately tracing back to the Latin word "optimus," meaning "best."  The suffix "-ised"/-ized is a common English suffix indicating the process of making something.
