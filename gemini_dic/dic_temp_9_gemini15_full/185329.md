retrieval: /rɪˈtriːvəl/ | n. |检索；取回；恢复 | The act of getting something back again; the process of recovering something lost or stored.  |例句：The retrieval of the lost data took several hours.  丢失数据的检索花了几个小时。

例句：Data retrieval is a crucial function of any database system. 数据检索是任何数据库系统的重要功能。


近义词：recovery, restoration, recapture | 反义词：loss, destruction

词源:  From Old French *retrieval, from *retraire "to withdraw, retract," from Latin *re- "back" + *trahere "to pull, draw."  The modern sense developed from the meaning of "to bring back something lost or stolen."
