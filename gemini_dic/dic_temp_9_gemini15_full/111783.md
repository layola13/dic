loft:/lɒft/ /lɔːft/| n. v.|阁楼；高层；举起；抬高 |A loft is a room or space at the top of a building, often under a sloping roof.  It can also refer to a high-ceilinged area in a building, often used as a studio or apartment. As a verb, it means to raise or lift something to a higher position.|例句：The artist had a spacious loft in the city.  这位艺术家在城里有一间宽敞的阁楼。

The workers lofted the heavy beam into place. 工人们将沉重的梁抬到适当位置。|近义词：attic, studio, elevate, raise|反义词：basement, lower, depress
