humanising: /ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使人性化；使人道化 | To make something more humane or human-like; to imbue something with human qualities or characteristics.  | 例句：The company is trying to humanise its image by supporting local charities. 公司正试图通过支持当地慈善机构来提升其人性化的形象。

例句：The novel humanises the experience of refugees, showing their resilience and hope. 这部小说使难民的经历人性化，展现了他们的韧性和希望。


近义词：humanize, personalize, personalize, empathize with


反义词：dehumanize, depersonalize


词性:动词
