semidivision:/ˌsɛmiˈdɪvɪʒən/| n. |半分割，半划分|A semidivision is a partial division or separation of something into two or more parts.  It implies an incomplete or imperfect division, often leaving some connection or overlap between the divided parts.|例句：The company underwent a semidivision, creating two largely independent operating units but retaining some shared resources. 公司进行了半分割，创建了两个在很大程度上独立的运营部门，但保留了一些共享资源。|近义词：subdivision, partial division, segmentation|反义词：complete division, total separation|名词
