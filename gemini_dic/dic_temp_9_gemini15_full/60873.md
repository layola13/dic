elegit:/ˈɛlɪdʒɪt/ | n. | 选择权；（尤指）债权人的选择权；（古代英格兰）债权人强制执行判决的权利 | A writ of execution enabling a creditor to seize and sell the debtor's land.  It is a legal process used to recover a debt. | Example: The creditor obtained an elegit to seize the debtor's property.  债权人获得了选择权以没收债务人的财产。|近义词：writ of execution, judgment|反义词：N/A
