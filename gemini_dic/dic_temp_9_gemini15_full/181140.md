reinflates:/ˌriːɪnˈfleɪts/| v.|重新充气；使重新膨胀|To inflate again; to fill something with air or gas again.|例句：The mechanic reinflated the flat tire.  机械师给瘪了的轮胎重新充气了。|例句：The company reinflated its stock price after a period of decline. 公司在经历一段时间的下跌后重新抬高了股价。|近义词：inflate, refill|反义词：deflate
