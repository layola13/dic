disassociates: /ˌdɪs əˈsoʊʃieɪts/ | v. | 分离；使分离；脱离；断绝关系 | To separate or disconnect (something) from something else; to break a connection or association.  | 例句：The trauma caused him to disassociate himself from his family.  创伤导致他与家人疏远。

例句：The researcher disassociated the variables to determine their individual effects.  研究人员将变量分离，以确定它们各自的影响。


近义词：separate, detach, disconnect, disengage, sever


反义词：associate, connect, link, unite, join


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disassociates himself from the controversial project. 他与这个有争议的项目撇清关系。
The company disassociates its products from any unethical practices. 这家公司将它的产品与任何不道德行为区分开来。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

否定句:
