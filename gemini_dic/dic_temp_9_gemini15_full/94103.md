impeached:/ɪmˈpiːtʃt/| v.|弹劾，指控|To formally accuse a public official of wrongdoing.  This typically involves a formal process with specific procedures and often leads to a trial or investigation.|例句：The president was impeached for perjury and obstruction of justice.  总统因作伪证和妨碍司法而被弹劾。|例句：The governor was impeached, but acquitted. 州长被弹劾，但被无罪释放。|近义词：accuse, indict, charge|反义词：acquit, exonerate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The House impeaches officials who commit serious crimes. = 众议院弹劾犯有严重罪行的官员。
He rarely impeaches the integrity of others. = 他很少质疑别人的正直。


一般过去时:
The Senate impeached the judge last year. = 参议院去年弹劾了这位法官。
They impeached the mayor for corruption. = 他们因腐败而弹劾了市长。


现在完成时:
The committee has impeached several members. = 委员会已经弹劾了几名成员。
No president has ever been successfully removed from office after being impeached.  = 从未有总统在被弹劾后成功地被免职。


过去完成时:
The evidence showed that the official had already been impeached before the scandal broke. = 证据表明，丑闻爆发前该官员已被弹劾。
By the time the trial began, the governor had been impeached for months. = 到审判开始时，州长已被弹劾数月了。


将来时:
The opposition party plans to impeach the president next year. = 反对党计划明年弹劾总统。
They predict that the mayor will be impeached soon. = 他们预测市长很快就会被弹劾。


虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would have impeached him. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会弹劾他。
I wish the congress had impeached him earlier. = 我希望国会早点弹劾他。


被动语态:
The official was impeached by the House of Representatives. = 该官员被众议院弹劾。
He was impeached for misconduct. = 他因行为不端而被弹劾。


疑问句:
Has the president been impeached? = 总统是否已被弹劾？
Will they impeach the governor? = 他们会弹劾州长吗？


否定句:
The court did not impeach the judge. = 法院没有弹劾法官。
They were not impeached for their actions. = 他们没有因为他们的行为而被弹劾。
