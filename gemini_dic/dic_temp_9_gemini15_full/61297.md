embargo:/əmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ | n. v. |禁运；贸易禁运 | An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.  |The government imposed an embargo on all oil imports from the country. 政府对来自该国的石油进口实施了禁运。|sanction, blockade, prohibition |free trade, open market |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government imposes an embargo on arms sales. = 政府对军火销售实行禁运。
A trade embargo is in effect. = 贸易禁运正在生效。


一般过去时:
The US imposed an embargo on Cuban sugar. = 美国对古巴糖实行禁运。
They lifted the embargo last year. = 他们去年取消了禁运。


现在完成时:
The country has been under embargo for many years. = 这个国家多年来一直处于禁运状态。
They have just announced a new embargo. = 他们刚刚宣布了一项新的禁运。


过去完成时:
The trade had already been under embargo before the conflict began. = 在冲突开始之前，贸易就已经处于禁运状态了。
They had imposed an embargo on goods several months prior. = 几个月前他们就已经对商品实施了禁运。


将来时:
The UN will impose an embargo if the violence continues. = 如果暴力继续，联合国将实施禁运。
They will likely lift the embargo next year. = 他们很可能明年解除禁运。


虚拟语气:
If they imposed an embargo, the economy would suffer. = 如果他们实施禁运，经济将遭受损失。
I wish they would lift the embargo soon. = 我希望他们能尽快解除禁运。


被动语态:
The goods were embargoed by the authorities. = 商品被当局禁止出口。
An embargo was placed on the import of certain products. = 对某些产品的进口实行了禁运。


疑问句:
Is there an embargo on this product? = 这个产品有禁运吗？
When will the embargo be lifted? = 禁运什么时候解除？


否定句:
There is no embargo on food supplies. = 食品供应没有禁运。
They did not impose an embargo on that specific item. = 他们并没有对该特定商品实施禁运。


PHRASE->
trade embargo = 贸易禁运
oil embargo = 石油禁运
weapons embargo = 军火禁运
embargo on exports = 出口禁运
lift an embargo = 解除禁运
