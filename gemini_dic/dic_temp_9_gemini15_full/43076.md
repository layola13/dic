cowpox:/ˈkaʊpɒks/| n. |牛痘|A mild viral disease of cattle, characterized by skin pustules, and historically important as the source of the vaccine for smallpox.| 例句：Cowpox is a zoonotic disease, meaning it can be transmitted from animals to humans. 牛痘是一种人畜共患病，这意味着它可以从动物传播给人。  The discovery of cowpox led to the development of the smallpox vaccine. 牛痘的发现导致了天花疫苗的研制。|近义词：vaccinia (病毒)|反义词：smallpox (天花)|名词
