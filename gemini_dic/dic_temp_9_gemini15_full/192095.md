scouring: /ˈskaʊərɪŋ/ | n. | 擦洗；磨光；彻底搜寻 |  The act of rubbing a surface hard to clean it; a thorough search.  | 例句：The scouring of the pots and pans took a long time. = 擦洗锅碗瓢盆花了很长时间。

例句：The police conducted a thorough scouring of the area for clues. = 警方对该地区进行了彻底搜寻以寻找线索。

近义词：scrubbing, cleaning, searching, investigation

反义词：neglecting, ignoring


scouring: /ˈskaʊərɪŋ/ | v. | 擦洗；磨光；彻底搜寻 | To rub a surface hard to clean it; to search thoroughly. | 例句：She spent the afternoon scouring the kitchen. = 她下午都在擦洗厨房。

例句：He scoured the internet for information on the topic. = 他在网上搜索有关该主题的信息。

近义词：scrubbing, cleaning, searching, exploring

反义词：neglecting, ignoring

词性: v., n.
