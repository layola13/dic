generalizes:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪz/| v. |概括；归纳；推广；普遍化 |To make a general statement about something; to form a general opinion; to apply something more widely.|例句：The study generalizes the findings to the entire population. 这项研究将调查结果推广到整个人群。
例句：He tends to generalize about people, which is unfair. 他倾向于对人进行概括，这是不公平的。|近义词：summarize, abstract, extrapolate |反义词：specify, detail, particularize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He generalizes his experiences to others. = 他将他自己的经验推广到其他人身上。
The scientist generalizes the data to create a theory. = 科学家将数据归纳起来以建立一个理论。


一般过去时:
She generalized her research findings. = 她对她的研究结果进行了概括。
The teacher generalized the lesson to help students understand. = 老师对课程进行了概括以帮助学生理解。


现在完成时:
They have generalized the problem to a larger context. = 他们已经将这个问题推广到更大的背景下。
Researchers have generalized their findings to a wider population. = 研究人员已将他们的发现推广到更广泛的人群中。


过去完成时:
He had generalized the concept before presenting it. = 他在提出这个概念之前已经对其进行了概括。
Scientists had generalized the data before drawing conclusions. = 科学家在得出结论之前已经对数据进行了概括。


将来时:
She will generalize her findings in her presentation. = 她将在她的演示中概括她的发现。
The company will generalize the new product to other markets. = 公司将把新产品推广到其他市场。


虚拟语气:
If he generalized less, his arguments would be stronger. = 如果他不那么概括，他的论点就会更有力。
I wish he would generalize less and give more specific examples. = 我希望他少一些概括，多举一些具体的例子。


被动语态:
The results were generalized to the entire sample. = 结果被推广到整个样本。
The theory was generalized from a small study. = 该理论是从一项小型研究中概括出来的。


疑问句:
Does he often generalize about people? = 他经常对人进行概括吗？
Can we generalize these findings to other cultures? = 我们能否将这些发现推广到其他文化？


否定句:
Don't generalize from a single incident. = 不要从单一事件中进行概括。
The study didn't generalize its findings to the control group. = 这项研究没有将其研究结果推广到对照组。


PHRASE->
generalize about = 概括…
generalize from = 从…概括
generalize to = 推广到…
generalize the findings = 概括研究结果
generalize a theory = 概括出一个理论
