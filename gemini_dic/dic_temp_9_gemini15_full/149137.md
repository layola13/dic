oxidizable: /ˌɒksɪˈdaɪzəbl/ /ˌɑːksɪˈdaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可氧化的 | Capable of being oxidized; easily oxidized.  A substance that is oxidizable readily combines with oxygen or loses electrons. | The metal is highly oxidizable in moist air. 这金属在潮湿的空气中很容易氧化。  Iron is an oxidizable metal. 铁是一种易氧化的金属。 |  combustible, flammable |  non-oxidizable
