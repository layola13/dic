goofing:/ˈɡuːfɪŋ/| n.|傻事，胡闹，笨拙的错误|The act of making mistakes or acting foolishly; silly behavior or actions.|例句：His goofing around in class got him into trouble. 他在课堂上胡闹惹上了麻烦。|近义词：fooling around, messing around, clowning around|反义词：seriousness, diligence
