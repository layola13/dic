corymbed:/ˈkɒrɪmbd/| adj.|伞房花序的；伞房状的 |Having flowers arranged in a corymb.  A corymb is a flat-topped or slightly rounded inflorescence where the individual flower stalks arise from different points on the stem but reach approximately the same height, giving the appearance of a flat-topped cluster.|例句：The plant has corymbed white flowers. 这植物开着伞房状的白色花朵。 The artist depicted the flowers in a corymbed arrangement. 艺术家描绘了伞房状的花朵排列。|近义词：racemose, umbelled|反义词：solitary (referring to flower arrangement)
