entailed:/ɪnˈteɪld/ | vt. | 含有；需要；使成为必需；继承(财产) | To involve (something) as a necessary consequence; to require or necessitate; to inherit (property) |  The job entailed long hours and hard work. 这份工作需要长时间工作和努力。

The inheritance entailed a large debt. 这笔遗产需要偿还一大笔债务。| involve, necessitate, imply |  exclude, disregard | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract entails a non-compete clause. = 合同包含一个竞业禁止条款。
This project entails significant risks. = 这个项目包含重大风险。

一般过去时:
The treaty entailed significant concessions from both sides. = 这项条约需要双方做出重大让步。
His decision entailed considerable financial loss. = 他的决定导致了相当大的经济损失。

现在完成时:
The renovations have entailed a considerable expense. = 装修已经花费了相当大的费用。
My research has entailed countless hours of work. = 我的研究已经花费了无数个小时的工作。

过去完成时:
The investigation had entailed interviewing dozens of witnesses. = 调查需要采访数十名证人。
The project had entailed more work than we initially anticipated. = 该项目比我们最初预期的工作量更大。

将来时:
The new policy will entail changes to our existing procedures. = 新政策将需要改变我们现有的程序。
The expansion will entail hiring additional staff. = 扩张将需要雇佣更多员工。

虚拟语气:
If the plan entailed too much risk, we would abandon it. = 如果这个计划风险太大，我们会放弃它。
I wish the job didn't entail so much travel. = 我希望这份工作不需要这么多差旅。

被动语态:
Significant changes were entailed by the new legislation. = 新立法需要进行重大改变。
Many difficulties were entailed in the completion of the project. = 完成该项目需要克服许多困难。

疑问句:
What does this job entail? = 这份工作需要做什么？
Does this agreement entail any legal obligations? = 这份协议包含任何法律义务吗？

否定句:
The contract does not entail any hidden fees. = 合同不包含任何隐藏费用。
The plan did not entail any significant changes. = 该计划不需要任何重大改变。


PHRASE->
be entailed in = 包含于…中
entailed upon = 施加于…
entailed by = 由…引起
entailed with = 与…有关
entailed in the process = 在过程中涉及
