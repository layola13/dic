dagaa:/daˈɡaː/| n.|小鱼；鱼苗|Small fish, especially those used as bait or eaten whole.  The word often refers to small, silvery fish, frequently used in Japanese cuisine.|例句：The fishermen caught a bucket of dagaa for bait. 渔民们捕了一桶小鱼作鱼饵。|例句：We had dagaa-shioyaki (grilled small fish with salt) for dinner. 晚饭我们吃了盐烤小鱼。|近义词：small fish, fry, minnow|反义词：whale, shark
