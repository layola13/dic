rustling:/ˈrʌs(ə)lɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|沙沙作响；轻微的摩擦声|The sound of leaves rustling in the wind. 风中树叶沙沙作响的声音。|近义词：whispering, murmuring, swishing|反义词：silence, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaves rustle in the wind. = 树叶在风中沙沙作响。
The silk dress rustles as she moves. = 她一动，丝绸衣服就发出沙沙声。


一般过去时:
The branches rustled in the night breeze. = 枝叶在夜风中沙沙作响。
I heard the paper rustling behind me. = 我听到我身后纸张沙沙作响的声音。


现在完成时:
The wind has rustled the leaves all day. = 风整天都在吹动树叶发出沙沙声。
The cat has rustled through the bushes. = 猫已经穿过灌木丛，发出沙沙声。


过去完成时:
The leaves had rustled before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前，树叶就已经沙沙作响了。
The mice had rustled through the attic before we got there. = 我们到达阁楼之前，老鼠已经在里面沙沙作响了。


将来时:
The leaves will rustle when the wind picks up. = 风一起，树叶就会沙沙作响。
I expect the pages will rustle as you turn them. = 我想你翻页时会发出沙沙声。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, the leaves would rustle more. = 如果风再大一些，树叶就会沙沙作响得更厉害。
I wish the rustling of the leaves would stop; it's disturbing my sleep. = 我真希望树叶沙沙作响的声音能停止，它扰乱了我的睡眠。


被动语态:
The leaves were rustled by the wind. = 树叶被风吹得沙沙作响。
The papers were rustled by the mischievous child. = 纸张被顽皮的孩子弄得沙沙作响。


疑问句:
Do you hear the leaves rustling? = 你听到树叶沙沙作响的声音了吗？
Is that the sound of silk rustling? = 那是丝绸沙沙作响的声音吗？


否定句:
The leaves didn't rustle in the still air. = 在静止的空气中，树叶没有沙沙作响。
The fabric doesn't rustle when it's dry. = 布料干的时候不会发出沙沙声。


PHRASE->
rustling leaves = 沙沙作响的树叶

a rustling movement = 沙沙的动静

the rustling of paper = 纸张的沙沙声


词源:
The word "rustle" comes from the Middle English word "rustelen," which is of imitative origin – that is, it's a word that imitates the sound it describes.  The sound is onomatopoeic.
