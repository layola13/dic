exploitable: /ɪkˈsplɔɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可利用的；可剥削的 |  capable of being exploited; susceptible to exploitation.  This refers to something that can be used to one's advantage, often unfairly or unethically. It can also describe something that is vulnerable to attack or misuse. | 例句：The company's security system had several exploitable vulnerabilities. = 该公司的安全系统存在多个可利用的漏洞。

The software contains several exploitable bugs. = 该软件包含几个可利用的错误。


近义词：usable, vulnerable, susceptible,  abusable | 反义词：secure, invulnerable, protected
