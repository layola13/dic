/kæst/ | vt. | 投掷；抛；铸造 | To throw or fling.  | He casteth his bread upon the waters. 他把面包抛在水上。(This is an example from the King James Bible and uses an archaic form.) | fling, hurl, toss | catch, receive | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He casts a fishing rod. = 他抛出鱼竿。
She casts a spell. = 她施魔法。


一般过去时:
He cast the stone into the river. = 他把石头扔进河里。
They cast their votes. = 他们投票。


现在完成时:
He has cast his vote. = 他已经投票了。
She has cast aside her worries. = 她已经抛开了她的烦恼。


过去完成时:
He had cast the net before the sun rose. = 太阳升起前他已经撒网了。
They had cast their doubts aside. = 他们已经抛开了他们的疑虑。


将来时:
He will cast his line in the morning. = 他明天早上将会垂钓。
She will cast a shadow on the wall. = 她将会在墙上投下阴影。


虚拟语气:
If he cast his vote, the result would be different. = 如果他投票，结果就会不同。
I wish he had cast his vote. = 我希望他投了票。


被动语态:
The spell was cast by the witch. = 这个咒语是由女巫施的。
The net was cast into the sea. = 网被撒进了海里。


疑问句:
Did he cast his vote? = 他投票了吗？
When will they cast the movie? = 他们什么时候上映这部电影？


否定句:
He did not cast his vote. = 他没有投票。
She did not cast a shadow. = 她没有投下影子。



PHRASE->
cast a shadow = 投下阴影
cast doubt on = 对...表示怀疑
cast aside = 抛弃；丢弃
cast off = 脱掉；解缆
cast about = 寻找；搜索
