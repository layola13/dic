sexualization:/ˌsɛkʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |性化；赋予性意味|The process of making something sexual or associating it with sex, often in a way that is objectifying or degrading.  This can involve focusing on a person's physical attributes to the exclusion of other qualities, or presenting them in a way that emphasizes their sexuality over their other characteristics.  It can apply to individuals, objects, or concepts.|例句：The sexualization of women in advertising is a controversial topic.  女性在广告中的性化是一个有争议的话题。|例句：The video game's sexualization of its female characters drew criticism from many players.  这款电子游戏对女性角色的性化激起了许多玩家的批评。|近义词：objectification, eroticization|反义词：desexualization
