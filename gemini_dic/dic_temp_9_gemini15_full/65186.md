ethologist:/ɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n. |行为学家|A scientist who studies the behavior of animals in their natural environment. Ethologists observe and analyze animal behavior to understand its evolutionary and ecological significance. They often focus on innate behaviors, such as instincts and reflexes, as well as learned behaviors, such as social interactions and problem-solving. |例句：The ethologist spent months observing the chimpanzees' social structures in the wild.  行为学家花了几个月时间在野外观察黑猩猩的社会结构。

例句：Jane Goodall is a renowned ethologist known for her groundbreaking work with chimpanzees. 简·古道尔是一位著名的行为学家，以其在黑猩猩方面的开创性工作而闻名。

近义词：animal behaviorist, zoologist (in some contexts)


反义词：无


词性：名词
