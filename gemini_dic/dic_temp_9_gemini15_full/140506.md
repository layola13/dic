oldsters:/ˈoʊldstərz/| n.|老年人| Elderly people, especially those who are retired.  The term often carries a slightly informal or even humorous connotation. | Example: The oldsters gathered at the park for their daily game of chess. 老年人聚集在公园里进行他们每天的国际象棋比赛。|近义词：senior citizens, the elderly, aged people|反义词：youths, youngsters, children
