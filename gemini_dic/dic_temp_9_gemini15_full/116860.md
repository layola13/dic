maxims:/ˈmæksɪmz/| n.|格言；箴言；座右铭|A maxim is a short, memorable statement expressing a general truth or rule of conduct.  It often serves as a guiding principle or a piece of wisdom.  Maxims are typically concise and easily understood, making them effective tools for conveying important ideas.|Example: "Honesty is the best policy" is a well-known maxim.  诚实为上策是一句众所周知的格言。|近义词：proverb, saying, motto, adage|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be applied to the specific meaning of an individual maxim.|名词
