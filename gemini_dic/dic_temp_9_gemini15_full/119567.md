microtomist:/ˈmaɪkrəʊtəmɪst/| n. |显微镜切片技师| A person skilled in the art of preparing thin sections of tissue for microscopic examination using a microtome.  Microtomists are crucial in histology, pathology, and other fields requiring detailed microscopic analysis of biological samples. |The microtomist carefully prepared the tissue samples for the pathologist. 显微镜切片技师仔细地为病理学家准备了组织样本。|histotechnician, histologist|  (无明确反义词)|名词
