hurrahs:/həˈrɑːz/| n. |欢呼声；喝彩声 |A collective shout of hurrah; a series of cheers.  |例句：The crowd erupted in hurrahs as the winning team took the field.  人群在获胜队伍进入赛场时爆发出阵阵欢呼。
The speech was met with a chorus of hurrahs. 这番讲话赢得了雷鸣般的掌声。|近义词：cheers, shouts, applause |反义词：groans, boos
