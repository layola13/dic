gangster:/ˈɡæŋstər/| n. |歹徒，匪徒，黑帮分子|A gangster is a member of a gang of criminals.  Gangsters are often involved in organized crime, such as drug trafficking, extortion, and violence.|例句：The gangster was arrested for armed robbery. 这个歹徒因持械抢劫被捕。|例句：He was known to be a ruthless gangster. 他以冷酷无情的匪徒而闻名。|近义词：thug, criminal, mobster, racketeer|反义词：law-abiding citizen, upstanding citizen|名词
