crevasses:/krəˈvæs/| n. |冰川裂缝；裂隙|A crevasse is a deep crack, or fracture, found in an ice sheet or glacier.  These fissures can be extremely dangerous, often hidden by snow bridges. They are formed by the movement and stress within the ice mass.|Example: The climbers carefully avoided the crevasses in the glacier. 登山者小心翼翼地避开了冰川上的裂缝。 The expedition was delayed due to numerous crevasses blocking their path. 探险队由于许多裂缝阻塞了他们的道路而延误了。|近义词：fissure, crack, rift, chasm |反义词：solid surface, unbroken expanse
