slates:/sleɪts/| n.|石板；页岩；石板瓦；课程表；(尤指学校的)功课；|A slate is a type of rock that splits easily into thin, flat pieces.  These pieces can be used to make roofing tiles, flooring, or even writing surfaces.  In education, a slate can refer to a schedule or a list of assignments. |The roof was covered in dark grey slates. 屋顶覆盖着深灰色的石板。

The teacher wrote the day's assignments on the slate. 老师把当天的作业写在石板上。

|近义词：flagstone, tile, schedule|反义词：none|名词
