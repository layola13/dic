precontemplates:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛmpleɪts/| v.|预先考虑，预想|To think about or consider something beforehand.  It implies a more thorough and deliberate consideration than simply "thinking about" something.|例句：She precontemplated the challenges of the project before she began. 她开始项目之前就预先考虑了项目的挑战。  He precontemplated his speech for hours. 他预先构思了他的演讲好几个小时。|近义词：consider, contemplate, ponder, meditate|反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard
