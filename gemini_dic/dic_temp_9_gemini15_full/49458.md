deltoids:/ˈdeltɔɪdz/| n. |三角肌|The deltoids are the muscles that cover the shoulders. They are responsible for the abduction, flexion, and extension of the arm.|例句：He strained his deltoids during weightlifting. 他举重时拉伤了三角肌。|近义词：shoulder muscles|反义词：无

词源:
From Greek *δελτοειδής (deltoeidēs), from *δέλτα (delta) "triangle" + *εἶδος (eidos) "form, shape".  The muscle is so named because of its roughly triangular shape.
