enroller:/ɪnˈroʊlər/ | n. |注册者；招生员；登记员 | A person who enrolls others or themselves in a program, course, or organization.  |Example: The enroller explained the benefits of the program to potential participants.|近义词：registrar, recruiter, registrant |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The enroller registers new students every semester. = 招生员每学期都注册新生。
He enrolls himself in a yoga class. = 他自己报名参加瑜伽班。


一般过去时:
She enrolled her son in a summer camp last year. = 她去年把她儿子送进了夏令营。
The company enrolled many new employees. = 公司招募了许多新员工。


现在完成时:
They have enrolled over 100 participants in the marathon. = 他们已经为马拉松比赛招募了100多名参赛者。
I have enrolled myself in a language course. = 我已经报名参加了一个语言课程。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had enrolled all the needed students. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经招募了所有需要的学生。
She had already enrolled in the university before he applied. = 在他申请之前，她已经入学了。


将来时:
We will enroll more members next year. = 我们明年将招募更多会员。
He will enroll in a doctoral program next fall. = 他明年秋天将攻读博士学位。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enroll in a painting class. = 如果我有更多时间，我会报名参加绘画班。
I wish I had enrolled in that course earlier. = 我希望我早点报名参加那个课程。


被动语态:
Many students were enrolled in the new program. = 许多学生报名参加了新的项目。
He was enrolled in a prestigious university. = 他被一所著名大学录取了。


疑问句:
Did you enroll for the workshop? = 你报名参加研讨会了吗？
Has she enrolled in any online courses recently? = 她最近报了什么在线课程吗？


否定句:
He didn't enroll in the class because it was full. = 他没有报名参加课程，因为课程已经满了。
She didn't enroll her daughter in ballet classes. = 她没有让她女儿参加芭蕾舞课程。
