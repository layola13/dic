overconsumption: /ˌoʊvər kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. |过度消费；消费过剩 | The excessive use or consumption of something, beyond what is necessary or sustainable.  It often refers to the consumption of goods and resources at a rate that exceeds the capacity of the environment to replenish them.  This can lead to environmental degradation, resource depletion, and economic instability. |  例句：The overconsumption of fossil fuels is contributing to climate change.  过度消耗化石燃料正在导致气候变化。

例句：Overconsumption of fast fashion leads to textile waste and environmental pollution.  过度消费快时尚导致纺织品浪费和环境污染。


近义词：excess consumption, extravagance, wastefulness, profligacy

反义词：conservation, frugality, restraint, moderation
