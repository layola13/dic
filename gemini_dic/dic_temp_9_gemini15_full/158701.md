plotwise:/ˈplɑːtwaɪz/| adv.|情节方面，就情节而言|Concerning or in terms of the plot of a story or play.  |The movie was a disaster plotwise, but the acting was superb.这部电影情节方面很糟糕，但演技很棒。|narratively, story-wise|unplotwise|adv.


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses, voice, or number.  It only modifies verbs and clauses.

Example 1:
Plotwise, the novel was weak. = 就情节而言，这部小说很薄弱。

Example 2:
The film, plotwise, is quite predictable. = 这部电影，就情节而言，相当可预测。


PHRASE->
There are no common multi-word phrases using "plotwise".  It's primarily used as a standalone adverb.
