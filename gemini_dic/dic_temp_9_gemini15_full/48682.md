deferring:/dɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 推迟；延期；遵从 |  To put off to a later time; to postpone; to yield to the judgment or wishes of another. |  例句：They are deferring the decision until next week. = 他们将决定推迟到下周。 | 例句：He deferred to his father's wisdom. = 他遵从父亲的智慧。 | 近义词：postponing, delaying, suspending | 反义词：expediting, accelerating, hastening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defers the payment every month. = 他每个月都推迟付款。
We defer to her expertise in this matter. = 在这件事上，我们遵从她的专业意见。


一般过去时:
The meeting was deferred due to the storm. = 由于暴风雨，会议被推迟了。
She deferred to her supervisor's judgment. = 她遵从了她主管的判断。


现在完成时:
They have deferred the project several times. = 他们已经几次推迟了这个项目。
We have deferred to his wishes on many occasions. = 我们多次顺从了他的意愿。


过去完成时:
They had deferred the announcement until after the election. = 他们在选举之后才宣布。
She had deferred to her parents' opinion throughout her youth. = 她年轻时一直遵从父母的意见。


将来时:
They will defer the decision until more information is available. = 他们将会等到有更多信息后再做决定。
He will defer to the expert's assessment. = 他将遵从专家的评估。


虚拟语气:
If they deferred the payment, they would avoid a penalty. = 如果他们推迟付款，他们就可以避免处罚。
I wish they had deferred to my advice. = 我希望他们当时听了我的建议。


被动语态:
The decision was deferred by the committee. = 委员会推迟了决定。
His opinion was deferred to by his colleagues. = 他的意见受到同事的重视。


疑问句:
Will they defer the deadline? = 他们会推迟最后期限吗？
Do you defer to his judgment on this matter? = 在这件事上你遵从他的判断吗？


否定句:
They did not defer the project. = 他们没有推迟项目。
She did not defer to his unreasonable demands. = 她没有屈服于他无理的要求。


PHRASE->
defer payment = 推迟付款
defer to someone = 遵从某人
defer judgment = 推迟判决
defer an action = 推迟行动
defer consideration = 推迟考虑
