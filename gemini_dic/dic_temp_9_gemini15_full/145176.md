overattentive: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 过分关心的；过于殷勤的 | Excessively attentive; showing too much attention or care.  |  He was overattentive to his girlfriend, calling her constantly and showering her with gifts. 他对女朋友过于殷勤，不停地打电话给她，还送她很多礼物。|近义词：solicitous, fussy, meddlesome | 反义词：neglectful, indifferent, unconcerned
