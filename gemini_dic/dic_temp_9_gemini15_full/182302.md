remunerator:/rɪˈmjuːnəreɪtər/| n. |报酬者；酬金支付者 |One who remunerates; one who pays compensation or reward.|The company is a good remunerator, offering competitive salaries and benefits. 这家公司是一家很好的报酬者，提供有竞争力的薪水和福利。|近义词：compensator, payer, rewarder |反义词：exploiter, defrauder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company remunerates its employees fairly. = 公司公平地报酬其员工。
He regularly remunerates his freelance writers. = 他定期支付他的自由撰稿人的报酬。


一般过去时:
The company remunerated its employees for their overtime. = 公司支付了员工加班的报酬。
She remunerated the gardener for his services. = 她付钱给园丁以报答他的服务。


现在完成时:
The company has remunerated all its employees for their hard work. = 公司已经报答了所有员工的辛勤工作。
They have remunerated the consultant for his expertise. = 他们已经付钱给顾问以报答他的专业知识。


过去完成时:
The company had remunerated the contractors before the project was completed. = 项目完成前，公司已经支付了承包商的报酬。
She had remunerated the artist for his painting before the exhibition. = 展览前，她已经付钱给艺术家购买了他的画。


将来时:
The company will remunerate the employees for their extra hours next week. = 公司下周将支付员工额外工时的报酬。
He will remunerate the translator once the work is finished. = 他将在工作完成后支付翻译的报酬。


虚拟语气:
If the company remunerated its employees better, they would be more motivated. = 如果公司更好地报酬员工，他们就会更有动力。
I wish the government would remunerate teachers fairly. = 我希望政府能公平地报酬教师。


被动语态:
The employees were remunerated for their hard work. = 员工因辛勤工作而得到报酬。
The contractors will be remunerated upon completion of the project. = 项目完成后将向承包商支付报酬。


疑问句:
How does the company remunerate its employees? = 公司如何报酬其员工？
Will they be adequately remunerated for this difficult task? = 他们这项困难的任务会得到足够的报酬吗？


否定句:
The company did not remunerate the employees for their extra work. = 公司没有支付员工额外工作的报酬。
He has not yet been remunerated for his services. = 他尚未收到他的服务报酬。
