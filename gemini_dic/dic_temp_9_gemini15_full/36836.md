This term often refers to the suitability of a place for colonization, considering factors like climate, resources, and the existing population's resistance.  It can also imply the vulnerability of a population or area to colonization. |  The high colonizabilities of the Caribbean islands made them attractive to European powers.  = 加勒比海岛屿极高的可殖民性使它们对欧洲列强具有吸引力。 The study assessed the colonizabilities of different microbial species in various environments. = 这项研究评估了不同微生物物种在各种环境中的可殖民性。 |  vulnerability, susceptibility |  resistance, immunity, independence
