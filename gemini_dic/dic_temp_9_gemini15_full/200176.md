skimpy:/ˈskɪmpi/| adj.| 少量的；不足的；暴露的；吝啬的 |  Meaning:  giving or providing only a small amount; barely sufficient; (of clothing) revealing too much of the body. | Example: The meal portions were skimpy.  = 饭菜分量很少。

The skirt was skimpy and inappropriate for the office. = 这条裙子很短，不适合在办公室穿。

|近义词: meager, scanty, inadequate, insufficient, paltry, revealing |反义词: ample, abundant, generous, plentiful, substantial, modest
