It is a form of rigorous questioning aimed at clarifying concepts and exposing inconsistencies in thinking. | Example: Socrates employed the elenchus to challenge the unexamined beliefs of his interlocutors.  苏格拉底运用反驳法来挑战其对话者的未经检验的信念。|近义词：cross-examination, interrogation, refutation |反义词：confirmation, affirmation |名词
