osteodystrophic:/ˌɒstiːoʊdaɪˈstrɒfɪk/| adj. |骨营养不良的，骨发育不良的 | Relating to or characterized by osteodystrophy.  The term describes a condition affecting bone development and structure, often involving abnormalities in bone growth, density, and mineralization. This can manifest in various ways depending on the underlying cause. | Example: The patient presented with symptoms consistent with osteodystrophic changes in the long bones.  这位病人表现出与长骨骨营养不良变化一致的症状。|近义词：skeletal dystrophy, bone dysplasia |反义词：bone health, normal bone development
