martinet:/ˌmɑːrtɪˈnet/| n. |严厉的纪律执行者；严苛的人|A martinet is a person who demands strict discipline and obedience from others.  They are often inflexible and unforgiving.  They enforce rules rigidly, regardless of circumstances. |Example: The sergeant was a martinet, demanding absolute obedience from his troops.  这位军士是个严厉的纪律执行者，要求他的士兵绝对服从。

Example:  He was known as a martinet in the workplace, leaving no room for error or flexibility. 他在工作中以严厉著称，不容许任何错误或变通。

近义词：tyrant, disciplinarian, authoritarian, stickler

反义词：lenient, permissive, tolerant, lax

词性:名词
