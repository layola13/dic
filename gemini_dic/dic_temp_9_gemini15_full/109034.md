legacies:/ˈlɛɡəsiːz/| n.|遗产；遗留物；传统；|The effects of past actions or events, often something that is passed down from one generation to the next.  It can refer to material possessions, cultural traditions, or even intangible things like reputations.|例句：His legacy is one of great accomplishment and service to the country. 他的遗产是巨大的成就和对国家的贡献。|例句：The company's legacy of innovation continues to this day.  这家公司的创新传统至今仍在延续。|近义词：inheritance, heritage, bequest, tradition|反义词：nothingness, oblivion
