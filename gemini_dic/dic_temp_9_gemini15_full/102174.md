it's:/ɪts/| pron. conj.|这是，它是，那是|It's a contraction of "it is" or "it has". It functions as a pronoun and sometimes as a conjunction, depending on the context.  It replaces a previously mentioned noun or idea. |例句：It's a beautiful day.  (It is a beautiful day.) 今天天气真好。|例句：It's been raining all day. (It has been raining all day.)  今天一直在下雨。|近义词：it is, it has|反义词: N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It's sunny today. = 今天阳光明媚。
It's a long way to go. = 这路程很远。


一般过去时:
It's been a long day. = 今天很漫长。
It was a great party. = 那是一个很棒的派对。


现在完成时:
It's been a pleasure meeting you. = 很高兴认识你。
It's rained a lot this week. = 这周雨下得很多。


过去完成时:
It had already started when I arrived. = 我到的时候它已经开始了。
It had been snowing for hours before the sun came out. = 太阳出来之前已经下了好几个小时的雪了。


将来时:
It's going to be a hot summer. = 将会是一个炎热的夏天。
It will be finished soon. = 它很快就会完成了。


虚拟语气:
It's important that you be on time. = 你准时很重要。
It's time we left. = 我们该走了。


被动语态:
It's said that he's very rich. = 据说他非常富有。
It was decided that the meeting would be postponed. = 决定会议将延期举行。


疑问句:

Is it far from here? = 离这儿远吗？（it's 的疑问句形式）


否定句:
It's not fair. = 这不公平。
It isn't finished yet. = 它还没完成。
