let-outs:/ˈlɛtˌaʊts/| n.|豁免；逃避责任的借口；(合同中的)免责条款|A let-out is something that allows you to avoid a responsibility or obligation, or a clause in a contract that releases one party from liability.|例句：He used his illness as a let-out to avoid the work. 他以生病为借口逃避工作。  The contract contained a let-out clause in case of unforeseen circumstances. 合同中包含了不可预见情况下的免责条款。|近义词：excuse, loophole, escape clause, exemption|反义词：obligation, responsibility, liability|名词
