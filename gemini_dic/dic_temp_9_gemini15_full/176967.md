reallotting:/ˌriːˈælɒtɪŋ/| v.|重新分配，再分配 |The act of allocating something again; distributing or assigning something anew.|例句：The company is reallotting resources to its new project. 公司正在将其资源重新分配给新项目。 The government is reallotting funds to address the housing crisis. 政府正在重新分配资金以解决住房危机。|近义词：reassigning,redistributing|反义词：concentrating, centralizing
