centrifuging: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒɪŋ/ | v. |离心；离心分离 | The process of separating substances of different densities by rotating them at high speed in a centrifuge.  Centrifuging is commonly used in laboratories to separate blood components, purify chemicals, and analyze samples. |例句：The technician is centrifuging the blood sample to separate the plasma from the red blood cells.  技师正在对血液样本进行离心分离，以将血浆与红细胞分离。

The lab uses centrifuging to purify the protein solution. 实验室用离心法纯化蛋白质溶液。


近义词：spinning, whirling, rotating | 反义词：无
