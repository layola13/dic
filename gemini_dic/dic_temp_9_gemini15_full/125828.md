murr:/mɜːr/| n.|咕噜声，轻微的隆隆声|a low, rumbling sound, like that of a cat purring or distant thunder.  |例句：The cat sat on my lap, emitting a soft murr.  一只猫坐在我的腿上，发出轻柔的咕噜声。|例句：A faint murr came from the engine. 发动机发出轻微的隆隆声。|近义词：purr, rumble, growl (in some contexts)|反义词：无|名词
