cinematograph:/ˌsɪnəˈmætəɡræf/ | n. |电影摄影机；电影放映机 | A device for photographing and projecting moving pictures.  It's a historical term, now largely replaced by "movie camera" and "projector". | Example: The early cinematograph was a marvel of engineering. 早期的电影摄影机是工程学的奇迹。 |近义词：movie camera, projector | 反义词：None.
