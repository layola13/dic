nomenclatures:/ˈnɒmənˌkleɪtʃərz/ | n. |命名法；术语；命名系统 |A system of names or terms, or the rules for forming these.  It often refers to a specialized vocabulary used in a particular field or area of study. | Example: The nomenclatures of botany and zoology are complex and require careful study. 植物学和动物学的命名法很复杂，需要仔细研究。 The chemical nomenclatures are used to identify substances. 化学命名法用于识别物质。|近义词：terminology, terminology system, system of naming|反义词：无
