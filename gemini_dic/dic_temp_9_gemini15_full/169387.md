pronephric:/proʊˈnɛfrɪk/| adj.| 前肾的；原肾的 | Relating to the pronephros, the most anterior part of the embryonic kidney.  |Example: The pronephric duct is an important structure in the development of the urinary system. 前肾管是泌尿系统发育中的重要结构。|近义词：protorenal (less common)|反义词：metanephric, mesonephric
