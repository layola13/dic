pro-Baptists:/ˌproʊˈbæptɪsts/| adj. |准浸信会教徒的；支持浸信会的|Referring to individuals or groups sympathetic to or supportive of Baptist principles and practices, but not necessarily formally affiliated with a Baptist church.  They may share some Baptist beliefs but not all, or may be in the process of joining a Baptist church.|例句：Many pro-Baptists attended the interfaith dialogue, expressing their admiration for Baptist social justice initiatives. 许多准浸信会教徒参加了宗教间对话，表达了他们对浸信会社会正义倡议的钦佩。|近义词：Baptist sympathizer, Baptist supporter|反义词：Anti-Baptist
