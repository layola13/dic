quagmire:/ˈkwæɡmaɪər/| n. |沼泽；困境；泥潭 |A quagmire is a soft, wet area of ground that is difficult to walk across.  It can also refer to a difficult or complicated situation. |例句：The negotiations have become a quagmire. 谈判陷入了僵局。
The troops got bogged down in a quagmire. 部队陷入了泥潭。|近义词：mire, bog, morass, predicament |反义词：easy situation, clear path

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is a quagmire. = 这个项目是一个泥潭。
This situation is a quagmire. = 这种情况是一个困境。


一般过去时:
The deal became a quagmire. = 这笔交易变成了一场困境。
The car got stuck in a quagmire. = 车子陷进了泥潭。


现在完成时:
We have been mired in a quagmire of problems. = 我们一直陷入了一堆问题的泥潭中。
The investigation has become a quagmire. = 调查已经变成了一场泥潭。


过去完成时:
The company had already sunk into a quagmire of debt before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经陷入债务的泥潭。
They had found themselves in a political quagmire. = 他们发现自己陷入了政治的泥潭。


将来时:
This decision will lead to a quagmire. = 这个决定将导致一个困境。
The negotiations will likely become a quagmire. = 谈判很可能会陷入僵局。


虚拟语气:
If we had planned better, we wouldn't be in this quagmire. = 如果我们计划得更好，我们就不会陷入这个困境了。
If he had not interfered, the situation would not have become a quagmire. = 如果他没有干涉，情况就不会变成一个泥潭了。


被动语态:
The team was bogged down in a quagmire of bureaucratic red tape. = 该团队被官僚主义的繁文缛节所拖累，陷入困境。
The project was turned into a quagmire by unforeseen circumstances. =  由于不可预见的情况，该项目变成了一个困境。


疑问句:
Is this project turning into a quagmire? = 这个项目是不是正在变成一个泥潭？
Has the situation become a quagmire? = 情况是否已经变成了一个困境？


否定句:
The situation is not a quagmire yet. =  情况还不是一个困境。
The path was not a quagmire, despite the rain. =  尽管下雨了，这条路也不是泥潭。


PHRASE->
陷入泥潭: get stuck in a quagmire
政治泥潭: political quagmire
困境：a quagmire of problems
复杂的局面: a bureaucratic quagmire
摆脱困境: extricate oneself from a quagmire


词源:
The word "quagmire" comes from the Middle English word "kwagmyre," which is a combination of "kwaggen" (to shake or sink) and "mire" (muddy ground).  The ultimate origins trace back to Proto-Germanic roots.
