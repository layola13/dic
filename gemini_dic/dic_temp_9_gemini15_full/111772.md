lodicule:/ˈlɒdɪkjuːl/| n.|小鳞片，(禾本科植物)小舌|A small scale-like appendage found at the base of the lemma in some grasses.  Lodicules are thought to play a role in the opening of the floret during anthesis (flowering).|The lodicules of this grass species are particularly large and prominent.|近义词：palea (but not a perfect synonym, as palea refers to a different structure)|反义词：无
