miltonic:/mɪlˈtɒnɪk/| adj.|弥尔顿式的；具有弥尔顿风格的 | Relating to or characteristic of John Milton, his works, or his style, especially characterized by grandeur, sublimity, and a complex, often intricate, structure.  |例句：The poem's Miltonic grandeur and soaring imagery left the audience breathless. 这首诗歌弥尔顿式的宏伟和高耸的意象让观众屏住了呼吸。
例句：His Miltonic prose, though challenging, revealed a depth of understanding rarely found.  他那弥尔顿式的散文，虽然具有挑战性，但却展现出一种罕见的深刻理解。|近义词：grandiose, sublime, elevated, majestic |反义词：simple, plain, unadorned, prosaic
