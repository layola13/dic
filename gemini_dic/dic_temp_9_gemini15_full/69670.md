fended:/fɛndɪd/ | v. | 过去式和过去分词 | 防御；保护；抵挡；为…辩护 |  The warrior fended off the attacker with his shield.  战士用盾牌挡开了攻击者。|  近义词：defended, protected, shielded, ward off | 反义词：attacked, unprotected, exposed


The word "fended" is the past tense and past participle of the verb "fend".  There is no present tense form "fended" as it's already a past tense verb.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fends for himself. = 他自己谋生。
She fends off criticism well. = 她很好地抵挡了批评。


一般过去时:
They fended off the wolves. = 他们击退了狼群。
He fended for his family. = 他养家糊口。


现在完成时:
She has fended for herself since she was 16. = 自从16岁以来，她一直靠自己生活。
They have fended off several attacks. = 他们已经抵挡了几次攻击。


过去完成时:
He had fended off numerous challenges before achieving success. = 在取得成功之前，他已经克服了许多挑战。
She had fended for herself for years before finding a stable job. = 在找到稳定的工作之前，她独自生活了很多年。


将来时:
They will fend off any attempts to take over the company.  = 他们将击退任何企图接管公司的企图。
He will fend for himself in the big city. = 他将在繁华的大都市里独立生活。


虚拟语气:
If he had fended off the attack, he might still be alive. = 如果他能挡住攻击，他可能还活着。
If she had fended for herself better, she wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果她能更好地照顾自己，她就不会陷入这种境地。


被动语态:
The attack was fended off by the brave soldiers. = 勇敢的士兵们击退了袭击。
The family was well fended for by the generous grandfather. = 慷慨的祖父很好地照顾了这个家庭。


疑问句:
Did they fend off the invaders successfully? = 他们成功地击退了侵略者吗？
How long has she been fending for herself? = 她自己生活多久了？


否定句:
He didn't fend off the criticism effectively. = 他没有有效地反驳批评。
They couldn't fend for themselves in the harsh environment. = 他们在恶劣的环境中无法生存。


PHRASE->
fend for oneself = 自力更生
fend off = 击退；避开
fend for = 努力维持生计
fend a blow = 挡开一击
fend against = 抵挡；反抗
