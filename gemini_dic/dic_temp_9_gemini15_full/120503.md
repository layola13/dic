minuses:/ˈmaɪnəsɪz/ | n. |缺点，不足之处；减号 |The minuses of this plan outweigh the pluses.  这个计划的缺点超过了优点。|近义词：drawbacks, disadvantages, shortcomings |反义词：advantages, pluses |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project has several minuses. = 这个项目有几个缺点。
There are many minuses to this approach. = 这种方法有很多缺点。


一般过去时:
The minuses of the old system were numerous. = 旧系统的缺点很多。
We discussed the minuses of the proposal. = 我们讨论了该提案的缺点。


现在完成时:
We have identified several minuses in the design. = 我们已经确定了设计中的几个缺点。
They have weighed the pluses and minuses carefully. = 他们仔细权衡了利弊。


过去完成时:
The minuses had already been pointed out before the meeting. = 会议之前就已经指出了缺点。
By then, we had realized the minuses of our decision. = 到那时，我们已经意识到了我们决定的缺点。


将来时:
The report will highlight the minuses of the current policy. = 该报告将突出当前政策的缺点。
We will discuss the potential minuses of the new strategy. = 我们将讨论新战略的潜在缺点。


虚拟语气:
If there were fewer minuses, we would adopt the plan. = 如果缺点少一些，我们会采纳这个计划。
I wish there were no minuses to this otherwise perfect solution. = 我希望这个本来完美的解决方案没有任何缺点。


被动语态:
The minuses of the plan were clearly explained. = 该计划的缺点得到了清晰的解释。
The minuses were carefully considered before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前，仔细考虑了缺点。


疑问句:
What are the main minuses of this product? = 这个产品的缺点是什么？
Are there any serious minuses to this approach? = 这种方法有什么严重的缺点吗？


否定句:
There are no significant minuses to this method. = 这种方法没有明显的缺点。
The report didn't mention any minuses of the project. = 报告没有提到该项目的任何缺点。


PHRASE->
the minuses of = ...的缺点
weigh the pluses and minuses = 权衡利弊

address the minuses = 解决缺点
