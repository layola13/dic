footballs:/ˈfʊtbɔːlz/| n.|足球（复数）|The plural form of "football," referring to multiple spherical balls used in the sport of football.  It can refer to American footballs, soccer balls, or other types of footballs depending on context.|例句：The children kicked the footballs around in the park.孩子们在公园里踢足球。

例句：He collected footballs from different countries. 他收集了不同国家的足球。


近义词：soccer balls (if referring to soccer), gridirons (if referring to American footballs, though this refers to the field, not the ball)


反义词：无


词性:名词
