skewbalds:/ˈskjuːbɔːldz/| n.|  杂色马；花斑马 | A skewbald is a horse with a white coat and patches of another color, usually a dark color.  The patches are irregular in shape and distribution.  It is distinguished from a piebald, which has black and white patches. | Example: The skewbald horse galloped across the field, its patches of white and brown a striking contrast against the green grass. | 近义词：piebald,  roan,  dapple | 反义词：uniformly colored
