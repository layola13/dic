repanel:/ˌriːˈpænəl/| v.|重新安装面板；更换面板 | To replace or reinstall panels, especially in a vehicle or aircraft.  |例句：The mechanic had to repanel the car door after the accident.  = 事故后，技工不得不重新安装车门的面板。


例句：The old airplane needed extensive repaneling before it could fly again. = 那架旧飞机在再次飞行前需要大规模更换面板。


近义词：refit, repair, replace


反义词：remove panels, strip
