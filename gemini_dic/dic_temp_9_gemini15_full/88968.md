holidaying: /ˈhɒlədeɪɪŋ/ | /ˈhɑːlədeɪɪŋ/ | v. | 度假；休假 | The act of spending a holiday; taking a vacation.  |  例句：We're holidaying in the Caribbean this year. 今年我们在加勒比海度假。
例句：They are holidaying in Spain for two weeks. 他们在西班牙度假两周。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are holidaying in France. = 他们在法国度假。
We are holidaying in the mountains next week. = 我们下周要去山里度假。


一般过去时:
We holidayed in Italy last summer. = 我们去年夏天在意大利度假。
They holidayed in Greece for a month. = 他们在希腊度假一个月。


现在完成时:
They have holidayed in many different countries. = 他们去过许多不同的国家度假。
We haven't holidayed abroad this year. = 我们今年还没有出国度假。


过去完成时:
They had holidayed in Hawaii before they visited Japan. = 他们在去日本之前就去过夏威夷度假了。
We had holidayed in Mexico twice before our last trip. = 在我们上次旅行之前，我们已经去过墨西哥两次度假了。


将来时:
We will be holidaying in Bali next month. = 我们下个月将在巴厘岛度假。
They will holiday in Scotland next summer. = 他们明年夏天将在苏格兰度假。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would be holidaying in the Maldives. = 如果我们有钱一点，我们就会去马尔代夫度假。
I wish I were holidaying on a beach right now. = 我真希望我现在正在海滩上度假。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)  The passive voice is not naturally used with this verb because it describes an active undertaking.


疑问句:
Are you holidaying anywhere this year? = 你今年去哪里度假？
Where were they holidaying last Christmas? = 去年圣诞节他们在哪里度假？


否定句:
We aren't holidaying this year due to financial constraints. = 由于经济原因，我们今年不去度假了。
They didn't holiday anywhere last year. = 他们去年没有去任何地方度假。


PHRASE->
go holidaying = 去度假
be holidaying = 正在度假
holidaying abroad = 出国度假
holidaying with family = 和家人一起度假
holidaying in the sun = 在阳光下度假

近义词：vacationing, taking a vacation, on holiday


反义词：working, toiling (These are antonyms in the sense of being the opposite activity)

词性：动词
