realization's:/ˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |领悟；实现；认识；了解 |The understanding or becoming aware of something.  It often refers to a sudden understanding or insight.  The possessive 's' indicates possession or belonging to something else.  For example, "the realization's impact" means the impact of the realization.|

例句：
The realization's impact on him was profound.  =  他对这一领悟的影响深远。

His realization's clarity surprised even himself. = 他领悟的清晰程度甚至让他自己都感到惊讶。


近义词：understanding, comprehension, awareness, perception, insight

反义词：ignorance, unawareness, misunderstanding


词性：名词(复数)
