hewing:/ˈhjuːɪŋ/ | vt. | 砍伐；劈；凿；塑造 | To cut or shape (something) with an axe, chisel, or other tool.  | 例句：The sculptor was hewing a statue from a block of marble. = 雕刻家正在从一块大理石中凿出一尊雕像。

The farmer was hewing wood for the winter. = 农民正在砍柴过冬。


近义词：chopping, cutting, carving, shaping | 反义词：building, constructing, creating (in the sense of building up rather than carving away)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hews wood every morning. = 他每天早上都砍柴。
She hews a path through the jungle. = 她砍出一条路穿过丛林。


一般过去时:
They hewed the stone into shape. = 他们把石头砍成形状。
The pioneers hewed a farm from the wilderness. = 拓荒者们在荒野中开垦了一个农场。


现在完成时:
The artist has hewed many sculptures from wood. = 这位艺术家已经用木头雕刻了许多雕塑。
They have hewed a new path through the mountains. = 他们已经开辟了一条通往山区的新路。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, they had hewed all the firewood they needed. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经砍好了所需的所有柴火。
The workers had hewed the timbers before the construction began. = 工人们在施工开始前已经砍好了木材。


将来时:
We will hew a new course for the company. = 我们将为公司开辟一条新道路。
They will hew the stone for the new building. = 他们将为新建筑砍凿石头。


虚拟语气:
If I had a chisel, I would hew the stone myself. = 如果我有一把凿子，我就会自己凿石头。
If he had more time, he would hew the wood more carefully. = 如果他有更多的时间，他会更仔细地砍柴。


被动语态:
The wood was hewn by hand. = 木头是手工砍的。
The statue was hewn from a single piece of marble. = 这尊雕像是用整块大理石雕刻而成的。


疑问句:
Is he hewing the wood properly? = 他砍柴的方法对吗？
Were they hewing the stone with a chisel? = 他们是用凿子砍石头吗？


否定句:
They didn't hew the wood carefully enough. = 他们砍柴不够仔细。
He didn't hew the path correctly, so we got lost. = 他没有正确地开辟道路，所以我们迷路了。


PHRASE->
hewing to the line: 严格遵守规则
hewing a path: 开辟道路

词源:
源自古英语 *hēawan，意为“砍，劈”。与hew有相同的词根。
