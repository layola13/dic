extralogical:/ˌɛkstrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 超逻辑的，非逻辑的 | Beyond the scope or principles of logic; not conforming to the rules of logic.  It refers to something that is outside or independent of formal logical systems.  | Example:  His argument, while persuasive, was ultimately extralogical, relying on emotional appeals rather than reason.  他的论点虽然有说服力，但最终是不合逻辑的，依靠的是情感诉求而不是理性。 | 近义词: illogical, irrational, unreasonable, nonsensical | 反义词: logical, rational, reasonable
