lobs:/lɒbz/| /lɑːbz/ | vt. & n. | 投掷；猛击；用力投掷；大块；球；重击 | To throw something, especially in a casual or careless way.  Also refers to a heavy blow or a large lump. | 例句：He lobbed the ball over the net. 他把球高高地抛过网。

He lobbed a question at the speaker. 他向演讲者提出了一个问题。


近义词：toss, hurl, fling, heave, pitch | 反义词：catch, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lobs the ball gently. = 他轻轻地把球抛过去。
She lobs the parcel onto the table. = 她把包裹扔到桌子上。


一般过去时:
He lobbed the grenade into the trench. = 他把榴弹扔进了战壕。
She lobbed a heavy stone at the dog. = 她朝狗扔了一块大石头。


现在完成时:
He has lobbed several questions at the witness. = 他已经向证人抛出了几个问题。
She has lobbed the ball across the field. = 她已经把球扔过了球场。


过去完成时:
They had lobbed the anchor before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经抛锚了。
He had lobbed the letter into the mailbox. = 他已经把信投入邮箱。


将来时:
He will lob the ball to his teammate. = 他将把球传给他的队友。
She will lob a complaint to the management. = 她将向管理层投诉。


虚拟语气:
If he lobbed the ball harder, he might have scored. = 如果他把球扔得更用力，他可能就进球了。
I wish I had lobbed that opportunity. = 我真希望我抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:
The ball was lobbed high into the air. = 球被高高地抛向空中。
The complaint was lobbed at the wrong department. = 投诉递交给了错误的部门。


疑问句:
Did he lob the ball successfully? = 他成功地把球抛了吗？
Will she lob another question? = 她还会再问一个问题吗？


否定句:
He didn't lob the ball far enough. = 他没有把球扔得足够远。
She didn't lob the package carefully. = 她没有小心地投递包裹。


PHRASE->
