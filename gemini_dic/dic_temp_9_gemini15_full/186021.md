rewild:/ˌriːˈwaɪld/| vt. |让…恢复野性；使…重返野外；让…回归自然|To restore (an area of land) to its natural uncultivated state, especially by introducing native species of plants and animals.|例句：The organization is working to rewild large areas of the countryside.  该组织正在努力使大片乡村地区恢复自然状态。
例句：They plan to rewild the abandoned farmland by planting native trees and shrubs.他们计划通过种植本土树木和灌木来使废弃农田恢复野性。|近义词：restore, regenerate, reclaim|反义词：domesticate, cultivate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They rewild abandoned land. = 他们使废弃的土地恢复野性。
We rewild the forest. = 我们让森林回归自然。

一般过去时:
The government rewilded the national park. = 政府使国家公园恢复了野性。
They rewilded the river by removing dams. = 他们通过拆除水坝使河流恢复了自然状态。


现在完成时:
They have rewilded a significant portion of the land. = 他们已经使很大一部分土地恢复了野性。
Scientists have rewilded several areas successfully. = 科学家们已经成功地使几个地区恢复了野性。


过去完成时:
The area had been rewilded before the new development. = 在新的开发项目之前，该地区已经恢复了野性。
They had rewilded the habitat before releasing the animals. = 他们在放生动物之前已经使栖息地恢复了野性。


将来时:
We will rewild the coastal areas. = 我们将使沿海地区恢复野性。
The project will rewild the mountains. = 该项目将使山区恢复自然状态。


虚拟语气:
If we rewilded the river, the fish population would increase. = 如果我们使河流恢复自然状态，鱼类数量将会增加。
I wish we could rewild more of the countryside. = 我希望我们能够使更多乡村地区恢复自然状态。


被动语态:
The land is being rewilded by the conservationists. = 这片土地正在被环保主义者们恢复野性。
The forest was rewilded after the fire. = 大火之后，森林恢复了野性。


疑问句:

否定句:

We cannot rewild the entire region. = 我们无法使整个区域恢复自然状态。
