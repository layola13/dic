redemocratizes:/ˌriːdɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使重新民主化 | To restore democratic principles and processes to (a country or society).  The verb implies a process of reversing an undemocratic regime or system, often involving significant political and social change. | Example: The country is slowly redemocratizing after decades of authoritarian rule. = 这个国家在几十年的独裁统治后正在缓慢地重新民主化。  The movement aims to redemocratize the electoral system. =  这场运动旨在使选举制度重新民主化。| 近义词：democratize, liberalize | 反义词：authoritarianize, totalitarianism
