predischarge: /ˌpriːˈdɪstʃɑːrdʒ/ | adj. | 预出院的；出院前的 | Relating to the period before a patient is discharged from a hospital or other medical facility.  It often refers to planning, preparation, and assessment activities that occur before discharge. | Example:  The predischarge planning meeting included discussion of medication management and follow-up appointments.  预出院计划会议包括讨论药物管理和后续预约。


The patient received predischarge instructions on wound care.  病人收到了关于伤口护理的出院前指导。|近义词：pre-discharge, pre-release (in a broader context) |反义词：postdischarge

词源:  The word "predischarge" is a compound word formed from the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the noun "discharge" (meaning "the act of releasing or dismissing someone from a place, especially a hospital").  Therefore, it literally means "before discharge."
