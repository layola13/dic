reorientations:/ˌriːˌɔːriənˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |重新定向；重新定位；重新调整方向；改变方向 | The process of changing someone's or something's direction, focus, or perspective.  It often involves a fundamental shift in understanding or approach. |例句：The company's reorientations towards sustainable practices have led to increased customer loyalty. 公司转向可持续发展实践的重新定位导致了客户忠诚度的提高。

The recent political reorientations in the region have created uncertainty.  该地区最近的政治方向转变造成了不确定性。|近义词：realignments, readjustments, shifts, transformations |反义词：stagnation, persistence, continuation
