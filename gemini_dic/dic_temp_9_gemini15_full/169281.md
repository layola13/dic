promiscuous:/ˈprɒmɪskjuəs/ /prəˈmɪskjuəs/| adj.|滥交的；混杂的；杂乱的|Having or involving many different sexual partners.  Or,  lacking discrimination; haphazard. |例句：His promiscuous lifestyle led to several sexually transmitted diseases. 他的滥交生活导致了几种性传播疾病。

例句：The promiscuous arrangement of furniture made the room look cluttered.  家具杂乱无章的摆放使房间显得凌乱。


近义词：wanton, indiscriminate, unselective, haphazard| 反义词：chaste, selective, discriminating, monogamous
