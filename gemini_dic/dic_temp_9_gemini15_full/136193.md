nonrelativity:/ˌnɒnˌreləˈtɪvɪti/| n. |非相对性，非相对论|The absence or denial of relativity;  a state or theory that does not account for relativistic effects.|  The theory of nonrelativity is a simplified model, useful in certain contexts where relativistic effects are negligible.  非相对性理论是一个简化模型，在某些相对论效应可以忽略不计的情况下很有用。|近义词：absolutism (in some contexts)|反义词：relativity
