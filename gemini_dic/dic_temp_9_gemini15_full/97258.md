inflicters: /ɪnˈflɪktərz/ | n. |施害者，加害者 | Those who inflict harm or suffering.  People or things that inflict something unpleasant. |例句：The inflicters of violence must be brought to justice. 施暴者必须绳之以法。

The inflicters of this damage are unknown.  造成这种损害的施害者身份不明。|近义词：perpetrators, offenders, aggressors |反义词：victims, sufferers

词源:
The word "inflicter" is derived from the verb "inflict," which comes from the Latin word "infligere," meaning "to strike in, to dash against."  The suffix "-er" is an agent noun suffix, indicating the one who performs the action.
