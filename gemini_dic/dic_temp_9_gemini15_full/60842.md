electrothermally:/ˌɪˌlɛktroʊˈθɜːrməli/| adj. | 电热地，电热式地 | Relating to or produced by the combined action of electricity and heat.  This term describes processes or devices that use electricity to generate heat, or where electrical and thermal effects are intertwined. | Example:  The material was electrothermally treated to improve its conductivity.  材料经过电热处理以提高其导电性。|近义词: electrically heated, thermoelectrically | 反义词: mechanically cooled,  cryogenically cooled
