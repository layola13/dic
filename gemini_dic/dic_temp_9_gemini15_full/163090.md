precognitive: /ˌpriːˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/ /ˌpriːˈkɑːɡnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 预知的；有预知能力的 | Possessing the ability to know something before it happens; relating to precognition.  | Example:  She had a precognitive dream about the accident. 她做了一个关于事故的预知梦。

The psychic claimed to have precognitive abilities.  那个通灵者声称自己有预知能力。


近义词: prescient, prophetic, visionary | 反义词:hindsight, retrospective
