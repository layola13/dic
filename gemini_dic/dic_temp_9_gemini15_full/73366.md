foregoes:/fɔːrˈɡoʊz/ | v. |放弃；不享受；省略 | To do without (something desirable); relinquish.  |例句：He foregoes his lunch break to finish the project. 他放弃午休时间来完成项目。

例句：She foregoes the dessert to stay on her diet. 她为了保持身材放弃了甜点。


近义词：relinquish, waive, forgo, surrender, abandon | 反义词：retain, keep, embrace, accept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foregoes his coffee every morning. 他每天早上都放弃喝咖啡。
She foregoes many luxuries to save money. 她放弃许多奢侈品来省钱。


一般过去时:
They forewent the opportunity to travel abroad. 他们放弃了出国旅行的机会。
He forewent his right to appeal. 他放弃了他的上诉权。


现在完成时:
I have foregone many pleasures for this goal. 为了这个目标，我已经放弃了很多乐趣。
She has foregone her vacation to meet the deadline. 为了赶上最后期限，她放弃了她的假期。


过去完成时:
They had foregone their chances for promotion before the restructuring. 在重组之前，他们已经放弃了晋升的机会。
He had foregone his usual morning run due to illness. 由于生病，他放弃了他平时的晨跑。


将来时:
We will forego the extra expense and stick to the budget. 我们将放弃额外的开支，并坚持预算。
She will forego the party to study for the exam. 她将放弃派对去准备考试。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the risks, I would have foregone the investment. 如果我知道风险，我就不会进行这项投资了。
I wish I had foregone that argument; it was unproductive. 我真希望我没有进行那场争论；那是不值得的。


被动语态:
The right to appeal was foregone by the defendant. 上诉权被被告放弃了。
The dessert was foregone due to dietary restrictions. 由于饮食限制，甜点被放弃了。


疑问句:
Will you forego your claim to the inheritance? 你会放弃你对遗产的索赔吗？
Did he forego the opportunity to speak? 他放弃了发言的机会吗？


否定句:
He did not forego the chance to ask her out. 他没有放弃约她出去的机会。
She did not forego her afternoon tea. 她没有放弃她的下午茶。


PHRASE->
forego the pleasure of  放弃...的乐趣
forego an opportunity 放弃一个机会
forego one's rights 放弃权利
forego all hope 放弃所有希望
forego consideration of  不考虑...


词源:
Old English *forgān, from for- (“completely”) + gān (“to go”).  It literally meant "to go without," hence its current meaning.
