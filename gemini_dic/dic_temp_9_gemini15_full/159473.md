policemen:/pəˈliːsmən/ | n. |警察（复数）|Male members of the police force.  The term is now considered somewhat outdated and potentially offensive due to its gender-specific nature.  Modern usage prefers "police officers" as a more inclusive term. |例句：Two policemen arrived at the scene of the crime. 两名警察到达了犯罪现场。
The policemen apprehended the suspect. 警察逮捕了嫌疑人。|近义词：police officers, cops, constables |反义词：criminal, offender.
