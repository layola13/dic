incensed:/ɪnˈsɛnst/| adj.| 愤怒的；激怒的 | Extremely angry; enraged.  |例句：He was incensed by their blatant disregard for the rules.  他因为他们公然无视规则而非常愤怒。

例句：The unfair decision left her feeling incensed and betrayed.  不公平的决定让她感到愤怒和被背叛。


近义词：angry, furious, enraged, infuriated, irate

反义词：calm, peaceful, serene, tranquil


词源:  From Middle English *incensen, from Old French encencer "to burn incense," from Latin incendere "to burn," from in- "in" + candēre "to be white, to glow." The modern sense of "to inflame with anger" developed from the earlier sense of "to perfume," implying a strong, almost overwhelming sensation.
