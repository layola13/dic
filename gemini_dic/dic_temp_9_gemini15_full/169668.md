proportioned:/prəˈpɔːrʃənd/| adj.| 成比例的；相称的；匀称的 | Having or showing the correct proportions; well-balanced.  It refers to the harmonious or balanced relationship between the parts of something, or the size, shape, or amount of something in relation to something else. |例句：The building's design is well-proportioned, with a pleasing balance of size and space. 这座建筑的设计比例协调，尺寸和空间的平衡令人赏心悦目。


例句：Her features are perfectly proportioned, giving her a classic beauty.  她的五官比例完美，使她拥有古典美。


近义词：balanced, symmetrical, harmonious, commensurate


反义词：disproportionate, unbalanced, asymmetrical


词源:  From Middle English *proporcioned, from Old French *proporcioné, past participle of *proporcioner ("to proportion"), from Latin *proportionāre, from proportio ("proportion").  The ultimate roots trace back to Latin *pro ("forward") and *portio ("part, share").
