overplows:/ˈoʊvərplaʊz/ | v. |过度耕作，过度犁地 |To plow (land) excessively, often to the point of causing soil degradation.  This typically leads to soil erosion, nutrient depletion, and reduced agricultural productivity. |The farmer overplowed his fields, leading to significant soil erosion the following spring. = 农民过度耕作了他的田地，导致第二年春天严重的土壤侵蚀。


The continuous overplowing of the land has depleted its fertility. =  土地持续过度耕作已经耗尽了它的肥力。|近义词：overcultivate, exhaust, deplete |反义词：conserve, fallow, restore
