nonconvertibility:/ˌnɒnkənˌvɜːtəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可兑换性，非兑换性|The inability to convert something, typically currency or assets, into another form or currency.  This often refers to situations where a currency cannot be freely exchanged for another currency or for gold.|例句：The country suffered from economic hardship due to the nonconvertibility of its currency.  这个国家由于货币不可兑换而遭受经济困难。|例句：The nonconvertibility of the bonds limited their appeal to investors. 债券的不可兑换性限制了其对投资者的吸引力。|近义词：inconvertibility,  unconvertibility|反义词：convertibility
