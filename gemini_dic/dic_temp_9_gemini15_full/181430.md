rejailing:/ˌriːˈdʒeɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 再监禁，再次入狱 | The act of imprisoning someone again after a previous release.  It often implies a violation of parole or probation. | Example:  His rejailing was a direct result of his repeated offenses. = 他再次入狱是由于他屡犯的罪行。  The judge ordered his rejailing for contempt of court. = 法官下令因藐视法庭而再次监禁他。| 近义词：reimprisonment, reincarceration | 反义词：release, parole, pardon | 动词
