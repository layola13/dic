secretes:/sɪˈkriːts/ | vt. |分泌；泄露；隐瞒 | To produce and release a substance from a cell, gland, or organ.  To keep something secret; to hide something. |例句：The gland secretes hormones into the bloodstream.  腺体将激素分泌到血液中。

The government secretly secretes classified information. 政府秘密泄露机密信息。|近义词：exudes, releases,conceals, hides |反义词：reveals, discloses
