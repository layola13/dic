pseudomonastic:/ˌsjuːdoʊməˈnæstɪk/| adj.|似隐修的，貌似隐居的|Resembling or suggesting the practices or lifestyle of a monk or hermit, but not genuinely so.  It often implies a superficial or affected adoption of monastic qualities.|例句：He led a pseudomonastic life, spending his days in solitary study but occasionally indulging in lavish meals and social gatherings. 他过着看似隐居的生活，白天独自学习，但偶尔也会享受奢华的饭菜和社交聚会。  例句：Her pseudomonastic routine of early rising and rigorous self-discipline was ultimately undermined by her frequent trips to the city. 她看似隐居的规律生活，早起和严格的自我约束，最终被她频繁的城市旅行所破坏。|近义词：quasi-monastic, seemingly monastic, outwardly ascetic|反义词：worldly, hedonistic, dissolute
