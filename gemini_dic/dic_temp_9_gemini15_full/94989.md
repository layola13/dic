in the teeth of the wind: /ɪn ðə tiːθ əv ðə wɪnd/ | phrase | 逆风；顶风；不顾困难 | This phrase describes doing something despite significant opposition or difficulty,  similar to facing a strong headwind.  It emphasizes the challenges overcome. | 例句：The ship sailed in the teeth of the wind. 这艘船逆风航行。

The climbers pressed on in the teeth of the wind and snow. 登山者顶着风雪继续前进。


近义词：against the odds, despite the difficulties, in spite of, notwithstanding | 反义词：with the wind, easily, effortlessly | 词性: phrase
