capsicum:/ˈkæpsɪkəm/| n. |辣椒；甜椒；柿子椒|A capsicum is the fruit of plants belonging to the genus *Capsicum*, which includes chili peppers, bell peppers, and paprika.  They vary widely in shape, size, color, and pungency (heat).|例句：I added some chopped capsicum to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一些切碎的辣椒。|例句：The farmer grew a variety of capsicums, from mild bell peppers to fiery chilies. 这位农民种植了各种各样的辣椒，从温和的甜椒到火辣的辣椒。|近义词：pepper, chili, chilli, pimento|反义词：无
