irresistless:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstləs/| adj. |无法抗拒的，令人难以置信的|The word "irresistless" is an obsolete or rarely used synonym for "irresistible".  It means something so compelling or attractive that it is impossible to resist.  It implies a powerful force or allure that overwhelms the will.|例句：The allure of the forbidden fruit was irresistless to Eve.  夏娃无法抗拒禁果的诱惑。


The temptation to indulge in the rich desserts was irresistless.  沉溺于丰富的甜点诱惑是无法抗拒的。|近义词：irresistible, compelling, tempting, alluring |反义词：resistible, repellent, repulsive|词性:形容词
