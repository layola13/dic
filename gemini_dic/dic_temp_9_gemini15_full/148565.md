overtakable: /ˌoʊvərˈteɪkəbəl/ | adj. | 可超越的，可赶上的 |

英文解析: Capable of being overtaken; surpassable.  Describes something that can be overtaken or surpassed by someone or something else.


例句:

The smaller car was overtakable on the straight.  = 这辆较小的车在直道上是可以超越的。

His record is overtakable with enough hard work. = 只要足够努力，他的纪录是可以超越的。


近义词: surpassable, outstrippable


反义词: insurmountable, unbeatable, insuperable


词性: adjective
