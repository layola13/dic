gusty:/ˈɡʌsti/| adj.|阵风的，狂风的；性情急躁的|Characterized by strong, sudden gusts of wind; impulsive or changeable in mood or behavior.|例句：The gusty wind made it difficult to walk.  阵阵狂风使行走困难。|例句：He had a gusty temper and often shouted. 他脾气暴躁，经常大喊大叫。|近义词：blustery, windy, stormy, impetuous|反义词：calm, gentle, mild, placid
