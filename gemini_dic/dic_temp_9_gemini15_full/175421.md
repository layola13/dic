rambunctious:/ræmˈbʌŋktʃəs/| adj. |喧闹的；难以驾驭的；狂暴的 |Characterized by unrestrained exuberance; boisterous and noisy.  The word suggests a lively, energetic, and sometimes unruly quality.  |例句：The children were rambunctious and difficult to control during the party. 孩子们在聚会上很喧闹，难以管教。

例句：The rambunctious crowd cheered wildly as their team scored a goal.  喧闹的人群在他们的球队进球时疯狂欢呼。


近义词： boisterous, unruly, turbulent, rowdy, riotous

反义词：calm, quiet, peaceful, subdued, docile


词源:The word's origins are uncertain, but it likely combines elements suggesting a chaotic or noisy movement. The "ram" part might relate to the forceful impact of something, while the "-bunctious" part could be linked to words suggesting movement or noise. The exact etymology remains debated among linguists.
