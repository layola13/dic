emperil:/ɪmˈperəl/| vt.|危及；使危险；危害|To put something or someone in danger; to jeopardize.|例句：The reckless driver emperiled the lives of others.  鲁莽的司机危及了其他人的生命。|例句：The storm emperiled the small fishing boats. 暴风雨危及了小型渔船。|近义词：endanger, jeopardize, risk, threaten|反义词：protect, safeguard, secure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The reckless actions emperil the project. = 鲁莽的行为危及该项目。
Does this decision emperil our future? = 这个决定会危及我们的未来吗？


一般过去时:
The fire emperiled the entire building. = 大火危及了整栋建筑物。
His poor choices emperiled his career. = 他糟糕的选择危及了他的事业。


现在完成时:
The recent economic downturn has emperiled many businesses. = 最近的经济衰退已经危及了许多企业。
Years of neglect have emperiled the bridge's structural integrity. = 多年的忽视已经危及了桥梁的结构完整性。


过去完成时:
The faulty brakes had emperiled the passengers' safety. = 刹车故障已经危及了乘客的安全。
They had emperiled the mission by their carelessness. = 他们因粗心大意而危及了任务。


将来时:
Ignoring safety regulations will emperil the workers. = 忽视安全规章制度将会危及工人的安全。
The impending storm will emperil coastal communities. = 即将来临的暴风雨将危及沿海社区。


虚拟语气:
If he had followed the instructions, he would not have emperiled his life. = 如果他按照指示去做，他就不会危及自己的生命。
I wish they hadn't emperiled the mission with their recklessness. = 我真希望他们不要因为鲁莽而危及任务。


被动语态:
The project was emperiled by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目因不可预见的情况而受到威胁。
Many lives were emperiled by the earthquake. = 许多人的生命因地震而受到威胁。


疑问句:
Does the new policy emperil our market share? = 新政策会危及我们的市场份额吗？
Will this decision emperil the company's reputation? = 这个决定会危及公司的声誉吗？


否定句:
The updated safety procedures did not emperil the workers. = 更新后的安全程序没有危及工人的安全。
We took precautions to ensure the mission was not emperiled. = 我们采取了预防措施以确保任务不会受到威胁。
