recognizes:/ˈrɛkəɡnaɪzɪz/ | vt. |认出，识别，承认 |To identify someone or something or to accept something as true or legal. |例句：I recognized him from his photograph. 我从他的照片上认出了他。|例句：The government recognizes the need for reform. 政府承认需要改革。|近义词：identify, acknowledge, appreciate |反义词：ignore, overlook, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She recognizes her mistakes. = 她认识到自己的错误。
The computer recognizes the password. = 电脑识别了密码。


一般过去时:
He recognized his childhood friend. = 他认出了他儿时的朋友。
The court recognized the validity of the contract. = 法院承认了合同的有效性。


现在完成时:
I have recognized the importance of this project. = 我已经认识到这个项目的重要性。
Science has recognized the dangers of global warming. = 科学界已经认识到全球变暖的危险。


过去完成时:
They had recognized the pattern before it was too late. = 他们在为时未晚之前就已经认出了模式。
She had recognized the symptoms of the disease early on. = 她很早就已经认识到疾病的症状。


将来时:
I will recognize him when I see him. = 我看到他时就会认出他。
The company will recognize the employee's contributions. = 公司将认可员工的贡献。


虚拟语气:
If I recognized the danger, I would have acted differently. = 如果我认识到危险，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had recognized the signs sooner. = 我希望我早点发现这些迹象。


被动语态:
His talent was finally recognized by the critics. = 他的才能最终得到了评论家的认可。
The problem was recognized as serious. = 这个问题被认为很严重。


疑问句:
Do you recognize that song? = 你认得那首歌吗？
Does the system recognize your voice? = 系统能识别你的声音吗？


否定句:
I didn't recognize him at first. = 我一开始没认出他。
The judge didn't recognize the evidence. = 法官没有承认证据。


PHRASE->
recognize the importance of = 认识到……的重要性
recognize the need for = 认识到……的必要性
recognize someone's achievement = 认可某人的成就
widely recognized = 广为人知的
recognize a problem = 认识到一个问题
