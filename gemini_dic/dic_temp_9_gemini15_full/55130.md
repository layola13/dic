dissatisfactions:/ˌdɪsˌsætɪsˈfækʃənz/| n. |不满，不满意 |The plural form of dissatisfaction, referring to multiple instances of a feeling of unhappiness or discontent.  It implies a lack of fulfillment or a sense of being let down. |例句：Her numerous dissatisfactions with the company led to her resignation. 她在公司诸多不满导致她辞职了。
例句：The report highlighted the widespread dissatisfactions among the workforce. 报告强调了员工中普遍存在的不满情绪。|近义词：discontents, grievances, complaints |反义词：satisfactions, contentment, fulfillment |名词
