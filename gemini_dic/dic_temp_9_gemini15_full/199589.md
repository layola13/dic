sinic:/ˈsɪnɪk/| adj.|中国的；中国式的；汉语的|Relating to China, its people, or its culture.  The term often carries a connotation of being influenced by or characteristic of traditional Chinese culture.|例句：The sinicization of Buddhism in China led to unique religious practices.  中国的佛教化导致了独特的宗教习俗。|近义词：Chinese, Sino-,|反义词：Western, non-Chinese
