purée:/pʊˈreɪ/| n. |泥糊状物；果泥；菜泥 | A purée is a smooth, creamy mixture made by blending or pureeing food until it has a thick, paste-like consistency. It is often made from fruits, vegetables, or legumes.  |Example: The baby enjoyed the sweet potato purée.  婴儿喜欢吃红薯泥。The chef created a delicious tomato purée for the pasta sauce.  厨师为意面酱做了一种美味的番茄泥。|近义词：mash, pulp, paste |反义词：chunks, pieces
