self-analyst:/ˈsɛlf ˈænəlɪst/| n. |自我分析者，自我反省者 |A person who engages in self-analysis; someone who critically examines their own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  |例句：He's a self-analyst, constantly evaluating his strengths and weaknesses. 他是一个自我分析者，不断地评估自己的优缺点。|例句：She became a self-analyst after a period of deep introspection. 在一段深刻的反省之后，她成为一个自我分析者。|近义词：introspector, self-examiner, self-reflector |反义词：None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with someone who avoids self-reflection.
