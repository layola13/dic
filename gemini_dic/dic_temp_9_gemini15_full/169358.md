promulgation:/ˌprɒmʌlˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |颁布；公布；宣传 | The official promulgation of the new law took place last week.  |近义词：publication, dissemination, announcement |反义词：suppression, concealment


英文解析：The act of promulgating, or making something known publicly; especially of laws or doctrines.  It implies a formal and official announcement, often with the intention of widespread dissemination.

例句：

The promulgation of the new regulations caused widespread confusion among the employees. 新规的颁布给员工带来了广泛的困惑。

The government's promulgation of its economic policies was met with both support and criticism. 政府公布其经济政策受到了支持和批评。

一般现在时:
The government promulgates new laws regularly. 政府定期颁布新法律。
They promulgate their beliefs through various channels. 他们通过各种渠道传播他们的信仰。


一般过去时:
The company promulgated its new marketing strategy last month. 公司上个月公布了新的营销策略。
The king promulgated a decree that affected the entire kingdom. 国王颁布了一项影响整个王国的法令。


现在完成时:
The organization has promulgated several important documents this year. 今年该组织已经颁布了几份重要文件。
The church has promulgated its teachings for centuries. 教会几个世纪以来一直在传播其教义。


过去完成时:
They had promulgated the news before the official announcement. 他们在正式宣布之前就已经公布了这个消息。
The court had promulgated a decision that was later overturned. 法院曾发布一项后来被推翻的裁决。


将来时:
The president will promulgate the new environmental protection act next week. 总统下周将颁布新的环境保护法。
The school will promulgate its new admission policy in the fall. 学校将在秋季公布新的招生政策。


虚拟语气:
If the government promulgated stricter regulations, the problem might be solved. 如果政府颁布更严格的法规，这个问题或许就能解决。
I wish they would promulgate a clearer explanation of the rules. 我希望他们能对规则做出更清晰的解释。


被动语态:
The new guidelines were promulgated by the regulatory authority. 新的指导方针是由监管机构颁布的。
The decision was promulgated to the public via a press release. 该决定通过新闻发布的方式向公众公布。


疑问句:
When will the new regulations be promulgated? 新规什么时候颁布？
Has the company promulgated its financial report yet? 公司是否已经公布了财务报告？


否定句:
The company did not promulgate its findings publicly.  该公司没有公开发布其调查结果。
The government has not yet promulgated the revised legislation. 政府尚未颁布修订后的立法。
