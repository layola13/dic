overmodernizing: /ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdərnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度现代化 | The act of making something excessively modern, often to the detriment of its original character or functionality.  This often involves replacing older elements with newer ones without considering the overall harmony or historical context. |  The city's overmodernizing of its historic district alienated many residents.  = 城市对其历史街区的过度现代化疏远了许多居民。  The overmodernizing of the museum’s exhibits resulted in the loss of some valuable artifacts. = 博物馆展品的过度现代化导致一些珍贵文物丢失。 | modernizing, updating | traditionalizing, preserving | 动词
