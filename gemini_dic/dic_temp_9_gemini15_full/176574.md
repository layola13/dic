re-weight:/ˌriːˈweɪt/| vt.|重新赋予权重，重新加权|To assign weights again; to adjust the weights of something.|例句：We need to re-weight the variables in the model to improve accuracy.我们需要重新调整模型中的变量权重以提高精度。


例句：The researcher decided to re-weight the survey data to account for sampling bias.研究人员决定重新调整调查数据以考虑抽样偏差。


近义词：rebalance, readjust, recalculate|反义词：ignore, neglect|词性：动词
