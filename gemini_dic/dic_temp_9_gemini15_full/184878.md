retail:/ˈriːteɪl/| n. v.|零售；零售业；以零售价出售|The act of selling goods to consumers, typically in small quantities.|例句：The retail price of the item is $20.  该商品的零售价为20美元。|例句：She works in retail.  她在零售业工作。|近义词：wholesale(反义词),  resell|反义词：wholesale|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retails clothing. = 他从事服装零售。
This store retails a wide variety of goods. = 这家商店零售各种各样的商品。


一般过去时:
They retailed their products online last year. = 他们去年在网上零售他们的产品。
The company retailed books at a discount. = 该公司以折扣价销售书籍。


现在完成时:
She has retailed antiques for over twenty years. = 她从事古董零售已有二十多年了。
We have retailed our products successfully in the market. = 我们已经在市场上成功地销售了我们的产品。


过去完成时:
They had retailed all their stock before the sale. = 他们在大甩卖之前已经卖光了所有的库存。
The shop had retailed many items at a loss before closing down. = 商店在倒闭前亏本销售了许多商品。


将来时:
We will retail our new products next month. = 我们下个月将开始销售我们的新产品。
They will retail their goods at a competitive price. = 他们将以具有竞争力的价格销售他们的商品。


虚拟语气:
If they retailed at a lower price, they would sell more. = 如果他们以较低的价格销售，他们会卖得更多。
I wish I could retail my handmade crafts online. = 我希望我能在线销售我的手工制品。


被动语态:
The goods are retailed through various channels. = 商品通过各种渠道进行零售。
The new product is being retailed at a premium price. = 新产品正在以高价销售。


疑问句:
Do they retail organic products? = 他们零售有机产品吗？
Where do you retail your goods? = 你在哪里销售你的商品？


否定句:
We don't retail electronics. = 我们不销售电子产品。
They didn't retail that brand last year. = 他们去年没有销售那个品牌。
