grinding:/ˈɡraɪndɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|磨碎；碾碎；折磨；单调乏味的工作；令人厌烦的；| to make (something) into powder or small pieces by crushing it |The machine is grinding the wheat into flour. 这台机器正在把小麦磨成面粉。|近义词：milling, pulverizing, crushing |反义词：smoothing, polishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grinds coffee beans every morning. = 他每天早上都磨咖啡豆。
The machine grinds the wheat into flour. = 这台机器把小麦磨成面粉。

一般过去时:
She ground the spices before adding them to the stew. = 她把香料磨碎后再加入炖菜中。
They ground the lenses to a precise shape. = 他们将镜片磨成精确的形状。

现在完成时:
He has ground the pepper for the soup. = 他已经把胡椒粉磨碎用来做汤了。
I have been grinding my teeth in my sleep. = 我睡觉时一直在磨牙。


过去完成时:
She had ground the coffee before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经磨好了咖啡。
They had ground the tools to a sharp edge.  = 他们把工具磨得很锋利。


将来时:
I will grind the lentils for the dahl tomorrow. = 我明天将磨碎扁豆做达尔汤。
The farmer will grind the corn after the harvest. = 农民将在收割后磨碎玉米。


虚拟语气:
If I had a mill, I would grind my own flour. = 如果我有一台磨坊，我就会自己磨面粉。
I wish I hadn't ground my teeth so much last night. = 我真希望昨晚我没有磨那么多牙。
