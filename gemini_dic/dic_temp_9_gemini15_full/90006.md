hortulan:/ˈhɔːrtjʊlən/| adj.|园艺的；种植园的|Relating to gardens or gardening; cultivated.  The word evokes a sense of careful cultivation and tending of plants, often implying a smaller, more intimate garden setting than a large farm or estate.|例句：The hortulan society held a competition for the best-grown tomatoes.  园艺协会举办了一场评选最佳种植番茄的比赛。

例句：Her hortulan skills were evident in her meticulously arranged flowerbeds.  她的园艺技巧在她精心布置的花坛中显而易见。
