customizes:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzɪz/ | vt. |定制，个性化 | To modify (something) to suit a particular individual or task.  |例句：The company customizes software to meet the specific needs of its clients.  这家公司定制软件以满足客户的特定需求。|例句：Many car manufacturers allow customers to customize their vehicles with various options.许多汽车制造商允许客户通过各种选择来定制他们的车辆。|近义词：personalize, tailor, adapt, modify |反义词：standardize, generalize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She customizes her own clothes. = 她自己定制衣服。
The shop customizes bikes to order. = 这家店根据订单定制自行车。

一般过去时:
He customized his computer with new software. = 他用新软件定制了他的电脑。
They customized the marketing campaign for a younger audience. = 他们为年轻观众定制了营销活动。

现在完成时:
I have customized my phone settings. = 我已经定制了我的手机设置。
She has customized her profile to reflect her interests. = 她已经定制了她的个人资料以反映她的兴趣。

过去完成时:
He had customized the car before he sold it. = 他在卖车之前已经定制了这辆车。
They had customized the presentation for the specific client. = 他们已经为特定客户定制了演示文稿。

将来时:
I will customize my website next week. = 我下周将定制我的网站。
We will customize the training program for each participant. = 我们将为每位参与者定制培训计划。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would customize my entire house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会定制我的整个房子。
I wish I could customize the software to add more functions. = 我希望我可以定制软件以添加更多功能。

被动语态:
The software is customized to meet your needs. = 该软件已定制以满足您的需求。
The car was customized by a professional mechanic. = 这辆车是由专业技工定制的。

疑问句:
Do you customize your workspaces? = 你会定制你的工作空间吗？
Can this software be customized? = 这个软件可以定制吗？

否定句:
I don't customize my clothes; I buy them ready-made. = 我不定制衣服；我买现成的。
The company doesn't customize its products for individual customers. = 这家公司不为单个客户定制其产品。
