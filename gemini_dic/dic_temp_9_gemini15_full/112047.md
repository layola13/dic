longboat:/ˈlɒŋˌboʊt/| n. |大划艇，长艇|A large, open boat propelled by oars, often used by ships for carrying cargo or people to shore.|The longboat was lowered to take the passengers ashore.  长艇放下以便乘客上岸。|The lifeboat was lowered and used to rescue the survivors.|救生艇放下用来营救幸存者。|近义词：boat, barge, launch|反义词：无
