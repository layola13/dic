cruise:/kruːz/| /krʊz/ | n. vi. |巡航；乘船游览；轻松愉快地度过 | a pleasure voyage on a ship or boat.  |例句：We took a cruise to the Caribbean. 我们乘船游览了加勒比海。|例句：They cruised around the Mediterranean. 他们在地中海附近巡航。|近义词：voyage, tour, trip |反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cruises the coast every summer. = 他每年夏天都在沿海巡航。
She cruises the internet for hours daily. = 她每天在网上冲浪数小时。

一般过去时:
We cruised to Alaska last year. = 我们去年乘船去了阿拉斯加。
They cruised down the river in a small boat. = 他们乘小船沿着河顺流而下。

现在完成时:
I have cruised through the entire catalog. = 我已经浏览了整个目录。
She has cruised the Mediterranean Sea twice. = 她已经两次游览了地中海。

过去完成时:
He had cruised the area before, so he knew the best spots. = 他以前曾巡航过这个区域，所以他知道最好的地点。
They had cruised the Pacific Ocean for months before they reached their destination. = 他们到达目的地之前已经在太平洋上航行了几个月。

将来时:
We will cruise to Hawaii next spring. = 我们明年春天将去夏威夷乘船游览。
They will cruise the canals of Venice. = 他们将游览威尼斯的运河。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would cruise around the world. = 如果我有钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I could cruise the Caribbean islands. = 我希望我能游览加勒比海岛屿。

被动语态:
The ship was cruised by pirates. = 这艘船被海盗劫持了。 (This example uses a less common passive voice construction)
The area is regularly cruised by patrol boats. = 该区域定期由巡逻艇巡逻。

疑问句:
Do you often cruise? = 你经常乘船游览吗？
Will you cruise this summer? = 你今年夏天会乘船游览吗？

否定句:
We didn't cruise last summer. = 我们去年夏天没有乘船游览。
They don't cruise very often. = 他们不经常乘船游览。
