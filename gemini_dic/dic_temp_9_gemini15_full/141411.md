ophiomorphite:/ˌɒfiəˈmɔːfaɪt/| n. |蛇迹石|Ophiomorphite is a type of trace fossil, specifically an ichnofossil, that represents the dwelling burrows of marine organisms, often crustaceans.  These burrows are typically vertical or slightly inclined, cylindrical, and exhibit a distinctive branching or meandering pattern. The term "ophiomorphite" itself refers to the fossilized burrow structure, not the organism that created it.  The study of ophiomorphites provides valuable information about ancient marine environments and the organisms that inhabited them.|例句：The sandstone layer contained numerous well-preserved ophiomorphites, indicating a shallow marine depositional environment.  砂岩层包含许多保存完好的蛇迹石，表明这是一个浅海沉积环境。  The paleontologists carefully documented the size and orientation of the ophiomorphites to reconstruct the ancient seabed ecosystem. 古生物学家仔细记录了蛇迹石的大小和方向，以重建古代海底生态系统。|近义词：ichnofossil, trace fossil, burrow fossil|反义词：body fossil
