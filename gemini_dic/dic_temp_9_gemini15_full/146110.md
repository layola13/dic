overembellishing: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/ | v. |过度装饰；夸大其词 |  The act of excessively decorating or exaggerating something.  It implies adding too much detail or ornamentation, often to the point of being unrealistic or deceptive. |

例句：

He was overembellishing his accomplishments in the interview. = 他在面试中夸大了自己的成就。

The story was overembellished with unnecessary details. = 这个故事被添加了不必要的细节，显得过于夸张。


近义词：exaggerate, overstate, embellish (but with a stronger sense of excess), inflate, gild the lily


反义词：understate, downplay, minimize


词性: 动词
