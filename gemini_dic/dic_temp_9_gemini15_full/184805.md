resurrects:/ˈrezərekt/| vt.|使复活；使复兴；重新启用|To bring back to life; to revive or restore something.|例句：The magician resurrected the dead rabbit. 魔术师使死去的兔子复活了。|例句：The company resurrected its old marketing campaign. 公司重新启用其旧的营销活动。|近义词：revive, revitalize, reinstate|反义词：kill, destroy, abolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resurrects old traditions. 他复兴古老的传统。
The church resurrects the faith of its followers. 教堂使信徒的信仰复活。

一般过去时:
They resurrected the abandoned project. 他们使被放弃的项目重新启动。
The artist resurrected an old painting technique. 艺术家使一种古老的绘画技巧复活。

现在完成时:
She has resurrected her career after a long break. 她经过长时间的休息后，使自己的事业重振旗鼓。
The government has resurrected the debate on tax reform. 政府已经重新启动了关于税制改革的辩论。

过去完成时:
He had resurrected his reputation before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经恢复了自己的名誉。
The company had resurrected its profits by the end of the year. 到年底，公司已经恢复了盈利。

将来时:
They will resurrect the old building as a museum. 他们将把旧建筑改建成博物馆。
The band will resurrect their classic album next year. 乐队明年将重新发行他们的经典专辑。

虚拟语气:
If they resurrected the old factory, it would create jobs. 如果他们使旧工厂重新运转，那将创造就业机会。
I wish they would resurrect the old TV show. 我希望他们能把那部旧电视剧重新制作。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
