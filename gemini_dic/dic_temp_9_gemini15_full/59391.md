earshot:/ˈɪəʃɒt/| n. |听力范围，能听到说话声的距离|The distance within which one can hear.  It refers to the range of sound that a person can hear.|He spoke in a low voice, just within earshot. 他低声说话，就在听力范围内。

The area within hearing distance.  They were close enough to be within earshot.|They whispered so that no one else within earshot could hear them. 他们低声耳语，以免在听力范围内的人听到他们。|近义词：hearing range, hearing distance|反义词：out of hearing, inaudible
