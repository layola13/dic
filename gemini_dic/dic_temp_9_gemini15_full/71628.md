flatliners:/ˈflætˌlaɪnərz/| n. |平线，（心电图上的）直线，电影名|A flatline is a straight line on a graph, especially a cardiogram, indicating the absence of a heartbeat.  The word also refers to a state of inactivity or stagnation.|例句：The doctor watched the flatline with growing concern. 医生越来越担忧地看着那条平线。|例句：The film "Flatliners" explores the themes of death and the afterlife. 电影《平线》探讨了死亡和来世等主题。|近义词：straight line, inactivity, stagnation|反义词：heartbeat, activity, progress
