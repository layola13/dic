The Narragansett tribe played a significant role in the colonial history of Rhode Island. 纳拉甘西特部落在罗德岛的殖民历史中扮演着重要的角色。

词源：The name "Narragansett" is derived from the Narragansett word *nārāhansett*, meaning "people at the point of land," or "people of the Narragansett Bay."  The etymology reflects their historical location along the shores of Narragansett Bay in Rhode Island.
