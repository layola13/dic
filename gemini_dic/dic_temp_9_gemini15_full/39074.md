conforms:/kənˈfɔːrmz/ | v. |符合，遵照，一致 |To be in accordance with or agreement; to comply with rules, standards, or expectations. |例句：The building conforms to all safety regulations. 这座建筑符合所有安全条例。|例句：His behavior conforms to the highest ethical standards. 他的行为符合最高的道德标准。|近义词：complies, accords, agrees |反义词：violates, disobeys, contravenes |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conforms to the company's dress code. = 他符合公司的着装规范。
The data conforms to the expected pattern. = 数据符合预期模式。


一般过去时:
The product didn't conform to the specifications. = 产品不符合规格。
She conformed to the pressure from her family. = 她屈服于来自家庭的压力。


现在完成时:
The company has conformed to the new regulations. = 公司已经遵守了新规章。
He has always conformed to the rules. = 他一直遵守规则。


过去完成时:
They had conformed to the tradition for generations. = 他们几代人都遵循着这个传统。
The building had conformed to the city's building codes. = 这座建筑符合城市的建筑规范。


将来时:
The new software will conform to industry standards. = 新软件将符合行业标准。
We will conform to the agreement we signed. = 我们将遵守我们签署的协议。


虚拟语气:
If he conformed to the rules, he wouldn't be in trouble. = 如果他遵守规则，他就不会有麻烦。
I wish he would conform to the company's policy. = 我希望他会遵守公司的政策。


被动语态:
The design must conform to the client's requirements. = 设计必须符合客户的要求。(主动语态更常用)
The results were found to conform to the hypothesis. = 结果被发现符合假设。(主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Does this design conform to the safety standards? = 这个设计符合安全标准吗？
Does the product conform to the customer's requirements? = 产品符合客户的要求吗？


否定句:
The building does not conform to building codes. = 这座建筑不符合建筑规范。
His actions do not conform to our values. = 他的行为不符合我们的价值观。


PHRASE->

conform oneself to = 使自己适应
