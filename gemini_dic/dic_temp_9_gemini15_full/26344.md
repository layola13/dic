buzzingly:/ˈbʌzɪŋli/| adv.|嗡嗡作响地；充满活力地；喧嚣地 |  Describes a state of being full of activity, excitement, or noise, often associated with a buzzing sound.  |例句：The city buzzed with activity.  这座城市充满了活力。

The cafe was buzzingly busy with customers. 这家咖啡馆顾客盈门，热闹非凡。|近义词：excitedly, vibrantly, bustlingly, excitedly |反义词：quietly, silently, calmly
