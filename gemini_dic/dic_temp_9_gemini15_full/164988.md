preharvests:/priːˈhɑːrvɛsts/ | n. |收获前处理，采收前准备 | Activities and processes undertaken before the actual harvesting of crops.  This includes practices like irrigation, pest control, and monitoring crop maturity. | Example:  Preharvests are crucial for maximizing yield and quality. 收获前的准备工作对于最大限度地提高产量和质量至关重要。 | 近义词：pre-harvest activities, pre-harvest management | 反义词：postharvest
