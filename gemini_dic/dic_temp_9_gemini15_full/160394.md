poplins:/ˈpɒplɪnz/ | n. |府绸，涤棉布 |A lightweight plain-woven fabric, typically made of cotton, but sometimes with other fibers such as silk or polyester.  It's known for its smooth, slightly lustrous surface and crisp texture.  |例句：The shirt is made of fine poplins. 这件衬衫是由精细的府绸制成的。|例句：She chose a poplin dress for the summer wedding. 她选择了一件府绸连衣裙参加夏季婚礼。|近义词：broadcloth, cotton, calico |反义词：无 |名词
