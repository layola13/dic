crateriform:/ˈkreɪtərfɔːrm/| adj.| 火山口形的；杯形的 | Resembling a crater; cup-shaped.  The term is used to describe geological formations, impact structures, or other objects that have a bowl-shaped depression. | Example: The crateriform depression in the landscape was caused by a meteorite impact.  这个景观上的火山口状凹陷是由陨石撞击造成的。|近义词：cup-shaped, bowl-shaped, crater-like |反义词：peak-shaped, conical


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations.  Its usage is descriptive.

Example 1: The volcanic rock displayed a striking crateriform structure. 这块火山岩展现出引人注目的火山口形状结构。
Example 2: Microscopic examination revealed the crateriform pitting on the metal surface. 显微镜检查显示金属表面有火山口状的凹坑。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "crateriform".  The word itself is used as a descriptive adjective within geological or scientific contexts.
