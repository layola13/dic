septuagenary:/sepˈtuːədʒənəri/| adj. |七十岁的；七十年代的；| Relating to or denoting a period of seventy years or a person who is seventy years old.  |He's a septuagenarian who still enjoys hiking. 他是一位七十多岁仍然喜欢远足的人。|七十岁的，古稀之年的 | octogenarian, nonagenarian |youthful, young


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective,  so grammatical examples are limited.  It doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It's mainly used to describe someone's age or a period of time.


PHRASE->
a septuagenarian actor 一位七十多岁的演员
a septuagenarian woman 一位七十多岁的妇女
in her septuagenary years 在她七十多岁的时候
