condemners:/kənˈdɛmnərz/ | n. |谴责者，责难者 |Those who express strong disapproval of something. People who condemn something or someone. |例句：The government's actions drew sharp criticism from condemners across the political spectrum. 政府的行为招致了来自各政治派别的谴责者的强烈批评。

例句：The condemners of the war marched in protest.  谴责战争的人们游行抗议。


近义词：critics, censurers, objectors, detractors

反义词：supporters, approvers, defenders


词性:名词
