purple:/ˈpɜːrpəl/ /ˈpɜːrpl/| adj. n. |紫色的；紫色 |Purple is a color that is a mix of red and blue.  紫色是由红色和蓝色混合而成的颜色。|  The sunset painted the sky with streaks of purple. 日落给天空涂上了紫色的条纹。 |violet, lilac, magenta|  yellow, orange, green| adjective, noun

Her dress is a deep purple. = 她的裙子是深紫色。
