nonmetric:/ˌnɒnˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 非度量的；非公制的 | Not based on or involving a system of measurement.  It refers to data or scales that do not have equal intervals between values.  For example, categorical data like colors or types of animals are nonmetric. | Example:  The survey used a nonmetric scale to assess customer satisfaction.  这项调查使用了非度量标度来评估客户满意度。  |近义词：qualitative, categorical | 反义词：metric, quantitative
