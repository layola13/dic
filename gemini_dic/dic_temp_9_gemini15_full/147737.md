overprocessing:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsɛsɪŋ/|/ˈoʊvə(r)ˈprɑːsɛsɪŋ/| n.|过度处理；过度加工|The act of processing something excessively, often resulting in undesirable outcomes.  This can apply to various contexts, including image editing, data analysis, and even emotional responses.|例句：Overprocessing the image resulted in a loss of detail and unnatural colors.  过度处理图像导致细节丢失和颜色不自然。

例句：His overprocessing of the grief led to further emotional distress. 他对悲伤的过度处理导致了进一步的情绪困扰。|近义词：overworking, overdoing, overrefinement|反义词：underprocessing, simplification
