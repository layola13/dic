isometrical:/ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkəl/| adj.|等角的，等大的；等轴测的 |Of or relating to an isometric projection; showing a three-dimensional object in two dimensions in such a way that all three axes are equally foreshortened.  An isometric drawing shows the object at a 30-degree angle. | 例句：The isometric drawing clearly shows the dimensions of the building. 等轴测图清楚地显示了建筑物的尺寸。

例句：The architect used an isometric projection to create a three-dimensional representation of the design. 建筑师使用等轴测投影来创建设计的三个维度表示。


近义词：equiaxed, equal-angled,  isometric projection

反义词：perspective (in the context of projection)
