scragly:/ˈskræɡli/| adj.|瘦骨嶙峋的，蓬乱的，粗糙的|thin and bony; unkempt and scraggly; rough and uneven|例句：The scragly dog looked malnourished. 那只瘦骨嶙峋的狗看起来营养不良。
The scragly beard needed a trim.  那蓬乱的胡须需要修剪一下。|近义词：gaunt, ragged, unkempt, shaggy|反义词：well-fed, groomed, smooth
