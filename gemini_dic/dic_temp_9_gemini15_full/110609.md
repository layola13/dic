limos:/ˈlɪmoʊz/ | n. |豪华轿车，礼宾车 |A limousine is a large, luxurious car, often with a partition separating the driver from the passengers.  Limousines are typically used for formal occasions or by high-profile individuals. |例句：He arrived at the wedding in a long black limousine. 他乘坐一辆黑色长款豪华轿车到达婚礼现场。|例句：The company provides limousine service to the airport. 这家公司提供机场豪华轿车服务。|近义词：luxury car, town car|反义词：economy car, compact car|名词
