redresser:/rɪˈdrɛsər/ | n. |纠正者；矫正者；赔偿者 | One who redresses wrongs or grievances; one who remedies or sets right.  A redresser of grievances works to correct injustices. | The mediator acted as a redresser of grievances between the two feuding families. 调解员充当了这两个争吵不休的家庭之间的纠正冤屈的人。 |近义词：rectifier, reformer, compensator |反义词：aggressor, wrongdoer
