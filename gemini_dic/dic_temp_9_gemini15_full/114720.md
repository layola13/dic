mallet:/ˈmælit/ | n. | 木槌；槌球杆 | A mallet is a type of hammer with a large head, typically made of wood or rubber, used for hitting things without causing damage.  It is also a type of club used in the game of croquet. | 例句：He used a rubber mallet to gently tap the furniture into place. 他用橡胶锤轻轻地敲打家具使其就位。

He skillfully wielded his mallet, sending the croquet ball towards the wicket. 他熟练地挥舞着球杆，将槌球击向球门。| 近义词：hammer, club, maul | 反义词：None specifically, but a delicate instrument would be an antonym in the context of its use.
