playwright:/ˈpleɪˌraɪt/| n.|剧作家|A playwright is a person who writes plays.|例句：Shakespeare is a renowned playwright.莎士比亚是一位著名的剧作家。|近义词：dramatist, scriptwriter|反义词:actor, actress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The playwright writes a new play. = 剧作家正在写一部新剧。
She writes plays for the theatre. = 她为剧院写剧本。


一般过去时:
The playwright wrote a successful play last year. = 剧作家去年写了一部成功的戏剧。
He wrote many plays during his career. = 他在职业生涯中创作了许多戏剧。


现在完成时:
The playwright has written five plays this year. = 剧作家今年已经创作了五部戏剧。
She has written extensively on the subject of drama. = 她已经广泛地撰写关于戏剧的主题。


过去完成时:
The playwright had written several drafts before submitting the final script. = 剧作家在提交最终剧本之前已经写了好几个草稿。
They had written the play before the director arrived. = 导演到达之前，他们已经写好了剧本。


将来时:
The playwright will write a new play next year. = 剧作家明年将创作一部新剧。
He will have finished the play by next month. = 他下个月将完成这部戏。


虚拟语气:
If the playwright were here, he would explain the plot. = 如果剧作家在这里，他会解释情节。
I wish the playwright had written a happier ending. = 我希望剧作家写一个更快乐的结局。


被动语态:
The play was written by a famous playwright. = 这部戏是由一位著名的剧作家创作的。
The new play is being written by a young playwright. = 新剧正在由一位年轻的剧作家创作。


疑问句:
Who wrote this play? = 谁写的这部戏？
Is she a successful playwright? = 她是一位成功的剧作家吗？


否定句:

She hasn't written a play for many years. = 她很多年没写过戏了。
