entomologies:/ˌɛntəˈmɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |昆虫学(复数)|The scientific study of insects.  It encompasses their morphology, physiology, development, behavior, ecology, and classification. |例句：Her entomologies studies focused on the impact of pesticides on bee populations.她的昆虫学研究集中在杀虫剂对蜜蜂种群的影响上。  |例句：The professor delivered a fascinating lecture on various aspects of entomologies.教授就昆虫学的各个方面作了一场引人入胜的讲座。|近义词：insect studies, entomology (单数)|反义词：无
