compassions:/kəmˈpæʃən(z)/| n.|同情，怜悯|A feeling of deep sympathy and sorrow for another who is stricken by misfortune, accompanied by a strong desire to alleviate the suffering.|例句：He showed great compassion for the victims of the earthquake. 他对地震中的受害者表现出极大的同情。|例句：Her compassion extended to all creatures, great and small. 她的同情心延伸到所有大小生物。|近义词：empathy, sympathy, pity|反义词：indifference, apathy, cruelty|名词
