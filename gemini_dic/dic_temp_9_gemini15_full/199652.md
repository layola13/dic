sinter:/ˈsɪntər/ | n. v. | 烧结；熔结；粘结 | The process of heating a powdered material to a temperature below its melting point, causing the particles to bond together.  This process is often used in the production of ceramics, metals, and other materials. | The ceramic pieces were sintered at a high temperature. 陶瓷碎片在高温下烧结。 The metal powder was sintered to form a solid block. 金属粉末烧结成一个固体块。 | fuse, consolidate, weld |  disintegrate, separate |  n. v.
