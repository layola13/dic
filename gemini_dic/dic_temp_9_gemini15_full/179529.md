reeking:/ˈriːkɪŋ/| adj.|恶臭的；充满…臭味的 | strongly smelling of something unpleasant.  |例句：The room was reeking of stale cigarettes and sweat. 这房间里充满了陈旧的香烟味和汗臭味。|例句：The garbage was reeking, attracting a swarm of flies. 垃圾散发着恶臭，吸引来一群苍蝇。|近义词：stinking, foul-smelling, fetid, malodorous |反义词： fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The garbage reeks. = 垃圾散发着恶臭。
The room is reeking of fish. = 房间里充满了鱼腥味。

一般过去时:
The fish reeked. = 鱼散发着臭味。
The drain reeked after the blockage. = 下水道堵塞后散发着恶臭。

现在完成时:
The incident has reeked of corruption. = 这事件充满了腐败的味道。
The room has been reeking all day. = 这房间一整天都臭气熏天。

过去完成时:
The meat had reeked before it was discovered. = 肉在被发现之前就已经臭了。
The car had reeked of gasoline after the accident. = 事故后，汽车充满了汽油味。


将来时:
The landfill will reek for years to come. = 垃圾填埋场在未来几年都会散发恶臭。
The sewage will reek if not properly treated. = 如果污水不经过适当处理，将会散发恶臭。


虚拟语气:
If the fish had been fresh, it wouldn't have reeked. = 如果鱼是新鲜的，就不会有臭味了。
I wish the room didn't reek of smoke. = 我希望房间里没有烟味。


被动语态:
The air was reeked by the factory emissions. = 空气被工厂排放物污染得臭气熏天。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The situation is reeked with suspicion. =  这种情况充满了嫌疑。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Does the garbage reek? = 垃圾臭吗？
Is the room reeking of something unpleasant? = 房间里散发着令人不快的味道吗？


否定句:
The flowers didn't reek; they smelled sweet. = 花不臭，它们闻起来很香。
The air didn't reek of pollution. = 空气中没有污染物的臭味。


PHRASE->
reek of something:  散发...的气味
reek with something:  充满...
reek to high heaven: 臭不可闻
reek with suspicion: 充满了嫌疑
reek with dishonesty: 充满了欺诈


词源:
Old English *rēcan (“to smoke, reek”), from Proto-Germanic *rēukan (“to smoke”), from Proto-Indo-European *(h₁)reuk- (“to smoke”).  The modern meaning developed from the original sense of emitting smoke or vapor.
