coalescing: /ˌkoʊəˈlɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 联合；合并；融合 |

英文解析:To come together and form one mass or whole; to merge or unite.  The process of combining or merging things into a single entity.

例句:
The small droplets of water were coalescing to form a larger drop.  = 小水滴正在合并成更大的水滴。

The different factions within the party are slowly coalescing around a single leader. = 党内不同的派系正在慢慢地团结在一个领导人周围。


近义词: merging, combining, uniting, fusing, amalgamating, consolidating

反义词: separating, dividing, splitting, dispersing


词源: From Middle English *coalescen, from Old French coalescer, from Latin coalescere "to grow together," from co- "together" + alescere "to grow, nourish."
