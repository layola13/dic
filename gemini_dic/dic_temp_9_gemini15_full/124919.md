mudsill:/ˈmʌdˌsɪl/| n. |泥基石，基础；社会底层的人|A mudsill is the lowest horizontal timber in a frame building, resting on the foundation.  The term is also used figuratively to refer to the lowest or most despised class of people in society.|例句：The old house was built on a solid mudsill. 这栋老房子建在坚实的泥基石上。


例句：He considered the factory workers to be the mudsills of society. 他认为工厂工人是社会的底层人士。


近义词：foundation, base, bottom, lowlife, dregs


反义词：elite, aristocracy, upper class
