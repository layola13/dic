intimidate: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/ | vt. | 恐吓；威胁；使胆怯 | To frighten or overawe (someone), especially in order to make them do what one wants.  | 例句：He tried to intimidate his opponent into withdrawing from the race. 他试图恐吓对手退出比赛。

例句：The bully tried to intimidate the smaller children.  那个恶霸试图恐吓那些更小的孩子。


近义词：threaten, bully, scare, daunt, cow

反义词：encourage, embolden, reassure

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intimidates his colleagues with his aggressive behavior. 他以其咄咄逼人的行为恐吓同事。
She intimidates me with her knowledge.  她的知识让我胆怯。


一般过去时:
The loud noises intimidated the young child.  巨大的噪音吓坏了那个小孩子。
The interview intimidated her.  面试让她感到害怕。


现在完成时:
His aggressive tactics have intimidated many opponents. 他的侵略性策略已经吓倒了许多对手。
The company's strong stance has intimidated competitors.  这家公司的强硬立场吓退了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
She had been intimidated by his previous success. 她一直被他之前的成功所震慑。
They had intimidated the witness into silence.  他们恐吓证人保持沉默。


将来时:
He will intimidate his rivals into submission. 他将恐吓他的对手屈服。
The new regulations will intimidate small businesses. 新规定将吓退小型企业。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't intimidated the witness, the trial would have been different. 如果他没有恐吓证人，审判就会有所不同。
I wish he wouldn't intimidate me so much.  我希望他不要再那么吓唬我了。


被动语态:
The witness was intimidated into giving false testimony.  证人被恐吓而作伪证。
Many people are intimidated by public speaking. 许多人害怕公开演讲。


疑问句:
Does he intimidate you? 他会吓唬你吗？
What intimidates you about this task?  这项任务中什么让你害怕？


否定句:
He didn't intimidate me; I stood my ground. 他没有吓唬我；我坚持住了。
She wasn't intimidated by his threats. 她没有被他的威胁吓倒。


PHRASE->
intimidate into: 恐吓…去做…
intimidate out of: 恐吓…不做…
intimidation tactics: 恐吓战术
feel intimidated: 感到害怕
sense of intimidation: 恐吓感
