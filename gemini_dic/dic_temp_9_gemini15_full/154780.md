pertinacy:/ˈpɜːrtɪnəsi/|/ˈpɝːtənəsi/| n. |固执；顽固；执拗 |The quality of being stubbornly persistent; the refusal to change one's opinion or course of action despite reason or persuasion.  |例句：Her pertinacy in the face of overwhelming evidence was astonishing.  她的固执，即使面对压倒性的证据也令人吃惊。

例句：Despite the warnings, his pertinacy led him to disaster. 尽管有警告，他的固执还是导致了他灾难性的后果。


近义词：obstinacy, stubbornness, tenacity, doggedness

反义词：yielding, compliance, flexibility, pliability


词源：源自于拉丁语“ pertinax”，意为“顽固的，坚持的”。  "per-" 表示“贯穿”，"tenax" 表示“坚持的，牢固的”。
