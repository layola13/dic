hyperemphasize:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | vt. |过度强调，夸张地强调 | To give excessive or undue emphasis to something.  It suggests an over-the-top or disproportionate focus on a particular point, often to the detriment of other important aspects. |例句：He hyperemphasized the negative aspects of the report, ignoring the positive findings. 他过度强调了报告中的负面内容，忽略了积极的发现。

例句：The advertisement hyperemphasized the product's benefits, making unrealistic claims. 这则广告夸大了产品的优点，做出了不切实际的承诺。
