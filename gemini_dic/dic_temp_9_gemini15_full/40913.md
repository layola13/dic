cooled-down: /kʊldˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 冷静下来的；冷却下来的 |  Describes something that has become less intense or agitated, either in temperature or in emotion.  It is a past participle used adjectivally. |


例句：

The cooled-down lava was still hot to the touch. = 冷却下来的熔岩摸起来仍然很烫。

After a cooled-down discussion, they reached an agreement. = 冷静讨论之后，他们达成了协议。


近义词：calm, relaxed, quiet, temperate, cooled

反义词：excited, agitated, heated, fervent, inflamed


词性:形容词
