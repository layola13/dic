motivates:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪts/ | vt. | 激励；驱动；促使 | To provide with a reason for doing something; to stimulate to action.  | 例句：The teacher's encouragement motivates her students to study harder. 老师的鼓励激励她的学生们更加努力学习。
The desire for success motivates many people to work hard.  对成功的渴望激励许多人努力工作。|近义词：inspire, encourage, impel, spur | 反义词：discourage, demotivate, deter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy motivates employees to be more productive. = 新政策激励员工提高生产力。
His passion for music motivates him to practice every day. = 他对音乐的热情激励他每天练习。


一般过去时:
The speech motivated the audience to take action. = 这篇演讲激励听众采取行动。
The reward motivated him to finish the project on time. = 奖励激励他按时完成项目。


现在完成时:
Her success has motivated many young people to pursue their dreams. = 她的成功激励了许多年轻人追求梦想。
The teacher's positive feedback has motivated the students to improve. = 老师的积极反馈激励学生们取得进步。


过去完成时:
His previous failures had motivated him to work even harder. = 他之前的失败激励他更加努力工作。
The news of the award had motivated her to continue her research. = 获奖的消息激励她继续研究。


将来时:
The upcoming competition will motivate the athletes to train harder. = 即将来临的比赛将激励运动员更加努力训练。
The new bonus system will motivate employees to improve their performance. = 新的奖金制度将激励员工提高绩效。


虚拟语气:
If the prize money were higher, it would motivate more people to enter the competition. = 如果奖金更高，就会激励更多人参加比赛。
I wish that my teacher's words had motivated me more when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻时老师的话能更多地激励我。


被动语态:
The students were motivated by the teacher's inspiring words. = 学生们被老师鼓舞人心的言语所激励。
The team was motivated by the prospect of winning the championship. = 球队被赢得冠军的希望所激励。


疑问句:
What motivates you to get up early every morning? = 什么激励你每天早上早起？
What motivates these young people to work so hard? = 什么激励这些年轻人如此努力工作？


否定句:
The lack of recognition demotivates many talented individuals. = 缺乏认可会使许多有才华的人失去动力。
The boring work did not motivate him to stay in the company. = 单调的工作并没有激励他留在公司。
