casks:/kæskz/| n.|大木桶；酒桶|A cask is a large container, typically made of wood, used for storing liquids, especially wine or beer.|例句：The wine was aged in oak casks. 这葡萄酒是在橡木桶中陈酿的。|The old brewery still uses traditional wooden casks. 这家老酿酒厂仍在使用传统的木桶。|近义词：barrel, keg, tub|反义词：bottle (smaller container)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The winery stores its wine in large casks. = 这家酒庄把酒储存在大型酒桶里。
Many breweries still use casks for fermentation. = 许多啤酒厂仍然使用木桶进行发酵。
