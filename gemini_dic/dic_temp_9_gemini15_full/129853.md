non-imposition:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpəʊzɪʃən/| n. |不强加，不施加，不干涉|The absence of imposition; a state or policy of not imposing something, such as beliefs, rules, or taxes.  It emphasizes freedom of choice and autonomy.|例句：The government's policy of non-imposition on religious beliefs is widely praised. 政府在宗教信仰方面的不干涉政策受到了广泛赞扬。

The principle of non-imposition is crucial for maintaining peaceful coexistence. 不干涉原则对于维护和平共处至关重要。|近义词：tolerance, permissiveness, laissez-faire |反义词：coercion, imposition, enforcement |名词
