misogyny:/mɪˈsɒdʒɪni/ | n. |厌女症，仇恨女性 |  A dislike of, contempt for, or ingrained prejudice against women.  It can manifest as individual attitudes or systemic practices. | Example:  His misogyny was evident in his constant belittling of female colleagues. 他的厌女症表现在他不断贬低女同事身上。

His misogyny prevents him from seeing women as equals. 他的厌女症使他无法将女性视为平等的人。|近义词：sexism, misandry (反义词，针对男性的厌恶), gynophobia (对女性的恐惧) |反义词：feminism, equality, respect for women |
