nonconsumptively:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmptɪvli/ | adv. |非消费性地，非消耗性地 |  Describes an action or process that does not involve the consumption or depletion of resources. It suggests a use that preserves or maintains the resource rather than using it up.  | Example: The data was accessed nonconsumptively, meaning no changes were made to the original files.  数据是以非消耗性方式访问的，这意味着原始文件没有发生任何更改。|近义词：conservatively, sustainably | 反义词：consumptively, destructively
