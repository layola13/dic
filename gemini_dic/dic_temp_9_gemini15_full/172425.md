pycnogonid:/ˈpɪknəɡənɪd/| n. |海蜘蛛；泛指海蜘蛛类动物|A pycnogonid is a marine arthropod of the class Pycnogonida, commonly known as sea spiders.  They are characterized by their long, thin legs and small bodies.  They are found in all the world's oceans.|Example:  The researchers discovered a new species of pycnogonid in the deep sea.  研究人员在深海发现了一种新的海蜘蛛物种。|近义词：sea spider|反义词：无
