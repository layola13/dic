prestandards:/ˈpriːˌstændərdz/| n.|预标准，预先制定的标准|Preliminary standards or specifications established before final standards are set.  These are often used as a guide or framework for the development of the final standards.  They might be subject to revision or refinement before formal adoption.|Example: The company developed prestandards for the new software to ensure consistency during the development phase.  公司制定了新软件的预标准，以确保开发阶段的一致性。|近义词：draft standards, preliminary specifications|反义词：final standards, established standards|名词
