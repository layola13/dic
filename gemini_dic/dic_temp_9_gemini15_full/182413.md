renew:/rɪˈnuː/ | vt.  | 更新；续借；复兴 | To make something new or as if new again; to extend the validity or duration of something. | 例句：The company renewed its commitment to environmental protection. 公司重申了其对环境保护的承诺。

例句：We renewed our vows on our 25th anniversary.  我们在结婚25周年纪念日上重温了我们的誓言。


近义词：refresh, revive, restore, extend | 反义词：terminate, end, cancel | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renews his driver's license every two years. 他每两年更新一次驾照。
The library renews books for a maximum of three weeks. 图书馆最多续借书籍三周。


一般过去时:
They renewed their subscription to the magazine. 他们续订了杂志订阅。
She renewed her passport before her trip. 她旅行前更新了护照。


现在完成时:
I have renewed my gym membership. 我已经续办了健身房会员卡。
The government has renewed its efforts to fight crime. 政府已经加大了打击犯罪的力度。


过去完成时:
He had renewed his contract before the deadline. 他在截止日期前已经续签了合同。
They had renewed their faith in the team after the victory. 他们在取得胜利后重新燃起了对球队的信心。


将来时:
We will renew our lease next month. 我们下个月将续租。
The company will renew its investment in renewable energy. 公司将继续投资可再生能源。


虚拟语气:
If they renewed their efforts, they might succeed. 如果他们加倍努力，他们可能会成功。
I wish I had renewed my passport earlier. 我真希望我早点更新护照。


被动语态:
The contract was renewed for another year. 合同续签了一年。
His membership will be renewed automatically. 他的会员资格将自动续期。


疑问句:
Will you renew your subscription? 你会续订吗？
Does the hotel renew towels daily? 酒店每天更换毛巾吗？


否定句:
They did not renew their membership. 他们没有续费。
The company will not renew the contract. 公司不会续约。



PHRASE->
renew one's commitment  重申承诺
renew one's efforts 加倍努力
renew a contract 续约
renew a license 更新执照
renew a subscription 续订订阅


词源:
The word "renew" comes from the Old French word "renouveler," which is a combination of the prefix "re-" (again) and "nouvel" (new).  This ultimately traces back to the Latin word "novus" (new).
