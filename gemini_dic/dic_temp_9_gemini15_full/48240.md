decontaminator:/dɪˈkɒntæmɪneɪtə(r)/| n. |去污器；净化器；消毒剂|A decontaminator is a person, device, or substance that removes contaminants from something.  It can refer to equipment used to remove hazardous materials, chemical agents, or biological weapons from people, objects, or environments. | The hospital uses a specialized decontaminator to sterilize surgical instruments. 医院使用专业的去污器对外科手术器械进行消毒。  The soldiers used a chemical decontaminator after exposure to nerve gas. 士兵在接触神经毒气后使用了化学去污剂。| purifier, sterilizer, cleaner | contaminator | 名词
