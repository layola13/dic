fabian:/ˈfeɪbiən/| adj. n.|法比亚的；渐进的；稳健的；|Relating to Fabianism, a political movement advocating gradual social reform rather than revolutionary change.  The word can also be used to describe someone who is cautious and gradual in their approach.|例句：He adopted a Fabian approach to the problem, tackling it step by step.|近义词：gradual, cautious, incremental|反义词：radical, revolutionary, hasty
