nevermore:/ˈnevərˈmɔːr/|/ˈnɛvərˈmɔr/| adv.|永不再; 再也不|The word "nevermore" means "no more," "not ever again," or "never again."  It's an emphatic way of expressing the finality of something. It often carries a sense of sadness or despair.|例句：The raven croaked "Nevermore," signifying the speaker's unending grief.|近义词：no more, never again, not anymore|反义词：always, forevermore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nevermore visits his old friends. = 他再也不去看望他的老朋友了。
They nevermore complain about their work. = 他们再也不抱怨他们的工作了。


一般过去时:
She nevermore spoke to him after that day. = 从那天以后，她再也没有和他说过话。
We nevermore returned to that haunted house. = 我们再也没有回到那座鬼屋。


现在完成时:
I have nevermore seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我再也没有见过如此美丽的日落。
He has nevermore felt so lonely. = 他再也没有感到如此孤独。


过去完成时:
They had nevermore experienced such a devastating loss. = 他们从未经历过如此毁灭性的损失。
She had nevermore believed his lies. = 她再也不相信他的谎话了。


将来时:
I will nevermore forget your kindness. = 我永远不会忘记你的好意。
We will nevermore allow this to happen again. = 我们绝不会让这种情况再次发生。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would nevermore have gone. = 如果我知道的话，我绝不会去了。
I wish I nevermore had to face this situation. = 我希望我再也不必面对这种情况。


被动语态:
The mistake will nevermore be repeated. = 这个错误将不会再犯了。
His name was nevermore mentioned in the conversation. = 他的名字再也没有在谈话中被提及。


疑问句:
Will you nevermore return? = 你再也不会回来了吗？
Could we nevermore achieve our goal? = 我们还能实现我们的目标吗？（反问，表示难以实现）


否定句:
It is not that I nevermore think of you. = 也不是说我再也不想你了。(否定否定，表达仍然想念)
I don't believe that he will nevermore try. = 我不相信他再也不会尝试了。


PHRASE->
nevermore again = 再也不
nevermore to = 再也不会...
nevermore shall = 再也不会
from nevermore = 从此以后再也不
once nevermore = 一次也不
