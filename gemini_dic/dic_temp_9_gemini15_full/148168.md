overservices:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪsɪz/ | n. |过度服务；服务过多 |The act of providing more service than is necessary or requested, often leading to inefficiency or wasted resources.  This can manifest in various ways, from excessive attention to unnecessary features in a product to overly elaborate customer service interactions. | Example:  The restaurant's overservices annoyed the customers, who just wanted a quick meal.  = 餐厅的服务过于周到，让只想快速用餐的顾客感到厌烦。|近义词：over-catering, excessive service, superfluity of service |反义词：underservice, neglect, insufficient service |
