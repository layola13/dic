gyroscopically:/ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɑːpɪkli/ | adv. |陀螺仪地；回转地 |  Relating to or exhibiting the properties of a gyroscope; characterized by rotation or spin.  This adverb describes something that behaves or operates in a manner similar to a gyroscope, maintaining its orientation or exhibiting stability due to its rotation.  | 例句：The spacecraft maintained its course gyroscopically. = 宇宙飞船通过陀螺仪稳定地保持航向。


The gyroscopically stabilized platform ensured smooth image capture. = 陀螺仪稳定的平台确保了图像捕捉的平稳性。 | 近义词：rotationally, spinwise | 反义词：unsteadily, erratically

USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It describes the manner in which an action occurs.

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "gyroscopically".  Its use is primarily in technical contexts.
