sera:/ˈsɛrə/| n. |血清；血清疗法|Serum is a clear, yellowish fluid that separates from blood when it clots. It contains antibodies and other proteins that help fight infection.|例句：The doctor prescribed a serum to treat the infection.  医生开了血清来治疗感染。|例句：The scientist developed a new serum to combat the virus. 科学家研发了一种新的血清来对抗病毒。|近义词：antiserum, antitoxin|反义词：无|名词

词源:
The word "serum" comes from the Latin word "serum," meaning "whey" or "watery part of milk."  Its application to blood is a later development, reflecting the similar watery appearance.  The medical usage evolved over time, referring to the liquid component separating from blood after clotting.
