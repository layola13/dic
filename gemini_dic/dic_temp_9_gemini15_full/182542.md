renumbering:/ˌriːˈnʌmbəriŋ/| n. |重新编号，改编号码 |The process of assigning new numbers to items in a sequence.  This often involves changing the existing numbering system. For example, renumbering pages in a document after adding or deleting some content. |Example: The renumbering of the chapters took several hours.  重新编号章节花费了好几个小时。The building's apartments underwent renumbering after the renovation.  翻新后，大楼的公寓进行了重新编号。|近义词：renumeration, reordering|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be maintaining the existing numbering. |名词
