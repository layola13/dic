ninepence:/ˈnaɪnpɛns/| n. |九便士|Ninepence refers to a former British currency unit equal to nine pence.  It was used before the decimalization of the British pound in 1971.  The term is now largely archaic, but may be encountered in historical contexts or literature set in a period before decimalization.|例句：He paid ninepence for the loaf of bread. 他花了九便士买了一条面包。


例句：The old coin collection included several ninepences from the Victorian era.  这个旧的硬币收藏包括几枚维多利亚时代的九便士硬币。


近义词：nine pennies (though less concise)


反义词：无


词性：名词
