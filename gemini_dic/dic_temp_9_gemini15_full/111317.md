livable:/ˈlɪvəbəl/ | adj. |宜居的，适于居住的 |  capable of being lived in; pleasant or satisfactory to live in.  A livable place offers comfort, convenience, and a generally positive environment suitable for human habitation. It implies a level of comfort and amenities that make living there enjoyable or at least tolerable.  |例句：The apartment is small but livable. 这套公寓虽小，但尚可居住。

例句：This city is known for its livable neighborhoods.  这座城市以其宜居的街区而闻名。


近义词：habitable, inhabitable, comfortable, pleasant


反义词：uninhabitable, unbearable, unpleasant


词性:形容词
