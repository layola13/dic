deadliest:/ˈdedliːst/| adj.|最致命的，最危险的|Most likely to cause death; extremely dangerous.|例句：The deadliest snake in the world is the inland taipan. 世界上最毒的蛇是内陆太攀蛇。
例句：The deadliest weapon in their arsenal was a nuclear bomb. 他们军火库中最致命的武器是核弹。|近义词：most lethal, most dangerous, most fatal|反义词：harmless, safe, innocuous

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus is the deadliest threat to human life.  = 这种病毒是对人类生命最致命的威胁。
This weapon is the deadliest in the arsenal. = 这件武器是军械库中最致命的。


一般过去时:
