fueled:/ˈfjuːld/| vt.| 刺激；推动；加剧；供以燃料 | To provide fuel for; to stimulate or encourage. |例句：The rapid economic growth was fueled by increased exports.  迅速的经济增长是由出口增加推动的。|例句：His anger was fueled by years of resentment.  他的愤怒源于多年的怨恨。|近义词：power, drive, stimulate, ignite |反义词：dampen, quell, extinguish |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine fuels the car. = 引擎为汽车提供动力。
Fear fuels the conflict. = 恐惧加剧了冲突。


一般过去时:
The fire was fueled by dry leaves. = 大火是由干树叶引燃的。
The rumor fueled speculation. = 谣言引发了猜测。


现在完成时:
The protest has been fueled by public anger. = 抗议活动因公众愤怒而加剧。
Years of hardship have fueled his determination. = 多年的磨难磨练了他的决心。


过去完成时:
The argument had been fueled by misunderstandings. = 争论因误解而加剧。
His ambition had been fueled by his early success. = 他早期的成功激发了他的雄心壮志。


将来时:
Increased investment will fuel economic growth. = 投资增加将推动经济增长。
This discovery will fuel further research. = 这一发现将推动进一步的研究。


虚拟语气:
If the project were fueled by sufficient funding, it would succeed. = 如果该项目有足够的资金支持，它就会成功。
If his passion had been fueled earlier, he could have achieved more. = 如果他的热情早些被激发，他本可以取得更大的成就。


被动语态:
The debate was fueled by media attention. = 这场辩论因媒体关注而加剧。
The fire was fueled by strong winds. = 大火被强风助长。


疑问句:
What fueled the economic boom? = 什么促成了经济繁荣？
What fueled his sudden change of heart? = 什么促使他突然改变主意？


否定句:
Lack of support did not fuel his enthusiasm. = 缺乏支持并没有激发他的热情。
The low turnout did not fuel the campaign. = 低投票率没有推动竞选活动。
