flip: /flɪp/ | vt. vi. n. |轻弹；快速翻转；突然改变；轻率；无礼；兴奋；激动 | To move or cause to move with a sudden quick motion; to turn over quickly.  |例句：He flipped the coin. 他抛了硬币。 He flipped the pancake. 他翻了煎饼。|近义词：toss, throw, reverse,  |反义词：flop, stay, maintain.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flips the pancake every minute. = 他每分钟翻一次煎饼。
She flips through the magazine quickly. = 她快速翻阅杂志。

一般过去时:
I flipped the switch and the lights went on. = 我拨动开关，灯亮了。
He flipped out when he heard the news. = 他听到这个消息后大发雷霆。

现在完成时:
I have flipped through this book several times. = 我已经翻阅过这本书好几次了。
She has flipped her hair many times before. = 她之前已经多次甩动头发了。

过去完成时:
He had flipped the page before I could ask him a question. = 我还没来得及问他问题，他就翻过了那一页。
They had flipped the car several times before it landed in a ditch. = 汽车翻了好几圈后才掉进沟里。

将来时:
I will flip the coin to decide. = 我将抛硬币来决定。
She will flip out if she sees that. = 如果她看到那个，她会大发雷霆。

虚拟语气:
If I flipped the switch, the light would come on. = 如果我拨动开关，灯就会亮。
If I had flipped the coin earlier, I would have known. = 如果我早点抛硬币，我就知道了。


被动语态:
The pancake was flipped perfectly. = 煎饼翻得非常完美。
The switch was flipped by mistake. = 开关被误拨了。

疑问句:
Did he flip the coin fairly? = 他抛硬币公平吗？
Will you flip the pancake for me? = 你能帮我翻煎饼吗？

否定句:
I didn't flip the switch. = 我没有拨动开关。
He didn't flip out at the news. = 他听到这个消息并没有大发雷霆。


PHRASE->
flip out = 发疯；抓狂
flip through = 快速翻阅
flip side = 反面；另一面
flip chart = 旋转式图表
flip-flop = 改变主意；人字拖
