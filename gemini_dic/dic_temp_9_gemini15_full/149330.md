paces:/ˈpeɪsɪz/| n.|步，步速，步伐|The word "paces" refers to steps taken while walking or running, often used to measure distance or describe the speed of movement.  It can also refer to a rate of progress or development.|例句：He walked at a steady pace. 他以稳定的步速行走。

例句：The project is progressing at a slow pace.  这个项目进展缓慢。|近义词：steps, strides, gait|反义词：dash, sprint, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paces the room nervously. = 他紧张地在房间里踱步。
She paces herself during the marathon. = 她在马拉松比赛中控制自己的节奏。

一般过去时:
He paced the floor all night. = 他整夜在房间里踱步。
She paced herself carefully during the exam. = 她在考试中仔细地控制自己的节奏。

现在完成时:
He has paced the length of the hallway five times. = 他来回走了五次走廊的长度。
She has paced herself well throughout the competition. = 在整个比赛中，她一直很好地控制着自己的节奏。

过去完成时:
He had paced the room for hours before she arrived. = 在她到达之前，他已经在房间里踱步了好几个小时。
She had paced herself conservatively in the first half of the race. = 在比赛的前半段，她保守地控制了自己的节奏。

将来时:
He will pace himself during the upcoming race. = 他将在即将到来的比赛中控制自己的节奏。
She will pace the stage before her speech. = 她在演讲前会在舞台上踱步。


虚拟语气:
If he paced himself better, he would have finished the race. = 如果他更好地控制节奏，他就能完成比赛了。
If she had paced herself differently, she might have won. = 如果她调整一下节奏，她可能会赢。

被动语态:
The distance was paced out carefully. = 距离被仔细地丈量了。
The race was paced to be challenging. = 比赛的节奏安排得很具有挑战性。

疑问句:
How fast does he pace himself? = 他控制节奏的速度有多快？
What is the usual pace of a marathon runner? = 马拉松运动员的正常配速是多少？

否定句:
He didn't pace himself properly. = 他没有很好地控制自己的节奏。
She didn't pace the room; she sat still. = 她没有在房间里踱步；她一动不动地坐着。
