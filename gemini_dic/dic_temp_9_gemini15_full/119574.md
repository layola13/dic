microvillous: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈvɪləs/ | adj. | 微绒毛的 | Covered with or relating to microvilli.  Microvilli are tiny, finger-like projections on the surface of certain cells, increasing the surface area for absorption or secretion. | Example: The microvillous surface of the intestinal lining facilitates nutrient absorption.  肠道内壁的微绒毛表面促进了营养物质的吸收。| 近义词：microvilliferous | 反义词：smooth (lacking microvilli)

词源:
The word "microvillous" is derived from "microvillus," which is a combination of "micro-" (small) and "villus" (hair or tuft of hair).  "Villus" itself comes from the Latin word for "shaggy hair".  Thus, "microvillous" literally means "having small, hair-like projections."
