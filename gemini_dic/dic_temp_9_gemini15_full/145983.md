overdoers:/ˌoʊvərˈduːərz/ | n. | 过度者，做得过分的人 | People who do things to excess; those who exaggerate or go beyond reasonable limits.  |  The overdoers at the party drank far too much and caused a scene. 派对上的过度狂欢者喝得太多，并引起了骚动。 | 近义词：excesses, extremists, fanatics | 反义词：underachievers, minimalists
