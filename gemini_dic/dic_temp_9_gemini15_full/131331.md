noncharacter:/ˈnɒnkærəktə(r)/| n. |非字符，非文字字符|A noncharacter is a character that is not a letter, number, or symbol typically used in written language.  It might be a control character, a formatting character, or a character from an unsupported encoding.  Noncharacters are often invisible or appear as strange symbols.|Example:  Many Unicode code points are noncharacters, used for internal purposes within the encoding system.  许多Unicode代码点是非字符，用于编码系统内部。|近义词：control character, invalid character|反义词：character, alphanumeric character|名词
