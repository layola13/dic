self-estimation: /ˌsɛlfˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 自我评价，自我评估 | The act of judging one's own abilities, qualities, or worth.  It involves forming an opinion of oneself based on one's perceived strengths and weaknesses.  This can be influenced by various factors, including past experiences, social comparisons, and feedback from others. | Example:  Her self-estimation was quite high, leading her to apply for the challenging role. 她对自己的评价很高，这导致她申请了这个具有挑战性的角色。


Example:  Accurate self-estimation is crucial for career success.  准确的自我评价对于职业成功至关重要。


近义词: self-appraisal, self-assessment, self-judgment


反义词:  There isn't a direct antonym, but words suggesting a lack of self-awareness or an inaccurate view of oneself could be considered opposites, such as:  delusion, overestimation, underestimation.


词性: noun
