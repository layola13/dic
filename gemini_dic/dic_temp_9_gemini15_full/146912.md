overinsurance:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊərəns/ | n. |过度保险，保险金额过高 |The act of insuring something for more than its actual value.  This can be due to intentional misrepresentation or simply a misunderstanding of the appropriate coverage amount.  It's generally considered undesirable because it leads to unnecessary premiums and might not provide significantly more protection. |Example:  The business was found to be overinsured after a thorough risk assessment.这家企业经过彻底的风险评估后，被发现投保金额过高。  |近义词： excessive insurance, superfluously insured |反义词： underinsurance
