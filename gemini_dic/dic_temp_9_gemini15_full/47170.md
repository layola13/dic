dawnlike: /ˈdɔːnlaɪk/ | adj. | 黎明般的，破晓似的 | Resembling the dawn; having the pale light or soft colors of the dawn.  The adjective describes something that looks or feels like the dawn, often implying a soft, pale light or gentle colors. | Example: The dawnlike sky was painted with delicate hues of pink and orange. 黎明般的空中绘着淡粉色和橙色的微妙色彩。  The painting had a dawnlike quality, with soft, ethereal light. 这幅画有一种黎明般的质感，柔和、空灵的光线。| 近义词:  rosy, pale, ethereal,  luminescent | 反义词: dark, gloomy, harsh, bright
