puckery:/ˈpʌkəri/| adj.| 皱的；酸涩的；令人不快的 | Having a puckered or wrinkled appearance; also, having a sharply sour or astringent taste.  The word evokes a sensation of tightness and sometimes discomfort. |例句：The lemon's puckery juice made my mouth water.  这柠檬酸涩的汁水让我口水直流。

例句：Her face was puckery from the sour candy. 她吃了酸糖，脸皱成一团。


近义词：wrinkled, shriveled, astringent, tart, sour

反义词：smooth, unwrinkled, sweet, bland

词性：形容词
