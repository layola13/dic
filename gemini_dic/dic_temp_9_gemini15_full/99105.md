intercarotid: /ˌɪntərˈkærətaɪd/ | adj. |颈动脉间的；颈动脉之间的| Relating to the space or area between the carotid arteries.  |The intercarotid space is crucial for surgical access to certain areas of the neck. 颈动脉间隙对于某些颈部区域的外科手术通路至关重要。|近义词：interarterial (if referring to arteries in general)|反义词：intracarotid (within a carotid artery)


USAGE->
This word is primarily used as an adjective, and thus doesn't follow typical verb conjugations.  Therefore, traditional verb tense examples are inapplicable.


PHRASE->
intercarotid body: 颈动脉体 (a chemoreceptor)
intercarotid anastomosis: 颈动脉吻合
intercarotid space: 颈动脉间隙
intercarotid plexus: 颈动脉丛
intercarotid variations: 颈动脉变异


词源:

* **inter-**:  From Latin, meaning "between" or "among".
* **carotid**: From Greek *karōtis*, meaning "deep sleep" or "stupor," referring to the belief that pressure on the carotid artery could induce unconsciousness.
