dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. |注册营养师，营养学家 | A dietitian is a healthcare professional who is trained to assess, diagnose, and treat nutritional problems.  They help people plan and manage their diets to improve their health and well-being.  Dietitians often work in hospitals, clinics, schools, and private practices. | Example:  The dietitian advised her to increase her intake of fruits and vegetables.  注册营养师建议她增加水果和蔬菜的摄入量。  The hospital employs several dietitians to help patients with special dietary needs. 这家医院雇佣了几位注册营养师来帮助有特殊饮食需求的病人。 | 近义词：nutritionist | 反义词： (无直接反义词, 可用lack of nutritional guidance代替) |
