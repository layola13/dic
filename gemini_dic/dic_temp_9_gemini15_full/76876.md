gangrenate:/ˈɡæŋɡrɪneɪt/| v.|坏疽；使坏死；|To undergo or cause to undergo gangrene.  The process of becoming gangrenous; tissue death due to insufficient blood supply. |例句：The untreated wound began to gangrenate.  未经治疗的伤口开始坏疽。 The lack of blood flow caused the limb to gangrenate. 血液循环不足导致肢体坏疽。|近义词：necrose, mortify|反义词：heal, regenerate|动词
