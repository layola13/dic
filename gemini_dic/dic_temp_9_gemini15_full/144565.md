outwallop:/ˌaʊtˈwɒləp/| vt.|彻底击败；压倒性地战胜|To defeat decisively; to overwhelm.|例句：Our team outwalloped their team by a score of 10 to 0.  我们的球队以10比0的比分彻底击败了他们的球队。
例句：The argument outwalloped any counterarguments.  论点压倒了任何反驳。|近义词：defeat, rout, crush, overwhelm|反义词：lose, be defeated
