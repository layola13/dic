fact-finding:/ˈfæktˌfaɪndɪŋ/| n.|事实调查；查明事实|The process of collecting and analyzing information to establish the facts of a situation or event.  Fact-finding often involves interviews, document reviews, and site visits.|例句：The committee conducted a thorough fact-finding mission to investigate the cause of the accident.  委员会进行了一次彻底的事实调查以查明事故原因。

The investigation included extensive fact-finding to determine the extent of the damage.  调查包括广泛的事实调查以确定损害程度。|近义词：investigation, inquiry, research, exploration|反义词：speculation, conjecture, assumption|
