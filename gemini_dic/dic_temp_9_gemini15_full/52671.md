digitule:/ˈdɪdʒɪtjuːl/| n. |小指；指状突起|A digitule is a small finger or toe, especially one of the small projections on the hand or foot of a primate.  It can also refer to a finger-like projection on a plant or other organism.|例句：The primate's digitules helped it grip branches.  灵长类动物的小指帮助它抓住树枝。 The botanist noted the delicate digitules on the orchid flower. 植物学家注意到兰花上精致的指状突起。|近义词：fingerlet, small finger, projection|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a large digit or appendage.
