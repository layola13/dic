etymology:/ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒi/ | /ˌɛtɪˈmɑːlədʒi/| n. | 词源学；词源；语源 | The study of the origin of words and the way in which their meanings have changed throughout history.  It involves tracing words back to their earliest known forms and exploring how their meanings have evolved. | Example:  The etymology of the word "nice" is surprisingly complex, tracing back to a Latin word meaning "foolish." | 近义词：word origin, derivation | 反义词：无
