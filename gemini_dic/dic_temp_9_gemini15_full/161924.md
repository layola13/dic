praised:/preɪzd/| v. |称赞；赞扬|To express approval of or admiration for; commend.|The teacher praised the student for their hard work. 老师表扬了学生辛勤的付出。|近义词：commended, lauded, applauded|反义词：criticized, condemned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He praises his team regularly. 他经常表扬他的团队。
She praises her children's efforts. 她称赞孩子们的努力。

一般过去时:
They praised the singer's performance. 他们称赞了歌手的表演。
The boss praised his employees' dedication. 老板表扬了员工的奉献精神。

现在完成时:
I have praised him many times for his kindness. 我多次称赞他的善良。
She has praised the project's success. 她称赞了项目的成功。

过去完成时:
He had praised her work before the meeting. 他在会议前就赞扬了她的工作。
They had praised the team's effort before the announcement. 他们在宣布之前就赞扬了团队的努力。

将来时:
She will praise the winners at the ceremony. 她将在仪式上表扬获奖者。
They will praise the volunteers for their hard work. 他们将赞扬志愿者们的辛勤工作。

虚拟语气:
I wish he had praised my work. 我希望他之前赞扬过我的工作。
If she praised my efforts, I would be very happy. 如果她称赞我的努力，我会非常高兴。

被动语态:
His work was praised by his colleagues. 他的作品受到了同事的称赞。
The movie was praised by critics and audiences alike. 这部电影受到了评论家和观众的一致好评。

疑问句:
Did they praise your presentation? 他们称赞你的演示了吗？
Has she been praised for her achievements? 她因成就而受到赞扬了吗？

否定句:
He did not praise her efforts. 他没有称赞她的努力。
They haven't praised the new policy yet. 他们还没有称赞这项新政策。
