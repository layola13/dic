jasper:/ˈdʒæspər/ | n. |碧玉，红玉髓 | A hard, opaque variety of chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline quartz, that is typically red, brown, or yellow in color, but may also be green or blue. It is often used as an ornamental stone. |The necklace was made of beautiful jasper. 这条项链是用美丽的碧玉制成的。|agate, carnelian |—|  n.
