coaxingly:/ˈkoʊksɪŋli/| adv.| 哄骗地；恳求地；诱导地 |In a coaxing manner; persuasively and gently.  It implies trying to persuade someone to do something by gentle urging or flattering. |例句：She coaxingly asked him to stay a little longer. 她哄骗他多留一会儿。

例句：He spoke coaxingly to the frightened child. 他哄着受惊吓的孩子。


近义词：persuasively, enticingly, temptingly, alluringly


反义词：harshly, roughly, forcefully, aggressively


词性: 副词
