immured:/ɪˈmjuːrd/| vt.|监禁；封闭；砌入墙内|to enclose or confine within walls; to imprison; to build into a wall|例句：The prisoner was immured in a dungeon.  囚犯被监禁在地牢里。

例句：The ancient relic was immured within the city walls.  古老的文物被砌入城墙内。


近义词：imprison, confine, incarcerate, enclose

反义词：release, liberate, free


词性:动词
