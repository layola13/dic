protesting:/proˈtestiŋ/ | v. |抗议，反对 |The act of expressing strong disapproval or opposition, typically in a public way.  It often involves expressing grievances or objections to a person, policy, or situation. |例句：Students are protesting the increase in tuition fees. 学生们正在抗议学费上涨。

例句：The workers are protesting unfair labor practices. 工人们正在抗议不公平的劳动行为。


近义词：demonstrating, objecting, complaining, rebelling |反义词：approving, supporting, agreeing |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They protest the government's new policies. = 他们抗议政府的新政策。
We protest against injustice. = 我们抗议不公正。


一般过去时:
They protested the decision loudly. = 他们大声抗议这个决定。
The crowd protested the war. = 人群抗议战争。


现在完成时:
They have protested several times this year. = 今年他们已经抗议过几次了。
We have been protesting for months. = 我们已经抗议了好几个月了。


过去完成时:
They had protested before, but to no avail. = 他们以前抗议过，但没有用。
The workers had protested for higher wages for years. = 工人们多年来一直在抗议要求更高的工资。


将来时:
They will protest again next week. = 他们下周将再次抗议。
We will continue protesting until our demands are met. = 在我们的要求得到满足之前，我们将继续抗议。


虚拟语气:
If they protested more effectively, they might achieve better results. = 如果他们更有效地抗议，他们可能会取得更好的结果。
I wish they had protested earlier. = 我希望他们早点抗议。


被动语态:
The protest was violently suppressed by the police. = 抗议活动遭到警方的暴力镇压。
The new policy is being protested by many people. = 许多人正在抗议这项新政策。


疑问句:
Are they protesting the new law? = 他们正在抗议新法律吗？
Why are they protesting? = 他们为什么抗议？


否定句:
They did not protest the decision. = 他们没有抗议这个决定。
We will not stop protesting until we see change. = 除非看到变化，否则我们不会停止抗议。


PHRASE->
protest against = 反对...
protest at = 抗议...
protest over = 就...抗议
in protest = 表示抗议
under protest = 在抗议下
