replenishment: /rɪˈplɛnɪʃmənt/ | n. | 补充；再装满；补充供应 | The act of replenishing something; the state of being replenished.  It refers to the process of making something full or complete again, often after it has been used or depleted. |例句：The company is responsible for the replenishment of stock. 公司负责补充库存。

The timely replenishment of supplies is crucial for the success of the mission.  及时的物资补充对任务的成功至关重要。|近义词：restock, refilling, restoration, renewal |反义词：depletion, exhaustion, emptying
