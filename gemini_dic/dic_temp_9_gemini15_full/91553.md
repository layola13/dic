hyper-intelligence: /ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. | 超级智能，超智慧 |  Intelligence far exceeding the capabilities of ordinary humans.  It often refers to hypothetical forms of intelligence surpassing human intelligence,  potentially achieved through advanced artificial intelligence or future human evolution. | Example:  The development of hyper-intelligence could revolutionize many aspects of society.  超级智能的发展可能会彻底改变社会的许多方面。| Near synonyms:  superintelligence,  transhuman intelligence | Antonyms:  intellectual disability,  stupidity |  n.
