lavages:/ˈlævɪdʒɪz/| n.|冲洗；洗涤；灌洗|The act of washing or flushing out a cavity or wound with a liquid.  It often refers to medical procedures involving the cleansing of a body part or organ.|例句：The doctor performed lavages to clear the infection from the wound. 医生进行了冲洗以清除伤口感染。|例句：The nurse carefully administered gastric lavages to the patient. 护士仔细地为病人进行了胃部灌洗。|近义词：irrigation, flushing, washing|反义词：infection, contamination
