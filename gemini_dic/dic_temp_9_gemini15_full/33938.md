They are characterized by their feathery appendages, called cirri, which they use to filter feed.  | Example: Barnacles are a common example of a cirriped.  藤壶是蔓足类动物的一个常见例子。|近义词：barnacle (though barnacle is a specific type of cirriped)|反义词：None.
