don't know:/doʊnt noʊ/| v. |不知道|To lack knowledge or information about something.  It's a contraction of "do not know".|I don't know the answer. = 我不知道答案。|I don't know where he is. = 我不知道他在哪里。|know, understand |know, understand, be certain|verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I don't know the way. = 我不知道路。
They don't know the truth. = 他们不知道真相。

一般过去时:
She didn't know him. = 她不认识他。
We didn't know what to do. = 我们不知道该怎么办。

现在完成时:
I haven't known him for long. = 我不认识他很久了。
They haven't known about the accident. = 他们还不知道事故的事。

过去完成时:
I hadn't known he was ill. = 我不知道他生病了。
She hadn't known the consequences. = 她不知道后果。

将来时:
I won't know the results until tomorrow. = 我要到明天才知道结果。
They won't know the answer until the test is over. = 考试结束前他们不会知道答案。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't know him, I wouldn't have helped him. = 如果我不认识他，我就不会帮他了。
I wish I didn't know the truth. = 我真希望我不知道真相。

被动语态:
(Not typically used in passive voice as it is a contraction of "do not know," which is not normally used passively.)  The information wasn't known to them. = 他们不知道这个信息。(This uses "known" instead of "don't know")

疑问句:
Don't you know the rules? = 你不知道规则吗？
Doesn't he know the answer? = 他不知道答案吗？

否定句:
I don't know the answer. = 我不知道答案。
He doesn't know how to swim. = 他不会游泳。


PHRASE->
I don't know about that. = 我对此表示怀疑/我不确定。
I don't know for sure. = 我不确定。
How should I know? = 我怎么知道？
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道说什么好。
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
