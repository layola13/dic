melanotype:/ˈmɛlənoʊtaɪp/| n. |黑素型，肤色类型 |A melanotype is a classification system for human skin color based on the amount and type of melanin present.  It's often used in dermatology and research related to sun exposure and skin cancer risk.  The system typically uses Roman numerals (I-VI) to categorize skin tones, with I being the fairest and VI the darkest. | Example:  Her melanotype was classified as III, indicating a moderately tanned complexion.  她的黑素型被归类为III型，表示中等程度的晒黑肤色。|近义词：skin type, skin tone, complexion |反义词：无
