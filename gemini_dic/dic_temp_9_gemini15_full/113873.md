madman:/ˈmædˌmæn/| n. |疯子，狂人 |A madman is a person who is mentally ill and behaves in a dangerous or unpredictable way.  They may exhibit irrational thoughts, actions, or emotions.|例句：The madman ran down the street, shouting and waving a knife.  这个疯子在街上跑来跑去，又喊又挥舞着刀子。

例句：They locked him away in an asylum because he was a dangerous madman. 因为他是危险的疯子，他们把他关进了精神病院。


近义词：lunatic, maniac, lunatic, psychopath |反义词：sane, rational, composed


词源:  The word "madman" is a compound word.  "Mad" comes from Old English *mǣd, meaning "angry, furious, enraged,"  eventually evolving to mean "insane, mentally ill."  "-man" is a common suffix in English, denoting a male person.  Therefore, "madman" literally means "an angry/insane man."
