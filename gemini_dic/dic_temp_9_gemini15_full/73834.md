formidably:/ˈfɔːrmɪdəbli/ | adj. | 可怕地；令人敬畏地；极大地 |  Formidably describes something that inspires fear, respect, or awe because of its impressive size, power, or difficulty. It suggests a degree of overwhelmingness or intimidation. |例句：The fortress was formidably defended. 这座堡垒防御严密。

例句：The task ahead seemed formidably difficult. 前面的任务似乎极其困难。


近义词：intimidatingly, impressively, tremendously, awesomely, powerfully


反义词：easily, weakly, insignificantly, trivially


词性：形容词
