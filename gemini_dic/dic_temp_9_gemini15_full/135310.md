nonpathologic: /ˌnɒnˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 非病理性的；非致病的 | Not caused by or related to disease.  It describes something that is not indicative of or associated with a disease or illness. |


The patient's condition is nonpathologic, indicating no underlying disease. | 患者的情况是非病理性的，表明没有潜在的疾病。

The nonpathologic changes were attributed to aging. | 这些非病理性的变化归因于衰老。


近义词：benign, normal, healthy, physiological


反义词：pathologic, diseased, unhealthy, morbid


词性：形容词
