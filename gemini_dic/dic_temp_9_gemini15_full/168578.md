procession:/prəˈseʃən/| n. |行列，队伍，游行 | A procession is a group of people or vehicles moving together in an orderly way, often as part of a ceremony or celebration.  It can also refer to the act of moving in a procession. |例句：The wedding procession moved slowly down the aisle. 婚礼队伍缓缓地走过过道。

The funeral procession was long and somber. 葬礼队伍又长又庄严。


近义词：parade, march, train, cortege |反义词：disorder, chaos |词性：名词
