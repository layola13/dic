saran:/ˈsæræn/| n. |莎兰（商标名），保鲜膜|A brand name for a type of plastic food wrap.  The term is often used generically to refer to plastic food wrap, regardless of brand.|Example:  I need some saran to wrap up these leftovers.  我要一些保鲜膜来包这些剩菜。|近义词：plastic wrap, cling film|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word, as a brand name, doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical usages like "what" does.  It's primarily used as a noun.  Therefore, grammatical examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
a roll of saran 一卷保鲜膜
a piece of saran 一块保鲜膜
wrap in saran 用保鲜膜包裹
seal with saran 用保鲜膜密封
saran wrap 保鲜膜 (this is often used interchangeably with saran)
