overinvolving: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɒlvɪŋ/ | v. |过度参与；过分介入 |  The act of involving oneself excessively or unnecessarily in something.  It implies a level of participation that is beyond what is appropriate or helpful, often leading to negative consequences. |


例句：

He was criticized for overinvolving himself in his daughter's college applications.  = 他因为过度参与女儿的大学申请而受到批评。

Her overinvolving in the office politics ultimately harmed her career. = 她过度参与办公室政治最终损害了她的职业生涯。


近义词：meddling, interfering, intruding,  intervening (in a negative sense)


反义词：uninvolved, detached, aloof, indifferent


词性: 动词
