inexpertness:/ˌɪnˈɛkspɜːtnəs/| n. |不熟练；缺乏技巧；外行 |Lack of skill or expertise in a particular field.  It often implies a lack of experience or training, leading to mistakes or poor performance. |例句：His inexpertness in handling the delicate equipment led to its damage.  他的操作不熟练导致设备损坏。

例句：The inexpertness of the repairman was evident in the shoddy work. 修理工的不熟练在粗劣的修理工作中显而易见。


近义词：incompetence, unskillfulness, clumsiness, awkwardness

反义词：expertise, skill, proficiency, mastery

词源:  The word "inexpertness" is formed from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") + "expert" (meaning "skilled, knowledgeable") + "-ness" (a suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Therefore, it literally means "the state of not being expert."
