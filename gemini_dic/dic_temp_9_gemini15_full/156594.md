picks:/pɪks/| n. v.|采摘；挑选；选择|英文解析：The noun "picks" refers to the act of selecting or choosing something, or the things that have been selected. As a verb, "picks" means to select, choose, or gather something.  |例句：He picks the best apples from the tree. 他从树上摘最好的苹果。
She picks her clothes carefully. 她仔细挑选衣服。|近义词：selects, chooses, gathers, plucks|反义词：leaves, discards


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He picks flowers every morning. = 他每天早上都采摘鲜花。
She picks her outfits for the week on Sunday. = 她星期天挑选一周的衣服。

一般过去时:
They picked berries in the woods. = 他们去树林里采摘浆果。
He picked the winning lottery numbers. = 他选中了中奖彩票号码。

现在完成时:
I have picked all the ripe tomatoes. = 我已经摘了所有成熟的西红柿。
She has picked a beautiful dress for the party. = 她已经为派对挑选了一件漂亮的裙子。

过去完成时:
They had picked the fruit before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经采摘了水果。
She had picked a fight with her brother earlier that day. = 那天早些时候，她和哥哥吵了一架。

将来时:
We will pick you up at the airport. = 我们将在机场接你。
He will pick a college next year. = 他明年将选择一所大学。

虚拟语气:
If I picked the right lottery numbers, I would be rich. = 如果我选中了正确的彩票号码，我就发财了。
I wish I had picked a different career path. = 我真希望我选择了一条不同的职业道路。

被动语态:
The apples were picked by the farmers. = 苹果是由农民采摘的。
The winner will be picked at random. = 获胜者将随机抽取。

疑问句:
What fruits will you pick today? = 你今天要采摘什么水果？
Which dress did she pick for the wedding? = 她为婚礼选了什么裙子？

否定句:
He didn't pick up the phone. = 他没有接电话。(pick up在此处是“接听”的意思)
She didn't pick any flowers. = 她没有采摘任何鲜花。


PHRASE->
pick up: 捡起，接人，学会
pick out: 挑选，辨认出
pick on: 欺负
pick at: 挑剔，少量地吃
pick and choose: 挑三拣四
