godsons:/ˈɡɒdˌsʌnz/| n. |教子；教男|The plural form of "godson," referring to males who are sponsored in a religious ceremony (typically a baptism) by a godfather.|例句：He is the godfather of three godsons. 他是三个教子的教父。

例句：My godsons are all successful men now. 我的教子们现在都事业有成。


近义词：spiritual son (less formal)


反义词：无


词性:名词
