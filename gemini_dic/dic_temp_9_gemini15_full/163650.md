predebate:/ˌpriːˈdeɪt/| n. |预辩论；辩论之前的讨论|A predebate is a discussion or preparation that takes place before a formal debate.  It often involves strategizing, outlining arguments, and anticipating the opponent's positions.  |Example: The candidates engaged in a predebate to refine their talking points.  预备候选人进行了一次预辩论以完善他们的论点。|近义词：rehearsal, preparation, preliminary discussion|反义词：post-debate, aftermath|名词
