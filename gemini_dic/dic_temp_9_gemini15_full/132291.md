nondefensively:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈfɛnsɪvli/| adv. | 不防御地；不戒备地；不设防地 |  Not in a defensive or protective manner; calmly and openly.  |例句：He responded nondefensively to the criticism, acknowledging his mistakes. 他对批评作出了不设防的回应，承认了自己的错误。

例句：She answered the tough questions nondefensively, showing her willingness to engage. 她不带防御地回答了那些棘手的问题，展现了她愿意参与的意愿。


近义词：calmly, openly, receptively, peacefully


反义词：defensively, aggressively, guardedly, protectively


词性：副词
