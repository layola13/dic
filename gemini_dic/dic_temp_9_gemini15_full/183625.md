reraise:/ˌriːˈreɪz/| vt.|重新提出；再提出；提高价格|To raise again; to raise to a higher level.  This typically refers to raising something that has already been raised once, whether it's a topic, a bid, or a salary.|例句：The committee decided to reraise the issue of funding at the next meeting.  委员会决定在下一次会议上重新提出资金问题。
The company decided to reraise the price of their product after the increase in raw materials costs. 公司决定在原材料成本上涨后再次提高产品价格。|近义词：reintroduce, reiterate, increase, boost|反义词：withdraw, lower, decrease, reduce|动词
