flipped:/flɪpt/ | v. | 翻转；突然改变；抛弃 | To turn something over or upside down; to change suddenly or unexpectedly; to reject or abandon something. |例句：He flipped the pancake. 他翻了煎饼。|例句：Her mood flipped from happy to sad. 她的情绪从快乐转为悲伤。|近义词：turned, reversed, overturned, changed |反义词：righted, corrected, maintained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flips the pancakes every morning. = 他每天早上都翻煎饼。
She flips through the magazine. = 她快速翻阅杂志。


一般过去时:
I flipped the coin to make a decision. = 我抛硬币来做决定。
He flipped out when he heard the news. = 他听到这个消息后大发雷霆。


现在完成时:
They have flipped the house completely. = 他们已经彻底翻新了房子。
She has flipped her hair many times. = 她已经多次甩动头发了。


过去完成时:
He had flipped the script before the play started. = 在戏剧开始之前，他已经改写了剧本。
She had flipped her car before she called for help. = 在她打电话求助之前，她已经翻车了。


将来时:
I will flip the switch to turn on the lights. = 我将要拨动开关来打开灯。
They will flip the house and sell it at a higher price. = 他们将翻新房子并以更高的价格出售。


虚拟语气:
If I flipped the coin, it would land on heads. = 如果我抛硬币，它会正面朝上。
