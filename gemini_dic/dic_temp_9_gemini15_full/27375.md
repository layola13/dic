came-back:/keɪm bæk/| v.|回来，恢复|This word is a phrasal verb formed by the past tense of "come" and the adverb "back". It signifies a return to a previous place, state, or condition.  |例句：He came back from his trip last night. 他昨晚从旅行回来了。

They came back to life after the medicine.  他们在服药后恢复了活力。|近义词：returned, reverted|反义词：left, departed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes back every weekend. = 他每个周末都回来。
She comes back to her hometown often. = 她经常回到家乡。


一般过去时:
The bird came back to its nest. = 鸟儿回到了它的巢穴。
They came back late last night. = 他们昨晚回来得很晚。


现在完成时:
He has come back from his travels. = 他已经从旅行回来了。
She has come back to work after her maternity leave. = 她产假后已经回到工作岗位了。


过去完成时:
He had come back before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经回来了。
They had come back from the store when it started to rain. = 他们从商店回来的时候就开始下雨了。


将来时:
He will come back tomorrow. = 他明天会回来。
She will come back as soon as possible. = 她会尽快回来。


虚拟语气:
If he came back, I would be very happy. = 如果他回来，我会很高兴。
I wish she came back soon. = 我希望她很快回来。


被动语态:
(Not applicable as "come back" is an intransitive phrasal verb)


疑问句:
Did he come back yet? = 他回来了吗？
When will she come back? = 她什么时候回来？


否定句:
He didn't come back yesterday. = 他昨天没有回来。
She hasn't come back from her holiday yet. = 她还没有从假期回来。
