depredate:/ˈdɛprɪdeɪt/| vt.|掠夺；破坏；贬值|To plunder and pillage; to prey upon; to diminish in value.|例句：The invading army depredated the countryside.  侵略军掠夺了乡村。|例句：The constant storms depredated the coastline.  持续的风暴破坏了海岸线。|近义词：plunder, pillage, ravage, despoil|反义词：conserve, protect, enhance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army depredates the villages. = 军队掠夺村庄。
The inflation depredates the value of savings. = 通货膨胀贬低储蓄的价值。


一般过去时:
The Vikings depredated the coastal towns. = 维京人掠夺了沿海城镇。
The drought depredated the farmland. = 干旱破坏了农田。


现在完成时:
The locusts have depredated the entire harvest. = 蝗虫已经毁掉了全部收成。
Years of neglect have depredated the building's structure. = 多年的忽视已经破坏了建筑物的结构。


过去完成时:

The disease had depredated the village's population before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，疾病已经损害了村庄的人口。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
