deportations:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈteɪʃənz/| n.|驱逐出境；驱逐出境行为|The act of deporting someone,  the process of officially forcing someone to leave a country.  It often involves individuals who have violated immigration laws or pose a threat to national security.|例句：The recent increase in deportations has sparked widespread protests. 最近驱逐出境人数的增加引发了广泛的抗议。|例句：Mass deportations are a controversial topic. 大规模驱逐出境是一个有争议的话题。|近义词：expulsions, removals, banishings|反义词：admissions, immigrations|名词
