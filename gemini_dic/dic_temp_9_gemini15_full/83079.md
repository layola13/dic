guru:/ˈɡʊruː/| n. |大师，专家，指导者 |A guru is a spiritual teacher, especially in Hinduism and other Eastern religions.  The term is also used more broadly to refer to an expert or leader in a particular field. |例句：He considers himself a guru of modern marketing. 他自认为是现代营销大师。|例句：She consulted a yoga guru to improve her flexibility. 她咨询了一位瑜伽大师来提高她的柔韧性。|近义词：expert, master, mentor, teacher, guide |反义词：novice, beginner, apprentice
