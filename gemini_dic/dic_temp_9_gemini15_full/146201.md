overexpansively:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænsivli/| adv.| 过分扩展地；冗长地；过于详尽地 |  Describes something done in an excessively detailed or lengthy manner, often to the point of being tiresome or unnecessary.  It implies an overabundance of information or explanation. | Example: He explained the theory overexpansively, losing the audience's attention. 他解释这个理论过于冗长，失去了听众的注意力。|近义词：prolixly, verbosely, diffusively, tediously |反义词：concisely, succinctly, briefly
