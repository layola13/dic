lation:/leɪˈʃən/ | n. |行为；状态；关系；过程；群体|The act of doing something; a state of being; a relationship; a process; a group or collection of people or things.  |例句：The population of the city is growing rapidly.  城市的居民数量正在快速增长。|例句：His explanation of the situation was quite clear. 他对情况的解释非常清楚。|近义词：action, process, condition, group, system |反义词：inaction, stasis


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The population increases every year. = 人口每年都在增长。
The relation between them is good. = 他们之间的关系很好。


一般过去时:
The inflation rate fell last year. = 通货膨胀率去年下降了。
The situation changed dramatically. = 情况发生了巨大的变化。


现在完成时:
The government has implemented new regulations. = 政府已经实施了新的规章制度。
The company has improved its relation with customers. = 公司已经改善了与客户的关系。


过去完成时:
The problem had already been solved before he arrived. = 他到达之前问题就已经解决了。
The relationship had deteriorated before the argument. = 在争吵之前，关系就已经恶化了。


将来时:
The project will require a lot of collaboration. = 这个项目将需要大量的合作。
The situation will improve in the near future. = 情况在不久的将来会好转。


虚拟语气:
If the population were larger, we would need more resources. = 如果人口更多，我们将需要更多资源。
If the situation had been different, the outcome would have been better. = 如果情况不同，结果就会更好。


被动语态:
The problem was solved by the team. = 这个问题由团队解决了。
The situation is being monitored closely. = 情况正受到密切关注。


疑问句:
What is the relation between these two variables? = 这两个变量之间的关系是什么？

否定句:
There is no relation between the two events. = 这两个事件之间没有任何关系。
