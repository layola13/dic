half-consciously:/ˈhæf ˈkɒnʃəsli/|/ˌhæf ˈkɑːnʃəsli/ | adv. | 半清醒地；迷迷糊糊地 |  In a state of being only partly aware;  drowsily or vaguely.  |例句：He half-consciously reached for the glass of water. 他迷迷糊糊地伸手去拿一杯水。

例句：She half-consciously mumbled a response. 她迷迷糊糊地咕哝了一句回应。


近义词：drowsily, vaguely, dazedly, sleepily

反义词：consciously, fully aware, alertly


词性：副词
