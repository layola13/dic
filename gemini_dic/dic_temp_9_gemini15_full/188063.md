roulette:/ruːˈlɛt/| n. |轮盘赌；命运；偶然性 |A game of chance in which a small ball is spun around a wheel and lands in one of numbered compartments.  The player bets on which compartment the ball will land in.  It can also refer to a situation or series of events that seems to be determined by chance. |例句：He lost all his money playing roulette. 他玩轮盘赌输光了钱。

例句：Life can sometimes feel like a roulette wheel, with unpredictable twists and turns.  生活有时感觉像一个轮盘，充满了不可预测的曲折。


近义词：gambling, chance, lottery |反义词：certainty, predictability |词性：名词
