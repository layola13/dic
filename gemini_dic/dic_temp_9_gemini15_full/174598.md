quish:/kwɪʃ/| v.|压制, 征服, 熄灭|To suppress or extinguish (something, especially a feeling or rebellion).| The rebels were quickly quished by the army.  叛乱分子很快就被军队镇压了。|近义词：suppress, subdue, quell, extinguish|反义词：ignite, encourage, incite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quishes his anger. = 他压制住他的怒火。
She quishes the flames. = 她熄灭了火焰。


一般过去时:
The riot was quickly quished. = 暴动很快就被平息了。
He quished his doubts. = 他压制了他的疑虑。


现在完成时:
The fire has been quished. = 火已经被扑灭了。
They have quished the rebellion. = 他们已经平息了叛乱。


过去完成时:
The army had quished the enemy before nightfall. = 部队在夜幕降临前已经征服了敌人。
She had quished her fear before the performance. = 她在演出前已经克服了恐惧。


将来时:
The government will quish the opposition. = 政府将会压制反对派。
We will quish the rumors. = 我们将会消除谣言。


虚拟语气:
If only he could quish his fear of failure. = 如果他能够克服对失败的恐惧就好了。
I wish I could quish my anxieties. = 我希望我能消除我的焦虑。


被动语态:
The rebellion was quished swiftly. = 叛乱被迅速平息了。
The fire was eventually quished. = 火最终被扑灭了。


疑问句:
Can we quish the rumors effectively? = 我们能有效地消除谣言吗？
Will the government quish the protests? = 政府会镇压抗议活动吗？


否定句:
They could not quish the uprising. = 他们无法平息起义。
He failed to quish his feelings. = 他未能压制住他的感情。


PHRASE->
quish a rebellion = 平息叛乱
quish a fire = 扑灭大火
quish an uprising = 镇压起义
quish dissent = 压制异见
quish opposition = 压制反对
