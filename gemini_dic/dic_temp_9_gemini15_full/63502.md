enucleation:/ˌiːnuːkliˈeɪʃən/| n. |摘除；核的去除；去核|The removal of a nucleus, especially of the eye.  This procedure is often performed to remove a cancerous tumor or severely damaged eye. |例句：The surgeon performed an enucleation to remove the cancerous growth in his eye. 外科医生进行了眼球摘除手术以切除他眼中的癌性生长物。

例句：Enucleation of the cells was necessary for the experiment. 实验需要去除细胞核。


近义词：excision, removal, extraction|反义词：implantation, insertion|词性：名词
