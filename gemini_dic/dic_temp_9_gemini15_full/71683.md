flauntier:/ˈflɔːntiər/| adj.|更炫耀的，更夸耀的|More ostentatious or showy.  It suggests a greater degree of showing off than "flaunting".|例句：Her flauntier jewelry was a stark contrast to her simple dress.  她的炫耀性首饰和她简单的衣服形成了鲜明的对比。|例句：His flauntier behavior at the party alienated many guests. 他在派对上炫耀的行为疏远了许多客人。|近义词：showier, more ostentatious, more boastful|反义词：modest, humble, unassuming
