jigsaw:/ˈdʒɪɡsɔː/| n. |拼图游戏；拼图玩具；难题 |A jigsaw is a picture or design printed on a thin piece of material, such as cardboard, that is cut into many small, irregularly shaped pieces which have to be fitted together.  It can also refer to a complex or difficult situation that needs careful thought to solve.| 例句：The children enjoyed putting together the jigsaw puzzle. 孩子们喜欢拼拼图游戏。

例句：Solving this problem is like trying to complete a giant jigsaw. 解决这个问题就像试图完成一个巨大的拼图。

词源:
The word "jigsaw" is a combination of "jig," meaning a device for guiding or holding something, and "saw," a tool for cutting.  The term originated from the tool used to cut the pieces of the puzzle—a jig saw, a type of saw with a reciprocating blade.
