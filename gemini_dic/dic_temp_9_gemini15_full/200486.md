slanderous:/ˈslændərəs/| adj.|诽谤的；中伤的；污蔑的|  Characterized by slander; defamatory; false and malicious.  It refers to statements that are made with the intention of damaging someone's reputation.  |例句：He spread slanderous rumors about his competitor. 他散布了关于他竞争对手的诽谤谣言。

例句：The article contained several slanderous accusations against the mayor. 这篇文章包含了几项针对市长的诽谤指控。


近义词：defamatory, libelous, calumnious, scandalous, malicious, injurious

反义词：truthful, honest, honorable, complimentary, laudatory
