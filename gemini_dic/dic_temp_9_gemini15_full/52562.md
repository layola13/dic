diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːz/ | adj., v. |弥漫的；扩散；传播 | spreading or causing something to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people. | The aroma of freshly baked bread diffused throughout the kitchen. 新鲜烘焙的面包的香味弥漫在整个厨房。The news diffused rapidly across the internet. 这个消息迅速在互联网上传播。| widespread, scatter, disseminate | concentrated, localized |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scent diffuses throughout the room. = 香味弥漫在整个房间。
He diffuses information to the public. = 他向公众传播信息。

一般过去时:
The dye diffused slowly into the fabric. = 染料慢慢地渗透到织物中。
The teacher diffused the tense situation in the classroom. = 老师缓和了教室里紧张的局势。


现在完成时:
The rumor has diffused widely. = 谣言已经广泛传播。
The gas has diffused throughout the atmosphere. = 气体已经扩散到整个大气层。


过去完成时:
The news had diffused before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，消息就已经传开了。
The color had diffused unevenly across the canvas. = 颜色在画布上扩散得不均匀。


将来时:
The technology will diffuse rapidly into the marketplace. = 这项技术将迅速进入市场。
The smoke will diffuse into the air. = 烟雾将会散到空气中。


虚拟语气:

I wish the fragrance would diffuse more evenly. = 我希望香味能更均匀地扩散。
