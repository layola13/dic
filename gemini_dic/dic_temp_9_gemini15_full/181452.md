rejig:/riːˈdʒɪɡ/| vt.|重新调整；改组；修补|To reorganize or rearrange something, often in a minor way.|例句：We need to rejig the schedule to accommodate the new speaker. 我们需要重新调整日程以适应新的演讲者。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, revamp, adjust|反义词：disorganize, dismantle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rejig the marketing strategy every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会调整营销策略。
He regularly rejigs his work schedule. = 他经常调整他的工作日程。

一般过去时:
She rejigged the furniture arrangement in her living room. = 她重新布置了客厅的家具。
The company rejigged its management structure last year. = 公司去年重组了管理架构。

现在完成时:
They have rejigged the website to improve user experience. = 他们已经重新设计了网站以改善用户体验。
We have rejigged the plan several times. = 我们已经多次修改了计划。

过去完成时:
They had rejigged the budget before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前已经调整了预算。
She had rejigged her essay several times before submitting it. = 她提交论文之前已经修改了好几次。

将来时:
We will rejig the team's roles next month. = 我们下个月将调整团队的角色。
They will rejig the production line to increase efficiency. = 他们将调整生产线以提高效率。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rejig the entire project. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新调整整个项目。
It would be better if they rejigged their approach. = 如果他们调整一下方法会更好。

被动语态:
The schedule was rejigged to fit the new deadline. = 日程被调整以适应新的截止日期。
The software is being rejigged to fix the bugs. = 软件正在被修改以修复错误。

疑问句:
Will they rejig the system before the launch? = 他们会在发布之前重新调整系统吗？
Have they rejigged the marketing campaign yet? = 他们已经调整了营销活动了吗？

否定句:
We did not rejig the plan, despite the problems. = 尽管出了问题，我们也没有修改计划。
They will not rejig the website without proper testing. = 他们不会在没有进行适当测试的情况下修改网站。
