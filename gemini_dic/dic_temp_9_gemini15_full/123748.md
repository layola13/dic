moonwalks:/ˈmuːnwɔːks/| n. v.|月球漫步；模仿迈克尔·杰克逊的标志性舞蹈动作|The act of performing a dance move popularized by Michael Jackson, characterized by a gliding effect that creates the illusion of walking on the moon.  It also refers to walking on the moon itself.|例句：He tried to moonwalk across the stage, but stumbled. 他试图在舞台上表演月球漫步，但却摔倒了。

例句：The astronauts took their first moonwalks on the lunar surface. 宇航员在月球表面进行了他们的首次月球漫步。

近义词：glide, slide, shuffle|反义词：stomp, march
