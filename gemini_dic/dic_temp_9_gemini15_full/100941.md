inundate:/ˈɪnʌndeɪt/| vt.|使泛滥；淹没；大量涌入 |To overwhelm (someone or something) with a great quantity of something.  |例句：The city was inundated by the flood.  = 城市被洪水淹没了。
例句：We were inundated with phone calls after the advertisement. = 广告播出后，我们接到了大量的电话。|近义词：flood, overwhelm, deluge |反义词：drain, dry up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant emails inundate my inbox. = 不停的邮件淹没了我的收件箱。
They inundate the market with cheap products. = 他们用廉价产品充斥市场。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain inundated the entire village. = 大雨淹没了整个村庄。
The company was inundated with job applications. = 公司收到了大量的求职申请。


现在完成时:
The coastal areas have been inundated by rising sea levels. = 沿海地区已被上升的海平面淹没。
We have been inundated with requests for information. = 我们收到了大量的信息请求。


过去完成时:
The dam had been inundated before the engineers could repair it. = 在工程师们能够修复之前，大坝已经被淹没了。
The small town had been inundated with tourists during the festival. = 节日期间，小镇已经被游客挤满了。


将来时:
If the river continues to rise, it will inundate the low-lying areas. = 如果河水继续上涨，它将会淹没低洼地区。
The new marketing campaign is expected to inundate social media with advertisements. = 新的营销活动预计将用广告充斥社交媒体。


虚拟语气:
If the storm hadn’t hit, the town wouldn't have been inundated. = 如果暴风雨没有来，小镇就不会被淹没。
If I hadn't accepted so many projects, I wouldn't have been inundated with work. = 如果我没有接受那么多项目，我就不会工作繁忙。


被动语态:
The fields were inundated by the unexpected storm. = 田地被突如其来的暴风雨淹没了。
The customer service department is often inundated with complaints. = 客服部门经常被投诉淹没。


疑问句:
Will the rising tide inundate the beach houses? = 上涨的潮水会淹没海滩房屋吗？
Are we being inundated with more work than we can handle? = 我们是不是被安排了超出我们能力范围的工作？


否定句:
The floodwaters did not inundate the higher ground. = 洪水没有淹没地势较高的地区。
The new product launch did not inundate the market as expected. = 新产品的发布并没有像预期的那样充斥市场。


PHRASE->
be inundated with = 被……淹没
inundate the market = 充斥市场
inundate the country = 淹没全国
inundate the city = 淹没城市
inundate with requests = 充斥着请求
