clonking:/ˈklɒŋkɪŋ/| v.|当啷声，沉闷的撞击声|The sound of something heavy hitting something else with a dull thud.  It often implies a repetitive or continuous sound.|例句：I heard a clonking sound coming from the engine. 我听到引擎发出当啷当啷的声音。

例句：The old ship's engine was clonking rhythmically. 老船的引擎有节奏地发出当啷声。


近义词：clanging, banging, thudding, bumping


反义词：tinkling, whispering


词性:动词
