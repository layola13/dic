passing-along: /ˈpæsɪŋ əˈlɒŋ/ | v. | 传递；转告；分发 | To give or send something to someone else; to communicate information to someone else. |


例句：

He's passing along the news to everyone. 他正在把这个消息告诉每个人。

Please pass along this message to John. 请把这个消息转告约翰。


近义词：transmit, convey, forward, relay, circulate

反义词： keep, withhold, retain

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He passes along the information to his team every week. 他每周都把信息传递给他的团队。
She passes along compliments to her colleagues regularly. 她经常向同事们表达赞扬。


一般过去时:
They passed along the package to the next person in line. 他们把包裹传递给下一个排队的人。
He passed along the gossip he heard at the meeting. 他转告了他在会议上听到的小道消息。


现在完成时:
She has passed along all the necessary documents to the client. 她已经把所有必要的文档传递给了客户。
They have passed along the good news to their families. 他们已经把好消息告诉了他们的家人。


过去完成时:
He had passed along the warning before the incident occurred. 事故发生前他已经发出警告了。
Before the meeting, she had passed along the agenda to all attendees. 会议前，她已将议程分发给所有与会者。


将来时:
I will pass along your message as soon as I see him. 一看到他就把你的消息转告给他。
She will pass along the updated report next week. 她下周会传递更新后的报告。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would pass along your message. 如果我看到他，我会把你的消息转告给他。
I wish I had passed along the information sooner. 我真希望我早点把信息传达出去。


被动语态:
The information was passed along to the relevant authorities. 信息已传递给相关部门。
The package was passed along by courier. 包裹是由快递员传递的。


疑问句:
Will you pass along this message for me? 你能帮我转告这个消息吗？
Did you pass along the instructions to the team? 你把指示传达给团队了吗？


否定句:
I didn't pass along the message because I forgot. 我没有转告消息，因为我忘了。
He didn't pass along the information to the right person. 他没有把信息传递给合适的人。
