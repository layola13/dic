homoptera:/hɒˈmɒptərə/| n.|同翅目昆虫|Homoptera is an order of insects that includes cicadas, aphids, leafhoppers, and planthoppers.  These insects are characterized by their piercing-sucking mouthparts and typically have four wings of similar size and texture.  The classification of Homoptera has been revised, and many of its former members are now classified within the order Hemiptera.|例句：Many insects belonging to the order Homoptera are significant agricultural pests.  许多属于同翅目昆虫都是重要的农业害虫。
