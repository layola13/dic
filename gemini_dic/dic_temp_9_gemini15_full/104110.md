jumper:/ˈdʒʌmpər/ | n. | 套衫；跳跃者；电桥；（英）连衣裙 | A jumper is a garment, typically a knitted or crocheted sweater, worn on the upper body.  It can also refer to someone who jumps or a connecting wire (electrical jumper). In British English, it can also mean a dress. |例句：She wore a wool jumper to keep warm. 她穿着一件羊毛套衫保暖。

例句：The electrician used a jumper wire to temporarily connect the circuits. 电工用一根电桥线暂时连接电路。


近义词：sweater,pullover,dress(BrE) | 反义词：None specifically, depends on context.


词源：源自动词jump，最初指用于跳跃的人或物，后来引申到指套头衫，因为其易于穿脱如同跳跃一般迅速。
