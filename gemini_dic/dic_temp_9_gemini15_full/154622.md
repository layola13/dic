persist:/pərˈsɪst/| vi. vt.|坚持，持续，固执|to continue to exist or occur; to continue doing something despite difficulties or opposition.|例句：The pain persisted for several days.疼痛持续了好几天。|例句：Despite the setbacks, she persisted in her efforts. 尽管遭遇挫折，她仍然坚持努力。|近义词：continue, endure, persevere|反义词：cease, stop, discontinue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He persists in his denial. = 他坚持否认。
The problem persists despite our efforts. = 尽管我们努力了，问题仍然存在。

一般过去时:
She persisted with her studies despite the difficulties. = 尽管有困难，她还是坚持学习。
The rain persisted throughout the night. = 雨整夜不停地下着。

现在完成时:
The symptoms have persisted for weeks. = 症状持续了好几周。
He has persisted in his belief despite evidence to the contrary. = 尽管有相反的证据，他仍然坚持自己的信念。

过去完成时:
The pain had persisted for days before he finally sought medical attention. = 疼痛持续了好几天，他才最终寻求医疗帮助。
They had persisted in their efforts even after repeated failures. = 即使屡屡失败，他们仍然坚持努力。


将来时:
The drought is likely to persist for many months. = 干旱可能会持续数月。
I am determined that I will persist in my search for the truth. = 我决心坚持寻找真相。


虚拟语气:
If he persisted, he might succeed. = 如果他坚持下去，他可能会成功。
I wish he would persist in his training. = 我希望他能坚持训练。


被动语态:
The problem was persisted with for too long. =  这个问题被坚持了太久。(被动语态在此用法较为罕见，通常用主动语态)
The belief in the conspiracy theory has been persistently maintained. = 对阴谋论的信仰一直被坚持着。(强调持续的被动)


疑问句:
Will this problem persist? = 这个问题会持续吗？
How long will the effects persist? = 效果会持续多久？


否定句:
The symptoms did not persist. = 症状没有持续。
He didn't persist in his efforts. = 他没有坚持他的努力。


PHRASE->
persist in: 坚持
persist with: 坚持做某事
persistently: 持续地，坚持不懈地
insistently persist: 执拗地坚持
persistently oppose: 持续地反对
