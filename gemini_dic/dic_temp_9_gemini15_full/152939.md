pediatrics:/ˌpiːdiˈætriks/| n. |儿科学|The branch of medicine dealing with the development, care, and diseases of children.  It encompasses the medical, surgical, and psychological aspects of childhood.  |例句：She's specializing in pediatrics. 她专门研究儿科。

例句：The hospital has a dedicated pediatric ward. 这家医院有一个专门的儿科病房。


近义词：pediatric medicine, child healthcare


反义词：geriatrics (老年医学)


词性：名词
