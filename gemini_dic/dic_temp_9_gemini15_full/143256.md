词源:
The word "outdevil" is a compound word formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "to surpass" or "to exceed") and the noun "devil" (meaning "an evil spirit").  It's a relatively rare word, mainly used figuratively.  Its usage reflects the inherent metaphorical power of associating exceeding wickedness with the devil.
