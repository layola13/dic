chromatic:/kroʊˈmætɪk/| adj.|彩色的，色调的；半音阶的| Relating to or characterized by color; relating to or containing notes outside the diatonic scale.  |例句：The chromatic aberration in the lens caused a blurry image.  镜头中的色差导致图像模糊。

例句：The composer used a chromatic scale to create a dissonant effect.  作曲家使用了半音阶来创造不和谐的效果。|近义词：colorful, multicolored, polychromatic|反义词：monochromatic, achromatic
