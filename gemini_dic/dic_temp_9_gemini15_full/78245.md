geriatrician:/ˌdʒɛriəˈtrɪʃən/| n. |老年病学家|A geriatrician is a physician specializing in the medical care of older adults.  They diagnose and treat diseases and conditions common in the elderly, and also focus on promoting healthy aging.|例句：My grandmother is under the care of a skilled geriatrician who is helping her manage her multiple health issues.  我的祖母正在一位熟练的老年病学家的照料下，他正在帮助她处理她的多种健康问题。|近义词：gerontologist (sometimes used interchangeably, but gerontologists may have broader focus)|反义词：pediatrician (focuses on children's health)|名词
