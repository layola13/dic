nonascetical: /ˌnɒnəˈsɛtɪkəl/ | adj. | 非禁欲的；非苦行的 | Not characterized by or given to asceticism; not ascetic.  This word describes someone or something that lacks the characteristics of asceticism, which is a lifestyle characterized by abstinence from worldly pleasures, often for religious reasons.  It implies a more worldly or indulgent approach to life.


例句：

His nonascetical lifestyle was a stark contrast to his father's monastic existence.  他的非禁欲生活方式与他父亲的修道院生活形成了鲜明对比。

The nonascetical nature of the festival was evident in the abundance of food and drink. 节日的非禁欲性质在丰富的食物和饮料中显而易见。


近义词：

hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, self-indulgent, sensual, voluptuous


反义词：

ascetic, austere, abstinent, self-denying, monastic


词性：形容词
