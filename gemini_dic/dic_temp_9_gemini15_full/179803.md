reexcavate:/ˌriːˈɛkskəveɪt/| vt.|重新挖掘；再次挖掘|To excavate something again.  This usually implies a further investigation or a renewed effort to uncover something that was previously excavated but not fully understood or explored. |例句：Archaeologists decided to reexcavate the site after new evidence emerged. 考古学家们决定在出现新的证据后重新挖掘该遗址。

例句：The team will reexcavate the area to look for more artifacts.  团队将重新挖掘该区域以寻找更多文物。


近义词：re-explore, reinvestigate, reexamine

反义词：bury, conceal, cover


词性:动词
