cowed:/kaʊd/| vt.|使胆怯；威吓；|to make someone feel afraid and lose their confidence|The bully's threats cowed the younger children into silence.  暴徒的威胁使孩子们吓得不敢出声。|He was completely cowed by her aggressive manner. 他完全被她咄咄逼人的态度吓住了。|intimidate, frighten, subdue|embolden, encourage


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The loud noise cows the dog. = 响亮的声音吓到了狗。
Her aggressive demeanor cows her opponents. = 她咄咄逼人的态度吓倒了她的对手。


一般过去时:
The storm cowed the sailors. = 暴风雨吓坏了水手们。
His harsh words cowed the witness into silence. = 他严厉的话语把证人吓住了。


现在完成时:
The constant criticism has cowed him. = 持续的批评使他胆怯了。
Years of bullying have cowed the child. = 多年的欺凌使这个孩子胆怯了。


过去完成时:
The previous defeat had cowed the team. = 此前的失败使球队胆怯了。
She had been cowed by his earlier threats. = 她被他先前的威胁吓住了。


将来时:
The new regulations will cow many small businesses. = 新的规章制度将会吓倒许多小型企业。
I think the presentation will cow him. = 我认为这次演示会吓到他。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't cowed by the task, I would start right away. = 如果我不害怕这项任务，我会立即开始。
If the witnesses hadn't been cowed, the truth would have come out. = 如果证人们没有被吓倒，真相就会大白。


被动语态:
He was cowed by the experience. = 他被这次经历吓到了。
The workers were cowed into submission. = 工人们被吓服了。


疑问句:
Was he cowed by the threat? = 他被威胁吓倒了吗？
Are the students cowed by the strict teacher? = 学生们被严厉的老师吓倒了吗？


否定句:
The challenge didn't cow her. = 这项挑战并没有吓倒她。
He wasn't cowed by their threats. = 他没有被他们的威胁吓倒。


PHRASE->
be cowed into submission  被吓服
cowed into silence  被吓住不敢说话
feel cowed 感到害怕
utterly cowed 完全被吓倒
cowed by the circumstances 被环境吓倒
