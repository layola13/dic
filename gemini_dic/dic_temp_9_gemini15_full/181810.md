reliquidate:/ˌriˈlɪkwɪdeɪt/| vt.| 再清算；重新清算 | To liquidate again; to settle accounts a second time.  The process involves a second round of asset sales or debt repayment, often to address remaining liabilities after an initial liquidation. |例句：The company was forced to reliquidate after the initial sale of assets failed to cover all debts. = 公司在最初的资产出售未能偿还所有债务后，被迫再次清算。

The court ordered the reliquidation of the bankrupt firm's assets to satisfy outstanding creditor claims. = 法院下令对破产公司的资产进行再次清算，以满足未偿还债权人的索赔。|近义词：reappraise, reassess, settle (accounts again)|反义词：accumulate, amass, increase
