nightstick:/ˈnaɪtstik/| n. |警棍|A nightstick is a heavy, short stick carried by police officers as a weapon.  It's typically made of wood or a similar material.  The term is now less common than "baton" or "truncheon".|The officer drew his nightstick to break up the fight.|近义词：baton, truncheon, club|反义词：无
