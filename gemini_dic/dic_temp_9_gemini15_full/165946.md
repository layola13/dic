prep-schools: /ˈprɛpˌskuːlz/ | n. |预备学校|Private schools that prepare students for secondary school or university.  They often emphasize rigorous academics and extracurricular activities. |Many students attend prep schools before going to prestigious universities.|近义词：preparatory schools, private schools|反义词：public schools
