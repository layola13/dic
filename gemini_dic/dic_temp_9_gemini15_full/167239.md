pretermission:/ˌpriːtərˈmɪʃən/| n. |省略；遗漏；疏忽|The omission or passing over of something.  It often implies a deliberate choice not to mention or include something, rather than a simple oversight.  |例句：The pretermission of this detail in the report is significant. 报告中省略了这个细节，这很重要。


例句：The author's pretermission of the crucial evidence weakened his argument. 作者忽略了关键证据，削弱了他的论点。


近义词：omission, neglect, oversight, skipping


反义词：inclusion, attention, consideration


词性：名词
