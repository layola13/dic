hylobate:/ˈhaɪləbeɪt/| n.|长臂猿|A hylobate is a small, arboreal ape belonging to the family Hylobatidae.  They are known for their long arms, which they use for brachiation (swinging through trees).|Example: The gibbon is a type of hylobate.|近义词：gibbon|反义词：无
