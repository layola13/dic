pressure-cooking: /ˈprɛʃərˌkʊkɪŋ/ | n. |高压锅烹饪；压力烹调 | The process of cooking food in a pressure cooker, which uses steam pressure to increase cooking temperature and shorten cooking time.  |  例句：Pressure-cooking significantly reduces cooking time for tough cuts of meat. 高压锅烹饪大大缩短了韧性肉类的烹饪时间。

例句：I prefer pressure-cooking beans because it makes them incredibly tender. 我更喜欢用高压锅煮豆子，因为它能让豆子变得非常嫩。


近义词：pressure cooking (同义词，但通常作为名词短语使用) , speed cooking (快速烹饪)


反义词：slow cooking (慢炖)


词性: 名词
