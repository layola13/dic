sawmiller:/ˈsɔːmɪlər/| n.|锯木工人；锯木厂老板|A sawmiller is a person who operates a sawmill, cutting logs into lumber.  They may own the sawmill or work for someone else. The term can also refer to the owner of a sawmill.|例句：My grandfather was a sawmiller for over forty years.  我的祖父做了四十多年的锯木工人。|例句：The local sawmiller provided lumber for the new school. 当地的锯木厂老板为新学校提供了木材。|近义词：lumberjack, sawmill operator|反义词：N/A|名词
