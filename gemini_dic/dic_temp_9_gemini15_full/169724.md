proctor:/ˈprɑːktər/|/ˈprɒktər/| n. |监考员；监督人；代理人 |A proctor is a person who supervises students during an examination or test to prevent cheating.  Proctors also may oversee other activities to ensure regulations are followed. |例句：The proctor carefully watched the students to make sure no one cheated during the exam. 监考员仔细观察学生，确保考试期间没有人作弊。|例句：The university appointed a proctor to oversee the student elections.  大学委任了一名监督人来监督学生选举。|近义词：supervisor, invigilator, monitor, examiner |反义词：student (in the context of examination supervision)
