fossils:/ˈfɑːsəlz/| n. |化石|The remains or impression of a prehistoric organism preserved in petrified form or as a mold or cast in rock.  Fossils provide valuable evidence of past life on Earth and are crucial for understanding evolution and geological history.  |例句：The museum has a remarkable collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆收藏了大量的恐龙化石。

例句：Scientists discovered many fossils embedded in the rock. 科学家们在岩石中发现了许多化石。


近义词：remains, relics


反义词：living organisms, contemporary specimens


词性：名词
