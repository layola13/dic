deigns:/deɪnz/| v.|屈尊；俯就；赏赐；|To condescend to do something; to deem oneself worthy enough to do something.  It implies a sense of superiority or reluctance on the part of the subject.|例句：The queen deigned to speak to the commoner. 女王屈尊与平民百姓说话。

She deigned to accept our invitation. 她屈尊接受了我们的邀请。|近义词：condescends, vouchsafes, grants|反义词：refuses, rejects
