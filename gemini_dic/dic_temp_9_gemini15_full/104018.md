juicing:/ˈdʒuːsɪŋ/| n. v.|榨汁；果汁；兴奋；活力|The process of extracting juice from fruits or vegetables;  the act of making or drinking juice;  a feeling of excitement or energy.|例句：She enjoys juicing fruits and vegetables every morning. 她每天早上都喜欢榨果汁。|例句：The juicing of the oranges filled the kitchen with a fresh scent. 榨橙汁的香味充满了厨房。|近义词：extracting, pressing, squeezing |反义词：dehydration
