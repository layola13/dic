seamen:/ˈsiːmən/| n.|水手，海员|A seaman is a person who works on a ship as a member of the crew.  Seamen are skilled in navigation, sailing, and maintaining the ship.|例句：The experienced seamen skillfully navigated the stormy seas.  经验丰富的水手们熟练地驾驭着暴风雨中的大海。  The seamen were exhausted after weeks at sea.  水手们在海上待了几个星期后筋疲力尽了。|近义词：sailor, mariner, sailorman|反义词：landlubber
