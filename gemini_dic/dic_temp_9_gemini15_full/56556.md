doming:/ˈdoʊmɪŋ/ | n. |拱起；隆起；穹顶状 | The process of becoming dome-shaped or rounded;  a rounded or dome-like shape or structure.  It often refers to a surface that has a convex shape, like a dome. |例句：The glass was showing doming after being heated.  加热后玻璃出现了拱起。 The doming of the earth's surface created a hill. 地球表面的隆起形成了一座小山。|近义词：arching, bulging, swelling, convexity |反义词：concavity, depression, hollowing
