dismissed:/dɪsˈmɪst/| vt.|解雇；驳回；不理会；|To send someone away; to reject something; to ignore something.|例句：He was dismissed from his job for misconduct. 他因行为不端而被解雇。

例句：The judge dismissed the case due to lack of evidence. 由于证据不足，法官驳回了此案。


近义词：fired, rejected, ignored, disregarded|反义词：hired, accepted, considered, heeded|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher dismisses the class at 3 pm. = 老师下午3点下课。
He dismisses my concerns as trivial. = 他认为我的担忧微不足道而忽略不计。


一般过去时:
They dismissed the employee for lateness. = 他们因为迟到而解雇了这位员工。
She dismissed his apology as insincere. = 她认为他的道歉不真诚而将其忽略。


现在完成时:
The court has dismissed the charges against him. = 法院已经驳回了对他提起的指控。
I have dismissed that idea as unrealistic. = 我已经认为那个想法不切实际而将其抛弃。


过去完成时:
The manager had dismissed the worker before the incident. = 事发之前，经理已经解雇了那个工人。
She had dismissed his warnings as exaggeration before the accident happened. = 事故发生前，她曾认为他的警告是夸大其词而忽略了。


将来时:
The judge will dismiss the case tomorrow. = 法官明天将驳回此案。
They will dismiss the meeting early due to the emergency. = 由于紧急情况，他们将提前结束会议。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the judge would not have dismissed the case. = 如果证据更充分，法官就不会驳回此案了。
If I had known better, I would have dismissed his offer promptly. = 如果我当时更明智，我会立即拒绝他的提议。


被动语态:
The proposal was dismissed by the committee. = 该提案被委员会驳回了。
The student was dismissed from school for cheating. = 该学生因作弊而被学校开除。


疑问句:
Was the case dismissed? = 此案被驳回了吗？
Will the workers be dismissed? = 工人们会被解雇吗？


否定句:
The jury did not dismiss the evidence. = 陪审团没有忽略证据。
They did not dismiss the possibility of a strike. = 他们没有排除罢工的可能性。


PHRASE->
dismiss sb from a job = 解雇某人
dismiss a suggestion/idea = 驳回建议/想法
dismiss a case = 驳回案件
dismiss complaints = 驳回投诉
dismiss fears = 打消疑虑
