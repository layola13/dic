physicalization: /ˌfɪzɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 物理化；实体化；具体化 | The process of making something physical or concrete, or of representing something abstract in a physical form.  It often involves translating abstract concepts, data, or processes into tangible or observable manifestations.  | 例句：The physicalization of data through visualizations made the complex information easier to understand. 数据通过可视化进行物理化，使复杂的信息更容易理解。

The physicalization of the virtual world in augmented reality games provides an immersive experience.  增强现实游戏中虚拟世界的实体化提供了身临其境的体验。


近义词：materialization, embodiment, concretization, instantiation

反义词：abstraction, dematerialization
