conventioner: /kənˈvɛnʃənər/ | n. | 代表；与会者；惯例遵守者 | A person who attends a convention, especially a political convention.  Someone who adheres to conventions or traditions. | 例句：Many conventioners attended the political rally. 许多代表参加了政治集会。

The conventioners debated the party platform.与会者就党的纲领进行了辩论。| 近义词：delegate, attendee, participant | 反义词：dissenter, rebel, nonconformist
