landladies: /ˈlændˌleɪdiz/ | n. | 女房东；女地主 | A woman who owns or manages a property and rents it out to tenants.  A landlady is responsible for maintaining the property and collecting rent. |  She's a kind landlady who always helps her tenants with their problems. 她是一位善良的女房东，总是帮助她的房客解决问题。  The landlady raised the rent significantly last year.  去年房东大幅提高了房租。 |  landlord (for males), property owner, house owner | tenant, renter |
