jiggling:/ˈdʒɪɡlɪŋ/| v.|摆动；摇晃；颤动|To move or cause to move slightly and repeatedly with quick, short movements.|The jelly jiggled on the plate.  果冻在盘子上晃动。|The baby jiggled its legs excitedly.  婴儿兴奋地晃动着腿。|shaking, trembling, wobbling|still, stationary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog is jiggling its tail happily. = 狗高兴地摇着尾巴。
The leaves are jiggling in the breeze. = 叶子在微风中摇晃。


一般过去时:
He jiggled the handle, trying to open the door. = 他摇动把手，试图打开门。
She jiggled the loose tooth until it fell out. = 她摇晃着松动的牙齿，直到它掉出来。


现在完成时:
I have been jiggling this loose screw for ten minutes. = 我已经晃动这个松动的螺丝十分钟了。
The earthquake has jiggled the buildings in the city. = 地震震动了城市里的建筑物。


过去完成时:
The car had been jiggling violently before it finally stopped. = 车子剧烈晃动后才最终停了下来。
She had jiggled the key in the ignition for ages before it started. = 她在点火开关处晃动钥匙很久才发动起来。


将来时:
He will jiggle the lever to start the machine. = 他将摇动杠杆启动机器。
The wind will jiggle the branches of the trees. = 风将摇动树枝。


虚拟语气:
If I jiggled the door handle harder, it might open. = 如果我用力摇动门把手，它可能会打开。
I wish I hadn’t jiggled the table so much; I almost knocked over the vase. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地摇桌子；我差点把花瓶打翻了。


被动语态:
The machine was jiggled by the vibrations. = 机器被震动摇晃了。
The loose tooth was jiggled out by the child. = 松动的牙齿被孩子摇掉了。


疑问句:
Is the car still jiggling? = 车子还在晃动吗？
Why is the picture jiggling on the wall? = 为什么墙上的画在晃动？


否定句:
The machine didn't jiggle at all. = 机器根本没有晃动。
He didn't jiggle the handle; he just pushed it. = 他没有摇动把手；他只是推了它一下。
