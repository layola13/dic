postgenital:/ˌpoʊstˈdʒɛnɪtəl/| adj.|性器后的；性器以外的|Relating to or occurring after the genital stage of psychosexual development.  This term is primarily used in psychoanalytic theory, referring to the period of life following the resolution of the Oedipus or Electra complex.  It's often associated with mature sexuality and the establishment of adult relationships.|Example:  The postgenital phase of psychosexual development is characterized by mature object relations.  性器后期的性心理发展阶段的特点是成熟的客体关系。|近义词：post-Oedipal, post-Electra (in psychoanalytic context)|反义词：pregenital (in psychoanalytic context)
