decembrist:/dɪˈsɛmbrɪst/ | n. |十二月党人；(尤指)十二月起义的参与者 | A Decembrist was a member of a group of Russian revolutionaries who staged an uprising in December 1825.  The term often refers specifically to participants in the Decembrist Revolt of 1825. |Example: The Decembrists' rebellion, though ultimately unsuccessful, had a significant impact on Russian history.  十二月党人的叛乱，虽然最终失败了，但却对俄罗斯历史产生了重大影响。|近义词：rebel, revolutionary |反义词：loyalist, conservative |名词
