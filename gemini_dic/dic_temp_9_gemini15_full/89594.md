hoodlike:/ˈhʊdˌlaɪk/| adj.|像兜帽的，像头巾的|Resembling a hood; having the characteristics of a hood.  The term often describes shapes or coverings that resemble a hood, such as a cowl or a head covering.|例句：The cloud formation had a hoodlike appearance.  云的形状像个兜帽。

The dark, hoodlike cloak concealed her from view.  那件深色的、像兜帽一样的斗篷遮住了她的身影。|近义词：cowl-like, caplike, hooded|反义词：open, exposed
