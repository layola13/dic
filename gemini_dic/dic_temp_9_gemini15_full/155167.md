petunias:/pəˈtuːniəz/| n. |矮牵牛属植物，矮牵牛花|Petunias are flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.  They are known for their trumpet-shaped flowers and are popular garden plants.  They come in a wide variety of colors and cultivars.|例句：The petunias in her garden were a vibrant splash of color. 她花园里的矮牵牛花色彩鲜艳，十分夺目。

例句：We planted several different varieties of petunias in hanging baskets. 我们在吊篮里种了几种不同品种的矮牵牛。


近义词：flowering plants, bedding plants|反义词：无|词性:名词
