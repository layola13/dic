mystifyingly:/mɪsˈtɪfaɪɪŋli/ | adv. |令人费解地，神秘地 | in a way that is difficult to understand or explain; mysteriously. | The magician performed mystifyingly, making the audience gasp in awe. 魔术师表演得令人费解，让观众惊叹不已。| puzzlingly, enigmatically, strangely | understandably, clearly


USAGE->

一般现在时:
It mystifies me how he does it. = 我不明白他是怎么做到的。
She mystifyingly ignores my emails. = 她令人费解地无视我的邮件。


一般过去时:
The disappearance mystified investigators for years. = 这次失踪多年来一直让调查人员感到困惑。
He acted mystifyingly, leaving everyone confused. = 他行为神秘，让每个人都感到困惑。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
