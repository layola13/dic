self-promoters:/ˈsɛlf prəˈmoʊtərz/| n.|自我推销者，自我宣传者|People or things that promote themselves or their own interests, often excessively or without regard for others.  They actively seek attention and recognition for their achievements or qualities.|例句：Many social media influencers are self-promoters, constantly sharing updates about their lives and work. 许多社交媒体网红都是自我推销者，不断分享他们生活和工作中的更新。|例句：The company's self-promoting marketing campaign backfired, alienating potential customers. 该公司的自我推销营销活动适得其反，疏远了潜在客户。|近义词：boasters, egotists, self-aggrandizers|反义词：self-deprecators, modest people|名词
