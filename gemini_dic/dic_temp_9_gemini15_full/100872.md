intrudes:/ɪnˈtruːdz/| v.|侵入；打扰；闯入|To enter or intrude upon a place or situation without invitation or welcome.  It implies an unwelcome or forceful entry.|例句：The noise from the party intruded on my quiet evening.  噪音扰乱了我平静的夜晚。|例句：The climber intruded into a dangerous area without permission. 登山者未经许可闯入了危险区域。|近义词：invade, trespass, encroach|反义词：withdraw, depart, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intrudes on my privacy frequently. = 他经常侵犯我的隐私。
The sunlight intrudes into the room. = 阳光射入房间。


一般过去时:
The dog intruded into the garden. = 狗闯进了花园。
She intruded upon their conversation. = 她打断了他们的谈话。


现在完成时:
The weeds have intruded upon the flowerbed. = 杂草已经侵入花坛了。
He has intruded on my time too often. = 他太频繁地占用我的时间了。


过去完成时:
The rain had intruded upon our picnic. = 雨已经扰乱了我们的野餐。
Before I knew it, she had intruded into my office. = 我还没反应过来，她就闯进了我的办公室。


将来时:
The new building will intrude on the view. = 新建筑会遮挡视线。
He will intrude upon the meeting unexpectedly. = 他会意外地打断会议。


虚拟语气:
If he intruded again, I would call the police. = 如果他再次闯入，我会报警。
I wish he wouldn't intrude on our private time. = 我希望他不要打扰我们的私人时间。


被动语态:
Our privacy was intruded upon by the loud music. = 喧闹的音乐扰乱了我们的隐私。
The garden was intruded by wild animals. = 花园被野生动物侵入了。


疑问句:
Does the noise intrude on your concentration? = 噪音会影响你的注意力吗？
Did they intrude on your conversation? = 他们打断你们的谈话了吗？


否定句:
The building does not intrude on the skyline. = 这栋建筑物不会影响天际线。
He didn't intrude on our conversation. = 他没有打断我们的谈话。


PHRASE->
intrude into: 闯入…
intrude on/upon: 打扰…
intrude oneself: 自行闯入
intrude thoughts: 打扰思绪
intrude into someone's life: 干涉某人的生活
