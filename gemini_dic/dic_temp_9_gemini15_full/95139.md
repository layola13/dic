inapprehensible: /ˌɪnæprɪˈhɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可理解的，难以捉摸的 |  incapable of being understood or grasped by the mind; inconceivable.  The complexity of the universe is often described as inapprehensible.  | 例句：The sheer scale of the disaster was inapprehensible to those who had not witnessed it firsthand.  这场灾难的规模之大，对于那些没有亲眼目睹的人来说是难以想象的。
The artist's latest work is inapprehensible; its meaning eludes even the most seasoned critics.  这位艺术家的最新作品令人费解，其含义甚至连最资深的评论家也无法理解。| 近义词：incomprehensible, unfathomable, inconceivable, inscrutable | 反义词：comprehensible, understandable, intelligible

词源:
The word "inapprehensible" is formed from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "apprehensible," which itself comes from the Latin word "apprehendere," meaning "to seize, grasp, understand."  Therefore, "inapprehensible" literally means "not able to be grasped or understood."
