non-Japanese:/ˌnɑːnˈdʒæpəniːz/|/ˈnɒn dʒæpəˈniːz/| adj.|非日本人的；非日本的|Not Japanese; relating to or characteristic of something other than Japan or its people.  The term is used to describe people, things, or concepts that are not associated with Japan.|例句：He is a non-Japanese citizen living in Tokyo. 他是一位居住在东京的非日本公民。

例句：The restaurant serves non-Japanese cuisine. 这家餐厅供应非日本料理。


近义词：foreign, non-Japanese, alien(to Japan)
反义词：Japanese


词性:形容词
