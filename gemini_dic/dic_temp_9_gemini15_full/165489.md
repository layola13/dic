prelook:/priːˈlʊk/| v.|预看，预读|To look at or examine something beforehand; to preview.|例句：I prelooked the presentation slides before the meeting. 我在会议前预先查看了演示文稿幻灯片。
例句：She prelooked the chapter before class. 她课前预读了章节。|近义词：preview, glance over, skim|反义词：ignore, overlook
