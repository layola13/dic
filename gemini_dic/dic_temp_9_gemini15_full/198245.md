shorting:/ˈʃɔːrtɪŋ/| n. v.|卖空；做空；短路|The act of selling borrowed securities in anticipation of a price decline, hoping to buy them back later at a lower price and profit from the difference.|例句：Shorting the stock market can be very risky. 做空股市风险很大。|近义词：selling short, going short|反义词：going long, buying long|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is shorting Tesla stock. = 他正在做空特斯拉股票。
They are shorting the market. = 他们正在做空市场。

一般过去时:
She shorted the stock last week. = 她上周做空了这只股票。
We shorted the bond market. = 我们做空了债券市场。

现在完成时:
They have shorted that stock for months. = 几个月来他们一直在做空这只股票。
I have shorted several stocks this year. = 今年我已经做空了几只股票。

过去完成时:
He had shorted the stock before the price dropped. = 价格下跌之前，他已经做空了这只股票。
They had shorted the market for a long time. = 他们做空市场已经很久了。

将来时:
She will short Apple stock next week. = 她下周将做空苹果股票。
We will short the market if the conditions are right. = 如果条件合适，我们将做空市场。

虚拟语气:
If I were to short this stock, I would need more capital. = 如果我要做空这只股票，我需要更多资金。
I wish I had shorted that stock earlier. = 我真希望我早点做空那只股票。

被动语态:
The stock was shorted aggressively by many investors. = 这只股票被许多投资者积极做空。
The market is being shorted heavily. = 市场正在被大量做空。

疑问句:
Are you shorting any stocks right now? = 你现在做空任何股票吗？
Were they shorting the market at the time? = 他们当时做空市场了吗？

否定句:
I am not shorting any stocks currently. = 我目前没有做空任何股票。
They did not short the stock last year. = 他们去年没有做空这只股票。
