prestudied:/priːˈstʌdid/ | adj. | 预先学习的；预先研究的 | Having been studied beforehand.  This adjective describes something that has been studied or examined in advance.  It implies preparation and prior knowledge. | 例句：The prestudied material helped him ace the exam. = 预先学习的材料帮助他考试取得了优异成绩。

The prestudied data revealed a clear trend. = 预先研究的数据揭示了一个清晰的趋势。|近义词：pre-examined, pre-learned, beforehand |反义词：unstudied, unprepared, impromptu


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prestudies his notes before every exam. = 他每次考试前都会预习笔记。
The professor prestudies the upcoming lecture's material. = 教授预习即将进行的讲座材料。


一般过去时:
She prestudied the historical context before writing her essay. = 她写论文之前预习了历史背景。
They prestudied the map before embarking on their hike. = 他们远足前预先研究了地图。


现在完成时:
He has prestudied the subject for months. = 他已经预习这个科目好几个月了。
She has prestudied the case files thoroughly. = 她已经彻底预习了案卷。


过去完成时:
They had prestudied the script before the rehearsal. = 排练前他们已经预习了剧本。
He had prestudied the poem for weeks before reciting it. = 他在背诵这首诗之前已经预习了几个星期。


将来时:
I will prestudy the chapters before the next class. = 我将在下节课前预习章节。
She will prestudy the interview questions carefully. = 她将仔细预习面试问题。


虚拟语气:
If I had prestudied harder, I would have gotten a better grade. = 如果我预习更努力，我会得到更好的成绩。
I wish I had prestudied more before the presentation. = 我希望我在演示之前预习更多。


被动语态:
The material was prestudied by the students. = 材料已被学生预习。
The questions were prestudied by the candidate. = 问题已被候选人预习。


疑问句:
Have you prestudied the material for tomorrow's test? = 你预习了明天的考试材料了吗？
Did they prestudy the route before their road trip? = 他们在公路旅行之前预习路线了吗？


否定句:
He did not prestudy for the quiz. = 他没有预习小测。
They didn't prestudy the manual sufficiently. = 他们没有充分预习手册。
