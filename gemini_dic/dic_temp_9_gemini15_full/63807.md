epidermal:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrməl/| adj.|表皮的；角质层的| Relating to the epidermis, the outer layer of skin.  The epidermis protects the body from the environment. |例句：Epidermal cells constantly regenerate. = 表皮细胞不断再生。|例句：An epidermal growth factor is involved in wound healing. = 表皮生长因子参与伤口愈合。|近义词：cutaneous, dermal (in the context of skin)|反义词：dermal (referring to the deeper layer of skin)
