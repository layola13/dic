innocence: /ˈɪnəsəns/ | n. |天真；无辜；清白 | The state or quality of being innocent; freedom from guilt or wrongdoing.  It can also refer to a lack of experience or knowledge, often associated with youth. |例句：The look of innocence in her eyes was heartbreaking. 她眼中天真的神情令人心碎。

例句：His innocence was proven in court.  他在法庭上证明了自己的清白。


近义词：purity, guiltlessness, blamelessness, naivete, artlessness

反义词：guilt, culpability, wickedness, corruption, experience
