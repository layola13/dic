hikes:/haɪks/| n. v.|远足；徒步旅行；增加|A hike is a long walk, especially in the country.  The verb means to increase something. |例句：We went on a hike in the mountains. 我们在山上远足。 They hiked the prices of their goods. 他们提高了商品的价格。|近义词：walk, trek, climb, increase, raise|反义词：decrease, lower


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hikes every weekend. = 他每个周末都远足。
The company hikes its prices annually. = 公司每年都会提高价格。

一般过去时:
We hiked to the summit yesterday. = 我们昨天徒步走到山顶。
They hiked the interest rates last month. = 他们上个月提高了利率。

现在完成时:
She has hiked the Appalachian Trail. = 她已经徒步穿越了阿巴拉契亚山脉。
The government has hiked taxes several times this year. = 政府今年已经几次提高税收了。

过去完成时:
They had hiked for hours before reaching the waterfall. = 他们在到达瀑布之前已经徒步走了几个小时。
The company had hiked its prices before the announcement. = 公司在宣布之前就已经提高了价格。

将来时:
We will hike in Yosemite National Park next summer. = 我们明年夏天将在优胜美地国家公园远足。
The bank will hike its lending rates next quarter. = 银行将在下个季度提高贷款利率。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hike the Inca Trail. = 如果我有更多时间，我会徒步走印加古道。
If they had not hiked the prices, we would have bought more. = 如果他们没有提高价格，我们会买更多的。

被动语态:
The trail is often hiked by tourists. = 这条小路经常被游客徒步走过。
The prices were hiked unexpectedly. = 价格出乎意料地被提高了。

疑问句:
Do you like to hike? = 你喜欢远足吗？
Will they hike the fees again? = 他们还会再次提高费用吗？

否定句:
We didn't hike yesterday because of the rain. = 因为下雨，我们昨天没有远足。
They did not hike the wages this year. = 他们今年没有提高工资。


PHRASE->
go for a hike = 去远足
hike up = 攀登；提高
hike through = 穿越...远足
day hike = 一日游
long hike = 长途跋涉
