reappraisement:/ˌriːəˈpreɪzmənt/ | n. | 再评估；重新估价 | The act of reappraising something; a new evaluation or assessment.  It involves reconsidering a previous judgment or valuation. | Example: The reappraisement of the property revealed a significant increase in its value.  重新估价该房产揭示了其价值的显著增长。 The company's reappraisement of its marketing strategy led to improved sales. 公司对其营销策略的重新评估导致销售额提高。 | 近义词：reevaluation, reassessment, revaluation | 反义词：initial appraisal, first assessment
