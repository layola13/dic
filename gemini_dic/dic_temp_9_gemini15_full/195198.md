The term often describes situations or issues that create mild discord or disagreement within a group, but not necessarily a complete schism. | Example: The proposed policy change generated a semidivisive response, with some members supporting it and others expressing reservations.  The debate was semidivisive, with some strong opinions on either side, but no outright fracturing of the group. | 近义词：partially divisive, somewhat divisive, fractionally divisive | 反义词：unifying, unifying, conciliatory
