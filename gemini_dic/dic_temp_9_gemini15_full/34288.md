clarichord:/ˈklærɪkɔːrd/ | n. |克拉维琴 | A stringed keyboard instrument similar to a harpsichord but with a different mechanism for plucking the strings.  It produces a softer, more muted sound than a harpsichord.  |Example: The composer specified the use of a clarichord in his latest sonata.|近义词：harpsichord, dulcimer |反义词：None specifically, but instruments with louder sounds (piano, organ) could be considered antonyms in terms of volume.|名词
