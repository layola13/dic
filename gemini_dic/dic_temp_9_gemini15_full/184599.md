restowed:/riːˈstoʊd/| v.|重新存放；重新装载|To put or pack (something) in a place of storage again.  This implies a previous storage that was perhaps disturbed or incomplete.|例句：After the storm, the crew restowed the cargo.  暴风雨过后，船员们重新装载了货物。

例句：The librarian restowed the returned books onto the shelves. 图书管理员把归还的书重新放回书架上。


近义词：repack, reload, reposition, restock|反义词：unload, remove, discard|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They restow the supplies regularly. = 他们定期重新存放物资。
The store restows its shelves daily. = 这家商店每天都重新整理货架。


一般过去时:
He restowed the luggage in the trunk. = 他把行李重新放进后备箱。
She restowed the documents in the filing cabinet. = 她把文件重新放回文件柜。


现在完成时:
They have restowed all the damaged goods. = 他们已经重新存放了所有损坏的货物。
We have restowed the equipment in the warehouse. = 我们已经把设备重新存放进仓库。


过去完成时:
The captain had restowed the sails before the storm hit. = 船长在暴风雨来临之前已经重新收好船帆。
They had restowed the paintings in the vault before the museum closed. = 博物馆关闭前，他们已将画作重新存放于金库。


将来时:
We will restow the furniture after the renovation. = 装修后我们将重新摆放家具。
They will restow the archives in a new facility. = 他们将在新的设施中重新存放档案。


虚拟语气:
If we had more space, we would restow the items more efficiently. = 如果我们有更多空间，我们会更有效率地重新存放物品。
I wish I had restowed the tools properly. = 我真希望我把工具放好。


被动语态:
The cargo was restowed after inspection. = 货物经过检查后重新装载了。

疑问句:

否定句:
