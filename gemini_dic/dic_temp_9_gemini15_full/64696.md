escapisms:/ɪˈskeɪpɪzəm/| n.|逃避现实；逃避主义|The act of escaping from unpleasant realities, often through fantasy or other forms of distraction.  It refers to the tendency to seek refuge in fantasy or distraction rather than confronting problems.|例句：Her love of romance novels was a form of escapism.  她对言情小说的热爱是一种逃避现实的方式。|例句：Many people turn to escapism during times of stress. 许多人在压力大的时候会转向逃避现实。|近义词：evasion, avoidance, flight, refuge|反义词：confrontation, realism, facing reality|词性：名词
