defragment:/ˌdiːˈfræɡmɛnt/| vt.|磁盘碎片整理|To reorganize the files on a hard disk drive so that they are stored in contiguous locations, improving access speed and performance.  This process eliminates fragmentation, where files are scattered in non-contiguous blocks across the disk.|例句：You should defragment your hard drive regularly to improve performance. 你应该定期整理硬盘碎片以提高性能。
例句：The system automatically defragments the hard drive overnight. 系统会在夜间自动整理硬盘碎片。|近义词：optimize, consolidate|反义词：fragment
