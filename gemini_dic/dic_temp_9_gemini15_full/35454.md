co-workers: /ˈkoʊwɜːrkərz/ | n. |同事们 | People who work together at the same workplace.  Co-workers share a common work environment and often collaborate on projects or tasks. | Example: I get along well with most of my co-workers. 我和大多数同事相处融洽。  |近义词: colleagues, associates, workmates |反义词: N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My co-workers often help each other. = 我的同事们经常互相帮助。
We discuss work-related problems with our co-workers. = 我们与同事讨论工作相关的问题。

一般过去时:
My co-workers celebrated my birthday last week. = 我的同事们上周庆祝了我的生日。
We helped our co-workers move last month. = 我们上个月帮助我们的同事搬家。

现在完成时:
I have worked with these co-workers for five years. = 我和这些同事一起工作了五年。
We have known our co-workers for a long time. = 我们认识我们的同事很久了。

过去完成时:
I had already met some of my co-workers before the first day. = 我在第一天之前就已经认识了一些同事。
We had completed the project before our co-workers arrived. = 我们在同事到达之前就已经完成了项目。

将来时:
I will meet my new co-workers tomorrow. = 我明天将要见我的新同事。
We will celebrate our team success with our co-workers next week. = 我们下周将与同事一起庆祝团队的成功。

虚拟语气:
If I had better co-workers, I would be happier. = 如果我有更好的同事，我会更快乐。
I wish my co-workers were more supportive. = 我希望我的同事更支持我。

被动语态:
The project was completed by my co-workers and me. = 项目由我和我的同事完成。
The problem was solved effectively by our co-workers. = 问题被我们的同事有效地解决了。

疑问句:
Do you get along with your co-workers? = 你和你的同事相处得好吗？
Have you introduced yourself to your new co-workers? = 你已经向你的新同事介绍自己了吗？

否定句:
I don't know all of my co-workers' names yet. = 我还不知道所有同事的名字。
We didn't have many co-workers in our previous company. = 我们在之前的公司没有很多同事。
