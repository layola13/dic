harre:/hɑːr/| v.|  使粗暴对待；骚扰；折磨 | to harass or annoy persistently; to torment | 例句：The bully harried the smaller children.  = 那个恶霸骚扰那些更小的孩子。 | 例句：The constant demands harried him into a state of exhaustion. =  不断的需求使他疲惫不堪。| 近义词：persecute, badger, pester | 反义词：soothe, comfort, appease | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harries his employees with excessive demands. = 他用过多的要求骚扰他的员工。
They constantly harry the neighbors with loud music. = 他们不断地用大声的音乐骚扰邻居。

一般过去时:
The soldiers harried the retreating enemy. = 士兵骚扰撤退的敌人。
She harried him with questions all evening. = 她整个晚上都在用问题烦他。


现在完成时:
The boss has harried the staff for weeks with unrealistic deadlines. = 老板几周来一直用不切实际的期限骚扰员工。
They have harried the local wildlife with their noisy activities. = 他们的吵闹活动已经骚扰了当地的野生动物。


过去完成时:
The villagers had been harried by bandits for months before the army arrived. = 在军队到来之前，村民们几个月来一直受到土匪的骚扰。
He had harried himself with worry before the exam. = 考试前，他一直为考试担心。

将来时:
The new regulations will harry small businesses with excessive paperwork. = 新规章将用过多的文书工作来骚扰小型企业。
She fears that her creditors will harry her with demands for payment. = 她担心她的债权人会向她索要付款。

虚拟语气:
If they harried us any more, we would have to complain. = 如果他们再骚扰我们，我们就必须投诉。
I wish they wouldn't harry me with trivial matters. = 我希望他们不要用琐事来烦我。


被动语态:
The villagers were harried by the invading army. = 村民们受到入侵军队的骚扰。
He felt harried by the pressure of his job. = 他感到工作压力很大。


疑问句:
Are you being harried by your colleagues? = 你是否受到同事的骚扰？
Was he harried by the police? = 他是否受到警方的骚扰？


否定句:
The police did not harry the suspect. = 警方没有骚扰嫌疑人。
They will not harry us with their demands. = 他们不会用他们的要求来骚扰我们。
