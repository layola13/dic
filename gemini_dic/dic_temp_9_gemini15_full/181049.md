reinclude:/ˌriːɪnˈkluːd/| vt.|重新包含，再次包含|To include again or anew.  This word is formed by the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "include." It implies a previous inclusion that was perhaps removed or overlooked and is now being added back.|例句：The revised edition reincludes the chapter that was mistakenly omitted.  修订版重新收录了被错误省略的那一章。

The company decided to reinclude the feature after receiving customer feedback. 公司在收到客户反馈后决定重新加入该功能。

|近义词：reincorporate, reinsert|反义词：exclude, omit|动词
