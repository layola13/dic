on-the-go:/ˌɑːnðəˈɡoʊ/ (美式)/ˌɒnðəˈɡəʊ/ (英式)| adj.|忙碌的；四处奔波的；随时待命的|Busy, active, and always moving or traveling.|例句：She's always on the go, juggling work and family. 她总是很忙碌，忙于工作和家庭。


例句：He prefers on-the-go snacks to full meals. 他更喜欢随身携带的零食而不是正餐。


近义词：busy, active, mobile, in transit


反义词：stationary, idle, inactive, at rest


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always on the go. = 他总是很忙碌。
She is on the go all day. = 她整天都在忙碌。


一般过去时:
They were on the go all weekend. = 他们整个周末都很忙碌。
He was on the go preparing for the presentation. = 他一直在为演示做准备而忙碌。


现在完成时:
I have been on the go since morning. = 我从早上就一直在忙碌。
She has been on the go for years running her business. = 她经营她的生意多年来一直很忙碌。


过去完成时:
They had been on the go for hours before they finally rested. = 他们忙碌了好几个小时才最终休息。
We had been on the go all day and were exhausted. = 我们整天都在忙碌，精疲力尽了。


将来时:
I will be on the go all next week. = 下周我将一直很忙碌。
They will be on the go attending several conferences. = 他们将忙于参加几次会议。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't always on the go, I would have more time for hobbies. = 如果我不总是很忙碌，我就会有更多时间从事爱好。
I wish I weren't always on the go. = 我希望我不总是那么忙碌。


被动语态: (This adjective doesn't typically use passive voice)


疑问句:
Are you always on the go? = 你总是很忙碌吗？
Is she on the go today? = 她今天很忙碌吗？


否定句:
He isn't on the go today.  = 他今天不忙。
They weren't on the go this weekend. = 他们这个周末不忙。
