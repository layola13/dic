fur:/fɜːr/| n.|毛皮，兽皮|The soft, hairy coat of a mammal.  Fur is often used for clothing and other products.|例句：The coat is made of luxurious fox fur. 这件外套是用奢华的狐狸毛皮制成的。|例句：She brushed the fur from her cat. 她刷掉了她猫身上的毛。|近义词：pelage, fleece|反义词：skin (without fur)
