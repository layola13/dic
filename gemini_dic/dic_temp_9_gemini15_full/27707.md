candy-striped: /ˈkændiˌstraɪpt/ | adj. | 条纹糖果色的；彩条的 | Having stripes in colors reminiscent of candy.  The term typically evokes a cheerful, playful, and somewhat retro visual effect, often using red and white stripes. |例句：The candy-striped beach umbrella provided a splash of color on the sand.  那把彩条遮阳伞为沙滩增添了一抹色彩。

The candy-striped shirt was perfect for a summer picnic. 那件条纹糖果色衬衫非常适合夏日野餐。


近义词：striped, banded, striped (with candy colors) | 反义词：unstriped, plain, solid


词源:  The word is a compound of "candy" and "striped."  "Candy" refers to the colorful, sweet confectionery, and "striped" describes the pattern of parallel lines or bands of color. The combination suggests a visual similarity to the colorful stripes found on some candies.
