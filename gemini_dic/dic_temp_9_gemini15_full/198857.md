siderosis:/ˌsaɪdəˈroʊsɪs/| n. |铁沉着症，矽肺病|A condition caused by the deposition of iron-containing dust in the lungs, often associated with prolonged exposure to iron-containing dust in certain occupations.  It can lead to varying degrees of pulmonary fibrosis and respiratory impairment. |Example:  The prolonged exposure to iron dust in the mine resulted in siderosis in several workers.  长期暴露于矿井中的铁尘导致几名工人患上了铁沉着症。|近义词：haemosiderosis (if related to blood), pneumoconiosis|反义词：N/A
