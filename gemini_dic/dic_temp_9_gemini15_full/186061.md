rewriter:/ˈriːraɪtər/| n. |改写者，润色者|A rewriter is a person who rewrites text, often to improve its clarity, style, or accuracy.  They may also adapt text for different audiences or purposes. |Example: The editor hired a skilled rewriter to polish the manuscript before publication.  编辑聘请了一位熟练的改写者在出版前润色手稿。|近义词：editor, reviser, writer, paraphraser |反义词：original author (in some contexts)
