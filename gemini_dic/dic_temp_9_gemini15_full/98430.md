inspectional:/ɪnˈspɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 检查的；视察的；检验的 | Relating to or involving inspection.  The adjective "inspectional" describes something connected with the act of inspecting or examining something carefully. | Example: The inspectional process was thorough and meticulous.  检查过程彻底而细致。  The building underwent a rigorous inspectional review before opening. 这栋建筑在开放前进行了严格的检查审查。 | 近义词：examining, supervisory, scrutinizing | 反义词：neglectful, careless
