patios:/ˈpætiˌoʊz/| n.|天井；露台；庭院|An outdoor area, typically paved, adjacent to a house, used for dining or relaxing.|例句：We had a lovely dinner on the patio. 我们在天井上吃了一顿美味的晚餐。|例句：The hotel boasts a beautiful patio overlooking the ocean. 这家酒店拥有一个美丽的俯瞰大海的天井。|近义词：courtyard, terrace, deck|反义词：interior, inside
