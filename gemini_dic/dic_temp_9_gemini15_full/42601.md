counterprotest:/ˈkaʊntərˌproʊtest/| n.|反抗议，对立抗议|A demonstration or protest held in opposition to another protest.|例句：The planned anti-war protest was met with a large counterprotest from pro-war groups.  一场计划中的反战抗议活动遭到了来自亲战团体的强烈反抗议。
例句：Several counterprotests took place near the main demonstration, causing significant traffic disruption. 在主要示威活动附近发生了多起反抗议活动，造成了严重的交通中断。|近义词：demonstration, opposition, counter-demonstration|反义词：support, endorsement, approval|名词
