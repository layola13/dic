revolutionaries:/ˌrevəˈluːʃənɛriz/ | n. |革命者，革命分子 | People who actively participate in or support a revolution.  They aim to overthrow an existing system or government and establish a new one, often through radical or violent means. | Example: The revolutionaries stormed the Bastille. 革命者们攻占了巴士底狱。  The revolutionaries fought for a more equitable society. 革命者们为一个更加公平的社会而战。|近义词：insurgents, rebels, radicals |反义词：conservatives, loyalists, reactionaries |名词
