links'/lɪŋks/| n. |链接，联系；关系；环节|Links are connections between things.  Links can refer to hyperlinks on the internet, or to connections between ideas, people, or events.|例句：The website contains many useful links.  这个网站包含许多有用的链接。|例句：There are strong links between diet and health. 饮食和健康之间有密切的联系。|近义词：connections, relationships, ties |反义词：disconnections, separations
