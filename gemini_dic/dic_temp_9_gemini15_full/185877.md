revokes:/rɪˈvoʊks/| vt.|撤销；废除；取消|To make (a law, decision, or agreement) no longer valid; to take away (a privilege, right, or license).|例句：The government revoked his driving license. 政府吊销了他的驾驶执照。

例句：The court revoked the previous ruling. 法院撤销了之前的裁决。


近义词：cancel, annul, rescind, repeal|反义词：grant, approve, ratify, confirm|动词
