monde:/mɔ̃d/| n.|世界，社会|The word "monde" refers to the world, especially the social world or the fashionable world.  It often implies a particular social sphere or a sophisticated, elegant society. |例句：The Parisian monde is known for its elegance and sophistication. 巴黎上流社会以其优雅和精致而闻名。  The literary monde was abuzz with the news of the award. 文学界对获奖的消息一片哗然。|近义词：world, society, sphere, circles|反义词：nothingness, void


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The world turns. = 世界在转动。
This society thrives on gossip. = 这个社会靠八卦兴盛。


一般过去时:
The world changed dramatically after the war. = 战后世界发生了巨大的变化。
High society celebrated lavishly. = 上流社会举行了盛大的庆祝活动。


现在完成时:
The world has seen many changes. = 世界经历了许多变化。
Society has become more accepting of diverse lifestyles. = 社会对多元化的生活方式更加包容。


过去完成时:
The world had already changed significantly before he realized it. = 在他意识到之前，世界已经发生了巨大的变化。
The social circles had already fractured before the scandal. = 在丑闻爆发之前，社交圈就已经破裂了。


将来时:
The world will continue to evolve. = 世界将继续发展。
Society will likely face new challenges in the future. = 社会未来可能会面临新的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If the world were a better place, there would be less suffering. = 如果世界更好，痛苦就会减少。
If society were more just, inequality would be reduced. = 如果社会更公正，不平等就会减少。


被动语态:
The world is shaped by its inhabitants. = 世界是由其居民塑造的。
Society is influenced by many factors. = 社会受到许多因素的影响。


疑问句:
What does the world need now? = 世界现在需要什么？
How does this society function? = 这个社会是如何运作的？


否定句:
The world is not always fair. = 世界并非总是公平的。
This society does not tolerate discrimination. = 这个社会不容忍歧视。


PHRASE->
tout le monde = everyone  (法语，意为“所有人”)
le monde entier = the whole world (法语，意为“全世界”)
monde professionnel = professional world (法语，意为“职业世界”)
jet set monde = the jet-set world (意为“上流社会”)
monde politique = the political world (法语，意为“政界”)
