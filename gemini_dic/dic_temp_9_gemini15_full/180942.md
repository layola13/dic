reillustrate:/ˌriːˈɪləstreɪt/| vt.|重新举例说明；再次阐明；重新图解|To illustrate again or anew; to explain or clarify something again with examples or illustrations.|例句：Let me reillustrate the point with a different example. 让我用不同的例子来重新解释这一点。

例句：The professor decided to reillustrate the complex theory with simpler diagrams. 教授决定用更简单的图表来重新解释这个复杂的理论。


近义词：reexplain, clarify, elucidate, exemplify

反义词：obscure, confuse


词性：动词
