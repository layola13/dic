descants:/dɪˈskænts/ | n. v. |  详述；高音独唱；随想曲 |  A descant is a melody sung or played above a given melody, often in a more elaborate or ornamented style. It can also refer to a detailed discussion or discourse.  | 例句：The soprano sang a beautiful descant over the organ's hymn. 女高音在管风琴的圣歌之上唱了一段优美的华彩乐段。

The professor's lecture was a descant on the complexities of quantum physics. 教授的演讲详细阐述了量子物理学的复杂性。 | 近义词：discourse, elaboration, embellishment, improvisation, variation | 反义词：summary, brevity, condensation
