energetic:/ˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/ | adj. |精力充沛的，充满活力的| full of energy; vigorous and active.  |例句：She's a very energetic young woman. 她是一位非常精力充沛的年轻女子。
例句：The energetic music got everyone dancing.  充满活力的音乐让每个人都跳起了舞。|近义词：vigorous, dynamic, lively, spirited |反义词：lethargic, sluggish, inactive, passive
