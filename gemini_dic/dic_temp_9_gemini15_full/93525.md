images:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪz/ | n. |图像；形象；映像 |Pictures or representations of something.  Images can be photographs, drawings, paintings, or even mental pictures.  |例句：The images on the website were stunning. 网站上的图片非常惊艳。

例句：The artist's images captured the essence of the city. 艺术家的作品捕捉到了这座城市的精髓。


近义词：pictures, photos, illustrations, representations | 反义词：None (antonyms depend on context; e.g.,  reality if referring to mental images) | 词性:n.
