re-charting:/ˌriːˈtʃɑːrtɪŋ/ | v. |重新绘制图表；重新规划；重新制定图表 | To create a new chart or graph, often based on updated or revised data; to replan or reorganize something systematically, often using a visual representation.  | Example:  The company is re-charting its marketing strategy after the recent downturn. 公司在最近的经济低迷后正在重新规划其营销策略。 | 近义词:remapping, replanning, restructuring | 反义词: maintaining the status quo


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are re-charting the course of the project. = 他们正在重新规划项目的进程。
We re-chart the sales figures every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新绘制销售数据图表。

一般过去时:
The team re-charted the company's growth trajectory. = 团队重新绘制了公司的增长轨迹图。
She re-charted her life after the divorce. = 她离婚后重新规划了自己的生活。


现在完成时:
They have re-charted the entire marketing campaign. = 他们已经重新规划了整个营销活动。
The scientists have re-charted the geological formations. = 科学家们已经重新绘制了地质构造图。

过去完成时:
They had re-charted their escape route before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经重新规划了逃生路线。
The cartographers had re-charted the coastline after the earthquake. = 地震后，制图员们重新绘制了海岸线。


将来时:
We will re-chart the company's future direction next month. = 我们下个月将重新规划公司的未来方向。
They will re-chart the patient's progress following the surgery. = 手术后，他们将重新绘制病人的恢复进度图。

虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we would re-chart the entire process. = 如果我们有更多的数据，我们会重新规划整个流程。
It's time we re-charted our course if we want to succeed. = 如果我们想成功，就该重新规划我们的路线了。


被动语态:
The market trends were re-charted by the analysts. = 市场趋势被分析师重新绘制图表了。
The company's strategy is being re-charted by the new CEO. = 公司的战略正在由新任首席执行官重新规划。

疑问句:
Will they re-chart the project timeline? = 他们会重新规划项目时间表吗？
Are you re-charting the sales data for this year? = 你正在重新绘制今年的销售数据图表吗？


否定句:
We did not re-chart the financial reports this time. = 我们这次没有重新绘制财务报表。
They will not re-chart the entire process unless necessary. = 除非必要，他们不会重新规划整个流程。
