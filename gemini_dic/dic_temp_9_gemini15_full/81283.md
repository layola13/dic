grantee:/ˌɡrænˈtiː/| n. |受赠人，受让人|A person or organization that receives a grant or something granted.  The grantee is legally bound by the terms and conditions of the grant.|例句：The grantee must submit a progress report annually. 受赠人必须每年提交进度报告。 The grantee of the patent is responsible for its commercialization. 专利受让人负责其商业化。|近义词：recipient, beneficiary |反义词：grantor


USAGE->

The grantee receives funding for their research. = 受赠人获得研究经费。
The grantee submits regular reports. = 受赠人提交定期报告。


一般过去时:
The grantee received the grant last year. = 受赠人去年获得了拨款。
