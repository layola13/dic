setiger:/ˈsɛtɪdʒər/| n.|多毛动物|A setiger is an organism, typically a marine invertebrate, that possesses setae (bristles or hairs).  These setae can serve various functions, including locomotion, feeding, and sensory perception.  Setigers are found in a variety of marine environments and are characterized by their segmented bodies and the presence of numerous setae. |例句：Many polychaetes are setigers, using their setae for swimming and burrowing.  许多多毛类动物是多毛动物，利用它们的刚毛游泳和掘穴。|近义词：polychaete (in some contexts)|反义词：asetiger (lacking setae)|名词
