chasing:/ˈtʃeɪsɪŋ/| v.|追赶；追求；追捕|The act of pursuing or hunting something or someone.  It can refer to literal pursuit or a metaphorical pursuit of a goal or ambition.|例句：The dog was chasing its tail.  这只狗在追自己的尾巴。

例句：She's been chasing her dream of becoming a doctor. 她一直在追求成为一名医生的梦想。|近义词：pursuing, hunting, following|反义词：escaping, avoiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chases the ball every day. 他每天都追球。
They are chasing their dreams. 他们在追逐他们的梦想。

一般过去时:
The police chased the thief through the streets. 警察追捕小偷穿过街道。
I chased my childhood dreams. 我追逐过我童年的梦想。


现在完成时:
He has been chasing that promotion for years. 他多年来一直在追求那次晋升。
They have chased away the birds from the garden. 他们把花园里的鸟赶走了。


过去完成时:
The hunter had chased the fox for hours before he finally caught it. 猎人追捕狐狸几个小时才最终抓住它。
She had chased many opportunities before she found the right one. 在找到合适的机会之前，她已经追求过许多机会。


将来时:
I will chase my dreams until I achieve them. 我会追逐我的梦想直到实现它们。
They will chase the sunset on the beach. 他们将在海滩上追逐日落。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would chase my dreams. 如果我是你，我会追逐我的梦想。
I wish I had chased my dreams more aggressively when I was young. 我真希望我年轻的时候更积极地追逐我的梦想。


被动语态:
The thief was chased by the police. 小偷被警察追捕。
The ball was chased by the dog. 球被狗追赶。


疑问句:
What are you chasing? 你在追什么？
Why are they chasing him? 他们为什么追他？


否定句:
He doesn't chase after money. 他不追逐金钱。
They didn't chase the thief. 他们没有追赶小偷。
