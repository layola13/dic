chicano:/tʃɪˈkɑːnoʊ/| n. adj.|墨西哥裔美国人；奇卡诺人（文化）|A Chicano is a Mexican American, especially one who is politically active and identifies with the Chicano movement. The term is often used to refer to the cultural identity and political activism of Mexican Americans.  |The Chicano movement fought for social justice and equal rights for Mexican Americans.  奇卡诺运动为墨西哥裔美国人的社会正义和平等权利而斗争。|Mexican American, Hispanic|Anglo (in contrast)|名词，形容词
