nondeterministic: /ˌnɒndɪtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | /ˌnɑːndɪtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非确定性的 |  Not determined; having many possible outcomes; not predictable.  It describes systems or processes where the next state is not uniquely determined by the current state.  This is in contrast to deterministic systems, where the current state completely determines the next state. |


例句：

The outcome of the experiment was nondeterministic, meaning that different runs could produce different results. = 实验结果是非确定性的，这意味着不同的运行可能会产生不同的结果。

The program's behavior is nondeterministic because it relies on random number generation. = 该程序的行为是非确定性的，因为它依赖于随机数生成。


近义词：uncertain, unpredictable, probabilistic, stochastic

反义词：deterministic, predictable, certain


词性：形容词
