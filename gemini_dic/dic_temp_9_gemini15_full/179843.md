reexperiencing:/ˌriːɪkˈspɪəriənsɪŋ/| v. |再次经历；重新体验 |The act or process of experiencing something again.  It often refers to reliving a past traumatic event, typically in the context of PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder).|例句：He was reexperiencing the trauma of the car accident. 他正在再次经历车祸的创伤。|例句：The therapy helped her to process and stop reexperiencing the abuse.  治疗帮助她处理并停止重新体验虐待。|近义词：reliving, revisiting, undergoing again|反义词：forgetting, ignoring, avoiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reexperiencing the pain of his childhood. = 他正在重新体验童年时期的痛苦。
She reexperiences the joy of her wedding day frequently. = 她经常重新体验她婚礼当天的喜悦。


一般过去时:
He reexperienced the trauma in a vivid nightmare. = 他在一个生动的噩梦中再次经历了那次创伤。
She reexperienced the intense fear she felt during the robbery. = 她再次体验到了抢劫时感受到的强烈恐惧。


现在完成时:
I have reexperienced that feeling of loss several times since then. = 从那以后，我已经好几次重新体验到了那种失去的感觉。
They have reexperienced the same difficulties in their new project. = 他们在新的项目中再次经历了同样的困难。


过去完成时:
She had reexperienced the humiliation several times before seeking help. = 在寻求帮助之前，她已经多次重新体验了这种羞辱。
By then, he had reexperienced the same mistake multiple times, leading to further setbacks. = 到那时，他已经多次重犯了同样的错误，导致了进一步的挫折。


将来时:
I fear I will reexperience the anxiety again before the presentation. = 我担心在演示之前我会再次经历焦虑。
She suspects she will reexperience the symptoms once she stops taking the medication. = 她怀疑一旦停止服用药物，她就会再次出现症状。


虚拟语气:
If I were to reexperience that moment, I would handle it differently. = 如果我要再次经历那一刻，我会以不同的方式处理它。
I wish I didn't have to reexperience the pain of losing him. = 我希望我不必再次经历失去他的痛苦。


被动语态:
The trauma was reexperienced by the patient during therapy. = 患者在治疗过程中再次经历了创伤。(较为少见，主动语态更为常用)
The difficult emotions were reexperienced intensely in the group session. = 在小组会议中，那些艰难的情绪被强烈地重新体验到。(较为少见，主动语态更为常用)


疑问句:
Are you reexperiencing any symptoms from your past injury? = 你有没有再次经历你过去受伤的任何症状？
Does he reexperience flashbacks related to his time in the military? = 他是否会再次经历与他在军队服役相关的闪回？


否定句:
He is not reexperiencing any symptoms at the moment. = 他目前没有再次经历任何症状。
She did not reexperience the same level of fear this time. = 她这次没有再次体验到同样程度的恐惧。
