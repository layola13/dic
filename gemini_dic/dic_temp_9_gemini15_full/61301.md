embargos:/ɛmˈbɑːrɡoʊz/ | n. |禁运；贸易禁运 | An embargo is an official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.  It is often used as a political or economic sanction. |The government imposed an embargo on oil imports from the hostile nation.  政府对来自敌对国家的石油进口实施了禁运。|sanctions, blockade |free trade, open market |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government imposes an embargo on certain goods. = 政府对某些商品实行禁运。
This company respects all international embargos. = 这家公司遵守所有国际禁运。


一般过去时:
The United States imposed an embargo on Cuba. = 美国对古巴实施了禁运。
The country lifted the embargo after negotiations. =  在谈判之后，该国取消了禁运。


现在完成时:
Several countries have imposed embargos on arms sales. =  几个国家已经对武器销售实施了禁运。
The effects of the embargo have been devastating to the economy. = 禁运的影响对经济造成了毁灭性的打击。


过去完成时:
They had already imposed an embargo before the conflict escalated. = 在冲突升级之前，他们就已经实施了禁运。
The company had anticipated the embargo and adjusted its strategy. = 公司预料到了禁运并调整了其策略。


将来时:
The international community will likely impose further embargos. = 国际社会可能会实施进一步的禁运。
The country will lift the embargo if certain conditions are met. = 如果满足某些条件，该国将取消禁运。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't imposed an embargo, the economy would have thrived. = 如果政府没有实施禁运，经济就会蓬勃发展。
I wish the embargo had never been imposed. = 我希望禁运从未被实施过。


被动语态:
The embargo was imposed to pressure the regime. = 实施禁运是为了向政权施压。
Several trade routes were affected by the imposed embargo. =  几条贸易路线受到了实施禁运的影响。


疑问句:
Is there an embargo on the import of these products? = 这些产品的进口有禁运吗？
What are the consequences of violating the embargo? = 违反禁运的后果是什么？


否定句:
There is no embargo on the export of food. = 食品出口没有禁运。
The company did not violate the embargo. =  该公司没有违反禁运。


PHRASE->
trade embargo = 贸易禁运
oil embargo = 石油禁运
arms embargo = 军火禁运
economic embargo = 经济制裁 (也可以指经济禁运)
embargo on goods = 对商品的禁运


词源:
The word "embargo" originates from the Spanish word "embargar," meaning "to seize" or "to attach."  This reflects the original function of embargos as a seizure of goods to prevent their export or import.
