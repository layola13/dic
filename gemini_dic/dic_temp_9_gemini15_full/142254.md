orphanages:/ˈɔːrfənɪdʒɪz/| n. |孤儿院|A place where orphans (children who have lost one or both parents) live and are cared for.|Many orphanages are run by charities and rely on donations.|近义词：children's home|反义词：family home


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The orphanage provides food and shelter for the children. = 孤儿院为孩子们提供食物和住所。
Many orphanages struggle with funding. = 许多孤儿院都在为资金问题而挣扎。


一般过去时:
The orphanage closed down last year. = 孤儿院去年关闭了。
He spent his childhood in an orphanage. = 他在孤儿院度过了童年。


现在完成时:
The government has invested heavily in orphanages. = 政府已大力投资孤儿院。
