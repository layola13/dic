conspiratress:/kənˈspɪrətɹɛs/| n. |阴谋者（女性）|A woman who is involved in a conspiracy.  A conspiratress is a female participant in a secret plot to do something unlawful or harmful.|The conspiratress was finally apprehended and charged with treason.|近义词：plotter, schemer, accomplice (female)|反义词：innocent, bystander
