internationalization: /ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 国际化 | The process of adapting a product or service to multiple languages and cultures.  It involves considerations beyond simple translation, encompassing cultural nuances, local customs, and legal requirements.  | Example: The internationalization of the software involved translating the user interface into several languages and adapting the features to suit different cultural preferences. | 近义词：globalization, localization | 反义词：localization (in the sense of focusing on a single region)
