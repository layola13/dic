galantine:/ˈɡæləntiːn/| n.|肉冻；法式肉冻|A galantine is a dish made from a boned and stuffed piece of poultry or game, typically poached and served cold.  It often involves shaping the meat into its original form before cooking. |The chef prepared a beautiful galantine of chicken for the special occasion.|近义词：pâté, terrine|反义词：无
