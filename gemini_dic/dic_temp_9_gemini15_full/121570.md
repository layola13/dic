misnavigating:/ˌmɪsˈnævɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 导航错误，迷失方向 |To fail to navigate correctly; to go astray.|例句：The ship misnavigated in the fog and crashed into the rocks.  这艘船在雾中导航错误，撞上了岩石。
The hikers misnavigated the trail and ended up lost in the woods.  徒步旅行者走错了路线，最终迷路了。|近义词：misdirect, lose one's way, get lost |反义词：navigate successfully, find one's way
