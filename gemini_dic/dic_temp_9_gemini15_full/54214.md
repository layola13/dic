disfigured:/dɪsˈfɪɡərd/| adj.|毁容的；变形了的；损坏的|Having the form or appearance spoiled; marred or damaged.|The accident left him disfigured.  事故使他毁容了。|The storm disfigured the landscape.  暴风雨破坏了景观。|beautiful, pretty, attractive |unblemished, perfect, intact

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The accident disfigures his face. = 事故毁了他的容貌。
This building disfigures the landscape. = 这座建筑物破坏了风景。


一般过去时:
The fire disfigured the old church. = 大火毁坏了那座古老的教堂。
The harsh winter disfigured the garden. = 严酷的冬天毁坏了花园。


现在完成时:
The years have disfigured her once-beautiful face. = 岁月已毁了她曾经美丽的脸庞。
The constant sun exposure has disfigured the paintwork. = 长期日晒已使油漆损坏。


过去完成时:
The war had disfigured the city before the peace treaty was signed. = 和平条约签署之前，战争已毁坏了这座城市。
The disease had disfigured his body before he received treatment. = 他接受治疗之前，疾病已使他面目全非。


将来时:
The proposed development will disfigure the coastline. = 拟建的开发项目将破坏海岸线。
Years of neglect will disfigure this beautiful building. = 多年的忽视将会使这栋美丽的建筑物变得面目全非。


虚拟语气:
If the sculpture hadn't been dropped, it wouldn't have been disfigured. = 如果雕塑没有掉落，它就不会损坏。
I wish the renovations hadn't disfigured the historic house. = 我真希望翻新工程没有破坏这座历史悠久的房子。


被动语态:
His face was disfigured in the accident. = 他在事故中毁容了。
The painting was disfigured by a careless cleaner. = 画作被粗心的清洁工弄坏了。


疑问句:
Was his face disfigured by the fire? = 他的脸是被火烧伤的吗？
Is the building disfigured by graffiti? = 这座建筑物被涂鸦破坏了吗？


否定句:
The accident didn't disfigure her face. = 事故没有毁容。
The storm didn't disfigure the landscape as much as expected. = 风暴并没有像预期的那样破坏景观。
