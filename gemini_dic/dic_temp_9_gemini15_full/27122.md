calipers:/ˈkælɪpərz/| n.|卡尺，测径器|A caliper is a measuring instrument used to accurately determine the dimensions of an object.  It typically consists of two legs or jaws that can be adjusted to fit the object being measured.|例句：The machinist used calipers to precisely measure the diameter of the shaft. 技工用卡尺精确测量了轴的直径。
The engineer employed calipers to check the thickness of the metal plate.工程师用卡尺检查金属板的厚度。|近义词：gauge, dividers|反义词：None
