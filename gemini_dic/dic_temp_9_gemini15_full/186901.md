riotous:/ˈraɪətəs/| adj.|喧闹的；狂暴的；放荡的 | Marked by or given to excessive merrymaking or revelry; turbulent or disorderly.  |例句：The party was riotous, with music blaring and people dancing wildly.  派对非常喧闹，音乐震耳欲聋，人们疯狂地跳舞。|例句：The riotous mob stormed the streets. 暴怒的暴徒冲进街道。|近义词：turbulent, boisterous, unruly, disorderly |反义词：calm, peaceful, orderly, quiet
