getgo:/ˈɡɛtɡoʊ/ | n. |一开始，起初 |From the very beginning; from the start.  The term suggests a rapid and immediate start, often implying a decisive action or understanding from the outset.|例句：He understood the problem from the get-go. = 他一开始就明白了这个问题。  I was against the idea from the get-go. = 我从一开始就反对这个主意。|近义词：beginning, outset, start, commencement |反义词：end, conclusion, finish
