katsura: /ˈkɑːtsʊrə/ | n. | 桂树；假发；(日本) 桂氏家族 |  A katsura is a type of tree, a wig, or a Japanese surname.  The word's meaning depends heavily on context.  As a tree, it refers to *Cercidiphyllum japonicum*, known for its heart-shaped leaves and sweet scent. As a wig, it refers to a specific style. As a surname, it's a prominent Japanese family name. |  例句：The katsura tree turned golden in the autumn. 秋天的桂树变成了金色。 The geisha wore a beautiful katsura wig.  艺妓戴着一顶美丽的桂冠假发。  The Katsura family has a long history in Japan. 桂氏家族在日本有着悠久的历史。| 近义词：(tree)  maple,  ginkgo  (wig)  wig, hairpiece | 反义词： N/A | 名词
