quince:/kwɪns/| n.|木梨，番梨|A quince is a fragrant, yellowish-white, pear-shaped fruit that is related to apples and pears.  It is typically used in jams, jellies, and desserts. |例句：The quince tree bore a plentiful harvest this year. 今年木梨树结了很多果实。|例句：She used quince paste in her recipe for membrillo.  她在制作葫芦干果酱的食谱中使用了木梨酱。|近义词：apple, pear|反义词：无
