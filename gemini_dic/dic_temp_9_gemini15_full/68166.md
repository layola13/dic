fainer:/ˈfeɪnər/| adj.|更乐意,更情愿的|More willing or eager.  The word suggests a greater degree of willingness than "willing" alone. It is somewhat archaic and not frequently used in modern English.|He was fainer to stay at home than go to the party. 他更愿意待在家里而不愿去参加聚会。|近义词：more willing, readier, keener|反义词：reluctant, unwilling
