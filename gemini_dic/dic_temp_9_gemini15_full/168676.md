procrastinates:/prəˈkræstɪneɪts/| v.|拖延；延误|To delay or postpone something.|He procrastinates on his assignments until the last minute. 他把作业拖到最后一分钟才做。|近义词：delay, postpone, defer|反义词：expedite, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He procrastinates every day. = 他每天都拖延。
She procrastinates on her work. = 她工作拖延。


一般过去时:
He procrastinated on the project. = 他拖延了那个项目。
She procrastinated until the deadline. = 她拖延到截止日期。


现在完成时:
I have procrastinated all week. = 我整个星期都在拖延。
He has procrastinated on his responsibilities. = 他一直推卸责任。


过去完成时:
I had procrastinated so much that I failed the test. = 我拖延了太多，以至于考试不及格。
She had procrastinated for so long that it was too late to submit. = 她拖延了太久，以至于提交太晚了。


将来时:
I will procrastinate no more. = 我不会再拖延了。
He will procrastinate until he has no choice. = 他会拖延到没有选择为止。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't procrastinated, I would have finished on time. = 如果我没有拖延，我就按时完成了。
I wish I hadn't procrastinated on that important task. = 我真希望我没有拖延那项重要的任务。


被动语态:
The project was procrastinated upon. = 项目被拖延了。
The decision was procrastinated for weeks. = 这个决定被拖延了几周。


疑问句:
Does he often procrastinate? = 他经常拖延吗？
Why do you procrastinate so much? = 你为什么拖延那么多？


否定句:
I don't procrastinate often. = 我不经常拖延。
She doesn't procrastinate on important matters. = 她不会在重要的事情上拖延。


PHRASE->
procrastinate on = 拖延...
procrastinate with = 用...来拖延
procrastinate about = 犹豫是否...
procrastinate over = 迟迟不做...
avoid procrastinating = 避免拖延
