decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/ | vt. |破译，解读，解释 |To convert (a text, message, etc.) written in code into normal language; to find the meaning of something that is difficult to understand.  |例句：The detectives tried to decipher the coded message.  侦探试图破译密码信息。

例句：She spent hours trying to decipher the ancient inscription. 她花了数小时试图解读古老的铭文。


近义词：decode, interpret, translate, unravel, solve | 反义词：encode, encrypt
