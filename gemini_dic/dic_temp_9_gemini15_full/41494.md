cornucopias:/kɔːrˈnjuːpiəz/ | n. | 丰饶角；应有尽有；富饶之地 | A cornucopia is a symbol of abundance and plenty, often depicted as a goat's horn overflowing with fruits, vegetables, grains, and other goods.  It represents prosperity, richness, and a plentiful harvest. | Example: The farmer's market was a cornucopia of fresh, seasonal produce.  这个农贸市场是应季新鲜农产品的丰饶之地。 The Thanksgiving table was a veritable cornucopia of delicious food. 感恩节的餐桌摆满了美味佳肴，真是应有尽有。| 近义词：abundance, plenty, profusion, bounty | 反义词：scarcity, dearth, famine
