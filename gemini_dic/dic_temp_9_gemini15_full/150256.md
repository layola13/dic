pandit:/ˈpændɪt/| n. |学者；婆罗门教祭司；专家|A learned person, especially a Hindu scholar or priest; an expert.  |例句：The pandit gave a learned discourse on the scriptures.  这位学者对圣经做了深刻的阐述。|例句：He consulted a pandit about his astrological chart. 他咨询了一位婆罗门教祭司关于他的星盘。|近义词：scholar, expert, guru, Brahmin|反义词：ignorant, layman
