quaternary:/ˈkwɑːtənɛri/| adj. n.|四元的；四进制的；第四纪的|Of or relating to the number four; consisting of four parts or elements.  Also referring to the Quaternary Period in geology, the most recent geological period. |例句：The quaternary structure of a protein is determined by the interaction of multiple polypeptide chains. 蛋白的四级结构是由多个多肽链的相互作用决定的。


例句：The Quaternary Period saw the rise of humans. 第四纪时期见证了人类的兴起。


近义词：quadruple, fourfold, quaternate


反义词：binary, ternary


词性: adjective, noun
