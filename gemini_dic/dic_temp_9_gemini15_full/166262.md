prereceives:/ˌpriːrɪˈsiːvz/| v.|预先收到，提前收到|To receive something before it is officially or generally available.|The company prereceived the shipment a week early. 公司提前一周收到了货物。|近义词：receive beforehand, obtain in advance|反义词：receive later, obtain subsequently|

They prereceive important news from their sources. 他们从消息来源提前收到重要新闻。
