scrutinising: /ˈskruːtənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 仔细检查；细察；严格审查 | Examining or observing something very carefully.  |例句：The police are scrutinising the evidence very carefully.  警察正在非常仔细地审查证据。


例句：She scrutinised the contract before signing it. 她签字前仔细检查了合同。


近义词：examining, inspecting, investigating, studying, reviewing | 反义词：overlooking, ignoring, neglecting

He scrutinises every detail of the report. 他仔细检查报告的每一个细节。
They scrutinise the application carefully before making a decision. 他们在做决定之前仔细审查申请。
