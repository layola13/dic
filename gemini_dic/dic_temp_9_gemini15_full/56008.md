do-gooding:/ˈduːˌɡʊdɪŋ/| n.|做好事的人；假仁假义的人|A person who does good deeds, often implying insincerity or self-righteousness.  It can refer to someone genuinely charitable or someone who performs acts of charity for self-promotion or to appear virtuous.|例句：He's a do-gooding philanthropist, always looking for photo opportunities at charity events. 他是个假仁假义的慈善家，总是在慈善活动上寻找拍照机会。
例句：Despite her do-gooding reputation, she secretly manipulated the charity's funds.  尽管她有做好事的声誉，但她秘密地操纵了慈善机构的资金。|近义词：philanthropist, benefactor, altruist (for genuine acts); sanctimonious, self-righteous, holier-than-thou (for insincere acts)|反义词：malefactor, villain, wrongdoer|名词
