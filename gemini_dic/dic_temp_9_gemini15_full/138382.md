词源:
The word "notorhizal" is a combination of the Greek prefix "not-" (meaning "not" or "false") and the words "rhiza" (meaning "root") and "-al" (an adjectival suffix).  Therefore, it literally means "not from the root," referring to the atypical origin of the roots.
