congratulation:/kənˌɡrædʒuˈleɪʃən/| n.|祝贺；恭喜|Expressions of pleasure at someone's success or good fortune.|My congratulations on your success!  祝贺你成功！|近义词：felicitations,commendation|反义词：condolences


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Congratulations are in order. = 该祝贺了。
My congratulations are extended to the winners. = 我向获胜者表示祝贺。


一般过去时:
I offered my congratulations. = 我表示了祝贺。
They received many congratulations on their wedding. = 他们婚礼上收到了很多祝贺。


现在完成时:
We have received many congratulations on the birth of our baby. = 我们收到了很多关于我们宝宝出生的祝贺。
She has extended her congratulations to the team. = 她向团队表示了祝贺。


过去完成时:
By the time they announced the winner, I had already sent my congratulations. = 在他们宣布获胜者之前，我已经送上了我的祝贺。
They had received congratulations from all over the world before the ceremony. = 典礼之前，他们收到了来自世界各地的祝贺。


将来时:
I will send my congratulations when they win the game. = 当他们赢得比赛时，我会送上我的祝贺。
They will receive many congratulations on their graduation. = 他们毕业后会收到很多祝贺。


虚拟语气:
If I had known they won, I would have sent my congratulations earlier. = 如果我知道他们赢了，我会早点送上祝贺。
I wish I could have offered my congratulations in person. = 我希望能够亲自表示祝贺。


被动语态:
Their success was met with many congratulations. = 他们的成功受到了许多祝贺。
Congratulations were offered to the team by the coach. = 教练向团队表示了祝贺。


疑问句:
What kind of congratulations did he receive? 他收到了什么样的祝贺？
Are congratulations in order? 该祝贺吗？


否定句:
I didn't offer my congratulations. = 我没有表示祝贺。
They didn't receive many congratulations. = 他们没有收到很多祝贺。
