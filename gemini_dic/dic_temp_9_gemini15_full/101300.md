invulnerable: /ɪnˈvʌlnərəbl/ | adj. | 不易受攻击的；无懈可击的；安全的 |  Incapable of being wounded, damaged, or harmed.  | 例句：The fortress was considered invulnerable. 这座堡垒被认为是坚不可摧的。

The new security system makes the network virtually invulnerable to hacking attempts. 新的安全系统使网络几乎不会受到黑客攻击。 | 近义词：impervious, invincible, immune, impregnable | 反义词：vulnerable, susceptible, weak

USAGE->
