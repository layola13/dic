peroneal:/pəˈroʊniəl/ | adj. | 腓骨的；腓神经的 | Relating to the fibula (the outer and smaller of the two bones of the lower leg) or the peroneal nerve.  | Example:  The injury affected his peroneal nerve, causing foot drop.  这次受伤影响了他的腓神经，导致足下垂。

The peroneal muscles are essential for ankle stability. 腓骨肌对于踝关节的稳定性至关重要。 | 近义词：fibular | 反义词：tibial (relating to the tibia, the inner and larger bone of the lower leg)


Etymology:  From Late Latin *peroneus, from Latin perōna "brooch; anklebone," from Greek περόνη (perónē) "brooch; fibula."  The Latin word perōna referred to a type of brooch that was shaped like the fibula bone, leading to the anatomical term's adoption.
