round-ups:/ˈraʊndʌps/| n.|总结，综述， roundup (集合名词)| a brief summary of news events, etc.|例句：The news program ended with a round-up of the day's headlines.  新闻节目以当天头条新闻的总结结尾。

例句：This report is a comprehensive round-up of recent research.这份报告是对近期研究的全面总结。


近义词：summary, overview, recapitulation, résumé|反义词：detail, specifics


PHRASE->
