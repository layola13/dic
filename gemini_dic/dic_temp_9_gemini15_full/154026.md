performatory: /pərˈfɔːrmətɔːri/| adj. |表演的；具有表演性质的；(言语行为)具有实施效力的| Relating to or characteristic of a performance or action;  especially in linguistics,  describing a speech act that does not merely describe a state of affairs but actually performs an action.  For example, saying "I now pronounce you husband and wife" is a performatory utterance because it actually performs the act of marrying the couple.|The wedding ceremony included many performatory acts, such as the exchange of vows and rings.|近义词：ceremonial, ritualistic, effective|反义词：descriptive, constative
