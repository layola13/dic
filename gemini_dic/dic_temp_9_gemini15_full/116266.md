massages:/ˈmæsɑːʒɪz/| n.|按摩；揉捏|The act of rubbing and kneading the body to improve circulation and relieve tension.  Massages can be therapeutic and relaxing.|例句：She gets regular massages to relieve muscle pain. 她定期按摩以缓解肌肉疼痛。|例句：The spa offers a variety of massages, from Swedish to deep tissue. 水疗中心提供各种按摩，从瑞典式到深层组织按摩。|近义词：rubdowns, treatments|反义词：None
