plosives:/ˈplɒsɪvz/| n.|爆破音|A plosive is a consonant sound produced by completely stopping the airflow in the vocal tract, then releasing it suddenly.  This creates a brief burst of sound.  Examples include /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, and /ɡ/ in English.|The letter 'p' in 'pop' is a plosive sound.|近义词：stop consonant|反义词:fricative, continuant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child plops down on the floor. = 孩子扑通一声坐到地板上。
She pops the bubble wrap. = 她啪啪地戳破气泡膜。


一般过去时:
He popped the balloon. = 他弄破了气球。
The tires popped. = 轮胎爆了。


现在完成时:
They have popped all the balloons. = 他们已经把所有气球都弄破了。
I have popped my buttons. = 我把扣子崩掉了。


过去完成时:
She had popped the question before he could answer. = 她在他能回答之前已经求婚了。
The pressure had popped the seal. = 压力已经把密封盖弄开了。


将来时:
He will pop the champagne. = 他将打开香槟酒。
The cork will pop. = 软木塞会砰的一声跳出来。


虚拟语气:
If I had popped the question, she might have said yes. = 如果我求婚了，她也许会答应。
I wish I had popped the balloon earlier. = 我真希望我早点把气球弄破。


被动语态:
The balloon was popped by the child. = 气球被孩子弄破了。
The cork was popped with a loud bang. = 软木塞砰的一声打开了。


疑问句:
Did you pop the question? = 你求婚了吗？
Has the balloon popped? = 气球爆了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pop the balloon. = 他没有弄破气球。
The bottle didn't pop open. = 瓶子没有自己打开。
