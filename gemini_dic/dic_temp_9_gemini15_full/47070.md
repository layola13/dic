dates:/deɪts/| n.|日期，日子；约会；枣子|Dates refer to specific days, especially when considering calendar entries or appointments. They can also refer to a social engagement or a type of fruit.|例句：The dates of the conference are October 26th and 27th.  会议日期是10月26日和27日。|例句：We have a date for dinner tonight. 我们今晚有约会。|近义词：day, appointment, engagement|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The date today is October 26th. = 今天是十月二十六日。
What date is your birthday? = 你生日是几号？


一般过去时:
The date of the meeting was last Friday. = 会议日期是上周五。
What date did you last see him? = 你最后一次见他是什么时候？


现在完成时:
I haven't decided on the date yet. = 我还没有决定日期。
Have you set a date for the wedding? = 你已经确定婚礼日期了吗？


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the date had already passed. = 我到达的时候，日期已经过了。
They had already booked the venue before finalizing the date. = 他们在最终确定日期之前就已经预订了场地。


将来时:
The concert will be on the 15th of next month. = 音乐会将在下个月15日举行。
What date will you be arriving? = 你哪天到达？


虚拟语气:
If the date were earlier, I could attend. = 如果日期早一点，我就能参加。
I wish the date were sooner. = 我希望日期早一点。


被动语态:
The date was changed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，日期被更改了。
The final date has not yet been decided upon. = 最终日期尚未确定。


疑问句:
What is today's date? = 今天几号？
What date is the next meeting? = 下次会议是什么时候？


否定句:
The date isn't suitable for me. = 这个日期我不合适。
I don't know the exact date. = 我不知道确切日期。
