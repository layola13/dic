pantisocratic: /ˌpæntiˈsɒkrætɪk/ | adj. | 平等共产主义的；提倡人人平等的 |  Relating to or characteristic of pantisocracy, a system of government based on the principle of complete equality and the communal sharing of resources.  It is a utopian ideal combining "pan" (all) and "iso" (equal) with "cracy" (rule). | The early Romantic poets Robert Southey and Samuel Taylor Coleridge briefly embraced pantisocratic ideals, dreaming of establishing a utopian community in the United States.  | 近义词: egalitarian, utopian, communistic | 反义词: hierarchical, authoritarian, capitalistic |
