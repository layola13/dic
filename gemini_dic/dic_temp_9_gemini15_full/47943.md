decid:/dɪˈsaɪd/| v.|决定，判决|To make a judgment or decision; to settle or resolve (a question, dispute, etc.).|例句：I haven't decided what to wear to the party. 我还没决定去派对穿什么。|例句：The judge decided the case in favor of the plaintiff. 法官判决原告胜诉。|近义词：determine, resolve, settle|反义词：hesitate, waver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He decides quickly. = 他决定得很快。
She decides what to eat every morning. = 她每天早上都决定吃什么。

一般过去时:
They decided to go to the beach. = 他们决定去海滩。
We decided against buying a new car. = 我们决定不买新车。

现在完成时:
I have decided to quit my job. = 我已经决定辞职了。
She has decided on her major. = 她已经选定了自己的专业。

过去完成时:
He had decided to leave before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经决定离开了。
They had decided on a plan before the meeting.  = 他们在会议之前就决定了一个计划。

将来时:
We will decide later. = 我们稍后再决定。
She will decide where to go on vacation. = 她将决定去哪里度假。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would decide to go. = 如果我是你，我会决定去。
I wish I had decided to study harder. = 我真希望我当时决定努力学习。

被动语态:
The matter was decided yesterday. = 这件事昨天就决定了。
The date of the meeting has not yet been decided. = 会议日期尚未决定。

疑问句:
Have you decided yet? = 你决定了吗？
What have they decided to do? = 他们决定做什么？

否定句:
I haven't decided what to do yet. = 我还没决定做什么。
She didn't decide to go to the concert. = 她决定不去参加音乐会。


PHRASE->

decide in favor of = 决定支持…
