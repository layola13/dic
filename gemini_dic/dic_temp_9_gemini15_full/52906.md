/ˈdɪmi/| adj.|昏暗的；朦胧的；模糊的|Dim means not bright or clear. It can refer to light, colors, sounds, or even memories.  |例句：The room was dim, lit only by a single candle.  房间很暗，只有一支蜡烛照明。

The distant mountains were dim through the mist.  远处的山峦在雾中朦胧。
