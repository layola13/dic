precaution:/prɪˈkɔːʃən/| n. |预防措施，警惕|A measure taken in advance to prevent something undesirable from happening.|例句：It's always a good precaution to save your work regularly.  经常保存你的工作总是一个好的预防措施。|例句：They took precautions against fire by installing smoke alarms. 他们安装了烟雾报警器来预防火灾。|近义词：preemptive measure, safeguard, preventative measure|反义词：negligence, carelessness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes precautions against accidents. = 他采取预防措施以防意外。
She always takes precautions when traveling abroad. = 她出国旅行时总是采取预防措施。


一般过去时:
They took precautions to avoid infection. = 他们采取预防措施以避免感染。
The company took precautions to protect its data. = 公司采取预防措施来保护其数据。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
