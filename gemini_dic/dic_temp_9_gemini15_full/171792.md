punctuate: /ˈpʌŋktʃueɪt/| vt.|加标点符号；强调；点缀|To add punctuation marks to (text). To interrupt or emphasize something. |例句：Please punctuate this sentence correctly. 请正确地给这个句子加标点符号。  例句：The silence was punctuated by occasional shouts. 沉默不时被喊叫声打断。|近义词：interject, interrupt, intersperse|反义词：omit, leave out|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He punctuates his writing carefully. = 他仔细地给他的文章加标点符号。
She punctuates her speech with gestures. = 她用姿态来强调她的讲话。


一般过去时:
He punctuated the letter with exclamation points. = 他在信中用感叹号来强调。
The birdsong punctuated the quiet morning. = 鸟鸣声点缀着宁静的早晨。


现在完成时:
She has punctuated her essay with many examples. = 她已经在她的论文中加了很多例子来强调论点。
The rain has punctuated the dry spell. = 雨水打破了干旱期。


过去完成时:

The storm had punctuated the calm weather. = 暴风雨打破了平静的天气。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
