manteau:/ˈmæntoʊ/| n.|  外衣；(尤指)斗篷；(比喻)混合物；综合体 |Amanteau is a type of long, loose coat, often a woman's cloak.  It can also refer to a combination or blending of things. |例句：She wore a beautiful velvet manteau. 她穿着一件美丽的丝绒斗篷。|例句：The novel is a manteau of different genres. 这部小说是不同类型小说的混合体。|近义词：cloak, cape, shawl, hybrid, composite |反义词：separate, distinct
