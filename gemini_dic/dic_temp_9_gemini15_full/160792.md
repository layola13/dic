positivism: /ˈpɒzɪtɪvɪzəm/ /ˈpɑːzɪtɪvɪzəm/ | n. | 实证主义 |

英文解析:Positivism is a philosophical theory stating that positive knowledge is based on natural phenomena and their properties and relations.  It emphasizes empirical evidence and the scientific method, rejecting metaphysics and speculation.  Different forms of positivism exist, with varying degrees of emphasis on observation, experimentation, and the role of theory.

例句:

Auguste Comte is considered the father of positivism. = 奥古斯特·孔德被认为是实证主义之父。

The research project employed a positivist methodology, focusing on quantifiable data. = 该研究项目采用了实证主义方法，侧重于可量化数据。


近义词: empiricism, scientism

反义词: idealism, metaphysics, speculation


词性: noun
