photomosaic: /ˌfoʊtoʊmoʊˈzeɪɪk/ | n. | 照片马赛克；照片拼贴画 | A photomosaic is an image composed of many smaller images.  These smaller images are carefully selected and arranged to create a larger, usually recognizable picture. The effect is similar to a mosaic made of small tiles, except the "tiles" are photographs. | Example: The artist created a stunning photomosaic of the Eiffel Tower using thousands of individual photographs.  The photomosaic was a breathtaking piece of art. | 近义词：mosaic, collage | 反义词：single image, plain photograph
