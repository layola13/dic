prolapses:/ˈproʊlæpsɪz/ | n. |脱垂；（器官的）下垂 | The downward displacement of an organ or tissue from its normal position, often due to weakened supporting structures.  This can affect various organs, including the uterus, rectum, bladder, and stomach.  The severity can range from mild to severe, requiring different treatments. |Example:  The patient experienced a uterine prolapse after childbirth. 产后病人发生了子宫脱垂。

The doctor diagnosed a rectal prolapse, requiring surgical intervention. 医生诊断为直肠脱垂，需要手术干预。


近义词：hernia, displacement, sagging

反义词：retention, support
