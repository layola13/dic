preaddresses:/ˌpriːˈædrɛsɪz/| n.|预先致辞，事先准备好的讲话|A preaddressed letter is one that has already had the recipient's address printed on the envelope. 预先写好地址的信是指信封上已经印好收件人地址的信。|近义词：prepared speech, advance address|反义词：impromptu speech

She preaddresses the invitations for the wedding. 她为婚礼预先写好请柬地址。
