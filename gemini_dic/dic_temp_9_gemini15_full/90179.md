hotpress:/ˈhɒtˌprɛs/ | n. |热压机；热压印刷|A hotpress is a machine that uses heat and pressure to bind or finish paper or fabric.  It's often used in bookbinding and print finishing to create a smooth, flat surface. | Example: The bookbinder used a hotpress to create a professional finish on the hardback book.  = 装订工人用热压机为精装书制作专业的表面处理。 |近义词：calender, press|反义词：N/A|名词
