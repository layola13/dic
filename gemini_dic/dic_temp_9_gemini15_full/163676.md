predecessors:/ˈpredɪˌsesərz/| n. |前任，前辈；前身|Those who came before, either in a position or in a lineage.  They can refer to people who held a particular job before the current holder, or to earlier versions of something.|例句：His predecessors had laid a strong foundation for the company's success. 他的前任为公司的成功奠定了坚实的基础。

例句：The new operating system is a significant improvement over its predecessors. 新的操作系统比其前身有了显著的改进。

近义词：antecedents, forerunners, precursors

反义词：successors
