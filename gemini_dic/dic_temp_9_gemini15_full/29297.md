castanet:/ˌkæstəˈnet/| n.|响板|A castanet is a small, shell-shaped percussion instrument, typically made of wood or plastic, that is clicked together to produce a rhythmic sound.  They are often used in pairs and are held in the hand. |The flamenco dancer clicked her castanets rhythmically.  西班牙弗拉明戈舞者有节奏地敲击着她的响板。  The orchestra included a section dedicated to castanets.  管弦乐队中包括一个专门演奏响板的乐器组。|近义词：percussion instrument|反义词:None.
