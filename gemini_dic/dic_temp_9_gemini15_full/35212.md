cluckiest:/ˈklʌkiɪst/| adj.|最咯咯叫的；最絮叨的；最唠叨的|Most characterized by clucking sounds; excessively talkative or fussy.|The hen was the cluckiest in the coop, constantly making noise. 这只母鸡是鸡舍里最爱咯咯叫的，不停地发出噪音。|近义词：chatteriest, most talkative, fussbudge|反义词：silent, quiet
