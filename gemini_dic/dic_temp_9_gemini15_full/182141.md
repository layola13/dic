remitting:/rɪˈmɪtɪŋ/| v.| 缓解；汇款；赦免；(疾病)减轻 | To remit something means to send it, especially money. It can also mean to forgive or pardon something, or for a disease to lessen in severity. |


例句：

The company remitted the payment to our account.  = 公司将款项汇到了我们的账户。

The judge remitted the prisoner's sentence. = 法官赦免了囚犯的刑罚。


近义词：

transmit, forward, pardon, forgive, alleviate, abate, ease


反义词：

aggravate, worsen, intensify, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regularly remits money to his family. = 他定期向家人汇款。
The company remits payments electronically. = 公司通过电子方式汇款。


一般过去时:
She remitted the fee via online banking. = 她通过网上银行支付了费用。
They remitted the funds last week. = 他们上周汇款了。


现在完成时:
We have remitted all outstanding payments. = 我们已经支付了所有未付的款项。
He has remitted his taxes on time. = 他按时缴纳了税款。


过去完成时:
They had remitted the money before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前汇款了。
She had remitted the payment before the account was frozen. = 在账户被冻结之前，她已经汇款了。


将来时:
I will remit the balance next month. = 我下个月将支付余额。
The company will remit the payment upon receipt of the invoice. = 公司收到发票后将付款。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would remit it to charity. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会把它捐给慈善机构。
I wish I could remit the debt immediately. = 我希望我能立即偿还债务。


被动语态:
The payment was remitted promptly. = 款项已及时汇出。
The sentence was remitted by the governor. = 判决被州长赦免了。


疑问句:
Have you remitted the payment yet? = 你已经汇款了吗？
When will the funds be remitted? = 款项何时汇出？


否定句:
He did not remit the payment on time. = 他没有按时付款。
The company failed to remit the taxes. = 公司未能缴纳税款。



PHRASE->
remit payment = 汇款
remit a fine = 缴纳罚款
remit a sentence = 赦免判决
remit a debt = 偿还债务
remit sins = 赦免罪孽


词源：

From Old French *remetre, from Latin remittere, from re- "back" + mittere "to send".  The sense development from "send back" to "forgive" is due to the practice of sending back something (like a fine or punishment) as a sign of forgiveness.
