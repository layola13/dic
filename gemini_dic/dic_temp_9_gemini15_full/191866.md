scoff:/skɒf/| /skɑːf/| vt. n.|嘲笑；讥笑；不屑一顾；狼吞虎咽|To express derision or scorn; to laugh at contemptuously; to eat something quickly and greedily.|例句：He scoffed at my suggestion. 他嘲笑我的建议。|例句：She scoffed down the sandwich. 她狼吞虎咽地吃掉了三明治。|近义词：mock, jeer, sneer, deride, ridicule|反义词：praise, commend, applaud
