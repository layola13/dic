pleasantly:/ˈplɛzəntli/ | adj. |令人愉快地；舒适地；愉快地 |  Pleasantly describes something that is agreeable, enjoyable, or satisfying. It indicates a pleasant manner or feeling. |例句：The afternoon sun shone pleasantly on our faces.  下午的阳光温暖地照在我们的脸上。

The music played pleasantly in the background. 音乐在背景中轻快地播放着。


近义词：agreeably, delightfully, enjoyably, pleasantly, sweetly

反义词：unpleasantly, disagreeably, unhappily


词性：形容词
