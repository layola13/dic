harridan:/ˈhærɪdən/| n.|泼妇，悍妇|A harridan is a nagging, scolding, or shrewish old woman.  The term often implies a unpleasant and domineering personality. |例句：She was portrayed as a wicked harridan in the play. 她在这部戏中被描绘成一个邪恶的泼妇。|例句：The old woman, a veritable harridan, chased the children away from her garden.  那个老太婆，一个不折不扣的悍妇，把孩子们赶出了她的花园。|近义词：shrew, virago, termagant, scold|反义词：sweetheart, darling, angel
