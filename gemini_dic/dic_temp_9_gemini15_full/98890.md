/ɪnˈtɛnsɪveɪtɪv/ | adj. | 加强的；集中的；强化性的 |  Intensive means done with great effort, or involving a lot of activity in a short period of time. It can also refer to something concentrated or focused. | The students participated in an intensive language course.  学生们参加了一个强化语言课程。|  focused, concentrated, thorough | superficial, casual, relaxed | 形容词
