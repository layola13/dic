composes:/kəmˈpoʊzɪz/ | vt. |创作；组成；构成；使镇定 |To create or produce (something, typically a work of art, music, or literature); to form or constitute (something) by combining constituents; to bring (something) into being; to calm or quieten (someone).|例句：She composes beautiful music. 她创作优美的音乐。  The committee is composed of five members.  委员会由五名成员组成。|近义词：create, write, form, constitute, make up, calm, soothe |反义词：destroy, disrupt, agitate, excite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He composes music every day. = 他每天创作音乐。
The team composes of five players. = 这个队由五名队员组成。

一般过去时:
She composed a poem last night. = 她昨晚写了一首诗。
The jury composed their verdict. = 陪审团作出了裁决。


现在完成时:
He has composed several symphonies. = 他已经创作了几部交响乐。
The committee has composed its final report. = 委员会已经完成了最终报告。


过去完成时:
She had composed the letter before he arrived. = 他到达之前，她已经写好了信。
The group had composed a plan before the meeting. = 会议之前，小组已经制定了一个计划。


将来时:
He will compose a new song next week. = 他下周将创作一首新歌。
They will compose a response to the complaint. = 他们将对投诉作出回应。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
