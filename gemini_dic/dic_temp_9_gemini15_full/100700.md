intractablenesses:/ˌɪntrækˈtæbəlnəsəz/| n. |难以处理的特性；棘手问题的集合|The intractablenesses of the problem are numerous and complex, making a solution elusive.  这个问题的棘手之处众多且复杂，难以找到解决方案。|近义词：difficulties, complexities, problems, challenges|反义词：simplicities, easiness, tractabilities|名词


词源:

* **in-:**  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **tract-:** from Latin *tractare*, meaning "to handle," "to manage," or "to deal with."
* **-able:** suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being"
* **-ness:** suffix forming nouns meaning "the state or quality of being"
* **-es:** plural suffix


英文解析:

Intractableness is the state or quality of being intractable;  difficult or impossible to manage or control.  Intractablenesses refers to multiple instances or aspects of this intractable nature. It highlights the multiplicity of difficulties or challenges presented by a situation or problem.


例句:

The intractablenesses of the political situation prevented any meaningful progress.
政治局势的种种棘手问题阻碍了任何有意义的进展。


The report detailed the many intractablenesses faced by the company in its attempt to launch the new product.
这份报告详细说明了该公司在试图推出新产品时面临的诸多难题。
