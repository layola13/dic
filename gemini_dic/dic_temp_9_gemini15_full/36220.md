cognate:/ˈkɒɡneɪt/ /ˈkɑːɡneɪt/| n. |同源词，同根词 |A cognate is a word that has the same origin as a word in another language.  Cognates often share similar spellings and meanings.  They are useful in learning new languages because recognizing a cognate can help you understand the meaning of a word. |Example: The English word "brother" is a cognate of the German word "Bruder".|近义词：related word, equivalent |反义词：N/A
