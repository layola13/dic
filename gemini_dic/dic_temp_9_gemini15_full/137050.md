nonstimulants:/ˈnɑːnˈstɪmjələnts/| n.|非兴奋剂|Substances that do not stimulate the central nervous system.  These are often used to treat attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) as an alternative to stimulants.|例句：Nonstimulants are sometimes preferred for ADHD treatment because they have fewer side effects than stimulants. 非兴奋剂有时更适合治疗多动症，因为它们的副作用比兴奋剂少。|近义词：non-stimulating agents, non-amphetamine medications|反义词：stimulants
