shoveled:/ˈʃʌvld/ | v. |铲；铲除；迅速地送入|To move (something) with a shovel.  To move (something) quickly or forcefully.|例句：He shoveled the snow from the driveway. 他用铲子铲走了车道上的雪。

例句：The workers shoveled the grain into the silo. 工人们迅速地把谷物送入筒仓。


近义词：scooped, dug, pitched| 反义词：unloaded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shovels snow every winter. = 他每年冬天都铲雪。
She shovels the driveway regularly. = 她定期铲车道。


一般过去时:
I shoveled the walk yesterday. = 我昨天铲了人行道。
They shoveled the dirt into the hole. = 他们把土铲进洞里。


现在完成时:
We have shoveled all the snow. = 我们已经把所有的雪都铲完了。
He has shoveled the path several times today. = 他今天已经铲了几次路了。


过去完成时:
They had shoveled the snow before the storm hit. =暴风雪来临之前，他们已经铲好了雪。
She had shoveled the garden before planting flowers. =她在种花之前已经把花园铲好了。


将来时:
I will shovel the snow tomorrow. = 我明天会铲雪。
They will shovel the grain into the truck. = 他们会把谷物铲到卡车里。


虚拟语气:
If it snowed, I would shovel the driveway. = 如果下雪，我会铲车道。
If I had a shovel, I would shovel the snow myself. = 如果我有铲子，我会自己铲雪。


被动语态:
The snow was shoveled by the city workers. = 雪是由市政工人铲的。
The grain is being shoveled into the trucks. = 谷物正在被铲进卡车。


疑问句:
Did you shovel the snow? = 你铲雪了吗？
Have they shoveled the driveway yet? = 他们铲车道了吗？


否定句:
I didn't shovel the snow. = 我没有铲雪。
They haven't shoveled the path yet. = 他们还没有铲路。
