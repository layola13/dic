lemonades:/ˌlɛməˈneɪdz/| n. |柠檬水；柠檬汽水|Lemonade is a refreshing drink made from lemons, sugar, and water.  柠檬水是一种由柠檬、糖和水制成的清爽饮料。|  近义词：lemon squash,  citrus drink|反义词：无
