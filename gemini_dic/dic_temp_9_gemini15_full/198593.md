shudders:/ˈʃʌdərz/| v.|颤抖，哆嗦|To shake slightly because of cold, fear, or disgust.|例句：She shuddered at the sight of the blood. 她看到血就颤抖了。
例句：A shudder ran down his spine. 他脊背一阵发冷。|近义词：tremble, shiver, quiver|反义词：calm, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shudders at the thought of spiders. = 他一想到蜘蛛就发抖。
The old house shudders in the wind. = 老房子在风中颤抖。

一般过去时:
She shuddered when she heard the news. = 她听到这个消息时打了个寒颤。
The ground shuddered violently during the earthquake. = 地震期间地面剧烈震动。

现在完成时:
I have shuddered every time I've watched that horror movie. = 每当我观看那部恐怖电影时，我都会不寒而栗。
The building has shuddered several times since the last earthquake. = 自从上次地震以来，这座建筑物已经震动了几次。

过去完成时:
She had shuddered at the thought of facing him alone. = 她一想到要独自面对他，就感到不寒而栗。
The old bridge had shuddered under the weight of the heavy trucks. = 那座老桥在重型卡车的重量下颤抖过。

将来时:
The entire city will shudder if the volcano erupts. = 如果火山爆发，整个城市都会震动。
I'm sure I'll shudder when I see the results of the test. = 我确信当我看到考试结果时我会颤抖。

虚拟语气:
If I saw a snake, I would shudder. = 如果我看到蛇，我会颤抖。
I wish I hadn't shuddered so much during the presentation. = 我真希望在演示过程中我没有那么颤抖。

被动语态:
The house was shuddered by the explosion. = 房子被爆炸震动了。
His body was shuddered by the cold. = 他的身体因寒冷而颤抖。

疑问句:
Did the building shudder during the storm? = 暴风雨期间建筑物是否震动？
Does the thought of public speaking make you shudder? = 一想到公开演讲你会不会害怕得发抖？

否定句:
The audience did not shudder at the gruesome scene. = 观众并没有被那可怕的场景吓到颤抖。
He didn't shudder at the prospect of hard work. = 他并不害怕辛苦的工作。
