centrolecithal: /ˌsɛntroʊˈliːθəl/ | adj. | 中心卵黄的 |  Relating to or characterized by a centrally located yolk in an egg.  The yolk is concentrated in the center of the egg, with the cytoplasm surrounding it. This is in contrast to telolecithal eggs, where the yolk is concentrated at one pole. | Example:  "The insect egg is centrolecithal, allowing for a specific pattern of cleavage." = “昆虫卵是中心卵黄的，允许特定的卵裂模式。” | 近义词: centrally yolked | 反义词: telolecithal
