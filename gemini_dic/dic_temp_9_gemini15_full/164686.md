preexpression:/priːɪkˈspɹɛʃən/| n.|预先表达；预先表示|A preexpression is an expression or statement made in advance, before something else happens or is said. It can be a prediction, a warning, or simply a statement of intention.|例句：His preexpression of support for the project was crucial to its success. 他事先表示支持该项目，这对项目的成功至关重要。|近义词：prediction, forecast, anticipation|反义词：postscript, afterthought
