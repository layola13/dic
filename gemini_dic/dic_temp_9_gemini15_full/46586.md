damaging: /ˈdæmɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 有害的；损害的；毁坏的 | Causing damage or harm.  |例句：His careless words were incredibly damaging to her reputation.  他的粗心话语严重损害了她的名誉。

例句：The storm caused damaging winds and heavy rain.  暴风雨带来了破坏性的狂风和暴雨。


近义词：harmful, destructive, detrimental, injurious | 反义词：beneficial, helpful, advantageous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gossip is damaging his career. = 流言蜚语正在损害他的事业。
This policy is damaging the environment. = 这项政策正在损害环境。


一般过去时:
The accident caused damaging effects to the building. = 事故对建筑物造成了破坏性影响。
The criticism was damaging to his confidence. = 批评损害了他的信心。


现在完成时:
The scandal has been damaging to the company's image. = 这场丑闻已经损害了公司的形象。
His actions have been damaging to his relationships. = 他的行为已经损害了他的关系。


过去完成时:
The faulty wiring had already been damaging the equipment before they discovered it. = 在他们发现之前，故障的线路已经损坏了设备。
The prolonged drought had been damaging the crops for months. = 长期的干旱已经损害了数月的农作物。


将来时:
The new regulations will be damaging to small businesses. = 新规将损害小型企业。
Continued pollution will be damaging to the ecosystem. = 持续的污染将损害生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, it will be damaging to our health. = 如果我们继续这样下去，将会损害我们的健康。
I wish the rumors hadn't been so damaging to his prospects. = 我真希望谣言没有那么损害他的前景。


被动语态:
His reputation was damaged by the false accusations. = 他的名誉被不实的指控所损害。
The car was damaged in the accident. = 汽车在事故中受损。


疑问句:
Is this behavior damaging to the team's morale? = 这种行为会损害团队士气吗？
Are the chemicals damaging to the environment? = 这些化学物质会损害环境吗？


否定句:
The evidence was not damaging to the defendant. = 证据对被告没有不利。
The report was not damaging to his credibility. = 报告没有损害他的信誉。



PHRASE->
damaging evidence:  不利证据
damaging effects:  有害影响
damaging criticism:  批评性毁谤
potentially damaging:  可能有损害的
self-damaging: 自毁的
