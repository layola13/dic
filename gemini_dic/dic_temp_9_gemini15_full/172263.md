The manager pussyfooted through the presentation, afraid to commit to a decision. 经理在演示中畏首畏尾，不敢做出决定。


近义词：timid, hesitant, cautious, evasive, indirect, circumspect


反义词：bold, direct, forthright, assertive, decisive


词性：形容词，动词


词源：The word originates from the image of a cat walking softly and quietly, hence "pussy" (referring to a cat) + "footed" (referring to the manner of walking). The combination suggests a stealthy and cautious approach.  The term gained popularity in the 20th century.
