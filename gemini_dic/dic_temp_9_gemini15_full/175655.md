ransoms:/ˈrænsəmz/| n.|赎金；赎价|Money paid to a kidnapper or someone else to secure the release of a person or property.  It often involves a negotiation and a threat of harm if the ransom is not paid.|例句：The kidnappers demanded a large ransom for the safe return of the child.  绑匪要求支付一大笔赎金才能安全归还孩子。

例句：The company paid a ransom to recover the stolen data. 公司支付赎金以找回被盗数据。


近义词：redemption price, price, payoff


反义词：None directly opposite, but concepts like "free release" or "recovery without payment" could be considered contrasting.


词性:名词
