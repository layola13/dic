multipolar:/ˌmʌltiˈpoʊlər/ | adj. | 多极的；多极化的 | Having or characterized by multiple centers of power or influence.  The term is often used to describe the international system, where power is not concentrated in one or two states, but distributed among several.  |例句：The end of the Cold War ushered in a multipolar world order. 冷战的结束开启了一个多极世界秩序。

例句：The current geopolitical landscape is increasingly multipolar, with rising powers challenging established ones. 当前的地缘政治格局日益多极化，新兴大国正在挑战既有的强国。


近义词：polycentric, multifaceted, multi-lateral


反义词：unipolar, bipolar


词性：形容词
