nematoblast:/ˈnɛmətoʊblæst/| n. |线虫母细胞，刺细胞母细胞|A nematoblast is an undifferentiated cell that develops into a nematocyst (a stinging cell) in cnidarians (such as jellyfish, sea anemones, and corals).  It is the precursor cell to the stinging cell.  |Example: The nematoblasts in the tentacles of the jellyfish were ready to fire.|近义词: cnidoblast (though nematoblast specifically refers to the precursor of the nematocyst)|反义词: nematocyst (mature stinging cell)|名词
