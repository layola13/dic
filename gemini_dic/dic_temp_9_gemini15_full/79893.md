gnatlike:/ˈnætˌlaɪk/| adj. |像蚊子的；微小的；讨厌的 |Resembling a gnat; tiny; annoying.  The word combines "gnat" (a small flying insect) with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. It suggests something small, insignificant, or irritating, similar to the nuisance a gnat can be. |例句：The tiny, gnatlike insects swarmed around the picnic.  这些像蚊子一样的小昆虫在野餐周围嗡嗡作响。  The criticism was gnatlike in its pettiness.  批评因其琐碎而显得微不足道。|近义词：minuscule, insignificant, trifling, pesky|反义词：gigantic, enormous, significant, important|形容词
