repudiator:/rɪˈpjuːdieɪtər/ | n. | 否认者；拒绝者；抛弃者 | One who repudiates; one who rejects or disowns something or someone.  | The repudiator of the contract was sued for breach of contract.  合同的否认者因违约而被起诉。| denier, rejector, disowner | supporter, accepter, approver | 名词


USAGE->

She repudiates the accusations against her. = 她否认对她提出的指控。
