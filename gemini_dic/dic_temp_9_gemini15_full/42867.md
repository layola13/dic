courthouses:/ˈkɔːrtˌhaʊzɪz/ | n. |法院（复数）|Buildings that house courts of law.  They are where legal proceedings take place, trials are held, and judgments are delivered.|例句：Many courthouses across the country are undergoing renovations.  许多全国各地的法院正在进行翻修。


例句：The new courthouses are designed to be more accessible to people with disabilities. 新法院的设计更方便残疾人使用。


近义词：court buildings, judicial buildings | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词
