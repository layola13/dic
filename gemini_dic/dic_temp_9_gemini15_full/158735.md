plow-through: /plaʊ θruː/ | v. | 艰难地完成；克服；强行通过 |  To persistently continue doing something difficult or unpleasant until it is completed.  It suggests overcoming obstacles and persevering despite challenges. |例句：She plowed through the mountain of paperwork. 她艰难地完成了堆积如山的文书工作。


例句：He plowed through the dense forest. 他艰难地穿过了茂密的森林。


近义词：persevere, struggle through, push through, forge ahead


反义词：give up, quit, abandon
