proliferous:/prəˈlɪfərəs/| adj.|多产的；迅速繁殖的；大量的 | Abundantly productive; rapidly producing new growth or offspring.  |例句：The proliferous nature of the weeds made it difficult to keep the garden clean.  杂草的疯长使得保持花园清洁变得困难。

例句：The company experienced a proliferous period of growth, expanding into new markets and doubling its workforce. 这家公司经历了一段高速发展的时期，扩张到新的市场，员工人数翻了一番。


近义词：fertile, prolific, abundant, burgeoning, teeming

反义词：scarce, barren, unproductive, sterile


词性：形容词
