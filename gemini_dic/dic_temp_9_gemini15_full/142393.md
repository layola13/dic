orthotopic:/ɔːrθoʊˈtɒpɪk/| adj.| 原位的；正常的；同位的 | Situated in the normal or usual place; relating to or denoting a transplant of tissue or an organ to its normal anatomical location.  | Example: The orthotopic liver transplant was successful.  原位肝移植成功了。|近义词：in situ, normal, native|反义词：ectopic, heterotopic|词性:形容词


USAGE->
