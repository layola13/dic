democracy: /dɪˈmɒkrəsi/ /dəˈmɑːkrəsi/ | n. | 民主；民主制度 |

英文解析:  A system of government by the whole population or all the eligible members of a state, typically through elected representatives.  It emphasizes citizen participation in political decision-making, often through voting and elections.  Key features include majority rule, protection of minority rights, and the rule of law.

例句：

Many countries around the world strive for democracy.  = 全世界许多国家都在努力争取民主。

The success of a democracy relies on informed and engaged citizens. = 民主的成功依赖于知情且积极参与的公民。


近义词：self-governance, popular sovereignty, republic (in some contexts)

反义词：autocracy, dictatorship, totalitarianism, oligarchy


词性：名词
