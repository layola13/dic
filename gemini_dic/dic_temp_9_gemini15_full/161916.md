prairial:/ˈpreəriəl/| n. |牧月（法国共和历的第七个月）|The word "prairial" refers to the seventh month of the French Republican Calendar, lasting from May 20 to June 18.  It is derived from the French word "prairie," meaning "meadow" or "pasture," reflecting the season of lush meadows and pastures.  The French Republican Calendar was a calendar system implemented during the French Revolution, aiming to replace the Gregorian calendar. |The French Republican Calendar was used from 1792 to 1806. Prairial was the month of meadows and pastures. |近义词：May (partially, in terms of the time of year) |反义词：None directly related to its meaning as a calendar month.
