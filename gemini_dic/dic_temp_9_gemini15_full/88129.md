hiker:/ˈhaɪkər/| n. |徒步旅行者，远足者 | A person who goes on hikes, especially for pleasure.  |例句：The hiker was enjoying the scenic views.  这位徒步旅行者正在欣赏美丽的景色。|例句：Experienced hikers should be prepared for unexpected weather.  经验丰富的徒步旅行者应该为意外天气做好准备。|近义词：trekker, rambler, walker |反义词:sedentary person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hiker climbs the mountain every weekend. = 徒步旅行者每个周末都爬山。
He hikes through the forest regularly. = 他经常在森林里远足。


一般过去时:
The hiker climbed a challenging peak last week. = 徒步旅行者上周攀登了一座充满挑战的山峰。
She hiked for ten miles yesterday. = 她昨天徒步走了十英里。


现在完成时:
He has hiked many trails in the national park. = 他已经在国家公园里走了许多小路。
She has just finished her hike. = 她刚刚完成了徒步旅行。


过去完成时:
Before reaching the summit, the hiker had already consumed all his water. = 在到达山顶之前，徒步旅行者已经喝完了所有的水。
They had hiked for hours before finding shelter. = 他们在找到住所之前已经徒步走了几个小时。


将来时:
The hiker will hike to the top of the mountain tomorrow. = 徒步旅行者明天将徒步登上山顶。
They will be hiking in the Alps next month. = 他们下个月将在阿尔卑斯山徒步旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I were a hiker, I would explore the Appalachian Trail. = 如果我是一个徒步旅行者，我会探索阿巴拉契亚小径。
I wish I had time to go hiking this weekend. = 我希望这个周末我有时间去远足。


被动语态:
The trail was hiked by many people last year. = 这条小路去年被很多人走过。
The new hiking route is being explored by the park rangers. = 公园管理员正在探索新的徒步路线。


疑问句:

Where are the hikers going? = 徒步旅行者要去哪里？


否定句:
