hugs:/hʌɡz/| n. vt.|拥抱；紧抱|A hug is an embrace, typically expressing affection, comfort, or sympathy.  |例句：She gave her child a warm hug. 她给了她的孩子一个温暖的拥抱。|例句：He hugged his friend tightly. 他紧紧地拥抱了他的朋友。|近义词：embrace, clasp, cuddle|反义词：push away, reject

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hug my dog every day. = 我每天都拥抱我的狗。
She hugs her children before bedtime. = 她每天睡前都会拥抱她的孩子。

一般过去时:
He hugged me goodbye. = 他拥抱我道别。
They hugged each other after the victory. = 胜利后他们互相拥抱。

现在完成时:
I have hugged him many times. = 我已经拥抱他很多次了。
She has hugged her teddy bear since she was a child. = 从小她就一直抱着她的泰迪熊。

过去完成时:
I had hugged her before she left. = 在她离开之前我拥抱了她。
He had hugged his father before he died. = 他在他父亲去世前拥抱了他父亲。

将来时:
I will hug you when I see you. = 我见到你时会拥抱你。
She will hug her daughter at the airport. = 她会在机场拥抱她的女儿。

虚拟语气:
If I saw her, I would hug her. = 如果我看到她，我会拥抱她。
I wish I could hug my grandmother one last time. = 我真希望我能最后一次拥抱我的祖母。

被动语态:
The child was hugged by his mother. = 孩子被他的母亲抱住了。
The teddy bear was hugged tightly. = 泰迪熊被紧紧地抱住了。

疑问句:
Do you hug your parents often? = 你经常拥抱你的父母吗？
Will they hug each other at the reunion? = 他们在重逢时会互相拥抱吗？

否定句:
I didn't hug him because I was angry. = 我没有拥抱他，因为我很生气。
She doesn't hug strangers. = 她不拥抱陌生人。


PHRASE->
give someone a hug = 给某人一个拥抱
a bear hug = 熊抱 (a very tight hug)
a warm hug = 温暖的拥抱
a long hug = 长长的拥抱
a comforting hug = 安慰性的拥抱
