nonabsolutist: /ˌnɒnˈæbsəluːtɪst/ | adj. | 非绝对主义的 |  Relating to or characterized by nonabsolutism;  rejecting the existence of absolute truths or values.  It emphasizes the relativity of truth and morality, depending on context, perspective, or culture. |  例句：His nonabsolutist approach to morality allowed him to understand different cultural perspectives.  他的非绝对主义道德观使他能够理解不同的文化视角。


例句：The judge's nonabsolutist interpretation of the law led to a more nuanced decision.  法官对法律的非绝对主义解释导致了更细致的判决。


近义词：relativist, contextualist, pluralist | 反义词：absolutist, fundamentalist


词性: adjective
