llama:/ˈlɑːmə/ /ˈlɑːmɑː/| n.|美洲驼|A llama is a domesticated South American camelid, related to the alpaca.  They are known for their soft wool and are used as pack animals in the Andes Mountains.| Example: The llama carried a heavy load of supplies across the mountain pass.  = 美洲驼驮着大量的物资越过山口。  The farmer sheared his llamas for their valuable wool. = 农场主剪了他的美洲驼的毛，因为它们的毛很值钱。|近义词：alpaca, guanaco|反义词：None
