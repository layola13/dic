cytoblastema:/ˌsaɪtoʊblæˈstiːmə/| n. |细胞芽基，细胞母体|The formative substance of cells, considered as a primitive undifferentiated mass from which cells are believed to develop.  It is an outdated term from early cytology. |Example:  The older literature sometimes refers to the development of cells from a cytoblastema. = 较早的文献有时会提到细胞由细胞芽基发育而来。|近义词:  primitive cell mass, formative substance (in the historical context)|反义词:  differentiated cell, mature tissue|
