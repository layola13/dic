malls:/mɔːlz/| n.|购物中心，商场|A large building containing many shops, usually with a covered area for pedestrians.|例句：We spent the afternoon shopping at the new malls. 我们在新的购物中心逛了一下午。|近义词：shopping center, shopping arcade|反义词:None.
