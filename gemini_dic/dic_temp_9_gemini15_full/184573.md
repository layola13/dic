restokes:/riːˈstəʊks/| vt.|重新点燃；再次激起；重新引发|To rekindle or reawaken (a feeling, activity, or situation).  It implies a revival of something that had subsided or been dormant.|例句：The politician's speech restoked the debate on immigration.  这位政治家的演讲再次引发了关于移民的辩论。|例句：The discovery of new evidence restoked the investigation.  新证据的发现重新启动了调查。|近义词：reignite, revive, rekindle, reawaken|反义词：extinguish, quell, suppress|动词
