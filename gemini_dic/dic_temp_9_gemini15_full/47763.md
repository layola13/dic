decamp:/dɪˈkæmp/| v.| 离开，撤离，逃走 | To leave a place suddenly, especially because of danger or trouble; to depart secretly or quickly. | 例句：The army decamped in the dead of night.  军队在深夜撤离了。 | 例句：  They decamped from the city after the riot. 暴乱之后，他们离开了这座城市。|近义词：depart, leave, evacuate, abscond | 反义词：arrive, settle, stay


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He decamps every weekend to his country cottage. = 他每个周末都去乡下的别墅。
They decamp to the mountains every summer. = 他们每年夏天都去山上避暑。


一般过去时:
The soldiers decamped after the battle. = 战斗结束后，士兵们撤离了。
The family decamped from their flooded home. = 这家人离开了被洪水淹没的家。


现在完成时:
The rebels have decamped to a safer location. = 叛乱分子已经撤到一个更安全的地方。
The circus has decamped after a successful season. = 马戏团在一个成功的季节后离开了。


过去完成时:
The thieves had decamped before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷们已经逃跑了。
The birds had decamped from their nests before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已经离开了鸟巢。


将来时:
We will decamp at dawn. = 我们将在黎明时分离开。
They will decamp as soon as the situation becomes dangerous. = 一旦情况变得危险，他们就会离开。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy attacked, we would decamp immediately. = 如果敌人攻击，我们会立即撤离。
I wish we could decamp to a quieter place. = 我希望我们能搬到一个更安静的地方。


被动语态:
The camp was decamped by the soldiers. = 营地被士兵们撤离了。(This is less common usage)
The area was decamped by the tourists after the earthquake. = 地震后，游客们离开了这个地区。(This is less common usage)


疑问句:
Will they decamp before dark? = 他们会在天黑前离开吗？
When did the circus decamp? = 马戏团是什么时候离开的？


否定句:
They did not decamp despite the threat. = 尽管受到了威胁，他们也没有离开。
We will not decamp until morning. = 我们要等到早上才会离开。


PHRASE->
decamp from = 从...撤离
decamp to = 撤离到...
decamp in haste = 匆忙撤离
decamp under cover of darkness = 在黑暗的掩护下撤离
secretly decamp = 秘密撤离
