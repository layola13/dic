sharded:/ˈʃɑːrdɪd/ | adj. | 分片的；碎片化的；分布式的 |  Divided into shards or fragments; relating to a database or system that is distributed across multiple servers.  | Example: The sharded database improved performance significantly.  分片数据库显著提高了性能。  | 近义词：fragmented, distributed, partitioned | 反义词：unified, centralized, monolithic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The database is sharded across multiple servers. = 数据库被分片到多个服务器上。
This application uses a sharded architecture. = 这个应用程序使用了分片架构。

一般过去时:
We sharded the database last week. = 我们上周对数据库进行了分片。
The system was sharded to improve scalability. = 系统进行了分片以提高可扩展性。

现在完成时:
They have sharded the application for better performance. = 他们已经对应用程序进行了分片以获得更好的性能。
The company has successfully sharded its massive dataset. = 公司已经成功地将其海量数据集进行了分片。

过去完成时:
