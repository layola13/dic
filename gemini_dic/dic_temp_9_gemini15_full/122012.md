misstarts:/ˈmɪsˌstɑːrts/| n.| 失败的开端；错误的开始；蹒跚起步 |A misstart is a false start or a beginning that is unsuccessful or flawed.  It often implies an initial attempt that is interrupted or doesn't progress as planned.  |例句：The project suffered from several misstarts due to poor planning.  这个项目由于计划不周，屡屡受挫。

The company's new marketing campaign got off to a series of misstarts.  公司的新的营销活动一开始就接连受挫。|近义词：false starts, setbacks, failures, stumbles |反义词：successful beginnings, smooth starts, successes
