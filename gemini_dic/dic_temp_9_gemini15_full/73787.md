formalizing: /ˈfɔːrməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 正式化；使…正式；使…制度化 | To give a definite or systematic form to something; to make something formal or official. | 例句：The company is formalizing its procedures.  公司正在使它的程序正规化。

例句：They are formalizing the agreement. 他们正在使协议正式化。


近义词：systematizing, structuring, codifying, regularizing

反义词：informalizing, casualizing
