etceteras:/ɛtˈsɛtərəz/| n.|等等；诸如此类|The list included apples, bananas, oranges, etceteras.  清单包括苹果、香蕉、橘子等等。|近义词：and so on, and the like, et cetera|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a plural noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It functions similarly to "and so on" or "and the like," adding to a list without explicitly naming all items.  Therefore, verb tenses, passive voice, etc. are not applicable to the word itself.  It modifies the preceding list.


PHRASE->
and etceteras:  并且等等
among other etceteras:  在其他等等之中
various etceteras:  各种各样的等等
a list of etceteras:  一份等等的清单
the usual etceteras:  通常的等等
