re-review:/ˌriːrɪˈvjuː/| vt.|再次审查，复审|To review something again, often with more detail or from a different perspective.  It implies a more thorough or critical examination than a single review.|例句：The committee will re-review the proposal before making a final decision. 委员会将在做出最终决定前再次审查该提案。|例句：We need to re-review the data to ensure its accuracy. 我们需要再次审查数据以确保其准确性。|近义词：re-examine, reassess, reconsider|反义词：approve (in the context of final approval after review)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-review the contract every year. = 他们每年都会重新审查合同。
The professor re-reviews the students' papers. = 教授重新审查学生的论文。


一般过去时:
The court re-reviewed the case last week. = 法院上周重新审查了此案。
She re-reviewed her notes before the exam. = 她考试前重新复习了笔记。


现在完成时:
The board has re-reviewed the budget several times. = 董事会已经多次重新审查了预算。
He has re-reviewed his application and made corrections. = 他已经重新审查了他的申请并做了修改。


过去完成时:
They had re-reviewed the evidence before presenting their findings. = 他们在提交调查结果之前已经重新审查了证据。
She had re-reviewed the manuscript several times before submitting it. = 在提交之前，她已经多次重新审查了手稿。


将来时:
We will re-review the project next month. = 我们下个月将重新审查该项目。
They will re-review the policy before implementing it. = 他们将在实施该政策之前重新审查该政策。


虚拟语气:
If they had re-reviewed the data, they might have reached a different conclusion. = 如果他们重新审查了数据，他们可能会得出不同的结论。
I wish I had re-reviewed my notes before the test. = 我希望我在考试前重新复习了笔记。


被动语态:
The report was re-reviewed by the experts. = 该报告由专家重新审查。
The proposal is being re-reviewed by the committee. = 该提案正在由委员会重新审查。


疑问句:
Will the committee re-review the application? = 委员会会重新审查申请吗？
Have they re-reviewed the evidence yet? = 他们已经重新审查证据了吗？


否定句:
They did not re-review the document before submitting it. = 他们在提交文件前没有重新审查。
We will not re-review the decision. = 我们不会重新审查这个决定。


PHRASE->
re-review the findings = 重新审查调查结果
re-review the evidence = 重新审查证据
re-review the decision = 重新审查决定
re-review the literature = 重新审查文献
re-review the proposal = 重新审查提案
