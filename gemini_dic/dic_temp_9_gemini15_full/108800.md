leatherette:/ˌleðəˈret/| n.|人造革，皮革制品|A material resembling leather, typically made from a plastic or fabric base coated with a polyurethane or other synthetic material.  It is often used as a cheaper alternative to genuine leather.|The car seats were made of comfortable leatherette.|近义词：artificial leather, imitation leather, vinyl|反义词：genuine leather, real leather
