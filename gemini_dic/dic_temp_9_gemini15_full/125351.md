multilinguist:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwɪst/| n. |通晓多种语言的人|A multilinguist is a person who can speak, read, and write in several languages fluently.  They often have a deep understanding of the cultures associated with those languages. |She is a gifted multilinguist, speaking five languages fluently. 她是一位有天赋的多语种人才，流利地掌握五种语言。He's a multilinguist who works as an interpreter for the United Nations. 他是一位精通多种语言的人，在联合国担任翻译。| polyglot, polylinguist | monolingual | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He speaks several languages fluently. 他流利地说几种语言。
She understands many different cultures. 她理解许多不同的文化。


一般过去时:
He translated the document into three languages. 他将文件翻译成三种语言。

She has learned five languages so far. 到目前为止，她已经学习了五种语言。
