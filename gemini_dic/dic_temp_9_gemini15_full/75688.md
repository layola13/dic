fulgurate:/ˈfʌlɡəreɪt/| vt.|电灼；使…闪光|To destroy tissue by using electricity.  The term often refers to medical procedures.  |例句：The surgeon will fulgurate the cancerous tissue to prevent its spread.  外科医生将电灼癌变组织以防止其扩散。

例句：The lightning fulgurated the tree, leaving it charred and smoking.  闪电电灼了树木，使其烧焦冒烟。
