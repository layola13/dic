quasi-managerial: /ˈkwɑːzaɪmænɪˈdʒɪəriəl/ | adj. |准管理的，类似管理的 |  Describing tasks, roles, or responsibilities that have some, but not all, of the characteristics of managerial work.  It often implies a level of responsibility and authority above a purely operational role, but without the full scope of a formal management position. | Example:  He held a quasi-managerial role, overseeing the team's daily tasks but lacking the authority to hire or fire. 他担任准管理职位，负责监督团队的日常工作，但没有招聘或解雇的权力。| Near synonyms: supervisory, coordinative, semi-managerial | Antonyms:  subordinate, operational, entry-level |
