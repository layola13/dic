rewarded:/rɪˈwɔːrdɪd/ | adj. | 值得奖励的；受到奖励的 |  Having received a reward; given a reward.  |例句：She felt rewarded by the positive feedback from her clients. 她从客户的积极反馈中感到欣慰。

例句：His hard work was rewarded with a promotion. 他的努力工作得到了晋升的奖励。


近义词：compensated, recognized, acknowledged | 反义词：punished, penalized

词性：过去分词 (过去式和过去分词都为rewarded)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is rewarded for his efforts. = 他因他的努力而受到奖励。
They are rewarded handsomely for their work. = 他们因工作而得到丰厚的报酬。


一般过去时:
She was rewarded for her bravery. = 她因勇敢而受到奖励。
He was rewarded with a gift certificate. = 他得到了一张礼品券作为奖励。


现在完成时:
He has been rewarded for his years of service. = 他因多年的服务而受到奖励。
She has been justly rewarded for her hard work. = 她因努力工作而得到了应有的奖励。


过去完成时:
The team had been rewarded before the ceremony began. = 团队在仪式开始前就已经获得奖励。
They had been rewarded for their efforts prior to the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他们就已经因为他们的努力而获得奖励。


将来时:
He will be rewarded for his loyalty. = 他将因他的忠诚而获得奖励。
She will be handsomely rewarded for her contribution. = 她将因她的贡献而得到丰厚的奖励。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he would have been rewarded. = 如果他工作更努力，他就会得到奖励。
If she had finished the project, she would have been rewarded appropriately. = 如果她完成了项目，她就会得到相应的奖励。


被动语态:
The winner will be rewarded with a trophy. = 获胜者将获得一个奖杯作为奖励。
The employees are rewarded based on performance. = 员工的奖励是根据业绩来定的。


疑问句:
Was he rewarded for his invention? = 他因为他的发明而得到奖励了吗？
Will they be rewarded for their participation? = 他们会因为参与而得到奖励吗？


否定句:
He was not rewarded for his efforts. = 他没有因为他的努力而得到奖励。
They were not sufficiently rewarded for their hard work. = 他们辛勤的工作没有得到足够的奖励。


PHRASE->
be rewarded with = 以…作为奖励
well rewarded = 得到很好的报酬
richly rewarded = 得到丰厚的奖励
handsomely rewarded = 得到丰厚的报酬
fairly rewarded = 得到公平的报酬
