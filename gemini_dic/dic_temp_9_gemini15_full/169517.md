propped:/prɒpt/| /prɑːpt/ | vt. |支撑；扶持；倚靠|To support or hold something up, typically in a makeshift way.  |例句：He propped the ladder against the wall. 他把梯子靠在墙上。|例句：She propped her chin on her hand. 她用手托着下巴。|近义词：support, sustain, rest |反义词：topple, overturn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He props the painting against the wall. = 他把画靠在墙上。
She props her books up with a dictionary. = 她用字典把书撑起来。

一般过去时:
The workers propped up the damaged roof. = 工人们支撑住了损坏的屋顶。
I propped my bicycle against a tree. = 我把自行车靠在一棵树上。

现在完成时:
They have propped the tent up with branches. = 他们用树枝把帐篷撑了起来。
She has propped herself up on her elbows. = 她用胳膊肘撑起了自己。


过去完成时:
He had propped the door open with a brick. = 他用砖头把门撑开了。
They had propped the fallen statue upright. = 他们把倒下的雕像扶正了。

将来时:
We will prop the fallen tree back up tomorrow. = 我们明天会把倒下的树扶起来。
She will prop the plant with a stake. = 她会用木桩支撑植物。

虚拟语气:
If I had a stronger stick, I would prop the shelf more securely. = 如果我有一根更结实的棍子，我会把架子支撑得更牢固。
I wish I had propped the ladder better. = 我真希望我当时把梯子支撑得更好。

被动语态:
The painting was propped carefully against the wall. = 画被小心地靠在墙上。
The injured man was propped up with pillows. = 受伤的男人用枕头支撑着。

疑问句:
How is the roof propped up? = 屋顶是怎么支撑起来的？
Is the table properly propped? = 桌子支撑得稳吗？

否定句:
He didn't prop the door open. = 他没有把门撑开。
She didn't prop the plant adequately, so it fell over. = 她没有充分支撑植物，所以它倒了。


PHRASE->
prop up = 支撑；扶持
prop open = 撑开
prop oneself up = 撑起自己

be propped up = 被支撑着
