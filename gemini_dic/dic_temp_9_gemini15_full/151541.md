parsnips:/ˈpɑːrsnɪps/ | n. |欧洲萝卜，防风草|A parsnip is a root vegetable with a long, tapering, pale yellowish-white root.  It has a slightly sweet and earthy flavor. Parsnips are often used in soups, stews, and roasted dishes.| Example:  Roasted parsnips are a delicious side dish.  烤防风草是一道美味的配菜。|近义词：carrot (胡萝卜), turnip (芜菁)|反义词：无

词源:
The word "parsnip" comes from the Old English word "pasneppe," which is ultimately derived from the Latin word "pastinaca."  "Pastinaca" referred to the pastinaca sativa plant, the species of parsnip that is commonly cultivated.
