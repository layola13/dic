compendiarious:/ˌkɒmpɛnˈdeəriəs/| adj.|简明的，扼要的，摘要的|giving a comprehensive summary; concise.  This word is rarely used in modern English. |例句：The professor gave a compendiarious lecture summarizing centuries of research. 教授作了一个简短的讲座，总结了几个世纪的研究成果。

例句：He presented a compendiarious account of the company's financial situation. 他简要地介绍了公司财务状况。


近义词：concise, succinct, summary, brief, abridged

反义词：verbose, lengthy, detailed, exhaustive


词源:  From Latin *compendiarius ("shortened, abridged"), from compendium ("summary, abridgment").  The Latin word itself derives from *compendere ("to weigh together, estimate"), from com- ("together") + pendere ("to weigh").  Thus, the original sense implied a concise summary that captured the essence of something larger.
