reinquires:/ˌriːɪnˈkwaɪərz/ | v. | 再询问；重新询问 | To inquire again; to ask again.  |  The customer reinquired about the shipping date. 客户再次询问了发货日期。| inquire, ask |  ignore, disregard | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinquires about the price every year. 他每年都重新询问价格。
She reinquires about her order status regularly. 她定期重新询问订单状态。

一般过去时:
They reinquired about the availability of tickets. 他们再次询问了门票的供应情况。
He reinquired whether the meeting was still on. 他再次询问会议是否还在进行。


现在完成时:
I have reinquired several times, but haven't received a response. 我已经多次重新询问了，但还没有收到回复。
She has reinquired about the lost package. 她已经再次询问了丢失的包裹。


过去完成时:
They had reinquired before making a final decision. 他们在做出最终决定之前已经再次询问了。
He had reinquired twice before giving up. 他放弃之前已经两次重新询问了。


将来时:
I will reinquire next week if I don't hear back. 如果我没有收到回复，我下周将再次询问。
She will reinquire with the manager tomorrow. 她明天将向经理再次询问。


虚拟语气:
If I needed more information, I would reinquire. 如果我需要更多信息，我会再次询问。
It's essential that he reinquire if there's any doubt.  如果有什么疑问，他必须再次询问。


被动语态:
The matter was reinquired into by the committee.  委员会对这件事进行了再次调查。
The question was reinquired several times. 这个问题被反复询问了好几次。


疑问句:
Will you reinquire about the delivery? 你会再次询问送货事宜吗？
Should we reinquire about the details? 我们应该再次询问细节吗？


否定句:
I didn't reinquire about the delay. 我没有再次询问延误的原因。
They did not reinquire after the first unsatisfactory response. 他们第一次得到不令人满意的答复后就没有再询问了。
