cortege:/kɔːrˈteɪʒ/ | n. |  行列；队伍；车队 | A cortege is a procession, especially a ceremonial one, such as a funeral procession. It can refer to a group of people moving together, or a line of vehicles.  | The funeral cortege slowly made its way through the town.  葬礼队伍缓缓地穿过小镇。 | procession, train, parade |  disarray, chaos | 名词
