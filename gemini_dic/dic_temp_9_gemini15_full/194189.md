self-killers:/ˈsɛlfˌkɪlərz/| n.|自杀者；自我毁灭者|People or things that cause their own destruction or demise.  The term often implies a process or system that contains inherent flaws leading to its own downfall.|例句：The company's aggressive expansion strategy proved to be its self-killers, leading to financial ruin. 这家公司的激进扩张战略最终导致了它的破产，成为其自我毁灭的原因。|近义词：suicide bombers, destroyers, saboteurs|反义词：life-savers, preservers, benefactors|名词
