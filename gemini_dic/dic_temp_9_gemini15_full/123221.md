monocondylian:/ˌmɒnəʊkənˈdɪliən/| adj.|单髁的；只有一个髁的|Having only one condyle.  A condyle is a rounded articular process (projection) of a bone that articulates with another bone.  This term is often used in zoology and anatomy, particularly in describing the structure of the skull and jaw.|Example:  The  mammalian skull is monocondylian, while the reptilian skull is typically dicondylian.|近义词:unicondylar|反义词:dicondylian
