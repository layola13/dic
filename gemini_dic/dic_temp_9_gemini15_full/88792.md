hoed:/hoʊd/| v.|锄地；除草|To cultivate or work (land) with a hoe.  The act of using a hoe to remove weeds or cultivate soil.|例句：He hoed the rows of corn all afternoon. 他整个下午都在锄玉米地。|例句：The farmer hoed the field to prepare it for planting. 农民锄地以准备播种。|近义词：cultivate, till, weed|反义词：neglect, leave untended

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hoes the garden every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都锄花园。
She hoes the weeds around her roses. = 她锄掉玫瑰花周围的杂草。

一般过去时:
They hoed the field before planting the seeds. = 他们播种前锄了地。
I hoed the potatoes yesterday. = 我昨天锄了土豆。


现在完成时:
He has hoed the entire field this week. = 他这周已经锄完了整个地。
She has hoed the garden three times this month. = 这个月她已经锄了三次花园。


过去完成时:
They had hoed the garden before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经锄过花园了。
The farmer had hoed the field before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，农民已经锄完了地。


将来时:
I will hoe the garden tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上要锄花园。
They will hoe the cornfield next week. = 他们下周将要锄玉米地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hoe, I would hoe the garden. = 如果我有一把锄头，我会锄花园。
I wish I had hoed the garden yesterday. = 我真希望昨天锄了花园。


被动语态:
The field was hoed by the farmer. = 田地被农民锄了。
The garden has been hoed recently. = 花园最近被锄过了。


疑问句:
Have you hoed the garden yet? = 你锄过花园了吗？
Did he hoe the field yesterday? = 他昨天锄地了吗？


否定句:
I didn't hoe the garden today. = 我今天没有锄花园。
They haven't hoed the potatoes yet. = 他们还没有锄土豆。


PHRASE->
