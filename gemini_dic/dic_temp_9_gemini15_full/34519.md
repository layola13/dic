cleaning:/ˈkliːnɪŋ/| n.| 清洁；打扫 | The act of making something clean.  It can refer to the process itself or the result.  |例句：The cleaning of the house took all morning.  打扫房子花了一上午的时间。

例句：The cleaning is almost finished. 清洁工作快完成了。|近义词：tidying, scrubbing, sanitizing |反义词：soiling, dirtying, fouling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cleans the house every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都打扫房子。
He is cleaning his car right now. = 他现在正在擦他的车。

一般过去时:
I cleaned my room yesterday. = 我昨天打扫了我的房间。
They cleaned the kitchen after dinner. = 他们晚饭后打扫了厨房。

现在完成时:
I have cleaned the entire apartment. = 我已经打扫了整套公寓。
She has been cleaning all day. = 她已经打扫了一整天了。

过去完成时:
We had cleaned the house before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我们已经打扫了房子。
He had finished cleaning before the party started. = 派对开始之前，他已经打扫完了。

将来时:
I will clean the bathroom tomorrow. = 我明天将打扫浴室。
They will be cleaning the office after hours. = 他们下班后将打扫办公室。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would clean the garage. = 如果我有更多时间，我会打扫车库。
I wish I had cleaned the kitchen earlier. = 我希望我早点打扫厨房。

被动语态:
The house is cleaned every week. = 这房子每周都打扫。
The car is being cleaned at the moment. = 车子正在清洗中。

疑问句:
Are you cleaning the windows? = 你在擦窗户吗？
Did you clean the dishes? = 你洗碗了吗？

否定句:
I didn't clean my room today. = 我今天没有打扫我的房间。
He hasn't cleaned the car yet. = 他还没洗车。
