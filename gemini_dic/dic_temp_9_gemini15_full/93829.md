imminently:/ˈɪmɪnəntli/ | adv. |即将地；迫在眉睫地 |  Approaching very soon; about to happen.  The adverb "imminently" describes something that is likely to occur in the immediate future. It suggests a sense of urgency and closeness. |例句：The storm is imminently approaching.  暴风雨即将到来。

The deadline is imminently approaching; we need to finish the project soon. 截止日期迫在眉睫；我们需要尽快完成项目。|近义词：shortly, presently, immediately, soon |反义词：remotely, distantly, far off


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train arrives imminently. = 火车即将到达。
He speaks imminently on the topic. = 他即将就这个话题发言。

一般过去时:
The war seemed imminently possible. = 战争似乎迫在眉睫。
The guests arrived imminently after the announcement. = 宣布后客人很快就到了。(虽然用过去时，但imminently仍然指代将来很快发生)

现在完成时:
The danger has become imminently clear. = 危险已经变得非常明显（即将发生）。
We have been waiting imminently for the results. = 我们一直在焦急地等待结果（结果即将到来）。


过去完成时:
It had become imminently clear that the project would fail. = 项目即将失败已经变得非常清楚了。
They had waited imminently for the news, knowing it was coming soon. = 他们焦急地等待着消息，知道消息很快就会到来。


将来时:
The decision will be made imminently. = 决定即将做出。
They will imminently begin the construction. = 他们即将开始施工。


虚拟语气:
If the threat were imminently dangerous, we would evacuate. = 如果威胁迫在眉睫，我们将撤离。
I wish the solution were imminently available. = 我希望解决方案即将出现。


被动语态:
The problem is imminently to be addressed. = 这个问题即将得到解决。
The decision will be imminently announced. = 决定即将宣布。


疑问句:
Is the deadline imminently approaching? = 截止日期是否迫在眉睫？
Will the results be imminently available? = 结果即将公布吗？


否定句:
The attack was not imminently expected. = 袭击并非意料之中（并非即将发生）。
The danger is not imminently present. = 危险并不迫在眉睫。


PHRASE->
imminently threatened = 迫在眉睫的威胁
imminently clear = 非常明显（即将发生）
imminently possible = 非常有可能（即将发生）
imminently dangerous = 极其危险（即将发生）
imminently expected = 即将预期


词源:
From Latin *imminēns ("overhanging, impending"), present participle of imminēre ("to overhang, impend").  The -ly suffix transforms the adjective "imminent" into an adverb.
