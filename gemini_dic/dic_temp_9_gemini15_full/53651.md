discharger:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒər/ | n.  | 放电器；卸货者；释放者 | A device or person that discharges something.  This can refer to electrical discharge, the unloading of cargo, or the release of something. | Examples:  The battery charger is also a discharger in reverse. (电池充电器反过来也是放电器。) The port discharger worked efficiently to unload the containers. (港口卸货人员高效地卸下了集装箱。) | 近义词：releaser, unloader, emitter | 反义词：charger (in the context of batteries), loader
