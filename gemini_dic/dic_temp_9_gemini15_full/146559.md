overgracious:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪʃəs/| adj.|过分殷勤的，过分客气的|excessively gracious or polite; excessively flattering or obsequious.  It suggests an insincerity or an attempt to impress that undermines the genuine politeness.|例句：Her overgracious manner made me uncomfortable.  她的过分殷勤让我感到不舒服。

例句：His overgracious compliments felt forced and insincere. 他过分殷勤的赞美听起来很勉强，不真诚。


近义词：obsequious, fawning, ingratiating, overly polite, fulsome


反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, gruff


词性：形容词
