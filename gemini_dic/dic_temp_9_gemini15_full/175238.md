railleur:/reɪˈlɜːr/| n. |嘲弄者，讽刺者 |A railleur is a person who habitually uses mockery or ridicule. They often make sarcastic or witty remarks intended to belittle or humiliate others.  |例句：He was a skilled railleur, his words sharp as needles. 他是一位熟练的嘲弄者，他的话尖锐如针。

例句：The railleur's barbs stung more than any physical blow. 嘲讽者的尖刻话语比任何肉体打击都更令人痛苦。


近义词：mocker, scoffer, taunter, jeerer |反义词：supporter, flatterer


词源：源自法语“railler”，意为“嘲笑，戏弄”。  最终源于拉丁语“ridere”，意为“笑”。
