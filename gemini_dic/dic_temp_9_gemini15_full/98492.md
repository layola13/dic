instance:/ˈɪnstəns/| n. |例子，实例；情况，场合 |An instance is a single occurrence of something.  It can refer to a specific example or a particular situation. |Example:  This is a clear instance of plagiarism. 这是一个剽窃的明显例子。

The recent economic downturn is a prime instance of the dangers of unregulated markets. 最近的经济衰退是缺乏监管的市场危险的一个主要例子。|example, case, sample |non-example, opposite|名词
