intertubular: /ˌɪntərˈtjubjələr/ | adj. |肾小管间的；肾小管之间的| Situated or occurring between renal tubules.  The term refers to the space or structures found between the tubules in the kidney. |The intertubular fluid plays a crucial role in maintaining the body's fluid balance. 肾小管间液在维持人体体液平衡中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: intertubal (less common in this context)|反义词: intratubular
