despairer: /dɪsˈpɛərər/ | n. |绝望的人，悲观者 |A person who has lost all hope and is overwhelmed by despair.  Someone who is filled with despair and hopelessness. |例句：He became a despairer after losing his job and his home. 他在失去工作和家后变成了一个绝望的人。
The despairer sat alone in the dark, unable to find solace.  绝望的人独自坐在黑暗中，无法找到安慰。 |近义词：pessimist, defeatist, cynic |反义词：optimist, hopeful


USAGE->
