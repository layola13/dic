nonrealistic: /ˌnɒnˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 不现实的，非现实主义的 | Not conforming to or representing reality; unrealistic.  It suggests something is impractical, improbable, or fanciful. | The novel's plot was completely nonrealistic, filled with fantastical creatures and impossible events.  这部小说的情节完全不现实，充满了奇幻的生物和不可能发生的事件。|近义词:unrealistic, impractical, improbable, fanciful, imaginary, visionary|反义词:realistic, practical, probable, real, true
