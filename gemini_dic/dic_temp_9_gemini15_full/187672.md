rooftops:/ˈruːftɑːps/ | n. |屋顶（复数） | The tops of roofs; a plural form of rooftop.  Rooftops often provide a good view of the city.  | 例句：The city looked beautiful from the rooftops.  城市从屋顶上看很美。

The rooftops were covered in snow. 屋顶上覆盖着积雪。|近义词：housetops |反义词：basements
