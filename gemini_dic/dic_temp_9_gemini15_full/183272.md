repriming:/ˌriːˈpraɪmɪŋ/| n. |再启动；再灌注；再引发；重新启动|The act of priming something again;  restoring something to its original state or function by priming it anew.  This often involves repeating a process or applying a substance again.|例句：The repriming of the pump restored water flow. 再启动水泵恢复了水流。 The repriming of the economy requires significant investment. 经济的重新启动需要大量的投资。|近义词：reactivation, restarting, reinitiation |反义词：deactivation, shutdown
