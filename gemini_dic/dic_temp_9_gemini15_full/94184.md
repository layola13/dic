imperatives:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvz/| n.|祈使句；命令；强制性|A command or instruction; something that is absolutely necessary.  The grammatical mood that expresses a command or request. |例句： "Stop!" is a simple imperative.  “停下！”是一个简单的祈使句。

The doctor's imperatives were clear: rest and hydration. 医生开的指令很明确：休息和补水。

近义词：commands, directives, mandates, injunctions |反义词：requests (in some contexts), suggestions


词性：名词
