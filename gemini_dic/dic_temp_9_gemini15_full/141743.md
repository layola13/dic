orangeism:/ˈɒrɪnʤɪzəm/| n. |橙色主义；奥兰治主义|A political movement in Northern Ireland, historically associated with Protestant loyalists and opposition to Irish nationalism.  It is characterized by a strong sense of British identity and often involves displays of pro-British sentiment.|The Orange Order is a prominent organization associated with Orangeism.  |近义词：Loyalism (in this context)|反义词：Irish nationalism, Republicanism
