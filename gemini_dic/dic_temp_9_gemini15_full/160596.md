porridge:/ˈpɒrɪdʒ/ /ˈpɔːrɪdʒ/| n. |粥；麦片粥 |A soft food made by boiling oatmeal, other grains, or sometimes other ingredients in water or milk.  It is often eaten for breakfast. |Example: I had a bowl of porridge for breakfast this morning.  今天早上我早餐吃了一碗粥。

Example:  She prefers her porridge with a spoonful of honey. 她喜欢在粥里加一勺蜂蜜。


近义词：oatmeal, gruel, mush

反义词：无
