junglekeepers: /ˈdʒʌŋɡəlˌkiːpərz/ | n. |丛林守护者，热带雨林保护者 | People who work to protect and conserve jungles and their ecosystems.  They may be involved in various activities such as reforestation, anti-poaching efforts, sustainable resource management, and community engagement. | Example: The junglekeepers tirelessly worked to prevent deforestation and protect endangered species.  丛林守护者们不知疲倦地工作以防止森林砍伐和保护濒危物种。|近义词：conservationists, environmentalists, rainforest protectors |反义词：deforesters, poachers
