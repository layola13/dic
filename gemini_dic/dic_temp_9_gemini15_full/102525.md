jade:/dʒeɪd/ | n. |翡翠，玉|A hard, translucent gemstone typically green, highly valued for its beauty and used in jewelry and ornaments.  It is also sometimes used as a verb, meaning to weary or tire. |例句：The necklace was made of exquisite jade. 这条项链是用精美的翡翠制成的。

例句：He was jaded after years of working in the city. 他在城市工作多年后感到厌倦了。|近义词：jewel, gemstone, precious stone |反义词：common stone, worthless material
