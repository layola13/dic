impassable: /ɪmˈpæsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可逾越的；无法通行的 |  Incapable of being passed or traversed; blocked.  This usually refers to roads, paths, or other routes, but can also apply to problems or situations that are impossible to overcome. |


例句：

The road was impassable due to the heavy snow.  这条路因为大雪而无法通行。

The problem proved impassable, despite our best efforts.  尽管我们尽了最大努力，这个问题还是无法解决。


近义词：blocked, unpassable, inaccessible, insurmountable


反义词：passable, accessible, manageable


词性：形容词
