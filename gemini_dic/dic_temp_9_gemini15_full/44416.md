cross-over-to-the-other-side:/ˌkrɒsˈoʊvər tu ði ˈʌðər saɪd/|/ˈkrɒsˌoʊvər tu ði ˈʌðər saɪd/| phr. v.|穿过到对面；转变；转换阵营|This phrase describes the act of moving from one side to another, either literally or figuratively.  It can refer to physically crossing a boundary, changing affiliations, or undergoing a significant transformation.|例句：The hikers crossed over to the other side of the mountain.  徒步旅行者翻过了山到对面。

The politician crossed over to the other side, joining the opposing party.  这位政治家转投了反对党。|近义词：transition, switch, transfer, change sides|反义词：remain, stay, persist|词性:phrasal verb
