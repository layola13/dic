complimentary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/ | adj. | 赞美的；免费的；补充的 |  Given free of charge; expressing praise; completing or enhancing something else.  |


例句：

The hotel offered complimentary breakfast.  = 酒店提供免费早餐。

She received many complimentary remarks on her dress. = 她收到了许多关于她衣服的赞美之词。


近义词：

free, gratis, laudatory, praising, additional


反义词：

chargeable, costly, critical,  detracting


词性：形容词
