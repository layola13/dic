devils:/ˈdɛvəlz/| n.|魔鬼们，恶魔们|The word "devils" is the plural form of "devil," referring to evil supernatural beings.  It can also be used figuratively to describe wicked or mischievous people.  |例句：The devils danced in the flames. 魔鬼在火焰中跳舞。

The legend speaks of devils inhabiting the dark forest. 传说讲述了居住在黑暗森林中的魔鬼。


近义词：demons, fiends, evil spirits|反义词：angels, saints, good spirits|名词
