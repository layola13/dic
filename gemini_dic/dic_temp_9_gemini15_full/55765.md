diversified:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/| adj.| 多样化的；多元化的 | Having a variety of different elements; varied.  |例句：The company has a diversified portfolio of investments. 这家公司拥有多元化的投资组合。|例句：Her interests are remarkably diversified. 她的兴趣爱好非常多样化。|近义词：varied, diverse, multifarious |反义词：homogeneous, uniform


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is diversified across several markets. = 这家公司在多个市场实现了多元化。
His interests are diversified. = 他的兴趣很广泛。


一般过去时:
The farmer diversified his crops last year. = 农民去年使他的农作物多样化了。
The company diversified its product line. = 公司使其产品线多样化了。


现在完成时:
The portfolio has diversified significantly since its inception. = 自成立以来，投资组合已显著多元化。
The economy has diversified its industries. = 经济体使其产业多样化了。


过去完成时:
They had diversified their investments before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘之前已经使他们的投资多元化了。
The company had diversified its operations into new markets. = 公司已将其业务多元化到新市场。


将来时:
We will diversify our product offerings next year. = 我们明年将使我们的产品多样化。
The government plans to diversify the economy. = 政府计划使经济多元化。


虚拟语气:
If the company had diversified earlier, it would not have faced such difficulties. = 如果公司早些实现多元化，就不会面临如此大的困难。
I wish I had diversified my investments years ago. = 我真希望几年前我的投资就已经多元化了。


被动语态:
The company's investments were diversified by the financial advisor. = 公司的投资由财务顾问进行了多元化处理。
The economy has been diversified through government initiatives. = 经济通过政府的倡议实现了多元化。


疑问句:
Are your investments sufficiently diversified? = 你的投资是否足够多元化？
Is the school's curriculum diversified enough? = 学校的课程设置是否足够多样化？


否定句:
The company's product line is not diversified enough. = 公司的产品线不够多元化。
His skills are not sufficiently diversified for this job. = 他的技能对于这份工作来说不够多元化。

词源:
diversify (v.) + -ed (past participle suffix)

diversify 源自于法语 "diversifier,"  最终源于拉丁语 "divertere,"  意思是“转向不同的方向”。 "di-" 表示"分开"，"vertere" 表示"转动"。因此，diversified 的根本含义是“转向不同的方向”，从而形成了“多样化”的含义。
