extractor:/ɪkˈstræktər/| n. |提取器，萃取器|A device or process that extracts something.  It can refer to a machine, a tool, or a method used to separate or remove a substance from a mixture or to obtain information from a source.|例句：The coffee maker has a built-in bean extractor. 这台咖啡机有一个内置的咖啡豆提取器。|例句：The scientist used a special chemical extractor to isolate the compound. 科学家使用了一种特殊的化学提取器来分离该化合物。|近义词：separator, remover, isolator|反义词：injector, inserter|名词
