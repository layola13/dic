gearings:/ˈɡɪərɪŋz/ | n. | 传动装置；齿轮系；配合；组织 | The system of gears by which power is transmitted from one part of a machine to another.  It refers to the arrangement of gears and their interaction to achieve a specific mechanical function, such as speed or torque changes. |例句：The car's gearings were worn out, requiring a complete overhaul. 这辆车的传动装置磨损严重，需要彻底检修。

例句：The intricate gearings of the clock mechanism ensured its precise timekeeping.  钟表的复杂齿轮系确保了其精确的计时。


近义词：transmission, mechanism, gearing system | 反义词：None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with something like "direct drive" (without gears)  | 词性：名词
