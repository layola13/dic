It describes something that is standing upright or in a vertical position.  The term is frequently used in biological contexts to describe the posture of hominids. | Example:  Homo erectus was a species of hominid characterized by its erect posture.  直立人是一种以其直立姿态为特征的原始人类。  | 近义词：upright, vertical, straight | 反义词：prostrate, recumbent, horizontal |  形容词
