decentest:/ˈdiːsntɪst/| adj.| 最正派的；最体面的 |  Superlative form of "decent," implying the highest degree of propriety, respectability, or quality. It's rarely used in modern English because superlative forms of adjectives ending in "-ent" are often considered awkward or overly formal.  |  例句：It's hard to say which of his novels is the decentest. 很难说他的哪部小说最体面。


近义词：most respectable, most proper, most suitable | 反义词：worst, most improper, most indecent | 词性：形容词
