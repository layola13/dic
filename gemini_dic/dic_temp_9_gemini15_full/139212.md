objectless:/ˈɒbdʒɪktləs/ /ˈɑːbdʒɛktləs/ | adj. | 无目标的；无对象的；缺乏目标的 | Lacking an object; without a goal or purpose.  The word describes something that doesn't have a specific target, aim, or recipient.  It often implies a sense of aimlessness or purposelessness. |例句：His life felt objectless after he retired.  他退休后感觉生活失去了目标。

His actions seemed objectless, lacking any clear intention. 他的行为似乎毫无目标，没有任何明确的意图。


近义词：purposeless, aimless, pointless, directionless, without purpose


反义词：purposeful, meaningful, goal-oriented, intentional


词性：形容词
