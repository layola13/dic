cystitides:/sɪsˈtaɪtaɪdiːz/| n.|膀胱炎（复数）|Inflammation of the urinary bladder.  The plural form of cystitis.  It refers to multiple instances of bladder inflammation.|Example:  Several cases of cystitides were reported in the hospital last week.  上周医院报告了几例膀胱炎病例。|近义词：bladder inflammation,  urocystitis (if specifying the urethra's involvement)|反义词：bladder health, absence of bladder inflammation|名词
