predelinquently:/ˌpriːdɪˈlɪŋkwəntli/ | adv. | 犯罪前地，预先犯罪地 |  Describes behavior or actions that precede or foreshadow delinquent behavior. It suggests a pattern of actions or attitudes that, if left unchecked, could lead to delinquency.  The word combines "pre-" (before) and "delinquent" (showing a tendency to commit crimes). |


例句：He was acting predelinquently, skipping school and engaging in petty theft, long before his arrest. = 他在被捕前就表现出犯罪倾向，逃学并参与小偷小摸。

The child's predelinquently aggressive behavior worried the teachers. = 孩子预先表现出的攻击性行为让老师们担忧。


近义词：antecedently delinquent, pre-criminally


反义词：law-abidingly, obediently


词性：副词
