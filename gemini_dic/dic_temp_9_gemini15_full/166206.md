prepurchases:/ˈpriːpɜːrtʃəsɪz/ | n. |预购，预先购买 | Purchases made in advance.  This term is primarily used in a business context, referring to orders placed before the official release or availability of a product or service.  | 例句：The company saw a surge in prepurchases for their new gaming console.  这家公司的新游戏机预购量激增。

| 例句：Prepurchases for the concert tickets exceeded expectations.  演唱会门票的预购量超出了预期。 | 近义词：preorders, advance orders, advance purchases | 反义词：post-purchases, after-sales |  词性：名词
