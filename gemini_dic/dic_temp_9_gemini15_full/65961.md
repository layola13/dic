exaggerate:/ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | vt. |夸大；夸张 | To represent (something) as being larger, better, or worse than it really is.  |例句：He exaggerated the size of the fish he caught. 他夸大了他钓到的鱼的大小。 |例句：Don't exaggerate your achievements; be modest. 不要夸大你的成就；要谦虚。|近义词：overstate, magnify, inflate |反义词：understate, downplay, minimize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exaggerates his problems. = 他夸大了他的问题。
She exaggerates her role in the project. = 她夸大了她在项目中的作用。

一般过去时:
He exaggerated the difficulty of the task. = 他夸大了这项任务的难度。
She exaggerated her illness to get out of work. = 她夸大了自己的病情以逃避工作。

现在完成时:
He has exaggerated his abilities throughout his career. = 他职业生涯中一直夸大自己的能力。
She has exaggerated the risks involved in the investment. = 她夸大了这项投资所涉及的风险。

过去完成时:
He had exaggerated his previous successes to impress the interviewer. = 他为了给面试官留下好印象而夸大了之前的成功。
She had exaggerated her knowledge of the subject to appear more intelligent. = 为了显得更聪明，她夸大了自己对这个主题的了解。

将来时:
He will exaggerate the impact of the news. = 他将会夸大这个新闻的影响。
She will exaggerate her feelings to get attention. = 她会夸大自己的感受以引起注意。

虚拟语气:
If he exaggerated, he would lose credibility. = 如果他夸大其词，他会失去可信度。
I wish she hadn't exaggerated the situation. = 我真希望她没有夸大其词。

被动语态:
The dangers of smoking are often exaggerated. = 吸烟的危险性往往被夸大。
His achievements were exaggerated by the media. = 他的成就被媒体夸大了。

疑问句:
Does he exaggerate frequently? = 他经常夸大其词吗？
Is she exaggerating the severity of the injury? = 她是否夸大了伤势的严重程度？

否定句:
He didn't exaggerate; he told the truth. = 他没有夸大；他说的是实话。
She didn't exaggerate the importance of the meeting. = 她没有夸大这次会议的重要性。
