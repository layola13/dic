factor-in: /ˈfæktər ɪn/ | vt. | 把…考虑进去；计入 | To include something as a part of a calculation or plan.  |例句：We need to factor in the cost of transportation. 我们需要把运输成本考虑进去。

例句：They factored in potential delays when setting the deadline. 他们在设定最后期限时考虑到了潜在的延误。


近义词：include, incorporate, take into account, consider, allow for

反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We factor in all expenses when budgeting. = 我们在预算时把所有费用都考虑进去了。
They factor in customer feedback when designing new products. = 他们在设计新产品时会考虑客户的反馈。


一般过去时:
She factored in the extra time needed for travel. = 她把额外的旅行时间考虑进去了。
The company factored in inflation when setting prices. = 公司在定价时考虑到了通货膨胀。


现在完成时:
We have factored in the potential risks. = 我们已经把潜在的风险考虑进去了。
They have factored in all the relevant data. = 他们已经考虑了所有相关数据。


过去完成时:
They had factored in the possibility of rain before the picnic. = 野餐前他们已经考虑到了下雨的可能性。
She had factored in the cost of materials before starting the project. = 在开始项目之前，她已经考虑了材料的成本。


将来时:
We will factor in the new regulations when updating our policies. = 我们将在更新政策时考虑新的规章制度。
They will factor in the market trends when making their investment decisions. = 他们在做出投资决策时将考虑市场趋势。


虚拟语气:
If we factored in the unforeseen circumstances, the outcome would be different. = 如果我们考虑了不可预见的情况，结果就会不同。
I wish we had factored in the possibility of a strike. = 我希望我们当时已经考虑到了罢工的可能性。


被动语态:
The additional costs were factored in by the project manager. = 项目经理已经把额外的成本考虑进去了。
All relevant factors should be factored in before making a decision. = 在做决定之前，所有相关因素都应该被考虑进去。


疑问句:
Have you factored in the travel time? = 你考虑了旅行时间吗？
Did they factor in the potential risks? = 他们考虑了潜在的风险吗？


否定句:
They did not factor in the rising fuel costs. = 他们没有考虑燃油成本上涨。
We didn't factor in the possibility of bad weather. = 我们没有考虑坏天气的可能性。
