sacrilegious:/ˈsækrɪˌleɪdʒəs/| adj.|亵渎神明的；不敬的；冒犯的| Profaning or violating something sacred; showing contempt or lack of reverence for something considered sacred.  |例句：It would be sacrilegious to paint the church.  把教堂粉刷了，这将是亵渎神明的。

例句：His remarks were considered sacrilegious by many members of the audience.  他的言论被许多听众认为是亵渎神明的。


近义词：blasphemous, impious, irreverent, profane

反义词：reverent, pious, respectful


词性：形容词

词源:
From Middle French *sacrilegieux, from Late Latin sacrilegius, from Latin sacer ("sacred") + legere ("to gather, collect").  The original meaning referred to the theft of sacred objects.  The meaning has broadened to encompass any act of disrespect towards something sacred.
