chromograph:/ˈkroʊməɡræf/| n. |色谱图；显色记录|A chromograph is a visual record of the separation of components of a mixture by chromatography.  It typically shows peaks representing different components, with their retention times and relative amounts. |Example: The chromograph clearly showed the presence of three distinct compounds in the sample.  色谱图清楚地显示样品中存在三种不同的化合物。|近义词: chromatogram |反义词:  (无明显的反义词)|名词
