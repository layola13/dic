outwishing:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃɪŋ/| v.|胜过，超过（愿望）|To surpass in wishing; to exceed in the intensity or fervor of one's wishes.|例句：He was outwishing everyone else for the promotion. 他比任何人都渴望得到晋升。|近义词：outshine, surpass, exceed|反义词：underestimate, undervalue

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am outwishing my colleagues for the new project. = 我比我的同事们更渴望得到这个新项目。
They are outwishing their competitors for market share. = 他们在争夺市场份额方面胜过他们的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She outwished her sister for the lead role in the play. = 她比她姐姐更渴望在戏剧中担任主角。
We outwished them for the better seats at the concert. = 我们比他们更渴望在音乐会上获得更好的座位。


现在完成时:
He has outwished everyone for a chance to travel abroad. = 他一直都比任何人都渴望有机会出国旅行。
They have outwished the other teams for the championship title. = 他们一直都比其他队伍更渴望赢得冠军头衔。


过去完成时:
She had outwished him for years for the same position. = 多年来，她一直比他更渴望得到同一个职位。
They had outwished their rivals for the contract before the negotiations even began. = 在谈判甚至开始之前，他们就已经比竞争对手更渴望获得这份合同。


将来时:
I will outwish them all for the top spot. = 我将比他们所有人都更渴望获得第一名。
They will outwish their opponents for victory in the upcoming election. = 在即将到来的选举中，他们将比对手更渴望获胜。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could outwish my anxieties. = 我希望我能克服我的焦虑。
If only I could outwish this feeling of inadequacy. = 但愿我能克服这种自卑感。


被动语态:
His desires were outwished by her ambition. = 他的愿望被她的雄心壮志所超越。
The team's hopes were outwished by the superior skills of their opponents. = 球队的希望被对手高超的技能所超越。


疑问句:
Does he outwish others for the prize? = 他是否比其他人更渴望获得奖品？
Are they outwishing their rivals in this competition? = 在这场比赛中，他们是否比竞争对手更渴望获胜？


否定句:
I don't outwish anyone for material possessions. = 我并不比任何人更渴望物质财富。
They didn't outwish their colleagues for the promotion, they simply worked harder. = 他们并没有比同事们更渴望晋升，他们只是工作更努力。

PHRASE->
outwish for success = 渴望成功
outwish for a better life = 渴望更好的生活
outwish for recognition = 渴望得到认可
outwish for happiness = 渴望幸福
outwish for a brighter future = 渴望更美好的未来
