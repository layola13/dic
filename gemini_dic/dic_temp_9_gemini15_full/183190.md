reprehensory:/ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsəri/| adj.|应受谴责的；可责备的；值得批评的| deserving blame or censure; blameworthy.  The reprehensible behavior of the company led to a loss of public trust. 公司应受谴责的行为导致公众信任的丧失。|近义词：blameworthy, censurable, reprehensible|反义词：laudable, commendable, praiseworthy

词源:
reprehensory  comes from the Latin word "reprehendere," meaning "to rebuke, reprimand, or find fault with."  The suffix "-ory" forms adjectives meaning "tending to," "serving to," or "of the nature of."  Therefore, "reprehensory" means "tending to rebuke or blame."
