disponge:/dɪˈspʌndʒ/| v.|使沮丧；使灰心；|to lose courage or hope; to become despondent|例句：The constant rain dispirited the hikers.  持续的雨水使徒步旅行者沮丧。|例句：His failure to get the job dispirited him for weeks. 他没有得到这份工作，这让他沮丧了好几个星期。|近义词：depress, discourage, demoralize |反义词：encourage, inspire, uplift|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant negativity dispirits her. = 持续的消极情绪让她沮丧。
The news dispirits many investors. = 这个消息让许多投资者灰心丧气。

一般过去时:
The bad weather dispirited the team. = 恶劣的天气使球队士气低落。
The loss dispirited the players. = 失败让球员们沮丧。


现在完成时:
Repeated failures have dispirited him. = 反复的失败使他灰心丧气。
This setback has dispirited the whole project team. = 这次挫折使整个项目团队士气低落。


过去完成时:
He had been dispirited by previous failures. = 他之前因为屡次失败而灰心丧气。
They had been dispirited by the lack of progress. = 他们因为进展缓慢而感到沮丧。


将来时:
This news will disspirit many. = 这个消息将会让很多人灰心。
Further setbacks will disspirit the team. = 进一步的挫折将会使球队士气低落。


虚拟语气:
If I had known this, it would have dispirited me. = 如果我知道这个，我就会灰心丧气。
I wish that news hadn't dispirited me so much. = 我真希望那个消息没有让我这么沮丧。


被动语态:
The team was dispirited by the loss. = 球队因为失败而士气低落。
The workers were dispirited by the lack of recognition. = 工人们因为缺乏认可而感到沮丧。


疑问句:
Does this news disspirit you? = 这个消息让你沮丧吗？
Will this failure disspirit the team? = 这次失败会让球队士气低落吗？


否定句:
The news didn't disspirit me as much as I thought it would. = 这个消息并没有像我想象的那样让我沮丧。
The challenge did not disspirit her. = 这个挑战并没有让她灰心。
