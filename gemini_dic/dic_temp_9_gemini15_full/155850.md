phosphorize: /ˈfɒsfəraɪz/ | vt. | 使磷化；用磷处理 | To treat (a substance) with phosphorus.  The process often involves coating a substance with a phosphor to make it luminescent. | 例句：The screen was phosphorized to enhance its brightness.  屏幕经过磷化处理以增强亮度。| 近义词：phosphoresce (不及物动词，指发磷光) | 反义词：dephosphorize (脱磷)
