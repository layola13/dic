landslidden:/ˈlænzˌlɪdn/| adj.| 山崩的；滑坡的 |  Describes something that has been affected by a landslide.  It typically modifies a noun, indicating that the noun has been damaged or moved by a landslide. |例句：The landslidden road was impassable.  山崩后的道路无法通行。  The landslidden hillside was unstable.  滑坡的山坡不稳定。|近义词：landslide-affected,  collapsed, devastated |反义词：stable, intact


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The road is landslidden. = 道路发生了山崩。
The house is landslidden. = 房子被山体滑坡掩埋了。


一般过去时:
The village was landslidden. = 村庄被山体滑坡摧毁了。
The bridge was landslidden last week. = 上周桥梁发生了山体滑坡。


现在完成时:
The area has been landslidden. = 该地区发生了山体滑坡。
Several houses have been landslidden by the recent storm. = 最近的风暴导致几座房屋被山体滑坡掩埋。


过去完成时:
The road had been landslidden before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，道路就已经发生了山体滑坡。
The entire slope had been landslidden by the time the rain stopped. = 等雨停的时候，整个斜坡都发生了山体滑坡。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
