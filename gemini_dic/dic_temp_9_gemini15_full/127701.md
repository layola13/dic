neglect:/nɪˈɡlekt/| vt. n.|忽视，忽略，疏忽；玩忽职守 | to fail to care for properly; to give little attention or consideration to |The garden was neglected and overgrown. 花园杂草丛生，无人照管。|近义词：ignore, disregard, overlook |反义词：attend to, heed, cherish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He neglects his studies. 他忽视他的学习。
She neglects her health. 她忽略了她的健康。

一般过去时:
They neglected to inform us. 他们忘了通知我们。
He neglected his duties. 他玩忽职守。

现在完成时:
I have neglected my responsibilities. 我疏忽了我的责任。
She has neglected her friends. 她冷落了她的朋友们。

过去完成时:
He had neglected to lock the door. 他忘记锁门了。
They had neglected their promises. 他们没有遵守诺言。

将来时:
I will not neglect my work. 我不会忽视我的工作。
They will neglect their children. 他们将忽视他们的孩子。

虚拟语气:
If he had not neglected his studies, he would have passed the exam. 如果他没有忽视他的学习，他就能通过考试了。
I wish I hadn't neglected my health. 我真希望我没有忽视我的健康。

被动语态:
His warnings were neglected. 他的警告被忽视了。
The garden was neglected for years. 花园多年无人照料。


疑问句:
Why did you neglect your duties? 你为什么玩忽职守？
Did they neglect to tell you? 他们有没有忘记告诉你？


否定句:
I will not neglect my family. 我不会忽视我的家人。
He didn't neglect his responsibilities. 他没有忽略他的责任。
