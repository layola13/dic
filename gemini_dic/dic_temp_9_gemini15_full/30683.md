certainest: /ˈsɜːrtnɪst/ | adj. | 最确定的，最可靠的 | Most certain; most reliable.  This word is a superlative form of "certain," emphasizing the highest degree of certainty.  It is less frequently used than "most certain" or "surest." |

例句：

Of all the theories presented, his was the certainest. = 在所有提出的理论中，他的是最确定的。

The certainest way to succeed is through hard work and dedication. = 成功最可靠的方法是努力工作和奉献。


近义词：surest, most certain, most reliable, most dependable

反义词：most uncertain, least reliable, most doubtful


词性：形容词
