ocularly:/ˈɑːkjʊləri/| adv.|用眼睛地；视觉上地|By means of sight; visually.  It refers to something perceived or experienced through the eyes. |例句：Ocularly, the painting is stunning. = 从视觉上看，这幅画令人惊叹。|例句：He ocularly witnessed the accident. = 他亲眼目睹了事故。|近义词：visually, perceptually|反义词：inaudiblely, intangibly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have different tenses like verbs.  It describes the manner in which something is experienced.  The grammatical structures are simple.  It's used in statements, questions (less common), and rarely in other constructions.
