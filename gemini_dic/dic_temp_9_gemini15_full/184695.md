resubjugate:/ˌriːˈsʌbdʒʊɡeɪt/| vt.|重新征服；再次征服|To conquer or bring under control again; to subdue again.|例句：The empire attempted to resubjugate the rebellious province.  帝国试图重新征服叛乱的省份。

例句：The occupying forces tried to resubjugate the population through fear and intimidation. 占领军试图通过恐惧和恐吓来再次征服人民。


近义词：reconquer, subdue, subjugate, conquer|反义词：liberate, emancipate|动词
