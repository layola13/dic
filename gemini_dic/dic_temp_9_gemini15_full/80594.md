googlies:/ˈɡuːɡliz/| n. |怪球，难题，意外事件 |A googly is a type of ball bowled in cricket that spins in an unexpected way, making it difficult for the batsman to hit.  It can also refer to something unexpected or puzzling.  |例句：The bowler bowled a googly that completely deceived the batsman. 投球手投出一个怪球，完全骗过了击球手。

The unexpected twist in the plot was a real googly.  情节中出人意料的转折是一个真正的难题。|近义词：surprise, puzzle, curveball |反义词：predictable, expected
