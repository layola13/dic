saluted:/səˈluːtɪd/ | v. |敬礼；致敬；欢迎；赞扬 | To greet someone in a formal way, often by raising a hand to the head.  It can also mean to express approval or admiration. |例句：The soldiers saluted the flag. 士兵们向国旗敬礼。

The children saluted their teacher. 孩子们向老师敬礼。


近义词：greeted, honored, acclaimed, praised | 反义词：ignored, disrespected, scorned | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salutes the flag every morning. 他每天早上都向国旗敬礼。
She salutes her colleagues with a friendly smile. 她以友好的笑容向同事们致意。


一般过去时:
The soldier saluted his commanding officer.  士兵向他的指挥官敬礼。
They saluted the bravery of the rescuers. 他们赞扬了救援人员的英勇行为。


现在完成时:
He has saluted the flag countless times. 他无数次向国旗敬礼。
The crowd has saluted the champion's victory.  人群为冠军的胜利欢呼。


过去完成时:
