forktail:/ˈfɔːkteɪl/| n.|叉尾鸟；(某些鸟类的)叉形尾羽|A bird with a forked tail.  The term often refers to birds in the family Motacillidae (wagtails and pipits), but can also be used more generally for any bird with a deeply forked tail.|例句：The black-backed forktail is a common sight in the Himalayas.  喜马拉雅山区经常可以看到黑背叉尾鸟。

The forktail's long, elegant tail feathers are a distinctive feature. 叉尾鸟又长又优雅的尾羽是一个显著特征。|近义词：wagtail (for specific types), bird with a forked tail|反义词：无|词性:名词
