disenchanted: /ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃæntɪd/ | adj. |不再着迷的；醒悟的；幻灭的 | No longer believing in something that one previously believed in; disillusioned. |例句：After years of disappointment, she became disenchanted with her job.  多年失望后，她对工作感到幻灭。|例句：The once-optimistic young man became disenchanted with politics after witnessing corruption firsthand.  曾经乐观的年轻人亲眼目睹腐败后，对政治感到幻灭。|近义词： disillusioned, disappointed, disillusioned, jaded, cynical |反义词：enchanted, charmed, delighted
