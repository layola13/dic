fabrications:/ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 捏造；虚构；伪造 | The act of inventing or creating something false; a lie or a falsehood, often deliberately created.  It can refer to a single instance of fabrication or a collection of them. | Example: His entire story was a fabrication; the police found clear evidence to disprove his claims.  他的整个故事都是捏造的；警方找到了明确的证据来反驳他的说法。


Example: The journalist was accused of fabricating evidence to support his controversial article.  这位记者被指控伪造证据以支持其有争议的文章。


近义词：inventions, concoctions, forgeries, lies, falsehoods | 反义词：truths, facts, realities |  词性：名词
