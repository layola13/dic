crafted:/kræftɪd/| adj.| 精巧制作的；精心制作的 | skillfully made or done; carefully planned and executed.  |例句：The handcrafted jewelry was beautifully crafted. 这件手工珠宝制作精良。
The speech was carefully crafted to appeal to a wide audience. 这篇演讲经过精心设计，以吸引广大听众。|近义词：fashioned, designed, made, constructed |反义词：crude, rough, clumsy, ill-made


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The artist crafts beautiful sculptures. = 艺术家创作美丽的雕塑。
They craft innovative solutions to complex problems. = 他们为复杂的问题设计创新的解决方案。


一般过去时:
She crafted a compelling narrative for her novel. = 她为她的小说创作了一个引人入胜的故事。
He crafted a winning strategy for the chess game. = 他为这场象棋比赛制定了一个制胜策略。


现在完成时:
They have crafted a reputation for excellence. = 他们已经树立了卓越的声誉。
She has crafted several successful businesses. = 她已经创建了几家成功的企业。


过去完成时:

They had crafted a response to the criticism before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前准备好对批评的回应。


将来时:

They will craft a new marketing campaign for the product launch. = 他们将为产品发布制定一个新的营销活动。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would craft a more detailed proposal. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会制定一个更详细的提案。
I wish I could craft a solution to this problem. = 我希望我能找到解决这个问题的办法。


被动语态:

The website was crafted to be user-friendly. = 网站的设计易于用户使用。


疑问句:
