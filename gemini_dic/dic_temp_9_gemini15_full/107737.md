lapa:/ˈlæpə/| n.|树懒的爪子；（某些鸟类的）喉咙；洞穴；小房间|The fleshy pad on a sloth's hand or foot, used for gripping branches.  Also, the throat of certain birds, or a cave or small room.|例句：The sloth used its lapa to cling to the tree branch.  树懒用它的爪子抓住树枝。|近义词：claw, hand, pouch, den|反义词：None readily applicable.
