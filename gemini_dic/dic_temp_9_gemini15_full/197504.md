sheilas:/ˈʃiːləz/| n. |姑娘们，女孩们（澳大利亚俚语）|A colloquial term in Australia, primarily used to refer to a group of women or girls.  The term carries a generally informal and friendly connotation, although it can sometimes be perceived as slightly dated.|例句：We saw a bunch of sheilas sunbaking on the beach. 我们看到一群姑娘在沙滩上晒太阳。


例句：The sheilas at the party were all having a great time. 派对上的姑娘们玩得很开心。


近义词：girls, women, ladies (less informal)


反义词：无


词性:名词
