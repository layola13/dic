nlink:/ˈnɪŋk/| n. |网络链接，超链接|A hyperlink, or nlink, is a word, phrase, or image that you can click on to jump to a new document or a new section within the same document.|Example:  The article contained many nlinks to relevant websites. 这篇文章包含许多指向相关网站的超链接。|近义词：hyperlink, URL|反义词：offline link(无网络链接)|名词
