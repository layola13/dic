enthusing:/ɪnˈθuːzɪŋ/| v.|热心；热情洋溢地谈论| expressing great enthusiasm; showing or feeling great excitement and eagerness.|例句：She was enthusing about her trip to Italy. 她热情洋溢地谈论着她去意大利的旅行。|例句：The children were enthusing over their new toys.孩子们对他们的新玩具非常兴奋。|近义词：exulting, rhapsodizing, raving|反义词：apathy, indifference


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enthuses about his new job every day. = 他每天都对他的新工作充满热情。
They are enthusing over the latest technology. = 他们对最新的科技感到兴奋不已。


一般过去时:
She enthused about the concert for hours. = 她兴奋地谈论了几个小时的音乐会。
He enthused to his friends about his successful project. = 他兴致勃勃地向朋友们讲述了他的成功项目。


现在完成时:
I have enthused about this idea since I first heard it. = 从我第一次听到这个主意以来，我一直对此充满热情。
They have enthused about the potential of renewable energy. = 他们一直对可再生能源的潜力充满热情。


过去完成时:
She had enthused about the book before she even read it. = 在她甚至读这本书之前，她就对它充满了热情。
He had enthused about the possibility of a promotion before he got it. = 在他得到晋升之前，他就对晋升的可能性充满了热情。


将来时:
She will enthuse about her wedding plans all day. = 她会一整天都兴致勃勃地谈论她的婚礼计划。
They will enthuse about their trip to the beach. = 他们会兴致勃勃地谈论他们去海滩的旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would enthuse more about opportunities. = 如果我是你，我会对机会更加热情。
I wish I could enthuse about this task, but I find it boring. = 我希望我能对这项任务充满热情，但我发现它很无聊。


被动语态:
The new product was enthused over by many customers. = 许多顾客对这款新产品赞不绝口。
His ideas were not enthused over by his colleagues. = 他的想法并没有得到同事的认可。


疑问句:
Does he enthuse about his work? = 他对他的工作充满热情吗？
Were they enthusing about the new policy? = 他们对新政策感到兴奋吗？


否定句:
He didn't enthuse about the movie. = 他对这部电影并不热衷。
They didn't enthuse about the changes. = 他们对这些改变并不热情。


PHRASE->
enthuse about = 热衷于……
enthuse over = 对……兴奋
enthuse with = 对…充满热情
to enthuse somebody = 使某人兴奋
enthuse someone about something = 使某人对某事充满热情
