nonrepayable: /ˌnɒnˈriːpeɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可偿还的 |  Not able to be repaid; irrecoverable.  This term typically refers to loans or grants that do not require repayment. | Example: The government provided nonrepayable grants to small businesses. 政府向小型企业提供了不可偿还的拨款。 |近义词：irredeemable, unrepayable | 反义词：repayable, refundable
