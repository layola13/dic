microsofts:/ˈmaɪkrəˌsɒfts/| n. |微软公司（复数形式）|The possessive form of Microsoft, referring to the company's products, services, or holdings.  It's less common than "Microsoft's" but grammatically correct.|例句：Microsoft's new operating system is impressive. 微软的新操作系统令人印象深刻。|近义词：Microsoft's (singular possessive)|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a noun and functions similarly to other company names used possessively.  Therefore, tense conjugations don't apply directly to the word itself, but rather to verbs used in sentences containing it.
