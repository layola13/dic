denuders:/dɪˈnuːdərz/| n.|脱衣者；去除者；（尤指）大气污染物去除剂|A denuder is a device or process that removes something, especially pollutants from the air.  It can refer to a person who removes clothing, but this usage is less common and often carries a negative connotation.|例句：The researchers used a denuder to remove acidic gases from the air sample. 研究人员使用去除器从空气样本中去除酸性气体。  The denuders in the factory are designed to reduce emissions. 工厂里的污染物去除装置旨在减少排放。|近义词：remover, purifier, scrubber|反义词：polluter, contaminator|名词
