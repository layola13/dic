self-discovery: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈskʌvəri/ | n. | 自我发现，自我探索 | The process of learning about one's own personality, strengths, weaknesses, values, and beliefs.  It often involves introspection, reflection, and experimentation. | Example:  Through years of self-discovery, she finally realized her passion for art. 通过多年的自我探索，她最终发现了自己对艺术的热情。 |近义词：self-awareness, self-understanding, introspection | 反义词：self-deception, ignorance
