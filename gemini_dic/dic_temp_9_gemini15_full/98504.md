instantaneousness:/ˌɪnstənˈteɪniəsnəs/ | n. |瞬时性；即时性 |The quality or state of being instantaneous; happening or existing at a particular instant.  It refers to the immediacy and lack of delay in something's occurrence or effect.  |例句：The instantaneousness of the internet allows for immediate communication across the globe.  互联网的瞬时性使得全球即时通讯成为可能。

The instantaneousness of the reaction surprised everyone.  反应的瞬时性让每个人都大吃一惊。|近义词：immediacy, simultaneity, rapidity |反义词：delay, gradualness, slowness
