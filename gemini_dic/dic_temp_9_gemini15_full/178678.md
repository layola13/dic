recriminate:/rɪˈkrɪmɪneɪt/| vt.|反责；反控；互相指责|To accuse in return; to make counter-accusations.  It implies a mutual exchange of blame, often with escalating accusations.|例句：The two siblings constantly recriminate each other, making it difficult to understand the truth of the matter.  两个兄弟姐妹不断互相指责，很难弄清事情的真相。

例句：After the accident, the drivers began to recriminate, each blaming the other for the collision. 事故发生后，司机们开始互相指责，每个人都责怪对方导致了碰撞。


近义词：counter-accuse, retort, reciprocate accusations, blame, accuse

反义词：excuse, forgive, pardon, absolve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recriminate each other daily. = 他们每天互相指责。
