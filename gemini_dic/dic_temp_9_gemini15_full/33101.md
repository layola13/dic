christens:/ˈkrɪs(ə)n/| vt.|为……举行洗礼；命名；|To perform a Christian baptism on; to give a name to (a person, place, or thing).|例句：The priest christened the baby. 牧师为婴儿举行了洗礼。

例句：They christened their new boat "The Sea Serpent". 他们为他们的新船命名为“海蛇”。|近义词：baptize, name, dedicate|反义词：unname
