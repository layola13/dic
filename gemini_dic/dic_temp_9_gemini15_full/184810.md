resurveys:/ˌriːˈsɜːrveɪz/| v.|重新测量；再次调查|To survey again; to conduct a survey again.|The team resurveyed the land to correct errors in the previous map.  团队重新测量了土地以纠正先前地图中的错误。

The city council decided to resurvey public opinion on the proposed new park. 市议会决定再次调查公众对拟建新公园的意见。|近义词：reexamine, reinvestigate|反义词：initially survey|动词
