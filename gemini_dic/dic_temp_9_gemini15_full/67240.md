expulse:/ɪkˈspʌls/ | vt. |驱逐；逐出；排出；喷出 |To drive or force out (someone or something) from a place, group, or organization.  |例句：The students were expelled from school for cheating. 学生们因作弊而被学校开除。

例句：The volcano expelled a cloud of ash into the air. 火山喷出一团火山灰到空中。

近义词：eject, banish, expel, oust, dismiss

反义词：admit, welcome, include

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school expels students who cheat. = 学校开除作弊的学生。
The engine expels exhaust fumes. = 发动机排出废气。


一般过去时:
They expelled him from the club. = 他们把他逐出俱乐部。
The government expelled the diplomat. = 政府驱逐了这位外交官。


现在完成时:
The company has expelled several employees for misconduct. = 公司因行为不端已解雇了几名员工。
The volcano has expelled massive amounts of lava. = 火山喷出了大量的熔岩。


过去完成时:
They had expelled him before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经把他开除了。
The country had expelled the ambassador before the conflict escalated. = 在冲突升级之前，该国已经驱逐了大使。


将来时:
The school will expel him if he cheats again. = 如果他再次作弊，学校将会开除他。
The rocket will expel its fuel during launch. = 火箭在发射过程中将排出燃料。


虚拟语气:
If he were expelled, he would appeal the decision. = 如果他被开除，他会对这个决定提出上诉。
I wish they hadn't expelled her from the team. = 我真希望他们没有把她逐出球队。


被动语态:
He was expelled from the university for plagiarism. = 他因剽窃而被大学开除。
The waste products are expelled from the body. = 废物从体内排出。


疑问句:
Was he expelled for his actions? = 他因为他的行为而被开除吗？
Will the protesters be expelled from the building? = 示威者会被驱逐出大楼吗？


否定句:
They did not expel him from the group. = 他们没有把他逐出团体。
The machine does not expel enough air. = 这台机器排出的空气不够多。


PHRASE->
expel from = 从…中驱逐
expel by force = 强制驱逐
expel someone from a country = 将某人驱逐出境
expel air = 排出空气
expel gas = 排出气体


词源:
From Middle French expulser, from Latin expellere "to drive out," from ex- "out" + pellere "to drive, push."
