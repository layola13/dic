multidisciplined: /ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪnd/ | adj. | 多学科的，跨学科的 |  Drawing on knowledge and methods from multiple academic disciplines.  It implies an integrated approach, not simply a collection of separate disciplines. | 例句：The project requires a multidisciplined team of engineers, designers, and marketers.  这个项目需要一个由工程师、设计师和营销人员组成的多学科团队。

The research employed a multidisciplined approach to address the complex problem. 这项研究采用了多学科的方法来解决这个复杂的问题。


近义词：interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary | 反义词：single-disciplined, specialized


词源:  The word is a combination of "multi-" (meaning many or multiple), "discipline" (referring to a branch of knowledge or learning), and "-ed" (a past participle suffix indicating a state or condition).  The term emerged in the latter half of the 20th century as interdisciplinary studies gained prominence.
