reshot: /ˌriːˈʃɒt/ | vt. |重拍，重新拍摄| To film or photograph something again.  This usually implies a previous attempt was unsatisfactory.| 例句：The director decided to reshoot the scene because the lighting was bad. 导演决定重拍这场戏，因为灯光不好。


The scene needed to be reshot after the actor forgot his lines.  演员忘记台词后，需要重拍这场戏。| 近义词：refilm, re-photograph | 反义词：first take, original shot | 动词


USAGE->

They reshoot the commercial every year. = 他们每年都重拍广告。
He reshoots the scene until it's perfect. = 他一遍遍重拍场景直到完美。
