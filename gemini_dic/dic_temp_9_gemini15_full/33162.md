chromatoscope:/ˈkroʊmətəskoʊp/| n. |色谱仪，显色镜|A chromatoscope is an instrument used to analyze the colors of a substance, often by separating the components of a mixture based on their different properties, such as their absorption or reflection of light.  It is primarily used in the fields of chemistry and material science.|Example: The chemist used a chromatoscope to identify the different pigments in the paint sample.  化学家用色谱仪来鉴定油漆样品中的不同颜料。|近义词：spectroscope, colorimeter|反义词：N/A
