The word "comparation" is not a standard English word.  The correct word is **comparison**.


comparison: /kəmˈpærɪsn/ | n. | 比较，对照 | The act of comparing; a consideration or estimate of the similarities or dissimilarities between two or more things.  | 例句：The comparison of the two paintings revealed subtle differences in technique.  两幅画作的比较揭示了技巧上的细微差别。|近义词：contrast, similitude, parallel | 反义词: none specifically, but words suggesting lack of similarity could be used contextually, such as difference, dissimilarity. | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a comparison between the two books. = 他比较这两本书。
The teacher makes a comparison of different writing styles. = 老师比较不同的写作风格。


一般过去时:
She made a comparison of the two candidates' policies. = 她比较了两位候选人的政策。
We compared the prices of different models. = 我们比较了不同型号的价格。


现在完成时:
They have completed a thorough comparison of the data. = 他们已经完成了对数据的全面比较。
I have made several comparisons between these two theories. = 我已经对这两种理论进行了多次比较。


过去完成时:
After I had made a comparison, I found the differences were minimal. = 在我比较之后，我发现差异很小。
She had already made a comparison before presenting her findings. = 在她提出研究结果之前，她已经进行了比较。


将来时:
We will make a comparison of the results next week. = 我们下周将对结果进行比较。
The scientist will make a comparison of his data with others. = 科学家将把他的数据与其他数据进行比较。


虚拟语气:
If I had made a more thorough comparison, I would have noticed the error. = 如果我进行了更全面的比较，我就会注意到错误。
I wish I had made a comparison earlier. = 我希望我早点进行比较。


被动语态:
The products were compared in terms of price and quality. = 产品在价格和质量方面进行了比较。
A comparison of the two methods is being made. = 正在对这两种方法进行比较。


疑问句:
What is the basis for your comparison? = 你比较的基础是什么？
Can you make a comparison between A and B? = 你能比较一下 A 和 B 吗？


否定句:
He didn't make a fair comparison. = 他没有进行公平的比较。
There is no comparison between the two events. = 这两件事没有可比性。


PHRASE->
in comparison to/with = 与...相比
by comparison = 相比之下
make a comparison = 进行比较
draw a comparison = 作比较
comparative analysis = 比较分析
