refreshingly:/rɪˈfreʃɪŋli/| adv. |令人耳目一新地；清爽地；令人振奋地 |In a way that is pleasantly new, different, or exciting.  It suggests a positive feeling of novelty and revitalization. |例句：The new design is refreshingly different from the old one.  新的设计与旧的设计相比令人耳目一新。
The cool breeze was refreshingly invigorating after the hot day. 炎热的一天过后，凉爽的微风令人精神焕发。|近义词：invigoratingly, stimulatingly, surprisingly, pleasingly |反义词：disappointingly, tediously, predictably
