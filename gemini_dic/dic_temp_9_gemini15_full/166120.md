prepping:/ˈprɛpɪŋ/| v. |准备；预备；|The act of preparing something. It is often used informally.|例句：I spent the weekend prepping for the exam. = 我周末都在为考试做准备。  
例句：She's prepping dinner. = 她在准备晚餐。|近义词：preparing, getting ready, readying |反义词：neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am prepping for the interview. = 我正在为面试做准备。
He preps his meals in advance. = 他提前准备他的饭菜。

一般过去时:
I prepped all morning for the presentation. = 我整个上午都在为演示做准备。
They prepped the car for the long journey. = 他们为长途旅行准备了汽车。

现在完成时:
I have prepped all the ingredients. = 我已经准备好了所有的配料。
She has prepped the documents for the meeting. = 她已经为会议准备好了文件。

过去完成时:
I had prepped the slides before the presentation started. = 演示开始前我已经准备好了幻灯片。
They had prepped the house for the party guests. = 他们已经为派对客人准备好了房子。

将来时:
I will prep the vegetables tonight. = 我今晚将准备蔬菜。
She will prep her speech tomorrow. = 她明天将准备她的演讲。

虚拟语气:
If I had prepped more, I would have done better on the test. = 如果我准备得更多，考试成绩会更好。
I wish I had prepped for the unexpected questions. = 我真希望我为那些意料之外的问题做好了准备。

被动语态:
The materials were prepped by the team. = 材料是由团队准备的。
The food is being prepped for the event. = 正在为活动准备食物。

疑问句:
Are you prepping for the marathon? = 你正在为马拉松做准备吗？
Have they prepped the equipment yet? = 他们已经准备好设备了吗？

否定句:
I didn't prep enough for the test. = 我没有为考试做足够的准备。
He hasn't prepped his notes for the meeting. = 他还没有为会议准备他的笔记。


PHRASE->
prep for = 为...做准备
prep ahead = 预先准备
prep school = 预备学校
prep work = 预备工作
prep course = 预备课程
