overexercising: /ˌoʊvərˈɛksərˌsaɪzɪŋ/ | v.ing | 过度运动 | The act of exercising excessively, leading to potential negative consequences for physical and mental health.  | Example: Overexercising can lead to injuries, burnout, and decreased performance.  过度运动会导致受伤、倦怠和表现下降。 |近义词: overtraining, excessive exercise | 反义词: undertraining, insufficient exercise
