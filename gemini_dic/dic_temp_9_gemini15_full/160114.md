It's typically shaped like an apple, hence its name.  The term is derived from the French word "pomme," meaning apple. |The antique chair featured elegant pommelions on its arms.  古老的椅子扶手上装饰着精致的苹果形装饰物。| finial, knob, boss |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with plain, undecorated surfaces) | 名词
