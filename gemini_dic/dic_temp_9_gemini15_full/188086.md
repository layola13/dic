roundabouts:/ˈraʊndəbaʊts/| n.|环岛，旋转木马|A roundabout is a circular road junction where traffic flows around a central island.  It is designed to improve traffic flow and reduce accidents compared to traditional intersections. A roundabout can also refer to a merry-go-round or carousel.|例句：We took the roundabout to avoid the traffic jam. 我们绕了环岛以避开交通堵塞。

例句：The children loved riding on the roundabout at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐会上乘坐旋转木马。|近义词：rotary, traffic circle, merry-go-round|反义词：intersection (traditional), four-way stop
