in-your-face:/ˌɪn jɔːr ˈfeɪs/ | adj. | 粗鲁的；侵略性的；直截了当的；毫不掩饰的 |  Describes something that is extremely obvious, blunt, or confrontational, often in an aggressive or shocking way. It suggests a lack of subtlety or tact. | 例句：His in-your-face attitude offended many people.  他的粗鲁态度冒犯了许多人。

例句：The artist's in-your-face paintings were both captivating and unsettling.  这位艺术家的直白绘画既引人入胜又令人不安。


近义词：aggressive, confrontational, blunt, blatant, obvious, shocking, upfront

反义词：subtle, tactful, indirect, understated, delicate
