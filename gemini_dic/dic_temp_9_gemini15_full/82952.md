gunfight:/ˈɡʌnfaɪt/| n.|枪战|A fight in which guns are used.|例句：The gunfight left two men dead and several injured.  这场枪战造成两人死亡，数人受伤。|近义词：shootout, firefight|反义词：peaceful encounter, negotiation

Gunfights rarely happen in this peaceful town. = 在这个宁静的小镇，枪战很少发生。
