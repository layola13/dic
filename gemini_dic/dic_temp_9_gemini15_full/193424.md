seizer:/ˈsiːzər/| n. v.| 癫痫发作；夺取者；没收者；|  A seizer is someone or something that seizes.  This can refer to a person who takes something forcefully, a medical event like an epileptic seizure, or a legal action like the seizure of property. |例句：The police made a seizer of the illegal drugs.  警方查获了这些非法毒品。

The sudden seizer left him unconscious. 突发的癫痫发作使他失去了知觉。|近义词：attacker, confiscator, arrester |反义词：releaser, giver
