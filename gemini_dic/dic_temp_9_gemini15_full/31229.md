chanted:/ˈtʃæntɪd/| v.|吟唱，反复念诵|to repeat or say something again and again in a rhythmic way, often in a monotonous tone.|例句：The monks chanted prayers all night.  僧侣们整夜都在吟诵祈祷文。
例句：The crowd chanted the team's name.  人群反复高喊着球队的名字。|近义词：recited, intoned, hummed|反义词：whispered, mumbled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chant slogans at every rally. = 他们在每次集会上都高喊口号。
He chants mantras every morning. = 他每天早晨都诵念咒语。


一般过去时:
The choir chanted a beautiful hymn. = 合唱团吟唱了一首优美的圣歌。
She chanted a lullaby to her baby. = 她对着婴儿吟唱催眠曲。


现在完成时:
They have chanted for peace for many years. = 他们多年来一直为和平而高呼。
The fans have chanted their support throughout the game. = 在整场比赛中，球迷们一直在高声表达支持。


过去完成时:
They had chanted the anthem before the president arrived. = 总统到达之前，他们已经唱完了国歌。
The protestors had chanted slogans for hours before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，抗议者们已经高喊口号几个小时了。


将来时:
They will chant their demands at the demonstration. = 他们将在示威活动中高喊他们的要求。
The supporters will chant the team's name throughout the match. = 支持者们将在整场比赛中高喊球队的名字。


虚拟语气:
If they chanted louder, they might be heard. = 如果他们喊得更大声，他们可能会被听到。
I wish they chanted less during the movie. = 我希望他们在电影放映期间少喊叫一些。


被动语态:
The song was chanted by the whole congregation. = 整個會眾一起吟唱这首歌。
The mantra was chanted repeatedly throughout the ceremony. = 在整个仪式中，咒语被反复吟诵。


疑问句:
What did they chant at the protest? = 他们在抗议活动中喊了什么？
Were they chanting religious hymns? = 他们是否在吟唱宗教圣歌？


否定句:
They did not chant during the sermon. = 他们在布道期间没有吟唱。
She didn't chant the spell correctly. = 她没有正确地念出咒语。
