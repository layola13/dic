homicide:/ˈhɒmɪsaɪd/ /ˈhɑːməsaɪd/| n.| 杀人，凶杀案 |The unlawful killing of one person by another.  Homicide encompasses a broad range of actions, from intentional murder to accidental killings. |例句：The police are investigating the homicide. 警方正在调查这起凶杀案。

The suspect was charged with first-degree homicide.  嫌疑人被指控犯有一级谋杀罪。


近义词：murder, manslaughter, killing |反义词：birth, creation
