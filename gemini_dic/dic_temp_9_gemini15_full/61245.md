emanatist:/ˈɛmənətɪst/| n. |散发主义者，气息论者|A person who believes in emanatism, a philosophical theory asserting that reality is fundamentally spiritual and that material things are emanations or outflows from a spiritual source.  This belief often involves a hierarchical structure with a supreme spiritual being at the top.|Example:  The emanatist argued that the physical world is merely a shadow of the true spiritual realm.|近义词：idealist, spiritualist|反义词：materialist, pragmatist|名词
