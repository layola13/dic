disaventurous: /ˌdɪsædˈvɛntʃərəs/ | adj. | 不爱冒险的；胆小的 |  Lacking a spirit of adventure; timid.  It describes someone who avoids risk and excitement. | 例句：He led a disaventurous life, preferring the comfort of routine to the thrill of the unknown. 他过着不爱冒险的生活，宁愿享受日常生活的舒适，也不愿体验未知的刺激。

例句：Her disaventurous nature prevented her from applying for the challenging job overseas.  她胆小的性格使她无法申请那份具有挑战性的海外工作。


近义词：timid, cowardly, cautious, unadventurous

反义词：adventurous, daring, bold, intrepid

词性: adjective
