czarina:/sɑːˈzɑːrɪnə/| n.|女沙皇，女皇|A czarina is the female equivalent of a tsar, the title of the emperor of Russia.  The term derives from the Slavic word for Caesar.|Example: Catherine the Great was a powerful czarina of Russia.  叶卡捷琳娜大帝是俄罗斯一位强大的女沙皇。|近义词：empress, queen|反义词：tsar
