commercializes: /ˈkɔːmɜːrʃəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使商业化；商业化 | To exploit (something) for commercial gain.  To adapt or change something to make it suitable for commercial purposes. |例句：The company commercializes its research findings by selling licenses to other firms.  这家公司通过向其他公司出售许可证来将它的研究成果商业化。

The university is accused of commercializing its research.  这所大学被指控将其研究商业化。


近义词：monetize, market, exploit, profit from | 反义词：de-commercialize, preserve, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company commercializes its new technology. = 这家公司正在将其新技术商业化。
They commercialize their ideas through various channels. = 他们通过各种渠道将他们的想法商业化。


一般过去时:
The artist commercialized his paintings by selling prints. =这位艺术家通过出售印刷品将他的绘画作品商业化。
The government commercialized the state-owned enterprises. = 政府将国有企业商业化。


现在完成时:
The firm has commercialized several products this year. =该公司今年已将几种产品商业化。
They have commercialized their software and made it available online. = 他们已经将他们的软件商业化，并使其在线可用。


过去完成时:
The company had commercialized the product before the competitor did. = 在竞争对手之前，该公司已经将该产品商业化。
Before the launch, they had commercialized the marketing strategy. = 在发布之前，他们已经将营销策略商业化。


将来时:
The startup will commercialize its innovative app next year. = 这家初创公司将于明年将其创新的应用程序商业化。
They will commercialize the technology through partnerships. = 他们将通过合作关系将这项技术商业化。


虚拟语气:
If they commercialized the technology, they would make a fortune. = 如果他们将这项技术商业化，他们将赚一大笔钱。
I wish they hadn't commercialized that beautiful artwork. = 我真希望他们没有将那件美丽的艺术品商业化。


被动语态:
The new drug is being commercialized by a major pharmaceutical company. = 一家大型制药公司正在将这种新药商业化。
The technology was commercialized too quickly, leading to problems. = 这项技术商业化得太快，导致了一些问题。


疑问句:
How will the company commercialize its latest invention? = 公司将如何将其最新发明商业化？
Have they successfully commercialized their research? = 他们成功地将其研究成果商业化了吗？


否定句:
The artist refused to commercialize his work. =这位艺术家拒绝将其作品商业化。
The government did not commercialize the public services. = 政府没有将公共服务商业化。


PHRASE->
commercialize a product = 将产品商业化
commercialize an idea = 将想法商业化
commercialize research = 将研究成果商业化
commercialize a technology = 将技术商业化
fully commercialize = 完全商业化


词源:
commercial + -ize (动词后缀)  commercial 源自法语 commerical，最终源自拉丁语 commercium (贸易，商业交往)
