self-dialog: /ˈsɛlfˌdaɪəlɒɡ/ | n. | 自我对话，内心独白 | Internal conversation or discourse that one has with oneself;  introspection.  It can be a conscious process of reasoning or problem-solving, or a less conscious stream of thoughts and feelings. | 例句：Engaging in self-dialog can help clarify thoughts and feelings.  进行自我对话有助于理清思路和感受。

例句：She used self-dialog to work through her anxieties before the presentation. 她利用自我对话来克服演讲前的焦虑。


近义词：inner speech, introspection, soliloquy, internal monologue


反义词：external conversation, dialogue with others


词性：名词
