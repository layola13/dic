failingly:/ˈfeɪlɪŋli/ | adv. |失败地；不足地；不够好地 |  In a failing manner; inadequately; insufficiently.  It describes an action or state characterized by failure or deficiency. |例句：He failingly attempted to explain his actions. 他试图解释自己的行为，但解释得不够充分。 He answered the question failingly. 他对这个问题的回答不够好。|近义词：inadequately, insufficiently, imperfectly, defectively |反义词：successfully, effectively, adequately, perfectly
