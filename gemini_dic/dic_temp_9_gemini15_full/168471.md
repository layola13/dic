probationerships:/prəˌbeɪʃənərʃɪps/ | n. | 试用期；见习期 | Periods of probationary employment or training.  Probationerships are typically a trial period to assess suitability for a permanent role or qualification. | Example:  The company offers probationerships for all new hires. 这家公司为所有新员工提供试用期。  Many teachers begin their careers with probationerships. 许多教师的职业生涯都是从试用期开始的。 | 近义词：apprenticeships, traineeships, internships | 反义词：permanent positions, established roles
