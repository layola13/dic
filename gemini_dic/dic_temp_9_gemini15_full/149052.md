owner-occupied:/ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjupaɪd/ | adj. | 业主的，自住的 |  Describes a property that is owned and lived in by its owner, as opposed to being rented out.  | Example: The majority of homes in this neighborhood are owner-occupied.  这个街区的房子大部分都是业主自住的。  |近义词：self-occupied, owner-inhabited | 反义词：rented, leased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house is owner-occupied. = 这房子是业主自住的。
He is owner-occupied. = 他住的是自己的房子。


一般过去时:
The apartment was owner-occupied until last year. = 这套公寓直到去年还是业主自住的。
They were owner-occupied for many years. = 他们住了很多年的自有房产。


现在完成时:
The property has been owner-occupied since 2010. = 这处房产自2010年以来一直是业主自住的。
She has been owner-occupied for over a decade. = 她自住房产超过十年了。


过去完成时:
The building had been owner-occupied before it was converted into apartments. = 这栋楼在改建成公寓之前是业主自住的。
They had been owner-occupied until the unexpected job relocation. = 他们在意外的调动工作之前一直是自住的。


将来时:
The house will be owner-occupied after the renovations are complete. = 装修完成后，这房子将成为业主自住的。
We will be owner-occupied by the end of the year. = 到今年年底，我们将住进自己的房子。


虚拟语气:
If the mortgage rates were lower, we would be owner-occupied. = 如果抵押贷款利率更低，我们将拥有自住房产。
I wish I were owner-occupied instead of renting. = 我希望我是自住房而不是租房。


被动语态:
The data showed that many properties were owner-occupied. = 数据显示，许多房产都是业主自住的。
It is believed that a large portion of the housing stock is owner-occupied. = 人们认为很大一部分住房是业主自住的。


疑问句:
Is this house owner-occupied? = 这房子是业主自住的吗？
Are most homes in this area owner-occupied? = 这个地区的大多数房屋都是业主自住的吗？


否定句:
The building is not owner-occupied; it's a rental property. = 这栋楼不是业主自住的；它是一处出租房产。
That house isn't owner-occupied; it's for sale. = 那栋房子不是业主自住的；它待售。
