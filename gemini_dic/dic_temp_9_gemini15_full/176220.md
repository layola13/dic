re-booking:/ˌriːˈbʊkɪŋ/| n. |重新预订，改签|The act of booking something again, often after a previous booking has been cancelled or changed.  It refers to the process of making a new reservation for a service or product, typically travel or accommodation.|例句：Due to unforeseen circumstances, I need to make a re-booking for my flight.  由于不可预见的情况，我需要重新预订我的航班。

例句：The hotel offered a free re-booking after the initial reservation was mistakenly cancelled.  酒店在最初的预订被错误取消后提供了免费重新预订的服务。|近义词：re-reservation, re-scheduling|反义词：cancellation|名词
