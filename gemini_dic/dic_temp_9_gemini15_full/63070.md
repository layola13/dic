enrols:/ɪnˈroʊlz/ | v. |注册，登记，招收 |To officially register as a member of a course, school, or group.  |He enrols in a French course. 他报了一个法语课程。|register, enroll, sign up |withdraw, unregister


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enrols in a new university every year. 他每年都注册一所新的大学。
She enrols her children in swimming lessons. 她为孩子们报了游泳课。

一般过去时:
I enrolled in a yoga class last month. 我上个月报了一个瑜伽班。
They enrolled their daughter at a private school. 他们把女儿送进了一所私立学校。

现在完成时:
He has enrolled in three different courses this semester. 这个学期他已报了三门不同的课程。
She has enrolled her son in a summer camp. 她已经为儿子报了一个夏令营。

过去完成时:
Before the deadline, she had already enrolled in the advanced program. 在截止日期之前，她已经报了高级课程。
They had enrolled their child in music lessons before realizing it was too expensive. 他们在意识到太贵之前已经为孩子报了音乐课。


将来时:
I will enroll in a coding bootcamp next year. 明年我将参加一个编程训练营。
She will enroll her son in kindergarten next fall. 秋天她将把儿子送进幼儿园。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enroll in that painting class. 如果我有更多时间，我会报那个绘画班。
I wish I had enrolled in that course sooner. 我希望我早点报了那门课。


被动语态:
Many students were enrolled in the introductory course. 许多学生都报了入门课程。
He was enrolled in the program without his knowledge. 他在不知情的情况下被注册到该项目中。


疑问句:
Will you enroll in the advanced class? 你会报高级班吗？
Have you enrolled in any summer courses? 你报了暑期课程吗？


否定句:
I did not enroll in the course because it was full. 我没有报那门课，因为它满了。
She did not enroll her child in the dance class. 她没有让她的孩子参加舞蹈课。


PHRASE->
enrol in: 注册…
enrol on:  在...注册
enrol with: 向…注册
enrol as: 以…身份注册
enrol for: 为…报名
