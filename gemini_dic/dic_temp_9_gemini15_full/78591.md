giants:/ˈdʒaɪənts/| n.|巨人；巨物；杰出人物|A giant is a person or thing of immense size, power, or importance.  Giants can refer to mythical creatures, exceptionally tall people, or something exceptionally large or powerful.|例句：The story featured a friendly giant who helped lost children. = 这个故事讲述了一个友好的巨人帮助迷路的孩子。

例句：The company is a giant in the technology industry. = 这家公司是科技行业的巨头。


近义词：goliath, titan, colossus, behemoth|反义词：dwarf, pygmy, midget|词性:名词
