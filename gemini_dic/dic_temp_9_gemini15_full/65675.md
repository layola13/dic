even though:/ˌiːvən ðoʊ/| conj.|即使；尽管；虽然|This conjunction introduces a clause that contrasts with the main clause, but doesn't negate it. It indicates that something is true despite the existence of a contrasting fact.  |例句：Even though it was raining, we went for a walk. = 即使下雨，我们还是去散步了。|例句：Even though she's tired, she still works hard. = 尽管她很累，她仍然努力工作。|近义词：although, though, despite, notwithstanding|反义词:only if, solely if
