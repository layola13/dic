fuckups:/ˈfʌkʌps/| n.| 搞砸的事；错误；失败 | Mistakes, errors, or failures.  The word is a plural noun formed from the verb "fuck up".  It is highly informal and considered vulgar. |例句：His presentation was full of fuckups.  他的演讲充满了错误。

例句：There were several fuckups in the project timeline.  这个项目的进度安排出了好几个岔子。


近义词：blunders, mistakes, errors, screw-ups, bloopers, botches


反义词：successes, triumphs, achievements
