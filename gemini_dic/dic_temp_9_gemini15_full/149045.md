own-up:/oʊn ʌp/| v.|承认，坦白|To confess or admit to something, especially a wrongdoing or mistake.|例句：He finally owned up to his mistake. 他最终承认了他的错误。

例句：She owned up to breaking the vase. 她承认打破了花瓶。


近义词：confess, admit, acknowledge|反义词：deny, conceal, hide|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He owns up to his mistakes regularly. 他定期承认自己的错误。
She owns up to her shortcomings. 她承认自己的缺点。


一般过去时:
He owned up to the crime after days of interrogation. 经过几天的审讯后，他承认了罪行。
She owned up to lying to her parents. 她承认对父母撒了谎。


现在完成时:
He has owned up to his involvement in the scandal. 他已经承认自己参与了丑闻。
She has finally owned up to her part in the accident. 她终于承认了自己在事故中扮演的角色。


过去完成时:
He had owned up to his mistake before anyone else found out. 在别人发现之前，他已经承认了自己的错误。
She had owned up to her feelings before he left.  在他离开之前，她已经承认了自己的感情。


将来时:
He will own up to his actions when he is ready. 他准备好后会承认自己的行为。
She will own up to the truth eventually. 她最终会承认真相。


虚拟语气:
If he owned up to his mistake, it would be easier for everyone. 如果他承认自己的错误，对每个人都会更容易。
I wish she had owned up to her feelings sooner. 我希望她早点承认自己的感情。


被动语态:
His involvement in the crime was owned up to by him eventually.  他的罪行参与最终被他承认了。(此用法较少见，主动语态更常用)
The truth was finally owned up to by the witnesses.  证人们最终承认了真相。(此用法较少见，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Will he own up to what he did? 他会承认他做了什么吗？
Did she own up to her deception? 她承认了自己的欺骗行为吗？


否定句:
He didn't own up to his mistake. 他没有承认自己的错误。
She wouldn't own up to her involvement. 她不肯承认自己的参与。

own up to responsibility 承担责任
own up to the fault 承认错误
