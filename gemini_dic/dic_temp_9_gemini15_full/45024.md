ctenoidean:/ˌtiːnɔɪˈdiːən/| adj. |栉鳞的；有栉鳞的 |Having ctenoid scales. Ctenoid scales are characterized by a comb-like edge.  They are common in many species of fish, particularly in higher teleosts. |Example: Many species of perch have ctenoid scales.|近义词：scaly (as a general term)|反义词：cycloid (referring to cycloid scales)
