foibles:/ˈfɔɪblz/ | n. | 缺点；癖好；弱点 |  A foible is a minor weakness or eccentricity in someone's character. It's a small flaw, often endearing or amusing rather than serious.  |  Her only foible is her tendency to talk too much.  她的唯一缺点就是太爱说话了。  |  weakness, quirk, peculiarity, eccentricity | strength, virtue
