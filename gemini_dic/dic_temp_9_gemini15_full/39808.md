consortiums:/kənˈsɔːrʃiəmz/| n.|财团；联盟；协会|A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments  that cooperate for a common purpose.  Consortiums are often formed to undertake large-scale projects that require significant resources and expertise, such as infrastructure projects, research initiatives, or joint ventures.|Example: Several banks formed a consortium to finance the construction of the new bridge.  几个银行组成了一个财团来资助新桥的建设。|近义词：alliance, coalition, partnership, syndicate|反义词：individual effort, independent action|名词
