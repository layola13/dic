nonpantheistic:/ˌnɒnˌpænθiˈɪstɪk/| adj. |非泛神论的|Not pantheistic; not identifying God with the universe.  This term describes beliefs or systems that do not equate God with the universe or all of existence.  It emphasizes a distinction between the divine and the created world.|The philosopher presented a nonpantheistic view of God, arguing for a personal deity separate from creation.|近义词：theistic, transcendental|反义词：pantheistic
