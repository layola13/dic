pyritical:/paɪˈrɪtɪkəl/| adj.| 黄铁矿的；含黄铁矿的 | Relating to or containing pyrite (iron disulfide, also known as iron pyrites or fool's gold).  Pyritical rocks often contain other minerals. |例句：The geologist identified the pyritical deposits in the rock sample. 地质学家鉴定了岩石样本中的黄铁矿矿床。
The pyritical smell was strong in the mine.  矿井里弥漫着浓烈的黄铁矿气味。


近义词：sulfurous (if referring to the smell), containing pyrite.
反义词：None directly opposite, but one could say "non-pyritical" or "free of pyrite."
词性：形容词
