referential:/ˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj. | 参照的；指称的；涉及参照的 | Relating to or denoting the relationship between a word or phrase and the object or concept it refers to.  It emphasizes the act of referring or pointing to something else. |The referential meaning of "the dog" in the sentence is the specific canine being discussed.  这个句子中“狗”的指称意义是指正在讨论的那只具体的犬类。

The essay lacks referential integrity; many claims are unsupported. 这篇文章缺乏参照完整性；许多说法都没有证据支持。


近义词：referring, allusive, indicative


反义词：non-referential, abstract (in the sense of lacking concrete reference)


词性：形容词


词源：源自 refer (v.) + -ential (adj. suffix)  “refer”  最终来自拉丁语 *referre “to carry back, bring back”, from re- “back” + ferre “to carry”. "-ential" 是形容词后缀，表示“与…相关的”。
