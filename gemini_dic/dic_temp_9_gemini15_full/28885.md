carpoolings:/ˈkɑːrpuːlɪŋz/| n. |拼车；合伙用车 |The act of sharing a car for transportation, typically to reduce costs or environmental impact.  Carpoolings are becoming increasingly popular as a way to commute to work.|例句：Many employees participate in carpoolings to reduce traffic congestion and save money on gas. 许多员工参与拼车以减少交通拥堵并节省汽油费用。|例句：The company encourages carpoolings among its employees as part of its sustainability initiative.  该公司鼓励员工之间拼车，作为其可持续发展倡议的一部分。|近义词：ride-sharing, car-sharing|反义词：driving alone, solo driving|名词
