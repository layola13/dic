immediacies:/ɪˈmiːdiəsiːz/| n. |直接性；迫切性；即时性|The quality or state of being immediate; directness or urgency.  It often refers to the direct and unmediated experience of something, or the pressing need for immediate action.|例句：The immediacies of the crisis demanded immediate action.  这场危机的紧迫性要求立即采取行动。

例句：He was overwhelmed by the immediacies of his new job. 他被新工作的紧迫性压垮了。


近义词：directness, urgency, immediateness, urgency, promptness

反义词：remoteness, distance, delay, procrastination


词性：名词
