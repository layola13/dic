obturator:/ˈɒb tjʊreɪtə(r)/| n. |闭孔器；闭孔肌；封闭物|A structure that closes an opening; specifically, in anatomy, a muscle or membrane that closes an opening.  It can also refer to a device used to close an opening, such as in photography or surgery.|例句：The obturator foramen is a large opening in the hip bone. 闭孔是髋骨上的一个大开口。

The surgeon used an obturator to close the wound. 外科医生用闭孔器来缝合伤口。|近义词：closure, shutter, plug|反义词：opener
