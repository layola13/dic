The loathly villain was despised by all.  人人憎恨那个令人厌恶的恶棍。|近义词：abhorrent, repugnant, repulsive, disgusting, hateful|反义词：pleasant, delightful, appealing, attractive

词源:
The word "loathly" comes from the Old English word "lāþlic," which means "hateful," "odious," or "despicable."  The suffix "-ly" is a common English adverbial suffix, but in this case, it's functioning as an adjective suffix, modifying the noun.
