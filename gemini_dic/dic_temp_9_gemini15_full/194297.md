self-oscillate:/ˌsɛlfˈɑːsɪleɪt/| v.|自激振荡|To oscillate without the application of an external periodic force; to generate its own oscillations.|例句：The circuit self-oscillates at a frequency of 1 kHz.  该电路以1kHz的频率自激振荡。

例句：The pendulum self-oscillates due to the conversion of potential energy to kinetic energy and back again.  摆锤由于势能和动能的相互转换而自激振荡。


近义词：vibrate, fluctuate, swing


反义词：remain static, stay constant


词性:动词
