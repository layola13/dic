nonimputableness: /ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtəbəlnəs/ | n. |不可归咎性，非归咎性 | The state or quality of not being attributable or accountable.  It refers to something that cannot be blamed or assigned responsibility to a particular person or thing.  | Example: The court ruled that the damage was due to nonimputableness, as no one could be shown to be at fault. 法院裁定损害是由于不可归咎性，因为没有人能被证明有过错。|近义词:unaccountability, irresponsibility, inexcusability |反义词:accountability, responsibility, culpability |名词
