fractionalism:/ˈfrækʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |派系主义；分裂主义；分割主义|The state or practice of being divided into factions or groups.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting conflict and instability. |例句：The rise of fractionalism within the party led to its downfall.  派系主义的兴起导致了该党的垮台。|近义词：factionalism, sectarianism, divisionism|反义词：unity, cohesion, solidarity
