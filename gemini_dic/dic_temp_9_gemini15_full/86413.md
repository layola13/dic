heeded:/ˈhiːdəd/| v. |注意；理会；听从|To pay attention to and follow; to take notice of.|He heeded the warnings and stayed home during the storm. 他注意到了警告，在暴风雨期间待在家里。|obeyed, attended to, regarded|ignored, disregarded, neglected|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heeds my advice. = 他听从我的建议。
She heeds the warnings of the doctors. = 她听从医生的警告。

一般过去时:
They heeded the call for help. = 他们响应了求救呼声。
He didn't heed my warnings. = 他没有理会我的警告。

现在完成时:
Have you heeded the instructions? = 你注意到了说明了吗？
She has always heeded her mother's advice. = 她一直都听从她母亲的建议。

过去完成时:
I wished I had heeded his advice earlier. = 我真希望我早点听从他的建议。
They had heeded the signs of danger, so they escaped unharmed. = 他们注意到了危险的迹象，所以毫发无损地逃脱了。

将来时:
He will heed the advice of his mentor. = 他将听从他导师的建议。
She will heed the warnings and take precautions. = 她会注意警告并采取预防措施。

虚拟语气:
If only he had heeded the warnings! = 但愿他当时听了警告！
I wish she had heeded my advice. = 我真希望她听了我的建议。

被动语态:
The warnings were heeded by the sailors. = 水手们注意到了警告。
His advice was not heeded. = 他的建议没有被采纳。

疑问句:
Did you heed the instructions? = 你注意到了说明吗？
Will they heed the call to action? = 他们会响应行动号召吗？

否定句:
He did not heed the warnings. = 他没有理会警告。
They failed to heed the advice given. = 他们没有听从给出的建议。
