mordente:/mɔːrˈdɛnteɪ/| n.|装饰音，加花音|A mordente is a musical ornament consisting of a grace note, typically a half-step or whole step above or below the main note, played quickly before the main note.|例句：The violinist skillfully executed the mordente in the passage.  小提琴家熟练地演奏了乐段中的装饰音。|近义词：grace note, appoggiatura|反义词:None
