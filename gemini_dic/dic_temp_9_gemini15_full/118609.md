messengers:/ˈmɛsɪndʒərz/| n. |信使；报信者；传递信息者 |People or things that convey messages. Messengers can be individuals, animals, or even inanimate objects used to transmit information.  |The messengers arrived with urgent news.|近义词：bearers, couriers, emissaries |反义词：receiver (in the context of message transmission) |名词
