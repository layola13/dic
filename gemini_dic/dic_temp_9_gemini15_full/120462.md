minority:/maɪˈnɒrɪti/ | /məˈnɔːrəti/ | n. |少数，少数民族，少数派 | A group or number of people considered less powerful than the majority in a particular society.  It can also refer to a group forming less than half of a larger group. | Example:  Ethnic minorities are often subject to discrimination. 少数民族经常受到歧视。

The racial minorities in this city have their own distinct cultures. 这个城市的少数种族拥有自己独特的文化。 | 近义词：few, small number, fraction, underrepresented group | 反义词：majority, preponderance
