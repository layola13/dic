precerebellar: /ˌpriːˌsɛrɪˈbɛləər/ | adj. |小脑前的；小脑之前的 | Relating to the structures located in front of the cerebellum.  This term is primarily used in anatomical contexts to describe the location of structures relative to the cerebellum, a major part of the brain responsible for motor control, coordination, and balance.  | The precerebellar nuclei receive input from various brain regions and relay information to the cerebellum.  小脑前核接收来自大脑不同区域的输入，并将信息传递给小脑。|近义词: antecerebellar (less common) | 反义词: postcerebellar | 词性:形容词
