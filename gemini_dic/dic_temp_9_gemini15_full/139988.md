odourful:/ˈoʊdərfl/| adj.|芬芳的；有气味的；臭的|Having a strong smell, either pleasant or unpleasant.  The word implies a noticeable scent, not necessarily a positive one.|例句：The odourful roses filled the garden with their sweet fragrance.  芬芳的玫瑰花充满了花园的甜蜜芬芳。The odourful swamp was avoided by most travelers.  大多数旅行者都避开了臭气熏天的沼泽。|近义词：fragrant, aromatic, scented, pungent, redolent|反义词：odorless, scentless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roses are odourful. = 玫瑰花很香。
The garbage is odourful. = 垃圾很臭。

一般过去时:
The cheese was odourful. = 奶酪很臭。
The flowers were odourful. = 花很香。

现在完成时:
The room has become odourful. = 房间变得很臭了。
The air has been odourful all day. = 一整天空气都很香。


过去完成时:
The room had become odourful before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，房间就已经很臭了。
The garden had been odourful all morning. = 一上午花园都很香。

将来时:
The flowers will be odourful tomorrow. = 明天花会很香。
The fish will become odourful soon. = 鱼很快就会变臭。

虚拟语气:
If the flowers were odourful, I would pick them. = 如果花很香，我会摘它们。
I wish the garbage wasn't so odourful. = 我真希望垃圾不要这么臭。


被动语态:
The air is often made odourful by blooming jasmine. = 盛开的茉莉花常常使空气变得芬芳。
The room was made odourful by a spilled perfume. = 一瓶洒了的香水使房间变得很香。


疑问句:
Is the food odourful? = 这食物有气味吗？（指好坏皆可）
Is the air odourful? = 空气有味道吗？（指好坏皆可）

否定句:
The air is not odourful. = 空气没有味道。
The perfume is not odourful. = 香水没有香味。
