salud: /saˈluːd/ | n. |健康；祝你健康；问候|

英文解析：Salud is a Spanish word that primarily means "health." It's commonly used as a toast, similar to "cheers" or "to your health" in English.  It can also be used as a general greeting, though less frequently than its use as a toast.  The word's meaning extends beyond simple physical well-being; it can encompass overall well-being and prosperity.

例句：

¡Salud! = Cheers! / To your health!

Espero que tengas buena salud. = I hope you are in good health.


近义词：health, well-being, prosperity (in the context of overall well-being) , cheers (in the context of a toast)

反义词：illness, sickness, disease (in the context of health)


词源：The word "salud" comes from the Latin word "salus," meaning "health," "safety," or "welfare."  This Latin root is shared with words in other Romance languages that also mean health.
