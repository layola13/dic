knick-knack:/nɪkˈnæk/| n. |小摆设，小玩意儿 |A small decorative object, especially one of little value.  These are often collected and displayed in homes. |例句：Her shelves were covered in knick-knacks. 她的架子上摆满了小摆设。

例句：The antique shop was full of interesting knick-knacks. 这家古董店里摆满了有趣的小玩意儿。

近义词： trinket, gadget, gewgaw, ornament

反义词：  (无明确的反义词，可考虑与“大型家具”或“实用物品”相对)


USAGE->
(由于knick-knack是名词，所以不适用动词的各种时态变化)


PHRASE->
a collection of knick-knacks = 一堆小摆设
a shelf full of knick-knacks = 一架子小摆设
useless knick-knacks = 无用的摆设
decorative knick-knacks = 装饰性的小摆设
old knick-knacks = 旧的小玩意儿
