incorporates:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪts/ | vt. |包含；吸收；合并；使成为公司 |To include something as part of something else; to combine things into a single entity; to form a corporation. |例句：The new design incorporates many eco-friendly features.  新的设计融合了许多环保功能。

The company incorporates in Delaware. 这家公司在特拉华州注册成立。|近义词：include, integrate, combine, consolidate |反义词：exclude, separate, divide |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plan incorporates several key elements. = 该计划包含几个关键要素。
This software incorporates the latest technology. = 此软件包含最新技术。


一般过去时:
The artist incorporated natural materials into his sculpture. = 艺术家在他的雕塑中融入了天然材料。
They incorporated the feedback into the final product. = 他们将反馈意见融入最终产品中。


现在完成时:

She has incorporated yoga into her daily routine. = 她已将瑜伽融入她的日常生活中。


过去完成时:

They had incorporated the new data into their analysis. = 他们已将新数据纳入他们的分析中。


将来时:

We will incorporate your suggestions into the next version. = 我们将在下一个版本中加入您的建议。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
