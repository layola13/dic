drive-at:/draɪv æt/| v. |试图解释；意在；暗指；攻击；猛击| to try to explain or understand something that is not clearly stated; to aim or direct something at a particular target; to criticize or attack someone repeatedly; to hit or strike something forcefully|例句：I think he's driving at something more important than just the numbers. 我认为他意图表达的比仅仅是数字更重要。 He drove at the ball with all his might. 他使出全力猛击球。 |近义词：imply, suggest, hint at, aim at, attack |反义词：clarify, explain directly
