gourmands:/ˈɡʊrmɑːnz/| n. |美食家，贪吃者 |A gourmand is a person who enjoys eating and drinking good food and wine.  They are often knowledgeable about food and appreciate fine dining.  |例句：He's a gourmand who travels the world in search of culinary delights. 他是一位美食家，周游世界寻找美食佳肴。|例句：The gourmand devoured the entire plate of pasta in minutes.  这位美食家几分钟内就吃光了一整盘意大利面。|近义词：epicure, gourmet, bon vivant |反义词：ascetic, abstemious
