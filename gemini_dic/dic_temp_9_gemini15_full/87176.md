herder:/ˈhɜːrdər/| n. |牧羊人，牧人，看护人 | A person who tends or herds livestock, especially sheep or cattle.  |Example: The shepherd, a weathered herder, guided his flock across the mountain pass.  这位牧羊人，一位饱经风霜的牧人，带领他的羊群越过山口。|近义词：shepherd, herdsman, drover |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who doesn't work with livestock.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The herder guides the sheep to pasture. = 牧羊人引导羊群去牧场。
He herds cattle on the vast plains. = 他在广阔的平原上放牧牛群。


一般过去时:
The herder guided the sheep back to the fold. = 牧羊人引导羊群回到羊圈。
They herded the cattle into the corral. = 他们把牛赶进畜栏。


现在完成时:
The herder has herded sheep for over twenty years. =这位牧羊人养羊超过二十年了。
She has successfully herded the wild horses. = 她成功地把野马赶到了一起。


过去完成时:
The herder had already herded the sheep before sunrise. = 牧羊人在日出前就已经把羊赶好了。
They had herded the cattle for hours before finding a suitable watering hole. = 他们在找到合适的饮水点之前已经放牧了几个小时。


将来时:
The herder will herd the sheep to a new pasture tomorrow. = 牧羊人明天将把羊群赶到新的牧场。
He will be herding cattle in the mountains next week. = 他下周将在山上放牧牛群。


虚拟语气:
If I were a herder, I would live a simpler life. = 如果我是一个牧羊人，我会过着更简单的生活。
I wish I could be a herder and live close to nature. = 我希望我能成为一名牧羊人，并接近大自然。


被动语态:

疑问句:
