opened:/ˈoʊpənd/ | v. | 打开；开启；开放；开始；展开 | The past tense and past participle of "open".  It describes the action of making something accessible or available, or the beginning of something. |例句：She opened the door. 她打开了门。

例句：The store opened at 9 am. 商店早上9点开门。

近义词：unclosed, started, commenced, launched | 反义词：closed, shut, ended


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He opens the window every morning. 他每天早上都打开窗户。
The shop opens at 8am. 商店早上8点开门。

一般过去时:
She opened the present carefully. 她小心地打开了礼物。
They opened a new restaurant last week. 他们上周开了一家新餐馆。

现在完成时:
I have opened all the letters. 我已经打开了所有的信。
He has opened a bank account. 他已经开了一个银行账户。

过去完成时:
After I had opened the door, I saw him. 我打开门后，看见了他。
She had opened the book before she went to bed. 她睡觉前已经把书打开了。

将来时:
They will open a new branch next year. 他们明年将开设一个新的分公司。
We will open the gifts later. 我们稍后再打开礼物。

虚拟语气:
If I opened that box, I might find something interesting. 如果我打开那个盒子，我可能会发现一些有趣的东西。
I wish I had opened the window earlier. 我真希望我早点把窗户打开。

被动语态:
The door was opened by the wind. 门被风吹开了。
The museum is opened to the public on weekends. 博物馆周末对公众开放。

疑问句:
Did you open the package? 你打开包裹了吗？
Has the store opened yet? 商店开业了吗？

否定句:
He didn't open the letter. 他没有打开信。
The door wouldn't open. 门打不开。
