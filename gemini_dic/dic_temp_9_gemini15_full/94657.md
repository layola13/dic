impressive:/ɪmˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|令人印象深刻的，感人的|causing admiration because of its size, quality, or skill.  It suggests something that makes a strong and positive impression on the observer.|例句：His performance was truly impressive. 他的表演真是令人印象深刻。

例句：The scale of the project is impressive.  这个项目的规模令人印象深刻。


近义词：remarkable, striking, stunning, magnificent, awe-inspiring|反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, ordinary, mediocre


词源:  The word "impressive" comes from the verb "impress," which means to affect deeply or strongly.  "Impress" itself originates from the Old French word "empresser," meaning "to press, urge, or imprint."  The "-ive" suffix is added to create the adjective form.
