goosefish:/ˈɡuːsfɪʃ/ | n. | 琵琶鱼 |  A goosefish is a type of anglerfish characterized by its large, flat head and a lure it uses to attract prey.  They are bottom-dwelling fish found in various oceans. | Example: The fisherman caught a large goosefish on his deep-sea fishing trip.  渔夫在深海捕鱼旅行中钓到了一条大琵琶鱼。  |近义词: anglerfish, monkfish | 反义词:  (无直接反义词)
