nontransportabilities:/ˌnɒnˌtrænspɔːtəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可搬运性；不可转移性 | The inherent difficulties or impossibilities associated with moving or transferring something.  This term often refers to intangible aspects, like data or knowledge, but can also apply to physical items with significant limitations on transportation.  | Example: The nontransportabilities of the massive database presented a significant challenge to the project.  数据库的巨大规模使其难以转移，这对项目提出了重大挑战。| 近义词：immovability, untransferability, inflexibility | 反义词：portability, transferability, mobility |  名词
