preaccounts:/ˌpriːəˈkaʊnts/| n. |预先会计；预估账户|Preliminary accounts; estimates of accounts before finalization.  Preaccounts are used for planning and forecasting purposes.|例句：The preaccounts showed a projected profit of $100,000. 预估账户显示预计利润为10万美元。|例句：The company used preaccounts to prepare its budget for the next fiscal year. 公司利用预估账户编制下一财年的预算。|近义词：pro forma accounts, preliminary statements|反义词：final accounts, audited accounts|名词
