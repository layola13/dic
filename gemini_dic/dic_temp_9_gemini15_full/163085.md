precogitates:/priːˈkɒdʒɪteɪts/|/ˈpriːkɑːdʒɪteɪts/| v.|预想；预谋；预先考虑 | To think or plan beforehand; to premeditate.  |例句：She precogitated her response to the difficult question before the interview.  她面试前预想好了对那个难题的回答。

例句：The general precogitated a strategic maneuver to outflank the enemy. 将军预谋了一个战略性调动以包围敌人。

近义词：consider, contemplate, premeditate, plan, deliberate

反义词：improvise, act impulsively

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He precogitates his every move. = 他预先考虑他的每一个举动。
She precogitates the consequences of her actions. = 她预先考虑她行为的后果。


一般过去时:
They precogitated their escape route. = 他们预先计划了他们的逃生路线。
He precogitated his speech for hours. = 他预先构思了他的演讲好几个小时。


现在完成时:
She has precogitated her entire presentation. = 她已经预先构思好了她的整个演示。
They have precogitated a solution to the problem. = 他们已经预先想出了解决这个问题的方案。


过去完成时:
He had precogitated his response before the journalist asked the question. = 在记者提问之前，他已经预先想好了他的回答。
She had precogitated the potential risks before embarking on the project. = 在开始这个项目之前，她已经预先考虑了潜在的风险。


将来时:
They will precogitate their strategy for the upcoming election. = 他们将预先计划他们即将到来的选举策略。
He will precogitate his arguments before the debate. = 他将在辩论前预先构思他的论点。


虚拟语气:
If I had precogitated the difficulties, I would have been better prepared. = 如果我预先考虑到了困难，我会准备得更好。
I wish I had precogitated the consequences. = 我真希望我预先考虑到了后果。


被动语态:
The plan was precogitated over several months. = 这个计划是经过数月预先计划的。
The escape route had been precogitated in detail. = 逃生路线已被详细预先计划好。


疑问句:

否定句:
He did not precogitate his response. = 他没有预先考虑他的回答。
They did not precogitate the risks involved. = 他们没有预先考虑所涉及的风险。
