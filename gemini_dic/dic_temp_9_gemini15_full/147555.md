overpenalizing:/ˌoʊvərˈpiːnəlˌaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度惩罚；处罚过重 |  The act of punishing someone or something too severely.  This often implies that the punishment is disproportionate to the offense. | Example:  The judge was criticized for overpenalizing the young offender.  法官因对年轻罪犯处罚过重而受到批评。|近义词: excessively punishing, unduly punishing | 反义词: underpunishing, leniently punishing
