serpula:/ˈsɜːrpjʊlə/| n. |管虫属；蠕虫；管状物| A genus of marine polychaete worms that build calcareous tubes.  They are often found attached to rocks, shells, or other substrates in shallow marine environments.  | Example: The divers observed several *serpula* tubes encrusted on the coral reef.  潜水员观察到珊瑚礁上附着着几根管虫的管子。|近义词：tube worm, polychaete|反义词：无
