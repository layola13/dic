checkbook:/ˈtʃɛkˌbʊk/| n.|支票簿|A book containing a series of numbered checks that can be detached and used to make payments from a bank account.  It's a traditional method of writing checks.|I need to balance my checkbook before paying the bills. 我需要在支付账单前核对我的支票簿。|account book, check register|cash, credit card|名词
