coincides:/koʊˈɪnsaɪdz/| v. | 同时发生；一致；符合 | To happen at the same time; to be in agreement or harmony. |例句：His birthday coincides with Christmas. 他的生日恰逢圣诞节。
The two accounts coincide in every detail. 这两个说法在每个细节上都一致。|近义词：correspond, concur, agree |反义词：differ, clash, conflict |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting coincides with the holiday. = 会议与假日同时举行。
Her views coincide with mine. = 她的观点与我的一致。


一般过去时:
The eclipse coincided with the full moon. = 日食与满月同时发生。
Their stories coincided on several key points. = 他们的故事在几个关键点上吻合。


现在完成时:
Several events have coincided to create this crisis. = 几个事件同时发生导致了这场危机。
My interests have coincided with the company's goals. = 我的兴趣与公司的目标一致。


过去完成时:
The arrival of the guests coincided with the completion of the preparations. = 客人的到来与准备工作的完成同时进行。
It turned out that their schedules had coincided perfectly. = 原来他们的时间安排完美地吻合了。


将来时:
The festival will coincide with the national holiday. = 节日将与国庆节同时举行。
Our vacations will coincide this year. = 我们今年的假期会同时进行。


虚拟语气:
If the dates coincided, we could attend both events. = 如果日期吻合，我们就能参加两个活动。
I wish my holiday coincided with yours. = 我希望我的假期能和你的一致。


被动语态:
The timing of the announcement was carefully chosen to coincide with the election. = 公告的时间被仔细选择以与选举同时进行。
The results were found to coincide with the predictions. = 结果被发现与预测一致。


疑问句:
Does your appointment coincide with the conference? = 你的预约和会议时间冲突吗？
Does the data coincide with the theory? = 数据与理论相符吗？


否定句:
His statement didn't coincide with the evidence. = 他的陈述与证据不符。
The two versions of the story did not coincide. = 这两个版本的故事并不一致。


PHRASE->
coincide with = 与...同时发生
coincide in = 在...方面一致
roughly coincide = 大致相同
perfectly coincide = 完全一致
generally coincide = 一般来说一致
