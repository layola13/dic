recension:/rɪˈsɛnʃən/| n.|评论，勘误，修订|A critical review or essay, especially of a book or other literary work; a revised version of a text.|例句：The book received a scathing recension in the New York Times. 这本书在《纽约时报》上受到了严厉的批评。|例句：The second edition included several important recensions to the original text. 第二版对原文进行了若干重要的修订。|近义词：review, critique, revision|反义词：original (in the sense of an uncorrected or unrevised version)
