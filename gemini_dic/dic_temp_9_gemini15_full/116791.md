maw:/mɔː/ | n. | 胃；大嘴；深渊 | The word "maw" refers to the mouth or throat, especially of a voracious animal.  It can also refer to a large opening or chasm. |例句：The bird opened its maw to swallow the fish.  这只鸟张开大嘴吞下鱼。|例句：The maw of the volcano spewed forth lava. 火山的深渊喷出熔岩。|近义词：mouth, gullet, chasm, abyss |反义词：tiny opening, small mouth


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  The usage examples below showcase the noun's use in different sentence structures.

Simple Sentence:
The shark's maw was filled with sharp teeth. = 鲨鱼的嘴里充满了锋利的牙齿。

Compound Sentence:
The eagle descended, and its powerful maw clamped down on its prey. = 老鹰俯冲下来，它强壮的大嘴钳住了猎物。


Complex Sentence:
Because the crocodile's maw was so wide, it could easily swallow a whole animal. = 因为鳄鱼的嘴很大，所以它很容易吞下一整个动物。


Passive Voice:
The fish was swallowed whole by the whale's enormous maw. = 这条鱼被鲸鱼巨大的嘴巴整个吞了下去。
