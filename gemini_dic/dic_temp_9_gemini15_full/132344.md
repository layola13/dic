nondegenerative: /ˌnɒnˌdɪdʒɛnərətɪv/ | adj. | 非退化的；不退化的 | Not degenerating; not deteriorating.  This adjective describes something that does not worsen or decline over time, particularly in the context of diseases or conditions. |例句：The researchers found that the mice experienced nondegenerative cognitive function throughout the study. 研究人员发现，在整个研究过程中，老鼠的认知功能没有退化。

例句：This type of arthritis is nondegenerative, meaning it doesn't involve the breakdown of cartilage. 这种类型的关节炎是非退行性的，这意味着它不涉及软骨的分解。


近义词：stable, unchanging, static, persistent | 反义词：degenerative, deteriorating, declining
