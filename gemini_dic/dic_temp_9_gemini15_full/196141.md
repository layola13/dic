septic:/ˈsɛptɪk/| adj.|败血症的；感染的；腐败的| Relating to or caused by sepsis; characterized by the presence of pathogenic microorganisms or their toxins in the blood or tissues.  Septic conditions are often characterized by inflammation, fever, and potentially life-threatening complications. |例句：The wound became septic and required immediate medical attention.  伤口感染化脓，需要立即就医。


The patient developed a septic shock after surgery.  病人术后出现了败血性休克。

近义词：infected, contaminated, putrid, purulent|反义词：sterile, clean, healthy|词性：形容词
