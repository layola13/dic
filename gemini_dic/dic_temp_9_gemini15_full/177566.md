rebreaking: /ˌriːˈbreɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 再破坏，再次破坏，重新破坏 | The act of breaking something again, or breaking it in a different way.  It implies a previous instance of breaking. | 例句：The vase, already cracked, suffered rebreaking during the move.  花瓶已经裂了，搬家时再次被弄坏。


The continuous rain caused rebreaking of the already weakened dam.  持续的降雨导致已经脆弱的坝体再次决堤。


近义词：refracture, breaking again, further breaking | 反义词：repairing, mending, fixing | 词性：动词
