eyereach:/ˈaɪriːtʃ/| n.|视线可及之处，伸手可及之处|The area that one can see or reach.  It often implies a limited distance.|例句：The book was within easy eyereach.  那本书很容易就能看到。|例句：The remote control is within eyereach on the coffee table. 遥控器就在咖啡桌上，伸手就能拿到。|近义词：reach, range, grasp, view|反义词:out of reach, beyond sight.
