paranotum: /ˌpærəˈnoʊtəm/ | n. |侧背板|The hardened, lateral portion of the dorsal surface of certain insects, especially those in the order Hemiptera.  It is part of the insect's exoskeleton and often contributes to its protective covering.  The paranotum is typically located behind the pronotum (the dorsal surface of the thorax's first segment).|The brightly colored paranotum of the assassin bug helps to deter predators.|近义词:tegmen (in some contexts)|反义词:None specifically, but terms describing the ventral surface (e.g., sternum) could be considered contrasting.|名词
