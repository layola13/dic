misframes:/ˈmɪsfreɪmz/| vt.|曲解，误解；歪曲；错误地构建|To represent or interpret (something) inaccurately or unfairly.  To frame (something) incorrectly or misleadingly.|例句：The media misframed the politician's comments, making them seem more extreme than they were. 媒体曲解了这位政治家的言论，使其看起来比实际情况更极端。

The report misframes the issue by focusing solely on negative aspects. 这份报告通过仅仅关注消极方面来曲解了这个问题。|近义词：misrepresent, distort, misinterpret|反义词：accurately represent, clarify, explain correctly|动词
