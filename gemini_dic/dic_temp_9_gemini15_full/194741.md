selfness:/ˈsɛlfnəs/| n. |自我, 自我意识, 自我中心 |The state or quality of being self; self-awareness or self-consciousness; sometimes, selfishness.  Selfness is a complex concept that encompasses one's sense of self, identity, and individuality. It can refer to a deep understanding and acceptance of oneself, including one's strengths and weaknesses.  However, it can also be used to describe excessive self-preoccupation or selfishness. |例句：He exhibited a remarkable selfness in his ability to empathize with others, despite his own struggles. 他在能够设身处地为他人着想方面展现了非凡的自我意识，尽管他自己也面临着困境。

例句：Her overwhelming selfness made it difficult for her to form lasting relationships.  她极度的自我中心使她难以建立持久的关系。


近义词：self-awareness, self-consciousness, individuality, identity, ego, selfishness (in a negative context) |反义词：selflessness, altruism, other-centeredness |词性:名词
