oceans:/ˈoʊʃənz/| n.|海洋；大海|The vast expanse of salt water that covers most of the earth's surface.  Oceans are interconnected and form a single global ocean.|例句：The Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean in the world. = 太平洋是世界上最大的海洋。

例句：We sailed across the Atlantic Ocean. = 我们横渡了大西洋。


近义词：seas, waters


反义词：land, continents


词性:名词
