paginates:/ˈpædʒɪneɪts/| vt.|分页；编页|To divide (text) into pages; to number the pages of (a book, document, etc.).|例句：The software automatically paginates the document.  软件自动对文档分页。
The book was poorly paginated; the page numbers were all out of order. 这本书分页很糟糕；页码都乱了。|近义词：number, index|反义词：None
