chain-store:/ˈtʃeɪn stɔːr/| n. |连锁店|A chain store is a retail store that is part of a larger group of stores, all owned and operated by the same company.  These stores typically have a standardized format, branding, and merchandise. |Example: Starbucks is a successful chain store found worldwide. 星巴克是一个在世界各地都能找到的成功的连锁店。  Many clothing retailers operate as chain stores. 许多服装零售商都以连锁店的形式经营。|近义词：franchise, retail chain|反义词：independent store
