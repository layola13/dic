pull-up:/ˈpʊlʌp/| n. v.|引体向上；拉起；提升；改进|

英文解析:As a noun, a pull-up refers to an exercise where you raise your body by pulling yourself up on a bar. As a verb, it means to raise or lift something, to improve something, or to stop something from falling.

例句:
He did ten pull-ups. 他做了十个引体向上。

The crane pulled up the heavy container. 起重机吊起了沉重的集装箱。


近义词:chin-up, lift, hoist, raise, improve, enhance

反义词:lower, drop, decrease, worsen


词性:noun, verb
