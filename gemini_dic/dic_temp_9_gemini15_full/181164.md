reingested:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɛstɪd/| v. |重新摄入，再次摄取 | To ingest (food, etc.) again.  This usually implies a previous ingestion, often followed by expulsion or regurgitation.  |Example: The bird reingested the worm after it had dropped it.  这只鸟在蠕虫掉落后又重新吞了下去。

The owl reingested the pellet after it had been examined.  这只猫头鹰在检查完吐出的食团后又重新吞了下去。|近义词：swallow again, take in again |反义词：expel, eject
