simmer:/ˈsɪmər/ | vt. vi. n. | 炖，慢煮；（液体）冒泡；（情绪）酝酿 | To cook (a liquid) gently just below the boiling point.  To be in a state of suppressed excitement or anger.  |例句：The sauce simmered gently on the stove.  酱汁在炉子上轻轻地炖着。The tension simmered beneath the surface. 紧张气氛潜伏在表面之下。|近义词：boil, stew, brew,  |反义词：boil furiously, rage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soup simmers gently. = 汤在轻轻地炖着。
He simmers with resentment. = 他心中充满了怨恨。


一般过去时:
She simmered the vegetables until tender. = 她把蔬菜炖到软烂。
The anger simmered down after a while. = 过了一会儿，怒气消退了。


现在完成时:
The sauce has simmered for an hour. = 酱汁已经炖了一个小时了。
He has simmered in his anger for days. = 他已经生气好几天了。


过去完成时:
The stew had simmered for hours before it was served. = 炖菜在端上桌之前已经炖了几个小时。
The conflict had simmered for years before it exploded. = 冲突酝酿多年后才爆发。


将来时:
I will simmer the tomatoes until they soften. = 我会把西红柿炖到软为止。
The situation will simmer for a while before anything changes. = 局势会在有所改变之前持续一段时间。


虚拟语气:
If I simmered it longer, it would be more flavorful. = 如果我炖的时间更长，味道会更好。
I wish I hadn't simmered in my resentment for so long. = 我真希望我没有怨恨这么久。


被动语态:
The vegetables were simmered until they were soft. = 蔬菜被炖到软烂。
The issue was allowed to simmer for several weeks. = 这个问题被搁置了几周。


疑问句:
Is the sauce simmering properly? = 酱汁炖得合适吗？
Are you simmering with anger? = 你在生闷气吗？


否定句:
Don't simmer the soup too long, or it will be too thick. = 不要把汤炖太久，否则会太浓。
He didn't simmer down easily. = 他不容易平静下来。


PHRASE->
