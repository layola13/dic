popularly:/ˈpɑːpjələrli/| adv. |普遍地，流行地，大众地 |In a popular or widespread manner; commonly.  It describes something that is widely accepted or used by many people. |例句：The theory is popularly believed to be true.  这个理论普遍被认为是正确的。


例句：This restaurant is popularly known for its delicious seafood. 这家餐馆以其美味的海鲜而闻名。


近义词：commonly, widely, generally, usually


反义词：rarely, uncommonly, exceptionally


词性：副词
