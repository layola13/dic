exped:/ɪkˈsped/| vt.|加快；促进；派遣；迅速处理 |to speed up the progress of; to send on a mission; to deal with something quickly and efficiently. |例句：The government expedited the visa application process. 政府加快了签证申请流程。

例句：They expedited the delivery of the emergency supplies. 他们加快了紧急物资的运送。


近义词：hasten, accelerate, dispatch, facilitate |反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct, impede

词源:  From Middle English *expeden, from Old French espeder "to dispatch," from Vulgar Latin *expedire, from Latin expedire "to disentangle, extricate," from ex- "out" + ped-, stem of pes "foot."  The sense evolution is from "to free from entanglements" to "to make progress easier" to "to hasten."
