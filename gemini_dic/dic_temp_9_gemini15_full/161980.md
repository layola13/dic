prater:/ˈpreɪtər/| n. |喋喋不休的人；饶舌者；空谈家|A prater is someone who talks excessively and often about unimportant things. They may be considered verbose, garrulous, or loquacious.  |例句：He was a prater, always filling the room with his endless chatter. 他是个喋喋不休的人，总是用他没完没了的闲聊填满房间。|近义词：babbler, chatterbox, windbag, blabbermouth |反义词：silent, quiet, reserved
