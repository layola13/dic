monatomic:/ˌmɒnəˈætɒmɪk/| adj. |单原子的；单原子的|Consisting of one atom.  A monatomic molecule is a molecule consisting of only one atom.  For example, the noble gases (helium, neon, argon, krypton, xenon, and radon) exist as monatomic gases under standard conditions.| Example: Helium is a monatomic gas. 氦气是一种单原子气体。|近义词:uniatomic|反义词:polyatomic
