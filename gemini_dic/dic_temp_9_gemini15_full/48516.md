deerstalking:/ˈdɪəˌstɔːkɪŋ/| n. |偷猎鹿；猎鹿|The act of hunting deer, typically in a stealthy and skillful manner.  It often involves stalking the deer over a period of time to get within shooting range.|例句：Deerstalking is a popular sport in Scotland.  猎鹿在苏格兰是一项流行的运动。

例句：He spent hours deerstalking before finally getting a shot. 他花了数小时潜伏猎鹿才最终射中。


近义词：deer hunting, stalking game


反义词：deer conservation, wildlife protection


词性：名词
