raisings:/ˈreɪzɪŋz/| n.|筹款；抚养；上升；饲养|The act of raising something, such as money or animals, or the process of something rising.|例句：The charity event had successful raisings. 此慈善活动筹款成功。|近义词：fundraising, cultivation, elevation, breeding|反义词：lowering, decrease, decline


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is raising funds for a new project. = 公司正在为一个新项目筹集资金。
The farmer is raising cattle on his land. = 农民在他的土地上饲养牛。


一般过去时:
They raised a lot of money for the charity. = 他们为慈善机构筹集了很多钱。
She raised her hand to ask a question. = 她举手提问。


现在完成时:
They have raised over $10,000 for the cause. = 他们已经为这项事业筹集了超过10,000美元。
The temperature has been raising steadily throughout the day. = 温度全天都在稳步上升。


过去完成时:
They had raised enough money before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前筹集到了足够的资金。
The water level had raised significantly after the storm. = 暴雨过后水位显著上升。


将来时:
They will raise awareness about the issue. = 他们将提高人们对这个问题的认识。
The company will be raising prices next month. = 公司下个月将提高价格。


虚拟语气:
If they raised more money, they could expand their business. = 如果他们筹集到更多资金，他们就可以扩大业务。
If the temperature had raised higher, the ice would have melted. = 如果温度升得更高，冰就会融化。


被动语态:
Funds are being raised for the victims of the disaster. = 正在为灾难受害者筹集资金。
The children are being raised by their grandparents. = 孩子们由他们的祖父母抚养长大。


疑问句:
Are they raising enough money? = 他们筹集的钱够吗？
Is the company raising salaries this year? = 公司今年会加薪吗？


否定句:
They are not raising enough awareness. = 他们没有提高足够的认识。
The company is not raising prices this year. = 公司今年没有涨价。
