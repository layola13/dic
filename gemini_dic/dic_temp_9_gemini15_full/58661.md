dumfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/| adj.| 惊呆的；目瞪口呆的；困惑的 | extremely surprised and confused; unable to speak |  The unexpected news left him dumbfounded.  意想不到的消息让他惊呆了。| astonished, amazed, bewildered, flabbergasted | calm, composed, collected | 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dumbfounded by the magician's trick. = 他被魔术师的戏法弄得目瞪口呆。
The complex problem leaves her dumbfounded. = 复杂的问题让她困惑不解。


一般过去时:
I was dumbfounded when I saw the accident. = 当我看到事故时，我惊呆了。
They were dumbfounded by the sudden announcement. = 突如其来的宣布让他们大吃一惊。


现在完成时:
She has been dumbfounded by the sheer scale of the project. = 她一直被这个项目的巨大规模搞得不知所措。
We have been dumbfounded by his unexpected success. =  我们被他意外的成功惊呆了。


过去完成时:
He had been dumbfounded by the news before he could react.  = 他在能做出反应之前就被这个消息惊呆了。
I had been dumbfounded by her sudden departure. = 我对她突然离开感到非常震惊。


将来时:
The audience will be dumbfounded by the singer's performance. = 观众将会被歌手的表演惊呆。
You will be dumbfounded by the beauty of the scenery. = 你将会被风景的美景所震撼。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen that, I would have been dumbfounded. = 如果我看到那样的事情，我会惊呆的。
I wish I hadn't been dumbfounded by his anger. = 我真希望我没有被他愤怒的样子吓到。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
