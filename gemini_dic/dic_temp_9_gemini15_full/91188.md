hydrations:/haɪˈdreɪʃənz/ | n. | 水合作用；补水 | The process of combining a substance with water.  It can refer to the absorption of water by a living organism or the incorporation of water into a chemical compound.  | Example:  Proper hydration is crucial for athletic performance.  适当的水合作用对于运动表现至关重要。
The dehydration of the soil impacted plant growth. 土壤脱水影响了植物生长。(This example uses the opposite, showing the understanding of hydration) |近义词：rehydration, watering |反义词：dehydration | 名词
