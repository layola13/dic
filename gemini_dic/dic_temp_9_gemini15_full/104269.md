juster:/ˈdʒʌstər/| adj.|更公正的；更正直的|More just; more equitable.  It implies a greater degree of fairness or righteousness than "just."|The judge's decision was juster than the initial ruling.  法官的裁决比初步裁决更公正。|  The new policy is much juster than the old one. 新政策比旧政策公正得多。|近义词：fairer, more equitable, more righteous|反义词：unjuster, more unfair, more inequitable
