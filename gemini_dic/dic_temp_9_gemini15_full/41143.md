copublishers:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃərz/| n. |合出版商|Publishers who jointly publish a book or other work.  They share the costs and responsibilities of production and distribution. |Example: Several universities acted as copublishers for the extensive research project.|近义词: joint publishers|反义词: sole publisher
