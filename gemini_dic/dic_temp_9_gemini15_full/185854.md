revivifier:/rɪˈvaɪvɪfaɪər/| n. |复兴者；使复苏的人或物 |A revivifier is a person or thing that revives or restores something to life, activity, or vigor.  It can refer to a person who inspires renewed energy or a substance or process that restores something. |Example: The new CEO acted as a revivifier for the struggling company.  新的首席执行官成为这家苦苦挣扎的公司复兴的推动者。|近义词：restorer, revitalizer, rejuvenator |反义词：destroyer, annihilator |名词
