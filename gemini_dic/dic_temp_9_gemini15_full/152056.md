patented:/ˈpætəntɪd/| adj.|已获得专利的；受专利保护的|Protected by a patent; having been granted a patent.|The company uses a patented process to manufacture its products. 这家公司使用一项专利工艺来生产其产品。|proprietary, protected|unpatented, public domain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This technology is patented. = 这项技术已获得专利。
The design is patented in several countries. = 该设计在多个国家获得专利。

一般过去时:
The invention was patented last year. = 该发明于去年获得专利。
His new method was patented in 1990. = 他的新方法于1990年获得专利。

现在完成时:
The company has patented several innovative designs. = 该公司已获得几项创新设计的专利。
Many of their products have been patented worldwide. = 他们的许多产品已在全球获得专利。

过去完成时:
They had patented the technology before their competitors did. = 他们在竞争对手之前就获得了这项技术的专利。
The company had patented several key components before launching the product. = 公司在推出产品之前已经获得了几项关键部件的专利。

将来时:
We will patent the new discovery soon. = 我们很快就会为这项新发现申请专利。
They expect to patent the invention within the next year. = 他们预计在明年内获得该发明的专利。

虚拟语气:
If the invention were patented, it would be protected from infringement. = 如果这项发明获得了专利，它将受到保护，免受侵权。
I wish I had patented my idea earlier. = 我真希望我早点为我的想法申请专利。

被动语态:
The process is patented by a large pharmaceutical company. = 该工艺已获得一家大型制药公司的专利。
The design has been patented and is now protected. = 该设计已获得专利，现在受到保护。

疑问句:
Is this product patented? = 这款产品有专利吗？
Has the process been patented? = 该工艺已获得专利吗？

否定句:
The design is not patented yet. = 该设计尚未获得专利。
The software is not patented in this country. = 该软件在此国未获得专利。
