progressivist:/prəˈɡrɛsɪvɪst/| n. |进步主义者；革新主义者 |A progressivist is an advocate or supporter of progressivism, a broad political philosophy that emphasizes social reform and progress.  Progressivists generally believe in using government to address social and economic problems, often advocating for policies such as social justice, environmental protection, and economic equality. |例句：Many progressivist politicians championed women's suffrage in the early 20th century. 许多进步主义政治家在20世纪初大力倡导妇女参政权。

Many progressivist thinkers advocate for policies that aim to reduce income inequality. 许多进步主义思想家倡导旨在减少收入不平等的政策。|近义词：reformist, liberal, leftist |反义词：conservative, reactionary, traditionalist
