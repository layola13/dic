caoutchouc:/ˈkuːtʃuk/| n. |橡胶，生橡胶|A natural rubber obtained as a milky exudate from various plants, especially Hevea brasiliensis (the Pará rubber tree).  It is a polymer of isoprene and is the basis of many rubber products.| Example:  The early tires were made from natural caoutchouc. 早期的轮胎是用天然橡胶制成的。|近义词：rubber, latex|反义词：无
