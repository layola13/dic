reindicted:/ˌriːɪnˈdaɪtɪd/| v.|再次起诉|To indict someone again, usually after a previous indictment has been dismissed or a trial has resulted in a mistrial.|例句：The defendant was reindicted on more serious charges after the first trial ended in a hung jury.  被告在第一次审判陪审团无法达成一致后，被以更严重的罪名再次起诉。

例句：After the initial charges were dropped, the suspect was reindicted on similar allegations. 在最初的指控被撤销后，嫌疑人被以类似的指控再次起诉。


近义词：re-arraigned, reprosecuted


反义词：acquitted, discharged


词性：动词
