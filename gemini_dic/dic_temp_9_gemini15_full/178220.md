recommit:/ˌriːkəˈmɪt/| vt.|重新承诺，再次奉献|To commit again; to pledge or dedicate oneself anew.  It implies a renewed commitment after a lapse or a stronger commitment than before. |例句：He recommitted himself to his fitness goals after a period of inactivity. 他在一段时间的懒散后重新致力于他的健身目标。

例句：The company recommitted to its environmental sustainability initiatives. 公司重新致力于其环境可持续发展倡议。


近义词：rededicate, reaffirm, recommence, renew


反义词：abandon, forsake, renege


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recommits to his studies every semester. = 他每个学期都重新投入到学习中。
She recommits to her diet plan every Monday. = 她每个星期一都重新开始她的节食计划。


一般过去时:
He recommitted to his fitness regime after his injury. = 他在受伤后重新投入到他的健身计划中。
She recommitted to the project after a brief hiatus. = 在短暂中断后，她重新致力于该项目。


现在完成时:
He has recommitted himself to volunteering after a long break. = 在长时间休息后，他再次投入志愿工作。
She has recommitted to her artistic pursuits after a period of self-doubt. = 在一段时间的自我怀疑之后，她重新投入到她的艺术追求中。


过去完成时:
He had recommitted himself to his faith before his passing. = 他在去世前重新致力于他的信仰。
She had recommitted to the relationship after a period of conflict. = 在一段时间的冲突之后，她重新致力于这段关系。


将来时:
He will recommit to his career after his sabbatical. = 休假后，他将重新致力于他的事业。
She will recommit to her studies once she returns from her travels. = 等她从旅行回来后，她将重新投入学习。


虚拟语气:
If he recommitted to his training, he would win the competition. = 如果他重新投入训练，他就会赢得比赛。
If she recommitted to her artistic vision, she would create a masterpiece. = 如果她重新致力于她的艺术视野，她就能创作出杰作。


被动语态:
The plan was recommitted to the board for review. = 该计划被重新提交给董事会审查。
His loyalty was recommitted to the cause after a period of uncertainty. = 在一段时间的犹豫不决之后，他对事业的忠诚再次得到肯定。


疑问句:
Will he recommit to the project after this setback? = 这次挫折后，他会重新致力于这个项目吗？
Has she recommitted to her goals after facing adversity? = 在面对逆境之后，她是否重新致力于她的目标？


否定句:
He did not recommit to his promise. = 他没有履行他的承诺。
She did not recommit to the company after the merger. = 合并后，她没有重新致力于这家公司。


PHRASE->
recommit oneself to = 重新致力于…
recommit to a cause = 重新致力于一项事业
recommit to a relationship = 重新致力于一段关系
recommit to a goal = 重新致力于一个目标
recommit to a project = 重新致力于一个项目
