idiomorphous:/ˌɪdiˈɔːrmɔːfəs/| adj.|自形晶的，自生的|Of or relating to crystals that have well-defined crystal faces and are not deformed or distorted.  Idiomorphic crystals grow freely in a space allowing them to develop their characteristic shapes.|Example: The quartz crystals in this sample are idiomorphic, exhibiting well-defined hexagonal prisms.|近义词：euhedral, automorphic|反义词：xenomorphic, allotriomorphic
