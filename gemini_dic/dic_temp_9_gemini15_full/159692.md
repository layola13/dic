polyarchist:/ˈpɒliˌɑːkɪst/| n. | 多元政治论者；多中心主义者 | A proponent of polyarchism, a political system characterized by the diffusion of power among multiple centers of authority.  Polyarchists believe that concentrating power in a single entity, such as a government, is inefficient and prone to abuse. They advocate for a more distributed and decentralized system. | Example: The prominent political scientist is a staunch polyarchist, arguing for a more participatory and decentralized form of governance.  这位著名的政治学家是坚定的多元政治论者，主张更具参与性和分散化的治理形式。 |近义词: decentralist, pluralist |反义词: centralist, authoritarian
