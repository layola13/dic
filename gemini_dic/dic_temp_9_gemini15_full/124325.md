motherly:/ˈmʌðərli/| adj.|慈母般的，母亲般的|Having the qualities of a mother; showing care, kindness, and protectiveness.  It describes a behavior or attitude that is characteristic of a loving and caring mother.|例句：She showed a motherly concern for her students. 她对她的学生表现出慈母般的关怀。

例句：The teacher had a motherly way of calming down upset children.  这位老师有一种慈母般的方式来安慰哭闹的孩子。


近义词：maternal, protective, caring, nurturing, tender


反义词：uncaring, harsh, cold, unmotherly, neglectful


词性:形容词
