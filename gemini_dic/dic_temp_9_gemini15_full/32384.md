chiliastic:/ˌkɪliˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 千禧年的；相信千年王国来临的；| Relating to or characteristic of millenarianism; relating to or denoting a period of one thousand years.  | 例句：The chiliastic prophecy foretold a golden age of peace and prosperity.  千禧年的预言预示着一个和平与繁荣的黄金时代。

The chiliastic beliefs of some religious groups often involve a literal interpretation of biblical prophecies. 一些宗教团体的千禧年信仰通常涉及对圣经预言的字面解释。


近义词：millenarian, apocalyptic | 反义词：None directly opposite, but terms suggesting the absence of belief in a millennium could be used, such as secular, non-religious.


词源:  From Late Latin *chiliasticus, from Greek χιλιαστικός (chiliastikós), from χίλια (chília) "thousand" + -ιστικός (-istikos) suffix forming adjectives.
