kvetchy: /ˈkvɛtʃi/ | adj. |爱抱怨的；牢骚满腹的 |  Characterized by complaining or grumbling frequently; querulous.  | 例句：He's been rather kvetchy lately, ever since his promotion was denied.  他最近有点爱抱怨，自从他的晋升被拒绝后。


例句：The kvetchy customer kept complaining about the slow service.  这位爱抱怨的顾客一直在抱怨服务太慢。


近义词：complaining, grumbling, whiny, querulous, petulant


反义词：cheerful, optimistic, content, happy


词性：形容词
