comatulid:/kəˈmætjuːlɪd/| n. |海百合类动物|A comatulid is any member of the order Comatulida, a group of feather stars within the class Crinoidea (sea lilies and feather stars).  They are characterized by their free-swimming lifestyle, unlike their stalked relatives.  They have multiple arms, often branched, used for feeding and locomotion.|Example: The researchers found several species of comatulid in the deep-sea trench.|近义词：feather star (less precise)|反义词：无
