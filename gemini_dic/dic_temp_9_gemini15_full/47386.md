deadborn:/ˈdedˌbɔːrn/ | adj. | 死胎的；胎死腹中的 | Stillborn; born dead.  The term refers to a fetus that dies in the uterus before birth.  | 例句：The pregnancy ended in a deadborn child. = 妊娠以死胎告终。

The unfortunate news was that their baby was deadborn. =不幸的消息是他们的婴儿是死胎。


近义词：stillborn, lifeless at birth | 反义词：liveborn, viable
