overeasily:/ˌoʊvərˈiːzəli/| adv.|过分容易地，轻易地|easily to an excessive degree|He overeasily believed her lies. 他轻易地相信了她的谎言。|easily, readily|difficultly, hardly|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overeasily agrees to requests. = 他轻易答应请求。
She overeasily accepts compliments. = 她轻易接受赞美。

They overeasily gave up on the project. = 他们轻易放弃了这个项目。

现在完成时:

She has overeasily fallen for scams. = 她很容易上当受骗。


过去完成时:

He had overeasily believed the rumors. = 他先前轻易相信了谣言。
