fimble:/ˈfɪmbəl/| adj.|纤细的，柔软的；易碎的|  A fimble is a term used in agriculture, specifically referring to a female hemp plant.  It is characterized by its finer, more delicate structure compared to the male plant (a "hible"). The term is now largely archaic or dialectal.  |例句：The farmer carefully separated the fimble hemp plants from the stronger hible plants.  = 农民小心翼翼地将细弱的雌性大麻植株与更强壮的雄性大麻植株分开。The fimble stalks were too delicate for rough handling. = 雌性大麻的茎杆太纤细，经不起粗暴的处理。|近义词：delicate, slender, frail, fragile|反义词：strong, robust, sturdy
