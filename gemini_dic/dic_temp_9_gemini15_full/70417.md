fightbacks:/ˈfaɪtˌbæks/| n.|反击；反攻；反弹|A fightback is a determined effort to resist or overcome something, typically a difficult situation or attack.  It refers to a renewed effort to fight back after being attacked or disadvantaged.  |例句：The team staged a remarkable fightback in the second half, scoring three goals to win the match.  球队在下半场打出了精彩的反击，攻入三球赢得比赛。

The company launched a fightback against its competitors' aggressive marketing campaign. 公司发起了反击，对抗竞争对手咄咄逼人的营销活动。

|近义词：counterattack, comeback, resistance, retaliation|反义词：surrender, defeat, capitulation|名词
