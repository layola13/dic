preeminent:/prɪˈɛmɪnənt/| adj.|卓越的；杰出的；首位的|Standing above or higher than others in rank, power, importance, or achievement.  It suggests a superior position or quality that is clearly evident and widely recognized.|例句：Her preeminent skill as a violinist was undeniable. 她作为小提琴家的卓越技巧是不可否认的。|例句：The company achieved preeminent status in the industry.  这家公司在业内取得了卓越的地位。|近义词：eminent, outstanding, leading, foremost, supreme|反义词：inferior, insignificant, unimportant
