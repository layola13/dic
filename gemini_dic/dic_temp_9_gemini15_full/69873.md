ferruling:/ˈfɛrəlɪŋ/| n. |套管；套管连接；镶嵌|The act of fitting a ferrule to something, or the ferrule itself.  A ferrule is a metal ring or cap fitted to the end of something, such as a cable or a tool handle, to protect it or reinforce it. |例句：The technician carefully applied the ferruling to the fiber optic cable. 技师小心地将套管套在光纤电缆上。

The ferruling process ensured a secure connection between the two pipes. 套管过程确保了两个管道之间的安全连接。|近义词：fitting, sleeve, bushing |反义词：unfitting, removal
