conveying:/kənˈveɪɪŋ/| v.| 传达；运送；表达 | The act of making something known or understood, or of transporting something from one place to another.  | 例句：He was conveying a sense of urgency. 他正在传达一种紧迫感。 | 例句：The trucks were conveying goods to the warehouse.卡车正在把货物运送到仓库。|近义词：transmitting, communicating, transporting, carrying | 反义词：concealing, withholding, suppressing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conveys his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地表达了他的想法。
The train conveys passengers to the city. = 火车把乘客运送到城市。

一般过去时:
She conveyed her message effectively. = 她有效地传达了她的信息。
The ship conveyed the cargo across the ocean. = 船只把货物运送过海洋。

现在完成时:
They have conveyed their concerns to the management. = 他们已经向管理层表达了他们的担忧。
The company has conveyed numerous products this year. = 今年公司已经运送了大量的产品。

过去完成时:
He had conveyed the news before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经传达了这个消息。
The messenger had conveyed the package before the deadline. = 信使在截止日期前已经送达了包裹。

将来时:
We will convey our appreciation in a letter. = 我们将在信中表达我们的感谢。
The pipeline will convey oil to the refinery. = 管道将输送石油到炼油厂。

虚拟语气:
If he conveyed his feelings, she would understand. = 如果他表达了他的感受，她就会理解。
I wish he had conveyed the information sooner. = 我希望他早点传达这个信息。

被动语态:
The message was conveyed through email. = 信息是通过电子邮件传达的。
The goods were conveyed by air. = 货物是通过空运的。

疑问句:
How did you convey your ideas? = 你是如何表达你的想法的？
What method will be used to convey the supplies? = 将采用什么方法来运送物资？

否定句:
He didn't convey his anger effectively. = 他没有有效地表达他的愤怒。
The system failed to convey the data accurately. = 系统未能准确地传送数据。



PHRASE->
convey a message = 传达信息
convey an idea = 表达一个想法
convey feelings = 表达感情
convey one's meaning = 表达某人的意思
convey a sense of = 传达一种…的感觉
