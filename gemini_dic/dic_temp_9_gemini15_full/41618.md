corralling: /kəˈrælɪŋ/ | v. | 驱赶；围捕；控制 | The act of gathering and confining animals, typically livestock, within an enclosure.  It can also refer to the act of controlling or managing something in a forceful or effective way. | 例句：The cowboys were corralling the cattle. = 牛仔们正在驱赶牛群。  The teacher struggled to corral the unruly children. = 老师努力控制那些不守规矩的孩子们。 | 近义词：rounding up, gathering, herding, managing, controlling | 反义词：releasing, freeing, liberating
