rethickening: /ˌriːˈθɪkənɪŋ/ | n. | 再增稠；重新增稠 | The process of making something thicker again, often referring to a liquid or semi-liquid substance that has become thinner and needs to be thickened again.  This often involves adding a thickening agent. | 例句：The sauce required rethickening after it had been simmered for too long.  酱汁炖煮太久后需要重新增稠。The soup needed rethickening after adding too much broth. 汤加了太多的肉汤，需要重新增稠。|近义词：re-thickening, thickening, reconcentration |反义词：thinning, diluting | 名词
