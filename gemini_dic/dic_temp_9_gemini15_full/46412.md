daftie:/ˈdæfti/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A daftie is a foolish or silly person.  The term is primarily used in Scotland and some parts of northern England.|例句：He's such a daftie; he fell for that old trick. 他真是个傻瓜，居然上了那个老套的当。

例句：Don't be such a daftie; think before you act.别傻了，行动前先想想。


近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, nincompoop|反义词：genius, intelligent, clever, smart|词性: 名词
