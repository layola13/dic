pleaded:/ˈpliːdɪd/| v.|恳求；抗辩；认罪| Past tense and past participle of "plead."  To plead means to make an earnest or emotional request; to argue a case in a court of law; or to admit guilt to a charge. |例句：She pleaded with him to stay. 她恳求他留下。

The defendant pleaded not guilty.  被告申辩无罪。


近义词：begged, implored, argued, confessed|反义词：denied, refuted

一般现在时:
He pleads for mercy. 他恳求怜悯。
She pleads innocence. 她为自己辩解清白。

一般过去时:
He pleaded guilty to the charges. 他对指控认罪。
She pleaded with her parents to let her go. 她恳求父母让她走。

现在完成时:
He has pleaded not guilty throughout the trial. 他在整个审判过程中都拒不认罪。
She has pleaded with me many times for help. 她多次向我求助。

过去完成时:
He had pleaded with her for days before she finally agreed. 他恳求了她好几天，她才最终同意。
She had pleaded innocence, but no one believed her. 她一直辩解清白，但没有人相信她。

将来时:
He will plead guilty if he is offered a reduced sentence. 如果给他减刑，他会认罪。
She will plead for her life if she is captured. 如果她被抓到，她会为自己的生命而辩解。


虚拟语气:
I would plead with him, but I know it's useless. 我想恳求他，但我知道没用。
If she had pleaded guilty earlier, she would have received a lighter sentence. 如果她早些认罪，她就会得到较轻的判决。

被动语态:
His case was pleaded by a renowned lawyer. 他的案子由一位著名的律师辩护。
The matter was pleaded before the judge. 这件事已在法官面前陈述。

疑问句:
Did he plead guilty or not guilty? 他认罪还是拒不认罪？
Will she plead for leniency? 她会请求宽大处理吗？

否定句:
He did not plead guilty. 他没有认罪。
She did not plead for help. 她没有求助。

词源:
The word "plead" comes from the Old English word "plēdian," meaning "to discuss, debate, or argue."  This ultimately derives from the Proto-Germanic *plōdijan, related to words meaning "to speak" or "to converse." The legal sense evolved from the idea of formally arguing a case in court.
