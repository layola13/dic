multipinnate:/ˌmʌltiˈpɪneɪt/| adj.|多羽状的，多回羽状的|Having many pinnae (leaflets) arranged in a compound manner, typically multiple times divided.  This describes a leaf structure where the leaflets are themselves further divided into smaller leaflets. |The fern showed a multipinnate frond, a beautiful example of its complex branching pattern.|近义词：multifoliate, much-divided|反义词：simple, unipinnate
