pyramidoid:/ˌpɪrəˈmɔɪdɔɪd/| n. |类锥体，锥体状物 |A pyramidoid is a solid figure resembling a pyramid but not necessarily having a polygonal base.  It can be a truncated pyramid, a frustum, or a more irregular shape.  The term often implies a structure that tapers to a point or apex. | Example: The architect designed a building with a pyramidoid roof. 建筑师设计了一座带有锥体状屋顶的建筑物。 The crystalline structure exhibited a pyramidoid shape.  晶体结构呈现出锥体状。|近义词：pyramid, cone, frustum |反义词：cylinder, cube
