durante:/dʊˈrænti/ | preposition | 在…期间；在…过程中 | During the war, many people lost their homes.  战争期间，许多人失去了家园。|  近义词：throughout, throughout the course of, within | 反义词：after, before


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works during the day. = 他白天工作。
She studies during the week. = 她一周内学习。

一般过去时:
They lived in Paris during the 1920s. = 他们20世纪20年代住在巴黎。
We travelled extensively during our summer vacation. = 我们在暑假期间进行了广泛的旅行。

现在完成时:
I have worked hard during this project. = 在这个项目期间我努力工作了。
She has made significant progress during her studies. = 在她的学习期间她取得了显著的进步。

过去完成时:
They had already finished the project before the deadline during the previous year. = 他们在前一年截止日期之前就已经完成了项目。
She had learned a lot about history during her prior research. = 在她之前的研究中，她已经学到了很多关于历史的知识。

将来时:
We will visit Rome during our next trip. = 我们下次旅行期间将参观罗马。
They will be working on the new project during the coming months. = 未来几个月他们将致力于新的项目。

疑问句:

Were you at home during the storm? = 暴风雨期间你在家吗？


否定句:
