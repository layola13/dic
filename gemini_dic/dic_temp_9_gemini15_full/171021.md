pseudosiphonic:/ˌsuːdoʊˈsaɪfɒnɪk/|/ˌsjuːdəʊˈsaɪfɒnɪk/| adj.| 假拟管的；似管状的 |  Describing a plant structure that resembles a siphon but is not formed by the fusion of cells, but rather by a close arrangement of filaments or hyphae giving the appearance of a tube.  | Example: The algae exhibited a pseudosiphonic structure, mimicking the appearance of a true siphon.  藻类呈现出假拟管状结构，模仿了真正虹吸管的外观。 | 近义词:siphonous (in appearance) | 反义词:cellular, truly siphonic
