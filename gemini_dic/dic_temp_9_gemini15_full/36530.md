collaborates:/kəˈlæbəreɪts/| v. |合作，协作|To work jointly on an activity or project.  It implies a shared effort towards a common goal. |The two companies collaborate on research and development. 这两家公司在研发方面合作。| cooperate, work together, team up | compete, oppose, fight against |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collaborate on many projects. = 他们合作许多项目。
The team collaborates effectively. = 这个团队有效地合作。


一般过去时:
The artists collaborated on a mural. = 这些艺术家合作创作了一幅壁画。
We collaborated closely on the report. = 我们密切合作完成了这份报告。


现在完成时:
They have collaborated on several successful films. = 他们已经合作拍摄了几部成功的电影。
She has collaborated with many renowned musicians. = 她已经与许多著名的音乐家合作过。


过去完成时:
They had collaborated for years before the project began. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经合作多年了。
The scientists had collaborated extensively on the research. = 科学家们在这个研究上进行了广泛的合作。


将来时:
We will collaborate on the new marketing campaign. = 我们将合作开展新的营销活动。
The two companies will collaborate to develop a new product. = 这两家公司将合作开发一款新产品。


虚拟语气:
If they collaborated more effectively, they would achieve better results. = 如果他们更有效地合作，他们将取得更好的成果。
I wish they had collaborated more closely on the project. = 我希望他们在项目上能更紧密地合作。


被动语态:
The project was collaborated on by several teams. = 这个项目是由几个团队合作完成的。
The research was collaborated on by scientists from different countries. = 这项研究是由来自不同国家的科学家合作完成的。


疑问句:
Do they collaborate often? = 他们经常合作吗？
Will they collaborate on the next project? = 他们会在下一个项目中合作吗？


否定句:
They do not collaborate well. = 他们合作得不好。
The two companies did not collaborate on that project. = 这两家公司没有在那个项目上合作。


PHRASE->
collaborate with = 与…合作
collaborate on = 合作做…
collaborate to = 为了…而合作
collaborative effort = 合作努力
collaborative project = 合作项目
