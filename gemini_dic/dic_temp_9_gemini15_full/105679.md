kingside:/ˈkɪŋˌsaɪd/| n.|国王一方（国际象棋术语）|The kingside refers to the half of the chessboard on the side where the king starts the game.  It typically includes the king, queen's rook, and the squares adjacent to them.|例句：He launched a kingside attack, sacrificing a pawn to gain control of the key squares. 他发动了国王一方的进攻，牺牲了一个兵来控制关键棋格。


例句：The game was decided by a brilliant kingside maneuver. 这盘棋是由精彩的国王一方调动决定胜负的。


近义词：king's side


反义词：queenside
