potent: /ˈpoʊtənt/ | adj. | 强效的；有效的；有力的 | Powerful; having a strong effect.  |例句：The medicine is potent and should be taken only as directed. 这药效很强，必须严格按照医嘱服用。
例句：He delivered a potent speech that moved the audience to tears. 他发表了一篇强有力的演讲，感动了听众。|近义词：powerful, strong, effective, mighty |反义词：weak, ineffective, powerless |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine is potent. = 这药效很强。
This argument is potent. = 这个论点很有力。

一般过去时:
The potion was potent enough to kill a dragon. = 这药水强效到足以杀死一条龙。
His speech was potent and persuasive. = 他的演讲有力且具有说服力。

现在完成时:
The new policy has proven potent in boosting the economy. = 新政策已被证明在促进经济方面非常有效。
Her influence has been potent throughout the company. = 她的影响力在公司一直很强大。

过去完成时:
The evidence had been potent enough to convict him. = 证据已经强大到足以定他的罪。
The rumour had been potent enough to damage his reputation. = 这谣言已经强大到足以损害他的声誉。

将来时:
This new technology will be potent in the near future. = 这项新技术在不久的将来将非常有效。
The upcoming election will be potent in shaping national policy. = 即将到来的选举将对塑造国家政策产生重大影响。

虚拟语气:
If the medicine were more potent, he would have recovered sooner. = 如果药效更强，他就会恢复得更快。
If the evidence had been more potent, the jury would have reached a different verdict. = 如果证据更充分，陪审团就会做出不同的裁决。

被动语态:
The effects of the drug are widely considered potent. = 该药物的作用被广泛认为是有效的。
The argument was considered potent by the judge. = 法官认为该论点很有力。

疑问句:

Is this argument potent enough to convince them? = 这个论点足以说服他们吗？

否定句:
The argument was not potent enough to sway the jury. = 这个论点不足以影响陪审团。
The evidence was not potent enough to support a conviction. = 证据不足以支持定罪。
