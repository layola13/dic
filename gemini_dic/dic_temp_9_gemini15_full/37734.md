compartmentalising: /kəmˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 分隔；划分；区隔 | The act of dividing something into separate compartments or sections;  treating different aspects of one's life or experience as entirely separate and unrelated.  | 例句：He compartmentalises his work and personal life very effectively. 他非常有效地将工作和个人生活区分开来。


例句：She compartmentalises her emotions, rarely letting them affect her professional decisions. 她将自己的情绪划分开来，很少让它们影响她的职业决策。


近义词：secting, partitioning, isolating, separating, dividing


反义词：integrating, unifying, blending, merging


词性:动词
