schemed:/skiːmd/| v.|策划；图谋；密谋|To plan or plot something, especially something secret or dishonest.|例句：They schemed to overthrow the government.他们密谋推翻政府。

例句：He schemed to get a promotion. 他策划获得晋升。|近义词：plot, plan, conspire, intrigue|反义词：act honestly, cooperate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They scheme to get rich quickly. = 他们密谋快速致富。
He schemes against his rivals. = 他策划对付他的对手。


一般过去时:
She schemed to win the competition. = 她策划赢得比赛。
They schemed a way to escape. = 他们密谋了一条逃跑路线。


现在完成时:
He has schemed his way to the top. = 他通过策划达到了顶峰。
They have schemed against us for years. = 他们多年来一直在暗中算计我们。


过去完成时:
She had schemed for months before she finally acted. = 在她最终采取行动之前，她已经策划了几个月。
They had schemed to steal the jewels before the security arrived. = 在保安到达之前，他们已经密谋偷窃珠宝。


将来时:
We will scheme a surprise party for her birthday. = 我们将策划一个惊喜派对为她庆祝生日。
They will scheme to get revenge. = 他们将密谋报复。


虚拟语气:
If they had schemed better, they might have succeeded. = 如果他们策划得更好，他们也许会成功。
I wish I hadn't schemed against my friend. = 我真希望我没有算计我的朋友。


被动语态:
The plan was schemed by a small group of people. = 这个计划是由一小群人策划的。
A surprise attack was schemed by the enemy. = 敌人策划了一次突然袭击。


疑问句:
What are they scheming? = 他们在密谋什么？
Who is scheming against the company? = 谁在暗中算计公司？


否定句:
They didn't scheme to harm anyone. = 他们没有策划伤害任何人。
We did not scheme to win unfairly. = 我们没有策划不公平地获胜。


PHRASE->
scheme of things = 大局；整体情况
elaborate scheme = 精心策划的计划
grand scheme = 宏伟计划
a cunning scheme = 一个狡猾的计划
