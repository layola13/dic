curlicue:/ˈkɜːrlɪkjuː/| n. |卷曲；花体字；螺旋形花纹 |A curlicue is a decorative flourish in writing or drawing, typically a spiral or a series of curves.  It can also refer to something that is curly or coiled. |例句：The artist added delicate curlicues to the border of the painting.  艺术家在画的边框上添加了精致的卷曲花纹。

The swirling curlicue of smoke rose from the chimney.  从烟囱里升起一缕缕盘旋的烟雾。|近义词：curl, swirl, spiral, scroll |反义词：straight line, straight

词源:
The word "curlicue" is a combination of "curl" and "cue,"  reflecting its visual appearance.  "Curl" implies a curved shape, while "cue" suggests a decorative flourish or embellishment.  The word evolved in the 19th century.
