delphinus:/dɛlˈfaɪnəs/| n. |海豚|The word "delphinus" refers to dolphins, a group of marine mammals known for their intelligence and social behavior.  They are characterized by their streamlined bodies, powerful tails, and highly developed brains.  Delphinus is also the genus name for the common dolphin. |例句：The delphinus genus includes several species of dolphins found in various oceans.|近义词：dolphin (common usage)|反义词：无
