presolution:/ˌpriːsɒˈluːʃən/| n. |预先解决方案，预防措施|A presolution is a plan or action taken in advance to prevent a problem from occurring.  It's proactive rather than reactive.  It anticipates a potential issue and aims to address it before it arises.|Example:  The company implemented a presolution to prevent data breaches by investing in advanced cybersecurity measures. 公司通过投资先进的网络安全措施实施了预防数据泄露的预先解决方案。|近义词：preemptive measure, preventative measure, proactive approach|反义词：reaction, remedy, cure|名词
