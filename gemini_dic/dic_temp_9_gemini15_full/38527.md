conciseness:/kənˈsaɪsnəs/ | n. | 简洁；简明；扼要 | The quality of being brief and to the point.  Conciseness is essential in effective communication.  It avoids unnecessary words and focuses on the core message. |例句：The report lacked conciseness; it was far too long and rambling.  这篇报告缺乏简洁性；它太冗长且冗余了。

The author praised the conciseness of the editor's revisions. 作者称赞编辑修改的简洁性。|近义词：brevity, succinctness, terseness |反义词：verbosity, wordiness, prolixity
