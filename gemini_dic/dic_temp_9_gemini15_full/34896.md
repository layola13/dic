clique:/kliːk/| n. |小集团，派系 |A small, exclusive group of people; a group bound together by shared interests, tastes, or social status, often excluding outsiders.  |例句：There's a clique of popular girls who always sit together at lunch.  她们中间有个小团体总是坐在一起吃午饭。|例句：He tried to join the clique, but they rejected him. 他试图加入那个小团体，但他们拒绝了他。|近义词：circle, coterie, cabal, set |反义词：outsiders, majority
