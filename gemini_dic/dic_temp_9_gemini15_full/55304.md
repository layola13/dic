dissolvabilities:/ˌdɪsɒlvəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |溶解度（复数）|The capacity of a substance to dissolve in a solvent.  This term refers to the plural form, indicating multiple dissolving capacities or the dissolving capacities of multiple substances.  It encompasses various factors influencing the ability to dissolve, such as temperature, pressure, and the nature of both solute and solvent.| Example: The experiment investigated the dissolvabilities of various salts in different solvents at varying temperatures.  这个实验研究了不同溶剂中各种盐在不同温度下的溶解度。|近义词：solubilities |反义词：insolubilities
