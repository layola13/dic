gave:/ɡeɪv/| v.|给予，赠送|Past tense of "give".  It indicates a past action of providing something to someone or something.|He gave me a book. 他给了我一本 书。|She gave her all to the cause. 她为事业奉献了一切。|provided, presented, offered|received, took, kept|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I give you my word. = 我向你保证。
They give generously to charity. = 他们慷慨地捐赠给慈善机构。


一般过去时:
He gave her a flower. = 他给了她一朵花。
I gave a speech yesterday. = 我昨天做了一个演讲。


现在完成时:
She has given everything she has. = 她已经付出了一切。
They have given up smoking. = 他们已经戒烟了。


过去完成时:
He had given up hope. = 他已经放弃了希望。
She had given him many warnings. = 她已经给他很多警告了。


将来时:
I will give you a call later. = 我晚些时候会给你打电话。
They will give a presentation tomorrow. = 他们明天将做一个演示。


虚拟语气:
If I had given more thought to it, I wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果我当时多考虑一下，我就不会陷入这种境地了。
I wish I had given him a chance. = 我真希望我给了他一个机会。


被动语态:
The award was given to him. = 这个奖颁给了他。
The money was given to the charity. = 这笔钱捐给了慈善机构。


疑问句:
Did he give you the money? = 他给了你钱吗？
What did she give you for your birthday? = 她给你过生日送了什么礼物？


否定句:
I didn't give him the answer. = 我没有给他答案。
She didn't give up easily. = 她没有轻易放弃。



PHRASE->
