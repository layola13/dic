couldn't:/ˈkʊdnt/| v.|不能，不可能|Contraction of "could not," indicating inability or impossibility. It is the past tense of "can not."|


例句：I couldn't go to the party because I was sick. = 我因为生病所以不能去参加派对。

例句：She couldn't believe her eyes. = 她不敢相信自己的眼睛。


近义词：was unable to, wasn't able to


反义词：could, was able to


词性:动词
