chairs:/tʃɛrz/ | n. |椅子；主席团 | A chair is a seat with a back, typically for one person.  Chairs are commonly found in homes, offices, and other public places. |  There are four chairs around the dining table.  餐桌周围有四把椅子。

The children sat on their chairs and listened attentively. 孩子们坐在椅子上专心致志地听着。
