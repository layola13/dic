irrelevances:/ɪˈrɛləvənsɪz/ | n. | 不相干的事；无关紧要的事 |  Things that are not relevant or important.  They are often considered unimportant or distracting from the main issue. | Example:  His comments were full of irrelevances and digressions.  他的评论充满了无关紧要的话题和离题之言。


|近义词: insignificances, trivialities, non sequiturs |反义词:  relevances, essentials, importances |词性:n.
