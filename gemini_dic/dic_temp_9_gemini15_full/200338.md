skunkhead:/ˈskʌŋkˌhed/| n.|臭鼬头，（尤指因其行为而被如此称呼的）令人讨厌的人|A skunkhead is a derogatory term for a person, especially one considered unpleasant or offensive, often due to their behavior.  The term alludes to the unpleasant odor of a skunk.|例句：He was called a skunkhead after he pulled that nasty prank. 他因为恶作剧而被称为臭鼬头。

例句：The bully was labeled a skunkhead by his classmates.  那个恶霸被他的同学们称为臭鼬头。|近义词：pest, nuisance, troublemaker, jerk|反义词：saint, angel, darling
