saccular:/ˈsækjuːlər/| adj.|囊状的；袋状的 | Relating to or resembling a sac or pouch.  A saccular structure is one that is shaped like a sac or pouch.  |例句：The saccular aneurysms are often asymptomatic. 囊状动脉瘤通常无症状。|例句：The lungs have a saccular structure. 肺部具有囊状结构。|近义词：sac-like, pouch-like, vesicular |反义词：tubular, cylindrical
