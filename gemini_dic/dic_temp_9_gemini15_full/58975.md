dutchwoman:/ˈdʌtʃˌwʊmən/| n. |荷兰妇女|A female native or inhabitant of the Netherlands.  A woman of Dutch descent. |例句：I met a charming Dutchwoman at the conference. 我在会议上遇到一位迷人的荷兰妇女。

例句：The Dutchwoman spoke fluent English and French.这位荷兰妇女能流利地说英语和法语。


近义词：Dutch lady, Netherlandish woman


反义词：无


词性：名词


词源:  The word is a compound word. "Dutch" derives from the Middle Dutch word "Duuts," meaning "German,"  which was later used to refer specifically to the people of the Netherlands. "-woman" is a suffix indicating a female person.  The evolution reflects historical linguistic and geopolitical relationships.
