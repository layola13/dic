omnigraph:/ˈɒmnɪɡræf/ | n. |全景图，万花筒式图像|A comprehensive or panoramic representation or depiction, often suggesting a vast amount of interconnected information or data. It may be visual, textual, or a combination of both.|例句：The omnigraph displayed the complex relationships between various global economic systems. 全景图展示了各种全球经济体系之间的复杂关系。|近义词：panorama, overview, synthesis|反义词：detail, fragment
