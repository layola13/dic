inseminates:/ˈɪnsɛmɪneɪts/| vt.|人工授精；播种；使充满|To introduce semen into (a female animal or person) to induce fertilization or conception; to introduce something (such as an idea) into something else.|例句：The farmer inseminates his cows artificially to improve the breed.  农民对他的奶牛进行人工授精以改良品种。

例句：The professor inseminated the students' minds with new ideas. 教授给学生们灌输了新的思想。

近义词：impregnate, fertilize, inoculate|反义词：sterilize
