partitioning:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənɪŋ/| n. |分区；划分；分割 |The act of dividing something into parts or sections.  It often refers to the process of dividing a hard drive, database, or network into smaller, more manageable units.  |Example:  The database administrator performed partitioning to improve query performance. 数据库管理员进行了分区以提高查询性能。

The system administrator is responsible for partitioning the hard drive. 系统管理员负责对硬盘进行分区。|近义词：division, subdivision, segmentation |反义词：unification, integration, consolidation
