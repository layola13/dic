escallop:/ɪˈskæləp/| n. vt.|扇贝；去壳；切成扇贝状|A scallop is a type of shellfish with a fan-shaped shell.  The verb "to escallop" means to cook (usually bake) food in a scalloped shape, or to cut food into scallop shapes.|例句：We had delicious grilled scallops for dinner.  我们晚餐吃了美味的烤扇贝。|例句：She escalloped the potatoes before roasting them. 她先把土豆切成扇贝状再烘烤。|近义词：shellfish, pecten|反义词：无|名词，动词
