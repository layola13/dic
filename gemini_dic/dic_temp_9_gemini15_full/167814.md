primitively:/prɪˈmɪtɪvli/| adv.|原始地；简陋地；粗糙地 |In a primitive manner; in a way that shows the characteristics of an early stage of development.  It suggests a lack of sophistication, refinement, or complexity. |例句：The tools were primitively made, suggesting a lack of advanced technology.  这些工具制作粗糙，表明缺乏先进技术。

例句：The society lived primitively, relying on hunting and gathering for sustenance.  这个社会过着原始的生活，依靠狩猎和采集维持生计。


近义词：rudely, simply, roughly, crudely

反义词：sophisticatedly, elegantly, subtly, advanced
