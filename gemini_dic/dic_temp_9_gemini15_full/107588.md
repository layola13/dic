landscape:/ˈlændskeɪp/| n.|风景，景色；地貌；（绘画、摄影等的）风景画；|The word "landscape" refers to a view of a region, especially one of natural beauty.  It can also refer to the physical features of an area of land, or a painting or photograph depicting such a view. |例句：The landscape was breathtaking, with rolling hills and a clear blue sky.  这片风景令人叹为观止，有着连绵起伏的山丘和湛蓝的天空。

例句：The artist painted a stunning landscape of the Tuscan countryside.  这位艺术家画了一幅令人惊叹的托斯卡纳乡村风景画。|近义词：scenery, vista, view, scene|反义词：portrait (in the context of art)|词性:名词
