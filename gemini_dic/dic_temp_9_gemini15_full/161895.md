praetorium:/prɪˈtɔːriəm/| n.|将领营帐；总督府；罗马皇帝的宫殿|The praetorium was the general's tent in a Roman army camp, later evolving to refer to the governor's official residence in a province and eventually the imperial palace in Rome.  It was a place of authority and command.|Example: The Roman governor resided in the magnificent praetorium overlooking the city. 罗马总督居住在俯瞰城市的宏伟总督府里。|近义词：palace, headquarters, command post|反义词：field, outpost
