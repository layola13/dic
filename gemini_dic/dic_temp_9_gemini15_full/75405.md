fructification:/ˌfruktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |结果实；结果；成果；丰收|The process of producing fruit; the development of fruit from a flower.  It can also refer to the production of spores or other reproductive structures in plants.  Furthermore, it can be used figuratively to describe the successful outcome or culmination of an effort or process.|例句：The orchard showed a bountiful fructification this year.  今年果园获得了丰收。|例句：The fructification of their years of research resulted in a groundbreaking discovery.  他们多年研究的成果导致了一项突破性发现。|近义词：bearing fruit, production, yield, fruition |反义词：barrenness, sterility, failure
