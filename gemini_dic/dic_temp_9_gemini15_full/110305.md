light-footed:/ˈlaɪtˈfʊtɪd/| adj.|轻快的，轻盈的|Moving with ease and grace; nimble.  It implies a quiet and delicate movement, often associated with agility and speed.|例句：The dancer moved light-footed across the stage.  舞者轻盈地跨过舞台。  The cat moved light-footed through the tall grass.  猫轻盈地穿过高高的草丛。|近义词：nimble, agile, swift, sprightly|反义词：clumsy, heavy-footed, lumbering
