nonraiseable: /ˌnɒnˈreɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 不可提升的，无法提升的 |  Describes something that cannot be raised, lifted, or increased.  This typically applies to a status, level, or value that is fixed or cannot be changed. | Example: The error message indicated a nonraiseable exception.  这个错误信息表明了一个不可提升的异常。 The employee's salary was nonraiseable due to company policy. 由于公司政策，员工的工资无法提升。 | 近义词: unraisable, immovable, inflexible | 反义词: raiseable, adjustable, modifiable
