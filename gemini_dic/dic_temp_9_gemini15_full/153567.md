pentahedrous:/ˌpɛntəˈhiːdrəs/| adj.|五面体的；有五个面的|Having five sides or faces.  A pentahedrous crystal has five faces.|例句：The mineral displayed a distinct pentahedrous structure.这种矿物呈现出明显的五面体结构。  The geode showed a beautiful, almost perfectly pentahedrous formation.  该晶洞展现出美丽、几乎完美的五面体结构。|近义词：five-sided, pentagonal (if referring to a plane figure)|反义词：polyhedrous (with many sides), tetrahedrous (with four sides)
