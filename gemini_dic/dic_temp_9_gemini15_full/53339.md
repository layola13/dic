disadvantage: /ˌdɪsˈædvəntɪdʒ/ | n. | 缺点；不利条件 | A condition or circumstance that puts someone or something at a disadvantage.  |例句：One disadvantage of living in the city is the high cost of housing.  住在城市的一个缺点是高昂的住房成本。

例句：The main disadvantage of this method is its complexity.  这种方法的主要缺点是其复杂性。


近义词：drawback, handicap, impediment, shortcoming | 反义词：advantage, benefit, asset


词源:  The word "disadvantage" is formed from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of"), and the noun "advantage."  The word "advantage" itself originates from Old French "avant" (before) + "tager" (to touch).  Therefore, "disadvantage" literally means "the opposite of being in a favorable position."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The high cost of living presents a disadvantage to many families. = 高昂的生活成本对许多家庭来说是一个不利因素。
This job has several disadvantages, including long hours. = 这份工作有几个缺点，包括长时间工作。


一般过去时:
The bad weather was a disadvantage to the team's performance. = 恶劣的天气对球队的表现造成了不利影响。
His lack of experience proved to be a disadvantage in the interview. = 他缺乏经验在面试中被证明是一个不利因素。


现在完成时:
The company has faced many disadvantages since the economic downturn. = 自经济衰退以来，该公司面临着许多不利因素。
His illness has put him at a significant disadvantage in the competition. = 他的病让他在比赛中处于明显的劣势。


过去完成时:
The team had already been at a disadvantage before the game even started. = 比赛开始之前，球队就已经处于劣势了。
Because of his injury, he had been at a disadvantage throughout the entire season. = 由于受伤，他在整个赛季都处于劣势。


将来时:
The new regulations will put small businesses at a disadvantage. = 新规定将使小型企业处于不利地位。
Lack of funding will disadvantage our research project next year. = 缺乏资金将使我们的研究项目明年处于不利地位。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we wouldn't be at such a disadvantage. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们就不会处于如此不利的境地。
I wish the lack of funding wasn't such a disadvantage to our team. = 我希望资金短缺不会对我们的团队造成如此大的不利影响。


被动语态:
Small businesses are often disadvantaged by unfair competition. = 小型企业常常因不公平竞争而处于不利地位。
The team was disadvantaged by the biased refereeing. = 球队受到了偏袒裁判的不利影响。


疑问句:
What are the disadvantages of this new policy? = 这项新政策的缺点是什么？
Does this approach have any significant disadvantages? = 这种方法有什么显著的缺点吗？


否定句:
This product has no significant disadvantages compared to its competitors. = 与竞争对手相比，该产品没有明显的缺点。
The plan has few disadvantages; its advantages far outweigh them. = 这个计划的缺点很少；它的优点远远超过缺点。
