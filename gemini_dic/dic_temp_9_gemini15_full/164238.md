predonation: /ˌpriːˌdoʊˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 预先捐赠，提前捐赠 |  A donation made before an event or need arises.  It's often used in the context of charitable giving or fundraising, where individuals or organizations pledge money or resources in advance of a specific project or campaign. | Example: The company announced a predonation of $1 million to the disaster relief fund.  该公司宣布向救灾基金预先捐赠100万美元。 |近义词：advance donation, pre-donation, prior donation | 反义词：postdonation, delayed donation
