ponder:/ˈpɒndə(r)/, /ˈpɑːndər/ | vt. |沉思，考虑，琢磨 | To think deeply and carefully about something.  |例句：I pondered the question for a long time. 我仔细考虑了这个问题很久。|例句：She pondered over her options before making a decision.  她在做决定之前仔细权衡了自己的选择。|近义词：consider, contemplate, reflect, meditate |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I ponder the meaning of life. = 我在思考生命的意义。
He ponders his next move in the game. = 他正在思考在游戏中下一步该怎么走。

一般过去时:
I pondered the implications of her words. = 我仔细思考了她话语的含义。
They pondered the problem for hours. = 他们思考这个问题几个小时。

现在完成时:
I have pondered this issue for weeks. = 我已经思考这个问题好几个星期了。
She has pondered the possibilities carefully. = 她已经仔细考虑了各种可能性。

过去完成时:
I had pondered the matter before I spoke. = 我在说话之前已经考虑过这个问题了。
They had pondered their options before making a decision. = 他们做出决定之前已经仔细考虑过自己的选择。

将来时:
I will ponder this decision carefully. = 我将会仔细考虑这个决定。
We will ponder the consequences of our actions. = 我们将会考虑我们行为的后果。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ponder the matter further. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会进一步考虑这个问题。
I wish I had pondered the consequences more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地考虑过后果。

被动语态:
The problem was pondered by the committee. = 这个问题被委员会仔细考虑了。
The decision was pondered for many days. = 这个决定被考虑了很多天。

疑问句:
Have you pondered the implications? = 你考虑过这些含义吗？
What are you pondering? = 你在思考什么？

否定句:
I did not ponder the consequences. = 我没有考虑后果。
He didn't ponder the offer long before accepting it. = 他接受这个提议之前并没有仔细考虑。


PHRASE->
