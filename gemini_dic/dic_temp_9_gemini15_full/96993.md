infantilizing: /ˈɪnfæntaɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使幼稚化；以婴儿般的方式对待 | Treating someone as if they were a baby, thus hindering their development and independence.  This often involves patronizing language, oversimplification of tasks, or excessive protection. |


例句：

He infantilizes his wife by constantly making decisions for her without consulting her. = 他总是替妻子做决定而不征求她的意见，这让她感觉很幼稚。

The company's infantilizing marketing campaign portrayed adults as helpless children needing their product. = 该公司的营销活动将成年人描绘成需要其产品才能自理的无助儿童，这种做法让人感觉很幼稚。


近义词：

baby, coddle, pamper, patronize, overprotect


反义词：

empower, encourage independence, treat as an adult


词性：

动词
