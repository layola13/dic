detained:/dɪˈteɪnd/ | v. |拘留；扣留；耽搁 | To keep someone in official custody, often for questioning or as a punishment; to delay or hinder. |例句：The police detained him for questioning. 警方将他拘留接受问话。

The suspect was detained overnight. 嫌疑犯被拘留了一夜。


近义词：arrest, hold, confine, keep, delay |反义词：release, free, liberate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police detain suspects for questioning. = 警方拘留嫌疑犯进行讯问。
They detain the package at customs. = 他们在海关扣留包裹。


一般过去时:
The police detained him for twenty-four hours. = 警方将他拘留了二十四小时。
She was detained by a traffic jam. = 她被交通堵塞耽搁了。


现在完成时:
They have detained the prisoner since yesterday. = 他们从昨天起就拘留了囚犯。
The bad weather has detained us for hours. = 恶劣天气使我们耽搁了好几个小时。


过去完成时:
The police had detained the suspect before the trial. = 警方在审判前已将嫌疑犯拘留。
The snowstorm had detained the travelers for days. = 暴风雪使旅客们耽搁了好几天。


将来时:
The police will detain the criminal until the trial. = 警方会将罪犯拘留到审判为止。
The meeting will detain me until late afternoon. = 会议会让我耽搁到下午晚些时候。


虚拟语气:
If they had detained him earlier, the crime might have been prevented. = 如果他们早点拘留他，罪行也许就能避免了。
I wish the flight hadn't been detained by the fog. = 我希望航班没有因为雾而延误。


被动语态:
He was detained on suspicion of murder. = 他因涉嫌谋杀而被拘留。
The shipment was detained at the port. = 货物在港口被扣留。


疑问句:
Was the suspect detained? = 嫌疑犯被拘留了吗？
Why was the flight detained? = 为什么航班延误了？


否定句:
The police did not detain him. = 警方没有拘留他。
The bad weather did not detain us. = 恶劣天气没有耽搁我们。



PHRASE->
be detained by = 被...耽搁
detained for questioning = 被拘留问话
under detention = 在拘留中
detained without trial = 未经审判而被拘留
lengthy detention = 长期拘留

词源:
From Old French *detener, from Latin detinēre "to hold back, keep," from tenere "to hold."  The prefix "de-" intensifies the meaning of the verb.
