corrupts:/kəˈrʌpts/| vt. |使腐败；使堕落；破坏；篡改|To make someone or something morally bad or dishonest.  To damage or destroy the integrity of something.|例句：Power corrupts. = 权力使人腐败。
The scandal corrupted the reputation of the company. = 丑闻损害了公司的声誉。|近义词：spoil, contaminate, pervert, debase |反义词：purify, improve, enhance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Corruption corrupts the political system. = 腐败败坏了政治制度。
He corrupts the data in the file. = 他篡改了文件里的数据。


一般过去时:
The king's advisors corrupted him with flattery. = 国王的顾问们用奉承腐蚀了他。
The hackers corrupted the company's database. = 黑客破坏了公司的数据库。


现在完成时:
Years of dishonesty have corrupted his character. = 多年的不诚实已经败坏了他的品格。
The virus has corrupted several files on my computer. = 病毒已经破坏了我电脑上的几个文件。


过去完成时:
The evidence had been corrupted before the trial. = 证据在审判前已经被篡改了。
They had corrupted the election results before the announcement. = 他们在宣布结果之前已经操纵了选举结果。


将来时:
Absolute power will corrupt him absolutely. = 绝对权力将使他彻底腐败。
The software update may corrupt existing files. = 软件更新可能会破坏现有文件。


虚拟语气:
If he were not corrupted by wealth, he would be a good man. = 如果他不被财富腐蚀，他就会成为一个好人。
I wish the virus hadn't corrupted my important files. = 我真希望病毒没有破坏我的重要文件。


被动语态:
The data was corrupted during the transfer. = 数据在传输过程中被破坏了。
His reputation was corrupted by false accusations. = 他的名誉被不实的指控所败坏。


疑问句:
Has the software corrupted your files? = 软件是否破坏了你的文件？
Did the bribery corrupt the judge's decision? = 行贿是否腐蚀了法官的判决？


否定句:
The new system is designed to prevent data from being corrupted. = 新系统旨在防止数据被破坏。
The evidence did not corrupt the jury's verdict. = 证据并没有影响陪审团的判决。


PHRASE->
corrupt a file = 破坏文件
corrupt a system = 腐蚀系统
corrupt a government = 腐蚀政府
corrupt a witness = 收买证人
corrupt someone's morals = 败坏某人的道德


词源:
The word "corrupts" comes from the Latin word "corruptus," the past participle of "corrumpere," meaning "to break," "to destroy," or "to ruin."  The sense of moral decay developed later.
