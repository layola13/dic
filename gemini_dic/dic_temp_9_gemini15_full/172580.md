pyrometer:/paɪˈrɒmɪtər/| n. |高温计，辐射高温计|An instrument for measuring high temperatures, typically by measuring the intensity of the infrared radiation emitted by a hot object.  Pyrometers are used in various industrial settings to monitor the temperature of furnaces, molten metals, and other high-temperature processes.|例句：The steel mill uses a pyrometer to monitor the temperature of the molten steel.  钢铁厂使用高温计来监测熔融钢的温度。
The scientist used a pyrometer to measure the temperature of the star. 科学家用高温计测量恒星的温度。|近义词：thermometer (for lower temperatures),  thermocouple|反义词：无
