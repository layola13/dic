panged:/pæŋd/ | v. | 刺痛；感到剧痛 |  To feel a sudden, sharp pain, especially of grief or regret.  |例句：My heart panged with guilt after I lied to my friend.  我的心因为对朋友撒谎而感到内疚的剧痛。

例句：A sharp pang of jealousy shot through her when she saw him with another woman. 当她看到他和另一个女人在一起时，一股强烈的嫉妒之痛击中了她。


近义词：ached, throbed, stabbed, smarted | 反义词：soothed, calmed, eased | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My heart pangs with grief. = 我的心因悲伤而阵阵作痛。
This tooth pangs constantly. = 这颗牙一直隐隐作痛。


一般过去时:
My head panged after the accident. = 事故后我的头剧烈疼痛。
He panged with remorse after his outburst. = 他在爆发之后感到深深的悔恨。


现在完成时:
My conscience has panged ever since I broke my promise. = 从我失信以来，我的良心一直不安。
She has panged with regret for years over her hasty decision. = 多年来，她一直为她草率的决定而后悔不已。


过去完成时:
He had panged with anxiety all day before the exam. = 考试前一整天，他都焦虑不安。
She had panged with guilt before confessing the truth. = 在坦白真相之前，她一直感到内疚。


将来时:
I know my heart will pang when I leave home. = 我知道当我离开家时，我的心会感到阵阵作痛。
His conscience will pang if he doesn't tell the truth. = 如果他不说实话，他的良心将会不安。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, my heart would have panged with sorrow. = 如果我知道真相，我的心就会悲痛欲绝。
I wish my conscience hadn't panged so much yesterday. = 我希望昨天我的良心不要那么不安。


被动语态:
His heart was panged by the loss of his loved one. = 他因失去爱人而心痛不已。(很少用被动语态)
His conscience was panged by his actions. = 他的行为让他良心不安。(很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does your head pang often? = 你的头经常痛吗？
Did your heart pang when you heard the news? = 你听到这个消息时心痛吗？


否定句:
My heart didn't pang at all. = 我的心一点也不痛。
His conscience didn't pang even after he lied. = 即使他撒谎了，他的良心也没有不安。
