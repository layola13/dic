reasoner:/ˈriːzənər/| n. |推理者；论证者；思考者|A person who reasons or is skilled in reasoning.  Someone who uses logic and evidence to form judgments and conclusions. |例句：He is a skilled reasoner, able to deduce complex solutions from seemingly disparate facts. 他是一位熟练的推理者，能够从看似不同的事实中推导出复杂的解决方案。|例句：The detective, a keen reasoner, quickly solved the mystery.  这位侦探，一位敏锐的推理者，很快破了案。|近义词：thinker, logician, analyst, philosopher|反义词：ignoramus, simpleton
