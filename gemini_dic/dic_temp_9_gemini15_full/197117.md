shamrock:/ˈʃæm.rɒk/| n. |三叶草；酢浆草| A shamrock is a three-leaf clover, especially the variety traditionally associated with Ireland and St. Patrick.  It is often used as a symbol of Ireland.|The Irish flag features a shamrock.|近义词：three-leaf clover, trefoil|反义词：无
