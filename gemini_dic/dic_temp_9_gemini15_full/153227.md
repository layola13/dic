peltier:/pɛlˈtiər/| n. |珀耳帖 |A Peltier device, also called a thermoelectric cooler or TEC, is a solid-state device that uses electricity to transfer heat from one side to the other. It works on the principle of the Peltier effect, where an electric current passed through a junction of two different conductors causes a temperature difference.|Example: The Peltier cooler kept the drinks cold during the picnic. 珀耳帖冷却器在野餐期间使饮料保持冰凉。|近义词：thermoelectric cooler, TEC |反义词：heater
