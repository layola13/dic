nonprobation:/ˌnɒnproʊˈbeɪʃən/| n. |非缓刑；未被缓刑 |The state of not being on probation.  This term is typically used in legal contexts and refers to the status of someone who has not been placed on probation after a conviction or legal proceeding.  | Example:  After his successful completion of community service, he was granted nonprobation.  他成功完成社区服务后，被判处非缓刑。 | 近义词: non-probationary status, unconditional release | 反义词: probation, parole
