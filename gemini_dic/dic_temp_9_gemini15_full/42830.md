courrier:/kʊrɪeɪ/| n.|信使；快递；信件；字体|A courier is a person or company that delivers messages, packages, or documents.  It can also refer to a type of typeface. |例句：He hired a courier to deliver the important documents. 他雇了一个信使来递送重要的文件。|例句：The courier service is very reliable.  这家快递服务非常可靠。|近义词：messenger, delivery service, express mail|反义词：N/A|名词

词源:
The word "courrier" comes from the Old French word "correier," meaning "runner" or "messenger." This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "currere," meaning "to run."  The connection is clear: couriers were originally people who ran to deliver messages.
