ponzi:/ˈpɒnzi/ /ˈpɑːnzi/| n. |庞氏骗局 |A Ponzi scheme is a fraudulent investing scam promising high rates of return with little risk to investors.  The high returns are paid to earlier investors with funds from more recent investors.  The scheme is unsustainable and eventually collapses when the operator cannot attract enough new investors to pay the promised returns.  It is named after Charles Ponzi, who perpetrated a well-known scheme in the early 20th century.|  The Madoff Ponzi scheme defrauded thousands of investors.  庞氏骗局诈骗了成千上万的投资者。| pyramid scheme, investment fraud | legitimate investment, honest business | 名词
