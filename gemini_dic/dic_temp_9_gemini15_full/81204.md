grandmaster:/ˈɡrændˌmɑːstər/| n. |特级大师；宗师；大师级人物 | A grandmaster is a person who has achieved the highest level of skill in a particular game, especially chess, or a field of expertise.  They are typically recognized for their exceptional abilities and contributions to their field. | Example: Magnus Carlsen is a chess grandmaster.  卡尔森是位国际象棋特级大师。  He's a grandmaster of strategy. 他是一位战略大师。 | 近义词：master, expert, virtuoso, maestro | 反义词：novice, beginner, amateur
