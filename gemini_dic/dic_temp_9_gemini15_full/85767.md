haybote:/ˈheɪboʊt/ | n. |干草权，割草权 |The right to cut and carry away hay from another's land.  This right is typically granted through a lease or agreement. | The farmer had haybote on the lord's land.  这位农民拥有在领主土地上割草的权利。| 近义词：right of common, estovers | 反义词：no right to forage
