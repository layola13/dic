kneels:/niːlz/| v.|跪下；屈服|To go down on one's knees.  It can also imply submission or yielding.|例句：He knelt before the altar. 他在祭坛前跪下。|例句：She knelt down to pray. 她跪下祈祷。|近义词：bend, genuflect|反义词：stand, rise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kneels every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都跪下。
She kneels to pray before bed. = 她睡前跪下祈祷。

一般过去时:
He knelt down in the garden. = 他在花园里跪了下来。
She knelt before the king. = 她跪在国王面前。

现在完成时:
He has knelt before the queen several times. = 他已经几次跪在女王面前了。
She has knelt to clean the floor. = 她已经跪下来擦地板了。

过去完成时:
He had knelt for hours before the interview. = 面试前他已经跪了几个小时了。
She had knelt so long her knees were sore. = 她跪了这么久，膝盖都疼了。

将来时:
He will kneel when the priest enters. = 牧师进来的时候他会跪下。
She will kneel and ask for forgiveness. = 她会跪下请求原谅。

虚拟语气:
I wish I could kneel before my hero. = 我真希望我能跪在我的英雄面前。
If I were a believer, I would kneel and pray. = 如果我是个信徒，我会跪下祈祷。

被动语态:
(Kneel is rarely used in the passive voice because it requires a volitional action.)  A more natural phrasing would be: He was forced to his knees. 他被迫跪下。

疑问句:
Does he kneel during the service? = 他在做礼拜的时候会跪下吗？
Will she kneel before the judges? = 她会在法官面前下跪吗？

否定句:
He did not kneel during the ceremony. = 典礼上他没有下跪。
She would not kneel to anyone. = 她不会向任何人下跪。

PHRASE->
kneel down = 跪下
kneel before = 跪在...面前
fall to one's knees = 突然跪下
bend the knee = 屈服
bring someone to their knees = 使某人屈服
