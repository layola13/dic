plasticises: /ˈplæstɪsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使塑化；使可塑 | To make (a substance, especially a polymer) plastic or more easily molded.  The process involves incorporating a plasticizer, a substance that increases the flexibility and workability of the material. |  The manufacturer plasticises the PVC to make it more flexible for use in clothing. 制造商对PVC进行塑化处理，使其更柔软，以便用于服装。  Heat plasticises the clay, making it easier to shape. 热量使粘土塑化，使其更容易成型。 |  soften,  flexibilize |  harden, solidify |
