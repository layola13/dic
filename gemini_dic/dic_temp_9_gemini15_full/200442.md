slagable: /ˈslæɡəbəl/ | adj. | 可熔化的；可变成渣的 |  Able to be converted into slag; capable of being slagged.  The term often refers to materials in metallurgical processes that can be converted into slag during smelting or refining.  | 例句：The high-temperature furnace is designed to make the ore slagable.  高温炉的设计目的是使矿石可熔化成渣。

The high silica content of this material makes it slagable at lower temperatures.  这种材料的高二氧化硅含量使其可在较低温度下熔化成渣。


近义词：fusible, meltable | 反义词：infusible, unmeltable


词源：源自"slag" (渣) + "-able" (可…的) , indicating the capacity to become slag.  The word reflects the metallurgical context where slag formation is crucial.
