sarcolemma:/ˌsɑːrkəˈlɛmə/| n. |肌膜|The sarcolemma is the cell membrane of a muscle fiber (myocyte). It plays a crucial role in muscle contraction and excitation.  It is selectively permeable and maintains the cell's internal environment. The sarcolemma's structure allows for the transmission of electrical signals that trigger muscle contraction. |The sarcolemma's permeability is essential for the proper functioning of muscle cells. |近义词：myolemma|反义词：无
