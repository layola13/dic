loadsman: /ˈloʊdzmən/ | n. |装卸工，搬运工|A person employed to load and unload ships or other vessels.|The loadsman skillfully maneuvered the heavy crates onto the ship. 装卸工熟练地将沉重的板条箱搬运到船上。|stevedore, longshoreman|  


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loadsman loads the cargo carefully. = 装卸工小心地装载货物。
He loads the containers onto the truck. = 他把集装箱装上卡车。


一般过去时:
The loadsman loaded the ship yesterday. = 装卸工昨天装载了船只。
They loaded all the boxes onto the train. = 他们把所有的箱子都装上了火车。


现在完成时:
The loadsman has loaded all the containers. = 装卸工已经装载了所有集装箱。
They have loaded the ship and are ready to depart. = 他们已经装载了船只，准备出发。


过去完成时:
The loadsman had loaded the goods before the ship sailed. = 船只启航前，装卸工已经装载了货物。
They had loaded everything before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把所有东西都装好了。


将来时:
The loadsman will load the next shipment tomorrow. = 装卸工明天将装载下一批货物。
We will load the truck with furniture this afternoon. = 我们今天下午将用家具装载卡车。
