consequence:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛns/| n. |结果，后果，影响|The consequence of his actions was the loss of his job.  他的行为导致他丢了工作。  The consequences of climate change are already being felt. 气候变化的后果已经显现。|result, outcome, effect|cause, reason|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The consequence of this decision is unclear. = 这个决定的后果尚不清楚。
What are the consequences of ignoring this warning? = 忽视这个警告的后果是什么？


一般过去时:
The consequence of the accident was significant damage. = 事故的后果是严重的损失。
What were the consequences of the storm? = 暴风雨的后果是什么？


现在完成时:
The consequences of his actions have been devastating. = 他行为的后果是毁灭性的。
What consequences have resulted from the policy change? = 政策变化带来了什么后果？


过去完成时:
The consequences of his earlier mistakes had already become apparent. = 他先前错误的后果已经显而易见。
What consequences had occurred before the investigation began? = 在调查开始之前发生了什么后果？


将来时:
The consequences of this decision will be far-reaching. = 这个决定的后果将是深远的。
What consequences will we face if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动，我们将面临什么后果？


虚拟语气:
If he had listened, the consequences would have been less severe. = 如果他听了劝告，后果就不会那么严重了。
I wish the consequences hadn't been so dire. = 我希望后果不至于如此可怕。


被动语态:
The consequences were felt by many. = 许多人都感受到了后果。
The consequences are being assessed carefully. = 后果正在被仔细评估。


疑问句:
What are the consequences? = 后果是什么？
What will be the long-term consequences? = 长期后果是什么？


否定句:
There were no serious consequences. = 没有严重的后果。
The consequences were not as bad as expected. = 后果没有预期的那么糟糕。


PHRASE->
