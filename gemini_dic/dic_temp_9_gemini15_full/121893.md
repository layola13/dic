missample:/ˈmɪsˌsæmpəl/| n. |误采样，错误样本 |A missample is a sample that is not representative of the population from which it was drawn.  This can occur due to various reasons, including biased sampling methods or errors in data collection.  The presence of a missample can lead to inaccurate conclusions and unreliable results in statistical analysis. |Example: The survey results were unreliable due to a missample of the target population.  这个调查结果不可靠，因为目标人群样本抽取有误。|近义词：biased sample, flawed sample, unrepresentative sample |反义词：representative sample, accurate sample |名词
