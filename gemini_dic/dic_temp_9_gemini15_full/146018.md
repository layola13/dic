overdrainage:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnɪdʒ/| n.|过度排水；排水过多|The excessive removal of water from soil or a drainage basin.  This can lead to various environmental problems, such as soil degradation and reduced water availability.|例句：Overdrainage of the wetland has led to the loss of several rare plant species.  过度排水导致几种珍稀植物物种的消失。|例句：The prolonged period of overdrainage severely impacted the agricultural yields. 长期过度排水严重影响了农业产量。|近义词：overwatering, excessive drainage|反义词：waterlogging, underdrainage|名词
