orientalize:/ˌɔːriənˈtælaɪz/ | vt. |使具有东方特色；以东方人的眼光看待；歪曲东方文化|To represent or depict (someone or something) in a stereotyped way that conforms to Western notions of the Orient.  This often involves exoticizing, romanticizing, or demonizing aspects of Eastern cultures. |例句：The novel orientalizes the culture of Japan, portraying it as exotic and mysterious. 这部小说歪曲了日本文化，将其描绘成奇异而神秘的。  The film orientalizes its characters, reducing them to one-dimensional stereotypes. 这部电影以东方人的眼光看待其角色，将其简化为单一维度的刻板印象。|近义词：exoticize, romanticize, exotify |反义词：represent accurately, portray authentically |动词
