shearwaters: /ˈʃɛərˌwɔːtərz/ | n. | 海燕 | A seabird of the family Procellariidae, characterized by long wings and a gliding flight.  Shearwaters are typically found in the open ocean and are known for their long migrations. | Example:  Many species of shearwaters breed on remote islands.  许多种类的海燕在偏远岛屿上繁殖。


The shearwaters were sighted far out at sea.  在远海发现了海燕。
