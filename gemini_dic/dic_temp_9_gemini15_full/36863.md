color-blind:/ˈkʌlərblaɪnd/ | adj. |色盲的，辨色能力差的 |  Unable to distinguish some or all colors.  This condition can range from mild difficulty distinguishing shades to complete inability to see color. | He is color-blind and can't tell the difference between red and green. 他是色盲，分不清红色和绿色。| color deficient, colour deficient |  (not applicable; it's a condition, not an action or state with a direct opposite)

He is color-blind. = 他是色盲。
