ginkgo:/ˈɡɪŋkoʊ/| n.|银杏，白果|Ginkgo is a genus of highly unusual non-flowering plants, commonly known as ginkgo or ginkgo trees. They are unique in their evolutionary history and possess distinctive fan-shaped leaves.  |The ginkgo tree is a living fossil, having survived for millions of years.|近义词:maidenhair tree|反义词:  (无直接反义词)|名词
