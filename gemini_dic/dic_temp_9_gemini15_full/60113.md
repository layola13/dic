efface:/ɪˈfeɪs/ | vt. |抹去；擦掉；使消失；使黯然失色 | to remove or obliterate something, especially a mark, writing, or inscription; to make (something) indistinct or imperceptible | The inscription on the tombstone had been effaced by time. 墓碑上的铭文已被时间磨损。  The scandal threatened to efface his reputation. 这场丑闻有可能毁掉他的名声。 | erase, obliterate, remove | enhance, emphasize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He effaces his mistakes. = 他抹去他的错误。
The rain effaces the footprints. = 雨水冲刷掉了脚印。

一般过去时:
She effaced the drawing. = 她擦掉了图画。
They effaced the evidence. = 他们销毁了证据。

现在完成时:
Time has effaced many details of the past. = 时间已经抹去了许多过去的细节。
The years have effaced the inscription on the monument. = 岁月已经磨去了纪念碑上的铭文。

过去完成时:
The rain had effaced the writing on the paper. = 雨水已经把纸上的字迹冲掉了。
They had effaced all traces of their activities. = 他们已经抹去了他们所有活动的痕迹。

将来时:
The coming storm will efface the tracks. = 即将来临的风暴将会抹去痕迹。
They will efface the graffiti from the wall. = 他们将清除墙上的涂鸦。

虚拟语气:
If I had a magic eraser, I would efface all my worries. = 如果我有一块神奇的橡皮擦，我会抹去我所有的烦恼。
I wish I could efface the memory of that painful experience. = 我真希望我能忘记那段痛苦的经历。

被动语态:
The inscription was effaced by the rain. = 铭文被雨水冲刷掉了。
The old photographs were slowly effaced by time. = 旧照片慢慢地被时间磨损了。

疑问句:
Will the cleaning solution efface this stain? = 清洁剂能清除这个污渍吗？
Can the past be effaced completely? = 过去能完全被抹去吗？

否定句:
The artist refused to efface his work. = 艺术家拒绝擦掉他的作品。
You cannot efface the facts. = 你无法抹杀事实。
