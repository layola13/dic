dongle:/ˈdɒŋɡəl/| n. |小型适配器；(USB)加密狗|A small device that plugs into a computer or other device to add functionality, often used for connecting peripherals or providing security.  It is typically small and easily portable.  The term often refers to USB dongles, but can apply to other connection types.|例句：I need a USB dongle to connect my printer to the laptop. 我需要一个USB加密狗来连接我的打印机到笔记本电脑。

例句：He uses a security dongle to protect his sensitive data. 他使用安全加密狗来保护他的敏感数据。


近义词：adapter, connector, key
