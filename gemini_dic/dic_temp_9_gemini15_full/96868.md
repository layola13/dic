inexhaustible: /ɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbl/ | adj. | 无穷无尽的，用之不竭的 |  unending; incapable of being used up or exhausted.  |  The ocean's resources seemed inexhaustible to early explorers.  早期探险家认为海洋资源取之不尽，用之不竭。|  boundless, limitless, infinite, unending | exhaustible, finite, limited
