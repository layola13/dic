puzzolan:/pʌzəlˈɑːn/| n. |火山灰土，火山灰质土|A siliceous or siliceous-aluminous volcanic material that, when finely divided, forms a cementing material in the presence of water.  It is used in concrete and mortar.  Puzzolana is a naturally occurring material, but similar materials can be produced artificially.|Example: The Romans used puzzolana extensively in their construction projects.|近义词：pozzolana|反义词：无
