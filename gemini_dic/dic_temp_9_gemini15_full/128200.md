neurilemma:/ˌnʊəriˈlɛmə/| n. |神经鞘膜|A thin membranous sheath enveloping a nerve fiber, especially a myelinated axon; it is formed by Schwann cells.  It plays a vital role in nerve regeneration.|Example: Damage to the neurilemma can impair nerve function and regeneration. 神经鞘膜的损伤会损害神经功能和再生。|近义词：Schwann sheath|反义词：无
