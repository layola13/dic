outrowing:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɪŋ/ | v. | 超越划船；胜过划船 |  The act of rowing faster or further than someone else.  It implies a competition or race in rowing. |例句： The team was outrowing their rivals by a significant margin.  这支队伍比他们的对手快得多。

例句： He was outrowing everyone else in the race. 他在比赛中划船速度超过了所有人。


近义词：outsailing, outpacing, surpassing, exceeding


反义词：being outrowed, lagging behind



USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outrows his opponent every time they race. 他每次和对手比赛都划得比他快。
They outrow the other team consistently.  他们总是比其他队划得快。


一般过去时:
She outrowed her competitor in the final sprint.  她在最后的冲刺中超过了她的竞争对手。
The experienced rower outrowed the newcomer easily.  经验丰富的划手轻松地超过了新手。


现在完成时:
Our team has outrowed all the other teams this season. 我们队本赛季超过了所有其他队。
She has outrowed her personal best time. 她已经超过了她个人的最佳成绩。
