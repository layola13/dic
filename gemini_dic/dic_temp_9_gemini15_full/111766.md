lodgerdom:/ˈlɒdʒədəm/ | n. |寄宿者群体；寄宿生活 |The state or condition of being a lodger; the collective body of lodgers.  It often implies a sense of community or shared experience among lodgers, or the atmosphere of a place where many lodgers reside. |Example: The lodgerdom of the boarding house was a lively and often noisy affair.  寄宿公寓里的寄宿者们生活热闹而喧嚣。 The peculiar lodgerdom of that old Victorian house held many secrets. 那座古老的维多利亚式房屋里独特的寄宿生活隐藏着许多秘密。|近义词：boarding-house life, tenant community |反义词：owner-occupied housing, single-family dwelling
