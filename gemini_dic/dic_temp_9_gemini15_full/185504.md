reusing:/ˌriːˈjuːzɪŋ/| v. |再利用；重复使用 |The act of using something again. This verb describes the action of employing something that has already been used once or more.  It emphasizes the repeated use of an item or resource. |例句：We are reusing plastic bottles to make planters. 我们正在重复使用塑料瓶来制作花盆。

例句：The company is committed to reusing water in its manufacturing process. 这家公司致力于在其制造过程中重复利用水。


近义词：recycling, repurposing, re-employing |反义词：discarding, disposing of, wasting

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reuse plastic bags. = 他们重复使用塑料袋。
We reuse water in our garden. = 我们在花园里重复利用水。


一般过去时:
She reused the shopping bags. = 她重复使用了购物袋。
He reused the containers for storage. = 他重复使用了这些容器来储物。


现在完成时:
We have reused many items this year. = 今年我们已经重复使用了许多物品。
They have reused the old wood for the fence. = 他们已经重复使用了旧木头来做篱笆。


过去完成时:
They had reused the materials before the project started. = 项目开始前他们已经重复使用了这些材料。
She had reused the glass jars for canning. = 她之前已经重复使用了玻璃罐来装罐头。


将来时:
We will reuse the paper for notes. = 我们将把纸张重复用于做笔记。
They will reuse the boxes for shipping. = 他们将重复使用这些箱子进行运输。


虚拟语气:
If we reused more, we would reduce waste. = 如果我们更多地重复使用，我们将减少浪费。
I wish we reused our water bottles more often. = 我希望我们更频繁地重复使用我们的水瓶。


被动语态:
The containers are being reused for storage. = 这些容器正在被重复用于储物。
The plastic is being reused to make new products. = 塑料正在被重复利用来制造新产品。


疑问句:
Are they reusing the old furniture? = 他们正在重复使用旧家具吗？
Do you reuse your shopping bags? = 你重复使用你的购物袋吗？


否定句:

They didn't reuse the packaging. = 他们没有重复使用包装。
