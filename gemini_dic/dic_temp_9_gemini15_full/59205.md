dysphorias:/dɪsˈfɔːriəz/ | n. |焦虑；烦躁；苦闷；（尤指）痛经 | A feeling of unease, anxiety, or dissatisfaction;  a state of dissatisfaction or unease.  The term is often used in the context of gender dysphoria. | 例句：She experienced intense dysphorias after the surgery. 她手术后经历了强烈的焦虑和烦躁。 | 例句：The patient's dysphorias were treated with medication and therapy. 病人的焦虑和烦躁通过药物和治疗得到缓解。|近义词：distress, anxiety, unease, malaise, discomfort |反义词：euphoria, contentment, satisfaction | 名词
