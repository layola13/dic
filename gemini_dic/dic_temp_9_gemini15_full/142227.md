ornithotomist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒtəmɪst/| n. |鸟类解剖学家|A person who dissects birds for scientific study.  Ornithotomists are specialists in avian anatomy, often contributing to our understanding of bird evolution, physiology, and behavior through detailed examination of their internal structures.|The ornithotomist meticulously examined the bird's skeletal structure.| zoologist, anatomist|  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "ornithologist" if focusing on the broader study of birds rather than dissection)|名词
