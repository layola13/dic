indestructibly:/ˌɪnˈdɛstrʌktəbli/| adv.|不可摧毁地，坚不可摧地|incapably of being destroyed or damaged;  permanently.|例句：The fortress was built indestructibly, withstanding countless sieges.  这座堡垒建造得坚不可摧，经受住了无数次围攻。
The diamond is indestructibly hard. 这颗钻石坚硬无比。|近义词：irresistibly, unbreakably, unyieldingly|反义词：destructibly, breakably, vulnerably
