percolated:/ˈpɜːrkəleɪtɪd/ | v. |渗透；过滤；逐渐传播 | To filter (a liquid) through a porous substance; to spread gradually | The aroma of coffee percolated through the air. 咖啡的香味弥漫在空气中。|The news slowly percolated through the organization.  消息慢慢地在组织内部传播开来。 | diffused, spread, seeped | concentrated, stopped |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The coffee percolates slowly. = 咖啡慢慢地渗透。
The rumor percolates through the town. = 谣言在镇上慢慢传播。


一般过去时:
The water percolated through the soil. = 水渗透到土壤中。
The idea percolated through his mind. = 这个想法在他的脑海中逐渐形成。


现在完成时:
The news has percolated to the public. = 消息已经传到了公众那里。
The influence of the event has percolated throughout society. = 此事件的影响已渗透到整个社会。


过去完成时:
The information had percolated to the higher ups before the meeting. = 在会议之前，信息已经传到了高层。
The feeling of anxiety had percolated through the team before the presentation. = 在演示之前，焦虑的情绪已经蔓延到整个团队。


将来时:
The scent will percolate through the room soon. = 香味很快就会弥漫整个房间。
The information will percolate to the media eventually. = 信息最终会传到媒体。


虚拟语气:
If the liquid percolated more quickly, the process would be faster. = 如果液体渗透得更快，过程就会更快。
If the news had percolated faster, we could have reacted sooner. = 如果消息传播得更快，我们就能更快地做出反应。


被动语态:
The coffee was percolated slowly. = 咖啡慢慢地被过滤。
The rumor was percolated throughout the city. = 谣言在整个城市传播开来。


疑问句:
Did the liquid percolate through the filter? = 液体是否通过过滤器渗透？
Has the news percolated to all the employees? = 消息是否已经传达给了所有员工？


否定句:
The water did not percolate through the hard ground. = 水没有渗透到坚硬的地面。
The message did not percolate effectively to the intended audience. = 信息没有有效地传达给目标受众。


PHRASE->
percolate through = 渗透，传播
percolate down = 逐渐向下渗透
slowly percolate = 慢慢渗透
percolate into = 渗入
percolate out = 渗出
