scal:/skæl/| n. v.|鳞；鳞片；刻度；（鱼等的）鳞；攀登；（用工具）刮削；|a thin, flat plate, like a fish scale;  a small division or unit on a measuring instrument; to climb or move quickly; to scrape or remove with a tool.|The fish has shiny silver scales. 这条鱼有闪亮的银色鳞片。|He scaled the cliff with ease. 他轻松地攀登了悬崖。|近义词：scale, flake, climb|反义词：smooth
