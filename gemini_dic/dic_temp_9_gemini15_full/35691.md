cobranded:/ˈkoʊˌbrændɪd/| adj.|联合品牌的；合作品牌的|Combining the brands of two or more companies on a single product or service to leverage the strengths of each brand.  This strategy aims to increase brand awareness, reach a wider audience, and boost sales for all participating brands.|例句：The new credit card is a cobraneded product of Visa and a major retailer. 这张新的信用卡是Visa和一家大型零售商联合推出的产品。|例句：This coffee shop offers cobraneded merchandise featuring both its own logo and that of a popular local artist. 这家咖啡店提供联合品牌的商品，上面印有它自己的标志和一位受欢迎的当地艺术家的标志。|近义词：joint-branded, partnered, collaborative|反义词：independent, standalone
