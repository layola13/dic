prefinancing: /ˌpriːˈfaɪnænsɪŋ/ | n. | 预先融资，前期融资 | The act of providing funding before a project or venture begins.  It often involves securing capital to cover initial expenses before revenue generation starts.  | Example: The startup secured prefinancing from angel investors to develop its prototype.  = 这家初创公司从天使投资者那里获得了预先融资来开发其原型。| 近义词：advance funding, upfront financing, seed funding | 反义词：post-financing
