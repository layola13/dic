packaged:/ˈpækɪdʒd/ | adj. | 包装好的；成套的 |  Something that has been packaged is prepared and enclosed for sale or distribution. It often implies a complete and convenient unit.  | Example:  The packaged software is easy to install.  这个软件包易于安装。 |近义词：wrapped, bundled, prepared |反义词：loose, unwrapped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company packages its products carefully. = 公司仔细包装其产品。
This software is packaged with all the necessary drivers. = 这个软件包含所有必要的驱动程序。


一般过去时:
They packaged the goods for shipment yesterday. = 他们昨天打包货物准备发货。
The gifts were beautifully packaged. = 这些礼物包装精美。


现在完成时:
We have packaged and shipped all the orders. = 我们已经包装并发货了所有订单。
The company has packaged a new marketing strategy. = 公司已经制定了一个新的营销策略（比喻用法）。


过去完成时:
They had packaged the presents before the party started. = 他们早在派对开始前就已包装好礼物。
The team had packaged the proposal before submitting it. = 团队在提交提案前已完成提案的整理和包装（比喻用法）。


将来时:
We will package the products tomorrow. = 我们明天将打包产品。
The new software will be packaged with improved features. = 新软件将包含改进的功能（被动语态）。


虚拟语气:
If we packaged the items more attractively, we would sell more. = 如果我们把商品包装得更吸引人，就能卖出更多。
I wish they had packaged the food better; it was all squashed. = 我希望他们把食物包装得更好些；现在都压扁了。


被动语态:
The goods are packaged in sturdy boxes. = 货物被装在结实的箱子里。
The software was packaged with a user-friendly interface. = 该软件配备了用户友好的界面。


疑问句:
Are the products packaged individually? = 产品是单独包装的吗？
How are the gifts packaged? = 礼物是怎么包装的？


否定句:
The items were not packaged properly. = 这些物品包装不当。
The software isn't packaged with the necessary documentation. = 该软件没有附带必要的文档。
