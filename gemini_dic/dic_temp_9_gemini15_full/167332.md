pretransmitting: /ˌpriːtrænˈzmɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先传送的；预先传输的 |  Describing the action of transmitting something before the main transmission or event.  It implies a preparatory or preliminary transmission.  | Example:  The pretransmitting signal ensured the receiver was ready for the main data stream. 预先传送的信号确保接收器已准备好接收主要数据流。 | 近义词:  preliminary transmitting, advance transmitting | 反义词: post-transmitting
