collective:/kəˈlɛktɪv/| adj., n. |集合的；集体的；集体；团体|

英文解析:relating to or done by a group of people acting together.  A collective noun refers to a group of individuals considered as a single unit.

例句:

The collective strength of the team allowed them to win the championship.  团队的集体力量使他们赢得了冠军。

The farmers formed a collective to negotiate better prices for their crops.  农民们组成了一个集体来协商更好的农作物价格。


近义词:group, combined, joint, united, aggregate

反义词:individual, separate, single, disparate


词性: adjective, noun
