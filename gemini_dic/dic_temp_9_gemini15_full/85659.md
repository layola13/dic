haut: /o/ | adj. | 高的；高级的；傲慢的 | High; lofty; arrogant.  | The haut cuisine of the restaurant was exquisite. 这家餐厅的高级菜肴非常精致。  | high, lofty, superior | low, humble, modest |  形容词


例句：

The haut society gathered for the charity ball. 高级社会人士聚集参加慈善舞会。

He spoke with a haut manner, looking down on everyone else. 他说话很傲慢，看不起其他人。


词源：

From Old French *haut, from Frankish *hauhiz, from Proto-Germanic *hauhō, from Proto-Indo-European *kau- "high".  The word is related to English "high".


近义词：

elevated, exalted, superior, distinguished


反义词：

low, humble, lowly, inferior
