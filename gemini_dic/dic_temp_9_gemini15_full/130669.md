nonadvocate:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkət/ | n. |非拥护者，反对者 | A person who does not advocate or support a particular cause, policy, or person.  Someone who remains neutral or actively opposes a certain idea or action. | Example:  He remained a nonadvocate throughout the entire debate. 他在整个辩论中都保持中立。  The nonadvocate presented a counterargument.  这位反对者提出了反驳意见。 |近义词：opponent, critic, doubter | 反义词：advocate, proponent, supporter |
