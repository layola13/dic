excipient:/ɪkˈsɪpiənt/| n. |赋形剂，辅料|An inactive substance used as a carrier for active ingredients in a medicine, food, or cosmetic.|The tablet contained a variety of excipients to help it bind together and break down in the stomach.|近义词：additive, vehicle, carrier|反义词：active ingredient
