modder:/ˈmɒdər/| n.|修改者，改装者|A person who modifies something, typically a video game, software, or hardware.|例句：He's a skilled modder, known for his creative game modifications. 他是一位熟练的修改者，以其富有创意的游戏修改而闻名。|近义词：modifier, tinkerer, customizer|反义词：originalist
