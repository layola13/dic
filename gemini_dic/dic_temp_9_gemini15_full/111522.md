loathed:/loʊðd/| v.|憎恨，厌恶 | to feel intense dislike or disgust for  |例句：I loathed the way he spoke to me. 我讨厌他对我说话的方式。|例句：She loathed the smell of fish. 她讨厌鱼腥味。|近义词：abhor, detest, despise, hate |反义词：love, adore, cherish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I loathe spinach. = 我讨厌菠菜。
He loathes public speaking. = 他讨厌公开演讲。

一般过去时:
I loathed that movie. = 我讨厌那部电影。
She loathed her job. = 她讨厌她的工作。

现在完成时:
I have loathed him since childhood. = 我从小就讨厌他。
She has loathed the taste of olives her whole life. = 她一辈子都讨厌橄榄的味道。

过去完成时:
I had loathed the idea for years. = 我已经讨厌这个主意很多年了。
We had loathed the constant noise before we moved. = 在我们搬家之前，我们一直讨厌持续不断的噪音。


将来时:
I will loathe this assignment until it's finished. = 在这个任务完成之前，我都会讨厌它。
He will loathe the consequences of his actions. = 他将讨厌他行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
I would loathe to be in his shoes. = 我不愿意处于他的境地。
If I knew he was like that, I would have loathed him sooner. = 如果我知道他那样，我会更早讨厌他。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His behavior was loathed by everyone. = 他的行为遭到所有人的厌恶。(较为正式，不常用)
The idea was loathed by the committee. = 这个想法遭到委员会的厌恶。(较为正式，不常用)


疑问句:
Do you loathe broccoli? = 你讨厌西兰花吗？
Does he loathe the sound of nails on a chalkboard? = 他讨厌指甲刮黑板的声音吗？


否定句:
I don't loathe all vegetables. = 我并不讨厌所有的蔬菜。
He doesn't loathe classical music anymore. = 他不再讨厌古典音乐了。


PHRASE->
loathe to do something = 不情愿做某事
loathe the thought of = 讨厌想到…
loathe the very idea of = 非常讨厌…的想法
couldn't loathe someone more = 再讨厌不过某人了
loathe and despise = 憎恨和鄙视
