marge:/mɑːrdʒ/| n. |玛姬（女子名）；边缘；利润；（美）污水处理厂|The word "marge" primarily functions as a proper noun, a female given name.  It can also be a shortened form of "margin," referring to an edge, border, or profit. In American English, it can also refer to a wastewater treatment plant.|例句：My friend's name is Marge.我的朋友的名字是玛姬。The profit margin is too low.利润率太低了。|近义词: (as a name) Margaret, Maggie; (as margin) edge, border, profit, brink |反义词: (as margin) center, interior; (as profit) loss
