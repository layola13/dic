inputer: /ɪnˈpʊtər/ | n. | 输入者，输入设备 | A person or device that inputs data.  | Example: The inputer entered the data quickly and accurately.  输入者快速而准确地输入了数据。 |近义词: data entry operator, feeder | 反义词: outputter


USAGE->

The inputer enters data into the system. = 输入者将数据输入系统。
He inputs the information daily. = 他每天输入信息。

The inputer entered the data yesterday. = 输入者昨天输入了数据。
She inputted the information last week. = 她上周输入了信息。
