self-troubled: /ˌselfˈtrʌbld/ | adj. |  苦恼的；自寻烦恼的 |  Troubled or distressed by one's own thoughts, actions, or feelings; characterized by internal conflict or anxiety.  | 例句：He was a self-troubled man, constantly worrying about things he couldn't control. 他是一个自寻烦恼的人，总是为无法控制的事情担忧。  |  近义词：worried, anxious, tormented, distraught, agitated | 反义词：calm, peaceful, serene, composed, tranquil |  词性:形容词
