joining:/ˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/| v. n. |加入；连接；结合 |The act of joining or the state of being joined;  the process of uniting or connecting two or more things. |例句：He's joining the army next month. 他下个月要参军。
The joining of the two companies created a powerful new entity. 两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。|近义词： uniting, connecting, merging, combining |反义词： separating, disconnecting, parting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is joining the club. = 他正在加入俱乐部。
They are joining forces to fight the disease. = 他们正联手对抗这种疾病。


一般过去时:
She joined the choir last year. = 她去年加入了合唱团。
We joined the party late. = 我们晚些时候才参加了聚会。


现在完成时:
I have joined the gym and started working out. = 我已经加入了健身房并开始锻炼了。
They have joined the project recently. = 他们最近加入了这个项目。


过去完成时:
He had joined the company before the merger. = 在合并之前他已经加入了公司。
She had joined the team before the competition started. = 比赛开始前她就加入了队伍。


将来时:
I will be joining you for dinner tomorrow. = 明天我将和你一起共进晚餐。
They will join the protest march. = 他们将参加抗议游行。


虚拟语气:
If he joined the team, we would have a better chance of winning. = 如果他加入球队，我们获胜的机会就更大。
I wish I had joined the trip. = 我真希望我参加了那次旅行。


被动语态:
The two pipes were joined together. = 这两根管道连接在一起。
The two groups will be joined later. = 这两个小组稍后将合并。


疑问句:
Are you joining us for lunch? = 你要和我们一起吃午饭吗？
Will he be joining the meeting? = 他会参加会议吗？


否定句:
He didn't join the meeting. = 他没有参加会议。
She won't be joining us tonight. = 她今晚不会和我们一起。
