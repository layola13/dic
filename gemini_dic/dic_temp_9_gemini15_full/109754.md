levied:/ˈliːvaɪd/| vt.|征收（税款等）；课以（罚款等）|to impose or collect (a tax, fee, fine, etc.)|The government levied a new tax on gasoline. 政府对汽油征收新税。|The judge levied a fine against the driver. 法官对司机处以罚款。|impose, assess, exact|refund, remit


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government levies taxes on income. = 政府对收入征税。
They levy a fee for parking. = 他们对停车收取费用。


一般过去时:
The king levied heavy taxes on his subjects. = 国王向他的臣民征收重税。
The court levied a fine on the company. = 法院对该公司处以罚款。


现在完成时:
They have levied a new tax on luxury goods. = 他们对奢侈品征收了一种新税。
The council has levied fines on several businesses. = 理事会已经对几家企业处以罚款。


过去完成时:
The state had levied taxes before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，国家已经征收了税款。
The judge had already levied a fine before the hearing concluded. = 在听证会结束之前，法官已经处以罚款。


将来时:
The government will levy new taxes next year. = 政府明年将征收新的税款。
They will levy a charge for late payments. = 他们将对逾期付款收取费用。


虚拟语气:
If they levied a higher tax, they could fund the project. = 如果他们征收更高的税，他们就能为该项目提供资金。
If the court had levied a harsher penalty, the crime rate might have decreased. = 如果法院判处更严厉的惩罚，犯罪率可能会下降。


被动语态:
A new tax was levied on cigarettes. = 对香烟征收了新税。
Fines were levied against several drivers. = 对几名司机处以罚款。


疑问句:
Will a new tax be levied on imports? = 将对进口商品征收新税吗？
Were fines levied against the protesters? = 对抗议者处以罚款了吗？


否定句:
The government did not levy any new taxes this year. = 政府今年没有征收任何新税。
No fines were levied against the participants. = 没有对参与者处以罚款。
