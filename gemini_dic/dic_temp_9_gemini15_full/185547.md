revaporize:/ˌriˈveɪpəraɪz/| vt.|再汽化；重新汽化|To vaporize something again; to convert something from a liquid or solid state back into a gaseous state a second time.|例句：The scientist revaporized the liquid nitrogen to conduct further experiments.  科学家再次汽化液氮以进行进一步的实验。

例句：After the initial vaporization, the substance was cooled and then revaporized under controlled conditions.  在初始汽化后，物质被冷却，然后在受控条件下再次汽化。


近义词：re-vaporize, re-gasify|反义词：condense, solidify|动词
