clefted:/ˈklɛftɪd/| adj.|分裂的；劈开的；有裂缝的|Having a split or cleft; divided into two or more parts.|The clefted rock face was a dramatic sight.  那块裂开的岩石面是一幅壮观的景象。 The clefted lip needed surgery. 唇裂需要手术。|近义词：split, fissured, cracked|反义词：whole, unbroken, intact
