connexion:/kəˈnekʃən/| n.|连接，联系，关系|A connexion is a relationship or link between things.  It can refer to a physical connection, like a wire connecting two devices, or a more abstract connection, like the connection between two ideas. |例句：The connexion between the two events is unclear.  这两个事件之间的联系尚不清楚。

例句：There is a strong connexion between diet and health.  饮食和健康之间有着密切的联系。


近义词：link, connection, relationship, association|反义词：disconnection, separation, isolation|名词
