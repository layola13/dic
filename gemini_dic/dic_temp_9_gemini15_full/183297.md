reprivatizing: /ˌriːpraɪˈveɪtʌɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再私有化；重新私有化 | The act of transferring ownership or control of something from public or state control back to private individuals or entities.  This often involves the selling off of state-owned assets or the deregulation of industries previously under government control. | Example: The government is considering reprivatizing the national railway system.  政府正在考虑将国家铁路系统重新私有化。 | 近义词：privatizing again, re-privatizing | 反义词：nationalizing, socializing
