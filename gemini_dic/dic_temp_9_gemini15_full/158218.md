platypoda:/ˈplætɪpɒdə/ | n. | 广足类动物；鸭嘴兽科 |  The word "platypoda" refers to an order of semi-aquatic, egg-laying mammals, characterized by their broad feet.  The order primarily contains the single extant family Ornithorhynchidae, encompassing the echidnas and  duck-billed platypus. | Example: The study focused on the unique adaptations of platypoda to their semi-aquatic environment.  这项研究的重点是广足类动物对其半水生环境的独特适应性。| 近义词:Monotremata (单孔目, a broader taxonomic classification)| 反义词:  None (no direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic group)| n.
