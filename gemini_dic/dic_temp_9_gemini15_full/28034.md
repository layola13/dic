capacity:/kəˈpæsɪti/ | n. | 容量；能力；生产力 | The ability to contain or accommodate; the power or ability to do, experience, or understand something.  | The stadium has a seating capacity of 50,000.  这个体育场的座位容量为 5 万。| The child showed remarkable capacity for learning languages. 这个孩子展现了惊人的语言学习能力。 |  volume, potential, ability | inability, incompetence


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bottle has a capacity of one liter. = 这个瓶子的容量是一升。
This engine has a high fuel capacity. = 这台引擎的燃油容量很高。

一般过去时:
The old factory had a limited production capacity. = 老工厂的生产能力有限。
The theater had a seating capacity of only 200. = 剧院的座位容量只有 200 个。

现在完成时:
The company has increased its production capacity significantly. = 公司已经显著提高了其生产能力。
We have reached the maximum capacity of the server. = 我们已经达到了服务器的最大容量。

过去完成时:
They had already exceeded the storage capacity before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前就已经超过了存储容量。
The reservoir had lost much of its water capacity due to the drought. = 由于干旱，水库已经失去了大部分的蓄水能力。


将来时:
The new plant will have a much larger production capacity. = 新工厂将拥有更大的生产能力。
We will need to upgrade the server to increase its capacity. = 我们需要升级服务器以提高其容量。


虚拟语气:
If the tank had a greater capacity, we could store more water. = 如果水箱容量更大，我们可以储存更多水。
I wish my computer had a larger memory capacity. = 我希望我的电脑有更大的内存容量。


被动语态:
The building's capacity was exceeded during the concert. = 音乐会期间，建筑物的容量被超过了。
The server's capacity is being tested right now. = 服务器的容量正在测试中。


疑问句:
What is the capacity of this hard drive? = 这个硬盘的容量是多少？
What is the seating capacity of the auditorium? = 礼堂的座位容量是多少？


否定句:
The container doesn't have the capacity to hold all the items. = 这个容器没有足够的容量容纳所有物品。
The network lacked the capacity to handle the increased traffic. = 网络缺乏处理增加的流量的容量。


PHRASE->

full capacity:  满负荷；最大容量

intellectual capacity: 智力
storage capacity: 存储容量
seating capacity: 座位容量
