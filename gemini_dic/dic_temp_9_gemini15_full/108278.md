laundromat:/ˈlɔːndrəˌmæt/| n. |自助洗衣店|A laundromat is a self-service laundry facility where people can wash and dry their clothes using coin-operated or card-operated machines.  It typically provides washing machines, dryers, and sometimes vending machines for detergents and other laundry supplies.  |例句：I dropped my clothes off at the laundromat this morning.  今天早上我把衣服送到自助洗衣店了。|例句：The laundromat was busy, with all the machines in use.  自助洗衣店很忙，所有的机器都在使用。|近义词：self-service laundry, wash house|反义词：无
