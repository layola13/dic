feign:/feɪn/| vt.|假装，伪装，模拟|To pretend to feel or have something; to imitate deceptively.|He feigned illness to avoid work. 他假装生病以逃避工作。|She feigned surprise at the news. 她假装对这个消息感到惊讶。|simulate, affect, pretend|be genuine, be real|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feigns ignorance. = 他假装不知情。
They feign interest in the lecture. = 他们假装对讲座感兴趣。

一般过去时:
She feigned sleep. = 她假装睡觉。
He feigned surprise when he received the gift. = 收到礼物时，他假装很惊讶。

现在完成时:
He has feigned illness several times this month. = 这个月他已经好几次假装生病了。
She has feigned innocence throughout the investigation. = 在整个调查过程中，她一直假装无辜。

过去完成时:
They had feigned agreement before the meeting. = 会议前他们曾假装同意。
He had feigned ignorance to avoid answering the question. = 他假装不知道以避免回答这个问题。

将来时:
She will feign illness to avoid the meeting. = 她将假装生病以避免参加会议。
He will feign indifference to her advances. = 他将假装对她示好无动于衷。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could feign ignorance about the matter. = 我真希望我能对这件事假装不知情。

被动语态:

Agreement was feigned by the participants. = 与会者假装达成了一致。(较少用被动语态)

疑问句:
Did he feign illness? = 他是假装生病吗？
Is she feigning innocence? = 她是在假装无辜吗？

否定句:
He did not feign interest in the conversation. = 他对谈话并不假装感兴趣。
She did not feign surprise at the announcement. = 她对这个公告并没有假装惊讶。
