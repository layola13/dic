nondeterminists:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪsts/ | n. |非决定论者 |Those who believe that events are not causally determined, but have an element of randomness or chance.  They reject the idea that all events are predetermined.  | Example:  Many physicists, particularly in quantum mechanics, are nondeterminists, arguing that some events are inherently probabilistic.  许多物理学家，尤其是在量子力学领域，都是非决定论者，他们认为某些事件本质上是概率性的。| 近义词：indeterminists | 反义词：determinists | 名词
