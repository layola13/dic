presetting:/ˈpriːsɛtɪŋ/ | n. |预设，预设值 | A presetting is a setting that is already in place before something begins; a default setting.  It refers to a configuration or parameter established in advance.  | Example: The camera's presetting for landscape photography provided excellent results.  相机的风景摄影预设提供了极佳的效果。

The factory's presetting for the machine ensured consistent production. 工厂机器的预设保证了生产的一致性。|近义词：default setting,  initial setting, configuration |反义词：custom setting, manual setting |名词
