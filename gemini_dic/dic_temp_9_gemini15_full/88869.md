hoister:/ˈhɔɪstər/| vt. n.|起重机；提升；举起|To lift or raise something, especially using a machine.|例句：They used a hoist to lift the heavy equipment.他们用起重机吊起了重型设备。

例句：The workers hoisted the flag to the top of the pole. 工人们把旗子升到旗杆顶端。

近义词：raise, lift, elevate|反义词：lower, drop


词源:  From Middle English *hoisten, from Old Norse *heysta "to hoist," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *heita "to call, name," from Proto-Germanic *haitjan.  The meaning evolved from the act of "hauling" or "pulling" to the specific sense of lifting with mechanical aid.
