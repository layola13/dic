jalousie:/ˈʒæluːzi/| n.|百叶窗；嫉妒；猜忌|A jalousie is a window blind made of horizontal slats that can be adjusted to control the amount of light and air entering a room.  The word also refers to feelings of jealousy or suspicion. |例句：The old house had beautiful wooden jalousies on every window. 这栋老房子每个窗户上都有漂亮的木百叶窗。

The intense jalousie between the sisters was evident to everyone. 姐妹俩之间的强烈嫉妒是显而易见的。|近义词：blind (for the window covering), envy, suspicion|反义词：openness (lack of jealousy), trust
