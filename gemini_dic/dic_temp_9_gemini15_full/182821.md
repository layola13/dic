repeatability:/ˌriːpiːtəˈbɪləti/| n. |可重复性，可复制性 |The ability of a process or experiment to produce the same results when repeated under the same conditions.  It is a crucial aspect of scientific research and industrial processes, ensuring reliability and consistency. |例句：The high repeatability of the experiment confirmed the validity of the findings.  该实验的高重复性证实了研究结果的有效性。


The experiment's low repeatability raised concerns about its methodology.  该实验的可重复性低，引发了对其方法论的担忧。

|近义词：reproducibility, consistency, reliability |反义词：inconsistency, unreliability, variability |名词
