nonemployee:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈplɔɪiː/| n. |非雇员|A person who works for a company or organization but is not an employee;  someone who is not on the payroll.  This often includes independent contractors, freelancers, consultants, and temporary workers.| Example: The company relies heavily on nonemployees for specialized projects. 这家公司非常依赖非雇员来完成专门的项目。|近义词: independent contractor, freelancer, consultant, temporary worker|反义词: employee
