USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," etc.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "imbecilely".  The word itself is quite strong and rarely used in everyday conversation.
