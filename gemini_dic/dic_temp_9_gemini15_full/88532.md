hit on:/hɪt ˈɒn/ | vt. |偶然发现；巧遇；想出；调情 | To discover something by chance or unexpectedly; to meet someone unexpectedly; to think of or devise something; to flirt with someone. |例句：I hit on a great idea for the project. 我偶然想出了一个好主意来完成这个项目。|近义词：discover, stumble upon, devise, flirt with |反义词：miss, overlook, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hits on new ideas every day. = 他每天都会想出新主意。
She often hits on her colleagues. = 她经常和同事调情。


一般过去时:
They hit on a solution to the problem. = 他们找到了解决这个问题的方法。
He hit on her at the party. = 他在派对上和她调情。


现在完成时:
I have hit on a way to improve the design. = 我已经找到了一种改进设计的方法。
She has hit on several men since her divorce. = 自从离婚后，她已经和好几个男人调情了。


过去完成时:
We had hit on the answer before the meeting. = 我们在会议之前就已经找到了答案。
He had hit on several women before he met his wife. = 在遇见他妻子之前，他已经和好几个女人调情了。


将来时:
I think I will hit on something brilliant soon. = 我想我很快就会想到一个绝妙的主意。
He plans to hit on her at the next party. = 他计划在下一次派对上和她调情。


虚拟语气:
If I had hit on that idea earlier, I would have finished the project sooner. = 如果我早点想到那个主意，我就能早点完成项目了。
I wish I had hit on her earlier. = 我真希望我早点和她调情。


被动语态:
A new solution was hit on by the research team. = 研究团队偶然发现了一种新的解决方案。
(Note:  The passive voice is less common with "hit on" especially in the sense of flirting.)


疑问句:
Did you hit on any good ideas during the brainstorming session? = 在头脑风暴会议期间，你想出什么好主意了吗？
Has he ever hit on you before? = 他以前有没有对你调情过？


否定句:
I didn't hit on anything useful during the meeting. = 我在会议上没有想出任何有用的东西。
He didn't hit on her, he just talked to her normally. = 他没有和她调情，只是和她正常地交谈。


PHRASE->
hit on a solution = 想出一个解决方法
hit on a problem = 发现一个问题
hit on an idea = 想出一个主意
hit on someone = 调情
hit on a theme = 偶然发现一个主题
