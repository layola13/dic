gritty:/ˈɡrɪti/| adj.|坚韧的；粗糙的；不愉快的；真实的；包含沙砾的|Containing or resembling grit; firm and strong in the face of adversity; unpleasant or harsh in nature; realistic and unvarnished.  |例句：The gritty detective solved the case.  这个坚韧的侦探破了案。

例句：The gritty texture of the sandpaper was rough against my skin. 砂纸粗糙的质地磨擦着我的皮肤。


近义词：tough, coarse, harsh, realistic, unyielding, determined

反义词：smooth, delicate, pleasant, idealistic, weak, yielding
