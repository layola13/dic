nonworkers:/ˈnɑːnˌwɜːrkərz/| n. |非劳动者，非就业人口|People who are not employed or actively seeking employment.  This term often encompasses those who are retired, disabled, students, homemakers, or otherwise not participating in the formal workforce.| Example: The number of nonworkers in the country has increased significantly in recent years.  近年来，该国的非劳动者人数显著增加。 The government provides various support programs for nonworkers, such as social security benefits. 政府为非劳动者提供各种支持计划，例如社会保障福利。|近义词：unemployed, idle, inactive|反义词：workers, employees
