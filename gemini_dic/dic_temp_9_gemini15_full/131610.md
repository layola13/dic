noncompliance:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪəns/| n. |不遵守，违规 |The failure to comply with a rule, order, or law.  It often refers to a situation where an individual or organization fails to meet a specific requirement or obligation.  |例句：The company faces penalties for noncompliance with safety regulations.  这家公司因不遵守安全条例而面临处罚。

The repeated noncompliance led to the suspension of their license.  屡次违规导致他们的执照被吊销。|近义词：violation, infringement, breach, transgression |反义词：compliance, obedience, conformity |词性：名词
