cithern:/ˈsɪθən/| n. |西瑟琴，一种拨弦乐器 | A cithern is a medieval stringed instrument, similar to a lute or a zither, played by plucking the strings.  It had a flat body and was often used in medieval music. | The minstrel played a lively tune on his cithern.  吟游诗人用他的西瑟琴演奏了一首欢快的曲子。| 近义词：lute, zither | 反义词：无
