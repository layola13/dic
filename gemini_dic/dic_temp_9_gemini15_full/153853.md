perceptualize:/pərˈsɛpʃənəlaɪz/| vt.|感知化；使…易于感知|To make something perceptible or understandable; to interpret sensory information and form a mental representation of it.|例句：The artist aimed to perceptualize the intangible emotions of love and loss through his work.  艺术家试图通过他的作品来感知爱情和失去的无形情感。

例句：We need to perceptualize the data to make it meaningful for decision-making. 我们需要感知数据，以便使其对决策有意义。


近义词：interpret, understand, conceptualize, visualize|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|动词
