references:/ˈrɛfrənsɪz/ | n. |参考文献；参考书目；证明；推荐信 | References are sources of information used in a research paper. 参考文献是研究论文中使用的信息来源。| 近义词：citations, sources, bibliography | 反义词：originality


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The researcher consults several references for his article. = 研究者为他的文章查阅了几篇参考文献。
This book lists many useful references on the subject. = 这本书列出了许多关于这个主题的有用参考文献。


一般过去时:
She cited numerous references in her thesis. = 她在论文中引用了许多参考文献。
He checked all the references before submitting the report. = 他在提交报告之前检查了所有的参考文献。


现在完成时:
They have gathered all the necessary references for the project. = 他们已经为该项目收集了所有必要的参考文献。
I have already reviewed the references you provided. = 我已经审查了你提供的参考文献。


过去完成时:
