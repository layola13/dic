half-contented: /ˈhæf kənˈtɛntɪd/ | adj. | 半满意；不太满足 | Partially satisfied; not fully content.  It suggests a feeling of contentment that is incomplete or lacking something. |例句：He felt half-contented with his promotion, wishing for more responsibility. 他对自己的晋升感到半满意，希望承担更多责任。

例句：She gave a half-contented sigh, realizing the work wasn't quite finished. 她叹了口气，半满意地意识到工作还没完全结束。


近义词：partially satisfied, somewhat content, mildly pleased | 反义词：completely dissatisfied, utterly discontent, thoroughly unhappy | 词性: adjective
