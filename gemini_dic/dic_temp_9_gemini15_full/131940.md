noncontradictions:/ˌnɒnkɒntrəˈdɪkʃənz/ | n. |非矛盾；一致性 | Statements or ideas that do not contradict each other; consistency or agreement.  The absence of contradiction. |例句：The two theories present noncontradictions, meaning they can coexist without conflict. 这两个理论之间没有矛盾，这意味着它们可以共存而不会冲突。


例句：The evidence presented shows a series of noncontradictions, supporting the defendant's alibi.  提供的证据显示出一系列不矛盾之处，支持了被告的不在场证明。


近义词：consistency, agreement, accord, congruity


反义词：contradictions, inconsistencies, discrepancies


词性：名词
