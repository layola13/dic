plumcot:/ˈplʌmkɒt/| n. |李子杏，杏李|A plumcot is a hybrid fruit, a cross between a plum and an apricot.  It combines characteristics of both parent fruits, resulting in a unique flavor and appearance.  They are typically smaller than plums, with a smoother skin and a slightly more tart flavor than apricots.|例句：I bought some delicious plumcots at the farmer's market. 我在农贸市场买了一些美味的李子杏。
例句：The plumcot tree bore a bountiful harvest this year.  今年李子杏树结了很多果实。|近义词：plum, apricot|反义词：无
