imprecation:/ˌɪmprɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 咒骂；诅咒；恶言 | A curse or malediction; an utterance of ill wishes.  Imprecations are often directed at someone or something considered to be responsible for misfortune or wrongdoing. | Example:  The villagers hurled imprecations at the sorcerer, blaming him for the drought.  村民们向巫师恶语咒骂，责怪他导致了干旱。|近义词：curse, malediction, execration | 反义词：blessing, benediction
