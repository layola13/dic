neurobiologist:/ˌnʊroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |神经生物学家|A scientist who studies the biology of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.  Neurobiologists investigate the structure, function, development, genetics, biochemistry, physiology, and pathology of the nervous system.|Dr. Ramirez is a renowned neurobiologist specializing in Alzheimer's disease.  拉米雷兹博士是一位专门研究阿尔茨海默病的著名神经生物学家。| neuroscientist, brain scientist |  | n.
