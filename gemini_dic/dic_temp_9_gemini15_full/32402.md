chillinesses:/ˈtʃɪlinɪsɪz/| n.|寒冷；寒意(复数)|The plural form of "chilliness," referring to multiple instances or types of coldness or a feeling of being cold.  It is less common than using "chills" or "cold spells".|例句：The frequent chillinesses of the autumn evenings made her reach for a warmer coat. 秋天夜晚频繁的寒冷让她不得不穿上更暖和的大衣。


例句：The sudden chillinesses throughout the day signaled the approaching winter storm. 白天突然的阵阵寒意预示着即将到来的冬季风暴。|近义词：cold spells, chills, cold snaps |反义词:warmth, heat
