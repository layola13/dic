illogicality:/ˌɪlˈɒdʒɪkælɪti/ | n. |不合逻辑；荒谬；不合理 | The illogicality of his argument was immediately apparent.  他的论点的不合逻辑之处立刻显而易见。|近义词：absurdity, inconsistency, paradox |反义词：logic, rationality, coherence


词源:

* **il-**:  Prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". (来自拉丁语 *in-*，表示否定)
* **logic-**: From "logic" (逻辑), ultimately derived from the Greek word  λόγος (logos), meaning "word," "speech," "reason," or "account." (来自古希腊语 λόγος)
* **-ality**: Suffix forming nouns of quality or state. (名词后缀，表示性质或状态)

因此，illogicality 字面意思是“非逻辑性”，指缺乏逻辑性或合理性的状态。


用法说明:

illogicality 主要用作名词，指某种论证、行为或情况缺乏逻辑性或合理性的状态。它通常用于描述令人困惑、矛盾或荒谬的事情。


例句:

* The illogicality of his plan was evident to everyone.  他的计划的不合理之处对每个人都很明显。

* There was a certain illogicality in her decision-making process. 她的决策过程中存在某种不合理性。

* The illogicality of the situation made it difficult to understand.  情况的不合理性使得难以理解。

*  He pointed out the illogicality in the professor's argument. 他指出了教授论证中的不合逻辑之处。

* The sheer illogicality of the claim was astounding.  这一说法完全不合逻辑，令人震惊。


注意：由于illogicality本身描述的是一种状态或性质，它通常不直接用于构成谓语动词。  你不会说"The argument illogicalities."  而是说"The argument shows illogicality." 或 "The argument is illogical."
