militarising: /ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使军事化；军事侵略 |  The act of giving military characteristics to something; making something more military in nature or purpose.  It often implies an increase in military power or influence, sometimes aggressively. |


例句：

The government is accused of militarising the police force. = 政府被指控使警察部队军事化。

The country is rapidly militarising its borders in response to the threat. =  该国正在迅速加强边境军事力量以应对威胁。


近义词：weaponizing,  fortifying,  armoring


反义词：demilitarizing, pacifizing


词性：动词
