flat:/flæt/ | adj., n. | 平的；公寓；一套房间 |  Flat describes something that is level and even, with no curves or slopes.  It can also refer to a self-contained housing unit, typically within a larger building. | 例句：The pancake was perfectly flat. 这块薄饼非常平整。

The family lives in a small flat. 这家人住在一套小公寓里。


近义词：level, even, apartment, suite | 反义词：steep, hilly, bumpy, curved


词性:形容词，名词
