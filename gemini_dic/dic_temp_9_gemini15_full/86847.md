It's a product of the action of pepsin on albumin. |  The analysis revealed the presence of hemialbumose in the patient's digestive tract.  | 近义词: proteose, albumose | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but albumin could be considered a contrasting term as it's the precursor) | 名词
