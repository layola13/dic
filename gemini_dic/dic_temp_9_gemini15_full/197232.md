sharefarming: /ˈʃɛərˌfɑːrmɪŋ/ | n. | 股份农业；合作耕作 |

英文解析:  Sharefarming is an agricultural practice where farmers share the costs and benefits of farming a piece of land. This can involve sharing labor, equipment, expenses, and the harvest.  Different arrangements exist, ranging from informal agreements between neighbors to more formal partnerships with legally binding contracts.  It’s often used as a way to reduce risk, share resources, and improve efficiency.

例句:

They entered into a sharefarming agreement to cultivate the large plot of land. = 他们签订了一份股份农业协议来耕种那块大的土地。

The sharefarming project proved successful, increasing yields for all participants. = 股份农业项目证明是成功的，增加了所有参与者的产量。


近义词:  cooperative farming, joint farming, partnership farming

反义词:  sole farming, individual farming


词性: 名词
