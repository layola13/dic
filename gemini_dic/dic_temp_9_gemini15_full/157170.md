ping-pong: /ˈpɪŋpɒŋ/ | n. |乒乓球；乒乓球运动 |  The word "ping-pong" is onomatopoeic, imitating the sounds made by the ball hitting the paddles and table.  It's used to refer to the game itself, the equipment used to play it, or the act of playing. | Example:  They played a game of ping-pong after dinner.  他们晚饭后打了一场乒乓球。  | 近义词: table tennis | 反义词:  (none directly related) |  名词
