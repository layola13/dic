narcissists:/ˈnɑːrsɪsɪsts/ | n. | 自恋者，自恋狂 | People with narcissistic personality disorder are excessively self-centered and have an inflated sense of self-importance. They often exploit others to achieve their own goals and lack empathy. |  He exhibited classic narcissistic traits, constantly seeking admiration and dismissing criticism. 他表现出典型的自恋特征，不断寻求赞赏并轻视批评。  The therapist helped the patient understand the roots of his narcissistic behavior. 治疗师帮助病人理解其自恋行为的根源。 | egotists, self-obsessed individuals | altruists, empathetic people | 名词
