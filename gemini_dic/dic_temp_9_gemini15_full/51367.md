deteriorating:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 恶化；变坏 |  Becoming progressively worse.  | The weather is deteriorating rapidly. 天气正在迅速恶化。 The patient's condition is deteriorating. 病人的病情正在恶化。 | worsening, declining, degenerating | improving, enhancing, ameliorating | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is deteriorating. = 情况正在恶化。
His health is deteriorating steadily. = 他的健康状况正在稳步恶化。


一般过去时:
The relationship deteriorated quickly. = 关系迅速恶化。
The building deteriorated over the years. = 这座建筑物年久失修。


现在完成时:
The economy has deteriorated significantly. = 经济已经严重恶化。
His eyesight has deteriorated since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，他的视力下降了。


过去完成时:
The situation had deteriorated before they intervened. = 在他们介入之前，情况已经恶化了。
The bridge had deteriorated to a dangerous state. = 桥梁已经损坏到危险的地步。


将来时:
The situation will deteriorate further without intervention. = 如果不干预，情况将进一步恶化。
The weather is forecast to deteriorate tomorrow. = 预报明天天气将恶化。


虚拟语气:
If the situation deteriorated further, we would have to take action. = 如果情况进一步恶化，我们将不得不采取行动。
I wish the situation weren't deteriorating so rapidly. = 我希望情况不要恶化得这么快。


被动语态:
The building was allowed to deteriorate. = 这座建筑物被允许年久失修。
The negotiations were deteriorating rapidly. = 谈判正在迅速恶化。


疑问句:
Is the situation deteriorating? = 情况正在恶化吗？
Are the talks deteriorating? = 会谈正在恶化吗？


否定句:
The situation is not deteriorating. = 情况没有恶化。
His health is not deteriorating. = 他的健康状况没有恶化。


PHRASE->
rapidly deteriorating = 快速恶化
steadily deteriorating = 稳步恶化
significantly deteriorating = 显著恶化
continuously deteriorating = 持续恶化
gradually deteriorating = 逐渐恶化
