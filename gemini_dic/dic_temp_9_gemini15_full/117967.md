memo:/ˈmɛmoʊ/| n.|备忘录；便条|A memo is a short written message, often used in a business context, to communicate information or instructions to one or more people.  It's typically less formal than a letter.|Example: I sent a memo to the team outlining the project timeline. 我给团队发了一份备忘录，概述了项目时间表。|近义词：note, reminder, message|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I write a memo every week. = 我每周写一份备忘录。
She sends a memo to the staff. = 她给员工发了一份备忘录。

一般过去时:
He wrote a memo yesterday. = 他昨天写了一份备忘录。
They sent a memo last week. = 他们上周发了一份备忘录。

现在完成时:
I have written several memos this month. = 这个月我已经写了几份备忘录。
She has sent a memo to the board. = 她已经给董事会发了一份备忘录。

过去完成时:
He had written the memo before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经写好了备忘录。
They had sent the memo by Friday. = 他们星期五之前已经发出了备忘录。

将来时:
I will write a memo tomorrow. = 我明天将写一份备忘录。
She will send a memo next week. = 她下周将发一份备忘录。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would write a memo. = 如果我有时间，我会写一份备忘录。
I wish I had written a memo sooner. = 我希望我早点写一份备忘录。

被动语态:
The memo was written last night. = 备忘录是昨晚写的。
The memo will be sent this afternoon. = 备忘录将在今天下午发出。

疑问句:
Did you receive the memo? = 你收到备忘录了吗？
Has the memo been sent yet? = 备忘录发送了吗？

否定句:
I didn't write a memo. = 我没有写备忘录。
She hasn't sent the memo yet. = 她还没有发送备忘录。


PHRASE->
internal memo = 内部备忘录
memorandum of understanding (MOU) = 谅解备忘录
draft a memo = 起草一份备忘录
circulate a memo = 传阅一份备忘录
action memo = 行动备忘录
