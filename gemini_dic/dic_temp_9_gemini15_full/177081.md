reappear:/ˌriːəˈpɪr/| v.|再次出现；重新出现 |To appear again after a period of time.  |例句：The lost dog reappeared after three days.  丢失的狗三天后又出现了。

例句：The sun reappeared after the storm.暴风雨过后，太阳又出现了。


近义词：return, reappear, reemerge, resurface| 反义词：disappear, vanish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun reappears every morning. 太阳每天早晨都会重新出现。
He reappears on stage after a short break.他在短暂休息后重新回到舞台上。


一般过去时:
The missing child reappeared after several weeks. 失踪的孩子几周后又出现了。
Hope reappeared in their hearts after hearing the good news. 听到好消息后，希望重新燃起了他们的希望。


现在完成时:
The symptoms have reappeared since I stopped taking the medication.自从我停止服用药物以来，症状又出现了。
He has reappeared in the public eye after a long absence. 他在长时间缺席后重新出现在公众视野中。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the sun had reappeared. 当我们到达时，太阳已经重新出现了。
Before the meeting ended, the issue had reappeared. 会议结束前，这个问题又出现了。


将来时:
The comet will reappear in the night sky next year. 这颗彗星明年将在夜空中再次出现。
I'm sure he will reappear at the party later. 我确信他稍后会在派对上再次出现。


虚拟语气:
If the symptoms reappeared, I would go back to the doctor.如果症状再次出现，我会去看医生。
I wish the lost cat would reappear.我希望丢失的猫能够再次出现。


被动语态:
The missing painting was never reappeared. 失踪的画作从未再次出现过。
The problem has not been reappeared since the last update.自从上次更新以来，这个问题没有再次出现。


疑问句:
Will the star reappear in the next few years? 这颗星星在未来几年还会出现吗？
When will the band reappear on tour? 乐队什么时候会再次巡演？


否定句:
The problem hasn't reappeared since the fix.自从修复以来，问题就没有再次出现。
He didn't reappear at the meeting. 他没有在会议上再次出现。
