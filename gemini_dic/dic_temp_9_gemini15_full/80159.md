goddess:/ˈɡɒdəs/ /ˈɡɑːdəs/| n. |女神，女神像|A female deity; a woman of exceptional beauty, grace, or talent.|例句：Athena is the Greek goddess of wisdom. 雅典娜是希腊智慧女神。

例句：She was treated like a goddess by her adoring fans.  她被她崇拜的粉丝们当作女神一样对待。


近义词：deity, divinity, fairy

反义词：mortal, human
