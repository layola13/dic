self-timer:/ˈsɛlfˌtaɪmər/ | n. | 自拍定时器；自拍功能 | A device or function that automatically activates a camera or other device after a set delay, allowing the user to be in the photograph.  | Example:  I used the self-timer on my camera to take a picture of myself and my family. 我用相机上的自拍定时器拍了一张我和家人的照片。


Example: The self-timer on my phone allowed me to take a group photo without needing a separate photographer. 我的手机自拍定时器让我无需额外摄影师就能拍集体照。|近义词：automatic timer, delay timer |反义词：immediate trigger, instant release |词性：名词
