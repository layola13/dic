reinvites:/ˌriːɪnˈvaɪts/| v.|再次邀请；重新邀请 | To invite someone again.  This implies a previous invitation that was perhaps declined or not acted upon. |例句：He reinvited her to the party after she explained she couldn't make it the first time. 他在她解释第一次不能来之后再次邀请她参加聚会。

例句：The company reinvited all the applicants for a second interview. 公司再次邀请所有申请者进行第二次面试。


近义词：reinvite, ask again, invite anew | 反义词：decline, refuse


USAGE->

They reinvite their friends to the party. = 他们再次邀请朋友参加聚会。
