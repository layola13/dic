canonicalness: /ˌkænˈɒnɪkəlnəs/ | n. |规范性，标准性 | The state or quality of being canonical; conformity to a standard or rule.  It often refers to the adherence to established norms, especially in areas like data structures, representations, or religious texts. | Example:  The canonicalness of the data structure ensures interoperability between different systems.  规范的数据结构确保了不同系统之间的互操作性。 The canonicalness of the biblical text is a subject of ongoing scholarly debate. 圣经文本的规范性是持续进行的学术争论的主题。|近义词：conformity, regularity, standardization |反义词：irregularity, nonconformity, deviation
