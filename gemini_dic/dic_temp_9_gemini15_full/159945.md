polyphyllous: /ˌpɒliˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 多叶的 | Having many leaves.  This term is typically used in botany to describe a plant structure where many leaves are present, often arranged in a whorl or cluster.  It contrasts with terms indicating fewer leaves.

例句：
The polyphyllous flower head of the hydrangea is a stunning display.  | 绣球花的多叶花序令人叹为观止。

The polyphyllous arrangement of the leaves maximizes sunlight capture. | 叶子的多叶排列最大限度地捕获阳光。


近义词：multifoliate, many-leaved


反义词：unifoliate,  oligo-phyllous (few-leaved)


词性:形容词
