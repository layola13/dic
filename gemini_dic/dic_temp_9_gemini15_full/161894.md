praetorian:/prɪˈtɔːriən/| adj. n.|禁卫军的；保镖的；皇帝的；专制的|Relating to or characteristic of the Praetorian Guard, the elite bodyguards of Roman emperors; hence, characterized by the protection or support of those in power; often implying oppressive or authoritarian methods.|The praetorian guard was fiercely loyal to the emperor.  = 禁卫军对皇帝极其忠诚。|The praetorian methods used by the regime to maintain power were widely condemned. = 该政权为维持权力而使用的专制手段受到了广泛谴责。|authoritarian, tyrannical, imperial|democratic, liberal|adj. n.
