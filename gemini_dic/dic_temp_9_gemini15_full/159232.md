poetical:/ˈpəʊɪtɪkəl/| adj.|诗意的；诗一般的；具有诗人气质的| Relating to poetry or poets; having qualities characteristic of poetry; expressing oneself in a way that is imaginative and beautiful, like poetry.| The poem had a poetical rhythm and flow. 这首诗具有诗意的节奏和流畅性。|The sunset created a poetical scene. 日落创造了一个诗意的景象。|literary, poetic, lyrical|prosaic, commonplace
