nyctitropism:/ˌnɪkˈtɪtrəpɪzəm/| n. |向光性（夜间）|The directional movement of a plant in response to darkness; the turning or bending of a plant organ toward darkness.|Some plants exhibit nyctitropism, raising their leaves at night and lowering them during the day.|近义词：negative phototropism|反义词：phototropism
