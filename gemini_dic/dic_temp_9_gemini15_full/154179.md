periodicals:/ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəlz/| n.|期刊，杂志|Publications that are issued at regular intervals, such as weekly, monthly, or quarterly. They often cover specific subjects or fields of interest.|Example:  I subscribe to several scientific periodicals to stay up-to-date on new research. 我订阅了几种科学期刊，以便及时了解最新的研究成果。|近义词：journals, magazines, publications|反义词：monographs (single publications)
