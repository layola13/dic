plunges:/plʌndʒɪz/| v.| 猛冲；骤降；投入；陷入|To dive or jump suddenly into something; to fall or drop suddenly and sharply; to thrust or push something forcefully into something else; to become deeply involved in something.|例句：The bird plunged into the water to catch a fish.  鸟儿猛扎入水中捉鱼。

例句：The stock market plunged after the news broke.  消息传出后，股市暴跌。


近义词：dive, plunge, drop, plummet,  immerse


反义词：rise, ascend, climb, soar


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plunges into his work every day. = 他每天都投入工作。
The river plunges over the cliff. = 河水从悬崖上倾泻而下。


一般过去时:
She plunged into the cold water. = 她跳入冰冷的水中。
The temperature plunged overnight. = 温度一夜之间骤降。


现在完成时:
The country has plunged into recession. = 该国已经陷入经济衰退。
The value of the dollar has plunged recently. = 美元最近贬值了。


过去完成时:
They had plunged into debt before they realized it. = 他们还没意识到就已深陷债务。
The ship had plunged to the bottom of the ocean. = 船只已经沉入海底。


将来时:
The economy will plunge into crisis if the government doesn't act. = 如果政府不采取行动，经济将陷入危机。
The plane will plunge towards the earth if the engines fail. = 如果发动机失效，飞机将坠落地面。


虚拟语气:
If he plunged into the river, he would be in danger. = 如果他跳入河中，他会很危险。
I wish I hadn't plunged into that business venture. = 我真希望我没有投入那项商业冒险。


被动语态:
The city was plunged into darkness by the power outage. = 停电使城市陷入黑暗。
The economy was plunged into chaos by the war. = 战争使经济陷入混乱。


疑问句:
Will the price of oil plunge further? = 油价还会进一步下跌吗？
Did the swimmer plunge into the deep end? = 游泳者跳入深水区了吗？


否定句:
He didn't plunge into the project without careful planning. = 他没有在没有仔细计划的情况下就投入项目。
The sales figures didn't plunge as drastically as expected. = 销售额并没有像预期那样大幅下降。


PHRASE->
plunge into:  投入
plunge headfirst:  一头扎进
plunge oneself into:  全身心地投入
take the plunge:  冒险一试
plunge a knife into:  将刀刺入
