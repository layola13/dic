reduct:/rɪˈdʌkt/ | n. v. | 还原，还原剂；减少 | To reduce or be reduced; the product of reduction.  |The reduct of the reaction was a colorless liquid.  反应的还原产物是一种无色的液体。| reduction, decrease | increase, oxidation |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chemist reduces the iron oxide. = 化学家还原氧化铁。
The company reduces its carbon footprint. = 公司减少碳足迹。


一般过去时:
They reduced the price of the product. = 他们降低了产品的价格。
The scientist reduced the compound. = 科学家还原了化合物。


现在完成时:
The factory has reduced its emissions. = 工厂已经减少了排放。
We have reduced the risk of failure. = 我们已经降低了失败的风险。


过去完成时:
They had reduced the temperature before the experiment. = 他们在实验前已经降低了温度。
The team had reduced the workload significantly. = 团队已经大幅度减少了工作量。


将来时:
We will reduce our dependence on fossil fuels. = 我们将减少对化石燃料的依赖。
The government will reduce taxes next year. = 政府明年将减税。


虚拟语气:
If we reduced costs, we could invest more. = 如果我们降低成本，我们就能进行更多投资。
I wish they had reduced the noise. = 我希望他们能降低噪音。


被动语态:
The price was reduced by 10%. = 价格降低了10%。
The problem was reduced to a simple equation. = 问题被简化为一个简单的方程式。


疑问句:
Did they reduce the number of employees? = 他们是否减少了员工人数？
Can we reduce the complexity of the design? = 我们能否简化设计的复杂性？


否定句:
They did not reduce the speed of the vehicle. = 他们没有降低车辆的速度。
We cannot reduce the impact of this event. = 我们无法减轻这一事件的影响。
