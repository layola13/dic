reconquered:/ˌriːˈkɒŋkəd/| v. |重新征服；收复|To conquer again; to regain control or possession of something that was previously lost or taken.|The army reconquered the lost territory.  军队收复了失地。|The king reconquered his stolen throne. 国王收复了他被夺走的王位。| regained, recovered, recaptured |lost, surrendered, ceded |动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconquer lost lands every year. 他们每年都重新征服失地。
The empire reconquers its former colonies. 这个帝国正在重新征服其以前的殖民地。

一般过去时:
The general reconquered the city after a long siege. 将军在长期围困后收复了这座城市。
Spain reconquered Granada in 1492. 西班牙在1492年收复了格拉纳达。

现在完成时:
The government has reconquered the rebellious province. 政府已经收复了叛乱省份。
They have reconquered their lost market share. 他们已经收复了他们失去的市场份额。

过去完成时:
The king had reconquered the northern territories before his death. 国王在他死前已经收复了北部领土。
By the time the reinforcements arrived, they had reconquered the fort. 当增援部队到达时，他们已经收复了要塞。

将来时:
The army will reconquer the occupied lands. 军队将收复被占领的土地。
We believe that we will reconquer the top position in sales. 我们相信我们将重新获得销售额的领先地位。

虚拟语气:
If they had a stronger army, they would have reconquered the lost city. 如果他们有更强大的军队，他们就会收复失落的城市。
I wish we could reconquer our lost innocence. 我希望我们能够找回失去的天真。

被动语态:
The lost territories were reconquered by the victorious army. 被占领的领土被获胜的军队收复了。
The stolen jewels were reconquered by the police. 被盗的珠宝被警方追回。

疑问句:
Will the rebels reconquer the capital? 反叛者会收复首都吗？
Has the country been completely reconquered? 这个国家已经被完全收复了吗？

否定句:
The invaders did not reconquer the entire region. 侵略者并没有收复整个地区。
He could not reconquer his lost love. 他无法找回失去的爱。
