shisha:/ˈʃiːʃə/| n. |水烟；水烟筒|A shisha is a water pipe that is used to smoke flavored tobacco.  It is also known as a hookah.|例句：He ordered a shisha with apple flavor. 他点了一份苹果味的水烟。|例句：Smoking shisha is a popular pastime in many Middle Eastern countries. 在许多中东国家，吸水烟是一种流行的消遣活动。|近义词：hookah, hubble-bubble|反义词：N/A|名词
