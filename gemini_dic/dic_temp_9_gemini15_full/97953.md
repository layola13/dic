innuendos: /ˈɪnjuːɛndoʊz/ | n. |影射；暗讽；弦外之音 | Indirect or subtle suggestions of something improper; hints.  It often implies something suggestive or scandalous without explicitly stating it.  |例句：His speech was full of innuendos about the mayor's private life. 他的演讲充满了关于市长私生活的影射。


例句：The comedian's act was filled with innuendos about relationships and politics.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了关于人际关系和政治的影射。


近义词：insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint


反义词：direct statement, explicit statement


词性：名词
