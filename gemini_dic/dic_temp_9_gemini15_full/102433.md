jackdaws:/ˈdʒækˌdɔːz/| n. |寒鸦；|A jackdaw is a small, black bird with grey nape and neck feathers, belonging to the crow family. They are known for their intelligence and ability to mimic sounds.  |Example:  We saw a flock of jackdaws swooping over the castle. 我们看到一群寒鸦在城堡上空盘旋。  |近义词：crow, rook |反义词：None specifically.
