flouriness:/ˈflaʊrɪnɪs/| n.|面粉状；粉状；面粉质感|The quality or state of being floury; resembling flour in texture or appearance.  It often refers to a powdery, soft, and slightly grainy texture.|The cake had a delightful flouriness, almost like a cloud.|近义词：powderiness, mealiness|反义词：coarseness, graininess


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense discussions are not applicable.  The usage focuses on describing a noun's quality.


Example Sentences:

The baker admired the flouriness of the freshly milled flour. = 面包师欣赏着新磨出的面粉的粉状质感。

The potatoes had a pleasant flouriness after boiling. = 土豆煮熟后有一种令人愉悦的粉状质感。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "flouriness".  The word is descriptive, and its usage is context-dependent within sentences.
