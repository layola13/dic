originator:/ˈɒrɪdʒɪneɪtə(r)/| n. | 发起人；创始人；开创者 | The person or thing that originates something.  An originator is the person or entity that starts or initiates a process, idea, or product.  They are responsible for the initial creation or conception. | Example:  He is the originator of the new marketing strategy. 他是这个新的市场营销策略的发起人。

The originator of the conflict was never identified. 冲突的始作俑者从未被认定。


近义词：founder, initiator, creator, inventor, pioneer

反义词：follower, imitator, recipient
