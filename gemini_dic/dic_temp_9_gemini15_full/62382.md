词源:
The word "endocardial" is derived from the Greek words "endon" (ἐνδον), meaning "within," and "kardia" (καρδία), meaning "heart."  The suffix "-al" indicates relating to or pertaining to.  Therefore, endocardial literally means "relating to the inside of the heart."
