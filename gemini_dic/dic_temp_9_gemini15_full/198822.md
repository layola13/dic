sidecar:/ˈsaɪdˌkɑːr/| n. |边车；摩托车侧车|A sidecar is a vehicle, typically with one or two wheels, attached to the side of a motorcycle, providing extra passenger space.  It's often used for transporting a passenger or cargo.|例句：He rode a motorcycle with a sidecar. 他骑着一辆带边车的摩托车。|例句：The sidecar was rusty and in need of repair.  这辆边车生锈了，需要修理。|近义词：motorbike attachment, passenger carriage (in this context)|反义词：none|名词
