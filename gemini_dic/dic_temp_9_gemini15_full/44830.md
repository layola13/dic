crustacea:/krʌsˈteɪʃə/| n. |甲壳类动物|Crustacea are a large group of invertebrate animals that includes crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles. They are characterized by their hard exoskeletons, segmented bodies, and jointed appendages.|例句：Crabs, lobsters, and shrimps are all examples of crustaceans.  螃蟹、龙虾和虾都是甲壳类动物的例子。|例句：The study focused on the reproductive behavior of various crustacean species. 这项研究侧重于各种甲壳类动物的繁殖行为。|近义词：shellfish, arthropod|反义词:vertebrate
