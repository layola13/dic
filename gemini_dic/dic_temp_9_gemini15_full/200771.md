slewth:/sluːθ/| n. |侦探，侦探队 |A sleuth is a detective, especially one who is clever and persistent in solving mysteries.  The term often carries a connotation of old-fashioned or somewhat romanticized detective work. |例句：The famous sleuth, Sherlock Holmes, solved many complex cases.  著名的侦探夏洛克·福尔摩斯破获了许多复杂的案件。
The police sleuths worked tirelessly to track down the culprit. 警探们夜以继日地追踪罪犯。|近义词：detective, investigator, private eye |反义词：criminal, culprit
