preventing:/prɪˈvɛntɪŋ/ | v. |阻止；预防；防止 |The act of stopping something from happening or someone from doing something.  |例句：He was preventing me from finishing my work. 他阻止我完成工作。

例句：Regular exercise can help preventing heart disease.  规律的锻炼有助于预防心脏病。


近义词：avoiding, hindering, obstructing, stopping | 反义词：allowing, permitting, enabling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are preventing accidents by improving safety measures. = 我们正在通过改进安全措施来防止事故。
They are preventing the spread of the virus. = 他们正在阻止病毒的传播。

一般过去时:
The rain prevented us from going out. = 雨阻止了我们外出。
She prevented him from making a mistake. = 她阻止了他犯错。

现在完成时:
We have prevented a major disaster. = 我们已经 предотвратили  一场大灾难。
They have been preventing pollution by investing in renewable energy. = 通过投资可再生能源，他们一直在防止污染。

过去完成时:
I had prevented him from leaving early. = 我已经阻止了他早退。
They had prevented the fire from spreading further. = 他们已经阻止了火势进一步蔓延。

将来时:
We will prevent future incidents by implementing new policies. = 我们将通过实施新政策来防止未来的事件。
They will be preventing access to the building. = 他们将阻止进入该建筑物。

虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have prevented the accident. = 如果我们早点行动，就能防止事故发生。
It is important to prevent this from happening again, lest it should cause greater harm. = 重要的是要防止这种情况再次发生，以免造成更大的伤害。

被动语态:
The spread of the disease was prevented by vaccination. = 疾病的传播通过疫苗接种得到了预防。
Access to the website is being prevented by a firewall. =  防火墙正在阻止访问该网站。

疑问句:
What can we do to prevent climate change? = 我们能做些什么来防止气候变化？
How are they preventing the theft? = 他们是如何防止盗窃的？

否定句:
The measures did not prevent the problem from worsening. = 这些措施并没有阻止问题恶化。
He failed to prevent the damage. = 他未能阻止损害。


PHRASE->
prevent from = 阻止…
prevent…from = 阻止…
prevent the spread of = 防止…的传播
prevent accidents = 预防事故
prevent disease = 预防疾病
