paradisian:/ˌpærəˈdaɪziən/| adj.|天堂的；极乐的；令人愉快的|Relating to or characteristic of paradise; idyllic; heavenly.  The word evokes a sense of beauty, peace, and perfection, often associated with tropical locations.|例句：The paradisian beaches were a welcome sight after our long journey.  (在漫长旅程后，天堂般的海滩成为一道令人欣慰的景象。)  The resort offered a paradisian escape from the stresses of city life. (度假村提供了一个远离城市生活压力的天堂般的休憩场所。) |近义词：heavenly, idyllic, utopian, blissful|反义词：hellish, infernal, nightmarish
