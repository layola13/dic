sanitary:/ˈsænɪteri/| adj.| 卫生间的；卫生的；清洁的 | Relating to sanitation or public health; free from dirt, infection, etc.  |例句：The sanitary conditions in the camp were appalling.  营地的卫生状况令人震惊。


例句：We need to maintain high sanitary standards in the food preparation area. 我们需要在食品准备区保持高标准的卫生。


近义词：hygienic, clean, pure | 反义词：dirty, unclean, unhygienic


词源:  From Latin *sanitarius "of health," from sanitas "health," from sanus "sound, healthy."  The word's evolution reflects its connection to health and the absence of disease.
