caudata:/kɔːˈdeɪtə/| adj.| 尾状的；有尾的 | Having a tail.  The term is often used in zoology to describe animals with tails, particularly in taxonomic classifications. | Example:  Many caudate amphibians, such as salamanders, are found in moist environments.  许多尾状两栖动物，例如蝾螈，都生活在潮湿的环境中。 | 近义词：tailed, appendaged | 反义词：tailless, acaudate
