pandemic:/pænˈdɛmɪk/| n.|大流行病，瘟疫|A pandemic is an epidemic that has spread over a large region, for instance multiple continents, or even worldwide.  It's a widespread outbreak of an infectious disease.|例句：The COVID-19 pandemic has had a devastating impact on the global economy.  新冠肺炎大流行对全球经济造成了毁灭性影响。

例句：The Spanish flu pandemic of 1918 killed millions of people.  1918年的西班牙流感大流行夺去了数百万人的生命。|近义词：epidemic, outbreak|反义词：endemic (地方性的)|词性:名词
