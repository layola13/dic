roboticize:/roʊˈbɑːtɪsaɪz/| vt.|使自动化；使机器人化|To make something automated or controlled by robots.|The factory is being roboticized to increase efficiency.  工厂正在实现自动化以提高效率。| automate, mechanize | dehumanize, manualize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They roboticize the assembly line. = 他们使装配线自动化。
We roboticize many tasks in our daily lives. = 我们在日常生活中使许多任务自动化。


一般过去时:
The company roboticized its manufacturing process. = 公司使其制造流程自动化。
They roboticized the entire warehouse. = 他们使整个仓库自动化。


现在完成时:
Many industries have roboticized their operations. = 许多行业已经使他们的运营自动化。
Scientists have roboticized many aspects of surgery. = 科学家已经使外科手术的许多方面自动化。


过去完成时:
They had roboticized the system before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经使系统自动化。
The factory had roboticized its processes years ago. = 该工厂几年前就将其流程自动化了。


将来时:
They will roboticize the entire plant next year. = 他们明年将使整个工厂自动化。
We will roboticize the data entry process. = 我们将使数据录入流程自动化。


虚拟语气:
If we roboticized the process, we would save money. = 如果我们使这个流程自动化，我们将节省资金。
It would be better if we roboticized the entire operation. = 如果我们使整个操作自动化会更好。


被动语态:
The system is being roboticized. = 系统正在自动化。
The tasks will be roboticized soon. = 任务很快将实现自动化。


疑问句:

否定句:
