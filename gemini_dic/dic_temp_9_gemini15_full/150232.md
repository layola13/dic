pandemonians: /ˌpæn.dəˈmoʊ.ni.ənz/ | n. | 恶魔们，群魔 | Demons; a chaotic and tumultuous assembly.  The word evokes a sense of widespread disorder and evil. It often refers to a large, unruly, and destructive group. |例句：The pandemoniums of the underworld wreaked havoc on the land. = 地狱里的群魔在人间造成了巨大的破坏。 The pandemoniums rioted in the streets, causing widespread destruction. = 群魔在街上暴动，造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：demons, fiends, devils, evil spirits |反义词：angels, saints, benevolent beings |名词
