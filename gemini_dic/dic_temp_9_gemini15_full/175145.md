raga:/ˈrɑːɡə/|/ˈrɑːɡɑː/ | n. |拉格(印度古典音乐的旋律模式) |A raga is a melodic framework within the system of Indian classical music.  It is characterized by a specific set of notes (swaras), a characteristic melodic contour, and often associated with particular times of day or emotional moods. |Example:  The musician played a beautiful raga in the evening.  这位音乐家在晚上演奏了一段优美的拉格。|近义词：melody, mode, scale |反义词：None
