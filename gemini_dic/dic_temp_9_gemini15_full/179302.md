rednecks:/ˈrɛdnɛks/| n. adj.|乡巴佬，白人穷困落后分子|A derogatory term for a white person from the rural Southern United States, typically perceived as uneducated, poor, and having conservative views.  The term is often associated with stereotypes about backwardness, racism, and a lack of sophistication.  Its usage is highly offensive and should be avoided.|例句：He was labeled a redneck for his outspoken views on race and politics. 他因其在种族和政治问题上的直言不讳而被贴上乡巴佬的标签。|例句：The comedian's jokes about rednecks were met with boos from the audience.  这位喜剧演员关于乡巴佬的笑话遭到观众的嘘声。|近义词：hillbilly, yokel, backwoodsman|反义词：sophisticate, urbanite
