re-connecting:/ˌriːkəˈnɛktɪŋ/ | v. |重新连接，恢复联系 | The act of connecting again; reestablishing a connection that was previously lost or broken.  This can refer to physical connections (like reconnecting a wire), or figurative connections (like reconnecting with an old friend). |例句：He's trying to reconnect with his estranged brother. 他正试图和疏远的兄弟重新联系。

例句：After the storm, the technicians worked to reconnect the power lines.  暴风雨过后，技术人员努力重新连接电力线路。


近义词：reunite, rejoin, restore contact, re-establish contact | 反义词：disconnect, sever, estrange
