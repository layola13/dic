overhunt:/ˈoʊvərhʌnt/ | vt. |过度狩猎，猎捕过度 | To hunt to excess; to hunt animals to the point of endangering their survival.  |例句：The wolves were nearly overhunted to extinction. 狼几乎被过度捕猎到灭绝的边缘。
例句：Overhunting led to a sharp decline in the deer population. 过度狩猎导致鹿群数量急剧下降。|近义词：overkill, deplete, exhaust |反义词：conserve, protect, preserve
