darn:/dɑːrn/| vt. n.|该死，讨厌；缝补|to repair a hole or tear in a piece of cloth by sewing| 例句：I need to darn this sock. 我需要缝补这只袜子。|近义词：mend, repair, patch|反义词：rip, tear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I darn my socks every week. = 我每周都缝补我的袜子。
He darns his clothes regularly. = 他定期缝补他的衣服。

一般过去时:
She darned the hole in her sweater. = 她缝补了她毛衣上的洞。
They darned the ripped trousers. = 他们缝补了撕破的裤子。

现在完成时:
I have darned all the holes in my clothes. = 我已经缝补了我衣服上的所有洞。
She has darned that sweater several times. = 她已经缝补过那件毛衣好几次了。

过去完成时:
He had darned the socks before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前已经缝补好袜子了。
They had darned the curtains before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经缝补好了窗帘。

将来时:
I will darn this shirt tomorrow. = 我明天会缝补这件衬衫。
She will darn the dress before the party. = 她会在派对前缝补好这条裙子。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would darn my jeans. = 如果我有时间，我会缝补我的牛仔裤。
I wish I had darned the hole sooner. = 我希望我早点把洞缝补好。

被动语态:
The hole in the sweater was darned by my grandmother. = 毛衣上的洞是我祖母缝补的。
The socks need to be darned. = 袜子需要缝补。

疑问句:
Have you darned the blanket yet? = 你缝补好毯子了吗？
Will you darn my trousers for me? = 你会帮我缝补我的裤子吗？

否定句:
I didn't darn the socks. = 我没有缝补袜子。
She hasn't darned the dress yet. = 她还没缝补好裙子。


PHRASE->
darn it = 该死
darn good = 非常好
darn near = 几乎
darn right = 完全正确
darn well = 当然
