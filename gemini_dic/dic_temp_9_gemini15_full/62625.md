enforcedly: /ɪnˈfɔːrsɪdli/ | adv. | 强制地；被迫地 |  Done or happening as a result of force or compulsion.  It emphasizes that something is done against someone's will or without their consent. | 例句：He was enforcedly kept in the room. 他被迫待在房间里。  The rules were enforcedly applied, leading to much resentment. 这些规则被强制执行，导致了许多怨恨。 | 近义词：compulsorily, forcibly, inevitably | 反义词：voluntarily, willingly
