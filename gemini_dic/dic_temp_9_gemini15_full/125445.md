multiplex-cinema:/ˈmʌltɪpleks ˈsɪnəmə/| n. |多厅电影院 |A multiplex cinema is a movie theater with multiple screens showing different films simultaneously.  It typically features a large complex with many auditoriums, concessions, and sometimes additional amenities like restaurants or arcades. |The new multiplex cinema opened last week, and it's already very popular.|近义词：multi-screen cinema, megaplex|反义词：single-screen cinema
