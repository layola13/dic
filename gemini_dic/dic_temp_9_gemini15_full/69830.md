ferreted:/ˈfɛrɪtɪd/| v.|搜索；挖掘；查出；|to search for something persistently, especially something that is difficult to find.  It implies a thorough and perhaps slightly sneaky search.|例句：She ferreted out the information from old newspapers. 她从旧报纸中搜寻到了这条信息。

例句：The detective ferreted out the truth behind the crime.  侦探查出了犯罪背后的真相。


近义词：search, hunt, delve, investigate, uncover

反义词：conceal, hide, obscure

词性:动词
