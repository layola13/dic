milreis:/ˈmɪlreɪz/| n. |米雷斯（葡萄牙旧货币单位）|A milreis was the basic monetary unit of Portugal before the adoption of the euro.  It was also used in other Portuguese colonies and territories.  The plural form is milreis.|The old Portuguese coin, the milreis, was eventually replaced by the escudo and later the euro.|近义词: escudo (former Portuguese currency), real (Brazilian currency)|反义词: None.
