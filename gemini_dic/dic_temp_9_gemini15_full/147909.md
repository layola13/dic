overreflectiveness: /ˌoʊvərˌrɪˌflɛkˈtɪvnəs/ | n. |过度反思；反复思考 | Excessive thinking and analysis, often to the point of being unproductive or harmful.  It involves dwelling on past events, mistakes, or potential problems, leading to anxiety, depression, or inaction. | Example:  Her overreflectiveness prevented her from taking risks and pursuing her goals.  她的过度反思阻止了她冒险并追求自己的目标。 | 近义词：rumination, introspection (when excessive), overthinking | 反义词：impulsiveness, decisiveness
