describe:/dɪˈskraɪb/ | vt. |描写，叙述，形容 | To give an account of; tell about.  To represent or depict in words or pictures. |例句：Can you describe the man you saw? 你能描述一下你看到的那个男人吗？

例句：She described her childhood in vivid detail. 她生动地描述了自己的童年。


近义词：portray, depict, delineate, recount, narrate | 反义词：misrepresent, obscure, conceal | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He describes the scene vividly. 他生动地描述了这个场景。
She describes her work as challenging but rewarding. 她形容她的工作很有挑战性，但也很有成就感。


一般过去时:
He described his trip to Paris. 他描述了他去巴黎的旅行。
She described the accident to the police. 她向警方描述了事故经过。


现在完成时:
She has described the painting in detail. 她已经详细地描述了这幅画。
He has described his feelings on several occasions. 他已经多次描述过他的感受。


过去完成时:
He had described the situation before the meeting. 他在会议之前已经描述过这种情况。
She had described the process thoroughly in her report. 她在报告中已经详尽地描述了这个过程。


将来时:
She will describe the new product to the clients. 她将向客户介绍这款新产品。
He will describe his experience in the interview. 他将在面试中描述他的经验。


虚拟语气:
If I could describe it, I would. 如果我能描述它，我会的。
I wish I could describe the beauty of the sunset. 我真希望我能描述日落的美景。


被动语态:
The event was described in the newspaper. 这件事在报纸上被报道了。
The painting is being described as a masterpiece. 这幅画被誉为杰作。


疑问句:
How would you describe your personality? 你如何形容你的性格？
Can you describe what happened? 你能描述一下发生了什么吗？


否定句:
I cannot adequately describe the feeling. 我无法充分形容这种感觉。
He didn't describe the incident accurately. 他没有准确地描述这件事故。


PHRASE->
describe in detail = 详细描述
describe as = 将…描述为
give a description of = 给出…的描述
vivid description = 生动的描述
accurate description = 准确的描述
