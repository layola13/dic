drawling:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋ/| adj. n. | 缓慢的；拖长的；慢吞吞的；拉长的声音 |  A drawling manner of speaking, characterized by prolonged vowel sounds and a slow, deliberate pace. It can also refer to something that is slow and tedious. |例句：He spoke in a slow, drawling accent. 他说话带着缓慢拖长的口音。

例句：The meeting was a drawling affair, lasting far longer than expected.  会议冗长乏味，持续时间远超预期。


近义词：slow, languid, protracted, monotonous, deliberate

反义词：quick, rapid, brisk, hasty


词性: adjective, noun
