/ɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 表达情感；流露感情 | The act of expressing or conveying emotion.  It often involves outward displays of feeling through facial expressions, body language, and vocal tone. |  He was emoting powerfully on stage. 他在舞台上激情澎湃地表演。 She was emoting her grief openly. 她公开表达了她的悲伤。 | expressing, conveying, showing | suppressing, hiding, concealing | 动词
