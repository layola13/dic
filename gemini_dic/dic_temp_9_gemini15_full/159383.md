poky:/ˈpəʊki/ | adj. | 狭小的；逼仄的；缓慢的；迟钝的 |  Describes something small, cramped, and unpleasant, or someone slow and sluggish.  It can refer to physical spaces or to people's actions or movements. |

例句：The poky little room felt claustrophobic.  =  这间狭小的房间让人感觉很压抑。

例句：He made poky progress on the project. = 他在项目上的进展缓慢。


近义词：cramped, confined, small, sluggish, slow, tardy

反义词：spacious, roomy, vast, swift, rapid, quick


词性:形容词
