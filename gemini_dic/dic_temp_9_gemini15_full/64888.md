essayistical: /ˌɛseɪˈɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 散文体的；论述的；随笔式的 |

英文解析：Relating to or characteristic of an essay; having the qualities of an essay, especially its informal and reflective style.  It suggests a writing style that is thoughtful, personal, and often digressive, exploring a topic in a less structured and more exploratory way than a formal academic paper.

例句：

His essayistical style, while charming, sometimes lacked the precision needed for a scientific paper.  他的散文体风格虽然迷人，但有时缺乏科学论文所需的精确性。


Her essayistical approach allowed her to connect with readers on a personal level. 她的散文式方法让她能够在个人层面上与读者建立联系。


近义词：reflective, discursive, rambling, informal, personal, meditative


反义词：formal, structured, concise, precise, objective


词性：形容词
