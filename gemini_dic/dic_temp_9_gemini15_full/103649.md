joking:/ˈdʒoʊkɪŋ/ | v. |开玩笑，说笑话 | To say or do something that is intended to be funny, not serious.  |  He was only joking; he didn't mean to hurt your feelings. 他只是开玩笑，并非有意伤害你的感情。 | jesting, teasing, kidding | serious, earnest | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I'm joking. = 我在开玩笑。
They are joking around. = 他们在开玩笑。


一般过去时:
He was joking about the exam. = 他在开玩笑关于考试的事。
We were joking with each other. = 我们互相开玩笑。


现在完成时:
Have you been joking all this time? = 你一直在开玩笑吗？
She has been joking around with her friends. = 她一直在和朋友们开玩笑。


过去完成时:
I had been joking with him before he got angry. = 我在他生气之前一直在和他开玩笑。
They had been joking about the situation before realizing the seriousness. = 他们在意识到事情的严重性之前一直在开玩笑。


将来时:
I will be joking with my friends later. = 我稍后会和朋友们开玩笑。
They will be joking around at the party. = 他们在派对上会开玩笑。


虚拟语气:
If I were joking, I wouldn't feel so bad. = 如果我在开玩笑，我就不会感觉这么糟糕。
I wish I had been joking when I said that. = 我真希望我说那句话的时候是在开玩笑。


被动语态:
The joke wasn't taken seriously; it was understood as joking.  = 这个笑话没有被认真对待；它被理解为玩笑。
His intentions were misunderstood; his joking was taken as a threat. = 他的意图被误解了；他的玩笑被当成了威胁。


疑问句:
Are you joking? = 你在开玩笑吗？
Were they joking when they said that? = 他们说那句话的时候是在开玩笑吗？


否定句:
I wasn't joking; I was serious. = 我不是在开玩笑；我是认真的。
They weren't joking; they meant every word. = 他们不是在开玩笑；他们说的每一个字都是认真的。


PHRASE->
just joking = 开玩笑的
not joking = 不是开玩笑
stop joking = 别开玩笑了
be joking = 开玩笑
I'm only joking = 我只是开玩笑
