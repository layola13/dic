reequipped:/ˌriːɪˈkwɪpt/| vt.|重新装备，再配备|To equip again or anew; to furnish again with equipment.|例句：The army was reequipped with the latest weapons.  军队重新装备了最新的武器。

The factory was reequipped with modern machinery. 工厂重新配备了现代化机器。


近义词：refit, re-tool, renovate|反义词：disarm, strip


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reequip the laboratory every year.  他们每年都重新装备实验室。
We reequip our offices with new computers. 我们用新电脑重新装备我们的办公室。


一般过去时:
The company reequipped its production line last month. 公司上个月对生产线进行了重新装备。
The school reequipped the science lab after the fire.  火灾后，学校重新装备了科学实验室。


现在完成时:
The hospital has reequipped its operating rooms with state-of-the-art technology. 医院已经用最先进的技术重新装备了手术室。
They have reequipped their fleet of vehicles with hybrid engines. 他们已经用混合动力发动机重新装备了他们的车队。


过去完成时:
They had reequipped the factory before the new product launch. 他们在新产品发布前已经对工厂进行了重新装备。
The team had reequipped its equipment before the expedition.  团队在探险前已经重新装备了设备。


将来时:
The government will reequip the armed forces with advanced weaponry. 政府将用先进武器重新装备军队。
The company will reequip its manufacturing plant next year. 公司明年将重新装备其制造工厂。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we would reequip the entire department. 如果我们有更多资金，我们会重新装备整个部门。
