hipping:/ˈʃɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 船运；海运；装运；运输 | The process of transporting goods by ship or other means.  It encompasses all aspects from packaging to delivery. | Example: The shipping cost was unexpectedly high.  = 运费出乎意料地高。 The shipping of the goods will be completed next week. = 货物的运输将于下周完成。 | 近义词：carriage, conveyance, freighting, delivery | 反义词：receiving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Shipping costs vary depending on the destination. = 运费根据目的地而有所不同。
He ships goods all over the world. = 他向世界各地运送货物。

一般过去时:
They shipped the package yesterday. = 他们昨天寄出了包裹。
The company shipped thousands of products last quarter. = 公司上季度运送了数千种产品。

现在完成时:
We have shipped all the orders. = 我们已经寄出了所有订单。
They have shipped the goods to the client. = 他们已经将货物运送给客户。

过去完成时:
The goods had been shipped before the payment arrived. = 货款到达之前，货物已经发出。
They had shipped the package before realizing the address was wrong. = 他们在意识到地址错误之前已经寄出了包裹。

将来时:
We will ship the order tomorrow. = 我们明天将寄出订单。
The company will ship the products next month. = 公司下个月将运送产品。

虚拟语气:
If we shipped it today, it would arrive by Friday. = 如果我们今天发货，它将在星期五到达。
I wish we had shipped the goods earlier. = 我希望我们早点发货。

被动语态:
The package was shipped last week. = 包裹是上周寄出的。
The goods are being shipped as we speak. = 货物正在运输中。

疑问句:
When will the shipment arrive? = 货物什么时候到达？
Has the shipping been confirmed? = 货运是否已确认？

否定句:
We did not ship the order on time. = 我们没有按时发货。
The goods have not yet been shipped. = 货物尚未发出。
