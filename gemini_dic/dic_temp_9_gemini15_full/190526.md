satiating: /ˈseɪʃieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人满足的；解饱的 |  Providing sufficient satisfaction or fulfillment; satisfying a need or desire, especially hunger or thirst.  |例句：The rich, satiating meal left us feeling pleasantly full.  丰盛而令人满足的饭菜让我们感到非常饱。

The enormous dessert was incredibly satiating. 巨大的甜点令人难以置信的满足。


近义词：satisfying, fulfilling, satisfying, quenching | 反义词：unsatisfying, unsated, meager


词源:  Comes from the verb "satiate," which originates from the Latin word "satiare," meaning "to satisfy fully."  The suffix "-ing" forms the present participle, used here as an adjective.
