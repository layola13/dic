installations:/ˌɪnstəˈleɪʃənz/| n.|安装；装置；设施；安装工程|The process of installing something, or the thing that has been installed.  It can refer to a single item or a collection of items. |例句：The installations of the new software took longer than expected. 新软件的安装比预期的要长。

The museum's permanent installations are impressive.  博物馆的永久性展品令人印象深刻。|近义词：setup, erection, fitting, implementation|反义词：removal, dismantling, demolition|名词

一般过去时:
