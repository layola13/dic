diminish: /dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/ | vt. vi. | 减少；降低；贬低 | To make or become less; to reduce in size, extent, or importance.  | 近义词：decrease, reduce, lessen, lessen | 反义词：increase, enlarge, augment, enhance |


英文解析:
The verb "diminish" means to make or become less. This can refer to a reduction in size, amount, intensity, importance, or value.  It can be used transitively (taking a direct object) or intransitively (without a direct object).


例句:
His influence has diminished considerably in recent years. = 他近年来的影响力大大减弱了。

The pain gradually diminished after he took the medicine. = 他服药后疼痛逐渐减轻。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun diminishes in size as it sets. = 太阳落山时体积变小。
His power diminishes with each passing day. = 他的权力日渐衰退。


一般过去时:
The value of the dollar diminished sharply last year. = 美元去年大幅贬值。
The number of students in the class diminished after several dropped out. = 几个学生辍学后，班上的学生人数减少了。


现在完成时:
His confidence has diminished since his failure. = 自从失败后，他的信心下降了。
The supply of water has diminished due to the drought. = 由于干旱，水供应减少了。


过去完成时:
His influence had diminished significantly before he resigned. = 他辞职前，他的影响力已大大减弱。
The storm had diminished in intensity by the time it reached land. = 当风暴登陆时，其强度已经减弱了。


将来时:
The effects of the drug will diminish over time. = 药物的疗效会随着时间的推移而减弱。
The importance of this issue will diminish in the future. =  这个问题的重要性将来会降低。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, our chances of success will diminish. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，我们成功的几率就会降低。
I wish the noise would diminish. = 我希望噪音能小一点。


被动语态:
His authority was diminished by the scandal. = 他的权威因丑闻而受损。
The problem was diminished by the new solution. = 新的解决方案减少了这个问题。


疑问句:
Does his power diminish over time? = 他的权力会随着时间推移而减弱吗？
Will the effect of the medicine diminish quickly? = 药物的效果会很快减弱吗？


否定句:
The problem did not diminish despite our efforts. = 尽管我们努力了，问题并没有减少。
His enthusiasm did not diminish throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，他的热情丝毫未减。
