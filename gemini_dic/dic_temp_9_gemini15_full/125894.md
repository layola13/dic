muscularity:/ˌmʌs kjʊˈlærəti/ | n. |肌肉发达；强壮；肌肉；体力|The quality or state of being muscular; strength and development of muscles.  It often implies a powerful or impressive physique.|例句：He was renowned for his incredible muscularity. 他以其令人难以置信的肌肉发达而闻名。

例句：The boxer's muscularity was evident in his powerful punches. 拳击手的强壮肌肉在他有力的拳击中显而易见。


近义词：strength, power, physique, musculature

反义词：weakness, frailty, delicateness

词性: 名词
