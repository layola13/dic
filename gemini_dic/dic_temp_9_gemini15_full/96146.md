indenter:/ɪnˈdentər/| n. |缩进器，压痕器|A tool or device used to create indentations or recesses.  It can refer to a mechanical device or a software function.|例句：The carpenter used an indenter to create the decorative grooves in the wood. 木匠用压痕器在木头上刻出装饰性的凹槽。|近义词：indent tool, embosser (for raised indentations) |反义词：expander (opposite action)|名词
