microelectronically:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlekˈtrɑːnɪkli/| adv.|微电子地，以微电子方式|Adverb describing something done using microelectronic technology or principles.  It indicates a process or action performed at a microelectronic level or with microelectronic components.|例句：The circuit was microelectronically controlled.  这个电路是微电子控制的。

The signal was processed microelectronically.  信号是以微电子方式处理的。|近义词：electronically, digitally|反义词：mechanically, manually|无
