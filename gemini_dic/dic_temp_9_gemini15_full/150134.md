pamphlets:/ˈpæmflɪts/| n. |小册子；宣传册 |A pamphlet is a small booklet containing information or arguments about a single subject.  It is often used for advertising or propaganda. |例句：The company distributed pamphlets advertising their new product.  这家公司散发宣传册来宣传他们的新产品。

例句：I picked up a pamphlet about local hiking trails. 我拿了一本关于当地远足路线的小册子。


近义词：brochure, leaflet, booklet |反义词：None
