playbooks:/ˈpleɪbʊks/| n.|剧本；策略手册；操作手册|A playbook is a detailed plan or set of instructions for achieving a specific goal, often used in sports, business, or theatre.  It can be a literal script for a play or a figurative guide for achieving success.|例句：The football team studied their playbook before the game.  橄榄球队在比赛前研究了他们的战术手册。|例句：The marketing team developed a comprehensive playbook for launching the new product.  市场团队制定了一个全面的新产品发布策略手册。|近义词：manual, guide, strategy, plan|反义词：improvisation, ad-lib
