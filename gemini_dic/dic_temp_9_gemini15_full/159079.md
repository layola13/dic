poachable:/ˈpoʊtʃəbəl/ | adj. | 可偷猎的；可挖角的；易于煮的 | Easily poached; able to be poached (as in poaching an egg).  Also, relating to the act of poaching, such as an employee being poached from another company. | 例句：The salmon is perfectly poachable. 这条鲑鱼非常容易煮。 The company's top engineer is poachable by a rival firm.  这家公司的首席工程师很容易被竞争对手公司挖角。 | 近义词：easily cooked (for the culinary meaning), recruitable, headhunted (for the employment meaning) | 反义词：unpoachable (for both meanings), difficult to cook
