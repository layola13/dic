reenter:/ˌriːˈɛntər/ | v. |重新进入，再进入 | To enter again; to come or go back into. |例句：The astronauts successfully reentered the Earth's atmosphere. 宇航员成功地重新进入地球大气层。

例句：After leaving the building, he had to reenter to retrieve his keys. 他离开大楼后不得不重新进去取回钥匙。


近义词：rejoin, return to | 反义词：exit, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reenters the room every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就重新进入房间。
She reenters the competition next year. 她明年将重新参加比赛。


一般过去时:
The pilot reentered the aircraft after the emergency landing. 飞行员在紧急着陆后重新进入飞机。
They reentered the building through the back door. 他们从后门重新进入大楼。


现在完成时:
The company has reentered the market with a new product.  该公司已推出新产品重新进入市场。
She has reentered the race after a long break. 她经过长时间的休息后重新参加了比赛。


过去完成时:
After he had reentered the house, he locked the door. 他重新进入房子后，锁上了门。
They had reentered the conversation before I could interrupt.  在我打断之前，他们已经重新开始谈话了。


将来时:
The spacecraft will reenter the atmosphere tomorrow. 宇宙飞船将于明天重新进入大气层。
He will reenter the hospital for further treatment. 他将再次入院接受进一步治疗。


虚拟语气:
If he reentered the competition, he might win. 如果他重新参加比赛，他可能会赢。
I wish he had reentered the race last year. 我希望他去年重新参加比赛。


被动语态:
The building was reentered by the police. 这座建筑物被警方重新进入。
The data was reentered into the system after the crash. 数据在崩溃后重新输入系统。


疑问句:
Will he reenter the race? 他会重新参加比赛吗？
Did she reenter the building after dark? 她天黑后重新进入大楼了吗？


否定句:
