modal:/ˈmoʊdəl/| adj.|情态的，模态的；模式的|A modal verb is an auxiliary verb that expresses modality, such as possibility, necessity, permission, or ability.  Modal words or phrases express an attitude toward the content of a sentence.|The modal verb "can" expresses ability.  The modal phrase "it is possible that" expresses possibility.|近义词：typical, exemplary, representative|反义词：actual, real, factual
