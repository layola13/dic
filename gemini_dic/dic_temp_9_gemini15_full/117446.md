meditates:/ˈmɛdɪteɪts/| v.|沉思，冥想|To think deeply and quietly, especially about spiritual matters.|例句：He meditates every morning to find inner peace. 他每天早上冥想以寻求内心的平静。
例句：She meditates on the meaning of life. 她沉思生命的意义。|近义词：contemplates, reflects, ponders|反义词：acts, rushes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He meditates every day. = 他每天都冥想。
She meditates before making important decisions. = 她做重要决定之前都会冥想。

一般过去时:
He meditated for an hour yesterday. = 他昨天冥想了一个小时。
She meditated on the problem before finding a solution. = 她在找到解决方法之前仔细思考了这个问题。

现在完成时:
I have meditated for years. = 我冥想很多年了。
She has meditated on this issue for days. = 她已经思考这个问题好几天了。

过去完成时:
He had meditated for hours before he felt at peace. = 他冥想了几个小时后才感到平静。
They had meditated on the consequences before acting. = 他们在采取行动之前已经考虑过后果。

将来时:
I will meditate tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会冥想。
She will meditate before the exam. = 她考试前会冥想。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would meditate more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地冥想。
I wish I could meditate more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地冥想。

被动语态:  (Meditate is rarely used passively)  The problem was deeply meditated upon. = 这个问题被仔细思考过。
The spiritual meaning of the text was meditated on by the scholars. = 学者们对文本的精神意义进行了深入的思考。

疑问句:
Do you meditate regularly? = 你经常冥想吗？
Does she meditate before sleep? = 她睡前冥想吗？

否定句:
He doesn't meditate often. = 他不经常冥想。
She didn't meditate before the test. = 她考试前没有冥想。


PHRASE->
meditate on/upon = 沉思；仔细考虑
meditate quietly = 安静地冥想
deep meditation = 深度冥想
guided meditation = 引导式冥想
mindful meditation = 正念冥想
