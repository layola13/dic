self-transformed:/ˌsɛlf trænsˈfɔːrmd/ | adj. | 自我改造的；自我转化的 |  Describes something that has undergone a significant change through its own internal processes or efforts, rather than by external forces.  It implies a fundamental alteration in character, nature, or form. | Example: The self-transformed caterpillar emerged as a beautiful butterfly.  自我改造的毛毛虫蜕变成了美丽的蝴蝶。|近义词：metamorphosed, transfigured, reformed, evolved | 反义词：unchanged, static, stagnant
