impuberal:/ɪmˈpjuːbərəl/| adj. |青春期前的，未发育的|Not having reached puberty; prepubescent.  The term refers to the stage before the onset of puberty, characterized by the absence of secondary sexual characteristics.|Example:  The impuberal child showed no signs of physical development associated with puberty.  青春期前的孩子没有表现出与青春期相关的身体发育迹象。|近义词：prepubescent, preadolescent|反义词：pubescent, mature
