shorties:/ˈʃɔːrtiːz/| n.|矮个子，短裤，（俚语）年轻人|Short people; short trousers;  informal term for young people, often women.|例句：The shorties danced enthusiastically. 矮个子们热情地跳舞。

例句：She wore cute shorties to the beach. 她穿着可爱的短裤去海滩。


近义词：shorts, little people, youngsters (for young people meaning)
反义词：tall people, elders (for young people meaning), long trousers
