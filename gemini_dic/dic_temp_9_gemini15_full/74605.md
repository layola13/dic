freaky:/ˈfriːki/| adj.|古怪的，怪异的；令人毛骨悚然的|

英文解析:  Strange, bizarre, or abnormal; causing a feeling of unease or fear.  Often used informally.

例句:

That movie was totally freaky! = 那部电影太诡异了！

He has a freaky obsession with collecting antique spoons. = 他对收集古董勺子有一种怪异的迷恋。


近义词: weird, bizarre, strange, odd, creepy, spooky

反义词: normal, ordinary, typical, conventional
