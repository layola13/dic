morelles:/mɔːˈrɛlz/| n.| 摩雷尔樱桃；黑樱桃 | Morelles are a type of dark-colored cherry, typically smaller and more tart than sweet cherries. They are often used in jams, jellies, and pies.  | Example:  The tartness of the morelles made for an excellent pie filling.  摩雷尔樱桃的酸味使它成为极好的馅饼馅料。 |近义词：black cherries, sour cherries |反义词：sweet cherries
