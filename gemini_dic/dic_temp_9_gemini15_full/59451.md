earwig: /ˈɪərwɪɡ/ | n. |蠼螋 | A small insect with a pair of forceps at the end of its abdomen.  Earwigs are often mistakenly believed to crawl into people's ears, though this is a myth. | 例句：I found an earwig in my garden. = 我在我的花园里发现了一只蠼螋。  There's a common myth that earwigs crawl into people's ears. = 有一种普遍的误解认为蠼螋会爬进人们的耳朵里。| 近义词：forficula (scientific name) | 反义词：无
