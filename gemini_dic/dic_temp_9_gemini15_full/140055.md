oesophagi:/ɪˈsɒfədʒaɪ/ | n. | 食管（复数） | The oesophagi of birds are often shorter than those of mammals. | 近义词：gullets, food pipes | 反义词：无


英文解析：The plural form of oesophagus, referring to the muscular tube connecting the pharynx to the stomach.  It is responsible for transporting food and liquids from the mouth to the stomach through peristalsis (wave-like muscle contractions).


例句：

The study examined the oesophagi of various bird species to understand their digestive processes. 这项研究检查了各种鸟类的食管以了解它们的消化过程。

Microscopic examination revealed inflammation in the oesophagi of the infected animals. 显微镜检查显示受感染动物的食管发炎。


词源：From Late Latin oesophagus, from Greek οἰσοφάγος (oisophágos), from οἴσω (oísō) "I shall carry" + φαγεῖν (phageîn) "to eat".  Literally meaning "food-carrier".
