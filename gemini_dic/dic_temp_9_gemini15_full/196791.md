sextets:/ˈsɛkstsɛts/| n.|六重奏；六行诗；六个一组的事物|A sextet is a musical composition for six voices or instruments, or a poem of six lines.  It can also refer to a group of six things.|例句：The composer wrote a beautiful sextet for strings.  这位作曲家为弦乐器写了一首优美的六重奏。

The poem is a perfect example of a Shakespearean sextet. 这首诗是莎士比亚六行诗的完美范例。|近义词：ensemble (for musical context), stanza (for poetic context)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be a composition or poem with a different number of parts.
