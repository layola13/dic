imputers:/ɪmˈpjuːtərz/| n.|归咎者，归因者，(计算机)输入者|Those who attribute something (usually blame or fault) to someone or something.  In computer science, it refers to those who input data.|例句：The imputers of the crime were finally apprehended. 犯罪的归咎者最终被逮捕了。 The imputers of data ensured accuracy before submission. 数据输入者在提交前确保了准确性。|近义词：accuser,blamer,ascriber|反义词：exonerator,absolver|名词
