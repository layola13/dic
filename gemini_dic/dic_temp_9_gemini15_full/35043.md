close-with:/kloʊz wɪð/| v. |以…结束；和…一起结束|To conclude or finish something in a particular way or with a particular person or thing.  It often implies a sense of finality or a specific action taken at the end.|例句：The ceremony closed with a prayer.  仪式以祈祷结束。|例句：He closed with a powerful final argument. 他以强有力的最后论点结束陈述。|近义词：conclude, end, finish, terminate|反义词：begin, commence, start|动词
