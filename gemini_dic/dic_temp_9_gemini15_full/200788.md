slicked:/slɪkt/| adj. v. |光滑的；油滑的；巧妙的；（头发）抹油的 |  made smooth or shiny, especially by combing or applying a substance;  (of a person) superficially charming but insincere; cleverly done or achieved | 例句：He slicked back his hair. 他把头发往后梳理得光滑。
The slicked-back hair made him look sophisticated.  油光锃亮的头发让他看起来很精致。


近义词：smooth, polished, glossy, suave, cunning, artful | 反义词：rough, unkempt, clumsy, honest, straightforward | 词性：形容词，过去分词作形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slicks his hair every morning. = 他每天早上都把头发梳理光滑。
She slicks her hair back into a ponytail. = 她把头发往后梳成马尾辫。

一般过去时:

She slicked on some lip gloss.  = 她涂了一些唇彩。

现在完成时:
He has slicked back his hair for years. = 他多年来一直把头发梳理光滑。
She has slicked her way to the top of the company. = 她凭借圆滑的处世方式爬到了公司的高层。

过去完成时:
He had slicked his hair before he met his date. = 他在见约会对象之前已经把头发梳理好了。
She had slicked the presentation before delivering it. = 她在做演示之前已经把演示文稿润色好了。

将来时:
