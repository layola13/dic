locking: /ˈlɒkɪŋ/ | /ˈlɑːkɪŋ/ | v. n. adj.| 锁定；固定；卡住；闭锁 | The act of securing something with a lock; the state of being fastened or secured; a mechanism for fastening or securing. | 近义词：fastening, securing, clamping | 反义词：unlocking, opening, releasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is locking the door. = 他正在锁门。
The mechanism is locking reliably. = 这个机构可靠地锁定了。


一般过去时:
She locked the cabinet. = 她锁上了橱柜。
The brakes locked suddenly. = 刹车突然锁死了。


现在完成时:
They have locked the safe for the night. = 他们已经把保险箱锁了一夜了。
I have been locking my bike every day. = 我每天都锁我的自行车。


过去完成时:
He had locked the door before leaving. = 他离开前已经锁上门了。
The system had locked up before we could save the file. = 系统在我们可以保存文件之前已经死机了。


将来时:
I will lock the car once I get in. = 我一进去就会锁车。
They will be locking the gates at sunset. = 他们日落时分将锁上大门。


虚拟语气:
If I locked the door, I would feel safer. = 如果我锁上门，我会感觉更安全。
I wish I had locked the car. = 我真希望我把车锁上了。


被动语态:
The door was locked from the outside. = 门是从外面锁上的。
The files are being locked to prevent access. = 文件被锁定以防止访问。


疑问句:
Is the gate locked? = 门锁上了吗？
Are all the windows locked? = 所有窗户都锁上了吗？


否定句:
Don't lock the door. = 不要锁门。
The door wasn't locked properly. = 门没有锁好。
