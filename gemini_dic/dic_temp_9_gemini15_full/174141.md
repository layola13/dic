queazily:/ˈkweɪzɪli/| adv.| 颤抖地；不安地；迟疑地 |  In a shaky or unsteady manner; nervously. It describes an action or state characterized by nervousness, uncertainty, or a lack of steadiness. |例句：He walked queasily toward the edge of the cliff. 他颤抖地走向悬崖边。

He spoke queasily, unsure of his words. 他说话吞吞吐吐的，对自己的话语没有把握。|近义词：nervously, shakily, unsteadily, hesitantly|反义词：steadily, confidently, firmly|词性：副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tenses like verbs. It describes how an action is performed.  The tense is determined by the verb it modifies.

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "queazily".  The word is used to directly modify a verb describing movement or speech.
