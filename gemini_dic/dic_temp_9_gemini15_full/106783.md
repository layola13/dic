labellum:/ləˈbɛləm/| n.|唇瓣，舌状突起|The labellum is a modified petal, often highly specialized for attracting pollinators or providing a landing platform.  It's a characteristic feature of orchids and some other flowering plants.|例句：The orchid's labellum was a vibrant purple, attracting numerous bees.  这株兰花的唇瓣是鲜艳的紫色，吸引了许多蜜蜂。|近义词：lip, petal (in certain contexts)|反义词：无
