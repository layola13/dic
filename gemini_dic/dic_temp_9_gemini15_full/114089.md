It emphasizes the greatness or excellence of the action or manner. |例句：The castle stood magnificently on the hill.  城堡雄伟地矗立在山上。

The orchestra played magnificently, captivating the audience. 管弦乐队演奏得精彩绝伦，迷住了观众。|近义词：splendidly, gloriously, superbly, wonderfully, impressively|反义词：poorly, badly, inadequately, meanly
