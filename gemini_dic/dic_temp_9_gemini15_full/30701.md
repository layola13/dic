certified:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 经认证的，合格的 |  Officially recognized or approved; having met specific standards or requirements. |  例句：He's a certified public accountant. 他是一位注册会计师。 |  例句：This product is certified organic.  该产品是经过认证的有机产品。|近义词：authorized, accredited, qualified, licensed | 反义词：uncertified, unqualified, unauthorized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is certified to teach English. = 他有资格教授英语。
The document is certified as authentic. = 该文件被认证为真实的。


一般过去时:
The software was certified last year. = 该软件去年获得了认证。
She was certified as a yoga instructor. = 她获得了瑜伽教练资格认证。


现在完成时:
They have been certified since 2020. = 他们从2020年开始就获得了认证。
The building has been certified as environmentally friendly. = 这座建筑已被认证为环保建筑。


过去完成时:
The product had been certified before it went on sale. = 该产品上市前已获得认证。
He had already been certified as a pilot before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经获得了飞行员执照。


将来时:
The company will be certified next month. = 公司下个月将获得认证。
She will have been certified by the end of the year. = 她将在年底前获得认证。


虚拟语气:
If he were certified, he could get the job. = 如果他获得了认证，他就能得到这份工作。
I wish I were certified to handle this situation. = 我希望我有资格处理这种情况。


被动语态:
The results were certified by an independent auditor. = 结果由独立审计师认证。
The building is being certified for LEED standards. = 这座建筑正在申请LEED认证。


疑问句:
Is the diamond certified? = 这颗钻石有证书吗？
Are you certified to operate this machinery? = 你有资格操作这台机器吗？


否定句:
The product is not certified. = 该产品未经认证。
He is not yet certified to practice medicine. = 他还没有行医资格。
