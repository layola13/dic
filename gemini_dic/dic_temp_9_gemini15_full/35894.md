cocooned:/kəˈkuːnd/| adj. v. |包裹的；茧状的；与世隔绝的 |Wrapped in or as if in a cocoon; sheltered or isolated from the outside world. |例句：The baby bird was cocooned in its nest. 小鸟被包裹在它的巢穴里。 The writer cocooned himself in his study for months. 那位作家把自己关在书房里好几个月。 |近义词：sheltered, isolated, protected, enveloped |反义词：exposed, vulnerable, open


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He cocoons himself in his work. = 他埋头工作。
The caterpillar cocoons itself before transforming into a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前把自己裹成茧。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
