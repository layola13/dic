centralizers:/ˈsɛntrələˌzaɪzərz/| n.|中心器；中心化装置；中心化者|A centralizer is a device or process that brings something together or concentrates it in a central location or point.  It can refer to a physical device or a more abstract concept.|例句：The company implemented new centralizers to improve data management. 公司实施了新的中心化装置以改进数据管理。|例句：The government’s policies acted as centralizers of power. 政府的政策起到了权力中心化的作用。|近义词：concentrators, consolidators|反义词：decentralizers, dispersers|名词
