postdoc:/ˈpoʊstdɑːk/| n.|博士后|A postdoctoral researcher is a researcher who has completed their doctorate (PhD) and is conducting further research, often under the supervision of a professor or senior researcher.  They typically hold a temporary position, often for a few years, to gain further experience and expertise before seeking a permanent academic position or moving into industry. |例句：She's currently a postdoc at Harvard University studying the effects of climate change. 她目前是哈佛大学的一名博士后，研究气候变化的影响。|例句：After completing his PhD, he secured a prestigious postdoc position at MIT.  完成博士学位后，他在麻省理工学院获得了一个享有盛誉的博士后职位。|近义词：postdoctoral researcher, postdoctoral fellow|反义词：undergraduate, graduate student
