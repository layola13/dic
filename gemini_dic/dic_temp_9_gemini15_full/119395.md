microelectronics:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪks/| n. |微电子学 |The branch of electronics dealing with the design and application of extremely small electronic circuits and components.  It encompasses the study and manufacturing of integrated circuits, transistors, and other miniature devices. |Microelectronics has revolutionized the computing industry. 微电子学彻底改变了计算机产业。|nanotechnology, semiconductor technology |macroelectronics |
