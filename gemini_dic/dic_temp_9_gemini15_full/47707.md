dec:/dek/| n. adj.|十二月；十进位；判定；判决|December, the twelfth month of the year; a system of numbers based on the number ten; a formal judgment or decision|例句：December is the last month of the year. 十二月是一年的最后一个月。  The judge delivered his dec on the case. 法官对该案做出了判决。|近义词：judgment, ruling, determination|反义词：undecided, indeterminate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The judge decides the case. = 法官决定此案。
December always has 31 days. = 十二月总是31天。


一般过去时:
The jury decided the verdict. = 陪审团决定了判决结果。
He decided to quit his job last month. = 他上个月决定辞职。


现在完成时:
They have decided to move to a new house. = 他们已经决定搬到新房子。
I have decided what to do. = 我已经决定做什么了。


过去完成时:
We had decided to go to the beach before the rain started. = 下雨之前我们已经决定去海滩了。
She had already decided on her major before the semester began. = 学期开始之前她就已经决定了她的专业。


将来时:
They will decide the matter tomorrow. = 他们明天将决定此事。
We will decide where to go for vacation next year. = 我们明年将决定去哪里度假。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would decide quickly. = 如果我是你，我会迅速决定。
I wish I had decided to study harder. = 我真希望我当时决定更加努力学习。


被动语态:
The case was decided by the judge. = 此案由法官决定。
The date of the meeting has been decided. = 会议日期已确定。


疑问句:
Have you decided yet? = 你决定了吗？
When will the winner be decided? = 冠军什么时候决定？


否定句:
I haven't decided what to wear. = 我还没决定穿什么。
They did not decide to participate in the project. = 他们决定不参与这个项目。


PHRASE->

decide on = 决定
decide against = 决定反对
decision-making = 决策
