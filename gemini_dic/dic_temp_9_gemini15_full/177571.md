rebreeding: /ˌriːˈbriːdɪŋ/ | n. | 再繁殖；重新育种 | The process of breeding animals or plants again, often with a specific goal in mind, such as improving a breed or restoring a population.  It can also refer to the act of breeding animals or plants that have already been bred before. | Example:  The rebreeding program for the endangered panda population has shown promising results.  再繁殖濒危熊猫种群的项目已显示出可喜的成果。 |近义词:  reproduction, breeding, propagation | 反义词: extinction, eradication
