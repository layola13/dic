词源:
From *dedicate* + *-ory*.  The word "dedicate" comes from the Latin *dēdicāre*, meaning "to declare sacred, to consecrate,"  formed from *dē-* ("down, away") and *dicāre* ("to proclaim, dedicate").  The suffix "-ory" forms adjectives meaning "of the nature of," "relating to," or "serving for."
