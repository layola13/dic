sea-lion: /ˈsiːˌlaɪən/ | n. |海狮|A marine mammal with smooth fur, flippers, and powerful jaws, belonging to the family Otariidae.  Sea lions are known for their barking vocalizations and their ability to move on land using their flippers.|例句：We saw a colony of sea lions basking in the sun on the rocks. 我们看到一群海狮在岩石上晒太阳。  The sea lion playfully barked at the tourists.  那只海狮顽皮地对着游客叫唤。|近义词：seal (although seals are a broader category)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with land mammals.
