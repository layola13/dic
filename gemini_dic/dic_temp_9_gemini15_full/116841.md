maximation:/ˌmæk.sɪˈmeɪ.ʃən/| n. |最大化；极大化|The act or process of maximizing something; making something as large or great as possible.  It often refers to increasing profit, efficiency, or output.|例句：The company's focus on maximation of profits led to cost-cutting measures. 公司专注于利润最大化导致了削减成本的措施。|例句：The goal of the project is the maximation of renewable energy resources. 项目的目标是最大限度地利用可再生能源。|近义词：optimization, amplification, intensification|反义词：minimization, reduction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company maximizes profits. = 公司最大化利润。
We maximize efficiency in our workflow. = 我们最大限度地提高工作流程效率。


一般过去时:
They maximized their investment returns. = 他们最大化了投资回报。
The engineer maximized the engine's power output. = 工程师最大化了发动机的功率输出。


现在完成时:
We have maximized our production capacity. = 我们已经最大限度地提高了生产能力。
They have maximized their market share. = 他们已经最大限度地提高了市场份额。


过去完成时:
They had maximized their resources before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经最大限度地利用了资源。
The team had maximized their efforts to meet the deadline. = 团队已经尽最大努力来赶上截止日期。


将来时:
We will maximize our use of technology. = 我们将最大限度地利用技术。
The company will maximize its profits next quarter. = 公司下个季度将最大化其利润。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would maximize our output. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们将最大化我们的产量。
I wish we could maximize our impact on the community. = 我希望我们能够最大限度地影响社区。


被动语态:
Profits were maximized through strategic planning. = 通过战略规划最大化了利润。
Efficiency is maximized by streamlining the process. = 通过简化流程来最大化效率。


疑问句:
How can we maximize our chances of success? = 我们如何才能最大限度地提高成功的机会？
What strategies can maximize productivity? = 哪些策略可以最大限度地提高生产力？


否定句:
They did not maximize their potential. = 他们没有最大限度地发挥自己的潜力。
The system does not maximize energy efficiency. = 该系统没有最大限度地提高能源效率。
