palatial: /pəˈleɪʃəl/ | adj. | 宫殿般的；宏伟的；壮丽的 |  Descriptive of something resembling a palace in grandeur, spaciousness, or magnificence.  It suggests luxury, opulence, and impressive scale. | 例句：The hotel had palatial suites with breathtaking views. 这家酒店拥有带令人叹为观止景色的豪华套房。

The new museum is a palatial building.  新的博物馆是一座宏伟的建筑。 | 近义词：magnificent, grand, splendid, majestic, imposing | 反义词：humble, modest, small, cramped
