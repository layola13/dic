fibromata:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈmeɪtə/| n. |纤维瘤(复数)|Fibromata are benign tumors composed of fibrous connective tissue.  They can occur in various parts of the body. |例句：Multiple fibromata were found during the surgical procedure. 手术过程中发现了多发性纤维瘤。|例句：The patient presented with several skin fibromata. 病人出现了几处皮肤纤维瘤。|近义词：fibroid,  fibroma (单数)|反义词：cancer (in the context of malignant tumors)|名词
