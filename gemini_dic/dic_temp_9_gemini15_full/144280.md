outsparkle:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəl/| vt.|胜过…的光彩，比…更闪亮|To surpass in brilliance or sparkle; to outshine.|例句：Her diamond necklace outsparkled all the other jewelry in the room.  她的钻石项链的光彩胜过房间里其他的珠宝。

例句：The newly polished silverware outsparkled the dull, tarnished pieces. 新擦亮的银器比那些暗淡无光的银器更闪亮。


近义词：outshine, outdazzle, eclipse|反义词：be eclipsed by, be outshone by|词性:动词
