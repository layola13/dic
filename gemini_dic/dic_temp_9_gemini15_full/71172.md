fissipedia:/ˌfɪsɪˈpiːdiə/| n. |裂足类动物；裂足亚目|A suborder of carnivorous mammals characterized by having five-toed feet with non-retractible claws.  They are generally terrestrial or semi-aquatic and include dogs, cats, bears, and weasels. |Example:  Dogs, cats, and bears are all examples of fissipedes.  裂足类动物的例子包括狗、猫和熊。|近义词：carnivora (in a broader sense)|反义词：无|
