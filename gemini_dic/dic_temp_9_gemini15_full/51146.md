despondency:/dɪˈspɒndənsi/| n. |沮丧；失望；意志消沉 |A state of low spirits caused by loss of hope or courage.|例句：His despondency was evident in his slumped shoulders and downcast eyes. 他的沮丧从他无精打采的肩膀和低垂的眼睛中显而易见。
例句：After failing the exam, she fell into a state of deep despondency. 考试不及格后，她陷入了深深的沮丧。|近义词：depression, dejection, despair, discouragement |反义词：elation, euphoria, optimism, hope |名词
