psyche-up: /ˈsaɪkiˌʌp/ | v. |  精神准备，激励自己 | To mentally prepare oneself for a demanding or challenging situation; to boost one's confidence and determination. |  例句：I need to psyche myself up before the big game. 我需要在大型比赛前做好心理准备。  |  近义词：motivate, energize, pump up, steel oneself | 反义词：discourage, demoralize, depress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He psyches himself up before every presentation. = 他每次演讲前都会做好心理准备。
She psyches up her team before the competition. = 她比赛前会激励她的团队。

一般过去时:
I psyched myself up for the marathon. = 我为马拉松做好了心理准备。
They psyched up their athletes before the final match. = 他们决赛前激励了他们的运动员。

现在完成时:
She has psyched herself up for the upcoming interview. = 她已经为即将到来的面试做好了心理准备。
He has psyched up his team for many years. = 他多年来一直激励着他的团队。

过去完成时:
I had psyched myself up for the difficult conversation. = 我已经为那场艰难的谈话做好了心理准备。
They had psyched themselves up for a long journey. = 他们已经为漫长的旅程做好了心理准备。

将来时:
I will psyche myself up for the exam tomorrow. = 我明天将为考试做好心理准备。
We will psyche up the children before the play. = 我们将在演出前激励孩子们。

虚拟语气:
If I could psyche myself up, I would feel more confident. = 如果我能做好心理准备，我会更有信心。
I wish I could psyche myself up for that difficult challenge. = 我希望我能为那个艰难的挑战做好心理准备。

被动语态:  (被动语态较少用于此词)
The team was psyched up by their coach. = 球队受到了教练的激励。（较为生硬，不常用）
The athletes were psyched up for the Olympics. = 运动员们为奥运会做好了心理准备。（更侧重于“被激励”）

疑问句:
How do you psyche yourself up for a stressful situation? = 你如何为压力大的情况做好心理准备？
Will you psyche up your friend for the speech? = 你会激励你的朋友去演讲吗？

否定句:
I couldn't psyche myself up for the meeting. = 我无法为会议做好心理准备。
He didn't psyche himself up enough for the race. = 他为比赛做的准备不足。


PHRASE->
psyche oneself up for: 为...做好心理准备
psyche up for a challenge: 为挑战做好心理准备
psyche up before a performance: 演出前做好心理准备
psyche up to do something:  鼓起勇气去做某事
get oneself psyched up:  让自己做好心理准备
