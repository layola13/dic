femtosecond:/ˈfɛmtoʊˌsɛkənd/| n. |飞秒|A femtosecond is a unit of time equal to one quadrillionth of a second (10⁻¹⁵ seconds).  It's an extremely short duration, often used in describing ultra-fast processes in physics and chemistry.  |Example:  The laser pulse lasted only a few femtoseconds. 激光脉冲只持续了几飞秒。
The chemical reaction occurred within a single femtosecond. 化学反应在一飞秒内发生。|近义词: attosecond (even shorter)|反义词:  None (as it's a unit of time, there's no direct opposite)|词性:n.
