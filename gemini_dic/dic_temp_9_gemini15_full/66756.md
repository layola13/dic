exoskeletal:/ˌɛksoʊˈskɛlətəl/| adj.|外骨骼的；外骨骼似的| Relating to or denoting an exoskeleton.  An exoskeleton is a rigid external covering for the body in some invertebrate animals, especially arthropods, providing both protection and support.|例句：The beetle's exoskeletal armor protected it from predators.  甲壳虫的外骨骼盔甲保护它免受捕食者的侵害。 The robot's exoskeletal design allowed for increased strength and mobility.  机器人的外骨骼设计使其具有更强的力量和移动性。|近义词：external, shell-like|反义词：endoskeletal
