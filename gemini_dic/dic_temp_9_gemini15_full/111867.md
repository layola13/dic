logjam:/ˈlɒɡdʒæm/| n.|阻塞；僵局；停滞|A logjam is a blockage in a river caused by a large accumulation of logs.  Figuratively, it refers to a situation where progress is stopped because of disagreement or obstacles. |例句：The negotiations reached a logjam when neither side would compromise.  谈判陷入僵局，因为双方都不愿妥协。

例句：The river was impassable due to a massive logjam.  由于大量的原木阻塞，这条河无法通行。

近义词：deadlock, impasse, standstill, bottleneck

反义词：progress, breakthrough, solution

词性：名词
