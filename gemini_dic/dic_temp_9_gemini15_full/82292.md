groupage:/ˈɡruːpeɪdʒ/ | n. |拼箱，拼装，成组运输 |The consolidation of several small shipments into one larger shipment for transport.  This reduces the cost per unit for the shipper. |例句：The company specializes in groupage shipments to Europe. 这家公司专门从事拼箱运输到欧洲。 |例句：Using groupage, we were able to significantly reduce our shipping costs. 通过拼箱，我们能够显著降低运输成本。|近义词：consolidation, combined shipment |反义词：full container load (FCL) |名词
