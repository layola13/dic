misbestows:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊz/| vt.|错置；滥用；糟蹋|To bestow wrongly or inappropriately; to use or apply something in a wrong or wasteful way.|例句：He misbestowed his talents on trivial pursuits. 他把自己的才能浪费在琐碎的事情上。|例句：The company misbestowed its resources on a failing project.  这家公司把资源错置在一个失败的项目上。|近义词：misapply, misuse, misspend, squander|反义词：bestow wisely, utilize effectively, apply judiciously|动词
