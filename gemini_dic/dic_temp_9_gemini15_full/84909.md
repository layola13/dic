hanky:/ˈhæŋki/| n. |手帕；(俚语)少量；一点点|A handkerchief, especially a small one.  The word is a shortened form of "handkerchief".  In informal contexts, it can also refer to a small amount or a little bit of something.|例句：She pulled a hanky from her pocket to wipe her tears. 她从口袋里掏出手帕擦眼泪。

例句：He only gave me a hanky of the money he owed me. 他只给了我一点点他欠我的钱。|近义词：handkerchief, tissue|反义词：N/A
