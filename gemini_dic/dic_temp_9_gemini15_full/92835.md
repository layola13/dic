icons:/ˈaɪkɒnz/ /ˈaɪkɑːnz/| n.|图标；偶像；标志|A visual representation of a file, function, or other item in a graphical user interface.  Icons can also refer to people who are highly admired or influential.|Example: The desktop icons were neatly arranged.  桌面图标排列整齐。|The application uses clear and intuitive icons. 该应用程序使用清晰直观的图标。|symbols, emblems, signs|anti-icons (this is more of a conceptual opposite, not a direct antonym)|名词
