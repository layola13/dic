meditater: /ˈmɛdəˌteɪtər/ | n. |冥想者，沉思者 | A person who practices meditation.  Someone who engages in contemplation or reflection. | 例句：The meditater sat quietly, focusing on her breath.  冥想者静静地坐着，专注于她的呼吸。

例句：Experienced meditators often report feeling a sense of peace and tranquility.  经验丰富的冥想者经常报告说感到平静和安宁。


近义词：contemplator, reflector, thinker | 反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider someone very active or distracted.


词源:  Derived from the verb "meditate," ultimately from Latin "meditari" meaning "to think, ponder, plan."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He meditates every morning. = 他每天早上冥想。
She meditates to relieve stress. = 她冥想以缓解压力。

一般过去时:
I meditated for an hour yesterday. = 我昨天冥想了一个小时。
They meditated before the exam. = 他们考试前冥想。

现在完成时:
He has meditated for years. = 他冥想多年了。
She has meditated daily since last month. = 自从上个月以来，她每天都冥想。

过去完成时:
I had meditated before I went to sleep. = 我睡觉前冥想过。
They had meditated for hours before the decision. = 他们在做出决定之前冥想了几个小时。

将来时:
I will meditate tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会冥想。
She will meditate regularly to improve her focus. = 她会定期冥想以提高专注力。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would meditate more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地冥想。
I wish I could meditate without distractions. = 我希望我能不受干扰地冥想。

被动语态:  The verb "meditate" is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a self-initiated action.  (A passive construction would sound unnatural.)


疑问句:
Do you meditate regularly? = 你定期冥想吗？
Does he meditate before important meetings? = 他在重要的会议前冥想吗？

否定句:
I don't meditate often enough. = 我冥想不够频繁。
She didn't meditate yesterday. = 她昨天没有冥想。
