overrun:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/| vt. n.|超过；泛滥；侵扰|To spread over and cover (a surface)；To extend beyond the limits of (something)；To overwhelm or defeat (someone or something)|例句：The weeds have overrun the garden.  杂草已经蔓延到整个花园。 The project was overrun by several months. 项目超期了好几个月。|近义词：invade, overwhelm, flood|反义词：underestimate, control


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weeds overrun the garden. = 杂草蔓延到花园里。
The project often overruns its budget. = 这个项目经常超支。


一般过去时:
The army overrun the enemy's territory. = 军队占领了敌人的领土。
The flood overrun the village. = 洪水淹没了村庄。


现在完成时:
The insects have overrun the crops. = 昆虫已经侵害了庄稼。
The costs have overrun the initial estimate. = 成本已经超过了最初的估计。


过去完成时:
The rebels had overrun the city before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，叛军已经占领了城市。
The project had overrun its deadline by a week. = 项目已经超期一周了。


将来时:
The flood will overrun the low-lying areas. = 洪水将淹没低洼地区。
They fear the budget will overrun next year. = 他们担心明年预算会超支。


虚拟语气:
If the army had overrun the city, the war would have ended quickly. = 如果军队占领了城市，战争就会迅速结束。
If the costs had not overrun, the project would have been profitable. = 如果成本没有超支，项目本来就会盈利。


被动语态:
The garden was overrun by weeds. = 花园被杂草淹没了。
The budget was overrun. = 预算超支了。


疑问句:
Has the project overrun its budget? = 项目预算超支了吗？
Was the village overrun by the flood? = 村庄被洪水淹没了吗？


否定句:
The weeds did not overrun the entire garden. = 杂草并没有蔓延到整个花园。
The project did not overrun its deadline. = 项目没有超期。


PHRASE->
overrun with = 充满...；挤满...
overrun by = 被...淹没；被...侵占
overrun the schedule = 超出进度
overrun the budget = 超出预算
overrun the deadline = 超出期限
