integrative:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 综合的；整合的；一体化的 | Combining or coordinating different things into a whole.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness of various elements or disciplines. | 例句：The course takes an integrative approach, combining theory and practice. 这门课程采用综合方法，将理论和实践结合起来。

例句：His research uses an integrative methodology, drawing on insights from psychology, sociology, and anthropology. 他的研究采用了一种综合方法，借鉴了心理学、社会学和人类学方面的见解。


近义词： holistic, comprehensive, unified, combined, synergistic

反义词： fragmented, isolated, disparate, disjointed, separated

词性：形容词
