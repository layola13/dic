euplectella:/juːplɛˈkɛlə/| n. |偕老同穴(一种玻璃海绵)|A genus of deep-sea glass sponges, characterized by their intricate, lattice-like skeletons.  They are also known as Venus' flower baskets because of their delicate, vase-like shape.  Some species are inhabited by a pair of shrimp that live their entire lives within the sponge.|The euplectella is a fascinating example of symbiosis. |近义词:glass sponge, Venus' flower basket|反义词:N/A
