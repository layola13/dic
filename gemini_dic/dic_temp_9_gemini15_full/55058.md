disrate:/dɪsˈreɪt/| vt.| 降级；降低…的级别；贬低；使…失去名誉 |To reduce someone or something in rank, status, or estimation; to degrade or devalue.|例句：The captain was disrated for his incompetence.  船长因其不称职而被降级。|例句：His actions disrated him in the eyes of his colleagues. 他的行为使他在同事眼中名誉扫地。|近义词：demote, downgrade, devalue, discredit|反义词：promote, upgrade, enhance, honor|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company disrates employees who consistently miss deadlines. = 公司会降低那些总是错过最后期限的员工的级别。
He disrates the importance of education. = 他贬低教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
They disrated him after he made a serious mistake. = 他犯了一个严重的错误后，他们降了他的职。
The general disrated the soldier for cowardice. = 将军因士兵胆怯而降了他的职。


现在完成时:
The government has disrated several officials for corruption. = 政府因腐败问题已降低了几位官员的级别。
She has disrated his contributions to the project.  = 她贬低了他对项目的贡献。


过去完成时:
They had disrated him before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们就已经降了他的职。
The company had disrated the product before it went on sale. = 这家公司在产品上市前就降低了它的级别。


将来时:
They will disrate him if he continues to perform poorly. = 如果他继续表现不佳，他们将会降他的职。
The board will disrate the proposal if it lacks feasibility. = 如果提案缺乏可行性，董事会将降低它的级别。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to make mistakes, he would be disrated. = 如果他继续犯错，他将会被降级。
If they had known his true character, they would have disrated him earlier. = 如果他们早知道他的真实性格，他们就会更早地降他的职。


被动语态:
He was disrated for his unprofessional conduct. = 他因不专业的行为而被降级。
The plan was disrated due to lack of funding. = 该计划由于缺乏资金而被搁置。


疑问句:
Will he be disrated for his misconduct? = 他会因为不当行为而被降级吗？
Was the project disrated because of the delays? = 项目是因为延误而被降级吗？


否定句:
They did not disrate him despite his mistakes. = 尽管他犯了错误，他们也没有降他的职。
The committee did not disrate the proposal although it had some flaws. = 尽管该提案有一些缺陷，委员会并没有降低它的级别。
