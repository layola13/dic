self-sold:/ˌsɛlfˈsoʊld/| adj.| 自销的；自行销售的 |  Describes goods or services sold directly by the producer or creator, without intermediaries like retailers or distributors.  It implies a direct connection between the seller and the buyer, often suggesting a more personal or customized approach. | Example: The artist sold her paintings self-sold at the local art fair.  这位艺术家在当地的艺术博览会上自销她的画作。|近义词：directly sold, independently sold |反义词：retail-sold, wholesale-sold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He self-sells his pottery online. = 他在网上自销他的陶器。
The farmer self-sells his produce at the market. = 这位农民在市场上自销他的农产品。

一般过去时:
She self-sold her handmade jewelry at craft fairs. = 她曾在工艺品展销会上自销她手工制作的珠宝。
They self-sold their excess crops to local restaurants. = 他们曾将多余的农作物自销给当地餐馆。

现在完成时:
He has self-sold hundreds of paintings through his website. = 他通过他的网站已经自销了数百幅画作。
The company has self-sold all its inventory this quarter. = 公司本季度已经自销了所有库存。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, she had self-sold all her artwork. = 到年底，她已经自销了她所有的艺术品。
Before opening the shop, they had self-sold their goods online for two years. = 在开店之前，他们已经在网上自销商品两年了。

将来时:
We will self-sell our products at the upcoming trade show. = 我们将在即将到来的贸易展销会上自销我们的产品。
They will self-sell their services through social media marketing. = 他们将通过社交媒体营销自销他们的服务。

虚拟语气:
If I were an artist, I would self-sell my creations. = 如果我是艺术家，我会自销我的作品。
I wish I could self-sell my book and avoid the publisher. = 我希望我能自销我的书并避免出版商。

被动语态:
The products are self-sold by the artisans. = 这些产品由工匠们自销。
The tickets were self-sold through the band's website. = 门票通过乐队的网站自销。

疑问句:
Does the company self-sell its products? = 公司自销其产品吗？
Are you planning to self-sell your artwork? = 你计划自销你的艺术品吗？

否定句:
The company does not self-sell its products; it uses distributors. = 公司不自行销售其产品；它使用经销商。
They didn't self-sell their goods; they used a wholesaler. = 他们没有自销商品；他们使用了批发商。
