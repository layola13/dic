parkways:/ˈpɑːrkwēz/ | n. | 公园大道，林荫大道 | A parkway is a wide road, often landscaped with trees and other vegetation, typically designed for recreational driving.  It may have divided lanes and limited access. | Example: We took a scenic drive along the parkway. 我们沿着公园大道进行了风景优美的驾车旅行。 | 近义词：boulevard, avenue, highway | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a narrow, unlandscaped road.
