call-back:/ˈkɔːlˌbæk/|/ˈkɑːlˌbæk/| n. v.|回电；回调；反馈；召回|A call-back is a return phone call, often in response to a previous call or message.  It can also refer to a function in programming that is called back after an event, or the act of recalling a product due to defects.|例句：I left a message and am waiting for a call-back. 我留了言，正在等待回电。

The company issued a call-back for all its defective products. 公司对所有有缺陷的产品进行了召回。


近义词：return call, follow-up call, recall|反义词：initial call, first contact|名词，动词
