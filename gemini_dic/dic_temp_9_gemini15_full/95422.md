incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentəli/| adv.|顺便说一句；附带地 | By the way; as a matter of incidental interest.  It is used to introduce a piece of information that is not the main topic of conversation, but is related to it in some way. | 例句：Incidentally, I saw your brother yesterday. 顺便说一句，我昨天看到你兄弟了。

Incidentally, did you remember to water the plants? 顺便问一下，你记得浇花了吗？

近义词：by the way, incidentally, in passing, as an aside,  

反义词：N/A (no direct antonym)

词性:副词
