gurgles:/ˈɡɜːrɡlz/ | v. |咕噜咕噜地响，发出汩汩声 |To make a sound like water flowing quickly over stones or through a narrow space.  It can also describe the sound of someone struggling to speak or the sound of liquid moving in the stomach. |例句：The stream gurgled merrily over the smooth stones.  溪流愉快地发出汩汩声流过光滑的石头。

The baby gurgled contentedly as she drank her milk.  婴儿喝奶时发出满足的咕噜声。

近义词：bubble, gurgle, murmur, ripple |反义词： roar, crash
