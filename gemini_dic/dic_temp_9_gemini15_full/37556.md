commonwealths:/ˈkɒmənˌwɛlθs/ | /ˈkɑːmənˌwɛlθs/ | n. |英联邦诸国；联邦；自治体 |  A commonwealth is a self-governing state that is a member of the Commonwealth of Nations.  It usually implies a shared history and allegiance to the British Crown, although this is not always the case.  The term can also refer to a political community founded for the common good or a republic. |例句：The Commonwealth Games bring together athletes from many commonwealths. 英联邦运动会汇集了来自许多英联邦国家的运动员。|例句：Many former British colonies are now independent commonwealths.许多前英国殖民地现在都是独立的英联邦国家。|近义词：federations, republics, states |反义词：empire, monarchy (in the sense of a single, centralized rule) |名词
