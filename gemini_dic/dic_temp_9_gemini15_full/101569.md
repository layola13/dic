ironwoods: /ˈaɪərnˌwʊdz/ | n. |  铁木树；铁木属植物 |  A type of tree with very hard, dense wood, often belonging to the genus *Ostrya* (hop-hornbeams) or *Licania*.  Ironwoods are known for their durability and strength. |  例句：The fence posts were made of incredibly durable ironwood.  篱笆柱是用极其耐用的铁木做的。

例句：Many species of ironwood are found in tropical and subtropical regions. 许多种类的铁木产于热带和亚热带地区。


近义词：hardwood, tough wood | 反义词：softwood


词性: 名词
