pair-off:/pɛərˈɔːf/ | v. | 成对；配对；分成对 | To form into pairs; to match or associate in pairs.  | 例句：The students paired off to work on the project. 学生们配对合作完成项目。
The dancers paired off before the performance. 舞蹈演员们在演出前配对。| 近义词：couple, match, team up | 反义词：separate, divide individually
