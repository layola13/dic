septoic:/ˈsɛptɪk/| adj.|败血症的；感染的；腐败的 | Relating to or caused by sepsis (the presence of pus-forming bacteria in the blood or tissues).  Septic conditions are characterized by the presence of harmful bacteria and their toxins in the bloodstream, often leading to serious illness. | Example: The patient developed a septic wound after the surgery. 手术后病人伤口感染化脓。|近义词：infected, contaminated, purulent |反义词：aseptic, sterile, clean
