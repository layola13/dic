flyover:/ˈflaɪˌoʊvər/ | n. |高架桥；高架公路；飞越；俯瞰 |A flyover is a structure that carries a road or railway over another road or railway, typically at an intersection. It can also refer to a brief aerial view or flight over a place.  |例句：The new flyover will alleviate traffic congestion. 新建的高架桥将缓解交通拥堵。

The plane made a flyover of the city.  飞机飞越了这座城市。|近义词：overpass, bypass, viaduct |反义词：underpass
