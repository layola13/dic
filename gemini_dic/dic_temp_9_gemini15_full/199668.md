sinus:/ˈsaɪnəs/| n. |鼻窦；正弦曲线；弯曲；凹陷|A sinus is a cavity or hollow space, especially one in a bone, such as the air-filled spaces in the bones of the skull.  It can also refer to a curve in mathematics.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's sinuses. 医生检查了病人的鼻窦。

例句：The graph shows a sinusoidal wave.  图表显示了一个正弦波。|近义词：cavity, hollow, recess |反义词：protrusion, prominence |词性:名词
