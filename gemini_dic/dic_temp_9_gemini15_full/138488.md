novene:/nəˈviːn/| n.|九日祈祷|A novena is a nine-day period of prayer, often dedicated to a particular saint or intention.  It typically involves saying specific prayers or devotions each day. |Example: She completed a novena to Saint Jude to ask for help finding a job. 她完成了一次向圣裘德祈祷的九日经，以祈求找到工作。|近义词：devotion, prayer series|反义词：None
