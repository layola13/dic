They are often invoked by poets for inspiration.  The term can also refer to poets themselves, especially those of exceptional skill.|例句：The poet invoked the Pierides for inspiration in his latest work. = 这位诗人在他的最新作品中祈求缪斯女神的灵感。  The young Pierides of the academy showed great promise. = 学院里的年轻诗人们展现出非凡的才华。|近义词：Muses, poets|反义词：无
