porter:/ˈpɔːrtər/ /ˈpɔːrtɚ/| n. |搬运工；行李搬运工；门房；（啤酒）黑啤酒|A porter is a person whose job is to carry luggage or goods, especially at a railway station, airport, or hotel.  A porter can also refer to a type of dark beer.|例句：The porter helped me carry my heavy suitcase to my room.  搬运工帮我把沉重的行李箱搬到了我的房间。

The hotel porter was very friendly and helpful.旅馆门房非常友好和乐于助人。|近义词：carrier, baggage handler, doorman,  (for beer) stout|反义词：N/A
