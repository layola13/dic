save:/seɪv/| vt. n.| 保存；拯救；节省；储蓄 | to keep something safe from harm or loss; to rescue someone from danger; to store something for later use; to avoid wasting something | 例句：He saved the drowning child. 他救了那个溺水的孩子。  

例句：I saved the document to my computer. 我把文件保存到了我的电脑上。


近义词：rescue, preserve, conserve, store | 反义词：waste, destroy, lose, squander


词性:动词(vt.)，名词(n.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saves money every month. 他每个月都存钱。
She saves all her emails. 她保存所有邮件。

一般过去时:
They saved the cat from the tree. 他们把猫从树上救了下来。
I saved the file before closing the program. 我在关闭程序前保存了文件。

现在完成时:
I have saved enough money for a vacation. 我已经攒够了度假用的钱。
She has saved many lives during her career as a doctor.  在她当医生的职业生涯中，她已经挽救了许多人的生命。

过去完成时:
He had saved a lot of money before he bought the house. 他买房子之前存了很多钱。
We had saved the project file before the computer crashed. 电脑崩溃之前我们已经保存了项目文件。

将来时:
They will save the planet by reducing carbon emissions. 他们将通过减少碳排放来拯救地球。
I will save this photo for later. 我会保存这张照片以备后用。

虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a car. 如果我存了更多的钱，我就能买一辆车了。
I wish I had saved that email. 我真希望我保存了那封邮件。

被动语态:
The document was saved automatically. 文件已自动保存。
Many lives were saved by the firefighters. 消防员们救了很多人的命。

疑问句:
Did you save the game before quitting? 你退出游戏前保存了吗？
Have you saved your work? 你保存你的工作了吗？

否定句:
I didn't save the file. 我没有保存文件。
He hasn't saved any money this month. 这个月他没存任何钱。
