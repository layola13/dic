chocolate-box: /ˈtʃɒkələt bɒks/ | /ˈtʃɑːklət bɑːks/ | adj. | 巧克力盒式的；完美的；理想化的 |  Descriptive of something idyllically picturesque and charming, often to the point of being artificial or overly sentimental.  It evokes images of quaint villages, charming cottages, and scenes often depicted on chocolate boxes. | 例句：The village was a chocolate-box scene, with thatched cottages and a babbling brook.  这个村庄就像巧克力盒上的画面一样，有着茅草屋和潺潺的小溪。

The countryside looked incredibly chocolate-box perfect.  乡村景色看起来完美得像巧克力盒上的图画一样。


近义词：picturesque, idyllic, charming, quaint, pretty, idealized

反义词：ugly, grim, unpleasant, realistic, stark
