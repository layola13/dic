self-contained:/ˌselfkənˈteɪnd/ | adj. | 自给自足的；独立的；独立运行的 |  Containing everything necessary within itself; independent and not needing outside help.  | 例句：The cabin is completely self-contained, with its own water supply and electricity generator. 这间小屋完全自给自足，拥有自己的供水和发电机。

The software is self-contained and doesn't require any additional libraries.  这个软件是独立运行的，不需要任何额外的库文件。

| 近义词：independent, autonomous, self-sufficient, complete | 反义词：dependent, reliant, interconnected | 词性：形容词
