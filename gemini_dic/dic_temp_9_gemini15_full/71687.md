flaunts:/flɔːnts/| vt.|炫耀，夸示|To show off something in a proud or boastful way.  |例句：She flaunted her wealth by driving a luxurious car. 她开着一辆豪华轿车炫耀她的财富。

例句：He flaunted his knowledge of ancient history. 他炫耀他对古代史的知识。


近义词：show off, boast, parade, display

反义词：hide, conceal, suppress, downplay

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flaunts his new car every chance he gets. = 他抓住一切机会炫耀他的新车。
She flaunts her success to everyone she meets. = 她向遇到的每一个人炫耀她的成功。


一般过去时:
He flaunted his medals at the reunion. = 他在聚会上炫耀他的奖章。
She flaunted her artistic skills at the party. = 她在派对上炫耀她的艺术才能。


现在完成时:
He has flaunted his wealth throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都炫耀他的财富。
She has flaunted her connections to influential people. = 她一直炫耀她与有影响力的人的关系。


过去完成时:
He had flaunted his achievements before the interview. = 他在面试前就炫耀了他的成就。
She had flaunted her beauty to attract attention. = 她为了吸引注意而炫耀她的美貌。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:

词源:
The word "flaunt" ultimately derives from the Old French word "flambant," meaning "flaming" or "brightly colored."  This connection suggests an original meaning related to displaying something brightly and conspicuously, which evolved into the modern sense of boastful display.
