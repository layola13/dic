contention:/kənˈtɛnʃən/| n. |争论；争辩；论点；论题 |The act of contending; strife; a struggle; contest; dispute; quarrel.  It can also refer to a point or statement made in an argument. |例句：The main contention of his argument was that the government was inefficient.  他的论点主要在于政府效率低下。

例句：There was considerable contention over the inheritance.  遗产继承问题引起了很大的争议。


近义词：dispute, argument, disagreement, conflict, controversy |反义词：agreement, harmony, accord, consensus |词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contention is that the plan is flawed. = 争论的焦点在于该计划有缺陷。
Their contention centers around the budget. = 他们的争论集中在预算上。


一般过去时:
The contention was resolved peacefully. = 争论和平地解决了。
Their contention lasted for hours. = 他们的争论持续了数小时。


现在完成时:
The contention has been ongoing for years. = 这场争论已经持续多年了。
This contention has led to many problems. =  这场争论导致了许多问题。


过去完成时:
The contention had already been settled before the meeting. = 会议之前争论就已经解决了。
By then, the contention had escalated into a full-blown fight. = 到那时，争论已经升级成一场全面的争斗。


将来时:
The contention will likely continue for some time. = 这场争论很可能会持续一段时间。
We predict that the contention will be resolved soon. = 我们预测这场争论很快就会解决。


虚拟语气:
If there were no contention, the project would proceed smoothly. = 如果没有争论，项目将会顺利进行。
I wish there were less contention in the workplace. = 我希望工作场所的争论少一些。


被动语态:
The contention was unfairly dismissed by the judge. = 法官不公平地驳回了这一论点。
The contention is being debated by experts. = 专家们正在讨论这一论点。


疑问句:
What is the main contention of this essay? = 这篇文章的主要论点是什么？
Is there any contention regarding the new policy? = 关于这项新政策有任何争议吗？


否定句:
There was no contention during the negotiation. = 在谈判期间没有争论。
The team showed no signs of contention. =  团队没有表现出任何争论的迹象。



PHRASE->
point of contention: 争论点
subject of contention: 争论的主题
ground for contention: 争论的理由
cause for contention: 争论的原因
bone of contention: 争论的焦点 (idiom)


词源:
源自拉丁语 *contendere，意思是“努力，奋斗”。  它与英语单词“contend”（竞争，争论）密切相关。  “Contention”作为名词，发展演变为指争论或争辩本身。
