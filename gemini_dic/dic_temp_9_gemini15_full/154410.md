permissible:/pəˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可允许的，许可的 |  Admissible; allowed; permitted.  Something that is permissible is allowed or acceptable according to rules or laws. | The use of mobile phones is permissible in this area.在这个区域允许使用手机。|  allowed, acceptable, legitimate | forbidden, prohibited, illegal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That action is permissible. = 那个行为是可以被允许的。
This method is permissible under the current regulations. = 根据现行规定，这种方法是允许的。

一般过去时:
The judge ruled that the evidence was permissible. = 法官裁定该证据是可以接受的。
That behavior was permissible in those days. = 在那些日子里，那种行为是可以被接受的。


现在完成时:
Many things have become permissible since the law changed. = 自从法律改变以来，许多事情都变得可以被接受了。
The committee has ruled that the proposed changes are permissible. = 委员会已裁定拟议的更改是可以接受的。


过去完成时:
It had been permissible to smoke in that building until last year. = 直到去年，在那栋建筑物里吸烟是被允许的。
The lawyer argued that the action had been permissible under the previous law. = 律师辩称，根据之前的法律，该行为是被允许的。


将来时:
Will it be permissible to bring guests? = 可以带客人来吗？
The new regulations will make many previously impermissible actions permissible. = 新规将使许多以前不被允许的行为变得可以被允许。


虚拟语气:
If it were permissible, I would attend. = 如果允许的话，我会参加。
If it had been permissible, I would have gone. = 如果允许的话，我早就去了。


被动语态:
The use of cameras is not permissible. = 不允许使用相机。
The document states that certain actions are not permissible. = 文件规定某些行为是不允许的。


疑问句:
Is this procedure permissible? = 这个程序允许吗？
Are outside drinks permissible at the event? = 活动允许自带饮料吗？


否定句:
That's not permissible! = 那是不允许的！
The plan is not permissible under current guidelines. = 根据目前的指导方针，该计划是不允许的。
