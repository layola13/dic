microcephalous:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛfələs/ | adj. |小头畸形的；小头的 | Having an abnormally small head.  This condition is often associated with incomplete brain development. | Example: The child was diagnosed with microcephalous at birth. 这个孩子出生时被诊断为小头畸形。 The infant presented with classic symptoms of microcephalous, including a small head circumference and developmental delays.  这个婴儿表现出小头畸形的典型症状，包括头围小和发育迟缓。|近义词：small-headed |反义词：macrocephalic
