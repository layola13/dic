nonreferences:/ˌnɒnˈrefərənsɪz/ | n. | 非参考文献；未引用的资料 |  Materials or information not cited or referenced in a scholarly work.  This can include background information, general knowledge, or personal observations that are not directly supported by published sources. | Example: The essay contained numerous nonreferences, weakening its academic credibility.  这篇文章包含许多未引用的资料，削弱了它的学术可信度。 | 近义词：uncited sources, undocumented information | 反义词：references, citations | 名词
