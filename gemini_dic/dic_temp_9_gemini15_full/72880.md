follow-ons: /ˈfɒləʊɒnz/ | n. |后续行动，后续事件，后续产品| Subsequent events, actions, or products that follow a previous event, action, or product.  They often refer to things that continue or expand upon something that has already happened or been created. |例句：The company is excited about the follow-ons to their successful product launch. 公司对其成功产品发布后的后续产品感到兴奋。

The success of the initial project led to several promising follow-ons. 初始项目的成功导致了一些有希望的后续行动。|近义词：sequels, continuations, subsequent developments|反义词：precursors, antecedents


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are the follow-ons to this policy change? = 这一政策变化的后续措施是什么？
Are there any planned follow-ons to this research project? = 这个研究项目有什么计划中的后续工作吗？


一般过去时:
What were the follow-ons from the summit meeting? = 峰会后的后续行动是什么？
What follow-ons did the team implement after the initial findings? = 团队在初步调查结果后实施了哪些后续措施？


现在完成时:
What follow-ons have been developed since the initial product launch? = 自初始产品发布以来，已经开发了哪些后续产品？
Have there been any significant follow-ons to the recent legislation? = 最近的立法有哪些重要的后续措施？


过去完成时:
What follow-ons had been considered before the final decision was made? = 在做出最终决定之前，都考虑过哪些后续行动？
What follow-ons had the company implemented before the market crash? = 在市场崩盘之前，该公司已经实施了哪些后续措施？


将来时:
What follow-ons will the government take to address the crisis? = 政府将采取哪些后续措施来应对这场危机？
What follow-ons will we see in the next phase of the project? = 我们将在项目的下一阶段看到哪些后续行动？


虚拟语气:
If the project were successful, there would be many exciting follow-ons. = 如果项目成功，将会有许多令人兴奋的后续行动。
I wish we had foreseen the negative follow-ons of that decision. = 我希望我们预见到那个决定的负面后续影响。


被动语态:
Several follow-ons are being developed to improve the original design. = 正在开发一些后续产品来改进原始设计。
The necessary follow-ons were being considered by the board. = 董事会正在考虑必要的后续措施。


疑问句:
What are the likely follow-ons of this political development? = 这一政治发展可能带来的后续影响是什么？
Are there any planned follow-ons to the current marketing campaign? = 当前营销活动有什么计划中的后续行动吗？


否定句:
There were no immediate follow-ons to the announcement. = 声明没有立即产生任何后续行动。
The company did not anticipate such negative follow-ons to their new policy. = 公司没有预料到其新政策会带来如此负面的后续影响。
