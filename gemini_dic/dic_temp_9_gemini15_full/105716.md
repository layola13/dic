kinship:/ˈkɪnʃɪp/| n. |血缘关系；亲属关系；族类；血统|The state of being related; family relationship; a feeling of kinship or connection with others.|例句：There is a strong sense of kinship among the members of the tribe.  部落成员之间有着强烈的血缘关系。|例句：Our kinship with other primates is evident in our DNA. 我们与其他灵长类动物的血缘关系在我们的DNA中显而易见。|近义词：relationship, family ties, affinity, blood relation|反义词：estrangement, alienation
