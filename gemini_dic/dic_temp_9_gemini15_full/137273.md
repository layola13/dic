nonsympathizer: /ˈnɒnˈsɪmpəθaɪzər/ | n. |不同情者，不同情的人| A person who lacks sympathy or compassion for others; someone who does not support or agree with a particular cause or group. | Example:  He was considered a nonsympathizer by his colleagues due to his lack of empathy during the crisis.  他因为在危机期间缺乏同理心而被同事们认为是一个冷漠的人。|近义词：unsympathetic person, apathetic person, unfeeling person |反义词：sympathizer, compassionate person, empathetic person
