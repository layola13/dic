half-cotton: /ˈhæf ˈkɑːtn/ | adj. | 半棉的 |  Half-cotton refers to a fabric or textile that is composed of 50% cotton and 50% of another fiber, such as polyester, nylon, or linen.  The exact blend varies depending on the manufacturer and intended use. | Example:  The shirt is made of a comfortable half-cotton blend. 这件衬衫是由舒适的半棉混纺面料制成的。|近义词:cotton-blend, part-cotton|反义词:pure cotton, 100% cotton |形容词
