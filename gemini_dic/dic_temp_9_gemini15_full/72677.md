foaming:/ˈfoʊmɪŋ/|/ˈfəʊmɪŋ/| adj. |起泡沫的，泡沫状的|Producing or covered with foam.  The action or process of forming foam.|The soapy water was foaming.肥皂水起泡了。 The sea was foaming furiously. 海浪汹涌澎湃地翻滚着泡沫。|frothy, bubbly, sudsy|still, calm|形容词
