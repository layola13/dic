corresponder:/ˌkɔːrɪˈspɒndə(r)/| n. v.| 通信者；对应者；符合者 |  A person who corresponds with another; something that corresponds to something else.  |例句：He is a frequent corresponder with his pen pal in France. 他经常与他在法国的笔友通信。  |近义词：writer, correspondent, equivalent |反义词：discrepant, different


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two maps correspond closely. = 这两张地图非常吻合。
His account of the event corresponds with hers. = 他对事件的描述和她的一致。

一般过去时:
Their descriptions of the accident corresponded in most details. = 他们对事故的描述在大多数细节上是一致的。
The address on the letter corresponded to the one on file. = 信上的地址与档案中的地址相符。

现在完成时:
My observations have corresponded with the predictions. = 我的观察结果与预测相符。
The results of the two studies have corresponded well. = 两项研究的结果非常吻合。

过去完成时:
The evidence corresponded with what he had already suspected. = 证据与他之前已有的怀疑相符。
The witness testimony corresponded with what had been discovered earlier. = 目击证人的证词与之前发现的情况相符。

将来时:
The results of the experiment should correspond to our predictions. = 实验的结果应该与我们的预测相符。
The two datasets will likely correspond in many aspects. = 这两个数据集很可能在许多方面相符。

虚拟语气:
If the two sets of data corresponded, it would support our hypothesis. = 如果两组数据相符，那将支持我们的假设。
If the accounts corresponded, the mystery would be solved. = 如果账目相符，谜团就能解开。

被动语态:
The two documents were found to correspond exactly. = 发现这两份文件完全一致。
The data points were shown to correspond to a linear trend. = 数据点显示与线性趋势相符。

疑问句:
Do your findings correspond to the existing literature? = 你的研究结果与现有文献相符吗？
Does the information provided correspond to the reality? = 提供的信息与现实相符吗？

否定句:
His statement did not correspond to the facts. = 他的陈述与事实不符。
The witness accounts did not correspond with each other. = 目击证人的证词彼此不符。



PHRASE->
correspond with = 与…通信
correspond to = 与…相符；相当于
correspond in = 在…方面相符
in correspondence with = 与…通信中
enter into correspondence with = 开始与…通信
