词源:
From Middle English *ornaten, from Old French orner "to adorn, furnish," from Latin ornare "to equip, furnish, adorn," from *or- "fit, equip" + -nare, suffix of inchoative verbs.  The word's evolution reflects a consistent focus on embellishment and decoration.
