droned:/drəʊnd/ | v. |嗡嗡作响；单调乏味地说话；用无人机飞行|To make a continuous low humming sound; to speak in a monotonous tone; to fly using a drone.|The bees droned lazily in the summer air.  蜜蜂在夏日的空气中懒洋洋地嗡嗡作响。


The lecturer droned on for hours, losing the attention of his audience.  演讲者滔滔不绝地讲了几个小时，失去了听众的注意力。


近义词：hummed, buzzed, mumbled, monotoned | 反义词：shouted, exclaimed, articulated clearly
