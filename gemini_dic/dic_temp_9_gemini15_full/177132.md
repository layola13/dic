reappropriates:/ˌriːəˈproʊprieɪts/| vt.| 挪用；重新诠释；盗用 |To take something for one's own use that was originally intended for another purpose or group.  This often involves altering the original meaning or context.  It can be a positive act of reclaiming cultural identity or a negative act of exploitation, depending on context.|例句：The artist reappropriates images from popular culture in her work.  艺术家在她的作品中挪用了流行文化中的图像。


The Indigenous community reappropriated the term, stripping it of its negative connotations and imbuing it with a sense of pride.  土著社区重新诠释了这个词，去除了它的负面含义，并赋予它一种自豪感。

近义词：reclaim, repurpose, reinterpret, appropriate (in a negative sense)

反义词：abandon, relinquish, forsake
