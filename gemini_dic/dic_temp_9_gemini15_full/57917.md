driller:/ˈdrɪlər/| n. |钻孔机；钻探者；钻头|A driller is a person or machine that drills holes.  Drillers are used in various industries, from construction to oil exploration.  A rock driller is a specific type of driller used in mining and quarrying.|例句：The construction worker used a powerful driller to make holes for the support beams.  建筑工人用一台强大的钻孔机为支撑梁打孔。

The oil driller spent months searching for new sources of petroleum.  石油钻探者花了几个月的时间寻找新的石油来源。|近义词：borer, digger|反义词：filler (in the context of filling holes)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The driller drills holes in the rock. = 钻孔机在岩石上钻孔。
He drills holes every day. = 他每天都钻孔。
