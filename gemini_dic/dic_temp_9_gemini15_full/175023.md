radiocarbon-dated: /ˌreɪdiˈoʊkɑːrbənˌdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 经放射性碳测年法的；用碳十四测定的 |  Describes something whose age has been determined using radiocarbon dating, a method that analyzes the amount of carbon-14 remaining in organic material to estimate its age.  |例句：The radiocarbon-dated artifacts were found to be approximately 3,000 years old.  经放射性碳测年法的文物被发现大约有3000年的历史。


The radiocarbon-dated bones provided evidence of early human settlement in the region.  经放射性碳测年法的骨骼为该地区早期人类定居提供了证据。| 近义词：carbon-dated | 反义词：undated
