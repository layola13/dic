mab:/mæb/ | n. |单克隆抗体|Monoclonal antibody (mAb) is a laboratory-produced antibody that is identical and targets a specific antigen.  These are used in various medical applications, including cancer treatment and other immune-related diseases.|例句：This new mab is showing promising results in treating advanced lung cancer.  这种新的单克隆抗体在治疗晚期肺癌方面显示出令人鼓舞的结果。|近义词：monoclonal antibody, antibody|反义词：polyclonal antibody
