imbreed:/ɪmˈbriːd/| vt.|近亲繁殖|To breed (animals or plants) from closely related individuals, especially over many generations.|例句：The farmer decided to imbreed his prize-winning horses to maintain their desirable traits.  这个农民决定让他的获奖种马近亲繁殖以保持其理想的特性。|例句：Continuous imbreeding can lead to genetic defects and reduced vitality in the offspring.  持续的近亲繁殖会导致后代出现基因缺陷和活力下降。|近义词：inbreed, interbreed (with a nuance of breeding within a smaller group)|反义词：outbreed|动词
