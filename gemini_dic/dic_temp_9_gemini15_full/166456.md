prereviewing: /ˌpriːrɪˈvjuːɪŋ/ | v. | 预先查看；预审 | To view or examine something beforehand, often to assess its quality or suitability before a main viewing or presentation.  | 例句：The committee prereviewed the proposals before the final meeting. 委员会在最终会议之前预先审查了这些提案。


例句：The film critic prereviewed the movie and gave a positive early assessment.  电影评论家预先观看了这部电影，并给出了积极的早期评价。


近义词：pre-screening, previewing, pre-examining


反义词：postviewing, reviewing (after the main event)


词性:动词
