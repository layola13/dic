jumpstart:/ˈdʒʌmpˌstɑːrt/ | vt. n. | 快速启动；使迅速发展；给…以动力 | To give something a sudden increase in activity or progress.  |例句：The new CEO jumpstarted the company's sales. 新任CEO使公司的销售额迅速增长。 |例句：We need to jumpstart the economy. 我们需要刺激经济。|近义词：boost, revitalize, stimulate |反义词：halt, hinder, impede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new marketing campaign jumpstarts sales. = 新的营销活动刺激了销售额。
He jumpstarts his car every morning. = 他每天早上都用搭电启动他的车。


一般过去时:
The government jumpstarted the economy with a stimulus package. = 政府通过刺激计划来刺激经济。
She jumpstarted her career by taking on a challenging project. = 她通过承担一个具有挑战性的项目来推动她的职业生涯。


现在完成时:
The new technology has jumpstarted the company's growth. = 新技术促进了公司的增长。
They have jumpstarted their fundraising efforts. = 他们已经启动了他们的筹款工作。


过去完成时:
The company had jumpstarted its research and development before the market crash. = 公司在市场崩盘前就启动了研发工作。
He had jumpstarted his writing career with a successful short story. = 他凭借一篇成功的短篇小说启动了他的写作生涯。


将来时:
The new policy will jumpstart the development of renewable energy. = 新政策将促进可再生能源的发展。
They will jumpstart the engine with a booster pack. = 他们将用搭电器启动发动机。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could jumpstart the project. = 如果我们有更多的资金，我们就能启动这个项目。
I wish we could jumpstart the negotiations. = 我希望我们能够启动谈判。


被动语态:
The economy was jumpstarted by government intervention. = 经济因政府干预而得到刺激。
The car was jumpstarted using jumper cables. = 汽车用搭电线启动了。


疑问句:
Can we jumpstart this project before the deadline? = 我们能在截止日期前启动这个项目吗？
How can we jumpstart the stalled negotiations? = 我们如何才能启动停滞的谈判？


否定句:
The company failed to jumpstart its declining sales. = 公司未能扭转其下降的销售额。
They couldn't jumpstart the car because the battery was dead. = 他们无法启动汽车，因为电池没电了。
