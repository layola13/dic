letterings:/ˈlɛtərɪŋz/ | n. | 字母设计；刻字；题字 | The art or process of designing and arranging letters, especially for inscriptions or decorative purposes.  It often involves the style, size, and spacing of letters. | Example: The elegant letterings on the monument were a testament to the sculptor's skill.  纪念碑上优雅的题字证明了雕刻家的技艺。The shop offers custom letterings for signs and banners. 这家商店提供定制的招牌和横幅字母设计。|近义词：inscription, lettering style, calligraphy, typography |反义词：无
