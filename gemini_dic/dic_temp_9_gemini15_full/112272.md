lophophore:/ˈlɒpəfɔːr/ | n. |腕足 | A horseshoe-shaped or circular structure bearing tentacles, found in many invertebrate animals, used for feeding and respiration.  It is a crown of ciliated tentacles used in filter feeding. | Example: The lophophore of a brachiopod is a remarkable adaptation for capturing microscopic food particles.  腕足动物的腕足是捕获微小食物颗粒的非凡适应。|近义词:tentacle crown (部分情况)|反义词:  None readily available.
