freebootery:/ˈfriːˌbuːtəri/| n. | 掠夺；海盗行为；强盗行为 | The act of plundering or pillaging, especially by a band of armed individuals; robbery; piracy.  It often implies a lawless and violent taking of goods or property. | Example: The island was frequently the target of freebootery from passing ships.  这个岛屿经常成为过往船只掠夺的目标。 | 近义词：plunder, piracy, brigandage, marauding | 反义词：peaceful trade, lawful acquisition
