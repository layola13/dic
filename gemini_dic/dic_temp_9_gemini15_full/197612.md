shepherdesses:/ˈʃɛpədəsɪz/| n. |牧羊女们，女牧羊人|The plural of shepherdess, referring to multiple women who tend sheep.  It evokes a sense of pastoral life and often appears in literature and art depicting idyllic rural scenes.|例句：The shepherdesses led their flocks to greener pastures. 牧羊女们把羊群带到了更青翠的牧场上。
The painting depicted three shepherdesses playing flutes under a willow tree.  这幅画描绘了三个牧羊女在柳树下吹笛子的场景。|近义词：shepherds (if gender is not specified), herdwomen|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting urban or industrial life could be considered contrasting.|名词
