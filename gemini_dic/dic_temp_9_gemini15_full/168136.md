pro-Australians: /ˌproʊ ɔːˈstreɪliən/ | adj. | 支持澳大利亚人的；亲澳大利亚的 |  Supporting or favoring Australians and Australian interests.  This term typically refers to a political stance or sentiment. |  例句：The pro-Australians in the debate emphasized the importance of national sovereignty.  支持澳大利亚人在辩论中强调了国家主权的重要性。


例句：Many pro-Australian policies were enacted during the time of economic prosperity.  在经济繁荣时期，许多亲澳大利亚政策被颁布。


近义词：Australia-supporting, pro-Australian government, patriotic (in relation to Australia)


反义词：anti-Australian, Australia-bashing

词性：形容词
