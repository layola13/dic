nuraghe:/nuˈrɑːɡeɪ/| n.|努拉格斯（撒丁岛古代塔楼）|/nʊˈrɑːɡi/|  |努拉格，古代塔楼|A nuraghe is a type of prehistoric tower found in Sardinia, Italy.  They are made of stone and are believed to have been built by the Nuragic people between the 19th and 11th centuries BC.  Their purpose is still debated, with theories ranging from defensive structures to religious sites or even residences.|The nuraghe of Barumini is a UNESCO World Heritage site.|近义词：tower, fortification, structure|反义词：无
