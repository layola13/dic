disbark:/dɪsˈbɑːrk/ | vt. |使（动物）下船；使（人）下车；解雇|To remove (a person or animal) from a ship, vehicle, or aircraft.|The passengers disbarked from the ferry onto the bustling pier.  乘客们从渡轮下船，来到了熙熙攘攘的码头上。|The captain disbarked the unruly passenger for his disruptive behavior. 船长因其扰乱秩序的行为而将不守规矩的乘客赶下了船。|embark, disembark |--|
