converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ | vi. | 汇聚；趋同；收敛 | To move towards the same point from different directions or to become similar or the same.  | The two roads converge at the traffic lights.  两条路在交通灯处汇合。 |Several factors converged to cause the crisis.好几个因素共同导致了这场危机。| diverge, deviate | diverge, separate |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lines converge at a single point.  这些线在一个点汇聚。
Rivers converge to form larger streams.  河流汇聚形成更大的水流。


一般过去时:
The protesters converged on the government building.  抗议者聚集在政府大楼。
All the roads converged at the town square. 所有道路都在城镇广场汇合。


现在完成时:
The evidence has converged to support the theory.  证据已经汇聚起来支持这个理论。
Many opinions have converged on this issue.  许多意见在这个问题上达成一致。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, the birds had converged in a large flock.  暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已汇聚成一大群。
By the end of the meeting, opinions had converged on a single solution.  会议结束时，意见已达成一致。


将来时:
The two armies will converge on the battlefield tomorrow.  两支军队将于明天在战场上会师。
The delegates will converge in Geneva next week. 代表们下周将在日内瓦会聚。


虚拟语气:
If all the roads converged here, it would cause significant traffic congestion. 如果所有道路都在这里汇合，那将造成严重的交通拥堵。
If the data converged, we would have a clearer picture. 如果数据趋于一致，我们将会有更清晰的图景。


被动语态:
The opinions were converged into a single consensus.  意见最终达成共识。
The different viewpoints were carefully converged in the report. 报告中仔细地整合了不同的观点。


疑问句:
Do these lines converge at any point?  这些线在任何点上汇合吗？
Where do the three rivers converge? 三条河流在哪里汇合？


否定句:
The opinions did not converge during the discussion.  讨论中意见没有达成一致。
The paths do not converge at this point.  路径在此处不相交。



PHRASE->

converge with: 与……会合
converge around:  围绕……汇聚
convergent evolution: 趋同进化
