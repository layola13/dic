filmiest:/ˈfɪlmiːst/| adj.|最薄的，最像电影的，最虚幻的 |Most resembling film; extremely thin; unreal or flimsy.  The adjective is the superlative form of "filmy," meaning resembling a film or thin layer. It can also describe something that seems unreal or lacking substance. |例句：The filmiest of veils barely concealed her identity. 最薄的面纱几乎遮不住她的身份。

The filmiest of dreams faded as reality set in. 最虚幻的梦境随着现实的到来而消散。|近义词：thinnest, sheerest, flimsiest, most tenuous |反义词：thickest, densest, most substantial
