cogitatingly:/ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪtɪŋli/|/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪŋli/| adv.|沉思地；若有所思地|In a thoughtful or pensive manner; while cogitating.  This adverb describes an action performed while deeply thinking or pondering.|He sat cogitatingly by the fire, lost in thought.|近义词：thoughtfully, reflectively, pensively|反义词：thoughtlessly, carelessly
