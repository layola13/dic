overheat:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːt/ | vt. |过热；使过热 |To become excessively hot; to cause something to become excessively hot.  |例句：The engine overheated and stalled.  发动机过热并熄火了。|例句：Don't overheat the oil. 不要把油烧得太热。|近义词：superheat, scorch |反义词：cool down, chill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine overheats easily in hot weather. = 发动机在炎热的天气下很容易过热。
He overheats the food every time he cooks. = 他每次做饭都把食物烧焦了。


一般过去时:
The computer overheated and shut down. = 电脑过热并关机了。
My car overheated on the highway. = 我的车在高速公路上过热了。


现在完成时:
The engine has overheated several times this summer. = 这个夏天发动机已经过热好几次了。
She has overheated the milk and it's curdled. = 她把牛奶烧糊了，现在结块了。


过去完成时:
The system had overheated before the technician arrived. = 技术员到达之前系统已经过热了。
The engine had overheated, causing the car to break down. = 发动机已经过热，导致汽车抛锚。


将来时:

The engine will likely overheat if you don't check the coolant level. = 如果你不检查冷却液水平，发动机很可能会过热。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the engine would overheat, I would have checked the coolant. = 如果我知道发动机会过热，我会检查冷却液。
I wish I hadn't overheated the chocolate; now it's ruined. = 我真希望我没有把巧克力烧糊；现在它毁了。

疑问句:

否定句:

I didn't overheat the sauce this time. = 我这次没有把酱汁烧糊。
