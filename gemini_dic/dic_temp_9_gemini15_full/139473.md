obstinate:/ˈɒbstɪnət/ /ˈɑːbstənət/| adj.|固执的，倔强的|Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.  Characterized by stubborn persistence or resistance.|例句：He was obstinate in his refusal to admit his mistake. 他固执地拒绝承认自己的错误。

例句：Despite all the evidence, she remained obstinate in her belief. 尽管有所有的证据，她仍然固执地相信自己的想法。


近义词：stubborn, headstrong, intractable, inflexible, persistent

反义词：yielding, compliant, amenable, flexible, docile

词性：形容词
