misclaimed:/ˌmɪsˈkleɪmd/| v.|错误声称，误称|To claim something incorrectly or falsely.  This involves asserting ownership, rights, or facts that are not true.|例句：He misclaimed ownership of the land, leading to a lengthy legal battle. 他错误地声称拥有这块土地，导致了一场漫长的法律纠纷。

例句：The company misclaimed the effectiveness of its new product, resulting in customer disappointment and legal action.  这家公司错误地夸大了其新产品的功效，导致客户失望并面临法律诉讼。


近义词：misrepresent, misstate, falsely claim, wrongly assert


反义词：accurately claim, correctly state, truthfully assert


词性：动词
