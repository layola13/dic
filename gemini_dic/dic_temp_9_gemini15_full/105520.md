kilometers:/kɪˈlɒmɪtərz/| n.|千米，公里|A kilometer is a unit of length equal to 1000 meters.  It's commonly used in many countries as the standard unit for measuring distances.|The distance between the two cities is approximately 300 kilometers. = 这两座城市之间的距离大约是300公里。

The race is 10 kilometers long. = 比赛长10公里。


近义词：kilometres(英式拼写)
反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distance is 5 kilometers. = 距离是5公里。
It takes 2 kilometers to get to school. = 到学校要走2公里。


一般过去时:
We walked 10 kilometers yesterday. = 昨天我们走了10公里。
The journey was 20 kilometers long. = 这段旅程长20公里。


现在完成时:
I have run 5 kilometers this week. = 我这周跑了5公里。
They have cycled over 100 kilometers. = 他们骑自行车超过100公里了。


过去完成时:
We had already run 5 kilometers before we stopped. = 我们停下来之前已经跑了5公里了。
She had driven over 100 kilometers before the car broke down. =  她的车坏掉之前已经开了100多公里了。


将来时:
We will walk 15 kilometers tomorrow. = 我们明天将走15公里。
They will cycle 30 kilometers next weekend. = 下周末他们将骑自行车30公里。


虚拟语气:
If I ran 10 kilometers, I would be exhausted. = 如果我跑10公里，我会精疲力尽。
I wish I could run 5 kilometers without stopping. = 我希望我能一口气跑5公里。


被动语态:
The distance was measured in kilometers. = 距离是用公里来衡量的。
The race is usually run over 20 kilometers. = 比赛通常在20多公里处进行。


疑问句:
How many kilometers is it to the city center? = 到市中心有多少公里？
How far did you run in kilometers? = 你跑了多少公里？


否定句:
The distance is not 10 kilometers, it's longer. = 距离不是10公里，更远。
We didn't run 5 kilometers, only 3. = 我们没有跑5公里，只有3公里。
