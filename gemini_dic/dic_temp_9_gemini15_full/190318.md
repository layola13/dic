sarcode:/ˈsɑːrkəʊd/| n. |肉质原生质；细胞质|The living substance of a cell, especially of a protozoan.  Sarcode is often used interchangeably with protoplasm, but it specifically emphasizes the gelatinous, semi-fluid nature of the cell's contents.  |例句：The amoeba moved by extending its sarcode.变形虫通过延伸它的原生质移动。|例句：The scientist observed the sarcode of the single-celled organism under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下观察了单细胞生物的原生质。|近义词：protoplasm, cytoplasm |反义词：无
