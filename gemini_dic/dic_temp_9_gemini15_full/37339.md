commencing:/kəˈmɛnsɪŋ/| v. |开始，着手 | 开始；启动；着手进行 | The meeting is commencing at 2 p.m.  会议将于下午2点开始。 |The project is commencing next week.  项目将于下周启动。|starting, beginning, initiating|ending, finishing, concluding|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are commencing the project today. = 我们今天开始这个项目。
They commence their journey tomorrow. = 他们明天开始他们的旅程。

一般过去时:
The ceremony commenced at noon. = 典礼在中午开始。
He commenced his speech with a joke. = 他以一个笑话开始了他的演讲。

现在完成时:
We have commenced the negotiations. = 我们已经开始了谈判。
They have commenced their studies. = 他们已经开始了他们的学习。

过去完成时:
The play had commenced before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，戏剧已经开始了。
The rain had commenced before the game started. =  比赛开始前就已经下起了雨。

将来时:
The concert will commence at 8 pm. = 音乐会将于晚上8点开始。
Work on the new building will commence next month. = 新建筑的施工将于下个月开始。

虚拟语气:
If we commenced now, we could finish on time. = 如果我们现在开始，我们就能按时完成。
I wish we had commenced the project earlier. = 我希望我们早点开始这个项目。

被动语态:
The meeting was commenced by the chairman. = 会议由主席开始。
The investigation has been commenced. = 调查已经开始。


疑问句:
When will the meeting commence? = 会议什么时候开始？
Has the construction commenced yet? = 建筑工程已经开始了吗？

否定句:
The game did not commence until late. = 比赛直到很晚才开始。
They have not yet commenced their work. = 他们还没有开始工作。
