/ˌoʊvərˈweɪstɪd/ | adj. |过度浪费的；被过度使用的| excessively wasted; depleted beyond reasonable use.  The overwasted resources led to the project's failure.  过度浪费的资源导致了项目的失败。|近义词：squandered, depleted, exhausted |反义词：conserved, preserved, efficient

This resource is overwasted in many industries. = 这种资源在许多行业中被过度浪费。
