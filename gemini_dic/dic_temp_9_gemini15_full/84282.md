half-time:/ˈhɑːfˌtaɪm/ | n. |中场休息；半场；半时 |The interval between the two halves of a game or match.  It can also refer to the period of time that is half of a specified duration.|例句：The halftime show featured a popular band. 中场表演请到了一支很受欢迎的乐队。

The team scored two goals in the second half after a disappointing halftime.  球队在下半场进了两球，此前中场休息时表现令人失望。|近义词： intermission, interval, break |反义词：full time, entire game
