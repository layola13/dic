professes:/prəˈfɛsɪz/ | vt. |公开宣称；声称；信奉；自称 |To declare or affirm openly; to claim; to profess a belief or faith; to claim a certain skill or quality. |例句：He professes his innocence. 他宣称自己是无辜的。|例句：She professes a deep love for her country. 她公开表示对祖国的热爱。|近义词：avow, declare, assert, claim |反义词：deny, refute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He professes his love for her daily. = 他每天都表达他对她的爱。
The company professes a commitment to environmental protection. = 这家公司声称致力于环境保护。


一般过去时:
She professed ignorance of the matter. = 她声称对这件事一无所知。
He professed to be a skilled musician. = 他自称是一位技艺精湛的音乐家。


现在完成时:
He has professed his faith in God throughout his life. = 他一生都信奉上帝。
They have professed their support for the new policy. = 他们已经表示支持这项新政策。


过去完成时:
She had professed her love for him before he left. = 他离开之前，她已经向他表达了爱意。
They had professed their innocence to the police. = 他们之前已经向警方宣称自己是无辜的。


将来时:
He will profess his love to her tomorrow. = 他明天将向她表白。
The company will profess its commitment to transparency. = 公司将宣称致力于透明化。


虚拟语气:
If he professed his love, she might accept him. = 如果他表白，她可能会接受他。
I wish he professed his feelings more openly. = 我希望他能更公开地表达他的感情。


被动语态:
His innocence was professed by him repeatedly. = 他反复宣称自己是无辜的。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The company's commitment to sustainability is professed in their annual report. = 公司对可持续发展的承诺体现在他们的年度报告中。


疑问句:
Does he profess any religious beliefs? = 他信奉任何宗教信仰吗？
Does the company profess to be ethical? = 这家公司声称自己是道德的吗？


否定句:
He does not profess to be an expert. = 他并不自称是专家。
She did not profess any interest in the project. = 她对这个项目没有表现出任何兴趣。


PHRASE->
profess ignorance = 宣称不知情
profess one's faith = 表达信仰
profess one's love = 表达爱意
openly profess = 公开宣称
profess to be = 自称是
