fierce:/fɪərs/| adj.|凶猛的；强烈的；激烈的|fierce means showing a lot of strong emotion, especially anger. It can describe people, animals, or even things like storms or competition.  |例句：The lion let out a fierce roar.  狮子发出一声凶猛的吼叫。

The competition was fierce. 竞争非常激烈。|近义词：intense, ferocious, violent, aggressive|反义词：gentle, mild, calm, meek
