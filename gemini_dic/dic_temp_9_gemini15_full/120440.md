minnesinger:/ˈmɪnɪˌsɪŋər/| n. |吟游诗人（中世纪德国）|A Minnesinger was a German lyric poet and composer of the medieval period, typically a knight or nobleman, who composed and performed songs, often about courtly love. Their works were typically set to music and performed at courts and tournaments.|The Minnesingers' songs often depicted idealized courtly love and chivalry.|近义词：troubadour, trouvère|反义词：N/A
