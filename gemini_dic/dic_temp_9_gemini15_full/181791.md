relinquish:/rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃ/| vt.|放弃，放手，让与|To voluntarily give up the possession, use, or control of something.|例句：He relinquished his claim to the throne. 他放弃了他的王位继承权。|例句：She reluctantly relinquished control of the company to her son. 她不情愿地把公司控制权交给了儿子。|近义词：abandon, surrender, cede, forgo|反义词：retain, keep, hold, claim
