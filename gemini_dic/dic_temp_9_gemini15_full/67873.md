fabrication:/ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|捏造；虚构；制造；伪造|The act of inventing or producing something, especially in a false or misleading way;  the process of manufacturing or constructing something.|The news report was later found to be a complete fabrication.  新闻报道后来被发现完全是捏造的。 The fabrication of the bridge took several years.  这座桥的建造花费了几年时间。|invention, fabrication, construction, forgery|truth, reality|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company fabricates car parts. = 这家公司制造汽车零件。
She fabricates stories to impress people. = 她编造故事来给别人留下深刻印象。

一般过去时:
They fabricated a false alibi. = 他们捏造了一个不在场的证明。
The artist fabricated a beautiful sculpture. = 艺术家制作了一个美丽的雕塑。

现在完成时:
He has fabricated a lot of lies over the years. = 多年来，他编造了很多谎言。
The team has fabricated a new prototype. = 团队已经制作了一个新的原型。

过去完成时:
They had fabricated the evidence before the trial. = 他们在审判前伪造了证据。
She had fabricated a convincing excuse for her absence. = 她为自己的缺席编造了一个令人信服的借口。

将来时:
They will fabricate the components next week. = 他们下周将制造这些部件。
She will fabricate a response to the accusations. = 她将编造一个回应来反驳这些指控。

虚拟语气:
If they fabricated the data, the results would be invalid. = 如果他们伪造数据，结果将无效。
I wish they hadn't fabricated that story. = 我希望他们没有编造那个故事。

被动语态:
The documents were fabricated by a rival company. = 文件是由竞争对手公司伪造的。
The prototype is being fabricated in the factory. = 原型正在工厂里制造。

疑问句:
Were the claims fabricated or genuine? = 这些说法是捏造的还是真实的？
Is the evidence being fabricated? = 证据是否正在被伪造？

否定句:
The report did not fabricate any information. = 该报告没有捏造任何信息。
They did not fabricate the results of the experiment. = 他们没有伪造实验结果。
