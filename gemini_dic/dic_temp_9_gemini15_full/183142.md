reposted:/ˌriːˈpoʊstɪd/| v. |再次发布，转发|To post (something) again, typically on a social media platform.  This often implies sharing content originally posted by someone else.|例句：I reposted the article on my Instagram. 我在我的Instagram上转发了这篇文章。

例句：She reposted her friend's tweet about the concert. 她转发了她朋友关于音乐会的推文。


近义词：reshared, retweeted, shared again|反义词：deleted, removed|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reposts funny memes every day. = 他每天都转发有趣的迷因。
They repost news articles regularly. = 他们定期转发新闻文章。


一般过去时:
I reposted that video yesterday. = 我昨天转发了那个视频。
She reposted his Instagram story. = 她转发了他的Instagram故事。


现在完成时:
I have reposted several of her tweets this week. = 我本周已经转发了她好几条推文。
They have reposted the announcement on all their platforms. = 他们已经在他们所有的平台上转发了这个公告。


过去完成时:
He had reposted the picture before I saw it. = 我看到之前，他已经转发了这张照片。
She had reposted the article before the controversy started. = 在争议开始之前，她就已经转发了这篇文章。


将来时:
I will repost your update tomorrow. = 我明天会转发你的更新。
They will repost the winning entry on their website. = 他们会在他们的网站上转发获奖作品。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would repost that amazing photo. = 如果我是你，我会转发那张令人惊叹的照片。
I wish I had reposted that important information sooner. = 我希望我早点转发那个重要的信息。


被动语态:
The photo was reposted thousands of times. = 这张照片被转发了数千次。
The announcement was reposted across multiple social media sites. = 这则公告在多个社交媒体网站上被转发。


疑问句:

否定句:
