peduncled:/pɪˈdʌŋkld/| adj.| 有花梗的；具花梗的 | Having a peduncle.  A peduncle is a stalk that supports a single flower or an inflorescence (a cluster of flowers).  The term describes structures that are attached to a stem by a stalk. | Example: The peduncled flowers swayed gently in the breeze.  这些有花梗的花在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词: stalked |反义词: sessile (lacking a stalk)
