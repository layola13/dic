noncombatant:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːmbətənt/| n. |非战斗人员，平民| A person who is not involved in fighting, especially in a war.  Noncombatants are civilians, medical personnel, and other individuals not directly participating in hostilities. |Example:  Civilians are considered noncombatants and should be protected during wartime.  平民被认为是非战斗人员，在战时应该受到保护。
|近义词：civilian, bystander |反义词：combatant, soldier
