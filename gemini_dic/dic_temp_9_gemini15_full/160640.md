portends: /pɔːˈtɛndz/ | vt. | 预示；预兆 | to be a sign that something, especially something bad, will happen in the future.  | The dark clouds portend a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。 | foreshadow, augur, presage |  (no direct antonym, but opposites could include:  bode well, promise well) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gathering dark clouds portend rain. = 乌云密布预示着要下雨。
These events portend a period of instability. = 这些事件预示着一段动荡时期。


一般过去时:
The strange silence portended trouble. = 奇怪的寂静预示着麻烦。
Ancient oracles portended the fall of empires. = 古代的预言预示着帝国的灭亡。


现在完成时:
Recent economic indicators have portended a recession. = 最近的经济指标预示着经济衰退。
The rising sea levels have portended serious environmental consequences. = 海平面上升预示着严重的生态环境后果。


过去完成时:
The uneasy atmosphere had portended the upcoming conflict. = 紧张的气氛预示着即将到来的冲突。
Historical records had portended the devastating earthquake. = 历史记录预示着那场破坏性地震。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the upcoming election will portend significant policy changes. = 分析师预测即将到来的选举将预示着重大的政策变化。
Experts believe that the current trends will portend a global energy crisis. = 专家们认为目前的趋势将预示着全球能源危机。


虚拟语气:
If the signs portended disaster, we should have heeded them. = 如果这些迹象预示着灾难，我们应该注意它们。
Were the omens to portend a war, we would prepare for defense. = 如果预兆预示着战争，我们将做好防御准备。


被动语态:
A period of economic hardship was portended by the recent job losses. = 最近的失业预示着一时期经济困难。
The collapse of the bridge was portended by earlier cracks in the structure. = 大桥的倒塌早被桥体上的裂缝所预示。


疑问句:
Does this ominous silence portend danger? = 这种不祥的寂静预示着危险吗？
What do the latest polls portend for the election? = 最新的民调对选举有什么预示？


否定句:
The initial reports did not portend any serious consequences. = 初步报告没有预示任何严重后果。
The calm before the storm did not portend the violence that followed. = 风暴前的平静并没有预示随后的暴力。
