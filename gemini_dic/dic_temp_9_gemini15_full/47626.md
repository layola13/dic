debilitants:/dɪˈbɪlɪtənts/| n.|使衰弱的事物；使虚弱的因素|Things that weaken or impair strength or function.  Debilitants can refer to diseases, conditions, or substances that cause weakness, debility, or exhaustion.|例句：The long illness was a debilitating experience. 长期疾病是一次使人衰弱的经历。
例句：Chronic pain can be a significant debilitant. 慢性疼痛可能是严重的致弱因素。|近义词：weakeners, impairers, enervators|反义词：tonics, strengtheners, invigorators|名词
