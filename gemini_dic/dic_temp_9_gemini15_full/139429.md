obsessiveness:/əbˈsɛsɪvnəs/ | n. | 过度执着；强迫性；着迷 | The state or quality of being obsessive; an excessive preoccupation with a particular thought, idea, or activity.  It often involves repetitive thoughts or behaviors that are difficult to control. | His obsessiveness with cleanliness made it difficult for him to maintain healthy relationships.  他的洁癖让他难以维持健康的人际关系。|  fixation, preoccupation, compulsion | detachment, indifference, unconcern | 名词
