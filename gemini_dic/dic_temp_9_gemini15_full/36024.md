codirect:/ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛkt/| vt.|共同执导，协同导演|To direct (a film, play, etc.) jointly with another person or other people.  It implies shared responsibility and creative input.|例句：The film was codirected by two acclaimed directors. 这部电影由两位著名的导演共同执导。

例句：They codirected a successful Broadway production. 他们共同执导了一部成功的百老汇作品。


近义词：jointly direct, collaborate on directing, share direction of


反义词：solely direct, independently direct


词性：动词
