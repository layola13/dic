pelmanism:/ˈpɛlmənɪzəm/| n. |培尔曼记忆法；记忆术|A mnemonic system, often involving the use of cards with paired pictures, designed to improve memory.  It's named after its inventor, Christopher Pelman.|Example: He used pelmanism to memorize long lists of names and numbers. 他用培尔曼记忆法来记忆长长的姓名和数字列表。|近义词：memory training, mnemonic technique|反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia
