quasi-popular:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpɑːpjələr/|/ˈkwɑːziː ˈpɒpjʊlə(r)/| adj. |准流行的；似是而非的流行的 |Something that is quasi-popular is almost but not quite popular; it has some of the characteristics of popular culture but lacks widespread appeal.  It might be popular within a specific niche or subculture, but not among the general population. |Example:  The band enjoyed quasi-popular success, gaining a dedicated following but never achieving mainstream recognition.  这个乐队获得了准流行的成功，赢得了忠实的粉丝，但从未获得主流认可。|近义词：niche, moderately popular, somewhat popular |反义词：mainstream, universally popular, unpopular
