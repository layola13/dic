folklores:/ˈfoʊkˌlɔrz/| n.|民间传说，民俗|The body of traditional customs, beliefs, and stories of a community, passed through generations by word of mouth.|例句：The book explores the rich folklores of the Appalachian Mountains. 这本书探究了阿巴拉契亚山脉丰富的民间传说。|近义词：legends, myths, traditions|反义词：reality, facts

The community preserves its folklores. = 这个社群保存着它的民间传说。
