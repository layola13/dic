selfed:/sɛld/ | adj. | 自交的；自花授粉的 |  Referring to a plant or organism that has undergone self-fertilization or self-pollination.  It implies reproduction involving only one parent. | Example: The selfed plants produced offspring with less genetic diversity. 自交的植物产生了遗传多样性较低的子代。 |近义词：inbred, self-fertilized | 反义词：outcrossed, cross-pollinated | 词性:形容词
