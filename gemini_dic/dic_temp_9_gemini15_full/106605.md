kufic:/ˈkuːfɪk/| n. |库法体；库尔德体|A style of Arabic script characterized by its angular, geometric forms, developed in Kufa, Iraq, in the 7th century.  It is known for its simplicity and lack of flourishes.|The inscription on the ancient stone was written in Kufic script.|近义词：Arabic script, angular script|反义词：Naskh script (a more cursive style)
