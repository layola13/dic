pseudo-buddhists:/ˈsuːdoʊˈbʊdɪsts/| n.|假佛教徒；挂名佛教徒|Those who claim to be Buddhists but do not follow the core tenets of Buddhism.|例句：Some pseudo-buddhists focus solely on meditation techniques without embracing the ethical principles of the faith.  一些假佛教徒只专注于冥想技巧，而不接受佛教的伦理原则。|近义词：imposters, charlatans, pretenders|反义词：true Buddhists, devout Buddhists
