hydrophid: /ˈhaɪdrəfɪd/ | n. | 水蛇；水蛇属动物 | A hydrophid is a type of venomous sea snake belonging to the family Hydrophiidae.  They are characterized by their flattened tails and adaptations for aquatic life.  Many species are highly venomous. | Example: The venom of the hydrophid caused immediate paralysis in its prey.  水蛇的毒液导致其猎物立即瘫痪。 | 近义词: sea snake (泛指) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑陆生蛇类) | 词性: 名词
