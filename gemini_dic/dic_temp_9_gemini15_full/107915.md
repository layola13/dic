larvas: /ˈlɑːrvəz/ | n. | 幼虫；幼体 | The immature, typically wormlike, feeding stage of an insect that undergoes complete metamorphosis.  Larvae are often very different in appearance and habits from the adult form. | 例句：The butterfly larvae feed on leaves. 蝴蝶幼虫以叶子为食。

The mosquito larvae wriggled in the stagnant water. 蚊子幼虫在死水中蠕动。


近义词：young,  immature stage,  grub (informal) | 反义词：adult, imago


词性: 名词
