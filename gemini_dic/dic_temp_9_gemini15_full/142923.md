out-take:/ˈaʊtˌteɪk/| n. |未采用的镜头；NG镜头；花絮|A piece of film or video that is not used in the final version.  It often refers to scenes or shots that were filmed but deemed unsuitable for the final cut of a movie, television show, or other production.|例句：The out-takes from the movie were funnier than the movie itself.  电影的未采用的镜头比电影本身更有趣。
The director showed some hilarious out-takes at the end of the screening.  导演在放映结束后放了一些搞笑的NG镜头。|近义词：deleted scene, blooper, discarded footage|反义词：final cut, master shot
