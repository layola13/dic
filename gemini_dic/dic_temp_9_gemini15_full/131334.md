noncharacterized:/ˌnɒnˈkærəktəraɪzd/ | adj. |未表征的，未鉴定的|Not yet identified or classified.  The term often refers to genes, proteins, or other biological entities whose function or characteristics are unknown.  |例句：Many genes in the human genome remain noncharacterized.  许多人类基因组中的基因仍然未被表征。

The researchers found several noncharacterized proteins in their samples. 研究人员在样本中发现了几种未表征的蛋白质。


近义词：unidentified, unknown, unclassified, undescribed


反义词：characterized, identified, classified


词性:形容词
