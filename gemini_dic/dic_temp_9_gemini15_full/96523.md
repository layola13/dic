indoctrinators:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪtərz/ | n. |灌输者，教条主义者 |Those who indoctrinate others, especially with a particular ideology.  People who systematically teach someone to accept a set of beliefs without questioning them. |例句：The government used indoctrinators to spread propaganda and suppress dissent.  政府利用灌输者传播宣传并压制异见。


例句：Religious indoctrinators often try to convert others to their faith. 宗教灌输者经常试图将他人转化为他们的信仰。


近义词：propagandists, teachers (in a negative context), brainwashers


反义词：educators (in a positive context), open-minded individuals


词性：名词
