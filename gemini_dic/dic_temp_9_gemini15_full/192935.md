sebiparous:/ˈsɛbaɪpərəs/| adj.| 胎生的；卵胎生的 | Producing young alive, either by bearing live young (viviparous) or by retaining eggs within the body until they hatch (ovoviviparous).  | Example:  The sebiparous snake gave birth to live young. 这条卵胎生的蛇产下了活的幼蛇。 | 近义词：viviparous, ovoviviparous | 反义词：oviparous
