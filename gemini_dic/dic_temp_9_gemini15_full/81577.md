grease:/ɡriːs/| n. vt.|润滑油，油脂，贿赂|a thick, oily substance, typically used as a lubricant.|The mechanic greased the car's engine.  机械师给汽车发动机上了润滑油。|oil, lubricant, fat|dry, clean|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He greases the gears every week. = 他每周都给齿轮上油。
She greases the baking pan to prevent sticking. = 她给烤盘涂油以防粘住。

一般过去时:
I greased the hinges to make them quieter. = 我给铰链上油使其更安静。
They greased the wheels of the cart. = 他们给手推车的轮子上油。

现在完成时:
We have greased the machine, so it should run smoothly now. = 我们已经给机器上过油了，所以现在它应该运行平稳。
She has greased her palms to get the contract. = 她为了得到合同行贿了。

过去完成时:
The mechanic had greased the engine before he started the car. = 机械师在启动汽车之前已经给发动机上过油了。
They had greased the tracks to make the train run faster. = 他们为了使火车跑得更快，给轨道上过油。

将来时:
I will grease the bicycle chain tomorrow. = 我明天会给自行车链条上油。
He will grease the palms of the officials to get the permit. = 他会贿赂官员以获得许可证。

虚拟语气:
If I had greased the wheels, the cart would have moved more easily. = 如果我给轮子上过油，手推车就会更容易移动。
