recoups:/rɪˈkuːp/| vt.|补偿，恢复，弥补|to regain or recover (something lost or expended)|The company recouped its losses within a year. 公司在一年内收回了损失。|近义词：recover, regain, recoup oneself |反义词：lose, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company recoups its losses through increased sales. = 公司通过增加销售额来弥补损失。
He recoups his energy by taking a long nap. = 他通过长时间小睡来恢复精力。


一般过去时:
She recouped her strength after a long illness. = 她大病一场后恢复了体力。
They recouped the cost of the repairs with insurance money. = 他们用保险金弥补了修理费用。


现在完成时:
We have recouped most of the money we invested. = 我们已经收回了大部分投资款。
The team has recouped from its recent defeat. = 球队从最近的失利中恢复过来了。


过去完成时:
They had recouped their losses before the end of the financial year. = 他们在财政年度结束前已经收回了损失。
She had recouped her composure before addressing the crowd. = 在向人群讲话之前，她已经恢复了镇静。


将来时:
We will recoup our expenses once the project is completed. = 项目完成后，我们将收回我们的开支。
He expects to recoup his investment within five years. = 他预计五年内能收回投资。


虚拟语气:
If the company had invested wisely, it would have recouped its losses sooner. = 如果公司投资明智，它本可以更快地收回损失。
If I had taken a break, I would have recouped my energy more effectively. = 如果我休息一下，我就能更有效地恢复精力。


被动语态:
The losses were recouped by the company through diligent work. = 公司通过勤奋工作弥补了损失。
His energy was recouped after a restful night's sleep. = 他在睡了一夜好觉后恢复了精力。


疑问句:
How long will it take to recoup the investment? = 收回投资需要多长时间？
Will they be able to recoup their losses this quarter? = 他们本季度能够弥补损失吗？


否定句:
The company did not recoup its losses in the first year. = 公司第一年没有收回损失。
He has not yet recouped his strength after the surgery. = 手术后他还没有恢复体力。
