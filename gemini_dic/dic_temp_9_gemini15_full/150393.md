panoramic:/ˌpænəˈræmɪk/| adj.| 全景的；全景式的 |  A panoramic view or picture shows a wide area of land.  It's often used to describe a wide view, especially one that is impressive or beautiful. | The panoramic view from the mountaintop was breathtaking.  山顶的全景令人叹为观止。|The panoramic window offered a stunning view of the city. 这扇全景窗户提供了令人惊叹的城市景色。| sweeping, wide-angle | close-up, detailed
