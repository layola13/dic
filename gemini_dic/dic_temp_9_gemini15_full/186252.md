词源:
From rhino- + scleroma.  "rhino-" is from Greek *ῥίς (rhis, "nose"), and "-scleroma" is from Greek σκληρός (sklēros, "hard") + -oma (a suffix indicating a tumor or swelling).  Therefore, the word literally means "hardening of the nose".
