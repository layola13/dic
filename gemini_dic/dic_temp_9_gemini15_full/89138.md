homecoming:/ˈhoʊmˌkʌmɪŋ/| n. |返乡；返校节|The act of returning home, especially after a long absence; also a social event, typically for alumni of a school or college.  |例句：His homecoming was a joyous occasion for the whole family. 他的返乡对全家来说都是一件令人高兴的事。

例句：The annual homecoming dance is always a highlight of the school year.  一年一度的返校舞会总是学校一年中最精彩的活动。

近义词：return, arrival, reunion |反义词：departure, leaving
