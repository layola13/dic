heliozoa:/ˌhiːliˈoʊzə/| n. |太阳虫；日光虫 |Sun-animalcules;  a group of mostly freshwater amoeboid protists characterized by their radiating, often spiny, pseudopodia that resemble sun's rays. They are typically spherical and lack cell walls.  | Example:  Heliozoa are fascinating single-celled organisms found in various aquatic environments.  太阳虫是存在于各种水生环境中令人着迷的单细胞生物。|近义词：acantharia (some species)|反义词：N/A
