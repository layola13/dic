obtained:/əbˈteɪnd/| v.|获得，得到，取得|To get, receive, or acquire something, especially after effort or difficulty.|例句：She obtained a degree in engineering. 她获得了工程学位。|例句：He obtained permission to leave early. 他获得了提早离开的许可。|近义词：acquired, gained, secured, achieved|反义词：lost, forfeited, relinquished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They obtain information from various sources. = 他们从各种来源获取信息。
We obtain the best results using this method. = 使用这种方法，我们获得了最好的结果。

一般过去时:
I obtained a visa last week. = 我上周获得了签证。
The researchers obtained significant data. = 研究人员获得了重要的数据。

现在完成时:
She has obtained all the necessary documents. = 她已经获得了所有必要的证件。
He has obtained a prestigious award. = 他获得了一个享有盛誉的奖项。

过去完成时:
They had obtained the necessary funding before starting the project. = 他们在项目开始之前已经获得了必要的资金。
She had obtained her driver's license several years ago. = 她几年前就拿到了驾照。

将来时:
We will obtain the results of the test tomorrow. = 我们明天将获得测试结果。
He hopes to obtain a scholarship for his studies. = 他希望获得奖学金资助他的学习。

虚拟语气:
If I obtained the necessary funds, I would start my business. = 如果我获得了必要的资金，我就会开始我的生意。
I wish I had obtained that job last year. = 我真希望去年得到那份工作。

被动语态:
The information was obtained from confidential sources. = 信息是从机密来源获得的。
The results were obtained after extensive research. = 在广泛的研究之后获得了结果。

疑问句:
How did you obtain this information? = 你是怎么获得这个信息的？
Where did they obtain the materials? = 他们从哪里获得这些材料？

否定句:
He did not obtain the necessary qualifications. = 他没有获得必要的资格。
They could not obtain a refund for the faulty product. = 他们无法获得有缺陷产品的退款。
