double: /ˈdʌbl/ | adj., n., v. | 双倍的；两倍的；双重的；两份的；加倍；使加倍 |

英文解析:  Double can function as an adjective, noun, or verb. As an adjective, it means consisting of two identical parts or things; twice as much or as many. As a noun, it refers to something that is double another, or a duplicate. As a verb, it means to make or become twice as much or as many; to fold or bend something over.


例句:

adj.  The room had double beds. = 房间里有双人床。

adj.  She earns double what I do. = 她的收入是我的两倍。


n.  He ordered a double espresso. = 他点了一杯双份浓缩咖啡。

n.  This is a double of the original painting. = 这是原作的复制品。


v.  We'll have to double our efforts. = 我们必须加倍努力。

v.  The recipe instructs you to double the ingredients. = 食谱指示你将配料加倍。


近义词:  twice, twofold, duplicate, twin, replicate

反义词:  single, half, one


词性: adjective, noun, verb

词源:

The word "double" originates from the Old French word "double," which itself comes from the Latin word "duplus," meaning "twofold" or "double."  The evolution reflects the consistent meaning throughout the languages.
