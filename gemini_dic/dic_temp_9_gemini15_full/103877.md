jubilation:/ˌdʒuːbɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |欢呼；庆贺；喜悦 |The feeling of great happiness and triumph.  It often involves outward expressions of joy, such as shouting, singing, and dancing. |例句：The team's jubilation was palpable after their victory.  球队获胜后，欢欣鼓舞之情溢于言表。

例句：Their jubilation knew no bounds when they heard the good news.  听到好消息后，他们的欢欣喜悦难以言表。


近义词：celebration, rejoicing, exultation, elation

反义词：despair, dejection, misery, sorrow

词性:名词
