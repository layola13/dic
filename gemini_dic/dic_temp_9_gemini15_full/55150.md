dissects:/dɪˈsɛkt/| vt.|解剖；仔细分析|To cut up (a body, plant, or other organism) in order to study its internal parts.|The biologist dissected the frog to examine its organs. 生物学家解剖青蛙以检查其器官。|近义词：analyze, examine, scrutinize|反义词：synthesize, integrate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist dissects the specimen. = 科学家正在解剖标本。
She dissects the argument point by point. = 她逐点剖析了这个论点。

一般过去时:
He dissected the frog in biology class. = 他在生物课上解剖了青蛙。
They dissected the problem to find a solution. = 他们仔细分析了这个问题以找到解决方案。

现在完成时:

We have dissected the data and found some interesting patterns. = 我们已经分析了数据并发现了一些有趣的模式。
