epidemic:/ˈɛpɪdɛmɪk/| n. |流行病，疫病|A widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community at a particular time.|例句：The flu epidemic of 1918 killed millions. 1918年的流感大流行夺去了数百万人的生命。|近义词：outbreak, plague, pandemic|反义词：endemic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
An epidemic is spreading rapidly. = 流行病正在迅速蔓延。
This disease is endemic in this region. = 这种疾病在这个地区是地方性的。


一般过去时:
A severe epidemic hit the city last year. = 去年一场严重的疫情袭击了这座城市。
The plague epidemic devastated Europe in the 14th century. = 14世纪鼠疫席卷欧洲。


现在完成时:
Several epidemics have occurred in recent years. = 近年来发生了几次疫情。
The country has successfully contained the epidemic. = 该国已成功控制了疫情。


过去完成时:
The government had already prepared for the epidemic before it broke out. = 政府在疫情爆发前就已经做好了准备。
The doctors had treated many patients during the previous epidemic. = 在之前的疫情期间，医生们已经治疗了许多病人。


将来时:
Experts predict a future epidemic of antibiotic-resistant bacteria. = 专家预测未来将出现耐抗生素细菌的疫情。
The government will implement measures to prevent an epidemic. = 政府将实施措施以预防疫情。


虚拟语气:
If there were an epidemic, the hospital would be overwhelmed. = 如果发生疫情，医院将不堪重负。
It is vital that the epidemic be contained quickly. = 迅速控制疫情至关重要。


被动语态:
The epidemic was declared a public health emergency. = 疫情已被宣布为公共卫生紧急事件。
Measures are being taken to prevent the spread of the epidemic. = 正在采取措施以防止疫情蔓延。


疑问句:
Is there an epidemic in the area? = 这个地区有疫情吗？
What caused the epidemic? = 导致这次疫情的原因是什么？


否定句:
There is no epidemic in this region. = 这个地区没有疫情。
The government denied that there was an epidemic. = 政府否认存在疫情。


PHRASE->

epidemic spread = 疫情传播

prevent an epidemic = 预防疫情
