repainted:/ˌriːˈpeɪntɪd/| v. |重新粉刷；重绘|To paint something again.  This often implies a previous coat of paint has been removed or is being covered.|The old house was repainted in a bright yellow.  这栋旧房子重新漆成了亮黄色。|The artist repainted the canvas, altering the composition.  艺术家重新绘制了画布，改变了构图。|painted, refurbished|unpainted

He repaints old furniture for a living. = 他靠重新粉刷旧家具为生。
