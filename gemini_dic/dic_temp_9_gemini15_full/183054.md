replunge:/rɪˈplʌndʒ/| v.|使重新陷入；再次投入|To plunge again; to immerse or involve oneself again in something.  The word implies a return to a previous state or activity, often one that is unpleasant or difficult. |例句：After a brief respite, the country replunged into civil war.  =短暂的休整之后，国家再次陷入内战。

例句：He replunged himself into his work after a short break. = 短暂休息后，他又投入到工作中。


近义词：reimmerse, reinvolve, submerge again, relapse

反义词：emerge, extricate, withdraw, rescue
