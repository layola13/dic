overspread:/ˌoʊvərˈsprɛd/ | vt. | 蔓延；覆盖；散布 | To spread or extend over or across a surface or area; to cover completely or extensively. |例句：The ivy overspread the entire wall.  常春藤爬满了整面墙。

The rumor overspread the town quickly.  谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。


近义词：cover, spread, envelop, blanket | 反义词：concentrate, gather, contract | 词性：动词


USAGE->
