dinette:/dɪˈnɛt/| n. |小餐厅；小餐桌；简易餐厅|A small dining area, often built into a kitchen or combined with a kitchen counter.  It typically includes a small table and chairs.|例句：The dinette set was perfect for our small apartment. 这套小餐桌非常适合我们的公寓。|例句：They ate breakfast in the dinette before leaving for work. 他们在上班前在小餐厅吃了早餐。|近义词：breakfast nook, kitchenette, small dining area |反义词：large dining room, formal dining area
