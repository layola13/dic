gloriously:/ˈɡlɔːriəsli/| adj.|光荣地；辉煌地；壮丽地 |  In a glorious manner; splendidly; magnificently.  It describes something done or existing in a way that is impressive and wonderful. | 例句：The sun shone gloriously on the sparkling sea.  阳光灿烂地照耀着波光粼粼的大海。


The flowers bloomed gloriously in the spring garden.  鲜花在春季的花园里盛开得异常美丽。


近义词：splendidly, magnificently, wonderfully, brilliantly, triumphantly


反义词：miserably, poorly, wretchedly, sadly


词源:  The word "gloriously" is derived from the adjective "glorious," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "gloriosus," meaning "full of glory."  The suffix "-ly" is an adverbial suffix, transforming the adjective into an adverb.
