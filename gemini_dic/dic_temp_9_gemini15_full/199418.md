simply:/ˈsɪmpli/ | adv. | 简单地；仅仅；只不过 |  Simply means in a straightforward or easy manner; without complexity. It can also mean merely or only.  |  例句：The instructions were simply explained.  = 指示说明很简单。


The solution is simply to add water. = 解决方法仅仅是加水。


近义词：easily, merely, just, only | 反义词：complexly, intricately, elaborately


词源:  The word "simply" is derived from the adjective "simple," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "simplex," meaning "single," "uncomplicated," or "straightforward."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.


用法说明:

一般现在时:
He simply reads the book. = 他只是看书。
She simply goes to work. = 她只是去上班。


一般过去时:
I simply walked home. = 我只是步行回家。
They simply ate dinner. = 他们只是吃了晚饭。


现在完成时:
We have simply finished the project. = 我们只是完成了项目。
She has simply gone to the store. = 她只是去了商店。


过去完成时:
He had simply left before I arrived. = 我到之前，他只是离开了。
They had simply finished eating when the phone rang. = 他们刚吃完饭，电话就响了。


将来时:
I will simply wait here. = 我就在这里等。
She will simply call you later. = 她稍后会给你打电话。


虚拟语气:
If I were simply honest, I would tell the truth. = 如果我坦诚一点，我会说实话。
I wish I could simply disappear. = 我希望我能够简单地消失。


被动语态:
The problem was simply solved. = 问题很容易解决。
The task was simply completed. = 任务轻松完成。


疑问句:
Is it simply a matter of time? = 这仅仅是时间问题吗？
Is it simply a misunderstanding? = 这仅仅是误会吗？


否定句:
It wasn't simply a mistake. = 这不仅仅是个错误。
It wasn't simply a coincidence. = 这不仅仅是巧合。


PHRASE->

simply amazing = 简直令人惊叹

simply beautiful = 简直太美了
