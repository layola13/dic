heptandrian:/ˌheptˈændriən/| adj.|七雄蕊的|Having seven stamens.  The term refers to flowers possessing seven stamens, the pollen-producing part of a flower.|Example: The heptandrous flower was a key characteristic for identifying the plant species.  七雄蕊的花是识别植物物种的关键特征。|近义词：septemstaminal (less common)|反义词：monandrous, diandrous, triandrous (referring to flowers with fewer stamens)
