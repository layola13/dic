journeywork:/ˈdʒɜːrnɪwɜːrk/ | n. | 旅行中的工作；游方工作；学徒期间的巡回工作 | Work done while traveling, especially by a journeyman or apprentice.  It often refers to the practice of traveling to different places to gain experience and find work. | Example:  He spent several years doing journeywork before settling down in one place. 他在定居在一个地方之前花了数年时间从事游方工作。 |近义词：itinerant work, traveling work |反义词：sedentary work, stationary work
