megawatts:/ˈmɛɡəˌwɒts/ | n. |兆瓦 | A megawatt (MW) is a unit of power equal to one million watts.  It's used to measure the rate of energy transfer or consumption, often for large-scale power generation or consumption.  | The power plant generates 1000 megawatts of electricity.  这家发电厂发电1000兆瓦。The new wind farm is expected to produce 50 megawatts of clean energy. 新的风力发电场预计将产生50兆瓦的清洁能源。|kilowatts, gigawatts | milliwatts | noun
