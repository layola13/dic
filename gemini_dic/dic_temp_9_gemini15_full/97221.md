inflates:/ɪnˈfleɪts/| vt.|使膨胀；使充气；夸大；提高（价格）|to increase in size, amount, or value, or to make something do this |The balloon inflated quickly.  气球很快充气了。|The company inflated its profits. 公司夸大了其利润。| expands, swells, exaggerates |deflates, shrinks, diminishes|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inflates the tires every week. = 他每周都给轮胎充气。
The government inflates the currency. = 政府正在膨胀货币。


一般过去时:
She inflated the balloon for her son's birthday. = 她为儿子的生日充了气球。
They inflated the price of the tickets. = 他们抬高了票价。


现在完成时:
The hot air has inflated the balloon. = 热空气使气球膨胀了。
The company has inflated its earnings reports for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在夸大其收益报告。


过去完成时:
They had inflated the tires before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经给轮胎充气了。
The news had inflated the public's fear. = 新闻夸大了公众的恐惧。


将来时:

The central bank will likely inflate the money supply. = 中央银行可能会增加货币供应量。


虚拟语气:
