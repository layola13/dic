implanter:/ɪmˈplæntər/| n. |植入器；移植者 |A device or person that implants something, such as a medical device or an idea.  |例句：The surgeon used a sophisticated implanter to place the microchip.  外科医生使用精密植入器植入微芯片。

例句：He was a skilled implanter of new business strategies. 他是一位熟练的新业务战略实施者。

近义词：inserter, introducer |反义词：extractor, remover
