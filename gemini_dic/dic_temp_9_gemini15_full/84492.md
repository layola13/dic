halts:/hɔːlts/ | v. | 停止；中断；犹豫 | To bring or come to a stop; to cease; to hesitate. | The train halted at a small station. 火车在一个小站停了下来。 The negotiations halted due to a disagreement.  由于意见分歧，谈判停止了。 | stop, cease, pause | continue, proceed | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bus halts at every stop. = 公共汽车在每一站都停靠。
He always halts before making a decision. = 他做决定前总是犹豫。

一般过去时:
The army halted its advance. = 军队停止了前进。
She halted in surprise. = 她惊奇地停住了。

现在完成时:
The project has halted due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，项目已经停止了。
They have halted their search for the missing child. = 他们已经停止了寻找失踪儿童的行动。

过去完成时:
The car had halted just before the cliff edge. = 汽车在悬崖边停了下来。
We had halted for a rest before continuing our journey. = 我们在继续旅程之前停下来休息了一下。

将来时:
The construction will halt for the winter months. = 建筑工程将在冬季暂停。
The meeting will halt until further notice. = 会议将暂停，直至另行通知。

虚拟语气:
If the rain halted, we would continue our hike. = 如果雨停了，我们将继续远足。
I wish the noise would halt. = 我希望噪音能停止。

被动语态:
Production was halted by the strike. = 罢工使生产停止了。
The car was halted by the police. = 汽车被警察拦住了。

疑问句:
Does the train halt at this station? = 这列火车在这站停吗？
Will the negotiations halt? = 谈判会停止吗？

否定句:
The work did not halt despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气不好，工作并没有停止。
The driver didn't halt for the pedestrian. = 司机没有为行人停车。
