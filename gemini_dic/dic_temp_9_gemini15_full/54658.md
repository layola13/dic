disobeyed:/ˌdɪsoʊˈbeɪd/ | v. |不服从，违抗 | To refuse to obey a person, rule, or law.  |例句：He disobeyed his parents and went to the party. 他不听父母的话去了派对。|例句：The soldiers disobeyed orders and deserted the battlefield. 士兵们违抗命令逃离了战场。|近义词：defy, flout, disregard |反义词：obey, comply, conform
