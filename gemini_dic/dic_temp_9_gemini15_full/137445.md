nontherapeutically:/ˌnɒnˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkli/ | adv. | 非治疗性地 |  Not for the purpose of treating a disease or condition.  This adverb describes actions or uses that are not intended to heal or cure. |

例句：The drug was used nontherapeutically in the experiment.  =  该药物在实验中被非治疗性地使用。

The substance was administered nontherapeutically to observe its effects. =  该物质被非治疗性地施用以观察其效果。


近义词：medically, therapeutically的反义词，for non-medical purposes, for research purposes


反义词：therapeutically, medicinally


词性: 副词
