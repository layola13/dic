passing-over:/ˈpæsɪŋ ˈoʊvər/ | n. |逾越节，忽略，放过 |The act of passing over something or someone;  specifically, the Jewish festival of Passover, commemorating the liberation of the Israelites from slavery in Egypt. |例句：The passing-over of the Red Sea is a central event in the Jewish faith. 逾越红海是犹太教信仰中的一个中心事件。

The company's passing-over of his application was a disappointment.  公司没有采纳他的申请让他很失望。|近义词：omission, neglect, ignoring, Passover |反义词：attention, consideration, inclusion
