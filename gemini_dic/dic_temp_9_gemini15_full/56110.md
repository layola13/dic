doctress:/ˈdɒktərɪs/| n. |女医生|A female physician or medical doctor.  The term is now largely considered outdated and potentially offensive, with "doctor" being the preferred and inclusive term.|例句：She was the first doctress in the small town. 她是在这个小镇上的第一位女医生。

例句：The doctress examined the patient carefully.  这位女医生仔细检查了病人。


近义词：female doctor, physician (female), medical practitioner (female)

反义词：无直接反义词，但可以与“male doctor”形成对比


词性:名词
