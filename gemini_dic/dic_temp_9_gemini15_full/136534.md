nonruminations: /ˌnɒnˌruːmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |非反刍；非沉思；不胡思乱想|The absence of rumination or contemplation; a state of not dwelling on thoughts or worries.  It is typically used in a context where rumination is expected or common.|例句：Her surprising calmness was a stark contrast to the nonruminations of the other stressed participants. 她令人惊讶的冷静与其他压力大的参与者不停的胡思乱想形成了鲜明对比。


近义词：lack of reflection, absence of brooding, uncontemplation, thoughtlessness (in the sense of lack of deep thought)


反义词：rumination, contemplation, brooding, reflection, introspection


词性：名词
