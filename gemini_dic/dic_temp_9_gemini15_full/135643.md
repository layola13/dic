nonprecipitation: /ˌnɒnˌprɛsɪpɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |非降水；无降水 | The absence of precipitation, such as rain, snow, sleet, or hail.  It refers to a period or condition where no form of water falls from the atmosphere. | Example: The prolonged period of nonprecipitation led to severe drought conditions.  长时间的无降水导致了严重的干旱。  The forecast predicts several days of nonprecipitation in the region. 预报显示该地区将有几天无降水。| 近义词：dry spell, drought (in the context of prolonged absence) | 反义词：precipitation, rainfall, snowfall |  名词
