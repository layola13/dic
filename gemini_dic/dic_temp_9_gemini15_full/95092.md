inamiable:/ɪnˈeɪmiəbl/ | adj. | 不友善的；脾气不好的 | Unpleasant; not friendly or sociable.  It suggests a persistent lack of friendliness, often stemming from a bad temper or general dislike of others. | 例句：He was an inamiable character, always grumpy and quick to anger. 他是一个不友善的人，总是脾气暴躁，容易生气。


例句：Her inamiable demeanor put everyone off. 她不友善的态度让每个人都敬而远之。


近义词：unfriendly, disagreeable, unpleasant, surly, ill-tempered


反义词： amiable, friendly, pleasant, agreeable, good-natured


词性:形容词
