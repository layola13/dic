hurricane:/ˈhʌrɪkən/| n. |飓风，暴风|A hurricane is a powerful, rotating storm system characterized by strong winds and heavy rainfall. It forms over warm ocean waters and typically moves towards land, causing significant damage.  |例句：The hurricane caused widespread devastation. 飓风造成了大范围的破坏。

The hurricane forced the evacuation of coastal areas. 飓风迫使沿海地区撤离。|近义词：typhoon, cyclone, tempest|反义词：calm, breeze
