microphytal:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈfaɪtəl/| adj.|微小的植物的；小型植物的|Relating to or characteristic of microphytes (small plants).  The term often refers to plants that are small in size, often microscopic, and typically found in aquatic or terrestrial environments with specific adaptations. |例句：The study focused on the microphytal communities inhabiting the intertidal zone. 这项研究的重点是居住在潮间带的微小植物群落。|近义词：minute, diminutive, tiny, microscopic|反义词：macrophytal, large, gigantic
