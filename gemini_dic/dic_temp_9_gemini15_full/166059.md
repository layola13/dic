prepolished:/ˌpriːˈpɒlɪʃt/ | adj. | 预先抛光的；预先研磨的 |  Already polished or buffed before use or further processing.  Pre-polished surfaces are smoother and require less further treatment.  | 例句：The prepolished granite countertop was ready for immediate installation. 预先抛光的石英石台面可以立即安装。

The prepolished metal parts were shipped directly to the assembly line. 预先抛光的金属部件直接运送到装配线。


近义词：pre-finished, smoothed, refined | 反义词：unpolished, rough, unfinished

词性:形容词
