phasing-in: /ˈfeɪzɪŋ ɪn/ | v. |逐步实施，逐步采用|The gradual implementation of a new system, plan, or policy over a period of time.  This allows for adjustments and minimizes disruption.|例句：The company is phasing in a new software system over the next six months. 公司将在未来六个月内逐步实施新的软件系统。

例句：We are phasing in the new training program, starting with the sales team. 我们正在逐步实施新的培训计划，从销售团队开始。


近义词：introducing gradually, implementing gradually, rolling out, gradual introduction


反义词：phasing out, immediate implementation, abrupt introduction


词性：动词
