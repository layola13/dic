quasi-infinite:/ˈkwɑːziˈɪnfɪnɪt/| adj. |准无限的，近乎无限的 |Approaching infinity; extremely large in magnitude or extent, but not truly infinite.  It implies a practical limit, even if it's very high.|例句：The universe is often described as quasi-infinite in its extent. 宇宙在广阔程度上通常被描述为准无限的。

The processing power of modern supercomputers is quasi-infinite compared to older models. 与旧型号相比，现代超级计算机的处理能力几乎是无限的。


近义词：nearly infinite, virtually infinite, immense, vast, enormous |反义词：finite, limited, small, restricted

词性：形容词
