differentiating: /ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 区分，区别，使差异化 |  The act of making or becoming different; distinguishing.  It involves recognizing and highlighting the unique characteristics of something to set it apart from others.  This can apply to ideas, objects, or groups of people. |

例句：
The scientist is differentiating between the two types of bacteria. = 科学家正在区分这两种细菌。

The marketing campaign aims to differentiate our product from the competitors. = 这场营销活动旨在将我们的产品与竞争对手的产品区分开来。


近义词：distinguish, discriminate, separate, discern

反义词：confuse, homogenize, unify


词性: 动词
