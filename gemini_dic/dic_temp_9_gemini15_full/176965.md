reallocations:/ˌriːæləˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |重新分配，重新配置 |The act of allocating resources again; the distribution of something in a new way.|例句：The company's reallocations of funds led to increased profits. 公司资金的重新分配导致利润增加。
例句：The government announced new reallocations of resources to address the crisis. 政府宣布重新分配资源以应对危机。|近义词：redistribution, realignment, reassignment |反义词：concentration, centralization


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company reallocates resources annually. = 公司每年重新分配资源。
We reallocate our budget every quarter. = 我们每个季度重新分配预算。

一般过去时:
They reallocated the funds to the marketing department. = 他们将资金重新分配给了市场部。
The government reallocated emergency aid to the affected regions. = 政府将紧急援助重新分配给了受灾地区。

现在完成时:
The team has reallocated its priorities since the project delay. = 自项目延期以来，团队已重新分配了其优先事项。
They have reallocated staff to address the increased workload. = 为了应对增加的工作量，他们已经重新分配了员工。

过去完成时:
The manager had reallocated the tasks before the meeting. = 会议之前，经理已经重新分配了任务。
They had reallocated the budget before the unexpected expenses arose. = 在意外支出出现之前，他们已经重新分配了预算。

将来时:
We will reallocate the resources based on the new data. = 我们将根据新数据重新分配资源。
The company will reallocate its marketing budget next year. = 公司明年将重新分配其营销预算。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reallocate our efforts. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新分配我们的精力。
I wish we could reallocate the budget to hire more staff. = 我希望我们可以重新分配预算来雇佣更多员工。

被动语态:
The funds were reallocated to support the research project. = 资金被重新分配以支持研究项目。
The tasks were reallocated among the team members. = 任务在团队成员之间重新分配。

疑问句:
How will the resources be reallocated? = 资源将如何重新分配？
Will the budget be reallocated this year? = 预算今年会重新分配吗？

否定句:
The company did not reallocate the budget this year. = 公司今年没有重新分配预算。
They will not reallocate staff without proper justification. = 他们不会在没有正当理由的情况下重新分配员工。
