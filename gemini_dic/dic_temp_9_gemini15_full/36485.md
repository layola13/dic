colemouse:/ˈkoʊləmaʊs/ | n. |鼠兔；(尤指)北美鼠兔 | A colemouse is a small mammal resembling both a mouse and a rabbit, belonging to the Ochotonidae family.  They are herbivores and inhabit mountainous regions. | Example: The colemouse scurried among the rocks, seeking shelter from the sun.  鼠兔在岩石间窜动，躲避阳光。 |近义词：pika (more common and preferred term) | 反义词：无
