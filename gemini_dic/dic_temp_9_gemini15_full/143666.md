outlandish:/ˈaʊtlændɪʃ/| adj.|古怪的；奇异的；异国的；不寻常的 |  Something outlandish is strikingly unusual or bizarre; it may seem strange or unfamiliar, often because it comes from a foreign place or culture.  It can also refer to something excessively extravagant or flamboyant. |例句：He wore an outlandish costume to the party. 他在派对上穿了一套古怪的服装。

例句：Her ideas were so outlandish that nobody took them seriously.  她的想法太古怪了，没有人认真对待。


近义词： bizarre, strange, peculiar, eccentric, unusual, odd, unconventional

反义词：conventional, ordinary, normal, commonplace, familiar
