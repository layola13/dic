recleaning:/ˌriːˈkliːnɪŋ/| n.|再次清洁；重新清洁|The act of cleaning something again, often after an initial cleaning was deemed insufficient.  It implies a more thorough or repeated cleaning process.|例句：The recleaning of the carpets was necessary after the party.  派对之后，地毯需要重新清洁。|例句：The kitchen requires recleaning after the spill. 厨房需要在洒了东西之后重新清洁。|近义词：re-cleaning, second cleaning, cleaning again|反义词：soiling, dirtying

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel recleans the rooms daily.  酒店每天都重新清洁房间。
Recleaning the gutters is part of their maintenance. 清洁排水沟是他们维护工作的一部分。


一般过去时:
They recleaned the entire house after the flood. 洪水过后，他们重新清洁了整栋房子。
She recleaned the windows because they were still streaky. 她重新擦了窗户，因为上面还有条纹。


现在完成时:
We have recleaned the pool three times this week.  本周我们已经三次重新清洁了游泳池。
They have recleaned the office since the last inspection. 自上次检查以来，他们已经重新清洁了办公室。


过去完成时:
The chef had recleaned the kitchen before the health inspector arrived.  卫生检查员到达之前，厨师已经重新清洁了厨房。
They had recleaned the spill before anyone noticed. 他们在任何人注意到之前已经重新清洁了洒出来的东西。


将来时:
We will reclean the carpets next week. 我们下周将重新清洁地毯。
They will reclean the bathroom after the plumber leaves. 水管工离开后，他们将重新清洁浴室。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reclean the entire house. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新清洁整个房子。
I wish I had recleaned the oven sooner. 我真希望我早点重新清洁烤箱。


被动语态:
The carpets were recleaned yesterday. 地毯昨天重新清洁了。
The spill was recleaned quickly and efficiently. 洒出来的东西被迅速有效地重新清洁了。


疑问句:
Has the kitchen been recleaned yet? 厨房重新清洁了吗？
Will the office be recleaned before the meeting? 会议之前办公室会重新清洁吗？


否定句:
They didn't reclean the bathroom. 他们没有重新清洁浴室。
The restaurant hadn't recleaned the kitchen properly. 餐厅没有正确地重新清洁厨房。
