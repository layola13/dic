counterbalances:/ˈkaʊntərˌbælənsɪz/ | v. | 使平衡；抵消；制衡 | To counteract or balance the effect of something.  |The company's strong export sales counterbalanced its weak domestic market. 这家公司的强劲出口销售额抵消了其疲软的国内市场。|  balance, offset, neutralize | exacerbate, worsen |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He counterbalances his aggressive nature with careful diplomacy. 他用谨慎的外交手段来平衡他侵略性的本性。
The government counterbalances the needs of the economy with environmental concerns. 政府在经济需求和环境问题之间取得平衡。


一般过去时:
Her confidence counterbalanced her nervousness before the presentation.  她在演讲前的自信抵消了她的紧张情绪。
The strong winds counterbalanced the effects of the heat wave. 强风抵消了热浪的影响。


现在完成时:
The increase in wages has counterbalanced the rise in inflation. 工资的增长已经抵消了通货膨胀的上升。
Recent investments have counterbalanced the company's previous losses. 最近的投资已经抵消了公司之前的损失。


过去完成时:
The extra funding had counterbalanced the shortfall in the budget. 额外的资金已经弥补了预算的不足。
The earlier rains had counterbalanced the effects of the drought. 早些时候的雨水已经抵消了干旱的影响。


将来时:
Increased production will counterbalance the rising demand.  产量增加将抵消需求的增长。
New policies will counterbalance the negative effects of the previous ones. 新政策将抵消先前政策的负面影响。


虚拟语气:
If we increased our marketing budget, it would counterbalance the decline in sales. 如果我们增加营销预算，它将抵消销售额的下降。
If the project were successful, it would counterbalance years of losses. 如果项目成功，它将抵消多年的损失。


被动语态:
The negative effects of the policy were counterbalanced by government intervention. 该政策的负面影响被政府干预所抵消。
The risks of the investment were counterbalanced by the potential rewards. 投资的风险被潜在的回报所抵消。


疑问句:
How can we counterbalance the negative impacts of climate change? 我们如何才能抵消气候变化的负面影响？
Does the benefit counterbalance the cost of the project?  这项计划的好处是否抵消了它的成本？


否定句:
The company's efforts did not counterbalance the damage caused by the scandal. 该公司的努力并未抵消丑闻造成的损害。
The positive aspects of the new system did not counterbalance its complexity. 新系统的积极方面并未抵消其复杂性。


PHRASE->
counterbalance to something: 对…的制衡
counterbalance against something:  抵消…
counterbalance each other: 相互抵消
counterbalance the effects of: 抵消…的影响
work to counterbalance:  努力以平衡
