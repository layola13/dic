features:/ˈfiːtʃərz/ | n. | 特点，特征，容貌 | The distinctive features of the landscape included towering cliffs and sandy beaches.  景观的显著特征包括高耸的悬崖和沙滩。 |近义词：characteristics, attributes, traits |反义词：unremarkableness


The word "features" refers to prominent or important aspects, qualities, or characteristics of something. It can describe physical attributes, significant details, or noteworthy elements.  It also refers to a special article or item in a newspaper, magazine, or other publication.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This car features a powerful engine. = 这辆车有一个强大的引擎。
The article features an interview with the president. = 这篇文章刊登了对总统的采访。


一般过去时:
The conference featured several prominent speakers. = 会议邀请了几位著名的演讲者。
Her face featured high cheekbones and a strong jawline. = 她的脸部特征是高颧骨和强劲的下颌线。


现在完成时:
The new phone has featured many innovative technologies. = 这款新手机采用了多种创新技术。
The museum has featured several temporary exhibitions this year. = 博物馆今年举办了几个临时展览。


过去完成时:
The book had featured a captivating story about a young explorer. = 这本书讲述了一个关于年轻探险家的引人入胜的故事。
The newspaper had featured the scandal on its front page. = 报纸的头版报道了这起丑闻。


将来时:
The next issue will feature an article about climate change. = 下一期将刊登一篇关于气候变化的文章。
The concert will feature a famous musician. = 音乐会将邀请一位著名的音乐家。


虚拟语气:
If the product featured a better design, it would sell better. = 如果产品设计更好，它就会卖得更好。
I wish this game featured more challenging levels. = 我希望这个游戏有更具挑战性的关卡。


被动语态:
The problem was featured in the news report. = 这个问题在新闻报道中被报道。
His achievements were featured in the local newspaper. = 他的成就被当地报纸报道。


疑问句:
What features does this software have? = 这个软件有什么功能？
Does this magazine feature any articles about space exploration? = 这本杂志刊登了关于太空探索的文章吗？


否定句:
The movie didn't feature any violence. = 这部电影没有任何暴力镜头。
The presentation didn't feature any data. = 这次演示没有任何数据。
