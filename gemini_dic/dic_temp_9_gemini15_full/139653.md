occupancies:/ˈɑːkjʊpənsiz/| n.| 占用率；入住率；占有情况 |The number of units or spaces occupied, often expressed as a percentage.  It refers to the degree to which something is filled or used.  For example, in hotels, it's the percentage of rooms occupied; in real estate, it's the percentage of units rented. |例句：The hotel's occupancies were high during the holiday season.  酒店在假期期间入住率很高。

The apartment building boasts high occupancies year-round. 这栋公寓楼全年入住率都很高。|近义词：occupancy rates, rental rates, fill rates |反义词：vacancies
