myalgia:/maɪˈælʒə/| n.|肌痛，肌肉痛|Muscle pain.  It is a symptom, not a disease itself, and can be caused by various factors including injury, overuse, infection, or underlying medical conditions.|例句：She experienced severe myalgia after her strenuous workout. 她剧烈运动后感到严重的肌肉疼痛。|例句：The flu often presents with symptoms such as fever, cough, and myalgia. 流感通常伴有发烧、咳嗽和肌肉疼痛等症状。|近义词：muscle pain, muscular pain, aching muscles|反义词：muscle comfort, muscle relaxation|名词
