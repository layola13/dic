empressement:/ɑ̃.prɛs.mɑ̃/| n.|热切；殷勤；过分热情| Eagerness or zeal, especially in offering one's services; excessive eagerness.|例句：His empressement to help was touching, but slightly overwhelming.  他的热心帮助令人感动，但也略显过度。|例句：She was met with such empressement that she felt slightly uncomfortable. 她受到了如此热情的接待，以至于感到有些不自在。|近义词：zeal, eagerness, fervor, enthusiasm, ardor|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, lethargy, unconcern
