playlists:/ˈpleɪlɪsts/| n. |播放列表，歌单|A playlist is a list of audio or video files, typically songs, that are grouped together for playback.  It is often created and managed by a user on a media player or streaming service.|Example: I created a playlist of my favorite songs for my road trip. 我为我的公路旅行创建了一个我最喜欢的歌曲播放列表。|近义词：collection, selection|反义词：None
