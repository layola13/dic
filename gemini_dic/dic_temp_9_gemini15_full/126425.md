mystery:/ˈmɪstəri/| n. |神秘；谜；悬疑小说 |Something that is difficult or impossible to understand or explain.  A feeling of mystery surrounds the disappearance.  |The disappearance of the painting is a mystery.  这幅画的消失是一个谜。The novel is a mystery thriller. 这部小说是一部悬疑惊悚片。| enigma, puzzle, riddle | clarity, solution | 名词
