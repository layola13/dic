nondeflation:/ˌnɒnˌdiːˈfleɪʃən/| adj.|非通货紧缩|The absence of deflation; a period of stable or rising prices.|例句：The economy experienced a period of nondeflation, avoiding the negative consequences of falling prices.  经济经历了一段非通货紧缩时期，避免了物价下跌的负面影响。
例句：Central banks often aim for nondeflation, striving to maintain price stability. 中央银行通常的目标是非通货紧缩，努力保持物价稳定。|近义词：price stability, inflation, disinflation|反义词：deflation
