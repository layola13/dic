heterotaxy:/ˌhɛtərəˈtæksi/| n.|异位症，器官异位|A congenital condition characterized by multiple malformations of the body, often involving the heart, lungs, spleen, and other organs.  These malformations result from disruption of the left-right body axis during embryonic development. |Example:  The infant was diagnosed with heterotaxy syndrome, requiring complex cardiac surgery.  婴儿被诊断出患有异位症，需要进行复杂的心脏手术。|近义词： situs inversus, situs ambiguous|反义词：normal situs, situs solitus|名词
