oxidizing:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 氧化的；氧化作用的 |  Relating to or causing oxidation.  The process of oxidizing involves the loss of electrons or an increase in oxidation state by a molecule, atom, or ion. | Example:  Oxidizing agents are used in many chemical processes.  氧化剂被用于许多化学过程中。  The oxidizing atmosphere contributed to the corrosion.  氧化的环境导致了腐蚀。 |近义词：corrosive, oxidizing,  | 反义词：reducing |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chemical is oxidizing rapidly. = 这种化学物质正在迅速氧化。
This process is oxidizing the metal. = 这个过程正在使金属氧化。


一般过去时:
The fruit oxidized quickly after being cut. = 水果切开后很快氧化了。
The iron oxidized and rusted. = 铁氧化并生锈了。


现在完成时:
The metal has oxidized significantly. = 金属已经显著氧化了。
The substance has been oxidizing for hours. = 该物质已经氧化了数小时。


过去完成时:
The paint had oxidized before it was applied. = 油漆在涂抹之前就已经氧化了。
The components had been oxidizing for days. = 这些部件已经氧化了好几天了。


将来时:
The material will oxidize if exposed to air. = 如果暴露在空气中，这种材料会氧化。
The reaction will be oxidizing the substance completely. = 该反应将使物质完全氧化。


虚拟语气:

If we had used a different material, it wouldn't have oxidized so quickly. = 如果我们使用了不同的材料，它就不会氧化得这么快。


被动语态:
The metal was oxidized by the acid. = 金属被酸氧化了。
The substance is being oxidized in the reaction. = 该物质正在反应中被氧化。


疑问句:

否定句:
The substance is not oxidizing under these conditions. = 在这些条件下，该物质没有氧化。
The reaction did not oxidize the material completely. = 反应没有完全氧化该物质。
