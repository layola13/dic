miscalculate:/ˌmɪsˈkælkjuleɪt/ | vt. | 误算；算错；估计错误 | To make a wrong calculation; to wrongly estimate something.  | 例句：He miscalculated the amount of paint needed and ran out halfway through the job. 他误算了所需的油漆量，中途用完了。

例句：They miscalculated the time it would take to complete the project, resulting in a significant delay. 他们误算了完成项目所需的时间，导致了严重的延误。


近义词：err, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate | 反义词：calculate correctly, accurately assess
