dovish:/ˈdʌvɪʃ/| adj.|和平主义的；温和的；偏软的|Relating to or characteristic of a dove; peaceful, conciliatory, or gentle in approach.  The term often refers to a monetary policy that favors lower interest rates and increased money supply.|例句：The central bank adopted a dovish stance, lowering interest rates to stimulate economic growth.  中央银行采取了温和的立场，降低利率以刺激经济增长。
The dovish senator opposed the war resolution.  这位温和的参议员反对战争决议。|近义词：pacific, gentle, lenient, accommodating|反义词：hawkish, aggressive, belligerent, hardline
