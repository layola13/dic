cautioned:/ˈkɔːʃənd/| v.|警告，告诫|To warn or advise (someone) of a danger or risk.|例句：The police cautioned him about his driving. 警方就他的驾驶行为对他进行了警告。|例句：She cautioned me against going alone. 她告诫我不要独自前往。|近义词：warn,advise, alert|反义词：encourage, incite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cautions his children about strangers. = 他告诫他的孩子们要小心陌生人。
The teacher cautions students against cheating. = 老师告诫学生不要作弊。

一般过去时:
The doctor cautioned her about the side effects. = 医生告诫她注意药物的副作用。
My parents cautioned me to be careful. = 我父母告诫我要小心。


现在完成时:
They have cautioned him repeatedly about his behavior. = 他们已经多次警告过他的行为。
She has cautioned me against making hasty decisions. = 她已经告诫我不要做出草率的决定。


过去完成时:
The officer had cautioned the suspect before questioning him. = 警官在审问嫌犯之前已对他进行了警告。
He had cautioned her about the dangers of the mountain path. = 他曾警告过她山路上的危险。


将来时:
The manager will caution the employees about safety regulations. = 经理将警告员工注意安全规章。
I will caution you if you continue this behavior. = 如果你继续这种行为，我会警告你。


虚拟语气:
If I had cautioned him earlier, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果我早点警告他，就不会发生这件事了。
I wish I had cautioned her about the risks involved. = 我真希望我曾警告过她所涉及的风险。


被动语态:
He was cautioned by the police for speeding. = 他因超速驾驶而受到警方的警告。
The children were cautioned about playing near the road. = 孩子们被警告不要在路边玩耍。


疑问句:
Were you cautioned about the dangers of the project? = 你是否被警告过该项目的危险性？
Has he been cautioned about his work performance? = 他是否因为工作表现而受到警告？


否定句:
The teacher did not caution the students about the upcoming test. = 老师没有警告学生即将到来的考试。
They didn't caution him against the risks of investing. = 他们没有告诫他投资的风险。


PHRASE->
caution against = 告诫...不要
caution someone to do something = 告诫某人做某事
issue a caution = 发布警告
under caution = 在警告之下
without caution = 鲁莽地
