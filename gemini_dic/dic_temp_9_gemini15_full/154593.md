persevere:/ˌpɜːrsɪˈvɪər/|/v.|坚持；不屈不挠|To continue trying to do something even though it is difficult.|例句：She persevered in her studies despite many difficulties. 她尽管遇到许多困难，仍然坚持学习。

例句：He persevered with his training even when he felt exhausted. 他即使感到筋疲力尽，也坚持训练。


近义词：persist, endure, maintain, continue|反义词：give up, quit, surrender|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perseveres in his work despite the challenges. = 他尽管面临挑战，仍然坚持他的工作。
They persevere in their efforts to find a solution. = 他们坚持努力寻找解决方案。


一般过去时:
She persevered through the storm. = 她坚持挺过了暴风雨。
He persevered in his pursuit of knowledge. = 他坚持不懈地追求知识。


现在完成时:
She has persevered for many years in her artistic endeavors. = 她多年来一直坚持她的艺术追求。
They have persevered despite numerous setbacks. = 尽管屡遭挫折，他们仍然坚持了下来。


过去完成时:
He had persevered for months before finally achieving his goal. = 他坚持了几个月才最终实现目标。
She had persevered through many hardships before she succeeded. = 在成功之前，她经历了许多磨难并坚持了下来。


将来时:
We will persevere in our fight for justice. = 我们将坚持为正义而战。
He will persevere in his efforts to improve his skills. = 他将坚持努力提高自己的技能。


虚拟语气:
If he had persevered, he would have succeeded. = 如果他坚持下去，他就会成功。
I wish I had persevered in my studies. = 我真希望我当时坚持学习。


被动语态:
The project was persevered with despite the funding cuts. = 尽管资金被削减，项目仍然坚持进行。
His determination was persevered in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，他的决心依然坚定不移。（被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）


疑问句:
Will you persevere in your efforts? = 你会坚持你的努力吗？
Did he persevere in the face of opposition? = 他在面对反对时坚持了吗？


否定句:
He did not persevere in his attempts to climb the mountain. = 他没有坚持尝试攀登这座山。
They did not persevere with the project, and it was abandoned. = 他们没有坚持这个项目，它被放弃了。


PHRASE->
persevere in: 坚持…

persevere through: 坚持度过…
persevere against: 坚持对抗…
persevere despite: 尽管…仍然坚持…
