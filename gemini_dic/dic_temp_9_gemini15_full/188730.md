runnable:/ˈrʌnəbl/| adj.| 可运行的；可执行的 | capable of being run or executed;  suitable or fit to be run.  |例句：The program is runnable after fixing the bug.  修复bug后，程序可运行了。|例句：This code is not runnable in its current state.  这段代码在其当前状态下不可运行。|近义词：executable, operational, workable |反义词：inoperable, non-functional, broken
