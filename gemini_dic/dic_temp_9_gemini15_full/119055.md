metisse:/mɛˈtis/| n.|混血儿女子，尤指白人和黑人混血儿女子|A woman of mixed race, especially one of white and Black parentage.  The term often carries a specific historical and cultural context, particularly in reference to Creole cultures.|例句：She is a beautiful metisse with striking features. 她是一位拥有醒目特征的美丽混血儿女子。|近义词：mulatta (less common now, and potentially offensive), mixed-race woman|反义词：purebred (in the context of race),  unmixed (in the context of race)|名词
