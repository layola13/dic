finner:/ˈfɪnər/| n. |鲸鱼捕鲸者；鳍状物；(尤指鱼类的)鳍；|A person who hunts whales.  Also refers to a fin-like structure, especially on a fish. |例句：The whaling ship was crewed by experienced finners. 捕鲸船的船员都是经验丰富的捕鲸者。

例句：The fish used its powerful finner to propel itself through the water.  这条鱼用它强壮的鳍在水中推进自己。


近义词：whaler (for the person), fin (for the structure)
