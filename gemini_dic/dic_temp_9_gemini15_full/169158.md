prolegate:/ˈprɒɡəɡeɪt/|/ˈprɑːɡəɡeɪt/| vt.|颁布；公布；传播|To prolegate something means to make it publicly known or to spread it widely.  It often implies an official or authoritative announcement.|The government prolegated the new law to the public.|近义词： promulgate, disseminate, publish, broadcast|反义词：suppress, conceal, withhold
