presolutions:/ˌpriːˌsɒljuːʃənz/ | n. | 预先解决方案；初步解决方案 |  A presolution is a preliminary solution or a solution proposed before a problem is fully understood or defined. It often serves as a starting point for further investigation and refinement.  | Example: The team brainstormed presolutions to the software bug, anticipating potential issues before diving into debugging.  团队集思广益地提出了软件错误的预先解决方案，在深入调试之前就预料到了潜在的问题。 |近义词: preliminary solutions, initial proposals, tentative solutions | 反义词: final solution, ultimate solution | 名词
