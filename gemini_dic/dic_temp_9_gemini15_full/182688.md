repace:/rɪˈpleɪs/ | vt. |替换，取代 |To put something or someone in the place of another.  |例句：We replaced the old chairs with new ones. 我们用新的椅子替换了旧的椅子。|近义词：substitute, exchange, supplant |反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replaces the broken window. 他更换了破碎的窗户。
They replace the old equipment regularly. 他们定期更换旧设备。


一般过去时:
She replaced the battery in her car. 她更换了汽车电池。
The mechanic replaced the faulty engine part. 技工更换了有故障的发动机部件。


现在完成时:
They have replaced all the broken tiles. 他们已经更换了所有破损的瓷砖。
We have replaced the outdated software. 我们已经更换了过时的软件。


过去完成时:
He had replaced the tires before the long journey. 长途旅行之前，他已经更换了轮胎。
She had replaced the lightbulb before I arrived. 我到达之前，她已经更换了灯泡。


将来时:
They will replace the damaged roof next week. 他们下周将更换损坏的屋顶。
We will replace the old computer system soon. 我们很快就会更换旧的电脑系统。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would replace my old car. 如果我有更多钱，我会换掉我的旧车。
I wish I could replace this broken screen. 我真希望我能更换这块破损的屏幕。


被动语态:
The broken part will be replaced tomorrow. 破损的部件将在明天更换。
The old furniture is being replaced with modern pieces. 旧家具正在被现代家具所取代。


疑问句:
Will they replace the defective product? 他们会更换有缺陷的产品吗？
Can this part be replaced easily? 这个部件容易更换吗？


否定句:
He didn't replace the flat tire. 他没有更换瘪了的轮胎。
They won't replace the damaged goods without a receipt. 没有收据，他们不会更换损坏的货物。


PHRASE->
replace with: 用……替换
replace A with B: 用B替换A
replace something broken: 替换损坏的东西
replace a missing part: 替换丢失的部件
replace lost documents: 替换丢失的文件
