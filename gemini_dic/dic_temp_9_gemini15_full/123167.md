monking:/ˈmʌŋkɪŋ/| v.|修道院生活；过隐居生活；沉思|The act of engaging in monastic life or practices;  living a secluded or contemplative life.  It can also refer to the process of becoming a monk or nun.|例句：He spent years monking in a remote monastery. 他在偏远的修道院里度过了多年的隐居生活。|例句：The experience of monking changed his perspective on life.  隐居生活改变了他的人生观。|近义词：asceticism, contemplation, seclusion|反义词：hedonism, worldliness, revelry
