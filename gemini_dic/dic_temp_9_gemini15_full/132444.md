nondeposition:/ˌnɒndɛpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 非沉积；未沉积 | The absence of deposition;  the state or fact of not being deposited.  It often refers to a geological context where sediments or other materials have not been laid down.  |例句：The nondeposition of sediment in this area is likely due to strong currents.  这个区域沉积物的不沉积可能是由于强水流造成的。

The nondeposition of evidence led to the case being dismissed. 证据的未提交导致案件被驳回。|近义词：absence of deposition, lack of deposition |反义词：deposition, sedimentation
