reswears:/ˌriːˈswɛrz/ | v. |再穿；重新穿戴 |To wear (clothing) again after having worn it before.  This word is a relatively recent coinage, not found in traditional dictionaries. It combines "re-" (again) with "swears,"  implying a repeated action of wearing.   |例句：I'm going to reswear this dress for the party. 我打算把这件衣服再穿去参加派对。 She reswears her favorite coat every winter. 她每年冬天都重新穿上她最喜欢的外套。|近义词：re-wear, wear again |反义词：discard, dispose of |动词
