luckier:/ˈlʌkiər/| adj.|更幸运的，比较幸运的|More fortunate or having more good luck than others.  It is the comparative form of "lucky."|例句：She was luckier than her sister in the lottery. 她比她姐姐在彩票中更幸运。

例句：He felt luckier after finding a ten-dollar bill. 他在找到一张十美元的钞票后感觉更幸运了。


近义词：more fortunate, more blessed, more favored|反义词：unluckier, less fortunate


词源:源自古英语 *lūc,  意为“运气，机会”，与“luck”同源，添加比较级后缀“-er”构成。
