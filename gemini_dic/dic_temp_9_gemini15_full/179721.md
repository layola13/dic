reenumerated:/ˌriːɪˈnuːməreɪtɪd/| v. |重新编号；重新列举 |To number or list again; to enumerate again.  The items on the list were reenumerated after a few were removed.  |The database was reenumerated after the system crash.  | enumerate, list, itemize |  (no direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of "unlisted" or "unnumbered") |动词
