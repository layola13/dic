reendorsed:/ˌriːɪnˈdɔːrst/| v.|再次认可；再次背书|To endorse again; to give one's support or approval to something a second time.|The candidate was reendorsed by the party leadership.  候选人再次得到了党的领导层的认可。|The board reendorsed the project after reviewing the revised proposal.  董事会在审查了修订后的提案后再次批准了该项目。|endorse, approve, sanction|disapprove, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reendorse the policy every year. = 他们每年都会再次认可这项政策。
The company reendorses its commitment to sustainability. = 公司再次承诺致力于可持续发展。

一般过去时:
The committee reendorsed the plan after a lengthy debate. = 委员会经过长时间的辩论后再次批准了该计划。
He reendorsed his support for the charity. = 他再次表示支持该慈善机构。

现在完成时:
The government has reendorsed its commitment to education reform. = 政府已经再次承诺致力于教育改革。
The organization has reendorsed the use of renewable energy sources. = 该组织再次认可使用可再生能源。

过去完成时:
They had reendorsed the agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前再次批准了该协议。
The shareholders had reendorsed the CEO's strategy before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，股东们已经再次认可了首席执行官的战略。

将来时:
The party will reendorse its current leader. = 该党将再次认可其现任领导人。
The council will reendorse the building permit next week. = 理事会下周将再次批准该建筑许可证。

虚拟语气:
If they reendorsed the proposal, we would proceed. = 如果他们再次批准该提案，我们将继续进行。
I wish they had reendorsed the project earlier. = 我希望他们早点再次批准该项目。

被动语态:
