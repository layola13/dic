secularizes:/ˈsɛkjələrˌaɪzɪz/| vt.|世俗化；使脱离宗教影响|To make something secular; to remove something from religious influence or control.  The process of removing something from religious or spiritual influence or control, making it worldly or nonreligious. |例句：The government secularized the education system, removing religious instruction from schools.  政府将教育体系世俗化，取消了学校的宗教教育。

The increasing secularization of society is leading to a decline in religious observance. 社会日益世俗化导致宗教信仰的下降。|近义词：desacralize, de-sanctify, profane|反义词：sacralize, sanctify
