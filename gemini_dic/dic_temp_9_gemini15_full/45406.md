cupola:/ˈkjuːpələ/| n.|圆屋顶；穹顶；嘹望塔|A cupola is a small dome-like structure on top of a building, often used for ventilation or as an architectural feature.  It can also refer to a small tower or turret.|The church had a beautiful cupola, topped with a weather vane.|dome, turret, dome-shaped roof|flat roof|名词
