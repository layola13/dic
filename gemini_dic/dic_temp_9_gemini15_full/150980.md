parametritis:/ˌpærəməˈtraɪtɪs/| n. |输卵管炎|Inflammation of the parametrium (the connective tissue surrounding the uterus and fallopian tubes).  It is often caused by infection, typically spreading from the uterus or fallopian tubes. Symptoms can include pelvic pain, fever, and vaginal discharge.  Treatment usually involves antibiotics. |The patient was diagnosed with parametritis after experiencing severe pelvic pain and fever.|近义词：pelvic inflammatory disease (PID), salpingitis|反义词：无
