一般现在时:
The public outcries against the new policy. 公众强烈反对这项新政策。
Their children outcries every time they are disciplined. 他们的孩子每次受到管教都会大声哭喊。

一般过去时:
The villagers outcried against the unfair tax. 村民们强烈抗议不公平的税收。
The audience outcried their disapproval of the performance. 观众大声表达了他们对表演的不满。

现在完成时:
The environmentalists have outcried for years about the pollution.  环保主义者多年来一直在强烈抗议污染。
They have outcried against injustice for decades.  他们几十年来一直在强烈抗议不公正现象。

过去完成时:

She had outcried her feelings before collapsing. 她崩溃之前已经大声哭喊过她的感情了。

将来时:

We believe they will outcry for help if they are in danger. 我们相信如果他们有危险，他们会大声呼救的。

虚拟语气:
If they had outcried sooner, the situation might have been different. 如果他们早点强烈抗议，情况可能就不同了。
I wish they would outcry against the corruption. 我希望他们能强烈抗议腐败。
