recriticize:/ˌriːˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/| vt.|重新批评，再次批评|To criticize again or anew; to criticize further or more thoroughly.|例句：The professor recriticized the student's paper after reviewing it a second time. 教授在第二次审查后再次批评了学生的论文。

例句：The committee recriticized the initial proposal, suggesting several key improvements. 委员会再次批评了最初的提案，提出了几项关键改进建议。|近义词：re-examine, reassess, review|反义词：praise, commend

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recriticizes the plan every week. 他每周都重新批评这个计划。
They recriticize the artwork after the exhibition. 他们在展览结束后再次批评了这幅作品。

一般过去时:
She recriticized his essay after finding more grammatical errors.  她在发现更多语法错误后再次批评了他的文章。
The board recriticized the budget proposal due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，董事会再次批评了预算提案。

过去完成时:

The author had recriticized his own work before submitting it for publication. 作者在提交作品出版之前已经重新批评了自己的作品。
