destocking:/dɪˈstɒkɪŋ/| v.|去库存，减少库存 |The process of reducing the level of inventory held by a business.|例句：The company is destocking to reduce costs. 这家公司正在去库存以降低成本。

例句：The retailer initiated a destocking strategy to address overstocked items.  这家零售商启动了一项去库存策略来处理库存过多的商品。


近义词：inventory reduction, stock reduction, liquidation |反义词：stockpiling, accumulation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company destocks regularly to avoid overstocking. = 公司定期去库存以避免库存过剩。
They destock their products every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会清理库存产品。


一般过去时:
The store destocked its winter clothing line after the season ended. = 冬季过后，这家商店清仓处理了其冬季服装系列。
They destocked heavily in response to falling demand. = 由于需求下降，他们进行了大量的库存清理。

They have destocked their warehouse to make room for new products. = 他们已经清理了仓库的库存，为新产品腾出空间。
