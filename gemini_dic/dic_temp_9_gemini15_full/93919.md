immune:/ɪˈmjuːn/| adj.|免疫的；免除的|Protected from or resistant to a particular disease or condition.|The vaccine makes you immune to the disease.  疫苗使你对这种疾病免疫。|近义词：resistant, protected, unsusceptible|反义词：vulnerable, susceptible


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is immune to criticism. = 他对批评免疫。
She is immune to the virus. = 她对病毒免疫。

一般过去时:
He was immune to the effects of the poison. = 他对毒药的影响免疫。
The city was immune from attack. = 这个城市免受攻击。

现在完成时:
She has been immune to colds all winter. = 她整个冬天都没有感冒。
They have remained immune to the latest marketing trends. = 他们一直没有受到最新的营销趋势的影响。

过去完成时:
He had been immune to such flattery before. = 他以前就对这种奉承免疫。
The country had remained immune from foreign invasion for centuries. = 这个国家几个世纪以来一直免受外国侵略。

将来时:
She will be immune to the disease after the vaccination. = 接种疫苗后，她将对这种疾病免疫。
The new system will make the network immune to hacking attempts. = 新系统将使网络免受黑客攻击。

虚拟语气:
If he were immune to the disease, he wouldn't be worried. = 如果他对这种疾病免疫，他就不会担心了。
I wish I were immune to stress. = 我希望我对压力免疫。

被动语态:
He was made immune to the effects of the drug. = 他被使对药物的影响免疫。
The population is being made immune through a vaccination program. = 人口正在通过疫苗接种计划获得免疫力。

疑问句:
Is he immune to the effects of the medication? = 他对药物的影响免疫吗？
Are you immune to this type of virus? = 你对这种病毒免疫吗？

否定句:
He is not immune to the criticism. = 他对批评并不免疫。
She is not immune to the effects of stress. = 她并不免疫压力的影响。
