rhy:/raɪ/| n. |押韵；谐音| A rhyme is a repetition of similar sounds in two or more words, especially at the end of lines in poems or songs.  It's a literary device used to create musicality and memorability. | Example: "cat" and "hat" are perfect rhymes.  The poem uses a complex rhyme scheme. |近义词:rhythm, verse |反义词: dissonance
