shield:/ʃiːld/ | n. v. |盾牌；保护；庇护 | A protective covering or device.  |例句：He raised his shield to protect himself from the blow. 他举起盾牌保护自己免受打击。

例句：The government tried to shield the public from the bad news. 政府试图向公众隐瞒坏消息。|近义词：protect, defend, safeguard |反义词：expose, endanger, risk | 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shields his eyes from the sun. = 他用手遮挡阳光。
The mountain shields the valley from the wind. = 这座山保护山谷不受风吹。

一般过去时:
She shielded her child from the rain. = 她保护她的孩子免受雨淋。
They shielded the witness from the press. = 他们保护证人免受媒体的骚扰。

现在完成时:
He has shielded his family from the truth. = 他一直对家人隐瞒真相。
The forest has shielded the village from invaders for centuries. =  几个世纪以来，森林一直保护着村庄免受侵略者的侵害。

过去完成时:
She had shielded her secret for years. = 她多年来一直保守着这个秘密。
They had shielded the evidence from the police. = 他们此前一直向警方隐瞒证据。

将来时:
We will shield our company from the economic downturn. = 我们将保护我们的公司免受经济衰退的影响。
The new law will shield consumers from fraudulent practices. = 新法律将保护消费者免受欺诈行为的侵害。

虚拟语气:
If only I had shielded myself from that danger. =  如果我当时能保护自己免受那种危险就好了。
I wish I had shielded him from the truth. = 我真希望我当时没有告诉他真相。

被动语态:
The city was shielded from the enemy attack. = 城市免受了敌人的攻击。
The evidence was shielded by the lawyer. = 证据受到了律师的保护。

疑问句:
What can we do to shield ourselves from the storm? = 我们能做些什么来保护自己免受暴风雨的侵害？
How can we shield our children from the harmful effects of the internet? = 我们如何保护孩子免受互联网的有害影响？

否定句:
He didn't shield his eyes from the bright light. = 他没有遮挡刺眼的光线。
They failed to shield the crops from the frost. = 他们没能保护庄稼免受霜冻。


PHRASE->
shield from = 保护…免受…

词源：
The word "shield" comes from Old English *scild, meaning "shield, protection".  This word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings, and ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *skildō.
