declassify:/ˈdiːklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|解密；取消保密级别|To remove the classification from (a document or information), making it available to a wider audience.  The verb implies a formal process of removing security restrictions.|例句：The government decided to declassify the documents after 50 years. 政府决定在50年后解密这些文件。


例句：The agency declassified the files relating to the assassination. 机构解密了与暗杀相关的文件。


近义词：release, reveal, disclose, unseal


反义词：classify, encipher, redact, censor


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government declassifies sensitive information only under certain conditions. = 政府只有在特定条件下才会解密敏感信息。
They regularly declassify old documents. = 他们定期解密旧文件。


一般过去时:
The president declassified the files yesterday. = 总统昨天解密了这些文件。
The committee declassified the report last week. = 委员会上周解密了这份报告。


现在完成时:
They have declassified several important documents this year. = 今年他们已经解密了几份重要的文件。
The agency has declassified more information than ever before. = 该机构解密的信息比以往任何时候都多。


过去完成时:
The information had been declassified before the meeting. = 在会议之前，信息已经被解密了。
They had declassified the documents before the journalist could request access. = 在记者要求访问之前，他们已经解密了文件。


将来时:
The government will declassify the documents next year. = 政府明年将解密这些文件。
They will declassify further information in the coming months. = 他们将在接下来的几个月里解密更多信息。


虚拟语气:
If the government declassified the documents, the truth would be revealed. = 如果政府解密了文件，真相就会大白。
It would be beneficial if they declassified all the relevant documents. = 如果他们解密所有相关文件，将是有益的。


被动语态:
The documents were declassified after a lengthy review process. = 这些文件经过漫长的审查过程后被解密。
The information is being declassified gradually. = 信息正在逐步解密。


疑问句:
Will the government declassify the files? = 政府会解密这些文件吗？
When will the documents be declassified? = 文件什么时候会被解密？


否定句:
