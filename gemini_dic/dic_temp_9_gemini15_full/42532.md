countermanifesti: /ˌkaʊntərˈmænɪfɛstoʊ/ | n. | 反宣言 | A countermanifesto is a document that opposes or refutes the ideas presented in a previous manifesto.  It is often written in response to a political or ideological manifesto to challenge its claims or propose an alternative viewpoint.  | The student group issued a countermanifesto to the administration's proposed tuition hike.  学生团体发布了一份反宣言，反对学校提出的学费上涨。|  opposing statement, rebuttal, refutation |  manifesto (the original statement being countered) |
