get at: /ɡɛt æt/ | v. | 够得着；到达；理解；暗示；挖苦 | To reach or obtain something; to arrive at a place; to understand something; to imply or suggest something indirectly; to criticize or mock someone. | 近义词：reach, attain, understand, imply, insinuate | 反义词：miss, fail to understand


例句：

He tried to get at the apple on the high shelf. = 他试图够到高架上的苹果。

We finally got at the remote island after a long journey. = 在长途跋涉之后，我们终于到达了那个偏远的岛屿。


I couldn't get at what he meant. = 我不明白他的意思。

She got at the truth through careful questioning. = 她通过仔细的提问了解到了真相。


He was getting at me with his sarcastic remarks. = 他用他讽刺的言论挖苦我。

His comments were clearly getting at the inefficiency of the system. = 他的评论显然是在影射该系统的低效。
