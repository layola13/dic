cultishly:/ˈkʌltɪʃli/| adv.|狂热地，盲目崇拜地|In a manner characteristic of a cult or its followers; with excessive or uncritical devotion.  It suggests an intense, often unquestioning, adherence to a particular belief system or practice.|例句：He cultishly followed every instruction given by the guru. 他狂热地遵循大师的每一个指示。

例句：The band's fans cultishly memorized every lyric from their albums.乐队的粉丝们狂热地记住了他们专辑中的每一句歌词。


近义词：fanatically, zealously, obsessively, devotedly

反义词：indifferently, skeptically, critically

词性：副词
