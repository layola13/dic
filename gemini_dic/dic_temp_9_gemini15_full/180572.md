regionalists:/ˈriːdʒənəlɪsts/| n. |区域主义者，地方主义者 |Advocates for regional autonomy or cultural distinctiveness.  People who support regional interests and identities, often emphasizing local cultures and economies over national ones. | Example: The regionalists fought for greater control over their resources.  区域主义者为争取对其资源的更大控制权而斗争。|近义词：localists, sectionalists |反义词：nationalists, globalists |名词
