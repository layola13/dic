peloria:/pɪˈlɔːriə/| n.|  畸形花，辐射对称花 | A condition in plants where flowers that are normally zygomorphic (bilaterally symmetrical) develop radial symmetry (actinomorphic). This is often caused by genetic mutations.  | Example: The peloria in this snapdragon is striking, with its perfectly symmetrical petals. 这株金鱼草的畸形花非常醒目，花瓣完美对称。|近义词: abnormal flower, radially symmetrical flower |反义词: zygomorphic flower, bilaterally symmetrical flower
