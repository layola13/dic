diabolize:/ˈdaɪæbəlaɪz/| vt.|妖魔化；诽谤；歪曲|To represent as diabolic or evil; to slander; to distort.|例句：The media diabolize the opposition party's policies.媒体妖魔化了反对党的政策。|例句：He tried to diabolize his rival by spreading false rumors. 他试图通过散布谣言来诽谤他的竞争对手。|近义词：demonize, vilify, defame|反义词：glorify, praise, extol|动词
