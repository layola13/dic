post-diluvial:/ˌpoʊstdaɪˈluːviəl/| adj.| 洪水后的；大洪水以后的 | Relating to or denoting the period after the biblical Flood.  The term often implies a world transformed by the cataclysmic event, and sometimes suggests a simpler or more primitive state of society. | Example: Post-diluvial societies often had to rebuild their infrastructures and social structures. 洪水后的社会常常不得不重建他们的基础设施和社会结构。|近义词：post-flood, after the deluge |反义词：antediluvian
