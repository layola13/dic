europeanized:/ˌjʊərəpɪˈnaɪzd/| adj.|欧洲化的；具有欧洲特色的|Made to resemble or conform to European customs, styles, or practices.|The city has been significantly europeanized over the past century.  这座城市在过去的一个世纪里已经发生了显著的欧洲化。|近义词：westernized, modernized|反义词：localized, indigenized


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government europeanizes its policies gradually. = 政府逐渐将政策欧洲化。
This country is slowly becoming europeanized. = 这个国家正在缓慢地欧洲化。


一般过去时:
They europeanized the architecture of the building. = 他们使建筑的风格欧洲化。
The colonial powers europeanized many aspects of the local culture. = 殖民势力使当地文化的许多方面欧洲化。


现在完成时:
The country has been significantly europeanized since the end of the war. = 自战争结束以来，这个国家已经发生了显著的欧洲化。
Many aspects of their society have been europeanized over time. = 他们的社会许多方面随着时间的推移已经欧洲化。


过去完成时:
Before the reforms, the education system had not been fully europeanized. = 改革之前，教育体系尚未完全欧洲化。
They realized that the region had already been largely europeanized by previous administrations. = 他们意识到该地区已经被之前的政府在很大程度上欧洲化了。


将来时:
The government will europeanize the legal system within the next decade. = 政府将在未来十年内使法律体系欧洲化。
They predict that the region will be completely europeanized in the next fifty years. = 他们预测该地区将在未来五十年内完全欧洲化。


虚拟语气:
If they had europeanized the infrastructure earlier, the economic growth would have been faster. = 如果他们早些将基础设施欧洲化，经济增长就会更快。
I wish they had not europeanized the traditional festivals. = 我希望他们没有使传统节日欧洲化。


被动语态:
The educational system was europeanized during the 19th century. = 教育体系在19世纪被欧洲化了。
The local customs are being slowly europeanized by global influences. = 当地习俗正在被全球影响缓慢地欧洲化。


疑问句:
Has the country been fully europeanized? = 这个国家已经完全欧洲化了吗？
Was the architecture deliberately europeanized? = 建筑的设计是有意使其欧洲化吗？


否定句:
The government did not fully europeanize the economy. = 政府并没有完全使经济欧洲化。
They have not yet europeanized their educational curriculum. = 他们尚未使他们的教育课程欧洲化。
