checked:/tʃɛkt/ | adj. v. | 被检查过的；核实的；已勾选的；(动词过去式和过去分词)|  examined; verified; marked with a check;  |例句：I've checked my email. 我已经查看了我的邮件。The checked baggage is on the conveyor belt.  托运行李在传送带上。|近义词：verified, inspected, examined, confirmed | 反义词：unchecked, unverified


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He checks his email every morning. = 他每天早上都检查他的邮件。
She checks the spelling carefully. = 她仔细检查拼写。


一般过去时:
I checked the information before submitting the report. = 我在提交报告之前检查了信息。
They checked the equipment before starting the experiment. = 他们启动实验前检查了设备。


现在完成时:
I have checked the documents. = 我已经检查了文件。
She has checked the answers carefully. = 她已经仔细检查了答案。


过去完成时:
He had checked the engine before the race. = 他在比赛前检查过引擎。
We had checked our passports before we left for the airport. = 我们去机场之前已经检查了护照。


将来时:
I will check the results tomorrow. = 我明天将检查结果。
She will check the website for updates. = 她将检查网站更新。


虚拟语气:
If I had checked the map, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我当时查看地图的话，就不会迷路了。
I wish I had checked the weather forecast before leaving. = 我真希望我在离开之前查看了天气预报。


被动语态:
The documents were checked by the manager. = 文件由经理检查。
The system is checked regularly. = 系统定期检查。


疑问句:
Have you checked the address? = 你检查过地址了吗？
Did he check the oil level? = 他检查了油位吗？


否定句:
I didn't check the details. = 我没有检查细节。
She hasn't checked the booking yet. = 她还没有检查预订情况。


PHRASE->
check in = 登记入住
check out = 结账离开
check up = 检查身体
check with = 向…核实
check over = 仔细检查
