preaccepts:/ˌpriːəkˈsɛpts/| v.|预先接受，提前接受|To accept something before it is officially offered or presented.|The company preaccepts applications for their internship program. 公司提前接受实习项目的申请。|近义词：accept in advance, anticipate|反义词：reject, refuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preaccept applications every year. = 他们每年都提前接受申请。
The school preaccepts gifted students. = 学校预先录取优秀学生。


一般过去时:
The committee preaccepted his proposal. = 委员会提前接受了他的提案。
She preaccepted the job offer before the formal letter arrived. = 正式信函到达之前，她已经预先接受了这份工作邀请。


现在完成时:
They have preaccepted all the qualified candidates. = 他们已经预先接受了所有合格的候选人。
The university has preaccepted a record number of students this year. = 今年大学预先录取的学生人数创下了新纪录。


过去完成时:

The company had preaccepted several bids before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，公司已经预先接受了几份投标。
