paramento:/ˌpærəˈmɛntoʊ/| n. |祭衣；装饰品；礼服|Ornate vestments, especially those used in religious ceremonies.  A paramento is a decorative cloth or garment, particularly associated with religious or ceremonial use.  It can refer to the rich vestments worn by clergy or the decorative cloths used to adorn altars and other sacred objects.|The priest wore a magnificent paramento during the mass.  祭司在弥撒中穿着一件华丽的祭衣。|vestment, ornament, robe|  (无明确反义词，可根据语境选择如simple garment)|名词
