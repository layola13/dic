notable:/ˈnoʊtəbəl/| adj.|显著的；值得注意的；著名的|worthy of attention or notice; remarkable; distinguished.|The city boasts several notable landmarks. 这座城市拥有几个著名的地标。|remarkable, significant, prominent, important, famous|insignificant, unimportant, ordinary, common|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His achievements are notable. = 他的成就显著。
The museum houses many notable works of art. = 这个博物馆收藏着许多著名的艺术作品。


一般过去时:
The event was notable for its scale. = 这次活动以其规模而引人注目。
Her contribution to the project was notable. = 她对项目的贡献是显著的。


现在完成时:
Many notable changes have occurred in recent years. = 近年来发生了许多显著的变化。
Several notable scientists have won the Nobel Prize. = 一些著名的科学家获得了诺贝尔奖。


过去完成时:
They had already achieved notable success before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经取得了显著的成功。
The company had made several notable investments. = 这家公司已经进行了几项重要的投资。


将来时:
The upcoming conference will feature many notable speakers. = 即将举行的会议将邀请许多著名的演讲者。
His future work will be notable. = 他未来的作品将是引人注目的。


虚拟语气:
If his work were more notable, he would be more famous. = 如果他的作品更引人注目，他就会更出名。
I wish my contribution were more notable. = 我希望我的贡献更显著。


被动语态:
The problem was notable for its complexity. = 这个问题以其复杂性而引人注目。
His achievements are notably praised. = 他的成就得到了显著的赞扬。


疑问句:

否定句:

His performance was not notable. = 他的表现并不引人注目。
