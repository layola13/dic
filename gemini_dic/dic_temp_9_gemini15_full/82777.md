guiltier:/ˈɡɪltiər/| adj.| 更内疚的，更犯罪的 | More guilty; more culpable.  It implies a greater degree of guilt than "guilty." |例句：He felt guiltier after hearing the victim's testimony. 他在听到受害者的证词后感觉更内疚了。  She became guiltier with each passing day as the evidence mounted against her.  随着越来越多的证据指向她，她每天都感到更内疚。|近义词：more culpable, more at fault |反义词：less guilty, innocent
