humorize:/ˈhjuːməraɪz/ | vt. |使幽默化；使滑稽；迁就 |To make something humorous or to treat someone in a humorous way, often in a way that is indulgent or patronizing.  |例句：He humorized the situation by making a joke. 他通过开玩笑使情况变得幽默。
He tried to humorize his grumpy boss, but it didn't work. 他试图讨好他脾气暴躁的老板，但这并没有奏效。|近义词：amuse, entertain,  jest |反义词：serious, somber
