haulage:/ˈhɔːlɪdʒ/| n.|货物运输；搬运；运费|The transportation of goods, especially by road; the act of hauling.  It also refers to the cost of this transportation.|例句：The haulage of the heavy machinery was a complex operation.  重型机械的运输是一项复杂的操作。

例句：The haulage costs were unexpectedly high. 运费出乎意料的高。

近义词：carriage, transportation, freight, conveyance

反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of *delivery* if focusing on the movement aspect rather than the end result.

词性:名词
