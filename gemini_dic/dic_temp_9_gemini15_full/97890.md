inner-circle:/ˈɪnər ˈsɜːrkl/ | n. |核心圈，内部圈子 |A small group of people within a larger group who have more power or influence.  This term often refers to a close-knit group within an organization, social group, or family.  |例句：The inner circle of the company made all the important decisions. 公司的核心圈子做出了所有重要的决定。

The inner circle of his family knew about his illness before it was publicly announced.  他的家人内部圈子在他病情公开宣布之前就知道他的病情了。|近义词：inner group, elite, core group |反义词：outsiders, periphery, fringe
