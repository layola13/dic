pathways:/ˈpæθˌweɪz/ | n. |路径；途径；方法；通路 |  A pathway is a route or track, often a well-worn one,  or a series of steps or processes leading to a particular outcome.  It can refer to physical routes, like a garden pathway, or metaphorical routes, like career pathways or pathways to success. |例句：The garden pathways were lined with beautiful flowers. 花园小路两旁种满了美丽的鲜花。


The researchers are exploring new pathways for treating cancer. 研究人员正在探索治疗癌症的新途径。|近义词：routes, tracks, channels, avenues, approaches |反义词：dead ends, impasses
