hyperstimulation:/ˌhaɪpərˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |过度刺激；兴奋过度|Excessive stimulation, often resulting in an overreaction or negative consequences.  It can refer to various contexts, from physiological responses to emotional states. For example, in the context of ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome (OHSS), it refers to the excessive stimulation of the ovaries during fertility treatments.|The patient experienced hyperstimulation after receiving the medication.|overstimulation, overexcitation|understimulation, underactivation|名词
