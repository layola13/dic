oversolidify: /ˌoʊvərˈsɑːlədɪfaɪ/ | v. | 过度固化；凝固过度 | To solidify to an excessive degree; to become excessively hard or firm.  This term is primarily used in scientific and technical contexts, particularly in materials science and chemistry.  It describes a situation where a substance has become harder or more rigid than intended or desired. |


例句：

The concrete oversolidified due to the unusually hot weather. = 由于天气异常炎热，混凝土凝固过度。

The epoxy resin oversolidified, making it difficult to apply. = 环氧树脂凝固过度，难以涂抹。


近义词：overharden, excessively solidify

反义词：undercure, remain liquid


词性:动词
