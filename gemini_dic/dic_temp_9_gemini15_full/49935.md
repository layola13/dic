dendritic:/dɛnˈdrɪtɪk/| adj.|树状的，树枝状的|relating to or resembling a dendrite or dendrites; branching like a tree.  The term is often used to describe structures that have a branched, tree-like appearance.|例句：The dendritic structure of the neuron allows for extensive communication with other neurons.  神经元的树突状结构允许与其他神经元进行广泛的交流。

例句：The crystals exhibited a complex dendritic pattern.  这些晶体呈现出复杂的树枝状图案。


近义词：arborescent, ramified, branched, treelike|反义词：unbranched, linear, straight
