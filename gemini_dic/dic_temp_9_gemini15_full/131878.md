noncontagiousnesses:/ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒəsnisiz/| n.|非传染性；非接触性（复数）|The plural form of noncontagiousness, referring to instances or qualities of not being contagious or communicable.  It describes situations or conditions where something cannot be spread from one person or thing to another by contact or proximity.|例句：The study investigated the noncontagiousnesses of various viral infections in different populations. 这项研究调查了不同人群中各种病毒感染的非传染性。|近义词：non-communicabilities, non-transmissibilities|反义词：contagiousnesses
