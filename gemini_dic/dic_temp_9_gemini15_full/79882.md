gnat:/næt/| n.|小蚊子，蠓|A gnat is a very small flying insect, similar to a mosquito but usually not biting.|例句：A swarm of gnats buzzed around my head.  一群小蚊子在我头上嗡嗡叫。

例句：I swatted a gnat that landed on my arm. 我拍死了一只停在我胳膊上的小蚊子。|近义词：midge, mosquito|反义词：无
