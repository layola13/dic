disembark:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/ | vt. |下船；下车；下飞机 | To leave a ship, train, aircraft, or other vehicle.  |例句：Passengers disembarked from the ferry and headed towards the city.  乘客们下了渡轮，朝城市走去。

例句：The tourists disembarked at the airport and took a taxi to their hotel. 游客们在机场下飞机，然后乘出租车去酒店。


近义词：get off, alight, leave


反义词：embark


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disembark the train every morning. = 他们每天早上都下火车。
Passengers disembark at the designated platform. = 乘客在指定站台下车。


一般过去时:
We disembarked the plane and immediately went to the baggage claim. = 我们下了飞机，立刻去取行李。
The soldiers disembarked from the ship under the cover of darkness. = 士兵们在夜色的掩护下下了船。


现在完成时:
They have disembarked and are now waiting for their luggage. = 他们已经下船/车/飞机了，现在正在等行李。
The passengers have disembarked safely. = 乘客们安全下船/车/飞机了。


过去完成时:
We had disembarked before the storm hit. = 我们在暴风雨来临之前就已经下船/车/飞机了。
The crew had disembarked the passengers before the ship started to sink. = 船员们在船开始下沉之前已经让乘客下船了。


将来时:
We will disembark in London tomorrow. = 我们明天将在伦敦下船/车/飞机。
The flight will disembark at gate 12. = 这趟航班将在12号登机口下客。


虚拟语气:
If we had disembarked earlier, we wouldn't have missed the bus. = 如果我们早点下船/车/飞机，就不会错过公共汽车了。
I wish I had disembarked before the security check. = 我真希望在安检之前就下船/车/飞机了。


被动语态:
The passengers were disembarked quickly and efficiently. = 乘客们被迅速而有效地安排下船/车/飞机。
The luggage was disembarked first. = 行李先被卸下。


疑问句:
Where will we disembark? = 我们将在哪里下船/车/飞机？
When do the passengers disembark? = 乘客们什么时候下船/车/飞机？


否定句:
The passengers did not disembark until the captain gave the order. = 直到船长下令，乘客们才下船。
We couldn't disembark because of the strong winds. = 由于强风，我们无法下船/车/飞机。
