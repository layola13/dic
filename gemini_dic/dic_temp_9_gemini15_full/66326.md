excretion:/ɪkˈskriːʃən/ | n. | 排泄；排泄物 | The process of eliminating waste products of metabolism from the body.  It also refers to the waste product itself.  Examples include sweat, urine, and feces. | The excretion of toxins is vital for maintaining good health. 排泄毒素对维持健康至关重要。|  Elimination, discharge, secretion | Retention, absorption | 名词
