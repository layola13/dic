high-fived:/haɪˈfaɪvd/| v.|击掌庆祝|To simultaneously slap each other's raised open hands in a gesture of celebration, agreement, or congratulation.  This action is typically done with enthusiasm and excitement.|例句：We high-fived after successfully completing the project. 我们成功完成项目后击掌庆祝。|例句：They high-fived each other when their team scored a goal. 他们的球队进球后他们互相击掌庆祝。|近义词：slapped hands, celebrated, congratulated|反义词：ignored, snubbed
