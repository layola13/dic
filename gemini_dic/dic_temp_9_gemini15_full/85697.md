haven't:/ˈhævnt/ | v. | 没有，尚未 |  Contraction of "have not," used primarily in the present perfect tense to express a negative action or state that began in the past and continues to the present, or an action that has not yet happened. |


例句：I haven't finished my work yet. = 我还没完成我的工作。

例句：They haven't seen the movie before. = 他们以前没看过这部电影。


近义词：have not, do not have


反义词：have, possess


词性：动词(缩略形式)
