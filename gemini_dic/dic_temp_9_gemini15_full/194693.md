self-wearinesses:/selfˈwɪəri.nəsɪz/| n.|自我厌倦，自我疲惫|The plural form of "self-weariness," referring to multiple instances or aspects of feeling tired of oneself, one's actions, or one's personality.  It suggests a state of being burdened by one's own self, leading to feelings of exhaustion, dissatisfaction, and a sense of being overwhelmed by one's own existence. |例句：His self-wearinesses manifested as a profound apathy and a lack of motivation.  他自我厌倦的表现是极度冷漠和缺乏动力。


His numerous self-wearinesses led him to seek solace in solitude.他多方面的自我厌倦使他寻求独处的慰藉。|近义词：self-loathing, self-disgust, exhaustion, burnout, ennui|反义词：self-love, self-acceptance, vitality, energy|名词
