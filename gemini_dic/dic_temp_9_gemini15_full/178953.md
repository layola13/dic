redeclined:/ˌriːdɪˈklaɪnd/| v. |重新拒绝；再次谢绝 |To decline again; to refuse a second time.  |例句：He redeclined the offer, stating that the terms were still unacceptable. 他再次拒绝了这个提议，声称条款仍然不可接受。|例句：After initially considering the proposal, she redeclined, citing unforeseen circumstances. 在最初考虑了提议之后，她又拒绝了，理由是出现了不可预见的情况。|近义词：refuse again, reject again, turn down again |反义词：accept, agree to
