crabgrass:/ˈkræbˌɡræs/ | n. | 蟋蟀草，狗牙根 | A low-growing, spreading weed with hairy stems and leaves, common in lawns and gardens.  It is notoriously difficult to control. | 例句：The crabgrass was choking the life out of my lawn.  = 蟋蟀草正在扼杀我的草坪的生命。

The crabgrass spread rapidly across the neglected garden. = 蟋蟀草迅速蔓延到被忽视的花园里。| 近义词：weed, turfgrass (in a negative context) | 反义词：desirable lawn grass
