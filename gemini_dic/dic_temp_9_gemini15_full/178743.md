rection:/rɪˈɛkʃən/| n. |反应，反作用|A reaction is a response to something. It can be a physical, chemical, or emotional response.|例句：His reaction to the news was anger.  他的反应是愤怒。|例句：The chemical reaction produced heat.  化学反应产生了热量。|近义词：response, reply, effect|反义词：action (in some contexts), inaction|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chemical reaction produces heat. = 化学反应产生热量。
Her reaction to the news is surprising. = 她对这个消息的反应令人惊讶。

一般过去时:
His reaction to the insult was immediate. = 他对侮辱的反应是立即的。
The experiment showed a strong reaction. = 实验显示了一个强烈的反应。

现在完成时:
Scientists have studied this reaction extensively. = 科学家们已经广泛研究了这个反应。
My body has had an allergic reaction to the medication. = 我的身体对这种药物有过过敏反应。

过去完成时:
They had already anticipated the market's reaction. = 他们已经预料到了市场的反应。
The patient had shown no adverse reaction to the treatment. = 病人对治疗没有表现出任何不良反应。

将来时:
We will observe the reaction closely. = 我们将密切观察反应。
The scientists predict a violent reaction. = 科学家们预测会发生剧烈的反应。

虚拟语气:
If I had known his reaction, I would have acted differently. = 如果我知道他的反应，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish my reaction had been calmer. = 我希望我的反应能更冷静一些。

被动语态:
The experiment was carefully monitored for any unexpected reactions. = 实验被仔细监测以防出现任何意外反应。
The chemical reaction is being studied in detail. = 化学反应正在被详细研究。

疑问句:
What was his reaction to the proposal? = 他对这个提议的反应是什么？
What is the expected reaction time? = 预计的反应时间是多少？

否定句:
There was no immediate reaction to the announcement. = 宣布后没有立即反应。
She showed no visible reaction to the criticism. = 她对批评没有表现出任何明显的反应。
