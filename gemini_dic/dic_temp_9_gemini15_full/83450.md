habits:/ˈhæbɪts/| n.|习惯，习性|Established patterns of behavior.|例句：He has a bad habit of biting his nails. 他有咬指甲的坏习惯。|例句：It's important to develop healthy habits.  养成健康的生活习惯很重要。|近义词：customs, routines, practices|反义词：uncustomary, unusual


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has good study habits. = 他有良好的学习习惯。
She maintains healthy eating habits. = 她保持着健康的饮食习惯。

一般过去时:
His habits changed after the accident. = 事故后他的习惯改变了。
They had different habits in the past. = 他们过去有不同的习惯。

现在完成时:
I have developed some new habits this year. = 今年我养成了一些新的习惯。
She has broken several bad habits recently. = 她最近改掉了一些坏习惯。

过去完成时:
He had already formed those habits by the time he was ten. = 十岁时他已经养成了那些习惯。
They had never considered changing their habits before. = 以前他们从未想过改变他们的习惯。

将来时:
I will try to break these bad habits. = 我将尝试改掉这些坏习惯。
She will likely develop new habits in her new job. = 在她的新工作中，她可能会养成新的习惯。

虚拟语气:
If I had better habits, I would be more productive. = 如果我有更好的习惯，我会更高效。
I wish I had the habit of exercising regularly. = 我希望我有定期锻炼的习惯。

被动语态:
Habits are formed over time. = 习惯是随着时间推移而形成的。
Good habits are often praised. = 良好的习惯经常受到赞扬。

疑问句:
What are your daily habits? = 你每天的习惯是什么？
Does he have any unusual habits? = 他有什么不寻常的习惯吗？

否定句:
He doesn't have any bad habits. = 他没有任何坏习惯。
She doesn't want to change her habits. = 她不想改变她的习惯。
