holies:/ˈhoʊliz/ | n. |圣地；圣物 |The holies are places considered sacred and consecrated to God or a deity.  Holies often hold religious significance and are associated with spiritual practices and beliefs.  |例句：Many pilgrims travel to the holies to pray and seek spiritual guidance. 许多朝圣者前往圣地祈祷并寻求精神指导。
