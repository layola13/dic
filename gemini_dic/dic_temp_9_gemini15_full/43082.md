cowriter:/ˈkoʊraɪtər/| n. |合著者，共同撰写者 | A cowriter is a person who writes something, such as a book, article, or screenplay, together with another person or people.  They share the authorship and typically collaborate on the creative process. | Example:  She and her cowriter spent months refining the script. 她和她的合著者花了几个月的时间来修改剧本。


The novel was a collaborative effort, with three cowriters sharing the credit. 这部小说是合作的成果，三位合著者共同署名。|近义词：collaborator, coauthor |反义词：sole author, single author
