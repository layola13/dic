glyphs:/ˈɡlɪfs/ | n. | 象形文字；符号；刻痕 |  Symbols or characters, especially those used in a writing system.  Glyphs can be pictorial, representing objects or ideas directly, or abstract, representing sounds or concepts.  The term is often used in reference to ancient writing systems or in typography to refer to the visual shapes of letters and characters. | The ancient Egyptians used complex glyphs in their hieroglyphic writing system.  古代埃及人使用复杂的象形文字书写系统。
