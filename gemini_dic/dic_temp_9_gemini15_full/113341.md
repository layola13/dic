luxuriates:/ˈlʌkʃəriˌeɪts/| v.|尽情享受；沉溺于；豪华地装饰|To take great pleasure in; to indulge oneself.|例句：She luxuriates in the warmth of the sun. 她尽情享受阳光的温暖。|例句：The hotel luxuriates in marble and gold. 这家酒店装饰豪华，遍布大理石和黄金。|近义词：indulges, revels, wallows|反义词：suffers, endures, abstains


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He luxuriates in his success. = 他沉溺于自己的成功之中。
She luxuriates in a long hot bath. = 她享受着长时间的热水浴。

一般过去时:
They luxuriated in the beautiful scenery. = 他们尽情欣赏美丽的风景。
He luxuriated in the comfort of his armchair. = 他舒适地坐在扶手椅上享受着。

现在完成时:
She has luxuriated in the peace and quiet of the countryside. = 她一直享受着乡村的宁静与安详。
We have luxuriated in our newfound freedom. = 我们一直沉醉于我们新获得的自由。

过去完成时:
They had luxuriated in the sun all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在享受阳光。
She had luxuriated in the thought of her upcoming vacation. = 她一直在憧憬即将到来的假期。

将来时:
We will luxuriate in the spa this weekend. = 我们这个周末将在水疗中心尽情享受。
They will luxuriate in the comfort of their new home. = 他们将享受他们新家的舒适。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would luxuriate in a long nap. = 如果我有时间，我会痛痛快快地睡个午觉。
I wish I could luxuriate in a tropical beach. = 我真希望能在热带海滩上尽情享受。

被动语态:  (Luxuriate is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an active indulgence.)  


疑问句:
Does he luxuriate in his wealth? = 他沉溺于他的财富吗？
Do they luxuriate in the finer things in life? = 他们沉溺于生活中的美好事物吗？

否定句:
He doesn't luxuriate in idleness. = 他并不沉溺于懒散。
She doesn't luxuriate in the spotlight. = 她不喜欢成为众人瞩目的焦点。
