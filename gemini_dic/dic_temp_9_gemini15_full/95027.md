inaccordant:/ˌɪnəˈkɔːrdənt/| adj. |不一致的；不协调的；不和谐的 |Not in agreement; incongruous.  In geology, referring to rock strata that are not parallel. |例句：Their opinions on the matter were inaccordant. 他们在这件事上的意见不一致。

例句：The newly discovered rock layer was inaccordant with the surrounding strata. 新发现的岩层与周围的地层不一致。

近义词：discordant, inconsistent, incompatible, conflicting

反义词：accordant, consistent, compatible, harmonious

词性：形容词

词源:
The word "inaccordant" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "accordant," which itself derives from the Old French "acordant" and ultimately from the Latin "accordare" (to agree).  The geological usage reflects the lack of agreement or parallelism in rock formations.
