by half:/baɪ hɑːf/| adv.|一半地，减半地|This phrase means "to a considerable extent" or "significantly".  It implies a substantial reduction or decrease.|The price was reduced by half. 价格降低了一半。|近义词：considerably, significantly, substantially|反义词：fully, completely
