manufactory:/ˌmænjuˈfæktəri/| n. |工厂，制造厂 |A manufactory is a building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured.  It is often used in a more formal or historical context.  |The old manufactory has been converted into apartments.  这座旧工厂已被改造成公寓。|factory, plant, mill|workshop, studio (for smaller operations)
