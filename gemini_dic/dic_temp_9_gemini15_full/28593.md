caregiver:/ˈkerɡɪvər/ | n. | 保姆；护理人员；照顾者 | A person who cares for another person, especially a child, elderly person, or sick person.  | Example: My grandmother is a caregiver for her ailing husband.  我的祖母照顾她生病的丈夫。  She works as a caregiver in a nursing home.她在养老院做护理人员。 |近义词：nurse, attendant, guardian | 反义词：patient, recipient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The caregiver bathes the patient every morning. = 保姆每天早上给病人洗澡。
She provides care for her elderly mother. = 她照顾她年迈的母亲。


一般过去时:
The caregiver assisted the patient with their medication. = 保姆协助病人服药。
He cared for his sick father until his passing. = 他照顾他生病的父亲直到他去世。


现在完成时:
She has cared for her disabled brother for many years. = 她多年来一直照顾她残疾的弟弟。
The caregiver has administered the medication on time. = 保姆已按时给药。


过去完成时:
The family had hired a caregiver before the surgery. = 手术前，家人已经雇佣了一名护理人员。
The patient had received excellent care from his caregiver. = 病人已经从他的护理人员那里得到了很好的照顾。


将来时:

They will continue to provide care for their loved one. = 他们将继续照顾他们所爱的人。


虚拟语气:
If I were a caregiver, I would be more patient. = 如果我是护理人员，我会更有耐心。

被动语态:
