centurion:/senˈtʃʊəriən/| n.|百夫长，罗马军队中的一百人队队长|A centurion was a military officer in the Roman army commanding a century (a unit of 100 men).  Centurions were typically experienced soldiers who rose through the ranks. They played a crucial role in Roman military success.|Example: The centurion ordered his men to attack. 百夫长命令他的士兵进攻。  The brave centurion led his century into battle. 勇敢的百夫长带领他的百人队冲锋陷阵。|近义词：captain, commander, officer|反义词：private, soldier (in the sense of a lower rank)|名词
