remotivation:/ˌriːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃən/| n. |重新激励；再激励|The process of rekindling motivation or enthusiasm in someone who has lost it.  It often involves addressing the underlying reasons for the loss of motivation and providing new goals or incentives.|Example:  The remotivation program helped the patients regain their interest in life.  重新激励计划帮助病人重新燃起了对生活的兴趣。|近义词：reinvigoration, re-energizing, inspiration|反义词：demotivation, discouragement|名词
