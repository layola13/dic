claimer:/ˈkleɪmər/| n. |索赔人；声称者；(尤指)专利权人|A person who makes a claim, especially a claim for compensation.  A person who asserts a right or title to something.|例句：The insurance claimer submitted all the necessary documents.  保险索赔人提交了所有必要的证件。
例句：The software developer is the claimer of the patent.  软件开发者是该专利的专利权人。|近义词： claimant, asserter |反义词：denier


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The claimer requests a refund. = 索赔人要求退款。
He claims ownership of the land. = 他声称拥有这块土地的所有权。

一般过去时:
The claimer filed a complaint. = 索赔人提交了一份投诉。
She claimed she was innocent. = 她声称自己是无辜的。

现在完成时:
The claimer has provided all the evidence. = 索赔人已经提供了所有证据。
He has claimed the prize. = 他已经领取了奖品。

过去完成时:
The claimer had already submitted the forms. = 索赔人已经提交了表格。
She had claimed the reward before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，她已经领取了奖励。

将来时:
The claimer will appeal the decision. = 索赔人将对该决定提出上诉。
He will claim his inheritance. = 他将继承他的遗产。

虚拟语气:
If he were the claimer, he would have acted differently. = 如果他是索赔人，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish I were the claimer of that lottery. = 我希望我是那个彩票的获奖者。

被动语态:
The claim was denied by the company. = 该索赔被公司拒绝了。
His ownership was claimed by another person. = 他的所有权被另一个人声称。

疑问句:
Who is the claimer of this property? = 这处房产的索赔人是谁？
Is he the rightful claimer? = 他是合法索赔人吗？

否定句:
He is not the claimer of the stolen goods. = 他不是被盗物品的索赔人。
She did not claim any responsibility. = 她没有承担任何责任。
