eyedrop:/ˈaɪdrɑːp/| n. |眼药水|A liquid medicine or solution, typically sterile, that is instilled into the eye to treat infections, lubricate the eye, or relieve irritation.  Eyedrops are usually dispensed in small bottles with droppers for accurate application.|例句：The doctor prescribed eyedrops to alleviate the dryness in my eyes.  医生开了眼药水来缓解我的眼睛干涩。

例句：She carefully applied the eyedrops to her affected eye. 她小心地将眼药水滴入患病的眼睛。


近义词：ocular drops, eye lubricant


反义词：无


词性：名词
