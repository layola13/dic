fulling:/ˈfʊlɪŋ/| n. v.|使…起毛；缩绒；整理；填塞|The process of thickening and matting woolen cloth by beating or pressing it while wet.  This process fulls the fabric, making it denser and more resistant to wear.  |例句：The millers were busy fulling the wool. 磨坊主正忙着给羊毛缩绒。

The process of filling or stuffing something.  |例句：The cracks in the wall needed fulling with cement. 墙上的裂缝需要用水泥填塞。|近义词：felting, thickening, padding, stuffing|反义词：thinning, unraveling
