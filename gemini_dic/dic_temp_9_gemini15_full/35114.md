cloudberry:/ˈklaʊdbɛri/| n. |云莓|A small, reddish-orange aggregate fruit that grows in cool, northern climates.  It is tart and slightly sweet, often used in jams, preserves, and liqueurs.  The plant itself is a low-growing shrub with white flowers.| 例句：The cloudberries were ripe and ready for picking. 云莓成熟了，可以采摘了。
