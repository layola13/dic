friend:/frɛnd/| n. |朋友，友人|A person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection, typically exclusive of sexual or family relations.  Friends share common interests, support each other, and enjoy each other's company.|例句：He is my best friend. 他是我最好的朋友。|例句：I have many friends at work. 我在工作中有许多朋友。|近义词：companion, pal, buddy, mate|反义词：enemy, foe, adversary|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have many friends. = 我有很多朋友。
She is a good friend to me. = 她对我来说是个好朋友。

一般过去时:
I met a new friend yesterday. = 我昨天认识了一个新朋友。
We were friends in college. = 我们在大学时是朋友。

现在完成时:
I have known her for many years. = 我认识她很多年了。
We have been friends since childhood. = 我们从小就是朋友。

过去完成时:
I had already made several friends before the trip. = 在旅行之前我已经交了好几个朋友了。
They had been friends for ten years before they had a falling out. = 他们成为朋友十年后才闹翻。

将来时:
I will make new friends in my new city. = 我会在我的新城市结交新朋友。
We will remain friends forever. = 我们将永远是朋友。

虚拟语气:
If I had more friends, I would be happier. = 如果我有更多朋友，我会更快乐。
I wish I had a friend I could confide in. = 我希望我有一个可以信赖的朋友。

被动语态:
He was betrayed by his supposed friend. = 他被所谓的友人背叛了。
She was considered a good friend by everyone. = 她被大家认为是好朋友。

疑问句:

Who is your closest friend? = 你最亲密的朋友是谁？

否定句:

He is not my friend. = 他不是我的朋友。
