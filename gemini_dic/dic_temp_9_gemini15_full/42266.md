counter-blast:/ˈkaʊntəblæst/| n. |反击；回击；强烈的反驳| A strong reply or reaction to an attack or criticism; a counter-attack.  It often involves a forceful and direct response aimed at negating or discrediting the original attack. |例句：The company issued a counter-blast to the negative press coverage.  公司对负面新闻报道进行了强烈的反驳。|例句：His speech was a counter-blast against the government's policies.  他的演讲是对政府政策的强烈反驳。|近义词：retort, counterattack, rebuttal, rejoinder |反义词：surrender, acquiescence
