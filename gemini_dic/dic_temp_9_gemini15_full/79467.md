gloat:/ɡləʊt/| v.|幸灾乐祸，沾沾自喜|To feel or express great pleasure or satisfaction, especially in a smug or self-satisfied way, at someone else's misfortune or bad luck.|例句：He gloated over his rival's defeat. 他为对手的失败而沾沾自喜。|例句：She gloated about her promotion for weeks. 她得意洋洋地炫耀了她升职的消息好几个星期。|近义词：rejoice, revel, triumph, exult|反义词：mourn, grieve, sympathize

一般现在时:
He gloats over his success. = 他对自己的成功沾沾自喜。
They gloat about their wealth. = 他们炫耀自己的财富。

一般过去时:
She gloated over her exam results. = 她对她的考试成绩沾沾自喜。
He gloated about winning the game. = 他得意洋洋地炫耀赢了比赛。

现在完成时:
He has gloated about his victory ever since. = 从那以后他就一直为他的胜利而沾沾自喜。
They have gloated over their opponent's failure. = 他们一直为对手的失败而幸灾乐祸。

过去完成时:
She had gloated about her promotion before she even received the official confirmation. = 甚至在她收到正式确认之前，她就对自己的晋升沾沾自喜。
He had gloated over his rival's downfall for months. = 他为对手的垮台幸灾乐祸了好几个月。

将来时:
They will gloat if their team wins. = 如果他们的球队赢了，他们会沾沾自喜。
She will gloat over her success in the competition. = 她将会为她在比赛中的成功而沾沾自喜。

虚拟语气:
If he won, he would gloat incessantly. = 如果他赢了，他会没完没了地沾沾自喜。
I wish I hadn't gloated so much. = 我真希望我当时没有那么得意洋洋。

被动语态:
His success was gloated over by his friends. = 他的成功让他的朋友们沾沾自喜。
The victory was gloated over by the whole team. = 整个团队都为这场胜利而沾沾自喜。

疑问句:
Does he gloat about everything he achieves? = 他是否对取得的每一项成就都沾沾自喜？
Will they gloat if they win the tournament? = 如果他们赢得锦标赛，他们会沾沾自喜吗？

否定句:
He didn't gloat about his success; he was humble. = 他并没有为自己的成功而沾沾自喜；他很谦虚。
She didn't gloat, even though she knew she was better. = 即使她知道自己更好，她也并没有沾沾自喜。
