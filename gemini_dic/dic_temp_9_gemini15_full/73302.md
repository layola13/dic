forecasters:/fɔːrˈkæstərz/ | n. |预报员，预测者 |People who predict future events, especially weather.  They use data and models to make their predictions. |例句：The forecasters predicted a severe storm for tomorrow.  预报员预测明天将有一场严重的暴风雨。

例句：Weather forecasters are using advanced technology to improve the accuracy of their predictions. 气象预报员正在使用先进技术来提高预测的准确性。


近义词：predictors, prophets (in a less literal sense)


反义词：None directly opposite, but  "those who deny predictions" could be considered a contrasting group.


词性：名词
