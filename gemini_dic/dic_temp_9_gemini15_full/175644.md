ransacker:/ˈrænsækər/| n. |洗劫者；抢劫者；彻底搜查者 |A ransacker is someone who thoroughly searches a place, often violently and with the intention of stealing something.  They might be looting during a riot or burglarizing a home. |例句：The ransackers left the house in ruins.  洗劫者把房子弄得一片狼藉。

The police apprehended the ransackers after they escaped with the stolen goods.  警方在抢劫犯带着赃物逃跑后将其逮捕。|近义词：looter, plunderer, burglar, robber |反义词：protector, preserver
