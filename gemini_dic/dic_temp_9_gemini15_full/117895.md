melons:/ˈmɛlənz/ | n. |甜瓜；西瓜 |The word "melons" refers to the large, round fruit of various plants, especially those of the genus Cucumis and Citrullus, known for their sweet taste and juicy flesh.  Many varieties exist, including watermelons, cantaloupes, honeydews, and muskmelons.  They are commonly eaten fresh, juiced, or used in desserts and other culinary preparations. |例句：We bought a few watermelons and cantaloupes at the farmer's market. 我们在农贸市场买了几个西瓜和香瓜。

例句：The dessert included a scoop of ice cream and chunks of honeydew melon. 甜点包括一勺冰淇淋和几块哈密瓜。
