eaten:/ˈiːtən/| v. |吃过的，被吃掉的|Past participle of "eat".  It indicates that the action of eating has been completed.  It can function as an adjective or part of a verb phrase.|例句：The cake has been eaten.  蛋糕已经被吃掉了。
The apples were eaten by the birds. 苹果被鸟吃掉了。|近义词：consumed, devoured, ingested|反义词:uneaten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I eat breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上吃早餐。
They eat dinner at 7 pm. = 他们晚上七点吃晚餐。


一般过去时:
I ate a sandwich for lunch. = 我午餐吃了一个三明治。
She ate all the cookies. = 她把所有的饼干都吃掉了。


现在完成时:
I have eaten all the pizza. = 我已经把所有的披萨都吃掉了。
They have eaten lunch already. = 他们已经吃过午饭了。


过去完成时:
I had eaten dinner before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经吃过晚饭了。
She had eaten all the cake before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经把所有的蛋糕都吃掉了。


将来时:
I will eat dinner with my family tonight. = 今晚我将和家人一起吃晚餐。
They will have eaten by the time we arrive. = 我们到达时，他们就已经吃过了。


虚拟语气:
If I had eaten breakfast, I wouldn't be so hungry now. = 如果我吃过早餐，我现在就不会这么饿了。
I wish I had eaten less at the party. = 我真希望在派对上少吃点。


被动语态:
All the food was eaten. = 所有的食物都被吃掉了。
The cake is being eaten. = 蛋糕正在被吃。


疑问句:
Have you eaten yet? = 你吃了吗？
Has the dog eaten its food? = 狗吃过它的食物了吗？


否定句:
I haven't eaten anything today. = 我今天什么都没吃。
They didn't eat breakfast this morning. = 他们今天早上没吃早餐。


PHRASE->
eaten away: 被腐蚀掉
eaten up:  被吃光；被吞噬
eaten into: 侵蚀；损耗
eaten out: 在外面吃饭
have eaten: 吃过(表示经历)
