decisive:/dɪˈsaɪsɪv/| adj.|决定性的；果断的；明确的 |  Characterized by or displaying decisiveness; having the power or quality of deciding.  A decisive action or event settles a matter conclusively.  |例句：The battle was decisive, ending the war. 这场战役是决定性的，结束了战争。

The judge's ruling was decisive, leaving no room for appeal.  法官的裁决是决定性的，没有上诉的空间。


近义词：conclusive, definitive, conclusive, resolute, firm


反义词：indecisive, irresolute, hesitant, wavering


词性：形容词
