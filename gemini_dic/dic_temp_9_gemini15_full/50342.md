deploys:/dɪˈplɔɪz/| vt.|部署，调动，运用|To put into service or position; to utilize effectively.|例句：The army deployed its troops along the border.  军队沿边界部署了部队。|例句：The company deployed a new marketing strategy. 公司部署了一个新的营销策略。|近义词：implement, utilize, employ|反义词：withdraw, remove, abandon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company deploys new software every quarter. = 公司每季度部署新软件。
He deploys his time very efficiently. = 他非常有效率地利用时间。


一般过去时:
They deployed additional resources to the project. = 他们为项目部署了额外的资源。
The general deployed his troops strategically. = 将军战略性地部署了他的军队。


现在完成时:
We have deployed all available personnel. = 我们已经部署了所有可用人员。
The team has successfully deployed the new system. = 团队已成功部署了新系统。


过去完成时:
They had deployed the troops before the attack began. = 攻击开始前他们已经部署了军队。
The company had already deployed the marketing campaign before the product launch. = 产品发布前，公司已经启动了营销活动。


将来时:
We will deploy the new update next week. = 我们下周将部署新的更新。
They will deploy more staff to handle the increased workload. = 他们将部署更多员工来处理增加的工作量。


虚拟语气:
If we deployed more resources, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果我们部署更多资源，我们就能更早完成项目。
It would be wise to deploy a different strategy. = 部署不同的策略将会明智一些。


被动语态:
The software was deployed successfully. = 软件已成功部署。
New security measures are being deployed across the company. = 新的安全措施正在公司范围内部署。


疑问句:
When will the new system be deployed? = 新系统何时部署？
How many troops have been deployed to the region? = 已有多少军队部署到该地区？


否定句:
They did not deploy the necessary resources. = 他们没有部署必要的资源。
The plan was not deployed as intended. = 计划没有按预期部署。
