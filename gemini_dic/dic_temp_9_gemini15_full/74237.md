fowlings:/ˈfaʊlɪŋz/| n.|家禽肉；猎物；(尤指)家禽的肉|Poultry or game birds, especially when prepared as food.  The word often implies the birds were hunted or raised for consumption.|例句：The fowlings were roasted to perfection.  家禽肉烤得恰到好处。

例句：The hunter brought back a fine bag of fowlings.  猎人带回了一大包猎物。（主要是鸟类）


近义词：poultry, game, birds


反义词：无


词性：名词
