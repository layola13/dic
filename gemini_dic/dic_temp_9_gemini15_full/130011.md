non-obligatory: /ˌnɒnˈɒblɪɡətəri/ | adj. | 非强制性的，非义务性的 | Not compulsory; not required.  |  Attending the meeting is non-obligatory.  参加会议是非强制性的。 The extra work is non-obligatory.额外的活儿是非强制性的。| 近义词：optional, voluntary, discretionary | 反义词：mandatory, compulsory, obligatory


USAGE->

The extra work is non-obligatory. = 额外的活儿是非强制性的。
This course is non-obligatory for graduation. = 这门课不是毕业的必修课。


一般过去时:

If the task were non-obligatory, I would not do it. = 如果这项任务是非强制性的，我不会做。
