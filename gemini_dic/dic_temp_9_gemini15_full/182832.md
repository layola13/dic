repeats:/rɪˈpiːts/| v.|重复，反复|to say or do something again.|The teacher repeats the instructions.老师重复了指示。  The pattern repeats itself every four bars.  这个图案每四个小节重复一次。|近义词：reiterates, re-iterates, reiterates|反义词：varies, differs


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repeats the same mistake every time. 他每次都犯同样的错误。
She repeats the words slowly and clearly. 她缓慢而清晰地重复着那些话。


一般过去时:
The bird repeated its song.  鸟儿重复了它的歌声。
He repeated the question, hoping for a clearer answer. 他重复了这个问题，希望能得到更清晰的答案。


现在完成时:
She has repeated the experiment several times. 她已经重复做了几次实验。
He has repeated his apologies many times. 他已经多次道歉了。


过去完成时:
He had repeated the story many times before anyone believed him. 在有人相信他之前，他已经多次重复了这个故事。
She had repeated the exercise until she got it right. 她一直重复练习直到做对为止。


将来时:
I will repeat the experiment tomorrow. 我明天将重复这个实验。
They will repeat their performance next week. 他们下周将重复他们的表演。


虚拟语气:
If I repeated the process, I might get a different result. 如果我重复这个过程，我可能会得到不同的结果。
I wish I hadn't repeated that silly joke. 我真希望我没有重复那个愚蠢的笑话。


被动语态:
The experiment was repeated by another scientist. 实验被另一位科学家重复了。
The instructions were repeated for clarity. 指示被重复以求清晰。


疑问句:
Will you repeat that, please? 请你再说一遍好吗？
Does he often repeat himself? 他经常重复自己说过的话吗？


否定句:
He did not repeat the experiment. 他没有重复实验。
She didn't repeat her mistake. 她没有重复她的错误。
