It implies a reciprocal action, unlike simply being devoured. The prefix "inter-" signifies mutual or reciprocal action.  | Example: In the chaotic aftermath of the battle, the warring factions lay interdevoured, their armies decimated by mutual destruction.  在战斗混乱的余波中，交战各派互相吞噬，他们的军队被相互摧毁而消灭。|近义词：mutually destroyed, consumed each other | 反义词： mutually supported, cooperated | 动词
