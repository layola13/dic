heartwarming: /ˈhɑːrtˌwɔːrmɪŋ/ | adj. |令人暖心的，感人的|  Evoking a feeling of warmth and happiness; deeply moving and affecting the emotions in a positive way. | 例句：The children's heartwarming performance touched everyone's hearts. 孩子们令人暖心的表演打动了每个人的心。

The story had a heartwarming ending, leaving the audience with a sense of hope. 故事有一个令人暖心的结局，给观众留下了希望的感觉。


近义词：moving, touching, comforting, endearing, uplifting

反义词：heartbreaking, depressing, chilling, dispiriting
