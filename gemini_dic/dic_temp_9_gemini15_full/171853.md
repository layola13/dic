punishes: /ˈpʌnɪʃɪz/ | vt. |惩罚，处罚 | To inflict a penalty on (someone) for a wrongdoing.  |例句：The teacher punishes students who misbehave. 老师惩罚那些行为不端的学生。|例句：The court will punish the criminal according to the law. 法院将依法惩罚罪犯。|近义词：penalize, chastise, discipline |反义词：reward, pardon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school punishes students who cheat. = 学校惩罚作弊的学生。
He punishes his dog for biting the furniture. = 他惩罚他的狗咬家具。

一般过去时:
The judge punished the thief severely. = 法官严厉地惩罚了小偷。
My parents punished me for lying. = 父母因为我撒谎而惩罚了我。

现在完成时:
The government has punished several corrupt officials. = 政府已经惩罚了几名腐败官员。
She has never been punished for anything before. = 她以前从未因为任何事受到惩罚。

过去完成时:
He had already been punished for the same offense. = 他因同样的罪行已被惩罚。
They had punished him before he confessed. = 在他坦白之前，他们已经惩罚了他。

将来时:
The court will punish the defendant if he is found guilty. = 如果被告被判有罪，法院将对其进行惩罚。
They will punish him for his actions. = 他们将因他的行为而惩罚他。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't do that, lest you be punished. = 如果我是你，我不会那样做，以免受到惩罚。
I wish I hadn't lied; I wouldn't have been punished. = 我真希望我没有撒谎；我就不会被惩罚了。

被动语态:
The criminal was punished for his crimes. = 罪犯因其罪行而受到惩罚。
The students were punished for their disruptive behavior. = 学生们因其扰乱秩序的行为而受到惩罚。

疑问句:
Will he be punished for his actions? = 他会因为他的行为而受到惩罚吗？
How will the company punish the employee? = 公司将如何惩罚这位员工？

否定句:
The judge didn't punish the defendant. = 法官没有惩罚被告。
They won't punish him lightly. = 他们不会轻饶他。


PHRASE->
punish severely = 严厉惩罚
punish somebody for something = 因某事惩罚某人
punish by = 通过...来惩罚
punish oneself = 自我惩罚
capital punishment = 死刑
