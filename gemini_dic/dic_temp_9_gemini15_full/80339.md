goldfish:/ˈɡoʊldfɪʃ/| n. |金鱼|A small, orange or gold-colored fish, commonly kept as a pet.  Goldfish are known for their relatively low maintenance needs and their ability to tolerate a wide range of water conditions. They are popular among both children and adults.|例句：I have two goldfish in a small tank in my bedroom. 我卧室里一个小鱼缸里养了两条金鱼。

例句：My daughter loves feeding her goldfish every morning. 我女儿每天早上都喜欢喂她的金鱼。


近义词：carp (鲤鱼，金鱼的祖先),  ornamental fish (观赏鱼)
反义词：None (没有合适的反义词)
词性:noun
