regreening:/ˌriːˈɡriːnɪŋ/ | n. | 再绿化；重新绿化 | The process of restoring vegetation to an area that has been deforested or degraded.  This involves planting trees, shrubs, and other plants to increase green cover.  It's often undertaken to combat desertification, improve soil health, and enhance biodiversity. | 例句：The regreening project aims to restore the degraded landscape.  再绿化项目旨在恢复退化的景观。

The government's ambitious regreening initiative has already shown some positive results. 政府雄心勃勃的再绿化计划已经显示出一些积极成果。| 近义词：reforestation, afforestation, revegetation | 反义词：deforestation, desertification | 名词
