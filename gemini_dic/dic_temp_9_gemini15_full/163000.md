precis:/ˈpresiː/| n. |摘要；概要；梗概 |A concise summary of a larger work.  A precis is a shortened version of a text that retains the original's main ideas and arguments. It typically omits details and examples. |例句：The professor asked us to write a precis of the assigned reading. 教授让我们写一篇阅读作业的摘要。

例句：She gave a precis of the meeting to her boss. 她向老板做了会议的概要。


近义词：summary, abstract, synopsis, epitome

反义词：expansion, elaboration, detail


词源:  From the French word "précis," meaning "precise" or "exact."  It entered English in the 18th century.
