obtainable: /əbˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可获得的，可得到的 |  Something that is obtainable can be acquired or achieved.  It emphasizes the possibility of getting something, often after effort or search. |


例句：

The information is readily obtainable online. = 这些信息很容易在网上获得。

Fresh produce is obtainable at the local farmers market. = 当地的农贸市场可以买到新鲜农产品。



近义词：available, accessible, achievable, procurable

反义词：unobtainable, inaccessible, unattainable


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That information is obtainable through various channels. = 通过各种渠道可以获得该信息。
The tickets are obtainable online or at the box office. = 票可以在网上或售票处购买。


一般过去时:
The documents were obtainable only through official channels. = 这些文件只能通过官方渠道获得。
The rare stamp was obtainable at a high price. = 这枚稀有的邮票价格很高。


现在完成时:
The book has been obtainable since last year. = 这本书从去年开始就可以买到了。
The data has become obtainable through improved technology. = 由于技术的改进，数据变得更容易获取。


过去完成时:
The report had been obtainable only to a select few before the release. = 在发布之前，只有少数人才能获得这份报告。
The recipe had been obtainable from her grandmother. = 这个食谱是她从祖母那里得到的。


将来时:
The results will be obtainable after the experiment is finished. = 实验结束后将可以获得结果。
The new software will be obtainable for download next week. = 新软件将于下周可以下载。


虚拟语气:
If the resources were obtainable, the project would have succeeded. = 如果资源可以获得，项目就会成功。
I wish that rare book were obtainable at a reasonable price. = 我希望那本珍稀的书能以合理的价格买到。


被动语态:
The information is obtainable by contacting customer service. = 可以通过联系客户服务获得信息。
The tickets are obtainable only through pre-registration. = 票只能通过预先注册获得。


疑问句:
Is the medication obtainable without a prescription? = 这药不用处方就能买到吗？
Are the tickets still obtainable? = 票还能买到吗？


否定句:
The antique is not obtainable at present. = 目前该古董无法获得。
The limited-edition shoes are not readily obtainable. = 限量版鞋子不容易买到。


PHRASE->

easily obtainable = 易于获得的

词源:

Obtainable derives from the verb "obtain," which comes from the Old French word "obtenir," ultimately from the Latin "obtinēre," meaning "to hold, possess, obtain."  The suffix "-able" is added to create the adjective form, signifying "capable of being obtained."
