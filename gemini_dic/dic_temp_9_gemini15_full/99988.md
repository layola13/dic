interparochial:/ˌɪntərpəˈroʊkiəl/| adj. |教区间的，跨教区的|Relating to or involving more than one parish or church district.  The term often refers to cooperation or collaboration between different parishes.|The interparochial committee organized a joint fundraising event for the local schools.  跨教区委员会为当地学校组织了一场联合筹款活动。|近义词：inter-parish, ecumenical (in a broader sense)|反义词：intraparochial (within a single parish)
