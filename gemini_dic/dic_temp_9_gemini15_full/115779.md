marinus:/məˈraɪnəs/| adj.| 海的；海生的；海产的 | Relating to the sea; of or relating to the sea or marine life.  |例句：Marine biology is the study of ocean life. 海生物学是对海洋生物的研究。  |例句：Many marine species are threatened by pollution. 许多海洋物种受到污染的威胁。|近义词：nautical, oceanic, sea |反义词：terrestrial
