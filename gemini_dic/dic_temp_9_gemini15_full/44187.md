croaker:/ˈkroʊkər/| n. v.| 1. 乌鸦；（尤指）美洲黑鱼；爱抱怨的人； 2. 呱呱叫；发出嘎嘎声 |1. A croaker is a type of fish, specifically a type of drumfish known for its croaking sound.  It can also refer to a person who complains a lot. 2. To croak means to make a low, harsh sound like a frog or a raven. |例句：The croaker fish made a loud croaking sound.  =  美洲黑鱼发出响亮的呱呱声。

The old man was a croaker, always complaining about the weather. =  这位老人是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨天气。

近义词：grumbler, complainer (for the person);  grunt, rasp (for the sound)  |反义词：optimist (for the person);  chirp, sing (for the sound)|名词，动词
