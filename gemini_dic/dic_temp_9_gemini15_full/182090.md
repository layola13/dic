reminder:/rɪˈmaɪndər/ | n. |提醒物；提醒；暗示 | A thing that reminds one of something.  A reminder can be a note, an event, or anything that brings something back to memory. |例句：She left a reminder on my desk. 她在我桌上留了个便条提醒我。

例句：The nagging pain served as a constant reminder of her injury.  这阵挥之不去的疼痛时刻提醒着她自己的伤势。


近义词：memorandum, prompt, alert, notification | 反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion


词源:  The word "reminder" comes from the verb "remind," which is formed from the Old English prefix "re-" (again) and "mind" (to remember). Thus, "remind" means "to bring back to mind" or "to cause to remember."  The suffix "-er" turns the verb into a noun, indicating a person or thing that performs the action of reminding.
