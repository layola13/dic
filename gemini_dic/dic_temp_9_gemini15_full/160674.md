porticoed:/pɔːrˈtɪkoʊd/| adj.|有门廊的；有柱廊的|Having a portico or porticoes.  A porticoed building is one that has a portico, which is a covered structure supported by columns or pillars, typically found at the entrance of a building.|例句：The porticoed entrance to the museum was impressive.  博物馆有柱廊的入口令人印象深刻。

The grand hotel boasted a magnificent porticoed terrace overlooking the ocean.  这家豪华酒店拥有一个壮丽的带柱廊的露台，俯瞰着大海。|近义词：columned, pillared|反义词：portico-less
