proterandry:/ˌproʊtərˈændri/| n. |雄性先熟|The phenomenon in which an organism is male first and then female later in its life.  This is a type of sequential hermaphroditism.  Examples are common in some fish and invertebrates. |例句：Many species of fish exhibit proterandry, changing from male to female as they age.  许多鱼类都表现出雄性先熟现象，随着年龄的增长，它们会从雄性变成雌性。|近义词：protandry|反义词：protogyny
