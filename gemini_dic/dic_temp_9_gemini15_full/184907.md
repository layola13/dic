retakes:/ˈriːteɪks/| n.| 重拍；重考；补拍镜头 |  A retake is a repetition of a test, exam, scene, or photograph. It implies an attempt to improve upon a previous, unsatisfactory result. |例句：She failed the exam and had to take a retake. 她考试不及格，不得不重考。

例句：The director decided they needed a retake of the final scene. 导演决定他们需要重拍最后一幕。


近义词：redo, repeat, re-examination

反义词：original take, first attempt


词性: 名词
