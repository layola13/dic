self-expressive:/ˌsɛlfɪkˈsprɛsɪv/| adj.|自我表达的，表现自我的|Able to express one's own feelings, thoughts, and personality freely and openly.  It emphasizes the act of expressing oneself in a distinct and individualistic manner.|例句：Her paintings are incredibly self-expressive, reflecting her unique perspective on life.她的画作极富自我表达性，反映了她对生活的独特视角。

例句：His music is a self-expressive outlet for his emotions.他的音乐是他宣泄情绪的自我表达途径。|近义词：expressive, individualistic, unconventional|反义词：reserved, inhibited, repressed|词性:形容词
