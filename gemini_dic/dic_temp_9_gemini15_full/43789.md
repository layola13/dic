crematoriums:/krɛməˈtɔːriəm/| n.|火葬场；焚尸场|A crematorium is a building equipped for cremating bodies.  It typically includes facilities for storing bodies before cremation, conducting services, and scattering ashes.|例句：The family held a private service at the crematorium before scattering the ashes.  家人在火葬场举行了一场私人仪式，之后撒了骨灰。|近义词：crematory, funeral home (if cremation services are offered)|反义词：burial ground, cemetery
