refuser:/rɪˈfjuːzər/ | n. |拒绝者，拒收者 |A refuser is a person who refuses something.  This could be a request, an offer, or something else.  The context will determine the specifics. |例句：He was a known refuser of charity, preferring to work for his own living. 他是出了名的拒绝施舍的人，宁愿靠自己的劳动生活。|例句：The factory became a refuser of substandard materials. 工厂开始拒收不合格的材料。|近义词：objector, denier, rejector |反义词：accepter, approver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refuses to help. = 他拒绝帮忙。
She refuses all offers of assistance. = 她拒绝所有帮助。


一般过去时:
They refused my request. = 他们拒绝了我的请求。
He refused to answer the question. = 他拒绝回答这个问题。


现在完成时:
She has refused to cooperate with the investigation. = 她一直拒绝配合调查。
He has refused every job offer he's received. = 他拒绝了他收到的每一个工作机会。


过去完成时:
They had refused to believe him until they saw the evidence. = 在他们看到证据之前，他们一直拒绝相信他。
She had refused to go to the party, despite her friends' pleas. = 尽管朋友们恳求，她还是拒绝去参加聚会。


将来时:
He will refuse to sign the contract. = 他将拒绝签署合同。
They will refuse entry to anyone without a ticket. = 他们将拒绝任何没有票的人入场。


虚拟语气:
If he refused to comply, he would face consequences. = 如果他不遵守，他将面临后果。
I wish he had not refused my offer. = 我真希望他没有拒绝我的提议。


被动语态:
His request was refused. = 他的请求被拒绝了。
Entry was refused to those without proper identification. = 没有适当证件的人被拒之门外。


疑问句:
Will he refuse the offer? = 他会拒绝这个提议吗？
Why did she refuse to help? = 她为什么拒绝帮忙？


否定句:
He didn't refuse my help. = 他没有拒绝我的帮助。
She wouldn't refuse a cup of tea. = 她不会拒绝一杯茶。


PHRASE->
refuser of responsibility 责任推卸者
refuser of gifts 拒收礼品者
refuser of treatment 拒绝治疗者
habitual refuser 惯于拒绝者
a strong refuser 坚决的拒绝者
