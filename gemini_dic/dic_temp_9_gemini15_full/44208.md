crochet:/krəˈʃeɪ/|/ˈkroʊʃeɪ/| n. v.|钩针编织；钩针编织物|The art or process of creating fabric from yarn using a hooked needle.  It involves looping yarn through stitches to create various patterns and textures.|例句：She enjoys crochet and has made many beautiful blankets. 她喜欢钩针编织，已经钩了很多漂亮的毯子。
例句：I learned to crochet from my grandmother. 我从祖母那里学会了钩针编织。|knitting, knitting, needlework| weaving (反义词,  但不是完全的反义词，因为两者都是编织技术)|名词，动词

USAGE->

一般现在时:
She crochets every evening. = 她每天晚上都钩针编织。
They crochet beautiful scarves. = 他们钩织漂亮的围巾。

一般过去时:
I crocheted a hat last week. = 我上周钩了一顶帽子。
She crocheted a blanket for her baby. = 她为她的宝宝钩了一条毯子。

现在完成时:
I have crocheted several blankets this year. = 今年我已经钩了几条毯子。
She has crocheted since she was a child. = 她从孩提时代就开始钩针编织了。

过去完成时:
I had crocheted the entire scarf before the movie started. = 电影开始前我已经钩完了整条围巾。
She had crocheted many items for the charity bazaar. = 她已经为慈善义卖钩织了很多东西。

将来时:
I will crochet a sweater for winter. = 我将为冬天钩一件毛衣。
She will crochet a dress for her daughter's wedding. = 她将为女儿的婚礼钩一件礼服。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would crochet more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地钩针编织。
I wish I could crochet as well as my aunt. = 我希望我能像我阿姨那样擅长钩针编织。

被动语态:
The blanket was crocheted with great care. = 这条毯子是用极大的细心钩织的。
The scarf is being crocheted using a delicate yarn. = 这条围巾正在用精细的纱线钩织。

疑问句:
Do you crochet? = 你会钩针编织吗？
Have you ever crocheted before? = 你以前钩过针吗？

否定句:
I don't crochet very often. = 我不经常钩针编织。
He didn't crochet the entire project. = 他没有完成整个钩织项目。
