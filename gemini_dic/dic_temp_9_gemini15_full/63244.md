enterprise:/ˈɛntəpraɪz/| n. |事业；企业；公司；进取心|A project or undertaking, typically one that is difficult or requires effort.  It can also refer to a business organization. |例句：She showed great enterprise in starting her own business. 她创业展现出极大的进取心。|例句：He works for a large multinational enterprise. 他在一个大型跨国企业工作。|近义词： undertaking, venture, firm, company |反义词： inactivity, lethargy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This enterprise manufactures cars. = 这家企业生产汽车。
The enterprise is expanding its operations. = 这家企业正在扩大业务。

一般过去时:
The enterprise launched a new product last year. = 这家企业去年推出了一款新产品。
The enterprise faced many challenges in its early years. = 这家企业在早期面临许多挑战。

现在完成时:
The enterprise has achieved great success. = 这家企业已经取得了巨大的成功。
The enterprise has expanded into new markets. = 这家企业已经拓展到新的市场。

过去完成时:
By 2010, the enterprise had already established itself as a leader in the industry. = 到2010年，这家企业已经确立了自己在行业中的领导地位。
Before the merger, the enterprise had been struggling financially. = 在合并之前，这家企业一直在财务上挣扎。

将来时:
The enterprise will open a new branch next month. = 这家企业下个月将开设一个新的分公司。
The enterprise will invest heavily in research and development. = 这家企业将大力投资研发。

虚拟语气:
If the enterprise had received more funding, it could have grown faster. = 如果这家企业获得更多资金，它本可以发展得更快。
I wish the enterprise had been more successful. = 我希望这家企业更成功一些。

被动语态:
The enterprise was founded in 1980. = 这家企业成立于1980年。
New technologies are being implemented by the enterprise. = 这家企业正在实施新技术。

疑问句:
What kind of enterprise is this? = 这是一家什么样的企业？
What are the goals of this enterprise? = 这家企业的目标是什么？

否定句:
