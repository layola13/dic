/ˈlɪtəst/| adj. |最小的，最少的 |The littlest one is the cutest. 最小的一个最可爱。|近义词：smallest, least, tiniest |反义词：biggest, largest, greatest|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The littlest puppy is sleeping.  = 最小的幼犬正在睡觉。
He is the littlest boy in the class. = 他是班上最小的男孩。


一般过去时:
The littlest bird flew away. = 最小的鸟飞走了。
She was the littlest girl at the party. = 她在派对上是最小的女孩。


现在完成时:
The littlest kitten has found its mother. = 最小的猫崽找到了它的妈妈。
He has always been the littlest in his family. = 他一直是他家最小的。


过去完成时:
The littlest child had already fallen asleep. = 最小的孩子已经睡着了。
They had never seen such a littlest creature before. = 他们以前从未见过这么小的生物。


将来时:
The littlest one will be the first to finish. = 最小的一个将是第一个完成的。
She will be the littlest girl in the competition. = 她将是比赛中最小的女孩。


虚拟语气:
If I were the littlest, I would have more attention. = 如果我是最小的，我会得到更多关注。
I wish I were the littlest; things would be easier. = 我希望我是最小的；事情会更容易。


被动语态:
The littlest problem was overlooked. = 最小的问题被忽视了。
The littlest detail was carefully considered. = 最小的细节也被仔细考虑了。


疑问句:
Which is the littlest? = 哪一个是(些)最小的？
Is he the littlest of the three brothers? = 他是三个兄弟中最小的吗？


否定句:
That's not the littlest one. = 那不是最小的一个。
She is not the littlest girl in her class. = 她不是她班上最小的女孩。


PHRASE->
the littlest bit = 一点点
the littlest detail = 最小的细节
the littlest thing = 最小的事情
the littlest possible = 最小可能的
the littlest amount = 最小的数量

词源:
little (adj.) + -est (suffix)  "-est" 是英语中用来构成最高级形容词的后缀。  "little"本身源自古英语 *lytel,  与古高地德语 *liutil, 古诺尔斯语 *litr 相同，其词源可能来自原始日耳曼语 *luttila，最终可能源于原始印欧语根 *leudh-，意为“小”。
