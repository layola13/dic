illegalness:/ɪˈliːɡælənəs/| n. |非法性；违法性 |The illegalness of the transaction was immediately apparent.  交易的非法性是显而易见的。|近义词：illegality, lawlessness |反义词：legality, lawfulness


The state of being illegal; the quality or fact of being against the law.  It refers to the inherent unlawfulness of an action or condition.  The word is primarily used in formal contexts.

例句：

The illegalness of downloading copyrighted material is widely understood. 下载受版权保护材料的非法性是广为人知的。

The court ruled that the company's actions demonstrated a blatant disregard for the illegalness of their practices. 法院裁定该公司行为公然无视其行为的非法性。
