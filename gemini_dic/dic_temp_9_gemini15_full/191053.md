scandalling:/ˈskændəlɪŋ/| v.| 丑闻化；诽谤；中伤 | To cause a scandal; to defame or slander someone or something.  |The journalist was accused of scandalling the politician's reputation.  记者被指控诽谤政治家的名誉。|近义词：slandering, defaming, vilifying |反义词：praising, commending, extolling|动词


USAGE->

He is constantly scandalling his neighbours. = 他不断地诽谤他的邻居们。
She scandals her rivals with false rumours. = 她用谣言中伤她的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
They scandalled the company's image with their reckless actions. = 他们鲁莽的行动败坏了公司的形象。
