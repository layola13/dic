cooperator:/koʊˈɒpəreɪtər/| n. |合作者，协作者 | A person who cooperates or works together with others.  |The project was a success thanks to the hard work of all the cooperators.  这个项目的成功要感谢所有合作者的辛勤工作。|collaborator, teammate, partner |competitor, opponent, rival|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cooperates with his colleagues on the project. 他与同事一起合作完成项目。
She cooperates well with others in the team.  她在团队中与他人合作得很好。


一般过去时:
They cooperated on the research paper last semester. 上个学期他们合作撰写了研究论文。
He cooperated fully with the police investigation. 他全力配合警方调查。


现在完成时:
We have cooperated with that company for many years. 我们与那家公司合作多年了。
She has cooperated on several successful projects. 她已经参与合作完成了一些成功的项目。


过去完成时:
They had cooperated on several projects before this one. 在这个项目之前，他们已经合作过好几个项目了。
Before the merger, the two companies had cooperated on a few joint ventures. 在合并之前，这两家公司已经合作进行过一些合资企业。


将来时:
We will cooperate with them on the new marketing campaign. 我们将与他们合作开展新的营销活动。
They will cooperate to achieve their common goal. 他们将合作以实现他们的共同目标。


虚拟语气:
If he cooperated, the project would be finished on time. 如果他合作，项目就能按时完成。
I wish they had cooperated more effectively. 我希望他们合作更有效率一些。


被动语态:
The plan was cooperated on by many people. 这个计划得到了许多人的合作。
The information was cooperated on by multiple sources.  这些信息得到了多个来源的合作。


疑问句:
Will they cooperate on the upcoming project? 他们会在接下来的项目中合作吗？
Did they cooperate effectively during the negotiations? 他们在谈判期间是否有效地合作了？


否定句:
He did not cooperate with the investigators. 他没有配合调查人员。
They refused to cooperate with the authorities. 他们拒绝与当局合作。
