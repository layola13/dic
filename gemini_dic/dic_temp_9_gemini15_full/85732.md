hawkbill:/ˈhɔːkˌbɪl/| n. |鹰嘴龟；玳瑁；玳瑁壳|A hawkbill is a type of sea turtle known for its distinctive hooked beak-like mouth.  The term can also refer to the shell of this turtle, which is highly valued for its beautiful markings and used in various crafts. |例句：The hawkbill turtle is critically endangered due to habitat loss and poaching. 鹰嘴龟由于栖息地丧失和偷猎而濒临灭绝。

The beautiful patterns on the hawkbill's shell make it highly sought after. 玳瑁壳美丽的图案使其非常抢手。|近义词：玳瑁(Chinese translation)|反义词：无|
