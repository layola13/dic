greeter:/ˈɡriːtər/| n. |迎宾员，问候者 |A greeter is a person whose job is to welcome guests or visitors.  Greeters are often found in hotels, businesses, or events. |Example: The hotel greeter welcomed us with a warm smile.  酒店的迎宾员用温暖的笑容欢迎我们。 The conference greeter directed attendees to the registration desk. 会议迎宾员引导与会者前往登记处。|近义词：host, receptionist, usher |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The greeter greets every guest warmly. = 迎宾员热情地迎接每一位客人。
He greets his friends with a handshake. = 他以握手的方式向他的朋友们打招呼。


一般过去时:
The greeter greeted all the visitors politely. = 迎宾员礼貌地接待了所有来访者。
She greeted her colleagues cheerfully this morning. = 她今天早上兴高采烈地向同事们打招呼。


现在完成时:
The hotel has hired a new greeter. = 酒店已经聘请了一位新的迎宾员。
She has greeted hundreds of people today. = 她今天已经接待了数百人。


过去完成时:
The manager had already greeted the VIP guest before the others arrived. = 在其他人到达之前，经理已经接待了贵宾。
They had greeted each other before the meeting started.  他们在会议开始之前就已经互相打过招呼了。


将来时:
The company will hire more greeters for the upcoming event. = 公司将为即将到来的活动聘请更多迎宾员。
He will greet his family at the airport. = 他将在机场迎接他的家人。


虚拟语气:
If I were a greeter, I would always smile. = 如果我是个迎宾员，我会一直微笑。
I wish I had greeted her earlier. = 我真希望我早点跟她打招呼。


被动语态:
Every guest was greeted warmly by the staff. = 每位客人都受到了员工的热情接待。
The visitors were greeted at the entrance. = 来宾们在大门口受到了迎接。


疑问句:

否定句:

He didn't greet anyone at the party. = 他在派对上没有跟任何人打招呼。
