disadventure:/ˌdɪsædˈvɛntʃər/| n. |不幸的遭遇，灾难，不幸|An unpleasant or unlucky experience; a mishap.  It often implies a sense of unexpected misfortune or mischance, rather than a deliberate act of ill fortune.|例句：The expedition ended in disadventure when their boat capsized.  探险以船只倾覆的不幸遭遇告终。

例句：His disadventure in the mountains left him with a broken leg. 他在山区的不幸遭遇导致他腿骨折。


近义词：mishap, misfortune, mischance, calamity, setback

反义词：good fortune, success, triumph, adventure (in a positive sense)

词性:名词
