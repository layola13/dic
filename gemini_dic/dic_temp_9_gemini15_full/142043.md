organometallic: /ˌɔːrɡænəʊˈmɛtəlɪk/ | adj. |有机金属的|  Relating to or denoting compounds containing organic groups linked to metal atoms.  These compounds often exhibit unique properties and reactivities due to the interaction between the organic and metallic components. They are widely used in various fields, including catalysis, materials science, and medicine.


例句：
Organometallic chemistry is a rapidly developing field. = 有机金属化学是一个快速发展的领域。

The synthesis of this new organometallic compound is a significant achievement. = 这种新型有机金属化合物的合成是一项重大成就。


近义词：metalorganic (less common)


反义词：inorganic


词性: adjective


词源:  The word is a combination of "organo-" (from "organic," referring to carbon-containing compounds) and "metallic" (referring to metals).  The term reflects the nature of these compounds, which bridge the gap between organic and inorganic chemistry.
