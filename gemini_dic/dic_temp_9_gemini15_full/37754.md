compassionable: /kəmˈpæʃənəbl/ | adj. |富有同情心的；慈悲的 | Able to feel or show sympathy and concern for others; merciful. |例句：He was a compassionate and understanding man. 他是一个富有同情心和理解力的人。
例句：Her compassionate nature led her to volunteer at the homeless shelter.  她富有同情心的天性使她去无家可归者收容所做志愿者。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, merciful, kind, humane |反义词：cruel, unfeeling, heartless, callous, indifferent
