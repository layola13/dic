hilum:/ˈhaɪləm/| n. |种脐；(器官的)门；(植物的)种阜|The hilum is a scar on a seed where it was attached to the ovary wall.  It's often a point of entry for water and nutrients.|The hilum of the kidney is where the renal artery and vein enter and the ureter exits.|近义词：scar, mark|反义词：apex
