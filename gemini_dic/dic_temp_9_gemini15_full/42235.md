count-out:/kaʊnt aʊt/| vt.|数数排除；逐个点名；点名淘汰|To exclude someone or something by counting; to eliminate someone from a competition by counting them out.|The teacher counted out the students who were absent.老师数出了缺席的学生。|The boxer was counted out after the tenth round.拳击手在第十回合后被数出局。|eliminate, exclude, remove|include, keep in|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They count out the votes every election. = 每次选举他们都会清点选票。
We count out the apples one by one. = 我们一个一个地数苹果。


一般过去时:
The referee counted out the boxer. = 裁判数着拳击手出局。
She counted out the money carefully. = 她仔细地数着钱。


现在完成时:
They have counted out all the ballots. = 他们已经清点完所有选票了。
We have counted out the correct number of participants. = 我们已经数出了正确的参与人数。


过去完成时:
The judge had counted out the final contestant before the announcement. = 裁判在宣布结果前已经数出了最后一位参赛者。
They had counted out the ingredients before they started baking. = 他们在开始烘焙之前已经数好了配料。


将来时:
The judges will count out the scores after the performance. = 评委将在表演结束后计算分数。
They will count out the items for the shipment tomorrow. = 他们明天将清点装运物品。


虚拟语气:
If they counted out the votes fairly, the results would be different. = 如果他们公平地清点选票，结果就会不同。
I wish they had counted out the points more accurately. = 我希望他们能更准确地计算分数。


被动语态:
The votes were counted out by the election officials. = 选票由选举官员清点。
The participants were counted out one by one. = 参赛者被逐个点名淘汰。


疑问句:
Have they counted out all the supplies? = 他们已经清点完所有物资了吗？
Will the judge count out the contestants fairly? = 裁判会公平地数出参赛者吗？


否定句:
They didn't count out the damaged goods. = 他们没有清点损坏的货物。
The teacher didn't count out the students who were late. = 老师没有数出迟到的学生。


PHRASE->
count out of (排除在外):  They counted me out of the decision-making process. = 他们把我排除在决策过程之外。
count somebody out (别指望): You can count me out; I'm not participating. = 别指望我，我不参加。
count someone in (算上某人): Count me in! I want to join the game. = 算上我！我想参加游戏。
count on (依靠): I'm counting on you to help me. = 我指望你帮助我。
count down (倒计时): We're counting down the days until the vacation. = 我们正在倒计时等待假期。
