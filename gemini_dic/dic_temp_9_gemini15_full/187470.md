roll-ons:/ˈroʊlˌɒnz/| n. |滚珠式(化妆品)|A roll-on is a type of cosmetic or toiletry product, such as deodorant or perfume, that is applied using a rollerball applicator.  The product is contained in a bottle or tube with a small ball at the end that rotates, dispensing the product smoothly onto the skin. | Example: I prefer roll-on deodorant because it's easy to apply. 我更喜欢滚珠式除臭剂，因为它容易涂抹。  She uses a roll-on perfume for a subtle scent. 她使用滚珠式香水以获得淡淡的香味。|近义词：rollerball applicator, stick (for some products)|反义词：spray, pump|名词
