resonation:/ˌrɛzəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 共鸣；回响；共振 | The phenomenon where a vibrating object causes another object to vibrate at the same frequency.  It's the sustained sound produced by the vibration of an object.  This can be acoustic, as in the resonance of a musical instrument, or it can be in other systems, such as the resonance of a bridge under stress.  | 例句：The singer's voice resonated throughout the hall. 歌手的歌声在整个大厅回荡。

The deep resonance of the bass drum filled the room. 低音鼓的低沉共鸣充满了整个房间。


近义词：vibration, reverberation, echo, resonance | 反义词：silence, stillness


词性：名词
