reaffronts:/ˌriːəˈfrɒnts/| v.|再次冒犯；再次使生气|To offend or insult again; to cause offense or displeasure anew.|例句：His constant interruptions re-affronted the speaker. 他不断的插话再次冒犯了演讲者。

例句：The politician's latest statement re-affronted many voters. 这位政治家的最新声明再次激怒了许多选民。


近义词：insult again, offend anew, provoke further, irritate again|反义词：appease, placate, compliment


词性:动词
