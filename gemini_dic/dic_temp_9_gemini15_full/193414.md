seismometer:/ˈsaɪzmɒmɪtər/ | n. |地震仪|An instrument that measures and records ground motion, especially that caused by earthquakes.  Seismometers are crucial for detecting and analyzing seismic waves, providing valuable data for understanding earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, and other geological phenomena. |The seismometer recorded a magnitude 7.0 earthquake.  地震仪记录到7.0级地震。|  seismograph,  seismoscope |  (无明确反义词，可考虑使用"non-measuring device"之类的表达) | 名词
