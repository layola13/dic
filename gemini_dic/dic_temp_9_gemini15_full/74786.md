freighter:/ˈfreɪtər/| n.|货船|A large ship that carries goods.|例句：The freighter arrived in port carrying containers of manufactured goods. 这艘货船抵达港口，装载着制造商品的集装箱。|例句：The damaged freighter needed extensive repairs before it could sail again. 受损的货船需要进行大规模维修才能再次起航。|近义词：cargo ship, merchant ship|反义词：passenger ship


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The freighter transports goods across the ocean. = 这艘货船在海上运输货物。
That freighter carries a lot of cargo. = 那艘货船运载大量的货物。


一般过去时:
The freighter arrived late last night. = 货船昨晚到达很晚。

The freighter has already departed. = 货船已经启航了。
