jointedness:/ˈdʒɔɪntɪdnɪs/| n. |关节性；连接性；节状；分节；|The quality or state of being jointed;  the condition of having joints or being composed of parts joined together. It can refer to the physical structure of something with joints, or to a metaphorical sense of connection or articulation.|例句：The jointedness of the insect's legs allowed it to move with surprising agility.  昆虫腿部的关节性使其能够以惊人的敏捷性移动。 The jointedness of the argument was lacking, making it difficult to follow.  论证缺乏连贯性，难以理解。|近义词：articulation, connection, segmentation|反义词：solidity, seamlessness
