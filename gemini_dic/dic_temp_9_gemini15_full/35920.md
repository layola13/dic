coction:/ˈkɒkʃən/| n. |混合；调制；烹调；酿造|The process of cooking or preparing something, especially by mixing different ingredients.  It can also refer to a concoction or mixture. |例句：The coction of the herbal remedy involved a complex process of simmering and straining.  草药疗法的调制涉及一个复杂的煨煮和过滤过程。

例句：The chef's special coction was a delicious blend of spices and herbs.  厨师的特色菜是一种美味的香料和草药混合物。


近义词：preparation, concoction, mixture, brew

反义词：decomposition, destruction


词源:源自古法语 *coccion，最终源自拉丁语 coctio，意思是“烹调”。
