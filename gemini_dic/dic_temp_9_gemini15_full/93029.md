idle:/ˈaɪdl/ | adj., v., n. | 闲置的；懒惰的；空闲的；使…空闲；无所事事 | inactive; unemployed; lazy; to spend time doing nothing | The machine has been idle for weeks. 这台机器已经闲置了几周了。He idled away the afternoon. 他整个下午无所事事。  | busy, active, occupied | productive, working | adjective, verb, noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The engine idles smoothly. = 引擎运转平稳。
He idles away his time playing video games. = 他玩电子游戏消磨时间。


一般过去时:
The car idled at the traffic light. = 汽车在红绿灯处空转。
She idled away the afternoon reading a book. = 她下午读了一本书来消磨时间。


现在完成时:
The factory has idled for months due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，工厂已经停产数月了。
He has idled away most of his youth. = 他大部分青春都虚度了。


过去完成时:
