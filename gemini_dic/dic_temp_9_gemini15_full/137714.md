nonvacancies:/ˌnɒnˈveɪkənsiz/| n. |空缺职位,职位空缺|The term "nonvacancies" refers to positions within an organization that are not currently open for applications.  It denotes the opposite of job vacancies or openings.  The term is less common than "filled positions" or simply "positions".|例句：The company's internal announcement focused on current nonvacancies and employee development opportunities.|近义词：filled positions, occupied positions, existing roles|反义词：vacancies, job openings
