left-handers:/ˈlɛfthændərz/| n. |左撇子|People who use their left hand more skillfully than their right hand. They are also called southpaws.|例句：Left-handers often face challenges using tools and equipment designed for right-handed people. 左撇子在使用为右撇子设计的工具和设备时常常面临挑战。
例句：The percentage of left-handers in the population is relatively small. 人口中左撇子的比例相对较小。|近义词：southpaws|反义词：right-handers
