coffees:/ˈkɒfiz/ /ˈkɔːfiz/ | n. |咖啡(复数) |Coffees refers to multiple servings or types of coffee.  It is the plural form of "coffee".|I ordered three coffees for the meeting. 我为会议点了三杯咖啡。


He tried different coffees from around the world. 他尝试了来自世界各地的不同咖啡。|近义词：cups of coffee, brews |反义词：无
