词源:
photo- (from Greek phōs, phot-), meaning "light," + -phobia (from Greek -phobos), meaning "fear" or "aversion."  Therefore, photophobia literally means "fear of light" or "aversion to light."  Note that it's not necessarily a fear, but rather an intolerance or sensitivity.
