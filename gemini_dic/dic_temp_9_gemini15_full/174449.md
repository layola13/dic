quinces:/ˈkwɪnsɪz/| n.|木瓜； quince (单数)|A quince is a fragrant, pear-shaped fruit with a tart, slightly acidic flavor.  It is often used in jams, jellies, and preserves, and in some cultures, it holds significant symbolic meaning, particularly in celebrations of a girl's fifteenth birthday.|例句：My grandmother used to make delicious quince paste.  我的祖母过去常常做美味的木瓜酱。

例句：We celebrated my cousin's quinceañera with a big party. 我们举办了一个大型派对来庆祝我表妹的十五岁生日。|近义词：pear, apple (in terms of appearance)|反义词：None specifically, but fruits with a sweet flavor could be considered opposites in terms of taste. |词性 n.
