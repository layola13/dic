nonengagement: /ˌnɒnɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 不参与；不介入；未婚；未订婚 | The state or fact of not being engaged; lack of involvement or participation.  It can refer to a lack of commitment in a relationship or a refusal to participate in an activity or conflict. | 例句：The government's policy of nonengagement in the conflict drew criticism. 政府在冲突中不干涉的政策招致批评。

The company's nonengagement with its customers led to declining sales. 公司不与客户互动导致销售额下降。


近义词：disengagement, noninvolvement, abstention, neutrality | 反义词：engagement, involvement, participation, commitment
