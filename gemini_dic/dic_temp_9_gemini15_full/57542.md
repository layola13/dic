drainpipe:/ˈdreɪnpaɪp/| n. |排水管|A pipe that carries away waste water or other liquids.|The drainpipe was clogged with leaves.|近义词：waste pipe, sewer pipe|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drainpipe carries wastewater away from the house. = 排水管将污水从房子里排走。
Water flows through the drainpipe constantly. = 水不断地流过排水管。


一般过去时:
The drainpipe leaked last night. = 排水管昨晚漏水了。
We repaired the broken drainpipe yesterday. = 我们昨天修理了坏掉的排水管。


现在完成时:
The drainpipe has been clogged for days. = 排水管堵塞了好几天了。
We have finally fixed the leaking drainpipe. = 我们终于修好了漏水的排水管。


过去完成时:
The drainpipe had already burst before we called the plumber. =在我们叫来水管工之前，排水管已经爆裂了。
The house had suffered water damage because the drainpipe had been blocked. =由于排水管堵塞，房子已经遭受了水灾。


将来时:
We will replace the drainpipe next week. = 我们下周将更换排水管。
The plumber will inspect the drainpipe tomorrow. = 水管工明天将检查排水管。


虚拟语气:
If the drainpipe were blocked, the basement would flood. = 如果排水管堵塞，地下室就会被淹。
I wish the drainpipe hadn't burst during the party. = 我真希望排水管在聚会期间没有爆裂。


被动语态:
The drainpipe is regularly cleaned. = 排水管定期清洗。
The drainpipe was damaged during the storm. = 排水管在暴风雨中受损。


疑问句:
Is the drainpipe leaking? = 排水管漏水了吗？
Where is the drainpipe located? = 排水管在哪里？


否定句:
The drainpipe isn't blocked. = 排水管没有堵塞。
The drainpipe didn't burst. = 排水管没有爆裂。


PHRASE->
blocked drainpipe = 堵塞的排水管
broken drainpipe = 破裂的排水管

replace a drainpipe = 更换排水管

词源:
drain (v.) + pipe (n.)  排水（动词）+ 管子（名词）  组合而成。  "drain" 来自古法语 "drainer," 源于拉丁语 "trahere" （拉）, 指的是排出液体。 "pipe" 来自古法语 "pipe,"  源于拉丁语 "pipa" (管子)。
