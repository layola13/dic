rewritten:/ˌriːˈrɪtn/| v. |改写，重写|To write something again, often in a different way or to improve it.|例句：The essay was poorly written and needed to be rewritten.  这篇文章写得很糟糕，需要重写。|例句：She rewritten the script to make it more dramatic. 她改写了剧本，使其更具戏剧性。|近义词：revise, redraft, edit|反义词：write originally


USAGE->

He rewrites his articles frequently. = 他经常改写他的文章。
They rewrite the code for better efficiency. = 他们重写代码以提高效率。

She rewritten the letter to her friend. = 她重写了给朋友的信。
