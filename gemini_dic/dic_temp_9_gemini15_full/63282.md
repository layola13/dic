enthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/| vt.|加冕；使登基；拥戴；推崇|To place on or as if on a throne; to crown; to invest with regal power; to establish firmly in power or position. |例句：The new king was enthroned in a magnificent ceremony. 新国王在一个盛大的仪式上加冕。|例句：Her talent was quickly enthroned in the hearts of the public. 她的天赋很快就被公众推崇。|近义词：crown, install, elevate, exalt|反义词：dethrone, depose, degrade
