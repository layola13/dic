crystallite:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪt/| n. |微晶；晶粒 |A crystallite is a small crystal, usually one of many forming a larger polycrystalline aggregate. They are too small to be seen with the naked eye and are typically characterized by their size, shape, and crystallographic orientation.  |例句：The ceramic material is composed of numerous crystallites.  这种陶瓷材料由许多微晶组成。

The sample exhibited a high density of crystallites. 样品显示出高密度的微晶。|近义词：microcrystal, grain |反义词：single crystal
