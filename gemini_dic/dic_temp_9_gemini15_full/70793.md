fingerings:/ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋz/| n. |指法；按弦法|The way in which the fingers are used to play a musical instrument, especially a stringed instrument.  It also refers to the specific sequence of finger positions used in playing a piece of music.|例句：The pianist's fingerings were precise and elegant.  这位钢琴家的指法精准而优雅。

例句：Learning correct fingerings is crucial for playing the guitar well. 学习正确的指法对于良好地演奏吉他至关重要。


近义词：fingering (singular),  digital positions |反义词：None |词性:名词
