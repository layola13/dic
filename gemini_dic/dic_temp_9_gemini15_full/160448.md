popularizing: /ˈpɑːpjʊləraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 推广，普及 | Making (something) popular or widely known.  The process of making something widely accepted or known. | 近义词：promoting, disseminating, spreading, popularising (BrE) | 反义词：suppressing, obscuring, concealing


英文解析：The verb "popularizing" describes the act of making something popular or widely known. This could involve various methods, such as marketing, education, or simply widespread adoption. It implies a deliberate effort to increase the familiarity and acceptance of a concept, product, or idea among a larger audience.


例句：

The scientist is popularizing his research findings through public lectures. = 科学家正在通过公开讲座来推广他的研究成果。

The government is popularizing renewable energy sources to reduce carbon emissions. = 政府正在推广可再生能源以减少碳排放。
