captivator: /ˈkæptɪveɪtər/ | n. | 俘获者；迷住者；吸引人的事物 | Someone or something that captivates; someone who takes prisoners.  A person or thing that fascinates or enchants. | The magician was a true captivator, leaving the audience spellbound.  魔术师是一位真正的俘获者，让观众着迷。  His captivating performance was the highlight of the evening. 他迷人的表演是晚上的亮点。 | enchanter, charmer, fascinator | repellant, bore | 名词
