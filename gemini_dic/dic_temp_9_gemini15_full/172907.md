quake's: /kweɪks/ | n. | 地震的；颤抖的 | The possessive form of "quake," referring to something belonging to or associated with an earthquake or a trembling.  | Example: The quake's aftermath was devastating.  地震的后果是毁灭性的。 |近义词: tremor's, temblor's,  | 反义词:calm's, stillness's |  名词


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and does not have verb conjugations.  It describes something related to a quake.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.

PHRASE->
in the quake's wake = 在地震之后
the quake's epicenter = 地震震中
the quake's intensity = 地震强度
the quake's magnitude = 地震震级
the quake's damage = 地震造成的损失
