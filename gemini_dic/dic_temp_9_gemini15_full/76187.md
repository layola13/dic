fussing:/ˈfʌsɪŋ/| v.| 忙乱；大惊小怪；唠叨；过于关注细枝末节 |To be excessively concerned with minor details; to make a needless display of worry or anxiety.  |例句：Stop fussing over the small details; the big picture is what matters. 别再为这些小细节操心了，大局才是最重要的。

例句：She spent the morning fussing over her appearance. 她整个上午都在忙着打扮自己。


近义词：bothering, worrying, fretting, dithering,  nitpicking


反义词：calmness, indifference, unconcern


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fussing over his presentation. = 他正在为他的演示而忙乱。
She fusses over her children all the time. = 她总是为她的孩子们操心。

一般过去时:
They fussed over the arrangements for the party. = 他们为派对的安排忙得团团转。
He fussed over his tie before the interview. = 面试前他仔细地整理领带。

现在完成时:
I have been fussing over this report all day. = 我一整天都在为这份报告而忙乱。
She has fussed over every detail of the wedding. = 她为婚礼的每一个细节都操心。

过去完成时:
He had fussed over the cake for hours before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经为蛋糕忙活了几个小时。
She had fussed over her outfit until she was exhausted. = 她一直忙着打扮，直到筋疲力尽。

将来时:
I will be fussing over the dinner preparations tomorrow. = 我明天将忙于准备晚餐。
They will fuss over the baby all night. = 他们将整夜为婴儿操心。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't fuss over everything, I would have more free time. = 如果我不为所有事情操心，我会拥有更多空闲时间。
I wish I hadn't fussed so much about the trip. = 我真希望我没有为这次旅行操心那么多。

被动语态:
The arrangements were fussed over by the committee. = 这些安排被委员会反复琢磨。
The details were fussed over until they were perfect. = 细节被反复琢磨，直到完美无缺。

疑问句:
Why are you fussing so much? = 你为什么这么慌张？
Is she fussing over the children again? = 她又在为孩子们操心了吗？

否定句:
He didn't fuss over the minor problems. = 他没有为这些小问题而操心。
She didn't fuss over her appearance for the casual meeting. = 为了这次非正式会议，她没有刻意打扮。


PHRASE->
fuss over:  对…过于操心
fuss about:  为…而烦恼
make a fuss:  大惊小怪
fuss with:  摆弄；修补
fuss and bother:  大惊小怪；小题大做
