pineapple:/ˈpaɪnˌæpəl/| n. |菠萝|A tropical plant with a large fruit that is sweet and juicy, typically having a rough, brown skin and yellow flesh.  It is also the fruit itself. |例句：I love the sweet taste of fresh pineapple. 我喜欢新鲜菠萝的甜味。

例句：We bought a whole pineapple at the farmer's market. 我们在农贸市场买了一个完整的菠萝。


近义词：ananas (less common English term)


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性:noun
