confounded:/kənˈfaʊndɪd/| adj. |困惑的；令人困惑的；令人惊讶的|Confused and bewildered; causing confusion or bewilderment; surprising or astonishing.|例句：I was confounded by his sudden change of plans.  = 我对他突然改变计划感到困惑。|例句：The complexity of the problem confounded the experts. = 问题的复杂性难倒了专家们。|近义词：bewildered, puzzled, perplexed, amazed, astonished|反义词：clear, understandable, simple


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is confounded by the riddle. = 他被这个谜语难住了。
The instructions confound many users. = 这些说明让许多用户感到困惑。

一般过去时:
The unexpected news confounded me. = 意外的消息让我困惑不解。
The magician's trick confounded the audience. = 魔术师的戏法让观众们大惑不解。

现在完成时:
The sudden change has confounded all our plans. = 突然的变化打乱了我们所有的计划。
I have been confounded by this problem for days. = 我几天来都被这个问题难住了。

过去完成时:
They had been confounded by the lack of evidence. = 他们一直因为缺乏证据而感到困惑。
I had already been confounded by similar issues before. = 我之前已经因为类似的问题而感到困惑了。

将来时:
The new regulations will confound many businesses. = 新规将让许多企业感到困惑。
This complex design will confound even experienced engineers. = 即使是经验丰富的工程师，这个复杂的设计也会让他们感到困惑。

虚拟语气:
If I were not confounded by the jargon, I could understand it. = 如果我不被专业术语搞糊涂的话，我就能理解它。
I wish I weren't confounded by this difficult task. = 我真希望我不被这项困难的任务难住。

被动语态:
Many people are confounded by the government's policies. = 许多人对政府的政策感到困惑。
The students were confounded by the ambiguous question. = 学生们被含糊不清的问题难住了。

疑问句:
Are you confounded by the instructions? = 你对说明感到困惑吗？
Is he confounded by the complexity of the situation? = 他对局势的复杂性感到困惑吗？

否定句:
I am not confounded by this simple problem. = 我对这个简单的问题并不感到困惑。
She was not confounded by the unexpected turn of events. = 她并没有被意想不到的事件转折搞糊涂。
