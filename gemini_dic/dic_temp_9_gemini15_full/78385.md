get:/ɡɛt/ /ɡet/| v. |得到，获得，理解，到达 | To obtain or receive something. To arrive at a place. To understand something. |例句：I got a new car. = 我买了一辆新车。
I got to the station on time. = 我准时到达了车站。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get up early every day. = 我每天早起。
She gets good grades in school. = 她在学校成绩优异。

一般过去时:
I got a letter from my friend yesterday. = 昨天我收到朋友的一封信。
They got home late last night. = 他们昨晚很晚才回家。

现在完成时:
I have gotten used to the new job. = 我已经习惯了这份新工作。
She has gotten much better at playing the piano. = 她钢琴弹得比以前好多了。

过去完成时:
I had gotten ready before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经准备好了。
They had gotten lost before they found their way. = 他们在找到路之前迷路了。

将来时:
I will get a new phone next week. = 我下周会买一部新手机。
We will get there by bus. = 我们将乘公共汽车到达那里。

虚拟语气:
If I got a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我得到一百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I got better grades. = 我希望我的成绩更好。

被动语态:
The problem got solved quickly. = 问题很快得到了解决。
The letter got delivered this morning. = 信件今天早上送到了。

疑问句:
Do you get my point? = 你明白我的意思吗？
Did they get the message? = 他们收到信息了吗？

否定句:
I didn't get the joke. = 我不明白这个笑话。
He didn't get any sleep last night. = 他昨晚一点觉都没睡。


PHRASE->
get along = 相处融洽
get away with = 逃脱惩罚
get over = 克服；恢复
get rid of = 摆脱；去除
get back = 返回


近义词：obtain, receive, acquire, achieve, arrive, understand
反义词：lose, miss, fail to understand
词性：动词
