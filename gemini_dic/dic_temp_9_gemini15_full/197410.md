shed:/ʃɛd/ | n., v. | 棚屋；脱落；摆脱 | A simple structure used for storage or shelter; to cast off or lose (something); to get rid of. | The gardener stored his tools in the shed. / The snake shed its skin. / She shed tears of joy. |近义词:(n.) outhouse, cabin; (v.) cast off, discard, lose |反义词:(v.) retain, keep, acquire


USAGE->
