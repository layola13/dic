grandmothers:/ˈɡrænmʌðərz/| n. |祖母们；外祖母们 |The plural form of grandmother, referring to multiple grandmothers.  It can refer to a person's maternal or paternal grandmothers, or both. |My grandmothers were very different people, one a quiet artist and the other a boisterous businesswoman.|近义词:grandma(s), nana(s), granny(s) |反义词:grandchild(ren)
