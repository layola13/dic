hyperactive:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪv/| adj.|活动过度的；精力过剩的；极度活跃的| excessively active; showing excessive energy; extremely active.  The term often refers to a condition characterized by inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity.|例句：The hyperactive child couldn't sit still in class. 这个活动过度的孩子在课堂上坐不住。|例句：He was diagnosed with hyperactive disorder. 他被诊断患有活动过度症。|近义词：overactive, restless, energetic, excitable|反义词：lethargic, inactive, sluggish, apathetic

词源:
The word "hyperactive" is derived from the prefix "hyper-" (meaning "over" or "excessive") and the adjective "active" (meaning "engaged in action").  Therefore, it literally means "excessively active".
