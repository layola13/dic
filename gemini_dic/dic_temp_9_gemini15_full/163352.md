precongestion:/ˌpriːkənˈdʒɛstʃən/| n. |预拥堵，提前拥塞|The state of a network or system before it becomes congested, often characterized by increasing delays and reduced performance.  It's a proactive approach to managing congestion before it significantly impacts the system.|例句：The traffic management system detected precongestion on the highway and implemented diversion strategies.  高速公路交通管理系统检测到预拥堵并实施了改道策略。


The network administrator noticed signs of precongestion and initiated preventative measures. 网络管理员注意到预拥堵的迹象并启动了预防措施。|近义词：pre-saturation, incipient congestion|反义词：decongestion, free flow
