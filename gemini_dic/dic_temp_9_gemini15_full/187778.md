roquefort:/ˈrɒkfɔːrt/ /ˈroʊkfɔːrt/| n. |洛克福奶酪|A strong-smelling, pungent blue cheese from the Roquefort-sur-Soulzon region of France.  It is made from sheep's milk and has a characteristically crumbly texture and distinctive veined appearance due to the Penicillium Roqueforti mold. |The salad was topped with a generous crumble of Roquefort cheese.|近义词：blue cheese, Stilton|反义词：N/A
