mis-connecting:/ˌmɪskənˈɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 误接，连接错误 |  The act of incorrectly connecting something, leading to a failure in the intended function or a disruption in the system. This can refer to physical connections (like wires or pipes) or abstract connections (like ideas or data).  | Example: The technician apologized for mis-connecting the cables, causing a temporary network outage.  技术人员为接错电缆导致网络暂时中断而道歉。


The train mis-connected at the junction, delaying all passengers. 火车在枢纽站误接了，导致所有乘客延误。|近义词：malconnecting, wrongly connecting | 反义词：correctly connecting, properly connecting
