hebdomadary:/ˌhɛbdəˈmɛəri/| adj.|每周的；七天的|Relating to a week or a period of seven days.  The word is derived from the Latin "hebdomas," meaning "week," which in turn comes from the Greek "hebdomas" (ἑβδομάς), a combination of "hepta" (ἑπτά), meaning "seven," and "hēmera" (ἡμέρα), meaning "day."|The hebdomadary meeting is held every week. 每周都会举行例会。 The hebdomadary cycle of the liturgical year follows a seven-day pattern.  礼仪年中的每周循环遵循七天模式。|weekly, septenary|daily, momentary|形容词
