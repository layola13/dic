flutist:/ˈfluːtɪst/| n. |长笛演奏者|A flutist is a musician who plays the flute.  Flutists are often found in orchestras, bands, and as solo performers. They require significant skill and practice to master the instrument.|The flutist played a beautiful solo during the concert. 长笛演奏家在音乐会上演奏了一段优美的独奏。The young flutist practiced diligently every day.  年轻的长笛演奏家每天都勤奋练习。|musician, instrumentalist|non-musician
