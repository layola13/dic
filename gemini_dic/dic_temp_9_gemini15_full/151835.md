passible:/ˈpæsəbəl/| adj.|可忍受的；尚可的；可能的|Capable of being felt or experienced; possible.  It refers to something that can be endured or tolerated, though perhaps not ideally or pleasantly.  It can also imply something that is possible, though not certain.|例句：The pain was passible, but still quite uncomfortable.  疼痛是可以忍受的，但仍然相当不舒服。

The situation, though difficult, was passible. 尽管情况困难，但还是可以应付的。|近义词：tolerable, bearable, endurable, possible|反义词：unbearable, intolerable, impossible
