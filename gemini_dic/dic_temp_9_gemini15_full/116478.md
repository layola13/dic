materialist:/məˈtɪəriəlɪst/ | adj. n. |唯物主义者；唯物论的 |  A person who believes that matter is the only reality and that all things, including mental states and consciousness, are results of material interactions.  A philosophical position that emphasizes the importance of material things over spiritual or ideal ones. |例句：He is a staunch materialist who dismisses all talk of the soul. 他是一个坚定的唯物主义者，他驳斥所有关于灵魂的谈论。

例句：Many scientists adopt a materialistic worldview.许多科学家都采取唯物主义的世界观。


近义词：physicalist, pragmatist | 反义词：idealist, spiritualist |  词性：adj. n.
