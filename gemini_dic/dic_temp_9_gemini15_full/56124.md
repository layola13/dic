doctrines:/ˈdɒktrɪnz/| n. |教义；学说；信条 | Principles or beliefs, especially one held by a group or organization.  |例句：The doctrines of the church are based on the Bible. 教会的教义是基于圣经的。|例句：His political doctrines are considered radical by many. 他的政治学说是许多人认为激进的。|近义词：principles, beliefs, tenets, creeds |反义词：heresies (异端邪说) |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company follows strict doctrines regarding employee conduct. = 公司遵循关于员工行为的严格教条。
What doctrines does this religion uphold? = 这种宗教信仰什么教义？


一般过去时:
The ancient philosophers debated various doctrines. = 古代哲学家们争论各种学说。
The government adhered to protectionist doctrines in the past. = 政府过去坚持保护主义学说。


现在完成时:
Scientists have challenged many previously held doctrines. = 科学家们已经挑战了许多先前持有的学说。
The organization has revised its doctrines in light of new evidence. = 该组织根据新的证据修改了其教义。


过去完成时:
Before the reformation, the church had adhered to different doctrines. = 在宗教改革之前，教会坚持不同的教义。
They had already abandoned those outdated doctrines by the time the new leader arrived. = 新领导人到来时，他们已经放弃了那些过时的教义。


将来时:
Future generations will likely reinterpret these doctrines. = 后代人可能会重新解释这些教义。
The council will debate new doctrines next week. = 理事会下周将讨论新的教义。


虚拟语气:
If the company adopted these doctrines, it would be more successful. = 如果公司采纳这些教义，它将会更成功。
I wish they would reconsider their doctrines on environmental protection. = 我希望他们能重新考虑他们在环境保护方面的教义。


被动语态:
These doctrines were established centuries ago. = 这些教义是几个世纪前建立的。
The new doctrines are being implemented across the organization. = 新的教义正在整个组织中实施。


疑问句:
What are the core doctrines of this philosophy? = 这种哲学的核心教义是什么？
Which doctrines are most influential in shaping modern society? = 哪些教义对塑造现代社会最具影响力？


否定句:
The scientist did not accept the outdated doctrines. = 科学家不接受过时的教义。
We do not endorse those harmful doctrines.  = 我们不认可那些有害的教义。
