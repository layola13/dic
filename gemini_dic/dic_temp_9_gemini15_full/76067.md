furnishing:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 家具；陈设；供应 | The act of providing or equipping something, especially a room or building, with furniture and other necessary items.  It can also refer to the furniture and other items themselves. | Example: The furnishing of the apartment was modern and minimalist.  这套公寓的陈设现代而简约。  The new hotel boasts luxurious furnishings. 这家新酒店拥有豪华的家具。 |近义词：equipment, supplies, fittings |反义词：removal, depletion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop furnishes many homes with beautiful furniture. = 这家商店为许多家庭提供漂亮的家具。
He furnishes his apartment with antique pieces. = 他用古董家具装饰他的公寓。


一般过去时:
They furnished the house before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前布置了房子。
She furnished her office with new equipment. = 她用新设备布置了她的办公室。


现在完成时:
We have furnished the entire house. = 我们已经布置了整栋房子。
They have furnished their new apartment with modern designs. = 他们已经用现代设计布置了他们的新公寓。


过去完成时:
They had furnished the room before the party began. = 派对开始前他们已经布置好了房间。
She had furnished her studio with all the necessary equipment before starting her project. = 在开始她的项目之前，她已经用所有必要的设备布置了她的工作室。


将来时:
We will furnish the new office next week. = 我们下周将布置新办公室。
They will furnish their apartment with comfortable sofas and armchairs. = 他们将用舒适的沙发和扶手椅布置他们的公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would furnish my house beautifully. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把我的房子布置得很漂亮。
If she furnished her home differently, it would feel more spacious. = 如果她把家布置得不一样，感觉会更宽敞。


被动语态:
The room was furnished with antique chairs and a large table. = 房间里摆放着古董椅子和一张大桌子。
The hotel rooms are furnished to a high standard. = 酒店房间的装修标准很高。


疑问句:
How will you furnish your new home? = 你将如何布置你的新家？
Have they furnished the guest room yet? = 他们已经布置好客房了吗？


否定句:
They did not furnish the apartment completely. = 他们没有完全布置公寓。
We haven't furnished the living room yet. = 我们还没有布置客厅。
