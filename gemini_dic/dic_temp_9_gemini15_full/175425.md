rambutan:/ˈræmˌbʊtən/| n. |红毛丹|A rambutan is a fruit native to Southeast Asia.  It has a reddish-orange, hairy rind and a sweet, translucent white flesh.  The fruit is commonly eaten fresh or used in desserts and drinks.|例句：I bought some rambutans at the market today. 我今天在市场上买了些红毛丹。

例句：The rambutan trees were laden with ripe fruit. 红毛丹树上挂满了成熟的果实。


近义词：lychee (depending on context, as both are tropical fruits)
