compadre:/kəmˈpædreɪ/ | n. |哥们儿，朋友，老兄 | A close friend or companion, often implying a bond of shared experience or loyalty, especially among men.  The term often carries a connotation of camaraderie and mutual respect. | Example:  He and his compadre went on many adventures together. 他和他的哥们儿一起经历了很多冒险。|近义词：buddy, pal, mate, friend |反义词：enemy, foe, adversary |名词
