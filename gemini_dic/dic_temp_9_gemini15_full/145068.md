overanalytic: /ˌoʊvərænəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 过度分析的；分析过度的 | Excessively given to analysis; tending to analyze things to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  |  His overanalytic approach to the problem prevented him from finding a simple solution. 他对这个问题的过度分析妨碍了他找到一个简单的解决方案。 | 近义词：hypercritical, overly critical, meticulous, pedantic | 反义词：superficial, cursory, simplistic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is overanalytic in his interpretation of the data. = 他对数据的解读过于分析。
She is overanalytic about her work, often missing the bigger picture. = 她对工作过于分析，常常忽略大局。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
He has been overanalytic throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都过于分析。

过去完成时:
