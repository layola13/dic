proofreads:/ˈpruːfriːdz/ | v. | 校对；校读 | To read and correct the errors in (a text) before publication or distribution.  |  She proofreads all her articles before submitting them. 她会在提交所有文章之前仔细校对。 | edits, revises, checks |  |verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proofreads manuscripts for a living. = 他以校对稿件为生。
She proofreads documents for accuracy. = 她校对文件以确保准确性。


一般过去时:
I proofread the report last night. = 我昨晚校对报告了。
They proofread the book before it was printed. = 他们这本书在印刷前进行了校对。

She has proofread many documents this week. = 她本周已经校对了很多文件。
