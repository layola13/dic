fireproofing:/ˈfaɪəpruːfɪŋ/| n. adj. |防火，防火处理 |The process of making something resistant to fire.  It can refer to the process itself or the materials used. |例句：The building's fireproofing was inspected annually. 这座建筑的防火设施每年都会进行检查。

The fireproofing materials are highly effective.  这些防火材料非常有效。|近义词：fire protection, flame retardant |反义词：flammable, combustible
