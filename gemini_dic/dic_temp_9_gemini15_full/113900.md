madrigaler:/ˈmædrɪɡələr/| n. |创作或演唱牧歌的人|A composer or singer of madrigals.  Madrigalers were often highly skilled musicians who understood the intricacies of polyphony and word painting.|The madrigaler's performance was breathtaking, showcasing the beauty of the Renaissance music.|近义词：madrigalist, composer (of madrigals), singer (of madrigals)|反义词：无
