depolish:/diːˈpɒlɪʃ/ | vt. | 去光泽；磨去光泽 | To remove the polish from a surface.  | 例句：He depolished the table to give it a more rustic look. 他磨去了桌子的光泽，使其看起来更质朴。


例句：The craftsman depolished the wood before applying a new stain. 工匠在涂抹新的污渍之前先磨去了木材的光泽。


近义词：dull, matte, roughen, de-gloss


反义词：polish, shine, gloss


词性：动词
