feedback:/ˈfiːdbæk/ | n. | 反馈，回应 | Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc. which is used as a basis for improvement.  |例句：The teacher gave us feedback on our essays. 老师给了我们关于我们论文的反馈。  |例句：We need to collect feedback from customers to improve our service. 我们需要收集客户的反馈来改进我们的服务。|近义词：response, reaction, comments, input |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The customer gives feedback on the product. = 客户对产品给出反馈。
They provide feedback regularly. = 他们定期提供反馈。

一般过去时:
She gave positive feedback on the presentation. = 她对演示文稿给予了积极的反馈。
The team received feedback on their project. = 团队收到了关于他们项目的反馈。

现在完成时:
We have received valuable feedback from users. = 我们已经收到了用户宝贵的反馈。
They have not yet submitted their feedback. = 他们尚未提交他们的反馈。

过去完成时:
The manager had already reviewed the feedback before the meeting. = 在会议之前，经理已经审查了反馈。
They had collected all the feedback by the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前收集了所有反馈。

将来时:
The company will send out a feedback survey next week. = 公司下周将发送一份反馈调查问卷。
We will analyze the feedback and make improvements. = 我们将分析反馈并进行改进。

虚拟语气:
If we had more feedback, we could improve the product. = 如果我们有更多反馈，我们可以改进产品。
I wish we had received more constructive feedback. = 我希望我们收到更多建设性的反馈。

被动语态:
The feedback was carefully analyzed by the team. = 反馈由团队仔细分析。
The suggestions were incorporated into the new design after receiving feedback. = 在收到反馈后，建议被纳入新的设计中。

疑问句:
Have you received any feedback on your work? = 你收到关于你工作的任何反馈了吗？
What kind of feedback are you expecting? = 你期待什么样的反馈？

否定句:
They didn't receive any negative feedback. = 他们没有收到任何负面反馈。
We haven't gotten much feedback so far. = 到目前为止，我们没有得到很多反馈。


PHRASE->
positive feedback =积极反馈
constructive feedback =建设性反馈
negative feedback =负面反馈
customer feedback =客户反馈
actionable feedback =可操作的反馈
