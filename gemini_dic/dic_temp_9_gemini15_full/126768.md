nannyish:/ˈnænɪʃ/| adj.|像保姆的；爱唠叨的；过于关心琐事的|Having the characteristics of a nanny; excessively fussy or interfering.  The word suggests a slightly negative connotation, implying an overbearing or meddlesome nature.|例句：Her nannyish behavior annoyed everyone. 她的像保姆一样的行为惹恼了所有人。

例句：He gave his son nannyish instructions on how to cross the street. 他给了儿子关于如何过马路的啰嗦的指示。


近义词：fussy, overprotective, interfering, meddlesome, motherly (in a negative context)


反义词：indifferent, unconcerned, detached, laissez-faire


词性:形容词
