double-faults:/ˈdʌbəl fɔːlts/| n.|双误, 双失误|A double fault in tennis is when a server commits two consecutive faults, resulting in a point for the receiver.|例句：He lost the point due to a double fault. 他因为双误输掉了这一分。  He committed a double fault, gifting his opponent a point. 他犯了一个双误，送给对手一分。|近义词：error, mistake, blunder|反义词：ace
