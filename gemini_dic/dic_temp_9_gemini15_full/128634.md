nickelling:/ˈnɪkəlɪŋ/| n. |镀镍；少量镍；欺诈|The process of coating a metal with nickel to protect it from corrosion or enhance its appearance.  It can also refer to a small amount of nickel, or, informally, a fraudulent practice.|例句：The car parts underwent nickelling to prevent rust. 汽车零件经过镀镍处理以防止生锈。 The company was accused of nickelling and diming its customers. 这家公司被指控对顾客进行敲诈勒索。|近义词：plating, nickel plating, defrauding|反义词：de-nickelling, honesty
