recognizor: /ˈrɛkəɡnaɪzər/ | n. |识别器，辨认者 | A recognizor is a system or device that identifies or recognizes something, such as a pattern, voice, or face.  It can be a software program, a hardware component, or a combination of both.  | Example: The facial recognizor unlocked the phone.  面部识别器解锁了手机。 The speech recognizor transcribed the audio accurately. 语音识别器准确地转录了音频。|近义词：identifier, detector, discriminator |反义词：obfuscator (in the context of hiding information)
