gunmanship:/ˈɡʌnmænʃɪp/| n.|枪法；枪械技能；枪手本领|The skill and expertise in using guns.  It can also refer to the violence or aggression associated with the use of firearms.|例句：His gunmanship was legendary among his peers.  他的枪法在同龄人中是传奇般的。

例句：The outlaw's exceptional gunmanship allowed him to escape many shootouts.  那个亡命之徒精湛的枪法让他逃脱了许多枪战。


近义词：marksmanship, firearm proficiency, shooting skill

反义词：None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with pacifism or non-violence.


词性：Noun
