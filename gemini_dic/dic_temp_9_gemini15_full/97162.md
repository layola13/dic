infinity:/ɪnˈfɪnɪti/| n. |无限，无穷大 |  Infinity refers to something without any limits or end, a boundless or endless quantity or expanse. It's a concept often used in mathematics, philosophy, and cosmology.  | Example: The universe may stretch to infinity. 宇宙可能延伸到无限。  The possibilities are infinite.  可能性是无限的。|近义词：limitlessness, boundlessness, eternity |反义词：finiteness, limitation
