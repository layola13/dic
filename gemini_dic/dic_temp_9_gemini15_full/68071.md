faddiness:/ˈfædɪnəs/| n. |狂热；一时流行；反复无常|The fleeting nature of fads and the rapid rise and fall of faddiness are well-documented.  一时流行的短暂性和快速兴衰已得到充分证明。|近义词：whimsicality, capriciousness, fickleness |反义词：consistency, constancy, stability
