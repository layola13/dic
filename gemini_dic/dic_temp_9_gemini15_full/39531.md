conquest:/ˈkɒŋkwest/ /ˈkɑːŋkwest/| n., v.|征服；占领；克服|The act of conquering a place or people; the state of being conquered;  the overcoming of a difficulty or challenge. |例句：The Norman conquest of England changed the course of history.  诺曼人征服英格兰改变了历史进程。 The conquest of Everest was a monumental achievement.  征服珠穆朗玛峰是一项了不起的成就。|近义词：victory, triumph, subjugation, domination|反义词：defeat, surrender, liberation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conquers his fear. = 他克服了他的恐惧。
She conquers new markets. = 她征服了新的市场。

一般过去时:
The Romans conquered Gaul. = 罗马人征服了高卢。
They conquered the mountain. = 他们征服了这座山。

现在完成时:
They have conquered many challenges. = 他们已经克服了许多挑战。
She has conquered her addiction. = 她已经克服了她的瘾。

过去完成时:
He had conquered his fear before the speech. = 演讲之前他已经克服了他的恐惧。
They had conquered the disease by the time the new treatment arrived. = 新疗法到来时，他们已经征服了这种疾病。

将来时:
We will conquer this problem. = 我们将克服这个问题。
They will conquer the summit tomorrow. = 他们明天将征服山顶。

虚拟语气:
If only we could conquer hunger. = 如果我们能够征服饥饿就好了。
I wish I could conquer my shyness. = 我希望我能克服我的害羞。

被动语态:
The city was conquered in a single day. = 这座城市在一天之内就被征服了。
The disease has not yet been conquered. = 这种疾病尚未被征服。

疑问句:
Can we conquer this obstacle? = 我们能克服这个障碍吗？
Has humanity conquered disease? = 人类已经征服疾病了吗？

否定句:
They did not conquer the land easily. = 他们并没有轻易征服这片土地。
We could not conquer our doubts. = 我们无法克服我们的疑虑。
