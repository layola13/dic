outwaved:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvd/| v.|超过，胜过，压倒|To surpass or exceed in power, influence, or importance.|The new technology outwaved the old system in efficiency.|近义词：surpass, exceed, overshadow|反义词：undermined, surpassed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new design outwaves the old one in terms of functionality. = 新设计在功能方面胜过旧设计。
His influence outwaves that of any other leader in the region. = 他的影响力超过该地区任何其他领导人。


一般过去时:
Her performance outwaved all expectations. = 她的表现超出了所有预期。
The storm outwaved the previous one in intensity. = 这场风暴的强度超过了之前的风暴。


现在完成时:
The company's success has outwaved all initial predictions. = 公司的成功已经超过了最初的所有预测。
Technological advancements have outwaved traditional methods in many fields. = 在许多领域，技术进步已经超过了传统方法。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, their efforts had outwaved those of their competitors. = 到项目结束时，他们的努力已经超过了竞争对手。
The ancient civilization's achievements had outwaved those of many other societies of that era. = 这个古代文明的成就已经超过了那个时代许多其他社会。


将来时:
The upcoming innovation will outwave current technologies in efficiency. = 未来即将推出的创新将在效率方面胜过当前技术。
The new strategy is expected to outwave the competition in the market. = 预计新战略将能够在市场上胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, our results would have outwaved the competition's. = 如果我们投资更多，我们的结果就会超过竞争对手。
I wish our team's performance had outwaved the others'. = 我希望我们团队的表现能够超过其他团队。


被动语态:
The old system was outwaved by the new one in terms of speed and accuracy. = 旧系统在速度和准确性方面被新系统超越。
Their ideas were outwaved by the innovative proposal. = 他们的想法被创新的提案超越。


疑问句:
Does the new product outwave its competitors in terms of price? = 新产品在价格方面是否胜过竞争对手？
Will this technology outwave the existing ones in the long run? = 从长远来看，这项技术能否胜过现有的技术？


否定句:
The small company's efforts did not outwave those of the larger corporation. = 这家小公司的努力并没有超过大公司的努力。
His contribution did not outwave the collective work of the team. = 他的贡献并没有超过团队的集体工作。
