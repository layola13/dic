reprieve:/rɪˈpriːv/| n. vt.|缓刑；暂缓；赦免；减刑；解脱|The temporary postponement of punishment.  A respite; a period of relief.|例句：The prisoner received a reprieve from the death penalty. 犯人被免于死刑。

例句：The rain provided a welcome reprieve from the heat.  雨带来了令人欣慰的凉爽。


近义词：pardon, respite, postponement, stay, relief|反义词：execution, punishment, condemnation


词性:名词，动词
