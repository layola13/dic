redistillation: /ˌriːˌdɪstɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 再蒸馏；重新提炼 | The process of distilling something a second or further time.  It is used to purify a substance or to separate different components of a mixture more completely. | Example: The redistillation of the alcohol yielded a purer product. 再蒸馏酒精产生了更纯净的产品。 |近义词：rectification, purification |反义词：暂无
