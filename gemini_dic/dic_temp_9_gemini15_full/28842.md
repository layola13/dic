carpenter:/ˈkɑːrpəntər/| n. |木匠，木工|A carpenter is a skilled worker who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures.  They use various tools and techniques to create things like furniture, houses, and other wooden items.|例句：My uncle is a skilled carpenter who built our entire deck.  我的叔叔是一位技艺精湛的木匠，他建造了我们整个露台。

例句：The carpenter carefully measured the wood before cutting it. 木匠在切割木材之前仔细地测量了它。


近义词：joiner, woodworker|反义词：N/A
