col:/kɒl/ /kɑːl/| n. |柱，栏，列；颈项；煤层| a vertical structure, usually supporting something; a vertical arrangement of items; the neck or back of the neck; a seam or layer of coal |例句：The columns of the temple were impressively tall.  寺庙的柱子高耸壮观。

The data is organized into neat columns in the spreadsheet.  数据在电子表格中被组织成整齐的列。


近义词：pillar, post, row, line | 反义词：row (in the sense of a horizontal arrangement)
