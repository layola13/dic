line-up:/ˈlaɪn ʌp/| n., v.|阵容；队列；使排队；(使)整齐排列 |A group of people or things arranged in a row; to arrange in a row or in order. |例句：The band's line-up includes several famous musicians.乐队的阵容包括几位著名的音乐家。

The police made the suspects line up for identification.警察让嫌疑犯排队接受辨认。|近义词：formation, arrangement, array, lineup, roster|反义词：disorder, chaos, disarray|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team lines up before the game. = 球队在比赛前排好队。
They line up for the bus every morning. = 他们每天早上都排队等公交车。

一般过去时:
The soldiers lined up in perfect formation. = 士兵们排成了完美的队列。
She lined up all her trophies on the shelf. = 她把所有的奖杯都整齐地排列在架子上。

现在完成时:
The band has lined up a great tour this year. = 乐队今年安排了一次很棒的巡演。
They have lined up all the chairs for the meeting. = 他们已经为会议摆好了所有的椅子。

过去完成时:
The players had lined up before the coach arrived. = 教练到达之前，球员们就已经排好队了。
She had lined up all the ingredients before starting to cook. = 她开始做饭之前已经准备好所有的食材了。

将来时:
The company will line up new products for the next quarter. = 公司将为下一季度推出新产品。
We will line up the volunteers for the charity event. = 我们将为慈善活动安排志愿者。

虚拟语气:
If they lined up earlier, they wouldn't have missed the bus. = 如果他们早点排队，就不会错过公交车了。
I wish I had lined up my schedule better. = 我真希望我更好地安排了我的时间表。

被动语态:
The suspects were lined up for identification. = 嫌疑犯被排队接受辨认。
The chairs were lined up neatly in the hall. = 椅子被整齐地排列在大厅里。

疑问句:
Are the students lined up yet? = 学生们排好队了吗？
How many people are lined up for the concert? = 有多少人排队等候音乐会？

否定句:
The cars didn't line up properly. = 车子没有排好队。
They didn't line up for the tickets. = 他们没有排队买票。
