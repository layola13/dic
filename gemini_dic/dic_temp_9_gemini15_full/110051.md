licensure:/ˈlaɪsənʃər/| n. |许可证；特许；授权 |The granting of a license to practice a profession or trade.  It signifies official permission to engage in a specific activity, often requiring the fulfillment of certain requirements, such as education, training, and examinations. |例句：He finally received his licensure as a physician after years of training. 他经过多年的培训后终于获得了行医执照。|例句：The state board of nursing oversees the licensure process for registered nurses. 州护士委员会负责注册护士的许可程序。|近义词：authorization, certification, permit, accreditation |反义词：prohibition, ban, restriction |名词
