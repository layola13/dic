cymogene:/ˈsaɪmədʒiːn/| n. |细胞发生素，细胞发生基因|A hypothetical substance or gene believed to be responsible for the initiation of cell division and differentiation.  The term is largely outdated and not widely used in modern biology.|Example: The research attempted to isolate the cymogene responsible for the rapid cell growth observed in the tumor.  这项研究试图分离出导致肿瘤中观察到的快速细胞生长的细胞发生素。|近义词: morphogen (in some contexts), growth factor|反义词:None directly applicable, but concepts like cell death inhibitor could be considered opposite in effect.
