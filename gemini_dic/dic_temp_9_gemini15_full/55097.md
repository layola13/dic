disrespects: /ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛkts/ | v. | 不尊重；轻视 | To show a lack of respect for someone or something.  |例句：He disrespects his elders by talking back to them. 他对长辈无礼，顶撞他们。|近义词：insults, offends, slight, belittles | 反义词：respects, honors, reveres | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disrespects his teacher regularly. = 他经常不尊重他的老师。
They disrespects the rules of the game. = 他们不尊重比赛规则。

一般过去时:
She disrespected her boss by leaving early. = 她提前离开，对老板很不尊重。
He disrespected the national flag. = 他不尊重国旗。


现在完成时:
They have disrespected the memory of the victims. = 他们不尊重遇难者的记忆。
She has disrespected me on multiple occasions. = 她多次不尊重我。


过去完成时:
He had disrespected his parents many times before he finally apologized. = 在他最终道歉之前，他已经多次不尊重他的父母。
We had disrespected the traditions of our ancestors. = 我们不尊重祖先的传统。


将来时:
I fear he will disrespect the court's decision. = 我担心他会不尊重法院的裁决。
She will disrespect her commitments if she continues this way. = 如果她继续这样下去，她会不尊重她的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If he disrespected the law, he would face consequences. = 如果他违反法律，他将面临后果。
I wish he hadn't disrespected my family. = 我真希望他没有不尊重我的家人。


被动语态:
His actions were disrespected by many people. = 他的行为受到了许多人的谴责（被动语态中，disrespected 更偏向于“被轻视”）。
The decision was disrespected by the public. = 这个决定受到了公众的轻视。


疑问句:
Does he disrespect authority figures? = 他不尊重权威人士吗？
Did they disrespect the agreement we made? = 他们违背了我们达成的协议吗？


否定句:
He doesn't disrespect his colleagues. = 他不轻视他的同事。
They did not disrespect the property. = 他们没有损坏财产。


PHRASE->
show disrespect to = 对…表示不敬
have no respect for = 对…没有尊重
with disrespect = 不敬地
lack of disrespect = 缺乏尊重
express disrespect = 表达不尊重
