hagiographist:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfɪst/| n. |圣徒传记作者；谄媚的传记作者|A hagiographist is a writer of hagiographies, which are biographies of saints or other holy figures, often idealized and focusing on their virtuous acts and miracles.  Hagiographies can sometimes be considered biased or uncritical in their portrayal of the subject. |The hagiographist meticulously detailed the saint's life, emphasizing his miracles and unwavering faith.  圣徒传记作者一丝不苟地详细记述了圣徒的一生，强调了他的奇迹和坚定不移的信仰。| biographer, panegyrist | detractor, critic |名词
