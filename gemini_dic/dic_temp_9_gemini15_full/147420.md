overnourishments:/ˌoʊvərˈnʊrɪʃmənts/| n. |营养过剩；过度滋养|The excessive intake of nutrients leading to negative health consequences.  This can manifest in various ways depending on the specific nutrient in excess. For example, overconsumption of certain vitamins can lead to toxicity, while excessive calorie intake can result in obesity and related health problems.|The child's overnourishments led to obesity and health complications.|近义词：overfeeding, hyperalimentation|反义词：malnutrition, undernourishment|名词
