creases:/kriːsɪz/ | n. | 折痕；皱纹；褶皱 | Lines or folds formed in cloth, paper, or skin.  |例句：The dress had several creases after being packed in the suitcase.  这件衣服在行李箱里压过后有好几道褶皱。

The map was covered in creases. 这张地图满是折痕。


近义词：wrinkles, folds, pleats | 反义词：smoothness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The iron removes creases from the shirt. = 熨斗能去除衬衫上的褶皱。
Many creases appear on his face after years of hard work. =  多年辛劳后，他脸上出现了许多皱纹。


一般过去时:
She carefully smoothed out the creases in the tablecloth. = 她仔细地抚平了桌布上的褶皱。
The accident caused deep creases in the car's body. = 事故在汽车的车身上造成了深深的凹痕。


现在完成时:
The sun has creased the leather of the old boots. = 太阳晒得旧靴子的皮革起了皱。
Years of worry have creased her forehead. = 多年的忧虑使她的额头布满了皱纹。


过去完成时:
The letter had been creased and crumpled before I opened it. = 我打开信之前，它已经被弄皱了。
He had already creased the paper before he started writing. = 他在开始写字之前就已经把纸弄皱了。


将来时:

Repeated folding will crease the fabric. = 反复折叠会使织物起皱。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't ironed the shirt, it would have many creases. = 如果我没有熨衬衫，它就会有很多褶皱。

被动语态:

The photograph was creased in my pocket. = 照片在我的口袋里被弄皱了。


疑问句:

否定句:
