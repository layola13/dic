incitant: /ˈɪnsɪtənt/ | n. |煽动者；激励物 | A person or thing that incites or stirs up a feeling, action, or event.  | 例句：The incitant of the riot was never found. 煽动暴乱的人从未被找到。 |近义词：instigator, agitator, stimulus, incentive | 反义词：pacifier, deterrent
