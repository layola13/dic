claimed:/kleɪmd/| vt.|声称，认领，索赔|To state something as a fact, although it has not been proved.  To say that something is yours.|例句：He claimed to have seen a ghost. 他声称看到过鬼。

He claimed the reward. 他认领了奖赏。|近义词：asserted, alleged, maintained|反义词：denied, refuted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He claims he is innocent. = 他声称自己是无辜的。
She claims to be a doctor. = 她声称自己是一名医生。

一般过去时:
They claimed they were not involved. = 他们声称他们没有参与其中。
He claimed the prize last night. = 他昨晚领取了奖品。

现在完成时:
She has claimed responsibility for the accident. = 她已经对这起事故承担了责任。
They have claimed all the available seats. = 他们已经占据了所有可用的座位。

过去完成时:
He had claimed the land before anyone else. = 他在其他人之前就已宣称拥有这块土地。
She had claimed she didn't know anything. = 她之前声称她什么都不知道。

将来时:
He will claim his inheritance next month. = 他下个月将要认领他的遗产。
They will claim damages for the injury. = 他们将索赔因受伤造成的损失。

虚拟语气:
If he claimed the prize, he would be very happy. = 如果他赢得奖品，他会非常高兴。
I wish he had claimed his rights. = 我希望他曾主张过他的权利。

被动语态:
The land was claimed by the government. = 这块土地被政府宣称拥有。
The prize was claimed by a lucky winner. = 奖品被一位幸运的赢家领取了。

疑问句:
Did he claim the money? = 他要钱了吗？
What did she claim to have seen? = 她声称看到了什么？

否定句:
He didn't claim to know anything about it. = 他声称对这件事一无所知。
She didn't claim the lost property. = 她没有认领丢失的物品。


PHRASE->
lay claim to: 宣称拥有

claim responsibility: 承担责任
stake a claim: 提出要求
false claim: 虚假主张
