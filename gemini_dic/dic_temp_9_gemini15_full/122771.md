mojos:/ˈmoʊdʒoʊz/| n.|魔力，魅力，运气，节奏感|magical charm or power;  a feeling of confidence and energy; a distinctive style or quality|例句：He's got the mojo to win this election. 他有赢得这场选举的魔力。

例句：She's lost her mojo lately. 她最近失去了魅力。


近义词：charisma, charm, magic, flair, style, rhythm

反义词：ineptitude, clumsiness, lack of confidence


词源:  The word "mojo" originates from the West African term *mojo*, meaning "magic charm" or "amulet."  It entered English slang in the early 20th century, initially referring to a magical object or charm, but its meaning broadened to encompass charisma, confidence, and even a distinctive style or rhythm.
