flexbox:/ˈflɛksbɒks/ | n. | 弹性盒子布局 | A CSS layout module that allows for flexible and efficient one-dimensional layout of items within a container. It offers powerful features for aligning and distributing space among items, making it ideal for creating responsive and adaptable layouts.  | Example: We used flexbox to center the logo on the webpage. 我们用弹性盒子布局将logo居中在网页上。 | 近义词：grid layout (网格布局)  | 反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，更多的是与其他布局方式对比) | 名词
