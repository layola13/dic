jiving:/ˈdʒaɪvɪŋ/| v.|跳吉格舞；闲聊；欺骗|To dance the jive; to talk casually or informally; to trick or deceive someone.  |例句：They were jiving to the music.  他们在随着音乐跳吉格舞。
They were just jiving, don't take it seriously. 他们只是在闲聊，别当真。|近义词：dancing, chatting, deceiving, swindling|反义词：being serious, being honest
