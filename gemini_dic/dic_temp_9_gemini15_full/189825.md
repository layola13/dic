salvoed:/ˈsælvəʊd/ | v. |齐射；挽救；解救 |  To fire a volley of shots; to rescue or save from danger or harm. | 例句：The battleship salvoed its guns at the enemy fleet.  = 战列舰向敌舰队进行了齐射。 The firefighters salvoed the trapped family from the burning building. = 消防员将被困的家庭从燃烧的建筑物中救了出来。 | 近义词：fired, rescued, saved | 反义词：destroyed, lost, condemned
