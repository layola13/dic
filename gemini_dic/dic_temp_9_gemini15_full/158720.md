ploughshare: /ˈplaʊʃeər/| n. |犁铧；犁头 |The ploughshare is the metal blade of a plough that cuts and turns the soil.  It's the crucial part of the plough that makes tilling the land possible.  |The farmer sharpened the ploughshare before beginning to plough the field.| 近义词：share, blade | 反义词：N/A
