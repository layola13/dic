extraterritorial: /ˌɛkstrætɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 治外法权的；领土外的 |  Existing or exercised outside of a country's normal territorial limits; not subject to the jurisdiction of the local authorities.  |例句：The embassy enjoys extraterritorial rights.  大使馆享有治外法权。


The extraterritorial application of U.S. law is controversial.  美国法律的域外适用是有争议的。


近义词：extra-territorial, foreign, international | 反义词：domestic, internal | 词性: adjective
