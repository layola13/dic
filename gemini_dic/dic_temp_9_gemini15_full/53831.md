disconsecrate:/dɪsˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/| vt.| 亵渎；取消…的圣洁；(尤指教堂)废弃不用 | To remove the consecration or sacred character from (something, especially a church or object).  To profane or violate the sanctity of. |例句：The temple was disconsecrated after the earthquake. 地震后，寺庙被废弃不用。


The old church was disconsecrated and converted into a museum. 旧教堂被废弃不用，改建成了博物馆。| 近义词：desecrate, profane, violate | 反义词：consecrate, sanctify | 动词


USAGE->

They disconsecrate the old altar. = 他们亵渎了旧祭坛。
The church disconsecrates buildings that are no longer used for worship. = 教会将不再用于礼拜的建筑物废弃不用。


一般过去时:
The government disconsecrated the land. = 政府取消了这块土地的圣洁。

现在完成时:
They have disconsecrated many sacred sites. = 他们已经亵渎了许多圣地。

过去完成时:
The vandals had disconsecrated the tomb before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，破坏分子已经亵渎了坟墓。
They had disconsecrated the temple long before the discovery. = 他们在发现之前很久就已废弃了寺庙。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish they hadn't disconsecrated the ancient burial ground. = 我真希望他们没有亵渎那片古老的墓地。


被动语态:
The statue was disconsecrated by the invading army. = 雕像被入侵的军队亵渎了。
The land was disconsecrated to allow for commercial development. = 这块土地被取消了圣洁以允许商业开发。


疑问句:
Was the building disconsecrated after the fire? = 大火之后，那座建筑物被废弃不用了吗？
Why was the ancient temple disconsecrated? = 为什么古代寺庙被废弃不用？


否定句:
They did not disconsecrate the sacred grove. = 他们没有亵渎这片圣林。
The church authorities would not disconsecrate the historic cathedral. = 教会当局不会废弃这座历史悠久的教堂。


PHRASE->
disconsecrate a church = 废弃一座教堂
disconsecrate sacred ground = 亵渎圣地

disconsecrate a tomb = 亵渎坟墓

词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + consecrate (使神圣化，奉献)  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word *consecrare*, meaning "to make sacred".
