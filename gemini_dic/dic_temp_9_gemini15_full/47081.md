datsun:/ˈdætˌsʌn/| n. |达特桑（日产汽车品牌）|Datsun was a Japanese automobile marque, originally established in 1931 as DAT Motorcar Company, and later revived by Nissan.  It is known for its affordable and reliable vehicles, particularly popular in the 1960s and 70s. The name "Datsun" is a portmanteau of the letters "DAT" from its predecessor and "sun".  The brand was discontinued in 1986, but revived in 2013 focusing primarily on emerging markets. |My grandfather owned a Datsun 240Z; it was a fantastic car.  我的祖父拥有一辆达特桑240Z；那是一辆很棒的车。|近义词：Nissan (parent company)|反义词：无
