nonreversibleness:/ˌnɒnˌrɪvɜːˈsɪbəlnəs/ | n. |不可逆性，不可逆转性 | The inability to be reversed or undone.  It refers to a process or state that cannot be returned to its original condition.  | Example: The nonreversibleness of the chemical reaction made it impossible to recover the original reactants.  不可逆的化学反应使得无法回收最初的反应物。 |近义词:irreversibility, finality |反义词:reversibility
