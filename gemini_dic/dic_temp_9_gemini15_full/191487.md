schlepping:/ˈʃlɛpɪŋ/| v. |费力地搬运；拖着走；(尤指)长途跋涉 |To carry or drag something heavy or cumbersome, often implying a long distance or great effort.  It often suggests a tedious or unpleasant task.|例句：I spent the whole morning schlepping groceries from the car to the apartment.  我整个上午都在费力地把杂货从车里搬到公寓。

例句：She schlepped her luggage across the airport. 她拖着行李箱穿过机场。


近义词： lugging, hauling, dragging, transporting, carrying


反义词：carrying lightly, floating


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He schleps groceries every Saturday. 他每周六都费力地搬运杂货。
They schlep their kids to school every morning. 他们每天早上都费力地把孩子们送到学校。

一般过去时:
I schlepped all the furniture upstairs myself. 我自己把所有的家具都搬上了楼。
She schlepped her heavy suitcase for miles. 她拖着沉重的行李箱走了好几英里。

现在完成时:
He has schlepped countless boxes for this move.  为了这次搬家，他已经搬运了无数个箱子。
We have schlepped all the equipment to the site. 我们已经把所有设备都搬运到现场了。

过去完成时:
She had schlepped the bags all the way from the station. 她已经把包从车站一直拖到这儿了。
They had schlepped the heavy equipment up the hill before dark. 他们在天黑前把重型设备搬上了山。

将来时:
I will schlep the boxes to the storage unit tomorrow. 我明天要把箱子搬到储物单元。
They will schlep the furniture to the new house next week. 他们下周将把家具搬到新房子。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I wouldn't have to schlep all these bags on the bus. 如果我有车，我就不用把这些包都搬到公共汽车上了。
I wish I didn't have to schlep all this stuff to my apartment. 我真希望我不必把所有这些东西都搬到我的公寓。


被动语态:
The luggage was schlepped to the hotel by a porter. 行李由搬运工搬到了酒店。
The heavy boxes were schlepped to the truck by several workers.  几个工人把沉重的箱子搬到了卡车上。


疑问句:
Did he schlep all those boxes by himself? 他一个人搬了所有那些箱子吗？
Will they schlep the equipment up the mountain? 他们会把设备搬到山上吗？


否定句:
I didn't schlep the groceries; my husband did. 我没有搬运杂货；我丈夫搬的。
They won't schlep the furniture without help. 他们没有帮助不会搬运家具。


PHRASE->

schlepping things:搬运东西
schlepping to:费力地搬到...
schlepping through:艰难地穿过
schlepping along:拖着走


词源:
Yiddish שלעפּן (shlep)  meaning "to drag" or "to haul".  The word ultimately derives from the Middle High German *sleppen, meaning "to drag".
