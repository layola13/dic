sledge:/sledʒ/| n. v.|雪橇；猛击；|A sledge is a vehicle used for transporting goods or people over snow or ice, typically pulled by animals or people.  It can also be a large hammer used for breaking things.|例句：The children enjoyed sledging down the snowy hill. 孩子们喜欢在雪坡上滑雪橇。
The worker used a sledgehammer to break up the concrete. 工人用大锤砸碎混凝土。|近义词：sleigh, toboggan (n.)；hammer, batter (v.)|反义词：None (n.); None (v.)
