labs:/læbz/| n.|实验室；实验工作|A lab is a place where scientific research and experiments are conducted.  It can refer to a single room or a large complex of buildings.|例句：The scientists worked late into the night in the labs. 科学家们在实验室里工作到深夜。|例句：The university has state-of-the-art labs for its students. 这所大学为学生配备了最先进的实验室。|近义词：laboratory, research facility|反义词：field (in contrast to controlled environment)|名词
