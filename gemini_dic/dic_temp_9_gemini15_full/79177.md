glamour:/ˈɡlæmər/| n.|魅力，魔力，诱惑力|The alluring and exciting quality that attracts and fascinates; a magical or enchanting quality.  It often refers to an idealized and often artificial beauty and attractiveness, particularly associated with wealth, fame, and excitement.|例句：The glamour of Hollywood attracted many aspiring actors.好莱坞的魅力吸引了许多有抱负的演员。|例句：The old house had a certain decaying glamour.这座老房子有一种独特的颓废魅力。|近义词：allure, charm, fascination, allure, appeal|反义词：dullness, ordinariness, drabness|名词
