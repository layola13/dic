kick the bucket:/kɪk ðə ˈbʌkɪt/ | phrasal v. | 死，去世 | To die.  This is an informal idiom. The origin is uncertain, but some theories suggest it relates to kicking a bucket used to support a person being hanged, or perhaps to the kicking of a bucket in the process of slaughtering a pig. | Example:  My grandfather kicked the bucket last year.  = 我爷爷去年去世了。  She kicked the bucket peacefully in her sleep. = 她安详地睡梦中去世了。|近义词：die, pass away, depart, perish |反义词：be born, come to life


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kicks the bucket every day. (figurative, implies frequent failure or demise of a project etc.) = 他每天都失败/项目都完蛋。(比喻，暗示项目经常失败或消亡等)
They kick the bucket regularly. (figurative, implies frequent failure or demise of a project etc.) = 他们经常失败/项目都完蛋。(比喻，暗示项目经常失败或消亡等)


一般过去时:
He kicked the bucket last week. = 他上周去世了。
The project kicked the bucket. = 项目失败了。


现在完成时:
My grandmother has kicked the bucket. = 我祖母去世了。
The plan has kicked the bucket. = 计划失败了。


过去完成时:
He had kicked the bucket before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经去世了。
The deal had kicked the bucket by then. = 到那时交易已经失败了。


将来时:
He will kick the bucket someday. = 他总有一天会死的。
The company might kick the bucket if they don't improve. = 如果他们不改进，公司可能会倒闭。


虚拟语气:
If he kicked the bucket, who would inherit his fortune? = 如果他死了，谁会继承他的财产？
I wish the project hadn't kicked the bucket. = 我真希望这个项目没有失败。


被动语态:
(This phrase is rarely used in passive voice due to its idiomatic nature.)


疑问句:
Did he kick the bucket? = 他死了吗？
Has the business kicked the bucket yet? = 那家公司倒闭了吗？


否定句:
He didn't kick the bucket. = 他没死。
The project hasn't kicked the bucket yet. = 项目还没有失败。


PHRASE->

kick the bucket peacefully = 平静地去世
kick the bucket suddenly = 突然去世
