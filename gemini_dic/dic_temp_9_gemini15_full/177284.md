reassimilates:/ˌriːəˈsɪməleɪts/| v.|重新同化；重新吸收|To absorb or integrate (something) again, especially into a larger whole.  This often implies a previous process of assimilation that was disrupted or incomplete.|例句：The society reassimilates immigrants through cultural programs. 社区通过文化项目重新同化移民。

例句：The body reassimilates the nutrients after digestion. 身体在消化后重新吸收营养物质。


近义词：reintegrate, reabsorb, incorporate

反义词：segregate, exclude, expel
