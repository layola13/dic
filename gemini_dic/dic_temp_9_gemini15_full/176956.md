realliances: /ˌriːəˈlaɪənsɪz/ | n. | 再联盟；新的联盟 |  The formation of new alliances or the reformation of old ones.  It refers to the act of forming new alliances, often after a period of dissolution or significant change in existing alliances.  This term highlights the dynamic nature of international relations and power dynamics. | Example: The shifting geopolitical landscape led to a series of realliances among nations.  新的地缘政治格局导致各国之间一系列新的联盟的形成。 | Near synonyms: realignment, reformation, restructuring | Antonyms: dissolution, breakup |  Noun
