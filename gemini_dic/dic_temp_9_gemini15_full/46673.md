dampening: /ˈdæmpənɪŋ/ | v. | 抑制；减弱；使潮湿 | To make something less strong or intense; to make something wet. | 例句：The rain dampened our spirits.  雨水使我们情绪低落。  The government's policies are dampening economic growth. 政府的政策正在抑制经济增长。| 近义词：reducing, weakening, diminishing, moderating, dampening | 反义词：intensifying, strengthening, boosting, enhancing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain dampens the fire. = 雨水浇灭了火。
The cold weather dampens my enthusiasm. =  寒冷的天气降低了我的热情。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
