salutations:/ˌsæluˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 问候；敬辞；致意 | Greetings or expressions of goodwill, often at the beginning of a letter or speech.  | Example: The letter began with formal salutations. 这封信以正式的问候语开头。 |近义词：greetings, regards, compliments |反义词：farewells, goodbyes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The speaker offers warm salutations to the audience.  = 演讲者向观众致以热烈的问候。
She sends her salutations to her colleagues. = 她向她的同事们致意。


一般过去时:
He gave a formal salutation before his speech. = 他在演讲前做了正式的致辞。
They exchanged polite salutations at the meeting. = 他们在会议上互相进行了礼貌的问候。


现在完成时:
She has sent salutations to the birthday girl. = 她已经向寿星送上了祝福。
He has received numerous salutations on his recent achievement. = 他因最近的成就收到了许多祝贺。


过去完成时:
They had already exchanged salutations before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他们已经互相问候过了。
She had sent her salutations before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经送上了祝福。


将来时:
He will offer his salutations at the beginning of the conference. = 他将在会议开始时致辞。
She will send her salutations to her family. = 她将向她的家人致意。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would offer my salutations. = 如果我见到他，我会向他问候。
I wish I had sent my salutations earlier. = 我希望我早点送上祝福。


被动语态:
The guests were greeted with warm salutations. = 客人们受到了热烈的欢迎。
His contributions were acknowledged with formal salutations. = 他的贡献得到了正式的认可。


疑问句:
What salutations are appropriate for this occasion? = 这次场合合适的问候是什么？
What kind of salutations did you use in your letter? = 你在信中使用了什么类型的问候语？


否定句:
He offered no salutations before beginning his presentation. = 他在开始演示前没有打招呼。
The letter lacked any formal salutations. = 这封信缺少任何正式的问候语。



PHRASE->
formal salutations = 正式问候
warm salutations = 热烈的问候
polite salutations = 礼貌的问候
exchange salutations = 互相问候
offer salutations = 致意/问候
