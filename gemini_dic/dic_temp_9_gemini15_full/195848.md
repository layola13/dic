senhor:/sɛˈnɔːr/| n. |先生；阁下|A Portuguese and Brazilian term of address for a man, equivalent to "Mr." or "Sir" in English.  It is a title of respect.|Exemplo: Senhor Presidente, gostaria de fazer uma pergunta. (Mr. President, I would like to ask a question.)|近义词：mister, sir, gentleman|反义词：senhora (madam, lady)|名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
There are no common verb phrases directly involving "senhor".  It functions as a title.  Phrases using it would involve other verbs.  Examples:


O senhor está bem? = Are you alright, sir?
O senhor pode me ajudar? = Can you help me, sir?
Bom dia, senhor. = Good morning, sir.
Com licença, senhor. = Excuse me, sir.
Obrigado, senhor. = Thank you, sir.
