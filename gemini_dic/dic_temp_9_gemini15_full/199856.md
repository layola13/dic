sitters:/ˈsɪtərz/| n. |保姆；照看者；（尤指儿童的）临时照看者|People who look after children, especially temporarily.  It can also refer to those who sit for a portrait or photograph.|例句：We hired sitters for the children while we went out for the evening. 我们晚上出去的时候雇了保姆照看孩子们。

例句：The artist needed several sitters for his group portrait.  艺术家需要几个模特来为他画集体肖像。


近义词：babysitters, caregivers, guardians, models (in the context of posing for art)
反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms could relate to abandoning or neglecting children.


词性:名词
