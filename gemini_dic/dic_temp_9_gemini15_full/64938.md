estatly: /ˈɛsteɪtli/ | adj. |庄严的；雄伟的；高贵的 | stately means imposing and dignified in appearance or manner. It suggests grandeur and magnificence, often associated with buildings, people, or events. |例句：The stately home stood proudly on the hill.  庄严的宅邸自豪地矗立在山丘上。


The stately procession moved slowly through the streets.  庄严的队伍缓慢地穿过街道。 |近义词：grand, majestic, imposing, dignified |反义词：humble, modest, mean, insignificant |词性：形容词
