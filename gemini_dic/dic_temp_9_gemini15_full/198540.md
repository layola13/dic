shrouds:/ʃraʊdz/| n.|裹尸布；遮蔽物；掩盖物|Something that covers or obscures something.|The ship was shrouded in mist. 那艘船被雾笼罩着。|近义词：veil, cover, cloak|反义词：reveal, uncover


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shroud covers the body. = 裹尸布盖着尸体。
Thick fog shrouds the mountain. = 浓雾笼罩着山峰。


一般过去时:
A dark cloud shrouded the city. = 一团乌云笼罩着城市。
They shrouded the body in a white sheet. = 他们用白布裹尸。


现在完成时:
Mystery has shrouded the case for years. = 多年来，这个案件一直笼罩在神秘之中。
The truth has been shrouded in secrecy. = 真相一直被秘而不宣。


过去完成时:
The evidence had been shrouded in doubt. = 证据一直被怀疑。
The details of the accident had been shrouded in secrecy. = 事故的细节一直被保密。


将来时:
Darkness will shroud the land. = 黑暗将会笼罩大地。
The town will be shrouded in snow. = 小镇将被大雪覆盖。


虚拟语气:
If the truth were not shrouded in secrecy, we would know more. = 如果真相没有被秘而不宣，我们会知道更多。
I wish the matter weren't shrouded in mystery. = 我希望这件事不要笼罩在神秘之中。


被动语态:
The scene was shrouded in darkness. = 现场被黑暗笼罩。
The village is shrouded in mist. = 村庄被雾笼罩。


疑问句:
Is the truth shrouded in mystery? = 真相是否被神秘笼罩？
Is the mountain shrouded in fog? = 山是否被雾笼罩？


否定句:
The details were not shrouded in secrecy. = 详情没有被保密。
The future is not shrouded in uncertainty. = 未来并非充满不确定性。


PHRASE->
shroud of darkness = 黑暗的覆盖
shroud in mystery = 笼罩在神秘之中
shroud of secrecy = 秘密的掩盖
shroud of silence = 沉默的掩盖
be shrouded in = 被...所覆盖
