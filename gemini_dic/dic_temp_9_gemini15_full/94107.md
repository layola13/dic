impeaching: /ɪmˈpiːtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 检举；控告；弹劾 | Accusing someone, especially a public official, of wrongdoing.  The process often leads to a formal trial. |例句：The House of Representatives is impeaching the president.众议院正在弹劾总统。

The governor is being impeached for corruption. 州长因腐败而被弹劾。

近义词： accusing, indicting, prosecuting | 反义词： acquitting, exonerating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are impeaching the mayor for misuse of funds. 他们正在因为挪用公款而弹劾市长。
The committee is impeaching several officials. 委员会正在弹劾几位官员。

一般过去时:
The Senate impeached the judge last year. 参议院去年弹劾了那位法官。
They impeached the president for perjury. 他们因伪证罪弹劾了总统。

现在完成时:
The legislature has impeached three governors in the last decade. 立法机关在过去十年中弹劾了三任州长。
The country has never impeached a president before. 这个国家以前从未弹劾过总统。


过去完成时:
The evidence showed that they had impeached the official unjustly.  证据表明他们不公正地弹劾了这位官员。
Before the scandal broke, no one had ever considered impeaching him. 在丑闻爆发之前，没有人想过要弹劾他。


将来时:
The opposition party will likely impeach the prime minister next year. 反对党很可能会在明年弹劾首相。
They will impeach him if they find sufficient evidence. 如果他们找到足够的证据，他们将会弹劾他。


虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would impeach him immediately. 如果他们有更多证据，他们会立即弹劾他。
I wish they would impeach the corrupt officials. 我希望他们能弹劾那些腐败的官员。


被动语态:
The president was impeached by the House of Representatives. 总统被众议院弹劾。
Several officials are being impeached for bribery. 几位官员因受贿而正在被弹劾。


疑问句:
Is the president being impeached? 总统正在被弹劾吗？
Will they impeach him? 他们会弹劾他吗？


否定句:
They did not impeach the governor. 他们没有弹劾州长。
The court will not impeach the judge without sufficient evidence. 法院如果没有足够的证据就不会弹劾法官。


PHRASE->
impeach for treason  因叛国罪而弹劾
impeach on grounds of  以…为由弹劾
impeachment proceedings 弹劾程序
face impeachment 面临弹劾
grounds for impeachment 弹劾理由

词源:
From Old French empescher "to impede, hinder," from em- "in, on" + peschier "to catch, seize," from Latin pedica "fetter, snare."  The modern meaning developed through a shift in the sense of "hinder" to "accuse."
