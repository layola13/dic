craniology:/kreɪniˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |颅相学，颅骨学 | The scientific study of the skull, including its structure, development, and variations.  Craniology encompasses both the anatomical study of the skull and its relation to the brain, as well as its use in anthropological and forensic investigations. | Example:  The craniology of ancient Egyptian mummies revealed much about their lifestyle and health.  颅相学研究揭示了古埃及木乃伊的生活方式和健康状况。| 近义词:cephalometry, skull study | 反义词:  None directly applicable.
