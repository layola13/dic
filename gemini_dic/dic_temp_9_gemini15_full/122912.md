molters:/ˈmɒltərz/| n.|蜕皮者，换羽者|Those who molt or shed their skin or feathers.|The molters were busy shedding their old skin. 蜕皮者正忙着蜕去旧皮。|近义词：shedder, slougher|反义词:  None specifically antonymous, but could consider "those who retain"  (无确切的反义词，但可以考虑“那些保留的人”)


USAGE->

The bird molts its feathers every year. = 这只鸟每年换羽。
The snake molts its skin several times a year. = 蛇一年蜕皮几次。
