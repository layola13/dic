pitter-patters: /ˈpɪtəpætərz/ | v. | 细细的滴答声；轻快的脚步声 | The sound of light, quick steps or the gentle sound of rain | The rain pitter-pattered against the windowpanes. 雨点轻轻地敲打着窗玻璃。  The children's feet pitter-pattered down the hallway. 孩子们的脚步声轻快地回荡在走廊里。|近义词：drip, patter, tap |反义词：None

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain pitter-patters softly. = 雨轻轻地滴答作响。
The little girl's feet pitter-patter as she runs. = 小女孩跑起来，脚步声轻快地响着。

一般过去时:
The rain pitter-pattered all night. = 雨整夜淅淅沥沥地下着。
Her tiny shoes pitter-pattered on the pavement. =她的小鞋子在人行道上发出轻快的脚步声。

现在完成时:
The rain has pitter-pattered for hours. = 雨已经淅淅沥沥地下了好几个小时了。
The children have pitter-pattered through the house all morning. = 孩子们整个上午都在家里跑来跑去。

过去完成时:
The rain had pitter-pattered before we went inside. = 我们进屋之前，雨已经淅淅沥沥地下了一阵了。
The little dog had pitter-pattered up the stairs before I could stop him. =在我阻止他之前，小狗已经轻快地跑上楼了。

将来时:
The rain will pitter-patter throughout the night. = 雨将会整夜淅淅沥沥地下。
I expect her shoes will pitter-patter down the stairs soon. = 我预计她很快就会穿着鞋子轻快地跑下楼。

虚拟语气:
If it pitter-pattered all night, we would have stayed inside. = 如果雨整夜淅淅沥沥地下，我们就待在屋里了。
I wish the rain would pitter-patter softly instead of pouring down. = 我希望雨能轻轻地滴答而不是倾盆而下。

被动语态: (Pitter-patter is rarely used in the passive voice.)  该词很少用被动语态。


疑问句:
Is the rain pitter-pattering against the window? = 雨是否正在轻轻地敲打着窗户？
Does the sound of little feet pitter-pattering annoy you? = 轻快的脚步声是否让你感到厌烦？

否定句:
The rain wasn't pitter-pattering; it was pouring. = 雨不是淅淅沥沥地下，而是倾盆而下。
The children's feet didn't pitter-patter; they stomped loudly. = 孩子们的脚步声不是轻快的，而是大声地跺着脚。


PHRASE->
Pitter-patter of rain: 雨的细细滴答声
Pitter-patter of feet: 轻快的脚步声
Pitter-patter of tiny hands: 小手轻轻拍打的声音
Pitter-patter of little paws: 小爪子轻轻拍打的声音
The pitter-patter of life: 生活中细微美好的事物
