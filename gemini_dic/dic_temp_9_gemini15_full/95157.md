inarticulate: /ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪt/ | adj. |  表达不清的；口齿不清的；表达能力差的 | Unable to speak clearly or express oneself clearly; lacking the power of articulate speech.  |  His inarticulate rage was frightening. 他表达不清的愤怒令人恐惧。  The baby's inarticulate cries alerted the mother. 婴儿含糊不清的哭声惊动了母亲。|articulate, fluent, eloquent | articulate, fluent, clear |

词源:
