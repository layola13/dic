nonentrants:/ˌnɒnˈɛntrənts/| n.|未进入者，未入选者|Those who did not enter a competition or a place.  People or things that are excluded.|例句：The nonentrants were disappointed by the results. 未入选者对结果感到失望。

例句：The number of nonentrants in the marathon was surprisingly high. 马拉松比赛中未参赛的人数出奇地高。


近义词：outsiders, excluded, ineligible


反义词：entrants, participants, contestants


词性：名词
