disenshrouded: /ˌdɪsɛnˈʃraʊdɪd/ | adj. |  揭露的；显露的 |  Revealed; uncovered; made visible or known.  The word implies a previous state of concealment, often mysterious or secretive. |


例句：The truth, finally disenshrouded, was shocking. | 真相最终被揭露，令人震惊。

The disenshrouded evidence proved his innocence. | 揭露的证据证明了他的清白。


近义词：revealed, uncovered, exposed, unveiled, disclosed


反义词：concealed, hidden, obscured, masked, shrouded


词性：形容词


词源：

* **dis-**:  prefix indicating removal or reversal (similar to "un-")
* **en-**: prefix indicating to cause to be in a certain state
* **shroud**: noun meaning a burial cloth; hence, something that covers or conceals.  The word ultimately derives from Old English *scrūd*, meaning "garment, clothing".
