disclaimed:/dɪsˈkleɪmd/| vt.|声明放弃；否认；拒绝承认|To state that something is not true or that you are not responsible for something.  It involves a formal or explicit renunciation of a claim, right, or responsibility.|例句：The company disclaimed all responsibility for the accident. 公司否认对事故负有任何责任。
例句：He disclaimed any knowledge of the affair. 他否认了解此事。|近义词：deny, repudiate, renounce|反义词：affirm, admit, acknowledge|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disclaims responsibility for the project's failure. 他否认对项目失败负责。
The company disclaims any warranty on the product. 公司声明对该产品不作任何保修。


一般过去时:
She disclaimed any involvement in the crime. 她否认参与了犯罪活动。
The witness disclaimed knowledge of the suspect's whereabouts. 证人否认知道嫌疑人的下落。


现在完成时:
They have disclaimed all liability for the damages. 他们已经放弃了对损失的所有责任。
The government has disclaimed responsibility for the economic crisis. 政府已经否认对经济危机负责。


过去完成时:
He had disclaimed his inheritance before his father's death. 他在他父亲去世前就放弃了继承权。
The lawyer had disclaimed any conflict of interest before taking the case. 律师在接手此案前已声明不存在任何利益冲突。


将来时:
She will disclaim any knowledge of the secret meeting. 她将否认知道秘密会议的事情。
The company will disclaim all responsibility for the defective products. 公司将否认对有缺陷产品的任何责任。


虚拟语气:
If he disclaimed responsibility, the consequences would be severe. 如果他否认责任，后果将不堪设想。
I wish he had disclaimed his involvement earlier. 我希望他早点否认参与其中。


被动语态:
Responsibility for the accident was disclaimed by the company. 公司否认对事故负责。
All liability was disclaimed by the manufacturer. 制造商否认所有责任。


疑问句:
Did the defendant disclaim any knowledge of the crime? 被告是否否认对犯罪有任何了解？

否定句:
The company did not disclaim responsibility for the product defect. 公司没有否认对产品缺陷的责任。
He wouldn't disclaim his love for his family. 他不会否认他对家人的爱。
