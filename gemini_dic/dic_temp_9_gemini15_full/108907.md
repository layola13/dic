ledges:/ˈlɛdʒɪz/| n.|岩架；壁架；窗台|A narrow horizontal shelf, especially of rock.  Ledges are often found on cliffs, mountains, or in caves. They can also refer to similar structures in buildings, such as window ledges.|例句：The climber rested on a narrow ledge halfway up the cliff. 登山者在悬崖半腰狭窄的岩架上休息。

The window ledge was filled with potted plants. 窗台上摆满了盆栽。|近义词：shelf, projection, recess|反义词：深渊，深谷
