misfiled:/mɪsˈfaɪld/ | v. | 归档错误；错放；误存 | To file something in the wrong place.  |  例句：I misfiled the important documents, causing a delay in the project. 我把重要文件归档错误，导致项目延误。

例句：The clerk misfiled the application, resulting in its rejection.  职员把申请放错了地方，导致申请被拒绝。


近义词：misplace, misindex,  erroneously filed

反义词：correctly filed, properly filed


词性：动词
