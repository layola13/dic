semi-nude:/ˈsɛmiˌnuːd/| adj.|半裸的，赤裸的|Partially naked; clothed only partially.  The term often implies a degree of intentional exposure, often for artistic or provocative purposes.  It's less extreme than "nude" but more revealing than "partially clothed".|例句：The statue was semi-nude, showing a significant portion of the figure. 这尊雕像半裸着，露出了大部分的身体。

The model posed semi-nude for the photographer. 模特为摄影师摆出半裸的姿势。|近义词：partially naked, scantily clad, topless (for women)|反义词：fully clothed, dressed, covered
