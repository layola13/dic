overcomes:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmz/| vt.|克服，战胜|To succeed in dealing with or controlling (a problem or difficulty).|例句：She overcame her fear of public speaking. 她克服了她对公开演讲的恐惧。|例句：The team overcame many obstacles to win the championship.  这支队伍克服了许多障碍赢得了冠军。|近义词：conquer, defeat, master, surmount|反义词：succumb, yield, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overcomes challenges with determination. = 他以决心克服挑战。
She overcomes her shyness gradually. = 她逐渐克服了她的害羞。

一般过去时:
He overcame his fear of heights. = 他克服了他对高处的恐惧。

现在完成时:

He has overcome his addiction to smoking. = 他已经克服了他对吸烟的瘾。

过去完成时:
