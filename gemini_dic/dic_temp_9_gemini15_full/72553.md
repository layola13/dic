flutter:/ˈflʌtər/|/ˈflʌtə/| n. vi. vt.|飘动；颤动；激动；慌乱|to move lightly and quickly; to wave or flap; to feel nervous or excited|The butterfly fluttered its wings.  蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。

The leaves fluttered in the breeze. 树叶在微风中飘动。


近义词：wave, flap, tremble, quiver, palpitate
反义词：remain still, be steady


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flutters its wings.  鸟儿拍打着翅膀。
The flag flutters in the wind. 旗帜在风中飘扬。


一般过去时:
The butterfly fluttered gracefully. 蝴蝶优雅地飞舞。
My heart fluttered when I saw him. 当我看到他时，我的心跳加速了。


现在完成时:
The leaves have fluttered down from the tree. 叶子已经从树上飘落下来。
She has fluttered around all day, unable to focus. 她整天都心神不宁，无法集中注意力。


过去完成时:
The bird had fluttered away before I could take a picture.  在我拍照之前，鸟儿已经飞走了。
She had fluttered nervously before the interview. 面试前她紧张地颤抖着。


将来时:
The flag will flutter proudly in the wind. 旗帜将在风中骄傲地飘扬。
I think my heart will flutter when I see her again. 我想当我再次见到她时，我的心跳会加速。


虚拟语气:
If the butterfly fluttered closer, I could take a better picture. 如果蝴蝶飞得更近一些，我就能拍张更好的照片。
If I hadn't fluttered so much, I might have performed better. 如果我不那么紧张，我可能会表现得更好。


被动语态:
The flag was fluttered by the strong wind. 旗帜被强风吹得飘扬。
Her feelings were fluttered by his words. 他的话让她心动不已。


疑问句:
Does the flag flutter in the wind? 旗帜在风中飘扬吗？
Does your heart flutter when you think of her? 想起她时，你的心跳会加速吗？


否定句:
The flag didn't flutter in the still air. 旗帜在静止的空气中没有飘动。
My heart didn't flutter, I was quite calm. 我的心跳没有加速，我很平静。
