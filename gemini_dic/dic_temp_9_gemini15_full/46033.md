cyborgs:/ˈsaɪbɔːrz/| n.|半机械人，人机结合体|A cyborg is a being with both organic and artificial (typically technological) parts.  The term often refers to a human being who has enhanced their physical or mental capabilities through the integration of technology.  It can also encompass other organisms with mechanical enhancements.|例句：The science fiction movie featured a cyborg with advanced weaponry. 这部科幻电影中出现了一个拥有先进武器的半机械人。

例句：Some scientists believe that cyborgs will become more common in the future. 一些科学家认为，未来半机械人会越来越普遍。


近义词：bionic human, bio-robot, human-machine hybrid


反义词：natural human, purely organic being

词性:名词
