numbed:/ˈnʌmbd/ | adj. | 麻木的；失去感觉的 |  Describes a state where sensation, feeling, or responsiveness is significantly reduced or absent, often due to injury, cold, or emotional shock.  | Example:  My fingers were numbed from the cold. 我的手指冻僵了。

My emotions were numbed after hearing the terrible news.  听到这个可怕的消息后，我的情绪麻木了。|近义词：deadened, benumbed, insensible |反义词：sensitive, feeling, alert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cold numbs my fingers. = 冷空气使我的手指麻木。
This medicine numbs the pain. = 这药能止痛。

一般过去时:
The shock numbed her for a moment. = 震惊使她短暂地麻木了。
The anesthetic numbed his leg before surgery. = 在手术前，麻醉剂使他的腿麻木了。

现在完成时:
The pain has numbed my leg. = 疼痛使我的腿麻木了。
My feelings have been numbed by repeated disappointments. = 反复的失望使我的感情麻木了。

过去完成时:
His hand had been numbed by the cold before he reached the shelter. = 他到达避难所之前，他的手已经被冻僵了。
The trauma had numbed her to the realities of the situation. = 创伤使她对事态的真相麻木不仁。

将来时:
The cold will numb my toes if I don't wear warmer socks. = 如果我不穿暖和的袜子，寒冷会使我的脚趾麻木。
This injection will numb the area before the procedure. = 注射会在手术前使该区域麻木。

虚拟语气:
If I had felt the pain, I wouldn't have been so numbed. = 如果我感觉到疼痛，我就不会这么麻木了。
I wish I weren't so emotionally numbed. = 我希望我不至于如此情感麻木。

被动语态:
His arm was numbed by a nerve injury. = 他的手臂因神经损伤而麻木。
The area will be numbed before the injection. = 注射前该区域会被麻醉。

疑问句:
Is your leg numbed? = 你的腿麻木了吗？
Are you numbed by the cold? = 你被冻麻木了吗？

否定句:
My fingers weren't numbed. = 我的手指没有麻木。
I wasn't numbed by the news. = 我并没有被这个消息麻木。
