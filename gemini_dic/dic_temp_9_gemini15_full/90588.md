hullabaloo:/ˌhʌləˈbəluː/ | n. | 喧嚣；吵闹；混乱 | A hullabaloo is a commotion or a fuss, a noisy and usually confused situation. It often implies a lot of excited or angry talk.  | There was a terrible hullabaloo when the fire alarm went off.  =  火警响起时，一片混乱。 The announcement caused quite a hullabaloo. =  这个公告引起了很大的骚动。 | commotion, uproar, fuss, to-do | quiet, peace, calm | 名词
