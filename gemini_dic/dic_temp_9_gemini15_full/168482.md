prober:/ˈproʊbər/| n. v.|探测器；探测者；调查者；探针；仔细检查者|A prober is a person or thing that probes.  It can refer to a physical device used to explore something, or a person who investigates or examines something thoroughly.|例句：The Mars rover is a sophisticated prober, sending back valuable data about the planet.  火星探测器是一个复杂的探测器，向地球发送关于这颗行星的宝贵数据。|例句：The journalist was a tireless prober, determined to uncover the truth.  这位记者是一位不知疲倦的调查者，决心揭露真相。|近义词：investigator, explorer, examiner, inquisitor|反义词：ignorer, neglecter|名词，动词
