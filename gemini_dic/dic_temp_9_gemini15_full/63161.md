ensures:/ɪnˈʃʊərz/| vt.|确保；保证|To make certain that (something) will happen or be the case.|The new security system ensures the safety of the building. 新的安全系统确保了大楼的安全。|guarantees, assures, secures|risks, jeopardizes, endangers|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ensures the project stays on schedule. 他确保项目按计划进行。
This policy ensures fair treatment for all employees.这项政策确保所有员工得到公平对待。

一般过去时:
She ensured that everything was ready before the guests arrived.  她在客人到达之前确保一切准备就绪。
The manager ensured the deadline was met. 经理确保了截止日期的完成。

现在完成时:
The company has ensured its continued success through innovation. 公司通过创新确保了持续的成功。
We have ensured that all necessary documents are submitted. 我们已经确保提交了所有必要的文档。

过去完成时:
He had ensured that the contract was signed before leaving for vacation. 他在休假前已确保合同已签署。
They had ensured the safety of the children before the evacuation. 他们在疏散前确保了孩子们的安全。

将来时:
The government will ensure that the necessary funds are available. 政府将确保有必要的资金。
We will ensure that your request is processed promptly. 我们将确保您的请求得到迅速处理。

虚拟语气:
If we had ensured better communication, the problem would have been avoided. 如果我们确保了更好的沟通，问题本来是可以避免的。
I wish I had ensured my work was backed up before the crash. 我真希望在电脑崩溃前我已确保我的工作备份了。

被动语态:
The safety of the passengers is ensured by strict regulations. 严格的法规确保了乘客的安全。
The project's success was ensured by the team's hard work. 项目的成功是由团队的辛勤工作所确保的。

疑问句:
How can we ensure the quality of the products? 我们如何才能确保产品的质量？
Does this system ensure data security? 这个系统能确保数据安全吗？

否定句:
The lack of funding ensures the project's failure. 资金不足导致项目失败。
He failed to ensure the accuracy of the report. 他未能确保报告的准确性。
