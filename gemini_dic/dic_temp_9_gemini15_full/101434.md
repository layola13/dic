iraqization:/ˌɪrækəˈzeɪʃən/| n. |伊拉克化；使伊拉克化|The process of making something Iraqi in character or style, especially the transfer of power and authority to Iraqi government and security forces.  This term is often used in the context of the post-2003 Iraq War, referring to the efforts by the U.S.-led coalition to transfer control of Iraq to Iraqi authorities.|例句：The iraqization of security responsibilities was a key part of the U.S. strategy for withdrawal from Iraq.  伊拉克安全责任的伊拉克化是美国从伊拉克撤兵战略的关键部分。|近义词：localization, indigenization|反义词：foreignization, westernization
