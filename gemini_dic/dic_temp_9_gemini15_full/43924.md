crewed:/kruːd/| adj.|载人的，有船员的|Having a crew; manned.|The crewed spacecraft landed safely.  载人航天器安全着陆了。 The ship was crewed by experienced sailors. 这艘船由经验丰富的船员操作。|近义词：manned, staffed|反义词：uncrewed, unmanned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is crewed by ten sailors. = 这艘船由十名水手操作。
The plane is crewed by a highly skilled pilot and co-pilot. = 这架飞机由一名技术娴熟的飞行员和一名副驾驶操作。


一般过去时:
The submarine was crewed by a team of experts. = 这艘潜艇由一个专家团队操作。
The bus was crewed by a friendly and helpful driver.  = 这辆公共汽车由一位友好而乐于助人的司机驾驶。


现在完成时:
The vessel has been crewed by the same team for five years. = 这艘船五年来一直由同一个团队操作。
This project has been crewed by a dedicated team of volunteers. = 这个项目一直由一群敬业的志愿者团队操作。


过去完成时:
The rocket had been crewed by astronauts from three different countries. = 这枚火箭曾由来自三个不同国家的宇航员操作。
The train had been crewed by the same conductor for over a decade. = 这列火车十多年来一直由同一位列车员驾驶。


将来时:
The new spacecraft will be crewed by a multinational team. = 新的宇宙飞船将由一个多国团队操作。
The expedition will be crewed by experienced climbers and guides. = 探险队将由经验丰富的登山者和向导组成。


虚拟语气:
If the mission were crewed by a more experienced team, it might have succeeded. = 如果任务由更有经验的团队操作，它也许会成功。
I wish the flight had been crewed by someone more attentive to safety. = 我希望这次航班是由更关注安全的人员操作的。


被动语态:
The aircraft was crewed effectively. = 飞机得到了有效的操作。
The lifeboat was crewed by brave volunteers. = 救生艇由勇敢的志愿者操作。


疑问句:
Is the helicopter crewed by a qualified pilot? = 直升机是由合格的飞行员操作的吗？
Was the space shuttle crewed by trained professionals? = 航天飞机是由训练有素的专业人员操作的吗？


否定句:
The mission was not crewed by enough personnel. = 这项任务没有足够的人员操作。
The vehicle was not crewed properly for such a dangerous journey. = 这辆车没有为如此危险的旅程进行适当的操作。
