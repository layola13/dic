hypotheticals:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəlz/| n.|假设的情况，假设性问题|Things that are hypothetical are based on assumptions or theories rather than facts.  They are imagined possibilities. |例句：The professor spent the lecture discussing various hypotheticals. 教授在讲座上花了时间讨论各种假设的情况。

例句：We need to consider all possible hypotheticals before making a decision. 在做决定之前，我们需要考虑所有可能的假设情况。


近义词：suppositions, speculations, possibilities, theories


反义词：realities, facts, certainties


词性: noun
