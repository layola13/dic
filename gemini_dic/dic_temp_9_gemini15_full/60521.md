elaborator: /ɪˈlæbəreɪtər/ | n. |阐述者；详细说明者；详述器 | A person or thing that elaborates or explains something in detail.  It can also refer to a device or system that adds detail or complexity to something. |例句：The professor served as an elaborator, clarifying complex theories for his students. 教授充当了解释者，为学生阐明复杂的理论。

例句：The software acts as an elaborator, adding intricate details to the basic design.  该软件充当详述器，在基本设计中添加复杂细节。


近义词：explainer, interpreter, elucidator, detailer |反义词：simplifier, summarizer
