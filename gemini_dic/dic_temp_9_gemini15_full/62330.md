ended:/ˈɛndɪd/ | v. | 结束，终止 | To bring or come to an end; to conclude.  | The meeting ended at 5 pm.  会议在下午5点结束。 |The rain finally ended and the sun came out. 雨终于停了，太阳出来了。| finish, conclude, terminate | begin, start, commence | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He ends his work early every day. 他每天都早早结束工作。
She ends her sentences with a flourish. 她以华丽的结尾结束她的句子。


一般过去时:
The game ended in a draw.  比赛以平局告终。
The movie ended abruptly. 电影突然结束了。


现在完成时:
The project has ended successfully. 项目已经成功结束。
The meeting has ended, and everyone is leaving. 会议结束了，每个人都离开了。


过去完成时:
The show had ended before we arrived. 我们到达之前演出已经结束了。
They had ended their argument before the boss arrived.  老板来之前他们已经结束了争吵。


将来时:
The concert will end at 10 pm. 音乐会将在晚上10点结束。
The meeting will end shortly. 会议很快就会结束。


虚拟语气:
If the war had ended sooner, many lives would have been saved. 如果战争早点结束，许多生命就能得救。
I wish the rain would end soon. 我希望雨能尽快停下来。


被动语态:
The meeting was ended early due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，会议提前结束了。
The game was ended prematurely because of the bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，比赛提前结束了。


疑问句:
Has the meeting ended yet? 会议结束了吗？
When will the show end? 演出什么时候结束？


否定句:
The party didn't end until late. 派对直到很晚才结束。
The rain hasn't ended yet. 雨还没停呢。
