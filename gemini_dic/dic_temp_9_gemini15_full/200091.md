ski-lift: /ˈskiːlɪft/ | n. |滑雪缆车| A ski lift is a type of conveyance used at ski resorts to transport skiers and snowboarders uphill, typically using a cable and chairs, or a similar system.  They are essential for efficient uphill travel, allowing skiers to repeatedly access slopes without expending significant energy. | Example: The ski lift was closed due to high winds.  滑雪缆车由于强风关闭了。  The children were excited to ride the ski lift to the top of the mountain. 孩子们很兴奋地乘坐缆车到达山顶。 | 近义词：chairlift, cable car, funicular | 反义词：N/A
