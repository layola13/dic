re-edits:/ˌriːˈɛdɪts/ | n. |重新编辑，再次编辑 |The process of editing something a second time, often to improve it or correct errors.  It often implies a more thorough or extensive editing process than the initial edit. |例句：The author spent weeks on the re-edits, refining the prose and tightening the plot. 作者花了几个星期时间重新编辑，润色了散文，并收紧了情节。

例句：After the initial edits, the editor requested further re-edits to address inconsistencies. 初次编辑后，编辑要求进一步重新编辑以解决不一致之处。|近义词：revisions, amendments, corrections |反义词：original draft, first draft |名词
