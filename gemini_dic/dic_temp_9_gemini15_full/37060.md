combatant:/kəmˈbætənt/| n. |战斗人员，交战者 |A person who fights in a battle or war.  A combatant is anyone involved in a fight or conflict, whether they are a soldier, a civilian, or a member of a militia. |例句：The combatants agreed to a ceasefire.  交战双方同意停火。|例句：Both combatants suffered heavy losses. 双方战斗人员都遭受了重大损失。|近义词：fighter, warrior, soldier, belligerent |反义词：civilian, non-combatant
