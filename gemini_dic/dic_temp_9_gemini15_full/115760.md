marinading:/ˌmærɪˈneɪdɪŋ/| v.|腌制；浸渍|The process of soaking food, typically meat or fish, in a seasoned liquid to tenderize and flavor it.  Marinading involves submerging the food in a marinade, usually for several hours or overnight. |例句：I'm marinading the chicken for tonight's dinner. 我正在腌制今晚晚餐的鸡肉。

例句：The steak was incredibly tender after marinading it in red wine for 12 hours.  腌制12小时的牛排非常嫩。


近义词：soaking, pickling, brining| 反义词：roasting, grilling
