circumnavigating:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.|环航，环绕航行|The act of sailing or otherwise traveling completely around something, typically a large body of water such as the Earth.|例句：Magellan was the first person to circumnavigate the globe. 麦哲伦是第一个环球航行的人。

例句：The yacht is currently circumnavigating the island. 这艘游艇目前正在环绕岛屿航行。

近义词：encircling, going around, sailing around

反义词：crossing (directly), traversing (directly)

词性：动词


USAGE->
