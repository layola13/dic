pretend-to:/prɪˈtɛnd tuː/| v. |假装，装作|To behave as if something is true when it is not, often to deceive someone.|例句：He pretended to be asleep. 他假装睡着了。|例句：She pretended to know the answer. 她假装知道答案。|近义词：feign, affect, simulate|反义词：be honest, be truthful|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pretends to be a doctor. = 他假装是医生。
They pretend to understand. = 他们假装理解。


一般过去时:
She pretended to be happy. = 她假装很开心。
He pretended not to see me. = 他假装没看见我。


现在完成时:
They have pretended to be busy all day. = 他们一整天都假装很忙。
She has pretended to forget our agreement. = 她假装忘记了我们的约定。


过去完成时:
He had pretended to be ill to avoid the meeting. = 他曾假装生病以避免参加会议。
They had pretended to be friends for years. = 他们假装是朋友很多年了。


将来时:
She will pretend to be surprised. = 她将会假装很惊讶。
They will pretend they didn't hear anything. = 他们会假装什么都没听到。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pretend to be busy. = 如果我是你，我会假装很忙。
I wish I could pretend to know the answer. = 我真希望我能假装知道答案。

否定句:
