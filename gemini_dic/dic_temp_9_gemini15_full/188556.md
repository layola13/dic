ruinate:/ˈruːɪneɪt/| vt.|毁坏；使破产；糟蹋 | to destroy or severely damage something | The storm ruined the crops.  暴风雨毁坏了庄稼。|His gambling ruined him financially. 他的赌博使他经济破产。|近义词：destroy, spoil, wreck |反义词：improve, enhance, restore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ruins his health by smoking. = 他抽烟毁了他的健康。
The rain ruins the picnic. =  雨毁了野餐。


一般过去时:
The earthquake ruined the city. = 地震毁了这座城市。
The bad weather ruined our holiday plans. = 糟糕的天气毁了我们的假期计划。


现在完成时:
The scandal has ruined his reputation. = 这场丑闻毁了他的名声。
Years of neglect have ruined the building. = 多年来的忽视毁了这座建筑物。


过去完成时:
He had ruined his chances of success by his carelessness. = 他因粗心大意而毁掉了成功的机会。
The company had been ruined before the new CEO took over. = 在新任CEO上任之前，公司就已经破产了。


将来时:
The new policy will ruin the small businesses. = 新政策将毁掉小型企业。
If he continues to drink, he will ruin his liver. = 如果他继续酗酒，他会毁了他的肝脏。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have ruined his future. = 如果他学习更努力，他就不会毁了他的未来。
I wish I hadn't ruined the surprise party. = 我真希望我没有毁掉这次惊喜派对。


被动语态:
The town was ruined by the flood. = 这个小镇被洪水毁了。
His career was ruined by a single mistake. = 他的事业因一个错误而毁了。


疑问句:
What ruined his marriage? = 什么毁了他的婚姻？
Will this decision ruin the company? = 这个决定会毁掉公司吗？


否定句:
The experience didn't ruin her. = 那次经历并没有毁了她。
The rain didn't ruin the party; we moved inside. = 雨并没有毁掉派对；我们搬到了室内。



PHRASE->
ruin one's life  毁掉某人的一生
ruin one's reputation 毁坏某人的名誉
ruin one's health 毁坏某人的健康
ruin the environment 破坏环境
be ruined 破产；毁灭
