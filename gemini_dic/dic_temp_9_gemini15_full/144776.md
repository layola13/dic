over-enthusiastically: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/ | adv. | 过分热情地，过于热心地 |  Describes an action or behavior performed with excessive enthusiasm, often to the point of being annoying or inappropriate.  | 例句：He over-enthusiastically volunteered for every task, leaving little work for others. 他过分热情地自告奋勇承担每一项任务，导致其他人几乎没有工作可做。


He over-enthusiastically embraced the new project, neglecting important details in his haste. 他过于热情地投入新项目，在匆忙中忽略了重要的细节。


近义词：excessively enthusiastically, zealously, fervently, passionately (but to an excessive degree) | 反义词：apathetically, unenthusiastically, indifferently, apathetically


USAGE->  (由于副词没有时态变化，此处仅提供例句)

He responded over-enthusiastically to the good news. 他对这个好消息反应过度热情。

She over-enthusiastically decorated the cake, making it look cluttered. 她过于热情地装饰蛋糕，使它看起来杂乱无章。
