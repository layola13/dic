non-destructive: /ˌnɒn dəˈstrʌktɪv/ | adj. | 无损的，非破坏性的 | Not causing damage or destruction.  This adjective describes a process, test, or method that does not damage the object being examined or tested. |


例句：

Non-destructive testing is crucial in ensuring the structural integrity of bridges.  = 无损检测对于确保桥梁的结构完整性至关重要。

We used non-destructive methods to analyze the ancient artifact. = 我们使用无损方法来分析古代文物。


近义词：harmless, safe, undamaged

反义词：destructive, damaging, harmful


词性：形容词
