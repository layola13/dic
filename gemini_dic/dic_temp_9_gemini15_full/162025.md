prayers:/ˈpreərz/| n.|祈祷；祷告|The act of praying; a solemn request for help or expression of thanks addressed to God or another deity.  Prayers are often said individually or in groups.|例句：She offered prayers for her sick mother. 她为她生病的母亲祈祷。|例句：The church was filled with prayers for peace. 教堂里充满了祈求和平的祈祷。|近义词：supplications, invocations, petitions|反义词：curses, imprecations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They offer prayers every Sunday. = 他们每个星期天都祈祷。
He prays before meals. = 他饭前祈祷。

一般过去时:
She prayed for guidance. = 她祈祷得到指引。
We prayed for rain. = 我们祈祷下雨。

现在完成时:
I have prayed for this all my life. = 我为此祈祷了一辈子。
They have prayed together many times. = 他们一起祈祷过很多次。

过去完成时:
He had prayed for forgiveness before confessing. = 他在忏悔前祈祷过得到原谅。
She had prayed for a miracle. = 她曾祈祷过奇迹出现。

将来时:
We will pray for your success. = 我们会为你的成功祈祷。
I will pray for her recovery. = 我会祈祷她康复。

虚拟语气:
I would pray for peace if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会祈祷和平。
If I had prayed harder, maybe things would be different. = 如果我祈祷得更努力，也许事情会不一样。

被动语态:
Prayers were said for the victims. = 为受害者进行了祈祷。
Prayers are often heard by God. = 祈祷常常被上帝听到。(This is a figurative use of passive voice)

疑问句:
Do you say your prayers every night? = 你每晚都祈祷吗？
What prayers were offered at the service? = 仪式上做了哪些祈祷？

否定句:
He didn't pray that night. = 他那天晚上没有祈祷。
They don't pray regularly. = 他们不经常祈祷。
