kindlily:/ˈkaɪndli/ | adv. | 亲切地；和蔼地；仁慈地 |Kindly means in a kind, gentle, or friendly way.  It is used to describe actions or requests made in a pleasant and considerate manner. |例句：She kindly offered me a cup of tea. 她亲切地给我倒了一杯茶。

例句：Would you kindly help me with this? 你能帮我一下吗？（委婉请求）


近义词：courteously, graciously, gently, politely

反义词：rudely, harshly, unkindly,cruelly

词性: 副词
