florettes:/flɒˈrɛts/ | n. |小花，小朵；(卷心菜等的)小叶 |A floret is a small flower, especially one of the many small flowers that together make up a larger flower head, such as in a daisy or sunflower.  It can also refer to a small leaf-like part of a plant, such as in a head of cauliflower.|例句：The sunflower's head is composed of hundreds of tiny florets.  向日葵的花头由数百朵小花组成。

The cauliflower florets were perfectly crisp after roasting. 烤制后的花椰菜小朵非常脆。|近义词：small flower, floret, blossom |反义词：None specifically applicable.
