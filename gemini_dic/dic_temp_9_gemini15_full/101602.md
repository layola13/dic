irrationals:/ɪˈræʃənəlz/| n. |无理数；非理性的人|Irrationals are numbers that cannot be expressed as a ratio of two integers.  They have decimal representations that neither terminate nor repeat.  The set of irrational numbers includes numbers like π (pi) and the square root of 2 (√2).|Example:  The number π is a famous example of an irrational.  π是一个著名的无理数的例子。 The set of irrational numbers is infinite. 无理数集合是无限的。|近义词: transcendental numbers (for some irrationals)|反义词: rationals|名词
