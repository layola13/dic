一般过去时:

现在完成时:

There have been many oppositions to the new trade agreement.  对新的贸易协定有很多反对意见。


过去完成时:

将来时:

We anticipate strong oppositions to the budget cuts next year.  我们预计明年对预算削减会有强烈反对。


虚拟语气:
If there were no oppositions, the project would proceed smoothly.  如果没有反对，项目将会顺利进行。
I wish there were fewer oppositions to positive change.  我希望对积极变化的反对少一些。


被动语态:

His ideas were constantly met with oppositions from his colleagues.  他的想法经常遭到同事们的反对。


疑问句:

否定句:
