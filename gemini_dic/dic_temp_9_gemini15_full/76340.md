gabs:/ɡæbz/ | n. |喋喋不休；闲聊 |  A continuous flow of talk, especially idle chatter.  |例句：He was full of gabs about his trip. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的旅行。|近义词：chatter, prattle, babble |反义词：silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gabs incessantly. = 他喋喋不休。
She gabs on the phone for hours. = 她在电话里聊上几个小时。

一般过去时:
They gabbed about their weekend plans. = 他们闲聊着他们的周末计划。
He gabbed continuously throughout the meeting. = 他在整个会议期间都在不停地说话。

现在完成时:
She has gabbed with her friends all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在和朋友们聊天。
He has gabbed so much that he's hoarse. = 他说得太多，嗓子都哑了。

过去完成时:
They had gabbed for hours before the movie started. = 电影开始前他们已经聊了几个小时。
She had gabbed with her sister about her problems. = 她和她姐姐聊了她遇到的问题。

将来时:
We will gab about this later. = 我们以后再聊这个。
They will gab about everything under the sun. = 他们会谈论天南地北的事情。

虚拟语气:
If he gabbed less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish she wouldn't gab so much during class. = 我希望她在课堂上不要说得太多。

被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in the passive voice.)

疑问句:
Does he often gab? = 他经常喋喋不休吗？

否定句:

She doesn't usually gab about her personal life. = 她通常不会谈论她的私生活。
