exploitations:/ˌɛksploɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|剥削；开发；利用；功绩|The act of using something or someone selfishly for one's own gain; the act of developing or utilizing a resource; notable achievements or feats.|例句：The exploitation of workers in sweatshops is a serious human rights issue. 血汗工厂对工人的剥削是严重的人权问题。

例句：The company's exploitation of the new oil field led to significant profits.  这家公司对新油田的开发带来了可观的利润。|近义词：abuse, exploitation, use, development|反义词：protection, conservation, fairness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company exploits its workers. = 这家公司剥削它的工人。
He exploits every opportunity. = 他利用每一个机会。


一般过去时:
They exploited the resources of the land. = 他们开发了这片土地的资源。
She exploited a loophole in the law. = 她利用了法律中的漏洞。


现在完成时:
They have exploited the situation to their advantage. = 他们已经利用了这种情况来为自己谋利。
We have exploited all available resources. = 我们已经利用了所有可用的资源。


过去完成时:
They had exploited the workers for years before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经剥削了工人多年。
The company had exploited the market before its competitors. = 在竞争对手之前，该公司已经开发了市场。


将来时:
They will exploit the new technology to improve efficiency. = 他们将利用这项新技术来提高效率。
The company will exploit the mineral resources of the region. = 公司将开发该地区的矿产资源。


虚拟语气:
If they exploited the market more aggressively, they would have made more profit. = 如果他们更积极地开拓市场，他们就会赚到更多利润。
I wish they hadn't exploited the workers so ruthlessly. = 我希望他们没有如此残酷地剥削工人。


被动语态:
The workers were exploited by the company. = 工人们被公司剥削了。
The natural resources are being exploited at an alarming rate. = 自然资源正以惊人的速度被开发。


疑问句:
Are they exploiting the workers? = 他们正在剥削工人吗？
How are they exploiting the situation? = 他们如何利用这种情况？


否定句:
They do not exploit their employees. = 他们不剥削他们的员工。
The company did not exploit the market opportunity. = 公司没有利用市场机遇。
