internucleon: /ˌɪntərˈnuːkliːɒn/ | n. |核子间的，核子间的力| Relating to the forces or interactions between nucleons (protons and neutrons) within an atomic nucleus.  The study of internucleon forces is crucial to understanding nuclear structure and stability. | Example:  The strong internucleon force is responsible for holding the nucleus together.  核子间的强力负责维系原子核的结合。|近义词：nuclear, nucleon-nucleon |反义词：interatomic (for forces between atoms), extranuclear (outside the nucleus)
