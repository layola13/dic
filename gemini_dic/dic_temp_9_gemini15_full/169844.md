proselytism:/ˈprɒsəˌlɪtɪzəm/| n. | 宗教宣传；拉拢入教；劝诱改宗 | The act or practice of proselytizing; the attempt to convert someone to a different religion or belief.  | Example:  The aggressive proselytism of the religious group caused tension within the community.  宗教团体的积极传教活动在社区内造成了紧张气氛。  |近义词：conversion, evangelization, missionary work |反义词：secularism, atheism, indifference
