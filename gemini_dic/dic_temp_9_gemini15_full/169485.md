propagandize:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændaɪz/| vt.|宣传；鼓吹|To spread propaganda; to promote a cause or idea by means of propaganda.|例句：The government propagandized the war effort to boost public support.  政府大力宣传战争努力以争取公众支持。|近义词：promote, advocate, publicize|反义词：suppress, silence
