middle-American:/ˌmɪdl əˈmɛrɪkən/| adj.| 中等美国人的；美国中产阶级的；|Relating to or characteristic of the middle class of the United States, often perceived as conservative, traditional, and having mainstream values.  The term can be used descriptively or pejoratively, depending on context.|例句：The candidate tried to appeal to middle-American values.  这位候选人试图迎合中等美国人的价值观。

例句：Their lifestyle is considered quintessentially middle-American. 他们的生活方式被认为是典型的中等美国人的生活方式。


近义词：mainstream, typical American, conventional


反义词：radical, unconventional, elitist


词性：形容词
