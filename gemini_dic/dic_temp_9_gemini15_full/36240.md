cognizable:/ˈkɒɡnɪzəbəl/| adj.|可认知的；可辨认的；可知的；可受理的 |Able to be perceived, known, or understood; capable of being recognized or identified.  It can also refer to something that a court of law has the power to deal with. |例句：The crime was cognizable by the local police. 当地警方有权处理这起犯罪案件。


例句：The symptoms of the disease were readily cognizable.  这种疾病的症状很容易辨认。

近义词：perceivable, recognizable, discernible, knowable, understandable

反义词：imperceptible, unrecognizable, indiscernible, unknowable, incomprehensible


词源：From Latin *cognoscibilis, from *cognoscere "to get to know," from *co- "together" + *gnōscere "to know."  The word shares roots with words like "cognition" and "recognize."
