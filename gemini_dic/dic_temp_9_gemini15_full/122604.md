modernize:/ˈmɒdənaɪz/ /ˈmɑːdərnaɪz/| vt.|使现代化；现代化改造|To make something more modern.  This often involves updating technology, methods, or designs.|例句：The company is modernizing its production facilities. 公司正在对其生产设施进行现代化改造。|例句：They modernized the old building by adding new windows and a new roof. 他们通过增加新的窗户和屋顶使旧建筑现代化。|近义词：update, renovate, upgrade|反义词：antiquate, obsolesce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They modernize their equipment regularly. = 他们定期对其设备进行现代化改造。
The factory modernizes its processes to improve efficiency. = 工厂对其流程进行现代化改造以提高效率。


一般过去时:
The government modernized the railway system last year. = 政府去年对铁路系统进行了现代化改造。
We modernized our office by installing new computers. = 我们通过安装新电脑使办公室现代化了。


现在完成时:
They have modernized their website to improve user experience. = 他们已经对其网站进行了现代化改造以改善用户体验。
The company has modernized its marketing strategies. = 公司已经将其营销策略现代化了。


过去完成时:
They had modernized the factory before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经对工厂进行了现代化改造。
The city had modernized its infrastructure before hosting the Olympics. = 这个城市在举办奥运会之前已经对其基础设施进行了现代化改造。


将来时:
We will modernize our house next year. = 我们明年将对我们的房子进行现代化改造。
The school will modernize its science labs soon. = 学校将很快对其科学实验室进行现代化改造。


虚拟语气:
If they modernized their system, they would increase productivity. = 如果他们对系统进行现代化改造，他们将提高生产力。
I wish they had modernized the library sooner. = 我希望他们早点对图书馆进行现代化改造。


被动语态:
The old building is being modernized. = 这座旧建筑正在被现代化改造。
The software was modernized last month. = 该软件上个月进行了现代化改造。


疑问句:

否定句:
