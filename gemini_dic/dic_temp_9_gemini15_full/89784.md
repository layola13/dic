horizons:/həˈraɪzənz/| n.|地平线；视野；眼界；范围|The limit of a person's understanding or experience.  The line at which the earth's surface and the sky appear to meet.|例句：The sun dipped below the horizon. 太阳落到地平线下。

The horizons of their knowledge expanded greatly after the trip. 旅行之后，他们的知识面大大扩展了。

近义词：view, outlook, prospect, scope|反义词：limit, confinement, restriction|名词
