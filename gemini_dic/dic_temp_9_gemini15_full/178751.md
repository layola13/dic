rectoral:/ˈrektərəl/| adj.|教长的；牧师的；校长职务的|Relating to a rector or a rectory.  The term often refers to the authority or jurisdiction of a rector, or the duties and responsibilities associated with the rector's position.|例句：The rectoral duties are demanding, requiring both spiritual and administrative skills. 教长职责要求很高，需要既有精神上的，也有行政上的能力。

例句：He holds a rectoral position at a prestigious university. 他在著名大学担任校长职务。


近义词：rectorial, parochial (in a limited sense), episcopal (if referring to a bishop's authority)


反义词：lay (not clerical), secular (not religious)


词性：形容词
