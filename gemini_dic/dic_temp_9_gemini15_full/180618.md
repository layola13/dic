reglorifies: /ˌriːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪz/ | v. |重新赋予荣耀，再次赞美 | To glorify again; to restore to glory or honor.  The word is a relatively rare and somewhat artificial formation, created by combining "re-" (again) and "glorify."
