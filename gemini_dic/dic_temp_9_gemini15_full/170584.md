pseudo-chinese:/ˈsuːdoʊˌtʃaɪˈniːz/|/ˈsjuːdəʊˌtʃaɪˈniːz/| adj. |伪中国的；貌似中国的|Apparently or seemingly Chinese, but not genuinely so; often used to describe words or phrases that resemble Chinese but lack genuine meaning or cultural significance.  This term is often used to describe words or phrases that are created to sound like Chinese, but are not actually Chinese words or phrases.  They may be invented for fictional purposes, or used in a humorous or ironic way.|例句：The restaurant's menu featured several pseudo-Chinese dishes that bore little resemblance to authentic cuisine. 这家餐馆的菜单上有几道伪中国菜，与正宗菜肴几乎没有相似之处。


例句：He used a string of pseudo-Chinese characters in his fantasy novel to add an exotic flair. 他在他的奇幻小说中使用了一串伪中国文字来增添异国情调。


近义词：faux-Chinese, mock-Chinese, imitation Chinese


反义词：authentic Chinese, genuine Chinese


词性：形容词
