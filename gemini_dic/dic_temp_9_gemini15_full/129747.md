non-Europeans:/ˌnɑːn juəˈroʊpiənz/ | n. |非欧洲人 |People who are not of European origin or descent.  This term refers to individuals from any country or region outside of Europe. The term can be considered broad and potentially insensitive depending on context, as it groups together a vast and diverse population. |Example: The conference included participants from various countries, both Europeans and non-Europeans.  这次会议包括来自不同国家的一些与会者，既有欧洲人，也有非欧洲人。|近义词: people from other continents, non-Europeans, foreigners (in some contexts) |反义词: Europeans |名词
