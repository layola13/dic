dupion:/ˈduːpiɒn/| n. |双宫绸|A type of silk fabric characterized by its slightly irregular surface texture and slubbed appearance, resulting from the use of two silk strands twisted together during the reeling process.  This creates a unique, subtly textured look. Dupion silk is known for its durability and lustrous sheen.|The dress was made of luxurious dupion silk.|近义词：silk, shantung|反义词:无
