codiscoverers:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvərərz/| n. |共同发现者，合作发现者 |Those who jointly made a discovery.  The word combines "co-" (meaning together or jointly) and "discoverers" (those who make a discovery).|例句：The codiscoverers of the new element shared the Nobel Prize.  共同发现新元素的科学家们分享了诺贝尔奖。

The codiscoverers of penicillin were honored for their work. 青霉素的共同发现者们因他们的工作而受到表彰。|近义词：joint discoverers, collaborators |反义词：sole discoverer, independent discoverer|名词
