collabora:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/| v. |合作，协作|to work jointly on an activity or project|The students collaborated on a science project.|近义词：cooperate, work together|反义词：compete, oppose|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collaborate on research projects. = 他们合作进行研究项目。
We collaborate closely with our partners. = 我们与合作伙伴紧密合作。


一般过去时:
The two companies collaborated on a new product. = 这两家公司合作开发了一种新产品。
They collaborated extensively on the book. = 他们为这本书做了大量的合作。


现在完成时:
We have collaborated on several successful projects. = 我们已经合作完成了几项成功的项目。
They have collaborated with many different organizations. = 他们已经与许多不同的组织合作。


过去完成时:
They had collaborated on many projects before this one. = 在这个项目之前，他们已经合作过许多项目。
She had collaborated with him on several occasions. = 她曾多次与他合作。


将来时:
We will collaborate on a new marketing campaign. = 我们将合作开展一个新的营销活动。
They will collaborate with international experts. = 他们将与国际专家合作。


虚拟语气:
If they collaborated more effectively, they would achieve better results. = 如果他们更有效地合作，他们将取得更好的成果。
I wish we had collaborated more closely on the project. = 我希望我们在项目中能更紧密地合作。


被动语态:
The project was collaborated on by several teams. = 这个项目是由几个团队合作完成的。
The new strategy is being collaborated on by the marketing and sales teams. = 营销和销售团队正在合作制定新的战略。


疑问句:
Will you collaborate with me on this task? = 你愿意和我一起完成这项任务吗？
Are they collaborating on the new design? = 他们正在合作设计新产品吗？


否定句:
They did not collaborate on the report. = 他们没有合作撰写报告。
We will not collaborate with companies that have unethical practices. = 我们不会与有不道德行为的公司合作。


PHRASE->
collaborate with = 与…合作
collaborate on = 合作做…
collaborative effort = 合作努力
collaborative work = 合作作品
collaborative learning = 合作学习
