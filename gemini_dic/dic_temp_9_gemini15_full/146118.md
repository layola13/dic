overemoting:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊʃənɪŋ/ | v. |过度表达情感；情感外露 |The act of expressing emotions excessively or inappropriately.  It often involves exaggerating one's feelings or reactions. |例句：He was overemoting during the presentation, distracting the audience. 他在演示过程中过度表达情感，分散了观众的注意力。
His overemoting made it difficult to take him seriously.  他的过度情感表达让他难以让人认真对待。|近义词：overreacting, melodramatic, histrionic |反义词：understating, stoicism, composure |
