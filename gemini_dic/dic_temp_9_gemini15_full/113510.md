ma'am:/mæm/| n. |夫人，女士 |A polite term of address for a woman.  It is a shortened form of "madam".|例句：Excuse me, ma'am, could you help me?  对不起，夫人，您能帮我一下吗？|例句：Yes, ma'am. 是的，夫人。|近义词：madam, lady |反义词：sir


USAGE->
This word is not a verb, adjective, or adverb, therefore tense and voice are not applicable.  It is only used as a noun in the vocative case (direct address).


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "ma'am" as a core component; it functions primarily as a term of address.  Phrases involving "ma'am" would be contextual and involve other words.  For example: "Yes, ma'am," "Excuse me, ma'am," "Thank you, ma'am."
