purringly:/ˈpɜːrɪŋli/ | adv. | 轻轻地，温柔地，像猫儿一样地 |  In a soft, gentle, murmuring way, like a cat purring.  It suggests a soothing, comforting, or sometimes sly and insinuating tone. | Example: He spoke purringly, his words dripping with false sweetness. 他温柔地说话，话语中充满了虚假的甜言蜜语。  |近义词：softly, sweetly, soothingly, caressingly | 反义词：harshly, roughly, gruffly
