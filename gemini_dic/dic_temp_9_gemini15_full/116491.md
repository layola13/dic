materially:/məˈtɪəriəli/ | adv. |实质上地；物质上地；非常 |In a material or physical way, as opposed to a spiritual or abstract way.  It can also mean "to a significant degree".|例句：The new policy materially altered the company's operations. 新政策实质上改变了公司的运营。

例句：The damage to the car was materially significant. 车辆损坏非常严重。


近义词：substantially, significantly, considerably, essentially | 反义词：immaterially, insignificantly, negligibly

词源:源自于拉丁语"materialis",  意思是"物质的"，"身体的"。  "-ally"是副词后缀。
