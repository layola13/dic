layer: /ˈleɪər/ | n. | 层；层次；阶层 | a relatively thin sheet or expanse of a substance or material | The cake has three layers of sponge and cream.  这个蛋糕有三层海绵蛋糕和奶油。  The earth's atmosphere is divided into several layers. 地球的大气层分为几层。| 近义词：stratum, level, tier | 反义词：surface


USAGE->

The artist applies a new layer of paint. = 艺术家涂上了一层新油漆。
This program has multiple layers of security. = 这个程序有多层安全保护。
