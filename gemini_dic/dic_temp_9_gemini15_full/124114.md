mortals:/ˈmɔːrtəlz/ | n. |凡人，人类 |Humans, as opposed to gods or other supernatural beings.  They are subject to death and limitations. |例句：Even mortals can achieve greatness if they work hard enough.  即使是凡人，只要足够努力，也能取得伟大的成就。

例句：All mortals are destined to die one day. 所有凡人都注定有一天会死去。
