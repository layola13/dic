/lɑ̃ɡ/| n. |语言|Languages are systems of communication used by humans.|Example: English is a global language.  英语是一种全球语言。|近义词：tongue,speech|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What language do you speak? = 你说哪种语言？
Which languages are taught at this school? = 这所学校教哪些语言？


一般过去时:
What language did he speak? = 他说的是什么语言？
What languages were spoken in ancient Rome? = 古罗马说的是什么语言？


现在完成时:
Have you learned any new languages recently? = 你最近学习了哪些新的语言？
How many languages has she mastered? = 她掌握了多少种语言？


过去完成时:
Had you learned French before you moved to Paris? = 你搬到巴黎之前学过法语吗？
What languages had they studied before the exam? = 考试前他们学过哪些语言？


将来时:
Will you learn a new language next year? = 你明年会学习一门外语吗？
What languages will be taught in the future? = 未来将教授哪些语言？


虚拟语气:
If I spoke Spanish, I could travel to Spain easily. = 如果我会说西班牙语，我就能轻松地去西班牙旅行。
I wish I knew more languages. = 我希望我懂更多语言。


被动语态:
Which languages are spoken in Canada? = 加拿大通行哪些语言？
Many languages were spoken in the ancient world. = 古代世界通行许多语言。


疑问句:
What is your native language? = 你的母语是什么？
What languages do they speak in that country? = 那个国家的人们说哪些语言？


否定句:
I don't speak any foreign languages. = 我不会说任何外语。
She didn't understand the language they were speaking. = 她不明白他们说的是什么语言。


PHRASE->
body language =肢体语言
sign language = 手语
first language = 母语
foreign language = 外语
programming language = 编程语言
