groans:/ɡroʊnz/| /ɡrəʊnz/| n., v.|呻吟，抱怨|to make a low, deep sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or disapproval.|例句：He groaned in pain after falling. 他摔倒后痛苦地呻吟着。|例句：She groaned about the extra work. 她抱怨额外的工作。|近义词：moan, sigh, complain|反义词：laugh, cheer

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He groans every time he bends down. = 他每次弯腰都会呻吟。
She groans about the traffic every morning. = 她每天早上都抱怨交通状况。

一般过去时:
He groaned loudly when the doctor touched his wound. = 当医生触碰他的伤口时，他大声呻吟。
She groaned after a long day at work. = 她在工作了一整天后呻吟着。

现在完成时:
He has groaned with pain all night. = 他整夜都在痛苦地呻吟。
She has groaned about her boss for weeks. = 她已经抱怨她老板几周了。

过去完成时:
He had groaned in pain before the paramedics arrived. = 在医护人员到达之前，他已经痛苦地呻吟了。
She had groaned about the project before they even started. = 在他们甚至开始之前，她就对这个项目抱怨了。

将来时:
He will groan when he hears the news. = 当他听到这个消息时，他会呻吟。
She will groan if she has to work overtime again. = 如果她不得不再次加班，她会抱怨的。

虚拟语气:
If he lifted that heavy box, he would groan. = 如果他搬起那个沉重的箱子，他会呻吟。
If she had known it would be so much work, she would have groaned. = 如果她知道会有这么多工作，她就会抱怨了。


被动语态:
A groan was heard from the next room. = 从隔壁房间传来一声呻吟。
Complaints were groaned about the new policy. = 人们对新政策抱怨连连。


疑问句:

Why are you groaning? = 你为什么呻吟？


否定句:
He didn't groan when he received the good news. = 当他收到好消息时，他没有呻吟。
She didn't groan about the extra work, surprisingly. = 令人惊讶的是，她没有抱怨额外的工作。
