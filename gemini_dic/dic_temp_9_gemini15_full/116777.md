mauvish:/ˈmoʊvɪʃ/| adj. |略带紫罗兰色的，紫红色的|Slightly purplish in color.  The word suggests a pale or muted shade of purple, not a deep, vibrant one.|例句：The sky was a mauvish gray at dawn. 黎明时分，天空呈现出略带紫罗兰色的灰色。

The walls were painted a delicate mauvish pink. 墙壁涂上了精致的淡紫粉色。|近义词：purplish, lilac, lavender|反义词：vivid, bright (in relation to color intensity)
