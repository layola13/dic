magnificent:/mæɡˈnɪfɪsnt/| adj.|壮丽的，宏伟的，极好的 |extremely beautiful, impressive, or excellent.|The magnificent view from the mountaintop took our breath away.  山顶壮丽的景色让我们惊叹不已。|The palace was magnificent, with towering spires and intricate carvings. 宫殿宏伟壮观，有着高耸的尖塔和精细的雕刻。|splendid, grand, glorious, superb|ordinary, unimpressive, mediocre


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The artwork is magnificent. = 这件艺术品非常壮丽。
The sunset is magnificent tonight. = 今晚的日落很壮观。


一般过去时:
The concert was magnificent. = 音乐会非常精彩。
The performance was magnificent last night. = 昨晚的演出非常精彩。


现在完成时:
We have seen many magnificent sights on our trip. = 我们在旅途中看到了许多壮丽的景色。
She has created a magnificent piece of art. = 她创作了一件非常棒的艺术品。


过去完成时:
They had already seen the magnificent castle before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经看过那座宏伟的城堡了。
The building had been magnificent in its prime. = 这栋建筑在鼎盛时期非常宏伟。


将来时:
The fireworks display will be magnificent. = 烟火表演将非常壮观。
The new museum will be magnificent when it opens. = 新博物馆开馆后将会非常宏伟。


虚拟语气:
If only I could see that magnificent sunset again! = 但愿我能再次看到那壮丽的日落！
It would be magnificent if we could afford that house. = 如果我们买得起那栋房子就好了。


被动语态:
The city was described as magnificent. = 这座城市被描述为壮丽的。
The building is often considered magnificent. = 这栋建筑经常被认为是宏伟的。


疑问句:
Isn't the view magnificent? = 景色不是很壮丽吗？
Is the new stadium magnificent? = 新体育场宏伟吗？


否定句:
The design was not magnificent, but functional. = 设计并非宏伟，而是实用的。
The painting wasn't magnificent, but it had a certain charm. = 这幅画并不宏伟，但它有一定的魅力。


PHRASE->

a magnificent view: 壮丽的景色
a magnificent achievement: 一项伟大的成就
a magnificent effort: 伟大的努力
a magnificent example: 一个极好的例子
magnificent obsession: 伟大的执念


词源:

From Middle French *magnifique, from Latin magnus "great" + -ficus, suffix denoting something that makes.
