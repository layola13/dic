kefir:/ˈkɛfɪr/| n. |开菲尔，克菲尔（一种发酵乳饮料）|A fermented milk drink originating from the Caucasus region, made with kefir grains (a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeasts). It has a slightly tangy and fizzy taste and is often considered a probiotic drink. |I drink kefir every morning for gut health. 我每天早上喝开菲尔来保持肠道健康。|yogurt, buttermilk|  (无明确反义词)|名词
