cosponsoring: /ˌkoʊˈspɑːnsərɪŋ/ | v. |共同发起，联名提出 |The act of two or more people or entities jointly sponsoring something, such as a bill in a legislature or an event.  This involves sharing responsibility and support. |例句：Several senators are cosponsoring the new environmental protection bill.  几个参议员正在共同发起新的环境保护法案。


例句：The university and a local company are cosponsoring a student scholarship program. 大学和一家当地公司正在联合发起一项学生奖学金项目。


近义词：jointly sponsoring, co-sponsoring


反义词：opposing, individually sponsoring


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cosponsor many events each year. = 他们每年共同发起许多活动。
The two senators cosponsor the bill. = 这两位参议员共同发起该法案。


一般过去时:
