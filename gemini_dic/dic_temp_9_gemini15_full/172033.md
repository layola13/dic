purim:/ˈpuːrɪm/ | n. |普珥节|The Jewish festival of Purim celebrates the deliverance of the Jewish people from Haman's plot to exterminate them, as recounted in the Book of Esther.  It is a joyous occasion marked by festive meals, costume parties, and the giving of gifts to the poor.|Example: We celebrated Purim with a costume party and lots of delicious food.  我们以化妆舞会和许多美味的食物庆祝普珥节。|近义词：festival, holiday|反义词：None|名词
