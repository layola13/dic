descender:/dɪˈsɛndər/| n. | 下降部分；下垂部分 | The part of a lowercase letter that extends below the baseline.  For example, the lowercase letters 'g', 'j', 'p', 'q', and 'y' all have descenders. | 例句：The descenders on the 'g's were slightly too long.  g的下降部分略微过长。| 例句：The typeface's distinctive feature is its elegant descenders.  这种字体的独特之处在于其优雅的下垂部分。| 近义词：tail, drop | 反义词：ascender
