pseudo-Greek:/ˈsuːdoʊɡriːk/|/ˈsjuːdəʊɡriːk/| adj. |伪希腊的；貌似希腊的 |Apparently Greek in origin or style but not genuinely so.  The term often describes words or styles that imitate Greek forms but lack genuine classical roots.  It can also refer to something that superficially resembles Greek culture or architecture but is not authentic. | Example: The building's pseudo-Greek columns were a cheap imitation of classical architecture.  这座建筑的伪希腊式柱子是古典建筑的廉价模仿。|近义词：pseudoclassical, sham, imitation |反义词：authentic, genuine, real
