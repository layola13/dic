oversensitive: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过度敏感的，易受伤害的 | Excessively sensitive; easily upset or offended.  |  She's oversensitive to criticism. 她对批评过于敏感。 He's become oversensitive since the accident. 自从那次事故后，他变得很敏感。|近义词：sensitive, hypersensitive, touchy |反义词：insensitive, thick-skinned, callous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is oversensitive to noise. 他对噪音过度敏感。
She is oversensitive about her appearance. 她对自己的外表过于敏感。


一般过去时:
He was oversensitive about his weight. 他过去对自己的体重很敏感。
She was oversensitive to any perceived slight. 她对任何被认为的轻蔑都过度敏感。


现在完成时:
I have become oversensitive in my old age. 我上了年纪后变得很敏感。
She has always been oversensitive to criticism. 她一直对批评都很敏感。


过去完成时:
He had been oversensitive about his past failures. 他一直对过去的失败耿耿于怀。
She had become oversensitive after the breakup. 分手后她变得很敏感。


将来时:
I will probably be oversensitive about my new job. 我可能会对我的新工作过度敏感。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
His oversensitivity was often misunderstood. 他的过度敏感经常被误解。

疑问句:

否定句:
