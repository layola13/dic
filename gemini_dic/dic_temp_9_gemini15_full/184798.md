resurrectionist:/ˌrezəˈrɛkʃənɪst/| n. |盗尸者，掘墓者 | A person who illegally removes bodies from graves, typically for the purpose of selling them to medical schools for anatomical study.  |The resurrectionists worked under the cover of darkness, robbing graves to supply the demand for cadavers. 盗尸者在夜色的掩护下工作，盗墓以满足对尸体的需求。|body snatcher, grave robber |(无明确反义词，可考虑：mortician, undertaker,  but these have different connotations)|名词
