fictionalized:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzd/| adj.|虚构的；小说化的|Presented as fiction; adapted or treated as fiction.  This involves taking real events or people and transforming them into a fictional narrative, often altering details for dramatic effect or to protect identities. |例句：The movie is a fictionalized account of his life. 这部电影是他生平的一个虚构故事。

The novel is a fictionalized version of a true story. 这部小说是对真实故事的小说化改编。|近义词：fabricated, invented, romanticized, dramatized|反义词：factual, realistic, true, authentic
