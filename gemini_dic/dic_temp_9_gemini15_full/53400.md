disallowed:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊd/| adj.|不被允许的，禁止的|Not permitted or allowed.  This word describes something that is forbidden or not accepted by rules, regulations, or authority.|例句：The judge disallowed the evidence because it was obtained illegally. = 由于证据是非法获得的，法官驳回了该证据。

例句：That maneuver is disallowed in this game. = 在这场游戏中，这种战术是不允许的。


近义词：prohibited, forbidden, banned, illegitimate, unauthorized|反义词：allowed, permitted, accepted, legitimate


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rule disallows running in the hallways. = 校规禁止在走廊里奔跑。
This policy disallows outside food. = 此项政策禁止携带外来食物。


一般过去时:
The referee disallowed the goal. = 裁判判罚进球无效。
The teacher disallowed the use of calculators on the test. = 老师禁止考试使用计算器。


现在完成时:
The committee has disallowed the proposal. = 委员会已否决了该提案。
The court has disallowed the appeal. = 法院已驳回了上诉。


过去完成时:
They had disallowed the amendment before the debate began. = 他们在辩论开始之前就否决了修正案。
The official had disallowed the entry of the foreign delegation. = 官员已禁止外国代表团入境。


将来时:
The board will disallow any further expenditures without prior approval. = 董事会将不允许在未经批准的情况下进行任何进一步的支出。
The authorities will likely disallow the demonstration. = 当局很可能会禁止示威游行。


虚拟语气:
If the rules disallowed it, we wouldn't have done it. = 如果规则不允许，我们就不会那样做了。
I wish the coach had disallowed that dangerous play. = 我希望教练当时禁止了那个危险的动作。


被动语态:
The application was disallowed due to incomplete information. = 由于信息不完整，申请被驳回。
His request was disallowed by the manager. = 他的请求被经理拒绝了。


疑问句:
Is smoking disallowed in this building? = 这栋楼里禁止吸烟吗？
Are pets disallowed in this hotel? = 这家酒店禁止携带宠物吗？


否定句:
The rule doesn't disallow pets, but they must be leashed. = 规定并没有禁止宠物，但必须系好绳子。
The regulation doesn't disallow photography, but flash photography is prohibited. = 规定没有禁止拍照，但禁止闪光灯拍照。
