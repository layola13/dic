skippers:/ˈskɪpərz/| n.|船长；（昆虫的）跳蚤；（板球的）守门员|A skipper is the captain of a ship or boat.  It can also refer to a small insect that jumps, or in cricket, the wicket-keeper.|例句：The skipper skillfully navigated the ship through the storm.  船长熟练地驾驶着船只穿过暴风雨。|例句：The skipper of the cricket team made a crucial catch. 板球队的队长接了一个关键的球。|近义词：captain, commander, skipper (insect)|反义词：crew member, subordinate
