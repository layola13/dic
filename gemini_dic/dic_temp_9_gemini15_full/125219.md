multicorporation: /ˌmʌltiˈkɔːrpəreɪʃən/ | n. | 多国公司，跨国公司 | A multicorporation is a large corporation that operates in multiple countries.  It typically has a complex organizational structure and engages in international trade and investment. | Example:  Many Fortune 500 companies are multicorporations with operations spanning the globe.  许多财富500强公司都是跨国公司，业务遍布全球。 | 近义词：multinational corporation, transnational corporation, global corporation | 反义词：domestic corporation, local corporation
