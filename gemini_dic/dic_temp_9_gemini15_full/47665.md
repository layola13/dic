debriefers:/ˌdiːˈbriːfərz/| n.| 汇报者；情况通报员 |People who conduct debriefings, gathering information from individuals after an event or experience.  They often work in fields like military, law enforcement, or aviation.|例句：The debriefers meticulously documented the pilot's account of the emergency landing.  汇报者一丝不苟地记录了飞行员关于紧急着陆的报告。|The debriefers interviewed all the witnesses to the accident.  汇报人员采访了事故的所有目击者。|近义词：interviewers, investigators, interrogators|反义词：briefers (those giving information)
