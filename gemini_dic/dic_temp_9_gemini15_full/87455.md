hesperidene:/ˈhɛspərɪdiːn/| n. |橙皮甙；橙皮素|A flavanone glycoside found in citrus fruits, particularly in the peel. It's a type of flavonoid with antioxidant properties.|例句：Hesperidin is a bioflavonoid found abundantly in citrus peels. 橙皮甙是柑橘皮中含量丰富的生物类黄酮。  Hesperidin supplements are sometimes marketed for their purported health benefits.  有时会销售橙皮甙补充剂，宣称其具有健康益处。|近义词：flavonoid, bioflavonoid|反义词：无
