on-the-fly:/ˌɒn ðə flaɪ/| adj.|即时的；临时的；动态的|done or happening without previous preparation or planning; improvised.  |例句：The decision was made on-the-fly. 这个决定是临时做出的。|例句：He gave an on-the-fly translation of the speech. 他即兴翻译了演讲。|近义词：improvised, impromptu, spontaneous|反义词：planned, pre-arranged, deliberate
