disclosing: /dɪˈskloʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 透露；揭露；公开 | The act of making something known that was previously secret or hidden.  It implies a revelation, often of something potentially sensitive or embarrassing. |例句：The company is disclosing its financial results next week. 公司下周将公布其财务业绩。

The government is disclosing classified information to the public. 政府正在向公众公开机密信息。


近义词：revealing, unveiling, divulging, exposing, uncovering | 反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, secreting | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disclosing sensitive information. 他正在泄露敏感信息。
She discloses her personal details on social media. 她会在社交媒体上公开她的个人信息。


一般过去时:
The whistleblower disclosed the company's illegal activities.  举报人揭露了公司的非法活动。
He disclosed his plans to his family. 他向家人透露了他的计划。


现在完成时:
They have disclosed all the relevant facts. 他们已经披露了所有相关事实。
The investigation has disclosed some disturbing information.  调查已经披露了一些令人不安的信息。


过去完成时:
She had disclosed the secret before anyone else.  她在其他人之前就泄露了秘密。
The witness had already disclosed everything he knew. 目击者已经将他所知道的一切都说了出来。


将来时:
The company will disclose its earnings next quarter. 公司将在下个季度公布其收益。
He will disclose the truth soon. 他很快就会说出真相。


虚拟语气:
If he disclosed the truth, he would be in trouble. 如果他坦白真相，他就会有麻烦。
I wish he had disclosed the problem earlier. 我希望他早点说出这个问题。


被动语态:
The information was disclosed to the press.  信息被透露给了媒体。
The details of the agreement were disclosed in a press release.  协议的细节在一个新闻稿中公布。


疑问句:
Will the company be disclosing its profits soon? 公司会很快公布其利润吗？
What information did he disclose in his testimony? 他在证词中透露了什么信息？


否定句:
He did not disclose his sources. 他没有透露他的消息来源。
The company refused to disclose any further details. 公司拒绝透露任何进一步的细节。


PHRASE->
disclosing information:  披露信息
fully disclosing:  充分披露
publicly disclosing: 公开披露
self-disclosing:  自我披露
disclosing a secret: 泄露秘密
