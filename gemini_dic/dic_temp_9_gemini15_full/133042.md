nonenvironmental: /ˌnɒnɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntl/ | adj. | 非环境的；与环境无关的 | Not related to or caused by the environment.  This term indicates something that doesn't originate from or impact the natural world. | Example: The company's nonenvironmental policies led to significant pollution. 这家公司的非环保政策导致了严重的污染。 | 近义词: extraneous, unrelated, unconnected | 反义词: environmental, ecological
