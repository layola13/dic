mutts:/mʌts/| n.|杂种狗；劣等货；笨蛋|A mutt is a dog of mixed breed.  The term is often used informally and can be considered slightly derogatory.|例句：That dog is a lovable mutt. 那只狗是一只可爱的杂种狗。

例句：He's a real mutt for forgetting my birthday. 他忘了我的生日，真是个笨蛋。|近义词：mongrel, cur, dog|反义词：purebred
