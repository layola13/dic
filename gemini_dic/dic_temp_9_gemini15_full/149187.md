oxygenant:/ˈɒksɪdʒənənt/| n. |助燃剂，氧化剂|A substance that provides or promotes oxidation, especially in a combustion process.  Oxygen itself is the most common oxygenant.|例句：Air acts as an oxygenant in the combustion of fuel.空气在燃料燃烧过程中充当氧化剂。

例句：The chemical reaction required an oxygenant to proceed.  这个化学反应需要氧化剂才能进行。


近义词：oxidant, oxidizer |反义词：reducing agent, reductant |词性：名词
