concealers: /kənˈsiːlərz/ | n. |遮瑕膏，掩饰者 | Cosmetics used to hide blemishes, imperfections, or dark circles under the eyes.  A concealer is a type of makeup that helps to cover up imperfections on the skin. | 例句：She applied concealer to cover her dark circles. 她涂抹遮瑕膏来遮盖黑眼圈。

She used a green concealer to neutralize redness on her face. 她用绿色遮瑕膏来中和脸上的红肿。


近义词：foundation, cover-up | 反义词：highlighter
