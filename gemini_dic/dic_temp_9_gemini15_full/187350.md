rockweed:/ˈrɒkwiːd/| n.|岩石藻, 海藻|A brown seaweed that grows on rocks in the intertidal zone. It is often used as fertilizer and has other applications.|例句：Rockweed is a common sight along the rocky coastlines of the North Atlantic.  岩石藻是北大西洋岩石海岸线上常见的景象。|例句：Many coastal communities harvest rockweed for its use as a fertilizer and soil conditioner. 许多沿海社区都收获岩石藻用作肥料和土壤改良剂。|近义词：seaweed, kelp|反义词:None.
