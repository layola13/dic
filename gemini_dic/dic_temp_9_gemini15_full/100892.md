intuiter:/ɪnˈtuːɪtər/ | n. | 直觉者，凭直觉感知者 | A person who has the ability to understand or know something without any proof or conscious reasoning.  Someone who relies on intuition. | Example:  She's a brilliant intuiter, often predicting market trends before anyone else.  她是一位杰出的直觉者，经常在其他人之前预测市场趋势。 |近义词:  intuitive person, seer, visionary | 反义词:  logical thinker, rationalist |
