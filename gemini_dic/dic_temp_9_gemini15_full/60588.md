elation:/ɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |兴高采烈；得意；兴奋 |A feeling of great happiness and excitement.  |例句：Her elation at winning the race was evident.  她赢得比赛后的喜悦显而易见。

例句：The elation she felt after finishing her novel was overwhelming. 完成小说后她感受到的喜悦是难以言喻的。


近义词：exhilaration, joy, delight, jubilation, rapture |反义词：despair, depression, dejection, gloom


词源:  From Middle English *elacioun, from Old French elacion, from Latin elatio "lifting up, elevation," from elatus, past participle of efferre "to carry away, lift up," from ex- "out, up" + ferre "to carry."  The sense evolution is from a physical "lifting up" to an emotional "lifting up" of spirits.
