one-piece:/ˈwʌnpiːs/| adj. n. |一件式的；连体服|A single piece of clothing, typically a dress or swimsuit, that is all in one. |例句：She wore a stylish one-piece swimsuit to the beach. 她穿着时尚的连体泳衣去海滩。|近义词：single-piece, all-in-one|反义词：two-piece, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress is a one-piece design. = 这件衣服是一件式的设计。
This swimsuit is a one-piece. = 这件泳衣是一件式的。


一般过去时:
She bought a beautiful one-piece dress. = 她买了一件漂亮的连衣裙。
He wore a one-piece jumpsuit to the party. = 他穿着连体裤去参加派对。


现在完成时:
I have worn this one-piece for years. = 我已经穿这件连体衣好几年了。
She has never owned a one-piece swimsuit. = 她从未拥有过一件连体泳衣。


过去完成时:
I had already put on my one-piece before she arrived. = 在她到达之前我已经穿好了连体衣。
They had never seen such a beautiful one-piece before. = 他们以前从未见过如此美丽的连体衣。


将来时:
I will buy a new one-piece for the summer. = 我将在夏天买一件新的连体衣。
She will wear a one-piece to the wedding. = 她将穿一件连体衣去参加婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If I had a one-piece, I would wear it to the beach. = 如果我有一件连体衣，我会穿它去海滩。
I wish I had a more stylish one-piece. = 我希望我有一件更时尚的连体衣。


被动语态:
The one-piece was designed by a famous fashion designer. = 这件连体衣是由一位著名的时装设计师设计的。
The one-piece is often worn by swimmers. = 连体衣经常被游泳者穿着。


疑问句:
Is that a one-piece or a two-piece swimsuit? = 那是连体泳衣还是分体泳衣？
Do you prefer one-piece or two-piece dresses? = 你更喜欢连衣裙还是分体裙？


否定句:
That's not a one-piece, it's a two-piece. = 那不是一件式的，它是分体的。
She doesn't own any one-piece swimsuits. = 她没有一件连体泳衣。
