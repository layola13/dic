circumfuse:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfjuːz/ | vt. | 灌注；遍布；环绕 | To pour or spread around; to surround or encompass.  The word implies a complete envelopment or permeation. | 例句：The aroma of coffee circumfused the room. = 咖啡的香味弥漫了整个房间。


The light circumfused the entire landscape. = 光线环绕着整个景观。| 近义词：pervade, envelop, imbue, suffuse | 反义词：exclude, restrict, confine | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma circumfuses the entire kitchen. = 香气弥漫整个厨房。
He circumfuses his speech with metaphors. = 他在演讲中大量运用比喻。


一般过去时:
The warm air circumfused the valley. = 暖空气环绕着山谷。
The sadness circumfused her heart. = 悲伤充满了她的内心。


现在完成时:
The news has circumfused the entire nation. = 这个消息已经传遍全国。
The color has circumfused the fabric. = 颜色已经染遍了织物。


过去完成时:
The fear had circumfused the town before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，恐惧已经笼罩了整个城镇。
The scent had circumfused the air before she even entered the room. = 她还没进房间，香味就已经弥漫在空气中了。


将来时:
The music will circumfuse the hall. = 音乐将会充满整个大厅。
The mist will circumfuse the mountain peaks. = 薄雾将会笼罩山峰。


虚拟语气:
If the light circumfused the room, we could see better. = 如果灯光充满房间，我们就能看得更清楚。
I wish the joy would circumfuse my heart. = 我希望快乐能充满我的内心。


被动语态:
The room was circumfused with a pleasant scent. = 房间里弥漫着令人愉悦的香味。
The town was circumfused with anxiety. = 城镇笼罩在焦虑之中。


疑问句:
Does the scent circumfuse the entire house? = 香味是否弥漫了整个房子？
Will the color circumfuse the entire painting? = 颜色会染遍整幅画吗？


否定句:
The silence did not circumfuse the room; instead, a murmur filled the air. = 沉默并没有充满房间；相反，低语声充满了空气。
The light did not circumfuse the darkness. = 光明没有驱散黑暗。


PHRASE->
circumfuse with = 以...弥漫；充满...
be circumfused with = 被...环绕；充满...
circumfuse the area with = 用...充满区域
circumfuse the atmosphere with = 用...渲染气氛
circumfuse oneself in = 沉浸于...之中

词源:
circum- (around) + fundere (to pour)  来自拉丁语。
