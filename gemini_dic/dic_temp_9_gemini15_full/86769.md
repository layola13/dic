helps:/hɛlps/ | v. |帮助，有助益 | to give assistance to someone or something; to make something easier or better. |  He helps his mother with the housework. 他帮助他母亲做家务。  | assists, aids, supports | hinders, obstructs, impedes | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helps me with my homework. 他帮我做作业。
This medicine helps relieve pain. 这药有助于缓解疼痛。


一般过去时:
She helped me find my lost keys. 她帮我找到了我丢失的钥匙。
The teacher helped the students understand the lesson. 老师帮助学生理解了这节课。


现在完成时:
This exercise has helped me lose weight.  这个练习帮助我减肥了。
The new policy has helped improve the economy. 新政策帮助改善了经济。


过去完成时:
I had helped him many times before. 我以前帮过他很多次。
She had helped prepare the dinner before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经帮忙准备好了晚餐。


将来时:
I will help you move tomorrow. 我明天会帮你搬家。
The new technology will help us work more efficiently. 新技术将帮助我们更高效地工作。


虚拟语气:
If he helped me, I could finish the project on time. 如果他帮我，我就能按时完成项目。
I wish he had helped me earlier. 我希望他早点帮我。


被动语态:
I was helped by a kind stranger. 我得到了一位好心陌生人的帮助。
The problem was helped by the new software.  这个问题在新软件的帮助下得到了解决。


疑问句:
Does this tool help you work faster?  这个工具能帮助你工作更快吗？
Can you help me with this problem? 你能帮我解决这个问题吗？


否定句:
He didn't help me at all. 他根本没帮我。
The medication didn't help with my headache.  药对我的头痛没有帮助。


PHRASE->
help out = 帮助；解救
help oneself to = 自取；随便吃
can't help but = 禁不住；不得不
help with = 帮助...
with the help of = 在...的帮助下
