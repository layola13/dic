interviewed:/ˈɪntərˌvjuːd/| v.|采访，面试|The past tense and past participle of "interview".  It means to have conducted an interview with someone. |例句：The journalist interviewed the president.  记者采访了总统。
The police interviewed several witnesses. 警方采访了几位目击者。|近义词：questioned, interrogated, surveyed|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I interview candidates every week. = 我每周都会面试候选人。
She interviews sources for her articles. = 她采访消息来源为她的文章。

一般过去时:
They interviewed me for the job yesterday. = 他们昨天面试了我。
The reporter interviewed the star after the concert. = 音乐会后记者采访了这位明星。

现在完成时:
I have interviewed over 100 people for this project. = 我已经为这个项目面试了100多人。
She has interviewed several experts on the topic. = 她已经采访了几位该领域的专家。

过去完成时:
They had already interviewed all the applicants before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经面试了所有申请者。
The committee had interviewed the candidates before making a decision. = 委员会在做出决定之前已经面试了候选人。

将来时:
I will interview the author next week. = 我下周将采访这位作者。
We will be interviewing candidates throughout the month. = 我们将在整个月进行候选人的面试。

虚拟语气:
If I had interviewed her earlier, I would have gotten more information. = 如果我早点采访她，我会得到更多信息。
I wish I had interviewed him before he died. = 我真希望在他死之前采访过他。

被动语态:
The witness was interviewed by the police. = 目击者被警方采访了。
Many candidates were interviewed for the position. = 许多候选人被面试了这个职位。

疑问句:
Were you interviewed for the position? = 你被面试了这个职位吗？
Have all the witnesses been interviewed? = 所有证人都被采访了吗？

否定句:
The company did not interview me for the job. = 公司没有面试我。
I haven't been interviewed yet. = 我还没有被面试。


PHRASE->
be interviewed for a job = 被面试一份工作
interview someone for a position = 面试某人一个职位
conduct an interview = 进行面试
give an interview = 进行采访
a job interview = 工作面试
