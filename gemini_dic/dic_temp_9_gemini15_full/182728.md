repairman:/rɪˈpɛərmæn/ | n. | 修理工，修理技师 | A person whose job is to repair things, especially mechanical or electrical devices.  | Example: We called a repairman to fix the broken washing machine. 我们叫了一个修理工来修理坏掉的洗衣机。 |近义词：repairer, serviceman, technician | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The repairman repairs appliances daily. = 修理工每天修理家用电器。
He regularly checks the equipment. = 他定期检查设备。


一般过去时:
The repairman repaired my computer yesterday. = 修理工昨天修好了我的电脑。
They fixed the leaky faucet last week. = 他们上周修好了漏水的龙头。


现在完成时:
The repairman has fixed several machines this week. = 修理工本周已经修理了几台机器。
He has already completed the repair. = 他已经完成了修理工作。


过去完成时:
The repairman had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时，修理工已经离开了。
They had finished the repair before the deadline. = 他们在大限之前完成了修理工作。


将来时:
The repairman will come tomorrow to fix the oven. = 修理工明天会来修理烤箱。
He will inspect the system next week. = 他下周会检查系统。


虚拟语气:
If the repairman were here, he would fix it immediately. = 如果修理工在这里，他会立即修理它。
I wish the repairman had arrived sooner. = 我希望修理工早点来。


被动语态:
The washing machine was repaired by the repairman. = 洗衣机被修理工修好了。
The problem is being addressed by the repairman. = 修理工正在处理这个问题。


疑问句:
Is the repairman available today? = 修理工今天有空吗？
When will the repairman arrive? = 修理工什么时候到？


否定句:
The repairman didn't fix the problem completely. = 修理工没有完全解决问题。
He couldn't repair the old radio. = 他无法修理那台旧收音机。
