nondefinable: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈfaɪ.nə.bl/ | adj. | 不可定义的，难以定义的 |  Unable to be defined or explained; defying definition.  The concept is so abstract and complex that it is essentially nondefinable.  | 近义词：undefinable, indescribable, inexpressible, unclassifiable | 反义词：definable, clear, explicit


例句：

The artist's vision was so unique and personal that it seemed nondefinable.  艺术家的视野如此独特和个性化，以至于似乎无法定义。

The exact nature of consciousness remains, for many, a nondefinable mystery.  对许多人来说，意识的确切性质仍然是一个无法定义的谜。

The situation isn't completely nondefinable.  情况并非完全无法定义。

词源:

The word "nondefinable" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "definable" (meaning "able to be defined").  "Definable" is derived from the verb "define," which comes from the Latin word "definire," meaning "to limit," "to mark the boundaries of," or "to explain the meaning of."  Therefore, "nondefinable" literally means "not able to be defined."
