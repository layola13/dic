consist of:/kənˈsɪst əv/ | v. |由…组成；包含；构成 |To be composed or made up of; to be formed from.  |The team consists of five players.  这个队由五名队员组成。| comprise, compose, include |lack, exclude |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This cake consists of flour, sugar, and eggs. = 这个蛋糕由面粉、糖和鸡蛋组成。
The committee consists of five members. = 委员会由五名成员组成。


一般过去时:
The book consisted of three chapters. = 这本书由三章组成。
Her presentation consisted of slides and a short speech. = 她的演讲由幻灯片和简短的讲话组成。


现在完成时:
The collection has consisted mainly of antique furniture. = 这个收藏主要由古董家具组成。
His work has consisted of writing and editing. = 他的工作一直是写作和编辑。


过去完成时:
The meal had consisted of soup, salad, and dessert. = 这顿饭由汤、沙拉和甜点组成。
The exhibition had consisted of paintings and sculptures. = 展览由绘画和雕塑组成。


将来时:
The next meeting will consist of a presentation and a discussion. = 下次会议将包括一个演示和一次讨论。
The project will consist of three phases. = 该项目将分为三个阶段。


虚拟语气:
If the team consisted of more experienced players, they would win more games. = 如果球队由更有经验的球员组成，他们会赢得更多比赛。
I wish the report consisted of fewer technical details. = 我希望报告包含较少的技术细节。


被动语态:
The problem is not easily solved because it consists of many parts.  问题难以解决，因为它由许多部分组成。(被动语态在这里用得较少，通常用主动语态)
The cake is made up of flour, sugar, and eggs. 这个蛋糕由面粉，糖和鸡蛋组成。 (使用更自然的被动表达)


疑问句:
What does this medicine consist of? = 这药由什么组成？
What does the course consist of? = 这门课程包括什么内容？


否定句:
The solution does not consist of simply adding more money. = 解决方法并非仅仅是增加资金。
The team does not consist of only experienced players.  这个团队并非只由经验丰富的球员组成。



PHRASE->
consist largely of = 主要由…组成
consist primarily of = 主要由…组成
consist mainly of = 主要由…组成
consist entirely of = 完全由…组成
consist in = 在于
