glocalization:/ˌɡloʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |全球本土化，全球化本土化 | The adaptation of internationally marketed products or services to meet the needs of a particular local market.  Glocalization considers both global and local factors to create a product, service or strategy.  It's a portmanteau of "globalization" and "localization". | 例句：Many multinational corporations use glocalization strategies to tailor their products to local tastes and preferences.  许多跨国公司使用全球本土化战略来调整其产品以适应当地的口味和偏好。


例句：The company's glocalization strategy involved adapting its marketing materials to reflect local cultural nuances. 公司的全球本土化战略包括调整其营销材料以反映当地的文化细微之处。


近义词：localization, adaptation, internationalization


反义词：standardization, globalization (in its pure, unadapted sense)


词性: 名词
