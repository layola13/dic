miser:/ˈmaɪzər/| n.|守财奴，吝啬鬼|A miser is a person who hoards wealth and is unwilling to spend it, even on necessities.  They are characterized by extreme frugality and often unhappiness despite their riches.|He was a miser who lived in a dilapidated house despite his vast fortune. 他是一个守财奴，尽管家财万贯，却住在破败的房子里。|hoarder, skinflint, penny-pincher|spendthrift, philanthropist|名词
