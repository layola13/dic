garden:/ˈɡɑːrdən/ /ˈɡɑːrdn/| n.|花园，菜园|A piece of ground, often near a house, used for growing flowers, fruit, or vegetables.|例句：We have a beautiful garden with roses and lilies. 我们有一个种着玫瑰和百合花的花园。|例句：He spends his weekends tending his vegetable garden. 他周末都在照料他的菜园。|近义词：yard, orchard, plot|反义词：wilderness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He tends his garden every morning. = 他每天早上都侍弄他的花园。
They grow vegetables in their garden. = 他们在家里的花园里种植蔬菜。

一般过去时:
We visited a beautiful botanical garden yesterday. = 昨天我们参观了一个美丽的植物园。
She planted flowers in her garden last spring. = 去年春天她在花园里种了花。

现在完成时:
I have planted many flowers in my garden this year. = 今年我在花园里种了很多花。
They have harvested a lot of tomatoes from their garden. = 他们从花园里收获了很多西红柿。

过去完成时:
We had already watered the garden before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前我们已经浇过花园了。
They had planted the seeds a month before the flowers bloomed. = 花开之前一个月他们就已经播种了。


将来时:
I will plant some new rose bushes in my garden next week. = 下周我将在我的花园里种植一些新的玫瑰花丛。
They will harvest their crops from the garden in the autumn. = 秋天他们将从花园里收割庄稼。

虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger garden, I would grow more vegetables. = 如果我有一个更大的花园，我会种植更多的蔬菜。
I wish I had a garden where I could grow my own herbs. = 我希望我有一个花园，在那里我可以种植我自己的香草。

被动语态:
The garden was destroyed by the storm. = 花园被暴风雨摧毁了。
The vegetables are being watered by the gardener. = 蔬菜正在被园丁浇水。

疑问句:
Do you have a garden? = 你有花园吗？
Where is your garden located? = 你的花园在哪里？

否定句:
We don't have enough space for a garden. = 我们没有足够的空间来弄个花园。
She didn't water the garden yesterday. = 她昨天没有浇花园。
