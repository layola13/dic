shipowners:/ˈʃɪpˌoʊnərz/| n.|船主；船东|The people or companies that own ships.  They are responsible for the operation and maintenance of their vessels, often employing crew and managing the commercial aspects of shipping.|例句：The shipowners are facing increasing pressure to comply with new environmental regulations.  船东们正面临越来越大的压力，需要遵守新的环保法规。|例句：Many shipowners are investing in more fuel-efficient ships to reduce their operating costs. 许多船东正在投资更省油的船舶以降低运营成本。|近义词：ship owners, vessel owners|反义词：ship charterers (租船人)
