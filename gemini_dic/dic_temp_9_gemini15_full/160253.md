pontile:/ˈpɒntaɪl/| adj.|桥状的；桥形的；|relating to or resembling a bridge or a small bridge.  A pontile structure is characterized by its arched or bridge-like form. |例句：The pontile arch of the aqueduct was a marvel of engineering.  渡槽的桥形拱门是工程的奇迹。The microscope slide was supported by a small, pontile structure. 显微镜载玻片由一个小的桥状结构支撑。|近义词：bridge-like, arched, spanning|反义词：flat, unbridged
