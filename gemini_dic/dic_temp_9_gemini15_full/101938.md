islets:/ˈaɪləts/ | n. |小岛，小群岛 |A small island, especially one of a group of islands.  Islets are often found near larger landmasses or in archipelagos. |例句：The turquoise waters lapped gently against the tiny islets.  碧绿的海水轻轻拍打着那些小岛。

例句：Several islets dotted the calm bay.  几个小岛点缀着平静的海湾。


近义词：islet, small island, isle, cay


反义词：continent, mainland


词性:名词


词源:  The word "islet" comes from the Old English "iglet," a diminutive of "ieg," meaning "island."  The "-let" suffix indicates smallness.
