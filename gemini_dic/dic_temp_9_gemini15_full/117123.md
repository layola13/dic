meanderer's:/miˈændərərz/| n. |蜿蜒而行的人（的）|The word "meanderer's" is the possessive form of the noun "meanderer," referring to something belonging to or associated with a person who meanders.  A meanderer is someone who follows a winding course; someone who wanders aimlessly. |The meanderer's path was barely visible through the overgrown vegetation.蜿蜒而行者的道路几乎被茂密的植被遮挡了。The meanderer's journal detailed his journey through the countryside.蜿蜒而行者的日记详细记录了他穿越乡村的旅程。|wanderer, rambler, rover|straight-line traveler, direct-route taker|名词

一般现在时:
The river meanders through the valley. = 河流蜿蜒流过山谷。
The hiker meanders along the trail. = 徒步旅行者沿着小路漫步。


一般过去时:
The stream meandered lazily towards the sea. = 小溪懒洋洋地蜿蜒流向大海。
They meandered through the streets of the old town. = 他们在老城区的街道上漫步。


现在完成时:
The river has meandered for centuries, carving its path through the rock. = 这条河蜿蜒流淌了几个世纪，在岩石中刻下了它的路径。
We have meandered through many different paths in life. = 我们在生活中走过了许多不同的道路。


过去完成时:
The river had meandered for miles before reaching the ocean. = 这条河在到达大海之前已经蜿蜒流淌了数英里。
They had meandered through the forest for hours before finding their way out. = 他们在森林里漫步了几个小时才找到出路。


将来时:
The hikers will meander along the coast tomorrow. = 徒步旅行者明天将在沿海漫步。
The river will meander its way to the sea. = 这条河将蜿蜒流向大海。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would meander through the countryside. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在乡间漫步。
I wish I could meander through those beautiful fields. = 我希望我能漫步在那些美丽的田野里。


被动语态:
The path was meandered by many travelers over the years. = 这条小路多年来被许多旅行者走过。 (This example is less natural;  "meander" is generally used actively.)


疑问句:
Does the river meander through the town? = 这条河蜿蜒穿过小镇吗？
Will they meander through the park? = 他们会在公园里漫步吗？


否定句:
The road doesn't meander; it's straight. = 这条路不蜿蜒；它是笔直的。
They didn't meander; they walked directly to their destination. = 他们没有漫步；他们直接走到目的地。
