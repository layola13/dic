exhaustment: /ɪɡˈzɔːstmənt/ | n. | 精疲力竭；耗尽；衰竭 | The state of being extremely tired and worn out, both physically and mentally.  It often implies a prolonged period of exertion or stress. |  例句：After weeks of working overtime, she felt complete exhaustion.  几周加班后，她感到精疲力竭。

例句：The long journey led to the exhaustion of the travelers. 漫长的旅程导致旅行者精疲力竭。


近义词：fatigue, tiredness, weariness, depletion, prostration | 反义词：energy, vigor, vitality


词源:  Derived from the verb "exhaust," meaning to drain completely of energy or resources.  The suffix "-ment" indicates a state or condition.  Ultimately from Old French "exhauster" (to empty, drain), from Latin "exhaurire" (to draw out, drain).
