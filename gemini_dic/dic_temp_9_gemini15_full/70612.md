filming:/ˈfɪlmɪŋ/| n. v.|拍摄，电影制作|The act of making a film or recording a video.  It can refer to the process itself or the resulting footage.|例句：The filming of the movie took place in London.  伦敦进行了这部电影的拍摄。

The filming was delayed due to bad weather.  由于天气恶劣，拍摄被推迟了。|近义词：shooting, recording, production|反义词：editing, post-production


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crew is filming a new action movie. = 剧组正在拍摄一部新的动作片。
They film documentaries about wildlife. = 他们拍摄关于野生动物的纪录片。

一般过去时:
We filmed the entire scene in one take. = 我们一次性拍摄了整个场景。
They filmed a music video yesterday. = 他们昨天拍摄了一个音乐视频。

现在完成时:
She has filmed several award-winning documentaries. = 她已经拍摄了几部获奖的纪录片。
They have filmed the majority of the scenes. = 他们已经拍摄了大部分场景。

过去完成时:
They had filmed the first act before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经拍摄了第一幕。
We had filmed all the exterior shots by sunset. = 日落之前，我们已经拍摄完了所有的外景镜头。

将来时:
We will film the next scene tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将拍摄下一场戏。
They will be filming in the mountains next week. = 他们下周将在山上拍摄。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have filmed a longer version. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会拍摄一个更长的版本。
I wish we could have filmed the entire concert. = 我真希望我们能拍摄整个音乐会。

被动语态:
The movie was filmed on location. = 这部电影是在外景地拍摄的。
The scenes are being filmed in a studio. = 场景正在工作室拍摄。

疑问句:
Are they still filming the commercial? = 他们还在拍摄广告吗？
Where is the next scene being filmed? = 下一场戏在哪里拍摄？

否定句:
They didn't film the interview. = 他们没有拍摄采访。
We haven't filmed the opening sequence yet. = 我们还没有拍摄开场序列。
