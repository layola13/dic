gameful:/ˈɡeɪmfʊl/ | adj. |  充满乐趣的；游戏化的；以游戏为中心的 |  Characterized by or relating to the qualities or principles of games; playful and engaging.  It often implies using game mechanics or design principles to make something more enjoyable or effective. |例句：The gameful learning approach made the lesson much more engaging for students. 游戏化的学习方法使课程对学生更具吸引力。

The company implemented a gameful system to incentivize employees. 公司实施了一个游戏化的系统来激励员工。|近义词：playful, engaging, fun, enjoyable, gamified |反义词：dull, boring, tedious, uninteresting, serious
