salesman:/ˈseɪlzˌmæn/| n. |推销员，售货员 |A salesman is a person whose job is to sell goods or services.  |例句：He's a very successful salesman for a large car company. 他是大型汽车公司非常成功的推销员。|例句：The salesman showed me several different models of washing machines. 售货员给我展示了几种不同型号的洗衣机。|近义词：sales representative, vendor, retailer |反义词：customer, buyer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The salesman shows us the new product. = 推销员向我们展示了新产品。
He sells cars for a living. = 他靠卖车为生。

一般过去时:
The salesman helped me choose a suit. = 售货员帮我选了一套西装。
He sold all his stock yesterday. = 他昨天卖掉了所有的存货。

现在完成时:
He has sold over 100 cars this year. = 他今年已经卖出了100多辆汽车。
The company has employed many new salesmen recently. = 公司最近雇佣了许多新的推销员。

过去完成时:
They had already sold out of tickets before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经把票卖完了。
He had been a salesman for five years before he started his own business. = 在他创业之前，他已经做了五年的推销员。

将来时:
The salesman will contact you next week. = 销售员下周会和你联系。
They will sell their house next year. = 他们明年将出售他们的房子。

虚拟语气:
If I were a salesman, I would be more confident. = 如果我是个推销员，我会更有信心。
I wish I had been a better salesman in my younger years. = 我真希望我年轻的时候是个更好的推销员。

被动语态:
The product was sold out quickly. = 产品很快售罄。
Many new cars are sold every day. = 每天都卖出许多新车。

疑问句:
Is he a good salesman? = 他是一个好的推销员吗？
How many products did the salesman sell today? = 今天推销员卖出了多少产品？

否定句:
He is not a very good salesman. = 他不是一个很好的推销员。
The salesman didn't convince me to buy the product. = 推销员没有说服我购买该产品。
