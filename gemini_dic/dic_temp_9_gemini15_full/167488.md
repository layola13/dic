preventuring:/prɪˈvɛntʃərɪŋ/| n. |预防性冒险；预防性探险|  A proactive approach to risk management involving calculated ventures to mitigate future problems or capitalize on opportunities before they become critical. It involves identifying potential problems and taking steps to prevent them before they occur, even if it requires some level of risk. | Example: The company's preventuring strategy in renewable energy sources proved highly successful, securing their market position before competitors could catch up.  公司在可再生能源方面的预防性冒险策略证明非常成功，在竞争对手赶上之前就巩固了其市场地位。|近义词：proactive risk management, preemptive action, forward-thinking strategy|反义词：reactive strategy, risk avoidance, neglecting precaution|名词
