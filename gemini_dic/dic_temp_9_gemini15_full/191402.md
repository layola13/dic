scheme:/skiːm/| n. v.|计划；方案；阴谋|A scheme is a plan or system for doing or achieving something.  It can also refer to a clever but often dishonest plan. |例句：He devised a scheme to make money quickly. 他设计了一个快速赚钱的计划。

例句：Their scheme to defraud the company was discovered. 他们诈骗公司的阴谋被发现了。


近义词：plan, project, plot, design|反义词：chance, accident, haphazard


词源：源自古英语 *scēma，源于拉丁语 schema，意为“形状，形式”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He schemes to get ahead. = 他正在策划着往上爬。
They scheme against their rivals. = 他们正在策划对付他们的对手。


一般过去时:
She schemed to win the election. = 她策划赢得选举。
He schemed and plotted his revenge. = 他策划并密谋了他的报复。


现在完成时:
They have schemed for years to achieve this goal. = 他们为了实现这个目标已经策划多年了。
We have schemed and planned this surprise party for months. = 我们已经策划这个惊喜派对好几个月了。


过去完成时:
He had schemed to get the promotion before he was transferred. = 他在他被调动之前就策划着升职。
They had schemed against him for a long time before he finally realized it. = 在他最终意识到之前，他们已经策划对付他很长时间了。


将来时:
She will scheme to get what she wants. = 她将会耍手段得到她想要的东西。
They will scheme and plot to overthrow the government. = 他们将密谋策划推翻政府。


虚拟语气:
If he had schemed less, he might have had a better life. = 如果他少耍些手段，他可能会过得更好。
I wish I hadn't schemed to get ahead; it made me unhappy. = 我真希望我没有耍手段往上爬；那让我很不开心。


被动语态:
The scheme was devised by a group of experts. = 这个计划是由一群专家设计的。
The scheme was quickly exposed as a fraud. = 这项计划很快就被揭露为骗局。


疑问句:
What scheme are you working on? = 你在策划什么计划？
What scheme did they use to achieve their goal? = 他们用什么计划来实现他们的目标？


否定句:
He didn't scheme to hurt anyone. = 他并没有策划伤害任何人。
They didn't scheme; they worked hard and honestly. = 他们没有耍手段；他们努力工作，并且诚实。
