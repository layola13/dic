richly:/ˈrɪtʃli/ | adv. | 丰富地；充分地；豪华地 | Abundantly; lavishly; opulently.  It describes something done to a great extent or degree, often with a sense of abundance, luxury, or extravagance. | 例句：The garden was richly planted with colorful flowers. 花园里种满了色彩鲜艳的花朵，非常丰富。

The cake was richly decorated with frosting and chocolate. 蛋糕上装饰着丰富的糖霜和巧克力。


近义词：abundantly, lavishly, opulently, luxuriously, profusely

反义词：scantly, meagerly, poorly, sparsely


词源:  The adverb "richly" derives from the adjective "rich," ultimately from Old English *rīċ, meaning "powerful," "mighty," or "wealthy."  The sense of material wealth evolved, and then the metaphorical extensions to abundance and luxuriousness followed.
