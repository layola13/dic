paprika:/ˈpæprɪkə/|/ˈpɑːprɪkɑː/| n. |红辣椒粉；红椒|A powdered spice made from dried red peppers, varying in color and pungency depending on the type of pepper used.  It's commonly used in Hungarian cuisine and adds a vibrant color and flavor to many dishes.|The goulash was richly flavored with paprika.|近义词：red pepper powder, chili powder|反义词：无
