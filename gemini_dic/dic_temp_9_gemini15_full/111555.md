lobbyism:/ˈlɒbiɪzəm/| n. |游说活动；游说行为|The practice and activity of lobbying; the act of attempting to influence decisions made by officials in a government or other institution.  Lobbying is often associated with attempts to influence legislation, regulation, or policy.  It can involve direct contact with officials, public relations campaigns, or contributions to political campaigns.|例句：The bill faced strong lobbyism from environmental groups. 这项法案面临着环保组织的强烈游说。

例句：Excessive lobbyism can undermine the democratic process. 过度的游说活动可能会破坏民主进程。


近义词：advocacy, pressure, influence peddling

反义词：opposition, resistance


词性:名词
