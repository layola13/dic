nonshrinkingly:/ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkɪŋli/| adv.|不收缩地，不缩减地 |  Not shrinking; without shrinking or reduction.  It implies a steadfastness or resistance to reduction in size, amount, or intensity. |例句：The company's market share grew nonshrinkingly throughout the economic downturn.  这家公司的市场份额在整个经济衰退期间持续增长。


The economy expanded nonshrinkingly despite the global crisis.  尽管面临全球危机，经济仍在持续扩张。


近义词：steadfastly, constantly, unyieldingly, persistently


反义词：shrinkingly, dwindlingly, decreasingly


词性：副词
