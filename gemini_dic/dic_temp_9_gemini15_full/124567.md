mourn:/mɔːrn/| v.|哀悼，悲伤|To feel or express great sadness, especially when someone has died.|例句：The family mourned the loss of their beloved pet.  家人为失去心爱的宠物而悲伤。|例句：She mourned the passing of her grandmother for many months. 她为祖母去世哀悼了几个月。|近义词：grieve, lament, grieve over|反义词：celebrate, rejoice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mourns his father every day. = 他每天都为他父亲哀悼。
They mourn the loss of their home. = 他们为失去家园而悲伤。

一般过去时:
She mourned the death of her friend. = 她为朋友的去世而哀悼。
We mourned the end of the summer. = 我们为夏天的结束而悲伤。

现在完成时:
They have mourned the loss of their jobs for weeks. = 他们为失去工作已经哀悼了几周了。
She has mourned the passing of her dog for months. = 她为爱犬的去世哀悼了几个月。


过去完成时:
He had mourned the loss of his wife for many years before he remarried. = 在他再婚之前，他已经为他妻子的去世哀悼多年了。
They had mourned the end of their childhood before realizing how much they missed it. = 在他们意识到自己有多怀念童年之前，他们已经为童年的结束而悲伤了。

将来时:
She will mourn the loss of her grandfather for a long time. = 她将为祖父的去世哀悼很长时间。
They will mourn the destruction of the forest for generations. = 他们将为森林的破坏哀悼几代人。


虚拟语气:
If I had known he was going to die, I would have mourned him properly. = 如果我知道他要死了，我就会好好地为他哀悼。
I wish I hadn't been so angry; I would not have mourned his passing so coldly. = 我真希望我当时没有那么生气；我不会那样冷淡地哀悼他的去世。


被动语态:
The loss of the soldiers was mourned throughout the country. = 整个国家都在为士兵的牺牲而哀悼。
The death of the famous artist was deeply mourned by the art community. = 艺术界的成员们对著名艺术家的去世深感悲痛。


疑问句:
Do you mourn the loss of your childhood? = 你会怀念你的童年吗？
Will they mourn the lost opportunities? = 他们会为失去的机会感到惋惜吗？


否定句:
He did not mourn the loss of the old house. = 他并没有为旧房子的损失而悲伤。
They didn't mourn the end of the relationship. = 他们并没有为这段关系的结束而悲伤。


PHRASE->
mourn the dead = 为死者哀悼
mourn a loss = 为损失而悲伤
mourn one's fate = 悲叹自己的命运
mourn silently = 默默哀悼
mourn deeply = 深切哀悼
