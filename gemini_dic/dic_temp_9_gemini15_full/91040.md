husklike:/ˈhʌsklaɪk/ | adj. |像谷壳的；干燥无味的；无生气的|Resembling a husk; dry, withered, and lifeless.  It suggests something that is the outer shell or covering, lacking substance or vitality. |The old house stood husklike against the stormy sky.  这座老房子在暴风雨的天空下显得干枯破败。The husklike remains of the plant offered little nourishment. 植物干枯的残骸几乎没有营养。|近义词：dry, withered, lifeless, barren,  chaffy|反义词：juicy, succulent, vibrant, lively
