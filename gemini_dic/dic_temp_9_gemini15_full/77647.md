gelatinously:/dʒɛləˈtɪnəsli/| adj.|胶状的；胶冻似的；摇晃的；不稳定地|Having the properties or consistency of gelatin; trembling or quivering like jelly.  The word evokes a sense of shapelessness, slow movement, and instability.|例句：The jellyfish pulsed gelatinously through the water. 水母在水中胶状地搏动。 The  newly formed government seemed gelatinously unstable. 新成立的政府显得摇摇欲坠。|近义词：jellylike, quivering, wobbly, shaky|反义词：firm, solid, stable, rigid
