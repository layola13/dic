homothermous: /hoʊmoʊˈθɜːrməs/ | adj. | 同温的；恒温的 | Maintaining a constant body temperature regardless of the surrounding environment.  This term is primarily used in biology and zoology to describe animals that regulate their internal body temperature.  | Example:  Birds are homothermous animals, maintaining a stable internal temperature despite external fluctuations.  鸟类是恒温动物，即使外部温度波动，也能保持稳定的体温。 | 近义词：homeothermic, warm-blooded | 反义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded |
