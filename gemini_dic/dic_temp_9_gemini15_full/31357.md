characterizing: /ˈkærəktəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 描绘…的特性；使具有…的特征 |  Describing the distinctive features or qualities of someone or something.  The act of characterizing something involves highlighting its key attributes and making it recognizable. |

例句：

The author skillfully characterizing the protagonist's inner turmoil. = 作者巧妙地刻画了主人公内心的动荡。

His aggressive behavior characterizing him as a bully. = 他具有侵略性的行为使他显得像个恶霸。


近义词：describing, portraying, depicting, defining, identifying

反义词：misrepresenting, obscuring, concealing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist is characterizing the new material. = 科学家正在描述这种新材料的特性。
We are characterizing the patient's symptoms. = 我们正在描述病人的症状。

一般过去时:
The historian characterized the era as one of great upheaval. = 历史学家将那个时代描述为一个动荡的时代。
She characterized his actions as irresponsible. = 她把他的行为描述为不负责任的。

现在完成时:
Researchers have characterized several new species of bacteria. = 研究人员已经描述了几种新的细菌物种。
They have characterized the disease's progression. = 他们已经描述了这种疾病的进展。

过去完成时:
The critics had characterized the play as a masterpiece before its opening night. = 批评家在开幕之夜之前就把这部戏描述为杰作。
Scientists had characterized the virus's genetic structure before developing a vaccine. = 科学家在研制疫苗之前已经描述了病毒的基因结构。


将来时:
They will characterize the effects of the new drug on patients. = 他们将描述这种新药对患者的影响。
We will characterize the chemical reaction in detail. = 我们将详细描述化学反应。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could characterize the phenomenon more accurately. = 如果我们有更多的数据，我们就能更准确地描述这种现象。
I wish I could characterize the music more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地描述这音乐。


被动语态:
The painting is characterized by its vibrant colors. = 这幅画的特点是色彩鲜艳。
The experiment was characterized by its rigorous methodology. = 这个实验的特点是方法严谨。


疑问句:
How would you characterize the current political climate? = 你如何形容当前的政治气候？
How can we best characterize this unusual behavior? = 我们如何最好地描述这种异常行为？


否定句:
The study failed to characterize the long-term effects of the treatment. = 这项研究未能描述该治疗的长期影响。
I cannot adequately characterize the complexity of the issue. = 我无法充分描述这个问题的复杂性。
