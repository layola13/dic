luv:/lʌv/| n. |爱，热爱 |Love is a powerful emotion. 爱是一种强大的情感。|近义词：affection, fondness |反义词：hate, dislike |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I love you. = 我爱你。
She loves her job. = 她热爱她的工作。


一般过去时:
I loved her deeply. = 我深深地爱着她。
They loved living in the countryside. = 他们喜欢住在乡下。


现在完成时:
I have loved you since the first day I met you. = 从我第一次见到你那天起我就爱上你了。
She has loved painting for many years. = 她多年来一直热爱绘画。


过去完成时:
I had loved him for years before he finally noticed me. = 在他最终注意到我之前，我已经爱了他好多年了。
They had loved each other since childhood. = 他们从小就相爱了。


将来时:
I will always love you. = 我会永远爱你。
He will love his new car. = 他会爱上他的新车。


虚拟语气:
If I loved her, I would tell her. = 如果我爱她，我会告诉她。
I wish I loved him more. = 我希望我更爱他。


被动语态:
(Love is rarely used in the passive voice, as it is usually a feeling expressed actively.)  爱很少用于被动语态，因为它通常是主动表达的一种情感。
He is loved by many people. = 他被许多人爱戴。（较为罕见用法）


疑问句:
Do you love me? = 你爱我吗？
Does she love her work? = 她爱她的工作吗？


否定句:
I don't love him anymore. = 我不再爱他了。
She doesn't love spicy food. = 她不喜欢辣的食物。


PHRASE->
fall in love = 爱上
be in love = 相爱
love at first sight = 一见钟情
make love = 做爱
for the love of = 为了…起见
