leucoline:/ˈluːkəlaɪn/| n. |白碱；喹啉|A colorless, oily liquid, a mixture of quinolines, formerly obtained from coal tar.  It is now largely synthesized.|例句：Leucoline was once a common byproduct of coal tar distillation. 白碱曾经是煤焦油蒸馏的常见副产物。

例句：The chemical properties of leucoline are similar to those of quinoline. 白碱的化学性质与喹啉相似。


近义词：quinoline (though not a perfect synonym as leucoline is a mixture)
反义词：无


词性：名词
