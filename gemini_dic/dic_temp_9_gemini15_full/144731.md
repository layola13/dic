over-again:/ˌoʊvər əˈɡen/| adv.| 再一次；反复地 |  To do something over again means to repeat it, often because it wasn't done correctly the first time or to emphasize the action. |例句：I had to do the entire project over again. 我不得不重新做整个项目。


例句：Let's try that over again. 我们再试一次吧。


近义词：again, anew, repeatedly


反义词：once, for the first time


词性: 副词
