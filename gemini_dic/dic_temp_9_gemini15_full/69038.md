feature:/ˈfiːtʃər/ | n. | 特征，特点；特写；容貌；(电影的)主要演员 | A distinctive attribute or aspect of something.  A prominent or important feature of something.  A newspaper or magazine article that focuses on a particular person, event, or topic. |例句：The main feature of the car is its fuel efficiency. 这辆车的主要特点是燃油效率高。

例句：The newspaper published a feature on the local elections.  报纸刊登了一篇关于地方选举的特写文章。


近义词：characteristic, trait, attribute, aspect, element | 反义词：background, insignificance


词源:  From Old French *feture, from Vulgar Latin *facturam, from Latin factura "making, form, shape," from facere "to do, make."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This house features a large garden. = 这栋房子有一个大花园。
The program features interviews with experts. = 这个节目有对专家的采访。


一般过去时:
The article featured the mayor's controversial policy. = 这篇文章以市长的有争议的政策为特色。
The film featured several award-winning actors. = 这部电影有几位获奖演员主演。


现在完成时:
Many films have featured this actor in leading roles. = 许多电影都让这位演员担任主角。
The magazine has featured articles on various environmental issues. = 这本杂志刊登过关于各种环境问题的文章。


过去完成时:
The previous edition had featured a similar topic. = 上一版曾刊登过类似的主题。
Before the interview, they had featured the guest's biography. = 在采访之前，他们已经介绍了客人的生平。


将来时:
The next issue will feature an interview with the president. = 下一期将刊登对总统的采访。
The conference will feature presentations from leading scientists. = 会议将有来自主要科学家的演讲。


虚拟语气:
If the movie featured a more compelling plot, it would be more successful. = 如果电影的情节更引人入胜，它会更成功。
I wish the article featured more detailed information. = 我希望这篇文章能提供更详细的信息。


被动语态:
The problem was featured prominently in the news. = 这个问题在新闻中得到了突出的报道。
His achievements are being featured in a documentary. = 他的成就正在一部纪录片中被报道。


疑问句:
What features does this software offer? = 这个软件有什么功能？
Does the article feature any new information? = 这篇文章有提供任何新信息吗？


否定句:
The car doesn't feature any advanced safety systems. = 这辆车没有任何先进的安全系统。
The presentation didn't feature any visual aids. = 这次演示没有使用任何视觉辅助工具。
