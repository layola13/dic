coursed:/kɔːrst/| v. | 奔流；流动；追逐；猎杀；修建水渠；开辟道路 | To flow or run in a course; to pursue; to hunt; to form channels or paths in. |例句：The river coursed through the valley.  河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。|例句：The hunters coursed the fox across the fields. 猎人们在田野里追逐狐狸。|近义词：flowed, streamed, pursued, chased |反义词： stagnated, halted


USAGE->

The river courses through the valley. = 河流流经山谷。
The dogs course the rabbits. = 狗追逐兔子。

The water coursed swiftly down the mountain. = 水迅速地从山上流下来。
The hounds coursed the deer through the woods. = 猎犬在树林里追逐鹿。

The river has coursed through this valley for centuries. = 这条河已经流经这个山谷几个世纪了。
The farmer has coursed the field to improve drainage. = 农民已经开辟了田地以改善排水。

PHRASE->
course through = 流经

词源:

源自古法语 *courser，意为“奔跑”，最终源自拉丁语 currere，意为“奔跑”。  这个词根也与“current”（电流，趋势）和“occur”（发生）相关。
