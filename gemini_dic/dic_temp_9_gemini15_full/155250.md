phalangister:/fəˈlæŋdʒɪstər/| n. |指猴|A small nocturnal lemur of Madagascar, characterized by its long, thin middle finger used for extracting insects from crevices.  |例句：The phalangister is an arboreal primate found only in Madagascar. 指猴是一种只在马达加斯加发现的树栖灵长类动物。

例句：The long, thin middle finger of the phalangister is perfectly adapted for its insectivorous diet. 指猴细长的手指非常适合其食虫的饮食习惯。
