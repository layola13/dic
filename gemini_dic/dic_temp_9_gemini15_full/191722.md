sciatic:/saɪˈætɪk/| adj. n.|坐骨神经的；坐骨神经痛|relating to the sciatic nerve or its branches.  Sciatica is pain that follows the pathway of the sciatic nerve, which branches from the lower back through the buttocks and down the legs.|例句：She suffers from sciatic pain. 她患有坐骨神经痛。  The sciatic nerve is the longest nerve in the human body. 坐骨神经是人体最长的神经。|近义词：neuralgic, ischialgic|反义词:None
