词源：
The word "repetition" comes from the Old French word "repetition," which itself derives from the Latin word "repetitio," a combination of the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "petere" (to seek, to strive for, to go to).  Therefore, the word literally means "a seeking again," or "a repeating."
