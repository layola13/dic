pretorium:/prɪˈtɔːriəm/| n.|将领营帐；罗马帝国皇帝的官邸；总督府|The pretorium was the general's tent, and later, the official residence of Roman emperors and governors. It was often a large and impressive building, serving as the center of military and administrative power.|The Roman governor resided in the pretorium. 罗马总督居住在总督府。 The pretorium was a symbol of Roman authority. 总督府是罗马权威的象征。|palace, headquarters|cottage, shack|名词
