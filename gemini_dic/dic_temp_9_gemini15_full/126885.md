/nɑːrk/| n. v.| 瘾君子；告密者；（非正式）毒品；告发；|  A person who is addicted to narcotics; an informer; a police officer who works undercover to catch drug dealers.  The verb form means to inform on someone; to act as an informer. |例句：He was a narc and betrayed his friends to the police. 他是个告密者，把朋友出卖给了警察。  The police narc infiltrated the drug ring.  卧底警察渗透进了贩毒集团。|近义词：informer, snitch, stool pigeon, addict|反义词：law-abiding citizen
