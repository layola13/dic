resubstantiates:/ˌriːsʌbˈstænʃieɪt/| vt.|重新证实；使…恢复实质|To establish or support (something) again; to restore the substance or essence of something.|例句：The new evidence resubstantiates the original claim. 新证据再次证实了最初的主张。|例句：The renovation project resubstantiates the historical significance of the building.  翻新工程使这座建筑的历史意义得以恢复。|近义词：reconfirm, reaffirm, corroborate, substantiate|反义词：refute, disprove, invalidate
