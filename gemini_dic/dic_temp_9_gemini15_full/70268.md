fiddle:/ˈfɪdl/ | n., v. | 小提琴；胡乱摆弄；欺骗 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "fiddle" refers to a stringed musical instrument, similar to a violin.  As a verb, it means to play the fiddle, to manipulate something clumsily or aimlessly, or to cheat or deceive.

例句:
He plays the fiddle beautifully. = 他拉小提琴拉得很漂亮。

She was fiddling with her keys. = 她正在摆弄她的钥匙。

They fiddled the accounts to hide their losses. = 他们篡改账目以掩盖损失。


近义词:
(noun) violin, stringed instrument
(verb) tamper, tinker, manipulate, cheat, swindle

反义词:
(verb)  honestly, accurately, truthfully (depending on context)


词性:
Noun, Verb
