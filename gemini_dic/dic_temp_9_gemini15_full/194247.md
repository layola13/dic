self-mortifications:/ˌsɛlfˌmɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |自我折磨；苦行；禁欲|The act of inflicting pain or suffering on oneself, often for religious or spiritual reasons.  It involves denying oneself physical comforts or pleasures. |例句：His self-mortifications included fasting and sleeping on a hard bed. 他的自我折磨包括禁食和睡硬板床。

例句：Through years of self-mortifications, the monk achieved a state of spiritual enlightenment. 通过多年的自我折磨，这位僧人达到了精神上的开悟。


近义词：penance, asceticism, self-denial, austerity |反义词：indulgence, self-indulgence, hedonism |词性:noun
