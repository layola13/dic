dehumanization: /ˌdiːˌhjuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非人化；没有人性；兽化 | The process of depriving a person or group of positive human qualities.  It can involve treating someone as if they are less than human, or as an object rather than a person. This can manifest in various ways, including through violence, oppression, exploitation, or denial of basic rights. | Example: The dehumanization of prisoners of war was evident in the brutal conditions of their captivity.  战俘的非人化在他们残酷的囚禁条件中显而易见。 |近义词：dehumanisation, objectification, degradation, brutalization | 反义词：humanization, empathy, compassion
