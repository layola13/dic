remingling: /ˌriːˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/ | v. |重新混合；再次混合 | The act of mingling again; mixing together once more.  It implies a previous mingling that was perhaps separated or interrupted, and now the process is being repeated. |例句：After the intermission, the guests resumed their remingling, chatting animatedly.  在中场休息之后，客人们又重新开始互相交谈，气氛热烈。

例句：The separated ingredients were carefully remingled to create the perfect texture. 分离的成分被小心地重新混合，以创造完美的质地。


近义词：remixing, recombining, reblending | 反义词：separating, segregating | 词性：动词
