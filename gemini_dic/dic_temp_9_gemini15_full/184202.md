resoftened:/ˌriːˈsɒftənd/ | adj. | 再软化了的；重新变软的 |  Describes something that has become soft again after having been hardened or stiff.  This word is not commonly used and is essentially a combination of "re-" (again) and "softened" (made soft).  It implies a process of softening that has occurred a second time. |The previously hardened clay was resoftened with water.  =  先前硬化的粘土用清水重新软化了。


The resoftened butter spread easily on the bread. = 重新变软的黄油很容易涂抹在面包上。|近义词: softened, re-moistened |反义词: hardened, solidified |形容词
