/ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃɪz/ | v. |过度惩罚，严惩|To punish someone more severely than is necessary or fair.  This implies an excessive or disproportionate punishment.|The judge overpunishes minor offenders, leading to overcrowding in prisons.  法官对轻微罪犯处以过重的刑罚，导致监狱人满为患。| severely punish, excessively punish | leniently punish, pardon | 动词
