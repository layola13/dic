gitano:/xiˈtɑːnoʊ/|/ˈɡitænoʊ/| n. |吉普赛人；流浪者 | A Gitano is a member of the Roma people, an ethnic group originally from the Indian subcontinent.  The term is often used in Spain and other parts of the Spanish-speaking world.  It can sometimes carry negative connotations depending on context. | Example:  Many Gitanos are known for their vibrant culture and musical traditions. 许多吉普赛人以其充满活力的文化和音乐传统而闻名。|近义词：Roma, Romany, Gypsy (Note: "Gypsy" can be considered offensive)|反义词：None.
