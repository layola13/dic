proofers: /ˈpruːfərz/ | n. |校对员，校稿员 | People who check for errors in written or printed material before publication.  | The proofers caught several typos before the book went to print. 校对员在书出版前发现了几个错别字。 | editors, revisers, copyeditors |  (无明确反义词，反义词可根据语境选择，例如：writers, authors) | 名词
