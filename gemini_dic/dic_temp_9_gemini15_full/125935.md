mushroom:/ˈmʌʃruːm/ | n. |蘑菇，菌类|A fleshy fungus with a stem and a cap, typically growing in damp places.|例句：We found many mushrooms growing in the forest. 我们在森林里发现许多蘑菇。|例句：The mushroom soup was delicious. 蘑菇汤很好喝。|近义词：fungus, toadstool|反义词：无

Mushrooms grow best in damp environments. = 蘑菇在潮湿的环境中生长最好。
