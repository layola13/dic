fellowess: /ˈfɛloʊɛs/ | n. | 女学者，女会员 | A woman who is a member of a learned society or professional organization; a female fellow.  |She was the first fellowess elected to the Royal Society. 她是有史以来第一位当选皇家学会会员的女性。|近义词：female member, associate, scholar | 反义词： (no direct antonym, context-dependent)
