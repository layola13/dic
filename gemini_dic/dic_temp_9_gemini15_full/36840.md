colonizationist:/ˌkɑːlənaɪˈzeɪʃənɪst/ | n. |殖民主义者 | A person who supports or practices colonization.  Someone who believes in or promotes the policy of establishing settlements in a new territory and imposing their own culture and government. | Example:  The colonizationists imposed their language and religion on the native population. 殖民主义者强迫当地居民接受他们的语言和宗教。  The actions of the colonizationists led to widespread resentment and conflict. 殖民主义者的行为导致了广泛的怨恨和冲突。|近义词：colonialist, imperialist, expansionist |反义词：anti-colonialist, independence advocate
