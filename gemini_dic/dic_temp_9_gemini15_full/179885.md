ref:/ref/| n. |参考，参照；参考文献；参考书目| a thing that is referred to; something that provides information or evidence.  It can also refer to a specific source of information, such as a book, article, or website.|例句：Please see the attached document for further ref.  请参阅附件了解更多信息。|例句：The professor cited several refs in his lecture. 教授在演讲中引用了几篇参考文献。|近义词：reference, citation, source |反义词：original source (in the sense of being the primary, unreferenced source)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report refers to several studies. = 这份报告提到了几项研究。
This paragraph refers back to the previous point. = 本段落指的是前一点。

一般过去时:
The lawyer referred to the case files. = 律师查阅了卷宗。
She referred me to a specialist. = 她把我介绍给了一位专家。

现在完成时:
They have referred to this issue many times. = 他们多次提到这个问题。
He has referred the matter to the committee. = 他已将此事提交给委员会。

过去完成时:
The article had referred to earlier research. = 这篇文章提到了早期的研究。
She had referred to the book several times before. = 她之前已经多次提到过这本书。

将来时:
We will refer to this document later. = 我们稍后会参考这份文件。
They will refer the case to the higher court. = 他们会将此案提交到上级法院。

虚拟语气:
If I had referred to the manual, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我参考了手册，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had referred to the dictionary earlier. = 我希望我早点查字典。

被动语态:
The book is often referred to as a classic. = 这本书常被称为经典之作。
The matter was referred to a higher authority. = 此事已提交给更高一级主管。

疑问句:
Does this paragraph refer to anything specific? = 本段指的是什么具体内容？
What sources are referred to in your paper? = 你的论文中引用了哪些资料？

否定句:
The document doesn't refer to that specific issue. = 该文件没有提及那个具体问题。
He didn't refer to the original source. = 他没有参考原始资料。


PHRASE->
cross-reference = 交叉引用
in reference to = 关于；至于
with reference to = 关于；就…而言
for reference = 供参考
by reference = 通过引用
