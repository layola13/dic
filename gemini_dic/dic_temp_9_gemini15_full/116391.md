英文解析:
Relating to or denoting the mastoid process, a bony projection of the temporal bone behind the ear.  The mastoid process is important because it serves as an attachment point for several muscles and contains air cells connected to the middle ear.  Inflammation of the mastoid process (mastoiditis) is a serious condition requiring medical attention.


例句:

The doctor suspected mastoiditis based on the patient's symptoms.  医生根据病人的症状怀疑是乳突炎。

He experienced severe pain in the mastoid region. 他在乳突区域感到剧烈疼痛。


词源:
From Late Latin *mastoidēus, from Greek mastos "breast" + -eides "resembling" (referring to its resemblance to a nipple).
