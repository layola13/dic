newsman:/ˈnuːzmæn/| n.|新闻记者，新闻工作者|A male journalist or reporter who gathers and presents news.  |Example: The newsman rushed to the scene of the accident.  这位新闻记者迅速赶往事故现场。|近义词：journalist, reporter, newscaster|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "source" or "reader".|名词

The newsman reports on current events. = 这位新闻记者报道时事。
He often interviews important figures. = 他经常采访重要人物。


一般过去时:
The newsman reported the breaking news last night. = 这位新闻记者昨晚报道了突发新闻。
He interviewed the mayor yesterday. = 他昨天采访了市长。


现在完成时:
The newsman has covered many significant stories. = 这位新闻记者已经报道了许多重要的新闻。
He has interviewed several celebrities. = 他已经采访了几位名流。
