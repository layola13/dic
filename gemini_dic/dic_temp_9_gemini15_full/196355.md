/səˈrɪndʒ/ | n. | 注射器 | A syringe is a medical instrument used to inject or withdraw fluids from the body.  It typically consists of a cylindrical tube with a plunger at one end and a nozzle at the other.  | Example: The nurse used a syringe to administer the medication. 护士用注射器注射药物。 |近义词：injector, hypodermic needle |反义词：N/A
