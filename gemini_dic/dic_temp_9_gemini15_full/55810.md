divested:/daɪˈvɛstɪd/| v.|剥夺；脱去；使…摆脱；|to deprive someone of something, especially power, rights, or possessions; to take off or remove (clothing, etc.).|例句：The company divested itself of its unprofitable subsidiaries.  公司剥离了其无利可图的子公司。|例句：She divested herself of her heavy coat. 她脱掉了厚重的外套。|近义词：deprive, strip, shed, divest oneself of|反义词：invest, endow, clothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He divests himself of his responsibilities. = 他卸下了他的责任。
The company divests its assets regularly. = 公司定期剥离资产。


一般过去时:
They divested the criminal of his weapons. = 他们搜走了罪犯的武器。
The government divested the company of its license. = 政府吊销了该公司的执照。


现在完成时:
She has divested herself of all her worldly possessions. = 她已经放弃了她所有的世俗财产。
The organization has divested itself from harmful practices. = 该组织已经摆脱了有害的做法。


过去完成时:
He had divested himself of his power before his death. = 他死前已经放弃了他的权力。
The company had divested its holdings in the failing market. = 公司已经剥离了它在失败市场中的资产。


将来时:
They will divest their holdings in the near future. = 他们将在不久的将来剥离其资产。
The government will divest itself of its controlling stake in the company. = 政府将剥离其在公司中的控股权。


虚拟语气:
If the company divested itself of its debt, it would be more profitable. = 如果公司摆脱了债务，它就会更有盈利能力。
I wish I had divested myself of that bad investment sooner. = 我真希望我早点摆脱那笔糟糕的投资。


被动语态:
The criminal was divested of his rights. = 罪犯被剥夺了权利。
The company was divested of its assets by the court. = 该公司被法院剥夺了资产。


疑问句:
Has the company divested itself of its foreign operations? = 公司是否已经剥离了其海外业务？
Will they divest themselves of their harmful practices? = 他们会摆脱有害的做法吗？


否定句:
He did not divest himself of his authority. = 他没有放弃他的权力。
The company has not divested itself of its toxic assets. = 公司没有剥离其有毒资产。


PHRASE->
divest oneself of = 摆脱；放弃
divest of power = 剥夺权力
divest of rights = 剥夺权利
divest of assets = 剥离资产
divest of property = 剥夺财产
