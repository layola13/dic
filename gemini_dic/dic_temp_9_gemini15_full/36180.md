coffins:/ˈkɒfɪnz/| n. |棺材，灵柩 |A coffin is a box in which a dead body is buried or cremated.  |例句：They lowered the coffin into the grave.  他们把棺材放进坟墓。
例句：The coffin was made of polished mahogany. 这口棺材是用抛光的桃花心木做的。|近义词：casket, bier |反义词:None

词源:
The word "coffin" comes from the Old French word "cophin," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "kophinos," meaning "basket."  Initially, coffins were simple baskets; over time, the meaning evolved to include the more elaborate containers we know today.
