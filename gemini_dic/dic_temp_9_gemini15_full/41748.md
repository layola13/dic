corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/| adj.|波纹状的，瓦楞的；起皱的 | Having parallel ridges and grooves; wrinkled.  |例句：The cardboard box was corrugated.  纸板箱是波纹状的。

例句：Her brow was corrugated with worry.  她因担心而皱起了眉头。

近义词：ridged, furrowed, wrinkled, fluted

反义词：smooth, flat, unwrinkled


词源:
The word "corrugated" comes from the past participle of the verb "corrugate," which itself derives from the Latin word "corrugare."  "Corrugare" is formed from "cor," meaning "together" or "with," and "rugare," meaning "to wrinkle" or "to fold." Thus, the word literally means "wrinkled together."
