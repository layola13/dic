promote:/prəˈmoʊt/| vt.|促进；提升；推销；宣传|to further the progress of something; to raise someone to a higher position or rank; to advertise or make known|例句：The company is promoting a new product.  公司正在推广一种新产品。

例句：He was promoted to manager. 他被提升为经理。

近义词：advance, further, uplift, advertise, publicize|反义词：demote, hinder, obstruct, suppress

词源:源自古法语promouvoir，由前缀pro-（向前）和mouvoir（移动）构成，字面意思为“向前移动”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company promotes its products through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体推广其产品。
She promotes healthy eating habits. = 她提倡健康的饮食习惯。


一般过去时:
They promoted him to senior manager last year. = 他们去年将他提升为高级经理。
The government promoted economic growth through tax cuts. = 政府通过减税来促进经济增长。


现在完成时:
The campaign has promoted awareness of environmental issues. = 这场运动提高了人们对环境问题的认识。
They have promoted several employees this year. = 今年他们已经提升了几名员工。


过去完成时:
The company had promoted him twice before he resigned. = 他辞职前，公司已经提升过他两次。
She had promoted the event extensively before the launch. = 在活动启动之前，她已经做了广泛的宣传。


将来时:
They will promote her to a higher position next month. = 下个月他们将把她提升到更高的职位。
The company will promote its new product at the trade show. = 公司将在贸易展会上推广其新产品。


虚拟语气:
If they promoted me, I would be very happy. = 如果他们提升我，我会非常高兴。
I wish they would promote more women to leadership roles. = 我希望他们能提拔更多女性担任领导职务。


被动语态:
He was promoted to CEO last year. = 他去年被提升为首席执行官。
The new product is being promoted through television commercials. = 新产品正在通过电视广告进行推广。


疑问句:
Will they promote me this year? = 今年他们会提升我吗？
How do they plan to promote the new film? = 他们计划如何宣传这部新电影？


否定句:
The company didn't promote him because of his poor performance. = 由于他表现不佳，公司没有提升他。
They did not promote the event effectively. = 他们没有有效地宣传这次活动。
