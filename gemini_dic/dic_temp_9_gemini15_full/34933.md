cloakrooms: /ˈkləʊkruːmz/ | n. | 衣帽间；厕所 |  A room, typically in a public building, where people can leave coats, bags, and other belongings.  It often also includes toilets and washing facilities. |  例句：The cloakroom is located near the entrance of the theatre. 剧院入口附近是衣帽间。

例句：We left our coats in the cloakroom before the concert.  音乐会前我们把外套放在衣帽间。


近义词：restrooms, lavatories, toilets, washrooms


反义词：None


词性: noun
