offer:/ˈɔːfər/ |/ˈɒfər/| n., vt. |提供，提议，出价 |to present or propose (something) to someone for acceptance or rejection; to state one's willingness to do something. |例句：He offered me a job. 他给我提供了一份工作。  |例句：I offered to help her. 我主动提出帮助她。|近义词：propose, suggest, present, bid |反义词：demand, refuse, reject |名词、动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He offers his help to everyone. = 他向每个人提供帮助。
The company offers a wide range of products. = 公司提供各种各样的产品。

一般过去时:
She offered me a ride home. = 她主动提出送我回家。
They offered a generous donation to the charity. = 他们向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。

现在完成时:
He has offered several solutions to the problem. = 他已经为这个问题提出了几个解决方案。
They have offered their support to the victims. = 他们已经向受害者提供了支持。

过去完成时:
She had offered to help before I even asked. = 我还没开口她就主动提出帮忙了。
They had offered a counterproposal before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前提出了一份反建议。

将来时:
I will offer my assistance if needed. = 如果需要，我会提供我的帮助。
The company will offer a discount for early bird registrations. = 公司将为提前注册的人提供折扣。

虚拟语气:
I wish I had offered to help them. = 我真希望我当时主动提出帮助他们。
If he had offered a better price, I would have bought it. = 如果他出价更高，我就买了。

被动语态:
A scholarship was offered to him by the university. = 他获得了大学提供的奖学金。
Many opportunities were offered to the graduates. = 毕业生们得到了许多机会。

疑问句:
What did they offer you? = 他们给你提供了什么？
Will you offer any suggestions? = 你会提供任何建议吗？

否定句:
He didn't offer any explanation. = 他没有给出任何解释。
They refused the offer and offered no alternative. = 他们拒绝了这个提议，也没有提供其他方案。


PHRASE->
offer a job = 提供工作
offer a suggestion = 提供建议
offer a price = 出价
offer a solution = 提供解决方案
offer an apology = 道歉
