rationing:/ˈræʃənɪŋ/| n. |配给；定量供应 |The controlled distribution of scarce resources, goods, or services.  Rationing is often implemented during times of war or economic hardship to ensure fair distribution and prevent shortages. |Example: During World War II, the government implemented rationing of sugar, coffee, and gasoline.  二战期间，政府对糖、咖啡和汽油实行配给制。

|Example:  Food rationing was necessary to feed the population during the famine.  饥荒期间，粮食配给是必要的措施，以养活人民。|近义词：allocation, distribution, allowance |反义词：abundance, surplus |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government rations essential goods during wartime. = 政府在战时对必需品实行配给。
This restaurant rations its popular dishes to avoid running out. = 这家餐馆对受欢迎的菜品实行配给，以免卖完。


一般过去时:
They rationed food and fuel during the crisis. = 他们在那次危机期间对食物和燃料实行配给。
The store rationed the sale of certain items to prevent hoarding. = 商店对某些商品的销售实行配给，以防止囤积。


现在完成时:
We have rationed our resources carefully throughout the drought. = 在整个干旱时期，我们都小心地对资源进行了配给。
The country has rationed gasoline several times in its history. = 这个国家在其历史上曾多次对汽油实行配给。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, they had already rationed water supplies. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经对供水进行了配给。
By the time the war ended, they had been rationing food for five years. = 到战争结束时，他们已经对食物进行了五年的配给。


将来时:
The city will likely ration water if the drought continues. = 如果干旱持续下去，这座城市很可能会对水实行配给。
The company will ration its new product to increase demand. = 公司将对新产品实行配给，以提高需求。


虚拟语气:
If we had rationed our resources earlier, we wouldn't be in this situation now. = 如果我们早点对资源进行配给，现在就不会处于这种情况了。
It would have been wise to ration fuel before the long journey. = 在长途旅行之前对燃料进行配给本来是明智之举。


被动语态:
Fuel was rationed throughout the country. = 整个国家都对燃料实行配给。
Supplies are being rationed to ensure fairness. = 正在对物资进行配给以确保公平。


疑问句:
Will the government ration electricity this winter? = 政府今年冬天会对电力实行配给吗？
Is food being rationed in that region? = 在那个地区是否正在对食物实行配给？


否定句:
The government did not ration sugar during peacetime. = 政府在和平时期没有对糖实行配给。
They didn't ration supplies adequately during the emergency. = 他们在紧急情况下没有充分地对物资进行配给。


PHRASE->
food rationing 食品配给
fuel rationing 燃油配给
water rationing 水资源配给
rationing system 配给制度
strict rationing 严格的配给


词源:
源自古法语 *racion,  最终源自拉丁语 *ratio,  意思是“比例，份额”。  The meaning evolved to refer to the assigning of a fixed portion or share.
