countercommercials: /ˌkaʊntər kəˈmɜːrʃəlz/ | n. | 反广告 | Commercials that attempt to counteract the effects of advertising, often by presenting a contrasting viewpoint or providing information that challenges the claims made in the advertisements. They are often used by advocacy groups, non-profit organizations, or government agencies to promote public health, safety, or environmental awareness.  | Example:  Public service announcements (PSAs) are a common type of countercommercial that aim to raise awareness about important social issues. 公益广告（PSA）是一种常见的反广告，旨在提高人们对重要社会问题的认识。  The tobacco company's advertisement was followed by a countercommercial showing the devastating effects of smoking. 烟草公司的广告之后播放了一则反广告，展示了吸烟的破坏性后果。 | 近义词: public service announcement (PSA), counter-advertising, advocacy advertisement | 反义词: advertisement, commercial
