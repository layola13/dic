hew:/hjuː/| vt.|砍，劈，凿；切割；塑造|To cut or shape (something) with an axe, knife, or other tool.  The sculptor hewed the marble into a beautiful statue.|近义词：chop, hack, carve|反义词：build, construct, create


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sculptor hews the stone. = 雕塑家正在雕刻石头。
He hews wood for the fire. = 他正在砍柴生火。

一般过去时:
They hewed the path through the jungle. = 他们砍出一条穿过丛林的路。
The farmer hewed the trees down. = 农民砍倒了树木。

现在完成时:
The artist has hewed many statues from marble. = 这位艺术家已经用大理石雕刻了许多雕像。
They have hewed a new path through the mountains. = 他们已经在山里开辟了一条新的道路。


过去完成时:
They had hewed the timber before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经砍好了木材。
The workers had hewed the stone for days. = 工人们已经砍石头好几天了。


将来时:
He will hew the wood tomorrow. = 他明天将砍柴。
They will hew a new road through the forest. = 他们将在森林中开辟一条新路。


虚拟语气:
If he had a sharper axe, he would hew the wood more quickly. = 如果他有一把更锋利的斧头，他就会更快地砍柴。
I wish I had hewed the wood before the rain started. = 我真希望我在下雨前就把柴砍好了。


被动语态:
The stone was hewed by a skilled craftsman. = 这块石头是由一位技艺高超的工匠雕刻的。
The path was hewed through the dense forest. = 这条路是穿过茂密的森林砍出来的。


疑问句:
Did they hew the wood themselves? = 他们自己砍的柴吗？
Will they hew the trees before planting? = 他们在种植之前会砍树吗？


否定句:
He did not hew the stone properly. = 他没有正确地砍石头。
They did not hew the path all the way. = 他们没有把路一直砍通。
