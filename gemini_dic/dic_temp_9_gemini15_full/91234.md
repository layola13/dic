USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and does not have verb conjugations or tenses like the example word "what".  The usage examples below show how the adjective "hydrochloric" functions in sentences.


Example Sentences:

词源:

The word "hydrochloric" comes from "hydro-" (from Greek *ὕδωρ (húdōr), meaning "water") and "-chloric" (from Greek χλωρός (chlōrós), meaning "greenish-yellow," referring to the yellowish-green gas chlorine).  The combination describes an acid derived from chlorine and containing water.
