emotionally:/ɪˈmoʊʃənəli/ | adj. | 情绪上地，感情上地 |  Describes something that relates to or is affected by emotions. It indicates a state or action influenced by feelings rather than reason or logic. | Example: She reacted emotionally to the news. 她对这个消息做出了情绪化的反应。 He was emotionally unavailable. 他在情感上难以亲近。 |近义词：sentimentally, feelingly, affectively | 反义词：rationally, logically, dispassionately


USAGE->

She emotionally connects with the music. 她与音乐产生情感上的共鸣。
