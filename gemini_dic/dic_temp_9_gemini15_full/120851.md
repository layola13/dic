misbegot:/ˌmɪsˈbɪɡɒt/| adj.|私生子的；不正当的；不幸的|Illegitimate; wrongly begotten; ill-starred.  The word implies a sense of shame or illegitimacy associated with the birth or origin. It suggests something that is ill-fated or unfortunate.|例句：He was a misbegot son, shunned by his community. 他是一个私生子，被他的社区所排斥。
The misbegot project was doomed from the start. 这个不幸的项目从一开始就注定要失败。|近义词：illegitimate, bastard, ill-starred, unfortunate|反义词：legitimate, rightful, fortunate
