intractable:/ɪnˈtræktəbl/ | adj. | 难处理的；倔强的；棘手的 |  difficult to manage or control; stubborn; not easily solved or cured | The intractable problem of poverty continues to plague many nations.  贫困这个棘手的问题继续困扰着许多国家。 The child's intractable temper made it difficult for his parents to discipline him.  孩子倔强的脾气使得他的父母难以管教他。 |  stubborn, obstinate, unmanageable, resistant | manageable, docile, yielding, amenable


USAGE->

The problem remains intractable. = 问题依然难以解决。
This material is intractable to cutting. = 这种材料难以切割。
