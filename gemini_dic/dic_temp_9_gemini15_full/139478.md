obstipation:/ˌɒbstɪˈpeɪʃən/ | n. |顽固性便秘；便秘；阻塞|The complete cessation of fecal evacuation.  A severe form of constipation characterized by the inability to pass stool.  It can be caused by various factors, including bowel obstruction, neurological disorders, and medication side effects.  |Example:  The patient presented with severe abdominal pain and obstipation. 患者出现剧烈腹痛和顽固性便秘。
The prolonged obstipation required immediate medical intervention.  长期顽固性便秘需要立即进行医疗干预。|近义词：constipation, fecal impaction, bowel obstruction|反义词：regular bowel movements, easy defecation|名词
