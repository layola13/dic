molting:/ˈmoʊlɪŋ/| n.|蜕皮；换羽；脱毛|The process of shedding an outer covering, such as skin, feathers, or fur, to make way for new growth.  It's a natural process for many animals, including insects, birds, and reptiles.|例句：The snake is molting its skin.  蛇正在蜕皮。

The bird is molting its feathers. 这只鸟正在换羽。|近义词：shedding, sloughing|反义词：growing (new skin/feathers/fur)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snake molts its skin annually. = 蛇每年蜕皮一次。
Birds molt their feathers in the fall. = 鸟类在秋季换羽。

一般过去时:
The caterpillar molted several times before becoming a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前蜕皮好几次。
Last year, my dog molted heavily in the spring. = 去年春天我的狗大量脱毛。

现在完成时:
The snake has molted twice this year. = 蛇今年已经蜕皮两次了。
The bird has finished molting its feathers. = 这只鸟已经完成了换羽。

过去完成时:
The lizard had molted before I caught it. = 我抓住它之前，蜥蜴已经蜕皮了。
The butterfly had molted several times before it emerged from its chrysalis. = 蝴蝶在从蛹中出来之前已经蜕皮好几次了。

将来时:
The snake will molt again next spring. = 蛇明年春天会再次蜕皮。
My dog will probably molt heavily next summer. = 我的狗明年夏天可能会大量脱毛。

虚拟语气:
If the bird were molting, it would be less active. = 如果鸟正在换羽，它会不那么活跃。
I wish my cat didn't molt so much. = 我希望我的猫不要掉那么多毛。

被动语态:
The skin was molted by the snake. = 蛇蜕掉了皮。（很少用被动语态）
The feathers are being molted by the bird. = 鸟正在换羽。（很少用被动语态）

疑问句:
Is the snake molting? = 蛇正在蜕皮吗？
When do birds typically molt? = 鸟类通常什么时候换羽？

否定句:
The snake is not molting right now. = 蛇现在没有蜕皮。
The bird hasn't molted yet this year. = 这只鸟今年还没换羽。
