overseas:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːz/ | adj. adv.| 海外的；在海外；向海外 |  Relating to or situated in countries across the sea; foreign.  |例句：Many students choose to study overseas. 许多学生选择去海外留学。|例句：The company has expanded its operations overseas. 这家公司已将业务扩展到海外。|近义词：foreign, international, abroad |反义词：domestic, inland

She lives overseas. = 她住在海外。
