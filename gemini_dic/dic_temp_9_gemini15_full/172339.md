putrefying:/ˈpjuːtrɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 腐烂的；腐败的 | undergoing the process of decay or rotting; becoming putrid.  The putrefying smell was overwhelming.  |The putrefying carcass attracted a swarm of flies. 腐烂的尸体吸引了一群苍蝇。 | decaying, rotting, decomposing | fresh, clean, sterile |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meat is putrefying in the heat. = 肉在高温下腐烂了。
The food is putrefying rapidly. = 食物正在迅速腐烂。


一般过去时:
The fruit putrefied quickly in the warm weather. = 水果在温暖的天气下很快腐烂了。
The body had begun to putrefy. = 尸体已经开始腐烂了。


现在完成时:
The garbage has putrefied, emitting a foul odor. = 垃圾已经腐烂，散发出恶臭。
The remains have putrefied beyond recognition. = 遗骸已经腐烂得无法辨认。


过去完成时:
The food had putrefied before we could eat it. = 我们还没来得及吃，食物就已经腐烂了。
The corpse had completely putrefied by the time it was discovered. = 尸体被发现时已经完全腐烂了。


将来时:
The fish will putrefy if it's not refrigerated. = 如果不冷藏，鱼会腐烂。
The leftovers will likely putrefy overnight. = 剩菜很可能会过夜腐烂。


虚拟语气:
If the meat were left out, it would putrefy. = 如果把肉放在外面，它就会腐烂。
I wish the food hadn't putrefied. = 我真希望食物没有腐烂。


被动语态:
The meat was putrefied by the heat. = 肉被高温腐烂了。(虽然语法上成立，但实际应用中很少这样表达。)
The vegetables were found to be putrefying. = 发现蔬菜正在腐烂。


疑问句:
Is the food putrefying? = 食物腐烂了吗？
Is that putrefying smell coming from the bin? = 那股腐烂味是从垃圾桶里飘出来的吗？


否定句:
The food isn't putrefying yet. = 食物还没腐烂。
The meat didn't putrefy because it was frozen. = 肉因为被冷冻所以没有腐烂。
