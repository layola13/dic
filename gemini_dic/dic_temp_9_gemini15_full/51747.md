devolvements:/dɪˈvɒlvmənts/ | n. |移交；转移；归属；（权力、财产等的）让渡 | The legal transfer of property or rights.  This term often appears in legal contexts, referring to the passing of property or power from one person or entity to another, often through inheritance or a formal process.  |例句：The devolvements of the estate were carefully documented by the lawyer. = 律师仔细记录了遗产的移交。

The devolvements of power to local governments led to increased autonomy. = 将权力下放给地方政府导致了更大的自主权。


近义词：transfers, assignments, conveyances, transmissions | 反义词：retentions, acquisitions, seizures
