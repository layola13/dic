countermyths:/ˈkaʊntərmɪθs/| n. |反神话；对神话的驳斥|  Countermyths are claims or beliefs that contradict or refute established myths or widely held misconceptions. They often aim to correct inaccuracies, biases, or harmful narratives associated with the original myths.  The term emphasizes a deliberate and active opposition to prevailing myths.| Example:  The documentary presented countermyths to the popular narrative surrounding the historical event.  这部纪录片提出了与围绕这一历史事件的流行说法相矛盾的反神话说法。|近义词：rebuttals, refutations, corrections|反义词：myths, legends, misconceptions|名词
