let the cat out of the bag: /lɛt ðə kæt aʊt əv ðə bæɡ/ | phrasal verb |泄露秘密；说出真相| To reveal a secret unintentionally or carelessly.  This idiom originates from a trick where a piglet would be substituted for a cat in a bag, and the deception would be revealed when the bag was opened. |例句：I accidentally let the cat out of the bag about the surprise party. 我不小心泄露了惊喜派对的秘密。

例句：Don't let the cat out of the bag about Sarah's engagement; it's supposed to be a surprise.  不要泄露莎拉订婚的消息；这应该是惊喜。


近义词：reveal, divulge, disclose, spill the beans


反义词：keep secret, conceal, hide


词性：phrasal verb
