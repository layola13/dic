precompensates:/ˌpriːkɒmˈpɛnseɪt/| vt.|预先补偿；提前补偿|To compensate in advance for something expected to happen in the future.|The company precompensates its employees for potential future expenses.|近义词：prepay, advance payment|反义词：postpone payment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company precompensates its employees for travel expenses. = 公司预先补偿员工的差旅费。
He precompensates his tax liability by making regular payments. = 他通过定期付款来预先补偿他的税务责任。

一般过去时:
They precompensated the contractor for the materials. = 他们预先补偿了承包商的材料费。
She precompensated her daughter's college tuition. = 她预先补偿了她女儿的大学学费。

现在完成时:
The government has precompensated the farmers for the crop failure. = 政府已经预先补偿了农民的歉收损失。
We have precompensated our supplier for the next shipment. = 我们已经预先补偿了我们的供应商下一批货物的费用。


过去完成时:
They had precompensated the workers before the strike began. = 罢工开始前，他们已经预先补偿了工人们的工资。
She had precompensated her taxes to avoid penalties. = 她已经预先缴纳了税款以避免罚款。


将来时:
The company will precompensate the employees for the upcoming holiday. = 公司将预先补偿员工即将到来的节假日工资。
He will precompensate the damages before the trial. = 他将在审判前预先赔偿损失。


虚拟语气:
If the company precompensated its employees, there would be less stress. = 如果公司预先补偿员工，压力就会减少。
I wish they had precompensated us for the delays. = 我希望他们能预先补偿我们因延误造成的损失。


被动语态:
The employees were precompensated for their overtime work. = 员工的加班费得到了预先补偿。
The damages will be precompensated by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司预先赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company precompensate us for the project delays? = 公司会因项目延误而预先补偿我们吗？
Did they precompensate the workers for the lost time? = 他们是否预先补偿了工人们因损失的时间？


否定句:
The company did not precompensate the employees for the unexpected expenses. = 公司没有预先补偿员工的意外开支。
They will not precompensate the damages without proper evidence. = 没有充分证据，他们不会预先赔偿损失。
