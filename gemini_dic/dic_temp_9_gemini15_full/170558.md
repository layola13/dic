pseudo-aryans:/ˈsuːdoʊˈɛəriənz/| n.|伪雅利安人|Those who falsely claim Aryan descent or who are incorrectly classified as such.  The term is often used in a derogatory or dismissive way, highlighting the problematic nature of racial classifications and the misuse of "Aryan" as a marker of supposed superiority.|Example:  The Nazi regime promoted a distorted view of history, classifying certain groups as "pseudo-Aryans" to justify their exclusion.|近义词：false Aryans,  pretend Aryans|反义词：genuine Aryans (though the concept of "Aryan" itself is problematic)
