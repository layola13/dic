overcommend:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmɛnd/| v. |过分推荐；过誉|To praise or recommend excessively; to overpraise.  The word is not commonly used and is considered somewhat archaic or informal. It implies an excessive or inappropriate level of praise, often to the point of being insincere or hyperbolic.|例句：He overcommended his son's abilities to the prospective employer, which backfired when the son failed to meet expectations. 他对潜在雇主过分夸赞儿子的能力，结果适得其反，因为儿子没有达到预期。|近义词：extol, laud, praise excessively, hype|反义词：criticize, belittle, downplay|动词
