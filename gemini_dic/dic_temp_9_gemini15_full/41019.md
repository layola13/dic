copartnery:/koʊˈpɑːrtnəri/| n. |合伙关系；合伙企业 | A copartnery is a business relationship between two or more individuals who agree to share in the profits or losses of a business.  It often implies a less formal arrangement than a corporation. |Example: They formed a copartnery to develop and market their new software. 他们组建了一个合伙企业来开发和销售他们的新软件。|近义词：partnership, joint venture |反义词：sole proprietorship
