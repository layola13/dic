memeing: /ˈmiːmɪŋ/ | v. | 模因化；创作或传播模因 | The act of creating, sharing, or participating in the spread of memes.  It often involves adapting existing memes or creating new ones to express ideas, humor, or commentary. | 例句：He's been memeing all day, creating hilarious variations on that cat video. 他整天都在创作模因，制作那个猫视频的搞笑版本。

例句：The students were memeing the professor's lecture by creating funny images and captions. 学生们通过创作有趣的图片和标题来“模因化”教授的讲座。


近义词：meming (less common variant), viraling (referring to the spread of the meme)

反义词：N/A (no direct opposite, perhaps "ignoring" or "suppressing" the spread of memes)

词性：动词
