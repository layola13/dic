cruel:/ˈkruːəl/ | adj. | 残酷的；无情的 |  Characterized by severity or harshness; causing suffering or pain.  It can refer to actions, people, or situations. |例句：The dictator's regime was known for its cruel treatment of prisoners.  独裁者的政权以其残酷的囚犯待遇而闻名。

例句：He showed a cruel disregard for the feelings of others. 他表现出对他人感受的残酷漠视。


近义词：brutal, savage, ruthless, merciless, inhuman, heartless, pitiless |反义词：kind, compassionate, merciful, gentle, humane, tender


词源:  The word "cruel" originates from the Old French word "cruel," which itself comes from the Latin word "crudelis."  "Crudelis" is believed to be related to the Proto-Indo-European root "*krew-," meaning "raw," "bloody," or "uncooked," suggesting a connection to the raw and unfeeling nature of cruelty.
