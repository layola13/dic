mutilator:/ˈmjuːtɪleɪtər/| n. |残害者；毁损者；使残缺不全的人或物|A mutilator is a person or thing that mutilates something or someone.  It refers to someone who inflicts serious damage, often resulting in disfigurement or destruction.  The term can also apply to devices or processes that cause such damage. |例句：The animal rights activist protested against the factory, calling it a mutilator of innocent creatures. 动物权利活动家抗议这家工厂，称其为残害无辜生物的罪魁祸首。

The suspect was identified as the mutilator of the ancient statue. 嫌疑人被认定为古代雕像的毁损者。|近义词：despoiler, destroyer, defacer, mangler |反义词：preserver, restorer, healer
