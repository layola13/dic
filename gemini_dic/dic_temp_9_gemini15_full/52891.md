diminish:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/| vt. |减少，减小；贬低，轻视 |to make or become less; to reduce in size, extent, or importance.  |例句：The sun diminished in size as it sank below the horizon.  太阳落山时，体积变小了。

例句：His influence has diminished since the scandal. 自丑闻以来，他的影响力下降了。


近义词：decrease, reduce, lessen, abate, depreciate |反义词：increase, augment, enhance, magnify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun diminishes in size as it sets.  太阳落山时体积变小。
He constantly tries to diminish my achievements. 他总是试图贬低我的成就。


一般过去时:
The value of the property diminished significantly after the flood.  洪水过后，房产价值大幅下降。
She felt diminished by his constant criticism.  她感到被他不断的批评贬低了。


现在完成时:
The company's profits have diminished over the past year.  公司去年的利润有所下降。
His confidence has diminished since he lost his job.  自从丢了工作后，他的信心下降了。


过去完成时:
His influence had diminished considerably before he resigned.  在他辞职之前，他的影响力已经大大减弱了。
They had diminished the problem's importance by ignoring it. 他们通过忽视问题来降低了它的重要性。


将来时:
The effects of the medication will diminish gradually.  药物的效果会逐渐减弱。
The power of the dictator will diminish with time.  独裁者的权力会随着时间的推移而减弱。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act soon, our resources will diminish to dangerous levels.  如果我们不尽快采取行动，我们的资源将会减少到危险的水平。
I wish my anxieties would diminish. 我希望我的焦虑能够减少。


被动语态:
His contributions were diminished by the committee's decision.  他的贡献被委员会的决定贬低了。
The problem was diminished by a creative solution.  一个创造性的解决方案减少了这个问题。


疑问句:
Does excessive stress diminish your immune system? 过度压力会削弱你的免疫系统吗？
Will this action diminish our chances of success?  这个行动会降低我们成功的几率吗？


否定句:
The problem did not diminish despite our efforts.  尽管我们努力了，问题并没有减少。
The government's actions did not diminish public anger. 政府的行为并没有减少公众的愤怒。
