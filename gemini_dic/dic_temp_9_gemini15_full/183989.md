reshod:/rɪˈʃʊd/| v. |重铺鞋底；翻新（鞋）|To put new soles on (shoes).  The verb implies a repair or refurbishment, rather than a complete replacement. |例句：The cobbler reshod my favorite boots, giving them a new lease on life.  鞋匠为我心爱的靴子重铺了鞋底，让它们焕发了新生。|例句：After years of wear, my hiking boots were reshod and are as good as new.  我的登山靴经过多年的磨损后，重新换了鞋底，现在跟新的一样好。|近义词：resole, repair|反义词：discard, throw away|动词
