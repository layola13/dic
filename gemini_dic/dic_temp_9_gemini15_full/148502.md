overstudious:/ˌoʊvərˈstuːdiəs/ | adj. | 用功过度的；学习过度的 | Excessively studious; studying too much.  It can imply a negative connotation, suggesting that the excessive studying is detrimental to other aspects of life or to one's well-being. | 例句：He became overstudious and neglected his health. 他学习过度，忽略了自己的健康。  She was overstudious and missed out on social events. 她学习过度，错过了社交活动。| 近义词：bookish, studious (but with a negative implication), excessively diligent | 反义词：lazy, idle, indolent
