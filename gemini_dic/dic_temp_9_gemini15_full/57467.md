draftiest:/ˈdræftɪst/| adj.|最通风的，最透风的，最寒冷的 |Most exposed to drafts;  extremely drafty.  It describes a place with significant airflow, often resulting in a chilly or uncomfortable environment. |例句：The draftiest room in the house was the one overlooking the lake.  这间房是最通风的，因为它临湖。

The old barn was draftiest in winter.  这旧谷仓冬天最透风。


近义词：airy, breezy, chilly, cold, windy |反义词： airtight, sealed, insulated, warm, cozy
