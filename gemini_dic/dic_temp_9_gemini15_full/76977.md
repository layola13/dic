garcinia:/ɡɑːˈsɪniə/| n. |藤黄属植物|Garcinia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Clusiaceae, comprising around 400 species of trees and shrubs.  Many species are known for their edible fruits, while others are valued for their medicinal properties. The genus is native to tropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Pacific Islands.|Example:  Garcinia cambogia is a popular weight-loss supplement. 藤黄果是一种流行的减肥补充剂。|近义词：暂无直接近义词，需根据具体物种描述|反义词：暂无直接反义词|名词
