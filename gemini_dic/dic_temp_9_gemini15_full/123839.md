moralises:/ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 道貌岸然地训斥；说教；  | To talk or write about morality in a way that is intended to teach or instruct, often in a pompous or self-righteous manner.  | He moralises constantly about the failings of others, but ignores his own shortcomings. 他不断地对别人的缺点说教，却忽略了自己的缺点。 | preach, lecture, sermonize |  behave badly, act immorally | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He moralises about the importance of honesty. = 他说教诚实的重要性。
She moralises to her children about good manners. = 她教训她的孩子要有良好的礼貌。


一般过去时:
The priest moralized about the sins of the congregation. = 牧师对会众的罪过进行说教。
He moralized at length about the dangers of gambling. = 他冗长地训斥赌博的危害。


现在完成时:
She has moralized endlessly since the scandal broke. = 自从丑闻爆发以来，她一直在没完没了地说教。
He has moralized to me about my lifestyle choices. = 他对我的人生选择进行过说教。


过去完成时:
They had moralized about his behavior for years before he finally changed. = 在他最终改变之前，他们多年来一直对他进行说教。
She had moralized to him repeatedly about the importance of hard work. = 她反复地对他进行关于努力工作重要性的说教。


将来时:
He will moralise again if you don't clean your room. = 如果你不打扫房间，他还会再说教。
She will moralise endlessly to anyone who will listen. = 她会没完没了地对任何愿意听的人说教。


虚拟语气:
If he moralized less, people would like him more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish he wouldn't moralize so much. = 我希望他不那么说教。


被动语态:
His behavior was often moralized upon by his elders. = 他的长辈经常对他进行说教。
Her choices were moralized against by the community. = 社区谴责她的选择。


疑问句:
Does he often moralize to his friends? = 他经常对朋友说教吗？
Why does she moralize so much? = 她为什么这么爱说教？


否定句:
He didn't moralize to me about my actions. = 他没有对我进行关于我行为的说教。
She doesn't moralize about things she herself does. = 她不会对她自己做过的事情进行说教。


PHRASE->
moralises about = 对…说教
moralises on = 对…发表道德评论
moralises against = 反对…进行说教
moralises with = 与…进行道德上的探讨
moralises to = 向…说教
