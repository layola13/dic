cloudier:/ˈklaʊdiər/| adj.|阴天的，多云的，模糊不清的|More cloudy; characterized by or having much cloudiness.|The sky grew cloudier as the afternoon wore on.  天空在下午变得越来越阴沉。|The future looks cloudier than ever. 未来看起来比以往任何时候都更加扑朔迷离。|clear, sunny|sunny, bright


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sky is cloudier today than yesterday.  今天的天空比昨天更阴沉。
The water in the lake looks cloudier after the rain. 雨后湖水看起来更浑浊。

一般过去时:
The weather was cloudier in the morning.  早上天气比较阴沉。
The picture became cloudier as I zoomed in.  当我放大图片时，它变得模糊不清。

现在完成时:
The sky has become cloudier since this morning.  自从今天早上以来，天空变得越来越阴沉了。
The situation has grown cloudier in recent weeks. 近几周情况变得越来越不明朗。

过去完成时:
The sky had already become cloudier before the storm arrived.  暴风雨来临之前，天空就已经变得阴沉了。
The evidence had become cloudier after several days of investigation.  经过几天的调查，证据变得越来越模糊了。

将来时:
The forecast predicts that it will be cloudier tomorrow.  预报说明天会阴天。
The future of the company will likely be cloudier without new investments.  如果没有新的投资，公司的未来可能会更加黯淡。

虚拟语气:
If the sky were cloudier, we wouldn't go to the beach. 如果天空更阴，我们就不会去海滩。
I wish the situation were not so cloudier. 我希望情况不要那么不明朗。

被动语态:
The image was made cloudier by the poor quality lens.  劣质镜头使图像变得模糊。
The issue is made cloudier by conflicting reports.  相互矛盾的报道使问题更加复杂。

疑问句:
Is the water cloudier than usual?  这水比平时更浑浊吗？
Does the future seem cloudier to you? 你觉得未来看起来更不明朗吗？

否定句:
The sky isn't cloudier today.  今天天空并不阴沉。
The explanation wasn't cloudier than necessary.  解释并不比必要的更模糊。
