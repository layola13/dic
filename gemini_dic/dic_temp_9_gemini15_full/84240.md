half-siblings:/ˈhɑːf ˈsɪblɪŋz/| n.|同父异母或同母异父的兄弟姐妹|Half-siblings share only one parent, either the mother or the father.|例句：She has two half-siblings from her father's previous marriage. 她有两个同父异母的兄弟姐妹。  例句：He discovered he had a half-sibling he never knew existed. 他发现他有一个他从未知道存在的同父异母或同母异父的兄弟姐妹。|近义词：half-brother, half-sister|反义词：full siblings
