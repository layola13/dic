durries:/ˈdʌriz/| n.|卷烟，香烟(通常指廉价的)|  A durrie is a slang term, primarily used in Australia and New Zealand, referring to a cigarette, often implying a cheap or low-quality one.  The term is considered informal and somewhat dated.| Example: "He lit a durrie and stared out the window."  = 他点燃一支香烟，望着窗外。|近义词：cigarette, fag (UK slang), smoke|反义词：无
