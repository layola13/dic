immunodeficiencies:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənsiz/ | n. | 免疫缺陷（疾病） |  A state or condition characterized by a deficiency or absence of the immune response, making an individual susceptible to infections.  Immunodeficiencies can be inherited (primary) or acquired (secondary) and range in severity from mild to life-threatening. |  The child suffers from severe combined immunodeficiency (SCID), a rare genetic disorder.  这个孩子患有严重的联合免疫缺陷病（SCID），这是一种罕见的遗传疾病。

The patient's immunodeficiencies were attributed to long-term use of immunosuppressant drugs.  患者的免疫缺陷归因于长期使用免疫抑制药物。 |  immunodeficiency, immune deficiency |  immunocompetence | n.
