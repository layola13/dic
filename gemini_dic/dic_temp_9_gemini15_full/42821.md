courb: /kʊrb/ | n. |路缘；抑制；控制 | A curb is a stone or concrete edging to a street or path.  It can also refer to the act of restraining or controlling something. |例句：The car mounted the curb. 这辆车上了路缘。 The government imposed a curb on inflation. 政府控制了通货膨胀。|近义词：restraint, check, control |反义词：uncontrolled, unrestrained |名词

The curb prevents cars from driving onto the sidewalk. = 路缘石阻止汽车驶上人行道。
