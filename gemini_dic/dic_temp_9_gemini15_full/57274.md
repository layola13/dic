downright:/ˈdaʊnraɪt/| adj.|彻底的；完全的；坦率的；直率的|Completely; thoroughly; frankly; bluntly.  The word implies a degree of unexpectedness or emphasis. It can be used to intensify both positive and negative qualities. |例句：He gave a downright lie. 他撒了个彻头彻尾的谎。
例句：She was downright rude to the waiter. 她对服务员粗鲁至极。|近义词：utter, complete, absolute, sheer, frankly, plainly|反义词：mild, subtle, indirect, polite
