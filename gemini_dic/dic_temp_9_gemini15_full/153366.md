penetrations:/ˌpɛnɪˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. |渗透；入侵；穿透；洞察力 |The act of penetrating or the state of being penetrated;  the forceful entry into something.  It can refer to physical penetration, metaphorical penetration (like understanding), or the act of breaking into a secure system. |例句：The enemy's penetrations of our defenses were a serious concern.  敌人的突破对我军的防线造成了严重的威胁。


The security system detected multiple penetrations. 安全系统检测到多次入侵。


近义词：intrusions, breaches, entries, insights |反义词：exits, closures, blockades


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rain penetrates the soil. 雨水渗透土壤。
His words penetrate my heart. 他的话语触动我的心弦。


一般过去时:
The bullet penetrated the wall. 子弹穿透了墙壁。
The army penetrated the enemy lines. 军队突破了敌人的防线。


现在完成时:
The idea has penetrated the public consciousness. 这个理念已经深入人心。
The roots have penetrated deep into the ground. 根部已深深扎入地下。


过去完成时:
The thieves had penetrated the building before the alarm sounded.  窃贼在警报响起前已经潜入了大楼。
The rain had penetrated the tent, leaving everything wet. 雨水已经渗透了帐篷，导致所有东西都湿透了。


将来时:
The missile will penetrate the enemy's defenses. 导弹将穿透敌人的防御工事。
The new marketing campaign will penetrate the target market. 新的营销活动将渗透目标市场。


虚拟语气:
If the knife had penetrated deeper, it would have been fatal. 如果刀子扎得更深，就会致命。
I wish my ideas had penetrated more effectively. 我希望我的想法能够更有效地被理解。


被动语态:
The building was penetrated by thieves. 大楼被窃贼入侵了。
The secret was penetrated by the press. 这个秘密被媒体泄露了。


疑问句:
How did the water penetrate the roof? 水是怎么渗透到屋顶的？
Has the message penetrated to the right people? 信息是否传达给了正确的人？


否定句:
The bullet did not penetrate the armor. 子弹没有穿透装甲。
His arguments did not penetrate my defenses. 他的论点没有说服我。
