fucus:/ˈfjuːkəs/ | n. | 石蕋属植物；海藻 | A genus of brown algae, commonly known as wrack or rockweed, often found in the intertidal zone.  Fucus is characterized by its leathery fronds and air bladders. | Example:  The rocky shore was covered with a thick carpet of fucus.  岩石海岸上覆盖着厚厚的一层石蕋属植物。|近义词：seaweed, kelp, wrack |反义词：None specifically, but terms referring to land plants could be considered contrasting. |名词
