keratonyxis:/ˌkɛrəˈtɒnɪksɪs/| n. |角膜穿刺术|Surgical puncture of the cornea.  This procedure is rarely performed now, having been largely superseded by other techniques.  It was historically used to treat certain eye conditions, particularly glaucoma. |例句：Keratonyxis, while historically significant, is now seldom employed due to advancements in ophthalmic surgery. 角膜穿刺术尽管在历史上意义重大，但由于眼科手术的进步，现在很少使用了。 |近义词：corneal puncture, corneal incision (in a broader sense)|反义词：corneal transplantation (in a functional sense)|名词
