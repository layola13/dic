glide:/ɡlaɪd/ | v. | 滑翔；滑动；悄悄地走；平稳地移动 | To move smoothly and easily, often without much effort.  | The birds glided effortlessly through the air.  鸟儿轻而易举地滑翔在空中。The dancer glided across the stage. 舞者轻盈地滑过舞台。| slide, slip, drift | stumble, crash | 动词


USAGE->

He glides across the ice with ease. 他轻松地在冰面上滑行。
The boat glides silently on the water.  小船静静地漂浮在水上。
