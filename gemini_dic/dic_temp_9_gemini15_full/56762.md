doozying:/ˈduːziɪŋ/ | v. |令人震惊的；极好的；极度兴奋的 |  Describes something exceptionally impressive, amazing, or overwhelming, often in a positive sense. It can also suggest something extremely difficult or challenging. The word is informal. |例句：The concert was doozying!  = 音乐会太棒了！ The exam was a doozying challenge. = 这场考试极具挑战性。| 近义词：amazing, incredible, astounding, challenging, difficult | 反义词：boring, mundane, easy
