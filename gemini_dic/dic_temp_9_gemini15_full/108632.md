leadman:/ˈliːdmæn/ | n. |领班；组长；工头 |A leadman is a worker who supervises a small group of other workers and is responsible for their work.  They often have some experience and expertise in the task at hand, and act as a liaison between the workers and management. |例句：The leadman instructed the crew to start on the west wing.  领班指示工作人员从西翼开始。

例句：He was promoted to leadman after demonstrating his skills and leadership. 他在展示了他的技能和领导能力后被提升为领班。


近义词：foreman, supervisor, crew chief, team leader |反义词：worker, subordinate
