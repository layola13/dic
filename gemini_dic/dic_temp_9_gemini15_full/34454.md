clavicles:/ˈklævɪkəlz/| n. |锁骨（复数）|Collarbones; the bones connecting the sternum (breastbone) to the scapulae (shoulder blades). They are also known as the collar bones.  Clavicles are S-shaped bones that play a crucial role in shoulder movement and stability. They protect underlying nerves and blood vessels.|The doctor examined his fractured clavicles.|近义词：collarbones|反义词：无
