hrt:/hɑːrt/ | n. |心脏；核心；中心 | heart refers to the muscular organ that pumps blood through the circulatory system.  It can also refer to the central or most important part of something. |例句：My heart beats fast when I see her.  当我看到她时，我的心跳加速了。|例句：The heart of the city is bustling with activity. 城市中心熙熙攘攘，热闹非凡。|近义词：core, center, essence |反义词：exterior, periphery
