jiff:/dʒɪf/| n.|瞬间，片刻|A very short period of time.  It is often used informally.|例句：Just give me a jiff to finish this.  给我一点时间完成这个。

例句：I'll be with you in a jiff.  我马上就来。


近义词：moment, second, instant, blink


反义词：eternity, age, long time


词性:名词


词源:The origin is uncertain, but it's likely related to  "jiffy," a shortening of "jiffle," meaning "a slight, quick movement."  The word's informal nature suggests a colloquial origin and evolution.
