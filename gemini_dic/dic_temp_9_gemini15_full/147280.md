overmixes:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪksɪz/| v.|过度混合；搅拌过度|To mix or blend something to an excessive degree, resulting in a less desirable outcome.  This typically applies to food preparation, where overmixing can lead to tough baked goods or a less appealing texture. |例句：Don't overmix the cake batter, or the cake will be tough. 不要过度搅拌蛋糕糊，否则蛋糕会很硬。

例句：Overmixing the cookie dough resulted in flat, hard cookies.  过度搅拌曲奇面团导致饼干又平又硬。


近义词：overbeat, overblend


反义词：undermix
