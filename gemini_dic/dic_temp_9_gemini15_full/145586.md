overconcentration: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |过度集中；注意力过于集中 |The excessive focusing of resources, attention, or effort on a single area or task, often to the detriment of other important areas.  This can lead to inefficiencies and missed opportunities.  |例句：The company's overconcentration on one product line left it vulnerable when that product's market share declined. 公司过度专注于单一产品线，导致该产品市场份额下降时，公司变得脆弱不堪。

例句：His overconcentration on work led to burnout and neglecting his family. 他过度专注于工作导致了倦怠和忽视家庭。


近义词：hyperfocus, concentration, focus


反义词：diversification, distribution, decentralization


词性: noun
