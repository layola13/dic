out-winging: /ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；胜过 |  To surpass or exceed, especially in a contest or competition.  It implies a significant margin of victory. | 例句：The underdog team out-winged their opponents in the final match. =  弱势球队在决赛中击败了对手。

The innovative design out-winged all previous models in terms of efficiency. =  创新的设计在效率方面超越了所有以前的型号。


近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass, exceed, outdo | 反义词：underperform, lose to, be defeated by


词性：动词

USAGE->

The new strategy out-wings the old one in effectiveness. = 新策略在有效性方面胜过旧策略。
Our sales team consistently out-wings the competition. = 我们的销售团队始终胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year's sales figures out-winged all expectations. = 去年销售额超出了所有预期。
Their performance out-winged that of the rivals. = 他们的表现胜过了竞争对手。


现在完成时:

Before the final decision was made, the board had out-winged the initial proposal. = 在做出最终决定之前，董事会已经否决了最初的提议。


将来时:

With this new approach, we expect to out-wing our competitors in customer satisfaction. = 通过这种新方法，我们预计会在客户满意度方面胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:

I wish our sales team had out-winged their rivals last quarter. = 我希望我们的销售团队在上个季度能够胜过竞争对手。


被动语态:
The old system was out-winged by the new one. = 旧系统被新系统超越了。
Their proposal was out-winged by a more innovative approach. = 他们的提案被一个更具创新性的方法所超越。


疑问句:
Will our new product out-wing the existing ones in the market? = 我们的新产品会在市场上超越现有产品吗？
Does our marketing strategy out-wing that of our competitors? = 我们的营销策略是否胜过竞争对手？


否定句:
Our efforts did not out-wing the challenges we faced. = 我们的努力没有克服我们面临的挑战。(Note:  "out-wing" implies success;  negating it describes failure to surpass.)
The company failed to out-wing its rivals in the crucial market segment. = 该公司未能在这个关键市场领域超越竞争对手。
