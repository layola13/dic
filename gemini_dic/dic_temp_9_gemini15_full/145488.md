overclimb: /ˌoʊvərˈklaɪm/ | vt. |攀登超过；超越|To climb over or beyond; to surpass or exceed in climbing or achievement.|例句：The climbers overclimbed the previously unreachable peak.  登山者攀登了以前无法到达的山峰。 |例句：His ambition overclimbed his abilities. 他的雄心壮志超过了他的能力。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend|反义词：underclimb (not a common word)
