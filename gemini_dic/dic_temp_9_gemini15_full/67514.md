extracting:/ɪkˈstræktɪŋ/ | v. |提取；摘录；榨取 |The act of removing or obtaining something from a source.  It implies a process of separating or isolating a desired component. |例句：Scientists are extracting valuable data from the research. 科学家们正在从研究中提取宝贵的数据。

例句：The dentist is extracting a wisdom tooth. 牙医正在拔智齿。


近义词：removing, obtaining, deriving, isolating | 反义词：inserting, adding, injecting | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are extracting oil from the well. = 他们正在从油井中提取石油。
We extract information from various sources. = 我们从各种来源提取信息。

一般过去时:
The team extracted the relevant data from the database. = 该团队从数据库中提取了相关数据。
She extracted a key passage from the long article. = 她从冗长的文章中摘录了一段关键文字。

现在完成时:
They have extracted all the necessary information. = 他们已经提取了所有必要的信息。
Scientists have extracted a new compound from the plant. = 科学家们已经从这种植物中提取了一种新的化合物。

过去完成时:
He had extracted the information before the meeting started. = 他在会议开始前就提取了信息。
They had extracted the essence of the argument. = 他们已经提炼出了论点的精髓。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:

词源:
The word "extracting" comes from the Latin word "ex" (out) and "trahere" (to draw or pull).  Therefore, it literally means "to draw out".
