over-fixed:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪkst/| adj.|过度固定的；过于僵化的| excessively fixed or rigid; lacking flexibility or adaptability.  The over-fixed schedule left no room for unexpected events.  这个过于僵化的日程安排没有为意外事件留下任何空间。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, inflexible, unyielding|反义词：flexible, adaptable, fluid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is over-fixed and needs adjustments. = 系统过于僵化，需要调整。
This approach is over-fixed and prevents innovation. = 这种方法过于僵化，阻碍创新。

一般过去时:
The rules were over-fixed and stifled creativity. = 规则过于僵化，扼杀了创造力。
The plan was over-fixed, leading to its failure. = 计划过于僵化，导致失败。

现在完成时:

The company has over-fixed its strategies, hindering its growth. = 公司的战略过于僵化，阻碍了其发展。

过去完成时:
