deadpanned:/ˈdedpænd/| v. |面无表情地说；一本正经地说|To say or deliver (something) with a deliberately impassive expression.  It implies a lack of emotion, often to humorous or ironic effect.|例句：He deadpanned, "That's just great." 他面无表情地说：“真是太好了。”

例句：She deadpanned the punchline, completely undermining the joke's intended effect. 她面无表情地说出笑点，完全破坏了笑话的预期效果。


近义词：said flatly, delivered dryly, stated emotionlessly|反义词：exclaimed, shouted, gushed


词性：动词
