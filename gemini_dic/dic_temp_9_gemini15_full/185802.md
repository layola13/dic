revisionary:/rɪˈvɪʒənəri/| adj. | 修正的；批判性的；复审的 | Relating to or involving revision; characterized by a revision or revisions.  It often implies a critical examination and potential alteration of existing ideas, systems, or practices. |例句：The revisionary interpretation of the novel challenged traditional readings.  = 这部小说的修正主义解读挑战了传统的解读方式。

The revisionary committee met to discuss the proposed changes to the curriculum. = 修订委员会开会讨论了拟议的课程变更。

近义词：corrective, amendatory, reformative

反义词：conservative, traditional, uncritical
