petal-like:/ˈpetəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|花瓣似的，像花瓣的|Resembling the petals of a flower in shape, appearance, or texture.  It often describes something delicate, soft, and often brightly colored.|The cake was decorated with petal-like frosting.|近义词：flower-like, blossom-like, delicate|反义词：rough, coarse, jagged
