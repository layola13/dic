incineration:/ɪnˌsɪnəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |焚烧；火葬 |The process of burning something completely.  |例句：The incineration of waste materials is a controversial topic.  废物焚烧是一个有争议的话题。|例句：The body was disposed of by incineration.遗体通过火葬处理。|近义词：cremation, combustion, burning |反义词：preservation, conservation
