ratchety:/ˈrætʃəti/| adj. | 嘎吱作响的；易怒的；脾气暴躁的 |  Characterized by a harsh, grating, or clicking sound, like that of a ratchet; also, irritable, grumpy, or easily angered. |例句：The old door was ratchety and hard to open.  这扇旧门嘎吱作响，很难打开。

The old man was ratchety and refused to help.  这个老头脾气暴躁，拒绝帮忙。|近义词：grating, creaky, irritable, grumpy, cantankerous |反义词：smooth, quiet, pleasant, amiable
