chiaroscurist:/ˌkiˌɑːrəˈskjʊrɪst/| n. |明暗对比画家，运用明暗法绘画者 |A chiaroscurist is an artist who specializes in the use of chiaroscuro, a technique of painting that uses strong contrasts between light and dark to create dramatic effects.  Chiaroscuro is particularly associated with Renaissance and Baroque art.  Chiaroscurists skillfully manipulate light and shadow to model forms, create depth, and evoke mood and emotion. |Example: Caravaggio was a master chiaroscurist, his paintings renowned for their dramatic use of light and shadow. 卡拉瓦乔是一位大师级的明暗对比画家，他的绘画以其戏剧性的明暗运用而闻名。|近义词：painter, artist, shade painter|反义词： (无明确反义词，但可以考虑与注重均匀光照的画家对比)|名词
