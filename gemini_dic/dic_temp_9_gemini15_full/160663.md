portfolio:/ˌpɔːrtˈfoʊli.oʊ/ | /pɔːtˈfəʊli.əʊ/ | n. | 作品集；投资组合；文件夹 | A portfolio is a collection of a person's work, such as a writer's published articles or an artist's paintings, which is used to demonstrate their skills and abilities to potential clients or employers.  It can also refer to a range of investments held by an individual or organization. | 例句：She showed her prospective employer a portfolio of her design work. = 她向潜在雇主展示了她的设计作品集。  He has a diverse investment portfolio. = 他拥有多元化的投资组合。 | 近义词：collection, compilation, folder | 反义词：N/A
