enchain:/ɛnˈtʃeɪn/| vt.|束缚；连接；连锁|to bind or link together; to connect in a chain or series; to restrain or confine|The prisoners were enchained in the dungeon.犯人们被锁在地牢里。The events enchained in a dramatic sequence.事件以戏剧性的顺序连接在一起。|近义词：bind, link, chain, connect|反义词：free, release, liberate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enchains his thoughts to his work. 他把思想专注于工作。
They enchain the prisoners with heavy chains. 他们用沉重的锁链锁住囚犯。


一般过去时:
The blacksmith enchained the gate securely. 铁匠牢牢地锁上了大门。
She enchained her emotions, refusing to show her sadness. 她克制住自己的情绪，拒绝表现出悲伤。


现在完成时:
The writer has enchained his readers with a compelling story. 这名作家以引人入胜的故事吸引住了读者。
They have enchained the dog to the fence. 他们已将狗拴在栅栏上。


过去完成时:
He had enchained himself to a life of routine. 他把自己束缚在例行公事的生活中。
The workers had enchained themselves to their demanding jobs. 工人们把自己束缚在他们要求很高的工作上。


将来时:
She will enchain her heart to a worthy man. 她将会把她的心托付给一个值得的人。
They will enchain the escapees to prevent further escapes. 他们将把逃跑者锁起来以防止进一步逃跑。


虚拟语气:
If he enchained his energy, he could achieve more. 如果他能集中精力，他就能取得更多成就。
I wish I hadn't enchained myself to that contract. 我真希望我没有和那份合同绑在一起。


被动语态:
His emotions were enchained by grief. 他的情绪被悲伤所束缚。
The dog was enchained to a post. 狗被拴在一根柱子上。


疑问句:
Are the prisoners enchained? 囚犯们被锁了吗？
Is his fate enchained to hers? 他的命运与她的命运相连吗？


否定句:
He did not enchain himself to his past. 他没有让自己受过去束缚。
They did not enchain the suspect. 他们没有逮捕嫌疑人。
