counterproductive:/ˌkaʊntərpˈrɒdʌktɪv/| adj. |适得其反的，收效相反的 |Producing the opposite of the desired effect.  Something that is counterproductive has the opposite effect to what was intended. |例：Trying to force him to cooperate was counterproductive; it just made him more resistant.  试图强迫他合作适得其反；这只会让他更抗拒。

His attempts to help were actually counterproductive. 他的帮助反而适得其反。|近义词：self-defeating, detrimental, unhelpful, adverse |反义词：productive, beneficial, helpful, advantageous
