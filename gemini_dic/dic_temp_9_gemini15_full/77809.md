general plan:/ˈdʒɛnərəl plæn/| n.|总体规划，总计划|A comprehensive plan outlining the main objectives and strategies for achieving a particular goal or outcome.  It often lacks specific details and serves as a framework for more detailed plans.|例句：The general plan for the city's development includes expanding public transportation and building new parks.  城市发展总体规划包括扩建公共交通和建设新的公园。

例句：The company's general plan is to increase market share by 15% within the next year.  公司的总体规划是在明年将市场份额提高15%。


近义词：master plan, overall plan, strategic plan, blueprint

反义词：detail, specific plan


词性:名词
