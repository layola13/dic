revolutionizes: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənaɪzɪz/ | vt. |彻底改变；革命性地改变 | To change something completely and fundamentally, often in a way that is considered to be a great improvement. |例句：The internet has revolutionized the way we communicate.  互联网彻底改变了我们的沟通方式。


例句：This new technology revolutionizes the manufacturing process.  这项新技术彻底改变了制造流程。


近义词：transform,  transform radically,  remake,  reinvent,  upend


反义词：maintain the status quo, leave unchanged


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new software revolutionizes how we work. = 新软件彻底改变了我们的工作方式。
This invention revolutionizes the industry. = 这项发明彻底改变了这个行业。

一般过去时:
The invention of the printing press revolutionized communication. = 印刷术的发明彻底改变了沟通方式。
The discovery of penicillin revolutionized medicine. = 青霉素的发现彻底改变了医学。

现在完成时:
The internet has revolutionized the way we access information. = 互联网已经彻底改变了我们获取信息的方式。
Digital technology has revolutionized many aspects of modern life. = 数字技术已经彻底改变了现代生活的许多方面。

过去完成时:
By the time the war ended, the industrial revolution had revolutionized warfare. = 到战争结束时，工业革命已经彻底改变了战争。
Before the discovery of electricity, the world had never been revolutionized in such a way. = 在发现电之前，世界从未以这种方式发生过彻底的改变。

将来时:
Artificial intelligence will revolutionize many industries in the coming years. = 在未来几年中，人工智能将彻底改变许多行业。
This new approach will revolutionize the treatment of this disease. = 这种新方法将彻底改变这种疾病的治疗方法。

虚拟语气:
If we adopted this new strategy, it would revolutionize our market share. = 如果我们采用这个新战略，它将彻底改变我们的市场份额。
If this technology were widely adopted, it would revolutionize the transportation sector. = 如果这项技术得到广泛采用，它将彻底改变交通运输行业。

被动语态:
The industry was revolutionized by the introduction of automation. = 该行业因引进自动化而发生了彻底的改变。
The way we learn is being revolutionized by online education. = 在线教育正在彻底改变我们的学习方式。

疑问句:
Will this new policy revolutionize education? = 这项新政策会彻底改变教育吗？
Does this technology truly revolutionize the existing system? = 这项技术真的能彻底改变现有系统吗？

否定句:
The proposed changes did not revolutionize the company's performance. = 提出的变更并没有彻底改变公司的业绩。
The new law has not yet revolutionized the healthcare system. = 新法律尚未彻底改变医疗保健系统。


PHRASE->
revolutionize the way  彻底改变…的方式
revolutionize the industry  彻底改变行业
revolutionize the market  彻底改变市场
revolutionize healthcare  彻底改变医疗保健
revolutionize education  彻底改变教育


词源:  Comes from "revolution" (a sudden, radical, and complete change) + "-ize" (to make or become).  The word reflects the impact of major societal shifts like the Industrial Revolution, which fundamentally altered ways of life.
