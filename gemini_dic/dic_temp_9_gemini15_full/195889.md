sensationalize:/ˌsɛnˈseɪʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使轰动；夸大其词；渲染 | To present (something) in a sensational manner; to exaggerate or distort in order to create a sensation.  | The media sensationalized the story, focusing on the most dramatic aspects and ignoring the underlying causes. 媒体夸大了这个故事，关注最戏剧化的方面，而忽略了根本原因。 |  exaggerate, hype, dramatize | downplay, understate, minimize |动词
