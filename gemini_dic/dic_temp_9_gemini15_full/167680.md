prickly:/ˈprɪkli/| adj.| 扎人的；易怒的；棘手的 | Having a sharp point or points; easily irritated or annoyed; causing difficulty or trouble. |例句：The prickly pear cactus has sharp spines.  仙人掌有尖刺。

The prickly child refused to share his toys.  那个易怒的孩子拒绝分享他的玩具。


近义词：thorny, spiny, irritable, touchy, difficult |反义词：smooth, pleasant, easy, agreeable
