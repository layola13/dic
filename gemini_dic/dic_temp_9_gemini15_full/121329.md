misgauge:/mɪsˈɡeɪdʒ/| vt.|误判，误估，错误地判断|To judge or assess incorrectly; to misunderstand.|例句：He misgauged the depth of the river and almost drowned. 他误判了河的深度，差点淹死。|例句：The company misgauged the public's reaction to its new product.  这家公司误判了公众对其新产品的反应。|近义词：misjudge, miscalculate, underestimate|反义词：accurately assess, correctly judge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misgauge the market demand. = 他们误判了市场需求。
He misgauges his opponent's strength. = 他误判了对手的实力。
