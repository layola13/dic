east-south-east: /ˌiːstˌsaʊθˈiːst/ | adj. | 东南偏东 |  East-south-east is a compass direction that lies between southeast and east. It is approximately 112.5 degrees from true north.  | The wind was blowing from east-south-east. 风是从东南偏东方向吹来的。 | southeast, east | northwest | adjective
