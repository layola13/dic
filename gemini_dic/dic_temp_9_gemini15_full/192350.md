scrimping:/ˈskrɪmpɪŋ/| v.| 吝啬；节俭；节省；勉强维持|To use or spend as little as possible; to be frugal or economical, often to an excessive degree.|例句：We're scrimping and saving to buy a new house. 我们正在省吃俭用买新房子。


例句：They were scrimping on food to make ends meet. 他们为了维持生计而节衣缩食。


近义词：saving, economizing, pinching, parsimonious|反义词：squandering, splurging, extravagant|词性:动词
