riden:/ˈrɪdn/| v.| 过去分词 | 骑；乘坐；被骑 | ridden is the past participle of the verb "ride". It describes a state of having been ridden or carried on something.  | The horse was ridden hard. 这匹马被骑得很辛苦。|The children were ridden on the carousel. 孩子们乘坐旋转木马。| rode, ridden | unridden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rides his bike to school every day. 他每天骑自行车上学。
She rides the bus to work. 她坐公交车上班。

一般过去时:
I rode my horse yesterday. 我昨天骑了我的马。
They rode the roller coaster. 他们玩了过山车。

现在完成时:
He has ridden that horse many times. 他骑过那匹马很多次了。
She has ridden a camel before. 她以前骑过骆驼。

过去完成时:
I had ridden my bike for hours before I stopped. 我骑自行车好几个小时才停下来。
They had ridden the train all night. 他们坐火车坐了一整夜。

将来时:
I will ride my bike to the park tomorrow. 我明天将骑自行车去公园。
She will ride the train to the city. 她将坐火车去城市。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would ride it every day. 如果我有一匹马，我每天都会骑它。
I wish I had ridden that roller coaster. 我真希望我玩过那个过山车。

被动语态:
The horse was ridden by a young girl. 这匹马被一个年轻女孩骑着。
The bike was ridden into a tree. 自行车撞到了一棵树上。

疑问句:
Have you ever ridden a motorcycle? 你曾经骑过摩托车吗？
Did he ride his bike to school today? 他今天骑自行车上学了吗？

否定句:
I didn't ride my bike today. 我今天没骑自行车。
She hasn't ridden a horse in years. 她好多年没骑过马了。


PHRASE->
be ridden with:充满（某种不好的东西）
ride shotgun:坐在副驾驶座上
ride herd on:严加看管；监督
ride the wave:顺势而为
ride out:渡过（难关）
