primus:/ˈpraɪməs/| adj. n.|第一的；首位的；最好的；| first in rank or importance; chief; primary; principal.  A leading or preeminent person or thing.| 例句：He was the primus inter pares among the negotiators. 他是谈判人员中的佼佼者。| 例句：The primus of the choir sang a beautiful solo. 合唱团的第一人唱了一首优美的独唱。|近义词：first, chief, principal, foremost, leading|反义词：last, secondary, inferior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The primus inter pares leads the team. = 团队领袖领导着团队。
This product is the primus in its category. = 这个产品在其类别中是最好的。


一般过去时:
He was the primus of his class. = 他是他班上的第一名。
The primus of the orchestra conducted the symphony. = 乐团的第一小提琴演奏家指挥了交响乐。


现在完成时:
She has always been the primus in her field. = 她一直是她领域的佼佼者。
This company has remained the primus in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面一直保持领先地位。


过去完成时:
He had been the primus of the debate team before graduating. = 他在毕业前一直是辩论队的领袖。
The team had already established itself as the primus in the league before the final match. = 在决赛之前，该队已经在联赛中确立了领先地位。


将来时:
He will be the primus of the next generation of leaders. = 他将成为下一代领导人的佼佼者。
This technology will likely become the primus in the market. = 这项技术很可能成为市场上的领先者。


虚拟语气:
If he were the primus, he would have more influence. = 如果他是领袖，他就会有更大的影响力。
I wish I were the primus in my profession. = 我希望我能成为我职业领域的佼佼者。


被动语态:
He was chosen as the primus by the committee. = 他被委员会选为领袖。
The new product was marketed as the primus in its class. = 新产品被推销为同类产品中的佼佼者。


疑问句:
Is he the primus of the organization? = 他是这个组织的领导者吗？
Which company is the primus in the industry? = 哪个公司是该行业的领导者？


否定句:
He is not the primus of the group. = 他不是这组人的领导者。
This model is not the primus in terms of efficiency. = 就效率而言，这款型号并非最佳。
