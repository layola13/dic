fluffiest:/ˈflʌfiːst/ | adj. | 最蓬松的，最柔软的 | Most soft and light; resembling fluff.  It is the superlative form of "fluffy". |  The fluffiest clouds drifted lazily across the sky.  最蓬松的云朵懒洋洋地飘过天空。The cat's fluffiest tail brushed against my leg.  猫最蓬松的尾巴扫过我的腿。 | fluffy, soft, downy | hard, stiff, coarse | 形容词
