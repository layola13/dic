hent:/hɛnt/| n.|暗示；线索；诱饵|A hint or suggestion; an indirect indication.  It often implies something is subtly communicated or suggested, rather than explicitly stated.|例句：He gave me a hent that he was unhappy. 他暗示他不开心。|例句：The detective followed the hent and found the missing documents.  侦探循着线索找到了丢失的文件。|近义词：clue, suggestion, implication, intimation|反义词：explicit statement, clear explanation
