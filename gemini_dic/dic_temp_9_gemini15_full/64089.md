epodic:/ɪˈpɑːdɪk/| adj.|诗节的；(诗歌)分节的；| Relating to or characteristic of an epode; consisting of or arranged in epodes.  An epode is a lyric poem which is usually a short poem with a distinct structure.  |The epodic structure of the poem enhanced its rhythmic impact.|近义词：strophic, lyrical|反义词：monostrophic
