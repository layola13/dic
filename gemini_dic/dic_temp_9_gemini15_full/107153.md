laevo:/ˈleɪvoʊ/| adj.|左旋的，左方的|Left-rotating;  describing a substance that rotates the plane of polarized light to the left (counterclockwise).  It is often used in chemistry and pharmacology to describe the optical isomer of a chiral molecule. |例句：The laevo isomer of lactic acid is found in sour milk. 左旋乳酸存在于酸奶中。The drug's laevo form is more potent than the dextro form. 该药物的左旋异构体比右旋异构体更有效。|近义词：levorotatory, left-handed (in a figurative sense)|反义词：dextro, dextrorotatory, right-handed (in a figurative sense)|形容词
