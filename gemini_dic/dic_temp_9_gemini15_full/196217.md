sequestree:/sɪˈkwɛstriː/| n. | 扣押财产；被扣押的财产 | A sequesteree is property or assets that have been seized by a court or other authority.  This is often done to ensure the property is available to satisfy a judgment or to prevent its dissipation. | Example: The judge ordered the sequestration of the defendant's assets.  法官下令扣押被告的资产。|近义词：attachment, impoundment |反义词：release, liberation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court sequesters the property. = 法院扣押该财产。
He sequesters his emotions. = 他压抑自己的情绪。

一般过去时:
The judge sequestered the assets. = 法官扣押了资产。
