sigher:/ˈsaɪɡər/| n. |叹息者，叹气的人|A person who sighs frequently or dramatically.  A sigher is someone who expresses sadness, weariness, or relief through frequent sighing.|例句：He was a constant sigher, his breaths heavy with unspoken worries. 他是个经常叹气的人，他的呼吸充满了未言明的担忧。|近义词：complainer, melancholic|反义词：optimist, cheerful|名词
