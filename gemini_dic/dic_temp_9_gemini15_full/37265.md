comites:/kəˈmaɪtiːz/| n.|委员会；同人会|A comites is a group of people who come together for a common purpose, often to advise, manage, or oversee something.  It can refer to a formal committee with specific responsibilities or a more informal group of associates.|例句：The school's comites decided on a new curriculum. 学校的委员会决定了一个新的课程。|近义词：committee, council, board|反义词:individual, lone person.
