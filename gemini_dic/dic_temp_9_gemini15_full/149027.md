owler:/ˈaʊlər/| n. |猫头鹰捕猎者；（尤指）偷猎猫头鹰的人|A person who hunts or (especially) illegally kills owls.|The owler was caught red-handed with a dead great horned owl.  偷猎者被当场抓住，手里拿着一只死去的角鸮。| poacher, hunter |  (no direct antonym)|名词
