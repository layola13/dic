morgan:/ˈmɔːrɡən/| n. |摩根（姓氏）；摩根币（古币）|Morgan is a common surname of Welsh origin, meaning "sea-born" or "of the sea". It is also the name of an ancient type of coin.|例句：His name is Morgan Freeman.他的名字是摩根·弗里曼。

例句：The Morgan dollar was a popular coin in the late 19th century.摩根银元在19世纪后期很流行。|近义词：sea-born (as a meaning)|反义词：无
