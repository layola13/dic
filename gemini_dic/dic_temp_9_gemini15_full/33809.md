circumflexed:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌflɛkst/ | adj. | 带抑扬符的；弯曲的；拱形的 | Having a circumflex accent mark; curved or arched.  The word describes something that has the shape of a circumflex, a diacritical mark resembling a curved line. It can refer to both the mark itself and the shape it represents. |例句：The word "août" is circumflexed in French. 法语单词“août”带有抑扬符。

The letter "â" is circumflexed. 字母“â”带有抑扬符。|近义词：arched, curved, bent |反义词：straight, unbent, flat
