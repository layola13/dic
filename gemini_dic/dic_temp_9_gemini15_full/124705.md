mows:/moʊz/| vt.|割草，修剪|To cut down (grass, etc.) with a mowing machine or scythe.|例句：He mows the lawn every Saturday. 他每个星期六都割草。|例句：The farmer mows the hay with a scythe. 农民用镰刀割草。|近义词：cut, trim, reap|反义词：grow, cultivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mows the lawn every week. = 他每周都割草。
She mows the grass with a new mower. = 她用新的割草机割草。


一般过去时:
He mowed the lawn yesterday. = 他昨天割了草。
They mowed the field before the storm. = 暴风雨来临前他们割了田里的庄稼。


现在完成时:
He has mowed the lawn already. = 他已经割了草。
She has mowed the entire field. = 她已经割完了整个田地。


过去完成时:
He had mowed the lawn before it rained. = 下雨之前他已经割了草。
They had mowed the hay before the sun set. = 日落之前他们已经割好了干草。


将来时:
He will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 他明天将要割草。
She will mow the field after the rain stops. = 雨停之后她将要割田。


虚拟语气:
If he had a lawnmower, he would mow the lawn himself. = 如果他有一台割草机，他会自己割草。
I wish I had mowed the lawn earlier. = 我真希望我早点割草。


被动语态:
The lawn is mowed every week. = 草坪每周都被割。
The field was mowed by the farmer. = 田地是由农民割的。


疑问句:
Does he mow the lawn himself? = 他自己割草吗？
Who mows the lawn in your house? = 你们家谁割草？


否定句:
He doesn't mow the lawn often. = 他不经常割草。
She didn't mow the lawn yesterday. = 她昨天没有割草。
