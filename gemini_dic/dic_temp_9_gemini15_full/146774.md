overidealistic: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiːəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于理想化的；理想主义过头的 | Excessively idealistic; characterized by unrealistic optimism and a tendency to ignore practical considerations.  |  His overidealistic plans for world peace were ultimately doomed to fail.  他的过于理想化的世界和平计划最终注定要失败。 |近义词：utopian, unrealistic, impractical, visionary | 反义词：realistic, pragmatic, practical
