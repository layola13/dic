millionary: /ˌmɪljəˈneri/ | n. | 百万富翁 | A person who has a million or more dollars.  | He hopes to become a millionaire one day. 他希望有一天能成为百万富翁。 | billionaire, tycoon | pauper, beggar |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a millionaire. = 他是一个百万富翁。
She is a successful millionaire. = 她是一位成功的百万富翁。

一般过去时:
He was a millionaire before the economic crisis. = 经济危机前他是个百万富翁。
My grandfather was a self-made millionaire. = 我的祖父是一位白手起家的百万富翁。

现在完成时:
He has become a millionaire through hard work. = 他通过努力工作成为了百万富翁。
She has been a millionaire for many years. = 她已经成为百万富翁很多年了。

过去完成时:
He had already been a millionaire before he started his charity. = 他在开始做慈善之前就已经是百万富翁了。
She had never thought she would become a millionaire. = 她从未想过自己会成为百万富翁。

将来时:
He will be a millionaire by the time he is forty. = 到他四十岁的时候，他将成为百万富翁。
She will probably be a millionaire if her business succeeds. = 如果她的生意成功，她很可能会成为百万富翁。

虚拟语气:
If I were a millionaire, I would travel the world. = 如果我是个百万富翁，我会环游世界。
I wish I were a millionaire so I could help the poor. = 我希望我是个百万富翁，这样我就能帮助穷人了。

被动语态:
(This word rarely uses passive voice.  A passive construction would be awkward and unnatural.)


疑问句:
Is he a millionaire? = 他是百万富翁吗？
Are they millionaires? = 他们是百万富翁吗？

否定句:
He is not a millionaire. = 他不是百万富翁。
She is not yet a millionaire, but she's working on it. = 她还不是百万富翁，但她正在努力。
