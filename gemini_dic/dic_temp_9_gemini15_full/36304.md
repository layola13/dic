cohesion's:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ | n. |内聚性；凝聚力；结合；团结 |The state of sticking together; the act or state of uniting or combining.  It refers to the action or state of forming a united whole.  In different contexts, it can refer to the internal forces holding a material together, the unity of a group, or the logical connection within a piece of writing.|


例句：

The cohesion of the team was essential to their success.  = 团队的凝聚力对他们的成功至关重要。

The essay lacked cohesion; the ideas weren't logically connected. = 这篇文章缺乏连贯性；观点没有逻辑联系。


近义词：unity, solidarity, coherence, adhesion

反义词：disunity, fragmentation, incoherence, discord


词性：名词 (复数形式，表示cohesion的所属格)
