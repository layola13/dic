sampler:/ˈsæmplər/ | n. | 样品；采样器；示例；（早期刺绣）样品绣| A sampler is a small, representative sample of something, often used to show the quality or variety of a larger product or collection. It can also refer to a device used for taking samples, or an example or illustration.  In a historical context, a sampler is a piece of needlework showing various stitches. |例句：The company sent us a sampler of their new perfume.  这家公司寄给了我们他们新香水的样品。|例句：The chef prepared a sampler platter of appetizers. 主厨准备了一个开胃菜拼盘样品。|近义词：sample, specimen, example, illustration |反义词：whole, entirety
