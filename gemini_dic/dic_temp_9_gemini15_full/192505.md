scrupulize:/ˈskruːpjʊlaɪz/ | v. | 仔细考虑；认真斟酌；过分小心 | To examine or consider something very carefully and cautiously, often to an excessive degree.  | He scrupulized over every detail of the contract before signing. 他在签合同之前仔细斟酌了合同的每一个细节。 | ponder, deliberate, contemplate | act rashly, rush into | 动词


USAGE->

They scrupulize before making a commitment. = 他们在做出承诺之前会仔细斟酌。

过去完成时:

词源:
源自拉丁语 *scrūpulus，意为“小石头；顾虑；犹豫”。  这个词根也与英语单词 scruple（顾虑，不安）相关。
