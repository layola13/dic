hircinous: /ˈhɜːrsɪnəs/ | adj. | 粗鲁的；令人作呕的；令人厌恶的 |  Having a strong, unpleasant smell; rank; fetid.  The word often implies a smell associated with goats or other strong-smelling animals.  | 例句：The hircinous odor emanating from the barn was almost unbearable.  = 从谷仓散发出的令人作呕的气味几乎让人无法忍受。


The hircinous smell of the unwashed clothes filled the room. = 未洗衣服的臭味充满了房间。


近义词：fetid, rank, malodorous, foul-smelling, reeking


反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic


词源:  From Latin *hircus "goat," related to Greek  *khīros "goat," and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷḗr̥- "goat".  The suffix "-inous" indicates a quality or characteristic of.  Therefore, "hircinous" literally means "goat-like" in terms of smell.
