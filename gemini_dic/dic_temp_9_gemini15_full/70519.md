filet:/fɪˈleɪ/| n.|菲力牛排；净肉|A filet is a cut of meat, typically beef, that is tender and lean.  It is often considered a delicacy.  The term can also refer to the tenderloin, the most tender part of the beef. |例句：The filet mignon was cooked to perfection.  菲力牛排做得恰到好处。

The restaurant is known for its delicious filet. 这家餐厅以其美味的菲力牛排而闻名。|近义词：tenderloin,loin|反义词：tough cut, fatty cut|名词

词源:

The word "filet" comes from the Old French word "filet," meaning "thread" or "strip."  This refers to the long, thin shape of the cut of meat.  The culinary usage evolved from this original meaning.
