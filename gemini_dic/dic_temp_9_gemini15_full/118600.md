mess about:/mes əˈbaʊt/| v.|胡闹，瞎搞，浪费时间 |to spend time doing unimportant things or activities, often in a playful or unproductive way.  |例句：They spent the afternoon messing about on the beach. 他们下午在海滩上瞎玩。|例句：Stop messing about and get on with your work! 别胡闹了，赶紧工作！|近义词：fool around, muck about, dawdle, idle|反义词：work diligently, be productive


USAGE->

They mess about in the garden every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在花园里瞎玩。
We often mess about with our friends on weekends. = 我们周末经常和朋友们一起胡闹。

一般过去时:
He messed about all day and didn't finish his homework. = 他整天瞎混，没完成作业。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.  The focus is always on the active messing about.)
