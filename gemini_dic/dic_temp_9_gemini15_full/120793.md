misarticulates:/ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪts/| vt.|发音不准，表达不清|To pronounce (a word or words) incorrectly or unclearly.  The act of pronouncing words incorrectly or unclearly.|例句：He misarticulates many words, making him difficult to understand. 他很多单词发音不准，让人难以理解。

例句：The child misarticulates frequently due to a speech impediment.  孩子因为口吃经常发音不准。


近义词：mispronounce, slur, mumble, stammer


反义词：articulate clearly, pronounce correctly


词性:动词
