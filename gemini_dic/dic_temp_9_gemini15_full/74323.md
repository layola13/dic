fragger:/ˈfræɡər/| n. v.|碎片制造者；（游戏术语）使用碎片武器的人|A person who uses fragmentation grenades or other explosive devices that create fragments.  In gaming, it often refers to someone who frequently uses frag grenades.|例句：He's a skilled fragger in the game; his grenades always land perfectly. 他是游戏中熟练的碎片武器使用者；他的手榴弹总是精准投掷。|近义词：grenadier, bomber|反义词：healer, support
