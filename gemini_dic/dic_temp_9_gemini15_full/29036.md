cartoonists:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪsts/ | n. |漫画家 | A person who draws cartoons.  Cartoonists often work for newspapers, magazines, or websites, creating humorous or satirical illustrations. |Many talented cartoonists contributed to the magazine's success.| 近义词：comic artists, illustrators | 反义词：无
