penetrate:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪt/|/ˈpɛnɪtreɪt/| vt. vi. n.|穿透；渗透；洞察；理解|to force a way into or through something; to affect or influence something deeply|例句：The rain penetrated the tent. 雨水渗透了帐篷。The knife penetrated his heart. 刀子刺穿了他的心脏。|近义词：pierce, perforate, permeate, understand|反义词：surface, exterior, shallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sunlight penetrates the leaves. =阳光穿透树叶。
The aroma penetrates the air. = 香气弥漫在空气中。

一般过去时:
The bullet penetrated the wall. = 子弹穿透了墙壁。
She penetrated the enemy's defenses. = 她突破了敌人的防线。

现在完成时:
The idea has penetrated the public consciousness. = 这个想法已经深入人心。
The rain has penetrated the soil. = 雨水已经渗透到土壤中。

过去完成时:
The roots had penetrated deep into the earth. = 根已经深入地下。
The message had penetrated his defenses. = 信息已经突破了他的心理防线。

将来时:
The missile will penetrate the enemy's shield. = 导弹将穿透敌人的防御盾。
The truth will eventually penetrate. = 真相最终会大白。

虚拟语气:
If the knife had penetrated deeper, he would have died. = 如果刀子刺得更深，他就会死了。
If only the message had penetrated, the conflict could have been avoided. = 如果信息能传达出去，冲突本来是可以避免的。

被动语态:
The wall was penetrated by the bullet. = 墙壁被子弹穿透了。
The enemy's defenses were penetrated by the troops. = 敌人的防线被军队突破了。

疑问句:
Did the message penetrate? = 信息传达了吗？
Can light penetrate this material? = 光线能穿透这种材料吗？

否定句:
The water didn't penetrate the roof. = 水没有渗透屋顶。
His words didn't penetrate her defenses. = 他的话没有打动她。

词源:
penetrate comes from the Latin word "penetrare," meaning "to go through," "to pierce," or "to enter."  The prefix "pen-" is related to words like "per-" and implies thoroughness or completeness, while "-trare" is connected to words indicating movement or action.
