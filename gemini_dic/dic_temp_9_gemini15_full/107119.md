ladybirds:/ˈleɪdiˌbɜːrdz/ | n. |瓢虫，七星瓢虫 |A ladybird is a small, round beetle, typically red or orange with black spots.  They are beneficial insects because they eat aphids and other plant pests. |例句：The children were delighted to find a ladybird crawling on a leaf. 孩子们高兴地发现一片叶子上爬着一只瓢虫。

例句：We saw many ladybirds in the garden.我们在花园里看到了许多瓢虫。
