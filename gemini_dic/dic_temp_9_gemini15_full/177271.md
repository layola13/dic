reassessments:/ˌriːəˈsɛsmənts/ | n. |重新评估；再评估 |The act of assessing something again, often after new information or circumstances have arisen.  It involves reviewing previous judgments, data, or plans and making adjustments based on updated knowledge. |例句：The project requires reassessments of its budget and timeline. 项目需要重新评估其预算和时间表。

例句：After the earthquake, engineers conducted reassessments of the building's structural integrity. 地震后，工程师们对建筑物的结构完整性进行了重新评估。


近义词：reevaluations, re-examinations, reviews |反义词：initial assessments, first evaluations |词性:名词
