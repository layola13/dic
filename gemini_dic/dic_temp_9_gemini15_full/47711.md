decachordon:/dekəˈkɔːrdən/| n. |十弦琴；十弦乐器|A decachordon is a ten-stringed lyre or other musical instrument.  The term is primarily found in historical contexts relating to ancient Greek music.  It is rarely used in modern musical terminology.|Example: The ancient Greek poet Sappho is said to have played the decachordon.  古代希腊诗人萨福据说演奏过十弦琴。|近义词：ten-stringed instrument, lyre (if referring to a lyre-type instrument)|反义词：None specifically, but terms referring to instruments with fewer strings could be considered contrasting.|名词
