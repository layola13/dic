diverberate:/daɪˈvɜːrbəreɪt/| vt.|使回响；使回荡；反复思考|to resound or reverberate; to think over repeatedly|The thunder reverberated through the mountains. 雷声在山谷中回荡。|The news reverberated around the world.  这个消息传遍了全世界。|resonate, echo|muffle, silence|动词


USAGE->

The sound reverberates through the hall. = 声音在大厅里回荡。
She reverberates her concerns to her boss. = 她向老板反复表达她的担忧。
