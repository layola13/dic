gravitrons:/ˈɡrævɪtrɒnz/| n. |重力子（假想粒子）|A gravitron is a hypothetical elementary particle that mediates the force of gravity in some theories of quantum gravity.  It's analogous to the photon, which mediates the electromagnetic force.  The existence of gravitons has not been experimentally confirmed.|例句：Some theories propose the existence of gravitrons to explain gravity at the quantum level.  一些理论提出重力子的存在来解释量子水平的引力。

The search for gravitons is a major challenge in modern physics.寻找重力子是现代物理学中的一大挑战。|近义词：graviton (singular)|反义词：无
