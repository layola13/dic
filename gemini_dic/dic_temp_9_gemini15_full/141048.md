onlooker:/ˈɒnˌlʊkə(r)/| n. |旁观者，目击者 | A person who is present at an event or incident but does not participate.  |例句：The onlookers watched in horror as the building collapsed. 旁观者们惊恐地看着大楼倒塌。|例句：Several onlookers gave statements to the police.  几个旁观者向警方作了证词。|近义词：observer, spectator, witness |反义词：participant, actor

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The onlooker watches the accident. = 旁观者看着事故。
He observes the situation from afar. = 他从远处观察情况。

一般过去时:
The onlookers watched the parade. = 旁观者观看了游行。

He has observed many changes in the city. = 他观察到城市里发生了许多变化。
