nonmineral: /ˌnɒnˈmɪnərəl/ | adj. |非矿物的| Not consisting of or derived from minerals; organic.  This adjective describes substances or materials that are not composed of minerals.  It indicates the absence of mineral components, suggesting an organic or synthetic origin instead.

例句：
The soil contained a high percentage of nonmineral matter. = 这土壤含有很高比例的非矿物质。

The analysis revealed a significant amount of nonmineral components in the sample. = 分析显示样品中含有大量的非矿物成分。


近义词：organic, nonmetallic

反义词：mineral


词性：形容词
