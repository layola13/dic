premiere:/prɪˈmɪər/ | n., v.| 首映式；首次公演；首发；首演 |  A premiere is the first public performance or showing of a film, play, or other work. As a verb, it means to have its first public performance or showing. | 例句：The film's premiere was a huge success.  这部电影的首映式非常成功。

The play premiered in London last year. 这部戏剧去年在伦敦首演。

近义词：debut, opening, launch | 反义词：re-release, encore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The film premieres next week. = 电影下周首映。
She premieres her new collection tonight. = 她今晚首发她的新系列。


一般过去时:
The movie premiered last night. = 电影昨晚首映。
The band premiered their new album last month. = 乐队上个月首发了他们的新专辑。


现在完成时:
The play has already premiered in New York. = 这部剧已经在纽约首演了。
They have premiered three new songs this year. = 他们今年已经首发了三首新歌。


过去完成时:
The director had premiered several films before this one. = 这名导演在此之前已经首映过几部电影了。
The artist had premiered her work in several galleries before this exhibition. = 这名艺术家在此次展览之前已经在几家画廊展出过她的作品。


将来时:
The show will premiere next month. = 这部剧将于下个月首映。
The new game will premiere on all major platforms. = 新游戏将在所有主要平台上首发。


虚拟语气:
If the play premiered earlier, it would have been a bigger hit. = 如果这部剧早点首演，它会更受欢迎。
I wish the movie had premiered sooner. = 我希望这部电影早点首映。


被动语态:
The movie was premiered at the Cannes Film Festival. = 这部电影在戛纳电影节首映。
The album was premiered on radio stations across the country. = 这张专辑在全国各地的广播电台首播。


疑问句:
When does the new album premiere? = 新专辑什么时候首发？
Where will the play premiere? = 这部剧将在哪里首演？


否定句:
The show did not premiere as scheduled. = 这部剧没有按计划首映。
The new song hasn't premiered yet. = 这首新歌还没首发呢。


PHRASE->
world premiere: 世界首映
premiere performance: 首演
premiere screening: 首映式放映
have its premiere: 首映
a gala premiere: 隆重的首映式


词源:
From French *première*, feminine singular of *premier* "first", from Latin *primus* "first".
