insolvency: /ɪnˈsɒlvənsi/ | n. | 破产；无力偿债 | The state of being insolvent; inability to pay one's debts.  | Example: The company's insolvency led to the loss of many jobs.  这家公司的破产导致许多人失业。 | 近义词：bankruptcy, default, insolvency | 反义词：solvency

His insolvency is a serious problem. = 他的无力偿债是个严重的问题。

一般过去时:
