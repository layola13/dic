frier:/ˈfraɪər/| n. |油炸锅；油炸者|A frier is a device used for deep-frying food, or a person who fries food.  It can also refer to a type of bird. |例句：The deep-fat frier is a very useful kitchen appliance.  = 深油炸锅是一种非常有用的厨房用具。

The chef, a skilled frier, prepared crispy fries. =这位厨师，一位熟练的油炸工，准备了脆薯条。|近义词：deep fryer, fryer (as a noun)|反义词：baker (in the context of food preparation)
