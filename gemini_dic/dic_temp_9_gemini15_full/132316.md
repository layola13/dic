nondefining:/ˌnɒnˈdɪfaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|非限定的，非限制性的|  A nondefining clause (also called a nonrestrictive clause) adds extra information about a noun or pronoun, but it's not essential to the meaning of the sentence.  It's set off by commas. |例句：My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor. (我的哥哥住在伦敦，他是一名医生。)  My sister, a talented musician, plays the piano beautifully. (我的妹妹是一位有天赋的音乐家，她钢琴弹得非常好。) |近义词：nonrestrictive, supplementary|反义词：defining, restrictive
