shocks:/ʃɑːks/| n. v.|震惊；冲击；震动|A sudden, unexpected, and usually unpleasant event or experience.  To surprise or upset someone greatly.|例句：The news of his death came as a terrible shock.  他的死讯令人震惊。
The earthquake shocked the city. 地震震动了这座城市。|近义词：surprise, stun, jolt, impact|反义词：calm, soothe, comfort|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news shocks me. = 这个消息让我震惊。
This event shocks the public. = 这件事震惊了公众。


一般过去时:
The news shocked her deeply. = 这个消息深深地震惊了她。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
