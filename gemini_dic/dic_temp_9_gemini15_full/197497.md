sheikhdoms:/ˈʃeɪkdəm/| n.|酋长国；酋长辖地|The plural of sheikhdom, referring to territories ruled by a sheikh (a male leader in some Arab societies).  These are often small, independent states. |例句：The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven sheikhdoms.  阿联酋是由七个酋长国组成的联邦。|例句：The history of these sheikhdoms is complex and intertwined. 这些酋长国的历史复杂且相互交织。|近义词：principalities, emirates|反义词：kingdoms (in the sense of scale and centralized power)|名词
