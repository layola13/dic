harbour:/ˈhɑːbər/ /ˈhɑːrbər/| n. vt.|港口；避风港；藏匿处；怀有（感情等）|A place on the coast where ships can find shelter, especially one with special facilities for loading and unloading goods.  A harbour can also refer to a place of refuge or shelter, or to harboring feelings or thoughts.|例句：The ship sailed into the harbour. 这艘船驶进了港口。

The small town has a picturesque harbour. 这个小镇有一个风景如画的港口。


例句：She harboured a secret resentment towards her brother. 她对她的哥哥怀有一种秘密的怨恨。

He harboured hopes of winning the lottery. 他怀抱着赢得彩票的希望。


近义词：port, haven, shelter, harbor (US spelling)|反义词：open sea, expose
