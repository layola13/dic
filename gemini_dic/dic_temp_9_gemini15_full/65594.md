evaluate:/ɪˈvæljuˌeɪt/ | vt. |评估；评价；估价 | To form an idea of the amount, number, or value of something.  |例句：The teacher will evaluate your performance based on your test scores and class participation.老师会根据你的考试成绩和课堂参与情况来评估你的表现。

例句：We need to evaluate the risks before making a decision. 我们需要在做决定之前评估风险。


近义词：assess, appraise, judge, rate | 反义词：neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He evaluates the situation carefully. 他仔细评估形势。
She evaluates her employees' performance regularly. 她定期评估员工的表现。


一般过去时:
The committee evaluated the proposals last week. 委员会上周评估了这些提案。
They evaluated the damage caused by the storm. 他们评估了风暴造成的损失。


现在完成时:
We have evaluated the project's progress. 我们已经评估了项目的进展。
She has evaluated all the applicants for the job. 她已经评估了所有求职者的申请。


过去完成时:
They had evaluated the risks before investing. 他们在投资之前已经评估了风险。
The manager had evaluated the employee's performance before the meeting.  经理在会议之前已经评估了员工的表现。


将来时:
We will evaluate the results next month. 我们下个月将评估结果。
The board will evaluate the new strategy in the coming year.  董事会将在来年评估新的战略。


虚拟语气:
If I had more information, I could evaluate the situation better. 如果我有更多信息，我就能更好地评估形势。
It would be helpful if we could evaluate the options before deciding. 如果我们在决定之前能够评估各种选择，那将很有帮助。


被动语态:
The proposal was evaluated by the committee. 提案由委员会进行了评估。
The damage was evaluated to be extensive.  损失被评估为巨大。


疑问句:
How will you evaluate the success of the project? 你将如何评估项目的成功？
How did they evaluate the quality of the products?  他们是如何评估产品质量的？


否定句:
They did not evaluate the long-term consequences. 他们没有评估长远的后果。
We have not yet evaluated the full impact of the decision. 我们尚未评估该决定的全部影响。


PHRASE->

evaluate the evidence 评估证据
evaluate the impact 评估影响
evaluate the risks 评估风险
evaluate the options 评估选择
