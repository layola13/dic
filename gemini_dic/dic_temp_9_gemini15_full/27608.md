cancellation:/ˌkænsəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |取消；作废；废止 | The act of canceling something; the state of being canceled.  It refers to the act of officially ending or stopping something that was planned or scheduled. This could be anything from a flight to a contract to a subscription. |

例句：

The cancellation of the flight caused significant disruption. =航班取消造成了很大的混乱。

The contract cancellation was due to a breach of agreement. =合同的取消是由于违反协议造成的。


近义词： annulment, revocation, termination, voiding

反义词： confirmation, ratification, approval


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The airline often makes cancellations due to bad weather. = 航空公司经常因为恶劣天气而取消航班。
This company rarely makes cancellations. =这家公司很少取消订单。


一般过去时:
They announced the cancellation of the concert last night. = 他们昨晚宣布取消音乐会。
The sudden cancellation of the meeting surprised everyone. = 会议的突然取消让每个人都感到惊讶。


现在完成时:
There have been several cancellations due to the storm. = 由于暴风雨，已经取消了好几个航班。
She has received notification of her order cancellation. = 她收到了订单取消的通知。


过去完成时:
They had already received the cancellation notice before they bought the tickets. = 他们在买票之前就已经收到了取消通知。
The project had been cancelled before they started working. = 项目在他们开始工作之前就被取消了。


将来时:
They will announce the cancellation if the weather worsens. =如果天气恶化，他们将宣布取消。
We will inform you immediately of any cancellations. =我们会立即通知您任何取消的情况。


虚拟语气:
If there were more cancellations, they would lose money. = 如果有更多取消，他们会赔钱。
I wish there hadn't been a cancellation of my flight. = 我真希望我的航班没有取消。


被动语态:
The event was canceled due to low ticket sales. =由于门票销售低迷，活动被取消了。
Many flights were canceled because of the snowstorm. = 由于暴风雪，许多航班被取消了。


疑问句:
Are there any cancellations today? =今天有什么取消的吗？
Was the meeting cancellation necessary? = 取消会议有必要吗？


否定句:
There were no cancellations of the train schedule. = 火车时刻表没有取消。
The show was not cancelled despite the rain. = 尽管下雨，演出并没有取消。
