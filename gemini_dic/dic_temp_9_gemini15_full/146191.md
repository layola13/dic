overexertion:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrʃən/| n. |过度劳累；用力过度|The act of exerting oneself too much; excessive effort.  It often leads to physical or mental exhaustion.|例句：His overexertion during the marathon led to muscle cramps. 他在马拉松比赛中过度用力导致肌肉痉挛。


例句：The overexertion of the workers resulted in a significant drop in productivity. 工人们过度劳累导致生产力大幅下降。


近义词：overstrain, overwork, exhaustion, fatigue


反义词：rest, relaxation, inactivity


词性：名词
