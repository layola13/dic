lopers:/ˈloʊpərz/| n.|长途跋涉者，慢跑者|A looper is someone who travels long distances on foot, often for leisure or exercise, or someone who runs slowly for an extended period.|例句：The hikers, experienced lopers, easily navigated the challenging terrain.  这些徒步旅行者，经验丰富的长途跋涉者，轻松地穿越了具有挑战性的地形。  例句：He is a keen looper, completing a marathon every year. 他是一个狂热的慢跑者，每年都完成一次马拉松。|近义词：hiker, jogger, walker, runner|反义词：sprinter (in the context of speed)
