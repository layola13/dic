face-toward: /ˈfeɪsˌtɔːrd/ | adj. | 面向...的，朝向...的 |  Describes something oriented or directed towards a particular direction or person.  It indicates the direction something is facing. | Example: The house is face-toward the east.  这房子朝东。 The audience faced toward the stage. 观众面向舞台。 |近义词: facing, directed toward, oriented toward | 反义词: away from, back to
