shockproofs: /ˈʃɒkpruːfs/ | n. | 防震装置；抗震措施 |  Protective devices or measures designed to prevent damage from shocks or impacts.  The plural form indicates multiple such devices or measures. | Example: The packaging included several shockproofs to protect the fragile equipment during shipping.  包装中包含多个防震装置，以保护易碎设备在运输过程中的安全。 | 近义词: shock absorbers, cushioning, protection | 反义词: shock-sensitive, vulnerable
