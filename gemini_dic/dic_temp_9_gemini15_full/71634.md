flatmate:/ˈflætmeɪt/ | n. |室友 |A person who shares a flat (apartment) with another person or people.  |Example: I'm looking for a flatmate to share the rent and bills. 我正在寻找一个室友来分担房租和账单。|近义词: housemate, roommate |反义词: N/A
