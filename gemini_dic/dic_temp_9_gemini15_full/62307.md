endearment:/ɪnˈdɪər.mənt/| n. |钟爱；疼爱；可爱之物|The act of endearing or the state of being endeared; fondness; affection.  It refers to a feeling of great love and affection, often associated with tenderness and fondness.  This can be directed towards a person, pet, or even an object. |例句：His endearment for his grandchildren was evident in his actions. 他对孙辈的疼爱在他的行动中显而易见。

例句：The old teddy bear held a special endearment for her.这只旧泰迪熊对她来说有着特殊的意义。(此处endearment指可爱之物)


近义词：affection, fondness, love, attachment, tenderness|反义词：dislike, hatred, aversion, contempt, indifference|词性：名词


词源：源于动词 "endear",  它本身来自古英语"ge-endearian",  意思是 "使…变得可爱".  前缀 "en-" 表示 "使…成为"，而 "dear" 则表示 "亲爱的，珍爱的"。
