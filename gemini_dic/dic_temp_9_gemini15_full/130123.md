non-rationalist: /ˌnɒnˈræʃənəlɪst/ | adj. | 非理性主义者；反理性主义者 |  Someone who rejects or doubts the power of reason to provide knowledge or solve problems;  one who opposes rationalism.  A non-rationalist approach often emphasizes intuition, emotion, experience, or faith over logical reasoning. | Example:  The artist embraced a non-rationalist approach, believing that intuition was the key to creative expression.  这位艺术家采用了非理性主义的方法，相信直觉是创造性表达的关键。 | 近义词: anti-rationalist, irrationalist | 反义词: rationalist |  词性: adjective
