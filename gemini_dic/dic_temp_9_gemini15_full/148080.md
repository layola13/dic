oversaw:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔː/|/ˌəʊvəˈsɔː/| vt.|监督；俯瞰；掌管 |To supervise (a person or their work) or oversee (a process).  It also means to have a view from above. |例句：She oversaw the project from start to finish. 她从头到尾监督了这个项目。

例句：From the hilltop, we oversaw the entire valley. 从山顶上，我们俯瞰着整个山谷。|近义词：supervise, superintend, manage, direct |反义词：neglect, ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He oversees the daily operations of the company. 他监督公司的日常运作。
She oversees the marketing department. 她负责市场部的工作。


一般过去时:
The manager oversaw the team's work on the new product. 经理监督了团队在新产品上的工作。
He oversaw the construction of the new bridge. 他监督了新桥的建设。


现在完成时:
She has overseen numerous successful projects throughout her career. 她在职业生涯中监督了许多成功的项目。
They have overseen the implementation of the new policy.  他们已经监督了新政策的实施。


过去完成时:
He had overseen several similar projects before taking on this one. 在接手这个项目之前，他已经监督过几个类似的项目了。
The supervisor had overseen the training program for years.  主管已经监督这个培训项目多年了。


将来时:
She will oversee the merger of the two companies. 她将监督两家公司的合并。
He will oversee the development of the new software. 他将监督新软件的开发。


虚拟语气:
If she oversaw the project, it would be completed on time. 如果她监督这个项目，它就能按时完成。
I wish he had overseen the project more closely. 我希望他当时更密切地监督这个项目。


被动语态:
The project was overseen by a team of experienced managers.  这个项目由一个经验丰富的管理团队监督。
The construction of the building was overseen by a qualified architect. 建筑物的建造由一位合格的建筑师监督。


疑问句:
Who oversaw the investigation? 谁监督了这次调查？
Will anyone oversee the implementation of the new system? 将会有谁监督新系统的实施吗？


否定句:
He did not oversee the project properly. 他没有好好地监督这个项目。
No one oversaw the work, which led to many mistakes. 没有人在监督这项工作，导致了很多错误。
