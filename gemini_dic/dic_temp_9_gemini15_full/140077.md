/wɑːt/ | pron. | 什么，什么东西 |  "What" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or people. | What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？ |  which, who, whom | nothing, nobody  | pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you say? = 你说什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you came? = 你来之前吃了什么？
What had they planned before the trip? = 他们旅行前计划了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没有说他想要什么。

虚拟语气:
What would you have done if you had known? = 如果你知道的话，你会怎么做？
If only I knew what to do! = 要是我知道该怎么做就好了！

被动语态:
What was decided by the committee? = 委员会决定了什么？
What measures are being taken to prevent this? = 正在采取什么措施来防止这种情况？

疑问句:
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？

否定句:
I don't understand what you mean. = 我不明白你的意思。
She didn't tell me what happened. = 她没有告诉我发生了什么事。
