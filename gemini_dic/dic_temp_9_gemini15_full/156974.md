pillagers:/ˈpɪlədʒərz/| n.|掠夺者，抢劫者|People who plunder or loot, especially in wartime.  They are often characterized by their violence and taking of possessions by force.|例句：The pillagers ransacked the village, leaving behind only destruction. 掠夺者洗劫了村庄，只留下满目疮痍。

例句：Bands of pillagers swept through the countryside, stealing livestock and valuables.成群的掠夺者横扫乡村，偷窃牲畜和贵重物品。


近义词：looters, plunderers, raiders, marauders, brigands

反义词：benefactors, protectors, saviors
