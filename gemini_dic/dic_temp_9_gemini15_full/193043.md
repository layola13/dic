secretary:/ˈsɛkrəteri/| n.|秘书，书记，大臣|A person employed by an individual or organization to deal with correspondence, keep records, and make appointments.|例句：She works as a secretary for a large law firm. 她在一间大型律师事务所做秘书。|例句：The secretary of state addressed the press. 国务卿向媒体发表讲话。|近义词：clerk, assistant, administrator|反义词：boss, superior, employer
