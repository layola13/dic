saturnalia:/ˌsætərˈneɪliə/| n. |狂欢节；萨图尔纳利亚节 |A period of unrestrained revelry and merrymaking, especially in ancient Rome, dedicated to Saturn.  It was characterized by role reversal, feasting, drinking, gift-giving, and general suspension of social norms. |Example: The annual office party descended into a saturnalia of drunken revelry.  一年一度的办公室派对变成了醉酒狂欢的狂欢节。 The ancient Romans celebrated Saturnalia with great enthusiasm. 古罗马人以极大的热情庆祝萨图尔纳利亚节。|近义词：revelry, festivity, bacchanal |反义词：asceticism, austerity
