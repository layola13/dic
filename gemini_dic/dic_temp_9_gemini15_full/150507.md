pantometry: /pænˈtɒmɪtri/ | n. | 全身测量法；人体测量法 | The measurement of the entire body; anthropometry.  It involves measuring various body dimensions to assess physical characteristics.  | Example: Pantometry is used in ergonomics to design furniture suitable for a wide range of body sizes. 全身测量法用于人体工程学中，以设计适合各种体型的人体家具。 | 近义词：anthropometry, somatometry | 反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be a lack of body measurement.
