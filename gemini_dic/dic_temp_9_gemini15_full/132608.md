nondiplomatically:/ˌnɑːnˌdɪpləˈmætɪkli/|/ˌnɒnˌdɪpləˈmætɪkli/| adv. |不外交地；直率地；不圆滑地 |Acting or speaking in a way that is blunt and direct, lacking tact or diplomacy.  It implies a disregard for the social graces and sensitivities usually associated with diplomatic communication. |例句：He nondiplomatically told her that her dress was hideous. 他直率地告诉她她的裙子难看死了。

例句：The general responded nondiplomatically to the journalist's question, revealing sensitive military information.将军对记者的提问直言不讳，泄露了敏感的军事信息。


近义词：bluntly, frankly, directly, bluntly, forthrightly

反义词：diplomatically, tactfully, subtly, delicately


词性:副词
