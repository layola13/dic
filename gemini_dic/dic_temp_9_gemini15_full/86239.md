heats:/hiːts/| v.|加热，使变热|to make something hot or warmer|The sun heats the earth. 太阳加热地球。|The oven heats up quickly. 烤箱很快就能预热。|cooks, warms|cools

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun heats the earth every day.  = 太阳每天都加热地球。
This heater heats the room efficiently. = 这台加热器有效地加热房间。

一般过去时:
She heated the milk before giving it to the baby. = 她把牛奶加热后再给婴儿喝。
He heated up the leftovers for dinner. = 他把剩菜热了当晚餐。

现在完成时:
The fire has heated the room nicely. = 火已经把房间暖和起来了。
We have heated the water for a bath. = 我们已经把水烧热准备洗澡了。

过去完成时:
They had heated the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把食物加热好了。
She had heated the car seat to warm it up before getting in. = 她上车前已经把车座加热了，以使其暖和起来。

将来时:
I will heat the soup before we eat. = 我们吃饭前我会把汤热一下。
They will heat the pool tomorrow. = 他们明天将把游泳池加热。

虚拟语气:
