hydrologists:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |水文学家|Scientists who study the Earth's water, its movement, distribution, and quality.  They work with rivers, lakes, groundwater, and oceans, often focusing on issues like water resources management, flood control, and pollution. |例句：Hydrologists are crucial in predicting and mitigating the effects of droughts. 水文学家对于预测和减轻干旱的影响至关重要。

例句：The hydrologists collected data on river flow rates to model future water availability. 水文学家收集河流流量数据以模拟未来的水资源可用性。


近义词：water scientists, hydrological scientists|反义词：无|词性:名词
