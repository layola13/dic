eggplant:/ˈɛɡplænt/| n. |茄子|A usually dark-purple, oblong fruit with shiny skin, used as a vegetable.  It is botanically a fruit but culinarily a vegetable.|例句：I love eggplant parmesan. 我喜欢茄子帕尔马干酪。

例句： We used roasted eggplant in the pasta sauce. 我们在意大利面酱里用了烤茄子。
