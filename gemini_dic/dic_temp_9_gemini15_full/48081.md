declaring:/dɪˈklɛərɪŋ/ | v. | 宣布；声明；断言；申报 | To state something clearly, officially, or firmly.  |例句：The government declared a state of emergency. 政府宣布进入紧急状态。

例句：She declared her love for him. 她向他表白了爱意。


近义词： announcing, proclaiming, stating, asserting, affirming | 反义词： concealing, hiding, suppressing, denying
