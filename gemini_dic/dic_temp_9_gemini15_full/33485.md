chymification:/ˌkaɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |化学转化；(食物的) 糜化；|The process of converting food into chyme in the stomach.  This involves the action of gastric juices on food, breaking it down into a semi-fluid mass. |例句：The chymification of food is crucial for proper digestion.  食物的糜化对于正常的消化至关重要。|近义词：digestion,  gastric digestion |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be the lack of digestion.
