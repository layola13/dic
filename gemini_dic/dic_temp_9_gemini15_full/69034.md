fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/| adj. |愚蠢的；傻气的；轻浮的 |Foolish or silly, especially in a smug or self-satisfied way.  It implies a lack of intelligence or good judgment combined with an air of complacency. |例句：His fatuous remarks annoyed everyone. 他愚蠢的言论惹恼了所有人。

例句：She gave a fatuous smile and said nothing. 她傻笑了一下，什么也没说。

近义词：silly, foolish, inane, asinine, vapid

反义词：intelligent, sensible, wise, shrewd
