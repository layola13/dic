multivagous:/mʌlˈtɪvæɡəs/| adj.|杂食的，多种食物的|Feeding on or consuming many different kinds of food.  The term often refers to animals with diverse diets, encompassing both plants and animals. |Example:  The omnivorous bear is a multivagous creature, thriving on berries, fish, and honey.  这只杂食性熊是一种杂食动物，以浆果、鱼和蜂蜜为生。|近义词：omnivorous, polyphagous|反义词：monophagous
