inequities:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtiz/ | n. |不公平；不公正 |Unfairness or injustice.  Inequities often refer to systemic disadvantages or biases that affect particular groups of people.  |例句：The report highlighted the inequities in access to healthcare.  这份报告突显了医疗保健方面的不公平现象。

例句：The vast inequities in wealth distribution are a major social problem. 财富分配方面巨大的不公平是一个主要的社会问题。


近义词：injustice, unfairness, inequality, disparity

反义词：equity, fairness, justice, equality
