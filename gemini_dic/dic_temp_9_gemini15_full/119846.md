mildly:/ˈmaɪldli/ | adv. | 轻微地；温和地；适度地 |  Slightly; gently; moderately.  It describes something that is not extreme or severe. | 例句：He was mildly surprised by the news. 他对这个消息有点惊讶。

例句：The teacher mildly scolded the student for talking in class. 老师温和地责备学生在课堂上讲话。


近义词：slightly, gently, moderately, somewhat | 反义词：severely, intensely, extremely, violently


词性：副词
