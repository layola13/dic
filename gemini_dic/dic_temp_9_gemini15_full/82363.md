grown-upness:/ˈɡroʊn ʌpnəs/ | n. |成年人的行为或举止；成熟；成年 |The state or quality of being grown-up; maturity; adulthood.  It often implies a certain level of responsibility, independence, and social awareness. |例句：He showed surprising grown-upness in handling the crisis. 他在处理危机时表现出令人惊讶的成熟。

例句：Her grown-upness was evident in her thoughtful approach to the problem. 她处理问题的周到方法体现了她的成熟。


近义词：maturity, adulthood, sophistication, responsibility

反义词：childishness, immaturity, naiveté


词源：源自“grown-up”加上后缀“-ness”，表示“状态”或“性质”。“grown-up”本身是形容词和名词，意思是“成年的”。
