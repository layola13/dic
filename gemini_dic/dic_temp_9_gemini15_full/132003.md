noncorporeal:/ˌnɒnkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/ | adj. |非物质的；非肉体的 | Not having a physical body; spiritual; immaterial.  |例句：Many religions believe in a noncorporeal soul that survives the death of the body. 许多宗教相信灵魂是一种非物质的存在，可以在肉体死亡后继续存在。

例句：The ghost, a noncorporeal being, floated through the walls.  那个幽灵，一个非物质的存在，飘过墙壁。


近义词：immaterial, intangible, incorporeal, spiritual


反义词：corporeal, physical, material, tangible
