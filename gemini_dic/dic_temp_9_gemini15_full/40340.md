contract:/ˈkɒntrækt/ /ˈkɑːntrækt/| n. v.|合同；契约；感染；订约；使缩短|

英文解析:A contract is a legally binding agreement between two or more parties.  As a verb, it means to make or enter into a contract, or to become shorter or smaller.

例句：
The company signed a contract with a new supplier.  = 公司与一家新的供应商签署了合同。

We contracted to build the house within six months. = 我们签约在六个月内建好房子。


近义词：agreement, pact, treaty, deal, shrink

反义词：expansion, enlargement


词性:Noun, Verb
