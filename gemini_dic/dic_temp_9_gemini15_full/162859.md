precautious: /prɪˈkɔːʃəs/ | adj. | 谨慎的，小心的 | Extremely cautious; showing great care to avoid potential danger or mistakes.  |例句：He was precautious in his dealings with the unknown company, checking every detail of the contract before signing. 他在与那家不知名的公司打交道时非常谨慎，在签署合同之前仔细检查了每一个细节。

例句：Given the potential risks, a precautious approach is recommended.  考虑到潜在的风险，建议采取谨慎的方法。


近义词：careful, cautious, wary, circumspect | 反义词：reckless, careless, rash, imprudent


词性：形容词
