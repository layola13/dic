interregnal: /ˌɪntərˈreɪɡnəl/ | adj. | 摄政的；空位的；过渡期的 | Relating to the period between two reigns or between the death of a monarch and the accession of their successor.  It often describes a period of instability or uncertainty. |例句：The interregnal period was marked by political intrigue and instability.  摄政时期以政治阴谋和不稳定为特征。

The interregnal government struggled to maintain order.  过渡政府努力维持秩序。


近义词：transitional, interim, transitional, intermediate | 反义词：reigning, ruling, established


词源:  From Latin *interregnum (from inter "between" + regnum "reign").  The word directly evolved from the Latin "interregnum," meaning "between reigns."
