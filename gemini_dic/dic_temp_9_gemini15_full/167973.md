prioritizes:/praɪˈɔːrɪtaɪzɪz/| vt.|优先处理；优先考虑|To give something a higher priority than other things.|The company prioritizes customer satisfaction above all else.  这家公司把客户满意度放在首位。|She prioritizes her family over her career. 她把家庭放在事业之上。|focus on, emphasize, stress |de-prioritize, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prioritizes his work. = 他优先处理他的工作。
She prioritizes her health. = 她优先考虑她的健康。

一般过去时:
They prioritized the project. = 他们优先处理了这个项目。
He prioritized his studies. = 他优先考虑了他的学业。

现在完成时:
She has prioritized her goals. = 她已经优先考虑了她的目标。
We have prioritized customer service. = 我们已经优先考虑了客户服务。

过去完成时:
They had prioritized the emergency. = 他们已经优先处理了紧急情况。
She had prioritized her family's needs. = 她已经优先考虑了家人的需求。

将来时:
He will prioritize his health next year. = 他明年将优先考虑他的健康。
We will prioritize the most important tasks. = 我们将优先处理最重要的事情。

虚拟语气:
If I were the manager, I would prioritize employee well-being. = 如果我是经理，我会优先考虑员工的福祉。
I wish I had prioritized my education earlier. = 我真希望我早点重视我的教育。

被动语态:
The tasks were prioritized by the project manager. = 任务由项目经理进行了优先排序。
Customer satisfaction is prioritized by our company. = 客户满意度是我们公司优先考虑的。

疑问句:
What does he prioritize in his life? = 他生活中优先考虑什么？
What should we prioritize first? = 我们应该首先优先考虑什么？

否定句:
He doesn't prioritize his sleep. = 他不重视睡眠。
They didn't prioritize the meeting. = 他们没有优先考虑会议。
