estrogens:/ˈɛstrədʒənz/| n. |雌激素| Estrogens are a group of steroid hormones that promote the development and maintenance of female characteristics. They play a crucial role in the reproductive system, influencing the menstrual cycle, pregnancy, and other aspects of female physiology.  |  Estrogens are essential for the development of secondary sexual characteristics in females, such as breast growth and the widening of the hips.  |  hormones, steroid hormones | androgens  | 名词
