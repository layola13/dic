empire:/ˈɛmpaɪər/| n.|帝国，帝國；庞大的组织|A large territory ruled by a single person, family, or state.  An empire is characterized by its extensive power and influence, often encompassing diverse cultures and peoples under a single sovereign authority.|例句：The Roman Empire was one of the largest empires in history.  罗马帝国是历史上最大的帝国之一。  The British Empire once controlled a vast territory. 大英帝国曾经控制着广阔的领土。|近义词：kingdom, realm, dominion|反义词：colony, republic
