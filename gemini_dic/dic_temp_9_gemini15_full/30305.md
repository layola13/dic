cenotaphs:/ˈsɛnəˌtɑːfs/| n. |空冢，纪念碑|A cenotaph is a monument erected in memory of a person or group of people whose remains are elsewhere.|The cenotaph in the park commemorates the soldiers who died in the war. 公园里的纪念碑是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。|monument, memorial|tomb, grave|名词
