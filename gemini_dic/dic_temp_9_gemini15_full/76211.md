fusty:/ˈfʌsti/| adj.|发霉的；陈旧的；守旧的；古板的 |Musty, stale, or old-fashioned;  suggestive of decay or disuse.  It can refer to a physical smell or a feeling of being outdated and stagnant. |例句：The attic was fusty and filled with cobwebs.  阁楼里又潮湿又长满了蜘蛛网。

The old library had a fusty smell and dusty books.  古老的图书馆散发着霉味，书上蒙着一层灰尘。


近义词：musty, stale, stuffy, outdated, old-fashioned, antiquated, archaic,  mouldy,  dusty

反义词：fresh, modern, new, up-to-date, contemporary


USAGE->
