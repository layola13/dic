preconceding:/ˌpriːsəˈsiːdɪŋ/| adj.| 先前的，在…之前的 |  Preceding something means coming before it in time or order.  It emphasizes the immediacy of the preceding event or thing. | Example: The preconceding chapter explained the theory.  先前的章节解释了这个理论。 The preconceding events led to the current crisis.  先前的事件导致了目前的危机。|近义词： preceding, foregoing, prior |反义词：following, subsequent
