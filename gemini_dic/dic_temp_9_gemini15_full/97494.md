词源:
The word "ingest" comes from the Latin word "ingestus," the past participle of "ingerere," meaning "to carry in" or "to put in."  The prefix "in-" means "in" or "into," and the root "-gerere" is related to the word "gesture," suggesting a carrying or conveying action.
