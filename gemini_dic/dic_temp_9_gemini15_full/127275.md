navel-gazing: /ˈneɪvəlˌɡeɪzɪŋ/ | n. |沉思冥想；顾影自怜；自我陶醉 |  Excessive contemplation of oneself and one's own thoughts and feelings; self-absorption.  It often implies a lack of concern for external matters or the perspectives of others. | 例句：His navel-gazing prevented him from seeing the obvious solution.  他的沉思冥想使他看不到显而易见的解决方案。


His constant navel-gazing made him unpopular with his colleagues. 他总是沉溺于自我反省，这使他在同事中不受欢迎。


近义词： introspection, self-absorption, self-reflection, self-preoccupation, contemplation


反义词：  extroversion, altruism, objectivity, outward-looking


词源：The term "navel-gazing" is a metaphorical expression.  "Navel" refers to the belly button, and the image of someone gazing at their own navel suggests intense self-focus and a lack of attention to the world around them.  The term's origin is unclear, but it gained popularity in the late 20th century.
