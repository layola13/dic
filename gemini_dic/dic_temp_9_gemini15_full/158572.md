pleurisy:/ˈplʊərəsi/| n. |胸膜炎|Inflammation of the pleura, the membrane surrounding the lungs, causing sharp chest pain, especially during breathing.  The inflammation may be caused by infection, autoimmune disease, or injury.|例句：He was diagnosed with pleurisy and prescribed antibiotics. 他被诊断出患有胸膜炎，并被开了抗生素。|例句：The sharp pain in her chest turned out to be a case of pleurisy. 她胸部剧烈的疼痛结果是胸膜炎。|近义词：pleuritis|反义词：无
