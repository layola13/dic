outspeeding: /ˌaʊtˈspiːdɪŋ/ | v. | 超速行驶；超过速度 | The act of exceeding the speed of something or someone.  |例句：The new car's outspeeded all its competitors. 这辆新车的速度超过了所有竞争对手。


The cheetah's outspeeded the gazelle.猎豹的速度超过了瞪羚。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outpacing, outstripping | 反义词：underspeeding, lagging behind | 词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new engine outspeeds the old one. = 新引擎的速度超过旧引擎。
He outspeeds other runners in the race. = 他在比赛中超过其他跑步者。


一般过去时:
The race car outspeeded all the other cars. = 赛车超过了所有其他汽车。
She outspeeded her opponent in the final lap. = 她在最后一圈超过了她的对手。


现在完成时:
The company has outspeeded its rivals in the market. = 这家公司在市场上已经超过了它的竞争对手。
He has outspeeded his personal best time. = 他已经超过了他的个人最佳成绩。


过去完成时:
By the time I reached the finish line, she had outspeeded me. = 当我到达终点线时，她已经超过我了。
The train had outspeeded the bus before reaching the city. = 火车到达城市前已经超过了公共汽车。


将来时:
The rocket will outspeed all previous models. = 火箭将超过所有以前的型号。
They expect to outspeed the competition next year. = 他们预计明年将超过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If the car outspeeded the train, we would have arrived late. = 如果汽车超过火车，我们就会迟到。
I wish I had outspeeded that other car! = 我真希望我当时超过了那辆车！


被动语态:
The old model was outspeeded by the new one. = 旧型号被新型号超过了。
The runner was outspeeded in the last 100 meters. = 跑步者在最后100米被超过了。


疑问句:
Did the blue car outspeed the red car? = 蓝色汽车是否超过了红色汽车？
Will the new technology outspeed the current method? = 新技术是否会超过现有方法？


否定句:
The old bicycle didn't outspeed the new one. = 旧自行车没有超过新的。
He didn't outspeed his opponent in the race. = 他在比赛中没有超过他的对手。


PHRASE->
outspeed the competition = 超越竞争对手
outspeed the market = 领先市场
outspeed the others = 超越其他人
outspeed the record = 刷新记录
outspeed the limits = 超过限制


词源:
The word "outspeeding" is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") and the verb "speeding" (the act of moving or traveling at a high velocity).  It's a relatively modern formation, reflecting the increasing importance of speed in modern technology and transportation.
