sensualization: /ˌsɛnʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |感官化；感性化 | The process of making something sensual or appealing to the senses; the act of emphasizing or heightening the sensual aspects of something.  |例句：The sensualization of advertising often leads to unrealistic expectations. 广告的感性化往往导致不切实际的期望。

The sensualization of modern art is a topic of much debate. 现代艺术的感性化是一个备受争议的话题。


近义词：sensualization,  eroticization, aestheticization


反义词：desensitization, de-eroticization


词源:  From "sensual" + "-ization".  "Sensual" derives from Latin "sensualis," from "sensus" (sense, feeling). "-ization" is a suffix indicating the process of making something.
