dominie:/ˈdɒməni/| n.|乡村牧师；教师|A dominie is a schoolmaster, especially in Scotland, or a clergyman, especially in Scotland and the Netherlands.  The term carries a somewhat old-fashioned and often affectionate connotation. |例句：The dominie was respected by all the villagers for his wisdom and kindness. 乡村牧师因其智慧和善良而受到所有村民的尊敬。The old dominie taught generations of children in the small schoolhouse. 老教师在小校舍里教了几代孩子。|近义词：schoolmaster, teacher, clergyman, pastor|反义词：student, pupil
