carryable: /ˈkæriəbl/ | adj. | 可携带的，可搬运的 |  Something that is carryable is light enough or small enough to be carried.  |例句：The suitcase was surprisingly carryable, despite its size.  这个手提箱虽然很大，但出乎意料地轻便易携带。

例句：This lightweight laptop is easily carryable. 这台轻便的笔记本电脑很容易携带。


近义词：portable, transportable, manageable


反义词：unwieldy, cumbersome, heavy


词源:  Derived from the verb "carry" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or possibility).  The suffix "-able" is of Old French origin, ultimately from Latin "-abilis".
