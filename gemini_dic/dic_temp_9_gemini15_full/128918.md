nincompoop:/ˈnɪŋkəmˌpuːp/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋|A nincompoop is a foolish or incompetent person.  It is an informal and somewhat humorous term.|He's such a nincompoop; he spilled coffee all over his new shirt.|近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, simpleton|反义词：genius, mastermind, intellect
