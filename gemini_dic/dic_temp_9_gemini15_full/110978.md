liquidations:/ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃənz/| n.| 清算；变卖；结束 | The process of winding up the affairs of a business, usually by selling its assets to pay off its debts.  It can also refer to the act of converting something into cash. |例句：The company went into liquidation after years of losses.  这家公司在多年亏损后进行清算。

例句：The forced liquidation of assets resulted in significant financial losses.  资产的强制性清算导致了巨大的经济损失。


近义词：winding-up, dissolution, settlement, disposal

反义词：establishment, foundation, creation


词性: 名词
