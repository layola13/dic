intolerance:/ɪnˈtɑːlərəns/| n.|不容忍；不耐受；偏执|The inability or unwillingness to accept different views or beliefs.|例句：His intolerance of other cultures led to many conflicts. 他的不容忍其他文化导致了许多冲突。|例句：Her intolerance to lactose caused digestive problems. 她对乳糖的不耐受导致了消化问题。|近义词：bigotry, prejudice, bias |反义词：tolerance, acceptance
