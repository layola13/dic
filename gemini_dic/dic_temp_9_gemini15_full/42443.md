counterdemonstrator:/ˌkaʊntəˈdɛmənstreɪtər/| n. |反示威者，对立示威者|A person who participates in a demonstration against another demonstration.  They often hold opposing views and aim to disrupt or counter the message of the original protest.|Example: The police struggled to control the clash between demonstrators and counterdemonstrators.  警察难以控制示威者和反示威者之间的冲突。|近义词：protestor (in opposition), opponent, rival demonstrator|反义词：demonstrator|名词
