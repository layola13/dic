lay oneself out:/leɪ wʌnˈsɛlf aʊt/| phrasal verb|全力以赴；竭尽全力；炫耀；卖弄；铺开；摊开|To make a great effort; to expend one's energy and resources on something; to display oneself ostentatiously; to spread or arrange something flat.|例句：She laid herself out to impress the interviewers. 她全力以赴以给面试官留下深刻印象。


例句：He laid himself out on the beach to get a tan. 他躺在沙滩上晒太阳。


近义词：endeavor, strive, exert oneself, show off, flaunt|反义词：slack off,  shirk,  neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She lays herself out to help others. = 她全力以赴帮助他人。
He lays himself out in his work. = 他在工作中全力以赴。

一般过去时:
She laid herself out to win the competition. = 她全力以赴赢得比赛。
He laid himself out on the sofa to read. = 他躺在沙发上看书。

现在完成时:
She has laid herself out to achieve her goals. = 她一直全力以赴实现她的目标。
He has laid himself out for success. = 他一直为成功而努力。

过去完成时:
She had laid herself out to prepare for the exam. = 她为考试已经做好了充分准备。
He had laid himself out to impress his new boss. = 他已经全力以赴去给他的新老板留下好印象。

将来时:
She will lay herself out to support her family. = 她将全力以赴支持她的家庭。
He will lay himself out to complete the project. = 他将全力以赴完成这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If she laid herself out, she would succeed. = 如果她全力以赴，她会成功的。
If he had laid himself out earlier, he would have finished the work. = 如果他早点全力以赴，他就能完成工作了。

被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Will she lay herself out for this job? = 她会为这份工作全力以赴吗？
Does he usually lay himself out when he works? = 他工作时通常会全力以赴吗？

否定句:
She didn't lay herself out for the presentation. = 她没有为演示全力以赴。
He didn't lay himself out to help. = 他并没有全力以赴去帮忙。


PHRASE->
