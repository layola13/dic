herbalists: /ˈɜːrbəlɪsts/ | n. | 草药学家，药草师 | A person who practices herbalism, the study and use of herbs for medicinal purposes.  Herbalists use plants to treat illnesses and promote health. |  Many herbalists believe in the power of natural remedies.  许多草药学家相信天然疗法的功效。

Many herbalists are also trained in other holistic healing practices. 许多草药学家也接受过其他整体疗法方面的培训。|  近义词：herbalist, herborist, phytotherapist | 反义词：allopathic physician (practitioner of conventional medicine)
