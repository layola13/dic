eurocentrism:/ˌjʊərəʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/ | n. |欧洲中心主义|The belief that European culture and values are superior to all others and should be the standard by which other cultures are judged.  It often involves a biased or distorted view of history and world events, emphasizing European achievements while neglecting or minimizing the contributions of other civilizations.  | Example: The textbook's Eurocentric perspective presented a biased account of world history, focusing solely on European developments.  欧洲中心主义的视角使得教科书对世界历史的描述带有偏见，只关注欧洲的发展。|近义词：ethnocentrism, Western-centrism |反义词：multiculturalism, global perspective
