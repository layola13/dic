hosteler:/ˈhɒstələ(r)/| n. |旅馆老板；旅店主人；寄宿生 |A hosteler is the keeper of a hostel or inn, or a person lodging in a hostel.  |例句：The hosteler greeted us warmly upon our arrival at the inn. 旅店老板在我们到达旅店时热情地迎接了我们。|例句：He was a hosteler at the university, living in a dorm. 他是大学的寄宿生，住在宿舍里。|近义词：innkeeper, landlord, lodger |反义词：tenant (in the sense of someone renting a property, not lodging temporarily)
