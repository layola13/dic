de-iced:/ˌdiːˈaɪst/| v.|除冰的；去冰的|To remove ice from (a surface, especially an aircraft or road).|例句：The airport workers de-iced the planes before takeoff. 机场工作人员在飞机起飞前为飞机除冰。例句：The roads were de-iced after the heavy snowfall. 大雪过后，道路上的冰被清除掉了。|近义词：defrost, thaw, ice-free|反义词：ice, freeze

词源:
The word "de-iced" is formed by adding the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to reverse") to the word "iced," which itself is derived from the noun "ice."  Therefore, "de-iced" literally means "removed ice from."
