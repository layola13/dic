geographically:/ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 地理上地；从地理学角度 | Relating to geography or the geographical distribution of something.  |例句：The two countries are geographically close but culturally distant. 这两个国家在地理位置上很近，但在文化上却相距甚远。

例句：The species is geographically isolated in a remote mountain range.  该物种在地理上与世隔绝于一个偏远的山脉。


近义词：spatially, regionally, topographically | 反义词： abstractly, conceptually


词性：副词
