Hilton:/ˈhɪltən/| n. |希尔顿（酒店）|A Hilton is a hotel belonging to the Hilton Worldwide hotel chain.  The word often refers to a specific hotel within the chain, or to the chain itself. |I'm staying at the Hilton in New York. 我住在纽约希尔顿酒店。| Marriott, Hyatt, Sheraton|  (No direct antonym, but could be considered opposite to smaller, independent hotels)|名词
