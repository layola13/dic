marabou:/ˈmærəbuː/| n.|秃鹳；(装饰用)长羽毛|A large, primarily African bird with a long neck, long legs, and a distinctive bald head.  The feathers are often used as decoration.|例句：The marabou stork is a scavenger.  秃鹳是一种食腐动物。

例句：She adorned her hat with marabou feathers. 她用秃鹳羽毛装饰她的帽子。


近义词：stork (广义上的鹳)


反义词：无


词性：名词
