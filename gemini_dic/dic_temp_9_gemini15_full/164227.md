predominates:/prɪˈdɒmɪneɪts/|/ˈprɛdəmɪˌneɪts/| v. | 占主导地位；支配；突出 | To be the most noticeable or important feature of something.  It implies a significant superiority or influence over other elements. | Example:  Red predominates in this painting. 这幅画以红色为主。

Red predominates in her wardrobe.  她的衣橱里红色衣服占主导地位。


近义词: dominates, prevails, outweighs, surpasses


反义词:  is subordinate to, is secondary to


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This color predominates in the design. 这个颜色在设计中占主导地位。
His opinion predominates in the discussion.  他的意见在讨论中占主导地位。


一般过去时:
Blue predominated in the artist's early works. 蓝色在艺术家早期的作品中占主导地位。
Fear predominated his feelings that day. 那天恐惧占据了他的情感。


现在完成时:
This style has predominated in architecture for decades.  这种风格在建筑中已占主导地位几十年了。
Concern for the environment has predominated in recent political debates.  对环境的关注在最近的政治辩论中占据主导地位。


过去完成时:
Classical music had predominated in the concert hall before the introduction of jazz.  在爵士乐引入之前，古典音乐在音乐厅占据主导地位。
Before the storm, calm had predominated.暴风雨来临之前，平静占主导地位。


将来时:
Digital technology will predominate in the future. 未来数字技术将占据主导地位。
We expect that sustainable energy will predominate in the coming years. 我们预计可持续能源将在未来几年占据主导地位。


虚拟语气:
If only reason predominated over emotion.  如果理性能够战胜情感就好了。
I wish that logic predominated in this argument.我希望逻辑在这个争论中占主导地位。


被动语态:
The color scheme was predominated by blues and greens. 色彩方案以蓝色和绿色为主。
The meeting was predominated by the CEO's opinions. 会议由首席执行官的意见所主导。


疑问句:
Does a particular style predominate in modern art?  现代艺术中是否存在某种主导风格？
Which element predominates in this composition?  在这首乐曲中哪个元素占主导地位？


否定句:
No single element predominates in this painting. 这幅画中没有任何单一元素占主导地位。
His kindness did not predominate over his ambition.  他的善良并没有压倒他的野心。


PHRASE->
predominate in: 在...中占主导地位
predominate over:  支配；胜过
predominantly: 主要地；显著地
predominating factor: 主要因素
predominating influence: 主要影响
