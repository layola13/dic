refocuses:/ˌriːˈfoʊkəsɪz/ | v. |重新聚焦；重新集中注意力 | To direct one's attention or efforts to something again.  |例句：He refocuses his efforts on completing the project. 他重新集中精力完成项目。

例句：The company refocuses its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司重新调整其营销策略以针对更年轻的人群。


近义词：reorient, redirect, concentrate, center


反义词：distract, diffuse, scatter


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refocuses his energy on his studies every semester. 他每个学期都会重新集中精力学习。
She refocuses her attention on the task at hand. 她把注意力重新集中到手头的任务上。


一般过去时:
The team refocused its efforts after the setback. 团队在挫折后重新集中了精力。
He refocused his camera on the distant mountain. 他把相机重新对准远处的山。


现在完成时:
She has refocused her career on environmental issues. 她已经将她的职业生涯重新集中在环境问题上。
They have refocused their marketing campaign after poor initial results. 他们初步结果不佳后，已经重新调整了营销活动。


过去完成时:
After the initial chaos, they had refocused their attention on rescue efforts. 在最初的混乱之后，他们已经将注意力重新集中到救援工作上。
She had refocused her priorities after realizing her mistakes. 在意识到自己的错误后，她已经重新调整了优先级。


将来时:
He will refocus his attention on his health after the project is completed. 项目完成后，他会将注意力重新集中到健康上。
The company will refocus its resources on product development next year. 公司明年将把资源重新集中在产品开发上。


虚拟语气:
If he refocused his efforts, he would succeed. 如果他重新集中精力，他就会成功。
I wish she had refocused her attention earlier. 我希望她早点把注意力重新集中起来。


被动语态:
The debate was refocused on the key issue. 辩论的焦点重新回到了关键问题上。
The company's strategy will be refocused by the new CEO. 公司的战略将由新任首席执行官重新调整。


疑问句:

否定句:
