fiddling:/ˈfɪdliŋ/| v. adj. n.| 拨弄；玩弄；瞎弄；无关紧要的事；|To touch or move something with your fingers in a nervous or restless way, or to play a musical instrument without skill or precision.|He was fiddling with his keys. 他在拨弄他的钥匙。|近义词：playing, trifling, messing|反义词：serious, important


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fiddles with his pen during meetings. = 他在会议期间拨弄他的钢笔。
She fiddles with her hair when she's nervous. = 她紧张的时候会拨弄她的头发。

一般过去时:
He fiddled with the radio until he broke it. = 他摆弄收音机直到把它弄坏。
She fiddled with the buttons on her coat. = 她摆弄她外套上的纽扣。

现在完成时:
I have been fiddling with this computer all day. = 我整天都在摆弄这台电脑。
She has fiddled away her entire afternoon. = 她整个下午都在虚度光阴。


过去完成时:
He had fiddled with the lock for hours before he finally opened it. = 他在最终打开锁之前已经摆弄了几个小时。
She had fiddled away most of her savings. = 她已经挥霍掉了大部分积蓄。

将来时:
I will be fiddling with this project for weeks. = 我将要在这个项目上花费几周的时间。
He will fiddle with the settings until he gets it right. = 他会调整设置直到弄对为止。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fiddle with this project. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会去研究这个项目。
I wish I hadn't fiddled with the settings. = 我真希望我没有去调整设置。

被动语态:
The machine was fiddled with, and now it's broken. = 机器被人摆弄过，现在坏了。
The settings were fiddled with, resulting in errors. = 设置被人改动过，导致了错误。

疑问句:
Were you fiddling with my phone? = 你刚才在玩弄我的手机吗？
What were you fiddling with? = 你在摆弄什么？

否定句:
He didn't fiddle with the evidence. = 他没有摆弄证据。
She didn't fiddle with the controls. = 她没有乱动控制装置。
