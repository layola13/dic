faker:/ˈfeɪkər/| n.|骗子；伪造者；假货|A faker is someone who pretends to be someone or something they are not, or who creates something false.  They often deceive others for personal gain or amusement.|例句：He's a faker; he doesn't really know anything about art. 他是个骗子；他对艺术一窍不通。|例句：That painting is a faker; it's not a genuine Monet. 那幅画是赝品；它不是莫奈的真迹。|近义词：impostor, fraud, charlatan, sham|反义词：genuine, authentic, real
