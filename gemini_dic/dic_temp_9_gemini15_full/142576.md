osteoclast:/ˈɒstiəʊklæst/| n. |破骨细胞|A large multinucleated cell responsible for the resorption of bone tissue.  Osteoclasts are essential for bone remodeling and repair. They secrete acids and enzymes that dissolve the mineral and protein components of bone. |Example:  Osteoclasts play a crucial role in maintaining calcium homeostasis in the body. 破骨细胞在维持体内钙平衡中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：bone-resorbing cell|反义词：osteoblast
