gallivanter:/ˈɡælɪvæntər/| n. v.|游手好闲的人；四处游荡的人|A gallivanter is a person who travels around a lot for pleasure, often without a clear purpose.  It implies a somewhat frivolous or irresponsible approach to travel.|例句：He's a confirmed gallivanter, always flitting from one exciting location to another. 他是个惯于四处游荡的人，总是从一个令人兴奋的地方飞奔到另一个地方。|近义词：roamer, wanderer, vagrant, rambler|反义词：homebody, stay-at-home


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gallivants around Europe every summer. = 他每年夏天都在欧洲四处闲逛。
They gallivant from city to city, never settling down. = 他们从一个城市到另一个城市游荡，从未安定下来。

一般过去时:
She gallivanted across the country after graduation. = 她毕业后在全国各地游荡。
We gallivanted through the streets of Paris, enjoying the sights. = 我们在巴黎街头闲逛，欣赏着景色。

现在完成时:
He has gallivanted all over the world. = 他周游世界各地。
They have gallivanted extensively throughout Southeast Asia. = 他们广泛地在东南亚各地游荡。

过去完成时:
She had gallivanted through several countries before she settled down. = 在她定居之前，她已经在几个国家游荡过。
They had gallivanted for years before they decided to buy a house. = 他们在决定买房之前已经游荡了很多年。

将来时:
We will gallivant across the Mediterranean next year. = 我们明年将在地中海沿岸四处游荡。
They will gallivant through South America for six months. = 他们将在南美洲游荡六个月。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would gallivant around the world. = 如果我有时间，我会周游世界。
I wish I could gallivant to exotic locations more often. = 我希望我能够更经常地去异国他乡游荡。


被动语态:
(Gallivant is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Does he often gallivant around the country? = 他经常在全国各地游荡吗？
Where did they gallivant last summer? = 他们去年夏天去哪里游荡了？


否定句:
He doesn't gallivant as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样经常游荡了。
They didn't gallivant this year due to work commitments. = 由于工作安排，他们今年没有游荡。
