heifers:/ˈhɛfərz/| n.|小母牛，未生育的母牛|A heifer is a young cow that has not yet had a calf.  Heifers are typically raised for breeding or for meat production.|例句：The farmer carefully selected the best heifers for breeding.  这位农民仔细挑选了最好的小母牛进行繁殖。

例句： Several heifers grazed peacefully in the pasture. 几头小母牛在牧场上平静地吃草。


近义词：young cow, calf (although a calf is younger)|反义词：cow (a mature female cow), bull (a mature male cow)


词性:名词
