camouflage:/ˈkæməflɑːʒ/|/ˈkæməflɑːʒ/| n. v. |伪装；掩饰；保护色|The act of concealing the presence of something by disguising its appearance, or the state of being so concealed.  It can refer to natural adaptations in animals or plants, or to deliberate actions taken by humans or animals to hide or blend in.|The chameleon's camouflage is remarkably effective. 变色龙的伪装非常有效。


The army used camouflage netting to hide their tanks.  军队使用伪装网来隐藏他们的坦克。|disguise, concealment, cover|exposure, revelation|名词，动词
