caulk:/kɔːk/| v.|填塞，堵塞|To fill or close (a gap or crack) with a sealant, such as caulking compound.|例句：He caulked the gaps around the window frame to prevent drafts. 他用填缝料堵塞了窗框周围的缝隙以防止漏风。例句：The plumber caulked the pipe joint to prevent leaks. 水管工用填缝料堵塞了管道接头以防止漏水。|近义词：seal, plug, stop up|反义词：open, expose


USAGE->

He caulks the cracks in the boat. = 他用填缝料堵塞船上的裂缝。
She caulks the bathtub regularly. = 她定期用填缝料堵塞浴缸。
