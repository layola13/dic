coexisted:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstɪd/| v. |共存；同时存在 | To exist at the same time or in the same place.  |例句：Dinosaurs and early mammals coexisted for millions of years. 恐龙和早期的哺乳动物共存了数百万年。

例句：Different cultures coexisted peacefully in the city. 不同的文化在这个城市和平共处。

近义词：cohabit, coexist, concur, exist together

反义词：be mutually exclusive, be incompatible

词性:动词

词源:
The word "coexisted" is formed from the prefix "co-" (meaning together or jointly) and the verb "existed" (from the Latin "exsistere," meaning "to stand out," "to exist"). Therefore, "coexisted" literally means "existed together."
