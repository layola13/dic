plumfield:/ˈplʌmfiːld/| n. |李子地，李子园|A field where plum trees are grown.  It can also refer to a place name.|例句：The plumfield was abundant with ripe plums this year.  今年李子园里李子丰收。|例句：We visited the charming village of Plumfield during our holiday. 我们在假期期间参观了迷人的普拉姆菲尔德村庄。|近义词： orchard (if referring to a cultivated area), plum orchard|反义词：无|名词
