secateurs:/ˈsɛkəˌtɜrz/| n. |修枝剪，枝剪|Hand-held shears used for pruning plants.  They typically have two blades that work together to cut branches and stems. |例句：I used my secateurs to trim the rose bushes. 我用修枝剪修剪玫瑰花丛。|例句：The gardener carefully pruned the hedges with sharp secateurs. 园丁用锋利的修枝剪仔细地修剪树篱。|近义词：shears, pruners, clippers|反义词：None
