disinterred:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɛrd/| vt.|掘出（埋葬的东西）；发掘；使重见天日|To dig up (something that has been buried), especially a corpse.  The act of disinterring often implies the unearthing of something that has been buried for a significant period.|例句：The archaeologists disinterred ancient artifacts from the tomb. 考古学家从坟墓中挖掘出古代文物。

例句：The body was disinterred for further examination.  尸体被掘出以便进一步检查。


近义词：unearth, exhume, recover

反义词：bury, inter, entomb

词性:动词
