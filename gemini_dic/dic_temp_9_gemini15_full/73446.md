forename:/ˈfɔːrneɪm/ /ˈfɔːrˌneɪm/ | n. |名字，教名|A person's given name, as opposed to their surname or family name.  It is the name given to a person at birth or christening. | Example: My forename is John, and my surname is Smith.我的名字是约翰，我的姓是史密斯。|近义词：given name, first name, Christian name |反义词：surname, family name, last name
