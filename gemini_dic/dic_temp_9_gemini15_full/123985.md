mormonite:/ˈmɔːrmənˌaɪt/|/ˈmɔːrmɒnˌaɪt/| n. |摩门教徒；摩门教信徒|A member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.  The term is sometimes considered offensive. |例句：He is a devout Mormonite and actively participates in church activities. 他是一位虔诚的摩门教徒，积极参与教会活动。|例句：Many Mormonites dedicate their lives to missionary work. 许多摩门教徒将一生献给传教工作。|近义词：Mormon, Latter-day Saint|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying opposition to Mormonism could be considered antonyms.
