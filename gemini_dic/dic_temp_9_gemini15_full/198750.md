sickbay:/ˈsɪkbeɪ/| n. |医务室，船上医院|A sickbay is a room or area on a ship or other vessel where sick or injured people are treated.  It's a smaller, simpler medical facility than a hospital, typically found on ships, submarines, or in military settings.|The sailor was taken to the sickbay after falling from the mast.|infirmary, dispensary|healthy ward,  hospital (in the sense of a more extensive facility)|
