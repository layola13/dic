democra: /dɪˈmɒkrəsɪ/ | n. | 民主；民主政体 |  The system of government by the whole population or all the eligible members of a state, typically through elected representatives.  It's a system characterized by free and fair elections, protection of civil liberties, and the rule of law. | Example:  Ancient Greece is considered the birthplace of democracy. 古希腊被认为是民主的诞生地。  The country transitioned to a democracy after decades of authoritarian rule.  这个国家在几十年的专制统治后过渡到民主制度。 | 近义词：self-government, popular sovereignty | 反义词：autocracy, dictatorship, totalitarianism | 名词
