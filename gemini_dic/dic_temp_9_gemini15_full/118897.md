metathetical:/ˌmɛtəˈθɛtɪkəl/ | adj. | 换位的；易位转化的 | Relating to or characterized by metathesis.  It describes a process where sounds or letters within a word change their positions.  This can occur in linguistics (sound change) or in chemistry (atomic rearrangement). | Example: The word "flit" is a metathetical variant of "lift".  The change of the position of the consonant sounds is a metathetical process. | 近义词：transposed, rearranged, inverted | 反义词：unchanged, unaltered
