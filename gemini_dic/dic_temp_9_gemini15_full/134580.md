nonloser: /ˈnɑːnˌluːzər/ | adj. n. | 非失败者，成功人士 |  Describes someone who is successful, avoids failure, and achieves their goals.  It is often used informally and can imply a sense of self-confidence and competence.  The term is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "loser," implying the opposite of a loser. |  He's a total nonloser; he got into Harvard and landed a great job. 他是个十足的成功人士；他考上了哈佛大学，还找到了一份好工作。|近义词：winner, achiever, high-achiever, successful person |反义词：loser, failure
