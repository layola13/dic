perithecium: /pɛrɪˈθiːʃəm/| n. |子囊壳| A perithecium is a type of ascocarp, the fruiting body of many Ascomycota fungi.  It is a flask-shaped structure with a pore or ostiole at the apex, through which ascospores are released. The ascospores are contained within asci (sac-like structures) inside the perithecium.  The perithecium is typically embedded in a stroma (a mass of fungal hyphae).|The perithecium of *Neurospora crassa* is a classic example studied in fungal genetics.|近义词：ascocarp|反义词：无
