siliculose:/ˌsɪlɪˈkjuːləʊs/| adj.|含硅的；硅质的|Containing or resembling silica; siliceous.  The term refers to a substance that contains or is composed of silica (silicon dioxide), often exhibiting characteristics associated with silica, such as hardness, brittleness, or a glassy appearance.|The geologist described the rock formation as siliculose, due to its high silica content.|近义词：siliceous, silicious|反义词：non-siliceous
