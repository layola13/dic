cancel:/ˈkænsəl/ | vt. |取消，作废 | To end or stop something that has been planned or arranged. |例句：The meeting has been cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances.  会议由于不可预见的情况而被取消。|例句：I had to cancel my flight because of the storm. 我不得不因为暴风雨取消我的航班。|近义词：abolish, annul, void |反义词：confirm, approve, ratify |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cancel the order every week. = 他们每周都取消订单。
We cancel the trip if it rains. = 如果下雨我们就取消旅行。

一般过去时:
He cancelled his subscription last month. = 他上个月取消了他的订阅。
She cancelled the appointment because she was sick. = 她因为生病取消了预约。

现在完成时:
They have cancelled the concert. = 他们已经取消了音乐会。
I have cancelled my plans for the weekend. = 我已经取消了我的周末计划。

过去完成时:
He had cancelled the reservation before they arrived. = 他们在到达之前他已经取消了预订。
She had cancelled the meeting because she was too busy. = 她因为太忙而取消了会议。

将来时:
They will cancel the game if it snows. = 如果下雪他们将会取消比赛。
We will cancel the project if we don't get enough funding. = 如果我们得不到足够的资金，我们将取消项目。

虚拟语气:
If I had the power, I would cancel the war. = 如果我有权力，我会取消战争。
I wish I could cancel my appointment. = 我希望我能取消我的预约。

被动语态:
The meeting was cancelled due to bad weather. = 会议由于恶劣天气被取消了。
The flight was cancelled because of mechanical problems. = 航班由于机械故障被取消了。

疑问句:
Did they cancel the meeting? = 他们取消会议了吗？
Will you cancel your order? = 你会取消你的订单吗？

否定句:
They did not cancel the project. = 他们没有取消项目。
I will not cancel my trip. = 我不会取消我的旅行。
