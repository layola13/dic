griffon:/ˈɡrɪfən/| n. |狮鹫，格里芬|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.  Griffons are often depicted as guardians of treasure or sacred places.|例句：The ancient tapestry depicted a magnificent griffon guarding a hoard of gold.  古老的挂毯描绘了一只守护着金矿的雄伟狮鹫。|例句：Legends tell of griffons soaring through the mountains, their sharp eyes ever vigilant. 传说中狮鹫翱翔于山间，锐利的双眼始终警惕。|近义词：gryphon|反义词：None
