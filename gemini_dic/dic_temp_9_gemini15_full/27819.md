cannulas: /ˈkænjuːləz/ | n. | 导管 (复数) | Thin tubes inserted into the body for the purpose of injecting or withdrawing fluids.  | Example: The doctor inserted cannulas into the patient's veins to administer fluids. 医生将导管插入病人的静脉中以输送液体。  The nurse carefully removed the cannulas after the transfusion was complete.  输血完成后，护士小心地拔出了导管。 | 近义词: tubes, catheters | 反义词: N/A
