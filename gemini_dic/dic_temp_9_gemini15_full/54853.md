display: /dɪˈspleɪ/ | n., vt. | 显示，展示，陈列 | To show or reveal something; to exhibit something prominently. |  例句：The museum displays a vast collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了大量古代文物。 |  例句：She proudly displayed her award to her family. 她自豪地向家人展示了她的奖项。| 近义词：show, exhibit, present, demonstrate | 反义词：conceal, hide, obscure | 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop displays new products every week. = 这家商店每周都展示新产品。
They display their artwork in the gallery. = 他们在画廊展出他们的艺术作品。

一般过去时:
The artist displayed his paintings at the exhibition. = 艺术家在展览会上展出了他的画作。
He displayed great courage in the face of danger. = 面对危险，他表现出极大的勇气。

现在完成时:
The company has displayed a strong commitment to sustainability. = 这家公司已经展现出对可持续发展的坚定承诺。
She has displayed remarkable talent in her field. = 她在她所在的领域展现出非凡的天赋。

过去完成时:
They had displayed their support for the cause before the protest began. = 在抗议开始之前，他们已经表达了对这一事业的支持。
The museum had displayed the painting for many years before its restoration. = 在修复之前，博物馆已经展出了这幅画多年。

将来时:
The museum will display a new collection of sculptures next month. = 博物馆下个月将展出一批新的雕塑作品。
He will display his skills during the competition. = 他将在比赛中展示他的技能。

虚拟语气:
If they displayed more empathy, they would be better leaders. = 如果他们表现出更多同情心，他们将成为更好的领导者。
I wish he displayed more confidence in his abilities. = 我希望他对自己的能力更有信心。

被动语态:
The products are displayed attractively in the store. = 产品在商店里被吸引人地展示。
The artwork was displayed prominently in the gallery. = 艺术品在画廊里被突出地展示。

疑问句:
Does the store display organic products? = 这家商店是否展示有机产品？
How will the information be displayed on the screen? = 信息将在屏幕上如何显示？

否定句:
The company did not display any interest in our proposal. = 公司对我们的提案没有表现出任何兴趣。
He didn't display the necessary skills for the job. = 他没有展现出这份工作所需的技能。
