mitigative:/ˈmɪtɪɡətɪv/| adj.| 减轻的；缓和的；缓解的 |  Something that is mitigative serves to lessen the severity or impact of something negative.  It describes actions, measures, or qualities that reduce the harmful effects of something. |例句：The government implemented mitigative measures to reduce the impact of the flood.  政府实施了减灾措施以减少洪水的影响。

The company's mitigative strategy aimed to lessen the environmental damage. 公司的减缓战略旨在减少环境破坏。|近义词：alleviating, ameliorative, palliative, softening |反义词：aggravating, exacerbating, intensifying
