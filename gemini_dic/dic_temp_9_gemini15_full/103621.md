jointuress:/ˈdʒɔɪntʃərɛs/ | n. | 享有嫁妆的已婚女子 | A woman entitled to a jointure.  A jointure is a provision in a marriage settlement,  guaranteeing a widow a certain income from her husband's estate after his death.  |Example:  She became a jointuress upon her husband's passing, securing a comfortable life for herself.  她丈夫去世后，她成为享有嫁妆的已婚女子，为自己保障了舒适的生活。|近义词：widow (with secured income) |反义词：无  | 名词
