preascetic:/ˌpriːæˈsɛtɪk/| adj.|禁欲主义之前的，前禁欲主义的 |Preascetic refers to a period or style that existed before the rise of asceticism, often characterized by a less strict and more moderate approach to self-discipline and worldly pleasures.  It implies a transition or evolution towards a more austere lifestyle. |The preascetic era saw a more relaxed attitude towards material possessions.  |近义词：proto-ascetic, pre-austere|反义词：ascetic, austere
