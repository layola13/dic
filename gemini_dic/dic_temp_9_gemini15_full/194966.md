semi-pro:/ˈsɛmiˌproʊ/| adj.|半职业的；准职业的|Partially professional; not fully professional, but more than amateur.  It describes someone or something that is between amateur and professional in status or quality.|例句：He's a semi-pro basketball player, playing for a team that competes regionally but doesn't offer full salaries.|近义词：part-time professional, near-professional|反义词：amateur, professional
