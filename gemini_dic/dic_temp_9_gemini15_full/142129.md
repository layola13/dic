originalist:/əˈrɪdʒənəlɪst/| n. | 原意论者，文本主义者 | A person who adheres to originalism in legal interpretation, believing that the Constitution should be interpreted according to the original intentions of its framers.  |  Many originalists believe that the Constitution should be interpreted based on the understanding of its authors at the time of its ratification. | textualist, constructionist | revisionist, progressive | 名词
