lithography:/lɪˈθɑːɡrəfi/| n.|平版印刷术；石版印刷术|The process of printing from a flat surface, traditionally a stone, on which the image areas are ink-receptive and the non-image areas are ink-repellent.|例句：Lithography was a revolutionary printing technique in the 19th century.  平版印刷术在19世纪是一项革命性的印刷技术。|例句：The museum showcased a collection of early lithographic prints.  博物馆展出了一批早期的平版印刷品。|近义词：planography, offset printing|反义词：letterpress (凸版印刷)|名词
