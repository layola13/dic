dilled:/dɪld/| adj.|腌渍的，用莳萝腌制的|dill-pickled; preserved in brine seasoned with dill.  The word refers to food preserved using dill, a herb.|例句：We had some delicious dilled cucumbers for a snack. 我们零食吃了一些美味的莳萝腌黄瓜。
例句：The dilled herring was a popular appetizer at the party.  派对上莳萝腌鲱鱼是很受欢迎的开胃菜。|近义词：pickled, brined|反义词：fresh, unpreserved|形容词
