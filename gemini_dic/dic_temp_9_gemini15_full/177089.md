reapplauded:/ˌriːəˈplɔːdɪd/| v. |再次鼓掌，重新鼓掌| To applaud again; to give further applause.  The performance was so captivating that the audience reapplauded enthusiastically.  The actor, deeply moved, bowed as the crowd reapplauded.|近义词：applauded again, re-applauded|反义词：booed, hissed
