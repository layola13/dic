heighten:/ˈhaɪtn/| vt.|提高；增强；加剧|To increase the degree or intensity of something.|The music heightened the emotional impact of the scene. 音乐增强了场景的情感冲击力。  The incident heightened tensions between the two countries.  该事件加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。|increase, intensify, amplify|lower, decrease, diminish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heightens the drama with his acting. = 他用他的演技增强了戏剧性。
They heighten the security measures. = 他们加强了安全措施。


一般过去时:
The storm heightened the waves. = 暴风雨加剧了波浪。
She heightened her voice to be heard. = 她提高了嗓音以便被听到。


现在完成时:
The conflict has heightened tensions in the region. = 冲突加剧了该地区的紧张局势。
Recent events have heightened public awareness of the issue. = 最近的事件提高了公众对这个问题的认识。


过去完成时:
The previous drought had heightened the risk of wildfires. = 先前的干旱加剧了野火的风险。
His aggressive behavior had heightened the tension in the room. = 他咄咄逼人的行为加剧了房间里的紧张气氛。


将来时:
The coming election will heighten political tensions. = 即将到来的选举将会加剧政治紧张局势。
Further investigation will heighten our understanding of the problem. = 进一步的调查将加深我们对这个问题的理解。


虚拟语气:
If they heightened the volume, we could hear better. = 如果他们提高音量，我们就能听得更清楚。
I wish they heightened the lighting in the theater. = 我希望他们能提高剧院的灯光亮度。


被动语态:
The volume was heightened to improve clarity. = 音量被提高以改善清晰度。
Security measures were heightened after the attack. = 袭击发生后，安全措施得到了加强。


疑问句:
Will this action heighten the risks? = 这个行动会增加风险吗？
Does this music heighten your emotions? = 这首音乐能激发你的情绪吗？


否定句:
They did not heighten the security measures. = 他们没有加强安全措施。
The incident did not heighten the existing tensions. = 此事件并没有加剧现有的紧张局势。
