counterprotests:/ˈkaʊntɚˌproʊˌtɛsts/ | n. | 反抗议；反示威 | Demonstrations or protests held in opposition to another protest.  Counterprotests often express opposing viewpoints or attempt to disrupt the original protest. | Example:  Counterprotests erupted outside the government building following the controversial policy announcement.  = 在有争议的政策公告发布后，政府大楼外爆发了反示威活动。 |近义词：counterdemonstrations, opposing protests | 反义词：protests, demonstrations | 名词
