sheriffship:/ˈʃɛrɪfʃɪp/ | n. | 治安官职位；警长职位 | The office or term of office of a sheriff.  It refers to the position itself, the duties, and the period of time a sheriff holds the office. | Example:  He sought the sheriffship for many years before finally winning the election. 他多年来一直寻求警长职位，最终才赢得选举。 |近义词：constableship, bailiwick (in a less formal context) |反义词：无
