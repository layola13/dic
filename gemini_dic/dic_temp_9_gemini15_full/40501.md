contrived:/kənˈtraɪvd/| adj.|人为的；做作的；不自然的 | artificially made or produced;  lacking naturalness or spontaneity; seeming unnatural or false. |The plot of the movie was contrived and unbelievable. 这部电影的情节是人为的，令人难以置信。|近义词：artificial, affected, forced, unnatural |反义词：natural, genuine, authentic, spontaneous


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The writer often contrives complex plots for his novels. = 这名作家经常为他的小说编造复杂的情节。
This situation seems contrived to me. = 这种情况在我看来是人为的。


一般过去时:
He contrived a plan to escape from prison. = 他精心策划了一个越狱计划。
She contrived a believable excuse for her lateness. = 她编造了一个合理的理由来解释她的迟到。


现在完成时:
They have contrived to keep their relationship secret. = 他们一直设法保守他们恋情的秘密。
She has contrived a way to solve the problem.  = 她已经想出了解决这个问题的方法。


过去完成时:
He had contrived a false alibi before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经编造了一个虚假的不在场证明。
They had contrived to meet secretly for months. = 几个月来，他们一直秘密会面。


将来时:
She will contrive a way to get what she wants. = 她会想方设法得到她想要的东西。
We will contrive a solution to this difficult issue. = 我们将想出一个解决这个问题的办法。


虚拟语气:
If I had contrived a better plan, we would have succeeded. = 如果我当时制定了一个更好的计划，我们就会成功。
I wish I had not contrived such a complicated excuse. = 我真希望我没有编造如此复杂的借口。


被动语态:
The evidence was contrived to incriminate him. = 证据是人为编造的，目的是为了陷害他。
A false story was contrived to deceive the public. = 一个虚假的故事被编造出来欺骗公众。


疑问句:
Was the ending of the story contrived? = 故事的结局是人为的吗？
Did he contrive this situation intentionally? = 他是故意制造这种情况的吗？


否定句:
The characters in the book were not contrived; they felt very real. = 书中的人物并非虚构的；他们感觉非常真实。
He didn't contrive to deceive anyone; it was a genuine mistake. = 他并没有试图欺骗任何人；这是一个真实的错误。


PHRASE->

contrive a plan: 制定计划
contrive an excuse: 编造借口
contrive to do something: 想方设法做某事
contrive a solution: 想出解决方案
contrive a story: 编造故事
