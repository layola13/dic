chowing-down:/tʃoʊɪŋ daʊn/| v. |狼吞虎咽地吃，大吃特吃|To eat something very quickly and enthusiastically.|He was chowing down on a huge burger. 他正在狼吞虎咽地吃着一个巨大的汉堡。|gobble, devour, guzzle, scarf down|starve, nibble|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chows down on his breakfast every morning. = 他每天早上都狼吞虎咽地吃早餐。
They are chowing down on pizza at the party. = 他们在派对上大吃披萨。

一般过去时:
She chowed down on a whole plate of spaghetti. = 她吃掉了一整盘意大利面。
We chowed down after the long hike. = 长途跋涉后，我们大吃了一顿。

现在完成时:
He has chowed down three hotdogs already. = 他已经吃了三个热狗了。
They have chowed down all the snacks. = 他们已经吃光了所有的零食。

过去完成时:
She had chowed down before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已经吃饱了。
We had chowed down a big lunch before we went to the beach. = 我们去海滩之前吃了一顿丰盛的午餐。

将来时:
I will chow down on that delicious-looking cake later. = 我一会儿要吃那个看起来美味的蛋糕。
They will chow down after the game. = 比赛结束后他们会大吃一顿。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would chow down on that pizza. = 如果我饿了，我会吃掉那个披萨。
I wish I could chow down on some chocolate right now. = 我真希望我现在能吃点巧克力。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively.)  The food was chowed down quickly.  食物很快就被吃光了。
The entire buffet was chowed down in an hour.  整个自助餐在一小时内就被吃光了。

疑问句:
Are they chowing down on anything? = 他们在吃东西吗？
Will you chow down with us tonight? = 你今晚会和我们一起吃饭吗？


否定句:
He didn't chow down on the meal because he wasn't hungry. = 他因为不饿所以没有狼吞虎咽地吃饭。
She doesn't usually chow down like that. = 她通常不会那样狼吞虎咽地吃。
