greeting:/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/| n.|问候；招呼；致意|The act of expressing a polite welcome or acknowledgement to someone.  It can be verbal, written, or through gestures.|例句：He offered a friendly greeting to the newcomers. 他向新来的人们友好的打招呼。|例句：The letter began with a formal greeting. 这封信以正式的问候开头。|近义词：salutation, welcome, acknowledgment|反义词：farewell, goodbye


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She gives a warm greeting to everyone. = 她热情地向每个人打招呼。
They offer a polite greeting before starting the meeting. = 他们在开始会议前进行礼貌的问候。

一般过去时:
He gave a short greeting before his presentation. = 他在演讲前做了简短的致意。
They exchanged greetings and then began their conversation. = 他们互相问候后开始了谈话。

现在完成时:
I have received many greetings on my birthday. = 我收到了许多生日问候。
She has given several greetings to the guests. = 她已经向几位客人打过招呼了。

过去完成时:
He had already given his greeting before the others arrived. = 在其他人到达之前，他已经打过招呼了。
They had exchanged greetings before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他们已经互相问候过了。

将来时:
I will give a brief greeting at the conference. = 我将在会议上做简短的致辞。
She will offer a warm greeting to the visitors. = 她将热情地迎接来访者。

虚拟语气:
If I had known him, I would have offered a more enthusiastic greeting. = 如果我认识他，我会给他更热情的问候。
I wish I had given a more thoughtful greeting. = 我希望我当时给了一个更周到的问候。

被动语态:
The guests were greeted warmly by the host. = 客人们受到了主人的热情款待。
A formal greeting was given at the beginning of the event. = 在活动开始时进行了正式的问候。

疑问句:
What kind of greeting did you receive? = 你收到了什么样的问候？
What is an appropriate greeting for this situation? = 在这种情况下，合适的问候是什么？

否定句:
He didn't give a greeting to anyone. = 他没有向任何人打招呼。
She didn't receive a single greeting all day. = 她一整天都没收到任何问候。
