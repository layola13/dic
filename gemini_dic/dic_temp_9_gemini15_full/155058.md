petrel:/ˈpɛtrəl/| n. |海燕，海燕属鸟类 | A small to medium-sized seabird with long, narrow wings, often seen far from land. Petrels are known for their gliding flight and ability to skim the ocean surface. They are typically found in the open ocean and are often associated with stormy weather. | Example:  A storm petrel skimmed the waves, its tiny body barely visible against the churning water.  一只海燕掠过波浪，它小小的身体在翻腾的水面上几乎看不见。  |近义词： seabird |反义词：None.
