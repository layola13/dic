massive:/ˈmæsɪv/| adj.|巨大的，大量的，结实的|extremely large in size, amount, or extent;  substantial and solid in construction.|例句：The massive oak tree stood proudly in the park. 这棵巨大的橡树骄傲地矗立在公园里。|例句：The project requires a massive investment of time and resources. 这个项目需要大量的时间和资源投入。|近义词：huge, enormous, immense, gigantic, substantial|反义词：tiny, minuscule, insignificant, small


USAGE->
