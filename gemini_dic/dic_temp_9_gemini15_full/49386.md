delirium:/dɪˈlɪriəm/ | n. | 谵妄；精神错乱；胡言乱语 | A state of confusion and disorientation, often accompanied by hallucinations, that is typically caused by illness or drugs.  |  The patient experienced delirium after surgery. 手术后病人出现谵妄。  The fever induced a state of delirium. 高烧导致了谵妄状态。|  confusion, frenzy, mania | lucidity, coherence, rationality | 名词
