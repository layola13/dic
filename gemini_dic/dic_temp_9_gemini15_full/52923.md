dimwits:/ˈdɪmwɪts/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋 |A dimwit is a foolish or stupid person.  The term is informal and often used humorously or contemptuously. |例句：He's such a dimwit; he can't even boil an egg. 他真是个傻瓜，连个鸡蛋都不会煮。

例句：Don't be such a dimwit; think before you act. 别那么傻，行动前先动动脑子。


近义词：fool, idiot, moron, dunce, simpleton


反义词：genius, intellect, mastermind, bright spark

词性:名词
