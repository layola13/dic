chilled-out:/tʃɪldˈaʊt/| adj.|轻松的，冷静的，放松的|relaxed and calm; not easily upset or worried.  The adjective is formed by combining "chilled" (past participle of "chill," meaning to relax) and "out" (an adverb intensifying the meaning).|例句：He's a very chilled-out guy, he never seems stressed.|近义词：relaxed, calm, laid-back, easygoing |反义词：stressed, anxious, tense, uptight
