debilitative: /dɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. |使衰弱的；使虚弱的 | weakening or debilitating; causing weakness or debilitation.  |The debilitative effects of the illness were severe.  这种疾病的虚弱作用很严重。 |近义词：weakening, enervating, incapacitating |反义词：strengthening, invigorating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The illness has debilitative effects on his body. = 这种疾病对他的身体有削弱作用。
This job is quite debilitating to people's mental health. = 这份工作相当损害人们的心理健康。


一般过去时:
The long journey had a debilitating effect on the travelers. = 漫长的旅程对旅行者产生了衰弱的影响。
The surgery left her feeling significantly debilitated. = 手术让她感觉明显虚弱。


现在完成时:
Years of overwork have left him debilitated. = 多年的过度劳累使他虚弱不堪。
The prolonged drought has debilitated the entire region. = 长期的干旱已经使整个地区衰弱。


过去完成时:
He had been debilitated by illness before the competition. = 他在比赛前就已经因病虚弱了。
She had become debilitated by years of poor diet and lack of exercise. = 多年不良饮食和缺乏锻炼使她虚弱不堪。


将来时:
Continued stress will likely debilitate him further. = 持续的压力可能会使他进一步虚弱。
Without proper treatment, the disease will debilitate her completely. = 没有适当的治疗，这种疾病会使她完全虚弱。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to work so hard, he would become debilitated. = 如果他继续如此努力工作，他会变得虚弱。
I wish I hadn't overworked myself; I feel quite debilitated. = 我真希望我没有过度劳累；我现在感觉相当虚弱。


被动语态:
He was debilitated by the heat. = 他被炎热的天气弄虚弱了。
The patient was severely debilitated by the disease. = 病人被疾病严重削弱了。


疑问句:
Is this medication known to have any debilitating side effects? = 这种药物是否有任何使人虚弱的副作用？
Are you feeling debilitated after your surgery? = 你手术后感觉虚弱吗？


否定句:
The new treatment wasn't debilitating; in fact, it strengthened him. = 新疗法并没有使他虚弱；事实上，它使他强壮了。
The exercise program was not debilitating; it improved her overall health. = 这项运动计划并没有使她虚弱；它改善了她的整体健康状况。
