ruts:/rʌts/| n.|车辙；惯例；刻板的套路|A rut is a deep track made in the ground by the repeated passage of vehicles or people.  It can also refer to a fixed, unchangeable pattern of behaviour, especially one that is dull and unproductive.  |例句：The car wheels spun in the deep ruts.  汽车轮子在深深的车辙里打滑。|例句：He fell into a rut after losing his job. 他失业后陷入了困境。|近义词：groove, track, habit, routine |反义词：innovation, change, novelty
