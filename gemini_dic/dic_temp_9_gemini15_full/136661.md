nonseasonably:/ˌnɒnˈsiːzənəbli/ | adj. |不合时宜地；反常地；过早地|  Occurring or done out of season; untimely.  It describes something happening at an unexpected or inappropriate time, typically referring to weather or events. |例句：The unusually warm weather in December was nonseasonably mild.  十二月份异常温暖的天气反常地温和。

The early blooming of the flowers was nonseasonably early this year.今年花开得过早，不合时宜。|近义词：unseasonably, untimely, inappropriately, prematurely|反义词：seasonably, timely, appropriately
