roughwork:/ˈrʌfˌwɜːrk/| n. |草稿；粗略计算；准备工作 |Preliminary or unpolished work; calculations or drafts done before a final version.  It often involves sketches, notes, or calculations that are not intended for final presentation. |例句：I did some roughwork before starting the painting.  我在开始绘画之前做了一些草图。

例句：The architect presented the roughwork for the building design to the client. 建筑师向客户展示了建筑设计的草图。


近义词：draft, preliminary work, preparatory work, sketch, calculation

反义词：final version, finished product, polished work

词性: 名词
