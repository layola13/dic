rhomb:/rɒm/| n. |菱形，斜方形 |A rhombus is a quadrilateral with all sides equal in length.  It is also known as a diamond. A rhombus is a parallelogram with all four sides having equal lengths.  |例句：The kite was shaped like a rhombus.  这只风筝是菱形的。

The tiles on the floor were arranged in a rhombus pattern. 地板上的瓷砖呈菱形排列。|近义词：diamond, lozenge |反义词：square (in the context of shape)
