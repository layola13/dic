maples:/ˈmeɪpəlz/| n.|枫树（复数）|The word "maples" is the plural form of "maple," referring to trees of the genus Acer, known for their characteristic palmate leaves and often vibrant fall foliage.  They are widely distributed across the Northern Hemisphere and are valued for their timber, syrup, and aesthetic appeal.|例句：The autumn leaves of the maples were a stunning display of red and gold.  枫树的秋叶展现出令人惊艳的红与金.|近义词：maple trees|反义词：无
