hogpen:/ˈhɑːɡpen/| n. |猪圈；猪舍 |A hogpen is a pen or enclosure where pigs are kept.  It is often a simple structure, sometimes muddy and unsanitary. |例句：The farmer cleaned out the hogpen every week.  = 农民每周都清理猪圈。 The pigs were rooting around in the mud of the hogpen. = 猪在猪圈的泥里拱来拱去。|近义词：pigsty, pigpen |反义词：mansion, palace
