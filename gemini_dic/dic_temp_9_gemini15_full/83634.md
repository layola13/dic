词源:
From haem(o)- + globin.  "Haemo-" is from Greek  αἷμα (haima, "blood"), and "-globin"  is from Greek γλωβίς (glōbís, "globe," referring to the spherical shape of the protein).  Therefore, the word literally means "blood globe protein".
