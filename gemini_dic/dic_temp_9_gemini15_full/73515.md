foreshew:/fɔːrˈʃuː/| v.|预示，预兆|To indicate or suggest beforehand; to foreshadow.|The dark clouds foreshewed a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。|The unusual silence foreshewed trouble.  不寻常的寂静预示着麻烦。|foretell, predict, portend|None|动词
