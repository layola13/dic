in-real-time:/ˌɪn ˈriːəl taɪm/ | adj. | 实时；即时 |  Happening or done immediately without delay; occurring at the same time.  | Example: The software provides in-real-time updates of the market data.  该软件提供市场数据的实时更新。

| Example: We are monitoring the situation in real-time. 我们正在实时监控局势。 | 近义词：live, instantaneous, concurrent | 反义词：delayed, deferred, retrospective |  词性：形容词
