cupidity:/kjuːˈpɪdəti/| n. |贪婪，贪心 |An extreme greed for wealth or material gain.  Cupidity often drives individuals to make unethical or immoral choices in pursuit of riches or possessions. |例句：His cupidity led him to embezzle funds from the company. = 他的贪婪导致他挪用了公司的资金。

例句：Driven by cupidity, he risked everything for a chance at a vast fortune. = 受贪婪驱使，他为了获得巨额财富而孤注一掷。


近义词：avarice, greed, covetousness, rapacity |反义词：generosity, altruism, selflessness |词性:名词
