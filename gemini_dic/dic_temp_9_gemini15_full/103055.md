jemmies:/ˈdʒɛmiz/| n.|撬棍，（尤指）短撬棍|A jemmy is a short, strong, flat tool used for forcing open locks or doors.  It's often used in burglaries.  The term is also sometimes used more generally to refer to any type of crowbar.|例句：The burglar used a jemmy to force open the back door.  = 窃贼用撬棍撬开了后门。

The police found jemmies and other tools at the scene of the crime. = 警方在犯罪现场发现了撬棍和其他工具。|近义词：crowbar,jimmy,pry bar|反义词：None specifically, but tools used for securing would be antonymous in function.|名词
