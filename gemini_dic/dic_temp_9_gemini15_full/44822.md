crusher:/ˈkrʌʃər/| n. |压碎机，粉碎机|A machine used to crush or grind materials, such as rocks, ore, or other solid substances.|例句：The quarry uses a powerful crusher to break down the rocks.  采石场使用强大的压碎机来破碎岩石。
例句：The recycling center has a crusher to compact the aluminum cans. 回收中心有一个压碎机来压实铝罐。|近义词：breaker, grinder, pulverizer|反义词：assembler, constructor
