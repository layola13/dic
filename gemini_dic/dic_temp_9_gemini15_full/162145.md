pre-Germanics: /ˌpriːˈdʒɜːrmənɪks/ | adj. | 前日耳曼语的 | Relating to the languages ancestral to the Germanic languages, existing before the Proto-Germanic language developed.  These languages are reconstructed based on evidence from later Germanic and other Indo-European languages.  They are not directly attested. | The study of pre-Germanic languages offers valuable insights into the development of the Germanic branch of the Indo-European language family. | 近义词：proto-Indo-European (更广泛的范畴) | 反义词：post-Germanic (没有直接的反义词，因为这是一个时间概念)
