gendarme:/ˈʒɑːndɑːrm/|/ˈʒændɑːrm/| n. |宪兵，警官|A gendarme is a member of a military police force, especially in France.  They typically have law enforcement powers and are often involved in maintaining public order. |The gendarme stopped the speeding car.  宪兵拦下了超速行驶的汽车。|police officer, policeman, law enforcement officer|civilian, criminal|名词

词源:
The word "gendarme" comes from the Old French word "gens d'armes," which literally translates to "men-at-arms."  This term originally referred to a type of medieval mounted soldier, and the role evolved over time to encompass the modern-day law enforcement function.
