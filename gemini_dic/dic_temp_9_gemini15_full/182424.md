renewed:/rɪˈnjuːd/| adj. |更新的；恢复的；续签的| renewed interest in the subject 指对该主题的兴趣重新燃起；a renewed contract  一份续签的合同 | The company showed renewed commitment to environmental protection. 这家公司展现出对环境保护的 renewed commitment (重新承诺)。|近义词：restored, revived, refreshed |反义词：terminated, ended, canceled


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The project shows renewed energy. = 项目展现出新的活力。
His renewed interest in art is inspiring. = 他重拾对艺术的兴趣令人鼓舞。

一般过去时:
They renewed their vows after 20 years. = 他们结婚20年后重温了誓言。
The contract was renewed last month. = 合同在上个月续签了。

现在完成时:
The city has renewed its commitment to sustainable development. = 该城市已经更新了其对可持续发展的承诺。
We have renewed our subscription to the magazine. = 我们已经续订了杂志订阅。

过去完成时:
He had renewed his passport before the trip. = 他在旅行前已经续签了护照。
The agreement had been renewed several times before the dispute. = 在争议之前，协议已经续签过几次。

将来时:
They will renew their efforts to find a solution. = 他们将重新努力寻找解决方案。
The lease will be renewed automatically. = 租赁合同将自动续签。

虚拟语气:
If the contract were renewed, we could continue the project. = 如果合同续签，我们就可以继续项目。
I wish the feeling of excitement could be renewed. = 我希望这种兴奋感能够重现。

被动语态:
The agreement was renewed by both parties. = 协议得到了双方续签。
His hope was renewed by the good news. = 好消息使他的希望重燃。

疑问句:
Will the contract be renewed? = 合同会续签吗？
Has the interest in this topic been renewed? = 对这个主题的兴趣是否重新燃起？

否定句:
The negotiations did not result in a renewed contract. = 谈判没有达成续签合同的结果。
The museum did not renew its exhibition. = 博物馆没有续展。


PHRASE->

renewed efforts:  renewed efforts to control pollution (控制污染的 renewed efforts (新的努力))
renewed interest:  renewed interest in classical music (对古典音乐 renewed interest (重新燃起的兴趣))
renewed commitment: renewed commitment to social justice (对社会正义 renewed commitment (重新承诺))
renewed hope: renewed hope for peace (对和平 renewed hope (重燃的希望))
renewed vigor:  He approached the task with renewed vigor. (他以 renewed vigor (新的活力) 投入到这项任务中。)
