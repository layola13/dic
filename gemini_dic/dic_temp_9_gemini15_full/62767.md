engrain:/ɪnˈɡreɪn/| vt.|使根深蒂固；铭刻；染牢|To fix something firmly in someone's mind or nature.  To dye (fabric) deeply and permanently.|例句：His beliefs were deeply engrained in him. 他的信仰已根深蒂固。

The experience was engrained in my memory. 这段经历深深地铭刻在我的记忆中。


近义词：implant, inculcate, instill, imbue|反义词：erase, remove, obliterate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engrains these values into his children. 他把这些价值观灌输给他的孩子们。
She engrains the lesson in her mind. 她把教训铭记在心。


一般过去时:
They engrained the principles of democracy into their society. 他们将民主原则根植于他们的社会。
The teacher engrained the vocabulary into the students' minds. 老师把词汇深深地印在了学生们的脑海里。


现在完成时:
Years of practice have engrained the technique in his body. 多年的练习已使这项技术融入他的身体。
She has engrained the habit of reading before bed. 她养成了睡前阅读的习惯。


过去完成时:
He had engrained a strong work ethic in his sons before he died. 他去世前已经在儿子心中灌输了强烈的职业道德。
They had engrained the importance of honesty in their children's upbringing.  他们在孩子的成长过程中灌输了诚实的重要性。


将来时:
The training program will engrain the necessary skills in the employees. 培训计划将把必要的技能灌输给员工。
This experience will engrain a lifelong lesson in him.  这次经历将会给他一个终生的教训。


虚拟语气:
If I had engrained better habits earlier, I would be in better shape now. 如果我早点养成更好的习惯，我现在的情况就会更好。
I wish I had engrained those lessons in my youth. 我真希望我年轻时就铭记那些教训。


被动语态:
The principles were engrained into the students by the teacher. 这些原则被老师灌输给了学生们。
These values are engrained into the company culture. 这些价值观已经融入公司文化中。


疑问句:

否定句:
