compatriotism: /kəmˈpætɹiəˌtɪzəm/ | n. |爱国主义，同胞情谊 |The feeling of patriotism and love for one's country and fellow citizens.  It often involves a sense of shared identity, culture, and history.  |例句：The government fostered compatriotism through nationalistic propaganda. 政府通过民族主义宣传来培养爱国主义精神。

例句：Their compatriotism was evident in their willingness to fight for their nation. 他们愿意为国家而战，这充分体现了他们的爱国主义精神。


近义词：nationalism, patriotism, loyalty

反义词：treason, disloyalty


词源：来自拉丁语 *com- (together) + patria (fatherland) + -ism (belief or doctrine)
