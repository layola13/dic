glosses:/ˈɡlɒsɪz/ | n. |注释；解释；词语解释 | Glossing refers to the act of providing brief explanations or translations of words or phrases, often within a text or alongside a piece of writing.  It can involve simple definitions, paraphrases, or more detailed linguistic analysis. Glosses are commonly used in academic study, particularly in the fields of linguistics and literature.  | Example: The professor provided glosses for all the difficult vocabulary words in the ancient text.  The student's translation included helpful glosses clarifying the nuances of the original language. | 近义词：interpretations, explanations, annotations, translations | 反义词：obfuscation, concealment
