scram:/skræm/| v.| 急忙离开；滚开；逃跑 | To leave quickly; to go away hurriedly; to flee.  |例句：Scram! Get out of here! 滚！给我滚出去！|例句：The police told the protesters to scram. 警察命令抗议者离开。|近义词：clear out, beat it, get lost, vamoose |反义词：stay, remain, linger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrams when he sees the police. = 他看到警察就跑。
They scram every time the boss comes around. = 老板一来他们就赶紧离开。


一般过去时:
He scrammed as soon as he heard the siren. = 他一听到警笛声就跑了。
They scrammed out of the building when the fire alarm went off. = 火警警报响起时，他们从大楼里逃了出来。


现在完成时:
He has scrammed and we don't know where he is. = 他已经逃跑了，我们不知道他在哪里。
They have scrammed several times this week to avoid trouble. = 为了避免麻烦，他们本周已经逃跑了数次。


过去完成时:
He had scrammed before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他已经逃跑了。
They had scrammed already when the news broke. = 消息传出时，他们已经逃跑了。


将来时:
He will scram if he sees any trouble. = 如果他看到任何麻烦，他会逃跑。
They will scram as soon as they get the chance. = 他们一有机会就会逃跑。


虚拟语气:
I wish he would scram and leave me alone. = 我希望他能赶紧离开，别烦我。
If he saw the police, he would scram immediately. = 如果他看到警察，他会立刻逃跑。


被动语态:
(Scram is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.)


疑问句:
Will he scram before we catch him? = 我们抓住他之前他会逃跑吗？
Did they scram when they heard the noise? = 他们听到噪音时逃跑了吗？


否定句:
He didn't scram; he stood his ground. = 他没有逃跑；他坚守阵地。
They wouldn't scram even when threatened. = 即使受到威胁，他们也不愿离开。


PHRASE->
Scram! = 滚！
Scram outta here! = 从这儿滚开！
Just scram! = 快滚！
Get out of here, scram! = 给我滚出去！
Scram and don't come back! = 滚，别再回来了！
