desiccated:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.| 干燥的；脱水的；枯竭的 | extremely dry; shriveled; lacking moisture.  |例句：The desiccated fruit was hard and tasteless.  干果又硬又难吃。

例句：The desert landscape was desiccated by the relentless sun.  无情的太阳使沙漠景观干涸。


近义词：dried, dehydrated, parched, withered, arid

反义词：moist, hydrated, saturated, damp

词性:形容词
