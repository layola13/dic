meningitises:/ˌmenɪnˈdʒaɪtɪsiːz/| n. |脑膜炎(复数)|The plural of meningitis, referring to multiple instances of the disease or different types of meningitis.  Meningitis is an inflammation of the protective membranes covering the brain and spinal cord.  It can be caused by various infections, such as bacterial, viral, or fungal infections.|例句：The doctor suspected meningitises after observing the patient's symptoms.|近义词：encephalitides (脑炎，复数)|反义词：None|名词
