predisclose:/ˌpriːdɪˈskloʊz/| vt.|预先披露；提前公开|To reveal or disclose something before it is officially or publicly announced.|例句：The company predisclosed its earnings to a select group of investors. 公司向一小部分投资者预先披露了其收益。  例句：They predisclosed the information to gain a competitive advantage. 他们提前公开信息以获得竞争优势。|近义词：pre-reveal, leak, divulge|反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress|动词
