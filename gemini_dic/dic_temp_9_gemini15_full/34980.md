cloggers:/ˈklɒɡərz/| n.|木底鞋；（尤指）荷兰木鞋；阻塞物；阻碍物|Wooden shoes, especially those of Dutch design; something that blocks or obstructs.|例句：The clogs are traditional footwear in the Netherlands.  荷兰木鞋是荷兰的传统鞋履。

例句：The fallen tree was a major clogger in the river. 倒下的树是河里主要的阻塞物。

近义词：sabots, clogs, obstructions, impediments

反义词：clearances, unobstructions
