protestantization: /ˌprɒtɛstənˈtaɪzeɪʃən/ | n. | 新教化；新教徒化 | The process of converting or being converted to Protestantism.  The imposition of Protestant beliefs and practices on a population or region. |例句：The forced protestantization of the country led to widespread unrest.  强迫该国新教化导致了广泛的不满。


The systematic imposition of Protestant religious practices and beliefs upon a Catholic population. This often involved suppression of Catholic institutions, replacing them with Protestant ones, and changes in religious and social practices.

近义词：Protestantification, Reformation (in a specific context)


反义词：Catholicisation, Counter-Reformation


词性: 名词
