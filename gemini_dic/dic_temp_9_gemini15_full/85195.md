haricot:/ˈhærɪkɒt/| n. |菜豆；四季豆|French word for kidney bean or string bean,  often used in culinary contexts to refer to various types of beans, especially when prepared in a particular way.  It can also refer to a type of French stew. |例句：The haricots verts were perfectly cooked.  (青豆煮得恰到好处。)  The recipe called for haricots in a rich tomato sauce. (食谱需要用丰富的番茄酱来烹饪菜豆。) |近义词：kidney bean, string bean, French bean |反义词：无
