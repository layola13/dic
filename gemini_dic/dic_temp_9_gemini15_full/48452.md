degree:/dɪˈɡriː/ | n. |度数，程度，学位 | A degree is a unit of measurement of angles, temperature, or other quantities.  It can also refer to an academic qualification granted by a college or university. | Examples:  The temperature is 25 degrees Celsius. (温度是摄氏25度)  She has a degree in engineering. (她拥有工程学位) |近义词：level, extent, rank |反义词： (根据语境而定，例如：absolute zero for temperature)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature is 30 degrees. = 温度是30度。
What degree are you studying? = 你学习的是什么学位？


一般过去时:
The temperature was 20 degrees yesterday. = 昨天温度是20度。
He received his degree last year. = 他去年获得了学位。


现在完成时:
The temperature has risen several degrees. = 温度已经上升了几度。
She has completed her degree. = 她已经完成了她的学位。


过去完成时:
The temperature had dropped several degrees before dawn. = 在黎明前温度已经下降了几度。
He had already earned his degree before he started working. = 在他开始工作之前他已经获得了学位。


将来时:
The temperature will be 10 degrees tomorrow. = 明天温度将是10度。
She will receive her degree next month. = 她下个月将获得学位。


虚拟语气:
If the temperature were lower, we would go outside. = 如果温度低一些，我们会出去。
If I had a degree, I would have a better job. = 如果我有学位，我会找到一份更好的工作。


被动语态:
The temperature is measured in degrees. = 温度是用度来测量的。
The degree was awarded to him. = 学位授予了他。


疑问句:
What degree is the angle? = 这个角度是多少度？
What degree did he get? = 他获得了什么学位？


否定句:
The temperature is not below zero degrees. = 温度没有低于零度。
He doesn't have a degree in physics. = 他没有物理学学位。


PHRASE->
to a degree:  在某种程度上
to some degree: 在某种程度上
a high degree of: 高度的
a large degree of:  很大的
in degree: 以度为单位
