pathfinders:/ˈpæθˌfaɪndərz/ | n. |探路者，先驱者 | People or things that explore new areas or possibilities, often facing challenges and uncertainty. They are innovators and leaders who pave the way for others.  |Example: The early explorers were pathfinders, venturing into unknown territories. 早期探险家是探路者，他们冒险进入未知的领地。

The Lewis and Clark expedition were pathfinders of the American West.  刘易斯和克拉克探险队是美国西部的探路者。|近义词：pioneers, explorers, innovators, trailblazers |反义词：followers, imitators |名词
