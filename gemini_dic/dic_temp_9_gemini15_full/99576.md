interinfluence:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluəns/| n. |相互影响，互相作用|The mutual impact or effect of two or more things on each other.  It refers to a reciprocal relationship where each element influences and is influenced by the others.  This process often leads to changes or adaptations within the interacting elements. |例句：The interinfluence of climate and agriculture is a complex issue. 气候和农业之间的相互影响是一个复杂的问题。

The interinfluence between social media and politics is undeniable. 社交媒体和政治之间的相互影响是不可否认的。


近义词：interaction, reciprocal influence, mutual impact, interplay

反义词：independence, isolation, unilateral influence

词性:名词
