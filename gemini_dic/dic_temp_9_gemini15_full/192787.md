seamans:/ˈsiːmən/| n.|水手，海员|A seaman is a person who works on a ship, especially as a member of the crew.|例句：He was a seasoned seaman, having sailed the seven seas. 他是一位经验丰富的水手，航行过七大洋。|例句：The ship's crew, including the captain and several seamen, were rescued. 船员，包括船长和几名水手，都被救了。|近义词：sailor, mariner, seafarer|反义词：landlubber
