innkeepers:/ˈɪnˌkiːpərz/ | n. | 旅馆老板们，客栈老板们 | People who own or manage inns or hotels.  Innkeepers are responsible for the daily operations of their establishment, including guest services, staff management, and maintaining the property. | The innkeepers welcomed us warmly and showed us to our rooms. 旅馆老板们热情地欢迎我们并带我们去房间。 The innkeepers are known for their excellent customer service and delicious breakfasts.  客栈老板们以其优质的客户服务和美味的早餐而闻名。 | hoteliers, landlords |  (无明确反义词，可考虑反义词为guests) | 名词
