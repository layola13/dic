nuances:/ˈnuːɑːnsɪz/ | n. |细微差别，细致之处 | Subtle differences in or shades of meaning, expression, or sound.  Nuances are often difficult to articulate or define precisely, but they significantly impact the overall impression or meaning. |例句：The nuances of the poem were lost in translation. 这首诗的细微之处在翻译中丢失了。

The nuances of his expression revealed his true feelings. 他表情的细微之处流露出了他的真实感受。

|近义词：subtleties, refinements, shades, gradations |反义词：obviousness, crudeness |词性：名词
