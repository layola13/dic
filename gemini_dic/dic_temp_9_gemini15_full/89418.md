homotype:/ˈhɒmətaɪp/| n. |同型；同种型；同型异体|A homotype is a specimen or illustration that closely resembles the type specimen of a species and is used as a reference point for identification.  It serves as a representative example of the species.  |Example: The newly discovered fossil is considered a homotype of the previously described species.|近义词：type specimen, representative specimen|反义词：paratype, neotype
