confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/|/kənˈfaʊnd/| vt.  |使困惑；使混淆；证明...是错的|To confuse and bewilder someone.  To prove (someone or something) wrong; refute.|The complexity of the instructions confounded me. | confuse, bewilder, perplex | clarify, enlighten |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noise confounds me. = 噪音让我困惑。
This evidence confounds the defendant's alibi. = 这证据驳斥了被告的不在场证明。


一般过去时:
The sudden change in plans confounded our efforts. = 计划的突然改变使我们的努力付诸东流。
His arguments confounded his opponents. = 他的论点驳斥了他的对手。


现在完成时:
The unexpected results have confounded the scientists. = 意想不到的结果让科学家们困惑不解。
Years of research have confounded the skeptics. = 多年的研究已经驳斥了怀疑论者。


过去完成时:
The unexpected twist in the plot had confounded the readers. = 情节中出人意料的反转让读者困惑不解。
The evidence presented had confounded the prosecution's case. = 提出的证据已经驳斥了检方的说法。


将来时:
The new regulations will confound many businesses. = 新规将使许多企业困惑。
His new theory will confound the scientific community. = 他的新理论将使科学界困惑不解。


虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't confounded the instructions, I could have succeeded. = 如果我没有弄混说明书，我本来可以成功的。
If the evidence had not confounded his arguments, he might have won the debate.  = 如果证据没有驳斥他的论点，他或许就能赢得辩论。


被动语态:
The witnesses were confounded by the lawyer's skillful questioning. = 证人们被律师巧妙的提问弄糊涂了。
His claims were confounded by irrefutable evidence. = 他的说法被无可辩驳的证据驳斥了。


疑问句:
What confounds you the most about this problem? = 关于这个问题，什么让你最困惑？
Does this evidence confound the theory? = 这个证据是否驳斥了这个理论？


否定句:
The explanation did not confound me. = 这个解释没有让我困惑。
The new data did not confound the existing hypothesis. = 新数据并没有驳斥现有的假设。


PHRASE->
confound sb's expectations = 违背某人的期望
confound sb's plans = 打乱某人的计划
confound the critics = 驳斥批评家
confound all expectations = 出乎意料
be confounded by sth = 对某事感到困惑
