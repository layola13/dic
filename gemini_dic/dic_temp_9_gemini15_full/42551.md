countermure:/ˈkaʊntərmjʊər/| n. |反墙，外墙|An outer wall built around a fortification, especially a second wall behind a main wall.  It provides additional defense.  The term is often used in historical contexts, particularly in relation to medieval castles and fortifications.|例句：The castle boasted a formidable countermure, adding another layer of protection against siege.|近义词：outer wall, secondary wall, curtain wall|反义词：inner wall, main wall
