scorecard:/ˈskɔːrkɑːrd/ | n. |记分牌；成绩单；评估报告 | A scorecard is a record of scores in a game or competition, or a summary of performance or results.  It can also refer to a list of criteria used to assess something. |例句：The scorecard showed the home team was winning by a large margin. 记分牌显示主队以较大优势领先。

The teacher used a scorecard to assess each student's progress. 老师用成绩单来评估每个学生的进步。

近义词：record, report, assessment, evaluation | 反义词：None
