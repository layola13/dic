skam:/skæm/| n.|骗局，欺诈|A scam is a dishonest scheme; a fraud.  It's a plan to trick someone out of their money or other valuables.|例句：He was arrested for running a lottery scam. 他因策划彩票骗局而被捕。|例句：The elderly woman was the victim of a phone scam. 那位老太太是电话诈骗的受害者。|近义词：fraud, swindle, con, racket|反义词：legitimate, honest, genuine|名词
