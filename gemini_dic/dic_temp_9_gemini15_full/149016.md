owed:/oʊd/| vt.|欠（债、钱、人情等）|to be under obligation to pay or repay (something borrowed or received)|I owed him ten dollars.  我欠他十美元。|  He owed his success to hard work. 他把成功归功于努力工作。|  pay, be indebted to |collect, receive|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I owe you a favor. = 我欠你一个人情。
She owes the bank a large sum of money. = 她欠银行一大笔钱。


一般过去时:
I owed him money for months. = 我欠他钱好几个月了。
They owed their parents a great deal of gratitude. = 他们非常感激他们的父母。


现在完成时:
I have owed him money since last year. = 从去年开始我就欠他钱了。
She has owed her success to her persistence. = 她把成功归功于她的坚持。


过去完成时:
I had owed him money before he asked me to repay it. = 在他让我还钱之前，我就已经欠他钱了。
They had owed the company for several years. = 他们已经欠公司好几年了。


将来时:
I will owe him a bigger favour after this. = 这之后我将欠他更大的人情。
They will owe a lot of money after buying the house. = 他们买房后将欠下很多钱。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I wouldn't have owed him so much. = 如果我知道的话，我就不会欠他这么多钱了。
I wish I hadn't owed my friend so much money. = 我真希望我没有欠我朋友这么多钱。


被动语态:
The money is owed to the bank. = 这笔钱欠银行的。
A great debt is owed to the pioneers. = 我们非常感谢开拓者们。


疑问句:
Do you owe anyone money? = 你欠别人钱吗？
Does she owe you anything? = 她欠你什么吗？


否定句:
I don't owe you anything. = 我不欠你任何东西。
He doesn't owe them any explanation. = 他不必向他们解释。


PHRASE->
owe it to someone = 应归功于某人
owe someone a debt = 欠某人债
owe someone an apology = 欠某人一句道歉
owe something to somebody = 把……归功于某人
owe a living to = 依靠……生活
