self-impregnated:/ˌsɛlfɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪd/| adj. | 自我受精的，自孕的 |  Describes a process or organism capable of fertilization without external assistance; specifically referring to a plant or organism that can fertilize its own ovules or eggs. | Example:  The self-impregnated plant produced seeds without any cross-pollination.  自我受精的植物无需异花授粉就能产生种子。 |近义词：autogamous, self-fertilizing | 反义词：cross-pollinated, allogamous
