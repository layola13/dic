luminescent:/ˌluːmɪˈnɛsnt/ | adj. | 发光的，夜光的 |  Emitting or reflecting light, especially in the dark.  The phenomenon of emitting light without significant heat.  | 例句：Fireflies are luminescent insects. 萤火虫是发光的昆虫。

The moonstone displayed a soft, luminescent glow. 月长石发出柔和的夜光。


近义词：phosphorescent, bioluminescent, radiant | 反义词：dark, opaque
