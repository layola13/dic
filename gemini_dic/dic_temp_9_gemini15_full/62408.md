endogenicity:/ˌɛndəʊdʒɛˈnɪsəti/| n. |内源性；内生性|The inherent property of something originating from within a system or organism.  It often refers to substances or factors produced within the body or a biological system, as opposed to those introduced from outside. In economics, it refers to factors originating within the economic system itself.|例句：The study investigated the endogenicity of the observed behavioral changes. 这项研究调查了观察到的行为变化的内生性。

The researchers controlled for endogenicity in their statistical model. 研究人员在统计模型中控制了内生性。


近义词：internality, intrinsicness |反义词：exogenicity, externality |词性：名词
