gonotheca:/ˌɡɒnəˈθiːkə/ | n. |生殖巢；生殖囊 | A gonotheca is a protective case or structure that encloses the reproductive organs in certain hydrozoans (a class of aquatic invertebrates).  It is typically formed by a modification of the hydranth (the polyp-like individual) and serves to protect developing medusae (the jellyfish-like stage) or other reproductive structures.  | Example: The gonotheca of this hydrozoan species is distinctly shaped and easily identifiable under a microscope.  = 这种水螅纲动物的生殖囊形状独特，在显微镜下很容易识别。 |近义词: reproductive case, reproductive structure |反义词: N/A
