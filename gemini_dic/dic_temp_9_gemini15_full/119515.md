microprobe:/ˈmaɪkrəʊproʊb/| n. |微探针；微探测器 |A microprobe is an instrument used for microanalysis, typically employing a focused beam of electrons, ions, or X-rays to analyze the composition of a very small area of a sample.  It's used to determine the elemental and sometimes isotopic composition of a tiny area of a material. |Example: Scientists used a microprobe to analyze the composition of the ancient meteorite. 科学家们用微探针分析了这块古老陨石的成分。|近义词：microanalyzer, microbeam analyzer|反义词：macroscope (反义词较为宽泛，指大尺度观察仪器)|名词
