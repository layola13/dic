oxanilamide:/ˌɒksænɪˈlaɪmaɪd/| n. |氨茴酸酰胺|Oxanilamide is an organic compound with the formula C₇H₇NO₂.  It's a white crystalline powder, slightly soluble in water, and used primarily as an intermediate in the synthesis of other chemicals, particularly pharmaceuticals and dyes.  It's not typically used as a drug itself. |The synthesis of oxanilamide involves the reaction of aniline with oxalic acid.|近义词: (No direct synonyms due to its specific chemical structure and limited application as a standalone compound)|反义词: (Not applicable)|名词
