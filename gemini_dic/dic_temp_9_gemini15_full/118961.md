meter:/ˈmiːtər/ | n. | 米；公尺；计量器；韵律；节拍 | A unit of length equal to 39.37 inches; a measuring instrument; the rhythmic pattern of stressed and unstressed syllables in a line of verse. | The table is two meters long. 这张桌子两米长。  The gas meter is outside.  煤气表在外面。 | 近义词：gauge, measure, rhythm | 反义词：N/A
