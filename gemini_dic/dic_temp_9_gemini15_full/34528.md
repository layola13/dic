cleanly:/ˈkliːnli/ | adv. | 干净地；彻底地；熟练地 | In a clean manner; thoroughly; skillfully. |例句：She cleaned the house cleanly. 她把房子打扫得干干净净。  The surgeon performed the operation cleanly. 外科医生手术做得非常熟练。|近义词：thoroughly, completely, skillfully | 反义词：messily, sloppily, clumsily


USAGE->

She always writes cleanly and neatly. = 她总是写字写得很干净整洁。
