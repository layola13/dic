inter-pollinates:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləneɪts/ | v. |互相授粉；交叉授粉 |To transfer pollen between flowers of different plants, especially of different varieties or species, resulting in cross-fertilization.| The bees inter-pollinate the apple trees, ensuring a diverse and healthy crop. 蜜蜂给苹果树进行异花授粉，确保作物多样化和健康。 |近义词：cross-pollinate|反义词：self-pollinate
