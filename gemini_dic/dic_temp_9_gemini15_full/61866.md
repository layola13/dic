emporium:/ɛmˈpɔːriəm/| n.|大型商店；百货商店；专卖店 |A large retail store selling a wide variety of goods.  |例句：The city's emporium offered everything from clothing to kitchenware.  这个城市的百货商店出售从服装到厨具的一切商品。
|例句：He owns a small emporium specializing in antique maps. 他拥有一家专门经营古地图的小型专卖店。|近义词：store, shop, department store, bazaar |反义词：small shop, stall |名词
