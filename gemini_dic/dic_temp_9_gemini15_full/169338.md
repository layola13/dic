prompt:/prɒmpt/ /prɑːmpt/| pron. n. adj. v.|提示；促使；敏捷的；（戏剧）提词；|英文解析：A prompt is something that encourages someone to speak or act, a cue given to an actor, or a quick and efficient response. |例句：The actor forgot his lines and needed a prompt from the stage manager.  演员忘了台词，需要舞台经理提示。

The teacher gave the students a prompt to start their creative writing. 老师给学生们一个提示来开始他们的创意写作。


近义词：cue, suggestion, stimulus, reminder |反义词：delay, hinder, obstruction
