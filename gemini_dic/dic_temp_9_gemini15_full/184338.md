respectiveness:/rɪˈspɛktɪvnəs/| n. | 尊敬；尊重；适当的考虑 | The quality or state of being respective; due regard for the feelings, wishes, rights, or property of others.  It often implies a proper consideration of individual differences or positions. |  例句：The respectiveness shown by the students to their teachers was commendable. 学生对老师表现出的尊重值得称赞。

例句：The committee discussed the respectiveness of each proposal before making a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前讨论了每个提案的合理性。


近义词：respect, consideration, regard, deference | 反义词：disrespect, disregard, contempt, insolence | 词性:名词
