extinction:/ɪkˈstɪŋkʃən/ | n. |灭绝；消亡；熄灭 |The complete disappearance of a species from Earth.  Extinction can be caused by various factors, including habitat loss, climate change, and human activities.  |The extinction of the dinosaurs is a well-known event. 恐龙的灭绝是一个众所周知的事件。 The extinction of the fire was a relief. 火熄灭了是一种解脱。|  extinguishing, disappearance, annihilation | survival, existence, continuation | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The extinction of many species is a serious problem. = 许多物种的灭绝是一个严重的问题。
Habitat loss contributes to the extinction of animals. = 栖息地丧失导致动物灭绝。


一般过去时:
The dodo bird went extinct centuries ago. = 渡渡鸟几个世纪前就灭绝了。
The passenger pigeon suffered extinction in the early 20th century. = 旅鸽在20世纪初灭绝了。


现在完成时:
Several plant species have gone extinct in recent years. = 近年来已经有几种植物灭绝了。
Scientists have documented the extinction of numerous insect species. = 科学家已经记录了许多昆虫物种的灭绝。


过去完成时:
Before the arrival of humans, many megafauna had already gone extinct. = 在人类到来之前，许多巨型动物就已经灭绝了。
By the time the conservation efforts began, many species had already reached extinction. = 当保护工作开始时，许多物种已经灭绝了。


将来时:
Without intervention, many more species will face extinction. = 如果不采取干预措施，许多物种将面临灭绝。
Climate change could lead to the extinction of countless species in the future. = 气候变化可能会导致未来无数物种的灭绝。


虚拟语气:
If we continue to destroy habitats, many animals will face extinction. = 如果我们继续破坏栖息地，许多动物将面临灭绝。
It is feared that without conservation efforts, the panda would face extinction. = 人们担心，如果没有保护工作，熊猫将面临灭绝。


被动语态:
Many species are threatened with extinction due to human activities. = 许多物种由于人类活动而面临灭绝的威胁。
The extinction of the woolly mammoth was likely caused by climate change. = 长毛猛犸象的灭绝很可能是由气候变化引起的。


疑问句:
What caused the extinction of the Neanderthals? = 尼安德特人的灭绝是什么原因造成的？
Is the current rate of extinction concerning? = 目前的灭绝速度令人担忧吗？


否定句:
The species is not yet extinct, but its numbers are dwindling. = 该物种尚未灭绝，但数量正在减少。
Not all species that are endangered will go extinct. = 并非所有濒危物种都会灭绝。
