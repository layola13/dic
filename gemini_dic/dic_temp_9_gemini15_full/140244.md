offloads:/ˈɒfˈləʊdz/| /ˈɔːfˈloʊdz/| vt.|卸载，卸货，转移(负担)|to remove a load from; unload|例句：The truck offloads its cargo at the warehouse. 卡车在仓库卸货。

The company offloaded its debt by selling assets. 公司通过出售资产来转移债务。


近义词：unload, transfer, shift|反义词：load, burden
