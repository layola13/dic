facetiousnesses:/fəˈsiːʃəsnəsiz/| n.|滑稽可笑的行为；诙谐的言语|The plural form of "facetiousness," referring to instances of being facetious (playfully jocular; humorous).  It implies a series of lighthearted, often slightly inappropriate or sarcastic remarks or actions. |例句：His facetiousnesses, while amusing at first, eventually wore thin on the audience.  他的滑稽行为，虽然一开始很有趣，但最终让观众感到厌烦。|近义词：jocularities, quips, witticisms, pleasantries|反义词：seriousness, gravity, solemnity
