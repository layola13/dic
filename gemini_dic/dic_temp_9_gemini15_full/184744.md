resultless:/rɪˈzʌltləs/| adj.|无结果的，无效的，徒劳的 | Not producing any result; unsuccessful.  |例句：Their efforts proved resultless. 他们的努力证明是徒劳的。

例句：The search for the missing hiker was resultless.  搜寻失踪的徒步旅行者无果而终。

近义词：unsuccessful, fruitless, vain, unproductive, ineffective

反义词：successful, fruitful, effective, productive
