quickly:/ˈkwɪkli/| adv. |迅速地，快速地 |Quickly, she finished her work. 她迅速地完成了工作。|近义词：rapidly, speedily, swiftly |反义词：slowly, gradually |副词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quickly understands complex ideas. 他很快就能理解复杂的思想。
She quickly responds to emails. 她很快回复邮件。


一般过去时:
He quickly finished his homework. 他迅速完成了家庭作业。
She quickly ran to catch the bus. 她飞快地跑过去赶公共汽车。


现在完成时:
He has quickly learned a lot of new words. 他很快学习了很多新单词。
She has quickly adapted to the new environment. 她很快就适应了新的环境。


过去完成时:
He had quickly packed his bags before leaving. 他在离开前迅速地打包好行李。
She had quickly eaten her lunch before the meeting. 她在会议前迅速地吃完了午饭。


将来时:
He will quickly finish the project. 他将很快完成这个项目。
She will quickly get there by taxi. 她将很快打的到达那里。


虚拟语气:
If he worked quickly, he would finish on time. 如果他工作迅速，他就能按时完成。
If she had acted quickly, the accident could have been avoided. 如果她当时反应迅速，事故本来是可以避免的。


被动语态:
The task was quickly completed. 任务很快就完成了。
The problem was quickly solved. 问题很快得到解决。


疑问句:
Can you quickly finish this task? 你能快速完成这项任务吗？
Did he quickly respond to your email? 他很快回复你的邮件了吗？


否定句:
He didn't quickly respond to my question. 他没有很快回答我的问题。
She didn't quickly understand the instructions. 她没有很快理解指示。


PHRASE->
quickly learn 快速学习
quickly adapt 快速适应
think quickly 快速思考
act quickly 快速行动
get there quickly 快速到达

词源:
quickly源自古英语 *quiclice,  由 *quic "活泼的，敏捷的"  +  -lice (副词后缀)构成。  "quic"  与现代英语的 "quick"  同源。
