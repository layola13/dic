limey:/ˈlaɪmi/| n. adj.|英国人；英国佬；（俚语，贬义）|A person from Great Britain, especially England. Often used in a derogatory way.  The term's origins relate to the British navy's consumption of limes to prevent scurvy.|例句：He referred to the British soldiers as "limeys." 他称英国士兵为“英国佬”。|近义词：Brit, Englishman (less derogatory)|反义词：None specifically, but the connotation is opposite to terms of endearment for British people.
