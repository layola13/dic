microwaving: /ˈmaɪkroʊweɪvɪŋ/ | v. | 微波加热 | The process of cooking or heating food using a microwave oven.  | 例句：Microwaving leftovers is a quick way to have dinner.  微波加热剩菜是快速吃晚饭的一种方法。

例句：I prefer microwaving my lunch to packing a cold one. 我更喜欢用微波炉加热我的午餐而不是带冷的午餐。


近义词：heating, warming (in a microwave) | 反义词：baking, grilling, frying

词源:  The word "microwaving" is derived from "microwave," a combination of "micro" (meaning small) and "wave" (referring to electromagnetic waves).  The "-ing" suffix indicates the gerund or present participle form, denoting the action of using a microwave oven.
