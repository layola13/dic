resaddling:/ˌriːˈsædliŋ/| v.|重新上鞍；重新装备|The act of putting a saddle back on a horse or other animal, or of fitting something with a saddle again.  It can also refer to equipping or refitting something, metaphorically.|例句：After the fall, the rider carefully resaddled his horse.  = 摔倒后，骑手小心地重新给他的马上了鞍。

例句：The company is resaddling its marketing strategy for the new product launch. = 公司正在为新产品的发布重新调整其营销策略。

近义词：remount, refit, re-equip

反义词：unsaddling, dismounting
