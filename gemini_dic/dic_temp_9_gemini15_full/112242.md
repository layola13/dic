loot:/luːt/| n., vt.|战利品；抢劫；掠夺|Stolen goods or property; to steal goods from a place, especially during a war or riot.|例句：The soldiers looted the village after the battle.  士兵在战斗后洗劫了村庄。|例句：The thieves looted the bank and escaped with a large sum of money. 小偷洗劫了银行，带着一大笔钱逃跑了。|近义词：plunder, pillage, plunder, spoils, booty|反义词：donate, contribute


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They loot shops every night. = 他们每晚都抢劫商店。
He loots the abandoned houses. = 他洗劫废弃的房屋。


一般过去时:
The Vikings looted the coastal towns. = 维京人洗劫了沿海城镇。
The army looted the city after the siege. = 围城后，军队洗劫了这座城市。


现在完成时:
The rebels have looted several villages. = 反叛军已经洗劫了几个村庄。
They have looted the museum of its most valuable artifacts. = 他们已经洗劫了博物馆最珍贵的文物。


过去完成时:
The bandits had looted the train before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，土匪已经洗劫了火车。
The thieves had looted the store before the alarm sounded. = 警报响起之前，小偷已经洗劫了商店。


将来时:
The gangs will loot the city if the police don't intervene. = 如果警方不干预，帮派将洗劫这座城市。
They will loot the warehouse tonight. = 他们今晚将洗劫仓库。


虚拟语气:
If they looted the bank, they would be arrested. = 如果他们抢劫银行，他们就会被捕。
I wish they hadn't looted my house. = 我真希望他们没有洗劫我的房子。


被动语态:
The city was looted by the invaders. = 这个城市被入侵者洗劫了。
The museum was looted last night. = 博物馆昨晚被洗劫了。


疑问句:
Was the house looted? = 房子被洗劫了吗？
Did they loot the store? = 他们洗劫了商店吗？


否定句:
They didn't loot the village. = 他们没有洗劫村庄。
The soldiers did not loot the houses. = 士兵们没有洗劫房屋。
