deuteronomy:/djuːtəˈnɒməni/| n. |申命记|The fifth book of the Hebrew Bible, also known as the Book of Deuteronomy.  It recounts Moses's speeches to the Israelites before they entered the Promised Land, reiterating the Law and emphasizing obedience.  |The study of Deuteronomy revealed a deeper understanding of the covenant between God and his people.|近义词：Pentateuch, fifth book of Moses|反义词：无
