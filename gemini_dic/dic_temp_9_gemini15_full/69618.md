It encompasses various aspects, including physical attributes, behavioral traits, and social roles traditionally considered feminine.  The concept is complex and varies across cultures and time periods.|例句：Her dress and demeanor exuded femininity. = 她的穿着和举止都散发着女性气质。

例句：The artist captured the subtle femininity of the model's face. =艺术家捕捉到了模特脸上细微的女性气质。
