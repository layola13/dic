fairylike:/ˈfɛəriˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像仙女的；优美的；精致的 | Resembling a fairy; delicate and charming.  The word evokes a sense of ethereal beauty, grace, and often a magical quality. |例句：The ballerina’s movements were fairylike and graceful.  芭蕾舞演员的舞姿轻盈优美。 The garden was fairylike, with flowers of every color imaginable. 花园美如仙境，五彩缤纷的花朵应有尽有。|近义词：ethereal, delicate, dainty, graceful, magical |反义词：coarse, rough, clumsy, ungraceful
