convey:/kənˈveɪ/| vt.| 传达，运送，表达 | To communicate or make known (information, ideas, or feelings).  To transport or carry something from one place to another. | 例句：He conveyed his message clearly. 他清楚地表达了他的意思。

The truck conveyed the goods to the warehouse. 卡车把货物运送到仓库。|近义词：communicate, transmit, transport, carry |反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conveys his ideas effectively. = 他有效地表达了他的想法。
The bus conveys passengers to the city center. = 公共汽车把乘客运送到市中心。


一般过去时:
She conveyed her condolences to the family. = 她向家人表达了慰问。
The train conveyed the tourists to the mountain resort. = 火车把游客运送到山区度假村。


现在完成时:
They have conveyed their concerns to the management. = 他们已经向管理层表达了他们的担忧。
The company has conveyed all the necessary documents. = 公司已经运送了所有必要的文档。


过去完成时:
He had conveyed the news before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经传达了这个消息。
The messenger had conveyed the package before the storm hit. = 信使在暴风雨来临之前已经送达了包裹。


将来时:
She will convey the information to her team. = 她将把信息传达给她的团队。
The ship will convey the cargo across the ocean. = 船将把货物运过海洋。


虚拟语气:
If I could convey my feelings, it would be easier. = 如果我能表达我的感受，那就容易多了。
I wish I could convey the beauty of the scene. = 我希望我能表达出这场景的美丽。


被动语态:
The message was conveyed clearly. = 信息表达得很清楚。
The goods were conveyed safely to their destination. = 货物安全地运送到了目的地。


疑问句:
How did you convey your ideas? = 你是如何表达你的想法的？
How will the supplies be conveyed to the remote village? = 如何将物资运送到偏远的村庄？


否定句:
He failed to convey his meaning properly. = 他没能恰当地表达他的意思。
The company didn't convey the packages on time. = 公司没有按时运送包裹。


PHRASE->
convey a message = 传递信息
convey one's feelings = 表达感情
convey an impression = 给人留下印象
convey meaning = 传达意义
convey a sense of = 传达一种…的感觉
