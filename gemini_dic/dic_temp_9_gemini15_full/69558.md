fellowshiping: /ˈfɛloʊʃɪpɪŋ/ | v. | 参与；联谊；共享；结为伙伴 | Engaging in fellowship; sharing in a common activity or purpose; associating with others in a friendly or supportive way. |  例句：They were fellowshiping together after the service. = 他们礼拜结束后在一起联谊。

例句：The students are fellowshiping in a study group. = 学生们正在学习小组中互相帮助。


近义词：associating, communing, socializing, fraternizing | 反义词：isolating, segregating, alienating | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are fellowshiping at the church. = 他们在教堂里参与联谊活动。
We fellowship every Sunday. = 我们每个星期天都一起联谊。


一般过去时:
They fellowshipped together after the game. = 比赛结束后他们一起联谊。
We fellowshipped with the new members. = 我们和新成员们一起联谊。


现在完成时:
They have been fellowshiping for many years. = 他们已经联谊多年了。
We have fellowshipped with this group since college. = 从大学以来我们就和这个团体一起联谊。


过去完成时:
They had fellowshipped for years before the split. = 在分裂之前他们已经联谊多年了。
We had fellowshipped with them before moving to a new city. = 在搬到新城市之前，我们已经和他们联谊过。


将来时:
They will fellowship at the conference. = 他们将在会议上参与联谊。
We will fellowship with our friends next weekend. = 我们下周末将和朋友们一起联谊。


虚拟语气:
If they fellowshipped more, they would be happier. = 如果他们多参与联谊，他们会更快乐。
I wish we had fellowshipped more when we were younger. = 我希望我们年轻的时候能多参与一些联谊。


被动语态:  (此词很少用被动语态)
The new members were welcomed and fellowshipped with. = 新成员受到了欢迎并参与了联谊活动。(被动语态较为生硬)
The group is known for being welcoming and fellowshiping new members. = 这个团体以其欢迎和接纳新成员而闻名。


疑问句:
Are they fellowshiping tonight? = 他们今晚要联谊吗？
Will you be fellowshiping with us? = 你会和我们一起联谊吗？


否定句:
They did not fellowship with the opposing team. = 他们没有和对方球队联谊。
We don't fellowship with people who are disrespectful. = 我们不会和不尊重人的人联谊。


PHRASE->
Fellowship with: 与…交往/联谊
Fellowship in: 在…中参与联谊
Fellowship meeting: 联谊会
Fellowship hall: 联谊厅
Fellowship group: 联谊小组


词源:  The word "fellowshiping" is derived from the noun "fellowship," which ultimately comes from the Old English "feolahlǣp," meaning "companionship, association."  The "-ing" suffix turns the noun into a gerund, functioning as a verb.
