satchel:/ˈsætʃəl/| n.|小提包，书包|A satchel is a small bag, typically made of leather, used for carrying books or other personal items.  It often has a strap for carrying it over the shoulder.|例句：She carried her books in a brown leather satchel. 她把书放在一个棕色的皮革小提包里。

例句：The student carried a heavy satchel to school. 学生背着一个沉重的书包去上学。


近义词：bag, knapsack, backpack|反义词:None|词性:名词
