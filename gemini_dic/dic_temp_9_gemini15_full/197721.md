shiftier:/ˈʃɪftər/| adj.|狡猾的；变化迅速的；骗人的| characterized by deviousness; given to using tricks or deception; evasive; tricky; unreliable;  changeable |例句：He's a shifty character, always looking for an angle. 他是个狡猾的角色，总是在寻找机会。

He gave a shifty answer, avoiding the direct question. 他给了一个闪烁其词的答案，回避了直接的问题。


近义词：crafty, sly, tricky, evasive, deceitful, dishonest|反义词：honest, truthful, straightforward, candid, frank|词性：形容词
