self-reform:/ˌsɛlfˈrɪfɔːrm/| n. v.|自我改革；自我改进 |The act or process of reforming oneself; improvement of one's character, behavior, or habits.  |例句：The company underwent a period of self-reform after the scandal.  公司在丑闻后经历了一段自我改革时期。|例句：He committed to self-reform and overcame his addiction. 他致力于自我改革并克服了他的瘾。|近义词：self-improvement, self-correction, rehabilitation |反义词：self-destruction, stagnation, deterioration |名词/动词
