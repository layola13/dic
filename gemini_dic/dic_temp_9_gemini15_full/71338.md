flabellate:/ˈflæbəleɪt/| adj.|扇形的；扇状的|Having the shape of a fan; fan-shaped.  The term often refers to structures in biology, particularly in botany and zoology.|例句：The flower has flabelliform petals, creating a beautiful fan-like appearance. 这朵花的花瓣呈扇形， tạo ra vẻ ngoài giống như chiếc quạt rất đẹp。|近义词：fan-shaped, flabellate, flabelliform|反义词：linear, acicular
