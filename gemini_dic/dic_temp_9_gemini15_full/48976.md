degenerative:/dɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv/ | adj. | 退化的；变性的；衰退的 |  Describing a process of deterioration or decline in function or structure, often associated with age or disease.  It implies a gradual worsening of condition. |

例句：

The disease is a degenerative condition affecting the nervous system.  = 这是一种影响神经系统的退行性疾病。

He suffers from a degenerative joint disease. = 他患有退行性关节炎。


近义词：destructive, deteriorating, debilitating, worsening

反义词：regenerative, restorative, improving, progressive (in a positive sense)

词性：形容词
