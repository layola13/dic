enlist:/ɪnˈlɪst/ | vt. |招募；征募；争取；获得…的帮助 | To enroll (someone) in the armed forces; to obtain (someone's) help or support. |例句：The army is trying to enlist more soldiers.  军队正在努力招募更多士兵。

例句：We enlisted the help of a local guide. 我们寻求了一位当地导游的帮助。


近义词：recruit, enroll, engage, secure | 反义词：discharge, dismiss


词源:  From Old French *enlister  "to enroll," from en- "in" + lister "to list, enroll," from Latin *listare "to mark with a list," from littera "letter."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enlists in the army every year. 他每年都应征入伍。
They enlist the support of their friends. 他们争取朋友的支持。


一般过去时:
He enlisted in the navy after graduating from college. 他大学毕业后参军了。
She enlisted the help of her neighbors to move the furniture. 她请邻居帮忙搬家具。


现在完成时:
He has enlisted in the air force for five years. 他已在空军服役五年了。
She has enlisted the aid of several experts. 她已获得了几位专家的帮助。


过去完成时:
They had enlisted the services of a professional photographer. 他们此前已聘请了一位专业摄影师。
Before the project started, he had already enlisted the support of his colleagues. 在项目开始之前，他已经争取到了同事的支持。


将来时:
He will enlist in the marines next year. 他明年将加入海军陆战队。
They will enlist the help of a consultant. 他们将寻求一位顾问的帮助。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would enlist in the army. 如果我是你，我会参军。
I wish I had enlisted in the army sooner. 我真希望我早点参军。


被动语态:
Many young men were enlisted during the war. 战争期间，许多年轻男子被征召入伍。
The help of the community was enlisted to restore the park.  社区的帮助被争取来修复公园。


疑问句:
Will you enlist in the army? 你会参军吗？
Did they enlist the help of a lawyer? 他们寻求了律师的帮助吗？


否定句:
He did not enlist in the army. 他没有参军。
They did not enlist the services of a professional. 他们没有聘请专业人士。
