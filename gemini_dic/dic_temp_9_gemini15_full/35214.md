clucks:/klʌks/| v. n.|咯咯叫；啧啧声；唠叨|To make the characteristic sound of a hen.  Also, to express disapproval or impatience with a clicking sound.|The hen clucked at her chicks. 母鸡对着小鸡咯咯叫。|The teacher clucked her tongue at the noisy students. 老师对吵闹的学生啧啧舌。|近义词：cackle, chatter, scold|反义词：be silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hen clucks all day long. = 母鸡整天咯咯叫。
He often clucks his tongue in disapproval. = 他经常啧啧舌表示不满。


一般过去时:
The hen clucked loudly when she laid an egg. = 母鸡下蛋时大声咯咯叫。
She clucked her tongue at the messy room. = 她对凌乱的房间啧啧舌。


现在完成时:
The hen has clucked incessantly since morning. = 母鸡从早上就开始不停地咯咯叫。
The teacher has clucked at him several times today. = 老师今天已经对他啧啧了几次了。


过去完成时:
The hen had already clucked several times before I even got near the coop. = 我还没靠近鸡舍，母鸡就已经咯咯叫了好几声了。
She had clucked her disapproval before he even finished speaking. = 他话还没说完，她就啧啧表示不满。


将来时:
The hen will cluck when she sees her chicks. = 母鸡看到小鸡就会咯咯叫。
I'm sure she will cluck at your behavior. = 我肯定她会对你的行为啧啧不满。


虚拟语气:
If the hen was happy, she would cluck softly. = 如果母鸡高兴，它会轻轻地咯咯叫。
If I had known better, I wouldn't have clucked at him. = 如果我更明智些，我就不会对他啧啧不满。


被动语态:
Her clucking was heard throughout the farm. = 她的咯咯叫声传遍了整个农场。(很少用被动语态)
His disapproval was often expressed by clucking his tongue. = 他常常通过啧啧舌来表达不满。


疑问句:
Does the hen cluck often? = 母鸡经常咯咯叫吗？
Did she cluck at you? = 她对你啧啧不满吗？


否定句:
The hen didn't cluck today. = 母鸡今天没有咯咯叫。
He didn't cluck his tongue, but his face showed his displeasure. = 他没有啧啧舌，但他的脸色显示了他的不满。


PHRASE->
cluck at someone = 对某人啧啧不满
cluck disapproval = 表达不满

clucking sound = 咯咯声
