flagstones:/ˈflæɡˌstoʊnz/| n.|铺路石，旗石|Flat stones, often rectangular, used for paving.  They are typically made of durable materials like sandstone or slate. |例句：The garden path was made of smooth, grey flagstones. 花园小路是用光滑的灰色铺路石铺成的。

例句：We replaced the cracked flagstones in the patio. 我们更换了露台上的裂缝铺路石。


近义词：paving stones, stepping stones|反义词：None specifically, but words describing loose or soft ground could be considered antonyms in context. |词性:名词

词源:
The word "flagstone" comes from the Old English word "flægestān," which is a compound of "flæge" (meaning "flag" or "flat stone") and "stān" (meaning "stone").  The original meaning referred to a flat, broad stone, often used for paving.  The modern usage retains this core meaning.
