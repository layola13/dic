monsoonish: /mənˈsuːnɪʃ/ | adj. | 季风似的；潮湿闷热的 | Resembling or characteristic of a monsoon; hot, humid, and rainy.  The word evokes the feeling of a monsoon season, including its characteristic oppressive heat and humidity, often associated with heavy rainfall and stagnant air. |  The monsoonish weather made it difficult to enjoy the outdoor activities.  季风般的天气使得人们难以享受户外活动。The air hung heavy and monsoonish, thick with the scent of rain-soaked earth. 空气沉闷而潮湿，弥漫着雨水浸湿泥土的气味。 | humid, muggy, sultry | dry, arid
