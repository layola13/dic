self-bred:/ˈsɛlfˌbrɛd/| adj. | 自家繁殖的，自养的 |  Bred or raised by oneself; produced or grown by oneself, without outside assistance.  | Example:  The farmer boasted about his self-bred pigs, raised entirely on his own farm.  这家农民对自家饲养的猪很自豪，这些猪完全是在他自己的农场里养大的。 |近义词：homebred, homegrown |反义词：store-bought, commercially bred
