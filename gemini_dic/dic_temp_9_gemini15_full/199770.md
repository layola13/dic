sirrah: /ˈsɪrə/ | n. | 伙计，小子（旧用法，常带轻蔑） | A term of address to a man, especially one of low social standing; often used contemptuously.  | Example: "Sirrah, you'll pay for this!"  小子，你得为这事付出代价！ | 近义词：fellow, chap, lad (but these lack the contemptuous element) | 反义词：master, sir (in terms of social standing and respect) | 名词
