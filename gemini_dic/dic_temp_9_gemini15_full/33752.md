circularise:/ˈsɜːrkjʊləraɪz/ | vt. | 通知；通告；散发传单 | To send a circular or other communication to a number of people.  | The company circularised its shareholders about the upcoming merger.  公司向其股东通告了即将到来的合并事宜。 | disseminate, distribute, notify |  | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They circularise their new products to potential customers every month. = 他们每个月都会向潜在客户宣传他们的新产品。
The school circularises important information to parents regularly. = 学校定期向家长发布重要信息。

一般过去时:
The government circularised the new regulations to all relevant departments. = 政府向所有相关部门发布了新的规章制度。
The company circularised a memo to all employees about the upcoming holiday. = 公司向所有员工发布了一份关于即将到来的假期的备忘录。


现在完成时:
The organization has circularised its annual report to all its members. = 该组织已向所有成员分发了年度报告。
They have circularised the details of the event to all invited guests. = 他们已经将活动的详细信息发送给了所有受邀嘉宾。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, the chairman had circularised the agenda to all attendees. = 会议之前，主席已将议程分发给了所有与会者。
The team had circularised the proposal to the stakeholders before the final decision. = 在做出最终决定之前，团队已将提案分发给了利益相关者。


将来时:
The company will circularise the updated policy to all staff next week. = 公司下周将向所有员工发布更新后的政策。
The school will circularise the exam timetable to students before the exams begin. = 学校将在考试开始前向学生发布考试时间表。


虚拟语气:
If the company circularised its plans earlier, it might have avoided some misunderstandings. = 如果公司早点公布其计划，可能会避免一些误解。
It would be helpful if they circularised this information more widely. = 如果他们更广泛地传播此信息，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The new guidelines were circularised to all employees last month. = 新指南上个月已分发给所有员工。
The information will be circularised to the relevant parties as soon as possible. = 信息将尽快发送给相关方。


疑问句:
Has the company circularised the new pricing structure yet? = 公司是否已经发布了新的定价结构？
Will the details of the changes be circularised to all staff? = 这些变化的细节会发送给所有员工吗？


否定句:
The company did not circularise the information to all its branches. = 公司没有向所有分支机构发布该信息。
They haven't circularised the updated terms and conditions yet. = 他们还没有发布更新后的条款和条件。
