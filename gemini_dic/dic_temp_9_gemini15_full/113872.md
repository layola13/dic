madly:/ˈmædli/ | adv. | 狂乱地；疯狂地；极度地 |  In a mad or furious manner; intensely.  It describes an action or state performed or existing with great intensity, often in a chaotic or uncontrolled way.  | 例句：He laughed madly. 他疯狂地大笑。  She loved him madly. 她疯狂地爱着他。 | 近义词：frenziedly, wildly, frantically, intensely | 反义词：calmly, placidly, serenely
