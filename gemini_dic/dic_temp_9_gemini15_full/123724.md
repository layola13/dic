moonrise:/ˈmuːnraɪz/ | n. | 月出，月亮升起 | The appearance of the moon above the horizon.  It is a beautiful natural phenomenon often captured in photography and painting. | Example: The moonrise cast a silvery glow over the landscape. 月出给风景披上了一层银色的光辉。
The moonrise was spectacular, painting the sky with vibrant hues. 月出景象壮观，给天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。 | 近义词：sunrise, dawning | 反义词：moonset
