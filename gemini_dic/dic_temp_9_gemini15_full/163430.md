preconsumer: /ˌpriːkənˈsjuːmər/ | adj. | 预消费的；生产过程中的 | Relating to waste materials that are discarded before reaching the consumer.  These materials are typically generated during the manufacturing process.  | Example:  The company implemented a preconsumer recycling program to reduce waste. 这家公司实施了预消费回收计划以减少浪费。 | 近义词：manufacturing waste, industrial waste | 反义词：postconsumer
