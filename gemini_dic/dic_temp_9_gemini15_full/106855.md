labouring: /ˈleɪbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 辛勤工作的；努力的；费力的 |  Describes something involving hard work, effort, or difficulty.  It often implies a continuous and sometimes strenuous effort. | 例句：He was labouring under a heavy burden. 他在重负下辛勤工作。

He gave a labouring explanation of the complex theory. 他费力地解释了这个复杂的理论。


近义词：toiling, struggling, striving, strenuous, arduous | 反义词：easy, effortless, simple, relaxed


词源:  From the Old French *labourer, from Latin *laborare "to work."  The suffix "-ing" forms the present participle, functioning here as an adjective.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer is labouring in the fields. = 农民正在田里辛勤劳作。
She is labouring to finish her report before the deadline. = 她正在努力赶在截止日期前完成报告。


一般过去时:
The workers laboured tirelessly all day. = 工人们一整天不知疲倦地工作。
He laboured to understand the difficult poem. = 他努力理解那首难懂的诗歌。


现在完成时:
They have been labouring for years to achieve this goal. = 他们为了实现这个目标已经努力多年了。
She has laboured over this project for months. = 她已经在这个项目上辛勤工作了好几个月。


过去完成时:
He had laboured for many hours before he finally completed the task. = 他辛勤工作了好几个小时才最终完成了任务。
They had laboured under the misconception that the project was easy. = 他们一直误以为这个项目很容易，所以努力工作。


将来时:
We will be labouring to meet the new requirements. = 我们将努力满足新的要求。
He will labour to improve his skills. = 他将努力提高自己的技能。


虚拟语气:
If he laboured harder, he would succeed. = 如果他更努力工作，他就会成功。
I wish I hadn't laboured so much on that project. = 我真希望我没有在这个项目上花那么多力气。


被动语态:
The land was laboured by generations of farmers. = 这块土地经过几代农民的辛勤耕耘。
The point was laboured in the speech.  = 演讲中对这一点进行了反复强调（费力解释）。


疑问句:
Is he labouring under any misconceptions? = 他有什么误解吗？
Are you labouring under a false impression? = 你是不是有什么错误的印象？


否定句:
He didn't labour over the details. = 他没有在细节上过分纠结。
She didn't labour the point unnecessarily. = 她没有不必要地强调那一点。
