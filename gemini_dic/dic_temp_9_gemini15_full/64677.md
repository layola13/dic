escambio: /eˈskæmbɪoʊ/ | n. | 交换，交易 | Exchange, barter, trade.  It refers to the act of giving something in return for something else; a reciprocal exchange of goods, services, or information. | 例句：The two countries engaged in an intercambio of cultural artifacts. = 这两个国家进行了文化文物的交换。


例句：They reached an intercambio on the terms of the contract. = 他们在合同条款上达成了协议。(交换意见达成协议)

近义词：exchange, barter, trade, swap

反义词：retention, keeping


词性：名词
