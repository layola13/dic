infirmity:/ɪnˈfɜːrməti/| n.|虚弱；疾病；缺陷 | A weakness or ailment, physical or mental.  It can refer to a physical disability, a chronic illness, or a moral failing. |例句：His infirmity prevented him from participating in the marathon. 他的虚弱使他无法参加马拉松比赛。

例句：Age brings with it various infirmities. 年龄带来了各种各样的疾病。

近义词：weakness, ailment, disability, defect, flaw

反义词：strength, health, robustness

词性：名词
