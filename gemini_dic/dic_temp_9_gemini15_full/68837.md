fascitis:/ˌfæʃiˈaɪtɪs/| n. | 筋膜炎 | Inflammation of the fascia, the fibrous connective tissue that surrounds muscles and other organs.  Fasciitis can occur in various parts of the body, most commonly in the plantar fascia of the foot (plantar fasciitis).| 例句：He suffers from plantar fascitis, making it painful to walk.|近义词：fascial inflammation|反义词：无
