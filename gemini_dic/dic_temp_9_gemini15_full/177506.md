reblistering:/ˌriːˈblɪstərɪŋ/ | v. | 再次起泡；重新起泡 |  The process of blisters forming again after having been previously treated or healed.  This term often refers to skin conditions or materials that have developed blisters and are experiencing a recurrence of blistering. | Example: The sunburn was reblistering despite the aloe vera treatment.  日晒导致的灼伤尽管涂抹了芦荟胶，但仍在再次起泡。  The paint was reblistering due to moisture damage. 油漆因受潮而再次起泡。 | 近义词：re-bubbling, re-vesicating | 反义词：healing, smoothing
