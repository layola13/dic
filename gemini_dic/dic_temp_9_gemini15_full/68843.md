fashion-forward:/ˈfæʃən ˈfɔːrwərd/| adj. |时尚前卫的，走在时尚前沿的|  Describes someone or something that is ahead of current trends in fashion, adopting new styles before they become mainstream.  It implies a willingness to experiment with and embrace innovative and often daring fashion choices. | Her fashion-forward designs always turn heads at fashion week. 她的时尚前卫设计总是在时装周上引人注目。  The boutique is known for its fashion-forward clothing. 这家精品店以其时尚前卫的服装而闻名。| stylish, trendy, avant-garde, cutting-edge | outdated, old-fashioned, traditional, behind the times | 形容词
