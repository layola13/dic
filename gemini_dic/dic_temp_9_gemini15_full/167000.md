presubordinates: /ˌpriːˈsʌbɔːrdɪnɪts/ | n. | 下级；下属 |  A presubordinate is a person who is subordinate to someone who is themselves a subordinate.  This term is less common than simply "subordinate" and often implies a hierarchical structure with multiple layers of authority.  | Example:  The presubordinates were responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of the various departments.  下级负责确保各部门的顺利运作。 | 近义词：subordinate, underling | 反义词：superior, boss | 名词
