cobblers:/ˈkɒbləz/| n.|修鞋匠；笨拙的人；乱七八糟的东西；水果馅饼|a person who makes or repairs shoes; a clumsy or incompetent person; something clumsy or badly made; a fruit dessert with a top crust.|例句：He's a cobbler by trade. 他是修鞋匠。  The whole project was a cobbler of ideas.  整个项目是一堆乱七八糟的想法。|近义词：shoemaker, repairman, botch, mess|反义词：expert, professional, masterpiece
