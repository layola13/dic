hammered:/ˈhæmɚd/ | /ˈhæməd/ | adj. v. |  锤打的；喝醉的；猛击的；彻底击败的 |  Describes something that has been hit repeatedly with a hammer, or someone who is very drunk, or something that has been severely damaged or defeated. |

例句：

The metal was hammered into shape. = 金属被锤打成型。

He was hammered after drinking too much beer. = 他喝太多啤酒后醉得不省人事。


近义词：

(adj.)  intoxicated, drunk, smashed,  beaten, defeated, devastated

(v.)  struck, pounded, battered, thrashed


反义词：

(adj.) sober,  victorious, undefeated, undamaged


词性：

adj.  (形容词)
v. (动词)


USAGE->
