jitteriness:/ˈdʒɪtəriˌnɛs/ | n. | 紧张不安；颤抖；不稳定 | A state of nervousness or anxiety, often manifesting as trembling or shakiness.  It can also refer to instability or erratic behavior. |例句：Her jitteriness was evident in her trembling hands.  她的双手颤抖，显示出她的紧张不安。

例句：The jitteriness of the camera made the footage unusable.  摄像机的抖动使画面无法使用。


近义词：nervousness, anxiety, tremor, instability, shakiness, unsteadiness

反义词：calmness, composure, steadiness, stability


词源:  Derived from the word "jitter," meaning to move rapidly and irregularly, or to be nervous or agitated.  The suffix "-iness" adds the sense of a state or condition.  Ultimately, it's related to the Old English word "gītan," meaning "to shake" or "to tremble."
