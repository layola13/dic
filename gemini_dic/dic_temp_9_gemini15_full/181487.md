rejuggling:/ˌriːˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ/| v.|重新调整；重新安排；反复权衡|The act of juggling again, often implying a readjustment or re-evaluation of previously juggled items or tasks.  It suggests a process of reconsidering and reorganizing elements.|例句：After the unexpected cancellation, she had to rejuggle her work schedule.  重新安排工作计划。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, reconsider, re-evaluate|反义词：finalize, settle
