secretariate:/ˌsɛkrəˈtɛəriət/| n. |秘书处；办事处；秘书机构 | A secretariate is a group of people who work together as the administrative staff of an organization, especially a government department or international organization.  It often involves managing correspondence, scheduling meetings, and handling administrative tasks. |The UN secretariate is responsible for organizing the General Assembly. 联合国秘书处负责组织大会。| office, bureau, administration | chaos, disorder | 名词
