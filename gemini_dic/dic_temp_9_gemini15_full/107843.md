lardass: /ˈlɑːrdæs/ | n. |肥猪；懒汉；笨蛋 | A lardass is a derogatory term for a fat, lazy, or stupid person.  It combines "lard," referring to rendered pig fat, implying excessive weight and sluggishness, with "ass," a vulgar term for a foolish or contemptible person.  The word is highly offensive and should be avoided. | He called his opponent a lardass during the debate. 他在辩论中称他的对手为肥猪。 |近义词：fatso, slob, oaf, dolt | 反义词：slim, athletic, intelligent | 名词
