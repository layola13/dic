immaculately:/ɪˈmækjʊlətli/ | adj. | 完美地；无可挑剔地；纯洁地 |  Perfectly clean, neat, or tidy; free from fault or flaw.  |例句：The house was immaculately clean.  = 房子打扫得干干净净。

例句：Her performance was immaculately executed. = 她的表演完美无瑕。


近义词：perfectly, flawlessly, faultlessly, impeccably, flawlessly, pristine


反义词：messy, dirty, flawed, imperfect, stained


词性：形容词
