mansard:/ˈmæn.zɑːrd/| n. | mansard屋顶；法式屋顶 | A mansard roof is a roof with four sloping sides, each side having two slopes, the lower slope being steeper than the upper one.  This design maximizes usable attic space. | The house had a distinctive mansard roof. 这栋房子有一个独特的 mansard 屋顶。|gable roof, gambrel roof|flat roof|名词
