multicorporations:/ˌmʌltiˈkɔːrpəreɪʃənz/ | n. |跨国公司，多国公司 |  A multicorporation is a large corporation that operates in multiple countries.  It typically has a complex organizational structure and engages in international trade and investment. | Example: Many multicorporations are headquartered in developed countries but have manufacturing facilities in developing nations.  许多跨国公司的总部设在发达国家，但在发展中国家设有制造工厂。|近义词：multinational corporations, transnational corporations | 反义词：small business, local business | 名词
