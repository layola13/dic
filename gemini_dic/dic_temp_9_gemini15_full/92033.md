hyperpatriotism:/ˌhaɪpərˈpeɪtriəˌtɪzəm/| n. |极端爱国主义|Excessive or fanatical patriotism;  an extreme form of patriotism that often involves aggressive nationalism and intolerance of dissenting opinions.  It can manifest in actions that harm national interests, and is often associated with xenophobia and the suppression of individual rights.|例句：His hyperpatriotism led him to believe any criticism of his country was treasonous.|近义词：jingoism, chauvinism, nationalism|反义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism
