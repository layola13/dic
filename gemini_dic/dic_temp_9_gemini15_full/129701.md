non-dormant: /ˌnɒnˈdɔːmənt/ | adj. |非休眠的，活跃的 | Not dormant; active or operating.  The term implies a state of activity following a period of dormancy or inactivity.  It often refers to things that are capable of being dormant but are currently not. |


例句：

The volcano, after years of non-dormant activity, is once again showing signs of unrest. = 这座火山在多年活跃之后，再次显示出不安的迹象。

The non-dormant account was used to process transactions and update records. = 非休眠帐户用于处理交易和更新记录。


近义词：active, functioning, operating, lively


反义词：dormant, inactive, quiescent, sleeping


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not".
* **dormant**:  From Old French *dormant*, from Latin *dormiens*, present participle of *dormire* "to sleep".
