interlocutrix:/ˌɪntərˈlɒkjʊtrɪks/ | n. | 对话者（女） | A woman who takes part in a conversation or dialogue.  | Example: The interlocutrix readily agreed to participate in the study.  对谈者欣然同意参与这项研究。 |近义词：conversationalist, speaker, participant | 反义词：listener, audience


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  The usage depends on the context of the conversation.


PHRASE->
Phrases involving "interlocutrix" would describe the role or actions of the female participant in a conversation. For example:

* **The astute interlocutrix:**  敏锐的对话者
* **The hesitant interlocutrix:**  犹豫的对话者
* **The articulate interlocutrix:**  表达清晰的对话者
* **The knowledgeable interlocutrix:**  知识渊博的对话者
* **The engaging interlocutrix:**  引人入胜的对话者


WORD ORIGIN->
The word "interlocutrix" is derived from the Latin word "interlocutor," meaning "one who talks between," which combines "inter" (between) and "loquor" (I speak). The suffix "-trix" is a feminine suffix, indicating the female counterpart.  Therefore, "interlocutrix" specifically refers to a female participant in a conversation.
