non-preventable: /ˌnɒnprɪˈvɛntəbl/ | adj. | 不可避免的，无法预防的 |  Unable to be prevented or avoided.  | 例句：The accident was a non-preventable tragedy. 这场事故是一场不可避免的悲剧。

The economic downturn was a non-preventable consequence of the global pandemic. 全球疫情导致的经济衰退是不可避免的后果。

| 近义词：inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable | 反义词：preventable, avoidable | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disease is non-preventable in some cases. = 在某些情况下，这种疾病是无法预防的。
This outcome is non-preventable given the circumstances. = 鉴于当时的状况，这种结果是不可避免的。

一般过去时:
The damage was non-preventable. = 损失是不可避免的。
The error was non-preventable due to system malfunction. = 由于系统故障，错误是不可避免的。

现在完成时:
Many accidents have been non-preventable despite safety measures. = 尽管采取了安全措施，许多事故仍然是不可避免的。
This situation has proven non-preventable, despite our best efforts. = 尽管我们尽了最大努力，这种情况已被证明是不可避免的。


过去完成时:
The failure had been non-preventable due to previous neglect. = 由于之前的忽视，失败是不可避免的。
It had been non-preventable that the project would be delayed. = 项目的延误是不可避免的。


将来时:
Some complications will be non-preventable. = 一些并发症是不可避免的。
Further damage will be non-preventable if we do nothing. = 如果我们什么都不做，进一步的损害将是不可避免的。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, the damage might have been preventable, but it was non-preventable as things stood. = 如果我们早点采取行动，损失本来是可以避免的，但就目前情况而言，它是不可避免的。
Even with perfect foresight, some risks would still be non-preventable. = 即使有完美的预见性，一些风险仍然是不可避免的。

被动语态:
The loss was considered non-preventable by the experts. = 专家认为损失是不可避免的。
The crisis was deemed non-preventable by the government. = 政府认为这场危机是不可避免的。


疑问句:
Is this outcome truly non-preventable? = 这种结果真的不可避免吗？
Are all risks in this field non-preventable? = 这个领域的所有风险都是不可避免的吗？


否定句:
The problem is not entirely non-preventable; some measures can reduce its occurrence. = 这个问题并非完全不可避免；一些措施可以减少其发生率。
The accident was preventable; it was not non-preventable. = 事故是可以避免的；它不是不可避免的。

词源:
non- (否定前缀) + preventable (可预防的，由prevent派生而来，prevent源于拉丁语praevenire，意为“提前来，阻止”)
