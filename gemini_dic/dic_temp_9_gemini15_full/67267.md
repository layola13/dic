exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/ | adj. | 精美的；精致的；优雅的 | Extremely beautiful and delicate.  |例句：The antique vase was exquisite, a masterpiece of craftsmanship. 那只古董花瓶精美绝伦，是工艺的杰作。

例句：She wore an exquisite gown to the ball. 她穿着一件精致的礼服去参加舞会。


近义词：beautiful, delicate, elegant, superb, refined | 反义词：crude, rough, plain, ugly


词源:  From Old French *exquisit, past participle of *exquisir "to seek out, examine carefully," from Latin exquirere "to search out," from ex- "out, thoroughly" + quaerere "to seek."  The sense shifted to "choice, select" and then to "exquisite."
