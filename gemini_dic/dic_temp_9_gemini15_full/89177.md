homeostases:/ˌhoʊmiˈoʊsteɪsiːz/| n. |稳态(复数)|The maintenance of a stable internal environment within a biological system despite external changes.  Homeostases are crucial for the survival and proper functioning of organisms.|例句：Maintaining proper blood glucose levels is a key homeostases in the human body. 维持适当的血糖水平是人体关键的稳态之一。|例句：The body's homeostases are constantly working to regulate temperature, pH, and other vital factors. 人体的稳态机制不断地调节体温、pH值和其他重要因素。|近义词：equilibrium, balance, stability|反义词：instability, disequilibrium

词源:
The word "homeostases" is the plural form of "homeostasis."  "Homeostasis" is derived from Greek roots: "homoios" (similar, like) and "stasis" (standing still).  Therefore, it literally means "similar condition" or "a state of sameness."
