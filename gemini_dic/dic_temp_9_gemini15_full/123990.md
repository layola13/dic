morne:/mɔːrn/| n.|忧郁的;阴沉的;悲伤的|A morne is a feeling of sadness or depression, often associated with a sense of gloom or darkness. It suggests a deep and lingering sadness, rather than a fleeting moment of unhappiness.|例句：The morne settled over the city after the tragic news.  悲剧的消息之后，一股忧郁笼罩着这座城市。 The morne in his eyes betrayed his inner turmoil. 他眼中的忧郁出卖了他内心的骚动。|近义词：gloomy, melancholy, somber, dismal|反义词：cheerful, joyful, happy, bright|名词
