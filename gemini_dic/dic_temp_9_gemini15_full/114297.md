mainsheet:/ˈmeɪnʃiːt/| n. |主帆索|The main sheet is a rope used to control the angle of the mainsail on a sailboat. It is attached to the boom and runs to a winch or other control point.  The mainsheet's primary function is to adjust the sail's trim (shape and angle) to optimize performance in varying wind conditions.|Example:  The sailor adjusted the mainsheet to catch the wind more effectively. = 水手调整主帆索以更有效地捕捉风。

The skipper eased the mainsheet to prevent the boat from capsizing. = 船长放松主帆索以防止船只倾覆。|近义词：mainsail control, boom control|反义词：N/A
