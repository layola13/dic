circadianly:/sərˈkeɪdiənli/| adv.|  昼夜节律地，按生理节奏地 | In a circadian manner; relating to or occurring in cycles of approximately 24 hours.  |例句：The study examined how circadianly regulated genes influence sleep patterns. 这项研究考察了昼夜节律调控基因如何影响睡眠模式。

例句：Many physiological processes, such as hormone release, operate circadianly.  许多生理过程，例如激素释放，都是昼夜节律性的。


近义词：rhythmically, cyclically, periodically


反义词：irregularly, erratically, randomly

词性：副词
