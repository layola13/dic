nonimposition: /ˌnɒnɪmpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 不强加，不施加，不干涉 | The absence of imposition; freedom from being forced to accept something.  It emphasizes respect for autonomy and individual choice.  | Example: The teacher encouraged nonimposition of her own views on her students. 老师鼓励她自己不强加观点给学生。

The policy of nonimposition allowed for diverse perspectives to be heard.  不干涉政策使得各种不同的观点都能被听到。| 近义词：tolerance, forbearance, respect, autonomy | 反义词：coercion, imposition, force, compulsion | 名词
