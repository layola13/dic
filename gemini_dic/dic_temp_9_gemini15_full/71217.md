fitful:/ˈfɪtfəl/| adj.|断断续续的；一阵一阵的；不安定的| characterized by unsteadiness or irregularity; occurring in fits or starts; restless or agitated|例句：He suffered from fitful sleep. 他睡眠不安稳。

例句：The child experienced fitful bursts of energy throughout the day.  孩子一整天都断断续续地爆发精力。


近义词：intermittent, spasmodic, erratic, irregular, restless|反义词：continuous, steady, consistent, calm


词源:源自古英语 *fitt,  意为“a bout, fit, struggle”。  后缀-ful  表示“full of”。 因此，fitful 字面意思是“充满阵发的”。
