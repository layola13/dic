loquacity:/ləˈkwæsɪti/| n. |健谈；多话；饶舌|The quality of being excessively talkative; garrulity.  It refers to a fluency or volubility of speech, often to an excessive degree.|例句：Her loquacity made it difficult to get a word in edgewise.  她的健谈使得很难插上话。|例句：His loquacity was a charming trait, but sometimes it wore thin. 他的健谈是一个迷人的特质，但有时也会让人厌烦。|近义词：garrulousness, talkativeness, volubility, fluency|反义词： taciturnity, reticence, silence
