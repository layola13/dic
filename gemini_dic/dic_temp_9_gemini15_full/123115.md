mongrels:/ˈmʌŋɡrəlz/ | n. | 杂种狗；混血儿；劣等的东西 | A mongrel is a dog of mixed breed.  The term is often used pejoratively, implying inferiority. However, many people now embrace the term and celebrate the unique qualities of mixed-breed dogs. | Example: The stray dog was a scruffy mongrel with mismatched ears. 那只流浪狗是一只毛茸茸的杂种狗，耳朵大小不一。  |近义词：crossbreed, hybrid, cur | 反义词：purebred
