semimanagerial:/ˌsɛmiˌmænəˈdʒɪəriəl/| adj.| 半管理的，半管理人员的 | Relating to or characteristic of a position that combines managerial and non-managerial duties.  It suggests a role that has some supervisory or administrative responsibilities but also involves performing tasks typically done by non-managers. | Example:  He held a semimanagerial position, overseeing a small team while also contributing to the project's technical aspects. 他担任一个半管理职位，负责监督一个小团队，同时也为项目的技术方面做出贡献。|近义词：supervisory, administrative, managerial (to some extent) |反义词： purely managerial, purely non-managerial
