revel:/ˈrɛvəl/ | v. n. |狂欢；沉湎；泄露；显示 | to take intense pleasure or delight; to engage in boisterous merrymaking; to make something known |例句：They reveled in their unexpected victory. 他们为他们意外的胜利而狂欢。|近义词：celebrate, enjoy, disclose, reveal |反义词：mourn, conceal, hide |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They revel in their success. = 他们沉醉于他们的成功。
We revel in the beauty of nature. = 我们沉醉于大自然的美丽。

一般过去时:
They reveled all night long. = 他们整夜狂欢。
She reveled in the attention she received. = 她沉醉于她收到的关注。

现在完成时:
They have reveled in their newfound freedom. = 他们一直沉醉于他们新获得的自由。
We have reveled in the sunshine all day. = 我们一整天都在享受阳光。

过去完成时:
They had reveled in the victory before the news spread. = 在消息传开之前，他们就已经为胜利而狂欢了。
She had reveled in her success before the setbacks began. = 在挫折开始之前，她已经沉浸在她的成功中了。

将来时:
They will revel in the celebrations tomorrow. = 他们明天将在庆祝活动中狂欢。
We will revel in the warmth of the fire tonight. = 今晚我们将沉浸在炉火的温暖中。

虚拟语气:
If they won, they would revel in their victory. = 如果他们赢了，他们将会为他们的胜利而狂欢。
I wish I could revel in the moment without worry. = 我希望我能无忧无虑地享受这一刻。

被动语态:
The victory was reveled in by the whole team. = 全队都为这场胜利而欢庆。
The secret was unwittingly reveled by a careless remark. = 一个不经意的评论无意中泄露了秘密。

疑问句:
Will they revel in the party tonight? = 他们今晚会参加派对狂欢吗？
Do they often revel in their accomplishments? = 他们经常沉醉于自己的成就吗？

否定句:
They did not revel in their defeat. = 他们并没有为他们的失败而沮丧。
She didn't revel in the criticism she received. = 她并不沉醉于她收到的批评。
