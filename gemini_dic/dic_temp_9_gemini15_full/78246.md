geriatricians:/ˌdʒɛriəˈtrɪʃənz/| n. |老年病学家|A geriatrician is a physician specializing in the medical care of elderly people.  They diagnose and treat diseases and conditions common in older adults, and often focus on age-related health issues.|例句：My grandmother is under the care of a skilled geriatrician.  我的祖母正在一位经验丰富的老年病学家的照料下。|例句：The hospital is seeking to recruit more geriatricians to meet the growing demand. 这家医院正在寻求招聘更多老年病学家以满足日益增长的需求。|近义词：gerontologist (老年学家, 侧重研究), physician (医生, 更广义)|反义词：pediatrician (儿科医生)

词源:

The word "geriatrician" is derived from the Greek word "geras" (γηρας), meaning "old age," and the suffix "-iatric," which relates to medicine or healing.  The suffix "-ian" indicates a person who specializes in a particular field.  Therefore, a geriatrician is a medical professional specializing in the care of the elderly.
