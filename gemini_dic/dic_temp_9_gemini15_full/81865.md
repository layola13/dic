grids:/ɡrɪdz/ | n. | 格子；网络；图表；电网 | A grid is a network of lines that cross each other, often used to organize information or create a structure.  It can refer to a system of interconnected lines, like an electricity grid, or a pattern of squares or rectangles. | Example: The city map was laid out on a grid pattern.  城市地图按照网格图案绘制。 The power grid suffered damage during the storm.  电力网在暴风雨中受损。 |近义词：network, lattice, mesh, framework | 反义词：chaos, disorder | 名词
