grenade:/ɡrəˈneɪd/| n. |手榴弹|A small explosive device, typically thrown by hand, that detonates after a short delay.  It is used as a weapon in warfare and other conflicts.|例句：The soldier threw a grenade at the enemy bunker.  士兵朝敌人的碉堡扔了一颗手榴弹。

例句：The terrorist attack involved the use of several grenades.  恐怖袭击使用了多枚手榴弹。


近义词：bomb, explosive, projectile|反义词：none|词性：名词
