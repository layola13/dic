diastasis:/daɪˈæstəsɪs/| n. | 裂开；分离；(肌肉的)分离|Separation of normally joined parts; specifically, the separation of the rectus abdominis muscles of the abdomen.  Diastasis recti is a common condition, especially in postpartum women. |The diastasis between the bones was noticeable.|近义词：separation, divergence, cleft |反义词：union, conjunction, fusion
