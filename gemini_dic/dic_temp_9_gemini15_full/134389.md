They are typically consumed for their taste, refreshment, or other non-intoxicating properties. |Example:  Many people choose nonintoxicants like herbal teas or sparkling water as alternatives to alcoholic beverages.  许多人选择不含酒精的饮料，如草药茶或苏打水，作为酒精饮料的替代品。|近义词：soft drinks, non-alcoholic beverages|反义词：intoxicants, alcoholic beverages
