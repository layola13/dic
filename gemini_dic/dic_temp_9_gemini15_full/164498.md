preequipments:/ˌpriːɪˈkwɪpmənts/|n.|预先装备，预先准备的设备|Items or resources prepared or obtained in advance for a particular purpose or project.|The preequipments for the expedition included tents, sleeping bags, and plenty of food. 探险队的预先装备包括帐篷、睡袋和大量的食物。|近义词：advance preparations, prior arrangements|反义词：afterthought, improvisation
