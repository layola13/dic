replaces:/rɪˈpleɪsɪz/| v. |替换，取代|To take the place of (someone or something)|The old building will be replaced by a modern skyscraper.  旧建筑将被一座现代摩天大楼取代。|He replaced the broken window. 他更换了破损的窗户。|substitutes, supplants|keeps, retains|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replaces the old tires with new ones. 他用新的轮胎替换旧轮胎。
She replaces the broken lamp with a new one. 她用新的台灯替换了坏的台灯。

一般过去时:
They replaced the damaged roof last week. 他们上周更换了损坏的屋顶。
The mechanic replaced the faulty engine. 技工更换了故障的发动机。

现在完成时:
She has replaced all the old furniture. 她已经更换了所有旧家具。
They have replaced the outdated software. 他们已经更换了过时的软件。

过去完成时:
He had replaced the batteries before the trip. 他在旅行前已经更换了电池。
She had replaced the broken vase before her guests arrived.  她在客人到达之前已经换掉了破碎的花瓶。

将来时:
They will replace the broken pipe tomorrow. 他们明天将更换破裂的管道。
The company will replace the defective product. 公司将更换有缺陷的产品。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would replace my old car. 如果我有更多钱，我会换掉我的旧车。
I wish I could replace this damaged screen. 我真希望我能更换这个损坏的屏幕。

被动语态:
The old equipment was replaced by new machines. 旧设备被新机器取代了。
The broken window will be replaced tomorrow. 破碎的窗户明天将被更换。

疑问句:
Will they replace the damaged parts? 他们会更换损坏的部件吗？
What will replace the current system? 什么将取代目前的系统？

否定句:
He didn't replace the broken light bulb. 他没有更换坏掉的灯泡。
They won't replace the outdated technology. 他们不会更换过时的技术。


PHRASE->
replace with = 用...替换
replace A with B = 用B替换A
in place of = 代替
instead of = 代替
take the place of = 取代
