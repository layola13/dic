peats:/piːts/| n.|泥炭；泥煤|Partially decayed vegetable matter found in bogs, used as fuel.  |例句：The peat fire crackled merrily in the hearth.  泥炭火在壁炉里愉快地噼啪作响。|例句：Peat is a renewable energy source. 泥炭是一种可再生能源。|近义词：turf, bog, muck|反义词：None specifically, but "coal" could be considered an antonym in the context of fuel.

词源:  The word "peat" comes from the Old English word *pæt, meaning "peat," which itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Proto-Germanic *pett- "pit, hollow."  The association is likely due to the way peat is found in bogs and marshes.
