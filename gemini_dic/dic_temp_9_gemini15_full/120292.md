minibibliographies:/ˌmɪniˈbɪbliˈɒɡrəfiz/ | n. | 小型参考书目；简短的参考书目 | A minibibliography is a short list of books or articles on a particular subject.  It's typically shorter and less comprehensive than a full bibliography. | Example: The professor provided a minibibliography of relevant articles for the upcoming essay. 教授提供了一份与即将到来的论文相关的文章简短参考书目。 | 近义词：short bibliography, brief bibliography, selected bibliography | 反义词：full bibliography, extensive bibliography
