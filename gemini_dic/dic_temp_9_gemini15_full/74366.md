frails:/freɪlz/ | n. |脆弱的事物；易碎品；弱点 | Weaknesses or vulnerabilities; things that are easily broken or damaged.  |例句：The old woman's frails were evident in her trembling hands.  这位老妇人颤抖的双手显露出了她的衰弱。

例句：The antique vase is one of her most precious frails. 这只古董花瓶是她最珍贵的易碎品之一。

近义词：weaknesses, vulnerabilities, shortcomings, defects | 反义词：strengths, assets

词源:  From Old French *frail, from Latin fragilis ("easily broken").  The noun sense of "fragile things" developed later.
