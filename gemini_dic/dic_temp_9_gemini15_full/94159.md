impenetrability: /ˌɪmˌpɛnɪtrəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可渗透性；难以理解；难以克服 | The quality or state of being impenetrable;  inability to be penetrated or understood.  It refers to something that cannot be pierced, entered, or understood. |

例句：

The impenetrability of the jungle made exploration difficult.  丛林的难以穿越使得探险变得困难。

The impenetrability of his arguments left the audience bewildered. 他论点的难以理解让听众感到困惑。


近义词：impermeability, opacity, inscrutability, unintelligibility

反义词：permeability, transparency, understandability, accessibility

词性：名词
