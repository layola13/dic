hide-and-go-seek: /haɪd ænd ɡoʊ siːk/ | n. | 捉迷藏 | A children's game in which one or more players hide and the others search for them.  | The children were playing hide-and-go-seek in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩捉迷藏。 | tag,  I spy |  (no direct antonym,  but the opposite action would be "finding" or "being found") | noun
