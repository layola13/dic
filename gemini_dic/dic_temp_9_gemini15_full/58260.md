drumming:/ˈdrʌmɪŋ/ | n. v. |击鼓；持续的敲击声；|the act of playing a drum or drums; a continuous repetitive sound like that of a drum| The drumming of the rain on the roof kept me awake. 雨声持续不断地敲打着屋顶，让我无法入睡。The rhythmic drumming of the music filled the air.  音乐有节奏的鼓声充满了空气。| beating, rapping, pounding | silence |v.
