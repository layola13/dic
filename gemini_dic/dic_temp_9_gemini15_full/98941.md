inter-campus:/ˌɪntərˈkæmpəs/| adj.|校际的，跨校的|Relating to or involving more than one campus of a university or college.|例句：The inter-campus bus service is very convenient for students. 校际巴士服务对学生来说非常方便。|近义词：cross-campus, between-campus|反义词：intra-campus
