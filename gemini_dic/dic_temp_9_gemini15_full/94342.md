impingements:/ɪmˈpɪndʒmənts/ | n. | 侵犯；妨碍；影响 | The encroachments or infringements on something.  The act of impinging; the state of being impinged upon.  |例句：The new regulations are seen as impingements on individual liberties. 新规被视为对个人自由的侵犯。

The constant noise from the construction site was a major impingement on their quality of life. 建筑工地持续不断的噪音严重影响了他们的生活质量。 | 近义词：infringements, encroachments, intrusions, violations | 反义词：enhancements, improvements, aids | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant noise impinges on their concentration. = 持续不断的噪音影响他们的注意力。
These regulations impinge on our freedom of speech. = 这些规章制度限制了我们的言论自由。


一般过去时:
The storm impinged on the coastal areas. = 暴风雨侵袭了沿海地区。
His bad temper impinged on his relationships with others. = 他的坏脾气影响了他与他人的关系。


现在完成时:
The government's policies have impinged on the economy. = 政府的政策已经影响了经济。
His illness has impinged on his ability to work. = 他的病影响了他的工作能力。


过去完成时:
The previous administration's actions had impinged on the environment. = 前一届政府的行动已经对环境造成了影响。
The drought had impinged on the agricultural yields. = 干旱已经影响了农业产量。


将来时:
The new technology will impinge on many traditional industries. = 新技术将影响许多传统产业。
The coming economic downturn will impinge on job security. = 即将来临的经济衰退将影响就业安全。


虚拟语气:
If the project were to proceed, it would impinge on the local ecosystem. = 如果项目继续进行，它将影响当地的生态系统。
I wish these regulations didn't impinge so much on our daily lives. = 我希望这些规章制度不会如此影响我们的日常生活。


被动语态:
Their privacy was impinged upon by the media. = 他们的隐私受到了媒体的侵犯。
The project was impinged upon by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目受到了不可预见的情况的影响。


疑问句:
Does the new law impinge on our rights? = 新法律是否侵犯了我们的权利？
How does this decision impinge on the company's future? = 这个决定如何影响公司的未来？


否定句:
The decision did not impinge on his career. = 这个决定没有影响他的职业生涯。
The reforms did not impinge on the fundamental rights of citizens. = 改革没有侵犯公民的基本权利。
