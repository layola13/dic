defrosters:/dɪˈfrɒstərz/ | n. |除霜器，除霜装置 | A device or system used to remove frost or ice from a surface, such as a windshield or refrigerator.  | Example: The car's defrosters cleared the windshield quickly.  这辆车的除霜器很快清除了挡风玻璃上的霜。|近义词：ice remover, de-icer |反义词：icer (仅在特定语境下，指故意结冰的装置) |名词
