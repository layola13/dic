panacea:/pænəˈsiːə/| n. |万能药；灵丹妙药 |A panacea is a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases.  It suggests a cure-all, often implying an overly simplistic or unrealistic solution. |The proposed plan is not a panacea for all our problems.  这个提议的计划并不是我们所有问题的灵丹妙药。|cure-all, remedy, solution | none (antonyms describe the lack of a solution, not the solution itself) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is no panacea for poverty. = 贫困没有灵丹妙药。
A healthy lifestyle is often touted as a panacea for many ailments. = 健康的生活方式常被吹捧为许多疾病的灵丹妙药。


一般过去时:
They believed the new technology was a panacea. = 他们相信这项新技术是万能药。
The doctor offered a panacea for the patient's chronic pain. = 医生为患者的慢性疼痛提供了一种灵丹妙药。


现在完成时:
No single policy has proven to be a panacea for economic woes. = 没有一项单一政策被证明是解决经济困境的灵丹妙药。
Scientists have not yet discovered a panacea for cancer. = 科学家尚未发现癌症的灵丹妙药。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of penicillin, people desperately sought a panacea for bacterial infections. = 在青霉素发明之前，人们拼命寻找细菌感染的灵丹妙药。
They had hoped the new drug would be a panacea for all their health problems. = 他们曾希望这种新药能解决他们所有的健康问题。


将来时:
This new drug will not be a panacea for all diseases. = 这种新药不会是所有疾病的灵丹妙药。
They believe that artificial intelligence will be a panacea for many societal problems. = 他们相信人工智能将是许多社会问题的灵丹妙药。


虚拟语气:
If there were a panacea for aging, everyone would want it. = 如果有抗衰老的灵丹妙药，每个人都会想要。
I wish there were a panacea for climate change. = 我希望有解决气候变化的灵丹妙药。


被动语态:
The new treatment was advertised as a panacea for various conditions. = 新疗法被宣传为各种疾病的灵丹妙药。
The proposed solution is often criticized for being presented as a panacea. = 提出的解决方案经常因为被当成灵丹妙药而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is there a panacea for global warming? = 有解决全球变暖的灵丹妙药吗？
Do you believe in the existence of a panacea for all human suffering? = 你相信存在解决所有人类痛苦的灵丹妙药吗？


否定句:
There is no panacea for the complexities of human relationships. = 人际关系的复杂性没有灵丹妙药。
Education is not a panacea for all social ills. = 教育并非所有社会弊病的灵丹妙药。
