ris:/rɪs/| n.|风险；危险；冒险|The risk of failure is high. 失败的风险很高。|近义词：hazard, peril, danger |反义词：safety, security


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project carries a significant risk. = 这个项目存在很大的风险。
He assesses the risks before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前会评估风险。


一般过去时:
The company took a big risk investing in the new technology. = 公司投资这项新技术承担了很大的风险。
They underestimated the risks involved. = 他们低估了所涉及的风险。


现在完成时:
We have taken all necessary precautions to mitigate the risks. = 我们已经采取了所有必要的预防措施来减轻风险。
They have assessed the risks and decided to proceed. = 他们评估了风险并决定继续进行。


过去完成时:
He had already foreseen the risks before starting the project. = 他在开始项目之前就已经预见到了风险。
They had underestimated the risks, leading to significant losses. = 他们低估了风险，导致了巨大的损失。


将来时:
The investment will carry considerable risks. = 这项投资将承担相当大的风险。
We will assess the risks carefully before making a decision. = 我们将在做决定之前仔细评估风险。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't take such a risk. = 如果我是你，我不会冒这样的险。
It would be unwise to take that risk. = 冒那个险是不明智的。


被动语态:
The risks were carefully considered before the decision was made. = 在做出决定之前，风险得到了仔细考虑。
The project is perceived as high-risk. = 这个项目被认为是高风险的。


疑问句:
What are the risks involved? = 其中涉及哪些风险？
Is there any risk of failure? = 失败的风险大吗？


否定句:
There is no risk of infection. = 没有感染的风险。
He refused to take any risks. = 他拒绝承担任何风险。


PHRASE->
at risk:处于危险中

low risk:低风险
