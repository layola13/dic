overexercised: /ˌoʊvərˈɛksərsaɪzd/ | adj. | 运动过度；过度训练的 |  Describes a state where someone has engaged in excessive physical activity, often resulting in negative consequences.  This can refer to both physical and mental exertion. | 例句：He felt completely overexercised after running two marathons in one week. 他在一周内跑了两个马拉松后感到过度运动。  She was overexercised and mentally exhausted from juggling work and intense training. 她因为兼顾工作和高强度训练而过度运动和精神疲惫。 | 近义词：overtrained, overworked, exhausted, fatigued | 反义词：undertrained, rested, relaxed |
