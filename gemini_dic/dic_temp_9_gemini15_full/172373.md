putting:/ˈpʊtɪŋ/| v. |放置；摆放；表达；穿戴|The act of placing something somewhere.  It can also refer to the expression of something, such as an idea or feeling, or the act of putting on clothing.|例句：He's putting the groceries away. 他正在把杂货收起来。

例句：She's putting her best foot forward in the job interview. 她在面试中全力以赴。


近义词：placing, setting, expressing, wearing|反义词：removing, taking off, withholding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am putting the books on the shelf. = 我把书放在书架上。
He is putting on his coat. = 他正在穿上他的外套。


一般过去时:
I put the flowers in a vase. = 我把花插进花瓶里。
She put her hand on my shoulder. = 她把手放在我的肩膀上。


现在完成时:
I have put the dishes in the dishwasher. = 我已经把盘子放进洗碗机了。
They have put their differences aside. = 他们已经放下彼此的歧见。


过去完成时:
I had put the cake in the fridge before the party. = 派对前我已经把蛋糕放进冰箱了。
She had put on her makeup before going out. = 她出门前已经化好妆了。


将来时:
I will put the letter in the mailbox tomorrow. = 我明天会把信投入邮箱。
He will put his energy into his studies. = 他将把精力投入到学习中。


虚拟语气:
If I had put more effort, I would have succeeded. = 如果我当时更加努力，我就成功了。
I wish I had put my money in the bank. = 我真希望我把钱存进了银行。


被动语态:
The books were put on the table. = 书被放在桌子上。
The painting was put in a frame. = 画被装裱了。


疑问句:
Where are you putting the toys? = 你把玩具放在哪里？
What are you putting in the soup? = 你在汤里放什么？


否定句:
I didn't put the sugar in the tea. = 我没有在茶里放糖。
She didn't put her name on the list. = 她没有把她的名字写在名单上。


PHRASE->
putting on weight = 增重
putting it mildly = 委婉地说
putting someone down = 贬低某人
putting up with = 忍受
putting off = 推迟
