hates:/heɪts/ | v. | 憎恨，讨厌 | To feel intense dislike or disgust for (someone or something).  | He hates spiders. 他讨厌蜘蛛。 |She hates the smell of smoke. 她讨厌烟味。| loves, adores | likes, appreciates | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hates broccoli. = 他讨厌西兰花。
She hates loud noises. = 她讨厌大声的噪音。

一般过去时:
I hated school when I was young. = 我小时候讨厌上学。
They hated the movie. = 他们讨厌那部电影。

现在完成时:
I have hated this job for years. = 我已经讨厌这份工作好几年了。
She has hated him since their argument. = 自从他们吵架以来，她就一直讨厌他。

过去完成时:
He had hated her for a long time before he finally confronted her. = 在他最终和她摊牌之前，他已经讨厌她很久了。
We had hated the weather all week. = 我们整个星期都讨厌这个天气。

将来时:
I will hate leaving this place. = 我将讨厌离开这个地方。
She will hate the long journey. = 她会讨厌漫长的旅程。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't hate public speaking. = 我真希望我不讨厌公开演讲。
If I hated my job less, I might stay. = 如果我不那么讨厌我的工作，我可能会留下。


被动语态:
(The verb "hate" is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural way to express this would be to use a different verb.)  His actions were hated by many. = 许多人憎恨他的行为。(This is less common than the active voice.)
His ideas are generally disliked. = 他的想法通常不受欢迎。(A more natural alternative)


疑问句:
Do you hate Mondays? = 你讨厌星期一吗？
Does he hate his job? = 他讨厌他的工作吗？


否定句:
I don't hate him. = 我不讨厌他。
She doesn't hate cats. = 她不讨厌猫。



PHRASE->
hate to say = 不好意思说
hate to admit = 不得不承认
hate doing something = 讨厌做某事
can't stand/bear to hate = 无法忍受讨厌
hate the thought of = 一想到就讨厌
