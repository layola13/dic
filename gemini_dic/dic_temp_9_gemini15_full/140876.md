on-the-table:/ˌɒnðəˈteɪbl/| adj. |摆在桌面上；公开讨论的；正在考虑中的|公开讨论的；正在考虑中的;  Something that is "on the table" is being actively considered or discussed, usually in a formal setting like a negotiation. It implies that a proposal or idea is a viable option and is open for consideration.  |例句：The merger is on the table, but we haven't reached an agreement yet.  合并案正在讨论中，但我们尚未达成协议。

The new proposal is on the table for discussion.  新的提案正在讨论中。|近义词：under consideration, being discussed, up for debate |反义词：off the table, withdrawn, rejected |词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The proposal is on the table. = 这项提案正在考虑中。
The terms are on the table. = 条款正在讨论中。

一般过去时:
The offer was on the table for a week. = 这个提议摆在桌面上有一周了。
The contract was on the table, but they rejected it. = 合同摆在那儿，但他们拒绝了。

现在完成时:
The issue has been on the table for months. =这个问题已经讨论了几个月了。
Several options have been on the table since the beginning of the negotiations. = 从谈判开始以来，好几个方案都摆在了桌面上。

过去完成时:
The plan had been on the table before the crisis began. = 在危机开始之前，这个计划就已经摆上桌了。
The budget had been on the table but was ultimately rejected. = 预算方案已经摆上桌了，但最终被否决了。

将来时:
The decision will be on the table next week. = 决定将在下周摆上桌面。
The revised contract will be on the table by Friday. = 修改后的合同将在周五摆上桌面。

虚拟语气:
If the offer were on the table, I would accept it. = 如果这个提议摆在桌面上，我会接受它。
If the compromise had been on the table earlier, the strike could have been avoided. = 如果妥协方案早点摆上桌面，罢工是可以避免的。

被动语态:
The matter was put on the table by the chairman. = 主席把这个问题摆上了桌面。
The new policy is being put on the table for consideration. = 正在将新的政策摆上桌面供大家考虑。

疑问句:
Is the budget still on the table? = 预算还在讨论中吗？
Are all the options on the table? = 所有方案都在考虑之中吗？

否定句:
The previous proposal is no longer on the table. = 上一个提案不再被考虑了。
That idea is definitely off the table. =那个主意肯定不行了。


PHRASE->
put something on the table = 提出（建议、问题等）
take something off the table = 取消（建议、问题等）
keep something on the table = 继续考虑（建议、问题等）
everything is on the table = 所有事情都在讨论范围内
leave something on the table = 放弃一些东西(机会、利益等)
