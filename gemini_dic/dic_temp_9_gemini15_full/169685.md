proposition:/ˌprɒpəˈzɪʃən/ /ˌprɑːpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. |主张；提议；命题；论点 | A proposition is a statement that expresses a judgment or opinion. It can be a suggestion, a proposal, or a statement of fact that can be true or false.  It can also refer to a problem or task that needs to be solved or dealt with. |  He put forward a new proposition for increasing sales. 他提出了一个提高销售额的新方案。 The proposition that the earth is round is universally accepted. 地球是圆的这个命题是普遍接受的。 | proposal, suggestion, assertion, thesis, premise |  refusal, denial, objection | 名词

His proposition is based on flawed data. = 他的主张基于有缺陷的数据。

He made a surprising proposition. = 他提出了一个令人惊讶的提议。

词源:
