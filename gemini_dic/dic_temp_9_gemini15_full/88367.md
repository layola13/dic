hippyish: /ˈhɪpiːɪʃ/ | adj. |嬉皮士似的，嬉皮风格的 | Having the characteristics of hippies; resembling or suggesting hippies.  The word evokes the style, attitudes, and beliefs associated with the hippie counterculture of the 1960s.  This includes elements like long hair, casual clothing, pacifism, and a rejection of mainstream values. | 例句：He had a hippyish beard and wore brightly colored clothing. 他留着嬉皮士似的胡子，穿着色彩鲜艳的衣服。


The band's music had a distinctly hippyish vibe.  这个乐队的音乐有着明显的嬉皮士风格。


近义词：bohemian, free-spirited, unconventional, nonconformist


反义词：conventional, mainstream, conservative, conformist
