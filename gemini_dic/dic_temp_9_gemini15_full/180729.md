regrouping:/ˌriːˈɡruːpɪŋ/| n. |重新组合；重整；改组 |The act of reorganizing or reforming a group, typically after a setback or defeat.  It can refer to people, resources, or ideas. |例句：After the initial attack, the soldiers regrouped and planned their next move.  在最初的攻击之后，士兵们重新集结并计划了他们的下一步行动。

例句：The company is undergoing a major regrouping to improve efficiency. 这家公司正在进行大规模的改组以提高效率。

近义词：reorganization, reorganization, reformation, reconstitution |反义词：dispersion, scattering, disintegration |词性:名词
