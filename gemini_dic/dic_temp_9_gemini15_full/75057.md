frightening:/ˈfraɪtənɪŋ/ | adj. |令人恐惧的，可怕的 |  Describes something that causes fear or alarm.  It emphasizes the feeling of being scared or terrified. |例句：The storm was frightening.  = 暴风雨很可怕。
The thought of public speaking is frightening to me. =  想到公开演讲我就害怕。|近义词：scary, terrifying, dreadful, alarming |反义词：comforting, reassuring, calming, peaceful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The movie is frightening. = 这部电影很吓人。
That noise is frightening the cat. = 那个噪音把猫吓坏了。


一般过去时:
The story was frightening. = 这个故事很吓人。
The sudden loud bang was frightening. = 突然的一声巨响很吓人。


现在完成时:
The experience has been frightening. = 这段经历令人恐惧。
That film has frightened me. = 那部电影吓到我了。


过去完成时:
I had been frightened by the dog before. = 我以前被狗吓过。
The children had been frightened by the thunder. = 孩子们被雷声吓坏了。


将来时:
The results will be frightening. = 结果将令人恐惧。
I think the next part of the movie will be frightening. = 我认为电影的下一部分会很吓人。


虚拟语气:
It would be frightening to be alone in that house. = 单独待在那所房子里会很可怕。
If I saw a ghost, I would be frightened. = 如果我看到鬼，我会害怕。


被动语态:
The children were frightened by the loud noise. = 孩子们被巨大的噪音吓到了。
He was frightened by the news. = 他被这个消息吓到了。


疑问句:
Is the movie frightening? = 这部电影吓人吗？
Are you frightened of spiders? = 你怕蜘蛛吗？


否定句:
The situation wasn't frightening at all. = 情况一点也不吓人。
I wasn't frightened by the dog. = 我不怕那条狗。
