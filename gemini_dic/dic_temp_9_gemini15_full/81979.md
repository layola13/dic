grindle:/ˈɡrɪndəl/| vt.|磨碎；压榨；使…苦恼|To grind or crush something into small pieces; to oppress or exploit someone; to cause someone annoyance or distress.|例句：He ground the spices to a fine powder. 他把香料磨成细粉。 The company grinds its employees mercilessly.  这家公司无情地压榨员工。|近义词：grind, crush, oppress, harass|反义词：soothe, comfort, pamper|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grinds the coffee beans every morning. = 他每天早上都磨咖啡豆。
She grinds her teeth in her sleep. = 她睡觉时磨牙。

一般过去时:
The farmer grinded the wheat into flour. = 农民把小麦磨成面粉。
They grinded down the opposition with relentless propaganda. = 他们用无情的宣传压制了反对派。

现在完成时:
He has grinded all the peppercorns for the stew. = 他已经把炖肉用的所有胡椒粒都磨碎了。
The constant pressure has grinded her down. = 持续的压力使她精疲力尽。

过去完成时:

They had grinded the workers down with overwork. = 他们用过度工作压垮了工人。


将来时:

The new regulations will grind small businesses into the ground. = 新的法规将把小型企业压垮。


虚拟语气:
