encamps:/ɪnˈkæmps/| v.| 设营；扎营 |To establish a camp; to set up temporary lodgings outdoors.  |例句：The scouts encamped by the river.  侦察兵在河边安营扎寨。

例句：The army encamped on the plains before the battle.  军队在战前在平原上扎营。

近义词：camp, pitch camp, make camp |反义词：disperse, depart, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They encamp near the lake every summer. = 他们每年夏天都在湖边扎营。
The nomads encamp wherever they find water. = 游牧民族在任何找到水的地方安营扎寨。

一般过去时:
The explorers encamped in a sheltered valley. = 探险家们在一个隐蔽的山谷中扎营。
We encamped under the stars last night. = 我们昨晚在星空下扎营。

现在完成时:
They have encamped in this area for many years. = 他们在这个地区扎营多年了。
The army has encamped on the hilltop since the storm began. = 自从暴风雨开始以来，军队就驻扎在山顶上。

过去完成时:
They had encamped there for a week before the supplies arrived. = 补给到达之前，他们已经在那里扎营一周了。
The travelers had encamped by the river before nightfall. = 旅行者们在天黑前在河边扎营。

将来时:
We will encamp in the mountains next weekend. = 我们下周末将在山上扎营。
The expedition will encamp near the summit before their final ascent. = 探险队将在最后攀登之前在山顶附近扎营。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would encamp longer. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会扎营更长时间。
I wish we could encamp closer to the water. = 我希望我们能扎营更靠近水源。

被动语态:
The area was encamped by thousands of soldiers. = 数千名士兵在该地区扎营。
A new campsite will be encamped next to the old one. = 一个新的营地将在旧营地旁边建立。

疑问句:
Where did they encamp last night? = 他们昨晚在哪里扎营？
Will they encamp near the village? = 他们会在村庄附近扎营吗？

否定句:
They did not encamp near the river because of the flooding. = 由于洪水，他们没有在河边扎营。
We could not encamp there; the ground was too rocky. = 我们不能在那里扎营；地面太崎岖了。
