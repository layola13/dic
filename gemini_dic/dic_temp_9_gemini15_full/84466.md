haloscope:/ˈhæləskoʊp/| n.|晕轮镜，暗物质探测器|A haloscope is a type of detector used in experimental particle physics to search for axions, a hypothetical elementary particle. It works by exploiting the Primakoff effect, where axions convert into photons in a strong magnetic field.  The photons are then detected, providing evidence for the existence of axions.  It is primarily used in the search for dark matter.|Example: The researchers are using a sophisticated haloscope to search for dark matter particles in the galactic halo.|近义词：axion detector|反义词：无
