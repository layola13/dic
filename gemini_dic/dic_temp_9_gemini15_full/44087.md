crippledom:/ˈkrɪpəlˌdəm/| n. |残疾状态，残疾人的境况|The state of being crippled; the condition of being disabled.  It often refers to the social and systemic barriers faced by disabled people, rather than simply the physical disability itself.  The term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its negative connotations.|例句：He wrote about his experiences and the challenges of living with crippledom. 他写了关于他的经历以及生活在残疾状态下的挑战。|近义词：disability, impairment, handicap|反义词：abledness, normalcy
