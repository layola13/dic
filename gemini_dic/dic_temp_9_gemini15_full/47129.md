The term can also refer to a princess of France, specifically the daughter of a King of France, or a type of fish, the Dover sole. |例句：The dauphines were delicate and delicious. 多菲内酥点精致美味。  The recipe for these dauphines is a family secret. 这些多菲内酥点的食谱是家族秘方。|近义词：princess (in the context of French royalty), pastry,  (fish) Dover sole|反义词：N/A
