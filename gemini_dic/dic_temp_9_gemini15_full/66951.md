experiences:/ɪkˈspɪəriənsɪz/| n.|经验，经历|The plural form of "experience," referring to events or situations a person has lived through, impacting their knowledge and understanding.  It can also refer to the process of gaining knowledge through direct participation.|例句：My experiences in college shaped my career path. 我的大学经历塑造了我的职业道路。|例句：She shared her experiences traveling through Southeast Asia. 她分享了她游历东南亚的经历。|近义词：events, encounters, adventures|反义词：inexperience, naivety|名词
