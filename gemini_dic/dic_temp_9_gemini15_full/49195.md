delay:/dɪˈleɪ/ | n., v. | 耽搁；延迟 | To postpone something to a later time;  a period of time by which something is late. |例句：The flight was delayed due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气而延误。

The meeting has been delayed until next week. 会议推迟到下周举行。|近义词：postpone, defer, procrastinate |反义词：accelerate, expedite, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He delays his work every day. 他每天都拖延工作。
They often delay their decisions. 他们经常拖延做决定。

一般过去时:
She delayed her trip because of the rain. 因为下雨，她推迟了旅行。
The project was delayed by several weeks. 项目推迟了几周。

现在完成时:
We have delayed the announcement. 我们已经推迟了公告。
They have delayed the payment.  他们已经拖延付款。

过去完成时:
They had delayed the launch of the product. 他们已经推迟了产品的发布。
The train had delayed its departure. 火车已经延误了出发。

将来时:
I will delay my response until tomorrow. 我将把回复推迟到明天。
They will delay the construction for a month. 他们将把施工推迟一个月。

虚拟语气:
If I had not delayed, I would have finished on time. 如果我没有拖延，我就能按时完成了。
I wish I hadn't delayed making that decision. 我真希望我没有拖延做那个决定。

被动语态:
The game was delayed due to the storm. 比赛因暴风雨而推迟。
The project is being delayed by unforeseen circumstances. 项目因不可预见的情况而被推迟。

疑问句:
Will the train be delayed? 火车会延误吗？
Why was the meeting delayed? 会议为什么被推迟了？

否定句:
I will not delay my answer. 我不会拖延我的回答。
They did not delay their departure. 他们没有延误他们的出发。
