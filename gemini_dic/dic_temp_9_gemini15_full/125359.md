multilocular:/ˌmʌltiˈlɒkjʊlə(r)/| adj.|多房的；多小室的|Having or consisting of many small compartments or chambers.  The term is commonly used in medicine and biology to describe structures with multiple cavities or locules.  For example, a multilocular cyst has multiple compartments.|例句：The ovarian tumor was diagnosed as a multilocular cyst. 卵巢肿瘤被诊断为多房囊肿。

The lung showed a multilocular pattern on the CT scan.  CT扫描显示肺部呈现多房状。|近义词：multicompartmental, polylocular|反义词：unilocular, single-chambered
