privatization: /ˌpraɪvətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 私有化 | The process of transferring ownership of a business, industry, or service from the public sector (government) to the private sector (individuals or companies).  It involves the sale or lease of state-owned assets to private entities.  | Example: The privatization of the railway system led to improved efficiency and lower fares. 私铁系统的私有化提高了效率并降低了票价。 |近义词：denationalization, commercialization | 反义词：nationalization, socialization | 名词
