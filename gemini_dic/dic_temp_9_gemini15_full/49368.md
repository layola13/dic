deliquescing:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsɪŋ/| v. |潮解；溶解；衰败 |Becoming liquid or dissolving spontaneously.  This usually refers to a solid substance absorbing moisture from the air and turning into a liquid solution.  |例句：The salt is deliquescing in the humid air.  盐在潮湿的空气中潮解。

The caustic soda is deliquescing on the shelf. 苛性钠在架子上潮解了。

|近义词：dissolving, liquefying, melting |反义词：solidifying, crystallizing |动词
