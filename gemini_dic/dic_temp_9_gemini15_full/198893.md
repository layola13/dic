siding:/ˈsaɪdɪŋ/| n.| 外墙板；（房屋的）壁板；偏袒；附和 |  Exterior wall covering; the boards that cover the outside walls of a building; the act of supporting a particular person or group; agreement with a particular opinion. |The house needs new siding.  这房子需要新的外墙板。|The judge was accused of siding with the prosecution.  法官被指控偏袒检方。|近义词：cladding, paneling, siding with, supporting |反义词：opposing, disagreeing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The house siding is made of wood. = 房子的外墙板是用木头做的。
He always sides with his brother. = 他总是偏袒他哥哥。


一般过去时:
They sided the house last year. = 他们去年给房子装上了外墙板。
She sided with the underdog in the argument. = 在那场争论中，她站在弱者一边。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
