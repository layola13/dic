pro-Arabian: /ˌproʊˈærəbiən/ | adj. | 亲阿拉伯的，支持阿拉伯的 |  Supporting or favoring the Arab people or Arab nations.  The term often implies a political or ideological stance. | 例句：His pro-Arabian views were well-known.  他的亲阿拉伯观点是众所周知的。


The government's pro-Arabian policy caused tension with other nations. 政府的亲阿拉伯政策导致与其他国家关系紧张。| 近义词：Arabophile (n.), pro-Arab (adj.) | 反义词：anti-Arabian (adj.), anti-Arab (adj.) | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pro-Arabian. = 他是亲阿拉伯的。
She shows pro-Arabian sentiments. = 她表现出亲阿拉伯的感情。


一般过去时:
He was pro-Arabian in his youth. = 他年轻时是亲阿拉伯的。
The country maintained a pro-Arabian stance. = 该国保持着亲阿拉伯的立场。


现在完成时:
The organization has been consistently pro-Arabian. = 该组织一直以来都是亲阿拉伯的。
His actions have shown him to be pro-Arabian. = 他的行为表明他是亲阿拉伯的。


过去完成时:
They had been pro-Arabian before the conflict. = 在冲突之前，他们一直是亲阿拉伯的。
The government had already adopted a pro-Arabian policy. = 政府已经采取了亲阿拉伯的政策。


将来时:
He will remain pro-Arabian. = 他将继续保持亲阿拉伯的立场。
The situation will likely become more pro-Arabian. = 这种情况可能会变得更加亲阿拉伯。


虚拟语气:
If he were pro-Arabian, he would support their cause. = 如果他是亲阿拉伯的，他会支持他们的事业。
I wish the media were less pro-Arabian in their reporting. = 我希望媒体在报道中少一些亲阿拉伯的倾向。


被动语态:

否定句:
He is not pro-Arabian; he's neutral. = 他不是亲阿拉伯的；他是中立的。
The newspaper's stance is not pro-Arabian. = 这家报纸的立场并非亲阿拉伯的。

词源:  The word is a compound of "pro-" (in favor of) and "Arabian" (relating to Arabia or Arabs).  "Arabian" ultimately derives from "Arab," which has its roots in ancient Semitic languages.
