pandanus:/pænˈdeɪnəs/| n. |露兜树；螺旋状叶子的植物|A pandanus is a tropical plant with long, spiky leaves that often grow in a spiral pattern. They are known for their unique aerial roots and fruit.  Many species are used for food, fiber, or building materials. | Example:  The pandanus trees lined the beach, their leaves rustling in the breeze.  露兜树沿着海滩排列，树叶在微风中沙沙作响。|近义词：screw pine (common name)|反义词：无
