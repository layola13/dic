fibbers:/ˈfɪbərz/| n. |说谎者；纤维；|A fibber is someone who tells lies, often small or insignificant ones.  The word can also refer to a type of fiber, though this usage is less common. |例句：He's such a fibber; you can't believe a word he says. 他是个说谎者；你不能相信他说的话。|近义词：liar, prevaricator, storyteller (in a negative context) |反义词：truth-teller, honest person |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fibs frequently. = 他经常说谎。
They fib about their age. = 他们谎报年龄。

一般过去时:
She fibbed to her parents. = 她向父母撒谎。
He fibbed about where he was last night. = 他撒谎说他昨晚在哪里。

现在完成时:
I have fibbed before, but I won't again. = 我以前撒过谎，但我不会再撒谎了。
They have fibbed to us repeatedly. = 他们一再向我们撒谎。

过去完成时:
He had fibbed to me many times before I realized it. = 在我意识到之前，他已经多次向我撒谎了。
She had fibbed about her qualifications. = 她已经谎报了自己的资格。


将来时:
I will not fib to you. = 我不会对你撒谎。
He will fib again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次撒谎。


虚拟语气:
If I fibbed, I would feel guilty. = 如果我撒谎，我会感到内疚。
I wish I hadn't fibbed to her. = 我真希望我没有对她撒谎。


被动语态:
Lies were fibbed by him. = 谎言被他说了出来。(这个句子略显不自然，被动语态很少用于此词)
The truth was never fibbed about. = 事实从未被歪曲过。(这个句子更自然)


疑问句:
Did he fib about his accomplishments? = 他是否夸大了他的成就？
Are you fibbing to me? = 你在骗我吗？


否定句:
I don't fib. = 我不说谎。
She doesn't fib often. = 她不常说谎。


PHRASE->
a little fib = 一个小谎
a pack of fibs = 一堆谎话
tell a fib = 说谎
a harmless fib = 无害的谎言
white fib = 善意的谎言
