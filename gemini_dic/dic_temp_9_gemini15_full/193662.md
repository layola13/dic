self-chastisement: /ˌsɛlfˈtʃæstɪzmənt/ | n. |自我惩罚；自我责备 |  The act of punishing oneself, often for perceived failings or wrongdoing. It involves self-criticism, guilt, and a desire to atone for perceived errors.  | Example:  Her self-chastisement over the missed deadline led to sleepless nights.  她的错过截止日期的自我惩罚导致她夜不能寐。  |近义词：self-reproach, self-flagellation, penance, contrition | 反义词：self-indulgence, self-forgiveness
