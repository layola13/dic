defenselessness's:/dɪˈfɛnsləsnəsɪz/| n. |无防御能力；无助；脆弱性 |The state of being defenseless; vulnerability to attack or harm.  It often refers to a lack of protection or ability to defend oneself, either physically or emotionally. |例句：The defenselessness of the small nation made it vulnerable to invasion.  这个小国的无防御能力使其容易遭受入侵。


The defenselessness of the child in the face of such cruelty was heartbreaking. 面对如此残酷的待遇，孩子的无助令人心碎。 |近义词：vulnerability, helplessness, weakness, exposure |反义词：protection, defense, strength, resilience |词性:名词(Noun)
