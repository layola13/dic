crave:/kreɪv/| vt.|渴望，热切地希望|To have a strong desire for something.|例句：I crave chocolate. 我渴望巧克力。|例句：She craved excitement and adventure. 她渴望刺激和冒险。|近义词：long for, yearn for, desire, covet|反义词：dislike, abhor, despise|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I crave a vacation. = 我渴望一次假期。
They crave recognition for their work. = 他们渴望得到对他们工作的认可。

一般过去时:
He craved attention as a child. = 他小时候渴望得到关注。
She craved a simpler life. = 她渴望过一种更简单的生活。

现在完成时:
I have craved this food for weeks. = 我已经渴望吃这种食物好几个星期了。
They have craved freedom from oppression. = 他们一直渴望摆脱压迫。

过去完成时:
He had craved success for many years before he finally achieved it. = 在他最终取得成功之前，他已经渴望成功多年了。
She had craved a change of scenery for a long time. = 她渴望改变环境很久了。

将来时:
I will crave this comfort food when I am old. = 我老了以后会渴望这种舒适的食物。
They will crave independence as they grow older. = 他们长大后会渴望独立。

虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't crave sugar so much. = 我真希望我不那么渴望糖。
If I craved adventure, I would go backpacking. = 如果我渴望冒险，我会去背包旅行。

被动语态:
This dessert is craved by many. = 许多人都渴望这种甜点。
His achievements are craved by many young people. = 他的成就被许多年轻人所渴望。

疑问句:
Do you crave anything specific? = 你渴望什么特别的东西吗？
What do they crave most in life? = 他们生活中最渴望什么？

否定句:
I do not crave power or wealth. = 我不渴望权力或财富。
She does not crave attention; she prefers quiet solitude. = 她不渴望关注；她更喜欢安静的独处。


PHRASE->
crave for something = 渴望某物
crave something intensely = 强烈渴望某物
crave attention = 渴望关注
crave excitement = 渴望刺激
crave success = 渴望成功
