despatch's:/dɪˈspætʃ/| n.|派遣；发送；急件；|The noun "despatch" refers to the act of sending something or someone somewhere quickly, often officially or urgently. It can also refer to the thing sent, such as a message or goods.  The possessive 's indicates that it belongs to or relates to a despatch.|例句：The company's despatch of the goods was delayed. 公司的货物发送延误了。 The general's despatch was received this morning. 将军的急件今天上午收到了。|近义词：sending, dispatch, transmission, shipment|反义词：receipt, arrival|名词


USAGE->

The company despatches goods daily. = 公司每天都发货。
He despatches emails promptly. = 他迅速发送邮件。
