memorial:/məˈmɔːriəl/| n. |纪念物，纪念馆，纪念活动 |A memorial is something that is created to remind people of a person or event, especially one that is important or significant.  It can be a physical object, such as a statue or building, or an event, such as a ceremony or service. |例句：The Lincoln Memorial is a magnificent monument in Washington D.C.  林肯纪念堂是华盛顿特区一座宏伟的纪念碑。


The town erected a memorial to those who died in the war.  这个镇为在战争中死难的人树立了一座纪念碑。|近义词：monument, tribute, commemoration |反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion
