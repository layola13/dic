flashbacks:/ˈflæʃbæks/| n.|闪回，倒叙|A flashback is a scene in a movie, play, book, etc., that shows events that happened in the past.  It interrupts the normal chronological order of the story.|例句：The movie used flashbacks to show the hero's childhood. 这部电影用闪回展现了英雄的童年。
例句：Her sudden outburst was triggered by a painful flashback to her childhood trauma. 她的突然爆发是由痛苦的童年创伤闪回引发的。|近义词：retrospection, reminiscence|反义词：flashforward
