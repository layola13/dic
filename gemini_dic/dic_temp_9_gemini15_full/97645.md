inheritors:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtərz/ | n. | 继承人，承受者 | Those who receive something, such as property or a title, from someone who has died.  | The inheritors of the estate were surprised by its size.  这笔遗产的继承人对其规模感到惊讶。 | heirs, successors | disinheritors, relinquishers | 名词
