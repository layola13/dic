ichthus:/ˈɪkθəs/| n. |鱼；基督教的象征|A Greek word meaning "fish," Ichthus became an early Christian symbol, representing Jesus Christ through an acrostic: Iēsous Christos Theou Huios Sōtēr (Ἰησοῦς Χριστὸς Θεοῦ Υἱὸς Σωτήρ) – Jesus Christ, Son of God, Savior.|The ichthus symbol was used by early Christians as a secret sign of recognition.  |近义词：fish, symbol|反义词：None|名词
