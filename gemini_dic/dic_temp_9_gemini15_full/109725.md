levelling-off: /ˈlevəlɪŋ ɒf/ | v. | 趋于平稳；稳定；达到平稳状态 |  To reach a state of equilibrium or stability after a period of increase or decrease.  It describes a process of becoming level or constant. |

例句：

After years of rapid growth, the company's profits are finally levelling off. = 多年快速增长后，公司的利润终于趋于稳定。

The temperature has been levelling off around 20 degrees Celsius. = 温度一直稳定在20摄氏度左右。


近义词：stabilizing, plateauing, flattening out, reaching a plateau

反义词：escalating, increasing, surging, rising


词性: 动词
