oat:/oʊt/| n. |燕麦，麦片|A type of cereal grain grown for its seed, which is also called oats.  Oats are commonly used to make oatmeal, porridge, and other foods. | Example:  I had a bowl of oatmeal for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一碗燕麦粥。

Example: The horses were fed a diet of oats and hay. 马的饲料是燕麦和干草。


近义词：None (no direct synonym, but other grains like barley or rye could be considered similar in some contexts)
反义词：None
