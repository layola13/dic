self-pictured:/ˈsɛlf ˈpɪktʃərd/| adj.| 自画像的；自己描绘的 |Describes something that is depicted or imagined by oneself.  It often implies a subjective and potentially inaccurate representation. |例句：Her self-pictured ideal life involved a large house and a loving family.  她自己描绘的理想生活包括一栋大房子和一个充满爱的家庭。

The novel presents a self-pictured version of the author's childhood. 这部小说呈现了作者对自己童年的一种自我描绘。|近义词：self-portrayed, imagined, envisioned |反义词：objectively depicted, realistically portrayed |词性：形容词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc.  Therefore, typical verb tense examples are not applicable.  Its usage is descriptive.
