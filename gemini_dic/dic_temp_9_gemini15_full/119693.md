midlife:/ˈmɪdlaɪf/| n.|中年；中年的时期|The period of a person's life from approximately age 40 to age 65.  It's a time of reflection, reassessment, and sometimes, crisis.  This stage of life is characterized by a variety of physical, emotional, and social changes.|例句：She experienced a midlife crisis and bought a sports car. 她经历了中年危机，买了一辆跑车。

例句：Many people re-evaluate their career paths during midlife. 许多人在中年重新评估自己的职业道路。


近义词：middle age, adulthood's second half|反义词：youth, adolescence, old age|词性：名词
