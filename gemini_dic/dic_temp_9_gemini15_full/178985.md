redeems:/rɪˈdiːmz/ | vt. |赎回；补偿；挽回；履行；兑换 | to buy back something that you have sold or pledged; to make something better; to fulfill a promise; to exchange a coupon or voucher for goods or services. |例句：He redeemed his watch from the pawn shop. 他从当铺赎回了他的手表。|近义词：recover, reclaim, atone, fulfill |反义词：forfeit, lose, squander


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redeems his promise every time. = 他每次都信守诺言。
The store redeems coupons for discounts. = 这家商店用优惠券兑换折扣。


一般过去时:
She redeemed her lost reputation with hard work. = 她通过努力工作挽回了她失去的名誉。
They redeemed their bonds at maturity. = 他们到期赎回了债券。


现在完成时:
He has redeemed himself after making a mistake. = 他犯错后已经改过自新了。
She has redeemed several coupons this week. = 她本周已经兑换了几张优惠券。


过去完成时:
He had redeemed his pledge before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经履行了他的承诺。
They had redeemed their shares before the market crashed. = 他们在市场崩盘前已经赎回了他们的股票。


将来时:
She will redeem her loyalty points for a free flight. = 她将用积分兑换免费机票。
He will redeem the situation with an apology. = 他将通过道歉来挽回局面。


虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he might redeem his failing grades. = 如果他更努力学习，他可能会挽回不及格的成绩。
I wish I could redeem the lost time. = 我希望我能弥补失去的时间。


被动语态:
The bonds will be redeemed at maturity. = 债券将在到期时被赎回。
His reputation was redeemed by his subsequent actions. = 他随后的行动挽回了他的名誉。


疑问句:
Can I redeem these points for a gift? = 我可以用这些积分兑换礼物吗？
Will the company redeem the defective product? = 公司会回收有缺陷的产品吗？


否定句:
He failed to redeem his promise. = 他没有履行他的诺言。
The company will not redeem expired coupons. = 公司不会兑换过期的优惠券。
