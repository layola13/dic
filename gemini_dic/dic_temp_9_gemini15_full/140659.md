ombudsmanships:/ˈɑːmbʊdzmənʃɪp/| n. |监察员职务；调解；申诉制度|The practice or role of an ombudsman.  It involves investigating complaints and mediating disputes to achieve fair and equitable resolutions.  |Example: The company's ombudsmanship program proved effective in resolving customer grievances. 公司的监察员制度在解决客户投诉方面被证明是有效的。|近义词：mediation, arbitration, advocacy |反义词：neglect, disregard
