dweebs:/duːbs/| n.|呆子，笨蛋|A dweeb is a socially awkward or inept person, often portrayed as nerdy or uncool.  The term is often used informally and can be considered mildly offensive.|例句：He's such a dweeb; he spends all his time playing video games. 他真是个呆子；他把所有时间都花在玩电子游戏中。|例句：Don't be such a dweeb, loosen up a little! 别那么呆板，放松一点！|近义词：nerd, geek, dork, square, klutz|反义词：cool, popular, sophisticated
