dungheap:/ˈdʌŋˌhip/| n. |粪堆；垃圾堆 |A dungheap is a pile of dung, typically from animals.  It is often associated with unpleasant smells and unsanitary conditions.  The word carries a strong negative connotation, suggesting something dirty, neglected, and worthless. |例句：The farmer spread the dungheap on the fields as fertilizer.  农夫将粪堆撒在田里作为肥料。

The children avoided playing near the smelly dungheap. 孩子们避免在臭烘烘的粪堆附近玩耍。
