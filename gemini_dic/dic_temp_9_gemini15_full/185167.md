retitles:/ˌriːˈtaɪtlz/ | v. | 重新命名；改名 | To give a new title to something, such as a book, film, or article.  |The author retitled his novel after receiving feedback from his editor. 作者在收到编辑的反馈后重新为他的小说命名。|rename, relabel |  keep the original title | verb
