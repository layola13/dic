reconducted:/ˌriːkənˈdʌktɪd/| v. |重新进行，再次举行 |To conduct again; to carry out or perform again.  The experiment was re-conducted to verify the results.  实验重新进行以验证结果。|近义词：repeated,restarted,renewed |反义词：concluded,finished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-conduct the experiment every year. = 他们每年都重新进行实验。
We re-conduct the survey to get more accurate data. = 我们重新进行调查以获得更准确的数据。


一般过去时:
The meeting was re-conducted after a short break. = 会议在短暂休息后重新开始。
The police re-conducted the investigation following new evidence. = 警方根据新的证据重新进行了调查。


现在完成时:
They have re-conducted the tests several times. = 他们已经进行了多次测试。
The company has re-conducted its marketing strategy. = 公司已经重新制定了其营销策略。


过去完成时:
They had re-conducted the experiment before publishing the findings. = 他们在发表研究结果之前已经重新进行了实验。
The team had re-conducted the analysis to ensure accuracy. = 团队为了确保准确性，已经重新进行了分析。


将来时:
We will re-conduct the interview next week. = 我们下周将重新进行面试。
They will re-conduct the safety inspection. = 他们将重新进行安全检查。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they would re-conduct the study. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们会重新进行研究。
It is essential that the experiment be re-conducted. = 至关重要的是要重新进行实验。


被动语态:
The survey was re-conducted by an independent agency. = 调查由一个独立机构重新进行。
The election was re-conducted due to irregularities. = 由于违规行为，选举重新举行。


疑问句:
Will the concert be re-conducted? = 音乐会还会重新举行吗？
Should the experiment be re-conducted? = 实验应该重新进行吗？


否定句:
They did not re-conduct the experiment. = 他们没有重新进行实验。
The company decided not to re-conduct the market research. = 公司决定不重新进行市场调研。


PHRASE->
re-conduct a study = 重新进行一项研究
re-conduct an experiment = 重新进行一项实验
re-conduct an election = 重新举行选举
re-conduct an orchestra = 重新指挥一个乐队 (though less common usage of "re-conduct" in this context)
re-conduct an interview = 重新进行一次面试
