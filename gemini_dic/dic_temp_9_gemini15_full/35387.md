co-operating:/koʊˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |合作的；协作的 |  The act of working together towards a common goal. It implies a mutual effort and shared responsibility. |例句：The team is co-operating well on the project.  团队正在项目中很好地合作。

例句：The two companies are co-operating to develop a new product. 两家公司正在合作开发新产品。


近义词：collaborating, working together, assisting, uniting

反义词：competing, opposing, hindering, obstructing

词性：动词 (现在分词形式)
