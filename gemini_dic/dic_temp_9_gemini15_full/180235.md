refractometrically:/ˌriːfræktoʊˈmɛtrɪkli/| adv.|折射测定地，用折射计法|By measuring the refractive index of a substance, its concentration or purity can be determined refractometrically.|通过测量物质的折射率，可以折射测定地确定其浓度或纯度。|近义词：optically|反义词：None


USAGE->

This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It describes *how* something is done. There are no tenses or voices to conjugate.  Example sentences illustrate its usage.


例句1: The sugar concentration in the solution was determined refractometrically.  =  溶液中的糖浓度是用折射计法测定的。

例句2:  The purity of the sample was assessed refractometrically using a precision refractometer. = 样品的纯度是用精密折射计折射测定法评估的。


PHRASE->

The word "refractometrically" is not typically used in phrases.  It's a standalone adverb describing a specific method of measurement.
