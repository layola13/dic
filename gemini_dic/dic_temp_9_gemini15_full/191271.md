scatterplot:/ˈskætərplɒt/ | n. | 散点图 | A scatter plot (or scatter graph) is a type of plot or mathematical diagram using Cartesian coordinates to display values for typically two variables for a set of data.  The data is displayed as a collection of points, each having the value of one variable determining the position on the horizontal axis and the value of the other variable determining the position on the vertical axis.  | Example: The scatter plot clearly showed a positive correlation between ice cream sales and temperature. 散点图清楚地显示了冰淇淋销量与温度之间的正相关关系。 | 近义词: scatter diagram, point graph | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but concepts like bar chart or histogram could be considered alternatives depending on the context) |  词性：名词
