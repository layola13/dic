ousters:/ˈaʊstərz/| n. |驱逐者；罢免者|The act of ousting someone from a position or place.  Those who are ousted from power often try to regain it. 驱逐某人，罢免某人。那些被赶下台的人常常试图重新夺回权力。|The protesters demanded the ouster of the corrupt officials. 抗议者要求罢免腐败官员。|removal, dismissal, expulsion|retention, appointment|名词
