prorogate:/ˈprɒrəɡeɪt/ /ˈproʊroʊɡeɪt/| vt.|延期；延长；传播；使蔓延|To prolong; to extend the duration of something.  It also means to spread or disseminate something, often something undesirable.|The parliament voted to prorogue the session until after the election.  议会投票决定将会议延期到选举之后。|The government attempted to prorogue the debate on the controversial bill. 政府试图延长对有争议的法案的辩论。|近义词：extend, prolong, disseminate, propagate|反义词：abridge, curtail, shorten|动词
