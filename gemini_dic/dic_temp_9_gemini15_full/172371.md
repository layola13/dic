puttier:/ˈpʌtiər/| n. |腻子;油灰;嵌缝剂|A substance used to fill holes or cracks, especially in wood or metal.  It's typically a paste-like consistency and hardens after application.|例句：He used a putty knife to apply the putty to the cracks in the window frame. 他用油灰刀将腻子涂抹在窗框的裂缝上。

例句：The carpenter filled the gaps in the woodwork with putty. 木匠用腻子填补木制品中的缝隙。


近义词：filler, sealant, mastic|反义词：None specifically|词性：名词
