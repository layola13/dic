joust:/dʒuːst/| n., v.|比武，战斗；争论|A joust is a contest between two knights on horseback, armed with lances.  It can also refer to a vigorous contest or dispute.|例句：The knights prepared for the joust.  骑士们准备比武。

The two candidates engaged in a fierce joust during the televised debate. 两位候选人在电视辩论中进行了激烈的争论。|近义词：combat, contest, tournament, dispute, argument|反义词：agreement, harmony, peace

词源:
The word "joust" comes from Old French *joster, meaning "to tilt," "to push," or "to strike."  This, in turn, is related to the Old French word *joute, meaning "a tournament."  The ultimate origin is likely Germanic.
