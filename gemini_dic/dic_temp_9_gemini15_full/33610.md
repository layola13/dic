cinchonic:/sɪnˈkɒnɪk/| adj.|金鸡纳霜的；奎宁的|Relating to or derived from cinchona bark, especially quinine.  The term often refers to the alkaloids found in cinchona bark, such as quinine, quinidine, cinchonine, and cinchonidine, which are known for their antimalarial properties.|The cinchonic alkaloids are effective in treating malaria.|近义词：quinine-derived|反义词：无
