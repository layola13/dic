prealloted:/ˌpriːˈælɒtɪd/| adj.|预先分配的，预先拨给的|Already allocated or assigned beforehand.  It implies a prior determination or distribution of resources, responsibilities, or tasks.|例句：The project team had prealloted tasks to each member before the official start date.  项目团队在正式启动日期之前已为每个成员预先分配了任务。

例句：Each participant was given a prealloted time slot for their presentation. 每个参与者都被分配了一个预先确定的时间段来进行他们的演示。


近义词：preassigned, predetermined, allocated in advance, previously assigned

反义词：unassigned, unallocated, impromptu


词性：形容词
