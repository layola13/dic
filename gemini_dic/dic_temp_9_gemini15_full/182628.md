reorchestrations:/ˌriːˈɔːrkɪstreɪʃənz/| n. |重新编排；改编；重新配器|The act of re-orchestrating a musical piece;  a new arrangement of a musical composition.  It involves changing the instrumentation, or the way the music is distributed among the instruments.|例句：The conductor oversaw several reorchestrations of the symphony before settling on the final version. 指挥在最终确定交响乐的版本之前，对它进行了多次重新编排。

The new album features several interesting reorchestrations of classic songs. 新专辑收录了几首经典歌曲的有趣改编版本。|近义词：rearrangements, adaptations, revisions|反义词：original compositions, first versions
