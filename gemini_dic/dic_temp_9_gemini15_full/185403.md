retrospections:/ˌrɛtrəˈspɛkʃənz/ | n. |回顾；反思；追忆 |The act of looking back on past events or experiences; a survey of past events.  |例句：His retrospections on his childhood were filled with both joy and sorrow. 他对童年岁月的回顾充满了喜悦和悲伤。

例句：Her retrospections of the project revealed several crucial mistakes that could have been avoided. 她对项目的回顾揭示了几处本可以避免的关键性错误。


近义词：recollections, reflections, reminiscences, reviews |反义词：prospections, anticipations |词性:名词
