parlour:/ˈpɑːrlər/ /ˈpɑːrlɚ/| n.|客厅；会客室；（尤指旧时）接待室；（英）理发店；（美）沙龙|A parlour is a room in a house, typically a comfortable room for receiving guests.  It can also refer to a shop providing a particular service, such as a hair parlour.  The meaning varies slightly between British and American English.|例句：We gathered in the parlour to listen to the music. 我们聚集在客厅里听音乐。

例句：She went to the hair parlour for a trim. 她去理发店修剪头发。|近义词：sitting room, living room, salon, shop|反义词：bedroom, kitchen, bathroom|名词
