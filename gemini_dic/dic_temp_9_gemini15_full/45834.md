customized:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 定制的，客户化的 |  Made or done to a particular person's or organization's requirements.  It implies a modification of a pre-existing product or service to meet specific needs. | 例句：The software is customized to meet our specific needs.  这个软件是根据我们的特定需求定制的。 | 例句：She ordered a customized cake for her daughter's birthday. 她为女儿的生日订了一个定制蛋糕。| 近义词：tailored, personalized, bespoke, made-to-order | 反义词：standard, generic, off-the-shelf


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company customizes software for its clients. = 公司为客户定制软件。
We customize our services to meet individual needs. = 我们定制服务以满足个人需求。

一般过去时:
They customized the car with new wheels and a spoiler. = 他们用新轮子和扰流板改装了汽车。
The tailor customized the suit to fit perfectly. = 裁缝定制了这套西装，使其非常合身。

现在完成时:
We have customized several websites for our clients this year. = 今年我们已经为客户定制了几个网站。
She has customized her phone with a unique case and apps. = 她用独特的手机壳和应用程序定制了她的手机。

过去完成时:
They had customized the program before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前就定制了程序。
The team had customized the report to address the specific questions. = 该团队已定制报告以解决具体问题。

将来时:
We will customize the database to improve efficiency. = 我们将定制数据库以提高效率。
They will customize the training program for the new employees. = 他们将为新员工定制培训计划。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would customize the design. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会定制设计。
I wish I could customize this software more easily. = 我希望我可以更容易地自定义这个软件。

被动语态:
The software is customized by a team of experts. = 该软件由专家团队定制。
The report was customized to meet the client's requirements. = 报告是根据客户的要求定制的。

疑问句:

Does the company offer customized solutions? = 公司是否提供定制解决方案？

否定句:
The product cannot be customized further. = 该产品无法进一步定制。
They did not customize the template for this project. = 他们没有为此项目定制模板。
