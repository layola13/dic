princeless:/ˈprɪnsləs/| adj.|无价的，极其珍贵的|invaluable, priceless, beyond price all mean having a value too high to be measured.  Priceless stresses the impossibility of assigning a monetary value. |例句：The antique vase is priceless. 这只古董花瓶无价。

The painting is priceless; it's a national treasure. 这幅画是无价之宝；它是国宝。


近义词： invaluable, priceless, beyond price, precious


反义词： worthless, valueless, cheap
