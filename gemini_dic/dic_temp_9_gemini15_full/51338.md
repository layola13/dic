detectible: /dɪˈtɛktəbəl/ | adj. | 可探测的，可察觉的 | capable of being detected or discovered.  Something that is detectable can be found or noticed, often using special equipment or methods. |

例句：

The leak was barely detectable, requiring specialized equipment to find.  泄漏几乎无法察觉，需要专门的设备才能找到。

The faint scent of smoke was detectable even from a distance.  即使在远处也能闻到一丝淡淡的烟味。


近义词：discoverable, perceivable, observable, noticeable

反义词：undetectable, imperceptible, indiscernible


词性：形容词
