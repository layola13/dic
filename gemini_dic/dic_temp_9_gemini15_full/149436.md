pact:/pækt/ | n. |条约，协定，契约 |A formal agreement between individuals or groups.  |例句：The two countries signed a peace pact.  两个国家签署了一项和平条约。

例句：They made a pact to help each other.他们约定互相帮助。|近义词：agreement, treaty, contract, covenant |反义词：disagreement, conflict


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The countries maintain a strong pact. = 这些国家保持着牢固的条约。
He keeps his pact with his friend. = 他遵守与朋友的约定。


一般过去时:
They broke the pact last year. = 他们去年违反了协议。
The nations signed a pact in 1945. = 各国在1945年签署了一项条约。


现在完成时:
They have honored their pact. = 他们履行了他们的约定。
The two companies have maintained their pact for many years. = 这两家公司多年来一直保持着他们的协议。


过去完成时:
They had already broken the pact before the war started. = 在战争开始之前，他们就已经违反了协议。
The government had secretly broken the pact. = 政府秘密地违反了协议。


将来时:
They will renew the pact next year. = 他们明年将续约。
The two leaders will sign a new pact soon. = 两位领导人很快就会签署一项新的协议。


虚拟语气:
If they had kept their pact, the war would not have happened. = 如果他们遵守了协议，战争就不会发生。
I wish they had honored the pact. = 我希望他们遵守了协议。


被动语态:
The pact was signed by both parties. = 该条约由双方签署。
The pact is being renegotiated. = 该条约正在重新谈判。


疑问句:
What are the terms of the pact? = 该条约的条款是什么？
Is there a pact between these two countries? = 这两个国家之间有条约吗？


否定句:
They did not sign the pact. = 他们没有签署该条约。
There was no pact between the warring factions. = 交战各方之间没有协议。


PHRASE->
strike a pact = 达成协议

keep a pact = 遵守协议
