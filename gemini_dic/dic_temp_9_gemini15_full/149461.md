paddler:/ˈpædlər/| n. |划桨者，划船者|A person who paddles a boat or kayak.  |例句：The paddler expertly navigated the rapids.  这位划桨者熟练地驾驭激流。|例句：Experienced paddlers know how to handle challenging conditions.  经验丰富的划桨者知道如何应对具有挑战性的条件。|近义词：kayaker, canoeist, rower |反义词：None specifically.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The paddler paddles the canoe smoothly. = 划桨者平稳地划着独木舟。
He paddles across the lake every morning. = 他每天早上划船穿过湖泊。


一般过去时:
The paddler paddled the kayak swiftly. = 划桨者快速地划着皮划艇。
She paddled to the island yesterday. = 她昨天划船去了岛上。


现在完成时:
The paddler has paddled across the ocean. = 这位划桨者已经划过了海洋。
He has paddled many rivers in his life. = 他一生划过许多河流。


过去完成时:

She had paddled further than she expected. = 她划得比她预期的更远。
