merestone:/ˈmɛrɪstoʊn/| n. |界石，里程碑|A merestone is a stone set up as a boundary marker, often indicating distances along a road or path.  It can also be used figuratively to represent a significant point or stage in a process or journey.|例句：The old merestone marked the border between the two counties.  这块古老的界石标志着两个郡的边界。  The completion of the project felt like a merestone in my career.  项目的完成感觉像是我职业生涯中的一个里程碑。|近义词：milestone, landmark, boundary marker|反义词：无
