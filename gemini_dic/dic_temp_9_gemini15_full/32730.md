chloroform:/ˈklɔːrəfɔːrm/| n. |氯仿|A colorless, volatile, sweet-smelling liquid that was once used as an anesthetic but is now largely replaced due to its toxicity and carcinogenic properties. It is also used as a solvent and in the synthesis of other chemicals. |例句：Chloroform was once a common anesthetic, but its use has been largely discontinued due to safety concerns. 氯仿曾是一种常见的麻醉剂，但由于安全问题，其使用已基本停止。|近义词：anesthetic, solvent|反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "chloroform" comes from the combination of "chloro-" (from chlorine) and "form" (referring to formic acid, a related chemical compound).  It reflects the chemical structure containing chlorine atoms.
