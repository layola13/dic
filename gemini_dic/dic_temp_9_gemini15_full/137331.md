nontariff:/ˌnɒnˈtærɪf/| adj.|非关税的|Non-tariff barriers refer to trade restrictions that countries use to limit imports without imposing tariffs.|例句：Many countries use nontariff barriers to protect their domestic industries.  许多国家使用非关税壁垒来保护其国内产业。|近义词：nontax, duty-free |反义词：tariff


英文解析:  The adjective "nontariff" means relating to or denoting trade restrictions that do not involve tariffs (taxes on imports or exports). These barriers can take various forms, such as quotas, embargoes, sanitary and phytosanitary regulations, and technical barriers to trade.  They are used by governments to control the flow of goods and services into and out of their countries.


例句：

The government implemented new nontariff barriers to restrict the import of foreign cars. 政府实施了新的非关税壁垒以限制外国汽车的进口。


The WTO aims to reduce both tariff and nontariff barriers to trade. 世贸组织的目标是减少关税和非关税贸易壁垒。


近义词：

trade restrictive, protectionist (in context)


反义词：

tariff-based, tariff-related


词性：

adjective
