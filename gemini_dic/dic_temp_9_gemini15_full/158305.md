例句：The city's vibrant playgoing scene attracts tourists from around the world. 这个城市的繁荣的戏剧氛围吸引了来自世界各地的游客。


近义词：theatregoing, attending plays | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to activities opposing theatre attendance, such as staying home, reading, etc.|词性：名词
