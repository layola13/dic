inculcation:/ˌɪnˌkʌlˈkeɪʃən/| n. |灌输；谆谆教诲|The process of instilling an idea, attitude, or habit by persistent instruction.  It emphasizes repeated teaching and reinforcement to embed something firmly in someone's mind. |Example: The inculcation of moral values begins in childhood.  灌输道德价值观始于童年。The school's rigorous curriculum involved the inculcation of discipline. 学校严格的课程包括灌输纪律。|近义词：indoctrination, instillation, implantation|反义词：eradication, elimination|名词
