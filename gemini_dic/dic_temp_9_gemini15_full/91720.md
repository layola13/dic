hypercriticise: /ˌhaɪpəˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/ | vt. | 吹毛求疵地批评；过分批评 | To criticize someone or something severely and often unfairly.  |例句：He tends to hypercriticise everything, making it difficult to work with him. 他总是吹毛求疵地批评一切，这使得和他一起工作很困难。

例句：Don't hypercriticise her work; she's still learning. 不要过分批评她的作品；她还在学习。


近义词：criticize harshly, faultfind, nitpick, overcriticize


反义词：praise, commend, applaud


词源：hyper- (excessive) + criticize (to find fault with)
