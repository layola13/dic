godfathers:/ˈɡɑːdfɑːðərz/| n. |教父；教父们|A godfather is a man who acts as a sponsor at a baptism, promising to guide the child in their faith.  The term can also refer to a powerful and influential figure in a criminal organization.|例句：He was the godfather of his niece. 他是他侄女的教父。

The mafia boss was known as the godfather of the city.  这位黑帮老大被称为这座城市的教父。|近义词：sponsor, patron, protector |反义词：godmother (女教母)
