elses: /ˈɛlsɪz/ | pron. | 其他人的; 别的东西 |  The word "elses" is the plural possessive form of "else," meaning "other" or "different." It is used to refer to things or people other than those already mentioned.  | Example:  Nobody else's opinion matters. = 其他人的意见都不重要。  The others' opinions are irrelevant. = 其他人的观点无关紧要。 |近义词: others', other people's | 反义词:  one's own, self's
