/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərz/ | v. | 超过；胜过；比…更好 | To surpass or exceed in measure, quantity, or extent.  It implies a significant difference. | The new building outmeasures the old one in both size and elegance. 新建筑在规模和优雅方面都超过了旧建筑。 | surpass, exceed, excel, outstrip | fall short of, be inferior to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new plan outmeasures the old one in efficiency. = 新计划在效率上超过了旧计划。
This strategy outmeasures all others in terms of profitability. = 就盈利能力而言，此策略胜过所有其他策略。


一般过去时:
Last year's sales outmeasured our projections. = 去年销售额超过了我们的预期。
His performance outmeasured everyone else's in the competition. = 他在比赛中的表现超过了其他所有人。


现在完成时:
The team has outmeasured its previous records. = 该团队已经超过了之前的记录。
Our efforts have outmeasured expectations in terms of positive impact. = 就积极影响而言，我们的努力已经超出了预期。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, we had outmeasured our initial budget. = 到项目结束时，我们已经超过了最初的预算。
They had outmeasured their rivals in terms of market share before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们在市场份额方面已经超过了竞争对手。


将来时:
We anticipate that our new product will outmeasure the competition. = 我们预计我们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。
The upcoming event is expected to outmeasure all previous ones in attendance. = 预计即将举行的活动将超过以往所有活动的出席人数。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, our results would have outmeasured the current ones. = 如果我们投资更多，我们的结果就会超过目前的水平。
I wish our progress had outmeasured the challenges we faced. = 我希望我们的进步能够超过我们面临的挑战。


被动语态:
The previous record was outmeasured by the new team. = 新团队超过了之前的记录。
The initial estimations were outmeasured by the actual results. = 实际结果超过了最初的估计。


疑问句:
Does this design outmeasure the previous one in functionality? = 这个设计在功能上是否超过了之前的那个？
Will the new technology outmeasure its predecessors in performance? = 新技术在性能方面是否会超过其前身？


否定句:
The company's growth did not outmeasure its rivals. = 该公司的增长没有超过竞争对手。
His efforts did not outmeasure the obstacles he faced. = 他的努力没有克服他面临的障碍。
