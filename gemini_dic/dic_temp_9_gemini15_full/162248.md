pre-tertiary: /ˌpriːˈtɜːʃəri/ | adj. | 第三纪以前的；前第三纪的 |  Referring to the geological period before the Tertiary period, encompassing the Paleozoic and Mesozoic eras.  It's primarily used in geology and paleontology to describe rocks, fossils, or events that predate the Tertiary. | Example:  Pre-tertiary sedimentary rocks are found in many parts of the world. = 世界许多地方都发现了第三纪以前的沉积岩。  | 近义词: Paleozoic, Mesozoic | 反义词: Tertiary, Quaternary | 词性: adjective
