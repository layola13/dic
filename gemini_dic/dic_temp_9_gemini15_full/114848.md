mammifer:/ˈmæmɪfər/| n. |哺乳动物|A mammal is a vertebrate animal characterized by the possession of mammary glands in females, which secrete milk for the nourishment of their young.  They are also typically characterized by hair or fur, three middle ear bones, and a neocortex (a region of the brain).|例句：Whales, dolphins, and humans are all mammals. 鲸鱼、海豚和人类都是哺乳动物。

例句：Cats, dogs, and horses are common examples of mammals found around the world. 猫、狗和马是世界各地常见的哺乳动物。
