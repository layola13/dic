ri:/riː/| n. |里；英里；河流；(诗歌)河；(复数) 区域；范围 | a unit of linear measure equal to 5280 feet or 1760 yards; a natural stream of water larger than a brook; a river; a region or area |例句：The river flows to the sea. 这条河流向大海。The distance is five ri. 距离是五里。|近义词：mile, stream, area, region |反义词：point, spot
