devolution:/ˌdɛvəˈluːʃən/| n. |权力下放；移交；衰退；退化 |The transfer or delegation of power to a lower level, especially by central government to local or regional administration.  It can also refer to a decline or deterioration in a process or system. |例句：The devolution of power to regional governments has led to increased autonomy. 权力下放给地方政府导致了更大的自主权。|例句：The devolution of the empire was a gradual process. 帝国的衰落是一个渐进的过程。|近义词：transfer, delegation, decentralization |反义词：centralization, concentration
