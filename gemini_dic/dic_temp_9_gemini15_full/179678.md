reenlarging: /ˌriːɪnˈlɑːrdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 再扩大；重新扩大 | The act of enlarging something again, or making something larger than it was previously.  It implies a prior enlarging process. | 例句：The company is reenlarging its factory to meet the increased demand. 公司正在扩大工厂以满足日益增长的需求。  The photographer decided to reenlarge the print to better showcase the details. 摄影师决定重新放大照片以更好地展示细节。| 近义词：expanding, extending, augmenting | 反义词：reducing, shrinking, diminishing | 动词
