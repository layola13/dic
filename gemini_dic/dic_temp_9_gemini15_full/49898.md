demurring: /dɪˈmɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 提出异议；反对；犹豫；迟疑 |  Expressing doubt or opposition; hesitating or objecting. |

例句：

He demurred at the suggestion, saying it was too risky. = 他对这个建议表示反对，说风险太大。

She demurred slightly before accepting the invitation. = 她接受邀请前略微犹豫了一下。


近义词：objecting, protesting, hesitating, resisting, balking

反义词：agreeing, consenting, accepting


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He demurs to every new proposal. = 他对每一个新的提议都表示反对。
She demurs when asked to work overtime. = 当被要求加班时，她犹豫了。

现在完成时:
They have demurred at signing the contract. = 他们一直对签署合同犹豫不决。
He has demurred on several occasions. = 他在好几个场合都表示反对。


过去完成时:
She had demurred at the initial proposal, but later agreed. = 她最初对提案表示反对，但后来同意了。
He had demurred before, so they weren't surprised by his objection. = 他以前就反对过，所以他们对他的反对并不感到惊讶。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
