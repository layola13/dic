camoys:/ˈkæmoɪz/| n.|沙普鹿|A camoy is a type of deer, specifically the chamois.  It is known for its agility and ability to climb steep, rocky terrain.|Example: The chamois leaped gracefully across the chasm. 沙普鹿优雅地跃过深谷。|近义词：chamois,  goat-antelope|反义词：无
