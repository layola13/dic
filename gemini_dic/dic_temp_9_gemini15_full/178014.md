reckless:/ˈrɛklɪs/| adj.|鲁莽的，不顾后果的|Showing a lack of care for the consequences of an action; heedless.  It implies a disregard for danger or safety. |例句：His reckless driving led to an accident.  他的鲁莽驾驶导致了一起事故。

例句：She showed reckless abandon in her spending. 她花钱大手大脚，毫无节制。


近义词：rash, careless, negligent, heedless, impulsive, foolhardy

反义词：cautious, careful, prudent, wary, circumspect


词源:  The word "reckless" originates from the Old English word "rēccelēas," which combines "rēcce" (care) and "lēas" (without, lacking).  Therefore, its literal meaning is "without care."  The modern spelling and meaning evolved over time.
