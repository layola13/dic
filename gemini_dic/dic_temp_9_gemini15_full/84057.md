half-formed:/ˈhɑːf fɔːrmd/ | adj. | 半形成的；未完全发展的；不成熟的 |  Partially developed or formed; incomplete or rudimentary.  It implies a lack of completeness or finality, suggesting something is in a preliminary stage. |


例句：

The idea was still half-formed in his mind.  他的想法在他脑海中还只是半形成的。

He expressed a half-formed thought about the future. 他表达了一个关于未来的不成熟的想法。


近义词：incomplete, undeveloped, rudimentary, sketchy, embryonic

反义词：complete, fully developed, mature, finished, perfected


词性：形容词
