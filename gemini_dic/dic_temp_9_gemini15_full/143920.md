outproduced:/ˌaʊtprəˈduːst/| v. |超过产量，产量超过|To produce more of something than someone or something else.|例句：Our factory outproduced its rivals by 15%. 我们的工厂产量比竞争对手高出15%。|例句：Last year, the company outproduced its previous record by 20%.去年，该公司产量比之前的记录高出20%。|近义词：surpassed, exceeded, surpassed in production|反义词：underproduced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory outproduces its competitors. = 这个工厂的产量超过它的竞争对手。
She consistently outproduces her colleagues. = 她始终比她的同事生产得多。

一般过去时:
Last year, the farm outproduced its quota. = 去年，这个农场超额完成了它的配额。
The team outproduced their rivals in the final game. = 在决赛中，这个团队的产量超过了他们的对手。

现在完成时:
We have outproduced our targets this quarter. = 本季度我们已经超额完成了目标。
They have outproduced their competitors for many years. = 多年来，他们的产量一直超过竞争对手。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the company had outproduced its initial projections. = 到年底，该公司已经超过了最初的预测。
The artist had outproduced his previous works by a considerable margin. =这位艺术家之前的作品已经有了相当大的进步。

将来时:
We will outproduce our competitors if we invest in new technology. = 如果我们投资新技术，我们将超过我们的竞争对手。
The company expects to outproduce its previous year's output. =该公司预计将超过去年的产量。


被动语态:
Their production was outproduced by a newer, more efficient factory. = 他们的产量被一家更新、更高效的工厂超过了。
The small company was outproduced by the larger corporation. = 这家小公司被大公司超过了。

疑问句:
Did the team outproduce their opponents? = 这支队伍的产量超过了他们的对手吗？
Has the factory outproduced its quota for the month? = 这个工厂本月的产量超过了配额吗？

否定句:
The small business didn't outproduce the larger corporation. = 这家小企业没有超过大公司。
The farmer did not outproduce his neighbor this year. = 今年这位农民没有超过他的邻居。


PHRASE->

outproduce the previous year = 超过上一年产量
outproduce rivals = 超过竞争对手
outproduce expectations = 超出预期
