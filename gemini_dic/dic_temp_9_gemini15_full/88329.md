hippocampi:/ˌhɪpəˈkæmpaɪ/| n. |海马体（复数）|The hippocampi are paired, elongated structures located within the medial temporal lobe of the brain. They play a crucial role in the formation of new memories and spatial navigation.  | Example:  Studies have shown that damage to the hippocampi can lead to significant memory impairment.  研究表明，海马体受损会导致严重的记忆障碍。|近义词：None (as it's a specific anatomical structure)|反义词：None (as it's a specific anatomical structure)|名词
