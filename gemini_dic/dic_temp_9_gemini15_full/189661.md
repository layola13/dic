sallying:/ˈsælɪŋ/| v.| 出击；突袭；尝试；| A sudden attack or sortie.  The act of making a sally. |例句：The soldiers made a sally against the enemy's position. 士兵们向敌人的阵地发起了突袭。

例句：He made a sally into the unknown world of freelance writing. 他尝试涉足未知的自由撰稿人的世界。


近义词：attack, sortie, foray, raid, rush


反义词：retreat, withdrawal, defense


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sallying forth into the fray. = 他正在冲进战斗。
She sallys out to meet challenges. = 她勇敢地迎接挑战。


一般过去时:
The knights sallied forth from the castle. = 骑士们从城堡里冲了出来。
They sallied into battle with great courage. = 他们勇敢地投入战斗。


现在完成时:
The army has sallied out to defend the city. = 军队已经出击保卫城市。
She has sallied forth on many adventures. = 她已经经历了许多冒险。


过去完成时:
The troops had sallied forth before dawn. = 部队在黎明前就已出击。
They had sallied into the forest in search of food. = 他们曾进入森林寻找食物。


将来时:
The general will sally with his troops at sunrise. = 将军将在日出时率领他的军队出击。
They will sally into the market with their new product. = 他们将带着他们的新产品进军市场。


虚拟语气:
If he sallied forth, he might succeed. = 如果他出击，他可能会成功。
I wish I had sallied forth when I had the chance. = 我真希望我当时有机会就出击了。


被动语态:
The city was sallied against by the enemy. = 城市遭到敌人的攻击。
The position was rarely sallied from. = 这个位置很少有人从这里出击。


疑问句:
Will they sally against the enemy? = 他们会攻击敌人吗？
Did the soldiers sally from their trenches? = 士兵们从战壕里冲出来了吗？


否定句:
The army did not sally forth that day. = 军队那天没有出击。
He refused to sally into the dangerous territory. = 他拒绝进入危险的地区。


PHRASE->
sally forth = 出发；出击
sally into = 尝试；涉足
make a sally = 发起攻击；突袭
sally against = 攻击；袭击
a daring sally = 大胆的突袭


词源:
源自古法语 *salir，意思是“跳跃，跳出”，最终源自拉丁语 *salire，意思是“跳跃”。  这反映了“sally”作为军事行动中突然冲锋或突袭的含义。
