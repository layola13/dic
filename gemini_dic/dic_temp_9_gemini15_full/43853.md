crescendo:/krɪˈʃɛndoʊ/| n. v.|渐强；逐渐增强|A gradual increase in loudness, force, or intensity.|The orchestra built to a crescendo at the end of the movement. 乐队在乐章结尾处逐渐增强音量。  The tension in the room built to a crescendo. 屋里的紧张气氛越来越浓。|近义词：augmentation, intensification|反义词：decrescendo, diminuendo


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The music crescendos beautifully. = 音乐渐强得很美。
The tension crescendos throughout the play. = 全剧的紧张气氛逐渐增强。

一般过去时:
The sound crescendoed to a deafening roar. = 声音渐强到震耳欲聋的咆哮。
The argument crescendoed until they were shouting. = 争吵越来越激烈，直到他们开始喊叫。

现在完成时:
The protests have crescendoed into a full-blown revolution. = 抗议活动已经发展成一场全面的革命。
The band's popularity has crescendoed over the years. = 乐队的名气多年来一直在上升。

过去完成时:
The applause had crescendoed before the speaker even finished. = 演讲者还没说完，掌声就达到了高潮。
The storm had crescendoed in intensity before it finally broke. = 暴风雨在最终爆发前，强度已经增强了。

将来时:
The negotiations will crescendo in the coming weeks. = 谈判将在未来几周达到高潮。
The volume will crescendo as the song progresses. = 歌曲进行时，音量会逐渐增强。

虚拟语气:
If the tension crescendoed any further, the situation would have become volatile. = 如果紧张局势进一步升级，情况就会变得不稳定。
I wish the music hadn't crescendoed so suddenly; it startled me. = 我真希望音乐不要突然渐强，那样吓到我了。


被动语态:
The climax was crescendoed masterfully by the conductor. = 高潮被指挥家出色地处理得渐强。
The effect was crescendoed to create a sense of drama. = 效果被处理成渐强，以营造戏剧感。


疑问句:
Does the music crescendo at the end? = 音乐在结尾处渐强吗？
Will the conflict crescendo into a war? = 冲突会升级成战争吗？


否定句:
The music didn't crescendo as expected. = 音乐没有像预期的那样渐强。
The intensity did not crescendo throughout the performance. = 演出过程中强度没有逐渐增强。
