kabbalism:/ˈkæbəlɪzəm/| n.|卡巴拉主义，犹太神秘主义|The esoteric system of Jewish mysticism concerned with the mystical interpretation of the Bible and the nature of God.  It involves complex symbolic systems and rituals aimed at achieving spiritual enlightenment. |Example: The study of Kabbalism requires a deep understanding of Hebrew and Jewish tradition.  卡巴拉主义的研究需要对希伯来语和犹太传统有深刻的理解。|近义词：Jewish mysticism, esotericism|反义词：rationalism, materialism
