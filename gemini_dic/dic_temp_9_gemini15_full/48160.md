decommission:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃən/| vt.| 退役；使报废；停止使用 | To officially stop using something, especially a building, machine, or weapon,  or to remove someone from a job.  |例句：The power plant was decommissioned after 30 years of operation. 这座发电厂运行30年后被退役。

The old warship was decommissioned and turned into a museum. 这艘老军舰退役后变成了博物馆。


近义词：retire, disband, scrap, phase out |反义词：commission, activate, launch


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They decommission old equipment regularly. = 他们定期报废旧设备。
The company decommissiones its outdated software. = 这家公司淘汰其过时的软件。


一般过去时:
The government decommissioned the nuclear reactor. = 政府关闭了核反应堆。
They decommissioned the bridge after the inspection.  = 检查后，他们拆除了这座桥。


现在完成时:
They have decommissioned several oil rigs this year. = 他们今年已经退役了几座石油钻井平台。
The army has decommissioned many of its older tanks. = 军队已经淘汰了许多旧坦克。


过去完成时:
The plant had been decommissioned before the new regulations came into effect. = 新规生效前，工厂就已经退役了。
They had decommissioned the satellite before the mission ended. = 任务结束前，他们已经使卫星退役。


将来时:
They will decommission the dam next year. = 他们明年将拆除这座水坝。
The factory will be decommissioned in the near future. = 这家工厂不久将被关闭。


虚拟语气:
If they decommissioned the power plant, it would save money. = 如果他们关闭发电厂，就能省钱。
It would be better if they decommissioned the outdated machines. = 如果他们淘汰过时的机器会更好。


被动语态:
The old aircraft carrier was decommissioned last year. = 这艘老航母去年退役了。
The factory is being decommissioned due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，工厂正在被关闭。


疑问句:
When will the ship be decommissioned? = 这艘船什么时候退役？

否定句:
They did not decommission the weapon. = 他们没有使武器退役。
The old building has not yet been decommissioned. = 这座旧楼尚未退役。
