furniture:/ˈfɜːrnɪtʃər/|/ˈfɝːnɪtʃər/| n. |家具|Movable articles, such as chairs, tables, beds, etc., that are used in furnishing a house or other building.|The furniture in her apartment was modern and stylish.|近义词：furnishings, fittings|反义词：None
