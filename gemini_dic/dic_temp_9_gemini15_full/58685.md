dumpage:/ˈdʌmpɪdʒ/| n. |倾倒；垃圾堆；倾倒物|The act of dumping something, especially waste material.  It can also refer to the material that has been dumped.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying irresponsible disposal.|例句：The illegal dumpage of toxic waste contaminated the river.  非法倾倒有毒废物污染了河流。The roadside was littered with dumpage from passing cars. 路边散落着过往车辆倾倒的垃圾。|近义词：dumping, disposal, refuse, waste|反义词：recycling, reclamation, conservation
