rainbows: /ˈreɪnboʊz/ | n. |彩虹 | A meteorological phenomenon that is caused by reflection, refraction and dispersion of light in water droplets resulting in a spectrum of light appearing in the sky. It is typically seen after rain.  |  I saw a beautiful rainbow after the storm. 我在暴风雨后看到了一道美丽的彩虹。 |  arc, spectrum |  drought, darkness | 名词
