simulcast:/ˈsɪmjʊlˌkæst/| n. v.|同步广播，同时播放|The simultaneous broadcasting of a program on television and radio.  A simulcast allows viewers and listeners to experience the same content at the same time, regardless of their preferred medium.|例句：The concert was simulcast on both television and radio. 这场音乐会在电视和广播上同时播放。 The company will simulcast the event across multiple platforms.该公司将在多个平台上同时播放该活动。|近义词： simultaneous broadcast, simultaneous transmission |反义词： delayed broadcast, separate broadcast
