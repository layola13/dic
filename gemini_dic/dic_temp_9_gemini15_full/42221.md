counselling: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ | n. |咨询，辅导 | The act of giving advice and support to someone.  It often involves helping individuals cope with emotional, psychological, or social problems. | Example: She sought counselling to deal with her anxiety. 她寻求咨询来应对她的焦虑。 |近义词：advising, guidance, therapy, mentoring | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting |  名词
