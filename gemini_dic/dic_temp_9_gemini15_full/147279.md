overmixed:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪkst/ | adj. | 过度混合的 |  Describes something that has been mixed for too long or too vigorously, resulting in a less desirable texture or outcome.  This is often used in the context of cooking or baking, where overmixing can lead to tough baked goods or a less desirable consistency.  | Example: The cake batter was overmixed, resulting in a tough and dense cake.  蛋糕糊搅拌过度，导致蛋糕又硬又密。 |近义词: excessively mixed, overbeaten | 反义词: undermixed
