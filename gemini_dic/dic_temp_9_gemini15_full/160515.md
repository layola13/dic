poriferan:/ˈpɒrɪfərən/| n. |多孔动物|A poriferan is any member of the phylum Porifera, which includes sponges.  Sponges are simple, multicellular animals that lack true tissues and organs. They are characterized by their porous bodies, through which water flows, bringing in food and oxygen. | Example:  Sponges are poriferans, and they are the simplest multicellular animals.  多孔动物是海绵，它们是最简单的多细胞动物。|近义词:sponge (in this context)|反义词:None specifically, but could contrast with more complex animals.  |名词
