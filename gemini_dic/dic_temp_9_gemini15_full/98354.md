insist:/ɪnˈsɪst/ | vt. | 坚持，坚决主张 | To demand something forcefully, refusing to be persuaded otherwise.  |例句：He insisted on paying for the meal. 他坚持要付饭钱。|例句：She insisted that she was innocent. 她坚持说自己是无辜的。|近义词：urge, demand, maintain | 反义词：yield, concede, give in | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He insists on his rights. = 他坚持自己的权利。
They insist that the project is feasible. = 他们坚持这个项目是可行的。

一般过去时:
She insisted on seeing the manager. = 她坚持要见经理。
He insisted that he was telling the truth. = 他坚持说他讲的是实话。

现在完成时:
They have insisted on a full investigation. = 他们一直坚持要进行全面调查。
She has insisted that we should attend the meeting. = 她一直坚持我们应该参加会议。

过去完成时:
He had insisted that the report was accurate. = 他之前一直坚持报告是准确的。
They had insisted on their terms before the negotiation. = 谈判前他们一直坚持自己的条件。

将来时:
I will insist on getting a refund. = 我将坚持获得退款。
They will insist that the contract be revised. = 他们将坚持修改合同。

虚拟语气:
I insisted that he be present. = 我坚持他必须在场。(should be present)
She insisted that she had not done it. = 她坚持说她没有做过。(had not done it)

被动语态:
It was insisted that the rules be followed. = 人们坚持必须遵守规则。
The importance of the issue was insisted upon by the experts. = 专家们强调了这个问题的重要性。

疑问句:
Does he insist on his own way? = 他坚持己见吗？
Will they insist on a written agreement? = 他们会坚持签订书面协议吗？

否定句:
He did not insist on his opinion. = 他没有坚持自己的意见。
They didn't insist that we change our plans. = 他们没有坚持让我们改变计划。


PHRASE->
insist on/upon: 坚持
insist that: 坚决认为
insistently: 坚持地
cannot insist enough: 再怎么强调也不为过
insist on doing something: 坚持做某事
