computationally:/ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənəli/ | adj. | 计算上地；从计算角度来看 |  Relating to or involving computation.  This adjective describes something that is related to or involves the process of computation, often referring to the feasibility, efficiency, or complexity of a task from a computational perspective. | The problem is computationally expensive.  这个问题计算成本很高。

The algorithm is computationally intractable.  这个算法在计算上是难以处理的。


近义词：calculatively,  computationally intensive


反义词：manually, intuitively


词性：形容词
