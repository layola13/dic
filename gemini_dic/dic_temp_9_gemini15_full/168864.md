profanation:/ˌproʊfəˈneɪʃən/| n. |亵渎，玷污 |The act of treating something sacred or revered with contempt or disrespect; violating or abusing something considered holy or inviolable.  |例句：The graffiti on the church wall was a blatant profanation. 教堂墙壁上的涂鸦是对神圣的公然亵渎。

例句：The destruction of the ancient temple was considered a terrible profanation.  毁坏古老的寺庙被认为是可怕的亵渎行为。


近义词：desecration, defilement, violation, abuse, contamination


反义词：reverence, sanctification, consecration


词性: noun
