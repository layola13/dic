eternality:/ˌiːtərˈnælɪti/| n. |永恒；不朽；永久 |The state or quality of being eternal; endless or infinite duration.  It often refers to something existing outside of time or lasting forever.  The concept is often associated with spiritual or religious beliefs. |例句：The painting evoked a sense of serenity and eternality. 这幅画唤起了一种宁静和永恒的感觉。

例句：Many religions promise eternality of the soul after death. 许多宗教都承诺灵魂死后不朽。


近义词：perpetuity, immortality, infinity, timelessness |反义词：mortality, transience, temporality |词性:名词
