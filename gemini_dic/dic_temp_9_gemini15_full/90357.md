hovercraft:/ˈhʌvəkræft/| n.|气垫船|A hovercraft is a vehicle that travels over water or land on a cushion of air.  It uses powerful fans to create a high-pressure air cushion beneath its hull, lifting it above the surface and allowing it to move at high speeds. |例句：The hovercraft skimmed effortlessly across the water. 气垫船毫不费力地掠过水面。

例句：We took a hovercraft trip to the island. 我们乘气垫船去岛上旅行了。


近义词：air-cushion vehicle| 反义词：conventional boat, traditional ship


词源:The word "hovercraft" is a blend of "hover" (to remain suspended in the air) and "craft" (a vessel or vehicle).  The term emerged in the mid-20th century with the development of this new type of vehicle.
