cusk:/kʌsk/| n.|鼠尾鳕，黑鳕|A cusk is a type of deep-sea fish belonging to the family Brosmidae.  They are characterized by their elongated bodies, relatively large mouths, and a single barbel under their chin.  They are found in the Atlantic Ocean and are commercially fished.|Example:  The fisherman caught a large cusk in his net.  渔民在网里捕到了一条大鼠尾鳕。|近义词：brotula (a related fish)|反义词：N/A|名词
