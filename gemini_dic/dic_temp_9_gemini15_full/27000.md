calculated:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  计算出的；蓄意的；故意的 | Carefully planned or devised; deliberate.  |例句：The attack was a calculated move to destabilize the government.  这次袭击是旨在破坏政府稳定的一场蓄谋已久的行动。 |例句：The calculated risk paid off handsomely.  经过深思熟虑的风险获得了丰厚的回报。|近义词：deliberate, premeditated, intentional |反义词：accidental, unintentional, spontaneous |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The computer calculated the answer in a second. = 电脑在一秒钟内计算出了答案。
She calculated the total cost of the project. = 她计算了项目的总成本。

一般过去时:
He calculated the distance between the two cities. = 他计算了两个城市之间的距离。
They carefully calculated their next move. = 他们仔细地计算了他们的下一步行动。

现在完成时:
I have calculated the area of the circle. = 我已经计算出了圆形的面积。
She has calculated the budget for the year. = 她已经计算出了今年的预算。

过去完成时:
We had calculated the time needed to complete the task. = 我们已经计算出完成任务所需的时间。
They had calculated the trajectory before launching the rocket. = 他们发射火箭前已经计算好了弹道。

将来时:
I will calculate the results once I have all the data. = 一旦我有了所有数据，我就会计算结果。
The engineers will calculate the structural integrity of the bridge. = 工程师们将计算桥梁的结构完整性。

虚拟语气:
If I had calculated correctly, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我计算正确的话，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had calculated the travel time more accurately. = 我希望我更准确地计算了旅行时间。

被动语态:
The area was calculated using a complex formula. = 面积是用一个复杂的公式计算出来的。
The risks involved were carefully calculated by the team. = 相关的风险是由团队仔细计算的。

疑问句:
Have you calculated the total yet? = 你计算出总额了吗？
How was the profit margin calculated? = 利润率是如何计算的？

否定句:
I didn't calculate the tax correctly. = 我没有正确计算税款。
They hadn't calculated the potential consequences. = 他们没有计算潜在的后果。
