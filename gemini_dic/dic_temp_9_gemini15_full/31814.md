checks-out: /tʃɛks aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 符合；说得通；可信；被接受 | This refers to something being believable, valid, or acceptable. It can imply verification or confirmation.  |  例句：His story checks out. = 他的说法可信。 His alibi checks out. = 他的不在场证明站得住脚。| 近义词：verifies, confirms, is plausible, is credible | 反义词：is dubious, is implausible, is unbelievable |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His explanation checks out. = 他的解释是合理的。
The information checks out with our records. = 该信息与我们的记录相符。

一般过去时:
The detective's theory checked out. = 侦探的理论得到了证实。
Her story checked out with the witnesses. = 她的说法得到了证人的证实。

现在完成时:
Everything has checked out so far. = 到目前为止，一切都很顺利。
The police have checked out all the leads. = 警方已经调查了所有线索。

过去完成时:
The evidence had checked out before the trial. = 在审判前，证据已经过核实。
We had checked out the rumors before we acted. = 我们在采取行动之前已经核实了谣言。

将来时:
I hope the background check checks out. = 我希望背景调查能通过。
We'll see if the theory checks out. = 我们拭目以待理论是否成立。

虚拟语气:
If his alibi checked out, he would be released. = 如果他的不在场证明成立，他就会被释放。
I wish the results checked out differently. = 我希望结果不一样。

被动语态:
The statement was checked out by the authorities. = 该声明已由当局核实。
The facts have been checked out thoroughly. = 事实已被彻底查证。

疑问句:
Does his story check out? = 他的说法可信吗？
Does the evidence check out? = 证据可靠吗？

否定句:
His alibi didn't check out. = 他的不在场证明站不住脚。
The details didn't check out. = 细节与事实不符。


PHRASE->
