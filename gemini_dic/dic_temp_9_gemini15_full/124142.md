mortifier:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪər/| n. |使人蒙羞的人或事；令人难堪的事物 |Something or someone that causes mortification; a source of humiliation or shame.  |例句：The public humiliation was a mortifier for the politician. 公开的羞辱对这位政治家来说是一件令人难堪的事。|近义词：humiliation, embarrassment, disgrace |反义词：honor, pride, glory |名词
