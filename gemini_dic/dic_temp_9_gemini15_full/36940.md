colourant: /ˈkʌlərənt/ | n. |着色剂，染料 | A substance used to impart colour.  Colourants are used in a wide variety of applications, from paints and dyes to food and cosmetics. | Example:  The artist used a vibrant red colourant to paint the poppies.  艺术家用鲜艳的红色着色剂来画罂粟花。 |近义词：dye, pigment, stain |反义词：decolorizer, bleach
