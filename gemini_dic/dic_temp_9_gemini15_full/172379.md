puttying:/ˈpʌtiɪŋ/| v.|用油灰填补；修补|The act of applying putty to fill cracks or holes.  Puttying is a common task in home repair and renovation. |例句：He spent the afternoon puttying the window frames. 他一下午都在用油灰填补窗框。
例句：The carpenter carefully puttying the gaps between the wooden panels. 木匠仔细地用油灰填补木板之间的缝隙。|近义词：filling, sealing, caulking|反义词：removing, uncovering|动词
