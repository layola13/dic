dehydrate:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪt/| vt.|脱水；使干燥|To remove water from (something), especially food, to preserve it or to make it easier to store or transport.  It can also refer to the loss of water from a living organism, leading to dehydration.|例句：The sun dehydrated the land, leaving it cracked and barren.  太阳使土地脱水，使其龟裂荒芜。
例句：We need to dehydrate the fruit before we can store it for the winter. 我们需要在储存水果过冬之前将其脱水。|近义词：dry, desiccate|反义词：hydrate|动词
