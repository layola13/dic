reproduces:/ˌriːprəˈduːsɪz/ | v. | 复制；繁殖；再现 | To make a copy of something; to create a new organism from existing ones; to represent something again. | 例句：The machine reproduces documents quickly. 这台机器能快速复制文件。 | 例句： Rabbits reproduce rapidly. 兔子繁殖迅速。 | 近义词：copy, duplicate, replicate, multiply, propagate | 反义词：destroy, erase, annihilate
