rethatching:/ˌriːˈtætʃɪŋ/ | v. | 重新盖屋顶；更换茅草屋顶 | The act of replacing or repairing a thatched roof.  It involves removing the old thatch and replacing it with new materials. | The cottage needs rethatching; the old thatch is decaying. 这间小屋需要重新盖屋顶；旧的茅草屋顶正在腐烂。 The farmer is rethatching his barn after the recent storm.  暴风雨过后，这位农民正在重新给他的谷仓盖屋顶。|  repairing, replacing |  demolishing (in the context of the roof) | 动词
