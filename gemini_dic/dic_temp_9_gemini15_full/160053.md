polytunnel: /ˈpɒlitʌnəl/ | n. |塑料大棚，简易温室 | A polytunnel is a type of greenhouse made from a plastic covering stretched over a framework of hoops or arches.  It is used to protect plants from harsh weather conditions and to extend the growing season. | Example:  We built a polytunnel in the garden to grow tomatoes. 我们在花园里建了一个塑料大棚来种植西红柿。 | 近义词：greenhouse, hoop house, walk-in greenhouse | 反义词：open field
