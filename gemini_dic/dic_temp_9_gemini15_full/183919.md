reservative:/rɪˈzɜːrvətɪv/| adj.| 保守的，谨慎的 |Reluctant to spend money or use resources; cautious.  Characterized by or showing caution or restraint. |例句：He has a reservative approach to investing, preferring safer options. 他对投资采取保守的态度，更喜欢安全的选择。

例句：Her reservative nature prevented her from taking risks in her career. 她保守的性格阻止了她在事业上冒险。


近义词：cautious, conservative, prudent, wary |反义词：reckless, impetuous, rash, adventurous|词性：形容词
