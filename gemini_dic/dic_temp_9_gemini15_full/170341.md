provincial:/prəˈvɪnʃəl/| adj.|省的；偏狭的；乡下的|Having or showing the characteristics of a province; unsophisticated or narrow-minded.|例句：He has a rather provincial attitude towards other cultures. 他对其他文化持有相当偏狭的态度。|例句：The provincial government is responsible for education. 省政府负责教育。|近义词：narrow-minded, unsophisticated, rustic, rural|反义词：cosmopolitan, sophisticated, worldly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The provincial government implements new policies. = 省政府实施新政策。
This area remains largely provincial in its outlook. = 这个地区的观点在很大程度上仍然很狭隘。


一般过去时:
