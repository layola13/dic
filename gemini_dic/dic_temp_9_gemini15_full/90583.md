hulkster:/ˈhʌlkstər/| n. |大力士，魁梧的人|A hulkster is a large, strong, and often intimidating person; the term is often used informally and sometimes playfully, especially in reference to wrestlers.|例句：He's a real hulkster, capable of lifting incredible weights. 他是个真正的大力士，能举起难以置信的重量。  例句：The comedian played the role of a lovable hulkster. 喜剧演员扮演了一个讨人喜欢的大力士的角色。|近义词：brute, giant, behemoth|反义词：weakling, shrimp
