haematologists:/ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |血液学家| Specialists in hematology, the branch of medicine concerned with the study, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the blood.  They diagnose and treat a wide range of blood disorders, from anemia to leukemia. |Example:  The haematologists reviewed the patient's blood tests to determine the cause of their fatigue. 血液学家检查了病人的血液化验结果以确定他们疲劳的原因。


Example: The team of haematologists worked tirelessly to find a cure for the rare blood disorder.  血液学家团队夜以继日地工作以寻找这种罕见血液疾病的治疗方法。|近义词：hematologists|反义词：无
