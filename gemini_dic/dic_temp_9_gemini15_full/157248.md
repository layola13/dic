pinner:/ˈpɪnər/ | n. |大头针；别针；图钉；(Pinterest的) 用户；固定者 | A person who uses pins, especially for fastening things or for displaying items on a board;  a user of the social media site Pinterest. |例句：She used a pinner to fasten the fabric. 她用别针固定布料。


例句：He's a keen pinner on Pinterest, always sharing his latest finds. 他是 Pinterest 上的活跃用户，总是分享他最新的发现。


近义词：fastener, tacker,  (for Pinterest user)  sharer, poster


反义词：remover, unfastener


词性：名词
