messuage:/ˈmesɪdʒ/| n. |房屋及其附属土地；宅院|A messuage is a dwelling house with its outbuildings and adjacent land.  It is a legal term, often used in historical documents relating to property ownership.  The term encompasses the entire property, including the main house, any barns, stables, gardens, and surrounding land traditionally associated with the house. |The old deed described the property as a messuage and appurtenances.  这旧契约将该房产描述为一处宅院及其附属物。|  estate, property, tenement |  — | noun
