hotel:/hoʊˈtɛl/| /həʊˈtɛl/ | n. |旅馆，酒店 |A hotel is an establishment that provides paid lodging on a short-term basis.  Hotels range in size and features, from small, family-run inns to large, luxurious resorts. They typically offer various amenities, including rooms, restaurants, and sometimes recreational facilities. | We stayed at a charming boutique hotel in the heart of Paris. 我们住在巴黎市中心一家迷人的精品酒店。| inn, motel, inn, lodging | home, private residence | 名词
