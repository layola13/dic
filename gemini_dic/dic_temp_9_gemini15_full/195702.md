semispeculative:/ˌsemiˈspekjʊlətɪv/| adj.|半推测的，部分推测的 |Partly based on conjecture or speculation; not entirely factual or proven.  It suggests a degree of uncertainty or lack of complete evidence. |例句：The theory is semispeculative, relying on limited evidence and some assumptions.  这个理论是部分推测的，依赖于有限的证据和一些假设。


例句：His semispeculative investment strategy yielded mixed results.  他半推测的投资策略产生了喜忧参半的结果。


近义词：conjectural, hypothetical, presumptive, tentative


反义词：definitive, conclusive, certain, factual


词性：形容词
