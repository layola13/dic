leap-of-faith:/ˌliːp əv feɪθ/| n. |信念的飞跃；冒险的尝试；孤注一掷|An act of believing something despite a lack of evidence; a risk taken in hope of a positive outcome.|例句：Taking the new job was a leap of faith, but it paid off.  接受这份新工作是一次信念的飞跃，但它得到了回报。


例句：She took a leap of faith and invested all her savings in the startup. 她孤注一掷，把所有积蓄都投资到这家初创公司。


近义词：gamble, risk, venture, chance


反义词：certainty, surety, guarantee


PHRASE->
a leap of faith in someone: 对某人的信任的飞跃
a leap of faith for the future: 对未来的冒险尝试
take a leap of faith: 孤注一掷
have faith: 有信心
blind leap of faith: 盲目冒险
