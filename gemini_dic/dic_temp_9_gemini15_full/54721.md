disoxygenation:/dɪsˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | n. |脱氧；去氧；缺氧 |The process of removing oxygen from a substance or environment.  This can refer to biological processes, such as the depletion of oxygen in tissues, or industrial processes, such as the removal of oxygen from a gas stream. |Example:  The prolonged submersion caused disoxygenation of the brain tissues.  长时间的浸泡导致脑组织缺氧。

The disoxygenation of the water led to a fish kill. 水的脱氧导致鱼类死亡。|近义词：deoxygenation, anoxia |反义词：oxygenation
