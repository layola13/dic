peat:/piːt/| n.|泥炭；泥煤|Partially decayed vegetable matter found in bogs, used as fuel and soil conditioner.|例句：The farmer used peat to improve the drainage of his soil.  这位农民用泥炭改良了他的土壤排水。|例句：Peat fires can smolder for a long time. 泥炭火可以阴燃很长时间。|近义词：turf, bog|反义词：None specifically, but  mineral soil could be considered a contrast.
