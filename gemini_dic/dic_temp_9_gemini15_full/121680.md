misplays:/ˈmɪspleɪz/| v.|错误演奏；打错球；放错地方|To play (something, especially a musical instrument or a ball in a game) incorrectly or badly; to put something in the wrong place.|例句：He misplayed the final note of the song. 他把歌的最后一个音符弹错了。
The goalkeeper misplayed the ball, leading to a goal. 守门员失误，导致丢球。


近义词：mismanage, mishandle, botch, blunder|反义词：master, perfect
