kinetic:/kɪˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 运动的；动力的；动力学的 | Relating to or resulting from motion.  The energy of motion. |例句：Kinetic energy is the energy of motion.  动能是运动的能量。

例句：The kinetic sculpture moved slowly but gracefully. 这个动力雕塑缓慢而优雅地移动。


近义词：dynamic, energetic, active | 反义词：static, stationary, inert | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ball is kinetic when it rolls down the hill. = 球滚下山时是运动的。
Kinetic energy is important in many physical processes. = 动能在许多物理过程中都很重要。


一般过去时:
The car's kinetic energy was significant before braking. = 汽车在刹车前动能很大。
The moving train possessed considerable kinetic energy. = 移动的火车拥有相当大的动能。


现在完成时:
The runner has built up considerable kinetic energy.  = 跑步者已经积累了相当大的动能。
Scientists have studied kinetic energy for centuries. = 科学家们已经研究动能几个世纪了。


过去完成时:
The car had lost much of its kinetic energy before it stopped. = 汽车在停下来之前已经损失了大部分动能。
The experiment had demonstrated the principles of kinetic energy. = 实验已经证明了动能的原理。


将来时:
The rocket will have immense kinetic energy upon re-entry. = 火箭再入大气层时将具有巨大的动能。
The project will explore new aspects of kinetic energy. = 该项目将探索动能的新方面。


虚拟语气:
If the object were moving faster, its kinetic energy would be greater. = 如果物体移动得更快，它的动能就会更大。
If the system had more kinetic energy, it would be more efficient. = 如果系统有更多的动能，它将会更高效。


被动语态:
Kinetic energy is often converted into other forms of energy. = 动能经常被转化为其他形式的能量。

疑问句:

否定句:
