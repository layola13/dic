nucleiform:/ˈnjuːkliːfɔːrm/| adj.|核形的，核状的|Resembling a nucleus in shape or structure.  It is used to describe something that has a shape similar to a cell nucleus, typically round or oval with a dense center.|Example: The nucleiform structure of the protein was clearly visible under the electron microscope.  蛋白质的核形结构在电子显微镜下清晰可见。|近义词：nuclear, round, oval|反义词：elongated, irregular
