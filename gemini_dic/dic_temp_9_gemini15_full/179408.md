redtapism: /ˈrɛdˌteɪpɪzəm/ | n. |官僚主义；繁文缛节 | Excessive adherence to official rules and procedures; bureaucracy.  Red tape refers to the excessive paperwork and bureaucratic procedures involved in official processes. Redtapism describes the negative consequences of this excessive bureaucracy, often leading to inefficiency and frustration.  | The redtapism in the government department delayed the project by several months.  政府部门的官僚主义使该项目延误了数月。 | bureaucracy, officialism | efficiency, streamlining | 名词
