labored:/ˈleɪbərd/ | adj. |费力的；吃力的；不流畅的；沉重的 |  Difficult or slow; requiring or showing great effort.  It suggests something done with difficulty and strain. |例句：He gave a labored explanation of the situation. 他对情况做出了费力的解释。

例句：Her breathing was labored and shallow. 她的呼吸费力而浅。


近义词：strained, strenuous, arduous, difficult, heavy

反义词：easy, effortless, smooth, fluent


词源:源自于动词labor,  meaning "to work hard."  The adjective form reflects the effort and difficulty involved in the action or process.
