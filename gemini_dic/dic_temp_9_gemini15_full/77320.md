gathers:/ˈɡæðərz/ | v. | 收集；聚集；积聚 | To bring together; to accumulate; to assemble.  |例句：She gathers wildflowers every morning. = 她每天早上都采摘野花。
The crowd gathers in the town square. = 人群聚集在市中心广场。|近义词：collect, assemble, accumulate |反义词：scatter, disperse, dissipate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gathers information for his research. = 他收集信息用于他的研究。
She gathers her children every evening for dinner. = 她每天晚上都召集她的孩子们吃晚饭。

一般过去时:
The storm gathered force as it approached. = 暴风雨来临时威力越来越大。
They gathered around the campfire to tell stories. = 他们围坐在营火旁讲故事。

现在完成时:
She has gathered a lot of evidence to support her claim. = 她已经收集了很多证据来支持她的说法。
They have gathered enough signatures for the petition. = 他们已经为请愿书收集到了足够的签名。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had gathered all the necessary documents. = 会议之前，他们已经收集了所有必要的文档。
By the time the police arrived, the crowd had gathered. = 等警察到达时，人群已经聚集起来了。

将来时:
The clouds will gather and it will rain soon. = 云彩将会聚集，很快就会下雨。
We will gather at the park tomorrow afternoon. = 我们明天下午将在公园集合。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would gather more information. = 如果我有更多时间，我会收集更多信息。
I wish I had gathered more evidence before the trial. = 我希望在审判前我已经收集了更多证据。

被动语态:
The information was gathered from various sources. = 信息是从各种来源收集的。
The fruits were gathered and sold at the market. = 水果被收集起来并在市场上出售。

疑问句:
Where do you gather your news? = 你从哪里收集新闻？
How many people gathered for the protest? = 有多少人聚集起来参加抗议？

否定句:
He didn't gather enough courage to speak up. = 他没有足够的勇气大声说出来。
They didn't gather at the designated time. = 他们没有在指定时间集合。
