disforestation:/ˌdɪsfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |森林砍伐；森林破坏|The process of clearing forests, often on a large scale, resulting in the loss of trees and forest cover.  This can be caused by various factors, including logging, agriculture, and urbanization.|例句：The disforestation of the Amazon rainforest is a major environmental concern. 亚马逊雨林的森林砍伐是一个主要的环保问题。

例句：Extensive disforestation has led to soil erosion and biodiversity loss in many regions. 大规模的森林砍伐导致许多地区的土壤侵蚀和生物多样性丧失。


近义词：deforestation, forest clearance, woodland destruction |反义词：reforestation, afforestation |词性：名词
