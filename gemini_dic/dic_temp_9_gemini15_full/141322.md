opening: /ˈoʊpənɪŋ/ | n. | 开幕式；开始；空隙；开头 | The beginning or start of something.  A space or gap.  An act of opening something. | 例句：The opening ceremony of the Olympics was spectacular.  奥运会的开幕式非常壮观。 The opening of the new bridge will ease traffic congestion. 新桥的通车将缓解交通拥堵。 | 近义词：beginning, commencement, start, inauguration | 反义词：closing, end, conclusion | 名词
