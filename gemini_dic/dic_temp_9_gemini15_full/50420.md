depraved:/dɪˈpreɪvd/| adj.|堕落的；腐败的；邪恶的|Morally corrupt; wicked; perverted.  It suggests a state of moral corruption that is often accompanied by violence or cruelty.  The word emphasizes the degradation of moral principles and the perversion of natural instincts.|例句：The depraved killer showed no remorse for his crimes.  这个堕落的杀手对自己的罪行毫无悔意。


例句：He led a depraved life, indulging in drugs and violence. 他过着放荡不羁的生活，沉溺于毒品和暴力。


近义词：corrupt, wicked, perverted, degenerate, vicious, immoral


反义词：virtuous, moral, upright, righteous, pure
