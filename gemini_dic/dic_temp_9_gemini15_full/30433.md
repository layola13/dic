centralizer:/ˈsɛntrələˌzaɪzər/| n. |中心器；中心化装置；中心化者|A centralizer is a device or element that brings things together or unifies them around a central point or authority.  It can refer to a physical device, a person, or an abstract concept. In mathematics, it refers to a specific set of elements within a group.|例句：The new management implemented a centralizer to streamline communication across departments. 新管理层实施了一个中心化系统来简化跨部门的沟通。|例句：In group theory, the centralizer of an element is the set of elements that commute with it. 在群论中，一个元素的中心化子是与该元素可交换的元素的集合。|近义词：unifier, consolidator, concentrator|反义词：decentralizer, disperser
