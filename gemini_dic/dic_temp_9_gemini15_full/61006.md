eliminating: /ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 消除；排除；淘汰 | Removing or getting rid of something unwanted or undesirable.  |例句：Eliminating poverty is a major goal of the government. 消除贫困是政府的主要目标。

例句：The new software is designed to eliminate errors.  新的软件旨在消除错误。


近义词：removing, getting rid of, eradicating, expunging, deleting


反义词：adding, including, introducing, retaining


词性：动词(现在分词形式)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are eliminating waste in our production process. = 我们正在消除生产过程中的浪费。
They eliminate all the unqualified candidates. = 他们淘汰所有不合格的候选人。


一般过去时:
The doctor eliminated the possibility of a heart attack. = 医生排除了心脏病发作的可能性。
She eliminated all the distractions before starting to work. = 她开始工作前排除了所有干扰。


现在完成时:
We have eliminated most of the bugs in the software. = 我们已经消除了软件中的大部分错误。
They have eliminated the risk of infection. = 他们已经消除了感染的风险。


过去完成时:
He had eliminated all his opponents before the final round. = 在决赛前他已经淘汰了所有对手。
They had eliminated the problem before it became serious. = 他们在问题变得严重之前就解决了它。


将来时:
We will eliminate the use of plastic bags. = 我们将停止使用塑料袋。
The company will eliminate several positions due to restructuring. = 公司将由于重组而裁减几个职位。


虚拟语气:
If we eliminated this variable, the results would be different. = 如果我们消除这个变量，结果将会不同。
I wish we could eliminate hunger from the world. = 我希望我们能够消除世界上的饥饿。


被动语态:
The problem was eliminated quickly. = 问题很快得到了解决。
Many errors were eliminated during the editing process. = 在编辑过程中消除了许多错误。


疑问句:
How can we eliminate this unnecessary step? = 我们如何才能消除这个不必要的步骤？
What measures will be taken to eliminate corruption? = 将采取什么措施来消除腐败？


否定句:
We cannot eliminate all risks. = 我们无法消除所有风险。
They did not eliminate the possibility of fraud. = 他们没有排除欺诈的可能性。


PHRASE->
eliminate the competition = 击败竞争对手
eliminate the problem = 解决问题
eliminate the risk = 消除风险
eliminate the possibility = 排除可能性
eliminate the threat = 消除威胁

词源:
来自拉丁语 *ēlimināre，由 *ex- (out) 和 *līmen (threshold) 构成，字面意思是“把…从门槛上移开”，引申为“去除，排除”。
