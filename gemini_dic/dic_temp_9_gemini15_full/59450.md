earwax:/ˈɪrwæks/| n. |耳垢，耳屎 | A yellowish-brown waxy substance secreted in the ear canal.  It helps to protect the ear from infection and debris.  |Example:  The doctor examined the earwax to check for any signs of infection.  医生检查了耳垢以检查是否有感染迹象。


The build-up of earwax can cause hearing problems. 耳垢堆积会导致听力问题。|近义词：cerumen |反义词：无 |名词
