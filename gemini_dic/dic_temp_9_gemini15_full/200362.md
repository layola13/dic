skydiver:/ˈskaɪˌdaɪvər/| n.|跳伞运动员|A person who participates in skydiving.  A skydiver jumps from an aircraft and descends to the ground using a parachute.|例句：The experienced skydiver landed gracefully.|近义词：parachutist|反义词:None
