loneness:/ˈloʊnɪnəs/| n. |孤独；寂寞；偏僻 |The feeling of being lonely; a state of being alone and isolated, often accompanied by sadness or unhappiness.  |例句：The loneness of the countryside weighed heavily on her.  乡村的寂寞让她感到沉重。 |例句：He felt a deep sense of loneness after his wife passed away.  妻子去世后，他感到深深的孤独。|近义词：solitude, isolation, loneliness |反义词：companionship, togetherness
