halfpace:/ˈhæfpeɪs/| n. |半走廊，半楼梯间 |A halfpace is a small, intermediate space between two levels of a building, often halfway between a floor and the floor above.  It is typically a short flight of stairs or a landing.  It might be used as a resting place or a small, informal area. |Example: The old house had a charming halfpace leading to the second floor. 这栋老房子有一个通往二楼的迷人半楼梯间。|近义词：landing, intermediate space, halfway landing|反义词：full flight of stairs, complete staircase|名词
