economization:/ˌiːkənəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |经济化；节约；节省；效率化 |The process of making something more economical, often by reducing costs or waste.  It can refer to the act of making something more efficient or the state of being economical. |例句：The economization of resources is crucial for sustainable development. 资源的节约对于可持续发展至关重要。

例句：The company implemented several measures for economization, resulting in significant cost savings. 公司实施了几项节约措施，从而节省了大量成本。


近义词：efficiency, cost-cutting, streamlining, thrift, frugality |反义词：wastefulness, extravagance, profligacy |词性:名词

词源:  来自economize (动词) + -ation (名词后缀)。 economize 本身源于 economy (经济，节约)，而 economy 又源于古希腊语 oikonomia，由oikos (家) 和 nemein (管理)构成，字面意思是“家政管理”。  因此，economization 的根源在于对资源的有效管理和节约。
