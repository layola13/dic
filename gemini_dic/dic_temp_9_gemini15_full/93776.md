immense:/ɪˈmɛns/| adj.|巨大的，广阔的，无限的|extremely large or great; vast.|The immense size of the universe is difficult to comprehend. 宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。|近义词：huge, vast, enormous, colossal|反义词：tiny, minuscule, insignificant

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The task seems immense. = 这项任务看起来很艰巨。
His influence is immense. = 他的影响力巨大。

一般过去时:
The crowd was immense. = 人群极其庞大。
The damage caused by the storm was immense. = 风暴造成的损失巨大。

现在完成时:
They have made immense progress. = 他们已经取得了巨大的进步。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The possibilities will be immense. = 可能性将是巨大的。
The impact of this decision will be immense. = 这个决定的影响将是巨大的。

虚拟语气:
If the task were not so immense, we could finish it on time. = 如果任务没有那么艰巨，我们就能按时完成它。
I wish the project wasn't so immensely complicated. = 我希望这个项目没有那么复杂。

被动语态:
The benefits of this technology are immensely valued. = 这项技术的益处受到了极高的重视。
The problem was considered immense by the experts. = 专家们认为这个问题非常严重。

疑问句:

否定句:

The difference between the two options is not immense. = 这两个选项之间的差异并不大。
