grinders:/ˈɡraɪndərz/| n.|研磨机；磨牙者；（俚语）勤奋工作者|A grinder is a machine that grinds things, such as grains or coffee beans.  It can also refer to someone who grinds their teeth, or informally, someone who works very hard.  |例句：The coffee grinder needs cleaning. 这台咖啡研磨机需要清洗。

The old mill used massive grinders to crush the wheat.  古老的磨坊用巨大的研磨机碾碎小麦。


近义词：mill, crusher, worker (informal)
反义词：idler (informal)
