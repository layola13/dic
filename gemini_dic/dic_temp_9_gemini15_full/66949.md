experienced:/ɪkˈspɪəriənst/| adj.|有经验的，经验丰富的|Having or showing knowledge and skill in a particular field or activity gained through experience.  It implies a level of proficiency developed over time.|例句：He is an experienced surgeon. 他是一位经验丰富的外科医生。

例句：She has an experienced team working on the project. 她有一个经验丰富的团队在负责这个项目。


近义词：skilled, proficient, adept, veteran, seasoned|反义词：inexperienced, unskilled, novice


词源:  The word "experienced" comes from the verb "experience," which entered English in the 14th century from Old French "experiencier" and ultimately from Latin "experiri" meaning "to try, test." The suffix "-ed" transforms the verb into its adjectival form, indicating possession or quality.
