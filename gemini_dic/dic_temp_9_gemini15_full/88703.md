hobblers:/ˈhɒbləz/ | n. |蹒跚而行的人，跛行者 |People who walk with a hobble, often due to injury or disability.  They may use a cane or other assistive device. |例句：The old hobblers slowly made their way down the dusty road.  那些蹒跚而行的人慢慢地沿着尘土飞扬的道路走去。|The injured hikers were hobblers for the remainder of the trek. 受伤的徒步旅行者在剩下的行程中都只能蹒跚而行。|近义词：cripples, limpers, lame people |反义词：runners, sprinters
