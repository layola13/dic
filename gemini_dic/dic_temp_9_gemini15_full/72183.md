flopped:/flɑːpt/| /flɒpt/ | vi. & vt. | 失败；无力地落下；（使）扑通一声倒下 | to fail; to fall heavily and without grace |The business flopped after only six months.  这家公司只经营了六个月就倒闭了。

The bird flopped to the ground.  那只鸟扑通一声落到地上。

|近义词：failed, collapsed, crashed | 反义词：succeeded, thrived, prospered | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often flops on the sofa after work. 他下班后经常瘫在沙发上。
The comedian's new jokes flop every time.  这位喜剧演员的新笑话每次都失败。


一般过去时:
My attempt to bake a cake flopped miserably. 我尝试烤蛋糕惨遭失败。
The actor flopped dramatically onto the stage.  演员戏剧性地扑倒在舞台上。


现在完成时:
The restaurant has flopped since the new chef arrived. 自从新厨师来了之后，这家餐馆就倒闭了。
The plan has completely flopped. 计划彻底失败了。


过去完成时:
The project had already flopped before they secured new funding.  他们在获得新的资金之前，项目就已经失败了。
The comedian had flopped before he got his big break.  这位喜剧演员在成名之前曾经历过失败。


将来时:
I think the new product will flop. 我认为新产品将会失败。
The play is likely to flop. 这出戏很可能会失败。


虚拟语气:
If I had practiced more, I wouldn't have flopped at the audition. 如果我多练习一些，就不会在试镜中失败了。
If only I hadn't flopped so badly in the interview!  如果面试时我没有表现得那么糟糕就好了！


被动语态:
The movie was flopped by critics and audiences alike.  这部电影受到了评论家和观众的一致批评。
The business was flopped by poor management.  这家公司由于管理不善而倒闭了。


疑问句:
Did your business flop? 你的生意失败了吗？
Has the new product flopped yet? 新产品失败了吗？


否定句:
The play didn't flop; it was a huge success. 这出戏没有失败；它非常成功。
The company didn't flop despite the economic downturn.  尽管经济低迷，这家公司并没有倒闭。
