in-waiting:/ɪnˈweɪtɪŋ/| adj.|待命的；候命的；预备的|Ready and available to take on a role or position when needed.  Often used to describe someone who is next in line for a title or position.|例句：The princess is the heir in-waiting to the throne.  公主是王位的继承人。

例句：The new CEO's team is in-waiting, ready to launch the project. 新任首席执行官的团队正待命，准备启动项目。

近义词：on standby, ready, expectant

反义词：inactive, unavailable, retired

词性：形容词
