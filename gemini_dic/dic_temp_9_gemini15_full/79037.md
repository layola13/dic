give the air:/ɡɪv ði eər/| phrasal verb| 冷落；不理睬；抛弃| To ignore someone or something completely; to dismiss or reject someone or something.  |例句：He gave her the air after she insulted him. 他在她侮辱他之后就冷落了她。|例句：The company gave the project the air after discovering the high costs. 公司在发现高昂的成本后放弃了这个项目。|近义词：ignore, snub, dismiss, reject, abandon|反义词：attend to, pay attention to, welcome, embrace


USAGE->
This is a phrasal verb, not a single word, and therefore doesn't follow typical verb conjugation patterns like "give".  The tense is determined by the verb "give".

一般现在时:
He gives her the air every time she mentions her ex. = 他每次她提到她的前男友就冷落她。
They give the suggestion the air. = 他们不理会这个建议。


一般过去时:
He gave her the air after the argument. = 争吵后，他冷落了她。
The committee gave the proposal the air. = 委员会否决了这项提案。


现在完成时:
She has given him the air since their fight. = 自从他们吵架以来，她就一直不理他了。
They have given that idea the air for years. = 他们多年来一直漠视那个主意。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't give her the air; he just ignored her. = 他并没有冷落她；他只是无视了她。
They didn't give the problem the air; they tried to solve it. = 他们并没有忽视这个问题；他们试图解决它。
