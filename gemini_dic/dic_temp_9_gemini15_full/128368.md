词源:
The name "Nevada" comes from the Spanish word "nevada," meaning "snow-covered."  This refers to the Sierra Nevada mountain range, which extends into the state.  内华达州的名字来源于西班牙语“nevada”，意思是“被雪覆盖的”。这是指塞拉内华达山脉，它延伸到该州。
