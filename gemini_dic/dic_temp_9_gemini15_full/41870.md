coscripting:/koʊˈskrɪptɪŋ/| n. |共同创作，协同创作|The act of two or more people working together to create something, typically a script or screenplay, but can extend to other creative works.|Example:  The film's success was largely due to the effective coscripting of the director and screenwriter. 这部电影的成功很大程度上归功于导演和编剧的有效合作创作。|近义词：collaborative writing, joint authorship|反义词：sole authorship, independent creation|名词
