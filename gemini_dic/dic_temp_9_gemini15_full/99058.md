interassociation: /ˌɪntərəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |相互关联，相互联系|The process or state of being mutually associated or connected; reciprocal association.  It often refers to the connections or relationships between different elements, ideas, or individuals within a system or group. |例句：The study explored the interassociation between sleep quality and academic performance.  这项研究探讨了睡眠质量与学习成绩之间的相互关联。

例句：The interassociation of various social factors contributed to the rise in crime rates.  各种社会因素的相互关联导致犯罪率上升。


近义词：correlation, interconnection, interaction, interdependence, relationship

反义词：dissociation, separation, independence
