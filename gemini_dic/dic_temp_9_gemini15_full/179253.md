redissolved:/ˌriːdɪˈzɒlvd/| v. |再溶解；重新溶解 |To dissolve again; to go into solution again.  This implies a prior dissolution and a subsequent re-dissolving. |例句：The sugar redissolved in the warm water. 糖在温水中重新溶解了。

The salt, after having been precipitated, was redissolved in distilled water. 盐沉淀后，又重新溶解在蒸馏水中。|近义词：redissolve, resolubilize |反义词：precipitate, crystallize
