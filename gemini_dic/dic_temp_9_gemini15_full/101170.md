invigilation: /ɪnˌvɪdʒɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |监考；监督；监视 | The act of supervising or watching over someone or something, especially during an examination.  It implies a watchful and often formal oversight to ensure fair play or prevent cheating. | Example: The invigilation of the exam was strict, with proctors patrolling the room constantly.  监考非常严格，监考员不断巡视房间。 |近义词：supervision, monitoring, surveillance, proctoring |反义词：neglect, oversight (in the sense of failing to supervise)
