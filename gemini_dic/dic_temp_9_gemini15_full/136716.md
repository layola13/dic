nonsensationalistic: /ˌnɒnsɛnseɪʃənəlˈɪstɪk/ | adj. |非耸人听闻的，不哗众取宠的 | Not intended to create excitement or attract attention; presenting facts in a calm and objective manner.  |例句：The newspaper presented a nonsensationalistic account of the accident. 这家报纸对事故的报道客观冷静，没有夸大其词。


例句：The professor's lecture was refreshingly nonsensationalistic, focusing on facts rather than dramatic interpretations. 教授的演讲令人耳目一新，它专注于事实而非戏剧性的解读。


近义词：factual, objective, unbiased, unemotional, understated


反义词：sensationalistic, dramatic, lurid, hyperbolic, inflammatory


词性:形容词
