miscorrect:/ˌmɪskəˈrekt/| v.| 纠正错误；改正|To correct incorrectly; to make a mistake in correcting something.  It implies that the attempt to correct something resulted in a further error or a worsening of the situation.|The student tried to miscorrect the spelling, making it even worse.|近义词：incorrect,  err in correcting|反义词：correct
