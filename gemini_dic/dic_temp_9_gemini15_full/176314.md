re-embark:/ˌriːɪmˈbɑːrk/| vt.|重新上船；重新开始|To embark again; to begin something again after an interruption.|例句：After a brief layover, the passengers re-embarked on their journey.  乘客短暂停留后，重新开始了他们的旅程。
例句：The company decided to re-embark on the project after a period of review. 公司在经过一段时间的审查后，决定重新开始该项目。|近义词：recommence, resume, restart|反义词：disembark, abandon, cease
