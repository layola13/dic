/ˈkaʊntərmɑːrt/| n. |反市场；对抗市场；竞争市场 | A counter-market is a market that operates in opposition to or in defiance of a dominant market, often characterized by different pricing, regulations, or participants.  It might be a black market, a grey market, or a niche market that undercuts established players. | Example: The rise of online marketplaces created a counter-market to traditional brick-and-mortar stores.  在线市场的兴起为传统的实体店创造了一个反市场。| 近义词：black market, grey market, alternative market | 反义词：dominant market, mainstream market | 名词
