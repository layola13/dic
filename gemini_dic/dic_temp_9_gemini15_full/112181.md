lookup:/ˈlʊkʌp/| vt. n.|查询；查阅；抬头看；拜访|to search for information in a reference book or database; to look upward; to visit someone.|例句：I looked up the word in the dictionary. 我在字典里查了这个词。|例句：I need to look up my friend tonight. 我今晚需要去看望我的朋友。|近义词：consult, search, check, visit|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look up new words every day. = 我每天都查阅新单词。
They look up the address on the map. = 他们在地图上查找地址。

一般过去时:
She looked up the phone number. = 她查了电话号码。
He looked up his old friend. = 他拜访了他的老朋友。

现在完成时:
I have looked up that information several times. = 我已经多次查找过该信息。
We have looked up the meaning of this word before. = 我们以前查过这个词的意思。

过去完成时:
I had looked up the recipe before I started cooking. = 在我开始烹饪之前，我已经查阅了食谱。
They had looked up the train schedule earlier. = 他们早些时候已经查阅了火车时刻表。

将来时:
I will look up the translation tomorrow. = 我明天会查阅翻译。
She will look up her aunt next week. = 她下周会去看望她的阿姨。

I wish I had looked up the information earlier. = 我希望我早些时候就查找了信息。

否定句:
