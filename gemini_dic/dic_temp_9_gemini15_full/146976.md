overjoyfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfʊlnəs/ | n. |极度快乐，狂喜 |The state of being overwhelmingly joyful; extreme happiness.  It often implies a feeling of being so happy as to be almost overwhelmed. |例句：Her overjoyfulness was evident in her bright eyes and infectious laughter.  她的极度快乐从她明亮的眼睛和富有感染力的笑声中显而易见。

例句：The news of her success filled her with overjoyfulness.  她成功的消息让她欣喜若狂。


近义词：ecstasy, rapture, elation, exhilaration, bliss |反义词：despair, misery, sorrow, dejection
