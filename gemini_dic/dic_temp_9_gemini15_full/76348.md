gadd:/ɡæd/| v.|闲逛，游荡，闲聊|to go about idly or without purpose; to chatter idly|例句：They spent the afternoon gadding about the town. 他们下午在镇上闲逛。

例句：The children were gadding about in the park.孩子们在公园里闲逛。


近义词：wander, roam, stroll, ramble, chatter|反义词：stay, remain, be still|词性:动词
