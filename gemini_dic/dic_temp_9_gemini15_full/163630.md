predatorily:/ˈprɛdəˌtɔːrɪli/| adv.|掠夺性地；捕食性地|In a predatory manner; characteristic of a predator.  It implies aggression, exploitation, and a focus on obtaining something from another, often unfairly or forcefully.|例句：The company predatorily acquired smaller firms, driving many out of business.  这家公司以掠夺的方式收购小型企业，导致许多企业倒闭。


例句：He predatorily eyed the unsuspecting tourist. 他贪婪地注视着毫无戒心的游客。


近义词：aggressively, ruthlessly, exploitatively, rapaciously


反义词：benignly, kindly, helpfully, supportively


词性:副词
