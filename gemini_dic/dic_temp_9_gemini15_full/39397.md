conjures:/ˈkʌndʒərz/| vt.|变戏法；使想起；召唤；产生|To conjure is to perform magic tricks, or to make something appear unexpectedly.  It can also mean to summon something or someone, or to bring something to mind. |例句：The magician conjured a rabbit from a hat.  魔术师从帽子中变出一只兔子。

例句：The smell of woodsmoke conjured up memories of childhood holidays.  木材燃烧的气味唤起了童年假期的回忆。

近义词：evoke, summon, create, produce, invoke

反义词：destroy, banish, obliterate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The magician conjures rabbits from hats. = 魔术师从帽子中变出兔子。
She conjures up images of her childhood. = 她脑海中浮现出童年时的景象。


一般过去时:
He conjured a beautiful landscape with his words. = 他用言语描绘出一幅美丽的景象。
The witch conjured a storm. = 女巫召唤了一场暴风雨。


现在完成时:
She has conjured up a delicious meal from simple ingredients. = 她用简单的食材变出了美味佳肴。
The author has conjured a fascinating world in his new novel.  = 作者在他的新小说中创造了一个引人入胜的世界。


过去完成时:
He had conjured a plan to escape. = 他已策划了一个逃跑计划。
The old woman had conjured a spell to protect her home. = 老妇人施了咒语来保护她的家。


将来时:
They will conjure a solution to the problem. = 他们将会找到解决这个问题的办法。
The artist will conjure a masterpiece. = 艺术家将创作出一部杰作。


虚拟语气:
If I could conjure a wish, I would wish for world peace. = 如果我能许愿，我会许愿世界和平。
I wish I could conjure a way to help her. = 我希望我能想出一个办法来帮助她。


被动语态:
A magical atmosphere was conjured by the flickering candles. = 闪烁的蜡烛营造出一种神奇的氛围。
Memories were conjured by the old photograph. = 老照片唤起了人们的回忆。


疑问句:
Can you conjure a solution to this puzzle? = 你能找到解决这个谜题的方法吗？
What images does this music conjure for you? = 这段音乐在你脑海中勾勒出什么样的画面？


否定句:
He could not conjure away his fears. = 他无法消除恐惧。
She couldn't conjure up a single reason to stay. = 她想不出任何留下来的理由。


PHRASE->
conjure up = 产生；想起；使想起
conjure away = 使消失；消除
conjure with = 使用魔法；施法
conjure a spell = 施法
conjure an image = 想象


词源:
From Old French *conjurer, from Latin conjurare "to swear together," from con- "together" + jurare "to swear."  The modern meaning developed from the idea of magic rituals involving oaths and incantations.
