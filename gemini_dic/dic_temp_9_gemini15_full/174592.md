quirks:/kwɜːrk/| n. |古怪的行为；癖好；怪癖 |A quirk is a strange habit or feature of someone's personality or behavior.  It can also refer to a strange or unexpected feature of something. |例句：He has a quirky sense of humor. 他有一种古怪的幽默感。
|例句：There's a quirk in the system that causes this error. 系统中有一个缺陷导致了这个错误。|近义词：oddity, eccentricity, peculiarity |反义词：normality, regularity
