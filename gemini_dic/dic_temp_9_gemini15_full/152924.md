pedestrianize:/pɛˈdɛstriənˌaɪz/ | vt. |使行人专用；禁止车辆通行 | To make an area accessible only to pedestrians by restricting or prohibiting the use of motor vehicles.  This often involves closing streets to traffic and creating pedestrian-friendly spaces. | The city council voted to pedestrianize the main street, creating a more enjoyable environment for shoppers and residents.  市议会投票决定将主要街道行人专用，为购物者和居民创造更愉快的环境。| 近义词：close to traffic, make car-free,  | 反义词：motorize, open to traffic | 动词
