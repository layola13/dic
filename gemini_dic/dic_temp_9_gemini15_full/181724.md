reliant:/rɪˈlaɪənt/| adj.|依赖的，依靠的|Depending on someone or something for support, help, or supply.|The success of the project is reliant on the cooperation of all team members.  这个项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的合作。|近义词：dependent, dependent on, relying on|反义词：independent, self-sufficient|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reliant on his parents for financial support. 他依靠父母提供经济支持。
The company is reliant on its key clients for revenue. 这家公司依靠主要客户获得收入。


一般过去时:
She was reliant on her bicycle for transportation. 她过去依靠自行车出行。
The village was reliant on the river for its water supply. 这个村庄过去依靠这条河供水。


现在完成时:
We have been reliant on technology for communication. 我们一直以来依赖科技进行沟通。
The country has become reliant on foreign aid. 这个国家已经依赖外国援助。


过去完成时:
They had been reliant on the old system before the upgrade. 升级之前，他们一直依赖旧系统。
The business had been reliant on word-of-mouth marketing. 这家企业过去一直依靠口碑营销。


将来时:
She will be reliant on her savings for retirement. 她退休后将依靠自己的积蓄生活。
The economy will be reliant on renewable energy sources in the future. 未来经济将依赖可再生能源。


虚拟语气:
If I were reliant on others, I wouldn't be successful. 如果我依赖别人，我就不会成功。
I wish I weren't so reliant on caffeine. 我希望我不那么依赖咖啡因。


被动语态:
The project's success is not reliant on any single person. 项目的成功并不依赖于任何一个人。
Their decision was heavily reliant on the latest data. 他们的决定很大程度上依赖于最新的数据。


疑问句:
Is the system reliant on internet access? 这个系统是否依赖于互联网访问？
Are you reliant on public transportation? 你依赖公共交通吗？


否定句:
The software is not reliant on external libraries. 这个软件不依赖外部库。
He is not reliant on anyone for help. 他不需要任何人的帮助。


PHRASE->
be reliant on/upon: 依赖于
heavily reliant on:  严重依赖于
utterly reliant on: 完全依赖于
become reliant on: 开始依赖于
less reliant on: 减少对...的依赖
