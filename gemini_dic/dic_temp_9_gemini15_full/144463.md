outtalking: /ˌaʊtˈtɔːkɪŋ/ | v. |  滔滔不绝地说；比…说得更多；以雄辩压倒 |  To talk more than someone else; to surpass someone in a debate or argument through eloquence or volume of speech. |


例句：

He outtalked his opponent in the debate, winning the judges over with his passionate arguments.  = 他在辩论中滔滔不绝地驳倒了他的对手，凭借他充满激情的论点赢得了评委的青睐。

She completely outtalked me; I couldn't get a word in edgewise. = 她完全把我比下去了；我一句话也插不上嘴。


近义词：outspeak, outargue,  overwhelm (in a debate),  dominate (a conversation)


反义词：be silenced, be interrupted, be outdone (in a debate)


词性：动词
