fixings:/ˈfɪksɪŋz/| n.|配件；调料；准备工作|The necessary materials, components, or ingredients for something.  It can refer to the parts needed to assemble something, or the ingredients for a dish or meal. |例句：The cake fixings were all laid out on the counter. 蛋糕的配料都摆在柜台上。


例句：He gathered the fixings for his fishing trip. 他收集了他钓鱼旅行所需的装备。


近义词：ingredients, components, supplies, equipment, paraphernalia


反义词：None directly applicable; the antonym depends on the context (e.g., if referring to ingredients, "lack of ingredients").


词性: 名词
