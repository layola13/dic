shook:/ʃʊk/| v.|摇动；震动；使震惊|The past tense of "shake".  It describes the action of shaking something or someone, or the state of being shaken.  It can also refer to a feeling of being emotionally disturbed or surprised.|例句：He shook the bottle to mix the ingredients. 他摇晃瓶子以混合配料。

The earthquake shook the city violently. 地震剧烈地摇动了这座城市。


近义词：tremble, quiver, vibrate, jar, agitate|反义词：stabilize, calm, soothe|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shakes his head in disbelief. = 他不相信地摇摇头。
The tree shakes in the wind. = 树在风中摇晃。


一般过去时:
I shook his hand warmly. = 我热情地握了他的手。
The ground shook during the earthquake. = 地震期间地面震动。


现在完成时:
The earthquake has shaken the region badly. = 地震严重地震动了该地区。
She has shaken off her shyness. = 她已经克服了她的害羞。


过去完成时:
The building had shaken violently before it collapsed. = 建筑物剧烈摇晃后才倒塌。
They had shaken hands before the meeting. = 他们在会议前握过手。


将来时:
The wind will shake the leaves from the trees. = 风会把树叶从树上吹落。
I think the news will shake him. = 我认为这个消息会使他震惊。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
