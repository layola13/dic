dewiring: /diˈwaɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 去线, 去除电线 | The process of removing wires or cables from something.  | Example: The dewiring of the old building was a complex and time-consuming task.  去除旧建筑物的电线是一项复杂且耗时的任务。| 近义词:  unwiring, removing wires | 反义词: wiring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The electrician is dewiring the old house. = 电工正在拆除旧房子的电线。
They are dewiring the faulty circuit. = 他们正在拆除有故障的电路。

一般过去时:
The technician dewired the computer. = 技术人员拆除了电脑的线。
We dewired the old television. = 我们拆除了旧电视机的线。


现在完成时:
They have dewired the entire building. = 他们已经拆除了整栋建筑物的电线。
The workers have dewired the damaged section. = 工人们已经拆除了损坏的部分的电线。


过去完成时:
They had dewired the system before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经拆除了系统线路。
The engineers had dewired the faulty component. = 工程师们已经拆除了有故障的组件的线。


将来时:
We will dewire the old equipment next week. = 我们下周将拆除旧设备的电线。
The contractor will dewire the entire area. = 承包商将拆除整个区域的电线。


虚拟语气:
If we dewired the system, we could test it safely. = 如果我们拆除了系统线路，我们就可以安全地测试它。
I wish we had dewired the faulty cable earlier. = 我真希望我们早点拆除那根有故障的电缆。


被动语态:

疑问句:
