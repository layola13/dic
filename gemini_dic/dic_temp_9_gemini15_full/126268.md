myelomas:/ˌmaɪəˈloʊməz/ | n. | 骨髓瘤 (复数) | Malignant neoplasms (tumors) originating in the bone marrow, characterized by the proliferation of abnormal plasma cells.  Myelomas can cause a variety of symptoms depending on their location and extent. | Example: Multiple myelomas are a type of blood cancer. 多发性骨髓瘤是一种血液癌。 |近义词: plasma cell myeloma, plasmacytoma (singular form) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑healthy bone marrow)
