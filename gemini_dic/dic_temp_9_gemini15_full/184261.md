resonate:/ˈrezəneɪt/| v.|共鸣，引起共鸣；回响|To produce or have a strong effect on someone; to evoke a feeling or idea in someone; to sound loudly and deeply. |例句：Her speech resonated with many young people.  她的演讲引起了许多年轻人的共鸣。

例句：The singer's voice resonated throughout the hall.  歌手的歌声在整个大厅回荡。

近义词：vibrate, echo, reverberate, strike a chord

反义词：fall flat, fail to connect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The speaker's message resonates with the audience. = 演讲者的信息与听众产生共鸣。
His words resonate deeply within me. = 他的话深深地触动了我。


一般过去时:
The music resonated throughout the stadium. = 音乐在整个体育场回荡。
The announcement resonated powerfully with the employees. = 公告强烈地引起了员工的共鸣。


现在完成时:
Her ideas have resonated with people across the globe. = 她的想法已在全球各地引起共鸣。
His work has resonated with critics and audiences alike. = 他的作品在评论家和观众中都引起了共鸣。


过去完成时:
The news had resonated throughout the small town by the time I arrived. = 我到达时，这个消息已经在小镇上传开了。
The implications of the study had resonated long after its publication. = 这项研究的意义在其发表很久之后仍引起共鸣。


将来时:
I think this song will resonate with listeners for years to come. = 我认为这首歌在未来几年会引起听众的共鸣。
His message will surely resonate with those who are struggling. = 他的信息肯定会引起那些正在挣扎的人的共鸣。


虚拟语气:
If the message resonated more strongly, we would have seen better results. = 如果信息更强烈地引起共鸣，我们将看到更好的结果。
I wish his words had resonated more deeply with the audience. = 我希望他的话能更深刻地引起观众的共鸣。


被动语态:
The message was resonated by the entire community. = 整个社区都对这个信息产生了共鸣。(被动语态在resonate中使用较少，通常用主动语态)
The song was resonated throughout the concert hall. = 这首歌在整个音乐厅回荡。(被动语态在resonate中使用较少，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Does the theme of the film resonate with you? = 这部电影的主题与你产生共鸣吗？
Does the music resonate with the audience's feelings? = 音乐与观众的感受产生共鸣吗？


否定句:
The joke didn't resonate with the audience. = 这个笑话没有引起观众的共鸣。
His message failed to resonate with the voters. = 他的信息没有引起选民的共鸣。


PHRASE->
resonate with = 与…产生共鸣
resonate deeply = 深深地引起共鸣
resonate throughout = 回荡在…
resonate powerfully = 强烈地引起共鸣
resonate across = 引起…的共鸣 (跨越地域或群体)
