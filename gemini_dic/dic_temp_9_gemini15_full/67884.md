fabulist:/ˈfæbjʊlɪst/ | n. | 寓言家；说谎者；编造故事的人 | A fabulist is a person who composes or tells fables, or who invents or relates improbable stories.  They may be skilled storytellers, but their stories are often untrue or exaggerated.  | Example: Aesop is considered one of history's greatest fabulists.  爱索普被认为是历史上最伟大的寓言家之一。  |近义词: storyteller, mythmaker, liar, spinner of yarns | 反义词:truth-teller, realist
