nonconfederation: /ˌnɒnkənˌfedəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |非邦联；非联盟 | The absence or rejection of a confederation; a state of not being a confederation.  It refers to a situation where separate entities choose not to form a confederation or alliance.  This could be due to various reasons, such as conflicting interests, differing ideologies, or a preference for independent action. | Example:  The ongoing nonconfederation between the two nations resulted in trade disputes.  = 两个国家持续的非联盟状态导致了贸易纠纷。 | 近义词：disunion, separation, independence | 反义词：confederation, alliance, union | 名词
