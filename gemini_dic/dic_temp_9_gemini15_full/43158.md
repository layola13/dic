cozying-up: /ˈkoʊziːɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 变得亲密；变得舒适；偎依 | To become closer or more intimate; to make oneself or something more comfortable and snug.  | 例句：The couple were cozying up by the fireplace.  这对夫妇在壁炉旁依偎着。  They're cozying up to the new boss. 他们正在讨好新老板。 | 近义词： snuggling, cuddling, drawing close, becoming intimate | 反义词： distancing, separating, estranging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are cozying up to each other on the sofa. = 他们在沙发上互相依偎。
The cat is cozying up by the heater. = 猫正在暖气旁取暖。

一般过去时:
We cozied up by the fire after dinner. = 晚饭后我们依偎在火炉旁取暖。
The children cozied up together to share a blanket. = 孩子们挤在一起共用一条毯子。


现在完成时:
They have cozied up to the influential politician. = 他们已经讨好了那位有影响力的政客。
The dog has cozied up on my lap. = 狗已经偎在我的腿上了。


过去完成时:
They had cozied up to the investors before asking for funding. = 他们在寻求资金之前已经讨好了投资者。
She had cozied up in her favorite armchair with a book. = 她已经舒服地坐在她最喜欢的扶手椅上看书了。


将来时:
We will cozy up with a good movie tonight. = 我们今晚会舒服地看一部好电影。
They will cozy up to the new client to secure the deal. = 他们将讨好新客户以确保交易成功。


虚拟语气:
If it were colder, we would cozy up by the fire. = 如果天气更冷，我们会依偎在火炉旁。
I wish I could cozy up with a good book right now. = 我真希望现在能舒服地看本书。


被动语态:
The room was cozied up with soft blankets and pillows. = 房间里摆满了柔软的毯子和枕头，显得非常舒适。
(Note:  "Cozying up" is less frequently used passively)


疑问句:
Are they cozying up to get a promotion? = 他们是在为了升职而讨好别人吗？
Is the puppy cozying up to the new cat? = 小狗是在讨好那只新来的猫吗？


否定句:
They didn't cozy up to their competitors. = 他们没有讨好他们的竞争对手。
He wasn't cozying up to the idea. = 他并不热衷于这个主意。
