romanticist: /rəʊˈmæntɪsɪst/ | n. | 浪漫主义者 | A person who follows the principles of Romanticism, especially in literature or art.  A romanticist emphasizes emotion, imagination, and individualism over reason, order, and objectivity.  They often idealize nature and the past. | Example:  Many famous poets of the 19th century were romanticists.  许多19世纪著名的诗人都属于浪漫主义者。  | 近义词: idealist, dreamer, visionary | 反义词: classicist, realist
