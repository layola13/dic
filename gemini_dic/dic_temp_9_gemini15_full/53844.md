discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/| n. adj.|不满，不满足；不安；不快乐|a state of dissatisfaction; unhappiness;restlessness.  It can refer to a feeling or a condition.|例句：The workers expressed their discontent over low wages. 工人们表达了他们对低工资的不满。

例句：Her discontent with her job led her to seek a new career. 她对工作的 不满让她寻求新的职业。


近义词：dissatisfaction, unhappiness, unrest, dissatisfaction, disgruntlement |反义词：contentment, satisfaction, happiness, peace


词性:名词，形容词
