gulp:/ɡʌlp/| vt. n.|吞咽；大口喝；|To swallow something quickly, often because you are nervous or in a hurry; a quick swallow.|例句：He gulped down the water. 他咕咚咕咚地喝水。 He gave a nervous gulp. 他紧张地咽了口唾沫。|近义词：swallow, gulp down|反义词：spit out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gulps his coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都很快地喝咖啡。
She gulps her food when she's in a hurry. = 她赶时间的时候狼吞虎咽。


一般过去时:
I gulped the medicine down quickly. = 我很快地把药吞了下去。
He gulped nervously before speaking. = 他在说话前紧张地咽了口唾沫。


现在完成时:
She has gulped down three glasses of water. = 她已经喝了三杯水。
He has gulped down his fear and started to speak. = 他克服了恐惧开始说话。


过去完成时:
He had gulped the news before I could say anything. = 我还没来得及说什么，他已经把消息吞下去了。
She had gulped down her food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经把饭吃完了。


将来时:
I will gulp down this drink before the show starts. = 演出开始前我会把这杯酒喝掉。
They will gulp down their food and rush to the meeting. = 他们会狼吞虎咽地吃完饭然后赶去开会。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would gulp it down. = 如果我是你，我会把它喝下去。
If I hadn't gulped it so quickly, I wouldn't have choked. = 如果我没有那么快地把它吞下去，我就不会噎着了。


被动语态:
The medicine was gulped down by the patient. = 药被病人喝下去了。
The news was gulped down by the public. = 这个消息被大众迅速接受了。


疑问句:
Did he gulp down the entire bottle? = 他一口气喝完整瓶了吗？
Will she gulp down her fear and face the challenge? = 她会克服恐惧面对挑战吗？


否定句:
He didn't gulp down the food; he chewed it slowly. = 他没有狼吞虎咽，而是慢慢地咀嚼。
She didn't gulp down her words; she spoke carefully. = 她没有吞吞吐吐，而是认真地说话。


PHRASE->
gulp down = 快速吞咽
gulp of air = 一口气
a gulp of water = 一口水
gulp something down = 迅速吞下某物
take a gulp = 猛喝一口
