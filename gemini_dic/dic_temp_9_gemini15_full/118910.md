metayer:/ˈmɛteɪər/| n. |佃农；分享农作物收成者|A metayer is a tenant farmer who pays a share of the crop to the landlord as rent.  This system, known as metayage, was common in parts of Europe and other regions.  |Example:  In the 19th century, many French peasants were metayers, working the land and sharing the harvest with the landowners.  在19世纪，许多法国农民是佃农，耕种土地并与地主分享收成。|近义词：tenant farmer, sharecropper|反义词：landowner, landlord|名词
