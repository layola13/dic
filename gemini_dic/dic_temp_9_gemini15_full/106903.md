lacer:/ˈleɪsər/| vt.|撕裂；割伤；使…痛苦|To tear or cut the skin or flesh with something sharp.  It can also refer to something that causes emotional pain. |例句：The barbed wire lacerated his arm.  带刺的铁丝割伤了他的胳膊。

The criticism lacerated her pride.  批评伤害了她的自尊。|近义词：tear, rip, gash, wound|反义词：heal, mend, repair|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thorns lacerate my skin. = 刺伤了我的皮肤。
He constantly lacerates his colleagues with his sarcastic remarks. = 他不断用讽刺的言辞伤害他的同事。


一般过去时:
The broken glass lacerated her leg. = 碎玻璃割伤了她的腿。
His harsh words lacerated her feelings. = 他尖刻的话语伤害了她的感情。


现在完成时:
The accident has lacerated his reputation. = 这起事故损害了他的名誉。
Years of hardship have lacerated the community's spirit. = 多年的苦难已经摧残了这个社区的精神。


过去完成时:
The already frayed rope had lacerated his hands. = 已经磨损的绳子已经割伤了他的手。
Before the scandal, his reputation had never been lacerated. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他的名誉从未受损。


将来时:
The sharp edges of the metal will lacerate your skin if you're not careful. = 如果你不小心，金属的锋利边缘会割伤你的皮肤。
His continued lies will eventually lacerate his relationship with his family. = 他持续不断的谎言最终会破坏他与家人的关系。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have lacerated my hand. = 如果我更小心一点，我的手就不会受伤了。
If he hadn't been so cruel, he wouldn't have lacerated her self-esteem. = 如果他不那么残酷，他就不会伤害她的自尊心。


被动语态:
His arm was lacerated by the broken bottle. = 他的手臂被碎瓶子割伤了。
Her feelings were lacerated by his indifference. = 她的感情被他冷漠的态度伤害了。


疑问句:
Was his hand lacerated badly? = 他的手伤得很严重吗？
Were her feelings lacerated by the news? = 她对这个消息感到很受伤吗？


否定句:
The accident did not lacerate his body. = 事故没有伤到他的身体。
His words did not lacerate her heart as much as he thought. = 他的话并没有像他想象的那样伤透她的心。


PHRASE->

词源:
