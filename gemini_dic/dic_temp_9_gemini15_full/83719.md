flagship:/ˈflæɡʃɪp/| n.|旗舰；佼佼者；主要产品|The flagship of a company's product line is usually its most successful and well-known item.  It represents the best of what the company offers. A flagship store is also the company's most important and prominent retail location.|Example: Apple's iPhone is considered a flagship product.|近义词：leader, vanguard, paragon|反义词：also-ran, laggard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flagship store attracts many customers. = 旗舰店吸引了很多顾客。
This product is the company's flagship. = 这款产品是公司的旗舰产品。
