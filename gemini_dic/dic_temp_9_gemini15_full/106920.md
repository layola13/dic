lacertine:/ˈlæsərtaɪn/| adj.| 蜥蜴似的；像蜥蜴的 | Of or relating to lizards; resembling a lizard.  The word evokes the imagery of lizards, suggesting qualities like scaly skin, quick movements, or a cold-blooded nature. | Example:  The lacertine creature darted across the sun-baked rocks.  这只蜥蜴似的生物迅速穿过阳光普照的岩石。 | Near synonyms: reptilian, saurian | Antonyms: avian, mammalian | adjective
