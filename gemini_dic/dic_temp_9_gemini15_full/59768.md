economized:/ˈɪkənəmaɪzd/ | vt. | 节省；节约；简化 | To use or consume something sparingly; to reduce expenses or waste.  |  例句：The company economized by reducing its workforce. 公司通过裁员来节省开支。 |例句：We economized on heating by wearing extra layers of clothing. 我们通过多穿衣服来节省取暖费用。 | 近义词：conserved, saved, thrifted | 反义词：wasted, squandered, splurged | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They economize on electricity. = 他们节约用电。
She economizes on her grocery shopping. = 她精打细算地买菜。

一般过去时:
He economized on his travel expenses. = 他节省了旅费。
We economized by cooking at home more often. = 我们通过在家做饭更多来节省开支。

现在完成时:
They have economized significantly this year. = 他们今年已经节省了很多。
We have economized by switching to a cheaper energy provider. = 我们通过改用更便宜的能源供应商来节省开支。

过去完成时:
She had economized for months to afford the trip. = 她为了支付这趟旅行的费用已经省了好几个月了。
They had economized throughout the year so they could buy a new car. = 他们为了买新车，一年到头都在省吃俭用。

将来时:
We will economize on water usage next month. = 我们下个月将节约用水。
They will economize by using less heating oil. = 他们将通过减少燃油用量来节省开支。

虚拟语气:
If we economized more, we could afford a holiday. = 如果我们更节约一些，我们就能负担得起假期了。
I wish I had economized more when I was younger. = 我真希望我年轻的时候更节俭一些。

被动语态:
Resources were economized during the war. = 在战争期间，资源得到了节约。
Expenses should be economized to maintain profitability. = 应该节约开支以保持盈利。

疑问句:
How can we economize on our energy bills? = 我们怎样才能节省能源账单？
Did they economize on the materials used in the project? = 他们在项目中使用的材料是否节省了？

否定句:
They didn't economize enough to reach their savings goal. = 他们省得不够，没有达到他们的储蓄目标。
We didn't economize on quality when we bought the furniture. = 我们买家具时没有在质量上吝啬。


PHRASE->
economize on sth. = 节省…
economize in sth. = 在…方面节省
economize by doing sth. = 通过做…来节省
economize time = 节省时间
economize effort = 节省精力
