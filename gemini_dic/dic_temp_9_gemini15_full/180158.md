reformable:/rɪˈfɔːrməbl/ | adj. | 可改革的；可改良的 | Capable of being reformed or improved.  Something that is reformable can be changed for the better, often through a process of restructuring or improvement. | Example: The outdated laws are reformable and need urgent attention.  这些过时的法律是可以改革的，需要紧急处理。 The educational system is highly reformable. 教育体系极需改革。| 近义词：amendable, improvable, corrigible | 反义词：irreformable, unchangeable, inflexible |
