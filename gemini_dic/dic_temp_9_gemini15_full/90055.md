hospitalizes:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 将…送入医院；住院治疗 | To place someone in a hospital for medical treatment.  | The doctor decided to hospitalize the patient due to his serious condition.  医生决定因病人病情严重而将其送入医院。 | admit, confine | discharge, release | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital hospitalizes many patients every day. =这家医院每天收治许多病人。
They hospitalize children with serious illnesses. =他们收治患有严重疾病的儿童。

一般过去时:
The surgeon hospitalized the injured man immediately. =外科医生立即将受伤男子送往医院。
She was hospitalized after the car accident. =车祸后她被送进了医院。

现在完成时:
They have hospitalized over 100 patients this month. =这个月他们已经收治了100多名病人。
He has been hospitalized since the accident. =自从事故发生后，他一直住院。

过去完成时:
The doctors had hospitalized her before the surgery. =手术前医生已经把她送进医院了。
They had hospitalized him for a week before his condition improved. =在他病情好转之前，他们已经将他住院治疗了一周。

将来时:
They will hospitalize him for observation. =他们会将他送院观察。
The doctor said she will be hospitalized tomorrow. =医生说她明天将住院。

虚拟语气:
If his condition worsened, they would hospitalize him immediately. =如果他的病情恶化，他们会立即将他送院。
I wish they had hospitalized him sooner. =我希望他们早点将他送院。

被动语态:
Many patients are hospitalized each year due to heart disease. =每年都有许多病人因心脏病住院。
He was hospitalized for three weeks. =他住院了三个星期。

疑问句:
Will she be hospitalized? =她会住院吗？
Was he hospitalized after the accident? =事故后他住院了吗？

否定句:
They didn't hospitalize him because his injuries were minor. =他们没有将他送院，因为他的伤势较轻。
She wasn't hospitalized; she was treated at home. =她没有住院；她在家里接受治疗。
