lumbriciform:/ˈlʌmbɹɪsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|蚯蚓形的；蠕虫形的|Resembling an earthworm in shape; elongated and cylindrical with a tapering posterior end.  The term is often used to describe the body shape of certain animals, particularly those that move by peristaltic locomotion.|Example: The lumbriciform larva of the insect burrowed deep into the soil.  蚯蚓形的昆虫幼虫钻入土壤深处。The snake's lumbriciform body allowed it to navigate narrow crevices. 蛇的蚯蚓形身体使它能够在狭窄的缝隙中穿行。|近义词：vermiform, wormlike, serpentine|反义词：globular, spherical
