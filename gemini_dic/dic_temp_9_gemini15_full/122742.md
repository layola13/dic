moistening: /ˈmɔɪsənɪŋ/ | v. | 使湿润；使潮湿 | The act of making something moist or damp.  It often implies a gentle or slight wetting, rather than a thorough soaking. | 例句：The gentle rain was moistening the parched earth.  细雨滋润着干旱的土地。

The moistening of the soil improved plant growth. 土壤的湿润改善了植物生长。

近义词：dampening, humidifying, wetting | 反义词：drying, dehydrating | 动词

词源:
The word "moistening" is derived from the adjective "moist," which comes from the Old French word "moiste" and ultimately from the Latin word "mustus," meaning "new, fresh, sweet."  The "-ening" suffix indicates the process of making something moist.
