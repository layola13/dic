microfilmers:/ˈmaɪkroʊfɪlmərz/| n. |缩微胶片制作人员，缩微胶片拍摄者 |People or companies that create microfilms.  They operate the equipment to reduce documents to a smaller size for storage and preservation. |Example: The microfilmers carefully scanned each document to ensure high-quality microfilms. 缩微胶片制作人员仔细扫描每份文件，以确保缩微胶片的质量。|近义词: microfilm operators, document reduction specialists |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with)  document enlargers, digital archivists
