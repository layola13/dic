cobaltous: /koʊˈbɑːltəs/ | adj. | 钴的(二价的) |  Relating to or denoting cobalt in the +2 oxidation state.  Cobaltous compounds are those where cobalt has a +2 oxidation state. This is in contrast to cobaltic, where cobalt has a +3 oxidation state. | Example: Cobaltous oxide is used in ceramics. 钴氧化物用于陶瓷制品。  |近义词: divalent cobalt | 反义词: cobaltic
