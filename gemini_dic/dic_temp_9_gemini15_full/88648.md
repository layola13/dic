hoard:/hɔːrd/| n. v.|囤积；储藏；宝藏 | A large accumulation of something, typically something valuable or precious.  To accumulate a large quantity of something, typically in a secretive or greedy way. |例句：The dragon guarded its hoard of gold.  这条龙守护着它的金银财宝。 |例句：He hoarded his money, refusing to spend it. 他囤积钱财，不肯花钱。|近义词： stockpile, amass, cache, accumulate |反义词： disperse, distribute, squander


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hoards old newspapers. = 他囤积旧报纸。
She hoards information. = 她囤积信息。

一般过去时:
They hoarded food during the war. = 他们在战争期间囤积食物。
The farmer hoarded his grain. = 这位农民囤积了他的谷物。

现在完成时:
He has hoarded enough money for retirement. = 他已经攒够了退休金。
They have hoarded supplies for the coming storm. = 他们已经为即将到来的暴风雨囤积了物资。

过去完成时:
She had hoarded all her savings for years. = 她多年来一直囤积着所有的积蓄。
They had hoarded food before the famine. = 饥荒前他们囤积了食物。

将来时:
He will hoard his winnings. = 他将囤积他的奖金。
They will hoard resources for the future. = 他们将为未来囤积资源。

虚拟语气:
If I had a lot of money, I would hoard it. = 如果我有许多钱，我会囤积它。
I wish I hadn't hoarded all that junk. = 我真希望我没有囤积所有那些垃圾。

被动语态:
The treasure was hoarded for centuries. = 这笔宝藏被囤积了几个世纪。
Resources are being hoarded by the government. = 资源正被政府囤积。

疑问句:
Does he hoard anything valuable? = 他囤积任何贵重物品吗？
Are they hoarding essential goods? = 他们正在囤积必需品吗？

否定句:
He doesn't hoard money. = 他不囤积钱。
They didn't hoard food during the drought. = 干旱期间他们没有囤积食物。
