USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses, moods, or voices.  It's used to describe a condition.

例句:

The doctor ruled out true anemia and diagnosed the patient with a pseudoanemic condition.  医生排除了真正的贫血，并诊断病人为假性贫血。

Her pseudoanemic symptoms were caused by a nutritional deficiency, not a lack of red blood cells.  她的假性贫血症状是由营养缺乏引起的，而不是缺乏红细胞。
