depend:/dɪˈpend/ | v. |依靠，依赖，取决于 | To rely on someone or something for support, help, or supply.  |  My happiness depends on you.我的幸福取决于你。 We depend on the rain for our crops. 我们依靠雨水来种植庄稼。| rely on, count on | be independent of | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He depends on his parents for financial support. 他依靠父母提供经济支持。
The success of the project depends on teamwork.  项目的成功取决于团队合作。

一般过去时:
She depended on her friends for emotional support during the difficult time. 在困难时期，她依靠朋友的情感支持。
The outcome of the election depended on the turnout.  选举的结果取决于投票率。


现在完成时:
We have depended on fossil fuels for too long. 我们依赖化石燃料的时间太长了。
They have depended on each other for years. 他们互相依赖多年了。


过去完成时:
I had depended on him to finish the report, but he didn't. 我依赖他完成报告，但他没有。
The company had depended on that contract for its survival. 那家公司一直依靠那份合同来维持生存。


将来时:
We will depend on renewable energy sources in the future. 未来我们将依靠可再生能源。
The economy will depend on the government's policies. 经济将取决于政府的政策。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't depend on my car, I would use public transport more. 如果我不依赖我的车，我会更多地使用公共交通工具。
I wish I didn't depend so much on caffeine. 我希望我不那么依赖咖啡因。


被动语态:
The decision will be depended on the results of the survey.  决定将取决于调查结果。
The outcome of the case depended on the evidence presented.  案件的结果取决于提交的证据。


疑问句:
Does the plan depend on the weather? 计划取决于天气吗？
What does the outcome depend on?  结果取决于什么？


否定句:
The success of the project does not depend on one person. 项目的成功并不取决于一个人。
The result doesn't depend on luck alone. 结果不仅仅取决于运气。


PHRASE->
depend on/upon = 依靠，取决于
depend for = 依赖于...方面
it depends = 看情况
that depends = 那要看情况
depending on = 取决于
