shellfish:/ˈʃɛlfɪʃ/| n. |贝类，甲壳类动物|Any of numerous aquatic invertebrates, including crustaceans (such as crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and prawns) and mollusks (such as clams, mussels, oysters, and scallops), used as food.  |例句：I'm allergic to shellfish. 我对贝类过敏。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its fresh shellfish. 这家餐馆以新鲜的贝类而闻名。|近义词：crustacean, mollusk, seafood|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "land animals" or "vegetables".|名词
