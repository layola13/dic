mudbath:/ˈmʌdbɑːθ/| n.|泥浴，泥浆浴|A mudbath is a beauty treatment or therapeutic application involving the application of mud to the body.  It's often associated with spas and wellness centers. The mud is believed to have various skin benefits.|例句：She enjoyed a luxurious mudbath at the spa. 她享受了水疗中心豪华的泥浴。

例句：The volcanic mudbath was said to have healing properties.据说火山泥浴具有治疗功效。


近义词：mud pack, clay bath|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to a shower or bath with clean water.
