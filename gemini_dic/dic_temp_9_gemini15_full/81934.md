grimacer:/ɡrɪˈmeɪsər/| v.|做鬼脸，皱眉表示厌恶|To make a grimace; to distort one's face in an expression of disgust, pain, or disapproval. |例句：He grimaced at the bitter taste of the medicine. 他对药的苦味做了个鬼脸。|例句：She grimaced in pain after hitting her knee. 她撞到膝盖后痛苦地皱起了眉头。|近义词：wince, frown, scowl|反义词：smile, beam


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grimaces at the sight of blood. = 他看到血就做鬼脸。
She grimaces every time she eats spinach. = 她每次吃菠菜都做鬼脸。

一般过去时:
He grimaced when he heard the bad news. = 他听到坏消息时皱起了眉头。
She grimaced at the loud noise. = 她对巨大的噪音做了个鬼脸。

现在完成时:
He has grimaced throughout the entire painful procedure. = 在整个痛苦的过程中，他一直都在做鬼脸。
She has grimaced so much today that her face aches. = 她今天做了太多鬼脸，脸都疼了。

过去完成时:
He had grimaced at the sight of the rotten food before he threw it away. = 他在扔掉腐烂的食物之前，就对它做了个鬼脸。
She had grimaced throughout the entire movie because of the terrible acting. = 因为糟糕的演技，她在整部电影中都一直皱着眉头。

将来时:

She will grimace if she has to eat that again. = 如果她不得不再次吃那个东西，她会做鬼脸。
