residentship:/ˈrezɪdəntʃɪp/| n. |居住权；住院医师资格；居住身份|The state of being a resident; the period of time spent as a resident, especially in a hospital or medical training program.  It can also refer to the right to live in a particular place.|例句：He completed his residency in cardiology. 他完成了心脏病学的住院医师培训。|例句：Her residency in the country provided a welcome escape from city life.她在乡村的居住生活让她从城市生活中得到了一次受欢迎的逃离。|近义词：residence, inhabitation, dwelling|反义词：absence, departure
