pettywhin:/ˈpɛtiˌhwɪn/| n. |琐碎抱怨；唠叨；无足轻重的抱怨 |A pettywhine is a complaint or grievance that is considered trivial or insignificant. It often involves excessive complaining about minor issues.  |例句：Her constant pettywhines about the office temperature were driving everyone crazy.  她的不断抱怨办公室温度太高，把大家都逼疯了。|近义词：complaint, grumble, gripe, whinge, quibble |反义词：praise, compliment, commendation
