play-along: /ˌpleɪ əˈlɒŋ/ | v. | 附和；配合；参与；假装配合 | To participate in an activity, often pretending to be enthusiastic or knowledgeable, even if one isn't.  It can also mean to cooperate or go along with something. |

例句：

He played along with her joke, even though he didn't understand it. = 他附和她的玩笑，即使他不明白。

The actors played along with the director's unusual requests. = 演员们配合导演不同寻常的要求。


近义词：cooperate, participate, comply, pretend, feign


反义词：resist, oppose, object, defy


词性：动词
