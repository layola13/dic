disaffiliations:/ˌdɪsæˌfɪliˈeɪʃənz/| n.| 脱离；断绝关系；退会 | The plural form of disaffiliation, referring to multiple instances of separating oneself from an affiliation or organization.  It implies a formal or official severing of ties. | Example: The recent disaffiliations from the national organization signal a growing dissatisfaction among member groups. 近期从全国性组织的脱离表明成员群体中日益增长的不满情绪。| 近义词：separations, withdrawals, breakups | 反义词：affiliations, associations, unions | 名词
