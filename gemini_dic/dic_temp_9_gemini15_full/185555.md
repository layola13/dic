reveal: /rɪˈviːl/ | vt. |揭露；展现；显示 | To make (something) known or visible.  |例句：The investigation revealed a shocking truth.  调查揭露了一个令人震惊的真相。

例句：The actor finally revealed his true identity. 演员最终揭露了他的真实身份。


近义词：disclose, unveil, uncover, show, display | 反义词：conceal, hide, mask, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reveals his secrets to his close friends. 他向他的密友们透露他的秘密。
The painting reveals a hidden message. 这幅画揭示了一个隐藏的信息。


一般过去时:
She revealed the truth about the accident. 她揭露了事故的真相。
The scientist revealed his groundbreaking discovery. 科学家公布了他的突破性发现。


现在完成时:
The investigation has revealed new evidence. 调查已经发现了新的证据。
They have revealed their plans for the future. 他们已经透露了他们未来的计划。


过去完成时:
He had revealed his intentions before the meeting. 他在会议前已经透露了他的意图。
The documents had revealed a conspiracy. 文件已经揭露了一场阴谋。


将来时:
The company will reveal its new product next week. 公司将于下周发布其新产品。
He will reveal the secret at the right time. 他会在适当的时候揭露这个秘密。


虚拟语气:
If he revealed the truth, everything would change. 如果他揭露真相，一切都会改变。
I wish he had revealed his feelings earlier. 我希望他早点表达他的感受。


被动语态:
The truth was revealed to the public. 真相被公之于众。
The secret was revealed by an anonymous source. 这个秘密是由一个匿名消息来源透露的。


疑问句:
Will the report reveal any new information? 报告会透露任何新信息吗？
What will the results reveal? 结果会显示什么？


否定句:
He did not reveal his source. 他没有透露他的消息来源。
The company refused to reveal its financial details. 公司拒绝透露其财务细节。


PHRASE->
reveal oneself  自我展现
reveal the truth  揭露真相
reveal a secret  泄露秘密
reveal the details  透露细节
reveal one's feelings 表达感情

词源:
Old French *reveiller (to unveil, disclose), from re- + veiller (to watch).  The meaning shifted from "to watch carefully"  to "to make visible."
