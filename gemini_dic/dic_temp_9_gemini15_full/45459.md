curations:/kjʊˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |策展；精选；管理 |The careful selection and arrangement of items for exhibition or display.  It implies a thoughtful and considered process, often with a specific theme or narrative in mind. Curations can apply to art, music, food, experiences, and more. |例句：The museum's curations of contemporary art are always impressive.  博物馆的当代艺术策展总是令人印象深刻。

例句：The online store's curations of vintage clothing are highly sought after.  这家网店的复古服装精选非常抢手。


近义词：selection, collection, arrangement, exhibition

反义词：randomness, haphazardness


词性：名词
