disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/| vt.|打扰，扰乱，使不安|to interrupt someone or something; to make someone feel anxious or upset|例句：Please don't disturb me while I'm working.  请在我工作的时候不要打扰我。|例句：The noise disturbed her sleep. 噪音扰乱了她的睡眠。|近义词：interrupt, bother, upset, annoy|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog disturbs the neighbours every night. = 这条狗每天晚上都打扰邻居。
He disturbs me with his constant questions. = 他不断地提问题，让我很烦。

一般过去时:
The loud music disturbed my concentration. = 嘈杂的音乐扰乱了我的注意力。
The news disturbed him deeply. = 这个消息深深地扰乱了他。

现在完成时:
Nothing has disturbed the peace of the village. = 没有什么扰乱过这个村庄的宁静。
The scandal has disturbed many people. = 这场丑闻已经扰乱了许多人。

过去完成时:
The storm had disturbed the birds' nests. = 暴风雨已经毁掉了鸟巢。
They had disturbed the evidence at the crime scene. = 他们在犯罪现场扰乱了证据。

将来时:
The construction work will disturb the residents. = 建设工程将会打扰居民。
I fear the upcoming exam will disturb my sleep. = 我担心即将到来的考试会影响我的睡眠。

虚拟语气:
If you disturbed him, he would get angry. = 如果你打扰他，他会生气的。
I wish the noise hadn't disturbed my sleep. = 我真希望噪音没有扰乱我的睡眠。

被动语态:
His sleep was disturbed by the noise. = 他的睡眠被噪音扰乱了。
The meeting was disturbed by unexpected visitors. = 会议被不速之客打断了。

疑问句:
Does the noise disturb you? = 噪音打扰你吗？
Will the construction disturb the local wildlife? = 建设会干扰当地野生动物吗？

否定句:
The quiet atmosphere didn't disturb her. = 安静的氛围没有打扰她。
Please don't disturb the flowers. = 请不要弄坏这些花。
