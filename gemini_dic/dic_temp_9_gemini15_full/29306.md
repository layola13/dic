castellan:/ˌkæstɪˈlɑːn/| n.|城堡管理员；要塞指挥官；（尤指西班牙）城堡的管理员|A castellan is a person who is in charge of a castle or fortress, especially in Spain.  The term often implies responsibility for the castle's maintenance and security. |Example: The castellan proudly showed visitors the ancient castle's armory.  城堡管理员自豪地向游客展示了古老城堡的军械库。|近义词：governor, warden, custodian|反义词：inhabitant, resident|名词
