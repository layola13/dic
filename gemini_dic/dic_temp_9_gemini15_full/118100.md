menion:/ˈmɛnʃən/|v.|提及，说起|To make mention of; to refer to briefly.|He mentioned that he was going to the library. 他提到他要到图书馆去。|近义词：refer to, allude to, cite|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mentions his trip to Paris every time we meet. 他每次见到我们都提到他去巴黎的旅行。
She mentions her new job in every conversation. 她在每次谈话中都提到她的新工作。


一般过去时:
He mentioned seeing her at the store yesterday. 他提到昨天在商店看到她了。
She mentioned that she would be late. 她提到她会迟到。


现在完成时:
He has mentioned his plans to travel to Asia several times. 他已经多次提到他去亚洲旅行的计划。
She has mentioned the problem to her boss. 她已经向老板提到了这个问题。


过去完成时:
He had mentioned the project before the meeting. 他在会议之前就提到了这个项目。
She had mentioned her concerns earlier that day. 她那天早些时候就提到了她的担忧。


将来时:
He will mention the issue at the next meeting. 他将在下次会议上提及这个问题。
She will mention it to her parents soon. 她很快就会跟她的父母提起这件事。


虚拟语气:
I wish he hadn't mentioned that. 我真希望他没有提到那件事。
If he mentioned it, I would be surprised. 如果他提到它，我会很惊讶。


被动语态:
The incident was mentioned briefly in the news report.  新闻报道简要提到了这一事件。
His name was mentioned in the article.  他的名字在文章中被提到了。


疑问句:
Did she mention anything about the party? 她有没有提到聚会的事？
Has he mentioned when he'll be back? 他有没有提到他什么时候回来？


否定句:
He didn't mention anything about his family. 他没有提到任何关于他家人的事情。
She didn't mention seeing him. 她没有提到见过他。


PHRASE->
make mention of: 提及
a passing mention: 一带而过地提及
worthy of mention: 值得一提
with mention of:  提到…
fail to mention: 忘记提及
