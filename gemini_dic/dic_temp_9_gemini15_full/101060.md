inventers:/ɪnˈvɛntərz/| n. |发明者；创造者 |People who invent things, creating new devices, processes, or ideas.  Inventors are often driven by curiosity and a desire to improve existing technologies or solve problems.  They may work independently or as part of a team. |Example:  Many great inventors faced numerous setbacks before achieving success. 许多伟大的发明家在取得成功之前都面临着无数的挫折。

The Wright brothers were renowned inventors.  莱特兄弟是著名的发明家。|近义词：creators, innovators, designers |反义词：destroyers, dismantlers
