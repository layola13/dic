maidan:/ˈmeɪdæn/| n.|广场，空地|A large open space, especially in a city or town, often used for public gatherings or events.  It's often a central area in a city.|例句：The people gathered in the maidan to protest the government. 人们聚集在广场上抗议政府。|近义词：square, plaza, public space|反义词：building, enclosed area
