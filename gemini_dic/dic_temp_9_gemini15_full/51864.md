A dextrorotatory substance rotates the plane of polarized light clockwise. | Example: The solution exhibited a dextrorotatory effect on polarized light. 该溶液对偏振光显示出右旋效应。 | 近义词：right-rotating, dextro | 反义词：levorotatory
