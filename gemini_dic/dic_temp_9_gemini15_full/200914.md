slippering:/ˈslɪpərɪŋ/| v.| 拖着脚走，滑行，偷偷地移动 |  The act of slipping or sliding; moving smoothly and quietly, often stealthily.  It can also refer to the state of being slippery or prone to slipping. |例句：The thief was slippering through the shadows. 小偷在阴影中偷偷摸摸地走着。

例句：The car tires were slippering on the icy road.  汽车轮胎在结冰的路上打滑。


近义词：sliding, gliding, creeping, sneaking, skidding |反义词：striding, marching, walking firmly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is slippering across the floor. = 他正在地板上拖着脚走。
The cat is slippering through the grass. = 猫正在草丛中偷偷地走着。

一般过去时:
She slippered into the room quietly. = 她悄悄地溜进了房间。
The dog slippered on the wet tiles. = 狗在潮湿的地砖上滑倒了。

现在完成时:
He has been slippering around all day. = 他整天都在偷偷摸摸地走动。
The rain has made the path slippering. = 雨水使小路变得湿滑。

过去完成时:
They had slippered away before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经偷偷溜走了。
The ice had made the road slippering before the accident. = 事故发生前，冰雪已使道路变得湿滑。


将来时:

I think it will be slippering outside tomorrow. = 我想明天外面会很滑。


虚拟语气:
If the road wasn't so slippering, I would have arrived earlier. = 如果路不那么滑，我会早点到的。

被动语态:
The floor was slippered by the spilled water. = 地板被洒出的水弄湿了变得很滑。 (Note:  This usage is less common.  "Made slippery" would be more natural.)


疑问句:

否定句:
