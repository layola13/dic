pummels:/ˈpʌməlz/| vt.|猛击；痛打；连续猛击|To strike repeatedly with the fists or a similar object; to batter severely.|例句：The boxer pummeled his opponent until he was knocked out. 拳击手猛击对手直到将其击倒。|例句：The waves pummeled the shore relentlessly. 海浪无情地冲击着海岸。|近义词：beat, batter, thrash, pound|反义词：caress, soothe
