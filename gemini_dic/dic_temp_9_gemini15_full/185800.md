revision:/rɪˈvɪʒən/ | n. | 修订；修改；复习 | The process of making corrections or improvements to something.  It can refer to reviewing something to improve it, or studying something again to prepare for a test. | Example: The author made several revisions to the manuscript before publication. 作者在出版前对稿件做了几次修改。  The students are doing revisions for the upcoming exam. 学生们正在为即将到来的考试复习。 | 近义词：amendment, correction, review, study | 反义词：original, first draft | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes revisions to his essays regularly. = 他定期修改他的论文。
She revises her notes after each lecture. = 每次讲座后，她都会修改笔记。


一般过去时:
They revised the contract before signing it. = 他们在签署合同之前修改了合同。
The editor revised the article to improve clarity. = 编辑修改了文章以提高清晰度。


现在完成时:
I have revised my presentation several times. = 我已经修改了我的演示文稿几次了。
She has revised her opinion on the matter. = 她已经改变了她对这件事的看法。


过去完成时:
They had revised the plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经修改了计划。
He had revised his notes before the exam. = 他在考试前已经复习了他的笔记。


将来时:
We will revise the budget next week. = 我们下周将修改预算。
She will revise her application before submitting it. = 她将在提交申请之前修改她的申请。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would revise my work. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会修改我的作品。
I wish I had revised my essay more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地修改我的论文。


被动语态:
The document was revised by the committee. = 文件由委员会修改。
The plan is being revised at the moment. = 计划正在修改中。


疑问句:
Have you revised your notes for the test? = 你复习了考试笔记了吗？
Will the company revise its policy? = 公司会修改其政策吗？


否定句:
He did not revise his work before submitting it. = 他在提交作业之前没有修改他的作业。
She hasn't revised her essay yet. = 她还没有修改她的论文。


PHRASE->
under revision:  正在修改中
major revision:  大幅度修改
minor revision:  小幅度修改
final revision:  最终修改
revision control: 版本控制
