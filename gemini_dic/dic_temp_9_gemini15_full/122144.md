mistresses: /ˈmɪstrəsɪz/ | n. |情妇；女主人；女教师；女统治者 |

英文解析:The word "mistress" has multiple meanings, often depending on context.  Its primary modern usage refers to a woman who has a sexual relationship with a man who is married or in a committed relationship with someone else.  Historically, and still in some contexts, it can also refer to a woman who is the head of a household, a female teacher, or a woman who holds a position of power or authority.


例句:

He was caught having an affair with his mistress. = 他被发现与他的情妇有染。

The mistress of the house welcomed us warmly. = 女主人热情地欢迎了我们。


近义词:  (depending on context) lover, paramour, concubine, wife (in the sense of head of household), teacher,  governess


反义词:  (depending on context)  wife (in the sense of a legally married spouse), master, servant


词性: noun
