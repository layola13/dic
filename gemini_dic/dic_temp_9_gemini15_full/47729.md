decagon:/ˈdekəɡɒn/| n. |十边形 |A decagon is a polygon with ten sides and ten angles.  Decagons can be regular (all sides and angles are equal) or irregular (sides and angles are not equal).|Example:  The architect designed a building with a decagonal base. 建筑师设计了一座底座为十边形的建筑物。|近义词:ten-sided polygon|反义词:triangle, quadrilateral, pentagon (implying fewer sides)
