neutralised: /ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzd/ | v. | 中和；使无效；抵消 | To make something neutral or ineffective; to counteract or cancel the effect of something. |  The acid was neutralised with an alkali. 碱中和了酸。| The police quickly neutralised the threat.警方迅速化解了威胁。| counteracted, cancelled out | intensified, exacerbated | 动词
