overappraise:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪz/ | vt. | 过高评价；估价过高 | To appraise something at too high a value.  | 例句：The antique dealer overappraised the painting, hoping for a quick sale. 古董商高估了这幅画的价格，希望能快速出手。

例句：The real estate agent overappraised the house, leading to a difficult sale. 房地产经纪人高估了房子的价格，导致难以出售。


近义词：overvalue, overestimate, inflate


反义词：undervalue, underestimate


词性：动词
