menstruous:/ˈmɛnstruəs/| adj.|行经的；月经期的；|Having or relating to menstruation.  Characterized by or associated with menstruation.  Often used in a negative or euphemistic context. |例句：She felt unwell during her menstruous period. = 她在月经期间感到不舒服。

She experienced severe cramps during her menstruous cycle. = 她在月经周期中经历了严重的痉挛。


近义词：menstrual, menstruating |反义词：non-menstruating
