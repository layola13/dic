gena:/ˈdʒiːnə/| n.|基因|A unit of heredity which is transferred from a parent to offspring and is held to determine some characteristic of the offspring.|例句：Genes determine many of our physical characteristics.  基因决定了我们的许多生理特征。|近义词：allele, factor|反义词: phenotype
