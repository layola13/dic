militarizations:/ˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |军事化；军事行动|The process of equipping or organizing something for military purposes;  the act of making something military in character.  It can refer to a country, region, or even an organization. |例句：The militarization of the border region increased tensions between the two countries. 边境地区的军事化加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。
The rapid militarization of the police force sparked widespread protests. 警察部队的迅速军事化引发了大规模抗议。|近义词：armament, fortification, militarism|反义词：demilitarization, pacifism
