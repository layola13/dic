preconfigures:/ˌpriːkənˈfɪɡjərz/| vt.|预先配置；预先设定|To configure (something) beforehand or in advance.  This typically involves setting up parameters, options, or settings before the system or device is put into operation. |例句：The IT department preconfigures all new computers with the necessary software.  IT部门预先为所有新电脑配置必要的软件。

The factory preconfigures the machines with specific settings for optimal performance. 工厂预先为机器配置特定的设置以获得最佳性能。


近义词：preset, pre-set, configure beforehand, set up in advance


反义词：postconfigure (This word is not commonly used, the opposite action would be described as configuring after initial use or deployment.)


词性:动词

词源:
The word "preconfigures" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before" or "in advance") and the verb "configures."  "Configure" itself comes from the Latin "configurare," meaning "to form," "to shape," or "to arrange." Therefore, "preconfigures" literally means "to arrange or set up beforehand."
