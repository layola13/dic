outclerks:/ˈaʊtklɜːrks/ | n. | 外勤职员，(尤指银行的)出纳员 | An outclerk is a clerk who works outside of a main office, often handling transactions or deliveries.  This term is somewhat archaic and more common in historical contexts, particularly relating to banking. | The outclerk delivered the important documents to the client.  = 外勤职员将重要文件送达客户。The bank's outclerks handled the daily cash transactions. = 银行的外勤职员处理日常现金交易。|  clerk, messenger, runner |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with "in-office clerk") | 名词
