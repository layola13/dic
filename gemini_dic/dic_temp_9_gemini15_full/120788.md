misarrange:/ˌmɪsəˈreɪndʒ/| vt.|安排错，弄乱|To arrange something badly or incorrectly; to put something in the wrong order or place.|例句：He misarranged the files, making it difficult to find the important documents. 他把文件弄乱了，导致难以找到重要文件。


例句：The unexpected rain misarranged our outdoor picnic plans.  突如其来的雨水打乱了我们的户外野餐计划。|近义词：disarrange, disorganize, jumble, muddle|反义词：arrange, organize, order
