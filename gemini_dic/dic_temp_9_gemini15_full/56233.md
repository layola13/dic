doffed:/dɒft/ | v. |脱掉（帽子、衣服等）|To remove (something, especially a hat or clothing) from oneself or another.  The word often implies a formal or respectful removal.|He doffed his hat to the queen. 他向女王脱帽致敬。|近义词：remove, take off, shed|反义词：don


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doffs his hat to the ladies. = 他向女士们脱帽致敬。
She doffs her gloves before entering the building. = 她进入大楼前脱掉手套。

一般过去时:
He doffed his cap as he passed the old woman. = 他经过那位老妇人时脱下了帽子。
The knight doffed his helmet after the battle. = 骑士战斗结束后脱下了头盔。


现在完成时:
She has doffed her coat and is now comfortable. = 她已经脱掉了外套，现在很舒服。
He has doffed his shoes and is relaxing by the fireplace. = 他已经脱掉了鞋子，正在壁炉旁放松。


过去完成时:
He had doffed his uniform before entering the party. = 他在参加聚会之前已经脱掉了制服。
She had doffed her jewelry before going to bed. = 她睡觉前已经脱掉了首饰。


将来时:
He will doff his hat when he sees the president. = 他见到总统时会脱帽致敬。
She will doff her shoes before entering the temple. = 她进入寺庙前会脱鞋。


虚拟语气:
If he were a gentleman, he would doff his hat. = 如果他是个绅士，他会脱帽致敬的。
If she were to meet the king, she would doff her curtsy. = 如果她要见到国王，她会行屈膝礼的。


被动语态:
His hat was doffed in respect. = 他的帽子被恭敬地脱掉了。
Her gloves were doffed before the ceremony. = 仪式前她的手套被脱掉了。


疑问句:
Did he doff his hat to show respect? = 他脱帽是为了表示尊敬吗？
Will she doff her shoes before entering? = 她进入前会脱鞋吗？


否定句:
He didn't doff his hat to the stranger. = 他没有向陌生人脱帽。
She didn't doff her coat, even though it was cold. = 即使很冷，她也没有脱外套。


PHRASE->
doff one's hat to somebody = 向某人脱帽致敬
doff one's disguise = 脱掉伪装
doff one's clothes = 脱掉衣服
doff and don = 脱掉再穿上 (反复穿脱)
doff one's responsibilities = 放下责任


词源:
The word "doff" comes from the Old English "dofian," meaning "to do off."  It's a contraction of "do off," and its counterpart, "don," shares a similar origin, meaning "to do on."
