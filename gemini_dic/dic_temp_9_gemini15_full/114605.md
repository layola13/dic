maledict:/ˈmælədɪkt/| v. |诅咒，咒骂|To utter a curse or malediction upon; to speak evil of.  It implies a formal and often solemn curse, usually invoking harm or misfortune.|例句：The witch maledicted her enemies, wishing them ill fortune.  女巫诅咒她的敌人，祝愿他们厄运连连。


The villagers maledicted the drought, praying for rain. 村民们诅咒干旱，祈祷降雨。|近义词：curse, imprecate, execrate |反义词：bless, consecrate
