fantasized:/ˈfæntəsaɪzd/| v.|幻想，梦想|To imagine or create something in one's mind, often in a vivid or fanciful way.|例句：She fantasized about living in a castle by the sea. 她幻想住在海边的城堡里。

例句：He fantasized about winning the lottery and retiring early. 他幻想赢得彩票然后提前退休。


近义词：dreamed, imagined, daydreamed|反义词：faced reality, confronted reality|动词


USAGE->

I fantasize about traveling the world. = 我幻想环游世界。
She fantasizes about becoming a famous actress. = 她幻想成为一名著名女演员。
