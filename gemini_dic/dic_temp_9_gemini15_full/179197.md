rediscover:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌvər/| vt.|重新发现；再次发现|To discover something again after it has been forgotten or lost for a period of time.  It implies a previous discovery that had been lost or forgotten.|例句：He rediscovered his love for painting after a long break. 他在长时间的休息后重新发现了对绘画的热爱。
例句：Archaeologists rediscovered an ancient city buried under the sand. 考古学家重新发现了一个埋在沙子下的古城。|近义词：reinvent, recover, find again|反义词：lose, misplace


USAGE->

I rediscover my passion for music every time I listen to my favorite band. = 每当我听我最喜欢的乐队演奏时，我都会重新发现我对音乐的热情。
They rediscover new talents within their team regularly. = 他们定期在团队中重新发现新的才能。
