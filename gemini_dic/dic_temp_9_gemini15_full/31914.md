chef:/ʃɛf/ | n. |厨师，主厨 | A chef is a professional cook, typically the head cook in a restaurant or other establishment.  |例句：Gordon Ramsay is a famous chef.  戈登·拉姆齐是一位著名的厨师。|例句：The chef prepared a delicious meal. 主厨准备了一顿美味的饭菜。|近义词：cook, cook, culinary artist |反义词：customer, diner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef prepares the food. = 厨师准备食物。
He cooks delicious meals. = 他烹调美味佳肴。


一般过去时:
The chef prepared a special dish yesterday. = 厨师昨天准备了一道特别的菜。
She cooked dinner for her family. = 她为家人做了晚餐。


现在完成时:
The chef has prepared many meals. = 厨师已经准备了许多餐点。
He has cooked for numerous celebrities. = 他已经为许多名流做过饭。


过去完成时:
The chef had already prepared the ingredients before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，厨师已经准备好食材了。
She had cooked the main course before the dessert. = 甜点之前，她已经做好了主菜。


将来时:
The chef will prepare a new menu next week. = 厨师下周将准备一份新的菜单。
He will cook a special feast for the holiday. = 他将为节日准备一顿特别的盛宴。


虚拟语气:
If I were a chef, I would open my own restaurant. = 如果我是一名厨师，我会开一家自己的餐厅。
I wish I could cook as well as that chef. = 我希望我能像那位厨师一样擅长烹饪。


被动语态:
The meal was prepared by the chef. = 这顿饭是由厨师准备的。
The dishes are being cooked in the kitchen. = 菜肴正在厨房里烹制。


疑问句:

否定句:
