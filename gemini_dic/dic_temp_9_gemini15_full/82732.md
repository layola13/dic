guideway:/ˈɡaɪdweɪ/| n. |导向轨道；导轨；引导道 | A guideway is a specially constructed path or structure that guides the movement of vehicles or objects.  It can be used for various transportation systems like trains, automated guided vehicles (AGVs), and even roller coasters. | Example: The maglev train runs on a dedicated guideway.  磁悬浮列车运行在专用导轨上。 The automated guided vehicle follows the guideway precisely. 自动导引车精确地沿着导轨行驶。 |近义词：track, path, channel |反义词：open space, free path
