interrupts:/ˈɪntəˌrʌpts/| vt.|打断，中断，干扰|To break the continuity of (a process, activity, or course of events).  It can also refer to the act of stopping something from continuing.|例句：The loud noise interrupted my concentration.  大声的噪音打断了我的注意力。

例句：The meeting was interrupted by a sudden power outage. 会议因突然停电而中断。
