jump-board:/dʒʌmpˌbɔːrd/ | n. |跳板；跳跃板 | A platform or board used for jumping, often in sports or recreational activities.  |例句：He used the jump-board to launch himself into the air. 他用跳板把自己抛向空中。
                                    |例句：The gymnasts practiced their routines on the jump-board. 体操运动员在跳板上练习他们的动作。|近义词：springboard, trampoline (for certain types)|反义词：None specifically.
