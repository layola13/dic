RSA: /ˈɑːrsˌeɪ/ |abbr.|  RSA算法，公钥密码体制 |  RSA is an acronym for Rivest–Shamir–Adleman, a public-key cryptosystem.  It is widely used for secure data transmission.  The security of RSA relies on the practical difficulty of factoring the product of two large prime numbers, the factoring problem.  | 例句：RSA encryption is used to protect sensitive data online.  RSA加密用于保护在线敏感数据。 | 近义词：public-key cryptography, asymmetric cryptography | 反义词：无
