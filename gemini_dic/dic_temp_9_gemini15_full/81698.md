greenhill:/ˈɡriːnˌhɪl/| n. |绿山；绿丘|A green hill is a hill covered with green vegetation.  It can refer to a specific place or be a general term.  The term often evokes a sense of peacefulness and natural beauty.|例句：We walked up the green hill to enjoy the sunset. 我们走上绿山欣赏日落。

例句：The sheep grazed peacefully on the green hill.  羊群在绿色的山丘上平静地吃草。


近义词：green slope, verdant hill, grassy knoll|反义词：barren hill, rocky peak|词性:名词
