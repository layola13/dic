disinvolve:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɒlv/ | vt. | 使不参与；使脱离；使免除 | To remove (someone or something) from involvement; to disentangle; to free from involvement.  |例句：The company decided to disinvolve itself from the controversial project. 公司决定退出有争议的项目。

例句：He tried to disinvolve himself from the family's arguments. 他试图让自己不卷入家庭的争吵。


近义词：exclude, detach, disengage, extricate | 反义词：involve, engage, entangle | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disinvolve themselves from the conflict. = 他们让自己不卷入冲突。
The company disinvolves itself from risky ventures. = 公司避免参与风险投资。

一般过去时:
He disinvolved himself from the political campaign. = 他退出了政治竞选活动。
She disinvolved her son from the problematic situation. = 她让儿子摆脱了那个棘手的情况。

现在完成时:
They have disinvolved themselves from the charity. = 他们已经不再参与慈善活动了。
The government has disinvolved itself from the private sector. = 政府已经与私营部门脱钩了。

过去完成时:
He had disinvolved himself from the project before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经退出了该项目。
She had disinvolved her daughter from the toxic friendship before it caused more harm. = 在有害友谊造成更多伤害之前，她已经让女儿摆脱了它。

将来时:
They will disinvolve themselves from the deal. = 他们将退出这笔交易。
The organization will disinvolve itself from the controversial issue. = 该组织将不再参与有争议的问题。

虚拟语气:
If I could, I would disinvolve myself from this messy situation. = 如果可以的话，我会让自己摆脱这种混乱的局面。
I wish I had disinvolved myself from that project earlier. = 我真希望我早点退出那个项目。

被动语态:
He was disinvolved from the investigation. = 他被排除在调查之外。
The company was disinvolved from the partnership. = 公司退出了合作关系。

疑问句:
Will they disinvolve themselves from the project? = 他们会退出这个项目吗？
Can he disinvolve himself from the family drama? = 他能让自己摆脱家庭纠纷吗？

否定句:
They did not disinvolve themselves from the problem. = 他们没有让自己摆脱这个问题。
The government will not disinvolve itself from the economic crisis. = 政府不会置身于经济危机之外。


PHRASE->

disinvolve completely = 完全脱离
