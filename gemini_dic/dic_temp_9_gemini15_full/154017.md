performance-arts:/pərˈfɔːrməns ɑːrts/| n. |表演艺术|The performing arts encompass a range of disciplines involving artistic expression through performance.  These typically include dance, music, theatre, and opera, but can also extend to other forms like circus arts and puppetry. The emphasis is on the live execution of the art form before an audience. |Example:  The university offers a wide range of performance arts courses, from ballet to musical theatre. 大学提供各种表演艺术课程，从芭蕾舞到音乐剧。 The student's performance arts project was a stunning display of talent and creativity.  学生的表演艺术项目展现了令人惊叹的天赋和创造力。|近义词：spectacular arts, stage arts |反义词：visual arts, fine arts
