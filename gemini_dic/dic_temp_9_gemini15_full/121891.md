missal:/ˈmɪsəl/| n.|弥撒书，弥撒经书|A book containing the texts and rituals for celebrating the Mass in the Roman Catholic Church.|例句：The priest used a missal to guide him through the Mass.  = 牧师用弥撒书指导他完成弥撒。

例句：The ornate missal was a treasured heirloom. = 这本装饰华丽的弥撒书是珍贵的传家宝。


词源：源自拉丁语 *missale，意为“弥撒书”，由missa（弥撒）派生而来。


近义词：mass book, liturgical book


反义词：无


词性：名词
