nonmercenaries: /ˌnɒnˈmɜːrsənəriːz/ | n. | 非雇佣兵；非职业士兵 |  People who are not mercenaries; those who fight for reasons other than financial gain.  They are motivated by ideology, patriotism, or other non-monetary factors. | The nonmercenaries fought bravely for their country, driven by a deep sense of national pride.  非雇佣兵们为了他们的国家勇敢地战斗，他们的动力是强烈的民族自豪感。


近义词: volunteers, patriots, conscripts, regulars (in contrast to mercenaries)


反义词: mercenaries
