shakily: /ˈʃeɪkɪli/ | adv. | 颤抖地；摇晃地；不稳定地 |  Describes movement or action that is unsteady or wavering, often due to weakness, nervousness, or instability.  | 例句：He walked shakily across the room. 他摇摇晃晃地走过房间。

She spoke shakily, her voice barely a whisper. 她颤抖着说话，声音几乎是耳语。| 近义词：tremulously, unsteadily, weakly | 反义词：steadily, firmly, confidently
