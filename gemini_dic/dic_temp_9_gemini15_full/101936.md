islet:/ˈaɪlət/| n. |小岛，小洲 |A small island.  Islets are often found in groups or near larger islands. |例句：A tiny islet lay just off the coast.  一个小岛位于海岸附近。

例句： We sailed past several picturesque islets. 我们驶过了几个风景如画的小岛。


近义词：islet, small island, isle, cay


反义词：continent, mainland
