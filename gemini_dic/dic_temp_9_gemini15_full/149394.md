pack-it-in: /pæk ɪt ɪn/| phrasal verb|停止；结束；放弃|To stop doing something, especially something that is annoying or causing problems.  It's an informal expression. |例句：It's getting late, let's pack it in and go home.  该晚啦，咱们收工回家吧。|例句：The noise was unbearable; I told them to pack it in.  噪音太大了，我让他们停止。|近义词：stop, quit, cease, give up|反义词：continue, persevere


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pack it in at 5 pm every day. = 他们每天下午5点收工。
We pack it in when the weather gets bad. = 天气变坏时我们就停止。


一般过去时:
They packed it in early because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们早早收工了。
We packed it in after three hours of work. = 我们工作了三个小时后就停下来了。


现在完成时:
We've packed it in for the night. = 我们今晚收工了。
They've packed it in after a long and tiring day. = 他们经历了漫长而疲惫的一天后停工了。


过去完成时:
They had packed it in before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们就已经停工了。
We had packed it in long before the deadline. = 我们早在截止日期很久之前就完成了。


将来时:
We'll pack it in when we finish this project. = 我们完成这个项目后就收工。
They'll pack it in as soon as it starts to rain. = 一开始下雨他们就停止。


虚拟语气:
If we were tired, we would pack it in. = 如果我们累了，我们就停止。
If they had known it would rain, they would have packed it in earlier. = 如果他们知道会下雨，他们就会早点停工。


被动语态:
The project was packed in due to budget cuts. =  由于预算削减，项目被叫停。
The meeting was packed in early because of the emergency. = 由于紧急情况，会议提前结束。


疑问句:
Should we pack it in now? = 我们现在应该停止吗？
When are they going to pack it in? = 他们打算什么时候停止？


否定句:
We won't pack it in until we finish the job. = 除非我们完成工作，否则我们不会停止。
They didn't pack it in despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他们也没有放弃。


PHRASE->

pack it in and call it a day = 收工了
pack it in before it's too late = 在太迟之前停止
