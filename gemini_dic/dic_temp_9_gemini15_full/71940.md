flicks:/flɪks/| n.|电影，电影院|Movies, especially those shown in a cinema.  The word often implies a less serious or more casual type of film.|例句：Let's go see some flicks tonight. = 今晚我们去看电影吧。
例句：That new action flick was amazing! = 那部新的动作片太棒了！|近义词：movies, films, pictures|反义词：None specifically.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flicks the switch. = 他拨动开关。
The dog flicks its tail. = 狗摇着尾巴。

一般过去时:
She flicked the ash from her cigarette. = 她弹掉香烟上的烟灰。
He flicked through the magazine. = 他快速翻阅杂志。

现在完成时:
I have flicked the light switch several times. = 我已经拨动了几次电灯开关。
She has flicked through the book already. = 她已经快速翻阅过这本书了。

过去完成时:
He had flicked the lighter before the match struck. = 他在火柴点燃之前已经弹了打火机。
She had flicked the pages of the book before she found what she was looking for. = 在找到她想要的东西之前，她已经快速翻阅了书的几页。

将来时:
I will flick the switch when I'm ready. = 我准备好后就会拨动开关。
They will flick through the photos later. = 他们稍后会快速翻阅照片。

虚拟语气:
If I flicked the switch, the light would go on. = 如果我拨动开关，灯就会亮。
I wish I had flicked the switch sooner. = 我希望我早点拨动开关。

被动语态:
The switch was flicked by him. = 开关被他拨动了。
The pages were flicked through quickly. = 页数被快速翻阅了。

疑问句:
Did he flick the switch? = 他拨动开关了吗？
Will you flick through the report? = 你会快速翻阅报告吗？

否定句:
He didn't flick the switch. = 他没有拨动开关。
She didn't flick the ash off her cigarette. = 她没有弹掉香烟上的烟灰。
