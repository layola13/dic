/ˌɛntriːˈtiːtər/| n. |恳求者，哀求者 |A person who entreats; a beseecher.  An entreater begs or pleads earnestly for something.|例句：The entreater knelt before the king, pleading for mercy.  恳求者跪在国王面前，恳求宽恕。

The desperate entreater finally convinced the judge to reconsider.  绝望的恳求者最终说服法官重新考虑。

|近义词：supplicant, petitioner, pleader, beggar |反义词：refuser, denier|名词
