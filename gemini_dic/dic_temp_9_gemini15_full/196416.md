serpentinian:/ˌsɜːrpənˈtɪniən/| adj. |蜿蜒的；蛇形的；像蛇的|Of or resembling a serpent; winding or twisting like a snake. The word evokes the image of something sinuous, curving, and often unpredictable in its path.  |例句：The road wound through the mountains in a serpentinian path. 这条路蜿蜒穿过群山，像蛇一样。
The river followed a serpentinian course through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。

近义词：sinuous, winding, meandering, tortuous
反义词：straight, direct, linear

词性:形容词
