munificence:/mjuˈnɪfɪsəns/| n. |慷慨大方； щедрость |The generous bestowal of gifts or benefits; great generosity.  It implies a lavish and bountiful giving, often exceeding what is expected or required. |例句：His munificence was legendary, as he often donated large sums of money to charity.  他的慷慨大方是出了名的，因为他经常向慈善机构捐赠巨款。
例句：The king's munificence extended to all his subjects, ensuring a prosperous kingdom. 国王的慷慨惠及所有子民，确保了王国的繁荣。|近义词：generosity, liberality, bounty, magnanimity |反义词：stinginess, miserliness, parsimony
