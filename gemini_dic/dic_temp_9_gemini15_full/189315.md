safeguarded:/ˈseɪfɡɑːrdɪd/| v. | 保护；保障；维护 | Protected or kept safe from harm or loss.  The measure was designed to safeguard the interests of consumers.  |The government has implemented new laws to safeguard the environment.  政府已经实施了新的法律来保护环境。 | protected, shielded, preserved | endangered, threatened, jeopardized | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We safeguard our data with strong passwords. = 我们用强密码保护我们的数据。
The company safeguards its intellectual property. = 公司保护其知识产权。

一般过去时:
They safeguarded the children from harm. = 他们保护孩子们免受伤害。
The police safeguarded the crime scene. = 警察保护了犯罪现场。

现在完成时:
We have safeguarded our assets against inflation. = 我们已经保护了我们的资产免受通货膨胀的影响。
The organization has safeguarded its reputation through ethical practices. = 该组织通过合乎道德的实践维护了自己的声誉。

过去完成时:
They had safeguarded the documents before the fire broke out. = 大火发生之前，他们已经保护了文件。
The museum had safeguarded the artifacts for centuries. = 该博物馆已经保护这些文物几个世纪了。

将来时:
We will safeguard our privacy with advanced encryption. = 我们将使用高级加密来保护我们的隐私。
The company will safeguard its customers' data from cyberattacks. = 公司将保护客户数据免受网络攻击。

虚拟语气:
If we had safeguarded our investments, we wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果我们保护了我们的投资，我们就不会损失那么多钱。
It's important that we safeguard the rights of the vulnerable. = 保护弱势群体的权利很重要。

被动语态:
The children were safeguarded by their parents. = 孩子们受到父母的保护。
The company's data was safeguarded by a robust security system. = 公司的数据受到强大的安全系统的保护。

疑问句:
How can we safeguard our personal information online? = 我们如何保护我们在网上的个人信息？
What measures are in place to safeguard the environment? = 为了保护环境，有哪些措施到位？

否定句:
The company did not safeguard its employees' data properly. = 公司没有妥善保护员工的数据。
They failed to safeguard the building from intruders. = 他们未能保护建筑物免受入侵者侵害。

词源:

The word "safeguarded" is derived from the noun "safeguard" + the past participle suffix "-ed".  "Safeguard" itself comes from the Old English "sæf" (safe) + "weard" (guard).  Therefore, its roots indicate a combination of safety and protection.
