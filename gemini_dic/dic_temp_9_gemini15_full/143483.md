outgazed:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzd/| vt.|凝视，注视超过；比…看得更远|To look at someone or something for a long time, often with a particular feeling or purpose, exceeding in duration or intensity of gaze.|例句：He outgazed the others, his eyes fixed on the distant horizon. 他凝视着其他人，眼睛盯着远方的地平线。

例句：She was outgazed by the intense stare of the stranger. 她被陌生人的锐利目光看得有点发毛。


近义词：stare at, gaze at, contemplate, scrutinize|反义词：ignore, overlook, disregard


词性:动词
