nonlaminated:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. |未层压的，非层压的 | Not composed of or treated with layers.  This term typically refers to materials that haven't undergone a lamination process, where multiple layers are bonded together.  | Example: The nonlaminated glass shattered easily.  未经层压的玻璃很容易碎裂。|近义词：unlayered, single-layered | 反义词：laminated
