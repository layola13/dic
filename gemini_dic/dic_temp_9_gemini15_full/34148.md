clairvoyance:/ˈklɛərvɔɪəns/| n. |透视力；千里眼；预知能力 |The ability to see things that are not present to the physical eye, or to know things about the future or the past that cannot be known through normal means.  It often involves extrasensory perception (ESP).|例句：Her clairvoyance allowed her to predict the earthquake.她的透视能力让她预测到了地震。

例句：Many people believe in the power of clairvoyance to solve mysteries.许多人相信透视力可以解开谜团。|近义词：precognition, foresight, intuition |反义词：blindness, ignorance
