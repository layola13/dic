doused:/daʊzd/| vt.|浇湿；使…熄灭；使…充满；浸没|to pour a liquid over someone or something; to extinguish (a fire) by pouring water over it; to fill or cover completely|例句：The firefighters doused the flames with water. 消防队员用水扑灭了火焰。

例句：He doused himself with cold water to cool down. 他用冷水冲洗自己以降温。


近义词：soak, saturate, quench, drench|反义词：dry, dehydrate|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He douses the plants every morning. 他每天早上都给植物浇水。
She douses her hair with conditioner. 她用护发素浇湿她的头发。

一般过去时:
The rain doused the parade.  雨水淋湿了游行队伍。
The fireman doused the burning building. 消防员扑灭了燃烧的建筑物。

现在完成时:
The farmer has doused the fields with fertilizer. 农民已经用肥料浇灌了田地。
They have doused the bonfire with water. 他们已经用水浇灭了篝火。

过去完成时:
She had doused the candles before going to bed. 她睡觉前已经吹灭了蜡烛。(这里用doused更强调浇灭而非单纯吹灭)
He had doused the engine with oil before starting it. 他启动发动机前已经往发动机上浇了油。


将来时:
They will douse the hot coals with water. 他们会用水浇灭炽热的煤块。
The gardener will douse the flowers with water. 园丁将用清水浇灌鲜花。

虚拟语气:
If I had a fire extinguisher, I would douse the flames quickly. 如果我有一个灭火器，我会很快地扑灭火焰。
If it were raining, the fire would be doused naturally. 如果下雨的话，火就会自然熄灭。


被动语态:
The fire was doused by the firefighters. 火被消防员扑灭了。
The crops were doused with pesticides. 庄稼被杀虫剂喷洒了。


疑问句:
Did they douse the fire successfully? 他们成功地扑灭了火吗？
Will the team douse the car in paint?  队伍会把车涂满油漆吗？


否定句:
He didn't douse the fire quickly enough. 他没有足够快地扑灭火。
They didn't douse the plants with enough water. 他们没有给植物浇足够的水。


PHRASE->
douse in: 浸泡在…中
douse with: 用…浇
douse the flames: 扑灭火焰
douse oneself: 冲洗自己
douse the lights: 关灯 (虽然字面意思是浇灭灯光，但常用于戏剧性地描述关灯)
