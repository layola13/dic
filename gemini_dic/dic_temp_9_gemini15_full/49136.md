deitate:/ˈdiːeɪteɪt/| vt.|神化；尊为神|To regard or represent as a god or goddess; to elevate to divine status.|例句：The emperor deified himself after his victory. 皇帝在胜利后将自己神化了。例句：Ancient cultures often deified natural forces. 古代文化经常将自然力量神化。|近义词：deify, divinize, apotheosize|反义词：desecrate, profane
