polluters:/pəˈluːtərz/ | n. | 污染者；污染物排放者 | People or entities that contaminate the environment.  Those who pollute the environment through their actions or inaction. | Example:  The factory polluters were fined heavily for exceeding emission limits.  工厂污染者因超过排放限额而被处以巨额罚款。  The report identified major polluters in the region. 报告确定了该地区的主要污染者。 |近义词：contaminators, offenders, polluters, violators | 反义词：environmentalists, conservationists | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The factory pollutes the river. = 工厂污染了这条河。
These cars pollute the air. = 这些汽车污染空气。

一般过去时:
The company polluted the lake with chemicals. = 这家公司用化学物质污染了湖泊。
They polluted the environment with plastic waste. = 他们用塑料垃圾污染了环境。

现在完成时:
We have polluted the ocean for too long. = 我们污染海洋的时间太长了。
Industries have polluted the atmosphere with greenhouse gases. = 工业用温室气体污染了大气。

过去完成时:
They had polluted the river before the government intervened. = 政府干预之前，他们就已经污染了这条河。
The factory had polluted the soil for years. = 这家工厂多年来一直在污染土壤。

将来时:
They will pollute the air if they don't change their practices. = 如果他们不改变做法，他们将会污染空气。
The new plant will pollute the water if not properly managed. = 如果管理不善，新工厂将会污染水源。

虚拟语气:
If they were responsible polluters, they would clean up the mess. = 如果他们是负责任的污染者，他们会清理烂摊子。
I wish the polluters would take responsibility for their actions. = 我希望污染者能为他们的行为负责。

被动语态:
The river was polluted by industrial waste. = 这条河被工业废物污染了。
The air is being polluted by car emissions. = 空气正在被汽车尾气污染。

疑问句:
Who are the main polluters in this area? = 这个地区的主要污染者是谁？
What are the biggest polluters contributing to climate change? = 对气候变化贡献最大的污染者是什么？

否定句:
They are not responsible polluters. = 他们不是负责任的污染者。
The company did not intentionally pollute the water. = 这家公司并非故意污染水源。
