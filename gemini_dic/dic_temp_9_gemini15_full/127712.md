neglecting:/nɪˈɡlektɪŋ/ | v. | 忽视；忽略；疏忽 | To fail to care for properly; to disregard.  |例句：He was neglecting his studies. 他在忽略他的学习。|例句：She was neglecting her health. 她疏忽了自己的健康。 |近义词：ignoring, disregarding, overlooking, slighting |反义词：heeding, attending to, cherishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is neglecting his responsibilities. = 他正在忽略他的责任。
They are neglecting their garden. = 他们正在忽视他们的花园。

一般过去时:
She neglected to mention the important detail. = 她忘记提到那个重要的细节。
He neglected his duties as a father. = 他不尽父亲的责任。

现在完成时:
We have neglected our friends lately. = 我们最近疏忽了朋友。
They have neglected their health for too long. = 他们疏忽健康太久了。

过去完成时:
He had neglected to pay his bills, resulting in late fees. = 他忘记支付账单，导致滞纳金。
She had neglected her studies, so she failed the exam. = 她忽略了学习，所以考试不及格。

将来时:
They will neglect their work if they are not careful. = 他们如果不小心，就会忽略工作。
He will neglect his family if he keeps working so hard. = 如果他继续这么努力工作，他会忽略他的家庭。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't neglected my health, I wouldn't be so sick now. = 如果我没有忽视我的健康，我现在就不会这么病了。
I wish I hadn't neglected to call my mother. = 我真希望我没有忘记给我母亲打电话。

被动语态:
His needs were neglected by his family. = 他的需求被他的家人忽视了。
The problem was neglected for too long. = 这个问题被忽视了太久。

疑问句:
Are you neglecting your studies? = 你在忽略你的学习吗？
Is he neglecting his responsibilities again? = 他又在忽略他的责任了吗？

否定句:
I didn't neglect to lock the door. = 我没有忘记锁门。
She doesn't neglect her children. = 她没有忽视她的孩子。
