empty-headed: /ˈɛmptiˈhɛdəd/ | adj. | 脑袋空空;愚笨的;缺乏思想的 |  Lacking intelligence or good judgment; silly; foolish.  | Example: He made an empty-headed comment that offended everyone. 他说了一番愚蠢的话，冒犯了所有人。  |近义词:  silly, foolish, stupid, dense, unintelligent | 反义词: intelligent, clever, bright, sharp, insightful

She seems empty-headed, but she's actually quite clever. = 她看起来很傻，但她实际上很聪明。
