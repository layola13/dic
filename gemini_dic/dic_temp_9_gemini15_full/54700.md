disorganizer: /ˌdɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzər/ | n. | 瓦解者；扰乱者；混乱制造者 | A person or thing that causes disorganization or disorder.  | The sudden power outage proved to be a major disorganizer for the conference. 突如其来的停电对会议造成了很大的干扰。|近义词：troublemaker, disruptor, wrecker | 反义词：organizer, systematizer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The messy room is a disorganizer. =凌乱的房间是一个混乱制造者。
That loud music is a disorganizer for studying. = 那吵闹的音乐扰乱了学习。


一般过去时:
The unexpected visitor was a disorganizer for our plans. = 意外的访客打乱了我们的计划。
The storm acted as a disorganizer for the city's transportation system. = 暴风雨扰乱了城市的交通系统。


现在完成时:
His constant interruptions have been a disorganizer for the project. = 他不断的打断已经扰乱了这个项目。
The changes in management have been a disorganizer for the company's morale. = 管理层的变动已经影响了公司的士气。


过去完成时:
The prior power cuts had been a disorganizer for the entire week's production. = 此前的停电已经扰乱了整个星期的生产。
The earlier disagreements had been a disorganizer for the team's cohesion. = 早期分歧已经破坏了团队的凝聚力。


将来时:
The upcoming election will be a disorganizer for the market. = 即将来临的选举将会扰乱市场。
The new regulations will be a disorganizer for small businesses. = 新的规章制度将会扰乱小型企业的经营。


虚拟语气:
If he were not such a disorganizer, the project would have been completed on time. = 如果他不是那么爱捣乱，项目就能按时完成了。
I wish that unexpected rain wouldn't be such a disorganizer for the picnic. = 但愿这场突如其来的雨不会扰乱野餐。


被动语态:
The schedule was disorganized by the unexpected delay. = 计划因为意外的延误而被打乱。
The team's work was disorganized by the lack of clear instructions. = 由于缺乏明确的指示，团队的工作被打乱了。


疑问句:
Is the new software a disorganizer for workflow? = 新软件会扰乱工作流程吗？
Was the protest a disorganizer for the city's traffic? = 抗议活动扰乱了城市的交通吗？


否定句:
He is not a disorganizer; he's actually quite efficient. = 他不是一个扰乱者；实际上他非常有效率。
The meeting wasn't a disorganizer; it was productive. = 会议并没有造成混乱，它很有效率。
