logographer:/ləˈɡɒɡrəfər/ | n. |对照书写者；铭文刻写者；编年史作者 | A logographer is a writer of inscriptions, especially those on public monuments, or a writer of historical narratives.  They were particularly important in ancient Greece. | Example:  The logographer meticulously inscribed the names of the fallen soldiers on the monument.  对照书写者一丝不苟地在纪念碑上刻上了阵亡士兵的名字。|近义词：chronicler, historian,  inscriber | 反义词：N/A
