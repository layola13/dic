chiropodist:/kaɪˈrɒpədɪst/| n. |足病医生，足科医生 |A chiropodist is a healthcare professional who diagnoses and treats conditions affecting the feet and ankles.  They often specialize in the treatment of corns, calluses, and ingrown toenails. |例句：My chiropodist recommended special insoles for my plantar fasciitis. 我的足病医生建议我使用特殊的鞋垫来治疗足底筋膜炎。

例句：She made an appointment with a chiropodist to address her persistent heel pain. 她预约了一位足病医生来治疗她持续不断的脚跟痛。


近义词：podiatrist (This is often used interchangeably with chiropodist, although podiatrists may have broader scope of practice.)|反义词：N/A


词源:  The word "chiropodist" comes from the Greek words "cheir" (hand) and "pous" (foot),  combined with the suffix "-ist" (one who).  While the original Greek etymology refers to hand and foot, the term's modern usage focuses primarily on foot care.
