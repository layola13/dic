optimize:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/| vt.|优化，使最优|To make something as effective or functional as possible.|例句：We need to optimize our website for search engines. 我们需要优化我们的网站以适应搜索引擎。|例句：The company is trying to optimize its production process. 这家公司正在努力优化其生产流程。|近义词：improve, enhance, refine|反义词：worsen, impair, deteriorate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They optimize their code for speed. = 他们优化他们的代码以提高速度。
We optimize the system for maximum efficiency. = 我们优化系统以达到最大效率。

一般过去时:
She optimized her diet for weight loss. = 她优化了她的饮食以减肥。
He optimized the algorithm for better performance. = 他优化了算法以获得更好的性能。

现在完成时:
They have optimized their marketing strategy. = 他们已经优化了他们的营销策略。
We have optimized the software for mobile devices. = 我们已经优化了适用于移动设备的软件。

过去完成时:
They had optimized the design before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经优化了设计。
She had optimized her presentation before delivering it. = 她在演讲之前已经优化了她的演示文稿。

将来时:
We will optimize the database for faster queries. = 我们将优化数据库以加快查询速度。
They will optimize the supply chain for cost reduction. = 他们将优化供应链以降低成本。

虚拟语气:
If we optimized the process, we would save time. = 如果我们优化了流程，我们将节省时间。
I wish we had optimized the resources more effectively. = 我希望我们更有效地优化了资源。

被动语态:
The system is being optimized for better security. = 该系统正在被优化以提高安全性。
The software was optimized for compatibility. = 该软件经过优化以确保兼容性。

疑问句:
How do they optimize their workflow? = 他们如何优化他们的工作流程？
Have you optimized your website for mobile users? = 你是否已经为移动用户优化了你的网站？

否定句:
They did not optimize the algorithm properly. = 他们没有正确地优化算法。
We have not yet optimized the manufacturing process. = 我们尚未优化制造流程。


PHRASE->
optimize for = 为...优化
optimize performance = 优化性能
optimize resources = 优化资源
optimize efficiency = 优化效率
optimize the process = 优化流程

词源:
optimize源于拉丁语optimus（最佳的）+ize（动词后缀），表示“使达到最佳状态”。
