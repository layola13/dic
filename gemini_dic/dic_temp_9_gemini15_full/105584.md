kindlers:/ˈkɪndlərz/ | n. | 煽动者；点燃者 |Someone who kindles or ignites something, especially conflict or passion.  A kindler is someone who starts a fire or stirs up trouble.  | 例句：The kindlers of the revolution were young, idealistic students.  革命的煽动者是一些年轻的理想主义学生。

The speech acted as a kindler for the already simmering discontent. 这篇演讲加剧了民众本已存在的强烈不满。 | 近义词：incendiary, instigator, agitator | 反义词：peacemaker, conciliator |  名词
