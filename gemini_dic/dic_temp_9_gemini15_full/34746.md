clienteles: /ˌkliːənˈtɛlz/ | n. |顾客群体；客户群 | A group of clients or customers, especially of a particular business or professional.  | Example:  The boutique caters to a wealthy clientele. 这家精品店迎合富有的顾客群体。 The lawyer's clientele consists mainly of corporations. 这名律师的客户主要是公司。 |近义词：customers, clients, patrons |反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant attracts a diverse clientele. = 这家餐馆吸引了各种各样的顾客。
This salon serves a loyal clientele. = 这家沙龙拥有一批忠实的顾客。


一般过去时:
The shop lost a significant portion of its clientele after the price increase. = 价格上涨后，这家商店失去了很大一部分顾客。
The artist's clientele dwindled during the economic downturn. = 在经济低迷时期，这位艺术家的顾客减少了。


现在完成时:
The company has cultivated a strong clientele over the years. = 多年来，该公司已经培养了一批忠实的客户。
The hotel has maintained a high-end clientele since its opening. = 自开业以来，这家酒店一直保持着高端的客户群体。


过去完成时:
The business had already lost much of its clientele before the new management took over. = 新管理层接手之前，这家企业就已经失去了大部分客户。
The designer had built a substantial clientele before launching their own brand. = 在推出自己的品牌之前，这位设计师已经建立了相当大的客户群。


将来时:
The marketing campaign aims to attract a new clientele. = 营销活动旨在吸引新的客户群体。
The expansion will allow the company to serve a wider clientele. = 扩张将使公司能够服务更广泛的客户群体。


虚拟语气:
If the company improved its service, it would attract a larger clientele. = 如果公司改进服务，它将吸引更多的客户。
I wish we had a more diverse clientele. = 我希望我们有更多元化的客户群体。


被动语态:
The spa is frequented by a sophisticated clientele. = 这家水疗中心受到一群精致的顾客的青睐。(被…光顾)
The business's reputation is built upon its loyal clientele. = 这家企业的声誉建立在其忠实的客户群之上。(被…建立)


疑问句:
What kind of clientele does your business target? = 你的企业目标客户群体是什么样的？
What is the demographic profile of your clientele? = 你的客户群体的统计资料是什么？


否定句:
The cafe doesn't attract a large clientele. = 这家咖啡馆没有吸引大量的顾客。
The store hasn't been able to retain its existing clientele. = 这家商店无法留住现有的顾客。
