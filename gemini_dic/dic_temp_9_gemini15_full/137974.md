It often refers to nonylenic acid, a fatty acid found in some natural oils. | Example: The analysis revealed the presence of nonylenic acid in the sample of olive oil.  该橄榄油样本分析显示存在壬烯酸。| 近义词: nonenoic (referring specifically to the acid) | 反义词:  None readily available, as it describes a specific chemical characteristic.
