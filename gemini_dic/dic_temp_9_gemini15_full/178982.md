redeemer:/rɪˈdiːmər/| n.|救赎者，救星，赎回者|A person who saves someone or something from a dangerous or difficult situation; one who ransoms or redeems.  It can also refer to someone who atones for past sins or mistakes.|例句：He saw himself as a redeemer of his people. 他把自己看作自己民族的救星。

例句：The Messiah is often depicted as the ultimate redeemer of humanity.  弥赛亚常常被描绘成人类最终的救赎者。


近义词：savior, rescuer, liberator, deliverer|反义词：destroyer, corruptor, oppressor|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hero acts as the redeemer of the city. 英雄充当城市的救世主。
He redeems his mistakes with hard work. 他通过努力工作弥补自己的错误。

一般过去时:
The knight redeemed the princess from the dragon's clutches.  骑士从巨龙的魔爪中救出了公主。
She redeemed her lost honor through selfless service. 她通过无私的服务赎回了失去的荣誉。

现在完成时:
He has redeemed himself in the eyes of his family. 他在家人眼中已经赎清了自己的罪过。
The company has redeemed its reputation after the scandal.  丑闻过后，公司恢复了名誉。

过去完成时:
She had already redeemed her promise before the deadline.  她在截止日期前已经兑现了诺言。
He had redeemed his debts before the trial. 他在审判前已经还清了债务。

将来时:
The prince will redeem his kingdom from tyranny. 王子将从暴政中拯救他的王国。
They will redeem their vows with their actions. 他们将用行动兑现他们的誓言。

虚拟语气:
If he were to redeem himself, he would need to confess. 如果他要赎罪，就需要坦白。
I wish I could redeem the lost time. 我希望我能弥补失去的时间。

被动语态:
The lost child was redeemed by a kind stranger.  迷路的孩子被一位好心人救了。
His reputation was redeemed by his later achievements. 他后来的成就挽回了他的声誉。

疑问句:
Can he redeem himself from this situation? 他能从这种困境中解脱出来吗？
Will the hero be able to redeem the kingdom? 英雄能够拯救王国吗？

否定句:
He could not redeem his soul from the darkness. 他无法从黑暗中拯救自己的灵魂。
She failed to redeem her pledge. 她未能履行承诺。
