一般现在时:
He depatriates his political rivals. 他使他的政治对手丧失国籍。
The government depatriates those found guilty of treason. 政府驱逐那些被判犯有叛国罪的人。

一般过去时:
They depatriated him for his subversive activities.  他们因他的颠覆活动而剥夺了他的国籍。
The country depatriated many dissidents during the revolution. 在革命期间，这个国家驱逐了许多持不同政见者。

现在完成时:
The court has depatriated several individuals on grounds of fraud. 法院以欺诈为由剥夺了几人的国籍。
The regime has depatriated many of its opponents. 该政权已驱逐了许多反对者。

过去完成时:
They had depatriated him before he could appeal the decision. 在他能够对该决定提出上诉之前，他们就已经剥夺了他的国籍。
The authorities had depatriated the journalist before he could publish his exposé. 当局在记者能够发表他的揭露报道之前就驱逐了他。

否定句:

PHRASE->
There are limited common phrases directly using "depatriate".  The verb itself is quite formal and infrequent in everyday speech.  However, related concepts can be expressed through phrases such as:

to strip someone of their citizenship = 剥夺某人的国籍
to revoke someone's citizenship = 撤销某人的国籍
to lose one's citizenship = 丧失国籍
to be exiled from one's country = 被流放出国
to be banished from one's homeland = 被逐出家园
