rasterising: /ˈræstəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 光栅化 | The process of converting vector graphics into raster graphics (a grid of pixels).  This involves calculating the color of each pixel in the grid to represent the vector image.  | Example: The software rasterises the vector image before displaying it on the screen.  软件在屏幕上显示矢量图像之前将其光栅化。 | 近义词: pixelating, rendering (in this context) | 反义词: vectorizing
