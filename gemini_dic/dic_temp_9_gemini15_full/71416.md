flair:/flɛər/| n.|天赋，才能；风格，神韵；炫耀，卖弄|A natural talent, aptitude, or skill; a distinctive style or manner; ostentatious display.|She has a flair for languages. 她有语言天赋。|He writes with flair. 他写作风格独特。|近义词：talent, aptitude, style, grace|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows great flair in his painting. = 他在绘画方面展现了极高的天赋。
The chef's dishes have a certain flair. =这位厨师的菜肴别具一格。


一般过去时:
Her presentation lacked flair. = 她的演讲缺乏特色。
The dancer's performance showed remarkable flair. = 舞者的表演展现了非凡的才华。


现在完成时:
She has developed a flair for public speaking. = 她已经培养出了在公开场合演讲的才能。
He has shown his flair for design in his latest project. = 他在最新的项目中展现了他的设计天赋。


过去完成时:

将来时:

With more practice, she will develop a flair for writing. = 通过更多的练习，她将培养出写作的天赋。


虚拟语气:
