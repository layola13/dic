doggy:/ˈdɒɡi/ /ˈdɑːɡi/| n. adj.|小狗；可爱的；（非正式）狗的|A doggy is a young dog, often used as a term of endearment.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something cute or related to dogs. |例句：The doggy wagged its tail happily.  这只小狗高兴地摇着尾巴。

例句：That doggy sweater is so cute!  那件小狗毛衣真可爱！|近义词：puppy, pup, canine (n.)  cute, adorable (adj.)|反义词：adult dog (n.)  ugly, unpleasant (adj.)
