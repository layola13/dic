lobelet:/ˈloʊbəlɪt/| n. |小叶；小瓣 |A small lobe; a diminutive lobe.  A lobelet is a small, rounded projection or division of a larger structure, often found in anatomical contexts like the brain or lungs.  |例句：The microscopic examination revealed several lobelets within the larger lung segment. 显微镜检查显示更大的肺段内有几个小叶。

The detailed structure of the liver's lobelets was crucial to the study. 肝脏小叶的详细结构对这项研究至关重要。|近义词：lobule, leaflet (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but words implying a large, undivided structure could be considered antonyms in a relative sense.
