escalation:/ˌɛskəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 逐步升级；恶化；加剧 | The gradual increase in the intensity or seriousness of something.  | Example: The escalation of the conflict led to war.  冲突的升级导致了战争。

The slow escalation of tensions between the two countries was worrying.  两国之间紧张局势的逐步升级令人担忧。| intensification, aggravation, worsening | de-escalation, improvement, reduction | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is escalating rapidly. = 局势正在迅速恶化。
Conflict escalation is a serious concern. = 冲突升级是一个严重的问题。

一般过去时:
Tensions escalated after the attack. = 袭击之后，紧张局势升级了。
The argument escalated into a fight. = 争论升级为打斗。

现在完成时:
The problem has escalated beyond our control. = 问题已经升级到我们无法控制的地步。
The company has escalated its marketing efforts. = 公司已经加大了市场营销力度。

过去完成时:
The situation had escalated before we could intervene. = 在我们能够干预之前，局势已经升级了。
The dispute had escalated to a point of no return. = 争端已经升级到无法挽回的地步。

将来时:
The situation will escalate if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，局势将会恶化。
We predict that prices will escalate next year. = 我们预测明年的价格将会上涨。

虚拟语气:
If we hadn't intervened, the conflict would have escalated further. = 如果我们没有干预，冲突将会进一步升级。
I wish the situation hadn't escalated so quickly. = 我希望局势没有恶化得这么快。

被动语态:
The issue was escalated to the management team. = 这个问题已上报给管理团队。
The crisis was escalated to a higher level of authority. = 危机已上报给更高层的权威部门。

疑问句:
Will the conflict escalate further? = 冲突还会进一步升级吗？
How can we prevent further escalation? = 我们如何才能防止进一步升级？

否定句:
The situation did not escalate as we feared. = 局势并没有像我们担心的那样恶化。
We tried to avoid escalation of the conflict. = 我们试图避免冲突升级。


PHRASE->
wage escalation = 工资上涨

price escalation = 价格上涨
tension escalation = 紧张局势升级
escalation of violence = 暴力升级
