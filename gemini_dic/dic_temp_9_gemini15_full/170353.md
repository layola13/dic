provine:/ˈproʊvaɪn/| n. |省份，州；产地|A province is a principal administrative division of certain countries or empires.|The province of Quebec is known for its French culture.  魁北克省以其法语文化而闻名。|The wine is from a renowned province in France. 这款葡萄酒产自法国一个著名的产区。|territory, region, state|country, nation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This province produces a lot of wheat. = 这个省份生产大量的麦子。
The government is investing in the province's infrastructure. = 政府正在投资该省的基础设施建设。

一般过去时:
That province suffered greatly during the war. = 那个省份在战争期间遭受了巨大的损失。

现在完成时:

将来时:
The province will host the national games next year. = 该省明年将举办全国运动会。

虚拟语气:
If this province were independent, it would have its own laws. = 如果这个省份独立，它将有自己的法律。
