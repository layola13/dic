genesises:/ˈdʒɛnɪsiːz/ | n. | 起源；创始；发生；创世纪 | The plural of "genesis", referring to multiple origins or beginnings.  It often implies a complex or multifaceted origin story. | Example: The genesises of the two companies were quite different, one stemming from a family business, the other from a tech startup.  = 这两家公司的起源大相径庭，一家源于家族企业，另一家则源于科技初创公司。  |近义词：origins, beginnings, commencements |反义词：ends, conclusions |名词
