disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/|/dɪsˈbɜːrs/ | vt. |支付；发放；散布|To pay out (money) from a fund.  To distribute or spread something over a wide area.|The company disbursed bonuses to its employees. 公司向员工发放奖金。|The government disbursed funds for disaster relief. 政府拨款用于救灾。|近义词：pay out, distribute, dispense |反义词：collect, receive, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disburses salaries monthly. = 公司每月支付工资。
He disburses his time wisely. = 他明智地分配时间。


一般过去时:
They disbursed the funds quickly. = 他们迅速地支付了资金。
She disbursed the leaflets to the crowd. = 她向人群分发了传单。


现在完成时:
The organization has disbursed millions of dollars in aid. = 该组织已经拨款数百万美元用于援助。
We have disbursed all the necessary documents. = 我们已经分发了所有必要的文档。


过去完成时:
The treasurer had already disbursed the funds before the audit. = 审计之前，财务主管已经支付了资金。
They had disbursed the goods to various locations before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已将货物分发到各个地点。


将来时:
The government will disburse the aid packages next week. = 政府将于下周发放援助物资。
The school will disburse the scholarships at the ceremony. = 学校将在仪式上发放奖学金。


虚拟语气:
If the bank disbursed the loan, we could start the project. = 如果银行发放贷款，我们就可以启动项目了。
I wish they had disbursed the money earlier. = 我希望他们早点把钱发放了。


被动语态:
The funds were disbursed according to the agreement. = 资金按照协议支付了。
The supplies were disbursed evenly among the teams. = 补给品在各队之间平均分配。


疑问句:
When will the dividends be disbursed? = 股息什么时候发放？
How was the money disbursed? = 钱是怎么支付的？


否定句:
The company did not disburse the bonuses this year. = 公司今年没有发放奖金。
They didn't disburse the funds properly. = 他们没有正确地支付资金。
