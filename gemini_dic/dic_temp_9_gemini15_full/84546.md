hammed:/hæmd/| v.|  假装，装腔作势，夸张地表现 | To exaggerate or affect a particular manner or style, often in a theatrical or insincere way. |例句：He hammed up his performance, hoping to impress the audience. 他夸张地表演，希望能给观众留下深刻印象。

He hammed it up for the cameras. 他在镜头前夸张地表演。|近义词：overact, exaggerate, affect, |反义词：underplay, downplay, be genuine.
