hoicked:/hɔɪkt/| vt.|猛地拉起，猛拉；突然提起|To suddenly lift or pull something upwards with a jerk.  It often implies a forceful or abrupt movement.|例句：He hoicked the bucket of water onto the ledge. 他猛地把水桶拉到窗台上。

例句：She hoicked her bag onto her shoulder. 她猛地把包甩到肩上。


近义词：jerked, yanked, hauled, hoisted|反义词：lowered, dropped


词源:  The word "hoick" is of uncertain origin, possibly onomatopoeic (imitating the sound of the action). It's a relatively informal word.
