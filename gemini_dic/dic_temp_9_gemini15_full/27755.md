cannabin:/ˈkænəbɪn/| n. |大麻素|A chemical compound found in the cannabis plant.  Cannabinoids have various effects on the body, some of which are psychoactive.|Example:  The study focused on the effects of different cannabinoids on the brain. 这项研究侧重于不同大麻素对大脑的影响。|近义词：cannabinoid (更常用)|反义词：无
