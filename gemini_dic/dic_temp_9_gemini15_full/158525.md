pleochromatism:/ˌpliːoʊˈkroʊmətɪzəm/| n.|多色性，多色现象|The property of appearing different colours when viewed in different directions, due to the absorption of light varying with the direction of the incident light.|例句：The pleochromatism of the gemstone is remarkable, shifting from emerald green to deep blue depending on the angle.  宝石的多色性非常显著，其颜色会根据角度的不同而从翠绿色变为深蓝色。
例句：The intense pleochromatism of the alexandrite makes it a highly sought-after gemstone.  亚历山大石强烈的多色性使它成为一种非常抢手的宝石。|近义词：dichroism, trichroism, polychroism|反义词：monochroism
