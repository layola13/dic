skaddle:/ˈskæd(ə)l/| v.| 逃走；溜走 | To run away quickly; to flee.  |例句：The thief skaddled when he saw the police.  小偷看到警察就逃跑了。|例句：The children skaddled off to play hide-and-seek. 孩子们溜走了去玩捉迷藏。|近义词：flee, run away, bolt, abscond |反义词：stay, remain, linger
