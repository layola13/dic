heyday:/ˈheɪdeɪ/| n. |全盛时期；黄金时代；兴盛时期 |The period of a person's or thing's greatest success, popularity, or power.  It refers to a time of great success, prosperity, or popularity. |例句：The heyday of the Roman Empire was a time of great prosperity and expansion. 罗马帝国的全盛时期是繁荣和扩张的时代。

例句：Her acting career reached its heyday in the 1990s. 她的演艺事业在20世纪90年代达到顶峰。|近义词：prime, zenith, peak, blossom |反义词：decline, downfall, wane
