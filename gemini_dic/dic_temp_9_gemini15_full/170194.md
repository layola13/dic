protosulphide: /ˌproʊtoˈsʌlfʌɪd/ | n. |原硫化物| A binary compound of a metal with sulfur, in which the metal is in its lowest oxidation state.  It is often a less stable sulfide than other possible sulfides of the same metal. | Example: Iron protosulfide (FeS) is a common mineral.  铁原硫化物（FeS）是一种常见的矿物。  | 近义词: monosulfide (in some contexts)| 反义词:  polysulfide
