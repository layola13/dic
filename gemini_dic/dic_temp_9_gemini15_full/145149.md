overasserts:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrts/ | v. | 过分自信地表达；过于自信地主张 | To state or claim something too forcefully or confidently; to be overly assertive. |例句：He overasserts his opinions in meetings, often alienating his colleagues. 他在会议上过于自信地表达自己的意见，经常使同事疏远他。|例句：She overasserted her authority, leading to a conflict with her subordinates. 她过于自信地运用自己的权力，导致与下属发生冲突。|近义词：assert aggressively, domineer, bully |反义词：be submissive, be timid, be hesitant
