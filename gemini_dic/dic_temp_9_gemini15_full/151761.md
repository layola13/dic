pashminas:/pæʃˈmiːnəz/| n.|披肩，羊绒披肩|A pashmina is a fine shawl, traditionally made from the soft undercoat of the Changthangi goat.  It is known for its softness, warmth, and luxurious feel.|例句：She wrapped herself in a luxurious pashmina shawl. 她披上了一条奢华的羊绒披肩。

例句：The pashmina felt incredibly soft against her skin. 这条羊绒披肩摸起来非常柔软。

近义词：shawl, scarf, wrap

反义词：无
