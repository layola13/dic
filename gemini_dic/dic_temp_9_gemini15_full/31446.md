chark:/tʃɑːrk/| vt. |烧焦；烤焦；使…焦黑|To burn the surface of something, especially food, until it is black.  |例句：The steak was slightly charred on the outside. 牛排外面稍微烤焦了。
The fire charred the wooden fence. 火把木栅栏烧焦了。|近义词：burn, scorch, singe|反义词：unburn
