pretransaction: /ˌpriːtrænˈzækʃən/ | n. | 事务前，交易前 | Relating to the period or activities before a transaction takes place.  It often involves preparation, planning, and verification steps necessary to ensure the transaction's smooth execution.  | 例句：Pretransaction due diligence is crucial to mitigate risks. 事务前尽职调查对于降低风险至关重要。

例句：The pretransaction analysis revealed potential issues with the acquisition target. 事务前分析揭示了收购目标的潜在问题。


近义词：prior to transaction, before transaction, pre-deal, antecedent to transaction


反义词：posttransaction, after transaction, post-deal


词性: noun
