hydranet:/ˈhaɪdreɪnæt/ | n. |消防栓；消防龙头 | A hydrant is a fireplug; a nozzle attached to an underground water main that is used by firefighters to obtain water to extinguish fires.  | Example: The firefighters quickly connected their hoses to the hydrant. 消防员迅速将水管连接到消防栓上。 |近义词：fireplug, fire hydrant |反义词：N/A
