polysemantic: /ˌpɒliˈsɛmɪk/ /ˌpɑːliˈsɛmɪk/ | adj. | 多义的 | Having multiple meanings;  capable of being understood in more than one way.  A polysemantic word has several related meanings that have evolved from a common ancestor.  This contrasts with homonymy, where words share the same spelling and pronunciation but have unrelated meanings. |


例句：

The word "bank" is polysemantic; it can refer to a financial institution or the land alongside a river.  “bank” 这个词是多义词；它可以指金融机构或河边的土地。


The term "bright" is polysemantic, encompassing meanings related to intelligence, light, and color. “bright” 这个词是多义的，包含与智力、光线和颜色相关的含义。


近义词：ambiguous, equivocal, multivalent


反义词：unambiguous, univocal, unequivocal


词性:形容词
