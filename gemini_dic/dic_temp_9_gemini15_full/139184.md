objectifications:/ˌɒbdʒektɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 客体化；物化；将…视为客体 | The act of treating someone as an object rather than a person.  This often involves reducing a person to their physical attributes or sexual value, ignoring their personality, feelings, and individuality.  It can be a form of dehumanization and is often associated with sexism, racism, and other forms of prejudice. | Example: The objectification of women in advertising is a persistent problem.  广告中对女性的物化是一个持续存在的问题。 The objectifications of the refugees were evident in the media's coverage.  媒体报道中对难民的物化是显而易见的。 | 近义词：dehumanization, commodification, instrumentalization | 反义词：humanization, personalization, respect | 名词
