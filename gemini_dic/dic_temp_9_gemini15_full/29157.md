caseworker:/ˈkeɪsˌwɜːrkər/ | n. |个案工作者，社会工作者 | A caseworker is a professional who works with individuals or families facing various challenges, providing support, guidance, and resources to help them overcome difficulties.  They often work in social services, government agencies, or non-profit organizations. | 例句：The caseworker helped the family navigate the complex process of applying for welfare benefits.  个案工作者帮助这个家庭理清申请福利金的复杂流程。 |  近义词：social worker, welfare worker, counselor | 反义词：N/A
