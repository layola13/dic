excavators:/ˈɛkskəveɪtərz/| n.|挖掘机；挖掘者|A machine used for digging and moving earth or other materials.  Also refers to people who excavate. |例句：The construction workers used excavators to dig the foundation.  建筑工人用挖掘机挖掘地基。

例句：Archaeologists are excavators who carefully unearth ancient artifacts. 考古学家是仔细挖掘古代文物的挖掘者。

近义词：diggers, cranes (in some contexts)
反义词：builders (in a contrasting sense)
词性:名词
