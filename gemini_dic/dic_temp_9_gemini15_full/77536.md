/dʒiːsiː/|abbr.|高级普通教育证书|General Certificate of Education,  a set of academic qualifications offered in the United Kingdom, and other countries following the British education system.  These qualifications are typically taken by students aged 16-18 and are equivalent to high school diplomas in other countries.  They are often considered prerequisites for university admission.|例句：He achieved high grades in his GCE exams. 他在普通教育高级证书考试中取得了高分。
She's applying to university after completing her GCEs. 她完成普通教育高级证书考试后正在申请大学。|近义词：A-levels (in the UK)|反义词：None|abbr.
