multitoned:/ˈmʌltiˌtoʊnd/ | adj. | 多音调的，多音色的 | Having or characterized by multiple tones or pitches;  especially in music or speech.  | 例句：The multitoned melody was captivating.  多音调的旋律引人入胜。


The singer's multitoned voice filled the hall.  歌手多音色的嗓音充满了大厅。|近义词：polyphonic, varied, nuanced | 反义词：monotonous, single-toned


词源:  The word "multitoned" is a combination of "multi-" (meaning "many" or "multiple") and "toned" (referring to tone or pitch).  The "tone" element has roots in Old French "ton" and ultimately Latin "tonus,"  meaning "tone, sound, accent."
