pinto:/ˈpɪntoʊ/| adj. n.|斑点的；花斑的；（尤指马）有斑点的；平托豆；|a horse or other animal with spots of color on a white or light-colored coat, especially a horse with a dark brown or black coat and white markings. Also, a type of bean.|例句：He rode a pinto horse across the plains. 他骑着一匹花斑马穿过平原。

The pinto beans are a staple in Mexican cuisine. 平托豆是墨西哥菜肴中的主食。|近义词： dappled, speckled, piebald |反义词：unspotted, solid-colored
