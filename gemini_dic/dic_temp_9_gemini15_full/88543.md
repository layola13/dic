hit-the-roof:/hɪt ðə ruːf/| phrasal verb| 勃然大怒；大发雷霆 | To become extremely angry.  This idiom describes a sudden and intense outburst of anger, often to the point of being uncontrollable. The image is of someone being so angry they would literally hit the roof (the ceiling).|例句：He hit the roof when he saw the damage to his car. 他看到他的车受损后勃然大怒。  She nearly hit the roof when she found out about the missed deadline. 当她发现错过最后期限时，她几乎要气疯了。|近义词：explode, erupt, go ballistic, lose it, fly off the handle|反义词：remain calm, stay cool, keep one's temper
