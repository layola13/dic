reflected:/rɪˈflɛktɪd/ | adj. v. |反射的；反映的；沉思的 | Showing an image or likeness of something; throwing back light, heat, sound, etc.  To think deeply or carefully about something. | 例句：The lake reflected the mountains.  湖面映照着群山。

The incident reflected badly on the company's reputation.  这起事件严重损害了公司的声誉。


近义词： mirrored, showed, indicated, pondered, considered | 反义词： ignored, disregarded, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mirror reflects my face. = 镜子反射着我的脸。
His actions reflect his true character. = 他的行为反映了他的真实性格。


一般过去时:
The still water reflected the clear blue sky. = 静止的水面反射着湛蓝的天空。
She reflected on her past mistakes. = 她反思了过去的错误。


现在完成时:
The changes have reflected positively on the company's financial situation. = 这些变化对公司的财务状况产生了积极的影响。
I have reflected on your proposal and have some concerns. = 我仔细考虑了你的提议，有一些顾虑。


过去完成时:
The old painting had reflected the artist's mastery of light and shadow. = 那幅旧画曾反映出艺术家对光影的掌握。
She had reflected for hours before making her decision. = 在做出决定之前，她思考了几个小时。


将来时:
The new policy will reflect the government's commitment to environmental protection. = 新政策将反映出政府对环境保护的承诺。
I will reflect on this matter further before giving you a definitive answer. = 我会在给你一个明确的答案之前进一步考虑此事。


虚拟语气:
If the lake were calm, it would reflect the stars more clearly. = 如果湖面平静，它会更清晰地反射星光。
I wish I had reflected more carefully before speaking. = 我希望我在说话之前考虑得更周全一些。


被动语态:
The image was reflected in the polished surface. = 图像被反射在抛光的表面上。
His concerns were reflected in his hesitant response. = 他的担忧反映在他的犹豫不决的回应中。


疑问句:
Does the mirror reflect accurately? = 镜子反射准确吗？
What does this statistic reflect about the population's health? = 这个统计数据反映了人口健康的什么情况？


否定句:
The glass didn't reflect the light properly. = 玻璃没有恰当地反射光线。
His words did not reflect his true feelings. = 他的话语并没有反映出他的真实感受。


PHRASE->
reflect on/upon:  仔细思考
reflect badly on:  对…有负面影响
reflect credit on:  为…增光
reflect a feeling: 反映一种感觉
reflect the light: 反射光线
