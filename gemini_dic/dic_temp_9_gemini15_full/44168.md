criticizer:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzər/| n. |批评者，评论家 |A person who criticizes. Someone who expresses disapproval of someone or something.  |Example: He became a harsh criticizer of the government's policies. 他成了政府政策的严厉批评者。|近义词：critic, censurer, fault-finder |反义词：supporter, admirer, praiser

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The criticizer points out flaws in the design. = 批评者指出了设计中的缺陷。
That criticizer always finds fault with everything. = 那个批评者总是对一切吹毛求疵。


一般过去时:
The criticizer highlighted several inconsistencies in the report. = 批评者指出了报告中的一些前后矛盾之处。
The criticizer sharply attacked the new policy. = 批评者尖锐地抨击了这项新政策。


现在完成时:
The criticizer has consistently voiced concerns about the project. = 批评者一直以来都对该项目表示担忧。
The criticizer has offered several constructive criticisms. = 批评者提出了一些建设性的批评意见。


过去完成时:
The criticizer had already expressed his disapproval before the meeting. = 批评者在会议之前就已经表达了他的不满。
Before the presentation, the criticizer had prepared a detailed list of questions. = 在演示文稿之前，批评者已经准备了一份详细的问题清单。


将来时:
The criticizer will likely address the issue in his next article. = 批评者可能会在他的下一篇文章中讨论这个问题。
The criticizer will continue to monitor the situation. = 批评者将继续关注局势。


虚拟语气:
If the criticizer were more understanding, he would offer more helpful suggestions. = 如果批评者更能理解，他就会提供更有帮助的建议。
I wish the criticizer had been more constructive in his feedback. = 我希望批评者在反馈中更具建设性。


被动语态:
The government's policies were criticized by many. = 政府的政策受到了许多人的批评。（criticize是被动语态）
His work was relentlessly criticized by the criticizer. = 他的作品受到了批评者的无情批评。（criticize是被动语态）


疑问句:
Who is the main criticizer of this proposal? = 这个提案的主要批评者是谁？
What are the criticizer's main objections? = 批评者的主要异议是什么？


否定句:
He is not a criticizer of the arts. = 他不是一个艺术批评家。
She wasn't a harsh criticizer, but she did point out some problems. = 她不是一个严厉的批评者，但她确实指出了一些问题。
