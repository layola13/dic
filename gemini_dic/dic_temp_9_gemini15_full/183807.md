researched:/rɪˈsɜːtʃt/ | adj. | 被研究的；经过调查的 | Having been the subject of research or investigation.  |例句：The researched data shows a clear trend.  被研究的数据显示了一个清晰的趋势。 |例句：This is a well-researched article on the topic. 这是一篇关于该主题的研究充分的文章。|近义词：investigated, studied, examined |反义词：unexplored, unstudied


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist researches the new virus. = 科学家正在研究这种新病毒。
They research market trends regularly. = 他们定期研究市场趋势。


一般过去时:
The team researched the historical background. = 该团队研究了历史背景。
She researched the topic extensively before writing her paper. = 在写论文之前，她对这个话题进行了广泛的研究。


现在完成时:
We have researched the effects of climate change. = 我们已经研究了气候变化的影响。
They have researched this problem for years. = 他们已经研究这个问题很多年了。


过去完成时:
He had researched the subject thoroughly before giving the presentation. = 他在做演示之前已经彻底研究了这个主题。
The scientists had researched the disease for decades before finding a cure. = 科学家们在找到治疗方法之前已经研究这种疾病几十年了。


将来时:
We will research the new technology next year. = 我们明年将研究这项新技术。
They will research the impact of social media on teenagers. = 他们将研究社交媒体对青少年的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would research the topic further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步研究这个话题。
It is important that we thoroughly research the consequences before acting. = 在采取行动之前，彻底研究后果非常重要。


被动语态:
The effects of the drug were researched extensively. = 该药物的影响被广泛研究。
The case has been researched by the police for months. = 这个案子已经被警方调查了几个月。


疑问句:
Have you researched the matter carefully? = 你仔细研究过这件事了吗？
What aspects of the problem have been researched so far? = 到目前为止，问题的哪些方面已经被研究过了？


否定句:
They did not research the market adequately before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前没有充分研究市场。
He hadn't researched the historical context of the event. = 他没有研究事件的历史背景。
