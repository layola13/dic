quasi-eminent:/ˈkwɑːziˈɛmɪnənt/ | adj. | 准杰出的；名义上的杰出 |  Nearly but not quite eminent; having a semblance of eminence.  It suggests a degree of prominence or reputation, but lacking the full recognition or achievement typically associated with true eminence. | The quasi-eminent professor, while respected in his field, lacked the widespread recognition of a truly influential scholar. | 近义词：prominent, notable, distinguished | 反义词：obscure, unknown, insignificant
