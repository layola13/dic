powdermill: /ˈpaʊdərˌmɪl/ | n. | 火药厂；制粉厂 | A powdermill is a factory where gunpowder or other explosive powder is manufactured.  It can also refer to a mill that grinds materials into a powder. | The old powdermill was abandoned years ago and is now a dangerous ruin.  = 那座旧火药厂多年以前就被废弃了，现在成了一个危险的废墟。


The town's economy once relied heavily on its powdermill. = 这个镇的经济曾经严重依赖它的火药厂。


近义词：gunpowder factory, mill (in the sense of grinding to powder) | 反义词：无

词源:
The word "powdermill" is a compound word.  "Powder" comes from the Old French "poudre," ultimately from the Latin "pulvis" (dust). "Mill" comes from the Old English "mylen," from the Latin "molina" (mill).  Therefore, "powdermill" literally means a mill that processes powder.
