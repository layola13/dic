disproval: /dɪsˈpruːvəl/ | n. | 不赞成；反对；否决 | The act of disapproving; a feeling of disapproval; the expression of disapproval.  It indicates a negative judgment or rejection of something. | 例句：His proposal met with considerable disproval from the board.  他的提议遭到了董事会的强烈反对。

例句：The judge showed his disproval by slamming his gavel.  法官用重击法槌来表示他的反对。


近义词：disapprobation, objection, dissent, condemnation, censure

反义词：approval, acceptance, endorsement, sanction


词源:  dis- (否定前缀) + proval (源于prove的派生词，表示“证明”，“证实”)。  因此，disproval字面意思是“不证明”，引申为“不赞成”、“反对”。
