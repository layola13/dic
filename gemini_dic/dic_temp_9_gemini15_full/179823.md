reexhibit:/ˌriːɪɡˈzɪbɪt/| vt.|再展出，重新展出|To exhibit again; to show or display something again.|The museum will reexhibit the Van Gogh paintings next year.  博物馆明年将再次展出梵高的画作。|The art gallery decided to reexhibit the popular sculptures.  美术馆决定重新展出那些受欢迎的雕塑。| exhibit, display, showcase |  remove, take down


USAGE->
