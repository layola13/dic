prelitigation:/ˌpriːlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |诉讼前准备；诉讼前的阶段|The activities and processes that occur before a lawsuit is filed.  This can include investigations, negotiations, and attempts at alternative dispute resolution.  |例句：The prelitigation phase involved extensive document review and witness interviews. 诉讼前的阶段包括广泛的文件审查和证人访谈。

例句：They tried to settle the dispute through prelitigation negotiations. 他们试图通过诉讼前的谈判来解决纠纷。


近义词：pre-trial preparation, pretrial proceedings, dispute resolution


反义词：litigation, trial


词性：名词
