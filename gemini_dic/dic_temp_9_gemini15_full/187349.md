rocksucker:/ˈrɒksʌkə(r)/| n. |吸吮岩石的人/岩石吸盘(俚语)|A derogatory term for a person considered to be foolish, stupid, or inept;  It can also refer to a type of suction cup or device that adheres to rocks.  The term is highly offensive and should be avoided.|例句：He called him a rocksucker, a term that deeply offended him. 他称他为“吸吮岩石的人”，这个词深深地冒犯了他。|近义词：fool, idiot, moron, simpleton |反义词：genius, intellectual, savant |名词
