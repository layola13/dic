feasts:/fiːsts/| n.|盛宴；节日；宴会|A feast is a large meal, especially one held to celebrate a special occasion.  It can also refer to a period of celebration or enjoyment.|例句：They prepared a magnificent feast for their wedding.  他们为他们的婚礼准备了一场盛大的宴会。

例句：The harvest festival is a time of feasts and merrymaking. 收获节是盛宴和欢庆的时刻。


近义词：banquet, celebration, festival|反义词：famine, starvation


词性: 名词
