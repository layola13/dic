educable:/ˈɛdʒʊkəbəl/ | adj. | 可教育的，可教的 | Capable of being educated; teachable.  This word refers to someone or something that is able to learn and acquire knowledge or skills through education. | Example:  The child, though initially shy, proved to be highly educable.  这个孩子虽然一开始很害羞，但却证明非常容易教导。  The new software is educable, allowing users to customize its functions. 这款新软件是可学习的，允许用户自定义其功能。 | 近义词：teachable, trainable, instructable | 反义词：unteachable, ineducable
