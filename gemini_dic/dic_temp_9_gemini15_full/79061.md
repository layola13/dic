giveth:/ˈɡɪvɪθ/| v.|给予，赠予|Archaic third-person singular present indicative form of "give".  It is a rarely used form of the verb "to give" found primarily in older texts and religious contexts.|例句：And God giveth unto man dominion over the fish of the sea, and over the fowl of the air, and over every living thing that moveth upon the earth. = 上帝赐予人统治海里的鱼，空中的飞鸟，以及地上一切活动的生物。|近义词：bestow, grant, offer|反义词：take, withhold|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He giveth to the poor. = 他施舍穷人。
God giveth life and taketh it away. = 上帝给予生命，也夺去生命。


一般过去时:
He gave generously to charity. = 他慷慨地捐赠给慈善机构。
She gave me a book for my birthday. = 她送了我一本生日礼物。


现在完成时:
He has given much to the community. = 他为社区贡献良多。
She has given her all to her work. = 她全身心地投入工作。


过去完成时:
He had given everything he owned before he died. = 他死前已经捐出了他所有的财产。
She had given him many warnings. = 她已经给他很多警告了。


将来时:
He will give a speech tomorrow. = 他明天将发表演讲。
She will give you the answer later. = 她稍后会给你答案。


虚拟语气:
If he gave more, he would be happier. = 如果他付出更多，他会更快乐。
I wish he gave me a chance. = 我希望他能给我一个机会。


被动语态:
The prize was given to the winner. = 奖品颁给了获胜者。
The money was given to the charity. = 这笔钱捐给了慈善机构。


疑问句:
What did he give you? = 他给了你什么？
Will she give a presentation? = 她会做演示吗？


否定句:
He did not give me anything. = 他什么也没给我。
She will not give up easily. = 她不会轻易放弃。
