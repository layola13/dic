huffy:/ˈhʌfi/| adj.|暴躁的，生气勃勃的，怒气冲冲的|easily offended and angered; characterized by petulance or ill temper.  |例句：He got huffy when I criticized his work. 他被我批评他的工作后变得生气勃勃。|例句：She was feeling huffy after the argument. 争吵后她感到很生气。|近义词：irritable, grumpy, sulky, petulant |反义词：calm, placid, serene, good-humored|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is huffy with his brother. = 他对他哥哥很生气。
She is always huffy when she's tired. = 她累的时候总是很暴躁。


一般过去时:
He was huffy after the meeting. = 会议后他很生气。
She became huffy when he disagreed. = 他不同意时，她变得很生气。


现在完成时:
He has been huffy all day. = 他整天都闷闷不乐。
She has been huffy ever since the incident. = 自从那件事发生后，她就一直很生气。


过去完成时:
He had been huffy for days before he finally apologized. = 在他最终道歉之前，他已经生气了好几天了。
She had been huffy all morning, so nobody dared to talk to her. = 她整个上午都很生气，所以没有人敢和她说话。


将来时:
He will be huffy if you tell him the truth. = 如果你告诉他真相，他会生气的。
She will probably be huffy when she finds out. = 当她发现的时候，她可能会很生气。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so huffy, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么生气，他就会有更多的朋友。
I wish he weren't so huffy all the time. = 我希望他不要总是那么生气。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His behavior was described as huffy. = 他的行为被描述为生气勃勃的。
The situation was made huffy by his reaction. = 他的反应使情况变得紧张。


疑问句:
Is he still huffy about what happened? = 他还在为发生的事情生气吗？
Was she huffy because of the delay? = 她是因为延误而生气吗？


否定句:
He wasn't huffy at all; he was just tired. = 他一点也不生气，他只是累了。
She wasn't huffy; she was just concentrating. = 她没有生气；她只是在集中精力。


PHRASE->
get huffy = 生气
become huffy = 变得生气
feel huffy = 感觉生气
sound huffy = 听起来生气
a huffy tone = 生气的语气
