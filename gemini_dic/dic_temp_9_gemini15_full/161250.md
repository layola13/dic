postmaximal: /ˌpoʊstˈmæksɪməl/ | adj. | 最大值后的，超最大值的 |  Describing something that occurs or is measured after the maximal value or point has been reached.  It often refers to physiological or performance measures that continue after a peak effort. |

例句：

Postmaximal oxygen consumption was measured to assess recovery.  = 测量最大摄氧量后的耗氧量以评估恢复情况。

The athletes continued training in the postmaximal fatigue phase. = 运动员在最大疲劳阶段后继续训练。


近义词：postpeak, supramaximal (sometimes)

反义词：premaximal, submaximal


词性：形容词
