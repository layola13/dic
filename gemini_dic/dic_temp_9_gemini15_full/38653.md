concurring:/kənˈkɜːrɪŋ/| adj.|一致的；同时发生的|Agreeing; happening at the same time.  |例句：The jury returned a concurring verdict. 陪审团作出了相同判决。
例句：There were several concurring events that led to the crisis. 导致危机的有几件事同时发生。|近义词：agreeing, simultaneous, coincident|反义词：disagreeing, conflicting, disparate|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two reports concur on the main points. = 这两份报告在要点上是一致的。
Their opinions concur on this matter. = 他们在这件事上的意见一致。


一般过去时:
The judges concurred on the sentence. = 法官们对判决意见一致。
The witnesses concurred in their accounts of the accident. = 目击者们对事故的描述一致。


现在完成时:
All the experts have concurred that the project is feasible. = 所有专家都一致认为该项目是可行的。
The committee has concurred with the recommendations. = 委员会已同意这些建议。


过去完成时:
The lawyers had concurred on a settlement before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，律师们已就和解达成一致。
They had concurred that the risk was too high. = 他们之前已经一致认为风险太高。


将来时:
The board will likely concur with the CEO's proposal. = 董事会很可能会同意首席执行官的提议。
We expect that the two parties will concur on a solution. = 我们预计双方将会就解决方案达成一致。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence concurred with the defendant's statement, he would be acquitted. = 如果证据与被告的陈述一致，他将被无罪释放。
I wish the results concurred with our expectations. = 我希望结果与我们的预期一致。


被动语态:
The decision was concurred in by the majority of members. = 大多数成员同意了这一决定。
His analysis was concurred with by the scientific community. = 科学界同意了他的分析。


疑问句:
Do the two studies concur in their findings? = 这两项研究的结果是否一致？
Does the evidence concur with the witness's testimony? = 证据是否与证人的证词一致？


否定句:
The two sides did not concur on the terms of the agreement. = 双方在协议条款上未达成一致。
The committee did not concur with the proposed changes. = 委员会不同意拟议的更改。


PHRASE->
concur with = 同意
concur in = 同意（某个决定或意见）
concur on = 就…达成一致
to concur that = 一致认为
concurring opinion = 协同意见
