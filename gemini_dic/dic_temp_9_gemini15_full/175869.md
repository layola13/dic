ratatouille:/ˌrætəˈtuːi/|/ˈrætətuːi/| n. |法国杂菜炖|A Provençal French stew of vegetables, typically including tomatoes, zucchini, eggplant, peppers, and onions, often with garlic, herbs, and olive oil.|The ratatouille was simmered for hours until the vegetables were tender.|近义词：vegetable stew, vegetable medley|反义词：无
