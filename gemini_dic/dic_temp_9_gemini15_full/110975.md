liquidates:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪts/| vt.| 清算；偿付；结束；消灭 | To convert assets into cash; to settle accounts; to bring to an end; to eliminate or destroy. |例句：The company is liquidating its assets to pay off its debts. = 这家公司正在清算其资产以偿还债务。

例句：The government is trying to liquidate the rebel group. = 政府正试图消灭叛乱组织。

近义词：settle, wind up, terminate, eliminate, destroy

反义词：accumulate, establish, create, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company liquidates its assets every year. = 这家公司每年清算其资产。
He liquidates his debts promptly. = 他迅速偿还债务。

一般过去时:
They liquidated the business after many years of losses. = 他们经历多年亏损后清算了这家企业。
The court ordered the liquidation of the company's assets. = 法院命令清算公司资产。

现在完成时:
The bank has liquidated its holdings in the failing company. = 这家银行已经清算其在该失败公司的股份。
She has liquidated all her savings. = 她已经清算掉了所有的积蓄。

过去完成时:
The business had liquidated its assets before the bankruptcy filing. = 在申请破产之前，这家企业已经清算掉了其资产。
He had liquidated his investments before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他已经清算掉了他的投资。

将来时:
The company will liquidate its remaining inventory. = 公司将清算其剩余库存。
They will liquidate the partnership next month. = 他们下个月将清算合伙企业。

虚拟语气:
If the company were to liquidate, many people would lose their jobs. = 如果公司要清算，许多人将会失业。
I wish they had liquidated their assets sooner. = 我希望他们早点清算资产。

被动语态:
The assets were liquidated quickly and efficiently. = 资产被迅速有效地清算。
The company is being liquidated due to financial difficulties. =  由于财务困难，该公司正在被清算。

疑问句:
Will the company be liquidated? = 公司会被清算吗？
How will the assets be liquidated? = 资产将如何清算？

否定句:
The company did not liquidate its assets. = 公司没有清算其资产。
He has not yet liquidated his debts. = 他尚未偿还债务。


PHRASE->
liquidate assets = 清算资产
liquidate debt = 清偿债务
liquidate a company = 清算公司
liquidate a position = 平仓
orderly liquidation = 有序清算

词源:
liquidate源于拉丁语liquidare，意思是“使液体化”，引申为“清除，清理”。  其含义演变与商业活动中将资产变现（如同将固态资产变成现金这种流动资产）密切相关。
