olea:/ˈoʊliə/| n.|橄榄树；橄榄；橄榄油|The word "olea" is the Latin name for the olive tree and its fruit. It is the source of many words related to olives and olive oil.  The genus Olea includes various species of evergreen trees and shrubs.  |例句：The ancient Greeks cultivated the olea extensively. 古希腊人广泛种植橄榄树。|例句：Extra virgin olive oil, derived from the fruit of the olea, is highly prized. 来自橄榄果实的特级初榨橄榄油非常珍贵。|近义词：olive tree, olive plant|反义词：无|名词
