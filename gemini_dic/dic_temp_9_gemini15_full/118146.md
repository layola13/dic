menstruates:/ˈmɛnstruˌeɪts/| v.|月经来潮；行经|To experience menstruation; to have one's period.|例句：She menstruates regularly every month. 她每月都规律地来月经。|例句：Many women experience discomfort when they menstruate. 许多女性在月经期间都会感到不适。|近义词：have one's period, bleed (in the context of menstruation)|反义词：None (the antonym would describe the absence of menstruation, not the act itself).
