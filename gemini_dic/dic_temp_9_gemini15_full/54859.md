displays:/dɪˈspleɪz/| v. n.|展示；显示；陈列|To show or reveal something; to exhibit something publicly.|例句：The museum displays a vast collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了大量古代文物。

例句：The shop displays its goods attractively in the window. 商店把商品漂亮地陈列在橱窗里。


近义词：show, exhibit, present, demonstrate|反义词：conceal, hide, obscure|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He displays his artwork at the gallery. = 他在画廊展出他的艺术作品。
The store displays a wide variety of products. = 这家商店展示各种各样的产品。


一般过去时:
She displayed her talent during the competition. = 她在比赛中展示了她的才能。
The artist displayed his paintings at the local art fair. = 艺术家在当地艺术博览会上展出了他的画作。


现在完成时:
They have displayed remarkable resilience throughout the crisis. = 在整个危机中，他们表现出了非凡的韧性。
The company has displayed a commitment to sustainability. = 公司展现了对可持续发展的承诺。


过去完成时:
He had displayed a keen interest in science since childhood. = 他从小就对科学表现出浓厚的兴趣。
The museum had displayed the painting for several months before its restoration. = 在修复之前，博物馆展出了这幅画几个月。


将来时:
She will display her new collection at Fashion Week. = 她将在时装周上展示她的新系列。
The company will display its latest technology at the trade show. = 公司将在贸易展会上展示其最新技术。


虚拟语气:
If he displayed more confidence, he would succeed. = 如果他表现出更多的自信，他就会成功。
I wish he displayed more consideration for others. = 我希望他能多为别人着想。


被动语态:
The products were displayed beautifully in the showroom. = 产品在展厅里被漂亮地展示。
The paintings are being displayed in the new gallery. = 画作正在新的画廊展出。


疑问句:
How will the data be displayed on the screen? = 数据如何在屏幕上显示？
Where are the products displayed in the store? = 产品在商店的哪个地方展出？


否定句:
He didn't display any emotion during the interview. = 他在面试中没有表现出任何情绪。
The company doesn't display its financial statements publicly. = 公司不公开展示其财务报表。
