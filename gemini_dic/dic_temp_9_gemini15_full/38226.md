computatively:/kɒmˈpjuːtətɪvli/ | adv. | 计算上地；从计算角度来看 |  Adverb describing something done or considered in relation to computation or computing. It emphasizes the computational aspect of a process or result.  | The algorithm is computationally expensive. = 这个算法计算量很大。 |  computationally, numerically |  qualitatively, descriptively
