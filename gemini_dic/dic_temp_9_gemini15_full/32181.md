chiaroscuro:/ˌkiˌɑːrəˈskʊəroʊ/ | n. |明暗对比法，光影效果 | The dramatic use of light and shadow in an artwork, especially in painting and drawing, to create a three-dimensional effect and enhance the emotional impact.  It involves strong contrasts between light and dark areas, often used to highlight a particular subject or create a sense of depth and drama. | Example:  The painting's chiaroscuro was masterful, creating a powerful sense of mystery.  这幅画的明暗对比法技艺精湛，营造出一种强烈的神秘感。|近义词：contrasts, shading, light and shadow |反义词：uniform lighting, even illumination | 名词
