horserake: /ˈhɔːrsreɪk/ | n. | 马耙；大耙 | A large rake, typically used for gathering hay or other loose material in farming.  It is usually pulled by a horse or other animal, hence the name. | 例句：The farmer used a horserake to gather the hay after cutting. 农民用马耙收割完干草后收集干草。


The old barn was filled with the smell of hay and the sound of the horserake. 旧谷仓里充满了干草的气味和马耙的声音。| 近义词：rake, hay rake | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying scattering instead of gathering could be considered antonyms. | 名词
