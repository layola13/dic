risker:/ˈrɪskər/| n.|冒险者，喜欢冒险的人|A person who takes risks.  Someone who is willing to take chances and face potential dangers or losses in pursuit of a goal. |He's a risker; he invested all his savings in a startup.|近义词：adventurer, daredevil, gambler|反义词：cautious person, coward
