interalliance:/ˌɪntərəˈlaɪəns/| n. |国际联盟，国际同盟|A formal alliance or agreement between two or more nations or groups.  It often implies a cooperative arrangement for mutual benefit or defense.  The term suggests a more extensive and formal agreement than a simple alliance. |例句：The interalliance between the three countries ensured regional stability. 三国之间的国际联盟确保了区域稳定。
The formation of the interalliance was a significant step towards global peace. 国际联盟的形成是迈向世界和平的重要一步。|近义词：alliance, coalition, confederation |反义词：conflict, discord, antagonism |名词
