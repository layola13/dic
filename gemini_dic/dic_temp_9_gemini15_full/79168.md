glamorized:/ˈɡlæməraɪzd/| v.|使魅力四射；使具有魅力；美化；渲染；|to make something seem more attractive and exciting than it really is|The advertisement glamorized the life of a fashion model.  广告美化了时装模特的职业生活。|The movie glamorized violence. 这部电影美化了暴力。| glorified, romanticized | criticized, denigrated | 动词


USAGE->

They glamorize the celebrity lifestyle. = 他们美化了名流的生活方式。
She glamorizes her past experiences. = 她美化了她过去的经历。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
