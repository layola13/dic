elvanite:/ˈɛlvənaɪt/| n. |埃尔瓦岩|A fine-grained, porphyritic rock consisting essentially of quartz, alkali feldspar, and a mafic mineral (usually biotite or hornblende).  It is a type of felsic volcanic rock similar to rhyolite but with a distinctive texture. Elvanites are often found as dykes or sills intruding into other rocks.|The elvanite dykes in Cornwall are well-known for their distinctive appearance. |近义词：rhyolite, porphyry|反义词：暂无
