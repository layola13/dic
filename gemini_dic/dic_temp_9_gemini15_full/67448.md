extirpations:/ˌɛkstərˈpeɪʃənz/| n.|根除；消灭；拔除|The complete destruction or removal of something.|例句：The extirpations of invasive species are crucial for preserving biodiversity. 根除入侵物种对于保护生物多样性至关重要。

例句：The government's efforts at extirpations of poverty have yielded mixed results. 政府根除贫困的努力收效参半。|近义词：eradication, elimination, removal, extermination|反义词：cultivation, propagation, fostering
