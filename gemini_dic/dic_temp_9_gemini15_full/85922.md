headlight:/ˈhɛdˌlaɪt/ | n. | 车前灯，头灯 | A headlight is a powerful light at the front of a vehicle, used to illuminate the road ahead at night or in low-visibility conditions.  | Example: The car's headlights cut through the fog.  这辆车的车前灯穿透了雾。|近义词：headlamp | 反义词： taillight
