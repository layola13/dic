fictionally:/ˈfɪkʃənəli/ | adv. |虚构地，小说般地 |  In a fictional manner; as if in a work of fiction.  It describes something that is imagined or invented, not real. | 例句：The story is fictionally based on real events.  这个故事是虚构地基于真实事件。

The novel fictionally portrays the life of a famous painter. 这部小说虚构地描绘了一位著名画家的生活。

近义词：imaginatively, fancifully, hypothetically | 反义词：factually, realistically
