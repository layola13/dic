segmentation:/ˌseɡmenˈteɪʃən/| n. |分割；细分；部分化 |The process of dividing something into smaller parts.  It's used in various fields, including image processing, data analysis, and marketing. | Example:  The image segmentation algorithm successfully identified the objects in the picture. 图像分割算法成功地识别了图片中的物体。The market segmentation strategy targeted different customer groups. 市场细分策略针对不同的客户群体。|近义词：division, partitioning, subdivision |反义词：integration, unification
