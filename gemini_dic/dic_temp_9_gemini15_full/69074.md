faulture:/ˈfɔːltʃə(r)/| n.|故障；缺陷；缺点|A faulture is a defect, imperfection, or flaw. It refers to something that is not working correctly or is not as it should be.  This term is less common than "fault" or "failure".|例句：The faulture in the system caused a major disruption. 系统的故障导致了严重的中断。

例句：The engineers investigated the faulture of the engine. 工程师们调查了发动机的故障。

近义词：fault, defect, flaw, failure, imperfection

反义词：perfection, success
