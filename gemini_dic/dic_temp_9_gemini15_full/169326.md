promoted:/prəˈmoʊtɪd/| v. |提升；促进；推销| to raise someone to a higher rank or position; to further the progress of something; to advertise or make something known.|例句：He was promoted to manager. 他被提升为经理。  She promoted her new book on social media.她在社交媒体上推广她的新书。|近义词：elevate, advance, publicize|反义词：demote, downgrade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They promote their products aggressively. = 他们积极推销他们的产品。
The company promotes employee growth. = 公司提倡员工发展。

一般过去时:
She was promoted last year. = 她去年被提升了。
The government promoted the new policy. = 政府推广了这项新政策。


现在完成时:
He has been promoted twice this year. = 他今年已经升职两次了。
They have successfully promoted the event. = 他们已经成功地推广了这个活动。


过去完成时:
She had been promoted before she moved to the new office. = 她搬到新办公室之前就已经升职了。
The company had promoted the product for months before it launched. = 在产品发布之前，公司已经推广了几个月。


将来时:
He will be promoted next month. = 他下个月将被提升。
They will promote the new film in the coming weeks. = 他们将在接下来的几周内推广这部新电影。


虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he might be promoted. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能会被提升。
I wish I had been promoted sooner. = 我希望我早点被提升。


被动语态:
The new product is being promoted heavily. = 新产品正在大力推广。
He was promoted to senior manager. = 他被提升为高级经理。


疑问句:
Will she be promoted this year? = 她今年会被提升吗？
How will they promote the new product? = 他们将如何推广新产品？


否定句:
He wasn't promoted this time. = 他这次没有被提升。
They didn't promote the event effectively. = 他们没有有效地推广这个活动。
