flicker:/ˈflɪkər/ | v., n. | 闪烁；摇曳；颤动 | To shine with an unsteady light; to move with a quick, unsteady motion.  |例句：The candle flame flickered in the draft.  烛光在气流中闪烁。

The leaves flickered in the breeze. 叶子在微风中摇曳。|近义词：flutter, shimmer, glimmer, tremble |反义词：steadfast, stable, constant |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The light flickers constantly. 灯光不断闪烁。
The bird flickers its wings. 鸟儿拍打着翅膀。


一般过去时:
The screen flickered and then went black. 屏幕闪烁了一下然后变黑了。
The candle flickered and died. 蜡烛闪烁了一下然后熄灭了。


现在完成时:
The bulb has flickered all night. 灯泡整夜都在闪烁。
The image has flickered on and off. 图像一直在忽明忽暗。


过去完成时:
The lights had flickered several times before going out completely. 灯在完全熄灭之前闪烁了好几次。
The screen had flickered intermittently throughout the presentation. 整个演示过程中屏幕断断续续地闪烁。


将来时:
The lights will flicker if the power supply is unstable. 如果电源不稳定，灯就会闪烁。
The candle flame will flicker in the wind. 烛光会在风中摇曳。


虚拟语气:
If the lights flickered again, I'd call an electrician. 如果灯再闪烁，我就叫电工。
I wish the screen wouldn't flicker so much. 我希望屏幕不要闪烁得这么厉害。


被动语态:
The image was flickered by a faulty connection. 图像因连接故障而闪烁。
The lights were flickered by a sudden power surge. 灯光因突然的电源浪涌而闪烁。


疑问句:
Is the light flickering again? 灯光又在闪烁了吗？
Does the screen flicker when you play videos? 你播放视频时屏幕会闪烁吗？


否定句:
The lights didn't flicker at all. 灯光根本没有闪烁。
The image didn't flicker during the entire presentation. 整个演示过程中图像都没有闪烁。
