nonutilities:/ˌnɑːnjuːˈtɪlətiz/ | n. |非公用事业；非必需品 |Things or services that are not considered essential or necessary, unlike utilities such as electricity or water.  They are often considered luxuries or discretionary spending. |例句：Many nonutilities saw decreased demand during the economic downturn. 许多非必需品在经济衰退期间需求下降。

例句：The company focuses on nonutilities, such as entertainment and leisure products. 这家公司专注于非必需品，例如娱乐和休闲产品。


近义词：luxuries, discretionary spending, non-essentials


反义词：utilities, necessities, essentials


词性: 名词
