USAGE->
一般现在时:
The closure of the roads causes significant traffic delays. 道路封闭导致严重的交通延误。
This project requires the closure of several streets. 这个项目需要封闭几条街道。


一般过去时:
The company announced the closure of its European operations. 公司宣布关闭其在欧洲的业务。
The sudden closure of the bridge caused chaos. 桥梁的突然封闭造成了混乱。


现在完成时:
The authorities have ordered the closure of all schools. 当局已下令关闭所有学校。

过去完成时:
