hieronymite:/ˌhaɪəˈrɒnɪmaɪt/| n. |耶柔米会修士；耶柔米会修道士 |A member of the Order of St. Jerome, a monastic order known for its strict asceticism and scholarship.  The order was founded in the 14th century and emphasized a return to the original ideals of monastic life.  |  Many hieronymites dedicated their lives to prayer, study, and copying manuscripts. = 许多耶柔米会修士将他们的生命奉献给了祈祷、学习和抄写手稿。
The monastery housed numerous hieronymites who lived a life of strict discipline. = 这座修道院住着许多过着严格纪律生活的耶柔米会修士。|近义词：monk, friar |反义词：layperson
