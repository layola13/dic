questions:/ˈkwɛstʃənz/ | n. | 问题，疑问 | Questions are a fundamental part of learning.  问题是学习的根本组成部分。  |近义词：queries, inquiries, interrogations | 反义词：answers, solutions


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 晚会上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she accomplished in her career? = 她在事业上取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
what if = 如果...怎么样
what for = 为什么
what about = ...怎么样
what's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
no matter what = 无论如何
