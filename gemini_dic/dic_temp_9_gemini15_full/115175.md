manically: /ˈmænɪkli/ | adj. | 狂躁地，极度兴奋地 | Characterized by excessive energy and excitement; often indicative of a mental disorder.  |例句：He spoke manically, his eyes wide and unfocused. 他语无伦次地说着话，眼睛睁得大大的，目光涣散。


例句：She paced the room manically, unable to sit still. 她狂躁地在房间里踱步，坐不住。


近义词：frantically, wildly, feverishly, excitedly


反义词：calmly, placidly, serenely, quietly


词源:  Derived from "mania," meaning madness or excessive enthusiasm, ultimately from Greek "mainō" (to rage).  The suffix "-ically" indicates in a manner of.
