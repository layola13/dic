earthfork:/ˈɜːrθfɔːrk/ | n. | 挖掘机，挖土机 | A tool or machine used for digging or moving earth.  It often refers to a type of agricultural implement used for cultivating the soil. | Example: The farmer used an earthfork to loosen the hard soil before planting.  农民在播种前用挖掘机松土。|近义词：digger, excavator, plough (in some contexts)|反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., "paver" if referring to road construction).
