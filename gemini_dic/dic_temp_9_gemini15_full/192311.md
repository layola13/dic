scrib:/skrɪb/| v.|书写，涂写|To write or draw something quickly or carelessly.|He scribbled a note and shoved it in his pocket. 他匆匆写了个便条塞进口袋里。|write, jot, doodle|erase, delete|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I scribble notes during the lecture. = 我在课堂上匆匆记笔记。
She scribbles poems in her spare time. = 她利用空闲时间涂鸦诗歌。


一般过去时:
He scribbled his name on the contract. = 他在合同上潦草地签了名。
They scribbled down the address quickly. = 他们迅速记下了地址。


现在完成时:
I have scribbled a few ideas for the project. = 我已经为这个项目写下了一些想法。
She has scribbled a lot of notes in her notebook. = 她在笔记本上写了很多笔记。


过去完成时:
He had scribbled a message before he left. = 他离开前匆匆写了一条消息。
They had scribbled the answers on a piece of paper. = 他们把答案草草地写在了一张纸上。


将来时:
I will scribble a quick reply to the email. = 我会快速回复这封邮件。
She will scribble down the key points of the meeting. = 她将记下会议的要点。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would scribble a longer letter. = 如果我有时间，我会写一封更长的信。
I wish I could scribble more often, but I'm always so busy. = 我希望我能经常涂鸦，但我总是很忙。


被动语态:
The message was scribbled on a napkin. = 这条信息写在餐巾纸上。
The notes were scribbled in a hurry. = 笔记是匆忙记下的。


疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't scribble anything important. = 我没有写下任何重要的事情。
She doesn't scribble poetry anymore. = 她不再写诗了。
