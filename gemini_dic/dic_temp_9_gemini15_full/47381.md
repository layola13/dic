dead-to-rights:/ˌded tə ˈraɪts/| adj.|确凿无疑地；当场抓住的；罪证确凿的|Caught in the act; undeniably guilty.  The phrase implies a certainty of guilt, often involving being caught in the act of committing a crime.|例句：The police caught the thief dead-to-rights with the stolen goods in his hands. 警方当场抓住小偷，赃物就在他手里。  The evidence against him was dead-to-rights, leading to his immediate arrest.  针对他的证据确凿无疑，导致他被立即逮捕。|近义词：caught red-handed, undeniably guilty, irrefutably guilty|反义词：innocent, acquitted, unproven
