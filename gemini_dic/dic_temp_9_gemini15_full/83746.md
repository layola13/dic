hailstone:/ˈheɪlstəʊn/| n. |冰雹|A small ball of ice that falls from the sky as hail.|The hailstones were the size of golf balls and damaged many cars.|近义词：ice pellet, ice ball|反义词：raindrop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Hailstones fall from the sky during a hailstorm. = 冰雹在冰雹暴雨期间从天而降。
The hailstones damage the crops every year. = 冰雹每年都会损害庄稼。


一般过去时:
Large hailstones fell during yesterday's storm. = 昨天的暴风雨中下了很大的冰雹。
The hailstones damaged the roof of my car. = 冰雹损坏了我的车顶。


现在完成时:
Hailstones have damaged the farmer's crops this season. = 冰雹在这个季节损坏了农民的庄稼。

过去完成时:
