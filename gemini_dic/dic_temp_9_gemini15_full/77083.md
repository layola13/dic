It is typically used as a living space, especially by poor people.|例句：The artist lived in a small, cramped garret.  这位艺术家住在一个狭小拥挤的阁楼里。|例句：The family stored old furniture in the dusty garret. 这家人把旧家具储存在尘土飞扬的阁楼里。|近义词：attic, loft|反义词：basement, cellar|名词
