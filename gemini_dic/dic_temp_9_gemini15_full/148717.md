overtrace:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪs/ | vt. |描摹；复写；重描；过度追踪|To trace over again; to trace a line or design over; to trace (a line, etc.) again so as to make it more distinct; to follow (a route, etc.) repeatedly or unnecessarily.|例句：He overtraced the design until the paper tore. 他反复描摹设计图纸，直到纸张撕裂。例句：The detective overtraced the suspect's movements, wasting valuable time.  侦探反复追踪嫌疑人的行踪，浪费了宝贵的时间。|近义词：retrace, re-trace, copy, duplicate |反义词：erase, delete
