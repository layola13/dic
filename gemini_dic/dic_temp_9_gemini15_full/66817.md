expansionisms:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪzəm/| n. |扩张主义；扩张政策 |The policy or practice of a nation extending its power and influence, often through territorial acquisition.  It often involves the belief in the superiority of one's own culture or system and a desire to spread it to other parts of the world. |例句：The aggressive expansionism of the Roman Empire led to its vast territorial holdings.  罗马帝国的侵略性扩张主义导致了它拥有广阔的领土。

例句：American expansionism in the 19th century resulted in the acquisition of vast territories in the West.  19世纪美国的扩张主义导致其获得了西部大片领土。


近义词：imperialism, colonialism, aggrandizement, expansion, ascendancy |反义词：isolationism, pacifism, contraction


词性:名词
