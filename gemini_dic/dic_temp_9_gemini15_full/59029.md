dwarfing:/ˈdwɔːrfɪŋ/| v.|使矮小；使相形见绌；|The act of making something or someone appear smaller or less significant than it actually is, often by comparison.|例句：The towering skyscraper dwarfed the surrounding buildings.  高耸的摩天大楼使周围的建筑物相形见绌。

例句：His immense success dwarfed his earlier achievements. 他巨大的成功使他早期的成就相形见绌。


近义词：reducing, diminishing, overshadowing, belittling


反义词：magnifying, enlarging, enhancing, elevating


词性:动词
