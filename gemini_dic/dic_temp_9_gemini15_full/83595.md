WORD ORIGIN->
From "haematology" (the study of blood) + "-logically" (in a manner relating to).  "Haematology" itself derives from "haema/haemo-" (blood) + "-logy" (study of). The "haema/haemo-" prefix originates from Greek  αἷμα (haima), meaning "blood".
