catch:/kætʃ/| vt. n.|抓住，捕捉；感染；理解；赶上|To seize or grasp suddenly; to trap or ensnare; to overtake or come upon unexpectedly; to understand or comprehend; to become infected with a disease.|例句：The cat caught a mouse. 猫抓到了一只老鼠。|例句：I caught a cold. 我感冒了。|近义词：grab, seize, apprehend, understand|反义词：miss, lose, fail to understand|动词


caught:/kɔːt/| v.|过去式和过去分词|抓住，捕捉；感染；理解；赶上|Past tense and past participle of "catch".|例句：He caught the ball. 他接住了球。|例句：She caught the flu last week. 她上周得了流感。|近义词：grabbed, seized, apprehended, understood|反义词：missed, lost, failed to understand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I catch the bus every morning. = 我每天早上都乘公共汽车。
He catches fish in the river. = 他在河里钓鱼。


一般过去时:
I caught a cold yesterday. = 我昨天感冒了。
She caught the thief red-handed. = 她当场抓住了小偷。


现在完成时:
I have caught three fish so far. = 到目前为止，我已经钓到三条鱼了。
He has caught the flu. = 他得了流感。


过去完成时:
I had caught the train before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前，我已经赶上了火车。
She had caught many butterflies in her childhood. = 她童年时抓过许多蝴蝶。


将来时:
I will catch the early train tomorrow. = 我明天将乘坐早班火车。
They will catch the criminal soon. = 他们很快就会抓住罪犯。


虚拟语气:
If I caught a fish, I would cook it immediately. = 如果我钓到鱼，我会马上把它煮了。
I wish I had caught that ball. = 我真希望我接住了那个球。


被动语态:
The thief was caught by the police. = 小偷被警察抓住了。
The ball was caught by the fielder. = 球被外野手接住了。


疑问句:
Did you catch what he said? = 你听懂他说了什么了吗？
Can you catch the meaning of this sentence? = 你能理解这句话的意思吗？


否定句:
I didn't catch your name. = 我没听清你的名字。
He didn't catch the bus this morning. = 他今天早上没赶上公共汽车。
