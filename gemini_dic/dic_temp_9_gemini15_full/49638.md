demilitarise:/ˌdiːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| vt.|非军事化；解除武装|To remove military forces or weapons from (a place or area).  The process of removing military forces, weapons, and other military equipment from a specified area. |例句：The treaty calls for the demilitarisation of the border region.  该条约要求将边境地区非军事化。

例句：The island was demilitarised after the war ended.  战争结束后，该岛屿被非军事化。


近义词：disarm, neutralize, pacify |反义词：militarize, arm
