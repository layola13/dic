sacramental:/ˌsækrəˈmɛntəl/| adj.|圣礼的；神圣的；庄严的；神圣仪式用的| Relating to or connected with a sacrament; having the character or effect of a sacrament;  characterized by sacredness or solemnity.  The word implies a sense of deep religious significance and often involves a ritual or ceremony.|例句：The sacramental bread and wine were consecrated by the priest.  圣餐面包和葡萄酒被牧师祝圣了。

The sacramental nature of the ceremony filled the church with awe.  仪式的庄严性质使教堂充满了敬畏。

|近义词：sacred, holy, consecrated, solemn, religious|反义词：profane, unholy, secular, irreverent|词性：形容词
