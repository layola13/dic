placaters:/pləˈkeɪtərz/| n. | 安抚者；调解者 | People or things that placate.  Those who seek to appease or soothe.  |例句：The placaters tried to calm the angry mob with promises of reform.  安抚者试图通过承诺改革来平息愤怒的暴民。

例句：  His soothing words acted as placaters, calming the upset customer. 他温言软语起到了安抚作用，让不满的顾客平静下来。


近义词：pacifiers, appeasers, conciliators, mediators

反义词：provocateurs, agitators, antagonists


词性:名词
