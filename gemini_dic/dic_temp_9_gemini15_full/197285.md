sharpshooter:/ˈʃɑːrpˌʃuːtər/ | n. |神枪手，精准射手 | A sharpshooter is someone who is exceptionally skilled at shooting, especially with firearms, consistently hitting their targets with great accuracy.  They are known for their precision and expertise. |例句：He was a sharpshooter in the army, renowned for his incredible accuracy. 他是军队里的一名神枪手，以其惊人的准确性而闻名。

例句：The sharpshooter took down the target from 500 yards away.  神枪手在500码外击中了目标。


近义词： marksman, crack shot, expert shot | 反义词：poor shot, inaccurate shooter | 词性：名词
