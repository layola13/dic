kinesis'/kaɪˈniːsɪs/| n. |运动，动势，自动运动|The spontaneous movement of an organism in response to a stimulus, often involving changes in speed or direction.  This differs from taxis, which is directed movement toward or away from a stimulus.|例句：The kinesis of the woodlice increased in dry conditions.  干燥的环境下，鼠妇的运动增强了。|近义词：movement, locomotion, motion|反义词：stasis, immobility|
