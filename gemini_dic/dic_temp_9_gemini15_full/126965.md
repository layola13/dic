narrowcasting: /ˈnæroʊkæstɪŋ/ | n. |定向传播，目标受众传播 | The process of transmitting a message to a select group of people, rather than a mass audience.  This is often done through specific channels tailored to the interests of the target audience. | Example:  The company used narrowcasting to target its advertising campaign at young professionals.  这家公司利用定向传播将广告活动目标定位于年轻专业人士。 | 近义词: targeted advertising, focused communication | 反义词: broadcasting
