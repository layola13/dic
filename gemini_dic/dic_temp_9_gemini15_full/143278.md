outdoes:/ˌaʊtˈduːz/| vt.|胜过；超过|To surpass or exceed in performance, achievement, or skill.|例句：He outdoes his rivals in terms of dedication and hard work. 他在奉献和努力方面胜过他的竞争对手。|例句：Her latest novel outdoes all her previous works in terms of complexity and depth. 她的最新小说在复杂性和深度方面超越了她以前的所有作品。|近义词：surpass, excel, outperform, eclipse|反义词：underperform, fail


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outdoes everyone in the competition. = 他在比赛中胜过所有人。
She consistently outdoes her colleagues in sales. = 她在销售方面总是胜过她的同事。

一般过去时:
The team outdid their previous best score. = 这个团队超过了他们之前的最佳成绩。
He outdid himself in the final performance. = 他在最后的表演中超越了自己。

现在完成时:
She has outdone all expectations with her success. = 她凭借自己的成功超越了所有人的期望。
He has outdone himself this year in terms of productivity. = 今年他在生产力方面超越了自己。

过去完成时:
They had outdone their rivals by the time the game ended. = 在比赛结束时，他们已经胜过了对手。
She had outdone her own previous record. = 她已经打破了她自己的之前的记录。

将来时:
He will outdo his opponents in the upcoming election. = 他将在即将到来的选举中胜过他的对手。
She believes she will outdo her previous achievement. = 她相信她会超越她之前的成就。

虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he might outdo his brother. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能会胜过他的兄弟。
I wish I could outdo my current limitations. = 我希望我能克服我目前的局限性。

被动语态:
His achievements were outdone by her groundbreaking research. = 他的成就被她突破性的研究超越了。
The previous record was outdone by a newcomer. = 之前的记录被一位新人打破了。

疑问句:

Can she outdo the current market leader? = 她能胜过目前的市场领导者吗？

否定句:
He didn't outdo his opponent in the debate. = 他在辩论中没有胜过他的对手。
They failed to outdo the established champion. = 他们未能胜过现任冠军。
