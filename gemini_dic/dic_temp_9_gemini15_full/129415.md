nominately:/ˈnɒmɪneɪtli/ | adv. | 名义上地，表面上地 | In name only; ostensibly.  It suggests that the situation is not entirely true or accurate in reality. |例句：He was nominally in charge, but the real power rested with his assistant. 他名义上负责，但真正的权力掌握在他助手手中。


例句：The company is nominally independent, but it's heavily influenced by its parent company. 这家公司名义上是独立的，但它受到母公司的很大影响。


近义词：ostensibly, apparently, seemingly, purportedly


反义词：actually, really, truly, in fact


词性：副词
