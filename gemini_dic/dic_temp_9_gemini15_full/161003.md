postbag: /ˈpoʊstbæɡ/ | n. |邮袋；信件；邮件| A bag containing letters and parcels delivered by post.  |例句：The postman delivered a heavy postbag this morning.  今早邮递员送来一个沉重的邮袋。|例句：The postbag was overflowing with letters. 邮袋里装满了信件，都快溢出来了。|近义词：mailbag, mail, post |反义词：None
