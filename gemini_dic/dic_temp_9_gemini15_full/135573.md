词源:
The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "ponderosity" (meaning "heaviness; weightiness").  "Ponderosity" itself originates from the Latin word "ponderosus," meaning "heavy," related to "pondus," meaning "weight."
