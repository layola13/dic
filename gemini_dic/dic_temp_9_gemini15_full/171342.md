publication:/ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |出版物；发表；公布|The act of publishing or the state of being published; something that has been published, such as a book, journal, or article.  It can also refer to the act of making something publicly known.|例句：The publication of her new novel was a major event.  她的新小说出版是一件大事。|例句：The publication of the report caused a stir.  报告的发表引起了轰动。|近义词：printing, release, dissemination, issue|反义词：suppression, secrecy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company publishes many books each year. = 这家公司每年出版许多书籍。
This magazine publishes articles on various topics. = 这本杂志发表关于各种主题的文章。


一般过去时:
They published their findings in a scientific journal. = 他们在科学期刊上发表了他们的研究结果。
The newspaper published an article about the accident. = 报纸刊登了一篇关于事故的文章。


现在完成时:
She has published several articles on this subject. = 她已经发表了几篇关于这个主题的文章。
The author has published three novels so far. =  这位作者到目前为止已经出版了三部小说。


过去完成时:
They had published the book before the conference. = 他们在会议之前已经出版了这本书。
The journal had already published similar research. = 这本期刊已经发表过类似的研究。


将来时:
They will publish the results next month. = 他们下个月将公布结果。
The university will publish a new study on climate change. = 大学将发表一项关于气候变化的新研究。


虚拟语气:
If they published the findings, it would cause a controversy. = 如果他们发表这些研究结果，将会引起争议。
I wish they had published the book earlier. = 我希望他们早点出版这本书。


被动语态:
The article was published in the New York Times. = 这篇文章发表在《纽约时报》上。
The book is being published next year. = 这本书将于明年出版。


疑问句:
When was the book published? = 这本书是什么时候出版的？
Where was the article published? = 这篇文章在哪里发表的？


否定句:
They did not publish the results immediately. = 他们没有立即公布结果。
The research has not yet been published. = 这项研究尚未发表。


PHRASE->
a publication ban = 出版禁令
academic publication = 学术出版物
online publication = 在线出版物
periodical publication = 期刊出版物
recent publication = 最新出版物


词源:
From Old French *publicacion, from Latin publicatio (from publicare "to make public").
