diadem:/ˈdaɪədem/| n. |王冠；头饰 | A diadem is a jeweled crown or headband worn as a symbol of royalty.  It is often smaller and less elaborate than a full crown. | The queen wore a sparkling diadem at the coronation. 女王在加冕典礼上戴着一顶闪闪发光的王冠。  The ancient artifact was a diadem of gold and precious stones. 这件古代文物是一个由黄金和宝石制成的头饰。 | crown, coronet | simple headband | 名词
