reprocure:/ˌriːprəˈkjʊr/| vt.|重新获得；再次取得|To obtain or acquire something again; to get something back.|The company had to reprocure the vital components after the supplier went bankrupt. 公司供应商破产后不得不重新采购关键部件。|近义词：reacquire, regain, recover|反义词：lose, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reprocure essential supplies every month. = 他们每月重新采购必需的物资。
The company reprocures its raw materials from several suppliers. = 这家公司从多个供应商那里重新采购原材料。


一般过去时:
We reprocured the lost documents after a thorough search. = 我们经过仔细搜寻后重新找到了丢失的文件。
The government reprocured the land after a lengthy legal battle. = 政府经过漫长的法律诉讼后重新获得了那块土地。


现在完成时:
They have reprocured the damaged equipment and resumed production. = 他们已经重新采购了损坏的设备并恢复了生产。
The museum has reprocured a rare painting that was stolen years ago. = 该博物馆重新获得了一幅被盗多年的珍稀画作。


过去完成时:
Before the project restarted, they had reprocured all the necessary materials. = 在项目重启之前，他们已经重新采购了所有必要的材料。
The team had reprocured the software licenses before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前已经重新获得了软件许可证。


将来时:
We will reprocure the parts as soon as the new supplier is confirmed. = 一旦新的供应商确定，我们将重新采购零件。
The company will reprocure the software licenses next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度重新采购软件许可证。


虚拟语气:
If we had more budget, we would reprocure the advanced equipment. = 如果我们有更多预算，我们将重新采购先进设备。
I wish we could reprocure the antique furniture. = 我希望我们能够重新获得那些古董家具。


被动语态:
The lost data was reprocured from the backup server. = 丢失的数据是从备份服务器中重新获得的。
The necessary materials are being reprocured by the purchasing department. = 采购部门正在重新采购必要的材料。


疑问句:
Can we reprocure the defective products? = 我们能重新采购有缺陷的产品吗？
When will the damaged parts be reprocured? = 损坏的零件什么时候能重新采购？


否定句:
They did not reprocure the outdated software. = 他们没有重新采购过时的软件。
We could not reprocure the rare artifact. = 我们无法重新获得那件稀有的文物。
