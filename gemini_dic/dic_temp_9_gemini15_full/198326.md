shovels:/ˈʃʌvəlz/| n.|铲子，铁锹|A shovel is a tool with a broad blade and a handle, used for moving loose material like earth, snow, or coal.|例句：He used a shovel to dig a hole. 他用铲子挖了一个洞。
例句：The snow shovel was essential during the blizzard. 暴风雪期间，铲雪工具至关重要。|近义词：spade, scoop|反义词:None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shovels snow every winter. = 他每年冬天都铲雪。
They shovel gravel into the truck. = 他们用铲子往卡车上装碎石。

一般过去时:
I shoveled the driveway yesterday. = 我昨天铲了车道。
She shoveled the dirt into a wheelbarrow. = 她用铲子把土铲进独轮车里。

现在完成时:
We have shoveled all the snow. = 我们已经把所有的雪都铲完了。
They have shoveled the path already. = 他们已经铲好了路。

过去完成时:
He had shoveled the snow before the storm hit. = 暴风雪来临之前，他已经把雪铲完了。
They had shoveled the sand before the tide came in. = 涨潮之前，他们已经把沙子铲好了。

将来时:
I will shovel the walkway tomorrow. = 我明天会铲走人行道上的雪。
They will shovel the grain into the silo. = 他们将会把谷物铲进筒仓。

虚拟语气:
If I had a shovel, I would shovel the snow. = 如果我有一把铲子，我会铲雪。
I wish I had shoveled the snow earlier. = 我真希望我早点把雪铲了。

被动语态:
The snow was shoveled by the workers. = 雪是被工人们铲掉的。
The grain will be shoveled into the sacks. = 谷物会被铲进麻袋里。

疑问句:
Did you shovel the snow? = 你铲雪了吗？
Have they shoveled the path yet? = 他们铲好路了吗？

否定句:
I didn't shovel the snow. = 我没有铲雪。
He hasn't shoveled the driveway yet. = 他还没铲车道。
