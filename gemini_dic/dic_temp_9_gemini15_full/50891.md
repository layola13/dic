desertified:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 荒漠化的；沙漠化的 |  Describes land that has become desert-like due to deforestation, drought, or improper agricultural practices.  It indicates a loss of vegetation and fertility, transforming a previously productive area into a barren landscape. | The once fertile farmland has been desertified by years of drought.  曾经肥沃的农田因多年的干旱而沙漠化了。 The relentless sun beat down on the desertified land, turning it into a desolate wasteland.  无情的阳光照射着沙漠化的土地，把它变成了荒凉的荒地。 | arid, barren, parched | fertile, lush, green
