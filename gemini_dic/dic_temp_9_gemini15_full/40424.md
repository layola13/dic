contraries:/ˈkɒntrɛriz/| n.|对立面，相反的事物|Things that are opposite or contradictory to each other.  They represent opposing viewpoints, characteristics, or tendencies.|例句：Good and evil are contraries. 善与恶是对立面。

例句：Heat and cold are contraries.  热和冷是对立面。


近义词：opposites, antipodes, reverses

反义词：similarities, equivalents, parallels


词性:名词


词源:  From Old French *contrarie, from Latin contrarius "opposite, contrary," from contra "against" + -arius "-ary".
