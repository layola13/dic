evangelise:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/ | vt. | 传福音；宣扬福音；积极宣传 | To preach the gospel or Christian doctrine; to spread or promote (a belief, idea, or cause) widely. |  例句：He evangelised throughout the countryside. 他在乡下四处传福音。 |  例句：The company is evangelising its new product. 这家公司正在积极宣传其新产品。 | 近义词：preach, proselytize, promote | 反义词：suppress, silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He evangelises in the local community. = 他在当地社区传福音。
She evangelises the benefits of healthy eating. = 她积极宣传健康饮食的好处。

一般过去时:
They evangelised the message of peace. = 他们宣扬和平的信息。
He evangelised his new business ideas to potential investors. = 他向潜在投资者宣传他的新商业理念。

现在完成时:
She has evangelised across the nation for many years. = 她多年来一直在全国各地传教。
They have evangelised the importance of recycling. = 他们一直积极宣传回收的重要性。

过去完成时:
He had evangelised in several countries before settling down. = 他在定居之前曾在几个国家传教。
The company had evangelised its product to great success before the launch. = 在产品发布之前，该公司已成功地宣传了其产品。

将来时:
She will evangelise her new book at the upcoming book fair. = 她将在即将举行的图书博览会上宣传她的新书。
They will evangelise the benefits of renewable energy. = 他们将宣传可再生能源的好处。

虚拟语气:
If he evangelised more effectively, he would reach a wider audience. = 如果他更有效地传教，他就能接触到更广泛的受众。
I wish she had evangelised her ideas more clearly. = 我希望她能更清晰地宣传她的想法。

被动语态:
The gospel was evangelised throughout the Roman Empire. = 福音在整个罗马帝国都被传扬。
The new technology is being evangelised by tech companies. = 新技术正被科技公司积极宣传。

疑问句:
Does he evangelise regularly? = 他经常传教吗？
Is the company evangelising its products effectively? = 这家公司是否有效地宣传其产品？

否定句:
He does not evangelise door-to-door. = 他不挨家挨户地传教。
The organization did not evangelise its cause effectively. = 该组织没有有效地宣传其事业。
