restain:/riːˈsteɪn/| vt.|重新染色；抑制；阻止|To stain again; to restrain or check.|The artist decided to restain the wooden furniture a darker shade. 艺术家决定把木制家具重新染成深色。  He struggled to restain his anger. 他努力克制住怒火。|近义词：recolor, restrain, suppress|反义词：unleash, release
