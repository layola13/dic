词源:
The word "housewifely" is derived from "housewife" + "-ly".  "Housewife" itself comes from "house" + "wife," indicating a woman who manages a household.  The "-ly" suffix transforms the noun "housewife" into an adjective, describing something characteristic of or relating to a housewife.
