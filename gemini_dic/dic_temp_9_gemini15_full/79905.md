gnawing:/ˈnɔːɪŋ/ | v. | 啃咬；折磨；使苦恼 |  To bite or chew on (something) persistently.  It can also refer to a persistent feeling of anxiety or worry. |例句：The dog was gnawing on a bone.  这只狗在啃骨头。

The guilt was gnawing at his conscience. 负罪感折磨着他的良心。|近义词：chewing, biting, nibbling, worrying, tormenting |反义词：soothing, comforting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mouse gnaws on the cheese. = 老鼠啃着奶酪。
He gnaws his fingernails when he's nervous. = 他紧张的时候会啃指甲。


一般过去时:
The rabbit gnawed through the wire fence. = 兔子啃断了铁丝网。
She gnawed on her lip, deep in thought. = 她咬着嘴唇，陷入沉思。


现在完成时:
The rats have gnawed through the wooden beams. = 老鼠已经啃坏了木梁。
He has been gnawing on this problem for weeks. = 他已经苦恼这个问题好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
The termites had gnawed away most of the support beams before we discovered the damage. = 白蚁在我们发现损坏之前已经啃掉了大部分支撑梁。
She had gnawed her way through half the box of chocolates before she realized it. = 在她意识到之前，她已经啃掉了半盒巧克力。


将来时:
The squirrels will gnaw on the nuts all winter. = 松鼠整个冬天都会啃坚果。
I think this problem will gnaw at my mind for a long time. = 我认为这个问题会困扰我很久。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have so much work, I wouldn't feel this gnawing anxiety. = 如果我没有这么多工作，我就不会感到这种啃噬般的焦虑。
I wish this doubt wouldn't gnaw at my confidence. = 我希望这个疑问不会动摇我的信心。


被动语态:
The wood was gnawed by the insects. = 木头被昆虫啃咬了。
His conscience was gnawed by guilt. = 他的良心被罪恶感折磨着。


疑问句:
Is the dog gnawing on something again? = 狗又在啃什么东西了吗？
What's gnawing at you? = 你在烦恼什么？


否定句:
The dog wasn't gnawing on anything. = 狗没有啃任何东西。
Nothing seemed to gnaw at her peace of mind. = 似乎没有什么能扰乱她的平静。


PHRASE->
a gnawing hunger:  一阵难以忍受的饥饿
a gnawing doubt:  挥之不去的疑问
a gnawing fear:  挥之不去的恐惧
a gnawing sense of injustice:  强烈的正义感
gnawing anxiety:  啃噬般的焦虑
