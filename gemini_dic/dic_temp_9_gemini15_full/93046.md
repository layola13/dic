ido:/ˈaɪdoʊ/ | pron. | 我愿意，我愿意（结婚） |  A word used to express consent, especially in a marriage ceremony.  It's a shortened and informal version of "I do," indicating agreement or acceptance. | Example: "I do."  = "我愿意。" | 近义词：yes, I agree, I accept | 反义词：I refuse, I object


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do my homework every day. = 我每天都做作业。
He does the dishes after dinner. = 他晚饭后洗碗。


一般过去时:
I did my best. = 我尽力了。
She did well on the exam. = 她考试考得很好。


现在完成时:
I have done my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
They have done a great job. = 他们做得很好。


过去完成时:
I had done my homework before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经做完作业了。
She had already eaten before we arrived. = 我们到达之前她已经吃过了。


将来时:
I will do my best to help you. = 我会尽力帮助你。
He will do the project next week. = 他下周会做这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would do it differently. = 如果我是你，我会做得不一样。
I wish I had done better on the test. = 我希望我在考试中做得更好。


被动语态:
The work was done quickly. = 工作很快就被完成了。
The dishes are done. = 碗洗好了。


疑问句:
Do you like ice cream? = 你喜欢冰淇淋吗？
Does he know the answer? = 他知道答案吗？


否定句:
I don't like broccoli. = 我不喜欢西兰花。
He doesn't play the piano. = 他不弹钢琴。


PHRASE->
I do believe = 我确实相信
I do hope = 我确实希望
I do not = 我不（强调）
to do with = 与…有关
to do one's best = 尽力
