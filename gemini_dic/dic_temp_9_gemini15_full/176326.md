re-engine:/ˌriːˈɛndʒɪn/| v.|重新设计引擎；彻底改造；重新构想|To redesign or rebuild an engine, or by extension, to completely revamp or overhaul a system or process.|例句：The company decided to re-engine its marketing strategy after a series of poor sales. 公司在经历了一系列糟糕的销售业绩后，决定重新设计其营销策略。|例句：We need to re-engine our approach to customer service. 我们需要彻底改造我们的客户服务方式。|近义词：revamp, overhaul, redesign, reconstruct |反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-engine their software every year. = 他们每年都会重新设计他们的软件。
We re-engine our processes to improve efficiency. = 我们重新设计流程以提高效率。

一般过去时:
The mechanic re-engineered the car's engine. = 技工重新设计了汽车的引擎。
They re-engineered the entire production line. = 他们重新设计了整条生产线。

现在完成时:
The company has re-engineered its entire product line. = 公司已经重新设计了其整个产品线。
They have re-engineered the system to be more user-friendly. = 他们已经重新设计了系统，使其更易于用户使用。

过去完成时:
They had re-engineered the software before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经重新设计了软件。
The team had re-engineered the process to eliminate bottlenecks. = 团队已经重新设计了流程以消除瓶颈。


将来时:
We will re-engine the website next month. = 我们下个月将重新设计网站。
They will re-engine the manufacturing process to reduce costs. = 他们将重新设计制造流程以降低成本。

虚拟语气:
If we re-engineered the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果我们重新设计系统，它将更高效。
I wish we had re-engineered the product earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新设计产品。

被动语态:
The engine was re-engineered by a team of experts. = 引擎由专家团队重新设计。

疑问句:
