compoded:/kəmˈpoʊzɪd/ | adj. | 合成的；复合的；复杂的 | Composed means made up of several parts or elements.  It suggests a complex structure or a combination of different things. | The painting is a composed work of various textures and colors. 这幅画是由各种纹理和颜色组成的作品。| complex, compound, composite | simple, uncomplicated


USAGE->

The music is composed of various instruments.  这音乐是由各种乐器合成的。
The team is composed of highly skilled individuals.  这个团队由高技能人才组成。
