put an end to: /pʊt ən ɛnd tuː/ | phrasal verb | 结束；终止；消灭 | To cause something to stop or finish completely.  |  例句：The government promised to put an end to corruption. 政府承诺要结束腐败。

例句：We need to put an end to this violence. 我们需要制止这场暴力。


近义词：terminate, conclude, finish, abolish, eliminate, stop


反义词：start, begin, initiate, commence, perpetuate


词性：phrasal verb
