interscapulars:/ˌɪntərˈskæpjʊlər/| n. |肩胛间区域的；肩胛骨之间的|The area between the shoulder blades.  The term refers to the region of the back located between the scapulae (shoulder blades). This area is often associated with muscle pain, referred pain from internal organs, or skin conditions.|Example: The patient complained of intense pain in the interscapular region.  病人抱怨肩胛间区域剧烈疼痛。|近义词：dorsoscapular, interscapulohumeral|反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与其他身体部位相对|名词
