electromotor:/ˌɪˌlɛktrəˈmoʊtər/| n. |电动机|An electromotor is a motor that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy. It typically uses electromagnetic principles to generate rotational motion.  |例句：The electric car uses an electromotor to power its wheels. 这辆电动汽车使用电动机来驱动车轮。

例句：The factory relies on several large electromotors for its machinery.  这家工厂依靠几台大型电动机来驱动其机械设备。


近义词：electric motor, motor


反义词：generator (converts mechanical energy to electrical energy)


词性：noun
