deixis:/ˈdeɪksɪs/ | n. | 指示, 语境指示 | Deixis refers to words or phrases that cannot be understood without context, specifically the context of the speaker, the time of speaking, and the location of speaking.  These words rely on the situation to determine their meaning. Examples include pronouns (I, you, he, she, it, we, they), demonstratives (this, that, these, those), adverbs of time (now, then, today, yesterday, tomorrow), and adverbs of place (here, there). | Example: "I am here now."  (The meaning of "I," "here," and "now" depends entirely on who is speaking, where they are, and when they are speaking.) | 近义词：indication, reference | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is my book. = 这是我的书。
That is a beautiful flower. = 那是一朵漂亮的花。


一般过去时:
I was there yesterday. = 我昨天在那里。
He saw her then. = 他那时看到了她。


现在完成时:
I have been here for an hour. = 我在这里已经一个小时了。
We have just finished our work. = 我们刚刚完成了工作。


过去完成时:
I had already left when she arrived. = 她到的时候我已经离开了。
They had seen it before. = 他们以前见过它。


将来时:
I will be there tomorrow. = 我明天会在那里。
We will meet again soon. = 我们很快会再次见面。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go. = 如果我是你，我会去。
I wish I were there now. = 我希望我现在在那里。


被动语态:
This was made by hand. = 这是手工制作的。
That will be discussed later. = 那将稍后讨论。


疑问句:
What is this? = 这是什么？
Where are you going? = 你要去哪里？


否定句:
That isn't true. = 那不是真的。
I wasn't there. = 我不在那里。
