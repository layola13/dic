USAGE->

The museum carefully researches the provenance of each artwork. = 博物馆仔细研究每件艺术品的来源。

Researchers have established the provenance of many historical documents. = 研究人员已经确定了许多历史文件的来源。

过去完成时:

将来时:
Scholars will investigate the provenance of the newly discovered texts. = 学者们将调查新发现的文本的来源。
