nonphysician:/ˌnɑːnˈfɪzɪʃən/| n. |非医生；非医务人员|A person who is not a physician; someone without a medical degree or license to practice medicine.  Nonphysicians often play important roles in healthcare, such as medical assistants, nurses, or therapists.|例句：The study included both physicians and nonphysicians involved in patient care. 这项研究包括参与病人护理的医生和非医生。|例句：Nonphysicians are increasingly taking on expanded roles in healthcare delivery.  非医务人员在医疗保健服务中的角色越来越重要。|近义词：layperson, non-doctor, layperson, nonmedical personnel|反义词：physician, doctor, medical professional
