emphasize:/ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | vt. |强调，重视 |To give special importance or prominence to (something), to stress.  |The teacher emphasized the importance of regular study. 老师强调了定期学习的重要性。|stress, highlight, underscore |downplay, minimize, de-emphasize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emphasizes the need for patience. 他强调需要耐心。
She emphasizes the importance of teamwork. 她强调团队合作的重要性。

一般过去时:
The speaker emphasized the urgency of the situation. 演讲者强调了情况的紧急性。
The report emphasized the risks involved. 报告强调了所涉及的风险。

现在完成时:
They have emphasized the need for reform. 他们一直强调改革的必要性。
She has emphasized the positive aspects of the project. 她一直强调项目的积极方面。

过去完成时:
He had emphasized the dangers before the expedition. 他在探险之前就强调过危险性。
They had emphasized the importance of safety procedures. 他们此前已强调安全程序的重要性。

将来时:
She will emphasize the benefits of the new policy. 她将强调新政策的好处。
He will emphasize the importance of collaboration. 他将强调合作的重要性。

虚拟语气:
I wish he would emphasize the positive aspects more. 我希望他能更多地强调积极方面。
If I were you, I would emphasize my qualifications. 如果我是你，我会强调我的资历。

被动语态:
The importance of punctuality was emphasized. 守时的重要性得到了强调。
The risks involved were emphasized in the report. 报告中强调了所涉及的风险。

疑问句:
What points did the presentation emphasize? 演示强调了哪些要点？

否定句:
The report did not emphasize the negative consequences. 报告没有强调负面后果。
He did not emphasize the difficulties he faced. 他没有强调他面临的困难。
