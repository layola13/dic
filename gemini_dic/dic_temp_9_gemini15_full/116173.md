masculinity:/ˌmæskəˈlɪnəti/ | n. |男子气概；阳刚之气 |The quality of possessing the characteristics traditionally associated with men.  It encompasses physical, behavioral, and psychological traits, often varying across cultures and time periods.  The concept is complex and has been subject to significant debate and reinterpretation in recent years. |例句：He exudes masculinity. 他散发着男子气概。

例句：The film explores different representations of masculinity. 这部电影探讨了男子气概的不同表现形式。


近义词：manliness, virility, maleness | 反义词：femininity
