mismanages:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒɪz/ | vt. | 经营不善；管理不当 | To manage badly or inefficiently.  |例句：The company mismanaged its resources, leading to financial difficulties. 公司管理资源不善，导致财务困难。

例句：He mismanaged the project, resulting in delays and cost overruns. 他管理项目不力，导致延误和成本超支。


近义词： mishandle, misadminister, botch, blunder


反义词： manage well, administer effectively


词性：动词


USAGE->

The CEO mismanages the company's finances. = 首席执行官管理公司财务不善。
They mismanage their time consistently. = 他们总是时间管理不善。


一般过去时:
The previous manager mismanaged the team. = 上任经理管理团队不力。

过去完成时:
