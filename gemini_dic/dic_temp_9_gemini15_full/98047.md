what:/wɒt/|ˈhwɑːt/| pron.|什么，什么东西，多么|It is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What time is it?  现在几点了？


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished recently? = 她最近取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个地区正在建造什么？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you reading? = 你在读什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？

词性:代词
