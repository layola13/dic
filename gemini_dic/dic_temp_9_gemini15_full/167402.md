preutilizes:/ˌpriːˈjuːtɪlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |预先利用；提前使用 | To make use of something before it is normally used or available.  This word is relatively rare and often found in specialized contexts. It implies a proactive and anticipatory use, often for strategic or efficiency purposes. | Example: The company preutilizes its resources to minimize downtime during peak season.  公司在旺季提前利用资源以最大限度地减少停机时间。 The city preutilizes snow removal equipment to prepare for potential winter storms.  城市预先使用除雪设备以应对潜在的冬季暴风雪。 |近义词: utilize beforehand, employ in advance, pre-empt, anticipate |反义词: postpone, delay, defer |动词
