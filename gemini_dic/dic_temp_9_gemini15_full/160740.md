portulaca:/ˌpɔːrtuˈleɪkə/ | n. |马齿苋属植物，马齿苋 | Portulaca refers to a genus of succulent flowering plants in the family Portulacaceae.  They are known for their vibrant flowers and ability to thrive in hot, dry conditions.  Many species are cultivated as ornamentals, while others are used as food or traditional medicine. | Example:  Many gardeners appreciate the low-maintenance beauty of portulaca in their summer gardens.  许多园丁欣赏马齿苋在其夏季花园中的低维护美感。| 近义词: purslane (指马齿苋，一种常见的portulaca物种) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可根据语境选择) |  名词
