meannesses:/ˈminɪsnɪsiz/| n. |卑鄙行为；卑劣行径 |The plural form of "meanness," referring to multiple instances of unkind, ungenerous, or selfish behavior.  It implies a pattern of such actions rather than a single incident. |例句：His meannesses toward his employees finally led to a lawsuit.  他的卑鄙行为最终导致了诉讼。


例句：The company's meannesses were exposed in the media. 公司的卑劣行径被媒体曝光了。


近义词：vileness, baseness, wickedness, cruelty, pettiness, stinginess


反义词：kindness, generosity, benevolence, magnanimity


词性:名词
