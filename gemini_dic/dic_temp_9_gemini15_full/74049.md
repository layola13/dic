fosset:/ˈfɒsɪt/ | n. | 水龙头；（旧式）小喷泉 | A fosset is a spout or tap from which liquid, especially water, flows.  The term is now largely archaic, except in some regional dialects.  | Example: The old well had a rusty fosset.  这个旧井有一个生锈的水龙头。|近义词：tap, faucet |反义词：无
