pincoffin:/ˈpɪŋkɒfɪn/| n. |小棺材；微不足道的東西|A pincoffin is a small coffin, often used figuratively to refer to something insignificant or of little value.  It's a blend of "pin," suggesting smallness, and "coffin," representing something that contains something dead or worthless.|例句：His entire business plan was a pincoffin of poorly thought-out ideas. 他的整个商业计划就是一个由糟糕想法堆砌而成的微不足道的东西。|近义词：trifle, nothing, bagatelle|反义词：monumental, significant, important|名词
