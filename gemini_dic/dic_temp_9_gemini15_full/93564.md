imagining:/ɪˈmædʒɪnɪŋ/| v. |想象；设想 |The act of forming a mental image or concept of something not present to the senses.  It involves creating a picture in your mind, often based on experience, knowledge, or creativity. |例句：I was imagining a beautiful beach. 我正在想象一个美丽的沙滩。

例句：She spent hours imagining her future life. 她花了好几个小时想象她未来的生活。


近义词：contemplating, envisioning, visualizing, fantasizing |反义词：knowing, experiencing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am imagining a better future. = 我正在想象一个更美好的未来。
He imagines himself as a superhero. = 他想象自己是超级英雄。


一般过去时:
I imagined a different outcome. = 我想象了一个不同的结果。
They imagined what it would be like to live on Mars. = 他们想象在火星上生活会是什么样子。


现在完成时:
I have imagined this scenario many times. = 我已经多次想象过这种情况。
She has imagined a world without war. = 她已经想象过一个没有战争的世界。


过去完成时:
I had imagined that he would be taller. = 我曾想象他会更高。
They had imagined a much easier journey. = 他们曾想象过一段轻松得多的旅程。


将来时:
I will imagine a solution to the problem. = 我将会设想解决这个问题的方法。
We will be imagining our vacation all week. = 我们整个星期都会在想象我们的假期。


虚拟语气:
If I could imagine anything, I would imagine a world of peace. = 如果我能想象任何东西，我会想象一个和平的世界。
I wish I could imagine a way to solve this problem. = 我希望我能想出一个解决这个问题的方法。


被动语态:
A better future is imagined by many. = 许多人都在想象一个更美好的未来。(This usage is less common)
The scene was vividly imagined by the author. = 这个场景被作者生动地描绘出来。(被动语态更强调场景本身，而不是想象的动作)


疑问句:
What are you imagining? = 你在想象什么？
Can you imagine a life without technology? = 你能想象没有科技的生活吗？


否定句:
I cannot imagine doing that. = 我无法想象那样做。
She didn't imagine he would react so badly. = 她没料到他会反应这么激烈。


PHRASE->
imagine that = 想象一下
imagine if = 设想一下如果
just imagine = 想象一下
can't imagine = 无法想象
imagine doing sth = 想象做某事
