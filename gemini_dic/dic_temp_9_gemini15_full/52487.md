dieting:/ˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | v. | 节食；实行节食 | The act of restricting one's food intake in order to lose weight or for health reasons.  It often involves following a specific diet plan. |例句：She's dieting to fit into her wedding dress. 她为了穿上婚纱正在节食。

例句：He's been dieting for months and has lost a significant amount of weight. 他节食几个月了，体重减轻了很多。


近义词：weight loss, slimming,  reducing,  fasting


反义词：overeating, gorging, binge eating


词性：动名词 (gerund)  (衍生自动词diet)
