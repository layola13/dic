good-heartedly:/ˌɡʊdˈhɑːtɪdli/ | adv. | 好心地，慷慨地 |  Acting in a kind and generous manner; with kindness and good intentions.  |  She good-heartedly offered to help with the move. 她好心地主动帮忙搬家。  He good-heartedly donated a large sum to charity. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。 | kindly, generously, charitably | unkindly, selfishly, meanly | 副词
