noologist:/nuˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. | 沼泽学家 | A noologist is a scientist who studies bogs, mires, muskegs, or other types of wetlands.  Their research often focuses on the ecology, hydrology, and geochemistry of these environments. | Example: The noologist presented her findings on the unique plant life thriving in the peat bog.  沼泽学家展示了她关于在泥炭沼泽中繁盛的独特植物生命的发现。|近义词：wetland scientist, mire ecologist |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with scientists studying arid environments.
