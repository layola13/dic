epicardium:/ˌɛpɪˈkɑːrdiəm/| n. |心外膜|The epicardium is the outermost layer of the heart wall. It is a serous membrane that protects the heart. |例句：The epicardium plays a crucial role in protecting the heart muscle. 心外膜在保护心肌方面起着至关重要的作用。|例句：Damage to the epicardium can lead to serious heart problems. 心外膜损伤会导致严重的心脏问题。|近义词：visceral pericardium|反义词：myocardium, endocardium|名词
