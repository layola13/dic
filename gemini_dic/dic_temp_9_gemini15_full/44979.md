crystallises: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 结晶；明确表达；(观点等)变得清晰 | To form crystals; to become clear or definite;  to cause (something) to become clear or definite. |

例句：

The solution crystallises when cooled. =  溶液冷却后会结晶。

His ideas gradually crystallised into a coherent theory. = 他的想法逐渐形成一个连贯的理论。


近义词：solidify, coalesce, clarify, define

反义词：amorphous, vague, unclear
