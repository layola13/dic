hafting:/ˈhæftɪŋ/| n. |柄的安装；装柄；镶嵌|The process of attaching a handle or shaft to a tool or weapon.  This involves securing the haft (handle) firmly to the head or blade.|例句：The hafting of the axe was crucial to its effectiveness.  斧头的装柄对其效用至关重要。|近义词：fitting, mounting, attaching|反义词：detaching, removing|名词
