doze-away: /doʊz əˈweɪ/ | v. | 昏昏欲睡地度过；打瞌睡睡着 | To fall asleep gradually and lightly; to spend time sleeping lightly. |例句：I dozed away the afternoon in a comfortable chair. 我在舒适的椅子上昏昏欲睡地度过了下午。
He dozed away during the boring lecture. 他在枯燥的演讲中打瞌睡睡着了。|近义词：nod off, drift off, slumber |反义词：stay awake, be alert
