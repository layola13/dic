revivement:/rɪˈvaɪvmənt/ | n. | 复兴；复活；苏醒 | The revival of an interest in classical music.  古典音乐兴趣的复兴。 | 近义词：renewal, resurgence, rebirth | 反义词：decline, decay, extinction |


英文解析：The act or process of reviving something, or the state of being revived.  It can refer to the restoration of something to life, activity, or vigor, or a renewed interest or popularity in something.


例句：

The city experienced a cultural revival in the 1990s.  20世纪90年代，这座城市经历了一次文化复兴。

The revival of the old tradition brought the community closer.  古老传统的复兴使社区更加紧密。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

By the time the new manager arrived, the company had already begun its revival. 当新经理到达时，公司已经开始复兴了。

Before the new policy was implemented, the industry had experienced a long period of decline, and the revival had just begun. 新政策实施之前，该行业经历了长期的衰退，复苏才刚刚开始。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
