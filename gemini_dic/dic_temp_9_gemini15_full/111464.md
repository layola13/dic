lo-tech:/ˈloʊtɛk/ | adj. |低科技的，技术含量低的 | Low-tech refers to technology that is simple, inexpensive, and often uses older or less advanced methods.  It contrasts with high-tech, which refers to advanced and sophisticated technology.  Low-tech solutions are often favored for their reliability, ease of maintenance, and affordability. | Example:  The workshop used lo-tech methods to repair the antique clock.  车间用低科技手段修理了这台古董钟。 |近义词：simple, basic, rudimentary, unsophisticated |反义词：high-tech, advanced, sophisticated, cutting-edge |词性：形容词
