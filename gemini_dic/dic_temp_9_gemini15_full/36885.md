colorer's: /ˈkʌlərz/ | n. | 着色者（的）；染色者（的）|  A person who colors or dyes something (often used in the possessive case).  It refers to someone whose profession or hobby involves adding color to materials.  |例句：The colorer's tools were meticulously organized. = 着色者的工具被细致地整理好。 The colorer's palette was a vibrant array of hues. = 着色者的调色板是一系列鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：dyer's, tinter's, painter's (in some contexts)|反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be someone who removes color, like a bleacher. |n.
