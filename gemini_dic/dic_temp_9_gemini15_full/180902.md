rei: /raɪ/ | n. | 铃，雷声；(日本) 霊 (rei) |  A Japanese word meaning "spirit" or "soul," often used in compound words.  It can also refer to the sound of a bell or thunder. | 例句：The temple bells' *rei* echoed through the valley. 寺庙的铃声回荡在山谷中。  The ancient text described the *rei* of the mountain. 古代文本描述了山中的精灵。 | 近义词： (depending on context) spirit, soul, ghost, thunder, peal | 反义词：(depending on context) body, matter, silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bell rings. = 铃响了。
The thunder reiss. = 雷声隆隆。

一般过去时:
The bell rang. = 铃响了。
The thunder roared. = 雷声轰鸣。


现在完成时:
The bell has rung. = 铃响过了。
The thunder has roared. = 雷声已经轰鸣过。


过去完成时:
The bell had rung before I arrived. = 我到达之前铃已经响了。
The thunder had roared before the storm broke. =暴风雨来临之前雷声已经轰鸣过。


将来时:
The bell will ring at noon. = 铃声将在中午响起。
The thunder will roar later. = 雷声稍后会轰鸣。


虚拟语气:
If the bell rang, we would know. = 如果铃响了，我们就知道了。
If the thunder roared, we would seek shelter. = 如果雷声隆隆，我们就应该找个庇护所。


被动语态:
The bell is rung every hour. = 铃声每小时都会响起。
The thunder is rarely heard in this area. = 这个地区很少听到雷声。


疑问句:
Does the bell ring often? = 铃经常响吗？
Did the thunder roar last night? = 昨晚雷声隆隆吗？


否定句:
The bell didn't ring. = 铃没响。
The thunder didn't roar. = 雷声没轰鸣。
