schemist:/ˈkɛmɪst/| n. |化学家|A chemist is a scientist who specializes in chemistry.  Chemists study the composition, structure, properties, and reactions of matter.  They work in various fields, including research, development, and manufacturing. |例句：My uncle is a chemist working for a pharmaceutical company.  我的叔叔是一名在制药公司工作的化学家。|例句：The chemist carefully measured the chemicals before mixing them. 这名化学家在混合化学品之前仔细测量了它们。|近义词：chemical scientist,  research chemist|反义词：None.
