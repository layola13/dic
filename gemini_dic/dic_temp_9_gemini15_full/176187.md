re-annex:/ˌriːˈæneks/| vt.|重新兼并，重新吞并|To annex again; to take control of (territory) a second time.|The country attempted to re-annex the lost province.  这个国家试图重新兼并失去的省份。|The government re-annexed the territory after a period of independence. 政府在一段时间的独立之后重新兼并了该领土。|annex, reclaim|cede, relinquish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-annex territories through military force. = 他们通过军事力量重新兼并领土。
The empire re-annexes conquered lands. = 这个帝国重新兼并被征服的土地。


一般过去时:
The kingdom re-annexed the disputed region. = 王国重新兼并了有争议的地区。
