pitter:/ˈpɪtər/| v.|滴答声，轻拍声|To make a gentle, repeated tapping sound, like the sound of rain or small objects hitting a surface.|例句：The rain pittered against the windowpanes. 雨滴轻拍着窗玻璃。 The children's feet pittered down the hallway. 孩子们的脚步声轻轻地回荡在走廊里。|近义词：pat, tap, patter|反义词：boom, clang


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rain pitters against the window. = 雨滴轻拍着窗户。
The bird pitters its tiny feet on the branch. = 小鸟的细小脚爪轻轻地落在树枝上。

一般过去时:
The rain pittered gently all night. = 雨整夜轻轻地下着。
He pittered nervously with his fingers on the table. = 他紧张地用手指轻轻地敲打着桌子。

现在完成时:
The rain has pittered on and off all day. = 雨今天断断续续地下着。
She has pittered away at her knitting for hours. = 她已经连续几个小时在织毛衣了。


过去完成时:
The rain had pittered for hours before it finally stopped. = 雨下了好几个小时才停。
The children had pittered excitedly through the house before bed. = 孩子们睡前兴奋地在房子里跑来跑去。

将来时:
The rain will pitter against the roof tonight. = 今晚雨会打在屋顶上。
I think the little bird will pitter around the feeder this morning. = 我想今天早上小鸟会在喂食器周围跳来跳去。

虚拟语气:
If it pittered softly, we might not notice it. = 如果雨声很轻，我们可能不会注意到。
I wish the rain would pitter instead of pouring. = 我希望雨能下小点，而不是倾盆大雨。

被动语态:
(Pitter is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.)


疑问句:
Does the rain pitter outside? = 外面在下雨吗？
Do you hear that pittering sound? = 你听到那个轻微的拍打声了吗？

否定句:
The rain didn't pitter; it poured. = 雨不是下得淅淅沥沥的，而是倾盆而下。
The small bird didn't pitter on the branch; it flew away. = 小鸟没有在树枝上跳动；它飞走了。


PHRASE->
Pitter-patter:  轻柔的滴答声或脚步声 (This is the most common phrase with "pitter".)
Pitter patter of rain: 雨的轻柔滴答声
Pitter patter of little feet: 小脚丫轻快的脚步声
The pitter-patter of rain on the roof: 雨滴轻拍屋顶的声音
A pitter-patter of footsteps: 一阵轻快的脚步声
