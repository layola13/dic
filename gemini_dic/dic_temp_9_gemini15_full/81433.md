grater:/ˈɡreɪtər/| n.|擦菜板，磨碎器|A kitchen utensil used for grating food, such as cheese or vegetables.  It typically has a surface with many small holes or projections.|例句：I need a grater to shred the cheese for the pizza. 我需要一个擦菜板来磨碎比萨上的奶酪。

例句：She used a fine grater to zest the lemons. 她用细齿擦菜板擦柠檬皮。


近义词：shredder, rasp|反义词：None|词性:名词
