crankbird:/kræŋkˌbɜːrd/| n. |曲柄鸟，（俚语）古怪的人|A crankbird is a slang term for an eccentric or peculiar person; someone considered odd or strange.  The term likely combines "crank," meaning an eccentric person, with "bird," a common slang term for a person.|例句：He's a bit of a crankbird, always talking about conspiracy theories. 他有点古怪，总是谈论阴谋论。


He's such a crankbird, he believes the earth is flat. 他真是个怪人，他相信地球是平的。|近义词：eccentric, oddball, weirdo, nut, character|反义词：normal, conventional, ordinary
