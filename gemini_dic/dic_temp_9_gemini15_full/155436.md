phenanthroline:/fɪˈnænθrəliːn/| n. |菲啰啉|A heterocyclic organic compound with the formula C₁₂H₈N₂. It is a bidentate ligand that forms stable complexes with many metal ions, often used in analytical chemistry and coordination chemistry.  It exists in several isomeric forms, the most common being 1,10-phenanthroline.|Example: 1,10-Phenanthroline is frequently used as a chelating agent in analytical chemistry to determine the concentration of certain metal ions.  菲啰啉常被用作分析化学中的螯合剂，以测定某些金属离子的浓度。|近义词:chelate, ligand|反义词:None readily available.
