precorrects:/ˌpriːkəˈrɛkts/ | v. | 预先校正，预先修正 | To correct something before it is used or published.  This is typically done to identify and fix errors before they become more difficult or costly to address. | 例句：The editor precorrects all manuscripts before sending them to the typesetter. 编辑在将所有手稿发送给排版员之前会先进行校对。

例句：The software precorrects spelling errors as you type.  该软件会在你打字时预先校正拼写错误。


近义词：proofread, edit, revise, amend | 反义词：miscorrect, err


词性:动词
