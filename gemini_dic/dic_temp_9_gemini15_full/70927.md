firebrands:/ˈfaɪərˌbrændz/| n.|煽动者；捣乱分子；煽动性言论|A firebrand is a person who is very enthusiastic and passionate about a particular cause, often inciting strong feelings or actions in others.  They may be seen as troublemakers or agitators, especially if their methods are controversial or extreme.|例句：He was a firebrand in the student protests, always ready to lead a demonstration. 他是学生抗议中的煽动者，总是准备领导示威活动。

例句：The politician's fiery speeches made him a notorious firebrand.  这位政治家的煽动性演讲使他成为臭名昭著的煽动者。|近义词：agitator, troublemaker, incendiary, rabble-rouser|反义词：peacemaker, conciliator, appeaser|名词
