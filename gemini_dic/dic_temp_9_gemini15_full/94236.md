imperilled:/ɪmˈpɛrɪld/ | adj. | 处于危险中的；濒临灭绝的 | Exposed to danger; threatened with destruction or ruin.  |The imperilled species is on the brink of extinction.  濒危物种濒临灭绝。 | endangered, threatened, jeopardized | safe, secure, protected | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The environment is imperilled by pollution. = 环境受到污染的威胁。
The fragile ecosystem is imperilled by climate change. = 脆弱的生态系统受到气候变化的威胁。


一般过去时:
The company was imperilled by the economic downturn. = 公司因经济衰退而陷入困境。
The ancient city was imperilled by the rising sea levels. = 古城受到海平面上升的威胁。


现在完成时:
The rainforest has been imperilled by deforestation. = 雨林因森林砍伐而受到威胁。
The cultural heritage has been imperilled by neglect. = 文化遗产因疏忽而受到威胁。


过去完成时:
The project had been imperilled by a lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而陷入困境。
The negotiations had been imperilled by mistrust between the parties. = 双方之间的不信任使谈判陷入困境。


将来时:
The coastal communities will be imperilled by the rising tides. = 沿海社区将受到涨潮的威胁。
The economy will be imperilled by the trade war. = 经济将受到贸易战的威胁。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't act, the nation's economy would be imperilled. = 如果政府不采取行动，国家经济将面临危险。
If we continued polluting, the planet would be imperilled.  = 如果我们继续污染，地球将面临危险。


被动语态:
The plan was imperilled by unforeseen circumstances. = 该计划因不可预见的情况而陷入困境。
The peace talks were imperilled by the outbreak of violence. = 和谈因暴力爆发而陷入困境。


疑问句:
Is the project imperilled by the budget cuts? = 项目会因为预算削减而受到威胁吗？
Are the endangered animals imperilled by habitat loss? = 濒危动物会因为栖息地丧失而受到威胁吗？


否定句:
The situation is not imperilled, despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，但形势并未危及。
The agreement was not imperilled by the disagreements. = 协议并未因分歧而受损。
