cooperated:/koʊˈɑːpəreɪtɪd/| v.|合作，协作| cooperated is the past tense and past participle of the verb "cooperate". It describes an action that happened in the past where two or more people or groups worked together to achieve a common goal. |例句：The two companies cooperated on a new product. 两家公司合作开发了一种新产品。The team cooperated effectively to win the game. 团队有效地合作赢得了比赛。|近义词：collaborated, collaborated, worked together|反义词：opposed, resisted
