misfame:/ˈmɪsfeɪm/| n.|坏名声，恶名 | Ill repute; bad reputation; notoriety.  It refers to the state of being known for something bad or dishonorable. |例句：The misfame of the company spread quickly after the scandal.  这家公司丑闻爆发后，恶名迅速传开。

例句：His misfame preceded him; everyone knew of his dishonest dealings. 他的恶名先于他传到了；每个人都知道他不诚实的交易。
