coolish:/ˈkuːlɪʃ/| adj.| 凉爽的，有点酷的 | Somewhat cool; moderately cool.  It suggests a temperature that is slightly below average or comfortable, but not extremely cold. It can also imply a slightly cool or detached attitude or demeanor. |例句：The evening was coolish, so I wore a light jacket.  傍晚有点凉，所以我穿了一件轻便的夹克。


The weather is coolish today, perfect for a walk in the park.今天天气凉爽，很适合在公园散步。|近义词：chilly, cool, refreshing |反义词：hot, warm, sweltering
