mosey:/ˈmoʊzi/| v.|慢吞吞地走，闲逛|To walk at a slow, leisurely pace; to stroll.|例句：Let's mosey on down to the beach. 让我们慢悠悠地走到海滩去。例句：He moseyed into the bar and ordered a drink. 他慢吞吞地走进酒吧，点了一杯酒。|近义词：amble, stroll, saunter, meander|反义词：hurry, rush, sprint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moseys to the store every morning. = 他每天早上慢悠悠地去商店。
They mosey around the park after lunch. = 他们午饭后在公园里闲逛。


一般过去时:
She moseyed home after work. = 她下班后慢悠悠地回家了。
We moseyed through the countryside. = 我们在乡下慢悠悠地走着。


现在完成时:
I have moseyed around this town for hours. = 我在这个镇上闲逛了好几个小时了。
They have moseyed along the riverbank. = 他们沿着河岸慢悠悠地走了一路。


过去完成时:
He had moseyed into town before the shops opened. = 商店开门之前，他已经慢悠悠地进了镇子。
We had moseyed through the forest before it started to rain. = 下雨之前，我们已经在森林里闲逛了一阵子。


将来时:
I will mosey over to your place later. = 我晚点会慢慢地去你家。
They will mosey down to the beach tomorrow. = 他们明天会慢悠悠地去海滩。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would mosey through the museum. = 如果我有时间，我会慢慢地逛逛博物馆。
I wish I could mosey along the beach all day. = 我真希望我能一整天都慢悠悠地在海滩上走。


被动语态: (Mosey is rarely used in the passive voice)
The tourists were seen moseying through the market.  = 游客们被看到在市场上闲逛。 (This emphasizes the action of moseying, not the being moseyed)


疑问句:
Will you mosey along with me? = 你会和我一起慢慢走吗？
Did they mosey to the park this morning? = 他们今天早上慢悠悠地去公园了吗？


否定句:
He didn't mosey; he ran. = 他没有慢悠悠地走；他是跑的。
They didn't mosey through the town; they hurried. = 他们没有在镇上闲逛；他们匆匆忙忙的。


PHRASE->
mosey along: 慢慢走
mosey on: 继续走
mosey over: 走过去
mosey around: 闲逛
mosey down: 沿着...走下去
