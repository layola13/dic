corollate:/ˈkɒrəleɪt/| /ˈkɔːrəleɪt/| vt.|使相互关联；互相配合|To bring into a mutual relationship; to correlate.|The study correlated smoking with lung cancer. 这项研究将吸烟与肺癌联系起来。|The results of the two tests correlated well. 两次测试的结果非常吻合。|associate, connect, link|dissociate, disconnect, separate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists correlate data from different sources.  = 科学家们将来自不同来源的数据关联起来。
The two events correlate strongly. = 这两件事高度相关。


一般过去时:
Researchers correlated the disease with environmental factors. = 研究人员将这种疾病与环境因素联系起来。
He correlated his success with hard work. = 他将他的成功归功于努力工作。


现在完成时:
Studies have correlated exposure to sunlight with an increased risk of skin cancer. = 研究表明，日晒与皮肤癌风险增加之间存在关联。
They have correlated the economic downturn with rising unemployment. = 他们将经济衰退与失业率上升联系起来。


过去完成时:
By the end of the experiment, we had correlated all the data. = 到实验结束时，我们已经将所有数据关联起来。
Scientists had correlated similar findings in previous studies. = 科学家们已经在之前的研究中发现了类似的关联。


将来时:
We will correlate the findings with existing research. = 我们将把研究结果与现有研究联系起来。
They will correlate the test scores with student performance. = 他们将考试成绩与学生的学习成绩联系起来。


虚拟语气:
If we correlated the variables correctly, the results would be more meaningful. = 如果我们正确地关联了这些变量，结果将会更有意义。
I wish we could correlate these two seemingly unrelated events. = 我希望我们能够将这两件看似无关的事件联系起来。


被动语态:
The data were correlated using advanced statistical techniques. = 使用先进的统计技术对数据进行了关联分析。
The two factors are correlated in this study. = 在这项研究中，这两个因素是相关的。


疑问句:
Do these two factors correlate? = 这两个因素相关吗？
How do you correlate these different findings? = 你如何将这些不同的发现关联起来？


否定句:
The two variables did not correlate significantly. = 这两个变量没有显著的相关性。
The study failed to correlate diet with longevity. = 这项研究未能将饮食与寿命联系起来。
