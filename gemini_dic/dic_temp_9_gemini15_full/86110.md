heartbreak:/ˈhɑːrtbreɪk/ | /ˈhɑːrtbreɪk/| n. |心碎；悲痛；令人心碎的事 |Heartbreak is an overwhelming feeling of sadness and loss, typically caused by the end of a romantic relationship or other significant loss.  It can manifest physically and emotionally, leaving a person feeling devastated and lost. |例句：Her heartbreak was evident in her tear-filled eyes.  她的泪眼汪汪，显露出了她的心碎。

例句：The news of his death caused widespread heartbreak. 他去世的消息引起了广泛的悲痛。|近义词：anguish, sorrow, grief, despair |反义词：joy, happiness, elation | 名词
