icono:/aɪˈkɒnəʊ/ | n. |图标，偶像，标志 | An icono is a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something.  It can refer to a small picture or symbol on a computer screen, or a person who is widely admired and imitated. |例句：The company logo is a powerful icono of success. 这家公司的标志是成功的有力象征。

例句：The singer became an icono for a generation.  这位歌手成为了一代人的偶像。


近义词：symbol, emblem, icon, idol, figurehead | 反义词：antithesis, opposite


词性:名词
