paralysingly: /ˈpærəlaɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人瘫痪地；使人麻痹地；极其 | Extremely; to such an extent as to paralyze.  It describes something that is so overwhelming or shocking that it renders someone immobile or unable to function normally. |


例句：

The news was paralysingly shocking. = 这个消息令人震惊到麻木。

The heat was paralysingly intense. = 炙热难耐，令人瘫痪。


近义词：overwhelmingly, incredibly, intensely, devastatingly

反义词：slightly, mildly, barely


词性：副词
