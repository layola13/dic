reanimations:/ˌriːænɪˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |复生；复活；复苏；|The process of reviving something, especially a dead person or body, or of bringing something back to life or activity.  It often implies a restoration to a previous state. |例句：The scientist's research focused on the reanimations of extinct species. 科学家的研究集中在已灭绝物种的复活上。


例句：The novel depicts the gruesome reanimations performed by the mad doctor. 这部小说描绘了疯狂医生进行的可怕的复生实验。


近义词：resurrections, revivals, restorations


反义词：deaths, destructions, terminations


词性：名词
