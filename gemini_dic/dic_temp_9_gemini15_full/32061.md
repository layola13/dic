chert:/tʃɜːrt/| n. |燧石，硅质岩|A hard, sedimentary rock composed of microcrystalline quartz.  It is often found in nodules or layers within other sedimentary rocks, and is valued for its hardness and ability to create sharp edges, making it useful for tools in prehistoric times.|Example: The ancient arrowheads were meticulously crafted from chert.|近义词：flint, hornstone|反义词：无
