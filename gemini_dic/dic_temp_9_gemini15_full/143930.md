outpursue:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuː/| v.|超过，胜过，竭力追求|To pursue with greater effort or success than another; surpass in pursuit.|The determined athlete outpursued his rivals, securing the gold medal.  这位意志坚定的运动员超过了他的对手，获得了金牌。|surpass, excel, outstrip|fail, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outpursues his competitors in the market. 他在市场上胜过他的竞争对手。
They outpursue their goals with diligence. 他们勤奋地追求他们的目标并超越。


一般过去时:
She outpursued her dreams and became a successful writer. 她追逐梦想并成为一名成功的作家（并超越了其他人）。
The team outpursued their opponents in the final game. 该队在决赛中胜过了对手。


现在完成时:
He has outpursued all expectations in his career. 他在事业上已经超越了所有人的期望。
They have outpursued their rivals in the technology race. 他们在科技竞赛中已经超过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
She had outpursued her initial goals by the time she reached 30. 到30岁时，她已经超越了她最初的目标。
The company had outpursued its competitors in market share before the merger. 在合并之前，该公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。


将来时:
He will outpursue his rivals in the upcoming election. 他将在即将到来的选举中胜过他的对手。
They will outpursue their current record in the next season. 他们在下个赛季将超越他们目前的记录。


虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he would outpursue his competitors. 如果他更努力工作，他就能胜过他的竞争对手。
If she had been more persistent, she would have outpursued her goals. 如果她更坚持，她本可以实现她的目标并超越。


被动语态:
His achievements were outpursued by few.  很少有人能超越他的成就。
The project's success was outpursued by the team's dedication.  项目的成功得益于团队的奉献（团队超越了挑战）。


疑问句:
Can he outpursue the current champion? 他能胜过现任冠军吗？
Will they outpursue their previous best score? 他们会超越他们之前的最好成绩吗？


否定句:
He did not outpursue his rival in the race. 他在比赛中没有胜过他的对手。
They failed to outpursue their ambitious goals. 他们未能实现他们雄心勃勃的目标（未能超越）。
