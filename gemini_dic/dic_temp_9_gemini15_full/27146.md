call-backs:/ˈkɔːlˌbæks/ | n. |回调函数；回电；再次邀请 |A call-back is a function that is executed after another function has completed its execution, often used in asynchronous programming or event handling.  It can also refer to a return phone call or a second invitation. |例句：The system uses callbacks to update the user interface.  这个系统使用回调函数来更新用户界面。|例句：I received a callback from the company regarding the job application. 我收到了公司关于求职申请的回电。|近义词：return call, follow-up call, re-invitation|反义词：N/A
