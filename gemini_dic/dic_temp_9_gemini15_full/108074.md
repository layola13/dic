laterals:/ˈlætərəlz/| n.|边音，侧音|A lateral is a consonant sound produced by placing the tongue against one side of the mouth, allowing air to escape over the other side.|例句：English has several lateral consonants, such as /l/.  英语有几个边音辅音，例如/l/。|近义词：lateral consonant|反义词:  None specifically, but contrasting sounds like stops or fricatives.
