hermetic:/hɜːˈmɛtɪk/ | /hərˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. | 密封的；隐秘的；炼金术的；隔绝的 |  relating to or denoting the occult philosophy and practices of Hermes Trismegistus, traditionally associated with alchemy and astrology; sealed and airtight, preventing the passage of air or gas. |例句：The jar was hermetically sealed to preserve the contents.  这个罐子被密封起来以保存里面的东西。

The laboratory maintained a hermetic environment to prevent contamination. 实验室保持一个密闭的环境以防止污染。

近义词：airtight, sealed, isolated, occult, esoteric | 反义词：open, permeable, accessible, public
