primp:/prɪmp/| vt. vi.|打扮；梳妆打扮|To groom or dress oneself with elaborate care.  Primping often involves paying close attention to detail and making oneself look attractive.|She spent an hour primping before the party. 她花了1个小时在派对前打扮自己。 He primped his hair in the mirror. 他对着镜子梳理头发。|dress up, preen, groom|neglect, ignore|动词
