caiman:/ˈkeɪmən/| n. |凯门鳄|A caiman is a crocodilian reptile of the family Alligatoridae, closely related to alligators but smaller and with a narrower snout.|例句：The caiman lurked in the murky water.  这条凯门鳄潜伏在浑浊的水中。|例句：We saw several caimans basking in the sun on the riverbank. 我们看到几条凯门鳄在河岸上晒太阳。|近义词：alligator, crocodile|反义词:  无
