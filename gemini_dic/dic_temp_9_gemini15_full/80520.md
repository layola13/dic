good-time: /ˈɡʊd taɪm/ | adj. n. | 好时光的；乐观的；快乐的；寻乐的 |  A person or thing that is enjoyable and fun; a period of enjoyment.  |


例句：

He's a real good-time guy. = 他是个非常快乐的人。

She had a good time at the party. = 她在派对上玩得很开心。


近义词：fun-loving, enjoyable, merry, cheerful

反义词：gloomy, depressing, miserable, somber


词性：形容词，名词

词源:

The term "good time" is a straightforward combination of the adjective "good" and the noun "time."  It evolved naturally from the common usage of describing enjoyable periods as "good times." There isn't a specific historical etymology beyond this simple combination of words.
