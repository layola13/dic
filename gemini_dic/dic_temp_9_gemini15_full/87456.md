hesperides:/ˈhɛspəˌriːdiːz/| n.|赫斯珀里得斯（神话中守护金苹果的仙女）|/ˈhɛspəriːdiːz/|  |The Hesperides were nymphs in Greek mythology who guarded the golden apples in the Garden of the Hesperides.  They are often depicted as sisters, daughters of Atlas and Hesperis.  The exact number of Hesperides varies in different accounts.|例句：The hero Heracles had to retrieve the golden apples from the Hesperides as one of his twelve labors.  大力神赫拉克勒斯必须从赫斯珀里得斯那里取回金苹果作为他的十二项功绩之一。|近义词：nymphs, goddesses|反义词：N/A
