pan-Anglican:/ˌpænænˈɡlɪkən/| adj.|泛圣公会(的)，全圣公会(的)|Relating to or including all Anglican churches worldwide.  The term signifies a global perspective encompassing the Anglican Communion's diverse national and regional churches.|例句：The Pan-Anglican Congress brings together leaders from across the Anglican Communion. 泛圣公会大会汇聚了来自圣公会团契各地的领导人。

The Pan-Anglican Theological Consultation seeks to foster dialogue and unity among Anglican theologians worldwide. 泛圣公会神学研讨会旨在促进世界各地圣公会神学家之间的对话与团结。|近义词：ecumenical (in a broader sense)|反义词：denominational (referring to a single denomination within Anglicanism)|形容词
