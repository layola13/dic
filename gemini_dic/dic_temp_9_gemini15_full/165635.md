premise:/ˈprɛmɪs/| n. |前提，假设，论据 |A premise is a statement or an idea that forms the basis of a reasoning.  It's something assumed to be true before further reasoning is applied.  |Example: The premise of his argument was that all men are created equal.  他的论点的前提是人人生而平等。


Example: The premise of the story is that a young woman discovers a hidden treasure. 这故事的前提是一个年轻女子发现了一笔隐藏的财富。|近义词：assumption, supposition, hypothesis |反义词：conclusion, result, consequence |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The premise of the argument is clear. = 论点的论据是清楚的。
His premise supports his conclusion. = 他的前提支撑了他的结论。

一般过去时:
The premise of the debate was flawed. = 辩论的前提是有缺陷的。
Their premise proved to be incorrect. = 他们的前提被证明是错误的。

现在完成时:
The researchers have established a strong premise for their study. = 研究人员已经为他们的研究确立了一个坚实的前提。
We have accepted their premise as a working hypothesis. = 我们已经接受他们的前提作为一个工作假设。

过去完成时:
They had already accepted the premise before starting the experiment. = 他们在开始实验之前就已经接受了这个前提。
Scientists had questioned the premise of the theory for years. = 科学家们多年来一直质疑该理论的前提。

将来时:
The new evidence will challenge the premise of the investigation. = 新证据将挑战调查的前提。
Future research will likely confirm the premise of this study. = 未来的研究可能会证实这项研究的前提。

虚拟语气:
If the premise were true, the conclusion would follow. = 如果前提为真，结论就成立。
If the premise had been different, the outcome might have been different. = 如果前提不同，结果可能就不同了。

被动语态:
The premise was questioned by many experts. = 这个前提受到了许多专家的质疑。
The premise is being evaluated by the committee. = 这个前提正在由委员会进行评估。

疑问句:
What is the premise of your argument? = 你论证的前提是什么？
What is the underlying premise of this theory? = 这一理论的根本前提是什么？

否定句:
The premise of his argument is not valid. = 他论证的前提是无效的。
The study's premise is not supported by the data. = 这项研究的前提得不到数据的支持。


PHRASE->

false premise = 错误的前提
