ledging:/ˈlɛdʒɪŋ/| n.|搁置；登记；记录；支撑；突出部分|The act of placing something on a ledge or shelf; the process of recording or entering information into a ledger; a projecting part or edge.|例句：The careful ledgering of all transactions ensured accurate financial statements.  仔细记录所有交易确保了财务报表准确无误。

例句：The window ledge was cluttered with plants and knick-knacks. 窗台上摆满了植物和小摆设。


近义词：recording, registering, entry, projection, shelf|反义词：removal, erasure, recess
