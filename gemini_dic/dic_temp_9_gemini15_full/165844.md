preoccurred:/ˌpriːəˈkɜːrd/| v.|预先发生，先于…发生 | to happen before something else | The earthquake preoccurred the tsunami.  地震先于海啸发生。 The symptoms preoccurred the diagnosis. 症状先于诊断出现。|近义词：precede,antedate|反义词：follow, succeed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It preoccurs rarely. = 它很少预先发生。
Something preoccurs before the main event. = 在主要事件之前会发生一些事情。


一般过去时:
The problem preoccurred the solution. = 问题先于解决方案出现。
The rain preoccurred the picnic. = 下雨先于野餐。


现在完成时:
The event has preoccurred many times. = 此事件已经预先发生过很多次。
This has preoccurred several times in the past. = 这在过去已经发生过几次了。


过去完成时:
The accident had preoccurred the investigation. = 事故先于调查发生。
He had preoccurred his arrival by sending a telegram. 他通过发电报提前告知了他的到达。


将来时:
The meeting will preoccur the dinner. = 会议将在晚宴之前举行。
The preparations will preoccur the launch. = 准备工作将在发布之前完成。


虚拟语气:
If the warning had preoccurred the storm, damage would have been less. = 如果警告先于暴风雨发出，损失就会减少。
If it had preoccurred, we would have been better prepared. = 如果它事先发生过，我们就会有更好的准备。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Did the symptoms preoccur the illness? = 症状是否先于疾病出现？
What preoccurred the accident? = 事故之前发生了什么？


否定句:
