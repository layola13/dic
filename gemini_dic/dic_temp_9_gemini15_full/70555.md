filings:/ˈfaɪlɪŋz/| n.| 文件提交；备案；提交的文件 | Documents submitted to an authority, especially for official record.  The act of submitting these documents. |例句：The company's filings with the SEC revealed significant financial losses. 公司向美国证券交易委员会提交的文件显示出巨大的经济损失。

The court requires all necessary filings before the trial begins. 法院要求在审判开始前提交所有必要的文件。


近义词：submissions, registrations, applications |反义词：rejections, withdrawals| 词性：名词
