outvies:/aʊtˈvaɪz/| vt.|胜过，超过|To surpass or exceed in excellence, achievement, or ability.|例句：Her talent outvies that of her contemporaries.  她的才能胜过她的同时代人。|例句：His determination outvies his fear. 他的决心战胜了他的恐惧。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, exceed|反义词：underperform, be inferior to|动词
