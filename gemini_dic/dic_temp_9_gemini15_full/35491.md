coactor:/ˈkoʊæktər/| n.|合作者，共同行为者|A coactor is someone who acts or works together with others, often in a collaborative effort.  It implies a shared responsibility and joint action.|Example: The two scientists were coactors on the groundbreaking research project.  两位科学家是这项开创性研究项目的合作者。|近义词：collaborator, partner, accomplice|反义词：antagonist, opponent|名词
