eagers:/ˈiːɡərz/| n.|渴望的；热切的 | Enthusiastic and eager.  People who are eager are keen and enthusiastic about something. They are impatient to do it or have it. |例句：The children were eager to open their presents.孩子们渴望打开他们的礼物。

例句：She was eager for news of her son. 她渴望得到她儿子的消息。


近义词：keen, enthusiastic, ardent, fervent, zealous

反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, reluctant
