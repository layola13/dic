preinclusions: /ˌpriːɪnˈkluːʒənz/ | n. | 预先包含物；预先内含物 |  Substances or elements that are present within a material before any processing or treatment.  Preinclusions are often impurities or defects that exist in the original material.  They can significantly affect the properties and performance of the final product.  |  例句：The analysis revealed several preinclusions in the steel sample, affecting its tensile strength.  分析显示钢样中存在几处预先包含物，影响了它的抗拉强度。


The presence of numerous preinclusions compromised the integrity of the casting.  大量预先包含物的存在损害了铸件的完整性。| 近义词：impurities, contaminants, defects | 反义词：additions, enhancements | 词性: noun
