procuration:/ˌprɒkjʊˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 代理；授权；委托；代办 | The act of procuring or obtaining something; the authorization to act on someone else's behalf.  It often refers to a legal document granting such authority. | Example: The procuration allowed the lawyer to manage her client's affairs while she was abroad.  代理权允许律师在她出国期间管理她客户的事务。 |近义词：authorization, mandate, delegation, commission |反义词：prohibition, restriction | 名词
