conductorless: /kənˈdʌktərləs/ | adj. | 无导体的；无指挥的 |  Lacking a conductor; without a conductor.  This adjective describes something that operates or functions without a conductor, either in the context of electricity (lacking a physical conductor) or music (lacking a musical director).

例句：

The new technology uses conductorless energy transmission. = 新技术使用无导体能量传输。

The orchestra performed a conductorless rendition of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony. = 管弦乐队演奏了贝多芬第五交响曲的无指挥版本。


近义词：unconducted (in a musical context), wireless (in an electrical context)

反义词：conducted


词性:形容词
