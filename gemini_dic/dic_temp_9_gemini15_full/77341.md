gaucho-pants:/ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ pænts/| n.|高乔裤，宽松马裤|Wide-legged trousers, typically made of leather or heavy fabric, traditionally worn by gauchos (South American cowboys).  They are characterized by their loose fit, often featuring stirrups or similar attachments for riding.|例句：The gaucho pants were perfect for riding horses across the pampas.  高乔裤非常适合在潘帕斯草原上骑马。

例句：He wore a pair of worn leather gaucho pants, a testament to years spent working the ranch. 他穿着一件磨损的皮革高乔裤，这是多年在牧场工作的证明。


近义词：riding breeches, jodhpurs, chaps|反义词：tight trousers, skinny jeans|词性:名词
