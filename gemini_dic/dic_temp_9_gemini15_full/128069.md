nerves:/nɜːrvz/| n.|神经；胆量；焦虑|The word "nerves" refers to the fibrous tissues that transmit impulses of sensation, motion, etc., to and from the brain and other parts of the body.  It can also refer to a person's emotional state, particularly feelings of anxiety or nervousness.|例句：She had a lot of nerves before the exam. 她考试前很紧张。|例句：He showed great nerves during the crisis. 他在危机中表现出极大的勇气。|近义词：anxiety, nervousness, apprehension, courage, bravery|反义词：calmness, composure, bravery (in the context of courage)|名词
