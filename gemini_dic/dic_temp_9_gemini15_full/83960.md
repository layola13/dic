half-contemptuous: /ˌhæf kənˈtɛmptʃuəs/ | adj. | 半带轻蔑的，略带轻蔑的 |  Expressing or showing a feeling of mild contempt. It suggests a mixture of disdain and amusement, or a subtle, understated contempt.  | 例句：He gave her a half-contemptuous glance. 他给了她一个略带轻蔑的眼神。

He offered a half-contemptuous shrug. 他半带轻蔑地耸了耸肩。


近义词：disdainful, scornful, supercilious, condescending

反义词：respectful, admiring, deferential


词性：形容词
