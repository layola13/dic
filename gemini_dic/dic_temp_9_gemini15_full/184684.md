restung: /rɪˈstʌŋ/ | v. | 再刺，再蛰 |  Past participle of "restinge" (a less common variant of "to sting"). It describes the action of stinging again, or being stung again.  | Example:  The bee, having escaped, restung him on the neck.  蜜蜂逃脱后，又蛰了他的脖子。 | 近义词: stung again, bitten again | 反义词: healed, cured | 动词
