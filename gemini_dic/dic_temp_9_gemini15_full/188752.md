runs:/rʌnz/| v. n.|跑，运行，管理|The verb "run" has many meanings, depending on the context.  It can mean to move swiftly on foot, to operate or function (as in a machine), to manage or control (as in a business), or to extend or continue in a certain direction.  As a noun, it can refer to a continuous series or sequence, a short journey, or the act of running.|例句：The dog runs quickly in the park.  这只狗在公园里飞快地跑。|例句：The washing machine is running. 洗衣机正在运转。|近义词：jog, sprint, operate, manage, extend|反义词：stop, halt, stand still, cease

USAGE->
