connascent: /kənˈnæsnt/ | adj. |同时发生的；同时产生的|  Emerging or originating at the same time; simultaneous in origin.  The term often refers to things that are born or created together, implying a close relationship or interdependence. | 例句：The connascent development of the brain and nervous system is crucial for proper function.  = 脑和神经系统的同时发育对于正常功能至关重要。


The connascent growth of these two plants made it difficult to distinguish them. = 这两种植物的同时生长使得很难区分它们。

近义词：simultaneous, coincident, concurrent, coexistent | 反义词：separate, distinct, independent
