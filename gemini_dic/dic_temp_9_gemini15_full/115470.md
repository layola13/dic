manufacturers:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərərz/| n. |制造商，厂商|Those who make goods on a large scale.  They design, produce, and distribute products.  |例句：Many manufacturers are now focusing on sustainable practices. 许多制造商现在都专注于可持续发展实践。|例句：The car manufacturers are recalling their latest model due to a safety defect. 汽车制造商正在召回其最新型号，原因是存在安全缺陷。|近义词：producers, makers, suppliers|反义词：consumers, users
