psychogalvanic:/ˌsaɪkoʊɡælˈvænɪk/| adj.|心理电流的；精神电流的|Relating to or denoting the changes in the electrical conductivity of the skin that are associated with emotional states.  This term is now largely obsolete and replaced by more precise terms such as galvanic skin response (GSR) or electrodermal activity (EDA).|The psychogalvanic reflex was once used as a lie detector test.|近义词:galvanic skin response (GSR), electrodermal activity (EDA)|反义词:None.
