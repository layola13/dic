countersniper:/ˈkaʊntərˌsnaɪpər/| n. |反狙击手|A countersniper is a soldier trained to locate, track, and eliminate enemy snipers.  They use specialized skills and equipment to counter the threat posed by snipers.  |Example: The countersniper patiently waited for the enemy sniper to reveal his position.  反狙击手耐心地等待敌人狙击手暴露其位置。|近义词：anti-sniper, sniper hunter|反义词：sniper
