lexically:/ˈlɛksɪkəli/ | adv. | 词汇上地；词法上地 | In a way that relates to the vocabulary of a language.  It describes something concerning the words themselves, their meanings, and their relationships within a language's lexicon. | The word "run" is lexically ambiguous because it can mean to move quickly on foot or to operate a machine.  |近义词：semantically (if referring to meaning),  vocably | 反义词： grammatically (if contrasting with grammatical aspects)
