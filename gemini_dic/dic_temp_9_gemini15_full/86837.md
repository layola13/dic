hematuria:/ˌhiːməˈtʃʊəriə/| n. |血尿 |The presence of blood in the urine.  This can be a symptom of various medical conditions, ranging from minor infections to serious kidney diseases.  It's important to seek medical attention if you experience hematuria. |The patient presented with hematuria and flank pain.  这位病人出现血尿和腰痛。
The doctor ordered a urinalysis to determine the cause of the hematuria. 医生下令进行尿液分析以确定血尿的原因。|近义词：bloody urine|反义词：无
