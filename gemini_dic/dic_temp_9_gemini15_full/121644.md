misperceptions:/ˌmɪspərˈsɛpʃənz/| n. |误解；错误观念 |Incorrect understandings or interpretations of something.  Misperceptions often stem from a lack of information, biases, or flawed reasoning. They can lead to misunderstandings, conflicts, and poor decision-making. |例句：The article aims to dispel common misperceptions about climate change. 这篇文章旨在消除关于气候变化的常见误解。

例句：His misperceptions about the situation led him to make a bad decision. 他对局势的误解导致他做出了错误的决定。|近义词：misunderstandings, delusions, illusions, fallacies |反义词：correct understandings, accurate perceptions, truths |名词
