flaten:/ˈflætən/| vt.|使平坦；使扁平；使衰弱；使不景气|To make something flat or less steep; to reduce the intensity or importance of something.|例句：He flattened the cardboard box. 他把纸板箱压平了。|例句：The economy has flattened recently. 近期经济发展趋缓。|近义词：flatten, level, smooth|反义词：raise, elevate, increase
