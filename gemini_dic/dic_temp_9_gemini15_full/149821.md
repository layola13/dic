paleolithic:/ˌpeɪliəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|旧石器时代的|Relating to or denoting the early phase of the Stone Age, lasting from about 2.5 million years ago until about 10,000 years ago, characterized by the use of chipped stone tools.|The Paleolithic era saw the development of the first stone tools.|近义词：prehistoric|反义词：Neolithic
