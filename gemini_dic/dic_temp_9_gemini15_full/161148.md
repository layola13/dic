postfebrile:/ˌpoʊstˈfiːbraɪl/| adj. |发热后的，退烧后的|Relating to or occurring after a fever.  It describes the period following a fever, often referring to symptoms or conditions that persist or develop after the fever subsides. |Example:  The patient experienced postfebrile weakness for several days after the flu.  病人流感痊愈后几天都感到发热后的虚弱。|近义词：post-fever, convalescent|反义词：febrile
