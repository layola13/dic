overstrengthen:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛŋθən/| vt.|过度增强，强化过度|To make something stronger than is necessary or desirable, often to the point of causing harm or weakness.|The government's attempt to overstrengthen the economy led to inflation. 政府试图过度增强经济导致了通货膨胀。|strengthen, reinforce|weaken, undermine|动词
