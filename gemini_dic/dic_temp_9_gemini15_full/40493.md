contriter:/ˈkɒntrʌɪt/ /ˈkɑːntrʌɪt/| adj.|懊悔的；悔恨的；痛悔的|Feeling or expressing remorse for wrongdoing or sin.|He was contrite about his behavior and apologized sincerely. 他对自己的行为感到懊悔，并真诚地道歉了。|近义词：repentant, remorseful, penitent|反义词：unrepentant, remorseless, impenitent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is contrite for his mistakes. 他为自己的错误感到懊悔。
She feels contrite about hurting her friend. 她为伤害她的朋友而感到懊悔。


一般过去时:
He was contrite after the argument. 争吵后他感到懊悔。
She became contrite when she saw the damage she had caused. 当她看到自己造成的损害时，她变得懊悔了。


现在完成时:
He has been contrite ever since the incident. 自那件事发生以来，他一直很懊悔。
She has remained contrite about her actions. 她一直对自己的行为感到懊悔。


过去完成时:
He had been contrite for days before finally apologizing.  他在几天前就感到懊悔了，然后才最终道歉。
She had been contrite for weeks, but he still didn't forgive her. 她已经懊悔了好几个星期了，但他仍然没有原谅她。


将来时:
He will be contrite when he understands the consequences. 当他明白后果时，他会感到懊悔。
She will likely be contrite once she sees the error of her ways.  一旦她认识到自己行为的错误，她很可能会感到懊悔。


虚拟语气:
If he were contrite, he would apologize. 如果他感到懊悔，他会道歉的。
I wish she were more contrite about her actions. 我希望她对自己的行为更感到懊悔。


被动语态:
His remorse was felt to be contrite.  人们认为他的悔恨是真诚的（懊悔的）。
His apology was considered contrite by most.  大多数人认为他的道歉是真诚的（懊悔的）。


疑问句:
Is he contrite about what he did? 他对自己的所作所为感到懊悔吗？
Does she seem contrite to you? 她看起来对你感到懊悔吗？


否定句:
He was not contrite at all. 他一点也不懊悔。
She didn't appear contrite for her actions. 她对自己的行为并不显得懊悔。


PHRASE->
a contrite heart  一颗懊悔的心
contrite confession  真诚的忏悔
contrite apology  真诚的道歉
feel contrite 感到懊悔
show contrition 表现出懊悔


词源:
From Old French *conterit, past participle of contrere "to be contrite," from Latin contritus, past participle of conterere "to bruise, crush, grind," from com- "together" + terere "to rub."  The sense development is from "crushed in spirit" to "deeply remorseful."
