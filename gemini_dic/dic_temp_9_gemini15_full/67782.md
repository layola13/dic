eyelids: /ˈaɪˌlɪdz/ | n. | 眼睑 | The eyelids are the folds of skin that cover and protect the eyes. They are essential for blinking, which helps to keep the eyes moist and clean.  | 例句：He gently closed his eyelids. 他轻轻地闭上了眼睛。| 例句：She had heavy eyelids from lack of sleep. 她因为睡眠不足而眼皮沉重。| 近义词：eyelid (单数形式) | 反义词：无
