mislearning:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnɪŋ/| n. |误学，错误学习 |The act of learning something incorrectly or incompletely; a misconception acquired through learning.  It can refer to the process or the result of incorrect learning. |例句：His mislearning of the grammar rules led to frequent errors in his writing. 他的语法规则学习错误导致了他的写作中经常出现错误。

The mislearning of historical facts is a common problem in many educational systems.  对历史事实的误学在许多教育体系中是一个普遍问题。


近义词：mallearning, faulty learning, incorrect learning, misunderstanding |反义词：correct learning, accurate learning, proper understanding |名词
