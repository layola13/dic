co-operative:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/|/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| adj., n. |合作的；合作社；合作企业|

英文解析:Relating to or characterized by cooperation.  A cooperative is a business organization owned and operated by its members, who share in the profits or benefits.

例句:
They worked in a highly co-operative manner. = 他们以高度合作的方式工作。

The housing co-operative provides affordable housing for its members. = 这个住房合作社为其成员提供经济适用房。


近义词:collaborative, joint, united, mutual

反义词:uncooperative, antagonistic, competitive


词性:形容词，名词

词源:
From the Latin word “cooperari,” meaning "to work together."  The prefix "co-" means "together," and "operari" means "to work." The word evolved through various stages in Middle English and Old French before arriving at its modern form.
