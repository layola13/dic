seater:/ˈsiːtər/| n.| 座位数；座位的；座机 | A seater is a noun referring to the number of seats a vehicle or other object has, or an object designed for a specific number of seats. It can also be used as an adjective describing something with a certain number of seats. |  例句：This is a two-seater sports car. 这是一辆双座跑车。  例句：The restaurant has a number of four-seater tables. 这家餐馆有几张四座的桌子。|近义词：capacity, accommodation|反义词：N/A|名词

一般现在时:
This car is a four-seater. = 这辆车是四座的。
That airplane is a 300-seater. = 那架飞机是300座的。


一般过去时:
The bus was a 50-seater. = 那辆公共汽车是50座的。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had a four-seater car to accommodate my family. = 我希望我有一辆四座的车来容纳我的家人。
