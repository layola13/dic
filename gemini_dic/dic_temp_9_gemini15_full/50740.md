/ˈdɛrɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 起重；吊装；粗暴地对待 | The act of lifting or moving heavy objects using a derrick or crane.  It can also refer to rough or violent handling. | 例句：They are derricking the heavy machinery onto the truck.  他们在用起重机把重型机械吊到卡车上。

例句：The workers were derricking the steel beams into place. 工人们正在用起重机将钢梁吊装到位。


近义词：hoisting, lifting, craning | 反义词：lowering, gently handling
