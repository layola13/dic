rustproof:/ˈrʌstpruːf/| adj.|防锈的；耐锈的|Resistant to rust.  The term describes materials or coatings that prevent or inhibit the formation of rust.|例句：The car has a rustproof coating. 这辆车涂有防锈涂层。

例句：This stainless steel is rustproof.  这种不锈钢是防锈的。


近义词：corrosion-resistant, rust-resistant|反义词：rust-prone, easily rusted|词性：形容词
