shimming:/ˈʃɪmɪŋ/| n. v.|垫片；调整；微调|The act of placing shims to adjust something, or the state of being shimmed.|例句：The mechanic performed shimming on the car's engine to improve its performance. 技师对汽车发动机进行了垫片调整以提高性能。  The slight shimming of the door frame corrected the alignment issue. 门框的微调纠正了对齐问题。|近义词：adjusting, aligning, fine-tuning|反义词：misaligning, displacing|名词，动词

词源:
The word "shim" comes from the Middle English word "schym," likely of Scandinavian origin, meaning a thin piece of wood or metal.  Its usage in the sense of adjusting by placing thin pieces evolved over time.
