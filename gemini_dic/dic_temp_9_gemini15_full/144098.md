outsatisfy: /ˌaʊtˈsætɪsfaɪ/ | vt. | 超越……的满意程度；使……非常满意 | To satisfy beyond expectation; to exceed in satisfaction.  |  The new features outsatisfy even the most demanding users. 新功能甚至让最挑剔的用户都非常满意。 | surpass, exceed, delight | disappoint, dissatisfy | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new product outsatisfies customer expectations. = 新产品超越了客户的预期。
This restaurant outsatisfies all others in the city. = 这家餐厅比城里其他所有餐厅都更令人满意。


一般过去时:
The concert outsatisfied the audience's hopes. = 音乐会超出了观众的期望。
The vacation outsatisfied everything we had imagined. = 假期超出了我们想象的一切。


现在完成时:
The company has outsatisfied its investors this quarter. = 公司本季度已经超越了投资者的预期。
Their performance has outsatisfied all expectations. = 他们的表现已经超出了所有人的预期。


过去完成时:
The results had outsatisfied everyone's predictions. = 结果已经超出了所有人的预测。
We had outsatisfied ourselves with the outcome of our project. = 我们对项目的结果感到非常满意，甚至超出了预期。


将来时:
The new design will outsatisfy the current market demand. = 新设计将超越当前的市场需求。
I believe this strategy will outsatisfy our shareholders. = 我相信这个策略将让我们的股东非常满意。


虚拟语气:
If the product outsatisfied their needs, they would buy it. = 如果产品满足了他们的需求甚至超出了预期，他们就会购买。
I wish the service had outsatisfied my expectations. = 我希望服务能超乎我的预期。


被动语态:
The customers were outsatisfied by the exceptional service. = 客户对卓越的服务非常满意，甚至超出了预期。
The project was outsatisfied by the team's hard work. = 项目由于团队的辛勤工作而取得了超出预期的成功。


疑问句:
Does this plan outsatisfy your requirements? = 这个计划是否超出了你的要求？
Will the new software outsatisfy the user's needs? = 新软件是否会满足甚至超过用户的需求？


否定句:
The product did not outsatisfy the customer's expectations. = 产品没有满足客户的预期。
The service failed to outsatisfy our needs. = 服务未能满足甚至超过我们的需求。
