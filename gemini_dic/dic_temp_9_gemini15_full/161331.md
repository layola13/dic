postprandial:/ˌpoʊstˈprændiəl/| adj.|餐后的，饭后的|Relating to the period after a meal.|The postprandial blood sugar level was significantly elevated after the high-carbohydrate meal. 餐后血糖水平在高碳水化合物膳食后显著升高。|近义词：after-meal, postcibal |反义词：preprandial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The postprandial blood sugar is usually monitored after meals. = 餐后血糖通常在饭后监测。
Postprandial drowsiness is a common experience for many people. = 餐后嗜睡是许多人的普遍经历。


一般过去时:
The doctor checked the patient's postprandial blood pressure. = 医生检查了病人的餐后血压。
His postprandial fatigue was quite noticeable after the large lunch. = 他在吃了丰盛的午餐后，餐后疲惫相当明显。


现在完成时:
She has experienced several episodes of postprandial hypoglycemia. = 她经历过几次餐后低血糖发作。
They have consistently monitored his postprandial glucose levels. = 他们一直持续监测他的餐后血糖水平。


过去完成时:
He had already experienced postprandial discomfort before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经经历了餐后不适。
The study had shown a correlation between postprandial dyspepsia and certain foods before the publication. = 在发表之前，这项研究已经显示了餐后消化不良与某些食物之间的相关性。


将来时:
The researchers will analyze the postprandial effects of the new drug. = 研究人员将分析这种新药的餐后作用。
We will monitor the patient's postprandial heart rate closely. = 我们将密切监测病人的餐后心率。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about the postprandial effects, I wouldn't have eaten so much. = 如果我知道餐后效应，我就不会吃那么多。
It is essential that postprandial care be provided to patients with diabetes. = 为糖尿病患者提供餐后护理至关重要。


被动语态:

The study's findings on postprandial responses were widely reported. = 该研究关于餐后反应的发现被广泛报道。


疑问句:
What are the typical postprandial symptoms of indigestion? = 消化不良的典型餐后症状是什么？

否定句:

There were no reported instances of severe postprandial complications. = 没有报告严重的餐后并发症的案例。


PHRASE->
postprandial blood glucose: 餐后血糖
postprandial hyperglycemia: 餐后高血糖
postprandial hypotension: 餐后低血压
postprandial dyspepsia: 餐后消化不良
postprandial somnolence: 餐后嗜睡

词源:
post- (拉丁语，表示“后”) + prandial (拉丁语 prandium, 表示“午餐”)
