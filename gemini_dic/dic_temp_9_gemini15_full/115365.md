manpower:/ˈmænˌpaʊər/| n. |人力，劳动力|The company's manpower is its most valuable asset.  这家公司的人力资源是其最宝贵的资产。|近义词：workforce, personnel, labor|反义词：automation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory needs more manpower to meet the increased demand. = 工厂需要更多人力来满足日益增长的需求。
Our company's manpower is highly skilled and experienced. = 我们公司的劳动力技术精湛且经验丰富。
