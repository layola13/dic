imploratory: /ɪmˈplɔːrətɔːri/ | adj. | 恳求的；哀求的 | Characterized by imploring; expressing earnest entreaty.  It suggests a desperate plea, often made with humility and urgency. | 例句：Her imploratory gaze pleaded for mercy.  她的恳求眼神乞求怜悯。


例句：The prisoner offered an imploratory letter to the judge, hoping for leniency. 囚犯向法官递交了一封恳求信，希望能获得宽大处理。


近义词：supplicatory, beseeching, pleading, entreating


反义词：demanding, commanding, authoritative
