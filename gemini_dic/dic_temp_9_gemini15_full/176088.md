rawhides:/ˈrɔːhaɪdz/ | n. |生皮，未经加工的兽皮 | Untanned animal hides; hides or skins in their natural state before processing.  Rawhide is often used to make leather goods after further processing. | Example: The craftsman carefully selected the rawhides for his next project.  工匠仔细挑选了他下一个项目的生皮。  The dog chewed on the rawhide bone until it was frayed.  那条狗啃咬着生皮骨头直到它磨损。|近义词：hides, skins, pelts |反义词：leather (processed hide)
