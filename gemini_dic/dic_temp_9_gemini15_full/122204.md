misusers:/mɪsˈjuːzərz/| n.|滥用者，误用者|People who use something incorrectly or for a wrong purpose.  Those who misuse drugs or power are misusers.|例句：The report highlighted the dangers posed by drug misusers.  报告强调了吸毒者造成的危险。

The authorities are cracking down on software misusers. 当局正在打击软件滥用者。


近义词：abusers, wrongdoers, offenders|反义词：benefactors, proper users

词性:noun
