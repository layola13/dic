despairfully:/dɪsˈpɛərfʊli/ | adv. |绝望地，沮丧地 |  Expressing or characterized by despair. It indicates a state of hopelessness and dejection. |例句：He looked despairfully at the wreckage of his car. 他绝望地望着他那辆报废的汽车。


例句：She cried despairfully, unable to cope with the loss. 她绝望地哭泣，无法承受失去的痛苦。


近义词：hopelessly, despondently, dejectedly | 反义词：hopefully, optimistically, cheerfully | 词性：副词
