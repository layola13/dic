lobule:/ˈlɒbjuːl/| n. |小叶，小瓣|A small lobe, especially of the brain, liver, or lung.  A small rounded part of a larger organ.|例句：The cerebellum is divided into two hemispheres, each of which is further subdivided into several lobules. 小脑分为两个半球，每个半球又细分为几个小叶。

The lung is composed of many lobules.  肺由许多小叶组成。|近义词：lobe, segment, division|反义词：None
