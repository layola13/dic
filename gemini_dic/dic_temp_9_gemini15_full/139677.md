occurrence:/əˈkʌrəns/ | n. |出现，发生；事件 |The occurrence of earthquakes is unpredictable.  地震的发生是不可预测的。|近义词：happening, event, incident |反义词：absence, nonexistence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
An occurrence happens every day. = 每天都会发生一些事情。
The occurrence of this problem is frequent. = 这个问题经常发生。


一般过去时:
A strange occurrence happened last night. = 昨晚发生了一件奇怪的事情。
The occurrence of the accident was investigated. = 事故的发生经过了调查。


现在完成时:
There have been several occurrences of this type. = 已经发生过几次这种类型的事情了。
Many occurrences have been recorded in the past year. = 去年记录了许多事件。


过去完成时:
Before the incident, no such occurrence had ever been recorded. = 在事故发生之前，从未有过这样的记录。
By then, many occurrences had already taken place. = 到那时，已经发生了许多事情。


将来时:
A major occurrence will likely happen next year. = 明年很可能会发生一件大事。
We predict several occurrences will take place in the future. = 我们预测未来会发生几件事。


虚拟语气:
If this occurrence hadn't happened, things would be different. = 如果这件事没有发生，情况就会不一样了。
I wish this unfortunate occurrence hadn't happened. = 我希望这不幸的事情没有发生。


被动语态:
The occurrence was reported in the newspaper. = 这件事被报纸报道了。
The exact time of the occurrence is still being investigated. = 事故的确切时间仍在调查中。


疑问句:
What was the occurrence? = 发生了什么事？
What caused the occurrence? = 导致这次事件的原因是什么？


否定句:
There was no occurrence of note. = 没有值得注意的事情发生。
No such occurrence has been reported. = 没有报道过这样的事件。


PHRASE->
the occurrence of = ...的发生
a rare occurrence = 罕见事件
a frequent occurrence = 常见事件
a significant occurrence = 重大事件
a disturbing occurrence = 令人不安的事件
