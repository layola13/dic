lasted:/ˈlæstɪd/| v.|持续，维持|To continue for a particular period of time.  It implies the action or state had an end.|例句：The meeting lasted for three hours.  会议持续了三个小时。
例句：The drought lasted all summer.  旱灾持续了一整个夏天。|近义词：continued, endured, persisted|反义词：ended, finished, ceased|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The game lasts for two hours. = 比赛持续两个小时。
This feeling usually lasts for a few days. = 这种感觉通常持续几天。


一般过去时:
The movie lasted three hours. = 电影持续了三个小时。
The speech lasted longer than expected. = 演讲比预期的长。


现在完成时:
The project has lasted for over a year. = 项目已经持续了一年多。
The pain has lasted since the accident. = 疼痛从事故发生后就一直持续着。


过去完成时:
The war had lasted for five years before he was born. = 他出生前，战争已经持续了五年。
The drought had lasted months before the rains finally came. = 在雨水最终到来之前，旱灾已经持续了几个月。


将来时:
The concert will last about two hours. = 音乐会将持续大约两个小时。
The effects of the medicine will last for several days. = 药效将持续几天。


虚拟语气:
If the meeting had lasted longer, we could have discussed more. = 如果会议持续时间更长，我们本可以讨论更多。
I wish the vacation had lasted longer. = 我希望假期能持续更久。


被动语态:
The meeting was lasted for three hours. (This is grammatically awkward and unnatural.  It is much better to use a different phrasing, for example, "The meeting lasted for three hours" or "The meeting was three hours long.")
The performance was lasted for an hour and a half. (This is grammatically awkward and unnatural.  It is much better to use a different phrasing.)


疑问句:
How long did the party last? = 派对持续了多久？
How long will the treatment last? = 治疗将持续多久？


否定句:
The party didn't last very long. = 派对持续时间不长。
The battery didn't last all day. = 电池没有用一整天。


PHRASE->
last a long time = 持久
last for = 持续…
last through = 挺过…
last out = 坚持到底
it lasted = 它持续了
