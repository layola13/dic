refasten:/ˌriːˈfæsn/| vt.|重新系紧；重新扣紧|To fasten something again.  This verb implies that something was previously fastened but has become undone or loose.|例句：He had to refasten his seatbelt after the bumpy ride. 他在颠簸的旅程后不得不重新系好安全带。
例句：She refastened the button that had come undone. 她重新扣上了脱落的纽扣。|近义词：fasten, secure, tighten|反义词：unfasten, loosen
