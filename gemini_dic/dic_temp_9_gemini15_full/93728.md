immanuel:/ɪˈmænjuəl/| n. |以马内利| A name meaning "God with us," often used as a personal name. It is of Hebrew origin, combining the elements "Im" (with), "manu" (God), and "el" (God).  The name is significant in Christian theology, referencing a prophecy in the Book of Isaiah. |The name Immanuel is popular among Christians.|近义词:Emmanuel|反义词:None.
