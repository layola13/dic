luau:/ˈluːaʊ/| n.| 卢奥（夏威夷人的盛大宴会）|A luau is a traditional Hawaiian feast that typically includes food, music, dance, and other cultural performances.  It's a celebration often held outdoors.|例句：We attended a luau on the beach and enjoyed the kalua pig and hula dancing. 我们参加了海滩上的一个卢奥，享受了卡卢阿猪肉和呼啦舞。|近义词：feast, celebration, party|反义词：None.
