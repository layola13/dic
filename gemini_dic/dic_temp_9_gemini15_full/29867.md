causers:/ˈkɔːzərz/| n.|原因；起因者；导致者|Those who or that which causes something.  The term often implies agency or intention, though not necessarily. |例句：The primary causers of the conflict were the two rival factions. 主要导致冲突的原因是两个敌对派系。

例句：Identifying the causers of climate change is crucial for effective mitigation strategies. 确定气候变化的成因对于有效的缓解策略至关重要。


近义词：causes, agents, origins, sources, initiators


反义词：results, effects, consequences, outcomes


词性:名词
