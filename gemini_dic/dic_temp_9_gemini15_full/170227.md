protraction:/prəˈtrækʃən/| n. v.| 拖延；延长；伸出；突出 | The act of prolonging something in time; the state of being prolonged;  a drawing out or lengthening.  Also, the act of extending or thrusting forward. |例句：The protraction of the negotiations frustrated everyone.  谈判的拖延让每个人都感到沮丧。

The protraction of his neck made him look like a giraffe. 他伸长的脖子让他看起来像长颈鹿。|近义词： prolongation, extension, delay, deferment |反义词： abbreviation, shortening, curtailment |名词，动词
