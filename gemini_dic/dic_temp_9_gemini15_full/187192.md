roared:/rɔːrd/| v.|咆哮；怒吼；轰鸣|To make a very loud, deep sound.  It can refer to the sound of an animal, a person, or a machine. |例句：The lion roared loudly.  狮子大声咆哮。

The engine roared to life. 引擎轰鸣着启动了。|近义词：bellowed, howled, thundered|反义词：whispered, murmured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lion roars loudly at night. = 狮子在晚上大声咆哮。
The engine roars when it starts. = 引擎启动时会发出轰鸣声。

一般过去时:
The crowd roared its approval. = 人群大声喝彩表示赞同。
The tiger roared before attacking. = 老虎在攻击前发出咆哮。

现在完成时:
The volcano has roared several times this year. = 火山今年已经喷发了好几次了。
The machine has roared continuously for hours. = 机器已经连续轰鸣了几个小时。

过去完成时:
The audience had roared with laughter before the comedian finished his joke. = 在喜剧演员说完笑话之前，观众已经笑声如雷了。
The storm had roared through the night. = 暴风雨整夜都在肆虐。

将来时:
The crowd will roar with excitement when the band comes on stage. = 当乐队上台时，人群将会兴奋地欢呼。
The plane will roar overhead soon. = 飞机很快就会从头顶飞过，发出轰鸣声。

虚拟语气:
If the bear roared, I would run away. = 如果熊咆哮，我会跑开。
I wish the ocean didn't roar so loudly at night. = 我希望夜晚大海的轰鸣声不要那么大。

被动语态:
The stadium was roared with applause. = 体育场响起了雷鸣般的掌声。(The applause roared through the stadium.)
His name was roared by the cheering crowd. = 他名字被欢呼的人群大声喊叫着。(The cheering crowd roared his name.)

疑问句:
Did the engine roar when you started it? = 你启动引擎时它有没有发出轰鸣声？
What animal roared in the forest last night? = 昨晚森林里是什么动物在咆哮？

否定句:
The lion didn't roar this morning. = 狮子今天早上没有咆哮。
The machine didn't roar as loudly as expected. = 机器并没有发出预期的那么大的轰鸣声。
