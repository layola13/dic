preglacial: /ˌpriːˈɡleɪʃəl/ | adj. | 冰河时期以前的；冰期前的 | Existing or occurring before the last glacial period.  Relating to the time period before the most recent ice age. |

例句：

Pre-glacial deposits were found beneath the ice sheet. = 冰盖下发现了冰河时期前的沉积物。

The preglacial landscape was dramatically altered by the advancing glaciers. = 前冰河时期的景观被推进的冰川彻底改变了。


近义词：anteglacial, pre-Ice Age


反义词：postglacial, glacial


词性：形容词
