hyperpyrexia:/ˌhaɪpərpaɪˈriːksiə/ | n. |极度高热；体温过高 | Extremely high fever; body temperature significantly above normal.  Hyperpyrexia is a life-threatening condition characterized by a body temperature exceeding 41.1°C (106°F).  |Example: The patient presented with hyperpyrexia and required immediate medical intervention.  病人出现极度高热，需要立即进行医疗干预。|近义词：severe fever, extreme hyperthermia |反义词：hypothermia
