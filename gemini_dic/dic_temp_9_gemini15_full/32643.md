chiselling: /ˈtʃɪzəlɪŋ/ | v. | 凿刻；雕刻；慢慢地削减 | The act of shaping or carving something by using a chisel;  also, the gradual reduction of something. | 例句：He spent hours chiselling away at the stone. 他花了数小时凿石头。  The company is chiselling away at its costs. 公司正在慢慢削减成本。 | 近义词：carving, sculpting, whittling, eroding | 反义词：building, constructing, adding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chisels the wood every day. = 他每天都凿木头。
She chisels away at the problem. = 她一点一点地解决这个问题。

一般过去时:
They chiselled the statue from marble. = 他们用大理石雕刻了这尊雕像。
The artist chiselled details into the stone. = 艺术家在石头上凿刻细节。


现在完成时:
He has chiselled many sculptures in his career. = 他在他职业生涯中雕刻了许多雕塑。
They have been chiselling away at the debt for years. = 他们多年来一直在慢慢偿还债务。


过去完成时:
She had chiselled the design before painting it. = 她先雕刻好图案再绘画。
The workers had chiselled the stone for weeks before the unveiling. = 工人们在揭幕前已经凿刻了几个星期的石头。


将来时:
He will chisel a new design tomorrow. = 他明天将雕刻一个新的图案。
They will be chiselling the mountainside for the new road. = 他们将在山坡上凿路。


虚拟语气:
If he had a chisel, he would chisel the ice. = 如果他有一把凿子，他就会凿冰。
I wish I could chisel like Michelangelo. = 我希望我能像米开朗基罗一样雕刻。


被动语态:
