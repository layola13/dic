instrumentalist:/ˌɪnstrʊˈmɛntəlɪst/| n. |乐器演奏家|A person who plays a musical instrument, especially one who is skilled and performs professionally.  An instrumentalist can play various instruments, or specialize in one. |She is a talented instrumentalist, equally proficient on the piano and violin.  她是一位才华横溢的乐器演奏家，钢琴和小提琴都同样精通。| musician, performer | non-instrumentalist
