laborant: /ˈleɪbərənt/ | n. |实验员，技工| A person who works in a laboratory, typically assisting scientists or technicians.  This term is more common in some European languages than in English.  It often implies a more practical and less theoretical role compared to a scientist or researcher. |例句：The laborant prepared the samples for the experiment.  实验员为实验准备了样品。

The laborant meticulously cleaned the glassware. 实验员细致地清洗了玻璃器皿。

近义词：technician, assistant, laboratory worker | 反义词：researcher, scientist (in terms of theoretical focus) |词性:名词
