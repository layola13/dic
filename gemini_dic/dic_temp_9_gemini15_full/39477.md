conniving:/kəˈnaɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 阴谋诡计的，策划的 |  Secretly planning something bad; scheming.  It implies a sense of underhandedness and often involves deception. |例句：He was accused of conniving with the enemy. 他被指控与敌人勾结。

例句：The conniving CEO manipulated the stock market to his advantage.  这位阴险的首席执行官操纵股市以谋取私利。


近义词：scheming, plotting, intriguing, machiavellian | 反义词：honest, upright, straightforward, candid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always conniving to get ahead. = 他总是耍手段以求出人头地。
They connive to cheat the system. = 他们合谋欺骗系统。


一般过去时:
She connived to steal the inheritance. = 她密谋偷窃遗产。
They connived against their rivals. = 他们密谋对付他们的竞争对手。


现在完成时:
He has connived at many illegal activities. = 他曾参与过许多非法活动。
They have connived to ruin their business partner. = 他们合谋毁掉了他们的商业伙伴。


过去完成时:
She had connived to get the job before the interview. = 在面试前她就已密谋得到这份工作。
They had connived to frame their colleague. = 他们已经密谋陷害他们的同事。


将来时:
He will connive to get revenge. = 他将会密谋报复。
They will connive to overthrow the government. = 他们将会密谋推翻政府。


虚拟语气:
If he had not connived, he would not have been caught. = 如果他没有密谋，他就不会被抓。
I wish they hadn't connived to sabotage the project. = 我真希望他们没有密谋破坏这个项目。


被动语态:
The plan was connived at by several people. = 这个计划是由几个人秘密策划的。
The scheme was connived to defraud investors. = 该计划是密谋欺骗投资者的。


疑问句:
Was she conniving in this conspiracy? = 她是否参与了这场阴谋？
Are they conniving to harm others? = 他们是否正在密谋伤害他人？


否定句:
He did not connive to harm anyone. = 他没有密谋伤害任何人。
They did not connive with the opposition. = 他们没有与反对派同流合污。


PHRASE->
a conniving look = 一个阴险的眼神
a conniving smile = 一个阴险的笑容
conniving behavior = 阴险的行为
conniving politician = 阴险的政治家
conniving plot = 阴险的阴谋


词源:
From Middle French *conniver, from Latin connivere "to wink, close the eyes," from com- "together" + *nivere "to close the eyes."  The sense shifted from "to wink" to "to be in collusion" and then to "to plot secretly."
