disposer:/dɪˈspoʊzər/| n. |垃圾处理器；处理者；处置者 |A device, typically installed under a kitchen sink, that grinds up food waste and flushes it down the drain.  Also, a person or thing that disposes of something.|例句：We installed a new garbage disposer in the kitchen.  我们厨房安装了一个新的垃圾处理器。

例句：The company is responsible for the safe disposer of hazardous waste. 这家公司负责危险废物的安全处置。


近义词：garbage grinder, waste disposal unit, eliminator

反义词：saver, preserver, collector


词性：名词
