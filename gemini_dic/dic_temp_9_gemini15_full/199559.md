singlehood:/ˈsɪŋɡəlˌhʊd/ | n. |单身状态，独身生活 |The state or condition of being unmarried.  It often refers to the lifestyle and experiences associated with being single, not just the legal status. |Example:  She embraced singlehood after her divorce, traveling and pursuing her passions. 她离婚后欣然接受了单身生活，四处旅行，追求自己的爱好。|近义词：unmarried state, celibacy (if implying abstinence from sexual activity) |反义词：marriage, coupledom
