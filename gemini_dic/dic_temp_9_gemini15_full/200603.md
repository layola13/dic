slaveocracy:/ˌsleɪvˈɑːkrəsi/| n. |奴隶统治；奴隶主统治 |A system of government or society in which slaveholders have dominant power.  The term is often used to describe the antebellum South in the United States. |例句：The slaveocracy of the antebellum South maintained its power through violence and intimidation.  奴隶主统治的南方战前社会通过暴力和恐吓维持其权力。  The legacy of the slaveocracy continues to affect American society today.  奴隶主统治的遗产至今仍影响着美国社会。|近义词：oligarchy, plutocracy |反义词：democracy, egalitarianism |名词
