phenolate:/ˈfɛnəleɪt/| n. |酚盐|A salt or ester of a phenol.  Phenolates are formed when a phenol reacts with a base, replacing the acidic hydrogen atom with a metal or other cation.|例句：Sodium phenolate is a common example used in organic synthesis. 苯酚钠是常用的一种有机合成试剂。  The reaction of phenol with sodium hydroxide forms sodium phenolate and water.  苯酚与氢氧化钠反应生成苯酚钠和水。|近义词：phenoxide|反义词: N/A
