certitude:/ˈsɜːrtɪtjuːd/| n. |确定性；确信；确实|The state of being certain; firm conviction.  Certitude implies a complete absence of doubt.|He spoke with certitude about the future of the company. 他带着对公司未来的确信谈论着。|certainty, assurance, conviction|doubt, uncertainty, skepticism|名词
