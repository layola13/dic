prededicated: /ˌpriːˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先奉献的；预先指定的 |  The word "prededicated" combines the prefix "pre-" (meaning before or beforehand) with "dedicated," which implies a commitment or devotion to a specific purpose.  Therefore, "prededicated" describes something that has been committed or assigned to a particular purpose in advance. It suggests a prior planning and allocation of resources or efforts.  The word is relatively uncommon and often found in specialized contexts.  | Example: The funds were prededicated to the research project before the grant application was even submitted.  资金在提交拨款申请之前就已经预先指定用于该研究项目。 | 近义词：preassigned, predetermined, preallocated | 反义词：undedicated, unassigned
