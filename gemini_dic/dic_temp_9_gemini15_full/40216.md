contexts:/ˈkɒntekstz/ /ˈkɑːntekstz/| n. |语境；上下文；背景|The meaning of a word can change depending on its context.  一个词的意思可能会根据其语境而改变。|The historical context is crucial to understanding the poem.  理解这首诗的关键在于历史背景。|circumstances, background, setting, situation|non-context (反义词较抽象，无直接对应)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The context of the email is professional. = 这封电子邮件的语境是专业的。
The current context makes it difficult to solve the problem. = 当前的语境使得解决这个问题变得困难。


一般过去时:
The context of the meeting was very tense. = 会议的氛围非常紧张。（此处context指氛围，属于引申义）
The historical context explained the events. = 历史背景解释了这些事件。


现在完成时:
Researchers have considered the social context in their study. = 研究人员在他们的研究中考虑了社会背景。
We have established the context for further discussion. = 我们已经为进一步讨论奠定了背景。


过去完成时:
By that time, the context had changed dramatically. = 到那时，情况已经发生了巨大的变化。（此处context指情况，属于引申义）
They had already understood the context before starting the project. = 在开始项目之前，他们已经理解了背景。


将来时:
The context will be clearer after more information is available. =  更多信息可用后，情况将会更加明朗。（此处context指情况，属于引申义）
The new technology will change the context of the industry. = 新技术将改变该行业的背景。


虚拟语气:
If the context were different, the outcome might have been better. = 如果语境不同，结果可能会更好。
I wish the context was clearer. = 我希望语境更清晰。


被动语态:
The data was interpreted within its proper context. = 数据在其适当的语境下被解读。
The problem was explained in the context of the broader issue. = 这个问题是在更广泛问题的背景下解释的。


疑问句:
What is the context of this conversation? = 这段对话的背景是什么？
Can you provide more context for this statement? = 你能为这个陈述提供更多背景吗？


否定句:
The article lacked context. = 这篇文章缺乏背景。
He didn't understand the context of the situation. = 他不明白这种情况的背景。


PHRASE->
in context: 在上下文中
out of context: 脱离上下文
social context: 社会背景
historical context: 历史背景
cultural context: 文化背景
