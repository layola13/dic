pansies:/ˈpænziːz/| n.|三色堇，紫罗兰|Pansies are a type of flowering plant known for their vibrant colors and heart-shaped petals. They are often used in gardens and as decorative plants.  |例句：The garden was ablaze with colorful pansies. 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的三色堇。

例句：She planted pansies around the base of the birdbath.  她在浴鸟盆底周围种了三色堇。


近义词：violets, violas|反义词：无


词性:名词

词源:
The word "pansy" comes from the Old French word "pensée," meaning "thought."  This is because the flower was associated with thoughts and memories.  The modern English word evolved through Middle English "pensee".
