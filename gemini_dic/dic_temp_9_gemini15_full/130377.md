non-usurious: /ˌnɒnjuˈʒʊəriəs/ | adj. | 非高利贷的；非剥削性的 | Not involving or characterized by usury (the practice of lending money at unreasonably high rates of interest).  It implies fairness and ethical lending practices. | Example: The bank offered non-usurious loans to small businesses, helping them grow without excessive debt burdens.  这家银行向小型企业提供非高利贷贷款，帮助他们在没有过重债务负担的情况下发展壮大。| 近义词: fair, reasonable, equitable, just | 反义词: usurious, exploitative, exorbitant
