/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtərz/| v. |过度杂乱；塞满过多东西|To fill or cover something with too many things, making it messy and disorganized.  This is a newly coined word, not found in standard dictionaries,  but understandable from its components.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "clutters" (the present tense of the verb "to clutter").|The garage is overcluttered with old furniture and boxes.|近义词：overcrowd, overwhelm, overload, jam-pack|反义词：tidy, organize, declutter|动词
