hypnotism:/ˈhɪpnəˌtɪzəm/| n. |催眠术，催眠状态|The practice of inducing a state of hypnosis. It involves suggestions to influence a person's thoughts, feelings, or behaviors.  Hypnotism is often used therapeutically to treat anxiety, phobias, and other conditions.|例句：The therapist used hypnotism to help the patient overcome their fear of spiders.  催眠师用催眠术帮助病人克服对蜘蛛的恐惧。|近义词：mesmerism, suggestion|反义词：awakening, consciousness
