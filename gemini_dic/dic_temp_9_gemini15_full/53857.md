discontents:/dɪsˈkɒntents/| n.| 不满，不满的人 | Dissatisfaction, discontent; a feeling of unhappiness or dissatisfaction.  It can also refer to people who are dissatisfied. |例句：The growing discontents among the workers led to a strike. 工人们日益增长的不满导致了罢工。

The government is trying to address the widespread discontents over economic inequality. 政府正试图解决对经济不平等的普遍不满。


近义词：dissatisfaction, unrest, discontent, grievance, complaints |反义词：satisfaction, contentment, complacency |名词
