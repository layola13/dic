overcrowd:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊd/ | vt. |使过度拥挤；挤满 |To fill (a place) with too many people or things.  |例句：The beach was overcrowded with tourists. 海滩挤满了游客。|例句： Don't overcrowd the room with furniture. 不要把房间塞满家具。|近义词：congest, cram, pack |反义词：depopulate, thin out
