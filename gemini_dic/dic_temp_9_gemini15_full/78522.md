ghillie:/ˈɡɪli/| n. |吉利服，伪装服 |A ghillie suit is a type of camouflage clothing designed to provide concealment in a natural environment.  It typically consists of a loose-fitting suit made of burlap, netting, or other materials, often with attached leaves, grass, or other vegetation to blend in with the surroundings. |Example: The sniper wore a ghillie suit to blend in with the foliage.  狙击手穿着吉利服与树叶融为一体。The hunter used a ghillie suit for successful stalking. 猎人使用吉利服成功地潜伏狩猎。|近义词：camouflage, disguise, cover |反义词：uniform, exposed
