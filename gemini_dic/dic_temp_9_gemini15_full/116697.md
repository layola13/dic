maturest: /məˈtʃʊrɪst/ | adj. | 最成熟的，最完善的 | Most developed or advanced in a particular respect.  The word represents the superlative form of the adjective "mature". It signifies the highest degree of maturity or ripeness. |  例句：This is the maturest plan we've considered so far. 这是我们目前考虑过的最成熟的计划。

例句：Her maturest work is undoubtedly her latest novel.  她最成熟的作品无疑是她最新的那部小说。


近义词：most developed, most advanced, most ripe, most fully developed | 反义词：most immature, most undeveloped, most unripe, most green


词性：形容词
