regulates:/ˈreɡjʊleɪts/| vt.|调节；管理；控制|To control or maintain something such as speed, temperature, or a process within certain limits.|例句：The body regulates its temperature through sweating and shivering.  身体通过出汗和颤抖来调节体温。|例句：The government regulates the banking industry. 政府监管银行业。|近义词：control, govern, adjust, moderate|反义词：disorder, disrupt, deregulate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thermostat regulates the room temperature. = 恒温器调节室温。
The government regulates the price of essential goods. = 政府管控必需品的價格。

一般过去时:
The old system regulated the flow of water effectively. = 旧系统有效地调节了水流。
The committee regulated the competition fairly. = 委员会公平地管理了比赛。

现在完成时:
The new policy has regulated the market effectively. = 新政策已有效地规范了市场。
The central bank has regulated inflation successfully. = 中央银行已成功地控制了通货膨胀。

过去完成时:
The company had regulated its production before the crisis. = 公司在危机之前已经规范了生产。
They had regulated the speed of the machine before the experiment. = 他们在实验前已经调整了机器的速度。

将来时:
The automated system will regulate the traffic flow smoothly. = 自动系统将平稳地调节交通流量。
The new software will regulate the temperature more precisely. = 新软件将更精确地调节温度。

虚拟语气:
If the body didn't regulate its temperature, we would get sick easily. = 如果身体不能调节体温，我们很容易生病。
It is important that the government regulates the use of resources efficiently. = 重要的是政府要有效地规范资源的使用。

被动语态:
The speed of the vehicle is regulated by the cruise control. = 车速由巡航控制系统调节。
The water supply is regulated by a complex system of dams and canals. = 供水由复杂的坝渠系统控制。

疑问句:
Does the central bank regulate interest rates? = 中央银行是否调节利率？
How does the body regulate blood pressure? = 人体如何调节血压？

否定句:
The company does not regulate its waste disposal properly. = 该公司没有妥善地规范其废物处理。
The system failed to regulate the temperature effectively. = 系统未能有效地调节温度。
