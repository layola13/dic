phosphatic:/fɒsˈfeɪtɪk/| adj.|含磷酸的，磷的|Containing or relating to phosphates.  The term refers to materials containing phosphorus in the form of phosphates. These materials are often used in fertilizers due to phosphorus's importance in plant growth. |例句：The soil was enriched with phosphatic fertilizers. 这片土壤用含磷肥料改良了。  The analysis revealed a high phosphatic content in the rock sample. 分析显示岩石样本中磷酸盐含量很高。|近义词：phosphorous, phosphate-containing |反义词：phosphate-free
