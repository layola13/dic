noninfective:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfektɪv/ | adj. |非感染性的，非传染性的 | Not causing or capable of causing infection.  This term is used primarily in the medical field to describe diseases, conditions, or agents that do not spread from one person to another through infection. | Example: The rash was diagnosed as noninfective, ruling out contagious causes.  疹子被诊断为非感染性的，排除了传染性原因。 The doctor confirmed that the inflammation was noninfective. 医生确认炎症是非感染性的。| 近义词：noncontagious, aseptic | 反义词：infective, contagious, infectious
