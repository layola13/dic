ostensibly:/ɒˈstensɪbli/ | adv. |表面上地；似乎；名义上地 | Apparently or seemingly, but perhaps not actually.  It suggests a contrast between appearance and reality. | 例句：Ostensibly, the meeting was to discuss the budget, but the real purpose was to announce personnel changes.  表面上，会议是为了讨论预算，但真正的目的是宣布人事变动。


例句：The company ostensibly supports environmental protection, but its actions suggest otherwise.  该公司表面上支持环保，但其行动却表明并非如此。


近义词：apparently, seemingly, outwardly, superficially


反义词：actually, really, truly, in reality


词性：副词
