precommitted: /ˌpriːkəˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先承诺的；预先决定的 |  Having made a commitment beforehand; already pledged or decided.  This adjective describes something that has been decided or promised in advance.  |  例句：The company was precommitted to a large capital expenditure. 公司之前已经承诺了一大笔资本支出。

例句：He was precommitted to supporting the candidate, regardless of any new information. 他预先承诺支持该候选人，无论出现任何新信息。


近义词：committed, pledged, predetermined, decided, resolved


反义词：uncommitted, undecided, open, flexible


词性：形容词
