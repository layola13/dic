remained:/rɪˈmeɪnd/| v.| 仍然是；保持；留下 | To continue to exist or be in a particular state or condition after other things have changed. |例句：Despite the challenges, she remained optimistic. 尽管面临挑战，她仍然保持乐观。

例句：The problem remained unsolved.  问题仍然未解决。


近义词：stayed, persisted, endured | 反义词：changed, altered, vanished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remains silent. = 他保持沉默。
The situation remains unchanged. = 情况保持不变。


一般过去时:
She remained at home all day. = 她整天都待在家里。
The house remained empty for years. = 这房子空置了好几年。


现在完成时:
He has remained loyal to the company. = 他一直忠于这家公司。
The building has remained structurally sound. = 这座建筑物一直结构稳固。


过去完成时:
After the storm, the damage remained extensive. = 暴风雨过后，破坏仍然很严重。
The mystery remained unsolved until the next day. = 这个谜直到第二天才解开。


将来时:
The situation will likely remain tense. = 局势可能会依然紧张。
He will remain in charge until his successor is appointed. = 他将留任，直到他的继任者被任命。


虚拟语气:
If he remained silent, we would not know the truth. = 如果他保持沉默，我们就不会知道真相。
I wish he remained in the job. = 我希望他继续担任这份工作。


被动语态:
The question remained unanswered. = 这个问题没有得到回答。
The decision remained to be made. = 决定有待作出。


疑问句:
Did the problem remain unsolved? = 问题仍然没有解决吗？
How long will the situation remain unchanged? = 情况将保持不变多久？


否定句:
He did not remain silent. = 他没有保持沉默。
The situation did not remain unchanged. = 情况没有保持不变。


PHRASE->
remain silent 保持沉默
remain calm 保持冷静
remain seated 保持坐姿
remain in effect 保持有效
remain to be seen 尚待观察

词源:
From Old French *remeindre, from Latin remanere "to remain," from re- "back, again" + manere "to stay, remain."
