desk-top:/ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ | n. | 桌面；台式电脑 | A desk-top is the horizontal surface of a desk.  It can also refer to a personal computer designed to be used on a desk, as opposed to a laptop. | Example: I keep my computer on the desk-top. 我把我的电脑放在桌面上。  My desk-top computer is quite powerful. 我的台式电脑功能很强大。 |近义词：desktop computer, workstation | 反义词：laptop, notebook
