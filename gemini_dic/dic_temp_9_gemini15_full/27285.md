calumny:/ˈkæləmni/| n. |诽谤；中伤；诬蔑 | False and malicious statements designed to injure someone's reputation; slander.  |例句：He was the target of a vicious calumny campaign. 他成为一场恶毒诽谤运动的目标。

例句：The newspaper article was full of calumny and innuendo. 那篇报纸文章充满了诽谤和影射。


近义词：slander, defamation, libel, aspersion, vilification

反义词：praise, commendation, eulogy


词源:  From Latin *calumnia, from calumnari "to deceive, slander,"  of obscure origin.
