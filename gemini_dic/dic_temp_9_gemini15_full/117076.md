meadow:/ˈmɛdoʊ/| n. |草地，牧场 |A meadow is a field of grass, especially one used for hay.  Meadows are often found in valleys or near rivers and are typically characterized by wildflowers and other low-growing plants. |例句：The sheep grazed peacefully in the green meadow.  羊儿在绿色的草地上安静地吃草。

The children played hide-and-seek in the sun-drenched meadow. 孩子们在阳光普照的草地上玩捉迷藏。|近义词：field, pasture, grassland |反义词：forest, desert, jungle |名词
