microfarad:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌfærəd/ | n. |微法拉, 微法|A microfarad (µF) is a unit of electrical capacitance, equal to one millionth of a farad.  It represents the ability of a capacitor to store an electric charge.  The higher the capacitance in microfarads, the more charge the capacitor can store at a given voltage. |Example: The capacitor in this circuit has a capacitance of 10 microfarads. 这个电路中的电容器具有10微法的电容。|近义词:None (it's a unit of measurement)|反义词:None (it's a unit of measurement)|名词
