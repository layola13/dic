eavesdrops:/ˈiːvzdrɒps/| n.|窃听，偷听 |The act of secretly listening to a conversation.|例句：I heard eavesdrops from my neighbor's apartment. 我听到邻居公寓里的窃听声。|近义词：overhear, listen in|反义词：announce, broadcast


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eavesdrops on his neighbors' conversations. = 他偷听邻居的谈话。
She eavesdrops on the phone calls in the office. = 她偷听办公室里的电话。


一般过去时:
They eavesdropped on the meeting. = 他们偷听了会议。
I eavesdropped on their conversation last night. = 我昨晚偷听了他们的谈话。


现在完成时:
He has eavesdropped on our conversations many times. = 他多次偷听我们的谈话。
She has eavesdropped on her boss's calls for months. = 她已经偷听了她老板的电话好几个月了。


过去完成时:
They had eavesdropped on the politician's speech before the news broke. = 在新闻爆出之前，他们就偷听了这位政治家的演讲。
She had eavesdropped on their argument before intervening. = 在插手之前，她已经偷听了他们的争吵。


将来时:
He will eavesdrop on their meeting tomorrow. = 他明天将偷听他们的会议。
She will eavesdrop on the phone conversation later. = 她稍后将偷听电话谈话。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't eavesdrop on their conversation. = 如果我是你，我不会偷听他们的谈话。
I wish I hadn't eavesdropped on that private conversation. = 我真希望我没有偷听那次私人谈话。


被动语态:
Their conversation was eavesdropped on by a stranger. = 他们的谈话被陌生人偷听了。
The meeting was eavesdropped on by someone from the rival company. = 会议被竞争对手公司的人偷听了。


疑问句:
Were you eavesdropping on our conversation? = 你在偷听我们的谈话吗？
Is someone eavesdropping on us right now? = 现在有人在偷听我们吗？


否定句:
I didn't eavesdrop on their conversation. = 我没有偷听他们的谈话。
She doesn't eavesdrop on other people's conversations. = 她不偷听别人的谈话。


PHRASE->
eavesdrop on a conversation = 偷听谈话
eavesdrop on a phone call = 偷听电话
eavesdrop on someone = 偷听某人
eavesdrop in the hallway = 在走廊里偷听
eavesdrop secretly = 秘密偷听
