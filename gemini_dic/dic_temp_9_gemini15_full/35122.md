cloudiest: /ˈklaʊdiɪst/ | adj. | 最阴沉的，多云的 | Most cloudy; having the most cloud cover.  The word describes the superlative degree of cloudiness. |例句：Today is the cloudiest day of the week. = 今天是一周中最阴沉的一天。

例句：The cloudiest skies often precede a storm. = 最阴沉的天空往往预示着暴风雨的来临。


近义词：overcast, murky, gloomy, dark | 反义词：sunny, clear, bright | 词性：形容词

一般现在时:
The sky is cloudiest in the afternoon. = 下午的天空阴沉沉的。
It's usually cloudiest in the mountains. = 山区通常阴天最多。


一般过去时:
Yesterday was the cloudiest day of the month. = 昨天是这个月阴天最多的一天。
The sky was cloudiest just before the rain started. = 下雨前天空最阴沉。


现在完成时:
It has been the cloudiest week so far this year. = 这是今年迄今为止阴天最多的一周。
The weather has been cloudiest in the northern regions. = 北部地区的天气一直最为阴沉。


过去完成时:
It had been the cloudiest autumn on record before the sun finally came out. = 在太阳最终出来之前，那是历史上阴天最多的秋天。
Before the storm, it had been cloudiest in the western part of the state. = 暴风雨来临之前，该州西部地区阴天最多。


将来时:
The forecast predicts it will be cloudiest tomorrow. = 预报说明天阴天最多。
They say it will be cloudiest in the coastal areas next week. = 他们说下周沿海地区将阴天最多。


虚拟语气:
If it were cloudiest tomorrow, I wouldn't go hiking. = 如果明天阴天最多，我就不去远足了。
I wish it weren't cloudiest all the time. = 我希望它不要总是阴天。


被动语态:
The day was considered the cloudiest in years. = 那天被认为是多年来阴天最多的一天。
The region is often described as the cloudiest in the country. = 该地区经常被描述为全国阴天最多的地区。


疑问句:
Is today the cloudiest day of the year? = 今天是一年中最阴沉的一天吗？

否定句:
It isn't cloudiest everywhere; some areas are sunny. = 并不是到处都阴天；有些地区阳光明媚。
The weather wasn't cloudiest all day; there were periods of sunshine. = 天气并非一整天都阴沉；期间也有阳光。
