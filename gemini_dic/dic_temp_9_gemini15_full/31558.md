charwomen:/ˈtʃɑːrwʊmɪn/| n.| 女清洁工 | A charwoman is a woman employed to do cleaning work, typically in a house or office.  The term is now considered somewhat dated and potentially offensive. |The charwoman cleaned the office thoroughly every evening.  女清洁工每天晚上都彻底打扫办公室。  | cleaner, housekeeper |  (无明确反义词，可考虑"employer"或"person who makes a mess") |名词
