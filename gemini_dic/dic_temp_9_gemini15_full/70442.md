figurante:/fɪɡjʊˈrɑːnt/| n.|群众演员，临时演员|A figurant is an actor with a minor, often uncredited role in a film, play, or other performance. They typically appear in crowd scenes or as background characters. | Example: Many figurants were hired for the battle scene in the movie.  许多群众演员被雇佣来拍摄电影中的战斗场景。|近义词：extra, background artist |反义词：lead actor, star
