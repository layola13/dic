playtime:/ˈpleɪtaɪm/| n. |游戏时间；玩耍时间 |The time allotted for play or recreation.  It refers to a period specifically designated for recreational activities, often for children. |例句：Playtime is over, it's time for bed.  游戏时间结束了，该睡觉了。|例句：The children enjoyed their playtime at the park.孩子们在公园里玩得很开心。|近义词：recreation, fun time, leisure time |反义词：work time, study time
