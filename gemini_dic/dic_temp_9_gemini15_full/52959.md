dinghy:/ˈdɪŋɡi/| n. |小艇；救生艇 |A small boat, often used as a tender for a larger vessel or for recreational purposes.  It's typically lightweight and easily handled.|例句：We used the dinghy to go ashore. 我们用小艇上岸了。|例句：The yacht carried a small dinghy for exploring nearby coves. 那艘游艇上载着一艘小艇，用于探索附近的岩湾。|近义词：tender, rowboat, skiff |反义词：large ship, yacht
