ratifiers:/ˈrædəˌfaɪərz/| n. |批准者；签署者；认可者 |Those who formally approve or sign a treaty, agreement, or amendment.  They play a crucial role in the legal process of making such documents binding. |The ratifiers of the Paris Agreement are committed to reducing greenhouse gas emissions.  巴黎协定的批准者们致力于减少温室气体排放。|approvers, signatories, endorsers |objectors, opponents |名词
