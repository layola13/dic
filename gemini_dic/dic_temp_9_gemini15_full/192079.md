scoundrel:/ˈskaʊndrəl/| n. |恶棍；无赖；流氓 |A scoundrel is a dishonest and immoral person, typically one who is also a villain or rogue.  They are often characterized by their lack of integrity, deceitfulness, and disregard for others. |例句：He's a complete scoundrel; I wouldn't trust him with a penny. 他是个十足的恶棍；我一分钱也不相信他。

例句：The scoundrel stole the old woman's savings.  那个恶棍偷走了老妇人的积蓄。


近义词：villain, rogue, rascal, knave, blackguard |反义词：saint, angel, hero, paragon
