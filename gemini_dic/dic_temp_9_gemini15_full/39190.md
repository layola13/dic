congenialize: /kənˈdʒiːniəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使…融洽；使…和谐；使…适合 | To make congenial; to make suitable or agreeable.  The process of making something or someone more friendly, agreeable, or suitable.  |  例句：They attempted to congenialize the atmosphere by playing soft music. = 他们试图通过播放轻音乐来营造融洽的气氛。 They tried to congenialize the conflicting factions. = 他们试图使冲突的派系和谐相处。 | 近义词：harmonize, reconcile, adapt, accommodate | 反义词：estrange, alienate, antagonize | 动词
