foretells:/fɔːˈtɛlz/| vt.|预言，预示|To say or indicate beforehand that (something) will happen; predict.|The fortune teller foretold a bright future for me.  算命先生预言我会有一个光明的未来。|The dark clouds foretold a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨的来临。|predicts, forecasts, augurs|fails to predict, mispredicts|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foretells the future accurately. = 他准确地预言未来。
The ancient prophecy foretells a great war. = 古代预言预示着一场大战。


一般过去时:
The oracle foretold the king's death. = 神谕预言了国王的死亡。
She foretold the coming of the storm. = 她预言了暴风雨的来临。


现在完成时:
The prophet has foretold many events that have come to pass. = 先知已经预言了许多已经发生的事件。
Scientists have foretold the rise in sea levels. = 科学家已经预言了海平面上升。


过去完成时:
No one had foretold the magnitude of the disaster. = 没有人预料到灾难的严重程度。
They had foretold a bountiful harvest, but the crops failed. = 他们曾预言丰收，但庄稼却歉收了。


将来时:
Experts will foretell the outcome of the election. = 专家们将预言选举的结果。
The weather forecast will foretell tomorrow's weather.  天气预报将预报明天的天气。


虚拟语气:
If he had foretold the earthquake, many lives could have been saved. = 如果他预言了地震，许多生命本来可以挽救。
I wish I had foretold the market crash. = 我真希望我预言到了股市崩盘。


被动语态:
The future was foretold by the ancient seers. = 未来被古代先知预言了。
The impending doom was foretold in the scriptures. = 即将到来的厄运在经文中被预言了。


疑问句:
Does the horoscope foretell a good day for me? = 星座运势预言我今天会好吗？
Can this method foretell the stock market trends? = 这种方法能预言股市趋势吗？


否定句:
The soothsayer did not foretell the assassination. = 占卜者没有预言暗杀事件。
The signs did not foretell any immediate danger. = 迹象并没有预示任何迫在眉睫的危险。
