ecoterrorist:/ˌekoʊˈtɛrərɪst/| n. |生态恐怖分子|A person who uses or threatens to use violence against property or people in order to achieve environmental or animal rights goals.  Ecoterrorists often target businesses, government agencies, or individuals they believe are harming the environment.|例句：The ecoterrorist group claimed responsibility for the arson attack on the logging company. 这个生态恐怖主义组织声称对木材公司纵火袭击负责。|近义词：environmental extremist, radical environmentalist|反义词：environmental conservationist, environmental protector
