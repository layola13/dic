recentrifuge:/ˌriːˈsɛntɹɪfjuːdʒ/| v. |再次离心；重新离心|To centrifuge something again.  This usually implies a further purification or separation process.|例句：The scientist decided to recentrifuge the sample to remove any remaining impurities. = 科学家决定再次离心样品以去除任何残留的杂质。
The technician recentrifuged the blood to obtain a purer plasma fraction. = 技术人员再次离心血液以获得更纯净的血浆部分。|近义词：re-centrifuge, re-spin|反义词：无
