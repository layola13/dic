culmiferous:/kʌlˈmɪfərəs/| adj.|秆生的；有茎的；产秆的|Producing or relating to culms (the jointed stems of grasses).  The term often refers to plants that produce a stalk or stem, particularly those with a jointed structure like grasses and bamboos.|Example:  Many culmiferous plants are important sources of food and fiber.  许多禾本科植物是重要的食物和纤维来源。|近义词：stem-bearing, stalk-producing|反义词：culmless
