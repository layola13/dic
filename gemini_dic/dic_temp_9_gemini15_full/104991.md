kennel:/ˈkenəl/| n. |狗窝；犬舍；养狗场|A small house for a dog to live in; a place where dogs are kept.|例句：The dog sleeps in its kennel. 狗睡在它的狗窝里。
例句：The kennels provide boarding for dogs of all breeds.  这个养狗场为各种品种的狗提供寄宿服务。|近义词：dog house, shelter|反义词：None.
