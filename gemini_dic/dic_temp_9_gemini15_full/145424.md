overcharger:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒər/ | n. |过量充电器；超额收费者 |A device or system that charges a battery beyond its recommended capacity, potentially damaging it.  Also, someone who charges excessively high prices. |例句：The overcharger damaged my phone battery.  过量充电器损坏了我的手机电池。

例句：The car repair shop was an overcharger; they charged me double the usual price. 这家汽车修理厂漫天要价；他们收了我双倍的正常价格。

近义词：excessive charger, price gouger

反义词：fair charger, reasonable priced

词性:名词

词源:
The word "overcharger" is a compound word formed from "over" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") and "charger" (a device used for charging something, usually a battery).  Therefore, it literally means "something that charges excessively."
