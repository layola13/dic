impartations:/ˌɪmpɑːrˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 传授；给予；灌输 | The impartations of wisdom from the elders were invaluable.  长者的智慧传授弥足珍贵。|近义词：communications, transmissions, bestowals |反义词：withholdings, concealments

英文解析:The act of imparting something, especially knowledge, information, or a quality.  It refers to the process of sharing or bestowing something.

例句：

The teacher's impartations of knowledge helped the students excel. 老师的知识传授帮助学生们取得了优异的成绩。

His impartations of encouragement inspired the team to persevere. 他的鼓励给予激励了团队坚持不懈。


词源:

From the verb "impart," which comes from the Old French "impartir," meaning "to divide, share, distribute."  The Old French word itself derives from the Latin "impartire," a combination of the prefix "im-" (into, upon) and "partire" (to divide, share).
