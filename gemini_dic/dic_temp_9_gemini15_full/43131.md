coyote:/kaɪˈoʊt/| n. |郊狼；土狼 | A coyote is a wild dog native to North and Central America, known for its cunning and ability to adapt to different environments. They are omnivores with a diverse diet. | Example:  A coyote howled in the distance.  一只郊狼在远处嚎叫。  The farmer lost several chickens to a coyote.  农民损失了几只鸡给郊狼。|近义词：prairie wolf, brush wolf |反义词：domestic dog
