coevolution:/ˌkoʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/| n. |协同进化|The process whereby two or more species reciprocally affect each other's evolution;  a change in the genetic composition of one species (or population) in response to a genetic change in another.  It often leads to interdependent relationships between species. | Example: The long tongues of hummingbirds and the tubular flowers they feed on are a classic example of coevolution.  蜂鸟的长舌头和它们所采食的管状花朵是协同进化的一个经典例子。  The evolution of resistance in pests to insecticides and the development of more potent insecticides by humans is another example of coevolution, though a less harmonious one.  害虫对杀虫剂产生抗药性以及人类研制更有效的杀虫剂，是协同进化的另一个例子，尽管它不太和谐。|近义词：reciprocal evolution, interdependent evolution|反义词：independent evolution
