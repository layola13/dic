contrariwise:/ˈkɒntrəriwaɪz/| adv.|相反地；反之；另一方面|On the contrary; conversely.|例句：He said it was a good idea; contrariwise, I think it's terrible. 他说这是一个好主意；相反地，我认为它很糟糕。


例句：Contrariwise, if you fail to plan, you plan to fail. 反之，如果你不计划，你就计划着失败。

近义词：conversely, conversely, on the contrary, by contrast

反义词：similarly, likewise

词性：副词
