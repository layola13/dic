irreversible:/ˌɪrɪˈvɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 不可逆的，不能取消的 | incapable of being changed or reversed.  | The damage to the environment is irreversible. 环境破坏是不可逆转的。 | reversible, changeable | reversible, changeable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The process is irreversible. = 这个过程是不可逆转的。
This decision is irreversible. = 这个决定是不可改变的。


一般过去时:
The damage was irreversible. = 损坏是不可逆转的。
The contract was declared irreversible. = 合同被宣布为不可更改。


现在完成时:
The changes have proved irreversible. = 这些变化已被证明是不可逆转的。
The damage has become irreversible. = 损害已变得不可逆转。


过去完成时:
The situation had become irreversible before we intervened. =在我们干预之前，情况已经变得不可逆转了。
The effects had already become irreversible. = 效果已经变得不可逆转了。


将来时:
The consequences will be irreversible. = 后果将是不可逆转的。
This action will make the situation irreversible. = 这个行动将使局势不可逆转。


虚拟语气:
If we continued, the damage would be irreversible. = 如果我们继续下去，损害将是不可逆转的。
If we had acted sooner, the damage might not have been irreversible. =如果我们早点采取行动，损害可能不会是不可逆转的。


被动语态:
The decision was deemed irreversible. = 该决定被认为是不可逆转的。
The process is considered irreversible. = 该过程被认为是不可逆转的。


疑问句:
Is this damage irreversible? = 这种损害是不可逆转的吗？
Is the trend irreversible? = 这种趋势是不可逆转的吗？


否定句:
The situation is not irreversible; we can still fix it. = 情况并非不可逆转；我们仍然可以解决它。
The damage is not entirely irreversible. = 损害并非完全不可逆转。
