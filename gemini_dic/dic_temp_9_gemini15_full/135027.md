/ˈnɒnˌnɛtwɜːrks/ | n. |非网络；非网络系统 |  Nonnetworks refer to systems or entities that are not interconnected or part of a network.  This can apply to various contexts, such as communication, computer systems, or social structures.  For example, isolated computer systems not connected to a larger network would be considered nonnetworks. | The research focused on the limitations of nonnetworks in data sharing.  这项研究关注的是非网络在数据共享方面的局限性。  | isolated systems, disconnected systems | networks, interconnected systems | 名词
