redivisions:/ˌriːdɪˈvɪʒənz/| n. |再划分，再分割；重新划分|The process of dividing something again, often after a previous division.  It implies a further subdivision or reorganization of existing parts.  This often occurs in administrative, geographical, or organizational contexts.|例句：The redivisions of the electoral districts caused considerable controversy.  重新划分选区引起了相当大的争议。

例句：The company underwent several redivisions of its departments to improve efficiency. 公司对其部门进行了多次重新划分以提高效率。


近义词：redistributions, subdivisions, re-allocations


反义词：unifications, consolidations


词性：名词
