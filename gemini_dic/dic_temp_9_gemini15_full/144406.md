outstrode:/ˌaʊtˈstrəʊd/ | v. |超过(步行速度),超过(别人)|To walk faster than or surpass someone in walking; to exceed (someone) in walking.  |例句：He outstrode his companions on the mountain path. 他在山路上超过了他的同伴们。
例句：She outstrode all the other competitors in the race.她在比赛中超过了所有其他竞争者。|近义词：outwalk, surpass, exceed |反义词：lag behind, trail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstrides his brother in a marathon. = 他在马拉松比赛中超过了他的兄弟。
They outstride their rivals in the hiking competition. = 他们在徒步比赛中超过了他们的对手。

过去完成时:
