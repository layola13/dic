sin:/sɪn/ | n. |罪，罪恶；过失 | The sin of pride can lead to downfall.  骄傲的罪过会导致失败。 |近义词：offense, transgression, wrongdoing |反义词：virtue, righteousness, goodness |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sins frequently. = 他经常犯罪。
She sins against God. = 她犯了上帝的罪。


一般过去时:
They sinned against their conscience. = 他们违背了良心。
He sinned deeply. = 他犯了严重的罪。


现在完成时:
I have sinned many times in my life. = 我一生中犯过许多错误。
She has sinned and repented. = 她犯了罪并且悔过自新了。


过去完成时:
He had sinned before he met her. = 在他遇到她之前，他已经犯过罪了。
They had sinned against the law. = 他们触犯了法律。


将来时:
He will sin again if he doesn't change his ways. = 如果他不改变自己的方式，他还会再次犯罪。
They will sin no more. = 他们不会再犯罪了。


虚拟语气:
If he had not sinned, he would not be suffering now. = 如果他没犯罪，他现在就不会受苦了。
I wish I hadn't sinned. = 我真希望我以前没有犯罪。


被动语态:
No sin was forgiven without repentance. = 没有悔改，就不会赦免任何罪过。
The sin was confessed. = 罪行被供认了。


疑问句:
What sin did he commit? = 他犯了什么罪？
Has she ever sinned? = 她曾经犯罪吗？


否定句:
He did not sin intentionally. = 他并非故意犯罪。
She has never sinned in her life. = 她一生中从未犯罪过。


PHRASE->
sin of omission  = 疏忽之罪

deadly sin = 罪孽

mortal sin = 罪过
