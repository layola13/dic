overkilled:/ˌoʊvərˈkɪld/ | adj. | 过度杀伤的；杀伤过度；过分的；矫枉过正的 | Excessively intense, powerful, or thorough; going beyond what is necessary or appropriate.  | 例句：The presentation was overkill; we didn't need that much detail.  演示文稿过于冗长；我们不需要那么多细节。

The security measures were overkill; a simple lock would have sufficed. 安全措施过于严密；一把简单的锁就足够了。| 近义词：excessive, superfluous, extravagant, unnecessary | 反义词：minimal, insufficient, inadequate
