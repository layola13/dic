rumple:/ˈrʌmpl/| v. n. adj.|弄皱；褶皱；凌乱的|To make something wrinkled or uneven; a wrinkle or unevenness; untidy or disheveled. |例句：He rumpled his shirt. 他弄皱了他的衬衫。
The rumpled bedsheets showed someone had slept there.  皱巴巴的床单表明有人在那里睡过。

近义词：crease, wrinkle, crumple, muss, dishevel |反义词：smooth, straighten, iron
