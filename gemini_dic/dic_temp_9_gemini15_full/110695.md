linear-momentum: /ˈlɪniər məˈmɛntəm/ | n. | 线动量 |

英文解析:Linear momentum is a vector quantity, meaning it has both magnitude and direction. It is the product of an object's mass and its velocity.  It describes the quantity of motion an object possesses.  A change in linear momentum requires a net force acting on the object.

例句:
The linear momentum of a moving car is significantly greater than that of a bicycle at the same speed.  一辆以相同速度行驶的汽车的线动量远大于自行车的线动量。

The conservation of linear momentum is a fundamental principle in physics. 线动量守恒是物理学中的一个基本原理。


近义词:quantity of motion (运动量)


反义词:None (线动量没有直接的反义词，其大小可以为零，但不存在相反的线动量)


词源:
* **linear:** From Latin *linearis*, from *linea* "line."  This refers to the straight-line nature of the momentum considered in classical mechanics.
* **momentum:** From Latin *momentum*, meaning "movement, impetus, importance." This reflects the concept of the quantity of motion.
