pavane:/pæˈvɑːn/|/pəˈvɑːn/| n. |帕瓦纳舞曲|A pavane is a slow, stately dance of Spanish origin, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries. It is typically in 4/4 time and is characterized by its measured pace and dignified style.  The music is often solemn and majestic. | The dancers performed a graceful pavane to the court.  舞者们为宫廷表演了一支优雅的帕瓦纳舞。  The composer wrote a beautiful pavane for the string quartet. 作曲家为弦乐四重奏创作了一首优美的帕瓦纳舞曲。|minuet, sarabande|  |noun|
