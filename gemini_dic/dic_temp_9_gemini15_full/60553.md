elapsed:/ɪˈlæpst/| adj. n.|过去的，消逝的；经过的时间|  The time that has passed since an event began or a period of time began.  |例句：Two hours elapsed before the rescue team arrived.  救援队到达之前过去了两个小时。

例句：The elapsed time between the two events was significant.  这两个事件之间经过的时间相当长。

近义词：passed, gone, spent | 反义词：future, upcoming

词性: Adjective, Noun
