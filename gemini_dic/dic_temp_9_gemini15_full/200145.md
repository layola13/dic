skillfulness:/ˈskɪlfʊlnəs/| n. |熟练；技巧；技艺|The skillfulness of the surgeon was evident in the smooth and precise operation.  外科医生的熟练技术在流畅而精确的手术中显而易见。|近义词：expertise, proficiency, dexterity, mastery|反义词：clumsiness, ineptitude


skillfulness:  This noun refers to the quality of possessing or exhibiting skill; expertise; dexterity. It implies a high level of competence and ability in a particular area.  It suggests a refined and polished execution of a task, often implying grace and efficiency.


例句：

Her skillfulness at playing the piano was breathtaking. 她精湛的钢琴演奏技巧令人叹为观止。

The chef's skillfulness in preparing the dish was evident in its exquisite taste and presentation.  厨师在烹制这道菜时的技巧在其精致的味道和摆盘中显而易见。


近义词：

expertise (专业知识), proficiency (熟练), dexterity (灵巧), mastery (精通), adeptness (熟练), craftsmanship (技艺)


反义词：

clumsiness (笨拙), ineptitude (无能), awkwardness (笨拙), incompetence (无能)
