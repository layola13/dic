read-in:/ˈriːdɪn/ | n. |朗读会；集体朗读；(信息的)输入|A read-in is an event where people gather to read aloud, often poetry or other literary works.  It can also refer to the process of inputting information into a computer system.  |例句：The poetry society held a lively read-in last night. 诗歌协会昨晚举办了一场热闹的朗读会。
The software has a fast read-in function. 该软件具有快速的输入功能。|近义词：reading, recitation, input |反义词：output, write-out
