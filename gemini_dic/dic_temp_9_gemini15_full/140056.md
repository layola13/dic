oesophagus: /ɪˈsɒfəɡəs/ /əˈsɑːfəɡəs/ | n. | 食道 | The oesophagus is the part of the alimentary canal which connects the pharynx to the stomach.  It is a muscular tube that transports food from the mouth to the stomach through peristalsis. | Example:  The food bolus travels down the oesophagus to the stomach.  食物团通过食道到达胃部。  |近义词：gullet | 反义词：无
