completely:/kəmˈpliːtli/ | adv. | 完全地，彻底地 | Completely means in every way or to the greatest extent possible. It indicates a total or absolute degree. |例句：I completely forgot about the meeting. 我完全忘记了会议。

例句：The task was completely finished before the deadline. 任务在截止日期前完全完成。


近义词：totally, entirely, wholly, fully, utterly | 反义词：partially, incompletely, slightly


词源:  The word "completely" is derived from the Old French word "completer," meaning "to complete."  The "ly" suffix is an adverbial suffix indicating manner.  The word's evolution traces back to the Latin word "complere," meaning "to fill up," "to complete," or "to fulfill."
