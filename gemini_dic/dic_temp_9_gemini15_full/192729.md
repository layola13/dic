sea-walled:/ˈsiːwɔːld/| adj.| 海墙环绕的；建有海堤的 | Having walls built to protect against the sea.  |The sea-walled city was protected from the worst of the storm.  海墙环绕的城市免受了风暴的最严重影响。| protected, defended, fortified | undefended, exposed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sea-walled city protects its citizens from flooding. = 海墙环绕的城市保护其公民免受洪水侵害。
This area is sea-walled to prevent erosion. = 这个区域建有海堤以防止侵蚀。


一般过去时:
The ancient city was once sea-walled, but the walls have since crumbled. = 古城曾经有海墙环绕，但城墙后来坍塌了。
They sea-walled the harbor to protect the boats. = 他们修建了港口的海堤来保护船只。


现在完成时:
The government has sea-walled many coastal areas to prevent further damage. = 政府已经为许多沿海地区修建了海堤，以防止进一步的损害。
They have sea-walled the entire coastline. = 他们已经为整个海岸线修建了海堤。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, they had sea-walled the vulnerable areas. = 在风暴来临之前，他们已经为脆弱地区修建了海堤。
The villagers had already sea-walled their town before the typhoon hit. = 在台风袭击之前，村民们已经为他们的城镇修建了海堤。


将来时:
They will sea-wall the new development to protect it from rising sea levels. = 他们将为新的开发项目修建海堤，以保护其免受海平面上升的影响。
The city council will sea-wall the low-lying areas next year. = 市议会将在明年为低洼地区修建海堤。


虚拟语气:
If they had sea-walled the coast earlier, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果他们早些时候为海岸修建海堤，损失就不会那么严重了。
It would be wise to sea-wall the entire coastline, if we could afford it.  如果我们负担得起，为整个海岸线修建海堤将是明智之举。


被动语态:
Many coastal communities are being sea-walled as a precaution against rising tides. = 许多沿海社区正在修建海堤，以防范涨潮。
The harbor was sea-walled in the early 20th century. = 港口在20世纪初修建了海堤。


疑问句:
Is the town sea-walled? = 这个城镇有海墙吗？

否定句:
The old town is not sea-walled. = 老城没有海墙。
That part of the coast has not been sea-walled yet. = 那部分海岸还没有修建海堤。
