faraway: /ˌfɑːrəˈweɪ/ | adj. |遥远的，久远的，疏远的 |  Describes something that is a great distance away, either physically or figuratively. It can also suggest a feeling of remoteness or detachment. | 例句：The faraway mountains were shrouded in mist.  遥远的山峦笼罩在薄雾中。

The faraway look in her eyes suggested she was lost in thought. 她眼神中遥远的神情暗示着她陷入了沉思。


近义词：distant, remote, far-off, secluded

反义词：nearby, close, proximate, near


词性:形容词
