expressionisms:/ɪkˈspreʃənɪzəm/| n.| 表现主义；表现派作品 |A style of art, literature, and music characterized by a subjective and emotional approach, often with distortion of form and exaggeration of expression to communicate inner feelings.  It rejects objective representation and emphasizes the artist's inner world.|The paintings showed the strong expressionism of the artist's inner turmoil.|近义词：expressionistic art, emotionalism |反义词：realism, classicism
