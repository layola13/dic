/kiː/| n. |气，活力；精神；力量 |  The word "ki" (also spelled "qi," "chi," or "qì") refers to a fundamental life force or vital energy in various East Asian philosophies and practices, including traditional Chinese medicine, martial arts, and spirituality. It's believed to flow through the body and influence health and well-being.  | Examples:  He felt a surge of ki as he prepared for the battle. 他在准备战斗时感到一股强大的气。  The practice of Qigong aims to cultivate and balance ki within the body.  气功的练习旨在培养和平衡体内的气。 | 近义词: energy, vitality, spirit, life force | 反义词: weakness, lethargy, exhaustion | 名词
