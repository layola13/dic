de-democratization:/ˌdiːdɪˌmɒkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去民主化；民主倒退 | The process by which a country or society moves away from democratic principles and practices, often towards authoritarianism or totalitarianism.  This can involve suppressing political freedoms, undermining independent institutions, and concentrating power in the hands of a few. | Example: The country experienced a rapid de-democratization after the military coup.  这个国家在军事政变后经历了快速的去民主化。  |近义词：authoritarianism, autocratization,  dictatorship |反义词：democratization
