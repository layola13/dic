mortal:/ˈmɔːrtl/| adj., n.|凡人；致命的；终有一死的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "mortal" means subject to death; not immortal.  As a noun, it refers to a human being, emphasizing their mortality.


例句:
All mortals shall one day die. = 所有凡人都终有一死。

He received a mortal wound in the battle. = 他在战斗中受了致命的伤。


近义词:
adj.: deadly, fatal, lethal, human
n.: human being, person


反义词:
adj.: immortal, deathless, divine
n.: god, deity


词性:
adjective, noun
