note:/noʊt/|n.|笔记，注释，便条，钞票|a brief record of something written down to assist the memory or for future reference.|I made a note of the address. 我记下了地址。|memo, memorandum, remark|—|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I note the details carefully. = 我仔细地记下了细节。
She notes down every important point. = 她记下每一个重点。


一般过去时:
He noted the time of the meeting. = 他记下了会议的时间。
They noted the changes in the document. = 他们注意到了文件中的更改。


现在完成时:
I have noted all the key information. = 我已经记下了所有关键信息。
She has noted down the speaker's main points. = 她已经记下了演讲者的主要观点。


过去完成时:
He had noted the discrepancy before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经注意到了差异。
They had noted the problem earlier. = 他们早些时候就已经注意到了这个问题。


将来时:
I will note the results of the experiment. = 我将记录实验的结果。
She will note any significant changes. = 她会记录下任何重大变化。


虚拟语气:
If I noted the details, I could answer your question. = 如果我记下了细节，我就能回答你的问题。
I wish I had noted the address. = 我真希望我记下了地址。


被动语态:
The changes were noted by the manager. = 经理注意到了这些变化。
The important points were noted in the minutes. = 会议记录中记下了重点。


疑问句:
Did you note anything unusual? = 你注意到什么异常了吗？
Have you noted the deadline? = 你注意到截止日期了吗？


否定句:
I did not note the change in the schedule. = 我没有注意到日程安排的更改。
She didn't note the subtle differences. = 她没有注意到细微的差别。


PHRASE->
make a note of = 记下…
take note of = 注意…
note down = 记下…
side note = 附注
a promissory note = 期票
