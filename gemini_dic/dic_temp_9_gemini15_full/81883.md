grieves:/ɡriːvz/| v.|悲伤，忧愁|To feel or express deep sorrow or distress.|例句：She grieves for her lost son. 她为失去的儿子而悲伤。|例句：He grieves the loss of his job. 他为失去工作而悲伤。|近义词：mourns, laments, sorrows |反义词：rejoices, celebrates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grieves the loss of his pet. = 他为失去宠物而悲伤。
She grieves every day since her husband died. = 自从她丈夫去世后，她每天都悲伤。

一般过去时:
They grieved deeply after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们深感悲痛。
She grieved the loss of her childhood home. = 她为失去童年时的家而悲伤。

现在完成时:
He has grieved for months after the breakup. = 分手后，他已经悲伤了好几个月了。
She has grieved over the loss of her job for weeks. = 她为失去工作而悲伤了好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
They had grieved for years before they finally accepted the loss. = 在他们最终接受失去之前，他们已经悲伤了好几年了。
She had grieved silently for many months before seeking help. = 在寻求帮助之前，她默默地悲伤了好几个月。


将来时:
He will grieve the loss of his grandfather. = 他将会为失去祖父而悲伤。
She will grieve deeply when her friend moves away. = 当她的朋友搬走时，她会非常悲伤。


虚拟语气:
If he had known, he would have grieved more openly. = 如果他知道的话，他会更公开地表达悲伤。
I wish I hadn't caused her so much grief; she is grieving terribly. = 我真希望我没有给她带来这么多的悲伤；她现在非常悲痛。


被动语态:
The loss was deeply grieved by everyone. = 损失让每个人都深感悲痛。（很少用被动语态）
His death was grieved by his entire family. = 他的死让他的整个家庭都悲痛万分。（很少用被动语态）


疑问句:
Does he grieve the loss of his friend? = 他为失去朋友而悲伤吗？
How long will she grieve over this? = 她会为此悲伤多久？


否定句:
He doesn't grieve openly; he keeps his emotions bottled up. = 他不公开表达悲伤；他把情绪压抑在心里。
She didn't grieve as much as people expected her to. = 她没有像人们预期的那样悲伤。


PHRASE->
grieve over: 为...而悲伤
grieve for: 为...而悲伤
grieve the loss of: 为失去...而悲伤
grieve deeply: 深感悲痛
grieve silently: 默默悲伤
