completed:/kəmˈpliːtɪd/ | adj. | 完成的，结束的 |  Completed refers to something that has been finished or brought to an end. It implies a thoroughness and finality to the action.  | The project was completed on time.  项目按时完成了。| finished, concluded, finalized | incomplete, unfinished, ongoing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The task is completed. = 任务完成了。
He completes his work every day. = 他每天都完成他的工作。


一般过去时:
She completed the marathon. = 她完成了马拉松比赛。
They completed the project last week. = 他们上周完成了项目。


现在完成时:
I have completed my homework. = 我已经完成了我的家庭作业。
We have completed all the necessary preparations. = 我们已经完成了所有必要的准备工作。


过去完成时:
He had completed the report before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经完成了报告。
She had completed her studies before she got the job. = 她获得工作之前已经完成了学业。


将来时:
They will complete the construction next year. = 他们明年将完成建设。
We will have completed the project by the end of the month. = 我们将在月底前完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had completed the assignment, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我完成了作业，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had completed my thesis earlier. = 我希望我早点完成了我的论文。


被动语态:
The building was completed in 2020. = 这座建筑于2020年竣工。
The report is being completed now. = 报告正在完成中。


疑问句:
Is the task completed? = 任务完成了？
Has the application been completed? = 申请表填好了吗？


否定句:
The work is not completed yet. = 工作还没有完成。
He hasn't completed his degree. = 他还没有完成他的学位。
