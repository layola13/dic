词源：
The word "multiuser" is a compound word.  "Multi-" is a prefix meaning "many" or "multiple," derived from Latin "multus" (much, many). "User" comes from the Old French "usager," meaning "user," ultimately from Latin "usus" (use).  Therefore, "multiuser" literally means "having many users."
