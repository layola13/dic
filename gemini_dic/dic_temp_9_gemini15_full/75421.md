frugivora:/fruːˈdʒɪvərə/| n.|食果动物|A frugivore is an animal that feeds mainly on fruit.  Frugivores play a vital role in seed dispersal in many ecosystems.  |Example: Many bats are frugivores, contributing to the spread of seeds in tropical rainforests.|近义词：fruit-eater|反义词：carnivore, herbivore
