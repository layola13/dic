non-influential: /ˌnɒnˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 无影响力的，不起作用的 |  Lacking influence or power; unimportant.  |  His opinion was non-influential in the final decision. 他的意见在最终决定中不起作用。  The non-influential politician quickly faded into obscurity.  这位没有影响力的政客很快销声匿迹了。 | influential, powerful | influential, important, significant
