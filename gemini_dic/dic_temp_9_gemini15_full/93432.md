illuminated:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|被照亮的，阐明的，启发的|Shining brightly; lit up.  To make something clear or understandable; to enlighten.|例句：The city was illuminated by thousands of lights.  这座城市被数千盏灯照亮了。|例句：His speech illuminated the complex issue. 他的演讲阐明了这个复杂的问题。|近义词：lighted, brightened, enlightened, clarified|反义词：darkened, obscured, confused


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The moon illuminates the night sky. = 月亮照亮了夜空。
The sun illuminates the earth. = 太阳照亮了地球。


一般过去时:
The candles illuminated the room. = 蜡烛照亮了房间。
The artist illuminated the manuscript. = 艺术家为手稿绘制了插图。


现在完成时:
The new streetlights have illuminated the neighborhood. = 新的路灯照亮了这个街区。
The professor's lecture has illuminated the subject for me. = 教授的讲座使我明白了这个主题。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

If his explanation had illuminated the problem, I wouldn't be so confused. = 如果他的解释能阐明问题，我就不会这么困惑了。
