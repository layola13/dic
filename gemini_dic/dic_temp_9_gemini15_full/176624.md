reaccommodate:/ˌriːəˈkɒmədeɪt/| vt.|重新安排；重新安置；重新调整|To accommodate again or differently; to provide new or different accommodations for.|The hotel manager reaccommodated us in a quieter room after we complained about the noise.  酒店经理在我们抱怨噪音后，将我们安排到了一个更安静的房间。|The airline had to reaccommodate passengers after the flight was delayed. 航空公司在航班延误后不得不重新安排乘客。| accommodate, adjust, rearrange |dislodge, displace |动词
