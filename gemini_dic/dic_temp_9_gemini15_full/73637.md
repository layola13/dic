forfeiters:/ˈfɔːrfɪtərz/| n. |放弃者；丧失权利者|Those who forfeit something, such as a right, privilege, or possession, due to a violation of rules or conditions.  They are individuals who have lost something because of their actions or inactions.|例句：The forfeiters of the land were forced to leave their homes.  土地丧失者被迫离开家园。|例句：The team, declared forfeiters, lost all their points.  这支球队被判放弃比赛，失去了所有积分。|近义词：losers, relinquishers|反义词：winners, retainers
