lamenting:/ləˈmentɪŋ/| v. |悲叹；哀悼；抱怨|Expressing sorrow, grief, or regret, often in a mournful way.  It often involves expressing dissatisfaction or disapproval.|例句：She spent the evening lamenting the loss of her job. 她整晚都在为失去工作而悲伤。

例句：He was lamenting the lack of opportunities for young people. 他一直在抱怨年轻人缺乏机会。


近义词：mourning, grieving, bewailing, complaining, deploring

反义词：rejoicing, celebrating, praising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is lamenting the state of the economy. = 他正在悲叹经济现状。
They are lamenting the passing of their friend. = 他们正在哀悼他们朋友的去世。


一般过去时:
She lamented her lost youth. = 她为逝去的青春而悲伤。
He lamented his mistakes. = 他为自己的错误而后悔。


现在完成时:
We have been lamenting the poor weather all week. = 我们整个星期都在抱怨糟糕的天气。
They have lamented the decision for months. = 他们已经抱怨这个决定好几个月了。


过去完成时:
She had lamented the missed opportunity for years. = 她多年来一直为错失的机会而后悔。
They had lamented the loss before the funeral. = 他们在葬礼前就为失去而悲伤。


将来时:
I will lament this day for years to come. = 我未来几年都会为今天感到悲伤。
We will lament the outcome if the plan fails. = 如果计划失败，我们将为结果感到惋惜。


虚拟语气:
I would lament the situation if it were true. = 如果这是真的，我会为这种情况感到悲伤。
She wished she hadn't lamented so much. = 她希望自己没有那么悲观。


被动语态:
The loss of the ship was lamented by many. = 许多人都为船的沉没而悲伤。
The decision was lamented across the nation. = 全国上下都对这个决定感到惋惜。


疑问句:
Was she lamenting the lack of support? = 她是在为缺乏支持而抱怨吗？
Are they lamenting the outcome of the game? = 他们是在为比赛的结果而惋惜吗？


否定句:
He did not lament the outcome. = 他并没有为结果而感到惋惜。
She didn't lament her fate, instead she accepted it. = 她并没有为自己的命运而悲叹，而是接受了它。


PHRASE->
lament over = 为...而悲叹
lament the loss of = 哀悼…的损失
lament the death of = 哀悼…的死
lament one's fate = 悲叹自己的命运
lament the passing of = 哀悼…的逝去
