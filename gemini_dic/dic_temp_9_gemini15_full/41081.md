coppersmith:/ˈkɒpərsmɪθ/| n. |铜匠，铜器匠|A coppersmith is a person who works with copper, shaping and forming it into various objects.  They may make pots, pans, decorative items, or other copper products.  The term often evokes images of traditional craftsmanship and hand-made goods.|The skilled coppersmith hammered the copper sheet into a beautiful bowl.  这位技艺精湛的铜匠将铜板敲打成一个美丽的碗。|metalsmith, artisan| (no direct antonym, but a contrasting term could be goldsmith)|名词
