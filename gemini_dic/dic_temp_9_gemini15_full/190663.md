saudization:/ˌsɑːdiˈzeɪʃən/| n. |沙特化；沙特人化；沙特阿拉伯化|The process of replacing foreign workers with Saudi Arabian nationals in various sectors of the Saudi Arabian economy.|例句：The government's policy of saudization aims to reduce unemployment among Saudi citizens.  政府的沙特化政策旨在降低沙特公民的失业率。|例句：Saudization has had a significant impact on the job market in recent years. 近年来，沙特化对就业市场产生了重大影响。|近义词：nationalization, localization|反义词：expatriation, foreignization|名词
