day-trip:/ˈdeɪtrɪp/| n. |一日游，短途旅行 | A day-trip is a short journey, usually for leisure, that is completed within a single day, involving travel to and from a destination.  | We took a day-trip to the coast. 我们去海岸做了一日游。  | short excursion, outing | long journey, extended vacation | 名词
