indebting: /ɪnˈdɛtɪŋ/ | v. | 使负债；使感激 | To put someone into debt; to cause someone to feel gratitude. |  例句：The loan indebtted him to the bank. 这笔贷款使他欠了银行的债。  | 近义词：obligating, encumbering | 反义词：freeing, liberating | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is indebting the company significantly. = 这个项目正在使公司严重负债。
He is indebting himself to his parents by accepting their financial aid. = 他接受父母的经济援助，使自己欠下人情。


一般过去时:
The war indebtted the nation heavily. = 战争使国家背负了沉重的债务。
Her kindness indebtted me to her. = 她的好意让我对她感激不尽。


现在完成时:
The government has indebtted itself through excessive spending. = 政府通过过度支出而使自己负债累累。
We have indebtted ourselves to our friends for their help. = 我们因为朋友们的帮助而对他们心怀感激。


过去完成时:
They had indebtted themselves before the business even started. = 甚至在生意开始之前，他们就已经负债累累。
She had indebtted herself to her mentor for their guidance. = 她因为导师的指导而对其心怀感激。


将来时:
The new policy will indebt the country further. = 新政策将使国家进一步负债。
His actions will indebt him to his colleagues. = 他的行为将使他欠下同事的人情。


虚拟语气:
If he accepted the loan, he would indebt himself greatly. = 如果他接受贷款，他将负债累累。
If she had asked for help, she wouldn't have indebtted herself to others. = 如果她寻求帮助，她就不会欠下别人的情。


被动语态:
The company was indebtted by the failed venture. = 公司因投资失败而负债。
He was indebtted by the generosity of his friends. = 他因为朋友们的慷慨而心怀感激。


疑问句:
Will this decision indebt the organization? = 这个决定会使组织负债吗？
Did his actions indebt him to anyone? = 他的行为使他欠了谁的人情吗？


否定句:
The plan will not indebt the city. = 这个计划不会使城市负债。
His help did not indebt me. = 他的帮助并没有让我欠他什么。


PHRASE->
indebted to someone for something = 因为某事而感激某人
deeply indebted = 负债累累；非常感激
indebtedness to someone/something = 对某人/某事的感激之情
indebtedness to the nation = 对国家的债务
indebt oneself = 使自己负债
