reductive: /rɪˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 还原的；简化的；简略的 | Relating to or involving reduction; tending to simplify or oversimplify something.  | 例句：The reductive explanation ignored the complexities of the situation.  还原论的解释忽略了情况的复杂性。


例句：His reductive approach to history missed many important nuances.  他简化的历史方法忽略了许多重要的细微之处。


近义词：simplifying, simplistic, oversimplified, abbreviative


反义词：expansive, comprehensive, detailed, elaborate


词源：源自拉丁语 "reducere"，意为“减少，降低”。  "re-" 表示“向后，再次”，"ducere" 表示“引导，带领”。
