idolising: /ˈaɪdəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜 |  To admire and respect someone or something very much, often to an excessive degree.  |例句：She was idolising the pop star, buying all his albums and attending every concert. 她盲目崇拜这位流行歌星，买了他的所有专辑，还参加了他的每一场演唱会。

例句：The children were idolising their teacher, mimicking her every move. 孩子们盲目崇拜他们的老师，模仿她的每一个动作。


近义词：worshipping, venerating, revering, deifying

反义词：despising, scorning, contemning


词源:  From "idol" (a representation of a god, an object of worship) + "-ising" (present participle suffix). The root of "idol" traces back to Greek "eidolon" (image, phantom), ultimately related to the verb "eidos" (to see).
