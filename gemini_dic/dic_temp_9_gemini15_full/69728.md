feoffment:/ˈfiːfmənt/| n. |封土；授封；土地转让 |The legal transfer of land ownership, especially by feudal lords.|例句：The feoffment of the land was recorded in the manor court records. 这块土地的授封记录在庄园法庭的记录中。|近义词：grant, conveyance, transfer |反义词：repossession, forfeiture


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lord feoffs the land to his vassal. = 领主将土地授予他的封臣。
This process involves a formal feoffment ceremony. = 这个过程涉及一个正式的授封仪式。


一般过去时:
The king feoffed large tracts of land to his loyal supporters. = 国王将大片土地授予他的忠实支持者。
He feoffed his estate to his nephew before his death. = 他在死前将他的产业转让给了他的侄子。


现在完成时:
The family has feoffed the property to a charitable organization. = 这个家族已经将财产转让给了慈善机构。
They have feoffed several parcels of land over the years. = 这些年来，他们已经转让了几块土地。


过去完成时:
Before the war, he had feoffed most of his land. = 在战争爆发前，他已经转让了他大部分的土地。
The Duke had feoffed the property to his daughter long before his death. = 公爵在他死很久以前就已将财产转让给了他的女儿。


将来时:
The landowner will feoff the property to his son next year. = 地主明年将把财产转让给他的儿子。
She will feoff her inheritance to her chosen heir. = 她将把她的遗产转让给她选择的继承人。


虚拟语气:
If he had feoffed the land earlier, he would have avoided the dispute. = 如果他早些转让土地，他就能避免这场纠纷。
I wish I had feoffed the property before the tax increase. = 我真希望我在税收增加之前就把房产转让了。


被动语态:
The land was feoffed to the new owner. = 这块土地被转让给了新主人。
The property was feoffed under the terms of the will. = 该房产根据遗嘱条款被转让。


疑问句:
Was the land feoffed legally? = 这块土地的转让合法吗？

否定句:

The agreement did not include a formal feoffment. = 该协议不包括正式的土地转让。
