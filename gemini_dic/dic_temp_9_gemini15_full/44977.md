crystallinity's: /ˌkrɪstəˈlɪnətiz/ | n. | 晶体性；结晶度 | The state or quality of being crystalline.  It refers to the degree of structural order in a solid material, where atoms or molecules are arranged in a highly ordered, repeating pattern.  High crystallinity implies a strong, regular structure, while low crystallinity indicates a more disordered, amorphous structure. |

例句：

The crystallinity's of the polymer significantly impacts its mechanical properties.  = 聚合物的晶体性显著影响其机械性能。

The high crystallinity's of this material makes it strong and durable. =  这种材料的高晶体性使其坚固耐用。


近义词：crystallisation, crystallization (结晶), order (有序性)

反义词：amorphousness (非晶态), disorder (无序性)
