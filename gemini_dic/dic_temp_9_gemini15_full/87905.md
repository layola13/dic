hieroglyph:/ˈhaɪərəɡlɪf/| n. |象形文字|A hieroglyph is a character of a writing system, such as those of ancient Egypt and the Maya, that represents a word or a syllable by means of a pictorial representation.  Hieroglyphs are often complex and symbolic, and their interpretation can be challenging. |The Rosetta Stone helped decipher ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs.  |近义词：ideogram, pictograph|反义词：alphabet (in the sense of a phonetic writing system)
