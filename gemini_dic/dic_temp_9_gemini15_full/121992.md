misspecified:/ˌmɪsˈspesɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 错误指定的，错误说明的 |  Describes a model, variable, or parameter that has been incorrectly defined or specified.  This often occurs in statistical modeling or programming when the assumptions underlying the model do not accurately reflect the real-world data or process. |


例句：

The econometric model was misspecified, leading to inaccurate predictions.  经济计量模型指定错误，导致预测不准确。

The software's input parameters were misspecified, causing the program to crash. 软件的输入参数指定错误，导致程序崩溃。


近义词：incorrectly specified, wrongly defined, flawed, faulty

反义词：correctly specified, accurately defined


词性：形容词
