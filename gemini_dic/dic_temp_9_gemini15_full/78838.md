gimp:/ɡɪmp/| n. v.|跛行；残废；笨拙的人；(尤指)软件|A gimp is a person who limps or walks awkwardly, often due to a disability.  It can also refer to a clumsy or inept person.  More recently, it's become associated with the open-source image manipulation program GIMP (GNU Image Manipulation Program).|例句：He walked with a noticeable gimp. 他走路明显一瘸一拐的。|近义词：limp, hobble, cripple|反义词：strong, able-bodied
