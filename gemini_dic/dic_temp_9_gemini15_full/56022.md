do-with: /duː wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 处理；对付；应付；忍受 |  This phrasal verb implies dealing with something, often something challenging or unpleasant. It suggests managing a situation, problem, or person, often involving effort or patience.  |  例句：I don't know how to do with this difficult situation. = 我不知道该如何处理这个棘手的情况。
I'll do with a cup of coffee. = 我需要一杯咖啡。 | 近义词：handle, manage, cope with, deal with | 反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
How do you do with stress? = 你如何应对压力？
What do you do with your free time? = 你如何利用你的空闲时间？

一般过去时:
How did she do with the exam? = 她考试考得怎么样？
What did he do with the broken vase? = 他是怎么处理那个破碎的花瓶的？

现在完成时:
How have you done with the project so far? = 到目前为止你项目进展如何？
What have you done with my keys? = 你把我的钥匙放到哪里了？


过去完成时:
How had they done with the negotiations before the deadline? = 在截止日期之前，他们谈判结果如何？
What had she done with the report before the meeting? = 会议前她是怎么处理这份报告的？

将来时:
How will you do with the increased workload? = 你将如何应对增加的工作量？
What will you do with the extra money? = 你打算怎么用这笔额外的钱？


虚拟语气:
If I knew how to do with this problem, I would solve it. = 如果我知道如何处理这个问题，我会解决它。
I wish I knew how to do with this difficult person. = 我真希望我知道如何应付这个人。

被动语态:
The problem is being done with efficiently. = 这个问题正在有效地得到处理。
The situation was done with swiftly and decisively. = 情况得到了迅速而果断的处理。

疑问句:
How do you do with conflict? = 你如何处理冲突？
What are you doing with that knife? = 你拿那把刀做什么？


否定句:
I don't know how to do with this situation. = 我不知道该如何处理这种情况。
He didn't know what to do with the extra food. = 他不知道该如何处理多余的食物。
