overstatements:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪtmənts/ | n. |夸张的说法，言过其实 |A statement that exaggerates or magnifies the truth.  It is a statement that says more than is true or necessary. |例句：His claims about his achievements were pure overstatements. 他关于自己成就的说法完全是夸大其词。

例句：The advertisement contained several overstatements about the product's capabilities. 这则广告对产品的性能作了若干夸张的描述。


近义词：exaggerations, hyperboles, embellishments, inflations

反义词：understatements, downplaying, minimizing


词性：名词
