confucian:/kənˈfjuːʃən/| adj. n. |孔子的；儒家的；儒教的 |Relating to Confucius or his teachings; characterized by the principles of Confucianism.  |例句：Confucian philosophy emphasizes social harmony and ethical conduct.  儒家哲学强调社会和谐和道德行为。

例句：The Confucian temple is a significant historical site. 孔庙是一个重要的历史遗址。


近义词：Neo-Confucian (新儒家的)


反义词：None directly opposite, but terms representing opposing philosophies could be used contextually (e.g., Taoist, Buddhist).


词性：形容词，名词
