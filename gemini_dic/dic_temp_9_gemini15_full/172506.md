pyres:/ˈpaɪərz/| n. |火葬柴堆；火堆|A pyre is a large pile of wood built for burning a dead body, especially as a funeral rite.  The word often evokes images of ancient funeral practices and large-scale burning. |The body was laid upon the pyre and set alight.  尸体被放在火葬柴堆上点燃。| funeral pyre, cremation pyre |  | n.
