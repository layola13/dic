hangability: /ˌhæŋəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可悬挂性，易悬挂性 | The quality or state of being easily hung or capable of being hung.  It refers to how easily something can be hung, considering factors like its weight, shape, and the presence of suitable hanging mechanisms.  | Example: The artwork's hangability was compromised by its unusual shape and weight. 这幅艺术品的可悬挂性由于其形状和重量不寻常而受到影响。 |近义词: suspendability, hangableness | 反义词: unhangability
