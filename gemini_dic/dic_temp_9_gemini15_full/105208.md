keynote:/ˈkiːnoʊt/| n. |基调；主题演讲；要点 |The central theme or idea; a main speech at a conference or meeting. |例句：The keynote of his speech was the importance of education.  他的演讲的基调是教育的重要性。

例句：The keynote speaker at the conference was a renowned expert in artificial intelligence.  会议上的主题演讲者是一位著名的 AI 专家。


近义词：main point, central theme, leading idea, address, lecture

反义词：detail, minor point, aside

词性:名词
