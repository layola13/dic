observation:/ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃən/ /ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃən/| n.|观察；观测；备注；意见|The act of carefully watching someone or something.  It can also refer to something noticed or learned through observation.|例句：His observation of the bird's behaviour was meticulous.  他的鸟类行为观察非常细致。

例句：The doctor made several important observations about the patient's condition. 医生对病人的病情做了一些重要的观察。


近义词：notice, scrutiny, surveillance, remark, comment|反义词：neglect, disregard|词性：名词
