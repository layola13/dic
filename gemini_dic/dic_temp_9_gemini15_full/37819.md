compensations: /ˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃənz/ | /ˌkɑːmpənˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 补偿；赔偿；报酬 |  Something given to make up for something else, such as a loss, injury, or inconvenience.  It can refer to monetary payments, benefits, or other forms of redress. |

例句：

The company offered compensations to the victims of the accident. = 公司向事故受害者提供了赔偿。

He received generous compensations for his years of service. = 他因多年的服务获得了丰厚的报酬。


近义词：remuneration, recompense, indemnity, restitution, redress


反义词：penalty, punishment


词性：名词
