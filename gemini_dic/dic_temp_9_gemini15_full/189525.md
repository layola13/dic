词源:  The word "salamandroid" is a portmanteau (blend) of "salamander" and "android."  "Salamander" refers to a type of amphibian known for its regenerative abilities, while "android" refers to a humanoid robot.  The combination suggests a creature that blends biological and robotic characteristics.
