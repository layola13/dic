emulsify:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪ/ | vt. |乳化；使乳化 | To break a substance into very small droplets and disperse them throughout another substance, typically a liquid, forming an emulsion.  This process often requires the use of an emulsifier. | Example: The blender emulsified the oil and vinegar into a smooth dressing.  搅拌机将油和醋乳化成光滑的调味汁。

Example:  The egg yolks helped to emulsify the mayonnaise. 蛋黄有助于使蛋黄酱乳化。


近义词：mix, blend, homogenize | 反义词：separate, stratify
