Often used to describe something fleeting or transient. | Example: The meteorical shower was a spectacular sight.  流星雨是一场壮观的景象。  The meteorical rise and fall of his career was remarkable. 他事业的迅速兴衰令人瞩目。 |近义词：atmospheric, transient, ephemeral, fleeting | 反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring
