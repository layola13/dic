preedits:/ˈpriːˌɛdɪts/ | n. | 预先编辑；预编辑内容 | Pre-edits are changes made to a text before it is finalized.  预编辑是在文本定稿前所做的更改。 | The editor requested pre-edits from the author. 编辑要求作者进行预编辑。 |  proofreads, revisions |  final edits | 名词
