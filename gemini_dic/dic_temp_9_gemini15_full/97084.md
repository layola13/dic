infertile: /ɪnˈfɜːrtl/ | adj. | 不孕的；贫瘠的 | Unable to produce offspring; lacking fertility.  | 例句：The couple sought medical help for their infertility. 这对夫妇寻求医疗帮助以解决他们的不孕问题。  | 例句：The infertile land produced a meager crop. 这片贫瘠的土地只产出可怜的一点收成。 | 近义词：barren, unproductive, sterile | 反义词：fertile, productive
