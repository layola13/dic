psycholeptic: /ˌsaɪkoʊˈlɛptɪk/ | adj. |精神抑制的，镇静的 |  Describing a substance or agent that diminishes mental activity or excitement; having a calming or tranquilizing effect on the mind.  This term is less common than others with similar meanings, and often appears in older or specialized texts.  | The psycholeptic medication helped calm the patient's anxiety.  精神抑制药物帮助平息了病人的焦虑。|近义词：tranquilizing, sedative, calming, soporific |反义词：stimulant, excitatory
