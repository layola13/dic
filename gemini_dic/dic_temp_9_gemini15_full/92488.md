hypothecate:/haɪˈpɒθɪkeɪt/ | vt. |抵押；假设；假定 | To pledge (property) as security for a loan; to assume as a hypothesis.  |例句：He hypothecated his house to secure a loan. 他抵押了他的房子以获得贷款。

例句：The scientist hypothecated that the extinction of the dinosaurs was caused by a meteor impact. 科学家假设恐龙的灭绝是由陨石撞击造成的。


近义词：pledge, mortgage, assume, postulate | 反义词：unpledge, release


词源:  From Late Latin *hypothecāre, from Greek ὑποθήκη (hypothēkē) "a putting under, deposit, mortgage," from ὑπό (hupó) "under" + τίθημι (títhēmi) "to put, place."
