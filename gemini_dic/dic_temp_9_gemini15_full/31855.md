cheerfuller:/ˈtʃɪrfʊlər/| adj.| 更快乐的，更愉快的 | More cheerful; more joyful.  It's a comparative adjective, indicating a greater degree of cheerfulness than "cheerful." |例句：The weather was cheerfuller today than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更晴朗。  The children were cheerfuller after receiving their gifts. 收到礼物后，孩子们更开心了。|近义词：more joyful, merrier, happier |反义词：sadder, gloomier, less joyful
