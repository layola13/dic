missuppose:/ˌmɪs səˈpoʊz/| vt.|误解，误会|To have a wrong idea or understanding of something or someone.  It implies a misunderstanding based on incorrect information or assumptions.|例句：I missupposed his intentions; he wasn't trying to offend me. 我误解了他的意图；他并非有意冒犯我。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, misconceive|反义词：understand, comprehend
