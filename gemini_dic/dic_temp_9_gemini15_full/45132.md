cudded:/ˈkʌdɪd/ | adj. |反刍的，沉思的 |  Cudded refers to the action of chewing the cud, a process in which ruminant animals regurgitate and re-chew their food. It can also be used figuratively to describe someone lost in deep thought. | Example: The cow was cudded peacefully in the pasture.  那头牛在牧场上安详地反刍。 The artist sat cudded, lost in contemplation of his next masterpiece.  艺术家沉思着，沉浸在构思下一幅杰作的思绪中。|近义词：ruminating, contemplative, pensive | 反义词：unthinking, thoughtless |  adj.
