raps:/ræps/| n. v.|说唱；轻敲；斥责| 1. (n.) A style of music involving rhythmic speech that is often accompanied by a musical beat.  2. (v.) To deliver a rap song; to strike something lightly and repeatedly.|例句：1.(n.)  He's known for his fast-paced raps. 他以其快节奏的说唱而闻名。 2.(v.) The rain rapped against the window. 雨点轻敲着窗户。|近义词：(n.) rhymes, verses; (v.) tap, knock, rebuke|反义词：(v.) praise, compliment|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He raps about social injustice. = 他说唱社会不公。
She raps every night at the club. = 她每晚在俱乐部说唱。

一般过去时:
He rapped about his childhood. = 他说唱了他的童年。

现在完成时:
He has rapped at many concerts. = 他已在许多音乐会上演出说唱。
She has rapped with many famous artists. = 她已与许多著名艺术家合作说唱。


过去完成时:
He had rapped before a large crowd before. = 他以前曾在大型人群面前说唱过。
She had rapped the lyrics perfectly before the show. = 演出前她已完美地背诵了歌词。


将来时:
He will rap at the festival next week. = 他下周将在节日上演出说唱。
