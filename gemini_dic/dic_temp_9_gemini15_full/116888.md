mayday: /ˈmeɪdeɪ/ | int. | 求救信号 |  A distress call used in aviation and maritime contexts to signal a serious emergency.  It is derived from the French phrase "m'aidez," meaning "help me."  The word is not typically used in other contexts. |


例句：

The pilot shouted "Mayday! Mayday!" over the radio.  = 飞行员在无线电中大声喊叫“求救！求救！”

A mayday call was issued after the ship began taking on water. = 船开始进水后发出了求救信号。


近义词：SOS, distress call


反义词：无


词性:感叹词
