/ˈoʊvi/ | n. | 卵；蛋 |  The word "ovi" is a combining form meaning "egg".  It is used in scientific terminology to refer to eggs or egg-laying.  For example, "oviparous" means egg-laying. | Example: The ovi of the bird were carefully protected in the nest. 鸟蛋被小心地保护在鸟巢里。 | 近义词：egg (more common in general use) | 反义词：None directly, but could be considered the opposite of "viviparous" (live-bearing). | Noun
