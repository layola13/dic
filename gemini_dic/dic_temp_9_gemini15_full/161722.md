power:/ˈpaʊər/| n., v.|权力；力量；能力；动力；影响力 |The ability or capacity to do something or act in a particular way.|例句：The power of the president is limited by the constitution.总统的权力受到宪法的限制。|例句：The car has a powerful engine. 这辆车有一个强劲的发动机。|近义词：strength, authority, might, energy, influence |反义词：weakness, impotence, inability


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has the power to change the world. = 他有改变世界的力量。
This engine produces a lot of power. = 这台发动机产生很大的动力。


一般过去时:
She wielded her power ruthlessly. = 她无情地运用她的权力。
The old machine lacked power. = 那台旧机器缺乏动力。


现在完成时:
They have gained considerable power since the election. = 自选举以来，他们已经获得了相当大的权力。
The storm has cut off the power to the whole town. = 暴风雨切断了整个城镇的电力。


过去完成时:
He had lost his power before the coup. = 政变前他已经失去了权力。
The lights had gone out because the power had failed. = 灯灭了，因为停电了。


将来时:
She will have the power to veto the bill. = 她将有权否决该法案。
The new system will provide more power to the users. = 新系统将为用户提供更强大的功能。


虚拟语气:
If I had the power, I would end poverty. = 如果我有权力，我会结束贫困。
If the engine had more power, the car would be faster. = 如果发动机动力更强，汽车就会更快。


被动语态:
The country is run by a powerful government. = 这个国家由一个强大的政府管理。
The machine is powered by electricity. = 这台机器由电力驱动。


疑问句:
Do you have the power to do that? = 你有能力做到吗？
What is the power source for this device? = 这个设备的电源是什么？


否定句:
He doesn't have the power to influence the decision. = 他没有能力影响这个决定。
The signal is too weak; we don't have enough power. = 信号太弱；我们的功率不足。
