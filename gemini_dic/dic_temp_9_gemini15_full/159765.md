polyethene:/ˌpɒliˈiːθiːn/ /ˌpɑːliˈɛθɪn/| n. |聚乙烯|A synthetic thermoplastic polymer of ethylene, widely used in the manufacture of films, bags, bottles, and other products.  It is a versatile material known for its flexibility, durability, and low cost. |例句：Many plastic bags are made from polyethene. 许多塑料袋都是用聚乙烯制成的。|例句：The pipes are made of high-density polyethene.  这些管道是由高密度聚乙烯制成的。|近义词：polythene, polyethylene|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与其他材料如木材、金属对比)|名词
