hewed:/hjuːd/| vt.|砍伐；劈开；凿成；坚持；遵守|to shape or cut (something) with an axe, knife, or other sharp instrument; to adhere to or follow (something), especially a principle or belief. |例句：The sculptor hewed the statue from a single block of marble.  雕刻家从一块完整的汉白玉中凿出雕像。  He hewed to his principles despite the pressure.  尽管面临压力，他仍坚持自己的原则。|近义词：chop, cut, carve, cleave, adhere to |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->

He hews wood every day. = 他每天砍柴。
She hews a path through the jungle. = 她砍出一条路穿过丛林。
