rsvp'ing:/ˌɑːrsviːˈpiːɪŋ/| v.|回复邀请；回应；答复|The act of responding to an invitation, typically by indicating whether one will attend or not.  It's a shortened form of "responding to RSVP".|例句：I'm still rsvp'ing to the wedding. 我还在回复婚礼邀请。|例句：She's rsvp'ing to several events this week.  她这周正在回复好几个活动的邀请。|近义词：responding, replying, answering|反义词：ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am rsvp'ing to the party. = 我正在回复派对邀请。
They are rsvp'ing to all the invitations. = 他们正在回复所有的邀请。


一般过去时:
I rsvp'ed to the dinner last night. = 我昨晚回复了晚宴邀请。
They rsvp'ed to the conference earlier this week. = 他们在本周早些时候回复了会议邀请。


现在完成时:
I have rsvp'ed to three events this month. = 这个月我已经回复了三个活动的邀请。
She has rsvp'ed to the wedding and is excited to go. = 她已经回复了婚礼邀请，并且很期待去参加。


过去完成时:
I had already rsvp'ed before the deadline. = 我在截止日期之前就已经回复了。
They had rsvp'ed to the meeting before they received the agenda. = 他们在收到议程之前就已经回复了会议邀请。


将来时:
I will rsvp to the party tomorrow. = 我明天将回复派对邀请。
She will rsvp to the conference by Friday. = 她将在星期五之前回复会议邀请。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would rsvp sooner. = 如果我有时间，我会早点回复。
I wish I had rsvp'ed earlier. = 我希望我早点回复过。


被动语态:
The invitations were being rsvp'ed to quickly. = 邀请函正在被迅速回复。
All the RSVPs have been received. = 所有回复都收到了。(Note:  'rsvp'ing' itself isn't easily used passively)


疑问句:
Have you rsvp'ed yet? = 你回复了吗？
Are you rsvp'ing to the birthday party? = 你要回复生日派对邀请吗？


否定句:
I haven't rsvp'ed yet. = 我还没回复。
They didn't rsvp to the event. = 他们没有回复该活动的邀请。


PHRASE->
rsvp'ing to an event = 回复一个活动的邀请
rsvp'ing by email = 通过邮件回复
rsvp'ing by phone = 通过电话回复
rsvp'ing online = 在线回复
promptly rsvp'ing = 及时回复
