sharew:/ʃeər/| n. |股份；份额；共享；参与|A share is one of the equal parts into which the ownership of a company is divided.  Shares can be bought and sold on the stock market.|例句：He owns a significant share of the company. 他拥有公司很大一部分股份。|例句：We shared the cost of the pizza. 我们分摊了比萨的费用。|近义词：portion, part, stake|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They share a flat. = 他们合住一套公寓。
I share my toys with my brother. = 我和弟弟分享我的玩具。


一般过去时:
We shared a bottle of wine. = 我们一起喝了一瓶酒。
She shared her experiences with us. = 她和我们分享了她的经历。


现在完成时:
He has shared his knowledge with many students. = 他已经和许多学生分享了他的知识。
They have shared a lot of happy moments together. = 他们一起分享了许多快乐的时光。


过去完成时:
They had already shared the news before I called. = 我打电话之前，他们已经分享了这个消息。
She had shared her concerns with her friend earlier. = 她之前已经和她朋友分享了她的担忧。


将来时:
We will share the profits equally. = 我们将平均分配利润。
They will share their ideas at the meeting. = 他们将在会议上分享他们的想法。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would share my story with you. = 如果我有更多时间，我会和你分享我的故事。
I wish I could share your happiness. = 我希望我能分享你的快乐。


被动语态:
The profits were shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information was shared with the public. = 信息已与公众共享。


疑问句:
Do you share my opinion? = 你同意我的意见吗？
Will they share the expenses? = 他们会分摊费用吗？


否定句:
I don't share your views. = 我不同意你的观点。
He didn't share his secrets with anyone. = 他没有把他的秘密告诉任何人。
