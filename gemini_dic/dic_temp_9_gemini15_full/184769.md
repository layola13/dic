resupplier:/ˌriːsəˈplaɪər/| n. |再供应者，补给者 |A person or company that provides additional supplies or goods.  A resupplier ensures the continuation of essential materials or products.|例句：The army relied on a dependable resupplier to maintain its operations.  军队依靠可靠的补给者来维持其行动。

The expedition team contacted their resupplier to request extra food and fuel. 探险队联系了他们的补给者，请求额外的食物和燃料。|近义词：supplier, provider, replenisher |反义词：consumer, recipient
