globalizations:/ˌɡloʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 全球化现象 (复数) | The complex and multifaceted processes by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence or start operating on an international scale.  It refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through the exchange of goods, services, information, and ideas.  | Example: The effects of globalizations are seen in increased trade, migration, and cultural exchange. 全球化的影响体现在贸易、移民和文化交流的增加上。 | 近义词: internationalization, globalization (单数) | 反义词: isolationism, localization |  名词
