mutant: /ˈmjuːtənt/ | adj., n. | 突变体；变种；变异的 |

英文解析:  A mutant is an organism or a gene that has undergone a mutation.  A mutation is a change in the DNA sequence.  As an adjective, it describes something that has undergone mutation or is characterized by mutation.

例句:
The scientist studied the mutant strain of bacteria. = 科学家研究了这种细菌的突变菌株。

The X-Men are a team of mutants with extraordinary powers. = X战警是一个拥有非凡能力的突变体团队。


近义词: variant, aberration, anomaly, freak

反义词: normal, typical, standard, unaltered


词性: adjective, noun

This plant shows mutant characteristics. = 这种植物显示出变异特征。

PHRASE->

词源:
The word "mutant" comes from the Latin word "mutare," meaning "to change."  The suffix "-ant" indicates something that performs or causes the action.  Therefore, "mutant" literally means "something that changes" or "something that causes change."
