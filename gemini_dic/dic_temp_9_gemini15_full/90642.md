humanized:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzd/| adj.|人性化的；使人性化|  Made to have or show qualities considered typical of humans, such as kindness, compassion, or understanding.  It often implies making something more relatable or appealing to humans.|例句：The new software has a more humanized interface.  新的软件拥有更人性化的界面。

例句：The robot's movements were surprisingly humanized. 机器人的动作出奇地人性化。


近义词：personalized, personalized, compassionate, empathetic, humane

反义词：dehumanized, impersonal, inhumane, unfeeling, cold

词性:形容词

词源:
The word "humanized" is derived from the word "human," ultimately from the Latin word "humanus," meaning "human, belonging to mankind." The suffix "-ized" indicates the process of making something human or having the qualities of a human.
