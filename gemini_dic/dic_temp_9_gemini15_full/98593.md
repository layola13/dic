instroke:/ˈɪnstroʊk/| n.|划水(游泳时的一次划水动作)|The act of pulling an oar or paddle through the water, especially in rowing or swimming.  It refers to the propulsive phase of a stroke, as opposed to the recovery phase.|例句：Her instroke was powerful and efficient, propelling her through the water with ease.  她的划水有力而有效率，轻松地把她推进水中。

例句：The coach analyzed the swimmer's instroke to identify areas for improvement. 教练分析了游泳运动员的划水动作以找出改进之处。


近义词：stroke (in the context of swimming or rowing)


反义词：outstroke (the recovery phase of a stroke)


词性:名词
