glowing:/ˈɡloʊɪŋ/| adj.|发光的；容光焕发的；令人兴奋的|shining brightly; radiating warmth or happiness; extremely positive or enthusiastic. |例句：The fireflies were glowing in the twilight. 萤火虫在暮色中闪闪发光。

The glowing review praised the actor's performance. 这篇好评如潮的评论赞扬了这位演员的表演。|近义词：radiant, luminous, bright, enthusiastic|反义词：dull, dim, lackluster
