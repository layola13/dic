consigns:/kənˈsaɪnz/| vt. |托运；委托；交付；把…托付给|To entrust something to someone or something, especially for transportation or sale.  It implies a degree of responsibility and trust.|例句：The company consigns goods to distributors worldwide. 公司将货物托运给世界各地的经销商。

The manufacturer consigned a large shipment of shoes to the retailer. 制造商将一大批鞋子托付给零售商。


近义词：ship, entrust, commit, send, remit|反义词：retain, keep, withhold|动词
