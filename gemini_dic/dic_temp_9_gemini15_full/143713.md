outliner:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪnər/ | n. | 提纲编写者；大纲；轮廓线 | A person or thing that creates outlines;  a summary or plan that shows the main points of a topic or subject; a line indicating the outer edge or form of something. | Example:  He's a skilled outliner, able to create clear and concise outlines for complex topics. 他是一位熟练的提纲编写者，能够为复杂主题创建清晰简洁的提纲。


Example: The architect used an outliner to sketch the building's basic shape. 建筑师用轮廓线勾勒出建筑物的基本形状。


近义词:summarizer, planner, sketcher, drafter


反义词:detailer (in the sense of providing detailed information rather than a summary)


词性:名词
