foretelling: /fɔːrˈtɛlɪŋ/ | v. | 预言，预示 | Predicting or indicating a future event.  | The fortune teller was skilled at foretelling the future.  算命先生擅长预言未来。 | Predicting, prophesying, presaging |  Ignoring, disregarding | Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foretells the future. = 他预言未来。
She foretells the coming storm. = 她预言即将到来的风暴。

一般过去时:
The oracle foretold the king's demise. = 预言家预言了国王的死亡。
The prophet foretold a great famine.  = 先知预言了一场大饥荒。

现在完成时:
The ancient texts have foretold many events. = 古代文本预言了许多事件。
Scientists have foretold the rise in sea levels. = 科学家们预言了海平面上升。

过去完成时:
The seers had foretold the earthquake. = 预言家们已经预言了地震。
No one had foretold the sudden economic crash. = 没有人预言到突然的经济崩溃。

将来时:
Experts will foretell the outcome of the election. = 专家们将预测选举的结果。
The weatherman will foretell a sunny day tomorrow. = 气象员将预报明天阳光明媚。

虚拟语气:
If I could foretell the future, I would be rich. = 如果我能预言未来，我会很富有。
I wish I could foretell what will happen next. = 我希望我能预言接下来会发生什么。

被动语态:
The future was foretold by the mystic. = 未来被神秘主义者预言了。
The impending doom was foretold in the legends. = 迫在眉睫的厄运在传说中被预言了。

疑问句:
Can the fortune teller foretell my fate? = 算命先生能预言我的命运吗？
Does the crystal ball foretell the future accurately? = 水晶球能准确地预言未来吗？

否定句:
The fortune teller could not foretell the exact date. = 算命先生无法预言确切的日期。
The astrologer did not foretell the war. = 星象学家没有预言战争。
