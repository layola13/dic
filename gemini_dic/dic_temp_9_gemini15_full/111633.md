localize:/ˈloʊkəlaɪz/| /ˈləʊkəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使地方化；使本地化；确定…的位置 | To adapt something to a particular place or region, making it suitable for local conditions or customs.  It also means to find the exact location of something. | Example: The company localized its software for the Japanese market.  该公司将其软件本地化以适应日本市场。


The software has been localized for several countries. 该软件已针对多个国家/地区进行了本地化。


近义词: adapt, adjust, regionalize | 反义词: generalize, standardize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They localize their products for different markets. = 他们为不同的市场对产品进行本地化。
The doctor tries to localize the source of the pain. = 医生试图确定疼痛的来源。


一般过去时:
The company localized its website last year. = 该公司去年对其网站进行了本地化。
Researchers localized the tumor before surgery. = 研究人员在手术前确定了肿瘤的位置。


现在完成时:
They have localized their software for five different countries. = 他们已经为五个不同的国家/地区对其软件进行了本地化。
Scientists have localized the earthquake's epicenter. = 科学家们已经确定了地震震中的位置。


过去完成时:
The team had localized the problem before the presentation. = 团队在演示之前已经确定了问题所在。
They had localized the virus before it spread widely. = 他们在病毒广泛传播之前就已经确定了它的位置。


将来时:
We will localize the application for the European market next year. = 我们明年将把该应用程序本地化为欧洲市场。
They will localize the sound source using advanced technology.  他们将使用先进技术来定位声源。


虚拟语气:
If they localized the software, it would sell better. = 如果他们对软件进行本地化，它将卖得更好。
It would be helpful if we could localize the problem quickly. = 如果我们能够快速确定问题所在，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The game was localized for several Asian countries. = 该游戏已针对多个亚洲国家进行了本地化。
The source of the infection was localized by the health officials. = 感染源已被卫生官员确定。


疑问句:
Has the software been localized yet? = 软件已经本地化了吗？
Can we localize the problem to a specific component? = 我们能否将问题定位到某个特定组件？


否定句:
They haven't localized the application for the Chinese market yet. = 他们尚未将该应用程序本地化为中国市场。
The police couldn't localize the suspect's whereabouts. = 警方无法确定嫌疑人的下落。


PHRASE->
localize the problem = 定位问题
localize the damage = 确定损害位置
localize the infection = 定位感染
localize the search = 缩小搜索范围
localize the resources = 本地化资源


词源:
From "local" + "-ize" (a suffix meaning "to make or become").  The word reflects the growing importance of adapting products and services to meet the needs of specific regions and cultures.
