casings:/ˈkeɪsɪŋz/| n. |外壳；套管；枪弹壳；(香肠的)肠衣 |The protective covering or container of something.  It can refer to the outer covering of an object, such as a machine part, or the casing of ammunition.  It can also refer to the edible casing of sausages. |例句：The casings of the old computers were made of metal. 老式电脑的外壳是用金属做的。


The bullet casings littered the floor after the shootout.  枪战后，弹壳散落一地。


近义词：coverings, shells, containers, housings |反义词：interiors, contents
