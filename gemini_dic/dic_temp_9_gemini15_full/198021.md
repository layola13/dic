shockproofing: /ˈʃɒkpruːfɪŋ/ | n. | 防震, 抗震 | The process or quality of protecting something from shock or damage caused by shock;  materials or designs that are resistant to shock.  | 例句：The device features advanced shockproofing to protect sensitive components during transportation.  该设备具有先进的防震功能，可在运输过程中保护敏感部件。

The hard drive boasts excellent shockproofing, ensuring data integrity even during drops.  这个硬盘具有极好的防震性能，即使跌落也能确保数据完整性。| 近义词：impact resistance, vibration damping, protection | 反义词：shock susceptibility, vulnerability
