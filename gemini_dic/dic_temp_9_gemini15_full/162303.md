preaccords:/ˌpriːəˈkɔːrdz/| n. |预先协议；初步协议 |Preliminary agreements reached before a final agreement is made.  These agreements often outline the key terms and conditions that will be included in the final contract.  They are not legally binding until the final agreement is signed. |The two companies reached preaccords on the merger before engaging in formal negotiations.  两家公司在进行正式谈判之前就合并达成了初步协议。

The preaccords outlined the basic structure of the deal.  初步协议概述了交易的基本结构。|近义词： preliminary agreements, tentative agreements, understandings |反义词：final agreements, binding contracts |名词
