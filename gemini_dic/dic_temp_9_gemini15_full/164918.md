pregnancies:/ˈprɛɡnənsiz/| n. |怀孕次数；妊娠期 |The plural of pregnancy, referring to multiple instances of being pregnant.  It encompasses the entire period from conception to birth.  The term can be used in a medical context, referring to the physiological process, or more generally, to discuss a woman's experiences of being pregnant. |例句：She has had three pregnancies in the past five years. 她过去五年有过三次怀孕。

例句：The doctor carefully monitored all aspects of her pregnancies.  医生仔细监测了她所有怀孕期间的情况。


近义词：gestations (more formal) |反义词：None (antonyms don't exist for this concept) |词性：名词
