riffs:/rɪfs/| n.|反复出现的乐句，即兴演奏的乐段|A short, repeated musical phrase, often played on a guitar or other instrument, especially as an improvisation.  It can be a melodic or rhythmic pattern.|例句：The guitarist played some impressive riffs during the concert. 吉他手在音乐会上演奏了一些令人印象深刻的反复乐句。

例句：The song's memorable riff is instantly recognizable. 这首歌令人难忘的反复乐句很容易辨认。
