resprays:/riːˈspreɪz/| v.|再次喷洒；重新喷涂|To spray again; to apply a spray coating a second time.|例句：The farmer resprayed the crops after the rain washed away the insecticide.  农民在下雨冲走杀虫剂后再次喷洒了农作物。

例句：The artist resprayed the car to fix a chipped paint job.艺术家重新喷涂了汽车以修复磨损的油漆。


近义词：reapply, resprit, re-spray|反义词：remove, wipe
