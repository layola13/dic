interpolations:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊleɪʃənz/ | n. | 插值；内插；窜改；插入语 | The process of estimating values between known data points.  It can also refer to the insertion of new material into a text or other work. | Example:  The statistician used linear interpolation to estimate the missing data.  统计学家使用线性插值来估计缺失的数据。 The editor made several interpolations to clarify the author's meaning. 编辑做了几次插值以阐明作者的意思。 | 近义词：insertions, estimations, extrapolations (in some contexts) | 反义词：deletions, omissions
