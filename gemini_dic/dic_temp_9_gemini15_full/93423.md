illude:/ɪˈluːd/ | vt. |欺骗；迷惑；使…产生错觉 | To deceive or mislead someone, or to create a false impression.  | 例句：The magician illuded the audience with his skillful tricks. 魔术师用他娴熟的技巧欺骗了观众。

The shimmering heat illuded my eyes, making the distant oasis seem closer than it was.  闪动的热浪迷惑了我的眼睛，使远处的绿洲看起来比实际更近。|近义词：deceive, delude, mislead, trick | 反义词：enlighten, clarify, inform |动词
