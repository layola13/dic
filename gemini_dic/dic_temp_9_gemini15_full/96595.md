inductional: /ɪnˈdʌkʃənəl/ | adj. | 归纳的；感应的 | Relating to or involving induction; derived by induction.  The word refers to processes of reasoning from specific instances to general principles (deductive reasoning goes the other way). It can also refer to electromagnetic induction. |

例句：

The scientist used inductional reasoning to formulate a new theory.  = 科学家用归纳推理的方法制定了一个新理论。

His experiment demonstrated the inductional properties of the coil. = 他的实验证明了线圈的感应特性。


近义词：inferential, inductive

反义词：deductive


词性：形容词
