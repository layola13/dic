mirror: /ˈmɪrər/ | n. |镜子 |A reflecting surface, usually of glass coated with a metallic substance, that forms images by reflection.  |例句：I looked in the mirror and adjusted my tie. 我照镜子，整理了一下领带。|例句：The lake was like a mirror, reflecting the mountains. 湖面像一面镜子，倒映着山峦。|近义词：reflection, looking-glass |反义词：N/A
