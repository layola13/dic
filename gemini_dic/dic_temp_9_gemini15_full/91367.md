hydrostat:/ˈhaɪdrəʊstæt/| n. | 液压装置；静压计 | A device that uses the pressure of a fluid to perform work or measure pressure.  It often involves a contained liquid that is compressed or expanded to generate force or indicate pressure levels. | Example: The hydrostat in the agricultural machinery controls the hydraulic pressure for lifting and lowering implements.  液压装置控制农业机械的升降装置的液压。 |近义词：hydraulic press, pressure gauge |反义词：None specifically, but  mechanically-driven systems could be considered contrastive. |名词
