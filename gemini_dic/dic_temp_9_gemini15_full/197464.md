词源:
The word "sheepskinned" is a compound word. It combines "sheepskin" (the skin of a sheep, used to make parchment) with the suffix "-ed," indicating something is made of or covered in sheepskin.  The origin of "sheepskin" traces back to Old English "scēap" (sheep) and "skin" (skin).
