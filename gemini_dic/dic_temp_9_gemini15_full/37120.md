combusting:/kəmˈbʌstɪŋ/| v.|燃烧；爆燃；激动|The process of burning.  It involves a rapid chemical reaction between a fuel and an oxidant, usually oxygen, producing heat and light.  The term can refer to both controlled burning (like in an engine) and uncontrolled burning (like a fire).|例句：The engine was combusting fuel efficiently. 引擎正在高效燃烧燃料。

The bonfire was combusting merrily. 篝火熊熊燃烧。|近义词：burning, igniting, flaming|反义词：extinguishing, quenching
