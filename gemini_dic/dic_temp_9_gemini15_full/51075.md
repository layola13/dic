despairingly:/dɪˈsperɪŋli/| adv. |绝望地，灰心丧气地 | expressing or characterized by despair.  It describes an action or state performed or experienced with a feeling of hopelessness and loss of courage. |例句：He looked despairingly at the ruined house. 他绝望地看着那座毁坏的房子。

例句：She sighed despairingly, realizing the task was impossible. 她绝望地叹了口气，意识到这项任务是不可能的。


近义词：hopelessly, despondently, dejectedly

反义词：hopefully, optimistically, confidently

词性：副词
