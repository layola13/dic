inter-industry:/ˌɪntərˈɪndəstri/| adj.|跨行业的；行业间的| Relating to or involving more than one industry.  This term describes relationships, collaborations, or comparisons that span multiple industries. |例句：The inter-industry collaboration led to significant advancements in sustainable practices. 跨行业合作导致可持续实践取得重大进展。
例句：The report analyzed the inter-industry competition for skilled labor. 该报告分析了各行业对熟练劳动力的竞争。|近义词：cross-industry, multi-industry|反义词：intra-industry
