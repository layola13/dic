interregna:/ˌɪntərˈreɡnə/| n. |(权力)空档期，过渡时期，(统治的)间歇|A period between two reigns or regimes during which there is no ruler.  It often refers to a period of instability or uncertainty.|Example: The country experienced a period of interregnum after the king's death.|近义词：interim, hiatus, gap|反义词：reign, rule, governance
