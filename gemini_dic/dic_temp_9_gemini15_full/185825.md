revitalization: /ˌriːvaɪtəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 复兴；恢复活力；再生 | The act or process of revitalizing something; the state of being revitalized.  It often refers to the renewal or improvement of something that has become run-down, decayed, or obsolete.  | 例句：The city's revitalization project included new parks and affordable housing.  城市的复兴项目包括新建公园和经济适用房。

The revitalization of the old town brought in new businesses and tourists. 老城区的复兴吸引了新的企业和游客。| 近义词：renewal, regeneration, rejuvenation, rebirth | 反义词：decline, decay, deterioration |  名词
