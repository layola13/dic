palaeographer:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfər/| n. |古文字学家|A palaeographer is a scholar who studies ancient writings and scripts, particularly those from before the invention of the printing press. They analyze handwriting styles, decipher texts, and determine the authenticity and dating of documents.  |例句：The palaeographer meticulously examined the medieval manuscript, deciphering its faded script. 古文字学家仔细检查了中世纪的手稿，破译了其褪色的文字。

The palaeographer presented her findings on the newly discovered papyrus at the conference.  古文字学家在会议上展示了她对新发现的纸莎草文的发现。|近义词：scribe, codicologist, textual scholar|反义词：N/A
