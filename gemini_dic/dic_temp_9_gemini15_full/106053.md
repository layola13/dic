kneel:/niːl/| vi.|跪下，屈膝|To go down on one's knees.  The act of kneeling often shows respect, submission, or prayer.|例句：He knelt before the altar. 他在祭坛前跪下。|例句：She knelt down to tie her shoelaces. 她跪下来系鞋带。|近义词：bend, genuflect|反义词：stand, rise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kneels in prayer every morning. = 他每天早上跪着祈祷。
They kneel to show respect to the king. = 他们下跪以表示对国王的尊敬。


一般过去时:
She knelt down to pick up the dropped coin. = 她跪下捡起掉落的硬币。
The knight knelt before the queen. = 骑士跪在女王面前。


现在完成时:
He has knelt before the statue many times. = 他多次在雕像前下跪。
They have knelt for hours during the ceremony. = 他们在仪式期间跪了几个小时。


过去完成时:
She had knelt for so long that her knees were aching. = 她跪了这么久，膝盖都疼了。
The people had knelt in supplication before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，人们一直在祈祷。


将来时:
I will kneel before no one. = 我不会向任何人下跪。
They will kneel and pray for peace. = 他们将跪下祈祷和平。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would kneel and beg for forgiveness. = 如果我是你，我会跪下求饶。
I wish I had knelt and apologized sooner. = 我希望我早点跪下道歉。


被动语态:  (Kneel is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.)


疑问句:
Does he kneel during the service? = 他在做礼拜时会下跪吗？
Will they kneel before the altar? = 他们会在祭坛前下跪吗？


否定句:
He did not kneel during the anthem. = 他在奏国歌时没有下跪。
They refused to kneel before the tyrant. = 他们拒绝在暴君面前下跪。


PHRASE->
kneel down = 跪下
kneel before = 跪在...面前
fall to one's knees = 跪倒
get down on one's knees = 跪下
kneel in prayer = 跪着祈祷
