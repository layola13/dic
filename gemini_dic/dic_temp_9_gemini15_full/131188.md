nonbudgetary:/ˌnɒnˈbʌdʒɪtəri/ | adj. |非预算的；预算外的 | Not relating to or included in a budget.  This term describes items, activities, or funds that are not accounted for within a formal budget.  | Example: The company incurred significant nonbudgetary expenses due to unforeseen circumstances. 这家公司由于不可预见的情况而产生了大量的预算外支出。|近义词：extra-budgetary, off-budget |反义词：budgetary
