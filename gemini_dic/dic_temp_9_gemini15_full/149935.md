palliative: /ˈpælɪətiv/ | adj., n. | 缓解的；治标不治本的；姑息疗法 |

英文解析:  Relating to or denoting a treatment that relieves the symptoms of a disease or condition but does not cure it.  As a noun, it refers to a palliative treatment or medicine.

例句:

adj.
The medication provided only palliative relief from the pain. = 这药只能暂时缓解疼痛。

The government implemented palliative measures to address the economic crisis. = 政府实施了缓和经济危机的措施。


n.
The doctor prescribed a palliative to ease her suffering. = 医生开了缓解她痛苦的药。

Hospice care focuses on providing palliative care for terminally ill patients. = 临终关怀专注于为绝症患者提供姑息治疗。


近义词:  soothing, relieving, alleviating, mitigating, comfort,  anodyne (n.)

反义词: curative, remedial, restorative


词性: adjective, noun
