predelivering: /ˌpriːdɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/ | v. | 预先递送，提前交付 |  The act of delivering something before the scheduled or expected time.  It implies preparation and proactive action in getting something to its destination ahead of the normal process. | 例句：The company is predelivering orders to ensure timely product launch. 公司正在提前交付订单以确保产品及时上市。

The supplier is predelivering components to avoid production delays. 供应商正在提前交付零部件以避免生产延误。


近义词：advance delivery, early delivery, expedited delivery | 反义词：delayed delivery, late delivery


词性：动词
