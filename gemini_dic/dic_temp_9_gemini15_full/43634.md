creationisms:/kriˈeɪʃənɪzəm/| n. |神创论；创造论 |The belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.  神创论是相信宇宙和生物起源于神圣的创造行为，例如圣经中的记载，而不是通过进化等自然过程。|The debate between evolution and creationism continues to rage. 进化论和神创论之间的争论仍在继续。|evolutionism, intelligent design|evolution, naturalism|
