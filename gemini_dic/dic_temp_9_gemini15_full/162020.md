prayerless:/ˈpreər.ləs/| adj.| 不祈祷的；缺乏祈祷的 | Without prayer; lacking prayer.  The prayerless life often leads to spiritual emptiness.  不祈祷的生活往往导致精神空虚。|近义词：godless, irreligious |反义词：devout, pious


USAGE->

She remains prayerless despite her troubles. = 尽管她有很多麻烦，她仍然不祈祷。


一般过去时:
He was prayerless in his youth. = 他年轻时不祈祷。
They were prayerless for many years. = 他们多年来都没有祈祷。
