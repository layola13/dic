decursion:/dɪˈkɜːʃən/| n. |下倾；下弯；下垂|The arrangement of leaves or branches on a stem, where they gradually curve downward.  It describes a downward-curving or sloping arrangement, particularly of leaves or branches on a plant stem. |例句：The decursion of the leaves gave the plant a weeping appearance.  这植物的叶片下垂，呈现出垂柳状。|近义词：declination, droop, downward slope|反义词：ascension, upward slope,  erect|
