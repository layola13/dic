puncheon:/ˈpʌntʃən/| n.|大酒桶；冲床；木夯；|A puncheon is a large cask, typically used for holding liquids such as wine or spirits. It can also refer to a heavy tool used for driving piles or stakes into the ground, or a machine tool used for punching holes in metal.  |例句：The wine was stored in large oak puncheons. 这葡萄酒储存在大型橡木酒桶里。


The brewery used a puncheon to drive the stakes into the ground. 这家啤酒厂用木夯把桩打入地下。|近义词：cask, barrel, hogshead |反义词：small container, vial
