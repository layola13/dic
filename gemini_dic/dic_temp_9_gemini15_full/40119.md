contemplation:/ˌkɒntɛmˈpleɪʃən/ | /ˌkɑːntəmˈpleɪʃən/ | n. |沉思；冥想；注视 |The act of thinking deeply and carefully about something;  serious thought or reflection.  It can also refer to the state of being lost in thought, or a long and careful look at something. |例句：He sat in deep contemplation, lost in his own thoughts. 他陷入沉思，沉浸在自己的思绪中。


例句：After hours of contemplation, she finally made a decision.经过数小时的沉思，她终于做出了决定。


近义词：meditation, reflection, consideration, musing


反义词：action, haste, impulsiveness


词性：名词
