reoxygenate:/ˌriːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | vt. | 再充氧，使再氧化 | To supply oxygen again to (a tissue, organ, or organism).  This process is crucial in various medical contexts, particularly in the treatment of ischemic tissues where oxygen supply has been compromised. | Example:  The surgeons worked quickly to reoxygenate the damaged heart tissue. = 外科医生迅速工作以使受损的心脏组织重新充氧。

The damaged lung tissue needs to be reoxygenated. = 受损的肺组织需要重新充氧。


近义词：re-aerate, oxygenate | 反义词：deoxygenate, asphyxiate


词性：动词
