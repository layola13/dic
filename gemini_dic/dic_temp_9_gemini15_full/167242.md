preternaturalism:/ˌpriːtɜːrˈnætʃərəlɪzəm/| n. |超自然论；超自然现象说|The belief that events or phenomena exist that are beyond the laws of nature;  a philosophical or literary position that emphasizes the supernatural or inexplicable.  It often involves exploring themes of the uncanny, the mysterious, and forces beyond human understanding.|例句：The novel explores themes of preternaturalism, featuring ghosts, unexplained occurrences, and otherworldly beings. 这部小说探讨了超自然论的主题，其中包含鬼魂、无法解释的事件和超凡脱俗的存在。|近义词：supernaturalism, occultism, mysticism|反义词：naturalism, rationalism|名词
