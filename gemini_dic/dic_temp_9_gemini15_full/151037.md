parapetalous:/ˌpærəˈpɛtələs/| adj. |副萼的；副花冠的|Having or relating to petal-like appendages arising from the receptacle outside the calyx. These structures, often called para-petals, are distinct from true petals and sepals. They can play a role in attracting pollinators or providing protection.|The flower exhibited unique parapetalous structures, enhancing its attractiveness to insects.|近义词：petaloid (if referring to petal-like appearance)|反义词：sepalous (if referring to sepal-like structures)
