proattendance:/ˌproʊəˈtɛndəns/| n. |积极参与；高出席率|  The term "proattendance" is a relatively new coinage, combining "pro" (meaning "in favor of" or "supporting") and "attendance." It refers to a strong and positive attitude towards attending events, meetings, or classes, often implying a high rate of attendance.| Example:  The company implemented new incentives to encourage proattendance at team meetings. 公司实施了新的激励措施以鼓励团队会议的积极参与。|近义词: high attendance, strong participation, diligent presence|反义词: absenteeism, non-attendance, poor participation|名词
