electrification:/ɪˌlɛktrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |电气化；通电|The process of converting something to use electricity.  It refers to the widespread adoption of electricity for power and light.  This can encompass various aspects, from the installation of electrical grids and power plants to the conversion of appliances and machinery to run on electricity.|The electrification of the railway system improved efficiency and speed.  铁路系统的电气化提高了效率和速度。|The rapid electrification of the countryside brought electricity to remote villages.  农村地区的快速电气化使偏远村庄用上了电。|modernization, energization|de-electrification|名词
