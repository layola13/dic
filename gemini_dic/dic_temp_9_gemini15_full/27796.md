cannonade:/ˌkænəˈneɪd/| n., v.| 炮击；连续不断的轰击 | A continuous, heavy firing of cannons; a sustained bombardment.  Also, a prolonged and intense outburst of something (e.g., sound, criticism).| 例句：The enemy launched a cannonade against the city walls.  敌人向城墙发起了炮击。The speaker delivered a cannonade of criticism against the government.  演讲者对政府发起了猛烈的批评。| 近义词：bombardment, shelling, barrage, fusillade | 反义词：silence, lull, calm | 名词，动词
