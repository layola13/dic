personalities:/ˌpɜːrsəˈnælətiz/ | n. |性格；个性；人格；人物；名人 | The personalities of the team members are very different. |近义词：characters, temperaments, dispositions |反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the specific personality trait being discussed (e.g.,  introverted vs. extroverted).


英文解析：
Personalities refers to the distinctive qualities of a person's character, as they are perceived by others or as they reveal themselves in behavior or actions.  It can also refer to the famous or important people in a field or society.

例句：
The team's success is due to the diverse personalities of its members.  团队的成功归功于成员性格的多样性。

The book features interviews with several prominent personalities. 这本书刊登了几位知名人士的访谈。


词源：
From personality (16th century), from Late Latin personalitas, from Latin persona "person, character in a play," from Latin personare "to sound through, utter," from per- "through" + sonare "to sound."  The evolution of meaning from "mask worn by an actor" to "character of a person" reflects the gradual shift in understanding of identity and self-presentation.
