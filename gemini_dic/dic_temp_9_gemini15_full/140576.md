oligochete:/ˈɒlɪɡəkiːt/ | n. |寡毛类动物|A segmented worm belonging to the class Oligochaeta, characterized by a reduced number of setae (bristles) compared to polychaetes.  Oligochaetes include earthworms and many aquatic species. They play crucial roles in soil aeration and nutrient cycling. | Example: Earthworms are a common example of an oligochete.  蚯蚓是寡毛类动物的常见例子。|近义词: annelid (更广泛的分类)|反义词: polychaete (多毛类动物)|名词
