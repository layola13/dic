maddens:/ˈmædən/| vt.|使恼怒；使疯狂|To make someone extremely angry.|The constant noise maddens me.  这持续不断的噪音把我逼疯了。|The traffic maddened him. 交通堵塞让他抓狂。|annoy, irritate, infuriate|calm, soothe, appease|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The traffic maddens me every day. = 交通每天都让我抓狂。
This constant noise maddens the neighbours. = 这种持续不断的噪音让邻居们抓狂。


一般过去时:
The delay maddened the passengers. = 延误让乘客们抓狂。
His stubbornness maddened his wife. = 他的固执让他的妻子抓狂。


现在完成时:
His behaviour has maddened everyone. = 他的行为已经让每个人都抓狂了。
The heat has maddened the dog. = 炎热的天气已经把狗热疯了。


过去完成时:
The long wait had maddened the audience. = 漫长的等待已经让观众们抓狂了。
His constant interruptions had maddened the speaker. = 他不断的打断已经让演讲者抓狂了。


将来时:
The endless paperwork will madden him. = 无尽的文书工作将会让他抓狂。
This situation will madden everyone involved. = 这种情况将会让所有参与其中的人抓狂。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to act like that, it would madden her. = 如果他继续那样做，那会让她抓狂。
I wish that noise wouldn't madden me so much. = 我希望那种噪音不会让我如此抓狂。


被动语态:
He was maddened by the injustice. = 他被不公正对待激怒了。
The audience was maddened by the delay. = 观众们被延误激怒了。


疑问句:
Does the constant noise madden you? = 持续不断的噪音是否让你抓狂？
What maddens you most about this situation? = 在这种情况下，什么最让你抓狂？


否定句:
The news didn't madden him as much as he expected. = 这个消息并没有像他预期的那样让他抓狂。
The situation didn't madden her; she remained calm. = 这种情况并没有让她抓狂；她保持冷静。
