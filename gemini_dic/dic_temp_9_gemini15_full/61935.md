emulge:/ɪˈmʌldʒ/ | vt. | 排出；涌出；流出 | To pour out or discharge (a liquid); to exude or ooze.  | Example: The wound emulged a viscous fluid.  伤口流出粘稠的液体。  |近义词：exude, ooze, discharge | 反义词：absorb, retain | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The volcano emulges lava. = 火山喷出熔岩。
The plant emulges a milky sap. = 植物分泌出乳白色的汁液。


一般过去时:
The pipe emulged sewage into the street. = 管道向街道排出污水。
The abscess emulged pus. = 脓肿流出脓液。


现在完成时:
The wound has emulged blood for hours. = 伤口已经流血数小时了。
The damaged tank has emulged all its contents. = 受损的油罐已经排出所有内容物。


过去完成时:

The boiler had emulged steam for days before it failed. = 锅炉在故障前已经排放蒸汽好几天了。


将来时:
