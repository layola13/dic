configur:/kənˈfɪɡjər/ | vt. | 配置；组态；构成 | To arrange, order, or put together (something) in a particular way; to set up or adjust the parameters or settings of a system or device. | 例句：The technician configured the network for optimal performance. 技术人员配置了网络以获得最佳性能。

例句：The software automatically configures itself upon installation.  软件安装后会自动配置自身。


近义词：set up, arrange, adjust, organize | 反义词：disorganize, dismantle, disorder | 词性：动词
