counterview:/ˈkaʊntərvjuː/| n. |反驳的观点，反对意见|A counterview is an opposing viewpoint or perspective, often presented as a counter-argument to a prevailing or dominant opinion.|Example:  The article presented a compelling counterview to the government's proposed policy. 这篇文章对政府提出的政策提出了令人信服的反驳意见。|近义词：counterargument, opposing view, contrary opinion|反义词：supporting view, corroborating evidence|名词
