ground-breaking:/ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ/| adj. |开创性的，突破性的|  Innovative and important; creating new standards or methods.  This adjective describes something that is significantly new, different, and impactful, often leading to major advancements in a particular field. |例句：Her research was ground-breaking in the field of genetic engineering.  她的研究在基因工程领域具有开创性。


例句：The new drug is a ground-breaking treatment for cancer. 这种新药是癌症的突破性疗法。


近义词：innovative, pioneering, revolutionary, transformative, epoch-making


反义词：conventional, traditional, outdated, commonplace


词性:形容词
