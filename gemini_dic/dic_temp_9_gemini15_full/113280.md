词源:
From *lutein* (referring to lutein, a yellow pigment found in the corpus luteum) + *-izing* (suffix forming verbs and adjectives denoting action or process).  The corpus luteum is a temporary endocrine structure in the ovary that is crucial for reproductive function.  The naming reflects the hormone's role in the development and function of this structure.
