inroads:/ˈɪnˌroʊdz/ | n. | 侵蚀；进展；突破 |  Progress made into something new or difficult.  Inroads can refer to both literal and figurative progress, often implying a gradual but significant advance. | 例句：The company has made significant inroads into the Asian market. 这家公司已成功打入亚洲市场。

The new marketing campaign has made inroads into the younger demographic. 新的营销活动已成功吸引了年轻一代的顾客。

| 近义词：advance, progress, inroads into, penetration, breakthrough | 反义词：setback, regression, failure | 词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes inroads into new markets every year. = 这家公司每年都进军新的市场。
Our team is making inroads into solving this complex problem. = 我们的团队正在逐步解决这个复杂的问题。


一般过去时:
They made significant inroads in reducing poverty in the region. = 他们在减少该地区贫困方面取得了重大进展。
The army made inroads into enemy territory. = 军队向敌方领土推进。


现在完成时:
We have made significant inroads in understanding the disease. = 我们在了解这种疾病方面取得了重大进展。
The researchers have made inroads in developing a new vaccine. = 研究人员在研制新疫苗方面取得了进展。


过去完成时:
Before the new CEO arrived, little headway had been made;  no significant inroads had been made into the problem. = 在新任首席执行官到来之前，几乎没有取得进展；这个问题上没有取得任何重大突破。
By the time the project ended, they had made considerable inroads into the research. = 到项目结束时，他们在研究方面取得了相当大的进展。


将来时:
We will make inroads into the new technology sector next year. = 我们明年将进军新兴科技领域。
The government hopes to make inroads in combating climate change. = 政府希望在应对气候变化方面取得进展。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could make greater inroads into this research. = 如果我们有更多的资金，我们就能在这个研究领域取得更大的进展。
I wish we had made more inroads into the project earlier. = 我希望我们早些时候在项目中取得更多进展。


被动语态:
Great inroads were made in reducing pollution levels. = 在降低污染水平方面取得了巨大进展。
Significant inroads are being made in the fight against cancer. = 在抗击癌症的斗争中正在取得显著进展。


疑问句:
What inroads have we made so far? = 到目前为止我们取得了什么进展？
What inroads are they planning to make in the next quarter? = 他们计划在下个季度取得什么进展？


否定句:
We haven't made much inroads into the problem yet. = 我们在这个问题上还没有取得多大进展。
They made no inroads into solving the dispute. = 他们未能解决这场争端。


PHRASE->
make inroads into = 取得进展，取得突破
gain inroads = 取得进展
significant inroads = 重大进展
inroads in = 在...方面取得进展
limited inroads = 有限的进展
