rookies:/ˈrʊkiːz/| n.| 新手，菜鸟 | New people to a job, organization, or activity.  Typically used for people in their first year. |例句：The rookies on the team are still learning the ropes.  球队里的新人们仍在学习经验。

例句：This year's rookie class is full of potential stars. 今年的新秀阵容充满了潜在的明星球员。


近义词：newbies, beginners, novices, tyros


反义词：veterans, experts, professionals


词性:名词


USAGE->
