jaywalk:/ˈdʒeɪwɔːk/| v.|非法穿越马路|To cross a street illegally, without using a pedestrian crossing.  This is often done in disregard for traffic signals or rules.|例句：He was caught jaywalking and received a fine. 他因非法穿越马路而被罚款。

例句：It's dangerous to jaywalk, especially in busy streets. 在繁忙的街道上非法穿越马路很危险。


近义词：cross illegally, disregard traffic signals,  step into the street illegally


反义词：use a crosswalk, obey traffic laws
