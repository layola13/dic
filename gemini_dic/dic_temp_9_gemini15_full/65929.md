exacerbating: /ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 加剧；恶化 |  To make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse. |

例句：

The drought is exacerbating the food shortage.  = 干旱加剧了粮食短缺。

His aggressive behavior is exacerbating the conflict. = 他咄咄逼人的行为加剧了冲突。


近义词：aggravate, worsen, intensify, heighten, compound

反义词：alleviate, mitigate, improve, ease, ameliorate

词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drought exacerbates the food shortage. = 干旱加剧了粮食短缺。
His actions exacerbate the problem. = 他的行为加剧了问题。

一般过去时:
The storm exacerbated the flooding. = 暴风雨加剧了洪水。
Her anxiety exacerbated her health problems. = 她的焦虑加剧了她的健康问题。

现在完成时:
The conflict has exacerbated tensions in the region. = 冲突加剧了该地区的紧张局势。
Years of drought have exacerbated water scarcity. = 多年的干旱加剧了缺水问题。

过去完成时:
The previous government's policies had exacerbated the economic crisis. = 前政府的政策加剧了经济危机。
His careless words had exacerbated the situation. = 他粗心大意的话加剧了局势。

将来时:
Further cuts will exacerbate the already difficult situation. = 进一步的削减将加剧已经艰难的局面。
The rising sea levels will exacerbate coastal erosion. = 海平面上升将加剧海岸侵蚀。

虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the situation will only exacerbate. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况只会恶化。
I wish the government hadn't exacerbated the problem with its policies. = 我真希望政府不要用其政策加剧这个问题。

被动语态:
The situation was exacerbated by the recent events. = 近期发生的事件加剧了局势。
The problem is being exacerbated by a lack of funding. = 缺乏资金正在加剧这个问题。

疑问句:
What factors are exacerbating the crisis? = 哪些因素正在加剧这场危机？
Is the current policy exacerbating the situation? = 当前的政策是否正在加剧局势？


否定句:
The new law did not exacerbate the problem. = 新法并没有加剧问题。
Their efforts did not exacerbate the situation. = 他们的努力并没有加剧局势。


PHRASE->
exacerbate the situation = 加剧局势
exacerbate the problem = 加剧问题
exacerbate tensions = 加剧紧张局势
exacerbate conflict = 加剧冲突
exacerbate the crisis = 加剧危机


词源:

From Latin *exacerbare, from ex- "out, completely" + acerbus "sour, bitter, harsh."  The sense development is from "make bitter" to "make worse."
