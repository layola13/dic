palaestra:/pəˈleɪstrə/| n. |角斗场；练习场；体育馆|A palaestra was an ancient Greek wrestling school or gymnasium, often associated with athletic training and physical education.  It was a place where young men trained in various sports and activities, including wrestling, boxing, running, and discus throwing. The term can also refer to the activity or practice of wrestling itself.|Example: The young athletes spent hours each day training in the palaestra.  年轻运动员每天在角斗场训练数小时。|近义词：gymnasium, wrestling school, training ground|反义词：N/A
