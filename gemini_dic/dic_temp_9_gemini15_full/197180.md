shaolin:/ˈʃɑːoʊlɪn/ | n. |少林 (寺), 少林拳 | Shaolin refers to the Shaolin Temple, a famous Buddhist temple in Henan province, China, renowned for its martial arts tradition.  The term also often refers to the unique style of martial arts developed there. |例句：He studied Shaolin Kung Fu for many years. 他学习少林功夫多年了。|例句：The Shaolin Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage site. 少林寺是联合国教科文组织的世界遗产。|近义词：temple (in the context of a religious building), monastery |反义词：无
