honour: /ˈɒnə(r)/ /ˈɑːnər/ | n. v. | 荣誉；尊敬；给予荣誉 |  The high regard and respect given to someone or something; a source of pride or distinction; to respect or admire greatly; to do someone honor; to fulfill (a promise or obligation).|


例句：

He received many honours for his bravery. = 他因勇敢而获得许多荣誉。

She was honoured to be invited to the ceremony. = 她很荣幸被邀请参加典礼。


近义词：glory, prestige, respect, dignity, esteem


反义词：dishonor, shame, disgrace, disrespect


词性:名词，动词


词源: 源自古法语 honor  和 古英语  honour,  均来自拉丁语 honor-, honos "honor, respect, glory".


USAGE->

一般现在时:

They honour their commitments. = 他们信守承诺。

We honour our ancestors' memory. = 我们尊敬祖先的记忆。


一般过去时:

The queen honoured him with a medal. = 女王授予他一枚奖章。

They honoured their agreement. = 他们履行了协议。


现在完成时:

She has honoured her promise. = 她已经信守了诺言。

He has been honoured with numerous awards. = 他获得了无数奖项。


过去完成时:

They had honoured their debt before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经还清了债务。

The company had honoured its contract. = 公司履行了合同。


将来时:

We will honour our guests with a banquet. = 我们将设宴款待我们的客人。

The government will honour its treaty obligations. = 政府将履行其条约义务。


虚拟语气:

If I were to win the award, I would feel truly honoured. = 如果我获奖，我会感到非常荣幸。

I wish I could have honoured his request. = 我真希望我能满足他的请求。


被动语态:

He was honoured for his contributions to science. = 他因对科学的贡献而获奖。

The agreement was duly honoured. = 协议得到适当履行。


疑问句:

Will they honour the contract? = 他们会履行合同吗？

How will you honour your mother's memory? = 你将如何纪念你的母亲？


否定句:

They did not honour their agreement. = 他们没有履行协议。

The company failed to honour its commitments. = 公司未能履行承诺。



PHRASE->

in honour of = 为了纪念…；向…表示敬意

on my honour = 我以人格担保

a matter of honour = 荣誉问题

bring honour to = 给…带来荣誉
