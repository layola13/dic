microwaves:/ˈmaɪkroʊweɪvz/ | n. |微波，微波炉 | Electromagnetic waves with wavelengths ranging from about one millimeter to one meter.  Microwaves are used in various applications, including cooking (microwave ovens), telecommunications, and radar. |例句：The microwaves heated the food quickly. 微波炉很快就把食物加热了。  |近义词：radio waves, electromagnetic radiation |反义词：None specifically, but can be contrasted with other forms of radiation like visible light or X-rays. |名词
