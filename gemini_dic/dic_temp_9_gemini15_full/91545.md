hyper-conservative:/ˈhaɪpər kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/| adj. |极端保守的，顽固不化的|Extremely conservative; unwilling to accept any change or new ideas.|The hyper-conservative policies of the government stifled economic growth.  政府极端保守的政策扼杀了经济增长。|近义词：reactionary, ultraconservative, rigidly conservative |反义词：radical, liberal, progressive
