dryas:/ˈdraɪæs/| n.|山柳|A genus of small, low-growing flowering plants in the family Rosaceae, characterized by their small, white or yellow flowers and often found in arctic and alpine regions.  Dryas are important indicators of past climates due to their sensitivity to environmental changes.|Example:  The presence of *Dryas octopetala* pollen in the sediment core indicates a period of colder climate.  (沉积物岩心中*八瓣山柳*花粉的存在表明一段时期内气候较为寒冷。) |近义词：mountain avens (同义词，更常用的俗称)|反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与热带植物作对比|名词
