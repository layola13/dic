shales:/ʃeɪlz/| n.|页岩；页岩层|A shale is a fine-grained sedimentary rock composed of mud that is a mixture of flakes of clay minerals and tiny fragments of other minerals.  Shales are characterized by their ability to split easily into thin layers. They are often found in layers called shale beds or shale formations, and can be various colors depending on their mineral composition.|例句：The geologist identified several layers of shale in the exposed cliff face.  地质学家在裸露的悬崖峭壁上发现了数层页岩。|近义词：mudstone, claystone|反义词：sandstone, conglomerate
