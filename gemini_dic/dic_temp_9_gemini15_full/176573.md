re-usable: /ˌriˈjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 可重复使用的，可再利用的 | Capable of being used again; reusable.  | 例句：This water bottle is re-usable. = 这个水瓶是可以重复使用的。

例句：Many plastic containers are designed to be re-usable. = 许多塑料容器的设计目的是可重复使用的。


近义词：recyclable, reusable, repeatedly usable,  re-employable


反义词：disposable, single-use, one-time use


词性：形容词


词源：

The word "reusable" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") to the adjective "usable" (meaning "capable of being used"). "Usable" itself comes from the verb "use"  + "-able" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  Therefore, "reusable" literally means "capable of being used again". The hyphen in "re-usable" is optional, but its use helps to clarify the meaning and distinguish it from words like "re usable."
