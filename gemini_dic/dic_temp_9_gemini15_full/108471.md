lay-line:/ˈleɪlaɪn/| n. |航线，大圆航线|A rhumb line or great-circle route plotted on a chart to show the most efficient course between two points, often used in sailing.  It is a line of constant compass bearing. |Example: The captain plotted a lay-line to reach the destination efficiently. 船长规划了一条航线以高效到达目的地。  The lay-line took them across the Gulf Stream.  这条航线带他们穿越了墨西哥湾暖流。|近义词：course, route, track |反义词：detour, indirect route |名词
