dermal:/ˈdɜːrməl/| adj. |皮肤的，真皮的 |Relating to the skin, especially the dermis (the inner layer of the skin).|例句：The new cream has a dermal effect, improving skin elasticity.  这款新面霜具有皮肤深层作用，能改善皮肤弹性。
例句：Dermal fillers are used to reduce wrinkles.  皮肤填充剂用于减少皱纹。|近义词：cutaneous, skin |反义词：epidermal (relating to the epidermis, the outer layer of skin)
