nonphobic: /ˌnɑːnˈfoʊbɪk/ | adj. | 不恐懼的，無懼的 | Not having or showing a phobia; free from phobia.  |  He's nonphobic about spiders, unlike his sister. 他不怕蜘蛛，不像他姐姐。 |近义词： fearless, unafraid, undaunted | 反义词： phobic, fearful, afraid
