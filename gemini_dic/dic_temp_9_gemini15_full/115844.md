markis:/ˈmɑːrkɪs/| n. |侯爵|A marquis is a nobleman ranking above an earl and below a duke.  The title is hereditary and is often associated with significant land ownership and social standing.|Example:  He inherited the title of Marquis from his father. 他从他父亲那里继承了侯爵的头衔。|近义词：duke, earl, count|反义词：commoner
