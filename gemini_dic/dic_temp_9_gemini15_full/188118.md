roundtrip:/ˈraʊndtrɪp/| n.|往返行程，双程旅行|A journey to a place and back again.|例句：The roundtrip flight cost $500. 往返机票花费500美元。|近义词：return trip, two-way trip|反义词:one-way trip

This roundtrip ticket is valid for one year. = 这张往返票有效期为一年。
