deauthorization:/ˌdiːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |撤销授权；取消授权；授权的取消|The act of removing authorization or power; the state of being deauthorized.  It often involves revoking permissions, access, or privileges previously granted.|例句：The deauthorization of the software prevented further use. 软件的撤销授权阻止了进一步的使用。

例句：The company announced the deauthorization of several employees' access to sensitive data. 公司宣布撤销几名员工访问敏感数据的权限。


近义词：revocation, disauthorization, withdrawal of authority, termination of authorization|反义词：authorization, empowerment, enablement|词性：名词
