kitty-corner: /ˈkɪtiˌkɔːrnər/ | adj. | 斜对角的，成对角线的 |  Across a diagonal; diagonally opposite.  The term likely originated from the placement of kittens playing in opposite corners of a room. | 例句：The library is kitty-corner from the post office. 图书馆与邮局斜对面。

The bakery is kitty-corner to the park. 面包店与公园斜对面。


近义词：diagonally opposite, across from, on the opposite diagonal


反义词：adjacent, next to, beside


词性:形容词
