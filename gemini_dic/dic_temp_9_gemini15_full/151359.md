parklet:/ˈpɑːrklet/| n. |小停车位；路边停车位|A parklet is a small, temporary public space created by transforming parking spaces into pedestrian areas.  They often include seating, landscaping, and other amenities to encourage social interaction and create a more inviting urban environment.  |例句：The city council approved the installation of several parklets downtown to improve pedestrian access and create more green space.  市议会批准在市中心安装几个小停车位，以改善行人通行并创造更多绿地。


例句：Our neighborhood association is working to get a parklet built on our street to provide a place for people to relax and socialize.  我们社区协会正在努力在我们街上建造一个小停车位，为人们提供放松和社交的地方。


近义词：street plaza, mini-park, pocket park|反义词：parking lot, large parking area|词性:名词
