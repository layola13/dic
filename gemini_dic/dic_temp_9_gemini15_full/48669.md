defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/ | /dɪˈfɚ/| vt. vi. n.|推迟；延期；遵从；敬重 | to postpone something until a later time; to yield to someone's wishes or opinion; (n.) a postponement |例句：The meeting has been deferred until next week.  会议推迟到下周举行。

They deferred to the expert's judgment. 他们遵从专家的判断。


近义词：postpone, delay, adjourn, respect, obey |反义词：accelerate, hasten, disregard, disobey |词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We defer the decision until we have more information. = 我们推迟决定，直到我们有更多信息。
They defer to their parents' wishes. = 他们顺从父母的意愿。


一般过去时:
The game was deferred due to bad weather. = 比赛因恶劣天气而推迟。
She deferred to her boss's opinion. = 她遵从老板的意见。


现在完成时:
We have deferred the project several times. = 我们已经推迟了这个项目好几次。
They have deferred to the pressure from the public. = 他们屈服于公众的压力。


过去完成时:
They had deferred the announcement until after the election. = 他们把公告推迟到选举之后。
He had deferred to his sister's judgment on the matter. = 他在这个问题上听从了他姐姐的判断。


将来时:
We will defer the payment until next month. = 我们将把付款推迟到下个月。
They will defer to the committee's decision. = 他们将服从委员会的决定。


虚拟语气:
If we could, we would defer the trip until spring. = 如果可以的话，我们会把旅行推迟到春天。
I wish they had deferred to my advice. = 我希望他们当时能听从我的建议。


被动语态:
The decision was deferred until further notice. = 决定推迟到另行通知。
His request was deferred by the committee. = 他的请求被委员会驳回了。


疑问句:
Will the meeting be deferred? = 会议会推迟吗？
Do you defer to your parents' opinion on this matter? = 在这件事上，你听从父母的意见吗？


否定句:
We will not defer the project any longer. = 我们不会再推迟这个项目了。
He did not defer to anyone's opinion. = 他不听从任何人的意见。


PHRASE->
defer payment = 延期付款
defer judgment = 推迟判决
defer to someone = 遵从某人
in deferment = 延期中
defer an action = 推迟一项行动
