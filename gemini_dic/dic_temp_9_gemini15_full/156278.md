phyllomania:/ˌfɪləˈmeɪniə/| n. |叶狂热症；对叶子的痴迷|An abnormal or excessive preoccupation with leaves, often manifested as a compulsion to collect or arrange them.|The gardener exhibited phyllomania, meticulously arranging every fallen leaf in his garden.|近义词：leaf obsession, foliomania (less common)|反义词：None specifically;  could be considered the opposite of indifference to leaves.
