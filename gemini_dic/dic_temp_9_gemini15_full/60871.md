elegiographer: /ɪˌlɛdʒiˈɒɡrəfər/ | n. | 挽歌作者，哀歌诗人 | A writer of elegies.  Someone skilled in writing elegiac poetry, expressing sorrow or mourning, often for the dead. | Example:  The elegiographer penned a moving poem to commemorate the fallen soldiers. 挽歌作者写了一首感人的诗歌来纪念阵亡的士兵。 | 近义词：poet, lyricist, lamenter | 反义词：celebrator, eulogist
