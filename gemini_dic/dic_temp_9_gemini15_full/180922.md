reigned:/reɪnd/| v.|统治，当政，盛行|to rule as a king, queen, emperor, etc.; to be prevalent or widespread|例句：Queen Victoria reigned for over sixty years. 维多利亚女王统治了六十多年。|例句：Terror reigned in the streets after the earthquake. 地震过后，恐怖在街道上蔓延。|近义词：ruled, governed, dominated|反义词：was overthrown, was deposed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The queen reigns over the country. = 女王统治着这个国家。
Peace reigns in this village. = 这个村庄一片祥和。


一般过去时:
King Henry VIII reigned for many years. = 亨利八世国王统治了很多年。
A sense of calm reigned after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，一种平静的感觉弥漫开来。


现在完成时:
She has reigned for over twenty years. = 她已经统治了二十多年了。
Peace has reigned in the region since the conflict ended. = 自冲突结束以来，该地区一直保持和平。


过去完成时:
The old king had reigned for fifty years before he died. = 老国王在去世之前统治了五十年。
Silence had reigned before the announcement was made. = 在宣布之前，一片寂静。


将来时:
The new monarch will reign for many years to come. = 新君主将在未来许多年统治。
It is likely that chaos will reign following the coup. = 政变之后，很可能陷入混乱。


虚拟语气:
If he had reigned longer, the country might have prospered. = 如果他统治时间更长，国家可能会繁荣。
I wish peace would reign throughout the world. = 我希望世界各地都能和平。


被动语态:
The kingdom was reigned over by a wise king. = 王国由一位英明的国王统治。
(Note:  The passive voice is less common with "reign".)


疑问句:
How long did Queen Elizabeth I reign? = 伊丽莎白一世女王统治了多久？
For how long will the current president reign? = 现任总统将统治多久？


否定句:
The emperor did not reign for long. = 皇帝没有统治很久。
Order did not reign in the chaotic city. = 在混乱的城市里，秩序荡然无存。
