quasi-patronizingly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈpætrənaɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 带有伪善的施舍意味地；假装关切地 |  The word combines "quasi" (meaning seemingly or apparently) and "patronizingly" (meaning in a condescending way). It describes an action or tone that superficially appears helpful or caring, but actually reveals a hidden condescension or superiority.  | 例句：He offered his help quasi-patronizingly, as if implying I was incapable of managing the task myself. 他假装关切地提供帮助，好像暗示我自己无法胜任这项任务。


He spoke to her quasi-patronizingly, offering unsolicited advice that masked his underlying disdain. 他对她说话带着伪善的施舍意味，提供不请自来的建议，掩盖了他内心的轻蔑。


近义词：condescendingly, condescendingly helpful, superciliously, patronizingly


反义词：respectfully, sincerely, genuinely helpfully
