going-around: /ˈɡoʊɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | adj. phrasal verb | 流行的；四处传播的；正在流传的 |  Describes something that is widespread, prevalent, or circulating, often referring to illnesses, rumors, or news.  | 例句：The flu is going around this year. = 今年流感正在流行。  There's a rumor going around about the CEO's resignation. = 关于首席执行官辞职的谣言正在流传。|近义词：circulating, prevalent, widespread | 反义词：rare, uncommon, isolated |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A new virus is going around. = 一种新的病毒正在传播。
This song is going around on TikTok. = 这首歌在抖音上很流行。


一般过去时:
A bad cold was going around last winter. = 去年冬天感冒很流行。
The news about the merger was going around the office. = 关于合并的消息在办公室里四处流传。


现在完成时:
That rumor has been going around for weeks. = 那谣言已经流传了好几周了。
A particularly nasty flu has been going around the school. = 一种特别严重的流感一直在学校里流行。


过去完成时:
Before the announcement, the news about the layoffs had been going around. = 在宣布之前，关于裁员的消息已经在流传了。
By the time the meeting started, the gossip had been going around for hours. = 等会议开始的时候，流言蜚语已经传了好几个小时了。


将来时:
A new strain of the virus is expected to be going around next winter. = 预计明年冬天将出现一种新的病毒株。
They predict that this new fashion trend will be going around soon. = 他们预测这种新的时尚潮流很快就会流行起来。


虚拟语气:
If that rumor were going around, I'd be worried. = 如果那个谣言正在流传，我会很担心。
I wish that kind of negativity wasn't going around. = 我希望那种消极情绪不会传播。


被动语态:
It was said that a new disease was going around. = 据说有一种新的疾病正在流行。
The information was confirmed to be going around. = 证实了该信息正在传播。


疑问句:
Is there anything going around? = 有什么流行的东西吗？
Is that story really going around? = 那个故事真的在流传吗？


否定句:
Nothing serious was going around. = 没有什么严重的疾病正在流行。
That rumor wasn't going around.  = 那个谣言没有在流传。


PHRASE->
going around the clock = 全天候
going around in circles = 兜圈子；毫无进展
going around with = 和...交往
going around town = 在城里到处走动
going around the bend = 发疯；快要崩溃
