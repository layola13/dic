fea:/fiː/| n.|特征，特点，特质|a distinguishing characteristic or feature.|The fea of this car is its fuel efficiency. 这辆车的特点是燃油效率高。|近义词：characteristic, trait, attribute|反义词：insignificance, ordinariness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This product has many appealing features. = 这个产品有很多吸引人的特点。
The house boasts several unique features. = 这栋房子拥有几个独特的特点。


一般过去时:
The old building retained many of its original features. = 这座旧建筑保留了许多原始特色。
The software lacked key features. = 这软件缺少关键功能。

The project has revealed some unexpected features. = 该项目揭示了一些意想不到的特点。


过去完成时:
