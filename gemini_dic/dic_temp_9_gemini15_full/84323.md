halfasleep:/ˈhæfəˈsliːp/| adj. |半睡半醒的，昏昏欲睡的 |Half asleep describes a state between being fully awake and fully asleep, often characterized by drowsiness and a lack of full alertness.  It implies a state of light sleep or being on the verge of falling asleep.|例句：I was half asleep when the phone rang.  电话响的时候我正处于半睡半醒的状态。|例句：The lecture was so boring that I was half asleep by the end. 讲座太无聊了，到最后我几乎睡着了。|近义词：dozy, drowsy, sleepy, semi-conscious|反义词：wide awake, fully alert|形容词
