refrustrate:/ˌriːfrʌˈstreɪt/| v.|使再次受挫；再次使沮丧 |To frustrate again; to cause to be frustrated a second time.  This word is not commonly used and is essentially a combination of "re-" (again) and "frustrate".|例句：The repeated delays refrustrated the team, leading to further setbacks.  反复的延误再次使团队受挫，导致进一步的挫折。 The unexpected rain refrustrated our picnic plans.  突如其来的雨水再次打乱了我们的野餐计划。|近义词：discourage, dishearten, disappoint |反义词：encourage, inspire, motivate |动词
