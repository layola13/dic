sgt:/sɑːrdʒənt/|/ˈsɑːdʒənt/| n. |中士|Sergeant is a non-commissioned officer rank in many armies, air forces, and other uniformed services.|例句：He was promoted to sergeant after three years of service. 他服役三年后晋升为中士。|例句：The sergeant barked orders at the recruits.  中士向新兵大声下命令。|近义词：corporal, lieutenant|反义词：private, recruit|名词
