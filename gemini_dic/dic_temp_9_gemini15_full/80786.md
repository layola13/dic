/ɡɑːtə/ | phrasal verb | 必须，不得不 |  "Got to" is a colloquial contraction of "have got to" or "have to," indicating obligation or necessity.  It's less formal than "must" or "have to." | I got to finish this report by tomorrow. = 我必须在明天之前完成这份报告。

I got to go to the doctor's appointment. = 我不得不去医生那里就诊。


近义词: have to, must, need to | 反义词: don't have to, needn't


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I've got to go to work. = 我得去上班。
We've got to finish this project soon. = 我们得尽快完成这个项目。


一般过去时:
I had to leave early. = 我不得不早点离开。
She had to study for the exam. = 她不得不为考试学习。


现在完成时:
I've had to work overtime all week. = 我整个星期都不得不加班。
They've had to make some difficult decisions. = 他们不得不做出一些艰难的决定。


过去完成时:
I had had to cancel my plans. = 我不得不取消我的计划了。(指过去某个时间点之前)
She had had to borrow money. = 她不得不借钱了。(指过去某个时间点之前)


将来时:
I will have to call him tomorrow. = 我明天得给他打电话。
We will have to find another solution. = 我们得找到另一个解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would have had to go. = 如果我有更多时间，我就不得不去了。
I wish I hadn't had to work so hard. = 我真希望我不必这么努力工作。


被动语态:
The work had to be finished on time. = 工作必须按时完成。
The problem had to be solved immediately. = 问题必须立即解决。


疑问句:
Do I have to go? = 我必须去吗？
Does she have to work late? = 她必须工作到很晚吗？


否定句:
I don't have to go to the meeting. = 我不必参加会议。
You don't have to worry about it. = 你不必为此担心。


PHRASE->
got to get = 必须得到

got to do = 必须做
got to be = 必须是
got to have = 必须拥有
