signwriter: /ˈsaɪnraɪtər/ | n. |招牌制作人，指示牌书写员 | A person who creates and paints signs, often using hand-painted lettering.  They may specialize in traditional sign painting techniques or use modern methods. |The signwriter meticulously crafted the storefront sign, ensuring each letter was perfectly formed.  招牌制作人一丝不苟地制作了店面招牌，确保每个字母都完美无缺。|  letterer, painter, artist |  (No direct antonym, but a contrasting term could be "digital designer" if referring to modern methods) | 名词
