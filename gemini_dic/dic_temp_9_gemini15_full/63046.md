enricher:/ɪnˈrɪtʃər/ | n. | 丰富者；改良剂 | A person or thing that enriches something.  Someone or something that adds value, improves quality, or makes something more interesting or enjoyable. | Example:  The new teacher was an enricher of the school's curriculum.  这位新老师丰富了学校的课程。|近义词：improver, enhancer, ameliorator |反义词：depleter, impoverisher


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fertilizer enriches the soil. = 这种肥料使土壤肥沃。
The experience enriches her life. = 这段经历丰富了她的生活。

一般过去时:
The artist enriched the painting with vibrant colors. = 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩丰富了这幅画。
The program enriched the community. = 这个项目丰富了社区。

现在完成时:
Travel has enriched my understanding of different cultures. = 旅行丰富了我对不同文化的理解。
This book has enriched my knowledge of history. = 这本书丰富了我对历史的知识。

过去完成时:
They had enriched the soup with herbs before serving. = 他们在上菜前用香草调味丰富了汤。
She had enriched her presentation with compelling visuals. = 她用引人注目的视觉效果丰富了她的演示文稿。

将来时:
The new project will enrich the town's economy. = 新项目将丰富城镇的经济。
This experience will enrich his life in unexpected ways. = 这段经历将以意想不到的方式丰富他的生活。

虚拟语气:
If we added more spices, it would enrich the flavor. = 如果我们添加更多香料，它会使味道更浓郁。
I wish the music enriched the film more. = 我希望音乐能更多地丰富这部电影。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
