outpointed:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪntɪd/| vt.|胜过，得分超过|To score more points than (someone) in a contest or competition.|例句：He outpointed his opponent in the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛中得分超过了他的对手。|例句：The team outpointed their rivals by a significant margin.  这个队以显著的优势胜过了他们的对手。|近义词：surpassed, exceeded, bested|反义词：underpointed (非标准词，可理解为得分低于)|动词
