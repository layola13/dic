ox's:/ˈɑːksɪz/| n. |牛的；公牛的|The possessive form of "ox," indicating belonging to or relating to an ox.  It is used to show ownership or possession. |例句：The ox's horns were impressive.  牛的角很令人印象深刻。|例句：The farmer examined the ox's hooves carefully.  农夫仔细检查了牛的蹄子。|近义词：bull's, cow's (depending on context)|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses or aspects like the example word "what".  Its usage is always to show possession.
