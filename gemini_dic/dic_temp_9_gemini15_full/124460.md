mottled:/ˈmɑːtəld/| adj.| 有斑点的；杂色的；斑驳的 | Having spots or patches of different colors or shades.  The mottled surface of the stone showed years of weathering.  这块石头斑驳的表面显示出多年的风化痕迹。|近义词：speckled, blotched, dappled, variegated |反义词：uniform, plain, even

例句：

The cat's fur was mottled brown and white.  这只猫的毛是棕色和白色的斑点。

The old map was mottled with age and stains. 这张旧地图因年代久远和污渍而斑驳。
