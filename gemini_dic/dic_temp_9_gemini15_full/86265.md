heavenwardly:/ˈhɛvənwərdli/| adj. |天堂般的；向天堂的；极好的|  Adjective describing something that is related to heaven, moving towards heaven, or exceptionally good.  It implies a sense of beauty, peace, or spiritual elevation. |例句：The birds soared heavenwardly into the clear blue sky.  鸟儿向天堂般清澈的蓝天飞去。

The choir's voices were heavenwardly beautiful. 合唱团的声音美妙绝伦。


近义词：celestial, divine, heavenly, angelic, sublime|反义词：hellish, infernal, earthly, mundane|词性：形容词
