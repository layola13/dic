mediaevalism:/ˌmiːdiˈiːvəlɪzəm/| n. |中世纪主义；中世纪复兴；对中世纪的兴趣|The study and imitation of the art, architecture, and social customs of the Middle Ages.  It encompasses a range of approaches, from scholarly historical studies to artistic and literary movements inspired by the medieval period.  It can also refer to a romanticized or nostalgic view of the Middle Ages.|例句：The Gothic revival in architecture was a prime example of mediaevalism.  哥特式建筑的复兴是中世纪主义的典型例子。

The novel displays a strong sense of mediaevalism, with its knights, castles, and courtly love. 这部小说强烈地表现出中世纪主义，其中有骑士、城堡和宫廷爱情。|近义词：medieval revival, medievalism,  romanticism (in relation to the Middle Ages)|反义词：modernism, enlightenment thinking|名词
