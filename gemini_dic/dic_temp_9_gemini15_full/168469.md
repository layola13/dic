probationers:/prəˈbeɪʃənərz/| n.|缓刑期的人；见习人员；试用期员工|People who are on probation, a period of time during which someone's conduct or work is observed before they are fully accepted.  It often refers to individuals undergoing a trial period in a profession or job, or those serving a sentence under probation. |例句：The probationers were closely monitored during their first three months.  见习人员在最初三个月受到了密切关注。

The new probationers are eager to prove themselves. 新的试用期员工渴望证明自己。|近义词：trainees, novices, apprentices|反义词：veterans, experts
