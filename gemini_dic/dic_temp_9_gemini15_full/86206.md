heathenism:/ˈhiːðənɪzəm/| n. |异教；异教徒的习俗或信仰|The belief and practices of people considered to be non-believers in the dominant religion, often involving polytheism, animism, or other spiritual systems outside mainstream religious norms.  Heathenism encompasses a wide range of beliefs and practices across different cultures and time periods. It is important to avoid using the term pejoratively.|例句：The Roman Empire initially suppressed early forms of Christianity, considering it a form of heathenism. 罗马帝国最初压制早期基督教，认为它是一种异教。

The study of ancient heathenism reveals diverse spiritual practices and social structures.  对古代异教的研究揭示了多种多样的精神实践和社会结构。|近义词：paganism, idolatry, polytheism|反义词：monotheism, orthodoxy
