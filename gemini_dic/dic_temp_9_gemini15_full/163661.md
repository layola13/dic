predecease:/ˌpriːdɪˈsiːs/| vt.|先于…去世；在…之前去世|To die before someone else.|His father predeceased him by ten years.  他的父亲比他早逝十年。|近义词：precede in death, die before|反义词：survive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He predeceases his wife every year by a week. = 他每年都比妻子早逝一周。
The old king predeceases his heir. = 老国王先于继承人去世。


一般过去时:
My grandfather predeceased my grandmother. = 我的祖父比祖母先去世。
She predeceased her husband by many years. = 她比丈夫早逝多年。


现在完成时:
Many have predeceased him in this battle. = 许多人在这场战斗中先于他去世。
His father has predeceased him, leaving a large fortune. = 他的父亲先于他去世，留下了一大笔遗产。


过去完成时:
His father had predeceased him, so he inherited the estate. = 他的父亲在他之前就去世了，所以他继承了遗产。
The queen had predeceased her husband by several months. = 女王在几个月前就先于她的丈夫去世了。


将来时:
I fear he will predecease me. = 我担心他会先于我而去世。
It is predicted that she will predecease her twin brother. = 预计她将先于她的孪生兄弟去世。


虚拟语气:
If he had predeceased her, she would have been devastated. = 如果他先于她去世，她将会非常悲痛。
I wish my mother hadn't predeceased my father. = 我希望我母亲没有先于我父亲去世。


被动语态:
He was predeceased by his parents. = 他的父母先于他去世。
The king was predeceased by his eldest son. = 国王的长子先于他去世。


疑问句:
Did his father predecease him? = 他的父亲是否先于他去世？
Will she predecease her husband? = 她会先于她丈夫去世吗？


否定句:
His wife did not predecease him. = 他的妻子没有先于他去世。
The child did not predecease its mother. = 这个孩子没有先于其母亲去世。
