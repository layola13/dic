nonstudent:/ˈnɑːnˌstuːdənt/| n. |非学生；非在校学生|A person who is not currently a student.  This term often refers to someone who has graduated or never attended a formal educational institution.|例句：The library offers special discounts for students, but nonstudents can still use the facilities. 图书馆为学生提供特别折扣，但非学生仍然可以使用这些设施。|近义词：graduate, alumnus, former student |反义词:student
