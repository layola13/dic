repatriations:/ˌriːˌpætriˈeɪʃənz/| n.|遣返；归还；汇款|The act of returning someone to their own country.  It often refers to the return of refugees, prisoners of war, or citizens who have been living abroad.|例句：The government organized mass repatriations of refugees after the war.  政府在战争结束后组织了大规模的难民遣返工作。|例句：The company facilitated the repatriations of its employees' funds from overseas accounts. 公司协助其员工从海外账户汇回资金。|近义词：repatriation, return, restoration |反义词：expatriation, exile
