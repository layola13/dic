heliolater:/ˈhiːliəʊˌleɪtər/ | n. | 拜日教徒 | A worshipper of the sun.  Heliolatry is the worship of the sun as a deity.  The term combines "helios" (Greek for sun) and "-latre" (Greek for worship). | Example:  Ancient Egyptians were heliolaters, building magnificent temples to Ra, their sun god.  古代埃及人是拜日教徒，他们为他们的太阳神拉建造了宏伟的寺庙。 | 近义词: sun-worshipper | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could consider someone who actively rejects sun worship)
