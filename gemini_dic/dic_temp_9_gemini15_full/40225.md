contextualize:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使具有语境；解释语境；将…置于语境中 | To place (something) within its context; to explain or describe the context of (something).  | 例句：It's important to contextualize these findings within the broader research. 将这些发现置于更广泛的研究背景中非常重要。 | 例句：The professor contextualized the historical events by explaining the social and political climate of the time. 教授通过解释当时的社会和政治气候来解释这些历史事件的背景。| 近义词：situate, place, interpret, explain | 反义词：decontextualize, isolate
