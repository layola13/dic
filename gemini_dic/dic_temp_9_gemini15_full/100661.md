intoners:/ɪnˈtoʊnərz/ | n. | 吟诵者；诵经者；(尤指)唱诗班成员 |  Those who intone, especially in a religious context.  They are often part of a choir or group that chants or sings in a liturgical setting. | 例句：The intoners led the congregation in prayer. 吟诵者带领会众祈祷。| The intoners chanted the ancient hymns beautifully. 吟诵者优美地吟唱古老的圣歌。|近义词：chanters, singers, reciters | 反义词：listeners, audiences
