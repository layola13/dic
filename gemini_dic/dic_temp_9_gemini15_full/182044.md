remembers:/rɪˈmɛmbərz/| vt.|记得，想起|To have a picture in your mind of something that happened in the past; to keep something in your memory.|She remembers her childhood in the country. 她记得她在乡下的童年。|recall, recollect|forget, overlook|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I remember his name. = 我记得他的名字。
She remembers the details of the meeting. = 她记得会议的细节。


一般过去时:
He remembered to lock the door. = 他记得锁门了。
I remembered seeing her yesterday. = 我记得昨天见过她。


现在完成时:
I have remembered everything you told me. = 我记住了你告诉我的所有事情。
She has remembered to bring her keys. = 她记得带钥匙了。


过去完成时:
He had remembered to book the tickets before leaving. = 他在离开前记得预订了票。
I had remembered that I had a meeting that afternoon. = 我记得那天午后有个会议。


将来时:
I will remember this day for the rest of my life. = 我会记住这一天一辈子。
She will remember to call you back later. = 她会记得晚点给你回电话。


虚拟语气:
If I remembered his number, I would call him. = 如果我记得他的号码，我会给他打电话。
I wish I remembered where I put my keys. = 我希望我记得我把钥匙放在哪里了。


被动语态:
The event will be remembered for years to come. = 这件事将被人们铭记多年。
His kindness will be remembered by everyone. = 他的善良会被每个人铭记。


疑问句:
Do you remember what happened? = 你记得发生了什么吗？
Does she remember the song? = 她记得这首歌吗？


否定句:
I don't remember his name. = 我不记得他的名字。
She doesn't remember seeing him. = 她不记得见过他。


PHRASE->
remember me to… = 代我向…问好
remember when… = 还记得…的时候吗？
as I remember = 据我记得
I'll never forget/remember = 我永远不会忘记/记得
try to remember = 努力记住
