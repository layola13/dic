inescapably: /ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbli/ | adj. | 不可避免地，必然地 |  Inevitably; in a way that cannot be avoided.  | The evidence inescapably points to his guilt.  证据无可辩驳地表明他有罪。 The crisis is inescapably linked to climate change.这场危机与气候变化不可避免地联系在一起。 | unavoidably, inevitably, necessarily | avoidably, optionally |  形容词


USAGE->

The consequences are inescapably linked to the actions. = 后果与行为不可避免地联系在一起。
We inescapably face the challenges of the future. = 我们不可避免地要面对未来的挑战。
