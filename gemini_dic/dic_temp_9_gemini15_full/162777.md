preblooming: /ˌpriːˈbluːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先开花；即将开花的 |  Describes the stage before a plant blooms, where buds are developing but flowers haven't yet opened.  It suggests an imminent flowering. | Example: The preblooming roses showed promise of a spectacular display.  这即将开花的玫瑰预示着将会有壮观的景象。 | 近义词：budding, nascent | 反义词：full bloom, flowering
