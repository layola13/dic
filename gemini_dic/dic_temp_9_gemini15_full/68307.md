The word is now largely archaic or used in specialized contexts, such as historical descriptions of clothing or textiles. | Example: The meticulous faldage of the silk gown was admired by all.  丝绸长袍细致的褶皱受到了所有人的赞赏。|近义词：fold, pleat, crease | 反义词：unfolding, smoothing
