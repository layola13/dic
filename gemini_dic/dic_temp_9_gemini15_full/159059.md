pneumonitis:/ˌnjuːməˈnaɪtɪs/| n. |肺炎；肺部炎症|Inflammation of the lungs.  It can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and exposure to certain substances.|例句：The doctor diagnosed him with pneumonitis after reviewing his chest X-ray.  医生在检查了他的胸部X光片后诊断他患有肺炎。|例句：  Exposure to toxic fumes can lead to severe pneumonitis. 接触有毒烟雾会导致严重的肺炎。|近义词：lung inflammation, pulmonary inflammation|反义词：healthy lungs
