revocable:/ˈrɛvəkəbəl/| adj. | 可撤销的，可废止的 | Capable of being revoked or repealed; able to be canceled or reversed.  | The contract is revocable within 30 days.  该合同在30天内可撤销。 | 近义词：cancellable, rescindable, reversible | 反义词：irrevocable, irreversible

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The agreement is revocable at any time. = 这份协议随时可以撤销。
This permit is revocable if misused. = 如果滥用，这个许可证可以被撤销。

一般过去时:
The judge revoked the order. = 法官撤销了命令。
The license was revoked for violation of rules. = 由于违反规定，执照被吊销了。

现在完成时:
They have revoked his driving license. = 他们已经吊销了他的驾照。
The company has revoked its previous offer. = 公司已经撤回了之前的提议。

过去完成时:
The court had already revoked the permit before the incident. = 事故发生前，法院已经撤销了许可证。
He had revoked his statement before the trial ended. = 在审判结束之前，他已经撤回了他的声明。

将来时:
The government will revoke the law if necessary. = 必要时，政府将废除这项法律。
They will revoke his membership if he continues to violate the rules. = 如果他继续违反规章制度，他们将取消他的会员资格。

虚拟语气:
If the contract were revocable, we would have done it differently. = 如果合同可以撤销，我们会采取不同的做法。
I wish the decision were revocable. = 我希望这个决定可以撤销。

被动语态:
The contract can be revoked by either party. = 合同可以由任何一方撤销。
His membership will be revoked if he fails to pay the fees. = 如果他不缴纳会费，他的会员资格将被取消。

疑问句:
Is the decision revocable? = 这个决定可以撤销吗？
Are these permits revocable after a year? = 这些许可证一年后可以撤销吗？

否定句:
The contract is not revocable once signed. = 合同一旦签署，就不能撤销。
The decision is not revocable, so we must accept it. = 这个决定是不可撤销的，所以我们必须接受它。
