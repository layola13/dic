informed:/ɪnˈfɔːrmd/| adj.|消息灵通的；见多识广的；被告知的|Having or showing knowledge or understanding of a subject or situation.|例句：He is well informed about current events. 他对时事很了解。|例句：She kept me informed of her progress. 她一直让我了解她的进展。|近义词：educated, knowledgeable, aware|反义词: ignorant, unaware, uninformed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is informed about the latest technology. = 他了解最新的科技。
They are informed of the meeting's cancellation. = 他们被告知会议取消了。


一般过去时:
She was informed of the bad news yesterday. = 她昨天被告知了坏消息。
He was well informed on the subject before the debate. = 在辩论之前，他对这个话题很了解。


现在完成时:
I have been informed that the project is delayed. = 我已被告知项目延期了。
They have kept themselves informed about the market trends. = 他们一直关注市场趋势。


过去完成时:
She had been informed of the problem before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已被告知了这个问题。
We had informed him of the risks involved beforehand. = 我们事先已经告知他所涉及的风险。


将来时:
You will be informed of the results next week. = 你下周将被告知结果。
He will keep us informed as the situation develops. = 情况发展时，他会随时让我们了解。


虚拟语气:
If I were informed earlier, I could have prepared better. = 如果我早点知道，我就能更好地准备。
I wish I had been informed sooner. = 我希望我早点被告知。


被动语态:
The employees were informed of the company's new policy. = 员工们被告知了公司的新政策。
He was informed that his application was rejected. = 他被告知他的申请被拒绝了。


疑问句:
Are you informed about the upcoming election? = 你了解即将到来的选举吗？
Was he informed of the changes? = 他被告知这些变化了吗？


否定句:
He was not informed of the decision. = 他没有被告知这个决定。
She remained uninformed about the entire situation. = 她对整个情况一无所知。


PHRASE->
well-informed: 消息灵通的
fully informed: 完全知情的
kept informed: 被告知
stay informed: 保持知情
informed consent: 知情同意
