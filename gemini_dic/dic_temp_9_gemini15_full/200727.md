sleets:/slit/| n.|冻雨，雨夹雪|Frozen rain; a form of precipitation consisting of raindrops that freeze upon impact with the ground or other surfaces.|The sleet made driving conditions extremely hazardous.|近义词：freezing rain, ice pellets|反义词：sunshine, fair weather
