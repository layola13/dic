/ɛpɪˈnaɪʃəl/| adj.|表面的；外在的；非本质的|Epiphenomal refers to a secondary phenomenon that is a consequence of, but does not causally influence, a primary phenomenon.  It often describes something that appears significant but is actually a superficial effect of an underlying process.|The beauty of the sunset is an epiphenomenal effect of the scattering of sunlight in the atmosphere. 日落的美丽是大气中阳光散射的一种表面的效应。The perceived complexity of the software is epiphenomenal to its underlying simplicity. 软件的感知复杂性与其底层简单性无关。|近义词：superficial, incidental, secondary|反义词：essential, fundamental, intrinsic|
