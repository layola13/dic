clasher:/ˈklæʃər/ | n. |冲突者；发生冲突的事物；（尤指）发出刺耳声响的东西 | A person or thing that clashes.  Especially something that makes a harsh, jarring sound. |例句：The two political parties were bitter clashers. 这两个政党是激烈的冲突者。

例句：The cymbals were such loud clashers during the concert.  音乐会上铙钹的声响刺耳极了。


近义词：combatant, contender, antagonist | 反义词：harmonizer, peacemaker


词源:  The word "clasher" is derived from the verb "clash," which ultimately comes from the Middle English word "claschen," meaning "to strike together with a loud noise."  This, in turn, is likely of onomatopoeic origin, imitating the sound of colliding objects.
