crushproof: /ˈkrʌʃpruːf/ | adj. | 防压碎的，抗压的 |  Resistant to being crushed or broken.  This adjective describes something that can withstand significant pressure without being damaged. | Example: The crushproof box protected the fragile contents during shipping.  防压碎的盒子在运输过程中保护了易碎物品。 | 近义词：indestructible, unbreakable, impact-resistant | 反义词：crushable, fragile, breakable
