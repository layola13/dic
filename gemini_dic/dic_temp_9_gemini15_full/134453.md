nonitemizers:/ˌnɒnˈaɪtəmaɪzəz/ | n. |非逐项列举者；非明细账使用者 | Individuals or businesses that do not itemize deductions on their tax returns.  They instead use the standard deduction. | Example:  Many nonitemizers find the standard deduction more convenient. 许多非逐项列举者发现标准扣除额更方便。 | 近义词：standard deduction users, those who use the standard deduction | 反义词：itemizers
