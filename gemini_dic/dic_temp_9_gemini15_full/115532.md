map-projection:/ˈmæp prəˈdʒɛkʃən/| n. |地图投影|The systematic transformation of the three-dimensional surface of the Earth onto a two-dimensional plane.  This involves distortions in shape, area, distance, or direction, depending on the type of projection used.|Example: The Mercator projection is a common map projection, but it significantly distorts areas near the poles.|近义词：cartographic projection|反义词：None
