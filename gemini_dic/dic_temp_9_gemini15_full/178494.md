recontemplation:/ˌriːkənˌtɛmpleɪʃən/| n. |再思考，重新考虑|The act of considering something again; a renewed contemplation.  It implies a more thorough or deeper consideration than a simple second thought.|例句：After much recontemplation, she decided to accept the job offer.  再三考虑之后，她决定接受这份工作。

例句：His recontemplation of the evidence led him to a different conclusion. 他重新审视证据后得出了不同的结论。


近义词：reconsideration, review, reflection, re-evaluation

反义词：impulsiveness, hastiness, rashness

词性：名词
