depicts:/dɪˈpɪkts/ | vt. | 描绘；描述；刻画 | To represent or portray (someone or something) in a picture or story.  | The painting depicts a stormy sea. 这幅画描绘了一片暴风雨的海。 The novel depicts life in a small village. 这部小说描绘了乡村生活。 | portrays, represents, illustrates | obscures, misrepresents | 动词
