mccoy:/məˈkɔɪ/ | n. |麦考伊，真货，好东西 |The McCoy refers to something genuine or authentic, the real thing, not an imitation.  It can also refer to a specific person or thing, particularly in the context of a family name. |例句：This is the real McCoy, not a cheap copy.  这是真货，不是廉价的复制品。|例句：The McCloys are hosting a party next week.  麦考伊家族下周要举办一个派对。 |近义词：genuine, authentic, real, the real deal |反义词：fake, imitation, counterfeit, sham
