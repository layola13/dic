legitimates:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪts/| v.|使合法化；使正当；证实|To make something legal or acceptable.  To give a reason or justification for something.|The government legitimized the new law by passing it through parliament. 政府通过议会通过了这项新法律，使其合法化。|The evidence legitimized his claim. 证据证实了他的说法。|validate, justify, authorize|illegitimate, invalidate, disapprove|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He legitimizes his actions with his strong beliefs. = 他用自己坚定的信念使自己的行为合法化。
The court legitimizes the marriage. = 法院使这桩婚姻合法化。


一般过去时:
The revolution legitimized the new government. = 革命使新政府合法化。
She legitimized her anger with a valid reason. = 她用一个正当的理由使她的愤怒变得合理。


现在完成时:
The recent election has legitimized the president's power. = 最近的选举使总统的权力合法化。
His explanation has legitimized his absence. = 他的解释使他的缺席变得合理。


过去完成时:
They had legitimized their claim before the trial. = 他们在审判前已经使他们的说法合法化。
The evidence had legitimized the suspect's guilt. = 证据已经证实了嫌疑人的罪行。


将来时:
The new law will legitimize the practice of online voting. = 新法律将使网上投票的做法合法化。
The upcoming report will legitimize their findings. = 即将来临的报告将使他们的发现具有合理性。


虚拟语气:
If the government legitimized the protests, the situation would improve. = 如果政府使抗议合法化，情况就会好转。
If he had legitimized his actions earlier, the consequences wouldn't have been so severe. = 如果他早点使自己的行为合法化，后果就不会那么严重了。


被动语态:
The new policy was legitimized by the Supreme Court. = 这项新政策得到了最高法院的合法化。
His claim was legitimized by irrefutable evidence. = 他的说法得到了无可辩驳的证据的支持。


疑问句:

否定句:
