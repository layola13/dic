judges:/ˈdʒʌdʒɪz/| n.|法官；裁判；评判者|People who are appointed to make decisions in a court of law. They also can be people who assess the quality of something.|例句：The judges deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict. 法官们商议数小时后才做出判决。
例句：The judges at the talent show were impressed by her performance. 才艺表演的评委们对她的表演印象深刻。|近义词：referees, umpires, arbitrators|反义词：defendants, contestants
