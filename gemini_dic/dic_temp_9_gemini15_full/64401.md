erf:/ɜːrf/| n.|误差函数|The error function (erf) is a special function (non-elementary) of sigmoid shape that occurs in probability, statistics, and partial differential equations describing diffusion.  It is defined as the integral of the Gaussian function. |The erf function is crucial in calculating probabilities related to the normal distribution.|近义词：Gaussian integral|反义词：无
