recondemns:/ˌriːkənˈdɛmz/| vt.|重新谴责；再次谴责|To condemn again; to condemn anew.  The court recondemned the criminal to a longer sentence. 法院再次判处罪犯更长的刑期。|近义词：reproach, denounce, censure|反义词：praise, commend, approve|动词


USAGE->

The government recondemns the violence. = 政府再次谴责暴力行为。
He recondemns the actions of the company. = 他再次谴责公司的行为。

一般过去时:
