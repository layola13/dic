enfold:/ɪnˈfoʊld/| vt.| 包裹；包含；围绕；笼罩 | To wrap up or enclose completely; to surround or encompass.  |例句：The fog enfolded the mountain.  雾笼罩着山。

The mother enfolded her child in a warm hug. 母亲温暖地抱着孩子。


近义词：wrap, embrace, envelop, encompass | 反义词：unwrap, unfold, reveal | 动词


USAGE->
