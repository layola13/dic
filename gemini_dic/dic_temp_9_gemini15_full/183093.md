report-out: /rɪˈpɔːrt aʊt/ | vt. |汇报，报告；提交报告 | To give a report on something; to present information formally.  |例句：He will report out on the progress of the project next week. 他下周将汇报项目的进展。
The committee will report out its findings to the board. 委员会将向董事会汇报调查结果。|近义词：present, submit, deliver, communicate |反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They report out on the findings every month. = 他们每月汇报调查结果。
The team reports out its progress regularly. = 团队定期汇报进展。


一般过去时:
She reported out on the sales figures yesterday. = 她昨天汇报了销售数据。
He reported out his research findings at the conference. = 他在会议上汇报了他的研究结果。


现在完成时:
They have reported out on the incident to the authorities. = 他们已经向当局汇报了此事。
The team has reported out all the necessary data. = 团队已经汇报了所有必要的数据。


过去完成时:
They had reported out the initial findings before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经汇报了初步调查结果。
She had reported out the results of the experiment before leaving for vacation. = 她休假前已经汇报了实验结果。


将来时:
He will report out on the new strategy tomorrow. = 他明天将汇报新的战略。
They will report out their conclusions at the end of the year. = 他们将在年底汇报他们的结论。


虚拟语气:
If he had more time, he would report out in more detail. = 如果他有更多时间，他会更详细地汇报。
It would be helpful if they reported out more frequently. = 如果他们更频繁地汇报，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The results were reported out to the public. = 结果已向公众公布。
The progress is being reported out regularly. = 进展正在定期汇报。


疑问句:

否定句:
