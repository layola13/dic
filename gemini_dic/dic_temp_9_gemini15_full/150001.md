palmitate:/ˈpæl.məˌteɪt/ | n. |棕榈酸酯；棕榈酸盐| A salt or ester of palmitic acid. Palmitates are found in many fats and oils, and are used in various industrial applications.  |Example: Sodium palmitate is a common soap ingredient.  棕榈酸钠是肥皂中常见的成分。|近义词：palmitate ester|反义词：无
