converges:/kənˈvɜːrdʒəz/| v.| 收敛；趋同；汇聚 | To move towards the same point from different directions or to become similar or alike.  |  The two roads converge at the traffic lights. 两条路在交通灯处汇合。| The opinions of the experts converged on a single point. 专家们的意见趋于一致。| converge on, converge to, converge upon | diverge | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two rivers converge near the city. = 两条河流在城市附近汇合。
The paths converge at the top of the hill. = 小路在山顶汇合。


一般过去时:
The lines converged at a single point. = 线条汇聚于一点。
The research findings converged on a similar conclusion. = 研究结果得出相似的结论。


现在完成时:
All the evidence has converged to support the theory. = 所有证据都汇聚在一起支持该理论。
The opinions of the panel have converged on a consensus. = 专家组的意见已达成共识。


过去完成时:
The paths had converged before reaching the summit. = 小路在到达山顶之前已经汇合。
The arguments had converged on the central issue. = 争论已经集中在核心问题上。


将来时:
The two streams will converge downstream. = 两条小溪将在下游汇合。
The scientists believe that the data will converge towards a definitive answer. = 科学家们相信数据将趋向于一个明确的答案。


虚拟语气:
If the lines converged, they would form a triangle. = 如果线条汇合，它们将形成一个三角形。
If the trends converged, the market would stabilize. = 如果趋势趋于一致，市场将稳定。


被动语态:
The data were converged into a single database. = 数据被整合到一个单一的数据库中。
The different viewpoints were eventually converged into a unified plan. = 不同的观点最终被融合成一个统一的计划。


疑问句:
Do these lines converge at any point? = 这些线在任何一点汇合吗？
Where do the two rivers converge? = 两条河在哪里汇合？


否定句:
The opinions did not converge. = 意见没有达成一致。
The two roads do not converge here. = 这两条路在这里并没有汇合。


PHRASE->
converge on a point = 汇聚于一点
converge towards a goal = 趋向于一个目标
converge upon a solution = 找到一个解决方案
converge into a single stream = 汇集成一股水流
converge with another group = 与另一组汇合
