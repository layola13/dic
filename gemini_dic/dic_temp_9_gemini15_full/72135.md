floodings: /ˈflʌdɪŋz/ | n. | 洪水泛滥；水灾 | The overflowing of water onto normally dry land.  Floodings can be caused by heavy rainfall, overflowing rivers, or coastal storms.  | 例句：The recent floodings have caused significant damage to homes and infrastructure. 近期洪水泛滥对房屋和基础设施造成了重大破坏。

The persistent floodings in the region have displaced thousands of people.  该地区持续的洪水泛滥已导致数千人流离失所。| 近义词：inundations, deluges, overflows | 反义词：droughts
