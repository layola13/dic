restamping:/riːˈstæmpɪŋ/| v.|重新盖章；再次冲压|The act of stamping something again;  applying a stamp or impression a second time.  It can refer to physically stamping something (like a document) or to a process of re-applying a mark or pattern,  often in manufacturing.|例句：The factory is restamping all the defective parts. 工厂正在重新冲压所有有缺陷的部件。

例句：The government decided to restamp all the expired passports. 政府决定重新盖章所有过期的护照。

近义词：re-marking, re-imprinting, re-applying

反义词：removing stamp, obliterating
