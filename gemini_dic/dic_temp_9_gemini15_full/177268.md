reassessing:/ˌriːəˈsesɪŋ/ | v. |重新评估；重新考虑 |  The process of evaluating something again, often with new information or a changed perspective. It implies a more thorough and critical examination than a simple review. |例句：The committee is reassessing the project's budget after unforeseen costs emerged.  委员会在出现不可预见成本后正在重新评估项目的预算。

例句：We are reassessing our marketing strategy in light of declining sales.鉴于销售额下降，我们正在重新评估我们的营销策略。


近义词：reevaluate, reconsider, re-examine, review


反义词：accept, confirm, ratify


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reassess their investment strategies every quarter. = 他们每季度都会重新评估他们的投资策略。
We reassess the risk factors regularly. = 我们定期重新评估风险因素。


一般过去时:
The government reassessed the environmental impact of the project. = 政府重新评估了该项目的环保影响。
The company reassessed its marketing campaign after poor results. = 公司在业绩不佳后重新评估了其营销活动。


现在完成时:
They have reassessed the situation and decided to proceed. = 他们已经重新评估了情况，并决定继续进行。
We have reassessed our priorities since the merger. = 自合并以来，我们已经重新评估了我们的优先事项。


过去完成时:
They had reassessed the risks before making the final decision. = 他们在做出最终决定之前已经重新评估了风险。
The team had reassessed their approach after the first failure. = 在第一次失败后，团队重新评估了他们的方法。


将来时:
They will reassess the situation next week. = 他们下周将重新评估情况。
The board will reassess the company's performance at the end of the year. = 董事会将在年底重新评估公司的业绩。


虚拟语气:
If we reassessed our strategy, we might achieve better results. = 如果我们重新评估我们的策略，我们可能会取得更好的结果。
I wish we had reassessed the risks earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新评估风险。


被动语态:
The project's budget is being reassessed by the committee. = 该项目的预算正在由委员会重新评估。
The situation was reassessed by the experts. = 情况已由专家重新评估。


疑问句:
Will the team reassess their approach? = 团队会重新评估他们的方法吗？
Are they reassessing the project's feasibility? = 他们正在重新评估项目的可行性吗？


否定句:
They did not reassess their plans despite the setbacks. = 尽管遇到挫折，他们也没有重新评估他们的计划。
The company will not reassess its pricing strategy this year. = 公司今年不会重新评估其定价策略。
