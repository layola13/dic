pleads:/pliːdz/| v.|恳求；请求；为…辩护|To make an earnest request or appeal.  To argue a case in a court of law.|例句：The lawyer pleaded with the judge for leniency. 律师恳求法官从轻发落。

The defendant pleaded not guilty. 被告申辩无罪。|近义词：beg, implore, supplicate, argue|反义词：demand, insist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pleads guilty to the charge. = 他对指控认罪。
She pleads for her life. = 她为自己的生命而恳求。


一般过去时:
He pleaded innocence. = 他申辩无辜。
She pleaded with him to stay. = 她恳求他留下。


现在完成时:
He has pleaded not guilty in court. = 他在法庭上已申辩无罪。
She has repeatedly pleaded for help. = 她一再请求帮助。


过去完成时:
He had pleaded with her for days before she agreed. = 他在她同意之前已经恳求了她好几天。
She had pleaded for mercy, but to no avail. = 她曾请求宽恕，但没有用。


将来时:
He will plead guilty to avoid a longer sentence. = 他将认罪以避免更长的刑期。
She will plead her case before the judge tomorrow. = 她明天将在法官面前陈述她的案情。


虚拟语气:
If he pleaded guilty, he might receive a lighter sentence. = 如果他认罪，他可能会得到较轻的判决。
I wish he had pleaded not guilty. = 我希望他当时没有认罪。


被动语态:
The case was pleaded before the High Court. = 此案已在上诉法院审理。
His innocence was pleaded by his lawyer. = 他的律师为他辩护无罪。


疑问句:
Did he plead guilty or not guilty? = 他认罪还是不认罪？
Will she plead for mercy? = 她会请求宽恕吗？


否定句:
He didn't plead guilty. = 他没有认罪。
She didn't plead for help. = 她没有请求帮助。


PHRASE->
plead guilty = 认罪
plead not guilty = 不认罪
plead for mercy = 请求宽恕
plead with someone = 恳求某人
plead a case = 辩护


词源:
源自古英语 *plēdian，意为“辩论，争论”。  最终源自拉丁语 *placitare,  意为“取悦，使满意”，暗示通过辩论来赢得法官或听众的欢心。
