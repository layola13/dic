slave-trader:/ˈsleɪvˌtreɪdər/| n.|奴隶贩子|A person who buys and sells slaves.|The slave-trader profited immensely from the suffering of others.  奴隶贩子从他人的痛苦中获利颇丰。|近义词：slave merchant, trafficker in human beings|反义词：abolitionist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The slave-trader transports slaves across the ocean. 奴隶贩子将奴隶运送过海洋。
He buys and sells slaves for profit. 他买卖奴隶以获取利润。


一般过去时:
The slave-trader shipped hundreds of slaves to America. 奴隶贩子将数百名奴隶运送到美洲。
He brutally treated the slaves he owned. 他残酷地对待他拥有的奴隶。


现在完成时:
The slave-trader has amassed a fortune from his inhumane business. 奴隶贩子从他没有人性的生意中积累了一笔财富。
He has exploited countless individuals for his own gain. 他为了自己的利益剥削了无数人。


过去完成时:
The slave-trader had already sold most of his slaves before the abolition of slavery. 在废除奴隶制之前，奴隶贩子已经卖掉了大部分奴隶。
The authorities had been investigating his activities for months. 当局已经调查了他的活动数月。


将来时:
The slave-trader will face severe consequences for his actions. 奴隶贩子将为他的行为承担严重的后果。
He will continue his illegal activities until caught. 他将继续他的非法活动，直到被抓住。


虚拟语气:
