ravish: /ˈrævɪʃ/ | vt. |使狂喜；使陶醉；强奸；掠夺；破坏 |

英文解析：To ravish means to fill someone with intense delight or joy, often to the point of overwhelming them. It can also mean to seize or carry off by force, particularly in the context of rape.  The word carries a strong emotional weight and can evoke feelings of both intense pleasure and intense violation depending on the context.

例句：

The beauty of the sunset ravished her senses.  = 夕阳的美丽使她心醉神迷。

The invaders ravished the countryside, leaving destruction in their wake. = 侵略者掠夺了乡村，留下了满目疮痍。


近义词：enchant, delight, enthrall,  (in the sense of rape) violate, assault, abuse

反义词：(in the sense of delight) disappoint, sadden, depress; (in the sense of rape) protect, defend, safeguard
