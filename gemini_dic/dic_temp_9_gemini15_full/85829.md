hazel:/ˈheɪzəl/| n. adj.|榛子；榛色；淡褐色|A hazel is a type of nut, and hazel can also describe a light brown color.  The word is also used to describe eyes of a light brown color.|例句：The squirrels were busy burying hazelnuts for the winter. 松鼠正忙着为冬天埋藏榛子。

The color of her eyes is hazel. 她的眼睛是榛色。|近义词：nut (for the nut), brown, auburn (for the color)|反义词：black (for the color)
