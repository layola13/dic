shrinker:/ˈʃrɪŋkər/| n. |缩小器；精神科医生；（衣服）缩水者|A shrinker is a person or thing that causes something to shrink.  This can refer to a machine that shrinks clothes, a person who shrinks (as in a psychologist or psychiatrist), or something that causes reduction in size. |例句：The new washing machine is a great shrinker, my sweaters are much smaller now. 这台新的洗衣机很会让衣服缩水，我的毛衣现在小多了。|例句：She went to see a shrinker to deal with her anxiety. 她去看精神科医生治疗焦虑。|近义词：reducer, minimizer, psychologist, psychiatrist|反义词：expander, enlarger
