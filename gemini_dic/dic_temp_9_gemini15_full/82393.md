grubbing:/ˈɡrʌbɪŋ/ | v. | 费力地寻找；挖掘；清除杂草；勉强维持生活 |  To search for something persistently and with difficulty; to dig or root around in the earth; to remove weeds or unwanted vegetation; to live in a meager or impoverished way. |例句：He spent hours grubbing around in the attic for old photographs. 他在阁楼里花了几个小时费力地寻找旧照片。

例句：The gardener spent the morning grubbing out weeds from the flower beds. 园丁花了整个上午从花坛里清除杂草。


近义词：digging, searching, foraging, scavenging, scraping by | 反义词： luxuriating, thriving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are grubbing for worms in the garden. = 他们在花园里费力地寻找蚯蚓。
He grubs for gold in the riverbed. = 他在河床上淘金。


一般过去时:
We grubbed around in the old chest for treasure. = 我们在旧箱子里翻找宝藏。
She grubbed out the stubborn weeds with a trowel. = 她用铲子费力地清除顽固的杂草。


现在完成时:
They have been grubbing for food all day. = 他们已经找了一整天的食物了。
He has grubbed out all the weeds in his garden. = 他已经把花园里的杂草都除掉了。


过去完成时:
They had grubbed for days before they found anything valuable. = 他们在找到任何有价值的东西之前已经找了好几天了。
She had grubbed out the old rose bushes before planting new ones. = 在种植新的玫瑰花丛之前，她已经把旧的玫瑰花丛除掉了。


将来时:
We will be grubbing in the dirt all afternoon. = 我们整个下午都会在泥土里挖来挖去。
He will grub for answers in the dusty archives. = 他将在尘土飞扬的档案馆里寻找答案。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would grub around in that old trunk. = 如果我有更多时间，我会仔细翻找那个旧箱子。
I wish I hadn't grubbed out those flowers; they were rare. = 我真希望我没有挖掉那些花；它们是很稀有的。


被动语态:
The weeds were grubbed out by the gardener. = 杂草被园丁除掉了。
The treasure was eventually grubbed up from the seabed. = 宝藏最终从海底被挖了出来。


疑问句:
Are they still grubbing for gold? = 他们还在淘金吗？
What are you grubbing around for? = 你在费力地寻找什么？


否定句:
They didn't grub around much before giving up. = 他们在放弃之前并没有费力地寻找很多。
He didn't grub out the weeds properly, so they grew back. = 他没有彻底清除杂草，所以它们又长出来了。
