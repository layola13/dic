player:/ˈpleɪər/ | n. | 玩家，球员，演员 | A person who plays a game, a sport, or a musical instrument; an actor.  |例句：He's a talented player on the basketball team. 他是篮球队里一名有天赋的球员。

例句：The lead player in the movie was excellent. 这部电影的主演非常出色。


近义词：gamer, performer, actor, athlete | 反义词：spectator, audience


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays basketball every day. = 他每天都打篮球。
She plays the piano beautifully. = 她钢琴弹得很好。


一般过去时:
They played football yesterday. = 他们昨天踢足球了。
He played the guitar at the party. = 他在派对上弹吉他。


现在完成时:
I have played this game many times. = 我玩过很多次这个游戏了。
She has played the violin since she was five. = 她从五岁起就开始拉小提琴了。


过去完成时:
We had played the game before he arrived. = 他来之前我们已经玩过这个游戏了。
They had played many matches before the final. = 决赛之前他们已经参加过很多场比赛了。


将来时:
They will play tennis tomorrow. = 他们明天将打网球。
She will play the role of Juliet. = 她将扮演朱丽叶的角色。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better player, I would win. = 如果我是一个更好的球员，我就会赢。
I wish I could play the guitar like him. = 我希望我能像他一样弹吉他。


被动语态:
The game was played fairly. = 这场比赛打得很公平。
The song was played on the radio. = 这首歌在广播里播放了。


疑问句:
Do you play any instruments? = 你会演奏任何乐器吗？
Who played the winning goal? = 谁进了制胜球？


否定句:
He doesn't play video games anymore. = 他不再玩电子游戏了。
She didn't play well in the match. = 她在比赛中表现得不好。


PHRASE->
key player = 关键人物
power player = 有权势的人物
lead player = 主角，主要演员
supporting player = 配角
star player = 明星球员
