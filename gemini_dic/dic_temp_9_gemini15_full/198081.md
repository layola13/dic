shon:/ʃɒn/ | v. |照耀，发光| shone 是 shine 的过去式和过去分词，表示光线或光亮照耀或发光。它通常指持续性的光亮，而不是短暂的闪光。|The sun shone brightly all day.  太阳整天都明亮地照耀着。
The stars shone in the clear night sky. 星星在晴朗的夜空中闪耀。| glowed, beamed | obscured, darkened |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun shines brightly. = 太阳明亮地照耀着。
The lamp shines a warm light. = 灯发出温暖的光。


一般过去时:
The sun shone brightly yesterday. = 昨天太阳明亮地照耀着。
The moon shone in the night sky. = 月亮在夜空中闪耀。


现在完成时:
The sun has shone all day. = 太阳整天都照耀着。
The star has shone brightly for centuries. = 这颗星星已经明亮地照耀了几个世纪。


过去完成时:
The sun had shone all morning before the clouds came. =  云来之前，太阳整个上午都照耀着。
The light had shone on the painting for years, fading the colors. = 光线照射在画上多年，使颜色褪色。


将来时:
The sun will shine tomorrow. = 明天太阳会照耀。
I hope the sun will shine on our wedding day. = 我希望在我们婚礼那天阳光灿烂。


虚拟语气:
If the sun shone every day, life would be better. = 如果太阳每天都照耀，生活会更好。
I wish the sun shone more often in this city. = 我希望这个城市阳光更多。


被动语态:
(Shone doesn't typically use passive voice because it's an intransitive verb.)  (shine 通常不用被动语态，因为它是不及物动词。)


疑问句:
Did the sun shine yesterday? = 昨天太阳照了吗？
Does the lamp shine brightly enough? = 灯够亮吗？


否定句:
The sun didn't shine yesterday. = 昨天太阳没照。
The stars didn't shine because of the clouds. = 因为有云，星星没有闪耀。
