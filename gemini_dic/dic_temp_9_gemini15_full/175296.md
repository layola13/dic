rainn:/reɪn/ | n. |雨，雨水 |Rain is water that falls from the sky in drops.|例句：The rain is coming down hard.  雨下得很大。|例句：We got caught in the rain. 我们淋雨了。|近义词：shower, downpour, precipitation |反义词：sunshine, drought


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It rains a lot in Seattle. = 西雅图经常下雨。
The rain falls softly. = 雨轻轻地下着。

一般过去时:
It rained heavily yesterday. = 昨天雨下得很大。
The rain stopped an hour ago. = 雨在一小时前停了。

现在完成时:
It has rained all day. = 一整天都在下雨。
It has rained steadily since morning. = 从早上开始就一直在下细雨。

过去完成时:
It had rained all night, so the ground was wet. = 整夜都在下雨，所以地面很湿。
They had been rained out of their game. = 他们的比赛因雨取消了。


将来时:
It will rain tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
It is going to rain soon. = 马上就要下雨了。


虚拟语气:
If it rained tomorrow, we would cancel the picnic. = 如果明天下雨，我们就取消野餐。
I wish it hadn't rained during the game. = 我真希望比赛期间没有下雨。


被动语态:
The field was rained on all night. = 田地整夜都被雨淋着。
The crops are being rained on. = 庄稼正在被雨淋着。


疑问句:
Is it raining outside? = 外面在下雨吗？
Does it often rain here? = 这里经常下雨吗？


否定句:
It didn't rain yesterday. = 昨天没有下雨。
It hasn't rained for weeks. = 好几个星期没下雨了。
