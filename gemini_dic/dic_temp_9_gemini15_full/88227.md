hindgut:/ˈhaɪndɡʌt/| n.|后肠；后肠道|The hindgut is the posterior part of the alimentary canal, extending from the ileum to the anus. It includes the large intestine and rectum.  It plays a crucial role in water absorption and waste elimination.| Example:  The study focused on the microbial communities residing within the hindgut of termites.  这项研究关注白蚁后肠中存在的微生物群落。|近义词：posterior gut, large intestine|反义词：foregut
