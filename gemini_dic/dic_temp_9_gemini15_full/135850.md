nonprosecution: /ˌnɒnˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 不起诉；免于起诉 | The act of deciding not to prosecute someone who has been accused of a crime.  This decision is often made based on insufficient evidence, public interest considerations, or a plea bargain. |

例句：

The prosecutor announced the nonprosecution of the case due to lack of evidence. = 由于证据不足，检察官宣布不起诉此案。

The suspect received nonprosecution in exchange for cooperation with the investigation. = 嫌疑人以配合调查为交换条件获得了不起诉。


近义词：dismissal, exoneration, acquittal


反义词：prosecution, indictment


词性: 名词
