misdiagnosed:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəɡnoʊzd/| adj.|误诊的|Having received an incorrect diagnosis of an illness or condition.|The patient was misdiagnosed with the flu, when actually he had pneumonia.|近义词：incorrectly diagnosed, wrongly diagnosed|反义词：correctly diagnosed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor misdiagnoses many patients every year. = 医生每年误诊许多病人。
He misdiagnoses illnesses due to carelessness. = 他因为粗心而误诊疾病。


一般过去时:
The doctor misdiagnosed my condition. = 医生误诊了我的病情。
They misdiagnosed her illness, leading to a delay in treatment. = 他们误诊了她的疾病，导致治疗延误。


现在完成时:
The hospital has misdiagnosed several cases this month. = 这个医院这个月已经误诊了好几例。
She has been misdiagnosed repeatedly, causing her much distress. = 她被反复误诊，给她带来了很大的痛苦。


过去完成时:
It turned out that he had been misdiagnosed several times before his actual condition was discovered. = 事实证明，在他真正病情被发现之前，他已经被误诊了好几次。
The patient had been misdiagnosed for years, resulting in serious complications. = 这位病人被误诊多年，导致严重的并发症。


将来时:
I fear that they will misdiagnose his symptoms again. = 我担心他们会再次误诊他的症状。
If the doctor is not careful, he will misdiagnose the patient. = 如果医生不小心，他会误诊病人。


虚拟语气:
If the doctor had not misdiagnosed him, he might have recovered sooner. = 如果医生没有误诊他，他可能会恢复得更快。
It would have been a tragedy if she had been misdiagnosed and left untreated. = 如果她被误诊且未经治疗，那将是一场悲剧。


被动语态:
Many patients are misdiagnosed every year due to limited resources. = 由于资源有限，每年都有许多病人被误诊。
The case was misdiagnosed, resulting in a lawsuit. = 此案被误诊，导致诉讼。


疑问句:
Was the patient misdiagnosed? = 病人被误诊了吗？
Could she have been misdiagnosed? = 她有可能被误诊吗？


否定句:
The doctor did not misdiagnose the patient. = 医生没有误诊病人。
He wasn't misdiagnosed; his symptoms were correctly identified. = 他没有被误诊；他的症状被正确识别。
