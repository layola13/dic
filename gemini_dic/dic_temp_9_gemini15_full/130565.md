nonactionably:/ˌnɒnˈækʃənəbli/ | adj. | 不可诉诸法律的，不构成诉讼理由的 | Not capable of being the subject of a lawsuit; not actionable.  This term describes a situation where a claim or grievance cannot be pursued through legal channels.  | 例句：The court ruled that the plaintiff's complaint was nonactionably vague. 法院裁定原告的申诉含糊不清，不构成诉讼理由。

The incident, while regrettable, was deemed nonactionably.  虽然令人遗憾，但此事被认为不构成诉讼理由。 | 近义词：unactionable, irremediable (in this context) | 反义词：actionable, remediable
