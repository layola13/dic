expressing:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪŋ/ | v. |表达，表示 |The act of conveying or communicating information, feelings, or ideas.  It involves making something known or understood.  This can be done through various means, including words, actions, or art. | 例句：Expressing your feelings openly can improve relationships.  表达你的感受可以改善人际关系。


He is expressing his concern about the project. 他正在表达他对这个项目的担忧。


近义词：conveying, communicating, articulating, conveying | 反义词：concealing, suppressing, hiding | 动词
