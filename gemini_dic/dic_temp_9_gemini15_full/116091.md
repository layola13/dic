martyrization:/ˌmɑːrtɪraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |殉道；受难；折磨|The act of making someone suffer as a martyr; the process of being made to suffer or die for a cause or belief. It often implies an unjust or cruel suffering.|例句：The martyrization of the early Christians strengthened their faith.  早期基督徒的殉道加强了他们的信仰。

The systematic martyrization of political opponents was a hallmark of the regime.  对政治对手的有系统迫害是该政权的标志。|近义词：persecution, suffering, victimization |反义词：glorification, celebration, liberation
