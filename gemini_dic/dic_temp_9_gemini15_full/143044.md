outbluffs: /ˌaʊtˈblʌfs/ | vt. | 故意说谎以战胜对手；以更大的谎言战胜（某人） | To deceive someone by making a more convincing false statement than theirs; to win by making a bolder bluff. | 例句：He outbluffed his opponent in the poker game by pretending to have a royal flush. 他在扑克游戏中假装有一手皇家同花顺，从而战胜了他的对手。


例句：The seasoned negotiator outbluffed his younger counterpart, securing a better deal.  经验丰富的谈判者以更大的谎言战胜了他的年轻对手，从而达成了一笔更好的交易。


近义词：outwit, outsmart, deceive, trick, hoodwink


反义词：be outbluffed, be tricked


词性:动词
