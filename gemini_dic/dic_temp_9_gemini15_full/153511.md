pensived:/ˈpɛnsɪvd/| adj.|沉思的；忧郁的；若有所思的 | Thoughtful and quiet, often because of sadness or unhappiness.  The word often implies a pleasant or wistful melancholy, rather than deep despair. |例句：She sat by the window, looking pensive. 她坐在窗边，若有所思。

例句：He fell into a pensive mood after receiving the bad news.  收到坏消息后，他陷入了沉思。


近义词：thoughtful, reflective, meditative, contemplative, wistful, dreamy

反义词：cheerful, joyful, merry, carefree, lighthearted

词性：形容词
