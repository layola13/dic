mousetail: /ˈmaʊsteɪl/ | n. |鼠尾草；(植物)鼠尾草属植物 | A plant, typically with a long, thin flower spike resembling a mouse's tail.  The term often refers to plants in the genus *Myosurus*, but can also be used more broadly for other plants with similar inflorescences. | Example: The meadow was dotted with delicate mousetails swaying in the breeze.  = 草地上点缀着在微风中摇曳的纤细鼠尾草。  | Near synonyms:  grass, weed (in some contexts) | Antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym) | noun
