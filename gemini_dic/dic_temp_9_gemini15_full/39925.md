constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/| vt. |压缩；束缚；限制 |To make something narrower or tighter, or to become narrower or tighter.| The snake constricted its coils around its prey.  蛇用身体缠绕着猎物。|The tight collar constricted his breathing.  紧密的衣领使他呼吸困难。|近义词：squeeze, tighten, compress |反义词：expand, widen, loosen |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band constricts the flow of blood. = 乐队限制了血流。
He constricts his emotions. = 他克制自己的情绪。


一般过去时:
The cold weather constricted the pipes. = 寒冷的天气使管道收缩。
She constricted her spending. = 她减少了开支。


现在完成时:
