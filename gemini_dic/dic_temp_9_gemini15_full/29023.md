carton:/ˈkɑːrtən/| n.|纸盒；纸箱|A container made of cardboard, typically used for packaging goods.|例句：He packed the books in a sturdy carton. 他把书装进一个结实的纸箱里。|例句：The carton of milk was almost empty. 这盒牛奶几乎空了。|近义词：box, container|反义词：无


USAGE->

The carton holds twelve eggs. = 这个纸盒装12个鸡蛋。
We use cartons to ship our products. = 我们用纸箱来运输我们的产品。
