noncling: /ˈnɒnklɪŋ/ | adj. |非粘性的，不粘的| Not clinging; not adhesive; not tending to stick or adhere.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" to the word "clinging," indicating a lack or absence of the quality of clinging.|例句：The noncling fabric made the dress comfortable to wear. 这款不粘的布料使这件衣服穿起来很舒服。

The noncling design of the wallpaper made it easy to remove.  壁纸的不粘设计使其易于去除。|近义词：tack-free, non-adhesive, loose|反义词：clingy, adhesive, sticky|
