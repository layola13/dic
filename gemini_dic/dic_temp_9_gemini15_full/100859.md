introspects:/ˈɪntrəʊspekt/ | v. |内省，反省 | To examine one's own thoughts or feelings.  |例句：She introspected on her recent failures and tried to learn from them. 她反省了她最近的失败，并试图从中吸取教训。|近义词：reflect, contemplate, ponder |反义词：ignore, disregard |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He introspects daily to improve himself. = 他每天进行内省以提升自我。
She introspects after every major decision. = 每次做出重大决定后，她都会进行反省。


一般过去时:
I introspected deeply after the argument. = 争吵过后，我进行了深刻的反省。
They introspected on their shared experiences. = 他们对共同的经历进行了反思。


现在完成时:
She has introspected a lot since the breakup. = 分手后，她进行了大量的反省。
He has introspected on his life choices. = 他一直在反思自己的人生选择。


过去完成时:
I had introspected for weeks before reaching a conclusion. = 在得出结论之前，我已经反省了好几个星期。
They had introspected extensively before making their decision. = 在做出决定之前，他们进行了广泛的反思。


将来时:
I will introspect on this matter further. = 我将进一步思考这个问题。
She will introspect before responding. = 她会在回应之前进行反思。


虚拟语气:
If I had introspected more, I would have made a better decision. = 如果我当时更多地进行反省，我就会做出更好的决定。
I wish I had introspected more before acting impulsively. = 我希望在冲动行事之前，我能够更多地进行反省。


被动语态:
Her feelings were introspected upon by the therapist. = 她的感受受到了治疗师的仔细分析。 (This is less common)
His behaviour wasn't introspected upon enough. = 他的行为没有得到足够的反思。 (This is less common)


疑问句:
Do you introspect regularly? = 你经常进行内省吗？
Does he introspect before making important decisions? = 他在做重要决定之前会进行内省吗？


否定句:
He doesn't introspect enough. = 他反省得不够。
She didn't introspect before speaking. = 她说话前没有进行反省。


PHRASE->
introspect deeply = 深入反省
introspect carefully = 仔细反省
introspect honestly = 诚实地反省
introspect objectively = 客观地反省
introspect critically = 批判性地反省
