castrations:/kæsˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. |阉割；去势 | The act of removing the testicles of a male animal or human.  This term often carries negative connotations, relating to violence, control, or a violation of bodily autonomy.  It can refer to the surgical procedure itself or the state of being castrated. |例句：The illegal castrations of the animals caused public outrage.  动物的非法阉割行为激起了民愤。

例句：Historical accounts detail the widespread castrations practiced in some ancient societies.  历史记载详细描述了某些古代社会广泛存在的阉割行为。


近义词：emasculation, sterilization (in the context of rendering infertile) | 反义词：procreation, fertilization
