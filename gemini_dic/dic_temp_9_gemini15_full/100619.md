intimidatingly: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人害怕地；使人胆怯地 | in a way that makes someone feel intimidated;  threateningly. |例句：He stared intimidatingly at the other team. 他用吓人的目光盯着对方球队。


例句：The large dog barked intimidatingly, causing the child to run away.  那条大狗吓人地狂吠，导致孩子跑开了。


近义词：threateningly, dauntingly, menacingly, fearsomely


反义词：reassuringly, comfortingly, encouragingly


词性: 副词
