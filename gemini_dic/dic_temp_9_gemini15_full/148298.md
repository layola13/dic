oversoftness: /ˌoʊvərˈsɒftnəs/ | /ˌoʊvərˈsɔːftnəs/ | n. | 过分柔软；柔软过度 | Excessive softness; an extreme degree of softness.  This word describes a condition or quality where something is too soft, often to the point of being undesirable or lacking necessary firmness.  It implies an excess of softness beyond what is considered ideal or appropriate.


例句：

The oversoftness of the mattress made it uncomfortable to sleep on.  = 床垫太软了，睡着不舒服。

The oversoftness of the clay prevented it from holding its shape. = 粘土太软了，无法保持形状。


近义词：excessive softness, undue softness, limpness, flabbiness

反义词：firmness, rigidity, hardness, stiffness


词性: 名词
