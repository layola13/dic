malt:/mɔːlt/| n.|麦芽|Germinated barley or other grain, used in brewing and distilling.  It's produced by allowing grain to sprout, activating enzymes that convert starches into sugars.|例句：The brewer used high-quality malt to create his award-winning beer.  酿酒师用优质麦芽酿造了他屡获殊荣的啤酒。|近义词：germinated grain|反义词：ungermianted grain|名词
