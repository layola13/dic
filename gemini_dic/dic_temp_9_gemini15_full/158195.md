platonizer:/ˈpleɪtənaɪzər/ | n. |柏拉图主义者；模仿柏拉图的人|One who interprets or applies the philosophies of Plato.  Someone who engages in abstract or idealistic thinking, often to the exclusion of practical concerns.  A person who excessively idealizes or romanticizes things.|例句：He's such a platonizer, always lost in philosophical debates and ignoring the realities of life. 他是个十足的柏拉图主义者，总是沉迷于哲学辩论，而忽略了生活的现实。


例句：She's a platonizer; her romantic relationships are always doomed because she expects unrealistic perfection. 她是个理想主义者，她的爱情关系总是注定要失败，因为她期望不切实际的完美。


近义词：idealist, dreamer, visionary, philosopher


反义词：realist, pragmatist, materialist


词性:名词
