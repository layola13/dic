mislabels:/ˈmɪsleɪbəlz/| v.|误标，错贴标签|To label incorrectly or inappropriately.|The company mislabeled the product, leading to customer confusion. 公司对产品贴错了标签，导致顾客困惑。|近义词：misidentify, misname, wrongly label|反义词：correctly label, accurately label|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mislabel the products regularly. = 他们经常给产品贴错标签。
The store mislabels many of its items. = 这家商店很多商品都贴错标签了。


一般过去时:
The factory mislabeled a batch of goods last week. = 工厂上周错贴了一批货物的标签。
He mislabeled the files, causing a delay in the project. = 他给文件贴错了标签，导致项目延误。


现在完成时:
They have mislabeled several packages this month. = 这个月他们已经贴错好几个包裹的标签了。
The company has mislabeled its products repeatedly, damaging its reputation. = 这家公司反复给产品贴错标签，损害了其声誉。

将来时:
