carbonising: /ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 碳化；使成炭 | To convert (a substance) into carbon or charcoal; to char.  The process involves the removal of volatile constituents, leaving behind primarily carbon. | 例句：The wood was carbonising in the intense heat.  木材在高温下正在碳化。


The intense fire carbonised the remains of the building.  猛烈的火灾将建筑物的残骸碳化了。


近义词：charring, coking | 反义词：None directly opposite, but  ‘decarbonizing’ is a contrasting process. | 词性:动词
