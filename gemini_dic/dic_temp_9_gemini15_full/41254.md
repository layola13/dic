corbels:/ˈkɔːrbəlz/| n.|托座，壁架|A corbel is a structural element projecting from a wall to support a weight above it, often decorative.  They are commonly used in architecture to support beams, shelves, or other architectural features.  |例句：The medieval church featured intricately carved corbels supporting the vaulted ceiling. 中世纪教堂以支撑拱形天花板的复杂雕刻托座为特色。

The ancient Roman aqueducts utilized corbels to help distribute the weight of the water channels. 古罗马渡槽利用托座来帮助分配水道的重量。|近义词：bracket, console,  projecting support|反义词：无
