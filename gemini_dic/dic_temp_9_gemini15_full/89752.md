hopplebush:/ˈhɒpəlˌbʊʃ/| n. |荚蒾属植物|A shrub or small tree of the genus Viburnum, especially Viburnum lantana, having clusters of white flowers followed by red berries that turn black when ripe.  The name likely derives from the way the berries are clustered and possibly their use in making a kind of hopped drink. |The hopplebush grew wild along the hedgerows.|近义词：Viburnum, wayfaring tree|反义词:无
