misregulate:/ˌmɪsˈreɡjuleɪt/| vt.|错误调节；管理不善|To regulate incorrectly or ineffectively; to manage or control badly.|The government's misregulation of the financial market led to a crisis. 政府对金融市场的错误监管导致了危机。|regulate, mismanage|properly regulate, manage well|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misregulate the traffic flow. = 他们错误地调节交通流量。
The company misregulates its waste disposal. = 这家公司管理不善其废物处理。


一般过去时:
The factory misregulated its production process last year. = 去年这家工厂错误地调节了其生产流程。
The previous administration misregulated the healthcare system. = 上届政府管理不善医疗体系。


现在完成时:

将来时:
