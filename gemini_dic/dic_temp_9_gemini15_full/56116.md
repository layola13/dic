doctrinalism:/ˈdɒktrɪnəlɪzəm/ | n. |教条主义；教义论 | The rigid adherence to doctrines or tenets, often without regard for practical considerations or differing viewpoints.  It emphasizes the importance of established doctrines and their literal interpretation, sometimes leading to inflexibility and intolerance. |  The professor's doctrinalism stifled any critical discussion in the class. 教授的教条主义扼杀了课堂上任何批判性讨论。  His doctrinalism prevented him from considering alternative solutions. 他的教条主义使他无法考虑其他解决方案。 | dogmatism, rigidity, orthodoxy | pragmatism, flexibility, open-mindedness | 名词
