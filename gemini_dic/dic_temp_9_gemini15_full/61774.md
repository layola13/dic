impeach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/| vt.|弹劾；指控；质疑|To formally accuse a public official of misconduct in office.  This typically involves a formal process with specific procedures and potential consequences, such as removal from office.|The House of Representatives impeached the president.众议院弹劾了总统。|accuse, indict, charge|vindicate, exonerate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The House impeaches officials for serious crimes. = 众议院因严重犯罪而弹劾官员。
He impeaches the accuracy of the report. = 他质疑报告的准确性。


一般过去时:
The Senate impeached him. = 参议院弹劾了他。
They impeached the witness's testimony. = 他们质疑证人的证词。


现在完成时:
The court has impeached the evidence presented. = 法院已经对提交的证据提出了质疑。
Congress has impeached several presidents in history. = 历史上国会已经弹劾过几位总统。


过去完成时:
The lawyer had impeached the witness's credibility before the trial. = 律师在审判前已经质疑了证人的可信度。
The committee had impeached the validity of the contract. = 委员会已经质疑了合同的有效性。


将来时:
The opposition party will impeach the prime minister. = 反对党将弹劾首相。
They will impeach the findings of the investigation. = 他们将质疑调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If they had enough evidence, they would impeach him. = 如果他们有足够的证据，他们就会弹劾他。
I wish they would impeach the corrupt officials. = 我希望他们能弹劾那些腐败的官员。


被动语态:
The president was impeached by the House. = 总统被众议院弹劾。
The witness's testimony was impeached by the defense. = 证人的证词被辩方质疑。


疑问句:
Will they impeach the governor? = 他们会弹劾州长吗？
Has the defendant's character been impeached? = 被告的品格受到质疑了吗？


否定句:
The court did not impeach the witness. = 法院没有质疑证人。
They did not impeach the president. = 他们没有弹劾总统。


PHRASE->

impeach a statement: 驳斥一项声明
