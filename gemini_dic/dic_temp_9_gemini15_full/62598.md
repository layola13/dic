enfeoff:/ˈɛnfiːf/| vt.|授予土地所有权；给予封地|To grant (land) in feudal tenure; to invest with a fief.  The term is archaic and refers to the legal process of transferring land ownership under the feudal system.|例句：The lord enfeoffed his loyal vassal with a large tract of land.  领主将一大片土地授予了他忠诚的封臣。

The king enfeoffed his son with a dukedom. 国王将一个公国封给了他的儿子。|近义词：grant, bestow, invest|反义词：revoke, confiscate|动词
