/ˈælɪɡeɪtər/| n. |短吻鳄|A large reptile of the crocodilian family, inhabiting freshwater habitats in the southeastern United States and China. They are characterized by a broad, flat head and a shorter, wider snout than crocodiles.|Example: The alligator lurked in the murky water, waiting for its prey.  这条短吻鳄潜伏在浑浊的水中，等待着它的猎物。|近义词：crocodile (but with key differences in snout shape and habitat)|反义词：无|名词
