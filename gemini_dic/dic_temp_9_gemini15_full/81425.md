grateful:/ˈɡreɪtfəl/| adj. |感激的，感谢的|Feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful.|I am grateful for your help. 我很感激你的帮助。|thankful, appreciative, indebted|ungrateful, unappreciative, thankless|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am grateful for your kindness. = 我感激你的好意。
We are grateful to have you as a friend. = 我们很感激有你这样的朋友。


一般过去时:
I was grateful for the opportunity. = 我很感激这次机会。
She was grateful that he helped her. = 她很感激他帮助了她。


现在完成时:
I have been grateful for all your support. = 我一直很感激你们所有的支持。
They have been grateful for the chance to learn. = 他们一直很感激学习的机会。


过去完成时:
I had been grateful for his guidance before. = 我之前一直很感激他的指导。
She had been grateful for the warm welcome. = 她一直很感激热烈的欢迎。


将来时:
I will be grateful for your advice. = 我会感激你的建议。
We will be grateful to receive your feedback. = 我们将感激收到你的反馈。


虚拟语气:
I would be grateful if you could help me. = 如果你能帮我，我会非常感激。
It would be grateful if you could attend the meeting. = 如果你能参加会议，我们将不胜感激。


被动语态:
His help was gratefully received. = 他的帮助受到了感激。
Their efforts were gratefully acknowledged. = 他们的努力得到了感激的承认。


疑问句:
Are you grateful for what they did? = 你感激他们所做的一切吗？
Isn't she grateful for the gift? = 她难道不感激这份礼物吗？


否定句:
I am not grateful for his interference. = 我不感激他的干涉。
They were not grateful for his criticism. = 他们不感激他的批评。


PHRASE->
