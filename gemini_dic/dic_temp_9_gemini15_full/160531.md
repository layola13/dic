porker:/ˈpɔːrkər/| n.|阉猪，肥猪|A pig, especially a male pig that has been castrated.  The term often implies a fattened pig raised for meat.|例句：The farmer raised several porkers for the upcoming county fair.  这个农民养了几头肥猪准备参加即将到来的县集会。
例句：We had roast porker for Sunday dinner.  我们周日晚餐吃的是烤乳猪。|近义词：pig, hog, swine|反义词：lean pig (相对词)|词性:名词
