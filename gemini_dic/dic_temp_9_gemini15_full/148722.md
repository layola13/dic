overtraining: /ˌoʊvərˈtreɪnɪŋ/ | n. |过度训练|The condition of training too intensely or too frequently, leading to decreased performance, injury, and burnout.|例句：Overtraining can lead to fatigue, injury, and decreased performance.  过度训练会导致疲劳、受伤和成绩下降。

例句：He experienced a period of overtraining after trying to increase his training volume too quickly. 他在试图过快增加训练量后经历了一段过度训练的时期。


近义词：overexertion, overwork, strain|反义词：undertraining, rest, recovery|词性：名词
