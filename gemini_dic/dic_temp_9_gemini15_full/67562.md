extraphysical: /ˌɛkstrəˈfɪzɪkəl/ | adj. | 超物理的，形而上的 |  Beyond the physical realm; relating to or characterized by phenomena that cannot be explained by physical laws.  It often refers to spiritual or metaphysical experiences and concepts. |


例句：

The extraphysical experiences described in the book were quite compelling.  = 书中描述的超物理体验非常引人入胜。

Many believe in an extraphysical world beyond our senses. = 许多人相信在我们感官之外存在着一个超物理世界。


近义词：metaphysical, spiritual, paranormal, supernatural


反义词：physical, material, tangible


词性：形容词
