naturalization:/ˌnætʃərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |归化；入籍；使自然化 |The process by which a person who was not born in a country becomes a citizen of that country.  It involves fulfilling certain legal requirements and taking an oath of allegiance. |例句：He achieved naturalization after living in the US for five years. 他在美国住了五年后获得了入籍资格。|例句：The naturalization ceremony was a moving experience for the new citizens.  归化仪式对新公民来说是一次感人的经历。|近义词：citizenship, incorporation |反义词：expatriation, denaturalization |名词
