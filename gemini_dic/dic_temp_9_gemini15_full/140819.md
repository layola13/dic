on-line: /ˌɒnˈlaɪn/ /ˌɑːnˈlaɪn/ | adj. | 在线，联网的 | Connected to or operating through a computer network.  |例句：The system is on-line and ready for use.  系统已上线并准备使用。|例句：We'll need to go on-line to access the database.我们需要上线才能访问数据库。|近义词：online, connected, networked |反义词：offline, disconnected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop is online. = 这家店在线营业。
We are online now. = 我们现在在线。

一般过去时:
The website was online yesterday. = 网站昨天是上线的。
They were online for hours. = 他们在线好几个小时了。

现在完成时:
I have been online all morning. = 我整个上午都在线。
The server has been online since Monday. = 服务器从星期一开始就一直在线。

过去完成时:
The system had been online for a week before the crash. = 系统在崩溃前已经上线一周了。
They had been online for hours before they logged off. = 他们在下线前已经在线好几个小时了。

将来时:
The new service will be online next week. = 新服务将于下周上线。
We will be online later tonight. = 我们今晚稍后会上线。

虚拟语气:
If the system were online, we could access the data. = 如果系统在线，我们就能访问数据。
I wish the website were online. = 我希望网站能上线。

被动语态:
The website is being brought online. = 网站正在上线。
The system was taken offline for maintenance. = 系统因维护而下线。

疑问句:

Are you online right now? = 你现在在线吗？

否定句:
The website is not online. = 网站不在线。
I was not online yesterday. = 我昨天不在线。
