expungement:/ɪkˈspʌndʒmənt/| n. |清除；删除；注销|The act of removing or erasing something, especially a record or entry from an official document or database.  This often refers to criminal records, but can also apply to other types of records.|例句：He applied for expungement of his criminal record. 他申请清除他的犯罪记录。
例句：The expungement of the data was a complex process.  数据的删除是一个复杂的过程。|近义词：removal, erasure, deletion, cancellation|反义词：inclusion, retention, preservation
