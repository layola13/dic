re-dialing:/ˌriːˈdaɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 重拨电话 | The act of dialing a telephone number again, usually after a failed attempt to connect.  This often involves using a feature on the phone to automatically redial the last number. |  例句：After the line went dead, I tried re-dialing, but still couldn't get through.  线路中断后，我尝试重拨，但仍然无法接通。

例句：He kept re-dialing her number, hoping she would answer. 他不断重拨她的号码，希望她会接电话。


近义词：calling back, dialing again | 反义词：disconnecting, hanging up | 词性:动词
