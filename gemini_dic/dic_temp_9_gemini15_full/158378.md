pleas:/ˈpliːz/ | interj. | 请；拜托 | An interjection used to politely request something or express a wish. | Please pass the salt. 请把盐递给我。|近义词：please, kindly | 反义词:  None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Please help me. = 请帮助我。
Please be quiet. = 请安静。


一般过去时:
I pleased him with my performance. = 我用我的表现取悦了他。
He pleased her by buying her flowers. = 他买花取悦了她。


现在完成时:
I have pleased my parents with my good grades. = 我用我的好成绩取悦了我的父母。
She has pleased her teacher with her hard work. = 她用她的努力工作取悦了她的老师。


过去完成时:
I had pleased my teacher by completing my homework early. = 我通过提前完成作业取悦了老师。
She had pleased her boss by finishing the project ahead of schedule. = 她通过提前完成项目取悦了老板。


将来时:
I will please you with my actions. = 我将用我的行动取悦你。
She will please her friends by inviting them to her party. = 她将通过邀请朋友参加她的派对来取悦他们。


虚拟语气:
If I pleased him, he would be happy. = 如果我取悦了他，他会很高兴的。
I wish I could please everyone. = 我希望我能取悦所有人。


被动语态:
The audience was pleased with the performance. = 观众对演出很满意。
He was pleased to be invited to the party. = 他很高兴被邀请参加聚会。


疑问句:
(Please is not typically used in questions, except perhaps rhetorically)  Would you please help me? = 你能帮我一下吗？


否定句:
Please don't go. = 请别走。
Don't please him; he's spoiled. = 别惯着他；他已经很被宠坏了。


PHRASE->
