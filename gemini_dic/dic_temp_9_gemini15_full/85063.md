hard-drive: /ˈhɑːrd draɪv/ | n. | 硬盘 | A hard drive is a high-capacity, non-volatile storage device that stores and retrieves digital information using rapidly rotating platters and magnetic heads.  It's a crucial component in most computers and other digital devices. | Example: My computer's hard drive crashed, and I lost all my data. 我的电脑硬盘崩溃了，我丢失了所有数据。 | 近义词：hard disk, storage device | 反义词：RAM (random access memory)
