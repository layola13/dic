marred:/mɑːrd/| v. adj.|损毁的；有瑕疵的；玷污的|To impair the appearance, quality, or value of something.  To spoil or damage the beauty or perfection of something.|例句：The party was marred by a sudden downpour.  这场派对被突如其来的暴雨破坏了。

The beautiful painting was marred by a large scratch.  美丽的画作被一条大划痕破坏了。|近义词：spoiled, damaged, impaired, defaced, blemished|反义词：improved, enhanced, perfected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flaws mar the overall beauty of the sculpture. = 瑕疵损害了雕塑的整体美感。
Rain often mars outdoor events. = 雨水常常会破坏户外活动。


一般过去时:
The accident marred his reputation. = 那次事故损害了他的名誉。
A single mistake marred an otherwise perfect performance. = 一个小小的错误破坏了本来完美的演出。


现在完成时:
The scandal has marred the company's image. = 这场丑闻损害了公司的形象。
Years of neglect have marred the beauty of the old house. = 多年的忽视损坏了这座老房子的美丽。


过去完成时:
The argument had marred their relationship for years. = 那场争吵多年来一直破坏着他们的关系。
The previous owner's poor maintenance had marred the car's value. = 前车主的糟糕维护损害了汽车的价值。


将来时:
The upcoming storm will mar the picnic. = 即将来临的风暴将会破坏野餐。
Poor planning will mar the project's success. = 糟糕的计划将会损害项目的成功。


虚拟语气:
If only the rain hadn't marred the wedding. = 如果雨没有破坏婚礼就好了。
I wish the noise hadn't marred the concert. = 我希望噪音没有破坏音乐会就好了。


被动语态:
His career was marred by a series of unfortunate events. = 他的职业生涯被一系列不幸的事件所破坏。
The painting was marred by a careless touch. = 这幅画被粗心的一笔给毁了。


疑问句:
Was the trip marred by bad weather? = 旅行是否被坏天气破坏了？
Has the project been marred by delays? = 项目是否因为延误而受损？


否定句:
The movie wasn't marred by poor acting. = 这部电影并没有因为糟糕的演技而受损。
Despite the challenges, the event wasn't marred by any significant problems. = 尽管面临挑战，活动并没有出现任何重大问题。


PHRASE->
marred by = 被…破坏
marred with = 充满…的（负面含义）
severely marred = 严重损坏
deeply marred = 深受损害
irrevocably marred = 不可挽回地损毁
