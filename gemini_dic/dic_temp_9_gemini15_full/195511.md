semipassiveness:/ˌsemiˈpæsɪvnəs/| n. |半被动性，半被动状态 |The state of being partly passive; a condition in which an entity or system exhibits characteristics of both passive and active behavior.  It often implies a reluctance or inability to fully engage or react, a state between complete passivity and full activity. | Example: The company showed semipassiveness in responding to the market changes, leading to a loss of market share. 公司对市场变化的反应表现出半被动性，导致市场份额的损失。|近义词: partial passivity, qualified passivity, subdued activity |反义词:  full activity, complete engagement, proactive behavior |名词
