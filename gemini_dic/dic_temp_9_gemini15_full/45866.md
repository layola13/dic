cutaneous: /kjuːˈteɪniəs/ | adj. | 皮肤的；皮下的 | Relating to the skin.  | The ointment is for cutaneous application.  | 近义词：dermal, integumentary | 反义词：internal, visceral


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rash is cutaneous. = 皮疹是皮肤病。
This cream is used for cutaneous problems. = 这种面霜用于治疗皮肤问题。


一般过去时:
The doctor examined his cutaneous lesions. = 医生检查了他的皮肤损害。
Her cutaneous reaction to the medication was mild. = 她对药物的皮肤反应很轻微。


现在完成时:
She has experienced several cutaneous infections. = 她经历过几次皮肤感染。
He has developed a cutaneous allergy to nickel. = 他对镍过敏。


过去完成时:
The patient had already displayed cutaneous symptoms before the diagnosis. = 在诊断之前，病人已经表现出皮肤症状。
They had noticed the cutaneous changes a week ago. = 他们在一周前就注意到了皮肤的变化。


将来时:
The new treatment will improve cutaneous health. = 新疗法将改善皮肤健康。
He will likely experience some cutaneous side effects. = 他可能会经历一些皮肤副作用。


虚拟语气:
If the reaction were purely cutaneous, it would be less serious. = 如果反应纯粹是皮肤性的，那就不会那么严重。
I wish my cutaneous condition would improve faster. = 我希望我的皮肤状况能更快好转。


被动语态:
The sample was analyzed for cutaneous bacteria. = 样本被分析以检测皮肤细菌。
The skin can be damaged by excessive sun exposure, leading to cutaneous problems. = 皮肤会因过度日晒而受损，从而导致皮肤问题。


疑问句:
Is the pain cutaneous or internal? = 疼痛是皮肤上的还是内脏的？
Are there any other cutaneous manifestations of the disease? = 这种疾病还有其他皮肤表现吗？


否定句:
The problem is not cutaneous; it's internal. = 问题不在于皮肤；它在内部。
The medication did not cause any noticeable cutaneous reactions. = 该药物没有引起任何明显的皮肤反应。
