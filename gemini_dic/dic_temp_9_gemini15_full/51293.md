detacher:/dɪˈtætʃər/| n. |分离器，拆卸器，脱离者|A detacher is a person or thing that detaches something.  It can refer to a device used to separate or remove something, or a person who performs such an action. |例句：The mechanic used a special detacher to remove the engine from the car. 机械师用一个特殊的拆卸器将发动机从汽车上拆下。

The surgeon acted as a detacher, carefully separating the tissues. 外科医生充当分离器，小心地分离组织。|近义词：separator, remover, disconnector |反义词：attacher, connecter, joiner
