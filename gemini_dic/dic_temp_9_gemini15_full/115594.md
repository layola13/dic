marble:/ˈmɑːrbl/ | n. |大理石；弹珠；弹子 |A metamorphic rock composed of recrystallized carbonate minerals, most commonly calcite or dolomite.  It is often used in sculpture and building materials due to its beauty and durability. Also refers to a small, usually glass, sphere used in games.|例句：The statue was carved from a single block of white marble.  这尊雕像是用整块白色大理石雕刻而成的。

The children played marbles in the playground.孩子们在操场上玩弹珠。|近义词：limestone, stone, sphere |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with materials like wood or plastic.
