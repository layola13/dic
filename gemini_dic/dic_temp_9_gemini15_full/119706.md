midrash:/ˈmɪdrɑːʃ/| n. |释经，犹太教的诠释性教义|Midrash refers to a genre of rabbinic literature that interprets the Hebrew Bible. It involves exploring the text's meaning through various methods, including homiletical exposition, allegory, and the use of parables.  The goal is not simply to understand the literal meaning, but also to extract deeper spiritual and ethical lessons.|Example:  The study of Midrash offers valuable insights into the development of Jewish thought and practice.  犹太教释经的研究为犹太思想和实践的发展提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：exegesis, interpretation, commentary |反义词：N/A
