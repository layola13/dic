going-up: /ˈɡoʊɪŋ ʌp/ | adj. | 上升的；上涨的 |  Describes something that is increasing in value, level, or amount.  It often implies a continuous or gradual increase, rather than a sudden jump. | 例句：The price of gas is going up. = 油价正在上涨。

The stock market is going up steadily. = 股市正在稳步上涨。


近义词：rising, increasing, escalating, climbing, ascending | 反义词：going down, decreasing, falling, declining, descending | 词性:形容词 (participle used adjectivally)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature is going up. = 温度正在上升。
The demand for electric cars is going up. = 对电动汽车的需求正在上升。

一般过去时:
The water level was going up rapidly. = 水位正在迅速上涨。
The number of participants was going up during the event. = 活动期间参与人数不断增加。

现在完成时:
The sea level has been going up for decades. = 海平面几十年来一直在上升。
The company's profits have been going up since the new CEO took over. = 自从新任CEO上任以来，公司的利润一直在增长。

过去完成时:
The river had been going up all night. = 河水整夜都在上涨。
The cost of living had been going up before the recent economic downturn. = 在最近的经济衰退之前，生活成本一直在上涨。

将来时:
Interest rates will be going up next year. = 利率明年将会上涨。
The population of the city is going to be going up significantly. = 城市人口将大幅增长。

虚拟语气:
If the inflation rate were going up, we would have to adjust our budget. = 如果通货膨胀率上涨，我们就必须调整预算。
I wish the value of my investments were going up faster. = 我希望我的投资升值速度更快。

被动语态:
The prices are being carefully monitored to see if they are going up. = 正在仔细监测价格，看看它们是否上涨。
The matter is being discussed to see how to prevent the costs going up. = 正在讨论这个问题，看看如何防止成本上升。

疑问句:
Is the unemployment rate going up? = 失业率正在上升吗？
Are house prices going up in this area? = 这个地区的房价在上涨吗？

否定句:
The value of the currency isn't going up. = 货币的价值没有上涨。
The sales figures aren't going up as quickly as we hoped. = 销售额的增长速度不如我们预期的那样快。


PHRASE->
Going up in smoke: 付之一炬；化为乌有
Going up against: 与…对抗；与…竞争
Going up the ladder: 晋升；提升
Going up against the odds: 逆境求胜；克服困难
Going up in flames: 燃烧；着火
