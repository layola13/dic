embodier:/ɪmˈbɒdi/| vt.| 体现；包含；使具体化 | To represent or express something in a physical or visible form.  | The statue embodies the spirit of the nation.  这尊雕像体现了民族精神。 |  represent, incarnate, exemplify |  abstract, formless | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embodies the ideals of his generation. 他体现了他那一代人的理想。
The novel embodies the author's experiences. 这部小说体现了作者的经历。

一般过去时:
The painting embodied the artist's emotions. 这幅画体现了艺术家的情感。
The film embodied the struggles of the working class. 这部电影体现了工人的斗争。

现在完成时:
Her work has embodied many different styles. 她的作品融合了许多不同的风格。
This building has embodied architectural innovation. 这座建筑体现了建筑创新。

过去完成时:
The sculpture had embodied the artist's vision for years. 这尊雕塑多年来一直体现着艺术家的愿景。
The constitution had embodied the principles of freedom and equality. 这部宪法体现了自由和平等的原则。

将来时:
The new museum will embody cutting-edge technology. 新博物馆将体现尖端技术。
The play will embody the themes of love and loss. 这部戏剧将体现爱与失去的主题。

虚拟语气:
If the design embodied more modern ideas, it would be more appealing. 如果设计体现更多现代理念，它会更吸引人。
I wish the project embodied a clearer sense of purpose. 我希望这个项目体现更明确的目标。

被动语态:
The principles of democracy are embodied in the constitution. 民主原则体现在宪法中。
The company's values are embodied in its mission statement. 公司的价值观体现在其使命宣言中。

疑问句:
Does this painting embody the artist's true feelings? 这幅画是否体现了艺术家的真实情感？
Does this policy embody the government's intentions? 这项政策是否体现了政府的意图？

否定句:
The film did not embody the complexity of the historical event. 这部电影没有体现历史事件的复杂性。
The building does not embody the modern architectural style. 这座建筑没有体现现代建筑风格。
