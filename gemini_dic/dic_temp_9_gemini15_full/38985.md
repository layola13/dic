confirmability:/ˌkɒnfɜːməˈbɪləti/| n. |可确认性，可验证性 |The degree to which research findings can be confirmed or verified by others.  It refers to the objectivity and repeatability of research.  High confirmability means that the findings are not dependent on the researcher's biases or subjective interpretations. |The study's high confirmability was ensured by the use of multiple data sources and rigorous analysis methods. 这项研究的高度可确认性是通过使用多个数据来源和严格的分析方法来保证的。|verifiability, validity, reliability |unreliability, invalidity, subjectivity |名词
