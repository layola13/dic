collator:/ˈkɒləteɪtər/| n. |校对者；整理者；对照器|A collator is a person or a machine that compares and arranges items in a specific order, often for the purpose of sorting or verifying data.  It can refer to a person who checks and corrects errors in a text, or a machine used to combine multiple sheets of paper into a single document. |例句：The collator carefully checked the manuscript for errors before sending it to the printer. 校对者仔细检查了手稿中的错误，然后才将其送去印刷。The collator merged the different sections of the report into a single document.  整理者将报告的不同部分合并成一个文档。|近义词：sorter, comparer, verifier, proofreader |反义词：disorganizer, confuser
