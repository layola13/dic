mustachio:/məˈstæʃioʊ/| n. |胡子（上唇的）|A mustache is the hair that grows on a man's upper lip.  It can be styled in various ways, from neatly trimmed to long and flowing. |He sported a magnificent handlebar mustache.|近义词：mustache, upper lip hair|反义词： beardless
