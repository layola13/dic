outwrenches: /ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃɪz/ | v. | 超过…的力气；比…更有力 | To surpass in strength or power; to overpower. |  This new engine outwrenches its predecessor. 这台新发动机比它的前代产品更有力。|近义词：overpower, surpass, exceed | 反义词：be weaker than, be inferior to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong man outwrenches the weaker one.  强壮的人胜过弱者。
This machine outwrenches all others in its class. 这台机器比同类机器都更有力。


一般过去时:
He outwrenched his opponent in the arm wrestling match. 他在掰手腕比赛中战胜了他的对手。
The team outwrenched their rivals in the final game.  这个团队在决赛中战胜了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
She has outwrenched all expectations with her performance. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。
The company has outwrenched its competitors in the market.  这家公司在市场上战胜了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
He had outwrenched his doubts and fears before the competition.  在比赛之前，他已经克服了自己的疑虑和恐惧。
The engineers had outwrenched the technical challenges before the launch.  工程师们在发射前克服了技术挑战。


将来时:
They will outwrench their opponents with their superior technology. 他们将凭借其先进的技术战胜对手。
This new design will outwrench all previous models in efficiency.  这种新的设计将在效率上超过所有以前的型号。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outwrenched his rival. 如果他训练更刻苦，他就能战胜他的对手。
If they had better resources, they could have outwrenched the competition. 如果他们有更好的资源，他们就能战胜竞争对手。


被动语态:
His opponent was outwrenched by his superior strength. 他的对手被他的强大力量击败了。
The old system was outwrenched by the new, more efficient one. 旧系统被新的、更高效的系统取代了。


疑问句:
Will the new design outwrench the old one? 新的设计会超过旧的设计吗？
Does this engine outwrench its competitors in terms of power?  这台发动机在动力方面是否超过了竞争对手？


否定句:
He did not outwrench his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮没有战胜对手。
The small company could not outwrench the large corporation. 这家小公司无法战胜这家大公司。
