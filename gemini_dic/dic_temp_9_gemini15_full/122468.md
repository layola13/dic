mobile:/ˈmoʊbaɪl/ /ˈməʊbaɪl/ | adj., n. |移动的；手机 |

英文解析:As an adjective, "mobile" means capable of moving or being moved readily. As a noun, it commonly refers to a mobile phone, a portable telephone.  The word's origins trace back to the Latin word "mobilis," meaning "movable."

例句:
The mobile sculpture swayed gently in the breeze. =  轻柔的微风吹拂着移动的雕塑。

Her mobile phone rang loudly during the meeting. = 会议期间，她的手机响得很响。


近义词: portable, movable, cellular (phone), hand-held


反义词: stationary, immobile, fixed


词性: adjective, noun
