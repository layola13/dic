sheering:/ʃɪəriŋ/ | v. n. | 剪羊毛；（使）突然转向；急转弯 |  The act of cutting or removing wool from a sheep; a sudden sharp turn or swerve.  |例句：The farmer is sheering his sheep.  = 农民正在剪羊毛。

The car sheered off the road. = 汽车突然偏离了道路。| 近义词：clipping (v.), shearing (n.), swerving (v.) | 反义词：straightening (v.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shears the sheep every spring. = 他每年春天都剪羊毛。
The ship shears to avoid the iceberg. = 船急转弯以避开冰山。


一般过去时:
They sheared the wool from the sheep. = 他们从羊身上剪下羊毛。
The cyclist sheered sharply to avoid a collision. = 骑自行车的人急转弯以避免碰撞。


现在完成时:
The farmer has sheared all his sheep. = 农民已经剪完了他所有的羊。
The driver has sheared off the road twice this month. = 这个司机这个月已经两次偏离道路了。


过去完成时:
The sheep had been sheared before the festival. = 羊在节日之前已经被剪过毛了。
The car had sheared off the road before it crashed. = 汽车在撞车之前已经偏离了道路。


将来时:
We will shear the sheep next week. = 我们下周将剪羊毛。
The plane will shear to the left to avoid the storm. = 飞机将向左急转弯以避开风暴。


虚拟语气:
If the wind were stronger, the ship would shear. = 如果风更大，船就会急转弯。
If I had known the road was so icy, I would not have sheared off. = 如果我知道路面这么滑，我就不会偏离道路了。


被动语态:
The sheep were sheared by the farmer. = 羊被农民剪了毛。
The car was sheared off the road by the strong wind. = 汽车被强风吹离了道路。


疑问句:
Have you ever sheared a sheep? = 你曾经剪过羊毛吗？
Did the car shear off the road because of the ice? = 汽车是因为冰而偏离道路的吗？


否定句:
He didn't shear the sheep himself. = 他没有自己剪羊毛。
The driver didn't shear off the road intentionally. = 司机不是故意偏离道路的。
