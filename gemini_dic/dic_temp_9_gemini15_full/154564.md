perries:/ˈpɛriz/ | n. | 梨子酒；(复数)梨园；渡口 | Fermented alcoholic beverage made from pears.  Also refers to ferry crossings or a collection of pear trees. | Example: The local pub serves a delicious perry made from their own orchard's pears. 当地酒吧供应用自家果园梨子酿造的美味梨子酒。 | 近义词：cider (苹果酒),  wine (葡萄酒) | 反义词：N/A
