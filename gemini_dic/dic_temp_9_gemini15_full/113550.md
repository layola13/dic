macadamization:/ˌmækədəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |碎石铺路；用碎石铺路法铺筑道路|The process of constructing or surfacing a road with macadam, a type of road surface made of compacted layers of broken stones.  Macadamization involves carefully graded and compacted layers of stone, often bound with tar or bitumen. | The macadamization of the rural roads significantly improved transportation.  乡村道路的碎石铺路大大改善了交通状况。|paving, surfacing|unpaving, leaving unsurfaced
