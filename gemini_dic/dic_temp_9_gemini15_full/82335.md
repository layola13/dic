grovels:/ˈɡrɑːvəl/| v.| 卑躬屈膝；匍匐；奴颜婢膝 | To crawl or creep on the ground; to behave in an excessively humble way in order to please someone.  |例句：He groveled at her feet, begging for forgiveness. 他在她脚下匍匐，乞求原谅。|例句：She groveled to her boss, hoping for a promotion. 她对老板卑躬屈膝，希望能得到晋升。|近义词：crawl, cringe, fawn, prostrate, kowtow |反义词：stand tall, defy, resist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grovels to his superiors. = 他对上司卑躬屈膝。
They grovel before power. = 他们对权力卑躬屈膝。

一般过去时:
He groveled before the king. = 他在国王面前卑躬屈膝。
She groveled to get the job. = 她为了得到这份工作而卑躬屈膝。

现在完成时:
He has groveled to everyone he's met. = 他对他遇到的每一个人都卑躬屈膝。
She has groveled enough for one lifetime. = 她这一辈子已经够卑躬屈膝了。

过去完成时:
He had groveled to her for years. = 他对她卑躬屈膝多年了。
They had groveled before the dictator. = 他们曾在独裁者面前卑躬屈膝。

将来时:
He will grovel to get what he wants. = 他为了得到想要的东西会卑躬屈膝。
She will grovel if she needs help. = 如果她需要帮助，她会卑躬屈膝。

虚拟语气:
If he needed the job, he would grovel. = 如果他需要这份工作，他会卑躬屈膝。
I wish he wouldn't grovel so much. = 我希望他不要那么卑躬屈膝。

被动语态:
His pride was groveled upon. = 他的自尊心受到了践踏。(被动语态略有不自然，很少使用)
The situation was such that he felt compelled to grovel. = 当时的处境迫使他不得不卑躬屈膝。(被动语态更侧重情况的被动)


疑问句:
Does he always grovel to get his way? = 他总是卑躬屈膝以达到目的吗？
Will she grovel to save her job? = 她会为了保住工作而卑躬屈膝吗？


否定句:
He didn't grovel; he stood his ground. = 他没有卑躬屈膝；他坚持了自己的立场。
She wouldn't grovel, even under pressure. = 即使在压力下，她也不会卑躬屈膝。


PHRASE->

grovel and beg = 卑躬屈膝地乞求


词源:
The word "grovel" ultimately derives from the Old French word "gruveler," meaning "to lie face down." This Old French word itself likely comes from a Germanic root, possibly related to words meaning "ground" or "earth."  The sense of humiliating subservience evolved over time.
