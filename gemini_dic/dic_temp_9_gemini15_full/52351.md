dictator:/ˈdɪkteɪtər/ | n. |独裁者，专政者 | A ruler who holds absolute power, often obtained through force and maintained through suppression of opposition.  Dictators typically disregard democratic principles and human rights. | Example: The dictator ruled the country with an iron fist, suppressing any dissent. 独裁者铁腕统治国家，压制任何异议。  The people suffered greatly under the dictator's oppressive regime.  人民在独裁者的压迫统治下饱受苦难。|近义词：tyrant, autocrat, despot |反义词：democrat, constitutional monarch
