musician:/mjuˈzɪʃən/| n. |音乐家，乐师 |A person who plays a musical instrument or sings, especially professionally.  |例句：She is a talented musician, proficient in both piano and violin. 她是一位才华横溢的音乐家，精通钢琴和小提琴。|例句：Many musicians contributed to the success of the concert. 许多音乐家为音乐会的成功做出了贡献。|近义词：player, performer, artist |反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays the guitar every day. = 他每天都弹吉他。
She sings beautifully. = 她唱歌很美。


一般过去时:
He played the piano at the concert last night. = 他昨晚在音乐会上弹钢琴。
She sang a beautiful song. = 她唱了一首美丽的歌。


现在完成时:
I have played the violin since I was five. = 我从五岁起就开始拉小提琴了。
She has sung in many different countries. = 她曾在许多不同的国家演唱过。


过去完成时:
They had already played their set before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经演奏完了。
She had sung that song many times before. = 她以前唱过很多次那首歌了。


将来时:
He will play the trumpet at the festival. = 他将在节日上吹小号。
She will sing at the wedding. = 她将在婚礼上唱歌。


虚拟语气:
If I were a musician, I would travel the world. = 如果我是个音乐家，我会周游世界。
I wish I could sing like her. = 我希望我能像她一样唱歌。


被动语态:
The song was played on the radio. = 这首歌在广播里播放了。
The concert was enjoyed by everyone. = 音乐会让每个人都很享受。


疑问句:
Are you a musician? = 你是音乐家吗？
Does he play any instruments? = 他会演奏任何乐器吗？


否定句:
I am not a musician. = 我不是音乐家。
He doesn't play the piano. = 他不会弹钢琴。

PHRASE->
