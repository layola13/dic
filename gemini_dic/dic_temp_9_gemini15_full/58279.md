drunkest:/ˈdrʌŋkɪst/| adj.|最醉的，烂醉的|The superlative form of "drunk," indicating the highest degree of intoxication. It describes someone who is extremely intoxicated by alcohol.|例句：He was the drunkest person at the party. 他是派对上醉得最厉害的人。|近义词：most intoxicated, completely inebriated, plastered|反义词：sober, straight
