move:/muːv/|ˈmuːv/| n., v.|移动；搬迁；感动；提议|to change position; to go from one place to another; to transfer something from one place to another; to cause something to change position; to propose something formally; to affect someone emotionally|例句：The car moved slowly down the street.  汽车缓缓地驶过街道。|例句：They moved to a new house last year. 他们去年搬到了新房子。|近义词：shift, relocate, transfer, propose, affect|反义词：stay, remain, keep, oppose, unmoved

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moves quickly. = 他行动迅速。
The earth moves around the sun. = 地球绕着太阳转。

一般过去时:
She moved to London last year. = 她去年搬到了伦敦。
He moved the furniture to the new house. = 他把家具搬到了新房子。

现在完成时:
We have moved three times in the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，我们搬了三次家。
They have moved the meeting to next week. = 他们把会议推迟到下周。

过去完成时:
The train had already moved before we arrived at the station. = 我们到达车站之前，火车已经开了。
She had moved out before I could talk to her. = 我还没来得及和她说话，她就搬走了。

将来时:
We will move to a bigger house next year. = 我们明年将搬到一栋更大的房子。
They will move the statue to the museum. = 他们将把雕像搬到博物馆。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would move to a better apartment. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会搬到更好的公寓。
I wish I could move the mountain. = 我真希望我能搬动那座山。

被动语态:
The furniture was moved to the new house. = 家具被搬到了新房子。
The meeting has been moved to next week. = 会议已被推迟到下周。

疑问句:
Will you move the chair? = 你会移动椅子吗？
Did they move the boxes? = 他们搬动箱子了吗？

否定句:
I can't move the piano. = 我搬不动钢琴。
He didn't move a muscle. = 他一动不动。
