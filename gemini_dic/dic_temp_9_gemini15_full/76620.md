galloped:/ˈɡæləpt/| v.|飞奔，疾驰|To run at a fast pace, especially of a horse.|例句：The horse galloped across the field.  马飞奔过田野。|例句：The children galloped down the hill. 孩子们飞快地跑下山。|近义词：cantered, raced, dashed|反义词：walked, strolled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gallops his horse every morning. = 他每天早上骑马飞奔。
The horse gallops across the field. = 马飞奔过田野。


一般过去时:
The horse galloped away. = 马飞奔而去。
They galloped through the forest. = 他们飞驰穿过森林。


现在完成时:
He has galloped many miles today. = 他今天已经跑了许多英里。
The rider has galloped his horse to exhaustion. = 骑手已经把他的马跑到了筋疲力尽。


过去完成时:
The horse had galloped before the race began. = 在比赛开始之前，马已经飞奔了一阵子。
They had galloped for hours before they found shelter. = 他们飞奔了好几个小时才找到庇护所。


将来时:
He will gallop his horse in the race tomorrow. = 他明天将在比赛中骑马飞奔。
The horses will gallop across the plains. = 马匹将在平原上飞奔。


虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would gallop across the country. = 如果我有一匹马，我会骑着它飞奔过整个国家。
I wish I could gallop like the wind. = 我希望我能像风一样飞奔。


被动语态:
(Not commonly used in passive voice, as "gallop" usually requires a subject performing the action.)  The race was won by the horse that galloped fastest.  (This emphasizes the speed of galloping, not the act of being galloped.)


疑问句:
Did the horse gallop fast enough to win? = 马跑得够快赢了吗？
Will they gallop across the desert? = 他们会穿越沙漠飞奔吗？


否定句:
The horse didn't gallop very far. = 马没有跑得很远。
They didn't gallop; they walked. = 他们没有飞奔；他们步行。


PHRASE->
gallop away = 飞奔而去
gallop towards = 飞奔向...
full gallop = 全速飞奔
at a gallop = 飞奔着
a wild gallop = 狂奔
