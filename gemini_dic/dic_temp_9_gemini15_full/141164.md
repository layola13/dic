ooesphere:/ˈoʊəˌsfɪər/| n. |卵球; 卵细胞; 卵黄球|The oosphere is the female gamete of algae and many plants.  It is a large, non-motile cell that contains the female genetic material. The oosphere is fertilized by a sperm cell to form a zygote, which then develops into a new organism. |例句：The oosphere, once fertilized, develops into a new plant.  受精卵会发育成新的植物。
The maturation of the oosphere is a crucial step in plant reproduction. 卵球的成熟是植物繁殖的关键步骤。|近义词：ovum, egg cell |反义词:sperm, spermatozoon
