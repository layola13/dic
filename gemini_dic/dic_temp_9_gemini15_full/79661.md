glost:/ɡlɒst/| n.|失光，光泽消失|The loss of luster or shine, typically in ceramics.  It refers to the dulling or matting of a glazed surface due to a chemical reaction or other process. This often occurs in pottery where the glaze has become dull or opaque. |例句：The ancient pottery showed signs of glost, indicating its age.  古老的陶器显示出失光现象，表明了它的年代。

The glost on the vase had faded over time.  花瓶上的光泽随着时间的推移而褪去了。|近义词：dulling, matting, fading |反义词：gloss, shine, luster|名词
