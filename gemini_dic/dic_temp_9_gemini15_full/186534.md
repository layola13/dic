rid:/rɪd/ | vt. |摆脱，除去，使…免除 | To free from something unwanted or unpleasant; to get rid of. |例句：We need to rid the house of mice. 我们需要把老鼠赶出房子。
例句：He tried to rid himself of his bad habits. 他试图改掉自己的坏习惯。 |近义词：remove, eliminate, free |反义词：inflict, impose, burden |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rids his garden of weeds every week. = 他每周都清除花园里的杂草。
They rid the classroom of distractions. = 他们清除了教室里的干扰。


一般过去时:
She ridded her apartment of clutter. = 她清理了公寓里的杂物。
The farmer ridded his field of pests. = 农民清除了他田地里的害虫。


现在完成时:
He has rid himself of his anxiety. = 他已经摆脱了他的焦虑。
They have ridded the streets of crime. = 他们已经清除了街头的犯罪。


过去完成时:
She had ridded the house of the infestation before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经清除了房屋的虫害。
They had rid themselves of their debts by the end of the year. = 到年底他们已经还清了债务。


将来时:
He will rid his desk of paperwork tomorrow. = 他明天将清理他办公桌上的文件。
We will rid the city of pollution in the future. = 我们将来会清除城市的污染。


虚拟语气:
If only I could rid myself of this headache! = 要是我能摆脱这个头痛就好了！
I wish I could rid my life of stress. = 我希望我能摆脱生活中的压力。


被动语态:
The house was rid of the infestation. = 房屋里的虫害被清除了。
The streets are being rid of litter. = 街道上的垃圾正在被清理。


疑问句:
How can we rid ourselves of this problem? = 我们该如何解决这个问题？
How did they rid the ship of the leak? = 他们是如何清除船上的漏水的？


否定句:
He couldn't rid himself of the feeling of guilt. = 他无法摆脱内疚感。
They failed to rid the area of mosquitoes. = 他们未能清除该地区的蚊子。


PHRASE->
get rid of = 摆脱，去除
rid oneself of = 摆脱（某种感觉或东西）
rid...of = 使…摆脱…
be rid of = 摆脱了…
free from = 摆脱
