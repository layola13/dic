re-anchor:/ˌriːˈæŋkər/| vt.|重新固定；重新抛锚|To secure or fasten something again; to drop anchor again.|例句：The boat needs to re-anchor in the calmer waters. 这艘船需要在较平静的水域重新抛锚。

例句：We had to re-anchor the tent after the storm.  暴风雨过后我们不得不重新固定帐篷。


近义词：refasten, resecure, resettle|反义词：unmoor, cast off
