sheltered: /ˈʃɛltərd/ | adj. | 受保护的；庇护的；隐蔽的 |  protected from something harmful or unpleasant; shielded.  |例句：The children played in the sheltered courtyard. 孩子们在避风的庭院里玩耍。

例句：The plants need a sheltered position to survive the winter.  这些植物需要一个避风的位置才能安全度过冬天。


近义词：protected, shielded, secure, guarded, safeguarded | 反义词：exposed, unprotected, vulnerable, open


词源:源自古英语 *scylter,  含义为 "shelter,"  本身又源自原始日耳曼语 *skeldō,  与 "shield" 有关联。
