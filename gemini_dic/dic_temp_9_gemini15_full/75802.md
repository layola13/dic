fume:/fjuːm/| n. v.|烟，气体；怒气，愤怒|  to give off smoke, gas, or vapor; to be filled with or express anger|例句：The factory fumes polluted the air.  工厂的烟雾污染了空气。
The driver fumed at the traffic jam. 司机对交通堵塞感到愤怒。|近义词：vapor, steam, rage, anger|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The volcano fumes constantly. = 火山不断地冒烟。
He fumes about the injustice. = 他对不公正的事情感到愤怒。


一般过去时:
The engine fumed black smoke. = 发动机冒出黑烟。
She fumed after the argument. = 争吵后她怒气冲冲。


现在完成时:
The car has fumed all day. = 这辆车整天都在冒烟。
He has fumed about it for weeks. = 他为此生气了好几个星期。


过去完成时:
The factory had fumed toxic chemicals for years. = 工厂多年来一直在排放有毒化学物质。
She had fumed silently before finally speaking. = 她默默地生气了好久才最终开口说话。


将来时:
The volcano will fume lava. = 火山将喷出熔岩。
He will fume if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不遂心愿，他会勃然大怒。


虚拟语气:
If the engine fumed excessively, I would call for help. = 如果发动机冒出过多的烟，我会打电话求助。
If I were you, I wouldn't fume so much. = 如果我是你，我不会这么生气。


被动语态:
The air was fumed by the factory's emissions. = 空气被工厂的排放物污染了。
His anger was fumed at the injustice. = 他的愤怒源于不公正。


疑问句:

否定句:
The engine didn't fume at all. = 发动机根本没有冒烟。
She didn't fume about the minor inconvenience. = 她并没有因为那点小不便而生气。
