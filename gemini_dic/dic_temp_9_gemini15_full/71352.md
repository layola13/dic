flack:/flæk/| n. v.|吹嘘，宣传；抨击，批评；新闻发言人；公关人员 |a. to act as a publicist for; to criticize harshly; a publicist; harsh criticism |例句：The company hired a flack to improve its image. 公司聘请了一位公关人员来改善其形象。

The journalist gave the politician a lot of flack.  记者对这位政治家进行了猛烈的批评。


近义词：publicist, spokesperson, press agent, critic, attack |反义词：praise, compliment, support


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flacks for a major corporation. 他为一家大公司做宣传。
She flacks the new product relentlessly. 她不遗余力地宣传新产品。


一般过去时:
They flacked their new movie to the press.  他们向媒体宣传了他们的新电影。
The candidate flacked his opponent's record.  这位候选人抨击了他的对手的记录。


现在完成时:
The PR firm has flacked for many successful companies. 这家公关公司已经为许多成功的公司做过宣传。
The newspaper has flacked the government's policies. 这家报纸一直在抨击政府的政策。


过去完成时:
They had flacked the product extensively before its release.  他们在产品发布之前就做了大量的宣传。
The media had flacked the celebrity's personal life for years.  媒体多年来一直在抨击这位名人的私生活。


将来时:
She will flack the new product at the trade show. 她将在贸易展上宣传新产品。
They will flack the opposition party's proposals.  他们将抨击反对党的提案。


虚拟语气:
If they flacked harder, they might sell more products. 如果他们更卖力地宣传，他们可能会卖出更多产品。
If she hadn't flacked so much, she wouldn't have made so many enemies. 如果她没有那么多的批评，她就不会树立这么多敌人。


被动语态:
The product was flacked heavily on social media.  该产品在社交媒体上受到了猛烈的抨击。
The new policy is being flacked by the opposition.  反对派正在抨击这项新政策。


疑问句:
Who flacks for that company? 谁为那家公司做宣传？
Does he usually flack his opponents so aggressively? 他通常会如此积极地抨击他的对手吗？


否定句:
They didn't flack the product effectively. 他们没有有效地宣传该产品。
She didn't flack the government's decision. 她没有抨击政府的决定。


PHRASE->
give someone flack: 批评某人
take flack: 承受批评
get flack: 受到抨击
a lot of flack: 大量批评
no flack: 没有批评
