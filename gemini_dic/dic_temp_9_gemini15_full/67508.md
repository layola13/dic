extractable: /ɪkˈstræktəbəl/ | adj. | 可提取的，可萃取的 | Capable of being extracted.  Something that is extractable can be removed or obtained from something else, often through a process of separation or purification.  | Example:  The essential oils from the plant are extractable using steam distillation.  植物中的精油可以使用蒸汽蒸馏法提取。 | 近义词：removable, extractible, retrievable | 反义词：inextractable, unobtainable

词源:
The word "extractable" is derived from the verb "extract," which comes from the Latin word "extrahere," meaning "to draw out" or "to pull out."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
