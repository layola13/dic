irreconcilably: /ˌɪrɛˈkɒnsɪləbli/ | adj. | 不可调和地；无法和解地 |  in a way that cannot be reconciled or resolved;  impossible to resolve or harmonize.  | The two sides were irreconcilably opposed on the issue of the treaty.  双方在条约问题上存在不可调和的矛盾。 | reconcilable, resolvable | reconcilable, agreeable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The differences between them are irreconcilably opposed. = 他们之间的分歧是不可调和的。
He is irreconcilably opposed to the new policy. = 他坚决反对这项新政策。

一般过去时:
The conflict remained irreconcilably opposed. = 冲突依然不可调和。
They were irreconcilably opposed to each other's views. = 他们对彼此的观点持截然相反的意见。

现在完成时:
Their views have remained irreconcilably opposed for years. = 多年来，他们的观点一直是不可调和的。
The factions have been irreconcilably opposed since the beginning. = 自始至终，这些派系都是势不两立的。

过去完成时:
The parties had been irreconcilably opposed before the negotiations began. = 在谈判开始之前，各方就已经是势不两立的了。
It had become clear that their differences were irreconcilably opposed. = 很明显，他们的分歧是不可调和的。

将来时:
The two countries will likely remain irreconcilably opposed for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，这两个国家可能会继续保持不可调和的对立状态。
The debate will likely leave the two sides irreconcilably opposed. = 这场辩论很可能会使双方陷入不可调和的对抗。


虚拟语气:
If they were reconcilable, the problem would be solved easily. = 如果他们能够和解，问题就能很容易解决。(反面例子，说明irreconcilably 的含义)
If only their views were not irreconcilably opposed! = 但愿他们的观点不是不可调和的！(反面例子，说明irreconcilably 的含义)


被动语态:
The issue was considered to be irreconcilably opposed. = 这个问题被认为是不可调和的。
The differences were found to be irreconcilably opposed by the mediator. = 调解员发现这些分歧是不可调和的。


疑问句:
Are their differences irreconcilably opposed? = 他们的分歧是不可调和的吗？
Is the situation irreconcilably opposed to a peaceful solution? = 这种情况是否无法和平解决？


否定句:
Their differences were not irreconcilably opposed; a compromise was possible. = 他们的分歧并非不可调和；妥协是可能的。
The conflict is not necessarily irreconcilably opposed; dialogue is still possible. = 冲突不一定是不可调和的；对话仍然有可能。


PHRASE->
irreconcilably opposed: 不可调和地对立
irreconcilably different: 不可调和地不同
irreconcilably at odds: 严重不和
irreconcilably conflicting:  严重冲突的
irreconcilably divided:  严重分裂的

词源:
ir- (否定前缀) + reconcilable (可调和的)  reconcilable 来自于拉丁语 *reconciliare (使和谐，使和解)
