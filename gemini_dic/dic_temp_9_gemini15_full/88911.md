hold-ground:/hoʊld ɡraʊnd/ | v. | 保持阵地；坚守立场；维持现状 | To maintain one's position or ground, especially in the face of opposition or difficulty.  | The army held its ground against the enemy's attack.  军队抵挡住了敌人的进攻。 | maintain, defend, withstand | retreat, surrender, yield | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company holds its ground in the market despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，该公司仍在市场上保持着地位。
He holds his ground on the issue, refusing to compromise. = 他在这个问题上坚持己见，拒绝妥协。


一般过去时:
The soldiers held their ground for hours under heavy fire. = 士兵们在猛烈的炮火下坚守阵地数小时。
She held her ground in the argument, despite being outnumbered. = 尽管人数处于劣势，她在争论中仍然坚持己见。


现在完成时:
The team has held its ground throughout the season, maintaining its top ranking. = 该队本赛季一直保持领先地位。
Despite the criticism, he has held his ground and remained true to his beliefs. = 尽管受到批评，他仍然坚持自己的立场，忠于自己的信仰。


过去完成时:
They had held their ground for days before reinforcements arrived. = 在增援部队到达之前，他们已经坚守阵地数日了。
She had held her ground throughout the negotiations, ultimately securing a favorable deal. = 在整个谈判过程中，她一直坚持自己的立场，最终达成了有利的协议。


将来时:
We will hold our ground until we receive further instructions. = 我们将坚守阵地，直到收到进一步指示。
He believes he will hold his ground in the upcoming debate. = 他相信在即将到来的辩论中他会坚持自己的立场。


虚拟语气:
If we had held our ground earlier, the situation would be different now. = 如果我们早点坚守阵地，情况现在就会不同了。
I wish I had held my ground and not given in to the pressure. = 我真希望我当时能坚持立场，而不是屈服于压力。


被动语态:
The position was held ground by the defending forces. = 这个阵地被防守部队坚守住了。
His beliefs were not held ground by the majority. = 他的信仰没有得到大多数人的支持。


疑问句:
Will they hold their ground against such a powerful opponent? = 他们能抵挡住如此强大的对手吗？
Did she hold her ground during the intense questioning? = 在严厉的质问下，她坚持己见了吗？


否定句:
The army did not hold its ground and retreated. = 军队没有坚守阵地，而是撤退了。
He didn't hold his ground and compromised on the key issue. = 他没有坚持己见，在关键问题上做出了妥协。
