convertism:/kənˈvɜːrtɪzəm/| n. |皈依主义，改宗行为 |The belief that converting others to one's own religion or ideology is a moral imperative.  A system of beliefs characterized by an intense focus on conversion and proselytization.  |例句：The missionary's fervent convertism led him to travel the globe spreading his faith.  传教士狂热的皈依主义驱使他周游世界传播他的信仰。|近义词：proselytism, conversionism |反义词：tolerance, secularism
