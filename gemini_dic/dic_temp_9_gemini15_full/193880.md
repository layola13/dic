self-dispatches:/ˌsɛlfˈdɪspætʃɪz/| n.| 自行派遣；自行处理；主动呈报|The act of sending oneself or something oneself, often implying independent action or initiative.  It can also refer to the act of sending a report or communication without the need for external prompting.|例句：The journalist's self-dispatches from the war zone provided invaluable firsthand accounts.  这位记者从战区的自发报道提供了宝贵的第一手资料。

例句：The company's self-dispatches of quarterly reports streamlined the reporting process. 公司自行呈报季度报告简化了报告流程。|近义词：self-reporting, independent reporting, initiative, self-submission|反义词：external dispatch, mandated reporting, compelled submission|名词
