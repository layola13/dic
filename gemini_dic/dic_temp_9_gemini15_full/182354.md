render:/ˈrɛndər/| vt. n.|呈现；翻译；给予；使成为；粉刷；熬制；表演|to cause someone or something to be in a particular state or condition; to present something in a particular way; to translate something; to perform a piece of music, a role, etc.; to melt down fat to make lard|例句：The artist rendered the scene with great skill.  艺术家以高超的技艺描绘了这幅场景。

例句：The translator rendered the poem into English. 翻译者将这首诗翻译成英语。

近义词：represent, depict, translate, provide, give, make, perform, produce, portray

反义词：obscure, conceal, withhold, destroy, neglect

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist renders the scene beautifully. = 艺术家精美地描绘了这个场景。
The software renders the 3D model in real time. = 软件实时渲染3D模型。


一般过去时:
He rendered assistance to the victims. = 他向受害者提供了援助。
She rendered the song with passion. = 她充满激情地演唱了这首歌。


现在完成时:
The committee has rendered its decision. = 委员会已经做出了决定。
The chef has rendered the fat into lard. = 厨师已经将脂肪熬制成猪油。


过去完成时:
They had rendered the building uninhabitable. = 他们已经使那座建筑物无法居住。
The storm had rendered the roads impassable. = 暴风雨已使道路无法通行。


将来时:
The new software will render outdated methods obsolete. = 新软件将使过时的方案过时。
The jury will render a verdict tomorrow. = 陪审团将在明天做出裁决。


虚拟语气:
If he rendered assistance, she would be grateful. = 如果他提供帮助，她会很感激。
I wish he had rendered more help. = 我希望他当时能提供更多帮助。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The software failed to render the image properly. = 软件未能正确渲染图像。
