registrar:/ˌrɛdʒɪˈstrɑːr/| n. |注册官；注册员；登记员；记录员 |An official who keeps records, especially of births, deaths, and marriages, or who is responsible for admissions to an educational institution.  |The registrar issued the marriage certificate.  注册官颁发了结婚证书。|The university registrar handles student enrollment. 大学注册员负责学生入学注册。|clerk, recorder, enrollment officer|—|
