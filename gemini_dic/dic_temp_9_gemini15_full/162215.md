pre-question:/priːˈkwɛstʃən/ | adj. |预先提问的，预备问题的|  Describes a question asked before another, often to clarify or prepare for the main question.  It implies a preliminary or introductory inquiry. |例句：The pre-question survey helped us refine the main questionnaire. 预先的调查问卷帮助我们完善了主要问卷。

例句： His pre-question about the budget ensured a smoother meeting. 他关于预算的预先提问确保了会议的顺利进行。


近义词： preliminary question, introductory question, preparatory question | 反义词： follow-up question, subsequent question | 词性：形容词
