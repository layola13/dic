/ˈhʌtʃɪsən/| n. |哈切森（姓氏）|Hutchison is a surname of Scottish origin. It is derived from the given name "Hutchinson," which itself is a patronymic surname meaning "son of Hutchin."  Hutchin is a diminutive of various names, most likely Hugh.
