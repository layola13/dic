modernday:/ˈmɒdəndeɪ/ /ˈmɑːdəndeɪ/| adj. |现代的，当代的|  Of or relating to the present time; contemporary.  The adjective "modern" refers to the present era, often contrasted with the past. "Day" simply adds emphasis, though the word is often used without "day."|例句：Modern-day technology has revolutionized communication. 现代科技彻底改变了通讯方式。

例句：Many modern-day problems stem from historical injustices. 许多现代问题源于历史的不公正。


近义词：contemporary, current, present-day


反义词：ancient, archaic, obsolete


词性:形容词
