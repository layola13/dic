coboose:/kuːˈbuːs/| n.|守车，尾车|A small compartment at the rear of a freight train, originally used as a sleeping car for the brakeman and later as an office or tool car.  |例句：The brakeman rode in the coboose at the end of the train.  列车员坐在火车尾部的守车里。|例句：The old coboose was a relic of the railroad's past.  那辆旧守车是铁路过去的一个遗迹。|近义词：caboose (more common)|反义词：locomotive, engine
