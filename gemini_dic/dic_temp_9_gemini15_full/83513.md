hacqueton:/ˈhækɪtɒn/ | n. | 札凯顿（一种中世纪的棉质或亚麻质衬衣） | A padded, quilted garment worn as under-armor in the late medieval period (roughly 13th-15th centuries).  It provided some protection against cuts and blows, and was worn under a surcoat or other outer garment.  | Example: The knight wore a hacqueton under his chainmail for extra protection.  骑士在锁子甲下穿着一件札凯顿以获得额外的保护。 | 近义词：gambeson, aketon | 反义词：无
