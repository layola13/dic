enclosers:/ɪnˈkloʊzərz/ | n. |附件，附件物 |Things enclosed or included with something else, often a letter or package.  It refers to any item(s) inserted inside another item, usually for additional information or to complete the main item. For example, in a business letter, enclosures might be contracts, invoices, or other relevant documents. |Example: Please find the contract enclosed as an enclosure with this letter.  请在信中找到附上的合同。 |近义词：attachments, inserts, inclusions |反义词：N/A
