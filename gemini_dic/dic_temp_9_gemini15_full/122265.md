mitigant:/ˈmɪtɪɡənt/| n.|减轻因素；缓解剂|Something that lessens the severity of something else, especially something unpleasant or dangerous.  A mitigating factor reduces the seriousness or extent of something.|The judge considered the defendant's remorse a mitigating factor in sentencing.  法官认为被告的悔恨是量刑的减轻因素。|近义词：alleviator, palliative, emollient|反义词：aggravator, intensifier|名词
