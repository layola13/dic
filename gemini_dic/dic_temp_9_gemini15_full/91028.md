husk:/hʌsk/| n.|外壳；皮；谷壳；残余|The outer covering of a seed or fruit; the dry, outer layer of a grain of cereal.  It can also refer to something that is left behind or is worthless.|例句：The farmer removed the husk from the corn.  农民去除了玉米的皮壳。

例句：The old building was just a husk of its former glory. 这栋旧建筑物只是它昔日辉煌的残骸。


近义词：shell, peel, chaff, rind, hull|反义词：core, kernel, essence


词源：源自古英语 *hūsca，含义为“外壳，皮”。与古高地德语 *hūsca 和古诺尔斯语 *hǫsk 同源。


USAGE->

The machine husks the grain efficiently. = 这台机器高效地脱谷。

将来时:
