nonsustaining: /ˌnɑːn səˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不能维持的；不可持续的 | Not able to be maintained or continued; unsustainable.  | The company's current business model is nonsustaining in the long term.  这家公司的现有商业模式从长远来看是不可持续的。|近义词：unsustainable, untenable, temporary | 反义词：sustainable, lasting, enduring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The current policy is nonsustaining. = 目前的政策是不可持续的。
This method of farming is nonsustaining. = 这种耕作方式是不可持续的。

过去完成时:
The government realized the policy had been nonsustaining. = 政府意识到这项政策一直以来都是不可持续的。

将来时:
The current trend will likely be nonsustaining. = 当前的趋势很可能无法持续。
Without reform, the system will become nonsustaining. = 如果没有改革，这个系统将变得不可持续。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:

The argument that the practice is nonsustaining is not entirely accurate. = 认为这种做法不可持续的说法并不完全准确。
