dialectologies:/ˌdaɪˌælɛkˈtɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 方言学 | The study of dialects; the branch of linguistics that deals with the geographical and social variations of language.  | Example:  His research focuses on the dialectologies of the Appalachian region. 他的研究集中在阿巴拉契亚地区的方言学。 | 近义词：dialectology, linguistic geography | 反义词：standard language studies
