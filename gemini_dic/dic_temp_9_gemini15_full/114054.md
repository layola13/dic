magnetising: /ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 磁化；激发；使着迷 |  To magnetize; to impart magnetic properties to; to excite or stimulate; to fascinate or enthrall. |


例句：

The process of magnetising the metal involved exposing it to a strong magnetic field. = 磁化金属的过程包括将其暴露在强磁场中。

The speaker's passionate words magnetised the audience. = 演讲者的激情洋溢的言语吸引了听众。


近义词：energize, excite, fascinate, enthrall, attract


反义词：demagnetize, repel, bore


词性：动词
