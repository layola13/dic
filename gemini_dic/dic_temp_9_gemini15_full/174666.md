quoin:/kwɔɪn/| n.|角石；墙角石；印刷机上的角铁；|A quoin is a structural element, typically a large, roughly hewn stone, used at the corner of a building or wall.  It can also refer to a wedge-shaped piece of metal used in printing presses to secure the type. |例句：The old building's quoins were made of granite.  这座古老建筑的墙角石是用花岗岩制成的。|例句：The printer used quoins to tighten the type in the press. 印刷工使用角铁来固定印刷机中的活字。|近义词：cornerstone, angle stone |反义词：center, middle
