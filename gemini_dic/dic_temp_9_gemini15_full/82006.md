gripeful:/ˈɡraɪpfəl/| adj.|牢骚满腹的，抱怨的，不满的|Feeling or expressing complaint or dissatisfaction.|He was gripeful about the long wait. 他对漫长的等待感到不满。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, disgruntled|反义词：content, satisfied, pleased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gripeful about his job. = 他对他的工作牢骚满腹。
She is always gripeful about the weather. = 她总是抱怨天气。


一般过去时:
He was gripeful after the meeting. = 会后他满腹牢骚。
They were gripeful because of the delay. = 他们因为延误而感到不满。


现在完成时:
She has been gripeful all day. = 她整天都抱怨个不停。
He has become gripeful since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，他变得抱怨多多。


过去完成时:
They had been gripeful for weeks before they finally complained. = 他们在最终抱怨之前已经抱怨了好几个星期了。
She had been gripeful about the situation for months. = 她几个月来一直对这种情况不满。


将来时:
He will be gripeful if he doesn't get a promotion. = 如果他不被提升，他会抱怨的。
They will likely be gripeful if the project is delayed again. = 如果项目再次延误，他们可能会抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If he were less gripeful, he'd be happier. = 如果他不那么爱抱怨，他会更快乐。
I wish he weren't so gripeful all the time. = 我希望他不要总是那么爱抱怨。


被动语态:
His complaints were often dismissed as him simply being gripeful. = 他的抱怨常常被认为是他只是爱抱怨。
The situation was described as making many people gripeful. =  这种情况被描述为让很多人感到不满。


疑问句:
Is he gripeful about something? = 他是不是在抱怨什么？
Are they always so gripeful? = 他们总是这么爱抱怨吗？


否定句:
He wasn't gripeful at all; he was actually quite happy. = 他一点也不抱怨；事实上他相当高兴。
She wasn't gripeful about the extra work. = 她并不抱怨额外的工作。


PHRASE->
a gripeful tone = 抱怨的语气
feel gripeful = 感到不满
become gripeful = 变得抱怨
a gripeful expression = 抱怨的表情
sound gripeful = 听起来抱怨
