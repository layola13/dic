hangmen:/ˈhæŋmən/| n.|刽子手，绞刑执行者|A hangman is the person who carries out executions by hanging.  The term often evokes a sense of grim duty and historical context.|例句：The hangman adjusted the noose before the execution.  刽子手在行刑前调整了绳套。|例句：Throughout history, hangmen have been figures of both fear and morbid fascination. 纵观历史，刽子手既令人恐惧，又引人注目地着迷。|近义词：executioner|反义词：N/A
