dangled:/ˈdæŋɡld/| vt.|悬挂；摇摆不定；诱惑；拖延|To hang or swing loosely; to offer something tempting or alluring; to delay or prolong something.|例句：He dangled the key just out of reach. 他把钥匙悬挂在够不着的地方。|例句：The possibility of a promotion dangled before him. 升职的可能性摆在他面前，诱惑着他。|近义词：hung, swung, swayed, tempted|反义词：secured, fastened, attached

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dangles the fishing rod. = 他摇晃着钓鱼竿。
She dangles her earrings. = 她晃动着她的耳环。

一般过去时:
The pirate dangled the treasure map. = 海盗晃动着宝藏地图。
He dangled the carrot in front of the donkey. = 他把胡萝卜悬在驴子面前。

现在完成时:
They have dangled the offer for weeks. = 他们已经悬而未决地提供这个机会好几个星期了。
She has dangled the possibility of a raise for months. = 她已经几个月都在暗示可能会加薪了。

过去完成时:
He had dangled the bait before the fish took it. = 鱼上钩之前，他已经把诱饵悬挂在那里了。
They had dangled the promise of freedom for years. = 他们多年来一直许诺自由。

将来时:
The artist will dangle the mobile above the crib. = 艺术家将会把活动玩具悬挂在婴儿床的上方。
They will dangle the contract until the last minute. = 他们会把合同拖到最后一分钟。

虚拟语气:
If he dangled the bait correctly, he would have caught more fish. = 如果他正确地悬挂诱饵，他就会钓到更多鱼。
If she had dangled the promotion, he might have worked harder. = 如果她暗示升职，他可能会更加努力工作。


被动语态:
The ornament was dangled from the ceiling. = 装饰品悬挂在天花板上。
The carrot was dangled before the donkey. = 胡萝卜悬在驴子面前。

疑问句:
Was the key dangled out of reach? = 钥匙是悬挂在够不着的地方吗？
Is the offer being dangled to entice potential buyers? = 这个提议是为了吸引潜在买家而提出的吗？

否定句:
He didn't dangle the bait properly. = 他没有正确地悬挂诱饵。
She didn't dangle the possibility of a raise. = 她没有暗示可能会加薪。


PHRASE->
dangle a carrot = 许以好处

dangle from = 从...悬挂
dangle over = 悬在...之上
