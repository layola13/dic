nonlivings:/ˈnɑːnˌlɪvɪŋz/ | n. |非生物 |Nonlivings refer to things that are not alive, lacking the characteristics of living organisms such as growth, reproduction, and response to stimuli.  They encompass a vast range of objects and substances found in the environment. |Example: Rocks, water, and air are examples of nonlivings.  岩石、水和空气是非生物的例子。 The study focused on the interaction between livings and nonlivings in the ecosystem. 这项研究侧重于生态系统中生物与非生物之间的相互作用。|近义词：inanimate objects, lifeless things |反义词：livings, organisms
