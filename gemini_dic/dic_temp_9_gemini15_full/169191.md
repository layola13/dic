prolificacy: /ˌprɒlɪˈfɪkəsi/ | n. | 多产；丰饶；丰富 | The ability to produce a large amount of something, especially offspring, works of art, etc.  |  例句：The prolificacy of the artist is evident in his vast body of work.  这位艺术家的多产在他的大量作品中显而易见。


例句：Her prolificacy as a writer is undeniable, with over twenty novels published. 她作为一名作家的多产是不可否认的，已经出版了二十多部小说。


近义词：fertility, fecundity, abundance, productivity


反义词：scarcity, barrenness, sterility


词性: 名词
