reemitting:/ˌriːˈɪmɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新发射，再发射|The process of emitting something again, often radiation or light.|例句：The sun is constantly reemitting energy received from other stars. 太阳不断地重新发射从其他恒星接收到的能量。
例句：The fluorescent light reemitting ultraviolet light into visible light. 荧光灯将紫外线重新发射成可见光。|近义词：re-radiating, re-releasing|反义词：absorbing
