/ˈkɔːzɪŋ/| v. |导致；引起；造成| causing is the present participle of the verb "cause". It describes an action that is in progress or is habitual.  It indicates the reason or origin of something. |The storm is causing widespread power outages.  暴风雨导致大范围停电。

The heavy rain is causing flooding in the streets. 大雨导致街道洪水泛滥。


近义词：producing, generating, leading to, resulting in, bringing about|反义词：preventing, stopping, hindering|动词
