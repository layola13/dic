formalised:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzd/ | adj. | 正式的；规范化的；制度化的 |  Made formal or official; established as a standard or norm.  The process of making something formal or official. | 例句：The agreement was formalised in a written contract. = 这项协议已以书面合同的形式确定。

The company has formalised its internal procedures. = 公司已经将其内部程序规范化了。


近义词：official, standardized, regulated, systematized | 反义词：informal, casual, unstructured


词源:  From "formalise" + "-ed".  "Formalise" itself comes from the French "formaliser" and ultimately from the Latin "forma" (shape, form).
