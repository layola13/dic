pretension:/prɪˈtɛnʃən/| n.|自负；矫饰； претензия (俄语)|An attempt to appear more important, intelligent, or cultured than one really is; an affected or unnatural way of behaving.|例句：His pretentious mannerisms annoyed everyone.  他的做作举止惹恼了所有人。

例句：Her pretentious claims of artistic genius were laughable. 她自诩为艺术天才的说法令人发笑。


近义词：affectation, ostentation, pomposity|反义词：humility, modesty, simplicity|词性:名词
