imbursement:/ˌɪmˈbɜːrsmənt/| n. |偿还；报销；赔偿| The act of reimbursing or being reimbursed; repayment.  It refers to the repayment of an expense or a loss. |例句：The company provides reimbursement for travel expenses.  公司报销差旅费。|例句：She received reimbursement for the damaged goods. 她收到了损坏商品的赔偿。|近义词：refund, repayment, compensation |反义词：payment (in the sense of initial payment rather than repayment)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reimburses employees for business expenses. = 公司报销员工的业务费用。
He reimburses his friend for the gas money. = 他偿还朋友的汽油钱。

一般过去时:
They reimbursed me for the cost of the repairs. = 他们赔偿了我修理的费用。
The insurance company reimbursed her for the stolen jewelry. = 保险公司赔偿了她被盗的珠宝。


现在完成时:
She has been reimbursed for her medical expenses. = 她的医疗费用已经得到报销。
They have not yet been reimbursed for the damages. = 他们的损失还没有得到赔偿。


过去完成时:
The client had already been reimbursed before the invoice arrived. = 发票到达之前，客户的款项已经报销了。
He had reimbursed her before leaving for vacation. = 他在去度假之前已经偿还了她。


将来时:
The company will reimburse you for the conference fees. = 公司将报销您的会议费。
We will be reimbursed for our travel expenses next week. = 我们下周将报销差旅费。


虚拟语气:
If the company reimbursed me promptly, I would be happier. = 如果公司及时报销我的费用，我会更高兴。
I wish they had reimbursed me for the lost luggage. = 我希望他们能赔偿我丢失的行李。


被动语态:
The expenses will be reimbursed by the end of the month. = 费用将在月底报销。
The damage was reimbursed by the responsible party. = 损失由责任方赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company reimburse me for this? = 公司会报销这个吗？
Have you been reimbursed yet? = 你已经报销了吗？


否定句:
The company does not reimburse for personal expenses. = 公司不报销个人开支。
They were not reimbursed for their time. = 他们的时间没有得到补偿。


PHRASE->
expense reimbursement = 费用报销
travel reimbursement = 差旅费报销
reimbursement claim = 报销申请
seek reimbursement = 寻求报销
partial reimbursement = 部分报销
