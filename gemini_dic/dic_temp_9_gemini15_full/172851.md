quadruple:/ˈkwɒdruːpl/ /ˈkwɑːdruːpl/| adj., v., n.|四倍的；四重奏；使四倍|  To make or become four times as great; consisting of four parts or things; a group or set of four. |例句：The company's profits quadrupled last year.  去年公司利润增长了四倍。|例句：They performed a delightful quadruple at the concert.他们在音乐会上演奏了一段令人愉悦的四重奏。|近义词：fourfold, quadruple, multiply by four|反义词：halve, quarter
