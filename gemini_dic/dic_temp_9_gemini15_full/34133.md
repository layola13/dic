claggy:/ˈklæɡi/| adj.|粘的；泥泞的；湿粘的；沉闷的 |  sticky, gluey, or muddy;  also used to describe something dull or depressing.  |例句：The clay was claggy and difficult to work with.  这粘土又粘又不好操作。

The weather was claggy and miserable. 天气又潮湿又糟糕。

|近义词：sticky, gluey, muddy, soggy, glutinous, heavy |反义词：dry, crisp, light, airy
