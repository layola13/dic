intelligible:/ɪnˈtɛlədʒəbəl/ | adj. | 可理解的；清晰易懂的 | Easily understood; clear.  | The speaker's English was perfectly intelligible.  说话人的英语非常清晰易懂。| comprehensible, understandable, clear | unintelligible, obscure, ambiguous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His explanation is intelligible to everyone. = 他的解释每个人都能理解。
The instructions are intelligible and easy to follow. = 说明清晰易懂，很容易理解。


一般过去时:
Her speech was intelligible, even though she was nervous. = 尽管她很紧张，但她的讲话还是清晰易懂的。
The message was intelligible after we deciphered the code. = 我们破译密码后，信息就变得清晰易懂了。


现在完成时:
The professor's lectures have always been intelligible to his students. = 教授的讲座一直以来都让学生们容易理解。
We have made the instructions more intelligible for the users. = 我们已经为用户使说明更加容易理解。


过去完成时:
The ancient script had been intelligible only to a few scholars. = 只有少数学者才能理解古代文字。
After many hours of work, the document had become intelligible. = 经过数小时的工作，文件变得清晰易懂了。


将来时:
The translation will be intelligible to a wider audience. = 翻译将能被更广泛的受众理解。
With the new software, the data will be more intelligible. = 使用新软件，数据将更易于理解。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions were more intelligible, fewer people would make mistakes. = 如果说明更清晰易懂，犯错的人就会更少。
I wish the report were more intelligible; it's full of jargon. = 我希望报告更清晰易懂；它充满了专业术语。


被动语态:
The instructions were made intelligible by the addition of diagrams. = 通过添加图表，说明变得清晰易懂了。
The message was rendered intelligible through careful analysis. = 通过仔细分析，信息变得清晰易懂了。


疑问句:
Is the contract intelligible to both parties? = 合同对双方都清晰易懂吗？
Are the directions intelligible enough for a novice user? = 说明对于新手用户来说足够清晰易懂吗？


否定句:
The handwriting was unintelligible; I couldn't read it. = 字迹难以辨认；我无法阅读。
His explanation was not intelligible to the majority of the audience. = 他的解释大多数听众都听不懂。


PHRASE->
make intelligible: 使...易于理解
perfectly intelligible: 完全清晰易懂
highly intelligible: 非常清晰易懂
easily intelligible: 容易理解的
become intelligible: 变得清晰易懂
