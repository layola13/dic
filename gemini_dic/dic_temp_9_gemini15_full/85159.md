hardware:/ˈhɑːrdˌweər/| n. |硬件|The physical components of a computer system, such as the central processing unit, memory, and input/output devices.  It refers to the tangible parts of a computer or other electronic system.|Example:  The computer's hardware includes the motherboard, CPU, and RAM. 这台电脑的硬件包括主板、CPU和内存。

|Example:  We need to upgrade our hardware to run this software. 我们需要升级我们的硬件才能运行这个软件。|近义词:equipment, machinery, apparatus|反义词:software|词性:名词
