misinformation:/ˌmɪsˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən/| n. |错误信息，虚假信息 |False or inaccurate information, especially that which is deliberately intended to deceive.  |Example: The spread of misinformation on social media can have serious consequences. 社交媒体上虚假信息的传播可能造成严重后果。|近义词：falsehood, disinformation, deception |反义词：truth, accuracy, factual information |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Misinformation spreads rapidly online.  = 虚假信息在网上迅速传播。
The website contains a lot of misinformation. = 该网站包含大量错误信息。


一般过去时:
They spread misinformation about the election. = 他们在选举中散布虚假信息。
The newspaper published misinformation about the accident. = 这家报纸发表了关于事故的虚假信息。


现在完成时:
They have spread misinformation for years. = 他们多年来一直在散布虚假信息。
Social media has amplified the spread of misinformation. = 社交媒体放大了虚假信息的传播。


过去完成时:
Before the debate, they had already spread misinformation. = 在辩论之前，他们就已经散布了虚假信息。
We realized that we had been given misinformation. = 我们意识到我们收到了错误信息。


将来时:
They will continue to spread misinformation. = 他们将继续散布虚假信息。
The government will combat the spread of misinformation. = 政府将打击虚假信息的传播。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't spread misinformation, the situation would be different. = 如果他们没有散布虚假信息，情况就会不同。
I wish they hadn't spread that misinformation. = 我真希望他们没有散布那些虚假信息。


被动语态:
Misinformation was spread through various channels. = 虚假信息通过各种渠道传播。
The public was misled by the misinformation. = 公众被虚假信息误导了。


疑问句:
Is this information accurate, or is it misinformation? = 这个信息准确吗，还是错误信息？
How can we combat the spread of misinformation? = 我们如何才能打击虚假信息的传播？


否定句:
The report did not contain any misinformation. = 该报告不包含任何错误信息。
We tried to avoid spreading misinformation. = 我们试图避免传播虚假信息。
