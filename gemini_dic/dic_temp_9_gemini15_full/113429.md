lymphoma:/lɪmˈfoʊmə/| n. |淋巴瘤|A cancer that begins in the lymphocytes, a type of white blood cell important part of the body's immune system.  Lymphoma can develop in many parts of the body, including the lymph nodes, spleen, bone marrow, and other organs. There are many different types of lymphoma, which are categorized based on how quickly they grow and spread, and the type of lymphocytes involved. |例句：The doctor diagnosed her with non-Hodgkin's lymphoma. 医生诊断她患有非霍奇金淋巴瘤。|例句：He is undergoing chemotherapy treatment for his lymphoma. 他正在接受化疗治疗他的淋巴瘤。|近义词：lymphosarcoma, lymphoid cancer |反义词：None (it's a disease)
