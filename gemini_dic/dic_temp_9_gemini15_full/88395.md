hirling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/| n.|掷球运动；投掷；猛投；|A fast-paced team sport played in Ireland using a hurley (a wooden stick) and a sliotar (a small ball).  It involves hitting the ball into the opposing team's goal.| Example: Hurling is a very popular sport in Ireland. 掷球运动在爱尔兰非常流行。|近义词：shouting, throwing, flinging|反义词：catching, receiving
