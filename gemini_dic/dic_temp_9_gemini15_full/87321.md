herniation:/ˌhɜːrniˈeɪʃən/| n. |疝气，突出；(器官等的)脱出 |The protrusion of an organ or part of an organ through the wall of the cavity that normally contains it.  This often happens due to weakness or injury in the containing wall.  Examples include hiatal hernia (stomach protrudes into the chest) and spinal disc herniation (intervertebral disc material protrudes into the spinal canal).|The patient experienced severe pain due to a herniation of an intervertebral disc.  这位病人因椎间盘突出而感到剧烈疼痛。
