pro-Swedish: /ˌproʊˈswiːdɪʃ/ | adj. | 亲瑞典的，支持瑞典的 | Favoring or supporting Sweden or Swedish interests.  |The pro-Swedish sentiment in the region was strong during the war.  这个地区的亲瑞典情绪在战争期间很强烈。 | pro-Danish, pro-European | anti-Swedish


USAGE->

He is pro-Swedish. = 他亲瑞典。
She supports pro-Swedish policies. = 她支持亲瑞典的政策。


一般过去时:
He was pro-Swedish during his youth. = 他年轻时亲瑞典。
