familyhoods:/ˈfæmɪliˌhʊdz/ | n. | 家族关系；家庭氛围；家族群体 |  A sense of belonging and connection within a family, encompassing shared values, traditions, and experiences. It also refers to a group of families with shared characteristics or living in close proximity.  This word is a relatively new coinage, blending "family" and "hood." | Example:  The strong familyhoods in their community fostered a sense of unity and support.  这个社区强大的家族关系培养了团结与互助的精神。  The familyhoods of the small town were closely knit, sharing traditions and events. 小镇的各个家族关系紧密相连，共同分享传统和活动。 | 近义词: kinship, family ties, clan, community | 反义词: isolation, estrangement
