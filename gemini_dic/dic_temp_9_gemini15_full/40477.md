contributions:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃənz/ /ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃənz/ | n. |贡献，捐款，投稿 | Acts of giving or supplying something.  The things that are given or supplied. |例句：Her contributions to the project were invaluable.  她的项目贡献非常宝贵。

例句：The museum relies heavily on public contributions.  博物馆严重依赖公众捐款。


近义词：donations, offerings, gifts, input, assistance


反义词：hindrances, obstacles, detriments


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His contributions improve the team's performance. = 他的贡献提高了团队的表现。
Their contributions make a significant difference. = 他们的贡献造成了显著的不同。


一般过去时:
Her contributions significantly advanced the field. = 她的贡献极大地推动了该领域的发展。
His contributions to the charity were substantial. = 他对慈善事业的贡献很大。


现在完成时:
We have received many contributions to the fundraiser. = 我们已经收到了许多募捐款项。
She has made significant contributions to science. = 她对科学做出了重大贡献。


过去完成时:

By the end of the year, they had received numerous contributions. = 到年底，他们已经收到了大量的捐款。


将来时:
We expect significant contributions from our partners next year. = 我们预计明年我们的合作伙伴将做出重大贡献。
His upcoming contributions will shape the future of the company. = 他即将做出的贡献将塑造公司的未来。


虚拟语气:
If everyone made contributions, the project would succeed. = 如果每个人都做出贡献，项目就会成功。

疑问句:

否定句:
