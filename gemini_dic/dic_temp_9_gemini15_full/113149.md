lunulite:/ˈluːnjuːlaɪt/| n. |月牙形矿物|A mineral, typically consisting of calcite, that forms in cavities, often exhibiting a crescent or fan-shaped structure.  It is often found in association with other minerals in hydrothermal veins and sedimentary rocks.|例句：The specimen displayed a beautiful example of lunulite, its characteristic crescent shape clearly visible.|近义词：crescent mineral|反义词:无
