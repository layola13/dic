inter-strain: /ˌɪntərˈstreɪn/ | adj. |菌株间的，种系间的 | Relating to or occurring between different strains of the same species of organism, especially bacteria or viruses.  |例句：Researchers investigated the inter-strain variation in antibiotic resistance. 研究人员调查了抗生素耐药性菌株间的差异。 |近义词：intraspecific |反义词：intrastrain
