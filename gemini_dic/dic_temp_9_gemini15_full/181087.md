reindoctrinates: /ˌriːɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪts/ | vt. | 再灌输；重新教导 | To teach (someone) a particular belief or set of beliefs again, often forcefully or in a way that prevents independent thought.  | 例句：The cult leader reindoctrinates his followers every Sunday.邪教领袖每周日都会对他的信徒进行再灌输。  The government attempted to reindoctrinate the population after the revolution. 革命后，政府试图对人民进行再灌输。| 近义词：re-educate, brainwash, indoctrinate (again) | 反义词：deprogram, enlighten, liberate
