freshmen:/ˈfrɛʃmən/| n. |新生（复数形式为freshmen）|A student in their first year at a university, college, or high school.|例句：The freshmen are attending orientation week. 新生们正在参加迎新周。|近义词：first-year students, newcomers|反义词：seniors, graduates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The freshmen study hard. = 新生们努力学习。
Freshmen attend many orientation events. = 新生们参加许多迎新活动。

一般过去时:
The freshmen participated in a campus tour. = 新生们参加了校园参观。
The freshmen completed their first exams. = 新生们完成了他们的第一次考试。

现在完成时:
The freshmen have already made new friends. = 新生们已经交到了新朋友。
The freshmen have adapted well to college life. = 新生们已经很好地适应了大学生活。

过去完成时:
The freshmen had finished their assignments before the deadline. = 新生们在截止日期前完成了作业。
Before the semester began, the freshmen had registered for classes. = 学期开始前，新生们已经选好了课程。

将来时:
The freshmen will graduate in four years. = 新生们四年后将毕业。
The freshmen will participate in the upcoming academic competition. = 新生们将参加即将到来的学术竞赛。

虚拟语气:
If the freshmen were more organized, they would have better grades. = 如果新生们更有条理，他们的成绩会更好。
I wish the freshmen had more time to relax. = 我希望新生们有更多时间放松。

被动语态:
The freshmen were welcomed by the university. = 新生们受到了大学的欢迎。
The freshmen are being guided through the registration process. = 新生们正在接受注册流程的指导。

疑问句:

否定句:
