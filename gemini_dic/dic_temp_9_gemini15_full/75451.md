fruitless:/ˈfruːtləs/| adj.|徒劳的，无结果的，无效的 |Not producing or achieving any useful result.  |例句：Their attempts to reach a settlement proved fruitless.  他们的和解尝试最终证明是徒劳的。

例句：All his efforts were fruitless. 他的所有努力都白费了。


近义词：unsuccessful, unproductive, vain, ineffective, abortive

反义词：successful, productive, effective, fruitful
