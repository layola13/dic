inverse:/ˈɪnvɜːrs/| adj., n.|相反的；逆的；反函数|Opposite or contrary in position, direction, order, or effect;  a mathematical operation that reverses another operation.|The inverse of addition is subtraction, and the inverse of multiplication is division.  The inverse of a matrix can be used to solve systems of linear equations. |近义词：opposite, converse, reciprocal|反义词：direct, forward

His actions are the inverse of his words. = 他的行为与他的话语相反。
