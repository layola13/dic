navigator:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtər/| n. |导航员，领航员，导航设备|A navigator is a person who navigates, especially one who determines a course or route of a ship or aircraft.  A navigator can also refer to a device that assists in navigation.|例句：The ship's navigator plotted a course around the storm. 这艘船的领航员规划了一条绕过风暴的航线。

The GPS navigator guided us to our destination. GPS导航仪引导我们到达目的地。|近义词：pilot, guide, leader|反义词：follower, led

词源:
The word "navigator" comes from the Latin word "navigare," meaning "to sail."  The suffix "-or" indicates an agent or doer of the action.  Therefore, a navigator is someone who performs the act of navigating.
