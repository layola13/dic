prerenal: /ˌpriːˈriːnəl/ | adj. | 肾前性的；肾前期的 | Relating to the period or conditions before the kidneys;  specifically, referring to kidney failure caused by factors outside the kidneys themselves.  This usually involves reduced blood flow to the kidneys. | 例句：Prerenal azotemia is a common cause of acute kidney injury.  肾前性氮质血症是急性肾损伤的常见原因。


Prerenal failure is often reversible if the underlying cause is treated promptly. 肾前性衰竭如果及时治疗根本原因通常是可逆的。


近义词：pre-renal | 反义词：renal, postrenal


词性：形容词
