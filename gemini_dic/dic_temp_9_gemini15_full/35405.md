co-optations:/ˌkoʊɑːpˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 联合；吸收；篡夺；挪用 | The act of co-opting; the state of being co-opted.  This can refer to the adoption of ideas or practices from another group, the inclusion of someone into a group, or the appropriation of something for a different purpose. | Example: The company's co-optation of user data raised privacy concerns.  这家公司挪用用户数据引发了隐私担忧。 The co-optation of opposition voices into the ruling party weakened the democratic process.  反对派声音被执政党吸收削弱了民主进程。| 近义词：appropriation, adoption, usurpation | 反义词：rejection, exclusion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company co-opts user data for targeted advertising. = 这家公司挪用用户数据进行精准广告投放。
They co-opt ideas from different cultures to create their designs. = 他们从不同的文化中吸收灵感来创作他们的设计。


一般过去时:
The government co-opted the opposition leader into their ranks. = 政府将反对派领导人吸收到了他们的阵营中。
The rebels co-opted the abandoned factory for their headquarters. = 反叛者将废弃的工厂据为己有作为他们的总部。


现在完成时:
The corporation has co-opted many aspects of open-source software. = 该公司已经吸收了开源软件的许多方面。
They have co-opted the language of their opponents to confuse the public. = 他们已经挪用了对手的语言来迷惑公众。


过去完成时:
The dictator had co-opted all media outlets before the election. = 独裁者在选举前已经控制了所有媒体。
The company had co-opted the technology before its competitors did. = 这家公司在竞争对手之前就吸收了这项技术。


将来时:
The new CEO will likely co-opt the existing management team. = 新任首席执行官可能会吸收现有的管理团队。
They will co-opt the resources of the rival company. = 他们将夺取竞争对手的资源。


虚拟语气:
If they co-opted the opposition, they would solidify their power. = 如果他们吸收了反对派，他们就会巩固自己的权力。
I wish they hadn't co-opted our ideas without giving credit. = 我真希望他们没有未经许可就挪用我们的想法。


被动语态:
The innovative ideas were co-opted by the larger corporation. = 这些创新理念被更大的公司吸收了。
The resources were co-opted by the government for military purposes. = 资源被政府用于军事目的。


疑问句:
Were the protesters' demands co-opted by the government? = 抗议者的要求是否被政府吸收了？
Will the company's culture be co-opted by the merger? = 公司文化会在合并后被改变吗？


否定句:
The movement did not co-opt the mainstream narrative. = 这个运动并没有吸收主流叙事。
They did not co-opt the technology, but instead developed their own. = 他们没有吸收这项技术，而是开发了自己的技术。
