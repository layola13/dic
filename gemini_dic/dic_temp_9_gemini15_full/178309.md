reconciliations:/ˌrɛkənˈsɪlieɪʃənz/| n. |和解；调和；协调；对账|The act of making one thing compatible with another; the resolution of conflict.  It also refers to the process of checking and correcting discrepancies in financial accounts.|例句：The reconciliations between the warring factions were fragile.  战争派系之间的和解非常脆弱。

例句：The bank statements need to be reconciled with the company's records. 银行对账单需要与公司的记录进行核对。


近义词：reconciliations, settlements, resolutions, adjustments, harmonizations

反义词：estrangements, conflicts, disagreements, discrepancies
