reconsiderations: /ˌriːkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 再考虑；重新考虑；反复考虑 | The act of reconsidering something; a review or re-evaluation of a previous decision or opinion.  It often implies a more thorough or careful thought process than a simple change of mind. |例句：After much deliberation and reconsiderations, the committee finally approved the proposal.  经过多次商讨和重新考虑后，委员会最终批准了该提案。

例句：Her reconsiderations of the job offer led her to decline it. 她重新考虑了这份工作邀请后决定拒绝。


近义词：reconsiderations, re-evaluations, reviews, re-examinations

反义词：finality, confirmation, acceptance


词性:名词
