resentence:/ˌriːˈsɛntəns/| vt.| 改写句子；重新措辞 |To express (something) in different words, typically in order to improve clarity or conciseness.  |Example:  The judge resentenced the criminal to a longer term.  法官将罪犯的刑期改判为更长。|近义词：rephrase, reformulate, rewrite |反义词：leave unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resents his boss's unfair treatment. = 他对老板的不公平待遇感到愤恨。
She resents being interrupted. = 她讨厌被打扰。

一般过去时:
They resented his interference in their affairs. = 他们讨厌他干预他们的事务。
I resented having to work overtime. = 我讨厌不得不加班。

现在完成时:
He has resented her criticism for years. = 多年来他一直对她持批评态度耿耿于怀。
We have resented their condescending attitude. = 我们一直对他们居高临下的态度感到不满。

过去完成时:
She had resented his arrogance for a long time before she finally confronted him. = 在她最终与他对质之前，她早就对他傲慢的态度感到不满。
They had resented the lack of recognition for their hard work. = 在他们辛勤工作的长期以来，他们一直对缺乏认可感到不满。

将来时:
I will resent any attempt to control me. = 我将对任何试图控制我的企图感到愤怒。
She will resent your interference in her personal life. = 她会对你干涉她的私生活感到愤怒。


虚拟语气:
If I were in your shoes, I would resent such treatment. = 如果我是你，我也会对这样的待遇感到愤怒。
I wish I hadn't resented his help; it would have made things easier. = 我真希望我没有对他提供的帮助心存不满；那样会让事情更容易。

被动语态:
His behavior was resented by everyone in the office. = 他的行为遭到办公室里所有人的反感。
The new rules were resented by many students. = 许多学生对新规不满。

疑问句:
Do you resent his constant complaints? = 你讨厌他不断的抱怨吗？
Does she resent being treated differently? = 她讨厌被区别对待吗？

否定句:
I don't resent your advice; I appreciate it. = 我并不讨厌你的建议；我很感激。
He doesn't resent her success; he admires it. = 他并不嫉妒她的成功；他钦佩她。


PHRASE->
resent doing something: 讨厌做某事
resent someone's actions: 讨厌某人的行为
resent being treated unfairly: 讨厌被不公平对待
deeply resent: 深深地憎恨
bear a resentment: 心存怨恨
