pome:/poʊm/| n.|小诗；果实；苹果|A short poem, especially one that is lyrical and musical in tone.|例句：He wrote a lovely pome about the sunset. 他写了一首关于日落的优美小诗。|近义词：poem, verse|反义词：prose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes a pome every week. = 他每周写一首小诗。
She composes a pome in her spare time. = 她利用空闲时间创作小诗。


一般过去时:
He wrote a pome for his beloved. = 他为他的爱人写了一首诗。
She composed a pome about nature. = 她创作了一首关于大自然的诗歌。


现在完成时:
He has written many pomes this year. = 他今年已经写了很多诗。
She has composed several pomes on love. = 她已经创作了几首关于爱情的诗歌。


过去完成时:
He had written several pomes before he published his first book. = 在出版他的第一本书之前，他已经写了几首诗。
She had composed a long pome before the competition. = 比赛前她已经创作了一首长诗。


将来时:
He will write a pome about his journey. = 他将写一首关于他旅程的诗。
She will compose a pome for the upcoming festival. = 她将为即将到来的节日创作一首诗。


虚拟语气:
If he had more time, he would write a longer pome. = 如果他有更多的时间，他会写一首更长的诗。
If she were a poet, she would compose a more beautiful pome. = 如果她是诗人，她会创作一首更美丽的诗歌。


被动语态:
A pome was written by the famous poet. = 这首诗是由著名的诗人写的。
A beautiful pome is being composed by the young artist. = 一首优美的诗歌正在由这位年轻的艺术家创作。


疑问句:
Did he write a pome yesterday? = 他昨天写诗了吗？
Has she ever composed a pome before? = 她以前写过诗吗？


否定句:
He didn't write a pome today. = 他今天没有写诗。
She hasn't composed a pome yet. = 她还没创作诗歌。
