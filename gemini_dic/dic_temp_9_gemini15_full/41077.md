copperish: /ˈkɒpərɪʃ/ | adj. |略带铜色的，铜红色的 |Slightly resembling copper in color; reddish-brown.  The word suggests a less intense or pure copper color than "coppery." It implies a hint or tinge of copper rather than a vibrant, metallic shine. | 例句：The sunset cast a copperish glow on the old buildings.  夕阳给古老的建筑投下了一层略带铜色的光芒。

The antique vase had a copperish patina.  那个古董花瓶带有一种铜红色的包浆。


近义词：bronze, brownish-red, reddish-brown |反义词：silvery, bluish, greenish


词性:形容词
