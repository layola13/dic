parleyed:/ˈpɑːrliːd/|/ˈpɑːrliːd/| v.|谈判；会谈；交涉|To engage in a conference or discussion, especially one intended to end a dispute.|The two sides parleyed for hours, but reached no agreement.  双方谈判了数小时，但没有达成协议。|The warring factions finally parleyed to discuss a ceasefire. 交战各派最终会谈以讨论停火。|negotiate, confer, discuss|fight, battle, war|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They parley with the enemy. = 他们与敌人谈判。
We regularly parley about business strategies. = 我们定期就商业策略进行谈判。


一般过去时:
The diplomats parleyed throughout the night. = 外交官们整夜都在谈判。
The two leaders parleyed to find a solution. = 两位领导人会谈以寻求解决方案。


现在完成时:
They have parleyed with the rebels several times. = 他们已经与叛军谈判过几次了。
The negotiators have parleyed for days without a breakthrough. = 谈判人员已经谈判了几天，但没有取得突破。


过去完成时:
They had parleyed for weeks before the final agreement. = 他们在最终协议达成之前已经谈判了几个星期。
The opposing armies had parleyed before engaging in battle. = 对峙的军队在交战前曾进行过谈判。


将来时:
They will parley tomorrow to discuss the treaty. = 他们明天将举行会谈讨论条约。
The representatives will parley in an attempt to resolve the conflict. = 代表们将进行会谈，试图解决冲突。


虚拟语气:
If they had parleyed earlier, the conflict might have been avoided. = 如果他们早些谈判，冲突本可以避免。
I wish they would parley and find a peaceful solution. = 我希望他们能谈判并找到和平的解决方案。


被动语态:
The issue was parleyed over for many hours. =  这个问题经过了数小时的讨论。
A ceasefire was parleyed into existence. = 通过谈判达成了停火协议。


疑问句:
Will they parley soon to discuss terms? = 他们会很快谈判讨论条款吗？
Have they parleyed with the other party yet? = 他们已经与对方谈判了吗？


否定句:
They did not parley with the terrorists. = 他们没有与恐怖分子谈判。
The two sides refused to parley, leading to further conflict. = 双方拒绝谈判，导致进一步的冲突。


PHRASE->

parley with someone = 与某人谈判
enter into a parley = 开始谈判
a fruitless parley = 无效的谈判
a secret parley = 秘密谈判
break off a parley = 中断谈判
