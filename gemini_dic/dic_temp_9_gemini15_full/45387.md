cup:/kʌp/|n.|杯子|A small, usually cylindrical, container, typically with a handle, used for drinking from.|例句：I drank a cup of tea. 我喝了一杯茶。|She put the cup on the saucer. 她把杯子放在碟子上。|近义词：mug, goblet, glass|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drinks a cup of coffee every morning. = 他每天早上喝一杯咖啡。
She uses a special cup for her tea. = 她用一个特别的杯子喝茶。

一般过去时:
I dropped my cup and broke it. = 我打碎了我的杯子。
They filled their cups with water. = 他们用清水装满了杯子。

现在完成时:
I have finished my cup of coffee. = 我已经喝完我的咖啡了。
She has spilled her cup of tea. = 她把茶洒了。

过去完成时:
I had already emptied my cup before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经喝完我的茶了。
They had broken their cups before the party started. = 派对开始之前他们已经打破了杯子。

将来时:

I wish I had a bigger cup. = 我希望我有个更大的杯子。

被动语态:

否定句:
