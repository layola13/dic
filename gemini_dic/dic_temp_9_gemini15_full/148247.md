overskip:/ˈoʊvərˌskɪp/| v.|跳过，略过|To skip or omit something, often intentionally.  It implies skipping over something more significantly than a simple skip. |例句：He overskipped several chapters in the book because he was bored. 他因为无聊而跳过了书中好几章。  She overskipped the introductory material and went straight to the main points. 她跳过了导言部分，直接去看重点。|近义词：omit, bypass, skip, neglect|反义词：include, consider, attend to|动词
