postverbal:/ˌpoʊstˈvɜːrbəl/| adj.|后置的；位于动词之后的|Following or placed after a verb.  This term is primarily used in linguistics to describe elements, typically adverbs or phrases, that come after the verb in a sentence. It contrasts with preverbal elements which precede the verb.|例句：The adverb "quickly" is in a postverbal position in the sentence "He ran quickly." 他迅速地跑掉了。(quickly位于ran之后)|近义词：postpositive|反义词：preverbal
