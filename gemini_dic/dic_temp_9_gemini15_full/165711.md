premuster:/ˌpriːˈmʌstər/| n.| 预备集合；预备动员 | A preliminary muster or assembly, especially of troops before a battle or campaign.  It refers to the gathering or assembling of something or someone in preparation for a larger event or action. | 例句：The premuster of the troops took place at dawn. = 部队的预备集合在黎明时分进行。  The general ordered a premuster before the final assault. = 将军下令在最终攻击前进行预备集合。 | 近义词：muster, assembly, gathering, preparation | 反义词：disbandment, dispersal
