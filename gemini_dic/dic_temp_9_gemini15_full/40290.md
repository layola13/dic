continuing:/kənˈtɪnjuɪŋ/| adj.|持续的，继续的|Continuing education is crucial for professional development. 继续教育对专业发展至关重要。|近义词：ongoing, persistent, uninterrupted |反义词：stopped, ceased, finished |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is continuing as planned. = 项目正在按计划进行。
The rain is continuing throughout the day. = 雨一整天都在下。

一般过去时:
The meeting was continuing when I arrived. = 我到达时会议还在继续。
The music was continuing even after everyone left. = 即使每个人都离开了，音乐还在继续。

现在完成时:
The construction has been continuing for months. = 建筑工程已经持续了几个月。
She has been continuing her studies despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，她仍在继续学习。

过去完成时:
The game had been continuing for hours before the rain stopped it. = 雨停之前，比赛已经持续了几个小时。
They had been continuing their research for years before they made a breakthrough. = 他们在取得突破之前已经进行了多年的研究。

将来时:
The negotiations will be continuing into next week. = 谈判将持续到下周。
The festival will be continuing throughout the summer. = 节日将持续整个夏天。

虚拟语气:
If the rain were continuing, we wouldn't go out. = 如果雨还在下，我们就不会出去了。
I wish the concert were continuing longer. = 我希望音乐会能持续更久。

被动语态:
The experiment is being continued by the researchers. = 实验正在由研究人员继续进行。
The investigation is being continued by the police. = 调查正在由警方继续进行。

疑问句:
Is the performance continuing? = 演出还在继续吗？
Are they continuing their work? = 他们还在继续工作吗？

否定句:
The meeting is not continuing. = 会议没有继续进行。
They are not continuing their relationship. = 他们没有继续他们的关系。


PHRASE->
continuing education 继续教育
continuing care 持续护理
continuing contract 续约合同
continuing resolution 预算决议
continuing story 连载故事
