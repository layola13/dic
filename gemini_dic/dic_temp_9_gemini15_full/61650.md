emissary:/ˈɛmɪsɛri/ | n. |使者，特使 | A person sent on a special mission, usually as a diplomatic representative.  |例句：The president sent an emissary to negotiate with the rebels. 总统派了一个特使去和叛军谈判。|例句：She acted as an emissary between the two warring factions. 她充当了两个交战派系之间的使者。|近义词：messenger, envoy, delegate, representative |反义词： N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company sends an emissary to every major trade show. = 公司派特使参加每一个大型贸易展。
He regularly acts as an emissary for his government. = 他经常充当政府的使者。

一般过去时:
The king dispatched an emissary to the neighboring kingdom. = 国王派遣了一名使者到邻国。
They sent an emissary to assess the situation. = 他们派了一个特使去评估情况。

现在完成时:
The organization has sent several emissaries to the affected region. = 该组织已向受灾地区派遣了几名特使。
She has served as an emissary for peace for many years. = 她多年来一直担任和平特使。

过去完成时:
Before the war began, they had already sent an emissary to negotiate. = 在战争开始之前，他们已经派了一个特使去谈判。
The government had dispatched emissaries to various countries before the summit. = 在峰会之前，政府已向各个国家派遣了特使。

将来时:
The company will send an emissary to the conference next week. = 公司下周将派一名特使参加会议。
They will dispatch an emissary to investigate the matter further. = 他们将派遣一名特使进一步调查此事。


虚拟语气:
If I were an emissary, I would try to find a peaceful solution. = 如果我是特使，我会努力寻找和平的解决办法。
I wish I could be an emissary for world peace. = 我希望我能成为世界和平的特使。

被动语态:
An emissary was sent to deliver the message. = 派遣了一名特使去传达信息。
The situation was carefully assessed by the emissary. = 特使仔细评估了情况。

疑问句:
Was an emissary sent to the conference? = 有没有派特使去参加会议？
Who was chosen as the emissary? = 谁被选为特使？

否定句:
They did not send an emissary to the meeting. = 他们没有派特使去参加会议。
No emissary was sent to negotiate a ceasefire. = 没有派特使去谈判停火。


PHRASE->
secret emissary 秘密使者
special emissary 特使
personal emissary 私人使者
official emissary 官方使者
trusted emissary 可信赖的使者

词源:
From Middle English *emissarie, from Old French *emissaire, from Medieval Latin emissarius, from Latin emittere "to send out," from ex- "out" + mittere "to send."
