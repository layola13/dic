sages:/ˈseɪdʒɪz/ | n. |智者，圣人，贤人 |Wise people, especially those who are old and have a lot of experience and knowledge. They often offer advice and guidance.  |The sages of ancient Greece shaped Western philosophy.  古代希腊的智者塑造了西方哲学。|  wise men, elders, gurus, philosophers |fools, ignoramuses, simpletons |名词
