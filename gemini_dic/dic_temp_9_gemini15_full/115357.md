manorialism:/məˈnɔːriəlɪzəm/| n. |庄园制度；领主制度 |The economic and social system of feudal Europe, based on the manor and the relationship between lord and serf.  It involved a hierarchical structure where the lord owned the land and the serfs worked it in exchange for protection and the right to use a portion of the land.|例句：The decline of manorialism in England was a gradual process spanning centuries.  英格兰庄园制度的衰落是一个跨越几个世纪的逐渐过程。|近义词：feudalism, serfdom |反义词：capitalism, free market
