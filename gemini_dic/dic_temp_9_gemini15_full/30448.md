centreboards:/ˈsɛntərˌbɔːrdz/| n.|中板（船）|A retractable keel, typically found on sailing boats, that can be lowered to provide stability and prevent capsizing.  It is housed in a slot along the boat's centerline.|例句：The centreboard was lowered to improve the boat's stability in the strong winds.  中板放下以提高船在强风中的稳定性。

The sailboat's centreboard was raised to reduce drag when sailing upwind.  为了减少逆风航行时的阻力，帆船的中板被升起。|近义词：keel, retractable keel|反义词：fixed keel
