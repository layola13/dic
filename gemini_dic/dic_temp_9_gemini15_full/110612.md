limousine:/ˈlɪməziːn/| n. |豪华轿车，大型轿车 |A limousine is a large, luxurious car, typically with a partition separating the driver's compartment from the passenger area.  It's often used for chauffeured transportation. |例句：He arrived in a long black limousine. 他乘坐一辆黑色加长轿车到达。|例句：The company provides limousine service to the airport.  该公司提供机场豪华轿车接送服务。|近义词：luxury car, sedan (in some contexts) |反义词：economy car, budget car |名词
