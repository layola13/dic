polyvalence:/ˈpɒliˌveɪləns/ /ˌpɑːliˈveɪləns/ | n. |多价，多功能性，多用途性 |The ability of something to have multiple functions or meanings.  It refers to the capacity of a single entity to exhibit diverse characteristics or serve various purposes.  This concept is applicable across numerous fields, including chemistry, linguistics, and social sciences. |例句：The polyvalence of the word "run" allows it to be used in many different contexts.  这个单词“run”的多义性使其能够在许多不同的语境中使用。

The polyvalence of this tool makes it suitable for various tasks.  这个工具的多功能性使其适用于各种任务。


近义词：versatility, multifunctionality, adaptability, flexibility |反义词：singularity, unidimensionality, monofunctionality |词性：名词


词源:

The word "polyvalence" is derived from the combination of two elements:

* **poly-**:  A prefix derived from Greek, meaning "many" or "much."
* **valence**:  Derived from Latin, referring to the capacity of an element to combine with other elements, particularly in chemistry.  It has broadened its meaning to encompass the capacity for multiple functions or meanings.
