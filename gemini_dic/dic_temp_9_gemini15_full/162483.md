preaffidavit:/ˌpriːˈæfɪdeɪvɪt/| n. |预先宣誓书；预先声明|A written statement made under oath but before a formal affidavit is completed.  It often serves as a preliminary statement or a draft of a future affidavit.  It's not legally binding in the same way as a formal affidavit.|例句：The lawyer asked the witness to prepare a preaffidavit outlining the key events before their official testimony.  律师要求证人准备一份预先宣誓书，概述关键事件，以便日后正式作证。|近义词：preliminary statement, draft affidavit|反义词：affidavit (正式宣誓书)
