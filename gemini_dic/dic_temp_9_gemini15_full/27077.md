calibers:/ˈkælɪbərz/ | n. |口径；才干；等级 | The diameter of the bore of a firearm.  It also refers to the quality, ability, or skill of a person or thing. | 例句：The rifle's caliber is .308.  这支步枪的口径是.308英寸。  He's a man of considerable caliber. 他是一位能力相当强的人。 | 近义词：diameter, gauge, ability, capacity, quality | 反义词：inability, incompetence
