reflexion: /rɪˈflɛkʃən/ | n. | 反射；反省；映像；沉思 | The act of reflecting; the image reflected; serious thought or consideration. | 例句：The reflexion of the sun in the water was dazzling.  阳光在水中的反射令人眼花缭乱。

例句：Her reflexion on her past mistakes led to positive change. 她对过去错误的反思带来了积极的改变。

近义词：reflection, consideration, contemplation, musing | 反义词：action, inaction(在特定语境下)
