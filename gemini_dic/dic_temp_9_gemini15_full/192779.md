seallike:/ˈsiːlˌlaɪk/| adj.|海豹似的，像海豹的|Resembling a seal in appearance or behavior.  The word combines "seal" (a marine mammal) with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The smooth, grey stones were seallike in their shape and texture.  这些光滑的灰色石头在形状和纹理上像海豹一样。

The creature had seallike eyes and flippers.  那个生物长着像海豹一样的眼睛和鳍状肢。

近义词：seal-shaped, seal-resembling

反义词：unseallike (not commonly used)
