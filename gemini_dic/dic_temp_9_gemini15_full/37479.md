commitment:/kəˈmɪtmənt/ | n. | 承诺；奉献；承担的义务 | A commitment is a promise or pledge to do something. It also refers to the dedication or involvement one has to a cause, task, or relationship.  |  例句：He made a commitment to finish the project by the deadline. 他承诺在截止日期前完成项目。

例句：Her commitment to her family is unwavering. 她对家庭的承诺坚定不移。


近义词：promise, pledge, dedication, obligation, involvement | 反义词：neglect, disregard, abandonment


词源:  The word "commitment" comes from the Old French word "commettre," meaning "to entrust, commit," which itself is derived from the Latin word "committere," meaning "to entrust, commit, put together."  The suffix "-ment" is a noun-forming suffix indicating a state or action.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a strong commitment to his work. 他对他的工作做出了强烈的承诺。
Their commitment ensures the project's success. 他们的承诺确保了项目的成功。


一般过去时:
She committed herself to helping the homeless. 她致力于帮助无家可归的人。
They committed to a long-term investment. 他们承诺进行长期投资。


现在完成时:
We have committed significant resources to this project. 我们已经为这个项目投入了大量资源。
She has shown a remarkable commitment to her studies. 她对学习表现出了非凡的投入。


过去完成时:
They had already committed to the deal before the details were revealed.  在细节透露之前，他们已经承诺了这笔交易。
He had committed himself to the cause years ago. 多年前他就已经投身于这项事业了。


将来时:
I will commit to providing regular updates. 我将承诺提供定期更新。
They will commit to the new strategy. 他们将致力于新的战略。


虚拟语气:
If he committed to the plan, it would succeed. 如果他致力于这个计划，它就会成功。
I wish she had committed to finishing the book. 我希望她能坚持完成这本书。


被动语态:
Significant funds were committed to the research. 大量资金投入到了这项研究中。
The company is committed to providing excellent customer service. 公司致力于提供优质的客户服务。


疑问句:
Are you committed to this relationship? 你对这段感情投入了吗？
Is he fully committed to the project? 他完全投入到这个项目中了吗？


否定句:
He didn't commit to a definite answer. 他没有给出明确的答案。
They were not committed to the long-term plan. 他们没有致力于长期计划。
