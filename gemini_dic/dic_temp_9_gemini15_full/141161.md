ooephytic:/ˌoʊ.əˈfɪtɪk/| adj. | 异养的；非自养的 |  Relating to or denoting plants that derive their nourishment from organic matter, typically decaying matter, rather than from photosynthesis.  Such plants lack chlorophyll and are often found growing on other plants or decaying organic material. | Example: Many fungi are ooephytic, obtaining their nutrients from decaying wood or other organic matter.  许多真菌是异养的，从腐烂的木材或其他有机物中获取养分。|近义词：heterotrophic, saprophytic | 反义词：autotrophic
