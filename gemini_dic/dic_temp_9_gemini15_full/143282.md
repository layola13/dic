outdoorish: /ˈaʊtˌdɔːrɪʃ/ | adj. |略微户外的；有点像户外的 | Slightly outdoorsy; having some characteristics of being outdoors.  This word implies a less intense or committed connection to the outdoors than "outdoorsy." It suggests a preference for outdoor activities but perhaps not to the exclusion of indoor activities. |例句：He has an outdoorish style, preferring casual clothes suitable for hikes or picnics. 他有一种略带户外风格，更喜欢适合远足或野餐的休闲服装。


The style of his house is quite outdoorish, with large windows and a patio. 他的房子风格有点户外感，有大窗户和露台。


近义词：outdoorsy, semi-outdoor, somewhat outdoor |反义词：indoorsy, indoor

词源:  The word "outdoorish" is a relatively recent coinage, formed by adding the suffix "-ish" to "outdoor."  The suffix "-ish"  adds a sense of approximation, slightness, or resemblance.  Therefore, "outdoorish" means having qualities somewhat resembling those of the outdoors.
