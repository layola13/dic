exagitate: /ɪɡˈzædʒɪteɪt/ | vt. | 使激动；使不安；加剧 | To make (someone or something) agitated or more agitated; to worsen or intensify (a problem or situation). | 例句：The news report further exacerbated the already tense situation.  新闻报道进一步加剧了本已紧张的局势。
The constant noise is enough to exagitate even the calmest person.  持续的噪音足以让即使是最冷静的人也感到不安。| 近义词：agitate, excite, inflame, stir up | 反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, tranquilize | 动词
