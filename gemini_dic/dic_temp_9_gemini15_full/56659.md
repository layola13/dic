词源:
The word "donut" is a shortened form of "doughnut."  The word "doughnut" itself comes from the Dutch word  "oliebollen," which means "oil balls."  The term evolved through various spellings and pronunciations over time, eventually settling on the common forms "doughnut" and "donut."
