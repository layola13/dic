ill-gotten: /ˌɪlˈɡɒtən/ | adj. | 不义之财的，非法获得的 | Obtained illegally or dishonestly.  |例句：He amassed an ill-gotten fortune through shady dealings. 他通过不正当交易积累了不义之财。
例句：The ill-gotten gains were eventually confiscated by the authorities.  非法所得最终被当局没收。| 近义词：illegitimate, unlawful, illicit, dishonest, corrupt | 反义词：legitimate, honest, rightful
