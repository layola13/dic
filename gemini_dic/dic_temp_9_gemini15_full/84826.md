hang around:/ˌhæŋ əˈraʊnd/| v.|闲逛，逗留|To spend time in a place without doing very much.  It often implies a lack of purpose or a casual attitude.|例句：I like to hang around the coffee shop and people-watch. 我喜欢在咖啡馆闲逛，观察人们。|例句：They hung around the mall for hours. 他们在商场闲逛了好几个小时。|近义词：loaf, loiter, linger, idle|反义词：rush, hurry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hang around the park every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在公园闲逛。
We hang around the cafe after work. = 我们下班后经常在咖啡馆闲逛。

一般过去时:
I hung around the library all day yesterday. = 我昨天一整天都在图书馆闲逛。
They hung around the beach until sunset. = 他们一直待在海滩上直到日落。

现在完成时:
She has hung around this town for years. = 她在这个镇上闲逛了好几年了。
We have hung around waiting for the bus. = 我们一直在等车。

过去完成时:
He had hung around the corner before she arrived. = 她到达之前，他一直在拐角处闲逛。
They had hung around the bar all night before going home. = 他们整晚都在酒吧闲逛才回家。

将来时:
I will hang around until he gets back. = 我会一直等到他回来。
We will hang around here for a while. = 我们会在这一带待一会儿。

虚拟语气:
If I had nothing better to do, I would hang around. = 如果我没有更好的事做，我会去闲逛。
I wish I could hang around longer. = 我希望我能多待一会儿。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it is intransitive.)


疑问句:
What do you usually hang around? = 你通常去哪里闲逛？
Where do they hang around? = 他们通常在什么地方闲逛？


否定句:
I don't usually hang around malls. = 我通常不去商场闲逛。
They didn't hang around after the party. = 派对结束后他们没有闲逛。



PHRASE->
hang around with = 和...一起闲逛
hang around the house = 在家闲逛
hang around doing nothing = 无所事事地闲逛
hang around all day = 一整天都闲逛
hang around for a while = 闲逛一会儿
