prescheduling: /ˌpriːˈskɛdʒʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 预先安排，预先排程 | The act of scheduling something in advance.  It often involves reserving resources or time slots before they are actually needed. |

例句：

The prescheduling of appointments ensured a smooth workflow. = 预先安排预约确保了工作流程的顺畅。

Effective prescheduling of tasks helped us meet the deadline. = 有效的预先安排任务帮助我们按时完成。


近义词：advance scheduling, prior scheduling, pre-planning

反义词：last-minute scheduling, impromptu scheduling


词性：名词
