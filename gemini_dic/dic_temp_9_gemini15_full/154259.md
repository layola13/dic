perished:/ˈpɛrɪʃt/| v.|消亡；死于；毁灭；枯萎 | to die, especially in a violent or sudden way; to be destroyed or ruined.  |例句：Many people perished in the earthquake.  许多人在地震中丧生。
Many plants perished in the drought.  许多植物在干旱中枯萎了。|近义词：die, expire, be destroyed, decay|反义词：survive, thrive, flourish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perishes from lack of food. 他因缺乏食物而死去。
The plants perish in the frost. 植物在霜冻中枯萎。


一般过去时:
Many soldiers perished in the battle. 许多士兵在战斗中丧生。
The ancient civilization perished centuries ago. 古代文明在几个世纪前消失了。


现在完成时:
Thousands have perished in the war. 数千人在战争中丧生。
The crops have perished due to the extreme heat. 由于酷暑，庄稼已经枯萎了。


过去完成时:
The explorers had perished before help arrived.  救援到来之前探险家们已经遇难了。
The evidence had perished with time. 证据随着时间的推移消失了。


将来时:
If we don't act quickly, many people will perish. 如果我们不迅速采取行动，许多人将会丧生。
Without water, the plants will surely perish. 没有水，植物肯定会枯萎。


虚拟语气:
If he had not sought shelter, he would have perished in the storm. 如果他没有寻找庇护所，他就会在暴风雨中丧生。
If the fire had continued, the whole forest would have perished. 如果大火继续燃烧，整个森林都会被烧毁。


被动语态:
Many lives were perished in the flood. 洪水中丧失了许多生命。(注意：perish 通常不用于被动语态，此句为特殊情况)
The ancient manuscripts were perished by fire. 古代手稿被大火烧毁了。(此句更强调被火烧毁的结果)


疑问句:
Did many people perish in the accident? 事故中很多人丧生了吗？
Will the flowers perish in this cold weather? 这些花会在这么冷的天气里枯萎吗？


否定句:
He did not perish in the fire. 他没有死于这场大火。
The plants did not perish despite the drought. 尽管干旱，植物却没有枯萎。
