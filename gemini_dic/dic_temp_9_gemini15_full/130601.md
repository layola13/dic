nonadept:/ˌnɒnˈædept/| adj.|不熟练的，外行的|Not skilled or proficient in a particular area or activity.  A nonadept is someone lacking skill or expertise in a particular field.|例句：He was a nonadept at cooking, often burning the food. 他做饭不熟练，经常把食物烧焦。|例句：Despite her years of experience, she remained a nonadept at public speaking. 尽管她有几年的经验，但在公开演讲方面仍然不熟练。|近义词：inept, unskilled, unskillful, amateur, clumsy|反义词：adept, expert, proficient, skilled, master
