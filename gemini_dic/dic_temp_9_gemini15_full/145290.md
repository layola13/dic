overbrag:/ˈoʊvərbræɡ/ | v. | 吹牛过头，过分夸耀 | To brag excessively or boastfully; to exaggerate one's accomplishments or possessions. |例句：He overbragged about his supposed connections to Hollywood celebrities. 他夸大了自己与好莱坞名人的所谓关系。

例句：Don't overbrag about your achievements; let your work speak for itself. 不要过分夸耀你的成就；让你的工作自己说话。

近义词：boast, exaggerate, show off, swagger

反义词：understate, downplay, minimize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overbrags about his wealth. = 他吹嘘他的财富。
She overbrags about her children's accomplishments. = 她夸大她孩子们的成就。

一般过去时:
He overbragged about his trip to Europe. = 他夸大了他去欧洲的旅行。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
