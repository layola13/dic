interchanged:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒd/| v. |交换；互换|The meaning of the word "interchanged" is to exchange or substitute one thing for another.  It implies a reciprocal action, where two or more things are switched.  This action could be physical, as in swapping positions, or conceptual, as in exchanging ideas.|例句：The two teams interchanged players during the game.  两队在比赛中互换了球员。

The two countries interchanged ambassadors. 两国互换了大使。|近义词：exchanged, swapped, substituted|反义词：kept, retained|动词
