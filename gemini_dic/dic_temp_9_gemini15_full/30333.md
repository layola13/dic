censureship:/ˈsɛnʃərʃɪp/| n. |审查制度；非难；责备|The control or suppression of what can be published, broadcast, or otherwise disseminated.|例句：Government censorship prevented the publication of the controversial article. 政府审查制度阻止了这篇有争议的文章的发表。|近义词：control, supervision, criticism|反义词：freedom of speech, liberty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Censorship prevents the spread of certain information. = 审查制度阻止了某些信息的传播。
The government censors all news reports. = 政府审查所有新闻报道。

一般过去时:
The dictator imposed strict censorship on the media. = 独裁者对媒体实行严格的审查制度。
They censored the book before its release. = 他们在出版前审查了这本书。

现在完成时:
Censorship has stifled free speech in many countries. = 审查制度扼杀了许多国家的言论自由。
They have censored countless articles and books. = 他们审查了无数的文章和书籍。

过去完成时:
Before the revolution, the government had implemented a harsh censorship system. = 在革命之前，政府已经实施了严厉的审查制度。
They had censored the news reports before they were broadcast. = 他们在新闻报道播出之前就对其进行了审查。

将来时:
The new law will increase censorship on the internet. = 新法律将加强对互联网的审查。
They will censor any content that is deemed offensive. = 他们将审查任何被认为具有冒犯性的内容。

虚拟语气:
If there were no censorship, we would have more freedom of expression. = 如果没有审查制度，我们将有更多的表达自由。
I wish there were less censorship in our society. = 我希望我们社会审查制度少一些。

被动语态:
The article was censored by the authorities. = 这篇文章被当局审查了。
Many books have been censored throughout history. = 历史上许多书籍都被审查过。

疑问句:
Is censorship necessary in a democratic society? = 在民主社会中，审查制度有必要吗？
What forms of censorship are currently in use? = 目前正在使用哪些形式的审查制度？

否定句:

There was no censorship during the election campaign. = 在竞选期间没有审查制度。
