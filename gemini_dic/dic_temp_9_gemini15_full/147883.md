overread:/ˌoʊvərˈriːd/| vt.|粗略阅读；漏读；误读|To read something too quickly or carelessly, resulting in a misunderstanding or missed information.|例句：I overread the instructions and missed a crucial step. 我粗略阅读了说明，错过了关键的一步。
例句：He overread the contract and signed it without noticing the hidden clause. 他草率地阅读了合同，没有注意到隐藏条款就签了字。|近义词：misread, skim, scan|反义词：scrutinize, peruse
