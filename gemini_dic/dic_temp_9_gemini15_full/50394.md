deposers:/dɪˈpoʊzərz/ | n. |作证者；证人；废黜者|People who give sworn testimony, especially in a legal context.  Also refers to those who depose or overthrow someone from power. |例句：The deposers in the trial provided crucial evidence against the defendant.  审判中的证人提供了对被告至关重要的证据。The deposers finally succeeded in overthrowing the tyrannical king. 废黜者最终成功地推翻了暴君。|近义词：witnesses, informants, ousters|反义词：defendants, accused, supporters
