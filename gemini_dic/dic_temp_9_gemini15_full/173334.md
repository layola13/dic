quasi-demandingly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪdɪˈmændɪŋli/ | adv. | 近乎要求地，似有要求地 |  The word implies a subtle but insistent pressure or expectation, falling short of a direct demand but conveying a strong suggestion or implication of one.  It suggests an indirect or implicit demand, often conveyed through tone or implication rather than explicit words.


例句：

He looked at me quasi-demandingly, expecting me to offer an explanation. 他近乎要求地看着我，期待我给出解释。

She spoke quasi-demandingly about the need for immediate action. 她似有要求地谈论了立即采取行动的必要性。


近义词：implicitly, suggestively, insinuatingly, indirectly


反义词：directly, explicitly, openly


词性：副词
