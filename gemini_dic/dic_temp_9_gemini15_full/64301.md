equivocacies:/ɪˌkwɪˈvɒkəsiːz/ | n. | 模棱两可；含糊其辞；含糊不清的话 |  Ambiguous or unclear statements or expressions; instances of using equivocal language.  The use of equivocal language allows one to avoid committing to a specific position or meaning.  It often involves using words or phrases that have multiple interpretations. | The politician's equivocacies made it difficult to understand his true intentions.  这位政治家的含糊其辞使得人们难以理解他的真实意图。
