rehinge:/ˌriːˈhɪndʒ/| vt.|重新装铰链；改变…的中心或基础|To attach a new hinge or hinges to (something). To change the central or fundamental aspect of (something).|例句：We had to rehinge the old cupboard door. 我们不得不给旧橱柜门重新装铰链。 The debate was rehinged on the question of funding. 这场辩论的中心问题变成了资金问题。|近义词：rehang, refit|反义词：remove hinge, dismantle


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He rehinges the door every year. 他每年都给门重新装铰链。
She rehinges her strategy on new market research. 她根据新的市场调查重新调整了她的策略。


一般过去时:
They rehinged the gate after the storm. 暴风雨过后，他们重新装上了大门铰链。
The company rehinged its marketing campaign after poor sales. 公司在销售不佳后重新调整了其营销活动。


现在完成时:
We have rehinged all the windows in the house. 我们已经重新安装了房子里所有的窗户铰链。
The government has rehinged its economic policy on sustainable growth. 政府已将经济政策的中心放在可持续增长上。


过去完成时:
They had rehinged the cabinet before we arrived. 在我们到达之前，他们已经重新安装了橱柜的铰链。
The artist had rehinged his approach to painting after years of experimentation.  这位艺术家经过多年的实验后，改变了他的绘画方法。


将来时:
We will rehinge the broken door tomorrow. 我们明天将重新安装坏的门铰链。
The company will rehinge its product line to meet changing customer demands. 公司将调整其产品线以满足不断变化的客户需求。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would rehinge the window. 如果我有工具，我会重新安装窗户铰链。
If they had rehinged their strategy earlier, they might have succeeded. 如果他们早点调整策略，他们也许会成功。


被动语态:
The door was rehinged by the carpenter. 门是由木匠重新装铰链的。
The company's marketing strategy was rehinged after negative customer reviews.  在收到负面客户评价后，公司的营销策略进行了调整。


疑问句:
Will you rehinge the gate this weekend? 你这个周末会重新装大门铰链吗？
Has the company rehinged its focus on innovation? 公司是否已将重点重新放在创新上？


否定句:
He didn't rehinge the cupboard door. 他没有重新安装橱柜门铰链。
The company did not rehinge its business model despite the losses.  尽管亏损，该公司并没有调整其商业模式。


PHRASE->
rehinge on (something):  以...为中心
rehinge around (something):  围绕...重新调整

rehinge the narrative:  重新调整叙事
