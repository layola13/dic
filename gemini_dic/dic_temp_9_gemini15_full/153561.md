pentagynous:/ˌpentəˈdʒaɪnəs/| adj.|五雌蕊的|Having five pistils (the female reproductive organ of a flower).  This term is primarily used in botany to describe flowers with five carpels, each potentially containing one or more ovules.|例句：The pentagynous flower displayed five distinct pistils, a characteristic feature of the species.  这朵五雌蕊的花展现了五个不同的雌蕊，这是该物种的特征。 The botanist carefully examined the flower's reproductive structures, noting its pentagynous nature. 植物学家仔细检查了花的生殖结构，注意到它的五雌蕊特性。|近义词：pentacarpellary (having five carpels)|反义词：monogynous (having one pistil), digynous (having two pistils)
