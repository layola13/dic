pentachlorophenate:/ˌpɛntəˌklɔːrəˈfiːneɪt/| n.|五氯酚盐|Pentachlorophenate is the salt or ester of pentachlorophenol (PCP), a broad-spectrum biocide used as a wood preservative, herbicide, and fungicide.  It's formed when pentachlorophenol reacts with a base.|The wood was treated with pentachlorophenate to prevent rot.  这木材经过五氯酚盐处理以防止腐烂。|近义词：PCP salt (less formal)|反义词：无
