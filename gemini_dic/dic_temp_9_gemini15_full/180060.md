reflector:/rɪˈflɛktər/ | n. |反射器，反光镜 | A reflector is a surface that reflects light, sound, or other waves.  It is often used to direct light in a specific direction or to increase the intensity of a signal.  | Example: The car's headlights use reflectors to maximize the light output. 汽车的大灯使用反光镜来最大限度地提高光输出。 The satellite uses a large reflector to receive and transmit signals.  卫星使用大型反射器来接收和发送信号。 |近义词：mirror, resonator |反义词：absorber
