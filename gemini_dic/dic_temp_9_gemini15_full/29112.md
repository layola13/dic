cascaded:/kæsˈkeɪdɪd/| adj. |层叠的；瀑布似的；级联的| Arranged or falling in a series of stages or layers, like a waterfall.  The term often implies a continuous flow or progression.|例句：The water cascaded down the mountainside.  水从山坡上层层叠叠地流下来。

The lights cascaded down the building's facade. 灯光层层叠叠地照在建筑物的正面。

近义词： cascading, tiered, layered, stepped |反义词：uniform, single-level

词源:  From the noun "cascade," ultimately derived from the Old Italian word *cascata*, meaning "waterfall," which in turn comes from *cadere*, meaning "to fall."  The "-ed" suffix indicates the past participle form, used here as an adjective.
