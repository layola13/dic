nonresurrection: /ˌnɒnˌrezəˈrɛkʃən/ | n. | 不复活；永不复生 | The absence or impossibility of resurrection; the state of remaining dead permanently.  It is a relatively rare and newly coined word, typically found in theological or philosophical contexts discussing the finality of death.  |  The belief in nonresurrection is a central tenet of some philosophical systems. = 一些哲学体系的核心信条是不复活的信仰。


The concept of nonresurrection contrasts with the theological belief in resurrection, which posits the possibility of a body's revival after death. | 近义词：eternal death, permanent demise, annihilation | 反义词：resurrection, rebirth, revival | 名词
