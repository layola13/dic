junk:/dʒʌŋk/| n.|垃圾，废物，破烂物|Useless or discarded material; something of little or no value.|The bin was overflowing with junk.  垃圾桶里堆满了垃圾。|There's too much junk food in our diet. 我们的饮食中垃圾食品太多了。|rubbish, trash, garbage, refuse|treasure, valuable items|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This store sells a lot of junk. = 这家商店卖很多垃圾。
That car is a piece of junk. = 那辆车是一堆破烂。


一般过去时:
He threw the junk in the dumpster. = 他把垃圾扔进了垃圾箱。
They cleared out all the junk from the attic. = 他们清理了阁楼上的所有垃圾。


现在完成时:
I have thrown away all the junk mail. = 我已经扔掉了所有的垃圾邮件。
We have accumulated too much junk over the years. = 多年来我们积累了太多的垃圾。


过去完成时:
They had already disposed of the junk before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经处理掉了垃圾。
She had filled several bags with junk before she started cleaning. = 她开始打扫之前已经用几个袋子装满了垃圾。


将来时:
I will take the junk to the recycling center tomorrow. = 我明天将把垃圾送到回收中心。
They will remove all the junk from the house next week. = 他们下周将把房子里的所有垃圾都搬走。


虚拟语气:
If this were a valuable item, I wouldn't call it junk. = 如果这是件值钱的东西，我不会称它为垃圾。
I wish I hadn't bought so much junk. = 我真希望我没有买那么多垃圾。


被动语态:
The junk was collected by the garbage truck. = 垃圾由垃圾车收集。
The old furniture was considered junk and thrown away. = 旧家具被认为是垃圾并被扔掉了。


疑问句:
Is this junk or treasure? = 这是垃圾还是宝藏？
Where should I put this junk? = 我应该把这些垃圾放在哪里？


否定句:
This isn't junk; it's an antique. = 这不是垃圾；这是一件古董。
That old radio isn't junk; it still works. = 那台旧收音机不是垃圾；它还能用。
