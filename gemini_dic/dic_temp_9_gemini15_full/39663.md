conservator:/kɒnˈsɜːveɪtə(r)/| n. |馆长；保护者；保守派人士 | A conservator is a person who preserves, protects, or restores something, especially works of art or historical objects.  They also may be a person who advocates for the preservation of traditional values and practices. | The museum hired a conservator to restore the damaged painting.  博物馆聘请了一位馆长修复受损的油画。

The conservator carefully cleaned the ancient artifact.  这位馆长仔细地清洁了古代文物。

|近义词：curator, preserver, keeper, restorationist |反义词：destructor, vandal, radical |名词
