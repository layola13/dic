satis:/ˈsætɪs/| v.|使满足；使满意|to fulfill a need or desire; to please or content.|The delicious meal satisfied my hunger.  美味的食物满足了我的饥饿感。He satisfied his curiosity by reading the book. 他通过阅读这本书满足了他的好奇心。|please, gratify, fulfill|dissatisfy, frustrate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new job satisfies her. = 这份新工作使她满意。
This explanation satisfies my questions. = 这个解释解答了我的疑问。


一般过去时:
The cake satisfied our sweet tooth. = 蛋糕满足了我们对甜食的渴望。
He satisfied his thirst with a glass of water. = 他喝了一杯水解渴。


现在完成时:
I have satisfied all the requirements for graduation. = 我已经满足了毕业的所有要求。
She has satisfied her ambition to become a doctor. = 她已经实现了成为医生的抱负。


过去完成时:
They had satisfied their hunger before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经吃饱了。
He had satisfied his curiosity about the matter before asking questions. = 在问问题之前他已经了解了这件事。


将来时:
This promotion will satisfy his desire for recognition. = 这次晋升将满足他对认可的渴望。
The new policy will satisfy the needs of the community. = 新政策将满足社区的需求。


虚拟语气:
If only I could satisfy my parents' expectations. = 但愿我能满足父母的期望。
I wish I could satisfy my thirst for knowledge. = 我希望我能满足我对知识的渴望。


被动语态:
His needs were satisfied by his family. = 他的需求得到了家人的满足。

疑问句:

否定句:
