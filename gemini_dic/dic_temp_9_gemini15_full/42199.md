council:/ˈkaʊnsəl/| n. |委员会；理事会；会议；地方议会|A group of people who are elected or appointed to make decisions or give advice on a particular subject.  It can also refer to a meeting of such a group.|例句：The city council voted to approve the new building.  市议会投票批准了新建筑。|例句：The school council met to discuss the new curriculum. 学校理事会开会讨论新课程。|近义词：committee, board, assembly |反义词：individual, single person
