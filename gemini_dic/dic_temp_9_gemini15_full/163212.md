precomprehensive:/ˌpriːkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪv/| adj. |初步理解的，未完全理解的 |  Not yet fully understood or grasped; preliminary in nature.  It implies a level of understanding that is incomplete but serves as a foundation for further comprehension.  | Example:  He had a precomprehensive understanding of the complex issue, needing more research to fully grasp it. 他对这个复杂的问题只有初步的理解，需要更多研究才能完全掌握。|近义词：partial, preliminary, incomplete, introductory | 反义词：comprehensive, complete, thorough
