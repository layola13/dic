prosthesis:/ˈprɒsθɪsɪs/ /ˈprɑːsθɪsɪs/ | n. |假体；义肢；修复体 |An artificial body part, such as a limb, eye, or tooth, used to replace a missing or damaged one.  It can also refer to a device used to support or improve a body part's function. |例句：He received a prosthetic leg after the accident. 他在事故后装上了假肢。|例句：The dentist fitted her with a prosthetic tooth. 牙医给她装了一颗假牙。|近义词：artificial limb, implant, replacement |反义词：natural limb, original body part
