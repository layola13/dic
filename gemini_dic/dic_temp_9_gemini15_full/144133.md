outsearching: /ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 超过…的搜索范围；比…搜索得更彻底 | To surpass in searching; to search more thoroughly than.  This is a relatively uncommon word, primarily used in specialized contexts.  It implies a more extensive or exhaustive search than another.


例句：
The police outsearched the initial investigation, uncovering new evidence. = 警方进行了比初步调查更彻底的搜查，发现了新的证据。

The research team outsearched previous studies on the topic, leading to groundbreaking discoveries. = 研究团队进行了比以往对该主题的研究更深入的研究，从而取得了突破性的发现。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, surpassing in thoroughness


反义词：undersearching, neglecting, overlooking


词性:动词
