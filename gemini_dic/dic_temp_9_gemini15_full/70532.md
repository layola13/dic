filibustered:/ˈfɪlɪbʌstərd/| v.|阻挠；冗长演说拖延议事|To obstruct (legislation) by prolonged speechmaking or other delaying tactics.  |例句：The senator filibustered the bill for hours, preventing a vote. 参议员冗长演说数小时阻挠法案表决。
例句：The opposition party filibustered the proposed tax reforms. 反对党阻挠了拟议的税收改革。|近义词：obstruct, delay, impede, stall|反义词：expedite, facilitate

一般现在时:
He filibusters every bill he disagrees with. 他对每个他不同意的法案都进行阻挠。
They filibuster regularly to delay important votes. 他们经常进行阻挠以拖延重要投票。


一般过去时:
The senator filibustered the bill until midnight. 参议员阻挠该法案直到午夜。
The minority party filibustered the budget proposal. 少数党阻挠了预算提案。


现在完成时:
The opposition party has filibustered several key pieces of legislation this session. 本届会议，反对党已经阻挠了几项关键立法。
They have filibustered for years to prevent progress on this issue. 为了阻止这个问题取得进展，他们已经阻挠了好几年了。


过去完成时:
They had filibustered the previous day, exhausting everyone. 他们前一天进行了阻挠，使每个人都筋疲力尽。
She had filibustered several times before, so her tactics were well-known. 她以前曾多次进行阻挠，所以她的策略众所周知。


将来时:
He will filibuster the bill again if given the chance. 如果有机会，他会再次阻挠该法案。
They will likely filibuster any attempt at reform. 他们可能会阻挠任何改革尝试。


虚拟语气:
If he had not filibustered, the bill would have passed. 如果他没有阻挠，法案就会通过。
I wish they hadn't filibustered; it wasted valuable time. 我希望他们没有阻挠；这浪费了宝贵的时间。


被动语态:
The bill was filibustered for days. 该法案被阻挠了好几天。
The proposed amendment was filibustered by the opposition. 拟议的修正案被反对派阻挠了。


疑问句:
Did they filibuster the bill? 他们阻挠该法案了吗？
Will the senator filibuster again? 参议员还会再次阻挠吗？


否定句:
He did not filibuster the bill. 他没有阻挠该法案。
They will not filibuster without good reason. 他们没有充分理由就不会阻挠。
