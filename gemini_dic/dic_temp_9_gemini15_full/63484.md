entry-card:/ˈɛntri kɑːrd/| n. |入门卡，入场券，登记卡 |A small card that allows someone to enter a place or event.  It often contains personal information and might be used for access control or registration.|例句：Please present your entry card at the gate. 请在门口出示您的入场券。|例句：The entry card allows you access to the conference. 入场券允许您进入会议。|近义词：access card, admission ticket, registration card |反义词：exit card
