hemianaesthesia:/ˌhɛmiænɛsˈθiːʒə/| n. |半感觉缺失|Loss of sensation on one side of the body.  This condition can be caused by a stroke, brain tumor, or other neurological disorders.  It can affect touch, temperature, pain, and proprioception (awareness of body position).|The patient presented with hemianaesthesia affecting the left side of her body following a stroke.|近义词：hemianalgesia (loss of pain sensation on one side), hemisensory loss|反义词：normal sensation
