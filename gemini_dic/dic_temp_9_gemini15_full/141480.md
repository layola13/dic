opinionative:/əˈpɪnjənətɪv/ | adj. |固执己见的；武断的；专横的 | Expressing opinions firmly and often forcefully, especially in a way that is annoying to others.  It suggests a strong adherence to one's own views, often without sufficient consideration of others' perspectives.  | Example: He's very opinionative and refuses to listen to anyone else's point of view. 他非常固执己见，拒绝听取任何人的意见。  He is known for his opinionative and often controversial stances on political issues. 他以其在政治问题上固执己见且经常引起争议的立场而闻名。|近义词：dogmatic, assertive, opinionated, headstrong, opinionated | 反义词：open-minded, receptive, tolerant, flexible
