lonelier:/ˈloʊnliər/| adj.|更孤独的，更加寂寞的|More lonely; feeling greater isolation or unhappiness due to being alone.|例句：She felt lonelier than ever after her friend moved away.  她朋友搬走后，感觉比以往任何时候都更孤独。
例句：The silence of the empty house made him lonelier. 空荡荡的房子里的寂静让他更加寂寞。|近义词：more isolated, more solitary, more desolate|反义词：happier, more cheerful, less lonely
