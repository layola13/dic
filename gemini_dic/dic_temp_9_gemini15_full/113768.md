It's often used in fields like systems thinking and complex systems analysis.  | Example: Using a macroprism approach, the researchers analyzed the interconnectedness of climate change, economic inequality, and social unrest.  |近义词:  holistic perspective, systems view, big picture | 反义词:microscopic view, narrow perspective | 名词
