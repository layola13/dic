greenfly:/ˈɡriːnflaɪ/| n. |蚜虫|A small, soft-bodied insect, usually green, that feeds on plant sap and is a common pest.  Greenflies are also known as aphids. They reproduce rapidly and can cause significant damage to plants.|Example: The rose bushes were covered in greenfly.|近义词：aphid, plant louse|反义词: None
