nonrendition:/ˌnɒnrenˈdɪʃən/| n. |非再现，不翻版|The absence of a rendition or performance; a lack of interpretation or representation.  It often implies a deliberate avoidance of re-creation or a refusal to represent something. For example, a museum might exhibit an artifact with a policy of "nonrendition" of its historical context to avoid biased interpretations.|Example: The museum's policy of nonrendition regarding the artifact's origins sparked controversy.  博物馆关于文物起源的“不诠释”政策引发了争议。|近义词：omission, absence, neglect |反义词：representation, interpretation, rendition
