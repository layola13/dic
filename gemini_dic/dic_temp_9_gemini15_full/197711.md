shieling: /ˈʃiːlɪŋ/ | n. |牧羊小屋；夏季牧场| A small, often temporary, shelter used by shepherds or others working in the hills, especially in Scotland and Ireland.  It's typically a simple structure, used for seasonal purposes, providing basic protection from the elements.  |例句：The shepherds built a shieling high up on the mountainside. 牧羊人在一座山腰上建了一座牧羊小屋。

The family spent the summer months in their shieling, tending to their sheep.  这个家庭在他们的牧羊小屋里度过了夏季，照料他们的羊群。|近义词：hut, bothy, cabin |反义词：mansion, palace
