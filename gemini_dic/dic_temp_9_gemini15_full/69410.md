feel-out: /fiːlˈaʊt/ | v. |试探；摸索；逐渐了解 | To gradually discover or understand something through experimentation or indirect questioning.  | 例句：The team needs to feel out the competition before making a strategic decision.  团队需要先试探一下竞争对手，然后再做出战略决策。


例句：She felt out the situation before making any commitments.  她在做出任何承诺之前先试探了一下情况。


近义词：test, explore, probe, sound out


反义词：ignore, disregard


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They feel out the market before launching a new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前会试探市场。
We feel out the situation to make a better decision. = 我们试探情况以便做出更好的决定。


一般过去时:
The negotiator felt out the other party's position. = 谈判者试探了对方的立场。
She felt out her new colleagues to understand their work styles. = 她试探她的新同事以了解他们的工作方式。


现在完成时:
They have felt out the public opinion on the new policy. = 他们已经试探了公众对新政策的意见。
We have felt out the potential risks of the project. = 我们已经试探了该项目的潜在风险。


过去完成时:
He had felt out the client's needs before presenting the proposal.  = 他在提交提案之前已经试探了客户的需求。
They had felt out the terrain before attempting the ascent. = 他们在尝试攀登之前已经试探了地形。


将来时:
They will feel out the market response to their advertising campaign. = 他们将试探市场对他们的广告宣传的反应。
We will feel out the possibilities before committing to a plan. = 我们会在承诺一个计划之前试探各种可能性。


虚拟语气:
If I had felt out the situation earlier, I would have avoided the problem. = 如果我早点试探一下情况，我就不会遇到这个问题了。
If they felt out the opposition, they might win the election. = 如果他们试探一下反对派，他们可能会赢得选举。


被动语态:
The situation was felt out by the team before proceeding. = 在继续进行之前，情况由团队进行了试探。
The market was felt out extensively before the product launch. = 在产品发布之前，市场受到了广泛的试探。


疑问句:
How will they feel out the customer's preferences? = 他们将如何试探客户的喜好？
Have you felt out the potential challenges? = 你是否已经试探了潜在的挑战？


否定句:
They did not feel out the market before releasing the product. = 他们在发布产品之前没有试探市场。
She didn't feel out the situation enough before making her decision. = 她在她做决定之前没有充分试探情况。


PHRASE->

feel out the competition = 试探竞争对手
feel out the ground = 探测情况
