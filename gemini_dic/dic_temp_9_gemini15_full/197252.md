shariah:/ʃəˈriːə/| n. |伊斯兰教法|Islamic law; the religious law of Islam, derived from the Quran and the Sunnah (teachings and practices of the Prophet Muhammad). It governs many aspects of Muslim life, including religious rituals, family law, criminal justice, and commercial transactions.  |Example:  Sharia law prohibits the consumption of alcohol.  伊斯兰教法禁止饮酒。

The application and interpretation of Sharia vary widely among different Muslim communities and schools of thought. |近义词：Islamic law, religious law |反义词：secular law
