nonpresentational: /ˌnɒnˌpreznˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非表现性的，非演示性的 | Not intended for presentation or display;  lacking a focus on visual appeal or outward demonstration.  It emphasizes functionality or underlying processes over outward appearance. |


例句：

The internal workings of the software were nonpresentational;  the user interface was designed to be intuitive and visually appealing. = 该软件的内部运作是非表现性的；用户界面设计得直观且具有视觉吸引力。

The company's new strategy adopted a nonpresentational approach, focusing on efficiency and cost reduction rather than public image. = 公司的新战略采取了一种非表现性的方法，侧重于效率和成本降低，而不是公众形象。


近义词：functional, utilitarian, pragmatic, unshowy, understated

反义词：showy, ostentatious, presentational, demonstrative, flashy


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **presentational**:  relating to presentation or display; derived from "presentation," which comes from the Latin "praesentatio," meaning "a presenting" or "a showing."
