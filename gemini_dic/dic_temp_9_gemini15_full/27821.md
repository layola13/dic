canny:/ˈkæni/| adj.|精明的；狡猾的；谨慎的；节俭的 |Shrewd; skillful; careful; frugal.  It suggests a cleverness that is often combined with caution and resourcefulness. A canny person is someone who is practical and resourceful, often using their intelligence to achieve their goals. They are not necessarily deceptive, but they are aware of the complexities of a situation and act accordingly.  |例句：He was a canny businessman who always looked for a good deal. 他是一位精明的商人，总是寻找划算的交易。

He made a canny investment that paid off handsomely. 他做了一笔精明的投资，获得了丰厚的回报。


近义词：shrewd, astute, clever, wise, cautious, prudent|反义词：naive, foolish, reckless, imprudent|形容词
