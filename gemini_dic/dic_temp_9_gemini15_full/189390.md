sags:/sæɡz/ | v. | 下垂；松弛；衰退 | To sink or droop; to become less firm or strong.  The meaning often implies a gradual decline or weakening. |例句：The branches sagged under the weight of the snow.  树枝在雪的重量下下垂了。

The old house sagged in the middle.  老房子中间下沉了。


近义词：droop, slump, sink, decline | 反义词：rise, lift, elevate, strengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roof sags slightly. = 屋顶稍微下垂。
The old man's shoulders sag. = 老人的肩膀下垂。


一般过去时:
The rope sagged and broke. = 绳子下垂然后断了。
The tent sagged in the storm. = 帐篷在暴风雨中下垂了。


现在完成时:
The bridge has sagged considerably over the years. = 这座桥多年来已经严重下垂了。
The market has sagged since the announcement. = 自从公告发布以来，市场一直在下跌。


过去完成时:
The branch had sagged before it finally broke. = 树枝在最终断裂之前就已经下垂了。
The building had sagged noticeably before the repairs began. = 在维修开始之前，建筑物已经明显下垂了。


将来时:
The shelf will sag if you put too many books on it. = 如果你在架子上放太多书，架子就会下垂。
The floor might sag under the weight of the new furniture. = 地板可能会承受不住新家具的重量而下垂。


虚拟语气:
If the building sagged any more, it would collapse. = 如果建筑物再下垂一些，它就会倒塌。
I wish the hammock wouldn't sag so much. = 我希望吊床不要下垂得那么多。


被动语态:
The wire was sagged by the strong wind. = 电线被强风吹得下垂了。
The roof was found to be sagging dangerously. = 发现屋顶危险地向下垂。


疑问句:
Is the ceiling sagging? = 天花板下垂了吗？
Does the hammock sag too much? = 吊床下垂得太多吗？


否定句:
The bridge didn't sag under the weight of the cars. = 桥梁没有在汽车的重量下下垂。
The table doesn't sag even when fully loaded. = 桌子即使装满东西也不会下垂。

sagging sales = 下滑的销售额
sagging muscles = 松弛的肌肉
