re-relate:/ˌriːrɪˈleɪt/ | vt. |重新叙述；重新建立联系；重新关联 |To tell or explain something again, often in a different way; to establish a connection again. |例句：He re-related the story from a different perspective. 他从不同的角度重新讲述了这个故事。|例句：After the fight, they needed time to re-relate. 争吵过后，他们需要时间来重建关系。|近义词：retell, recount, reconnect, re-establish |反义词：disconnect, sever, estrange


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-relates the incident to the police. = 他向警方重新讲述了这一事件。
She re-relates her experiences in her blog. = 她在博客中重新讲述了自己的经历。


一般过去时:
The witness re-related the events of that night. = 目击者重新讲述了那天晚上的事件。
They re-related their childhood memories. = 他们重新讲述了他们童年的回忆。


现在完成时:
He has re-related the story several times. = 他已经好几次重新讲述了这个故事。
She has re-related to her family after years of estrangement. = 多年疏远后，她已经和家人重新建立了联系。


过去完成时:
He had re-related the information before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经重新讲述了这些信息。
They had re-related their feelings before making a decision.  = 他们在做决定之前已经重新梳理了他们的感受。


将来时:
She will re-relate her experiences in her upcoming book. = 她将在即将出版的书中重新讲述她的经历。
He will re-relate the facts to the court. = 他将向法庭重新陈述事实。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-relate the entire story. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新讲述整个故事。
If they had re-related earlier, the misunderstanding might have been avoided. = 如果他们早些重新沟通，误解也许就能避免。


被动语态:
The story was re-related by many people. = 这个故事被许多人重新讲述。
The information needs to be re-related in a clearer way. = 这些信息需要以更清晰的方式重新阐述。


疑问句:
Will you re-relate the incident to the committee? = 你会向委员会重新讲述这件事吗？
Has she re-related her position on the matter? = 她是否重新阐述了她对这件事的立场？


否定句:
He did not re-relate the details accurately. = 他没有准确地重新讲述细节。
They refused to re-relate the events of the accident. = 他们拒绝重新讲述事故发生的经过。


PHRASE->
re-relate the facts = 重新陈述事实
re-relate the experience = 重新讲述经历
re-relate to the audience = 与观众重新建立联系
re-relate the context = 重新阐述语境
re-relate the findings = 重新阐述研究结果
