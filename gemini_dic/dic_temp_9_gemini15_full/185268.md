retransmitted:/ˌriːtrænzˈmɪtɪd/| v. |再发送，重新传送|Sent again; transmitted a second time.  The message was retransmitted after the initial attempt failed.  |The signal was retransmitted after experiencing interference.|近义词：rebroadcast, relayed|反义词：received, deleted.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retransmit the data every hour. = 他们每小时重新传输一次数据。
The satellite retransmits signals across the globe. = 卫星在全球范围内重新传输信号。

一般过去时:
The radio station retransmitted the emergency announcement. = 收音机电台重新播送了紧急通知。
The server retransmitted the corrupted file. = 服务器重新传输了损坏的文件。

现在完成时:
The network has retransmitted the package successfully. = 网络已经成功地重新传输了数据包。
We have retransmitted the message several times. = 我们已经重新发送了这条消息几次。

过去完成时:
The system had retransmitted the data before the power outage. = 在停电之前，系统已经重新传输了数据。
They had retransmitted the information multiple times before it finally went through. = 在信息最终传输成功之前，他们已经重新传输了多次。

将来时:

They will retransmit the signal once the interference clears. = 一旦干扰消除，他们将重新传输信号。
