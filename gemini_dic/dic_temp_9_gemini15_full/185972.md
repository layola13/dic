rewarming:/ˌriːˈwɔːrmɪŋ/ | n. | 再加热；复温；回暖 | The process of heating something again, especially food that has been cooked and cooled.  It also refers to the warming of something that has cooled down, such as the Earth's climate. | Example: The rewarming of the leftovers was quick and easy in the microwave.  剩菜在微波炉里很快就被重新加热了。

The rewarming of the planet is a serious concern. 地球变暖是一个严重的问题。|近义词：heating, warming |反义词：cooling, freezing |名词
