retransferences:/ˌriːtrænzˈfɜːrənsɪz/ | n. | 再转移；再转让 | The act of transferring something again, especially rights or ownership.  It often implies a secondary or subsequent transfer after an initial transfer.  The term suggests a complex process involving multiple parties and stages of transfer. |  The retransferrences of the property were complicated by legal disputes.  房产的再转让因法律纠纷而变得复杂。 The company’s retransferences of its intellectual property rights raised concerns among investors. 公司对其知识产权的再转让引起了投资者的担忧。 | transfers, conveyances |  retention, keeping | 名词
