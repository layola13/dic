excreation:/ˌɛksk riˈeɪʃən/| n.|排泄；分泌；排泄物|The process of excreting waste matter from the body.  It also refers to the waste matter itself.  The term is often used in biological contexts, particularly when discussing bodily functions.|例句：The excreation of toxins is crucial for maintaining good health. 排泄毒素对维持健康至关重要。
The kidneys are responsible for the excreation of waste products from the blood. 肾脏负责从血液中排出废物。|近义词：secretion, elimination, discharge|反义词：ingestion, absorption|名词
