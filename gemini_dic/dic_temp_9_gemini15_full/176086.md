rawhide:/ˈrɔːhaɪd/| n. |生皮， сыромятная кожа |Untanned animal hide; leather in its raw state.  Rawhide is often used to make whips, belts, and other leather goods before further processing.|例句：The cowboy used a rawhide whip to control his cattle.  牛仔用生皮鞭子赶他的牛群。

The craftsman carefully worked the rawhide into a sturdy belt.  工匠小心地将生皮制成一条结实的皮带。|近义词：hide, leather (after tanning) |反义词：tanned leather
