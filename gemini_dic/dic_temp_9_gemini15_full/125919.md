museum:/mjuˈziːəm/| n.|博物馆|A building in which objects of historical, scientific, or artistic interest are kept and displayed.|例句：The museum houses a vast collection of ancient artifacts.  这个博物馆收藏了大量的古代文物。|近义词：gallery, collection|反义词：None


USAGE->

The museum opens at 9 am. = 博物馆早上9点开门。
Museums often host special exhibitions. = 博物馆经常举办特别展览。

We visited the museum yesterday. = 我们昨天参观了博物馆。
