citer:/ˈsaɪtər/| n. |引用者，引证者|A person or thing that cites something.  Someone who quotes a source or refers to a work in support of a statement.|例句：The citer of the statistic failed to provide the source.  引用该统计数据的引用者未能提供来源。|近义词：quoter, referrer|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cites several examples in his paper. = 他在他的论文中引用了几个例子。
She cites the research of Dr. Smith. = 她引用了史密斯博士的研究。


一般过去时:
The lawyer cited previous case law. = 律师引用了以前的案例法。
He cited a relevant article to support his argument. = 他引用了一篇相关的文章来支持他的论点。


现在完成时:
She has cited this source many times. = 她已经多次引用过这个来源。
They have cited numerous studies to prove their point. = 他们已经引用了大量研究来证明他们的观点。


过去完成时:
He had cited that article in his previous work. = 他在他之前的作品中引用过那篇文章。
The professor had cited many historical examples before concluding. = 教授在总结之前引用了许多历史例子。


将来时:
She will cite this study in her upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将到来的演讲中引用这项研究。
They will cite relevant legislation in their defense. = 他们将在辩护中引用相关的立法。


虚拟语气:
If he cited the correct sources, his paper would be more credible. = 如果他引用了正确的来源，他的论文将更可信。
I wish he had cited the original author. = 我希望他引用了原作者。


被动语态:
The study was cited by many researchers. = 这项研究被许多研究人员引用。
His work has been widely cited in the field. = 他的作品在该领域被广泛引用。


疑问句:
Did he cite any sources in his report? = 他在他的报告中引用了任何来源吗？
Which sources did she cite to support her claim? = 她引用了哪些来源来支持她的说法？


否定句:
He did not cite any supporting evidence. = 他没有引用任何支持性证据。
She failed to cite the original author's work. = 她未能引用原作者的作品。


PHRASE->
cite a source = 引用一个来源
cite an example = 举个例子
cite evidence = 引用证据
cite a case = 引用一个案例
cite a precedent = 引用先例


词源:
The word "citer" comes from the verb "cite," which originates from the Latin word "citāre," meaning "to summon, to call, to quote."  The meaning evolved to include the act of quoting a source or authority.
