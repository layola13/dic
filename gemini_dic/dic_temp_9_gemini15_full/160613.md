portage:/ˈpɔːrtɪdʒ/| n. v.|搬运；陆路运输；水陆联运|The act of carrying boats or goods overland between navigable waterways;  a route or carrying place used for this purpose.  Also, the carrying of goods or supplies over land, especially between bodies of water.|例句：The portage around the falls was difficult and time-consuming.  瀑布周围的陆路运输既困难又费时。|近义词：transport, carry, haul|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They portage the canoes around the rapids. = 他们在激流附近搬运独木舟。
We portage supplies to the remote campsite. = 我们将补给品搬运到偏远的营地。


一般过去时:
The explorers portaged their equipment across the isthmus. = 探险家们将他们的装备搬运过了地峡。
He portaged the canoe over the rocky terrain. = 他将独木舟搬运过了岩石地形。


现在完成时:
They have portaged many times along this route. = 他们沿着这条路线搬运过很多次了。
We have portaged all the necessary equipment to the summit. = 我们已经将所有必要的设备搬运到了山顶。


过去完成时:
They had portaged the heaviest items before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前已经搬运了最重的物品。
Before reaching the lake, they had portaged their boat for several miles. = 在到达湖泊之前，他们已经将他们的船搬运了几英里。


将来时:
We will portage the canoes tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将搬运独木舟。
They will portage the supplies to the construction site. = 他们将把补给品搬运到建筑工地。


虚拟语气:
If we had more help, we could portage the goods more quickly. = 如果我们有更多帮助，我们可以更快地搬运货物。
I wish we had portaged the supplies earlier. = 我希望我们早点搬运补给品。


被动语态:
The canoes were portaged around the falls by the guides. = 独木舟由向导们搬运过了瀑布。
The heavy equipment was being portaged to the new location. = 重型设备正在被搬运到新的地点。


疑问句:
Will they portage the boats today? = 他们今天会搬运船只吗？
How long will it take to portage all the supplies? = 搬运所有补给品需要多长时间？


否定句:
They did not portage the canoe across the river. = 他们没有将独木舟搬运过河。
We could not portage the equipment due to the heavy rain. = 由于大雨，我们无法搬运设备。

词源:
From the French word *portage*, meaning "carrying," ultimately derived from the verb *porter*, meaning "to carry."
