relabeling:/ˌriːˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. |重新标记；重新贴标签 |The act of changing the labels on something.  This often involves assigning new categories or identifiers to items, data, or concepts.  It can be done for various reasons, such as improving clarity, correcting errors, or adapting to new standards.|例句：The relabeling of the products improved their sales.  重新贴上产品标签提高了销量。

The company's relabeling of its products was a necessary step to comply with new regulations. 公司对产品的重新贴标签是遵守新规定的必要步骤。|近义词：relabeled, re-identification, reclassification |反义词：labeling, original labeling|名词
