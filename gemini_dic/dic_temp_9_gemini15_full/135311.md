nonpathological: /ˌnɒnˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj. |非病理性的，非致病的|Not caused by or related to disease; normal or healthy.  It describes something that is not indicative of disease or illness.|The patient's symptoms were deemed nonpathological after thorough examination. 彻底检查后，患者的症状被认为是非病理性的。|近义词：normal, healthy, physiological, benign |反义词：pathological, diseased, unhealthy
