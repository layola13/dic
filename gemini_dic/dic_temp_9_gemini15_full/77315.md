gatherer:/ˈɡæðərər/ | n. | 收集者；采集者 | A person who gathers things, especially plants or information.  |Example: He's a keen gatherer of rare stamps. 他是一位热衷于收集珍稀邮票的人。|近义词：collector, assembler, accumulator |反义词：disperser, scatterer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer gathers the crops every autumn. = 农民每年秋天都收割庄稼。
She gathers information for her research. = 她收集信息用于她的研究。

一般过去时:
They gathered around the campfire. = 他们聚集在篝火旁。
He gathered his belongings and left. = 他收拾好东西离开了。

现在完成时:
She has gathered a lot of evidence. = 她已经收集了很多证据。
The museum has gathered many ancient artifacts. = 这个博物馆已经收集了许多古代文物。

过去完成时:
They had gathered all the necessary materials before starting the project. = 他们在开始项目之前已经收集了所有必要的材料。
She had gathered her courage before speaking to him. = 在跟他说话之前，她已经鼓起了勇气。

将来时:
We will gather for a family reunion next month. = 我们下个月将举行家庭聚会。
They will gather data for the upcoming report. = 他们将为即将发布的报告收集数据。

虚拟语气:
If I were a gatherer, I would collect seashells. = 如果我是一个收集者，我会收集贝壳。
I wish I had gathered more information before making the decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我已经收集了更多信息。

被动语态:
The information was gathered from various sources. = 信息来自各种来源。
The crops were gathered by hand. = 庄稼是手工收割的。

疑问句:
Where do they gather? = 他们在哪里聚集？
What kind of data are you gathering? = 你收集什么类型的数据？

否定句:
He didn't gather enough evidence to win the case. = 他没有收集到足够的证据来赢得官司。
They didn't gather for the meeting. = 他们没有参加会议。
