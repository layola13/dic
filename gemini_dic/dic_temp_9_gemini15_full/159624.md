polluted:/pəˈluːtɪd/ | adj. | 污染的，受污染的 |  Describes something that has been made dirty or impure by harmful substances.  | Example: The river is polluted with industrial waste. 这条河被工业废弃物污染了。  The air in the city is heavily polluted.  城市里的空气严重污染。 | 近义词：contaminated, tainted, defiled | 反义词：clean, pure, uncontaminated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is polluted. = 这条河被污染了。
The air is polluted by car exhaust. = 空气被汽车尾气污染了。

一般过去时:
The lake was polluted by a chemical spill. = 湖泊被化学品泄漏污染了。
The factory polluted the river for years. = 工厂多年来污染了这条河。


现在完成时:
The environment has been polluted by human activity. = 环境已经被人类活动污染了。
Many rivers have been polluted over time. = 许多河流都随着时间的推移而被污染了。


过去完成时:
The land had been polluted before the cleanup efforts began. = 在清理工作开始之前，土地已经被污染了。
The air had been polluted for decades before the new regulations were implemented. = 在新的规章实施之前，空气已经被污染了几十年了。


将来时:
The ocean will be polluted if we continue to dump waste. = 如果我们继续倾倒垃圾，海洋将会被污染。
The city will be more polluted if public transport isn't improved. = 如果公共交通不改善，城市将会更加污染。


虚拟语气:
If the factory hadn't polluted the river, the fish would still be thriving. = 如果工厂没有污染这条河，鱼类仍然会茁壮成长。
I wish the air wasn't so polluted in this city. = 我希望这个城市的空气不要那么污染。


被动语态:
The water was polluted by the factory discharge. = 水被工厂排放物污染了。
The soil is being polluted by agricultural runoff. = 土壤正在被农业径流污染。


疑问句:
Is the water polluted? = 水被污染了吗？
Are the lungs of the people polluted? = 人们的肺部被污染了吗？


否定句:
The river isn't polluted. = 这条河没有被污染。
The air is not polluted in this area. = 这个地区的空气没有被污染。


PHRASE->
