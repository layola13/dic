punch:/pʌntʃ/| n., v.| 打；冲压；潘趣酒；猛击 | A punch is a quick, forceful hit with a fist.  It can also refer to a tool used to make holes or to a type of alcoholic beverage. |例句：He threw a punch at his opponent. 他朝对手挥了一拳。  The machine punches holes in the metal sheet. 机器在金属板上冲孔。 He ordered a fruit punch. 他点了一杯水果潘趣酒。|近义词：blow, strike, hit, jolt |反义词： caress, soothe


USAGE->

一般过去时:
