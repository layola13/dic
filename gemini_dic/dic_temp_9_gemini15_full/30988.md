chalet:/ʃæˈleɪ/| n. |山间小屋，别墅|A chalet is a type of building, typically a wooden house, found in mountainous regions, especially in Switzerland and France.  They are often characterized by their steeply pitched roofs, designed to withstand heavy snow. |例句：We spent a week in a charming chalet in the Swiss Alps. 我们在瑞士阿尔卑斯山区的一个迷人的山间小屋里度过了一周。

例句：The chalet had a stunning view of the valley below.  这座山间小屋可以俯瞰下面的山谷，景色令人惊叹。


近义词：cabin, lodge, cottage |反义词：skyscraper, high-rise


词源:The word "chalet" comes from the French word "chalet," which ultimately derives from the Franco-Provençal word *tsalét*, meaning "small mountain hut" or "shepherd's hut."  Its ultimate origin is likely from a Romance word related to the Latin word *casella*, meaning "small house" or "hut."
