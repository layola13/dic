elbowed:/ˈɛlboʊd/| v.|用肘推挤；强行挤过|to push or force one's way past someone using one's elbow; to push or shove with the elbow.|例句：He elbowed his way through the crowd. 他用肘部挤过人群。

例句：She elbowed me aside to get to the front. 她用胳膊肘把我推到一边以便到前面去。


近义词：pushed, shoved, jostled, nudged|反义词：helped, assisted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elbows his way through the crowd every morning. 他每天早上都用胳膊肘挤过人群。
She elbows her way to the front of the line. 她挤到队伍的最前面。


一般过去时:
He elbowed his way to the bar. 他挤到酒吧。
She elbowed past me without saying a word. 她一言不发地把我挤到一边。


现在完成时:
He has elbowed his way to the top of the company. 他凭借自己的努力爬到了公司的高层。
She has elbowed aside many competitors to achieve success. 她为了成功而排挤了许多竞争对手。


过去完成时:
He had elbowed his way through the throng before I could even see him. 我还没看到他，他就已经挤过人群了。
She had elbowed her way to the front of the queue before the doors opened. 门打开之前她就已挤到队伍的最前面了。


将来时:
He will elbow his way to the front of the line. 他将会挤到队伍的最前面。
She will elbow her way through the crowd to reach her friend. 她将会挤过人群去找到她的朋友。


虚拟语气:
If he elbowed his way through, he could reach the stage. 如果他挤过去的话，他就能到达舞台。
If she had elbowed her way through sooner, she would have gotten a better seat. 如果她早点挤过去，她就能拿到更好的座位。


被动语态:
He was elbowed aside by a larger man. 他被一个更大的人挤到了一边。
She was elbowed out of the way by the rushing crowd. 她被汹涌的人群挤到了一边。


疑问句:
Did he elbow his way to the front? 他是挤到最前面的吗？
Was she elbowed out of the competition? 她是被挤出比赛的吗？


否定句:
He didn't elbow his way through the crowd; he politely asked for passage. 他没有挤过人群；他礼貌地要求通行。
She didn't elbow anyone aside; she waited her turn patiently. 她没有把任何人挤到一边；她耐心地等待轮到自己。


PHRASE->
elbow grease (努力工作):  They used plenty of elbow grease to clean the house. 他们用了很大的力气打扫房子。
elbow room (活动空间):  The office needs more elbow room.  办公室需要更大的活动空间。
bend one's elbow (喝酒):  Let's bend our elbows tonight. 让我们今晚痛饮一番。
rub elbows with (与…交往): I got to rub elbows with some celebrities. 我有机会与一些名人交往。
out at the elbow (衣服磨损): His coat is out at the elbow. 他的外套肘部磨损了。


词源:
源自古英语 *elnboga,  由 *elnbōg (肘部) + *boga (弯曲)构成。  因此，elbowed 本身是动词elbow的过去式和过去分词形式，其含义直接反映了肘部的动作。
