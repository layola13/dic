populism:/ˈpɒpjʊlɪzəm/|/ˈpɑːpjʊlɪzəm/| n. |民粹主义|A political approach that strives to appeal to the concerns and prejudices of ordinary people. It often involves a strong anti-establishment sentiment and a simplification of complex issues.  |例句：The rise of populism in many countries reflects a growing distrust of elites and traditional political parties. 民粹主义在许多国家的兴起反映了对精英和传统政党日益增长的不信任。

例句：The candidate's populist rhetoric resonated with many voters who felt ignored by the government.  这位候选人的民粹主义言论引起了许多感觉被政府忽视的选民的共鸣。


近义词：demagoguery, nativism


反义词：elitism


词性:名词
