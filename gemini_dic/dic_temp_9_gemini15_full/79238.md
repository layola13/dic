glasnost:/ˈɡlæznəst/| n.|公开性；公开化；信息公开|The policy of open discussion and increased transparency in Soviet politics during the Gorbachev era.  It involved greater freedom of speech and expression, and a relaxation of censorship.| Example: Gorbachev's policy of glasnost led to increased freedom of the press in the Soviet Union.  戈尔巴乔夫的公开性政策导致苏联新闻自由度的提高。|近义词：openness, transparency, candor|反义词：secrecy, censorship, opacity|名词
