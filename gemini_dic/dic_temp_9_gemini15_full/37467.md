commissioner:/kəˈmɪʃənər/ | n. |专员；委员；警长 |A commissioner is a person who is appointed to a particular official position, often with responsibility for a specific area or task.  They are typically appointed by a higher authority, such as a government or a governing body.  |The police commissioner is responsible for maintaining law and order in the city.  警察局长负责维护城市的治安。|official, delegate, representative | subordinate, underling |名词
