overbleach:/ˈoʊvərblɪtʃ/| vt.|漂白过度；过度使用漂白剂|To bleach something excessively, resulting in damage or discoloration.|例句：The fabric was overbleached and now it's too weak.  织物漂白过度，现在太弱了。 The sun overbleached the rug, fading its vibrant colors.  阳光过度漂白了地毯，使其鲜艳的色彩褪色。|近义词：bleach excessively, overwhiten|反义词：underbleach
