fortress: /ˈfɔːtrəs/ | n. |堡垒，要塞 | A fortress is a strong building or place, usually a castle, that is built to be defended against attack.  It is often surrounded by walls and other defensive structures. |The medieval fortress stood strong against the siege.  中世纪的堡垒在围攻中屹立不倒。  The island served as a natural fortress.  这个岛屿充当天然的堡垒。 | stronghold, citadel | open city, undefended place | 名词
