clk:/klɒk/| /klɑːk/| n.|时钟；滴答声|a mechanical device that shows the time|The clock on the wall is slow. |墙上的钟慢了。|timepiece, watch|fast, slow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clock ticks every second. = 时钟每秒滴答作响。
The clock shows the correct time. = 时钟显示正确的时间。

一般过去时:
The clock stopped working yesterday. = 昨天钟停了。
The old clock chimed at noon. = 旧钟在中午敲响。

现在完成时:
The clock has been running for years. = 这钟已经运行多年了。
The clock has just struck twelve. = 钟刚刚敲响十二点。

过去完成时:
The clock had already stopped before I woke up. = 我醒来之前钟就已经停了。
The clock had chimed before we left the room. = 我们离开房间之前，钟已经敲响了。

将来时:
The clock will strike one soon. = 钟很快就要敲响一点了。
The clock will need repairing next week. = 下周这钟需要修理。

虚拟语气:
If the clock were working, we would know the time. = 如果钟在走，我们就知道时间了。
I wish the clock would stop ticking so loudly. = 我真希望钟别滴答滴答响得这么大声。

被动语态:
The clock is being repaired. = 钟正在修理。
The clock was set by my grandfather. = 这钟是我祖父设置的。

疑问句:
What time does the clock say? = 钟显示几点？
Is the clock working? = 钟在走吗？

否定句:
The clock doesn't work. = 这钟不走了。
The clock didn't chime this morning. = 今早钟没有敲响。
