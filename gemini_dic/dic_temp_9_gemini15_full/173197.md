quasi-capable:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkeɪpəbl/| adj.|准能干的；似有能力的|Almost but not fully capable; possessing some but not all the qualities necessary for full competence.|The software is quasi-capable, meaning it can perform most tasks but has some significant limitations.|近义词：partially capable, somewhat competent|反义词：incapable, incompetent


USAGE->

He is quasi-capable of completing the project. = 他几乎能够完成这个项目。
She is quasi-capable in handling complex situations. = 她处理复杂情况的能力尚可。
