dysfunctionality:/dɪsˌfʌŋkʃəˈnæləti/| n.|功能障碍；机能失调；故障；失常|The state or quality of being dysfunctional; impairment or abnormality of function.|例句：The dysfunctionality of the family led to the children's behavioral problems. 家庭功能失调导致了孩子们的行为问题。

例句：His chronic pain is a symptom of a deeper dysfunctionality within his body.他的慢性疼痛是他体内更深层次功能障碍的症状。


近义词：malfunction, impairment, disorder, breakdown|反义词：functionality, efficiency, effectiveness, normalcy|词性:名词
