collegatary:/kəˈlɛɡətəri/| n. |同事，合作者|A collegatary is a person who holds a benefice or office jointly with others.  This term is primarily used in a historical or legal context, referring to individuals sharing responsibility or rights within a specific institution or organization.  The word is less common in modern usage.|Example: The three collegataries shared the responsibilities of managing the monastery's finances.  三个合作者共同负责管理修道院的财务。|近义词：colleague, associate, partner|反义词：rival, opponent|名词
