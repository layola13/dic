noneternally:/ˌnɒnɪˈtɜːrnəli/ | adv. |非永恒地，非永久地 |Not eternally; not for all time.  It emphasizes the temporary or finite nature of something. |例句：The exhibition is only noneternally; it will close after three months.  展览并非永久性的；三个月后就会结束。


例句：This feeling of happiness is noneternally, it will eventually fade.  这种快乐的感觉并非永恒的，最终会消退。


近义词：temporarily, transiently, ephemerally


反义词：eternally, perpetually, forever


词性：副词
