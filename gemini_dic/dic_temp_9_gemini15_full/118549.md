mesonephric:/ˌmɛzəˈnɛfrɪk/ | adj. | 中肾的；中胚肾的 | Relating to the mesonephros, an embryonic kidney.  The mesonephric duct is also known as the Wolffian duct, a duct that develops in the embryo and plays a role in the development of the male reproductive system. | The mesonephric ducts in the male embryo develop into the epididymis and vas deferens.  中胚肾管在雄性胚胎中发育成为附睾和输精管。 | 近义词：Wolffian (in reference to the duct) | 反义词：metanephric (relating to the permanent kidney)
