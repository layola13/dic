词源:  It's a blend of "intelligence" and "-cia," a suffix indicating a group or class (similar to "mafia" or "gentry").  It's essentially a collective noun referring to the intelligentsia.  It carries a slightly more informal or even slightly cynical connotation compared to the more formal "intelligentsia."
