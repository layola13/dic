consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛnt/| adj.|结果的；随之发生的|Following as a result or effect.  It implies a causal relationship between events.  |例句：The consequent damage was extensive. =  随之而来的损害是巨大的。
例句：His consequent actions were regrettable. = 他随后采取的行动令人遗憾。|近义词：resulting, ensuing, subsequent, resultant|反义词：preceding, prior, antecedent|形容词
