readed:/ˈrɛdɪd/ | adj. | 已读的；阅读过的 |  Past participle of "read," indicating something has been read.  It's often used informally in digital contexts. | Example: I have a readed email in my inbox. 我收件箱里有一封已读邮件。 | 近义词：read, perused | 反义词：unread


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This email is read by many people. 这封邮件被很多人阅读过。
I read a book every week. 我每周读一本书。

一般过去时:
I read that book last week. 我上周读了那本书。
She read the letter carefully. 她仔细地读了这封信。

现在完成时:
I have read that book several times. 我读过那本书好几次了。
He has read all the emails. 他已经读了所有的邮件。

过去完成时:
I had read the report before the meeting. 我在会议前已经读过报告了。
She had read the novel before the movie came out.  她在那部电影上映前就已经读过那本小说了。

将来时:
I will read that book tomorrow. 我明天会读那本书。
They will have read the document by Friday. 他们会在周五之前读完那份文件。


被动语态:
The email was read by the manager. 这封邮件被经理读了。
The book is being read by many students. 这本书正在被许多学生阅读。


疑问句:
Have you read the instructions? 你读过说明书了吗？
Did he read the newspaper this morning? 他今天早上读报纸了吗？


否定句:
I haven't read that book yet. 我还没读那本书。
She didn't read my email. 她没有读我的邮件。


PHRASE->
read between the lines  体会字里行间的意思
read up on  研究；查找资料
read out  大声朗读
read into  解读；曲解
speed reading 快速阅读
