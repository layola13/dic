insanableness:/ˌɪnˈseɪnəbləs/ | n. |疯狂；极度愚蠢；荒谬 | The state or quality of being insane; extreme foolishness or absurdity.  It is rarely used in modern English and often considered archaic or overly formal. |The insanableness of his actions was evident to everyone.  他的行为的疯狂程度对每个人都很明显。  |近义词：madness, lunacy, absurdity, folly |反义词：sanity, reasonableness, sense
