myth:/mɪθ/| n. |神话；虚构故事；谬误|A myth is a traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events.|例句：The myth of Persephone and Hades is a classic example of Greek mythology.  珀耳塞福涅和哈迪斯的神话是希腊神话的经典例子。|近义词：legend, fable, tale|反义词：reality, truth
