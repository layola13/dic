pipets:/paɪˈpɛts/| n.|移液管，吸管|A pipet (or pipette) is a laboratory instrument used to transport a measured volume of liquid.  It's a slender tube, often made of glass or plastic, used for accurate transfer of small amounts of liquid.|例句：The scientist carefully filled the pipet with the solution. 科学家小心地用移液管吸取溶液。

例句：She used a micropipet to transfer the DNA sample. 她用微量移液器转移DNA样本。


近义词：pipette, dropper|反义词：None|词性：Noun
