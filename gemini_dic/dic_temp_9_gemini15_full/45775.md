cuspid:/ˈkʌspɪd/| n. |尖点；尖头；犬齿|A cuspid is a pointed or projecting part of something, especially a pointed tooth, such as a canine tooth.  It can also refer to the point where two curves meet. |Example: The crown of the cuspid was slightly chipped.  这颗犬齿的牙冠略微碎裂。|近义词：point, apex, peak |反义词：base, bottom
