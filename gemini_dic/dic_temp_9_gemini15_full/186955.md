rippling:/ˈrɪplɪŋ/| adj. v.|波动的；涟漪的；|having or producing ripples; marked by small waves or undulations.|The rippling stream reflected the sunlight.  潺潺的小溪反射着阳光。|The flag rippled in the breeze. 旗帜在微风中飘动。|undulating, waving, flowing|still, calm


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water ripples gently. = 水轻轻地泛起涟漪。
The silk ripples beautifully in the sunlight. = 丝绸在阳光下美丽地飘动。

一般过去时:
The pond rippled as the fish swam by. = 鱼游过时，池塘泛起涟漪。
The fabric rippled when she moved. = 她移动时，布料泛起涟漪。

现在完成时:
The wind has rippled the surface of the lake. = 风已使湖面泛起涟漪。
The news has rippled through the community. = 这个消息已经在社区中传播开来。

过去完成时:
The ripples had already spread across the pond before I threw the stone. = 我扔石头之前，涟漪已经蔓延到整个池塘。
The effect of the announcement had rippled through the market before the close. = 在收盘前，公告的影响已波及整个市场。

将来时:
The breeze will ripple the wheat field. = 微风将使麦田泛起波浪。
The news will ripple across the nation. = 这个消息将传遍全国。

虚拟语气:
