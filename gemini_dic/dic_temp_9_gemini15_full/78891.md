ginning:/ˈdʒɪnɪŋ/| n.|棉花去籽；棉花加工；开端|The process of separating seeds from cotton fibers.  It's a crucial step in cotton production, preparing the fiber for spinning into yarn.|例句：The ginning process significantly increases the value of raw cotton. 棉花去籽过程显著提高了原棉的价值。|例句：The ginning of cotton was revolutionized by Eli Whitney's invention. 棉花去籽由伊莱·惠特尼的发明彻底改变了。|近义词：processing, preparation, commencement|反义词：completion, end
