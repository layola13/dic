safflower:/ˈsæflaʊər/| n. |红花；红花油|A thistle-like plant with orange or yellow flowers, cultivated for its seeds, which yield a valuable oil.  Safflower oil is used in cooking and in the manufacture of paints and cosmetics. The flowers also contain a red dye. |Example:  Safflower is grown extensively in many parts of the world. 红花在世界许多地区广泛种植。

The safflower oil is rich in unsaturated fats. 红花油富含不饱和脂肪。|近义词：carthamus|反义词：无|
