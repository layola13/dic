nationalizing: /ˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 国有化；使成为国有 |  The act of bringing something under state control, typically an industry or company.  This often involves the government acquiring ownership or significant control. |

例句：The government is nationalizing the railway system. = 政府正在将铁路系统国有化。

例句：The nationalizing of the banks caused widespread economic uncertainty. = 银行国有化导致了广泛的经济不确定性。


近义词：socializing, collectivizing

反义词：privatizing

词源:
nationalize (v.) + -ing (present participle suffix)
